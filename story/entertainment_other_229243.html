The people who are always good to you are your parents.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-26 23:58:56

Suddenly, I felt it had been a long time since I last contacted my parents.

My father's birthday was a few days ago.

As usual, I still didn't know.

When my mother told me,

I kept cursing myself in my heart.

From next year on, I will never forget every year after that.

Today, I called my mother,

but no one answered.

Although I knew that my mother went out to play cards and forgot to bring her phone,

I still kept calling by the phone.

I alternated between calling my parents' phones.

Finally, I got through to my father's phone.

At the sound of "hello", I recognized my father's voice.

His voice was still so loud and clear.

"Dad, it's me."

I simply asked about the recent situation at home.

My mother had good luck playing cards during the summer vacation.

There were only two more days before school started, so Mom decided to have fun for a few days.

I always felt very guilty about forgetting Dad's birthday.

After asking about the situation, I awkwardly mentioned Dad's birthday.

Dad didn't say much.

The less he said, the more guilty I felt.

My family has always thought a lot about my future.

Our family is not rich.

When I was studying, my family always saved money for my education.

After finishing my studies,

my family is still so frugal.

They say they're saving money to buy me a house and for my wedding.

Sigh,

my parents worked hard all their lives just for me.

While talking with my dad about my work,

thinking about the daily trivial matters of my parents made tears well up in my eyes uncontrollably.

Even though it hasn't been long since I left home,

and I've left home before,

I'm no longer a child who can't grow up.

But I still often think about home.

I want to stay with my parents.

Parents have always worried about me.

For so many years, their hearts have never been at peace.

I am not a capable person.

Perhaps I will never be outstanding in my life.

But I am very content.

If I can be someone who makes my parents feel reassured, I will be satisfied.

My parents have spent most of their lives focusing on me.

What I can do is work hard,

so they worry less about me.
