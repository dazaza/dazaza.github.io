Curtain rail February 8 daily flash report of prices for various light textile materials within the country

by wdkg3duan on 2012-03-09 11:13:48

On February 8, the PTA spot price slightly increased, while MEG prices remained stable. Semi-dull polyester chip and bright polyester chip prices continued to consolidate. CDP chip prices remained steady, as did PET bottle chip prices. Acrylic short fiber (1.5D×38mm) and acrylic tow (3D×102mm) prices were both adjusted. CPL prices remained stable, but nylon-6 chip prices increased.

The polyester filament market remained relatively quiet with average trading volumes. Prices in the market stayed stable, but there was uneven shipping of different varieties. In Shengze, Tongxiang, and Taicang, polyester filament quotations remained stable. For example, a mainstream factory's POY 75D/72F was quoted at 13,450 yuan/T. However, for locally produced semi-dull FDY, actual bulk transactions were mainly negotiated between parties, with a downward trend of 50-100 yuan/T from the quoted price. Currently, downstream enterprises are still very cautious in purchasing, lacking buying power support, making the polyester filament market outlook pessimistic. Spinning mills have inconsistent sales and production situations. With upstream material fluctuations and high polyester filament inventory, spinning mills may lower prices to boost sales. Most people believe that the overall polyester filament market will continue to be weak until the global textile fiber market reaches 93 million tons in 2015.

Full cotton yarn trading volume is not large, but some varieties continue to increase in price. The demand for 32s full cotton yarn is good, mainly used for air jet nylon-cotton series. Other specifications trade more generally. In blended yarn products, some polyester-cotton yarn varieties continue to increase in price; polyester-viscose yarn series product prices remain stable with slight increases, but trading volumes are not large. Pure polyester yarn prices show an upward trend, while trading volumes are not large. Polyester short fiber prices remain stable. Rayon yarn market shows an upward trend with prices continuing to rise across the board. Viscose short fiber prices remain strong. It is expected that the rayon yarn market will continue to adjust upwards.
