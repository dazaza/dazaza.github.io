More than ten fund companies will move into Taobao; online stores may open in September.

by anonymous on 2013-08-20 15:57:10

It is reported that the Taobao shops of dozens of fund companies, including a public offering fund, will officially open in September. The fund products sold in these online shops will mainly focus on fixed-income products with relatively lower risks.

According to the fund companies, the opening day of the Taobao shop is likely to be on September 9th, but there is also a possibility of further postponement. This is mainly because the relevant regulatory department's approval process has not been completed yet, and the "birth certificate" for fund companies to open Taobao shops has not been approved.

Another reason is the technical对接issue. Due to the inconsistent systems of various fund companies - some use self-developed systems, others use Hengsheng Electronics or JZ Company systems - all these systems need to be real-time connected with Taobao’s system, requiring multi-faceted testing to ensure everything goes smoothly.

Meanwhile, choosing the best timing for entry is also what the Taobao side values. Just like Yu'ebao, which became an instant hit when it was launched during the "money crunch" in June.

It is understood that Taobao has provided more than two weeks of basic knowledge training for fund companies regarding shop decoration and other aspects. Currently, all shops are undergoing intensive "renovation".

From the current homepage layout of the fund Taobao shops, the fund products sold in these online shops mainly focus on fixed-income products with relatively lower risks. China Sea Fund primarily promotes guaranteed funds, and other types of funds will also be available. Guotou Ruian mainly promotes its money market funds and offers a promotion activity where customers receive 20 points for collecting items. The "Investment Weather Station" at the top indicates that different types of products will be promoted in different periods. Penghua Fund mainly promotes money market funds, bond funds, and real estate REITs products. Huatai Berui mainly promotes quantitative investment and ETF investment products. Guohai Franklin's official flagship store primarily promotes money market funds and offers an opening gift event.

Other fund companies such as Minsheng Jiayin, Wanjia, ChinaAMC, HFT, Guotai, and Dacheng Funds mainly promote money market funds and T+0 wealth management services based on money market funds.

Taobao stated that after the Taobao shops of various fund companies go online, Taobao will provide professional content training for these fund companies, including how to formulate marketing strategies and how to perform system search optimization.

It is reported that apart from the dozen or so fund companies about to open their shops, more than 20 other fund companies are also setting up Taobao shops. By National Day, except for a few companies that have abandoned the Taobao channel, the rest of the fund company's flagship stores are expected to be fully launched.
