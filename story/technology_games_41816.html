General Principles of Dirty Tactics

I think the key to TvZ is "precise timing of attacks and perfect control."

As for ZvT dirty tactics, it's about controlling the rhythm (including dirty tactics) to make the Terran player feel extremely uncomfortable, like someone who can't cum without discomfort.

■5D

Actually, 5D isn't that simple; only those with high intelligence can pull it off (W_Y_W smirks). But there are also plenty of low-IQ players using 5D (555.. getting beaten up badly). The most crucial part lies in the operation of the 8 dogs. When close, don't fight the enemy's workers head-on. First, bite the SCV building barracks, then the SCV building structures, and finally the lone SCV. The pathfinding worker must maximize its harassment potential. In short, operation is very important, and don't forget that his pathfinding SCV might be messing with your workers.

However, due to this tactic being too impolite, it's banned in major competitions. You can occasionally use it with friends, but don't blame me if you get scolded. -_-

■9D

More and more Z users are starting with 9D now, especially with so many BT Terran players around. 8BB 9BB makes friends who like to expand and go for 2H suffer immensely. If used well, 9D is very beneficial for controlling the situation. Build a blood pool after 9D and send a spy drone with an OVERLORD. Be sure to pay attention to the 12 o'clock and 3 o'clock positions. You can change the way the OVERLORD explores, slowly approaching their base. Some 3 o'clock T users like to send an SCV to the top-left corner of their base and then stop exploring at 12 o'clock. Give them a surprise!
