Wuxi Paradise Memorial Network

by 173764792 on 2012-02-04 09:26:37

Wuxi Paradise Memorial Network (wx.waheaven.com) is a globally accessible online memorial service platform with independent intellectual property rights, co-founded by Hong Kong Paradise International Group Limited and Zhuhai Paradise Network Technology Co., Ltd. (Software Copyright Registration No. 2010SR040272). Since its official launch in January 2010, the network has developed rapidly. By December 2010, it had more than 250,000 users of online memorial halls (cemeteries). The website's Alexa global ranking consistently ranked first among similar domestic and international websites, making it the most professional, service-oriented, and influential memorial site.

Xinhua News Agency, People's Daily Online, China News Service, Phoenix News, Xinmin Network, Sina.com, Guangzhou Daily, Southern Metropolis Daily, Express Newspaper, Information Daily, Xiaoxiang Morning Post, Zhuhai Special Economic Zone Daily, Southern Television Station, Zhuhai Television Station, Wen Wei Po, Hong Kong Commerce, Korea Chosun Ilbo and other mainstream media at home and abroad have all reported on the development of Paradise Memorial Network and its related activities.

Service Content: Paradise Memorial Network mainly focuses on providing users with the following three major service platforms:

1. Personalized and highly realistic professional memorial platform

Archive preservation: Storing introductions to the lives of the deceased, photos of the deceased and their relatives, audio and video materials, etc., allowing the spirit and appearance of the deceased to remain forever.

Memorial articles: Users can write remembrance articles, leave messages, create memorial blogs anytime and anywhere for their deceased loved ones to express their feelings of remembrance.

Customizable online tomb: Offering over 200 different styles of online tombs (accompanied by paradise real estate certificates, feng shui explanations, etc.) and memorial hall templates for users to choose from. Users can also customize their own personalized online tombs, tombstones, and inscriptions.

Memorial services: Users can perform various memorial activities such as playing songs, burning incense, lighting candles, burning paper offerings, offering flowers, pouring wine, and presenting offerings anytime and anywhere. The rich variety of offerings can meet users' diverse needs.

Reminder services: Based on user settings, the website can send reminder notifications via mobile SMS on special days like the deceased's birthday or death anniversary. It can also conduct memorials on behalf of the user.

Resurrection: According to the user's emotional needs, technical processing of the deceased's photos can make them speak as they did in life, with natural expressions and demeanor.

2. Online genealogy and clan ancestral temple construction platform

Genealogy search: You can check the origin and historical development of your surname and famous historical figures of your clan anytime online.

Create genealogy: You can create a genealogy for your family online at any time and continuously update and improve it.

3. Online obituary publishing platform

Instant release: You can publish obituary information for your recently deceased relatives or friends anytime and anywhere.

SMS release: You can enjoy personalized SMS release services, sending obituaries via SMS to all designated mobile users through our platform.
