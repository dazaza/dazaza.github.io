How to light and turn off the QQ Huaxia icon?

by lwf225 on 2009-09-01 14:50:09

Tags: Light up the QQ Xia icon, Light up QQ Xia, Light up QQ icon, Turn off Xia icon. After downloading the QQ Xia game client and logging into "QQ Xia" with your QQ number, once your character reaches level 25, after 24 hours, you will be able to own this special "QQ Xia" icon: a mighty dragon coiling around the character "Hua," representing QQ Xia, giving an ancient and elegant appearance, like a pillar reaching to the sky.

To better serve players with the icon lighting service, starting from January 13, 2009, the QQ Xia icon lighting system will undergo a comprehensive upgrade and optimization adjustment. After the system upgrade, the icon lighting service will be faster and more efficient.

At the same time as this optimization and upgrade, we will also activate new rules for icon lighting. Therefore, during the system adjustment period from January 13 to January 21, all users who have already lit up their QQ Xia icons will automatically turn off starting today, and it will not be possible to light them up during the adjustment period. After the system upgrade is complete, the icon lighting system will resume service, and users whose icons were turned off during this period can automatically restore their icons after January 21.

The new QQ Xia icon lighting rules are as follows:

First-time lighting: For QQ client versions above "QQ2008", if one of the characters under the QQ account reaches level 25, and waits for 24 hours, the QQ Xia icon can automatically light up.

Automatic turning off: If the QQ account that has lit up the QQ Xia icon does not log in to QQ Xia within a continuous month (30 days counted as a month from the day the icon lights up), the QQ Xia icon will automatically turn off.

Restoring lighting: After the QQ Xia icon turns off, if the QQ account that has previously lit up the QQ Xia icon logs in to QQ Xia at least once using the QQ account or domain passport account, and accumulates an online duration of 1 hour, the icon will restore lighting after 24 hours. If the icon turns off again, then restoration must follow these steps again.
