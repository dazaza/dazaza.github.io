Quanzhou Training Institution Evaluation Results - Geometry 3D Design Training Center Rated as Double-Excellent Quanzhou Training Institution

[Mr. Lin, our company urgently needs experienced mold designers with a monthly salary of seven thousand yuan. If anyone knows someone who is looking for a job change, please introduce them to me.] At the Quanzhou Mold Technology Training Center, Mr. Gao from a mold manufacturing plant sought help from Mr. Lin, the teacher at the mold training center.

In recent years, the shortage of skilled workers nationwide has been increasing. Companies often offer high salaries ranging from six thousand to ten thousand yuan to recruit senior skilled workers, yet it's still difficult to find satisfactory talent. Under this labor market backdrop, some training institutions target market demands and have opened mold design training classes. Due to the large number of applicants, the Quanzhou Geometry Mold Training Center has also become highly profitable.

According to statistics, among the current technical workers in China, only 1.5% are technicians or senior technicians, 3.5% are senior skilled workers, 35% are intermediate skilled workers, and over 60% are初级workers. In developed countries, among technical workers, 35% are senior skilled workers, 50% are intermediate skilled workers, and only 15% are初级skilled workers.

The Geometry 3D Design Training Center was jointly established by the Geometry Design Office and Dacheng Mold Company as a technical center. It should be noted that Geometry is neither a school nor a factory but rather a product research and development platform for enterprises in the Quanzhou area, involving various light industries such as electronics, electrical appliances, toys, daily necessities, and communications. This platform provides industrial design technical services to enterprises, including product development, mold design, three-dimensional laser scanning measurement, CNC machining, and advertising design. Over many years of enterprise cooperation, its design capabilities have been recognized. Therefore, some enterprises entrust our center with pre-job training and further education for design technicians. After the new Labor Law was introduced, our center became a technical center for industrial design in the Quanzhou area. Every year, the design personnel we send out receive good reviews. Currently, in the Quanzhou area, industrial product design, mold design, packaging and advertising design, and CNC technicians account for 40% of the workforce.

The Geometry 3D Design Training Center told reporters: [We are responsible for employment placement for students in one-year programs. In this aspect, we have several advantages: first, there are more than 4,000 enterprises across the province that have business dealings with our center, and they recognize our technology and management style. Additionally, we track the progress of the personnel we send out, providing guidance and coordination for any technical or interpersonal issues they encounter at work. To ensure every student gets an employment opportunity, we conduct surveys on corporate job demands each year before determining the number of admissions. Furthermore, in the last two months, we arrange internships at employment units to help students adapt and achieve smooth employment. Given the current fraudulent practices in society, students sign relevant agreements when enrolling. Unlike university-style batch teaching, we adopt small class sizes divided into several groups, each supervised by corresponding teachers to ensure every student receives guidance. We use case-based teaching methods where students digest and understand lessons on-site in class and are assigned practical example homework after class. This is something universities cannot match.]

With years of training experience, Principal Lin is confident. Under the market demand for skilled workers, many training institutions have opened mold design training courses, teaching students about various mold structures and their processing technologies, as well as using CAD and PRO/E drawing software. The Geometry 3D Design Training Center told reporters: [Both the Pearl River Delta and Yangtze River Delta regions are severely lacking in senior skilled workers. To provide better employment opportunities for apprentice workers, the school integrates mold training schools, mold factories, and electrical appliance factories into one entity, allowing students to practice fully during their studies. This not only satisfies the demand for skilled workers in enterprises but also opens up new career paths for more young people.]

It is reported that mold design training, CNC software training, and other technical training courses are very popular. Not only do they bring substantial profits to training schools, but they also create high-paying opportunities for trainers and salespeople in the training industry. The average salary for training business managers can reach over six thousand yuan.
