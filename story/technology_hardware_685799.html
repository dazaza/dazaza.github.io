Easy Image Website Optimization: A Brief Discussion on How to Obtain Direction Links

by jackntav9612 on 2012-03-01 17:06:04

1. B2B Industry Websites: Since it's the logistics industry, our promotion staff registered a large number of yellow pages and B2B websites, publishing logistics service information. Because many logistics industry websites and yellow page websites have relatively high weight, they are indexed faster, and external links can be built up quickly.

2. Utilizing High-Weight Blogs: In online marketing, this is actually a very important aspect. Everyone must be doing blogs, but one point to note is that the articles you post should be indexed by search engines, otherwise it would be a waste of effort. As for how to get indexed, I believe everyone knows that Sina, NetEase, Sohu, Hexun, and Baidu Space blogs all have high weights.

3. Skilled Use of Q&A Websites: Q&A platforms, being encyclopedic in nature, see a large number of people asking questions every day. Therefore, you can ask questions or provide answers. The more mundane and common the question, the better. When answering, skillfully include your company's website URL. This method has proven effective in practice and many small websites or platforms also tend to reprint the content. However, building external links on Q&A sites has become increasingly difficult as many links either don't display or aren't approved. Currently, high-weight Q&A platforms like Baidu Zhidao, Soso Wenwen, Tianya Wenda, iAsk, and Knowledge Hall are great platforms, but many do not allow external links. With some techniques, though, external links can still be included on Baidu Zhidao and Soso Wenwen.

4. Writing a Good Soft Article: Persistently writing soft articles every day is an important way to obtain external links. How to write soft articles, how to incorporate website keywords into them, and how to promote effectively - I wrote an article about this before. But many people still don't know what to write. The reason isn't necessarily that they can't write; it's because they keep observing without actively trying to write themselves. Once you write more, experience and insights will naturally come to you.

5. Daily Visits to Forums and Tieba: Forums and Tieba are also good ways to gain external links. Find high-weight forums and Tieba to leave external links, reply to posts, or even just add a personalized signature. Many platforms allow URLs in signatures, so don't underestimate their effectiveness. Of course, you need to manage your time well. Promoting is like a laborer handling various tasks, with a lot of things to do. But during your spare time, casually browsing, replying, or posting can increase a few external links unnoticedly.
