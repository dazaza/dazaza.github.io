Shen Bing, a woman as flexible as water, from Guangzhou Hualan Biotechnology Co., Ltd. Initially, her love with Xiao Meng was like any ordinary couple in the city, loving each other and striving for a simple happiness and a house to live in. However, they chose a city like Beijing. But can this be considered a mistake? If it weren't for that apartment, if it weren't for the down payment of 80,000 yuan, if it weren't for Xiao Meng wanting Shen Bing to live a life where she doesn't have to look at the price tag when shopping, perhaps they wouldn't have deviated from their original life.
