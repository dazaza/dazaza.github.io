Buying planes, we all support. Speculation on Academician Yuan Longping's new ride

by ap03041 on 2008-07-31 15:59:31

Recently, the issue of Academician Yuan buying a car has been quite noisy. Some people say that Academician Yuan ordered a Mercedes-Benz SLK sports car, but it is not true. However, netizens support Academician Yuan's choice of car, believing that whatever car he chooses is not excessive. Indeed, Academician Yuan's contribution is worthy of everyone's respect, and he can buy any car without being criticized.

Field Operation Chapter:

Selected Car Model: Great Wall Pickup - Fengjun

Academician Yuan has worked hard for China's rice industry all his life. The technology he developed in the past solved the food problem for one quarter of the world's population. Now, at the age of 78, Academician Yuan still goes to the fields to check on things. In the past, Academician Yuan rode a bicycle to the fields, then later when life got better, he switched to a motorcycle, and after that, he drove his Chevrolet Sail to the fields, which greatly reduced his burden, especially in the hot summer days. It at least provided some coolness for Academician Yuan.

However, the Chevrolet Sail cannot withstand the bumpy roads in the countryside. At this point, a pickup truck is just what is needed. The Great Wall Pickup is reliable in quality and has been exported for years. It can easily handle the rugged roads in the countryside. Moreover, the pickup has a large cargo bed, which is very practical for carrying seeds, fertilizers, and other agricultural products. Although the comfort level of a pickup is not as good as a small car, it still has air conditioning. Driving it in the summer countryside is not much worse than driving a Sail.

The Great Wall Pickup Fengjun is equipped with a Mitsubishi 4G69SN engine with a displacement of 2.4L and a maximum power output of 100kW. Its emissions meet the Euro IV standard, and it was the first in the pickup category to be equipped with an electronic engine anti-theft system. Its top speed reaches 160km/h, comprehensively surpassing all its competitors in the pickup category. Moreover, the price range of 77,800-104,800 yuan makes its cost-performance ratio very prominent.

Leisure Chapter: Toyota Prius

Academician Yuan is also a car enthusiast. Cars are indispensable in his daily life. A car that is both environmentally friendly and fuel-efficient should be suitable for Academician Yuan. Driving to work during the week and taking the family out for a drive on weekends, the Prius is a very good choice.

The Prius is the first mass-produced hybrid car, born in 1997. Through Toyota's oil-electric hybrid power system, it combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor. Under the premise of high-level fuel economy and environmental performance, it achieves excellent power performance and creates a smooth driving experience and good quietness. In urban conditions, the 1.5-liter Prius achieves the power performance equivalent to a 2.0-liter traditional model, while consuming fuel equivalent to a 1.0-liter traditional model. This is absolutely fuel-saving.

The Prius has a fashionable and generous appearance, spacious and comfortable interior space, full of futuristic technological atmosphere, and its operation feel is not much different from ordinary cars. The manual-automatic transmission is easier to operate, which is very important for Academician Yuan who is 78 years old.

University Lecture Chapter: Lingyu

As an academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, Academician Yuan inevitably participates in some social activities, such as lectures. For such activities, the car used should be understated. Academician Yuan once said there is no need to drive a Mercedes-Benz or BMW to show off. A car that looks dignified, mature, and steady would be more suitable for such occasions. The Passat Lingyu, which has weathered many storms, represents this type of car. With a dignified appearance, mature interior, and good quality, this is the way of moderation.

The Lingyu's design is very simple. The big-mouth front face design adds a lot of vitality, giving a feeling of youthful vigor despite old age, which is similar to Academician Yuan's non-elderly personality characteristics. The body lines of the Lingyu are smooth, and Volkswagen's excellent craftsmanship gives the Lingyu outstanding quality. The strong body is very sturdy. In terms of interior, the Lingyu follows the consistent style of Volkswagen cars, which should also suit Academician Yuan's low-key attitude towards dealing with matters.

The Lingyu is 4789mm long, 1765mm wide, and has a wheelbase of 2803mm. This places it at a good level among mid-size cars. The internal space performance is sufficient to put people's minds at ease, and the comfort is also convincing. Equipped with a 1.8T turbocharged engine, the Lingyu's power performance is strong, making it capable of meeting Academician Yuan's needs for social use.

Award Ceremony Chapter: Volvo S80

With remarkable achievements, Academician Yuan naturally deserves awards, and frequently attends various award ceremonies. For such occasions, the car used should be of a higher class. After all, it wouldn't do to drive a Sail to the ceremony, as it wouldn't match well under the flashes of numerous journalists' cameras. The mature and low-key style of the Volvo S80 is very suitable for such occasions. The Volvo S80 has always been highly regarded, without the luxury of BMW and Mercedes-Benz, and without the official air of Audi, the S80 is dignified and stable.

The shape of the S80 is very reserved and introverted, with delicate and elegant body lines, which perfectly matches Academician Yuan's profound temperament. Of course, Volvo's world-renowned safety features have been further enhanced in the S80. Various high-tech active and passive safety devices such as ACC adaptive cruise control and BLIS blind spot warning system are included in the S80's equipment list. This is Volvo's biggest promise to the owner, and safety performance is of utmost importance to Academician Yuan.

In terms of interior, the S80 is luxurious but not extravagant, presenting a rare Nordic style. The 2835mm wheelbase means that the S80 has an ultra-long seating space, and comfort is naturally guaranteed. In terms of power, the S80 shows a more advanced power performance compared to its peers. The 2.5, 3.6, and 4.4V8 engines give the S80 strong and abundant power, ensuring that Academician Yuan will not be late for the award ceremony.

Ultimate Chapter: Private Jet

Some netizens said that even if Academician Yuan buys a plane, it would not be excessive! Compared with domestic rich people buying luxury cars and villas, compared with foreign rich people who often buy yachts and private jets, Academician Yuan is really frugal. Considering that Academician Yuan frequently travels to various places to participate in activities, the journey is tiring for an elderly person of his age. If he had a private jet, it would save a lot of time and reduce the fatigue of traveling.

Actually, owning a private jet is not a far-fetched dream. The country will gradually open up certain airspace, and the number of people in China with pilot licenses is increasing. Nowadays, the price range of private jets varies widely. There are those costing tens of millions, millions, and even hundreds of thousands. Of course, if safety performance is emphasized, a private jet costing several million yuan would suffice.

With a private jet, Academician Yuan would find it much more convenient to travel to other places in the future. Besides, the jet could also be rented out, generating a considerable income. The functions of the aircraft go beyond this; it can also be used for sowing and pesticide spraying, which would be greatly beneficial to Academician Yuan.
