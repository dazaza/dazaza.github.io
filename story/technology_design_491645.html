Only because whoever

by haitaodtj on 2011-09-19 18:07:11

So-called love or no love has already become unimportant, trust or not is also not the most critical thing anymore. I know the air is toxic, can I stop breathing? I know the water is poisonous, can I stop drinking it? We all know we will die, should we stop living because of that?

No matter how far a place is, you can still go back.

Are you lonely? The words almost slipped out but were swallowed back. Though we've never met, I wanted to cry when I first saw his face. Is it because I always feel this lonely?

I just want to create a world for him, where we rise and fall together, and struggle side by side. Even if I can only stand behind him, at least when he steps back, he will fall into my arms.

Looking at each other with longing but unable to meet in spring. If I cannot fly across the River of Forgetfulness, then I'll drown in the sea of desires. Endlessly biting and hurting each other. That's all there is, that's all.

There are a thousand kinds of people and a thousand kinds of love. So why envy others? What matters is, the person I love is right here within my reach.

The past seventeen years have been like a recurring nightmare from which I couldn't wake up. Fortunately, in the end, when I opened my eyes, I realized life could be so gentle and beautiful.

You said you'd come back when the peach blossoms fall, but all flowers have fallen and you haven't returned.

I looked out of the window at the huge clouds floating by, without speaking. Knowing where to go, what difference does it make? To me, wherever I go, it's all the same as going to the edge of the sky.

Stranger. Give me one more second, and I would fall in love with you.

There's a demon deep in everyone's heart. Even if you crush it, grind it into powder, it won't disappear. But we all have the right to gain a little happiness.

If two people are together but cannot be happy, is freedom really that important?

He is still waiting, stubbornly waiting. It seems as long as he waits patiently, that person will come back, will continue to live, living in his blurred vision.

All I want is a little warmth, a caring inquiry, an affectionate smile, and real possession.

In hopeless love, the feeling of unrequited love is similar to the feeling of suffocating in water.

I tiredly put away my tiring smile and bent down to touch his face. Over and over again, I gently stroked it.

I was not born strong. I yearn for love, and I yearn to be loved. But the things I have been striving for until now have made me afraid.

He understands the way to protect something important is to keep it in his heart, never giving anyone, even himself, the chance to comment or slander it.

I, without wings, don't want to fly; I, without rosy glow, have no intention to bloom. Faintly, I watch the curtain of prosperity fall, tired, weary, I turn into a shade of night, resting on your heart.

I want to stand at the same height as him, truly making him look at me differently, instead of merely following behind him chasing his footsteps. I want to stand shoulder to shoulder with him, I want to have the qualification to stand beside him.

I taught the person I like how to love, but unfortunately... the person he loves is not me.

Because, no matter who it is, they all have a fatal black hole in their hearts. Though we don't know how much time, how many years lie behind that black hole, the deep pain that passes through it remains.
