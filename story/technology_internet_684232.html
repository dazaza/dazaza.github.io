[Remedy One]

30 grams of mulberry bark, 15 grams of gallnut, 60 grams of blackseed. Decoct in water, take the juice for external washing.

[Remedy Two]

Appropriate amount of dogwood bark, fresh rehmannia root, dodder seed, eucommia bark, polygala root, and calamus. Honey pills or decocted in water for oral use.

[Remedy Three]

100 grams of sour pomegranate, 150 grams of gallnut, 50 grams of sesame leaves. Grind into coarse powder, put into ironware water, take the juice for external use.

[Remedy Four]

500 grams of Ligustrum lucidum fruit, 250 grams of Cuscuta chinensis seeds. Decoct in water. Take 20 milliliters each time, 2-3 times daily, swallow with warm water.

This formula is an excellent remedy for white hair caused by yin deficiency and blood dryness.

[Remedy Five]

Two cloves of garlic, one piece of ginger. Crush the herbs into a paste, rub on the scalp, then rinse with water. You can spray some perfume to reduce the smell of garlic. Continue rubbing for 3-4 months to see results.

[Remedy Six]

90 grams of mulberry bark. Crush the mulberry bark finely, boil for 5-6 times, remove the residue, frequently apply to the temples, and the hair will not fall out.

This formula can be used as a hair care agent, easy to obtain and use, suitable for all ages.

[Remedy Seven]

520 grams of Ligustrum lucidum fruit, 300 grams each of early lotus grass and mulberry fruit. First dry the Ligustrum lucidum fruit in the shade, then soak in wine for one day, steam thoroughly and dry in the sun, dry the early lotus grass and mulberry fruit in the shade. Grind the three ingredients into fine powder, make honey pills, each pill weighing 10 grams. Take one pill in the morning and evening, swallow with light salt water.

[Remedy Eight]

150 grams each of black sesame powder and Polygonum multiflorum powder. Add an appropriate amount of sugar to the medicine, cook into a paste, take with hot water, one bowl every night.

After half a year of this formula, white hair can turn gray, and gray hair can turn black.

[Remedy Nine]

30 grams of tangled hair (your own), 50 grains of pepper. Wash your tangled hair, add 50 grains of pepper for every 30 grams, seal with mud, burn in the furnace until it turns black like lacquer, grind finely into powder, take 3 grams with white wine.

[Remedy Ten]

2000 grams of dried rehmannia root, 500 grams of apricot kernels (soak in boiling water, remove skin and tip, double kernels, grind into paste), 250 grams of chebulic myrobalan peel. Pound the medicine into powder, mix with apricot kernel paste evenly, mix with honey, pound 200-300 times, make pills the size of parasol seeds. Take with warm water each time, take before meals, gradually increase to 40 pills as needed, avoid raw green onions, radishes, and garlic.
