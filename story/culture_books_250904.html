"Nine to Five" is set against the backdrop of foreign enterprises' workplace, narrating Shen Yu's growth journey as a headhunter and the ups and downs of several female friends around her. It truthfully discloses the fierce competition within the headhunting industry and the emotional confusion of the white-collar class, making it an incredibly captivating and moving novel about the workplace in foreign enterprises.

Part One

"An Gongzi's birthday celebration starts with dinner at West River and then heads to the nightlife scene. Can you finish work on time? I'll come pick you up."

An Gongzi is the crown prince of the West River Catering Group, unlike most idle rich who do nothing but loaf around. After graduating from university, he started an architectural design company, which has been doing well. I met him on the tennis court, where we both admired each other's skills. We soon became regular mixed doubles partners and were almost invincible among our circle of friends. He is known as the Guangzhou bar guide, seemingly having been to every nightclub in Guangzhou. This naturally earned him the admiration of Cat, another night-life enthusiast, who follows his lead like a disciple.

Part Two

This private club named "Official Residence" was remodeled by connecting two three-story villas, covering an internal area of more than 2,000 square meters. It features two bars of different sizes, KTV rooms, private leisure areas, as well as billiard and mahjong rooms. Its lush garden also includes several barbecue pavilions and a lotus pond. From the outside, the club appears unremarkable, but inside, it hides its secrets, with extremely luxurious decoration. It's said that the entire building is covered with the world's top sound filtering layer, ensuring no disturbance to neighbors even if it gets noisy inside. Clearly, the investors have put a lot of thought into it. Qingqing told me that her brother-in-law Mr. Hua is also one of the behind-the-scenes bosses.

Part Three

A nearby female model with wavy hair and an explosive figure exhaled a puff of smoke and said, "Why don't you sell yourself? Always pretending to be high and mighty, what's your pride worth? Last time, when Tianzhong Film invited you to go abroad with their investor, you refused and even criticized them, ending up getting slapped twice. You're truly out of your mind. Otherwise, you'd be the leading actress by now!"

Weiwei picked up a strong liquor from the table and drank it all down, grabbing a cigarette and lighting it. Without replying to that person, she leaned close to my ear and said, "I'm not proud; that guy was just too disgusting, dripping with oil, clearly someone who likes playing SM. Tell me, why can't I meet someone young and accomplished? Hehe."

Part Four

Cat has described countless times in front of me how tall, handsome, and elegant he is. I said words are not enough, and I need to see it to believe it. Bring him over for me to check out first.

This infatuated girl immediately became very alert, deliberately looking me up and down, and earnestly said, "I won't be so stupid. Women should never introduce their good friends to their potential conquests, especially those better off than themselves."

Part Five

"Oh no, I can't take it anymore, I'm cramping up."

Cat clutched her abdomen and got down from the Sit-up machine, grimacing in pain. Fang Wen, seeing the situation, quickly went over to help massage her. "I already said you shouldn't do so many at once."

"Wen, leave her alone. She's the type who won't cry until she sees the coffin. I think her attempt to reduce belly fat will end up breaking her back."

While I made sarcastic remarks and slowed down the treadmill speed, I was actually running out of energy myself.

Cat glared at me weakly, "Don't be smug. Just wait till you're my age, and you'll understand."
