Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Department (Consultation Telephone Number:)

Electrical Automation Technology (College-Level Key Major, Contract Training with Shuanghui Group, Huizhou Zorlu Electronics, Wuxi Sharp, etc.)

Major Characteristics:

This major cultivates students familiar with the working principles of common electrical equipment, possessing basic theoretical knowledge in electrical and electronic technology, electrical control and automation technology, and computer control technology. Students will study and master the theory and skills of automatic control principles and systems, have strong abilities in electrical system engineering design, installation, debugging, operation, maintenance, testing, and technical renovation of electrical automatic control equipment and power distribution systems, and can work as advanced technical application professionals in industrial automation and related fields.

Core Courses:

Electric Machines and Drives, Electrical Control and PLC, Electronic Design Automation Technology and Applications, Variable Frequency Principles and Applications, Automatic Control Principles and Systems, Microcomputer Interfaces and Single-Chip Machine Principles, Factory Power Supply, Power Electronics Conversion Technology, Advanced Control, Configuration Software.

Employment Directions:

Engage in power supply management and distribution equipment installation and maintenance for enterprises, installation, debugging, management, and maintenance of various automatic control equipment, installation, debugging, management, and maintenance of electric locomotives' AC/DC transmission, modern building industry's power supply equipment installation and repair, various lighting equipment management and repair, relevant scientific research and teaching units' electrical operation and management, electrical automatic control system's engineering construction, technical development, etc.

Numerical Control Technology (Provincial Characteristic Major, Contract Training with World Link Group, etc.)

Major Characteristics:

Adopting a "work-study combination" training model, focusing on enhancing students' professional ability, methodological ability, and social ability education. Cultivate front-line production personnel who engage in numerical control programming, numerical control processing, numerical control equipment installation and debugging, repair, numerical control machine tool equipment operation, maintenance, and numerical control processing technology management as advanced technical application professionals.

Core Courses:

Fundamentals of Mechanical Manufacturing, Electric and Electronic Technology, Mechanical Manufacturing Processes and Fixtures, CAD, CNC Machines and Operations, CNC Machining Processes and Programming, CAM Software Applications (PRO/E, UG), and Metalworking Internships and Production Practice.

Employment Directions:

Engage in the preparation of CNC machining processes, CNC machining program compilation, CNC machine tool operation and processing, installation, debugging, maintenance, and repair of CNC machine tools, ordinary machine tools' numerical control transformation, mechanical product design and development, workshop team and other grassroots department management work.
