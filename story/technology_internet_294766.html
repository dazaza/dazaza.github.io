Imperial Concubine Curses the World

by vy6688mo on 2009-12-06 01:25:47

"Fei Huo Tianxia" Introduction: Fei Huo Tianxia. Haven't heard of someone getting struck by lightning and then time-traveling, but I was that unlucky person! Heaven must have thought I had it too easy before, so it sent me here to suffer. The handsome guy is indeed handsome, but he's prickly! Suspecting me for no reason, imprisoning me, torturing me, well, isn't it just pain? Passing out means no feeling! I'll endure it! Demoting me to a lowly slave, forcibly taking me, yet still looking down on me, calling me a loose woman, shaving my hair off, no matter, short hair is also quite trendy. Carving his words in private places on my body, doesn't matter either, consider it a tattoo! I wanted to leave, but he used the child as an excuse to hold me back, confining me. He has a wife, I didn't want to care, having me and then a new love interest, even letting her enter on the same day as me, just that she is a secondary consort while I am a concubine! Finally one day, when I was framed, caught in bed with his guard, and when the child in my belly was cruelly aborted by him, my heart slowly turned cold-blooded. I shouldn't and won't believe anyone anymore. From now on, I will no longer be weak, I need to become strong! On the day I left him, I resolutely told him, "I hope we never meet again in this life, and if we do, we will be enemies!" —————————————— At the banquet for the coronation of the Crown Prince, Si Mu Ying sat on the Nine-Dragon Auspicious Gold-Plated Sandalwood Chair, his innate majesty and noble elegance blending perfectly, extraordinarily handsome, and I sat by his side, in the most prestigious position among the consorts. "Zheng'er, come and meet Consort Lan," Si Mu Ying waved to Si Mu Zheng. "Your subject..." When he clearly saw my face, shock filled his eyes... He stood there like a stone statue, the person he had been crazily searching for night and day finally appeared before him under such circumstances... His father's woman... Never could have imagined... ——————————————— However, I am not satisfied with this. The revenge has just begun, and I can no longer stop... Fierce revenge, in the end, I lost myself... I used my power, cultivated loyal followers, interfered in politics, eliminated opponents. A political upheaval, self-destruction! But unexpectedly, the mantis catching the cicada, the oriole behind... And at this point, I was shocked to discover that what I considered my biggest chip was not at all what I thought it was... National disgrace, family revenge, internal worries and external troubles, fratricidal strife, I am the culprit... I regretted it immensely, but those who loved me, and those I loved, were all hurt because of me... Heaven, I already know I was wrong! Can you give me another chance, to start over?
