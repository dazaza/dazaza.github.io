Baosteel's "11th Five-Year Plan" energy saving and emission reduction efforts achieve marked results

by dknfkkgfed1 on 2012-02-06 09:56:24

Baosteel's "11th Five-Year Plan" achievements in energy conservation and emissions reduction - Steel Information - Industry News - Building Materials Network. In 2010, the desulfurization project of two 265 square meter sintering machines at Baosteel officially started. After the project was put into operation, it could reduce sulfur dioxide emissions by 10,543 tons per year and smoke dust emissions by 516 tons. This was one of the major measures taken by Baosteel to seriously fulfill its corporate and social responsibilities, play the role of environmental protection, restore the blue water and sky of Benxi, and effectively increase the intensity of energy conservation and emissions reduction.

In accordance with the national steel industry development policy, during the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, Baosteel conscientiously implemented the scientific development concept, greatly strengthened energy conservation, emissions reduction, and structural adjustment, focused on the energy conservation and emissions reduction goals, further increased investment in key pollution sources treatment, implemented various environmental governance and pollution reduction projects, significantly reduced pollutant emissions, and achieved obvious results in energy conservation and emissions reduction work. Now, Baosteel has entered the fast lane for developing a circular economy and achieving scientific development, steadily advancing towards becoming an internationally competitive resource-conserving and environment-friendly high-quality plate base.

The steel industry is the sector with the greatest potential for energy conservation and emissions reduction and holds a pivotal position in the work of energy conservation and emissions reduction. As a state-owned extra-large steel enterprise with a history of over a hundred years, Baosteel has an inescapable responsibility for energy conservation and emissions reduction to ensure the sustainable development of the enterprise. The decision-makers of Baosteel have a long-term vision, a broad perspective, and a clear understanding that energy conservation and emissions reduction are major measures to transform Baosteel's development mode, improve the quality and benefits of industrial development, and achieve sustainable development. It is also an important means to promote the elimination of backward production capacity and a necessary requirement for following the path of new industrialization with low consumption, low emissions, high efficiency, and high output. Energy conservation and emissions reduction are not only needs for enterprise production and construction but also long-term behaviors for enterprise development. They are the winning strategies to ensure Baosteel's sustainable development. Therefore, Baosteel established a comprehensive, coordinated, and sustainable scientific development concept, led enterprise development with the "green steel" concept, comprehensively promoted the steady implementation of Baosteel's energy conservation and emissions reduction structural adjustment projects, and strived to achieve a win-win situation in economic benefits, social benefits, and environmental benefits. Not only does it aim to build an internationally competitive high-quality plate base with complete varieties and high quality, but it also aims to build an internationally competitive resource-conserving and environment-friendly high-quality plate base.

During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, Baosteel did not only regard energy conservation, emissions reduction, and the elimination of backward production capacity as the obligations of the enterprise but also regarded them as social responsibilities to be fully fulfilled. Under the impetus of these "double" responsibilities, in order to build Baosteel into a green and ecological enterprise and restore the blue water and sky of Benxi, Baosteel took effective measures and "strongly acted" by implementing 78 types of environmental governance and pollution reduction projects, among which 22 were the main pollutant reduction projects verified and confirmed by the National Environmental Protection Department. Through the adoption of large-scale equipment and various advanced technologies, Baosteel vigorously carried out energy conservation and emissions reduction work. During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, Baosteel completely shut down and eliminated a large number of backward industrial facilities such as the original No. 1 Iron Plant and increased efforts in energy conservation, emissions reduction, and comprehensive utilization of resources. It successively completed technological renovations and constructions such as 4747 cubic meters of energy saving and emissions reduction, the construction of large-scale energy-saving and environmentally friendly blast furnaces and dry quenching coke, 180-ton steelmaking converters and refining, and many other high-tech energy conservation and emissions reduction transformations. Baosteel also organized comprehensive technical attacks on reducing consumption and increasing water conservation benefits, effectively reducing the comprehensive energy consumption and fresh water consumption per ton of steel. Additionally, a series of high-precision and advanced projects such as Baosteel's largest 2300mm wide plate slab hot continuous rolling unit, automobile plates, and high-end home appliance plates have made Baosteel take a big step towards becoming an environmentally friendly enterprise with clean production, optimizing and upgrading the product and process structure, significantly improving the overall equipment level, and some production processes have reached the world's leading levels.

The strong promotion and implementation of various energy conservation and emissions reduction works have effectively promoted Baosteel's scientific development and achieved gratifying results. During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, Baosteel realized a total reduction of sulfur dioxide emissions by 32,576 tons, particulate matter emissions by 28,845 tons, wastewater emissions by 187.3 million tons, and COD emissions by 2,148.6 tons; compared to 2005, in 2010, steel production increased by 53.68%, sulfur dioxide emissions per ton of steel decreased from 5.48 kg to 2.4 kg, decreasing by 56.2%; particulate matter emissions per ton of steel dropped from 4.95 kg to 1.58 kg, decreasing by 68.1%; wastewater emissions per ton of steel fell from 5.94 tons to 1.95 tons, decreasing by 67.17%; COD emissions per ton of steel decreased from 0.41 kg to 0.1 kg, decreasing by 75.6%. The comprehensive emission qualification rate of pollutants improved from 92.46% to 96.91%, increasing by 4.45%. The strong promotion and implementation of various energy conservation and emissions reduction works have made the sky bluer, the water clearer, and the air fresher in Benxi. Just from the perspective of air quality, the number of good weather days has increased from 291 days in 2005 to the current 343 days, and Baosteel has made outstanding contributions to the improvement of air quality in Benxi.

During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, Baosteel adhered to optimizing the adjustment of energy conservation and emissions reduction structures, realizing the transformation from building an internationally competitive high-quality plate base to constructing a resource-conserving enterprise, enhancing product competitiveness and enterprise economic benefits, and making due contributions to revitalizing the old industrial bases in Northeast China and improving regional environmental quality. Facing the new blueprint of the "12th Five-Year Plan" development, Baosteel's energy conservation and emissions reduction work remains arduous and requires relentless effort and perseverance. We believe that on the green development path, Baosteel will move forward more firmly and resolutely.
