Emerald Valley Color Pool -- the best shooting place for "Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon"

by jj0505 on 2012-02-18 14:21:11

Not only does Huangshan City have the world's first mountain - Mount Huangshan Scenic Area, which is naturally formed, but also at its foot there is a equally beautiful natural scenery. That is the Feicui Valley (Emerald Valley), known as the "world's first clear water", and once served as the filming site for Ang Lee-directed "Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon" and "The Lover's Valley".

Emerald Valley is about 12KM away from Tangkou Town in Huangshan District, and is the longest valley of Mount Huangshan East Sea, with a depth of about 20 kilometers. There are more than 200 natural color pools of various sizes in the valley, glittering and colorful. It was named "Lovers' Valley" because in 1988, 36 young men and women from Shanghai met in this valley while traveling to Mount Huangshan. At that time, this scenic spot had not been developed, the road was bumpy, and even no way to go. They could only encourage each other, support each other, and overcome many difficulties to escape from danger. After they returned to Shanghai, 10 pairs of them became lifelong partners, and many of them were first acquainted in the Emerald Valley, so someone suggested renaming this valley as "Lovers' Valley".

The best tourist season for Emerald Valley is summer, and the touring time is generally 2 hours. The touring route is roughly as follows:

Blue Lion Pool -> Clam Pool -> Dragon Phoenix Pool -> Flower Mirror Pool -> Lovers' Bridge -> Green Hairpin Pool -> Love Pavilion -> Love Stone -> Alchemy Platform

The most essence part of the journey includes numerous colorful color pools such as Blue Lion Pool, Clam Pool, etc., and the "beauty sitting love map" love stone that is most widely spread on the Internet.

Entrance fee for Emerald Valley: 50 yuan per person (students, soldiers, people over 60 years old, and teachers during summer vacation can enjoy half-price discounts with valid documents)

Transportation guide: Take the bus from Huangshan City Long-distance Bus Passenger Transport Station to Taiping via Tunxi, about 1.5 hours to reach the entrance of Emerald Valley Scenic Area, ticket price: 15 yuan per person.

Precautions: Many stones in the scenic area are relatively smooth due to long-term rainwater flushing, please pay attention to safety when taking photos. The pool water is deep and cold, do not swim or take a bath in the scenic area.
