War Soul Shenjun: An elementary school teacher was criminally detained for molesting five female students in class one after another.

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-29 15:00:45

Evening Post report: A teacher in Shou County, Lu'an City, was found to have openly molested female students in class. After investigation by relevant authorities, it was confirmed that the teacher had molested five female students. Currently, the teacher has been criminally detained by the local police.

**Phenomenon: Students cry when they hear about going to school**

After the start of the spring semester in 2012, parents of several female students from Class (2), Grade 5 at the No. 1 Primary School in Sanjiao Town, Shou County, Lu'an City, noticed their children were reluctant to go to school. Some students even cried and made a fuss when they heard about going to school.

One parent told reporters that after the start of the spring semester, his daughter asked to be transferred to another class and refused to return to her original class. He also noticed that her math scores had dropped significantly compared to grades two and three. When picking up and dropping off his child, he learned that many classmates in her class wanted to transfer as well.

"Is it a problem with the teaching method of the math teacher or something else?" The parents became curious about why the children wanted to transfer classes.

**Subsequently**, after inquiries at the school, several parents learned that one girl from Class (2), Grade 5, had already transferred schools, and there were cases where girls felt nervous and fearful when it came to math classes.

**Reason: Teacher molested female students in class**

What exactly happened to make the students so afraid of going to school and want to transfer classes? After repeated questioning by one girl's grandmother, she finally said, "It's an ugly thing, I don't want to talk about it."

After careful questioning by the parents, they finally received a shocking message: the head teacher and math teacher, Mr. Lü, openly molested students in class.

A parent told reporters that their child said during math class, "Mr. Lü would reach inside the child’s collar and touch them." This situation mostly occurred when Mr. Lü assigned homework in class. Students also reflected that most of the girls who were "touched" by Mr. Lü sat in the back row. Parents also learned that Mr. Lü's actions against the girls were all carried out openly in class, and the whole class knew about it.

Because they were afraid of Mr. Lü, the girls who were "bullied" chose to endure silently, neither daring to tell other teachers nor their parents.

**Outcome: The involved teacher was criminally detained**

According to the principal of the No. 1 Primary School in Sanjiao Town, Mr. Wang, after investigation by the local police, it was confirmed that Mr. Lü had molested five students. Principal Wang introduced that Mr. Lü is 54 years old and was transferred to the school in September 2010, serving as both a homeroom teacher and math teacher. In the autumn semester of 2011, Mr. Lü served as the homeroom teacher for Class (2), Grade 5, and taught math to that class.

"The students didn’t say anything, so our school had no idea," Principal Wang said. Before the case broke, the school had never received any reports from students or parents regarding Mr. Lü's inappropriate behavior towards female students.

On February 23, angry parents reported the case to the Sanjiao Police Station in Shou County. That afternoon, Mr. Lü was taken away by the local police. Reporters learned from the Shou County Public Security Bureau that Mr. Lü has been criminally detained on suspicion of molesting children.
