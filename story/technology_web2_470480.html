Century互联reports a net loss of $3.5 million in the second quarter

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-04 09:37:44

Sina Technology News, Beijing Time, August 4th, early morning news, Century互联 (Nasdaq: VNET) today announced its unaudited financial results for the second quarter of the 2011 fiscal year ended June 30. The report shows that Century互联's net revenue in the second quarter was RMB 230.4 million (approximately USD 35.6 million), an increase of 104.6% compared to RMB 112.6 million in the same period last year; net loss from continuing operations was RMB 22.8 million (approximately USD 3.5 million), Zhengzhou Google Optimization, compared to a net profit of RMB 5.8 million from continuing operations in the same period last year.

Key Performance Indicators:

- Century互联's net revenue in the second quarter was RMB 230.4 million (approximately USD 35.6 million), an increase of 104.6% compared to RMB 112.6 million in the same period last year;

- Century互联's adjusted EBITDA (earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization) in the second quarter was RMB 47.2 million (approximately USD 7.3 million), an increase of 195.0% compared to RMB 16 million in the same period last year. Century互联 defines adjusted EBITDA as EBITDA excluding share-based compensation expenses and changes in the fair value of contingent consideration payable.

- Century互联's adjusted EBITDA margin in the second quarter was 20.5%, higher than 14.2% in the same period last year. Century互联 defines adjusted EBITDA margin as the percentage of adjusted EBITDA in total net revenue.

- Century互联's net loss from continuing operations in the second quarter was RMB 22.8 million (approximately USD 3.5 million), compared to a net profit of RMB 5.8 million from continuing operations in the same period last year;

- Century互联's adjusted net profit in the second quarter was RMB 34 million (approximately USD 5.3 million), an increase of 229.5% compared to RMB 10.3 million in the same period last year. Century互联 defines adjusted net profit as net loss from continuing operations minus share-based compensation expenses, amortization expenses of intangible assets from acquisitions, changes in the fair value of contingent consideration payable and related deferred tax assets, unrecognized tax benefits, tax incentive income, and differences in external tax basis.

Financial Analysis:

Net Revenue:

Century互联's net revenue in the second quarter was RMB 230.4 million (approximately USD 35.6 million), an increase of 104.6% compared to RMB 112.6 million in the same period last year.

Century互联's revenue from hosting and related services in the second quarter was RMB 145.7 million (approximately USD 22.5 million), an increase of 60.2% compared to RMB 90.9 million in the same period last year, mainly due to the growth in hosting services driven by customer demand at self-built and co-operation data centers. Century互联's net revenue from network management services in the second quarter was RMB 84.7 million (approximately USD 13.1 million), an increase of 290.4% compared to RMB 21.7 million in the same period last year, mainly due to the inclusion of the operating performance of two Chinese network management service providers acquired by the company in September 2010. Excluding the operating performance of these two providers, Century互联's net revenue from network management services in the second quarter was RMB 22.1 million (approximately USD 3.4 million), an increase of 1.7% compared to RMB 21.7 million in the same period last year, mainly due to organic network capacity growth in data transmission services.

Gross Profit:

Century互联's gross profit in the second quarter was RMB 61.9 million (approximately USD 9.6 million), Google Optimization Company, an increase of 119.7% compared to RMB 28.2 million in the same period last year. Century互联's gross margin in the second quarter was 26.9%, higher than 25.0% in the same period last year.

Excluding RMB 0.5 million in share-based compensation expenses and RMB 6.8 million in amortization of intangible assets from acquisitions, Century互联's adjusted gross profit in the second quarter was RMB 69.2 million (approximately USD 10.7 million), an increase of 141.6% compared to RMB 28.7 million in the same period last year. Century互联's adjusted gross margin in the second quarter was 30.0%, higher than 25.4% in the same period last year. Century互联 defines adjusted gross margin as the percentage of adjusted gross profit in net revenue.

Operating Expenses:

Century互联's total operating expenses in the second quarter were RMB 92.6 million (approximately USD 14.3 million), higher than RMB 17 million in the same period last year.

Century互联's sales and marketing expenses in the second quarter were RMB 18.5 million (approximately USD 2.9 million), higher than RMB 7 million in the same period last year, mainly due to the expansion of the sales team and the inclusion of RMB 1.4 million (approximately USD 0.2 million) in share-based compensation expenses.

Century互联's general and administrative expenses in the second quarter were RMB 17.9 million (approximately USD 2.8 million), higher than RMB 6.9 million in the same period last year, mainly due to the hiring of several mid-level and senior personnel to enhance the executive team, continued expansion of existing subsidiaries to serve growing customer needs, and the inclusion of RMB 6.4 million (approximately USD 1.0 million) in share-based compensation expenses, Guiyang Google Optimization.

Century互联's research and development expenses in the second quarter were RMB 8.1 million (approximately USD 1.3 million), higher than RMB 3 million in the same period last year, mainly due to further measures taken by the company to expand and improve its own services.

The change in the fair value of contingent consideration payable by Century互联 in the second quarter was RMB 48.1 million (approximately USD 7.4 million).

Excluding share-based compensation expenses and changes in the fair value of contingent consideration payable, Hong Kong Google Optimization, Century互联's adjusted operating expenses in the second quarter were RMB 36 million (approximately USD 5.6 million), higher than RMB 17 million in the same period last year. Century互联's adjusted operating expenses as a percentage of net revenue in the second quarter were 15.6%, higher than 15.1% in the same period last year.

Adjusted EBITDA:

Century互联's adjusted EBITDA in the second quarter was RMB 47.2 million (approximately USD 7.3 million), an increase of 195.0% compared to RMB 16 million in the same period last year. Century互联's adjusted EBITDA margin in the second quarter was 20.5%, higher than 14.2% in the same period last year. Century互联's adjusted EBITDA in the second quarter did not include RMB 9.1 million (approximately USD 1.4 million) in share-based compensation expenses and RMB 48.1 million (approximately USD 7.4 million) in changes in the fair value of contingent consideration payable.

Net Profit/Loss:

Century互联's net loss from continuing operations in the second quarter was RMB 22.8 million (approximately USD 3.5 million), compared to a net profit of RMB 5.8 million from continuing operations in the same period last year.

Adjusted Net Profit:

Century互联's adjusted net profit in the second quarter was RMB 34 million (approximately USD 5.3 million), an increase of 229.5% compared to RMB 10.3 million in the same period last year. Century互联's adjusted net profit in the second quarter did not include RMB 9.1 million in share-based compensation expenses, RMB 6.8 million in amortization expenses of intangible assets from acquisitions, and RMB 40.9 million in changes in the fair value of contingent consideration payable. Century互联's adjusted net profit margin in the second quarter was 14.8%, higher than 9.2% in the same period last year. Century互联 defines adjusted net profit margin as the percentage of adjusted net profit in net revenue.

Earnings/Loss Per Share:

Century互联's basic and diluted loss per ordinary share in the second quarter was RMB 0.11 each, equivalent to a loss of USD 0.10 per American Depositary Share (ADS). Century互联's adjusted diluted earnings per ordinary share in the second quarter were RMB 0.09 each, equivalent to earnings of USD 0.085 per ADS. Each Century互联 ADS represents six ordinary shares.

Balance Sheet:

As of June 30, 2011, Century互联 held a total of RMB 1.6418 billion (approximately USD 254 million) in cash and cash equivalents, compared to RMB 833 million as of December 31, 2010. As of June 30, 2011, Century互联 had a total of 334.2 million basic shares outstanding, or 55.7 million ADSs outstanding.

Outlook:

Century互联 expects net revenue for the third quarter of the 2011 fiscal year to be between RMB 246 million and RMB 250 million, and adjusted EBITDA to be between RMB 50 million and RMB 52 million. This outlook reflects the company's current and preliminary views, which may be adjusted in the future.
