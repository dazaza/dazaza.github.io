Precautions for patients with big three positive hepatitis B

by 342002122 on 2010-03-19 16:02:32

Commonly known as "big three positives", it refers to the positivity of surface antigen (HBsAg), e antigen (HBeAg) and core antibody (HBcAb) in the two sets and a half test for hepatitis B. Its manifestation in the test report is HBsAg(+), HBeAg(+), anti-HBc(+). Patients with "big three positives" should maintain an optimistic mood, not drink alcohol, avoid over fatigue, not eat raw and greasy food, not eat spicy and stimulating food, eat more fruits, vegetables and beans. Be careful with drugs that damage the liver. Have regular liver function and liver ultrasound tests every six months.

　　Expert Mu Wenguang from the 463 Hospital of the PLA pointed out: Precautions for patients with big three positives include禁忌overeating, especially eating too much meat and sugar. The excessive protein in meat and sugar are converted into fat, which is stored in various parts of the human body, including the liver. Over time, if weight increases excessively, fatty liver will be formed inevitably, adding extra burden to the already diseased liver, leading to deterioration of hepatitis B. The patient had better arrange a diversified and balanced diet and control his/her own weight. The following foods are recommended in daily life: all kinds of staple food, lean meat, fresh aquatic products, eggs, bean products, various vegetables, fruits, vegetable oil, appropriate sugar; less animal fat, fried food, salted meat, whole milk etc.

　　Precautions for patients with big three positives 2 taboo excessive drinking, the main component of alcohol is ethanol, ethanol can be converted into acetaldehyde in the liver, both of which directly harm the liver, causing degeneration and necrosis of liver cells. Hepatitis B patients themselves have liver lesions, drinking alcohol is like adding frost on snow, which can accelerate the evolution of the disease towards cirrhosis, or even liver cancer. It is unconditional and necessary for hepatitis B patients to ban alcohol, including white wine and beer. In social gatherings, patients can use beverages and mineral water instead of alcohol.

　　Precautions for patients with big three positives 3 taboo abuse of hormones and antibiotics, medicine is toxic, drugs are harmful to liver and kidney, patients with liver disease must take medicine reasonably under the correct guidance of doctors.

　　Precautions for patients with big three positives 4 taboo high copper diet, when liver function is incomplete, it cannot regulate the balance of copper in the body well, while copper is easy to accumulate in the liver. Studies have shown that the amount of copper stored in the liver of liver disease patients is 5-10 times that of normal people, and the amount of copper in the liver of patients with bile liver cirrhosis is 60-80 times higher than that of normal people. Medical experts pointed out that excessive copper storage in the liver may lead to liver cell necrosis. At the same time, excessive copper in the body may cause renal insufficiency. Therefore, patients with liver disease should eat less foods containing more copper, such as jellyfish, squid, shrimp, snails, etc.

　　Precautions for patients with big three positives 5 taboo excessive physical and mental labor, excessive fatigue consumes a large amount of nutrition and oxygen, resulting in a sharp reduction in the energy supply of the liver, weakening the disease resistance of the liver, and the hepatitis B virus will spread rapidly, destroying liver function until irreversible changes occur. When the condition of hepatitis B patients is stable, we advocate combining work with rest, proper exercise, proper rest, mastering the "degree", and the activity standard is not to feel tired, nauseous, and waist fatigue; live a regular life, get up and go to bed regularly, and do not break the good living routine easily; during the fluctuation period of the disease, it is best to lie down and rest, and recover by resting quietly.
