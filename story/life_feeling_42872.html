Classic love quotes that make people feel sad

by jijianlin189 on 2008-01-14 09:34:11

1. Many things we thought we would never forget will be forgotten in the days when we keep them in mind.

2. I know I am not a good recorder, but I like looking back on my way more than anyone else. I look back and stand still constantly, but time still rushes forward spectacularly without me.

3. You give me a teardrop, and then I see the entire ocean in your heart.

4. If God wants to destroy a person, he must make him crazy first. But why does God not destroy me after I have been crazy for so long?

5. Will the love carved behind the chair be like the flowers on the cement, blooming into a lonely forest without wind?

6. In this sorrowful and bright March, I ride past my thin youth, passing through violas, passing through kapok trees, passing through the intermittent sorrows and impermanence.

7. When you smile once, I can be happy for several days; but when I see you cry once, I feel sad for several years.

8. Those things we thought we would never forget are forgotten in the process of our remembering them.

9. Lonely people always remember carefully every person who appears in their lives. So I cannot help thinking of you repeatedly. On each star-falling night, I count my loneliness over and over again.

10. Whenever I look at the sky, I don't like talking anymore. Whenever I talk, I don't dare to look at the sky anymore.

11. I count your smiles every day, but even when you smile, it seems so lonely. They say your smile is both beautiful and disheveled.

12. There is only so much warmth in my life, and I gave it all to you. But now that you've left me, how can I ever smile at others again?

13. Once there was a smile in my life, but it eventually dissipated like fog. And that smile has become a swift river deeply buried in my heart, impossible to cross. The sound of the river has become my desperate singing day and night.

14. The noise and brightness of the world, the happiness and joy of the secular world, are like clear streams flowing slowly in the wind before my eyes. Warmth gushes out like a spring. I do not expect much, I just want you to be happy and not sad...

15. The wind blows and stirs up the shattered years like flowers, and your smile sways and becomes the most beautiful embellishment in my destiny. Look at the sky, look at the snow, and look at the deep shadows of the seasons.
