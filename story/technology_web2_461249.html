15-Year-Old Girl Missing for 4 Days After Leaving Home, Claims She Ran Away to Work and Seek Happiness

by zxyasda20 on 2011-07-12 15:13:47

The photo of Qingqing disclosed by her family. Reproduced from the Liangjiang Forum of CQ New.

CQ New, November 25, 8:00 am (Digital reporter Hai Huang) - A 15-year-old third year junior high school girl, Qingqing, has been missing for four days and her whereabouts are still unknown. Last night (November 24), her mother, Shuli Gong, and her family began posting online to seek help in finding her. According to Shuli Gong, in the 80+ hours since Qingqing left home, she received only one call and four mysterious text messages from her daughter's phone. Additionally, she learned that last night at 8 pm, a girl named Fan who disappeared with Qingqing had a brief conversation with her family. Fan said that she was deceived into the mountains but denied being with Qingqing. Currently, the local police have already filed a case and are conducting an investigation.

Leaving Home to Work for Happiness, Refusing to Talk to Mother

At 9 pm last night, Qingqing's cousin, Hui Zhang, posted on the Liangjiang Forum of CQ New and a Chongqing forum, stating that his sister was missing and the family was urgently searching for her. He also disclosed Qingqing's and her family's contact information, which caught the attention of Chongqing netizens. According to Hui Zhang's post, on November 21, Qingqing, who lived in Sanyao Town, Hechuan District of Chongqing, left home and her whereabouts are still unknown.

A reporter from CQ New immediately contacted Qingqing's mother, Shuli Gong, by phone. Shuli Gong said that at 6 pm on November 21 (last Sunday), Qingqing left home claiming to return to school. The next day, she sent a text message saying, "Mom, I'm sorry, I want to work and find my own happiness." That day, Shuli Gong called Qingqing and was told that she and her classmates had found jobs in Dazu County of Chongqing and were living in a rented house. When Shuli Gong asked her daughter to come home, Qingqing quickly hung up the phone. After that, Shuli Gong never received any more accurate information about Qingqing.

Shuli Gong stated that after a brief call with Qingqing on the 22nd, she received only three text messages from Qingqing's phone. The content of these messages was vague and mysterious, refusing to talk to her mother. Shuli Gong suspected that the phone was no longer in her daughter's possession and the messages were not sent by her daughter.

Another Girl Also Missing, Calls Saying She Was Deceived and Escaped

After Qingqing left home, Shuli Gong and her family searched everywhere. They learned from the family of Qingqing's former classmate, Fan, that Fan also disappeared simultaneously with Qingqing. Shuli Gong learned that last night (November 24) at 8 pm, Fan called her family and said that she was deceived and had escaped, now lost in a large mountain area, and indicated that she was not with Qingqing. This call was cut off before it could be finished.

According to Shuli Gong, the 15-year-old Qingqing showed signs of teenage rebellion during her studies. She often went out to play at night and was discovered to have started dating early. Before leaving home, there was no conflict between Qingqing and her family. However, according to the family, Qingqing previously met a male internet friend from Dazu County, and they suspect that Qingqing's disappearance is related to this person.

Currently, the local police in Sanyao Town have already filed a case and are conducting an investigation. (The names Qingqing and Fan in the article are pseudonyms)

Communication between Qingqing and her family within 87 hours after she left home:

November 21

6 pm

Qingqing lied and said she was going back to school and left home without taking the weekly allowance from her parents.

November 22

8 am

Shuli Gong went to the school but didn't see her daughter, and calls to her daughter's phone went unanswered.

9 am

Shuli Gong received the first text message from her daughter after she left home: "Mom, I'm sorry, I want to work and find my own happiness."

10 am

Shuli Gong connected with her daughter on the phone.

Mother: Where are you?

Daughter: I'm in Dazu.

Mother: Who are you with?

Daughter: I'm with some classmates.

Mother: Come home quickly, Mom is worried!

Daughter: I've found a job, don't worry.

Mother: Where do you live in Dazu?

Daughter: We've rented a house.

Mother: Come home quickly, Mom misses you.

Qingqing hung up the phone.

23rd morning 10:30

Qingqing sent a text message: "Mom, you don't need to worry, I'm fine, I really like my current life."

24th noon

Shuli Gong and her family kept calling her daughter's phone, but it was turned off. They also continuously sent text messages asking her to come home and reminding her not to fall victim to deception.

At 12 pm, Qingqing replied to Shuli Gong's text message: "I haven't fallen victim to deception, I'm really fine."

24th afternoon

Shuli Gong repeatedly sent text messages to Qingqing saying, "Please give Mom a call, Mom wants to hear your voice."

Qingqing replied to Shuli Gong: "I don't want to hear you cry, I'm really fine, don't worry, take care of yourself, I will miss you."

As of the time of publication, Shuli Gong has not received more information about Qingqing.
