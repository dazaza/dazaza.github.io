Microsoft 120GB Zune Player Product Images Leak

by yinyin on 2008-09-06 13:34:07

The 120GB version of the Microsoft Zune player was previously introduced on the FCC website, and foreign media outlets have now released product images. Also released were images of the 16GB version of the Zune player; however, the authenticity of these images cannot currently be confirmed. It is reported that these images were released by Canadian netizens and were taken with a mobile phone.

According to previous reports, in terms of appearance, there is no difference between the 120GB Zune player and the 80GB Zune player because the size of the 120GB hard drive is the same as that of the 80GB hard drive.

Currently, the 80GB Zune player is priced at $249, and it is expected that the price of the 120GB Zune player will be around $299.
