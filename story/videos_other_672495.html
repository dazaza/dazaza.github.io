The Golden Rule of Men's Dressing

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-22 22:20:08

1. The global economic downturn has made black a symbol of wealth and even luxury, so a man's first suit should be plain black.

2. A man's second suit should be plain deep gray, followed by plain deep blue, deep gray fine stripes, deep blue fine stripes, and deep gray checks.

3. The finer the stripes or checks, the better; choose fabric where the pattern is only visible upon close inspection.

4. European-style double-breasted suits, due to the lower button position, create an illusion of a longer upper body, so shorter men should wear them cautiously.

5. Shirts with collars that have buttons should never be paired with double-breasted suits.

6. If you own a coat, it should be gray. The second coat should be black, the third one coffee-colored, and the fourth navy blue.

7. Shoes should be spotless and shiny, so they should never look worn out. Men's suit sets.

8. A 300-yuan pair of shoes will last less than half as long as a 600-yuan pair, while a 1200-yuan pair might last a lifetime.

9. Even a 1200-yuan pair of shoes should not be worn continuously for more than three days.

10. If your husband still cannot confidently use perfume, then he must choose a soap with a fresh scent.

11. Some pure cotton shirts are very cheap, but remember their lifespan is also very short and they cannot withstand ironing.

12. If not wearing a tie, do not fasten the collar of the shirt.

13. For a mature gentleman who values propriety, in winter men's clothing coordination, ties with cartoon characters or animals, portraits cannot be matched with suits.

14. The tip of the tie should not fall below the buckle of the belt, but it should not be higher either, casual men's clothing.

15. Belts and shoes should be consistent in material and color.

16. Gentlemanly demeanor starts from the feet; formal suits can only be paired with traditional, solemn lace-up shoes.

17. Do not wear non-black shoes in formal, grand occasions. Even if they are polished to look dignified, it will make you appear as if you do not understand dignity.

18. Never buy socks made entirely of synthetic fibers. Wool, silk-wool blends, wool-cotton blends, or pure cotton socks are best.

19. After the age of 24, white socks should be abandoned as they make you look like a student.

20. Regardless of age, patterned socks are always unsuitable for men.
