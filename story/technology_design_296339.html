by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-06 07:36:23

Tonight's live broadcast: The hesitation at the "crossroads" (note the packaging) - Shao Anlin, Gao Jian. Oral introduction: Everyone knows that in recent years, the transportation in our city has developed rapidly, and it is not an exaggeration to describe it as having smooth access in all directions. Especially, the construction of the BRT express bus channel can be said to be the icing on the cake. But with more roads, citizens don't know how to navigate them anymore.

Interviewee 123: "This road is a bit chaotic, there is indeed some confusion. There must be a little disorder." This was the live interview conducted by reporters this morning at the intersection of Qingda Road and Jiefang Road in the urban area. I believe that what these few citizens said might represent the thoughts of most people. There are many such intersections in the urban area, and between flower beds, curbs, and lanes, a situation has arisen where things are tangled and hard to sort out.

Interviewee 12: "It has its beautifying aspect, but also unreasonable places. People who don't drive know it's unreasonable."

The reporter randomly stopped a citizen who was illegally parked while waiting for the red light, let's see what she has to say.

Interviewee: "I'm not used to driving this way, it's not that I don't follow the rules."

Most citizens expressed that now the road conditions have improved, but they still easily get confused, making it relatively troublesome to distinguish directions. Not just ordinary people, even traffic police officers sometimes feel a bit bewildered.

Live Broadcast: Traffic Police Brigade, Fourth Team, Traffic Police, Ba Chao: "In my opinion, the design isn't very reasonable, it's very inconvenient to navigate. For example, when cars come from the opposite direction and cars also come from this side, their waiting affects other motor vehicles turning right."

Officer Ba said that directing traffic at such intersections now is quite challenging. Before this intersection was repaired, it didn't need anyone to manage it, but now it's basically congested every day, which is inconvenient.
