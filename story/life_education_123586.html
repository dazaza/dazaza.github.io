Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:45:09

The school adopts a combination of work and study, a frugal schooling model, first-class training facilities in the province, a simple, fast, and accurate teaching model, fully enclosed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29 Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road. It has superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "liberating thoughts, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking diligently for change." We continuously carry out teaching and management reforms, firmly establish a "market competition" awareness, take the market as the guide, and use enterprise needs as the standard to explore new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching. We have accumulated rich teaching and management experience. After employment, our students have received widespread praise from employers.

1. Configured first-class teaching facilities in the province based on enterprise standards.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan and built a numerical control (NC) training workshop equipped with more than 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, conventional lathes, and conventional milling machines. The school has also built communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape rooms, and new teaching buildings among other supporting facilities.

2. Created an enterprise-based teaching model guided by job positions.

Established a teaching model mainly based on practice. In the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students assume roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work." They are evaluated through regular assessments and exams, and outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day's "work" situation is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. Based on the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, composed of workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative in learning and enhances their competitive awareness, enabling them to better master the knowledge and skills they learn.

Through enterprise-based teaching reforms, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their time at school, meeting the needs of job positions and initially reaching enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-based teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

3. Created a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students based on enterprise needs and formulated daily norms and practical handbooks for professional quality training.

Comprehensively learning from enterprise employee management models, we cultivate qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises not only need skilled workers but also emphasize employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, and competitive awareness. The cultivation and improvement of overall quality require the efforts and collaboration of all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs throughout the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school highlights two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Adhering to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," we hire retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from daily life systems, clothing, behavior, etiquette, and housekeeping, we let students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline styles, and quickly become disciplined talents with good military qualities.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Professional Quality Education Management

In the learning process, we offer professional quality courses divided into theoretical lectures and practical applications. These two aspects complement each other and improve together. Theoretical lectures on professional quality mainly focus on practical combat to transform students' thinking, while practical training consolidates teaching results through students' "personal experiences," turning these professional quality requirements into their voluntary behaviors.

For professional quality practical training, our school signs agreements with enterprises to send our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students understand society and enterprises before truly starting their internships, entering the state of formal enterprise employees in advance, laying a solid foundation for adapting to job positions.

4. Employment-oriented, properly completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi areas, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our CNC major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are top 500 mechanical manufacturing enterprises in the world. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

5. Thoughtful后勤services, uniquely created the "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students in our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus based on students' physical development needs, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adapting to intensive studies, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students eating outside the school.

The school's student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, phone booth, and bathhouse service facilities are complete. The logistics department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, considerate, thoughtful, and convenient in service.

6. Only miscellaneous fees are charged. Source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility derived from society and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-thinking educational concepts, we maximize cost savings for students' parents. Students coming to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees, and the three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through the combination of work and study, i.e., professional quality training. The school has also specifically established a "Hongzhi Class," fully or partially exempting all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly comprehensive quality students from poor families. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per student per year provided by the government.

During the educational process, our school constantly summarizes experiences and lessons learned, creatively extracting our school culture.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines our grand blueprint and direction for struggle. The practice of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.
