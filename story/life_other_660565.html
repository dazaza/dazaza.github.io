Only afraid of not meeting again

Times Online Submission

On this winter night in the north which is not cold, I ended a relationship that I considered to be meaningful. It was a relationship that I cared about and looked forward to. In these two days, I always had the feeling that a heavy rain was about to come, it was very damp. No matter what I did, his shadow was always in my heart. Every time I thought about it, my eyes were filled with tears, and when my eyes could no longer hold them, the tears fell naturally. In fact, I know that it's over. Perhaps in others' eyes, it hasn't even started, let alone ended. But in my heart, I feel like I've missed a lot, and once I miss it, it will never happen again.

I met him through a good friend's introduction on September 22nd this year. When I saw him, I had a strange feeling. Before, my mother told me that people say it should be a heartbeat feeling, and I think I found this feeling. Coincidentally, the day we met was the Mid-Autumn Festival, a day symbolizing completeness. This gave me more expectations in this superstitious color. I felt that maybe the feeling I have been insisting on has been found in him. I felt happy, really.

In fact, for feelings, I am a very pure person, pure to the point of being somewhat idealistic and not realistic enough. Even in our relationship, I never took into account any other conditions such as economic foundation, family, or work because I just wanted to maintain the feelings between us, nothing more. We went climbing, singing, to Nanluoguxiang, watching movies, eating delicious food...

In the process of our relationship, I admit that from his attitude and actions towards me, I could feel something was off. But I wanted to continue, and he was also maintaining it with me. Maybe it really takes time to understand and adjust, I always told myself this way. Until recently, this feeling became stronger, so I thought it was time to talk about something, and thus we had our last meeting.

Our last meeting was at a small coffee shop near my company, very quiet. I don't know why, but I really wanted to hear him speak first, even though I raised my doubts, he didn't speak first, so I spoke first. I said out my recent feelings, my feelings all along, and some heartfelt words. I still couldn't control my emotions, and I cried. In fact, I had already guessed the result. Before we met, I thought I wouldn't care about the result, but it turned out that I cared a lot. It ended, and I stayed in the coffee shop alone for a long time, feeling very upset. In fact, the final outcome, I wanted to appear nonchalant, or I should have been nonchalant, saying things like we are still friends, contact if you need help, but at least in that moment, I couldn't be so calm and composed. But now thinking about it, I really want to express this meaning to him, I have been conflicted whether to send him the message, actually I know that I just want to keep in touch with him. Although I know it's not good, at least not good for myself.

Everything has ended, although I still care in my heart, thinking about whether the reasons he said are true, recalling every detail, I don't know if there is anything wrong with the pace of my relationship or my behavior that made him not want to continue with me, but these are things I can't know anymore. Although during our meeting that day, I still kept a small percentage of hope to overturn my feelings, but it still failed.

I feel very upset, feeling like I've missed a lot. In fact, I don't know, I am wondering, in this vast city, I wonder if we will meet again, I'm afraid that we will really never meet again.

Dedicated to the end of the very sad year of 2010, dedicated to this relationship that seems trivial to others, dedicated to my future emotional life, not for anything else, just to tell myself what I like.

Thinking about this, I am already in tears...
