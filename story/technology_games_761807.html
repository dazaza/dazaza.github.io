Renren Inc. carries out large-scale layoffs, affecting Renren Games and Nuomi.com

by geekzhang on 2013-05-10 19:03:21

On the afternoon of May 10th, news broke that Renren Inc. has recently started a large-scale layoff campaign. The departments affected include Renren Network, Renren Games, and Nuomi Network. Taking Renren Games as an example, some teams have experienced a layoff ratio close to 50%. It is currently unclear what the layoff ratios are for Nuomi Network and Renren Network.As early as yesterday, there were rumors in the industry that the 3G department of Renren had a layoff ratio as high as 75%. The Renren 3G department belongs to Renren Wireless Division, responsible for the group's wireless business outside of Renren Network.Renren responded by stating that the organizational restructuring of the wireless division is an attempt at an internal entrepreneurship mechanism, which will affect some employees. Thanks to the huge development potential of business units such as Renren Games and Nuomi Network, the company will provide internal job opportunities for relevant employees.It is understood that Renren’s so-called adjustments are not limited to the wireless department; Renren Network, Renren Games, and Nuomi Network have also been affected. Taking Renren Games as an example, with a scale of 700 people, directors have assigned layoff quotas to each team leader. According to sources, Renren Games includes more than ten service teams, including game development, community, and player centers, with some teams experiencing layoff ratios close to 50%. Renren Games is currently negotiating compensation plans with laid-off employees. It is still unclear what the layoff ratios are for Nuomi Network and Renren Network.On May 14th, Renren will release its Q1 2013 financial report. In the 2012 fiscal year, Renren Company fell into a comprehensive loss. With a net loss of $21.1 million in the fourth quarter (a year-over-year shift to losses) and a full-year net loss of $75 million, Renren reported profits of $41.3 million in 2011. The immense pressure on performance could be the direct cause of Renren's current layoffs.Looking at various businesses, Renren's online advertising revenue for the entire year of 2012 decreased by 9.7% year-over-year. Since the second quarter of 2012, Renren's advertising revenue has seen a year-over-year decline for three consecutive quarters.In terms of group buying, although Nuomi Network, which Renren had placed high hopes on, has continuously narrowed its quarterly losses, its full-year net loss of $27.3 million still exceeds its net revenue scale.Renren Games, despite rapid growth last year, began showing signs of contraction this year. According to the aforementioned sources, Renren Games' four original game operation groups have been merged into two, and key game planning leaders have resigned in the past two months.
