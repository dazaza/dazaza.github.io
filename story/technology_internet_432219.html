Noah's Third Quarter Net Loss $44.4 Million

by chomeyudream on 2011-05-17 10:27:08

Sina Technology News, early morning of May 13 Beijing time, Noah's Ark (NYSE:NED) today released its unaudited financial report for the third quarter of the 2011 fiscal year ended March 31. The report shows that Noah’s Ark's net revenue for the third quarter was RMB 72.7 million (approximately USD 11.1 million), a decrease of 71.3% compared to RMB 253.7 million in the same period last year; the net loss was RMB 290.9 million (approximately USD 44.4 million), compared to a net profit of RMB 36 million in the same period last year.

Main Performance:

-Noah's Ark's net revenue for the third quarter was RMB 72.7 million (approximately USD 11.1 million), a decrease of 71.3% compared to RMB 253.7 million in the same period last year;

-The total operating loss for Noah’s Ark in the third quarter was RMB 43.3 million (approximately USD 6.6 million), compared to an operating profit of RMB 32.9 million in the same period last year;

-Noah's Ark's gross profit for the third quarter was RMB 23.9 million (approximately USD 3.7 million), a decrease of 80.8% compared to RMB 124.3 million in the same period last year. Noah's Ark's gross margin for the third quarter was 33.0%;

-The net loss for Noah’s Ark in the third quarter was RMB 290.9 million (approximately USD 44.4 million), compared to a net profit of RMB 36 million in the same period last year;

-The basic and diluted losses per share for Noah’s Ark in the third quarter were both RMB 8.04 (approximately USD 1.23), compared to basic and diluted earnings per share of RMB 0.94 and RMB 0.92 respectively in the same period last year. Excluding non-recurring items (excluding equity-based compensation expenses), the basic and diluted losses per share for Noah’s Ark in the third quarter were both RMB 7.99 (approximately USD 1.22), compared to basic and diluted earnings per share of RMB 1.00 and RMB 0.98 respectively in the same period last year.

Performance Outlook:

Noah's Ark expects revenue from educational services in the fourth quarter of the 2011 fiscal year to be between RMB 24.5 million (approximately USD 3.7 million) and RMB 26 million (approximately USD 4 million). Noah's Ark also expects revenue from educational services in the 2011 fiscal year to be between RMB 88.5 million (approximately USD 13.5 million) and RMB 90 million (approximately USD 13.7 million). This forecast reflects Noah's Ark's current and preliminary views, which may be adjusted in the future.
