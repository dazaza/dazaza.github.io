Worrying Stripes

Real Leather Wrinkles

The collagen in the dermis that supports the skin gradually decreases, causing a loss of elasticity and weakening the skin's "spring" function. As a result, the skin shows stripes with dips and rises.

Epidermal Wrinkles

Due to insufficient moisture, the skin becomes dry, rough, and loose, leading to unevenness in the epidermis and causing the skin to collapse.

Dryness-induced Wrinkles

If the skin surface lacks moisture, fine lines will appear just like cracks on dry land.

In terms of condition, these wrinkles are not very noticeable right after bathing, but as time passes, they become more evident. They are especially obvious when applying foundation. The state after lightly pressing with your fingers is not very noticeable.

The above symptoms are caused by dryness-induced fine lines. Right after bathing or skincare, the skin is in a moisturized state, so the lines aren't very noticeable at the time. However, once the moisture is lost, the fine lines become clear. Since these are fine lines that remain on the surface of the skin, gently pulling the skin makes them less visible.

The solution for such fine lines is hydration! Adequately replenish moisture and then lock it in with oil to effectively conceal wrinkles. Being in a dry state is evidence that the skin's ability to store water internally is gradually weakening. Therefore, once you notice dryness-induced wrinkles, it’s important to recognize this as a sign that the skin is beginning to age.

Expression Wrinkles

Expression wrinkles often appear between the eyebrows and on the forehead. Repeated movements in the same place, direction, and with the same force can produce longitudinal or transverse wrinkles. Although expression wrinkles are somewhat related to the original bones and muscles, repeated facial expressions are the main factor. People who frequently raise their eyebrows may develop forehead wrinkles, while those who often frown may have wrinkles between the eyebrows. The general method of dealing with this is to pay attention to bad habits in daily life. Going deeper, we should consider the habitual expressions we've formed and when they began. Are the wrinkles between the eyebrows due to worry or poor eyesight? Finding the cause and addressing it accordingly is one of the best methods.
