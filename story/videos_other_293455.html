We believe that the theme of reorganization still deserves further exploration in the future market, such as Lianchuang Optoelectronics (600363). The company focuses on LED and optoelectronic cables as its main industries, with the actual controller being the Jiangxi Provincial SASAC. In order to expand and strengthen the LED leading industry, the company has a strong expectation for future reorganization.

Low-carbon LED Concept Benefiting from National Policies

The company is a key high-tech enterprise under the national torch plan and a demonstration enterprise for the "indium gallium nitride LED epitaxial wafer, chip industrialization" project. The company focuses on LED and optoelectronic cables as its main industries, forming a relatively complete industrial chain and large-scale production, including LED epitaxy, chips, devices, backlight sources, and semiconductor lighting sources. LED green lighting conforms to the development of low-carbon economy, marking the fourth revolution in human lighting methods after incandescent lamps, fluorescent lamps, and energy-saving lamps. Vigorously promoting efficient lighting products is an important measure for China to achieve its "11th Five-Year Plan" energy-saving and emission-reduction targets. Currently, China's LED industry is still in its infancy, with the government continuously increasing subsidies and a large market demand. As a core enterprise of the national semiconductor lighting engineering industrialization base, the company’s leading product, optoelectronic devices, ranks among the top in domestic market share, showing strong future development potential.

Possibility of Change in Control and Expected Asset Restructuring

The company's largest shareholder is Jiangxi Electronics Group, with the actual controller being the Jiangxi Provincial SASAC. In order to expand and strengthen the LED leading industry, the company has a strong expectation for future reorganization. On October 28, the controlling shareholder, Jiangxi Electronics Group, stated that the actual controller, Jiangxi SASAC, is discussing the transfer of equity in the electronics group with a third party, which may lead to a shift in the actual control of the company and its controlling shareholder. Previously, there were market rumors that the company's major shareholder is in contact with Hong Kong Sun Hung Kai International Group to seek integration of the upstream and downstream of the LED industry. Hong Kong International Group will indirectly control the listed company Lianchuang Optoelectronics by controlling Jiangxi Electronics Group. Although the company denied this, the idea of introducing strategic investors to integrate the industry and improve the company's fundamentals has never stopped. The company's LED products are mainly applied in home appliances and mobile communication fields, capable of producing backlights up to 12 inches but have not yet entered the LED lighting field on a large scale. Currently, the company cannot produce outdoor lighting LED products; indoor lighting will be the focus of the company's future development. In the recent published industrial development plan, the company mentioned that it will actively introduce strategic investment partners, appropriately carry out external mergers and acquisitions to integrate LED enterprises, enhance the competitive advantage of the company's products, and promote the continuous growth and expansion of the company's LED industry. Therefore, in order to expand and strengthen the leading industry, the company has a strong expectation to introduce strategic investors through reorganization and integrate the industrial chain. Once the company successfully reorganizes, the current operating conditions of the company will greatly improve, and it is expected to realize the overall development of the company's LED industry through the platform of semiconductor lighting source industrialization, with a bright prospect.

In terms of secondary market performance, the stock has shown relatively strong momentum recently, with steady price increases. From the moving averages, multiple lines such as 5-day, 10-day, and 20-day moving averages all show an upward dispersion trend, supporting the possibility of accelerated price increases in the future market. Investors can pay close attention.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you want to buy or sell, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Moreover, the commission is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to trade, the quantity, and the price at which you wish to buy or sell the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, while the stock code is six digits long. When placing a commission, the code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks traded: the quantity for purchasing stocks must be an integer multiple of 100, whereas the quantity for selling stocks does not have to be an integer multiple of 100.

There are four types of commissions: counter submission commission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
