Q1. If you want to open an online store, think about what you want to sell?

Q2. Where does the inventory come from?

A: Help from relatives and friends is indispensable. Ask around if any of your relatives or friends are doing business and need to increase sales.

You can also look online. Newly launched company products usually require a promotion process. You're the first one, so the profit is the highest. Of course, the easiest job is to be an agent. Q3. How do you take product pictures?

A: Don't easily pull images from the internet; be careful not to get sued for infringement! Take pictures of the front and back, inside and outside, top and bottom angles, and logos of the product yourself. Another key point is the choice of camera and shooting environment. The camera performance and focus must be good. Nowadays, digital cameras are generally easy to operate;

Lighting is the hardest to handle. It's either too dark or overexposed. At this time, you need two flashlights and some PS software. I've been using "Magic Hand" to process photos, and it's pretty good.

Regarding additional tips for newbies opening online stores:

One, Store decoration

When starting a store, it is recommended that you do not activate旺铺(Wangpu). The best use of Wangpu is: If you are still a newbie or have just opened a store and not many people are paying attention, then you'd better not rush to join直通车(Zhigu Che). Instead, you should first try to increase store traffic, lay a solid foundation, and then participate in promotional activities. Otherwise, if no one knows your store, you spend money on activities, but still few people see your store! Your money will be wasted! Only when the store has a certain customer base, buying Wangpu will be twice the result with half the effort!! So you don't necessarily need to decorate your store yet.

Two, What to sell

For newbies opening stores, the most important thing is positioning. If you don't have a good positioning, it will be very difficult to make progress.

So it is suggested that you can enter Taobao's community more often, learn from the experiences of predecessors, and combine them with your own environment to determine what products you want to sell. If you blindly follow what others sell, you won't succeed.

Three, About Promotion

If you open a store and don't manage it, who will know about your store? It's like opening a store in a sparsely populated mountainous area! Therefore, you need to find ways to let more people know and see your store, increasing the number of visitors to your store. You can also increase your own visibility because wherever you appear, your store information will be included.

General Methods:

1. Use all chat tools (Wangwang, QQ, MSN, etc.) to promote to your friends.

2. Choose a good store name.

3. Design a personalized signature and decorate a beautiful store.

4. Make good use of your recommendation spots and display windows.

5. Give your items names that are easy to search.

6. Participate in forum activities, post high-quality posts and replies, earn silver coins to buy item recommendations.

7. Make full use of free Paipai promotion functions.

8. Proactively contact buyers and provide pre-sales, mid-sales, and after-sales services.

9. Friendship links.

10. Gift packaging.

11. Have continuous promotions in the store.

12. Pay attention to the peak times for item listings (3-5 PM, 8-10 PM).

13. When posting items, set the time to 7 days as much as possible. If you choose 14 days, Taobao will automatically place your items at the back when they are posted.

14. Create a good personal space. Posting and replying will become a good window for promoting you. Of course, the premise is that you first manage your personal space signature picture well.

15. Post more articles on relevant forums, paste classic item pictures, which is also very effective.

Whenever you leave messages in forums, Wangwang, and other public places, your Taobao ID will naturally be left behind, allowing others to enter your store through your Taobao ID. The greater your influence in those places, the more people will naturally enter your store. The sea of learning is boundless, keep it up! Your business will surely improve!!

Four, How to Increase Store Promotion

Problem One: Not having enough time to manage the store

Not opening a store on Taobao, always thinking that opening a store on Taobao saves time, effort, and worry, but it's not actually true. Many Taobao users say that opening a store on Taobao is more tiring than opening a physical store and requires more attention, which is indeed the case! Not being able to guarantee enough time is indeed a big problem! If customers come and see that you're not there, they'll leave, which is very regrettable! This problem mostly occurs among part-time sellers. Solutions and remedies:

1. If you can't be online, try to keep your computer on, keep Wangwang online, and set automatic replies. The automatic reply should include: main business scope, recent discount activities, your contact information, phone, mobile phone, QQ, email, MSN, etc. There's a chance they might collect your items and store while waiting for you to return~ Now there's another method called Mobile Wangwang. You can hang Wangwang via text message just like hanging QQ via text message. Sending messages usually costs 0.1 yuan per message, so you don't have to keep your computer on all the time.

2. Try to stay online during peak customer flow hours. Staying online during the golden hours of customer traffic is extremely important. High customer traffic indicates that more people are really buying during this period, and the chances of making a sale are very high.

High traffic periods are generally from 7:00 PM to 3:00 AM. Don't assume that no one shops at midnight; there are plenty of people who love to shop online!
