Website promotion is also what we webmasters care about most. Especially for new websites, without traffic or popularity, posting information every day by yourself can be a very headache-inducing task. When my global second-hand website just started, I read many soft articles on Admin5 about website promotion, and they all seemed well-written. However, the more soft articles I read, the more problems arose because there were too many methods, and I didn't know where to start or which method was suitable for promoting my own site. After operating for nearly ten months, I have gained some experience. Here, I would like to exchange with everyone a few practical methods for online promotion.

Firstly, forum publicity. Firstly, you need to choose forums or forum sections related to your website. Secondly, fill in your personalized signature with your website information. The next step is to bump threads and post threads. When bumping threads, it's best to solve the original poster's problem thoroughly so that others find it trustworthy. You can casually include a URL without making it look like an advertisement. When posting threads, it’s better if it's original content. You can edit some of your website's content and post it on the forum, including a URL is also acceptable. If you have alternate accounts on the forum, it's even better to bump your own threads. This can greatly increase the success rate of your posts.

Secondly, blog promotion. Firstly, register a blog with keywords related to your website inserted in the title, name, and description, trying not to repeat them. Then, when publishing articles, try to write original content, or pseudo-original content. When your website's target keywords appear in the article, you can link them to your website (don't overdo it, one or two keywords are enough) to concentrate on improving keyword rankings. Or at the end of the article, use ellipses followed by "Read More" or "View More" text, adding a source link that leads to your website. Another point is to add links to your own website on the blog's friend links section. The last important point is to add a certain number of friends daily, leave comments promoting your website, and engage with them. This is crucial for driving traffic.

Note: I've registered many blogs, but feel that Sina and NetEase are the most effective.

Thirdly, QQ group promotion. This doesn't need much explanation. Generally, create or join QQ groups related to your website, regularly send relevant updates and emails within the group. It's best to do this between 9:30-11:00 AM and 2:30-4:00 PM, which are the times with the most online users.

Fourthly, writing soft articles. Soft article promotion helps search engine spiders crawl your website's information and can effectively drive more traffic. I recommend publishing soft articles on Admin5.

Fifthly, question-and-answer promotion. On platforms like Baidu Zhidao, SouSou WenWen, and Tianya WenDa, choose some website-related keywords to answer others' questions; you can also ask and answer your own questions, adopting the best answers yourself, which also increases your visibility.

These five website promotion methods are what I personally find most effective. Other methods such as images, affiliate advertising, hype, videos, etc., also work but are not as fast. For new sites, you can start with these five points slowly, rather than doing too many things at once. Doing these five points well is already quite good. Focusing on doing things well will lead to gradually increasing website traffic and popularity.
