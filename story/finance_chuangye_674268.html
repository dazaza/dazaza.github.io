Ningbo university students can set up stalls on campus

by lew501 on 2012-02-24 11:25:21

Ningbo Supports Self-employment and Encourages Entrepreneurship II: Universities Can Apply to Set Up Stalls on Campus

On April 19, 2010, the Ningbo Administration for Industry and Commerce issued the "Opinions on Supporting Self-employment and Encouraging Entrepreneurship Development" (hereinafter referred to as the "Opinions").

College students currently enrolled or who have graduated within two years, registering a company in Ningbo to engage in e-commerce, cultural creativity, animation design, software development, and other service-related business projects, can register a limited company with a registered capital of less than 5 million yuan (excluding single-member limited companies) with "zero down payment." The full registered capital must be paid within two years from the establishment of the company.

In addition, the "Opinions" were the first in the province to explicitly encourage college students currently enrolled or who have graduated within two years to use their acquired knowledge to provide door-to-door tutoring. They can apply for business registration, with the scope of operations defined as "door-to-door tutoring."

At the same time, the "Opinions" also encourage currently enrolled college students to engage in mobile trading within the campus. For non-permitted business projects conducted by currently enrolled college students within their university, upon issuance of proof by the school's student management department, they can apply to register as individual entrepreneurs without a fixed place of business. The business location on the business license will be designated as "within a certain university." This means that as long as the school agrees, students can officially apply to set up stalls on campus.

This way, college students starting businesses, whether registering a limited company or an individual entrepreneur, no longer need to worry about registration funds or addresses. College students with entrepreneurial aspirations who wish to register a company in Ningbo will find great convenience brought by policy incentives.
