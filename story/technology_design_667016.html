Bus driver skips stops to send an elderly man with a sudden heart attack to the hospital (picture)

by xixi84tom9n on 2012-02-16 13:18:57

Good driver Yang Chunlin. Photographed by Ren Guilin.

On the bus, 86-year-old mother Liu Shanmei suddenly suffered from myocardial obstruction and collapsed in her seat. Without hesitation, the driver of bus No. 72 drove quickly to the nearest hospital. The doctor said, "Just a few minutes later, I wouldn't have seen her." Special thanks to this driver whose car's license plate ends with 77!" Yesterday, a thank-you Weibo post moved the citizens of Nanjing under the cold rain. Many netizens forwarded the Weibo post, expressing their admiration for the lifesaving bus driver. Yesterday, Ms. Liu's family came to the fleet to thank the lifesaving driver.

Family comes to thank, the station master thought they were coming to complain

At 5:30 PM yesterday, at the bottom station of Dongbao Road on bus No. 72, the station master was signing off and dispatching buses as usual. Suddenly, seven or eight people walked into the control room, saying they were looking for a bus No. 72 driver whose license plate number ended with "77". The station master immediately stopped, thinking someone had come to complain, and quickly asked for clarification. "We're not here to complain, we're here to commend him," the first man to enter told the station master. If it weren't for this driver, our loved one might have been lost forever.

"This driver is named Yang Chunlin, his license plate number is SU A77177, but he wasn't on duty this afternoon," the station manager said. A lady then took out a red envelope containing 1,000 yuan in cash and asked the station master to definitely give it to the driver. "Absolutely cannot accept the red envelope, my family doesn't lack money, this kind of thing is just a small favor, saving a life is what we should do," Yang Chunlin instructed the station master over the phone, insisting not to accept the gratuity. "Since you don't want the red envelope, then we'll come back tomorrow to present a banner!" That lady said.

An 86-year-old elderly woman fell unconscious on the bus, and the driver rushed her to the hospital

On the morning of November 11, Sun Yanfeng accompanied her 86-year-old mother, Liu Shanmei, to visit relatives at Zhongyang North Road Military Retirement Home from their home at Zhongbao Yuan. At 10:40, after leaving the relatives' house, the two took a No. 25 bus and transferred to No. 72 at Zhongshamen North Station to go home. After getting on the bus, the old lady sat in the "elderly" designated seat, while her daughter sat across from her.

The mother and daughter chatted happily along the way. Around 11:30, when the bus stopped at the He Fangxi Garden intersection for a red light, the daughter noticed that her mother had closed her eyes tightly and leaned against the seat. "Mom, Mom, what's wrong?" Sun Yanfeng panicked: her mother had just placed a piece of candy in her mouth, how could she lose consciousness in an instant! No matter how Sun Yanfeng called out, Liu Shanmei didn't respond. Sun Yanfeng touched her mother's hand, which was cold, and when she placed her hand near her mother's nostrils, she couldn't feel any breathing. "Driver comrade! My mom is in trouble! What should we do?" Sun Yanfeng shouted loudly.

After understanding the situation, driver Yang Chunlin informed the more than 10 passengers on the bus: "The elderly person has lost consciousness, we need to send her to the hospital quickly. The hospital isn't far, it will only take a few minutes. Can everyone cooperate?" "Saving lives is important, hurry up!" "We'll take care of the patient on the bus," the passengers immediately responded, urging him to save the person. Yang turned the bus into an "ambulance," rushing straight to the hospital without stopping. On the bus, some passengers helped lift Ms. Liu, others pinched her Renzhong acupoint.

Four minutes later, the vehicle stopped in front of the Armed Police Fire Hospital. Just as they arrived, there was a nurse nearby, so Yang Chunlin grabbed her and said there was an elderly person on the bus who was almost gone. The nurse brought a stretcher with Yang Chunlin, and the passengers helped carry the stretcher and the patient into the hospital.

"Could you leave me your phone number?" Sun Yanfeng held onto a female passenger because helping her delayed many passengers for about 40 minutes. "We won't leave our numbers, I just checked the driver's license plate, but I can't remember clearly, the last two digits are 77," the female passenger said.

Elderly saved, family sends Weibo gratitude

Ms. Sun said her mother has a history of heart disease and hypertension, but it had never occurred before, she didn't know why this time she suddenly had a myocardial infarction on the bus. "Although my mother's illness came unexpectedly, the driver's response and measures were faster," Ms. Sun said. At the time, if the driver had stopped the car by the roadside and called 120, the long wait would have missed the best rescue time. "The doctor said, a few minutes later, we wouldn't have seen our mother," currently, Liu Shanmei has returned home to recuperate quietly. To thank the driver, Ms. Sun immediately thanked this good driver on Weibo.

Doctors remind older people with medical histories who travel alone or use public transportation that they should ideally be accompanied by family members and carry emergency medicine, because missing the optimal rescue time for some acute diseases can endanger the patient's life.
