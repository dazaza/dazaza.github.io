A pep talk for those who want to open an online store (written for those who haven't opened an online store or just started)

Author: Tao Tao's Little House

Recently, I have a lot of... many friends who heard that I do online stores, all showed interest in opening an online store and wanted to ask me about the specific methods. Some friends even asked me to help them start their online store, one question after another came flying at me, non-stop.

With the popularization of the Internet and the increasing living standards, what was once mysterious e-commerce has gradually come into our lives. Online shopping is becoming more common. More and more people are seeing the potential in e-commerce. Among the people I know, there are also many bosses who want to join the ranks of online sales. HOHO. Under my influence and guidance, some friends have already opened their stores.

I am a bit lazy, so to avoid repeating the same work in the future, I specially made this simple collection for everyone! This blog is aimed at newbies, so the veterans can skip it, hehe. But perhaps you often encounter friends asking you how to open an online store too. You might consider forwarding this blog to them, saving you some breath ~~ Everyone's opinions are welcome and valued.

Below, I will use several questions that friends who want to open an online store often ask as leads to express what I want to say to prospective sellers.

1. Question: I want to open an online store, can I make money?

Answer: Many people definitely earn money by opening online stores. Nowadays, more and more people are fully engaged in running online stores, which is their means of livelihood. Additionally, many online stores have good business volume and have developed into online sales companies, hiring dozens or hundreds of people to manage the online store. Some are professional sellers in an industry for many years, transitioning from physical stores to online stores, expanding their market through online stores. However, many people don't make much money, just earning pocket change. It's like physical stores, some earn while others lose. However, because the overall investment is relatively small, the risk is less than opening a physical store. Many friends who plan to open a physical store but have no business experience choose to start with an online store first. Personally, I think it's a pretty good way, worth a try.

2. Question: What do I need to prepare to open an online store?

Answer: First, you must have basic material conditions: a computer connected to the Internet. If you're selling physical products, you generally need a digital camera because the characteristic of online sales is that buyers cannot see the actual product; product pictures are an important way to understand the product.

If you have better photography skills and know how to use some image processing software, that's even better. Product pictures are the buyer's first impression of the product. Whether they look nice and clear significantly impacts the transaction. Of course, if you don't know now, it's okay. You can learn and explore slowly after opening your store. Many Taobao sellers learned slowly after opening their stores. There are many related tutorials on Taobao that you can refer to. If you don't want to learn, you can spend some money asking Taobao shop owners who specialize in store decoration to help you process the product images and decorate your store. Making your store beautiful is definitely beneficial for business, just like decorating a physical store.

The software you must download for opening a store: Wangwang and Taobao Assistant. Wangwang allows timely communication with buyers, resolving their doubts. Using Taobao Assistant makes it more convenient to list the items we want to sell compared to online editing, and it must be used.

You also need a heart willing to serve others. As a seller, you not only provide products to buyers but also services. Good service will give buyers a big positive review. Credit level on Taobao is very important.

Also, you need firm belief and determination not to give up easily. Many Taobao sellers have no business after opening their stores for a period of time, then many sellers give up. The beginning of opening a store is the hardest time, the most challenging time for sellers. This test period could be a month, a few months (of course, there are also sellers whose businesses are good right after opening, but they are very few). If your endurance is insufficient and you can't hold out, giving up halfway is a waste of time and energy.

3. Question: What products should I sell? Which products are the most profitable?

Answer: Choosing the right product is extremely important, directly affecting how well it sells, which is very critical.

Many friends have asked me this question, but I don't know specifically which product is the most profitable. The most profitable ones are often done by many people, and when everyone jumps on the bandwagon, it becomes hard to make money. Also, I think the most profitable product isn't necessarily the most suitable for oneself. Not everyone selling the most profitable thing can make a lot of money. Instead, you should ask yourself what interests you the most, what you know best, what is most convenient for you to do, and what sources of goods you have. It's best to treat opening such a store as a hobby. Because we can only operate well the products we love! We will be happier and more determined to keep doing it! We all know that doing what suits us best and what we are most interested in is the best.

If you can get goods directly from a company or factory, that's the best. A friend of mine is a beauty consultant, an expert in beauty products, and can get the company's products. This friend also has a lot of time to surf the internet, so opening a cosmetics online store undoubtedly has significant advantages.

4. Question: Where can I find the goods? How much should I invest approximately?

Answer: You can go to wholesale markets, or check out Alibaba for online wholesale purchases. There are merchants who support small batch purchases for Taobao buyers. Personally, I think buying from physical wholesale markets is better. For the first purchase, it's suggested not to buy too much. Try to have more varieties with fewer quantities per item. After purchasing, understand the market reaction and take advice from other similar shops before making further decisions. If you can get goods directly from the manufacturer, it would be even better, with more price advantages. There are also other channels.

If you feel uncertain, you can try drop shipping initially when starting the store. Some fairly successful stores on Taobao recruit online store agents who can ship on your behalf. Some wholesale malls can also do drop shipping for online stores. This method has low risk. Pay a membership fee of 100 to a few hundred yuan, or even zero membership fee, then pay when you sell and let the other party ship the goods. Zero inventory, no risk of stockpiling (please choose carefully, many are scammers or have poor product quality). But there are many disadvantages. The profit is small, and it's not conducive to your own development because you can't see the actual product. When buyers consult you, it's more troublesome, and many aspects are constrained by the other party. Moreover, if there are mistakes in shipping or problems with quality, you may have to endure and compensate for the buyer's losses.

It's recommended not to do drop shipping long-term. To develop, you still need to buy your own goods.

5. Question: Can we meet and you teach me how to open a store face-to-face?

Answer: I would love to teach you everything I know all at once, but there are just too many things related to opening a store. It can't be finished in a day or two. The basic registration and store-opening process has a very professional FLASH display on Taobao (the link is at the end of the article). Friends can follow it, it's very easy to understand.

If you are familiar with the network, you don't even need to look at the tutorial to set up the store. Each step of the Taobao store-opening operation has relevant prompts. A friend who didn't originally plan to open a store suddenly managed to set it up.

Other relevant knowledge, friends can first visit Taobao University (the link is at the end of the article), read more posts. There, you can gather the strengths of many experts who spend a lot of time sharing their experiences. I'm just a one-diamond seller, not yet worthy of speaking much. It's better to directly learn from these super sellers' posts.

You can also join some Wangwang groups with a good mutual-help atmosphere. You can ask questions in the group. A group can accommodate up to 200 people. With more people, there's more strength. Someone may know what you don't, helping you solve doubts promptly.

Explore and think more on your own. If you still can't find answers, then seek help, which will be more helpful for your growth.

Additionally, some good friends asked me to help them set up their online store. After thinking about it, I refused because the experience of going from nothing to something is more fulfilling when done entirely by oneself. What do you think, friends?

When I was preparing to open my store, I consulted a friend I've known for a long time. She only said, "Go look on the forum." Then I, like a headless fly, went to the Taobao forum, gradually familiarized myself with various sections, searching for useful information, slowly exploring everything on my own, looking at posts when I had doubts, occasionally asking others. Hehe, you guys are luckier than me, aren't you?

6. Question: After opening the store, do I just wait for others to buy things and that's it?

Answer: If you think listing the items is the end of the job, then you're wrong. It's best to keep Wangwang online for a long time. It's like guarding a physical store. Although buyers can place orders even if the online store owner is offline, if the seller is online, they can communicate better with buyers, handle their doubts promptly, greatly increasing the chances of a transaction. Many buyers, when choosing products, may directly pass over if they see the seller is offline. Sometimes when I search for items, I directly search for items where the seller's Wangwang is online.

Promotion is also an extremely important part. With so many Taobao stores, how will buyers find you? Doing some promotions and participating in promotional activities organized by Taobao will greatly help our business. Related promotion methods can be referenced from introductions on Taobao University and posts on Experience Sharing.

Another point I want to mention is that since buyers and sellers rate each other after transactions on online stores, this is an important reference for future buyers. So, treating your buyers sincerely, their reviews will become your promotional assistant. If you don't do well, the reviews will negatively affect your business.

7. Question: I want to quit my job and do online stores full-time. My family opposes it, thinking I won't make money. What do you think?

Answer: Recently, I've heard many friends say they want to do online stores full-time and stop working for others, earning money freely. However, they hesitate because they fear they won't make money. Personally, I think this question should be asked to yourselves. Do you believe in yourself? If you don't believe in yourself, how can your family and friends believe in you? Of course, sometimes things turn out well unexpectedly, hehe. Some things become clearer as you do them. Trying once is not bad, but you need to think more about how you will make enough money to live on Taobao and how confident you are.

It's suggested to start by part-time opening an online store. Once the store reaches a certain scale, credit level accumulates to a certain height, and you master Taobao-related knowledge to a certain extent, then consider quitting your job and doing online stores full-time. This is a more stable approach.

However, personally, I think doing Taobao part-time is more fun, earning some pocket money without too much pressure. Now the competition on Taobao is also fierce, standing out is not easy.

If you've read all the text above and don't plan to open a store, I still suggest you become a Taobao buyer. My position is very simple because there are really so many good things on Taobao. There's nothing you can't buy, and they're usually very affordable. I really enjoy online shopping~~
