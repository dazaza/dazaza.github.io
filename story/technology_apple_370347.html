iPhone User's Essential in Winter: Agloves Gloves with Capacitive Screen Support

by shootero on 2010-11-03 09:31:04

With the popularity of large-screen touch phones, nowadays you can see people holding this type of phone everywhere; truly, elegance is revealed in a single touch. However, come winter, using a capacitive screen phone becomes quite difficult because it takes a lot of determination to expose your fingers to the freezing cold air. Especially this year, which is claimed to be the coldest in a millennium, if you live in the north and own an iPhone, then it's time to think about how to get through this winter. And that’s where Agloves come in - they are designed to save your fingers.

Agloves claims to incorporate all-new scientific research findings. The entire glove, including all ten fingers and the palm, is knitted with silver yarn, so operating a capacitive screen while wearing these gloves is exactly the same as doing so barehanded. At the same time, they also offer waterproofing and excellent warmth retention.
