Pato praises teammates as brothers, Galliani signals full preparation for Juventus.

by bpxwoxrt on 2012-02-21 15:12:20

A delighted Adriano Galliani praised the whole team for the victory, "Jankulovski was excellent today, I would say extraordinarily good; Van Bommel also performed outstandingly, and I celebrate the players' victory. Thiago Silva? He is an extraordinary defender, and with Nesta, he will grow into a championship-level player. Today our substitutes were also exceptional, Emmanuelson tried his best, which made me very excited. Bojan's shot was incredibly astonishing, Pato was also very good, and Ibrahimovic was simply brilliant." In the 27th round of Serie A, AC Milan defeated Napoli 3-0, leading Inter Milan, who are in second place, by 5 points and continuing to lead the league. Pato performed excellently in this match and scored the decisive goal; after the game, the Brazilian Golden Boy accepted an interview, "We played a great game today, both the coach's instructions and the team's performance were excellent; I am happy with my performance today, I feel very calm, and I hope this state can be maintained, so that I can continue to perform at this level in the future. I like the Milan team, my teammates are all great championship-level players, they are not just like my friends but more like my brothers, playing with these players makes it easier to perform well."

Jan Koller, who has been out of action for a long time, started and performed stably, effectively suppressing Napoli's wing attack; after the game, Jankulovski said winning this game signified that Milan had taken another step towards the championship, "I am glad to have started, we played a great game, defeating Napoli means we have taken another step towards the championship; there are still many games left this season, and we need to keep working hard until the end of the season."

This weekend, Milan will face Juventus away in the 28th round of the league, Pato said the team would strive for victory, "In the next game, we will face Juventus, and we will strive for victory with all our strength, but I think the result of the game is not enough to decide the league championship, what we need to do now is give our all in every game."

Galliani was satisfied with the team's performance and said it was time to prepare well for Juventus, "This was an extraordinary victory, I told President Berlusconi before the game that Milan would definitely win this game, we played better than Napoli, they didn't even have one threatening shot in the game, while we had multiple threatening attacks during the game. This Saturday we will face Juventus, and next we need to prepare well."
