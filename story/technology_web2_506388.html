Class Rules: After a month of trial operation, the new class rules have been officially announced after minor detail modifications as follows: The new semester class system is divided into two parts - routine and study.

1. Routine:

- Includes eye exercises, road teams, dining tables and chairs, etc.

- Implementing a credit system, one point per day, with a basic score of five points per week. Violation of any item will result in the deduction of one point for that day. Generally, the discipline officer will remind first, and if there's no correction after reminding, then points will be deducted.

- There are also extra points added on top of the basic score. The following situations can add points - In the weekly group evaluation, each member of the best group gets an additional point; children who receive special praise or are selected as smart boys or girls can also get an additional point.

- Monthly settlement items: Responsibility for not forgetting items and homework, deduct one point for each forgetfulness; excellent homework display and excellent short sentences reading, add one point each time; contributions to the class and participation in class or school competitions can be appropriately rewarded with points.

- Feedback: The individual scores of the week will be settled every Friday and recorded in the home-school contact book. Two contents will be recorded: the child's own score for the week and the highest score in the class. The total score for the month will be settled once and also recorded in the home-school contact book.

2. Study:

- Weekly selection of "Good Listener" and "Listening Progress Star"

- Good Listener: Two requirements - focused listening and active speaking; Music, PE, art classes require serious lessons, while Chinese, math, and English require focused listening and active participation in classroom discussions. Every Friday, I will check with the two subject teachers about the children's classroom performance for the week. Children criticized by the teacher will have their student number registered in the Good Listener evaluation table, and they will not be eligible for the Good Listener evaluation this week.

- Listening Progress Star - Children who show obvious progress compared to last week.

- The children who are evaluated every week will not receive certificates but will receive a star sticker on the wall package. The star score will be included in the final score at the end of the term.
