March 24th big three components quotation: memory price rises again, CPU price rises

by anonymous on 2012-03-03 11:35:45

Today, in the Intel processor spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, the prices of Intel SNB processors and car decoders have increased across the board. The increase ranges from 5 to 50 yuan, with most increases falling between 10 to 30 yuan. The price increase for Intel's low-end processors is relatively small, offering better cost performance compared to AMD. Notably, the loose G530 and G620 dual-core processors are worth mentioning. Relevant products include car decoders, car signal jammers, car unlocking tools, and car remote control decoders.

Today, in the AMD processor spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, AMD processor prices have risen again. The older 45nm Athlon processors, which had previously experienced continuous price drops, have become the main drivers of this price hike. Their prices are gradually losing appeal. Today's price increase ranges from 5 to 15 yuan, with most increases between 10 to 15 yuan.

In the desktop memory spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing today, memory prices have risen again, with the 2GB/1333 model seeing the fastest price increase. Kingston regular memory leads the rise in prices, while the 4GB/1333 by ADATA has not yet followed suit, remaining at 105 yuan. Merchants stated that Samsung DDR3-1600 and ADATA 8GB/1333 memories have been launched, making it harder to sell the 2GB/1333 memory despite its price increase.

In the laptop memory spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing today, there were no changes in the prices of notebook memory (car decoder-related). Due to the longer sales cycle of notebook memory, the speed of price decline is slower than that of desktop memory. The Kingston 4GB/1333 notebook memory is quoted at 115 yuan. ADATA and Apacer 4GB/1333 notebook memory is quoted at 112-113 yuan.

In the desktop hard drive spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing today, hard drive prices remain generally stable. As the wave of early stockpiling and speculative selling of hard drives approaches its end, the sustained week-long slight price drop of hard drives has been curbed, and hard drive prices are gradually aligning with those of the Hong Kong general distributor.

In the laptop hard drive spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing today, Hitachi notebook hard drive prices remain stable. The Hitachi single-platter 500GB/5400rpm notebook hard drive is quoted at 510 yuan; the double-platter 500GB is quoted at 500 yuan.
