Mastering workplace fashion pairing from Taobao

by xuezhen5557g on 2012-02-14 15:40:45

For women in the workplace, a stylish suit is definitely a must-have, but wearing suits all the time can be a bit monotonous and lacks novelty. Below, we will teach you how to skillfully use suits to create charming workplace looks with Taobao's fashion tips. Nude-colored suits exude a unique sense of fashion and modernity this autumn and winter. The neutral tailoring and special longer length create a relaxed and carefree style for clothing from Taobao. The masculine pocket detail highlights a gentlemanly flair.

The suit jacket is versatile and fits well into various settings; however, during the cool early autumn weather, a shiny embellishment T-shirt might not suffice, so the little jacket comes in handy again, and it doesn't fail its mission. The sapphire high heels beautifully shape the leg lines.

The vintage short black suit jacket is truly an essential trick to save an outfit. As a popular color, sapphire blue brings out the femininity when worn as a little dress alone, but paired with a seemingly casual yet meticulously crafted belt, it adds a touch of authoritative womanhood.
