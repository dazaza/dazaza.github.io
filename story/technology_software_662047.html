What are the key points to note when treating epididymitis?

by fdsg on 2012-02-09 16:47:09

What should be noted in the treatment of epididymitis? Epididymitis is caused by bacteria such as E. coli, Staphylococcus or Streptococcus. Experts point out that due to the special structure of the epididymis, bacteria are likely to remain after invading, making it difficult to cure completely. In order to prevent epididymitis from lingering and not healing, whether it's acute or chronic epididymitis, thorough treatment is necessary. So, what should be paid attention to when treating epididymitis? Below is a detailed introduction by experts on the treatment of epididymitis: 1. Avoid excessive sexual activity to reduce the congestion of sex organs and lighten the workload of the epididymis, which is beneficial for improving local resistance. 2. Choose appropriate antibiotics. Acute epididymitis should be treated until all symptoms disappear, then continue medication for another 4-5 days; Chronic epididymitis requires continuous medication for 2-3 weeks to consolidate the therapeutic effect. 3. Refrain from heavy drinking and smoking, eat less spicy and sour stimulating foods to prevent local congestion of the epididymis. 4. Hot compresses on the scrotum should be done frequently. People who stand or walk too much should wear cloth scrotal supports. The purpose of lifting the scrotum is to improve the blood circulation of the epididymis, which helps the inflammation subside.
