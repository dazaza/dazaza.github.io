by xubyfngdlr on 2012-02-15 10:51:05

From the first day of the Chinese New Year to the second, I have bothered my elder brother countless times and delayed his meals many times. In the process of me having sweet dreams, Elder Brother Qi walked alone on the road to the west. A few years later, with the improvement of economic conditions, seeing most of the colleagues in the yard move away and live in bigger houses, I began to have new expectations. My unit is a market management department, and dealing with individual industrial and commercial households is part of our job. Once, younger brother Fei gave me an apple when we took a car from Songyang to Chizhou; I don't remember its size or redness. I was very surprised when I received it - how did his family have apples? The children don't let me go out, but I feel meaningless being cooped up at home all day.

I still remember the scene on the 22nd day of the last lunar month, the day before the Minor New Year, when my father brought home 80 yuan. That was the most money we had ever seen that belonged to us, and it was from then on that our rural life began to change. Until 1980, when my father was rehabilitated and our whole family returned to the city, our hard rural life finally ended. The brightness of the lights tells the progress of the city, the flatness of the roads shows the advancement of society, and the density of the car streams narrates the improvement of life.

The world is vast and varied. There are 13 specialized botanical gardens, with more than 250 varieties of ornamental trees, totaling over 2 million plants, earning the reputation of "a green treasure house" in Wuhan. Every sleepless night, you appear; one of the specialized botanical gardens is East Lake Plum Garden, one of the four major plum gardens in southern China, and East Lake Cherry Blossom Garden, the third largest cherry blossom viewing base in the world, as well as盆景园、杜鹃园、竹园 etc.

Wandering between alleys, I yearn for and wait, not knowing what kind of scenery lies behind the alleys. It might be a broad avenue filled with bustling prosperity; it might be a muddy path with countless potholes; or perhaps there's nothing there, just ourselves and our shadows... But I know I should smile and pass through these alleys, to meet those touching moments, those people, and those unclear problems.

One day, when I suddenly found myself idle, my heart felt so empty. If the price is agreed upon, it’s like a shout, followed by whirlwind-like devouring of the goods on the truck. Very quickly, a truck loaded with dozens of tons of goods will be cleaned up completely. Then, they receive a few red banknotes from the boss and start dividing them amidst quarrels and explanations. Within just a few minutes, they put on their coats again and look around at other vehicles.

At the age of eighteen or nineteen, one can only be said to be at the stage of a blank sheet of paper, not yet learned to identify, not yet polished, not yet exercised, not yet experienced, somewhat like the feeling of first trying water, weakly and lightly stepping down, thus walking into this big dye vat of society. This period of secular life is quite noisy, but I don't find it annoying, because my heart is empty.
