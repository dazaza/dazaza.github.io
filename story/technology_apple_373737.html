Jobs turns into "Beatle" as the Beatles finally move into Apple iTunes

by geekzhang on 2010-12-08 13:28:13

After years of entanglement, The Beatles has finally entered the Apple iTunes store, with all works by the band available for sale on iTunes.

Although Steve Jobs was a fan of The Beatles, trademark disputes have only now allowed Jobs to see his favorite music on iTunes. Who knows? Perhaps he wished to become a member of The Beatles?

Victoria Kitchingham boldly created a funny video that transformed Jobs into the "Fifth Beatle" performing with the band, singing about the difficult journey of The Beatles entering iTunes.
