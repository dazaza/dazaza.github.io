Cufflinks are one of those accessories that are often overlooked, but when properly matched with your outfit, they can create a positive image in the eyes of the beholder. Born in the seventeenth century, cufflinks remain one of the most essential fashion accessories worn today by men of energy and elegance to reinforce their image and establish their status within a group of individuals. Although it is true that there are as many types of cufflinks as there are personalities and occasions, we must also note that not every cufflink will match your personality, your outfit, and most importantly, your career. Therefore, we should define the typically essential career-related cufflinks.

Projecting Professionalism through Cufflinks:

Career-related cufflinks were created to do one thing: project your level of professionalism through an elegant lens. Nothing creates an aura of distinction more than elegant enamel-based cufflinks that have been delicately crafted to match your career orientation and professionalism. By now, you may be wondering, "Why should I wear a career-specific cufflink when I could use a generic cufflink and do just fine?" - Great question. The fact of the matter is that you don’t need to create false illusions about your personality or level of professionalism. For example, each gentleman's help and guidance for placing might suggest that you might like a pair of cufflinks that have playing card designs, but if you are an engineer, such cufflinks could not speak truthfully about your knowledge and personality. All they say is that you like to gamble, a reality that might not be seen with kind eyes if you are dealing with investors.

Moreover, many people might believe that giving so much meaning to cufflinks is overstepping into uncharted territory. However, take the following thought into consideration: When speaking of the law of attraction, it is often mentioned that over 70% of the things people perceive about you are picked up not by what you say verbally but by the expression of your body or body language. This means that a good lady could be attracted to your charms even before you open your mouth to say Hi.

There are numerous types of cufflinks, and each should be used in accordance with your career and the image you are trying to create. This means that if you work in the financial sector, there are cufflinks designed specifically for this type of user; the same can be said about engineer cufflinks, law cufflinks for lawyers, military cufflinks, photography cufflinks, and such. Besides projecting professionalism, these cufflinks are intimately tied to New York fashion and even urban styles used today by singers, actors, and successful people in cosmopolitan areas, so there’s a lot to be said about these fascinating fashion accessories.

Fascinating Facts:

7 out of 10 people who match their suits with the proper cufflinks are given priority regarding business offers. These numbers prove correct the theory that "acting as if" you were a big shot does indeed send the message that you are a big shot or an important person.

Specialist cufflinks sport a great amount of enamel, which is a material so compounded as to be opaque or amazingly transparent, colorful yet colorless. The way this material reflects light is completely unique. Enamel was a material highly regarded in Egypt, Rome, and Greece by jewel makers as well as those in power; it continues to be seen the same way by important people all over the world. Vitreous enamel or porcelain enamel is the colorful but elegant form of enamel used today to give a sleek and glossy finish to one-of-a-kind cufflinks. Enamel has a higher melting point than aluminum.

There are 12 different methods used today to apply enamel to various surfaces and materials, and the effect created by each enameling technique is quite unique.
