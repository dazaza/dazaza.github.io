To achieve the goal of enhancing the understanding of policies and agricultural scientific knowledge among all cadres and the masses in Jinniu Town.

by vlockr15 on 2011-06-11 18:27:44

The municipal Party committee's certain batch of rural work teams stationed in a certain town for poverty alleviation is composed of a certain government office or factory/mine from our city. The entire team consists of a certain number of members, and they are assigned to provide targeted assistance to a certain village in the town. Over the past year, adhering to the determined work strategy at the time of deployment—"focusing on one key task, upholding two necessities, practicing the 'Three Represents,' taking enhanced investigation and research as the starting point, doing practical things for the masses as the focal point, and helping the designated village out of poverty as the ultimate goal"—the team has worked hard, persevered, and successfully completed their poverty alleviation tasks. They have prioritized the needs of the masses, thought about what the people need, lived, stayed, and worked alongside them, exchanging sweat and hard work, sincerity, and dedication for further economic growth in the assisted village, social harmony and stability, and unanimous praise from the villagers.

1. Emphasizing investigation and research, formulating feasible development plans

After the formation of the work team, they officially stationed in the designated village on February 10th of year XX. After entering the village, the work team conducted a series of surveys on village conditions and public sentiment through home visits, holding various levels of discussion meetings, distributing questionnaires, etc., thus establishing communication channels with the villagers. Over the course of a year, they visited more than 1,000 households, held over 150 discussions with village cadres, old party members, villager representatives, large-scale farmers and livestock breeders, and distributed questionnaires to XX individuals. This allowed them to basically grasp the development projects, cultural environment, and other village conditions in the assisted villages. Based on this, they further clarified their development ideas, and each village formulated feasible development plans, providing targeted guidance for the conduct of poverty alleviation work.

2. Deeply carrying out publicity and education activities, allowing advanced culture to occupy the ideological high ground in the countryside

Strengthening the education and publicity of peasants is both a fundamental task of the poverty alleviation work team and a prerequisite for conducting poverty alleviation work well. Over the past year, the work team has consistently integrated the education and publicity of farmers throughout the entire process of poverty alleviation work. First, they carried out publicity and education regarding the Party's guidelines, policies, enhancing the voluntaryness and firmness of cadres and masses in implementing the Party's various guidelines and policies in rural areas, making the Party's policies deeply rooted in the hearts of the people, guiding their actions. Second, they conducted legal knowledge education, encouraging farmers to learn, understand, and abide by laws, breaking away from gambling, feudal superstitions, extravagant celebrations for weddings and funerals, and other ugly phenomena and outdated customs, creating a long-term peaceful social environment for economic development. Third, they promoted agricultural scientific and technical knowledge, training farmers to master applicable agricultural science and technology skills, striving to acquire the ability to get rid of poverty through their own efforts, ensuring that each person can master at least one or two applicable skills. A certain coal mine continuously subscribed to "Friends of the People" and "Science and Technology Information" for the poverty alleviation point village for four years. The Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative established the "Shennong" website, providing technological information to farmers and linking with more than 20 national agricultural websites. By now, they have published 3,000 pieces of information, including over 20 pieces of information about corn, potatoes, sunflowers, rapeseed, millet, small grains, beef cattle, and other agricultural products for a certain town. Fourth, they organized rich and colorful cultural activities, flourishing rural cultural life. Over the past year, the work team hosted 20 training classes and lectures, screened 40 science and education films, and organized five stage performances, striving to make the cultural life of the entire town vibrant. Through these entertaining educational activities, they aimed to improve the policy theoretical quality and agricultural scientific and technical knowledge level of all cadres and masses in the town.

3. With emotion, truly helping the poor, doing everything possible to do practical things and good deeds for the masses

Over the past year, each poverty alleviation work team in the designated villages increased the intensity of raising funds, gathering resources from multiple sources, doing everything possible to do practical things and good deeds for the masses, transforming "blood transfusion"-style poverty alleviation into "development"-style poverty alleviation.

(a) In adjusting the industrial structure, standing on local resource advantages, developing characteristic animal husbandry, investing 400,000 yuan from last year to this year to construct two large-scale, intensive animal husbandry companies: a certain animal husbandry shareholding company and the pig farm in Village X. These have played a exemplary role in promoting the development of animal husbandry in the entire town. A certain power plant has invested 300,000 yuan over three years to support the construction of the "Meat Cattle Breeding Shareholding Company" in Village Z. In year XX, they invested another x million yuan to renovate and expand the cattle sheds, currently growing to 300 heads of meat cattle, and was designated as one of the seven major seed bull breeding bases in the entire city this year. A certain group invested 100,000 yuan to support the construction of the "Seed Bull Breeding Shareholding Company" in a certain village, currently growing to 300 heads of seed bulls.

(b) This year is the road construction year for hardened roads in a certain village. During the construction of the village-to-village hardened road project, the work team held two special meetings, actively mobilizing all poverty alleviation units, securing 305,000 yuan in place, assisting in constructing 13 kilometers of hardened roads, fundamentally solving the backward transportation conditions in all villages in the town and addressing the problem of difficult travel for the masses. The leader of the poverty alleviation group of a certain village, the deputy director of the Municipal Department X, personally contacted the engineering team to ensure the quality of the road connecting the village, and led the engineering technical personnel to the construction site for guidance. A certain group contributed 100,000 yuan for road construction fees. The group's party committee deputy secretary personally led the company's professional technical personnel to the village to measure and plan the village-to-village road, formulating a construction plan for "hardened, greened, beautified" cement roads. During the critical period of paving, despite suffering from gastritis, he stayed in the designated village for several days to help contact construction materials and transportation.

(c) In terms of primary and secondary school dilapidated house renovation, the work team provided 70,000 yuan in assistance funds, newly building, expanding, and improving one elementary school each. The insurance company invested 30,000 yuan to assist in the new construction of a certain elementary school, while the Industrial and Commercial Bank invested 10,000 yuan to improve the facilities of a certain village elementary school.

(d) In terms of solving drinking water difficulties for humans and livestock, the work team provided 50,000 yuan in assistance funds, helping build a water tower in a certain area, investing 20,000 yuan to help dig a deep well in a certain area, and investing another 20,000 yuan to lay 1,200 meters of water pipelines, solving drinking water problems for 1,000 people and 300 large livestock.

(e) Pairing assistance between poverty alleviation units is an important task. The poverty alleviation work team actively carried out paired assistance and warmth-giving activities, providing a total assistance of over 70,000 yuan throughout the year. The manager of the insurance company donated 1,000 yuan to Zhao Erzhu in a certain village for medical treatment; the general manager of the power plant helped a special hardship household Yin Runcheng in a certain village with 1,000 yuan; a certain group gifted seven superior sheep worth over XX yuan to its one-on-one assistance target; the chairman of a certain coal mine personally funded XX yuan to purchase four small-tailed Han sheep for his assistance target Yang Jianming; the captain of the work team from a certain mine and Shang Sanqing in a certain village formed a one-on-one assistance pair. The captain provided 1,500 yuan in cash to purchase breeding sheep, and during the New Year, sent a TV and blanket. Shang Sanqing subsequently became wealthy through sheep farming and embarked on the path to prosperity. At the same time, the work team would always console teachers on Children's Day, showing love to students by gifting schoolbags and stationery, and donating clothes and money to specially impoverished households during the Spring Festival. Throughout the year, the accumulated value of donations amounted to over 60,000 yuan.

In the past year, the poverty alleviation work team helped establish a good branch committee in the stationed village, found a good development path, and did some practical things and good deeds for the masses, achieving certain results. However, there is still a gap compared to the requirements of the municipal Party committee and the expectations of the masses. In the future, they will follow the requirement of the municipal Party committee—"not withdrawing the work team until the stationed village is out of poverty"—and further increase the intensity of work, resolutely fighting and winning this tough battle of poverty alleviation.
