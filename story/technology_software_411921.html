20 years of development creating wealth miracles

Founded in 1992, Yunnan Weitong Building Materials Co., Ltd. is a large-scale building ceramics distribution company specializing in the sale of mid-to-high-end wall and floor tiles and sanitary ware series products. Over nearly 20 years, Weitong has consistently adhered to the concept of beautifying the living environment for customers, upholding the corporate mission of creating greater value for customers. It was the first to implement the marketing strategy of Honest Weitong·Clear Pricing, with Harmonious Weitong·Win-Win for You and Me as the corporate vision, enjoying a good reputation within the industry and among customers. Now it has developed into one of the benchmark enterprises in Yunnan Province's home building materials industry, known as a wealth creation miracle. It is understood that the soon-to-be-reopened Weitong Building Materials' Supreme Palace (Weitong Building Materials Headquarters) is located at the core position of Kunming Dashanghui International Building Materials City, with an investment of nearly ten million yuan and exhibition area exceeding 4000 square meters. It is the most luxurious and prominent home building materials showroom in terms of decoration and display effect within the industry. Additionally, there are five branch stores set up at Huayang Home Plaza, Jiajun Home, and Hongshengda Building Materials Market, with sales network covering all 16 prefectures and cities in the province and extending to other provinces and cities in southwestern China. The company owns more than 10,000 square meters of product showrooms and over 3000 square meters of warehousing base. Currently, Weitong Building Materials exclusively represents six first-tier sanitary ware and tile brands: Roca, Dongpeng Sanitary Ware, Mingsheng·Yasuli, Tedi Ceramics, Character Tiles, and JNJ Mosaics. These products can be found in numerous government agencies, enterprises and institutions, high-end leisure clubs, and foreign-style villas in the city.

Terminal Marketing Expanding Rapid Cooperation Channels

In terms of marketing and management models, Weitong Building Materials has pioneered many firsts. First to promote clear pricing sales, first to sell products with complementary services, first to provide comprehensive spatial solutions... In Yunnan's building materials industry, diversified product operations are common, and large-scale supreme experience stores and scenario museums are increasing in scale. How to mutually enhance the various operated products has become a science. Once again, Weitong became the pioneer, showcasing tiles and sanitary ware of the same level. This not only helps consumers clearly and brightly choose the products needed for home decoration but also firmly grasps the development trend of complementary demand in the building materials industry.
