Engineering Department MI/CAM Engineer Job Summary

Importance of the Position:

In a PCB manufacturing plant, one of the most fundamental success factors is the design, routing, and preparation of preliminary files for the printed circuit board. This directly determines the quality of the entire product's manufacturing process. This is where the Engineering Department's CAM/MI engineer comes in - they are the main driver of product manufacturing and the designer of quality.

Job Description:

MI Engineer: In all PCB manufacturing plants, there are various types of PCB boards, and the Gerber files provided by different customers also vary. To make the customer-provided data applicable to production within our factory, technical processing and interpretation of the data must be conducted under conditions that meet customer requirements. This involves redesigning or converting relevant technical designs into essential technical data required by the factory to comply with production needs.

CAM Engineer: Computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) mainly involves converting or designing the primary MI data and files provided by the customer into a complete set of electronic production tools that meet the production line’s requirements, such as film data, drilling programs, and forming programs.

Market Demand for MI/CAM Engineers:

In 2003, China's printed circuit revenue exceeded $6 billion, making it the world's second-largest producer of printed circuits. According to incomplete statistics, there are approximately 7,800 professional PCB manufacturers nationwide, with over 4,000 located solely in Guangdong Province. PCB experts predict that China will become the global base for PCB manufacturing. Currently, there are about 5,000 PCB companies in the Pearl River Delta, around 1,500 in the Yangtze River Delta, and about 500 in other regions. The annual demand for CAM/MI engineers exceeds 7,000; however, due to the lack of specialized schools on the mainland, training can only be conducted internally within enterprises. As a result, the PCB industry faces a significant shortage of electronic circuit design talent, providing excellent employment opportunities.

Advantages of Huitong:

1. Two full-time teachers come from the engineering departments of well-known PCB companies, possessing extensive professional backgrounds and experience teaching multiple batches of internal MI/CAM engineers (both lecturers and engineers).

2. Teaching materials originate from confidential internal PCB company resources, ensuring practical and direct application.

3. Fully utilizing the actual workflow of PCB companies, simulating current engineering department internal training models. Each student has their own machine for hands-on learning, progressing from basic knowledge to real-world applications and advanced techniques, with detailed explanations and practical operations.
