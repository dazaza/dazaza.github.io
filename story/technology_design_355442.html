The graduation certificate number for university students is compiled by the school according to the coding rules set by the Ministry of Education. There are 18 digits in total, and they are arranged with a certain pattern: the first five digits represent the school code, the sixth digit represents the type of education, the seventh to tenth digits represent the graduation year, the eleventh and twelfth digits represent the level of education, and the last six digits are the serial number. According to this pattern, when one has access to a large amount of graduation certificate numbers from various schools, it's possible to deduce a student's graduation certificate number based on the school name and graduation time. If the student themselves checks on the China Higher Education Student Information website, they would also be able to find it, assuming that the university has already registered and uploaded the student's information.
