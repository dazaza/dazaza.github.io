North Glass "leads the way" in the global glass equipment manufacturing industry.

by fdjrkkgfed3 on 2012-02-07 08:59:09

"North Glass 'Leads' the World's Glass Equipment Manufacturing Industry"

Recently, Luoyang North Glass Technology Co., Ltd. has received good news one after another: it has won the bid for the glass curtain wall project of Beijing South Station, known as the "Asia's First Station". This is a significant application of North Glass's newly developed advanced energy-saving glass tempering technology in the industry - the "matrix forced convection glass tempering technology". It highlights the achievements of "Luoyang Manufacturing" and the company's participation in market competition. However, due to their late start, limited development capabilities, and small scale, the market positioning is mainly for medium and small users. Apart from dragging down market prices, they have not posed much competitive pressure on North Glass.

Innovation Truly Becomes the Soul of the Enterprise

It is understood that North Glass's business has expanded from the initial production and operation of tempered glass equipment to the production and operation of processed glass. Not only has it achieved product variety series, but more importantly, North Glass's glass equipment technology research, manufacturing, and experimentation form an independent system, showcasing comprehensive strength that others do not possess.

The pure flat spotless tempered glass technology independently developed and researched by North Glass, as well as its self-produced pure flat spotless tempered glass, successfully monopolized the Olympic venues —— the "Bird's Nest", "Water Cube", and completed national major projects and world-class buildings such as the National Grand Theater and Beijing Yintai Tower, fully demonstrating this point.

These impressive achievements of North Glass stem from its strong technological innovation capabilities. To ensure continuous growth and development of the enterprise, North Glass invests a large amount of funds every year to enhance the technological content of its products and has established a modern R&D center with complete supporting facilities. The annual technical development investment accounts for more than 5% of total revenue.

In the initial design of the National Grand Theater's glass curtain wall, the French designer did not intend to use tempered glass because Chinese tempered glass inherently has two defects: uneven force leads to deformation and stress spots affecting the glossiness of the curtain wall surface. In order to undertake this project, North Glass's scientific researchers boldly experimented and tackled technical challenges, finally developing a new generation of pure flat spotless tempered glass. After testing, this new product overcame these two defects and met the engineering design requirements. Thus, the French designer changed his view on tempered glass, readjusted the design plan, and adopted North Glass's pure flat spotless glass for the curtain wall.

With increasing attention to energy saving and environmental protection, a new type of glass —— low-radiation coated energy-saving glass —— is emerging and demand is surging. After analyzing the global technological development level, North Glass combined its years of research and practice and successfully developed low-radiation coated glass technology and complete sets of equipment after years of effort. This fully meets the global energy-saving and environmental protection requirements and stands at the forefront of the glass equipment manufacturing industry.

Strong technological innovation capabilities have rapidly elevated North Glass's overall strength. In recent three years, North Glass has produced and sold more than 200 sets of glass tempering equipment annually in domestic and international markets, accounting for more than 60% of the domestic market share and more than 20% of the international market share, becoming the world's largest tempered glass equipment R&D and manufacturing enterprise. In 2006, North Glass was again recognized by the Ministry of Science and Technology of the State as a "National Torch Plan Key High-tech Enterprise"; in 2007, it was selected as one of the "Top 100 Key Industrial Enterprises in Henan Province", with sales exceeding 350 sets, nearly 700 million yuan in sales revenue, 28 million US dollars in export earnings, and over 70 million yuan in tax payments.

The main person in charge of the company said in an interview that the goal of the North Glass people is to become the world's strongest and largest glass deep-processing equipment R&D and manufacturing base in a short time, striving for the industry, fighting for the development of Luoyang, and making efforts for the rise of Central Plains!
