Sichuan branch of China Telecom launches Tianyi Space App Mall - Xiantian IT.Net

by xjyzhenai on 2009-09-03 12:05:39

image cool Sichuan branch of China Telecom has recently launched a very forward-looking value-added service: Tianyi Space Application Mall. It is committed to providing high-quality one-stop services for mobile phone and PC terminal users, enabling customers to enjoy a pleasant shopping experience. The application software covers categories such as audio-visual entertainment, news information, games, finance, practical tools, finance, books, travel, social networks, etc., with hundreds of products available for free trial and paid purchase.

image

Tianyi is the 3G mobile communication brand of China Telecom. Since Sichuan Telecom has introduced Tianyi Space, its positioning must be aimed at mature 3G mobile applications. Indeed, its application mall mainly provides resources for mobile phones. As the carefully crafted Tianyi Space Application Mall by Telecom, it not only offers mobile application downloads to a wide range of users but also provides an online sales platform for mobile application developers.

Currently, it is mainly divided into 11 categories of applications: games, themes, entertainment, life, reading, communication, business, travel, education, finance, and tools. Now, all applications can be downloaded and used for free!

image

The developer community is part of the CTmarket.cn website of its application mall. This is a charming stage where creativity can be unleashed and infinite possibilities can be achieved. The community provides full support and services for application developers from research and development to market promotion. By publishing applications through the community, they can go online for sale after meeting commercial conditions, helping developers realize their dreams of creating wealth and developing careers; meanwhile, here you can share and obtain creative exchanges, experience testing, technical documents, etc. 7*24 online provides free advice, in-depth knowledge, and friendly face-to-face professional technical support, guiding you to complete the project and ultimately creating commercial benefits for you. image

In addition, the mobile client of the Tianyi Space Application Mall was grandly launched simultaneously with the mall. The interface of the mobile client is refreshing and simple, providing the most user-friendly operation interface for users.

cool Sichuan Telecom is a provincial branch company with a relatively high level of informatization application. For an old-style state-owned enterprise, having this concept is indeed forward-looking. The entire project's concept and interface are very dynamic and fashionable Web2.0 style elements. It allows users to not only obtain from the mall but also become entrepreneurs in the mall if you have a certain amount of business acumen or development ability, thereby gaining commercial benefits. And for Telecom, such resource interaction can be considered a low-cost win-win case.

Currently, China Telecom is in the second phase of transformation, focusing on using informatization applications to drive the development of traditional businesses and becoming a comprehensive information service provider. Various informatization applications are flourishing, which is also a reflection of China Telecom's exploration of the "Blue Ocean." Although Telecom has a significant advantage in the 3G field, as a mobile operator, its experience is not stronger than China Mobile and China Unicom, which specialize in mobile services. China Telecom has transitioned from traditional businesses such as fixed-line telephones and broadband, and its senior management's thinking is often confined to the planned economy era. As a telecommunications professional, JarryJarry greatly admires the transformation of the senior management's thinking in Sichuan Telecom, actively accepting new things is indeed not easy. As a 3G mobile application service, relying solely on this platform to create substantial profits is definitely unrealistic. The cost of purchasing one year of informatization product services is less than the monthly call fee of a mobile phone user. The purpose of opening various information services is to enhance the cohesion of the Tianyi brand and increase the overall value of 3G, driving the development of the 3G industry with rich and comprehensive informatization applications.
