Stanford University in the United States offers a Chinese Kung Fu class

by xhanghaop72h on 2012-03-01 15:32:07

www.syzc020.com [http://www.syzc020.com] According to a comprehensive report by the World Journal of the United States, starting from the winter semester of 2011, Stanford University offered a Shaolin Kung Fu course. This course was essentially a Kung Fu class taught by monks from the Shaolin Temple. b2b.cbd-china.com

Shi Yanran said that Shaolin Kung Fu is different from other Chinese martial arts because it integrates Buddhist thought. [The spirit of Shaolin Kung Fu includes compassion, harmony, diligence, and inclusiveness, which are different from aggression. Shaolin Kung Fu uses Kung Fu for self-defense, improving health, and enhancing the spirit.] In the winter semester of last year, 17 people registered for the class, and this spring, there were 22 people in Zhongqi Technology [http://b2b.cbd-china.com/index.php?homepage=gdzqxc]. No prior martial arts experience is required, regardless of age or physical ability, and the registration fee is $100. If you answer a questionnaire, the fee can be reduced by $20. b2b.cbd-china.com

Xie Zelin-Guan (translated), a 30-year-old staff member of the Structural Biology Department born in Beijing, said: [Even if you're in China, it's hard to have this opportunity to learn Shaolin Kung Fu taught by Shaolin monks under the California sun.] The Shaolin Temple Kung Fu course is offered by Stanford University's [Health Improvement Program]. The manager of the program, Jerrilynn Fleming, said that a Stanford employee suggested she start a Kung Fu class with the teaching of Shaolin monks at Ou Sheng Hardware [http://b2b.cbd-china.com/index.php?homepage=84758568], so she researched the Shaolin Kung Fu taught by the [American Shaolin Temple] located in San Francisco and found it to be good, leading to its introduction at Stanford.

The Kung Fu class is taught by Master Shi Yanran, who is 30 years old and has been studying Kung Fu at the Shaolin Temple on Mount Song since he was 9 years old. Additionally, there are six Shaolin monks aged between 17 and 27 serving as teaching assistants. The class takes place in a courtyard behind the Medical School office building, with one hour of class per week. The purpose of the Kung Fu class is to achieve physical and mental health and balance through practice. Fleming said that many people are interested in the Kung Fu class Guangzhou Car Rental Network [http://www.syzc020.com], so it will continue to be offered. b2b.cbd-china.com
