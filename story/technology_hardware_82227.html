If you see such an ambiguous text message on your phone, would you call?

Today I'm leaving. After thinking for a long time, I still decided not to call you. If you dial my number 1259025374 and listen to my voicemail, you will understand everything. I sent this message with someone else's number, so please don't return the call.

After dialing the number, you will hear a very unpleasant female voice saying: "What you are listening now is provided by Guangzhou xx company..."

Kao! GAME OVER!

This is just one example to tell you how these junk messages get onto your mobile phone:

Such companies are called SP merchants. They cooperate with China Mobile to promote their services and have users subscribe to them. China Mobile charges users, then splits the profit with the SP merchant. But now China Mobile is restricted by the rules of the Ministry of Information Industry, which in turn restricts these SP merchants. So now there are fewer cases of mysterious subscriptions happening on cellphones. Remember, as long as you do not actively reply or call the numbers from unfamiliar texts, you won't be subscribed to their services.

1. For unfamiliar text messages, check the sender’s number first. If it is not a mobile phone number but a string of digits, never reply or return the call.

2. For unfamiliar calls, wait 8-10 seconds before answering. If it is a friend who has changed their number, they will keep calling you until you pick up. If it is a nuisance call, they will hang up within a second to make you curious and encourage you to return the call. Once you return the call, it is considered an "active" behavior, and you may be subscribed to some service!

3. Here is another way to identify unfamiliar calls. Don't dismiss all unfamiliar calls, because it might be a friend trying to reach you! When you receive an unfamiliar call, check where the call is coming from first. You can go online and search www.baidu.com and input the phone number to see the location of the caller. In Guangdong area, you can also call 1008615 for free and input the unfamiliar number to check the origin of the call.

4. Do not leave your mobile number on random websites, because some companies specialize in collecting phone numbers.

In conclusion, be vigilant about unfamiliar calls and text messages. Don’t act impulsively, don’t get angry, and don’t be curious! This way, unfamiliar text messages will gradually decrease.
