More than 20 fire trucks deployed

"There is thick smoke and fire in the audience seats of the Hefei Opera Hall, it's burning and spreading, come quickly to put out the fire." This morning at nine o'clock, the quiet Hefei Grand Theatre suddenly echoed with urgent cries for help. A fire had broken out in the audience seats, the flames growing more intense, covering a large area, and posing great danger. The fire department immediately responded and carried out emergency rescue operations.

Don't worry, this was actually a concentrated drill conducted by the provincial fire department with Hefei as the key area. This fire drill even adopted an advanced live streaming method so that fire departments from other cities across the province could watch the firefighting process on-site, experiencing the tense atmosphere of the rescue scene without being physically present. "Staff members, please immediately activate the voice broadcast system to evacuate mall personnel and start the internal fire facilities to extinguish the fire." The Hefei Grand Theatre immediately initiated the square fire emergency rescue response plan.

The Tian'e Lake Company, Huizhou Park Company, High-tech Zone Company, Shushan Zone Company, Luyang Zone Company, Wanghu Zone Company, and Binhai Zone Company of the Hefei Fire Brigade immediately went to the opera hall of the Hefei Grand Theatre to fight the fire. At the scene, aerial ladder trucks and high-spray vehicles simultaneously sprayed water to extinguish the fire. Staff from the fire department told reporters that a fire had occurred in the opera hall of the Hefei Grand Theatre, with large flames covering about 1000 square meters, and people trapped inside. "We have mobilized seven companies, 25 fire trucks, and 100 officers to go to the scene to fight the fire," according to reports. The Tian'e Lake Company and High-tech Zone immediately organized reconnaissance of the fire situation and search and rescue of people, accurately grasping the fire situation and promptly evacuating and rescuing people; the Luyang Zone Company and Huizhou Park Company immediately carried out smoke extraction at the fire scene to improve visibility; the Tian'e Lake Company, Shushan Zone Company, Wanghu Zone Company, and Binhai Zone Company each laid down one main water hose line to enter the building's interior for interior attack firefighting.

Ten companies from the Hefei Brigade, 30 fire trucks, and 150 officers subsequently arrived at the scene for rescue. During the drill, the earthquake rescue teams from Hefei, Wuhu, and Fuyang, as well as the logistics support detachments from Hefei, Wuhu, Bengbu, and Anqing, quickly gathered and waited for orders to provide reinforcements.

In this exercise, the Fire Corps adopted a rescue method combining live streaming of the firefighting scene with on-site command from the control room. The Corps' think tank sat in the command hall, scheduling the scene in real time.
