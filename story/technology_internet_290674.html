1. After the non-public offering, the company's main business will increase the production and sales of heavy-duty trucks and chassis. Before the non-public offering, Sinoma Automobile's main business was bulk cement trucks, concrete mixer transport vehicles, and concrete pump vehicles, making it the largest domestic producer of tank-specific vehicles. In the first half of 2009, the company's revenue structure showed that the income from concrete mixer transport vehicles accounted for 63% of total revenue, bulk cement truck income accounted for 29%, and concrete pump vehicles and dump trucks each accounted for 4%. The main business of the acquired asset, Hualing Automobile, is the production and sales of heavy-duty trucks, vehicle chassis, and automotive parts. From the revenue in the first three quarters of 2009, Hualing Automobile's revenue was 2.5 billion yuan, which is 1.3 times the listed company Sinoma Automobile's 1.9 billion yuan. It achieved a net profit of 200 million yuan, which is 3.9 times the listed company Sinoma Automobile's 53.06 million yuan.

2. Acquiring assets can enhance the company's gross margin. Before the non-public offering, Sinoma Automobile's main raw material, the vehicle chassis, was mainly purchased from Hualing Automobile, constituting related-party transactions. After the completion of the non-public offering, it is expected to improve the gross margin of existing products. Additionally, after the decline in raw material prices, Hualing Automobile's product gross margin has improved somewhat. In the first three quarters of 2009, the gross margin was 13.36%, higher than the original product specialized vehicles' 11.16%. Therefore, it is expected that after the asset integration is completed, the overall gross margin of the company can be improved.

3. Positive outlook on the heavy truck industry where the acquired asset company is located. Currently, Sinoma Automobile's specialized automobile manufacturing ranks first domestically in the same industry, with a market share of about 30%. Hualing Automobile ranks eighth in the domestic heavy truck industry, with a market share of about 3%. With the 4 trillion yuan investment driving infrastructure construction increases and the logistics industry warming up, since 2009, heavy truck sales have recovered from the trough, and the industry growth rate has changed from a 60% decrease in January to a 71% increase. It is expected that the heavy truck industry will gradually become more prosperous as the economy warms up, which will also benefit the increase in company profits.

4. The purchase of assets at a relatively low price can enhance the company's overall profitability. Calculated based on the disclosed net assets of the target assets for the first three quarters of 2009, the PB ratio for the acquisition assets is 2 times, and the PE ratio is 8 times. Meanwhile, the PB ratio for the listed company in the first three quarters is 3 times, and the PE ratio is 32 times. The acquisition price is relatively low, which is beneficial for optimizing the listed company's assets.

Profit forecast assumptions: 1. Completion of the non-public offering in 2009; 2. Expected future improvement in the prosperity of the specialized automobile and heavy truck industries as the economy warms up; 3. Improvement in gross margin as the company integrates; 4. Deduction of associated transaction revenues according to the 2008 proportion. Assuming the company's non-public offering to acquire assets is completed in 2009, it is estimated to increase EPS by 0.2 yuan in 2009 and 0.23 yuan in 2010. Therefore, it is predicted that the EPS for 2009-2011 will be 0.61 yuan, 0.71 yuan, and 0.86 yuan respectively. Calculated based on the closing price of 9.72 yuan on November 30th, the corresponding dynamic P/E ratios are 14 times, 12 times, and 10 times respectively. After the transaction is completed, the company's profitability will be enhanced, and its valuation will be relatively advantageous. Calculated based on the reasonable P/E ratio of 20 times for 2010, the corresponding price is 14.3 yuan. Due to the company's currently low valuation and positive outlook on the heavy truck industry, the company rating has been upgraded to 'Buy'.

Risk提示s: 1. Approval risk for this transaction; 2. Risks regarding the progress and price changes of the restructuring; 3. Risk of the impact of supplementary tax payments on current earnings; 4. Risk of fluctuations in raw material prices.
