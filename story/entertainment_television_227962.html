Introduction to the Plot of Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated Season 3:

According to Wudi Entertainment News, the second season of Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated has not yet cooled down in terms of viewership ratings, and Hunan TV has already begun preparations for the filming of "Ugly Woman Season 3." The Ugly Woman's Transformation:

As an inspirational drama, the female protagonist Lin Wudi, with her braces, large glasses, braided pigtails, and outdated fashion sense, strives hard, works diligently, and possesses a kind heart, eventually earning the admiration and trust of her boss while also winning over a wide audience. The image of the unbeatable ugly woman reflects the reality of the majority of people, satisfying a self-examination and self-confirmation effect. However, just as "aesthetic" fatigue can occur, so too can "ugly" fatigue. To avoid such a situation, "Season 3 of Ugly Woman" will make significant adjustments to both the plot and character designs. It is understood that Wudi will experience her own romance in the new season, and after encountering her true love, her appearance will undergo significant changes. This may even include Wudi removing her iconic braces and oversized glasses in Season 3.

Replacing the Male Lead:

Compared to the transformation of the ugly woman, a more notable change is that Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated Season 3 will replace its male lead. Upon hearing this news, many enthusiastic viewers have recommended their ideal candidates for the new male lead to the director's team, including Han Geng, Zheng Yuanchang, Wu Chun, RAIN, Lee Joon-gi, and Liu Qian among others. The organizers revealed that the Ugly Woman director's team will fully respect the opinions of the audience, and the selection of the new male lead will be decided by the audience and the fans who adore the Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated series.

Currently, the casting event for the new male lead of Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated Season 3 - "Wudi No. 1" - co-hosted by Hunan TV, Hunan Entertainment Channel, Beijing Xiangchao, and Golden Eagle Network has started accepting entries. Any male individual aged 18 years or older can sign up to participate. The selection process for "Wudi No. 1" will break away from traditional talent shows; starting from the preliminary rounds, there will no longer be star judges, but instead, the fan base of Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated will form the "Wudi Fans Club," consisting of the Invincible Girl Group, Invincible Leftover Women Group, and Invincible Mother Group led by actors from the Ugly Woman Can't Be Defeated series. They represent three stages of a woman's life in viewing men and also reflect the standards by which female viewers select the new male lead.

The preliminary rounds of this event will air on Hunan Entertainment Channel at 7:30 PM starting February 27th. The grand finale will be broadcast simultaneously on Hunan TV, Hunan Entertainment Channel, and Golden Eagle Network on March 29th. At that time, the selected "Wudi No. 1" will compete with Ferdinand and become the new male lead in "Ugly Woman Season 3," pursuing Wudi in a special way.
