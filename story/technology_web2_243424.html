Ye Guhong, played by Carol Cheng, is an unlucky woman. She is quite pretty, but no one pays attention to her because not only does she constantly experience misfortunes, but those who get close to her also share in her bad luck. Although she has considerable knowledge of Feng Shui, metaphysics, astrology, Tarot cards, Gypsy fortune-telling, and more, unfortunately, these things do not help her change her misfortune. In Hong Kong, there is a famous Feng Shui numerologist, Lai Liaobu, played by Tony Leung, who is proficient in metaphysics and holds a master's degree. However, his ancestors left a warning not to associate with people surnamed Ye, as it would surely lead to loss. Despite their compatibility, Lai Liaobu repeatedly avoids Ye Guhong due to her surname. It turns out that behind Ye Guhong, there has always been a metaphysical expert, Duanxie, played by Zheng Zhongji, causing disruptions and affecting her fortune, making everything go wrong for her. After learning this, Lai Liaobu decides to challenge Duanxie to allow Ye Guhong to live a normal life. Why does Duanxie want to harm Ye Guhong? It turns out that there is a three-life grudge involved. Can Ye Guhong reverse her fate in the end? Will Lai Liaobu suffer divine retribution for assisting someone surnamed Ye?
