Retired worker sentenced to death in the first trial for collecting 80 million yuan by fraud

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-04 10:12:48

□Oriental Today reporter Zhang Bo A highly publicized case of capital collection fraud among Anyang residents was publicly tried at the Intermediate People's Court of Anyang City on the morning of January 14. After more than three hours of trial, the Intermediate People's Court of Anyang City made a first-instance verdict: defendant Liu Hongfei was guilty of capital collection fraud and sentenced to death, deprived of political rights for life, and had his personal property confiscated; defendant Liu Hongfei was ordered to compensate victims for their losses totaling 39,417,512 yuan.

The court found that Liu Hongfei is 41 years old and a retired worker from Ansteel Group due to illness. From August 14, 2005 to October 10, 2011, Liu Hongfei claimed that he could profit from investing in stocks and needed large amounts of funds. He concealed the fact that his investments in stocks and futures had been losing money year after year, luring unspecified individuals in society with high interest rates to illegally collect capital. The total amount collected was 85,370,102 yuan. Apart from the already returned part of the principal and interest, there is still 39,417,512 yuan that cannot be repaid.

After the case broke out, the investigating authorities recovered cash and goods valued at 373,169.39 yuan, resulting in actual losses totaling 39,044,342.61 yuan. Suspected of committing capital collection fraud, Liu Hongfei was criminally detained on October 11, 2011, and arrested on October 21 of the same year.

The court ruled that defendant Liu Hongfei's behavior constituted capital collection fraud. During the illegal capital collection period, defendant Liu Hongfei concealed the fact that his stock trading had been suffering serious annual losses. Knowing that he was unable to repay the loans to the victims, he still used the method of collecting capital while returning part of the principal and interest to deceive the trust of the victims, defrauding large amounts of funds. The amount of capital collection fraud by defendant Liu Hongfei was extremely huge, causing particularly major losses to the masses, with extremely serious consequences and great social harm. He should be severely punished. Accordingly, the Intermediate People's Court of Anyang City made the aforementioned first-instance judgment.

At the trial scene, many citizens participated as spectators. One of them, Mr. Du, an Anyang resident who had previously participated in this illegal capital collection, said that public trials allow the public to promptly understand the progress of the case, providing clarity to the citizens. Another citizen, Ms. Zhang, believed that truly solving capital collection fraud cases relies on the government. Taking extreme measures would only affect the progress of the investigation. Publicly trying Liu Hongfei's capital collection fraud case was a good example, and she hoped that local judicial authorities would increase efforts to investigate such cases and legally impose severe punishment on lawbreakers.
