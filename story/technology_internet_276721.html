Linyi's Month of High-quality Services in Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine, New Guarantee for Women's Health

by lyzxy692 on 2009-11-25 10:42:40

Today, the development of gynecological medical science and technology has enabled more patients to feel the charm of the achievements of medical science and technology. At the same time, with the progress of social science and technology, people have also put forward higher requirements for the quality of medical services.

The modernization of medical standards, the humanization, transparency, and efficiency of medical service quality have become a social consensus. In order to actively respond to the new medical reform people-oriented policies, Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital has indeed achieved this point, allowing medical technology and service levels to develop scientifically, harmoniously, and sustainably, which has now become a trend. Caring for women groups and paying attention to the prevention and treatment of gynecological diseases that are harmful to women groups. Provide high-quality guarantees for women's health.

In July 2009, under the support of all sectors, Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital hosted the "Red Feather Public Welfare Medical Quality Service Month Activity." While improving the level of medical services at every link in medical care, the hospital also introduced a series of preferential public welfare medical policies, allowing the masses to truly feel the benefits of affordable medical care.

Below, let us listen together to the voices of the experts involved in the activity.

Progress in the integration of traditional Chinese and Western medicine makes me feel proud from the bottom of my heart

Dean Huang Baoshan, former director of the Department of International Cooperation of the National Family Planning Commission and honorary dean of Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital:

As the honorary dean of Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital, I am somewhat surprised by the presence of such technically mature and advanced specialized gynecological institutions in Linyi. In order to better provide frontier minimally invasive medical health services for women in Linyi, this time our China Population Welfare Foundation and the Chinese Medical Foundation are collaborating with some local medical institutions and media in Linyi to carry out the "Quality Service Month Activity" at Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital.

Under the premise of strictly implementing the "Medical Quality Management Year," Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital continues to carry out the "Quality Service Month" activity within the hospital. On one hand, the hospital ensures that patients feel the tangible benefits of affordable medical care while guaranteeing the level of medical services; on the other hand, it makes patients feel a sense of belonging where "disease has support." To improve the overall service level of the hospital and wholeheartedly serve the patients, the hospital further improves the initial diagnosis and follow-up appointment system, shortens the waiting time for patients, and ensures that patients can be seen according to their scheduled appointment times. All services make patients feel "welcomed like guests," making the service impeccable. Through quality services, problems existing in the hospital's work will be comprehensively improved, presenting a brand-new image to provide the public with more satisfactory and high-quality services. We take pride in every advancement made by the integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine hospital.

Popularizing the achievements of medical reform and benefiting the people of Yimeng,

This is the major task of our current medical practitioners

Professor Cao Zeqi, former vice minister of the International Health Department and expert advisor of Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital:

On April 6, 2009, the State Council officially announced the final draft of the new medical reform plan. The basic principle of the new plan is to ensure that all people have access to medical care. For the first time, the plan explicitly proposed to safeguard people's health from the height of a "basic system," aiming to alleviate the difficulty and high cost of medical care by 2011. The new medical reform plan contains more elements of concern for people's livelihood, promoting health, and ensuring people's enjoyment, destined to add a significant stroke to the construction of a harmonious society. This time, I came to Linyi not only to bring academic discussions and exchanges on gynecological minimally invasive technologies but also to introduce and promote the people-oriented policies of medical reform. Popularizing the achievements of medical reform and benefiting the people of Yimeng is the major task of our current medical practitioners. It is hoped that good medical policies can bring a new atmosphere to medical institutions and bring more substantial benefits to a wide range of patients. Borrowing a phrase often used by the Dean of Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital: "Let every person in Yimeng afford medical care and get good medical care."

Displaying the humanitarian care of modern medical practices

Director Duan Zhihui, Gynecology Department of Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital:

Through the "Red Feather Love Public Welfare Medical Quality Service Month Activity," we conducted clinical experience discussions on gynecological minimally invasive surgeries with superior units and renowned gynecologists across various regions. Through the exemplary medical skills and medical ethics of these famous doctors, we, as integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine doctors, benefited greatly. Although our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine gynecology has already achieved leading results in the field of gynecological minimally invasive surgery in Linyi's gynecological industry, through academic exchanges, we discovered more effective new approaches and technologies to alleviate patients' pain, further broadening our horizons. With the strong support of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and various renowned doctors, we are more determined to use cutting-edge advanced medical technology to relieve the pain of our sisters.

A competent physician team and advanced equipment are the two keys to successfully conducting the quality service month

Director Yan Lifang, responsible for family planning specialties at Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital, with over 10,000 consecutive painless abortion surgeries without error:

Gynecological minimally invasive technology has been very maturely implemented in our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine practice. The operating rooms of integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine use advanced surgical instruments and equipment, successfully bidding farewell to the scalpel. Since the establishment of our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine, we have always provided humanistic medical services to a wide range of women as our prerequisite goal. With "Precision・Safety・Humanism・Care" as the core principle of strengthening the hospital through technology, through continuous learning and efforts, relying on advanced and reliable cutting-edge gynecological minimally invasive surgical instruments and equipment, supported by a highly skilled minimally invasive surgical team, and guided by the thoughts of the "Medical Quality Management Year," all clinical surgeries in our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine gynecology have successfully replaced the scalpel with minimally invasive technology. Visible surgeries, pain-free surgeries, nearly bloodless surgeries, surgeries without unsightly scars, surgeries considerate of women's psychological feelings, surgeries with lower costs than conventional surgeries... Precisely because of a competent physician team and advanced equipment, these are the two keys to the successful implementation of minimally invasive surgeries in integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine. We are delighted to see that with outstanding minimally invasive surgical technology, Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital is already leading far ahead in the forefront of the gynecological field.

A small step for integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine, a big step for women's health

Dean Zhou, Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital:

From the statistics of patient registration for scheduled surgeries, the "Public Welfare Promotion Activity for Gynecological Minimally Invasive Surgery" has reached the goals set by both the organizers and hosts, indicating that women friends have increased their understanding of minimally invasive surgery and that minimally invasive surgery, as an advanced surgical method, has gained the favor of women. This time, our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine gynecology was fortunate to host this event, directly guided and supported by the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences and renowned doctors from Beijing and Shanghai, collaborating with high-end medical technology, allowing our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine minimally invasive surgical technology to undergo another rare refinement and achieve a qualitative leap.

Our initial goal in establishing the integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine hospital was to allow every sister to afford medical care and receive good treatment. Through this "Public Welfare Promotion Activity for Gynecological Minimally Invasive Surgery," our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine gynecology will take the lead in responding to the new medical reform people-oriented policies in Linyi's gynecological field, truly fulfilling the social responsibilities of medical practitioners in saving lives and healing wounds, making "happy medical visits" even more substantial in content.

The successful promotion of gynecological minimally invasive technology represents a small step for our integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine, but facts prove that women's health has received more protection. As a leader in the gynecological field, Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital will continue to collaborate with high-end medical technology, establish a scientific, harmonious, and sustainable development perspective, and strive to set more exemplary roles within the industry, working hard to ensure that every sister can enjoy a worry-free healthy life.

This also testifies that Linyi Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital has gradually gained the support and trust of the general public over the years. They will continue to work harder, and I believe that integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine will have a better development platform, just as their slogan says: "Let integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine go to the world, and let the world come to integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine!"
