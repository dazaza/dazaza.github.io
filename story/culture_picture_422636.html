The Happy Life of a Pet Monkey

by fotomen on 2011-04-29 13:41:41

This set of black-and-white portraits of primates depicts the daily lives of the gorillas and monkeys adopted by ROBIN SCHWARTZ's family. Each of them has its own name and unique personality. Some are mischievous and active like children, while others have expressions just like adults. Their eyes and every move are truly hilarious!

Sweep away the gray mood and create a colorful Changcheng, Shanxi.
