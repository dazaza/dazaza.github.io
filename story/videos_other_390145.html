Steps to open a store online: The first step, log in to Taobao and register, fill in the account - set password - set email (this is the Alipay account). Second step, log in to the mailbox - activate - Alipay activation successful. Third step, real-name authentication (first, copy the Alipay account, hold your ID card to the Construction Bank to handle the Alipay cartoon and open the online banking function): (1), log in to the Alipay homepage with your Alipay account and choose to enter "My Alipay"; (2), after entering "My Alipay", select "Account Management Information"; (3), after entering "Account Management Information", select "Bank Account Management"; (4), after entering "Bank Account Management", accurately fill out the form, then click "Save Immediately" after completion; (5), Alipay will inform you that your account information has been saved, and the bank account binding is complete! (6), check the Alipay remittance at the bank within three days. If you see amounts like 0.05 yuan, that's it. (7), log in to Taobao - Alipay - certification - input the number 0.05. (8), log in to your Alipay email - click activate - the system automatically returns to the Alipay page - successfully activated! Then go to Taobao, click on free store opening, then you need to take an exam before opening the store, 60 points to pass. Upload 10 items and you're done.
