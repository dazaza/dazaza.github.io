Shi's son introduced that his father Shi is from Hangbu Town, Shucheng County. In 2005, to improve the family's economic situation, his father went alone to Hefei to do wooden dragon bone business in a building materials market, while his mother Shen stayed at home to take care of the housework. As the business gradually became prosperous, the initially poor family became wealthy, but his father's heart also became restless. His father first kept a mistress, a woman from Hunan, and then proposed divorce to his mother. Heartbroken, his mother attempted suicide by cutting her wrists. Fortunately, the neighbors discovered it in time and avoided a tragedy. However, before one incident was settled, another arose. In 2009, his mother was diagnosed with early-stage cervical cancer in Hefei. His father ignored it completely, and all medical expenses were paid by his two sisters.

According to the judge handling the case, in June 2010, the Hunan woman kept by Shi could not agree on the "separation fee" with Shi. Enraged, she called Shen and informed her that Shi had met a female high school student online and cohabited with her. Realizing his misconduct was exposed, Shi immediately transferred the store he operated at a high price and disappeared with more than 300,000 yuan. Shi's suppliers couldn't get their payment, so they came to Shucheng to ask Shen for money. Faced with emotional distress, illness, debt, and the high tuition fees for his son's university education, Shen almost collapsed. In desperation, Shi's son posted the above "wanted" notice about his biological father on the internet. Recently, in despair, Shen filed for divorce. The court, based on the plaintiff's evidence, confirmed that defendant Shi had an improper extramarital affair, violating the obligation of mutual fidelity and assistance between spouses. The court ruled in favor of the divorce, and all current family property was awarded to Shen. (Communicated by Zhang Zhiguo, reported by Fang Ronggang)

"He is an irresponsible father. He has been married to my mother for 26 years, yet he keeps a female high school student as a mistress! As a divorce case was being tried, the mysterious poster was revealed - he turned out to be a third-year college student from Shucheng.
