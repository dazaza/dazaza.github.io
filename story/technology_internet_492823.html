Yesterday (November 10), I finished the exams of several courses in the short-term semester. In the evening, I watched the hosting competition of Peking University and suddenly felt that the study life of postgraduate has passed one eighth.

Originally, I wanted to change my lifestyle and indulge in university life. Unexpectedly, after coming here, it overturned my dream. The main theme of life is busy. The degree of busyness is more than twice as much as when I was working. Here, I need to have class at eight o'clock, discuss cases, write homework, and stay up until one or two o'clock in the morning. I miss the leisure days of going to work on time, drinking tea, and exercising outside. It's too normal to stay in the yard of Peking University for one or two weeks without going out. Life and death, eating, drinking, urination and defecation can all be solved here.

Well, finally came to Peking University. Is PKU my dream? No, I've never dreamed of it. But I came here so confusedly. After coming here, I found that it was nothing special. However, it can not be denied that PKU does have many advantages, such as rich cultural and entertainment life, excellent talents and teachers. Last night, I watched the hosting competition and couldn't help sighing that there are really a lot of talented people in Peking University. I went to college for four years and my school didn't hold such a grand evening party. I was unhappy because I was like a frog at the bottom of a well. In fact, the world outside is very big.

The feeling of learning is very good and full.
