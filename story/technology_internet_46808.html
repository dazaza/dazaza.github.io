Soubars' original slide function

by yinyin on 2008-02-03 10:46:29

Support users to browse their own posts in slideshow mode, thus bringing a more pleasant picture browsing experience. After users finish creating a post, they just need to check "add to slideshow" when publishing, and the pictures will automatically be added to the slideshow mode. And all posts with added slideshow mode will display a slideshow icon. After clicking it, users can watch the post in slideshow mode.
