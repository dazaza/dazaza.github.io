Brief Analysis of Large CNC Machining Centers: Large CNC Machining - CNC is the abbreviation for Computerized Numerical Control in English (Computer Digital Control), also known as large computer milling machining. A large CNC machining numerical control lathe automatically processes parts according to a pre-compiled processing program. We take the part's machining process route, process parameters, tool motion trajectory, displacement amount, cutting parameters (spindle speed, feed rate, depth of cut, etc.), and auxiliary functions (tool change, spindle forward rotation, reverse rotation, cutting fluid on/off, etc.), and write them into a machining program sheet according to the instruction codes and program format specified by the CNC machine tool. Then, we record the content of this program sheet onto a control medium (such as punched paper tape, magnetic tape, floppy disk, magnetic bubble memory), and input it into the CNC device of the CNC lathe, thereby commanding the lathe to process the part.

Large CNC machining numerical control lathes, or computer numerical control lathes, are currently the most widely used type of CNC machine tool in China, accounting for about 25% of the total number of CNC machine tools. CNC machine tools are mechatronic products that integrate multiple technologies such as mechanics, electronics, hydraulics, pneumatics, microelectronics, and information technology. They are mother machines in mechanical manufacturing equipment with advantages of high precision, high efficiency, high automation, and high flexibility.

The technical level of CNC machine tools and their percentage in the output and total ownership of metal cutting machine tools is one of the important indicators for measuring the national economic development and overall level of industrial manufacturing in a country. CNC lathes are one of the main varieties of CNC machine tools, holding a very important position among CNC machine tools. For decades, they have been universally valued by countries around the world and have developed rapidly.
