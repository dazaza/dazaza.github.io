Three-chamber thermal shock test chamber, technical parameters of three-chamber thermal shock test. Performance: The performance is measured after stabilizing for 2 hours without test load and no shelf. In addition, the temperature rise and fall time refers to the performance of air-cooled type at room temperature +25℃, under no-load conditions, cold-heat shock test chamber. 2.1 Internal chamber dimensions: W400H350D350 (mm), 50L; 2.2 External dimensions: approximately W1550H1680D1700 (mm) (the external appearance is subject to the final physical product); 2.3 High and low temperature range: A. High temperature section: RT+10℃～+200℃; B. Low temperature section: -10℃～-55℃; C. Shock range: (+60℃～+150℃)/(-40℃～-10℃); 2.4 Test method: Pneumatic damper switching between two or three temperature zones; 2.5 Temperature rising and falling rate: Heating time: +60℃～200℃ in 20min, Cooling time: +20℃～-55℃ in 60min.

**Material specifications for the three-chamber thermal shock test chamber:**

- Outer box material: SUS304# stainless steel or A3 double-sided galvanized steel plate with surface plastic spraying treatment (if "galvanized A3 cold-rolled steel plate with surface electrostatic spraying" shell is selected, the construction period will be extended by three days.)

- Inner box material: Stainless steel plate SUS#304.

- Box insulation material: Rigid polyurethane foam + glass fiber.

- Door insulation material: Rigid polyurethane foam.

- Air regulation channel: Fan, heater, evaporator, damper, temperature sensor.

- Standard configuration: 2 sample partition shelves, 4 leveling feet, 6 adjustment wheels.

**Electrical control system for the three-chamber thermal shock test chamber:**

1. Controller: Touchscreen bilingual display + PLC (control software) + temperature control module.

2. Operation mode: Program mode.

(1) Control objects:

- Test area exposure temperature

- Preheating temperature of high-temperature constant temperature zone

- Preheating temperature of low-temperature constant temperature zone

- Defrosting temperature of low-temperature constant temperature zone

(2) Indication accuracy: 0.1℃.

(3) Input: Thermocouple TDIN.

(4) Control method: Microcomputer PID+SSR control.
