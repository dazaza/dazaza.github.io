Guidance

To successfully cashback, clear your browser cookies first

On various cashback forums, many netizens have expressed that despite following normal procedures, they still did not receive the corresponding cashback. Regarding this situation, Buyren from YiGou.com introduced that to correctly obtain cashback, the following steps are quite important. First, clear the cookies in your browser before logging in. Cookies are data placed by websites to identify users, and previous cookies may contain records of logging into related malls through other methods. Clearing cookies and then logging into related cashback websites ensures that the website records where you came from and guarantees that you receive cashback. Second, before completing the transaction after entering the shopping mall through the cashback website channel, do not visit other websites or use other search engines. Otherwise, you may lose the record of cashback due to switching searches. If you used another search tool, it is recommended to re-enter from the related cashback website before making a purchase. Lastly, note that successful cashback does not mean you can immediately exchange it for cash. Because the entire transaction completion includes shipping after shopping and after-sales services such as returns, final confirmation of a single cashback often requires one to two months.

Zixuan from Nancheng, Dongguan, also indicated that to prevent unsuccessful cashback, the simplest operation for netizens is to first target products on the related mall, and then directly purchase through the cashback URL channel. This avoids mistakes made during the selection process.

Clarification

Directly consult merchants to verify website authenticity

Despite this, many netizens still have significant doubts about the authenticity of cashback websites and how to choose them. Experienced online shopper Ai Feng introduced that when choosing cashback websites, the following points should be noted. First, choose larger or well-known websites. If it's a newly launched cashback website, you can communicate with the merchant of the product you intend to buy to verify the authenticity of the website. To verify the authenticity of merchants linked through cashback websites, you can simultaneously use other search engines to open the same merchant's webpage to ensure its accuracy.

Although various cashback websites claim up to 50% cashback on Taobao, netizens need to be aware that these cashback websites generally only cooperate with some Taobao sellers, not all sellers on Taobao. According to Xiao Haikun, Senior Manager of the South China Center of Alibaba Group's Marketing Department, because Taobao already has many large merchants whose product volumes even exceed those of some small B2C websites, general cashback websites would not overlook this market segment.
