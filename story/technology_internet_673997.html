How Much Do You Know About the New Ways to Apply Trendy Wallpapers?

The water-soaking construction method is an incorrect way because the wallpaper backing absorbs too much water after soaking, making it hard to adhere properly, leading to insufficient glue absorption, weak adhesion, and subsequently, gaps and detachment after installation.

2. Keep the Labels Well. Keeping the labels well allows you to replenish supplies according to the color number and batch number on the label in case there is any shortage of wallpaper quantity.

3. Wipe Overflowed Glue with Wet Sponge as Much as Possible. Use a clean wet sponge to wipe off the glue that overflows during construction; using a cloth makes it harder to clean the glue in the wallpaper texture thoroughly, affecting the decorative effect.

4. Prepare the Wall Surface Before Construction. Sand the painted walls to help the glue adhere better to the wall; remove the powdered surface on plastered walls so that when applying wallpapers, no curling or detachment will occur.

5. Avoid Glue Overflow When Applying Textile Wall Fabrics [http://www.asheu.com/chanpinweb/001osqz001.htm]. Absolutely prevent glue from overflowing when mounting textile wall fabrics. If the glue gets on the surface of the fabric, it cannot be cleaned and will gradually cause the fabric to turn yellow.

6. Where to Apply the Glue? There are two types of materials on the backside of wallpapers: paper base and cloth base. (1) Paper-based wallpapers: The paper base expands upon absorbing water, so it is essential to apply glue on the paper base and let it sit for 5 minutes to allow the paper base to absorb the glue and flatten. (2) Cloth-based wallpapers: Since cloth-based wallpapers do not expand, just apply glue on the wallpaper itself.

7. How to Mix Glue with Appropriate Viscosity? When mixing glue, to judge its viscosity, insert a chopstick into the prepared glue. If the chopstick falls over, the glue is too thin; if the chopstick stands upright, the viscosity is basically appropriate.

8. Reverse Folding Method. After applying glue to the wallpaper backing, fold it face-to-face and back-to-back to avoid the glue sticking to the wallpaper surface.

9. Three Benefits of Applying Primer to Walls Before Construction: ① Makes future wallpaper replacement easier; ② Enhances waterproofing and anti-mildew effects; ③ Isolates acidic and alkaline substances seeping out from within the walls.
