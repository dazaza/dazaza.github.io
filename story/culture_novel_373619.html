Ghosts Online - Unlocking the Door to the Netherworld with ICQ

by bryanbian on 2010-12-07 12:04:55

I was at home alone, staring blankly at the computer, and without any awareness, I actually used ICQ to search for a group called "5174" - now you know how bored I was, right?

The search result - "The group number you entered is incorrect, please re-enter" - no suspense, no surprise. Disappointed, I closed the search window, propped my chin in my hand, and yawned.

Bang bang bang, bang bang bang - about half a minute later, the system message knocking sound suddenly came out of the computer speaker. I clicked on the flashing little speaker in the lower right corner of the screen, and a message popped up - "Administrator Yinhelie (5174) has approved your group request".

I was half asleep at that time, so I casually clicked OK. After clicking, I blinked my eyes and suddenly realized! What was that just now?! 5174 approved my group request? ! I immediately brought up the ICQ system message record, but the day's record was blank.

I stared at the ICQ panel with a sense of frustration, thinking why ICQ was acting so strange today, clearing contacts for no reason, and then asking me if I was there for no reason? Where the hell are you, where am I going? While I was angry, a name on ICQ caught my attention - Tom Liu! Tom Liu... I kept repeating this name in my mind. Wasn't Tom Liu the former head of our company's planning department, Liu Changfu? And didn't he die in a car accident?!

I cursed my bad luck - only one person left in the colleague group, and it's someone who has already passed away. How unlucky!

I wanted to drag his name to the blacklist with my mouse, because I really feared he might talk to me again and scare me, but when I moved the mouse halfway, I froze - I noticed the only two names in my classmates group: Shao Qiang and Luo Dandan.

Shao Qiang was my classmate from high school, a troublemaker who spent all day in pool halls and gaming rooms. He would do anything to deceive others while in school. I remember he even borrowed 50 yuan from me. We never contacted each other after graduation, and he never returned the money. Later, I heard he was sentenced to death for robbery and murder. The other one, Luo Dandan, was my college classmate who died shortly after graduation due to kidney failure.

In an instant, I felt my brain go blank with a loud boom!

My hand holding the mouse was shaking uncontrollably, and the mouse hit the table making a clinking sound. My heart tightened, and I stretched my neck, dumbly staring at the ICQ panel. At this moment, ICQ suddenly rang again. When I focused, it was Luo Dandan! Luo Dandan was actually talking to me!

How dare I check what message she sent. I tried to close ICQ with the mouse, but my hand was shaking too much that I couldn't click on the small "X". In my anxious state, suddenly a dialog box popped up - Luo Dandan was requesting a video chat with me!
