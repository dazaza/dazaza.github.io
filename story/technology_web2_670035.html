They love each other very much.

by chao7wudi7 on 2012-02-20 14:38:35

That was a poor family, and two little lives came into the world. They were twins, one named Xinmei and the other named Xinli. They grew up gradually. When they were 7 or 8 years old, under the pressure of poverty, the family decided to send one away. Finally, they decided to send away the elder sister Xinmei. Xinli grew up in the warmth of family love, while Xinmei was sold to a poor mountain village and became a child bride.

Time passed by, and soon Xinli was 20 years old. She had her own promising company and met her ideal person in the company. Xinmei, on the other hand, escaped from the mountain village when she grew up and came to this city to work. She also had a boyfriend, and they loved each other deeply.

Perhaps fate played tricks, and the two sisters met again. It happened when Xinli was off work. The scene was awkward. Two people who looked almost identical stared at each other, but there was only coldness. After that encounter, they never saw each other again. When Xinli was 22, she married Chen Fang, and everyone celebrated. But not long after, something was wrong with Chen Fang, but Xinli couldn't find anything abnormal.

One day, Xinli discovered it. In the album of Chen Fang, there was a photo of "herself" and him together. She clearly knew that the person in the photo who looked exactly like her was her sister Xinmei. After Chen Fang came home from work, Xinli showed him the photo angrily. Chen Fang was very anxious because he thought Xinli and Xinmei were the same person. He thought Xinmei was just teasing him! More terrifyingly, Xinli found that the bride in the wedding photo was not herself but Xinmei. Chen Fang was stunned too. However, this matter should not be publicized, so no one said it out loud.

A year later, Xinli gave birth to a baby, and named her Haiyao. Since the last encounter, Xinli hadn't seen her sister anymore, and she didn't think much about it. Until one day, she returned from a business trip and found Xinmei walking with Chen Fang, which was sensationalized by the media. She didn't go straight to them but followed secretly behind. She found that Chen Fang brought Xinmei back to their home. This angered Xinli, and she rushed into the house, only to find Chen Fang alone.

Chen Fang's response shocked her. He said, "Why didn't you say anything when you came back? You scared me." Xinli lay in bed sleepless at night. Moreover, she found that Haiyao no longer cried like other children but stared at her intently. In a panic, she got out of bed and turned on the light. What made her crazier was that there was another "her" on the bed. But she couldn't wake up Chen Fang no matter how she tried, and she couldn't even make a sound. Suddenly, Xinli felt a pain in her chest and lost consciousness. After a long time, Xinli opened her eyes, but she couldn't touch anything. Yes, she was dead. A ghost can't touch things.

She couldn't leave. She watched the man she once loved happily with another "her". She understood that what Chen Fang loved was not her but her wealth. Chen Fang and her sister had deceived her money and killed her. Xinli was dead, and she knew how she died and all the truth. Chen Fang was first Xinmei's boyfriend. To get rich, he approached her, and finally created a terrifying scenario, causing her, who had heart disease, to die of shock. And the "Haiyao" in the baby basket was fake. It was the child of Chen Fang and her sister, and her own child was gone long ago. She was full of hatred and wanted revenge, but she knew she couldn't touch anything because she was a ghost.

Until one day she met an old ghost, who told her that as long as she borrowed someone's body, she could do what she wanted like a living person. But she must wait until her soul was strong enough, otherwise, she would easily disintegrate. Xinli waited and waited, and finally, she successfully entered a woman's body, yes, the owner of a contact lens store. She borrowed her body and originally wanted to kill Xinmei's child, fake Haiyao. But she escaped, and she was seriously injured due to the urgency of escape.

After recovering, she entered another girl's body and pretended to be Haiyao's distant cousin. On Haiyao's birthday, she killed her. After Haiyao's death, Chen Fang and her sister adopted another child named Zhi Zhi, and she successfully killed her too. Then, anyone related to Xinmei would die, including Chen Fang!

The letter fell to the ground. At this moment, Xinmei squatted on the ground crying helplessly, and Chen Fang beside her was also frightened. Xinmei didn't tell Chen Fang what she wanted to say, but only said, "This day has come." She walked towards the bedroom. Chen Fang thought she was stimulated, so he prepared to buy some medicine at the pharmacy. He drove away. While driving, he found an extra person in the rear seat through the mirror, but it was Xinmei. "When did you get in? I didn't notice!" "I sneaked in!" Xinmei smiled and said. "Can I ask you a few questions?" "Of course." "Do you remember Xinli?" The car slowed down significantly. "Why do you suddenly ask this?" Chen Fang was surprised. "Remember." "Hmm, have you ever liked her?" His reply was silence. "I promised I wouldn't get angry," Xinmei added. "I liked her, but because of you, I did something wrong to her."

"Do you still remember where I asked you to throw her child into the river?" "This..." Chen Fang's sweat dripped down his forehead. The car stopped. "Sorry, Xinmei, I lied to you, sorry..." Xinmei paused and asked, "What did you lie to me about?" "Since you asked, I'll tell you. Actually, at that time, I didn't throw Xinli's child into the river, but sent our child to the orphanage. You always thought that Haiyao at home was ours, but actually, she was my child with Xinli. Because I felt killing Xinli was already wrong to her, I couldn't bear to let go of the child either. Unfortunately, Haiyao ended up dying, and I failed as a father, sorry Xinli."

Xinmei was expected to be furious in the back seat, but Chen Fang found that she was crying, she was crying!!! Then she said, "Let's go home." Seeing her cry bitterly, Chen Fang immediately turned around and drove home. After getting home, Chen Fang and Xinmei lay in bed, Xinmei hugged him and kept crying, and finally, Chen Fang coaxed her to sleep. The next morning, Chen Fang found that Xinmei disappeared, so he went out to look for her immediately.

Not far away, at an orphanage, Xinmei stood under a tree with a child, touched her head, said something, and then left. The next day, Xinmei was found under a bridge, alive, but without hands. Next to her was a letter, written as follows: "Thank you, you didn't disappoint me completely. My resentment caused Haiyao's real death. Maybe this is my punishment. I decide to let go, but it doesn't mean I don't hate you. Your hands are stained with my blood, so I want you to lose them. I hope you cherish life. I'm leaving and won't disturb you anymore. Take 'Haiyao' from the orphanage home, she needs a family! See you in the next life, sister, Chen Fang!"

Xiaoyang is seven years old this year. His grandfather gave him a long-life lock that looks round and flat. This lock is strange. It is dark red all over, occasionally with unknown sticky red things like thickened blood. More strangely, his grandfather mysteriously died after giving Xiaoyang the long-life lock, and no one knows exactly how he died. Before his death, he was physically fit and had no illness.

"The sound of blood flowing is pleasant to hear. Xiaoyang likes red blood, and the blood keeps flowing... It seems like a dying child moaning..." This was the sixth time Xiaoyang sang such a song that made people's hair stand on end. No one taught him, but he sang it fluently and maturely. Since he had this long-life lock, his mind was unclear, his words and deeds were somewhat crazy, and his behavior was somewhat strange. Even if his mother cried and begged him to stop, he seemed to hear nothing and see nothing, treating the people around him as transparent, not eating or drinking.

Relatives and friends all said Xiaoyang was "possessed". Finally, one day, his mother invited a Taoist priest to exorcise the demon. As soon as the Taoist entered the room, he frowned and muttered, "Why is there such heavy yin energy?" (Heavy yin energy indicates that this ghost did not die normally. It might have experienced unbearable grievances and sufferings before being murdered, resulting in posthumous yin-cold energy. Or it might have been suicide, with extreme anger and unresolved wishes, wearing a red skirt.)

Seeing the Taoist frown, Xiaoyang's mother anxiously asked, "Master Taoist, this...?" "Don't speak, let me check." The Taoist carefully walked ahead, cautiously looking around. The deeper he went, the more his eyebrows furrowed. This ghost has been dead for at least a hundred years, and before death, it was extremely yin-cold. It died unjustly and had the ability to suck souls... "Probably, even I alone cannot handle it. Even if my master and I combined forces, we might not be able to deal with it." The Taoist sighed and slowly said, "Alright, my junior brother's abilities may not match mine, but if all ten of my junior brothers come, there might be a glimmer of hope. I will go back first and call my master and juniors. Before we arrive, you need to splash chicken blood on your son every day, wash it off with clear water after an hour, and then apply yellow rice until we arrive."

Every day, Yang's mother splashed chicken blood on Xiaoyang and applied yellow rice. Xiaoyang's actions became less frequent, but the terrible songs never stopped. Not long after, all the Taoists gathered at Xiaoyang's house. They set up a platform, sprinkled black dog blood, pasted yellow talismans, placed wine cups, and began the ceremony. "The five gods of heaven and earth grant me strength, gather the essence of the sun and moon, the spirit of heaven and earth, expel demons and eliminate evil spirits..."

The voices of the Taoists rose and fell. Xiaoyang was tied to the platform, his hands and feet bound tightly with ropes leaving deep red marks. Around his neck hung the dark red long-life lock, its red color having a silent allure, tempting people to reach out and grab it. "The sound of blood flowing is pleasant to hear. Xiaoyang likes red blood, and the blood keeps flowing... It seems like a dying child moaning..." The voice sounded, accompanied by eerie childlike singing, making it even stranger.

Suddenly, there was a "thud... thud... thud" sound, like a woman wearing high heels. Very rhythmic, it grew louder as it approached. The Taoists held their breaths, watching the ghost appear. Upon closer inspection, it was a female ghost wearing a red strapless dress. "You've come?" The female ghost's voice was extremely gloomy, exuding chilling coldness. "Female ghost, we've come to subdue you today." The leading Taoist spoke rudely, grabbing a spiritual talisman and rushing forward alone. Just as he approached the female ghost, she vanished instantly. Then, she suddenly appeared behind him, her long hands pinching his neck, and with a great force, he was unable to resist. The female ghost opened her big mouth and viciously sucked the Taoist's soul. The old Taoist struggled to paste the yellow talisman on the female ghost's forehead, causing her to stagger back a few steps. Seeing this, the other Taoists rushed forward, surrounding the female ghost. They chanted spells and splashed dog blood, immobilizing the female ghost. Finally, the old Taoist swung his sword, and with an "ah", the incident subsided.
