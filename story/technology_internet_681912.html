Training Prospects

The second article of the Ministry of Labor and Social Security of the People's Republic of China Order No. 6, "Regulations on the Recruitment of Technical Workers," points out that employers recruiting workers engaged in technically complex work as well as those involving state property, people's lives safety, and consumer interests must hire from individuals who have obtained the corresponding occupational qualification certificates. Therefore, Suzhou electrician training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/] maintenance electricians must hold national occupational qualification certificates. National occupational qualifications are divided into five levels, starting from the primary level up to the senior technician.

An occupational qualification certificate is proof that a worker possesses the necessary knowledge and skills for a particular occupation. It serves as a qualification voucher for job seekers, employees, and entrepreneurs. It is also the main basis for employers to recruit and hire workers and an effective document for skill level notarization for overseas employment and foreign labor cooperation personnel Suzhou accounting training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com].

Qualification Certification

Through the corresponding occupational qualification appraisal, those who pass can simultaneously obtain the intermediate certificate of "Maintenance Electrician" issued by the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security of the People's Republic of China, which is electronically registered and can be queried online (query website: http://www.osta.org.cn/[http://www.osta.org.cn/]) and is universally recognized nationwide. Moreover, one can apply for the special operations operation certificate (IC card) issued by the State Administration for Market Regulation.
