◆ Hall Cleaning

Hall cleaning work [http://www.shcibj.com] is mainly carried out at night, with maintenance and upkeep during the day:

1. Use a floor mop to cyclically wipe the floor to keep it clean at all times.

2. Mopping should be done according to specified routes. Upon reaching the endpoint, shake off the dust attached to the mop before proceeding in a circular manner.

3. During the operation, appropriately avoid guests and areas where guests gather based on actual conditions. After the guests disperse, continue cleaning. Greet guests when encountered.

4. Focus on frequently used entrances/exits and easily dirty areas, increasing the frequency of mopping.

5. In case of snow or rain, place a doormat at the hall exit to prevent guests from slipping. Regularly beat the doormat to remove dust or wash it.

6. Wipe the floating dust, fingerprints, and dirt stains on the glass doors and windows of the hall to make them bright and clean.

7. Clean the staff passage stairs daily with clear water, ensuring that there is no phlegm in the spittoon, no garbage, cigarette butts, or paper scraps on the stairs.

8. Regularly clean escalators and elevators. Ensure that the carpet inside the elevator is clean without stains or debris, and the glass inside the elevator is clean and bright, maintaining cleanliness and tidiness.

9. Clean the furniture, sofas, telephones, ashtrays, table lamps, and service desks placed on the ground in the hall to make them shiny and dust-free. The telephone should be free of stains and odors.

10. Timely wipe the standing ashtray, ensuring that the number of cigarette butts in the tray does not exceed three.

11. Frequently change the guest ashtrays, ensuring that the number of cigarette butts in the ashtray does not exceed three. When changing, cover the dirty ashtray with a clean one and then replace it with a clean one.

12. If you see paper scraps or other debris on the coffee table or on the ground, pick them up and clean them promptly.

◆ Floor Washing Procedure

1. Prepare the necessary tools: warning sign, mop, small bucket with adhesive device, broom, cleaner.

2. Before washing the floor, sweep away dust and trash.

3. Before starting to wash the floor, place the [Floor Wet] sign first, then start washing from the direction of the water source.

4. Wash the mop in the bucket, then use the adhesive device to wring it out.

5. Place the bucket 1.5 meters behind the water source.

6. Spread your feet shoulder-width apart while washing the floor, keeping your back straight.

7. Wash the floor three times before cleaning the mop once, and change the water if necessary.

◆ Mirror Cleaning Procedure

1. Use a semi-wet glass cleaning cloth to wipe from one end of the mirror to the other, wiping from top to bottom and left to right (to remove dust).

2. Use a clean dry cloth to wipe the mirror until it shines (check for dust spots and ensure they are completely removed).

3. Identify mirrors that need focused cleaning.

4. Use a glass cleaner to wipe from one end of the mirror to the other, wiping up and down, left and right until the entire surface is covered.

5. Spray the glass cleaner in the center of the mirror, only spraying once (to make it easier to clean).

6. Wipe the mirror until it shines brightly.

7. Observe the mirror from the side to check the cleaning effect, re-wiping as necessary if unsatisfied.
