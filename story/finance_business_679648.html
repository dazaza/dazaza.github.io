Font size: T|T 0 participants 0 comments Print Forward. Independent Director of Anhui Wan Energy Co., Ltd. Independent opinion on the company's non-public offering of shares involving related party transactions.

According to the relevant provisions of the "Corporate Governance Guidelines for Listed Companies" and the "Guidance on Establishing an Independent Director System in Listed Companies" issued by the China Securities Regulatory Commission, as well as the "Articles of Association of Anhui Wan Energy Co., Ltd." and the "Work System of Independent Directors of Anhui Wan Energy Co., Ltd.", as independent directors of the company, we hereby issue the following independent opinions on the non-public issuance of shares involving related party transactions:

1. This non-public issuance complies with national laws, regulations, and other normative documents, providing financial security for the company's subsequent long-term healthy development. The funds raised through this non-public issuance are intended to be invested in the "Expansion Project of Unit 6 of Hefei Power Plant" and to acquire 80% equity in Anhui Electric Power Fuel Co., Ltd., 50% equity in Linhuan Zhongli Power Generation Co., Ltd., and 2% equity in Qinshan Nuclear Power Joint Venture Co., Ltd. held by Anhui Energy Group Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Wan Energy Group"). These actions will help further optimize the structure of the company's power assets, ensure fuel procurement channels, reduce operating risks, and enhance the company's profitability, which is in the interest of the company and all shareholders without harming the interests of small and medium shareholders. Wan Energy Group's subscription to the company's non-publicly issued shares will help improve the company's market image and maintain stock price stability.

2. The content and signing procedures of the conditional effective "Share Subscription Agreement" signed between the company and Wan Energy Group comply with national laws, regulations, and other normative documents. The agreed subscription price in the agreement is fair, in line with the interests of the company and all shareholders, without harming the interests of small and medium shareholders. Wan Energy Group has committed not to transfer the non-publicly issued shares it subscribes to for 36 months from the date of completion of this issuance.

3. The content and signing procedures of the conditional effective "Equity Transfer Agreement" signed between the company and Wan Energy Group comply with national laws, regulations, and other normative documents. The agreed transaction pricing method in the agreement is fair, in line with the interests of the company and all shareholders, without harming the interests of small and medium shareholders.

4. After the completion of this issuance, Wan Energy Group will still be the controlling shareholder of the company, and the Anhui Provincial SASAC will still be the actual controller of the company. This issuance has not resulted in a change in the actual controller of the company. Moreover, Wan Energy Group has committed not to transfer the non-publicly issued shares it subscribes to for 36 months from the date of completion of this issuance, in compliance with relevant laws, regulations, and normative documents.

5. The voting procedures for the above-mentioned related party transactions comply with the provisions of laws, administrative regulations, departmental rules, and other normative legal documents and the "Articles of Association" of the company.

6. We agree to submit the above-mentioned non-public issuance of shares-related motions to the shareholders' meeting of the company for deliberation. At the same time, we request that related parties abstain from voting when the shareholders' meeting deliberates on the related party transaction proposals.

Independent Directors: Xu Changming, Ruan Yinguo, Cheng Guangjie

February 24, 2012
