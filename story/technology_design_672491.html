5 Secrets to Quickly Upgrade Your Style

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-22 22:18:02

Secret 1: Shirt

The choice of shirt colors is quite extensive; the top picks are single tones such as white, light blue, and light gray. Bright colors like bright red or green should be avoided, and coordination with tie patterns and colors must be considered. Remember, the shirt sleeves should be 1-2 centimeters longer than the suit jacket sleeves, and the collar should be 1-1.5 centimeters higher than the suit jacket collar to show the layering of clothing.

Secret 2: Belt

In summer, belts will shine brightly on the fashion stage. This season, although there is no change in how the belt is fastened, its position has changed. You need to "equip" it outside your trench coat, jacket, cardigan, or other clothing. You can use a belt that matches the color of your clothes, or one that contrasts with the color of your clothes. In short, revealing your belt is something you need to get used to this summer.

Secret 3: Men's Bags

No matter what profession you are in, the right bag is your invisible business card. For men, having a very textured bag is the most basic equipment. In terms of bag color selection, classic black and brown should still be the main choices as they are easy to pair with anything. A special note is that if you have a fuller figure, avoid using small bags, as it will not only make you look fatter but also give people the impression of being petty. Conversely, slimmer individuals should not pair with oversized bags either. Therefore, during the selection process, carefully consider the proportion between your body type and the bag. Don't find it troublesome, nor keep saying you're in a hurry; more attempts will help you find the right choice for casual men's wear.
