Love, what exactly is it?

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-27 15:53:56

What exactly is love?

My heart started trembling, the bright sunlight was blinding. In an instant, the distance between you and me had become distant. Later, when we met again, our memories were framed in a photograph.

When excuses are no longer recognizable, and regrets cannot be remedied, what is left to compromise on? Loneliness is hard to express in words, just like the flowers of the other shore deeply rooted in the mind, they can be touched but not reached. If only I could have calmed down at the time, how would it have turned into such bitter fruit? The recklessness of youth drove me to the brink.

Her name is Xiaofeng, just like her name suggests, she loves freedom. Since my first confession failed, I didn't give up, but became more determined that she is my future.

Every night, the void in my mind was filled only with her back figure.

When I saw her again, I was focused on reading books in the library. She tapped my shoulder, "Why are you here? Do you also like reading?" I looked at her somewhat absent-mindedly, the tranquil atmosphere made my face blush.

"Yes, recently I've been feeling suffocated, so I came to buy some books to relieve the boredom."

Afterwards, we would make appointments to come together to buy books, this became our "old place." She read books with great concentration, her deep eyes revealed a kind of gentleness. And I finally understood, feelings aren't always something one can control, she was like a magnet, walking into my heart without restraint.

That street, today still brightly lit. But the old mill from yesterday is gone. This place has memories of us, that winter, a boy and a girl squatted by a small snow pile making snowmen.

And now, this street remains prosperous. After eating noodles for dinner at a small shop with Xiaofeng, she kept fidgeting with her collar. I asked her, "Are you cold?" She wasn't wearing much, in this cold city, you must dress as thick as a snowman to feel warm. I shyly held her hand, "Xiaofeng, are you cold?"

"Mm-hmm."

She didn't refuse, which strengthened my determination to pursue Xiaofeng. Under the night sky, we walked on the snowy ground, an accidental look back showed our footprints densely packed. No matter what, those are our memories.

Actually, being with Xiaofeng made me feel some fear. I clearly knew we belonged to two different worlds, our love would only hurt us more, bring us more pain.

Xiaofeng was born into wealth, her parents were officials, the family was feudal, accustomed to marrying within the same social class.

Waking up at night, my heart ached, it was the impulse of wanting to love but unable to. I don't know why I overthought it, lit a cigarette, gazing at her city. It was already 3 a.m., but this city full of wealth remained brightly lit. My village had already turned off all the lights 5 hours ago. Perhaps, this is also the distance between her and me.

Until one day, all the beauty became meaningless.

That night, returning home from the library with Xiaofeng, a car stopped beside us. The window rolled down, seeing Xiaofeng's astonished expression, "Dad, Mom... how did you come here?"

Xiaofeng's father didn't give me a good look, "So you're the little guy, just because your family owns a few acres of farmland, you think you can climb up to my daughter? Forget it in your next life!"

In an instant, everything went black before my eyes. I originally thought that as long as we loved each other and took care of each other, we could be together. I was wrong, embarrassingly wrong. I couldn't tolerate such humiliation, which led me to go south to struggle. Little did I know, this departure would last five years.

In a strange city, people come and go, constantly brushing past each other. Sitting alone in a coffee shop, taking a sip, the bitter taste spreads through every nerve in my body.

Outside the window, a man and a woman were arguing. The woman wanted to leave, the man tightly hugged her. They were a couple, ultimately a slap landed on the man's face. The woman left, perhaps she could be described as ruthless. A heavy rain began, the man stood in the rain, head bowed, a chill rose in my heart.

I was thinking, if facing love could be like drinking coffee, although the taste is bitter, but only temporary, then, what would life be like now?

Deep into the night, the rain continued, I always looked outside the window with a calm gaze.

"Closing time"

The server's words woke me up. Looking at my watch, it was already past 1 a.m. The air carried a cold sensation. Wandering alone down a small alley, there was a stray cat on the wall. Back in my rented room, merely 6 square meters, with a single bed and a chair. But who would know, in such an environment, this tiny 6-square-meter room, I lived for five years.

No one knows how I endured during this period, but when my career was successful, I prepared to return to marry Xiaofeng. What was once a beautiful dream was suddenly shattered.

A Mercedes S350 drove past my car, through the window I vaguely recognized a familiar face. The car stopped in front of her house. The door opened, a man in a suit got out of the car, turned around, looking gentlemanly as he extended his left hand towards the inside of the car. A hand reached out, under the sunlight, the ring on the ring finger shone particularly brightly. She got out of the car, her hair was black, shiny black, dressed in a white dress, white high heels, this was Xiaofeng's favorite color.

That was Xiaofeng, at this moment even breathing seemed superfluous. I don't know when, my eyes started to moisten, muttering words I couldn't even hear myself: "You said you would wait for me..."

I indulged in that state for a long time, reluctant to leave. Once upon a time, vows of eternal love, promises of till death do us part, now seem like the intrigues in Romance of the Three Kingdoms, cold and clear.

The blue rose in my hand, with a letter attached: "Xiaofeng, I haven't disappointed you, I've succeeded, after five years I've finally made it. Darling, I'm sorry for making you wait so long, now I'm back, Xiaofeng, will you marry me?" I smiled, threw it into the trash can.

Such a beautiful letter, but it could only sway in the wind.

We were merely passersby in each other's lives, we met just to miss each other, no one was the last, nor the only one.

The more I felt, the sharper the pain became, waking up to find my white shirt stained red with wine.

Two months later, I received an invitation from Xiaofeng, she was getting married. I laughed wildly looking at the invitation, such a vivid red. Turning around, I lit a cigarette, then burned the invitation.

Taking out my phone, I sent a message to Xiaofeng, "Xiaofeng, wish you happiness." Without waiting for her reply, I disassembled my phone, smiled, and threw the card, filled with our memories, into the trash can.

Once youthful dreams, like the wind, disappeared instantly.

Time played a joke on us, letting us meet at the wrong time, but separate at the right time.
