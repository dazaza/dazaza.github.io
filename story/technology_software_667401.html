When you have the right level of fitness, you can execute the necessary backswing and then release with maximum energy and power for the downswing. Consequently, a heavier club might hit the ball further than a lighter one. So, one initial change you could make is switching to a heavier club to increase your distance. By focusing solely on the follow-through, you can improve your swing speed and gain more distance on your shots.
