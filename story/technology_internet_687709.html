Regarding shampooing, it's not just about washing hair but also caring for it, which involves washing followed by conditioning, fundamentally treating the hair. Shampooing is the preparatory step before various hair services like cutting, perming, dyeing, and conditioning. It’s not only a preliminary step but also a crucial moment for customer evaluation of the hairstylist's communication skills. Shampooing service is a valuable sales auxiliary activity. Shampooing includes both washing the hair and scalp, each with different methods and purposes. Washing the scalp ensures that the scalp and hair remain undamaged.

Standard shampooing: The proper rinsing method involves using shampoo twice, rinsing twice, then applying conditioner, evenly coating it, and leaving it on for over 30 seconds before rinsing.

Dry shampoo materials required: Brushes, combs, short-bristled brushes, long-bristled brushes, detergents, dry and wet towels.
