Fierce battle in the second half of the real estate market or house price decline

by huihenet on 2011-08-11 17:13:40

Vanke's first half sales increase by nearly 80% year-on-year to reach 65.65 billion yuan

On August 9, Vanke Enterprise Co., Ltd. announced its interim report for 2011, showing that the company's sales area in the first half of the year was 5.655 million square meters, and the sales amount was 65.65 billion yuan, increasing by 76.7% and 78.6% respectively compared to the same period last year. By the end of June, the company held 40.78 billion yuan in cash.

The national market share increased by more than 40%

In the first half of the year, Vanke achieved a sales area of 5.655 million square meters and a sales amount of 65.65 billion yuan, increasing by 76.7% and 78.6% respectively compared to the same period last year. The proportion of Vanke's sales in the national commercial housing sales rose from 1.86% in the same period last year to 2.67%, an increase of 43.5%. Vanke ranked first in the commercial housing market in eight cities: Shenzhen, Dongguan, Foshan, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Wuxi, and Qingdao. In Vanke's overall sales structure, the Shenzhen region (including some projects in Huizhou and Dongguan) and the Beijing region are still Vanke's top tier, each accounting for about 30%.

Current land reserves sufficient for development over the next two years

In the first half of the year, Vanke achieved operating revenue of 19.99 billion yuan and net profit of 2.98 billion yuan, increasing by 19.2% and 5.9% year-on-year respectively. The interim report showed that by the end of June, Vanke held approximately 40.78 billion yuan in cash, far exceeding the short-term borrowings and temporary borrowings due within one year, which amounted to 23.04 billion yuan. The net debt-to-equity ratio was 20.8%, continuing to remain at a relatively low level in the industry. Holding so much money, the first is safety, and the second is to seek opportunities. At the press conference, Yu Liang, president of Vanke, admitted that in the first half of the year, Vanke intentionally acquired fewer lands and kept the cost of land acquisition at a level comparable to five years ago. Yu Liang stated that currently, Vanke has land reserves of more than 30 million square meters, which can ensure development for the next two years even if no new land is acquired starting now.

Seven more projects to be launched before the end of the year

Regarding whether Shenzhen Wanke will reduce prices to sell off inventory, Yu Liang stated that since 2008, Vanke has adopted an active pricing strategy, so there is no issue of reducing prices to sell off inventory. Tan Huajie, secretary of the board of directors of Vanke, explained that the "Wanke Group War" was due to the current lack of available properties for sale in Shenzhen, and the company hoped to promote upcoming projects through this event. An insider close to Shenzhen Vanke interpreted the "Wanke Group War" as a customer interception campaign. This insider stated that under the background of purchase restrictions, Vanke hopes to make the effective customer group willing to wait for the projects that Vanke is about to launch. It is reported that Shenzhen Vanke plans to launch Phase II of Vanke Jinse Field in Bao'an at the end of August, Phase II of Vanke Red in Buji and Pingshan Jinyu Tixiang in October, mainly targeting the Shenzhen market with Huidong Shuangyue Bay, and Phase II of Longgang Vanke Tianyu, Vanke Wuyuan Puyueshan, and Baoshan Shajing Feili Manor in November.

Executive Vice President Yuan Binyin resigns

Just when we saw the name of Executive Vice President Yuan Binyin in Vanke's interim report, we received the news that Yuan Binyin would leave Vanke to join Hongxing Meikailong as group president. This is Vanke's third senior management change this year. On the afternoon of August 9, Vanke President Yu Liang announced the resignation of Executive Vice President Yuan Binyin and stated that the media learned of this news 5 minutes earlier than Vanke employees. According to the introduction, Yuan Binyin joined Vanke in 2007 from Home Depot.

Regarding Yuan Binyin, Vanke's internal communication gave high praise. Yu Liang stated that this shows that Vanke's professional managers not only bloom inside but also outside. Liu Aiming and Yuan Binyin have joined companies with a certain scale and are preparing for listing, indicating that Chinese enterprises at this stage need management levels, and Vanke is the Whampoa Military Academy for professional managers.

China Merchants Property will continue to accelerate sales in the second half of the year

Under the continuous deepening of regulation, developers who achieved rapid growth in the first half of the year have earned good results. China Merchants Property's announcement of its semi-annual report yesterday showed that the company's land acquisition volume, start-up volume, and sales volume all increased significantly year-on-year. The start-up volume even exceeded the whole year of last year, and the overall strategy has changed from last year's observation to accelerating development and sales. The annual sales target of 20 billion yuan remains unchanged, and the company will continue to accelerate sales in the second half of the year. Said Liu Ning, Secretary of the Board of Directors of China Merchants Property.

Start-up volume exceeds the whole year of last year

In the first half of this year, China Merchants Property successively acquired land in Foshan, Wuhan, Zhenjiang, and other places. The semi-annual report shows that China Merchants Property has entered 17 cities, maintaining land reserves of more than 10 million square meters. Among them, in the first half of this year, the company newly acquired 5 projects, expanding by 1.03 million square meters. Last year, the total increment of land resources achieved by China Merchants Property was only 1.767 million square meters. The growth rate of start-up volume is even more significant. In the first half of this year, China Merchants Property started construction on 25 new projects, totaling 2.07 million square meters; currently, it has 38 ongoing projects, totaling 4.51 million square meters. Both indicators have exceeded the whole year of last year—last year, it started construction on 22 new projects, totaling 1.42 million square meters, and had 2.95 million square meters under construction at the end of the year.

It can be seen that with the continuous deepening of regulatory policies, China Merchants Property has changed from last year's observation to accelerating development this year and actively acquiring land during this adjustment period to achieve countercyclical growth.

Subscription amount reaches 12.047 billion yuan in the first half of the year

Consistently, China Merchants Property also shifted towards faster sales in the sales aspect. The report shows that in the first half of the year, China Merchants Property had 24 residential complexes for sale in 12 cities across the country, achieving subscription areas of 6.073 million square meters and subscription amounts of 12.047 billion yuan. Among them, in Shenzhen, two new projects, Guanyuan and Yongjingwan, were launched for sale, both with prices significantly lower than expected, even lower than the peak price of the same area, same developer's project sold last year. Our sales target for this year is 20 billion yuan. Although policies and market conditions are still improving, this target has not been adjusted. Said Liu Ning. Calculated by subscription amount, more than half has already been achieved.

General cities or projects may reduce prices to promote sales

Including property leasing operations, intermediaries, property management, etc., China Merchants Property achieved a total operating revenue of 8.091 billion yuan in the first half of the year, increasing by 15.90% year-on-year. This is thanks to the adjustment of strategies this year, and to achieve the growth of annual performance, sales must continue to be accelerated in the second half of the year. Relevant responsible persons in charge of marketing at China Merchants Property said that new projects will still enter the market at high prices, and price reductions to promote sales cannot be ruled out in general cities or projects. In the Shenzhen market, in addition to the phase II new products of newly launched Guanyuan and Yongjingwan, the Guling Garden in Xixiang and the Woods Apartments in Shekou will also be launched for sale.

China Overseas Land achieves record-high sales of 52.23 billion yuan in the first half of the year

Yesterday, China Overseas Land announced its interim report for the first half of the year, showing that in real estate sales, China Overseas Land achieved a record-breaking 52.23 billion Hong Kong dollars in the first half of the year, increasing by 85.9% year-on-year. At the same time, the sales area reached 3.07 million square meters, increasing by 38.2%. Among them, the real estate sales in mainland China reached 50.11 billion Hong Kong dollars, increasing by 78.5% year-on-year, and the sales area in mainland China was approximately 3.06 million square meters, increasing by 37.5% year-on-year. It was learned that in the first half of this year, China Overseas Land completed 21 projects in 16 cities in mainland China, with a total development area of approximately 1.42 million square meters and a salable area of approximately 1.13 million square meters. By the end of June, nearly 80% of the completed projects had been sold, corresponding to a sales amount of 14.39 billion Hong Kong dollars and a sales area of approximately 0.9 million square meters.

At the same time, to ensure continuous development, China Overseas Land continued to expand its land reserves through different methods. The report shows that within half a year, it added 12 new plots of land in 11 cities, with a developable area of approximately 6.775 million square meters. Adding the 0.985 million square meters of land reserves added by four new plots of land in four domestic cities by China Overseas Grand Ocean, the total new land reserves in mainland China reached 7.76 million square meters. Among them, in the first half of the year, China Overseas Land added the Nanling plot in Longgang District, Shenzhen, with a land area of 0.49 million square meters and a total floor area of 2.42 million square meters. In addition, China Overseas Land also added two new plots of land in Hong Kong. By the end of June, the group's total land reserves were approximately 36.2 million square meters.

Despite the expectation that the market environment in the second half of the year may become more complex, China Overseas Land still adheres to its original sales target, which is to achieve no less than 6.3 million square meters of sales area and 7 million square meters of completion area throughout the year. Kong Qingping, Deputy Chairman and General Manager of China Overseas Land, indicated that if the market and sales situation is good in the second half of the year, the company will consider increasing the amount of land acquisition.
