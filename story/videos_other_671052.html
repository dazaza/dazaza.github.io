Car insurance renewal through telesales is 15% cheaper than through a 4S store, and a 10% discount on the vehicle price is appropriate.

by xuntian8746 on 2012-02-21 15:08:32

As the year-end approaches, it is once again the peak season for car owners to purchase or renew their vehicle insurance. Through telephone and agent channel interviews, our reporter has compiled a guide for renewing car insurance at the end of the year:

If a vehicle had no claims in the previous year's insurance period, renewing through a 4S dealership will continue to offer a 30% discount, while direct sales channels provide an additional 15% discount on this basis.

For high-end luxury vehicles, it is recommended to renew through a 4S dealership. As the year-end approaches, Ms. Chen, who bought a new car last year, has recently received many promotional calls from various car insurance companies recommending their own insurance products. Ms. Chen told the reporter, "Some car insurance quotes sound particularly cheap and attractive, but I am worried that the claims service may not be as good as buying through a 4S dealership."

The reporter learned that many citizens are confused when faced with a variety of car insurance options purchased through different channels.

When renewing car insurance, how should the insured amount be calculated? How should the types of insurance be combined? Which ones should be purchased and which can be skipped? Where should one buy car insurance to save the most money? Our reporter specifically interviewed experts regarding these issues.

Renewal Fees:

Direct sales are 15% cheaper than 4S dealerships.

According to current regulations by various insurance companies, if a vehicle had no claims or only a few small claims during the previous year's insurance period, it generally continues to enjoy a 30% discount in the second year of renewal. Therefore, under such circumstances, renewing through a 4S dealership offers a 30% discount, while direct sales channels provide an additional 15% discount. For ordinary cars, experts suggest using direct sales channels, which are more cost-effective without any difference in claims services, such as Ping An Online Car Insurance.

For high-end luxury vehicles, insurance experts recommend renewing through a 4S dealership. High-end cars should purchase "designated specialized repair" when selecting car insurance; otherwise, insurance companies will only compensate according to the repair costs of ordinary auto repair shops, resulting in significant losses for the car owner. For some car owners with excessive claims records and large amounts, insurance companies will cancel discounts. If there are too many claims, insurance companies may even increase rates, up to a maximum of 20%.

Renewal Amount:

Decrease by 10% based on the new car price.

Regarding the previously heatedly discussed "high insurance, low compensation" car insurance cases, insurance experts remind that since the amount of insurance directly corresponds to the price of car insurance, policyholders can fully insure according to the actual price of the vehicle. Taking Ms. Liu's car as an example, the new car purchase price was 150,000 yuan. Considering depreciation factors, the second-year vehicle price can decrease by up to 25%. However, experts suggest that the price should not decrease too much, with a 10% decrease being ideal.

"Although the lower the insured amount, the cheaper the premium, the compensation you receive in case of an accident will also be proportionally reduced," said a salesperson. "The actual price of the vehicle can be referenced from the second-hand car market price. However, considering that if the vehicle is completely written off, a too-low insured amount would be unprofitable for the car owner, so a general 10% decrease is recommended."

Renewal Types:

Reduce Scratch Insurance, Add Water Immersion Insurance

Mr. Huang Guangrong, General Manager of the Brand Department of FANHUA Insurance, told the reporter that generally, first-year car owners can buy comprehensive insurance, while in the second year, they can skip scratch insurance and add water immersion insurance. Many insurance companies set very high thresholds for scratch insurance, with some explicitly stating that the car price must exceed 60,000 yuan to be eligible for scratch insurance. Some vehicles over three years old are uniformly rejected for scratch insurance.

Insurance experts remind that during the frequent rainstorm seasons in Guangzhou, the probability of vehicles being waterlogged increases. Purchasing water immersion insurance provides peace of mind. To receive compensation for a "waterlogged vehicle," the owner must have purchased both "special engine loss insurance" and "vehicle loss insurance." Both of these are supplementary insurances and are not included in the regular full coverage typically purchased when buying a car.

Tips:

1. Third-party liability insurance should be at least 300,000 yuan. For novice drivers with older cars, due to unpredictable risks to third parties, commercial third-party liability insurance must be purchased, choosing a higher insured amount, ideally at least 300,000 yuan, or even up to 500,000 yuan. While compulsory traffic insurance can partially cover third-party responsibility, it is only basic protection with limited coverage, far from sufficient.

2. Do not overlook occupant liability insurance, especially if there are children in the family. Children are easily injured in vehicles, so it is best to purchase between 20,000 to 50,000 yuan. Occupant liability insurance covers personnel in designated seats of the vehicle. It can be purchased for all seats or specific seats. The number of insured seats must be specified, and some people prefer only the front seats but forget to specify them.

3. Vehicle damage insurance is a must-buy type. When compensating for vehicle damage, compulsory traffic insurance pays 2,000 yuan, and the excess is covered by the vehicle damage insurance in commercial insurance. Compulsory traffic insurance is compensated by the other party's insurance company, while vehicle damage insurance is compensated by your own insurance company. The insured amount is determined based on the value of your vehicle.
