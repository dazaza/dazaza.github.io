Internet integrity supervision requires the wisdom of all parties

by jdhgsa078 on 2011-08-25 16:18:20

At present, the Internet industry lacks not only the constraints of laws and regulations, but also the supervision of the government and the industry association. Of course, what is missing is also the self-discipline of enterprises. Network users who have used Baidu search engine may have such an experience: they want to search for the webpage of Union Hospital, but the first few items of the search results may all be fake Union Hospital, while the real Union Hospital ranks behind. It is also possible that you want to find a reliable home appliance repair company, but the company you find according to the clues may be a fraudulent company.

Such contrary search results are all due to an open secret in the industry: bidding ranking. That is to say, when users input keywords, the search results obtained are artificially processed. Because the search engine company has already auctioned off many keywords. Who offers the highest price in the market will appear at the forefront of the search results of this keyword. The most extreme individual case is that when KFC is obviously entered, the search result at the forefront is McDonald's.

Recently, CCTV continuously reported the inside story of Baidu's bidding ranking. In the eyes of professionals in the industry, this only reveals the tip of the iceberg of the credibility problems of the Internet.

Data shows that Baidu, a representative company of Chinese search engines, has entered the top 10 global Internet companies with the highest market value. In the next two or three years, the number of Internet users in China will increase sharply. As the most promising enterprise, how should Baidu and other Internet giants fulfill their social responsibilities? Today, at a special forum specially held by the Youth Computer Science Forum of the China Computer Federation, people in the industry all believe that China's Internet urgently needs to build a new credibility environment.

The balance point between profit and integrity was broken

Studies show that the information appearing in the top 10 of the search is very important, and the attention of Internet users to the content after 10 items will quickly decline. Further data shows that the public has a 50% browsing rate for the top 10 contents, and the attention rate of the contents after 10 names will sharply decrease to less than 10%.

It is based on the characteristics of the Internet users' attention that the search engine company invented the bidding ranking profit model. The decoration market trend, the first few places of the search results can be sold, just like the advertisements of traditional media. In some foreign search engine companies, they also do bidding rankings, only divided into two halves of the search results page, the right side is the content of the bidding ranking, and the left side is the content that customers really need to search. Or, all advertisements are marked with special colors and noted as sponsored by advertisers.

The problem exposed by Baidu is that advertisements and content are mixed together without being separated, and the advertising content is ranked ahead because of the fee paid.
