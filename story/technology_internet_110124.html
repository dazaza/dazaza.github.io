How to get out of an economic crisis through the Internet?

by langmzz on 2008-12-09 21:49:29

The global "financial tsunami" triggered by the US "subprime crisis" has become increasingly serious, and is gradually evolving into a global economic crisis. Although the Internet was one of the first to be hit hard in this crisis, it is undeniable that even in an economic crisis, no platform can replace the role of the Internet. During the financial crisis, Obama successfully became the next president of the United States. A large part of his supporters came from the Internet, and Obama also successfully used the Internet platform to publish his election program. After being elected, he continued to use the Internet to announce his future governance program and some policies and measures to solve the economic crisis. The Internet has become a platform for successful communication between him and his supporters.

The reason why the Internet still has a huge market during the economic crisis is that it has entered people's lives. For example, in the face of an economic crisis, some people cut spending, changed their habit of consuming high-end products, and looked for low-end substitutes, but they still need mobile phones and cannot give up the habit of using mobile phones because of the economic crisis. The same is true for the Internet; people cannot abandon the habit of using the network because of the economic crisis. On the contrary, people who do not often use the Internet may begin to use it to save costs. For example, some people used faxes to communicate with customers in the past, but now they have switched to emails to save costs.

The above two examples are intended to illustrate that the Internet has become part of people's lives and work, and it is irreplaceable. Therefore, what people should discuss now is how to make good use of the Internet during the economic crisis, rather than talking about the "winter of the Internet".

In the past few years, the rapid development of the Internet in China undoubtedly provided Chinese small and medium-sized enterprises with a new marketing channel and development opportunity, and also cultivated a group of "net merchants" who rely on online operations in China. Through online trade, Chinese small and medium-sized enterprises have deepened their understanding of the network and global trade, allowing the network to open up innovative ideas for enterprises. The world has come to know China more closely through the network and has seen China's boundless development potential. The network not only connects the global ocean, but also continuously delivers the abundant water source of "Made in China" to the world. However, in recent months, due to the impact of the financial crisis, many enterprises, especially export-oriented enterprises, have been greatly affected. According to reports, data from Chinese customs show that in November, China's export trade experienced negative growth for the first time. Behind this news, the phenomenon is that a considerable number of foreign trade enterprises have either gone bankrupt or switched to domestic sales in the past few months. Regardless of whether it is for export or domestic sales, for most enterprises, sales channels have become the biggest problem. However, enterprises that have always used the Internet platform for sales are obviously doing better than those that do not use this platform well.

Therefore, despite the current impact of the economic crisis on the Internet, Internet operators as sales platforms still have "a place for heroes to show their prowess." With the continuous maturity of the network market and the protection of relevant laws and regulations, the trust in network marketing in China is gradually increasing. Especially some vivid cases where small and medium-sized enterprises have obtained significant returns through low-cost network marketing have fundamentally magnified the charm of network marketing. As a result, many small and medium-sized enterprises believe that as long as the enterprise adds a few computers, rents a website space, or simply opens a store on some B2C websites, it can become a network merchant and open up a new channel for products.

At present, under the influence of the economic crisis, many enterprises, especially small enterprises including Internet companies, if they want to safely get through the winter, the most direct and effective way to profit is still through the Internet. The responsibility of the network industry is to develop more effective and targeted commercial platforms for enterprises.
