The report exclusively released by the National Information Center on the China Securities News on December 3 believed that, next year, the growth rate of China's GDP is expected to reach about 8.5%, basically the same as in 2009. The economic growth and price are expected to achieve a "double stability" operation trend. The possibility of obvious inflation in the short term is not great. However, it is not advisable to further increase the strength of expansionary policies in the future. Analysts believe that there was not too much novelty in the whole report, which was basically within everyone's expectations. The tendency of the policy will not change and will be fine-tuned for anti-inflation. The key point is whether the growth rate of M2 can be controlled at around 18%. This year, the growth of M2 is expected to reach 30%. On such a high basis, continuing with so much money supply will still make the liquidity in society loose, benefiting the stock market and real estate market, stock forum. Due to the factor of the suspension of new share issuance in the first half of this year, it is expected that enterprises' financing scale from the securities market in 2010 will be greater than this year. Judging from the recent rapid rise in issuance speed, the new share issuance speed tomorrow will not be slow either. The capital pressure on the market will be greater than this year. After offsetting each other, the difficulty of achieving such a high increase as this year in the 2010 market has increased. With the increasing uncertainty factors in the market next year, the operational difficulty will further increase. Large-cap blue chips performed weaker than small- and medium-sized stocks. Sinopec fell by 1.55%, PetroChina fell by 0.66%, and Ping An Insurance fell by 1.82%. The initial picture of Chinese shareholders of Shanghai Disneyland has taken shape. Lujiazui Group holds 60% of shares, and the Disneyland concept has been reactivated. Lujiazui rose by 5.73%. LME base metals touched the 2009 highs on Wednesday, and the non-ferrous metal sector continued to rise in the morning session. Zhongfu Industry rose by 4.73%. The general manager of the Shenzhen Stock Exchange said that margin trading and short selling were ready, and the broker stocks performed well in the morning session. Haitong Securities rose by 3.11%. The ChiNext continued yesterday's big rise, among which Dayu Water Conservation hit the daily limit up. Agricultural stocks continued their recent strong performance and rose against the trend today, and Denghai Seeds hit the daily limit up. In addition, shipbuilding, media entertainment, and instrument manufacturing sectors ranked at the top in terms of gains, while aircraft manufacturing, real estate, and home appliance sectors were relatively weak. Institutions believe that after three consecutive trading days of sharp rises, although there is a possibility of wide-range fluctuations in the short term, under the impetus of improving economic data and positive policy support, the upward trend of the index in the medium term will continue. It was reported that the Central Economic Work Conference would be held this weekend. Therefore, moderate fine-tuning and structural adjustment at the operational level might have a significant impact on the A-share market.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Moreover, this commission is only valid on the day the commission is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, quantity, and the price of buying or selling stocks. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. When entrusting to buy or sell, the code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of buying and selling stocks: That is, the quantity of entrusting to buy stocks must be an integral multiple of 100, but the quantity of entrusting to sell stocks does not have to be an integral multiple of 100. There are four ways to commission: counter submission commission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
