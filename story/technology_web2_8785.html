Many members of the Pineapple team come from Microsoft. At the beginning of Pineapple's establishment, its founder Gu Shaofeng decided to integrate the advantages of Microsoft and Google to form Pineapple's own corporate culture. Similar to Google, every employee at Pineapple has 20% of their time to manage themselves. Anyone with new ideas can use their free time to make it happen and then release it internally for everyone to use. If it gets recognized by everyone, they will release it on the internet, a larger laboratory, for a wider range of users to use. If it again gets recognized by users, Pineapple will turn it into a product. That's why they have the Pineapple Dream Factory, where there are new things and good ideas from Pineapple...
