The introduction of the plot of "Dating My Ex-Wife" narrates the story of Ma Yong (played by Geng Le) and Yu Xiaohong (played by Song Jia), a newly divorced couple. The shadow of divorce has not yet completely faded, so when they meet, they can't help but retain some of the small quarrels and mutual criticism from their marriage. But at the same time, there are also some subtle cares that only exist between couples. In order to let the past life become history as soon as possible, they both strive to start new emotional lives. However, the more they try to eliminate each other, the closer their lives become. After divorcing, Ma Yong and Yu Xiaohong treat each other with generosity, restraint, and care as ordinary friends. After experiencing some things, they realize that this way of getting along is exactly what they gradually lost during their seven-year marriage. Marriage has extinguished their passion and tested their patience, while divorce has instead repaired their flaws. During the days after divorce, they truly understand what marriage is and how to coexist harmoniously. Their love in their hearts has not been eroded by time but has instead deepened. However, what should be done about the new relationships that have already begun? If the two were to re-enter marriage, would they no longer make the same mistakes as before? What kind of marriage do we need? How should we treat the person we love? Through unique plot designs and extremely lifelike and hilarious lines, "Dating My Ex-Wife" makes the audience reconsider the content of marriage after laughing heartily, realizing the responsibilities of both men and women in romantic and marital relationships. Anyone who has loved will sincerely sigh after watching this drama, which is like a marriage and love psychological counselor answering questions about issues in marriage. This is an extremely unique love story. Ma Yong is a daily newspaper reporter, and Yu Xiaohong is an evening newspaper reporter.
