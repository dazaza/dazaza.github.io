The ancients used "colorful clouds appear" to refer to Yunnan, this mysterious cloud ridge plateau. Let's not talk about the picturesque scenery, on this plateau, there are 26 different ethnic groups living here, you can imagine how interesting the local folk culture is. Each ethnic group in Yunnan has unique habits in clothing, food, housing, transportation, marriage, funeral, birth, festivals, etiquette, language, writing, totems, religion, taboos, aesthetics, etc., with distinct personalities. Among them, the Dongba culture of the Naxi nationality, the Bai culture of Dali, the Beijye culture of the Dai nationality, and the Bema culture of the Yi nationality are the most famous.
