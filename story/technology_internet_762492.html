Agent reveals: how does 360 compete with Baidu?

by anonymous on 2013-07-24 15:46:22

After causing a stir online, 360 Search ultimately still needs to focus on monetization. Thus, establishing a search marketing system has become one of the main tasks for 360 this year. Under the immense pressure from Baidu's nationwide channel network, how is 360, as a latecomer, able to penetrate the market?

The reporter interviewed some local agents of 360 Search to uncover the expansion situation of 360's search marketing system. We learned that 360's rapidly growing market share made the agents smell an opportunity to make money. Additionally, Baidu's tightening of agency rights in recent years indirectly contributed to the agents switching sides. The number of 360's agents and order volume have both seen significant increases compared to the beginning of the year.

Specific details are as follows:

1. In fact, the agency rights for 360 Search are highly sought after by the agents. So far, 360 Search agents cover 16 provinces and cities, totaling 27 agencies. Four months ago, this number was only 13. Moreover, many local agents have already started developing sub-agents. The agency rights for 360 Search are quite popular locally. "Just like during Baidu's early development phase, many agents back then gained considerable benefits. Therefore, when 360 entered the search market this time, they were all eager to secure the agency as soon as possible," said an agent from Henan Province who told Sina IT that they obtained the 360 agency rights last month.

2. The development of 360 agents also benefited from the gap formed by Baidu reclaiming its agency rights. The aforementioned Henan agent mentioned, "After so many years of development, Baidu has reclaimed many local agency rights and set up branches or provincial chief agents." 360 has not established branches in various places yet and still mainly relies on agents. Among these agents, some have previously handled Baidu advertisements, others have handled Sogou or Sou advertisements, and even some have handled 3721 advertisements. However, currently, they have shifted their focus to 360, with most agents establishing independent companies specifically for handling 360 advertisements.

3. Low prices are an important advantage of 360 Search advertisements in local markets. An agent from Wuhan believed that the current advantages of 360 Search advertisements are: less competition, lower prices, and higher conversion rates. "Some keyword advertisement prices for 360 Search are less than one-fifth of Baidu's," he said. With the reputation of being the "second largest search engine" and the market education provided by Baidu earlier, the agent claimed that promotion is not too difficult. However, many customers are still observing, as they want to see if 360 is truly willing to focus on improving its search engine.

4. In just half a year, the transaction volume of 360 Search advertisement orders has grown rapidly. In March, we reported that 360 Search had about 1,000 transactions in Beijing over three months. A salesperson from Tiandizai Online, one of the Beijing area agents for 360 Search, told us that now they can exceed 600 orders per month alone. There are two agents for 360 Search in Beijing, one being Tiandizai Online and the other Jutao Culture, which entered later and is relatively smaller in scale. Henan Sanbai Liu, which just secured the agency rights last month, has already completed more than 1,000 transactions.

5. The average customer order price is around ten thousand yuan, which is not low. The minimum opening fee for 360 Search advertisements is 4,600 yuan, including a 600 yuan service fee and a 4,000 yuan pre-deposit for ad placement. The Wuhan agent revealed that the current average order price is approximately ten thousand yuan. For comparison, Baidu's average customer revenue last quarter was 14,500 yuan.

6. 360 Search advertisements have covered all industries. Currently, 360 Search advertisements have covered all industries except healthcare. Recently, they also opened up the previously restricted health supplement and adult products industries. Notably, 360 currently only accepts corporate clients and does not accept individual clients, and each company can only place ads for one site (Baidu accepts individual clients). Multiple agents mentioned that they are developing offline clients in industries such as education and consulting.

But can 360 threaten Baidu? No.

Although 360's agent system has initially taken shape, its competition with Baidu is still at a very preliminary stage. When talking to a Baidu agent, he frankly stated, "Currently, 360 does not pose a threat to Baidu."

From the feedback of various 360 agents, the current competition is not yet intense. "360 Search is currently giving customers a new market and new choices," one agent said. Many customers will place ads on both platforms.

However, this is a phased situation. 360's search volume is still small and requires more subsequent investment. Nonetheless, the monetization of search advertisements will undoubtedly be the next most imaginative income growth point for 360. When 360's client base truly grows and prices rise, there may inevitably be another direct confrontation between 360 and Baidu.
