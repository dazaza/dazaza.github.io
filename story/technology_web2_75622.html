Web 2.0 Mini Review (7) - Video Sharing (Podcast) Category | Net Walker's Journal

by cpxxpc on 2008-06-26 09:04:17

Six Rooms [Brief Review]: A well-known domestic video sharing website. [Submission]: Online upload, Super Upload (supports batch upload, video clipping, online recording, requires plugin support), single file 100M, supports common formats, does not support swf animation format, also supports mobile uploads. [Management]: Provides short domain personal homepage (supports skin change and page style settings, skin change requires reaching a certain level, level explanation), user center management functions are complete, supports albums (provides joint broadcasting, external link codes, etc.), groups, friends, subscriptions, collections, on-demand lists (supports joint broadcasting) and other functions. [Characteristics]: Fast upload and playback speed, high traffic; a dedicated Phoenix Satellite TV section for playing Phoenix Broadband programs; a million fund plan encouraging original content.

Tudou.com [Brief Review]: A well-known domestic video sharing website, slogan - Everyone is the director of life. [Submission]: Online upload, client-side upload (iTudou, supports batch upload, resume interrupted uploads), single file 100M, supports common formats, does not support swf animation format. [Management]: Provides personalized video blog homepages with custom domains (supports page style settings, log posts associated with videos), user center management functions are complete, supports Tudou Lists (i.e., albums, provides custom addresses, joint broadcasting, external link codes, etc.), tags (distinctive function), groups, friends, subscriptions, collections, convenient Tudou Lists (on-demand lists, supports joint broadcasting, adding or creating new Tudou Lists) and other functions. [Characteristics]: Video albums (local upload or network capture); video download (requires client support); speed-up tool (Superfast Tudou, requires installation); tags (a more flexible way to gather videos outside of albums, supports short addresses and self-editing); after becoming a certified user (Doujiao), single file upload increases to 200M; Digg function provided on the homepage and play pages (Digging Potatoes). [Shortcomings]: In some network environments, uploading and playback can be unstable, submitting group videos is inconvenient.

Wole [Brief Review]: A relatively well-known domestic video sharing website, claimed to be - the largest free video sharing platform in the country. [Submission]: Online upload, Super Upload (supports batch upload, resume interrupted uploads, network video upload, requires plugin support), online recording, single file 200M, supports common formats, does not support swf animation format. [Management]: Personal space 15G (can be expanded using points, point rules), provides short domain personal homepage (supports changing and customizing templates), user center management functions are complete and centralized, supports albums (provides joint broadcasting, external links, etc.), Tieba (groups), friends, subscriptions, collections, and other functions. [Characteristics]: Video albums (local upload or network capture, plugins can be used for batch upload); network video upload (i.e., online capture of videos from other podcast websites); rich personal homepage templates; Digg function provided on the play page.

Youku.com [Brief Review]: A relatively well-known domestic video sharing website, claimed to be - China's No.1 video website. [Submission]: Online upload, single file 200M, supports common formats, does not support swf animation format. [Management]: Complete and organized user center (My Youpan) management functions, allows custom module addition, supports albums (provides joint broadcasting), clubs (groups), friends, subscriptions, collections, on-demand lists (supports joint broadcasting, joining or creating albums). [Characteristics]: PK Arena (can independently initiate, join, participate in video PK activities); Digg function provided on video channels and play pages. [Shortcomings]: Single video upload method, does not support client-side or online recording and other common functions.

Ku6.com [Brief Review]: A new and sharp video sharing website, claimed to be - the first video sharing portal. [Submission]: Online upload (can choose to participate in multiple activities), single file M, supports common formats, does not support swf animation format. [Management]: Provides short domain personal video blog homepage (supports module and layout settings, template changes, video-related log posts, friendly links, music playback, etc.), user center management functions are complete and centralized, supports albums (provides joint broadcasting, external link codes, etc.), classification (for easier management), friends, subscriptions, collections, and other functions. [Characteristics]: Audio upload (supports mp3, wma formats), image upload, video albums (local upload or network capture); user center adopts tabbed browsing, neat and clear; V History video magazine; unique revenue management that ties video display to user income (original videos earn 0.01 yuan per ad display, re-posted videos earn 0.0010 yuan per ad display; during the site anniversary celebration period, registering and verifying will grant 6 yuan in earnings). [Shortcomings]: Single video upload method, does not support client-side or online recording and other common functions; video albums and image uploads have not been organically integrated.
