I'm a bit diligent.

by vvqfeifeik on 2011-09-22 16:28:43

The little guy has been giving the old man a hard time. Apart from cooking, the father has taken on washing clothes and dishes all by himself, as he doesn't want me to be tired and won't let me help. He also says that while he is still able to move, he wants to help me more... This afternoon, the child called me to report that grandpa forgot to turn off the liquefied gas at noon, and it kept burning for more than 4 hours until he discovered it when school was out, almost causing an accident...

Sigh, I must make up my mind to hire a nanny, no matter how expensive it is. We have been discussing this topic every day when we come home, but there are always two obstacles: too expensive! After the Spring Festival, the cost of hiring a nanny skyrocketed, so should we hire one or not? We have been hesitating.

Yes, I am a bit lazy and don't know what to write about.

During these months of interning at the law firm, life has changed like a dream. Leaving the familiar environment, I go out early and come back late, green trees and birds' chirping, ponds and fields are no longer around me. The texts I face every day are no longer simple... Especially regarding the child and father, leaving the young and the old behind feels a bit like letting them fend for themselves.

Reflecting deeply, how do I feel now? Father has already given me more than enough, I need to fulfill my filial duty and let father enjoy family happiness...

This morning, I met an uncle upstairs who is two years younger than my father. After a few days of not seeing him, he suddenly seemed much older, with unsteady steps. Seeing father every day, does he also appear... old in other people's eyes?

On the way home, I suddenly remembered the saying "the tree wants to be still but the wind won't stop; the child wants to support the parents but they're no longer waiting," and my heart felt sour. After getting off the car, I ran to get boxed meals and then went to the playground to fetch the child back. The child was making a fire in the rice field beside the playground. Winter branches broken by heavy snow were piled up by the field, and three children had their faces and noses full of ash and sweat. After returning home, father stopped his writing and took the child to wash. Later, during dinner, father fell asleep in his chair...

Expensive or not, hire, definitely hire.
