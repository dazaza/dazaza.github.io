To prevent abnormal leukorrhea, I have a trick.

by jydw2010 on 2012-01-28 11:34:16

Abnormal leucorrhea is divided into physiological leucorrhea and pathological leucorrhea. Pathological leucorrhea is mostly caused by inflammation. Common pathological leucorrhea seen in clinical practice includes: colorless and transparent sticky leucorrhea; white or gray-yellow foamy leucorrhea; curd-like leucorrhea; watery leucorrhea, etc.

Abnormal leucorrhea is a sign of various diseases, such as homogeneous gray-white fishy leucorrhea indicating bacterial vaginitis, transparent sticky leucorrhea indicating chronic cervicalitis, ovarian dysfunction, etc., therefore, female friends should actively go to the hospital for treatment while preventing abnormal leucorrhea, and corresponding preventive work should be done. Let's see how the Gynecology Department of Xi'an Sanqin Hospital gives advice!

1. Partner's active treatment of phimosis: Patients with phimosis should undergo circumcision as soon as possible. Although men with long foreskin do not necessarily need this surgery, they should at least clean it thoroughly before intercourse.

2. Pay attention to sexual hygiene: Both male and female partners should wash their genitals every day, paying more attention to cleaning all areas that may come into contact during sex before intercourse. In addition, it is necessary to avoid sexual intercourse during menstruation.

3. Women should pay attention to personal hygiene: Women should wash themselves before sex, urinate and wash again after sex. In addition, women should pay attention to menstrual hygiene, washing the vulva daily with warm water and changing underwear frequently. They should also use separate towels and basins for washing feet.

4. Try to use squat toilets, if sit toilets must be used, then use toilet paper padding, wipe handkerchiefs from front to back, don't be stingy with sanitary napkins, etc., which can prevent abnormal leucorrhea.

5. Pay attention to diet: In terms of diet, eat less spicy and greasy cold food, and eat more spleen-benefiting kidney-nourishing and heat-clearing dampness-removing foods, such as lotus seeds, red dates, yam, coix seed, winter melon seeds, etc. Avoid eating some cooler foods in daily diet, such as Chinese cabbage, green tea, radish, melon, tangerine, etc.

6. Pay attention to physical exercise: Do not overwork or stay up late, pay attention to physical exercise, and enhance resistance.
