Zhang Ziyi's boyfriend admits that their wedding in August is off. Has their relationship changed?

by kaxiya on 2009-07-15 14:47:51

Zhang Ziyi and her boyfriend have never shied away from displaying their affection in public, and rumors of their impending marriage have been circulating repeatedly. It seems that the date of her wedding remains uncertain.

The 30-year-old Zhang Ziyi and Israeli billionaire Vivi Nevo have consistently shown their love for each other. Last year, when they attended the annual media conference of an investment bank held in Sun Valley, USA, they mentioned their engagement. Recently, when Vivi attended the same conference alone, he revealed that their planned wedding on a private Italian island next month had been unfortunately postponed due to their busy schedules.

Planning a wedding on an Italian island

Zhang Ziyi and Vivi have been dating for over two years with a good relationship, and they don't hesitate to talk about their private lives with the media. Last year, Vivi mentioned planning to get married this year. This year, Vivi attended the meeting as the chairman of NV Investments Inc., while Zhang Ziyi was busy promoting her new film "Very Perfect" and couldn't accompany him. When asked about their wedding plans, Vivi disclosed that their August wedding would be indefinitely postponed.

A friend of Vivi also revealed to American media: "They were planning to hold the ceremony on a friend's private island in Italy in August, but towards the end of last year, they realized their schedules wouldn't allow it, so they had to choose another date. But they still haven't decided." Reports also indicated that both have a special attachment to Italy, and Vivi even sent people to scout various Italian islands for a suitable location for the wedding.

Is their relationship changing?

In fact, both Zhang Ziyi and her boyfriend are very busy individuals, which is why their wedding date has been changed multiple times. Recently, Zhang Ziyi has been busy promoting her new movie "Very Perfect," set to premiere next month in mainland China. Earlier, she was filming a jewelry commercial in New Zealand. Vivi's business empire continues to grow, and his busy schedule matches that of his girlfriend. However, during a recent interview with the Chinese version of "Elle," Zhang Ziyi seemed hesitant about marriage. She expressed her lifelong desire to start a family, possibly influenced by her boarding school experience as a child. She openly admitted her aspirations to marry and have children but also confessed her fear of marriage, saying, "What if I make a bad choice? Once I get married, like my parents, it’s for life because everyone around me has a happy marriage."

Zhang Ziyi also believes that in any relationship, especially for women, one is willing to give a lot and change one's lifestyle and personality for the other person. If one realizes that the relationship isn't worth these changes, doubts arise. While being willing to go through fire and water for love, maintaining a long-term relationship proves difficult. These words, combined with the news of the postponed wedding, suggest a possible change in their relationship. We will see whether her fiancé attends her movie premiere in Beijing next month to determine if it's a change in wedding plans or in their feelings.
