Small car was illegally parked 126 times in two years, the owner was fined more than ten thousand yuan but showed no repentance

by qiyouchax64 on 2011-05-15 08:16:40

The traffic police records show that the Roewe car with the license plate number of Min DAN978 has parked illegally multiple times since 2008. Up to now, it has done so 126 times. Strangely, even after paying more than ten thousand yuan in fines, the car owner shows no sign of repentance and continues to park illegally on the same road section.

A. Security Guard: Even when there are parking spaces available, it is rarely seen parking in them.

B. Female Car Owner: Often can't find a parking space when returning.

"Even if she can't find a parking space when she returns from work, she could park further away and then drive the car back to a parking space near the neighborhood at night," said the security guard. The most common place for this car to park illegally is usually near bus stops, which obstructs the buses' ability to stop and pick up/drop off passengers.

C. The person paying the fines for the illegal parking does not know the car owner?

Records show that on July 28th last year, someone paid 3,200 yuan in fines for 31 instances of illegal parking for this car. Then on April 21st this year, just before the car was due for its annual inspection, another payment of 8,020 yuan was made for 65 instances of illegal parking. However, from the end of April to early August, this car has accumulated another 30 records of illegal parking near Huaqiao Haijingcheng, resulting in fines totaling 4,050 yuan, which have yet to be paid.

D. Has money to pay ten-thousand-yuan fines

But lacks public morality

"Showing contempt for regulations and repeatedly being penalized without making any corrections," said the police officer. Since the fines have been paid, it is clear that the car owner knows about the multiple violations in this area, but still doesn't correct their behavior after handling the tickets. In just over three months, they've quickly accumulated 30 more violation records. It's really unclear what the car owner is thinking?

The police officer said that this car owner can afford to pay more than ten thousand yuan in fines, indicating that their financial situation is fine, but they should pay more attention to public morality and not inconvenience others for their own convenience, especially when it comes to obstructing the smooth flow of traffic. Moreover, the location where the car frequently parks illegally also has a bus stop nearby.

The police stated that once this car parks illegally near a bus stop again, they will dispatch a tow truck to temporarily impound the car. When the car owner comes to retrieve the car, they will receive severe education.
