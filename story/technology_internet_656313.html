Taobao, which we are familiar with, has attracted most online shopping users.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-01-07 09:08:54

In the past, e-commerce websites have always pursued speed and scale. With the development of circumstances, they should also pay more attention to the operational capabilities of the website. The lessons learned from previous large group-buying websites such as 24quan and Lashou tell us that ignoring the operational capabilities of the website and only focusing on its speed and scale will not lead to success.

Nowadays, spending time and effort on how to retain users is more important than focusing on repeat purchase rates, product unit prices, and profit conversion rates. Most e-commerce websites spend large amounts of money buying traffic and use small profits or minor advantages to attract users in order to retain more users and turn them into loyal customers. However, any e-commerce website that has truly implemented these methods should know that after a period of implementation, the number of users who are truly retained by these methods is minimal. Most users disappear after obtaining these small benefits. So, what is the reason for this? The following points are crucial.

Take Taobao, which we are all familiar with; it attracts most online shopping users. Why does everyone focus on Taobao? Why do people like to "browse" around on Taobao? The most important reason lies in the diversity of Taobao's products and the vast number of stores on the platform. It has millions of products and countless large and small shops. There are different activities and discounts every day. Therefore, for online shoppers, Taobao is the biggest attraction. Only by checking it daily can users stay updated with the latest activities and take advantage of the best offers without missing any opportunities.

Firstly, make online shopping a habit for users.

Operational capability cannot be improved overnight; it is a process that requires long-term accumulation. This includes setting reasonable unit prices for products, improving product conversion rates, and enhancing user loyalty, all of which require meticulous management, continuous summarization, and practice. In the past year, why did most group-buying websites suffer significant losses while Ctrip could make a profit of 3 billion yuan within a year? The most critical reason is meticulous management. Ctrip adopts a first-class assembly line model, continuously improving service standards. How to awaken the hibernation of e-commerce? Are you ready? Do a good job retaining users and become the king of e-commerce!

The degree of user retention is one of the cores of successfully operating an e-commerce website, directly reflecting the popularity and attractiveness of the website. The readability of content and the aesthetics of the website are all key factors in attracting users. The time users spend browsing pages and the number of clicks are parameters for evaluating the level of user retention on a website.

We all know that online shopping has become a trend, and almost every user talks endlessly about it when it comes up. Many users, regardless of whether they're buying small items like rice and oil or big-ticket items like cars and villas, consider purchasing them through e-commerce channels. Although these items can be bought online, online shopping still only occupies a small part of users' lives and hasn't become as indispensable as eating, working, or sleeping. Therefore, what e-commerce websites need to do is figure out how to keep users engaged and make online shopping an essential part of their lives.
