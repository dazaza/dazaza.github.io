Will the big stock Luxin Hi-tech (600783) surge to 40 yuan?

by xxywing on 2009-02-11 16:24:20

On February 10, Lushxin Hi-tech reached a high of 19.99 yuan, setting a historical record. However, it closed on that day with a medium bearish line accompanied by long upper and lower shadows. Today, it experienced another wide-ranging fluctuation, dropping by 3.02%, and closed at 18.28 yuan. These two days have both ended in decline. Has this stock come to an end at 19.99 yuan? Or, as some stock friends say, will it surge to 40 yuan?

From the comments left by netizens on various stock forums, it was found that most netizens believe they should continue holding their shares without any action, while a smaller group thinks it's currently in a stage of dumping after a sharp rise – good while it lasts. In one post titled "Heartfelt: I'm Crazy for Lushxin," it was written: "Hold or keep buying! Wait for the gap-up opening and limit-up; second, the direct limit-up phase; third, after consecutive limit-ups with heavy volume, that’s when you can sell." An interesting excerpt read: "Stock friends who get off the train, please walk well. We bid you farewell with a 10% rise (applause)! Please scold me more when you regret it in the future because I really couldn't stop you from leaving. I'll bless you at the height of 40.00!" It seems that most netizens remain optimistic about this stock. To some extent, it is a kind of hope for investors, as those holding shares always wish for the stock to only rise and never fall. As for whether Lushxin Hi-tech will reach 40 yuan as the stock friends said or stop here, we might as well take a look at the real-time trading operations of investors and compare their trading strategies. This may prevent us from being overly extreme (optimistic or pessimistic).

This investor comes from the Super Fund Real-Disc Trading Competition. His nickname is "Handsome Brother." He opted for taking profits when they were good, starting to invest in Lushxin Hi-tech on January 20, 2009. The purchase cost was approximately 11.12 yuan per share, which was precisely at the beginning of a rapid rise, showing excellent timing in building his position. However, if considering the over 220% increase from the lowest price of 3.47 yuan in November 2008 to January 20, purchasing at that time indeed required great courage and technical expertise. During this period, he engaged in short-term trading, selling part of his holdings of 4600 shares on January 21 and February 2, respectively, making about a 15% profit. Subsequently, he bought more shares on February 4, increasing his holdings to 9000 shares at an average price of 12.61 yuan. On February 11, he sold all his shares at the closing price of 18.85 yuan, realizing a 50% profit. By February 11, his total return had reached 77.57%, with FAW Car (600166) also contributing significantly to his gains.

With the recent continuous rally in A-shares, a pullback could happen anytime. Perhaps investors need to stay calm since the stock market cannot rise indefinitely. Maybe "Handsome Brother's" decision to sell today was wise.
