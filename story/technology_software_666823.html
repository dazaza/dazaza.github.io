San Siro stadium reacts with disappointment to Inter Milan's loss! The championship already belongs to Milan.

by lz3771hk on 2012-02-16 10:36:42

The first time was when the game reached 36 minutes in the first half, at that time AC Milan was still passing the ball in their own half. The San Siro Stadium erupted with enthusiastic cheers. At this moment, the TV cameras focused on the big screen, and the score announcement read: Parma 1-0 Inter Milan! At this point, all the fans stood up, cheering and jumping. AC Milan was already leading 1-0 at the time, which meant that if the scores remained unchanged, AC Milan would be 8 points ahead of Inter Milan. Currently, AC Milan's championship prospects are widely favored. Regarding AC Milan's chances of winning the title, former Inter Milan star and commentator Ferrri said: "I don't think Napoli will put pressure on AC Milan. I believe AC Milan will ultimately come out on top." Former AC Milan goalkeeper Dida then expressed: "I admire Leonardo, but the final champion will belong to AC Milan. From this season, Inter Milan won't be as unlucky anymore. This will be AC Milan's season because they are the strongest team. Every time I see my former teammates on TV, I cheer for them."

The applause at the 82nd minute of the match was even more enthusiastic. At that time, the big screen showed the news that Parma was leading Inter Milan 2-0, while AC Milan's score was already 3-0. Considering the little remaining time in the game, it also meant that the 8-point gap became a reality. After the applause, the fans of San Siro started making human waves voluntarily. Captain Gattuso also ran to the sidelines to stir up the emotions of the fans. In the end, both games ended with these two scores. With five rounds left in Serie A, AC Milan is leading Inter Milan by 8 points!

From now on, AC Milan can indeed forget about Inter Milan and consider Napoli more. In one less round of matches, Napoli is only 6.5 points behind AC Milan (in the case of equal points in this season's Serie A, the ranking is decided by head-to-head results, and AC Milan has double-killed Napoli this season). However, considering that Napoli will face Udinese, Inter Milan at home, and Juventus away in the last seven rounds, they are truly facing a "devilish schedule". From the current situation, AC Milan's championship prospects are really bright.

AC Milan and Inter Milan's games simultaneously started in the evening, a situation not often seen. Also due to this reason, the large screen at San Siro underwent some changes this time, displaying the live score of Inter Milan's away game against Parma. The two times the entire San Siro cheered louder than the home team's goals were also because of this.
