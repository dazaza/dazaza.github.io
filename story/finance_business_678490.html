Not even instant noodles can be eaten with peace of mind - 4 brands of instant noodles contain plasticizers.

by uefbp54187 on 2012-02-27 14:48:53

Recently, according to reports by Hong Kong Cable TV, the television station found after sending for inspection that the seasoning powder and sauce of 4 brands of instant noodles contained plasticizers. These four brands of instant noodles are "Yu Pin Huang Sheng Mian Xiang Nong Niu Rou Wei" (Yu Pin Huang Fresh Noodles with Rich Beef Flavor), "Da Jiang Chao Mian Ri Shi Shao Zhu Wei" (General's Fried Noodles with Japanese Barbecue Sauce) produced by Nissin, "Xin Ramyeon Extra Spicy Mushroom Flavor" produced by Korea's Nongshim in Shanghai, and Jin Fen brand "He Fen Wang Yuan Zhi Niu Lan Wei" (Rice Noodle King Original Beef Flavor).

The 21st Century Business Herald learned that Carrefour, Lotus and other hypermarkets have already notified their stores yesterday (June 13) to urgently take the problematic products off the shelves, and will make further handling after receiving the national certification test report from suppliers. According to Hong Kong media reports, Wellcome Supermarket, the exclusive agent for "Yu Pin Huang Beef-flavored Fresh Noodles", has completely taken the problematic instant noodles off the shelves.

The reporter noticed that according to the test results published by Hong Kong Food Safety Department on June 8, the noodles and seasoning powder of the product produced by Shanghai Nongshim did not detect DEHP, DINP, DBP. On June 12, Korea's Nongshim Xin Ramyeon released a statement via Sina Weibo: "Official test results from the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region government show that this product does not contain plasticizer components. For any inaccurate media reports and any irresponsible malicious defamation, Nongshim will uphold its rights through legal procedures."

As for whether "Yu Pin Huang Beef-flavored Fresh Noodles" is sold in mainland China, the reporter called the company located in Hong Kong area and sent an interview outline yesterday, but as of press time, no response was received from the company. Which company produces the exposed Jin Fen brand "He Fen Wang Yuan Zhi Niu Lan Wei"? The reporter discovered this product on the official website of a company named Lechu Food (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. The reporter called the company yesterday, but the phone of the relevant department was unanswered all the time.

"We have already made the decision to immediately take this product off the shelves, and our supermarket does not sell the other three products," said a related person in charge of Carrefour yesterday. Lotus also stated that it has already notified its stores yesterday to conduct self-inspection, and once these four products are found, they will be taken off the shelves immediately. The reporter did not find "Yu Pin Huang Beef-flavored Fresh Noodles" and Jin Fen brand "He Fen Wang Yuan Zhi Niu Lan Wei" in several supermarkets in Shanghai yesterday.

Nissin Foods (Hong Kong) Company Limited issued a statement on June 10: "Nissin Foods (Hong Kong) confirms that it has not used raw materials that violate the standards of Hong Kong's Food and Environmental Hygiene Department... In order to ensure consumer safety and peace of mind, the company has started safety analysis work." A person in charge of the sales promotion department of Guangdong Shunde Nissin Foods Co., Ltd. said in an interview with the 21st Century Business Herald yesterday, "The 'General's Fried Noodles with Japanese Barbecue Sauce' is produced by Guangdong Nissin under the commission of Nissin Foods (Hong Kong), but this product is not sold in the mainland."

A person in charge of the marketing department of Shanghai Nissin Foods Co., Ltd. said that relevant departments in the Hong Kong region did not require withdrawal or recall, and the product is still on sale in the Hong Kong region.

A related person in the marketing department of Shanghai Nongshim Foods Co., Ltd. said: The report said that DEHP was detected in the sauce of "Xin Ramyeon Extra Spicy Mushroom Flavor" instant noodles, but our product does not have a sauce pack, only a seasoning powder pack and dehydrated vegetable pack.
