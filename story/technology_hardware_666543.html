Introduction to Hong'en Classic Little Traditional Chinese Learning: "Three-Character Classic" The first Chinese animated full-process interpretation of traditional Chinese classics! The "Three-Character Classic," "Hundred Family Surnames," and "Thousand Character Classic" are excellent children's primers from ancient China. They embody thousands of years of Chinese civilization and traditional culture, reflecting the profound cultural essence of the Chinese nation; their writing is smooth, majestic, and rich in vocabulary, with coherent sentences that are easy to read aloud. They are rich in content, containing knowledge such as astronomy, geography, history, state governance, self-cultivation, morality, ethics, and more, truly deserving the title of traditional Chinese learning classics. Throughout history, countless children have gained knowledge and cultivated their character by studying these works.

**Reading Guidance:**

- Leading reading, detailed explanation, following reading, silent memorization.

- Related stories: Mother Meng's Three Relocations, He Shi Offering Jade, Kong Rong Giving Away Pears, Cang Jie Creating Characters, Separating Heaven and Earth, Flowing Water Against High Mountains, Five Sons Achieving Success, Confucius Reading Thrice-Bound Bamboo Strips, Wen Gong's Alarm Pillow, Yu the Great Controlling the Floods, Sleeping on Firewood and Tasting Bitter Gall, Burning the Red Cliffs, Confucius Seeking a Teacher, Writing on Rush Mats, Standing in the Snow at Cheng's Door, Dao Yun Composing Snow Poems, Reading at Night with Fireflies, Rising at the Cockcrow, Su Qin Piercing His Thigh, Turning an Iron Rod into a Needle.

**Including Complete Texts of "Three-Character Classic," "Hundred Family Surnames," and "Thousand Character Classic":**

In addition to the complete texts, it also includes animated representations of related idioms, historical stories, surname origin stories, and famous people events, helping children understand difficult words in classical texts and better comprehend the meaning of the text.

**Five-Step Teaching Method:**

1. **Origins:** By explaining the origins of the "Three-Character Classic," "Hundred Family Surnames," and "Thousand Character Classic," it stimulates learning interest through parent-child games.

2. **Leading Reading:** Through listening to the teacher's reading, establish a preliminary impression for learning.

3. **Following Reading:** Simulating real classroom leading reading scenarios, children learn classics gradually.

4. **Silent Reading:** Accompanied by beautiful music and soft prompts, test the learning results.

5. **Related Stories:** Enrich children's knowledge in history, geography, society, and more.

**Quickly Master Knowledge and Improve Cultivation:**

The texts of the "Three-Character Classic," "Hundred Family Surnames," and "Thousand Character Classic" use three-character and four-character formats, with neat antithetical couplets and rhyming schemes, making them easy for children to read and recite. Through reciting these classics, children not only receive the熏tration of traditional culture but also enhance their memory, observation, and expression abilities, especially beneficial for primary and middle school students.
