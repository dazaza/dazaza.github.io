A bunch of roses for a love that has ended

by wcicc15h23 on 2012-02-21 21:11:37

There was a love that passed by me, and I regretted it only after I lost it. If God could give me another chance, I would certainly cherish it. Of all the girls I've ever seen, Yu is the only one who satisfied me. Although her appearance is not very outstanding, she is very good at dressing herself; her voice is very pleasant, as if she has studied broadcasting; and what pleases me most is her empathy, so we became lovers.

Yu and I met through a matchmaker's introduction. Her home is in the suburbs, far from me. We first met at the bus station. She was very shy and didn't like to talk much. We just simply talked about our family situations and work situations. I told her that my job was very busy and I wouldn't have much time to spend with her, hoping she could understand me. She smiled and said it was okay, everything was for the future. After exchanging phone numbers, we ended our first meeting.

Afterwards, I called her every night. I worked at a dedicated car factory, and at that time, the unit was very busy, with a large number of vehicles dispatched every day, and it was also very tiring. But no matter how tired I was, as long as I talked to her on the phone, all the fatigue would disappear. I don't know why, but my colleagues at work also joked with me, saying I was like on stimulants. I told her that my job was very busy, and it wasn't good to take too many leaves, so I could only see her once a week. She said she could understand, telling me to focus on my work and not to be distracted, taking care of my health. I knew I had met an empathetic girl, she would be the other half I chased.

Time flew by quickly for 3 months, and during this period, things happened between us like most lovers. I knew I was getting more and more involved, I really fell in love with her. I was very happy to find my other half, but then something unexpected happened.

That day, we were walking in the Jiangnan Century Square in Jilin City, both in great moods, chatting while walking. At this moment, a little girl dressed in very dirty clothes came from the opposite direction, holding several bunches of roses, exaggeratingly praising them to Yu, the only reason being to sell her roses. I said to Yu: "I really dislike this way. If you like, I can buy you some from the flower shop." Yu hesitated and said: "Never mind, let's not." Then the little girl thrust the flowers into my hands, I asked Yu what to do, she said put them on the chair, let's go. So we left unhappily, and I didn't realize that the opportunity had slipped away from me, it was too late.

The next day, I called Yu. After I said a lot, Yu finally spoke, but said a sentence that shocked me: "Let's break up, you have never cared about my feelings." I was stunned, time seemed to stop for a long time, I knew it was too late. I could only say a sentence I was extremely unwilling to say in my life: "Forget it, I hope you will still remember me as a friend in the future, there was a love that passed by me."

Just like that, I lost Yu. It was only then that I realized how foolish I was, not cherishing her properly, and what made me angry was that a bunch of roses ended my beautiful love.

Afterwards, I never met a girl as good as Yu again...
