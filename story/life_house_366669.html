Property tax reform pilot program to expand nationwide; only child's home to be taxed at "half rate"

by dwzhanghao on 2010-09-30 10:00:39

Property tax reform pilot to expand nationwide, only housing "half" levied deed tax

Family's only housing enjoys "half" preferential deed tax

Original: Third is to adjust the deed tax and personal income tax preferential policies in the housing transaction环节. Strengthen supervision and inspection of land value-added tax collection, focusing on real estate development projects with prices obviously exceeding the surrounding housing price level for land value-added tax settlement and inspection. Accelerate the pilot work of property tax reform and gradually expand it nationwide.

Interpretation:

In response to the above-mentioned new policies of deed tax and personal income tax preferences, the Ministry of Finance, the State Administration, and the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development have quickly jointly issued specific details last night, that is, from October 1, 2010, the purchase of ordinary housing and this housing belongs to the family's only housing, the deed tax will be halved. For taxpayers who sell their own housing and repurchase within one year, personal income tax will no longer be exempted.

Only housing "half" levied deed tax

The document clearly states that in terms of deed tax policy in the real estate transaction环节, for individuals purchasing ordinary housing, and this housing belongs to the only housing of the family (the scope of members includes the purchaser, spouse, and minor children), the deed tax will be halved. For individuals purchasing ordinary housing of 90 square meters and below, and this housing belongs to the family's only housing, the deed tax will be levied at a rate of 1%. For ordinary housing purchased by individuals, if it does not meet the above regulations, it cannot enjoy the above preferential policies.

According to the current deed tax policy in Beijing before, the deed tax collection rate is 3%, but the deed tax for first-time housing under 90 square meters is levied at 1%, and those meeting the standard of ordinary commercial housing are halved, i.e., levied at 1.5%. From the new policy, starting tomorrow, October 1, only first-time homebuyers, and the ordinary housing being the family's only housing, can enjoy the preferential discount of half the deed tax; similarly, ordinary housing under 90 square meters enjoying the preferential deed tax rate of 1% must also be the family's only housing to enjoy the preferential treatment. Those who buy ordinary commercial housing but still own multiple commercial housing or public housing, housing reform housing, etc., can no longer enjoy the preferential deed tax policy. Similarly, the preferential policy for personal income tax has also been "shrunk", the new policy clearly states that for taxpayers who sell their own housing and repurchase within one year, personal income tax will no longer be exempted.

Official first mention of "property tax pilot work"

The statement in the new policy regarding "accelerating the pilot work of property tax reform and gradually expanding it nationwide" is not only the official first mention of "property tax pilot work" but also the first mention of "the pilot will gradually expand nationwide".

Industry insiders judge that as a tax policy that can stabilize the real estate market in the long term, the current proposal to accelerate the pilot work and gradually expand the pilot nationwide will once again make "property tax" a sword hanging over the real estate market, affecting housing price expectations.

Cities with rapid housing price increases will increase land supply

Original: Fourth is to effectively increase housing supply. All regions should increase the supervision and assessment of the actual completion of the 2010 housing construction plan and land supply plan in each region, and earnestly implement the construction plan and land supply plan for small-medium sized ordinary commercial housing and affordable housing. Cities with rapid housing price increases should increase the total supply of residential land. Seriously implement the tax preferential policies supporting public rental housing construction. Encourage financial institutions to support the construction of affordable housing, and quickly formulate medium-long term loan policies supporting public rental housing construction.

Interpretation:

Industry insiders believe that "increase supply" is a problem mentioned in many real estate control policies since the introduction of the "Ten Measures". Currently, including Beijing, the newly added housing supply is indeed beginning to increase, and the housing inventory in Beijing has recently returned to more than 100,000 units from the warning line of "supply falling short of demand". "If a series of measures to increase supply can be implemented, next year's stable housing market will have an additional ace."

Illegal real estate companies will be suspended from land purchases and loans

Original: Fifth is to increase the inspection intensity of the housing transaction market, and investigate and deal with according to law behaviors such as brokerage agencies stir-buying and selling, hyping up housing prices, inciting customers to sign "yin-yang contracts", etc. For illegal and disciplinary violations by real estate developers such as idle land, changing land use and nature, delaying start and completion times, withholding stock and holding back sales, continue to increase the exposure and punishment. For real estate developers with such illegal and disciplinary records, suspend their issuance of stocks, corporate bonds, and new land purchases, and all commercial banks stop issuing new development project loans and loan extensions to them.

Interpretation:

Regarding the provision "investigate and deal with according to law behaviors such as brokerage agencies stir-buying and selling, hyping up housing prices, inciting customers to sign 'yin-yang contracts'", industry insiders believe that this is the first time that several ministries jointly issuing control policies have mentioned investigating brokerage agencies' stir-buying and selling and "yin-yang contracts". Compared to the new policy in April, this provision targets the main issues existing in the market and refines targeted details. Taking Beijing as an example, most of the current second-hand house transactions pay only 50% of the actual taxes due. Once the new policy is fully implemented, second-hand speculators will face high costs of speculation.

As for the punishment for developers engaging in illegal activities such as idle land, changing land use and nature, delaying start and completion times, withholding stock and holding back sales, it has been specifically detailed from previous vague provisions to "suspend their issuance of stocks, corporate bonds, and new land purchases, and all commercial banks stop issuing new development project loans and loan extensions", making it highly operable. Relevant persons from Yingcan Real Estate believe that since the resumption of IPOs, financing through domestic listings or shell listings, listing in Hong Kong, and debt issuance have become important sources of funds for developers. In the future, if intentional delays in construction or withholding stock and holding back sales are discovered, stock trading will be prohibited, which will undoubtedly be effective for listed companies.

New Policy·Analysis

Second-tier cities may see dual decline in prices and volume

The new policy released yesterday, what kind of impact it will have on the real estate market is the most watched. In response to this, last night, relevant responsible persons from developers such as Vanke, Shouchuang, Gemdale, Shoukai, SOHO China, and brokerage institutions such as Centaline Property and Lianjia Property, as well as homebuyers who saw the release of the new policy through news broadcasts and the internet, were discussing this. Summarizing their views, it is relatively consistent that the new policy released yesterday may have a greater impact on the expectation of housing prices in Beijing's real estate market than substantive impacts, causing the market to observe again; while for other places, especially some second-tier cities that did not strictly enforce the "Ten Measures" in the past, it will have substantial impacts, dual decline in prices and volume might appear soon.
