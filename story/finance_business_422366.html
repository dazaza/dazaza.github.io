If we say that photographers value performance, enthusiasts value taste, then ordinary people look at the cost-performance ratio when buying a camera. Although "you get what you pay for", there is no need to pay extra for those functions we don't need, nor is it necessary to pursue low prices by buying too basic products. After all, digital cameras have become more and more integrated into our lives.

On the premise of high cost-effective, "easy to use" and "user-friendly" are the indicators we value. The five compact cameras introduced below have mainstream performance configuration and high usability, and are cheap suitable for home users to buy. They are also the top five products in the current netizen attention ranking, so it's not wrong to make the same choice as everyone else.

Compact Camera Attention Ranking No.1 - Canon IXUS115 HS

Reference price: 1500 yuan

The Canon IXUS115 HS camera has 12 million effective pixels, 1/2.3 inch CMOS sensor. It also has a 4x optical zoom 28mm wide-angle anti-shake lens. The Canon IXUS115 HS uses DIGIC4 image processor, equipped with a 3-inch 230,000-pixel LCD screen. It supports 1920×1080 Full HD video shooting and dynamic stabilization technology.

Canon IXUS115 HS

The Canon IXUS115 HS has an automatic scene shooting function that can recognize up to 32 scenes and has an automatic tracking focus shooting function. The IXUS115 HS has a built-in high-speed shooting mode that supports high-speed shooting at 8.2 frames per second. There are multiple photo shooting aspect ratios to choose from, such as 16:9, 3:2, 4:3, and 1:1.

Canon IXUS115 HS

The Canon IXUS115 HS has handheld night view and automatic best shot selection shooting modes, and built-in creative shooting functions such as model effect, fisheye effect, LOMO effect, etc. The IXUS115 offers five body colors for consumers to choose from, namely silver, blue, gray, orange, and pink. The body size of the Canon IXUS115 HS is: 93.1 x 55.9 x 19.9 mm, weight: 121g.
