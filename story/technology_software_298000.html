Related thematic articles: Yesterday morning, I met Mr. Nong, the martial arts coach who was robbed, at Pinghu Shanxia Hospital. "I have liked martial arts since I was a child, and now I am a coach at a martial arts gym in坂田," Mr. Nong introduced that at around midnight on the 5th, he returned to坂田from a friend's house and passed by the gate of a railway transportation company in Pinghu Street when five bandits suddenly appeared. "They asked me for money, and I lied and agreed, intending to resist them. The bandit standing behind me stabbed my leg with a knife..." Mr. Nong recalled, "I swung my fists and hit the bandit who stabbed me on the head, and that bandit stabbed me several more times..." With blood flowing profusely, Mr. Nong fought fiercely and called out for help while continuing to resist with his fists. "They all rushed at me and pinned me down. I turned my body forcefully, threw two of them to the ground, and snatched the knife from another bandit." In the fight, two men were injured by Mr. Nong's resistance, and the other three bandits saw the situation turn against them and snatched Mr. Nong's backpack before fleeing. "At that time, I shouted 'Help'!" Mr. Nong said, "Patrol officers quickly arrived and blocked the surrounding roads, working together to subdue four of the bandits!"

In the bed next to Mr. Nong's, a young man wearing handcuffs lay sideways on the bed. His medical record showed that this man surnamed Ma, aged 19, was from Guangxi. Xiao Ma told the reporter that he and four other men were fellow townspeople, all working at a nearby factory. That night, after drinking some alcohol, one of their fellow townspeople suggested they were short on cash and wanted to get some money, and two others even took out two knives. So, they decided to rob passersby. While wandering outside, they encountered Mr. Nong. "I didn't expect to meet such a tough guy with such good hand-to-hand skills," Xiao Ma regretted, "After arriving at the hospital, I learned that he is a martial arts coach. It's really unfortunate!" The bandit Xiao Ma deeply regretted his actions. On the second floor, the reporter saw another suspect, a man surnamed Lu, aged 18, whose neck had been slashed and could no longer speak.

Encountering a robbery, Mr. Nong, the martial arts coach, resisted fiercely and suffered serious stab wounds to his legs. The police stated that the two suspects who were not injured had been criminally detained, while the other two temporarily remained in the hospital for treatment, to be criminally detained again once they recover. According to Mr. Nong's recollection, there were a total of five men who robbed him. Currently, the police are actively investigating the last fugitive.

According to our news report (with photos by reporter Yu Xueqing), at around midnight on the 5th, five bandits attempted to rob a martial arts coach at the gate of a railway transportation company in Pinghu Street, Longgang District. During the struggle, the martial arts coach who was stabbed and the patrol officers who arrived upon hearing the call successfully subdued four of the bandits, with one escaping. Yesterday, the Longgang police formally issued an arrest warrant for the fugitive bandit.

Fellow townspeople gathered for dinner and planned the robbery. Four were caught, one escaped.

Yesterday afternoon, the Longgang police formally issued an arrest warrant for the fugitive bandit. "After hearing Mr. Nong's cry for help, patrol officers nearby quickly arrived at the scene and found Mr. Nong seriously injured, lying in a pool of blood. The patrol officers called the hospital while contacting colleagues nearby via walkie-talkies to surround and capture the fleeing suspects. Soon, two uninjured suspects were captured by the patrol officers."

Yesterday afternoon, lawyer Liu Maotong told reporters that according to Article of the Criminal Law, implementing justifiable defense must meet four conditions: First, it must occur only when national public interests, one's own or others' legitimate rights are illegally infringed; Second, it must occur during the illegal infringement; Third, the defense must be directed at the person committing the illegal act and not at unrelated third parties; Fourth, the justifiable defense cannot exceed the necessary limit, causing unnecessary damage. "Based on the materials obtained by the police, judging according to these four points, it was determined that Mr. Nong's actions during the incident constituted justifiable defense," Liu Maotong stated.

Subsequently, the police officers and patrol officers continued searching in the surrounding area and eventually captured two more injured suspects at the Shanxia Martyrs Cemetery." Said the Longgang police.

Preliminary police determination

For Mr. Nong's actions of injuring the suspects while resisting the robbery, the police preliminarily determined it as justifiable defense, exempting him from legal responsibility.
