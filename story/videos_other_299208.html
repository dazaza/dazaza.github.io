A woman looking for her long-lost biological father discovered that he was dressed as a woman and wanted to transgender.

by dongshan01 on 2009-12-08 16:46:33

Emily and Dad Cliff

According to British media reports on the 29th, Emily Wallis, a 22-year-old British girl, only learned at the age of 19 that Jack, whom she had always regarded as her father, was not her biological father. Her real father is actually Cliff Harrison, a former boxer, who is a lover outside of her mother Ann's marriage.

Ann told Emily that she became pregnant with Cliff during an extramarital affair and later gave birth to Emily. Her husband Jack had no idea that this daughter was not his biological child. Before Ann realized she was pregnant, her lover Cliff had left the UK for Australia, so Cliff also did not know that Ann had given birth to a daughter for him. When Emily was 7 years old, Ann's marriage with her husband Jack began to crack, and they divorced shortly after. Emily lived with her mother but continued to visit her father Jack regularly.

After Emily found out that her biological father was someone else, she begged her mother to help her find her biological father Cliff. However, Emily's efforts to find her father were fruitless. It wasn't until earlier this year when one of Ann's old friends informed her that Cliff, now 52 years old, had returned from Australia to the UK, which thrilled Emily.

What Emily could never have dreamed of was that Ann’s friend then dropped a "bombshell" on them: this friend said Cliff had now become a woman named "Chloe." Ann made contact with Cliff and discovered he was indeed living as a woman and desired gender reassignment surgery to become a true woman. He pleaded with Ann not to tell Emily the truth because he was worried his daughter would feel ashamed.

Nevertheless, Emily still longed to reunite with her never-before-met biological father Cliff (now Chloe), so they arranged to meet in a bar.

The "biological father" Emily met was not a robust former boxer but a "sexy woman" wearing a female wig, a dress, and high heels. Cliff's appearance had transformed from what Emily imagined as a "mighty dad" into a "sexy mom," looking more fashionable and well-dressed than her daughter. There were as many as 82 pairs of women's high heels in her home.

Emily found that she and her biological father shared many unexpected "common points" beyond their blood relationship: Emily also discovered that she and Cliff had almost identical tastes in high heels, skirts, and cosmetics.

Cliff told Emily that when he had an affair with her mother Ann, he was also married, and his wife was a former beauty queen. But he suffered from gender dysphoria since childhood, always troubled by his gender identity and believing himself to be a woman mistakenly born into a male body. He started secretly trying on his mother's clothes at the age of 5. Gradually, he began openly dressing as a woman and changed his name to the feminine "Chloe," living as a woman in the world. Cliff now fully enjoys the freedom of being a woman and plans to undergo complete gender reassignment surgery next year.

Although the biological father she had been searching for had turned into a "mother," the fact of their reunion still made Emily extremely happy. Emily said, "When I get married in the future, I still hope Chloe can walk me down the aisle."

Chloe, who has transformed into a "mom," said, "Emily said she loves me, and I can't believe how lucky I am; it's like I've become a mother again at the age of 52."
