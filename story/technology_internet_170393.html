Horizontal Comparison of Well-known Navigation Websites

by kaxiya on 2009-07-04 10:35:31

The current crop of websites, they are like grandchildren + grandchildren, multiplying very quickly, but only a few truly do a good job. This time, we have specially set aside time to compare this market and conduct a horizontal comparison of several well-known website directories. Your participation is welcome.

First team www.hao123.com and www.265.com

These are the oldest website directories. Their traffic is top-notch, and their revenue is even more outstanding, with an annual income of over 1 million yuan.

Such strong old forces still target those domestic groups who are not familiar with the URL market. They urgently need a URL navigation guide to help them access the internet and use it as a platform to understand more about the online market. That's why they are so popular. It's simple: the number of people going online is still increasing, and they can't do without these sites.

Second team www.114.com.cn

This is an emerging force, coming on strong and making great achievements. After conducting an internal diagnosis of www.114.com.cn, we learned that this is an emerging internet company that promotes its URL site through large-scale investment. www.114.com.cn is indeed commendable, but the question is how many companies have the same level of substantial capital? There aren't many. Therefore, in the era where capital rules all, 114 belongs to the golden type of network URL site, bringing traffic through heavy investment promotion.

Third team www.aicha123.com, www.gjj.cc, and www.369.com

Why are these considered the third team? Because they all belong to personal websites with relatively small teams. Especially www.aicha123.com, which locks onto the campus group by positioning the market. Their experience is minimal investment, high traffic, free inclusion, and no shortage of money. www.gjj.cc is an old webmaster from Chongqing, but they are still a small team with few members, so they are all grouped into the third team. As for the 369 team, we've learned that they promote through viral methods, so they are also categorized into the third team. Compared to the second team, what they lack is money, so they resort to viral promotion, achieving good traffic. However, this determines that they are destined to be the third team.

Fourth team Copycat grandson sites

These are categorized as non-mainstream grandson sites because they are all products of plagiarism and copying. Hence, they are grouped into the grandson sites. Only a few grandson sites are done relatively well, but there aren't many. Since they lack technical components and extra funds, they can only drift along, meaning their fate is never to become strong sites.

As analyzed above, we pay more attention to latecomers such as www.aicha123.com and www.369.com, because one should respect the younger generation. If you agree, please come along and let's discuss the heroes over wine.
