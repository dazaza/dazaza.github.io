An air hostess revealed her basic salary of 1500, too afraid to go shopping, chose to be a homebody

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-03 13:29:35

This log comes from QQ12348 space, welcome to reprint.

Sharing introduction: In fact, the real income of air hostesses is not as high as everyone imagines. At present, the monthly salary of ordinary post air hostesses (domestic airlines) is about 5000 yuan. According to the mechanism of "the more you fly, the more you get", the adjustment of international and domestic routes and the change of posts, the income will fluctuate to different degrees. In addition, some airlines have introduced the "pre-flight attendant" employment system, which gives more people who pass the training examination the opportunity to become flight attendants, and the competition is constantly intensifying.

First of all, let me introduce myself. I am a stewardess. Not very old. The flight experience is not very rich. Just an ordinary CC, and failed to reach the position of chief stewardess. Today, after many years, my friends asked about my income. Of course, in their eyes, I am a high-income and high-profession woman. Despite this, the flight attendant is still the career that a large number of young girls aspire to.

In addition, some airlines directly link the number of complaints received by flight attendants with passengers with their income, which also increases the pressure on cabin crew. (When I first got on the plane, I was complained and fined 3000 yuan, so I won't go into details)

Intern flight attendant: earn a thousand yuan a month and pay for the training fee yourself

During the six-month internship period when we first entered the airline, we could only get a salary of 900 yuan a month, and did not enjoy various benefits of regular employees. After being hired by the airline, we must also receive several months of pre-job training. After passing the test and internship, we can become real cabin crew. And the pre-job training fee of general airlines is generally between 8000 and 10,000 yuan, which is generally paid by the stewardess herself.

Domestic route: too little subsidy, only earn five or six thousand

I have a friend Liu Tao who is a flight attendant at Hainan Airlines and mainly responsible for domestic route flights. Her monthly basic salary is less than 2000 yuan, and the various subsidies per hour of flight are generally between 20 and 30 yuan. It's very tiring and hard for cabin crew to fly domestic lines and the subsidies are relatively small. Plus now there are too many flights, each person works less than 30 hours a week, so the total income is only five or six thousand yuan. However, if you encounter "May Day", "National Day" and the Spring Festival, everyone will get a corresponding increase in overtime pay and bonus.

International route: the more you fly, the more you earn over ten thousand a month

In our company, the monthly basic salary for flying international routes is 1700 yuan, then you can get 10 to 12 dollars per hour for flight hour fees and flight subsidies. Generally, you have to fly 40 hours a week, and more than 100 hours a month, with an income of more than ten thousand yuan. The basic salary of first-class cabin attendants is relatively high, 2000 yuan per month, but the hourly fee and flight subsidy are the same. But if you reach the level of chief stewardess, not only can you get a monthly salary of 3000 yuan, but you can also get 15 dollars per hour for hourly fee and flight subsidy, and your monthly income can reach more than 20,000 yuan.

Foreign flight attendants: good treatment becomes the quasi-gold-collar in the industry

After 1997, with the unification of the standards for overseas subsidies by civil aviation management departments and the reduction of income of domestic airlines, the pockets of flight attendants began to shrink. Statistical data from Beijing Foreign Airlines Service Company showed that the monthly salary of foreign flight attendants generally reached 13,000 to 15,000 yuan, and more could reach more than 20,000 yuan, becoming the quasi-gold-collar among flight attendants. A classmate of mine at Thai Airlines flies only 50 to 60 hours a month, and the flight allowance reaches 20,000 yuan; another classmate at Japan Airlines, flies 70 hours a month, and the income reaches 13,000 to 14,000 yuan. Currently, Singapore Airlines and Lufthansa hire the most Chinese flight attendants. In recent years, Northwest Airlines, Finnair, Asiana Airlines, Malaysia Airlines, and Thai Airways have also increased the recruitment of Chinese flight attendants.

Admission Requirements: Strict requirements for appearance and physique

A few schools in China with qualifications to recruit flight attendants need to conduct interviews before admission. Qualified interviewees also need to take the national unified college entrance examination. Some colleges that recruit students from society, such as Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, require candidates to take the **college entrance examination. At the same time of the interview, a strict physical examination will also be conducted. Generally speaking, candidates should not have congenital or acquired abnormal diseases and active, potential, acute or chronic diseases, as well as sequelae after trauma, injury or surgery. They are required to have regular features, good skin color, well-proportioned figures, open-mindedness, and graceful demeanor, with a height of 160 cm to 172 cm. (I am 166 cm.)

Foreign airline threshold: more emphasis on psychological quality and other internal qualities

Foreign airlines have much looser requirements for external conditions when recruiting Chinese flight attendants, Wenzhou Google optimization, paying more attention to psychological quality and other internal qualities. It is understood that the initial selection conditions for flight attendants of foreign airlines are: unmarried, healthy, beautiful and dignified, good vision, not less than 1.60 meters in height, aged between 20 and 26 years old, with a junior college degree or above, able to speak standard Mandarin and English. (Of course, the better your foreign language ability, the more opportunities you will have)

Base salary: 1500. Flight allowance: 10 USD per hour (102 hours) USD is 6.91 this month. Expenditure: mortgage: 3722.3 yuan property fee 120 yuan/month average; phone bill: 100 yuan send money home: 2000 yuan daily necessities: 100 yuan cosmetics: 300 yuan clothing: 300 yuan friends gathering: generally AA system 200 yuan. Save: 1500 yuan generally fly abroad. Stay in a hotel. Rest at home, stay at home, eat at home. Live at home. I am very homebound, I am a homebody, rarely go out and spend money recklessly. By the end of the month, there is basically nothing left.

Editor's postscript: In our impression, flight attendants are always bright and beautiful, Shijiazhuang Google optimization, dressed beautifully and earning high salaries.
