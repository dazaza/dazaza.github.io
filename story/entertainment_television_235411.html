Newcomer Han Ji-Ho will appear in MBC weekend drama "Jewel Bibimbap"

According to Korean media reports, newcomer actor Han Ji-Ho will appear in MBC's new weekend drama "Jewel Bibimbap" as a popular singer.

Han Ji-Ho appears in the first episode of "Jewel Bibimbap" broadcast at 9:45 pm on September 5, playing the role of popular singer Tae-Seong. Han Ji-Ho, who possesses both looks and talent, sang Rain's "September 12th" at the drama filming site, showcasing his singing ability, and received enthusiastic applause from the staff on-site.

Han Ji-Ho said: "Since I am playing a singer in the drama, I have been practicing singing seriously for three weeks in preparation for the filming. 'September 12th' is a song I've liked since I was young. At first when filming started, I felt very nervous singing in front of everyone, but with everyone's encouragement, I quickly completed the filming."

Han Ji-Ho also appeared in KBS drama "Please, Mr. Allow" as one of the F4 members, a friend of Yoon Eun-Hye's character, recreating the F4 from "Boys Over Flowers," attracting much attention.
