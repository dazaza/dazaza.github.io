Young man was rescued twice in a day after jumping off the Yangtze River Bridge

by hj1718004 on 2011-09-14 19:40:35

Our press (reporter Xiao Juan, correspondent Chen Ji and Liu Zhi Hui) - A man surnamed Zeng from Fujian Province attempted to commit suicide by jumping off the Yangtze River Second Bridge twice in one day. Both times, he was promptly discovered and rescued by the police of the Second Bridge Traffic Detachment.

Yesterday at 11:35 AM, Officer An Ying on duty in the command room of the Yangtze River Second Bridge noticed through video surveillance that a person near the southern cable section of the bridge, heading southbound, showed signs of wanting to jump. Immediately, he informed Officer Chen Shen Ru, who was on duty at the Jiangnan post, to rush to the scene for investigation.

Upon reaching the scene, Chen Shen Ru found that a short-haired man in his 20s, wearing a white shirt, was preparing to jump. His two feet were on the edge of the bridge deck, with his toes already hanging in mid-air. Chen Shen Ru patiently persuaded him, intentionally stalling for time.

When supporting officer Lu Hao arrived, the two officers exchanged glances and gestures, preparing for the rescue. A few minutes later, as the man began climbing over the railing, Chen Shen Ru rushed forward and grabbed his throat with his right hand. Other officers quickly moved in, pulling the suicidal individual back onto the safe area of the bridge deck, rescuing the young man.

Subsequently, the young man was transferred to the Xujia棚 Police Station for arrangements. It was learned that he is surnamed Zeng, born in 1985, and hails from Dehua County, Fujian Province. Due to career setbacks and conflicts with his wife, he had momentarily lost hope and intended to leave this world.

Unexpectedly, around 14:48 PM yesterday, the command room again detected someone preparing to jump. Officers Lu Hao and Chen Shen Ru were once more dispatched to the scene. Upon arrival, they realized it was the same person from the morning. The traffic police continuously worked to stabilize Zeng's emotions. After 20 minutes, the man's emotions escalated again, and Officer Chen Shen Ru acted swiftly, pulling him back onto the bridge deck. Subsequently, the man was taken back to the traffic detachment. After persuasion, Zeng abandoned his suicidal intentions.
