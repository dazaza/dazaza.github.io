Why do newbies choose to start their online stores by doing top-ups? February 18, 2011 Category: Personal Diary

There are many reasons for this. 1) Online store operations emphasize credibility. As a consumer, you would definitely choose a seller with some credibility. However, new store owners lack credibility, but doing virtual top-ups can quickly boost credibility. If you start with physical goods (clothes, shoes, etc.), it's very difficult to build credibility. You also have to deal with shipping companies and consider where to purchase inventory, costs, and more. A transaction may take over a day. Doing top-ups eliminates these concerns; they're all instant, no need to worry about logistics, inventory, or stockouts. Transactions can be completed in minutes. Therefore, credibility is raised quickly. Once credibility is high, selling physical goods becomes easier and more feasible.

2) Doing top-ups doesn't require you to keep your computer on all the time. There is an offline management feature available. Selling physical goods requires long-term computer use.

3) The investment for doing top-ups is low, only a few hundred yuan. Selling physical goods involves a much larger investment.

4) The market for top-ups is large.

5) The operation is simple, easy to manage, and you can become proficient in just a couple of days.

How Much Money Do You Need to Start an Online Store? February 6, 2011 Category: Personal Diary

Hello everyone, how much money do you need to start a store and do mobile phone top-ups? Many friends have this question. In fact, starting a store to act as an agent for mobile phone top-up software only requires a fixed fee of 300 yuan or 50 yuan (50 yuan is the monthly version, meaning 50 yuan per month, while 300 yuan is the agency version which can be used for life). These 300 yuan are for fixed agency software usage. Additionally, there’s working capital (it's recommended to have around 100 yuan, but this can vary depending on needs). Working capital refers to the money you use to recharge the software. For example, if you recharge the software with 100 yuan, you will then have 100 yuan in your software account, and you can start recharging for customers. For instance, if a customer requests a 100-yuan top-up, the software will automatically deduct around 96-98 yuan, leaving you with a few yuan profit. After the top-up, your software account will have 2-4 yuan left. You can then add more money from the payment the customer gave you (this process is quick, all done instantly), and the money in the software account can be withdrawn at any time. You can also use it to recharge your own phone or help friends recharge. Thus, the total investment is 400 yuan, with 300 yuan fixed and 100 yuan as working capital. What You Need to Prepare for Opening an Online Store January 22, 2011 Category: Personal Diary

Friends planning to open a store need to prepare real ID information for Taobao's real-name registration, as well as a bank card that has online banking activated (if you don’t have one, you can go to Agricultural Bank of China, Construction Bank of China, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, etc., to apply for a bank card and tell the staff to activate online banking).

You should also think about the store name and products you intend to sell, and make good preparations. As the saying goes: "A good beginning is half the battle." Wishing you success!
