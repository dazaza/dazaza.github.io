1 google.com 142M+ U.S. monthly people. The site attracts a more affluent, more educated audience.

2 yahoo.com 124M+ U.S. monthly people. The site appeals to a more educated crowd.

3 live.com 113M+ U.S. monthly people. The site appeals to a more educated crowd.

4 msn.com 101M+ U.S. monthly people. The site caters to an adult, more educated audience.

5 facebook.com 83M+ U.S. monthly people. The site appeals to a more affluent, teen and young adult, very slightly female biased following.

6 youtube.com 79M+ U.S. monthly people. The site appeals to a more youthful following.
