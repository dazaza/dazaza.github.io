Aucma - The Most Influential Brand in China's Electric Vehicle Industry Four Times in a Row

by aucma on 2009-04-16 10:02:26

Recently, authoritative medias such as "People's Daily", "China United Business and Trade Newspaper", "Brand Magazine" etc officially announced the list of the most influential brands in various industries for the year 2008. In this steaming list which includes heavyweight enterprises from all walks of industry, Aokema electric bicycles has become one of the shining members of "the most influential brands" together with Qingdao enterprises like Haier and Hisense.

Honors bear witness to industry influence

Aokema electric vehicles have won the title of "the Most Influential Brand in China's Electric Vehicle Industry" for four consecutive years. This is a full affirmation of Aokema's strict quality control over the years and its pursuit of product quality with perfectionism. It also bears witness to the process of the development and strengthening of Aokema's electric vehicle brand. Winning the title of "the Most Influential Brand in the Electric Vehicle Industry" for four consecutive years is not only the trust and recognition of authoritative media at all levels, but also that of consumers nationwide towards Aokema electric bicycles.

Profession forging refined quality

Senior officials of Aokema electric vehicles expressed that winning the title of "the Most Influential Brand in the Electric Vehicle Industry" for four consecutive years does not mean that Aokema will relax its quality supervision. In fact, in terms of quality inspection, Aokema itself has a quality inspection system that could even be said to be higher than national standards. This is embodied in the 360° service concept, starting from research and development, then manufacturing, sales, after-sales service, and finally returning to research and development, forming a closed-loop service system which interacts and provides feedback with market user needs, running through all aspects of the company's operations. To put it vividly, it means that consumers' needs, environmental needs, and even societal forces are supervising various aspects of Aokema electric bicycles' quality, quality, and after-sales service.

New product launch seizing the market

Recently, Aokema electric vehicles launched several new products upgraded with new technologies, aiming to seize the 2009 electric vehicle market with the momentum of winning the "Most Influential Brand in the Electric Vehicle Industry" four times in a row. These fashionable masterpieces carefully crafted by Aokema electric vehicles under the concept of "there is no best, only better" either exude graceful charm, or cold beauty, or heroic demeanor, their profound heritage cannot help but make people feel an overwhelming attraction and love at first sight!

Under the dazzling personalized exterior lies a more profound functional foundation. The whole plastic parts fit tightly, the assembly is compact, the shock-absorbing frame pays attention to every detail in pursuit of perfection, reflecting Aokema's pursuit of perfection in products. The brushless high-speed motor effectively extends the range; large torque, strong hill-climbing ability; the battery, motor, and controller are optimized and designed for strong compatibility. Equipped with M-ABS and front and rear hydraulic dual shocks, the ride is more comfortable. Meanwhile, Aokema electric vehicles' relentless pursuit of humanized technology makes this new product shine with the light of humanity everywhere. Some new products are equipped with child seats, children's cushions, and unique and novel rear racks, which are thoughtful cares for consumers. And the double safety settings of anti-theft alarm and motor-following lock keep the electric vehicle free from theft worries.

From this year's market trends, the appearance design of electric bicycles has already become the key focus for all manufacturers. Design taste guides and influences consumers' preferences for fashionable products, and consumers, on the basis of satisfying functional needs, increasingly value the feelings brought by the product and the integration with their own appreciation tastes. How to create a personalized, eye-catching product has become the "important homework" for every electric bicycle manufacturer. The multiple new models of electric bicycles launched by Aokema completely break the traditional design concept in terms of appearance, integrating the currently most popular fashion original colors and taste connotations into the appearance design of electric bicycles, giving consumers a brand-new aesthetic experience. It is believed that it will ignite consumers' strong desire to purchase. We can't help but admire Aokema's almost perfect industrial design and exquisite craftsmanship. The high praise from industry insiders for Aokema's appearance also verifies this point from another side.

The Aokema people will continue to surpass themselves with the corporate belief of "there is no best, only better", adhering to the principle of market-driven, firmly grasping the market pulse, always providing customers with high-quality products and fast, thoughtful services. They will be committed to the research and development of green transportation, providing environmentally friendly, convenient, and economical electric vehicles to the public, facilitating public travel, and actively protecting our common beautiful home through practical actions.
