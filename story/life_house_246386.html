Comparison of Home Decoration Styles in Ten Major Cities

by longersing on 2009-10-11 18:38:01

Each city has its own style. Take decoration for example, the west has the western style, the east has oriental cultural colors, the south has the beauty of mountains and waters, and the north has the beauty of winter. There are also great differences in decorating styles between Beijing and Shanghai, and some other representative cities.

Beijing: Prevailing simple style

The Beijing people's pursuit of home is becoming increasingly natural, fresh, comfortable, practical, and simple. Among them, light, colorfulness, and texture are the elements of a simple-style home. Good color matching and light settings can make the room more beautiful. Warm tone lights create a warm and soft feeling, especially suitable for bedrooms, which natural light cannot match.

The simple style pursues bright and simple tones such as white and ivory as the main tones of the home, combined with natural light, making people feel exceptionally comfortable when stepping inside, instantly forgetting all unhappiness and fatigue. The combination of different textures is something that an increasing number of simple style enthusiasts pay special attention to. Rough textures give a sense of natural rugged simplicity, while delicate ones provide a warm and detailed sensation.

Shanghai: Mediterranean-style garden houses

When it comes to Shanghai people's living conditions, what impresses people most is the narrow small houses built in "a temple in a snail shell." However, before that, there were garden houses and Shikumen residences that made the Shanghai people proud and nostalgic.

Garden houses gave the Shanghai people inspiration and passion to pursue fashion. People like to decorate their houses in a very Mediterranean style, whether it be round arches, white tones, or staircases inlaid with blue flower tiles... every detail is spot on, making visitors feel as if they are in a foreign land.

Guangzhou: Pursuing clarity and brightness, health is the first standard

With a developed economy and fast-paced urban life, in recent years, many Guangzhou residents have set "healthy living environment" as the first standard in their home concepts. The home market, just like the colors themselves showing increasingly clear and bright patterns, is moving further towards the goals of refinement, individuality, and health. Meanwhile, the brand advantages of home products are becoming increasingly consolidated.

Tianjin: Home styles are becoming increasingly diverse

Nowadays, with Tianjin's housing forms becoming increasingly rich, international cultural exchanges becoming more frequent, interior designers' horizons broadening, and citizens' aesthetic tastes improving, the European classical style of interior design, with its pursuit of symmetrical spatial beauty, elegant curve beauty, and independent home decor beauty, is once again rejuvenated.

Chongqing: Home decoration focuses on practicality

As everyone knows, Chongqing is famous for its delicious food. Common people seem to focus more on food than on their homes. When it comes to decoration, the most consideration is given to practicality. Additionally, due to its location in a basin with much fog, people pay particular attention to the selection and design of lighting.

Suzhou: Natural and elegant "Jiangnan water town style"

With nature as the main theme, advocating the relaxation of tense nerves, seeking green moods in urban life, and creating a natural, primitive, and return-to-nature atmosphere through home arrangements, releasing complete freedom and joy. Under the natural style, colors become more lively, large geometric shapes are widely used, and fun shapes are boldly applied to home decoration, releasing total happiness. Any unpleasant matters from the outside world should stop at the doorstep.

Wuhan: Typical southern style, focusing on energy saving and ventilation

Wuhan people prefer relatively square designs, not too unconventional. When choosing house types, functionality must also be considered. For example, if the living room and dining room are together, it's best to have a balcony and a large window to bring in the outside scenery. The orientation is also carefully studied; the master bedroom should face south. Ventilation is emphasized, especially in the south.
