Trends: Furniture-like Stairs

With the continued popularity of high-end real estate, Shanghai solid wood stair materials [http://www.china-kewen.com], which originally had a low market share, are now also selling well. The good sales of stair materials are due to the increase in detached villas, duplexes, and staggered level houses in the real estate market. These houses all require stairs during renovation. When visiting building material supermarkets in various cities, it’s not hard to find that although the number of stalls occupied by stair brands is not many, each shop’s display is very meticulous, showcasing various miniaturized versions of stairs to customers. So what kind of material stairs are favored by consumers? Wooden stairs. In the future development of furniture design, the furniture-like transformation of stairs will be an inevitable trend. This is the type with the largest market share. Consumers like it mainly because wood itself gives a warm feeling, plus it easily matches with floor materials and colors, and construction is relatively convenient. Consumers choosing wooden products for stairs should pay attention to matching the size of the floor with the stairs. Currently, the most common floor size on the market is 90 cm long and 10 cm wide. However, stair floors can be paired with 120 cm long and 15 cm wide floors, meaning one step of the stairs only requires two regular floors. This reduces one seam, making construction and maintenance easier. For the choice of columns and handrails, one should ensure that the wood and style are as matching as possible. Although wooden stairs may seem to lack variability, they have their own advantages. Texture, purity.

Iron stairs. Compared to pure wooden stairs, this type of stair seems to add a bit more lively interest. It is actually a composite stair made of wood and iron. Some stair handrails and guardrails are iron products while the stair treads are still wooden products. Others have iron guardrails, with wooden handrails and treads. There are also quite a few customers who choose such stairs. Now, there is a wide range of choices for forged patterns in stair guardrails, including column types and various pattern designs; colors can be antique or in the original color of copper and iron. These kinds of stair handrails are custom-made, complex to process, and relatively expensive. Cast iron stairs have fewer styles in comparison. Generally, manufacturers have fixed styles to choose from, and colors can be processed according to customer requirements. People pursuing diverse forms would choose this type of stair.

Marble and wood stairs [http://www.china-kewen.com]. Customers choosing this type of stair usually already have marble laid on the ground floor. To maintain the unity of indoor colors and materials, they continue to lay marble on the stairs. However, most retain wooden handrails to add a bit of warmth to the cold space. The price of this type of decoration mainly depends on the quality of the marble. It gives a very high-end feel.

Glass stairs. This is a newer style suitable for young modernists. Glass is mostly frosted, not fully transparent, with a thickness of over 10mm. Some of these stairs also use wooden handrails, which are slightly cheaper than imported marble.

Steel stairs. This is a somewhat unconventional choice, but it has become a trend. Usually, paint is sprayed on the surface of the material. Since this paint is matte, it does not have a dazzlingly bright and irritating feeling. The materials and processing costs for this type of stair are relatively high. Additionally, there are wire ropes and hemp ropes used as guardrails, paired with wooden treads and handrails, which look pretty good. This trendy yet somewhat natural decorative staircase is inexpensive and stylish.
