I have been practicing on HF with over ten small numbers, www.lzgfz.com. I've basically tried all the professions.

Yesterday, I read an article on 131 about leveling up to 1-15 in two days. As long as you don't waste PL (Player Level) points recklessly, it's relatively easy to reach level 15.

In two days, with 700 PL points and 1000 weekly LP points, a reasonable allocation will suffice.

Link for level-up fatigue consumption: http://dn.131.com/jingyan/824757_1.html

I just want to point out a few key points. From level 1-7, doing both main and side quests is very fast. However, at this stage, you'll encounter a hurdle where you're just short of experience to level up to 8, which is required to continue the main quest line for level 8. [img] I recommend brushing these two maps at normal difficulty.

These two maps are easy to achieve SSS ratings. The difficulty and normal modes consume the same PL points. Normally, easy mode gives around 9000+ experience, while normal mode gives over 10000.

I usually brush these two maps until level 10 before continuing quests. From levels 8-10, I don't do quests because I found that levels 12 and 14 also require brushing maps instead of relying solely on quests since higher-level maps later on won't give as much experience as these two maps.

By the time you reach level 10, you would have used up most of your PL points for the day and around 100 weekly PL points.

Log off and sleep... On the second day, clear all the tasks in Frost Canyon to reach level 11, then proceed with Crystal Valley quests.

Here, you need to differentiate between normal and easy difficulties. Generally, use easy difficulty except for regular tasks requiring normal difficulty.

Following the quest lines from levels 11-14, by the time you finish the quests, you still won't be level 15. By this time, you should have used up your PL points for the second day. Use your weekly PL points to brush maps.

This map provides the highest experience here, so keep brushing on easy difficulty until level 15.

Once you reach level 15, specialize and continue brushing. With good performance evaluations and completing quests simultaneously, you can reach level 16 using the two-day PL points plus weekly PL points.

This is solo brushing.

Currently, I'm researching four-person and two-person team setups. The PL usage differs for four-person, three-person, and two-person teams compared to solo brushing. There should be faster methods.

This is how I level a character to 15 in two days, almost reaching level 16.
