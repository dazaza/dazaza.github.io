I. Assemble a Core Team

No matter how lively a forum is, there are only a few core individuals. Netizens just want to find something fresh to satisfy their curiosity, and they usually won't take the initiative to share interesting things. Therefore, I must assemble a strong team, which is usually composed of administrators and several moderators. Who is responsible for finding topics, who is responsible for maintenance, and who is responsible for creating an active atmosphere must be clearly divided. Especially the work of finding topics must be very meticulous. Only by providing distinctive content from time to time can the website have vitality. This way, when netizens come to the forum, they will become interested and then turn into loyal users.

II. Guide Netizens to Participate in Interaction

The vitality of a forum lies in whether users can participate. However, most netizens are lazy. Even with exciting content, they just watch the show and rarely take the initiative to reply. This requires moderators to guide effectively. For example, I can change my avatar to support posts and intentionally make some controversial comments. This way, when netizens see it, they can't help but express their opinions, making the post popular. Additionally, I can hold floor-kicking activities or set up posts so that replies are visible only after viewing the post. These are all good ways to guide netizens to participate in discussions.

You can conduct activities such as gold coin exchange for phone credit, gold coin exchange for gifts, etc. Under the attraction of "free lunch," members will be more willing to post and reply because at this point, the virtual currency has actual value. IV. Always Care About Users

As the saying goes, everyone has feelings. While caring for and being responsible to users, you will also gain their favor. For example, now that the weather is hot, you can find some good methods for cooling and relieving heat to provide to everyone. A few days ago on Qixi, I could send out a few blessing messages in bulk. Although these are just small concerns, they warm people's hearts and play a significant role in increasing user stickiness.

V. Timely Respond to Members' Posts
