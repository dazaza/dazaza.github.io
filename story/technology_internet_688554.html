5 Key Factors Why Your Business Website's Online Marketing Isn't Working Well

by chaojip57li on 2012-03-03 11:25:20

Nowadays, many traditional small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are venturing into the field of online marketing. Some companies have already tasted the benefits that online marketing has brought to their businesses. However, according to the Caigen Tan Online Marketing Institution, 90% of enterprises are not performing satisfactorily in online marketing. This data is not exaggerated. Even cases that were once hailed as excellent by many training institutions may not be so substantial in reality. Generally speaking, the unsatisfactory performance in online marketing can be summarized into the following situations:

1. No traffic!

2. Traffic but no inquiries!

3. Inquiries but no sales!

So, why does this situation occur? What factors affect the effectiveness of a company's online marketing? After analyzing the online marketing results of many companies, Caigen Tan has summarized the five major factors affecting the effectiveness of corporate online marketing as follows:

1. Having a website, but not a marketing-oriented one

Currently, almost all web development companies claim to offer marketing-oriented website construction services. Imagine this: a company without professional online marketing planners, without planning and strategizing a website from a marketing perspective, sales perspective, or communication perspective, and instead using the same program module to build template websites for clients - how could they possibly create what is called a marketing-oriented website?

A marketing-oriented website refers to one that is planned based on the company's marketing goals. It has good search engine performance and user experience, a complete effect evaluation system, and can organically utilize various means to convert visitors into customers, serving the company's marketing objectives.

Whether a company's website has strong marketing capabilities, good credibility, and communication power is the most basic prerequisite for successful online marketing. In the entire online sales process, customers will ultimately use the website to find the products or services they intend to purchase, eventually finding their way to your doorstep. Therefore, having a website with strong marketing capabilities is crucial for SMEs conducting online marketing.

Compared to ordinary corporate websites, marketing-oriented websites have obvious advantages and characteristics. They comprehensively analyze the company's website from multiple different angles and needs, ultimately integrating various website functions and services, giving it extremely strong marketing functions and value, pursuing ultimate marketing effects.

Currently, almost all web development companies claim to offer marketing-oriented website construction services, but when helping companies build websites, they lack the analysis, planning, and brand product planning stages of online marketing. There are no professional online marketing planners involved; they do not plan or strategize a website from a marketing, sales, or communication perspective. Instead, they only view the website from a technical or visual standpoint, using the same program module to build template websites for clients, merely changing the company logo, name, and redesigning the interface to create another replica! Check if your website falls into this category!

2. Conducting online promotion, but with no results

There are many current methods of online promotion, a dazzling array indeed. Online advertising, Weibo marketing, email marketing, soft article public relations, forum marketing, blog marketing, viral promotion, and more. However, SMEs know very little about how to effectively promote their company websites, only grasping the surface. Although they may feel inspired during certain so-called online marketing training classes, once back home, they revert to their usual routines. They don't know how to effectively adopt suitable promotional methods to promote their company websites, often lacking depth and creativity. Such ineffective promotions, even if done excessively, ultimately equal zero, being both laborious and fruitless.

Frequently, clients complain that even Baidu bidding brings no results, desperately seeking so-called effective promotional methods! In fact, there are only a few promotional methods, and not every method suits you. You need to formulate reasonable and effective promotional plans based on your product, target audience, and different promotional stages! Only what suits you is the best!

3. Extremely low website conversion rates

Many factors influence conversion rates, from the initial planning, strategy, and formulation of your online marketing project and daily promotion operations, to the online sales system and customer service, all of which impact your website's conversion rate.

Many companies, after establishing their corporate websites, simply leave contact addresses or telephone numbers on the site, believing that customers will come knocking. This single, passive conversion method clearly makes it difficult to promptly convert visitors into customers. Important conversion tools such as business opportunity information messages, free callback phone calls, instant chat tools, enterprise map navigation, customer ordering functions for products, etc., are often absent from corporate websites.

Another point is whether your online customer service personnel are professional enough, whether they understand the products and services clearly, whether they possess good online sales techniques and service awareness, and whether they have strong sales guidance thinking! The human factor is crucial!

4. Lack of effective data analysis

Many companies that started their online marketing work relatively early and have dedicated personnel responsible for network promotion are doing quite well in online marketing. Yet, surprisingly, not a single person knows the daily IP visits, average daily consultation volume, or final sales transaction data of their website. They haven’t conducted any statistical monitoring work regarding data, let alone data analysis.

Data statistics and analysis is an extremely important link in each aspect of the online marketing system. Data allows us to identify problems, thereby helping companies adjust strategies, solve problems, and improve the overall operational efficiency of the website. Generally speaking, we mainly focus on SEO data monitoring and analysis, website visit data statistics and analysis, and inquiry and conversion statistics analysis.

5. People doing online marketing, but no true talent

Looking at the entire online marketing system, regardless of which part of the work, ultimately it all comes down to people executing it. Once again, it is emphasized that talent is the key determinant of the success of online marketing.

Thus, for enterprises still unfamiliar with online marketing, facing the surging era of online marketing, questions like what kind of website to build, how to conduct effective online promotion, and how to manage the team, often leave them at a loss, unsure where to start.

Whether it's planning an online marketing project, building a marketing-oriented website, conducting effective online promotion, or managing an efficient online marketing team, it all requires a professional person to handle it. With the rapid development of online marketing, companies' demand for talents in this area has always been greater than supply. Meanwhile, under this trend, truly experienced professionals in this industry are currently few and far between, leading to phenomena such as high-salary poaching within the industry and demands for annual salaries of hundreds of thousands after just 2-3 years of experience.

Of course, besides the above five points, there are many other aspects that also affect the effectiveness of a company's online marketing, such as the planning and strategy formulation of online marketing projects, the completeness of the online marketing system, etc., which Caigen Tan has also introduced before. Corporate online marketing is a highly systematic task, and enterprises must control every link in the process of conducting online marketing to achieve success!
