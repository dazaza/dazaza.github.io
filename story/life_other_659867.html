My Love

This winter, I think of you in the cold. Are you cold? Is where you are the same as where I am? How nice it would be if you were by my side. That way, I could pull your little hand and without hesitation, place it inside my chest, to see your warm smile...

But that's all in my imagination. If we were together, would you want to? Would you want to hide in my arms during these cold days? Would you let me hold you tightly and give you warmth? Would you let me lift you up and spin you around in the snow?...

I don't know. No, maybe I do know, but I'm afraid to face the answer...

I stay in the cold, thinking about you alone with a sense of sadness. Do you know? In my sight, there's only you. My dear, I love you. Why have you forgotten me here? Does my love put too much pressure on you? Or has my love taken away your freedom?...

Look, this cold wind can make one forget to breathe from the chill. No, it shouldn't be said that your indifference and distance made me forget to breathe. And yet, I still wear thin clothes, just as I persist and remain steadfast in my love...

My hands are icy cold, no matter how I try to warm them, they still lack warmth. I thought that if I became numb, my heart wouldn't hurt anymore. But I was wrong; the loneliness of missing you still pains my heart...

Do you know what I both want to do and yet dare not? It's finding out and collecting information about you. I want to because I still deeply love you, loving you means caring about everything about you, hoping you're doing well. I don't dare to because even an intentional or unintentional word about your love or marriage might shatter my heart into pieces instantly...

Actually, I really want to know what the distant place you're in looks like. Why does it attract you so much that you can forget everything about me here, including my kindness, concern, and deep affection for you...

Even if you are far away, my dear, I love you. Can you let me come to where you are? I want to walk through life with you. Even if I can't keep up with your pace, let me come, okay? Because on the road where I chase after you, I can smell your scent, even from afar, which makes me feel happy...

In that familiar scent, I will imagine that we are still together, I will feel that you are still by my side, warming me, giving my heart a place to belong, a place for my feelings to rest...

For you, leaving may seem like a refreshing thing, freedom seems to be all you desire. But you should know that when heaven gave me a whole heart full of love for you, it won't bestow another heart more sincere than mine in this lifetime. Everyone needs peace and warmth in love because people need warmth and security. If you refuse, I'm afraid you will regret it one day...

Whether for yourself or for me, I really don't want us to have a day filled with regrets and guilt. After all, you once loved me, didn't you?...

My dear, I miss you in the cold of winter. My heart hasn't frozen stiff despite the cold, and it still aches unbearably. As time passes, my heart hasn't become numb. You should know that I truly love you and don't want you to merely be a haunting memory in my heart...

It's very cold outside. Are you cold? Would you like a hug or a warm red stove from me? My dear, if we could go back to the first time we met, I could embrace you then. I know that warmth would be enough to withstand the entire winter's cold. If we could love each other for a lifetime, why would we want countless cycles of eternal love? Because we can't be sure or know if there's an afterlife, we can only cherish this life!

Isn't it true? For me, being able to be with you in such happiness and sweetness for a lifetime is enough. What about you?...

Wishing you a warm and peaceful winter.
