Dear, please come back to marry me when you are mature!!!

by showhz811 on 2012-02-27 16:57:07

1. An immature man will call you nicknames like pighead, dear, or fool. A mature man will affectionately call you baby.

2. An immature man will kiss you forcefully, regardless of whether you like it or allow it. A mature man will close his eyes and gently kiss you when the moment is right, allowing you to feel his tender love.

3. An immature man will not care if you want to sleep during phone calls or late-night chats, deciding himself when to hang up. A mature man will continuously ask if you're tired or if it's delaying your other plans, then decide the next step. He might even stay up with you if you don't want to sleep, even if he's tired.

4. An immature man will ask countless questions about what you did today, who you were with, etc., but never use his brain to think about many self-evident issues. A mature man rarely asks too many questions unless it's something they can't figure out or find any clues about. Otherwise, very little escapes their analysis and thinking ability.

5. An immature man will always tell you about others' faults or gossip. A mature man rarely talks badly about others in front of you and instead teaches you some truths.

6. An immature man will have you cook, wash clothes, and give him back rubs. Occasionally, he'll make a few dishes with average taste but criticize your flaws. A mature man will quietly, whenever he has time, make delicious meals for you, hand you fruit, wash clothes and do housework. Even if the food you make doesn't taste good, he'll happily eat it all.

7. An immature man will laugh loudly in public places, disregarding others and playing around with you. A mature man will do his own thing, joyfully and smilingly handling various situations while respecting and caring for you.

8. An immature man will protect you or tell you to leave during fights. A mature man won't fight when you're around but will calmly use his wits.

9. An immature man will drag a group of friends to bars and nightclubs for fun, flirt with beautiful women, and get overly excited. A mature man goes only when necessary, quietly smoking and drinking while chatting with friends.

10. An immature man always has endless questions, wanting to understand you more. A mature man understands you with his heart.

11. An immature man likes drinking and opens a bottle of alcohol wherever he eats. A mature man dislikes drinking and only drinks when necessary.

12. An immature man smokes mostly to pass time or out of habit. A mature man smokes to think or calm himself.

13. An immature man will ask you what to do when facing difficulties. A mature man won't tell you about his problems but will bear them himself.

14. An immature man likes expensive, plain things, pursuing superficial appearances. A mature man prefers quality and tasteful items over ostentatious ones.

15. An immature man likes a luxurious life and sometimes wastes money. A mature man, no matter how rich, is frugal with himself but generous with others, especially with you.

16. An immature man rarely goes home to be with his parents and seldom cares about them. He often does things without considering his parents' feelings. A mature man, even if busy, finds time to go home and accompany his family, buying them the best things. If his parents are sick, he stays by their bedside every day, considering everything from their perspective.

17. An immature man will fight with you over the TV remote. A mature man will watch the shows you like with you.

18. An immature man will force you to like his favorite songs and talk endlessly about them. A mature man listens to his favorite songs but also understands yours. Occasionally, he'll cater to your preferences; seeing your smile makes him happy.

19. An immature man will joke around and hold your hand while driving, constantly looking at you. A mature man drives seriously, tells funny jokes and stories to keep you entertained, and won’t joke around because he values life, especially yours.

20. An immature man will say you're heavy and need to lose weight while holding you, calling you a little pig. A mature man likes to carry you around, saying how light you are and that you don't need to lose weight, letting nature take its course.

21. An immature man will hesitate before buying you what you like and later nag about what he bought you. A mature man will buy it for you without hesitation if you like it. Or he'll secretly buy what you like and surprise you. He loves you and wants to make you happy.

22. An immature man will throw a party to celebrate holidays or birthdays. A mature man will take you to a cozy place to celebrate, just the two of you, creating your own little world.

23. An immature man boasts about his woman and relationship everywhere, regardless of whether you care. A mature man proudly raises his head when you're around. Without saying anything, his friends will know about your relationship. You love him, he loves you, and sometimes love is as simple as that.

24. An immature man likes you to dress flamboyantly so others notice you on the street, and friends and relatives praise your girlfriend for being fashionable and beautiful. A mature man doesn't like you to dress or make up too ostentatiously. They prefer the natural and simple you. Even if you're unmade-up and "ugly," they still think you're the most beautiful.

25. An immature man's love can't withstand tests, lacking depth and persistence. A mature man's love is vast and profound, loving without reservation, yet firm and persistent, like fatherly love. He treasures you as a precious gem.

26. An immature man always wastes time. A mature man feels there isn't enough time to strive and only wants you to be happy.

27. An immature man does many irresponsible things and tries every way to avoid responsibility. A mature man bravely takes on responsibility and won't do anything to hurt you.

28. An immature man, when faced with problems or conflicts, will ask you what to do. A mature man calmly thinks of solutions.

29. An immature man will make you cry and get angry. A mature man will make you laugh, feel comfortable, and be happy.
