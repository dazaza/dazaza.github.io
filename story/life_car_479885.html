The Urgent Need for Industry Standards in the Car Beauty Market

After investigating the car beauty market, most car enthusiasts believe that the car decoration market is very chaotic, with asymmetrical resource and information. It's difficult for car consumers to clearly understand the changes in this market. More importantly, there is a lack of knowledge about cars; they don't know what kind of car beauty services their vehicles should undergo. For example, they are unsure about what kind of car decorations they need and are afraid of being overcharged after making a purchase. This is one of the main reasons why the automotive industry has been stagnant. Many car consumers have called on relevant government departments to establish corresponding industry standards to promote the healthy development of the market.

Urgent Need for the Release of Industry Standards

Facing the situation where consumers cannot distinguish between car beauty products and lack brand recognition, how to guide them out of the chaotic market is crucial for the car beauty and decoration industry. According to a staff member of a car beauty company, most consumers know very little about maintaining, decorating, or repairing their cars. Solving this problem requires not only an improvement in the discernment ability of car owners but also the establishment of industry standards by the entire decoration industry, so that consumers can trust and be willing to buy various decorative items from the car market.

According to relevant authorities, any industry needs to go through a process of moving from chaos to order. This is especially true for the automotive products industry, which is known as a sunrise industry. Under the premise of ensuring profit, it is necessary to fully enhance technical content and industry standards, allowing consumers to consume with confidence. This is the prerequisite for the industry to truly develop sustainably. Achieving this goal will require a process.

For new car owners who have purchased a car within the last year, how much money do they actually spend on their beloved cars during this period, and are they satisfied with these decorative items? To address this, reporters conducted a survey in Hangzhou. For newly car-buying owners, how to decorate their cars becomes a top priority. Whether a car is decorated beautifully directly reflects the owner's personality and taste. More importantly, it gives the owner a sense of safety and comfort.

A Chaotic Market Leaves Owners Doubtful

Although new car owners are willing to invest in their beloved cars, the mixed automotive products market causes many consumers to encounter numerous problems during actual consumption. "I just picked up my car in early July and wanted to tint the windows at the 4S store, but the price was too high, so I had to go to the market," said Mr. Qiu from the Car Beauty Talent Network. "It's hard to tell the quality of window tints on the market. You can't really tell if it's genuine just by looking at it." After comparing many options and still being unable to decide, he eventually got a genuine 3M film through a friend's connection. If there were unified industry standards in the market, consumers wouldn't have to go through such trouble.

"Spending some money is fine as long as the car accessories can attract attention when used on the car," this is the most common response heard during interviews and represents the shared sentiment of car owners. From the surveyed group of over a hundred car owners, the majority spent their money on seat covers, car decals, window tints, glazing, underbody armor, etc., with expenses ranging from thousands to tens of thousands of yuan. Over half spent more than ten thousand yuan, mostly young people under 30 years old.

Ms. Huang, the owner of a BMW, said, "By August 3rd, my car will be a year old. Carefully calculating, I've already spent nearly 10,000 yuan on car decorations." When she first picked up her new car, she went to a decoration shop and added external trim, costing over 10,000 yuan. She then spent another 600 yuan on car window tinting, engine guard plates, and paint sealing. She also frequently adds small accessories, bringing her total spending to over ten thousand yuan. Perhaps your car might cost even more.

From the investigation results, whether in car 4S stores or decoration shops, many consumers have doubts about the quality of the added beauty products and whether the prices are reasonable. Most are afraid of being cheated but also dare not buy something too expensive.
