In the new era's model of face-to-face consulting, with the increasing commercialization of legal affairs, its reach has extended into every corner of daily life. The more we can sell ourselves, the more people who buy our ideas, the greater our chances of success. Not only do salespeople need to understand their products and gain customer recognition, but everyone needs to cultivate this ability. Imagine, if office workers cannot articulate their own ideas, how can they gain their boss's approval? If doctors cannot explain their expertise, how can they gain patient trust? If teachers cannot convey their knowledge, will students follow them?

Nowadays, successfully closing deals has become increasingly difficult. As a salesperson, how much confidence and character do you possess? In the vast sales market, how do you wield your sales magic? Under fierce competition, are you a skilled helmsman or a disheartened casualty? Success follows a path, the key is: do you have the perseverance? Do you possess proficient skills? Do you truly listen to your customers' voices?

Familiar with computer networks, customers can now search online and have a good understanding of your product information. They may even directly order online what they need. Therefore, they no longer seek basic product information or features from you personally. The traditional method of introducing products has been greatly challenged. Customers now care less about what your product does and more about what benefits it brings them and how the sales process makes them feel. Hence, the face-to-face consulting model has emerged.

Not only is it an action, but also an art. In recent years, the economy has generally been sluggish, but have you noticed that there are always a few top-notch salespeople in any company? When most people see their performance plummet, these individuals still maintain several times more performance than others, unaffected by conditions. Why? When everyone sells the same quality products and services at the same price, and even spend the same time visiting customers, why do your results remain lackluster? The reason lies in those high-performing salespeople using the face-to-face consulting model. Modern conditions dictate that the role of personnel must move away from simply selling goods and instead become consumption consultants for customers. You must learn to listen carefully to the customer's voice, uncover their unique needs, fully utilize the special selling points of the product and your personal charm, to effectively stimulate the customer's purchasing desire.

The book draws on the most practical experiences of top salespeople across society, covering all aspects of sales. By making good use of this book, you can attract more customers, achieve record-breaking performance, and double your wealth.
