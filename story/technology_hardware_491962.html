Life is indeed fleeting.

by vghfeifei on 2011-09-20 11:26:18

If someone still holds on, it's because he is a poet; if someone strives to seek, it's because he is young; if someone dedicates his whole life to it, then he is a fool. Distance, it is merely a measure and cannot reach the goal; just like happiness, it will always be elsewhere. People in other places may also be looking at your happiness with longing eyes. To live and struggle hard for a distant place is actually a choice of a way of life. You don't have to be surprised by the arrival of your goal, you can certainly not worry about being elsewhere, and you don't need to dwell on temporary failures and lament over them, because happiness has already taken residence in your heart. In a certain sense, the farthest place is still your own heart, so happiness can only reside in your inner self.

The distant place and the happiness elsewhere in life are all emotions that arise and dissipate under the pressure of reality. The distant place and elsewhere cannot solve a depressed mood, but rather, in long-term habitual living, they form a resistance to the ordinary, a deviation from the rules, and an enticement for self-release.

In everyone's heart, there is a beautiful distant place. No matter how big we grow or how far we go, beautiful things not only swirl in our hearts, but more so in the distant places we cannot reach. You can look afar to the horizon, where the distant reverie lures you, filling you with happy aspirations for tomorrow, for the future, for your upcoming days. This is the comfort that life around you cannot provide, this is the tenderness that the present cannot display.

Elsewhere, can only be someone else's beautiful scenery; elsewhere, is simply a kind of life around you that you fail to notice; elsewhere, opposes our reality firmly and mistakenly. It is not a flower waiting to bloom, it is not a low-hanging ear of wheat. Its touch more often allows humans to have more ideas and vitality, to pursue, to approach, to misperceive, and even to yearn for that distant today, that nearby elsewhere.

Beauty lies in the distance, it is the unfamiliar feeling that gives you its beauty. It is a place you cannot reach, just like how you cannot enter the world of fairy tales, but can only gaze through thick wooden fences at those paradises made of pictures and words. So what is the distant place? Is the beauty within it as you imagine? Is the distant place the beautiful image in your imagination? Design and ideals are always beautiful dreams, and the distant place happens to be a design of human emotions, an ideal destination for the pursuit of life.

Although life is short, the process left for life is very long, with incredibly rich content. However, in this short yet long process, accompanied by experiences of happiness, countless troubles, countless pains, countless humiliations, countless oppressions, and even the soul facing death numerous times, all swirl, settle, renew, and pass by.

Everyone's gaze can feel eternal happiness, forever standing elsewhere. True happiness is elsewhere, the landscapes, the lives, the people elsewhere, and even the emotions elsewhere, seem to stand at a distance, becoming a transcendence of reality, a moisturizing feeling with an appreciative sentiment, all becoming irresistible ideals. We often find our own and pitifully happy lives in others' happiness, find the beautiful outer layer of things after they've been painted, find a sweetness in illusion.

Happiness elsewhere is always a misconception of humanity, whether it be the distant place or elsewhere, distance is an instinctive feeling, it leads human life and errors slowly with a dizziness-inducing halo. The distant elsewhere seeps into us in various ways, including our outings, our marriages, our drugs, and even our peeping into extramarital affairs. In the life around us, because of the rough fairness of the common people's hands, everyone is happy, it's just that you think your happiness is elsewhere, and not in your own heart.

Happiness is the highest level pursued by life, happiness is the greatest reward of life, happiness is the gratitude of each person who has lived in this world towards life, and happiness is also your re-recognition and re-experience of what's around you.

When others admire you, their happiness is also in the distance, that beautiful distant place is you; when they deeply feel the joy of life, your happiness becomes their elsewhere.

Happiness is still elsewhere!
