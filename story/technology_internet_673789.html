Listen to the moving game theme song and sing the classic melody.

by ig27t4e66 on 2012-02-23 21:36:09

Listen to the touching game theme songs, and spread the classic melody. As an indispensable part of the game, game music has a role that other art forms cannot replace in setting the atmosphere of the game and increasing the player's sense of involvement. In the increasingly internationalized game world, music can transcend the limitations of different language environments. The passionate or gentle game background music can faithfully convey the atmosphere of the game to players. Compared with the background music of the game, the theme song of the game sung by singers is easy to spread and can leave a deeper impression on gamers. Perhaps many games have gradually faded out of people's sight, like fleeting clouds, and few people will revisit the game process, but those touching game theme songs will always stay in a corner of the heart. Some games you may forget, but some songs are unforgettable. Some games you may just start to contact, but those wonderful theme songs will make you sigh and marvel. Classic game theme songs have such charming magic that can break through the boundaries of time. Do you still remember the online game "A3" which has stopped operating in mainland China? Many players were fascinated by the magnificent soundtrack of this game. It took one year and cost tens of millions to produce the same-name game soundtrack, making "A3" become the first online game with a theme song in South Korea. Although the game has become a thing of the past, its music is indeed worthy of being called a classic. A Korean theme song "The End of Waiting Is You" is beautifully sentimental. The singer uses a wide vocal range and deep performance to fully express the aesthetic realm of the whole song, deeply touching the hearts of listeners. The song "Perfect World" performed by Shuimu Nianhua on CCTV Spring Festival Gala in 2006 left a deep impression on many listeners. After being broadcast on radio and television stations across the country, it gained the love of a wide range of music fans. This song produced by the well-known Little Sun Music is the same-name theme song of the domestic popular online game "Perfect World". The song breaks the traditional mode of online game theme songs and highlights the characteristics of the game and the real game experience with its beautiful and thought-provoking style. The song gives people a feeling of freely soaring in the sky, and when listening to the music, one can truly feel that dreamlike perfect world. CCR developed and produced "RF Online", which not only has swords and magic but also integrates high-tech mechanical civilization. It is an SF MMORPG game with a vast universe background, reflecting the advanced fantasyism and science fictionism in the world view. The theme song of this online game, "ONE", is passionately performed by South Korean singer Lee Soo-young. The singer's distant voice line harmonizes with the majestic background music, bringing you top-notch auditory enjoyment. Even in everyday life, you can quietly listen to this song. The melodious and fresh voice flows like a clear stream over the heart, and all fatigue and exhaustion will disappear. I believe many people have heard the theme song of "Final Fantasy VIII", "Eyes On Me", performed by Faye Wong. The classic game and the classic song complement each other, emitting a dazzling and intoxicating brilliance. The heavenly voice softly sings in the ear, as if a bright beam of sunlight shines into the heavy darkness, and worldly worries are laughed off. The melodious flute sound rises, letting the listener's heart fly freely. The new version of the online game "Battlefield" is called "Legend of Ice Wind". The game's theme song is very touching, wonderfully performed by South Korean singer Kim Jung-min. It has a strong sense of rhythm, sometimes soft and melodious, making one feel as if they were in another world, and sometimes lively and stirring, awakening the passionate blood hidden deep in your heart, giving the listener a very shocking feeling. "Xenogears" is an RPG game for PS made by Square company. Its world view, plot performance, and music attract many players and have a large number of fans. This sequel "Xenogears II: Beyond Good & Evil" features "SMALL OF TWO PIECES", which is performed with great emotion and elegance, loved by players. The theme song of "Ragnarok Online" has always been favored by players. The Japanese version of the theme song included in the official original soundtrack album has a cute and fresh style, with a smooth and gentle melody. The singer's moving soft voice makes one deeply moved. In addition to the above wonderful theme songs, there are many other classic game songs that are very outstanding, such as "Kingdom Hearts" theme song "Hikari" performed by Hikaru Utada, and the touching theme melody of "Sakura Wars". In the kingdom of music, they occupy an important position. Games become more wonderful because of their existence and get closer to the players' hearts. Game theme songs let us know that games can not only be played and listened to, but also sung with joyous voices.
