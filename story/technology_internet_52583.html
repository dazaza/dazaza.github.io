The first Top Ten Outstanding Entrepreneurial Women

by seosh on 2008-03-11 09:03:35

In the rapid development of Kunming's economy and society, women have played an active role. On the eve of the "March 8th" International Women's Day in 2008, Kunming City selected its first top ten outstanding entrepreneurial women.

Li Haiyan - Vice President of Kunming Huaxi Animal Husbandry Group Co., Ltd.

In 2001, Kunming Huaxi Animal Husbandry Group Co., Ltd., managed by Li Haiyan, became the first company in China to receive an organic food certificate from the EU ECOCERT certification center. Huaxi eggs were the first in Yunnan to obtain a pollution-free certificate, with ecological characteristics ranking first nationwide and egg-laying chicken scale ranking among the top five nationwide. The company is also the most famous poultry farming enterprise in Yunnan, a national poverty alleviation leading enterprise, and a key leading enterprise of agricultural industrialization in Yunnan Province. Li Haiyan has successively received titles such as "National March 8th Red Flag Bearer" and "China Outstanding Entrepreneurial Woman."

Xiao Yan - General Manager of Kunming Fubao Culture City Co., Ltd.

Under Xiao Yan's careful management, Kunming Fubao Packaging Materials Co., Ltd. was developed into one of the top 200 advanced packaging enterprises in China and the largest packaging enterprise in Yunnan Province. Kunming Fubao Culture City has become the largest multi-functional resort in Southwest China in terms of reception scale. The water world of Fubao Culture City holds a Guinness World Record, and "Futian Baodi" is the largest water theater in China, having received more than 5 million Chinese and foreign tourists cumulatively. Xiao Yan has also been awarded titles such as "National Starfire Science and Technology Leader" and "Yunnan March 8th Red Flag Bearer."

Gong Yanqiong - General Manager of Xundian Zhongling Mountain Agricultural Technology Development Co., Ltd.

In 1997, Gong Yanqiong purchased 5700 acres of barren mountains and slopes, registering Xundian Hui and Yi Autonomous County Zhongling Mountain Agricultural Technology Development Co., Ltd. The company's modern chicken farm produces 2 million chicks annually, 250,000 de-temperature chickens, 320,000 commercial chickens, over 10 acres of modern energy-saving drip irrigation flower greenhouses growing cut flowers and high-end potted flowers; also includes a thousand-acre efficient premium fruit planting demonstration base, 1300 acres of economic forest and timber forest; and maintains 2000 acres of natural state-owned forests. Currently, the company owns assets exceeding 20 million yuan, with an annual output value reaching over 9 million yuan. Gong Yanqiong has been honored with titles such as "National March 8th Red Flag Bearer" by the All-China Women's Federation.

Li Meiying - Chairman of Yunnan Xinong Technology Co., Ltd.

From 2001 to 2003, Li Meiying used less than three years to make the company-produced onion essence surpass that of the world production giant USA, becoming the world leader in onion essence. In 2001, the independently developed onion essence extract by Xinong Technology Co., Ltd. obtained a national invention patent and exclusive production rights; in 2004, she formed a large-scale industry with an annual output value, driving tens of thousands of households to grow onions and become wealthy. Within two years, China became the world's largest and only industrialized producer of onion essence. She has received titles such as "Outstanding Private Science and Technology Entrepreneur of the Year."

He Xuekui - Chairman of Yunnan Green Earth Biotechnology Co., Ltd.

He Xuekui founded Yunnan He Kou Green Earth Industry Co., Ltd. in 1996, which is the largest urban greening specialty seedling production and supply enterprise in Yunnan. Through the method of "expropriating or leasing land," she organized the production of flowers and seedlings and adopted the "order agriculture" model to achieve win-win results. To date, she has cumulatively driven more than 3500 households to plant landscaping seedlings and ornamental plants on over 5000 mu, increasing household income by more than 3000 yuan on average, and solving employment for more than 2000 rural surplus laborers. She has received honors such as the "Outstanding Contribution Award for Western Development by the Chinese Association for Science and Technology."

Zhang Yanping - General Manager of Kunming Tangma Fengchi Media Co., Ltd.

At the end of 2003, Zhang Yanping served as the general manager of Fengchi Media. Since 1995, the company has continuously entered the top 50 of China's advertising industry for 12 years, becoming a first-class comprehensive media enterprise with international background, forming a "value convergence, integration of people and enterprise" win-win enterprise management model. At the thirteenth China Advertising Festival in 2006, Fengchi Media won 1 gold, 1 silver, 4 bronze, and 21 shortlisted awards, breaking the "zero" record of the highest award in Yunnan's advertising industry. Zhang Yanping has received titles such as one of the 100 most influential advertising managers in China.

Huang Cuiping - Deputy General Manager of Kunming Wantong Industry (Group) Co., Ltd., General Manager of Minxi Yuan Resort Villa Conference Center

In 1996, Huang Cuiping was appointed as the general manager of Minxi Yuan Resort Villa Conference Center. In early 2004, she led the establishment of "Lijia Biological Development Co., Ltd.", investing over 2.6 million yuan in introducing winter peach varieties in Funing and achieving success, recognized by the Yunnan branch of the China Academy of Sciences as a "Winter Peach Planting Demonstration Garden" and obtaining the "National Green Food Certification." Surrounding farmers have become wealthy due to planting nearly 1000 mu of winter peaches. Currently, Huang Cuiping's enterprises have created over 1200 job opportunities locally.

Chen Jie - Chairman of Kunming Yanzhiling Digital Printing Co., Ltd.

In 2000, Chen Jie registered Kunming Yanzhiling Digital Printing Co., Ltd., being the first to invest 200,000 yuan in importing American Epilog laser engraving machines and Belgian Royal product Easybind VIP spine binding machine VIP cover, producing hardcover books available immediately, leading to a continuous rise in performance. She subsequently purchased three color digital printing machines worth over 2 million yuan and other high-end digital equipment using her own funds, opening six chain stores and a graphic equipment sales company. In 2007, Chen Jie was named an excellent entrepreneur in the provincial printing industry, and the enterprise was awarded the title of "China Quality Service Customer Satisfaction Unit."

Zhao Jianzhong - Chairman of Kunming Jinlong Department Store Co., Ltd.

At the beginning of Jin Ge Department Store, Zhao Jianzhong proposed the business strategy of positioning Jin Ge Department Store in the high-end department store industry and focusing on brand effects, successfully guiding Kunming's consumption pattern and taste to a new concept and higher level. Over the past 10 years, under Zhao Jianzhong's leadership, Jinlong Store of Jin Ge Department Store bravely faced the increasingly fierce competition in the high-end department store industry, continuously analyzing potential risks in enterprise development and implementing countermeasures, making the enterprise a trustworthy, socially recognized, and storm-resistant honest enterprise. She has therefore been awarded the glorious title of "Yunling Female Elite."

Chai Liqiong - Chairman of Yunnan Xin Yi Hong Phosphorus Chemical Co., Ltd.

Chai Liqiong is a seasoned corporate elite who has created provincial top 100 and municipal top 50 enterprises. She owns a modern industrial enterprise with 6 million yuan of fixed assets, an annual output value exceeding 27 million yuan, and cumulative tax payments to the country exceeding 20 million yuan. Currently, the enterprise's annual production capacity reaches over 100,000 tons, with food-grade phosphoric acid exported to Japan. In the past two years, it has cumulatively created profits exceeding 3 million US dollars, paying hundreds of thousands of yuan in national taxes annually, becoming a brilliant gem in the chemical industry of Jinning County. Chai Liqiong has also received titles such as "March 8th Red Flag Bearer."
