Under public anticipation, *The New Story of a Concubine* finally met the audience. Last night, "The New Story" aired on Hunan TV, and not only could the audience watch wonderful episodes, but also the main actors such as Dun Cuizhen, Qiu Xinchi, and Li Sheng appeared as guests on the same night's airing of *Happy Camp*. The evening's *Happy Camp* was seamlessly connected with *The New Story of a Concubine*, building momentum for the premiere and adding popularity to the first night of the show.

On the premiere night, all the creators of the new version gathered together to watch the special episode of *Happy Camp* and the first two episodes of *The New Story of a Concubine*. As a highly anticipated TV series this summer, the new version received attention from all sides even before filming began. Although everyone still fondly remembers the old version, the new adaptation featured an entirely new cast, giving more young actors valuable learning opportunities. Many of the creators were participating in their first formal TV drama, and they cherished the opportunity to be part of such an important production.

After 13 years, the classic is back again. *Happy Camp* took the lead by assembling Gege (Princess) and A-Ge (Prince), causing chaos at Malanpo. The creators who gathered to watch were very excited, and had more expectations for their performances. After the lively opening of *Happy Camp*, the creators frequently interacted with the Happy Family members through Tencent Weibo. Members of the Happy Family, including He Jiong, Xie Na, Li Wei Jia, Wu Xin, and Du Hai Tao, as well as the staff of the Great Base Camp, gave encouragement and support to these newcomers via Weibo, creating a warm atmosphere.

Subsequently, the premiere began, and while watching, the creators participated in three batches of exclusive Tencent micro-interviews. Xiao Yanzi (Li Sheng), Ziwei (Hai Lu), Wu A-Ge (Zhang Rui), Er Kang (Li Jia Hang), Jin Suo (Sun Yao Qi), Han Xiang (Madi Na), Er Tai (Lu Hong), Xiao Jian (Gao Zi Qi), and the new character Benjamin and artistic director He Xiuqiong all interacted with netizens through Tencent Weibo. The number of Weibo posts under the topic "Questioning the New Huanzhu" surged to 100,000 within an hour and then rose to 900,000, showing extremely high popularity.
