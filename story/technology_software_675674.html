Yesterday afternoon, a woman from the Hongxing Community on Bingchang Street in Suzhou's Jin Chang District fell from her fifth-floor home. Her clothes were caught on the triangular iron outside the fourth-floor balcony used for drying clothes, leaving her hanging upside down about 10 meters above the ground. At the critical moment, the owners of three small shops on the street rushed to the fourth floor first, and after about 20 minutes of effort, successfully rescued the woman.

At around two o'clock yesterday afternoon, when the reporter arrived at the scene of the incident, the woman who had fallen from the building had already been sent to the hospital. It was understood that the woman was a resident of Room 503, Building 2, No. 6 Hongxing Community, around 50 years old. Mr. Wang, who runs a rice shop nearby, told reporters that at around 1:30 PM yesterday, he was watching TV inside his store when he suddenly heard someone shouting outside, "Help! Save me!" Upon hearing the cries for help, he rushed out immediately, "I saw a woman hanging outside the fourth-floor balcony across the street, head down and feet up, caught by the triangular iron used for drying clothes." Hearing the cries for help, many residents also came out. More and more people gathered downstairs, and soon, someone recognized the person who had fallen, "Isn't she the resident of the fifth floor? She had a stroke and stayed at home a year ago, how did she fall from the building?"
