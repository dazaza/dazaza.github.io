Wang Yang says that the government's power decentralization requires legal breakthrough, and wishes for support at a higher level

by king3h0a on 2012-03-07 15:07:41

Yesterday morning, at the group discussion of the Guangdong delegation, national people's congress representative Zhong Nanshan and the secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee Wang Yang had a boring discussion on the issue of political system reform. Wang Yang frankly said that the biggest difficulty currently faced by the government in delegating power is involving existing laws and regulations, and he hopes to get support from higher levels, [if the law is an obstacle, we will petition together].

Video: Wang Yang said changing the economic development method needs to strengthen legal protection.

Source: Guangdong TV Station "Guangdong Satellite News"

□ Regarding Government Delegation of Power

Delegation of Power Requires Legal Breakthrough

Yesterday, Zhong Nanshan mentioned in his speech that as of last October, there were 30,535 various social organizations in Guangdong, most of which have not truly played their roles. [The government should clearly define what it should manage and what it should not. Without institutional reform, it is difficult to mobilize the enthusiasm of social organizations.]

The previous day, Guangdong Governor Zhu Xiaodan proposed to start reforming the government approval system and delegate part of the government's power. Currently, all government agencies are reporting projects that can be exempted from approval. In response, Zhong Nanshan asked, [which ones can be reported? Which ones cannot be reported? What is the perspective? Is it based on the size of benefit relations, or the acceptance level above? Is there any possibility of implementation?]

Wang Yang replied that a committee has been established to evaluate which projects are suitable for delegation. The committee includes experts and retired leaders. [The current problem is that laws and regulations stipulate what powers each department has. Each department says, we also want to delegate, but the rules state that this power belongs to me.] He indicated that some legal provisions make departmental interests legalized. [We are currently in contact with relevant departments of the State Council to break through legally.]

Government System Reform Must Be Done

After hearing Wang Yang's explanation, Zhong Nanshan proposed whether it is possible not to rely on the center to break through, but to adopt a bottom-up reform method. [During the 15th Congress, when discussing the nature of the shareholding system, other provinces thought it was a new concept, but Guangdong had already done it long ago.]

Wang Yang stated that the current government system reform is different from the reforms back then, because back then it mainly affected our own interests, while now departmental delegation of power, if poorly handled, could affect central departments, [if Guangdong takes the lead, other provinces will follow, how will you handle it?]

Zhong Nanshan agreed, the transformation of the government is indeed not just Guangdong's matter. [Then how to change? Is it top-down, or first pilot some centers to accumulate experience?]

Wang Yang indicated, [we must do it, but need some departments that can transcend departmental interests to speak up, so that our reform and delegation of power might be smoother.] Wang Yang expressed that as long as it conforms to the "three benefits", [if the law is an obstacle, we will petition together to resolve it. This is a good thing, if some departments are more open-minded, they will allow us to try first. Mutual communication, it will be better later.] He indicated that government delegation of power requires legal changes.

□ Regarding Transformation and Upgrading

Economic Transformation Needs Legal Support

At the group discussion, Wang Yang stated that accelerating the transformation of the economic development model and promoting strategic adjustment of the economic structure must have legal guarantees and support.

Wang Yang recalled that during his visit to Singapore, he asked about transformation experiences, and everyone told him to strengthen vocational skills education and train talents. Currently, the laws and regulations surrounding transformation and upgrading, training talents, are not sound.

In addition to legal establishment, enforcement efforts also need to be strengthened. [If 5-10 years ago foreigners asked us to protect intellectual property, now we ourselves require it. If there is no sound intellectual property protection system, without strict enforcement, transformation and upgrading will fail.]

Questioning Domestic Products Taking Foreign Names

[Dongguan, if it does not change, will become Greece of Guangdong.] Wang Yang used Dongguan as an example, Dongguan is a typical representative of the traditional development model, and with the rise of the RMB exchange rate, rising raw material prices, etc., many enterprises are struggling to continue. This also affects rural residents' income, and many villages now can only rely on debt distribution.

Wang Yang believed that the mindset of rural residents has not been adjusted well, unwilling to endure the pain of transformation. [If no one is willing to bear the pain, it will bring long-term pain.]

At the same time, he questioned why domestic products take foreign names. Wang Yang believed that confidence in domestic brands should be cultivated.

Criticism of Short-Sighted Political Phenomena

Wang Yang believed that a scientific view of achievements needs to be established, criticizing short-sighted political phenomena, [everyone thinks I will only serve for a few years, these few years the speed should not drop, the ranking should not drop, unwilling to do long-term work, not doing basic work.]

He gave an example, why many people believe Jiangsu has great development potential, it is because long-term education has been done well. [I once discussed with foreign institutions, why research and development institutions are located in the Yangtze River Delta instead of the Pearl River Delta, the answer is that the Pearl River Delta can provide enough undergraduates, but lacks master's and doctoral students.]

□ Regarding Meeting Style Reform

Everyone Standing Underneath Cursing in Their Hearts

The Guangdong delegation also talked multiple times about improving meeting styles during yesterday's conference discussion. Ou Guangyuan, director of the Guangdong Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee, suggested that meetings should be simplified.

Wang Yang picked up on the topic, suggesting not to specifically introduce leaders, [sometimes the host introduces one by one, and the leaders have to stand up and bow one by one, too much trouble, the weather in Guangdong is not good, some outdoor activities, everyone stands underneath, actually cursing in their hearts. But this matter must be said by the leader himself, if you don't say it, others dare not not introduce you. If no one introduces, gradually a rule will form.]

He also criticized the phenomenon of leading applause before the meeting ends, [why force others to applaud if they don't want to?] Additionally, he believed that too many meeting souvenirs are unnecessary.
