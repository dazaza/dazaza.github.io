Recommendation rationale: A powerful merchant, representing a strong brand, leading the local market development. Second-line brands have great potential for growth. Potential merchants: Hefei Jinpeng Lighting, Anhui Liangdi Lighting Technology, Hefei Ruizhan Lighting Trading.

Merchant Landscape: Everyone knows the Opple brand in home lighting, creative lighting enriches life, and new lamps are also well-known in Anhui Province. It is understood that Opple operates in Anhui in the form of branch companies, with a very extensive channel network, having more than 20 sales outlets just in Hefei City. Despite the severe economic situation this year, annual sales still exceeded 50 million yuan, decreasing by about 17% compared to the same period in previous years. However, they still achieved excellent results and established a brand impression in the minds of the people. Currently, the high-end home lighting market in Anhui is basically dominated by Opple.

Jia Mei Lighting in Anhui's commercial lighting sector can be considered a network-expanding type brand, with over 80% of its outlets located in county-level markets, focusing on Hefei, Anqing, Wuhu, and Bengbu. After more than three years of development, Jia Mei Lighting has gained a large market share in Anhui, with both its reputation and popularity continuously rising. This year, Jia Mei's performance in Anhui was particularly significant, consistently exceeding targets each month, especially in August when it surpassed its target by 200%, marking a brilliant page in its history. Now, they have already completed 90% of their annual tasks and are expected to reach 15 million yuan, which is the best result Jia Mei has ever achieved in Anhui. The engineering field has also seen substantial development, with great potential.

Kaidi Optoelectronics is also a formidable force in Anhui's market. This year, their LED products were launched, enriching the product categories of dealers and enhancing market competitiveness. Currently, Kaidi's network throughout Anhui Province has penetrated into the county-level markets, equipped with more than 30 sales personnel stationed across the province. Due to regional differences in Anhui, Kaidi Lighting performs exceptionally well in northern Anhui. After more than a year of market competition, Kaidi has gained a high reputation in Anhui.

As in a battlefield, to survive, one must learn to adapt and find new breakthroughs or methods after adapting; otherwise, no matter how many ideas one has, they will all go up in flames.

Brand Landscape

Brand Opinions

Recommendation Rationale: Great influence locally, extensive network coverage, leading in sales volume.

Hefei Huangshi Lighting Co., Ltd. is a company with a 20-year history of lamp operations, originating from Xi'an and taking root in Hefei in 1996. Today, it is a well-known heavyweight dealer nationwide, currently operating first-line brands such as NVC, Xin Telai, Bao Hui, Jin Da, and DTL, with an operating area reaching 3200 square meters and more than 100 employees. Their comfortable shopping environment creates a pleasant atmosphere, highly evaluated by consumers, some of whom even travel from other cities or counties to purchase lamps specifically. In the eyes of other dealers, it is an excellent learning model, driving a portion of dealers to actively renovate their shopping environments and introduce good brands. Up to now, it has become a well-known, reputable, multi-brand, and robust lamp operating enterprise, and has successively been rated as "China's Excellent Lamp Dealer" and "Top 500 Chinese Lamp Dealers" by the Ancient Town Lamp Weekly.

In the leading brands of Hefei's commercial lighting, NVC comes first. Represented by Hefei Huangshi Lighting Co., Ltd., NVC Lighting's network channels in Anhui Province have now covered the county-level markets entirely presented in the form of specialty stores or image stores; annual sales exceed 60 million yuan, becoming the most influential industry brand in Anhui's commercial lighting. Whether in the engineering field, retail market, or channel aspects, NVC has formed a good reputation among consumers and merchants. Some consumers are deeply attached to the NVC brand and would unhesitatingly choose it when purchasing lighting products. NVC lamps can be seen in many malls in Hefei, especially clothing stores, because both malls and clothing stores need lighting effects to match the surrounding environment, creating a comfortable visual shopping environment.

There are approximately 2000 brands in the Hefei market at present, with the better-developed ones including NVC, Opple, Philips, TCL, Hongyan, Zhengtai, etc. After many years of development, the lamp brands in Hefei City have become relatively complete. From the overall development potential of the Hefei lamp market, although the first-line brands have occupied the dominant position, the second-line brands still have a lot of room for development, and they will continue to chase the first-line brands and generate more brand influence.

Chairman Chen Dezhong of Hefei Huangshi Lighting

Anhui Achao Lighting Co., Ltd. has been rolling in the Hefei market for 12 years and is familiar to dealers, especially Chairman Song Jichao. Starting from 2004, he upgraded Achao Lighting's mall, expanded the operating area, increased the number of operating varieties, and created a chain of lamp supermarkets. Currently, Anhui Achao Lighting Co., Ltd. owns five branches in Hefei, Huainan, Wuhu, and Bengbu, as well as one in Hangzhou, operating brands such as Qilang, Best Special, Life·Element, Mingkedar, and Sanxiong·Aurora, with a total operating area of 12,000 square meters. Primarily focused on retail, Anhui Achao Lighting Co., Ltd. established an engineering division last year, thus embarking on the engineering journey, with annual sales exceeding 15 million yuan. It has successively been rated as "China's Excellent Lamp Dealer" and "Top 500 Chinese Lamp Dealers" by the Ancient Town Lamp Weekly.

Recommendation Rationale: They have gained a certain level of fame in the market and show signs of vigorous market development, forming new brand forces in Anhui.
