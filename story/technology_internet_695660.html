**Usage:** Suitable for drilling operations in various complex environments, especially for steel structures. It is particularly applicable to the shipbuilding industry, papermaking industry, bridge construction, machinery installation, steel structure projects, subway construction, large equipment maintenance, power plant repairs, and all pipeline engineering.

**Features:** Compact size, high power, high efficiency, and precise positioning. It can perform drilling operations at different angles by utilizing the magnetic force of the base. When used with core drill bits, the effect is even better. It also solves the problem of cleaning waste slag in pipelines, provides satisfactory results for overlapping steel plate drilling, and effectively handles drilling tasks on stainless steel pipes and plates of a certain thickness.

**Imported Unibor Magnetic Drills** have independently developed various models to meet the needs of different customers:

- **E35N**: The lightest magnetic drill in the import series. Core drilling range: 12-35mm.

- **E50/EQ50**: Economical and practical magnetic drill. Twist drill range: 1-19mm, core drilling range: 12-50mm.

- **E100/EQ100**: Twist drill up to 32mm, core drilling up to 100mm. Known as the "Drilling King" among magnetic drills.

- **ET100/E100T**: A multifunctional magnetic drill capable of both tapping and drilling. Tapping range: M5-M30.

- **E40**: Small variable-speed magnetic drill with two-speed gearshift.

- **E40FR/E40F/R**: Core drilling 12-45mm, tapping 1-20mm with high precision.

- **SE100/E100S**: Magnetic base can rotate 100 degrees left and right, and move forward and backward by 20mm. Capable of twist drilling and core drilling.

- **SET100/E100ST**: Magnetic base can rotate 100 degrees left and right, and move forward and backward by 20mm. A multi-functional magnetic drill with both tapping and drilling capabilities, featuring forward and reverse functions.

- **Airbor**: Pneumatic magnetic drill, suitable for explosion-proof environments. Maximum hole diameter up to 50mm. Operated by air source without electricity. Equipped with a pressure cooling system. Ideal for the petroleum, natural gas, chemical, and mining industries.

- **Hydrobor Huw200**: Hydraulic magnetic drill, suitable for underwater construction and flammable/explosive environments. Powered by a hydraulic pump.
