Dell introduces the Dell Inspiron DUO, a flip-flop tablet

by geekzhang on 2010-09-19 12:04:35

When everyone is crazy for the iPad, various manufacturers have copied the same style of tablet computers, there is nothing new. Dell came up with a very different tablet computer on the IDF this time, it looks like a small laptop, its screen can be rotated directly from the inside, rather than the left and right rotation, so that the tablet computer also has a different play.

 

The Dell Inspiron DUO has the specs of a small laptop, with a 10-inch screen, an Intel Atom 550 processor, and a Windows 7 operating system. The attraction is that the built-in tablet dedicated interface, you can easily let you surf the Internet, see photos, play games, in the video demonstration point can open the "plants vs. zombies", it does not seem to lose the iPad.

In addition to having a better interface than the general tablet computer, by flipping the screen, you can immediately turn the tablet computer into a small laptop with a keyboard, and the flipping method is not like the past is directly rotating the entire screen, but the external frame, only rotating the screen part, this design can make the screen turned over, there is a more beautiful visual sense. It's not like the old boxy screens, which don't even look good when you turn them around.
