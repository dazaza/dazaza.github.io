The green small phone icon is an exclusive symbol for WAPQQ users. Users who log in via WAPQQ can light up this green small phone icon for free. Once a user logs into WAPQQ service even once, they become a prestigious WAPQQ user and the small icon will remain lit for the next three months. This icon is displayed in the new version of QQ client QQ2008 Beta2 and subsequent versions, and does not apply to QQ client versions prior to QQ2008 Beta2.

How to activate the WAPQQ green small phone icon?

Currently, after successfully logging into WAPQQ with a mobile device by entering your QQ number and password, the WAPQQ green small phone icon can be activated.

After logging into WAPQQ, click on your nickname to view your personal QQ information. In the information section, WAPQQ information including the current WAPQQ level and coins will be displayed. As usual, below is the official introduction.

1. What is WAP?

Answer: WAP is mobile internet access. "Wow Pu" is its Chinese pronunciation. To use WAP services, please first confirm that your phone supports WAP services! Then, by visiting Mobile Dream Network or Interactive World, you can browse content as rich and diverse as on the Internet.

2. What specific services does WAP offer?

Answer: WAP services are very diverse, covering multiple aspects such as chatting, entertainment, and lifestyle. For example, WAPQQ online chat, mobile ringtone download, mobile movies, etc.

3. How are WAP services charged?

Answer: WAP service fees will be deducted from your monthly bill. Fees are divided into: information fees and traffic fees. Information fees are charged by the service provider, and traffic fees are charged according to local operator policies.

4. What is WAPQQ service?

Answer: WAPQQ is Tencent's official chat product based on WAP. WAPQQ can not only communicate with mobile friends but also chat freely with QQ client users anytime.

5. Where can I find and use WAPQQ?

Answer: Mobile users: Mobile login Mobile Dream Network->Friendship->QQ Chat

Unicom C-network users: Enter Interactive World->Instant Messaging->QQ Chat

Unicom G-network users: Enter Interactive World->Community->Chat Appointment->QQ Chat

(Suggest adding QQ Chat as a bookmark for easy access next time)

6. Does using WAPQQ require downloading?

Answer: No. As long as your phone can access the internet, no download is required, and there are no restrictions on phone brands.

7. How can I use WAPQQ without a QQ number?

Answer: If you are a new user, please click on "Beginner's Guide (Apply for QQ Number)" on the QQ login page, and our system will assign you a QQ number.

8. What privileges does WAPQQ have?

Answer: WAPQQ offers basic functions such as online QQ chat, applying for QQ numbers, finding new friends, and mobile friends; as well as privileged functions like leveling up, QQ Show, forums, families, and my internal space. Meanwhile, WAPQQ users enjoy the exclusive privilege of a green VIP mobile icon in the new QQ client.

9. After entering my QQ number and password and confirming billing, the page prompts me with "Password Incorrect." What should I do?

Answer: If the prompt says "Password Incorrect," simply re-enter and confirm your QQ number and password, then choose to log in again. This step will not incur additional charges.

All billing information for WAP services will pop up a reminder page, and billing will occur only after the user clicks to confirm.

10. Will the icon go out after being lit? Can it be canceled?

Answer: If the user hasn't logged into WAPQQ services within the last three months, the icon will automatically turn off.

Once a user logs into WAPQQ services, they become a prestigious WAPQQ user, and the small icon will remain lit for the next three months.

This icon cannot be canceled.

For example, if user A lights the icon on June 1, 2008, and if user A does not log into WAPQQ within the next three months, the icon will automatically turn off on September 1, 2008. If the user logs in again on July 12, 2008, the validity period of lighting the icon will be extended until October 12, 2008.

If you don't want to wait three months, or if the icon hasn't turned off after three months, you can call customer service to manually turn off the WAPQQ green small phone icon.

Phone number: 8008309138 (free for members) or 0755-83765566. After connecting, select the Super QQ type, then choose manual assistance, and tell them that it has been three months, so why hasn't the WAPQQ icon turned off yet, and they will manually extinguish it for you.
