Exclusive look inside Facebook's New York office

by anonymous on 2013-09-08 19:52:15

【Sohu IT News】On September 6th, according to foreign media reports, when people think of Facebook's offices, they often envision the Menlo Park campus in California. However, in New York, Facebook's office has rapidly expanded in scale. Initially, there were only dozens of engineers here, but now it has gathered 200 employees. It is reported that Facebook will move out of the Bank of America building in downtown Manhattan and plans to open a new office in SoHo, New York. Before the move, Business Insider specially visited Facebook's New York office. 1. In the corridor leading to Facebook's office, we can see a striking slogan: Proceed and be bold. This slogan is even posted on the walls behind the doors and inside the elevators.

2. Guests need to register using an iPad app. We need to input the name of the person we wish to meet, and the app can display their Facebook photo.

3. Upon entering Facebook's office, a massive signature wall catches the eye immediately.

4. Many celebrities have signed on the wall, such as Madonna, Rihanna, and Jimmy Fallon.

5. Here is the dining area where Facebook employees can enjoy free food and also hold meetings and work.

6. Chinese cuisine

7. On the day of the visit, Facebook had just prepared salmon.

8. Of course, Facebook also offers exquisite desserts.

9. A wide variety of beverages

10. After lunch, two engineers were having a basketball contest. The dining area also provides a ping-pong table.

11. Such slogans can be seen everywhere in Facebook's office. Some are thought-provoking, such as "Done is better than perfect."

12. Some are very interesting, such as "Likes gonna like."

13. This is undoubtedly the best slogan! "Move fast and break things" is Facebook's most popular slogan.

14. Engineers can choose a standing desk. With just a button press, this desk rises.

15. This is Jessica Traynor from Facebook's research team. She is holding a fox plushie, which commemorates the fox family living at Facebook's California campus.

16. Facebook employees can receive office decoration funds. Sometimes, employees pool their funds together and then complete the decoration collaboratively. Facebook calls this "Space Hacking."

17. The letter "F" made from New York subway cards.

18. The carefully crafted Facebook "Like" symbol by the employees.

19. A palm designed by a Brooklyn artist for Facebook. The artist adjusts the surrounding decorations every so often.

20. Graffiti by Facebook employees.

21. Working on soft bean bag chairs is truly a pleasure.

22. During breaks, employees don't forget to play chess. See the skateboard under the table? It's visible all over the office.

23. Every Facebook office has art paintings.

24. He is Facebook's computer repair person, and the Star Wars-themed painting behind him was created by him.

25. Between the engineer offices is a living room, and Facebook also provides drinks.

26. Childhood photos of some Facebook employees hang on the walls of the living room.

27. Automatic vending machines provide items like keyboards, cables, USB drives, etc., which employees can obtain for free.

28. Facebook's New York office can view real-time video from the California headquarters. Through cameras, New York employees can also chat with those in California.

29. Each office is equipped with these large-screen devices. We can browse all employees' Facebook photos and search for their seats.

30. Each Facebook meeting room has its own name, such as this one called "In Appropriate."

31. This one is called "Going Crazy."

32. The best meeting room is called "In Toxicated."

33. The walls between the meeting rooms are beautifully decorated.

34. Due to the rapid expansion of the number of employees, Facebook's New York headquarters had to open an office area on another floor.

35. The new office area still feels quite crowded.
