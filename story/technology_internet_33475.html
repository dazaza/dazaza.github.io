The online free transfer function of the domain name of Wanwang will be online today.

by netbeta on 2007-11-06 18:47:20

China Net.cn (www.www.net.cn) will launch the online free domain name transfer function today.

Users need to first apply for this function online, and after China Net.cn reviews it, they will activate the free transfer permission. After that, users can submit transfers online without having to send documents to China Net.cn every time.

This function is only open to channel users, direct sales users cannot apply.

Specific methods:

1. After logging into China Net.cn, users can find on the left "Product Purchase - Domain Name Service - Online Transfer Application", agree with the online agreement, and apply to activate the online transfer function.

2. Within 7 working days, China Net.cn's review staff will activate the online transfer permission in the BC backend for qualified applicants. (For already activated users, their permissions can also be closed at any time here.)

3. After activating the online transfer, users can click on their domain name, submit a transfer (online transfer) on the left, and it will succeed.

4. After the transfer, the system will send an email to the domain's management contact person, notifying them of this change.

We have noted on the page that users should submit transfer materials within 60 days, which is a post-audit. If users do not submit materials within 60 days, they can still realize the transfer but are not allowed to transfer the domain out of China Net.cn.
