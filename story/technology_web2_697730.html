Foreign gamers propose in high style, creating an unforgettable Lover's Day

No matter at what moment in life, everyone will eventually find their soulmate and walk together into the marriage hall. After a period of interaction, every couple always finds some common ground, some shared language that can spark fireworks. From there, newlyweds who tie the knot might consider having a special wedding. If one of them is a gaming enthusiast, they may face a challenge tougher than any boss or mission in a game—can you guess what it is? Without a doubt, a creative wedding can leave a lifelong impression. For post-80s gamers, many are now starting to enter the marital stage.

Foreign gamers propose in grand style

Consider incorporating gaming elements into your proposal plans. Thanks to gaming, you don't have to rack your brains for elaborate proposals anymore. Try out these slightly creative ideas. However, please note that the prerequisite for implementing these ideas is that your girlfriend must also enjoy gaming; otherwise, prepare for disappointment.

**Halo Cosplay Theme**

Cosplayers can bring people together, embodying characters from games, allowing everyone to act silly without feeling awkward. Playing cosplay can enhance the passion between partners, sometimes beyond your imagination. At the 2011 Anime Expo, in front of RoosterTeeth’s booth, two passionate fans, Sniper1120 and Zonbi, dressed as their favorite characters from Red vs. Blue. When they approached the booth, they performed an impromptu act in front of everyone. At the end of their performance, Sniper1120 removed his helmet, stepped forward, and pulled out a ring. This was undoubtedly a huge surprise for Zonbi, who joyfully accepted the proposal, making this the highlight of the cosplay performance!

**LittleBigPlanet Strategy**

LittleBigPlanet is a very interesting game where players can create their own levels during adventures. Gaming and interaction are never difficult topics. If you've ever played games with someone special, you might want to take advantage of this opportunity. Indeed, someone used this opportunity to make their dream come true. This person is DimmuJed from PSN, who created a special level. Although quite short, he cleverly utilized pop-up questions requiring choices of "YES" or "NO." Fortunately, the goddess of his heart chose "YES," undoubtedly earning the highest score.
