Imagegravy is a free service website related to images, which mainly allows users to upload photos to its server. It is very different from other image hosting sites. Users can get rebates from this ImageGravy image hosting site, that is, make money. ImageGravy has a unique sharing model. Here, you can not only have free image space (some people call this space a gallery), but also when users create image spaces and upload images, as long as you introduce your images to your friends or family members, then their clicks will bring feedback, and you can make money through their clicks.

Making money on ImageGravy is simple, it's about making money based on the click-through rate of your images. The amount of income a user earns in a month is based on the income ImageGravy receives. Once ImageGravy determines the total amount to be distributed, the total amount will be evenly distributed among the number of clicks per image, so users can earn corresponding benefits according to the number of clicks their images receive. For example: In May, ImageGravy decided to allocate 1000 yuan, and the total number of clicks on images in May was 50,000 times, so the revenue per click is 0.02 yuan. Moreover, if the same image is clicked countless times by the same user in one day, it will only be counted as one click. At the same time, no matter how many pictures a user clicks, the system will only record that each user can click up to 5 different pictures from the same person as valid click records.

It is impossible to post pornographic images on the ImageGravy website, otherwise your membership will be revoked. The ImageGravy website is open to the world and allows people from all countries to participate. When the points in your account reach $20, the website will pay you the corresponding dollars. It is worth reminding that no matter which country you are in, they will pay you in dollars. Currently, all payments are made through PayPal (Paypal is a popular free credit card on the Internet now, and it is open to nearly 40 countries including China). Perhaps soon there will be additional payment methods, such as check payments or other ways to pay.
