Three Knights of the Dusty Winds

by lk0hngame on 2009-06-15 02:04:26

At the start of the game, you begin at the ethereal peak. Ascend to level four on the mountain and you can safely descend. Upon reaching Guailai Town, you learn that Yue Xie has been rampaging here. Thus, you come to a cave in the east where you discover him in a secret passage. After defeating him, three rather strong bandits appear. After leveling up a few more times, you head to Baiyu Palace.

Before the great array he has set up, stepping on the white floor tiles will make the black ones turn the screen pitch black, but it is harmless; you just need to remember the path and proceed. The blue ones will deduct 2.0 HP points. You will then see Yue Xia being defeated by a masked person. After a fierce battle, you finally drive him away and pick up two golden arrows.

The messenger of the golden arrow, Gao Qiu, takes one and says he will investigate the truth, then leaves. Chen Ling and Rose also rush to the big lake to meet up. When they arrive, they learn at the inn that the golden arrow messenger was taken to the Baili family. So they go to check it out. It turns out that the golden arrow messenger is indeed Gao Qiu, who after a fierce battle with the three grandmasters, captures a hostage and heads east to Shaolin Temple. However, there's no chance for conversation. After repelling Baili Changxiao and the Shaolin monks, they head to Dahu Town.
