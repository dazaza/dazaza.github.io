Key features:

1. Drive shaft: Universal joints provide a more compact and efficient power transmission mechanism compared to belt drives.

2. Variable angle during rotation: The structure allows for telescoping or single-side extension.

3. Substitute coupling: Universal joints can operate smoothly and reduce assembly time.

4. Substitute bevel gears: Universal joints are smaller in size and offer high processing accuracy without gear difference. They can also connect pneumatic cylinders (AIR CYLINDER).

5. Cost consideration: When selecting any manufacturer's joints, the same conditions should be considered, including purchase cost, additional engineering fees, and installation time.
