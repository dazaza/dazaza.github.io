How can China do without "financial customers"?

by fenqu on 2007-12-16 00:52:17

How could China do without "caike" (financial management enthusiasts)?

After Channel 2 of CCTV reported on the online accounting website yesterday, the traffic to Caike Online Accounting Website (www.coko365.com) has been surging. The number of new registered members yesterday and today has already exceeded 1000. This year, China has experienced a noticeable rise in prices and inflation, so many people are complaining about high consumption and an increasing number of people are becoming "moonlight clan" members. Naturally, the advantages and functions of the Caike Online Accounting Website have become fully apparent.

Caike Online Accounting Website was established on February 10, 2006, making it the earliest online accounting website in China. Initially, the website was named "Wealth Management Ah La". Later, we collectively referred to those who enjoy keeping accounts and managing finances as "caike", and changed the website's name to "Caike Online". We estimate that in the next two years, China will see the rise of a group of people who like to manage their finances through online accounting. The development of this group will be similar to the initial development pattern of blogs. In 2006, there were very few media reports on the online accounting website model and caike. In 2007, more and more newspapers and TV stations began reporting on online financial management models like Caike Online, firmly believing that this emerging network model would attract a large number of young people.

However, after reviewing some website directories and news reports across various fields, I found that not enough attention has been given to this new term "caike". Some media or websites refer to people who like online accounting as "zhangke" ("accounting guests") or "zhangke" ("ledger guests"). Regardless of whether they are called caike, zhangke, or ledger guests, none of these terms have attracted much attention from industry insiders. Currently, only the group of people who enjoy online accounting are quietly enjoying the free online accounting and financial management services provided by Caike Online Accounting Website. They are all people who enjoy keeping accounts and managing finances. From using diaries for bookkeeping in the past, they now use the internet for bookkeeping. Simplicity, convenience, and practicality are the main characteristics and advantages of online accounting websites. Moreover, there are many distinctive services, such as the unique features introduced by Caike Online such as "automatic bookkeeping, multi-account application function, receivables and payables, customer management, project management," etc. Currently, only Caike Online offers these original and distinctive functional services. Traditional diary bookkeeping can only record data but cannot provide timely statistics.

Between 2006 and 2007, many new terms emerged in the network world, such as "weike" (crowdsourcing), "pinke" (group buying), "buke" (bloggers), "podcasters", "juke" (data miners), "hackers", "shaike" (show-offs), "taoke" (online shoppers), "yinke" (print-on-demand users), "photojournalists", "weike" (Wikipedia contributors), "huanke" (barterers), "dingke" (supporters), "xiuke" (performers), "red hackers", "flash animators", "fenke" (fans), "ceke" (testers), and "trial users". However, the term "caike" has been overlooked. Reports by CCTV, Beijing Television, News Morning Post, Hangzhou Evening News, Xinmin Evening News, Quanzhou Evening News, Market Daily, Financial Management Weekly, and many other media outlets indicate that more and more traditional media and traditional individuals are paying attention to and supporting the "caike" group. Why are so many traditional individuals interested in "caike"? Because the caike group involves money, good guidance can help young people learn from others, learn financial management, and can revolutionarily enhance the financial awareness of Chinese people. It is estimated that the next generation will start learning how to manage finances and keep accounts from a young age. This shows the power and development potential of "caike", which should not be ignored by internet professionals.

Currently, this special group of caike has been neglected by internet professionals and has not received due attention. Instead, it has been favored by people in traditional industries, who are enthusiastically reporting on this emerging network model. With the efforts of Caike Online Accounting Website, it is believed that the caike group will grow larger, attracting more people to join online accounting websites and become "caike". Helping more people manage their finances and keep accounts!
