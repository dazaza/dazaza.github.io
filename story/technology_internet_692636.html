Latest oral new question prediction for 2012 by Hangzhou Global IELTS and Bei Shidan

by hzhuanya on 2012-03-06 10:46:34

**Descriptions of a Person (7)**

1. Describe the elderly person you like to talk with (high-frequency topic; examiners are often older and enjoy hearing young people's opinions about seniors).

2. Describe a leader you admire (political, business, sports).

3. Describe two family members who are similar.

4. Describe a person who did something kind for you.

5. Describe a person who gave you something important.

6. Describe a person who taught you some useful skills (such as sports, swimming).

7. Describe a student who studies well.

---

**Descriptions of a Place (14)**

1. Describe a workplace, such as an office, a factory, or a public place (high-frequency topic).

2. Describe a place you want to travel to (high-frequency topic).

3. Describe an interesting place in the city you like.

4. Describe a city you know well and enjoy visiting.

5. Describe a restaurant (high-frequency topic).

6. Describe a place you like to visit outdoors.

7. A shop...

8. Describe a house or apartment you like to live in.

9. Describe a public place.

10. Describe an old building (can also be a historical building).

11. Describe a tourist attraction you want to visit.

12. Describe a helpful website you visited (high-frequency topic).

13. Describe a popular place for swimming.

14. Describe a city you traveled to during your childhood.

**Descriptions of an Object (24)**

1. Describe a book you like to read again (high-frequency topic).

2. Describe a foreign movie (high-frequency topic).

3. Describe a piece of foreign music.

4. Describe an advertisement (high-frequency topic).

5. Describe a wild animal in your country.

6. Describe a gift you like to give to others.

7. Describe a TV program.

8. Describe a family photo.

9. Describe something you would buy if you had enough money.

10. Describe a job you want to do in the future / an ideal job / what you want to do in the future (high-frequency topic).

11. Describe something expensive you bought.

12. Talk about the holiday you like.

13. Describe a letter you received.

14. Describe an old thing.

15. Describe something interesting about another country’s culture (high-frequency topic).

16. Describe a special meal.

17. Describe a product you bought from another country (high-frequency topic).

18. Describe a family photo.

19. Describe something you lost.

20. Describe the food you like.

21. Describe the subject you are interested in.

22. Describe something you like to collect.

23. Describe a piece of furniture in your house that you like (high-frequency topic).

24. Describe something useful that you made.

**Descriptions of an Event (12)**

1. Describe a situation where you were angry (high-frequency topic).

2. Describe a trip you would like to take in the future.

3. Describe a trip you took during your childhood.

4. Describe a positive change in your life (high-frequency topic).

5. Describe the outdoor activities you enjoy.

6. Musical event.

7. Describe a wedding you attended.

8. Describe a trip you experienced during your childhood.

9. Describe the changes in a city or town (high-frequency topic).

10. Talk about the influence of the internet on children's education.

11. Describe a small business you want to start (high-frequency topic).

12. Describe an occasion when others congratulated you (new topic).
