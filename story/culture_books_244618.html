The Growth Experience of a Bandit Chief: Me and My Bandit Grandma Author: Gao He

by jqrxass on 2009-10-01 15:52:25

I lay in the embrace of my mother's corpse, barely alive, when my grandmother adopted me on her way back from a raid. My grandmother was the internal manager for the boss of Dogwa Mountain Bandits. The boss had one wrongdoing - a young woman who was not his wife but slept with him. We called this woman Second Mother. My grandmother forced me to practice shooting and martial arts, and trained me into a highly skilled bandit. After the boss died, I, at fifteen, became the leader of the bandits known as "Little Boss" by chance. When I was tormented by the desires of puberty, Second Mother opened the door to life for me, giving me the most primitive lesson of humanity, making me a real man.

The big landlord "Cannibal" was killed by the boss, his white brain matter splashing everywhere. Li Dongqing, the son of Cannibal, gathered the security teams from three counties and eighteen towns to wipe us out. The boss was killed in battle, and thus began my bloody rivalry with Li Dongqing. The Anti-Japanese War paused our struggle as we united to fight the Japanese. After the Japanese surrendered, we started a new round of fighting...

Part One: Me and My Bandit Grandmother

Before the boss could finish speaking, a burst of gunfire erupted from the eastern hilltop. Bullets pelted around us like hail, kicking up dust and gravel. Someone let out an agonizing cry, clearly hit. Instinctively, we dropped to the ground as bullets whistled above our heads like startled sparrows scattering in all directions. From the western hilltop, someone shouted at us: "Surrender, brothers of Dogwa Mountain! You're surrounded. Surrender, and we'll exchange one gun for ten silver dollars."

Part Two: Me and My Bandit Grandmother

I wore those new shoes, which felt different from the start. It was as if walking became lighter, almost floating. Second Mother saw me off at the door, inviting me to visit whenever I wanted. I said sure, I'd come when I wanted sour soup noodles or to watch a play. When my grandmother saw that I came back with new shoes, she asked where they were from. I said Second Mother made them for me. My grandmother sneered and said, "That sly fox really knows how to act. Why didn't she make you shoes before? Now that you've become the boss, she's trying to seduce you again. Stay away from her; you won't learn anything good from actresses."

Part Three: Me and My Bandit Grandmother

She smiled and said, "You were never a good kid, and now that you've grown up, you've gotten worse." As she spoke, she began unbuttoning her clothes, layer by layer, until there was a bright red bellyband inside. Her white shoulders and chest dazzled me, and suddenly the cool cave room became unbearably hot. She pulled my hand under the red bellyband and pressed it onto her soft breasts: "Come on, Second Mother lets you touch. Touch... it's yours, it's yours... touch..." Her voice trembled violently, unclear, and I saw her eyes close...

Part Four: Me and My Bandit Grandmother

The village was eerily silent, without human voices or even the sounds of chickens and dogs. Li Dongqing led us to a mud house at the edge of the village and said, "Kicking a widow's door and digging up an infertile household's grave are the two most immoral acts. This is Widow Wang's house. She got married less than half a year ago, and her husband died soon after. She has been a widow for over a decade, and the county even erected a chastity arch for her. Now, her over a decade of chastity has been ruined by your men, so today, Little Commander, you must take responsibility." Saying this, he kicked open the door of the mud house. Inside, a woman screamed in shock, and a man cursed angrily, "Damn, have you lived too long? Haven't you seen me sleeping with your mother?"

Part Five: Me and My Bandit Grandmother

I was chatting idly with my grandmother when Li Dagesi, looking like a deflated balloon, gasped and rolled over. He immediately sat down upon seeing me and my grandmother. He had always been in charge of agriculture and animal husbandry at the foot of the mountain, responsible for guarding the perimeter. His sudden personal appearance early in the morning must mean something big happened. My grandmother and I asked simultaneously, "What's wrong? What happened?" "Jap... Japan... Japanese devils..."
