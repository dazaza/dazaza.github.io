MAX-ORT Aging Room Constant Temperature System

by dimazvjink on 2012-02-28 20:49:57

Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room technical parameters 1. Main technical parameters of the aging room: 1.1 Temperature range: Ambient temperature +5℃--60℃ (Ambient temperature +5℃---80℃) 1.2 Temperature fluctuation: ±0.5℃; 1.3 Temperature deviation: Maximum accuracy ±3℃ (can be designed according to customer requirements, under certain conditions, the highest accuracy can reach ±1℃) 1.4 Room size: as required; 1.5 Operation mode: Adjustable temperature, constant operation or program operation (touchscreen program operation optional) 1.6 Installation power: AC~380V; 50 Hz; 1.7 Noise level: ≦75 decibels Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room room structure and material description: 2.1 Wall materials: The body is made of double-sided color steel insulated panels (EPS board, polyurethane board or rock wool board) assembled together. The panel thickness is selected based on both the requirements of the aging room itself and the customer's requirements. The panel thicknesses are available in specifications such as 50mm, 60mm, 70mm, and 100mm. The color steel plate thickness has specifications such as 0.326mm, 0.5mm, etc. The entire body uses a steel frame structure, aluminum profiles, etc., for fixed support. The inside and outside of the body are edged with aluminum profiles or stainless steel plates. (The ground treatment part is determined according to customer requirements), sealant glass glue is applied at the joints, effectively ensuring the sealing and aesthetics of the room. (The combustion performance of the room material meets the provisions of Grade B2 in the "Grading of Combustion Performance of Building Materials". 2.2 Door opening and window design: As per actual requirements; Aging room, constant temperature aging room, high-temperature aging room wind force constant temperature system; The wind force duct system complies with the GB50243-2002 standard "Code for Acceptance of Construction Quality of Ventilation and Air Conditioning Engineering", usually consisting of a circulation system, heating system, and over-temperature exhaust system. The circulation system, over-temperature exhaust system, heating system, and accompanying duct systems are installed on the top of the aging room, not occupying other spaces. The circulation system uses circulation fans and matching ducts for circulation, ensuring uniform temperature in the test area. The heating system uses electric heaters for heating, with the heater placed in the circulation duct on the top of the aging room, surrounded by fireproof insulation material. PID temperature module control is used. After the temperature reaches the required temperature of the product, it automatically adjusts the power of the heater according to the indoor temperature fluctuations, cooperating with the insulation of the insulated panels to make the indoor temperature accurately and stably at the set temperature value. It can raise the room temperature to the set temperature within a specified time. When the temperature rises to the set value, the heater stops heating. The heater has an overheating protection device (EGO). If the customer's own product generates heat, then the overheat exhaust system is used. For small heat generation, automatic negative pressure exhaust using electric louvers is adopted. For large heat generation, low-noise fan exhaust is used, controlled by a frequency converter to adjust its speed. Circulation temperature control process: When starting up, the heater begins to heat. When the temperature reaches the set value, the heater stops heating. Over time, the product area temperature will gradually rise. When the temperature exceeds the upper limit, the exhaust system starts to discharge the overheated gas from the product area to the outdoors. The frequency converter controls the outdoor exhaust fan to operate. When the temperature drops to the lower limit, the fan stops exhausting, and the exhaust system also closes. During the aging of the products, the circulation system remains in a circulating state to ensure even temperature. The entire system has stable performance, precise control, small temperature fluctuations, high balance, and low noise characteristics. Noise treatment: Low-decibel high-quality circulation fans are used, and the ducts are wrapped with 3mm thick asbestos, which is both insulating and reduces noise. According to acoustic principles, all dynamic parts are treated with soft connections using canvas and springs, striving to reduce the noise to the lowest standard.
