Chinese people's own Google Earth - LTEarth

by cacard on 2007-08-09 17:57:53

Official Introduction: "LTEarth is a virtual earth product released on the Internet. It realistically simulates the real world, allowing users to browse high-definition, textured building models in the IE browser."

ZOL Report:

LTEarth is a new release by Lingtu Company, a virtual earth product based on internet publishing. It presents the true terrain and landforms of the world with precise digital 3D modeling. Through this product, users can browse various high-definition, detailed building models in the IE browser.

LTEarth is compact, easy to install, and convenient to use. Users only need to skillfully use their mouse to explore every aspect of the outside world without leaving home. Whether it's crossing provinces, towns, or even countries, LTEarth can handle it all.

Users can get an aerial view of the majestic Mount Everest; freely navigate through the deep and serene Yarlung Zangbo Grand Canyon; or stroll down the bustling Wangfujing Commercial Street. For all these experiences, users can simply use their mouse or keyboard to rotate, tilt, move, zoom in, zoom out, and pull closer to the terrain in LTEarth.

Additionally, the product includes a search function. Users just need to input the destination name in the search box, and the system will quickly fly and locate to the specified place.

Regarding the 3D models, this should be considered one of the key highlights of LTEarth. Every building that appears in LTEarth, from large commercial buildings to small doorways and streets, each scene is captured through on-site photography and then processed manually. Therefore, the architectural shapes on the map are almost identical to those in the real world. While browsing these various buildings, users can also use the mouse to rotate, look up, and look down at them from multiple angles.

Imagine being amidst familiar or unfamiliar clusters of buildings or walking on wide, clean streets, without the usual hustle and bustle of pedestrians and traffic congestion. Instead, you can enjoy a solo exploration, viewing all the landscapes of this world and observing everything before your eyes comprehensively, enjoying fun comparable to computer 3D games. This should be quite a pleasant experience.

In summary, LTEarth is a very good 3D map product. It incorporates the experiences of similar map products while integrating its own advantages and characteristics. For map enthusiasts who prefer 3D formats, it is definitely a product not to be missed. In short: Welcome to join the brand-new world of LTEarth!

System Requirements for LTEarth:

CPU >= P4 2.0GHz

Memory >= 512MB

Graphics Card >= 64MB VRAM, mainstream ATI, GeForce, Intel graphics cards (equivalent to MX400 or above)

Hard Disk Space 2GB

Network Bandwidth >= 1Mbps

Operating System Windows XP Home/Professional, Win2000 Professional

Software Requirements DirectX 9.0

Browser IE6.0

Support Plugin Install LTEarth client plugin
