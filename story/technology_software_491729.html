According to the introduction by the witness

by lieencq09 on 2011-09-19 20:12:36

"I had just taken out my keys when the dog suddenly pounced and bit my left leg," Mr. Zhang recounted, feeling great pain. Instinctively, he kicked his leg, causing the yellow dog to release its bite and instead "jump up and bite my left arm." The owner of a snack shop at the entrance of the farmers' market clearly saw Mr. Zhang shake his left arm, prompting the dog to switch to biting his right arm. When he moved his right arm, the dog then went for his right chest. Every time he moved, the dog would change its target and continued biting him.

This yellow dog was large in size, about one meter long, and could easily rest its front paws on Mr. Zhang's shoulders who is 1.73 meters tall when standing up. Onlookers said that the yellow dog was specially trained as a "fighting dog," with strong attacking power and "a vicious temperament, never letting go once it bites."

After Mr. Zhang took off his short-sleeved shirt, the onlookers couldn't help but admire his courage. In fact, Mr. Zhang had multiple bite wounds on his back, chest, and abdomen, with blood streaming from the wounds.

Mr. Zhang, wearing shorts and a short-sleeve shirt, revealed multiple wounds on his exposed limbs. Among them, the wound on his left calf was both deep and long, still bleeding, causing his entire calf to swell slightly. Additionally, there were several wounds of varying depths on his hands, wrists, and elbows, with the surrounding skin already red and swollen.

Before the incident, many vendors at the Zhangxiang Farmers' Market had seen the yellow dog loitering around the entrance. Mr. Zhang had just come out of the market and walked to his motorcycle near the dog, intending to handle some counterfeit goods.

The yellow dog pounced and wouldn't let go.

After the police restrained the yellow dog, they immediately began an investigation to find the dog's owner but found no leads.

At around 12:30 PM on August 28th, after receiving the rabies vaccine, Mr. Zhang returned to the Zhangxiang Farmers' Market. As soon as people saw him, they surrounded him, showing concern and asking how he was doing.

Around 10 AM on August 28th, at the entrance of the Zhangxiang Farmers' Market in Hefei, a large yellow dog suddenly attacked a man, biting him and causing over ten injuries all over his body. Subsequently, the dog was shot dead by the police at the scene. According to eyewitnesses, this dog had already bitten three people within two days.

Later, Mr. Zhang was sent to the hospital by his family to receive the rabies vaccine, while the yellow dog continued to wander around the entrance of the farmers' market.

"Not long after, a boy of about ten years old passed by the yellow dog, and it pounced again, ready to bite the child. Fortunately, the child managed to dodge," said the snack shop owner. When the yellow dog prepared to attack the boy again, the police arrived in time at the scene. "That dog was even ready to bite the police, but fortunately, the police had batons. One strike and the dog became subdued."

The biting yellow dog was eventually shot dead by the police at around noon. The onlookers finally breathed a sigh of relief. "It was really dangerous. If that child had been targeted by the dog, he might not have survived. This dog's ferocity when biting is truly terrifying!" reported our journalist Wang Liang / text, Zhuo / photo.

On the morning of August 28th, when the reporter arrived at the scene, the biting large yellow dog was lying in front of a supermarket, guarded by the police to prevent anyone from getting too close.

The fruit stall owner inside the farmers' market said that the attacking yellow dog had only appeared these last two days, "loitering around the market entrance, biting anything it sees, refusing to let go once it starts." Other onlookers recalled that on the evening of August 27th, this yellow dog also bit an adult near the roadside close to the farmers' market.

With no means of defense, Mr. Zhang seized the moment when the yellow dog temporarily left him and quickly mounted his motorcycle to escape. However, the yellow dog pounced again, firmly biting onto his leg. Eyewitnesses said that Mr. Zhang rode his motorcycle in circles with the yellow dog hanging from his left leg until the dog finally released its grip. Before Mr. Zhang rode away, the yellow dog jumped up and attacked his back and buttocks.

The police ultimately shot the yellow dog dead with their guns.

The man was left with numerous scars from the bites.

A small cat cautiously approached the yellow dog.

Customers and employees hid in nearby stores, afraid to come out.
