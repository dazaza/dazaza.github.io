Below, we will dissect each level's gameplay for our fellow Pops players.

Level One: The chicks go crazy. In front of the sunny chicken coop, one by one little chicks with polluted green eggshells on their heads approach the Pops warriors step by step. Without hesitation, grip your weapons and fire - taking out 16 little chickens completes the mission. Clearly, teaming up for battle will be twice as effective with half the effort.

Level Two: Escape from the chicken coop. This level is essentially a siege of the chicken coop. Fellow Pops players face formidable enemies in the form of three fat hens guarding the coop. They stand on wooden stakes at different heights, frequently using their incredibly sturdy helmets to block the players' firepower and unleash deadly eggs, egg combos, and straw splashes to attack the players. Therefore, everyone must keep a close eye on the hens' status and seize the moment when they put on their helmets to adjust angles and take them down one by one. Remember, never fly too close to the hens or you'll be pecked to death instantly.

Level Three: Defense of the chicken coop. The main task in this level is to destroy the coop under the fierce attacks of Rooster Skinny Neck and Hen Plump. This will focus on testing the players' accuracy and health defense. On an open field, Skinny Necks and Plump Hens continuously spawn from the damaged coop. Skinny Neck approaches the players step by step while Plump uses long-range attacks to stop the humans. Therefore, while defending against these attacks, we need to seize the opportunity to bombard the coop. Once both states of the coop are completely destroyed, the final level can be smoothly entered.

Level Four: Humans can no longer stop the chickens. This level is a combined operation to kill the boss and rescue the little chickens. Odor Egg Dumb is extremely vicious, using its beak strike, squatting, and omelet scatter techniques to launch single-target attacks on the players; when it takes a certain amount of damage, it moves near the chicken cage and releases even more toxic odor pollution, causing area-of-effect damage to the rescue team. Facing such a powerful enemy, everyone must pay attention to the attack sequence: initially, the chicken cage will be in an invincible state, only by concentrating fire to defeat the boss can the cage be opened to rescue the little chickens.
