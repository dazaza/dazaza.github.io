About Oule

Oule Space has always revolved around the children's cultural and artistic industry, integrating art training, children's audio-visual production, child actor promotion, children's competitions, children's cultural exchange, and children's program production into one. It aims to create a comprehensive platform for children from training, packaging to display. By the autumn of 2010, Oule Space had developed a series of internal teaching materials in areas such as children's musicals, young children's language performance, little host training, and child actor training, and also owned its own performing team - the Magic School Musical Troupe. The Oule Space child actors have participated in the recording of various programs including CCTV's "Big Windmill", "Animation City", "Green Field Adventure", "Big Warehouse", "Samsung Intellectual Express", "Growing Online", "CCTV New Year New Poetry Meeting", "Tree of Wisdom", etc.; Beijing TV's "Happy Take-off", "Seven Colors Light", "Xile Wu"; Shanxi Satellite TV's "Super Boy" etc.; they have also participated in the shooting of TV movies MTV such as "Confucius", "Mars Baby No Problem", "Latitude and Longitude Heaven and Earth", "Ma Xiaotiao", "Green Apple Paradise", "I'm In Charge Today", "Regeneration Edges", "Xun Huisheng", "Kung Fu Kid", CCTV 2010 New Year MTV "Year After Year". The musical drama child actors have performed in many theaters such as "Haidian Theatre" and "Avant-garde Theatre"; the musical drama child actors have also conducted touring performances of children's musical dramas "Spirit Covenant" and "Magic School" in many primary schools and kindergartens in Beijing. The play "Panda侠切断水流" is planned for public performance during the summer vacation of 2011. Oule Space strives to provide the largest and most attractive national platform for each Oule child actor, combining art learning with stage practice, cultivating professional actors in their spare time, and art education can create geniuses!

Oule Faculty:

Oule Space has a group of full-time teachers who are passionate about children's art training. They all graduated from prestigious institutions such as the Central Academy of Drama, Beijing Film Academy, Communication University of China, Beijing Dance Academy, and Central Conservatory of Music. All teachers have rich stage and teaching experience. Most of them have dedicated their lives to children's art education and continue to strive for it! Oule also has a group of active CCTV junior program hosts and child actors serving as guest teachers, who will share their hosting and performance experiences with more young art students!

Winter Vacation Course Introduction:

Magic School Musical Troupe

The highest art integrated course at Oule Space. Comprehensive training in voice, stage, shape, and expression. The musical "Panda Hero Cut Off Water Flow" will be publicly performed in the summer of 2011. There is an opportunity to participate in the winter vacation fragment tour performance. Entrance examination required.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students. New students need to take an entrance test.

Teachers: Teacher Jiajia, Teacher Baobao, Teacher Guan, Teacher Lele, and other top-tier teachers at Oule Space.

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.

TV Hosting in Language Performance Group:

Intermediate and advanced courses in language performance at Oule Space. Utilizing TV camera multimedia teaching, focusing on training TV hosting skills, including psychological training for TV hosts, speaking freely in front of the camera, creating simple TV hosting scripts, and preliminarily understanding TV program types, preparing for opportunities to appear on CCTV and other TV programs. Entrance examination required.

Admission Target: Elementary and middle school students with certain hosting and performance foundation.

Teacher: Teacher Lele

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.

Classic Recitation:

Increase extracurricular reading volume, analyze classic literary works; each student should master no less than 5 classic recitations, select and master 1-2 individual recitation works, preparing for participation in various recitation competitions and TV drama auditions.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students, all new and old students at Oule Space can participate.

Teacher: Teacher Hu (Winner of the First Prize at the "First Xia Qing Cup National Recitation Competition" in 2010 hosted by Central People's Broadcasting Station)

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.

Basic Class for Little Hosts:

Mainly recruiting three types of students:

1. Children who like hosting, reciting, performing, xiangsheng or language performance arts;

2. Children who are introverted and hope to change their personalities and improve social skills;

3. Students hoping to participate in drama specialty selection for junior high school entrance interviews in the future.

After training in the basic class for little hosts, students can join higher-end courses at Oule Space such as the Classic Recitation Class and the Language Performance Art Group, with opportunities to participate in CCTV programs and movie productions.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students. New students need to take an entrance test.

Teacher: Teacher Hu, Teacher Li

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.

Magic Animation:

Magic Animation course teaches children how to draw stories. This course can cultivate children's logical thinking ability, creativity, screenwriting ability, drawing skills, and directing ability. Moreover, Magic Animation is the sister course of the Magic School Musical Troupe, so the advanced Magic Animation class will directly participate in the main creative work of the musical.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students who love animation art and creation.

Teacher: Teacher Baobao, Teacher Chao Ren

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.

Vocal:

Learn and master certain children's music pieces, use scientific vocal methods, correct bad pronunciation habits based on children's age and vocal cord development, improve pitch and rhythm, appropriately expand the vocal range, enhance song expressiveness, have opportunities to participate in national children's song contests, and can take vocal exams.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students who like vocals or hope to improve their pronunciation conditions.

Teacher: Teacher Xiaowen

Class Size: Very small-class teaching, limited to 4-6 students per class.

Movie English:

Lead children to appreciate classic English movies, consolidate through means such as English short plays, recitation, speeches, and English games, improving English listening and speaking abilities in a relaxed environment, understanding and learning foreign culture and movie knowledge. Most importantly, in the highly dramatic movie storylines, stimulate children's interest in English learning, train English sense, accumulate certain vocabulary and grammar, gradually achieving autonomous English learning.

Admission Target: Middle and primary school students who hope to improve their English sense, listening, and speaking abilities.

Teacher: Teacher Mark

Class Size: Small-class teaching, limited to 12 students per class.
