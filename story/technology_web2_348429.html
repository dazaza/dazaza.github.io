A young man in Jiangning, Nanjing found a brand-new mobile phone on the bus. Discovering that the owner of the phone was a beautiful girl, he started to have some strange thoughts. He then proposed to the girl via QQ chat, suggesting she spend the night with him in exchange for her phone back. The girl eventually "agreed." However, after returning the phone, he was immediately shoved into a minivan by five burly men. After being subjected to violence and torture, they also extorted 6000 yuan from him.

On March 28 at around 1 PM, a bruised and battered young man appeared flustered at the Jiangning Science Park Police Station to report an incident. "I was kidnapped and blackmailed for 2000 yuan. They beat me, burned me with cigarette butts, and made me drink...drink..."

The young man reporting the incident was named Jiao. He is 21 years old, from Lianyungang, and works at a company in Jiangning, Nanjing. On March 18, while taking Bus No. 8 to work, Jiao found a new mobile phone on the ground when he got off the bus. Upon returning home, Jiao played with the phone and discovered that its owner was a pretty girl with many sexy pictures inside. This stirred something in Jiao's mind. Coincidentally, the phone had the girl’s mobile QQ account, allowing Jiao to contact the owner, Xiao Li.

"I'll return your phone if you sleep with me." During their chat, Xiao Li asked Jiao to return her phone, and Jiao actually made this demand. Xiao Li initially scolded him harshly. On March 27, during another QQ chat, Xiao Li suddenly agreed and arranged to meet at around 4 PM that afternoon. At 4 PM, Jiao arrived as scheduled near a factory on Wanan East Road in Jiangning and indeed saw the beautiful Xiao Li. "Give me my phone, and I'll immediately find a hotel with you," Jiao said joyfully and promptly returned the phone to Xiao Li. Unexpectedly, shortly after handing over the phone, several unfamiliar men jumped out from a parked minivan and forcibly dragged him into the vehicle.

"You really have some nerve trying to touch our boss's woman!" These men drove Jiao to the foot of Qinglong Mountain in the suburbs, then took him deep into the mountains. It turned out Xiao Li's boyfriend, referred to as the "boss" by these men, had been waiting there all along. They demanded 6000 yuan as "compensation for emotional distress." When Jiao refused, they punched, kicked, and burned his face with cigarettes. "Alright, alright, I have money in my dormitory; here are the keys, go get it..." Jiao handed over a set of keys to them and tried to escape while they were distracted. However, he was quickly caught again. The men stripped Jiao naked, took him to a residential area, and illegally detained him for 16 hours. During this time, they tortured and abused Jiao, forcing him to drink urine and dirty water from a toilet bowl.

Finally, Jiao contacted a friend who transferred 2000 yuan to these men through postal savings. On the morning of March 28 at 11 AM, after receiving the money, these men blindfolded Jiao and dumped him near the Jinbao Market in Jiangning before driving away.

Based on clues provided by Jiao, the police discovered the residential area where Jiao was detained in Baijia Lake Yinxin New Apartments within eight hours. In the early morning of March 29 at 6 AM, officers arrested suspects Du, Zhai, and Tao in Room 205, Building 7 of the neighborhood. Subsequently, two more suspects, Yan and Zhao, were also captured.

Upon investigation, it was revealed that Xiao Li's boyfriend Zheng and Yan were acquainted with some troublemakers. Upon learning that his girlfriend had been subjected to "sexual blackmail," Zheng became furious and conspired with Yan and others to teach Jiao a lesson and make some money in the process. Currently, Yan and four other criminal suspects have been criminally detained, and the police are pursuing Xiao Li and her boyfriend Zheng.
