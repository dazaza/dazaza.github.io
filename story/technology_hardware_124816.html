Absolutely available music album, lyrics synchronization method for MEIZU M8

by liupeng on 2009-02-15 20:26:05

As I mentioned earlier, the M8 is no longer my phone but is instead being used as a 2380 MP4. Although the M8 has disappointed me for a long time in terms of software and phone functionality, its music and movie features are indeed quite good. I didn't pay much attention to the music function before; I just simply copied songs into it and started listening without downloading lyrics or album covers. However, I recently came across an exceptionally excellent music player called "Green Apple Player." This player can automatically download the album cover while listening to songs, intelligently analyze the songs, modify the song's TAG information, download the lyrics, and most importantly, it can automatically synchronize portable devices. This made my M8 much richer. Moreover, I often download funny videos from Youku to watch on my M8, although the battery life is indeed not very good.

Here are the specific operational steps:

1. Download the Green Apple Player.

2. During installation, it will prompt you to connect online to download. Don't worry, there won't be any rogue software.

3. After downloading and installing, there will be an automatic guide that allows you to import songs into the Green Apple Player. There are three different ways to add songs: local disk (batch addition), network, and single MP3 file addition.

4. When you click to play a song, it will automatically download the album cover and lyrics of the song. The album cover will be located in the 'coverimage' directory of the installation folder, with a BMP format and a size of 350x350. If the lyrics prompted by Green Apple are incorrect, you can search for them manually.

5. After plugging in your MP3 or portable hard drive, it will prompt you whether to synchronize. Click agree, and the sidebar on the right side of the player interface will prompt you to select the music directory. You can directly drag the songs from the player interface to the folder on the right. For M8 users, you just need to drag the songs into the Music folder, and the cover will automatically synchronize to the Music/cover folder. The cover format is Album Name.jpg.thm. During synchronization, make sure to select the two options below: synchronize lyrics and change file names. Additionally, if some covers cannot be downloaded, you can create a 480x720 JPG cover yourself, then name it as the album name of the lyrics you want to bind. For example, if you have two songs, "JUST tell me" and "擦肩而过" ("Passing by"), both from the same TV series "Firewall 5788," you can change the album name in the operating system - properties - summary - album name, or modify the ID3 information of the two songs in the M8 and change the album name to the one you want to set as the album cover image. This will automatically bind the two songs.
