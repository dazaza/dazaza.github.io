Super Compound Photorejuvenation: A More Effective Spot Removal Method

However, Super Compound Photorejuvenation (5 photorejuvenation heads + 4 laser heads) allows the selection of different wavelength heads based on various skin issues, effectively removing or lightening sun-induced or worsened spots and pigmentation, leaving the skin flawless and perfect.

Step One: Remove Spots, Brighten the Skin

Light Ablation + Light Explosion: After the photorejuvenation gently ablates superficial spots on the skin, Q-switched laser instantly breaks down stubborn pigments into tiny pigment molecules that are metabolized and excreted from the body, thoroughly removing freckles, sunspots, and other blemishes, restoring the skin’s smoothness and freshness.

Step Two: Remove Red Blood Threads, Brighten the Skin - Light Sheet + Light Dot

The photorejuvenation in sheet form seals the surface network of fine red blood threads, while the laser in dot form seals deeper coarse blood vessels, working together to remove red blood threads, improving dull skin, and reinvigorating bright skin quality.

Step Three: Resolve Roughness, Hydrate the Skin - Light Stimulation + Light Circulation

After the photorejuvenation mildly stimulates collagen production, the laser performs point-by-point circulation, highly stimulating deep blood vessels to promote blood circulation and produce more collagen, solving issues like roughness and laxity, making the skin delicate, smooth, and hydrated.
