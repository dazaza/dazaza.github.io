The Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Trend Launch event was held in Shanghai, marking China as a key region for non-surgical aesthetic procedures. According to the Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Association's survey results, China has become the world's third-largest consumer base for injectable beauty products. Additionally, injectable aesthetics are gradually becoming popular among younger groups - the average age of users has decreased from 45 years old to 30 years old. These phenomena are the reasons why the first Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Trend Launch was chosen to be held in Shanghai, China. Hong Kong actress Hong Xin attended the launch event and shared her beauty insights with the guests on-site, adding more highlights to this beauty feast. Despite being in her forties, Miss Hong Xin, who starred in "The Last Princess", "Rebellious Princess", and "The Eighth Sin", still maintains a youthful figure and flawless skin. When talking about her skincare secrets, Hong Xin stated that maintaining a pleasant mindset, a healthy lifestyle, adequate sleep, and adopting some non-invasive high-tech beauty methods are crucial for resisting the signs of aging. Time is fair, but it tends to favor those who are smart, know how to take care of themselves, and cherish their well-being. She hopes that more women can benefit from these beauty techniques and continue to preserve their youth and beauty into their fifties and sixties.

On December 19, 2010, the Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Trend Launch was grandly held at the Jinmao Grand Hyatt Hotel by the Huangpu River in Shanghai. Experts in non-surgical aesthetics from the Asia-Pacific region and distinguished guests from various sectors were present at the event. The Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Trend Launch exclusively released the 2011 trends in minimally invasive medical aesthetics, revealing the latest international beauty dynamics and cutting-edge high-tech skin treatments. Furthermore, the participation of Hong Kong and Taiwan actress Hong Xin, who shared her beauty insights, added a touch of elegance to the beauty feast.

On December 19, the Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Aesthetic Trend Launch was held in Shanghai, where micro-injection experts and distinguished guests gathered to express their views on the future development direction of micro-aesthetics projects and believed that the plastic surgery industry would move towards "green plastic surgery."

It is reported that this event was the first time the Asia-Pacific Non-Surgical Medical Aesthetic Conference was held in Shanghai. Attending experts expressed their opinions on the development of non-surgical aesthetics and unanimously agreed that in the future, non-invasive, less invasive, faster recovery, and more body-compatible micro-aesthetics would become the direction of development for the plastic surgery and beauty industry. The plastic surgery industry will also move towards "green plastic surgery," offering limitless prospects and space for micro-injection beauty and laser beauty.

Liu Chunlong, the head of the organizing party, Shanghai Tian Da Medical Beauty Hospital, stated that China's micro-aesthetic beauty industry is still in its infancy. Currently, most of the approved injectable beauty fillers in our country are imported products, with limited product varieties. Some beauty salons have also ventured into the medical beauty market involving injections and lasers, which has had an adverse impact on injectable beauty. As the first Sino-Korean plastic surgery cooperation hospital, the designated appearance rescue station of the Ministry of Public Security for acts of justice, and a well-known professional plastic surgery and beauty hospital in Shanghai, Shanghai Tian Da Medical Beauty Hospital will use its own strength to promote international exchanges and cooperation in micro-aesthetics technology through the Asia-Pacific Micro-Aesthetic Development Forum, introduce advanced injection products, and actively promote the development of domestic injection beauty, allowing the public to enjoy safer and more diversified international advanced injection beauty.
