For the second consecutive year, Ergotron China Manufacturing Facility Receives Factory of the Year Award; Ergotron China factory wins the award for two consecutive years.

Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, PRC

Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, China

St. Paul, MN, February 1, 2012

Ergotron's China factory (DGE) received the "2011 Factory of the Year" award for Juzhou District of Shijie Town. The award was presented to the Ergotron Team at the DGE Annual Dinner in January by Secretary Huang Jintian.

Ergotron, one of the 144 companies in this district, was recognized for Management Team Leadership, sustained revenue growth, innovative products, compliance with local laws, environmental concerns and workplace safety.

[We're proud of the expertise and dedication the team brings to manufacturing the highest quality products being used throughout the world,] said Craig Thomas, General Manager of the Ergotron facility in China. [It is a great honor to have the local government and business community recognize this passion for excellence for the second year in a row, and to operate in this outstanding community.]

DGE is certified worldwide in accordance with ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 14001:2004, and is also certified in accordance with OHSAS 18001:1999. The China facility performs 100% end-of-line testing, life-cycle testing on each lot, and a host of other in-process tests, ensuring the highest quality products to all international customers.

Ergotron's Dongguan Gemay Precision Electronics Co., Ltd. (referred to as DGE) won the Excellent Enterprise Award for 2011 in Juzhou Village, Shijie Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province. Secretary Huang Jintian of Juzhou Village Committee presented the award to the Ergotron team at DGE's annual dinner held in January 2012.

Ergotron's China factory, one of the 144 companies in the Juzhou Industrial Zone of Shijie Town, was honored for its excellent management team, continuous revenue growth, innovative products, legal compliance, environmental protection, and workplace safety.

[We are proud of the technical level, contributions, and high-quality products widely used around the globe made by our entire team.] Said Craig Thomas, General Manager of Ergotron's China factory, [It is an honor to be recognized by the local government and business community for two consecutive years, and to operate in such an excellent environment.]

Ergotron's China factory DGE has obtained certifications according to ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System, ISO 14001:2004 Environmental Management System, and OHSAS 18001:1999 Occupational Safety Management System. All products undergo 100% final inspection, alternating load durability tests, and a series of process tests to ensure the delivery of top-quality products to global customers.

To learn about Ergotron and the entire Ergotron portfolio of industry-leading mount and mobility products, visit www.ergotron.com or call 800-888-8458.

About Ergotron

Founded in 1982 by VESA FDMI standard author and visionary Harry Sweere, Ergotron is a global manufacturer of digital display mounting and mobility products, headquartered in Saint Paul, Minnesota, with sales efforts in Amersfoort, the Netherlands, and Singapore. Ergotron's products have been improving the human interface with digital displays for over 25 years. Our history of innovation and passion for differentiation is evidenced in over 37 patents and our growing portfolio of award-winning products for computer monitors, notebooks, and large flat panel displays and TVs. Ergotron products enhance computing wellness, improve workplace productivity and create business process efficiencies, wherever and whenever they're used. Incorporating Ergotron's Constant Force lift and pivot motion technology, wall and desk mount arms, stands, mobile carts, pivots and vertical lifts require less effort to achieve more ergonomic motion, improving the user's experience when viewing any digital display for work or entertainment.
