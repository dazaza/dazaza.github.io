Woman lies about being recorded on a sex video to extort over 100,000 yuan from a one-night-stand partner

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-08-02 14:52:13

Li Hao, over fifty years old, had a stroke of peach blossom luck (a Chinese idiom for romantic or sexual encounters), and his lover in the hotel willingly came to him. Unexpectedly, after their passionate encounter, many troubles followed. Someone claimed that they recorded the entire "exciting moment" and made it into a "sex DVD," using it as leverage for extortion. To protect his family and reputation, Li Hao endured the humiliation, emptied his private savings, and sent three payments totaling 145,000 yuan to the blackmailer, but the extortionist's greed was insatiable.

The careful wife eventually discovered her husband's secret. Not only did she forgive him, but she also encouraged him to report the crime. When the police arrested the suspect, the person standing before Li Hao who initiated the "sex DVD" scheme turned out to be his lover, who initially claimed she was also a victim of extortion.

Trouble began in the hotel

Li Hao is a department leader of a provincial unit, already over fifty years old. In August 2009, he met Xiao Li, a businesswoman in Hefei, during a work-related event. Xiao Li was just over thirty, somewhat attractive, and during their interactions, she sensed that Li Hao was quite wealthy, thus forming the idea to extort money from him.

One afternoon in late August, Xiao Li called Li Hao, inviting him to meet at a hotel on Wangjiang Road where she had already booked a room. Li Hao agreed.

At around 17:00 that afternoon, Li Hao arrived at the hotel as scheduled. Upon entering the room, he found Xiao Li taking a shower. Afterwards, Xiao Li actively seduced Li Hao, and in a moment of weakness, Li Hao had sexual relations with her.

Afterwards, Xiao Li did not make any demands on Li Hao. Excited by the unexpected encounter, Li Hao considered it a stroke of good fortune and didn't think much about it. Both returned home separately.

However, Li Hao didn't realize that this one-time encounter was actually a carefully planned trap by Xiao Li, and he had fallen right into it.

A month later, Li Hao had completely forgotten about the incident when he unexpectedly received a call from Xiao Li. She told him: "Someone sent me a text message saying they recorded everything that happened between us in the hotel, made it into a DVD, and now wants me to transfer 60,000 yuan. If I don't do what they say, they will post the video online."

Facing the "DVD" blackmail, he endured silently while secretly filming recordings for extortion

Just minutes before Xiao Li called, Li Hao also received the same extortion text message.

Startled, Li Hao immediately met with Xiao Li to discuss a strategy. Since both had families, they were extremely worried about the matter being exposed, so they were very fearful of the extortionist. Xiao Li then played the role of a pitiful victim, indicating that she had no idea what to do and would follow Li Hao's lead.

They quickly discussed a plan, and Li Hao suggested that they each give the extortionist 60,000 yuan according to their demands to resolve the matter. Xiao Li agreed. Subsequently, they went together to a bank and transferred the money to the account provided by the extortionist. Xiao Li said she only had 50,000 yuan, so they ended up transferring a total of 110,000 yuan.

After the transfer, the extortionist claimed that they had destroyed the DVD. Although Li Hao felt frustrated, he could only consider it a silent loss, spending money to buy peace.

What he didn’t expect was that two months later, the troubling extortion messages came again, and this time the price was raised to 80,000 yuan.

Li Hao, fearing the exposure of the matter would harm his family, reluctantly complied and transferred another 80,000 yuan. By this point, Li Hao started to resent Xiao Li, blaming her for all the trouble, so he did not consult with her. Additionally, Li Hao had already begun to sense that the situation might not end well, so while agreeing to transfer the money, he also requested the "sex DVD" from the extortionist.

Frequent extortion

A year passed, yet the problem lingered, and all his private savings were depleted

When Li Hao asked for the DVD, the extortionist readily agreed to give it to him, telling him that the DVD was hidden in a locker at a mall near Sanli’an in the provincial capital. The key to the locker was hidden in Room 201 of a certain hotel. Following these instructions, Li Hao smoothly obtained the key, but there was no DVD in the locker. Ultimately, he returned home dejectedly.

Even though he didn’t get the DVD, Li Hao still thought that after transferring the money, the extortionist wouldn’t bother him anymore, considering he had already given 140,000 yuan, plus Xiao Li’s 50,000 yuan, making a total of 190,000 yuan, which was already a substantial amount. What else could they want?

Sure enough, as Li Hao expected, after this incident, the matter seemed to fade away quietly, and the extortionist never contacted him again.

But reality proved that the extortionist’s appetite hadn’t been fully satisfied. After a year of calm, in late July this year, Li Hao once again received that dreaded extortion text message. The extortionist claimed that their child had been admitted to university and demanded that Li Hao send 20,000 yuan as a red envelope.

Li Hao sought advice from Xiao Li, who said she had also received the same message. At this point, Li Hao only had 5,000 yuan left in his "private savings," and all the rest had been extorted. Nevertheless, he still transferred the money. Li Hao transferred 5,000 yuan, and Xiao Li transferred 10,000 yuan.

Confidentiality failure leads to wife encouraging husband to finally report the crime

This time, Li Hao's "confidentiality" efforts failed. His wife, while checking his phone, discovered the extortion text message. Considering Li Hao's recent low mood, his sensitive wife deduced that her husband was hiding something and proceeded to "interrogate" him.

Unable to face his wife, Li Hao ultimately confessed everything. Although his wife was very angry upon learning the truth, she still encouraged Li Hao to report the crime to the authorities. At this point, Li Hao also began to suspect that Xiao Li was involved in the incident.

Encouraged by his wife, Li Hao finally reported the crime.

After receiving the report, the Baghe Second Criminal Investigation Team of the Hefei Municipal Public Security Bureau immediately launched an investigation. Through analysis, they believed that the possibility of a familiar person committing the crime was significant, so they focused on interviewing his acquaintances while investigating the involved accounts.

Through investigations into the account's fund deposits and withdrawals, the police obtained surveillance information of the withdrawal person, who turned out to be a woman.

When Li Hao saw the suspect in the surveillance footage, he was surprised to find that it was Xiao Li.

Li Hao informed the police that he always received the extortion messages simultaneously with Xiao Li, and Xiao Li was always present when he made the transfers. Moreover, Xiao Li had detailed knowledge of his family situation and social relationships.

Repeated extortion turns out to be meticulously planned by the lover

Through further investigations, the police discovered that the person sending the extortion messages was also Xiao Li, and the account holder of the funds Li Hao transferred to was named Wu. The police found Wu and learned that his ID card had once been lost.

Connecting various clues, the police analyzed that it was likely Xiao Li used a picked-up ID card to open a bank account for the purpose of the crime and bought a new phone and SIM card, preparing thoroughly for the crime. This shows that Xiao Li had premeditated her plan of sexual extortion.

On September 6th, the police captured Xiao Li at her workplace and brought her back for review.

In the criminal investigation team office, Xiao Li tried to deny everything, claiming she didn’t commit extortion and was also a victim. However, under irrefutable evidence, she eventually confessed to the entire crime process.

Xiao Li told the police that she was living beyond her means, and her father was seriously ill and urgently needed money. After meeting Li Hao, she felt he was relatively wealthy, so she carefully set a trap to extort money from him. As for the claim of recording sex videos and making DVDs during the extortion, it was merely a scare tactic she used against Li Hao, and it was not actually carried out.

On September 20th, the police submitted a request to the procuratorate to arrest Xiao Li. (Due to privacy concerns, all names in the article are pseudonyms.)
