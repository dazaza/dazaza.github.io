Introduction to the final episode plot of "Snail House":

Since graduating from university and staying in Shanghai, Guo Haiping has been determined to save enough money for a down payment on a house. Haiping had no choice but to let her mother take Ranran back to their hometown for care while she immediately returned to work, continuing to save money to buy a house. Besides savings, Haiping planned to ask both sets of parents for some money, but they still lacked 20,000 yuan. She had no choice but to ask her younger sister, Guo Haezao. The two sisters, seven years apart in age, had always supported each other without distinction. Without hesitation, Haezao gave her only 8,000 yuan in savings to her sister, and when it wasn't enough, she asked her boyfriend Xiao Bei to contribute the money they had saved for their wedding. On one hand, he hesitated to ask them again, but on the other hand, his wife urged him repeatedly, reminding him that paying the down payment would free them from their housing troubles. In the end, Su Chun didn't ask his family for money. Instead, the usually honest and straightforward man chose to borrow high-interest loans from friends, concealing the source of the funds from Haiping.
