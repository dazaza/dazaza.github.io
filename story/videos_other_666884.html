Simple Breast Enlargement Recipe: Prawn, Silkweed and Winter Melon Soup

Ingredients: 2 prawns, 10g silkweed, 6g silkweed vine, a small amount of green onion segments, ginger threads, fine salt, and sesame oil.

Properties:

It also contains Vitamin A, Vitamin B1, Vitamin B2, etc., making it a delicious and highly nutritious premium aquatic product. Cooking Method:

(1) Wash the prawns and remove the veins.

(3) Place a pot on the fire, pour in an appropriate amount of water, bring to a boil over high heat, then add the prawns, silkweed, and winter melon vines. After simmering for a moment, add the green onion segments, ginger threads, and fine salt. Switch to medium heat and simmer until the prawns are almost cooked. Add a small amount of sesame oil, bring to a boil again, and the soup is ready to eat.

2. Blood Nourishing Breast Enlargement Stone Fish and Tofu Soup

Ingredients: 500g stone fish, 200g tofu, 100g lettuce, 3 slices of ginger, an appropriate amount of fine salt, pepper powder, salad oil, and Shaoxing wine.

Properties:

Stone Fish: Also known as tiger fish, it is a poisonous fish with a thick and round body with many tumor-like protrusions. Its body is earthy yellow or orange-yellow, with eyes located on its back, very small, and a deep indentation below the eyes. The poison glands of the stone fish are located at the base of the dorsal fin. If people are accidentally pricked by the fins, the toxin will quickly invade the body, causing swelling, pain, convulsions, or coma. Although the stone fish is poisonous, its flesh is tender and without fine bones, making it highly nutritious. It has the functions of generating body fluids, moistening the lungs, beautifying, and nourishing the complexion.

Cooking Method:

(1) Remove the skin and internal organs of the stone fish, wash clean, and drain the water.

(2) Rinse the tofu with water and cut into strips, set aside.

(3) Pour an appropriate amount of salad oil into the pot, heat to five-tenths hot, then add the stone fish. Fry thoroughly on both sides, then add ginger slices, tofu strips, Shaoxing wine, fine salt, and a little water. Cover the pot and stew until the soup turns white. Add lettuce and pepper powder, cook for a short while, and the dish is ready to serve.

Eating Method: Serve as a side dish with meals. Drink the soup and eat the meat, twice daily, each time 150 milliliters.

Effect: This soup has the effects of replenishing brain marrow, dispelling wind, calming the mind, nourishing blood, and breast enlargement. It is especially suitable for those with insufficient kidney yin and blood deficiency leading to poor breast development, flat and unfulfilled breasts, or insufficient milk production after childbirth.

Note: The stone fish is poisonous. It is best to seek professional help when handling it or purchase pre-processed stone fish for cooking.
