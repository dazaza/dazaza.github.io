Zhang Han: Zheng and I are not boyfriend and girlfriend

"For example, in 'Meteor Shower 1', in the scene where Chu Yuqin declared war on Yun Hai, I thought at the time that Xiaoshuang's acting style didn't need to be so angry, but she just wouldn't listen. The final effect was even more angry than I imagined. At that time, I thought, it was probably because she wanted to vent her dissatisfaction with me." When mentioning the friction between them back then, Zhang Han still remembered it vividly.

She just regards me as an older brother

Zhang Han frankly admitted that the turning point in his relationship with Zheng Shuang came from 'Meteor Shower 2'. Before shooting, the more they discussed during the company's script discussion meeting, the more they got along well. They were so excited that after all the other main creative staff left, they stayed behind to discuss the details of the performance. "That day we had a very sincere exchange, including how to act in the play, how we should cooperate, etc. After the discussion, we also had lunch with the director, hehe. Later, we slowly began to understand each other, began to think that the other person is actually a very nice person, and became very good friends."

Very much into filming 'Meteor Shower 2'

"Perhaps because of the lack of chemistry, everyone who watched 'Meteor Shower 1' would feel that there was always something missing between me and her, but in 'Meteor Shower 2' it will be better. Although the script for 'Meteor Shower 2' is not as good as the first part, the chemistry between Xiaoshuang and me should make up for some of the shortcomings." Zhang Han was confident about the emotional scenes between him and Zheng Shuang in 'Meteor Shower 2'. When talking about the most impressive scene he acted with Zheng Shuang, Zhang Han recalled: "It was a crying scene before Yun Hai and Chu Yuqin broke up. That episode should not have been broadcast on TV yet. That scene was also the one where I was most into it, because I felt that Xiaoshuang had improved. You know, when we study acting, our teachers taught us in school that we must trust each other when acting. Before that, Xiaoshuang always seemed confused, and many times she didn't know how to act. But in that scene, Xiaoshuang really let go. I could clearly feel her trust in me, so I acted happily."
