1. Short-term opportunities at the daily price limit down supported by technical support levels

Technical support levels are not accidental and cannot be formed overnight, thus making it difficult for them to be broken easily. Generally, when the stock price retreats to a technical support level, there will be a large influx of buy orders, which usually prompts the stock price to bounce back. Therefore, when the stock price approaches the support zone, investors can actively enter to capture the rebound, thereby creating profit opportunities.
