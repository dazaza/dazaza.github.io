Three Essential Sets of Clothing for Fashionable Expectant Mothers

This year, there are especially many expectant mothers among the ranks of new moms, and maternity wear has become the best gift that many friends or relatives give to expectant mothers. After visiting several markets, it was found that although there are many expectant mothers this year, maternity clothing brands are somewhat scarce. However, expectant mothers don't have to worry about falling out of fashion because they can become fashionable pregnant women with just a few small accessories.

[Correcting Misconceptions] Spending money on maternity clothes is too wasteful

Some expectant mothers believe that pregnancy only lasts ten months, and spending money on maternity clothes is too wasteful. Therefore, they first consider the price, regardless of fabric and style, as long as it's cheap. This is the biggest misconception. When purchasing maternity clothes, the safety and health of the fabric should be considered first, which is the most important.

[Market Discovery] Maternity Clothes Price Around 400 Yuan

Yesterday, I visited several malls in Xidan. Here, maternity clothes are mostly concentrated in the underwear counters. From here, I learned that current maternity clothes are generally divided into four types: casual home maternity wear, professional maternity wear, maternity formal wear, and anti-radiation maternity wear. And these maternity clothes are not cheap either, generally around 400 yuan, with some reaching more than 600 yuan.

Style and color are more fashionable

In people's minds, expectant mothers always give the impression of being elegant and dignified, and whatever they wear doesn't seem appropriate. However, the new styles of maternity wear will make expectant mothers look more beautiful and charming. Fashion no longer belongs solely to young girls; expectant mothers have also joined the ranks of fashionable women.

Nowadays, maternity wear has changed from the simple baggy and dragging styles of the past, which had problems such as being too loose when the baby bump was small and too tight when it was large. It now fully considers the physiological changes during pregnancy and is designed with adjustable waistbands, making it suitable for expectant mothers of all stages.

[Shopping Tips] Comfort and breathability are most important for maternity wear

Most pregnant women prefer overalls, which are the main garments in their wardrobes. This is because overalls are soft to the touch, easy to wear, and can be worn for a longer period of time. Overalls basically possess the characteristics that maternity wear should have, such as a loose abdomen and good comfort. However, since it is already summer by May, many saleswomen from various maternity wear brands told me that simplicity, clarity, and generosity should be pursued. As the body shape changes, the clothes should be loose and not tight around the waist. During this season, it is best to choose comfortable and breathable maternity wear, such as dresses, half-length tops, or nine-tenths pants. Overalls are more suitable for wearing in spring and autumn.

Cotton products are the best

For expectant mothers, pure natural material maternity wear is the best choice. The skin becomes sensitive during pregnancy, and frequent contact with synthetic fibers can easily cause allergies. Therefore, undergarments must be made of cotton or silk, and the style should be loose and comfortable to wear. Tight-fitting clothes should be avoided, and the waistband should not be tightened excessively to prevent pressure on the abdomen, which could affect the normal development of the fetus. Under external pressure, it can lead to fetal bone deformities, poor development of tissues and organs, and malposition. At the same time, it will make the pregnant woman's figure appear even bulkier.

Warm colors sell the best

In many specialty stores, I also noticed that maternity wear comes in neutral beige and bright pink colors. After visiting several specialty stores, I learned that colorful maternity wear is more popular among expectant mothers.

A light pink nine-tenths pants paired with white embroidered casual wear exudes warmth and femininity. If the expectant mother's complexion is not good, it is recommended to choose light and brighter colors to enhance the complexion. Women who usually do not wear bright colors can definitely try them during this time. These colors not only make the pregnant mother's complexion look rosy but also healthy and energetic.

Three essential sets for fashionable expectant mothers

Casual Wear - Sleeveless Dresses Are Preferred

Wearing sleeveless dresses in summer is preferred as they can be styled with various decorations to create handsome outfits. In other seasons, lightweight and convenient jumpsuits are indispensable in daily life for pregnant women, making them look extremely playful and adorable.

Professional Wear - A-line Shapes Are Best

Today, most working expectant mothers continue to work during their pregnancies. They need to maintain their professional image in office buildings and in front of clients. Therefore, professional maternity wear is being accepted and worn by more expectant mothers.

In the workplace, choose styles that beautifully highlight the bust line while minimizing the prominence of the protruding abdomen, such as an A-line shape that is smaller on top and larger at the bottom, giving the clothing a three-dimensional silhouette. Carrying a beautiful leather bag and wearing appropriate accessories can achieve a "finishing touch" effect.

Formal Wear - Preferably High-elastic Long Dresses

Expectant mothers should also prepare a formal maternity dress suitable for their identity and style, allowing them to remain charming during pregnancy. Formal wear should not be loose; instead, high-elasticity long formal dresses are preferable. Pregnancy formal wear differs from regular styles, being elegant and generous without restricting movement. Pregnant women exude a noble aura, making it your best evening gown.

[Fashion Trend] High-waist Dresses Are Popular This Year

Besides purchasing maternity wear, high-waisted skirts have also become the latest trend this year.

Matching Rule 1: Men's Shirts + Skirts

To ensure comfort, you can buy a men's shirt with buttoned collar points during pregnancy. These shirts are typically made of high-count cotton or Oxford cloth, making them relatively loose and suitable for the pregnant figure, providing comfortable wear. Moreover, shirts are easy to match. Pair them with a skirt and add a jacket for a professional work outfit, or combine them with a loose suspender pants for a casual weekend look. Purchase some soft and comfortable elastic knitwear, such as belted jackets, knit pants, and knit dresses, which can be matched with other styles or worn alone.

Matching Rule 2: Dresses

Your wardrobe should have at least one maternity dress suitable for social occasions. For example, a luxurious velvet dress or a finely crafted satin blouse paired with slim black pants. When purchasing clothes, don't worry about stylish cuts appearing sexy; a slightly revealing neckline dress enhances your feminine charm.

During pregnancy, the size of the breasts gradually increases, so it is necessary to purchase several bras that can accommodate and support the chest. It is best to choose ones made of cotton, which feels soft, breathable, and comfortable.
