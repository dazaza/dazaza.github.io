Jiangxi experiences continuous drizzle, with rainfall since March reaching five times the usual amount. Weather Forecast News Center, Sina.com

by p1a2c3x4 on 2012-03-08 23:02:03

From 8:00 on August 8th to 8:00 on August 9th, northern Jiangxi will have cloudy skies with light rain and localized moderate rain, central and southern Jiangxi will experience light to moderate rain with localized heavy rain. The Jiangxi Meteorological Observatory predicts that over the next three days, affected by cold and warm air masses, Jiangxi will continue to have overcast and rainy weather. The province's rainfall will be between 10-30 millimeters, with localized areas reaching 40-60 millimeters. Temperatures will also drop by 2-4℃. Specific forecasts are as follows:

According to China Weather Network reports, recently, persistent overcast and rainy weather in Jiangxi has affected crop growth. It is predicted that over the next three days, Jiangxi will still experience overcast and rainy weather with insufficient sunlight, and temperatures will also decrease. Farmers are advised to strengthen field management and take protective measures.

The continuous rainy weather has resulted in insufficient sunlight for crops, affecting the normal photosynthesis of vegetables. Coupled with low temperatures and high humidity, some vegetable root systems have shown signs of poor growth and delayed seedling emergence. According to surveys, currently, vegetable growth in Longnan County, Jiangxi, has slowed down, with the growing period delayed by 20-30 days. In the open-air vegetable bases of Congyu and Yangzi Zhou in Nanchang City, waterlogging is severe, and seedlings that have been sown have emerged late and unevenly. The replanting index of leafy vegetables has decreased, with some showing symptoms of root rot, leaf rot, and dead seedlings. Due to high humidity inside greenhouses, vegetable seedlings are growing slowly, the market time has been delayed by 15-20 days, and yields have decreased. Some greenhouses have experienced soft rot diseases.

Since the continuous overcast and rainy weather in Jiangxi Province will persist, experts suggest that farmers continue to do a good job in clearing ditches and draining water, digging deep trenches outside vegetable greenhouses to reduce soil moisture inside the greenhouse; strengthen patrols of greenhouse facilities and promptly reinforce any loosened structures; keep poultry breeding sheds dry and do a good job in epidemic prevention and control; appropriately increase lighting in egg-laying chicken and duck breeding sheds; early rice planting areas should quickly prepare seeds, fertilizers, and other materials, and timely cultivate and soak the seedling fields.

Since March 1st, Jiangxi Province has experienced persistent overcast and rainy weather. By 7:00 AM on March 7th, the average precipitation in the province was 147 millimeters, which is five times the average precipitation during the same period in normal years. From 8:00 AM on April 4th to 8:00 AM on April 5th, 60 counties and cities experienced heavy rain. The daily range of heavy rain was the widest and involved the most stations since complete meteorological records began, with Fan County in Henan Province experiencing a 30-day weather forecast.

From 8:00 AM on September 9th to 8:00 AM on September 10th, the whole province will have cloudy skies with light showers, with localized moderate rain in the south.

From 8:00 AM on July 7th to 8:00 AM on July 8th, the entire province will have overcast skies with light to moderate rain, with localized heavy rain in the east and south.
