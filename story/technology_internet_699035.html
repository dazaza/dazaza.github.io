Tunneling through mountains and across water to build expressways, the fourth ring road of Fuzhou is about to emerge.

by abczxlnt on 2012-03-09 12:55:44

Starting in Baitong in the west, spanning the Min River, the Fuzhou Ring Expressway Minhou section goes through mountains and waters, opening a fast large channel connecting the urban area of Fuzhou city to Minhou county. Starting from Xindian, it takes less than half an hour to reach the county seat of Minhou, saving 40 kilometers for trips from Nanping to Lianjiang and Ningde... The completion and opening of the Minhou section of the ring expressway brings not only speed but also fully closes the 100-kilometer-long Fuzhou Ring Expressway!

The northwest section of the Fuzhou Ring Expressway is part of the national expressway network, starting in Bailong Village, Zhufei Town, Minhou County, crossing the Min River, and ending at Yangmen, Lianjiang County. Among them, the first phase of the northwest section of the ring expressway (Tongpan-Xiling interchange to Yangmen section) was opened to traffic on National Day in 2010.

Today, the second phase of the northwest section of the ring expressway, namely the Minhou section, which is 12.99 kilometers long with six lanes in both directions, designed for a speed of 100 kilometers per hour, has been opened. The Minhou section starts in Bailong Village, Zhufei Township, Minhou County, with the starting point setting up the Bailong hub interchange connected to the Fuyin Expressway, passing through Baitou Village, Jingxi Town, then under the Xiangpu Railway at Hengqi, setting up the Fuzhou (Jingxi) toll station, then turning southeastward, passing near the Fuzhou Grain Wholesale Trading Market, and finally connecting with the Xiling interchange of the ring expressway.

[After the full line is completed, it will take only about ten minutes by car from the provincial sports center in the urban area to Jingxi, Minhou.] According to relevant persons in charge of the Ring Expressway Company, citizens can enter the northwest section of the ring expressway through Beidi North Road, Fufei Road, and Gan Hong Road, and in the future, they can also enter through Tongpan Road. Fuzhou has thus added a convenient channel out of the city, taking less than half an hour by car from the Xindian interchange to the county seat of Minhou, Ganshuai.

At the same time, the Minhou section of the ring expressway also has an entrance and exit in Jingxi, bringing Jingxi into the embrace of the mother city of Fuzhou, promoting the development of small towns in this area.

Impact: The high-speed road network of Rongcheng "rings" up

On National Day in 2010, the second phase of the Fuzhou Airport Expressway was completed and opened to traffic, connecting with the Shenhai Expressway (Qinkou to Mawei) and the Fuyin Expressway (Bailong to Qinkou), preliminarily forming the prototype of the Fuzhou Ring Expressway. With the completion and opening of the Minhou section of the ring expressway today, the last [horizontal] section of the Fuzhou Ring Expressway has been completed, achieving closure.

So, what impact will the highway weaving into a [ring] have on Fuzhou's transportation? Industry insiders in Fuzhou's transportation sector stated that the official closure of the ring expressway means, on one hand, that transit vehicles can be transferred without entering the city, reducing traffic pressure in the central urban area; on the other hand, vehicles in the urban area can also quickly go out through the circular radiating expressway network.

Before the ring expressway was opened, vehicles traveling from Nanping and Sanming to Lianjiang, Luoyuan, and Ningde had to detour through the urban area of Fuzhou City towards Mawei. This detour route was longer, slower, and also increased the traffic pressure in the urban area. After the full line of the northwest section of the ring expressway is opened, vehicles traveling from Nanping and Sanming to Lianjiang, Luoyuan, and Ningde can take the northwest section of the ring expressway via the Bailong hub interchange in Minhou, go straight ahead, pass through the Guixin Tunnel, and soon reach the Yangmen hub interchange of the Shenhai Expressway, shortening the journey by about 40 kilometers.

Meanwhile, the airport expressway, Shenhai Expressway, and Fuyin Expressway have all been completed and opened to traffic, and can be quickly reached via the ring expressway to Chang乐 Airport, Quanzhou, Jiangsu-Zhejiang, and Sanming areas. The under-construction Fuyong Expressway and Jing-Tai Expressway also reserve interchanges to connect with the ring expressway.

Vision: Expanding eastward and southward, along the river to the sea

According to our city's "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" expressway development plan, our city will basically form a "two rings eight radiations ten links" expressway network layout structure, realizing expressways reaching every county.

The "two rings" refer to the ring expressway and the circumferential expressway. The first ring of the expressway network - the ring expressway has already achieved closure, and after the southeast section of the circumferential expressway is completed and opened to traffic, the second ring of Fuzhou's expressways - the circumferential expressway will also be fully closed.

According to the plan, the main line of the southeast section of the circumferential expressway starts at Yangmen in Lianjiang, connects with the Yangmen hub interchange of the northwest section of the Fuzhou circumferential expressway, intersects with the Shenhai Expressway, passes through Lianjiang, Langqi, Chang乐 to the terminal Minhou Qingkou, and connects with the Qingkou hub interchange of the Shenhai Expressway, with a total length of 73.81 kilometers.

The southeast section of the Fuzhou circumferential expressway connects northward with the northwest section of the circumferential expressway and southward with the Fuyin Expressway Bai Long to Qingkou section, closing to form a circumferential expressway with a total length of about 160 kilometers. Therefore, some industry insiders also refer to the circumferential expressway as Fuzhou's "fourth ring".

Connecting the urban area with Lianjiang, Minhou, Chang乐 and other places, it also incorporates the Langqi Economic Zone into the central city planning area. According to industry insiders, the "fourth ring" will also communicate with the Luoyuan Bay Port Area and Chang乐 International Airport, not only alleviating the traffic pressure of the Fuzhou overpass section of the Shenhai Expressway, but also having important significance for promoting Fuzhou's "eastward expansion and southward advance, along the river to the sea" development strategy, improving port collection and distribution conditions, enhancing overall port competitiveness, etc.
