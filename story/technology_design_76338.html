The Essence - Four Steps to Website Construction, Easily Settling Online

Establishing a website generally includes the following major steps: applying for a domain name, building a web platform, web design and maintenance, as well as promoting the website externally. Here, we will discuss how to establish a site through these steps.

1. Apply for a Suitable Domain Name

By registering a domain, your company gains a unique identifier on the global Internet, which serves as the address or entry point for users to access your website. The URL formed by the domain can be widely circulated on the Internet like a trademark, and a good domain helps you shape your international online image in the future. Additionally, domains are globally unique and their resources are limited. Whoever registers first has the right to use it, so you should consider now whether you want to protect your intangible assets on the Internet. Commonly, ".com" is an international domain, while ".com.cn" is a domestic one. You can check availability at http://www.nicenic.com/domain. When defining a domain name, apart from considering the nature of your company and the characteristics of the information content, the name should also be concise, memorable, and impactful. We recommend applying for and registering a domain with Naisi Nick (http://www.nicenic.com).

2. Establish Your Own Web Platform

After obtaining your domain name, you need a space to build your company's presence on the Internet, and this space is a server. Typically, there are several ways available for companies to choose from:

- Virtual Hosting: Virtual hosting uses special software and hardware technology to divide each computer into a "virtual" host. To the outside world, a virtual host is indistinguishable from a real one. We suggest that businesses adopt the virtual hosting method when going online. Generally, virtual hosting providers offer varying amounts of virtual hosting space, such as 50M, 100M, or 200M. For most corporate websites, choosing around 150M to 300M of virtual hosting space suffices. Additionally, virtual hosts have different functionalities; a standard virtual host supporting ASP+FSO+ACCESS can meet the needs of general users. However, for medium-to-large enterprises with more online users, it is necessary to choose a virtual host suitable for business growth, such as those supporting JSP, .NET, PHP+MySQL, etc. This is an important factor to consider when selecting a virtual host. Finally, pay attention to the speed of the virtual host. If your customers or potential visitors are mainly from the north, you can prioritize the China Unicom data center space. If they're mainly from the south, you should primarily consider telecom space. For nationwide coverage, smart dual-line hosts are recommended. Suggested provider: http://www.nicenic.com/

- Dedicated Server: For companies with substantial economic strength and high business volume, purchasing a dedicated server is also an option. However, this requires much higher costs and significant human and material resource investments—costs that are dozens of times higher than using virtual hosting.
