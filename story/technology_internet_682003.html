Tote Bags: Niche Prints Active in the Big Market

Various kinds of tote bags.

In order to more effectively promote products and services, almost all merchants will pair their goods with exquisite tote bags. Whether it's a car exhibition, shopping at the mall, buying cosmetics, going on a shopping spree at a brand store, attending a new book launch by a publishing house, or participating in a product introduction event... people who go out for these activities often return carrying various paper bags. These paper bags come in different sizes - some are large, others small; colors range from elegant and understated to vibrant and bold, even dignified; patterns either stand out through unique shapes or playfully humorous designs. Some emphasize the product's usage effects while others highlight the product's features. These paper tote bags, big and small, create a colorful landscape on the streets.

When it comes to packaging items—tote bag printing [http://www.sxywgs.com], industry insiders chuckle because the scale of the tote bag printing market pales in comparison to the rigid demand for daily life packaging, books, and other printed materials—it’s considered even smaller than "child's play." According to reporters from the China News Publishing Report, tote bag printing business is concentrated in some medium and small-sized printing and packaging enterprises, and it is just one single item for these companies. Due to its small volume, its profit margin is higher than that of book printing. Industry insiders noted that in recent years, as society places increasing emphasis on environmental protection and the demand for various commodities grows, the print volume of paper tote bags has quietly increased significantly.

The popularity of paper bags began after the plastic bag ban.

It is understood that eco-friendly paper tote bags started gaining popularity after the plastic bag ban was enforced.

Nowadays, the paper tote bags commonly seen in daily life, such as gift bags, shopping bags, and promotional tote bags, are mostly made from heavy kraft paper, kraft cardstock, offset paper, lightly coated paper, white cardstock, embossed paper, copperplate paper, matte copperplate paper, and are produced through processes like offset printing, die-cutting, and gluing. Thanks to modern printing technology and equipment, the printed patterns are very delicate and rich in expression. Moreover, according to customer needs, they can be laminated to increase toughness and water resistance; some use a matte film, which highlights the quality of the product without diminishing the user's status; others are simply made from pure kraft paper, and with unique professional design, they can give the product a simple yet elegant style, appearing subtly luxurious and inadvertently revealing an aristocratic air.

Shanghai Jielong Art Printing Co., Ltd.'s salesperson Guo Yinghao told reporters from the China News Publishing Report that all modern printing techniques can be showcased on a small tote bag, such as gold stamping, embossing, UV, and die-cutting irregular shapes.

As for the price of tote bag printing, copper cardstock tote bags have the highest cost, followed by white cardstock, gray-backed white cardstock, and white-based white cardstock. If lamination is required, the production cost is slightly higher than that of kraft paper. Kraft paper has good stiffness and resilience, a relatively rough surface, and a base color, generally unsuitable for four-color printing, only suitable for single-color printing, and does not require surface lamination.

Market Scale Though Small But Fierce Competition

Although the scale of the tote bag printing market is much smaller compared to book printing, this market is not a "blue ocean."

Guo Yinghao said that printing companies typically engaged in tote bag printing are not very large, and paper bag printing is only a small part of some printing and packaging companies' business.

An unnamed insider told reporters that there is competition even in this small tote bag printing market. Compared to the low profits of book printing, when the volume of paper bag printing is high, the gross profit margin can be between 8%~18%, and some social orders can even reach a gross profit margin of 20%. This profit margin would make micro-profit book printing companies sigh with admiration.

With over 10 years of experience in the printing industry, Guo Yinghao stated that the volume of tote bag printing has significantly increased in recent years, especially with the rise of well-known domestic clothing, accessories, and retail enterprises. The sales volumes of various brands have been rising, leading to a corresponding increase in the use of tote bags. He introduced that currently, the monthly production and sales volume of paper bags at Shanghai Jielong Art Printing Co., Ltd. averages between 3 million to 5 million pieces.

An industry insider told reporters that previously, printing and packaging companies did not pay much attention to paper bag printing. However, with the increase in market demand, more orders have emerged. Some companies have adapted to market changes and increased investment in this area. In some places, paper bag printing orders have become highly sought-after by medium and small-sized printing and packaging companies.

The Demand for Paper Bags Will Continue to Grow Strongly

From the perspective of environmental societal demands and market needs, the future development space for paper bags is very large.

It was found that the annual gift demand for individuals in China is approximately 505.5 billion yuan, while the annual gift demand for groups is about 262.9 billion yuan, among which business and welfare gifts account for over 190 billion yuan. The total annual demand for domestic gifts is around 768.4 billion yuan, with the overall market approaching an 800 billion yuan scale.

With the rapid economic development, China's gift market has evolved into a gift economy, and the concept of gifts has undergone tremendous changes, encompassing items such as white wine, red wine, branded handbags, high-end cosmetics, famous paintings, and newly launched electronic products.
