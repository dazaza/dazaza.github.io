Girl takes care of high-level paraplegic male internet friend for 3 years

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 13:44:07

A Miracle Happens: Yantai Worker Becomes Paralyzed from the Chest Down

When asked if they would be together after he stands up again, Lei Zegun said with a worried face, "If I completely recover, I will definitely want to be with her. But if I can't get better, I won't drag her down. Now, our familial relationship is greater than romantic love."

The female internet friend has been taking care of him for three years. Time passed slowly, but Lei's mother back in his hometown always thought that her daughter was still working far away. "In order not to worry my mother, I kept telling her that I am working outside. As a result, this lie has lasted for three years," Yang Jiagun said guiltily. "I feel most sorry for my mother."

"Since you've come, play for a few days and then go back." Although Lei Zegun urged her to leave many times, she had already made up her mind to stay by his side.

Moreover, since Lei Zegun's family did not have any source of income, in order to improve his life, Yang Jiagun worked part-time jobs over the past three years to earn some money.

Encountering a car accident, Yang Jiagun could not let go of her feelings. On October 10, 2008, she rushed to Lei Zegun's home deep in the mountains of Fang County, Hubei Province. "When I pushed open the door of his house, I was stunned," she recalled seeing the once handsome Lei Zegun lying on a dirty bed with a sallow complexion.

During these three years, besides taking care of Lei Zegun's daily life, Yang Jiagun also tried her best to help him stand up again. "We borrowed some money and went to several hospitals, but the treatment effect was not ideal," Yang Jiagun recalled. After much inquiry, they learned that a hospital in Shandong Province could treat nerve damage, so they contacted the hospital.

In August 2008, Lei Zegun had a motorcycle accident. After surgery, he was diagnosed as paralyzed from the chest down. "After that, I stopped contacting her," Lei Zegun said.

Currently, after treatment, Lei Zegun can already stand for two hours with the help of equipment and can take steps. "Now the first round of treatment has ended. On the 12th, we will send them back to their hometown by car. We will bring them back after the New Year for further treatment," introduced Sun Tao, the executive vice president of the hospital. Lei Zegun may fully recover to the point where he can stand up on his own.

The young man stands up in Jinan.

Lei Zegun, who used to work in Yantai, became paralyzed from the chest down due to a car accident. A female internet friend from faraway Shenzhen voluntarily took on the responsibility of taking care of him and stayed for three years.

Yang Jiagun, whose hometown is in Liaohe Kou Office of Laohhekou City, Hubei Province, lost her father when she was in junior high school. After graduating from a secondary vocational school, she went to work in Shenzhen. In 2008, Yang Jiagun met Lei Zegun online. Because they were from the same hometown, they chatted very well. After a few detailed chats, Yang Jiagun learned that Lei Zegun was four years older than her, a tall and thin big boy, who was working in Yantai at the time.

Interestingly, Lei Zegun also lost his mother shortly after birth and lived with his disabled father. Similar fates made their relationship change subtly from mutual greetings and messages to becoming close friends who could talk about anything.

"In order to make her leave, I scolded her severely, but she wouldn't go," Lei Zegun cried and said, "At that time, I really didn't want to drag her down. We don't know how many times we argued because of this."

Due to paralysis in the lower body, Lei Zegun often had incontinence issues. The clean clothes just changed would be dirtied soon, requiring another change and wash. Besides taking care of Lei Zegun's hygiene, Yang Jiagun also gave him foot baths, turned him over, and carried him onto a wheelchair. To prevent muscle atrophy, she persisted in giving Lei Zegun massages every day for several hours.

"Slowly, slowly..." On the 10th, when I saw Lei Zegun at the rehabilitation room of the Shandong Red Cross Intervention Hospital, he was undergoing rehabilitation training with the support of his "internet friend" Yang Jiagun.

"These past three years, I've been to many places for treatment and was almost losing hope. I couldn't imagine being able to stand up now," Lei Zegun cried and said, "I am extremely grateful for Yang Jiagun's care over the years and the treatment provided by the hospital staff."
