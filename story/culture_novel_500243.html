Chapter One Hundred and Sixty-Eight: Breaking the Seal

by fanyete on 2011-10-02 21:37:58

The woman wore a light-colored skirt, her eyebrows were as delicate as emerald feathers, her skin was as white as snow, and her waist was as slender as a rope. Her face was covered by a thin veil, but her clear eyes shone through it, making the heavens themselves seem to lose their luster.

Such a woman, with such rare beauty and elegance, is truly a rarity in this world.

The slightly noisy foot of the mountain became silent at this moment. Although the woman's face was veiled, the faintly visible outline revealed an almost perfect facial contour, tempting one to tear off the veil and see the stunning beauty that could make flowers pale in comparison.

"What a beautiful woman!"

Lin Dong's gaze was fixed on the plain-skirted woman standing barefoot on a lotus flower, and he couldn't help but take a deep breath. Among all the women he had seen, Dong Su was the most enchanting, but compared to this mysterious woman, she still fell short.

However, although the woman's voice was light and ethereal, Lin Dong did not detect any gentleness in her crystal-clear eyes. Instead, there was a deep-seated coldness that repelled others.

This woman was clearly someone who appeared warm on the outside but was cold inside.

Such women are even harder to deal with than those who appear cold as ice.

"Who is this woman? Is she from the Huangpu clan?" Lin Dong turned his head and asked Lin Ke'er quietly.

"Not..."

Upon hearing this, Lin Ke'er furrowed her brows slightly, her eyes filled with doubt. The appearance and demeanor of this woman made even her feel a twinge of jealousy.

"This woman seems to have quite a background. She was invited here by the Huangpu clan. Her name seems to be Qingzhu Ling. As for her exact background, it's something I don't have the status to know." Tao Lao interjected at this point.

"Someone who can make even someone like Lin Langtian so respectful must not be of ordinary status." Lin Dong nodded slightly, muttering to himself.

"Hehe, I didn't expect you to also be interested in our Great Yan Dynasty's affairs." Wang Yan looked at the mysterious woman with unreserved fervor. Such a beauty would make any man fall for her, and he didn't bother hiding his feelings.

"It's just a coincidence."

Qingzhu Ling smiled and said, then her gaze shifted to Lin Langtian as she softly said, "Since everyone is here, shall we start tearing the seal?"

"Hehe, alright." Lin Langtian nodded gently, his gaze towards Qingzhu Ling also slightly different, though far less audacious than Wang Yan's.

"Three of you, this seal was set by a Nibbanic Realm expert. Although it has been around for many years, its power is still formidable. But if we focus on breaking specific points, it shouldn't be too difficult."

Upon hearing Lin Langtian's words, Wang Yan and the other two also nodded.

"Enough talk, let's move!"

Wang Yan acted the fastest. With a stomp of his foot, the golden spear beneath him began to tremble violently. A brilliant golden light rapidly coalesced at the tip of the spear, eventually forming a massive golden spear beam tens of yards long that shot out!

"Hoong!"

As the spear beam shot out, it left behind low rumbling sounds of air explosions, its momentum astonishing.

"Whish whish!"

When Wang Yan moved, Qin Shi, standing on the Fengluan bird, also smiled and lightly waved his deep blue feather fan, directly forming a rapidly spinning blue tide in front of him. This tide was filled with rolling Yuan Qi.

"Poison!"

With a flick of his feather fan, the blue tide roared out, resembling a giant wave crossing the sky.

"Wooom!"

Lin Langtian stood with his hands behind his back, seemingly motionless. However, above his head, a huge vortex spontaneously appeared. Terrifying Yuan Qi surged out from his crown, transforming into a colossal fiery red sword within the blink of an eye. Then, with a flash of red light, it tore through the sky, roaring out like a wildfire spreading across the heavens.

Compared to the grandeur of Lin Langtian's and the others' attacks, Qingzhu Ling's attack seemed rather calm. She simply raised her hand, causing a petal from the lotus flower under her feet to detach. It was then enveloped by a concentrated green glow and silently soared towards the seal covering the mountain top.
