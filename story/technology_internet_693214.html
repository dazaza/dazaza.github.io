Faye Wong - Not everyone can be called Tianhou

by youyouwanglongt on 2012-03-06 15:03:17

(1) In 1993, Wang Fi started a romantic relationship with Dou Wei. In 94, Wang Fi was seen at Dou Wei's Beijing courtyard apartment, exposing their romance. In 96, Wang Fi became pregnant out of wedlock and returned to Beijing to await the birth. In October of the same year, she officially married Dou Wei through a lawyer. In early 97, their beloved daughter Jingtong was born, and Wang Fi frequently traveled between China and Hong Kong. In early 99, rumors spread that Wang Fi's marriage was in trouble, and Dou Wei publicly announced his new love interest, Gao Yuan, accusing this marriage of being a conspiracy. In December of the same year, they officially divorced in Hong Kong, ending their three-year marriage.

Living with Dou Wei, Wang Fi had once been happy. Even if life was simple, even if the outside world was shocked by storms, the Empress still remained calm. Even when she was photographed in the morning going out to empty the chamber pot at the courtyard in Beijing, she didn't care. Perhaps Wang Fi herself liked such a lifestyle. Only then did she not yet meet the person who truly understood her.

Back then, Wang Fi loved Dou Wei boldly, but not necessarily with peace of mind. By 1999, the original vows had become the moon of the past. The wounded Wang Fi began to only love strangers; the strangers were not faces, but her choices. Such as in 2000, she chose Nicholas Tse.

(2) On March 12, 2000, Wang Fi and Nicholas Tse were exposed for their intimate behavior at a bar. With an age difference of 11 years and no previous intersection in their lives, the two became a couple, shocking the public. After the party, Wang Fi, facing a group of Hong Kong media cameras at the door, took the initiative to hold Nicholas Tse's hand for the first time, officially announcing their relationship. That night, Nicholas Tse was seen returning with Wang Fi to her Baosongyuan residence to spend the night, openly starting a cohabitation life. They also got matching tattoos and wrote songs for each other. Wang Fi seemed to have swept away all the unhappiness from her breakup with Dou Wei, becoming deeply affectionate with Nicholas Tse. This high-profile romance, shocking the world, brought a surge in popularity, and even if not well-received, it was still the focus of media attention. Going against the trend to the extreme is guiding the trend, and Wang Fi's grasp was so precise and appropriate.

However, Nicholas Tse soon showed various patterns. In January 2002, after Cecilia Cheung suffered serious injuries during a performance at TVB's charity fundraising program, Nicholas Tse immediately rushed to her side. Two months later, they held hands in Malaysia. In June 2003, Ting Fei (Wang Fi and Nicholas Tse) reconciled, but within half a year, it was over again. Nicholas Tse ultimately wasn't the one who could give her a happy family.

(3) Wang Fi met Li Yapeng at a friend gathering. It was said that Li Yapeng pursued Wang Fi for half a year via text messages. In 2003, while Wang Fi was filming in Shanghai, Li flew to Shanghai to celebrate her birthday and publicly expressed his love, finally winning her heart. In July 2004, Wang Fi and Li Yapeng cohabited in Beijing, and their romance was exposed. In 2005, Wang Fi and Li Yapeng finally tied the knot, officially forming a family and having their beloved child, Li Yan.
