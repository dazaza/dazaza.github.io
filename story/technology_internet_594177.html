LESA sincerely cooperates with you to "build a world-class city and create a world-class brand"!

by ftleisa on 2011-11-29 15:38:50

"Sincere cooperation" for "Building a world-class city and creating a world-renowned brand"! Esteemed Vice President Zhang Xiaoyu, all leaders, and distinguished guests: In the Huairou district of September, the autumn breeze is refreshing. On the occasion of the 52-meter pump truck's market launch, I would like to extend, on behalf of all employees of Foton Leyard Pumping Division, a warm welcome to everyone who has come to attend the ceremony today.

The market launch of Leyard's National IV 52-meter pump truck holds significant importance for both China's concrete machinery industry and the development of Leyard's pumping division. This significance first manifests in it being a completely self-made pump truck. It is well known that over 90% of domestic pump trucks are combinations of imported chassis and domestically produced upper structures. However, Leyard’s pump truck realizes the full meaning of 'Made in China,' with both the chassis and upper structure designed and manufactured by Foton as an integrated unit. In terms of this boom height, it is the first product of its kind domestically; furthermore, it is also the first pump truck to meet the National IV emission standards launched domestically, which holds great significance for advancing the emission upgrade of our country's integrated pump trucks. Meanwhile, this model fully embodies Foton's technological accumulation, sedimentation, and innovation dedicated to the development of concrete pumping products.

Firstly, this product adopts the industry-leading six-section arm technology, significantly surpassing traditional five-section arm products in pumping height.

Secondly, through the optimization design by our technical personnel, it achieves the largest placement range among same-arm products within the industry.

Thirdly, in terms of pumping capacity, due to the adoption of large-diameter, long-stroke conveying cylinders, after improving the feeding system, it achieves the industry's largest pumping capacity of 170 cubic meters per hour.

In addition, a large number of industry-leading technologies and patents, such as boom self-adaptive buffering technology, automatic concrete retreat technology, automatic fault diagnosis technology, feedback-type remote control technology, CAN bus technology, and low energy consumption, have been extensively applied to this product, resulting in a comprehensive upgrade of its functions and performance.

Reliability is the fundamental requirement of pumping products. To ensure the use reliability and performance leadership of the 52-meter product, we have activated the global gold supply chain in the selection of core components, perfectly integrating globally leading equipment and technologies such as Cummins, Eaton, ZF, Bosch Rexroth, and HBC into this product. Through finite element analysis during the R&D process, double the amount of pumping volume testing verification, and market customer verification, the technical matching and use reliability of the 52-meter product have been greatly improved.

To further enhance the competitiveness of this product, we have also specifically planned in aspects such as cost-performance control and integrated service support, making the 52-meter product not only worth its price but also exceeding expectations.

The 52-meter pump truck is a key product in Leyard's 12th Five-Year Strategic Plan. Its market launch will undoubtedly strongly promote the progress of Leyard's pumping business planning process and better promote the upgrading of domestic pump truck products.

In the future, we will continuously introduce new product platforms, constantly innovating from the perspectives of chassis technology and upper structure technology, providing stronger support to a wide range of dealers and delivering more highly profitable products to a wide range of users. Under the guidance of Foton Automobile's 2020 strategy, Leyard is willing to take quality products and services as carriers, sincerely cooperate with a wide range of users and dealers, coexist and win together, gather strength from all parties, and "build a world-class city and create a world-renowned brand"!

Thank you all.
