The Hollywood blockbuster "Iron Man 3" rode the last wave of the May Day holiday, raking in 130 million yuan on its opening day and setting four records on the mainland for midnight screenings, first-day box office, Wednesday openings, and single-day box office. This is undoubtedly another shot in the arm for the film industry, but no one would have expected that "Iron Man 3" would ignite interest in JD.com's customer service personnel.Thanks to the popularity of "Iron Man 3," JD.com recently launched a hot-selling Iron Man 3 wireless mouse. The product name displayed is YiBo EMS605GOAA-EF, with advertising slogans claiming it as a global first release and limited edition collectible. It is priced at 599 yuan.
