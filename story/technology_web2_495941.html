Hong Kong drama "Unwelcome Appointment"

Tae Feng heard Dong Jian cursing Zhi Da and Chu Qiao, and immediately thought of playing tricks on the two. After realizing this, Dong Jian did not allow him to take their lives. Tae Feng deliberately let Dong Jian hear the conversation between Chu Qiao and Zhi Da, making Dong Jian know that Chu Qiao intended to make things difficult for Hua Xi, while Zhi Da was cowardly and fearful. Tae Feng hoped that Dong Jian would choose one of them as a scapegoat, but Dong Jian still felt sorry for them. Suddenly, Dong Jian took Zhi Da to the lab for a physical examination, which made him feel very confused. Dong Jian and Zhi Da talked about many past events on the street and found that the father and son had never had such heartfelt conversations.

In Hong Kong drama "Unwelcome Appointment," Tae Feng decides to stay in the human world.

Dong Jian suddenly instructed Zhi Da to take good care of Hua Xi; Dong Jian brought flowers to the hospital intending to express his love to Xiao Fang, but he discovered that Xiao Fang and Yong Shen were already a couple. Dong Jian kept his promise and appeared in front of Tae Feng, even handing over his own name. Dong Jian's actions made Tae Feng find it extremely interesting. Dong Jian passed Tae Feng's test and could continue to survive. Just when Dong Jian thought Tae Feng would leave, Tae Feng said he wanted to stay in the human world to learn how to be a person and hoped to become friends with Dong Jian, leaving Dong Jian speechless and unable to refuse.

(All images come from the official website of Hong Kong drama "Unwelcome Appointment")

When Dong Jian entered his home, he saw Tae Feng appear again. Hua Xi happily informed her son that Tae Feng would have meals at their house. Dong Jian was helpless seeing the devil invade his home. Tae Feng boasted that he would bring his girlfriend to eat dinner at Dong Jian's house. Dong Jian secretly feared that Tae Feng might have designs on Xiao Fang. Xiao Fang met Tae Feng in the hospital corridor, and Tae Feng's humorous and witty words quickly attracted Xiao Fang's attention. When Xiao Fang realized that Tae Feng was using drug sales as an excuse to meet girls, she scolded him. Dong Jian secretly followed Tae Feng and laughed when he saw Tae Feng being scolded by Xiao Fang. He directly pointed out that Tae Feng would not win Xiao Fang's favor.

To stay in the human world, Tae Feng decided to work as a salesperson in Jing Tian's company. Dong Jian became increasingly confused by Tae Feng's behavior. Yong Shen was dissatisfied when Tae Feng was late for his first day of work, and Tae Feng immediately explained that he was late because he went to Dong Jian's clinic for medical treatment. Tae Feng brought good news to Jing Tian, saying that Dong Jian was willing to cooperate with Jing Tian in the development of new drugs, making Jing Tian very happy and immediately arranging for the team to have a meeting with Dong Jian. Dong Jian was urgently summoned by Tae Feng to Jing Tian's pharmaceutical factory. Tae Feng informed Dong Jian that they would cooperate with Jing Tian, which made Dong Jian quite resistant. Tae Feng warned Dong Jian of the consequences, making him finally agree to research antihistamine drugs while also helping Jing Tian develop drugs for Alzheimer's disease.

In Hong Kong drama "Unwelcome Appointment," the relationship between Xiao Fang and Tae Feng has greatly improved.

Yong Shen couldn't attend his appointment with Xiao Fang due to work, and upon learning this, Tae Feng volunteered to replace Yong Shen and accompany Xiao Fang at the restaurant. Yong Shen didn't suspect any foul play and was grateful for Tae Feng's help. Xiao Fang was shocked to see Tae Feng appear at the restaurant; unwilling to sit with Tae Feng, she agreed to go to Hua Xi's house for dinner. Hua Xi recognized Xiao Fang as the nurse who asked Dong Jian to donate organs and thus had no good feelings towards her. As Xiao Fang left Hua Xi's house, she felt disappointed for not receiving Yong Shen's call. Tae Feng consoled her. When Dong Jian returned home downstairs, he saw Xiao Fang and Tae Feng chatting happily and familiarly, assuming that Tae Feng used magic to charm Xiao Fang. Tae Feng pointed out that pursuing the opposite sex doesn't require magic. Xiao Fang went to the pharmaceutical factory to find Yong Shen but was summoned by Jing Tian instead. Jing Tian teased Xiao Fang, making her feel very embarrassed.

In Hong Kong drama "Unwelcome Appointment," Dong Jian and Zhi Da deepen their father-son relationship.
