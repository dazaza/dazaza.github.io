1. Since the day we opened our eyes to see this world, all we have seen are only two kinds of people - men and women. They belong to two different camps, trying their best to subdue each other in order to completely win over the opposite side. According to an ancient legend, there is something called love that can end this battle once and for all. Thus, everyone rushes to search and fantasize about finding it, often ending up bloodied from their efforts. It was then discovered that this emotion was also concocted by some people in their studies. Everyone becomes even more disheartened and pained, leading to even fiercer conflicts between men and women. In truth, men and women have never truly understood each other. Men are not difficult to understand; it's just that no one has ever told you.

2. I began to realize that everyone has a reason for liking another person. There is no such thing as love without reason or cause in this world. Besides wealth, status, beauty, and talent, there might be other reasons - seeking similar comfort, satisfying vanity, escaping the past, or venting emotions. All these are carried out under the guise of love. Some gradually fade away, while others genuinely fall in love with you. The causes themselves are not terrifying; what is frightening is refusing to acknowledge these reasons and instead romanticizing them as destiny.

3. Choice, the most important thing is choice. From the day we are born, except for our parents whom we cannot choose because they already exist before we are born, everything else is subject to choice. Choosing a good kindergarten, primary school, a pretty classmate, junior high, senior high, a good university, a good job, a good TV, computer, lamp, sound system, choosing different brands of cosmetics, cars; choosing men, women, men choosing women, women choosing men, men choosing men, women choosing women. Throughout our lives, our existence is filled with various choices, among which the most important for men is choosing between a beautiful woman with intelligence hovering around the pass mark and a talented woman who can effortlessly write several thousand-word essays. For women, it is choosing between a man who treats her well but has no money and a wealthy man who does not treat her well.

4. We are always making choices, weighing different criteria. Choosing someone rich, choosing someone 180cm tall, choosing bust size, choosing education level, choosing the direction of attack, choosing the route of retreat. Because life isn't perfect, we can only choose what is most beneficial to us, becoming the reason we aren't eliminated, and the tool that allows us to survive. Men waver between the awe-inspiring beauty of women and the pleasure brought by witty female intellectuals. Women only remember those who treated them poorly. Wealthy men used to treat them so well with money. We have no choice because we have long forgotten that when we truly want something, we don't need to choose.

Letting our lonely and isolated hearts find a little solace. After all, in this era and at this age, we rarely dare to seriously commit. Regardless, a bosom friend is the one who gives you confidence, courage, and strength. Such a precious person should not become a stranger due to excitement, loneliness, or loss.

7. Every morning, countless men and women leave their homes to go to work in different places. They have different dreams and jobs but belong to two distinct camps - men and women. These two camps have always been comparing, fighting, preserving their own privileges while trying to gain more from the other side. They are so greedy yet have never managed to reach a fair agreement. (When women are late on a date, it's justified, but when men are late, they're unreliable, no matter how much they explain!)

8. From the day men and women are born, they possess countless advantages and disadvantages. Why do men pursue women? Why do women keep running? When you, as the prey, are caught by me, why do you start giving me orders? In reality, men and women are like two perfectly fitting puzzle pieces. Only by finding your matching piece can you achieve happiness and satisfaction. At least, when Zuo Yongbang looked at Mi Qi crying endlessly, he suddenly realized a huge mistake. When a woman is extremely sad, all you can do is silently hold her. Then, cry even harder than her. No matter how you compare, every woman's innate maternal love is the greatest love you will ever experience.

9. Have you ever stalked anyone? Secretly and boldly following behind someone, wanting to know what they do when they're not with you. Are you catching a thief at this moment, or are you yourself the thief?

10. Why do we stalk unpleasant people? If we really catch them, what would we do? Is it for the thrill of total victory, or to satisfy our own grandeur? When everything is laid out before you, do you really have the courage to accept it?

11. Friends, we need friends from the smallest times. These companions grow with you, laugh with you, cry with you, accompany you through a painful yet joyful life. Some women say they'd rather change boyfriends than lose their close girlfriends. Because they were with you during your most emotionally vacant times, and they are the ones who criticize your new relationships.

12. Her friends carry all her past and yearn for all her future. When you love her, her friends can either stand by you in crisis or stab you in the back when things are stable. You cannot control or intervene. But if direct confrontation fails, remember, you have your own strategies. Recruit her friends to your side!

13. Haven't you had this experience? Initially, you just want to be with the person you like. When you finally get what you wish for, you hope the other person understands you - every glance, every movement, every word, just two words, and they know your thoughts. This is the person you seek for a lifetime. The problem is, you are always disappointed, yet you continue to hope again next time. In fact, in this world, no one is another's mind-reader. Spending all day holding back your feelings expecting the other to guess will eventually make you a nightmare in their heart!

14. We've all had this feeling. When we like someone, they seem a certain way. As we get closer, approach them, or wait anxiously until they like us back, suddenly we find they are not the person we originally thought they were. So where did we go wrong? Did they change, or was it our illusion from the beginning?

15. When we like someone, they appear confident, elegant, fashionable, humorous, considerate, and empathetic in public. We fall in love with that person, only to discover the many joys and sorrows within them. Their sadness, weakness, irrational tantrums, and more. You wonder how you could love such a person, unable to tolerate the aspects of them that are now exposed before you. At this point, we always forget one crucial thing: you can see all this because they also love you.

16. In romance, there is a common term called "pursuit". Men pursue women, which seems to be an indispensable part of dating, and women enjoy being chased. But is pursuit really necessary in love?

17. Love is natural. It arises naturally and begins naturally. When you come, I won't chase you. When you leave, I won't chase you either. But that doesn't mean I don't love you or haven't fallen deeply for you. Do you understand?

18. For two strangers meeting for the first time, each is fresh and starts anew. However, they don't realize that before this, both have had long histories. Do these past events matter to your present and future? Do you think about them? Who knows...

19. Everyone has a history. You have one, and so do I. Before we met each other, we each had our own experiences. The past cannot be changed, and it is because of the past that we became who we are today. We meet, fall in love, and the only thing we can change is the future. Each moment now determines the future, and each moment of the future lies in our hands.

20. We create things that didn't exist before, crafting another space. In your heart, there is also such a studio. When the lights come on and the camera starts rolling, the life and things you want to see will be created from this studio in your heart and realized in real life. How you perceive any event depends on your perspective.

21. We live in a rapidly changing world where love alone is not enough. Some things, you assume you are entitled to, but when you lose them, you realize you never had the right in the first place. It's like heaven just taking a stroll through your life. Fortunately, such an encounter makes me realize that I am more than just myself. A stronger version of me awaits in the future. And in every man's heart, there is much anxiety and fear because in this world full of wolves, I, this sheepdog, must abandon my gentleness and shine the brightest light upon the world.

22. Every February 14th has a special name: Valentine's Day. Some say there is only one day in a year when two people must be together no matter what, while others say that if you choose the right partner, every day is Valentine's Day. So, what truly defines Valentine's Day?

23. What exactly is Valentine's Day? Living in this world, you first encounter someone who harms you, then become disillusioned and distrustful of love. You learn to hurt others, thinking that choosing someone who doesn't care will prevent betrayal. One day, you realize that person will leave too, and they are capable of true love. Finally, you meet someone who, like you, has harmed others and been harmed. They know pain, thus knowing to cherish and show compassion. On that day, the person you meet is your true Valentine's Day.

24. Since the birth of human civilization, we have discovered the concept of etiquette. From dressing, eating, living, to traveling, every aspect has its own set of etiquettes. Etiquette varies by country and individual. While it has no practical use or form, it acts as a lubricant ensuring that every person, relationship, and situation in this city runs smoothly without abruptness or awkwardness. Once we get used to etiquette, we can't live without it. The first rule of etiquette in romantic relationships is that breakups must be painful, as it shows respect for the other person.

25. Many times, we forget that etiquette is merely a formality, representing nothing substantial. If we let it guide us, it can alter our fate. When you protect one woman and unintentionally hurt another, all notions of etiquette vanish, leaving no room for compromise. All you can do is softly say goodbye.

26. Loyalty, one of the most beautiful words in the world, has been deeply rooted in our hearts since humanity's inception. It has no legal definition yet is omnipresent. It has no written agreement, yet the standards for judgment are clear. From nations to political parties, social relations to religious beliefs, no one questions its existence. It leaves no room for negotiation, making it easy to gain and lose. In love, loyalty is like money—only when you lose it do you realize it once existed beside you.

How we long to linger in the very beginning, patting each other's shoulders, listening to each other's heartbeats, at this moment, you are at your most beautiful.
