Hefei issues invoices on behalf of others: a firefighter was seriously injured after using his shoulders to catch a woman who fell from a building

by guijuhiu003 on 2012-02-28 02:06:44

If a 50-kilogram heavy object fell from the sixth floor, which is nearly 20 meters high, and you were standing right underneath it, how would you react?

When a woman fell from a higher floor, Corporal Chen Zhangliang, 24 years old, from the Chuxiong Yi Autonomous Prefecture Fire Brigade in Yunnan Province, not only did he not dodge, but instead moved forward to cushion her fall with his thin shoulders, saving the life of the suicidal woman at the cost of sustaining serious injuries himself.

At 10:16 AM on February 22, the alarm bells rang suddenly at the Chuxiong Fire Station: A woman was attempting to jump off a building in Lizi Garden Community in Lucheng Town. Fourteen soldiers who were undergoing training immediately set out for the scene, and Chen Zhangliang, who was not on duty that day, also voluntarily joined the rescue team and jumped onto the vehicle.

When the firefighters arrived at the scene, the area was already crowded with onlookers, and the police had established a cordon line. A middle-aged woman stood on the edge of the railing on the sixth floor of the residential building, with almost half of her feet hanging in the air. She was emotionally agitated and talking to the people behind her who were trying to persuade her not to jump. The firefighters immediately divided into two groups; one group was responsible for setting up protective measures on the ground, while the other group quickly rushed upstairs to try to persuade the woman not to jump.

Chen Zhangliang and his comrades were tasked with setting up rescue facilities on the ground. However, there was a cement step about 50 centimeters high at the corner of the building directly below the woman, making it impossible to lay down an air cushion.

The soldiers immediately borrowed two quilts, and Chen Zhangliang, along with three other comrades, each held a corner of the quilt tightly, ready to catch the woman if she jumped.

All this was done within two or three minutes. Just as the second group of soldiers had rushed to the roof, the suicidal woman's body swayed, and she lost her footing. She tried to turn around to grab the railing but failed.

Warrior Chen Bo, who was downstairs holding the quilt tightly with Chen Zhangliang, still vividly remembers the heart-stopping moment. He recalled: "We originally expected her to jump outward, so we stood farther away from the wall. But unexpectedly, she almost slid down along the wall, and we didn't have enough time to move."

Standing diagonally opposite, Chen Bo clearly saw that just as Chen Zhangliang stepped onto the cement step, the woman was about to hit the ground. Seeing that the quilt wouldn't be able to catch her in time, Chen Zhangliang decisively rushed forward. The woman's waist landed directly on him, and they both fell to the ground instantly.

Later, it was diagnosed that the downward force of the falling woman was buffered by hitting Chen Zhangliang. Although seriously injured, she was not in danger of losing her life. Chen Zhangliang suffered a tear fracture of the right acetabulum, a crack in the second vertebrae of the cervical spine, multiple soft tissue contusions on the right side of his body, and a hematoma on the front of his scalp. Deputy Director Jiang Changhai of the Orthopedics Department of the Second Hospital of Chuxiong Autonomous Prefecture said, fortunately, the cervical spine was not hit directly. "If the high-level cervical spine injury occurred due to a more accurate impact, Chen Zhangliang might have died immediately."

On February 24, Chen Zhangliang lying in bed began to drink some porridge. His first words spoken in a weak voice were: "Don't tell my parents."

Huang Zhipeng, former company commander of Chen Zhangliang in the Chuxiong Fire Brigade, said that Chen Zhangliang comes from Xingyi, Guizhou, with poor family conditions. Both of his parents work outside their hometown, so he lives frugally and rarely takes leave to go out for entertainment or shopping. In his first year of service, his annual allowance was only over 3000 yuan, yet he sent 2000 yuan to his parents. In the third year after becoming a non-commissioned officer, his allowance increased, but his standard of living did not improve. He sent another 5000 yuan home in the past two months. Yet, this "stingy corporal" donated 1000 yuan when the company collectively funded the education of poor college students. Afterwards, the company officers considered his family situation and advised him to donate less, but he said: "I just sent some money home recently, leaving only 1000 yuan with me. If I had known that donations were needed, I would have sent less money home and helped these children more."

In the 2009 Chuxiong Yao'an 6.0 magnitude earthquake rescue, as a member of the earthquake relief assault team, Chen Zhangliang participated in the entire rescue mission. During the demolition of dangerous houses, Chen Zhangliang fought on the front lines, working tirelessly for dozens of hours. His sweat and mud soaked through his whole body, and his hands and feet were scratched by nails on the house pillars, but he never complained. After the rescue, Chen Zhangliang was awarded a personal third-class merit.

Chen Zhangliang also took charge of the search and rescue dog training task in the company. He had a deep bond with his search and rescue dog Bai Ling. Comrade Liu Tao said that when Bai Ling was young and couldn't bite the dog food, Chen Zhangliang would chew it up and spit it into his hand to feed her. These days, without seeing its master, Bai Ling, who never entered the barracks, ran into the dormitory area, searching room by room for Chen Zhangliang.

In Huang Zhipeng's eyes, this dark-skinned and lean young man usually doesn't talk much, appearing silent and serious, but he is "one of the best firefighters I've ever led." What makes him good? Huang Zhipeng thought for a moment and said, "Some people's goodness is very simple and honest, but it can't be reflected in the materials."

After hearing about Chen Zhangliang's heroic deeds, many citizens of Chuxiong went to the hospital to visit him. Teacher Ma Li from Chuxiong No.1 Middle School said: "What impresses us most is that the instinct of ordinary people is to dodge, but Chen Zhangliang's professional qualities overcame the instinctive reaction. Facing the enormous downward force, the hero used his flesh and blood body to move forward, saving a life."
