Nonchalant Network Marketing Strategy Five: Group Marketing

by long78 on 2011-02-26 11:31:56

Group marketing covers a very broad scope. Different groups have different marketing methods, but most group marketing methods share similarities. Here, Yangzhou SEO will only briefly touch upon group marketing, and the specific sharing is as follows:

1. Seven types of classifications of groups

1) Instant communication groups: including QQ groups, HI groups, Wangwang groups, Fafa groups, Weibo groups, UC groups, UT groups, YY groups, IS and other instant communication tool groups.

2) Blog groups: No matter what kind of blog you create, there are fixed blog groups, such as Sina's parenting group, technical group, emphasizing the other uses of blog groups. If you register different IDs in different ways on Sina, it can be said that this is a blog group, and also a site group.

3) Forum groups: The effect of forum groups is the same as blogs. There are fixed groups within the group. Having multiple IDs has the same effect. As long as you can put readers first and keep the network environment healthy in these groups, no one will oppose or mind your multiple IDs. This can also indirectly show your extensive knowledge. If you are willing to share, everyone will definitely like it.

4) Site groups: The main feature of site groups is to accurately position keywords based on different product positioning, and to be precise and professional. Related site groups, comprehensive site groups, local site groups, as long as they are related and your purpose remains unchanged, considering user experience, believe that such site groups will be liked by everyone from the perspective of the Internet and users.

5) Weibo groups: Also known as micro-groups, this emerging online platform gradually matured from 2011 and has become a favorite platform for many netizens. From pictures to videos, it is not only convenient and fast, but the main feature is that it can be linked with mobile phones and other communication tools. However, the only disadvantage is the text limit and picture size limit. It is precisely because of these features that it is more convenient for mobile phone users. The appearance of Weibo to micro-groups better reflects another direction of China's Internet - the era of 3G and mobile network communications will have a great development.

6) Email groups: Many instant tools have email reception and mass email sending, such as QQ mail, MSN, etc. As long as you join this group and have the permission, you can achieve email marketing through email.

7) Other groups: All communication groups are based on a database, just the size and principle of the database differ. Specific software will have specific groups. Achieving group marketing through database groups is both safe and targeted.

2. Five characteristics of groups

1) Targeted: Regardless of what kind of group, there is always a big direction. By accurately locating and joining groups, high targeting is achieved.

2) Extensive: Due to the special restrictions of groups, many similar groups have appeared. Therefore, the emergence of multiple groups in the same industry has become a fact.

3) Concealment: Groups themselves are hidden groups. You can only join the group through targeted searches, application for joining, and administrator review. Each step is necessary. Especially for technical groups, only the manager can add you, and if you violate the group rules, you will receive corresponding punishment.

4) Restrictive: Each group has certain restrictions. Every operation you perform may be restricted. Only by following the group rules can you exist.

5) Practicality: If you know how to do group marketing, believe that groups are the most practical marketing method. Because you are marketing on someone else's database. Considering all aspects, as long as you can achieve concealed soft guidance and every action you take maximizes user benefits, then you are the strong one.

3. Ten methods of group marketing

1) Email marketing: Everyone must be very familiar with email marketing. The most common QQ email, I believe everyone knows about it. But now, for QQ email marketing, the number of emails each ID can send has started to be gradually restricted, especially regarding the quality of articles.

2) Shared resources: The most profound feeling is QQ group sharing. Previously, shared resources did not require approval, but now with the development of the Internet, QQ resource sharing requires approval.

3) Technical discussion: From a technical point of view, if you are engaged in technical research and want to achieve group network marketing, you can completely guide it sideways because discussion is an interactive process. Strictly speaking, as long as the content you write is reserved, soft guidance can be completely achieved.

4) Entertainment and leisure: Entertainment and leisure belong to event marketing, such as cold jokes, celebrity discussions, travel exchanges, and life knowledge exchanges.

5) Accurate labels: From the perspective of micro-groups, labels represent your expertise or areas of interest. Therefore, clarifying the label is equivalent to telling everyone your direction and positioning.

6) Celebrity effect: There is no need to say much about celebrities; one action by a celebrity is worth many times our hard work day and night.

7) Picture effect: Compared to text, pictures give you a sensory advantage.

8) Article reprints: If your article considers the reader's perspective, readers will like it, reprint it, collect it, or share it with others. Of course, there are those who increase popularity by reprinting articles intentionally, such as some inexplicable people using QQ article reprints to attract my attention recently. As long as your QQ is online, there will be prompts for article reprints now; including reminders for space article content or topic discussions. Black hat networkers are everywhere, using various means to attract your attention, and the methods are endless.

9) Comments: Comments are the best way to communicate. If your language expression is subtle, it will attract many users to pay attention to you, your blog, group, forum, etc.

10) Video effects: Video is an advanced form of online expression, giving you a shock through the five senses. As long as there is quality, it will not lack attraction.
