Futian achieved remarkable results in ten years, and Futian Lei Sa was listed

by ftleisa on 2011-12-20 10:51:47

From 2001 to 2010, it was the "new three steps" phase in the development history of Foton Motor. It was regarded by Foton people as the golden decade of the company's development. During this period, Foton Motor successfully realized the transformation from being large to strong and shifted its business from pure light-duty trucks to full series commercial vehicles, laying a solid foundation for globalization. Starting with breaking through in medium and heavy-duty trucks, Foton Motor successfully achieved the development of full series commercial vehicles and established itself as a strong brand in China's commercial vehicle industry. The golden decade of Foton Motor's development also went through three major leaps.

Recently, China's first domestically branded Foton LeiSa 52-meter pump truck meeting the National IV standard was launched in Beijing and received high attention from industry insiders. Senior experts believe that this marks another step forward for China's pump trucks towards high technology, high environmental protection, and high-quality world standards.

While achieving great success in light-duty truck products, Foton Motor conducted an in-depth analysis of the macroeconomic background and market conditions. It believed that after China joined the WTO, it would enter the heavy chemical industry stage. The rapid development of infrastructure and real estate would greatly stimulate the development of commercial vehicles. Therefore, since 2001, Foton Motor has captured the medium and heavy-duty truck business, which is of utmost value and technological content in the commercial vehicle sector, and successfully achieved a breakthrough while meticulously planning the full series development of commercial vehicles.

In August 2001, Foton Auman heavy-duty trucks made their debut, taking the first step towards expanding into the medium and heavy-duty truck business. From 2001 to 2003, Foton Motor achieved significant breakthroughs in the core business of medium and heavy-duty trucks through "external expansion and scale-up."

From 2004 to 2006, Foton Motor focused on the automotive industry, upgraded commercial vehicle products, developed new energy vehicle products and multi-functional vehicle series, with "cultivation enhancement and structural adjustment" as the management direction, gradually perfecting the automotive product structure, successfully achieving the full series development of commercial vehicles, completing the second step of the golden decade development goal.

From 2007 to 2010, through continuous adjustments in product structure, improving group experience, consolidating development foundations, enhancing personnel capabilities, deepening international cooperation, and other measures, Foton Motor took the third step in the golden decade towards global development. In 2009 and 2010, Foton Motor won the crown of the world's leading commercial vehicle sales for two consecutive years, thereby firmly establishing itself as a strong brand in China's commercial vehicle industry. At the same time, Foton Motor actively and steadily developed overseas markets, laying the foundation for globalization.

Over the ten years, Foton LeiSa Automotive, through active international cooperation, integrating global resources, cultivating independent R&D experience, seeking domestic mergers and strategic alliances, seeking layouts in key components areas, resolutely implementing internal improvements, converting low-cost experience into cost-effective experience, and other efforts, successfully realized the transformation from being large to strong during the major development phase of the golden decade, transforming Foton from a Chinese company to a global one.

At the same time, during the golden decade, Foton Motor focused on enhancing the company's R&D experience, marketing experience, supply chain integration experience, and group experience. It formed a full series R&D experience for commercial vehicles, achieving domestic leadership in R&D experience; recreated advantages based on existing strengths, making the service network a core competitiveness; integrated global resources, seeking strategic layouts in key component areas; and perfected and finally formed a management model suitable for long-term development.

From 2002 to 2010, the total increase in Foton's brand value was nearly 30 billion yuan, with an average annual brand growth rate exceeding 32%. In 2009, Foton Motor ranked 34th among the "China's 500 Most Valuable Brands" with a brand value of 33.971 billion yuan, leading the commercial vehicle category.
