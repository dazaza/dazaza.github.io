Photos of Eight Grandpa and Xie Yuxin

At the rehearsal site for *Sheng Ming Da Zhen* (Fame Shock), Xie Yuxin's current boyfriend, Eight Grandpa, was by her side, looking extremely happy.

Everyone has been speculating about who Eight Grandpa is, but his identity remains a mystery to this day. It's also difficult to tell from the photos who he really is.

"Eight Grandpa has plunged me into a whirlpool of happiness."

In the eight weeks at the end of 2006 when Xie Yuxin came to Hunan TV to participate in *Sheng Ming Da Zhen*, the media revealed that "a mysterious man accompanied Xie Yuxin every time." Later, it was confirmed by the media that this person was none other than the legendary Eight Grandpa, Xie Yuxin's boyfriend. At every rehearsal for *Sheng Ming Da Zhen*, Xie Yuxin and Eight Grandpa were seen clinging to each other affectionately, envied by many.

When talking about her current boyfriend, Eight Grandpa, Xie Yuxin said with a face full of happiness: "I met Eight Grandpa at a friend's gathering. Although during *Sheng Ming Da Zhen*, he was always taking care of me, every time I rehearsed, he would bring soup for me to drink. But it wasn't soup he cooked himself—it was made by an aunt, and he just brought it over as a token of his care. At home, I take care of him. I am a very traditional woman in my thinking, and I spoil Eight Grandpa a lot. At home, I let him be a real 'Grandpa.'" Xie Yuxin also joked, saying, "When Eight Grandpa comes to my place, he can't even find the chopsticks."

Reflecting on her life experiences, Xie Yuxin couldn't help but sigh, "I always seem to meet people like this, which is another phase in the life of Xie Yuxin." Over the course of this year, the media continuously exposed rumors about Xie Yuxin being 'supported' financially, yet she felt... (the sentence trails off in the original text).
