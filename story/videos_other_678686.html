Chongqing Cost Engineer (civil building, installation) Training Base - *** Vocational Training

We solemnly promise: after graduation, you will not need a mentor anymore and can independently engage in construction drawing budgeting, project settlement, bidding, etc.

I: Employment Prospects

"Before the project starts, the cost must be determined first." The importance of cost engineers and cost clerks in the construction industry cannot be overstated. Chongqing is the youngest of the four municipalities. Chongqing's future is today's Shanghai. Currently, urban construction is just beginning. Construction is a symbol of economic and political prosperity in a city and will surely develop vigorously. The talent demand in construction companies focuses on professional technical personnel and middle-to-high-level management positions. Among all talent demands, cost clerks have become quite popular. With the construction of roads, bridges, water environment projects, and the preparation for the construction of urban rail transit, the entire construction industry has an intense need for professional talents. Since cost clerks control the key position of project pre-and post-settlement, they play a role in saving costs through the pre-and post-settlement of projects and cost control during the progress of the project. For a construction project, the quality of the cost clerk directly affects the success and quality of the project.

Currently, the construction market is very active, and the issue of cost calculation is a focal point for both the entrusting party and the entrusted party. Therefore, those who have worked in bidding or pre-and post-settlement for many years, familiar with the building materials market, quotas, and related software, and possess a cost engineer or cost clerk qualification certificate, have become sought-after positions in the workplace. It is understood that the average monthly salary of a cost clerk is around 3,000 yuan, while experienced and authoritative certified cost clerks can earn over 10,000 yuan per month.

Specialty: Construction budgeting (including decoration budget), Installation budget (water, electricity, heating)

Basic subjects: Construction drawing recognition, construction techniques, construction laws and regulations, construction organization design, 03G101 and 04G101 flat method teaching

Professional subjects: Construction drawing budget, structural drawing budget (manual calculation, Guanglianda budget software), compilation of bill of quantities, quota pricing, tender documents, project settlement, final account, project auditing, etc.

Introduction to the 2011 Civil Engineering and Installation Cost Clerk Practical Skills Training

I. Training Targets

1. Individuals intending to pursue a career in engineering cost estimation.

2. Individuals preparing to take the 2011 National Construction Project Cost Clerk Qualification Certificate Exam (preparation or review).

3. In-service, repositioned, or unemployed individuals (those with associate degrees, bachelor's degrees, or higher are eligible to apply).

II. Training Objectives

The school will use one or two sets of actual engineering drawings as teaching cases. First, students learn to read the drawings, understand the requirements of the questions, then proceed to calculate the bill of quantities and quotas (tender control price, tender offer documents). This ensures each student can independently complete the pre-and post-settlement work of an actual engineering project in practical work! (Teachers will focus on explaining how calculations are done in exams and practical work, emphasizing standard algorithms for exams and effective algorithms for work.)

III. Study Time

Classes start in 2011 for both civil engineering and installation programs.
