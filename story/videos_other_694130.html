Rescue "Poisoned" Critical Skin: 3 Steps to Clean Dirty Pores

Car exhaust, radiation, sandstorms... Unpreventable pollution makes the skin fragile. In traditional Chinese medicine for health care, looking gray and dirty is a minor issue, tolerating a few pimples is acceptable, but inexplicable itching, red spots, or even swelling is truly unbearable. How can we remove all the toxins brought by air pollution from our faces and let the skin breathe freely? This is the basic skincare routine that every girl must do nowadays!

Rescue Action: Gentle Cleansing

Critical Skin Analysis

Fluorides and dust in the air may not directly cause pimples, but after stimulating the skin, the stratum corneum will thicken. If the thickened dead skin cannot shed, it will clog pores, forming pimples and blackheads. Another direct consequence of thickened stratum corneum is rough skin. Dry and hard skin with oily pimples is something no girl can calmly face!

Rescue Action: Gentle Cleansing

Cleansing Keywords: Cleansing Gel, Massage, Nighttime

Although the appearance of dryness, roughness, and pimples makes you want to immediately grab the strongest exfoliating cream and scrub your face, stop! The more fragile winter skin determines that you can only use a gentler way to give your skin a thorough cleaning. Daily gentle cleansing is essential.

Compared to milky cleansers, which are more effective but not as harsh as soap, cleansing gel might be a good choice. After being tortured by the city air all day, the skin is already gray and fragile by night. Use the cleansing gel to thoroughly clean these pollutants. Don't forget to gently massage in circles for better results!

Rescue Action: Moisturizing Detoxification

Critical Skin Analysis

Photochemical smog, car exhaust, radiation, etc., seemingly intangible pollution is even harder to prevent. Although the sky appears dark, showing the deteriorated air quality of this city, photochemical smog is far less severe now than the Los Angeles photochemical smog event in the 1940s, during which many residents experienced flushed skin and swollen eyes, and some even died from inhaling too much smoke. However, the hazy smog and radiation can easily make originally smooth skin appear dull.

Rescue Action: Moisturizing Detoxification

Cleansing Keywords: Moisturizing Detox Cream, Hot Compress, Hydration

The frightening-sounding smog pollution isn't actually that dangerous, at least until we have to inhale large amounts into our lungs. Most of the time, smog and radiation pollution only come into contact with our skin, currently just causing issues like dullness, dryness, and freckles. Of course, such rampant pollution must be resolutely eliminated.

After cleansing, apply a hot compress to gently open the pores. Then use a moisturizing detox cream to give the skin a pure cleanse. Finally, don't forget to apply a moisturizer. Although detoxifying is important, hydration is still key when dealing with dry and dull skin.

Rescue Action: Balancing Repair

Critical Skin Analysis

These pollutants that wander in the air are the culprits behind itchy, allergic, and swollen skin. Long-term exposure to these pollutants can easily trigger chronic dermatitis. For fragile skin, learning the correct repair methods is necessary!

Rescue Action: Balancing Repair

Cleansing Keywords: Balancing Repair Water, Acid-Base Balance, Hydration
