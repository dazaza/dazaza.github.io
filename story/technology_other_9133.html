"Bug Me Not"

by moonswamp on 2006-11-16 16:30:39

Chinese Name: Chong Bu Zhi (Bug Me Not)

English Name: Bug Me Not

◎ Introduction

There is a group of young people, each possessing various supernatural powers. There's Xiaoyue, who can communicate with insects; Gaoyuan, skilled in "mountain boxing"; Shashazi, the flying charm queen; an ever-youthful auntie; Xiao Zhi, who can sense the past and future by touching objects; Xidan, with X-ray vision; Feizai, with incredible jumping power; Yujie and Bingqing, who possess telepathy and telekinesis; and Gechi, a brave single-pointed little beetle. However, in the face of an unprecedented catastrophe, all these superpowers seem unable to change the situation. Little do we know that the most magical energy in the world is the love and courage hidden within each person.

Xiaoyue has a secret crush on Gaoyuan, who is a few years older than her. Gechi, bold and straightforward, keeps encouraging Xiaoyue to express her feelings for Gaoyuan. Gechi even gives Xiaoyue a lesson in love: "To create a miracle, you have to take the initiative!" Xiaoyue understands this and learns from the direct courtship behavior of insects to approach Gaoyuan. However, her actions seem out of control, leading to Gaoyuan's rejection. An angry Xiaoyue ends up hurting the little beetle.

Gechi's insect friends, realizing that human behavior is too complicated, decide to disappear forever. Xiaoyue begins to no longer hear the language of insects, and all small lives in the city vanish overnight! A few days later, the air in the city starts to become polluted, birds die mysteriously from hunger, and the earth grows dim... It is then that she realizes that all the insects have gone into hiding, refusing to cooperate with nature. The food chain in the natural world has been cut off because plants, sympathizing with the insects, refuse to absorb waste gases and produce oxygen!

The auntie gathers all the super-powered youths, using their unique abilities to search for the little insects in the dim city. The darkened metropolis is fraught with danger, but everyone works together to fight back. In a critical moment, Xiaoyue saves the injured Gechi, deeply moving the insects and plants. Humanity is finally forgiven, and life in the city returns to normal.

It turns out that the most magical energy in the world lies in the love and justice hidden in the hearts of humans...
