Super Pickle's Sixth Lesson: Pickles and the Weasel

Pickle knew that becoming a superman was not an overnight matter. In the past, he would have needed to be familiar with astronomy, geography, the thoughts of various philosophers, ancient and modern Chinese and foreign knowledge, music, chess, calligraphy, painting, military books, swordsmanship, as well as all the unique skills of Shaolin monks. But now times had changed. He not only needed these skills, but also the abilities of a space superhero – to glow when needed, to catch fire when necessary, and to possess the power of thunder. However, even with these skills, he was still far from his current situation. He decided to start from where he was and first learn to release smelly farts from the weasel.

The reason he made this choice was because he believed that a superhero must have biochemical weapons. Relying solely on the martial arts of Shaolin Temple was outdated. For example, if someone pulled out a gun from dozens of meters away and fired a shot, you wouldn't even have time to squat down and prepare your stance before you were finished. Moreover, using biochemical weapons did not involve legal responsibility. If you hit someone, everyone would see it, and if they died or were injured, the evidence would be irrefutable and you couldn't escape. But if you used biochemical weapons, there would be no trace, and who would know? Mastering biochemical weapons was what made a true superhero.

The weasel’s farts... In the thirteenth episode of Super Pickle - Farewell Rose, Pickle had already become a superhero. He could fly in the sky, dive deep into the sea, walk on walls, see in the dark, speak many foreign languages, and understand the language of all animals.

After gaining the ability to fly and swim, Pickle was full of confidence and prepared to meet Rose. Before going, to get rid of the suspiciously brownish color of his skin, he soaked himself in vinegar for a whole night to make his skin whiter. Then he sprinkled perfume and wore a cloak made of white clouds. Drifting like a great swordsman, he was invisible and intangible, and could carry the cloak with him at all times. It was a purely natural biochemical weapon. Pickle had personally witnessed how one stinky fart from the weasel could knock out a large group of rats and frogs. Therefore, he decided to first learn how to fart.

Pickle became friends with the weasel, and everyone was puzzled. They said that Pickle, such an honest child, had gone bad after gaining some skills and spent all his time with the weasel, a little hoodlum who liked to steal chickens.

Pickle practiced for a long time, and his farts became louder and more powerful, but they never smelled. The weasel's farts could knock out rats and frogs, while Pickle's farts sounded like explosions, creating craters on the ground, but they never smelled. This made Pickle feel helpless and extremely admiring of the weasel. Whatever the weasel asked him to do, he would do it. Once when the weasel went to steal chickens and needed help, he tricked Pickle into going along to scatter rice to lure out the hens. Not only did they fail to steal any chickens and lost a bag of rice, but everyone found out that Pickle had participated in the theft.

When the news reached Mrs. Ma's ears, she scolded Pickle and forbade him from associating with the weasel. After staying at home for three days and reflecting on himself, Pickle realized that he was heading in the wrong direction. Hedgehogs' spines and weasels' farts were innate skills passed down through generations for hundreds of years. If he didn't use his brain and insisted on learning them by force, it would take years just to learn how to fart, leaving no time to learn anything else or become a superhero. At this rate, Rose would have married someone else by the time he finished learning. So, Pickle thought, he didn't need to learn how to fart; he just needed the smell that could knock people out. This way, he wouldn't have to strain himself every day trying to hold in farts and could focus on producing the smell.

Pickle locked himself in his room, neither eating nor bathing, desperately trying to figure out how to produce the smell. After being locked up for several days without coming up with a good method, when he finally stepped out of the door, he found that everyone who saw him pinched their noses. It turned out that because he hadn't bathed for a long time, his body had started to become sticky and smelly. Seeing how others were afraid of him, Pickle was overjoyed, thinking: truly, there is a god watching over me. I wanted the smell, and here it is.

Half a month later, Pickle finally figured out a way to make himself incredibly smelly. His smell not only knocked people out but also made them vomit uncontrollably. Wang Xiaoliang caught frogs, and his smell was even more powerful than the weasel's farts. Even the weasel gave him a thumbs-up saying: Pickle, you've graduated.

Since Pickle gained this skill, he hasn't stopped helping the villagers. That day when the urban management officers came to demolish Magpie's nest, claiming it was an illegal construction affecting the cityscape, Magpie was in a panic and called Pickle for help. As soon as Pickle stood there, he knocked out a few urban management officers. When the police arrived, they couldn't do anything about it because hygiene was a personal choice and not something the police could control. After this incident, the urban management officers no longer dared to bother Mrs. Ma's stall at the market. Seeing how effective this skill was, Mrs. Ma wanted Pickle to earn money with it. Pickle said that helping the villagers was fine, but he wouldn't do it for money because he still had to learn other skills. He was eager to become a superhero and meet Rose. Making money was too vulgar.
