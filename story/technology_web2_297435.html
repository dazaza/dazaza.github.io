1. What is mechanism charcoal? What are its characteristics?

Wuhan Chuidao Heavy Industry Co., Ltd. Mechanism charcoal is made by drying waste materials such as sawdust, peanut shells, rice husks, corn stalks, corn cores, alcohol residue, and sugarcane residue, then compressing them into round, square or square sticks with holes through a high-temperature and high-pressure process, and finally placing them in a carbonization furnace for dry distillation to form a black substance.

Characteristics: Compared with ordinary charcoal, the mechanism charcoal produced by Chuidao Heavy Industry has strong density, high carbon content, large heat output, small ash content, long burning time (generally three times that of ordinary tree charcoal), and no smoke or odor.

2. What raw materials are used to produce Chuidao Heavy Industry's mechanism charcoal?

Generally speaking, rice husks, peanut shells, fruit shells, cotton rods, soybean rods, hemp rods, corn rods, and corn core residues from rural areas; sugarcane residue from sugar factories; sawdust, wood shavings, bamboo and wood edge angle waste materials from furniture factories and bamboo product factories; dust particles and edge angle materials from plywood factories and fine wood board factories; reeds and small branches on the mountain can all be used as raw materials for producing mechanism charcoal.

3. Where should a Chuidao Heavy Industry charcoal factory be built?

It is generally advisable to build it within a radius of about 50 kilometers around the factory where there are sufficient raw materials. In addition, the cost of transporting raw materials must also be considered, trying to reduce production costs. To reduce transportation costs, processing sites for fuel rods (semi-finished products) can be set up in areas where raw materials are concentrated, then transported to the charcoal factory for centralized carbonization. The factory should be built in the suburbs of the city.

5. How much investment is needed to start a mechanism charcoal plant?

To open a medium-sized mechanism charcoal plant with an annual output of 500 tons, the total equipment investment is approximately RMB 88,000 yuan. Working capital is about RMB 10,000, which is used to purchase raw materials or pay the initial wages of workers. If there are ready-made raw materials such as sawdust, peanut shells, rice husks, and cotton rods, the working capital can be reduced. The factory area is more than 100 square meters. The total investment is about RMB 100,000 yuan.

To open a large-scale mechanism charcoal plant with an annual output of 100 tons, the total equipment investment is RMB 148,800 yuan. The factory area is about 200 square meters, plus worker wages and other fees. The total investment is about RMB 150,000 yuan.

After the equipment arrives at the factory, connect the power supply and production can begin immediately. Products can be sold back in 10 days. And setting up a personal enterprise with RMB 100,000 is more suitable for economic development in economically backward areas.
