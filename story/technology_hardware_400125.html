Baidu issues a notice: Baidu's C2C service "Youa" will be closed.

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 11:35:30

[Sohu IT News] (He Feng) March 31st - Baidu Youa today released a business adjustment announcement, declaring that it will start adjusting the Youa business from today. Within one month of the announcement's release, the product, store, and transaction-related functions of the Youa shopping platform will be closed. This also indicates that Baidu Youa, which has been in operation for less than three years, will officially close down, marking a setback for Baidu's self-built e-commerce layout.

Baidu announced that it would close the Youa shopping platform within one month.

Today, Baidu Youa released three announcements on its official website: the Youa Business Adjustment Announcement, the Announcement to Buyers, and the Announcement to Sellers. It was declared that within one month of the announcement's release, the product, store, and transaction-related functions of the Youa shopping platform would be closed. At the same time, Youa's mall business will be transferred to partners such as Rakuten Lekutian and Yaodian100. Currently, Rakuten Lekutian and Yaodian100 have also launched migration plans separately.

In the announcement to sellers, Baidu Youa suggests that sellers quickly withdraw important store information and delist products within one month. For sellers who have joined the consumer protection program, Youa will refund the deposit within 10 working days.

The announcement stated that Baidu is developing a new Youa platform.

In October 2007, Baidu announced its entry into the e-commerce sector and launched the beta version of Youa in October 2008, officially stepping into the online shopping field. However, unclear positioning, frequent changes in senior management, and intense competition have made Baidu Youa's development less smooth.

Starting last October, Baidu Youa attempted to transform by launching a lifestyle service platform and opening group-buying functions, but the results were not significant.

At the same time, in January last year, Baidu announced the establishment of a joint venture, Rakuten Lekutian, with Japan's Rakuten. Rakuten Lekutian went online and started recruitment in the second half of last year. The launch of Rakuten Lekutian was also seen by industry insiders as Baidu no longer hoping to expand e-commerce through Youa.

Below is the content of the Youa adjustment announcement:

Youa Business Adjustment Announcement

To further promote the development of China's e-commerce and provide more comprehensive and quality services to a wide range of Baidu users and e-commerce companies, we will begin adjusting the Youa business starting today. Youa's mall business will be systematically transferred to partners such as Rakuten Lekutian and Yaodian100. The new Youa platform is currently under intensive development, Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., and we are striving to provide services to users as soon as possible.

Baidu continues to see great potential and opportunities in the development of China's e-commerce, especially in the B2C sector. Baidu's e-commerce will increase technical investment and resource support with an open concept and innovative products, enhancing customer satisfaction, helping e-commerce companies succeed, and promoting the prosperity of China's e-commerce.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused by this business adjustment. At the same time, we thank everyone for their support and help with Youa.

For specific adjustment plans, please refer to the "Announcement to Buyers" and "Announcement to Sellers."

Full text of the Announcement to Buyers:

Dear buyers of the Youa shopping platform,

During the adjustment period of the Youa shopping platform business, i.e., within one month after the announcement of the Youa business adjustment, to effectively protect consumer rights, please pay attention to the following situations during this period:

1. One month after the release of this announcement, the product, store, and transaction-related functions of the Youa shopping platform will be closed. Please closely monitor and quickly handle ongoing transactions and transaction timeout issues. Meanwhile, you can still log in to "My Youa" to check previous transaction records (view "My Youa" - "Products I Purchased");

2. To ensure your financial security, please resist all non-guaranteed, offline transactions that carry significant risks. Do not click on links sent by the other party;

3. During this period, sellers will delist products and back up data information. We hope that buyers will give understanding and support to avoid generating new transactions through bidding on products, thus avoiding unnecessary disputes;

4. You can voluntarily click on relevant links to directly use your original Baidu user ID and password to log in and activate the Rakuten Lekutian user account: http://www.rakuten.com.cn/event/youa/ (please copy this link to the address bar), then you can continue to log in to the Rakuten Lekutian Mall using your Baidu user ID and password and use the purchase services provided by Rakuten Lekutian Mall;

Thank you for your long-term support and love for the Youa shopping platform! The official direct recharge services, life recharge services, and Baifu Bao payment services provided by Youa will continue to serve you. The new Youa platform is currently under intensive development, and we strive to provide services to users as soon as possible!

Baidu Youa Mall

March 31, 2011

Full text of the Announcement to Sellers:

Dear sellers of the Youa shopping platform,

During the adjustment period of the Youa shopping platform business, i.e., within one month after the release of the Youa business adjustment announcement, to smoothly cooperate with you in completing data information backup and shop migration work, please pay attention to the following situations during this period:

1. One month after the announcement's release, all product and store editing, management, publication, and transaction-related operational functions will be closed. We recommend that you quickly withdraw important store information and delist products. To facilitate your operations, we provide the Youa Store Assistant software to help you export and back up data;

2. At the same time, please closely monitor and quickly handle ongoing transactions and transaction timeout issues (view "My Youa", handle incomplete transactions). After the announcement's release, you can still log in to "My Youa" to check previous transaction records. Meanwhile, the direct recharge service and Baifu Bao payment service will not be affected, so please use them with confidence;

3. If you have joined the consumer protection program, we suggest you log in to "My Youa" to apply for withdrawal from the consumer protection program. Under circumstances that meet the requirements of the relevant clauses of the "Consumer Rights Protection Service," Youa will assist you in refunding the deposit within 10 working days (view "My Youa", apply for a refund of the consumer protection deposit);

4. Starting today, two Baidu e-commerce partners, Rakuten Lekutian Mall and Yaodian100 Mall, have fully commenced merchant application residency work. Please carefully understand the following migration plans and actively prepare relevant qualification documents and submit applications:

4.1 Youa merchants can voluntarily submit applications based on the relevant standards for merchant recruitment at Rakuten Lekutian under Baidu. Within three months of the announcement's release, Youa merchants migrating to reside in Rakuten Lekutian will receive certain preferential policies. For details, please refer to "Youa - Rakuten Lekutian Merchant Migration Plan: http://www.rakuten.com.cn/event/youa/youa20110331.html" (please copy this link to the address bar);

4.2 Youa merchants can also voluntarily submit applications based on the relevant standards for merchant recruitment at Yaodian100. For details, please refer to "Youa - Yaodian100 Merchant Migration Application: http://supplier.yaodian100.com/simplified_version/contact2.html?hpp=YOUa" (please copy this link to the address bar);

4.3 Relevant introductions:

Introduction to Rakuten Lekutian Mall: http://www.rakuten.com.cn/event/youa/ (please copy this link to the address bar);

Introduction to Yaodian100 Department Store Mall: http://www.yaodian100.com (please copy this link to the address bar)

Rakuten Lekutian and Yaodian100 are important e-commerce partners of Baidu. We expect this migration to concentrate advantageous resources and provide you with more professional and effective marketing services. Thank you for your support and love for the Youa shopping platform over the years. We wish you a prosperous business and success on the new platform!

The official direct recharge services and Baifu Bao payment services provided by Youa will continue to serve you. The new Youa platform is currently under intensive development, and we strive to provide services to users as soon as possible!

Baidu Youa Mall

March 31, 2011
