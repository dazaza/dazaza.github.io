Champions League - Manchester United shocks with 0-1 loss, ending a 5-year unbeaten home record in the Champions League.

by djbgufgire on 2009-11-26 11:51:11

Champions League - Manchester United shocked 0-1, 5-year unbeaten Champions League home record broken. At 3:45 AM Beijing Time on November 26 (8:45 PM local time in the UK on November 25), the fifth round of the 2009-10 season UEFA Champions League continued at Old Trafford, where Manchester United lost 0-1 to Beşiktaş, ending their five-year unbeaten Champions League home record since a 0-1 loss to AC Milan in 2005.

At the 20th minute, Tro scored with a long-range shot. In another match of this group, CSKA Moscow came back from behind to defeat Wolfsburg 2-1 at home.

In the first four rounds of Group B matches, Manchester United had already advanced with three wins and one draw for a total of 10 points, while Beşiktaş was eliminated after four games with one draw and three losses, making this an inconsequential game. In their first encounter, Manchester United won 1-0 away thanks to a goal by Scholes. Considering the upcoming league game against Portsmouth, Ferguson made extensive changes to the lineup, giving reserve players like Macheda, Welbeck, Gibson, Obertan, and Rafael starting opportunities.

The match began with Beşiktaş kicking off. At the 7th minute, Park Ji-sung took a powerful right-footed shot outside the box after feinting past Dag, forcing Rustu into a fumble, but Macheda was offside in his follow-up attempt. At the 9th minute, Fink's right-footed strike narrowly missed after evading Gibson's tackle. At the 13th minute, Anderson's corner kick from the left side found Vidic, whose header just missed the far post. At the 15th minute, Welbeck's pass led to Gibson's weak right-footed shot that went wide.

At the 20th minute, Ozulmes' long pass down the left flank found Tro, who took a left-footed shot from 20 meters out. The ball deflected off Rafael's face and flew into the far corner of the net, giving Beşiktaş an unexpected 1-0 lead over Manchester United! At the 23rd minute, Welbeck's cut-in shot from the left hit the side netting. At the 26th minute, Welbeck and Macheda executed a two-man combination before Welbeck's cross was saved by Rustu.

At the 31st minute, Welbeck turned in the penalty area and passed to Obertan on the right, whose shot was blocked by a defender. At the 38th minute, Gibson's long-range shot from 25 meters out after a tackle went high into the stands, prompting him to cover his face with his shirt. At the 40th minute, Beşiktaş nearly doubled their lead when Fink received the ball unmarked in the box and shot wide of the post! The first half ended with Manchester United trailing Beşiktaş 0-1.

In the second half, neither team made substitutions initially. At the 48th minute, Obertan drove forward and passed to Rafael on the left, whose cross saw Welbeck's effort blocked by a defender. At the 51st minute, Popo's shot from the right corner deflected off a teammate and narrowly missed the crossbar. At the 56th minute, Gibson's diagonal pass found Park Ji-sung, whose turn and left-footed shot went wide. At the 61st minute, Obertan dribbled past four defenders before unleashing a left-footed shot from the edge of the box, which was saved by Rescber.

At the 69th minute, Owen replaced Park Ji-sung. At the 70th minute, Anderson's breakthrough inside the penalty area was stopped by Guven, and Gibson's subsequent free-kick was caught by Rustu. At the 73rd minute, Carrick replaced Gibson, and Evra replaced Rafael. At the 80th minute, Welbeck's through ball was brought down by Guven, and Carrick's direct shot was blocked by the wall. At the 83rd minute, Evra's long-range shot was caught by Rustu. At the 86th minute, Evra's entry into the penalty area was ignored by the referee despite being fouled. Ultimately, Manchester United lost 0-1 to Beşiktaş at home.

Starting lineups:

Manchester United (442): 12-Foster / 2-Neville, 6-Brown, 15-Vidic, 21-Rafael (73’ 3-Evra) / 26-Obertan, 28-Gibson (73’ 16-Carrick), 8-Anderson, 13-Park Ji-sung (69’ 7-Owen) / 27-Macheda, 19-Welbeck

Beşiktaş (4231): 1-Rustu / 4-Kas, 27-Ferrari, 58-Tolun (66’ 44-Guven), 19-Ozulmes / 5-Fink, 28-Enst / 17-Dag, 14-Tro (75’ 15-Insman), 3-Koybası / 13-Popo (85’ 9-Karadeniz)
