Unexpectedly, this relatively minor land sale announcement drew significant attention from numerous Beijing developers and media outlets. According to Deputy Director Kang of the Land Bureau of the Yanjiao Development Zone Management Committee, he had recently received several groups of journalists from Beijing.

The reason why this plot has drawn such high attention lies in the somewhat bizarre conditions set for the sale in the announcement. The following conditions were imposed on the successful bidder: responsible for demolishing any attachments on the land that still affect development; within one year of commencement, responsible for demolishing buildings east of the awarded plot and west of Yan Shun Road, and compensating the displaced persons; within five days of being awarded the bid, sign a separate "Urban Village Reconstruction Agreement" with the Zao Lin Village Committee, and according to the agreement, compensate and settle matters related to the demolition of villager's homesteads, construction and settlement of villagers' relocation buildings, collective public accumulation retention, and other necessary compensation for the villagers; after the sale is finalized, convert part of the proceeds from the 304.56 mu land sale into the price of 299.21 mu land, deduct 25,000 yuan per person for the Zao Lin villagers as pension insurance from this amount, use the balance for already demolished compensation, and any surplus will be used as government land revenue. The remaining collective public accumulation retention, villager relocation building construction and settlement, compensation for the remaining undemolished portion, and other funds related to village reconstruction shall be independently resolved by the successful bidder according to the "Urban Village Reconstruction Agreement".

Carefully analyzing these terms, some people noticed that since there are still attachments affecting development on the offered plot, does this mean that the first-stage development of this land has not been completed?
