Hunan Chengbu TopSun Microelectronics Technology Co., Ltd. is located in the picturesque Chengbu County of Hunan Province. The company was founded in April 2009 and specializes in producing capacitive touch screens, bonding, and cover-lens for capacitive screens. Its predecessor, Shenzhen Jianfei Electronics Technology Co., Ltd., was established in 1998, mainly focusing on the production of LCD liquid crystal display TN, HTN, STN, CSTN series products. To optimize the product structure and enhance business benefits, the company implemented an industrial transfer strategy, cooperating with local enterprises to establish Chengbu TopSun Microelectronics Technology Co., Ltd.

The company's factory covers an area of 32,000 square meters with a building area of 8,600 square meters. It plans to invest a total of 2.5 billion yuan, introducing advanced domestic and foreign production equipment. By adopting a technology development strategy that combines introduction and self-research, it will invest in three phases to develop an annual production project of 30 million pieces of capacitive touch screens, bonding, and glass cover-lens. The products are mainly applied in industries such as mobile phones, GPS navigation systems, cameras, car DVD players, electronic dictionaries, PDAs, MP3s, and MP4 digital products. According to customer requirements, various types of capacitive screens from 1.5 inches to 7 inches can be produced.

The company advocates a professional, pragmatic, and innovative corporate spirit, implementing a talent strategy of appointing people based solely on merit and virtue. It has built a high-quality technical development team and professional sales team. After more than a year of effort, the first phase of the capacitive touch screen/bonding production line has been put into mass production, with products sold to some regions at home and abroad, receiving consistent recognition from customers. The company is accelerating its investment and development pace to meet the growing market demand.
