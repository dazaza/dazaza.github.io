14. Sleepless

There were too many people knocking on the window, so he couldn't sleep well, then he wrote a note and stuck it on the car window: "I don't know the time!" Too sleepy, the driver lay down again. 18. Three Turtles

Three turtles decided to go drink coffee. They just arrived at the coffee shop entrance when it started raining.

So the biggest turtle said to the smallest turtle, "Go home and get an umbrella."

The smallest turtle said, "If you don't drink my coffee, I'll go."

"We won't drink it," the other two turtles promised.

Two years later, the big turtle said to the middle turtle, "Okay, I guess he definitely won't come back, we can drink his coffee."

Just then, a voice came from outside the door, "If you drink it, I won't go."
