[Basic Introduction]

The free family version of "Bailuo NetCop" network control software is specifically designed by Shanghai Bailuo Information Technology Co., Ltd. for parents to manage the online behavior of teenagers and students. All parents across the country can download and use it for free.

In the design and development of the free family version of "Bailuo NetCop" network control software, the concepts of green internet usage and children's internet management have been thoroughly implemented. The free family version of Bailuo NetCop will be permanently provided free of charge to parents.

The free family version of "Bailuo NetCop" network control software is an auxiliary tool for managing children's use of the internet. It can filter out unhealthy websites and parent-defined websites, control and manage chat tools such as QQ, MSN, NetEase Bubble, Sina UC, Yahoo Messenger, and ICQ, record chat content from MSN, Yahoo Messenger, and ICQ, log emails sent and received through OUTLOOK and FOXMAIL, record content of emails sent via the web, control P2P downloads like BT, eMule, eDonkey, and Gnutella, and perform keyword filtering on email content, chat content, forum posts, and blogs. With the child’s IP address known, parents can remotely monitor their child’s online activities in real-time while at work. The software is powerful yet simple to use.

The family free version of "Bailuo NetCop" network control software differs significantly from other software on the market in terms of management philosophy. We believe that managing a child's internet usage should be based on respect and communication. Guidance and education should be emphasized rather than using forceful means to simply block or secretly monitor the child. This is why we have made the "Bailuo NetCop Family Free Edition" into a green software that can be uninstalled at will, processes can be closed, services can be stopped, and no hidden features are present. We hope that parents, after fully communicating with their children, can use the "Bailuo NetCop Family Free Edition" to comprehensively understand their child's online activities. Based on specific situations, parents can collaborate with their children to set up internet management strategies through the software settings to achieve green internet usage. This can help self-discipline or correct shortcomings, educate, and cultivate safe and proper internet habits for children, allowing them to fully enjoy the benefits of the internet.
