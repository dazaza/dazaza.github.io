Honey remedy for constipation:

Honey juice with Atractylodes treats constipation

Take 30 grams of Atractylodes, an appropriate amount of honey and water. Put Atractylodes in water, boil down to 200 ml, add honey and take it. Once a day, use for several consecutive days.

Analysis:

Atractylodes replenishes spleen, benefits stomach, dries dampness, generates blood, benefits qi, harmonizes middle energizer, accelerates blood circulation, lowers blood sugar, makes gastrointestinal secretion vigorous, and enhances peristalsis. Honey replenishes the center and moistens dryness, which can adjust spleen and stomach function. Honey juice with Atractylodes has better curative effect on constipation.

Taro and rice porridge treats constipation

Take 250 grams of taro, 50 grams of rice, a little salt. Peel and cut the taro into pieces, add rice and water to cook porridge, add salt to season when almost cooked, then it can be eaten.

Analysis:

Taro benefits stomach, widens intestine, promotes defecation, detoxifies. Rice strengthens spleen and stomach, supplements central qi, nourishes yin and generates body fluid, removes annoyance and quenches thirst. Taro and rice porridge has certain curative effect on constipation.

Honey juice with potato treats constipation

Take 200 grams of potato, an appropriate amount of honey. Peel and crush the potato, extract juice, then simmer the potato juice until it becomes viscous, add honey and mix evenly. Once a day, 30 milliliters each time, eat on an empty stomach.

Analysis:

Potato harmonizes stomach, strengthens spleen and benefits qi, benefits kidney and strengthens bone, reduces inflammation and detoxifies, can be used for cold stomach, gastrointestinal ulcer, chronic gastritis. Potato has better curative effect on habitual constipation. Choose honey with cooling properties that can purge fire, such as honey from荆flower,槐flower,黄连, and紫cloud.

Vinegar drink treats constipation

When getting up in the morning, drink a small spoon of aged vinegar on an empty stomach, then drink a glass of warm water immediately. In addition to drinking vinegar on an empty stomach, constipated people can also put a little aged vinegar in the soup or dishes of each meal, which is even better.

Analysis:

Aged vinegar contains various amino acids, multiple enzymes helpful for digestive function, and unsaturated fatty acids. It can promote intestinal peristalsis, regulate blood lipids, neutralize toxins, maintain bacterial balance in the intestinal environment, treat and prevent habitual constipation.
