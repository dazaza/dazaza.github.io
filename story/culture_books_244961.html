"Destined to Meet After a Thousand Years" - A White-Collar Worker's Story of Reincarnation and Escaping Marriage

After a car accident, Susan's soul woke up and found herself in the Tang Dynasty, a thousand years ago.

Ruan Ruoruo, daughter of a merchant family in Chang'an, was bullied by the mother and daughter of her father's main family because she was born from a concubine. They forced her to marry an immoral wastrel. To resist this undesirable marriage, she tried to commit suicide by hanging. However, it seemed that fate had other plans as Susan's wandering soul entered her body, taking over her life. How will this journey unfold?

The new Ruan Ruoruo certainly wouldn't submit to "parental orders" and be arranged to marry a "domestic violence man" from the Tang Dynasty. She decided to run away first, escaping from the Ruan household.

This escape set off a series of amusing stories...

"In the Light of Day, I merely called your name, why did you jump so much." Ruan Ruolong said with a half-smile, while asking and observing Ruan Ruoruo. He saw her disordered eyes, her flushed face from running, and her raised skirt, revealing her bare jade-like feet, with crystal-clear ankles adorned with a few blades of grass. He couldn't help but sigh inwardly.

"Although she has been saved, her personality has changed a lot. She is always silent and has forgotten many things from before, even not remembering me. Originally, among the guests at Huayuelou, she ignored everyone except for me, who she treated differently. It seems that the original Shui Bingqing had feelings for Ruan Ruolong, and they might have already made private vows. But now, the current Shui Bingqing is like someone else, making Ruan Ruolong feel like a disappointed person."

As soon as Ruan Ruoruo returned to the mansion, she was scolded by Master Ruan with great anger. After the scolding, she was ordered to copy "Nüjie," the amount doubled to six hundred times. And Master Ruan sternly declared that he would not allow her to leave it unfinished like last time. Not a single word less would be accepted, and he would come to "inspect" the work. She wouldn't be allowed out of her room until it was finished.

Yao Jizong must have just come out of a private room after eating and drinking his fill. He was already eight or nine parts drunk, with only one or two parts sober, which still allowed him to stagger out on his own. Somehow, through his drunken haze, he recognized Ruan Ruoruo and started纠缠ing her again. Ruan Ruoruo was getting a headache. Yao Jizong was already difficult to deal with when sober, let alone when drunk. Why did she keep encountering him? If this was considered fate, then it was truly a karmic relationship.

After dismissing Xing'er from the room, Ruan Ruoruo briefly mentioned yesterday's events to Yao Jizong. Upon hearing this, he beat his chest in frustration and exclaimed: "Such a good opportunity, why didn't you seize any of them? Both of these men are top-notch. One is a golden bachelor, the other a wealthy tycoon. If I were you, I'd want both. Eat at the Lis' and sleep at the Yüs', enjoying both material and spiritual pleasures."

For a long time, Ruan Ruoruo had kept love at arm's length, but suddenly she felt like a sleeping princess awakened by a prince's kiss, discovering such a pure and beautiful love. Her romance with Li Lue was currently completely underground, known only to Yao Jizong. This was because she had said that she would give Li Lue a three-month probation period, and only after passing would they officially announce their relationship. Li Lue didn't understand what a "probation period" was, so Yao Jizong educated him. After some explanation, he suddenly understood: "Why do you people in the 21st century make things so complicated? Do you really need to test if something is feasible first?"

That night at the hour of Hai, Ruan Ruoruo did not go out. Li Lue was angry, but she was even angrier, feeling that Li Lue was too immature, shouting at her over trivial matters. "Hmph, I was thinking of letting you 'turn official' after the three-month trial period, but with this kind of performance, the trial period definitely needs to be extended," she thought fiercely.

It was a refreshing autumn morning, with distant and nearby forests displaying golden paulownia leaves, crimson persimmon leaves, old green pines and cypresses, and newly purpled maples. The clear autumn mountains were beautifully adorned. Li Lue arrived outside the courtyard, captivated by the picturesque scenery. Ruan Ruoruo came out laughing and pushed him gently: "Hurry up and get ready; you'll have plenty of time to enjoy the good scenery when we go chop firewood on the mountain later."
