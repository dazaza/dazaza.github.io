"Yearning Milk" - The Tangled Love of a Quirky Rich Girl

by jqrxass on 2009-08-26 20:13:06

In her youth, she thought what she had was love. When the man she loved passed away, a part of her died too. In this bizarre world, she wore different masks. Thinking herself to be extremely happy and carefree, she couldn't face her true feelings. At the age of 23, she got entangled with three men, and some long-buried dark emotions began to erupt.

Between sinking and struggling, she finally achieved some long-cherished wishes. But that scar became more and more obvious, deeper and deeper...

She asked herself countless times: should she be happy? The happiness given by that person...

"Yearning Milk" Part One

She dressed very carefully, in a white suit skirt set, a pair of light blue patent leather high heels, and black stockings. She seemed like a white-collar worker who got up too early to practice English on the empty ground in front of her door. Unfortunately, this "white-collar worker" disturbed someone's "seclusion". The girl paused for a moment, put down the book in her hand, stared at him for a few seconds, and suddenly said, "What, are you here to cause trouble?"

"Yearning Milk" Part Two

In the cool breeze, vehicles came and went on the road ahead. She looked up and said nonchalantly, "If you promise me two things, I will tear up the IOU, and we will be even." He wasn't just a simple bookworm; he was actually an extremely simple bookworm. That's why he was 28 and still hadn't succeeded in his career, and his girlfriend had left him. That's why he had always been led by Qi Xiaoyu. He swallowed hard, feeling incredulous, and asked, "Are you saying you'll waive those 9,000 yuan?"

"Yearying Milk" Part Three

He was startled because the person in front of him had short brown straight hair, huge eyes, wore a blue striped T-shirt, white shorts with suspenders, and painted canvas shoes. This kind of neutral dressing gave off a youthful vibe. After scrutinizing her for a while, he eventually confirmed that this person was indeed Qi Xiaoyu. Surprisingly, he asked, "You... you... why are you dressed like this? Did you cut your hair?" In his mind, women should have long hair, just like his ex-girlfriend.
