".mobi grafted onto the 3G industry highlights the huge business opportunities of the wireless network"

by now on 2009-04-30 12:22:53

As the first year of 3G in 2009, it officially started the competition among major carriers for mobile Internet. The all-round competition is now underway. On April 16, Telecom took the lead in announcing this important move of releasing 3G numbers, holding high the banner of 3G data cards. It claimed that starting from April 16, the first batch of 120 cities nationwide would release 3G numbers, and at the same time cover more than 300 cities with a 3G network, expecting that by the end of October this year, all towns across the country could enjoy this service. Subsequently, China Telecom's 3G service made its debut at the Boao Forum for Asia 2009 Annual Conference. Minister of Industry and Information Technology Guohua Xi gave full affirmation to the initial application and service of the latest business and technology of 3G at the Boao Annual Conference.

In fact, since the issuance of 3G licenses, the driving force on various industries has been very obvious, especially significant in the performance of communication manufacturers. The development speed of various services of 3G phones is even more rapid, and major telecom operators have launched their own highlight businesses of 3G. Among them, mobile data cards, mobile music, mobile TV, mobile search, video calls, etc., are the first steps for telecom operators to capture the market. It can be seen that the internet speed of mobile and other wireless communication devices is constantly accelerating, and the increase in bandwidth provides broader space for the development of wireless Internet. Since domestic gold service providers like Era Interconnect took the lead in providing .mobi domain registration, including Internet companies such as Sina and Baidu, financial companies such as Bank of China and Industrial and Commercial Bank of China, and traditional well-known companies such as Wahaha, Yanjing, Yili, and Wuliangye, have timely registered .mobi domains.

From the current market situation, more and more enterprises have seen the potential market of mobile wireless networks, and have applied for .mobi wireless domains to build websites suitable for mobile browsing, hoping to extend corporate websites to mobile phones, allowing more people to understand and pay attention to the enterprise. A relevant person in charge of Era Interconnect (www.now.cn) said that .mobi domain is the first top-level domain specifically designed for users accessing the Internet via mobile phones. For ordinary users, .mobi may just be a domain name, but for those enterprises rapidly approaching mobile Internet, it is the key point to realize dreams. Enterprises can build their websites on wireless Internet so that users can browse enterprise information on their mobile phones, thus bringing more customers and business opportunities to enterprises. Therefore, if enterprises want to occupy the commanding height in the fierce business war of the 3G market, they must enter mobile commerce and take down the .mobi domain, otherwise, how can they have any advantage?

I believe that with the popularization of 3G in China, more people will build mobile websites. And according to relevant data, there are currently 670 million mobile users in our country, among which there are 117.6 million mobile Internet users, with a growth rate of 133%. This data reflects the strong potential of our country's mobile Internet. Therefore, only by realizing this earlier can we better meet market demands and promote continuous enterprise development.
