SEO optimization is not a mechanical and repetitive job.

by seo175110 on 2012-02-21 22:32:06

In the eyes of most people, SEO is simply about updating content and increasing external links. This is just the most basic work of SEO, and also the common work content for many who are new to SEO. In fact, SEO is a scientific and systematic project, not repetitive mechanized labor.

1. SEO Should Learn Keyword Analysis

Nowadays, many SEO professionals do not think twice and randomly pick a keyword to start with. As a result, although some websites have relatively good rankings and increased Baidu weight, they still do not receive many orders. This is because many people choose keywords based on assumptions, leading to situations where a keyword has too much competition, and after a long period of optimization, it still does not rank well. In other cases, due to keyword selection bias, although traffic is brought to the website, these users cannot be retained. Therefore, as a qualified SEO professional, one must learn to analyze various keyword data. The sources of keyword data include Baidu related searches, keyword tracking tools, Baidu promotion assistant, various Q&A platforms, etc. After accumulating the data, we screen out the most suitable keywords for optimization.

2. SEO Should Start Considering from Website Construction

Many friends always think that SEO is just about doing rankings, similar to bidding, but costing less money. Indeed, like what is said online, adding external links and content can achieve a better ranking in a short time when the keyword competition is not fierce. However, this ranking cannot be stable. Because our constructed website may not necessarily meet user needs. Therefore, we should consider SEO from the beginning of website construction. Nowadays, many websites are quite typical, especially medical sites, which resemble small versions of 39 Health Network, with abundant content. But although there is a lot of content, much of it is not what users want, and even many small pop-up windows are added. How can such websites retain users? Even if such websites have good rankings, they are useless.

3. Pursuing External Links Frenetically

I believe many external link personnel complain about their heavy workload, having to post a large number of links every day. Some even buy links for short-term benefits. By looking at the workload of external link personnel and the prosperity of the link market, one can see how crazily SEOs pursue external links. But have you thought about why we do SEO? It is to optimize the website to improve its ranking in search engines, thereby obtaining traffic and converting traffic into orders, right? However, if we only pursue external links, it might increase the ranking, but as mentioned in point two, if your website itself is bad, then what's the use?

4. How to Make SEO No Longer Repetitive and Mechanized

Step One: Do a good job in keyword mining and screening.

Step Two: If building a new site, first consider the layout of the website, including keyword layout, etc. If taking over an old site, diagnose the website. Generally speaking, when taking over an old site, you need to do a subtraction. Note that if it is a medium or small-sized website, the target keywords should not be too many, preferably no more than three. The quantity of long-tail keywords can be unlimited.

Step Three: Add appropriate content to the website. What is appropriate content? You can see many websites, especially corporate websites, add articles such as industry news, company news, etc., for optimization. Can such articles provide great value to users? Definitely not! So we need to add appropriate content to our website! For example, if we are an underwear enterprise website, there are two types of articles. One is "How to Choose Suitable Underwear," and the other is "Underwear Industry Sales Growth by 60%." Which article do you think would interest users? Obviously, the former.

Step Four: Perhaps this step will interest everyone, which is reasonably laying out the website's links. First is internal linking, how should internal links be laid out? Currently, perhaps the most popular method is to add internal link anchor texts on keywords in the article. However, the consequence of this practice is that it greatly affects the user's reading experience. We can add hot articles on the sidebars of the website and related articles at the bottom of the article, so as not to affect reading while adding internal links.

Next is external linking. Although with the changes in search engine algorithms, the importance of external links in ranking algorithms has gradually decreased, external links are still an indispensable part of ours. So how should we handle external links? Is it just sitting in front of the computer and posting all day? Of course not!

External links can be divided into general weight external links and high-weight external links. General weight external links include forum signature anchor texts, blogs, and other common external link methods. Taking forum signatures as an example, many people say forum signatures are useless, is that really true? Of course not! It is because we have not mastered the techniques well. We cannot go to a forum, add a personalized signature, and post without looking. This way, the effect is naturally poor! When we go to a forum, we first need to analyze what kind of posts would attract users' attention on that forum. For example, if a post has over 150 clicks and over 60 replies, we record such posts, find their commonalities, and extract the keywords. Then, we write our posts around these keywords, wouldn't our posts attract users' attention? Wouldn't users click on the anchor text in our personalized signature? This way, isn't the value of our external links improved?

High-weight external links, one refers to cross-linking. The main purpose of cross-linking is to obtain one-way links, generally between the main site and our resource site. Cross-linking operations are too complex to elaborate here. Another is friendship links, friendship links are a double-edged sword, because they are mutual links, so we must be cautious when handling friendship links. How to get friendship links? Through some friendship link platforms, such as GO9GO platform, http://seo175110.blog.163.com/blog/static/203584282201212011149137/, we can directly find suitable websites on it, ask the webmaster if they are willing to exchange, or post our friendship link information on the platform. From my personal experience, the quality of friendship links obtained from this platform is quite good, those interested can try it. There is one thing to note, assuming we have 10 friendship links, if 4 of them have been devalued, we cannot remove these sites all at once, because it would cause too much loss of external links, resulting in our website being devalued.

Step Five: Do a good job in data segmentation and accumulation. This is very important for SEO, I hope every friend engaged in SEO work can take it seriously. During the SEO process, we need to record the daily situation of external link releases, content updates, inclusion changes, ranking changes, etc. Of course, in many companies, the person responsible for SEO work is often only one person, so what should we do? We can use some webmaster tools to facilitate our work, such as Aizhan.com, which was the first to develop a Baidu weight query function.
