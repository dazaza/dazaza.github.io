The internet has once again been flooded with a shocking slogan: "Jianyang is a city that invigorates men."

by iface005 on 2011-04-21 11:52:57

Recently, a reporter saw on the homepage of a Sichuan forum a "shocking" online post titled "Thunderstruck! The slogan of Jianyang is too powerful!" The picture attached to the post reads "Jianyang - A city for invigorating yang," with small letters noting "Jianyang mutton soup - the best pot in the west." The poster exclaimed: "Jianyang can come up with almost anything!"

Is this really the slogan of Jianyang? On August 8, Director Wu Yanghai of the External Publicity Office of Jianyang Municipal Committee Propaganda Department said that the official side has never made such a statement. This is just a folk saying proposed five days ago by a post named "Jianyang × Search," roughly about how Jianyang mutton is nourishing and suitable for health preservation. "We oppose this kind of statement," Wu Yanghai expressed that this claim is incorrect and takes things out of context. Regarding whether they will hold relevant personnel accountable, Wu Yanghai stated: "We can only guide public opinion from a positive aspect." They will try to communicate with this private website as much as possible. According to *Tianfu Morning Post*.
