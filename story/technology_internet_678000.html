At around seven o'clock last night, the T180 train from Guangzhou to Jinan arrived in Jinan. A reporter from our website went to Jinan Railway Station (Weibo) to interview Li Chao, the train attendant who has moved countless netizens! Li Chao himself did not know that he is now an "internet celebrity." When the reporter asked him about using his mouth to help suck phlegm from a baby girl, he smiled and told the reporter that for him, this was just an ordinary thing and something he could do easily. Anyone would do this when others are in danger! His words moved the reporter again.

Seeing this, Wang Xiangsen quickly used the walkie-talkie to notify the train conductor Su Tiewan. On one hand, they notified the announcer to broadcast for a doctor, while Su the conductor, who was patrolling the 6th carriage, also rushed to the 9th carriage. "Please make way, don't gather around, keep the air circulating." The conductor Su separated the surrounding passengers and began maintaining order around them. "Conductor, I am a doctor from the Provincial Hospital. I heard on the broadcast that someone in carriage No. 9 was sick, so I came to take a look." A passenger with glasses approached Conductor Su. "Come, please take a look. I don't know what's going on, the child's face turned purple and she's trying hard but not crying." Wang Xiangsen introduced the child's condition to the doctor who had come. At this time, two more doctors arrived at carriage No. 9. After five minutes, the three doctors exchanged their views on the condition: "Madam, your child has some cold-induced phlegm which has surged up and blocked the trachea. The phlegm must be sucked out as soon as possible, but there is no medical equipment on the train..." Upon hearing the doctor's judgment of the child's condition, Ms. Wang choked back tears and fainted. "She just got overwhelmed for a moment, she'll be fine soon," said one of the doctors, pressing Ms. Wang's philtrum while turning back to Conductor Su, "But the child must be treated as soon as possible, any delay could endanger her life."

One-year-old infant fell ill on the train

The train attendant saved the baby by sucking out the phlegm

It turns out that Li Chao is the attendant on this train, trained in first aid, and thus was able to quickly suck out the phlegm. "Thank you so much!" Ms. Wang held the hands of Conductor Su and Li Chao, tears streaming down her face. "My husband and I were over 30 before we had this child, if anything happens to the child, I wouldn't want to live anymore... Thank you to the train conductor and attendants, and everyone else for their help." After saying this, Ms. Wang deeply bowed to the passengers and attendants present, and at this moment, carriage No. 9 erupted into applause...

At 2:30 PM on February 22, aboard the T180 train from Guangzhou to Jinan operated by the Jinan Passenger Transport Section in carriage No. 9, a sudden cry echoed through the carriage, "Baby, what's wrong with you?" Hearing this startled cry, passengers looked towards the passenger in berth No. 12. A young woman was holding a small infant, her eyes filled with anxiety and unease, while the little baby's face was purple, its mouth slightly open, gasping for breath, its little hands tightly clenched, eyes closed with tears streaming, but without making any sound.

Qilu Network, Jinan, February 23 (reporters Zhang Xiaobo, Peng Fang, Li Zheng, correspondent Zhang Minghua) - On February 22, Ms. Wang boarded the T180 train from Guangzhou to Jinan with her one-year-old daughter. Due to her daughter's weak constitution, cold, and high fever, her life was in danger. Fortunately, thanks to the assistance of the train crew from Jinan Passenger Transport Section and enthusiastic passengers, the situation was resolved. After our website published this news first yesterday, it caused strong social repercussions. Many netizens left messages on our website or called the hotline, wanting to know more details and learn more about Li Chao, the most beautiful Shandong person who moved them.

Trachea blocked, child in critical condition

"But we have just left Shangqiu South Station, there are nearly 20 minutes until the next station Heze. Will there be enough time?" The conductor Su frowned, attendant Wang Xiaoxia said, "If not, I can try to suck the phlegm out of the child's mouth, see if it works." Just then, Li Chao said, "I'm a man, with a larger lung capacity, let me try to suck the phlegm out of the child." Saying this, attendant Li Chao approached the child, bent down, and aligned his mouth with the baby's, sucking hard, once, twice, by the fourth attempt, a "snap" sound was heard. Li Chao wiped his mouth and gently patted the baby, "Don't worry, baby, be good..." "Wah wah wah..." At this moment, the baby's loud cries spread throughout carriage No. 9. "Baby..." The just-awakened Ms. Wang immediately hugged the baby and started crying, "Mom was so scared." Seeing this, all the surrounding passengers sighed in relief.

Li Chao explained that the reason he used his mouth to suck the phlegm was due to the urgent situation at the time, being "forced" to do it, and moreover, saving the child wasn't done solely by him; the child's "turning danger into safety" was the collective effort of everyone. Li Chao introduced the situation on the train at that time to the reporter from our website.

At this time, attendant Wang Xiangsen happened to pass by, seeing this scene, he urgently asked the child's mother, "What's going on? Why is the child's face turning purple?" "I don't know either, I just ate a bit of food, and when I turned around, I saw the child like this..." At this point, Ms. Wang had already burst into tears.

It turns out that Ms. Wang brought her daughter to spend the New Year with her husband in Guangzhou, and because the child didn't get enough rest during the holiday and the eating schedule was disrupted, the little one had been coughing continuously. However, the young Ms. Wang and her husband didn't take it seriously, and unexpectedly, the condition worsened on the return journey...
