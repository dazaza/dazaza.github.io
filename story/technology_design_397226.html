Writing a new chapter on the road to reform and shaping life on the way to development

Record of Zhu Jinbiao, the branch manager of涵Jiang Branch of ABC in Putian

Zhu Jinbiao is currently the branch manager of涵Jiang Branch of Agricultural Bank of China (ABC) in Putian. In his many years as a branch manager, he has been courageous in his endeavors and has achieved numerous successes, leading his branch to excel in all areas of business among its peers; he is skilled in management and adheres to compliance, promoting coordinated progress in the three aspects of civilization construction; he is strict with himself and serves the public with integrity, possessing outstanding personal charisma; he takes responsibility and adheres to facts, always leading by example and inspiring morale, uniting and leading all employees to write a new chapter in the reform and development of ABC.

Xiuyu District in Putian, Fujian Province, is the birthplace of Mazu, the goddess of world peace. Since it was established as a district in 2002, it has carried the strategic vision of the municipal Party committee and government to develop the city through ports and strengthen the city through industry, carrying the hopes of three million people in Putian. This is a land full of business opportunities for investment and entrepreneurship. That year, Zhu Jinbiao took office as the party secretary and manager of Xiuyu Branch.

Facing the unfavorable situation of insufficient capital scale, high proportion of non-performing assets, and low market share at the branch, Manager Zhu Jinbiao believed that the most fundamental solution to the problems of unsmooth management mechanisms and low employee enthusiasm was to build a team of cadres and employees capable of fighting tough battles. Upon taking office, Zhu Jinbiao did not rush to ignite the first fire of personnel system reform. He believed that stability during the reform process was very important and that only by carefully crafting and steadily advancing the reform plan could he gain the attention and participation of employees. The management team of the branch widely conducted opinion collection activities, held multiple seminars with cadres and employees, opened up channels for discussion, and reached a consensus. On this basis, the institutional personnel reform was officially launched. After more than a year of adjustment and adaptation, the flat organization structure and the young and professional cadre and employee teams were basically realized, giving a fresh look to all employees of the branch.

To use development to solve difficulties in progress. This is the motto that Zhu Jinbiao strongly advocates and consistently adheres to. In order to seize the commercial opportunities brought by the construction of a port city, he personally led teams to visit enterprises and find customers, innovating ways to provide financial support for local construction. Under his leadership, the marketing atmosphere of the entire bank reached an unprecedented height, winning one excellent customer battle after another, laying a solid foundation for the sustainable development of the entire bank. After several years of effort, Xiuyu Branch of ABC gradually emerged from its operational difficulties, achieving five major transformations and showing a new style. First, the transformation from a struggling branch to a profitable large branch, with profit indicators rising from the original 44th place in the province to entering the top ten profit-making branches in the province; second, the transformation from the last place in the same industry to the first place, with total deposits quadrupling by the end of March 2009, both stock and increment market shares of deposits and loans accounting for more than half of the total in the financial industry, holding an absolute advantage in the first place; third, the transformation from a high proportion of non-performing assets to a branch with no non-performing loans, maintaining a zero rate of newly issued non-performing loans for more than six years since 2003; fourth, the transformation from a mainly low-end customer structure to a significant increase in premium customers, with the proportion of premium customers reaching 98.2%, increasing by 22.3 percentage points, successfully marketing national-level premium enterprises such as Fujian LNG station lines, private capital and intelligence returning leading enterprises, and landing all one national brand product and five provincial famous trademarks in the area in the bank. Fifth, the transformation from partial creation of civilization to comprehensive and high-level creation. Xiuyu Branch has consecutively received evaluations as Class One branch in internal control comprehensive evaluation for six years, maintained no economic cases, no criminal cases, and no major liability accidents for twelve consecutive years, consecutively won the title of Provincial Civilized Unit (8th-10th editions), and successively received honors such as National Advanced Team for Learning, Civilized Construction Advanced Unit of the Fourth National Agricultural Bank System by the Head Office, Provincial Military-Civilian Joint Construction of Spiritual Civilization Advanced Unit, Provincial Advanced Team for Learning Organizations, Provincial Workers' Vanguard, and Provincial Agricultural Bank Financial Advanced Collective, Standardized Grassroots Party Organization Spiritual Civilization Construction Advanced Unit, Credit Management Advanced Unit, Non-Performing Asset Recovery Interest Advanced Unit, Asset Risk Management Advanced Collective, Top Ten Excellent Project Teams of the Branch, and Advanced Unit in Serving Xiuyu District Economy. Zhu Jinbiao was also successively awarded titles such as Advanced Individual in Spiritual Civilization Construction in Putian City, Major Project Marketing Advanced Individual in Fujian Branch, Financial Advanced Worker in Fujian Branch, Top Ten Customer Managers in Fujian Branch, Friend of Employees in Fujian Branch, and May Day Labor Medal of Honor in National Finance.

In the early stages of reform and opening-up, Hanchiang District in Putian City, Fujian Province, was once highly prosperous in commerce and was hailed as "Little Shanghai." However, in increasingly fierce market competition, many township enterprises were unable to adapt to changing circumstances and faded from history's stage, causing "Little Shanghai" to fall silent for a time. In recent years, riding the spring breeze of Haixi construction, "Little Shanghai," as a commercial center, has again embarked on a path of rapid development. In 2009, Zhu Jinbiao assumed the position of branch manager of Hanchiang Branch of ABC, continuing to write a new chapter of innovation and reform.

One must be strong oneself before one can strike iron. Facing a severe internal and external development environment, Manager Zhu Jinbiao explored and proposed the concept of "one body, two wings" development, meaning under market orientation, striving to promote innovation in operating mechanisms and transformation of business structures, focusing on customer needs to integrate products and innovate services, improving customer service quality and sales capabilities, adjusting and optimizing organizational structures and business processes, greatly enhancing the overall competitiveness of the entire bank. First, in terms of systems and mechanisms, seeking innovation. Starting from the performance appraisal mechanism that employees are concerned about, breaking the original interest distribution model, and based on sufficient arguments, issuing three appraisal measures: comprehensive performance appraisal methods for business outlets, comprehensive performance appraisal methods for functional departments, and product marketing pricing methods. And in the implementation process, timely modifying and improving the feedback opinions collected, forming an effective business development driven by interests. At the same time, in terms of management service mechanisms, advocating human-oriented management and customer-first service concepts, aiming to fully utilize talents and abilities, actively approaching the market and serving customers, focusing on enhancing the interdependence and loyalty between banks and enterprises. Second, in terms of customer marketing, seeking breakthroughs. Actively transforming business growth models, adhering to the principle of prioritizing the development of asset businesses to drive the collaborative marketing of liabilities and intermediary businesses, achieving a complete transformation from product-based to customer-based approaches. Relying on opportunities for local industrial revitalization and structural adjustments, key support is given to four traditional cluster industries: footwear and leather clothing, food processing, electronic information, machinery manufacturing, and emerging industries such as arts and crafts, cultural tourism, successfully marketing a number of high-quality customers like New Century Electronics, Huafeng Trading, Ant Semiconductor, Kaisheng Group, and Yifa Group. Further strengthening cooperation with well-known enterprises owning China Brand Names, China Famous Trademarks, and Fujian Brand Names, such as Yinbo Xuejin, Huida Fashion, Tianxia Farm, Sakura Packaging, and Huachang Jewelry, assisting in the construction and enhancement of corporate reputation while realizing mutual prosperity between banks and enterprises. Through methods such as police-bank co-construction and strategic cooperation, institutional customers in the political and legal system, such as Putian Prison, Public Security Bureau, Traffic Police, etc., have been successfully marketed, reserving development and mortgage projects such as Wenxian Square, Ant Real Estate, Wan Jia Real Estate, and signing a strategic cooperation agreement with Jiangkou Town to provide comprehensive financial services for provincial small town construction pilots. Supporting local economic construction while enhancing the overall operating benefits and social image of the entire bank, Hanchiang Branch has entered the ranks of billion-yuan profit-making branches, with loan market share rising from the third place in the same industry to the first place, and receiving the honor of Third-Class Merit for Service Economy in Hanchiang District. Meanwhile, Hanchiang Branch has consecutively obtained Class One Internal Control Evaluation for six years from the provincial branch, maintaining 24 years without economic cases, criminal cases, or major liability accidents, being awarded the titles of Outstanding Unit in Case Prevention by the Head Office and Outstanding Unit in Compliance Culture Construction.

In the face of achievements, Zhu Jinbiao did not relax his strict self-discipline, fighting long-term on the economic front line to maintain the bottom line, always remembering the purpose of the Party and the heavy entrustment of the higher-level bank party committee. He actively participated in senior management training, using his spare time to recharge himself, systematically studying to improve his ability to govern and seek development; strictly implementing the intra-party supervision regulations, establishing correct values, views on power, and professional ethics, constantly reminding himself to achieve self-respect, self-reflection, self-alertness, and self-motivation; good at uniting comrades, paying attention to emotional exchanges, acting out of public interest, and sincerely interacting, demonstrating the fine demeanor of contemporary financial managers with his actions.
