The full set of the ancient costume wuxia TV series "The Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber" starring Deng Chao and Ann Yixuan. The Deng Chao version of "The Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber". The Zhang Jizhong version of "The Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber". Introduction to the plot of the Zhang Jizhong version: At the end of the Yuan Dynasty, Zhang Cuishan, a disciple of Wudang, and Yin Susu, the daughter of the head of the Tian Ying Sect, got involved in the fight for the Dragon Saber and drifted to a deserted island at sea with Xie Xun, the Golden Lion King. Zhang and Yin got married and had a son named Wuji. Ten years later, Zhang Cuishan and his wife returned to Central China but were forced to commit suicide due to their involvement with the Dragon Saber, leaving behind their orphaned son, Zhang Wuji. By chance, Zhang Wuji mastered extraordinary martial arts skills, saved the Ming Sect from peril, and was elected as its leader. Princess Zhao Min of the Yuan Dynasty fell in love with Zhang Wuji after several encounters with the Ming Sect and ultimately gave up her title as princess and broke ties with her father and brother for him. In addition to Zhao Min, Zhang Wuji also developed romantic feelings with Zhou Zhiruo of the Emei Sect, his cousin Yin Li, and Xiaozhao, the Persian Holy Maiden. Zhou Zhiruo was the first to discover the secret of the Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber - that they contained maps to martial arts manuals. She stole the sword and saber, killed Yin Li, and framed Zhao Min. Unaware of the truth, Zhang Wuji resented Zhao Min, who was unable to defend herself and could only watch helplessly as Zhang Wuji married Zhou Zhiruo. However, news about Xie Xun interrupted Zhang Wuji's wedding to Zhou Zhiruo. Zhang Wuji finally realized that Zhao Min was his true love for life. After resolving the siege of Shaolin Temple by the Yuan army and deeply understanding the true meaning of martial arts, Zhang Wuji resigned as the leader of the sect and retired to the mountains with Zhao Min. This is the new version of "The Heaven Sword and Dragon Saber" starring Deng Chao.
