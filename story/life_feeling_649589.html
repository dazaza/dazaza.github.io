by huiming on 2011-12-31 01:00:21

Gray avatar, my fallen heart

We are still friends, those most distant friends. If time could flow backwards, what could we have grasped again?

In the lonely night, the dark and melancholy night slowly devoured me. I struggled helplessly, letting it erode my fragile heart.

The sorrow that rippled in my heart was not hidden by the night but grew even faster, wandering in the boundless night. Gradually, the night was filled with the taste of sorrow.

I opened the music player and listened to a song by Xu Song called 'Gray Avatar'. The sad melody played in my ears, with soft tunes and sorrowful lyrics. It's like the sharp cold wind of this winter season. Every note, every lyric interpreted my current mood so perfectly.

I scrolled through the avatars of my online friends, the dim ones remained silent. The shining ones didn't flicker for me. Maybe it was my coldness that distanced them from me, making our distance further and friendship drift away with the passage of time. Very helpless, there was a vague sadness in my heart, but I didn't care too much. Perhaps, where there is no thought of me, why should I linger? Without my concern, why should I care too much?

In this noisy world, there are too many people and things. Too many people eventually become passers-by, and too many things eventually become memories.

Without purpose, I scrolled through each friend and stopped at your avatar. Looking at the familiar yet strange avatar always made me feel a lot of sorrow. Your tenderness inadvertently flooded my mind, your unique face appeared intermittently. Time slipped through my fingers, leaving behind painful memories.

Your gray avatar will no longer flicker quietly, and my gray heart also stops beating coldly and silently. I just stared blankly at your gray avatar, holding my aching chest to stop tears from flowing.

The sorrow spreading in the long night can only be tasted alone. Who would listen to my sorrows? Who can understand this loneliness? I can only use my sad words on a thin piece of paper to write a full-of-sorrow yearning, and the disappointment of you being offline and my loneliness.

When I opened our chat interface, the chat record stopped at that moment, but I just stared blankly. We once made vows here, promising eternal love. I made a vow to you, and you gave me a promise. In the virtual network, we talked about the most real feelings. That cheap encounter has now turned into sadness.

Tonight, I am hesitating in place while you have already flown far away, to a place I cannot reach.

Tonight, I observe silently, praying for you, hoping you will be forever happy.

I think the farthest distance in the world is not life and death, not separation. Not standing in front of you while you don't know I love you! But rather, we used to support each other, and now we remain silent, becoming the most familiar strangers. Why is the most familiar stranger not the best outcome after breaking up, but the most common one?

A song "Gray Avatar" has already ended, but the lyrics still echo in my mind.

The final plea was never spoken

We are still friends

That kind of most distant friends

The tenderness you gave

Is all preserved in the records

Your gray avatar doesn't flicker anymore

Not even a simple greeting
