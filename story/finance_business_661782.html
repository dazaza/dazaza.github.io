53% ABV 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine 500ml

The bottle shape of the 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine is meticulously crafted through 105 processes, with a special introduction of British low-temperature red technology, 24K sand gold firing, and 24K bright gold pressing. The bottle's lines are smooth and graceful, with proper tension and relaxation. Its pure red color is both restrained and unrestrained, perfectly showcasing the extraordinary taste of the 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine.

The 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine is one of the key series launched by Sichuan Lang Wine Group Co., Ltd. It features a rich aroma, thick and mellow flavor, sweet and refreshing aftertaste, clean finish, and elegant style. The newly developed product innovates boldly in packaging while ensuring the original quality and style characteristics, breaking traditional boundaries and pioneering a super-fashion trend in白酒 packaging with its novel and unique design.

The 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine uses rare glutinous red sorghum from the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau at an altitude of over 1,000 meters, natural Lang spring water from deep mountains below 1,000 meters, adheres to ancient brewing methods passed down for thousands of years, and undergoes cyclic fermentation involving billions of microorganisms. Each bottle of Red Flower Lang Wine goes through nine rounds of steaming, eight fermentations, seven distillations, a production cycle of one year, and cave storage periods of more than 10 or even 15 years, undergoing countless refinements to create a precious sauce-flavor masterpiece containing over 400 aromatic components, green and healthy. The 10-Year Red Flower Lang Wine has prominent sauce fragrance, thick and clean freshness, elegant delicacy, long-lasting aftertaste, and enduring empty glass aroma.

- 1994: Lang Wine became the first in the national famous wine industry to pass international quality certification.

- 1996: Lang Wine was the first among national famous wineries to obtain the right to use the Green Food mark.

- 1997: The "Lang" brand trademark was recognized as a "China Famous Trademark" by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, enjoying special protection worldwide.

- 1999: The National Quality Supervision Bureau and Standard Sample Committee designated 39% ABV sauce-flavored Lang Wine as the standard sample wine for low-alcohol sauce-flavored Baijiu of the People's Republic of China.

- 2000: "Lang" brand 12-year Special Pure Lang Wine was listed as an exquisite Chinese famous wine.

- 2001: "Lang" brand Tianbao Cave Stored Wine was rated as a "Famous Innovative Brand of Chinese Baijiu" by the China Food Industry Association.

- 2004: Red Flower Lang Wine was awarded the title of "Designated Honor Product of the First Chinese Baijiu Science and Technology Conference" by the Baijiu Professional Committee of the China Food Industry Association.

- 2006: Lang Wine obtained the certification mark for pure grain solid-state fermented Baijiu.

- 2008: "Gulin Lang Wine Traditional Brewing Process" was included in the "National Intangible Cultural Heritage" protection list.

- 2009: Lang Wine was selected for the "China 500 Most Valuable Brands" with a brand value of 8.779 billion yuan, ranking 75th nationwide and 3rd in the Baijiu industry.
