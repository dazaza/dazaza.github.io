Personal views:

1. First of all, "Little Ya Reporting on the Two Sessions" itself is a very good idea from CCTV: "Little Ya" is a "unique appellation" for women in Chinese culture, whether it represents male preference or casual references to girls, it ultimately symbolizes the representative of disadvantaged groups in Chinese history; while the "Two Sessions" is the highest symbol of power in our country...

2. In terms of program content, especially the Little Ya Reporting on the Two Sessions in 2007, starting from small matters and focusing on people's livelihoods, it has bridged the gap between ordinary people and the Two Sessions...

3. Over seven years, "Little Ya Reporting on the Two Sessions" has already possessed all the elements of a television brand and an internet brand...
