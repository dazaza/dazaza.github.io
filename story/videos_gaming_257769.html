by nuannuanleng on 2009-11-09 22:16:20

The online game company 9you, which became famous together with "Audition", won the "most anticipated by users" award and the "best cute version game" award of this year's "Golden Wings Award" with a "Legendary Weapon" and "SD Gundam Online".

Originally, it was not worth mentioning that a messy company won a messy award. However, the problem is that after the award ceremony ended, the marketing general manager of 9you answered the media's question about whether 9you has obtained the operation rights of "Dragon Ball Online" in China: "9you has an absolute advantage, and currently both parties are still negotiating."

This may indeed be good news for 9you, but I think it is a terrible thing for players.
