Shanghai Patent Week: Self-Weighing Luggage Patent Gains Attention

At the opening ceremony of Shanghai Patent Week on November 10th, the ten outstanding patent products selected by experts were all awarded. Slightly different from previous years' awards, this evaluation placed more emphasis on the "production value" of patents, namely whether they had been successfully transformed into mass-produced goods and generated profits. A batch of products with not-so-"high-tech" but effective market development were selected.

Among the ten outstanding patent products that won the award this time, six were invention patents and four were utility model patents. Among them, the selection of "self-weighing luggage" from Shanghai Tingxin Bag Company particularly caught attention: in terms of technical content, this project is a utility model, slightly inferior to an invention patent; in terms of sales performance, it cannot be compared with chemical or manufacturing patents worth millions of yuan, but its excellent market development became the main reason for its selection.

According to Tingxin Company, this self-weighing luggage is equipped with an automatic weight sensing device, and a display window is added to the front of the luggage. Just before traveling, simply lift the packed luggage slightly, and the weight of the luggage will be displayed, avoiding the embarrassment of discovering over-weight luggage just before check-in, providing much convenience for business people who often travel. It is reported that this luggage sells well in overseas markets, and the company received a large number of orders at the Canton Fair this year. In the domestic industry where simple OEM production leads to decreasing profits, Tingxin Company's profit in the past three years reached 690,000 yuan.

There are also patent products with both high technical content and sales profits among the award-winning products this time, such as "Fingerprint Spectrum Analysis Method of Traditional Chinese Medicine Compound for Treating Cardiovascular Diseases" from Shanghai Hutchison Pharmaceuticals Co., Ltd., which has accumulated sales profits of 51.7889 million yuan in the past three years. In addition, high cold-resistant and hydrolysis-resistant reinforced nylon from Shanghai Jinfa Technology Development Co., Ltd., and the comprehensive management platform for information security and network security of communication networks from Shanghai Liangjiang Communication System Co., Ltd., have also created profits of millions of yuan within the first three years of patent implementation.

However, this evaluation also revealed some problems. Among the more than 200 participating invention patents, most of them are "written patents" that only stay on the patent certificate, without practical production, or producing only one or two sample machines per year; some have never been produced or sold, yet optimistically estimated "annual output value of millions of yuan" in their application materials... In the eyes of expert judges, these are hard to be called "excellent patent products." Xu Linson, deputy director of the Shanghai Intellectual Property Service Center, said, "We hope every enterprise can make good use of its patents and create as much value as possible."

Vice Mayor Zhao Wen attended the opening ceremony of the Patent Week.
