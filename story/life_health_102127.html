Salt and ginger water foot bath to remove foot odor

Massaging acupoints to treat athlete's foot and foot odor

When washing your feet, soak both feet in warm water in a basin for two or three minutes until both feet are warm. Use one foot's heel to press slightly behind the other foot's toe seam, then push the heel forward to the tip of the toes and return, pulling lightly backward and pushing heavily forward, being careful not to rub the skin raw. Rub each toe seam 50-80 times, alternating between both feet. The speed should be 100-120 times per minute. Do this once every night. If the upper part of the skin on the feet with severe athlete's foot is broken, this method is not suitable.

Cooking soybean water to treat athlete's foot

Use 150 grams of crushed soybeans to cook water, simmer for about 20 minutes, the water should be about over 1 kilogram. When the water is warm enough to wash your feet, use it to soak your feet, you can soak for a longer time. It has an excellent effect on treating athlete's foot, the feet do not peel, and the skin remains moisturized. Generally, soaking for three or four consecutive days will show results.
