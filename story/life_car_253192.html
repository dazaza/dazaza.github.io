Kumho Tire Service Vehicle: Enjoy a New Experience of Mobile Services

by ncxkvs on 2009-10-29 01:02:48

Recently, Kumho Tire successfully introduced the mobile "service vehicle" model to China, and held a grand launch ceremony at the Tire Pro car station on Qixun Road in Shanghai on August 19th. The event announced that this service model will soon be rolled out nationwide, and showcased the "full functionality" of the mobile service vehicle to the public.

The service vehicle is equipped with tire mounting and dismounting machines, tire balancing machines, air pumps, and other tire repair tools and instruments. Kumho Tire's professional customer service personnel provide on-site services to car owners, including tire rotation, repairs, and replacements, offering convenient mobile services. Additionally, the site provides timely safety inspections, tire pressure checks, and related suggestions for tire maintenance and care. In the future, car owners will be able to enjoy Kumho Tire's new "mobile service" anytime in areas with high vehicle density such as residential communities, highway rest stops, and parking lots.

According to reports, Kumho Tire has always been concerned about the driving safety of car owners. For two consecutive years, the company has hosted free tire inspection events across the country, dedicated to promoting safety knowledge regarding proper use and maintenance of tires, receiving widespread participation and positive feedback from car owners. During this year's summer thank-you event for customers, Kumho Tire's free tire inspection service was once again warmly welcomed by many car owners. According to the latest feedback from 100 designated Kumho Tire retail stores in 65 cities across the country, including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, consumer participation remains strong.

Kumho Tire General Manager Kim Hyung-kyun stated that the introduction of the service vehicle aims to provide convenient and professional services, creating a "mobile station" that consumers can trust. Last year, Kumho Tire introduced the "Tirepro Car Station" service model to China, truly realizing comprehensive one-stop services, thereby promoting the development of China's high-end tire service market. Eventually, Kumho Tire's service vehicles, which are the first in the industry to be launched, will become "mobile stations" spread across the country, actively providing the most complete and convenient services to consumers, allowing more Chinese consumers to experience the thoughtful and professional services offered by Kumho Tire.
