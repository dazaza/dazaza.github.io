Guilin self-driving tour routes (recommended)

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-31 11:19:56

Driving your beloved car through the picturesque landscapes of Guilin feels like traveling within a painting. This sense of leisure can only be found in Guilin, and we provide comprehensive self-driving services for you: including hotel reservations, scenic spot tickets, guide services, etc., to make your free trip more comfortable! Below are some recommended routes. You can tell us your requirements, and we will offer you professional advice and considerate services!

The guide fee for self-driving is 100 yuan per day per car.

**Guilin One-day Tour A Route**

One-day tour of the city's highlights (attractions: Ludi Cave 60 yuan/person, Fubo Mountain 15 yuan/person, Xiangbi Mountain 25 yuan/person, Diecai Mountain 20 yuan/person, Chuanshan Park 45 yuan/person, Jingjiang Palace 50 yuan/person, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area 50 yuan/person, Li River Ethnic Minority Scenic Area 50 yuan/person, Seven Star Park 35 yuan/person, Seven Star Cave 30 yuan/person, Ocean World 80 yuan/person, Dreamlike Li River 150 yuan/person, one-day water tour of the city's highlights 50 yuan/person, evening two rivers and four lakes ring city water system tour 149 yuan/person).

**Guilin Surrounding Areas One-day Tour B Route**

Guan Rock Guinness, Gudong Forest Oxygen Bar (attractions: Guan Rock 60 yuan/person (excluding up and down mountain slide car fees), Gudong Forest Waterfall tickets: 60 yuan/person).

**Guilin Two-day Tour**

Day 1: One-day tour of the city's highlights (Ludi Cave, Fubo Mountain, Xiangbi Mountain, Chuanshan Park, Duxiu Peak Park, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area, etc.) In the evening, take a boat tour of the "Two Rivers and Four Lakes" ring city water system.

Day 2: One-day tour of the Li River highlights, Yangshuo land scenery highlights tour, return to Guilin in the afternoon.

**Guilin Three-day Tour**

Day 1: One-day tour of the city's highlights (Ludi Cave, Fubo Mountain, Xiangbi Mountain, Yu Mountain Park, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area) In the evening, take a boat tour of the "Two Rivers and Four Lakes" ring city water system.

Day 2: One-day tour of the Li River highlights, Yangshuo land scenery highlights tour.

Day 3: One-day tour of Guan Rock (can add Gudong Waterfall). Driving route: Sanyidi → Gui-Mo Road → Dawei (turn right) → Caoping Hui Ethnic Township → Guan Rock (takes about 50 minutes along the way).

**Guilin Four-day Tour**

Day 1: One-day tour of the city's highlights (Ludi Cave, Fubo Mountain, Xiangbi Mountain, Yu Mountain Park, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area) In the evening, take a boat tour of the "Two Rivers and Four Lakes" ring city water system.

Day 2: One-day tour of the Li River highlights, Yangshuo land scenery highlights tour, return to Guilin in the afternoon.

Day 3: One-day tour of Guan Rock (can add Gudong Waterfall).

Day 4: One-day tour of Lemandiyi (can add Lingqu Canal). Driving route: Zhongshan North Road → Xing'an Linghu (takes about 1 hour 20 minutes).

**Guilin Five-day Tour A Route**

Day 1: One-day tour of the city's highlights (Ludi Cave, Fubo Mountain, Xiangbi Mountain, Yu Mountain Park, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area) In the evening, take a boat tour of the "Two Rivers and Four Lakes" ring city water system.

Day 2: One-day tour of the Li River highlights, Yangshuo land scenery highlights tour, return to Guilin in the afternoon.

Day 3: One-day tour of Guan Rock (can add Gudong Waterfall).

Day 4: One-day tour of Lemandiyi (can add Lingqu Canal). Driving route: Zhongshan North Road → Xing'an Linghu (takes about 1 hour 20 minutes).

Day 5: Special line tour of Xionghu Resort.

**Guilin Five-day Tour B Route**

Day 1: One-day tour of the city's highlights (Ludi Cave, Fubo Mountain, Xiangbi Mountain, Yu Mountain Park, Liu Sanjie Scenic Area) In the evening, take a boat tour of the "Two Rivers and Four Lakes" ring city water system.

Day 2: One-day tour of the Li River highlights, Yangshuo land scenery highlights tour, return to Guilin in the afternoon.

Day 3: One-day tour of Guan Rock (can add Gudong Waterfall).

Day 4, Day 5: Longsheng two-day tour (visit Longji Rice Terraces, hot springs, etc.). Driving route: City center → Airport Road → pass toll station (opposite Xionghu Resort) → go straight on the cement road → Longsheng County → Longsheng Hot Spring (takes about 2 hours 30 minutes).
