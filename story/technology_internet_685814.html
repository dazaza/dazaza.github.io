Chapter 1201: Mi Zian was hospitalized! "Dad, why aren't you answering the phone?" Bei Bei lent her nail clipper to Su Zi and then stood by to watch. Seeing Qin Luo's phone ringing but not answering it, she prompted him. "I will." Qin Luo smiled and pressed the answer button. "Hello." "Qin Luo, this is Si Xuan." The phone transmitted a woman's hurried voice. "Come to the hospital quickly, Zian had an accident." "Hospital? What happened to Zian?" Qin Luo was shocked. "What happened?" "You come quick. First Hospital, room 1106. There are too many people here, it's inconvenient to talk on the phone." Chen Si Xuan said.

Qin Luo didn't bother asking Su Zi to cut his nails anymore, he stood up and prepared to leave. He told Bei Bei, "Bei Bei, you stay with Aunt Su Zi and play with Jia Bao. I'll come pick you up later."

"There's nothing else we can do," Bei Bei helplessly spread her small hands.

It was easy to persuade Bei Bei, but Jia Bao, who kept her eyes on the TV screen, wouldn't let go. When she saw Qin Luo about to leave, she cried out and got up from the ground. She ran up and hugged Qin Luo's waist, refusing to let go.

"Qin Qin!"

Bei Bei could reason and bribe with benefits, but Qin Luo still hadn't found a way to deal with Jia Bao. You say something, she doesn't understand. You offer her advantages, she doesn't know what they are. The only method is to massage her to sleep. But doing this all the time could be harmful to her body. Qin Luo didn't want to change this natural human rule.

Qin Luo stopped, gently stroked Jia Bao's head, and smiled as he said, "Jia Bao, I'm going out to handle some things. Can you stay and watch TV?"

"Qin Qin Miao Miao"

Qin Luo felt reassured in his heart. He knew this was Jia Bao's way of expressing herself. Although this method of expression was still very vague, it was still a kind of progress, wasn't it? As long as they were given more time, he would definitely bring the lively and adorable Princess Jia Bao, who could speak fluent Chinese, to the world. What the Swedish public would think remains unknown.

"Is it that you want me to watch 'Tom and Jerry' with you?" Qin Luo smiled and asked. "Can Bei Bei accompany you? Bei Bei can also tell you stories. Bei Bei, you accompany Jia Bao."

"Hmph. I don't want to accompany her," Bei Bei coldly snorted. She was very dissatisfied with Jia Bao's attachment to Qin Luo. Dad is my dad, only I can act like this. Why can you?

Yes. Miss Bei Bei was jealous.

"Bei Bei, come on," Qin Luo was sweating profusely. Communicating with children was really a brain-consuming task. More importantly, he now had to deal with two of them.

Bei Bei did not respond. She pouted and glared at Jia Bao angrily.

Su Zi smiled, walked over to Bei Bei's ear, and whispered something.

Bei Bei's gloomy face immediately brightened, her eyes squinted into crescents as she said, "Really? Sister Su Zi, you're so nice."

After hugging and kissing Su Zi twice, Bei Bei proudly walked up to Qin Luo and said, "For Sister Su Zi's sake, I'll help take care of this little brat. But you must come back soon, Genius Doctor latest update."

"..."

Finally free, Qin Luo had Jesus drive to the Yanjing First Hospital immediately.

As soon as Qin Luo arrived at the hospital entrance, he realized something was wrong because there were quite a few reporters in the yard and hall, some carrying cameras and microphones. Others even carried camcorders for live broadcasts right at the entrance of the First Hospital.

"Damn," Qin Luo cursed. If the reporters knew, the situation would become even more complicated.

Qin Luo didn't know where Room 1106 was in the First Hospital, so he asked a nurse. The nurse looked at him cautiously and asked, "What do you want to do?"

"I came to visit a friend," Qin Luo replied.

The nurse looked down on him and pointed at the reporters outside, saying, "They all say that."

"..."

"Dear nurse, we really came to visit a friend," Jesus grinned and said. "Miss Mi is a very good friend of mine. We met in France."

The nurse glanced at Jesus and blushed slightly, softly saying, "It's not that I don't believe you... but even if I tell you where 1106 is, you won't be able to get in. It's best to have someone inside meet you."

Having no choice, Qin Luo called Chen Si Xuan again. Chen Si Xuan said Room 1106 wasn't in the general ward but in the VIP ward, which required walking to the back courtyard.

She asked Qin Luo to go to the staircase first, where security guards would be guarding the door, and normal people couldn't enter. She would come out to greet him.

Qin Luo agreed and then headed towards the back courtyard entrance with Jesus.

Soon, Chen Si Xuan, wearing a silver harem pants and a light pink butterfly sleeve T-shirt, rushed over. A black silk scarf was wrapped around her neck, disheveled due to the strong wind.

"Qin Luo!" Chen Si Xuan called out.

Seeing someone coming to pick them up, the security guard finally let them pass.

Qin Luo quickly went through the safety channel and greeted Chen Si Xuan, asking, "What exactly happened? Why is Zian hospitalized?"

"It's those bastards' fault," Chen Si Xuan cursed in front of Qin Luo. "Zian was rehearsing today, and one song required her to ascend via a lift platform. However, the power suddenly went out when the platform was halfway up, causing Zian to fall off. At that moment, the steel wires couldn't provide any protection."

Upon hearing this, Qin Luo also felt anger rising within him. What kind of place is Bird Nest? How could there be a random power outage?

Clearly, this was no accident; it was a deliberate event. Moreover, the person responsible should be an old acquaintance of Qin Luo.

"How is Zian now?" Qin Luo asked.

"Not too serious," Chen Si Xuan said. "But there is still a slight bone crack. Healing bones takes a hundred days, and the doctor said Zian needs three months of rest. Normally, it would be fine, but how can she rest now? There's only a week left until the concert. Posters have been put up, advertisements are everywhere, the venue has been rented, and tickets have already been sold out. How can it be delayed now? Zian is extremely anxious, Yu Jing reported the situation to the company, and their big boss is also on his way."

Qin Luo frowned deeply and said, "Let's go see Zian's condition first."

The conditions in the VIP ward were clearly much better than those in the ordinary wards outside, and it was an independent space without constant traffic or noise. There were fountains and artificial mountains, lush flowers and grass, making it very quiet and comfortable to stay here. It was like a paradise away from the world.

Of course, the prices of both places couldn't be compared.

Following Chen Si Xuan, they arrived at the eleventh floor of a building. As soon as they stepped out of the elevator, they saw the corridor filled with people. Not only were Mi Zian's bodyguards and assistants there, but also hospital staff and Bird Nest employees. No wonder Chen Si Xuan didn't want to talk much on the phone earlier—there were too many people here, and some matters could lead to endless speculation if leaked to the media.

The director of the Bird Nest Management Committee, whom Qin Luo had met once before, was also among them. Qin Luo forgot his name but remembered his ever-changing face clearly.

Qin Luo noticed very clearly that when the director saw him approaching, his eyes were filled with ridicule and disdain. "So what if you're a big star? What if you're a king or queen? Offend someone you shouldn't, and you're still worth less than dog shit."

Qin Luo didn't have the mood to deal with him at the moment and followed Chen Si Xuan into the ward.

There were fewer people in the ward, only Mi Zian's agent Yu Jing and two doctors were present, as well as writer Qiu Huadong discussing online literature.

Mi Zian was still wearing her shiny sequined performance outfit, lying listlessly on the bed. Her braids were undone in several places, looking somewhat messy. Of course, some might think this made her look cooler.

When she saw Qin Luo come in, she just managed to force a smile, her lips trembling slightly.

"What's the situation?" Qin Luo asked.

The doctor in the white coat glanced at Qin Luo and said, "Slight bone fracture. She cannot move in the short term, let alone perform on stage. Otherwise, it would pull the wound and make the bone crack worse. There's a possibility of paralysis."

"If she needs to be on stage in a week, is there any way?" Qin Luo asked.

Mi Zian's previously lackluster eyes lit up instantly, staring fixedly at Qin Luo and the two doctors examining her.

"I've already said it. Her bones are cracked, she can't move and needs some time to rest. Didn't you hear what I said?" This doctor was somewhat temperamental. "You say perform and she performs, whose responsibility would it be then? Are you the doctor or am I?"

"I am a doctor," Qin Luo said.

"You..."
