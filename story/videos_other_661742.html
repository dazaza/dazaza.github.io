Live happily.

by ruydyir02 on 2012-02-09 12:31:45

I don't particularly like children, it's not just distaste, but hatred. Due to the adults' favoritism and preference for children, I have lost a close friend in my life.

We all went through childhood, experienced the days of being doted on by our parents, but do you adults know that no matter who you love more, human lives are equal. You have no right to make us give up hope for life...

Lord, please look at all this with kindness, please save the souls that pray to you every day.. On that day, I gently placed a bunch of flowers in front of her tombstone. The her on the tombstone smiled brightly, dazzlingly. I stood quietly in front of the tombstone, letting the wind caress my face, letting the leaves chase and kiss my cheeks. I just stood there peacefully, with a faint smile on my face, but this smile was melancholy....

On a day in 2008, when I heard about the car accident at her house, I rushed to the scene. Because it was a rear-end collision, the parents sitting in the front row were unharmed, but she and her one-year-old brother sitting in the back were trapped in the car. The scene was chaotic, mixed with the cries of children and the shouting of adults. And I, heard the worst thing happen:

"Are you the parents of the children?"

"Ah, yes, yes."

"The two children can be rescued, but...the younger boy's lower body is completely paralyzed..."

"What can be done?"

"There is a way, but..." I heard the doctor pause. "Only if the older girl dies, can the younger boy be successfully rescued."

"Yes, as long as the younger one is fine, anything is fine!" I also heard their immediate response from her parents.

I rushed to them and shouted: "Is Luo not your child? Is it because of your favoritism that you let her die? It's too unfair to her!" I gritted my teeth, waiting for their answer. Her parents waved their hands: "Go away, it's our family affair, none of your business!" Hearing this, I felt disappointed. Yes, I had no ability to save her, no capital to save my good friend. My eyes were already covered with a layer of frost. I coldly laughed and turned to walk towards her who was covered in blood: This is the fate of light and darkness, the unavoidable betrayal in reality, the last bit of warmth will be completely abandoned. People always betray each other.

I walked to her side and quietly looked at her. She smiled at me: "Jing, you must live on, live happily!" I shook my head. Without her, wherever I am, I am in hell. She looked at me calmly and said with her last strength: "Including my share." After saying that, she rested peacefully, with a sweet smile, gently closing those crystal-clear eyes. The sunlight from the sky rained down, her eyes lost their color, white wings enveloped her.. She slept, never to wake up again....
