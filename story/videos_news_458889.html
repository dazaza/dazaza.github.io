Internet exposure: Changsha government vehicle hit a person after a car accident, then was surrounded by a thousand people who overturned the car

by anonymous on 2011-07-06 15:18:38

When the words "drunk driving", "hit and run", "assault", and "government department's car" are put in front of us, each one grabs attention and stirs up emotions. No matter how much power or status you have, when you're out there, you must act like a "grandson" and believe in the power of the people.

On July 1st, at the entrance of the Dawn Group on People's Middle Road in Changsha, Hunan, a police car with an Xiang 0 license plate fled after colliding with a taxi and injuring its driver. It was eventually blocked by taxis on this section of road. The driver of the police car locked the windows and doors tightly, refusing to get out and declining to accept the on-site traffic police inspection and apology. Thousands of onlookers gathered, causing a complete traffic jam in both directions. The crowd almost overturned the car. Even after the traffic police, patrol police (including Deputy Director Zhang of the Yuhua District Public Security Bureau) arrived at the scene, they were unable to control the situation. After a three-hour standoff, the driver reluctantly opened the window for a breathalyzer test, which showed an alcohol level of over 140 mg per milliliter. Three and a half hours later, the door was opened, and under the protection of special police and traffic police, the driver left the scene but was still beaten by more than a thousand onlookers!

The scene where the government vehicle collided with another car and assaulted someone caused a complete traffic jam in both directions. The crowd almost overturned the car. - Xiang 0-S: Various departments of the Provincial Public Security Department.

- Xiang 0-ST: Criminal Investigation Team.

- Xiang 0-SJ: Supervision Team.

- Xiang 0-SX: Fire Department.

- Xiang 0-R0: Police Association of the Provincial Public Security Department (can be purchased).

- Xiang 0-B: Represents Zhuzhou.

- Xiang 0-letter: Represents various cities.
