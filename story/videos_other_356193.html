The weather is like this.

From: 10086

Content Information: The Emergency Office of the Provincial Government and the Provincial Meteorological Bureau remind you: From the 22nd to the 23rd, there will be a large-scale heavy rainfall weather process in our province. Please pay close attention to the latest weather information and take timely preventive measures.

Wow, what a wonderful day! Never would have thought that Funan's weather forecast would be so accurate. One day in May, when I woke up today, I found that it was already raining heavily outside.

In such weather, I actually feel like staying in bed all day. It's been a long time since I've had this feeling. It's pouring heavily outside. Haha, I just went out to the balcony and saw that the streets in Taicheng were already flooded.

Fortunately, I live on a higher floor, so I'm not afraid of flooding. It's 8 o'clock now. I rarely wake up this early. To be precise, I woke up at around 6:30 today. A sense of oppression gradually rose in my heart, but then I fell asleep again until around 7:30. Haha, only then did I remember that the clothes outside hadn't been brought in yet. As expected, on the balcony, only mine were left. Finally liberated...

But they're all wet!

This morning, I didn't feel like sleeping instead. Thinking about my high school days back then, haha, those good friends, thank you for accompanying me through those three memorable years worth cherishing! In the past, every time it rained, some girls in the dormitory always asked for leave to go to the rooftop to get drenched in the rain. I also tried it many times back then...

But now I won't do that anymore. As I grow older, I've learned to cherish myself! But I still miss those days, the pure enjoyment of getting soaked in the rain, not because of any troubles or issues leading to the decision to get drenched. These are two different feelings...

So don't say how foolish we used to be. We've finally been liberated. It was just us enjoying the whimsy of our youth. Haha, on rainy days like this, I find myself reminiscing about our past...

What about you? Friend!
