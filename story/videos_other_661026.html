Because the case details were still unclear, the police were still investigating.

The village director of Dianzi Wang Village, Cao Shui Li, told reporters that besides Wang, some other villagers also received threatening calls from strangers. There were also incidents of strangers breaking into villagers' homes to cause trouble, and some villagers' vehicles were smashed. "Because of multiple competitions, someone intentionally created an atmosphere of urgency."

At around 8 a.m. on November 16, 2011, just as Wang woke up, a neighbor came to his house and told him that there was a paper bag stuck in front of his door, seemingly containing something. Wang tore off the bag and almost threw it to the ground when he opened it.

During my visit to Dianzi Wang Village, most villagers knew about Wang's family receiving bullets, and most people believed it was related to the election changeovers. "Wang was just elected as a branch committee member of the village party branch, naturally someone would be watching him closely."

After receiving the bullets, Wang's family felt pressured and dared not go out. He always disguised himself when going out alone, frequently looking around, closing the door tightly as soon as it got dark, and not daring to receive guests. "In these past half-month, it has been truly tormenting, the whole family hasn't lived peacefully." Due to this unexpected threat, Wang's child's marriage had to be postponed.

Gao陵 criminal police team participated in the investigation.

Wang said that although he suspected someone, because there was no evidence, he didn't dare confirm who did it. Two days before receiving the bullets, he saw a stranger who asked him to vote for someone else during the village election changeover, which he refused.

"One thinks of those bullets and the legs go weak, never encountered such strange things," said Wang from Dianzi Wang Village in Jingwei Town, Gao陵 County yesterday. More than ten days ago, his home suddenly had three bullets placed in a paper bag at his doorstep, making the whole family live in fear and not dare to leave the house.

"Wow, there were actually three bullets inside," Wang immediately didn't know what to do, surrounding crowds also came over upon hearing the news, everyone was scared by the scene in front of them. "Who could have done this, using bullets to intimidate people?" Someone recognized it as a Type 54 bullet, and officials quickly called the police. Officers from the Ma Jiawan Police Station of Gao陵 County Public Security Bureau arrived at the scene and took away the bullets.
