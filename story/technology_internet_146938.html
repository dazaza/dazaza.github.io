.Travel Lights Up Tourism Economy Under Financial Crisis

by now on 2009-05-07 16:15:17

The impact of the financial crisis on the global economy continues, and the development of all industries is still facing a severe test. The measure of boosting domestic demand through tourism has received great attention from relevant departments in our country. In such a situation, developing domestic tourism is particularly challenging. Before May Day, in order to attract more tourists, various regions across the country issued tourism cards to the public, making the tourism industry a boiling hotspot amid economic depression. Facing this hot market, .Travel, as the top-level domain name for the tourism industry, has taken on the task of accelerating the informatization development of tourism.

In the information age, expanding the tourism market requires more informatization services. Due to busy work schedules and short holidays, office workers who wish to travel need to search for a large amount of information online in advance as preparation before traveling. Therefore, the portal websites of related tourism enterprises become the bridge for developing the tourism economy. The tourism industry can integrate various activities such as travel, accommodation, tours, dining, shopping, and entertainment into one comprehensive industry involving many fields. It is reported that the .Travel domain name is specifically designed for the tourism industry. It can encompass all kinds of information related to tourism, providing people with a professional platform for searching for tourism information.

Our country's tourism market has enormous potential. Since .travel landed in China, it has undertaken the task of promoting the informatization of tourism services. The .Travel domain name records every tourism product and service information, providing travelers with a deeper and more convenient way to search for tourism information, allowing them to find reliable and legal tourism information in the shortest time possible. A relevant person in charge of Era Interconnect (www.now.cn) stated that .travel differs from any ordinary top-level domain currently used on the Internet because it ensures that all .travel websites are created by institutions related to the tourism industry. The information provided by these websites is accurate and true. On the basis of safeguarding the legitimate rights and interests of travelers when querying tourism products, it provides more convenient and secure tourism services for tourists. In today's bustling tourism market, not only do tourism-related enterprises need an online brand to expand their business, but tourists also need an authoritative portal website to search for real information. Why did the National Tourism Administration and various tourism branches rush to register after .travel landed in China? It was to create an authoritative online brand, establishing a milestone for tourism informatization, so that consumers can feel more assured when choosing tourism services through these iconic websites on the Internet.
