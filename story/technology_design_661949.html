The growth prospect of thiourea is promising.

by d8eks7dj on 2012-02-09 15:30:30

The prospects for thiourea growth are promising, with its application fields continuously expanding. With the development of the national economy, the usage of thiourea in pesticides, medicine, textiles, papermaking, electroplating, and metal smelting is gradually increasing.

Pesticides/Medicine Field: Thiourea can react with hydrazine hydrate to produce amino thiourea, which is a major intermediate for pesticides and medicine. It is mainly used to synthesize bromadiolone, which has better rodent-killing effects than tetramine, with minimal harm to humans and livestock. It is an acute rodenticide with great market potential, and the application of thiourea in this field will increase several times. Amino thiourea is also an intermediate for preparing sulfonamide anti-tuberculosis drugs. In recent years, some manufacturers have increased the production of amino thiourea, which will significantly promote the expansion of thiourea consumption in the pharmaceutical field.

Textile Industry: Thiourea can be used as a bleaching agent, dyeing agent, and antioxidant; thiourea formaldehyde can be used for printing and dyeing wool and silk; thiourea formaldehyde resin can greatly reduce the flammability of nylon, serving as a flame retardant.

Papermaking Industry: Thiourea is used to make diazo light-sensitive paper and can also be used as a reducing agent for blue and dark brown tones.

With the increasing efforts of the state to rectify polluting enterprises and the strengthening of people's environmental awareness, highly toxic cyanides in the electroplating and metal mineral flotation industries will be replaced by thiourea, thus significantly increasing the application of thiourea in this field.

Process Technology Improvements:

1. Traditional Production Process

The traditional calcium carbide-sulfide process involves using lime milk to absorb hydrogen sulfide to prepare calcium polysulfide, then adding calcium carbide (cyanamide) to synthesize thiourea solution. After filtration, the clear liquid undergoes vacuum evaporation concentration, and the concentrated solution is cooled and crystallized, centrifuged, washed with water, and dried to obtain the finished product thiourea.

2. New Production Process

After improvement, calcium carbide can directly absorb hydrogen sulfide to synthesize thiourea, eliminating the step of preparing lime milk into calcium polysulfide. The process is as follows: mix calcium carbide with water (recycled mother liquor or wash liquid) evenly in the synthesis reactor, while stirring and introducing hydrogen sulfide gas to generate thiourea solution. Filter the solution, freeze it to crystallize, separate the crystallization liquid by centrifugation, dry the crystals to obtain the product.

Advantages of the new process compared to the traditional process:

① Shortened the process flow and reduced product costs

Thiourea solution is generated directly from the absorption of hydrogen sulfide gas by calcium carbide solution. The precipitation of the product is changed from the original evaporation dilution and cooling crystallization to cold freezing crystallization. The new process eliminates three steps: preparation of lime milk, preparation of calcium polysulfide solution, and evaporation concentration of thiourea solution. Due to the shortened process flow, fewer operators, reduced engineering investment, and the change from hot crystallization to cold crystallization, the operating cost decreases, significantly reducing the production cost of thiourea.

② Cold freezing crystallization improves product quality

In the traditional process, part of the crystals precipitate during the evaporation of thiourea solution, resulting in smaller and uneven crystal sizes, even after final cooling crystallization, affecting product quality. In the new production process, the use of frozen crystallization produces larger and more uniform crystals, making the product look better and improving its quality.

③ Use of vibration fluidized bed drying technology further ensures product quality

Using vibration fluidized bed drying technology on the product, due to low drying temperature and easy control, the particle movement speed is small, not destroying the crystal structure, ensuring the product quality. In traditional production processes, rotary kilns and other equipment were used for drying, requiring screening to obtain products of various particle sizes after drying.

④ Reduced environmental pollution

In the traditional thiourea production process, the secondary steam generated during evaporation needs to be mixed and cooled with water, which is usually discharged directly. In the improved production process, since there is no evaporation process, pollution sources are reduced.

Calcium carbide produced by the electric stone method contains only about 20% nitrogen, and a certain amount of waste residue is generated during the thiourea synthesis process. Some research institutes are actively seeking new ways to synthesize calcium carbide or use other raw materials to synthesize thiourea to reduce environmental pollution, such as urea preparation of calcium carbide synthetic thiourea, urea dehydration preparation of cyanamide production thiourea, and direct synthesis of carbon, sulfur, ammonia, etc., new technologies under research and development.
