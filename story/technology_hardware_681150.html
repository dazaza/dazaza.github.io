The homeless man turns out to be a postgraduate of Fudan University. Wang Xiaoxu went upstairs and saw pots, pans, a quilt, a big bag of noodles, and an abandoned paint bucket. There were two holes in the paint bucket, one on the body and one at the bottom, which had been transformed into a stove.

The 110 emergency call was made by a boss who was looking for a factory to rent when he accidentally found someone lying in an abandoned factory.

Wang Xiaoxu began to slowly guide him, and finally, the homeless man was moved by his sincerity and opened up. To his surprise, the homeless man turned out to be a postgraduate in computer science from Fudan University who used to be a civil servant.

However, good fortune still did not come. A few days after arriving in Ningbo, his wallet and mobile phone were stolen by a pickpocket. In a strange city with no acquaintances and little money in his pocket, he had no choice but to stay in the abandoned factory in Qiuai.

In early January in Ningbo, even in the afternoon, the weather was still chilly. It had snowed a couple of days before, and there was still snow on the roadside in the suburbs.

Wang Xiaoxu told Xiao Wu that as a top student from Fudan University, he admired him greatly. As long as he could recover this time, there would be no obstacle he couldn't overcome. Afterwards, Wang Xiaoxu visited Xiao Wu every week with fruits and eight-treasure porridge. Under his persuasion, Xiao Wu's mood gradually became more cheerful.

A few days later, when he learned about the true identity of the homeless man, he could hardly believe his eyes. The homeless man said that he was a postgraduate in computer science from Fudan University and had once been a civil servant.

This is what exactly happened?

At the same time, he also suggested that Xiao Wu should communicate more with relatives and friends, return to the crowd, and not stay alone. Even if they don't necessarily give you money or housing, through communication, you will get spiritual comfort and release.

When Wang Xiaoxu arrived, there were some people gathered around. The homeless man lay weakly on the ground, his hair messy, his beard unshaven, almost frozen stiff.

On the first day, he fainted all day; on the second day, he wanted to ask for help, but there was no one in sight; on the afternoon of the third day, around 2 pm, when he heard the sound of a car driving over gravel far away, he shouted "Help!" with all his might.

The boss looking at the factory heard his shout.

The north wall of the third floor had collapsed, and there was a hole in the floorboard, which was likely where the homeless man fell from.

All this, what exactly happened?

After falling from the third floor to the first floor in the dark, he lay on the ground for three days.

Wang Xiaoxu sent the homeless man to the hospital and then went to the hospital to inquire about his condition after he got better.

After graduating, Xiao Wu initially told his family that he worked for a foreign company, engaged in software development. Afterward, he almost lost contact with his family, occasionally calling his brother.

Xiao Wu told Wang Xiaoxu that he felt dissatisfied with his work and his value was not being utilized. Soon, he resigned from the foreign company. He dreamed of finding a job where he would be appreciated and worthy of his knowledge, but how easy was that?

After graduation, he passed the county civil service exam. In the eyes of relatives and friends, he was a successful role model. But Xiao Wu had greater ambitions, not content to stay in a small county forever. After working for a while, he quit the "iron rice bowl" of being a civil servant and took the entrance exam for postgraduate studies at Fudan University.

Wang Xiaoxu finally understood why he didn't like being asked about his family affairs. He judged that such a strong-willed person, if sent back home like this, would definitely feel ashamed to face others.

"I talked to him about my experiences. At 19, I graduated from high school. My classmates all went to college, but I didn't. Later, I joined the army and thought I could still take the military academy exam. At 21, I succeeded..." Xiao Wu turned a paint bucket into a stove.

The "homeless man" lying in bed told Wang Xiaoxu about his tortuous experiences.

Subsequently, after investigating, Wang Xiaoxu confirmed Xiao Wu's identity and discovered that they were actually fellow countrymen from Anhui. When he decided to become Xiao Wu's older brother.

Yesterday, our reporter interviewed experts from the Blue Wave Psychological Counseling Center in Ningbo. Psychological counselor Zhou Mingsen said that Xiao Wu's experiences were unexpected. According to Maslow's theory, human needs generally have five levels: physiological needs, safety needs, belonging and love needs, respect needs, and self-actualization needs. These five levels are achieved step by step. Xiao Wu's problem might lie in the fact that he pursued higher-level needs while completely ignoring his basic needs.

Zhou Mingsen suggested that Xiao Wu divide his goals into several steps and achieve them bit by bit rather than trying to complete everything at once. For example, first earn a living, then choose a job based on personal interests.

Wang Xiaoxu arrived at the scene and found the factory very dilapidated. The homeless man lay weakly on the ground, bearded, and already somewhat frozen stiff. It was clear from his appearance that he had lain injured on the ground for several days.

The homeless man seemed seriously injured, unable to move. Afraid of causing secondary injuries, Wang Xiaoxu found some sticks to support Xiao Wu's back and hands while calling 120 to send him to the Sixth People's Hospital of Ningbo.

After the homeless man was hospitalized, Wang Xiaoxu, due to work needs, inquired about his basic situation as usual.

In the middle of the afternoon, Officer Wang Xiaoxu from Qiai Police Station in Yinzhou received a report that someone had found a homeless man in an abandoned factory, possibly with broken ribs, unable to move.

In 2007, he successfully enrolled in the computer science department of Fudan University. In January 2009, he graduated with a master's degree in computer science. Yesterday, reporters even found his 2008 master's thesis on software maintenance on "China National Knowledge Infrastructure".

Psychological expert: Xiao Wu, we expect you to bounce back from rock bottom.

He has an older brother and sister. In the family, he was the best student. For the family, being a civil servant was already an ideal job. Giving up the coveted "iron rice bowl" to pursue further study was not understood by the family. However, Xiao Wu had made up his mind.

To prepare for the graduate entrance exam, he resigned from his civil service position.

He bought a new pot, a bucket of Jinlong oil, a basin, and a large package of instant noodles. Additionally, he repurposed an abandoned paint can into a stove. Thus, in the abandoned factory, he set up a temporary home.

"Ah," with a cry, he missed a step in the dark and fell directly from the third floor to the first floor.

The abandoned factory was far from the road, and it being the end of the year, Xiao Wu's cries were lost in the vast night without anyone hearing them.

These days, the weather in Ningbo was particularly cold, with light snowfall. He fell on the ground, leaning against the wall, suffering multiple fractures and unable to move his lumbar vertebrae.

But Wang Xiaoxu vaguely felt that this homeless man might have hidden difficulties. Because judging from his speech, he seemed cultured; moreover, although he didn't say much, occasional revelations showed he was a very competitive person.

Wang Xiaoxu noticed that he was quite shy. No matter how he questioned him, he refused to disclose his origins or how to contact his family. His emotions were low, he was uncooperative with treatment, and even had suicidal thoughts.

Family members have already arrived in Ningbo to care for him.

Wang Xiaoxu found that he was relatively reserved, unwilling to talk about where he was from or how to contact his family. His mood was low, he wasn't cooperating well with treatment, and even had thoughts of suicide.

The doctor said that he had multiple fractures. If he had been brought in any later, amputation or even paralysis might have occurred.
