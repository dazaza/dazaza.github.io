The dog that bit four students in front of the gate of Dagou Primary School was beaten to death

by zxyhszzfp on 2011-07-13 13:14:49

The iron chain that the big dog broke free from.

At around 12 noon yesterday, a white large dog escaped its locked chain and ran out from a villager's house. At the gate of Mizi Store Primary School in Tongzhou District, it bit three primary school students and one middle school student consecutively. Upon hearing the news, the school security guards beat the dog to death with sticks. It was reported that among the four injured children, a six-year-old boy had severe facial injuries, while the other three children were not seriously injured. Currently, the police have intervened in the investigation.

>> Incident

The Big Dog Suddenly Attacked the Children

It was understood that the incident occurred during the lunch break at Mizi Store Primary School. The school stipulated that the school gates would open at 1:30 PM. At the time, many students waiting for class had already gathered outside the west gate of the school.

A villager told reporters that at around 12 noon, a white large dog suddenly appeared outside the south wall of the school. A little boy carrying a schoolbag and wearing a school uniform was knocked down by the large dog. At the time, the child used both hands to protect his face and struggled fiercely, but because he was too small, his face was still attacked by the large dog. This villager wanted to intervene but was too far away.

The villager said that the little boy was attacked by the large dog for nearly a minute. The large dog ran northward from the west entrance of the school's south wall towards the west gate where many students were gathered. Eyewitnesses claimed that the large dog then proceeded to bite two more primary school students. About ten meters away from the west school gate, the large dog attacked a boy riding a bike. The boy’s right leg was bitten, blood gushed out, and he fell to the ground immediately. Struggling, he ran towards the west gate of the primary school. Chaos ensued at the school gate.

Security Guards Beat the Dog to Death

According to the on-duty gatekeeper of Mizi Store Primary School, the first three children attacked were all students of their school, while the teenage boy over ten years old was a student from a nearby middle school. Initially, the attacked child was covered in blood with tears streaming from his eyes. That child called for help through the iron fence saying: "Grandpa, please save me! I've been bitten by a dog." The gatekeeper immediately opened the door to rescue him and informed the teachers and security guards of the school, as well as called the police.

Two security guards of the school then rushed out of the school gate wielding sticks. Outside the west school gate was a village road wide enough for two cars to pass side by side. Eyewitnesses stated that at the time, the teenage boy who was attacked was running towards the school gate, with the large dog chasing closely behind. The two security guards swung their sticks forward, hitting the large dog's head forcefully. The large dog tried to escape but was stopped by the security guards. Within a few minutes, the large dog was beaten to death.

According to the gatekeeper, there was a near-meter-long iron chain tied around the dog's neck, with the other end of the chain attached to an iron stake originally fixed to the ground.

>> Injuries

Six-Year-Old Child Risks Disfigurement

It was understood that the boy initially attacked was surnamed Zhang. According to the boy's father, Mr. Zhang, their home is only a five-minute walk from the school, and it sufficed for the child to leave home ten minutes before class. On the day of the incident, the child left before 1 PM, saying he wanted to meet up with classmates to go to school together. About ten minutes later, the homeroom teacher called, stating that the child had been bitten by a dog. Mr. Zhang said, "I initially thought it was just a bite on the leg, not a big deal, a few shots would suffice," but when he and his wife arrived at the school, they were shocked by what they saw, "our son's face was covered in blood, and we couldn't even see his eyes."

Mr. Zhang said that a teacher drove them first to the nearest town hospital, but since treatment was unavailable, they went to Tongzhou Luhe Hospital, where they were also told treatment was unavailable. Until 3 PM, the child was sent to the People's Hospital. The doctor first administered rabies vaccine to the child and performed surgery that evening.

According to the doctor at the People's Hospital, from the surface appearance, the child's left face was bitten, with severe swelling in the injured area, the skin on the bridge of the nose was flipped outward, and a ligament in the nose was broken. If this causes facial deformity, plastic surgery can be considered. From the surface appearance, the left eye and nose seem unharmed, but the specific injury condition still needs to wait for the surgical results.

It was understood that among the other three students attacked, two were bitten on the legs, while another one was injured on the chest, but none of the injuries were serious.

>> Investigation

Family Dog Escapes Causing Trouble

After the incident, officers from the Mizi Store Police Station arrived at the scene to conduct an investigation, searching within the village for the owner of the dog.

After the large dog was beaten to death by the security guard, the school gatekeeper buried the dog's body in a soil pit near the school gate. When the reporter arrived at the scene, the gatekeeper dug up the dog's body with a shovel. The reporter saw that the white large dog was nearly one meter long, a common mongrel dog, and its head had been smashed. Meanwhile, the elderly man also showed the collar and iron chain tied around the dog's neck to the reporter.

It was understood that the dog that caused harm was not a stray dog but was raised by a villager in the village, and prior to the incident, it had always been tied up in the yard. According to insiders, on the day of the incident, only young children were at the dog owner's house. At the time, the temperament of the large dog suddenly changed, becoming restless, pulling up the iron stake nailed to the ground by the root, breaking free and running out of the yard. Currently, the police are still investigating this case.

>> Close-up

Mother Prays on Her Knees for Son's Safety

At 8 PM last night, the reporter arrived at the People's Hospital. The child's father, Mr. Zhang, began tearing up when talking about his son.

The child's mother knelt outside the operating room, her expression solemn, looking down silently, praying silently for her son. Until 10:30 PM, the child's mother stood up. It seemed that the kneeling prayer had worked; soon after the mother stood up, the child was wheeled out of the operating room. The parents eagerly wanted to see their son, quickly standing up, their eyes tightly focused, faces filled with worry.

The child's face was wrapped in bandages, exposing only his right eye and mouth. His right eye was tightly closed, still unconscious, and after being taken out of the operating room, he was directly sent to the ward.
