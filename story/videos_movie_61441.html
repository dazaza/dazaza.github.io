Plot Introduction:

The story tells of the trials and tribulations that test one's spirit and soul. Only true feelings can move heaven and earth. Behind such sincerity lies a selfless devotion. The protagonist’s dream in her heart, after much hardship and perseverance, finally comes true. It narrates the touching story of a young girl who was abducted and sold, but through her genuine emotions and beliefs, she changes her fate and fights for her life. Despite the twists and turns in life, the sincere human connections and strong beliefs are like grass growing under rocks, never ceasing to thrive...

Shi Qiuguo - A sunny and radiant country girl.

In order to pick strawberries for her sick mother, she bid farewell to her playful companions and ventured deep into the mountains...

Gao Yanling - A successful young urban man.

He was speeding along winding mountain roads, completely unaware of the three mysterious cars trailing behind him. When he reached an isolated area, Gao Yanling was suddenly ambushed by the tailing cars and hastily drove deeper into the mountains...

Strawberries scattered all over the ground as Shi Qiuguo looked in astonishment at the disoriented Gao Yanling before her.

Shi Qiuguo gave directions to the lost Gao Yanling, who left without even thanking her...

A brief and hurried encounter marked their first unexpected meeting.

When Gao Yanling fell off the cliff, he woke up to an urgent call and saw Shi Qiuguo again, angelic-like. However, when Shi Qiuguo found people to rescue him, Gao Yanling had already disappeared...

Their second accidental encounter seemed to foreshadow the connection between two distant destinies from city and countryside...

Shi Qiuguo originally planned to marry Li Weiwei and then go to the city to work. She could never have imagined that Weiwei, her childhood sweetheart, would elope with Zhou Xiaojun, the fiancée of the notorious local thug known as "One-Eyed Dragon." Not only did One-Eyed Dragon relentlessly demand that Shi Qiuguo be given to him as a replacement bride, but Shi’s father also agreed to his demands just to protect their cattle. Her weak mother, neither wanting her daughter to jump into the fire nor daring to defy her husband's will, could only cry helplessly. But the stubborn Shi Qiuguo firmly refused... Early the next morning, she secretly followed the human trafficker Yunfei Che, who pretended to recruit workers, and left her hometown...

During the journey, Shi Qiuguo's ears were filled with the shocking secret her mother revealed to her on their last meeting. Originally, she had an older sister named Shi Qiuhua who went missing twenty years ago in Haibin City. Her mother placed her hopes of finding her sister on her, but the excited Shi Qiuguo had no idea that a nightmare of life was slowly unfolding...

Cousin Gao Yanling's disappearance caused Jiang Yufei to lose her temper with her subordinate Lin Feng. It turned out that in order to save her company from difficulties, she tacitly approved Lin Feng's plan to steal the supplier list carried by Gao Yanling halfway. Lin Feng also swore that he would not harm Gao Yanling. Privately, Lin Feng took the opportunity to incite others to harm Gao Yanling as revenge for being expelled from the Aura Company...

After a long journey, Shi Qiuguo and others were brought to an even more remote mountainous area. Only then did she realize that she had been sold like livestock by the human trafficker Yunfei Che... She regretted her impulsiveness and gullibility, but it was too late. Late at night, when Yu Luming lunged at her, Shi Qiuguo grabbed a piece of glass and held it against her throat... After several desperate struggles against ignorance and savagery, Shi Qiuguo still couldn't escape her misfortune... Watching an unopened flower wither instantly, yet her stubbornness refused to yield...

After multiple rescues, Gao Yanling finally opened his eyes, but brain damage led to memory loss and paralysis of the lower limbs. He could only lie motionless on the hospital bed. Jiang Yufei, claiming it was for Gao Yanling's recovery, isolated him in the most secluded ward of the hospital. Meanwhile, she seized control of the Aura Company.

Awaken Le, the reluctant human trafficker, could never shake off the guilt and torment plaguing his conscience. After seeing Shi Qiuguo's suffering, he finally decided to help her escape from the abyss. Shi Qiuguo reported the case to the police station and rescued her fellow sufferers Lu Yazhi and Liu Fengfeng...

To forget those painful experiences and to find her sister, Shi Qiuguo came to Haibin City. She worked as a nurse in the hospital and unexpectedly found herself caring for the amnesiac and paralyzed Gao Yanling. Out of kindness, Shi Qiuguo secretly helped Gao Yanling recover, but she was discovered by Jiang Yufei and expelled.

An unexpected shock made Gao Yanling stand up again. Determined to uncover the person who harmed him, he pretended to be paralyzed while secretly investigating everyone around him and planning retaliation...

In the vast sea of people, Shi Qiuguo and Gao Yanling met again unexpectedly, making one believe this was destined. Shi Qiuguo was happy about Gao Yanling's recovery, but Gao Yanling intended to involve her in his plans...

Carefully arranged by Gao Yanling, Shi Qiuguo reappeared in Aura Company with a new identity. Jiang Yufei was shocked to see the transformed Shi Qiuguo and couldn't equate her with the rural girl she once humiliated. Enraged, she went to the hospital to confront Gao Yanling. His attitude made Jiang Yufei feel threatened, so she began to exclude and bully Shi Qiuguo. However, using her wisdom and kindness, Shi Qiuguo gained the recognition of everyone and the favor of Gao Yanling. A tortuous triangular battle unfolded. The strong may not always be strong, and the weak may not always be weak. At this moment, Shi Qiuguo and Awaken Le met again unexpectedly...

Unpleasant memories resurfaced with every familiar face. Liu Fengfeng, unable to resist temptation, revealed Shi Qiuguo's origins. Jiang Yufei successfully expelled Shi Qiuguo from the company. Shi Qiuguo's sudden departure made Gao Yanling realize his feelings for her. Simultaneously, his pretense of illness was exposed before Jiang Yufei, earning him a harsh slap...

The proud and self-assured Jiang Yufei never expected Gao Yanling to betray her. She tried desperately to win him back, but Gao Yanling was determined to leave. At this time, she discovered a more shocking secret - Shi Qiuguo was actually the long-lost younger sister she had been searching for... To recognize or not? Faced with social status and wealth, she hesitated. Moreover, to cover up her past scheming against Gao Yanling and achieve her goals further, she didn't hesitate to harm Shi Qiuguo again, but nightmares also haunted her closely...

Faced with the cruel reality, Shi Qiuguo told Gao Yanling her experiences, sorrowfully shouting, "My body may have been bought, but my heart is pure!" At this moment, Gao Yanling's understanding and care awakened Shi Qiuguo's suppressed emotions. The two truly loving souls embraced tightly...

Under Jiang Yufei's manipulation, Gao's mother returned home joyfully for her birthday celebration, only to find her banquet turned into a farce, leaving her extremely angry. She ordered Gao Yanling to immediately leave the unclear relationship with Shi Qiuguo. At the banquet, the "pregnancy certificate" presented by Mr. Yu destroyed Gao Yanling's trust in Shi Qiuguo, testing their relationship again... Watching Gao Yanling's retreating figure, Shi Qiuguo shed tears of sadness. At this moment, Awaken Le brought the news of her mother's death, delivering a double blow to Shi Qiuguo, leaving her devastated...

Jiang Yufei, having achieved her wish, contentedly planned her future with Gao Yanling. In another corner of the city, wiping away her tears, Shi Qiuguo stood up once again. With Cheng Yumin's help, the "Flower Fruit Cashmere Store" opened its doors. Seeing the blessings from her partners, Shi Qiuguo smiled with relief.

But Lin Feng, now both penniless and powerless, refused to let things go easily. He colluded with the human trafficker Yunfei Che to plot another evil hand... Gao Yanling was suddenly kidnapped, and Shi Qiuguo, responsible for delivering the ransom, was deceived out of 2 million yuan under pressure. Amidst criticism and blame... Awaken Le brought news, and Shi Qiuguo went alone to rescue Gao Yanling. Unexpectedly, they were both locked in the deep mountains by One-Eyed Dragon and others. In times of difficulty, true feelings emerge. Gao Yanling and Shi Qiuguo, the lovers who endured numerous hardships, once again embraced each other in a cold straw house, encouraging and warming each other. But facing ignorance, their safety still remained worrying...

"Evil deeds will eventually be punished!" The villains received their punishment! After the storm, Jiang Yufei, deeply educated, was taken by Shi Qiuguo to her mother's grave. Shi Qiuguo told Jiang Yufei, "This less beautiful place is our hometown. We must use our hands to make it more beautiful!" The two sisters embraced before their mother...
