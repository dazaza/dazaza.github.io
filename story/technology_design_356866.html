The Feline Nature of Women

by babylogo9 on 2010-05-29 12:30:22

A friend's girlfriend was studying alone. It was too cold, so she sent a text message to her boyfriend asking him to bring her some clothes. However, he refused as he was playing games. This made her upset for two days until she calmed down. Although it was a small matter and wouldn't affect their future relationship, she said that she would remember this. In the future, when studying alone, she will definitely bring clothes. If she forgets one day, even if she freezes, she won't ask him to deliver clothes anymore. I can really understand this feeling.

A long time ago, a girl on a certain night felt particularly low and wanted someone's comfort badly. Then in the middle of the night, she called him and said she wanted to hear his voice. The person on the other end of the phone woke up from his dream and responded to her impatiently. Since then, whenever she felt sad and helpless, she never called him again. I found that women have a feline nature. When kittens are coquettish or make mistakes, they need others' comfort and teaching. If at this moment the owner strikes them, they will remember it well and not repeat it. Women have the self-esteem like cats, especially women who are in love... In others' eyes, it may be unimportant, but actually, they need care because love has already made their hearts soft. This bit of self-esteem is actually your concern for her.

I've read a very classic sentence: Sometimes women need a man just like escapees need a parachute. If at this moment he isn't there, then he doesn't need to be there afterward either. Yes, it's true. Ah, if one day the cat looks at you with a determined gaze and says I can, it means the cat has already prepared to leave! Women should be independent, but when they become independent to the point where they no longer act capriciously and unreasonably towards you, and no longer cling to you without reason, do you think how different are you from a passerby to her? Not every man is lucky enough to see the feline nature of women. Because they like you and care about you, they make the unique purring sound towards you. Others can only hear the cat meow, while this purring is only for you. How many people can understand this??
