【Intellectual Reasoning】─━ Who is the murderer? ━─

by guxu on 2010-09-21 10:13:09

On a certain day of a certain month in a certain year, seven people - Qin Yi Xiao, Li Gu, He Yan Qing, Hei Shui Lao Gui, Yi Si Jie Qian Chou, Wei Wei, and Mo Yan Bai - went to climb Mount Hua together.

Because it was very cold, there were few tourists. The seven climbed while enjoying the scenery along the way. By 6 PM, they finally reached the summit. They rested in the mountain dormitories and agreed to meet at 4 AM the next morning to watch the sea of clouds and sunrise. Then, they all returned to their rooms to sleep.

That night, a tragedy occurred.

At 7:30 AM, everyone was sitting around the table eating breakfast when they suddenly realized that Yi Si Jie Qian Chou hadn't gotten up yet. Qin Yi Xiao went to his room to call him and was shocked to find that Yi Si Jie Qian Chou had already died in bed! A knife had been thrust into his throat, likely causing him to die instantly. The scattered bloodstains on the sheets had already coagulated.

The sudden event terrified everyone. They decided to immediately descend the mountain to report the incident to the police. But when they opened the door, cold wind and snowflakes swept in. All they could see was a white, snowy landscape. The snow was too heavy, and the narrow mountain path was already blocked, making it impossible to walk. The six had no choice but to return to the house, enduring the fear in their hearts.

But the events did not end there.

On the evening of the second day, Li Gu noticed that He Yan Qing hadn't appeared since the morning. Her room was also empty. Thus, Li Gu and Hei Shui Lao Gui went out together to search for her. Under the cover of darkness, they shone flashlights as they walked and called out He Yan Qing's name. Not far from where they started, they found He Yan Qing. At that moment, both of them couldn't stop their teeth from chattering. He Yan Qing lay collapsed in the snow, her face dark blue, her eyes still filled with the terror of death, her body stiff for a long time. It seemed she had been strangled to death while taking a stroll. The two carried her back to the house and placed her corpse together with Yi Si Jie Qian Chou's in an empty room.

No one could eat dinner that night. No one could hide the fear in their hearts. It was a sleepless night, and ominous shadows slowly spread.

On the third morning, the five remaining individuals forced themselves to eat breakfast in the hall. Everyone looked haggard, distracted, and especially Wei Wei's condition was severe. Not only did he accidentally drop his breakfast on the ground, but he also inexplicably burst into laughter for a while. Then, he hastily left the table and locked himself in his room. Immediately, someone whispered softly, "Could it be... is the killer him?"

Everyone fell silent, and the hall became deathly quiet. The five no longer trusted each other.

The snow continued to fall, getting heavier and heavier. The wind whistled past the glass windows, producing a sharp, grating sound.

At 5 PM, Hei Shui Lao Gui noticed blood water seeping out from under the door of Wei Wei's room. He hurriedly tried to break down the door but discovered that it wasn't even locked. As expected, Wei Wei was dead too, with a knife pierced into his chest, blood spilling onto the floor. Perhaps he hadn't been hit fatally, so he struggled for quite some time before dying. There were bloodstains everywhere in the room, and the wooden floor was scratched by his nails. Hearing Hei Shui Lao Gui's startled cry, the remaining three rushed in and froze on the spot. The room was filled with heavy, rapid breathing. Someone cried out tearfully, "Is he... does he want to kill all of us?" No one answered; only the sound of snowflakes falling outside the window could be heard.

Time rolled forward to the fourth morning. While eating breakfast, Qin Yi Xiao noticed Li Gu occasionally showing a mysterious smile. When they were alone, Qin Yi Xiao cautiously asked him, "What's wrong? Did you discover something?"

Li Gu's eyes flickered mysteriously as he quietly replied, "I found Wei Wei's diary, unexpectedly discovering clues about the murderer."

"Really? Do you know who it is?" Qin Yi Xiao urgently questioned.

"Not yet, but I will definitely expose him by this afternoon!" Li Gu confidently said.

Time quickly moved to the afternoon. Qin Yi Xiao silently sat in his own room. Li Gu's room was right next to his.

At 4:30 PM, a horrifying scream suddenly came from Li Gu's room, "It was you—!"

Qin Yi Xiao rushed out of his room in three strides. Although the two rooms were adjacent, the doors were quite far apart. It took Qin Yi Xiao about thirty seconds to rush to Li Gu's door. The door was slightly ajar, and when Qin Yi Xiao pushed it open, he immediately drew in a sharp breath! Li Gu sat on a chair, tightly gripping a green notebook in his left hand. His eyes were wide open, as if he had seen something unbelievable. The wound was on his left chest, about an inch deep, piercing through his lung.
