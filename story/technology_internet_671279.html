by shuiya111100 on 2012-02-21 17:46:22

Product Introduction: 40 Linglong Baiyu Fen Wine 225ml Baiyu Fen wine was first created in 1904. It uses Fen wine as the base, combined with the plant-based Chinese medicine purple cinnamon for soaking and distillation to extract its fragrance, resulting in a unique natural aroma. During the Republic of China era, there was a saying that went "Fen wine is pure, bamboo leaf green, deep affection rose sweet Baiyu", where "sweet Baiyu" was used to praise its smooth and sweet taste. Time flows, but quality remains golden. Today, Shanxi Xinghuacun Fen Wine Factory Co., Ltd. has infused Baiyu Fen wine with a completely new brand connotation and precise positioning. This time, Baiyu Fen wine has launched two series of products, 20 Baiyu Fen wine and 10 Baiyu Fen wine, to meet different consumer demands. These are respectively packaged in Chinese celadon bottles and crystal frosted glass bottles, targeting high-end and mid-range markets respectively. As the national marketing general agent, Shanxi Xinghuacun Zhuyeqing Wine Marketing Co., Ltd. will closely follow the lifestyle trajectory and consumption patterns of the post-80s generation for marketing promotion, perfectly interpreting the concept of "wine like jade, feelings like jade," tailored specifically for the post-80s generation, aiming to make Baiyu Fen wine the first in China's soft and fragrant white wine category.

The post-80s generation, as a new force in today’s society, enjoys relatively stable income and advanced consumption concepts. They prefer smooth and sweet tasting wines and seek pure friendships amidst complex social interactions, hoping for sincere emotional communication. The brand proposition of Baiyu Fen wine, "wine like jade, feelings like jade," aligns perfectly with these target consumers' emotional needs. The integration of this wine style with emotional desires will undoubtedly make it the preferred choice for emotional connection among new mainstream consumer groups in the new era.

"Custom-made for the post-80s generation" locks in the target consumer group, entering the market boldly with the claim of being "China's first soft and fragrant white wine." By embedding the emotional appeal of "wine like jade, feelings like jade" into consumers' self-interpretation of Baiyu Fen wine, this series of perfect content exploration and marketing presentation opens up broader prospects for the market.
