The Anser is a special club for the serious golfer looking to enjoy a unique set of irons that you may not see in other golf clubs. The Anser has a wider sole and more forgiveness, which will make some look for something more compact. In summary, the Ping Anser Forged Irons share the same name as one of the early models Karsten Solheim produced before he decided to turn to creating new irons. However, the difference lies in the fact that the newest version of irons has been innovatively evolved from the old ones, pushing the forging process to create durable performance benefits not previously associated with a forged club. The Ping Anser Forged Irons combine an 8620 steel body and a tungsten sole to provide unique forged-iron performance. They also feature a forged, multi-metal design. The face and grooves are designed to add spin, and its hollow sole cavity design aims to improve feel and adjust the center of gravity for a higher launch.
