Mother's Day Messages: Gratitude for Mothers, Missing Mothers, Praising Mothers

Missing Mother

1. There is always a kind of care that makes you feel like basking in the spring sunshine, always a gaze that makes you understand strength, always a concern that makes you unforgettable, and always a sentiment that helps you grow up - and that is your mother. Wishing all mothers a happy Mother's Day!

2. Without a gentle breeze outside the window, I am melancholy; without a bright moon outside the window, I am disappointed. The breeze does not carry true feelings, and the moon cannot convey my longing. Fortunately, I have a message. Message content: Maternal love is the most genuine under heaven, everyone knows it, so why should it be elaborated. Recipient: Mother.

3. Being far away in a foreign land, but having your heart close by, your mother's smile is a beautiful kindness. Thinking of the past, wishing forever, the sincere prayer for your mother's health, on Mother's Day, wishing all mothers happiness, staying young forever.

4. As a child, nestled in your embrace, showing a smile of dependence, growing up and leaving your sight, flying with sincere attachment, my love for you will never change, blessing your great festival with happiness and eternal health.

5. Do not forget those first hesitant steps when she gave you strong support, do not forget her constant worry during your academic journey, do not forget her deep concern while you wander afar. Special blessings are given to the special one - our mom.

6. Can't forget the schoolbag you sewed late at night, can't forget your farewell advice, can't forget the comfort over the phone, can't forget the heavy New Year’s package, can't forget how tired you were coming to see me. Even if the mother is thousands of miles away, the son still worries. You must take care of yourself!

7. A poem "The Parting Son" reveals the greatness and selflessness of maternal love through thousands of years. Our lives are all given by our mothers. People say maternal love is an instinct, at any time caring for us as ever.

8. Whether it is the diligent students studying away from home, or the young people working away, if there is no time to visit mom, or too busy to send her a gift, then never forget to send her a blessing message or make a call.

9. Thinking of you brings back a flood of memories. Back then, I was small, and you watched me grow up. Now, I am grown, and you are old, yet you still watch over me, hoping I come home! Thank you for your steadfast watch, I won't let you down. Wishing you longevity!

10. Always unable to forget the water of the hometown, also unable to forget the clouds of the native place, often hearing mom's words in my ears, often remembering mom's kisses in my heart, dreaming of embracing mother's white hair, just wanting to say: Mom, happy holidays!
