20 Tragic Situations Boys Experience After Getting a Girlfriend!!

by lisimengok on 2008-05-06 22:55:09

The following is a monologue of a boy:

1. I got scholarships before I knew you, and after knowing you, I almost had to take make-up exams.

2. I endured the shame of being laughed at by others to wash your clothes.

3. One apple, I give it all to you. Two apples, I give the bigger one to you.

4. Eating the same fish, I let you eat the fish meat and I eat the fish head.

5. You are so ugly, but I still say you are pretty. I am so handsome, but you say I am ugly.

6. When we go out to play, I spend money. When I come back, I can only eat radishes (you save money and of course you can eat shark fins yourself).

7. I didn't have a girlfriend before I knew you. Before you knew me, you had two boyfriends.

8. When you are angry, I let you use me as a punching bag. When I am angry, I use myself as a punching bag.

9. You can kiss me whenever you want. When I want to kiss you, I have to get your permission.

10. You often hit me, but I have never hit you.

11. Once you asked me what would happen if you fell in love with another man, I said I would kill that man. I asked you what would happen if I fell in love with another woman, you said you would castrate me.

12. I bought a 200-yuan clothes for you and lied to you that it was only 50 yuan. You bought a 30-yuan watch for me and lied to me that it was 300 yuan.

13. I broke your headphones, and I bought new ones to compensate you. You lost my bicycle, and didn't even say sorry.

14. Every time you get sick, I lose four pounds. Every time I get sick, you gain four pounds (you came to my dormitory and ate all my snacks).

15. I didn't complain about your shortness, but you complained about my tallness.

16. When you come to my house, I sleep on the sofa. When I go to your house, you make me sleep on the ground.

17. That time when we went to the outdoor concert, you rode on my head and watched happily, while I was pressed underneath and cried in the crowd.

18. My family's dog gave birth, and I sent you the cutest puppy. But you sent me your dying goldfish which made me bury them just two days later.

19. I washed your socks N times and never lost any of them. You washed my socks twice when I was sick, and lost one from each pair.

20. When I accompanied you to buy things, I offered to carry them for you, but you never asked if you could help me carry anything.
