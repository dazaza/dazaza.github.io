Life must go on...

by mobuyybu on 2012-02-10 09:05:56

Life must go on... Is there a third part? It's both surprising and unsettling. Although the ending of the second part wasn't perfect, it was still relatively ideal. When I heard about the third part, I immediately borrowed it.

Reluctant to read it, just in case a certain kind of anticipation would disappear. Unexpectedly, I finished it on Women's Day. What else can I say? Many characters have become more ordinary, increasingly complex people have lost their pure beauty. But reality is exactly like this. The torrent of life can wash away one's original appearance. And unexpected accidents make things worse...

Nothing can be more precious than warm human relationships - the true beauty of life you feel is no more than this. (Sun Shaoping)

Only through labor can a person become strong in life. No matter who, ultimately should respect those workers who can create life with their hands. (Sun Shaoping)

Happiness is the same everywhere. In this desolate mountainous mining area, in these earth caves and sheds, people can still live happily and beautifully! (Wang Shicai)

Whether a person's spirit is full or whether his life has meaning mainly depends on his attitude towards labor. (Sun Shaoping)

Do not think that a person is always right because he is correct at one time, nor should you determine that he will never be able to join the ranks of the excellent because he has made mistakes. (Zhou Wenglong)

He will always be such a person: he relentlessly pursues life but does not dare to expect too much reward or favor from life, facing reality rationally and clearly. This may be the common mentality of all intellectuals who come from the countryside. (Sun Shaoping)

In life, real heroes are often silent, while those who constantly make noise are always those who consider themselves noble. (Sun Shaoping)

A person in a state of silent struggle, his spirit will be sublimated from trivial life. (Sun Shaoping)

An ordinary person will always feel overwhelmed by the waves of life... (Sun Shaoping)

What truly lasts forever is the nameless monument of ordinary people - the endless life of humanity itself. Yes, the tree of life remains evergreen. (Wang Shicai)

Happiness, or the value of existence, does not lie in what kind of work we do. (Sun Shaoping)

Life causes quarrels among brothers and sisters-in-law, yet they separately form friendly alliances with outsiders. (Jin family)

Only hard physical labor can turn spiritual pain into some kind of numbness, so as to limit thinking to mechanical activities. (Sun Shao'an)

Love should be truly established on the solid foundation of real life; otherwise, it is a flower that blooms on the living tree of life but does not bear fruit. (Run Ye)

This embrace is as broad and deep as the earth, comforting the wounded hearts of men, giving them warmth, happiness, and the strength to re-enter the storm! (Sun Shao'an)

Man is often fragile; but man is also the most tenacious! (Sun Shao'an)

Beautiful flowers are beautiful even when they wither. (Sun Shaoping)

Life is good after all, and life within it is so short; since we are alive, we should live well. Missing deceased loved ones should make us cherish every moment of our own lives. Spiritual depression is equivalent to suicide. Live normally as in the past! (Sun Shaoping)

No matter what setbacks and blows, grit your teeth and bear them, because you completely have the opportunity to rebuild your life; as long as you don't lose heart, every setback is just an ordinary stumbling block on the way to a new realm, and will never be fatal. Man, endurance, resilience, benevolence... (Wu Huiliang)

Poverty, this family weathered storms together; once the situation improved slightly, conflicts arose, leading to the division of the household. And after being impacted by one life storm after another, this family became so close again. (Sun family)

At fifty, know the will of heaven: treat life with a more tolerant, kind, open-minded, and elegant attitude. (Zhang Youzhi)

In fact, everyone's emotions possess poetic sentiment. (Sun Shaoping)

And the most incredible thing is man, human feelings. (Jin Bo)

Indeed, for the sake of love and happiness, any price paid is worth it! (Sun Shaoping)

Indeed, sometimes, an extremely accidental factor may change a person's life. (Wang Manshen)
