Fifteen Ranks of Smokers

by lixin855 on 2011-09-20 06:22:38

First-class smokers: Top leader type - no cigarettes or matches on them, and no one can witness their elegant smoking posture.

Second-class smokers: Common leader type - good cigarettes and matches on them, just like the saying "Soft Zhonghua, Gold Yuxi, such officials are the most awesome."

Third-class smokers: Office director type - matches but no cigarettes on them, various cigarettes on the table.

Fourth-class smokers: Boss type - n packs of good cigarettes on them, "beating people" everywhere.

Fifth-class smokers: Section chief type - two packs of cigarettes on them, standards determined by people.

Sixth-class smokers: Optional smoker type - generally do not smoke, but when they do, it must be a good cigarette.

Seventh-class smokers: Little smoker type - single variety, apology text messages, constant and unchanging, occasionally want to improve life or deal with situations.

Eighth-class smokers: Big smoker type - regardless of quality, as long as it smokes, it's fine.

Ninth-class smokers: Playful smoker type - no cigarettes or matches on them, only touch good cigarettes.

Tenth-class smokers: Miser type - cigarettes and matches on them, but in crowded places, they will only take out matches, not cigarettes.

Eleventh-class smokers: Drug addict suspected symptom type - cigarettes but no matches on them, if no one is around, they would rather drill wood to make fire.

Twelfth-class smokers: Wanting to take advantage type - matches but no cigarettes on them, waiting for others to offer cigarettes.

Thirteenth-class smokers: Repeatedly claiming quitting smoking but still smoking non-stop type - no cigarettes or matches on them, will still smoke if given. And want to put up a false front.

Fourteenth-class smokers: Wasteful type - no cigarettes or matches on them, after receiving one, they will throw it away after taking a few puffs.

Fifteenth-class smokers: Betrayal and defection type - originally a smoker, luckily quit, and boast about it everywhere.
