360 Video Mobile App Launches, Focusing on User Interest Subscription Model

by anonymous on 2013-09-11 14:05:12

September 10th news, the film and TV drama distribution platform under Qihoo 360, 360 Film and Television, held a joint launch ceremony today with the main creative team of "Witness for the Prosecution" led by Aaron Kwok and Sun Honglei. They announced that the mobile version of 360 Film and Television will officially go online on September 16th and will have in-depth cooperation with the movie "Witness for the Prosecution". Yu Guangdong, Vice President of 360, said that 360 Film and Television will ride on the film and television industry platform to have deeper and closer cooperation with the entertainment industry.

360 Film and Television stated that for many video apps, complex gestures such as swiping left and right have different operation effects in different scenarios, especially when switching between different video players, which can easily cause operational confusion. This time, the 360 Film and Television app only adopts two gestures: swipe up and pull down, aiming to reduce this confusion.

Another feature of the 360 Film and Television app released this time is the Magic Pocket, mainly targeting the situation where domestic mobile internet fees are still very expensive and Wifi environment coverage is insufficient. 360 Film and Television claims that when the user's phone connects to Wifi, the 360 Film and Television app will automatically help the user download some videos. When the user leaves the Wifi environment, such as during subway rides and outdoor leisure time, they can watch these downloaded videos offline.

In addition, the 360 Film and Television app uses a user interest subscription model. The homepage of most current video apps is essentially a miniature version of PC-end video websites on small mobile screens, including focus images, headlines, and other editor-recommended content. However, the 360 Film and Television client adopts a user subscription mechanism. Upon first entry, users need to subscribe to channels of interest. After entering the homepage, users can customize the position or delete/add the subscribed channels.

It is reported that with the rapid development of domestic video websites, industry competition has extended from the Web end to mobile clients. Major video websites are also striving to layout in mobile internet and compete for mobile entrances. 360 Film and Television stated that the newly launched 360 Film and Television app hopes to become another valuable and helpful entrance product for major video websites, besides the 360 Mobile Assistant.

Currently, besides 360, Baidu is also actively competing for this market. Baidu has already launched Baidu Video and Baidu Player on the mobile end. Since June this year, the Baidu Player team has started to be integrated into the Baidu Video team as a whole, and is now led by Hu Hao, former VP of Product at 360 Company, who was heavily recruited by Baidu to take charge of the new team.
