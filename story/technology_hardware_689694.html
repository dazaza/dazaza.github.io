NPD: 3D LCD TV Panel Shipments Up 27% Q/Q in 3Q

by anonymous on 2012-03-04 14:04:51

Sina Technology News, Beijing time, morning of November 25th - The latest data from the American market research company NPD shows that the shipment volume of 3D LCD TV panels reached 6.6 million units in the third quarter of this year, representing a 27% increase year-over-year. Among them, polarized and shutter glasses grew by 34% and 23%, respectively.

NPD's data indicates that panel manufacturers expect a 30% growth in the shipment of 3D LCD TV panels for the fourth quarter. Downstream manufacturers and brands continue to promote 3D TVs, while end-user awareness and interest in these products are also increasing. In the third quarter of this year, the proportion of 3D TV panels in the overall LCD TV panel shipments has already reached 12%.

Yihsien Hsieh, Vice President of NPD Greater China, said: "The TFT LCD industry has experienced six consecutive quarters of oversupply, which also affects the premium pricing level of 3D panels. Despite the continued existence of insufficient 3D content and services as well as terminal demand, LCD TV manufacturers are actively promoting 3D TVs through low prices and innovative low-cost technologies."

Although there was no growth in LCD TV panel shipments in the third quarter, 3D panel shipments achieved healthy growth, and panel manufacturers expect a 30% sequential growth in these products for the fourth quarter. According to NPD’s estimates, the shipment volume of 3D LCD TV panels this year will reach 21.5 million units, with the proportion of 3D panels in the overall LCD TV panels reaching 10% (polarized at 4%, shutter at 6%). Additionally, with the release of a series of newly developed low-cost 3D solutions, panel manufacturers expect the proportion of 3D panels in the overall LCD TV panels to reach 20% by the third quarter of 2012. The report points out that the increased penetration rate of 3D TVs will drive an increase in content.

NPD found that apart from 3D TVs, panel manufacturers are also actively promoting 3D monitor panels, mainly targeting the entertainment PC and gaming markets. In the field of 3D LCD monitors, polarized panel manufacturers are more active than shutter glass manufacturers. Panel manufacturers expect that after the second quarter of 2012, the shipment volume of 3D LCD monitor panels will exceed 1.5 million units per quarter, compared to only 250,000 units in the third quarter of this year.

The television industry faced significant challenges this year. Weak consumer demand in developed regions such as North America, Japan, and Western Europe has led to poor profitability for some TV and panel manufacturers. Due to supply chain inventory issues persisting into the middle of the year, many TV brands have reduced their shipment plans for 2011 and cut back on panel orders for the third quarter, leading to a wider decline in core panel prices. However, for consumers, the good news is that the upcoming Christmas shopping season will see more attractive TV prices, especially during the U.S.'s "Black Friday." (Si Yuan)
