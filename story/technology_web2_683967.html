Exclusive to the Essay Network, no reproduction without permission. If I mention Earth Day, what comes to your mind? Is it the garbage-filled rivers, the chimneys spewing black smoke as seen on Quanxun Network, or the seemingly endless trash in the stairwells of buildings, like those mentioned on www.bjsxdz.com, or even someone spitting on the ground? Every day, incidents of environmental neglect occur. Have you ever acted this way? If so, can you take the occasion of World Environment Day to reflect on your actions? If not, have you ever stopped others from doing so? Fellow students, I hope all of us can advocate together: protect the environment, defend our home, starting from every little action! Third grade student at Han Tie Elementary School: ltcllmn.
