China Safety Breast Enlargement Summit: Free Removal of Polyacrylamide Hydrogel (Omeida) Used for Breast and Nose Augmentation

China · Shanghai · February 25

Summary: On February 25th, the China Safety Breast Enlargement Summit will be grandly held at Yilemei Plastic Surgery Hospital in Shanghai. During the conference, Professor Luan Jie, Director of the Breast Plastic Surgery and Reconstruction Center at the Plastic Surgery Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, will comprehensively and deeply interpret the latest developments regarding the PIP incident for the public for the first time. In conjunction with the Yilemei Asia-Pacific Breast Augmentation Demonstration Base in Shanghai, a formal announcement will be made to all individuals across China who have undergone breast augmentation using inferior PIP implants from the 2005 batch or Omeida polyacrylamide hydrogel implants. A removal campaign will be conducted from February 18, 2012, to March 8, 2012.

Figure 1: Omeida Implant Incident Diagram

The Omeida incident is another significant public health event following the blood transfusion-related HIV/AIDS infections in the mid-1990s. Approved by the National Medical Products Administration (NMPA), "Omeida" was used as a soft tissue filler material for cosmetic surgeries such as breast augmentation, temporal augmentation, cheek augmentation, and buttock augmentation. Data from the National Adverse Drug Reaction Monitoring Center shows that from 2002 to November 2005, there were 183 reported adverse reaction cases. There were serious violations in the approval process by the Medical Device Department, and the non-compliance operations and false promises within the beauty industry, along with the lack of self-regulation, resulted in nearly 300,000 people suffering painful consequences.

To promote the construction of a healthy and safe plastic surgery breast enhancement environment in our country, improve medical aesthetic techniques in the field of breast augmentation, and strengthen the supervision and application of breast implant materials, under the close attention of the public, the "China Safety Breast Enlargement Summit" has emerged accordingly. This summit will invite the most authoritative breast enlargement experts domestically, renowned breast augmentation masters from famous plastic surgery hospitals nationwide, and relevant industry professionals to jointly discuss the issues and development directions facing the breast augmentation industry in an environment full of opportunities and challenges, promoting the healthy, safe, and long-term development of China's plastic surgery breast augmentation field.

Yilemei Plastic Surgery Hospital in Shanghai, the largest plastic surgery hospital in the country and an industry benchmark in the Chinese plastic surgery sector, as well as the host of the "China Safety Breast Enlargement Summit," aims to help women who have undergone breast augmentation using inferior PIP implants from the 2005 batch or Omeida polyacrylamide hydrogel implants to alleviate their troubles and pain. On the occasion of the convening of the "China · Shanghai Safety Breast Enlargement Summit," the Asia-Pacific Breast Augmentation Demonstration Base in Shanghai formally announces that it will provide free expert consultations, examinations, and assistance based on actual needs, waiving the surgical fees for the removal of inferior implants. The best services, experts, equipment, and thousand-grade laminar airflow clean operating rooms will be utilized. To ensure victims receive timely treatment, please make sure to provide proof of PIP breast implant materials from the 2005 batch or Omeida material certification before visiting the hospital.

For more details, please visit the hospital for consultation or contact your personal senior doctor assistant. You can also make an appointment via the Beauty Hotline or website.
