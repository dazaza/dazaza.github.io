Rumor: Firefox 3.5 will have a new Firefox icon

by cennatian on 2009-05-17 22:28:06

User Experience Design team member Alex Faaborg has revealed on the official Mozilla blog that Firefox 3.5 (Shiretoko) will soon have a new Firefox Logo, in which he gives a sketch of the new Firefox 3.5 icon. The new author icon is Anthony Piraino, inspired by a concept sketch by Jon Hicks in 2007. Alex Faaborg also provides more information about the new ICONS, including a large clear version, creative ideas, and more. From the sketch of the new icon, the overall Firefox icon remains the same, and the blazing fire in the tail part is a more obvious change, and the new icon should be an evolution of the old icon. Firefox 3.5 is about to release RC version, I don't know if this new Logo will meet with us at that time.
