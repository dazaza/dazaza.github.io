Young housewife meets a soulmate and gets lost in the online world, leading to the disappearance of her child.

Thereafter, Wang Hao also disappeared. Xiaojuan dialed his mobile phone number, but Wang Hao's phone was off. Xiaojuan then went online to contact Wang Hao through a chat tool, but he was always offline and did not respond. Poor Xiaojuan finally woke up from her dream. On the afternoon of January 9 this year, accompanied by her family, she reported the case to the criminal investigation team of Yanzhu Public Security Bureau, requesting the police to help her find her son. One day in September last year, the two met again. Wang Hao proposed taking Xiaojuan and Xuanxuan on an outing for relaxation. Naive Xiaojuan believed him and followed Wang Hao to Juye County, Heze City, Shandong Province, over a thousand kilometers away, staying at the home of Wang Hao's friend.

On the morning of February 13, the villagers from Chepan Office in Yancheng District warmly celebrated as they came to the front of the Yanzhu Public Security Branch with gongs and drums. The villagers placed plaques and banners there, expressing their heartfelt gratitude through the words written on them. I learned that this touching scene originated from the online experience of Xiaojuan (a pseudonym), a resident of Chepan Office. Despite the victim being unable to provide the suspect's real identity or specific address, the special task force officers still retrieved photos of more than a thousand men matching the described features using the national population information network, allowing Xiaojuan to identify them. Meanwhile, the special task force officers investigated based on the suspect’s accent in the areas of Wenxi County and Xia County in Yuncheng City. On the afternoon of January 13, when the officers showed Xiaojuan the photo of Liu Mou, a man from Shimmen Township in Wenxi County who had significant suspicion, Xiaojuan immediately recognized him as "Wang Hao," the "love killer" she trusted so much.

Xu Mou confessed after being arrested, but initially denied all charges despite the evidence presented against him. Eventually, he admitted selling Xuanxuan to Guo Mou from Juye County.

On the morning of January 31, 62-year-old Guo Mou was captured. It turned out that Guo Mou's daughter had been married for years without bearing children, so Guo Mou discussed with her and her husband to buy Xuanxuan for 41,000 yuan from Xu Mou. However, shortly after the Spring Festival, Guo Mou's daughter and her husband went to work in Shanghai, taking Xuanxuan with them.

The special task force patiently explained policies and laws to Guo Mou, making him realize that buying and selling children is a criminal act. Regretful, Guo Mou agreed to cooperate with the police to have his daughter return with the abducted child Xuanxuan to their hometown in Shandong.

Currently, suspects Liu Mou and Xu Mou have both been criminally detained by the Yanzhu Public Security Branch. Due to this, the villagers from the area where Xiaojuan and Xuanxuan live came to the bureau with gongs and drums to express their gratitude to the people's police.

After detailed questioning of Xiaojuan, the special task force officers preliminarily determined that Wang Hao had significant suspicion of committing the crime and must be found first. However, although Xiaojuan had met Wang Hao multiple times and even traveled with him, the personal information about Wang Hao she provided, after being checked by the police on the national population information network, did not match the Wang Hao she claimed.

Although Liu Mou could not specify the exact town or village of Xu Mou's home in Juye County, driven by a high sense of responsibility towards the victim, the special task force officers set out overnight, braving the cold to rush to Juye County in Shandong Province to arrest Xu Mou. With the cooperation of local public security organs, the special task force visited more than 200 villages and finally located Xu Mou's residence on January 21. However, Xu Mou had already left, his whereabouts unknown.

Unexpectedly, just as Xiaojuan was immersed in the joy of the new environment, Xuanxuan went missing unexpectedly. This panicked Xiaojuan, Wang Hao, and his friends, who searched everywhere. After searching fruitlessly, soon afterward, Xiaojuan returned to Yuncheng with Wang Hao.

On February 1, at the Heze bus station in Shandong Province, Xuanxuan was safely handed over to the special task force officers and returned to Yuncheng that day, reuniting with his mother Xiaojuan after more than 100 days of separation.

Upon receiving the report, Deputy Director Zhou Xin of the Yuncheng Municipal Public Security Bureau and Director of the Yanhu Branch attached great importance to the case and immediately convened Deputy Director Ning Junjie and the head of the Criminal Investigation Team Ma Xuemin to study the case. That evening, the "January 9" Child Trafficking Case Task Force of the bureau was established.
