Interiors Firm's Semi-Annual Report: Business Shrinks by Over 20%

by longersing on 2009-08-28 17:33:42

Interior decoration company's semi-annual report: business contraction exceeds more than two percent. Where does this data come from? Is it the decline during the summer peak season for renovation or is it affected by the real estate market? Shanghai Decoration Network has extracted some reports from various online sources to provide everyone, hoping it will be helpful for Shanghai's decoration companies.

"From last year's second half to now, home decoration business is getting increasingly difficult." According to Mr. Mo, a person in charge of a certain decoration company in Nancheng, in recent months, the company's home decoration business has sharply contracted, decreasing from originally over 80% of total business volume to now only accounting for about half. Due to the continuous lackluster industry, the company has no choice but to gradually shift its focus to commercial decoration.

Accompanying the sharp reduction in business volume is the decrease in home decoration profit margins. Mr. Mo revealed that previously, the gross profit margin for home decoration projects was generally around 40%, now any project that can yield a profit margin of over ten points "everyone scrambles to take on." The reason being there are too few home decoration projects, and many homeowners set very low budgets for their renovations. In order to secure business, decoration companies have no choice but to "endure the pain" and maximize the concession of profits to attract homeowners to sign contracts.

Mr. Mo gave an example saying, two years ago, the average renovation quote for a three-bedroom, two-living room apartment in Dongguan was around 80,000 yuan, now for the same type of apartment, a quote of 50,000 yuan would be considered good. Even worse, some homeowners only require walls to be painted and tiles laid, skipping even basic decorations, "how can one make money?"

It is understood that under the situation of a sluggish market, smaller scale decoration companies like Mr. Mo's are most significantly impacted. On one hand, these companies have limited strength and cannot compete with larger companies for high-end housing or commercial decoration "big orders"; on the other hand, their customer base mainly consists of white-collar workers and salaried classes, who are "highly sensitive" to price quotes. Any slight fluctuation in the market directly affects the decoration company. According to incomplete statistics within the industry, since the second half of last year, over 300 decoration companies in Dongguan have gone bankrupt, the vast majority of which are medium and small-sized decoration companies primarily focused on home decoration business.

Due to the inability to ensure sufficient traditional business volume, medium and small-scale decoration companies are pushed to the forefront of the market. A design director surnamed Qian from a well-known decoration company in Guancheng stated that Dongguan's decoration industry was originally unhealthy, plagued by issues such as malicious competition and setting consumer traps to deceive consumers. The persistent market "cold spell" objectively eliminated poorly operated small companies, also bringing new development opportunities to other rule-abiding enterprises.

In response to the issues of reduced contract signing volume and low quotations, many decoration companies in Dongguan have expanded into new businesses this year. Mr. Qian said that with the large-scale launch of housing units below 90 square meters, fully renovated houses have become a new profit growth point for home decoration companies. Some companies that previously only focused on villa or large luxury housing renovations have actively moved to seize the market share of fully renovated houses. Although the overall construction cost of fully renovated houses is not high, due to fewer design and construction stages, the profit margin is relatively generous. With a certain amount of signed contracts, the benefits can be quite substantial. Therefore, this year, some medium and small-sized decoration companies have introduced all-inclusive renovation packages at 398 yuan and 498 yuan per square meter, receiving good market reactions.

Moreover, some decoration companies have also targeted the secondary housing renovation business. It is reported that in recent months, the sales of secondary housing in Dongguan have continued to grow. The buyers of these houses mostly meet self-residence needs, and after acquiring the house, they generally need to renovate and transform the kitchen, bathroom, walls, and floors, thus creating a huge market demand. Many small-scale decoration companies have therefore promoted themselves as "old house renovation experts" to vie for this seemingly "insignificant" market share.

Industry insiders believe that regardless of how the market changes, as long as decoration companies can make timely adjustments according to the trends of the upstream real estate market, they can seize the initiative and gain a share in fierce market competition.

Currently, the main promotion by decoration companies is the fully renovated house business, which has received good market feedback. However, some consumers do have concerns: although the renovation prices have dropped, can the quality of renovation be guaranteed? An insider who once specialized in doing fully renovated houses for developers revealed many tricks involved in fully renovated houses, worthy of attention. This insider clearly stated that when decoration companies show homeowners the procurement list, they do not provide detailed lists of house renovation costs, only providing general brand names. Of course, these brands are mostly top-tier ones in the market, but there is much knowledge behind this. For the same brand, the differences can be significant. For example, regarding flooring, high-quality flooring from top-tier brands is approximately 600-700 yuan per square meter, but the special-priced ones from the same brand are around 200 yuan per square meter, which can significantly reduce costs. Therefore, when inspecting the house, homeowners should ideally find professionals to check it out and provide detailed lists. If there are detailed lists, many problems can surface.
