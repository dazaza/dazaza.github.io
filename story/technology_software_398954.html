Can you gain experience even when offline? This is the first time I've heard of it! In "Tank Wars Online," a leisure competitive online game, there's such a great feature. Players just need to log out of the game properly and upon logging back in, they can collect experience points, making full use of time to help them level up faster. This convenient system has been highly praised by the players of Tank Wars. Let's take a look below!

[Super Convenient! Gain experience even while offline]

Yes, you read that right! Now playing Tank Wars, you can accumulate experience even while offline. When you log back in, all you have to do is click your mouse lightly and you can easily obtain experience points! From level 1 to 50, as long as the cumulative offline time reaches 24 hours in one go, the offline experience pool will start accumulating experience. When you log back in, you can receive the offline experience reward. The longer the offline time, the more experience you can get. Of course, higher-level players will also gain more experience. However, please note that the offline experience pool will fill up in 3 days, so don't stay offline for too long. Your friends in the game will miss you!

Simple leveling with lots of benefits, Tank Wars is simply irresistible~

However, please note that you must use the [Exit Game] option in the game's [System] menu to normally accumulate experience. Directly closing the game page will not allow you to accumulate experience.

Log in and easily collect five-digit experience rewards

[Lying down and leveling up, it's just too good!]

Besides being able to accumulate experience while offline, are there any other lazy man's ways to level up? The answer is definitely yes! Players can collect experience and silver coins daily just by logging in, and can also upgrade through online training.

Every day, players can claim their daily login rewards, which include both experience and silver coins! Isn't it like collecting your daily salary? You can also accumulate sign-in times to claim even more rewards! These rewards include a large amount of silver coins, precious items, and high-level skill cards. Players can use silver coins to purchase powerful tanks in the game, combining these precious items and skill cards to ensure that you don't have to spend a single cent to fully enjoy Tank Wars.

After gaining experience and leveling up, no enemy will be a match for us!

Online training is a very popular way to level up in Tank Wars among players. Players only need to spend a small amount of game currency to experience the joy of leveling up quickly while lying down. Among these, advanced online training not only provides a large amount of experience but also increases tank category proficiency. Tank category proficiency is an attribute that can significantly enhance tank power! It allows you to improve both your level and strength simultaneously. Do you like this kind of leveling method?

Not only can you level up by fighting enemies!
