Advice for women aged 23-29 (Ladies, come and take a look)

by lige03996 on 2010-05-30 00:13:29

Don't boast about how much you can drink on the field, or how manly you are when drinking and playing finger-guessing games with others. Those who truly care about you won't let you get drunk. Get over the daydream of being a Cinderella or a princess. Your feet aren't that small to fit into the crystal shoes. If you have money to buy an electric shaver for your boyfriend, it would be better to buy a pair of socks for your dad. He's wearing the socks that your mom has mended several times. Have you noticed? Have a stable job and earn more money. Women must be financially independent to have an independent personality, so as not to become Nora in the future. As Mr. Lu Xun said, after Nora leaves home, she either comes back or becomes a prostitute. Middle-aged men, especially the rich ones, if they invite you to have dinner, sing karaoke, or go to bars, just refuse them. You can't outwit them. If a man who once betrayed you wants to come back, say no to him. Learn some clothing coordination. Don't wear red and purple together anymore when you go out. For the vows of immature boys in your youth, just laugh it off. He definitely didn't think before he said those words. Stick to principles on major issues, and learn to be flexible on minor issues. There's no need to argue with your boyfriend until you're both red in the face. If you're right, he'll think you're competitive and unyielding; if you're wrong, he'll think you're unreasonable and never-ending. If you have money to buy a high-end necklace, it's better than wearing fake jade and looking tasteless. Stop imitating aggressive girlfriends and beating up your man. If you break him, there will be no one to carry rice and flour bags for you. Don't fall in love with married men. When his wife, children, and elderly parents all act together, you will end up very tragically. Have a physical examination every year, especially for liver, kidney, and breast. Occasionally cook with your mother. It's always happier than eating and drinking with your girl friends for no reason. Be sincere to the boyfriend you really want to marry. Don't think that you're playing with other people's feelings. Your opponent may be more experienced than you. If you want to marry him, take good precautions; if you don't want to marry him, take even better precautions. Don't be a spendthrift. You should start saving some dowry money. Always remember that playing online games with boys day and night only belongs to the university period. Unless it's really necessary, there's no need to always switch to the latest mobile phone model. Otherwise, others will think you're a mobile phone salesperson. Understand your parents' health conditions. At this point, you should at least have a driver's license, whether or not you have a car. At least have one ambiguous opposite-sex friend (without sexual relations), who can be your mental treasure box or trash can. Don't get too close to your girlfriend's boyfriend, or you'll be scolded by your sisters behind your back. Don't expect your boyfriend to be good at earning money, washing clothes, and doing housework. He doesn't have Superman's underwear to wear outside. Don't repeatedly emphasize that your boyfriend must respect your parents after marriage, or even claim to bring your parents to live together. Let your husband get along with his in-laws. People are mutual. If he also brings his parents, your family will be more lively than you can imagine. Don't talk about marriage as soon as you start dating, and don't reject your boyfriend's proposal twice in a row. Don't think that you must find a Tony Leung or Terry Gou in your life because you're not Carrie Hsu. Don't date boys who are more than three years younger than you because you're not Faye Wong. Buy insurance. If your company offers social security, you don't need to. Buy some practical things for your mom. She will feel hundreds of times better than receiving flowers. Instead of going to SPA alone, accompany your mom to take a bath and give her a back rub. Follow the principle of "everything happens for a reason," but don't give up efforts. Don't put too much pressure on yourself, and don't try to be a domineering iron lady. You will become more and more lonely. If you have surplus funds, you can consider buying a house. The house for marriage doesn't necessarily have to be fully borne by your boyfriend. Don't make men seem too complicated. People often complicate each other unnecessarily, but also don't treat them as fools. Don't cling to your boyfriend all the time, give him some space, and the relationship will deepen. Occasionally visit your boyfriend's parents. It's more effective than just flattering him. Having multiple boyfriends is not something you can brag about. It will only make you seem unreliable. Love is not a showy thing, especially in front of many people. Calling your boyfriend around will make him lose face, and it will also make people think you're vulgar. At this point, if you're still idle, no one will praise you as a good girl anymore. Don't think smoking makes you look charming. It's bad for your skin, and it looks sleazy. If you can still read, then read books frequently to improve your self-cultivation. Don't look down on your sister who works seriously but earns little. She eats a bowl of noodles, which is her hard-earned money. Don't boast or think highly of yourself in front of friends (this is very annoying). Pay attention to national affairs. Don't say the Russian president is still Putin. Politics isn't just men's business. Care about your boyfriend occasionally, but caring doesn't mean nagging and supervision. When you eat out with your boyfriend, don't refuse to pay because you're a girl, thinking it's his duty to treat you. Why should he do that when you haven't eaten any less? Learn makeup. Plain beauty depends on age, and you'll soon lose this advantage. Most men think women should be as charming at night as they are during the day. If you don't have Lin Daiyu's talent, looks, and background, stop crying all the time. Even if you can recite poems like Lin Daiyu, you probably don't want to follow her bloody footsteps, especially since Baoyu eventually married someone else. At least maintain a healthy hobby, such as swimming, jogging, or table tennis. Remember, shopping doesn't count. Don't envy those who have more good sisters than you. Their troubles are proportional to the number. Never think other people's husbands are better than yours because they don't love you. Take care of your hair. A head full of messy grass cannot be beautified by cosmetics. Praise your boyfriend more. One word from you might be his motivation and bring economic benefits to you. The best boyfriend for further development should be introduced to your parents. Sometimes the eyes of the elders are sharper than yours. Don't learn to swear like men. You're not a little gangster girl, but you will become an old gangster girl. Be proud of being frugal and ashamed of eating snacks randomly. Clothes don't need to be changed every day. Being overly dressed can easily make people dizzy. Keep a certain distance from your boyfriend's male friends. If you're too far away, he'll say you're not enthusiastic enough towards his friends; if you're too close, things will get serious. No event or man is worth losing sleep over, getting drunk on the street, or buying ***. Women, their prime is only for a few years. After that, it's not just about looks but experience. Always smile gracefully.
