Relevant matters regarding the entrance examination:

1. Registration time and place for the Shenyang examination hall and registration requirements

1) First round registration time: December 9, 2008 - December 23, 2008, 8:30-16:30 (no rest on weekends)

2) Second round registration time: February 10, 2009 - February 20, 2009, 8:30-16:30 (no rest on weekends)

3) Registration location: South Campus Music Theater, Shenyang Conservatory of Music

4) Registration requirements:

a) Graduates or those with equivalent academic ability from ordinary high schools, secondary art schools, or secondary vocational schools can all apply; age limit does not apply.

b) Candidates for dramatic and film performance, musical performance, broadcasting and hosting, singing, dancing, basic music education, fashion model education should meet the height requirement of this major. For candidates with special talent, if recommended by two experts and approved by the college admission committee, the height requirement may be appropriately reduced; color blindness or weakness candidates cannot apply for the Department of Dramatic and Film Art Design, Stage Art Department, and Social Art Education Department.

c) When registering, candidates must hold original and copies of ID card or household book, three recent one-inch color photos without hat, and a copy of the "2009 Art Major Entrance Examination Admission Ticket" (if not yet obtained during registration, it must be sent to the South Campus Admissions Office of Shenyang Conservatory of Music no later than March 15, 2009).

d) According to the Ministry of Education's requirements, art candidates must simultaneously obtain the "Unified Examination Qualification Certificate" organized by the provincial admissions office where they are registered (except for provinces and majors that do not organize unified examinations) and the "Professional Qualification Certificate" from the South Campus of Shenyang Conservatory of Music in order to apply for the South Campus. If the candidate's unified examination affects enrollment, the consequences will be borne by the candidate themselves.

e) The registration and examination fee is 200 yuan, and each additional major requires an additional 100 yuan.

f) Out-of-town candidates can register by mail, and the deadline for mail registration is based on the postmark date when it arrives in Shenyang. The content of the letter should include: registration form (can be downloaded and filled out completely from the Shenyang Conservatory of Music website), ID card or household book copy, "2009 Art Major Entrance Examination Admission Ticket" copy, three recent one-inch color photos without hat, and a registration and examination fee of 200 yuan (each additional major requires an additional 100 yuan); candidates who register by mail must collect the "Admission Ticket" at the South Campus one day before the exam with their ID card.

g) Candidates who have obtained the level 10 certificate of relevant majors in the amateur grading examination of Shenyang Conservatory of Music can be exempted from the professional main subject examination, and the score will be calculated as 80 points, but other subjects must take the exam (relevant materials' original and copies must be provided during registration).

h) The preliminary and final exams for each major are completed once.

i) Candidates can choose either one of the two rounds of exams or both. If both rounds of exams are taken, under the condition that both exams are qualified, the highest total score among the two exams will be the candidate's final exam score.

j) Students within the third ring road of Shenyang city must attend school as commuting students.

2. Examination time and location for the Shenyang examination hall

1) First round examination time: December 29, 2008 - December 31, 2008

2) Second round examination time: February 26, 2009 - February 28, 2009

3) Examination location: South Campus, Shenyang Conservatory of Music

3. Registration and examination time and location for out-of-province examination halls

The South Campus will set up out-of-province examination halls in Hangzhou and Wuhan in January or February 2009. Specific registration and examination times and locations can be checked at any time on the recruitment information column of the South Campus website of Shenyang Conservatory of Music.

4. Methods for querying examination results and distribution standards for the "Professional Qualification Certificate"

1) Candidates can log into the South Campus website of Shenyang Conservatory of Music within the specified time to query their professional examination results (manual telephone queries are not accepted). The query time is detailed on the "Admission Ticket".

2) Each major direction sets passing lines for interview scores and written test scores. After passing the line, candidates are ranked according to the total scores of all professional course exams. Based on the enrollment plan, our school distributes the "Professional Qualification Certificate" at a ratio of about 1:3.5.

5. Filling out the application form

Candidates must participate in the national general higher education institution unified enrollment examination and fill out the application form at the designated time in their place of registration.

6. Enrollment

1) Based on the lowest cultural course score line set by the Liaoning Provincial Examination Office for art courses, the South Campus independently sets the cultural course qualification score line (no English score line), and enrolls students according to the ranking of professional course total scores, comprehensively evaluating and selecting the best students for each major.

2) Candidates applying for the Department of Art Literature must achieve the standard set by the South Campus in the Chinese language section of the college entrance examination.

3) Under equal conditions, candidates from the Wenchuan earthquake area will be given priority in enrollment.

4) Candidates who pass the professional course and whose first choice is the South Campus and whose total cultural course score reaches over 500 points will be given priority in enrollment.

5) Candidates whose total cultural course score ranks in the top five among the admitted candidates of the South Campus will be exempt from four years of tuition fees.

6) Other preferential treatment and priority enrollment methods will be implemented according to the relevant regulations of the Ministry of Education, Liaoning Provincial Department of Education, and the Provincial Examination Office.

7. Others

Any unresolved or contradictory matters in this introduction will be executed according to the relevant documents of the provincial admissions examination office and the relevant regulations of the Shenyang Conservatory of Music Admissions Committee.
