Treatment prescriptions for various gynecological inflammations

Xi'an Ciai Gynecology Hospital warmly reminds many women who are troubled by gynecological inflammations, and seek medical treatment everywhere. According to the experts' opinions, this article introduces some common treatments of gynecological diseases to help everyone.

1. Treatment of vulvitis

Firstly, test vaginal secretion, urine and secretions culture, then treat according to the cause. For example, treat diabetes, repair urinary fistula, treat cervical inflammation, etc. In addition, traditional Chinese medicine can be used: 15g of cnidium fruit, 15g of cortex dictamni, 15g of rhizoma smilacis glabrae, 15g of cortex phellodendri, and 6g of Sichuan pepper boiled in water for external washing, once or twice a day.

2. Treatment of non-specific vaginitis

Treat according to the cause, pay attention to hygiene, improve overall health, and strictly control sexual activity before cure. In addition, 1% lactic acid can be used for vaginal irrigation to correct the acidity and alkalinity of the vagina. Uterine perforation, antibiotic ointment or anti-inflammatory powder (such as Fupisan) can be applied inside the vagina, once a day for seven days.

3. Treatment of candidal vaginitis

a. Eliminate inducement. If you have diabetes, give automatic treatment; stop using broad-spectrum antibiotics and estrogen on time. Change underwear frequently, and boil used underwear, basins, and towels for disinfection.

b. Modify the acidity and alkalinity of the vagina. Use 2% to 4% sodium bicarbonate solution to flush the vagina to create conditions unfavorable for candida growth.

c. Antifungal agents. After flushing with alkaline solution, use one of the following drugs:

c1: Clotrimazole suppositories or tablets, once a night, each time one suppository or tablet, insert into the deep part of the vagina, continue for seven days;

c2: Daktarin (Miconazole nitrate) suppositories, insert one suppository into the vagina every night, continue for seven days;

c3: Nystatin suppositories or tablets, once a night, each time one suppository or tablet (100,000 U), continue for seven to ten days;

c4: Micatin effervescent vaginal tablets, insert one tablet into the vagina every night, continue for five days;

c5: Apply 1% gentian violet aqueous solution to the vagina, three to four times a week, continue for two weeks. The drug is inexpensive and effective, but attention should be paid to the concentration of the drug. If the drug is too concentrated or used too frequently, it may easily cause chemical vulvitis and epidermal ulceration. Because the drug easily stains clothes, it is now less clinically used.
