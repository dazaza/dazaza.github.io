Introduction:

A light comedy reflecting the love, marriage, career, and life of young people in rural areas, it is a bright and cheerful rural youth idol drama. Through the love stories of several pairs of young people such as college student Xie Yongqiang and rural woman Wang Xiaomeng, the drama portrays a group of vivid and lively images of young people in the new era, presenting us with a multi-angle depiction of contemporary rural youth's love life. It carries joy and humor while being full of hope and strength through its twists and turns. Love is not just for the young; the middle-aged couple, Village Chief Zhang Gui and Xie Dajiao, also vividly perform their own dramatic and passionate love story.

This time, both the male and female leads are newcomers. Zhao Benshan and Fan Wei no longer play leading roles but instead make cameo appearances as father and son. This is the first time that newcomers have taken on the main roles. The female lead is Wang Yabin from the Beijing Dance Academy, who previously served as a dance double for Zhang Ziyi in the movie "Ten Faces of Ambush." The male lead, He Shufeng, is a student at the Ben Shan Art Academy, selected by Zhao Benshan during a school visit due to his outstanding performance. In the drama, he plays Xie Yongqiang. The romance between the male and female leads will undergo hardships and trials, but ultimately ends happily.

Story outline: Forestry University graduate Xie Yongqiang returns to the village awaiting job placement. During this period, he falls deeply in love with Wang Xiaomeng, a local young woman. Xie Yongqiang's father, Xie Guangkun, has always hoped for his son to succeed, and upon hearing about his son's relationship with Wang Xiaomeng, strongly opposes it. Xie Guangkun personally seeks out Wang Xiaomeng’s father, Wang Laoqi, to interfere with the engagement.

Wang Laoqi, already concerned about Wang Xiaomeng reaching too high, expresses his stance: Wang Xiaomeng would not marry Xie Yongqiang under any circumstances. This places Xie Yongqiang and Wang Xiaomeng's love into a precarious situation.

The daughter of the village chief, Zhang Gui, named Xiangxiu, has always harbored feelings for Xie Yongqiang and fantasizes about his future job. For her daughter's happiness, Zhang Gui personally finds Qisantai, the uncle of Xie Yongqiang, to act as a matchmaker. The Xie family had always placed their hopes for finding jobs on Qisantai. Xiangxiu's inclusion causes significant waves within the Xie family. To secure Xie Yongqiang's job, Wang Xiaomeng persuades Xie Yongqiang to ostensibly agree to Xiangxiu's proposal. Xie Guangkun plays along, deceiving everyone, and gradually forces Xie Yongqiang closer to Xiangxiu. After a series of incidents, Wang Xiaomeng realizes the gap between herself and university graduate Xie Yongqiang, re-evaluating their relationship.

Meanwhile, Wang Xiaomeng's family asks neighbor Zhao Yutian to sell grain in the city, but an accident occurs resulting in Zhao Yutian injuring his leg. Zhao Yutian's future father-in-law, Liu Neng, worried about his son-in-law's condition, sends Xie Dajiao to inquire at the hospital. Misjudging the situation, Xie Dajiao mistakenly informs Liu Neng that Zhao Yutian might become disabled. Liu Neng forcibly breaks off the engagement between his daughter Liu Ying and Zhao Yutian.

By chance, Xie Yongqiang becomes engaged to Xiangxiu. The head of the village breeding farm, Liu Yishui, begins secretly pursuing the heartbroken Wang Xiaomeng. Xie Xiaomei, the urban girl who resigned to work at the breeding farm, feels disheartened.

Zhao Yutian's leg recovers fully, leaving Liu Neng regretful. Liu Ying visits Zhao Yutian, but Zhao Yutian demands an apology from Liu Neng. Unable to save face, Liu Neng hopes Zhao's family will seek him out, leading to a psychological battle between the Zhao and Liu families.

Wang Laoqi feels apologetic about Zhao Yutian's failed marriage and asks Xie Dajiao to arrange another match. Knowing his daughter Liu Ying is suitable for Zhao Yutian, Liu Neng hears about this and urgently pleads with Xie Dajiao not to proceed. Feeling guilty about misjudging earlier, Xie Dajiao secretly helps Liu Neng. Zhao Yutian's attempts at matchmaking fail repeatedly, leaving his family disheartened. In desperation, Wang Laoqi announces that if Zhao Yutian cannot find a wife, Wang Xiaomeng will be married to Zhao Yutian. Zhao Yutian pretends to be lame to win Wang Xiaomeng but is exposed by Liu Yishui.

Xie Yongqiang unexpectedly gets assigned to teach at a village elementary school instead of the city. Feeling lost and ashamed, Xie Yongqiang loses confidence. Regretting her decision to marry someone hoping to live in the city, Xiangxiu considers breaking off the engagement. Fearing angering Qisantai, Zhang Gui asks Xiangxiu to persist for some time. However, Xiangxiu develops feelings for Li Daguo, the urban driver at the breeding farm, distancing herself from Xie Yongqiang. Only now does Xie Guangkun realize Wang Xiaomeng's charm and embarks on the journey to propose to Wang Laoqi's family, only to be rejected.

In frustration, Xie Yongqiang writes a math problem on the blackboard: 3+2=Wang Xiaomeng, drawing ridicule from other teachers. Enraged, Xie Yongqiang leaves the school and focuses on studying to take the county civil service exam. However, without receiving any admission notices, Xie Yongqiang feels immense pain. Through the ordeal, Xie Yongqiang becomes more pragmatic, deciding to start anew. Leveraging his professional strengths, he develops barren mountains and plants fruit trees. Grounded Xie Yongqiang rekindles Wang Xiaomeng's affection for him, reminiscent of their initial romance. Wang Xiaomeng also helps him develop the barren mountain. When Xie Yongqiang's forestry base reaches a certain scale, the county admission notice arrives unexpectedly. Facing a vast orchard and the hard-won love with Wang Xiaomeng, Xie Yongqiang tears up the admission notice... Zhao Yutian's second attempt at matchmaking fails again...

Wang Laoqi hopes Wang Xiaomeng can marry Zhao Yutian and goes on a hunger strike at the hospital. Xie Dajiao reveals Liu Neng's interference in Zhao Yutian's matchmaking to protect Wang Xiaomeng. Realizing the truth, Zhao Yutian takes the initiative to reconcile with Liu Ying.
