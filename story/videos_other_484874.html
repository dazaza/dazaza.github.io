10-Year-Old Girl Abducted and Killed Two Days Before School Starts

by pex9wew8n on 2011-09-03 10:50:15

"About 1.31 meters tall, long hair, ponytail, slim figure, wearing a pink dress and pink sandals when she went missing..." On August 31st at around 4 PM, the official Weibo account of the Zhangjiagang Public Security Bureau issued a missing person notice for Xiao, a 10-year-old girl from Huayuan Village No.4 in Yangshe Town, Zhangjiagang City. The notice stated that Xiao carried a pink Konka bar phone and disappeared on the morning of August 30th at about 9 AM. "The adorable 10-year-old girl, we pray for your safe return to your mother's arms. Please don't hesitate to forward this!" This missing person notice caught the attention and was shared by many netizens, with over 2000 forwards and more than 400 comments within a short time.

At 6:50 PM on August 31st, suspect Xu (male, 42 years old, from Yangshe Town, Zhangjiagang City) surrendered to the police under pressure.

By reporter Chen Hongjiang

Yesterday was the first day of the new semester, but Xiao, a 10-year-old girl from Yangshe Town, Zhangjiagang, would never step into the classroom again. Her family was devastated as they faced her portrait. According to the Zhangjiagang police report, at around 9 AM on August 30th, Xu, a 42-year-old man from Yangshe Town, held Xiao hostage in Qianxi Alley intending to extort money from her family. Xiao was tragically killed during her struggle.

Currently, the police are conducting further investigations into the case.

According to Xu's confession, at around 9 AM on August 30th, he encountered the victim Xiao near his girlfriend's temporary residence in Qianxi Alley, Yangshe Town, Zhangjiagang, and had evil intentions. He held Xiao hostage intending to extort money from her family, but due to her resistance, he killed her. Relevant sources revealed that when suspect Xu attempted to control Xiao by covering her mouth, she struggled fiercely to call for help and was eventually strangled to death.

Why did Xiao lose contact with her family? Was she abducted by human traffickers or... As netizens speculated about the reasons for Xiao's disappearance and called for more people to join the search, news came that Xiao had unfortunately been killed, leaving netizens deeply saddened. At 10:30 PM on August 31st, the official Weibo account of the Zhangjiagang Public Security Bureau announced that Xiao had been killed by the suspect, her body found, and the suspect captured. "The police are currently reviewing the suspect, and further details will be officially released tomorrow!" In response to this terrible news, many netizens found it hard to believe. Netizen "Fan Ni Ka Jia Fa" said, "Dear child, are you really gone? If you've truly left, how will your parents and grandparents face your empty bedroom? Your classmates and teachers are still waiting for you to return to school! We all miss you!"

Early yesterday morning, a netizen posted that Xiao was killed near her grandmother's house and mentioned that she was about to enter the fourth grade. In the morning, the Zhangjiagang police gave a brief public update on the case. According to the report, at around 1:35 PM on August 30th, Yuan, a resident of Huayuan Village, reported to the authorities that her daughter Xiao had disappeared after leaving home at around 9 AM that day, with her mobile phone turned off. Upon receiving the report, the police immediately initiated an investigation protocol for suspected missing persons and quickly began a special case investigation.
