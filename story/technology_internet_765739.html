Get to Know YiXin, the App That Wants to Take On WeChat

by anonymous on 2013-08-20 14:51:02

On August 19th, YiXin (EasyChat) was launched to compete against WeChat. Jointly developed by China Telecom and NetEase, it is not the rumored "DingDing".

There may be several women in this world who don't eat, but there isn't a single woman who doesn't get jealous. Internet companies are like a group of women, all of them are envious of WeChat. Telecommunication operators don't have time to be jealous; they're being milked by WeChat. WeChat has embraced them closely, and the operators can't retreat. Can they cut off WeChat's milk supply and drive it back? The three major operators are pondering this question even in their dreams! FeiXin's first strike failed, and YiXin will re-enter the battlefield on Monday to fight WeChat again!

By this point, you might be laughing. You're laughing at how obsessed the traveler is. There is no precedent globally for telecommunication operators successfully operating internet businesses; WeChat is also laughing. However, even gods and Buddhas probably have their own pains.

Don't rush to conclusions just yet. Let's take a look at this new product. Android users can experience it first. Download it here: http://im.163.com/m/.

YiXin offers a "two-gifts-one-free" preferential policy for all TianYi users nationwide.

1. Register to receive 300M of China Telecom nationwide data: Users who successfully register for YiXin will receive 300M of nationwide data (data is given in the current month, valid for 30 days), with no restrictions on YiXin usage;

2. Active users can claim 60M of free data: Monthly active users (send more than 5 messages) will receive 60M of nationwide data the following month (users need to apply for redemption, data valid for 30 days), with no restrictions on YiXin usage.

3. Nationwide users will have YiXin instant messaging directed data charges waived: Depending on network deployment, future reductions in YiXin instant messaging directed data charges will be available for all China Telecom TianYi users nationwide.

Besides these, I would also like to talk about some other advantages of YiXin:

1. YiXin group chat limit is 100,

2. All YiXin personalized stickers are free, whether they're lewd, cute, sincere... various expressions are available

3. YiXin can even leave messages for landlines

4. NetEase accounts will soon be integrated with YiXin, at which point NetEase email, news, games, etc., will all be fully integrated with YiXin

5. YiXin will soon launch a public platform

Cooperation Framework

To counter WeChat, China Telecom and NetEase quickly came to an agreement. According to non-official channels, the key points of their cooperation are as follows:

1. Establish a joint venture, with China Telecom contributing traffic resources and ground marketing, and NetEase contributing funds;

2. YiXin provides mobile internet services without discrimination to mobile and landline users of China Telecom, China Unicom, and China Mobile;

3. Product development and operations are handled by the joint venture, fully led by Ding Lei;

4. The IDC costs required by YiXin, as well as inter-network settlements with Unicom and Mobile, are all borne by the joint venture, keeping relationships simple and clear.

The key point is that China Telecom handed over the operational control of the product to Ding Lei. This allows full utilization of China Telecom's resources and communication capabilities while isolating any potential adverse interference from China Telecom. In comparison, Unicom's cooperation with WeChat is self-defeating, and Mobile's FeiXin is constrained by its original system. Facing OTT, the three made different choices, and only time will tell who succeeds and who fails.

Experience + Free Violent Aesthetics?

Produced by NetEase, it guarantees an internet experience. YiXin's interface is relatively simple, sharing aesthetic designs with NetEase Cloud Music and NetEase News. Initially experiencing it, the functions are largely similar to WeChat: chatting, group chats, and a friend circle, none missing. It is learned that the next version will provide official accounts.

Meanwhile, China Telecom offers all YiXin communication functions for free: free data, free SMS, and free phone messages.

This combination has a touch of violent aesthetics.

- The use of YiXin does not distinguish between operators. Mobile users of China Telecom, China Unicom, and China Mobile can install and use it without restriction;

- Data fees are free. China Telecom users using YiXin will have all wireless data fees waived, including music, video, image services, and various official plugins called by YiXin. Short videos, song requests, and image sharing may replace text and become the main content of group chats and the friend circle;

- When sending free SMS and phone messages, there is no need to add as YiXin friends; simply select contacts from the address book;

- SMS fees are completely free. Chatting between YiXin users is free. If the recipient does not have YiXin installed, YiXin can still find and send via China Telecom's SMS gateway, with SMS fees waived. This applies to all mobile users of China Telecom, China Unicom, and China Mobile.

- Phone message fees are completely free. Use YiXin to send free voice messages to all mobile phones and landlines in your address book. If the recipient does not answer, they will receive a call reminder every hour and can reply by voice, equivalent to expanding WeChat's voice walkie-talkie function to any mobile or landline. Similarly, this applies to all mobile users of China Telecom, China Unicom, and China Mobile.

- All services reach the destination. As mentioned above, all SMS and phone messages sent by YiXin can reach any mobile or landline, regardless of whether you have installed this software.

- The information flow of the friend circle adopts the Facebook model, very suitable for building threads. There is no distinction between comments made by your friends and those made by non-friends. YiXin's next version will allow users to choose between the WeChat model and the Facebook model. The traveler searched through YiXin and did not find WeChat's "Shake Shake" convenience service. Perhaps considering that purely contact-based social networking, if increasing the social and family risks brought by "hookups," is unbeneficial.

Aiming to Return to Communication Itself

While WeChat is busy solving information overload, YiXin returns to communication itself. According to insiders within the telecom industry: we will integrate various services such as landlines, voice mail, and call centers into this platform as plugins. Ding Lei also expressed this view, "We must thank Tencent, they cultivated a huge, new user experience, which is speaking into a phone and transmitting recordings. Without their promotion, it would take a long time for this mode of communication to be cultivated. But once popularized, there is room for innovation and optimization." These statements can be understood as, communication will be the product tone of YiXin for the foreseeable future. China Telecom is using YiXin to manage all its communication services and offer them for free.

Of course, YiXin hasn't clearly stated that making calls to fixed lines or mobile phones will be free. Is it possible to go this far? This would absolutely be the "world-ending move" to destroy WeChat!
