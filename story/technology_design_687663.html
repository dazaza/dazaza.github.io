A 'Slow City' refers to creating a city form that slows down the pace of life. According to the regulations of the World Slow City Alliance, towns, villages, or communities that are awarded this title must have a population of less than 50,000, pursue a green lifestyle, oppose pollution and noise, support urban greening, support traditional handcrafted methods, and not set up fast-food areas or large supermarkets. Yaqi Town in Gaochun County, Nanjing, Jiangsu Province, was granted the 'Slow City' title by the World Slow City Organization in November. Yaqi focuses on ecology, building China's first ecological cultural village, skillfully combining beautiful tranquility with rural agriculture, and continuously maintaining its basic natural ecological characteristics. Now, the mobile top-advertisement registered by Mr. Chen for 'International Slow City' has officially gone online on the Roboo platform, focusing on promoting the ecological and cultural environment of Yaqi Town. The 3G website 'International Slow City' consists of several sections: navigation bar, Slow City introduction, news center, advertisement rental, and Slow City services. The navigation bar is arranged in two rows, clearly displaying the overall structure of the website, making it convenient for users to understand the website as a whole and find their interested sections. The Slow City introduction section explains the origin and definition of Slow Cities to users, enabling browsing users to have a clear understanding of Slow Cities, thereby viewing the introduction of Yaqi Town and better understanding its ecological environment and ecological culture. The news center provides news and information related to Slow Cities. The Slow City services section mainly introduces tourism, dining, and accommodation information about Yaqi Town. The advertisement space rental is used for enterprises or individuals collaborating with Mr. Chen to post their own advertisements, bringing Mr. Chen some income. These various sections effectively and comprehensively describe the detailed situation of 'International Slow City' Yaqi Town.
