Shao Qiutao believes that the massive and continuously growing Chinese economy will continue to alter our external environment, including society, consumption habits, politics, population structure, economic structure, etc. The entire society is undergoing drastic changes, and the pace of change is accelerating. In such a changing process, it can foster a group of rapidly growing industries that can lead and adapt to these changes. Based on entrepreneurial spirit and a complete incentive mechanism, there will always be a group of leading growth companies that stand out by being the first to use new technologies, invent new products, meet new demands, and discover new business models, thereby surpassing their peers. Moreover, fast-growing industries will break away from the low-level red ocean competition of traditional industries and achieve ultra-fast revenue growth and substantial profit returns in the blue ocean domain.

In the past six or seven years, Jiasih Fund has been dedicated to growth stock investment and has established a specialized growth investment team. Led by Shao Jian, known as the "growth stock excavator," currently managing two growth-style funds: Jiasih Growth and Jiasih Strategy, both of which have shown outstanding long-term performance. Among them, the Jiasih Growth Fund has become an excellent open-end fund with the second-highest cumulative return in the entire market; by the end of 2010, the Jiasih Strategy Fund ranked sixth, ninth, and thirteenth in the Morningstar Aggressive Allocation Fund rankings for one-year, two-year, and three-year periods respectively.

Looking back at the significant adjustment pressure that growth stocks have faced this year, most growth funds have performed unsatisfactorily. However, institutional investors with a long-term perspective have smelled opportunities brought by the decline. Shao Qiutao stated that in the long term, stable growth companies benefiting from expanding domestic demand and economic transformation will inevitably bring good long-term returns to holders.

We can expect that by carefully selecting leading growth enterprises in fast-growing industries across the entire market, with clear and feasible growth strategies, prominent core competitiveness, sound incentive mechanisms, and qualified corporate governance, we should be able to achieve substantial investment returns. This is the investment target we are looking for and the leadership we emphasize.

This process also determines that the path of growth will not be smooth sailing. If we have a long-term positive outlook on the Chinese economy, every adjustment brought about by external factors will be a good buying opportunity. In Shao Qiutao's view, the recent heavy adjustments in growth stocks are due to corrections for last year's excessive gains and high valuations, as well as a temporary deviation in market investment style. Looking forward, growth stock investments still hold great potential.

Shao Qiutao believes that leading growth enterprises possess five characteristics: First, mechanism leadership: having a well-functioning operating mechanism and a clearly defined and executable corporate development strategy; Second, team leadership: stable management realizing effective corporate governance, possessing leading team-building capabilities; Third, technology leadership: having unique technological advantages, mastering specific technologies or patents; Fourth, market leadership: the products and services provided by the enterprise are in a leading position in the market, forming a high brand awareness, with high customer loyalty; Fifth, business model leadership: maintaining leadership in business models, process management, and business innovation, making it difficult for competitors to imitate.
