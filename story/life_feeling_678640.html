There is a kind of love called "obstinate without repentance"

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-27 15:52:50

There's a kind of love called "obstinate to the death."

The story is straightforward, without elaborate language. I hope everyone will read it carefully.

Please don't turn a deaf ear to someone's heartfelt words!

Some things can only be understood through personal experience.

Some people, only when they leave, do you want to hold on to them.

Some loves, only when lost, do you think of cherishing.

Some things, some people, should we care too much about them?

There was a girl named Xue, she was a天真, lively, and open-minded girl, with an ivory-like complexion and a kind heart. There was a boy named Ping, he was a quiet, silent boy, very secluded in character and cold to others. Perhaps it was fate that brought them together at an ordinary high school, and thus the story began.

The boy had been away from his parents since he was young, coming alone to the city for schooling. He made many friends in the city, many, many, but few were trustworthy. So the boy was lonely, no one understood him, no one cared for him, he detested everything around him, hated those hypocritical people, liked to quietly hide in a corner by himself thinking about his troubles, got used to walking alone, got used to silence. The girl saw how quiet the boy was and wanted to help him, wanted him to change his character, to become a happy person.

The boy really liked the girl's liveliness, liked to see her smile, liked her in red clothes, liked her long hair, liked her bright eyes, liked the feeling of being with her. But all this love was hidden in his heart, he remained silent. One day, the girl said to the boy: "Your eyes are so beautiful." A faint smile appeared on the boy's face, even his smile was gentle. The clumsy boy could only reply to her: "Oh! Hmm, I know."

His words carried no emotion at all. In fact, the boy's heart was in turmoil. He didn't know how to face these feelings, like a child, for so many years, no one cared for him, no one loved him, no one walked with him, quietly hiding that lonely heart.

The girl told the boy about her dreams, she wanted to attend a good university, where her dreams lay. She said to the boy: "Let's strive for our dreams together." After hearing this, the boy fell silent, quieted down, his gaze became dim. He had never thought about going to university, had never had any dreams, those things didn't belong to him. He just wanted to live quietly, lead a simple life, not compete for those false fame and fortune, because he was tired, already weary.

Every day, the girl prepared breakfast for the boy, an egg, two breads, a bag of soy milk. She knew the boy was lazy, always entering the classroom after the bell rang. And the boy, he remembered all these things in his heart. At first, out of vanity, he wouldn't eat the breakfast she brought, saying he had already eaten, then enduring the rumbling of his stomach during class. The girl insisted he eat, her kindness moved him, her care, her love. For the first time, the boy felt what it was like to be loved, and thus slowly got used to it, got used to her goodness, her cute smiles, her innocent nature.

The girl infected the boy's heart. From then on, the boy entered the classroom on time every day, stopped skipping classes to go online, stopped playing games, stopped hanging out with those friends. Every day, he would send the girl home, she would tell him her stories, about her family, her friends, her hobbies, she liked strawberry-flavored Alpines. The boy listened attentively, remembering all of this in his heart, keeping all the love buried deep within. Being with the person he liked made him very happy, he felt the taste of happiness, and in his heart, he silently warned himself: I must make her happy.

The story developed like this, until one day, the peaceful life was broken, shattering the boy's heart. That evening, after self-study class was over, on the way home, the boy was surrounded by several people, blocking his way home. He knew he had gotten into trouble, this day had finally come, unable to escape the evil desires between people.

That group mocked him, insulted him, told him to let go. The boy remained silent, listening to their curses. That day, the boy endured immense pain, both physically and mentally. What kind of society? What kind of life? What kind of world?

That night, he thought for a long time, smoked a lot of cigarettes, pondered many things. In the end, he still decided that he couldn't continue like this, she would be harassed by others, bullied by others. He found the contact information of his old friends, returned to that past life, went back into that circle. Ultimately, he couldn't escape.

If there were no friends around, how could he protect her from getting hurt? Although he hated the life in the circle, this kind of life, what choices did he have?

From then on, the boy made many more friends, lived the same old life again. He had to make a name for himself, couldn't be bullied by others, had to protect the person he loved from being harmed by other men. The hypocrisy in the circle, the deceit among friends, some things you should say, some things you shouldn't say, some people you shouldn't provoke, even if you dislike them, you must pretend to like them, even if it's fake, it must seem real, even if you know you're being deceived, you must pretend you don't know. Power, money, how many innocents have they harmed?

But if you don't do this, how can you interact with others? Relying on your integrity, relying on your sincere heart, how much are they worth? Can they match up to fists? Can they prevent you from being bullied? During that period, it was very hard, very tiring, watching innocent people around you cruelly hurt, seeing their sorrowful faces, knowing you've done wrong, yet continuing to do wrong because the game has started, there's no way out.

The girl noticed the boy wasn't as happy as before, always quietly spacing out alone. When she asked him what was wrong, the boy said he didn't like studying, didn't want to go to school anymore. Saying this, he knew the distance between them grew wider, he couldn't disturb her studies, had to give her a good learning environment, couldn't let her be disturbed by others.

The boy fought for the girl, did things he disliked. Once, a friend insulted the girl, the boy angrily beat him severely. No one knew what he was thinking, no one knew why he cared so much about a woman?

In the circle, there's never any reason, only if you have power, have money, whatever you say goes, whatever you want to do, however you want to play, you can. The boy dealt with everyone who had intentions towards the girl. Although there were a few scars left on his body, compared to the pain in his heart, they meant nothing.

They weren't as happy walking together as before, the girl smiled less, the boy became even more silent. Each time they met, the boy would always give the girl a candy, and he didn't know what that candy represented?

He only knew the girl liked eating it, so there was always one in his pocket, whether he saw her or not, carrying that hope in his heart, burying this love deep inside. Two fools, neither knew what love was, only knew it was very hard, very tiring!

Time flowed slowly, like the tide, how could its unrestrained flow be held back?

The number of times the boy attended class decreased, the number of times he saw the girl decreased, because the college entrance exam was approaching, the boy's grades were poor, because he had never tried hard, looking at those unfamiliar characters, it troubled his mind even more. So, he simply stopped thinking about those things, living his own life. In the last month, the boy didn't attend class anymore, hiding in the internet cafe playing games, putting all his energy into the game, having no time to think about how the girl was doing?

Tired of studying? Can she achieve her dream? Sometimes, he would silently stand at the door of the classroom, standing where the girl couldn't see him, quietly watching the girl, she was doing homework, studying diligently, still striving. Then he would quietly walk away...

The boy's QQ was always online, he was waiting for the girl to put down her studies, log onto QQ to chat with him for a while. Every time he opened QQ, the boy would check if she was online first, then stare blankly at her dark avatar, look at her space, it hadn't been updated for a long time, the message board was filled with the boy's love. Work hard! The exam is coming soon! How have you been recently? ...

The college entrance exam ended quickly, the boy had already figured out what to do, how to face this relationship without an ending, only by letting go, giving up, could he do anything else now. On the day they parted, the boy said a lot of insincere words, said a lot of things that made the girl sad, he just wanted her to find her happiness quickly. The boy's helplessness, his powerlessness, his confusion, who could understand?

The boy naively thought this was all, escaping all memories about her, hiding in the internet cafe, finding a bit of peace there. Denying his cowardice in his heart, denying his incapability, did he lack the courage to possess this love? Or was he unworthy to take care of her? Or was he really incompetent? He didn't know, didn't want to know, just wanted to escape here quickly, leave this heart-breaking city, leave, leave quickly.

On the day the girl went to school, she asked a classmate to give the boy a letter, the almost calm life was stirred again. After seeing that letter, the boy's heart was in pain, originally the girl cared about him so much. All the tears that had been cold for many years were shed dry that day, originally his tears were so worthless, shedding so much.

The boy thought for a long time, in the end, he decided to repeat a year. He entered the review class with others' mockery, enduring loneliness and helplessness alone, the lifeless review class, everyone was working hard on their own tasks, no laughter, each person wrapped themselves tightly. The boy's grades were still as bad as before, no one helped him, due to his inferiority complex, he endured others' cold glances, reading books alone, memorizing those unforgettable formulas and theorems. Those lives, only those who experienced them could understand.

A terrible piece of news shattered the boy's heart completely, the girl had a boyfriend, a very excellent man. The boy's heart broke into pieces, losing hope, losing ambition, dragging his weary body aimlessly on this road with no path. All this, he had foreseen, such a beautiful girl, lively, pure, was the idol of all boys.

He had been deceiving himself all along, perhaps this was called reaping what you sow! He started smoking heavily, drinking heavily, playing with friends heavily. Making himself look wretched, already losing the courage to move forward, was this the end of his life? Never having anything, never losing anything. The boy had lost the ability to love others, silently enduring the pain.

This winter, the boy let his hair grow long, for no particular reason, just wanting it to retain a bit of warmth for him.

God always likes to toy with people's emotions, why must it be like this?

There's a kind of love called "obstinate to the death," how can one forget?

So, please cherish the ones you love now, don't wait until they're gone to regret. Men should control their personalities, care more about women. Some things, even if unsaid, everyone understands!

Thinking of the person you once loved, it hurts, deeply hurts, breaks your heart painfully! In the silent night, you shouldn't be missing them, because something is missing from your heart, and it can't be filled.

It's written in disorder, my heart is also in disorder! In such a world, should one pay with sincerity?
