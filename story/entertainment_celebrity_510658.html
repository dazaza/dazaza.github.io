The 50th anniversary commemoration of the first team championship win by the Chinese Table Tennis Team and the grand award ceremony celebrating 50 years of brilliance in Chinese table tennis was held in Zhongshan. Xu Yusheng, Li Fuguo, Zhuang Zedong, Qiao Hong, Wei Qingguang, Wang Nan, Wang Liqin, and several generations of distinguished players from the national team gathered together to witness the enduring success of Chinese table tennis. Zhuang Zedong and Xu Yusheng shook hands and encouraged each other, while happy mother Wang Nan recalled the past with tears welling up in her eyes. Wang Liqin and his girlfriend Liu Yanchi, who hosted the event, received blessings from everyone present. (Click on the image to enter Liu Yanchi's Weibo)

Xu Yusheng shakes hands with Zhuang Zedong

Fifty years ago, the Chinese Table Tennis Team won the men's team championship at the 26th World Table Tennis Championships held in Beijing. The champion team members included Rong Guotuan, Zhuang Zedong, Xu Yusheng, Li Fuguo, and Wang Chuanyao. Over these fifty years, the Chinese Table Tennis Team has won hundreds of championships in the three major world competitions, playing a positive role in promoting table tennis culture.

The athletes honored at this award ceremony are precisely the outstanding representatives who first brought glory to the Chinese team at the Olympics, the World Table Tennis Championships, and the Table Tennis World Cup. They include the first Olympic men's singles champion Liu Guoliang, the first women's singles champion Chen Jing, the first men's doubles champions Chen Longcan/Wei Qingguang, the first women's doubles champions Deng Yaping/Qiao Hong, the first men's team member Wang Liqin, Wang Hao, Ma Lin, and the first women's team members Wang Nan, Zhang Yining, Guo Yue.

In terms of the World Cup, Guo Yuehua, Deng Yaping, Qiao Hong, Wang Tao, Ma Wenge, Wang Hao, Xie Chaojie, Zhang Lei, Chen Zhe, Gao Jun, and others were recognized. In the World Table Tennis Championships, Rong Guotuan, Zhang Xuelin, Wang Zhiliang, Qiu Zhonghui, Lin Huiqing, Zheng Minzhi, Zhuang Zedong, Xu Yusheng, Li Fuguo, Wang Chuanyao, Liang Lizhen, Li Heming, and others became outstanding representatives.

Each champion who received an award was gifted a golden racket with the number "50" engraved on it. When Xu Yusheng and Zhuang Zedong shook hands and encouraged each other upon receiving the golden racket, they earned applause from the entire audience. Cai Zhenhua, deputy director of the General Administration of Sport, who could not attend due to work reasons, sent a congratulatory letter thanking the builders of Chinese table tennis for their achievements and looking forward to the next generation of Chinese table tennis creating even more brilliance.

Wang Nan makes stunning appearance in black dress

Since stepping into the venue in a black dress, Wang Nan has become the focus of the entire venue. After getting married and having children after the Beijing Olympics, she currently holds a position at the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. As a mother, Wang Nan appears more refined than during her athletic period, showcasing elegance and nobility with her jade necklace, large diamond ring, diamond watch, and Hermes handbag. As a representative of the champions invited to speak on stage, Wang Nan sincerely expressed: "I am very happy this time because I haven't seen everyone for a long time. After coming to Zhongshan, I met many old leaders and coaches, which made me truly very happy. Thank you to the organizers for giving me this opportunity to receive the same award as the predecessors on the same stage. My understanding of the predecessors was always through pictures and video materials. The good results maintained by the table tennis team over the years are the valuable legacy left by the older generation of athletes, and the spirit of teamwork is advocated. Although the achievements belong to individuals, there is more behind-the-scenes support from the coaching staff and workers. I hope our generation of table tennis players can carry forward the fine traditions of the Chinese table tennis team and let our national ball develop better." Wang Nan revealed that recalling her days as an athlete over the past few days with everyone has been deeply moving, and her voice choked up with tears welling up in her eyes as she spoke.

Wang Liqin shows affection with his girlfriend

Today's award ceremony was hosted by CCTV celebrity Zhang Bin and entertainment broadcaster Liu Yanchi, who happens to be Wang Liqin's girlfriend. This Sichuan girl is tall, beautiful, and articulate. After Zhang Xuelin received his award, he specially told Wang Liqin: "I carefully observed, and her nose looks very good." The praise from the elder made Wang Liqin smile throughout the ceremony. When it was Wang Liqin's turn to go on stage along with Wang Nan and Wei Qingguang, who especially came from Japan, Zhang Bin intentionally stepped aside after interviewing the other two, leaving Liu Yanchi no choice but to start a conversation with Wang Liqin under the applause of the entire audience. Wang Nan kept watching the awkward couple from the side. When Liu Yanchi suggested hugging herself, Wang Nan jokingly laughed: "Are you hugging the wrong person?" Encouraged by his girlfriend, Wang Liqin said that he had participated in the Sydney, Athens, and Beijing Olympics, but he still looked forward to the London Olympics. After the interview, Zhang Bin continued to create a lively atmosphere: "Generally, the interviewer and the interviewee should have a photo together." Wang Nan and Wei Qingguang quickly moved aside, and Wang Liqin and Liu Yanchi took a sweet photo together, receiving blessings from both on and off the stage, making this award ceremony seem like their wedding. Liu Yanchi also seized the opportunity to reserve Zhang Bin: "Next time when you come to officiate, you can only charge my friendship fee." Zhang Bin immediately replied: "She means her and Dali's wedding."
