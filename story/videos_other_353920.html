Appendix: MM Snatching Event Regarding Two Princes' Favorability Influence: Some say choosing Lei or Baloya in the MM Snatching Event will make them disappear, this is incorrect (Although generally indeed so), mainly because triggering this event too early causes Baloya's favorability to be low. I've tried frequently rejecting Lei before, choosing Baloya after Lei would also make both disappear. It should be set that after the prince appears, if favorability drops to a certain level they will disappear. So if you want to choose Lei but don't want Baloya to disappear, please trigger this event at least after the Black Street Snack Event. If you don't want Lei to disappear, do not frequently reject Lei before. Of course, the safest option is to directly reject this date (Actually rejecting Lei also increases favorability..) Also, this event's choice has no impact on the final ending, only affects the current prince's favorability.

Demonization Degree Chapter

1. Determination of Demonization Degree (Please note that most Human Realm endings require Demonization below 99, Demon Realm endings require Demonization above 150):

Demonization Degree 0: Teddy Bear: You can no longer hear my voice, right?

Demonization Degree Below 50: Jip: The young lady seems to have tendencies towards demonization.

Teddy Bear: Can you hear my voice? Call me whenever you're sad!

Demonization Degree Below 75: Jip: The young lady's demon power seems stronger than before.

Demonization Degree Below 99: Jip: The young lady's demon power has become very strong. She might have noticed it herself.

Teddy Bear: Can you hear my voice clearly now? Let's chat more!

Demonization Degree Below 125: Jip: The young lady's demon power has reached a dangerous stage in the human world.

Demonization Degree Above 150: Jip: Master, the young lady has almost become a full demon.

Teddy Bear: We will always be good friends~

2. Influence of Items on Demonization Degree

Holy Water: Demonization -5, Morality +5

Items sold at Demon World Store: Demonization +3

Fairy Essence: Demonization -3

Super Holy Water: Demonization -30 (This seems not very useful... Last time I drank a bunch and still became a demon bride... Drinking a few Holy Waters successfully turned into a human ending... Not sure if it's a version issue)

Clear Water of Demon World: Demonization +10

Forbidden Fruit: Demonization +15

Fairy Wheel: Holding decreases Demonization by 1 each month and halves Demonization speed when visiting Demon World (Does not stack with Dragon Dress effect)

Dragon Dress: Wearing halves Demonization speed when visiting Demon World (Does not stack with Fairy Wheel effect)

Holding items sold by the Mysterious Merchant (excluding Elf Ring and Inclined Dress) increases Demonization by 1 each month

3. Other Influences on Demonization

Church work reduces Demonization by 2-3 points per week

Visiting Demon World increases Demonization by 3 points per location visited (Except Dragon Valley and Mysterious Merchant House)
