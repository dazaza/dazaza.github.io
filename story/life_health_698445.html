Quanzhou Boshi Anus and Intestine Hospital is a large integrated TCM and Western medicine anus and intestine specialty hospital in the Minnan area, which integrates prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, scientific research and academic exchange. The hospital is located in Yiquan Building on Yiquan Street in Quanzhou, with elegant and simple environment and convenient transportation.

The hospital relies on the brand and technical advantages of the Minnan Anus and Intestine Disease Research Institute, gathers talents from all over the world, integrates Chinese and Western medicine, and establishes a systematic, scientific and safe diagnosis and treatment system in the field of Chinese anal and intestinal diagnosis and treatment. The hospital has established a long-term technical cooperation relationship with many well-known medical institutions, and owns a group of nationally renowned experts composed of leading figures and professors in the field of anal and intestinal diseases from the Minnan area, Southeast area and other places, with rich clinical experience and outstanding professional technology.

In terms of management, the hospital introduces international modern hospital management concepts for standardized and standardized management. The hospital strictly implements various health management rules and regulations at the national, provincial, municipal and district levels. Each functional management department continuously strengthens quality and safety management, attaches importance to the technical characteristics and rich experience of authoritative experts, effectively prevents the occurrence of medical disputes and accidents, and makes patients "treat with peace of mind and cure with comfort".

Authoritative experts are committed to building a nationally renowned department of proctology

As a key specialty of Quanzhou City, Boshi Anorectal Diagnosis and Treatment Center is equipped with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic equipment and technologies. It cooperates with the National Anorectal Society and the National Anorectal Treatment Center, and invites many top experts, medical consultants and well-known experts in the field of anorectal diseases for regular outpatient services. The hospital gathers the most authoritative people in the field of anorectal diseases from all over the country, and relies on the senior anorectal disease experts in Beijing and Shanghai to establish extensive academic exchanges with domestic and international hospitals and academic institutions, and is committed to building a nationally renowned department of proctology.
