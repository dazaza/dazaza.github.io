Xinhua launched a blog service today trial opening

by yinyin on 2007-03-01 17:39:23

Today at 2 PM, Xinhua Net "Xinhua blog" for the majority of Internet users trial operation. This is a new service launched by Xinhuanet to comply with the new trend of Internet development and enhance interaction with netizens. The opening of Xinhua blog will bring a new personalized platform for netizens to express their opinions, offer suggestions and publicize themselves.

Xinhua blog homepage

The new Xinhua blog has 16 main columns and 64 sub-columns. The main column includes current politics, finance, media, military, education, photography, city, sports, science and technology, culture, entertainment, life, emotions, cars, real estate, miscellaneous talk, etc. Xinhua blog supports Internet users to post text, pictures, audio, video and other forms of content, Internet users can choose their own blog according to their interests.

Xinhua blog provides personalized pages, and netizens can choose the plate, font and color of their blog home page according to their personal preferences. At present, the technical system provides 12 sets of templates, and users can also upload personalized pictures and change the style of the template. Xinhua blog has set up a search system in front and back office. Community total search can search all the articles of Xinhua blog by text, author, title, label, etc. On the homepage of personal blogs, bloggers and other Internet users can use personalized front and back search to search related articles.

The Xinhua blog and the "Development Forum" of Xinhuanet have access to each other and are connected with each other. Existing Development Forum users can automatically become blog users, and newly registered blog users can also become forum users at the same time.
