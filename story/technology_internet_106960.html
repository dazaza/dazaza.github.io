Internet Winter is Coming: Enterprises Have Tricks to Save Costs

by now on 2008-11-28 11:53:06

In the second half of this year, the financial crisis triggered by the subprime crisis has spread globally, bringing many adverse effects to most enterprises. Many companies have encountered difficulties in cash flow, and thus "increasing revenue and cutting expenditure" has become a survival tactic for numerous enterprises. Spending the least amount of money to purchase the most cost-effective products or to make the most effective promotion of their own brand is an ideal move for many enterprises under the current world economic environment.

Perhaps the worst times can also be the best times. The internet industry, similarly affected by the financial crisis, is undergoing a reshuffle, which purifies the industry, leaving space for development only for enterprises with strength and healthy management. Moreover, the onslaught of the financial tsunami has made enterprises that were previously focused on physical distribution more inclined towards making significant moves in e-commerce. The decline in all industries has made enterprises more pragmatic, paying more attention to the input-output ratio. Virtualized applications can save more resources for enterprises, thus receiving their favor. Internet insiders point out that for many small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in China, e-commerce is a breakthrough. Online marketing has low costs and wide coverage, making it the top choice for SMEs to cut marketing costs.

However, for enterprises to carry out e-commerce, they need to establish corporate websites, and the establishment of corporate websites cannot do without virtual hosting. Thus, many internet service providers have launched activities such as purchasing virtual hosting comes with free domain names and corporate email services, making all aspects of website building for SMEs complete and one-stop. A considerable number of virtual hosting users prefer to pursue the latest usage experience while placing greater emphasis on the security of the host. Various virtual hosting providers have responded to user demands by launching new technologies and advanced security measures. For instance, Now Network (www.now.cn) recently launched the 2009 Excellent Hosting Series, being the first to announce support for the Windows Server 2008 operating system, enhancing the stability of the host. It is reported that this series of hosts also has a series of innovations in terms of anti-intrusion and automation of management systems, with an intuitive operation interface allowing users to get started quickly and easily. To make it more convenient for users to conduct business, after purchasing this series of hosts, users can also receive five hundred corporate text messages as a gift.

Virtual hosting is an important means of reducing enterprise internet application costs and also a guarantee for the normal operation of enterprise e-commerce. In this "winter", enterprises might as well choose a suitable virtual host, manage e-commerce well, save expenses, and spend the winter more comfortably.
