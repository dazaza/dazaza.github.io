Duoduo Bird - Help you travel!

A pair of strong legs, a plump backpack, and a gaze looking into the distance - this is where the journey begins.

We set off without any specific or complicated reasons; traveling can be this simple.

Perhaps we are trying to escape something, perhaps we are searching for something, or maybe we just want to free our eyes and let them wander,

So we cross mountains and rivers, so we travel through the four seasons under the clouds.

People who travel are happy, unencumbered, and free.

Travelers are resilient, traversing thousands of mountains and rivers without tiring.

Travelers are open-minded, not chasing fame or profit, but simply responding to the call of their inner selves.

They are a group of modern hermits, children of nature,

Measuring the world with their footsteps, as well as their own breadth and depth.

In order to help more budget-conscious travelers with dreams of tourism but limited funds to fulfill their travel aspirations sooner, Duoduo Bird has established the "Poor Donkey Fund" to assist these travelers in setting out on their journeys!
