How to make employees "embrace change"? The most important strategy adopted by Jack Ma is to encourage talent mobility, and it is mandatory mobility. Ma once said in an internal meeting, "Alibaba's cadres should rotate, let salespeople come to the back office, see how the back office operates; let the back office people go to the front office, see how the front office operates." Alibaba's business managers also regularly transfer between cities across the country, allowing them to switch perspectives. This is one of the measures Alibaba takes to cultivate employees' ability to embrace change. In fact, almost every founding member and many old employees of Alibaba have experienced more than one job change.
