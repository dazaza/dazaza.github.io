Lee Chang-soo gives farewell blessing to Guoan and thanks the fans, frankly saying that making friends with Chinese fans is a wealth

by yinding999 on 2009-12-17 16:18:51

Lee Chang-soo bids farewell to Beijing Guoan and thanks the fans, candidly stating that befriending Chinese fans is a treasure.

Yesterday morning, Lee Chang-soo bid farewell to Beijing and returned to Korea. On this cold winter day, the most beloved iron coach of Beijing fans said goodbye to Chinese football with a lonely figure.

Report by reporter Zheng Xiaolong: Lee Chang-soo originally planned to leave Beijing last week, but due to his son handling school affairs, he had to delay his departure. This made the Iron Coach feel somewhat disheartened, as it coincided with Guoan's championship celebration on the 14th, so he chose to avoid it.

At 8:30 yesterday morning, Lee Chang-soo left the Guoan Hotel where he had lived for three years. Room 1803 had already been emptied. Mr. Lee had a lot of luggage, and Guoan sent a driver in a business car to take him to the airport. No one from the club came to see him off, and there were not many fans at the scene. However, the former Guoan goalkeeper coach Tomić appeared, seemingly sharing mutual respect.

At the airport, besides the media, what moved the Iron Coach the most was that some fans were holding up banners saying "We will always support you, Lee Chang-soo" and waiting patiently. Despite the cold weather, the Iron Coach insisted on taking photos and signing autographs with each fan, without minding that he was only wearing a gray suit. Facing familiar fans, he could even call out their nicknames in Chinese and warmly invited them, "If you have the chance to visit Korea, I will treat you."

Facing the interview, Mr. Lee said, "Now I am going back to Korea. I definitely can't leave football. Next, I will go to Europe to learn and observe, and also watch the World Cup. If everything goes smoothly, I will continue my coaching career in Korea." Would he return to China? Lee Chang-soo did not want to talk much about it, "I don't know, let's see the opportunity. If we have the fate, we will meet again."

Subsequently, the Iron Coach waved and walked towards the boarding gate, temporarily drawing a period on his relationship with Chinese football.

Final blessing for Guoan and gratitude to the fans

"Thank you fans, thank everyone!" On the morning of yesterday when he was about to leave China, what Lee Chang-soo said the most was gratitude, gratitude for the fans who cared about him.

Lee Chang-soo said, "The days in China are unforgettable, whether it's Chongqing, Qingdao or Beijing. For so long, I have regarded China as my second hometown. I am more famous in Beijing than in Seoul, which is thanks to the Beijing fans. I fully understand their affection for the club. When the team plays well, they cheer, and when they play poorly, they get angry. I understand that this is a sincere love for the team. My biggest gain in China is making so many friends among the fans, which is a treasure for my whole life."

Not leading Guoan to win the championship became a thorn in Lee Chang-soo's heart, "It's not true that there is no regret, but that's football. Being dismissed midway through my time in Beijing was the biggest setback in my coaching career, but I will never say anything bad about Guoan. Guoan is a pretty good club. I hope they continue to win championships whether in the CSL or the AFC Champions League. Wish Guoan good luck."

Team arrives later, missing each other

Just as Lee Chang-soo boarded the plane to Korea, Beijing Guoan also arrived at the airport, preparing to return to Kunming for continued winter training. However, due to timing issues, they and Lee Chang-soo missed each other.

According to the arrangement, Guoan returned to Hai Geng yesterday to continue physical training, preparing for the YOYO test organized by the Football Association in two weeks. Coach Hong Yuanshuo hopes the whole team can pass at once, then the team will return to Beijing on the 30th for a short rest. Guoan is currently actively contacting places for overseas training, and will then begin recruitment work and tactical drills.

The recruitment issue that Beijing fans are most concerned about has already taken shape. Ryan Griffiths (Little Ge) has basically determined to continue playing for Guoan next season, Matic will also return to China soon, Jinny from Chongqing Lifan, Otte from Zhejiang Greentown, and Ramires from Guangyao are all in contact with Guoan. The club will decide the candidates based on the specific situation of the coaching staff and negotiations.

Actually, Lee Chang-soo knew that the Guoan team would arrive at the airport shortly after, but he clearly avoided the question, "Now the relationship with the players is different. I am no longer the head coach, which makes it easier instead. We are still friends, we can still make phone calls in the future, so whether to send off or not is not important."

Championship also has Mr. Lee's contribution

After Lee Chang-soo left China, whether it's the Guoan bar on Baidu or other forums, a large number of fans posted messages expressing their longing for the Iron Coach.

From being dismissed from Guoan to leaving Beijing today, in these past three months, Lee Chang-soo stayed in China to accompany his son to complete his studies. During this time, his every move touched the hearts of Guoan fans. At the Guoan championship thank-you banquet held the night before last, fans at the scene were still shouting Lee Chang-soo's name. In recent days, many fans called hoping to bid farewell to Lee Chang-soo, but most were politely declined. It wasn't that Lee Chang-soo didn't want to, but recently he was too busy to handle everything, "I can only apologize to the fans. There are a lot of things to sort out, and a lot of miscellaneous tasks, there really isn't time. If there's an opportunity in the future, I will gather with everyone properly."

In many fan forums, many fans expressed that Guoan's championship win was thanks to Lee Chang-soo. A Beijing fan named "Guoan Hardcore" said, "Guoan fans will not forget Lee Chang-soo, will not forget the foundation he laid for the championship. Although there was some stubbornness and inadequacy in his use of people and tactics, this cannot erase Mr. Lee's contributions."
