Now it has to be said that the穿越电网project is one of the most classic projects in Beijing's outdoor expansion training. The project design is very imaginative, with no shortage of physical contact between people and the application of everyone's magical thinking, and even the use of leadership methods. It can be said that this project covers most of the training needs of expansion companies, which is why many expansion training company plans include this project. In the expansion training activities organized by Beijing's expansion companies, the穿越电网activity is also very popular. In everyone's view, the inherent danger coefficient of the activity is very low, suitable for most people to participate, and the participation time is very appropriate. It won't make the trainees feel bored because the project participation time is too long, and lose the original intention of outdoor training.

How exactly does the穿越电网project operate? In fact, it's quite simple. Here's a brief overview for you, expansion company. The穿越电网project is not constrained by the venue or is only slightly constrained. It can be carried out on playgrounds, in forests, or even in walkways. You just need to prepare two wooden stakes or poles in advance to tie both ends of the net. Of course, if there are no supporting items, two people holding it up temporarily will also work. After setting up the net, everyone stands on one side of the net and listens to the coach announce the rules. The general rules are as follows: within the specified time, everyone must pass through the net, and each person can only choose one hole in the net to use. During the crossing process, no one is allowed to touch the net. If anyone touches the net, that person must return and cross again, and the hole used the first time cannot be reused. One point to note is that no one is allowed to touch the net, including the team members currently crossing and those helping. If anyone touches the net, they must return and start over, and the hole where they failed cannot be used again.

However, whether you succeed or fail in this project, the project must end within the specified time. After completing the project, the coach will gather everyone together to talk about their feelings about the project and what they have learned from it. Everyone should speak, and the coach will comment on and evaluate each person's performance in the project to ultimately achieve the training purpose of this project. This project mainly requires everyone to understand their position in the team, find their place in the team, adjust their position, and do their own job well. There are no worst players in a team, only misplaced players. For example, in the穿越电网project, if you are very slim but insist on choosing a large hole to cross, although you may cross smoothly, it will make it difficult for tall people to cross other holes later, leading to a waste of resources in the work. The issue of leadership in the project is also very obvious. With the same crossing method, some teams perform excellently and quickly, while others have significantly lower efficiency, indicating that leadership in command also needs to be strengthened. The above is a brief overview of the穿越电网project in the expansion training.
