Sina Sports News - AC Milan achieved a 2-1 comeback victory over Roma, securing all three points. The first gain was in the points tally; the second gain was in confidence and spirit, as mentioned by Leonardo after the match. The third gain was Ronaldinho – with two consecutive outstanding performances and goals, both his confidence and form have improved.

Behind all this is no coincidence. In fact, as early as the national team game break, Corriere dello Sport had revealed that when players like Antonini went to Milanello for training, they found Ronaldinho already there practicing. Upon further inquiry, it was discovered that during this period, in order to quickly regain his form, Ronaldinho arrived at the training ground an hour earlier every afternoon to practice extra! Leonardo admitted after today's match: "I know it's easier with a central forward, but I made my decision based on form. Ronaldinho and Pato are in the best form."

Indeed, Ronaldinho may never return to his 2004 state. Even in this match, he lost possession many times in one-on-one situations and was dispossessed multiple times when attempting to dribble past opponents. But as Leonardo said: "You can already see some flashes of brilliance." For a player like Ronaldinho, a moment of inspiration could potentially help the team.

If scoring the penalty in such an important match was a test of mental toughness, then assisting Pato's goal to take the lead showcased Ronaldinho's exquisite skills as a former European Footballer of the Year. At the time, there were four Roma players around Ronaldinho. He held off one player, another came from behind to tackle him, and two more were nearby guarding him. With just one step, Ronaldinho fended off the tackling Roma player and turned smoothly while simultaneously dribbling the ball. Since Ronaldinho attracted several Roma players, there was only Risse near Pato in the penalty area. Ronaldinho then delivered a precise 50-meter through ball among the crowd. The ball’s power and accuracy were perfect, allowing Pato to control the ball with his chest, bypassing goalkeeper Doni, and score into an empty net. Ronaldinho's actions of breaking free, turning, and delivering the long pass were seamless. All Market described it vividly: "Ronaldinho's assist kissed Pato," who thanked afterward: "Thank you, Ronaldinho, your assist put me directly in front of the goal." In the last two matches, Ronaldinho scored twice and provided one assist.

Pato had another chance to be kissed by another Ronaldinho assist, but in the 82nd minute, although Ronaldinho's brilliant pass once again broke through the opponent's defense, Pato's attempt to accelerate was thwarted by Doni's timely intervention. Subsequently, due to Ambrosini's red card, Ronaldinho was substituted by Flamini. This time, Ronaldinho left the field smiling. Corriere dello Sport noted that at the moment of assisting Pato, Ronaldinho had regained memories of his old self.

Returning Ronaldinho to his Barcelona-era form might not be possible anymore, and excessive complaints about replacing Kaka with Ronaldinho will be unhelpful. Ronaldinho cannot replace Kaka, but he remains the best choice for AC Milan's attacking midfielder position. If he invests in training, such moments will become increasingly frequent. Additionally, the media has been overly harsh on Ronaldinho. It's true that being out partying until dawn deserves criticism, but attending a concert in France and playing drums as a guest should not have been criticized by L'Equipe. After all, that event was hosted by Flamini, and many AC Milan players attended. After that incident, Ronaldinho became angry, and his daily extra training and goals in the match were the best responses.
