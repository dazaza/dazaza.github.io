Long holiday of November 11, say "no" to the children who stay at home. The world of candy, colorful, orange, grape, apple, strawberry... full of sweet fruits on the ground, come and pick them~!

Canada: Strawberry Backpacking Tour

In Entertainment District, some charming Victorian urban villas with air-conditioned hotels have lush vegetation; there are only a dozen dormitory beds and a set of private rooms. Although the inn is often filled with European tourists, the chance of finding an empty bed here is still greater than elsewhere.

Backpackers Earth Village Hostel: The independently owned inn with color-changing hues was originally named Spadina Hotel. Celebrities such as Jack Nixon, the Rolling Stones, Leonardo Kuhn, and the Miserable Rap Group have all stayed here before. It is located in the best area and has its own bar, so it always exudes a festive atmosphere.

South Xinjiang: Orange Desert Self-Drive

Route: Korla - Weili County - Ruoqiang - Milan Ancient City - Hotan - Minfeng - Korla - Urumqi

If the car needs a "big supplement," don't hesitate; once you leave the city, car repair won't be this convenient anymore. Here, you can find almost all car brands, either at 4S stores or specialized repair shops.

Passing through Dabancheng, the largest wind power plant in Asia, windmills over 20 meters high are lined up along the way, creating a magnificent sight. If you want to take photos, do not stop on the emergency parking lane; continue driving forward, as there are designated parking lots for people to rest and take pictures, with restrooms available. Before getting out of the car to take photos, make sure to prepare adequately for the wind.

Additionally, you can try "beating person" food that's as hard as stones, roasted naan bread. When buying naan, note: if you're eating it on the same day, choose freshly baked naan that's softer and oilier, making it easier to eat without feeling thirsty. Of course, you can also buy some authentic dry naan to test the legendary "super" food that supposedly doesn't spoil for half a year.

Lijiang: Green Southwest Relics

If we're just going to Dali, taking Route 214 isn't worthwhile, but today's destination is Shaxi, southwest of Jianchuan County. Get off Route 214 at the Diannan intersection, and it's another 10 kilometers. The distance is close, but after crossing a small mountain, you'll arrive at a serene plateau like a utopia, where Shaxi is a small town scattered among it.

A quiet town with simple folk customs, old horse inns from the tea-horse road over a hundred years ago have been converted into guesthouses with comfortable mattresses. At noon, the market slowly disperses, and sitting in a donkey meat hotpot restaurant watching the various ethnicities and differently dressed pedestrians on the street will make you forget the passage of time and the deliciousness of the hotpot.

Shanxi: Bittersweet Youthful Memories

Driving route: Beijing - Datong - Hunyuan - Taiyuan - Pingyao - Linfen - Jixian

Cliff ancient architectural marvel - Hanging Temple, facing Mount Heng and backed by Cuiping Mountain, the buildings hang in the air with ingenious structure. At the foot of the Hanging Temple mountain lies Hunyuan County, a famous land of cool noodles. According to local residents, although there are thousands of cool noodles in Hunyuan, the most authentic ones are "Young Bride Cool Noodles" and "Couple Cool Noodles." Scoop up a spoonful, hmm, smooth and slippery, sour and spicy just right, really good!

Pair it with lotus beans and preserved eggs, mixed with bright red oil, and enjoy one or two bowls; it's truly a feast for the mouth.

Italy: Purple Romantic Passion

Those interested in history and art would know that there's Da Vinci's masterpiece "The Last Supper" here, and devout believers would know that the fourth largest church in the world is located here. Milan is like a kaleidoscope, showing countless faces and expressions.

No matter which country you come from, regardless of your cultural background, and irrespective of how different your behavior habits or ways of thinking may be, there must be a face of Milan that suits you perfectly. Like the face-changing masters in Sichuan Opera, no matter how they change, they are always heavily made-up, each having their own unique flavor.
