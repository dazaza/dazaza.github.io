Four people from Henan province, including Li and Wang who worked as a security guard in the bank where it happened, are under major suspicion.

by sujie5z60 on 2010-04-16 13:30:40

By reporter Xiao Cheng, correspondent Li Zijin: Every day, the boss's wife of a rice mill in Banfu Town would go to the Postal Savings Office to deposit money and remit funds. This regular routine was noticed by the head of the bank security team. The "inside man" security guard passed this information on to his fellow townspeople, resulting in the boss's wife being robbed at gunpoint outside the bank. A total of 340,000 yuan in cash was stolen.

This is the notorious "3?04" major armed robbery case from last year. Yesterday, the Zhongshan City Court announced that the case has been reviewed. The ringleader was sentenced to life imprisonment, while the inside man security captain received an eight-year sentence.

Two shots were fired outside the bank

At around 3 p.m. on March 4, 2009, Ms. Qiu, the boss's wife of Juxiang Rice Industry Co., Ltd. in Banfu Town, along with business manager Ms. Li, drove as usual to the Banfu Town Postal Savings Office to deposit a travel bag containing more than 340,000 yuan in cash. After parking the car, Ms. Li got out with the bag when misfortune struck.

Ms. Li recounted that as she reached the entrance, a man approached her and tried to snatch her handbag. The man claimed he had a gun and fired a shot into the air. Her grip loosened slightly and the man snatched the bag and fled. The boss's wife shouted and chased after him, whereupon the man fired another warning shot into the air before escaping on a motorcycle. It was understood that the stolen items included over 300,000 yuan in cash and one passbook.

Security guard leaks customer's whereabouts

After the incident, police investigations revealed that four Henan natives - Lü, Ku, Li, and Wang (who worked as a security guard at the bank) - were key suspects. Seven days later, the police arrested all four suspects in Henan and Shenzhen, recovering over 170,000 yuan in stolen money.

Wang confessed that he was the head of security at the Banfu Town Postal Savings Office. He observed that the boss's wife of the rice mill deposited at least 200,000 yuan every day. At the end of January 2009, Wang informed his fellow townspeople about this. They decided to rob the boss's wife and even specially acquired guns for the job. After the crime, Wang received 10,000 yuan and continued to stay in Banfu.

The ringleader sentenced to life imprisonment

In November last year, the Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court delivered the first-instance verdict for the five defendants involved in the case. Three of them appealed against the verdict, but the Guangdong Higher People's Court upheld the original judgment. The ringleader was found guilty of robbery and sentenced to life imprisonment, deprived of political rights for life, and had all personal property confiscated. The security captain was sentenced to eight years in prison and fined 10,000 yuan.

Judge Yang Ge of the Second Criminal Division of the Zhongshan Intermediate People's Court believed that this case was particularly malicious with distinctive features: the criminals committed an armed robbery outside a financial institution and fired warning shots to threaten the victim during the robbery, causing a severe negative impact on local social order. Despite no physical harm occurring in this case, the court still imposed a heavy sentence of life imprisonment on the ringleader according to the law.
