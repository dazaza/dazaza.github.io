by yangli on 2010-09-10 15:05:40

The unhappy Shanghai master who was sent to jail is surnamed Wu and named Kezhao, and he has always been the leader of our workshop study group. I remember that every morning when we go to work, we have to form a queue under his command and solemnly ask for instructions from the great leader in the morning. He loudly stomped his feet and waved his hands to lead us in shouting "Wish the great leader, the great teacher, the great commander, and the great helmsman Chairman Mao live forever! Live forever! Live forever!" And "Wish Vice Chairman Lin be healthy! Healthy! Always healthy!" Moreover, before he wishes, he creatively adds some jokes at any time, such as: "Chairman Mao ah Chairman Mao! Our hearts are singing for you! We have so many intimate words to tell you!" Then wish. Every day after work, he leads us to respectfully wish again by waving our hands and stomping our feet, and then he sincerely reports to the great man's head about how he made a revolution in his soul during the process of promoting production. But we never expected that one day, after he led us to respectfully wish, two PLA soldiers suddenly appeared behind him, kicked him down, and firmly tied him up like a big dumpling. At the same time, they read out his counter-revolutionary crime of using the convenience of having a Red Light brand radio at home to listen to enemy stations. They immediately took him to the single dormitory to search his "home", and that well-performing Red Light brand radio became evidence of his counter-revolutionary activities and was confiscated. After Wu Kesao was sentenced to five years in prison, he was immediately borrowed by a military management cadre to receive revolutionary education from eight model plays including The Red Lantern, and finally corrected his ways, playing a role in supporting the army.
