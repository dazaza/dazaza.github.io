The space that changes constantly

by zhaobin888 on 2012-02-09 14:37:08

Autumn has gone, winter has come, is spring still far away? With the blessings of the end of the year, we step into a future full of anticipation. There might be people who like things, and people who like summer, but every day there will be heat and cold. What can we do about it? Perhaps I would say that going with the flow is the best attitude. You may call me weak or incapable, but I just long for peace and wish to create beauty. Just like what Mr. Meng Fei wrote in his book, these years, struggling between pain and suffering, feeling melancholy between joy and sorrow ~ We grow up gradually as time passes ~ We are no longer the naive children we once were ~ We savor this boring yet interesting life, all the joys and sorrows have been forgotten in tears, lost in memories. We hold on to that dream, keep chasing it, growing, falling down and getting up again in betrayal and coercion, falling and rising again ~ Looking back on the days of these years, tears well up involuntarily, sighs escape involuntarily ~ How many events have I experienced in my life, I have grown up ~ Every time I sought help, every time my dignity was trampled upon, through several springs and autumns, there have been smiles, tears, and even more strength hidden behind disguises! I once said, I am not a god, I am just an ordinary person, I also have moments of fear, I am not impervious, my heart also aches and hurts, I no longer complain ~ But everything in the past has passed, I have no reason to complain, the betrayal of friendship, the hurt of love, they have all passed ~ I have learned to live in the present ~ because I love you, I am waiting for you. Let's work together for the beauty of the future, let the past dissipate like smoke, a good mindset determines a good life, life is a hard-to-understand topic, we don't need to live too complexly, just a simple attitude and a simple smile are enough. Life is not as complicated as people say. The upcoming Spring Festival marks the beginning of a new year. What kind of path will we walk? No matter what, it all depends on yourself, no one has the right to decide anything for you, no one is the judge of another's fate - Pinwenju. We are all striving for the jokes of our childhood, but for what? Has anyone thought about it? Or are we just living for the sake of living? Some people say that simple people achieve great things. It's not about their family background, nor is it about their extensive knowledge, but rather breaking down every big thing into small things, then doing each small thing perfectly, and finally persisting in this way, leading to success. Therefore, at this moment, I don't expect my parents to give me any kind of life, all I want is simplicity, a simple little happiness is enough, just that. In the future, we don't need luxury, we just need a shoulder when we're tired, a sentence saying "I'm here" is enough. In the future, we will together write every random note of life, we will together create every little surprise. On this big ship of life, we set sail together, with you, with me.
