Fang Ling apologizes for the 'skirt-stomping' incident, says she's willing to let Sun Feifei stomp on her without wearing a chest sticker.

by mlwang on 2011-12-15 17:40:25

Not long ago, Sun Feifei participated in the 2011 Fashion Man of the Year ceremony. Her dress was stepped on by guest host Fang Ling who walked on the red carpet at the same time, resulting in her dress falling off. Fortunately, she was saved from a full exposure thanks to the flesh-colored breast stickers. A few days later, Sun Feifei's agent angrily criticized Fang Ling for not apologizing all along. For this, Fang Ling apologized for the "stepped-on-dress incident" on Weibo yesterday, saying that she was willing to let Sun Feifei step on her without wearing breast stickers. When Sun Feifei attended a fashion event the other night, she appeared in a black one-shoulder long dress. The security around her was obviously upgraded, and her agent was always by her side, apparently prepared, fearing another exposure of the breast sticker incident. The agent also publicly criticized Fang Ling with great anger that night: "Feifei has always regarded her as a friend. We don't know if she did it intentionally or unintentionally, but it indeed caused a lot of harm to Feifei and her family. But so far, we haven't received any apology or explanation from her! I think she owes us an apology!" For this, Fang Ling apologized to Sun Feifei on Weibo at about 4 pm yesterday afternoon, and wrote a long article explaining why she didn't apologize for so long. To show sincerity, Fang Ling even said that she was willing to be stepped on by Sun Feifei once without wearing breast stickers. "First of all, I would like to say sorry to Feifei and everyone. It's not that I didn't respond or apologize, but some things recently have made me feel caught off guard. Please see the content in the picture. Once again, please let Feifei accept my sincere apology, and I can even let Feifei step on me once in a set of formal wear. I can even not wear breast stickers." In the long text Fang Ling published, she wrote that she had apologized to Sun Feifei through her agent and Weibo private messages right after the incident happened, and then flew to Yunnan for work. Fang Ling explained that she did not deliberately step on Sun Feifei's dress, "As a hostess, I should have had plenty of opportunities to step on female artists' dresses before, no need to wait until now." Fang Ling said that the reason she didn't apologize immediately was because she had been bombarded with crisis PR these days, so her reaction was a bit slow.
