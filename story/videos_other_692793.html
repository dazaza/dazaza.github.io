D1: Depart for Yangshuo - Gongcheng Peach Garden - West Street Bars (Includes: Lunch) Accommodation: Yangshuo Family Hotel In the morning, gather at the designated location and take a luxury air-conditioned tourist bus to Yangshuo. The journey is about 7 hours. The unique Karst landform integrates perfectly with the myriad scenes of the Lijiang River and its surrounding beautiful and enchanting pastoral scenery, forming the uniquely styled "Guilin landscape" known worldwide for its "green mountains, clear water, strange caves, and beautiful stones". It has earned the reputation of "the best landscape under heaven." On the way to Yangshuo, visit the Daling Mountain in Xiling Township of Gongcheng [Ten Thousand Mu Peach Garden] and stroll along the ten-mile peach blossom corridor (about 2.8 km in length). The mountain is a peach garden, and the peach garden is the mountain. Everywhere you look, there are blooming peach blossoms, dyeing the Daling Mountain red and displaying colorful peach blossoms in shades of pink, light red, and fiery red, all competing for beauty and presenting a dazzling array. Amid the spring scenery, experience the wonders of nature as you weave through the vast peach orchards. Spring comes to Gongcheng, where you can admire the floating peach blossoms and feel the deep sentiment of peach blossoms in March. (Free time for visiting inside the garden is approximately 90 minutes). Then proceed to Yangshuo and check into the hotel. Dinner is self-arranged at West Street and Yangshuo Square to taste local flavors (Dinner is not included, AA system applies). In the evening, you may watch the large-scale natural scenery performance directed by renowned director Zhang Yimou - Impressions of Liu Sanjie (Cost not included, 200 RMB/person), then visit Yangshuo Beer West Street, which has a history of three hundred years, listed in the "World Tourism Encyclopedia", filled with exotic charm. The ancient yet vibrant West Street is another particularly attractive scene due to the learning and exchange of languages and cultures between Chinese and foreigners, hence it is called "Foreigners' Street". D2: Yangshao - Lijiang Scenery - Ten Mile Gallery - Yan Mountain Park - Zhengyang Pedestrian Street (Includes: Breakfast, Lunch) Accommodation: Guilin Small Hotel After breakfast, visit the section of the Lijiang River known as the "Diamond Waterway" (Included), where many outdoor scenes of "Liu Sanjie" were filmed, referred to as the "Liu Sanjie Water Park". This scenic area is renowned for its "most flavorful river in China," allowing you to see the picturesque beauty of "Guilin's best landscape under heaven." Later, enjoy the view of the "Ten Mile Gallery," where many scenes from the movies "Liu Sanjie" and "Hero" were filmed. View from afar the Butterfly Spring, Worker-Peasant Bridge, Moon Mountain, and appreciate the charming riverside scenery of the Yulong River. You may also opt for an additional visit to Silver Cave praised by French geologists as a world-class karst wonder (Additional cost 90 RMB/person), taking about one hour. Silver Cave is named for its stalactites that are white as silver and brilliant like diamonds. A folk saying goes, "Visit Silver Cave, and you'll never lack money!" Alternatively, you can choose to visit Tianlai Butterfly Spring (Additional cost 80 RMB/person), taking about one and a half hours. The scenic area is famous for its mountains, waters, stones, caves, springs, waterfalls, and authentic Dong and Miao ethnic customs. You can interact closely with the descendants of the chief of the 101 Dong village and the offspring of the Miao king, gathering joyfully with the Dong brothers and Miao sisters, listening to the celestial sounds - the passionate Dong grand songs. In the afternoon, visit the Rising Sun Bamboo Charcoal Shop (About 45 minutes). Visit the national 4A-level scenic spot "Yan Mountain Park" (Included) (Approximately 70 minutes), which was the filming site for the movie "Little Monk of Shaolin Temple," the TV series "Xu Beihong," and the soon-to-be-released epic war film "Final Battle in Guilin." "The best Guilin scenery, seen in one park." Upon arrival in Guilin, visit the Tea Doctor to sample Guangxi famous tea (About 45 minutes). Check into the hotel afterward. For dinner, head to Guilin's Zhengyang Pedestrian Street for dinner (Dinner is AA system): Zhengyang Road is an ancient street with over a thousand years of history. It is a new tourism landscape in Guilin that combines commercial and tourist features. It has both national style and modern elegance, blending Chinese and Western elements seamlessly; it is praised as the "First Street in Western China" and also known as "South China's First Street." Guilin, Yangshuo Travel Tips Welcome to join the Guilin, Yangshuo Bus Tour. Before our team leaves Dongguan for Guilin or Yangshuo, please understand some basic information about Yangshuo. We hope everyone enjoys this trip, eats deliciously, shops happily, and spends this happy holiday cheerfully. Tour: Guilin, Yangshuo is a world-famous scenic holy land, known for its "green mountains, clear water, strange caves, beautiful stones." We specially arrange for you to visit attractions such as the Li River scenery and the source of "Guilin's best landscape under heaven" - Duxiu Peak and Wangcheng Scenic Area. This allows you to deeply appreciate the Guilin scenery. Li River: We recommend the most beautiful section of the Li River, called "Xingping Scenic Spot." Taking a bamboo raft cruise is the only way to truly experience the beauty of the Li River. The Li River has a feature called "the more you imagine, the more vivid it becomes." The beauty of the Li River's mountains and waters relies on your imagination. The most memorable thing is that you must prepare a 20-yuan banknote! Why? Because when we tour the Li River, we will take you to the real background of the 20-yuan banknote - "Yellow Cloth Reflection." Silver Cave: The magical white stalactites resemble silver, forming dozens of distinctive attractions one after another, dim and mysterious, winding and twisting, unpredictable. Being inside feels like being a guest in the Moon Palace, giving you a sense of floating like a fairy. "Silver Cave" (National Level 4A), why is it called "Silver Cave"? Originally, there was a myth circulating in the city that said there was a cave storing piles of silver. You must see it! Totem Ancient Path: The Totem Ancient Path mainly tells the cultural relics and living conditions of the Zhenpiyan people in Guilin over ten thousand years ago. In the misty peaks, a group of dark-skinned locals... Stepping into the Totem Ancient Path, decoding ancient mysteries. Let us experience extending our lives from another perspective! Folk Dance: In the sound of copper drums, wild dances unfold. Passing through stone tunnels, we reach a primitive matriarchal society, watching the Zhenpiyan people perform breathtaking stunts like climbing knife mountains and walking on fire seas. Peach Blossom Paradise: A place more beautiful than legend, a stopover on many friends' journeys. Experiencing Tao Yuanming's description of "mountains overlapping and streams intertwining, seemingly leading to nowhere, yet revealing a path through willows and flowers." Presenting a perfect painting of "ancient bridges, flowing water, fields, old villages, and water-bound ethnic villages integrated together." Yulong River Scenic Area: Known as "Small Li River" and "world's best mountains and waters." It is the filming site for segments of the movies "Liu Sanjie," "Hero," and "Ten Miles Ambush." The scenery along the banks is poetic and picturesque. Riding a bamboo raft to enjoy the infinite views on both sides, playing in the water, singing songs, throwing embroidered balls, watching cormorant fishing performances, etc., are leisurely and enjoyable! Impression Liu Sanjie: The world's largest and most spectacular natural scenery performance; covering two kilometers of the Li River waters, with 12 real background peaks; the world's most advanced lighting, sound, and fog engineering; globally talented director Zhang Yimou spent over five years meticulously creating; featuring 600 actors performing passionately on water, unforgettable once seen, regrettable if missed! Butterfly Spring: Named for its mountains, waters, stones, caves, suspended bridges, waterfalls, and authentic Dong and Miao ethnic customs. In the scenic area, you can closely interact with the chief of the famous 101 Dong village and the descendants of the Miao king. Listen to the celestial sounds from the depths of the high mountains! Shopping: Except for fruits, the best local specialties should be purchased in regular stores in the Guilin urban area (items in counties may not be as authentic). In Yangshuo, the must-buy fruit specialties are "Shatian Pomelo" (buying skills: buy large not small, heavy not light, pointed not round) and "Moon Persimmon" (the skin has a layer of silvery white persimmon honey frost, round shape; can be eaten raw or cooked). In Guilin, you should buy ginkgo nuts (commonly known as white fruit, consolidating lung energy, smoothing wrinkles, benefiting energy; best stewed with old duck), "Guilin Osmanthus Tea" (fragrant, smooth taste), Guilin fermented tofu, chili sauce (known as "Guilin's Three Treasures" along with three-flower wine, excellent dining condiments).
