Bering Strait – remote, cold, and untouched by man.

In 1962, the Italian luxury liner Antonia Graza vanished in this area of the ocean, leaving no trace behind. This majestic ship, comparable to a floating palace, featured a spacious ballroom capable of holding hundreds of guests who danced all night to live music, a large swimming pool for fun and relaxation, and top chefs directing their teams to create the finest Italian delicacies. The voyage on the open sea seemed like an undisturbed dream, free from external interference. Yet, in the blink of an eye, everything disappeared, leaving only icebergs and the sea.

The year is 2002. A plane flies over the waters off the coast of Alaska, and the pilot spots a ship floating on the water below. His curiosity piqued, he wants to uncover the mystery of this ship.

Sean Murphy is the captain of a salvage vessel, and Maureen Epps is the leader of the onboard rescue team. Together, they lead one of the most skilled teams in the salvage industry. Their job is to board abandoned ships, make them seaworthy again, and bring them back to shore to earn their employers' rewards. This time, their employer is Canadian Air Force pilot Jack Freeman, who discovered the mysterious large ship. That ship is none other than the Antonia Graza, which disappeared forty years ago.

According to maritime law, any ship found in international waters belongs to its discoverer, provided they have the luck and skill to find it and drag it back to shore. For the rescue team, this luxury liner represents a significant fortune. It's up to them to see if they can claim it. Sean sets out with his crew aboard the "Arctic Warrior," including his first mate Greer, technician Dodge, Mendel and Santos, along with Maureen, to board the Italian liner.

The ship is no longer in its original condition. Naturally, apart from the marks left by time, strange and terrifying events begin to unfold, more horrifying and deadly than anything they've ever experienced before. This ship was never truly abandoned. The crew's mission is no longer about acquiring wealth but uncovering the truth about what happened on this ship decades ago and unraveling all its secrets because the mysterious forces aboard are now threatening their very lives.
