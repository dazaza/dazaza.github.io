"Love Never Falls" lingers on the青春romance among three girls.

by jqrxass on 2009-09-26 11:39:37

"The Unfading Love" tells the story of several young people who grew up together in campus life. They reminisce about their youth, uphold their friendship and love, and pursue happiness and dreams.

The story is set against the backdrop of the romantic northern city of Dalian. The protagonist An Shuang is a kind and carefree doctor. He had a sad first love with the girl Sandy during high school. With this pain in his heart, he wavers between three kind and beautiful girls: one is Sandy, who hopes to rekindle their past romance; another is Han Liang, a postgraduate student who is straightforward, strong-willed, and passionate about love; the last one is Li Xiaolan, a good friend who has always silently cared for him.

Throughout An Shuang's romantic journey, there runs the main character's unwavering belief in goodness and beauty. In addition to the romance, the novel also interweaves many episodes that depict friendship.

Part One:

Among the five of us, I lived closer to Gaoqiang's house. After parting ways at an intersection, it was just the two of us when he suddenly told me confidentially that he actually knew what "that kind of thing" was, and that he had even had a spring dream last night. I was shocked because I am half a year older than him and haven't had one yet. I asked, "Really?" He confidently said, "Yes!" I asked who the woman in his dream was, and he blushed, saying it was our Chinese language teacher. Our Chinese teacher is a plump and gentle middle-aged woman who is usually very nice to Gaoqiang. I laughed, and Gaoqiang's face turned even redder.

Part Two:

I kept watching her walk into the dormitory building, but she didn't look back at me as I had hoped.

She really looks like Sandy, which made me both happy and a bit depressed, as if cursing me for being a stupid donkey, unable to break out of the circle. But what can I do? It's like some people prefer rice while others prefer steamed buns, but we can't call those who eat rice every day stupid donkeys.

Part Three:

"On what grounds did you let him take my picture? I've already apologized to you. How have I offended you again? Is it because you're still thinking about Sandy, so you resent me?"

"I just wanted to test how deep our feelings are. If they are deep, then you should act as if nothing happened, stay in the hospital and recover from your injuries, all expenses will be covered by me, and after recovery, you must not cause trouble for Gaoqiang; if they are not deep, I'll go find a brick now, let you hit me twice, we'll consider it even, medical expenses I will still cover, and similarly, you cannot bother Gaoqiang." This is probably the most awkward excuse in the world, and Zhang Xiaodong standing nearby couldn't help but laugh.

Part Four:

I know that Zhang Bin's teasing with Han Liang is partly for everyone's entertainment, and partly to achieve the purpose of sowing discord between Han Liang and me, ultimately aiming to separate us. Who knows why he wants to do this, but I won't fall for his tricks. I asked Han Liang, "If Zhang Bin jokes with you like this, I won't turn against him, will you be angry?" Her response surprised me greatly; she said, "Why should I turn against him? These are all irresistible forces, it's not your fault, nor his. If anyone is to blame, it can only be me, who am so charming." I said, "Han Xiaoliang, if you can think this way, it makes me want to marry you."

Part Five:

Suppose you fell in love with your friend's sister and told your friend, and your friend told you that for the sake of our long-lasting friendship, you shouldn't date his sister. But then, your friend's sister starts dating another of your friend's friends. What would you think? Amplify your thoughts and feelings tenfold, and that's exactly what Gaoqiang thinks and feels. Gaoqiang is an exceptionally sensitive child with deep thoughts.

An Qi smiled sweetly and asked me, "Brother, you won't break up the lovers, will you?" I lied and said I wouldn't. If my sister must find a boyfriend within my circle of friends, I would only agree for her to date Zhang Xiaodong. Compared to Zhang Bin, Zhang Xiaodong can almost be called a saint because he managed to keep his first love until the age of twenty-three.
