Confession Secrets to the Twelve Constellations

by vorx2473 on 2012-02-15 08:23:42

1. How to Confess to an Aries

Keywords: Straightforwardness, Success Index: ★★★★

Aries are straightforward people; if they like someone, they may confess directly. Therefore, if you want to confess to an Aries, remember not to be overly shy or act like a little wife. Being straightforward and clear will attract them more. However, if you choose to confess on Valentine's Day, using a sincere attitude and touching words will make the Aries look at you differently.

2. How to Confess to a Taurus

Keywords: Promise, Success Index: ★★★★☆

Tauruses are very patient people. Even if they have someone they fancy, they won't rush to confess but instead quietly protect the person they like. If you notice Taurus' silent protection, taking the initiative often yields good results. Confessing to a Taurus who appreciates promises on Valentine's Day, and making promises during your confession, will surely make the other party very moved.

3. How to Confess to a Gemini

Keywords: Unintentional, Success Index: ★★★★

For the ever-changing Geminis, asking them to confess is a troublesome matter, and they often lose the motivation to confess due to overthinking. But if you choose to take the initiative to confess to a Gemini on Valentine's Day, preparing many plans beforehand might not be as effective as saying it unintentionally without preparation. This approach aligns with Gemini’s unpredictable and hard-to-grasp nature, increasing the chances of success.

4. How to Confess to a Cancer

Keywords: Action-based confession, Success Index: ★★★★☆

In the eyes of Cancers, confessing is not just about saying a sentence; it also carries a sense of responsibility. Although Cancers usually hide their love, they still hope for someone to give them a sense of responsibility and security. On Valentine's Day, instead of just words, use actions to prove your sincerity, such as showing care in daily life and paying attention to small details. Then, on Valentine's Day, express your love fully, and it will definitely succeed.

5. How to Confess to a Leo

Keywords: Public setting, Success Index: ★★★★

Leos' love is grand and spectacular. For Leos who love passionately and openly, they also want the whole world to share their deep love. Although it may seem high-profile, only this way do Leos feel that their love is sufficiently expressed. On the beautiful night of Valentine's Day, declare your love to the Leo in public. They will definitely not refuse, and even if they wanted to, others wouldn't allow it.

6. How to Confess to a Virgo

Keywords: Normal mindset, Success Index: ★★★★

Virgos are also delicate and sensitive people. They don't like ostentatious things; what they want is a genuine feeling. In fact, loving a Virgo doesn't require overly complicated pursuit methods; maintaining a normal mindset is enough. Even if you're confessing on Valentine's Day, inviting them out for a meal or shopping doesn't need to be extravagant. The language before the confession should not be exaggerated, just like usual, because they love sincere people.
