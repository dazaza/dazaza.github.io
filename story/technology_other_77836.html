I. Company and Product Introduction

Guizhou Moutai Liquor Factory Group Beer Co., Ltd. is located in Huichuan District, Zunyi City, a famous historical and cultural city, and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of China Guizhou Moutai Liquor Factory Group. The company has world-class modern beer production lines produced by German Krones, Steinnik companies and Italian Sassib companies, realizing computerized management of the entire production process from raw material input, saccharification, fermentation, filtration, canning to packaging. At the same time, it has strong technical strength, with national-level brewing experts and national beer tasting committee members. In January 2003, it was the first in the industry to pass the integrated certification of ISO9000, ISO14000, OHSAS18000 at one time; and in December 2005, it passed the HACCP and QS system certification, realizing the systematization, proceduralization, and standardization of internal management.

The company's products include Moutai Beer, Moutai Beer King, Moutai 1098 Beer, Moutai Bifidus Factor Beer, and Plateau Beer. The company has successively won the titles of "National Excellent Enterprise in the Liquor Industry", "Trustworthy Unit for Consumers" at the provincial, municipal, and district levels, and "Contract-abiding and Credit-worthy Unit".

The "Moutai" series of beers are made from premium Australian malt and premium imported hop extract, high-quality glutinous rice, and deep mountain spring water with karst topography, unique formulas and brewing techniques, carefully crafted. The beer body is clear and transparent, with a golden color, pure and refreshing taste, harmonious and soft mouthfeel, prominent hop fragrance, white and delicate foam, persistent cup-hanging, and unique taste. It has successively won the titles of "China's High-quality New Brand Beer", "China's Famous Innovative Product in Beer", "Guizhou Province's Famous Brand Product". In December 2005, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse in Beijing, the low-alcohol high-concentration (bifidus factor) beer independently developed by the company passed the expert group appraisal of the China Food Industry Association. The appraisal showed that this product filled the blank of no low-alcohol high-concentration (bifidus factor) products in China's beer industry. The "Plateau" brand beer is a long-term favored old brand product by consumers, and has been awarded the honors of "National Food Industry Famous Brand Product" and "Guizhou Province's Famous Brand Product". The "Plateau" trademark has been rated as "Guizhou Province's Famous Trademark" several times.

Moutai Beer, the Moutai of beer —— During the development and reform process of Moutai Beer Company, it will continue to learn and apply advanced management concepts, advanced production processes and technologies from home and abroad, adhere to independent innovation, research and produce high-quality products, and offer a high-quality life to a wide range of consumers.
