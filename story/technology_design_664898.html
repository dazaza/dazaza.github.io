During the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, vigorously developing green economy and industries, and advocating energy-saving and low-carbon lifestyles became important development policies. The underfloor heating industry, as a key green industry promoted by the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, has evolved from simple models to intelligent, integrated, and comfortable systems over the past decade, making outstanding contributions to the development of China's green economy.

The seventh China International Underfloor Heating Industry Summit Forum "Mannarde Cup" was inaugurated on December 9-10, 2011. The event, co-hosted by the Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee of the China Construction Metal Structure Association, was held at the Jing Feng Hotel in Beijing. The summit included two parts: the seventh China International Underfloor Heating Industry Summit Forum (Beijing) and the third China International Wall-Hung Boiler Industry Summit Forum, sponsored exclusively by Mannarde Control Systems (Leqing) Co., Ltd. and Guangdong Vanward New Electric Appliance Co., Ltd., respectively.

Prominent guests attending the event included Vice President and Secretary General of the China Construction Metal Structure Association Liu Zhe, Director of the Office of the China Construction Metal Structure Association Zhao Zhibing, Chairman of the Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee of the China Construction Metal Structure Association Hua Mingjiu, Standing Deputy Chairman of the Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee of the China Construction Metal Structure Association Liu Hao, Vice President of the China Real Estate Research Association Tong Yuezhong, Director Jiang Wenbin of the Beijing Public Rental Housing Development Center, Deputy Director Liu Jingjiang of the Product Certification Department of the Residential Industrialization Promotion Center of the Ministry of Construction, Deputy Secretary-General Wang Zhanjie of the China Plastics Processing Industry Association, Director Wei Ruqi of the National Chemical Building Materials Testing Center, Director Wu Changshun of the National Wire and Cable Quality Supervision and Inspection Center, Director Zeng Yu of the National Infrared and Electric Heating Products Quality Supervision and Inspection Center, President Wang Fang of the Liaoning Decoration Association Ground Heating Branch, President Geng Liangang of the Hebei Ground Heating Industry Association, Director Fan Qingtang of the Hebei Electric Ground Heating Committee, Secretary Jin Jizong of the Beijing Architectural Engineering Material Association Ground Heating Branch, Deputy Director Gui Zhenghu of the Radiation Heating and Cooling Committee of the Hubei Building Energy Conservation Association, President Li Feng of the Jilin Ground Heating Association, Chairman Wang Qi of the China Gas Heating Professional Committee, and Secretary-General Shi Yunfei of the National Real Estate Chief Engineer Club. Nearly a thousand representatives from various sectors such as ground heating construction enterprises, ground heating product manufacturers, distributors, dealers, municipal construction departments, design institutes, supervisory units, and real estate developers across the country attended the conference.

Vice President and Secretary General of the China Construction Metal Structure Association Liu Zhe delivered an important speech at the opening ceremony of the seventh China International Underfloor Heating Industry Summit Forum "Mannarde Cup" on the morning of December 9. Standing Deputy Chairman Liu Hao of the China Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee presided over the meeting and extended a warm welcome to the guests, leaders, and participants. Liu Zhe congratulated on the successful convening of the conference and affirmed the contribution of the underfloor heating industry to the development of the national low-carbon economy during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period. General Manager Chen Linao of Mannarde Control Systems (Leqing) Co., Ltd. expressed his wishes for the success of the conference.

Chairman Wang Qi of the China Gas Heating Professional Committee delivered an important speech on the "Current Status and Prospects of the Chinese Gas Heating Furnace Industry", indicating that decentralized heating would be more widely promoted under the context of the national low-carbon economic development, and gas heating furnaces would experience healthier and longer-term growth. Subsequently, Vice President Tong Yuezhong of the China Real Estate Research Association gave an important speech on the future development trends of the real estate market, providing important reference experiences for the development of the Chinese underfloor heating industry and wall-hung boiler industry. The awarding ceremony for national evaluators of underfloor heating installers marked the temporary conclusion of the seventh China International Underfloor Heating Industry Summit Forum "Mannarde Cup".

In the afternoon of December 9, under the chairmanship of Standing Deputy Chairman Liu Hao of the China Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee, the conference reopened grandly. Two rounds of thematic reports and dialogue activities—"Dialogue between Affordable Housing and the Underfloor Heating Industry" and "How Underfloor Heating Enterprises Breakthrough under Severe Market Regulation: Review and Prospect of the Chinese Underfloor Heating Industry"—pushed the conference atmosphere to its climax. Underfloor heating material suppliers and construction enterprises reviewed and analyzed the industry development of 2010 from the perspective of the industry and conducted in-depth discussions and studies on the development trends and response measures of the industry in 2010.

Simultaneously with the grand opening of the conference, five thematic sessions—"Electric Underfloor Heating Sub-forum", "Heat Source System Sub-forum", "Marketing Management Sub-forum", "Design and Construction Sub-forum", and "Comfort Home Sub-forum"—were successively held. The clear meeting themes, cutting-edge technical information, authoritative expert interpretations, and lively interactive atmospheres were warmly welcomed and highly praised by the participating representatives.

During the same period, the third China International Wall-Hung Boiler Industry Summit Forum "Vanward Cup", the sixth China International Underfloor Heating Industry Trade Exhibition, the establishment ceremony of the "Chinese Underfloor Heating Financial Intelligence Salon and the 2011 Annual Review Activity of the Chinese Underfloor Heating Industry/Comfort Home Industry of the China Construction News", the standing work meeting of the Radiant Heating and Cooling Committee of the China Construction Metal Structure Association, the on-site PK finals of the fourth Underfloor Heating Construction Diary (Network) Competition "Anlette Cup", and the standing work meeting of the China Gas Heating Professional Committee were successively held.

Currently, despite the volatile international economic environment, China's overall economic situation remains favorable, bringing opportunities and challenges to practitioners in the underfloor heating and related industries. Against this backdrop, the grand convening of the seventh China International Underfloor Heating Industry Summit Forum "Mannarde Cup" undoubtedly pointed the way forward for the acceleration of the Chinese underfloor heating industry.

It is understood that this grand event received strong support and assistance from Anhui Anze Electric Co., Ltd., Hongyue Plastic Group Co., Ltd., Chengdu Anlette Cable Manufacturing Co., Ltd., SK Golden Wave Plastics (Yantai) Co., Ltd., Hangzhou Dayuan Artificial Environment Equipment Co., Ltd., Zhongshan Jiantai Industrial Co., Ltd., Guangzhou Disen Home Boiler Manufacturing Co., Ltd., Aikang Enterprise Group (Shanghai) Co., Ltd., Zhejiang Jiali Da Pipe Industry Co., Ltd., Taizhou Hengjie Copper Industry Co., Ltd., Jiangsu Qide Pipe Industry Co., Ltd., Rele (Beijing) Heating Cable Co., Ltd., Weifang Youjia Underfloor Heating Manifold Manufacturing Co., Ltd., Zhuhai Orient Jinmeng Trading Co., Ltd., Shanghai Xinhua Motor Factory Co., Ltd., Italian Orefeo Underfloor Heating, etc. The conference also received strong support and full coverage from media such as the China Construction News, China Underfloor Heating Network, China Real Estate News, Underfloor Heating Monthly, Wall-Hung Boiler Monthly, Underfloor Heating Monthly Network, Soufun.com, CCTV Network HVAC Window, etc.
