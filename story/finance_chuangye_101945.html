Test: How far has your entrepreneurial ability reached?

by huangxin27 on 2008-11-09 11:58:03

Your company is in an 8-story building. Which floor would you prefer to work on? (Please choose from the following options)

1. First floor

2. Second floor

3. Sixth floor

4. Eighth floor

Analysis:

First floor: You have a strong entrepreneurial spirit, starting from the basics and being down-to-earth. You are a practical entrepreneur but sometimes hesitate too much when encountering problems, which often leads to missing good development opportunities.

Second floor: You are realistic and possess the thought and ability to surpass others, making you an exceptionally capable entrepreneur. However, your excessive alertness may sometimes cause you to miss opportunities.

Sixth floor: You can seize opportunities and move forward with great market insight. You can listen to others' advice, making you a very rare and valuable entrepreneur.

Eighth floor: You are competitive, unwilling to lose, and have the confidence to dominate. You are full of confidence in your own entrepreneurship, but beware of setting goals based solely on events and prevent becoming overly competitive, which could lead to counterproductive results.
