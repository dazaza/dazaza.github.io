16-Year-Old Boy Kills Over Trivial Matters, Girlfriend Goes Into Hiding And Is Arrested

by nif2dfoe on 2012-02-08 11:42:25

Just because his partner's proposal was rebutted, Ge Li (a pseudonym) a 16-year-old teenager from Huangwu, Zhifu District got into an argument with the other party and a fight ensued. Armed with a knife, he stabbed one person to death and injured two others, before fleeing for half a month until he was captured by the police. On the morning of the 20th, the court in Zhifu District adjourned the trial of this case.

Because of where to go play

Two groups of teenagers fought

At 3 PM on May 29th near Yantai No.13 Middle School, two groups of sixteen or seventeen-year-old teenagers quarreled at a birthday gathering. One group proposed that the "birthday boy" go to the beach, while the other group disagreed.

Ge Li said that the one who suggested going to the beach was his good friend, and the rejection made his friend lose face. He wasn't familiar with the person whose birthday it was, nor did he know the people arguing with them; he was just there to have fun with his friends.

As the two groups continued to argue, the situation became more tense and they started fighting. Ge Li's group consisted of three males and three females, while the other group had seven or eight teenagers. Some were holding sticks, some were bare-handed, and Ge Li had a single-edged dagger. The situation was chaotic as Ge Li swung his knife around without knowing whom he had stabbed.

When both sides stopped fighting and calmed down, Ge Li's group threw the knives into the bushes and left the scene. Afterwards, when they heard that someone from the other side had died, Ge Li remained skeptical. It wasn't until May 31st that Ge Li confirmed through a friend that one person had indeed died, which made him panic.

After stabbing someone to death, he fled

And was caught half a month later

The forensic doctor determined that one young man had his axillary artery severed by Ge Li, causing hemorrhagic shock and death; another young man suffered minor injuries to his trunk and limbs, and slight facial injuries; the third young man had minor injuries to his trunk. Since only Ge Li had a knife during the fight, the police arrested Ge Li.

Upon hearing that he had committed murder, the 16-year-old Ge Li chose to flee. On May 31st, he found his girlfriend Chen Min (a pseudonym) and asked her for money to escape.

Chen Min, 17 years old and from Shaanxi Province, came to Yantai after graduating from junior high school to work. After the incident, the Zhifu police informed her about the case and urged her to report any information about Ge Li to the police immediately.

Knowing fully well that Ge Li had committed murder, Chen Min still gave him her newly issued salary and helped him hide in Rushan City, Weihai, until Ge Li was captured by the public security authorities on June 14th. Chen Min was arrested for suspected harboring of a criminal.

Parents' relationship was bad

Once divorced due to domestic violence

At 10:30 AM on December 20th, the court in Zhifu District opened the trial of this case. Ge Li, suspected of intentional injury, and Chen Min, suspected of harboring a criminal, sat in the defendant's seat. Ge Li's parents also attended the trial, and upon seeing their son being escorted to the courtroom, Ge Li's mother burst into tears. Ge Li sat down and buried his head between his legs, motionless.

Since this case involved minors, according to legal provisions, it should be tried in camera, so the journalist exited the courtroom before the trial began.

"The court has conducted multiple mediations before adjourning the trial, but Ge Li's father never showed up, only Ge Li's mother cooperated," said a court staff member. Ge Li's family lived in the countryside, and their financial conditions were poor. They borrowed money from all sides, and in the end, they could only compensate the victim's family with 100,000 yuan.

After the trial, Ge Li said that four days before the incident, he had an argument with his father and subsequently stayed at a friend's house. Most of these friends he met at KTVs and pool halls.

It was reported that Ge Li's parents divorced when he was in the first year of junior high school. Ge Li's mother said that his father often beat her, and their marital relationship had always been strained. However, three months later, due to concerns about no one taking care of the children, Ge Li's mother decided to remarry.
