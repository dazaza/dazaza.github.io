**Bid-based Promotion Q&A - Sogou Bidding Account Optimization Issues**

**Detailed Consultation on Account Issues for Shangdao VIP Students in Bid-based Promotion:**

**Account Diagnosis:**

**Issue #1:** The account only ran for one day and spent only 77 yuan. The client may have spent 77 yuan but saw no inquiries or orders, which made them afraid to continue spending. As a result, they paused the account.

**Issue #2:** Quality score is too low. Normally, the quality score of keywords in a Sogou account should be optimized to the highest level, which is 5 stars. A low quality score can lead to fewer clicks, higher cost-per-click (CPC) in Baidu bidding promotion, and also bring a lot of junk clicks. (Therefore, the success or failure of an account depends more than 80% on the optimization of the quality score, so this is very important.)

**Issue #3:** This customer made a very basic mistake, which was the fundamental reason for receiving no inquiries. What was the mistake? The visit URL and display URL were filled out incorrectly. What is the visit URL? The visit URL is the page that visitors land on after clicking, while the display URL is the website address shown below the search results.

Thus, when launching advertisements, it's essential to pay attention to these two details and not confuse the visit URL with the display URL. Otherwise, the ad you run will not match the displayed page, leading to irrelevant results.

**Important!! Solution:** How to Optimize Quality Score? Demonstration for this Account!

**Before Modification:**

- No significant changes

**After Modification:**

- Title made more stimulating to increase the click-through rate (CTR) of creative content.

- Two wildcard insertions added in the first line of the creative content.

- At least three creative ideas written for each unit instead of just one. In the original account, there was only one creative idea, which was a fatal error!

- Having only one creative idea was a crucial reason for the decrease in quality score.

**Guidance for Modifications:**

1. Make the title more stimulating to improve the click-through rate of the creative content.

2. Insert wildcards twice in the first line of the creative content.

3. Write at least three creative ideas for each unit instead of just one. In the original account, there was only one creative idea, which was a very serious mistake! Having only one creative idea was also an important factor leading to the decline in quality score.
