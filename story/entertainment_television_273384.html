Plot introduction of the Korean drama "The Secret of Coco Island":

"The Secret of Coco Island" tells the story of survival where ten supermarket clerks are stranded on a deserted island after encountering danger at sea while delivering supplies to an island on the west coast. Jung Il-woo appears in the series as character Yun-ho, while actress Park Haemi, who played his mother in "High Kick! You Are All Surrounded," portrays the sister of the supermarket's president.

Jung Il-woo expressed: "I feel very honored to make a guest appearance in this new work by director Kim Young-gi and writer Song Jae-jeong, with whom I previously collaborated on 'High Kick! You Are All Surrounded.'" He revealed that he will be riding a motorcycle in the first episode to draw audience attention.

What makes "The Secret of Coco Island" unique among sitcoms is its lack of a fixed filming location; it is only set outdoors. The production team and actors traveled between Seoul and Incheon, spending about five days each week at a retreat on Yeoui Island in Incheon for filming.
