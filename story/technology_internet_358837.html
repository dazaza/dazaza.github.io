French tourists can expect to study French in France. Whether for business or leisure travel, register yourself to study French abroad in one of France's most attractive cities. The language school in Nice, Alpha-B, is a well-known language school. It is located in the heart of the capital of the French Riviera, just a stone's throw from shops, beaches, and the old town. The language school offers 16 classrooms equipped with modern facilities, free internet access, and a WiFi zone as well as a library. To accommodate more students, the school has started Alpha B2 with 12 classrooms and a second facility of the same standard from July to September. It is located near the original language school building.

The Alpha-B language school provides flexible courses for students:

The Standard Course lasts from 2 to 48 weeks. This course offers lessons ranging from beginner to advanced levels, each lasting 45 minutes, with a total of 20 lessons per week. The minimum age requirement for this course is 16 years, and it accommodates up to 10 people.

The Intensive Course offers 30 lessons, each lasting 45 minutes. For more information, you can refer to or compare with the standard course.

Preparation for digital exams (levels A1, A2, B1, and B2). It offers a course lasting 4 weeks and ranges from intermediate to advanced levels. Lessons are provided for 30 sessions, each lasting 45 minutes. For more detailed information, please refer to or compare with the standard course.
