<p> Xinhua News Agency (Intern Reporter Yang Lin) - Three months ago, Lin Xiaobo from Chengjiang Town in Beibei District was repairing a window for Ms. Liu Hong. Yesterday morning, Liu Hong suddenly confronted Lin Xiaobo, accusing him of stealing a screw from her bathtub and demanding 100 yuan as compensation, or she would stab him to death. Yesterday, Liu Hong, with a knife, argued with Lin Xiaobo all the way until they ended up at the police station.</p>

<p> At 4 PM yesterday, when the reporter arrived at the Chengjiang Police Station, Lin Xiaobo and Liu Hong were still grappling. Lin Xiaobo was bare-chested, holding a torn vest in his hand, and blood was still flowing from one of his hands. Witnesses said that Lin Xiaobo's wounds were inflicted during the struggle with Liu Hong. Lin Xiaobo is 64 years old and was previously a teacher. After retirement, he often helped acquaintances repair furniture at home. Liu Hong is the daughter of one of his friends, and they occasionally kept in touch.</p>

<p> Three months ago, Liu Hong invited Lin Xiaobo to her house to repair the bathroom window. During the repair, an argument occurred between them. "Liu Hong insisted that I didn't fix it properly," Lin Xiaobo said. However, Liu Hong still paid 30 yuan for the repair fee at that time.</p>

<p> Yesterday morning, Liu Hong suddenly showed up at Lin Xiaobo's doorstep, claiming that three months ago, when Lin Xiaobo repaired the window for her, he took a screw from her bathtub and demanded 100 yuan in compensation. "She even pulled out a knife and said if I didn't compensate her, she would stab me to death." Lin Xiaobo said that he had never even touched Liu Hong's bathtub. Liu Hong, however, told the reporter that she hadn't used the bathtub for these three months, but when she used it yesterday, she found the screw missing and concluded that it must have been taken by Lin Xiaobo.</p>
