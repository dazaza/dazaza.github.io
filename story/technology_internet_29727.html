China's domain name market will reach 720 million, and the domain name investment economy is booming.

by now on 2007-09-13 09:46:41

According to the latest research report of iResearch Marketing, the scale of China's domain name market was 410 million yuan in 2006. It is predicted that in the following three years, China's domain name market will show a rapid growth trend with a growth rate of around 32%. In the future, the scale of China's domain name market will further expand and it is expected to reach 720 million yuan by 2008. The huge market has drawn the attention of the Internet industry and also led to the booming of domain name investment economy.

In fact, domain name investment is not a new investment project. As early as in the initial stage of the appearance of domain names, there were people actively investing in them. Since domain names are the address numbers on the network and have uniqueness like trademarks, generally following the principle of "first registered, first obtained", and without complicated procedures, they have become an investment project favored by investors. China's Internet started late but developed rapidly. In recent years, the domain name investment and trading market has been very popular in our country, and many professional speculators - "domain farmers" have emerged.

Investing in domain names focuses on their infinite potential value and is mostly for the purpose of trading and transfer. In fact, there are countless examples of high-priced domain name transactions, such as DVD.cn for 200,000 yuan; flower.mobi for 200,000 US dollars; B2B.CN and mayi.com for millions of yuan transfers; even more astonishingly, Business.com is worth over 300 million yuan. The hot domain name trading market has given rise to many domain name sellers and buyers, and at the same time, specialized domain name trading platforms have emerged.

A regular domain name trading platform operates in a standardized manner, provides professional services, and can offer supporting services such as domain name transfer and changing registrars, greatly facilitating domain name investors and buyers.
