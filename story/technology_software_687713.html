That night, I tossed and turned in bed unable to sleep. Looking at the clock, it was already past 1 AM. I got out of bed and turned on my computer, entering a chatroom on a certain website. After entering, I noticed there weren't many people around. A few were discussing what kind of women were the most detestable. I used a very common name -- "Country Girl."

With great enthusiasm, I greeted everyone: "Hello teachers! I'm new here. I don't know the rules, so please give me guidance!"

Someone called "Passionate Molecule" said: "Why leave your village if you're not going to stay there?"

Me: "My mom said I should come to the city to gain some experience and find a good husband when I go back."

Passionate Prince: "With you like this, you actually want to get married?"

Me: "My mom says I have a pretty face. Definitely, I'll be able to find a good husband. That's why I came to the city to gain experience. People say the internet has everything, so I just came to take a look. Teachers, please help me!"

Love Sick: "Who are your teachers? Why do you call everyone teacher so easily?"

Me: "In our place, anyone who can read is called teacher."

Heart Like Moon: "Silly sister, you made me laugh. Tell me, do you know who Lewinsky is?"

Me: "Is he a driver? Does he drive cars or tractors? In our village, we call truck drivers 'drivers.' I haven't heard of Lewinsky."

Many people exclaimed they were dizzy.

Me: "Aren't you online? Is your net unstable? Why are you all dizzy?"

Heart Like Moon: "I almost choked on my food. Silly sister, let me tell you, Lewinsky is the girl who had a scandal with the former American president."

Passionate Prince: "Ah Yue, why mention America to her? Does she even know where America is?"

Me: "I only know China. My teacher said, China is my motherland. Our country is vast and rich. What is America?"

Love Sick: "Silly girl, didn't you know that America is on the other side of the Earth?"

Heart Like Moon: "Silly sister, do you know the Earth is round?"

Me: "I know. My teacher said the Earth is really big. There was a fool who wanted to find a point to lift the Earth but couldn't find it."

Their net started to wobble again, and a large number of them fainted.

I asked Love Sick: "Teacher, I ask you. Don't Americans feel uncomfortable hanging upside down every day?"

Love Sick: "What upside down?"

Me: "The Earth is round, and you said they are under our feet. Aren't they upside down?"

Heart Like Moon: "Silly sister, you're too adorable. My stomach hurts from laughing."

Me: "Sister, my mom said not to drink unclean water, be careful of stomach pain. Did you drink it?"

Passionate Prince: "Do you have a mobile phone? I'll call you."

Me: "I do. I saw city girls wearing something around their necks that could talk. So I bought one too."

Love Sick: "Stop rambling. Quickly say your number. I'll call you."

Me: "No number, I forgot to ask the shopkeeper."

Passionate Prince: "Didn't they tell you how to use it when you bought it?"

Me: "They did. Press a number, and it sings a song. Very nice to listen to."

Heart Like Moon: "Where did you buy your mobile phone? How much did it cost?"

Me: "At the store. Two dollars and eighty cents. They asked for three dollars and fifty cents, but I gave them two dollars and eighty cents."

Their net began to wobble again, and another large group fainted.

Love Sick: "Silly girl, you type pretty fast. Who taught you?"

Me: "I didn't type. I spoke. A young brother typed for me."

Heart Like Moon: "Where is your home? How does your mom let you come out alone?"

Me: "My home is in the village. My mom let me come out to gain experience."

Passionate Prince: "Go back to the village. This is not a place for you. Be careful not to be kidnapped."

Me: "My mom said, when you see a man, call him grandpa. When you see a woman, call her grandma. Sweet words make things easier."

Love Sick: "Girl, you're not suitable to stay in the city. Listen to your elder brother and go home. The city will corrupt you."

Me: "Don't you city people like vegetables and fruits without pesticides? I didn't bring a pesticide bucket. How could I pollute?"

Heart Like Moon: "This child is too silly. Silly sister, don't stay in the internet cafe. Go home quickly."

Me: "I don't have a home. No place to live. Here, it costs only five yuan to surf the internet all night. Cheaper than staying in a hotel. So I came here. I also learned what the internet is. Teachers also told me that the President of the United States has a female driver. And it's a scandal."

Passionate Prince: "Haha. This girl is too interesting."

Me: "I have to go. It's almost dawn. I need to save money. Oh, I forgot to tell you, I've saved a lot of coins."

Heart Like Moon: "Why save coins? For dowry?"

Me: "To visit my brother in Beijing. My brother is a big celebrity. Everyone knows him."

Heart Like Moon: "Who is your brother?"

Me: "I mentioned my brother, but he doesn't know yet. I'm worried. Oh, his name is Luo Jing. From CCTV."

Passionate Prince: "Luo Jing is your brother?"

Me: "I consider him my brother, but he doesn't know yet. Once I save enough coins, I'll go tell him."

The net wobbled again. They fainted in large numbers.

Me: "Your net always wobbles. You faint as soon as I speak. I won't say anything anymore. I'm leaving. You guys keep fainting in the net."
