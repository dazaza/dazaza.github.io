Shijiazhuang Orient Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:48:20

The school adopts a work-study educational model, with first-class training facilities in the province, a simple, fast and accurate teaching model, full closed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fees in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "liberating thinking, being pragmatic and innovative, and seeking change through diligent thinking", continuously carrying out teaching and management reforms, firmly establishing the awareness of "market competition", taking the market as guidance, taking enterprise needs as standards, exploring new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience, and students have received widespread praise from employers after employment.

First, with enterprise standards as guidance, we have equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Sufficient and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, building a numerical control training workshop and a general vehicle training workshop equipped with more than 50 numerical control lathes, numerical control milling machines, ordinary lathes, and ordinary milling machines. The school has built communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body training rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

Second, with employment positions as guidance, we have created an enterprise-style teaching model.

Establishing a practice-oriented teaching model, during the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students take on roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work." They are evaluated through regular assessments and exams, and outstanding team leaders and skilled technicians are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative in learning.

Each day's "work" situation has quality monitoring indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. Based on the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, consisting of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively stimulates students' initiative in learning and competitive awareness, enabling them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned more quickly and better.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to the enterprise management model during their time at school, adapting to the needs of employment positions and initially reaching the enterprise's employment standards. The goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass the enterprise's employment standards.

Third, with enterprise demand as guidance, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military student management system and formulated a daily standard for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training.

Fully learning from the enterprise's employee management model, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises need not only skilled workers with solid technical skills but also focus on employees' overall qualities, such as teamwork spirit, obedience, execution ability, and competitive awareness. The cultivation and improvement of overall quality require efforts and collaboration from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs throughout the students' entire learning process. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school highlights two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," hiring retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, from dressing appropriately, behavior, etiquette, and tidying up personal belongings, students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, vocational quality courses are offered, divided into theoretical lectures and practical activities. These two aspects complement and enhance each other. The theoretical lectures mainly focus on practical approaches to transform students' thoughts, while practical activities use students' "personal experiences" to consolidate teaching results, making these vocational quality requirements become students' conscious behaviors.

For vocational quality practical training, our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends our students in batches to enterprise positions under the supervision of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before their actual internship and enter the formal enterprise employee state in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, with employment as guidance, we have properly completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are world-renowned top 500 mechanical manufacturing enterprises. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

Fifth, thoughtful后勤services, uniquely implementing the "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students of our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus based on the physical development needs of students, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adapting to intense studies, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by dining outside the school.

The school is equipped with complete service facilities such as student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms. The logistics department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, considerate, warm-hearted, and convenient.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years of study.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility, originating from society and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-thinking educational concepts, we aim to save costs for parents as much as possible. Students coming to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees, and the tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years will be covered through work-study integration and vocational quality training. The school has also specifically established the "Hongzhi Class," offering full or partial waivers of all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.
