"Iron-fisted producer exposes scam" Wang Li, Guangzhou Guangchen Business Trading Co., Ltd.: If you can't fix it, get out.

by anonymous on 2012-02-27 17:46:01

Recently, producer Wang Pujun was busy collecting relevant materials for the upcoming Chinese Wang Family Association conference. When he opened the Baidu webpage, a shocking and bloody headline immediately caught his eye - "Online reports claim Anhui 'official second generation' abused power, failed in love and disfigured a young woman." Immediately, this news caused a huge stir...

According to Xiao Song, assistant of producer Wang Lijun, Wang recently has been preparing for the premiere of the movie "The First Ship Crossing the River", the start of filming for the movie "Do You Want to Marry Me?" and the project initiation of the 30-episode TV series "Liaojil War Flames". The current top priority is to choose an absolutely beautiful outdoor shooting location, making the plot and images visually appealing and dazzling. Using harmonious colors, aesthetic pictures, simple narratives, slow rhythms, emotional music, and textured lenses, it tells a refined and restrained love story in the real world.

However, the report about "failed love leading to a young woman's disfigurement" left Wang deeply unsettled. That night, Wang Lijun urgently convened several members of the scriptwriting, directing, cinematography, lighting, and production teams for an emergency meeting, demanding immediate adjustments to the related plotlines of the movie "Do You Want to Marry Me?"

Why did producer Wang Lijun pay such close attention to this report? What does it have to do with the movie script?

After understanding from multiple sources, the answer was finally revealed: It turns out that the courtship plot in the reworked screenplay of the movie "Do You Want to Marry Me?" accidentally resembled the aforementioned incident. Unexpectedly, reality had indeed unfolded similarly. To make the story ending more educational for teenagers in their growth, Wang Lijun decided to focus on highlighting the reform and education of Xiao Yu, the son of the wealthiest family in the county, who wanted to disfigure the beautiful girl Wen Jiao after their romance failed. Xiao Yu is the local "playboy," and his narrow-minded rash actions and impure soul eventually drew widespread condemnation but also received punishment and purification. Under the warm concern of the Party and the serious education and encouragement from the detention center and prison staff, Xiao Yu realized his conscience, deeply reflected, and educated fellow inmates over the years they had spent together, guiding them to turn away from darkness towards light. Meanwhile, the beautiful girl Wen Jiao, who was disfigured by Xiao Yu, miraculously restored her former good looks... We will work together with you to improve the plot and refine the script, striving to touch China and move the world. While interpreting the era’s demand for "a great development and prosperity of culture in a harmonious China," we aim to educate young men and women across the country to interact rationally and achieve a wonderful future together!

Wang Lijun said that in recent years, many vulgar film and television products exuding "rebellious and ostentatious" vibes have flooded the market, severely poisoning the tender hearts of growing teenagers. Some people may become dissolute, frivolous, lethargic, and even addicted due to these influences—seemingly believing that without engaging in a few one-night stands, without dabbling in homosexuality, without some depression, and without abnormal romantic relationships, they wouldn't feel like they were truly in their youthful prime. However, all of these elements must be unconditionally curbed in scripts and films. It is essential to highlight the impact and infectiousness of the film, most importantly, its educational significance in keeping up with the times and promoting scientific development. Therefore, all departments including the scriptwriting team, production team, and directing team must strictly control quality at every level and take responsibility layer by layer. No one should be allowed to just jump in and mess around!
