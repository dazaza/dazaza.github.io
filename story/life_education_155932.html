Chinese universities with the highest-paid graduates - which one are you from?

by qxingsky on 2009-06-03 15:19:45

Jingjing Campus Network (www.13soso.cn) —— Chinese universities with the highest employment salaries

Renmin University of China

Renmin University has come out of its decline in recent years, and its excellent student body ensures high-quality students. Graduates from Renmin University are still one of the strongest competitive groups in the domestic university employment market. In the admission lists of many popular industries such as civil servants, law, finance and insurance, journalism, etc., a large number of students from Renmin University can be seen. Renmin University has an unparalleled advantage in mid-level alumni in the political field among domestic universities. Every year, Renmin University produces the most civil servants.

This is mainly due to Renmin University's continued strong position in the overall strength of social sciences among domestic universities, with the advantage of a large platform for social science training. Moreover, Renmin University students have a strong worldly spirit and are well-versed in the art of internship, job hunting, and interviews.

The strengths of Renmin University's law, sociology, journalism, economics, and other disciplines that are currently popular in employment still rank among the top in the country. However, due to the large number of students admitted by Renmin University, especially postgraduates, this somewhat dilutes its elite status.

Peking University

Peking University is undoubtedly a golden brand name in China, on par with Tsinghua University. However, Peking University, which ranks among the top in the country, does not seem to match its ranking in terms of employment status.

This is mainly because Peking University's academic structure is mainly based on liberal arts and sciences, with more graduates from relatively cold humanities and science majors, which have less connection with social demand, such as archaeology, geology, meteorology, mechanics, and the Marx and Lenin Institute, as well as many unpopular departments in the science faculty. These departments do not have good employment prospects, hence the frequent online exposure of news about Peking University students selling pork and sugar葫芦. However, apart from these, the employment of Guanghua School of Management, Economics Department, and International Relations Department is indeed enviable for the majority of schools.

The competitiveness of Peking University's science faculties also ranks among the top in the country, with the largest number of students going abroad to study at world-class universities each year.

Tongji University

Tongji University in Shanghai is a prestigious university with strong capabilities. Its three key professional settings are enough to ensure Tongji's place on the employment rankings. Automobile, architecture, and civil engineering are Tongji's three ace departments, with almost no rivals domestically except Tsinghua University in Beijing.

In the rapidly developing Chinese construction industry administration departments and large enterprises, Tongji graduates occupy important positions and exert significant influence. The recent boom in the automobile manufacturing industry and real estate market undoubtedly allows every graduating Tongji student to secure a highly satisfactory position.

Central University of Finance and Economics

The Central University of Finance and Economics is known as the "cradle of bank presidents." More than one-third of the management of major banks today graduated from CUFE. Located not far from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, the Central University of Finance and Economics holds a similar position within the industry as BUPT. Its graduates' influence in the financial industry is unmatched. Disciplines such as monetary banking and finance, which lead the pack among similar disciplines in national universities, lay a solid foundation for every graduate of CUFE, ensuring their long-term and stable development within the industry. In recent years, the heat in the financial sector has continued to rise, and the further development of China's economy cannot do without a sound financial system.

Therefore, there is reason to believe that in the coming years, CUFE's ranking on the employment list will continue to rise. Shanghai University of Finance and Economics also has a significant influence in the financial center of Shanghai but is only highly recognized in Shanghai and the Yangtze River Delta region, making it slightly inferior to the Central University of Finance and Economics in terms of ranking.

Beijing Foreign Studies University

Beijing Foreign Studies University also enjoys the reputation of being the "cradle of Chinese diplomats," with more than one-third of China's diplomats graduating from BFSU. This is because BFSU has inherent advantages, particularly in teaching first-line foreign languages such as English, French, and German, where it leads the nation.

As reform and opening-up deepen further, the demand for high-end foreign language talent will inevitably increase. Currently, the monthly income of simultaneous interpreters generally exceeds 30,000 yuan, and for better simultaneous interpreters, earning over a million yuan annually is not difficult. Therefore, if you're considering studying a foreign language-related major, Beijing Foreign Studies University is undoubtedly the top choice.
