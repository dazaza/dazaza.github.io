by jqrxass on 2009-10-11 18:23:59

An epic of the polar region based on the famous Arctic exploration events in history, a story more realistic than history itself, and a novel that will chill you to the bone.

They were part of the Franklin Expedition of 1845, the first team to use steam-powered ships in search of the legendary Northwest Passage, a mission that received unprecedented full support from the scientific community at the time. However, they unfortunately encountered two summers without ice melting in the Arctic Circle, trapped in a nightmarish environment constantly invaded by ice and darkness.

Minus 60℃ frost, two of the strongest exploration ships, the HMS Erebus and HMS Terror, two battle-hardened captains, a mysterious Eskimo girl who has lost her tongue, and a ghost-like creature that craves blood... This summer, embark on a polar odyssey that will chill you to the bone!
