1. **Formula Name Source**: Guang Ji Sheng Fa Gao, Formula Source: *Wai Tai Mi Yao* Volume 32

**Composition**:

600 ml of lotus herb juice, 600 g of bear fat, 200 g of pig tail ointment, 20 ml of raw hempseed oil, 600 g of mountain leek roots, and 600 g of roof moss (cut).

**Efficacy and Indications**: Promotes hair growth.

**Preparation and Usage**: Combine the above 7 ingredients in a copper pot for boiling until the ointment is formed. Remove the residue and store it. Before combing your hair, apply the ointment.

2. **Formula Name Source**: Zi Sheng Da Zhao Wan, Formula Source: *Ji Yan Liang Fang*

**Composition**:

30 g each of ginseng, fresh rehmannia, wolfberry, cuscuta seed, schisandra fruit, raspberry, tree peony seed, and baked turtle shell; 60 g each of deer antler glue, yam, psoralea fruit, Szechuan cow knee, duzhong bark, cornus fruit, and cistanche; one placenta, and 120 g of poria.

**Efficacy and Indications**: Blackens beard and hair.

**Preparation and Usage**: Grind all herbs into powder, mix with honey to form pills the size of a jujube seed. Take 12 grams in the morning, twice daily. Swallow with warm water or saltwater.

3. **Formula Name Source**: Wu Bai Yu Fen San, Formula Source: *De Sheng Tang*

**Composition**:

75 g each of calcined lime and chopped willow branches, 60 g of raw clam powder, 30 g of white lead, 15 g of soil powder, and 7.5 g of mercury sulfide.

**Efficacy and Indications**: Blackens hair.

**Preparation and Usage**: Grind all herbs into fine powder. Before bed, wash hair with starch water, then rinse with warm water or tea water. Apply the medicine to the scalp. Apply an appropriate amount to the mustache and hair, cover with lotus leaves, wrap with silk cloth, and ensure no ventilation. The next morning, wash with soapnut water, turning white as lacquer.

4. **Formula Name Source**: Di Huang Wine, Formula Source: *Sheng Hui Fang*

**Composition**:

500 g of fresh rehmannia (crushed), 3750 g of glutinous rice (steamed), and 2000 g of yeast (crushed).

**Efficacy and Indications**: Blackens hair.

**Preparation and Usage**: Mix the above 3 ingredients thoroughly in a basin, place in a non-leaking jar, seal tightly. Ferment for 21 days in spring and summer, 35 days in autumn and winter. After fermentation, extract 50 ml of green liquid, which is the essence, and drink first. Strain the remaining liquid through a cloth into a container, and drink at leisure. Continue brewing to maintain supply.

5. **Formula Name Source**: Gu Zi Hua San, Formula Source: *Ji Feng Pu Ji Fang*

**Composition**:

500 g of dark-colored balloon flower, 250 g each of angelica root and Szechuan pepper, 30 g of cinnamon, and 60 g of dried rehmannia.

**Efficacy and Indications**: Blackens hair.

**Preparation and Usage**: Grind all ingredients into fine powder. Take 15 g each time, mixed with porridge in the morning on an empty stomach, followed by food. Results appear after over a month of use.

6. **Formula Name Source**: Treatment for Women's Baldness, Formula Source: *Ancient Chinese Beauty and Hair Care Formulas*

**Composition**: Pumpkin vine juice (unlimited amount).

**Efficacy and Indications**: Promotes women’s hair growth.

**Preparation and Usage**: Cut pumpkin vines, collect their juice in a clean container, and apply the juice to bald spots. Hair grows immediately.

7. **Formula Name Source**: Women's Bald Temple Formula, Formula Source: *Simple and Effective Remedies*

**Composition**: 120 g of Szechuan pepper.

**Efficacy and Indications**: Promotes hair growth and blackens hair. Note: Particularly effective for women suffering from temple hair loss.

**Preparation and Usage**: Soak the above herbs in alcohol for half a month, keep indoors, and apply freely at any time.

8. **Formula Name Source**: Zhi Tao Gao, Formula Source: *Shou Shi Chuan Zhen*

**Composition**:

300 g of psoralea fruit (cleaned, soaked in yellow wine overnight, steamed, dried, and ground into powder), 600 g of walnut meat (soaked in warm water, crushed into paste), and 500 g of honey (white honey is better).

**Efficacy and Indications**: Turns gray hair black. Note: Avoid eating rape mustard greens and oilseed rape.

**Preparation and Usage**: Boil the honey in a pot for a few minutes, add the other two ingredients, stir evenly, and store in a ceramic jar. Take 200 ml before meals on an empty stomach, mixed with wine, or with hot water.
