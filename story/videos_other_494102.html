Fangfang, when young, was considered as the most beautiful woman in the county.

by ruexiaoyanv on 2011-09-23 17:26:02

In recent years, he even moved to sleep in the study. If Fangfang needs to use the computer in the study, he would stay in the living room and not enter the study. He doesn't seem to be having an affair either; every day after work, he goes home, either writes materials or plays computer games. He is almost never out.

When Fangfang was young, she was considered a beauty in the county. With her fair complexion and a pair of affectionate big eyes, due to training in swimming and long-distance running, she had a slim and athletic body. After joining the workforce, there were many people who came to propose marriage to her. Among them, the son of a family friend graduated from a prestigious university and worked for a provincial government agency. Tall and slim, wearing glasses, he spoke eloquently, and whether it was his appearance, family background, or job, he was truly a match made in heaven with Fangfang, a perfect couple. At that time, trial marriages or cohabitation before marriage were not popular, so after six months of dating, they got married. During their courtship, they held hands and kissed, reserving the most mysterious and precious experiences for after marriage. Unexpectedly, during their honeymoon right after their wedding, he only touched her twice! Now, it's once or twice a month. Although there is no instruction manual, she knows this should not be normal, but she doesn't know whom to talk to about it. Later, they had a child. After the child was born, the situation did not improve. Fangfang researched a lot of information and hinted that he should see a doctor, but he said: "How could I have a disease? If I were sick, how could we have a son!" Or: "Don't take this matter so seriously!"

2011, Divorce or Not?

Time flies, and the youthful maiden full of vitality is gone, replaced by a nearly forty-year-old mature woman, though Fangfang still looks beautiful and elegant. It's not that she hasn't thought about divorce, but wouldn't it affect the child psychologically if they divorced? Would it leave any deficiencies in his growth? What would others think of her if she divorced just because of this reason? Could she find another good partner?

At least it wouldn't be worse than now, right? According to the average data of Chinese couples having sexual intercourse sixty times a year, she still has a few good years ahead. Even if not, finding a partner who understands each other and shares household chores would be better than the current situation where they ignore each other completely.

Therefore, Fangfang shifted her focus to her work and quickly achieved some success in her unit, taking on multiple roles with heavy responsibilities; she also obtained many professional certificates. Her husband, lacking in this aspect, could at least show more care and concern towards her, but unexpectedly, he was a shirker, completely ignoring his son’s life and studies, and not doing any housework. When Fangfang couldn't handle everything and complained a bit, he would call his parents to help at home. In the eyes of outsiders, Fangfang was truly capable, balancing work and family perfectly; her husband worked in a great unit and was very busy, and the two seemed to respect each other like guests. The real pain, however, was something Fangfang found hard to express.
