A hairstyle is a hair style form created by applying corresponding technical processes to the natural hair above the hairline on people's heads. Hair formation beautifies one’s appearance, which is the main purpose for people needing hairdressing. People constantly beautify their hairstyles because they are not satisfied with the natural state of their own hair, wanting to create their own beautiful hairstyles according to the laws of beauty, making their appearance more perfect. IV. & nbsp; Regarding shampooing, it is not just about washing hair but also caring for it, meaning washing followed by conditioning, fundamentally treating the hair. Shampooing is the preliminary work before various hairdressing services such as cutting, perming, dyeing, and conditioning, not only serving as a preparatory step but also being an impression customers use to evaluate hairdressers. It is the best moment to demonstrate communication skills, and hair-washing service is a valuable sales auxiliary activity. Hair-washing includes two parts: washing hair and washing scalp, each having different methods and functions. Washing scalp ensures that the scalp and hair are not damaged.

Standard hair washing: The standard rinsing method involves using shampoo twice, rinsing twice, then applying conditioner, evenly coating it, and waiting for more than 30 seconds before rinsing.

Dry-washing materials required: brushes, combs, short-bristle brushes, long-bristle brushes, detergents, dry and wet towels.

Hair care: Correctly using shampoo for washing and conditioning hair can enhance hair volume, avoid issues like greasiness, split ends, dandruff, ensure no damage to hair quality, and allow normal hair growth.

VI. Bleaching —— The hydrogen peroxide contained in bleaching agents is a substance that can eliminate pigmentation. The oxygen in hydrogen peroxide softens the hair's outer layer, penetrates into the cortex, tightens the hair, and brings out the natural pigment molecules from the cortex to the surface. Adding bleach powder absorbs pigments, achieving the effect of reducing pigmentation, lightening the hair color. The pigmentation of hair changes during the bleaching process, depending on the amount of hair pigmentation, the concentration of the bleaching agent, and the duration of its stay on the hair.

Dyeing —— Dyeing agents penetrate into the hair cortex, depositing in the hair cortex to change the hair color and produce luster. When dye cream and hydrogen peroxide milk are mixed and applied to the hair, a chemical reaction occurs. Ammonia in the dye expands and makes the hair cuticle and scales swell. Then artificial pigments in the hydrogen peroxide dye enter the cortex, causing the natural pigmentation of the hair to fade while the artificial pigments increase in size and become difficult to seep out of the cortex, thus leaving artificial pigments on the hair and changing the natural color of the hair.

Perming —— The first liquid used in perming softens the hair. In terms of shape, the first liquid in perming gradually swells the hair, known as swelling after water penetration into the hair.

Handling when perming and dyeing are done simultaneously: Perming and dyeing are not mutually exclusive, but cannot be done simultaneously after a single wash. Experts suggest perming first, then dyeing 2-3 weeks later. Coloring is not subject to this restriction and can be done immediately after perming.

Handling new hair growth after perming: When the curled hair is ruined by newly grown straight hair roots appearing on the head, you can either trim if the original hair was relatively short, paying attention to removing the residual part of the perm, or perm again to maintain the beauty. However, perming for a third time is absolutely prohibited, as repeatedly perming fine or damaged hair will cause significant harm.

Generally, hair care agents are often used for daily care, but their role as auxiliaries to control wave size during perming is often overlooked. For example, if only a portion or segment needs slight curling, apply an appropriate amount of hair care agent before rolling the hair rods to control the size of the waves and maintain healthy hair.

Aftercare for permed hair: Avoid combing on the day of perming; do not wash hair within three days after perming; wash the roots first when washing; use wide-tooth combs for tangled or dry hair; avoid strong sunlight exposure.
