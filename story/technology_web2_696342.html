In stark contrast to the pure and bright large-scale colors of the previous season, designers for Spring/Summer 2012 have all moved towards another extreme: fresh, sweet, and pinkish fluorescent color palettes such as lake blue, peach pink, matcha green, coral pink, and tangerine red. The high brightness of these ice cream colors brings a refreshing and sweet breeze that invigorates the mind and body.

Even though it is still winter, spring collections have already been launched. Flipping through the Spring/Summer 2012 fashion weeks, you will be surprised to find that many big brands are going for a fresh look. The heavy flavors of Autumn/Winter 2011 have come to an end, and now in 2012, the soft and tender ice cream colors are becoming popular.

**LOUIS VUITTON 2012 Spring/Summer**

For Spring/Summer 2012, key colors include:

- **Peach Pink**: The fresh and sweet pink has captured the hearts of countless girls. This season, peach pink makes its return to the fashion stage, bringing with it a hint of shyness not found in the more vibrant pinks. LOUIS VUITTON and ALEXANDER MCQUEEN infuse their pink palettes with white, whether it's through delicate pleats or elegant lace cutouts, both enhancing the romantic atmosphere beautifully.

- **Coral Blue**: Blue, the color of the sky and sea, gives people a sense of comfort and relaxation. Coral blue reduces saturation while increasing brightness. BETSEY JOHNSON and DIANE VON FURSTENBERG incorporate coral blue into dresses and jumpsuits, where minimalist designs and precise tailoring enhance the clarity of the look.

- **Goose Yellow**: Francisco Costa, Creative Director of Calvin Klein Women's Collection, continues the brand's signature minimalism but excels in his choice of colors—beige, pale yellow, or silver. PPQ, on the other hand, highlights the playful and lively nature of women.

- **Violet**: The elegance and mystery of violet always inspire boundless imagination. This season, CALVIN KLEIN COLLECTION and MARIOS SCHWAB seem particularly fond of this light and refined color, using flowing chiffon and organza to create dreamlike effects.

- **Apple Green**: The crisp apple green seems to bring summer closer to us. LOUIS VUITTON’s design this season is filled with a fairy-tale quality, while JILL STUART focuses more on silhouette and cutting details, presenting a refreshingly poetic narrative.

- **Clashing Ice Cream Colors**: Clashing ice cream colors have less of the glaring fashion feel and more of a delicate refinement. The clash between apricot yellow and purple; the encounter between lemon yellow and aqua blue, both showcase the charm of ice cream colors. PREEN BY THORNTON BREGAZZI and 3.1 PHILLIP LIM highlight multiple colors to emphasize overall layering.

This season, fashion once again proves its unpredictability, moving from one extreme to another, showcasing a palette that is as varied and vibrant as the coming seasons themselves.
