Wellness: Sanjiang Forest Hot Spring offers a dual-nourishment product of "forest oxygen bar + hot springs." Surrounded by bird songs, floral fragrances, lush greenery, and flowing streams, you can breathe in the phytoncides and negative oxygen ions freely, purifying your body and mind. In the 70 baths of varying sizes and functions, you can comfortably absorb various mineral elements, feeling refreshed and relaxed. Ultimately, Sanjiang Forest Hot Spring allows you to enjoy nature and become one with it.
