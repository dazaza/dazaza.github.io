2. Definition of scratches: According to the different degrees of paint scratches, they can be divided into hairline scratches, shallow scratches, moderate scratches, and deep scratches. Hairline scratches are usually fine scratches caused by incorrect car washing, wiping, or minor friction, which generally cannot be felt by hand. Shallow scratches refer to those where the surface paint is damaged but the primer is not exposed. Moderate scratches refer to those where the surface paint is damaged and the primer is exposed. Deep scratches refer to those that expose the car's steel plate. Car scratch repair and quality requirements

Car beauty sealing or coating service and quality requirements

The main functions of sealing include anti-oxidation, acid and alkali resistance, long-lasting brightness, sealing, and scratch resistance, etc. After car sealing, it is like wearing "invisible glass car clothes", enhancing the car's ability to resist environmental damage. The magical and lasting gloss effect also fills car enthusiasts with confidence, always maintaining elegance, luxury, and brilliance.

1. Sealing process and quality requirements for new cars

Basic tools: Synthetic deer skin, toothbrush, towel

Products: Liquid glaze

Time requirement: 2~3 hours (1 person)

Operating steps:

Wash the car - Remove wax - Seal the glaze

First step: Wash the car

(Refer to "Car Washing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Second step: Remove wax

1. Clean the body and remove asphalt or wax layer and other dirt (use wax remover to remove wax);

2. Use professional car wash liquid to clean residual liquid and residual wax on the car;

Third step: Dry the water stains

Dry the body without water stains to prepare for sealing. (Refer to "Car Washing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Fourth step: First pass of sealing

Shake the product thoroughly and evenly, then apply it directly on the body. Work at room temperature, avoid direct sunlight, and the best time is after the body (hood) has cooled down. Use a clean soft cloth, quickly and forcefully "circle" until the mirror glaze disappears and a luster appears. (The greater the hand pressure, the stronger the stain removal force, the clearer the oxidation layer of the paint surface is removed, the more glossy the paint surface becomes, and the stronger the adhesion), this step can choose to use a sealing machine for sealing, the effect is the best.

Key points: Do not operate in the sun.

Quality standard: Lack of visual deep reflection and appear to have no thin film feeling, only extremely smooth feeling by touch.

Fourth step: Second pass of sealing

Repeat the first pass, dry for 10 to 20 minutes, then wipe it off, and the mirror glaze effect will immediately appear. Car beauty scratch repair and quality requirements

Quality standard: Visually have deep reflection and appear to have a thin film feeling, extremely smooth by touch.

Note:

1. The temperature, relative humidity, and rainy days during construction are quite particular. If the relative humidity is above 90%, do not construct, because the mirror glaze is difficult to dry and penetrate, resulting in poor effect;

2. Working at too high temperatures is also unsuitable. Make sure to lower the temperature of the entire car before construction; otherwise, it will solidify into "glaze" before penetrating.

2. Sealing or coating service process and quality requirements for old cars

Basic tools: Synthetic deer skin, toothbrush, towel

Products: Liquid glaze

Time requirement: 4~5 hours (1 person) need to be determined based on the situation

Operating steps:

First step: Wash the car (refer to "Car Washing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Second step: Polish (refer to "Polishing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Third step: Polish (refer to "Polishing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Fourth step: Remove wax (refer to "Waxing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Fifth step: Seal the glaze (refer to "New Car Glazing Service Process and Quality Requirements")

Key points to note: Pay attention to others and report problems found in time.

1. Implement the technical standards for paint surface polishing.

2. The paint surface should be clean and dry when sealing.

3. Sealing should be done in sections, ensuring that the mirror glaze is slightly dry but not completely dry on the paint surface before vibration polishing.

4. Do not perform vibration polishing after applying the glaze on the entire car paint surface.

5. After sealing, there should be an obvious hard film effect on the paint surface.

6. After the entire car is sealed, clean the glaze powder on the car surface and corners.
