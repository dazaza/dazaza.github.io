Thief broke into the ceiling of a jewelry store and stole 900,000 yuan worth of jewelry

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-04 14:36:32

Times reporter Ji Ya, text and photo, with correspondent Zhou Youjian and Cai Li for the chart. Around 10 p.m. the night before last, taking advantage of the large number of people coming and going, a small thief quietly sneaked into the Wumart supermarket on Moganshan Road, found a restroom to hide in, lay low for an entire night, and at around 6:30 a.m. yesterday morning, using the convenience of interconnected ceilings in the supermarket, he climbed into a jewelry store on the east side of the first floor and stole gold and jade worth about 9 million yuan.

After succeeding in his theft, this "thief on the beam" triggered the infrared alarm inside the store, and police officers from the Xiangfu Police Station along with officers from the public security prevention and control team arrived at the scene one minute after receiving the alarm.

The thief carrying the gold and jade was captured and taken back to the police station for investigation.

Lurking - January 6, 10:00 p.m.

Spent the whole night hiding in the supermarket ceiling

The location of the incident was a jewelry store on the east side of the entrance of the Wumart supermarket on Moganshan Road.

The male perpetrator's surname is Xu, born in 1984, from Longyou County, Zhejiang Province, about 1.7 meters tall, slim build, short hair.

According to Xu's confession, around 10 p.m. on January 6, taking advantage of the Wumart supermarket not yet being closed, he secretly hid in the supermarket restroom. After the supermarket closed its doors, all the people had left, he then took the opportunity to climb onto the urinal in the restroom, lifted the ceiling, and climbed up.

In the dark and quiet supermarket, he lay low for the entire night.

Stealing - January 7, 6:00 a.m.

Breaking into the jewelry store and stealing 9 million yuan worth of jewelry

Around 6:00 a.m. the next morning, Xu walked straight from the supermarket restroom ceiling to the ceiling above the jewelry store.

Then he lifted the ceiling; the ceiling was about 2 meters high from the jewelry store floor. He used a black rope, tied it securely, and slid down the rope into the jewelry counter.

He broke open the counter and stole 60 gold bracelets, 41 various types of emerald and jade items, etc., worth about 9 million yuan in gold and jade.

After succeeding in his theft, Xu placed these items into a black cloth backpack that he carried with him, measuring 40 centimeters long and 30 centimeters high.

The jewelry store was equipped with an infrared alarm system that would automatically sound if anyone moved inside the store.

Just as Xu was about to leave, he triggered the infrared alarm.

Emergency blockade - 6:50

Police, security guards, and citizens collectively catch the thief one minute after arriving at the scene

Xiangfu Police Station received the emergency call at 6:50 in the morning.

One minute later, the patrol car of the Gongshu District Mobile Public Security Prevention Team immediately arrived at the scene.

Team Leader Liu Baoxi of the Mobile Public Security Prevention Team saw the thief escaping with his flashlight -

A square-shaped ceiling panel in the jewelry store had been lifted, and Xu was just grabbing the hanging black rope to climb back up onto the ceiling, with half of his leg exposed...

Not good! The thief entered the supermarket.

Officer Liu quickly notified the on-duty security guards and employees of the supermarket to fully block all passages for a search and capture.

He himself immediately climbed onto the ceiling to pursue the suspect.

Climbing not far, above a shoe cabinet warehouse in the supermarket, he fell through the ceiling. The shoe cabinet door was open-style, making it easy to escape.

The broken ceiling had very sharp edges, and Officer Liu accidentally fell from the ceiling, injuring his leg.

Xu ran along the employee passage toward the south exit of the supermarket. This door could only be opened from the inside, providing favorable conditions for his escape.

He successfully escaped the supermarket and ran westward along Moganshan Road No. 841.

As Xu was about to escape...

Police, supermarket security, staff, and even passersby who noticed the chase joined forces in pursuit.

Xu didn't get far after escaping the supermarket—just a few hundred meters—and was caught and sent to the police station for investigation.

Confession upon Arrest - 10:30

"The first theft" Planned to steal gold with tools

At around 10:30 a.m. yesterday, I arrived at the police station.

Xu confessed without reservation to the theft.

Why did he go to steal from the jewelry store?

He said, "Because there's gold here." It was his first theft.

I saw that the man had tools like pliers and tape. Were the pliers used to cut the surveillance wires? What was the tape for?

"Fingerprints were left on the counter, and tape can be used to remove them," the man said.

Currently, the case is still under further investigation. Haircuts in Haikou now cost a minimum of 30 yuan due to rising labor costs.

Stolen Scene - 11:00

The stolen jewelry store has resumed normal business

The thief failed to break the glass door with a fire extinguisher

At around 11:00 a.m. yesterday, I arrived at the scene of the theft, where the jewelry store had already resumed normal operations.

The stolen counter was near the north entrance of the jewelry store. At the time, the staff had just repaired the lock on the counter. "The glass on the counter wasn't broken, but the lock was damaged," said Manager Zhu of the jewelry store. "He (the thief) broke the lock and took away the gold and jade displayed inside."

"The safety measures in the store were pretty good. Infrared alarm devices were installed throughout the store, and any movement would trigger the alarm," Manager Zhu indicated. "After receiving the alarm, the police immediately notified the store manager, and nearby employees rushed over."

When Manager Zhu arrived at the scene, he still saw a black rope hanging from the ceiling inside the store. Additionally, stools normally used to greet customers were knocked over, and the stolen counter was in disarray.

There were some marks on the glass door leading to the supermarket, which seemed to have been struck by hard objects. "It should be that he (the thief), when realizing someone had come, tried to break the door with a fire extinguisher to escape, but failed, so he climbed back up the ceiling and fled the original way," Manager Zhu said. "In my career, this is the first time I've encountered something like this."

● Police

At around 7:00 a.m. on January 7, the Gongshu police successfully cracked a major jewelry store theft case, arresting the suspect Xu on the spot and confiscating gold and jade worth about 9 million yuan.

At 6:50 a.m. on January 7, the Xiangfu Police Station received an instruction from the 110 command center that the alarm in a jewelry store on the first floor of the Wumart supermarket had sounded. Upon receiving the alarm, the on-duty police immediately rushed to the scene. Meanwhile, the Gongshu branch immediately dispatched mobile patrol and prevention forces to reinforce, working together with the police from the Xiangfu Police Station to conduct waiting patrols at each entrance and exit of the Wumart supermarket. During the patrol, the mobile patrol and prevention team members used flashlights to check the situation inside the store and discovered a foot of the suspect protruding from the ceiling opening in the jewelry store, thus organizing collective prevention and governance forces to surround the supermarket and block exits to prevent the suspect from escaping. In the process of fleeing, the suspect lost his way and fell into a shoe store counter within the supermarket via the ceiling channel, quickly trying to escape outward. Eventually, the suspect was captured under the joint encirclement of police, supermarket security, and passing citizens. After review, the suspect Xu (male, born in August 1984, from Longyou County) confessed to the crime of stealing goldware from a jewelry store on the east side of the first floor of the Wumart supermarket early on the morning of January 7.

According to Xu's confession: due to unemployment, he began planning to steal from a jewelry store several months ago.

At 9:00 p.m. on January 6, Xu carried screwdrivers, knives, and other criminal tools and hid in the ceiling of the supermarket toilet during its operating hours. By 6:00 a.m. on January 7, Xu crawled along the ceiling to the jewelry store to commit the theft. During the theft, he accidentally triggered the alarm of the jewelry store, subsequently climbing back onto the ceiling with the stolen goods and was caught on the spot. Upon investigation, Xu stole a total of 60 gold bracelets and 41 jade pieces, amounting to a total value of about 9 million yuan.
