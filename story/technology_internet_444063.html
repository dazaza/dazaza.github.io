Uploaded.to is a free online storage service from Germany with a high traffic volume, ranked 758 in the world by Alexa. You can upload files without registering as a member, with a single file limit of up to 250MB. To download files, you need to wait for 10 seconds before the download button appears. I tested it and found that the upload speed is good, with a dynamic Flash progress bar displayed during uploads. Moreover, there are no pop-up ads on the download page, which feels quite nice. It also supports downloading files from other servers directly into your account.

Uploaded.to is a relatively suitable free online storage service for sharing files (you can add passwords to your files). If you share your uploaded files with netizens and they download them, Uploaded.to will pay you a certain amount. The more downloads, the more you earn:

- €20.00 TDP* for worldwide traffic with files larger than 200MB

- €5.00 TDP* for worldwide traffic with files smaller than 200MB

- €3.00 for every new premium customer advertised

- €1.00 for every further order from your previously advertised premium customers

- Convert a 1-month premium term against 10,000 collected points

*TDP stands for Thousand Download Price, and compensation occurs for completed downloads (minimum file size 3MB).
