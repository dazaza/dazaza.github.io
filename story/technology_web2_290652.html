Huijin may increase holdings in the Big Three banks through dividends to ease capital raising pressure. After this year's sharp increase in credit assets, replenishing capital to ensure sustainable development has become an important issue for all banks next year. The key is what method to use and when to do it. At this time, the major shareholder - Central Huijin Company with a dividend reinvestment model, has become the most popular solution in the market. Therefore, we analyze that the possibility of Huijin using dividends to further supplement bank capital should be more than 50%. In fact, it is equivalent to Huijin increasing its holdings in listed banks again. This plan has less impact on the secondary market, is highly operable, and should be one of the preferred plans for listed banks. Let us make a bold assumption calculation. According to the prediction that Bank of China will achieve a net profit of 78.9 billion yuan in 2009, assuming a dividend payout ratio of 50%, Huijin holds 67.5% of Bank of China's shares, the dividend will reach 26.6 billion yuan. If this money is directly used to supplement capital, it will increase Bank of China's core and overall capital adequacy ratio by about 0.5 percentage points, alleviating part of the capital raising pressure.

Former CSRC Chairman Zhou Daosheng: Stock Index Futures will come out soon. PECC China Finance Committee Chairman and former CSRC Chairman Zhou Daosheng expressed on Thursday that China should focus on risk control and management, positively and steadily develop the domestic financial derivatives market, and roll out stock index futures as soon as possible. Zhou Daosheng stated at the "Fifth China (Shenzhen) International Futures Conference" on Thursday that because the domestic derivatives market is not developed, some large enterprises, especially central enterprises, have to rely more on the overseas financial derivatives market for hedging purposes; however, over-the-counter transactions with foreign investment banks have low transparency and poor liquidity, thus implying significant risks. He believes that a transparent market environment has significant advantages in reducing risks and can better prevent default risks. Therefore, China's financial derivatives market should particularly develop exchange-traded derivatives markets and start from futures products related to the real economy. Zhou Daosheng called for the accelerated introduction of the Futures Law and stated that "stock index futures will come out very soon." In the secondary market recently, individual stocks related to the futures concept have been performing well. Their trends align with today's relevant speeches by the management, and we can understand the meaning here. The market always shows its greatest power in such situations. Thus, before the introduction of the stock index, the market will experience a steady and substantial rise because the blue-chip camp is a firm alliance of multiple parties. Their stock accumulation is solely waiting for the arrival of valuation futures, thoroughly sealing the space for strong adjustments in the market. Therefore, in front of big policies, we firmly hold our beliefs. In such guiding thoughts and environments, we will heavily position ourselves to wait for the rapid rise of good individual stocks. Remember the words I've said countless times: believe me, don't easily sell the high-quality individual stocks in your hands. The market is just beginning, and fluctuations and washouts are common occurrences.

The Growth Enterprise Market (GEM) experienced another collective frenzy. Statistics show that since the opening of the GEM on October 30, until yesterday, among all the buying and selling seats announced by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange, institutional investors have only appeared less than ten times. The majority of seats were speculative trading departments. Among the more active trading departments, Guosen Shenzhen Tai Ran Jiu Road Trading Department, Haitong Securities (600837) Shangyu Bai Guan Town Trading Department, Guosen Securities Yiwu Chouzhou North Road Trading Department, Yingda Securities Shenzhen Yuan Ling San Street Trading Department, etc., appeared most frequently, with transaction amounts generally in the six-digit range. "I didn't expect that speculative funds would have such high interest in the GEM. Despite the current high calls for value return on the GEM, there is no sign yet of speculative funds withdrawing comprehensively. They will continue to be active in the future," a securities analyst told reporters. Additionally, he believed that the high P/E ratios of the GEM need to be viewed dialectically because the high P/E ratios currently seen are calculated based on 2008 performance. However, the first three quarters' performance growth of the 28 GEM companies was significant. Looking at this year's dynamic P/E ratio, it is not excessively high, and is similar to the P/E ratio of the SME board. This indicates that all global GEM markets enjoy excess returns in their early stages, lasting between 3 months to 2 years. In other words, the GEM will have excess returns at least in the first 3 months after its establishment. Before the compilation of the GEM index, there will be further opportunities for individual stock transformations. By the end of the year, during the high bonus distribution opportunities, they will enter a short-term acceleration paradise. While paying attention to the risks of GEM stocks, we must also notice their super opportunities in the market and the rotation speculation at the end of the year.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. Moreover, this commission is only valid on the day it is issued. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of four to three Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. When entrusting to buy or sell, the code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent. At the same time, there are certain regulations on the quantity of stocks bought and sold: the quantity of stocks commissioned for purchase must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks commissioned for sale does not have to be a whole multiple of 100. There are four ways to entrust: counter submission entrustment, telephone automatic entrustment, computer automatic entrustment, and remote terminal entrustment.
