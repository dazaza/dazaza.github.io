【Basic Introduction】

　　The Hao Fang gaming platform provides players with multiplayer online gaming services based on the Internet. It supports online battles for popular games such as CS, War3, SC, FIFA, etc. It also has a powerful community system and a complete peripheral service system. As the largest electronic sports alliance organization in China, Hao Fang also offers the most comprehensive competitive gaming news and online gaming information.

　　Hao Fang is a comprehensive online gaming service provider that serves global Internet users by offering battle gaming platforms, card and chess games, casual mini-games, MMORPGs, news websites, and other services. In September 2002, Hao Fang Online began formal operations of its self-developed "Hao Fang Gaming Platform" with full intellectual property rights. By July 2004, the Hao Fang Gaming Platform had become the world's largest interactive gaming platform with 35 million registered users and more than 400,000 concurrent online users. Card and chess games, casual mini-games, MMORPGs, news websites, virtual communities, AVATARS, and various other forms of online entertainment, along with the Hao Fang Gaming Platform, make up the largest comprehensive online gaming service portal in China.

　　The gaming platform provides multiplayer online gaming services to Internet users. It allows gamers on the Internet to easily play games via the Internet as if they were in the same local area network (LAN). Through network protocol conversion technology, the platform connects players from all over the world and provides real-time communication and interaction.

　　With the help of the Hao Fang Gaming Platform, any game that can be played through a LAN can now be played on the platform in a LAN-like manner without needing dedicated servers or remembering complex IP addresses. Players can conveniently enjoy gaming with others. Each room on the platform feels like being in the same Internet cafe where everyone can communicate freely through a LAN, playing and exchanging skills with other players, fully enjoying the endless fun brought by multiplayer gaming.
