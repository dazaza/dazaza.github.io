I've been lurking in the community for a long time, but today I couldn't resist posting. It's mainly because I just came back from beautiful Sanya, with lots of feelings and beautiful pictures. If you want to go, be sure to read carefully!

In 2010, I finally saw the real sea! Why do I say that? Because although I've seen the sea in Shanghai, it's not beautiful at all, no different from the Yangtze River or other large rivers. The seaside is full of ports or big trucks carrying sand, and the environment is terrible. Although I've also seen the sea in Shenzhen, it was only from afar at the top of the Window of the World, which doesn't count. So, I was extremely excited before going to Sanya this time!

After booking the round-trip tickets online along with the apartment and hotel, my companion and I set off! Let me tell you, if you're going to Sanya, the most cost-effective seasons should be March, April, and November. Why? Because as everyone knows, the Spring Festival is the peak season for Sanya. Right after the Spring Festival, March becomes the off-season. At this time, most cities are still winter weather, and the same goes for November. The National Day holiday is the second busiest season after the Spring Festival, so right after the busy season comes the small off-season in November. Generally, during these months, you can get airfares at 30% of the original price or even lower. But remember, if you can confirm your travel dates, be sure to book your tickets in advance! I once missed out on a discount by just one hour, which was really frustrating!

Alright, after sorting out the tickets, it's time for the hotel. There are many good hotels in Sanya, but they cost money. I'm not a rich woman, so I chose an apartment plus hotel. There are many family-style apartments available for reservation on Taobao, which are also very comfortable! They generally cost between 280-400 yuan per night, with single rooms and suites available. This time, I stayed two days in a family apartment and the last two days in a hotel. Also, here's a little tip: if you have enough courage and want to save money, you can also look for accommodation after arriving in Sanya. Ha ha, there's a small town next to the Sanya airport called Mango Village or Apple Village. Some of the local houses are quite clean, costing only 40 yuan per night, it's absolutely true!

Let's cut to the chase and directly show some pictures. When the temperature in Shanghai was only eight degrees and we were still wearing thick coats, we boarded the plane to Sanya, which would take three hours. It was the first time I had taken such a long flight, and it was really hard to endure. The outside light was too glaring, so I pulled down the window panel and rested for a while. After about two and a half hours, the announcement said we would land at Sanya Phoenix Airport in half an hour. Everyone on the plane became excited, and I immediately opened the window. Wow! Such a blue sky, I took a few pictures, and here they are. My photography skills aren't bad, right?

This is the blue sky I saw over Sanya from the plane. What a beautiful color, something I've never seen in Shanghai! The plane was about to land soon, and I managed to take another picture. At this point, the plane was already very close to the ground. In the distance, the deep blue is the sea, and the light blue is the sky. It was a very relaxing scene for the eyes, very comfortable.

As soon as we got off the plane, wow, it was so hot, 32 degrees Celsius. All the tourists on the plane had just come from the cold weather in Shanghai, dressed in heavy coats and down jackets. Everyone began to shed their clothes regardless of appearance, laughing and joking. Looking at the distant green mountains and coconut trees, we deeply inhaled the fresh air. What a beautiful coastal city!

Then we looked for Bus No. 8. Let me tell you, taking a taxi in the suburbs of Sanya does not use the meter, the driver says how much it is, and Sanya is a very small city. Actually, the public bus is enough, the key is knowing how to find it. After getting out of the airport, don't pay attention to the drivers trying to solicit customers, just walk straight ahead. The Sanya airport is very small, it takes only two minutes to walk out. Then you will see Bus No. 8. This bus can reach many places in Sanya City for only two yuan, haha, isn't that saving money? The key is that it's both economical and safe. It took us about half an hour from the airport to Lanhai Garden where I stayed, and the staff of the apartment met us at the station, hehe. The service was really good. After arriving at the room, the first thing we did was to quickly change into summer clothes. Moreover, it only took five minutes to walk to the beach from this apartment. Looking out from the balcony, we could see the first-line sea view, which felt very good! We could have breakfast here in the morning, hehe, it's very romantic. The following picture shows the view from the apartment balcony, a first-class sea view. This picture was taken with a compact camera, so the effect is not very good, hehe, but you can still see it! There's nothing blocking the view ahead!

We arrived at the apartment in the late afternoon. After changing clothes, we prepared to take a stroll along the beach. It's time for me to make an appearance!

How's that? Very cool, right? The weather is very pleasant. This picture was taken downstairs in the neighborhood. I was so excited when I got to the beach that I took off my shoes and walked barefoot on the sand. The sand was very fine and soft, it felt really comfortable underfoot. Many grandparents were playing with their grandchildren, and there were vendors selling shells, sun hats, and beach shoes, all at relatively reasonable prices. Two little girls were happily playing with the sand, and I captured them in a photo. I think I took it pretty well, hehe.

That's how the first day went by. That evening, we joined a local tour group and planned to visit Wuzhizhou Island. It's said that the seawater there is especially clear. Here's a little trick: although I didn't join a group tour, I traveled independently, but you can still arrange two days to join a local tour when you arrive, which is more cost-effective and convenient with pick-up and drop-off services.
