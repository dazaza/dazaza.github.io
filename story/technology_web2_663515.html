The director of the Yushu Welfare House wants to sell Tibetan Mastiffs to raise 2 million yuan to build the welfare house.

by chenxing308 on 2012-02-11 21:16:32

Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hot topic: Selling Tibetan Mastiffs, he is also very reluctant. Thanks to Bai Ridai Le's "picky" during the day, there is now that "most awesome welfare house". He gestures wildly on the construction site every day, scrutinizing everything from a bag of cement to a steel bar. When building the new welfare house, he will continue this spirit of "trivial comparison". "Yushu is in an earthquake zone, safety issues cannot be taken lightly at all. The design personnel told me that the new plan can withstand an 8-magnitude earthquake."

After this strong quake, although the welfare house stood firm, it was still identified as a dangerous building and had to be demolished and rebuilt. Bai Ridai Le encountered a new problem: the total budget for the house was 5.3 million yuan, but he was still short of 1.9 million yuan. After much thought, he decided to sell two mixed-breed Tibetan Mastiffs raised at home, each for 1 million yuan, just enough.

During these days Bai Ridai Le has been out, he often calls the children. The children call this 34-year-old man "brother", and he treats the children as his own brothers and sisters. From establishing the welfare house in 2005 until now, there have been many difficulties, but none of them could compare with the comfort brought by the children's smiles and clear eyes. Now the children are having classes in the temporary housing, waiting for their brother to return and tell them: "In October next year, we will move into our new home."

Total budget 6 million yuan, can accommodate 170 children, hardware surpasses 20 years

A completely different kind of "picky farmer"

The old welfare house could accommodate more than 100 children, later many children also wanted to live there, but there was no space. Bai Ridai Le could only secretly worry. This time when building the new welfare house, he expanded the scale to 150 people, with a maximum capacity of 170 children. The construction requires 5.3 million yuan, plus all the facilities inside, the total budget is about 6 million yuan. "I think if I can break down those facilities and keep the unit price not too high, someone should help."

The new plan surpasses at least 20 years

Bai Ridai Le wants to build for the children a much better welfare house than before. Compared with the previous buildings, the hardware exceeds at least 20 years. Bai Ridai Le said he wants the children to keep up with the times.

Bai Ridai Le chose to go to Hefei to sell the Tibetan Mastiffs because he had received help from Anhui's good-hearted people many times before, and he considered here his "lucky land".

The construction team competing with Bai Ridai Le is his old friend. Not everyone dares to cooperate with him. "I only gave 1.5 million yuan for the project cost, other construction teams do not understand me, they don't know if the later funds will be in place, so they dare not cooperate with me. This construction team we have worked together before, they trust my character, and I trust their construction technology." The steel and cement were specially ordered by Bai Ridai Le through friends. "These two kinds of building materials directly affect the quality of the project, I dare not take it lightly. Moreover, these funds are donations from all over the country, I must take responsibility."

On December 2nd, Bai Ridai Le set off with his two Tibetan Mastiffs, first arriving in Qinghai Dongning, waiting for Hefei to arrange accommodation for them and a place to settle the dogs. During this period, to save expenses, Bai Ridai Le and the dogs stayed in a small hotel without heating. A few days later, they got the information that they could set off, but then the plane flight had problems. "I originally wanted to fly directly from Xining to Hefei, but the flights on this route cannot provide cargo holds for the dogs, so we had to fly to Hangzhou, then took a 14-hour car ride to arrive in Zhejiang. The dogs were severely carsick, they stopped eating, and only started eating again at noon the next day." Bai Ridai Le said affectionately.

These two little Tibetan Mastiffs were born in February of that year, Bai Ridai Le clearly remembers their birthday is February 11th. "We Tibetans treat Tibetan Mastiffs like family members. These two little Tibetan Mastiffs were born in the welfare house, growing up with the children. The children were also very reluctant when they knew I was going to sell them, but they understood that I must have my difficulties, so no child opposed it." Bai Ridai Le said.

After the Yushu earthquake in 2010, the city was in ruins, but in Jiegu Town of Yushu Prefecture, a three-story Tibetan-style building stood intact. That was the Huade Children's Welfare House in Yushu Prefecture, the safest home for 105 children, and its director Bai Ridai Le was thus called the "most awesome welfare house director".

Now, the conditions of the two Tibetan Mastiffs have recovered quite well, they are being temporarily kept in a private mastiff garden, waiting for buyers. "I am still short of 2 million yuan, so each is priced at 1 million yuan, this price is not expensive for purebred Yushu Tibetan Mastiffs." Bai Ridai Le said. Now some people come to see and inquire about the price, but no one has expressed a clear intention to buy yet. He may also take these two Tibetan Mastiffs to other cities to look around. "I promised the children that once I sell the Tibetan Mastiffs and get the money, I will go back and celebrate the New Year with them."

The design of the new welfare house was completed free of charge by the China Planning and Design Institute. "Our leader of Yushu Prefecture specially invited the leaders of the China Planning and Design Institute to visit the welfare house, later they visited the children several times, and they had deep feelings for the children, so they were very careful when designing." The exterior of the new welfare house combines Tibetan style with modernization, with a library, computer room, activity room, dedicated bathroom where children can easily take showers, a medical room where children can receive treatment for minor illnesses within the welfare house, and flush toilets instead of the previous dry toilets. The new design widened the corridors for easier escape in case of earthquakes or fires. The new welfare house is no longer a single building, but a compound consisting of several buildings. The greening of the yard was also carefully considered, with plenty of sunlight in the middle of the yard, ensuring that the children can enjoy the sun during outdoor activities. "I also proposed not to occupy the neighbors' land and not to block their light, and the design experts agreed." Bai Ridai Le said.

After leaving Yushu for Hefei, anxiously waiting for buyers

After the planning was completed, Bai Ridai Le immediately began to raise funds quickly. Within a few months, he raised more than 3.5 million yuan. "The kind entrepreneur Hu De promised to donate 2 million yuan for the reconstruction. The Red Cross Society of Shantou, Gesanghua Western Assistance Education and other charitable organizations donated 1.48 million yuan to us. I reserved 80,000 yuan for the children to spend the winter, and added the 100,000 yuan on the welfare house account, making a total of 1.5 million yuan which I gave to the construction team." Bai Ridai Le said.

Construction started at the end of September, and stopped at the end of October. After buying the necessary building materials, the remaining money was only enough to maintain construction for one month. Bai Ridai Le also thought about seeking help from the local government, but after the Yushu earthquake, there were too many places that needed to be rebuilt, so Bai Ridai Le did not feel right asking the government for help, and decided to raise funds himself.

"The most awesome welfare house director" wants to build an "even better welfare house"

Hu De Welfare House used to only accommodate more than 100 children, later many more children wanted to come in, but unfortunately there was no more space. This time during the reconstruction, Bai Ridai Le decided to expand the scale of the welfare house, accommodating up to 170 children. The new design plan for the welfare house was done free of charge by the experts of the China Planning and Design Institute. Bai Ridai Le described it as a welfare house whose hardware surpassed at least 20 years. The only thing that remains unchanged is that Bai Ridai Le will still personally supervise the work, strictly checking every steel bar and every bag of cement. The new welfare house will still be a fortress-like "life shelter".

Bai Ridai Le always carries an engineering progress table and a daily detailed account in his mouth. He promised the children that they would move into the new house in October next year, and the schedule is very tight, so there can be no more delays. In addition, since the prices of steel bars and cement are relatively low in winter, for his already tight budget, such "discounts" are indispensable.

After the earthquake, the small building of Hu De Welfare House was identified as a dangerous building, and the children were temporarily settled in tents, then moved into mobile board houses. Bai Ridai Le promised to build new buildings for the children, and they would be bigger and better than before. In August and September, the new reconstruction plan for the welfare house was approved, with a total construction budget of 5.3 million yuan.

After much consideration, he decided to sell the two little Tibetan Mastiffs he raised at home. Except for these two dogs, he really didn't have anything valuable left to sell. Tibetan Mastiffs from Yushu are very famous, locals believe that selling Tibetan Mastiffs for money is a dishonorable thing, which made Bai Ridai Le feel very conflicted.
