Work hard and face life.

by zhaobin888 on 2012-02-16 09:50:17

Today, I came across an article written by Li Ka-shing. It says that working for others is the least effective investment. What does working invest in? Maybe this is a question many people have. Working, yes. Zero risk, zero investment. This is what many people think. Carefully consider life. Working, every day using your own hands, wasting ten or even twelve hours of time to create benefits for others. But what we get is not even one percent of the profit. Think about it, what is the most precious thing in the world? Maybe many people would say money, family, friendship. No...No...No. Time. Time is the most precious thing that belongs to oneself. Lu Xun once said, "A genius uses the time others spend drinking coffee on studying." There is no such thing in the world that yields returns without investment. Lurking in the city, working, commonly known as the moonlight clan. The monthly salary, ha... low to the point of being laughable... In our parents' generation, their thinking is a bit outdated. They think that working is the best investment. With the development of the world, we will gradually become obsolete. Every day, going to work, coming home from work, eating, sleeping. Are we wasting our youth or making what adults consider to be the best investment? Reflect on this. Post-90s. We are still young now. Why don't we take a brave step forward? Even if we fail, so what? At least we've tried. Even if we fail, so what? We can start over again. We are still young. Youth is capital. Although this phrase may not sound good, I think it suits us well. Although eighty percent of the world's population are employees, maybe only ten percent succeed as bosses. Without effort, there will be no return. Many people say that investing has risks, and if they lose, years of hard work will be wasted. Ha... What is risk-free? Eating, drinking water, walking? Are these risk-free? There are no geniuses in the world. A genius is one percent inspiration plus ninety-nine percent perspiration. There is no one in the world who was born a millionaire or even a billionaire. Still that sentence, without risk, there will never be any harvest. Perhaps, many people have thought about investing in their hearts. They always think about how to do this thing successfully, how to make more money, and what if they lose? No...No...No... Before doing something, don't always be indecisive. People who are indecisive find it hard to succeed. Better not to do than to do half-heartedly. Many people are just curious at the beginning, but after a few minutes of enthusiasm, they become lazy and lack motivation. Ha. Many people say, money is not easy to earn. Let's just work well. After retirement, we can still earn some pension. It's also a kind of security. Ha. This statement is quite laughable. Such people can only look up to others. When you go out shopping with your partner, are you willing to wait for the bus? It's so cold in winter and so hot in summer. When you see others wearing well, eating well, and carrying good things, aren't you envious? You are very envious. But you always find excuses saying that they have the ability. You only see their glory, but you don't see their efforts. Like inside, those who work honestly will eventually get rewarded, and then the previous frustrations and difficulties may become sweet memories that can amuse us. Post-90s, reflect on this. We are all approaching our twenties. What do we have? Cars? Houses? Money? No. We are nearing our twenties. In a few more years, we should get married. What can we use to take care of our loved ones and our children? Give it a try. Don't let yourself regret. Give it a try. Even if we fail, at least we've made an effort. Heaven will not treat anyone unfairly. Effort will definitely bring rewards.
