Melancholy diary, inescapable destiny, sister, please give my husband back to me.

by lwf225 on 2009-10-09 11:53:43

The person I loved the most betrayed me, and the person closest to me hurt me. The world was plunged into darkness, and life on earth seemed no different from hell. You can have your paradise on earth; I will never drink the soup of forgetfulness on the Bridge of Sighs. I believe in reincarnation, and in my next life, I will reclaim everything that is rightfully mine.

They say that two people in love are sometimes like twins. I say that two people who are twins share the same tastes, the same vision, and sometimes even fall in love with the same man, sharing the same love.

I was born in a rural area, and from the moment I let out my first cry, I was destined to be an unloved and unwanted ugly duckling. All because my sister was born three minutes before me, all because of one "good" word. My mother said one "good" word, with the character for woman before the character for son, hoping that her first child would be a girl. As she wished, she gave birth to twins, and my mother still saw my sister as a "good" omen. But I am also a girl, so why did my mother think that the firstborn female child would always be a symbol of good fortune? Therefore, my sister has always been more favored than me, and I could only secretly hide under the covers and listen to their laughter. Mother, do you know that while the character for "good" has the character for woman before it, not everything with the character for woman before it is good. The character for "slave" also has the character for woman before it. Are you not afraid that your sister might become a slave forever?

I hate, I hate all of you. Since I became aware, I knew I was the extra one in this family. You didn't even consider me. I don't understand, my sister and I look exactly alike. If it weren't for the mole on my arm that my sister doesn't have, I think even you would find it hard to tell who is the sister and who is the younger sister. Even our voices are indistinguishable. Why do you only care about those three minutes and thus refuse to love me?

I see other twins wearing the same clothes, eating the same food, using the same things, but us, from a young age, there was a difference in the clothes and toys mom bought for my sister and me. You always give the best things to my sister. During meals, you keep putting food into my sister's bowl until it's full, then you call me: Little Sister, eat more." It makes me sick.

Whenever you leave me alone at home, whenever I watch mom and dad take my sister's hand and walk out the door, whenever I hear your laughter slowly fade away at the turn of the stairs, I can't help but take a pen and stab it forcefully into an apple. I treat the apple as my sister, as a way to vent my inner dissatisfaction.

It was you who gave me life, shaped my reclusive personality, turned an originally innocent life into a devil filled with darkness, and cultivated a criminal.

I am lonely, I am cold. I know my whole life depends on myself, I yearn to escape the sea of suffering early, to be far away from this family.

Fortunately, my academic performance has always been on par with my sister's, which is the only thing that comforts me. After graduation, I was assigned to work at a rural bank, while my sister was assigned to teach in the city. When I saw my sister pack her luggage and board the special bus to school, I almost couldn't contain my excitement. I thought, finally, I've sent the pest away, leaving me as the only princess in this house. Isn't it time for me to get some attention?

My sister left, coming back home at most once or twice a month, each time staying only two days. And me, working in the countryside, I could go home every day after work. I thought my parents would focus their attention on me, but contrary to expectations, my parents would rather sit by the phone waiting dumbly for my sister's call than pay more attention to my life. I completely lost hope. My parents will never give me any of the love they have for my sister, and I will never replace my sister's place in their hearts.

At this time, a new male colleague arrived at the bank. His appearance painted a rosy glow in my gray sky. Tall stature, kind eyes, habitually having two shallow dimples at the corner of his mouth. A caring word from him, a friendly glance from him is enough to make me lose my mind. Since he came to the bank, my face has taken on a new expression, my life has gained a new vitality, and my heart has gained a new attachment.

People say that when a man chases a woman, there's a wall between them; when a woman chases a man, there's just a piece of paper between them. That's absolutely true. I like him, so I will chase him. I yearn to be loved, so I will obtain his love.

I began my pursuit. I knew he liked dancing, but I was too shy to ask him directly since I'm a girl. Finally, I came up with a good plan. After work, in the changing room, I sent him a text message with my phone: Tao, are you free tonight? Teach me how to dance. In no time, I received his reply: Sure, come if you're not afraid of me stepping on your feet, haha.

Hee hee, holding my phone, I jumped around wildly. He was always so humorous. I asked him to dance at the Golden Horse Dance Hall. For the first time, he held my waist, and I hooked my neck around his. At that moment, I had a thought: I want to hook onto him for life, making him belong to me forever.

"Tao, do you have a girlfriend?" I gently leaned my lips towards his ear.

"I do, what's wrong? Are you investigating my background?" He laughed.

I cried. He already has a girlfriend, I don't want to steal someone else's lover, nor do I want to be a third party: "No, just curious. Why haven't I heard you mention her?"

"You didn't ask, I can't be shouting all day that someone is already taken, or that someone's heart belongs to another, right?" He excitedly gripped my hand tighter.

"That's true, you're so excellent, how could you not have a girlfriend yet?" I was very disappointed and wanted to push him away and run out of the dance floor.

Really, I was disappointed, feeling quite foolish for my first date attempt ending in rejection. I tried, not to pursue him, not to compete with others for him, but to try not to have feelings for him, not to talk to him. However, why does the more I want to escape, the more I can't? I can control my words and actions, but not my heart.

Summer vacation arrived, my sister came back. Dad went fishing, mom killed chickens, preparing a feast for my sister's welcome home. At the dinner table, they kept calling out: "Bing, eat more. Take advantage of the summer break to nourish your body. Look at how you've tormented yourself this semester?"

The rice was already in my mouth, but I couldn't bring myself to open it. I stared straight at them, but they didn't notice my expression, ignoring my existence. How could I eat this meal? I excused myself saying I had a stomachache and ran off, but no one pretended to care or comforted me. Yes, it was an endless nightmare, I've been waiting for someone to take me away from all this trouble. But why is the world so vast, yet offers me no place to stay? Dazedly, I gazed at the sea and cried, wondering at this moment whether the seawater is saltier or my tears are saltier?

I called Tao, and within fifteen minutes, he arrived at the beach. Seeing me, he anxiously asked: "Xia, what's wrong? Why are you alone at the beach during dinner time?"

"Tao, don't ask why, I just need to lean on your shoulder. I'm so tired," Tao silently sat on the rock. I rested my head on his shoulder, closed my eyes, and let the sea breeze dry the tears on my face. Besides the sea breeze and the sound of the waves hitting the rocks, Tao was silent, and I didn't want to open my eyes, wishing time would stop at this moment forever.

"Xia, can you tell me what happened? I really want to share the burden with you."

Hearing Tao's voice, my tears involuntarily flowed again: "You already belong to someone else, even if I told you, it wouldn't change this fact."

"Xia, I don't belong to anyone. My heart already belongs to you. Haven't you felt it? Last time, it was just a joke I played on you."

Wiping away my tears, I raised my head and looked at him blankly. The past memories floated above the sea, yes, he was constantly paying attention to me. Every time I had something on my mind, he was always the first to notice. Every time I secretly shed tears, he quietly handed me tissues. Aren't these signs of concern for me?

Love is actually this simple. He silently gazed at me, and I foolishly stared back at him. We just looked at each other like this.

"Xia, stand up, let's go." Tao pulled my hand to stand up. On the beach, Tao picked me up and spun me around continuously, spinning and spinning, then we both fell onto the sand, lying down looking at the night sky. Love makes one forget all troubles. The sweetness at this moment has already covered the worries I brought here.

On the beach, I told Tao everything about my background. Tao tightly hugged me in his arms: "Xia, it's okay, everything will pass. I will use my entire life to protect you, cherish you, and make sure you are no longer wronged."

"Tao, is this real? If you really love me, take me away from here, away from this home."

Without hesitation, Tao pulled me into the car and took me to where he lived. Two bedrooms and a living room, the house was simple but tidy.

Tao spoke: "Xia, do you know why I've remained single all this time? I dared not love, nor accept love, because love means giving happiness to the other person. In my current state, how can I offer happiness to someone?"

A fellow wanderer in adversity, I tightly embraced Tao, listening to his story. Tao was born in a rural area, an only child. His father fell ill early, and to treat him, Tao sold the house allocated by his unit. A few years later, his father passed away, leaving Tao and his elderly mother to depend on each other for survival. His mother now lives in the countryside, while Tao rents this house alone. Tao's greatest wish is to own his own house soon and bring his elderly mother to live with him.

"Tao, as long as there is love, warmth can be found everywhere. Don't lose heart. Let's work together. I believe happiness is not far away."

Soon, I moved in with Tao, and we started cohabiting. Every day, Tao would cook for me, tell me fairy tales from his childhood, give me baths, and provide many surprises I've never experienced before, making me feel like a child again, a cherished child. I am no longer the unloved ugly duckling.

This period was the happiest time of my life, but why does happiness always slip away quietly from me?

After Tao's mother met me once, she also liked me very much, urging us to get married soon. She removed the only ring on her hand and gave it to me, saying she had decided that I would be her only daughter-in-law in this lifetime. How could I not want to get married soon? Tao was even more eager, after all, he is already in his thirties. A house became the stumbling block to our marriage.

Tao said he wanted to take a risk and buy stocks. Indeed, most colleagues at the bank were trading stocks, and that year, the stock market was doing well. Tao and I pooled all our savings, which amounted to over twenty thousand yuan, and we quickly invested it all in Shenzhen stocks. Being our first time buying stocks, we were very nervous, but the stock we bought rose quickly, and we eagerly sold it, making a profit of several thousand yuan.

Having had one exciting challenge, we quickly took the second step. We started going online, studying the stock market, and seeking advice from veteran experts at the bank. Haste makes waste, indeed we were equally impatient, wanting to seize the opportunity at the right moment. The second stock became the weapon that sent me to prison. To buy a house sooner, I secretly misappropriated public funds from the current account. I thought it should only take a few days to cover it up.

Who would have known it would be gone forever, losing over a hundred thousand yuan. Everything was done secretly by me, Tao didn't know anything about it. But when I was arrested at the bank, he just stared at me speechlessly...

Prison is hell, in that dark place, I suffered all sorts of humiliations. Fellow inmates bullied me, guards beat me, everyone was like a ferocious demoness. I'd rather die than live like this, gazing through the iron bars at nothingness.

After months of interrogation, Tao came to see me. Through the glass, I could clearly see Tao's tears.

"Xia, it's my fault. If I hadn't been with you, you wouldn't have committed such a mistake."

"Go back! Don't be foolish, forget about me!" Although I love him, I know very well that it's impossible between us now.

When he left, Tao said one sentence: "Xia, reform yourself well, I'll wait for you. No matter how long, I'll wait for you to be my beautiful bride."

I was sentenced to six years, six years, can a 33-year-old single man wait for a criminal for six years? Is that possible? I kept telling myself: Xia, don't be silly, it's impossible. Although I knew it was impossible, I still remembered what Tao said as he left: reform yourself well, wait for me.

Tao's one sentence gave me motivation, gave me a goal, gave me hope. For Tao, I must strive to achieve it. Every time my family brought me good food, I wouldn't eat it, instead, I shared it with the sisters in the same cell. Over time, they gradually accepted me as a friend. Prison isn't much different from the outside world; to survive, you rely on yourself, on your wits. Many things you don't want to do, you still have to do, still have to accept, that's life. The kindness of the inmates gave me space to express myself. I taught classes in the prison, with my highest level of education among them, I diligently completed every task the warden assigned to me, motivating the inmates.

Even though each day felt like a year, I still didn't give up, because I knew someone was waiting for me outside.

Everything ended, everything began anew, this is rebirth. Due to my excellent behavior, I was released two years early.

At the prison gate, only my parents came to pick me up, I didn't see Tao.

I didn't want to talk to my parents. If it weren't for them, I wouldn't be like this today.

Mom helped me carry the luggage: "Xia, let's go home! Mom rented a house for you outside the village, so you won't have to endure the villagers' gossip. Xia, find a job, live honestly, start anew!"

Suddenly, I looked at Mom again. This was the first time in my life I heard a warm word from Mom. I tightly held Mom's hand and followed them.

They rented a two-bedroom apartment for me, neatly arranged, with everything ready to use.

I called Tao, his mobile number was already disconnected. I asked Mom: "Mom, where's Tao? Why didn't he come to pick me up?"

"We didn't tell him the day you were released from prison. We'll inform him when we see him. Xia, your priority now isn't Tao, it's finding a job for yourself," Dad said to me.

That's all we could do. After resting at home for a few days, still no news from Tao.

I started looking for a job. No matter what, people must survive. I won't return to my old home in the countryside. Fortunately, in the city, not many people know me, no one knows my past. Mom thought pretty thoroughly, it seems Mom loves me, I just didn't feel it.

After searching for many days, I still couldn't find a suitable job, wandering aimlessly on the street. Tao, where are you? I miss you so much. You said you would wait for me, but now I'm out, and I can't find you.

I couldn't suppress myself anymore, I hailed a taxi on the street and headed straight to the rural bank.

At the entrance of the bank, I saw a romantic scene. My sister and Tao were holding hands as they walked out of the bank, then drove away together.

All of this happened suddenly, I couldn't believe my eyes, I couldn't believe it. There's no need for me to accept the reality, the reality is that Tao is mine, the reality is that Tao has been waiting for me somewhere. As for what I saw, there's no need to verify it, because even if my sister fell in love with Tao, Tao is mine, he will never belong to my sister.

Pushing the door of the old house in the countryside, everyone was shocked by my sudden appearance, not just them, even I doubted if I entered the wrong house. Under the old tree, my parents were teaching a child to walk, Tao was cooking in the kitchen, and my sister was folding clothes. Hearing the door open, everyone stopped what they were doing and stared at me dumbfoundedly, a perfect picture of a happy family.

Tell myself to stay calm, tell myself not to act impulsively, tell myself this is all an illusion, give myself various reasons to lie to myself, but it was all in vain. I went crazy, running around and grabbing each of them by their collars asking why? Why? No one answered me. They all stared at me like dead people, finally Mom hugged me: "Xia, calm down and listen to me. It was my idea, Tao didn't want to. I wanted Tao to be my son, you should understand, I didn't have a son, just you two sisters..."

"Enough, you don't just lack a son, you only have one daughter, I'm not your daughter, I'm not..." Accompanied by a mournful cry, I interrupted Mom's words. No, she is no longer my mom, I don't have such a mother. There is no place in this world for a mother like her.

Approaching my sister, I sneered at her and said: "In this life, you only win against me once, and that's because you won those three minutes, being born three minutes earlier than me. In this life, you are only fit to be a slave, a tool for our parents, a servant taking care of my husband. Tao loves your body, because you look exactly like me, you are just a substitute, just my shadow. And my soul will forever live in Tao's heart, Tao's soul will forever accompany me. Tao will live his whole life in guilt and remorse, and so will you. If you base your happiness on someone else's pain, is the happiness obtained with someone else's blood and tears still happiness?"

Laughing triumphantly and looking up at the sky, this is my home, my family, my love, these are the demons who pushed me into the abyss, making me suffer endlessly.

Everything is over. Standing on a high rock by the sea, listening to the sound of the waves crashing against the rocks, it's so beautiful, let me turn into a seagull, freely flying in the sky above the sea, letting this lonely soul forever accompany the sea.
