Air purifier

by nclep on 2008-11-14 11:20:30

The inspiration for this process comes from how the air is exceptionally fresh after a heavy rain, as rain can wash away dust from the air. Based on this concept, NCL Company has adopted a nasal washing system where polluted air is inhaled into the equipment and washed with a cleansing liquid, which traps the dust. Through the action of a pressure pump, the dust is then transferred to an automatic dust-removal filter for filtration and discharge, achieving the effect of automatically clearing dust and purifying the air. This process has low and steady wind resistance (15-20P), can replace medium-efficiency filters, and does not cause secondary pollution, known as the "dust removal method." According to customer needs, biochemical enzymes, ozone water, or alkaline water are added to the cleansing liquid to absorb the heat emitted by the motor group, maintaining a constant temperature, quickly neutralizing and decomposing radioactive substances and gaseous pollutants in the air, known as the "neutralization method." Aluminum honeycomb is used as a carrier, coated with a certain amount of nano-level nickel and titanium dioxide materials, photocatalytic materials, forming a metal photocatalyst mesh. Under specific light sources, it continuously adsorbs-catalyzes-decomposes, removing bacteria, viruses, and harmful gases from the air, known as the "sterilization method."

To "create a building that breathes" for high-end customers, NCL Company provides two solutions for customers to choose from.

Product Features:

1. Breaks away from the traditional purification product processes of filtering - adsorbing - re-filtering, preventing secondary pollution from the equipment itself, effectively avoiding the "intrusion" of traditional purification products on the mental and physical well-being of customers.

2. All our equipment is custom-made for each customer, without electrostatic adsorption or ultraviolet components, preventing direct harm caused by field strength, field pressure, or ultraviolet leakage.

3. Removes formaldehyde, toluene, ammonia, smoke, alcohol, and other living odors left over from construction and decoration, with a removal efficiency rate of over 99.8% for various harmful gases.

4. Clears dust from indoor air, with automatic filter cleaning, no blockage, and stable dust-removal efficiency. There will be no more dust on your floors and tables at home, allowing you to clean monthly.

5. Eliminates bacteria and viruses in the air, achieving a 100% removal rate of natural bacteria in the air, effectively preventing diseases spread through the air, providing extra care for the elderly and children.

6. Increases oxygen and negative oxygen ions in indoor air, keeping oxygen content above 21%, and the negative ion content reaching 80,000-500,000/cm³, offering forest-like and waterfall-like fresh air, beneficial for the physical and mental health of customers.

7. In the event of new viruses or malignant infectious diseases, this device can be upgraded with corresponding disinfection functions, making it the first choice for patients recovering at home.
