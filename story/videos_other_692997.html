Shenzhen Airlines Group Changes: Kunpeng Airlines Renamed to "Henan" Unaffected

by anonymous on 2012-03-06 13:53:58

December 8, the reporter learned from Kunpeng Airlines Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Kunpeng Airlines") that although currently the boarding operations are still being conducted under the name of "Kunpeng Airlines", the signboard at its Zhengzhou headquarters has been replaced, and aircrafts marked with "Kunpeng Airlines" have already changed to "Henan Airlines". "The formal renaming is just a matter of time, it will happen in the near future, the overall situation has been decided," on December 7, a senior official from Kunpeng Airlines told the reporter. This person who was unwilling to disclose his/her name said that the Henan Provincial Government and Kunpeng Airlines have reached an agreement on capital injection. As for specific details such as the proportion of capital injection, it is not convenient to disclose now. "Wait for the message from the provincial government." He said, Guangzhou moving company. The change of Kunpeng Airlines to "Henan Airlines" has further expanded the team of airlines localized under their respective regions. Since the beginning of this year, Shenzhen Airlines, Eastern Airlines, Hainan Airlines and other subsidiaries have successively formed alliances with local governments. Moreover, on the 18th of this month, Eagle Airlines will be renamed "Chengdu Airlines", making the atmosphere of "state advance and private retreat" in the civil aviation industry increasingly intense. Regarding the return of local governments' say in the aviation industry, the above-mentioned person frankly stated that various support policies offered by local governments to airlines are indeed very attractive. Henan Airlines takes the stage Starting from the beginning of this month, an aircraft of Kunpeng Airlines sprayed with the words "Henan Airlines" can already be seen on the apron of Zhengzhou Airport. It is understood that the other three aircrafts of Kunpeng Airlines will also complete modifications one after another. A person from Kunpeng Airlines said that Henan Airlines is about to officially take the stage, and preparations have entered the final stage. "Although there have been changes in the parent company Shenzhen Airlines, it will not affect the cooperation between Kunpeng Airlines and the Henan Provincial Government," the above-mentioned person indicated. Public data shows that in 2007, Shenzhen Airlines together with the U.S. regional airline Mesa Airlines Group and the U.S. Mountain Trust Company jointly invested in establishing Kunpeng Airlines, holding shares respectively at 51%, 25%, and 24%. Initially, Kunpeng Airlines' operation base and headquarters were set at Xianyang Airport in Xi'an. "We believe there will be greater development in Zhengzhou," a person from Kunpeng Airlines explained the reason for "moving". However, the industry has more interpretations regarding Kunpeng Airlines' relocation from the west to Central Plains, believing that Henan Provincial Government's intention to inject capital played an important role. So far, although both Kunpeng Airlines and the Henan Provincial Government are willing to talk about the cooperation, they insist on handling the specific amount of capital injection and the shareholding ratio low-keyly.
