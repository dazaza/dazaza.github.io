What birthday gift should I give my girlfriend? Can you bear to see your friend end up the same way as them?

by liyijiujiu on 2011-10-31 16:07:06

This is their last day of breaking up... Before that, the boy had dropped out of school and was helping his brother-in-law with a small business. The girl was a well-behaved girl who often helped her mother buy groceries. Once, twice, the boy and the girl got to know each other through buying and selling! The boy asked for the girl's phone number, they kept in touch via mobile phone, they chatted very well, talking about everything, finding happiness in each chat. One day, the boy expressed his love to the girl, but she rejected him. The boy knew the girl was pure, easily hurt, and hard to move her heart. The boy declared his love time and again, and finally his sincerity moved the girl's heart. The girl believed in the boy, accepted the boy, and chose to be together.

The boy dropped out of school in the third year of junior high, but every time school was over, the boy would run to the school alone and play with the girl, chatting happily. They were glad to have such a love, and under the tree, they made an oath. The girl said: "If you don't say break up, I won't say break up." The boy said, "As long as I live one day, I will love you one day, until the end."

The girl's father gambled on lottery numbers for money. On lottery nights, the girl took orders for her father and found time to see the boy. It was the boy's first girlfriend. He didn't know how to express his love, so he hugged the girl tightly. The boy didn't dare to give his first kiss as a gift to the girl. Then they went home respectively.

One night when they met again, the boy picked up the girl and teased her, "A bird fell down, look quickly!" The girl turned her face to look, and when she turned back, their lips met. This was the moment to give the first kiss. The boy put his tongue into the girl's mouth, and the girl bit the boy's tongue. The boy pretended to be in great pain, and the girl became nervous and said "...". When the boy heard the girl's question, he smiled. After this, they kissed consciously every time.

Unfortunately, the girl tested the boy's sincerity and proposed a breakup. The boy was stunned at this time. He didn't know what he did wrong. His heart was broken, and tears squeezed out from the corner of his eyes. The boy blamed himself and tried to win the girl back! After some time, the girl said again that they should break up. The boy felt cold from the warmth. On the 9th day, the girl proposed, "Let's break up, I don't want to make you sad anymore." The heartbroken boy said, "Alright, can you accompany me on our last day?" The girl nodded and agreed to the boy's request!

On the 10th, the boy and the girl met at nine o'clock and decided to go to Tangxi Reservoir to play. It was our most romantic memory! They sat quietly at the edge of the reservoir, with nothing to talk about. The boy picked up a pebble and threw it into the reservoir, then looked at the wandering fish and said, "There is no source of living water here, it's like a prison for the fish. In the water, there isn't even a single tear." The girl stood up and picked wild chrysanthemums by the reservoir, stepped on soft stones, lost her footing, and fell into the reservoir. Without hesitation, the boy jumped after her. Because the water was deep, it was far from the stone steps where they could stand. The girl couldn't swim, so the boy pulled her onto his back on the water surface. Swimming in the water was already exhausting, plus the weight of the girl pressing on him. The boy's head submerged underwater, and he swam forcefully towards the stone steps. With all his strength, he approached the steps without letting the girl drink any water. The boy stretched his hand out of the water and grabbed the girl's thigh, pushing her onto the steps. At this moment, the girl had the strength of someone who had narrowly escaped death. She stepped on the boy's shoulders and climbed onto the steps. When she turned around, the boy's struggling figure was already gone, sinking into the reservoir! The girl immediately ran to call nearby villagers. The villagers went into the reservoir to search for the boy. Ten minutes passed, twenty minutes... an hour later, they finally retrieved the boy's body. He had already stopped breathing, but his hands were still stiffly holding the position of pushing the girl, and his smiling little mouth was still there. The girl ran over and collapsed on the boy's body, unable to control her tears streaming down. She shouted, "Come back, I won't leave you!" Her shouts echoed continuously by the reservoir. The girl looked at the boy's body and found something bulging in his pocket. She took it out. It was a box. She gently opened the box, inside was a silver ring and a wet piece of paper with words copied on both sides! It read, "Shuzhen, today is your birthday, your most beautiful day. You should be happy! Happy Birthday! Inviting you to the reservoir to play is to give you memories. We've been together for so long, and I haven't given you any gifts. I keep you in my heart. My family is not wealthy, so I can't buy you a gold ring. This cheap silver ring is my deepest gift to you. I hope you don't mind and always wear it! I know every time you say break up, you are reluctant to let me go. If I really leave you, who will love you, care for you, follow you, and make you laugh... Don't say those foolish things. Remember our vows? Haha... I love you..."

The girl felt heartache, her tears flowed faster and faster. She regretted hurting the boy before and realized that only when you lose something do you realize how precious it is! The girl shared her story with her friends who were dating, telling them not to be like her, not to take blessings for granted, and to cherish the happiness in front of their eyes, whether it's a boy or a girl who loves them!!

After reading such a love story, did you cry? Friends, take the trouble to forward this to your space, let your friends also forward it, so everyone knows to cherish love! If you don't forward it, maybe your best friend doesn't know how to cherish it, and you wouldn't want them to have the same ending, would you? Take action and forward it!
