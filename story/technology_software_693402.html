The Shenzhen leg of the 2012 Zhang Xueyou World Tour will be held on March 2, 2012 at the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center Stadium. Currently, the 10% discounted concert tickets have been in high demand among fans. Perhaps the fans of Zhang Xueyou who are coming to Shenzhen for his concert also have the purpose of touring the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center Stadium? It's both fun and entertaining; while enjoying the concert of your idol, you can also play around. Why not?

The Shenzhen Bay Sports Center is located in the northeast corner of the Nanshan Back Sea Central Business District, adjacent to Hong Kong. It is also a key urban landscape and public activity space in Shenzhen's future. After its completion, it will become another landmark building in Shenzhen. The integrated design of the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center places the stadium, gymnasium, and swimming pool under a white giant grid-like steel structure roof. The beautifully designed roof resembles an incubator nurturing athletes breaking out and rushing towards the world, with novel ideas and unique shapes, similar to the design approach of the "Bird's Nest". However, the unique structure of "one field and two halls" makes the construction difficulty of the "Spring Cocoon" higher than that of the Bird's Nest. Meanwhile, the hardware, software, and various facilities of the Spring Cocoon can host major domestic and international brand competitions and large-scale performances. The "Spring Cocoon" is not only a new landmark in Shenzhen but also represents the city image of Shenzhen, bearing profound social significance and deep sentiment of Shenzhen.

The Shenzhen Bay Sports Center is mainly composed of the stadium, gymnasium, and swimming pool ("one field and two halls"). Taking advantage of the opportunity presented by the Universiade, relying on large-scale events, and focusing on physical fitness for all, it integrates sports competitions, athletic training, mass fitness, exhibitions, trade shopping, artistic performances, leisure and entertainment, and tourism sightseeing into an international comprehensive large-scale sports center. Both the city of Shenzhen and the Nanshan District where the sports center is located will fully develop and utilize the compound functions of the center to build four platforms:

**Brand Competition Platform**

Currently, the F1 motorboat race has settled in Nanshan for 10 years, and the finals of the 2008 Straight-line Speed Car King Challenge will also be prepared. The Shenzhen Bay Sports Center will rely on superior venue facilities, beautiful natural environment, and Shenzhen's unique climatic conditions, integrating various venue facilities, scientific research breakthroughs, nutritional catering, sports injury rehabilitation, and physiotherapy institutions. With high-tech, systematic, and multi-functional comprehensive services, it will attract teams and athletes of all levels to come for training. After a certain period of operation, development, and improvement, the sports center will become a professional training and amateur training service center with full coverage, multiple functions, and modernization, cultivating brand competitions suitable for Nanshan.

**Leisure Fitness Platform**

Besides ensuring training, competition, and business office needs, the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center will also open to the public, conducting diversified operations, providing rich and colorful leisure fitness activities, and meeting the sports consumption demands of Shenzhen citizens. In response to the characteristics of the population structure in Nanshan District, which is predominantly highly educated and technically skilled, as well as the general public's fitness and entertainment needs, the concept of a sports park can be introduced, emphasizing people's sports culture, sports leisure awareness, and the park-like transformation of large sports facilities. Utilizing the "one field and two halls" and outdoor spaces, basketball courts, small football fields, and table tennis tables can be built for the public to use for daily fitness and entertainment, or they can also be used as venues for community sports competitions such as three-person basketball and five-person football matches. The sports square provides morning exercise grounds for the elderly, and teenagers can also conduct roller skating activities here. For some higher-consuming groups within the district, a tennis club can be opened, and using the indoor space of the "one field and two halls", high-end fitness clubs can be established.

**Cultural Entertainment Platform**

The operation of the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center fully considers its strategic significance in sports and culture, emphasizing the combination of professional functions and comprehensive functions of sports venues, i.e., adhering to the combination of competitive sports and mass sports, sports and cultural entertainment, and sports and commerce. The stadium inside the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center has 20,000 fixed seats, and the gymnasium has 10,000 fixed seats. The modernization level of sound, lighting, TV broadcasting equipment is high, the service facilities are complete, the geographical location is advantageous, and transportation is convenient. After its completion, the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center will realize three main functions: hosting major sports events, meetings, art, exhibitions, and other business activities, and providing citizens with leisure, fitness, and entertainment.

**Commerce Service Platform**

The sports service industry has the advantages of traditional service industries with high added value, large output, and strong driving ability, and as an emerging service industry, it has the characteristics of strong absorption and aggregation capabilities, making it an exciting point in social and economic development. In the future, the Shenzhen Bay Sports Center will form a comprehensive commercial service system centered on sports, integrating conferences, exhibitions, characteristic catering, retail, health care, training, and other commercial service industries.
