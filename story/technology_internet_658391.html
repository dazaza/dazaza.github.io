Frequency Conversion Cabinet / Distribution Box

Specialized frequency conversion cabinet for fans and pumps: mainly used for speed adjustment of fans and pumps. Automatic and manual regulation of fan and pump frequency conversion cabinets achieve precise control of temperature, flow, and pressure, meeting different process requirements. Save electricity, protect and extend the life of the motor, increase comprehensive benefits, and reduce unnecessary energy loss. For example, traditional boiler blowers, induced draft fans, and water pumps operate at fixed speeds driven by motors, and then adjust the wind volume or water pressure by changing the baffle plate at the fan inlet or the regulating valve at the pump outlet.

In general cases, the opening degree of the baffle or regulating valve is only 50%-80%, while the motor still runs at full load, resulting in very low working efficiency of the motor. Such a control method causes significant energy loss. The most significant feature of fans and pumps is that the load torque is directly proportional to the square of the rotational speed, and the shaft power is directly proportional to the cube of the rotational speed. After using a frequency converter, the motor speed can be manually or automatically adjusted (via pressure, flow, or temperature sensors) to meet the process usage requirements for flow and pressure. This way, it saves a lot of electricity, extends the life of the motor, and reduces water and coal energy consumption.
