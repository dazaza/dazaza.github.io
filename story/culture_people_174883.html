Wang Yihan walks in the art of beauty CEO

by goodman on 2009-07-11 12:18:49

Wang Yihan, she is known as the "beautiful CEO walking in art", and her face is as clear as her voice. "Investment channels in China are relatively narrow. Among the three major investment categories of stocks, real estate and art, the threshold of art is undoubtedly the highest, unlike the other two, there is a clear announcement.

Executive Director and general manager of Beijing Zhongyibo Culture Communication Co., LTD

The so-called highest wisdom is to be able to adapt quickly to any situation, and to always be calm and peaceful.

Wang Yihan, a master of oil painting from the Department of Fine Arts of Capital Normal University, taught at Anhui Normal University from 1998 to 2000, served as the general manager and art director of Beijing Soka Art Center from 2001 to June 2005, and served as the supervisor of the board of directors of Beijing Zhongyibo Culture Communication Co., LTD in early 2002. Since July 2005, he has been the legal representative and executive director of China Art Fair, responsible for the organization and preparation of the 2006 China International Gallery Fair.

Compared with words such as beauty, intelligence, money and identity, she prefers others to describe her as a cultural person. Whether it is doing art or when the general manager of a gallery, or now the executive director of an international expo, Wang Yihan adheres to the same humanistic attitude.

Only she herself knew that when someone put the title of "strong woman" on her head and added the description of "too smart" to her, her inner struggle and pain.

"Who is this woman? What's her background?" Like the opening scene of a mystery film, Wang Yihan appears amid a chorus of questions and inquiries. Those who do not know her will certainly not believe that a woman without any background, who looks weak and beautiful, is capable of sitting in such a position of power and importance at such a young age - less than 30 years old - beauty is a double-edged sword, bringing convenience and increasing the index of success and trust.

This position refers to the executive director of the China International Gallery Fair, and as the highest-level gallery fair in the country, it does require a strong background to sit in this position. It is a testament not only to capital power but also to the support of the official system.

Wang Yihan's resume is only three lines: Bachelor of Fine Arts, Anhui Normal University; Master of Fine Arts, Capital Normal University, General Manager of Soka Gallery...

Only behind these three lines, slowly infiltrated such content: Anhui Normal University art department of the year's youngest provincial winner; The youngest member of Anhui Fine Arts Association at that time; The general manager of the Soka Art Center, one of Beijing's richest galleries, her youth makes her formidably powerful.

Only she herself knew that when someone put the title of "strong woman" on her head and added the description of "too smart" to her, her inner struggle and pain. This woman sitting there, as quiet as the lake, described her as vulgar. Just listening to her impassioned description of how to build the China International Gallery Fair into the fifth largest after the four major European fairs, I was really reluctant to use strong to describe her, and I could not help but add some "tough" words to her attributes.
