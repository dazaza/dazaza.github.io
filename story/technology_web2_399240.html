I. Company Introduction

XiKuan Data Center holds relevant licenses issued by the state for commercial ICP, ISP and other services, making it a legitimate IDC service provider.

XiKuan Quanyun Data Center and Beijing BGP Data Center are two major brands under XiKuan Data Center. The XiKuan Quanyun Data Center was built when Shandong Province hosted the National Games, mainly providing data collection services for the event. After the National Games, the XiKuan Quanyun Data Center was officially opened to the public. The Beijing BGP Data Center was built in 2004 in response to the government's informatization requirements, effectively solving the north-south interoperability issues between China Unicom and China Telecom. After six years of development, the Beijing BGP Data Center has become very mature in terms of hardware and software, providing safe, stable, and high-speed network services to thousands of users.

In addition to IDC services (including but not limited to server hosting, server rental, VM rental, virtual hosting, cabinet rental, etc.), XiKuan Data Center also involves domain name services, website creation, website optimization, and other business areas. It has now become a comprehensive network service provider integrating IDC services, domain name services, website creation, website optimization, and more.

II. Data Center Introduction

1. XiKuan Quanyun Data Center

The XiKuan Quanyun Data Center is located in the University Science and Technology Park in Jinan, the capital of Shandong Province, and is one of the largest IDC service providers in Shandong. The total building area of the data center is nearly 300 square meters. The XiKuan Quanyun Data Center was built according to the high standards and specifications required by the National Games, fully considering the needs of the games and reserving space for use by enterprises, institutions, and e-commerce categories. During the National Games, it successfully provided network support services such as data collection for the games. After the end of the National Games and its opening to the public, many well-known enterprises and institutions have moved in.

1) Building Facilities

The interior decoration uses dust-proof and flame-retardant materials; the floor is an anti-static floor, and the walls are coated with anti-static paint; the entire building can withstand an earthquake of magnitude eight, and each square meter of the ground can bear 800 kilograms. The data center and monitoring room are separated by 12mm thick clear glass, with complete curtain walls and monitoring equipment.

2) Network Resources

Each has 3.5G export bandwidth from China Unicom and China Telecom. The data center is directly connected to China Unicom and China Telecom without intermediate nodes, achieving a network connectivity rate of up to 99.6%. The main equipment and lines are redundantly designed to ensure uninterrupted network services in case of line failure.

3) Power Guarantee

The data center uses Class A municipal power supply with dual-line access (one of which is a backup line). In addition, there are sufficient batteries and high-power diesel generators to ensure circuit stability.

4) Air Conditioning System

A dedicated data center air conditioning system precisely controls the temperature and humidity of the data center space, providing optimal operating conditions for the customer's network system, ensuring that the IDC data center maintains a constant temperature of 23±2°C and humidity of 45%.

5) Fire Safety

The data center is equipped with a professional monitoring system, allowing real-time viewing of all locations in the data center through the monitoring system in the monitoring room, ensuring real-time knowledge of the data center situation.

The data center is equipped with dry powder fire extinguishers, highly sensitive ion smoke detection alarm systems, and temperature detection alarm systems, enabling the first discovery of fire hazards and immediate firefighting.

6) Access Control System

Dual verification of entering personnel using password + IC card access control system, preventing unauthorized personnel from entering, and maximizing the security and privacy of user machines.

2. Beijing BGP Data Center

In 2004, one of the earliest BGP dual-line data centers in Beijing was built domestically, with network exports reaching 6G, including 3G each from China Unicom and China Telecom, forming standard symmetric dual lines. To date, over 60% of the served customers belong to high-end user groups primarily consisting of government and enterprise units.

1) Basic Environment

Provides standard racks for placing servers and ancillary equipment; provides dual power supply, intelligent UPS, and diesel generators; provides a safe and stable environment with a network connectivity rate of up to 99.9%; provides advanced monitoring and fire protection systems.

2) Network System

Connects directly to mainstream operator networks like China Telecom and China Unicom using BGP technology, making the speed and judgment of north-south access higher, with fiber link backups, achieving redundancy at the INTERNET export, ensuring fast network access from all regions across the country.

3) Data Center Architecture

Uses load-bearing structural design, with a floor load capacity limit of 1200Kg/square meter, allowing users to place some special equipment. The data center provides steel standard racks (rack width 19 inches, height 2.4 meters).

4) Ventilation Facilities

Multiple air conditioners combine side delivery with duct delivery to make indoor temperature and humidity as evenly distributed as possible. Data center temperature: 18~26 degrees Celsius, relative humidity: 30%~70%.
