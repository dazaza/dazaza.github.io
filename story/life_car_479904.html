2011 Car Beauty Technician Latest Salary Treatment

Car beauty talents:

A car beauty technician went to learn for a period of time, and has mastered skills such as film application, floor laying, waxing, car washing, reversing radar installation, etc. However, the technician is not very proficient in installing central control locks, car DVD installations, and car audio modifications. If one wants to apply for a position, they may wonder about the latest salary treatment for car beauty technicians in 2011?

Expert Answer:

"Car beauty technician recruitment, with a monthly salary of around 2,000 yuan plus bonus." An advertisement from a car beauty shop in Hunan province has been posted on the China Automobile Network for a month and a half, but the ideal technician has yet to be recruited. The car beauty industry in Wuhan is roughly troubled by this issue: it's difficult to recruit new professional car beauticians and even harder to retain experienced ones.

Yesterday, during an interview at a car beauty shop in Hankou, we found that the technical supervisor of the store had been poached and became the store manager of another car beauty shop. A staff member of the shop said, "I have only been here for three months, and two people have already been poached." Because the threshold for car decoration and beauty shops is not too high, over the years many external capital investors have seen the huge potential of this industry and have entered it. High salaries are often the first choice for new stores to attract talent, and the phenomenon of "poaching" within the industry is not uncommon. This belongs to internal corporate operation issues; with a good working environment and benefits, good talents will not leave. Skilled technicians trained by local enterprises at great expense are not only poached by peers within the city but also flow to other places. The producer of Wuhan’s car professional media "Car Tribe Wind" introduced that the monthly income of car beauty technicians in Beijing and Shanghai is 1500-1800 yuan higher than in Wuhan, and many mature technicians have gone to work elsewhere.

According to the Wuhan Automobile Service Association, some people think that car beauty is as simple as washing cars and waxing. In fact, taking paint alone, there are six processes such as paint surface oxidation renewal, judgment and repair of paint surface scratches, and paint surface glazing care, let alone other "high-end" technologies like car interior sauna (sterilization and disinfection), anti-rain and anti-fog treatment for car glass. Car beauty technicians are considered senior talents with a promising future.

According to a director of Wuhan Weitai Car Beauty Decoration Company: The car beauty industry lacks specialized technical training institutions, and universities do not have related majors either. Most car beauty technicians are trained directly by enterprises. It takes at least 2 to 3 months to train an ordinary beauty technician.
