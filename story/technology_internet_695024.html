The Matchmaker said: "Marriage is predestined 500 years ago." Legend has it that the matchmaker ties the feet of a man and a woman together with a red string. The strings vary in length and tightness, so some couples meet from afar and become husband and wife, some elope at night, some stay together for life, while others have only a one-night stand. The matchmaker is merely the person who ties the red string, and the blame does not lie with him. On Folded Net [http://www.fanzhewang.com], everything in the world is a manifestation of cause and effect. The so-called predestined marriage refers to the causes sown in the previous life and the effects experienced in this life.

When it comes to the word "marriage," the elders always explain it as "predestined." In fact, marriage is arranged by heaven, and we can check our marital relationship in this life through the Three-Life Book. Today, let's learn together how to use the "Three-Life Book" to check marriage.

(Note: For women, men check their八字[Bazi])

Method of checking marriage in the Three-Life Book on Folded Net [http://www.fanzhewang.com]:

(1) Record your lunar birth month. For example: 2009, the ninth lunar month.

(2) Find out the "gan zhi" (Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches) of your birth day (you can find this out through a perpetual calendar). For example: 2009, the thirteenth day of the ninth lunar month, the day gan zhi is "wu shen," called "wu shen day."

(3) Refer to the following table to find the "Na Yin" of your "birth day gan zhi."

[http://photo.blog.sina.com.cn/showpic.html#blogid=5027agtnq&url=http://s7.sinaimg.cn/orignal/5027a397h7d69647fe276&690]

For example: 2009, the thirteenth day of the ninth lunar month, the birth day gan zhi is "wu shen," and according to the table, the "Na Yin" of "wu shen day" is "earth" on Folded Net [http://www.fanzhewang.com].

(4) Check the couple's emotional position table.

The fourth step is to check the couple's emotional position. When we know that on the thirteenth day of the ninth lunar month in 2009, the birth day gan zhi is "wu shen" and the Na Yin is "earth," then we look at the birth month, such as September, and his couple's emotional position is in the "Guan Dai" position.

[http://photo.blog.sina.com.cn/showpic.html#blogid=5027agtnq&url=http://s4.sinaimg.cn/orignal/5027a397h7d6964ada983&690]

(5) Determine the couple's feelings.

The final step is to find the interpretation of the couple's emotional position. For example: On the thirteenth day of the ninth lunar month in 2009, the birth day gan zhi is "wu shen," the Na Yin is "earth," and the couple's emotional position is in the "Guan Dai" position, which means: the couple can grow old together (exceptions apply if there are conflicting Bazi), they take care of each other, there are no household worries, and the husband is a middle-class gentleman.
