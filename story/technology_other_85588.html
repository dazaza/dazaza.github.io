Eight Types of Women Who Are Most Likely to Cheat After Marriage

by dineng on 2008-08-18 13:07:05

I once asked a married woman, "What do you think about extramarital affairs?" Her response was, "It's a need." When I asked her why it was a need, she said, "A need is a need." Perhaps every woman who has an affair has her own reasons, as categorized below. However, if the other side of happiness isn't happiness, one should still think twice before acting.

1. A broken string with no one to hear –

A talented and charming woman marries a wooden or muscular man. He will never have mysterious feelings about falling leaves or autumn winds, nor about flying snow and blazing suns, considering such actions ignorant and laughable. In your dreams, there has always been an elegant scholar with whom you can ride through life together, appreciating flowers and moons. One day, this person quietly arrives, seemingly an inescapable fate.

2. You're crazy and I'm dumb –

You can't find any moral flaws in him. But it's hard to capture the vitality of life from him while you are always brimming with passion. He often finishes things hastily, and you remain silent. The originally strange man lets you smell his strong breath and hear his vigorous heartbeat. You cannot help yourself, discovering the joy of being a woman from him, and of course tasting pain too. A force seems to be tearing you apart, and you need to find another force to glue you back together, but where is that force? What is it? You don't know.

3. Poor couple, hundred sorrows –

No matter what, he is a good man, and he treats you well. Once, you thought that was enough, willing to start from scratch with him, like swallows building their nests bit by bit, and indeed, that's what you've done. One day, you experience luxury cars and fragrant carriages, stay in glittering luxurious hotels, and you, like a gradually shrinking fruit, suddenly absorb enough water to look fresh again. You collapse completely. Could it be that you are a woman who can be lured by material wealth?

4. Drunken Wu accent, mutual charm –

Your husband is a hero in his career, invincible, envied by many. However, in his eyes, you occupy a low proportion. He briefly shows affection when idle, but when busy, you're just a speck of dust. Remember a line: "Drunk with Wu accent, mutually charming, whose old man and woman are these?" You understand why Helen left the brave Spartan king, willingly following that young Paris to Troy.

5. That smile’s charm –

Actually, everything is so simple; you just can't resist his fatherly or brotherly warm gaze. Your man, after marrying you, treats you as his natural mother and an unnecessary paid nanny. He behaves like a capricious child, able to expose his greed, ignorance, and laziness in front of you, and takes this as a symbol of love, as a sign of the birth of family affection. Initially, you also thought so, but one day you get tired. Thus, he appears in front of you as if arranged by heaven.

6. Suddenly looking back, that person is at the dimly lit place –

Originally, you thought that person was just a fantasy of your teenage years, non-existent in reality. The reason for marriage was actually due to age pressure; at that time, you thought that even if the person in your dream existed...

7. Green mountains beyond green mountains, towers beyond towers –

Where are the best men? They won't be around you, they won't be in sight, they are always far away in the unknown distance. The next man is always the best, this is your clear logic, existing in the hearts of many women but never publicly announced. Like a madman, no one knows what you want, not even yourself, yet you keep searching until the day your youth fades.

8. Lonely Chang'e spreads wide sleeves –

Although your husband is good, he is far away. The man in front of you may be average, but he gives you a tangible warmth. You cannot refuse, spreading your long sleeves to embrace him. Sometimes you feel ashamed, yet you cannot stop.

The word "affair" used to sound somewhat jarring, but now, no matter who talks about topics related to affairs, it all seems very natural, as simple as getting dressed or eating meals. Although discussions about people and events related to affairs still carry some mystery, everyone views affairs as a natural occurrence. Is it society that is changing, or are people changing?
