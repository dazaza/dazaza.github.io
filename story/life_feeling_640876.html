Thank you for flirting with my boyfriend.

by zhouzhou56 on 2011-12-22 16:23:36

As the saying goes, no matter how good the meat is, if you eat too much of it, you will still miss the taste of bok choy. So, when you made up your mind to seduce him and he was ready to fall for it, I didn't feel surprised at all.

In one's life, one will meet many people, but he chose me. He was willing to announce in front of all his good friends that I am his woman. However, because he works in engineering and has to attend social engagements day and night, and I am busy with my online store and have no time to care about other things, gradually, this silent distance between us led to a third party.

Men can't resist temptation, and my boyfriend is also a normal person, so he couldn't resist it either.

I love him, but what I pursue is that the person I love can be happy. If one day, you two meet and find that you are more suitable for each other, then why should I still be in the middle?

If you are not her, he will eventually come back to me. I can tolerate any fatal flaw of his because I love him. I can stay by his side when he is at the lowest point of his life. Can you?

I was concentrating on my little shop when he came over and said to me: let's break up. I thought he was joking. Shocked, I stopped typing on the keyboard.

"I've fallen in love with someone else" he didn't dare to look into my eyes. "How much do you love her?" "Very much..."

"When did it start?" I tried hard not to get angry...

"Six months ago, she is a tour guide, very kind and simple, easy to satisfy." When he said this, there was a glimmer of light in his eyes, and I felt heartbroken...

Recalling the days we spent together, I really felt heartbroken, but what can I do now? Let everything go according to your wishes. He looked at me, full of guilt.

Before the breakup, I invited him out for dinner. After a few drinks, he started talking more and even told me about that girl voluntarily.

"She is very innocent, even small things can make her happy. Shopping with her, winning a soap in a lottery; taking her to eat Beijing dumplings; giving her a 20-yuan electronic watch, buying her a soil cake... She would be ecstatic. Being with her, I feel very relaxed. I can smoke and fill the room with cigarette smell, I can play mahjong all night, drink with friends..." He was drunk in his own happiness, full of tenderness in his eyes.

And me, like all the yellow-faced wives, carefully calculated every expense, questioned every penny he spent, compared prices when buying a pair of socks. I didn't allow him to smoke, prohibited him from drinking, and opposed his gambling.

"Being with her, I feel my heart racing, doing anything fills me with strength." He was obviously already a bit drunk.

I interrupted him: "From now on, I am no longer your yellow-faced wife, no longer your unpaid maid. I can save the time I spend ironing your clothes and matching your ties to dress myself up; I can use the money I save from buying your clothes to buy myself some decent fashion items; I don't have to rack my brains searching for N ways to cook fish, I don't have to cater to your stomach, if I want to eat, I'll cook, if I don't want to cook, I can go eat fast food; I don't have to worry about you smoking and hurting your lungs, drinking and hurting your liver; I don't have to wash the messy sheets you vomited on; I don't have to cry and search the streets when you get drunk and sleep in some corner of the street; I don't have to worry about which relative of your hometown is celebrating a birthday today or getting married tomorrow, I don't have to send living expenses to your parents every month; I don't have to sit in the car for half a day, carrying big and small bags, walking more than ten miles of mountain road, just to have a New Year's Eve dinner with your parents... Ah, breaking up is really great!" After saying these words, tears streamed down my face, and he just stared at me blankly. I had always appeared very calm, but a little alcohol betrayed my inner feelings. For so many years, who doesn't care about the love they have nurtured for so long?

I laughed again: "Let's break up, see how long you can be proud after the divorce. You love her very much, right? She loves you very much, right? After being together for a few years, see if you will still feel your heart racing when you see her. What she gives you now, I gave you years ago. Go ahead and mess around! When you're done messing around, you'll realize that you've just repeated the path we've already walked."

"Are you drunk?" He looked at me nervously.

"Haven't I been innocent and pure? Haven't I been young and beautiful? I cherished the copper ring, the book, and the bookmark you gave me, knitted gloves for you in the cold. I loved deeply too, but entering marriage, a woman's role becomes complicated. While loving, she takes on a lot of responsibilities. She can't focus solely on loving one person anymore. She needs to take a part of her love to care for her in-laws, another part to care for her own parents, and yet another part to care for her children. The ten parts of love, after the baptism of marriage, only leave seven parts. When another ten parts of love attacks her happiness, she can't resist..."

Holding the hand of a mistress, a warm current surges in the heart; holding the hand of a lover, there are all kinds of flavors - sweet, sour, bitter, and spicy; holding the hand of a wife, it's like touching your left hand with your right hand. Both hands belong to you, after all, a wife is your right and left hand. Cutting your left arm and right leg with a knife, isn't it yourself who feels the pain?

In the end, he insisted on not breaking up.

I asked: Why?

He said, when you are sober, you are not as rational as when you are drunk, nor as smart. You scolded me awake. Indeed, this family is hard-won. No one knows me better than you, and no one can replace your position in my heart. I worked so hard for so long to build a happy family, and I almost destroyed it with my own hands. Wife, I'm sorry! The person who loves me the most has always been by my side, and the person who cannot live without the other is me.

Love is not making love; love is wanting to sleep with her and be pillow companions for life!

Thank you, someone, for seducing my husband.

Thank you, someone, for letting my husband know who he loves the most.
