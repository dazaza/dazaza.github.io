by jtonycpu45 on 2012-02-23 17:18:17

Seeing her mother crying and kneeling on the ground, Xiao Hu also knelt beside her mother, hugging her and shedding tears. The policemen helped the two up. After more than half an hour of mediation, Xiao Hu said to Xiao Wang, "I'm sorry, I can't leave," and then accompanied her mother to take a bus home. The police stated that children should obtain the consent of both parents before going out, and they should understand their mother's painstaking efforts.

Xiao Hu disregarded her mother Hu's opposition and let Xiao Wang stay in her home in Dayang Town. Xiao Wang, who was living with the family, made Hu feel uncomfortable, so she often paid attention to his whereabouts.

At around 1:00 AM that night, in the waiting room of the police office, 20-year-old Xiao Hu told the policeman that in January this year, she met Xiao Wang, who was two years older than her and from Jiangsu, on a social networking site. After detailed conversations, they found that they had both worked in Wenzhou and decided to meet. Not long after, the two confirmed their boyfriend-girlfriend relationship and discussed getting married.

When the policeman asked if it was true, Xiao Wang admitted it with his head down but repeatedly expressed, "The only one who truly loves me is Xiao Hu."

Thereafter, Hu demanded that her daughter break up with her boyfriend, but seeing her daughter cry bitterly, her heart softened. Three days ago, under Xiao Wang's instigation, Xiao Hu decided to elope with him and go back to work in Wenzhou. Their decision was overheard by her mother that evening.

Hu recalled that to avoid further arguments, early on the 4th, she took over a hundred yuan, packed a quilt, and came to the train station square first, waiting for her daughter to appear. Unfortunately, her mobile phone and money were stolen. Speaking of this, she cried to her daughter, "Your mother has no money left, and I've only eaten a piece of bread and drank a bottle of water all day. Can you bear to leave me behind?" Saying this, she knelt in front of her daughter.

According to the Morning Post, in order to win back her unfeeling daughter's heart, Hu, over 40 years old, had spent several days eating bread and drinking cold water, keeping watch at the entrance of Hefei Train Station. At around 0:00 on December 6th, at the check-in gate of Hefei Train Station, Xiao Hu, who planned to elope with her boyfriend, was finally intercepted by her mother. Hu knelt down in front of her daughter with a thud, and Xiao Hu immediately knelt down and cried together with her mother.
