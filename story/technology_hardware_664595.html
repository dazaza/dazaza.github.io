Sanitation workers picked up a handbag and more than 10 people falsely claimed it, reflecting the chaos of moral failure

by gangguo77sz on 2012-02-13 16:47:31

The incident of a sanitation worker picking up a handbag and attracting more than ten people to falsely claim it reflects the chaos of moral failure.

【Incident】Xu Fuying, a sanitation worker in Wuhan, found a handbag while cleaning the road. The bag contained 3300 yuan in cash and other items. She searched for the owner along the road, during which more than ten people watched and falsely claimed the bag. Some even reached out to try to rummage through the bag, but were all identified by Xu Fuying.

According to China National Voice's "News纵横", "If I gave the wallet to you, what would I give to the real owner when they come?" This is what a female sanitation worker said to those who falsely claimed the bag. A few days ago, Xu Fuying, a sanitation worker in Wuhan, Hubei, found an environmental protection bag containing a brown handbag with 3300 yuan in cash and documents inside while cleaning the road. Thus, she searched for the owner along the road, shouting, "Who lost something?" However, more than ten people watched and falsely claimed the bag, with some men even reaching out to try to rummage through the bag. Eventually, with the help of the Urban Management Bureau, Xu Fuying contacted the real owner of the handbag.

Commentary: Originally, this could have been just a story about finding lost property and returning it, where the simplicity and kindness of Sister Xu warmed people's hearts like a spring breeze; however, the appearance of more than a dozen false claimants dramatically reflected the awkwardness of moral failure in the face of "material desires." A lost handbag is also a touchstone for morality, allowing us to see the beauty and ugliness of human nature.
