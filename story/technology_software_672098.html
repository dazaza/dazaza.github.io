"Nosy Mom" Awaits Reunion, Pursues Plastic Surgery Aid in Shanghai

by aiipii12987 on 2012-02-22 15:38:43

, www.elikeme.com "Noseless Mother" Longs for Reunion, Seeks Plastic Surgery Assistance in Shanghai. The Lunar New Year of 2012 was a time for family reunions. In a shabby rented apartment in Baoshan District, Shanghai, Zhong Li and her husband simply prepared a few dishes to celebrate their third New Year after marriage in a somewhat cold manner. A set of empty bowls and chopsticks were placed on the table for their over one-year-old son, Leilei. Before the New Year, her mother-in-law came from Sichuan to Shanghai and took her son back to her hometown in Sichuan. This was the first time Zhong Li had been separated from her son for so long.

"As my son grows up day by day, I'm afraid that when he understands, he will know that his mother has no nose..." Zhong Li said softly. The 22-year-old woman in front of us, wearing a peach-red coat, was pretty, except for her nose with only one nostril that stood out awkwardly on her face.

Her Nose Was Bitten Off By A Rat As A Child, And The "Noseless Daughter" Was Not Accepted By Her In-Laws. When Zhong Li was just a few months old, her parents went out to work in the fields and left her alone at home, where her nose was bitten off by a rat, turning her into a "noseless girl." At the age of 16, Zhong Li followed the villagers and worked as a female worker in a garment factory in Fujian. At 19, she fell in love with Xiao Kuang, a fellow worker from the same factory, but was strongly opposed by his family who forbade them from seeing each other. In 2010, despite opposition from their families, they came to Shanghai to work together and held a simple wedding in Shanghai. Soon after, their son, Leilei, was born.

After the birth of their son, this small family was filled with warmth. Zhong Li and her husband worked hard to give their son a beautiful home. Zhong Li also hoped that her in-laws would accept her and her son. However, her in-laws did not accept Zhong Li. For three years, neither of them returned to their hometown in Sichuan.

The "Noseless Mother" Longs for Reunion and Seeks Plastic Surgery Assistance. In December 2011, her mother-in-law suddenly called, saying that she would take Leilei back to Sichuan to raise him. The elder's reason was very sufficient: as the child grew older, when he understood things, he would know that his mother had no nose and would be ridiculed by others. These words hit Zhong Li's weak point. Although she was extremely reluctant to part with her son, she could not find a reason to refuse. On the one hand, there were her parents, and on the other, there was her beloved wife, leaving Xiao Kuang in a difficult position.

After discussion, the two decided to let their parents take Leilei back to Sichuan first, while they began their long journey to seek medical help. "Even if we have to borrow a lot of money, I must help her fix her nose," said the usually quiet Xiao Kuang, glancing back at Zhong Li. Zhong Li's greatest wish is: after fixing her nose, she will take her son back and gain the acceptance and recognition of her mother-in-law.

We interviewed Professor Jiang Hua, an authoritative expert in plastic reconstruction. Professor Jiang Hua stated that for people with large or complete nasal defects, nasal reconstruction surgery is required to repair the defect through flap transplantation, which can restore the shape and function of the nose to a certain extent. Generally, it is done in two stages, starting with a flap expansion surgery, with the forehead flap being the best option. An expander is implanted in the forehead, expanded to the required capacity, then removed, and the expanded forehead flap is transferred for full nasal reconstruction.

Professor Jiang Hua pointed out that nasal reconstruction belongs to surgical reconstructive surgery, requiring high technical skills and experience from the doctor. Choosing a professional hospital is very important.

Currently, the reporter is helping Zhong Li contact various hospitals, hoping to help Zhong Li successfully obtain plastic surgery assistance.
