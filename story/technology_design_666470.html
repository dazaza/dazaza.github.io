8 Classic Style Fashion Clothing Combinations

by shenli470a on 2012-02-15 19:40:56

Maybe the fashion gurus all think that 5-inch pants are only for the summer, but in fact, as long as they are well coordinated with tops, 5-inch pants can appear in all four seasons and in various styles. Below, let's learn about eight classic style fashion outfits created by 5-inch pants.

Low-key Fashion Beachwear

The golden bikini is the most eye-catching beachwear. The gray knitted cover-up paired with black Audreys exudes an elegant aura during a leisurely beach trip. Here, the golden sunglasses echo the golden bikini, creating a low-key yet fashionable look.

Fresh and Simple Rustic Style

A plain floral long skirt paired with a vibrant long silk scarf, and Audreys adding the finishing touch, creates the most carefree rustic charm - alluring yet simple. Under the summer sun, it shines brilliantly.

Urban Sophisticated New Silhouette

"Less is more," the perfect pairing of a gray X-shaped short windbreaker with Audreys embodies the modern urban sophistication and chicness. The short-sleeved windbreaker highlights the overall outfit with its fashionable silhouette, while the black five-inch Audreys add a touch of summery casualness to the slightly exposed skin.

Delicate and Glamorous Party Wear

A semi-transparent chiffon shirt-dress, delicate and glamorous, paired with black Audreys, makes any formal occasion stand out. Even if faced with an unexpected banquet invitation, simply choose some golden accessories and a luxurious evening bag, and you'll instantly transform into an extraordinary Party Queen.

African Exotic Style

The exotic African style is always the most eye-catching summer attire. Batik dresses, leather woven sandals, and wooden bracelets complete the ambiance. The black Audreys here perfectly balance the modern touch, ethnic yet not overdone.

Comfortable and Refreshing Travel Wear

The silver short windbreaker emphasizes both practicality and style, paired with a hunting-style short pants and an oversized travel backpack, refreshing white sweeps away the sluggishness and fatigue often associated with travel. The short pants modified by Audreys are comfortable and elegant, with the exposed skin being just right.

Alluring Androgynous Wear

Wearing your boyfriend's white shirt, this outfit acclaimed as the most neutral yet sexy now has an outdoor version. Black Audreys paired with a neutral tie vest undershirt hidden under the oversized men's shirt showcases a masculine yet alluring female charm.

Vivid Geometric 60s

Exaggerated geometric patterns, bold and vivid colors, lively and interesting shapes, the 60s-originated Audreys suits this forever-in-style 60s look best. The simply designed dress easily conceals flaws under large color blocks, sculpting the desired good figure.
