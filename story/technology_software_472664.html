At many times, we lump corporate leaders and managers together, thinking that managers are leaders and leaders are also managers, not only blurring the concepts but also having a shallow understanding. In reality, if leaders want to possess extraordinary leadership, they have significant differences compared to managers. Leaders and managers (executives) under the trend of organizational flattening see more leading than managing required internally in organizations. Due to historical, cultural, and ideological backgrounds, there are clear differences between what is referred to as a leader in the East versus the West. Eastern leaders tend to be paternalistic, demanding loyalty, responsibility, and obedience from subordinates; whereas Western leaders emphasize adult-style leadership, requiring delegation, team building, and consensus.

Adjusting your leadership "type and style"

Generally speaking, leadership styles typically fall into five categories: laissez-faire, benevolent, authoritarian, middle-of-the-road, and integrative. Laissez-faire: unconcerned about subordinates' situations or unit performance, leaving everything to subordinates; Benevolent: overly focused on interpersonal relationships with subordinates, maintaining good relations by trusting them without strict demands or discipline, and making decisions after consulting subordinates; Authoritarian: goal-oriented, strictly controlling every move of subordinates, issuing all orders personally out of fear of subordinates making mistakes; Middle-of-the-road: equally valuing interpersonal relationships and productivity, but often adopting compromise measures to balance the two, failing to fully utilize the department's maximum potential; Integrative: through fully utilizing management skills and leadership abilities, balancing human relations and performance so that both complement each other and maximize effectiveness. Regardless of the maturity level or job type of subordinates, integrative leadership is the most appropriate leadership style. Leaders should adjust their leadership style according to different situations and needs to adapt to the enterprise and cultivate and expand leadership.
