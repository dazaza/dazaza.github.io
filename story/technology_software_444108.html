The Shelf Life of Love for the 12 Zodiac Signs

by huoe8394 on 2011-06-10 15:11:52

Virgo girls: 1 week

The love of Virgos begins just right and ends just in time. It is not overly resolute, nor does it drag on to the point of heartbreak. Virgos are well aware that if they love too deeply, they will be unable to extricate themselves, and love will become a destructive relationship. Therefore, they set one week as the deadline for love, stepping away before fully committing. Even amidst excitement and passion, reason and practicality survive, and Virgos often capture the peak moments of love.

Libra girls: 1 month

For Libras, love always seeks to reach an unknown feeling, which is also the most accurate definition of romance. One month of love is the best path to the unknown. In the first month of love, both parties try to hide their flaws and present their most perfect side. Perhaps by the one-month anniversary, all illusions may turn around. But Libras prefer only the most perfect posture of love, with infinite possibilities, without caring whether it is an illusion or not.

Scorpio girls: 1 night

Scorpios are sensual, and so is their love. In the hazy night, you cannot see each other's faces or understand the feelings of your lover, mysterious rhythms, complicated. Maybe, when dawn breaks, they part ways, then reminisce like a dream on a bridge. But Scorpios, like Cupid in Greek mythology who fell in love with Psyche but met only at night, may truly understand love, hiding the mysterious postures of love as if they were gods of love.

Sagittarius girls: 1 second

Sagittariuses cannot achieve a quick love, they are not as steadfast as cranes, but they can sensitively collide with love in countless moments, experiencing fleeting feelings. Perhaps, a fleeting glance, a smile, or love at first sight are terms invented by Sagittariuses. They lament the instability of love yet can continuously experience the sensation of being struck by lightning. Among the 28.6 billion seconds a person has in a lifetime, Sagittariuses are fortunate to experience such a famous second: love.

Capricorn girls: 10 years

Capricorns, once determined about something, will put all their efforts into making it work, and naturally, love is one of Capricorn's effort projects. Usually, in the first three years of love, Capricorns slowly warm up, accumulating energy; in the next five years, they strive hard and achieve some results; unfortunately, by the last two years, after much hard work, Capricorns realize that love is a circle. Halfway through, there might still be memories, but completing the full journey and returning to the starting point leaves a sense of blandness. For Capricorns, love is a calamity, fortunately, they don't experience many calamities in a lifetime.

Aries girls: 1 minute

Love exists, even if only for a minute, it’s not blindly arranged. It cannot be a game because Aries are not frivolous playboys; it also doesn't have time to weigh options because Aries cannot calmly direct when it comes to love; Aries also cannot understand its rules, which depend on intuition and true nature. However, because of this, spontaneous and passionate Aries do not have longer-lasting love. For them, one minute of love is the most suitable and beautiful.

Taurus girls: 7 years

Tauruses have a lasting and commendable sense of responsibility, and their love survives under the guise of this responsibility. Unfortunately, love is a habit, while responsibility needs constant encouragement. Responsibility is exhausting and ultimately cannot keep up with love. After seven years, Tauruses start itching, an uncontrollable urge. The love of Tauruses thus has a fixed term, but during these seven years, love may already be riddled with holes.

Gemini girls: 1 day

Geminis' love only requires twenty-four hours, enjoying every sweet moment and then refusing repetition. Geminis' love is realistic; they can completely disregard the future. It is also hedonistic, happiness is always their definition of love. Of course, it is sufficiently brief. However, those who have experienced love with a Gemini will, years later on a sunny afternoon, still remember that day, a warmth that is hard to forget.

Cancer girls: 1 season

Cancers' love is a mix of patience and romance. Watching flowers bloom and fade with their lover, enjoying a scene of life, should be the unity of both. If the time is longer, although Cancers have maternal brilliance, they inevitably fall into mundanity. If the time is too short, Cancers cannot comprehend love. After all, Cancer's love is not passionate; they need slow simmering. Thus, a season fulfills Cancer's love, long enough to recall, short enough to forget.

Leo girls: 1 hour

Enter gracefully, exit perfectly, the regal and authoritative Leos prefer to complete a love without any loss or pain. An hour, from eye contact, to pleasant conversation, to quietly saying goodbye, the timing is perfect, no suddenness, no surprise, no regret. Leos need a love without regrets. In the hustle and bustle of life, occasionally crossing paths, you look at me, I look at you, despite the intense Leo personality, love remains light.

Aquarius girls: 1 lifetime

If one were to choose the most sentimental zodiac sign among the twelve, Aquarius would undoubtedly be it. They are absolute masters of dramatizing boring emotions and indulging endlessly in their own romantic fantasies. Aquarians often make grandiose vows, pledging eternal devotion, releasing their love until the end of time. But in reality, a lifetime of love is just a label Aquarians impose on themselves, it's the ashes left after love burns out, yet they persist in placing these ashes on the altar.

Pisces girls: 1 year

In love, Pisces are a perfect zodiac sign. They pursue but do not demand; cherish but do not cling; grieve but do not suffer; persevere but do not say forever. The love Pisces want is a genuine record, 365 days of laughter and tears. After all, Pisces are always thinking of the other person in love, and their role is often one of companionship. Like the Little Mermaid's love, she only wishes to see the prince happy, considering the passing years a blessing.
