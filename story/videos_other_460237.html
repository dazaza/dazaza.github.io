Migrant worker donates 100,000 yuan after winning 6.22 million yuan lottery jackpot (pictures)

by zzf000zxye on 2011-07-11 13:09:43

Welfare Lottery staff display the 6.22 million yuan winning lottery ticket

The owner of the Yongchuan betting station is visiting and learning at the city's Welfare Lottery Center

"Excuse me, is this where you claim prizes?" At 9 o'clock on the morning of the 17th, a middle-aged man in his thirties rushed into the prize redemption hall of the city's Welfare Lottery Center. After receiving confirmation from the lottery staff, he couldn't wait to reveal that he was Mr. Wang who won the Double Color Ball grand prize last night in Yuzhong District.

An "8+1" compound ticket captures the 6.22 million yuan grand prize

According to Mr. Wang's introduction, on the 16th, while waiting for a friend in Shuixiangzi in Yuzhong District, after half an hour of waiting without seeing his friend, out of boredom, he walked into the No. 40040011 Welfare Lottery betting station located at No. 82 Shuixiangzi, preparing to buy a few Double Color Ball tickets to pass the time.

He chose 8 red balls based on his feeling, then looked at the Double Color Ball trend chart and selected blue ball 03, forming an "8+1" compound ticket.

Early in the morning, he took a taxi to claim the big prize

At around midnight on the 17th, when he saw the Double Color Ball winning numbers in the newspaper, he found they were very close to the numbers he had purchased. He then took out his lottery ticket and carefully compared each number one by one.

"Ah, I've won the grand prize!" Seeing that the numbers on his ticket matched exactly with the numbers published in the newspaper, Mr. Wang felt somewhat incredulous. After taking a deep breath, he double-checked the numbers on the ticket again. There was no mistake; he had indeed won the Double Color Ball grand prize.

He immediately took out his phone and informed his brother about winning the prize. "Are you sure? Don't make a mistake," his brother asked in surprise. "No mistake, I'm going to claim the prize now," he said as he carefully looked at the numbers once more. To avoid any delay, Mr. Wang immediately left home and directly took a taxi to the Welfare Lottery Center to claim the big prize.

Donating 100,000 yuan to care for left-behind children in his hometown

It was learned that Mr. Wang comes from Dianjiang County in our city and currently works in Chaotianmen, Yuzhong District. In his spare time, he enjoys buying Double Color Ball tickets. However, he usually spends only about 10 yuan per purchase. On the day of the win, it happened to be payday, so he chose the "8+1" compound ticket, resulting in winning the 6.22 million yuan Double Color Ball grand prize with a 56-yuan bet.

When asked about the secret to winning, Mr. Wang stated that he believed winning depends on luck and persistence. Since starting to play the lottery 11 years ago, he once won a third prize of 3,000 yuan in the Double Color Ball game. But he never gave up; whenever he had time, he would buy a lottery ticket, believing that one day he would definitely win a big prize.

During the interview, Mr. Wang voluntarily proposed to donate 100,000 yuan to help left-behind rural children in his hometown of Dianjiang. He said, "My wife and I both work in Chongqing, leaving our five-year-old son with his grandparents back in our old home in Dianjiang, which makes him quite lonely. There are many such children (editor's note: rural left-behind children) in Dianjiang. Now that I've won the big prize, I want to help them."

Double Color Ball ignited a "double salvo" the previous evening

Yuzhong and Yongchuan lottery players each won 6.22 million yuan grand prizes

Recently, a cold wave has hit, but the Double Color Ball fever in Chongqing has not abated. 8.32 million! 11.66 million! 12.5 million prizes have successively fallen on our city.

The previous evening, Yuzhong and Yongchuan lottery players in our city once again pocketed the 6.22 million yuan Double Color Ball grand prize. It is reported that these are the 25th and 26th Double Color Ball first prizes won in Chongqing this year.

First prize exploded with 15 notes worth 6.22 million each

On the evening of December 16, the 2010147th issue of the Double Color Ball was drawn, with the winning numbers being: Red balls 02, 08, 15, 18, 24, 30; Blue ball 03.

That evening, the Double Color Ball first prize saw its first small jackpot in nearly 12 issues, with a total of 15 notes worth 6.22 million yuan awarded to lottery players in Beijing, Shanxi, Liaoning, Jiangsu, Shandong, Sichuan, Shenzhen, Zhejiang (2 notes), Chongqing (2 notes), and Guangdong (4 notes) across ten provinces and cities; the second prize saw a large explosion with 342 notes, each worth only 58,000 yuan, with Guangdong, which won 4 first prizes, also securing 81 second prizes, while our city's lottery players also secured 5 notes, distributed across Wanzhou, Jiulongpo, Changshou, Nan'an, and Yuzhong.

Both big winners used the "8+1" compound ticket

Data retrieval shows that our city's two first prizes were claimed by lottery players in Yuzhong District and Yongchuan District respectively. The winning locations were Welfare Lottery Station No. 40040011 at No. 82 Shuixiangzi in Yuzhong District and Welfare Lottery Station No. 40170054 at No. 565 Xuanhua Road in Yongchuan District. Coincidentally, both big winners used the "8+1" compound ticket, with a betting amount of 56 yuan each.

After the draw, the current prize pool still holds 294 million yuan. The Double Color Ball first prize will still offer an additional 5 million yuan bonus during tomorrow night's draw, with a 2-yuan ticket having the chance to win 15 million yuan.

Live Connection:

Station owner helped recommend the 6.22 million yuan winning numbers

On the 17th, the owner of the betting station in Yongchuan District visited the city's Welfare Lottery Center for observation and learning. Coincidentally, Wu Shaoyong, the owner of Welfare Lottery Station No. 40170054 in Yongchuan, which won the 6.22 million yuan grand prize the previous evening, was also among them. Wu Shaoyong said that he knew last night that his station had produced the grand prize winner.

Wu Shaoyong recalled that when the grand prize winner was choosing the "8+1" compound ticket, he had asked what blue ball number to select. Relying on his superior number selection skills, Wu recommended the grand prize winner choose blue ball 03, and indeed, it resulted in winning the 6.22 million yuan Double Color Ball grand prize.
