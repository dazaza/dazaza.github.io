Jiangsu plans to build 16 new charging and swapping stations for electric vehicles

Nanjing, February 18th (reporter Ji Pan Wu) - Among the latest ten strategically emerging industries determined by Jiangsu Province, both smart grids and new energy vehicles are included. Vice President of Jiangsu Electric Power Company, Li Bin, stated on February 17th that accelerating the construction of a smart grid is a necessary preparation for the large-scale development of electric vehicles in Jiangsu.

"The important domestic base for electric vehicles" is the goal set for Jiangsu in this field in the "Special Plan Outline for the Development of Jiangsu's New Energy Vehicle Industry" (2009-2012). Information disclosed this week by the Jiangsu Provincial Development and Reform Commission shows that among the three automobile manufacturing projects approved in the province, there is the urban bus project of Jiangsu Changlong Bus Co., Ltd., with a total investment of 1.79 billion yuan. After its completion, it will have an annual production capacity of 20,000 additional energy-saving and new energy buses.

It is understood that Jiangyin Changlong has been researching and developing pure electric buses for 10 years, updating their products four times. In 2011, their fourth-generation pure electric bus using permanent magnet motors traveled at a steady speed of 60 km/h from Wuxi to Nanjing to participate in an auto show. The entire 190-kilometer journey only consumed 70% of the battery power, with an electricity consumption of 0.79 kWh per kilometer. In the same year, the new technology of the YS6120D pure electric large bus developed by the company was approved as a key new product plan project in 2011, making it the first project in the automotive industry of Jiangsu Province to be listed as a national key new product that year.

Yancheng is also a major hub for the new energy vehicle industry where Jiangsu places high hopes. There are already several new energy vehicle enterprises in the area with certain research and development and production capabilities. It is learned that Dongfeng Yueda Kia has officially started the development of new energy vehicles. One charging mode passenger car and four swapping mode passenger cars have been successfully modified, and a pure electric passenger car model under the joint venture's own brand is currently being reported to the MIIT for the automobile product announcement directory; Zhongda Group has obtained the production qualifications for new energy vehicles, and two pure electric buses have been listed in the MIIT automobile product announcement and recommended subsidy catalog; Aoxin Company has passed the qualification production condition acceptance for modified vehicles and is about to obtain the modified vehicle production directory, with the pure electric series electric passenger cars listed in the national key new product project plan.

As a well-known bottleneck for the development of electric vehicles in the industry, the high cost of batteries and inconvenient charging have long awaited solutions. In Yancheng, the power department cooperated with the local development zone to establish a demonstration operation company for new energy vehicles, attempting a Yancheng model of passenger car chassis battery swapping. This way, citizens purchasing electric vehicles just need to rent batteries at the charging station, return the used battery to the charging station, and then swap it for a fully charged battery to continue driving. Yancheng's charging and swapping infrastructure construction is also proceeding at full speed, with the Donghuan Road Charging Station and Songjiang Road Swapping Station completed and put into operation, and the Nantiao Substation Charging Station nearing completion.

According to Vice President Li Bin of Jiangsu Electric Power Company, the province's charging and swapping infrastructure is also being planned and laid out. In 2012, 16 charging and swapping stations will be completed, including the Nanjing Jiangning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine, Lijiang Road large intelligent charging and swapping storage integration demonstration power station, among which include 4 highway service area charging and swapping stations. Based on the intercity connectivity achieved last year in Suzhou, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, Wuxi and Nantong will also be included.

Li Bin said that smart grids and electric vehicles are also of great significance for energy conservation and emission reduction throughout society. She gave an account to the reporter: assuming that by 2020, the number of passenger-type electric vehicles in Jiangsu reaches 3 million, according to each electric vehicle traveling 10,000 kilometers annually and consuming 8 liters of oil per 100 kilometers, then 3 million electric vehicles can reduce gasoline consumption by approximately 1.75 million tons annually, reducing Jiangsu's external dependence on oil by about 2.2 percentage points.
