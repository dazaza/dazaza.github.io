Haikou Central Urban Area will no longer retain industrial projects; Coconut Tree and Coconut Island will move out of the city.

Date: 2006-01-20 15:57:35 Source: Hainan Daily

On January 20th, the Haikou Evening News reported that on January 19th, it was learned from the Haikou Development and Reform Bureau that after the Haikou European-style printing and dyeing factory moved out due to environmental pollution in the urban area, a batch of industrial projects in Haikou will also be moved out of the central urban area according to the Haikou city plan, moving to some industrial parks in Haikou, including Coconut Tree and Coconut Island enterprises which will all relocate.

It is understood that due to their location in the central urban area of Haikou, some industrial enterprises in Haikou cannot achieve better development, and some enterprises have caused complaints from some citizens due to environmental pollution issues. In response, Haikou City has decided to gradually relocate these industrial enterprises to some industrial parks in Haikou.

Currently, Haikou has basically determined the first batch of industrial enterprises that need to be relocated, including Lishen Coffee, Coconut Tree Group, Coconut Island Company, Modern Color Printing, etc. Among them, Coconut Tree Group plans to relocate to the Hong Kong-Macao Development Zone, while Lishen Coffee, Coconut Island Company, and Modern Color Printing are preliminarily planned to relocate to the Lion Ridge Industrial Park.

According to relevant officials from the Haikou Development and Reform Bureau, the relocation time of the first batch of relocation enterprises and the handling of the original land use are currently under discussion. From now on, Haikou will also continue to require some industrial enterprises in the urban center area to relocate. (Luo Xia, Xie Xiangrong)
