Leping Procuratorate: Striding Towards a Mountain-Water Garden Ecological City

by guangliana5923718 on 2012-02-04 10:58:37

At the beginning of the new year, Shu Daofu, one of "China's Top Ten Outstanding Jiangxi Businessmen", enthusiastically visited the urban construction projects with a group after returning to his hometown for the CPPCC meeting in Leping. Wherever he went, he saw newly developed neighborhoods with towering buildings and lush green trees, newly completed parks with beautiful scenery and flowers in full bloom, and newly constructed wide and straight roads that were well-connected in all directions. The scenes before him made him exclaim: "I haven't been back for more than a year, and Leping has become so beautiful!"

The just-concluded year 2008 was the fastest developing and most effective year in the history of urban development in Leping. Taking advantage of the opportunities to create a provincial-level garden city and health city, the city followed the overall development strategy of "administrative northward, commerce eastward, industry southward". It improved the urban infrastructure focusing on urban road network, parks, water supply and drainage, garbage disposal, and greening and lighting. It comprehensively promoted the construction of the northern part of the city and the Donghu New Area, and moderately renovated the old urban area. The city arranged more than 20 key projects including new district development, old city renovation, market construction, affordable housing projects, urban roads, and landmark buildings.

To broaden the financing channels for urban construction, the city gradually pushed all urban infrastructure that could be put into market operation to the market, activated various types of capital elements, revitalized existing land, and actively promoted vehicle economy and real estate industry to maximize the utilization and development of urban resources. Through methods such as investment attraction, government investment, and social fundraising, last year's urban construction completed an accumulated investment of more than 8 billion yuan, an increase of 68% over the previous year.

Clear urban construction ideas and sufficient funding have quickly brought about significant changes in Leping's urban appearance. The urban road network framework has been fully expanded, greatly improving the traffic conditions within the city. Roads such as Tianhu Road, Dongfeng Road, Min Dian Road, Construction Road, and Hubin Road are fully connected, forming a "five verticals and six horizontals" urban road pattern that is well-connected in all directions; high-standard planned residential areas such as Phoenix Century Huacheng, Dadi Sunshine, Jin Hui Lan Ting, and Julong Plaza compete in beauty, creating harmonious and livable environments. In the whole year, the real estate development investment reached 540 million yuan, with a construction area of 550,000 square meters; urban public facilities are increasingly complete. Facilities such as technical investigation, criminal investigation, vehicle inspection, road management, disease control center, and cultural museum have been successively built. The administrative complex centered around the people-serving service center, as well as the long-distance bus transport company, bus company, Donghu affordable housing, cultural center, and People's Hospital are being expedited in construction. While accelerating the construction of new areas, the renovation of the old urban area focuses more on improving functions and facilitating the public. The city invested more than 3 million yuan to renovate 15 old streets and alleys with a long history into culturally refined premium alleys. It also renovated 20 public toilets in the urban area, built 3 new garbage transfer stations, and added 1,000 streetlights. More than 10 new bus stop shelters were built, some buses were updated, and the installation of streetlights on urban trunk roads and most alleys was completed, with the streetlight brightening rate reaching 98%.

During the process of urban planning and construction, the city particularly emphasized the creation and protection of the ecological environment. In 2008, it invested more than 50 million yuan, utilizing the natural conditions of being surrounded by mountains and water, to build urban leisure ecological gardens such as Tianhu Park, Donghu Park, and Nanhe Park, planting 4,500 ornamental trees. It also invested more than 30 million yuan to plant large seedlings such as camphor and Du Ying on both sides of newly built roads like Dalian Road, Dongfeng Road, and Jingying Expressway connection lines, making them green scenic lines. Since last year, the built-up area of the city has added 10,000 square meters of green space, increasing the greening rate by 23%. Meanwhile, an ecological park integrating mountains, forests, and grass slopes has been designed and will soon start construction, which can be completed within the year. Additionally, to strengthen urban environmental protection, the newly built environmental monitoring building and urban garbage disposal site have basically been completed, and the urban sewage treatment plant is accelerating its progress. Following the goal of "building gardens in cities, building cities in forests," Leping is advancing towards becoming a mountain-water garden ecological city.
