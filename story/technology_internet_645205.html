Lessa - the most environmentally friendly pump truck

by ftleisa on 2011-12-27 11:37:57

Foton Resa's National IV standard pump truck, as China's first National IV standard self-own brand pump truck, is making a push towards new industry heights. In recent years, by mastering and breaking through in core self-own technologies, Foton Resa has quickly established its professional and solid market image. The National IV 52-meter pump truck launched this time has established the "three mosts" of Chinese pump trucks.

"It is the most environmentally friendly pump truck in China at present. The National IV emission standard has set a new benchmark for environmental protection in Chinese pump trucks; its pumping height reaches 52 meters, making it the highest pumping height domestically produced special chassis pump truck in China so far; in terms of performance, among the same 52-meter pump trucks, Resa's pump truck has the widest and highest range of concrete placement, and the largest pumping volume, making it the most advanced 52-meter pump truck in China so far," said Fan Xianjun, President of the Pumping Division of Foton Resa.

An even greater advantage of Resa's pump truck lies in its "integration" advantage derived from the Foton Motor Group. "What we mean by integration is: integrated development, integrated manufacturing, and integrated service. These are our three treasures to win the market. So far, most of China's pump truck manufacturers produce the chassis and superstructure separately, making it difficult to achieve seamless integration in development, manufacturing, and service. Foton Resa is different. Both our chassis and superstructure come from the Foton Motor Group, allowing us to achieve full-process integration from development to manufacturing to service, reaching new heights in pump truck technology, quality, and service," explained Fan Xianjun.

According to the plan, the core annual production capacity of this starter element will include 150,000 tons of automobile starter castings and 150,000 tons of automobile chassis castings, covering two main bodies of starter casting consumption and automobile chassis casting consumption, implemented in three phases. Phase one and two will focus on starter casting consumption with a capacity of 150,000 tons; phase three will be for automobile chassis casting consumption with a capacity of 150,000 tons, totaling 300,000 tons of capacity. Starter castings mainly satisfy the needs of Aoling starters, Leiwo starters, Foton Cummins starters, Chasing Heavy Truck starters, and SAIC Group starters.

Meanwhile, the second phase technical project of Foton Resa's pump machinery publicity factory will include the construction of a combined pump truck workshop, expansion of existing groups, and renovation of plant projects. After the project renovation is completed, it will achieve an annual standard production capacity of 9,000 special vehicles and a minimum annual production capacity of 10,600 vehicles. Among them, the standard annual production capacity of pump trucks is 500 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 600 vehicles; the standard annual production capacity of mixer trucks is 6,000 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 7,000 vehicles; and the standard annual production capacity of automotive mining self-dumping trucks is 2,500 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 3,000 vehicles.
