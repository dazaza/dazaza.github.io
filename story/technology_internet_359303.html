The love for beauty is inherent in everyone; beauty is a symbol of women, and every woman wants to be gorgeous. However, to achieve an enchanting effect, one must have a good figure. To maintain a good figure, daily life must pay attention to avoid obesity. To prevent obesity, it is necessary to lose weight. The term lose weight sounds good, but it's hard to do. How can one achieve a very good result in losing weight? Let's listen to what the experts say:

 

 

    In normal physiological conditions, most people are accustomed to three meals a day. The body's greatest expenditure occurs during the morning. Since the stomach has been empty after a night of digestion, if breakfast is skipped, then all the activities throughout the morning rely entirely on the dinner from the previous day, which far cannot meet the nutritional health needs. At lunch, when eating hastily due to hunger, the amount consumed often exceeds the total of breakfast and lunch combined, causing excess calories that are stored as fat within the body, leading to weight gain. Many obese individuals skip breakfast to lose weight and slim down, especially among young women. Doing this long-term can easily lead to acute gastritis, gastric dilation, acute pancreatitis, coronary heart disease, myocardial infarction, etc. If you eat late-night snacks, it will produce excess energy, with the remaining energy being converted into fat and stored, leading to weight gain. Therefore, not eating anything within three hours before sleep is the most ideal weight loss and slimming method, paying special attention to avoiding alcohol and meat-based foods.

 

 

    During weight loss, there is a principle: "Eat well in the morning, half full at noon, and sparingly in the evening." Breakfast should be nutritious, with nutrient-rich foods such as milk, eggs, and soybean milk being appropriately consumed. For lunch, animal proteins can still be eaten, generally choosing white meats that are less likely to cause weight gain, such as chicken, fish, shrimp, and rabbit. Eating until about 70-80% full is sufficient. Dinner, compared to lunch, can be more vegetarian, and the intake should ideally be controlled to around 50% full. Additionally, no food should be consumed after 8 PM. Another point is that during weight loss, regardless of how your diet is arranged, avoid overly greasy foods like fried foods, puffed snacks, and high-calorie carbonated beverages. Here are some recommended low-calorie vegetables and fruits, such as cucumbers, baby bok choy, tomatoes, watercress, green peppers, etc., which are relatively low in calories. Choose bone-in or shell-on items, not minced meat; 8. Eat fish with bones; 9. Eat shredded meat dishes, avoid large pieces of steak, pork chops, or chicken cutlets; 10. Remove the outer layer of batter-fried foods; 11. Rinse off the starch sauce with water before eating; 12. Prefer fruit over juice; 13. Chew thoroughly; 14. Eat only at the dining table; 15. Do not eat while watching TV or chatting; 16. Do not use your stomach as a trash bin for leftovers; 17. Brush your teeth immediately after eating; 18. Do not stock up on snacks at home; 19. Immediately pass on received cookies or cakes to others; 20. Do not go shopping when hungry. Reasonable weight loss principles based on your actual situation, create a weight loss plan. Persisting with these practices will definitely yield results. Also, consider eating smaller, more frequent meals: mornings with milk + fruit, lunch with a little rice + fruit + vegetables, and evenings with just vegetables + fruit.
