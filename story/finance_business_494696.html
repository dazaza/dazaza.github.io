I just focused on typing these characters and forgot to use the camera to capture some poetic pictures. Well, let this pile of messy words leave a little evidence of life.

On the way to work in the morning, the sky was still gloomy and clear with afterglow, but suddenly foggy, covering the sky, visibility dropped sharply, only a few meters away things can be seen clearly, trees, roads, buildings... all disappeared in the thick fog.

A heavy fog can close highways and make planes stop flying and landing. The inconvenience brought by dense fog to transportation is self-evident. The car lights are on, the speed drops, and on the road, drivers begin to be reckless.

Slowly, the thick fog gradually dissipated, and the sky appeared bright again. The fog comes and goes without a trace, it's really strange and magical.

The thin mist like gauze makes everything in the world look lively and exquisite. Watching flowers in the fog, you will appreciate the beauty of ambiguity, walking through the fog, it's like being in the Penglai fairyland, experiencing the beauty of vitality.

Suddenly, I remembered a book I read many years ago called "Flowing Fog", and I still have some vague memories of the plot in the book. Memories are beautiful. Sunny days can make the mind calm and ambiguous; and flowing fog can also bring warm memories to people.

I think that autumn is a profound season, and the clear weather is always on the face of Miss Autumn. In this season, you can almost see the sunrise from the east every morning. Although there are often unpleasant things in life, I still believe that life is full of sunshine.

Fog, a common natural phenomenon. The lushness of the fog, the ethereal lightness of the fog, is amazing and surprising.
