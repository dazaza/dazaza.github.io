Energy Shortage: Straw Gasifier Takes Action

The energy shortage is no longer an empty phrase; it's approaching us. The alarm bells are ringing incessantly. It is imperative to be prepared and replace non-renewable energy with new energy sources. The central government has issued relevant documents to strongly support enterprises engaged in the research and development of new energy. Beijing Zhongke Jiamai Technology Development Co., Ltd. actively responded to the Party's call, collaborated with prestigious universities, and organized a large number of scientific and technological personnel specializing in the research and development of new energy sources. After long-term market research and development, they successfully developed a series of gasifiers (http://www.zkjm168.com), including: "composite material gasifier, direct-burning energy-saving stove, general combustion type and infrared straw gasifier, heating type straw gasifier, commercial multifunctional straw gas gasifier," which have received praise from peers in the industry.

Zhongke Jiamai's straw gasifier (http://www.zkjm168.com) uses biomass as raw material, including: rice straw, cotton stalks, wheat straw, bran, corn stalks, sorghum stalks, rice husks, fruit shells, tree branches, sawdust, wood shavings, weeds, and various plant vines, stems, and leaves; waste residues from pharmaceutical plants, sugar plants, and tobacco plants, etc. Straw for producing biomass gas is a renewable resource, readily available with almost zero cost for raw materials. It can be used for boiling water, cooking, heating, bathing, and is suitable for various gas needs such as restaurants and canteens.

The installation of stoves is done through ordinary pipelines, arbitrarily installed. There is no need for steel bottles, and the combustion is safe and reliable (no pressure). A unique secondary filtration device ensures that the gas burns without blackening the pot, without smoke, and without any dust emissions. CO emission rate is below 0.1. When in use, simply add the crop straw directly into the furnace, and within 1 to 3 minutes, clean gas will be produced. Only 3-5 kilograms of straw per day are needed to meet daily requirements, saving more than 70% compared to ordinary firewood stoves.

When in use, electronic ignition is simple, allowing instant lighting when needed, with fierce and adjustable flames, blue flames reaching temperatures above 900°C, comparable to liquefied gas. A cigarette butt or a piece of newspaper can ignite it. Once lit, it can be used for a long time, with fire retention lasting up to 72 hours or more.

The straw gasifier uses advanced gas purification technology, pressure-free high-temperature cracking gasification technology, scientifically producing gas, leading domestically in quality and reliability. Clean, hygienic, energy-saving, and environmentally friendly, each new gas stove retails between 150-360 yuan, ready to use immediately without additional facilities, convenient and economical, easily accepted by average households.

The awareness of the energy crisis has deeply penetrated everyone's mind. Saving energy is a must. The phenomenon of consuming our ancestors' resources and depriving our descendants' livelihood no longer exists. Due to the increased public awareness, the straw gasifier (http://www.zkjm168.com), with its safety, environmental protection, and ability to realize renewable resources, has become a leader in the industry since its market launch.
