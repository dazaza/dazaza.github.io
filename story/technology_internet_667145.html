Brother and sister decline property boss's adoption and care for their critically ill uncle (photo group)

by oiwbognb on 2012-02-16 15:03:37

Reporter: Why don't you want to be adopted?

The mother loves the two siblings and even cooks for them, though it's not very tasty. After going five or six times, Qiu Lin and her younger brother realized that their new stepfather wasn't too welcoming. To avoid causing more trouble for their mother, the siblings rarely visit her anymore. Qiu Lin said they haven't seen their mother in almost half a year, and she must miss them dearly.

"Oriental Red, the sun rises..." Suddenly, Uncle Qiu Daquan started humming a song. "Doctor! Doctor..." Qiu Lin dropped her pen, stood up immediately, rushed out of the ward, and ran to the doctor's office. Qiu Mingxing panicked, climbed onto the bedhead with his left hand holding his uncle's hand, and with his right hand wiping the sweat off his forehead.

Deeply missing their mother

Regarding the current situation of this family, Zhou Wei, the Party Secretary of Pusun Town, said that Uncle Qiu Daquan has been provided with a low-income subsidy, and the two children have been identified as key assistance targets in the town. The principal of Jinhua Primary School in the town, Li Bihuai, said that Qiu Lin and her brother both attend this school, one in fourth grade and the other in second grade, both performing well academically. The school has already exempted their study fees and plans to adopt a foster care approach to take care of the two children.

Qiu Lin: We've never had milk or yogurt, we want to drink them, but what we want most now is for our uncle not to give us away.

Uncle is named Qiu Daquan, 62 years old, the two children are his second younger brother's children. The older sister is named Qiu Lin, 10 years old, and the younger brother is named Qiu Mingxing, 7 years old. Last year, their second younger uncle passed away, and their mother remarried, so the two children followed Qiu Daquan.

Qiu Daquan lives in Yujin Village Group 7, Pusun Town, Dianjiang County. In the Qiu family, he is the eldest, and Qiu Lin's father, Qiu Daozhi, is the second youngest. The Qiu family is very poor, still crowded into four low, humble mud houses.

Festival "gift"

Father deceased, mother remarried

Last year, during the second lunar month, Qiu Daozhi died from late-stage liver cirrhosis. Qiu Lin said that not long after her father's death, her mother remarried through an introduction. Later, there were people who came to adopt them. Qiu Lin said, although their parents are gone, their home is still here, and they don't want to leave. The siblings begged their uncle desperately, and eventually, they were allowed to stay.

Qiu Mingxing: We take care of him, cook, wash clothes, massage his back, help him walk, both my sister and I can do these things.

Yesterday was Children's Day. The sick uncle said he would give them a festival gift — to give them away. The siblings cried.

"I really want to fight with mom over chicken legs again." Qiu Lin bit her lip and said that actually, her mother still cares about them. From their home to her new home is only a half-hour journey, later when the siblings missed their mother, they went together to see her.

Chongqing Evening News reporter Peng Yun, photography report

Uncle wants to give the siblings away

Planting eggplants, planting corn, harvesting rice, the siblings vie to help their uncle work. Gradually, Qiu Daquan couldn't live without these two children, so he stopped mentioning giving them away.

Qiu Daquan suffers from heart disease, emphysema, and uremia, and has been hospitalized three times this year. His two daughters work in Guangdong, and his wife divorced him again. Last week, Qiu Lin took a week off to take care of him, but he sent her back to school. On Children's Day, which was a one-day holiday, Qiu Lin and her brother came to the hospital to accompany him.

This year, Qiu Daquan's condition worsened, and on May 3rd, he was admitted to the Dianjiang People's Hospital. Qiu Lin took leave and followed him into the city to serve her uncle. On the 22nd, Qiu Daquan's illness recurred, and Qiu Lin, who was cutting wheat, followed him into the city again to take care of him for a week.

After their father's death and their "mad mother" remarriage, they lived with their blind and disabled uncle. Now, their uncle also suffers from several diseases such as heart disease, emphysema, and uremia, and the siblings carefully wait by their uncle's side, waiting for him to stand up again.

"What kind of gift?" Qiu Mingxing was very curious, he wanted to buy a toy. Uncle touched the hands of the siblings and said, through a good person's introduction, there is a real estate boss in Dianjiang City who wants to adopt them...

"If I really go, what will you do?" After the pain subsided, Qiu Daquan muttered. Seeing the siblings silent, Qiu Daquan said, "Today is Children's Day, let me give you a gift."

"No!" Qiu Lin firmly said, and Qiu Mingxing also frowned. The uncle said, the people are rich, no worries about food and clothing, and in the future, they will send the siblings to university.

Reporter: Today is Children's Day, what do you want most?

Yesterday, in the second area of the inpatient department of the Dianjiang People's Hospital, room 8 of the neurology and respiratory department, bed 22. An elderly man lay quietly in bed, blind and missing his right arm; at the bedside were two children, the girl was diligently doing her homework on a stool, while the boy jumped around the bed restlessly.

Carefully taking care of their seriously ill uncle

Qiu Lin takes care of her uncle in the hospital, bringing him water and feeding him medicine in the morning, getting up early to buy buns and steamed bread for her uncle; when he gets intravenous drips, she quietly stays beside him, ready to call the nurse if needed; in the evening, she takes her uncle's hand and walks outside.

Begging their uncle to keep them

"To be good means you won't be given away." Qiu Lin told her brother, to stay in this home, they shouldn't upset their uncle, and should do more housework.

Qiu Mingxing sweetly recalled, every time her father sang in the market and came home, he would always buy chicken legs for the siblings. "Qiu Lin, Mao'er (Qiu Mingxing's nickname), you didn't bring me anything to eat." Upon hearing this, their mother would stand aside shouting and grab a chicken leg and run.

We want to drink yogurt

"Still want to fight with mom over chicken legs"

Facing the broken family, why don't Qiu Lin and her brother want to leave this home?

In fact, Qiu Daquan's life is also very difficult. In 1987, in an accident, his right arm was blown off by explosives, and his eyes were also blinded. Later, his wife divorced him; after his two daughters grew up and married, they worked outside and hadn't returned home for nine years. Qiu Daquan relies on feeling his way around for eating, dressing, and working. Can he support these two children?

Qiu Lin said before her father passed away, he told her that her uncle's eyes and hands were not convenient, and he borrowed money everywhere to help pay for his treatment, owing more than 20,000 yuan in debt. Her father asked them to remember their uncle's kindness and take good care of him.

Qiu Lin said after her mother remarried, there was a period of calm at home, but after some time, they still missed her, always feeling that even if she was mentally ill, it was better than having none. Later, the siblings heard from their relative Liu Daigui that their mother often shouted at her new home: "Qiu Lin, Mao'er, come home and eat meat!"

The siblings still said no, Qiu Mingxing turned his face, tears streaming down. Qiu Daquan said, he really doesn't want to give the children away, but because of his illness...

The siblings are very sensible

Now, apart from taking care of their uncle, Qiu Lin and her brother also miss their mother, even though she has mental illness.

Qiu Daquan said that Qiu Lin and her brother are very stubborn, but he is really worried about their future. Zhou Yizhen, his third sister-in-law, has been working outside for many years and recently returned home to take care of the whole family. She said, Qiu Lin and her brother are indeed pitiful and sensible, but without giving them away, this family cannot continue, her house is still a mud-tiled house, and her son is 26 years old and hasn't settled down yet...

A patient's family member, Deng Fayan, said that these two children are loved by everyone, and anyone would want to take them home to raise them. On the 25th, local businessman Pi Zhi met Qiu Lin, finding her smart, capable, and especially sensible, planning to adopt this pitiful little girl. After learning this, Qiu Lin refused Pi Aunt's kindness, saying she would not separate from her uncle and younger brother.

"Hurt and sing songs." Qiu Lin said, every time her uncle sings, he sweats. Every time they hear the singing, the siblings know that their uncle is having an episode, and they try to help alleviate his pain.

Qiu Lin said that when her uncle was sick, she called her two sisters more than ten times, each time they said "I'll be back in two days," then they said "I'll return after getting paid," and just a few days ago, they said they wouldn't return.
