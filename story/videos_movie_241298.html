Behind the Scenes:

According to Korean media reports, the new weekend drama "The Conditions of Happiness" (tentative title) by SBS has been renamed "Love You a Thousand Times" and is about to go into production.

On the 23rd, the head of the production company for this drama said, "It has been confirmed that actress Lee Soo Kyung will play the female lead, while the male lead is still being selected. As filming must start by mid-July, the selection for the male lead and other main characters will be finalized soon. The title has been changed from 'The Conditions of Happiness' to 'Love You a Thousand Times,' which not only sounds better but also fits the content of the drama more closely." The new SBS weekend drama "Love You a Thousand Times" mainly tells the story of Go Eun Sung, a strong-willed law school student from S University known as the "Iron Woman," whose life takes a dramatic turn while trying to find 30 million won for her father who was involved in an accident. "Love You a Thousand Times" is set to begin shooting in mid-July and is expected to air in mid-August or early September.

Fun Facts:

"Love You a Thousand Times" aims to reflect on society's obsession with lineage and foster social empathy regarding adoption. In the drama, Go Eun Sung (played by Lee Soo Kyung), a daughter from a poor family preparing for teacher certification exams, faces her father's illness and financial difficulties at home, urgently needing a large sum of money. She reluctantly chooses to become a surrogate mother, thus beginning a painful journey.

Jung Sang Hoon plays the role of husband Bae Kang Ho, who is deeply in love with Lee Soo Kyung but is drawn into increasing unbearable pain. This is his first time portraying a straightforward and bright character since the beginning of his career.

Additionally, Lee Hye Sang plays the role of mother-in-law Kang Suk, who doesn't trust her son-in-law after he has a child with another woman and insists that her grandson Se Heon (played by Ryu Jin) inherits the family property. She single-handedly creates misfortune for the family.

Lee Soo Kyung expresses her aspirations: "Although I was initially troubled by the fact that most people remember my previous image, from now on, I want to completely discard the comedic image from traditional dramas and reveal the hidden aspects of my acting."

Jung Sang Hoon is confident in his character: "Bae Kang Ho is a child born outside of marriage who understands even the mother who dislikes him, making him a character with a highly discerning inner world. I hope to gain the affection of female viewers."
