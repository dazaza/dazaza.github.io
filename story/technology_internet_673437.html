Regarding skin allergies, different symptoms indicate different types of allergies, which require symptomatic treatment. First, one needs to understand four common types of allergic dermatitis.

Seasonal Skin Sensitivity

Main Symptoms: Itching, peeling, red spots, itching all over the body, especially on the limbs.

Some people experience severe skin allergies every time the seasons change: dryness, itching, peeling, and red spots. Skin sensitivity is related to dry skin, environment, climate, age, food, and cosmetics [http://www.jiechuang360.com]. People prone to allergies should pay more attention to balanced dietary nutrition, eat more fruits and vegetables, and less fish, shrimp, beef, mutton, greasy, sweet, and stimulating foods.

After experiencing allergy symptoms, immediately stop using any cosmetics and observe and care for your skin. During this period, do not wash your face with too hot water to avoid irritating the skin, and also pay attention to sun protection.

Cosmetic Allergy

Main Symptoms: Worsening skin allergies, rough skin, appearance of pigmentation, often occurs on facial skin [http://www.jiechuang360.com].

Certain components in cosmetics can cause skin allergies, stimulating skin cells to produce antibodies, thus leading to allergies. If skin allergies are caused by improper use of cosmetics, you cannot randomly use ointments for general skin diseases; otherwise, it may lead to rough skin, pigmentation, and worsen skin conditions.

Neurodermatitis

Main Symptoms: Unbearable itching, commonly occurs around the neck, behind the elbows, buttocks, and sides of the calves.

Neurodermatitis is a common itchy skin disease in adults, generally considered to be related to psychological factors such as emotional excitement, excessive work stress, sorrow, and anxiety. Intense itching is a characteristic of this disease, often leading to thickened skin after scratching, but with clear boundaries and no scales. In the early stages, it appears as light red, and later there is local pigmentation. It commonly occurs around the neck, behind the elbows, buttocks, and sides of the calves.

Peach Blossom Dermatitis

Main Symptoms: Red spots, peeling, with small scales, commonly occurs on the face.

It is a skin allergic reaction where pale red, round small red spots appear on the face, accompanied by itching and slight peeling. Since it usually occurs during the warm and blooming season of spring, it is called "Peach Blossom Dermatitis."

Common allergic dermatitis mostly starts in spring, subsides in summer and autumn, and is more likely to occur in people with allergic constitutions. It is also related to excessive exposure to wind and sun, indigestion, insufficient vitamins, dry skin, and the use of inferior cosmetics. Do not misuse antifungal drugs, as it may worsen the condition.

Skin care should not be neglected even in warm weather

Regardless of the season, patients with allergies need to take extra care of their skin. Besides washing the face three times a day with warm water, they should also use some special therapeutic skin waters and creams to refresh and moisturize the skin consistently.

People with an allergic constitution particularly need to pay attention to dietary adjustments

Patients with allergies should focus on balanced nutritional intake, reducing consumption of greasy, sweet, and stimulating foods, tobacco, and alcohol. Some foods are also allergens, and they need to be identified carefully. Eating vitamin-rich foods can enhance the body's immune system.
