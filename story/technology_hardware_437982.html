Pirates of the Caribbean 4 "Ip Man 2" box office breaks 230 million yuan, booking the Chinese language film box office champion in the first half of the year - China

by letaoeoe on 2011-05-27 12:26:44

Since its preview on the evening of April 27th in 980 cinemas nationwide, "Yip Man 2: Legend of the Grandmaster" has been on for six weeks. According to statistics from the distributor, by June 3rd, "Yip Man 2" had achieved a box office revenue of 232.15 million yuan over a period of 38 days since its release. With the longest screening time and the best public response, this box office performance ranked first among Chinese films released in the first half of this year. It became the only film with a box office exceeding 200 million yuan, breaking several box office records of Chinese films while also securing the semi-annual champion title for Chinese films this year.

Among dozens of Chinese films released in the first half of this year, "Yip Man 2: Legend of the Grandmaster" has achieved a double win in terms of box office and reputation. Producer An Xiaofen expressed that the box office success of "Yip Man 2" proves that the quality of a film is the basic guarantee for gaining market recognition. It also greatly developed the market potential of the May Day holiday season. Due to severe online piracy issues in the second week after its release, the film company lost tens of millions of yuan in box office revenue. However, the performance of 230 million yuan and the good reviews from inside and outside the industry still prove that "Yip Man 2: Legend of the Grandmaster" is one of the best Chinese films of this year. The film company also stated that they will pursue the issue of piracy of "Yip Man 2" to the end.

Directed by Herman Yau, starring Donnie Yen, Sammo Hung, Huang Xiaoming, and Xiong Daolin, "Yip Man 2: Legend of the Grandmaster" broke through 230 million yuan in box office revenue as of yesterday (June 3rd), making it certain to secure the box office championship for Chinese films in the first half of this year. With this achievement, "Yip Man 2" has entered the top ten list of all-time best-performing Chinese films.

According to professional statistics, the total box office revenue for the first five months of this year has reached 4 billion yuan. In the entire month of June, more than 20 films will be released, and the box office revenue for the first half of the year is expected to exceed 5 billion yuan. Breaking 10 billion yuan for the whole year is without doubt. Among Chinese films, eight have exceeded 100 million yuan in box office revenue, with "Yip Man 2: Legend of the Grandmaster" ranking first. The remaining seven films have box office revenues between 100 million yuan and 200 million yuan. Among imported films, "Avatar" tops the list with an astonishing performance of 1.3 billion yuan, while "Alice in Wonderland", "Clash of the Titans", and "Iron Man 2" also have performances exceeding 100 million yuan.
