Say I love you again

1, Nowadays

~~~~~~~~~~~

I am such a woman, as the department manager of a five-star hotel, can be called a senior white-collar worker, with a generous annual salary and decent brands. However, under the bright appearance lies a lonely soul.

I am such a woman, somewhat pretty and somewhat graceful, somewhat competitive and somewhat arrogant, somewhat timid and somewhat selfish. I don't concede defeat in anything, but I am solely a loser in matters of the heart.

My parents named me Le, hoping that I would be happy all my life. And the former Le, shrank back under the shadow of love. Today's Le tastes the bitter wine of weakness alone, silently shedding tears of regret and heartbreak.

There was once a sincere love before my eyes. If I had been brave enough, if I had reached out to grab it, then happiness wouldn't have been so far away. Now thinking about it, what's the difference if it's not forever? What's the difference if it's not eternal? How can someone who isn't even happy at present dare to hope for eternal happiness and peace?

Han, a sunny and excellent boy, a boy who has taken great pains on me, a boy who has been hurt by me a lot, wrote two diaries full of content about Le: the words Le said, the actions Le did, Le's smile, Le's troubles, Le's crying... It tells the story of a charming Le, and it is filled with Han's compassion and longing for Le.

But I hurt Han too deeply, time and time again leaving Han behind, time and time again betraying Han's deep affection. Why do people only realize the value after losing it, only think of cherishing and reclaiming it after parting? But, is everything too late? Has everything become a thing of the past? And I even feel like he just turned around and went back.

Han, are you really going to become my past? Are you really going to become a passer-by in my life? But Le is waiting for you here, waiting and waiting, waiting for you to come back and love Le again!

2, Back then

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Tonight, the stars are twinkling, the breeze rolls up the curtains, and flips open Han's diary. Back then... back then...

Han, you said: "I'm glad to meet Le from the Hotel Management Department of our school, a very cute girl, I like her!"

Han, you said: "Why is she actually the girlfriend of a good friend of mine? Depressing! Too much pressure, because you shouldn't flirt with your brother's wife."

Han, you said: "When playing basketball, as long as Le is watching, I'll move quickly and perform excellently."

Han, you said: "Seeing Le cry makes me feel so bad, I want to punch someone, but I can't, I can only silently accompany her."

Han, you said: "Seeing Le laugh, even if it's not for me, still makes me happy. As long as Le is happy, it's fine to watch from afar."

Han, you said: "After graduation, I have to leave Le. It's best to leave quietly, most afraid of seeing Le's tears."

Han, you said: "They broke up, finally I can loudly tell Le 'Le, I like you!'"

Han, you said: "On January 1, 2007, at 00 hours, I finally told Le that from now on, Han will step out from behind the scenes, not just be Le's friend, but Le's boyfriend. Take that, Le!"

Han, you said: "Le's internship is so hard, starting as a waiter, working until 3 o'clock in the morning every day. To not miss Le, I set an alarm clock so I can send greetings when Le is most tired."

Han, you said... you said... In this diary, you said a lot, some I knew before, some I didn't know before, and found out later.

3, January 1, 2007, 00 hours

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

At January 1, 2007, 00 hours, Han told Le that he liked Le, and from then on, Han started appearing in Le's diary.

In Le's diary, it says: Knowing Han's care, moved by Han's attentiveness. Every time at 3 o'clock in the morning, always receive Han's phone call on time, saying many times not to call anymore, sleep well, otherwise it will affect the next day's class. But Han keeps calling.

In Le's diary, it says: The Spring Festival is coming, cannot go home, staying alone in Xiamen. Even though celebrating in a five-star hotel, still feels quite pitiful. Suddenly, Han from Beijing comes, saying "Le, I've come to spend the New Year with you!" Seeing Han, Le cries.

In Le's diary, it says: Strolling with Han, celebrating the New Year together, walking on the beach, blowing sea breezes. In the sound of the tides, Han promises Le to make a lot of money in the future, never letting Le work so hard again. Le feels a bit confused, a bit heartbroken, knowing that's the taste of happiness.

In Le's diary, it says: Facing such an excellent and affectionate Han, Le fears repeating the same mistake. The previous boyfriend was also outstanding and sunny, also saying he loved Le and making promises. But after graduation, they each went their separate ways, no one gave up or followed for the other. Love is just like a fleeting night-blooming cereus.

In Le's diary, it says: Han said... Han said... Han said a lot, Le just listened silently... Listening but not responding, accepting Han's care silently... Accepting but not giving back.

In Le's diary, it says: Afraid of not being able to bear the burden, afraid of failing, more afraid of losing again.

4, Then, Then

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Then, Le became a deserter of love, abandoning Han again, choosing ordinariness, choosing a very ordinary boy, thinking that would bring stability.

Then, Han handed over his two diaries to Le, saying: From now on, I won't write about Le anymore, unless Le becomes Han's wife.

Then, Le realized that when someone has another person in their heart, they can't be stable.

Then, Le started missing Han, looking for Han, writing a lot of letters to Han, asking for Han's forgiveness, but Han no longer came to see Le, Han didn't read Le's letters or remained indifferent and didn't reply after reading them.

Then, Le kept writing, constantly stuffing Han's mailbox.

Then, Le's work went smoothly, rising from a waiter to the department manager of a five-star hotel. But Han, remains completely out of touch.

Then, today, on such a sleepless night, Le misses Han. Unexpectedly receiving Han's call. Han said he was on a business trip in Qingdao. Such a beach, such a sandy shore, such a coastal city, made Han think of a girl named Le in Xiamen...

Then, Le cried and said: "Dear, can you love me again?"...

Then, Le chases Han who is ongoing... At this moment, let us bless, bless that lovers will eventually be together!
