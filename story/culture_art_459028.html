"Golden Years" Commemorative Album of 345 RMB Notes with the Last Three Serial Numbers Being the Same

by 365zpw on 2011-07-07 10:05:49

After 2011, RMB collection has become a destination for idle funds. In RMB collection, rare item collection is not suitable for everyone. Therefore, as a secondary option, the complete set of RMB collections has become the main method of investment. Among them, the third, fourth, and fifth sets of RMB are the most sought after. Here at Zhengpin Tianda, we recommend to everyone the "Flowing Golden Years" - a commemorative album with the last three digits being the same number from the 3rd, 4th, and 5th sets of RMB.

The "Flowing Golden Years" includes 10 banknotes from the third set of RMB, 9 banknotes from the fourth set, and 6 banknotes from the fifth set, along with 9 coins from the third, fourth, and fifth sets. Additionally, this set also comes with 7 silver miniaturized medals from the third set of RMB, 9 color silver miniaturized medals from the fourth set, and 6 color silver miniaturized medals from the fifth set. At the same time, all banknotes have the following features: the last three digits of the third set of RMB are the same; the last four digits of the fourth set of RMB are all the same; and the last eight digits of the fifth set of RMB are all the same.

The "Flowing Golden Years" has extremely high collection value. The fourth set of RMB is only received and not paid out by banks, and its circulation in the market is decreasing day by day. Therefore, a complete set of the fourth set of RMB with intact condition and identical tail numbers is waiting for the right price to be sold. As the longest circulating set, the third set of RMB is a favorite in the collection circle. Meanwhile, the two yuan and paper corner notes that were discontinued starting from the fifth set of RMB are included in this collection set, making the third and fourth sets of RMB and paper corner coins that have become rare editions even more valuable.

All the trailing digits of the third to fifth sets of RMB collected in this set are three identical numbers, and they are completely the same as the numbers on the collection certificate and the notarial deed, which means their appreciation space is enormous. The additional 22 silver miniaturized medals have exquisite styles and designs, making them the most valuable items for commemoration and collection.
