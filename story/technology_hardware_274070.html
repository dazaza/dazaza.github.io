Putian spends 3 million yuan to build forest ecological tourist attraction

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:26:04

Our city develops forest ecological tourism and has completed an investment of more than 3 million yuan to continue building one national-level forest park and seven provincial-level forest parks.

It is reported that our city vigorously promotes the "Forest Family Leisure Health Tour Activity". Currently, there are 12 officially operational "Forest Families" in the entire city, having received over 28,000 visitors, generating nearly 500,000 yuan in revenue, with a net income of 150,000 yuan.

From January to September, the city's forest ecological tourism received a total of 500,000 visitors, representing a comparable growth of 5%; comprehensive tourism revenue was 6.7 million yuan, showing a comparable increase of 12%, ticket revenue was 4.1 million yuan, representing a comparable growth of 45%. Among these: eight forest parks received 360,000 visitors, generating a comprehensive tourism revenue of 3.3 million yuan, with ticket revenue of 1.5 million yuan.
