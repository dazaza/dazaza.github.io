Introduction to "Online Game: Lord No Regrets"

Every Chinese person has their own martial arts dream in their heart. By using online games as a medium to interpret this dream, the characters from the works of masters like Jin Yong, Gu Long, and Huang Yi are vividly portrayed. The protagonist, Lord No Regrets (Yi Tian), runs through the entire work, which is "Online Game: Lord No Regrets". I hope everyone will like it!
