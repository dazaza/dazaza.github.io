The experience of female students in school earning 6000 yuan per month while attending school and running an online store

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 14:57:02

The online store is open, but there are few orders. I believe this is the most troublesome problem for the vast majority of store owners. Nowadays, opening an online store has become a very popular start-up project, and everyone is in favor of it. However, most people blindly pursue it without detailed research and study on the online store before opening it. They just hear that others earn a lot of money from their online stores, and then they immediately follow the trend and open their own stores. The result of doing so is often that the online store has been open for several months, but no one pays attention to it. Everyone knows how to open an online store, even elementary school students can do it now, but how many people can make money? Therefore, the skills of opening an online store are very important. Only by mastering targeted skills can you stand invincible and stand out under such huge competitive pressure.

Some store owners or beginners often say: "What kind of skills? It's just finding good sources of goods, and then spending more time taking care of the online store. It's normal that I don't make money because I don't know anyone, and I can't get cheap and good quality goods, and I have to work regularly, where do I have so much time to take care of my online store?" Here, the author wants to ask these people: Since you have no time and no good sources of goods, why did you choose to open a store with your own inventory at first? Furthermore, have you done targeted promotion and publicity? Do you check how many people visit your online store every day? Have you summarized the reasons why you are not profitable? If you want your online store to be profitable, you must promote it. Promotion is the most critical factor.

Zhang Qiao is a college student who will graduate soon. Her classmates are all busy preparing for job hunting. She is not worried at all because she already earns a fixed income of about 6000 yuan per month. When she first entered university, Zhang Qiao was just a student who knew a little about computers. Later, under the guidance of her roommate, she gradually came into contact with online shopping and became addicted to it. In her sophomore year, Zhang Qiao had the idea of opening an online store and gradually came into contact with cash-back online stores. Because apart from regular promotions and advertisements, such online stores do not need to worry about anything else. The key is to do well in promotion and advertisement. This business model is exactly what Zhang Qiao hopes for. She doesn't have much capital and also needs to attend classes, so buying goods and opening a store definitely isn't suitable for her. With the help of her roommates, Zhang Qiao contacted the online store developer and purchased a set of online stores.

After the online store opened, Zhang Qiao's first target for promotion was the students in the school because there were too many students in the school who liked online shopping. Every day, those express deliveries ran non-stop to the school. When Zhang Qiao promoted her online store, she told her classmates that her online store could help customers save money when shopping on nearly 200 well-known shopping websites. Who wouldn't want to save money? Moreover, if there's business, of course, you would take care of your own people. Almost all the classmates shopped at Zhang Qiao's online store.

But Zhang Qiao knew that the school was just a small market. To make more money, she still had to rely on the Internet. In the following time, Zhang Qiao used soft articles to promote her online store. She expressed the service of her online store that could save money through articles and posted them on many targeted shopping and consumption forums and websites. Combining online and offline approaches, the number of registered members of the online store increased rapidly. Plus, each time a customer shopped and got the saving service, the return rate of customers who shopped at her online store was almost 100%. In the first month, Zhang Qiao earned a commission of 2145 yuan. As the promotion efforts increased, Zhang Qiao's income became higher and higher. Of course, all this was done during her spare time. Zhang Qiao knew that her main task was still studying. So she spent 1-2 hours promoting after class every night.

Nearly three years passed quickly, and Zhang Qiao's savings were already close to 100,000 yuan. During these more than two years of schooling, she never took a penny from her family, and all the harvest was achieved through her own efforts. Zhang Qiao often says: "If you want to succeed in doing something, the premise must be perseverance. Another is to master the skills. Opening an online store is the same. Only in this way can you ensure that you exchange the maximum profit with the lowest cost and the least time."

Indeed, blindly following trends will only make you lose. The order volume of online stores is important for traffic, traffic is important for promotion, promotion is important for skills and persistence, and persistent targeted promotion can make money even as a part-time job without laying down goods. Since I started developing cash-back online stores, I have always adhered to this view. Through countless customer cases, this view has also been verified. Regarding experience and knowledge about online stores, I welcome everyone to communicate and discuss with me.
