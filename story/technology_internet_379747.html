Overclocking Wizard: Details of the Overclocking Three South China Sea 6 Exposed

by diglog on 2011-01-24 17:05:28

A good radiator is not just about cooling performance; appearance and manufacturing processes are also extremely important. The latest high-end product from Overclock3, the South Sea 6, excels in both product details and craftsmanship, combined with its powerful performance, making it highly favored by overclocking enthusiasts.

(1) Refined craftsmanship

South Sea 6 External Packaging

The South Sea 6 packaging primarily features a blue and white color scheme, simple yet stylish, with a unique perforated design that offers a more direct display of the product.

Overclock3 South Sea 6 Radiator

The overall dimensions of the South Sea 6 are 161mm (L) x 137mm (W) x 102mm (H), weighing 1040g. It adopts a dual-tower design with a centrally placed fan, paired with 6 efficient 6mm heat pipes and a pure copper base. The fin cooling area reaches up to 8382cm². Additionally, it is equipped with Overclock3's distinctive 12cm blue LED PWM smart speed control fan, supporting installation in three fan positions. Its powerful performance fully meets the cooling needs of overclocking enthusiasts.

Stylish top design

The top of the South Sea 6 features an "X" shaped design. As can be seen in the image, a refined Overclock3 metal logo is embedded in the center of the cover. These designs contribute significantly to enhancing its visual appeal.
