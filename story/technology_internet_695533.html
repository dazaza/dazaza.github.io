I. What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

1. SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is a popular form of online marketing in recent years. Its main goal is to increase the exposure rate of specific keywords to enhance website visibility and thereby increase sales opportunities. It is a technique that allows a website to secure a favorable ranking position on search engines. Through the understanding of search engines by SEO service providers, after conducting user analysis, page optimization, and data adjustment for the website, its search engine ranking can be improved, thus increasing website traffic and ultimately enhancing the website's sales capacity or corporate brand recognition.

2. Why do corporate websites need SEO?

The first source of site visits is search engines. Search engines are currently the most important way for users to look for information, products, and services. Nearly 90% of a website's traffic comes from search engines. As a method of website promotion, SEO has the best return on investment compared to other forms of online or offline marketing models.

3. Market Prospects of Baidu Fengchao Plan SEO

With the full implementation of Fengchao, Baidu will have three sponsor links at the top and bottom of the search results on the left side, and all original bidding customers will be moved to the right side. However, the easiest thing in the internet industry is to cultivate user behavior habits, while the hardest thing is to change user behavior. It is easy to let future new internet users accept the promotion information on the right side when they go online, but it is difficult to let the current 330 million Baidu users change and recognize. These changes by Baidu may significantly impact future promotional revenue and could also lead to a large-scale outbreak of optimization behaviors targeting Baidu, which will see many companies competing in the SEO market.
