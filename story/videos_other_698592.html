Man dissatisfied with strict parental discipline sets fire to home

by yanh181rxue on 2012-03-09 10:16:40

Dahe.com news (reporter Song Xiangle) On December 10, Zheng, a 19-year-old youth from Guanzhuang Town in Nanyang City, was often subjected to strict parental discipline due to repeated mistakes. In order to vent his dissatisfaction, he actually bought gasoline and set fire to his own home. His father and younger sister, who is less than 7 years old, were burned, and his mother jumped from the building and was injured.

At around 2 o'clock in the morning on December 10, a fire broke out at a resident's house on Bar Street in Guanzhuang Town. The family of three living upstairs were all injured to varying degrees. After receiving the report, the police immediately rushed to the scene for rescue, and the fire was extinguished half an hour later.

After investigation, this fire caused more than 10,000 yuan in economic losses. Based on the strong smell of gasoline at the scene and an unburned plastic canister, the police determined that this fire was deliberately set by someone using gasoline as the igniting material. Moreover, the suspect used a key to open the security door of the residence to commit arson, then locked the security door behind them before fleeing the scene.

The victim couple told the police that their 19-year-old son Zheng had a key to the security door. It was later learned that because Zheng had repeatedly made mistakes and refused to be disciplined, he had been expelled from the house that day. Additionally, someone saw Zheng buy a small can of gasoline that evening. As a result, the police arrested Zheng and eventually captured him at a small hotel.

According to Zheng's confession, his parents usually disciplined him very strictly, and when he made mistakes, their approach towards him was simple and brutal. That day, after making a mistake, he was driven out of the house by his parents, and his enraged father even claimed that he would sever the father-son relationship with him. To vent his hatred for his parents, Zheng took advantage of his family's deep sleep early on the morning of the 10th, quietly opened the security door and entered the house, poured the gasoline he had purchased earlier in the living room on the first floor, ignited it, then locked the security door again and fled the scene.

Currently, the 19-year-old suspect Zheng has been criminally detained. (Information provided by Jianfeng)
