Shop entrepreneurship how to choose management software

by nemo on 2010-09-17 13:27:18

With the continuous penetration of the Internet into all aspects of people's lives, e-commerce has been the rapid development, followed by the continuous entrepreneurial boom brought by the Internet, Taobao as the representative of the online store has become the mainstream, and even many entity business operators have opened online stores. However, most Taobao shop owners lack experience in business management and operation, internal management chaos is very common, and a lot of work remains in the manual stage, such as: Manual inventory, manual filling in the express form, manual record online shop order details, manual accounting transactions, which often lead to the wrong goods, missed delivery, increase in after-sales disputes, inventory difficulties, goods out of stock or backlog, unclear accounts and other problems, at the same time, repetitive labor occupies a lot of time, so that the shop's operating efficiency is low. In fact, some successful Taobao stores use management software to replace most of the manual management, so that the human from the complicated and trivial work free, put their energy on data analysis, market investigation, network marketing, customer relationship management and other more valuable work to go. According to statistics, the use of management software that meets their needs can save more than one-third of the time every day, that is, improve the work efficiency by 30%. The larger the size of the online shop, the more obvious this improvement effect. Then, what kind of management software is more suitable for such as Taobao store to e-commerce transactions as the core of the online shop enterprises? Online shop as one of the most common forms of e-commerce, although the essence of its management is to buy and sell goods, but compared with the traditional store management, there are still many differences, it is these differences, the formation of management problems, hinder the growth of online shop (Taobao store for example) : 1, order difficult to manage: Orders come from online shopping malls, merchants do not conduct face-to-face, one-to-one transactions with buyers; The order quantity is large, and the quantity of the kinds of goods sold is complicated. It is easy to make mistakes in the delivery process and miss the delivery, which affect the corporate image and reputation; 2, logistics difficult to manage: most of the orders are off-site transactions, need to be shipped through logistics express, so it is necessary to fill in a large number of logistics express orders and delivery details list. At the same time, there is also a need to settle logistics costs with logistics express companies; 3, difficult inventory management: the number of online goods and local actual inventory may be out of sync, resulting in inventory management problems, such as out of stock, or backlog; 4, the process is difficult to manage: the shop is in urgent need of process control. The sales customer service is responsible for communication and receiving orders, the warehouse manager is responsible for picking and packaging and shipping, and the financial department is responsible for collection and payment. How to assess and motivate, and how to coordinate the connection of various links? 5, personnel difficult to manage: the shop grass-roots staff turnover rate is a common problem, new employees to the post need to learn period, learning period is the high incidence of mistakes. In the current domestic online shop management software, there are not many management software that can really help shop owners solve these five major problems. Either the purchase-sales-inventory function is not perfect, or does not meet the characteristics of the online shop operation, or the software design is complex, difficult to learn, difficult to use, many shop owners are still struggling in manual management, wasting a lot of work time in repetitive labor, no time to take into account data analysis and business decisions.
