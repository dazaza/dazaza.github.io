Honey, wait for me on the way to heaven.

In October in the south, although it's not as cold as in the north, it's still quite cool at night. Especially today, with the wind blowing during the day, tonight is particularly cold.

There was a girl who worked in a square. She finished work very late every night and the road home was very dark. One day, she was riding her bike home as usual when she was tripped by something unexpectedly. As she stood up ready to leave, someone coughed, which scared the girl badly. The girl pushed her bike and ran away. After running one or two meters, she slowly stopped and stood there motionless. She thought about it and decided to turn back. She saw a boy lying on the ground and slowly approached him. "Hey! Hey! What's wrong?" The girl gently pushed the boy and called out. The boy didn't respond, so the girl called 120. When the ambulance arrived, she was about to leave but was called back by the nurse. Without saying anything, she went back to the hospital with the ambulance. However, before they even reached the hospital, the boy woke up. He was just like a normal person and wanted to get off the ambulance immediately. The nurse had no choice but to let them get off.

Thus, the two of them walked on the road without a word. When they reached a crossroads and were about to separate, the boy said: "Thank you!" The girl replied: "Oh, don't mention it." The boy was stunned for a long time, then squeezed out a few words from his teeth: "Leave a contact number, will you?" The girl laughed and asked: "Do you have a pen? Hey!" The boy didn't react quickly enough. The girl asked again: "Do you have a pen?" "Yes! Yes! Here." The boy looked at the girl and was completely dumbfounded. "Hehe, here, this is my phone number," the girl smiled and said. "Yuan Yuan, hehe," the boy also smiled and the girl asked: "What are you laughing at?" "No! No! This name sounds nice!" the boy said. "Alright, I should go home now," the girl said and was about to leave. "Oh, let me see you off, Yuan Yuan," the boy said somewhat reluctantly. "No need, I'll be there soon," the girl said and left.

Watching the girl's retreating back, the boy thought: If only I hadn't touched that thing back then, how good would it be now? After returning home, the boy never appeared in that place again. And that girl was looking for him everywhere, but in the end, she still couldn't find that boy.

Time passed quickly, a year has gone by. One day, the girl was riding her bike to work as usual. She stopped at the entrance of her workplace where many people were gathered. She also went over to take a look. To her surprise, it was that boy! He was sleeping on the street, shivering from the cold. The girl wanted to take him to the hospital, but he refused to go no matter what. Later, the girl had no choice but to ask a colleague to take a day off for her. The girl took the boy home.

When the girl opened the door, the pungent smell of cigarettes and alcohol made her uncomfortable. The boy's house was in such a mess that it was indescribable, with garbage everywhere. The girl, however, started cleaning up without a word, as if she were in her own home. In no time, the boy regained consciousness. He asked: "How did I get back here?" The boy looked around and found his house clean and tidy, almost unrecognizable. "Oh, I found your address in your pocket," the girl said. The boy stared at the girl, just watching her. Suddenly, the boy hugged the girl from behind. The girl accepted it silently. The girl said: "I don't even know your name yet?" The boy whispered into the girl's ear: "My surname is Qi, Qi Xiaodong." Holding the girl tightly, he said: "Yuan Yuan, be my girlfriend." The girl didn't answer him and rushed out hastily. He didn't chase after her, but sat on the sofa, blaming himself for his impulsiveness earlier, thinking how could he support her when he had nothing?

Not long after, Yuan Yuan came back carrying two large bags of food. Looking at him, she said: "Hey! Why aren't you helping me?" He hurriedly got up from the sofa to help her carry the things. That day, the two spent the day happily together. As the sky gradually darkened, she wanted to go home. The boy couldn't help but say: "Will you be my girlfriend?" The girl laughed and said: "Dummy, aren't we already?" He was extremely happy, hugging the girl and spinning her around for a long time, then shouting loudly: "I have a girlfriend! I have a girlfriend!!" After he put the girl down, she smiled and said: "Dummy!! Will my husband send me home?" The boy asked: "Ah! What did you call me just now?" The girl teased: "Qi Xiaodong, send me home!" "Not that sentence," the boy said mischievously. "You're bad! Hehe!" Then the girl ran away. She turned back at the gate and said: "Hey! Aren't you really going to send me home?"

The boy sent the girl home. Just as he got home, he had another episode. His whole body rolled around on the floor, looking very painful. He kept enduring, not wanting to touch that thing, but finally couldn't bear it anymore. He pulled out a white object from his pocket and put it in his mouth. Soon after, he returned to normal.

He had no job, idling around all day, and the things in his house were quickly consumed. One day, his phone rang, and a message read: "Husband, do you have time? I'm off today, come play with me." At that moment, Qi Xiaodong was extremely excited and quickly replied...

The boy hurriedly arrived, only to hear the girl laughingly say: "Hey! You're late for the first time, you're punished!" "How am I punished?" the boy asked. "You're punished by pushing my bike," she said. "Okay!" Qi Xiaodong accepted the punishment. The girl laughed, and they were both very happy. In the evening, the boy took the girl back to his place. Once home, the boy was busy preparing. He laid out the bed for the girl, fetched water for her to wash her face. Seeing this scene, tears welled up in the girl's eyes. She thought: He must be a good man. After covering the girl with a blanket, the boy said: "Honey, goodnight." Then the boy went back to his own room.

Yuan Yuan woke up in the middle of the night and heard some noise coming from Qi Xiaodong's room, so she went to check. What she saw shocked her - a grown man curled up on the floor, rolling around. Yuan Yuan tried to approach him, but he wouldn't let her. Watching him suffer so much, she eventually moved closer anyway. As soon as she got close, before she could react, he grabbed her hand and bit it fiercely like a mad dog. The girl said nothing, enduring the pain, allowing him to bite her like that. Gradually, he calmed down and fell asleep. Slowly, the girl carefully placed his head on her lap and sat on the cold floor. The girl also fell asleep.

When dawn broke, the boy woke up and saw the deep tooth marks on the girl's hand. The boy was utterly devastated, bursting into tears and slapping his own face repeatedly. The girl woke up and hugged the boy tightly, crying. "What's wrong with you? Can you tell me? Can you?" The boy wiped the tears from the corner of the girl's eye and said: "Once, a friend brought me to a bar and put drugs in my drink. I didn't know, I drank it, feeling very relaxed, like I was floating. Later, I couldn't stop using it. Every month's salary went into it. Now, without a job or money to buy more, every time I have an episode, I lock myself at home. During episodes, I lose control, biting and smashing things like a mad dog, sometimes even hurting myself." After hearing this, the girl buried her face in his chest. When the boy picked up her hand, he once again shed tears.

Later, with the girl's care, the frequency of the boy's episodes decreased, though they still occurred occasionally. Once, he had another episode, worse than ever before. He knelt in front of the girl, begging her to buy him some heroin. The girl had no money, but seeing her beloved one in such agony, she still went. She knelt in front of the drug dealer, pleading for a little mercy, but the dealer only recognized money. As the boss was about to leave, she said one sentence: "If I trade my body for it?" The boss laughed and nodded. They entered a room...

The girl quickly ran back to the boy with the heroin, lightly kissing his forehead, then left. Back home, she continuously washed herself with water, feeling dirty all over. Later, she remembered a rumor: Rumor had it that only washing oneself with all their blood could truly cleanse. So she filled the bathtub with water, cut her hand slightly with a knife, and placed it in the tub... Soon, her lips turned pale, and the water in the bathtub turned red. She realized this wasn't a rumor; death was cleanliness, death meant no more worries.

When the mother opened the bathroom door, Yuan Yuan was already gone from this world. The news quickly reached Qi Xiaodong's ears. Right after Yuan Yuan's funeral ended, the next day, there was a crowd gathered at Qi Xiaodong's doorstep. Inside the house lay a corpse, it was Qi Xiaodong himself. On his body rested a piece of paper, with just a few words written: "Honey, wait for me on the way to heaven, you'll get lost without me, you'll feel lonely without me, wait for me..."
