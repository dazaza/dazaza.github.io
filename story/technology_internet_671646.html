Violation inquiry www.biyaode.com On February 17, the provincial transportation union work conference was held in Changsha. The chairmen of transportation unions from various cities and prefectures, units directly under the department, and the provincial highway system attended the meeting. Chen Yongping, director of the Transportation Union of Hunan Province, delivered a work report titled "Highlighting Industry Characteristics, Strengthening Union Brands, Uniting a Broad Range of Employees to Strive for Steady Progress in Hunan's Transportation."

The work report summarized last year’s efforts from four aspects: combining with actual conditions (such as Xiamen motor vehicle violation inquiries) and earnestly conducting pioneering excellence activities; focusing on central tasks and organizing merit-establishing activities; building harmonious labor relations; consolidating work foundations, and enhancing work capabilities and levels (such as Jiuquan motor vehicle violation inquiries). The report also outlined this year’s work tasks in terms of widely and deeply carrying out labor competitions, launching model “Employee Home” and “Worker Vanguard” creation activities, further improving the management and service of labor models, safeguarding employees' legitimate rights and interests, maintaining stability within the workforce, and strengthening self-construction.

The conference distributed "Key Points of Work for the Hunan Provincial Transportation Union in 2012" and the "Implementation Plan for the 'Face-to-Face, Heart-to-Heart, Solid Service for Employees at the Grassroots Level' Activity." The meeting also commended advanced units (collectives) and individuals in the 2011 provincial transportation industry "Transportation Cup" labor competition. Representatives from the provincial road administration bureau, provincial expressway administration bureau, Xiangtan municipal transportation bureau union, Yueyang municipal traffic union committee, Xiangjiang shipping construction and development Co., Ltd., Hunan transportation planning survey and design institute, and Shaoyong Expressway Co., Ltd. union shared their union work experiences at the conference (such as Chizhou motor vehicle violation inquiries).
