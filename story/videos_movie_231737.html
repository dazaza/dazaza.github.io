The story tells about Go Eun Yu, a law examinee from S University with strong living abilities and known as "Iron Woman". In order to raise 30 million Korean won for her father who was involved in an accident, she chooses to be a surrogate mother for others, which reverses her life.

Episode 1:

Since the day of his birth, Woo Hyuk was abandoned by his biological mother. His twin sister shared the same fate. Later, Woo Hyuk was adopted by an Australian family, but misfortune struck again when he was abandoned once more. Growing up toughly in a foreign land without familial love, Woo Hyuk led an abnormal life. In Australia, he met his first love, but she eventually betrayed him and embraced another man's arms. On the day of her wedding, Woo Hyuk protected her by taking two bullets for her. The bullets hit his head, blood soaking his white shirt, forever changing his life...

Episode 2:

After recovering from his injuries, Woo Hyuk became even more violent and cold. With the money and endless guilt left by his first love, and a bullet still lodged in his brain, he returned to South Korea - if he had to die, he would rather die in his homeland. Upon returning to South Korea, Woo Hyuk met his sister whose IQ remained at a child's level and who was struggling hard to take care of her nephew. Their shared destiny reconnected the siblings. Through a tip-off from someone in the know, Woo Hyuk met his biological mother. Seeing his wealthy biological mother and his handsome, rich half-brother showered with affection, Woo Hyuk's already sorrowful heart became even more unbalanced, and plans for revenge began brewing in his mind.

Episode 3:

Woo Hyuk never expected that Eun Chae, whom he had saved in Australia, was actually the assistant of his half-brother. Yoon, his half-brother, was a popular singer in South Korea, drawing screams wherever he went. Eun Chae was his assistant and a childhood friend. Eun Chae secretly admired Yoon, but Yoon seemed to have eyes only for Min Joo, the actress. His love was simple, passionate, and even willing to die for the woman he loved. Through Eun Chae, Woo Hyuk gradually got closer to his biological mother and that mansion. An unexpected incident saw Woo Hyuk saving Yoon and Min Joo's lives, and from then on, Woo Hyuk started to intervene in this family that he envied and hated so much...
