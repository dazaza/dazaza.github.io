How to light up the QQ Xunwu icon?

by lwf225 on 2009-09-01 14:44:45

Tags: Activate QQ Xuanwu Icon, Activate QQ Icon

QQ Xuanwu icons have a new feature - they can now be upgraded! There are a total of 5 levels. Now you don't have to worry about having the same icon as everyone else. You can reach different levels in Xuanwu and then obtain a new icon to fully display your personality and show off your privileges.

How to activate the QQ Xuanwu icon:

1. First, you need to upgrade your QQ to version QQ2008 or higher.

2. Go to the Xuanwu icon activation special page for operation: http://x5.qq.com/act/a20080528dl/

Rules for activating the QQ Xuanwu icon:

To activate the icon, simply reach level 10 within the game and then go to the official website to operate the activation.

How to upgrade the QQ Xuanwu icon:

When you reach the corresponding levels within the game, you can go to the official website to activate icons of different statuses.

How does the QQ Xuanwu icon turn off:

If you do not log into Xuanwu for 14 consecutive days, the icon will be penalized and turn gray, indicating it is unlit.

How to revive the QQ Xuanwu icon:

After the icon turns off, if you log into "QQ Xuanwu" continuously for 2 hours, you can then go to the official website to operate and revive the icon.

Whoever has the highest level, their Xuanwu icon will be the most dazzling. What are you waiting for? Take action now!
