I have collected the ten most popular webmaster forums among webmasters.

by wap3g on 2008-03-19 12:56:57

Here are the ten most popular webmaster forums:

1. Outdated Webmaster Forum [url]www.im286.com[/url]

Founded in 2001 by "Fish that Refuses to Swim". After seven years of withstanding numerous attacks, it still stands strong with 500,000 registered members and over 20,000 daily posts, gathering webmasters from all levels. For many webmasters, becoming a member of "Outdated" is an honor. As the first home for Chinese webmasters, its future development is worth looking forward to.

2. China Webmaster Forum bbs.chinaz.com

Founded in 2002 by Afly. Through its three main services - "Rank Query, Statistics Service, Code Download", Chinaz is well-known among the webmaster community. Due to Afly's low attention on the forum itself and its overly commercial atmosphere, the Chinaz forum has always lacked outstanding performance matching its fame.

3. Webmaster Trading Forum bbs.admin5.com

Founded at the end of 2006 by Tuwang. Due to Tuwang's influence in the grassroots webmaster circle and a series of effective soft article promotions, the forum reached 50,000 registered members within a year, with thousands of daily posts. Currently, the website mainly focuses on webmaster transaction intermediary guarantees, having certain characteristics.

4. I Want La Webmaster Forum bbs.51.la

Founded in 2005 by Ajian. The Ajian statistics brand has a long history, and the 51.la forum relied on Ajian statistics to ensure extremely high popularity. After the Zitian incident in 2007, the Ajian brand was affected, leading to a decline in forum popularity.

5. China Website Building Forum bbs.jz173.com

Founded in 2004, this old second-tier webmaster site owns its own BBS brand, focusing on resource sharing. It was once prosperous but currently, the forum has started to decline with around 200 daily posts. Due to its early establishment, it holds some influence in the webmaster community.

6. Webmaster China Forum bbs.zzchn.com

Founded in 2007, with the concept of free sharing and cooperative sharing, it attracted many webmasters in just half a year. Within a year, the total number of visits exceeded hundreds of millions, with over a thousand daily posts, making it very popular. However, due to its short establishment time, it lacks brand influence which is its weakness.

7. 171 Webmaster Forum ww.171zz.com

Founded in 2005, under Tianxun Network. The main purpose of the forum is to drive traffic to Tianxun Network and bring in effective customers, making it difficult to develop into a top-tier site, thus staying between second and third tier. It made the list due to its unique features in free resources.

8. Grassroots Webmaster Forum [url]www.236z.com[/url]

Founded in June 2007, mainly providing a place for grassroots webmasters to exchange ideas. In just a few months, it had over ten thousand registered members and more than 500 new daily posts, indicating a certain scale. Due to its long-term provision of free logo design services, it enjoys a good reputation in the grassroots webmaster circle.

9. Chinese Webmaster Forum [url]www.admin258.cn[/url]

Founded in May 2007, after nearly a year of effort, the website developed from a simple personal forum into a comprehensive website construction technology community. With nearly five thousand registered members, hundreds of daily posts, and over a thousand online users, it is one of the more popular Chinese webmaster forums.

10. National Webmaster Trading Forum [url]www.pr888.com[/url]

Founded in 2008 by Guo Ke. After only a few months of effort, the forum already has several thousand members and posts reaching the thousands. Achieving such a level in just a few months truly reflects the spirit of webmasters.
