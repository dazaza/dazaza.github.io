Feeling upset while driving today. Attached: A list of Shanghai LPG gas stations for gas-powered vehicles.

by seosh on 2008-01-23 09:29:05

Today, from January 1st to 23rd in the morning, Shanghai SEO (SWJ) still drove my LPG wife to work. Looking at the gas meter, there was still a bit more than 3/1 gas left, so I thought it would be enough for the drive and I could refill at noon. But when I mentioned refilling, I realized that there was still plenty of time in the morning, so I decided to go refill... Unexpectedly, when we went to the Xujiahui refueling point first, they told us they no longer provide refueling services... Then SWJ took his wife to the refueling station at Xianxia Road and Linquan Road, only to find it under renovation, with a long red line surrounding the refueling area. It was frustrating. I originally thought we could refuel there, but now due to this situation, not only did we fail to refuel, but also my wife's gas is almost gone. Alas... In this environment in Shanghai, I spent over 10,000 RMB on this wife, and now there isn't even a place to refuel... What a pain! As soon as I started work, I found a list of Shanghai LPG gas vehicle refueling stations, and I will take my wife to refuel during the lunch break! Damn it!
