Lesson One: What is Google Ranking Technology?

After years of practice and research, among the dozens of commonly used website promotion methods, Google search engine ranking is the most effective one. Because:

Google is the world's most widely used search engine;

The quality of traffic coming from search engines is very high, with most being your potential customers;

Once you achieve a good ranking on Google, it will continuously bring you customers every day;

Only if you learn Google ranking technology, it will work for you for free, and its effect is better than hiring ten salespeople;

Using Google ranking technology as a foundation, you can develop various low-cost, risk-free ways to make money...

Everyone knows that the rankings on the left side of Google are different from those on the left side of other search engines. With other search engines, you can buy good rankings by paying money. However, for Google, paying money can only buy you the sponsored ad spots on the left side; you cannot buy the rankings on the left side. This is because Google's left-side rankings are naturally generated results based on its unique algorithm. Google aims to provide users with high-quality search results.

Since the marketing effects of Google's left-side rankings are very good and have substantial commercial value, there are dedicated people engaged in researching Google and other search engine ranking technologies. This profession is briefly referred to as - SEO. SEO stands for search engine optimization in English, which means 搜索引擎优化 in Chinese. Those who work in this field are called search engine optimizers. The technology that search engine optimizers use to improve a website's ranking in Google is known as Google ranking technology. This book mainly teaches everyone how to learn this technology and how to use it to make money.
