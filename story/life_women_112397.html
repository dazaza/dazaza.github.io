Breast augmentation with autologous fat

by 865512 on 2008-12-18 10:24:49

Principle of autologous fat breast augmentation: Fat from any part of the body is injected into the breast, allowing it to regrow and integrate with the natural breast tissue, making the breasts fuller and shaped. (The survival rate of thigh fat is the highest.)

Methods of autologous fat breast augmentation surgery:

1. Traditional method: The extracted fat cells are centrifuged and purified, and active fat cells are selected and injected into the retro-mammary space or subcutaneously. Injection is performed through the armpit or under the breast fold, leaving no incision. The injection volume is around 50-150ML. The survival rate is between 50%-60%.

2. Our hospital's method: Our hospital uses special instruments such as endoscopes and microscopes. After magnification, active fat cells can be more accurately selected and a certain amount of active growth factor is added. The surgeon then quickly injects the fat in layers stereoscopically into the chest to achieve a higher survival rate of about 80%. This method is a proprietary technique of our hospital doctors.

Advantages of autologous fat breast augmentation:

1. It is a globally recognized safe, effective, scientific, and natural method of breast augmentation that never needs to be removed.

2. No one can detect the signs of breast augmentation.

3. No rejection reaction.

4. Provides significant psychological confidence.

Suitable candidates for autologous fat breast augmentation:

1. Individuals who have a certain amount of fat on their body.

2. Anyone except those with Grade III breast ptosis can undergo the procedure.

3. Individuals who are in good health and do not have acute or chronic diseases.

Effects of autologous fat breast augmentation: Using our hospital's injection method combined with growth factors, satisfactory results can generally be achieved in one session. However, individuals with poorer base conditions may require a second injection. (Traditional methods require 3-5 sessions.)

Suggestion: For those who want to achieve overly large breasts in one session, we recommend silicone implant breast augmentation.

Postoperative care: Regular systematic massage is not required for 1-2 months, and it does not affect daily life and work.
