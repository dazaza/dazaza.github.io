On Women's Day, let's see which scenic spots are trying to attract customers! Various discounts are free of charge.

by ncgpvrph06 on 2012-03-03 20:46:37

Attractions Discount Information

Dead Sea: From March 8 to March 11, the China Dead Sea offers a special gift for women visitors who can enjoy a discounted ticket price of 38 yuan (the original price is 200 yuan per person). Invite your close girlfriends and experience a journey of transformation at the Dead Sea!

Ya'an Bifengxia Scenic Area:

1. Women visitors to Bifengxia receive an 80% discount on the large animal park scenic area tickets.

2. Women born on March 8 (with ID) can visit the Bifengxia Scenic Area and Panda Base for free.

3. Women whose names contain any of the six characters "Women's Day Bifengxia" (with ID) receive a 50% discount. If two or more of these characters are included in the name, they can visit the Bifengxia Scenic Area and Panda Base for free.

Hailuogou Scenic Area: Female visitors traveling to Hailuogou Scenic Area on March 8, 2012, will be exempt from entrance fees (but must pay 5 yuan per person for insurance and ticket cost).

Jiuzhaigou Scenic Area: Jiuzhaigou Scenic Area offers a "buy one get one free" discount policy for all female visitors. One valid ticket serves as one valid statistical basis. A woman purchasing a ticket for March 8 will receive a free ticket for that day.

Kangding Mugecuo Scenic Area: From March 8 to 11, during the four days of the festival, Kangding Qingge Scenic Area offers free entrance to all women.

Siguniang Mountain Scenic Area: From March 7 to 9, Siguniang Mountain Scenic Area offers free entrance to all women, including free access to the three gorges within the scenic area. Throughout March, all women visiting Siguniang Mountain Scenic Area will enjoy discounts. The Shuangqiaogou discount ticket costs 40 yuan, Changpinggou costs 35 yuan, and Haizigou costs 30 yuan.

Guose Tianxiang Amusement Park: On March 8, the original price of the amusement package ticket for women is 60 yuan (originally 80 yuan). The first 388 female visitors can ride 24 designated amusement facilities for free.

Happy Valley: On March 8, women purchasing Chengdu Happy Valley daytime tickets can enjoy a 60% discount (90 yuan per ticket), and night tickets are half-price. From March 3 to 18, with proof from enterprises and institutions in Chengdu, groups of 10 women can enjoy a 70% discount (105 yuan per person), while accompanying men pay 110 yuan per person.

Haichang Polar Ocean World: From March 8 to 11, women purchasing adult tickets priced at 130 yuan per ticket can enjoy a discounted price of 70 yuan per ticket (limited to women only).

Dagu Ice Mountain Scenic Area: Dagu Ice Mountain Scenic Area offers free entrance to all female visitors on "International Women's Day."

Mianyang Yaowanggu: From March 8 to 11, all women can enjoy a 50% discount on scenic area tickets and a 60% discount on accommodations at the Xinyili Flower Hotel. During this period, yoga wellness performances, free health consultations, customized beauty care plans, and complimentary scenic area beauty products are provided for female visitors.

Jiangyou Dou Tuanshan: From March 8 to November, all women friends visiting the scenic area can enjoy a special discount ticket price of 35 yuan per person.

Wulong Scenic Area in Chongqing: During the 102nd Women's Day, from March 1 to 31, women purchasing a ticket for the Tian Sheng San Qiao Scenic Area or Longshui Gorge Scenic Area will receive a free companion ticket (including scenic area shuttle fees).

Kangding Qingge Scenic Area: From March 8 to 11, the scenic area implements a free entrance policy for all female visitors (original ticket price: 70 yuan per person).

Huaying Mountain in Guang'an: Women born on March 8 can visit the scenic area for free with their ID.

Shunan Bamboo Sea: Women visiting the Shunan Bamboo Sea Scenic Area from March 7 to 9 can enjoy a 50% discount on tickets (57 yuan).

Chongqing Wansheng Black Valley Scenic Area: Launches a "free tour for women" event, waiving entrance fees for all female tourists during Women's Day.

Xiling Snow Mountain: Xiling Snow Mountain has a listed ticket price of 120 yuan per person. Women are exempt from entrance fees, while male friends pay 60 yuan (a 50% discount). The transportation cable car remains full price at 100 yuan per person.

Jiuzhaigou Scenic Area Event Details:

1. The gifted ticket is valid only on March 8, 2012, and will expire thereafter.

2. The gifted ticket is limited to use by women.

3. Travel agencies booking Jiuzhaigou tickets online on the same day implement a "buy one get one free" discount policy for female visitors, with the gifted tickets limited to use by women. Other insufficient tickets still need to be booked online.

4. Guides must present the IDs of the female visitors receiving the gifted tickets to collect them.

5. Female visitors purchasing tickets at the scenic area ticket office on the same day can also enjoy the "buy one get one free" discount policy, implemented by the Jiuzhaigou Administration Bureau Ticket Office.

6. The gifted ticket can only be held by female visitors entering the valley, with the final review conducted by the ticket office entry security.

7. This gifted ticket is limited to entrance fees. Travel agencies and individual visitors must book or purchase sightseeing vehicle tickets separately online or at the ticket office.

8. This gifted ticket does not qualify for the off-season policy of free re-entry.

The Dead Sea is most suitable for visits with close girlfriends! After all, it’s a women-only occasion! Women should enjoy themselves on their own holiday!
