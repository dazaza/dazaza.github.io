Farewell in late autumn has disordered a season of blooming flowers.

The setting sun is like blood, dancing lightly and gracefully, fascinating smiles. The fallen flowers do not understand, the exhaustion of sorrow.

-- Written at the beginning

Swinging an arc of lonely shadow, dimming the silence, fading the coolness of the autumn colors.

A brocade umbrella walks in Jiangnan, stepping on and breaking thousands of years of beautiful dreams. The sound of hooves moves slowly, who can wait for the expectation, or will beauty fade in the vicissitudes of time?

Farewell at the twilight of late autumn, withered leaves fall, taking away the remaining few traces of emaciation and loneliness from the branches. The bitter wind disrupts the predetermined trajectory, blurred tears obscure the whole world,

She still stubbornly believes that leaving is not the tree's abandonment.

Butterflies break for flowers

Flowers fly with the wind

The dance of the seasons conceals an irresistible force, it is the graceful and resolute farewell of the butterfly-like falling leaves.

Tightly holding in her hands is their initial touch, once those passionate vows, steadfast promises, once those forever inseparable agreements, now, do they still count?

Have their beautiful stories been rewritten by the merciless passage of time? Have their unforgettable memories been sealed by the long years?

The smoke of farewell is fixed into an eternal painting. Why does the heart, in disorder, still ache faintly?

The beauty leans on the railing and looks out. She doesn't know when the bronze mirror has been neglected. Covered with a light sadness, a cold and clear light shines through, just like her deeply affectionate eyes.

A woman adorns herself for the one who appreciates her. She has no interest in light or heavy makeup, only willing to live plainly. Music is played for the one who understands, but the soulmate has gone, confiding heart affairs to the zither, who listens when the strings are broken? Even though the yellow flowers spread their fragrance all over the courtyard, what cannot be intoxicated is her calm heart.

The wind continues to blow, disrupting the shadows of pines, making red leaves fall, and breaking the flow of water...

Several times we made appointments in the late spring, with colorful letters and short notes. Several times we met in dreams, with tears spilling onto the remaining red. How many times have I looked out of the window, seeing distant mountains like eyebrows, clouds and mist vast and boundless. How many times have I watched the snowflakes flying everywhere, asking myself, when will be the return date?

Paulownia trees accompanied by drizzle, until the evening, drop by drop. Who is drunk-dancing in the gaze of crimson tear-soaked robes? And who is telling the sorrow in the midst of the wind, flowers, snow, and moon?

Vows of the mountain remain, but feelings have become empty.

The solitary sail disappears in the distance, the music ends, people disperse, leaving only the sorrowful wind and rain. Reunions and separations, joys and sorrows, cry and damage the remaining years, leaving only a dream of prosperity.

The woman with fluttering sleeves drinks cup after cup of the sorrow of departure.

If youth really flows like water, why can't the沧桑performed by time be erased?

If life is really like a clear dream, why can't the dust of the sunset be awakened?

Past events cannot be pursued

A thought of love turns into a layer of ash

In the next life, turn into a lotus gatherer

Meet you again by the pond...
