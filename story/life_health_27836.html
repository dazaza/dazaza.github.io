Foods that affect men's "sexual happiness"

by the9th on 2007-08-17 17:12:05

★ Leek

Leek is not only tender and fresh in taste but also rich in nutrition. In the book "Compendium of Materia Medica", it says that leek replenishes the liver and the Gate of Life, treats frequent urination and enuresis. Due to its remarkable effect of warming and replenishing the liver and kidney, assisting yang energy, and consolidating essence, leek is named as "Yang Rising Grass" in the pharmacopoeia. The seeds of leek are stimulants with functions of consolidating essence, assisting yang energy, replenishing kidney, treating leukorrhea, and warming waist and knees. They are suitable for treating diseases such as impotence, enuresis, etc. By grinding leek seeds into powder, taking 15 grams each time in the morning and evening with boiling water, it is effective in treating impotence.

★ Dog Meat

Dog meat has a sweet and salty taste, warm in nature, and has the effects of benefiting the spleen and stomach and nourishing yang energy. In the book "Compendium of Materia Medica", it records that dog meat can "stabilize the five organs, lighten the body and invigorate qi, benefit the kidney and replenish the stomach. Warm the waist and knees, strengthen qi, replenish the five labors and seven injuries, replenish blood vessels" and other effects. Using black beans stewed with dog meat, eating the meat and drinking the soup, can treat impotence and premature ejaculation. Cooking dog meat with ginger-warmed Aconite can warm the kidney, strengthen yang energy, dispel cold and relieve pain. However, it should be noted that dog meat is warm in nature, excessive consumption may cause heatiness. Those who have hot sores or excessive yang fire should avoid eating it.

★ Loach

Loach contains high-quality protein, fat, vitamin A, vitamin B1, niacin, iron, phosphorus, calcium, etc. It has a sweet taste, neutral in nature, and has the effects of replenishing the middle energizer, benefiting qi, nourishing the kidney, and generating essence. It plays a good role in regulating sexual function. Loach contains a special protein that promotes sperm formation. Adult men often eat loach for nourishment and strengthening the body.

★ Shrimp

Shrimp tastes delicious and has high nutritional and medicinal value. Traditional Chinese medicine believes that shrimp has a sweet and salty taste, warm in nature, and has the effects of strengthening yang energy, benefiting the kidney, replenishing essence, and promoting lactation. Anyone who is weak after a long illness, short of breath, lacks strength, and has no appetite can use shrimp as a tonic food. Regular consumption of shrimp can strengthen the body.

★ Lamb

Lamb is an excellent winter tonic. In the book "Revised Herbal", it says that lamb can "replenish deficiency, invigorate qi, strengthen yang energy, open the stomach and strengthen the body". Eating cooked lamb and drinking the broth can treat male five labors and seven injuries and gastric deficiency impotence, and has the effects of warming the center and removing cold, tonifying blood and qi, promoting lactation, and treating leukorrhea.

★ Egg

Egg is a high-protein food, similar in composition to human protein, so the absorption rate of egg protein by the human body is as high as 99.7% (only 85% for milk). Experts point out that eggs are the best nutritional additive to enhance human sexual function. It is said that Arabs mainly eat scallion-braised eggs a few days before their wedding to ensure a satisfactory first night of marriage. Indian doctors suggest that couples should drink rice porridge cooked with eggs, milk, and honey before having sex. There is also a custom in our country's folk culture of eating fried eggs on the wedding dinner.

★ Seaweed

Seaweed contains more iodine than any other animal or plant, and iodine deficiency or insufficiency can lead to miscarriage, male sexual dysfunction, and reduced libido. Therefore, seaweed foods like kelp, laver, and wakame should be consumed regularly.

★ Fish

As early as the ancient Roman period, people found that fish is an ideal food for enhancing libido, especially shark meat, which still enjoys a prestigious reputation as a "catalyst" for love today. Studies show that fish meat is rich in phosphorus and zinc, which are very important for the health of male and female sexual functions, and is known as the "couple's sexual harmony substance".

★ Scallion

It is said that some ethnic groups in the Balkan Peninsula use scallions in wedding ceremonies, symbolizing hope for the health and happiness of the newlyweds. Modern medical research shows that scallions are highly nutritious. The various plant hormones and vitamins they contain can ensure normal hormone secretion in the human body, thereby playing a role in strengthening yang energy and replenishing yin energy.

★ Honey

Honey contains gonadal endocrine hormones, which have obvious biological activity that can make the gonads active. People whose sexual function has declined due to weakness or old age can persist in taking honey products to improve their condition.
