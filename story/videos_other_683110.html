Autumn Dryness: 5 Hydration Methods to Keep You Moisturized in Fall

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-29 11:48:47

Moisturizing Method One: Hydrating Toner

Every morning when you get up, if you look in the mirror and notice that your pores are still a bit large and your skin is still a bit oily, remember to wash your face and then apply a hydrating toner. After all, our faces have to endure wind and sun exposure throughout the day, so it’s important to take care of your skin in the morning. When applying toner, always use high-quality cotton pads to more effectively help your skin absorb moisture.

Moisturizing Method Two: Hydrating Essence

Beauty therapists often work in enclosed environments, and their secret to hydration usually involves using moisturizing essence. Before applying lotion to your skin, first apply some essence; the lotion will act like a cap, sealing the essence beneath the skin's surface layer, prolonging the time it takes for moisture to evaporate. Additionally, the molecular size of moisturizing essence is smaller than that of lotion, allowing it to penetrate beneath the stratum corneum and nourish dry cells.

Moisturizing Method Three: Hydrating Mask

My skin isn't actually very good to begin with, and due to working in front of a computer all day, my skin condition has been poor for a long time, often feeling dry. However, I still insist on applying a hydrating mask every night. In stores like Watsons, there are various types of masks at reasonable prices. Vacuum-sealed single-use quick masks are simple and convenient, and they are usually soaked in hydrating essence, providing noticeable results.
