Dining and entertainment are saturated, consumers want to buy mobile phones and home appliances in groups

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 12:07:30

The catering and entertainment sectors have become saturated; consumers are more interested in group-buying mobile phones and home appliances.

Report on China's online group-buying survey indicates that consumers prefer group-buying mobile phones and home appliances.

DCCI Internet Data Center recently hosted an industry salon event titled "Group Buying Reality: What choices are users making?" The released report, "Data Analysis of the Thousand Group Battle - China Online Group Buying Survey Report," shows that currently, the main products for group buying, catering, and entertainment, have oversupplied the market. 2.3% of consumers participate only once and then do not want to continue.

2.3% of consumers participate only once

This report indicates that during group buying, consumers face the following issues: 2.3% of people no longer wish to participate after trying group buying once. The primary reasons include the inability to guarantee consumer rights (51.5%), product quality problems (43.1%), inadequate after-sales service (38.9%), and low trustworthiness (34.7%).

Therefore, group-buying websites urgently need to improve product quality, protect consumer rights, and establish website credibility to reduce customer loss and avoid becoming a "one-time" site.

Twenty percent of consumers use mobile devices for group buying

The survey also found that the proportion of consumers using mobile devices for group buying has approached twenty percent, at 18.7%. The percentage is slightly higher for male and white-collar consumers, at 19.7%. Mobile marketing directly targets its audience, thus attracting those who browse group-buying sites with specific purposes. Among those browsing group-buying sites, students account for 17.5%, which is the same proportion as female consumers.

Catering and entertainment sectors are oversupplied

Currently, group-buying websites can roughly meet consumer needs. However, the proportion of consumers wanting to group-buy IT and digital products exceeds the actual group-buying rate. Group-buying websites excessively provide similar products like catering and entertainment, surpassing consumer demand, while the group-buying demands for other goods remain unmet. Among the most desired group-buying products by consumers, IT products, digital products, household appliances, and communication products rank highly.

Monthly expenditure mostly below 200 yuan

Additionally, people with middle incomes between 2001-4000 yuan form the main group of group-buying users. Many group-buying users have monthly online shopping expenditures between 51-200 yuan, but there are also many users whose monthly online shopping expenditure exceeds 600 yuan. Therefore, group-buying websites should set reasonable group-buying product prices based on user online shopping expenditure habits and actual income levels.

By Xu Yuan, Beijing Business Today Reporter
