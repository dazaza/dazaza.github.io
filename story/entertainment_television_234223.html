Hye-na was born into a wealthy family, living a princess-like life from birth due to her family's wealth. But can money buy happiness? Can money really buy Hye-na lifelong happiness?

Her feelings are torn between two people - one is the impoverished butler, the other is the second son of a wealthy family. Whom will Hye-na's heart lean towards?

Logically speaking, the princess and the second son of a wealthy family are the most compatible pair. However, there exists a master-servant relationship between the princess and the butler. Everyday bumps and bruises turn this master-servant pair into a couple of happy rivals. Between love, self-respect, and status, what choices will these men and women of different identities make?
