"Visiting is free, and education is priceless." A visitor who visited the An Yuan Railway and Mine Workers' Movement Memorial Museum for free wrote this in the guestbook. Yesterday, I learned that since the end of last year, our province was the first in the country to promote the free policy for patriotism education demonstration bases. This year, it has been listed as one of the seven pilot provinces for the nationwide free opening of museums and memorial halls. Currently, 84 qualified museums and memorial halls have been opened to the public for free.

According to regulations, among these 84 museums and memorial halls implementing free access, 61 were approved by the central government in 2008 to receive partial funding subsidies for free opening, while the other 23 are fully funded by our province to implement free opening. Our province has allocated 1.24 billion yuan in fiscal funds for this "green observation; escort".

In the future, our province will also push other provincial patriotism bases of museums and memorial halls to open for free at an appropriate time and ensure full funding so that people can better enjoy this free cultural feast.

Statistics show that since the free opening, the number of visitors to each exhibition hall has increased by an average of more than 95% compared to the same period last year. In the first five days after the Jinggangshan Revolutionary Museum opened for free, the number of visitors exceeded 60,000, averaging more than 10,000 people per day. From January 30 to October 5, the Nanchang August 1 Uprising Memorial Hall received more than 1.1 million visitors for free, and during the National Day holiday alone, it received nearly 70,000 visitors, both far exceeding previous reception volumes.

The free opening of our province's museums and memorial halls has also promoted the development of red tourism, with a significant increase in the number of visitors to red tourist attractions compared to the same period in previous years. During the National Day holiday, our province received a total of 9.9627 million domestic and overseas tourists, an increase of 21% compared to the same period last year, with tourism revenue reaching 3.342 billion yuan, an increase of 623 million yuan over last year's National Day Golden Week.
