Liu Yishou Chongqing Hot Pot

by anonymous on 2011-05-11 15:43:22

Chongqing hotpot, which was once a nationwide sensation, is currently searching for its future path. Liu Yishou, a prominent figure in the industry, stated that due to the impact of the Wenchuan earthquake, the profits of the Chongqing hotpot industry dropped by about 40% in May and June this year, leaving hotpot enterprises struggling. Liu Yishou's Hotpot is a pillar of Chongqing's catering industry, with local franchisees once reaching up to 5,000. Why has it suffered such a severe blow now?

Wu Renjun, chairman of Junzhiwei Hotpot in Chongqing, analyzed that the earthquake was just a trigger; the main reason lies in the sharp rise in prices over the past two years. Coupled with increases in rent, utilities, labor, and ingredient costs, the price of hotpot had to be raised. The average spending per person at Liu Yishou's has increased from 30 yuan two years ago to 45 yuan now, leading to a significant loss of customers.

Li Dejian said that the frequency of Chongqing residents eating hotpot is also decreasing. Through random surveys of thousands of samples each year, it was found that two years ago, each Chongqing resident ate hotpot an average of 2.1 times per month, which has now dropped to 1.2 times. The reason is the increase in various types of dishes, while the innovation in hotpot is very limited. Additionally, changes in consumer habits have also affected the development of the hotpot industry. A few years ago, it was common for citizens to hold wedding or birthday banquets at hotpot restaurants, but now, with improved living standards, many feel that eating Chinese cuisine reflects more sophistication.

In response to the difficult situation faced by the entire industry, Chongqing's hotpot entrepreneurs are all worried and seeking solutions to save Chongqing hotpot.

Firstly, they hope that Chongqing hotpot can achieve overall marketing. For example, outdoor advertising campaigns at key locations in Chongqing and displaying "Chongqing, the Capital of Chinese Hotpot" on CCTV weather forecasts could enhance the visibility of Chongqing hotpot. Even if broadcast for only one month, the effect would be significant, according to the hotpot bosses. Zhou Keqin, Director of the Chongqing Municipal Commerce Commission, expressed that the idea of promoting Chongqing hotpot will be included in the agenda.

Secondly, the third Safe Hotpot Evaluation Activity will be held. The Chongqing Hotpot Association will collaborate with the Municipal Health Bureau to conduct city-wide evaluations of numerous hotpot brands. Non-compliant hotpot stores will be cleaned up and hit, guiding consumers towards healthier hotpot consumption. Currently, this activity has already started. At the same time, the evaluation of star-rated hotpot restaurants has been initiated to standardize the market. The preliminary criteria for evaluation, including pricing, service, decoration, and kitchen standards, have already been established.

Additionally, the plan for this year's Ten-Thousand People Hotpot Banquet has been confirmed, set to take place on Nanbin Road between October and November. To enhance the image of Chongqing hotpot, this festival will be newly designed and packaged, inviting participation from across the nation through the collection of mascots and multi-day events.

Chongqing hotpot needs innovation

Liu Ke, director of the Chongqing Business纵横Research Institute and researcher at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, analyzed that Chongqing hotpot requires major breakthroughs, expansion, and development to welcome a new spring. In terms of products, diversified development is needed, such as fragrance varieties like Dingding Fragrance, DieDie Fragrance, ChuanChuan Fragrance, etc.; pot types like dry pot, cold pot, red soup pot, clear soup pot, yin-yang pot, etc. Generations include the first generation of street vendors, second generation of shop entry, third generation of decoration, fourth generation of culture, fifth generation of intelligence, etc.

The hotpot industry has reached a crossroads, facing difficulties. It's time for a hotpot revolution. Not long ago, Dezhuang launched a green pot base, immediately causing two changes: the broth turns green, appearing more nutritious and healthy, and the hotpot smell doesn't linger on clothes. After Dezhuang's direct stores introduced this soup base, more than half of the diners chose this pot base. Chongqing's Liu Yishou launched the fourth-generation Chongqing hotpot, not only improving the environment but also possessing high-end dining culture, making the fourth-generation Chongqing hotpot a symbol of Chongqing's high quality, high level, and high-end consumption. Meanwhile, Chongqing Xiyuan Hotpot launched the New School Four Seasons Hotpot, dividing the red soup or clear soup used throughout the year into seasonal offerings and introducing different flavors and nourishing effects based on the season.

Innovation is not only reflected in the pot base but also in the dining model, advancing towards fashion and urbanization. Qi Hotpot and Waipo Bridge launched boutique hotpots, decorating the hotpot stores stylishly and quietly, with dishes made small and exquisite, similar to Japanese-style dining. Some hotpot enterprises in Nanshan rely on their scenic advantages, launching outdoor ecological hotpots where diners can enjoy the scenery while eating hotpot.
