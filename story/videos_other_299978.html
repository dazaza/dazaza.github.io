Masked hoodlums rushed into the hotel, female boss was stabbed four times and smartly fought against the hooligans

by oozjsk90 on 2009-12-09 12:56:09

A female boss of a hotel in Laishan District lived alone in the hotel dormitory. One night, a thief sneaked into her room at midnight. She woke up from fright and tried to escape, during which the villain stabbed her four times. After identifying the thief's identity, the female boss cleverly "cooperated" with the thief's demands, winning his trust. She ultimately reported the incident to the police, leading to the thief's arrest.

Ms. Song runs a hotel in Laishan District. Due to her husband being away, she mostly lives alone in the hotel dormitory. At around 3 a.m. on July 11, she suddenly heard noises in her room while asleep. Upon opening her eyes, she broke out in a cold sweat immediately. A masked man was rummaging through the drawers in her room. She picked up a water glass beside her and threw it at the man, then pulled open the door and ran out. The masked man subsequently caught up and stabbed Ms. Song four times. Then, the man strangled her neck and dragged her back into the room.

After returning to the room, the man held a knife to her neck and demanded that she hand over all her money, or he would take her life.

Still shaken, Ms. Song had already identified the man based on his attire and accent. He was Li Mou, a fellow townsman from Yunnan of Wu Mou, who used to work at the hotel. Just a week before, Li Mou had stayed for a period in Wu Mou’s dormitory, leaving an impression on Ms. Song.

The intelligent Ms. Song did not reveal this realization but instead "actively cooperated" with Li Mou's demands. First, she handed over more than ten thousand yuan in cash from her wallet, then gave him several thousand yuan in loose change from the drawer.

After Li Mou took Ms. Song's mobile phone, he prepared to tie her up. To gain Li Mou's trust, Ms. Song proactively told him the best way to escape and promised not to report the incident to the police, urging Li Mou to leave quickly. Without hesitation, Li Mou abandoned the idea of tying Ms. Song and jumped out of the window to escape.

After receiving Ms. Song's report, the Laishan Public Security Bureau immediately deployed efforts to capture Li Mou. By this time, Li Mou and Wu Mou had already fled back to their hometowns. After more than two months of continuous pursuit, the Laishan Public Security Bureau finally brought Wu Mou and Li Mou back to Yantai.

Currently, the case is under further review.
