Inheriting the Haiyan Culture and Showing the Style of Yanfu

by jssscjzwds on 2009-12-01 22:41:57

Our Yancheng is a city named after salt. Since ancient times, salt culture has always been the main body and essence of our Yancheng culture. Last year, under the suggestion of CPPCC members, the construction of China Marine Salt Museum officially started, which will give us an ideal platform to comprehensively display Yancheng's salt culture. More than 2000 years of marine salt industry development has left a rich historical accumulation for Yancheng, and imprinted a heavy historical mark. Building a marine salt history and culture museum has long been the cherished wish and pursuit of more than eight million people in Yanfu, and has also become the goal that all sectors of our Yancheng strive for.

同期声：Deputy Director and Secretary General of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the China Democratic League, Liang Jianmin - For a region, for a nation, whether it has its own unique cultural character, and its own status in cultural history, and whether it can have a certain prominent status, is crucial for enhancing the national confidence of this nation or the people of this region. And for us, the people of Yanfu, the most important and dominant traditional culture on this land is the marine salt culture, which is exactly the cultural brand we want to establish.

On August 10, 2007, with the continuous rumbling sound of bulldozers by the Chuanchang River, the construction of China Marine Salt Museum, formally approved by the State Council, officially began in our Yancheng, which means that the long-cherished hope of the people of Yancheng has finally come true. Of course, this would not be possible without the proposal and advice of the members of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the China Democratic League.

同期声：Deputy Director and Secretary General of the Yancheng Municipal Committee of the China Democratic League, Liang Jianmin - While actively participating in this work, we can make valuable suggestions for the construction. For example, how to coordinate the content and form during the construction, how to carry out the research work of cultural relics well, how to improve the quality of the construction of this museum, and how to combine this museum well with the regional culture of Yancheng.

According to reports, the entire building was designed by Academician Cheng Taining, a famous engineering academician of our country, with a total investment of about 1 billion RMB, and a construction area of nearly 20,000 square meters. After completion, the venue will be divided into four floors, one underground and three above ground. Currently, the first stage of construction tasks has been basically completed.

同期声：Deputy Director of the Preparatory Office of the China Marine Salt Museum of the Municipal Construction Bureau, Qi Shizhong - The brick foundation project has been completed, and the foundation and earthwork projects are underway. The main project will be completed by the end of June 2008, and it will be delivered for use by the end of December.

After completion, the entire venue building complex will resemble a string of crystal-clear white salt crystals scattered by the Chuanchang River. It will be the first specialized museum in the country reflecting the ancient Chinese marine salt history and culture, and will also become another beautiful business card showcasing the image of Yancheng.
