Ten "whispers" written by women to men

by putianb2b24 on 2011-12-21 08:53:23

One: Men must remember never to compare your own woman with other women. Do not always talk about how good other people's wives are, do not criticize her appearance, it is your blessing that she marries you, and it is really inappropriate for you to say such things. For most women, hearing a word of praise from the person they love the most is more valuable than all the praises from others combined. Moreover, if you love her, would you have the heart to hurt her?

Two: Do not neglect her every now and then. Women are sensitive and suspicious, and they tend to think in negative ways. In fact, a quick kiss before going out or a hug when you open the door can make your woman feel deeply moved later on. These things are not difficult for men to do, are they? Remember to tell her when you're upset so that both of you can share the burden, and hold her hand and pass on the warmth when she's upset.

Three: Do not show a bad face to your woman, a man who loses his temper with a woman is despicable. You may look fresh and tidy in business settings, while she is busy inside and outside the house. The busy housework has already made her very angry, and you should know that everything she does behind you allows you to be carefree. Imagine a man coming home after work and saying: "Honey, you've been busy all day, thank you!" The woman smiles and says: "It's nothing, you're the one who's worked hard," what a harmonious picture!

Four: When men get together, they compare jobs and positions; when women get together, they compare men and children. So in front of her girlfriends, you must show your affection and cherishing for her, making her feel like a princess, owning the complete love that makes all women envious. If she is a reasonable woman, she will definitely give you enough face in front of your friends, allowing you to be a towering man in front of your friends.

Five: Just as men like to watch beautiful women, women also like to watch handsome men. You can be jealous or angry, but a woman who truly loves you actually has a simpler mindset when seeing a handsome man than a man seeing a beautiful woman. She just feels some admiration for seeing something beautiful, like looking at a beautiful painting, unlike men who might have more fantasies.

Six: A wife is taken to be cherished, and a cultivated man absolutely will not beat his wife. If you truly love her, then you must respect her and not resort to violence easily. If you no longer love her, then touch your conscience: Can I still make her happy? If the answer is no, then let her go, let her find someone who can truly treat her well. Let yourself stop making mistakes and let her be free!

Seven: Women like men's masculinity, which makes them feel safe. However, women are even more resistant to men's gentleness. If women's gentleness is a weapon against men, then men's unintentional gentleness is like nuclear weapons for women. But women have their innate nature too, which is the maternal complex. Occasionally, she may treat you like her child, pampering you everywhere, but remember not to take advantage of this.

Eight: Family always comes first. Men need to be responsible for work, but also need professional ethics, and derive joy from work, but do not become slaves to work. We work to be happier with our families and enjoy life. It is important to spend time enjoying family happiness with your wife and children regularly.

Nine: Your lover's parents are your own parents. Treat them as you would treat your own, love extends beyond self. Respect elders as you would respect your own, if you genuinely feel deep down that these are my own parents, the elderly will sense this sincerity. Besides, old people are like children, just like哄children, keeping them happy is sufficient. We ourselves will grow old one day, set an example for the younger generation, and teach them how to respect elders.

Ten: The two of you must be honest, trust each other, be tolerant, and understanding. Do not hide everything, but when necessary, tell white lies, the balance is up to you to grasp. Often stand in the other person's shoes, consider her situation, and empathize with her difficulties. Remember: You are her most beloved person, you need to understand her, support her, and be forgiving when she makes mistakes.
