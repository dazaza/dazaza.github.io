Helping to Fly the Legend Event, Experiencing the Thrill of Speed - Breitling Launches the "Renault Air"

by longtime9t on 2012-03-05 14:42:11

The grand event of the world aviation circle - the US National Air Race will be held in Reno on September 14 this year. As the main sponsor of the race, Breitling, a well-known Swiss watch manufacturer who also loves flying and enjoys challenges, launched the "Breitling Reno Air Races The Game" on June 20. This racing game designed specifically for smartphones and tablets will be available for free on iPhone, iPod touch, and iPad (iOS). With realistic audio-visual experience and passionate racing sensation, it is considered to be the most stunning Apple iOS game so far.

The Reno Air Races, a legendary aviation event, has been continuously held for nearly 50 years. This year's US National Air Races (National Championship Air Races) will take place again in Reno, Nevada, from September 14 to 18. This world-renowned aviation competition attracts a large number of beautiful aircraft and tens of thousands of aviation enthusiasts every year. During the five-day race, participants adhere to the motto of "low flying, high speed, left turn," competing on the "course" marked by the beacon tower, flying close to the ground at dizzying speeds in the vast and scorching desert, determined to continuously challenge limits in what is the world's fastest sports competition. It is thrilling and almost crazy. The famous "Unlimited Class" is the highest level of the Reno air race, with flight speeds exceeding 800 kilometers per hour (about 500 miles per hour), and flight altitude just a few feet above the ground!

Due to its deep ties with the aviation industry and to encourage the spirit of adventure, admirable courage, and rigorous precision, Breitling is the only brand that actively supports this aviation competition. Now, Breitling offers all iPhone, iPod Touch, and iPad users the opportunity to personally experience the high-speed excitement of the Reno Air Races, promoting this legendary aviation event. In the game, everyone can pilot their own Reno fighter and challenge their flying skills; they can race online with other players or complete self-challenges in single-player mode.

High-speed passion, immersive experience! The "Breitling Reno Air Races The Game" is undoubtedly an exciting experience that smartphone and tablet users cannot miss. A powerful 3D engine provides quite realistic graphics and dazzling light effects, making the breath-taking game scenes immerse players in a world full of tension and excitement; the gravity sensor makes players feel as if they are in the cockpit, spiraling and weaving between the beacon towers with the plane, with multiple perspective modes offering an unparalleled sense of presence that cannot fail to make one's blood boil! You, who love challenges, can compete with players from all over the world on the network.

Starting with the challenges of the "Unlimited Class" and "T-6 Class," players can explore different types of aircraft, piloting WWII vintage fighters specially tuned for the Reno air race, including the North American P-51 Mustang and Grumman F8F Bearcat, among other legendary models. Before entering the game, players can customize their own exclusive planes, painting the fuselage colors and adjusting mechanical performance to obtain a more personalized gaming experience.

High-speed passion, inviting you to personally challenge! Legendary fighter planes, awaiting your revival!

[Game Highlights]

- Tense and intense legendary competitive races

- Stunning 3D graphics

- Super-realistic Reno venue and race route

- Optimized turning and touch screen control

- 3 game modes: Speed mode, Single-player mode, Multiplayer mode

- Multiplayer mode supporting wireless local area networks and 3G networks, up to 8 people

- Multiple aircraft types and race routes

- Customizable planes: name, style, and color

- Racing flight tutorial

- Compatible with OpenFeint platform

Starting at the end of June 2011, the "Breitling Reno Air Races The Game" will be exclusively available for free on the Apple iOS operating platform. Before the opening of the Reno Air Races in September, the game content will be updated twice, adding more aircraft types and new models. Additionally, the Android platform game is expected to be released during the Reno Air Races in September 2011.
