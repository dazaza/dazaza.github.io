Love Story 2 (Dating Simulation Game)

[Basic Introduction]

"Lovestory 2" is the second generation of Fujitsu's popular dating simulation game "Lovestory". The story continues from "Lovestory Side Story ~ Magic Academy". The younger brother of the protagonist from the previous game starts a three-year academic life at the advanced department of Trifets Academy while his older brother goes on an adventure to the Waldearth continent. During this time, he will learn various magic techniques, make different friends, and try his best to win the heart of one of the five beautiful girls in his class before graduation to achieve a perfect ending...

Characters:

There are only five new beauties in the second generation, much fewer than the eleven in the previous game. However, they are all distinct personalities and charmingly beautiful. Some beauties from the first generation also make appearances, but due to their age, most appear as teachers in the academy. Their various hints will greatly influence the protagonist's ultimate ending, so be sure to pay attention.

Lindel: The martial arts-loving beauty from the previous game becomes the teacher for the magic and martial arts courses, but her competitive nature remains unchanged. She will leave the academy after March 22, 2008.

Nayshu: The heroine from the previous game, she now serves as the teacher for liberal arts courses, with an outstanding气质resembling a goddess. She is a key figure in achieving a perfect ending along Dora and Mer lines.

Floss: The beautiful science enthusiast from the previous game appears after Lindel leaves the academy to teach the science course, sparking a strange romance. In the later stages of the game, she will provide various important tips to the protagonist.

Zackshe: The dean of the Magic Academy, a kind old man who will provide various important tips to the protagonist in the later stages of the game.

Maru: The heroine of the second generation, she shares the same lineage as Nayshu, loves history, and hides a tragic past. She is a crucial character in obtaining a perfect ending.

Dora: One of the beauties in the second generation, she has an outgoing personality, excels in science, loves watching operas and shopping, and is a mysterious girl who enjoys teasing the protagonist and dislikes creating magic. In the middle of the game, if her affection level is high, it will trigger the "Necktie Incident" plot.

Mer: One of the beauties in the second generation, she has a weak constitution, loves painting, and often faints for no reason. If you meet her in the item shop during the middle of the game, it will trigger the "Flame of Heart" plot.

Philes: One of the beauties in the second generation, she often dresses as a man, loves sports, and enjoys martial arts magic, similar to Lindel from the previous game. In the middle of the game, if her affection level is high, it will trigger the "Underwear Theft" plot.

Artina: One of the beauties in the second generation, she loves reading and dislikes exercise. She is a literary beauty often encountered in the library (Tianjiao). In the middle of the game, if her affection level is high, it will trigger the "Play Drama" plot.
