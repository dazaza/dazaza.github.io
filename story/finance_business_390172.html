Step-by-Step Guide to Opening an Online Store on Taobao

Do you want to sell things online?

Do you want to become a big boss on Taobao?

Let me guide you step by step.

What's the charm of opening an online store?

Nowadays, opening an online store is not only about starting a business; many people use it as a way to expand their skills and for leisure. What exactly is the allure that draws everyone to open an online store? Summarizing the main points, there are several:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared to traditional stores, online stores don't require renting storefronts or paying taxes and utility bills. They only charge a small fee for listing items and transaction fees, with some even being free. Additionally, you can order goods based on demand without worrying about inventory buildup.

2. Flexible operation

Online stores aren't restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run them full-time or part-time without needing to spend large amounts of time managing the store.

3. Broad consumer base

Since online stores are on the internet, they cater to all possible consumers who might see the products, ranging from nationwide to global users.

How exactly do you open an online store?

The variety of items in online stores is staggering: cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each category has great development potential and a promising future. The key lies in how to proceed and what suits you best. Running an online store requires skill, so let me teach you step by step how to start one.

1. What should you sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is deciding what to sell. The choice of product should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, as well as product attributes and consumer needs.

Small tips for choosing items:

1. Preferably, choose items that aren't available everywhere, with unique features.

2. Don't select products from industries you're unfamiliar with.

3. Avoid bulky, heavy items with low prices.

2. Preparations before opening the store

After selecting the items to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, you also need to undergo corresponding identity and payment method verification.

I chose to open my store on Taobao because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: 1) Register as a member and pass certification; 2) List at least 10 items (including 10); 3) For convenience and safe transactions, it's recommended to activate online banking.

Sample store reference: http://zhgh.taobao.com/

1. User registration

Log onto http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. On the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation message. Open the link inside to confirm, completing the user registration.

Security tip: To ensure transaction safety, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It's suggested to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. After registering as a user, you need to undergo corresponding verifications (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification). Specific operational steps are as follows:

First step, log onto Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. On the opened page, click the "Click here" text beside "Want to sell items? Start with Alipay verification."

As shown in the figure.

Second step, on the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account hasn't been activated yet. Click "Activate Alipay account here." In the popped-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., then click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Third step, after successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay personal real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then continue by clicking the "I have read..." button.

Fourth step, first fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; next, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input bank card information, including the bank, card number, province, city, etc., complete the input, and wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly tip: If uploading ID copies online, control the image file size to within 200KB; if it's an IC card ID, provide the backside picture as well.

Fifth step, after one day, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Transfer Amount," then input the amount of funds Alipay injected into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

If the amount matches correctly, the entire Taobao identity verification process is completed.

3. Procurement, taking pictures

A crucial factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the procurement channel. For the same item, different procurement channels result in different prices.

After identity verification, you'll be busy organizing the items you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, photographing them is essential. It's best to use appropriate graphic/image processing tools to convert the picture format and size, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

Small tip: When taking pictures, it's recommended to use a simple background, like a white desk, highlight the main subject, ensure clarity, keep the picture size under 120KB, and the format should preferably be JPG.

4. Listing Items

To open a store on Taobao, besides meeting the membership certification conditions, you need to list over 10 items. Therefore, after organizing the product information and pictures, you need to start listing your first item.

Friendly tip: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can still list items, but they will only be listed in "Items in Warehouse," which buyers won't see. Only after passing the verification can they be listed for sale.

First step, log onto Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. On the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" listing methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

As shown in the figure.

Friendly tip: "Fixed Price" has a set price where buyers can purchase immediately; "Auction" starts with no bottom price, allowing buyers to bid.

Second step, choose a category suitable for your item. For example, I chose Women's Fashion Premium Items. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Third step, fill in the item information, which is very important. First, in the "Item Information" section, come up with a good title. Click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input the item description, quantity, start time, validity period, etc. Next, in the "Transaction Conditions" section, input the item's selling price, location, shipping cost, payment method, etc. Other information can remain default settings, such as using Alipay by default. Finally, click the "Confirmed, Submit" button to publish the item.

If published successfully, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the published item page, and click "Continue Publishing Items" to list more items.

Friendly tip: If no bids have been made, you can modify the published item information by going to "My Taobao - I am Seller - Items for Sale."

After publishing the items, it's best to regularly update and add new ones to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Small tips for listing items:

(1) Give the item a good title, adding appropriate keywords and symbols, such as ★●◆▲ characters.

(2) Use eye-catching, detailed item descriptions.

(3) Price the item reasonably, possibly at a mid-to-low price compared to similar items.

(4) Set reasonable shipping costs, referring to other similar items.

3. Obtain a Free Store

Taobao offers certified members the opportunity to open a store for free. As long as you list over 10 items, you can own your own store with an independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

To get my store up and running sooner, I initially listed 10 items, spending almost half a day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Next, on the opened page, I named my store "Tomato Fashion Hut"; in the "Store Category" section, I could choose the category for my items; in the "Store Introduction" section, I input the store's brief introduction content. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with the store's address.

4. Shop Decoration is Important

After opening the free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Just like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decorations," mainly including store logo design, item classification, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log onto Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left-hand "Store Management" section, click "Basic Settings." On the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; the main business project must be manually input; in the "Store Logo" section, click the "Browse" button to select a pre-designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" section, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Classification

Classifying items helps buyers find them easily. On the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left. Then, input a new classification name, such as "Hangzhou-style clothing, infant clothing," etc., and click the "Confirm" button to add it.

Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and through keyword searches, you can add the listed items for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

Taobao's "Recommended Items" feature allows you to showcase your best 6 items prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left. Then, in the opened page, select the recommended items and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choose a style with light colors that looks comfortable.

Friendly tip: After successfully opening the store, if the number of items sold in your store remains less than 10 for three consecutive weeks, you'll receive a reminder email from Taobao to replenish the item count; after another two weeks, if still less than 10, the store will be released; after another week, if the item count is still less than 10, the store will be deleted.

After decorating the store, a brand-new page appears before you.

5. Promotion is the Key to Success

The online store is open, items are listed, characteristics are defined, but weeks have passed without any transactions or buyer messages—this is a common problem for many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to promote through forums, exchange links, window recommendations, add friends, and collaborate with various shopkeepers for mutual collections, join more Wangwang groups, and promote the features of my store within these groups.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and utilizing signature lines.

The former can be done on provincial or municipal forums. If there's a section allowing advertisement posts, you can post ads with detailed content, beautiful product images, and maintain regular updates and top placements.

The latter involves changing your forum signature to include your store's URL, store logo, promotional phrases, and store name. Posting attractive threads can lead interested friends to visit your store via your signature.

2. Exchange Links

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus advertising your store for free. For instance, Taobao offers up to 35 friendly links. The method to add them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all stores and note down the names of popular shopkeepers.

Next, log onto http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download the Taobao communication tool "Wangwang," add these popular shopkeeper names, and propose an exchange link request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, input the shopkeeper name, and click the "Add" button.

Friendly tip: If you want to delete a friendly link, simply check the store name on this page and click the "Delete" button.

3. Window Recommendations

Taobao's "Window Recommendation" feature is a special function for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" based on categories, window-recommended items appear in the search results. To set up the "Window Recommendation" feature, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Items for Sale," select the items to recommend in the window (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

Friendly tip: Window recommendation promotes good items on Taobao, increasing views and click-through rates. Store recommendations display items on the store homepage's recommendation spot. The number of window recommendation spots is determined by your item count, monthly credibility, and transaction volume, defaulting to 10.

Small recommendation tip: To increase the chances of finding items, regularly check Taobao's popular search keywords. If related to your item category, such as "Korean style" or "weight loss," consider modifying the item title to include these popular keywords.

4. Friend Promotion

After opening the online store, inform your friends and family. For example, announce the store's opening to friends on platforms like Alibaba and MSN Messenger, changing your personalized signature to include the store's URL. This way, word-of-mouth will spread, and more people will know about your store.

6. Items on Sale

When buyers purchase items, they usually inquire about information related to the items, posing questions online and waiting for the seller's response before deciding whether to buy.

1. Check Buyer Messages

After opening my store and advertising, I daily checked "Taobao Wangwang" for buyer inquiries. If a buyer leaves a message in the store for an item, "Taobao Wangwang" automatically pops up a message notification window.

Alternatively, log onto Taobao, sequentially open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Buyer Messages/Reply," check the messages left by buyers on the opened page, and click "Awaiting My Reply" to reply to the messages.

Item sales tips:

(1) Discount strategy. Formulate different discount standards based on the quantity of purchases to attract buyers.

(2) Promotional strategy. Utilize holidays, store anniversaries, etc., to offer discounted items and boost store traffic.

(3) Auction strategy. Use auctioned items to promote your store, such as adding links to other items in the auction items.

2. Check if Items Are Sold

If the item is sold, it can be queried through three methods:

First, log onto "Taobao Wangwang," the system will notify that a buyer has purchased the item.

Second, check through "My Taobao" to see if the item is sold. Log onto Taobao, sequentially open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items."

Third, log onto the email used to register on Taobao, where Taobao sends relevant notification emails.

7. After Selling Items

After selling an item, besides receiving corresponding sale reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, request payment, ship the item, and evaluate or file complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao notifies the seller through "Taobao Wangwang" or email. Sellers can also log onto Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click "Leave Message" to send a message to the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer doesn't use "Taobao Wangwang," you can note down the buyer's ID and send a site message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it's recommended to use Alipay for payment.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent non-payment upon delivery, sellers generally adopt the "Payment Received Before Shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and at this point, the seller can safely ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao transfers the payment to the seller's Alipay account. Thus, the transaction is completed. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer to increase the chance of revisiting your store and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation, Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluation. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" for sold items, and based on actual situations, choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews, and input text content.

Friendly tip: How to become a highly-rated star store owner on Taobao?

To become a star store owner on Taobao, the credibility score must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credibility score comes from the buyer's evaluation after each successful Alipay transaction. A "Positive Review" adds one point, "Neutral Review" doesn't add points, and "Negative Review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star store owner, remember to provide honest service.

If there are cases of online transactions without purchase, receipt without payment, etc., sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to file complaints and reports, but need to collect evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer's signature receipts, Wangwang screenshots, etc.

Postscript: Although opening an online store has zero cost and low risk, examples of failing to make a single sale and "closing down" abound. To ensure the survival of your small store online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the hearts of buyers, gain good credit evaluations, and bring in wealth!
