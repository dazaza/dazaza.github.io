Enrollment requirements: Full-time undergraduate degree or above from higher education institutions, majoring in chemical engineering and technology, computer science and technology, Chinese language and literature, international trade; those with work experience in production safety emergency rescue are preferred; under 30 years old, healthy body for the 2011 Mid-Autumn Festival travel.

According to the Regulations on Public Recruitment of Personnel for Public Institutions in Guangdong Province (Order No. 139 of the Guangdong Provincial Government), due to work needs, Foshan Production Safety Emergency Rescue Command Center plans to publicly recruit staff. The following matters are hereby announced:

III. Recruitment conditions:

1. Enrollment time: September 8, 2011, September 9, 2011, September 12, 2011 (9:00 AM - 11:30 AM, 2:30 PM - 5:30 PM).

2. Enrollment location: Office of the Foshan Municipal Administration of Work Safety Supervision (7th Floor, No. 8 Pulan Second Road, Chancheng District), contact numbers: 83992253, 83997455.

The public recruitment is for one administrative management position.

The recruitment announcement will be posted on the website of the Foshan Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, with an announcement period of 10 working days. Enrollment can start after the announcement period of 10 working days.

Applicants must meet the basic conditions stipulated in the Regulations on Public Recruitment of Personnel for Public Institutions in Guangdong Province. In addition, they should also possess the following conditions:

I. Recruitment position:

Enrollment applicants shall fill out one enrollment form according to the recruitment conditions specified in this announcement, provide their resume, ID card, academic certificate, and relevant work experience certificates (originals and copies), as well as two recent front-facing bareheaded photos of small one-inch size. After passing the qualification review, the Office of the Foshan Municipal Administration of Work Safety Supervision will notify specific examination matters, including the ticket price of Guangdong Science Center.
