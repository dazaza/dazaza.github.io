The quenching process uses high-frequency heating equipment to heat the workpiece evenly. The shaft quenching equipment from Zhengzhou Guoyun ensures that the workpiece rotates at high speed during heating, followed by rapid cooling for quenching.

Quenching technology is widely applied in modern mechanical manufacturing industries. Important parts in machinery, especially steel components used in automobiles, airplanes, and rockets, almost always undergo quenching treatment. To meet diverse technical requirements, various quenching processes have been developed. For instance, depending on the area being treated, there are whole, partial, and surface quenching; based on whether the phase change is complete during heating, there are complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypo-eutectoid steels, this method is also known as subcritical quenching); according to the content of phase change during cooling, there are分级quenching, isothermal quenching, and sub-cooling quenching.

Zhengzhou Guoyun also provides a full set of CNC shaft pump pipe quenching equipment, including CNC quenching machines. These devices allow automatic rotation, lifting, water spraying, and descending of the workpiece, completing the entire quenching process with simultaneous water spraying. This significantly saves manpower and time, reduces costs, and ensures product quality and precision through a series of CNC programming, thereby enhancing the performance of the workpiece.
