Doing yoga at home on the weekend allows you to have fun while losing weight.

by cshelive9520 on 2012-03-02 21:44:25

With six body parts that women care most about - arms, chest, waist, abdomen, thighs, and calves - paired with different household chores such as wiping tables, walls, taking items off shelves, folding pants, mopping floors, sweeping stairs, etc., this aerobic exercise tutorial targets different parts of the body. Performing each exercise about 10 times daily can help burn all the fat.

**Arms**

Step 1: Hold a cloth in each hand, start wiping the table clockwise from the left side of your body.

Step 2: Gradually move to the other side, then switch to counterclockwise wiping after approximately 10 repetitions. This helps eliminate "bat wings" (flabby upper arms).

**Chest**

Step 1: Stand about an arm's length away from the corner of a wall, hold a cloth in each hand against both sides of the wall.

Step 2: Slowly slide your hands up along the wall until you reach the top, then slightly press down your chest for a breast-enhancing effect.
