Three-day food cultural theme tour in Tianjin

by afkqxoyh on 2010-05-26 18:10:02

Preparations:

Book tickets for the Mingliu Teahouse crosstalk performance, first-class tickets, 30 yuan per person. Phone: 022-27116382

Book a room at Home Inn Music Academy (Da Guangming Bridge branch, Shiyi Jinglu branch)

Prepare small change. Most Tianjin buses cost 1.5 yuan per person, so prepare plenty of small change.

&；nbsp；

Day One

&；nbsp；

1. Depart at 5:30 am, take bus 916 to Dongzhimen.

2. Arrive at Dongzhimen at 7:00 am, take line 2 and transfer to line 5, get off at Puhuangyu station, then take a taxi to Beijing South Station.

3. Buy intercity high-speed train tickets, depart at 9:00 am.

4. Arrive in Tianjin at 9:30 am, buy return train tickets and a Tianjin map (don't buy it inside the station, walk out a bit further before buying). Take a taxi to Home Inn Music Academy (Da Guangming Bridge branch, Shiyi Jinglu branch), about 10 yuan. Settle in and rest for a while.

5. Depart at 10:00 am, take bus 651 from Daguangming Bridge station (opposite the RT-Mart supermarket) to Gulou station, visit the Gulou museum for free.

6. At 11:30 am, walk east from Gulou for one kilometer to Ancient Culture Street. There are small restaurants where you can have a cheap and filling lunch. Visit Ancient Culture Street, Tianwang Temple, Ni Ren Zhang, Bèngdòu Zhāng, Guó Rén'ér Zhāng, enjoy local snacks, and buy small souvenirs.

7. At 15:00 pm, leave the street and take a taxi to Chifeng Road to visit Porcelain House (this house is built with porcelain), entrance fee 20 yuan. At 16:00 pm, go to the intersection of Hēilóngjiāng Road and Xinhua Road to pick up tickets for crosstalk performances at Mingliu Teahouse. Duration: two and a half hours.

8. At 18:30 pm, eat potato noodles or potato knife-cut noodles mixed at Jiědìliǎng Restaurant on Hēilóngjiāng Road, costs 5 yuan or 7 yuan respectively. Soft drinks are 1 yuan per bottle.

9. At 19:00 pm, check Tianjin daily rental website, take a taxi to Honey Bar at Xiawafang, bar hopping. (The cost depends on what alcohol you choose, generally half a dozen beers will do.) Taxi fare won't exceed 20 yuan.

10. By 22:00 pm, go back early to shower and sleep. Tomorrow will be tiring. A taxi fare of 8 yuan will suffice.

&；nbsp；

Day Two

1. Get up at 8:00 am, leave Home Inn and walk south. Turn west at the first intersection for 50 meters, there's a Kuncheng snack shop (old Tianjin fried sauce noodles opposite). Have breakfast here. Recommended food: tofu pudding, egg yuntun, gapai vegetables, fried dough sticks, box-packed eggs (Note: it's "box" not "egg roll"). Buy freshly fried dough sticks and box-packed eggs from the small stall next to Kuncheng instead of inside Kuncheng.

2. Depart at 9:00 am, buy a small bottle of white wine at the supermarket near the door. Remember not to open it, otherwise it won't pass security checks. Wait for bus 643 double-decker at Daguangming Bridge station next to Home Inn, get off at Zhongshanmen station, take the light rail to Tanggu station, 5 yuan per person. Follow the crowd after getting off, head to the Foreign Goods Market, which might seem boring, like an expanded version of Tianyi Market. But it's quite famous, worth a visit. Rent a hotel in Tianjin for daily use.

3. At 11:30 am, take bus 103 from the Foreign Goods Market station to Beita station, cross the road to buy seafood (Note: walk further in, there's a huge market in a small alley, don't assume it's just the couple of shops by the roadside, walk further). After buying seafood, process it at a roadside shop, drink some white wine for better experience, it kills bacteria and refreshes.

4. At 14:00 pm, the nearby market is the terminal stop for bus 103. Take the bus back to the Foreign Goods Market. If you wish, it's best to take the light rail from the first stop, Tedan station, to avoid standing as the Foreign Goods Market is very crowded. If you don't care or are okay with standing, you can take the subway from the Foreign Goods Market.

5. Return to Tianjin at 16:00 pm, take the same bus as when you came. Go to the Kuncheng restaurant behind the hotel to eat bobbie fish for 24 yuan and hot pot tofu. The bobbie fish is very tasty and strong-flavored, usually two people shouldn't order too much as the bobbie fish is quite large.

6. After dinner at 18:00 pm, stroll around the supermarket near the hotel entrance and buy necessary items. If you're not tired, you can take a milk bath or foot massage. You can also go to the Haihe River at Liberation Bridge to see the night view.

&；nbsp；

Day Three

1. Get up at 8:00 am, check out. If your luggage is heavy, you can store it at the hotel and come back to collect it when leaving in the afternoon. Breakfast is the same as above.

&；nbsp；

2. Take the double-decker bus 643 to Nankai University, admire the prestigious school's charm. Remember to enter from the East Gate and exit from the South Gate, there are statues.

3. At 10:30 am, take a taxi to Quanyechang Pedestrian Street, less than 20 yuan. Go shopping, ladies will be happy.

4. At noon 12:00 pm, go to Goubuli main store in the hutong opposite Binjiang Building in Quanyechang Pedestrian Street. It's quite expensive. It's recommended to try it once. Buy a set meal on the first floor, don't listen to the waiters who tell you to go upstairs, upstairs is for tourists, extremely overpriced, a beef bun costs 9 yuan each (note, it's not a typo, one bun costs 9 yuan). Eight pork buns cost 36 yuan, each smaller than my eyeball. Downstairs set meals cost more than 20 yuan, just have a taste.

5. After eating, continue shopping until your feet blister, then take a taxi to Xiniwa corner, about 10 yuan. Experience Tianjin subway, fewer people than Beijing's and quite advanced. Subway ticket costs 3 yuan per person.

6. Exit from D exit at Xiawafang station, across the road is Jinmen Crepes with a red sign, 5 yuan per set, with two fried dough sticks and two eggs. Taste what real crepe fruit is. Walk north while eating crepes, about 300 meters away, there's a Xiaoli Roast Chicken, buy two and take them with you. Pay attention to the calculation and weighing done by the staff. Staff there often make mistakes in calculations, and every time they make a mistake, it results in overcharging. After buying roast chicken, do a big thing - take a taxi from Xiaoli Roast Chicken to Guilifang Sesame Twist Store at Limin Road intersection, buy the famous Eighteen Street Big Sesame Twists. Note that only this one is genuine, all others are fake. Ask the driver to wait outside, after buying sesame twists, directly get back into the car and go back to Home Inn to pick up your luggage, ask the driver to wait, pick up the luggage and head straight to the train station to end your Tianjin trip. Taxi fare between 20~30 yuan. Note: In Tianjin, taxis charge for waiting, the amount depends on how fast you act.

Notes:

1. Taxi drivers are interesting to chat with, but most will say your choices aren't great and the attractions aren't fun. But traveling is about going to places you haven't been to, they live in Tianjin all the time, so they naturally think it's not great. Don't believe these things, we don't always go to Mutianyu either.

2. If you don't know the way, just ask. People in Tianjin are like Zhang Damin, very humorous and热情, very easygoing.

3. Wudadao and Italian Style Street are optional attractions. Actually, there's really nothing much to see, but taking pictures there is still nice. If you really want to visit, it's suggested to take a detour around there when taking a taxi to the train station. It's a bit far, but spending an extra 10~20 yuan to visit two attractions is actually quite worthwhile.
