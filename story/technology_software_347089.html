After all, she is Yu Shangtai now, a plain little male servant, no longer Miss Yun worthy of fancy shoes like climbing shoes.

As Yun Xiaoxiao was about to go upstairs after weaving through the crowd, she was spotted by Ou Qingmei who happily ran over and pulled her in front of Ou Shicheng excitedly saying,

"Come on, my third brother is an amiable person without any prejudice regarding family background or social status. Don't rush into work; let's have some fun first, like wearing Columbia outdoor shoes."

"..."

Yun Xiaoxiao, suddenly grabbed and brought before the third young master Ou and Fei Ji, found herself facing what she most wanted to avoid. Recalling their mention of that kiss, she felt resentment in her heart and awkwardness on her face, unsure where to look.

In her panic, her eyes still unconsciously stole a glance at Ou Shicheng.

Ou Shicheng coldly looked at Yun Xiaoxiao, his gaze falling on the arm Ou Qingmei was holding onto. His sister's overly familiar behavior with the little male servant made him somewhat displeased.

Yun Xiaoxiao wanted to quickly get away from this young miss, but Ou Qingmei had no intention of letting go, tightly pulling her arm and coquettishly shaking it.

"Just stay in the living room for a while. If we need anything, it'll be convenient to ask you! Third Brother, say something!"

Ou Shicheng helplessly glanced at his sister then gave Yun Xiaoxiao a warning look, unable to understand what charm this scrawny guy possessed.

"You stay here and serve!" Ou Shicheng said coldly.

"I knew it... Third Brother is the best..." Ou Qingmei held Yun Xiaoxiao's hand, acting overly intimate. Though both were women, Yun Xiaoxiao still felt embarrassed since she was playing the role of a man.

Fei Ji became somewhat nervous, glaring fiercely at Yun Xiaoxiao. What kind of smelly guy is this? Judging from the clothes... he must be a male servant.

Status is status. Ou Qingmei seemed overly enthusiastic towards this little male servant, even more so than towards Fei Ji.

This wasn't a good sign. Ou Shicheng frowned deeply. Although there wasn't a strong distinction of class in his mind, the identity of a servant seemed somewhat inappropriate.

"Go, bring me a glass of red wine!" Ou Shicheng coldly ordered.

Yun Xiaoxiao immediately understood the meaning behind those words. The third young master was unhappy and wanted to send her away, surely because Ou Qingmei had been too warm towards her.

"I'll go get the wine!" Yun Xiaoxiao forcibly withdrew, awkwardly retreating.

"Third Brother, why are you doing this? Why drink red wine right at this moment? You're deliberately being difficult!" Ou Qingmei was very unhappy, pouting and looking wistfully at Yun Xiaoxiao's retreating back, her expression truly causing concern.
