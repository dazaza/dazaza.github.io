[Qingdao Renovation] Teaches You Four Issues to Pay Attention to When Replacing a Mattress

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-06 16:17:39

I. Size - The measurement of the mattress length should ideally be at least 15 centimeters more than one's height, which is used to reserve space for the pillow and reduce the sense of compression during sleep. If you are relatively tall, it would be best to buy an extra-long mattress to avoid having your body curled up due to a mattress that is too short.

II. Elasticity and firmness - Since most human spines are in a shallow S-shape, a mattress with appropriate firmness and elasticity is needed to achieve comfortable sleep.

III. Economy and durability - Purchase a brand with good reputation and perfect after-sales service. This not only ensures consumer protection but also provides healthy and comfortable sleep quality. A longer usage period also means better economic benefits.

IV. Sleep habits - Everyone's sleep habits are different, so the demand for the softness and elasticity of the mattress varies from person to person. Before purchasing, one should first understand their daily sleep habits and try lying down and turning over in various positions to feel if the spring provides sufficient support for the spine and balances gravity. Good and even support for the spine can lead to the best sleep condition.

Of course, the color of the mattress and its coordination with the bed set also need attention. When they match well, a beautiful dream is already slowly waiting for you, isn't it?
