How to view the renovation company's quotation sheet

by bjqunban on 2010-05-24 19:08:23

Some homeowners who are new to renovation only look at the price column after receiving the renovation quotation form. They think it's acceptable if the price is low, and they will bargain fiercely if the price is high. In fact, this practice is wrong. If the price is not combined with the material standards, manufacturing or installation process technology standards, or if the quoted price on the quotation form does not specify which materials are used, or there is a material description but no mention of the origin, specifications, and varieties of the materials, then that price is a virtual number or a fake price. Therefore, the most important and most attention-worthy part of the renovation quotation form is not the price, but the "material structure and manufacturing installation process technology standards" section.

Several factors determine the high and low of the renovation quotation form: 1. Material structure and manufacturing installation process technology standards; 2. Space design function; 3. Choice of construction team; 4. The quality and distance of construction conditions; 5. The difficulty level of construction techniques. A detailed renovation project quotation form should list all the brands, specifications, units, unit prices, quantities, and total balances of the materials used. However, some decoration companies only list the brand, unit price, and total amount, ignoring the specifications and quantities. More often, unclear specifications are a common issue in quotation forms. Different material specifications can lead to significant price differences. Without specifying this clearly, future manipulations by the decoration company when purchasing materials become more likely.

1. Renovation Materials: For example, in the door frame packaging project, there are significant differences in the width and shape of the door frame wood strips used. If the decoration company only writes down the unit price of the door frame wood strip, how many linear meters are used, and the total cost, it is incomplete. It should clearly state its width and what kind of shape.

2. Construction Techniques: Some construction projects have several or even more construction methods. Different methods naturally result in significant price differences. Writing down just the cost of tiling or painting is too vague. Different construction techniques involve different types and quantities of main and auxiliary materials. If not written clearly, on one hand, the price has room for fluctuation, and the decoration company may charge according to one construction technique but use a simpler method. On the other hand, during construction, there would be no basis for supervising the work. Take the coating project as an example. There are many construction methods for wall paint coating projects. For handling wall cracks, materials such as tapes, white cloth, kraft paper, newspapers, etc., can be used, resulting in different effects and prices. The number of times putty is applied affects the usage amount and effect. Brushing pure acid clear varnish to prevent alkali return also makes a difference if this step is included or not. Each craft method changes the cost, and the water content is often mixed here. Therefore, a perfect quotation form should clearly state how the basic treatment should be done, whether bottom paint is needed, whether glue is needed, whether pure acid clear varnish is brushed, how many times putty is applied, how many times the paint is brushed, instead of simply stating: Nippon Paint Meidel ** yuan per square meter.

3. Loss Calculation: Materials will incur losses during construction, so the purchase should include a loss portion added to the actual usage. This quantity is included in the unit price in the quotation. However, in some quotations, another 10% loss fee is added after the total material amount, which is actually double charging. Consumers should be aware of this. Besides, the basic loss for any project will not exceed 10%. If it exceeds this ratio, a reasonable explanation from the other party should be requested.

Therefore, Qunbang Office Decoration reminds homeowners who need renovation: When signing the renovation quotation form, attention should be paid to the process description, area, and specification descriptions of the materials. No detail should be overlooked, and the quotation form must be thoroughly understood to choose the right decoration company.

The more detailed the renovation quotation form, the better. A detailed quotation form benefits both the decoration company and consumers. For consumers, in the detailed quotation form, not only is the source of the price explained, but the clearly written construction methods also allow consumers who are completely unfamiliar with renovation to supervise the construction themselves. For decoration companies, it avoids consumer regrets or non-acceptance of certain items after construction. With a clearly written quotation form beforehand, the decoration company saves a lot of trouble.

Qunbang Office Decoration Company - Solemnly reminds homeowners: A complete renovation quotation form is not just a simple reporting of numbers. It includes project name, unit, quantity, main materials, auxiliary materials, labor, material, and installation process descriptions. If any of these items are missing, it cannot be considered a qualified renovation quotation form.
