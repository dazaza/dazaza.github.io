by wsysjw on 2009-06-19 14:25:14

"Star Civilization Online" is an SLG sci-fi strategy online game. The game is set against the backdrop of the universe and has built a powerful star system. In the game, you can achieve colonization of other star systems by building planets and assembling fleets, thereby achieving the goal of continuously expanding your power. Recently, a new area called Aries Palace-Mu has opened, which can be searched on Baidu.

The game has the following characteristics:

1. Super mini client makes it simple to play the game.

The size of the "Star Civilization Online" client is equivalent to the hard disk space occupied by five songs. You can download it in one minute and start playing. However, a super mini-client does not necessarily mean shabby graphics. Star Civilization has an impressive battle performance, allowing players to enjoy intense battle scenes. Although it is a client-based game, "Star Civilization Online" also has the convenient and hassle-free features of web games. The thoughtfully designed boss key allows you to play with peace of mind anytime and anywhere. Playing games is that simple! Easy download, exciting to play anytime.

2. Six factions design for intense power struggles.

The game offers six factions for you to choose from. Factions can attack each other at will, and the existence of neutral forces - interstellar pirates - adds fun to the interstellar competition. Players of the same faction can team up to attack other factions. If you're cool enough, several factions might even unite to counter you! The well-designed faction system fully satisfies your desire for conquest, and the enemy factions scattered across the map stimulate the player's warlike nature. In the interstellar world, you can experience the most intense interstellar battles.

3. Strategy over operation, testing superior tactics.

"Star Civilization Online" tests the player's strategic thinking. From the first step of building a planet, you must have a plan in mind and carefully plan every action to achieve optimal resource allocation and the best fleet combination. How to quickly expand the star system, how to assemble the strongest fleet, what are the combat strategies... these are all contents that players need to carefully plan. In later stages, even the design of a single ship component is very important. Players must continue to delve deeper into the game to make progress. It's like playing chess; precise troop deployment is all for that ultimate victory! The game aims to provide you with the most free and broad platform to showcase extraordinary strategies. The wise strategist wins the world, it's all about IQ!

4. Relaxing and leisurely gameplay, no time-consuming grind.

Once you have a full plan, you don't need to spend a lot of time on the construction of planets in the game. After some resource allocation, you can entrust the star system. You can develop the star system even when offline. For players with little entertainment time, just spending a few points, even if you only go online for one hour a day, you can still play wonderfully! During the period of entrusting the planet, it can automatically carry out construction while continuously producing resources, constantly storing strength. When you wake up, you will find that your planet has a lot of resources again. All actions do not require players to stay in front of the computer. We want the most relaxing gaming attitude! There is no need to spend a lot of time immersed in the game. In the interstellar world, strategy determines success or failure.
