2011 Analysis Report of China Automobile Accessories Industry

Analysis Report of China Automobile Accessories Industry: According to the official automobile prediction, in 2011, the sales of most categories of automobile products market which mainly focus on automobile beauty will reach RMB 113 billion. In 2012, it will double and reach RMB 50 billion. If related supporting services are taken into consideration, the market space will further expand. According to the official speculation, by 2012, the scale of the whole automobile aftermarket will reach RMB 880 billion, second only to Japan in Asia and rank the second in Asia. Through a calm review of the bustling scene of many automobile manufacturers entering the automobile accessories industry, we found that although China's automobile accessories industry has developed beyond what it used to be, there are still objective problems that can not be ignored.

Analysis Report of China Automobile Accessories Industry believed that:

1. The price of China's automobile accessories market is very transparent and open, which may also be another defect existing in the current automobile accessories industry. It is not difficult to find if you walk around in the market: the same brand of car audio sells for more than 300 yuan in the wholesale market, while in high-end stores, it costs at least one thousand yuan or even thousands of yuan. The same brand seat cushion sells for only dozens of yuan in the wholesale market, but in high-end brand stores, it costs more than one hundred yuan; this huge price difference makes consumers confused, even professionals are at a loss.

2. The threshold of the automobile accessories industry is relatively low but the profit margin is quite large, and many investors join the production and sales army of automobile accessories without standard guidance. There is a scale of sales, but not many well-known brands, the reputation of automobile accessories is not high, and there are too many fake goods running rampant in the market.

3. Most enterprises neglect corporate culture, so that enterprises have no soul. Nowadays, the competition between enterprises is ultimately the competition of corporate culture. Compared with the successful corporate culture of whole vehicle enterprises, automobile accessories enterprises lack strength in shaping corporate culture, which leads to the lack of soul in enterprises.

4. The homogenization of automobile accessories is very serious, there is no innovation, only talk about profits, and among the automobile accessories produced and sold in the current market, there are not many products that show obvious individuality, the design is simple, the production is not delicate enough, and monotonous products obviously can hardly meet the needs of consumers. With the sharp increase in the number of private cars, consumers' demand for automobile accessories shows a strong trend of personalization, and they need more automobile ornaments that can reflect the individuality of car owners.

5. The automobile beauty industry is not professional enough, and beauty talents have become a bottleneck that restricts the development of the industry. Take automobile beauty as an example, automobile beauty technology has experienced the development of early technologies such as waxing, membrane crossing, and glazing.

After several years of development, the pattern of the automobile accessories industry has changed significantly. "Intensive" service and "one-stop" sales chain operation system will become the mainstream of the domestic automobile accessories market. Chinese and foreign-funded enterprises are all focusing on this point, using large and medium-sized cities such as Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Beijing as bases to accelerate the integration of resources and domestic layout. In fact, apart from the above-mentioned, there are many other factors. The markets of automobile decoration and automobile anti-theft devices will continue to launch new products, but the whole industry will not present a booming state. Car navigation is more in the stage of cultivating consumers and expecting more merchants to participate, and the country supports to jointly promote the development of the industry.

Business opportunities hidden in the automobile accessories industry According to the analysis of the profit situation of the world's top 11 automobile companies in the past 11 years, in a completely mature international automobile market, the sales profit of automobiles accounts for about 32% of the total profit of the automobile industry, the profit of parts supply accounts for about 31%, and 71% of the profit is generated in its service field.

With the continuous fulfillment of China's commitment to joining the World Trade Organization, China's automobile aftermarket has achieved remarkable development and presented ten business opportunities. After-sales repair service. Car advertising. Intelligent transportation. Car entertainment. Car club. Car maintenance. Parts and boutique retail. Car insurance. Car financing. Car information.

The newly issued "Automobile Industry Development Policy" clearly stated in Article 33: "The state encourages automobile, motorcycle, parts manufacturing enterprises and financial, service trade enterprises to learn from international mature automobile marketing methods, management experience and service trade concepts, and actively develop automobile service trade."

The next two years are important for the development of the automobile accessories industry. The automobile accessories industry is transitioning from the previous "dirty, messy, poor" to scaling, standardization, and specialization. Opportunities and challenges coexist, only by recognizing the situation can we seize opportunities in development and achieve greater results.
