When she feels her body starting to feel heavy and gain weight, she also chooses to diet for weight loss and slimming down. Among various diets, she chose the raw food diet, which is highly recommended among college students and celebrities.

Raw food means eating vegetables raw. Raw vegetables contain large amounts of fiber, which can reduce hunger pangs but also help clear feces from the body, offering outstanding skin beautification effects.

Eating raw foods while doing health diets can help you lose 5 kg in just one month.

Secrets of the Raw Food Diet

1. Raw food is rich in vitamins and minerals, but since it consists of food-based items, when eating two meals of raw food, the other meal must include fresh fish, eggs, lean meat, and other proteins.

2. When eating raw food for all three meals, because the calorie count is too low, after consuming it for a short period of 5~7 days, you must supplement with wild vegetables or boiled sweet potatoes, steamed potatoes, etc., so as not to harm your health.

3. Compared to eating raw food for all three meals, if you maintain a normal diet for lunch and only eat raw food for breakfast and dinner, the results are better.

Raw Food Diet Recipes

1. In the first week, eat raw food only for breakfast or dinner.

2. Starting from the second week, eat raw food for breakfast and dinner.

3. As your stomach gradually adapts to raw food, the feeling of hunger disappears, and you may even feel more relaxed mentally.

4. If you dislike eating raw food, try mixing it into 200ml of milk or soy milk for a unique flavor. Otherwise, you can also mix it with fruit juice or honey.

Foods Suitable for Eating with Raw Foods

Eating raw food with milk not only tastes great but also provides high nutrition, allowing you to intake calcium and other components simultaneously.

During summer, when eating raw food, make sure everything is prepared and cleaned properly. Remember to wash the vegetables thoroughly!
