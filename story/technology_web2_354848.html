Zhang Hui is promoting and implementing the newly revised Tobacco Monopoly Law and tobacco monopoly regulations.

by bjgdesign on 2010-05-10 23:25:56

On March 2, the State Tobacco Monopoly Administration held a seminar on publicity and implementation of the newly revised Tobacco Monopoly Law and the Administrative Penalty Procedures Regulation for tobacco monopoly. Vice President Zhang Hui attended the meeting and delivered a speech.

In his speech, Zhang Hui pointed out that in November 2009, the "Bulletin of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress" (special issue) published the "Tobacco Monopoly Law of the People's Republic of China" revised by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. On January 21, 2010, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology announced the revised "Regulations on Administrative Penalty Procedures for Tobacco Monopoly" through Order No. 12. These two newly revised legal documents are of great significance for consolidating and improving the national tobacco monopoly system.

Zhang Hui put forward two requirements for the tobacco industry to publicize, implement, and enforce these two newly revised legal documents:

Firstly, it is necessary to study carefully, understand deeply, and unify thoughts and understanding. It is important to accurately grasp the main spirit of the revision and improvement. Eliminating conflicts between legal provisions was the direct cause of the revision of the "Tobacco Monopoly Law". The inability to adapt to development needs was the main factor for revising the "Regulations". Ensuring the unity of national legal system is the key to understanding the dominant spirit of the revision of the "Tobacco Monopoly Law". It is necessary to deeply understand the significance of the revision and improvement. This revision of the two legal documents is not just a simple change in clauses or wording, but a key step in actively responding to various challenges. It is extremely important and has far-reaching implications for achieving legal uniformity between tobacco monopoly laws and other national laws, solving the issues of legal and reasonable procedures, and consolidating and improving the national tobacco monopoly system. High attention should be paid to six important changes brought about by the new legal provisions to tobacco monopoly management: with the publication and implementation of these two newly revised legal documents as a sign, China's tobacco monopoly legal system has been further improved; some existing management links need to be connected; work procedures need adjustment; operation mechanisms need standardization; work systems should be perfected; law enforcement standards need to be enhanced.

Secondly, it is necessary to fully implement, correctly enforce, and effectively improve the level of execution. The tobacco industry should regard learning, publicity, and implementation as a very important task at present, make meticulous plans, and carefully organize and implement them. According to the requirements of "clarifying responsibilities, meticulous organization, full coverage, and focusing on effectiveness", a leadership responsibility system should be implemented, and serious organization and implementation should be carried out; an implementation plan should be formulated to ensure comprehensive coverage; learning priorities should be highlighted, and layered training should be conducted; unified implementation should be coordinated, and cleaning and abolition should be implemented; quality and effect should be emphasized, and inspections and guidance should be strengthened to earnestly do a good job in learning, publicity, and implementation. In the current period and in the future, according to the requirements of "clear responsibilities, functional positioning, sound systems, and standardized operations", preparations for implementation should be made to achieve orderly and smooth connections; basic guarantees should be strengthened, and supporting institutions and personnel should be assigned; business qualities should be improved, and position skill training should be conducted; supervision and inspection should be strengthened to ensure the expected results, and the overall implementation work should be promoted.

Zhang Hui emphasized that studying and understanding the newly revised "Tobacco Monopoly Law" must focus on the following four points: first, the tobacco monopoly system remains unaffected; second, the original production and operation order of the tobacco industry remains unaffected; third, the intensity of punishing illegal activities does not decrease; fourth, lower-level laws do not need to be changed accordingly. When studying and understanding Order No. 12, six aspects should be focused on: implementing the principle of legal uniformity and eliminating conflicts with relevant national legal provisions; reflecting the practical principle and detailing operational regulations; highlighting the concept of keeping pace with the times and clarifying the legal status of innovative experiences; maintaining fairness and justice and paying more attention to protecting the legitimate rights and interests of parties; optimizing mechanisms and systems and strengthening internal legal supervision; balancing comprehensively and optimizing the penalty workflow.

The heads of all departments and units of the State Bureau and the General Corporation, members of the leadership teams of provincial, municipal, and county bureaus and managers of management departments, members of the leadership teams and managers of management departments of provincial industrial companies and their subordinate units, and members of the leadership teams and managers of management departments of other directly affiliated units in the industry attended the meeting.
