Experts at Yonghe Hair Transplant introduced issues to pay attention to after a hair transplant:

Here, the experts at Yonghe remind everyone: patients who have already reserved a recent hair transplant surgery should reduce social drinking after the Spring Festival and consider quitting smoking. Those familiar with hair transplants know that it is a microsurgical cosmetic surgery performed 2-3mm below the scalp surface, with bleeding during the procedure. To ensure proper coagulation of the scalp after surgery, patients should avoid alcohol beforehand, especially considering the likelihood of heavy drinking after the Spring Festival. These situations should be avoided in advance to prevent excessive alcohol from affecting the scalp's coagulation function and the use of antibiotics.

As for the impact of smoking on hair loss, studies show that while smoking does not directly cause baldness, it can exacerbate hair loss. Researchers from authoritative hair loss research institutions conducted long-term tracking surveys on thousands of men and found that if they smoke daily, these individuals are much more likely to experience severe hair loss. Researchers pointed out that smoking might damage hair follicles, affect blood and hormone circulation in the scalp, or lead to increased estrogen levels. Compared to European and American men, Asian men are less affected by baldness, but smoking removes this advantage. Experts at Yonghe Hair Transplant advise men showing early signs of hair loss to consider quitting smoking to prevent further deterioration.
