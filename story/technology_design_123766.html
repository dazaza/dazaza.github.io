Wealth, power, Gol D Roger, the Pirate King who once had it all, left behind one sentence before his death: "Want my wealth? Then come and find it! Everything I have is there, on the Grand Line!" Thus, more and more people rushed to the sea, sailed into the Grand Line, and the world ushered in the Great Pirate Era!

Many years later, a child swore to a one-armed pirate: "I will definitely become the Pirate King!" And then 10 years later, a great legend began.

This is a story about a group of children who love to dream. They have passion, perseverance, and companions...

Main Characters

MONKEY D LUFFY

The protagonist of this animated series. 17 years old, 172 cm tall, known as "Straw Hat Luffy." When he was little, he ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Rubber-Rubber Fruit), turning him into a rubber man with the ability to freely stretch his body. After ten years of training, he has mastered the abilities of the fruit. His motto is: "I want to become the Pirate King."

His trademark, the straw hat on his head, was given to him by the pirate Shanks as a token for their next meeting!

Personality: Very simple-minded, does what he thinks without considering the consequences (occasionally becomes smart).

Also, don't forget his terrifying appetite. For him, no matter what happens, eating is the most important thing, and the more the better. As long as it's food (especially meat), he can eat it, and at an incredibly fast speed.

RORONOA ZORO

Luffy's first companion. 19 years old, 178 cm tall.

Known as the Pirate Hunter in the East Blue.

When he was a child, he made a promise with his then-friend Kuina that he would become the world's greatest swordsman.

His mark is the pale green cloth wrapped around his waist and the dark green cloth on his left shoulder. During battles, he often ties the one on his shoulder around his head. He has no sense of direction, practices hard often, has strong wrist strength, loves to sleep, and is also the only swordsman in the world who can use three swords simultaneously, calling himself a "Santoryu" user.

NAMI

Luffy's second companion, 18 years old, 169 cm tall. Measurements: B86, W57, H86 (estimated by Sanji).

Previously a professional pirate thief, she is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She knows everything about meteorology, has excellent oratory skills, and is a negotiation expert. Her dream is to draw a complete world map with her own hands.

After her adoptive parent Bellemere was killed, she spent eight years drawing navigation maps for the Arlong Pirates, making a deal with Fishman Arlong: she would earn 100 million Berries to buy back the village where she grew up — Cocoyashi Village.

She has an open personality, drinks a lot, dresses meticulously, and frequently changes outfits. In battle, although she only knows staff techniques, when she gets angry, even Luffy can't beat her. She loves money and oranges and has them tattooed on her left arm. But for her, happiness is more important than anything. She hides a collapsible trident in her thighs, which was modified by Usopp into a Clima-Tact during their voyages.

USOPP

Luffy's third companion, 17 years old, 174 cm tall.

The gunner of the Straw Hat Pirates, son of the legendary pirate Yasopp. A young man who wants to become a brave warrior of the sea. Bragging is his daily routine. To comfort his mother and make Miss Kaya happy, he tells stories that make them laugh.

His mark is his very long nose, and he wears goggles during battles. Besides, he is multi-talented, able to draw, repair things, invent weapons, etc. Though naturally timid, he is very clever and often comes up with good plans. His weapon is a slingshot, and his offensive tactics involve deception and misdirection.

SANJI

Luffy's fourth companion, 19 years old, 177 cm tall, born in the NORTH SEA (North Blue).

The chef of the Straw Hat Pirates, originally the sous-chef of a floating restaurant. His dream is to see the mysterious sea ALL BLUE (a sea containing fish from all four seas), and he is a womanizer, loving all kinds of women. He is very strict about the quality of food.

His mark is the cigarette in his mouth, his suit, and his unique eyebrows.

To repay the upbringing of Chef Zeff, he became the inheritor of Zeff's unparalleled kick technique.

TONY TONY CHOPPER

Height varies, 15 years old, originally a reindeer beloved by Dr. Hiluluk, who passed down all his unmatched medical skills to him. Later, he became the ship's doctor and translator (Animal Language) of the Straw Hat Pirates.

From birth, he had a blue nose unlike other reindeers, giving him a strong sense of smell. Because he ate the Hito Hito no Mi (Human-Human Fruit), he gained human abilities (speech, standing, intelligence, etc.). By eating the Rumble Ball he developed himself, he can transform into seven different forms, but the effects only last for three minutes. Although he doesn't hide his feelings when speaking or acting, his heart has been deeply hurt.

NICO ROBIN

Former vice-commander of Baroque Works, 28 years old, actually an archaeologist. At just 8 years old, she single-handedly sank 8 navy ships, and because of this incident, the World Government labeled her as a dangerous person and placed a bounty of 79 million Berries on her head. However, for 20 years, she disappeared without a trace until she reappeared with Baroque Works.

As a user of the Hana Hana no Mi (Flower-Flower Fruit), she can create parts of her body anywhere she can see, making it impossible for enemies to escape.
