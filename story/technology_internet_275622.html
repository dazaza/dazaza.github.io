To the surprise of the onlookers, this car was a government official vehicle, and the person involved was the director of the Jinhua Wucheng District Government Office, a civil servant.

Driver Shi Qunyi of the Passat did not apologize for the accident and instead acted arrogantly, which drew the attention of many bystanders.
