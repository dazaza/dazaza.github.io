The court hearing for the case of Sun Yali (Weibo) from "If You Are The One" being asked to return a BMW commenced, with E Jiehao not present in court.

Basic Introduction

In the TV show "If You Are the One," after finding a marriage partner and discussing marriage, the female party suddenly backed out of the wedding. E Jiehao, 24, from Zhejiang Province, demanded that Sun Yali return a 300,000 yuan BMW car and a Sharp television.

E Jiehao claimed that in September 2010, during the recording of Jiangsu Satellite TV’s program "If You Are the One," he met Sun Yali and they established a boyfriend-girlfriend relationship, starting to discuss their marriage plans. Later, with the purpose of getting married, E Jiehao gave Sun Yali a Sharp 32-inch HD LCD TV and a BMW 318 car, which were registered under Sun Yali's name. On December 25, 2010, Sun Yali suddenly refused to marry E Jiehao and also refused to return the car gifted by him.

E Jiehao believed that although the BMW was registered under Sun Yali's name, it was actually a gift based on the purpose of marriage. Now that Sun Yali refuses to get married, she should logically return the gifts. Post-event Verification

The reporter verified this with Sun Yali's agent, who denied receiving a court summons and also denied that Sun Yali had received a BMW car from E Jiehao. He said: This is just hype. Sun Yali doesn't even know this person; she didn't meet him when she appeared on the show last September. He further stated: There are many such hyped-up stories now, and we are preparing to take legal action and clarify the matter on the show.

Character Response

At 8 o'clock on May 29, 2011, Sun Yali continuously publicly commented on this matter on her Weibo account. She claimed this was a publicity stunt and that she was being framed: Why frame me? Why use my name for publicity stunts? Why are people who don't know the truth slandering me? I will restore my innocence through the law. Beijing girls won't be defeated by someone from Huzhou framing me. She also told the media: All my friends in life and work know I am single, and there has been no engagement at home. Please do not support a bad person trying to use me for publicity stunts and harm me, a Beijing girl who shouldn't be slandered and hurt without reason.

Sun Yali responded via Weibo:

Why frame me? Why use my name for publicity stunts? Why are people who don't know the truth slandering me? I will restore my innocence through the law. Marrying just because you're involved with me is too far-fetched! Can't your family afford a TV? Your framing is so unprofessional. Your publicity stunt succeeded. You all say I have an engagement and that I broke off the engagement, but where is your evidence? All my friends in life and work know I am single, and there has been no engagement at home. This was Sun Yali's response on Weibo last night at around 8 PM. In the morning, the reporter contacted Sun Yali's agent by phone. It's malicious defamation and publicity stunts from them (referring to Sun and E). They are not boyfriend-girlfriend, nor have they discussed marriage. I'm not quite sure if they know each other, but at least I haven't heard about this person (referring to E Jiehao). Sun Yali's agent told the reporter that he learned about this yesterday afternoon, but hasn't had time to communicate with Sun Yali yet.

Case Progression

Due to her popularity from the TV show "If You Are the One," female guest Sun Yali was sued by male guest E Jiehao for backing out of their marriage and refusing to repay the gifted BMW car and TV. The case was first heard in Chaoyang Court this morning. Because Sun Yali raised jurisdictional objections before the trial, the case could not yet proceed to substantive review. This morning, Sun Yali's request to transfer the case to Dongcheng Court for hearing was rejected by Chaoyang Court. After the trial, E Jiehao's lawyer told reporters that he had sufficient evidence to prove that Sun Yali's BMW was a gift from E Jiehao, and that there was a marriage agreement between the two parties.
