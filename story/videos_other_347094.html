Thinking of this, she turned off the hot water faucet, and stormed out with great momentum, don't be afraid, don't lose heart! She strangely saw the butler, who was instructing those servants busily in the storeroom, seemingly almost done. The butler left in a hurry, it seemed like nothing was taken away? What's going on? When Yun Xiaoxiao entered the storeroom, she was astonished with her mouth wide open. There was a single bed, a beautiful and elegant single bed. How could it be? That was simply like a dream.
