Let's explore traditional Chinese medicine beauty together Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, let's change our taste and return to our traditional treasure - TCM adjustment. Traditional Chinese Medicine not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless Chinese doctors have spent their entire lives devising ways to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind rich experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, everyone, let's follow the editor and enter the world of Chinese herbal beauty together~

"Chinese Herbal Beauty" has a profound foundation of Chinese culture. After thousands of years of people's practice in beauty, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have gathered infinite oriental wisdom.

Whether it's the ancient imperial court with a long history or the modern city, women always go to great lengths to make themselves more beautiful.

And "Chinese Herbal Beauty" with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, is increasingly revered by those who value beauty and health.

But now, there are all kinds of Chinese herbal beauty products on the market. What kind of method will be effective for yourself? What kind of product will benefit you? How can we find the right product for ourselves? I think this is what everyone is most concerned about, and also the most headache-inducing problem.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found an issue: Supermarkets and malls basically don't sell Chinese herbal products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the advantages of their products, telling me how good they are after use, and that customers say they're excellent. But these are ultimately just one-sided opinions from the merchants, and it's hard for us to find actual users to understand unless our friends around us are using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product that can survive in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still prefer our traditional Chinese herbal beauty products.

Because of work, I often surf online, so I decided to use the internet to look around. Taobao is China's largest shopping and trading platform. Normally, I also frequently buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics on it. However, I haven't yet explored our own Chinese herbal beauty products online. After searching, I found there were quite a few items here. I decided to find a store with higher credibility to ask questions.

I clicked into a Chinese herbal beauty shop called "Green Woods." To be honest, what initially attracted me was the image of an ancient beauty on their products. Hehe, the desire for beauty exists in both men and women. After reading their introduction, it claims to be from a family of traditional Chinese medicine practitioners, and the products in the small shop are uniquely formulated by the old TCM doctor at home. This piqued my curiosity, wow, could he possibly prescribe a secret formula for me, turning me into a youthful beauty overnight?

After reading more about several products, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in the shop is about 50 years old, but looks like he's only 35 in the photo! That's really impressive, making me admire and envy him. I thought if I looked like this at 50, that would be fine too.

Thinking about this, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she would introduce her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, are all your products pure Chinese herbs?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure Chinese herbs. Is there anything you need?

Editor: I want to learn more first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. Which aspect would you like to know about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long will it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. It is different from general chemical or hormone-containing beauty products, which may show immediate effects. However, TCM can address both symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the issues. Our products come in one-month courses. The speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people may see results after one course, while others need more than two courses to see effects. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using the products after a few uses, claiming they don't work. Our products aren't magical elixirs, so it's impossible to see satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish complexions, are young, and have no other illnesses. These customers generally have qi and blood deficiency. We adopt a dual supplementation of qi and blood, combined with internal and external adjustments. For such customers, the adjustment usually takes at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, then claim the product doesn't work. As long as they persist, there will be effects.

Editor: It seems that using TCM methods for beauty cannot be rushed.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's condition is different, the time needed for adjustment varies. As long as customers can persist, their skin will be greatly improved compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I've learned a bit. If I need something next time, I'll come and buy. Wishing you a booming business!

Shopkeeper: Sure, you're welcome. Feel free to visit anytime.

I believe the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The shop's credibility is pretty good, with many repeat customers. I decided to check the feedback from their customers to verify the authenticity. Among many online buyers, I chose one with a high credit rating. After explaining my purpose, she was happy to chat with me. Below is part of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as A).

Editor: How long have you been using the Chinese herbal beauty products from this shop?

A: About 8 months ago. At that time, due to staying up late for a long time, my skin was in terrible condition. My complexion was dull, my skin was dry, there were fine lines, and I even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products. I wanted to find a gentle Chinese herbal spot-removal product. I stumbled upon this shop by chance and started trying their products from then on. Seeing good results, I continued using them.

Editor: What products did you use?

A: The shopkeeper helped me choose a whitening and spot-removal pill and an all-in-one whitening and spot-removal mask, along with a skin-nourishing hydration wrinkle-removal mask.

Editor: Did they really work?

A: Yes, after one month of use, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry anymore, the spots faded a little, and my skin became slightly whiter. Although I wasn't extremely white yet, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin now?

A: Now my skin is really whiter and softer, the spots have become very faint and are almost unnoticeable. With foundation, they're completely invisible. The fine lines have also become very shallow, and my skin looks moisturized and radiant. Even when I wake up in the morning, my skin still has a rosy glow. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I recommended some friends to try, and they also said the effects were good.

Editor: Have you experienced any side effects?

A: No, the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, so I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, since the efficacy of Chinese herbs is slow, it took a relatively long time.

Editor: Are you still using them now?

A: Yes. I've been taking the whitening and beauty-enhancing spot-removal pills for 6 months, and I've been consistently using the all-in-one mask and the skin-nourishing hydration wrinkle-removal mask twice a week for 8 months. The shopkeeper suggested that I take the pills every two months for another two months since it can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure really works well.

A: Yes, you can try it out and discuss with the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful aspiration for humanity, but all things in nature go through life and death, prosperity and decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective law. Therefore, with age, the human body will also age.

In TCM, although it is impossible to stay forever young, by replenishing the deficiencies of the organs and blood and qi, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the body, aging can indeed be delayed. In today's era where people value nature and green concepts, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulation, still has its unique charm in anti-aging, beauty, and health care.
