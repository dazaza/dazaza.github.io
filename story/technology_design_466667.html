Reporters investigate claims that Hebei academician Yan Peidong's company does not exist (picture)

□ Chief reporter of the Metropolis Channel, Dou Dongjie; Chief reporter of the Oriental Today, Shen Zichong

The person claiming to have 18 pieces of evidence proving the forgery of Cao Cao's tomb, "Yan Peidong," after being investigated by reporters in Hebei, revealed another name: "Liu Xubo." On the business card left by the fake Yan Peidong for Cui Xiande, the director of the Education, Science, Culture, Health, and Sports Committee of Weixian County People's Congress in Hebei, it clearly stated "Liu Xubo (Yan Peidong)" and "Director of Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center." He also claimed to be from the Longteng Shengshi company.

Yesterday afternoon, reporters from Henan TV's Metropolis Channel rushed to Beijing to continue their investigation.

[Police Station] There is no Art Village in Taiping Town, Beijing

At around 1 p.m. yesterday, the reporter traveled from Xingtai, Hebei, to Beijing. The address on the business card of the fake Yan Peidong was "Room 302, Unit 3, Building 3, Taiping Art Village, Tongzhou District, Beijing." On the commitment letter signed by the fake Yan Peidong for the People's Congress of Weixian County, there was a seal stating "Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center."

After much inquiry, the reporter only found out that there was a Taiping Village in Taiping Town in Tongzhou District. Taiping Town is more than 60 kilometers away from the city center and belongs to the suburbs.

At around 2 p.m., the reporter arrived at the Taiping Town police station in Tongzhou District, Beijing. "Taiping Art Village? What art village? Taiping Village is just Taiping Village," said the on-duty police officer, who mentioned that there were 48 villages under their jurisdiction but they had never heard of Taiping Art Village. The officer pointed to the business card left by "Yan Peidong" and said that the postal code 100032 should correspond to Xicheng District, Beijing, while the postal code for Taiping Town was 101116, "so this address is clearly fake."

A local farmer who came to the police station for business said he had lived here all his life and had never heard of a Taiping Art Village, "Are you looking for 'Artists' Village'?"

[Business Administration Office] "Longteng Shengshi Center" has never been heard of or registered

Opposite the Taiping Town government office is the Taiping Art Museum, which occupies a large area and is the only place locally related to art. There are many art companies inside, known locally as "Artists' Village." However, no one locally knows or has heard of "Yan Peidong," "Liu Xubo," or the "Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center."

At around 3 p.m., the reporter went to the Majiqiao Business Administration Office, where all enterprises in Taiping Town must register. The staff could not find any information about the "Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center" on the computer, "It should be fake. For such a company, the seal is also fake, and all procedures are fake."

Consulting a lawyer, the reporter learned that the behavior of the fake Yan Peidong may already constitute the crime of private carving of seals and contract fraud. The public security organs of Weixian County, Hebei, can completely initiate an investigation based on this.

Last night, Dou Dongjie told the reporter from the Oriental Today that up to this point, they had completely stripped off the mask of "Yan Peidong" and the so-called "Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center" as scammers, "This is a thoroughly fake company. Perhaps, precisely because of this, it dared not accept media interviews with an open attitude. And everything released by such people and such companies needs to be questioned for its authenticity."

[Reader] Calls the Oriental Today to analyze the "suspect"

"Through observation over the past period, I can almost definitely say now: Yan Peidong, Liu Xubo, and the somewhat famous planner Liu Moumou are actually three-in-one, one person, or one scheme, because there are too many coincidences here, which cannot help but make people speculate," a reader from the advertising planning industry called the reporter from the Oriental Today yesterday afternoon to analyze:

Firstly, Liu Moumou is one of the participants in the planning of the upcoming "Commemoration Ceremony of Cao Cao's 1855th Birthday" in October. "According to his style, it is very likely that he would set up a scheme to attract media attention. Moreover, during a previous interview with the media, he publicly announced that he would invite the 'anti-Cao faction' to participate in debates on the forum, and said 'Yan Peidong will definitely come.' How could he be so sure when the whole nation is still in the dark?"

Secondly, the fake Yan Peidong or Liu Xubo appeared as the "Beijing Longteng Shengshi Tourism Cultural Information Center," while Liu Moumou several years ago appeared as the "Beijing Longteng Zhongtian Performing Arts Company." "This cannot help but lead people to some natural associations."

This reader also admitted that his analysis was merely based on reasoning from existing reports, without solid evidence.

However, there are also people who hold different opinions about such analysis.

[Yan Peidong's Weibo (http://t.sina.com.cn)] Promising to reveal the truth but then disappearing

After being silent for 3 days, Yan Peidong's Sohu Weibo had new activity at around 7 p.m. yesterday. The new post only contained a short line: "Thoroughly concluding the issue of Cao Cao's tomb has begun entering the countdown, tonight I will respond to various doubts! I am currently in Henan! Yan Peidong."

As of 10 p.m. yesterday, there was still no "response" from "Yan Peidong."

Metropolis Channel will broadcast at 12:42 today in the program "Metropolis Report."
