Chunri Yue somewhat dejectedly lowered his gaze. Just now, he had involuntarily grasped Meiqi's hand; the feeling of having found a long-lost treasure stirred even his calm heart. But she loved only Qing. At this very moment, the main screen lit up.

On the screen was none other than Meiqi's father. In a white, windowless room, he was being held at gunpoint by a short woman with a cold expression.

Chunri Yue didn't look well, "Instructor," he sighed lightly.

Instructor Hopkins always cherished talent dearly. Although he lost his spiritual energy due to an operation, his rich combat experience made him the most outstanding instructor in the Special Execution Group.

"Miss Meiqi, don't you care about your father's safety? After examination, your father hasn't been infected. If you stop taking extreme actions, I will forgive you and let you join the Special Execution Group," Hopkins gradually undermined Meiqi's resolve.

"Dad!" Meiqi called out.

"Meiqi, what is going on here? Where is your mom? I remember after eating a peach with your mom, she got sick. The doctors said it was some kind of virus," Meiqi's dad, seeing his daughter, had much to say.

Meiqi paused, "You and mom ate peaches?"

Hopkins tightened his gun, "Enough chatter. Miss Meiqi, surrender."

Chunri Yue looked at the big screen with a pure and warm smile, "Instructor, your hateful daughter, Huona, was just asking me to take her to play CS live-action." That hateful and annoying Huona was the only weakness of the abnormal instructor.

Chunri Yue continued smiling, "Human life is really fragile. For example, those people parasitized by Sheyan were fine yesterday. Huona is the same; one careless moment might take away her life with a bullet."

Hopsin's facial muscles twitched twice, slowly lowering his gun.

The doctor, regardless of the menacing flying sword pointed at his head, shouted, "You said you ate peaches but weren't infected? Why is that? Could it be that there's an antibody in your body that can kill Sheyan?"

A doctor's duty is ruthless; as long as there's a glimmer of hope for the patient, he must seize it.

Something flashed through Meiqi's mind.

Could it be that Dad has the blood of the Tianyin clan flowing in his veins? Could this blood prevent Sheyan from parasitizing?

Waiting...

"Meiqi, actually, that annoying old man on the phone has an ugly name. His name is Chunri Ji," Chunri Yue hinted. Last time when he secretly confronted Meiqi, he discovered that she seemed to be able to control ancient flying swords. Since then, he had been paying attention to Meiqi's every move, and Su Qing's memory before death made him not only feel love but also realize that Meiqi was indeed the reincarnation of a witch from the Tianyin clan a thousand years ago.

Chunri? The Chunri family among the four guardian families? Meiqi's flying sword shone with a cold light. She hesitated slightly; for these thousands of lives, she might have to reveal her identity.

Meiqi tapped the doctor's head, rudely asking, "Did you just say that my dad's blood might contain antibodies?"

The audacious doctor adjusted his glasses, "We need to wait for detailed tests to confirm."

"I want to talk to one of your subordinates, Chunri Ji, preferably seeing his image on the screen," Meiqi's flying sword spun in her hand.

"No problem," the doctor smiled bitterly. Even Lord Chunri defected, so naturally, he would stand on the side of the young master.

"I am Chunri Ji," appeared on the screen, a middle-aged man with a handsome appearance and a cold demeanor. Seeing his rebellious son standing harmoniously with Meiqi, he was momentarily stunned.

"The current head of the Chunri family?" Meiqi smirked coldly, "Then things are simple. I have the Celestial Jade, and I order you to cancel the directive to cleanse Sheyan hosts and come over here."
