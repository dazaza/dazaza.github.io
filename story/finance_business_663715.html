Second, understand how much space you need. Different sized websites require different amounts of space. For building a regular website, around 100-500M should suffice; for business or gaming/entertainment sites, more space might be necessary. This also depends on the growth potential of your website. If you anticipate expanding your business and content in the future, it’s better to prepare more space upfront, say around 500M. Remember to clearly assess how much space your website actually needs to avoid wasting resources.

Third, what kind of script do you need? The type of script used during development is crucial. Otherwise, if the script is developed but the purchased space does not support the script type, it would be a waste. Additionally, if you plan to change your website from ASP to PHP later, and the space doesn’t support PHP, it could cause trouble. So, make sure to check the supported scripts before purchasing a virtual host.
