Female gamers回忆 their gaming experiences

by zxyhszzfz on 2011-06-21 09:49:16

Playing fun games with all one's might is already a thing of many years ago. Maybe when people are young, they are more persistent, and the older they get, the less fighting spirit they seem to have (hey, look at this沧桑劲儿~~), but those "little universe explosion" days are really nostalgic!!

The Wonderful Encounter of "Xianjian" and "Monopoly 4"

It took me less than three days to finish "Xianjian", including one day and night, which was also my first time staying up all night playing games. I remember it very clearly: the plot is that Li Xiaoyao and Zhao Ling'er fight to the general's tomb, walking through the maze all night, and finally come before the Sui Dynasty general's tomb. I only remember a red-clothed skeleton popping out, then using skeleton claws to dig the ground, a few hits and I was dead, and then a big red screen appeared, writing: please restart what what big侠. When I read the file again, I found that Xiao Li and Xiao Zhao were just standing dumbly at the entrance of the general's tomb. It turns out that I didn't save the game in excitement!

Then I looked at the clock, it was almost 5 o'clock in the morning, I was angry! Turn off the computer! Go to sleep! I don't know why, but in my dream, Xiao Li and Xiao Zhao were still traveling together. The annoying thing is that Xiao Li held a huge dice in his hand, throwing it once and moving one step (because he always threw 1 point). I was so angry, it doesn't matter if you move slowly, but the key is that the enemy encounter mode has also been changed to stepping on landmines, throw once, move one step, and meet an enemy once, how exhausting! Heaven, earth~~ Is this RPG game "Xianjian" or the dice-throwing "Monopoly 4"?!

"Tomb Raider" is originally a second-glance beauty

When I first bought a computer, I didn't know anything, so I borrowed a game disc from the GG next door. But this person is too honest, actually lending such an advanced game as "Tomb Raider 3" to a computer beginner. I installed the game blindly, entered it, and saw the rotating menu, but I didn't know how to enter the game hh. After a lot of trouble, I finally got into the game, but I didn't know how to operate it either. I just remember controlling a big-chested beauty sliding down a hill, then I started turning, and then running and encountering a monkey, in the melee I was solved by this "monkey king" monkey!

So I resolutely returned the game disc to the GG next door, and said resentfully: What a broken game, not fun at all! But this "broken game" was destined to become my favorite! The second time I met "Tomb Raider" was six months later, I discovered it on a pirated disc with viruses. It was the complete set of Tomb Raider, including the first edition, the second edition, the gold version of the second edition, and the gold version of the third edition. Obviously, my computer skills had improved rapidly at this time, and I had played a lot of games front and back, all kinds of values and experience levels went up crazily, so when I touched it again, I felt there was no difficulty, but I fell in love with this game absolutely because of the GG next door. He not only made "Tomb Raider" the first game I played on PC, but also let me see Lara's amazing somersaults and dives (showing off for a long time, actually pressing the shift key), so to speak, "Tomb Raider" is originally a second-glance beauty!

The Ultimate Skill in the DOS Version of "Jin Yong Qunxia Zhuan"

This is a game I have a lot of resentment towards! Many years later, I still have a deep grudge against it, deeply rooted. Because I am not a Jin Yong fan, I know nothing about the characters and plots in Jin Yong's works, but I mistakenly played "Jin Yong Qunxia Zhuan", what a great torment this will be for me! In those "nuclear explosion of little universe" days, I pondered over the puzzles in the game, lost in thought and lost appetite, at that time I was not cunning enough, had no good teacher or friend, and did not know how to check the strategy guide, I relied solely on my own wisdom to break through one problem after another.

At the beginning of the game, no one could win, I actually teamed up with the villain Tian Boguang, morality value plummeted! Making me do good deeds in the later stage, looking in the mirror every day to see if the morality value increased! And that damn wild ball fist is just garbage (later I heard that when the wild ball fist reaches level ten, other kung fu is all clouds, so I verified it in the second round, it's a pity!), so I must practice an invincible ultimate skill in the world. Fortunately, heaven favored me, allowing me to learn the ultimate skill that can attack four directions mm Taishan Eighteen Turns (later I heard that this is garbage kung fu, I thought it was a delicacy), then I actually passed the game with this "Taishan Eighteen Turns" hh. There's no need to say how difficult it was, it's all blood and tears!

"The Six Pulse Divine Sword of Tian Long Ba Bu" makes me regretful

Compared to Jin Yong Qunxia Zhuan, I hate "Tian Long Ba Bu" even more, because it is an extremely abnormal game. Its abnormality is not only reflected in the stupid pictures, weird operations, monotonous kung fu, shameless bugs, but also in its game difficulty, so I couldn't even finish it. How stupid the picture is can be seen from the picture below; the operation is weird because the direction control key actually uses Home, PageUp, End, PageDown; the kung fu is monotonous because Duan Yu only knows how to kick the left leg; the shameless bug will cause the plot to be stuck, forcing me to restart the game.

I was completely and thoroughly defeated by this game. I still remember the difficulties that prevented me from passing: at that time, Duan Yu was trapped in a square cave, with blood-replenishing stone steles at the four corners of the cave, and five skeleton monsters inside, moving very fast, with very strong attack power, about two slaps from a skeleton monster would make Duan Yu Game Over. But usually, the five skeletons would attack simultaneously, leaving no room for defense. Duan Yu, of course, still only knows the three-legged cat kung fu of kicking the left leg. I calculated that it takes about 20 kicks to kill a skeleton monster, but how would the skeleton monster stand still and let Duan Yu kick? Maybe before this foot is kicked, Duan Yu would be slapped to death. So I have to break them one by one and run around the whole field, but no matter how hard I try, the final outcome is still Game Over!!

All of the above are some interesting game memories. Since I have played countless games, there are many similar funny stories. Even I find it strange that a girl could be so interested in games and invest most of her time and energy in playing. But I think it's really worth it. Liking to play games is not like some people think, thinking that this is "playing things to lose ambition" or "being poisoned by games", zzfhhxy369_Baidu Space, at least I feel happy from games, and I get precious memories from games!
