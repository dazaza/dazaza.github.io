Man fled for 23 years after killing 60-year-old neighbor in a quarrel

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 15:31:51

According to reports, a man who calls himself "Wang Mazzi" from Yiyang's Anhua works in the mine area. He is about 50 years old, around 1.67 meters tall, with a long square face, simple clothing, short hair, able to speak Ningxiang dialect and Chenzhou dialect, introverted, and extremely vigilant. The pursuit personnel basically identified "Wang Mazzi" as the suspect Yi Wenfa, but due to the rectification of some small mines in the Ma'ao Mountain mine area, Yi Wenfa took the opportunity to escape from the mine area early on the 26th.

"Some people know that I have committed murder," Yi Wenfa said, because of his good interpersonal relationships, no one reported him to the public security organs.

Compared to fleeing, I would rather choose to accept legal punishment and serve my sentence honestly. Being captured is actually better for me, finally I can be at ease," Yi Wenfa said, deeply aware that he went down the path of crime because of impulsiveness. "In the detention center, I did not feel 'worse than death' as others said." Yi Wenfa told reporters that life here was very regular, and the guards treated him well. "If I knew it would be like this, I should have surrendered long ago. I know that the law will eventually catch up with me."

"He said I am lazy and indulges in drinking, gambling, and womanizing." Talking about the past, Yi Wenfa still became agitated.

Detective Ren told reporters that the pursuit team re-analyzed Yi Wenfa's activity trail with the police officers who handled the case originally on the day the team was established.

Living every day in fear.

Because of this incident, Yi Wenfa harbored even more hatred towards Liu Fucai, leading to increasingly fierce conflicts, frequent arguments, and fights. "Each time he cursed me saying I would have no descendants."

His first hiding place was a forest farm in Tonggu, Jiangxi, where several fellow townspeople were cutting trees. After working there for a year, the forest farm was dissolved, forcing him to find another place. Later, he fled to Yifeng County in Jiangxi, Nandan County in Guangxi, Liling County in Hunan, and returned to Chenzhou several times.

Yi Wenfa realized that he had committed a serious crime, so he hid in the mountains, secretly observing the situation at Liu's family. The next morning, hearing the sound of gongs and drums from Liu's house, he knew that he had killed Liu Fucai. He spent several difficult days and nights in the mountains before deciding to continue fleeing.

The pursuit team reported the situation to Police Chief Qi Shaoguang of Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau, who instructed: "Make sure to capture Yi Wenfa as soon as possible!" Subsequently, Deputy Director Liu Zhiliang of Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau made specific arrangements for the pursuit work again, further intensifying the investigation and pursuit efforts, seeking breakthroughs from all of Yi Wenfa's social relationships, and fully conducting the pursuit.

At 8 PM on the 29th, at the infrastructure construction site of "Shuian Xindu" in Tiaoma Town, a group of workers were sitting together playing cards. Suddenly, four men came in, asking to check their identity cards. Yi Wenfa, who was enjoying the game, thought "bad luck," and when the plainclothes police showed him the handcuffs, Yi Wenfa sighed deeply, ending his 23-year anxious and worrisome fugitive life.

"It's hard to get a girl to like me, and he goes to her family to say bad things about me." Yi Wenfa said, once he liked a girl, and she also had a good impression of him, but after her family visited Yi's home, she never contacted him again. Yi Wenfa found out that it was his neighbor Liu Fucai who spoke ill of him to her family.

During his 23-year fugitive life, Yi Wenfa stayed in Chenzhou the longest, over 10 years, because there was work available and he got familiar with the environment. "I haven't slept a single good night. Many times in dreams, I dreamt that I was wearing handcuffs." For many years, he could only behave himself outside, not daring to argue with anyone.

Yi Wenfa recalled that one day while he was taking a bath in the bathroom, he heard someone outside shouting "Run fast, they are coming to catch you," which scared him into running up the mountain in his underwear. After spending the night in the mountain, he escaped to Chaling.

Again narrowly missing the pursuit target, the pursuit team from Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau did not rush back but continued working in Chenzhou. In further visits, some people reflected that before leaving the mine area on October 26, 2011, "Wang Mazzi" mentioned going to Changsha, but the exact location was unknown. After nearly a month of intensive investigation, the pursuit team again determined that the suspect Yi Wenfa was highly likely to have fled to Tiaoma Town, Changsha County.

A tragedy caused by an argument

On October 2004, Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau received information that Yi Wenfa was using the alias "Wang Mazzi" in the Ma'ao Mountain Mine Area of Suchuan District, Chenzhou City, engaging in mining and illegal trading of ore business. The informant said, "Wang Mazzi" could speak fluent Chenzhou dialect and cohabited with a mistress locally. Ningxiang police immediately organized the pursuit of Yi Wenfa.

On October 26, 2011, Detective Zhang Haijun, Deputy Team Leader Fu Tao, and Director Xia Xueyu of the Letters and Visits Office rushed to Ma'ao Mountain Mine Area, Aoshang Town, Suchuan District, Chenzhou City, to conduct investigations. During the secret visitation and investigation over the next six days, many people provided information to the pursuit team.

Because of a few words of quarrel with his neighbor, 31-year-old Yi Wenfa beat his over-60-year-old neighbor to death with a stick. Then began a 23-year-long period of fleeing everywhere.

"Dreaming of myself wearing handcuffs"

In November 29, 2011, Deputy Director Liu Zhiliang of Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau led Detective Team Leader He Ren, Deputy Team Leader Fu Tao, and Officer Chen Shifang to Tiaoma Town urgently. With the strong support of Changsha Municipal Public Security Bureau and Changsha County Public Security Bureau, a large-scale pursuit net quickly unfolded, and the pursuit team conducted detailed checks on local infrastructure construction sites.

On August 15, 1988, while chopping wood, Yi Wenfa cut a tree from the village, discovered by then-guardian Liu Fucai, leading to another verbal altercation. "He cursed me 'you will have no descendants,'" Yi Wenfa said, thinking about being unmarried at 31 and being cursed like that, he could no longer bear it. He picked up a wooden stick and ran to Liu Fucai's house, intending to warn him. But Liu Fucai was not scared, and young and impulsive Yi Wenfa struck him on the head with a stick, causing Liu Fucai, over 60 years old, to fall down instantly.

Police Chief Qi Shaoguang of Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau told reporters from the Legal System Weekly that since the Ministry of Public Security deployed the "Clear Web Action," Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau has decomposed the task of pursuing online fugitives, strictly implementing the "six ones" work measures of "one fugitive, one file, one responsible leader, one pursuit team, one pursuit plan, and pursue until the end." Yi Wenfa was listed as the number one pursuit target, and Detective Team Leader He Ren was designated as the pursuit team leader.

Original case-handling officer Xia Xueyu recalled that in 2005, the masses again reported that "Wang Mazzi" appeared in a certain mine area in Suchuan District, Chenzhou City. Upon hearing the news, the case-handling officers immediately rushed to the mine area, but the arrest was unsuccessful again.

On November 29, 2011, the pursuit personnel from Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau of Changsha suddenly appeared in front of Yi Wenfa, who was happily playing cards with his coworkers. He sighed in sorrow, knowing that his fugitive life had come to an end.

Based on this, the police of the pursuit team analyzed that the possibility of Yi Wenfa still being active in the Chenzhou area was extremely high, and decided to go to Chenzhou again for investigation.

During the interview conclusion, Yi Wenfa suddenly grabbed the reporter's hand and said, "Thank you for giving me the opportunity to express the pain in my heart. I hope those who are on the run like me go to the public security organs to surrender as soon as possible, ending the fearful and inhumane fugitive life, and sleep soundly."

After being interrogated by Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau, the suspect Yi Wenfa confessed without reservation to the crime of killing the mountain guard Liu Fucai. Currently, the suspect Yi Wenfa has been criminally detained by Ningxiang County Public Security Bureau, and the case is under further investigation.

Yi Wenfa was born in 1957 in Dashi Village, Machaizu Group, Xiangzi Port Town, Ningxiang County, Hunan Province. Both of his parents passed away when he was young. He was the sixth child, with three elder brothers and two elder sisters. Liu Fucai was Yi Wenfa's neighbor, separated only by a wall, and the two families often argued over trivial matters. When young, Yi Wenfa was very "righteous," often helping others and making friends. After his parents passed away, Yi Wenfa lived with his older brother, but later moved out because he couldn't stand his sister-in-law's discipline. Due to poverty, he was still unmarried at 31.

When asked if he missed his family during these years of fleeing, Yi Wenfa lowered his head and told the reporter, "Not really, because they didn't care much about me either." He said that he had returned home several times in the past 23 years, but usually at night. He just looked around and never brought anything for his family.

For many years, he dared not stay in hotels, or even go to the hospital when sick. Yi Wenfa had several times considered surrendering, "but people around me told me that once in prison, it would be worse than death." So he ultimately didn't go.

"I have been fleeing for 23 years, which is more painful than serving a sentence," Yi Wenfa lived in constant fear and torment.

One midnight, Yi Wenfa sneaked back home to pack clothes, then began his fearful and nightmare-filled fugitive life. "I dared not take the bus or walk on main roads; I always detoured through the mountains," Yi Wenfa said, changing his name to Yi Jianwen, with the nickname "Wang Mazzi."

Recently, at Ningxiang County Detention Center, the reporter from the Legal System Weekly met Yi Wenfa, who was full of regret. At the end of the interview, he suddenly grabbed the reporter's hand and said, "Thank you for giving me the opportunity to express the pain in my heart. I hope those who are on the run like me go to the public security organs to surrender as soon as possible, ending the fearful and inhumane fugitive life, and sleep soundly."
