**Dedicated Three-way Pressure-free Faucet for Water Purifiers** (Imported from Japan):

- Made from lead-free brass to avoid secondary lead contamination.

- Structurally designed as pressure-free, when the faucet is closed, the water source entering the filter core is cut off, resulting in zero pressure inside the filter core, reducing the risk of filter core rupture and leakage to zero. This avoids potential water leakage risks that could threaten the luxurious decoration of homes.

- When the faucet is closed, the water in the water purifier can still be drained under atmospheric pressure until it is completely emptied, avoiding long-term immersion of the filter core in water and extending its lifespan, providing safety and economy for household drinking water.

- Remember: All water purification equipment should be equipped with pressure-free faucets. Using pressurized faucets is very dangerous, as the filter core might burst under sudden high water pressure.

- Distinguishing between the two types of faucets: A pressurized faucet can only connect to one soft tube, while a pressure-free faucet has three copper tubes.
