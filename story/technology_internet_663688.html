The Heart Sutra of Beauty

by wswen356 on 2012-02-12 12:54:56

Common people are always curious about what kind of cosmetics let stars have shiny skin and remain young forever. In fact, apart from various kinds of skincare products, they also have their own exclusive whitening experiences. Today, the editor will unveil the secrets for you! Kelly Chen is not only a good singer, but her naturally delicate skin is also enviable! In fact, Kelly has never deliberately taken care of herself. She thinks that one's skin quality is innate. Some people have normal diet and sufficient sleep, but their skin is not good, and will not change because of using some skincare products. However, Kelly attaches great importance to the basic care every day. She washes her face, applies toner and lotion every morning and evening without being lazy. Stacey Francis, who is famous for her ancient costume drama, also has a bit of "antique" concept in choosing skincare products. She believes in pure plant compound products. According to her, pure plant compounds can prevent women's skin from entering the aging stage in advance, further enhance the firmness and elasticity of the skin, keep the skin moist and bright for a long time, especially suitable for oriental women's skin quality. In addition to adhering to skincare, GiGi believed that it was necessary to coordinate all aspects to make the skin better. For example, sleep and diet were very important. For artists, adequate sleep was often a luxury, so they had to make up for it in other ways. GiGi improved and maintained her skin through her eating habits. She never ate fried or spicy food. Only in this way did GiGi realize her skincare plan. Generally speaking, women believe that it is the most appropriate time to start taking care of their skin at the age of 25. But in fact, there is no time limit for skin care. The earlier you start, the more you will gain. At the age of 7, Joey Yung started using Mama made skincare products, and at the age of 10, she knew how to make her own mask to take care of her skin. At the same time, after entering the music circle, Joey's work became increasingly busy, plus insufficient sleep, which was very unfavorable to skin care. The secret of Joey's still water spirit skin lies in the early start of skin care and maintenance. Maggie Cheung, who often shoots outside, pays great attention to the moisturizing and sun protection of her skin. In addition to using highly effective moisturizing skincare products, Maggie prepares a small kettle every day to replenish the water in her body at any time. In addition to moisturizing, Maggie also pays great attention to sun protection and whitening. She said that especially in winter, although the sunshine is not too strong, sun protection work should not be ignored. Sunscreen or isolation cream should be applied during outdoor activities and facing the computer.
