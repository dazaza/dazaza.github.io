How to effectively reduce fine lines? Professional demonstration

by liu514113345 on 2012-03-01 22:27:37

The eyelids blink more than ten thousand times a day. Over the years, this causes a loss of collagen and elastin within the eyelids. Moreover, the skin around the eyes is only 0.02~0.05mm thick, making it very thin. Additionally, as there is no sebum protection, the area is prone to dryness, which can lead to the formation of fine lines around the eyes! In this video, we will teach you how to use skincare and massage techniques to improve these fine lines and avoid them turning into deep wrinkles!

1. Differences between eye gel and eye cream

Eye gel: lighter texture, suitable for oily or acne-prone skin

Eye cream: anti-wrinkle and firming effects, suitable for those with early signs of wrinkles or dry skin

Summer usage tip: use a refreshing eye gel during the day and a more nourishing eye cream at night

2. What to do if wrinkles and fine lines have already formed?

We recommend using skincare products with anti-wrinkle effects.

These products can promote the production of collagen in the skin around the eyes, smoothing out fine lines and achieving a lifting effect simultaneously.

3. When to use eye gel or eye cream

We suggest using them after toner or serum.

If you are used to using serum, apply the eye gel or eye cream after the serum step. If not, then use them after the toner step.

4. Demonstration of basic eye massage techniques (daily care)

Tip: Use your ring finger for the eye massage.

When performing an eye massage, use your ring finger, which applies the least force, to avoid over-stretching the delicate skin around the eyes.

Product amount: Adjust according to the area covered, approximately the size of a grain of rice.

5. Demonstration of basic eye massage techniques (daily care)

Step 1: Gently dab the skincare product around the eyes and use pressing and tapping motions from the inner to outer corners to enhance absorption.

During the eye massage, be sure to move gently; avoid dragging or pulling to prevent negative effects.

6. Demonstration of basic eye massage techniques (daily care)

Step 2: For those with high eye pressure, focus on pressing specific acupoints around the eyes.

People who wear glasses often have higher eye pressure. At such times, you can press the acupoints around the eyes to promote blood circulation and relieve fatigue and discomfort.

7. Demonstration of eye massage techniques (to combat wrinkles, fine lines, and sagging)

Step 1: Apply eye cream around the eyes and use pressing motions from the inner to outer corners to enhance absorption.

For issues like wrinkles, fine lines, and sagging, we recommend using eye cream for the massage. Similarly, apply the eye cream around the eyes and use pressing motions from the inner to outer corners to enhance absorption.

Suggestion: If the wrinkles and fine lines are deeper, you can apply a bit more pressure for a deeper massage.

8. Demonstration of eye massage techniques (to combat wrinkles, fine lines, and sagging)

Step 2: Alternate lifting the skin under the eyebrows with your thumbs.

Through this simple skincare and massage routine, you can not only achieve a lifting effect but also improve swelling issues, making the area around the eyes brighter!

Skincare usually takes time to show results and is often neglected due to laziness. If you can form a habit and make it part of your daily skincare routine, over time, you will definitely see improvements.
