V. Cooperation Plan

Cooperation Plan One: Direct Purchase of Breeding Stock

General breeders (one set) can directly come to our company for on-site inspection and purchase breeding stock. We provide free professional breeding training, along with free breeding technical data, video, and a set of patent molds for breeding beds.

Large-scale breeders (three sets or more, including three sets) can directly come to our company for inspection and learning, sign the contract, and pay 30% down payment initially. According to customer requirements, our company can send technicians to guide the construction of the site. After the site construction is completed, the remaining 70% of the breeding stock fee will be paid, and the company will then issue all the mother breeding stock. The company will also give six sets of patent molds for breeding beds.
