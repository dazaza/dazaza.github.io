City appearance staff and vendors point fingers at each other over causing injuries (picture group)

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 09:39:25

Mr. Liu told the reporter that he is from Chuzhou, Anhui Province. He has been in Nanjing for several years and sells fruit on the side of the road at Bai Shui Qian Cheng in Mawei, Qixia District. At around 7 pm on January 19th, four enforcement vehicles with nearly 30 city appearance officers arrived at their fruit-selling spot and began confiscating items from street vendors. The city appearance officers not only forcibly seized the vendors' goods but also physically assaulted the vendors. During the enforcement process, they even pulled out pepper spray and sprayed it wildly. Even two female vendors and Mr. Liu's five-months-pregnant wife were not spared. The next day, Mr. Liu and a few small vendors who wanted their confiscated fruits back went to the city appearance management office. Mr. Liu found one of the city appearance officers who was on duty the night of January 19th, grabbed him, and asked him to take the injured pregnant woman to the hospital. Unexpectedly, this action angered the city appearance officer again, and Mr. Liu was beaten. When the city appearance officers beat people, they simply replaced the pepper spray with iron stools. On the evening of January 19th, the pregnant woman who was beaten that day did not escape either. In the scuffle, blood flowed from the corner of her mouth, and her stomach collided with the edge of the desk. The pregnant woman screamed in pain as she fell to the ground. However, it was not until 20 minutes later when the police arrived that the ambulance took the pregnant woman away. Mr. Liu believes that his wife, who was injured, has been hospitalized for several days. Initially, the pregnant woman was in great danger. But up until now, no leader from the city appearance department has come forward to resolve the matter. Moreover, Mr. Liu's younger brother also expressed that while accompanying his older brother and sister-in-law to the city appearance management office to retrieve the fruits, his head was struck by a city appearance officer with a stool, resulting in five stitches at the hospital.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter received a call from Mr. Liu, who does fruit business in Nanjing from Anhui Province, reflecting that on January 19th and 20th, his five-months-pregnant wife was beaten twice at Bai Shui Qian Cheng in Qixia District and the city appearance office in Nanjing. Now his wife is hospitalized, yet no one has stepped forward to resolve the issue. Regarding this, the reporter found the local city appearance management office. They believe that this incident was caused by the refusal of the small vendors to cooperate with law enforcement. Later, the small vendors brought people to attack the city appearance management office, injuring several city appearance officers, but during the rectification process, the officers were very restrained and did not retaliate. How exactly did this incident happen? The reporter conducted an investigation yesterday afternoon.

At 2:50 PM yesterday, the reporter found Mr. Liu who reported the situation at Zhongshan Hospital in Xialingwei, Nanjing. His wife was lying on the bed in the gynecology ward receiving treatment.

The reporter then went to the city appearance management office of Mawei Street. In the office, several eyewitness city appearance officers stated that the cause of the conflict on the 20th was that Mr. Liu brought people to attack the city appearance management office, injuring several of their officers. The most severely injured officer had his nasal bone broken by the people Mr. Liu brought.

At 4:30 PM yesterday, the reporter rushed to the Mawei Police Station where the case was handled. Instructor Li of the police station received the reporter's interview and stated that the police had already filed an investigation into the fighting incident. Instructor Li said that these small vendors belong to unauthorized roadside operations. On January 19th, when the city appearance personnel were dealing with the unauthorized roadside vendors, there had been disputes. Later, the police station also dispatched police forces to the scene to participate in coordination and handling. The city appearance management office indeed confiscated the fruits and other items of Mr. Liu and other vendors. In the morning of January 20th, the leaders of the police station and the city appearance office, along with the vendors, were negotiating to handle the incidents of the evening of January 19th. Unexpectedly, another fight occurred between the small vendors and the city appearance office. After the police arrived, they found that three city appearance officers had minor injuries and one had a nasal fracture, while the small vendor side had one minor injury. As for the pregnant woman being beaten, after preliminary investigation by the police, it seemed that no one laid a hand on her. For more detailed circumstances, relevant materials have been sent to the district criminal investigation team for filing, and further investigations and evidence collection will be carried out on both sides. The reporter subsequently learned from Wang Xiao, a female city appearance officer who witnessed the entire incident, that at around 2:30 PM on the 20th, she and another female officer were on duty at the unit when Mr. Liu and four men and two women came to the city appearance management office. Emotionally agitated, Mr. Liu and others kicked and threw office furniture and broke the tea cups of the city appearance officers. Fifteen minutes later, five male and two female city appearance officers returned one after another to deal with the situation. The first male officer, Wang Dong, was surrounded and attacked, and the returning city appearance officer Lin Zhi was also beaten. However, the officers were very restrained and no one retaliated. At this time, the second batch of city appearance officers on patrol returned. Officer Huan Jin returned to the office and unexpectedly was hit on the leg with an iron stool by several small vendors, resulting in a nasal bone fracture. Officer Huan Jin is currently staying on the fifth floor of Zhongshan Hospital. Officer Wang explained that during the disturbance caused by the small vendors, more than ten of us returned. If the city appearance officers had beaten them, they definitely would not have been able to leave the door of the office. Besides, everyone knows that pregnant women cannot be touched, let alone violently beaten. As for Mr. Liu's wife falling in the city appearance management office, Wang believed that it should be because Mr. Liu's wife saw her husband fighting with others, became overly nervous, or was accidentally pushed down while trying to mediate.
