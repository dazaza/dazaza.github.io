Nanjing's real estate market sees a price-cutting trend with the maximum reduction approaching 13% (Part I)

by dsdfsd32 on 2010-05-12 16:33:52

Nanjing's Jiangbei real estate market suddenly experiences a price-cutting storm yesterday. With super-large scale real estate projects standing tall and always leading in Nanjing’s development and sales volume, the Jiangbei real estate market suddenly went wild yesterday! Following the Xuri Shangcheng project offering a fixed price of 4500 yuan/sq m for over 300 units, Suning Properties also launched a fixed-price strategy for its three Qiaobei projects: the Tianruncheng project set a fixed price of 4088 yuan/sq m for 350 units, which is about 600 yuan/sq m cheaper than before. The continuous discounting and promotion by Qiaobei projects - does their collective price drop mean that the Jiangbei housing prices can no longer hold up? Is this a sign of an overall decline in Nanjing's housing prices?

[Price Drop] Three more projects offer fixed-price units

The super-large Tianruncheng project, which surprisingly offered a huge discount with a fixed price of 4088 yuan/sq m, has a total of 350 special-priced units distributed across different buildings.

15% of the total available units are on special offer

Coincidentally, two other projects in the Qiaobei area of the Jiangbei sector also adopted a fixed-price strategy: 150 units of the Venice Water City at a uniform price of 4480 yuan/sq m, and 50 units of the Tianhua Green Valley at a uniform price of 4188 yuan/sq m. However, it was learned that these two projects have strict restrictions on the floors of the units, such as Project B targeting the first floor and top floor units, and Project C targeting the 1st to 6th floors of high-rise buildings, the first floor and 6th floor of multi-story buildings.

In total, there are 550 special-priced units. What proportion do they account for among the available units on sale? According to data from Nanjing Online Real Estate, these three projects currently have a total of 3360 available units, meaning the 550 special-priced units account for more than 16% of the total available units.

An industry insider stated that for a major player like Suning in Qiaobei, 550 units spread across three projects are just a drop in the bucket. However, considering the current total number of available units, a ratio of 16% is still relatively large.

For specific groups only? Sales office says everyone can buy

Interestingly, Suning Properties' unified statement yesterday was: the 550 units from the three projects were specially selected to give back to society and show social humanistic care, targeting provincial and municipal model workers, advanced workers, soldiers, and others who have contributed to society.

However, when reporters asked separately at these three projects, the sales staff all said there were no such restrictions, and buyers could purchase normally.

Why is the developer being so secretive? An industry insider speculated that the developer might be concerned that previous buyers who feel they bought at a loss would demand refunds or returns.

[Public Reaction] Both those who want to buy and those who don't have reasons

Citizen Mr. Shi expressed that if the fixed-price discount only applies to the first floor and top floor units, it doesn't have much significance. But he noticed that some fixed-price offers aren't exclusively for the first and top floors; for example, Project C also offers fixed-price discounts for floors 1 to 6 in high-rise buildings. Units on the 4th and 5th floors priced at 4480 yuan/sq m are worth considering.

Nevertheless, Mr. Shi also believed that compared to the prices he had been paying attention to, these fixed-price projects are hundreds of yuan per square meter cheaper, and with five or six hundred units involved, the scope of the price cut is quite extensive. He decided to visit the properties over the weekend.

However, some citizens are indifferent to the price cuts in Jiangbei projects. Citizen Ms. Lu said that recently, she took a bus to Jiangbei to check out properties but got stuck in traffic for over 2 hours on the bridge. By the time she reached the property site, she had lost all enthusiasm. She feels that living there with such transportation conditions would be too inconvenient. Despite the significant price reduction, she remains cautious and won't consider buying a home in Jiangbei for now.

[Industry Analysis] Qiaobei's goal of reaching 5000 yuan/sq m bursts

Last year, projects in Jiangbei aimed to reach 5000 yuan/sq m, but now it seems they've returned to their original state. Vice General Manager Shi Dong of Nanjing Wangshang Real Estate Research Institution analyzed that from an average transaction price of less than 3500 yuan/sq m last February to struggling to maintain at 4500 yuan/sq m now, breaking through 5000 yuan/sq m has clearly failed.

This can also be seen from the comparison of sales volumes over the same period: in February this year, the sales volume of Pukou's projects dropped by 74% month-on-month, putting pressure on developers.

According to predictions by the Wangshang Real Estate Research Institution, this year, Jiangbei will see the launch of 3.313 million sq m of new commercial residential properties (slightly higher than 3.21 million sq m in 2007), including 2.865 million sq m of newly approved listings, leading all sectors in Nanjing. This year, the 45 ongoing projects in the Jiangbei sector will add 2.8 million sq m of new supply, and the concentrated and large-scale supply of commercial residential properties will inevitably intensify competition in the Qiaobei real estate market.
