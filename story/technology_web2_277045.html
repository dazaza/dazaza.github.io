Tianma River Manor Phase 2: Next to the metro, inside a park, beside the Tianma River

Tianma River Manor is located within the Tianma River International Park on Fengshen Avenue in Huadu District, Guangzhou City, west of the Xinhua toll station on the Guangzhou-Qingyuan Expressway, adjacent to the automobile city area which will be an important economic engine for Huadu District in the future. It is about 800 meters away from the "Automobile City" station of Guangzhou Metro Line 9. Tianma River Manor is a rare large-scale upscale community in the urban center of Huadu District with extensive ecological green space. The total land area exceeds 700 mu (about 46.67 hectares), and the total construction area exceeds 1 million square meters. It is a rare large community in the urban center of Huadu District. The first phase of Tianma River Manor consists entirely of Mediterranean-style villas and small high-rise apartment buildings, some of which have already been completed. The style is elegant and simple, with lush greenery in the gardens, featuring the characteristics of Mediterranean architecture. The "Blue Tianma River" group is the second phase of the Tianma River Manor, located to the south of the first phase group.

The "Blue Tianma River" group is expected to publicly release its first batch of units in December. The sales site is currently accepting preferential registration. Customers who register with their personal information will receive an additional 95% discount offered by the developer on the opening day. The publicly released price is expected to be around 5500-5800 yuan/square meter, with a 1000 yuan/square meter renovation package. The total volume of units in the "Blue Tianma River" group is more than 5000 sets, and the first batch will release 1000 apartment units at once. It is reported that the monthly residential transaction volume in the entire Huadu District is about 1000 sets, and the volume of the second phase of this project is equivalent to five months of the transaction volume in the entire Huadu District.

Fengshen Community (data, forum, resale)

Located on the north side of Fengshen Avenue in Huadu, Fengshen Community is expected to go on sale at the end of November. The unit types range from 90-210 square meters, including two-bedroom to five-bedroom units, with a bare unit price of 5500 yuan/square meter. The project is fully ready for immediate occupancy, and currently undergoing sincere registration, offering a 98% discount on home purchases and a 20,000 yuan home purchase discount.

Guangzhou Poly City Garden (data, forum, resale)

The apartment buildings at Guangzhou Poly City Garden, located at the intersection of Fengshen Avenue and Huagang Avenue in the automobile city area of Huadu District, have been completely sold out. Currently on sale are the "Windsor Castle" attached townhouses, ranging in size from 230-445 square meters, with only over ten sets available, starting at 10,000 yuan/square meter for bare units. A discount of 200,000 yuan per set is offered when purchasing the attached townhouses. Poly City will launch a new apartment group early next year, with an estimated volume of several hundred sets, mainly consisting of two or three-bedroom units.
