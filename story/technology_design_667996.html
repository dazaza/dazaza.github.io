User Manual for Jin Sanshun Connection Machine and Jin Sanshun Gaming Machine

◆ Complete company table system, capable of remote telephone accounting, simultaneous recording of accounts by the main unit and sub-units, power outage will not lose account records.

◆ The main unit displays real-time accounts, no need to stop the machine to check accounts. Just look at the account displayed on the main unit to hand over the shift.

◆ All music uses popular CD music, and can be selected through the main unit whether to play or not, convenient for different needs.

◆ Only one wire is needed to connect 8 machines, greatly improving the stability of the machine.

◆ New program, combining the procedures of 12-door animal connection and 12-door racing side line.

◆ This machine adopts Chinese LCD display. Account checking and adjustment all have Chinese prompts, simple and easy to understand, convenient operation. Stable reliability, fun to play, and guaranteed profit.

Second: Connection Usage Method

◆ After connecting the communication lines in order, start from No.8 machine and turn on the power one by one until finally turning on the power of No.1 machine (the main unit is inside No.1 machine). Alternatively, you can turn on the power of each of the 8 units and the main unit simultaneously. If there's no need for coin insertion or withdrawal, please disconnect the coin return key line on the control panel and seal the coin insertor.

Third: Main Unit Usage Method

◆ For normal use, just ensure that the communication line is properly connected and the power is turned on. During normal operation, the main unit displays the running status. During the betting process, the top row shows the remaining betting time before the lights start running, while the bottom row shows the total profit. During the process of the lights running and winning, the top row shows the remaining running time (i.e., how much time is left until coding, in minutes), it is best to code before the remaining 1000 minutes to avoid stopping the machine during play, the bottom row also shows the total profit. During normal operation, if a shift change is required, just record the total profit shown in the bottom row to clearly settle the accounts. Since this machine adopts the most advanced and reliable method currently available, verification is extremely strict. Therefore, before changing shifts, ensure that no points are added or deducted for two rounds so that the main unit can accurately record all sub-unit accounts.

◆ To report and check accounts, first enter the menu state. There are two ways to enter the menu: ① While the machine is running, press the "function key", showing "waiting to return". After one round ends, the menu will appear. ② In case of a fault with the main unit, hold down the function key and turn on the power to directly enter the menu. After entering the menu, there are four options: "check accounts", "report accounts", "parameters", "system". Press the "select" key to move the cursor. When the cursor points to the function you need, press confirm to enter the function. (Be sure to note: to avoid unnecessary trouble, make sure all sub-units must have their power on and normally connected before entering the menu.)

◆ If the main unit's LCD has backlighting but does not display or shows overlapping images or too dark colors, open the back cover of the main unit. On the main board, there is a color adjustment switch. Clockwise is lighter, counterclockwise is darker.

◆ After entering the account checking, you can choose to check the main unit or sub-unit accounts. Press the "move" key to check either the main or sub-unit accounts. After confirming, you can enter. Inside the main unit, you can check all the accounts of the main unit. The first page shows the line number on top and the machine number below. Confirm to enter the total profit (which means the total profit of all sub-units combined), confirm again to enter the total coins inserted (all sub-units' coins inserted + last time), confirm again to enter the total coins returned (all sub-units' coins returned + deduction), confirm again to enter the current profit (profit from the last barcode scan to now, i.e., total profit on the second page - total profit on the sixth page from the previous time), confirm again to enter the previous total profit (total profit of all sub-units at the last barcode scan), confirm again to enter the actual profit at the last barcode scan, confirm again to enter the running time (cumulative running time from the last barcode scan to now), confirm again to enter the number of times the barcode was scanned, confirm again to enter the machine number (to facilitate maintenance and reporting, each group of machines has a total payout - how many points all sub-units have won in total, the next six pages are backup pages, not yet used here, press confirm twice to return to the total profit, the entire account checking for the main unit is complete. Press return to exit the main unit account checking. Note: There are many things recorded here to make it more convenient to settle accounts when cooperating. If you cannot fully understand them, just record the total profit, which is sufficient.

◆ Press the move key to select checking sub-units, confirm to enter. After entering the sub-unit, input the number of the sub-unit you want to check to view the corresponding sub-unit accounts, including the total profit of each sub-unit, the total coins inserted by each sub-unit, and the total coins returned by each sub-unit. Press return to exit.

◆ After entering the account reporting, it shows the total profit of all sub-units from the last reset (page 00), the actual profit of all sub-units from the last barcode scan (page 03), the total number of times the barcode was scanned (page 07), the machine number (page 08), and the verification code (page 15). Finally, it prompts to input the barcode. Entering the correct barcode successfully reports the accounts. However, after entering the account reporting, all sub-units also enter the reporting state, all sub-units display 76, and it is not allowed to press return to exit. All sub-units and the main unit must be powered off to exit the reporting state.

◆ After entering the parameters, you can set: light-running payout rate (first press delete to remove the original payout rate, then press the numeric keys to input the desired payout rate, minimum 00.00 maximum 99.9, default is 94.0, note: decimal points should not be omitted) payout ratio (not used here), coin insertion ratio (press delete to choose, options include 1 coin = 100 points, 1 coin = 1 point, 1 coin = 2 points, 1 coin = 5 points, 1 coin = 10 points, 1 coin = 20 points, 1 coin = 50 points, 1 coin = 80 points, eight choices, default is 1 coin = 10 points, note: scoring ratio is ten times the coin ratio) maximum bet (press delete to choose, options include 99 points, 50 points, 95 points, three choices, default is 99 points) bet ratio (how many points per bet, arcade games, press delete to choose, options include 1 point, 2 points, 5 points, 10 points, four choices, default is 1 point), startup music (whether to sing after winning, press delete to choose, on and off, default is on, if you do not want to sing after winning, you can choose off), multiplier size (choose large multipliers 4-70x or small multipliers 4-46x, press delete to choose, default is small multipliers) bet time (minimum 15 seconds, maximum 60 seconds, first press delete to remove the original seconds, then press the numeric keys to input the desired seconds, default is 30 seconds). Note that all default parameters are set according to the majority of market needs by our company. If there is no special need, please do not modify. Because modifying any parameter requires the main unit to be connected to all sub-units, and the accounts must be reported twice to reset (for details on resetting, refer to the account reporting instructions).

◆ Entering the system requires the manufacturer password, which is not introduced here.

◆ Holding down the test key is inconvenient. Turn on the main unit power, input the sub-unit number to test the communication between the main unit and the sub-unit. The top shows the total number of communications, the bottom shows the number of errors. If there are errors, check the communication connection line.

Four: Fault Clearing

◆ Main unit fault: Internal error appears, report accounts twice to reset, which can eliminate most faults. 08 indicates reaching the highest account, please report accounts twice to reset. 09 indicates time reached, please report accounts once.

◆ Sub-unit fault: If a fault code starting with 4 appears, such as 40, 41, etc., it is a connection fault. First check if there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, if the connection line is not connected, or if the communication on the computer board is not plugged in. If all these are normal, you just need to turn on the power of this sub-unit and other sub-units, then report accounts twice to reset. If there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, hold down K3 of this sub-unit without releasing, turn on the power, press the start key to flip to page 8, then press the coin return key to modify the sub-unit number. Finally, after connecting the main unit and all sub-units, report accounts twice on the main unit to reset (sub-units must be connected normally and powered on), ten people playing fishing machines. If the sub-unit or main unit has been sent back to the factory for repair, when reconnected, the main unit must report accounts twice to reset (sub-units must be connected normally and powered on).

◆ Sub-unit faults can be handled in the following steps: ① Check the coin insertion/return switch for codes 30, 31, ② If there is no problem, hold down K0+K3 or the score up/wash score keys without releasing, turn on the power, show 66, this sub-unit resets, ③ If it still doesn't work, check if the fault code starts with 4, if so, check if the communication connection line is faulty, ④ If the connection is confirmed to be fine, check if there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, ⑤ If there aren't any, connect all sub-units and the main unit together, turn on the power, and continuously report accounts twice on the main unit to reset. Note: For convenience in repairing sub-units, in most cases of sub-unit faults, you don't need to turn off the power for repairs. We have specially made a hot start switch K2. In case of sub-unit faults, just press K2 to restart the sub-unit in hot start mode. However, K2 does not work if the sub-unit has no fault. Of course, you can also turn the machine on/off to resolve issues.

Five: Account Reporting Instructions

◆ First press the function key to enter the menu, press the move key to select account reporting, press confirm to enter account reporting. Fully report via short message or phone the contents of pages 00, 03, 07, 08, and 15. Finally, it will prompt you to "input barcode". After receiving the barcode; input the correct barcode to successfully report the accounts. Note: Without proper account reporting, the barcode cannot be calculated, or the calculated barcode cannot be entered.

◆ If you need to reset, repeat the above account reporting process, and you can reset. Note: During reset, sub-units cannot add or deduct points.

◆ When the main unit cannot check accounts due to some reason, you can check the accounts of all sub-units. Method for sub-unit account checking: Hold down the K0 key, or open the score lock and hold down the score key, turn on the power, page 0 total profit, page 1 total coins inserted, page 2 total coins returned, eight people playing fishing machines, page 3 current actual profit, press the start key to flip pages. Then add up the accounts of all sub-units, which is the account of the main unit.

◆ Special attention: For sub-unit account checking, if the JP light is on, it indicates a negative number. The JP light is located between the total score column and the score column.

◆ Additionally, when the main unit encounters faults such as 06, 01, etc., the stored main unit and sub-unit accounts in the main unit may be incorrect. At this time, the accounts of each sub-unit must be taken as the standard.
