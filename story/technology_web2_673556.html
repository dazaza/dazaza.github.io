Ruba helps "Jiangxi Fashion" upgrade in the new market

by robooseo514 on 2012-02-23 17:02:18

Roboo helps "Jiangxi Garments" to upgrade in the new market. In the early 21st century, China's commercial market welcomed the first wave of e-commerce, with B2B, B2C, and C2C models emerging one after another, and online shopping obviously became a fashion. In 2011, media reports about mobile internet flooded various websites, and the industry predicted that China's commercial market would welcome a second wave of e-commerce.

Before the arrival of the second wave of e-commerce, Dr. Han Song, CEO of Roboo Technology located in Suzhou Industrial Park, saw the potential for mobile market development as early as 2006. He believed that the future would be an era where mobile internet coexisted with computer internet. Thus, Dr. Han Song led a group of high-tech personnel to establish Roboo (http://www.roboo.com/) Technology Co., Ltd. in 2006, focusing on the research and development of mobile search engines. When mobile search technology became increasingly mature, Roboo launched its mobile top-advertising service (Roboo's mobile top-advertising service organically combines a company's brand logo or product pictures, text information, and one-touch phone call functionality, appearing at the very top of the first page of Roboo's mobile web search results. The price varies depending on different categories of keywords. Once a company purchases mobile top-advertising keywords, there is an opportunity to be found by over 700 million Chinese mobile users actively searching for these keywords, bringing more business opportunities to the company!), beginning its market operations.

Since the issuance of the 3G license in 2009, Roboo has developed partners across various regions, signing cooperation agreements with local partners who then sell mobile top-advertising keywords to regional customers. By the end of 2011, Roboo's partner in Jiangxi Province contacted Mr. Zhou, the general manager of Jiangxi Hengqin Garments Co., Ltd., and specifically explained to Mr. Zhou the development prospects of mobile internet and the detailed services of Roboo's mobile top-advertising. After learning about Roboo's mobile top-advertising information and combining it with his knowledge of mobile internet, Mr. Zhou believed that mobile top-advertising was an opportunity for his company's business development. Thus, in early December 2011, Mr. Zhou signed a five-year mobile top-advertising contract with Roboo, and Roboo staff provided free construction of dual-mode websites: a 3G mobile website and a PC-style computer website. Mid-December 2011, Mr. Zhou's registered dual-mode website "Jiangxi Garments" officially went online on Roboo's mobile search platform.

The 3G website "Jiangxi Garments (http://www.jxfz.roboo.com/)" is registered under Jiangxi Hengqin Garments Co., Ltd., which is a comprehensive garment manufacturer operating in multiple fields such as group clothing, branded clothing design, pattern making, production, sales, and processing. Since its establishment, the company has served many large enterprises with its solid quality and good service system, now possessing a team of excellent designers and tailors, advanced production equipment, and skilled production workers, while ensuring production and delivery times to meet various needs. The company has good cooperative relationships with major fabric suppliers, offering fabrics of all levels and diverse styles, and launching new, fashionable fabrics every season, providing comprehensive and high-quality services to various types of enterprises. Every step of production undergoes meticulous sewing and careful ironing, finally passing strict quality inspections to deliver perfect finished products to customers. Now, the 3G website has been successfully built and operated, containing sections such as user login, industry news, company introduction, clothing display, and advertisement leasing, simply and comprehensively showcasing the basic information and business scope of Jiangxi Hengqin Garments Co., Ltd. The functions of each section are as follows:

User Login: Used for users to register and log into the "Jiangxi Garments" 3G website. After logging in, users can post information, leave messages, and make online purchases.

Industry News: Displays the latest news within the garment industry, helping users understand changes in the garment market.

Company Introduction: Introduces the company's products, business scope, and corporate culture.

Clothing Display: Mainly displays various popular clothing styles, publishing the company's garment products externally.

Advertisement Leasing: Used for merchants cooperating with Jiangxi Hengqin Garments Co., Ltd. to post information, generating advertising revenue for the company.

The 3G website "Jiangxi Garments" registered by Jiangxi Hengqin Garments Co., Ltd., under the joint optimization of Mr. Zhou and Roboo's staff, continuously improves its website functions, with customer traffic constantly increasing. Even in its initial stage, it already has thousands of visitors and brings profits to the company. In today's rapidly developing mobile internet, Roboo has become an old-established enterprise in the mobile internet industry. Since 2006, it has been committed to the research and development of mobile search engines and continuously improving its mobile top-advertising services, earning a good reputation in the industry. As mobile internet develops vigorously, Mr. Zhou registered the mobile top-advertising "Jiangxi Garments." Believing in the push of the mobile trend and Roboo's services, "Jiangxi Garments" will also achieve greater improvements.
