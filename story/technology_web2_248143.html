The science fiction fantasy series "Haven" has been broadcast online. After the web series, created by a team, achieved great success, it was officially upgraded to a TV version. The first season premiered on January 16, 2009, on the American Sci-Fi Channel.

"Haven" is the latest series launched by the American Sci-Fi Channel and is produced by the team behind "Stargate". The original cast from "Stargate" and "Stargate Atlantis" also stars in this show. The first season consists of 13 episodes. In modern society, humans are constantly changing through stem cell research, genetic treatments, organ transplants, and cloning. However, human evolution also has its dark side — a reality that few dare to face. There exist alien creatures scattered around the world, and these aliens are the key to our race's future.

The character plays a 24-year-old doctor, one of the first female doctors from the Royal Academy during the British Victorian era. She was once engaged to Jack the Ripper and had a daughter with him, though she didn't know her father was Jack. The doctor's father founded a mysterious haven for these creatures, which were gathered from all over the world. While studying these creatures, the doctor discovers that she has gained immortality.

The second season of "Haven" will premiere on July 9. This new season will showcase more non-humanoid creatures, more historical legends related to these beings, and many familiar historical figures will make appearances. In a special time-travel episode, we will catch a glimpse of the main characters' futures, albeit briefly. The second season will continue the vampire storyline initiated in the first season and expand upon it. One of the most intriguing aspects of the second season is the introduction of other havens located globally, each with different missions.

The second season begins six weeks after the end of the first. The lead executive producer mentioned in an interview that the second season starts big and stunning, with emotional moments in the first episode. The climax at the end of the second season will be even more gripping than the first. Additionally, the start of the second season will consist of two episodes aired over two weeks. There will be significant changes within the haven, and the second episode will attempt to correct these changes.

The biggest change in the second season is the addition of new regular characters, who will play larger roles and have increased screen time.
