Promote the construction of a harmonious society.

by zlockw02 on 2011-06-13 11:50:10

The Central Economic Work Conference was held in Beijing from November 29 to December 1. Hu Jintao delivered a key speech at the conference, conducting a comprehensive analysis of the current international and domestic situation, summarizing the economic work of 2005, deeply analyzing the achievements and important insights gained during the "Tenth Five-Year Plan" period in terms of economic and social development, and clearly proposing the guiding ideology, overall requirements, and main tasks for the economic work of 2006. Wen Jiabao, in his speech, focused on comprehensively analyzing the current state of China's economy, elaborating on the major expected goals and key issues that need to be addressed for the socio-economic development of 2006, and specifically arranging the economic work for 2006.

The conference pointed out that 2005 was the final year of the "Tenth Five-Year Plan" period. We adhered to the guidance of Deng Xiaoping Theory and the important thought of "Three Represents," thoroughly implemented the spirit of the 16th National Congress and the Third, Fourth, and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the 16th Central Committee, firmly established and earnestly implemented the scientific outlook on development, tightly grasped development as the first priority of governance for national rejuvenation, continuously advanced reform and opening-up, continued to strengthen and improve macro-control, and all aspects of economic, political, cultural, social, and party construction achieved new progress. The national economy showed a trend of faster growth, better efficiency, stable prices, and enhanced vitality. Particularly, the stability of economic operations improved, the coordination of development improved, reforms in key areas and critical links made breakthroughs, and the main indicators proposed by the "Tenth Five-Year Plan" for socio-economic development could be smoothly achieved.

The conference emphasized that 2006 would be the opening year of implementing the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan." Doing a good job in the national economy and social development work in 2006 was of great significance. The overall requirement for the economic work of 2006 was: guided by Deng Xiaoping Theory and the important thought of "Three Represents," seriously implement the spirit of the 16th National Congress and the Fifth Plenary Session of the 16th Central Committee, adhere to using the scientific outlook on development to govern the overall situation of socio-economic development, maintain the continuity and stability of macroeconomic policies, focus on accelerating reform and opening-up, enhancing independent innovation capabilities, promoting economic structural adjustments and transformation of economic growth patterns, improving the quality and efficiency of economic growth, achieving both rapid and good growth, promoting the construction of a harmonious society, and ensuring a good start for the smooth implementation of the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan." The essence of this overall requirement was to effectively shift socio-economic development onto the track of scientific development. This was a significant and urgent task facing the socialist modernization construction of our country, and substantive steps must be taken in 2006.

The conference proposed the main tasks for the economic work of 2006:

First, stabilize macroeconomic policies and maintain a favorable momentum of steady and relatively fast economic growth. This is very necessary for strengthening overall national strength and effectively alleviating socio-economic conflicts. To achieve steady and relatively fast economic growth, the key lies in making efforts to expand domestic demand, continue to implement prudent fiscal and monetary policies, and continuously strengthen and improve macro-control. Expanding domestic demand is a long-term strategic guideline and fundamental foothold for China's economic development. Efforts should be made to adjust the relationship between investment and consumption, focusing on increasing residents' consumption, especially farmers' consumption, continuously broadening the scope of consumption, and improving the consumption environment. On the basis of vigorously increasing revenue and saving expenses, the structure of fiscal expenditure should be adjusted. Maintain stable growth in money and credit, optimize the structure of money and credit. Continue to strengthen the coordination of fiscal policy, monetary policy, industrial policy, etc., use well tax, interest rate, price, and other economic levers and legal means, improve and strictly enforce market access rules, guide the optimal allocation of resources. Carefully study the new situations and problems in economic operations, enhance the relevance and effectiveness of various control measures, pay more attention to differentiated treatment and classified guidance.

Second, earnestly promote the construction of a new socialist countryside and further do a good job in "agriculture, rural areas, and farmers" ("three rurals") work. Solving the "three rurals" problem is crucial to the overall effort of building a moderately prosperous society in all respects and achieving socialist modernization. It must always be regarded as the top priority of the entire Party's work. The general requirement for constructing a new socialist countryside is productive development, affluent living, civilized rural customs, neat village appearance, and democratic management. In constructing a new socialist countryside, it is necessary to make overall plans and specific arrangements, adhere to planning first, focus on pilot demonstrations, and gradually advance step by step, with plans and priorities. In the work, it is essential to take developing the rural economy as the core task, promoting the liberation and development of rural productivity, and promoting continuous income growth for farmers.
