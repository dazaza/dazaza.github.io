I don't know if this counts as a green hat.

by tj970 on 2008-03-15 02:42:26

In March 2007, I met her. At that time, I had not yet graduated from Shenzhen Vocational College. I always wanted her to come to Shenzhen because I really liked her. From her articles and words, she seemed to be a very emotional girl who cried easily. I heard that she had been separated from her boyfriend for two years. Moreover, she was introduced to me by a classmate of over 10 years. I have always paid attention to her. In June 2007, when she first came to Shenzhen, she played around for a month without finding a job. I asked my senior to introduce a job to her (because the job in Huizhou was also recommended by me to come to Shenzhen). When she said the internet cafe situation was not good, I directly gave her a desktop computer. Since I was in Bao'an District and she was in Nanshan District, she often went to bars all day because she couldn't find a job. If someone noticed her, she might be sold to hotels as a prostitute. I was afraid of this, so I didn't suggest her go to the internet cafe. Later, due to work issues, although working alone was boring, I was psychologically unbalanced. Maybe I always wanted to compare with others, so I did two jobs, having less time to care about her. Occasionally, when I asked her how she was, she always said she was fine. When I said I wanted to see her, she said it was unnecessary and even told me I was annoying. She said things were none of my business. After she started working in Shenzhen, she said she felt a lot of pressure and didn't want to talk to me. I just couldn't put her out of my mind...

In November, she told my friend that her ex-boyfriend came to Shenzhen (she didn't tell me, after all, I am not her anything), four days later her ex-boyfriend left. For some reason, she suddenly became nice to me. I was so happy at that time, feeling very blissful, chatting with her every day. I quit one of my extra jobs, thinking of spending more time with her...

However, a week later, I found something. In her online photo album, I found photos taken in November with her ex-boyfriend on the bed. The clothes she wore in the photo were bought in October. The creation time of the photo was November 17th, which was those few days. And I found out that almost all the expenses of her boyfriend in Shenzhen were paid by her. At that time, I called the classmate who introduced her to me. My hands were shaking when I called, really, I could hardly hold the phone steady. I asked him, do you know her? He said she was his friend's god-sister, introduced by his friend. He said he had another woman, so he introduced her to me. I was almost dizzy at that moment. I told him everything. He said, is it true? I directly showed him the photos. He said he would scold his friend for introducing such a girl to me. At that moment, I had nothing to say. Since I knew about these photos, I ignored her completely. I told her, if you like Beijing, go back. There are your dreams. In fact, I knew that the man left after playing with her, but I just couldn't accept it. Because when I was pursuing her, she was looking for her ex-boyfriend, clinging to him and having sex with him. That's why I realized why she suddenly became so nice to me. I felt so heartbroken. Until now, whenever she sent messages or called, I said I was busy. During the Spring Festival, she left. She departed from Shenzhen... Her blog wrote, "Goodbye, Shenzhen... No memories left in Shenzhen..." Hunan girls, Hunan girls, how can you say so many sentimental words after doing such things!
