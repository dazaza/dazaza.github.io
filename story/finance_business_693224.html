The real estate trust which was once regarded as the "life-saving straw" for financing by real estate developers has welcomed a concentrated repayment period in 2012. Although the data predicted by different market institutions have some differences, it is still reasonable to describe this redemption amount as "huge". Moreover, fulfilling the investment commitment exerts an irresistible "rigid" pressure on real estate enterprises and trust companies. In face of the trust repayment pressure, developers may accelerate the price-cutting sales to quickly recover funds. Such prediction has been recognized by the representatives and members of the two sessions. At the end of February, before the convening of the national two sessions, several national CPPCC members who were interviewed by China Economic Weekly all believed that the expired real estate trust might lead to price-cutting sales of some enterprises and projects. They believed that due to the policy influence, the price of the real estate market this year might still experience a slight downward trend.

The反击式破碎机and hammer crusher are commonly used crushed stone equipment in sand and gravel production lines. The反击式破碎机is a crusher device that uses impact energy to crush medium-hard brittle materials. In comparison, the反击式破碎机has greater crushing power and a wider range of materials that can be crushed. The working principle of the Xingbang Raymond mill is that the roller tightly rolls on the ring under the action of centrifugal force. The material is sent between the roller and the ring by the shovel blade. Under the action of grinding force, the material is crushed into powder. Then, under the action of the fan, the powdered material is blown up and passes through the analyzer. The material that meets the fineness requirements passes through the analyzer, while the material that does not meet the requirements returns to the grinding chamber for continued grinding. The material passing through the analyzer enters the cyclone separator for separation and collection. The exhaust air adopts industrial filter cloth for isolation and one-time powder formation.
