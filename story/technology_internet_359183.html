by www160la on 2010-06-26 14:25:46

   The network changes the new life! Recently, the professional online flower shop - Flowers Hub, which is the first in scale and strength in the industry, has joined hands with Alipay (China) Network Technology Co., Ltd., the largest independent third-party payment platform in China, to create a floral delivery miracle with Alipay's 300 million members. Alipay members can log in directly on the Flowers Hub website without registration and enjoy the new experience of flower delivery.

 

   The online express expert - Flowers Hub (www.xianhuahui.com) has risen rapidly. As the network gradually enters ordinary households, online flower delivery companies that mainly focus on flower retail and also operate gifts have emerged, impacting everyone's consumption threshold one after another. Flowers Hub, committed to providing the best experience for customers, will not fall behind the times at any time, and has never stopped exploring and innovating online services. It has been consolidating its leading position in online flower delivery step by step, becoming a professional online flower shop that is the first in scale and strength in the industry. They embody humanization and user experience in both convenience and thoughtfulness. After receiving orders, receiving payments, and completing order deliveries, "Flowers Hub" will promptly notify users via email or mobile text messages, allowing users to gain an interactive experience with merchants in the first time. The thoughtful "online shopping circle" module on the website is also an important part of the user experience, pioneering the integration of user experience and network additional services in the industry. However, the number of domestic online banking users is currently only over 20 million, which is significantly lower compared to Alipay's 300 million members, resulting in many people being excluded from the online consumer market due to payment issues.

   Strong alliance recreates the floral delivery myth. By July 6, 2009, Alipay (China) Network Technology Co., Ltd., the largest independent third-party payment platform in China, announced that its user base had officially surpassed the 200 million mark. This was the first time that a domestic third-party payment company had reached a user base of 200 million. According to reports, Alipay's user base first reached 100 million in late August 2008. From its initial appearance on Taobao in October 2003 to accumulating 100 million users, it took nearly five years. However, from 100 million users to 200 million users, Alipay only took 10 months, and currently, Alipay members have grown astonishingly to 300 million. This marks that online consumption is no longer a niche fashion activity for a few people, but has become a mainstream consumption method involving all citizens in the new economic era. As the top professional online flower shop in the industry, Flowers Hub's cooperation with Alipay is of great significance. A responsible person from Flowers Hub told reporters that the strong alliance between Flowers Hub and Alipay not only provides consumers with new payment channels, allowing the security and convenience of payments to be fully utilized, but also allows Flowers Hub to share Alipay's 300 million members, taking Flowers Hub a big step closer to its future goal. Relevant persons in charge of Alipay believe that Flowers Hub has won the Top 50 Award at the Fifth China Online Merchant Conference. Its unique thoughtful service, "online shopping circle" module, "flower guide," and many other exclusive functions will undoubtedly bring Alipay members a faster and more successful consumption revolution, playing an immeasurable role in promoting the development of the Alipay website.

   Industry insiders believe that in modern society where life rhythms are getting faster and faster, online consumption, because of its convenience and speed, which can save a lot of time costs, has become an indispensable lifestyle for many modern people; meanwhile, the proportion of spiritual consumption expenditure is also increasing. Flowers Hub's collaboration with Alipay undoubtedly follows this trend, bringing more convenient, faster, and simpler consumption experiences to a wide range of consumers. The strong alliance will lead to a large wave of a new online lifestyle!
