Many seniors have shared their good experiences of running online stores. While thanking these seniors, I would also like to share some of my experiences in running a physical store.

Since my online store has just opened, there are many things about online stores that I don't understand yet, and I still need to learn a lot. Many things that I know have already been mentioned by seniors on the forum, so what I can share is only some small psychological experiences that I have summarized during the process of opening my store.

Because I believe that mentality is as important as technical experience. Only with a good mentality can we do business well and expand it. Here, I hope that those who have just opened their stores or are hesitating about upgrading their stores will keep going.

As long as you work hard, there will be rewards, and I wish all Taobao store owners a booming business and great prosperity!

The ten indispensable mindsets for running an online store:

1. The mindset of persistence

No matter what you do, persistence is one of the keys to success. Once we have set our goals, we should strive step by step with the most dedicated spirit. Running a store on Taobao is the same as in reality - it's not just about getting the goods and putting them on the shelf. We also need to do a good job in service and promotion. Sometimes, even after initial investment, there may be a period when business is slow. If we lack the spirit of persistence, we will never see the day of success.

There have been reports that many people searching for water in the desert died very close to the water source. The reason was that they didn't persist. If they had given themselves more hope mentally and persisted in their beliefs, they might have found the water.

Running a store is the same principle.

2. The mindset of continuous learning

Continuous learning doesn't just mean learning from the experiences of seniors. It also means learning various skills. There are a lot of experiences from seniors on the forum, so I won't go into detail here. There are many other things to learn, such as visiting stores run by successful peers to see what good practices they have that you can borrow from - this is the simplest way of "stealing techniques."

Also, if you sell clothes, learn more about clothing coordination, color matching, fabric differentiation, and characteristics, etc. Be knowledgeable about everything so that customers asking questions can immediately see that you are professional.

If you don't know how to edit pictures, you can learn if you have time. Taking bad photos? Learn and practice more, and you'll get better over time. Don't be afraid of trouble or fatigue; many things become clear only after you've done them yourself. Only through continuous learning can you make progress. Remember, a person's life is spent in constant learning.

3. The mindset of being pragmatic and patient

Do your work solidly and ask when you don't understand. Don't pretend to be something you're not.

Don't think you can achieve success overnight. Successful people start from every small thing. Only by doing small things well can you prepare for big things.

In online stores, there are many repetitive tasks every day: store management, promoting items, posting and replying on forums... And then dealing with customers, some customers can be troublesome. Sometimes you talk to them for a long time, and finally, they tell you they want to buy something. But when it comes to payment, they change their minds and want something else, or they simply ignore all your efforts and buy nothing.

Another tricky situation is misunderstandings, which require enough patience to explain. Without sufficient patience, you will lose many customers and cannot do business well.

4. The mindset of optimism and cheerfulness

I think many people have heard this fable: "Two people each have a glass of water, and both have drunk half of it. One says: 'I only have half a glass left,' while another says: 'I still have half a glass.'" This story tells us that, facing the same situation, some people express helplessness and bitterness, extinguishing their own motivation. Others remain optimistic, constantly encouraging themselves to work hard.

Which of these two mindsets is more likely to succeed?

5. The mindset of being willing to give

There is a saying: Giving does not necessarily lead to harvest, but not giving definitely leads to no harvest. Sometimes we work hard and end up with nothing.

But in the process of effort, we unconsciously gain a lot, such as learning how to work hard and cherish things. Don't focus too much on short-term gains and losses. As long as you truly work hard and give, one day you will gain much more than you expect. A life devoted to serving others is the most valuable!

6. The mindset of innovation

This society changes too fast, and we must stay at the forefront of the times to avoid being eliminated.

Watch more news, learn more. Observe the changes in the world and the market around you. Always keep your thoughts aligned with the times and adaptable to society. Change bad habits and methods, and innovate more.

With this mindset, you will continuously improve and not fall behind.

7. The mindset of mutual assistance

In this world, everyone needs help from others, and no one can survive alone without others' support.

When you see someone seeking help, try your best to assist them, don't find it troublesome to write a few words. Helping others brings gratitude, and that feeling is even happier than selling products.

Sometimes, some customers look at items in other stores, and when they hesitate and ask you, you should do your best to help them, even if they don't buy from your store. Because they consider you a friend and open up to you. Isn't it a happy thing when someone trusts you? By helping others, they know your kindness, and it also promotes your store indirectly. Why not do it?

8. The mindset of treating customers as friends

Even if you cannot treat customers as gods, you should treat them as your friends.

Only by sincerely treating someone can business last and develop. If you always think about taking advantage of others, your store cannot develop further.

9. The mindset of taking responsibility bravely

Once there is a problem with your goods or other issues arise, when customers contact you, never make excuses or shirk responsibility.

Take responsibility bravely, apologize when necessary,

Even if you feel wronged,

When customers say harsh words out of anger, you cannot argue with them.

Having such courage earns trust from others. Misunderstandings will be resolved. With mutual trust, doing business is no longer a problem.

10. The mindset of being a boss

A boss's mindset means looking far ahead,

Putting a lot of energy and passion into every task, making decisions decisively, and treating their work as a lifelong career to fight for.

Without the mindset of a boss, focusing solely on how to make big money and only caring about immediate interests, and hesitating due to fear of hard work, your progress will not be far. A simple bread can become a globally renowned hamburger, so what is impossible?

Therefore,

Everyone needs to keep going, strive for the best!
