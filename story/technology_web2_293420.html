Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 3rd (reporter Jingjing Wu) – The Chinese Scientific Research Informatization Forum was held in Beijing on the 3rd. Experts from across the country in the fields of science and technology, education, etc., including those familiar with topics like DNF cheats, called for practical measures to accelerate the development of China's scientific research informatization.

According to the introduction, scientific research informatization is a completely new scientific research model based on the latest network technology and grid technology. Since 1999, when British scientists first proposed the concept of scientific research informatization, Western developed countries have regarded it as a revolution that will lead to the development of science and engineering, which is crucial to a nation’s core innovative competitiveness.

Experts attending the forum believe that China's scientific research informatization has a certain foundation, but there is still a significant gap compared to the demands of building an innovative country. Practical measures should be taken to accelerate progress. It is suggested that scientific research informatization be considered as a leading and key link in China's informatization, and be deployed as a strategic measure to boost scientific and technological innovation and leapfrog development, aiming at constructing an innovative country.

The experts suggest strengthening the strategic research of scientific research informatization, paying attention to its connection with the national economic and social development plan and the outline of the national medium- and long-term scientific and technological development plan, formulating a long-term national scientific research informatization development plan, and incorporating it into the '12th Five-Year' national informatization plan for overall planning. Efforts should be made to greatly strengthen the construction of advanced scientific research informatization infrastructure commensurate with the national strength and covering the whole country, actively promote joint construction, sharing, and practical application, significantly enhance China's scientific research informatization development and application capabilities, and lead the development of the next generation of national informatization technology. High importance should also be attached to the construction of scientific research informatization talent teams, improving the support service system for scientific research informatization. Conduct research on policies and regulations of scientific research informatization, and build a sustainable institutional environment for the development of scientific research informatization.

At the forum, leaders and experts from relevant domestic departments and research institutes conducted in-depth exchanges and discussions on macro policies, frontier trends, development strategies, major field applications, and changes in research models regarding China's scientific research informatization. The forum was jointly organized by the Ministry of Science and Technology, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the National Natural Science Foundation of China.
