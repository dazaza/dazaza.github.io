**How Do I Make Money?**

That’s the most important question, right? Some Zazzle store owners earn over $100,000 annually. Thousands of members earn a steady stream of income through their Zazzle Shop. The details: Every Zazzle item has a Base Price that includes costs associated with production. You simply set the retail price above the Base Price. When you sell something, Zazzle keeps the Base Price and you get the markup. For example:

- A plain T-Shirt has a base price of $8.98

- You decide to markup the price to $13.99

- If you sell one shirt, you make a profit of $5.00

- Zazzle sends you a PayPal deposit for your total markups once a month!
