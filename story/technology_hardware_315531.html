After in-depth investigation and learning, Dafeng City realized that compared with Southern Shandong and Northern Jiangsu, its greatest advantage lies in the coastal area, and coastal development is the hope for Dafeng to stand out in regional competition.

Soundbite: Zhao Xiaqing, Director of Dafeng City Development and Reform Commission [Give full play to comparative advantages, focus on coastal development, and strive to promote the city's economic leapfrog development with excellent work achievements. First, highlight infrastructure construction; second, focus on major and super-large projects. Accelerate the development of port-related industries, focusing on new energy industries, heavy petrochemical industries, and port logistics industries, and increase the intensity of investment promotion.]

In order to amplify its own advantages, Dafeng City focuses on building the "golden" brand image of Dafeng Port as a Class One open port, accelerates related key engineering constructions, and increases the efforts to promote Dafeng Port externally. It mainly targets major and super-large projects, implements a development strategy led by industrial development in coastal areas, with industrial park construction as the main focus, and advanced manufacturing as the main direction for the park's industries, fully amplifying the advantages of ports and land, and focusing on developing industrial chains and clusters.

Currently, all towns and enterprises in Dafeng City are striving to ensure a good start in the first quarter, laying a solid foundation for the doubling goals of this year and next year with a high starting trend.

Soundbite: Yin Fengshan, Chairman of Jiangsu Fengshan Group [Our sales target for 2008 is to double from 2007, reaching 1 billion yuan, and we expect to reach 2 billion yuan in 2009. Thus, in the national pesticide industry, we aim to be among the top 10.]
