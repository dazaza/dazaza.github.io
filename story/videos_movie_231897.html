Meanwhile, in the same town, a waitress named Temi (Jenny Baird) was brewing big plans for her future at a local diner. When she looked out the window at the stars, she unexpectedly spotted a meteor, which she felt was undoubtedly a good omen for her dreams.

But that glowing object mistaken for a meteor was actually a UFO from outer space. Like a fireball, it hurtled toward Earth and eventually crash-landed on a hill in the Californian desert... The only witnesses were Dick (Andrew Dunbar) and Penny (Sarah Smyth), who were making out nearby at a lovers' hideout.
