AC Milan officially announced the championship celebration arrangements, Coach Allegri: Inzaghi will make a comeback in the next round.

by wuyvzrno on 2012-02-17 14:12:48

Attached: In this game, Allegri's big list called up all 31 players from AC Milan's first team, including the injured Janakulovsky. I believe this is not mainly for participating in the game but for the championship celebration.

Speaking of mistakes, Gattuso has been a leading figure in the media recently. Allegri was unwilling to dwell on this matter: "Gattuso's issue is over, are you still interested? Wait for the next one (laughs)."

At the end of the press conference, Coach A expressed his gratitude to those who had helped him: "In my first season at Milan, the former veterans of Milan gave me a lot of help. Not only to me, they also made the players realize how much effort is needed to gain a place in Milan. Thank you." Regarding the endless praise from the outside world, Coach A humbly stated: "My work is not as perfect as people say. I make mistakes often too. But this year is extremely important for me. We won the championship, and I have a deep understanding of the entire team environment. You know, it's not easy, especially getting to know a new dressing room. Next season, I will set sail with greater psychological advantages."

Recently, Spanish media revealed that Mourinho hopes to exchange Kaka for Ibrahimovic. Allegri commented: "I know nothing about it. Whenever the season ends or the transfer market is about to open, these voices appear normally, and people have become accustomed to them."

Speaking of veterans, we must mention Milan's contract renewal work. The coach replied: "I will analyze the situation with the club. My responsibility is to evaluate everyone from a technical perspective and give suggestions on whether they stay or go, but the final decision is made by the club and the players together. Next week, I will hold a meeting with the management of the club and propose my suggestions. It can be confirmed that next season's Milan will still be strong." In addition to these veterans, Allegri also pays great attention to the training of young players: "Next season, we will definitely select some potential players from the youth team to supplement the team, especially since there will be four players joining the Copa America games in the summer. These young players in the team are not just filler or waiting to be used, but may even become decisive forces in the team. Look at Strasser and Merkle's performances this season, and Beretta's strength and characteristics displayed in Bologna. We look forward to such talents emerging from the youth team."

At midnight on Sunday Beijing time, the Serie A league will conduct the penultimate round of matches this season. After losing in the semi-finals of the Italian Cup to Palermo and missing out on creating the first record in Milan's 112-year history, Allegri will lead his team to face his former club Cagliari at their home ground this round. At the pre-match press conference, Coach A revealed a wish that had been buried in his heart all season.

According to the plan, before tomorrow's match against Cagliari, the Rossoneri will hold a grand parade to celebrate their championship win. The parade is expected to start at 17:15 local time on Saturday afternoon. All members of Milan's championship team will board an open-top bus and start from Via Turati, where Milan's headquarters is located. They will proceed amidst countless cheering Rossoneri fans, arriving at the famous Piazza del Duomo around 17:45. There, they will interact with all the supporters, then the team will head to San Siro to prepare for the match against Cagliari. After this match, there will be a ceremony at San Siro to present the Serie A trophy. Speaking of the team's series of celebratory activities, Allegri did not let the championship victory cloud his judgment. He reminded: "Tomorrow will be an unusual day, but everyone on the team should show our strength in front of the fans. Perhaps now we don't have the same pressure as in previous crucial matches, but now, like before important matches, we need to keep our minds clear so that we do not make mistakes. We should end this season with the best possible outcome; it is the team's responsibility. We cannot leave a bad impression on the fans; we are Milan."

Although Coach A brought the bad news that Nesta would not be able to play, there was also good news for everyone, which was Inzaghi's return: "Since Inzaghi's serious injury, today he completed his first full practice with the whole team after the injury. I hope his condition continues to recover and improve, so that by next week's game, we will have a fit Inzaghi. Tomorrow's game, he might sit on the bench, but if everything goes well, he will be included in the team list for the game against Udinese. We'll see, the important thing is that he is back."

At the press conference, Allegri was in a good mood, after all, winning the championship in his first season coaching Milan was a great achievement. He talked about the feeling of winning the championship: "After the last round of matches, many players organized celebratory events. I think this is very good. Milan deserves this championship. Winning the championship two rounds ahead in such fierce competition, I think this is what all the players and staff of Milan are proud of."

As Allegri said, the current Milan has almost no game pressure. Before the match, the media predicted that in the last two rounds, Coach A might dispatch those players who will leave the team at the end of the season, but Milan's coach did not have such intentions: "In this round of matches, I have listed all 31 players on the team list. If possible, I will send each of them onto the field because tomorrow will be our time to celebrate. I understand that everyone wants to participate in the game, but I will still follow the principle of selecting the best lineup based on form. We need to play a great game against Cagliari, and in the last round against Udinese, I will do the same." As for the specific content of tomorrow's starting lineup, Allegri mentioned some names: "There are many things I still need to check, but I can tell you that Abbiati, Abate, Thiago Silva, Yepes, and Zambrotta will all play in the game, while Nesta will not participate in tomorrow's game."

"Actually, at the beginning of the season, I had a wish in my heart, which was to celebrate the team's Serie A championship during this game against Cagliari. Now I have achieved my wish, and I am very happy." Regarding his former club Cagliari, Allegri is filled with gratitude: "I am very happy to see the chairman and my players again. Thank you for giving me the opportunity to come to Milan."
