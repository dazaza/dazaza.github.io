Serie A - 10-man Roma 1-0 Lazio, Montenegrin wonderkid provides brilliant assist

by djbgufgire on 2009-12-07 19:27:36

Serie A - 10-man Roma 1-0 Lazio: Montenegrin wonderkid provides a brilliant assist. At 3:45 AM Beijing time on December 7, a key match of the 15th round of Serie A kicked off, with Roma defeating Lazio 1-0 at home in the Rome Derby. Neither side scored in the first half, but in the second half, Savić hit the post, and Vučinić assisted Cassetti to score the winning goal. In the dying moments of the game, Pizarro was sent off with a red card. After this game, Lazio remained winless for 13 consecutive rounds, ranking 15th, only one point away from the relegation zone.

Totti pairs up with the wonderkid; Cruz absent. This was the 131st Derby between the two teams in Serie A history. In their previous 130 encounters in Serie A, Roma had an advantage with 43 wins, 54 draws, and 33 losses, scoring 157 goals while conceding 125. At home, Roma had a clear edge with 23 wins, 30 draws, and 12 losses, scoring 80 goals and conceding 54. Roma had been in good form recently, securing five wins and one draw in their last six matches across all competitions, while Lazio had not won in 12 league games, hovering near the relegation zone. There were rumors that if Lazio lost, coach Edy Reja would possibly be sacked.

In Roma's lineup, Vučinić started despite being rumored to be injured. He formed an attacking trio with Menez and Totti, while De Rossi also started, marking his 200th Serie A appearance. On the other hand, Lazio coach Edy Reja made an unusual arrangement, with Burdisso playing as a right-back, captain Rocchi on the bench, and Mauri and Savić leading the attack.

Fans almost caused a disturbance; both sides deadlocked in the first half. The game began with fierce tackling from both sides. In the 1st minute, Brocchi’s shot from outside the box was blocked by the defenders. In the 10th minute, Savić missed a golden opportunity after dribbling too much during a three-on-two counterattack. From the 13th minute onwards, due to fans continuously setting off fireworks, causing unrest among spectators in the stands, the referee temporarily halted the game, and a large number of police were deployed to control the situation. After about a 10-minute pause, the referee consulted both teams and decided to continue the match.

In the 31st minute, Menez crossed from the right flank, and Lichtsteiner cleared it dangerously inside the box, which Muslera picked up with his hands, but the referee did not award a penalty. In the 34th minute, Riise unleashed a powerful left-footed shot from outside the box, which went over the bar. In the 36th minute, Roma's corner from the right was poorly cleared by Lazio, and Riise's long-range cannon shot was blocked by De Rossi out for a corner. In the 37th minute, Mauri's left-footed cross-shot from the right went straight to the far corner, but Roma's goalkeeper made a fantastic save. In the 45th minute, Kolarov's left-footed shot from the left corner of the box also went over. In stoppage time of the first half, De Rossi escaped a red card for elbowing Savić. Mexes got injured and was substituted by Cassetti. Both sides went into halftime deadlocked.

Savić hits the post; Cassetti breaks the deadlock. In the second half, both teams swapped ends and made substitutions. Roma brought on Briatore for Menez, while Lazio introduced Foggia for Matuzalém. In the 52nd minute, Lazio's Barroni's free-kick from outside the box was blocked by Totti with his hand, and the referee awarded another free-kick outside the box, which Savić took and missed high. In the 55th minute, Cassetti's low right-footed shot from outside the box missed the left post. In the 58th minute, Cassetti's powerful right-footed shot from outside the box was saved by Lazio's goalkeeper.

In the 60th minute, Savić turned Burdisso inside the box and shot, the ball passing through Burdisso's legs, hitting the left post and bouncing out, with Mauri's follow-up shot being brilliantly saved by Roma's goalkeeper Bertainioli! In the 67th minute, Perrotta headed a cross from the left wide of the far post. In the 76th minute, Savić's left-footed turn-and-shoot from just outside the box went high.

In the 78th minute, Roma took the lead. Vučinić provided a low pass from the right, and Cassetti arrived late near the penalty spot to place a right-footed shot into the bottom-left corner of the goal. 1-0, Roma broke the deadlock.

In the 85th minute, Totti delivered a beautiful pass to the left, and Riise attempted a left-footed volley, which went high. In the 86th minute, Pizarro received a yellow card for fouling Lichtsteiner. In the 88th minute, Vučinić's shot from 20 meters out on the right went high. In the 89th minute, Pizarro's reckless tackle on Savić earned him a direct red card. The game ended with Roma narrowly winning 1-0, taking all three points.

Lineups for both teams:

Roma (4-3-2-1): 27-Bertainioli / 29-Burdisso, 5-Mexes (77-Cassetti, 45'), 4-Juan, 17-Riise / 16-De Rossi, 7-Pizarro, 20-Perrotta / 94-Menez (33-Briatore, 46'), 9-Vučinić (11-Taddei, 90') / 10-Totti

Lazio (5-3-1-1): 86-Muslera / 2-Lichtsteiner, 13-Djakit, 28-Stendardo, 26-Radu, 11-Kolarov / 32-Brocchi, 33-Barroni (6-Dabo, 90'), 8-Matuzalém (17-Foggia, 46') / 5-Mauri (9-Rocchi, 79') / 10-Savić
