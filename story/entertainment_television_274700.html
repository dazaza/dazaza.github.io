Plot Introduction of "Dad, Wake Me Up at Dawn":

The Republican-era family drama "Dad, Wake Me Up at Dawn" has been aired on the Oriental Film Channel. This is another period family drama that Liu Xuehua participated in after "Spring Step-Mother Heart." Although the "Mrs. Tian" in the drama still enjoys the affection of non-biological children around her, compared to the step-mother in "Spring," the screen image of "Mrs. Tian" is richer. Not only does it have emotional scenes with "Zhao Ade," but also interwoven with scenes of "Tiger Sir," "Fuming," and other people vying for love, as well as the involvement of many tenants in the courtyard. Thus, the life of "Mrs. Tian" as a stepmother becomes more bumpy and more exciting. Liu Xuehua said, "There are no scenes in the drama where the children grow up to become successful, but in terms of the upbringing process, Mrs. Tian is much happier than Xiuying."
