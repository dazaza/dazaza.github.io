With the increasing popularity of cars, car maintenance and car beauty have become topics of conversation. Although many car owners are familiar with some knowledge about car beauty, they still know little about how to choose car beauty products and car cleaning supplies. Today, we will unravel the mysteries for you and deliver the most timely and professional knowledge related to car beauty products and car cleaning supplies for car owners. Latest knowledge on car beauty products:

A. How to choose specialized car wax for car beauty?

1) Choose car wax according to the color of the car paint. To prevent discoloration of the car paint due to waxing, generally, dark-colored car paint should use black, red, and green car wax, while light-colored car paint should use silver, white, and pearl car wax, etc.

2) When performing car beauty, car owners should select car wax based on the different vehicles, the environment they are often in, and the external stimuli that the paint surface can withstand. Generally, anti-high temperature and anti-ultraviolet car wax should be used in summer, and silicone resin wax with stronger protective effect should be selected in harsh environmental conditions.

3) Choose car wax according to the quality and newness of the car paint. Generally, ordinary cars should use metallic paint type car wax, and high-end cars should choose corresponding high-end car wax; besides, new cars or newly painted cars should use polishing wax, while old paint types should use grinding wax for polishing. Professional car beauticians recommend using Turtle Wax from Jinan Guipai Dina Professional Car Beauty for your beloved car.

Latest knowledge on car beauty products:

B. What kind of towel (cleaning cloth, glass cloth) should be used for car beauty?

Because improper wiping of the car body paint can easily leave scratches, when choosing car cleaning supplies, it is necessary to purchase them from professional car beauty supply companies. Therefore, car cleaning supply manufacturers suggest:

1) If pure cotton towels are too hard, fabric softener can be added in the washing machine for washing, which will make the towels softer, more fluffy, and more absorbent, thereby reducing the damage caused by pure cotton towels to the car body.

2) Towels are generally recommended to be made of pure cotton cloth. Artificial synthetic car cloths may scratch the car surface due to their properties, while pure cotton cloth can protect the polished surface from being scratched by dust particles stuck between the car surface and the car cloth.

C. How to choose floor mats?

As one of the important car cleaning supplies, floor mats are very important for car owners. The most important thing in choosing floor mats is safety and non-toxicity, so it is necessary to choose genuine products from reputable manufacturers. Generally, it is recommended that car owners use floor mats made of high-quality PVC and other dedicated materials.

Additionally, home carpets can also be used as an alternative to floor mats inside the car to keep the car clean.
