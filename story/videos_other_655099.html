2012 send text messages Farewell words for graduation gifts among classmates

by jingdian101 on 2012-01-06 08:22:34

A person will experience countless partings and reunions in a lifetime. It is only when we face parting that we realize how precious friendship is. Here, I would like to share with you all some farewell words carefully selected by the editor: 1. Parting always brings a little sadness and melancholy, but there is no need to be sad, because parting means reunion next time; I will silently cheer for you from a corner of the earth, and always remember our agreement, one day we will achieve it! Remember to love yourself well! 2. The days we walked together are like red cotton flowers, although they retain their original color, they have fallen down, losing all their memories; dear friend, don't forget me - a friend who once laughed and cried with you! Let's go hand in hand towards tomorrow! 3. How many nights, how many drunkenness, how many farewells, how many tears, joy and sorrow, parting and reunion, brightness and darkness, full moon and eclipse, ancient difficulty to be complete. Please do not worry, we will be reunited someday! 4. The flowers that never wither are those blooming in the heart; the sprouts that never fall are those growing in the heart. Our friendship, New Year greetings, will remain in our hearts forever, bless friends, wish you peace, happiness, health and smooth sailing on your journey through life! 5. Today's laughter and joy, tonight's clear wind and bright moon, though beautiful, are not eternal. Before we have had time to share the last cup of wine, we must go our separate ways. Take care, my friend. We parted silently, just as we met silently at first. May this gentle breeze bring you my heartfelt blessings and prayers. 6. If flying geese did not have common goals, they would not form images of humans; if the sun did not give its brilliant light to the earth, the earth would not yearn for every sunrise; it is precisely because you and I have a common language that we can become best friends. 7. Meeting is the most beautiful sound in life, and parting is an indispensable interlude. In those ordinary days, we built an extraordinary friendship, which made me extremely happy. And now, you and I will part again. Friend, friend, may each new page you turn over reveal a brilliant memory! 8. Knowing each other is fate, meeting is by choice, adding them together forms friendship, having feelings leads to meaning, we can gather together because we are attuned to each other. Wishing all the brothers to own every day: vigorous passion, magnificent heroism, persistent enthusiasm, sweet love, unrestrained expression, cheerful demeanor, pleasant mood!
