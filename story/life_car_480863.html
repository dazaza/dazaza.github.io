Common Knowledge of Car Beauty and Maintenance

People who drive their own cars will find that: the fine cigarette ash falling on the footpad, the stains on the surface of the seat, the worn-out shoe prints on the lower side of the inner door panel, the dusty dashboard and ceiling. How to solve the cleaning and maintenance of car interior? Beauty experts point out that the cleaning and maintenance of car interior is not an easy task, so it is necessary to pay attention to the daily cleaning in the car and go to professional shops for regular interior maintenance.

Professional Cleaning and Maintenance

If you are a busy car owner, then all the daily maintenance introduced above can be done by a professional car beauty shop for you. Professional interior maintenance includes three parts: full car interior cleaning, leather protection, and steam sterilization.

Full Car Interior Cleaning: It strictly follows the three steps of dust removal, cleaning, and maintenance to thoroughly clean and comprehensively maintain the instrument control panel, ceiling, rear cylinder platform, seats, floor carpet, inner door panels, etc. The whole process is operated by four staff members and takes about two and a half hours. Neutral or weakly acidic cleaning agents are used during the cleaning process. Their residues do not produce harmful gases, and they automatically evaporate within a few seconds, safe and environmentally friendly, without causing any harm to the human body.

Leather Protection: Professional car beauty shops are equipped with a complete set of leather care tools, and different tools are selected for different parts.

In addition, professional leather protection has certain requirements for the dosage of cleaning agents and maintenance agents, because excessive liquid agents will remain on the leather surface and absorb dust from the air, polluting the car environment.

Steam Sterilization: Professional steam sterilization not only performs high-temperature sterilization on the car's air but also focuses on sterilizing the air conditioning outlets, seats, carpets, and other parts where dust and bacteria easily accumulate, ensuring the complete killing of those invisible mites, molds, and microorganisms, protecting the health of your family and yourself. At the same time, it will not damage the car's interior decorations and will not cause secondary pollution to the environment.

Instrument Control Panel: The instrument control panel is the easiest place to accumulate dust and has many blind spots. When cleaning it by yourself, you can use a brush to brush the instrument panel, air conditioning inlet, switches, buttons, etc., every day to prevent dust accumulation and make it difficult to remove. If the instrument control panel is dirty, you need to spray a special instrument panel cleaner and wipe it clean with a clean soft cloth. After cleaning, you can spray a layer of dashboard wax.

Ceiling: The ceiling is a place that is easily overlooked, and once it is cleaned, there will inevitably be residual liquid dripping onto the seats or carpets.

Experts suggest: You can use a car vacuum cleaner to remove dust regularly, and when you go to the car beauty shop for comprehensive interior maintenance, you can thoroughly clean the ceiling.

Rear Cylinder Platform: We usually pile up paper towel boxes, dolls, cushions, and other sundries on the rear cylinder platform. Paper towels and cushions are often used by people sitting in the car, so the cleaning and dust removal of the rear cylinder platform cannot be ignored. Its cleaning method can refer to the instrument control panel.

Seats: Seats are the interior parts that car owners touch the most and get dirty easily, so maintaining the seats,

First: Place fabric seat covers on the seats and prepare another set that can be replaced and washed at any time.

Second: Try to avoid food or liquids spilling on the seats, especially alcohol-based or perfume-based items. Once they stick, if they are not cleaned within 12 hours, these stains will be difficult to remove.

Third: Try to avoid metal objects scratching the leather surface.

Fourth: Regularly use leather cleaners and conditioners for cleaning and maintenance to prevent leather from aging, fading, and cracking.

Experts suggest: The leather accessories inside the car should be maintained every six months.

Floor Carpet: When the floor carpet gets dirty, it is difficult to clean because it cannot be taken out of the car. Therefore, most car owners choose to go to professional car beauty shops for cleaning, and lay a foot mat on the floor carpet for daily cleaning. After washing the foot mat, squeeze out the water. Professional car beauty shops have special mat washing machines, which make the mat washing quick and clean.

Inner Door Panels: The main dirt on the inner door panels of the car is shoe prints, and they are all concentrated on the lower edge of the door panels. Attention should be paid to this area regularly and clean it frequently. Car owners can use professional foam cleaners to clean the area and then gently wipe it with a clean soft cloth.
