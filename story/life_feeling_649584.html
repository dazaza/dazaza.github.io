The eye curtain is slightly cool, outlining the appearance of loneliness.

People disperse after dusk, all thoughts worried. The lonely rain curtain locks the clear seclusion, fallen leaves don't understand, scattered in pity into the dust mound.

—— Written by Zi Mo

At twilight, the drizzle scatters silently, the heart follows entwined, faintly evoking a frame of sorrow. The dim yellow hue leads to a full view of desolation, shadows gradually appear, thin and long carrying the passage of time. At the boundary between black and white, neither bright nor dark, who is the one with a mournful tune from the Song lyrics?

Dew hangs on the tip of leaves, seemingly drowning in the deep night, yet a streak of moonlight shatters its roundness, glittering and fading, losing all color, what it possesses is clarity. A few breezes brush away the red dust of past events, the subtle changes in the flow of years drift further, ultimately helplessly swaying, once again swaying into scattered petals on the ground. Subtle fragrance floats, beautifying the hidden sorrow, poeticizing the night.

Pen in hand, suspended in air, yet hesitating to write the first stroke. The tender emotions in my heart flow, yet are trapped within the walls of my heart. Am I too melancholy, or are past events too sorrowful? Putting down the pen, leaning against the window, gazing, contemplating alone under the cold moon. The wind gently blows, brushing across the damp thoughts in my heart. Gently gathering and pinching loneliness, how much bitterness!

Suddenly, a cool wetness at the corner of my eye. Who, unintentionally, left a tear hanging at my eye corner?

Our deep affection, yet alas to the crisscrossing divergence of time. When the music ends and people disperse, it must be stranded in the destined lack of connection! Your beauty dissipates, going to a place I cannot reach.

White gradually turns to black, black gradually fades into white, in the alternation of black and white, the city's prosperity remains unchanged, you blow away the tears on my cheek, blending into the prosperity, forming a frame of sorrow. Shadow on the left hand, yearning on the right, savoring the drunkenness while walking alone in the edge of the season. How to smooth out the ripples in a pool of moonlight? How to let go of the woven curtain of a dream? How to package the scattered pieces of a broken heart into scenery?

A gentle breeze passes through sparse bamboo but the bamboo doesn't retain the sound of the wind, clear spring water splashes against stubborn stones but the stones don't keep the essence of the spring. I only wish, you were a dragonfly, touching the center of my lake, leisurely spreading ripples, as the dragonfly flies away and the ripples gradually flatten. Now, the rings of the years have increased, who has awakened my thoughts in the night, flipping through scenes of the past?

Door rings attract copper green, quiet nights attract clear worries, wanting to speak but holding back, wanting to speak but holding back until the dream is over.

When tea blossoms fall into mud, who picks them up? Who murmurs in sorrowful nostalgia? Suddenly looking back, several seasons have passed, in the waiting of years, mist blurs the corners of my eyes...

Walking alone in loneliness, dreams broken leaving only sighs, the eroding pain known by whom?!
