However, according to a 15% margin ratio, a T+D mode investor only needs to take out 15,000 yuan to buy 100,000 yuan worth of gold and silver assets. The lower the margin ratio, the more obvious the leveraged effect, and the corresponding returns and risks are also higher.

Another advantage is diversified transactions, with Gold and Silver T+D having a short-selling mechanism. The short-selling mechanism of Gold and Silver T+D means that once a reversal occurs after entering a long position, the long position can be closed and short selling can be done instead. Currently, five banks offer Gold and Silver T+D wealth management products: Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC), Industrial Bank, Minsheng Bank, Shenzhen Development Bank, and Everbright Bank. The operation process for Gold and Silver T+D in these five banks is basically the same. You can register and open an agency physical gold account at the designated counters of the corresponding banks. You can also open a personal online bank account to register and open an agency physical gold account. After receiving the successful registration information of the gold account from the Gold Exchange, you can sign the Precious Metal Deferred Trading Agreement of the corresponding bank through online banking and start operating the Au (T+D) business.

Among them, ICBC's telephone banking only provides services for Gold T+D business, while Silver T+D business cannot be processed through telephone banking yet.

Currently, although none of the five banks compulsorily require U-Shield for opening online banking accounts for precious metal transactions, the daily payment amount of password card customers generally cannot meet the transaction needs of Gold and Silver T+D, so it is still necessary to apply for a U-Shield to trade Gold and Silver T+D.

The trading starting point for Gold T+D in these five banks is the same, with 1000 grams of gold and silver as the smallest trading unit. The margins and handling fees of these five banks are different.
