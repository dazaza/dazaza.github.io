Teenage girl addicted to online games imitates game scene by jumping down from the third floor - Age1983

by hexiaoyao on 2008-10-19 13:15:47

This world is too crazy. Some people become paralyzed because of online games, and some people imitate online games to jump off buildings……

October 16, 2008, 17:24

As long as you jump down from a high place, you can improve the character's "flying" ability, and your power will rise to a higher level - this is just a plot in an online game. Due to excessive addiction to online games, Xiaoping (a pseudonym), a high school student at a vocational school in Tongling County, who is in the bloom of youth, actually confused the virtual world with the real world, jumping down from the third-floor dormitory window. Fortunately, she only sprained her ankle. At around 17:30 on October 13, a female boarding student at a vocational school in Tongling County jumped off the balcony of the third-floor dormitory after getting drunk outside, imitating a scene from an online game. Around 17:35 that day, after learning about the incident, the school immediately called the emergency services and reported it to the police. The police and the 120 emergency medical staff quickly arrived at the scene to handle the situation. In front of one of the girls' dormitory buildings at the school, students and teachers gathered. A female student was lying on the ground groaning in pain.

After investigation, it was learned that the injured female student was named Xiaoping (a pseudonym), 17 years old, and a second-year high school student at the school. While the emergency center staff were sending Xiaoping, who was in agony, to the hospital for treatment, the police conducted an investigation and interview regarding Xiaoping's jump within the school. After interviewing several of Xiaoping's close friends and informed teachers, the police obtained an important clue: Xiaoping's jump had a great deal to do with a popular online game currently in vogue.

It turns out that Xiaoping had recently become addicted to a very popular online game, often skipping classes to go to internet cafes to play games. For this reason, Xiaoping's teachers and friends frequently advised her, but Xiaoping wouldn't listen, entrusting all her mental space to the online game world. On the afternoon of October 13, after Xiaoping returned to the dormitory drunk, she boasted to her friends that she would soon be able to fly. Since everyone knew that Xiaoping was talking about the online game, they didn't pay much attention. However, an unexpected event occurred. Taking advantage of her friends' unawareness, Xiaoping climbed onto the balcony and jumped down.

Through online verification, the police indeed found similar plots in the currently popular online game. Currently, after diagnosis by the medical team, apart from a sprained ankle, Xiaoping has no other major injuries. The police remind young friends to stay away from the bad temptations of the virtual world, and never become so obsessed that you cannot extricate yourself, or even imitate such acts, so as to avoid causing tragedies.

(Anhui Business Daily Wu Bin, Xu Xiang, Zhu Liwen)
