Synopsis:

As a young boy, Bruce Lee was not interested in academics but was fascinated by martial arts. He also acted in movies with his father when he was a child. At seventeen, despite having only recently been introduced to boxing, he became the champion boxer of all Hong Kong.

Young and brash, Little Dragon, relying on his reputation as the Hong Kong boxing champion, challenged many opponents and made numerous enemies, eventually being hunted down by a gang. His father, determined to change his ways, forced him to travel overseas alone to make a living in San Francisco. Despite his lofty ambitions, he had to rely on others for support.
