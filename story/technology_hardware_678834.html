Nanjing's 33 enterprises will be relocated from the main urban areas this year

by eh4jh6rop on 2012-02-27 16:49:56

http://www.sina.com.cn On February 4, 2012 at 5:21 AM, Yangtze Evening News reported that after Nanjing's heavy-handed rectification of the first batch of 173 "three highs and two lows" (high pollution, high energy consumption, high emissions, low efficiency, low output) enterprises last year, the Nanjing municipal government held a press conference yesterday to announce that it would continue to rectify another batch of 162 "three highs and two lows" enterprises this year. In order to complete the optimization and adjustment of the city's industrial layout, the city will also relocate 33 main urban area industrial production enterprises this year, including Jincheng Group and Yurun Food, which are also on the list.

Reported by our reporter, Wang Juan:

Continue to heavily rectify polluting enterprises

Last year's rectification of 173 enterprises had four "big name" enterprises that did not complete the task

Nanjing rectified 173 enterprises last year, most of which were polluting and disturbing enterprises, some were chemical enterprises for rectification, and others were backward capacity enterprises. These enterprises needed relocation, conversion of production, time-limited rectification, or even shutdown orders.

How is the situation of these enterprise rectifications? Yesterday’s press conference summarized: Among the first batch of 173 three highs and two lows enterprises in the city, a total of 155 have been completely shut down, project shut down, closed and relocated. There are 14 through time-limited rectification, totaling 169 completed, accounting for 97.7% of the entire completion target.

There are still four enterprises that have not completed the task, which are some well-known "big name" enterprises. Among them, three enterprises need to be rectified and relocated this year, namely Kumho Tire, Mei Shan Chemical, and Shuguang Chemical. In addition, Nanhua Company will also complete its rectification this year.

This year, another 162 enterprises will be rectified, with Jiangning having the most involved enterprises

This "environmental storm" is continuing to spread. This year, Nanjing has again sorted out a second batch of 162 seriously polluting and severely disturbing enterprises, and announced the list and rectification requirements to society. Any enterprise that is seriously polluted, has no approval procedures, has environmental safety hazards, and is strongly reflected by residents will be directly shut down. And the areas involved are wide-ranging, including Liuhe, Pukou, Qixia, Gaosheng, Lishui, and Yuhuatai District all have enterprises to be shut down.

Among all districts and counties in Nanjing, Jiangning has the most enterprises to be shut down and rectified, accounting for 42, while Xuanwu District has the least with only 2. The comprehensive rectification standards are high and the requirements are strict, with the most prominent feature being "shut down first before rectification". Based on this, each enterprise will be handled according to actual circumstances. For example, a large number of enterprises that do not conform to industrial planning and are seriously polluted will be firmly shut down; for a few enterprises that conform to Nanjing's industrial development direction and have certain market space, they will be moved to the chemical park, and developed on the basis of rectification; and for those enterprises that conform to industrial development planning and can control particle pollution to a very low level, there will be time-limited governance. After meeting the relevant environmental protection requirements and resolving the disturbances caused to residents, they will be allowed to resume production.

The final deadline for completing the rectification depends on the situation, either in June or December this year. At that time, if any district or county has not completed the rectification task, it will be criticized throughout the city.

Industrial enterprises move out of the main urban area into parks

Nanjing plans to relocate 128 main urban area industrial enterprises within 2 years

Nanjing plans to relocate 128 industrial production type enterprises within the ring road within 2 years, and this year will focus on relocating and transforming 33 enterprises in the main urban area. Relocating main urban area industrial enterprises is to initiate the adjustment of the main urban area industrial layout. Provincial Party Committee Standing Committee member and Nanjing Party Secretary Yang Weize said that moving main urban area industrial enterprises out of the main urban area and into parks will free up urban space for unified planning according to local conditions. After greening, public engineering construction, and commercial development of the land vacated by the relocated enterprises, it can greatly improve the urban ecological environment and enhance and improve the main functions of the city.

Move 33 enterprises this year

Including Jincheng Group and Yurun Food

"Traditional development is driven by industry leading the city, but now it is driven by services leading the city." Yang Weize said that implementing an industrial layout adjustment is not about killing enterprises, but about achieving their upgrading.

According to investigations by the government department, there are currently 128 industrial enterprises in the main urban area of Nanjing that occupy more than 3 mu of land and are still in normal production. In order to win the "first battle", Nanjing started with key municipal-owned industrial enterprises and initially formulated relocation plans for the production land of China Electronics Panda Group, NAC, Donghua, and Jincheng Group in the main urban area.

In the relocation list, I found that Nanjing Jincheng Group Co., Ltd. located at No. 518 Zhongshan East Road, Nanjing LG Panda Electronics Co., Ltd. located at No. 118 Haifu Alley, and Jiangsu Yurun Food Industry Group Co., Ltd. located at No. 10 Yurun Road are all included in this year's industrial enterprise relocation plan. In addition, Petrochemical Jinling Petrochemical Company Chemical Plant No. 1, Nanjing Second Thermal Power Plant, and Nanjing Second Machine Gear Machine Co., Ltd. are also "on the list".

According to the action plan for adjusting the industrial layout of the main urban area of the whole city: this year will focus on relocating and transforming 33 enterprises, ensuring that by the end of the year, the new site construction projects of the 33 enterprises will be launched. Before the end of 2014, the original factory sites of the industrial enterprises included in the layout adjustment scope will stop production, and the optimization and adjustment work will be fully completed by 2015.

Three questions

What will the vacated land be used for?

Building roads, building parks, and land development

With the shutdown of "three highs and two lows" enterprises and the relocation of industrial enterprises out of the city, Nanjing will free up a large amount of land space. How will it be utilized in the future? It is understood that part of it will be used for the construction of urban infrastructure such as roads, some plots will be used to build citizen parks and create urban environments, and some will be used for land development. It is introduced that Nanjing is currently promoting the construction of science and technology incubators, and part of the factories vacated after the relocation of industrial enterprises will be used as startup bases.

Who owns the land revenue?

Not owned by enterprises, used for special funds to solve historical burdens

During the relocation, many enterprises believe that the land revenue should belong to the enterprise. Nanjing Mayor Ji Jianye stated that the land development revenue is not linked to the relocation, part of the land revenue is used to pay for the relocation cost, and another part is used for infrastructure construction. For example, for 100 acres of land, possibly 50 acres may be used for roads and greenery, 20 acres for small parks, requiring overall arrangement and scheduling; another part will be taken out for centralized use, some factories have less land but higher relocation costs, the city will appropriately make adjustments; the remaining money will be specifically used for government funds, not for other purposes, mainly used to solve problems existing in the industrial system, such as historical burdens, etc., solving historical legacy issues.

Is the industrial enterprise "disliked"?

Not abandoning industry, but upgrading

A batch of industrial enterprises bid farewell to the main urban area, and many chemical enterprises face rectification. Does this mean that Nanjing no longer "likes" industrial production enterprises? Regarding this issue, the government considers more about the city's positioning. As the provincial capital and regional central city, modern services should be the main function and future development direction of Nanjing's city, developing financial industries, scientific design, modern property management, etc., only in this way can Nanjing's radiation and driving force be reflected.

However, the development of modern service industries also needs the support of advanced manufacturing. Manufacturing can bring production logistics, solve the employment of residents and government tax problems. There is no backward industry, only backward industry. In the national economy, the chemical industry is indispensable. For chemical enterprises, Nanjing is not blindly "killing them all". Nanjing also has a chemical industry park. Nanjing does not abandon the chemical industry, but needs large-scale and advanced chemical industries.

Nanjing Party Secretary Yang Weize: Two Time Nodes, Enterprises Not Completed Will Be Shut Down

Regarding Nanjing's strong measures to rectify three highs and two lows enterprises, Yang Weize said that first, this is a major event. It is not just about eliminating backward industries, but also relates to the overall urban planning, industrial layout, and Nanjing's regional layout. Second, this is a good thing. Through the adjustment of industrial layout and enterprise rectification, it can promote environmental protection and enhance the quality of the city. "Factory workshops are short and fat, but beside them are skyscrapers, which really don't match."

In addition, it can disperse the urban population and optimize the structure and layout of the urban population. "It can be seen that wherever enterprises move from the old urban area, they get reborn and develop. In the urban area, the environmental protection and living costs of these old enterprises are high; their process flow is not optimized, the logistics cost is high; due to geographical constraints, it is difficult to expand production."

Short-term pain. Yang Weize emphasized that the rectification of "three highs and two lows" enterprises must be strict, and the relocation of main urban area industrial enterprises must be "good," with good policies and proper arrangements for employees. And in terms of action, it must be "fast." Yang Weize stated that the two-stage goals must be resolutely achieved. By June 2014, the rectification of three highs and two lows enterprises will be completed, and by 2015, the optimization of the industrial layout will be completed. If by 2015, there are still enterprises that have not completed the task, they will all be shut down. Enterprises that fail to rectify effectively will be publicly exposed to society.

Nanjing Mayor Ji Jianye: Each district and county has a "Panda," but none have been nurtured

Nanjing's industrial enterprises are somewhat "stagnant." Ji Jianye repeatedly emphasized when talking about the relocation of industrial enterprises that these are not simple relocations, but rather technical renovations and upgrades through relocation.

He mentioned multiple times in conversations with the responsible person of the Panda Group that if the Panda Group had forward-looking planning and owned a scale of more than 4 or 5 square kilometers in any development zone in Nanjing, the enterprise could not only compete domestically but also internationally.

"Now we find Pandas everywhere in each district and county, but none have been nurtured. The Morning Light Group is the same, sometimes we almost have to go to township development zones to see Morning Light."

"If there was concentrated planning and focused development back then, these enterprises could have grown significantly." Ji Jianye said that not only must the Panda Group itself seek development, but each district and county in Nanjing must also cultivate their own "Pandas" and grasp their own key development industries. Ji Jianye indicated that Nanjing is comprehensively considering the adjustment of industrial layouts, and the direction is clearer now. Chemical enterprises will concentrate in chemical parks, and some heavily polluting ones will be shut down; enterprises in the upstream of the industrial chain and deep processing enterprises will also be relocated to chemical parks. In addition, citizens are familiar with companies like the Haifu Alley LG Panda Electronics Co., Ltd., the nearby South Auto Group in Heimo Ying, the Yurun Food Industry Group Co., Ltd. on Yurun Road in Jianye District, and the Tianhuan Group are all on the first relocation list. It is understood that preliminary relocation plans have already been formulated for the production land of China Electronics Panda Group, NAC, Donghua, and Jincheng Group in the main urban area.

In addition, industries such as automobiles will mainly concentrate in Jiangning, Pukou, and Lishui, especially in the bridge forest area of Pukou where automobile and parts industries will be planned and concentrated (the place our company is moving to). Moreover, communication-related and refrigeration industries will move to Jiangning, home appliances to Lishui, machinery to Gaochun, light industry textiles to Liuhe, and biomedicine to the Hi-Tech Zone. Meanwhile, our city will evaluate the completion of the "three highs and two lows" enterprise rectification in various districts and counties, with July this year and January next year as evaluation time points. Districts and counties that cannot complete the tasks will be disqualified from participating in various advanced evaluations across the city. Industrial enterprises determined to relocate from the main urban area, if they do not relocate according to the city's unified plan or overdue without relocating, will not be eligible for related relocation reward policies in the future.
