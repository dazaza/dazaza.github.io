When faced with the market data that companies need, one often feels at a loss; occasionally wanting to conduct a small survey, but not knowing where to start; homework assignments for surveys can drive one crazy. When it comes to understanding the market, no one is perfect. "A wise man is not born different, he just knows how to make good use of tools." If you're not capable yourself, why not rely on something else?

The original intention of "问道" (AskWay) is to be a tool, created for users to depend on. While AskWay's interface may not be glamorous, beneath its simplicity lies practical service; its functions may not be powerful, but its innovative widget mode makes surveys less tedious; AskWay has no community, allowing you to focus solely on functional services without wasting time on irrelevant chatting or exchanges.

AskWay is very simple. It aims only to provide functionality to users, whether it’s creating questionnaires, publishing them, conducting more novel external widget surveys, or analyzing data after the survey. Every step of AskWay strives to make the user experience as simple and intuitive as possible, offering easy service and effortless work.

What "问道" (AskWay) wants to achieve is to build a personal survey analysis platform for everyone.

Free registered users can create up to 10 survey questionnaires on AskWay. Although it appears simple and traditional, this survey website incorporates many Web 2.0 elements. Designing the survey page is very convenient. Through API applications, the generated survey questionnaire can be seamlessly integrated into your website pages using Flash, JS code, frames, and other methods, making it visually appealing.
