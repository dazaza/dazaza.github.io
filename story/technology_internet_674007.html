Network recruitment and franchise industry: Spring comes after the winter

by boy7s7e on 2012-02-24 09:37:19

Recently, the author read the Industry Development Report on Online Investment Opportunities issued by Analysys International in 2011. The report shows that the scale of China’s online investment opportunities market has broken through the billion-yuan barrier and reached RMB 1.16 billion in 2010. It is predicted that the market will reach nearly RMB 2 billion in 2011. From this, we can see that online investment opportunities websites have become an important platform for enterprises to expand their sales channels and individuals to look for entrepreneurial investment projects.

The prevalence of false investment opportunity information [http://www.jm58.cn/] leads to industry winter

As early as 2009, many media reported on the false fraud cases of online investment opportunity websites, which sent the entire investment opportunity industry into a winter. At that time, online investment opportunity websites were extremely popular and hired film and television stars with heavy capital to endorse products. Comprehensive promotion channels covered television, online media, and zero blind spots comprehensively. For a time, investment opportunity entrepreneurship became a "myth." After the network myth collapsed, millions of investors' hard-earned money was defrauded and evaporated. More seriously, investors and entrepreneurs no longer easily believed in information from online channels and took a wait-and-see attitude towards all investment projects, leading the investment opportunity industry into a round of winter.

Industry needs external pressure, and online investment opportunity platforms become inevitable choices.

In recent years, there are some problems within the investment opportunity industry that cannot be ignored. Many small and medium-sized investment opportunity enterprises encountered problems such as unsmooth marketing channels and increased marketing costs, even leading to serious crises threatening the survival of enterprises. Meanwhile, enterprises see the development of high channel construction and predict and look forward to future markets. With the help of online platforms for investment opportunities, many enterprises have re-emphasized this approach. Marketing methods via the internet, characterized by wide coverage, fast speed, no time or regional restrictions, multimedia transmission, vivid imagery, two-way communication, and rapid feedback, have become the inevitable choice for the continuous rapid development of the franchise industry. Online investment opportunity websites have gradually become one of the effective market promotion methods on par with traditional media like television after extensive market validation.

Lack of industry self-discipline requires legal regulations to provide protection.

The reason for the existence of false projects on investment opportunity websites is multifaceted. Fundamentally speaking, all commercial actions are profit-oriented. In addition, there are loopholes in our country's laws, regulations, and supervisory systems, which have allowed investment opportunity websites to adopt an indifferent attitude towards false information, causing industry chaos. Without corresponding laws and regulations being introduced, this situation will continue indefinitely, falling into a vicious cycle.

The Tort Liability Law, implemented in 2009, provided a remedy for this long-standing problem. Article 36 stipulates that if an investor discovers that an investment project is a scam, they should immediately negotiate with the investment opportunity website. If the website fails to handle it promptly, the investor can recover part of their losses from the website if they cannot find the scammer. This provision strengthens the review responsibility of online investment opportunity websites for the information they publish to a certain extent, forcing them to conduct strict reviews; otherwise, they might become scapegoats for false information.

It is impossible for investment opportunity websites to rely solely on corporate self-discipline. Multiple ministries must work together to increase supervision and penalties for online platforms. The improvement of laws, regulations, and supervisory systems will inevitably protect the next wave of new highs in the online investment opportunity industry.
