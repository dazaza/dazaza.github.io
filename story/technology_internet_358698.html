The 3rd International Eco-Construction Expo will be held in November.

by ditan360 on 2010-06-22 09:55:03

Exclusive to Low Carbon Network: In 2009, General Secretary Hu Jintao first elevated ecological civilization construction to a strategic level equal to economic, political, cultural, and social development, as an organic component of the overall layout for building socialism with Chinese characteristics. Shanghai, as the center of China's economic development, has completed numerous new or renovated urban infrastructure, master planning, and landscaping projects in preparation for the 2010 World Expo, making it a benchmark for the latest urban construction domestically and bringing boundless business opportunities to many enterprises in the ecological industry and related sectors.

According to the United Nations Environment Programme's statistical report on global ecological development at the end of 2009, over the past five years, global green industry investment has shown a development trend of exceeding double-digit annual growth. And China, as the leading developing country in economic development, will become the global center for green industry investment and green product consumption in the coming years.

Highlight Review

Since 2007, the successfully held International Ecological City Construction Forum and the International Ecological Construction Mayors' Forum have laid a solid foundation for this industry event. Participating companies fully showcased their corporate images and latest technologies and engaged in discussions and exchanges with more than 30 mayors from relatively developed regions such as the Yangtze River Delta and Pearl River Delta regarding spatial organization forms and ecological planning for urban development.

In April 2010, the successful hosting of ECO WORLD in Singapore marked the beginning of the global tour showcase of this industry event. An exhibition area exceeding 5,000 square meters gathered participation from many well-known enterprises including Panasonic, IBM, HP, 3M, KPMG, ecoWise, Hyflux, etc.

2010, Focus on China, Initiate a New Low-Carbon Lifestyle

The 2010 International Ecological Construction Expo will be grandly held from November 2-4, 2010, at the Shanghai New International Expo Center. China has maintained the fastest growth rate in the global green industry market for consecutive years. After experiencing the global economic crisis, it has undeniably taken the lead out of recession and is brimming with vitality.

Promote Sustainable Economic Development

Minimize any behavior that may adversely affect the environment and society;

Seize the opportunity to make positive contributions to building an environmentally friendly society;

Creating job opportunities is just a short-term benefit; the long-term environmental gains are more significant;

Sustainable productivity, consumption concept, and ecological benefits;

Environment

An increasing number of people are engaging in ecological environmental protection actions, including voluntarily improving climate conditions. They use their ways to make the environment healthier, such as reducing the impact of human activities on land, using recycled materials, eating organic food, increasing more green spaces, using environmentally friendly materials to build green buildings and offices, etc.

Carbon Management

China is entering a new stage of rapid development. The Chinese government is successively introducing a large number of guidelines and policies regarding core technologies and sustainable technologies. All these are aimed at promoting and guiding the development of new-generation high-efficiency, low-carbon, emission-reduction technologies based on coal, establishing a reasonable and advanced carbon trading system and market, formulating certification standards in the field of carbon management, and introducing advanced carbon capture, storage, and utilization technologies.

ECO WORLD China 2010 Event Highlights

For the first time, the China Council for the Promotion of Trade joins hands with the Singapore Trade Development Promotion Bureau to inject the latest international green energy into the exhibition. The strong alliance of domestic and international trade professional committees represents multiple professional voices and opinions in the green ecological industry to jointly create the most valuable industry event.

More than 200 exhibitors from 15 countries and regions, 20,000 professional visitors, and an exhibition scale of 11,000 square meters gather numerous well-known enterprises in the global green industry to share the latest industry information, products, and technologies.

Opening Speech and Award Ceremony

Important government leaders and industry leaders are invited to give excellent opening speeches, highlighting the development of the industry. At the same time, the conference will present the 2010 Eco World Enterprise Award and the 2010 Best Original Sustainable Development Award.

Exhibit Scope

Eco Building - Ecological Architecture

Building insulation systems and materials, thermal insulation materials, energy-saving doors and windows, new wall materials, shading systems, roof systems and paving technology, water-saving technology and equipment, solar photovoltaic building integration, new structural technology systems, steel structures, integrated house technology and materials.

Eco Auto Industry - Ecological Automobiles

Pure electric vehicles, hybrid cars, hydrogen energy, natural gas, and other new energy sources, clean fuels, hybrid cars, and various low-emission, environmentally friendly, and energy-saving cars; low-emission energy-saving engines, hybrid engines, and clean fuel engines; power batteries and management systems; automotive electronics, automobile purification equipment, etc.

Green ICT - Green Information Technology

Intelligent transportation, smart grids, intelligent hotels/guesthouses, intelligent offices, green IT.

Renewable Energy

Solar energy, wind energy, biomass energy, geothermal energy, nuclear energy, tidal energy, traditional energy low-consumption modification technology.

Environment - Comprehensive Environmental Management

Water resources management and efficiency, flood management, sustainable urban drainage systems, rainwater collection, waste treatment, atmospheric governance, carbon capture, storage, and trading, green finance, green consulting certification.
