For example, great anger can cause the liver "qi" to rise in reverse, fear can lead to the sinking of "qi," and when the body is invaded by cold "qi," it contracts. The specific method is: perform this before sleeping at night and after waking up in the morning. Empty your bladder, wash your hands, lie on your back with knees bent, relax your whole body, place your left hand on your abdomen with the palm facing the navel, and stack your right hand on top. First, rub your abdomen 50 times clockwise around the navel, then 50 times counterclockwise. When massaging, apply appropriate force, concentrate, and breathe naturally.
