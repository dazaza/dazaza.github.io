Compared with traditional stores, opening an online store does not require renting a storefront or paying taxes and utility bills. Online stores are not restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run the store full-time or part-time without needing to spend a lot of time managing it.

3. Wide consumer base

Since the store is on the internet, it faces all possible consumers who may see the products, which could be netizens nationwide or even globally.

How exactly do you open an online store?

The variety of online stores is vast, ranging from cosmetics, clothing, to digital products. Each project has great development potential and prospects. The key lies in how to implement it and what suits you best. Below, I will guide you step-by-step on how to open an online store.

1. What to sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of products should be based on personal interests, abilities, conditions, as well as product attributes and consumer demands.

Since I love shoes and have been in this industry for many years, naturally, I started selling shoes.

2. Preparations before opening the store

After choosing what to sell, before opening the store online, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, identity verification and payment method certification are required.

I chose to open a store on Taobao because it is completely free. To open a store on Taobao, three conditions must be met: First, register as a member and pass the certification; second, list at least 10 items; third, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, input username, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link in the email, confirm it, and the user registration is complete.

Security tip: To ensure transaction safety, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity verification

Taobao stipulates that only users who pass real-name verification can sell items and open stores. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification) are required. Specific steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to "Want to sell items? Complete Alipay verification first."

Step 2: In the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated. Click "Click here to activate the Alipay account." In the pop-up page, input real name, type of ID, ID number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate the Alipay account" button.

Step 3: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page, click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real-Name Verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, and then click the "I have read" button to continue.

Step 4: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then select ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of ID documents, click "Next"; then, input bank card information, including bank, bank card number, province, city, etc. After completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer money within one day.

Friendly tip: If uploading ID document copies online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, a backside picture is also needed.

Step 5: One day later, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Deposit Amount," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Sourcing goods, taking pictures

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the sourcing channels. For the same product, prices vary depending on different sourcing channels.

After passing identity verification, you should start organizing the items you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial. It is also recommended to use appropriate graphic/image processing tools for picture format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Listing Items

To open a store on Taobao, besides meeting the membership certification requirements, you also need to list more than 10 items. Thus, after preparing the product details and pictures, you should start listing your first item.

Friendly Tip: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can still list items, but they can only be listed under "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing the verification can you put them up for sale.

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" listing methods. Here, select "Fixed Price."

Friendly Tip: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a reserve price, letting buyers bid to purchase.

Step 2: Choose a category. Select an appropriate category based on your product. For example, I selected the detailed information of women's shoes. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step 3: Fill in item information, which is a very important step. First, in the "Item Information" section, create a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input the item description, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; then, in the "Transaction Conditions" section, input the item's selling price, location, shipping cost, payment method, etc. Other information can remain at default settings, such as using Alipay by default. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to list the item.

If the listing is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the listed item page, and click "Continue Listing Items" to list more items.

Friendly Tip: If no bids have been placed yet, you can modify the listed item information by going to "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Selling Items" for editing and modification. Wangdian Le (www.wangdian6.com) teaches you how to open an online store.

After the items are listed, it's best to update and add them regularly to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

3. Getting a Free Store

Taobao offers certified members the opportunity to open a free store. As long as you list more than 10 items, you can own a store with its own independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

To get my store up and running quickly, I initially listed 10 items, spending nearly half a day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Next, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes My Show"; in the "Store Category" section, I chose the item category; in the "Store Introduction" section, I entered the store's brief introduction content. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with the store's address.

4. Shop Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, the buyer can obtain their own space. Just like traditional shops, to operate normally and attract customers, it is necessary to decorate the store accordingly, mainly including shop logo design, item categorization, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left-hand "Store Management" section, click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; the main business items need to be manually inputted; in the "Logo" section, click the "Browse" button to select the designed logo image; in the "Announcement" section, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Clicking the "Preview" button allows you to check the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items helps buyers find them easily. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Categorization" on the left side. Then, input a new category name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and enter a sort number (indicating the order position), and click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the category, and through searching keywords, you can add listed items for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

Taobao's "Recommended Items" function allows you to showcase your best 6 items prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left side. Then, you can select recommended items in the opened page and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give different impressions to buyers. Generally, selecting a color scheme that is elegant and comfortable is sufficient. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, and the preview appears on the right side. Clicking the "Confirm" button applies this style.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears before you.

5. Promotion is Key to Success

The online store is opened, items are listed, and characteristics are established, but weeks have passed without any sales, not even a buyer comment, a common problem encountered by many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced this dilemma and decided to promote the store through four methods: forum promotion, link exchange, window recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and utilizing signature lines.

The former can be done on forums of various provinces or cities. If there is a section allowing advertisement postings, you can post ads with detailed content, exquisite product images, and keep them updated and pinned regularly.

The latter involves changing your signature line on the forum to include your store's URL, logo, promotional phrases, and store name. Post attractive threads so interested friends can visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the initial stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus advertising your store for free. For instance, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links. Adding them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to search for all stores and note down the names of popular store owners.

Next, log in to http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download Taobao's buyer-seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add the names of these popular store owners, and request a link exchange.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left side, then input the store owner's name, and click the "Add" button.

3. Window Recommendation

Taobao's "Window Recommendation" feature is a special function provided for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" and search by category, window-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Window Recommendation" function, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Selling Items," select the items you want to recommend to the window (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., those marked with "Recommend"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

6. After the Item is Sold

After the item is sold, in addition to receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, request payment, ship the item, and handle evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang" or email. The seller can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer is not using "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, they agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it is recommended to use Alipay as the payment method.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent situations where goods arrive but payments are not made, sellers generally adopt a "payment received before shipping" method.

First, require the buyer to make the payment, usually through Alipay. After making the payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items" to query. If the transaction status shows "Buyer has paid, waiting for the seller to ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and at this point, the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction inquiries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the goods from the seller, they confirm it in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao transfers the funds to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to maintain contact with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items again. Wangdian Le specializes in creating a communication platform for online store owners, featuring main channels: online store experience, Youa online store, Taobao online store, and online merchant stories, guiding you on how to open an online store.

4. Evaluation, Complaints

After the transaction is completed, both the buyer and seller can open "My Taobao" to evaluate. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on the sold items, choose a positive, neutral, or negative review based on actual circumstances, and input text content.

Friendly Tip: How to become a highly-rated star seller on Taobao?

To become a star seller on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The seller's credit score is based on the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction. A "positive review" adds one point, a "neutral review" adds no points, and a "negative review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star seller, remember to provide honest service. If situations such as online deals not being followed through or goods received without payment occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to complain and report, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, and Wangwang screenshots needs to be collected.

Although online stores have zero cost and low risk, examples of unsuccessful transactions and "closing down" are also common. To let your small store survive online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the trust of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, enabling further development!
