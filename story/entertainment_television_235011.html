Plot Summary:

On the eve of her wedding, female detective Ou Qingchun's fiancé was martyred in a drug bust operation. In her grief and anger, she dedicated herself to continuing her lover’s unfinished cause and donated his cornea to college student Xiao Tong, allowing him to regain his sight.

Xiao Tong is a passionate and simple young man. Ou Qingchun's sense of justice, her identity, as well as her beauty and maturity far beyond Xiao Tong's imagination, sparked his love for her. Ou Qingchun also had some feelings for Xiao Tong.

However, life is never that simple. Anti-drug squad captain Li Chunqiang has been pursuing Ou Qingchun for years. For Li Chunqiang's care, Ou Qingchun carefully avoided it. Xiao Tong met Mr. Ouyang Tian's daughter, Ouyang Lanlan, by chance, and this capricious rich girl immediately fell deeply in love with him.

The anti-drug work continues, clues indicate that the hidden drug lord may very well be big boss Ouyang Tian. Knowing that Xiao Tong does not have feelings for Ouyang Lanlan, Ou Qingchun asked Xiao Tong for help, infiltrating the Ouyang family to conduct investigative work. Unable to bear Xiao Tong's indifference, Ouyang Lanlan designed a scheme according to her father Ouyang Tian's plan, causing Xiao Tong to fall into the trap of drug addiction.

Based on the clues provided by Xiao Tong, Ou Qingchun and her comrades seized a large amount of heroin, bringing the campaign to a temporary conclusion. However, Xiao Tong's condition worsened day by day, eventually being expelled from school due to drug use. Learning that all Xiao Tong's actions were out of love for her, Ou Qingchun was deeply moved. Not only did she help Xiao Tong quit drugs, but despite Li Chunqiang's opposition, she took Xiao Tong home, asking her father to take care of and supervise him, further deepening their relationship. One day, Ou Qingchun returned home and saw a syringe with white liquid residue on the bed, mistakenly believing that Xiao Tong had relapsed into bad habits, so she angrily slapped Xiao Tong. After her father explained the misunderstanding, when she finally found Xiao Tong, Ou Qingchun could no longer suppress her feelings and embraced Xiao Tong in front of Li Chunqiang.

Given the lack of progress in the anti-drug case, the superiors proposed letting Xiao Tong re-enter the Ouyang family to do internal line work. Ou Qingchun hesitated; she didn't know what impact re-establishing contact with Ouyang Lanlan would have on Xiao Tong. However, Xiao Tong, who had just returned to normal life, accepted the task. He wanted to prove himself for Ou Qingchun and sought revenge against the Ouyang family.

Xiao Tong once again appeared before Ouyang Lanlan, this time as an intermediary in a drug deal, regaining the trust of Ouyang Tian's gang by pretending to relapse into drug use. The cunning Ouyang Tian arranged for his subordinates to carry out the transaction while instructing his daughter to take Xiao Tong as a hostage for mobile surveillance.

Li Chunqiang disguised himself as the buyer, leading the team to completely annihilate the drug dealers coming to deliver the goods. Upon hearing the news, Ouyang Tian fled in panic and went into hiding. Xiao Tong and Ou Qingchun were separated, missing each other. Ouyang Lanlan used drugs to control Xiao Tong again and became pregnant. She genuinely loved Xiao Tong and believed that his best path was to stay by her side forever.

When Xiao Tong and Ou Qingchun met again, Ou Qingchun couldn't accept the fact that Ouyang Lanlan was pregnant and didn't want to listen to Xiao Tong's explanations. Xiao Tong felt ashamed, thinking the distance between him and Ou Qingchun was growing wider.

Ouyang Tian decided to temporarily leave the country to evade capture. Before departure, Ouyang Lanlan called Xiao Tong to say goodbye. Ou Qingchun tracked her down and arrived just in time. Xiao Tong was once again caught between two women. Ou Qingchun directed everyone to surround Ouyang Tian's group. Seeing Xiao Tong calmly walking towards her, in an instant, Ouyang Lanlan suddenly realized that she could never buy love with money. This man truly hated her, so she pulled out her gun and killed the man she had always wanted to possess. Xiao Tong fell, Ou Qingchun rushed over, picked up his body, and cried uncontrollably.

At a ceremony held by the Public Security Bureau, Ou Qingchun bid farewell to Xiao Tong with a deep kiss.
