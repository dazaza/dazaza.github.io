Battlefield OL is the first FPS online game launched by Changyou Sohu, and also the online version of the classic FPS single-player masterpiece "Battlefield" under EA. Its biggest feature is breaking the traditional FPS online game's fixation on street battles. The exclusive 50V50 hundred-person map will give players the most shocking battle experience. In such a huge map, there are hundreds of land, sea, and air weapons and a commander system, allowing each player to play to their fullest potential in every war, and each map is a battle, opening up a new realm for EA's battle online game masterpieces. In addition, all the hundred-person maps in Battlefield OL are realistically simulated, and each map can be found through an online search for its prototype. Medical soldiers can treat wounded comrades to ensure the team's combat effectiveness. Well-trained medical soldiers have the duty to take care of all comrades' injuries and lead the whole group to victory. Its advantage is that it can hold assault rifles and excel in short-distance and mid-range combat. It can use first aid kits to treat comrades and itself, and also use defibrillators to treat unconscious comrades. It has high stamina and excels in long-distance running.

Snipers can attack the enemy from afar. Although they can only kill one enemy at a time, well-trained snipers are enough to disrupt the entire enemy force. Snipers can also place anti-infantry mines in areas where the enemy often appears. Its advantage is being skilled in unrebuttable long-distance combat, and when accuracy is high, one bullet can kill an enemy.
