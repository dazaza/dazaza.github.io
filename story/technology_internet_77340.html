The domestic pre-registration of .ME domain names begins.

by now on 2008-07-11 14:05:13

The reporter learned from Times Network (www.now.cn) the other day that the domestic pre-registration of .ME domain names has officially started. Before July 16, 2008, anyone can make a trial application for this new domain name.

It is reported that the .ME domain name is the top-level domain name registration right that Montenegro, a country in southern Europe, made public to all users around the world in May this year. The opening of domestic pre-registration service came nearly two months late, but it could not conceal the expectations of all sectors for it.

"This is a very interesting domain name. For example, you can register trust.me (believe me), qiu.me (football fans), sayloveto.me (say love me), wait.me (wait for me)... At the same time, the Chinese meaning of 'ME' is 'I', which also symbolizes people-orientedness. For those individuals who love creativity and some innovative enterprises, the uniqueness of the .ME domain name will definitely fascinate them," an insider couldn't help talking non-stop when introducing the .ME domain name to the reporter.

The person in charge of Times Network told the reporter that this .ME pre-registration service only provides registration for English letters, numbers, and their combinations (a-z, 0-9, hyphen -, cannot appear at the beginning), with a length between 3-63 characters. The initial registration period is at least 2 years, and the price is 319 yuan/year. It is understood that Times Network is one of the first domestic institutions to open up the pre-registration service for .ME domain names.

According to relevant statistics, there are already more than eighty kinds of domain name registrations in mainland China. The addition of .ME has increased the family of domain names, greatly enriching the available domain name resources. Individuals and enterprises have obtained more opportunities to highlight themselves, protect brands, start businesses, and engage in online marketing.
