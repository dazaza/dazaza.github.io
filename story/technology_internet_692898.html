I. Preparatory Work: Taking the First Step to Success

I. Account Registration

1. Fill in Information: Log into the page and click "Free Registration" at the top to enter the free Taobao ID registration page. Submit the relevant registration information.

2. Receive Email: After submitting the registration, you will see the following prompt: "Thank you for registering with Taobao! Now please follow these steps to activate your account!" Enter the email address you submitted earlier, open the confirmation email sent by Taobao titled "Dear XX, complete the last step, and your registration is successful!", and click "Confirm" to successfully register and use your account.

Note: Taobao stipulates that users must be at least 18 years old to register, and the email address provided during free registration will become your Alipay account!

III. Alipay Activation

When you click the "Confirm" button, successfully register your Taobao account, and see some content about Alipay in the secure transaction section of the registration success prompt page, your Alipay account name is the email address you provided, and the Alipay login password is the same as your Taobao login password, not your email password.

Next, we need to activate our Alipay account. Click the "Activate Alipay Account Here" button to enter the Alipay login interface. Enter the Alipay login password and the captcha code on the page to access the information filling page. Choose individual for opening a personal store, fill in your personal information and set up your Alipay account information. Then click the "Save and Immediately Activate Alipay Account" button. You will see the system prompt: Congratulations! You are now an Alipay member! (After registering as an Alipay member, you need to go to the bank to handle the "One Card Pass" service, i.e., "Binding Your Bank Card with Alipay" to conduct online transactions. If you plan to live long-term in another province in the future, you can also cancel the "Bank Card Binding with Alipay" service at the bank counter where you originally handled the "One Card Pass" business and then use your original Alipay account in another province. This avoids the inconvenience of paying withdrawal fees for inter-provincial transactions, so canceling "Bank Card Binding with Alipay" can be more cost-effective. Additionally, many buyers and sellers prefer to bind their mobile phones with Alipay on the "Alipay Website," allowing buyers to know whether the seller has shipped goods via text messages, and sellers to know whether the buyer's payment has arrived.)

Note:

1. The real name and ID number submitted in personal information must match. Please fill out honestly as personal information cannot be modified once submitted, and incorrect entries may affect your ability to make or receive payments!

2. When setting up Alipay account information, the login password and payment password should not be the same, and there are many passwords to remember during registration, so they must be memorized to complete transactions! (It is recommended to write them down to avoid forgetting or making mistakes.)

IV. Personal Real-name Verification of Alipay

After obtaining your Taobao account and Alipay account, to open a store and sell items, you must first undergo Alipay certification. We choose personal real-name verification for Alipay, which requires three steps: 1) Submit personal information; 2) Verify identity documents; 3) Verify bank account.

We open the page, log in with the ID, and click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. We can see the following prompt:

Click the blue "Please Click Here" button to perform personal real-name verification of Alipay. After accepting the terms, you will see the interface below, which includes the three steps required for personal Alipay verification: Fill in Personal Information, Submit Identity Documents, Verify Bank Account.

First Part: Fill in Personal Information: This step requires applicants to truthfully fill in their name (non-modifiable), contact address, and fixed telephone or mobile phone (modifiable).

Second Part: Submit Identity Documents: This step requires you to upload scanned copies of your ID card and ID number. Note: If using a new electronic IC ID card, both sides of the ID card must be scanned and uploaded.

Third Part: Verify Bank Account: This step requires filling in details such as bank account holder’s name, bank name, bank card number, branch name, and province/city where the bank is located. Since it involves money, please fill in accurately.

After submitting all the materials, the status of the first two steps will change to: "Your information has been submitted, click here to modify information." You can modify any modifiable information at any time, and the status of the third part, bank account verification, will change to: "Waiting for Alipay Company to transfer money."

To verify the validity of the bank card you submitted, Alipay will transfer less than one yuan to your bank card within 2-3 working days, waiting for you to confirm the amount transferred. At this point, the status of the third part will change to: "Alipay Company has transferred money to your card, waiting for your confirmation." If the amount is correct, you can pass the verification. Therefore, you must check the balance in your bank card when submitting the materials. For example, if your bank card balance was 500 yuan when you submitted the materials, and after the status prompts "Alipay Company has transferred money to your card, waiting for your confirmation," you find the balance increased to , it means Alipay transferred yuan to your bank card. You only need to enter yuan in the confirmation page and click confirm!

Note:

1. The names submitted in all three steps must be consistent.

2. When performing the third step, make sure to check the balance in your bank card when submitting it.

3. To facilitate checking the balance in your bank card and recharging Alipay after successful application, it is recommended to enable online banking for the submitted bank card, avoiding frequent trips to the bank. (Of course, you don't necessarily need to enable online banking since it incurs certain usage fees. People who do not enable online banking should personally go to the bank counter to check and handle other matters.)

After passing all three reviews, personal real-name verification of Alipay is completed, and you can conduct transactions on Taobao as an independent authorized individual!

II. Free Shop Preparation: Taking the Second Step to Success

After completing the above application and verification work, you now only need to upload 10 items to open a free shop! How exactly do you upload items on Taobao? Enter the page, log in with the ID, and click "Sell" and "Fixed Price" at the top of the page to upload items. When clicking "Fixed Price," you will see a classification directory selection operation page. For example, uploading "China's First Box -- Le Yiduo Preservation Box," simply input the keyword "preservation box" in the search bar to see all classifications related to preservation boxes. Select the most appropriate category, click "Confirm" to enter the detailed item upload page. Specific situations can be filled according to your item's detailed information for product listing.

The item's title, uploaded images, description, quantity, fixed price, and location are all filled according to actual circumstances. You can add your artistic touch. It is suggested to select "Now" for "Start Time" and "7 Days" for "Validity Period." For "New/Old Condition," select "Brand New" for new items and "Used" for half-used items. Beginners are advised to select "Buyer Bears Shipping Costs" for "Shipping Fees," which can be determined based on the item's actual weight and the average distance to the recipient. (Specific shipping costs can refer to the detailed shipping fee information provided on the logistics company's website.) Other contents can be system default options. After completing all contents, click "Upload Item" below, wait for the system to prompt "Successful Upload," and you have completed uploading one item. You can view the item you just uploaded under "My Taobao" -> "Items for Sale." If modifications are needed, click the button resembling a "Notepad" behind the item to enter the modification page.

Following the above method, after successfully uploading 10 items, click "Free Shop Opening" on the left side of "My Taobao" to create your own store.

Note:

1. When listing items, there are two types: "Fixed Price" and "Auction Price." Fixed price is the price others directly pay to purchase the item, while auction price starts from your starting bid within 7 or 14 days, and the highest bidder gets the item after the period ends. Because auctions require learning many methods, it is recommended that beginners use fixed price listings when starting a store.

2. Before listing items, we need to classify items on Taobao. It is suggested that beginners try to choose the right classification because the Taobao system automatically scans our items based on keywords in the product name. If the classification is too wrong, our items may be automatically removed. Within a natural quarter, if the rules for publishing items are violated and accumulate up to 30 items, a penalty of restricted trading for 1 week will be imposed, and if it accumulates up to 60 items, restricted trading for 1 month will be imposed.

3. It is recommended that sellers try to replace their item names with personalized ones, such as adding descriptive words, which can increase the rate of transactions.

III. Building the Store Framework: Taking the Third Step to Success

After clicking "Free Shop Opening," we enter the "Basic Settings" page of "My Store Management" (or through "My Taobao" -> "Manage My Store" -> "Basic Settings"). We can think of a unique, novel, or easily recognizable store name that tells customers what you sell. In the "Store Category," choose the general type of items you want to sell, and fill in the specific subcategory in the "Main Projects." Simultaneously, we can roughly introduce our small store in the "Store Introduction" to attract buyers' interest, increasing the likelihood of transactions.

Next, we can upload a "Logo" for our store. The logo picture is the symbol of your store, and a good logo picture can increase your store's browsing rate. Currently, logo pictures support gif and jpg formats, limited to 80K in size, with dimensions of 100*100px. You can search for suitable pictures online, and those familiar with Photoshop can create one according to their needs. After preparing the picture, click "Browse," upload the image, and it will be set.

Finally, we can write content in the "Announcement" section. This content, after editing, will roll in the announcement bar of our store, allowing buyers to see the store information you want to publish, such as newly arrived items, discounts, and your contact information, facilitating communication when buyers want to purchase items. A personalized store announcement can attract buyers' attention and achieve better promotional effects.

After completing all the steps, clicking "Confirm" completes the construction of the store framework. All the content filled in can be modified through "My Taobao" -> "Manage My Store" -> "Basic Settings." For more complex personalization methods, please refer to the tutorial "Beautify Your Store" below.

Tips and Methods for Finding Goods Sources for Online Stores and Issues to Consider

Entrepreneurship Network Cye experts analyze: After determining what to sell, it's time to find sources of goods. One of the important factors for online stores having space is the lower cost. Mastering high-quality and affordable sources of goods is key to successful e-commerce operations.

For example, with clothing items, some well-known brands are priced uniformly nationwide, usually discounted to 85% in regular stores, while online they can be sold at 70-80%. On the web, clothing prices are often 20-70% of mall prices.

So, how can you find low-cost sources of goods?

1. Become a Market Hunter

Closely monitor market changes and take advantage of discounted goods to find affordable sources. For instance, popular branded clothes sold online are often sourced during seasonal changes or clearance sales, utilizing regional or time differences to gain sufficient profit margins.

2. Focus on Foreign Trade Products

If you know foreign trade manufacturers, you can directly source goods from the factory. In leftover foreign trade orders, there are often many good items, mostly available in 1-3 pieces, styles that are currently or next year's hottest trends, priced at 40-70% of mall prices, making them highly marketable.

3. Purchase Brand Surplus Inventory

Some brands have a lot of surplus inventory, and some merchants simply sell all their stock to dedicated online sellers. If you have good bargaining skills and can buy their surplus inventory at low prices, you could earn substantial profits.

4. Search Websites Specifically Providing Goods for Online Stores

Goods information published online often comes with pictures and detailed references, sparing you the trouble of traveling around. You can also compare similar goods in detail.

5. Obtain Discounted Goods Abroad

World-class brands abroad are heavily discounted before the season or holidays. If the seller has relatives or friends abroad, they can help obtain attractive discounts for online sales, even if the selling price is 40-70% of traditional mall prices, still leaving a 10-40% profit margin.

6. Wholesale Goods

Visit regional wholesale markets frequently, such as Xizhimen, Xiushui Street, and Hongqiao in Beijing, or Xiangyang Road and Chenghuang Temple in Shanghai, to familiarize yourself with the market and get very cheap wholesale prices.

After finding the source, you can initially test-sell a small quantity of goods. If sales are good, consider increasing the order volume. Some sellers maintain good relationships with suppliers and only restock after selling the goods, avoiding capital lock-up and product accumulation.

In summary, regardless of the channel used to find sources of goods, low prices are the key factor. Finding high-quality and affordable sources lays the foundation for the success of your online store.

General Process of How to Make Money by Opening a Store on Taobao

I. Before Opening the Store:

A. Registration

Username: The name is crucial; a distinctive name can draw more attention and make you memorable. How to be distinctive varies depending on individual preferences. Avoid overly long English names or completely meaningless strings of numbers. We are Chinese, though some may have good English skills, I believe most Taobao buyers are like pig brains (if this analogy offends, please forgive). Many don't have proficient English skills, so to me, a string of letters is no different from a meaningless string of numbers, and even after seeing it N times, I might still not remember it.

B. Apply for Certification

Apply for certification: A simple action requiring just a few mouse clicks but needing patient waiting. It's best to use a digital camera or scanner to photograph or scan your ID card. Although faxes can also be used to upload ID cards, sometimes the quality is very poor, and many people have tried uploading multiple times without clear results, failing certification. Most sellers have digital cameras, so taking a clear picture shouldn't be difficult. Then wait three working days, and the certification result should be out. If for some reason you fail certification, Taobao customer service will contact you.

Taobao University: Waiting for certification is neither too short nor too long, and this time can be used for market research and learning. There is much to learn before opening a store. Taobao University is a good place. Besides considering sources of goods, you also need to consider pricing, delivery, after-sales issues, etc., all of which can be answered in Taobao University. Absorbing predecessors' experience is beneficial.

II. After Passing Certification

A. Restocking

Certification passed, but you still can't open a store yet; you need to maintain at least 10 items for sale. After a few days of considering what to sell and where to restock, you can start. Taobao University has many posts about sources of goods, and if unclear, you can review them. The essence posts in the Experience Sharing Section are also worth reading.

If you don't have a physical store or excellent sources of goods, I suggest selling trendy gadgets or unique items at relatively low prices. Most Taobao buyers are students or young office workers aged between 15 and 35. Identify the target audience and products, and you can start visiting similar shops on Taobao. Study high-level stores, observe their items, sales conditions, and characteristics, aiming to differentiate yourself from competitors.

B. Photography

Items are back, time to take beautiful photos of them. It's not recommended to use supplier-provided photos. Real-life photos give buyers a sense of authenticity and reflect the seller's dedication. Taobao University has many posts about photography and photo editing, worth learning. Aim to make the items look beautiful while maintaining realism. Over-edited photos may lead to discrepancies in future transactions.

After taking photos, you can add a light watermark with your store name (you should already have a store name in mind). Once the store opens, include the store URL to prevent others from stealing your item pictures. If someone does steal them despite the watermark, you can smile because they're helping promote your store for free.

C. Item Name

With beautiful item photos, it's time to list the items. Think about the keywords you would use to search for this product if you were the buyer. Besides giving the item a nice name, focus on commonly used keywords, which are crucial for ensuring your item appears in searches.
