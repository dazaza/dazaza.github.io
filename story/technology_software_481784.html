Jiamusi City of Heilongjiang Province is located in the Sanjiang Plain, and its agricultural mechanization level ranks first nationwide. The unique agricultural resource advantages, market advantages, policy advantages, industry advantages, and Heilongjiang Province's vigorous development of modern agriculture and implementation of the billion-pound grain production capacity project have all provided rare conditions and opportunities for the development of the city's new agricultural machinery equipment manufacturing industry. Nowadays, Jiamusi City has determined to make the agricultural machinery industry a pillar industry, and a national "modern agricultural machinery industry town" is rising. And this would not have been possible without the hard work of the employees of Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau...

"How is the progress of the park project, do you need any help?" On the morning of May 11th, Liu Haibo, director of Jiamusi Agricultural Machinery Industry Park welcomed an old friend, Liu Huabing, customer manager of Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau.

The Jiamusi Agricultural Machinery Industry Park is one of the key projects for Jiamusi City to build a core area for comprehensive agricultural development experiments. In 2011, the number of enterprises entering the Jiamusi Agricultural Machinery Industry Park will reach more than 30, with an estimated electricity capacity of 6290kVA.

In 2010, the construction of the Jiamusi Agricultural Machinery Industry Park began. At the beginning of the park's establishment, Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau actively contacted relevant responsible persons of Jiamusi City and the park, opened a green channel, and proactively provided door-to-door services. In the relocation and renovation construction of power facilities, special cases were handled specially, and vehicles and manpower were provided free of charge, working overtime to carry out construction. In August of that year, the customer service center of Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau learned that five companies, including Changfa Jialian, Longjia Agricultural Machinery, and Hua Yu Drying Cleaning Equipment Factory urgently needed construction power sources. Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau immediately assigned dedicated personnel to handle the temporary electricity use of the agricultural machinery industry park and feasibility research for formal electricity use. On August 20th, with a crisp sound of closing the switch, the construction machinery on the construction site of Changfa Jialian Agricultural Equipment Co., Ltd. started running. Wang Baicheng, the vice president of construction at Changfa Jialian, said excitedly, "I have built many enterprises, but it was the first time I encountered such a situation where electricity could be supplied within a week after applying for it. Thank you, comrades of the Electric Power Bureau, for giving our project such great support!"

The construction of the Jiamusi agricultural machinery production base also drove the rapid development of the agricultural machinery large market. Currently, a larger scale agricultural machinery large market is under construction in the southeast part of Jiamusi. One very difficult problem during the construction preparation process is relocation. In the construction address area of the Jiamusi agricultural machinery large market, residents, shops, and small factories are mixed together. Shao Dongming, the team leader of the internal line class of the Jiadong Power Supply Bureau of Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau, who is responsible for the internal line business in this area, said, "It's simply unfeasible to remove old electric meters and wires house by house in large quantities in this area. To avoid delaying the construction of the agricultural machinery large market, we will rush to the scene as soon as we receive a call to dismantle the meter."

While fully ensuring the power supply for the agricultural machinery industry park and the new agricultural machinery large market, Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau also undertook the power supply tasks for the two sessions of the China-Russia Agricultural Machinery Product Exhibition and Trade Negotiation Conference since 2010. It is understood that during the negotiation conference period, Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau conducted special inspections on the lines and equipment of the power protection venues, overhauled the transformer platforms supplying power, and assisted the property rights units in using infrared thermometers to comprehensively inspect the switches, knife switches, and joint nodes of the self-maintained lines and equipment of the power consumption venues. Meanwhile, to ensure power supply without any problems, Jiamusi Electric Power Bureau also equipped generator vehicles, accident emergency repair vehicles, and materials, mobilized backbone personnel, and maintained 24-hour duty shifts to deal with emergencies at any time. (Qu Ying, Chen Rujian)
