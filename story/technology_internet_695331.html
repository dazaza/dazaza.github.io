[Qingdao Travel] Cost-saving Tips for Watching Rapeseed Flowers in Wuyuan

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-07 15:23:10

How to Avoid Crowds

① Early Out, Early Back

Around 6 AM in March and April, you should reach the scenic area. At this time, it's quiet and allows for a good 1-2 hour tour. Tour groups generally start arriving between 7 AM and 7:30 AM. During peak season, plan to return to your accommodation early, especially if you're driving yourself. You should prepare to leave by 4 PM to avoid potential traffic surges. If you want to visit popular scenic areas without the crowds, it's best to stay at hotels or local accommodations near the scenic areas.

② Avoiding Crowds on Routes

The East Line is a must-visit during the spring peak season and is a prime scenic area. Visitors can avoid peak hours using the methods mentioned above, or they can adjust their routes. The East Line is a popular choice due to its convenient transportation between scenic spots. However, the North Line offers equally beautiful scenery but is further apart, making it less convenient for individual tourists who lack transportation. Travel agencies also consider the time arrangement of tours, so there won't be a large concentration of tour groups. Therefore, the North Line can be prioritized.

Tips for Viewing Rapeseed Flowers on a Budget

① Accommodation

In Wuyuan, you generally have two choices for accommodation: staying in hotels or guesthouses in the county town or scenic areas; or contacting local households to stay in farmhouses or villagers' homes. Prices are slightly higher in the county town, while those in scenic areas and farmhouses are relatively lower.

Many backpackers choose to stay in Xiaqi for their first night. Xiaqi's nightlife is rather dull, and prices are generally above 40 yuan. It's better to stay in Likeng (which is closer to the county town), where you can enjoy starry nights and the sound of streams. Room rates are cheaper, around 10 yuan, clean, with convenient bathing facilities.

② Food

Wuyuan has a simple and honest folk style. You can eat directly at a farmhouse (avoid well-known scenic spot farmhouses as their commercial awareness is gradually increasing). You can enjoy free-range chickens, fresh vegetables, and specialty pickles at an absolutely cheap price, usually about 10 yuan per person.

③ Transportation

Many people think Wuyuan is just a village that can be easily toured. However, upon arrival, they often get lost and don't know what to do.

④ Minibuses

Buses from various cities to [Wuyuan] refer to Ziyang Town, the location of Wuyuan County. The three tourist routes of Wuyuan all center around Ziyang Town. Buses coming from various directions usually stop at the West Station or North Station, with the North Station being the hub for buses to various towns. Buses to Jiangling, Qingyuan, Guankeng, and Xitou can be boarded at the East Gate Fruit Market. For the West Line, passengers can board buses on the street where the Qinghua Wuyuan International Hotel is located on Wengong North Road.

Many backpackers prefer to hire cars, but self-guided tours offer more freedom. Minibus services are convenient for both the East and North Lines, and you can simply hail them. They might charge more seeing that you're a tourist, but remember: short distances cost around 3 yuan, longer ones about 5 yuan.

⑤ Bicycles

There are dedicated bicycle rental points in Wuyuan County. You can contact local shop owners via phone or online beforehand. Cycling is not only a healthy and economical way to travel but also a great option.

⑥ Car Rentals

During off-season, the daily price for car rentals (East and North Line scenic routes) is around 150 yuan/day. In peak season, it ranges from 200 to 300 yuan/day.

Notice for Viewing Rapeseed Flowers

① Flower Season Forecast

Official information from the Wuyuan scenic area: On February 25th and 26th, after on-site observation along the Northeast lines, most of Wuyuan's local rapeseed varieties are in the bud stage awaiting bloom, with very few plots showing initial blooms. Snow fell in Qingyuan and Jiangling on the evening of the 25th. Non-local varieties — Victory Rapeseed — are still in the vegetable heart growth stage. If the weather remains normal, flowers will begin blooming sequentially starting early March, reaching peak viewing mid-March, though rapeseed in places like Qingyuan may not bloom until late March.

② Scenic Spot Distribution

To the west towards Jingdezhen is the West Line, featuring attractions such as Kaoshui, Wengong Mountain, Kengtou, Mandarin Duck Lake, You Mountain Village, Fengyou Mountain, etc.; To the north towards Qinghua Town is the North Line, featuring attractions such as Sixi Yan Village, Rainbow Bridge, Wolong Valley, Hundred Pillar Ancestral Hall, Shicheng, Lingyan Cave, Lipei, Huang Village, Zheyuan Long Tian Pagoda, Hongguan, Wu Chu Fen Yuan, Yantian Ancient Camphor Tree, Changxi, etc.; To the east towards Huangshan (Tunxi) is the East Line, featuring attractions such as Moon Bay, Likeng, Wangkou, Jiangwan, Huangling, Xiaqi, Jiangling, Yanshan, Xitou Bai Zhang Chong, ancient post road, high-altitude lakes, Qingyuan, Guankeng, etc.

③ How to Get to Wuyuan

Since Wuyuan does not have an airport and the high-speed rail is under construction, out-of-province visitors can only choose bus, train, or plane to reach Wuyuan directly. Generally, a transfer station is necessary, mainly recommended from six aspects:

Direct bus conditions from other provinces to Wuyuan:

Zhejiang Quzhou, Zhejiang Kaihua, Zhejiang Yiwu, Zhejiang Ningbo, Hangzhou, Shaoxing, Dongyang, Wenzhou, Yueqing, Shanghai, Anhui Huangshan, Anhui Hefei, Anhui Yixian, Hubei Wuhan (seasonally available).

Nanchang Transfer Station: Nanchang, the provincial capital of Jiangxi, has relatively convenient direct flights and trains from all over the country. Currently, there are direct buses from Qingshanhu Bus Station in Nanchang to Wuyuan, with four daily trips taking approximately three and a half hours. (Note for tourists from northern and northeastern Huadong provinces traveling by train or plane.)

Shangrao Transfer Station: Wuyuan belongs to Shangrao City. You can first take a train to Shangrao, where direct buses to Wuyuan are convenient, with one every half hour, taking roughly four hours. (Note for tourists from the Guangdong Pearl River Delta region.)

Jingdezhen Transfer Station: Jingdezhen is a city relatively close to Wuyuan, with frequent direct buses. The entire journey takes about 40 minutes on the highway. (Note for tourists flying from Beijing, Shenzhen, and Shanghai.)

Huangshan Transfer Station: Choosing Huangshan as a transfer station is also a good option. This place has an airport, railway station, and bus station. You can take a bus from the Huangshan Bus Station directly to Wuyuan, taking about one hour on the expressway, with very convenient transportation. (Note for tourists from Beijing, Chengdu, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Xiamen.)

Plenty of Rapeseed Flower Seas

Rapeseed flowers aren't only beautiful when seen in Wuyuan. Actually, during March and April, the "golden feast" of rapeseed flowers unfolds across the country in all directions. If you want to save money, avoid crowded places and find a less busy spot where you can still be surrounded by endless seas of flowers.

① Southern Anhui Mountains

A 60-year-old Nanjing grandmother, Chen Caiping, rode two folding bicycles over seven years, covering 12 provinces, over 50 cities, and hundreds of towns across the country. The "grandmother" went on her first "backpacking trip," which was precisely to the rapeseed fields of southern Anhui. She said, "Don't go to Wuyuan, it's too expensive. There are plenty of rapeseed flowers in southern Anhui, scattered in large patches around mountain villages. There's no need to specifically go to Wuyuan, where it's overcrowded."

② Ninghai Sangzhou in Zhejiang - More Vibrant Flowers on Slopes

Sangzhou has a simple and hardworking population, diligent in farming, fond of planting rapeseed. With many gentle slopes on the highlands, villagers have developed the habit of planting rapeseed in large patches on terraced fields. In early spring, during the blooming season, rapeseed flowers compete to open, especially in the Xiaozhang Village area of Nanshan Mountain in Sangzhou. During the rapeseed flowering season, the yellow and green colors intermingle, with golden rapeseed flowers covering the mountainsides, creating a stunning sight.

③ Brilliant Rapeseed Flowers on the Western Sichuan Plain

Early April is the most splendid time for rapeseed flowers to bloom on the western Sichuan plain. Centered around Chengdu, it radiates to surrounding suburban areas. This inland plain has a relatively large rapeseed planting area, attracting most tourism enthusiasts from the city and its suburbs for spring outings. However, rapeseed flowers in northern Sichuan highlands, similar to those in Qinghai and Tibet, bloom later, typically visible only from June to July.

④ Tongnan Chongkan Town, Chongqing

The overall terrain of Chongkan Town is a combination of hills and plains, with farmland and rivers crisscrossing each other. In early spring, during the peak blooming season of rapeseed flowers, it presents a unique beauty of water and mountains reflecting each other, with golden flowers spread everywhere. Tourists can either take the land route to enter the countryside, wander freely among the sea of rapeseed flowers; or choose the water route for sightseeing, taking a small boat to drift along the Qiong River, where the beautiful scenery on both banks will keep you amazed and delighted. The staggered farmland, houses surrounded by flowers and trees, and the yellow flowers reflected in the green water weave together into breathtaking natural paintings.
