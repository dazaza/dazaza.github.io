A sentence from the rescued miners slapped those officers!

by hu0chun88 on 2010-04-09 22:47:57

"Having grown up so much, I have never been treated so well." This was a soft remark by the rescued miner from Wangjialing, Nong Rongwu, to the medical staff standing guard beside him.

The background to this statement is the special train that transported the rescued miners arriving at Taiyuan Railway Station. Immediately upon stopping, 60 ambulances neatly lined up like a long dragon, each one only carrying one miner! Behind this scene, CCTV broadcasted the entire process live, and there were all sorts of leaders shaking hands and expressing endless gratitude.

Under the spotlight, the dignity of these miners shone brightly! Under everyone's gaze, at this moment, the miners were as precious as the national treasure, the panda! Discipline comes from under the stick, and dignity appears before the lens; attention reveals humanity... History has once again proven that nothing has changed, and nothing will change! The only thing left to do now is to wait for the Wangjialing incident to subside, slowly fading from people's memories, then there will be another place like Zhangjialing, LiJialing, or ZhaoJialing quietly burying lives without attention, without cameras, without anyone noticing.

What's stained with blood is not a lesson, but waiting — waiting for the next quiet disaster to come quietly, without needing CCTV, without needing cameras, without needing eyes, lives as insignificant as ants coming and going quietly! Housing prices are so high, wages are so low, life is so busy... no one will always focus on those disasters that keep happening over and over again, no one will keep their eyes on our distant compatriots! Thus, life declares again: it's still a life where survival depends on fate, wealth determined by heaven, the only difference being how many cycles you've gone through!

In this short period of time, these miners who were buried deep underground and came back to life experienced an honor beyond what they could possibly achieve in their lifetimes. It wasn't something they earned themselves; it was merely props needed for the performance under the lens! As soon as the flashlights disappear, all this will vanish without a trace along with the fading lights, quickly returning them to those familiar yet dreaded mines, fighting for survival once more with their lives against luck! But they don’t know if there will be any cameras pointing at them next time! Or if they will even be able to look at the camera with open eyes next time! If there is a next time!

There are too many things that can make people forget sorrow! Those tears shed under the lens will dry quickly in the wind, those faces filled with heart-wrenching pain just moments ago will soon change. Sitting in luxurious official cars, sipping tea, holding a coquettish and restless spirit in their arms, the dazzling neon lights in their eyes, that majestic office building, those palace-like villas built for needs, those smiling faces on banknotes—all these can make people forget about Wangjialing very quickly, very quickly indeed! There’s too much in this world that needs to be remembered! Sometimes, forgetting Wangjialing is even easier than forgetting your beloved pet dog "Puppy"!

Climb out, let me give you safety. Climb out, let me give you dignity. When living becomes a form of dignity, we no longer have the choice to need or not need dignity! While CCTV emotionally shouts about miracles, little do they know, this miracle is merely a small miracle under a much larger one. Surprisingly, there are still people amazed by the already commonplace mining accidents—this is truly a miracle! Living itself on this magical land is already a miracle!
