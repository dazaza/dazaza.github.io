Nature sun light beautiful collagen weight loss

by naturalsunlight on 2009-08-14 17:12:19

You won't believe this, but I've lost 8 jin (about 17.6 lbs) in the last month just by taking collagen!

Eight jin - that's not a small amount for me. Before, when I tried dieting to lose weight, the most I ever lost was less than five jin. And then I gained it all back... = =…. Because constant dieting is really painful. I need to eat meat, you know! Jiong.

Let's get back to the main topic. Actually, when I started taking collagen, I wasn't even thinking about losing weight.

Isn't it said that collagen can beautify and nourish the skin? A lot of my friends were taking it, so I was thinking of buying some to try. Then coincidentally, my aunt went to Hong Kong and brought back some things for my parents, including a box of collagen. My aunt said collagen is good for beauty and anti-aging, and she specially bought it for my mom. The brand isn't very common in China; it's called Nature’s Sunshine from the U.S. My mom said that at her age, what kind of anti-aging are we talking about? She's already aged… So she gave it to me instead. My mom also said "A beautiful daughter is true beauty"! Jiong~ Anyway, since I wanted to try it, I gladly accepted it.

I did a bit of research on how to take it and looked at the nutrition label...

And I actually found a line on the instructions: "Take 9 capsules before bed with lots of water for fat reduction effects." It was the first time I'd heard that taking collagen could help with weight loss! But the instructions didn't explain why it works. I looked it up online, and apparently high-purity collagen can consume a lot of calories when digested in the body...

It was an accidental discovery, so I thought I'd give it a try, no harm in it if it doesn't work. So I followed the instructions and took 9 capsules before sleeping, then drank a big glass of water.

One funny thing is that those collagen capsules are pretty big and milky white. When I poured 9 of them into my hand, they looked like a bunch of pills someone might take in a TV drama when committing suicide with sleeping pills.

Ah, life is too painful, I might as well find some peace... Haha, just kidding!

Back to the capsules, I have a relatively small throat, so big pills usually get stuck there for a while before going down. Therefore, I had to drink a large sip of water after each capsule. By the time I finished the 9th capsule, I had almost finished drinking the whole glass of water…

I kept doing this for about a month now, which is a little more than one bottle but less than two bottles, and I've really lost weight – 8 jin! It's so amazing, the effect is beyond my expectations. Of course, this has something to do with the recent hot weather decreasing my appetite, but in previous summers, I naturally lost at most 3-4 jin. This time, I lost 8 jin, so I think it must be related to taking collagen. As for changes in my skin, I don’t feel much difference, it seems to have become slightly smoother.

Anyway, regardless of anything else, I'm quite satisfied with the slimming effect! I plan to continue persisting...

By the way, my mom said she saw this Nature’s Sunshine collagen being sold in Huifeng Pharmacy in Shanghai the other day. I'm not sure if other brands of collagen have the same effect; it seems Amway's doesn't... I don't know about the others...

In any case, I'll come back to report after I take it for a while longer!!!
