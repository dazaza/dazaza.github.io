27 Emotional Truths - Which Sentence Made Your Heart Ache?

by lumoza81 on 2009-11-23 10:54:20

1. I thought the big bird couldn't fly across the sea because it lacked the courage to do so. But after ten years, I realized it wasn't that the bird couldn't make it; rather, there was no longer any waiting for it on the other side of the sea...

2. On the day you leave, I decided not to cry, holding back my tears with great effort, forcing myself not to blink...

3. Open your heart less, let me learn to shut up...

4. Carrying a cigarette, wandering to the ends of the earth...

5. The wood said to the fire: "Embrace me"! Water embraced the wood and the wood smiled as it turned into ashes! Water cried, its tears burning itself... When wood loves fire, it is destined to be burned...

6. When tears flow down, only then do you realize leaving is another kind of clarity.

7. I really love you. Closing my eyes, I think I can forget, but the tears streaming down haven't deceived myself...

8. The traveler on the way home cried, tears finally breaking down. Helplessly walking away, I no longer dared to proudly demand anything. What else can I say? What else can I do? I hope you will hear, because hating you drove you away...

9. Don't speak of love lightly, promises made are debts incurred!

10. What's mine will always be mine. I will ultimately be just a passerby in your life. You never loved me. It was destined that nothing would happen between us. No matter how I try to surpass, no matter how I try to get closer to you, you still leave me. I miss you so much, I want to see you so much...

11. The happiness on your face, others can see. But the pain in your heart, who can feel it?

12. After parting, we cannot remain friends because we have hurt each other. We cannot even be acquaintances because we have hated each other. So we became the most familiar strangers.

13. Some encounters between people are like meteors, bursting forth momentarily in dazzling sparks, but are destined to be fleeting.

14. Time will gradually settle, some people will become blurred in your mind. Learn to let go, your happiness needs to be accomplished by yourself.

15. Others can sense your pain, your unspoken helplessness... But when you act as if it doesn't matter, the more you do that, the more I suffer.

16. Sometimes, love means quietly concealing feelings. Avoiding is the shadow, but the hidden emotions cannot be concealed.

17. Only when lost does one realize the value. Many people don't know how to cherish what they possess until it's gone. What's most familiar is often the most precious.

18. Sometimes, love is also a kind of harm. Cruel people choose to hurt others, while kind people choose to hurt themselves.

19. You left, taking all my love with you, with just a word of separation. I held back my tears watching your retreating figure, wanting to hold you one last time, wanting to tell you one last time "I love you."

20. The air after the rain, the wounds grown weary, the fairy tales in my memory are slowly melting away.

21. Since you love, why not express it outright? Some things, once lost, can never be regained!

22. Love is a feeling. When this feeling is no longer there, and yet I continue to force myself, that's called duty! Separation is a kind of courage! When that courage is no longer there, and yet I continue to encourage myself, that's called tragic!

23. Life cannot proceed without loneliness. In loneliness, love has no end, and loneliness is the eternal theme of love. I am alone with my shadow. It says it has something quiet to tell me. It says it misses you very much ^ Originally, both me and my shadow are thinking of you...

24. A tragedy has just unfolded on the perfect shore. All the blood and tears have nurtured a bud among the lush thorns. It will undergo seven cycles of thunderstorms before blooming in the humid air...

25. The fish got hooked because it fell in love with the fisherwoman. It willingly gave up its life for the fisherwoman's smile...

26. Enjoying leaving marks of ownership on your body, but never forgetting that you belong to me...

27. In fact, I've always been waiting by your side, waiting for you to lean on my shoulder and pour out your heart. Will there ever be a day when all your tenderness belongs to me, when I no longer compete for your unhappiness, for your tears to stop flowing!
