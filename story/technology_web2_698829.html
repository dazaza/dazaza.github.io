7 Stylish Men's Fashion Outfit Tips for Different Occasions

by xuezhen5556v on 2012-03-09 11:26:41

Gentlemen of fashion must take a look at the seven occasions for stylish men's clothing, teaching you to be fashionable and graceful all the time.

Occasion One: You are going to attend a night fashion party or an art exhibition opening, or any event that requires wearing formal attire.

Dressing Tip: A pair of velvet fabric loafers, preferably with laces; never show your socks; a black nine-eighths pants or straight-leg pants, the material should absolutely not be pure cotton or reflective fabrics that appear frivolous, nor can it be heavy fabrics, the best choice would be a pair of casual suit fabric pants; the shirt on your upper body must have characteristics, as shown in the picture above, but it absolutely cannot be a white shirt. If you really don't have a special shirt like the one in the picture, you can choose a simple top and then select a pink, round dot, patterned tie or butterfly bow tie.

Occasion Two: Having a Western dinner with a girl you just met.

Dressing Tip: Casual sneakers + harem pants + casual suit + shirt (can also be replaced with a graffiti logo pure cotton T-shirt)

Occasion Three: Your boss is a foreigner, and he asks you to have dinner with his foreign friends over the weekend.

Taobao Men's Wear Tip: Light colors are the most effective in making strangers feel at ease and enhancing the good impression of the first meeting. Therefore, we have prepared a pair of white casual suit pants, a pair of brown shoes, and a very heartwarming pink shirt and tie for you!

Occasion Four: A small social gathering where you want to find her?

Dressing Skill: Jeans can make you look more natural and less serious. If you wear dress pants, it could easily make her feel that you value this social gathering too much, indicating that you are desperate for a partner, which might make her think you're not "in demand." Therefore, everything needs to be confident and high-profile.
