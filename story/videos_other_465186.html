Chapter Eighty Declaration of Love

Shen Xiangyan was startled and suddenly thought of another question. In a hurry, she said, "Brother Gao, there's an error! If people go missing in Moxuan Manor every day, and at least fifty or sixty people have disappeared since the beginning of the year, how come we didn't notice any abnormalities when we entered the manor? Instead, everyone seemed very gentle!" It was hard to believe that every commoner in Moxuan Manor had such high cultivation to remain indifferent in the face of such strange occurrences.

Manager Fang nodded repeatedly, praising, "Miss Shen has observed with great detail, truly impressive. However, this is exactly the second thing I and Young Master are worried about!"

Since returning from Jiang Nan, the nightmare would occur an hour earlier each year—originally at midnight, then at the hour of Hai, Xu, and You. By the start of this year, it gradually approached the hour of Shen. Initially, no one noticed the disappearances until more than half a month ago when a personal servant of Jiang Nan vanished overnight, revealing this enormous隐患.

Manager Fang checked the entire manor's roster and discovered that the number of missing people had reached as many as fifty-eight. Even more perplexing was that all the missing people were completely forgotten! This meant that even if your parents or children went missing, by the next morning, you would completely forget their existence!

Among them, only Jiang Nan and Manager Fang could retain some memory, which explained why the ordinary villagers seemed unaware of these strange changes. And neither Jiang Nan nor Manager Fang dared to speak out for fear of causing greater panic.

"Escape is impossible, hiding is futile. I originally thought we'd just wait to die, but fortunately, Brother Gao has arrived at Moxuan Manor—it truly is heaven-sent!"

Hearing this, Gao Yonghan remembered something and asked curiously, "Does Master Jiang seem to know our whereabouts?"

"Yes," Manager Fang replied, "thanks to tocotech’s attendance system! Early yesterday morning, Young Master informed me that Old Master had appeared in his dream, saying that at three o'clock in the afternoon, esteemed guests would arrive, perhaps relieving Moxuan Manor of its troubles. The names of the three esteemed guests were also revealed to Old Master in the dream."

If they wanted to rely on us three to resolve this nightmare, why not directly inform us, leading to such misunderstanding? Looking at Manager Fang's weary eyes, Gao Yonghan pondered and finally understood, "It seems Master Jiang and Manager Fang believed we, coming from the outside world, wouldn't be affected by the nightmare, and there's no reason to ask for help upon first meeting."

"Hmm," Gao Yonghan nodded and continued, "Could it be that after Master Jiang returned, no cultivators have visited Moxuan Manor?"

"Not just cultivators, not even ordinary passersby."

Upon hearing this, Gao Yonghan had a rough understanding and stopped questioning the details. He then asked, "Then please lead me to the pond, Manager Fang." All sorts of strange events in Moxuan Manor seemed to originate from some "ink pot," so he must see it.

Upon hearing this, Manager Fang realized Gao Yonghan had agreed to help, and couldn’t help but feel a glimmer of hope. Leading the way, they passed through several corridors, circumvented a few courtyards, exited Jiang’s estate through a side door, followed the ink river outside the estate, and arrived at a pond.

Manager Fang pointed and said, "Commander Gao, this is the pond submerged by the ink pot."

Gao Yonghan remained silent, squatted by the riverbank, dipped his hand into the water, stirred it slightly, then gradually brought it to his nose and smelled it. Indeed, there was an extremely strong scent of ink. After secretly testing, he found no magical fluctuations in the water. After a moment of contemplation, he sat cross-legged by the pond, released his divine consciousness, and carefully investigated, but still found nothing.

"Yan'er, does your Dan Ding Sect have any methods to explore strange spiritual forces?"

Shen Xiangyan thought for a moment and replied, "There is one method we can try!"

After the collapse of the nine realms, the then-sect master of the Dan Ding Sect, Dan Lou, was attacked by a group of undead spirits, nearly costing him his life. Since then, the Dan Ding Sect specifically created a spell to detect forces similar to undead or ghostly powers, called "Seeking Breath Spirit Light."

Although Shen Xiangyan's cultivation was far from being able to cast spells at will, she took out a blue stone shaped like an egg. Following the incantations, several blue runes appeared on the surface of the stone, becoming brighter and forming a floating blue light orb. However, instead of bright blue light, several faint blue lines entered the pond.

Soon, the pond water began to boil, large bubbles rising from the bottom and bursting on the surface, churning the ink-like water. Shen Xiangyan's complexion suddenly turned pale, and she let out a groan as her body swayed. Gao Yonghan quickly supported her, and the blue light orb fell back into a stone. Gao Yonghan grabbed the stone and helped Shen Xiangyan retreat ten zhang away.

At this point, the water slowly surged upward, forming a water column over a yard thick, surpassing the surrounding banks. Despite knowing a bit of magic, Manager Fang was startled by the sight and ran back to Gao Yonghan, asking fearfully, "Commander Gao, what is in the pond?"

Gao Yonghan had no time to respond, holding Shen Xiangyan's right hand and transmitting a stream of spiritual energy to check her condition. Thankfully, she was merely exhausted. Leaning softly against Gao Yonghan, she whispered, "Big Brother Gao, there's something strange beneath that pond, incredibly powerful!"

Gao Yonghan paid no heed to the rest, reaching into Shen Xiangyan's bosom to pull out a jade bottle, pouring two pills to feed her. Blushing, Shen Xiangyan shook her head lightly, "Big Brother Gao, it's not this bottle, it's another one!" As she spoke, her voice grew softer and shyer.

Manager Fang's attention was entirely captured by the water column, looking around at the pond while stuttering, "This pond, indeed, is, is strange!"

Glancing at the pond, Gao Yonghan saw the black water column twisting and spiraling upwards into the sky without a single splash of water. Given the situation, there was still some time left. Without further ado, he gritted his teeth and once again reached into Shen Xiangyan's bosom, exploring hastily. Inadvertently, he touched her ample chest, causing Shen Xiangyan to exclaim "Ah!" She closed her eyes in embarrassment, leaving Gao Yonghan equally flustered. Nevertheless, rescuing her was paramount, so he continued searching until he retrieved the jade bottle.

Daring not to open her eyes, Shen Xiangyan instinctively embraced Gao Yonghan, swallowing the two pills. At that moment, her heart was filled with sweetness, wishing she could always lie in Gao Yonghan's arms.

Gao Yonghan had no such thoughts, keeping his gaze firmly on the dark water column as it ascended higher and higher. Puzzlingly, aside from the water column, there were no other anomalies. Yet Gao Yonghan clearly knew this water column wasn't simple. Glancing at the sky, it was barely noon. Would the nightmare erupt at the hour of Shen? If it did, undoubtedly, the upcoming nightmare would be far more perilous than any before.

Realizing this, Gao Yonghan broke out in a cold sweat. He himself might not fear, but what about those hundreds of lives? They surely couldn't endure it. Immediately, he shouted at Manager Fang, who was still dazed by the water column, "Manager Fang, I want you to gather all the villagers within half an hour. You choose the place, but not a single person should be left behind!"

"Ahh, Ahh!" Manager Fang momentarily didn't hear clearly, only realizing after Gao Yonghan repeated himself, then rushed off.

Gao Yonghan looked up at Shen Xiangyan, noticing her complexion had improved significantly. He softly asked, "Yan'er, how are you feeling?"

Hearing this, Shen Xiangyan's face became even rosier. She could almost feel Gao Yonghan's breath near her, gently shaking off his arm, struggling to stand, and quietly replied, "No major issues. I took the pills earlier, and I'm much better now."

"That's good!" Gao Yonghan sighed in relief, continuing, "Yan'er, I need you to protect the commoners of Moxuan Manor. I'll set up a magical array, and you'll oversee it."

Hearing this, Shen Xiangyan was shocked, "Big Brother Gao, are you going to handle this nightmare alone?"

She shook her head vigorously, "No, I won't leave you!"

In Shen Xiangyan's mind, although her Dan Ding Sect techniques couldn't help much, they could at least detect hidden dangers. Plus, fearing the nightmare from last night, she refused to part from Gao Yonghan.

Gao Yonghan anticipated Shen Xiangyan's reaction, pointing at the increasingly towering water column and speaking solemnly, "Yan'er, I don't know what will emerge from beneath this pond. Staying with me would only put you in danger and hinder my full concentration. Moreover, Master Jiang and the others can't protect themselves. I need you to help them resist the nightmare."

Although capricious, Shen Xiangyan wasn't unintelligent. Knowing Gao Yonghan spoke the truth, despite her reluctance, she nodded, "Alright, Face Recognition! Big Brother Gao, be careful. If anything happens to you, I..." Suddenly pausing, her face flushed red again as she firmly declared, "I will accompany you!"

Gao Yonghan was deeply moved. Over these few days, he had somewhat sensed Shen Xiangyan's feelings towards him. However, having already someone named Zisou in his heart, there was no room for more. At this moment, Shen Xiangyan's straightforward confession left him somewhat at a loss, inwardly murmuring, "Yan'er, Yan'er, how am I supposed to bear this?"

He could only affirm, "Nothing will happen. Neither to you, nor to me!"

Regarding Gao Yonghan's slightly disordered reaction, Shen Xiangyan interpreted it differently, believing he was moved by her sincerity. Thus, she happily clung to Gao Yonghan as they walked toward the Jiang residence.

Along the way, they encountered continuously arriving villagers. With the crowd, they reached a vast courtyard. Under Manager Fang's command, despite the large number of seven or eight hundred people, it wasn't too chaotic. Seeing Gao Yonghan and Shen Xiangyan arrive, Manager Fang loudly announced, "Everyone, quiet down! The Master has something to say!"

Jiang Nan, evidently informed by Manager Fang, finally showed a glimmer of hope in his previously despondent eyes. Stepping under the eaves, he loudly addressed the villagers, "Yesterday, esteemed guests came to Moxuan Manor," pointing at Gao Yonghan and Shen Xiangyan, "These two are highly accomplished cultivators here to help us rid ourselves of the nightmare disturbances. Therefore, everyone must follow instructions and not wander around!"

Upon hearing this, the crowd initially paused, their gazes swiftly turning toward the doorway, followed by a "buzzing" sound of conversations. Gao Yonghan pulled Shen Xiangyan and lightly leaped over the heads of the crowd, landing beside Jiang Nan, shouting, "I will set up a magical array here. No matter what you see, do not step out of the array's boundary. As long as you stay inside, I guarantee your safety."

Gao Yonghan slowly scanned the surroundings, seeing expressions of shock, anticipation, confusion, and incomprehension among the villagers. He roared, "After tonight, none of you will suffer from nightmares anymore!"

Chapter Eighty-One Dream

Having spent a long time in the military, Gao Yonghan naturally carried an air of severity. Upon uttering these words, the villagers believed him to be eight-tenths trustworthy. When Gao Yonghan effortlessly dismantled eighteen massive wooden stakes, each requiring three men to embrace, and casually embedded them into the ground, everyone completely trusted that Gao Yonghan could certainly deal with the nightmare that had plagued them for years. Even Jiang Nan's expression became more eager.

After setting up the formation, Gao Yonghan pulled Shen Xiangyan under the largest wooden stake at the edge, his expression extraordinarily solemn, "Yan'er, this stake is the main pivot of the formation. Whatever happens, it must not fall!"

"I understand. As long as I still have a breath left, I will use all my strength to maintain the operation of the formation!" Although she said so, a trace of doubt still flickered in Shen Xiangyan's eyes.

Seeing Shen Xiangyan's robust shoulders, Gao Yonghan suddenly felt a pang of sympathy. This originally innocent and lively young girl had endured life-and-death tests for three consecutive days, truly making things difficult for her. Approaching Shen Xiangyan's ear, he softly said, "Yan'er, whatever happens, you must remain calm. As long as you can preserve the formation and yourself, I will take you to Tian Ji Peak!"

"Really?" A spark of joy flashed in Shen Xiangyan's eyes.

Gao Yonghan knew that his previous words had fully ignited Shen Xiangyan's fighting spirit. Deliberately maintaining a stern face, he scolded, "What nonsense, has Big Brother Gao ever deceived you?"

Shen Xiangyan giggled, sensing the ease emanating from Gao Yonghan's body, and suddenly felt that this wasn't such a daunting task. Feeling the weight of her responsibility, she smiled at Gao Yonghan, "Big Brother Gao, rest assured, I won't let you down!" Finishing, she turned to find Jiang Nan and Manager Fang, guiding the villagers into the formation.

Gao Yonghan felt a wave of relief. Only by constantly experiencing various tribulations could one quickly enhance one's cultivation. Successfully getting through this battle, Yan'er would surely gain some insights.

Suddenly, a cry of alarm came from Shen Xiangyan, "What did you say? That stinky boy who came with us, you didn't bring him over?"

Upon hearing this, Gao Yonghan silently cursed his fate, looking up toward the pond where the water column had already soared into the clouds. It was only three o'clock in the afternoon, yet the sky had completely darkened. In a few steps, he rushed to Shen Xiangyan's side, watching the troubled Manager Fang incessantly explaining, "I originally sent a few servants, but they said the room was empty. The Rong gentleman who came with you has vanished without a trace!"

"Alright, all of you stay here obediently. I'll find Rong Shujun!" Finishing, he patted Shen Xiangyan's shoulder, leapt onto the roof, and dashed toward the wing rooms.

Before Gao Yonghan had gone far, a huge mass of black mist descended. Looking afar, the entire Moxuan Manor was enveloped within it. Without hesitation, he turned back toward the pond. As long as he could shatter the dream, he would certainly retrieve Rong Shujun.

In the blink of an eye, the surrounding scenery drastically changed. Taking Gao Yonghan's location as the core, the surrounding houses began to spin, accelerating faster and faster until they eventually converged into a violent whirlwind
