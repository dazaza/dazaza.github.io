The American First Lady's Preference Boosts Edison Chen's Trendy Shoes Price

Previously, Edison Chen had released some photos on his personal blog showcasing the collaboration between the brand CLOT, which he helped design, and Converse. In the picture, the "main character" was none other than the First Lady of the United States, Michelle Obama. This Weibo post drew a large audience, and observant readers noticed that Michelle Obama in the program was wearing trendy shoes designed by Edison Chen. Subsequently, Chengdu Business Daily reporters found the video of Michelle Obama appearing on the show. She was attending a TV show hosted by American celebrity Ellen DeGeneres, dressed casually and even competing with the host to do push-ups. As everyone knows, as the First Lady of the United States, Michelle Obama's clothing style has always been a trend indicator in America. She was once awarded the "Special Tribute Award" at the CDFA (Council of Fashion Designers of America) Annual Awards, which is known as the "Oscars of Fashion," and she quickly rose to become a fashion icon.

CLOT and Converse Collaborate on CLOT x Converse Chuck Taylor New Model

This collaborative model, the gray version worn by Michelle Obama, was officially priced at 729 yuan. Nowadays, online sellers have inflated its price to over 2000 yuan. This shoe features a stable tone and an understated style, conveying a simple yet clean, fashionable, and harmonious trend concept. Michelle Obama's choice also reflects her low-key and casual personality. Her decision to wear the new shoes produced by Edison Chen clearly shows the American First Lady's recognition of his work in the fashion field.

Michelle Performs Push-Ups and Participates in Marathons, Raising Over 1 Million HKD in Charitable Donations

Apart from fashion, Edison Chen will also make significant moves in charity and music this year, hoping to change his image plagued by past negatives. During the Hong Kong Marathon held the other day, Edison Chen was spotted running. After four months of training, he completed the full marathon course in less than four hours. Mavis, the spokesperson for East Asia Entertainment News, stated that Edison Chen participated in this event sponsored by a sports brand and hoped to raise 2 million HKD through his actions. Mavis mentioned that Edison Chen had previously participated in various competitions in mainland China and the U.S., gaining practical experience. According to yesterday's statistics, the amount raised by Edison Chen has already exceeded 1 million HKD. When asked by Hong Kong media what inspiration he gained from completing the marathon, he responded, "I feel my thoughts have become clearer. I couldn't imagine achieving this before, and I hope young people can strive to pursue their goals."

Notably, to maintain a positive image, Edison Chen made an important decision; it is reported that he started quitting smoking from January 1st this year. His agent Peter said that due to exercise, Edison Chen's smoking cessation has been quite effective, his lung capacity has improved significantly compared to before, and he himself is very happy with this transformation.
