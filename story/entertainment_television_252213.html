Introduction to the episodes 1-41 of TVB's anniversary drama "The Gate of Wealth and Nobility" (English name: Born Rich), also known as "Money Temptation", produced by Alfred Cheung, with scriptwriters Eva Chau, Candy Lau, Michelle Lu, Carman Tsai, and Simon Yip. The drama is set in Hong Kong (TVB) and falls under the genre of modern family dramas. It has a total of 41 episodes and first aired on October 19, 2009 at 9:30 PM on Jade and HD Jade channels.

Cast includes: Louis Koo, Kenneth Ma, Bosco Wong, Maggie Cheung, Gigi Lai, Miki Tsui, Maria Cordero, Patrick Tse, Michael Tong, Marco Ng, Esther Kwan, and Lawrence Wong.

"The Gate of Wealth and Nobility" is TVB's 2009 anniversary drama, a highly anticipated series about wealthy family business warfare. Starring Louis Koo, Kenneth Ma, Bosco Wong, Maggie Cheung, Miki Tsui, and Gigi Lai among others.

In the drama, Louis Koo plays the heir to a wealthy family, while Kenneth Ma takes on a new role as the "bad guy." Louis Koo commended Kenneth Ma for his convincing performance as the villain, which is something for the audience to look forward to. Gigi Lai also changes her usual strong-willed woman image, portraying a "third party" character who uses every trick in the book, leading to many confrontational scenes with Miki Tsui, making it one of the highlights of the drama.
