Mother dies after using her body to shield daughter from collapsing building

by dtr452ugyi on 2012-02-29 13:50:46

Zhuoma introduced that when the doctor took the girl back for emergency treatment, they found that the girl just had a little wound on her head and there was no problem with other parts of her body. After investigation, the medical staff learned that the father of the girl also died in the earthquake and she became an orphan. Some people proposed that as the hospital conditions were so bad that even adults had to endure cold and hunger, it would be hard to ensure the safe growth of the baby. But the strong vitality of the baby finally made all the medical staff decide that they would beg if necessary but would not let the baby starve. The medical staff of the hospital introduced that at 12:00 noon on April 14th, several medical staff followed the Yushu police to rescue people in a local guesthouse. They dug out a young female corpse - she arched her whole body, with two hands spread, as if protecting something desperately. When the police pulled her out of the debris, they found that a small hand was tightly grabbing her. There was a baby girl under the corpse and the girl was still alive. The baby girl was less than one year old and her eyes were full of fear. At this time, people realized that they were a mother and daughter. When the earthquake happened, the young mother protected the baby by hugging her tightly and using her body to block the collapsing building. The mother died on the spot and saved her daughter's life with her blood. The police held the baby in their arms. The baby cried loudly and grabbed her mother's bloody clothes with her hands. The miserable scene made everyone present burst into tears. (According to Southern Metropolitan Newspaper) In the morning of April 16th, when the reporter came to the Yushu People's Hospital, the baby girl was lying in the arms of Zhuoma, a guide doctor. The little guy opened her bright big eyes. The medical staff said that her favorite posture was to deeply bury her head in the other party's arms and tightly hug the other party with her hands. It was hard for the baby girl to have food. As the baby was very young, the scarce instant noodles could not be her food. The baby needed milk powder, but there was not enough milk. Zhuoma had to invent a simple method. She first heated the milk with warm water and then added a little hot water. After the milk was diluted, the quantity naturally increased. The little guy did not mind and drank it in large gulps. "Her mother gave her life and exchanged her own life for her rebirth. Finally, she entrusted her to us by chance. We cannot let her starve!" Zhuoma said. According to the Times comprehensive message, in the Yushu disaster area, touching stories were constantly spreading: when the earthquake happened, a young mother hugged her less-than-one-year-old daughter tightly and used her body to block the collapsing building.
