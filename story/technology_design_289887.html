Log in to Taobao, there are countless small shops selling Korean-style clothing, and I believe everyone has been dazzled by them long ago. Today, the fashion chapter of the series "Mastering Taobao Small Shops" will lead you into "Happy Clothing Shop", because it is truly 100% Korean style.

Compared with the Blue Diamond and Huangguan online shops introduced in the previous issues, Happy Clothing Shop (http://shop58557207.taobao.com/) seems very ordinary, as it has only been open for more than three months and is still in the early stage of publicity and promotion. However, although it is relatively inexperienced, with a wide range of Korean-style down jackets, honest and enthusiastic service, this small shop has gradually gained a reputation among Taobao users. Currently, the number of daily logins to Happy Clothing Shop is increasing.

From the hundreds of product descriptions in Happy Clothing Shop, we can see that this shop can purchase clothes from more than 20 Korean brands on behalf of customers, almost covering all styles of mainstream Korean brands. According to the owner "Xiaozhi Wang Laowu", the shop has dedicated personnel regularly responsible for purchasing in Korea, and all products in the shop are genuine Korean products. All pictures in the shop are provided by the official Korean websites. Recently, "Japanese style" clothing has also been added, which is also very good! (Taobao friend review)

Among tens of thousands of fashionable online shops on Taobao, counterfeit products have always been an important means for shop owners to make profits, and integrity issues have become the hardest obstacle to constrain online shopping. Even if Taobao users choose shops with very high credit ratings, such as Blue Diamond shops, they cannot avoid accidents of being taken advantage of by large shops. In order to ensure long-term operation and brand development, Happy Clothing Shop spares no expense to obtain first-hand genuine resources, avoiding possible quality problems in secondary procurement.
