Appliances sales in the countryside exceed 50 billion yuan in the first ten months

by b32938329 on 2009-11-22 22:19:31

From November 18 to 19, a national rural home appliance promotion program experience exchange meeting of the industry and information technology system was held in Wuhan. Vice Minister of Industry and Information Technology Lou Qinjian said that the next step would be to vigorously conduct information technology training and application popularization work in rural areas, organize and promote the combination of "information下乡", "broadband下乡" and "rural home appliance promotion program", play the informatization function of electronic products such as computers, mobile phones, color TVs, etc., and promote the construction of a new socialist countryside. According to statistics from relevant departments, as of October 31, a total of 27.828 million units of rural home appliances promotion products were sold nationwide this year, with sales reaching 50.84 billion yuan. Among them, tickets and refrigerators have the largest sales volume and the highest sales in the rural home appliances promotion program, with a total of 13.67 million units sold and sales reaching 27.92 billion yuan. The sales volumes of air conditioners, color TVs, and washing machines are 2.43 million units, 5.79 million units, and 3.41 million units respectively, with sales reaching 6.45 billion yuan, 8.29 billion yuan, and 3.75 billion yuan respectively. The sales of rural home appliances promotion products in all provinces (regions, municipalities) have steadily increased. Henan, Shandong, and Sichuan provinces started the rural home appliances promotion program earlier and had relatively mature markets, ranking among the top in the country. The sales of these three provinces accounted for 39% of the national sales.
