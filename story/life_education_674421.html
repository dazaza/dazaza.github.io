During the case handling process, Wang and his family visited the county prosecutor's office many times, requesting that he be given a non-prosecution treatment. At around 10 o'clock on February 22nd, Wang Xiangcun, Wang Junsheng's sister, and Wang Junsheng himself successively went to the prosecutor's office, demanding that a non-prosecution decision be made on the same day. At 11:15 AM, the staff of the prosecutor's office heard unusual sounds from the second floor hall while doing ideological work with Wang Xiangcun, and immediately came out to check. They found Wang Junsheng, who had just arrived, lying on his side on the ground, holding a fruit knife in his right hand, with blood on his abdomen. They then called 120 and sent Wang Junsheng to the hospital for treatment. The diagnosis certificate issued by Fangcheng County People's Hospital showed: "A horizontal wound of about 2.0 cm can be seen on the upper right abdomen, with neat edges, deep into the abdominal cavity, slightly bleeding." After checking the surveillance video inside the court, it was determined that Wang Junsheng injured himself, not as rumored, attempted suicide by disembowelment.

On February 22nd and 23rd, 2012, several domestic online media outlets revealed and reposted claims that "a police officer in Fangcheng committed suicide by disembowelment at the prosecutor's office," which drew high attention from leaders of units such as the Nanyang Municipal Party Committee Political and Legal Affairs Commission and the Nanyang Municipal People's Procuratorate. Relevant leaders personally went to Fangcheng to investigate the truth of the matter. Reporters also rushed to Fangcheng in the first time to understand the facts.

The party involved indeed self-harmed inside the prosecutor's office, with no life-threatening danger and is currently receiving treatment.

At around 9 o'clock in the morning on the 23rd, reporters arrived at Fangcheng County People's Hospital, where they met Wang Junsheng, the party involved in the case, who was also the person reported online to have committed suicide by disembowelment at the prosecutor's office, in an ordinary ward on the fourth floor.

Chief physician Xu Xiaowei told reporters that the patient was brought to the hospital by the 120 emergency vehicle at 12:40 PM on the 22nd. The accompanying personnel from the prosecutor's office claimed that Wang Junsheng had previously stabbed himself in the abdomen with a fruit knife. After examination, the diagnosis was "flat abdomen, a horizontal wound of about 2.0 cm can be seen on the upper right abdomen, with neat edges, deep into the abdominal cavity, slightly bleeding, tenderness in the upper abdomen."

Party’s own account: Suicide unrelated to reporting village cadres' embezzlement

Wang Junsheng told reporters that the case originated from a fight between neighbors ten years ago. He himself was not present at the scene at the time; he arrived later and did not participate in the fight. After the incident, some parties were criminally detained or sentenced. Later, after coordination by the Fangcheng County Political and Legal Affairs Commission, both sides signed an agreement, compensation was in place, and both sides stated that they would not appeal further and would not hold each other accountable. But this time, the prosecutor's office re-investigated the matter, making him feel wronged. If he were really prosecuted and sentenced, he would lose his status as a public official.

When asked if this self-harm incident was related to reporting the corruption of the village secretary, Wang Junsheng replied: "It is not related."

Background: Wang Junsheng is 39 years old and hails from Houlin Village, Erlang Temple Township, Fangcheng County. He served in a certain unit and was transferred to the Fangcheng County Public Security Bureau after his military service.

Wang Junsheng told reporters that due to the previous case, he was detained for 12 days. Later, the prosecutor's office neither prosecuted nor gave a conclusion, preventing him from returning to work at the Public Security Bureau. Under the coordination of relevant departments, Wang's family reached an agreement with the other party they accused, agreeing not to sue each other and to stop petitioning. However, the other party soon broke the promise, leading the victims of the past fight to file complaints everywhere. In this situation, Wang's father passed away last year, his mother received multiple critical illness notices, and Wang divorced his wife, saying "I couldn't go on living anymore."

Fangcheng County Prosecutor's Office Chief Liang Zhimin said to reporters that after the so-called old case mentioned by Wang Junsheng happened, both sides indeed reconciled. But this case was a serious injury case, and the prosecution cannot be stopped just because the parties reconciled. By 2009, the villagers of Houlin Village reported revelations, and the Public Security Bureau handed over this case to the procuratorial organs for handling. Currently, the case is in the stage of review and prosecution. The reason why there has been no prosecution is because his relatives have repeatedly reflected strongly, requesting that the prosecutor's office not prosecute in order to preserve Wang's identity as a policeman. "We have studied this issue many times and are handling it cautiously."
