Small Knowledge of Men's Ties and Wearing Details

by shenzhen129r on 2012-03-07 10:56:09

As an ancient traditional product, ties have always been the annotation of prestigious status. A finely crafted tie with a soft texture is a classic accessory for every man. Therefore, men should learn about the small knowledge and wearing details of men's ties.

The patterns of ties are the only place in men's fashion that can change and reflect individuality, so the selection of tie patterns is very important and the characteristic of personality is also very obvious. Everyone has their own preferences and partialities. The author will only introduce the basic principles here.

In terms of color, ties can be divided into single color and multiple colors. Single-colored ties are suitable for official activities and grand social occasions, and blue, gray, black, brown, white, and purple-red are the most popular. Multi-colored ties generally should not exceed three colors and can be used in all kinds of occasions. Ties with overly bright colors are not widely applicable and should only be used in informal social gatherings or leisure time to avoid criticism.

If it is a patterned tie used in formal occasions, its pattern should be regular and traditional. The most common ones are diagonal stripes, horizontal stripes, vertical stripes, polka dots, checkers, and regular floral patterns, which often have certain meanings. Ties printed with characters, animals, plants, flowers, houses, landscapes, and strange mysterious patterns are only suitable for informal occasions. Ties printed with advertisements, group logos, or family crests should not be used indiscriminately.

From the perspective of this article's readers, students who have just entered the company rarely become supervisors right away; mostly they hold lower-level positions such as employees or assistants. People in these positions should avoid being too showy and should not let everyone in the company see them wearing a striking tie that outshines their boss. Therefore, dressing low-key is a principle that needs to be followed basically because outstanding performance at work should not depend on clothing, but on one's work achievements. So the choice of ties should be cautious. It is better not to choose bright red, eye-catching yellow, or silver-white, but rather darker colors like blue or black. If you want to show some youthful color, pink is also a good choice.

Besides, the patterns of men's wear on Taobao are also quite particular. For example, when wearing a striped shirt, avoid pairing it with a striped tie; when wearing a solid-colored shirt, it is best to pair it with a patterned tie, etc. This will be introduced in the subsequent chapter on clothing coordination.

The craftsmanship of the tie:

1. A standard tie is made up of three sections spliced together, with a total length of 145 cm.

2. The surface is smooth, the pattern is clear and symmetrical.

3. The back seam is vertically centered.

4. The consistency of the patterns at the splicing places.

5. Whether the color difference of the fabric of the whole short strap is consistent.
