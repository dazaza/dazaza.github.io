IT, how far is it from us

by pollypuxs on 2010-04-13 17:30:48

10 years ago, IT was a mysterious high technology to us. But 10 years later, we are all too familiar with IT because it has been integrated into our daily lives. IT is the abbreviation of Information Technology, which refers to the technologies in modern computer, network, communication and other information fields. The extensive application of IT marks the entry into an information-based society.

According to statistics, Chinese people spend on average 7 hours per day in contact with media. Computers are becoming increasingly closely related to our lives, leading many to ask: "Without computers, how would our lives, studies, and work continue?"

With the popularization of computers, jobs related to them have also become increasingly valued and admired. New professions such as network engineers have appeared in various recruitment fairs, and universities have also opened up disciplines such as information engineering and computer applications. The veil of mystery around computers has gradually been lifted. Its users are no longer limited to highly educated intellectuals in university classrooms; instead, like everyday commodities, they have entered ordinary households and become an indispensable part of our lives. The importance placed on IT talent is directly related to the demands of the government, enterprises, and society. It is reported that according to the government's plan, it is expected to cultivate and introduce over 50,000 network-related talents before 2010. However, currently, there are less than 10,000 professionals engaged in basic network research and development. Meanwhile, industry experts have also stated that the network industry urgently needs three types of high-level talents: network engineers, technical support engineers, and network administrators. Among them, the demand for network engineers remains consistently high, with salaries ranging from 3,000 yuan to 6,000 yuan.

It is precisely due to this demand that Peking University's青鸟 (Beidaqingniao) took the lead in starting specialized network training in China. Beidaqingniao helps students accumulate specific and practical project experience, completely摆脱ing the embarrassment of "talking strategy on paper." Nearly ten thousand students have realized their dreams of high-paying IT careers.

Career planning expert comments: IT life has transitioned from being distant to close, from mysterious to common, all due to the development of IT education and the resulting popularization of IT knowledge. If you still know nothing about IT now, it's definitely time to recharge your skills; otherwise, you will be eliminated by society.
