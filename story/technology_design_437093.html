Tongliang Centipede Breeding Technology, Wanzhou's Most Professional Centipede Breeding Base, Agricultural Science and Technology for the Benefit of People

by dfqfewrt16 on 2011-05-25 18:08:44

Advanced Technology Advantage: Wuhan Nongke Humin Biological Engineering Co., Ltd., in collaboration with several biological experts and professors of bioengineering from Huazhong Agricultural University, has cultivated a new generation breed called Humin No. 2 Golden Head Centipede through years of observation on breeding and the use of optimal purification technology means and biological genetic improvement engineering. After breeding observation, this breed is simple to raise, gentle in nature, strong in adaptability, fast-growing, highly reproductive, better in medicinal value, and more significant in economic value, making it an ideal investment breeding project for friends in the breeding industry.

Breeding Facilities: The company takes agricultural science as its advantage and life science as its characteristic, relying on experts and bachelors from the College of Animal Science and Technology. From the domestic market perspective, in recent years, with the rapid development of the traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) industry, the TCM market has become increasingly prosperous. Moreover, due to people's renewed trust in TCM and the great development of Chinese patent medicine research, there is a situation of insufficient supply of precious TCMs. Tongliang centipede breeding technology sees its market price constantly rising. In the early 1980s, the market purchase price of centipedes was only about 4 jiao per piece, but now, it has risen to 2 yuan, and still, there is a severe shortage of supply. The market supply of centipede venom is even more inadequate.

The economic benefits of breeding No. 2 scorpion centipedes are significant. While providing valuable TCMs to society and benefiting humanity, those who breed centipedes can also gain high economic benefits. As a valuable medicine, centipedes have a large market demand. Artificial centipede breeding has gradually become a promising cause.

Huazhong Agricultural University Support: A key university under the national "211 Project" – Huazhong Agricultural University collaborates with Wuhan Nongke Humin Biological Engineering Co., Ltd., to jointly study and be committed to scientific farming promotion and technological enrichment of farmers in centipede breeding technology: Humin No. 2 Golden Head Centipede.

Modern bioengineering, special breeding, and insect resource development form the core of our high-tech enterprise that integrates research, reproduction, teaching, promotion, technical services, production, sales, import and export trade. We possess large-scale ecological breeding areas, solar house breeding areas, greenhouse breeding areas, etc., and have set up technical training centers, seedling breeding centers, germplasm rooms, incubation rooms, gene transformation rooms, etc. Our foundation is excellent, and our system is complete.

Wuhan Nongke Humin Company: Upholding the tradition of "diligent reading and hard farming, self-establishment and helping others," our company takes "scientific farming promotion" as its purpose and adopts the business philosophy of "integrity, dedication, realism, innovation, and win-win" to promote the industrial development of centipede resources. We will be dedicated to providing our fellow farmers with higher-quality, high-yield seedlings, more advanced technology, and more perfect services, allowing us to keep pace with the times and jointly create a more brilliant tomorrow.

From the international market perspective, with the further deepening of China's reform and opening-up, Chinese TCM culture is rapidly spreading worldwide. Many countries, especially Japan and Southeast Asian countries, are importing increasing quantities of centipedes from China every year. On the other hand, centipede venom is very popular in the international pharmaceutical market, with each ounce selling for thousands of dollars.
