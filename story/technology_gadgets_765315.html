Lumia Phablet Coming Soon

by geekzhang on 2013-08-11 09:26:26

According to the news from The Verge, Microsoft engineers are currently testing WP8 GDR3. Judging from the information exposed so far, its most impressive part is that it will support larger screen sizes and higher resolutions, as well as quad-core processors.

Of course, WP8 GDR3 is even more significant for Nokia. Senior editor of The Verge, Tom Warren, revealed on Twitter that Nokia is also developing a phablet, and WP8 GDR3 has been specifically optimized for it.
