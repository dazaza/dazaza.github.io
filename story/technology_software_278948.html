Champions League - Juventus 0-2 Bordeaux, Must Beat Bayern Munich to Advance

At 0:00 AM Beijing Time on November 26, in the fifth round of Group A matches in the UEFA Champions League, Bordeaux faced Juventus at the Delmas Stadium. With goals from Fernando Menegazzo and Chammak, Bordeaux defeated Juventus 2-0...

At 0:00 AM Beijing Time on November 26, in a key battle of the fifth round in Group A of the UEFA Champions League, Barcelona officially announced the renewal of their full-back's contract until 2012 with a breach clause of 90 million euros, as Bordeaux faced Juventus at the Delmas Stadium. With goals from Fernando Menegazzo and Chammak, Bordeaux defeated Juventus 2-0. After five rounds, Bordeaux accumulated 13 points (4 wins and 1 draw), securing the top spot in the group. Juventus had 8 points (2 wins, 2 draws, and 1 loss). Due to Bayern Munich defeating Hapoel Haifa in this round, the German giant reached 7 points. Juventus will compete with Bayern Munich in the last round for an advancement spot.

After four rounds, Bordeaux ranked first with 3 wins and 1 draw, while Juventus closely followed with 2 wins and 2 draws. Since Bordeaux had already secured their place in the next round, they were able to play more relaxed. If Juventus could defeat Bordeaux away, they would secure their place in the next round one match earlier. If they drew or lost, they would have to wait for the results of Bayern Munich’s match. In the first leg, Juventus was held to a 1-1 draw by Bordeaux at home, and in previous European competition history, the two teams had met twice, each winning once.

Juventus lined up in a 4-4-2 formation, with Amauri and Del Piero partnering in attack. On the flanks were Camoranesi and Diego, with Sissoko and Melo forming a double pivot in midfield. In defense, Legrottaglie and Chiellini played centrally, Grosso and Cáceres on the left and right respectively, and Buffon remained in goal.

In the third minute, Bordeaux got their first chance from a corner kick. Wendel delivered the ball, and Diarra's header was blocked by a Juventus defender. Subsequently, Menegazzo took another shot from the right side of the box but missed wide. In the seventh minute, Legrottaglie fouled Wendel, giving Bordeaux a free-kick opportunity on the left flank. Wendel then directly shot the ball, which went wide of the right post. In the ninth minute, Chamal made a cross from the wing, but Caramak failed to hit the target properly.

In the eleventh minute, Tremoulinas crossed from the left flank, and Plasil's shot from the left side of the penalty area narrowly missed the near post. One minute later, Tremoulinas sent a through pass, but Plasil was offside. A minute after that, Juventus had a free-kick opportunity, Diego crossed the ball, and Chiellini's header was extremely dangerous but hit just above the crossbar. In the twenty-second minute, Menegazzo headed a corner kick but missed wide.

In the twenty-ninth minute, Plasil's central shot posed no great threat and was easily dealt with by Buffon. A minute later, Del Piero passed the ball into the penalty area, and Diego's shot was bravely saved by Carrasso. In the thirty-third minute, Camoranesi fouled Tremoulinas and received the first yellow card of the match. In the thirty-eighth minute, Chamal made a direct pass on the right flank, and Chammak had a golden opportunity to score, but his shot was brilliantly saved by Buffon. In the forty-first minute, Gouffran's long-range shot was caught by Buffon. In the forty-fifth minute, Melo received a yellow card. At halftime, both sides remained goalless.

In the second half, neither team made any substitutions. In the fifty-first minute, Legrottaglie received a yellow card, and subsequently, Planus' free-kick was blocked by Juventus players. In the fifty-fourth minute, Bordeaux was awarded a free-kick opportunity, Plasil delivered the ball into the small penalty area, and Fernando Menegazzo jumped up to head the ball into the bottom right corner of the net, giving Bordeaux a 1-0 lead over Juventus.

In the fifty-seventh minute, Melo's long-range shot with his right foot missed high. A minute later, Diego received a pass from Sissoko and attempted a long-range shot, which was collected by Carrasso. In the sixty-third minute, Tremoulinas made a direct pass on the left flank, and Wendel's shot from the edge of the penalty area slightly missed high. In the sixty-eighth minute, Juventus substituted Del Piero with Immobile. A minute later, Diego's corner kick found Legrottaglie, whose header missed wide. In the seventy-fifth minute, Bordeaux substituted Gouffran with Traoré.

In the seventy-seventh minute, Juventus substituted Amauri with Giaccherini. In the eighty-fourth minute, Diego's free-kick attempt narrowly missed the crossbar. In the eighty-ninth minute, Juventus substituted Sissoko with Marchisio. In the ninety-second minute, Wendel delivered a corner kick into the small penalty area, and Chammak scored with a header from four meters out in front of the goal, directing the ball into the bottom right corner of the net. Ultimately, Juventus lost 0-2 to Bordeaux, who secured the top spot in the group. Meanwhile, due to Bayern Munich defeating Hapoel Haifa, Juventus' lead over Bayern was reduced to only one point, and both teams will contest for an advancement spot in the final round.

Starting lineups:

Bordeaux (4-2-3-1): 1-Carrasso / 21-Chamal, 2-Ciani, 27-Planus, 28-Tremoulinas / 5-Menegazzo, 4-Diarra / 7-Gouffran (75', 24-Traoré), 18-Plasil, 17-Wendel / 29-Chammak

Juventus (4-4-2): 1-Buffon / 2-Cáceres, 33-Legrottaglie, 3-Chiellini, 6-Grosso / 16-Camoranesi, 4-Melo, 22-Sissoko (89', 8-Marchisio), 28-Diego / 11-Amauri (77', 20-Giaccherini), 10-Del Piero (68', 40-Immobile)
