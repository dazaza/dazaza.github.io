The forced smile from a moment ago can no longer be maintained.

by nikaiomi on 2012-02-20 00:19:45

She talked to the teacher about the accommodation issue, and the teacher was quite surprised but still agreed and quickly arranged a bed for her. However, when she was about to get off, the teacher told her that someone was looking for her. Bai Xinyu's heart skipped a beat. Who could be looking for her? She had a bad feeling!

When she arrived at the school reception room, she saw Hua Yongqi sitting there. The Hua family was also a prominent family in Qingyang City. The Hua family had donated to many schools in Qingyang City, and the new teaching building was co-donated by the Hua family and the Li family. At the time of the opening ceremony, both the Hua family and the Li family attended, and she remembered that Hua Yongqi was there too. The teacher naturally recognized this young lady from the Hua family, and as soon as she came, she was invited into the reception room. Seeing her, Hua Yongqi politely said to the teacher who was hosting her: "Teacher Zuo, I would like to have a private conversation with Bai Xinyu."

Teacher Zuo smiled, nodded, and then took a deep look at Bai Xinyu.

After the teacher left, Hua Yongqi said embarrassedly: "I originally just wanted to talk to you, but I didn't expect it to turn out like this, sorry."

Did she mean disturbing the teacher? Bai Xinyu bitterly laughed in her heart and said: "It's okay, what does Miss Hua want?"

Hua Yongqi wasn't in good spirits, her face was pale, and the forced smile she managed earlier couldn't be maintained anymore. She asked: "Was it really you with Mu Yang last night?"

If possible, Bai Xinyu hoped she would never have to mention this incident again. How should she respond to such an embarrassing memory?

Hua Yongqi found her answer on her face, her already pale face became even more colorless. She asked painfully: "Why were you in Mu Yang's room? Why did this happen to me?" She was still holding onto hope, up until now, she still refused to accept what happened last night.

"I also want to know the answer," Bai Xinyu said awkwardly.

Hua Yongqi didn't get the answers she wanted from her, and she helplessly sat back on the sofa. She tightened the hem of her skirt, and tears fell drop by drop.

Seeing her cry, Bai Xinyu remained silent. She wanted to cry more than Hua Yongqi did. Such an event happening would make people sympathize with Hua Yongqi, and her tears would evoke pity. On the contrary, her own tears were worth nothing.

"Was it you?" She couldn't bring herself to ask if Bai Xinyu pushed her into that room, because that would expose her secret to everyone, and she couldn't bear that fact.

Bai Xinyu was also confused about why Hua Yongqi hesitated to speak. It was normal for her to be sad; her boyfriend had relations with a housemaid, something no one could easily accept. But Bai Xinyu couldn't console her, because she herself was also a victim.

She thought everything would end here, but just a few days later, Li Mu Yang came looking for her. He directly rushed to the door of her classroom and loudly called out regardless of the ongoing class: "Bai Xinyu, come out."
