As is known to all, babies are very cute and popular. Moreover, for couples without children, babies can be said to be a "fatal" temptation. Many couples want a child after marriage, and after hospital examination, there is no disease of infertility. So how can they increase the chances of pregnancy? Some people who understand women's medical knowledge know that having sex during ovulation can increase the chances of conception. So, how to calculate the ovulation period? How do you know if a woman is in her ovulation period? During ovulation, women may experience some symptoms: 1. Decreased body resistance. 2. Strong sexual desire, greater longing for intercourse. 3. Loss of appetite, smaller food intake, most women will have this situation. 4. High energy, during the ovulation period, women's instinct makes them like to show themselves more. In addition, the ovulation period can also be calculated by various different methods: Measure basal body temperature. Women's basal body temperature changes periodically. The rise in basal body temperature after ovulation can indicate that ovulation has occurred. Ovulation usually occurs in the process of rising from low to high before the basal body temperature rises. Within three days when the basal body temperature is at a high level, it is the "easy pregnancy stage". But this method can only suggest that ovulation has occurred, not when it will occur. It should be noted that: Basal body temperature measurement must be done after 6 hours of adequate sleep, wake up and measure the temperature and record it before doing any activity. Any special circumstances may affect the change of basal body temperature, so they need to be recorded, such as sexual life the night before, recent colds, etc. Repeated tests are needed and the table points are connected. Normally, the body temperature rises 0.3-0.5℃ after ovulation, called biphasic body temperature. If there is no ovulation, the body temperature does not rise, the whole cycle presents a low flat body temperature, called monophasic body temperature. If menstruation is irregular or life is irregular, such as night shift, business trip, insomnia, mood changes, illness, etc., this method cannot be used to judge whether there is ovulation. Observe cervical mucus. By observing cervical mucus, we can know the "wet period" and "easy pregnancy period". Compared with normal, the mucus increases in quantity and becomes thin, which is the "wet period"; when women approach the ovulation period, the secretion of mucus becomes cool, lubricated and elastic, similar to egg white in shape, with high drawability and not easy to break. This kind of mucus appears on the last day ± 48 hours is the ovulation day, therefore, when the sense of wetness in the vulva appears, it is the ovulation period, also known as the "easy pregnancy period". Doctors suggest that if couples plan to conceive, they should choose the "wet period" before the ovulation period. Self-test with ovulation test paper. Ovulation test paper predicts ovulation by detecting the peak level of luteinizing hormone (LH). 24~48 hours before female ovulation, the luteinizing hormone (LH) in urine will reach a peak value, and self-test with ovulation test paper will show positive results. Specific testing methods are generally described on the packaging of ovulation test paper (must strictly follow the instructions to minimize errors). Different from early pregnancy test paper, morning urine cannot be used, and try to use the same time of urine sample every day. Reduce water intake 2 hours before collecting urine, because diluted urine will also hinder the detection of LH peak. For those with relatively regular menstrual cycles, it should be 14 days before the period (that is, the expected ovulation time), and continuous six-day measurements should be made in the three days before and after this time; if the menstrual cycle is irregular or abnormal, then generally start measuring on the third day after the period is clean. Until the two bars on the test paper are as deep or the second bar is deeper than the first bar, it means that ovulation will occur within 24~48 hours, other test results can refer to the diagram in the instructions. It should be reminded that this is the physiological phenomenon of standard ovulation. Although the test paper is convenient to use, due to the reasons of the production process and the testers themselves, the accuracy rate is about 75%, how to prevent sperm malformation. In addition, the test results are not simply "yes" or "no". Some people may have strong positives for several consecutive days, which may lead to ovulation (usually after the last day of strong positivity), or there may be no ovulation at all. Ultrasonic monitoring method. Through B-ultrasound examination, experienced doctors will see the process of follicles growing from small to large. This method should be the most accurate one. White discharge observation method. In a menstrual cycle, white discharge is not always the same. Most of the time, white discharge is relatively dry, thick, and less. On the day between the two periods, white discharge is clear, bright, and abundant, like egg whites, more like watery nasal mucus when having a cold. This day is the ovulation period. This is due to the high concentration of estrogen produced during ovulation, which acts on the columnar epithelial cells at the cervical os, making them secrete a lot of white discharge.
