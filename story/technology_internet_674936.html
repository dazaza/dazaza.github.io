### Interior Decoration Design Starting from the Carpet

#### Editor: French Auchan Carpets [http://www.asditan.com/]

Website: www.asditan.com [http://www.asditan.com/]

---

#### Article Summary:

Interior design aims to create a good indoor space environment. The carpet factory [http://www.asditan.com/] prioritizes meeting people's needs for production, life, work, and rest indoors. Therefore, several aspects must be fully considered during interior design.

---

1. **Decoration Has No Formula**

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

The designer is the first person the client trusts. When designing, the designer must understand that it is the client's home, not the designer's home. True originality requires you to get closer to nature and focus on sketching. The only reference for decoration design is the suitability of the space layout and the identity and taste of the residents. The perfect fusion of these two elements is enough to become [good decoration], and the home atmosphere will naturally follow.

2. **Design Needs a Soul**

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

If an indoor space does not match the resident's identity and aesthetic preferences but instead abstractly transplants many concepts into the details of the layout and decoration, it may appear fashionable or luxurious, yet lacks soul and charm. How can the resident's body and mind be liberated in this "shell"? Therefore, the status of decoration in overall interior design is increasingly prominent. Its importance not only requires designers to have overall expertise but also highlights the aesthetic taste and lifestyle of the residents, making it increasingly indispensable.

3. **Design Should Be Inclusive**

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

Elements of decorative design include shapes (points, lines, surfaces), color coordination, and the compatibility of various decorative materials themselves. Overall interior decoration design must start from the whole, determining the tone of the decoration based on the basic direction. Whether it's the wallpaper for the entire room or the tablecloth on the dining table, there must be inclusiveness between them. It is precisely these seemingly unrelated decorations that together build the overall mood of the living space.

4. **Let Decorative Materials Unify and Complement Each Other**

The physical properties of decorative materials, including carpets, convey different design languages and express different emotions. Different materials influence people and decorate the spatial environment in different ways, creating distinct decorative cultures.

5. **Design Serves People**

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

The ultimate purpose of design is to serve people. Of course, we often encounter many excellent design styles or trendy styles, but we cannot blindly copy them. What is beautiful in one environment may not necessarily remain beautiful in another. Designers must propose different design plans based on different clients and should not impose their personal preferences on the clients.

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

Any type of decorative design should be implementable. While design advocates boldness, innovation, and uniqueness, the feasibility of the design must be considered during the process, such as lighting effects, climate reasons, and specific construction conditions.
