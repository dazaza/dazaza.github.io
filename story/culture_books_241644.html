The Unlucky Girl's Grand Slam Love: Countdown to Heartbreak, Author: Xia Xuan

All along, any boyfriend who had a relationship with her would be plagued with bad luck, forcing her to count down the days of each romance. But this time, she actually met someone fearless. Being with her didn't result in a flat tire or getting knocked unconscious by a falling light fixture and ending up in the hospital; yet he remained persistent, even proposing to her in the hospital. She wasn't about to let go of such a good man...

Countdown to Heartbreak Part 1

"You're saying what? The boss of your company has run away?!" Cheng Wanxuan was shocked as she stared at He Wenzhuo, her boyfriend of nearly three months. For a moment, she thought her ears were playing tricks on her, that she had misheard him. "Sigh, this was announced by the boss's secretary this afternoon. It seems the boss hasn't been to the company for three days and can't be reached. No one knows where he's gone!" He Wenzhuo stood on the street, helplessly watching the vehicles pass by. Hearing his words, Cheng Wanxuan still couldn’t believe it and shook her head. "How could this happen so suddenly? There were no signs at all!"

Countdown to Heartbreak Part 2

As he heard more exaggerated stories, Jiang Muchong couldn't help but roll his eyes. "Even if someone had to die, it wouldn't be you." "That's right!" Li Weishao nodded vigorously, "Indeed, it wouldn't be me, but rather you! Haha, you even said you have no interest in her." "I didn't say I have absolutely no interest in her, I just... well, never mind, say whatever you want!" Jiang Muchong didn't feel like arguing with him, lest it make things worse.

Countdown to Heartbreak Part 3

After the accident involving the broken necklace, Cheng Wanxuan lost all interest in continuing to enjoy the world jewelry brand exhibition. She even felt she couldn't stay another minute. Seeing her so sad, Jiang Muchong took her out of the venue. Once in the car, Cheng Wanxuan finally couldn't hold back her tears. Thinking about how she had damaged such an expensive necklace and caused Jiang Muchong to pay such a large sum of money, she felt both sorrowful and guilty, unable to stop her tears.
