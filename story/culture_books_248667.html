The "Lamb Skewer" traffic accident shocked the entire city, the sudden death of a female radio host, the abrupt jump from a building by the secretary, the suicide of a discipline inspection commission staff member... Whose fate is being controlled behind these series of events?

A beautiful and elegant city has been marred by many scandals, thus beginning our story. A series of baffling cases hide many human desires. Under the layers of conspiracy, human nature is deteriorating. In the life of power, greed, and lust in official circles, people have become transactions.

"Exposure" Chapter One

When Qin Tai entered the bedroom and did not find Yang Huan's figure, he couldn't help but call out: "Little Yang, Little Yang, where are you? Are you in the kitchen? In the bathroom? In the toilet?" While calling out, Qin Tai searched room by room. For some reason, he suddenly had an ominous premonition, as if something unexpected was about to happen again. Finally, Qin Tai saw Yang Huan in the bathroom. To his surprise, the Yang Huan that Qin Tai saw was not alive but dead. Seeing this, Qin Tai immediately felt dizzy, lost his balance, and fainted.

"Exposure" Chapter Two

The instigator of this tragedy was the Hexi Primary School in Jiangshan City, a key elementary school. Most students in key schools come from wealthy and influential families, and since China implemented the one-child policy, many children have become their parents' only child. Therefore, such tragedies evoke even more sympathy.

"Exposure" Chapter Three

Yang Huan listened happily to Secretary Qin's promises, which seemed more melodious than sweet nothings. Yang Huan understood that getting close to Secretary Qin would bring revolutionary changes to her life, and her younger brother would soon rise to prominence in Jiangshan.

"Exposure" Chapter Four

Because of the naked female body served as a platter (a Japanese dish known as 'shirabe'), Liu Wenwu and Qin Tai's relationship became much closer. The relationship between people, whether opposite or same sex, is actually just a thin layer of paper. Once this layer is broken, both sides will talk freely about everything.

"Exposure" Chapter Five

In his vast office, Qin Tai faced the pain of Yang Huan's murder, recalling every rendezvous they had over the years. From when he met Yang Huan during his inspection of the television station to the pleasure of their affair at his home. From helping her younger brother Yang Xi collaborate with Singaporean businessman Liu Wenwu to build the first entertainment center in Jiangshan, to the storms of industry competition. Now, Yang Huan was gone; who killed her? Who did she offend? Is it related to the Tianle Entertainment Center?

"Exposure" Chapter Six

Qin Tai thought repeatedly that to uncover the mystery of Shi Dake's suicide by jumping from a building, he still needed to find a breakthrough through Ding Xiang. Even without any warning signs, Ding Xiang should understand Shi Dake's thoughts and actions before his death. Qin Tai firmly believed that no matter how smart a man is, once he is in a woman's arms, he becomes foolish, with his IQ dropping to around 20%. Although Shi Dake was also Qin Tai's confidant, Qin Tai believed there were some things Shi Dake wouldn't tell him but would tell Ding Xiang.

"Exposure" Chapter Seven

Mayor Yu Fan of Jiangshan City never expected that the discipline inspection commission staff member Man Xiaoyuan would fall in love with him. This was an awkward situation. As the mayor, Yu Fan always admired Man Xiaoyuan for her integrity, cuteness, and boldness. He always regarded her as a junior and cultivated her as a young cadre. However, this mischievous girl, how did she know he was divorced? Where did she get this information? Yu Fan remained puzzled.

"Exposure" Chapter Eight

The nourishment of the "sister-brother romance" made Ding Xiang rejuvenate and radiate vitality. And Xia Wen experienced an unparalleled kind of "happiness." They spent at least one night a week as husband and wife, had outdoor activities weekly, and singing karaoke at the Tianle Entertainment Center was a must-go place every week. Such activities could adjust their mental and physical states, releasing stress from their hearts.

"Exposure" Chapter Nine

As Qin Tai watched Man Xiaoyuan's graceful figure walk away, melancholy crept back into his heart. Originally, today he wanted to keep Man Xiaoyuan and do her ideological work, then take her back to his own home. If she was willing to follow him and sacrifice her youth like Yang Huan, Qin Tai decided to treat Man Xiaoyuan the same way he treated Yang Huan, allowing her to enjoy wealth and luxury, letting her experience and appreciate the splendor and opulence of the presidential suite at Longhu Mountain Villa. However, Qin Tai's wish could not be fulfilled. Being the Party Secretary of the city, he could not act like a thug on the street, smiling obnoxiously and touching her casually, nor could he tear off his mask and reveal his greedy nature. He could only use dignified language to probe her thoughts. Unfortunately, Man Xiaoyuan had no such intentions.

"Exposure" Chapter Ten

Yan Feifei had long heard of Secretary Qin's promiscuity and knew that the murdered female hostess Yang Huan was Secretary Qin's lover. She understood the implications of his words. What he meant by wanting to see them was asking for a service girl to accompany him. Among the 27 service girls, all wanted to accompany Secretary Qin, as it was an opportunity. For women to succeed, the shortest path is to attach themselves to rich businessmen or officials. Most women understand this life lesson, including Yan Feifei.

"Exposure" Chapter Eleven

Song Junyi's son Song Tianhe studied architecture and was very familiar with real estate development. He is currently in Beijing, sightseeing at the Great Wall with a young woman. This nearly fifty-year-old playboy remains unmarried but has several lovers. It is rumored that he is Song Junyi's illegitimate son, living on the mainland, with properties in Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan, usually residing in Shanghai. Now in Beijing, he is waiting for his father Song Junyi's news. Once the deal for the seven streets and eight alleys of Jiangshan is finalized, he will immediately fly to Jiangshan.

"Exposure" Chapter Twelve

Women are born with maternal instincts, and Yan Feifei is no exception. When the Party Secretary and the Public Security Bureau Director visited Longhu Mountain Villa last night, she guessed that there must be something important. Before their conversation, the moment she saw Secretary Qin, she collapsed drunk into his arms. She didn't expect that Secretary Qin was a charming politician who, in his romantic yet rough manner, guided Yan Feifei into a realm of expanded desire, making her unable to resist the needs arising from deep within her bones. Thus, a scene of trembling hearts and soaring flesh was permanently etched in her mind.

"Exposure" Chapter Thirteen

Although Vice Governor Cui can be considered a second-line cadre, he and Deputy Secretary Yan are both senior members of the provincial committee, wielding significant influence. Because they have cultivated many cadres, Vice Governor Cui and Deputy Secretary Yan are bitter enemies. More than ten years ago, Yan was the provincial party secretary while Cui was always the vice governor, missing out on becoming the governor by a mere step. Later, he blamed then-provincial party secretary Yan for not pulling him up, and thus they became mortal enemies.
