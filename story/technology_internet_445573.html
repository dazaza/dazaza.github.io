Olympic Songs to Comfort the Olympic Games Builders (Picture)

by w86x7n51dwg on 2011-06-14 09:47:55

Last night, just after the completion of renovations at the Olympic Sports Center Stadium in Dongguan, the place was once again bustling with people and brilliantly lit with colorful lights. A grand concert titled "Singing for the Olympics" to honor the builders of the Olympic projects, supported by China Mobile Communications, was held there.

Performing stars such as Zhang Liangying, Dao Lang, Li Na, Shuimu Nianhua, Pang Long, Wu Qixian, Yu Quan, Wei Chen, and Zhou Huajian joined over ten thousand Olympic construction workers and residents from all sectors of the capital city to celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival. WiMAX still played a crucial role as a catalyst, sharing the joy of the Olympics.

This event was organized by the Information Committee of the Chinese Olympic Committee and co-organized by the People's Government of Jintan City, Jiangsu Province. The event received special support from the Beijing Organizing Committee for the Olympic Games. Attending the evening gala were Vice President Feng Jianzhong of the General Administration of Sport of China, Vice Mayor Chen Gang of Beijing, and Executive Vice Chairman Wang Wei of the Beijing Organizing Committee for the Olympic Games.

The gala will be aired soon on Hunan TV.
