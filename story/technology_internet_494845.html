Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment" strives to explain the preciousness of humanity.

Dong Jian thought Tai Feng was about to take him straight to hell, scaring him out of his wits. Dong Jian negotiated with Tai Feng, stating that it was an unfair decision to let him die, and believed that the devil didn't understand what humanity was. Dong Jian took Tai Feng to visit an amputee patient, hoping to use his experience to explain to Tai Feng that human beings' will to survive is not out of cowardice but also has the brilliance of humanity. Dong Jian told Tai Feng the reason for his determination to do scientific research and stated that if Tai Feng insisted on taking him to hell, the world would lose a force that could save people. In the end, Tai Feng decided not to take Dong Jian's life for the time being.

Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment"

In Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment", Dong Jian is repeatedly forced to make decisions.

When Dong Jian thought everything was over, he never expected Tai Feng to reappear in front of him as a neighbor. Tai Feng brought both good news and bad news to Dong Jian. The good news was that Tai Feng had already found someone to replace Dong Jian's death. The bad news was that Tai Feng planned to use Hua Xi's life to replace Dong Jian's. After hearing this, Dong Jian was greatly shocked and tried to stop it. Tai Feng thought that Dong Jian had too many excuses and decided to let him choose a person who should die so that he could complete his task, making Dong Jian at a loss. Dong Jian was extremely troubled by Tai Feng's words, and Tai Feng often appeared in front of Dong Jian unintentionally, reminding him who should die, adding a lot of pressure to Dong Jian.

Tai Feng no longer had the patience to wait, so he took Dong Jian to a rooftop and asked him to randomly choose someone to replace him in death. Dong Jian refused the request, and finally, Tai Feng decided to give Dong Jian two days to make a choice. After hearing this, Dong Jian was greatly struck and stood there dumbfounded. When Dong Jian passed by a TV wall, he saw news about Jing Tian, and Tai Feng immediately suggested that Dong Jian find Jing Tian as the substitute ghost, which made Dong Jian recall all kinds of evil deeds committed by Jing Tian. Originally, Jing Tian had invited Dong Jian to collaborate on the development of cancer treatment drugs, but when the research reached the clinical trial stage, Jing Tian reduced the number of people receiving the trial drug due to high development costs, making Dong Jian very dissatisfied.

Cancer patient Qiang faced the threat of death because he could not obtain the second phase of the trial drug. His wife pleaded with Dong Jian for help but got nowhere, leaving Dong Jian deeply saddened. Yong Shen paced back and forth outside Jing Tian's office, uneasy about the layoff issue. Finally, unable to bear it, he argued with Jing Tian about it. Jing Tian thought Yong Shen was overly sentimental and ignored his son's suggestions, insisting on implementing layoffs. Yong Shen's timid character couldn't stand up to Jing Tian, so he could only have the secretary distribute the termination letters to the employees while staying away from the matter himself. Due to his inability to help the employees, Yong Shen felt emotionally low. At this moment, Tai Feng suddenly appeared in front of him.

Hong Kong drama "Uninvited Appointment" Tai Feng and Jing Tian discuss cooperation

During Jing Tian's lunch break, Yong Shen appeared with Tai Feng; Jing Tian became furious when Tai Feng disturbed his meal. Tai Feng explained his intention to see him and boldly claimed that he could find another "star drug" for Jing Tian, which immediately caught Jing Tian's attention. Tai Feng said he had a way to make Dong Jian cooperate again with Jing Tian in developing new drugs, earning him Jing Tian's respect. Jing Tian personally went to the university laboratory to find Dong Jian, showing interest in the antiallergic drug he developed. Dong Jian wasn't sure about Jing Tian's intentions and could only temporarily delay him. Dong Jian suspected that everything was Tai Feng's idea and questioned him. Tai Feng frankly admitted that he wanted to force Dong Jian to admit that Jing Tian was beyond redemption, naming Jing Tian as the substitute ghost so that he could smoothly complete his work. After hearing this, Dong Jian looked utterly bewildered.
