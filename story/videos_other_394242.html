I and the online store

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 09:43:54

When it comes to online stores, I believe everyone knows about them. If you don't know, it's fine because no one is born with all the knowledge. Through understanding, you will gradually become familiar with it, and I believe you will understand the advantages of online stores.

An online store is a convenient, cost-effective, and fast shopping platform. You can stay at home and just move your mouse to buy what you want.

Some people may ask: how do we purchase items? How do we pay the seller? This is done through our QQ Tenpay, which everyone knows about, as well as online banking and Alipay to conduct transactions with the seller.

As for me? I was someone who knew nothing. At first, online stores were a very confusing path for me. I couldn't see the road ahead, I was completely lost. My boyfriend used to buy clothes online. When the goods arrived, the quality was very good and also very cost-effective. Then we discussed trying to open an online store. Nowadays, the competition in online stores is very strong, but it's faster than opening a physical store. At least online stores are not so troublesome, there's no need to pay rent or taxes, no miscellaneous fees, and shipping doesn't have to be handled by ourselves. It's really convenient. That's why we started an online clothing store.

The business is doing alright, at least we haven't lost money. If the business isn't good, the monthly transaction volume is still around 5000~10000 yuan, and if it's good, reaching 80000 yuan in transaction volume is no problem.

Every month, the company updates its inventory. When there's no stock left, they will notify us. Clothes without stock will automatically be taken off the shelves, and new updated clothes will automatically be listed, so we don't have to worry about it.

With the increasing development of society, I believe that online shopping will become more popular in the future. Therefore, we want to keep doing this and not give up. We will work together to make our small shop better. The online store is our hope, dream, and wish.

Come on! Cheers!
