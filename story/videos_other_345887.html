There are two types of places to eat in the Expo Park area: one is the public area (which can be understood as a commercial area), the other is inside the exhibition hall. Public areas will open 128 restaurants, able to accommodate 32,000 people dining simultaneously, eating here is slightly cheaper, a set meal price ranges from 30 yuan to 100 yuan. There are 60 restaurants inside the exhibition hall, estimated to accommodate 10,000 people dining simultaneously, prices are relatively more expensive, per capita consumption is about 100 yuan to 400 yuan. Based on our experience, the value of the eating and drinking problem within the Expo Park is approximately equal to zero: only in the Expo staff restaurant, we have accepted the "banquet" from the Expo Bureau side, a banana, a serving of scrambled eggs with tomatoes, a serving of soup, a serving of sautéed shredded pork, a bowl of rice, because it is a "banquet", the price of this set meal is unknown.
