"Snow Wolf Valley" Tops the Ratings: Heart-wrenching Ending May Be Revised - www.d1kk.cc

by mkomko8f1h on 2012-03-02 14:23:56

The anti-Japanese war TV drama "Snow Wolf Valley" starring Lian Yiming, Du Chun and Zhang Jiayi is currently a hit on Jiangsu Satellite TV and Letv.com. It is reported that the show only took two days to achieve a viewership rating of 1.31%, ranking first among all satellite TV programs broadcast at the same time. The series will air its grand finale on February 23rd. When it was previously aired on local channels, the heroes had all died one by one. It is said that the version for satellite broadcast has been edited, possibly resulting in a less heart-wrenching ending.

In the "all-male lineup" of the series, Du Chun's portrayal of the hired guard Zhao Si Hai and Zhang Jiayi's role as the Eighth Route Army political commissar Hao Guozheng are also particularly outstanding. Director Lian Yiming revealed that his collaboration with Du Chun and Zhang Jiayi during filming brought many surprises, often leading to impromptu scenes. Notably, compared to the character Seventeenth Prince portrayed by Du Chun in the simultaneously popular TV drama "Palace of Pearls", Du Chun completely transforms himself in "Snow Wolf Valley". He not only abandons romantic entanglements but also steps up at critical moments, staging a painful deception before the enemy alongside his elder brother Lian De Kui, making the audience hold their breaths for him.

However, due to the successful portrayal of male characters in "Snow Wolf Valley", some viewers have criticized the female roles, such as Zhao Ziyuan played by Yang Ruoxi, as being mere "ornaments". In response to this criticism, Director Lian Yiming explained: "Filming should be pure, and the female characters' parts are just embellishments and enhancements."

As "Snow Wolf Valley" approaches its conclusion, the mysteries within the plot are gradually being unraveled amidst suspense. One of the most concerning topics for the audience is the fate of the seven valiant members of the Wild Wolf Gang. A netizen who had watched the series earlier disclosed on Weibo that the heroes of the Wild Wolf Gang all perished during their battles against the enemy. "I cried every time a member of the Wild Wolf Gang died while watching the show," said the netizen. This revelation left the audience feeling heartbroken, with many expressing their unwillingness to accept such story arrangements. Regarding the endings of the heroes in the series, Director Lian Yiming stated that some arrangements were necessary to express the core ideas of the plot. However, in response to the audience's hopes, Director Lian Yiming decided to keep the ending mysterious. It is understood that the version of "Snow Wolf Valley" to be aired on Jiangsu Satellite TV has undergone another round of cuts and revisions. Whether the big ending will change or if the characters' fates can be reversed will only be known after the broadcast. (Editor-in-Charge: Lorring)
