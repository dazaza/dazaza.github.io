A family of three was hacked, the couple were dead and their daughter was seriously injured, and their home was almost set on fire.

by fdy6d6jjfju on 2012-03-05 16:47:46

The deceased, Jiang (surname), is 42 years old this year, and Zhu is 38 years old. The two are husband and wife. They were already dead when they were found. Their daughter, Jiang (surname) is 17 years old this year. She was discovered in time by her relatives and after emergency treatment, she has escaped from danger. The couple has another son who is only 8 years old. He studies at a town primary school and stays there. He was not at home during the incident.

The two children narrowly escaped a disaster. Villagers speculated: "Jiang and his wife are indeed good people in the village, and both are quite capable. However, their family must have had something happen outside. Last summer, their house almost got set on fire by gasoline. Fortunately, there wasn't too much loss, and no one got injured. Later, this incident caused a stir at the police station, but we don't know the result." Another informed villager said: "There are some things that are not suitable to say now, but these incidents may be related to Jiang's emotional life."

Moving forward about 500 meters, a blue police cordon appeared in front of us. A large group of onlookers stood outside the cordon. Inside the cordon, there were three or four police cars. The location where the murder occurred was a grocery store. Through the glass, we could see that the room inside was slightly messy. The bodies of the two deceased were on the ground, and the public security personnel were investigating.

At 15:40, the public security personnel withdrew, and an hour later, the bodies were taken away by the funeral home. The scene was temporarily sealed off by the relatives.

Neighboring villagers gradually arrived at the scene, including Jiang's two nephews Wang Hai and Wang Jiang who lived in the same village. Upon learning that Jiang still had a breath left, they broke the glass of the grocery store, took out the barely breathing Jiang, and then used her family's taxi to take her to Gongzhuling City for rescue.

In the brain surgery ward of the hospital, Jiang had just finished the operation and was lying in the intensive care unit. Two relatives were taking care of her by her side. Jiang's hair had been shaved off, her face was pale. The doctor indicated that she had temporarily escaped from life-threatening danger.

Previously, the Jiang family had almost been set on fire.

Zhaojiatun Village is 29 kilometers away from Yitong Manchu Autonomous County. Yesterday at 7 o'clock, a shocking news quickly spread throughout the entire village, breaking the original tranquility of the mountain village. "In the morning, both Jiang and his wife from Team Three were killed, and his daughter almost didn't make it!"

The first person to discover the family being attacked was Jiang's niece Wang Shufen who lived in the same village. At around 7 o'clock yesterday, Wang Shufen, as usual, was standing at the village entrance thinking about buying a few pieces of tofu. "I waited for a while, the tofu seller hadn't arrived yet, so I decided to go warm up at my uncle's place. As soon as I opened the door, I saw my uncle and aunt both lying on the ground. There was blood all over their bodies and heads... My sister was unconscious on the bed in the inner room. I was scared to death, cried and ran out shouting for help. I have heart disease, and after that I took four quick-acting heart pills, and I'm still getting intravenous drips now." Wang Shufen recalled.

After the incident, although everyone was busy saving people, protecting the scene, and contacting the public security department, someone recalled that a middle-aged man was very suspicious in the chaos. "They all said that the man rode a red motorcycle. The motorcycle was parked at the door of the grocery store and never turned off. Some people also said that they saw this person putting an axe into a woven bag." Said one villager at the scene.

Yesterday afternoon, as soon as I opened my mouth at the village entrance, the villagers already knew my intention, "Do you want to ask how to get to the accident family? Just drive forward, and you'll see the caution line, there are lots of people at his doorstep."

It was learned that Jiang suffered from open cranial injury, with the main injuries concentrated on the left side of the head. There was an axe wound on the back of her left pillow, an axe wound behind her left ear, the entire left external auditory canal ruptured, and there was trauma on her left shoulder joint.

According to reports (by reporter Liu Yang and intern Liu Wenbo), at 7 o'clock yesterday, a brutal case occurred in Zhaojiatun Village, Jingtai Town, Yitong Manchu Autonomous County. Villager Jiang and his wife were brutally murdered at home, and their 17-year-old daughter was severely injured. The police initially determined it as a revenge killing, and the murderer is currently being pursued.

Upon hearing Wang Shufen's shouts, Li Zhonghua, who lived next to the grocery store, rushed to the scene, "Wang Shufen was scared to death at that time, I didn't dare to enter the room, and I called the police."

"Blood was all over their bodies and heads..."

The daughter of the deceased has temporarily escaped from danger.

Who would do such a cruel thing resulting in two deaths and one injury? "My younger brother is honest, and he also has a good reputation in the village. He is not someone who causes trouble. However, two years ago, he started running a taxi business and often went out. I don't know if he offended anyone outside." Said Jiang's sister.

After the incident, the Public Security Bureau of Siping City and the Public Security Bureau of Yitong Manchu Autonomous County attached great importance to this case. Currently, the public security department has basically confirmed the suspect, and the arrest work is ongoing.

It was learned that Jiang made a living by driving a taxi, and he was rarely at home during the day. His wife was unemployed and farmed at home with their daughter. The grocery store was operated by the couple. This store was separated from their home by a village road and was rented not long ago. It was said to be planned for their daughter to operate in the future.
