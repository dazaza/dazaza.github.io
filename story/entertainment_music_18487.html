Album Name: Magic Cyndi

Singer: Wang Xinling

Record Company: Avex Taiwan

Release Date: April 30, 2007

Album Introduction:

Heavenly and earthly magic, we've finally waited for Wang Xinling!

The Sweetheart Princess, Wang Xinling, transforms into a magical goddess of love in her new full-length album [Magic Cyndi].

The first lucky love track "Love's Heavenly Magic" sees Wang Xinling casting her spell of love.

Cherries are blooming across all of Asia.

Wang Xinling's stunning 2007 album [Magic Cyndi] makes its magical debut on April 30.

After fifteen months of anticipation, we've finally waited for Wang Xinling's full-length album [Magic Cyndi].

Visually stunning and breathtaking, she breaks boundaries to transform into the Pink Goddess of Love and the Cool Black Sprite.

Aurally, a brand-new experience awaits with ten tracks that promise success and good fortune.

After a year and a half since her last album, Wang Xinling returns with "Magic Cyndi," where the main song "Love's Heavenly Magic" features lyrics so sweet they make even the Sweetheart Princess want to fall in love. This is the result of much effort after going through the ups and downs of life and various controversies over the past period.

"Magic Cyndi" conveys the idea that in the world of music, Xinling imagines herself as someone with magical powers, capable of using magic to whisk away all unpleasantness, leaving only elements of joy. In this album, she also releases her first self-composed song, hoping that fans can hear the more diversified changes in her musical style and singing techniques from this new album.

The first single from the new album, "Love's Heavenly Magic," draws its lyrical inspiration from the single female friends around Xinling. Watching her friends and colleagues always busy asking which Moon-Old-Man temple is the most famous? Which method of attracting love is the most effective? She couldn't help but join in on the conversation. After all, every girl wants to meet her ideal partner sooner rather than later! This song, regarded by the record company as the strongest 2007 online wandering lucky love track "Love's Heavenly Magic," uses the Indian sitar (Sitar), paired with Indian rhythm drums, showcasing a new Oriental UrbanOriental style. The simple yet powerful rhythm is interspersed with the sounds of the Chinese erhu, flute, and bamboo instruments, creating an atmosphere that feels both ancient and refreshingly new. Besides disco & hip-hop drums, cymbals and gongs, which symbolize festive Chinese sounds, are added to the rhythm section, making the anticipation of love feel even more exhilarating. The lightning at the beginning and end represents the moment love arrives—surprising and astonishing excitement!
