Who Are You Korean Drama Plot Introduction

Yincai (Han Chae-yeong), who participated in the cooking competition, made ramen using green tea as an ingredient and won the grand prize. After returning home, Yincai proudly showed her mother the award she had received. However, her mother scolded her, saying that she didn't even get into university and was already bragging everywhere. Her mother believed that Yincai must attend university, receive higher education, and become a successful woman, so she sent Yincai to a review class. Completely uninterested in studying, Yincai filled her notebook with methods and ingredients for cooking. Unable to abandon her own dreams, Yincai finally went to study in Italy with the help of Hyun-seong (Lee Deok-hwi). Upon arriving at the culinary institute in Italy, Yincai caused a "commotion" with chili sauce.

Soo-yeon (Hong Soo-hyun) came to find Yincai and told her that if she could pass the exam at the culinary institute, the Daeyoung Group would fully cover all tuition and living expenses. Meanwhile, Yi-jun learned about his biological mother's whereabouts and boarded a train to Venice. Coincidentally, Yincai, also heading to Venice, boarded the same train but panicked upon realizing she had lost her train ticket. When Yi-jun refused to offer assistance, Yincai accused him of lacking "familial love."

Upon reaching a small inn, Yi-jun felt helpless due to the language barrier. Yincai informed Yi-jun that his mother had already moved from her previous residence. Due to a train strike, Yincai and Yi-jun took a bus and became alarmed when pursuing police officers raised their guns at them...

Han Yi-jun (Cho Sun-jai) and Yincai mistakenly boarded a smuggler’s vehicle and were taken to the police station. After being detained, thanks to Yincai's cleverness, Yi-jun managed to escape and immediately rushed to Sijin’s (Jung Ae-ri) restaurant. However, standing before Yi-jun was his mother dressed in a wedding gown, which shocked him and made him want to leave immediately. Yi-jun resented Sijin for not recognizing him and failing to recognize him as her son.

To comfort the disheartened Yi-jun, Yincai personally made spaghetti for him. After eating the noodles, Yi-jun remembered the spaghetti his mother used to make for him when he was little and shed tears while talking. Seeing Yi-jun cry, Yincai felt pity on him and embraced him warmly. As their eyes met, a warm current flowed into each other's hearts, followed by a passionate kiss. After spending the night with Yi-jun, Yincai was mentally confused, and upon learning the truth, Hyun-seong (Lee Deok-hwi) punched Yi-jun hard. After returning to Seoul, Yi-jun was scolded by his father and then served in the military.

Six years later, Yincai worked in a market restaurant, living a tough life with her son. The president of Han Corporation handed over the loss-making Western restaurant to Yi-jun, who had returned after studying in the United States, instructing him to revitalize it.

Additionally, the restaurant where Yincai worked closed down, but with Hyun-seong's help, she was hired to work at the Western restaurant managed by Yi-jun...
