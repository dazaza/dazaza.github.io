Must-know for successful men

by huangmingxing on 2008-09-11 22:22:05

1. Men are the main body of society, whether you believe it or not. So men should have a sense of responsibility.

2. Before the age of 25, please remember that love is usually false, or not as pure and eternal as you imagine. If you are over 25, then you should understand this truth.

3. Eating until you're seven-tenths full is the most comfortable. When dealing with your girlfriend, please also keep it at seven-tenths.

4. Before the age of 30, please take care of your body. In the first 30 years, you look for illness, in the next 30 years, illness finds you. If you are over 30, you will naturally understand this truth.

5. A career is far more important than love. If a career cannot be eternal, then love can only be considered a fleeting moment.

6. Do not easily accept girls who pursue you. It's easy for a woman to chase a man, but if you fall into it too easily, you will find that you will miss out on many things and lose many things.

7. Please believe that problems that can be solved with money are not problems. If you think money is king, that having money gets you women and lacking money means no women, then women are not a problem.

8. Always stay positive. Every man has his adorable side, but the only unadorable side is: not actively facing life.

9. Do not let the same woman get hurt twice in a row. There's reason behind the saying "a good horse doesn't eat回头grass". If you've seriously considered breaking up, then please don't take any actions that indicate reluctance to let go.

10. If you and your ex-girlfriend can be friends, then you need to ask yourself: why? If you can still be friends after breaking up, then there are only two possibilities: you were both just playing around before and didn't give each other your truest feelings; or: one person is silently paying without regrets!

11. Never fully trust what a woman says during a romance.
