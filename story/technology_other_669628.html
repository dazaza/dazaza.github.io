How to deal with the situation of insufficient firing of coal slime dryer

by hx515317 on 2012-02-20 09:12:52

(1) For the variation in calorific value of fuel inside the coal slime dryer, appropriately add external fuel to the coal slime dryer. If the calorific value of the current fuel is higher than that of the original fuel, reduce the amount of fuel added; otherwise, increase the amount of fuel added to the coal slime dryer to maintain a balanced heat inside the dryer.

(2) Monitor the temperature inside the coal slime dryer. Based on the trend of temperature changes within the dryer, make an accurate judgment of the internal temperature and use this as a basis to control the addition of external fuel. Ensure that the external fuel is added following the principle of multiple small additions to avoid excessive or insufficient fuel addition causing temperature rise not meeting standards or incomplete combustion.

(3) Reasonably control the air gate of the coal slime dryer to adjust the ventilation volume inside. When the ventilation volume inside the dryer is too large, promptly lower the air gate to ensure that the entire firing zone operates in a positive pressure environment. If necessary, control the air intake at the bottom of the greenware stack to reduce rear wind. After lowering the air gate, add dry fuel with low moisture content to the coal slime dryer. Once the internal temperature of the dryer returns to normal, gradually raise the air gate to promote the development of the front fire and accelerate ventilation inside the dryer to speed up fuel combustion and increase internal heat. When the ventilation volume is too small, increase the height of the air gate so that the front part of the firing zone is in a negative pressure environment and the rear part is in a positive pressure environment for material calcination. At the same time, maintain the air intake at the insulation position and cooling position to improve fuel combustion quality and heat exchange efficiency, eliminating underfiring within the coal slime dryer.

[Note]: The maintenance and care of the coal slime dryer is an extremely important regular task. It should be closely coordinated with its operation and inspection, and there should be dedicated personnel for monitoring and inspection.

Coal, as an energy resource, is indispensable in both industrial development and daily life. During the mining process, after washing and outdoor stacking, some coal is washed away by water and forms coal slime after sedimentation. This causes a loss of coal resources, and the difficulty in recycling and reusing these coal slimes is relatively high. The research and production of coal slime dryers are the most effective way to recycle and utilize coal slime.
