1. Definition of Tencent Game Life Points System

The points system is Tencent Game Life's way of rewarding all Tencent game users based on their activation of games, playing games, and payment status. Tencent game users can use the points in this system to participate in lucky draws, exchange for goods, and enjoy exclusive services.

2. Ways to Earn Game Points

Any user who activates any Tencent game, recharges through personal Q-coin/Q-point accounts or other channels, and reaches a specified online duration in a day will earn different amounts of Tencent game points.

3. Precautions for Accumulating Points

(1) The following situations do not accumulate points: recharging via mobile phone or broadband; (to be confirmed on Monday) using purchased shopping vouchers or other substitute coupons; online duration for a single game accumulates points by the hour, with no accumulation for less than one hour, one hour accumulation for over an hour, and so on. For example, if online for 2 hours and 30 minutes, then earn points for 2 hours. Other methods deemed unsuitable for accumulating points by Tencent Games.

(2) Points are accumulated per QQ number and cannot be transferred to another QQ number.

(3) Before the official launch of the Tencent Game Points System, the points earned from activating games will be paid out in one lump sum, with no retroactive points for consumption behavior or accumulated online duration.

4. Tencent Game Points Accumulation Unit

Tencent game points are accumulated based on each recognized single operation (single game activation, single payment, daily accumulated online duration). Activating a game earns 100 points, consumption is based on Q-points at 1.5 points per Q-point, and daily accumulated online duration of 1 hour earns 10 points. Online duration earns a maximum of 3 hours' worth of points per day per game.

Points Earning Methods Unit Points Rules

Game Activation 1 game 100 Per QQ number, cannot be repeated for one game

Consumption 1 Q-coin (10 Q-points) 15 Based on 1 Q-point, no upper limit.

Accumulated Online Duration 1 hour 10 Maximum 3 hours' worth of points per game.

For example, today I activated "Dungeon Fighter" and earned 100 points, consumed 5 Q-coins in the game earning 15*5=75 points, and accumulated 2 hours of online gaming earning 10*2=20 points, so today I earned a total of 100+75+20=195 points from the DNF game!

6. Effective Time of Tencent Game Points and Points Validity Period

Tencent game points are obtained immediately on the day of operation, and can be checked and used by users after accounting. Points are valid indefinitely until used up (to be confirmed).

7. Points Consumption Principle

Tencent points consumption follows the first-in-first-out principle. That is, the earliest obtained points are consumed first.

8. Points Inquiry Method

QQ users can log in to the Tencent Game Points System and go to [My Personal Information]-[My Points]-[Points Inquiry] page to check their Tencent game points situation.
