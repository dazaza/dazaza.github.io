Chapter One Hundred Fifty-Two: Intimidation

by fanyete on 2011-09-28 16:48:45

"Ziyue?!"

Looking at the familiar cold, pretty face, Lin Dong was stunned. Judging by Ziyue's position, she was clearly the commander of this so-called "Black Armor Guard."

"Hehe, Ziyue is the daughter of the city lord of Yan City." While Lin Dong was puzzled, Master Yan approached with a smile and explained.

Upon hearing this, Lin Dong finally understood why he always felt that the people from the Sushi Guild treated Ziyue with great respect. It turned out to be because of this identity of hers.

"All my attention has been on the Sushi Tower recently, I haven't paid much attention to external affairs. We had agreed to resolve the trouble between your family, the Lin family, and the Blood Wolf Gang." There was a hint of apology in Master Yan's voice. After learning about this matter, he immediately rushed over. As for Ziyue bringing the Black Armor Guard here, it was her own idea. After all, the Blood Wolf Gang was not an ordinary force. Without some substantial deterrent, given Yue Shan's nature, he probably wouldn't be willing to let things go easily.

Regarding Master Yan's apology, Lin Dong shook his head with a smile and didn't take it too seriously. They were already very considerate to come help at this time.

"In just a few months, this kid has managed to win over the Ten Thousand Gold Commerce Guild, the Sushi Guild, and the City Lord's Mansion..."

Looking at the three towering giants standing behind Lin Dong, even someone as strong as Yue Shan felt a wave of helplessness. He always thought the Lin family was easy to defeat, but current events made him realize that in the future, in Yan City, the Lin family would likely become one of the forces not to be provoked.

With the backing of these three major forces, plus Lin Dong's astonishing talent, one could imagine the potential of this family in the future.

Although Yue Shan strongly resisted the arrival of that day, at this moment, he also realized that Lin Dong had already developed into a significant force. He was no longer the kind of person who could be crushed with a flick of a finger when they first met.

Taking a deep breath, the dark look on Yue Shan's face quickly faded, and then a smile reappeared on his face. He bowed to Ziyue and said with a laugh, "Since even Miss Ziyue has personally intervened, if Yue Shan still refuses to back down, it would be too unwise."
