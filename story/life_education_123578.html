Shijiazhuang Orient Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:41:27

Integration of work and study, part-time work for financial aid, the best practical training facilities in the province, simple, fast and accurate teaching mode, fully enclosed quasi-military management of students, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the whole province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Municipal Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29 Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "liberating thinking, being pragmatic and innovative, and seeking change through diligent thinking", constantly carrying out teaching and management reforms, firmly establishing the awareness of "market competition", taking the market as the guide, taking the needs of enterprises as the standard, exploring new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience, and students have received widespread praise from employers after employment.

First, with enterprise standards as the guide, we have equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Sufficient and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, building a numerical control practice workshop equipped with more than 50 CNC lathes, CNC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines and other equipment, as well as an ordinary lathe practice workshop. The school has also built supporting facilities such as a communication electronics laboratory, piano room, electronic reading room, grinding wheel room, shape room, and a new teaching building.

Second, with employment positions as the guide, we have created an enterprise-like teaching model.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, in the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students take on roles such as team leaders, line managers, and employees during their "work". And through regular evaluations and examination results, outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day's "work" situation of the students has quality monitoring indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. Based on the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, consisting of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively stimulates students' initiative in learning and competitive awareness, enabling them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned faster and better.

Through enterprise-like teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models while still in school, adapting to the needs of employment positions and initially reaching the enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-like teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

Third, with enterprise needs as the guide, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students and formulated a daily student norm and a practical manual for vocational quality training.

Fully learning from enterprise employee management models, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises need not only skilled workers with solid technical skills but also emphasize employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require efforts and collaboration from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs throughout the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school focuses on two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," hiring retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, focusing on dressing, behavior, etiquette, and housekeeping, students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the learning process, we offer vocational quality courses, which are divided into theoretical lectures and extension practices. These two aspects complement each other and mutually improve. Vocational quality theory lectures mainly focus on practical applications, guiding students' ideological transformation, while extension training uses "personal experiences" to consolidate teaching outcomes, thereby transforming these vocational quality requirements into students' conscious behaviors.

Vocational quality extension training involves signing agreements with enterprises and sending our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students understand society and enterprises before actual internships, entering a formal enterprise employee state early, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, with employment as the guide, we have successfully completed employment placement work.

As the quality of our students continues to improve and employment channels continue to be perfected, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi areas, and the Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's CNC major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are top 500 mechanical manufacturing enterprises in the world. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

Fifth, considerate后勤services, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students in our school implement a "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, this reduces the burden on parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to students' physical development needs, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and intensive learning, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students dining outside the school.

The school's student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms are all complete service facilities. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, thoughtful, intimate, and convenient.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years for students.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from and giving back to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-thinking educational concepts, we save the maximum cost for parents, meaning students coming to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees. The three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through the integration of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specially established a "Hongzhi Class," fully or partially exempting all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and direction of struggle for our school. The process of practicing school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.
