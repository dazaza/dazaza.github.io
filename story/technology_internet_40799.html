It is imperative that the corporate post office enters the enterprise.

by now on 2007-12-29 09:48:47

According to the data organized by iResearch based on the information from Radicati Group, by 2011, the user information volume of corporate mailboxes will grow at an annual rate of 20%. Corporate mailboxes play a very important role in the process of enterprise informatization. With the deepening and widespread adoption of enterprise informatization, corporate mailboxes have become a "standard configuration" for enterprises.

Corporate mailboxes allow enterprises to quickly and efficiently exchange information or data, ensure smooth communication, promote internal collaboration within the enterprise, and enhance work efficiency. Enterprises can select appropriate corporate mailboxes and their value-added functions according to their own characteristics, achieving a tailored fit.

After using the corporate mailbox system, enterprises can use their domain names as email suffixes, formatted as: yourname@corporate_domain. This serves as a symbol of corporate identity, establishing a unified corporate brand image, promoting corporate online marketing, and facilitating e-commerce. A corporate mailbox is an independent operating system that often employs digital certificate encryption technology with identity authentication functions, ensuring email security during the sending and receiving process, enhancing the confidentiality and stability of corporate information.

CNET Technology Information Network reported on December 13th that in 2004, spam accounted for 70% of all emails. According to a report published by Barracuda Networks on December 12th, this proportion reached 95% in 2007. Issues such as mailbox stability, security defense features, sending speed, large amounts of spam, and occasional sending failures have always been of concern to enterprises and mailbox providers. The Times Enterprise Mailbox from Times Interconnect (http://www.now.cn/) boasts a virus elimination rate of up to 99.8%. The enterprise mailbox uses its unique Anti-SPAM anti-spam engine to intelligently identify and handle spam emails, as well as the professional Anti-VIRUS engine to automatically disinfect all emails in real-time online, which has always been favored by a wide range of users.

In the Internet era, owning a corporate mailbox is one of the basic standards for measuring the modernization of an enterprise, and corporate mailboxes have become an indispensable tool for enterprise online marketing. In the more developed regions of Europe and America, the penetration rate of corporate mailboxes has reached as high as 60%, while only 3% of domestic enterprises use corporate mailboxes. "As enterprises develop rapidly, the adoption of corporate mailboxes becomes inevitable."
