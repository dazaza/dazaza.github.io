Chen Linhui, a young woman from Xinye, lost her nose when she was three months old due to a rat bite. For 30 years, she has lived in the shadow of this disfigurement, too afraid to speak or be seen by others. Not long ago, having just learned how to use Weibo, she decided to share her story with her idol, the famous Hong Kong actor Luo Jialiang. To her surprise, Luo Jialiang and many kind-hearted netizens came to her aid.

Yesterday, Chen Linhui received free plastic surgery in Ningbo. (Click on the picture to enter Luo Jialiang's Weibo)

[Childhood Tragedy] Made Her A Monster In Others' Eyes

Chen Linhui is 30 years old and lives in a rural area of Xinye County, Nanyang City. When she was three months old, one day, while her parents were working in the fields, a rat climbed onto the bed and bit off part of the sleeping baby Linhui's nose, even damaging the cartilage of the nasal bridge. After her parents returned home and found out, they rushed her to the local health clinic where the doctor only gave her a simple bandage and sent them home. A few days later, Linhui's wound scabbed over, but most of her nose was gone.

Chen Linhui told reporters that she spent her childhood under the strange stares of others. At school, she was often mocked, and due to financial difficulties, she dropped out before finishing junior high school. Later, she learned tailoring from a relative and opened her own sewing shop.

At the age of 17, Chen Linhui took the 10,000 yuan she had saved up and went to a local hospital for treatment. The doctor admitted that because of the severity of her injury, even after surgery, she could only restore about 60% of her appearance, which would still be noticeably different from normal people. My heart turned cold.

At the age of 20, Chen Linhui married a poor young man who had been unable to find a partner. She insisted on not holding a wedding banquet, I don't look good, I don't want others to see me. Soon after, she and her husband went to work in Xinjiang, as there were fewer people there, and she didn't want to see anyone.

In 2004, Chen Linhui had a cute son. Recently, when she sent her son to school, he cried and told her that he didn't want to go to school anymore because his classmates made fun of him for having an ugly mother. At that moment, she once again resolved to repair her appearance, both for herself and for her family.

[Weibo Appeal] Help From A Hong Kong Star

On December 13th, shortly after learning how to use Weibo, Chen Linhui reached out to her idol, the famous Hong Kong actor Luo Jialiang, sharing her story with him: Before I could even speak, my nose was bitten off by a rat, and I have been without a nose ever since. Restoring my nose and meeting you, my idol, are my greatest wishes in life.

What Chen Linhui did not expect was that Luo Jialiang quickly replied to her. Not only did Luo Jialiang encourage her in his comments, but he also contacted the Meilai Yue Foundation and Sina Charity, hoping that more people would take action to help Chen Linhui.

This online charity relay quickly bore fruit, as the Meilai Yue Foundation and Sina Charity both extended a helping hand to Chen Linhui. The Meilai (Ningbo) Plastic Surgery Institution formed an expert team for Chen Linhui's case. It is reported that her plastic surgery will be divided into four phases and take nine months to complete, costing approximately 200,000 yuan, with all medical expenses and living costs in Ningbo fully covered by the Meilai Yue Foundation. The first phase of the surgery was carried out yesterday.

I used to dream of becoming beautiful, and now I finally have the chance to become a beautiful mom! Chen Linhui, waiting for surgery in Ningbo, excitedly said that for several consecutive days, she couldn't sleep, imagining what she would look like in the future. After she is discharged from the hospital, she plans to become a volunteer and help more people in need.
