Rather trust intuition than eyes (tragic beauty love)

by lwf225 on 2009-10-03 18:16:03

The girl and the boy always loved, trusted, and were happy together... until one day, the boy's good friend told him that he saw the girl and a handsome boy laughing and chatting warmly on the street, and they went to a lovers' boutique together. The boy felt some disturbance in his heart. He told himself there was nothing wrong, but he couldn't help himself from guessing and imagining. So, the boy immediately called the girl and asked her: "What are you doing?"

The girl laughed and said: "I'm not doing anything! What's up? Do you miss me?"

The boy heard a man talking softly to the girl on the phone, so he said: "Nothing, who are you with? I miss you, do you miss me?"

The girl laughed even more happily and said: "Of course I miss you! Who else would I miss? There's no one next to me, you must have heard it wrong!"

The boy then said: "If there's no one, let's have dinner together later! You haven't had meals with me for several days now, what are you busy with?"

The girl said: "Not good! I have something else to do later! Can we have dinner next time? Bye!"

Just as the boy was about to say something, the girl had already hung up the phone. The boy's heart was very sad and angry at this point. He thought the girl must have changed her mind, otherwise why would she lie to him! So, the boy went to wait for the girl at her doorstep, but waited all night without seeing the girl. It wasn't until the boy returned to school in the morning that he met the girl. She was sitting in a handsome boy's car and didn't see the boy. At this moment, the boy felt like his heart was broken. He knew he deeply loved the girl, so her actions hurt his heart very deeply. The boy walked back to school dispiritedly, thinking: tomorrow is our 3-year anniversary of love and also my birthday. Is this the surprise gift you give me? I deeply love you, but you treat me like this, hurting me. I will definitely make you regret it!

So, the boy went back to class and found a long-haired girl who had always liked him. He said he wanted to be with her. The long-haired girl was very happy that the boy came to ask her out, so she smiled and nodded, holding the boy's arm and walking out. When the girl arrived at school, she saw this heartbreaking scene. She approached the boy and asked with a hurt expression: "Can you tell me what's going on?"

The boy laughed lightly and said: "What do you think? You've seen it and still ask? Don't you know already?"

The girl took a step back and asked with red eyes: "Why? Didn't you say you love me? Didn't you say you would always love me?"

When the boy saw the girl's sad expression, he hesitated a bit in his heart. He didn't know if the girl was acting too well or if she was really sad. But when he thought about the past few days, he couldn't stay rational. He coldly laughed and said: "Love? What an ironic word. When I was with you, I said I loved you, and when I'm with her, I say I love her too. I never truly loved you. Stop dreaming. I don't want to be with you anymore, so please stop clinging to me, alright? Go away!"

The girl looked down for a long time without saying anything. Then, the girl said: "I want to talk to you properly. I'll wait for you at our old place in the woods tonight!" Then the girl turned around and left without looking up. But when the girl turned around, the boy clearly saw the girl's tears. The boy thought: why is she so sad when she has changed her mind? Is it just because she doesn't want me to break up with her? Or does she like me and also like someone else?
