A hundred years ago, they were forced to go to Nanyang and bravely fight for livelihood and support the revolution. A hundred years later, the country is prosperous, the nation is reviving, and the Chinese people are blessed.

The history of Chinese people going to Nanyang can be traced back to after Zheng He's voyages to the West during the Ming Dynasty. It stopped early in the Qing Dynasty due to the maritime prohibition, but became particularly prevalent in the late Qing Dynasty. The history of going to Nanyang is also a bloody and tearful history of Chinese people starting businesses overseas.

Chinese enterprises were first squeezed by British colonizers and then looted by Japanese aggressors during World War II. Until today, the Chinese enterprises in Nanyang still have the vigorous momentum to lead Asia, which is undoubtedly something to be proud of.

This drama uses a Chinese family in Nanyang to tell the rise and fall of several generations and the tremendous changes that have occurred in family businesses in such a great era today.

Li Kejin was wrongly arrested, and Wu Yue came to the island and asked Cheng Yuan to rescue Li Kejin. Cheng Yuan was deeply involved in the chaos of the family after his father's death. He had to marry Lida, the daughter of Mr. Fei, and stabilize the relationships on all sides of the family to get out of the mire.

The son of the eldest sister was Pipi, who was eyeing the power greedily. His loving second sister acted arbitrarily. Uncle Seven, the elder of the family, wanted him to be a puppet. Lida's father always wanted to swallow up the Cheng family. There were too many threads to comb through and too many truths to find out. From then on, Cheng Yuan and Wu Yue, two unrelated young people with completely different backgrounds, were both caught in a complex family struggle, spiraling deeper and deeper. Wu Yue relied on Cheng Yuan to save Li Kejin, while Cheng Yuan relied on Wu Yue to cover up his doubts.

In the process of family struggles, Wu Yue took a bullet for him, and Cheng Yuan found that he gradually fell in love with this Beijing girl by his side. However, his marriage to Lida was also a family responsibility, and responsibility was heavier than life. Cheng Yuan knew that he could not escape his fate. The second sister sent Cheng Yuan to Beijing for his safety and cleared the internal obstacles of the family at the cost of her own life. A frustrated Cheng Yuan met Wu Yue again. Once having experienced the vast sea, it's hard to be impressed by ordinary water. As his career gradually got back on track, he also found himself gradually sinking into darkness. This darkness came from hatred and revenge. Wu Yue slowly pulled him out of the darkness.

In the midst of turmoil, how will Cheng Yuan continue his grandfather's legend... Cheng Yuan no longer needs to set up his office in front of the sea like his ancestors and look forward to his homeland.
