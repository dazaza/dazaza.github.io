Girls' Generation and f(x) Airport Fashion Coordination PK

by shish843 on 2012-02-19 22:22:02

Cute Luna showed up at the airport in a short fur coat paired with a polka dot scarf. Although she was similarly dressed as Yoona in a fur coat + polka dot scarf + sneakers, Luna's outfit had a strong American street style vibe. Jessica (Cheng Shaoyan) looked cool and efficient in her black sunglasses and patchwork leather jacket. Yuri beautifully appeared in a tweed coat + leather boots; even the coldest weather can't stop these actresses from showing off their beautiful legs. Sooyoung made a sharp appearance in an office lady-like ensemble: a gray fitted suit + blue striped shirt, looking incredibly stylish! Her blue cross-body bag not only matched perfectly with her shirt but also echoed its color very cleverly, while the N-letter branded sneakers on her feet are one of the trendiest items this season. Yoona followed the loose-top tight-bottom dressing rule, pairing a loose sweater with tight leggings and sneakers, which kept her warm and slim, complemented by a dark blue polka dot scarf, making her look very casual and approachable. Taeyeon was seen in a pink collared coat + jeans, walking lightly and looking extremely adorable. Hyoyeon exuded youthfulness in her denim coat + pink cardigan, and her purplish-pink fringed snow boots were quite unique. Shirley appeared sharp and neat in her collared coat + jeans. Her black tassel large bag and apricot card wallet looked trendy and high-quality. Tiffany looked stunningly slim and beautiful in her black wool coat + furry boots, which is the chicest winter outfit for this season - a classic women's overcoat. HyunA turned heads in her red coat paired with black sunglasses, adding glamour with a shiny handbag. www.chuanyidapei.com On January 10th, Girls' Generation and f(x), two of SM Entertainment's top girl groups, traveled to Japan for an event. The airport became a runway for their dazzling private wardrobe showcase, featuring stylish sunglasses, beautiful bags, fashionable clothes, and blingy personal items. Follow along with our editor as we take a look at the trendy fashion combinations from these two sister groups! As the captain of f(x), Song Hye Qiao, a beauty from Qingdao, China, wore a uniquely tailored cape that exuded elegance. Similarly accessorized with black sunglasses, Song Hye Qiao greeted the camera with a beautiful smile, demonstrating an elegant yet youthful British-style outfit with her black sunglasses + cape + camel classic tote bag combination.
