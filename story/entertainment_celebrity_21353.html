Wang Zhengliang, you shouldn't have left -- Analysis of the elimination in the "Happy Boy" top 11 to top 9 round.

by kalage111 on 2007-06-03 15:30:30

Several highlights of the finals of Happy Boy 11 to 9

Last night, the finals of Happy Boy 11 to 9 were held. Focusing on the Children's Day theme, the scene was very warm and comfortable, as if it brought us back to our childhood. Hunan TV is good at catering to the festive atmosphere. Before each contestant sang, pictures of them as children were shown. The contestants used brushes to express their childhood dreams, allowing us to see their innocent side. I think this can also enhance the confidence of these cute contestants. I have to say the final result first. Rocker Yao Zheng and power singer Wang Zhengliang were eliminated, which was really a frustrating thing. Since then, all contestants from the Jinan area have been eliminated. Wang Zhengliang's elimination has sparked intense debate: Is singing well really not as good as being popular? Bao Xiaobo said, "Power losing to popularity is an unreasonable thing!" In the face of such results, we can only feel regret and deep helplessness.

There are highlights in every competition, and the game of 11 to 9 was also very exciting. If there were no remarkable things, then this game would be a failure because the audience would not remember this game. The impression was not deep enough, and frustration outweighed joy. I think there were still some highlights in this game---

1. Zhang Yuan was directly put on hold. Zhang Yuan, the champion of the Nanjing area, failed in the minor exam due to forgetting the lyrics, which shows that there is no room for any mistakes in this increasingly fierce competition. Throughout the game, Zhang Yuan stood alone on the stage, giving people a feeling of loneliness. In fact, Zhang Yuan's singing was still pretty good, with a pure voice, clean appearance, and powerful performance. After the opening, I always thought Zhang Yuan wouldn't be eliminated. Sure enough, he advanced smoothly without any problems!

2. Wang Likun duetted with Wu Kequn. Wang Likun ranked first in the minor exam and therefore duetted with Wu Kequn on the song "Big Tongue". It felt pretty good, and I couldn't see the pressure on Wang Likun on the stage. His tone was great, and his dolphin sound was unforgettable. I still want to listen to it now.

3. Zhang Jie seemed to be under a lot of pressure. Zhang Jie ranked first in the last minor exam but ranked fifth in this one, losing his position as captain. Throughout the game, it was obvious that he was under too much pressure, especially when competing with Su Xing. His expression was very serious, even heavy, but his performance was still decent. He had the charm of a superstar while dancing with his female dance partner, and he advanced smoothly. Congratulations to him!

4. Yao Zheng, the seemingly forgotten rock singer. I remember during the game of 13 to 11, when both sides chose their teammates, no one chose Yao Zheng. And in the game of 11 to 9, no one still chose Yao Zheng. He always naturally returned to the team last. Do people not like rock music? Yao Zheng must have felt very lonely. Only on the stage could he showcase his passion and talent, displaying his rock ideology. Although he was eliminated, we will always remember that in the 2007 Happy Boy, there was a rock boy: Yao Zheng!

5. Yang Erche Namu continues to seduce and colorfully comment on the contestants. There are already enough people cursing Yang Erche Namu. You should moderate a little bit. Throughout the game, it felt like she didn't know how to comment properly, and her comments were long-winded and nagging, reaching the point where the cigarette ash had scattered. I am increasingly disapproving of her. I hope she won't appear again in the game of 9 to 7, as she really affects the mood and appetite for watching the competition.
