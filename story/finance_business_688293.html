Tianhe District:

1. Tianhe Sports Center Business Circle:

The business framework of the Sports Center has matured: in terms of property, there are leading figures such as Tianhe City, Bookstore Center, Hongcheng Plaza, and Zhengjia Plaza; from the perspective of operators, there are powerful merchants like Tianmao Nanda and Jusco. The influx of numerous large properties will continue to boost the commercial development of this area over the next few years.

2. Tianhe North Business Circle:

Tianhe North is currently Guangzhou's most business-oriented CBD. There are more than 20 high-grade office buildings in the area, over 1600 foreign enterprises, more than 560 foreign institutions permanently stationed in Guangzhou, with an annual growth rate of over 15% in the expatriate rental market. The Tianhe North business office circle centered around CITIC Plaza has become the preferred location for foreign companies entering Guangzhou. Procter & Gamble, HSBC, AIA, IMG and other internationally renowned companies have rented office space in this area. This area gathers Class-A office buildings such as Zhongtai International Plaza, Jinlianglai Building, Wealth Plaza, as well as over 20 high-end residential complexes like Zhongyi City Garden, Dijing Garden, Fangcaoyuan, Eaton 18, Hilton Sunshine, etc. The residential shop areas in Tianhe North have attracted well-known big merchants such as IKEA, PARKnSHOP, and Wanjia, enhancing the commercial atmosphere in the region. Currently, Tianhe North has become a modern landmark integrating functions such as office work, business, dining, entertainment, and shopping.

3. Tianhe Shapai Business Circle:

Positioned as a comprehensive trade zone for IT products, technical service zone, and entertainment leisure zone, its characteristic is the regional brand of "IT Street."

4. Tianhe Tangxia Business Circle:

Centered around Tangxia Walmart, it is surrounded by multiple large residential complexes with a dense population. It is close to Science and Technology Parks and Software Parks, making it the primary consumption area for people working and residing nearby.

5. Tianhe Huajing New City Business Circle:

Located at the core of the new urban center of Tianhe District, it connects to Zhongshan Avenue to the south, links up with Tianhe North Road to the north, Beijing General Electric Refrigerator Repair, faces the South China Expressway to the west, and is adjacent to Tianhe Park, South China Normal University, Jinan University, and the Postal Telecommunications Research Institute, among others. With a strong cultural atmosphere, it occupies a key area of development for the Asian Games, gathering four major locational advantages: the platinum business circle of Tianhe North CBD, the research and education center of South China universities, the ecological circle of Tianhe Park, and the eastern Asian Games sector. The main roads of the community connect to the extended line of Tianhe North, with five bus lines' terminals set up within the district and over 20 bus lines passing through Zhongshan Avenue, providing convenient transportation throughout the city. Additionally, the district has complete living facilities, including provincial-level No. 113 Middle School, provincial-level Huajing Primary School, kindergartens, clubs, commercial streets, markets, etc., offering mature living amenities.

6. Tianhe Park Business Circle:

As Guangzhou's "Eastward" strategy deepens, the Tianhe Park area has become the "dream land" for Guangzhou's commercial development, occupying the core position of the "Golden Triangle" formed by the Tianhe North CBD, Zhujiang New Town CBD, and Pazhou Exhibition Business Circle. With the development of Guangzhou's economy, urban expansion, and the relocation of the Tianhe District government, this area has gradually developed into the political, technological, and educational center of Tianhe District.

Two. Yuexiu District:

1. Beijing Road Pedestrian Street Business Circle:

The Beijing Road business circle naturally centers around the Beijing Road Commercial Pedestrian Street. This famous "First Street of Guangzhou" has been the dominant commercial hub of Guangzhou for over 2000 years, gathering large shopping malls and well-known brands. Guangzhou Hundred Goods stands prominently, connected by Zhongshan Fifth Road which houses Xin Daxin and the "northern-flavored" Hualian Department Store. In recent years, a pedestrian street renovation project costing over 300 million yuan has made today's Beijing Road blend seamlessly with the Dafosi Temple, Nanyue King Mausoleum, and ancient academies, preserving a strong Lingnan flavor while exuding a modern urban atmosphere. The underground "Thousand-Year Ancient Road" artifact pit, covered by earthquake-resistant glass, allows visitors to admire historical artifacts while shopping, greatly beautifying the shopping environment. Besides traditional commercial functions, the Beijing Road business circle also shoulders the responsibility of preserving Guangzhou's history. Whether tourists or local citizens, it is a must-visit shopping paradise and tourist attraction. The once-famous Gao Di Street and Xihu Road night market have now faded away, replaced by Guangzhou Hundred Goods, Xin Daxin, and newly opened Ming Sheng Plaza, May Flower Plaza, Guangming Plaza, which house various brand clothing specialty stores. Retailers like Benetton and Bossini occupy hundreds of square meters each, while shoe brands like Zhenmeishi, Ralston, Xinbu, Cardis, Tiamayi, and bookstores like Xinhua Bookstore, Children's Bookstore, and Ancient Books Bookstore have established themselves here, adding a scholarly touch to Beijing Road. As the commercial level of Beijing Road gradually improves, the strong commercial atmosphere spreads to surrounding areas, forming a radiating commercial zone driven by the Beijing Road business circle.

2. Haizhu Square Business Circle:

Haizhu Square Station is located at the intersection of Qiyi Road, Yide Road, and Taikang Road, at the northern end of Haizhu Bridge, near Guangzhou Hotel, Haiyin Binbin Fashion Plaza, Huaxia Grand Hotel, Taikang Plaza, Taikang Decoration City, Art Garden Wholesale Center, Bar Street, etc. With Haizhu Square as the center, a traffic transfer and leisure shopping place has formed around it.

3. Nonglinxia Road Business Circle:

Nonglinxia Road is located in the heart of the old Dongshan area, forming a classic old business circle with strong Guangzhou characteristics. After the opening of the Dongshan Kou station on Metro Line 1, its role in gathering crowds in this section became quite prominent. Currently, Nonglinxia Road, along with nearby Dongshan Kou, Shuqian Road, and Gui Gang, forms a relatively mature business circle with Wangfujing Department Store and Dongbai Department Store as well-known merchants, enjoying high market recognition and long-term high rental levels. Recently, plans for upgrading the Nonglinxia Road business circle have further emerged, with hopes to develop it into a mid-to-high-end consumption venue, ensuring a continuously favorable commercial development prospect.

4. Zhonghua Plaza Business Circle:

The Zhonghua Plaza business circle is located at the Hero Plaza site on Zhongshan Second Road, mainly composed of Zhonghua Plaza, Underground Shopping Mall Pop Frontline, Emperor Plaza, Lingyuan West Communication Specialty Market, Martyrs Cemetery, Guangdong Stadium, etc., presenting the雏形of a comprehensive commercial area, becoming a newly emerging large business circle between the traditional commercial streets of Beijing Road and Nonglinxia Road in the mother city of Guangzhou - East District. The annual commodity sales volume of this area has exceeded 2 billion yuan, maintaining a double-digit good growth trend.

5. Taojin Business Circle:

In the mid-to-late 1990s, due to the rise of the commerce and office area along Guangzhou Huanshi East Road, it drove the development of retail commerce in the surrounding areas. The ground-floor residences in the Huanshi East Taojin residential area began evolving into specialty stores and professional stores. After more than ten years of development, the current Taojin Community Commercial Street has gradually taken shape, strongly influenced by the business office area. Since the street is located along Huanshi Road and influenced by the Huanshi East commerce and office area, a large number of middle-income white-collar workers provide strong support for consumption within the area. At the same time, business travelers staying briefly in the business area also become important consumers of the Taojin Community Commerce. Driven by the landmark commerce in the area - Guangzhou Friendship Store, which features many international top-tier and second-tier brands, representing avant-garde, fashion, and quality, the trendy consumption image of the Taojin area has gradually taken shape, attracting a large number of middle-to-high-end consumers to shop. Meanwhile, the Taojin Community Commercial Street not only continues the high-end route of Friendship Store but also offers a relatively lower consumption level compared to Friendship Store, effectively capturing the mid-to-low-end consumer group lost from Friendship Store. Additionally, the "Good and More" wholesale store attracts a significant number of mid-to-low-end customers, providing considerable assistance in drawing in customers for the Taojin Community Commercial Street. Close proximity to areas with strong purchasing power - the third important reason for the formation of the Taojin North Community Commercial Street is its proximity to overseas Chinese villages, Taojin Road neighborhoods, where foreigners and white-collar workers reside densely. Due to the substantial demand for fashionable consumption by these residents, it provides stable market support for the development of the commercial street.

6. Wuyang New City Business Circle:

On the new urban axis of Guangzhou, Tianhe North, Zhujiang New Town, and Pazhou Exhibition Business Circle divide the world into three parts, while Wuyang New City occupies the core position of the three major business districts, bringing together enterprises and white-collar workers due to the business circle economy, providing strong impetus to the Wuyang New City business circle.

7. Dasatou Business Circle:

People familiar with Guangzhou all know that the electrical appliance market, fabric market, and second-hand market in Dasatou have gained immense fame locally and even nationwide. Regardless of the uneven quality of operations in the electrical appliance and second-hand markets, just discussing the history of how it once spurred local electrical appliance consumption and led the trend of home appliance upgrades deserves commendation for the unique insight of local businessmen.

Electrical appliances and household goods trading have now become synonymous with Dasatou. The earliest appearance in the Dasatou-Haiyin area was the Haiyin Electrical Appliance Market, which is both the earliest and largest wholesale-retail market for electrical appliances in the country, forming scale at the end of the 1980s. In the early 1990s, Shaw collaborated with others to rent some residential podiums and old factories near the Dasatou wharf, eventually developing into the later famous Haiyin Electrical Appliance City. The enormous success of the "Haiyin Model" focused on specialized market operations has profoundly influenced the direction of Guangzhou's shop lease investment in recent years. Today, this area has formed the Haiyin Electrical Appliance Street in Guangzhou and even nationwide. Near the Haiyin Bridge on Dasatou Road and Donghu Road, there are gathered over ten large wholesale-retail markets for electrical appliances, including Haiyin Electrical Appliance Supermarket, Jinhaiyin Electrical Appliance Plaza, Hui Guang Home Appliances Market, Haiyin Electrical Appliance Trade Center, Xinhe Haiyin Electrical Appliance Plaza, Haiyin Kangyuecheng, etc., totaling over 3000 merchants. They sell various imported and domestic brand-name home appliances, making it the largest commercial electrical appliance specialty market in Guangzhou's Xidi area, almost encompassing all global home appliance brands.

Three. Liwan District:

1. Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street Business Circle:

Shangxiajiu Commercial Street is 800 meters long, with 238 arcade-style buildings constructed in the early 20th century. Approved by the Ministry of Commerce, it is Guangzhou's first commercial pedestrian street. The Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street is filled with medium and high-end department stores and restaurants, featuring renowned delicacies and snacks. Nearby, there are also historically significant specialized streets and the largest agricultural and miscellaneous goods market in the country. Even if you don't buy anything, walking through Shangxiajiu Road allows you to experience the unique Lingnan commercial culture, making it a "leisurely" shopping center.

2. Zhongshan Eight Business Circle:

Zhongshan Eight Road has always been the main westward passage for vehicles in the old town. The traffic network system formed around this road covers almost the entire central and western regions. Upon careful observation, it can be seen that Zhongshan Eight Road is actually a commercial road consisting of three main components: supermarkets, toys, and clothing. Zhongshan Eight Road is not only the terminal station for multiple eastbound car routes but also a major node for westbound vehicles, collectively forming a vast transportation network system. Every day, hundreds of thousands of people gather in Zhongshan Eight Road and then disperse to various locations within the city, including permanent residents along the road and transient populations. Such a massive flow of people, once slightly paused in Zhongshan Eight Road, is sufficient to make a group of forward-thinking merchants rich first. Taking the clothing mall on this road as an example, the retail sales during festivals alone account for more than half of the annual sales. People returning home from other places always remember to bring a stack of distinctive clothes for their relatives' children. Some merchants in Nantian Trade City have even collaborated with dealers in some towns in the west of Guangzhou to engage in cross-regional trading. Convenient transportation brings more than just easy access to Zhongshan Eight Road; it also foresees a predictable commercial atmosphere. Although Zhongshan Eight Road is only 1.3 kilometers long, there exist two different sizes of supermarkets. The supermarket inside Fuli Plaza and the Harbor Warehouse in Zhoumen Residential Area belong to neighborhood supermarkets. Residents of the neighborhood are the customer base for these supermarkets, which is the fundamental guarantee for their survival. Neighborhood supermarkets do not need to operate multiple businesses; they just need to sell food and groceries selectively. Neighborhood residents clearly prefer this warehouse-style food selling method. Apart from occasionally seeking the joy of haggling at the meat and vegetable market, most of their shopping is completed in the supermarket. The "Wanjia Department Store" at Dongjun Lijing Garden belongs to a centralized operating model. On the premise of facing the old residents of the western city, "Wanjia" has opened special sections for food, home appliances, and clothing, supplemented by fast-food restaurants, sushi shops, and pharmaceutical stores. However, residents are clearly only interested in food, especially ready-to-eat meals and bread being the most popular. Calculating solely based on the sales of food, the department store definitely has an advantage; however, considering the procurement costs of all goods, neighborhood supermarkets are clearly superior.

3. Shamian Business Circle:

Today, Shamian retains 53 cultural relics buildings and structures, along with the street layout, canals, and ancient trees formed in those days, creating unique tourism resources that attract domestic and international investors to build multi-element, high-end catering, shopping, entertainment culture, and exhibition venues successively. Shamian has become a calling card for Guangzhou's tourism.

4. Fangcun Huadi City:

At the intersection of Huadi Avenue North and Xianhua West Road in Huadiwan Commercial Circle, it is about 80 meters from Exit C and D of Huadiwan Station on Subway Line 1, 100 meters from Fangcun Bus Terminal; connecting to Zhujiang Tunnel and Inner Ring Road; multiple bus routes directly reach the market without traffic restrictions for out-of-town vehicles; closely adjacent to subway, bus, and main traffic arteries, with convenient transportation and radiation covering the Pearl River Delta region; having excellent locational advantages.

5. Train Station Business Circle:

It is understood that Guangzhou, located in the Pearl River Delta and neighboring Hong Kong and Macao, has always been at the forefront of China's fashion trends. The Liuhua Clothing Business Circle near Guangzhou Railway Station is one of the largest clothing wholesale distribution centers in the country, with an annual turnover reaching tens of billions of RMB. The business circle gathers large clothing production and wholesale merchants from Guangzhou, Humen, Zhongshan, Fujian, Zhejiang, Chongqing, Shenyang, and other places. Merchants from more than thirty provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities across the country, as well as Russia, Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, and other countries and regions, come continuously.

Four. Haizhu District:

1. Jiangnan West Business Circle:

Haizhu District is fully committed to building the Jiangnan West business circle, transforming 650,000 square meters of residential buildings on both sides into commercial use. At the same time, it integrates the 18,000 square meters of underground civil air defense projects nearby, turning Jiangnan West Road into a distinctive commercial pedestrian street. The convenience brought by the subway makes Jiangnan West one of the most prosperous areas in Haizhu District, with a daily footfall exceeding 300,000 people, demonstrating significant consumption potential.

2. Shi Er Gong Business Circle:

Shi Er Gong business circle is the oldest human settlement and commercial center in Haizhu District. This area boasts the largest wedding goods distribution market in South China. Surrounding residential communities are densely populated, with convenient transportation and a wide range of consumption levels, making it a focal point for merchants to this day.

3. Zhongda Business Circle:

The Zhongda area is home to multiple specialized wholesale markets, with merchants gathering. Among them, the Zhongda Fabric Wholesale Market is the most influential. The Guangzhou Textile Expo Center has been designated as a key project and commercial project of Haizhu District's "Eleventh Five-Year Plan." Industry insiders predict that the Zhongda textile business circle will be a major highlight in Guangzhou's 10-year commercial planning, and the Zhongda textile fabric business circle will become the largest wholesale market for clothing, fabrics, and garment accessories nationwide. Therefore, it is estimated that property prices around the Zhongda business circle will steadily increase.

4. Kezun Business Circle:

After the opening of Metro Lines 1 and 2 intersecting at Kezun, the commercial and residential atmosphere in the Kezun area has become more intense, also becoming one of the symbols of youth and fashion in this region. The potential favorable factors of the Kezun shop market lie in people, which cannot be separated from the two major hotspots of development in the Kezun area in recent years—subway and residential complexes. By the end of last year, Kezun was officially upgraded to a dual-subway intersection point, not only improving the traffic conditions in the area but also boosting the popularity of nearby subway shops, especially the podium shops at the Kezun subway station exit of Linying Huating, which are thriving, attracting large chain stores like Lotus and Yongle to settle near the subway entrance. Meanwhile, the residential market in the Kezun area has rapidly developed in recent years, with an increasing number of residents leading to corresponding increases in needs such as clothing, food, housing, and transportation, boosting the leasing of Kezun shops. Additionally, Kezun is close to Sun Yat-sen University and Guangdong Education College, and newly developed residential complexes target white-collar workers, making Kezun suitable for industries serving the community. Currently, since there are already large commercial centers like Guangzhou Hundred Goods nearby, as the residential density increases, developing small-scale businesses such as sundries, clothing, and food in Kezun holds great potential but also carries certain risks.

5. Pazhou Exhibition Business Circle:

With the continuous implementation of Guangzhou's "Southward Expansion" strategy, Haizhu District has become the central urban area, and the exhibition economy has become the "leading" economy of the district. Deng Weiqiang, Secretary of the Haizhu District Committee, stated that in the next five years, Haizhu District will seize the opportunity of relocating the district government to accelerate the development of the central area centered around the Pazhou region and the new urban central axis.
