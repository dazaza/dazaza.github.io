The second network marketing registered trainer seminar builds a school-enterprise employment platform

by mttemarket on 2010-04-16 10:02:07

With the rapid development of the Internet, the country is paying increasing attention to the development of an e-commerce strategy, and more and more traditional small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are also applying and developing online marketing. A comprehensive promotion of online marketing consultant service models fully guides SMEs in using network platforms well, allowing more SMEs to understand online marketing, change their sales mindset, alter their sales methods, enabling SMEs to adopt advanced online marketing models to improve corporate sales performance, making online marketing an important marketing method for enterprises. According to a survey by the Professional Manager Certification Management Office of the China E-Commerce Association, the main application of e-commerce implementation by SMEs is the application of online marketing, and the factor that constrains the effectiveness of enterprise online marketing is talent, with 76% of enterprises indicating that talent is a significant obstacle to their development of e-commerce and online marketing. The scarcity of professional and practical online marketing trainers significantly affects the development of training for professional online marketing talents in China, simultaneously causing a severe shortage of practical online marketing talents in SMEs.

(In order to comprehensively enhance the professional skills of online marketing faculty and promote the rapid cultivation of practical online marketing talents, in 2009, the China E-Commerce Association initiated the "Cultivation Project for Registered Trainers of Chinese E-Commerce Professional Managers (Online Marketing)". This project is co-hosted by the Professional Manager Certification Office of the China E-Commerce Association and the Online Marketing Professional Committee of the China E-Commerce Association, with Shenzhen Boyan Technology Co., Ltd. as the organizer. The cultivation project consists of four main components: practical online marketing courses, practical training in online marketing projects, trial lectures of practical online marketing courses, and visits and discussions at outstanding enterprises applying online marketing. Through training and assessment, the only current "Registered Trainer Qualification Certificate for Chinese E-Commerce Professional Managers (Online Marketing)" in the country will be awarded.)

To ensure the smooth implementation of the "Cultivation Project for Registered Trainers of Chinese E-Commerce Professional Managers (Online Marketing)", the second national Advanced Seminar for Certified Trainers of Practical Online Marketing was held in Shenzhen from April 26-30, 2010 (http://www.boyan.cn/em_teacher.html). The second Advanced Seminar recruited individuals with rich practical experience in e-commerce (online marketing) from society and faculty members related to e-commerce majors in colleges and universities, aiming to cultivate more high-end online marketing trainers and enterprise online marketing coaches who have practical online marketing experience and can teach practical online marketing courses.

Simultaneously, the Luohu District Science and Technology Bureau of Shenzhen, together with the Online Marketing Professional Committee of the China E-Commerce Association, hosted the first Shenzhen E-Commerce Talent School-Enterprise Connection Negotiation Meeting, organized by Shenzhen Boyan Technology Co., Ltd., the designated talent training institution of the Shenzhen Internet Industry Park. The negotiation meeting gathered 37 outstanding e-commerce enterprises from the Shenzhen Internet Industry Park and 60 IT companies and traditional enterprises from other districts in Shenzhen, holding a discussion conference with vocational colleges, secondary vocational schools, and technical schools from inside and outside the province, focusing on school-enterprise connections, order-based training, and e-commerce talent training bases, building a cooperative exchange platform for recruitment by enterprises and job placement (internships) for graduates by schools.
