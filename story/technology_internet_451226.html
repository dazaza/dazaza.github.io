"Cloud Special Zone" Sweeps the Globe: A Fiery Showdown of Speed and Passion

by nuo01 on 2011-06-24 10:15:19

According to relevant reports, for the needs of economic development, China will set up a "cloud special zone" that can get rid of the firewall. The special zone can obtain software and data from a huge backend through a computer. This move is like a fuse, causing a stir in the internet. Western media criticized China's "cloud special zone" as a big trap, claiming that "China's black clouds scare away American netizens". Whether it is true as the United States claims, only black clouds, how to turn clouds into rain and land smoothly, all parties are looking forward to the graceful dance of the "cloud special zone".

Currently, for foreign media claiming that China's "cloud special zone" is unsafe and may be a "trap", given the frequent appearance of Chinese hackers, they are not confident to store data on the "cloud special zone". These disputes are just guesses without any basis. The network information revolution keeps changing, experiencing the host era, personal computers, the Internet era, and then entering today's cloud computing. Every process requires a sharp change. Even the United States, which started cloud computing four or five years ago, needs to unite major Internet operators to jointly fight against hackers when encountering hacker challenges. Sony has also lost more than 100 million users' data. Facts speak louder than words. Even the United States cannot guarantee its own security. Only the fittest can survive.

"Cloud computing" has always been at the forefront of the IT field, and the industry has long regarded it as a cake, unwilling to fall behind in the competition. Cloud services cover all industries and related fields of the entire network. With the continuous changes in the global information industry, the United States still leads in core cloud computing technology. In order to enjoy more diverse and efficient IT services, we must create our own things to present a better network blueprint and take root in the Internet to gain more benefits for ourselves. The "cloud special zone" nurtured by China will be a major reform in the country's economic development and informatization construction.

From the current Chongqing "cloud special zone", this isolated "network forbidden area" has no internal network, no firewall, and directly connects to the international Internet through optical cables to provide data services for multinational companies. The outer perimeter is heavily guarded, making it as difficult to attack as the White House in the United States. It has attracted many terminal equipment manufacturers, global six major notebook brands, Foxconn, Quanta, Inventec, Pegatron, Compal and other contract manufacturers, and a large number of component supporting enterprises have also moved in. Chongqing also plans to build a terminal equipment IT industrial chain in the cloud special zone.

According to informed sources, within less than half a year, Chongqing's "cloud special zone" has made layouts in server, switching equipment, smartphones, tablet computers and other terminal products through investment promotion, catching the attention of outsiders. The establishment of the "cloud special zone" is timely and promotes the progress of the industrial chain upstream. From the current development perspective, in the near future, Chongqing will become the world's largest notebook base. Some netizens also expressed that the cloud special zone will become a new concession in the Internet era.

Coming and going in the clouds, as for what cloud computing is, no one can really explain it clearly. Many people may only have a vague concept in their minds, knowing only that it is a very advanced, high-tech network technology. There are various opinions on the Internet. Some say it is the behavior of using the Internet for data storage and then retrieving it according to demand; others in the industry introduce it as allocation through specific servers and software; the well-known domestic IDC service provider China Nuowang www.nuo.cn believes that cloud computing is a new technology based on the delivery and use mode of infrastructure on the network operation platform, which is a new type of computing model that unfolds through efficient storage and centralized processing, ensuring the security of data, and providing customized services according to customer needs. The choice of cloud services will depend on the user's device. Regardless of cloud computing, "cloud special zones", virtual hosts, or servers, they are all based on serving commercial activities.
