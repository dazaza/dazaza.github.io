Plot Introduction:

In a distant future world, the universe where humans live is multi-dimensional. Somewhere deep in the cosmos, there reside several exact replicas of humans. They look the same, have the same bodies, and even their friends are identical. These individuals can coexist and reproduce in parallel without interference in their respective dimensions, living peacefully. However, due to the advanced technology at the time, which allowed humans to travel freely between parallel dimensions, the corresponding management agency MAV was established.

Gabriel Yulan (Jet Li) is an officer with MAV. Although he is a police officer, most residents abide by the law, so his job mainly consists of routine patrols and investigations. He returns home each day to a beautiful and gentle wife, leading a solid and stable life.

However, one morning, he discovers that his life has been completely transformed - for some unknown reason, this ordinary man now possesses superhuman abilities. With immense strength, a casual kick can destroy a metal utility pole, and his body's agility and speed have greatly increased. Running is like lightning, and dodging crisscrossing cars is effortless.

Yulan is utterly perplexed by the changes happening within himself. What confuses him even more is that he suddenly becomes the target of a group of MAV special police officers who treat him as a serious threat. After narrowly escaping, Yulan eventually uncovers the truth - it turns out that a replica from another parallel dimension, the "bad Yulan," has illegally infiltrated this space. This bad Yulan has already killed several other replicas across different spaces, and with each kill, the powers of the remaining replicas increase. Now, the "bad Yulan" aims to eliminate the only surviving Yulan, seeking to annihilate him at any opportunity. The MAV has also been deceived by the "bad Yulan," believing that Yulan has become a deranged killer, leading to a massive manhunt against him.

Caught in a dilemma, Yulan must rally his spirit, evading capture by the MAV while simultaneously engaging in a battle of wits and strength with another version of himself...

Behind the Scenes:

The director and screenwriter of "The One" are well-known in the U.S., having created the popular sci-fi TV series "The X-Files," which broke viewership records. Their unique and imaginative writing style, especially their creative ideas about "parallel universes" and the relationships between characters across these spaces, strikes a balance between being grounded yet refreshingly novel. Combined with director James Wong's expertise in building suspense honed during his work on "The X-Files," "The One" promises not to disappoint fans of this genre.

After years of hard work in Hollywood, Jet Li carved out his niche with his rugged features and agile martial arts skills. From initially playing major villains in supporting roles to becoming the lead, and now portraying dual roles in this film, his share of screen time and popularity with audiences has risen alongside his skyrocketing paychecks. Following his $5.5 million salary for "The Kiss of the Dragon," it's rumored that Jet Li earned a staggering $7.5 million for "The One," plus additional global box office bonuses. Initially, the production team had intended to cast Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, who played "The Scorpion King" in "The Mummy Returns," as the main character. However, due to Johnson's sudden withdrawal, the crew turned to Jet Li immediately after his promotional tour for "The Kiss of the Dragon" ended, revising and tailoring the script specifically for him. Li's character in the film exudes Chinese cultural characteristics, both in personality and appearance, naturally incorporating his signature "Chinese Kung Fu." To distinguish between the two versions of Yulan in the film—one good and one evil—martial arts director Yuan Kwei designed two distinct sets of martial arts styles. The good Yulan practices the versatile and balanced Bagua boxing, while the evil Yulan wields the fierce and aggressive Xingyi boxing.
