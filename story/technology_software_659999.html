53% ABV Honghua Lang aged 10 years, 500ml. Market price: 705 RMB, Group-buying price: 428 RMB. The liquor has a rich aroma, mellow taste, aged flavor, sweet and refreshing finish, and a clean and comfortable aftertaste. The various fragrances are well-balanced, and its taste is closer to the Guiyang sauce-aroma style. Combining these two characteristics, it no longer has the slightly sour aftertaste of previous Lang wines, making it a highly cost-effective liquor.

The bottle shape of the Honghua Lang aged 10 years is carefully crafted through 105 processes, introducing British low-temperature red color, 24K sand gold floral burning, and 24K bright gold edge pressing. The bottle design features smooth and elegant lines with appropriate tension and relaxation. Its pure red color represents both restraint and passion, perfectly showcasing the exceptional taste of Honghua Lang aged 10 years.

Honghua Lang aged 10 years is one of the key series launched by Sichuan Langjiu Group Co., Ltd. It has the characteristics of rich aroma, mellow taste, aged flavor, sweetness, refreshment, clean and comfortable aftertaste, and an elegant style. On the basis of ensuring the original quality and style, the newly developed product innovates boldly in packaging, featuring novel and unique designs that break traditional boundaries and pioneer ultra-fashionable trends in baijiu packaging.

Honghua Lang aged 10 years uses rare glutinous red sorghum from the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau at an altitude of over 1,000 meters, natural Lang spring water from deep mountains below 1,000 meters, adheres to ancient brewing methods passed down for thousands of years, and undergoes fermentation involving billions of microorganisms. Each bottle of Honghua Lang wine goes through nine rounds of steaming, eight rounds of fermentation, seven rounds of distillation, a production cycle lasting a year, and cave storage periods of more than 10 or 15 years. After countless refinements, this results in a precious sauce-aroma liquor containing over 400 aromatic components, green and healthy. Honghua Lang aged 10 years is characterized by prominent sauce aroma, mellow and refreshing taste, elegant refinement, long-lasting aftertaste, and enduring fragrance in an empty glass.
