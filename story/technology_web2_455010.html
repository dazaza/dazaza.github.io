Determination Letter on Resolutely Completing the Civil Servants' "Smoking Quota" Task

by zxytyzzfe on 2011-07-01 13:26:54

Supreme Directive: The Hubei Public Security County Government issues an official document specifying the quantity of cigarettes for official use by various units. One such directive sets the annual smoking quota for all units in the county at 400 cartons, with a total plan of over 23,000 cartons for the entire county, calculated at a standard of 170 yuan per carton. This means that the county must spend nearly 4 million yuan on cigarettes annually to meet the target. Units that fail to complete their smoking plans will have their public expenses reduced. (Reported by Jingchu Network on May 3rd) Comrades, how do we judge whether a civil servant is competent? What is the standard? There is only one standard: whether they can maintain high consistency with their superiors and resolutely complete tasks assigned by their superiors. Now, our superiors have given us the glorious "smoking task." What kind of cigarettes to smoke, how many, and how to handle uncompleted tasks...the mission is clear, and the measures are effective. Now, my fellow civil servants, let's roll up our sleeves and smoke!

We must establish the belief of certain victory. With the wind blowing and war drums beating, who would fear whom in this era. If others can smoke 400 cartons, we have no reason to smoke less. It’s just smoking, we must not fall behind others. Smoke non-stop during work hours, and continue tirelessly at home in the evenings until the sky turns dark! Especially, leaders must set exemplary roles—smoke fast, rely on the leadership.

We must have the spirit of sacrifice. Smoking is harmful to health, and excessive smoking poses greater risks. However, since we are "public servants," we should possess a fearless spirit of sacrifice, overcoming all difficulties, moving forward courageously, smoking hard, smoking until death. Even if it results in serious health consequences, it doesn't matter—leaders won't forget us, the people won't forget us! I've heard that some places have also issued "drinking quotas" and shouted slogans like "Drinking is like battlefields, dedicating our stomachs to the Party." I've come up with a slogan for us: Smoking is like wind, not even one less cigarette is acceptable!

We must strictly adhere to principles. The "smoking task" is assigned to civil servants, so it should be independently completed by the civil servant corps. Absolutely, we cannot pass the responsibility to others or falsify records by giving the cigarettes to ordinary people to smoke. We must stick to the principle of "our own cigarettes we ourselves smoke," not cheating, not seeking shortcuts, ensuring the quality and quantity of completing the "smoking task."

Civil servant brothers, after a thousand days of training, now is the time to test us! We must resolutely complete the "smoking task," making the leaders feel reassured and satisfying the tobacco factory bosses. Since we are "public servants," we know the responsibilities are great; one person's hardship brings happiness to millions of families. Whether it's the "smoking task" or the "drinking task," whether it's the "massage task" or the "overseas inspection task," no matter how arduous the task is, we are not afraid! Please watch our actions, leaders!
