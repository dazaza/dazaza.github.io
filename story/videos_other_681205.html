What should I do if my baby gets enteritis? Enteritis is also a common problem for babies, and most parents will ask this question in daily life: what should I do if my baby gets enteritis? Below is a detailed answer provided by the experts of Zhengzhou Xiehe Hospital.

Enteritis is the most common disease for babies. When eating food, babies should mainly eat light and low-residue foods, eat more fruits and fresh vegetables, eat less or no fried foods, and should not eat too much. When the baby has diarrhea, the mother who is breastfeeding should not eat chicken, duck, fish, meat or strong-smelling vegetables. For babies drinking formula milk, it's best to consume formula milk made from skimmed powder.

What should I do if my baby gets enteritis?

1. Babies with severe vomiting and diarrhea should correct dehydration and electrolyte disorders in time. The best method is intravenous fluid replacement; for controlling infections, huangliansu (berberine) and lactobacillus can be given.

2. Pay attention to observing the frequency and shape of the baby's bowel movements in daily life. If necessary, stool tests can be repeated. Also, take care of the baby's skin on their bottom. After defecation, you can wash and dry with warm water, then apply talcum powder.

3. Stop all supplementary foods. When feeding, pay attention to gradually increasing the amount from small to large, chew slowly, eat small meals frequently. You can take oral lysine inositol solution, which can enhance the baby's resistance and improve the baby's appetite.

4. Pay more attention to dietary hygiene and reasonable feeding in daily life. If there are changes in weather conditions, timely attention should be paid to keeping the baby warm. Do not arbitrarily give the baby antibiotics to avoid intestinal flora imbalance leading to prolonged enteritis.
