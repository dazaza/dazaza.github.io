The Painful Experience of My First Online Shopping

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 13:34:41

When it comes to my first online shopping experience, it can only be described with four words - a complete disaster!

I've always enjoyed browsing online. Two years ago, I couldn't resist the temptation and applied for an Alipay卡通 (a type of Alipay account). However, I was completely clueless about this thing and didn't understand anything. So, I had to ask my best friend for help. She helped me apply for an email address, register a username, download a digital certificate, and install some plugins. After working on it for half a day, everything was finally ready. My best friend told me that I could start shopping anytime, so I kept thinking about finding an opportunity to try out online shopping.

Finally, I decided to buy a cross-stitch kit. Thinking that I could finally give it a try, I eagerly started chatting with the seller. After making the payment, a problem arose - I didn't know how to use Alipay.

Even my younger sister came online via QQ to offer advice. From 8 PM until 10 PM, I just couldn't complete the purchase, which made me extremely frustrated! Fortunately, the seller was quite accommodating and kept helping me. Eventually, she couldn't help but admire my clumsiness.

Having no other choice, I had to seek help from my best friend!

It was already past 10 PM, but as expected from a true best friend, she didn't hesitate and came over right away (thankfully, we lived close to each other). When she arrived and heard what happened, she let out a long sigh, thinking I had called her in the middle of the night because I was arguing with my husband and needed her to mediate! Haha.

She was truly my savior. As soon as she arrived, she quickly resolved the issue and completed the purchase. My admiration for her was boundless, like the endless flow of the Yellow River! After that experience, I decided never to use Alipay again. It was too troublesome. I would rather have my best friend help me shop than try to do it myself.

However, two years later, seeing everyone using Alipay, I temporarily forgot the lessons from my previous experience and went back to the bank to reapply for Alipay services. Once again, I had my best friend set everything up for me, but in the end, I still didn't know how to make a purchase. Sigh!! I had to ask my best friend for help again, and she bought me an eye essence. This time, I thought I would definitely be able to handle my next shopping trip on my own. But after only two days, my confidence was shattered. A colleague asked me to help buy a pair of pants, and I spent the entire evening trying but still couldn't figure it out.

This time, even I admired my own clumsiness. How could someone be as dumb as me?

I called my best friend again, and once more, she mistakenly thought I was arguing with my husband. Do I really look like someone who loves to argue with my husband?

My best friend had already gone to bed and couldn't come over, but she promised to help me the next day. After going through these few shopping experiences, I realized I should start using my brain a bit more. I couldn't keep being so clumsy! I looked up the reasons online by myself and, surprisingly, managed to fix the issue and successfully place an order. It was from Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd. Haha.

But then the seller informed me that I had overpaid. The item I selected had free shipping, but I paid before she could adjust the price, so I ended up paying too much.

I patiently asked the seller if the price could still be adjusted, and she said it couldn't be modified anymore. Sigh!!!!!!!!!!

There was nothing I could do but accept the seller's suggestion to receive a few extra gifts as compensation.

That's my real online shopping experience. Oh, my god.
