### **Introduction to Angel's Wings**

"Angel's Wings" is the last gift that Man Yin's father, Zhi Yuan, left for her. Zhi Yuan wanted Man Yin to learn the spirit of "Angel's Wings" and bravely face various trials of family and love.

In Episode 32 of *Angel's Wings*, Man Yin's grandmother, Su Yan Jun Ling, is a powerful businesswoman in the retail industry known as the "Empress." She highly values "successors" and "family background," despising the humble origins of her only daughter-in-law, Yu Feng. She spares no effort to break up her son's family. However, behind Su Yan Jun Ling's actions, Su Li Zhen, who has always regarded her brother Zhi Yuan as a rival, offers him a check to leave forever so she can secure the position of General Manager! To ensure his sister gets the recognition she deserves, Zhi Yuan chooses to leave... but tragically dies in a car accident while departing.

Zhi Yuan's death leads to the discovery of Man Yin, Su Yan Jun Ling's granddaughter, and intensifies her grandmother's desire to take Man Yin back. Refusing to lose her daughter, Yu Feng escapes with Man Yin, leading them to live a life on the run, hiding from the Su family.

In a small fishing village, Man Yin meets Lin Hao Yang, a brave young man who falls in love with her at first sight. Knowing about Man Yin's difficult circumstances, Hao Yang often helps her. In gratitude, Man Yin gives him the precious "Angel's Wings" that her father left her, sparking a deep bond between them.

### **Main Characters Introduction**

#### **Su Man Yin (played by Xia Ru Zhi)**

Man Yin is a warm, cheerful, and sunny girl. After the death of her beloved father Zhi Yuan, she struggles to understand why her mother must work as a hostess to survive, almost driving her to attempt suicide by jumping into the sea. Unaware that her mother has cancer, Man Yin is sent back to the Su family. Despite kneeling and pleading with her grandmother to be allowed to stay with her mother, she witnesses her mother dying in a fire. Devastated, Man Yin loses all her memories.

Her grandmother rebuilds Man Yin's past, making her believe she grew up under her grandmother's loving care. Mistakenly believing Yun Xiang is Hao Yang, she regards him as her true soulmate. Years later, she reunites with her childhood sweetheart Hao Yang and her biological mother, Yu Feng.

Despite being engaged to Yun Xiang, Man Yin feels inexplicably drawn to Hao Yang. On the night before her wedding, she is drunk and loses her virginity to Hao Yang. Heartbroken, Man Yin decides to marry Hao Yang to torment him for ruining her happiness.

#### **Lin Hao Yang (played by Dou Zhi Kong)**

Hao Yang exudes natural leadership wherever he goes. His pure first love with Man Yin nearly cost him his life. Later, after losing his mother in a fire, he becomes the adopted son of Yu Feng. Ten years later, they meet again, and Hao Yang still deeply loves her but cannot reveal that he is the person Man Yin once believed to be "the most important one."

On the night before Man Yin's wedding to Yun Xiang, Hao Yang drinks heavily due to heartbreak and doesn't remember what happened. He is confused when Man Yin suddenly breaks off her engagement and attempts to kill herself. Overwhelmed, Hao Yang decides to cut ties with Man Yin, only for her to propose marriage to him. Though initially elated, Hao Yang soon realizes this marriage will plunge him into an irreversible hell.

#### **Zhao Yun Xiang (played by Yang Yi Zhan)**

Before his mother's suicide, Yun Xiang was a gentle young man obedient to his father. After witnessing his mother's death due to suspected infidelity by his father, he transforms into a rebellious biker. Born into wealth as the son of a Miramar department store vendor, Yun Xiang lives a privileged life sheltered from hardship. With abundant resources, he transitions from a gentlemanly English nobleman to a playboy.

His father, Zhao Jian Hen, indulges Yun Xiang excessively to make up for neglecting him. No one dares to defy Yun Xiang—except Su Man Yin, the temporary housekeeper. Initially clashing, they eventually grow closer. For Yun Xiang, Cai Ni, his childhood friend, is like a sister, but Man Yin is his first love and the woman he desires. When Man Yin abruptly cancels her wedding and announces her marriage to Hao Yang, Yun Xiang retaliates by marrying Cai Ni on the same day, becoming Man Yin's brother-in-law.

#### **Su Yan Jun Ling (played by Chen Sha Li)**

A cold but internally warm-blooded CEO in the service industry, Su Yan Jun Ling is often referred to as the "Empress" of the retail world. She places immense importance on successors and family lineage, spending ten years waiting for her prodigal son Zhi Yuan to return. Despising the background of her only daughter-in-law, Yu Feng, she causes a tragic rift in the family. Yet, she reveals rare tenderness and sincerity only to little Man Yin.

After her son's accidental death, Su Yan Jun Ling spares no expense to reclaim her granddaughter. To give Man Yin a better life after losing her memory, she implants false memories and conceals everything about her real mother, Yu Feng. Unexpectedly, ten years later, Yu Feng reappears, dead set on regaining her lost life and taking over the company.

#### **Chen Yu Feng (played by Zhen Xiu Zhen)**

Once a small-time singer whose voice resembled Teresa Teng, Chen Yu Feng released a single record. Her love for Zhi Yuan was not accepted by the Su family, forcing her to become an unrecognized daughter-in-law living a wandering life outside the family. After Zhi Yuan's death and her diagnosis with cancer, she reluctantly sends Man Yin back to the Su family. Tragically, she survives a fire set by Hu De Chang, which disfigures her face. Determined to reclaim her daughter and her dignity, Yu Feng resolves to transform herself into a woman equal to the Su family, vowing to retrieve everything she has lost.
