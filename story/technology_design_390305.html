After talking about online store promotion, let's discuss the management of online stores. Management is the foundation of online stores and something store owners must master.

Let's first talk about the most concerning topic: How to increase store sales? Here we discuss from three aspects.

First: Product credibility

Not all products with beautiful pictures are good products. Now, "Rayli clothing" products look very nice, all models are beauties. Prices are not high, but do you know that many buyers find "Rayli" products are "cheap clothes from street vendors"? I believe many people have had this experience. Watching the pictures is indeed pleasing to the eye, but seeing the product makes you sad and cry. If you are the store owner, you are doomed: "bad review"!

So when finding products, don't just focus on appearance, but also on the substantial credibility of the product. Good products don't necessarily need a lot of beauties to take pictures. Instead, relatively successful online stores have rather simple products.

Second: Product price

Note - Product price is not necessarily better when lower; it should be proportional to product quality. Generally, people think low-priced products are easier to sell. In fact, this is not the case. The most important thing is whether the price matches the product quality.

For example: This pair of women's shoes is priced very low at 15 yuan, while another online store prices it at 45 yuan. The pictures look similar. If you were a buyer, which one would you buy? Understanding customer psychology is crucial. "Rather buy the expensive one than the unreliable one." Can 15 yuan buy good shoes? Such cases are countless.

The price of a product is determined by the store owner, and it requires some consideration. For instance, popular online clothing is priced between 50-200 yuan, so your pricing could be between 70-150 yuan. Higher pricing might affect sales. But why not price lower? The reason is that the main group of buyers who shop online are: white-collar office workers, private computer users, company secretaries, salespeople from physical stores... These people generally prefer fashionable and elegant or branded high-end products. Products below 50 yuan are hard for them to even mention. For example, 15-yuan shoes might seem like rural market stall shoes to them, completely ignored!

Therefore, the price of a product is determined by the store owner, and it's not necessarily better when priced low.

Third: Product variety

Many newly opened online stores want to sell multiple types of products. Women's bags, women's shoes, clothing, cosmetics, etc., all want to sell. Thus, the newly opened online store becomes chaotic, seemingly lively. In fact, this is wrong. It is suggested that the product categories should not exceed two, or even just one. The reason is simple: too many product categories make it obviously an unprofessional "general store." Buyers prefer to purchase single-category products from specialized stores. At this point, even if your products are of good quality or low price, they will be overlooked due to customer doubts. In fact, online stores sometimes resemble physical stores. Looking around at physical stores nearby explains why "specialty stores" and "chain stores" are so popular.

The above three aspects are the most basic. Now let's further elaborate and specify. These are accumulated experiences, and to deeply understand the art of operation, one still needs to explore and discover more in real combat. Below, I summarize 14 points, hoping they will be helpful to everyone.

1. Selling goods online, most of the time buyers cannot see the actual item, so clear and attractive product photos need to be taken, along with detailed product descriptions. This will attract buyers more effectively. If the product photos in your online store are unclear and the descriptions are too brief, it will be difficult to attract buyers.

2. The range of products in the online store should be complete, updated quickly. What is meant by complete range here is not selling all kinds of goods, but the range of a single category of goods. For example, if we only sell women's clothing, then tops, pants, skirts, blazers, etc., should all be available, allowing customers to mix and match freely. Also, new arrivals should be prominently displayed on the online store, but quantity should not be increased at the expense of quality. Do not stop restocking due to poor sales. If the products in the online store always remain the same, lacking novelty, buyers will not visit again after browsing a few times.

3. After-sales service should be thorough. After selling a product, contact the buyer as soon as possible. Taking Taobao as an example, fill in the shipping order number as soon as possible after shipping, so that the buyer can track the logistics immediately upon checking the order, giving the buyer peace of mind right away. Alternatively, send the buyer a shipping notification letter, preferably attaching a photo of the package receipt, allowing the buyer to clearly see the handwriting and specific number, making the buyer feel more secure and closer. This is important for attracting repeat customers.

4. Pay attention to the "credit rating" evaluation of the online store. Credit rating evaluations may vary across different platforms, but they are generally similar. Here, taking Taobao as an example, after a transaction is completed, both the buyer and seller have the obligation to evaluate each other's credit. High credit ratings are crucial for online store operations, as all buyers will use credit ratings to decide whether to buy from your store. Additionally, measures such as "unconditional returns within seven days" and "advance compensation" related to credit, if feasible, should be implemented, as these can sometimes be more effective than so-called credit levels.

5. Honesty comes first. Operating online, the most important thing is honesty. Online, each seller has a record of honesty that buyers can see, showing the seller's previous sales status and evaluations from other buyers. Online records document the honesty of any seller, making it difficult for dishonest people to operate online. Honest sellers can still sell their products even if their prices are slightly higher.

6. Initially, to increase popularity, consider setting up one-yuan auctions or selling at low prices to complete transactions. Having a large number of transaction records and positive reviews will make more buyers trust your store and shop there. One-yuan auctions can gather popularity in a short time, but be prepared to sell the item for one yuan. While conducting one-yuan auctions, it's best to combine them with some advertising to let everyone know about your "discount" policy. The purpose of the one-yuan auction is to attract buyers to casually browse your other products through this item.

7. Adopt some effective promotional strategies, offering regular discounts or gifts. Give discounts to returning customers, or offer gifts or discounts when shopping reaches a certain amount, or offer free shipping, etc. In conjunction with the event, change your signature to introduce the event and post the "promotional news" elsewhere. 8. Give small gifts as extras. To gain more positive reviews, do not let the buyer know beforehand. When they receive the item and gift you sent, they will certainly be happy. The gift doesn't have to be valuable, but it represents your goodwill. Regardless of whether your buyer will continue to shop in the future, treat them as friends, face them sincerely, and you will surely get good returns.

9. Calmly handle buyers who bid but do not buy. In online stores, encountering buyers who bid but do not buy is normal. As a seller, maintain a peaceful mindset and avoid conflicts with buyers. Since we sellers aim for long-term operation, while buyers can come and go freely, the party that suffers most in a conflict is us sellers. All problems can be resolved through proper channels, and only in this way can we face increasingly fierce competition in online stores.

10. Calmly handle bad reviews. Sometimes due to our own reasons or other factors, bad reviews may appear in our credit records. Face them calmly, take corresponding remedial measures, or explain the true situation online. At the same time, strive to gain more positive reviews to create a positive impression on buyers.

11. To determine whether a particular product sells well, you can first search for it on the website. See how many people are selling similar products, including identical or similar ones, check their sales situations, how product prices are set, how stores are designed, and how products are introduced, etc. This market research and analysis work can be described as "know thyself and know thy enemy, and thou shalt win a hundred battles without peril."

12. Running an online store should be done step by step. The first three months of opening a store are usually for adapting to online store operations and gathering popularity. During this time, learn from experienced sellers, adjust your operations according to the market in a timely manner, and accumulate some customers. These customers are your friends and the most meaningful people, so serve them well. Good service to old customers will continuously bring you new customers. Gradually, the operation of the online store will develop positively.

13. "Online bidding" attracts more browsers than "fixed price." However, bidding sometimes may result in being sold at a low price. In such cases, some store owners may ask their friends to participate in the bidding. This allows the price to be bid up to an acceptable amount, and if the buyers' bids are all very low, their friend can win the bid. This practice is a non-credible behavior and is not recommended. As long as you operate honestly, your store's popularity will grow stronger.

14. Running an online store requires "eyes in all directions and ears in all places," accurately grasping market conditions. While capturing information online, also take time to visit physical markets, and don't limit your vision to the internet alone.

Second category: Women's clothing and shoes

Compared to cosmetics, women's clothing and shoes have advantages and disadvantages. The styles of women's clothing and shoes are varied, and no matter how big your store is, it's impossible to include all styles and designs. Customers may browse through and leave if they don't find something they particularly like.

Advantages: 1. Extremely large consumer base; 2. High demand; 3. No expiry or spoilage issues.
