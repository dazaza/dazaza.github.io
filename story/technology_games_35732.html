Breaking Gravity and Riding the Wind - "Windwheel" Internal Testing within 20 Days

by 56963tg on 2007-11-19 14:31:43

The weather is getting colder day by day, but in this gentle autumn breeze, "Midstream" is about to detonate a super bomb - a new casual competitive game named 《Skate Wind》 (also called xplay) will start internal testing on November 20th! Fans who love sports and seek extreme sensory enjoyment are about to welcome a new wave of fashion. This game draws on the best elements of the most popular, explosive, thrilling, and entertaining sport in the world today: X-Games! It presents these elements to players, plus the powerful collaboration between South Korean developer KTH and domestic veteran operator China Game Center, which is sure to bring an extraordinary thrilling experience! Break through gravity, fly into the wind!

Challenge Extreme Speed

Friends who have played roller skates or regularly watched X-Games competitions must be deeply impressed by that exciting way of movement. The halfpipe skateboarding competition in X-Games is a must-watch program for fans every episode. Flashy moves, unique skills, dazzling to the extreme! But now, Skate Wind has moved the venue from the halfpipe to the streets!! Wow, just thinking about it gets you excited. Stairs, corridors, railings, any place that can be jumped on will become your stage, and you can use all kinds of flashy tricks to impress the audience and challenge yourself.

The game allows players to perform various flashy moves by combining the A, S, D, shift keys with direction keys. Using fancy moves will give an acceleration effect, so if you want to come in first, it depends on whether you can show off your cool moves throughout the race.

Individualistic Characters

《Skate Wind》 (Xplay) offers many game characters for players to choose from, each character having a unique style.

There are clever and cute little girls, mischievous little rascals, sexy and charming older sisters, alternative and rebellious sunshine boys, and even grandpas and grandmas joining in. If you like, that 71-year-old grandpa (why does he look so familiar?) will surely make you the most eye-catching focus on the field.

Speed Race & Prop Race

Speed Race

The speed race is the standard competition mode. In this mode, players need to rely entirely on their own skills to win, making it a very challenging mode that tests both player operation and skill. It's also the mode that best demonstrates the player's value. With some good practice, becoming a speed skating king is just around the corner.

Prop Race

The prop race is a more casual and entertaining mode where players can obtain props to play the game. There are many types of props available in the game, many with unexpected effects, which will surely keep you entertained.

In addition to the basic game modes, there are options for team races and individual races. Players can invite a few family and friends to compete together.

Rich Game Maps

Since the competition has been moved from the halfpipe to the streets, wouldn't it be a disappointment if there weren't some special features? The game won't disappoint you. There are dozens of tracks to choose from, such as Hong Kong's famous Nathan Road, Mong Kok Street, and even elevated highways and railways. Isn't that exciting? If you haven't been to Hong Kong, after a couple of rounds, you might already be familiar with Mong Kok Street. Ha ha.

Internal Test Rewards

The internal test lasts for one month, with 200 internal test numbers and rich gifts waiting for you to grab. Players can apply on the Midstream internal test number application page. Each ID card is limited to applying for one internal test number, along with Midstream account verification. Equipment, massive amounts of game coins, premium roller skates from Skate Wind, T-shirts, and laptops, giving you surprises every day!

For more information, please follow Midstream's 《Skate Wind》 official website.
