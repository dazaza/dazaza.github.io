How is genital herpes treated in Taiyuan?

by gjhuy on 2012-02-14 15:33:52

How to treat genital herpes in Taiyuan? Genital herpes is a sexually transmitted disease that tends to recur and can cause significant harm to the human body. Experts point out that necessary dietary restrictions are essential to prevent recurrence. Eating spicy or stimulating foods, smoking, and drinking alcohol are all detrimental to recovery. Drinking alcohol, in particular, can trigger a recurrence of the condition and exacerbate symptoms. Therefore, it is crucial not to drink alcohol after being diagnosed. Consuming foods rich in vitamins and proteins, such as fresh vegetables, fruits, milk, and eggs, can aid in the recovery process.

Simultaneously, when treating genital herpes, patients can also undergo auxiliary treatment at home.

How to treat genital herpes at home? Here are some expert tips for patients:

1. Crush an appropriate amount of coinleaf beggarticks (Ban Zhi Lian) and apply it to the affected area twice daily.

2. Mash an appropriate amount of purslane into a paste and apply it externally to the affected area, changing the dressing once daily.

3. Wash and crush several fresh fig leaves, add an appropriate amount of vinegar, mix evenly into a clay-like consistency, and apply it to the affected area, replacing it when dry.

4. Grind 30 grams each of rhubarb, coptis, and phellodendron, along with 15 grams each of myrrh and frankincense, into fine powder. When using, mix with sesame oil into a paste and apply it to the sores once daily.

Home treatment is merely an auxiliary measure for genital herpes. Therefore, patients should still seek regular medical treatment at a hospital to avoid misunderstandings about the disease and prevent its worsening.
