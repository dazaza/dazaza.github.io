Guilin - Yangshuo - Crown Cave - Gudong Oxygen Bar Two Nights and Three Days Eco-tour

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-30 09:10:18

Itinerary Arrangement:

Day 1:

In the morning, arrive at Guilin and visit the city emblem of Guilin - Elephant Trunk Hill. The entire hill resembles a giant elephant standing by the riverside drinking water, vividly fascinating. In the afternoon, visit Guilin's largest comprehensive park - Seven Star Park, Longyin Rock, Longyin Cave cliff carvings, Eight Hundred Heroes' Grave, Guihai Stele Forest. Stay in Guilin.

Guilin Tour, Yangshuo Tour, Lijiang River

Day 2:

After breakfast, take a boat to tour the hundred-mile long gallery of Lijiang River scenery (Mopan Mountain - Yangshuo). The clouds flow as the boat sails, Nine Dragons Playing with a Pearl, Langshi Misty Rain, Eight Immortals Crossing the River. The myriad green peaks on both banks are reflected in the river, breathtakingly beautiful. After landing, stroll along the unique cobblestone streets of West Street, experiencing the unique European charm and feeling the exotic atmosphere of foreigners. Stay in Guilin.

Day 3:

After breakfast, take a vehicle from the hotel to Guanyan Cave. Peaks compete in beauty, clear water conveys affection, and the cave has marvelous rocks. Appreciate the marvelously unparalleled "Four Wonders of Guanyan" - "Sky Window", "Sea Roar", "Snow Stone", "Golden Feather Harp". Experience the endless fun of "Sea, Land, Air" three-dimensional tourism with trains, drifting, and elevators. In the afternoon, take a vehicle to Gudong Waterfall: the only multi-level serial waterfall in the country formed by underground springs. There are eight waterfalls and nine pools, forests, and streams within the area, creating a primitive ecosystem that makes the air fresh and sweet, allowing you to fully enjoy the natural oxygen bar. With maple trees providing shade, winding paths lead to secluded spots, and waterfalls cascading down nearly a hundred meters from the mountain top, it is truly breathtaking and incredibly beautiful. After returning to Guilin, visit Zhengyang Pedestrian Street: which has the bustling atmosphere of Beijing temple fairs and the charm of Yangshuo's West Street. After dinner, return to conclude the pleasant journey!
