Zhang Yuqi speaks out after the emotional change: no sourness, no worries, no recklessness

2010-11-3 09:09 | Keywords: emotional change, Zhang Yuqi, speak out

"Anyway, I am very grateful for that relationship because it gave me a lot of happiness. We were together because we were happy, and we separated because we were not as happy. So, whether we are together or apart, it was the right choice. I really hope he can start a new happy life, otherwise I would still be worried."

The past six months have been eventful for Zhang Yuqi. In March, Wang Xiaofei wrote a blog to prove that he had no connection with Zhang Ziyi and said he deeply loved his girlfriend Zhang Yuqi; on April 25th, they both appeared hand-in-hand at a charity dinner; later they were photographed kissing at the airport; in early September, Zhang Yuqi wanted to terminate her contract with the company that made her famous, Starlight; when people speculated that Wang Xiaofei would help her with the lawsuit, by the end of October, Wang Xiaofei was suddenly engaged to Big S (Faye Wong), leaving Zhang Yuqi alone. Amidst all this public debate, Zhang Yuqi was photographed "drowning her sorrows in alcohol." There were even rumors that Zhang Yuqi's new boyfriend is Liu Tao's husband, Wang Ke... Facing increasingly intense rumors, Zhang Yuqi told our reporter yesterday, "I am full of gratitude for the current situation."

"Saving" Love - Not wanting everyone to misunderstand Wang Xiaofei

Beijing News: Regarding the public attention on Faye Wong and Wang Xiaofei's relationship and some of your affairs, Wang Fei's Weibo post (http://t.sina.com.cn) was quite interesting, saying "being calm also has its limits, beware of being so calm that everything becomes tasteless, so stable that everything suffocates." What do you think?

Zhang Yuqi: There is nothing about being calm or not calm. It's because we have been apart for a while, and this matter has nothing to do with me anymore. So when this thing came up, I felt it necessary to briefly explain our actual situation to everyone, to prevent them from misunderstanding him. Besides giving him my blessings, I really don't know what else to say.

Beijing News: You responded on Weibo saying Wang Xiaofei is an outstanding man, are you really that magnanimous?

Zhang Yuqi: Anyway, I am very grateful for that relationship because it once gave me a lot of happiness. We were together because we were happy, and we separated because we were not as happy. So, whether we are together or apart, it was the right choice. I really hope he can start a new happy life, otherwise I would still be worried.

Beijing News: But there are photos online showing you drunk, saying you were feeling down and drowning your sorrows in alcohol.

Zhang Yuqi: During the filming of "White Deer Plain," I drank with Teacher Zhang Fengyi and those guys, they should know the drinking capacity of Shandong girls (laughs). Actually, this time back in Beijing, I just wanted to relax, finding friends to drink a bit is normal, whether we break up or not, we can still drink, right? But because of this incident, people naturally associate it with other things.

Beijing News: How is your real state now?

Zhang Yuqi: I'm fine, meaning everything is basically normal. I prefer my current situation, it feels more grounded and free than before. Feelings are personal matters, whether they're good or bad, only oneself knows, there's no need to talk too much about them. All I can say is that I am full of gratitude for everything around me.

"New" Affection - If I have a new boyfriend, I will openly admit it.

Beijing News: In just half a year, from terminating contracts to breaking up, many things have happened to you. To outsiders, you seem unlucky, how do you see it?

Zhang Yuqi: Honestly, now for me, it is an important moment in life. Because taking part in such an important movie as "White Deer Plain" is the biggest opportunity since I started acting, I value it very much. Including the recent shooting in Hailar, with its vast sky and land, clean air, my mood was very pleasant. Working with talented people from all over the world also made me feel delighted. Director Wang Quan'an's requirements are very high, but he is also very fun, often making everyone laugh on set. He is someone who uses subtraction to face things, simplicity brings happiness, many people don't understand this simple truth, he let me experience it, I thank him.

Beijing News: Now there are still many rumors about your love life, like the rumor that you and Wang Ke (Wang Ke married Liu Tao in 2008, and they already have a pair of children)?

Zhang Yuqi: Love is sweet and happy, of course worth pursuing by everyone, so I will definitely have new love. But it will absolutely not be any one of the people currently being guessed at randomly. This is not something that must be completed as a performance, it doesn't have to rush to start and show off to everyone. Such things cannot be compromised, better to lack than to settle for less. So, I really need to clarify here, unless I personally tell everyone, otherwise, starting from now, any rumored or guessed so-called new boyfriend is impossible, continuing to spread such rumors is boring and uninteresting. I just want to say, once I have a new boyfriend, I will frankly tell everyone, because it is a good thing.

Beijing News: Do you consider matters of the heart now?

Zhang Yuqi: Whether to talk about feelings or not, it is all for gaining happiness. I already understand clearly, true happiness comes from within, not finding another person. The reason I say this is because I have more recognition and discovery of myself.

Ex-boyfriend's "romance" tracking

Wang Xiaofei denies paying a 6 million yuan breakup fee

According to reports, Wang Xiaofei recently denied through Weibo: "Anything should have its limit, fabricating baseless stories has formed a personal attack against us. Fabricating marriage and divorce is absurd, not to mention owing child support fees. On the other side, they are also fabricating that I paid Zhang Yuqi 6 million yuan for the breakup, how illogical are these story fabricators? Suddenly portraying me as a penniless man with only 1000 yuan, then turning into a millionaire carrying hundreds of thousands of cash! Ridiculous!"

In addition, reporters learned from the "White Deer Plain" production team that Zhang Yuqi also finds the claim of a 6 million yuan breakup fee ridiculous and stated she would not pay attention to it.

Wang's mother denounces defamation

According to reports, yesterday, Wang Xiaofei's mother, the owner of "Qiao Jiangnan" Group, Zhang Lan, also issued a statement regarding the rumors, clarifying false reports, stating:

"For the recent individual media reports claiming Wang Xiaofei was once married, I am deeply shocked. Because this is completely unfounded and false reporting. As a mother, I hereby solemnly clarify this matter. For all existing and possible future false reports, we reserve the right to pursue responsibility through legal means. As a mother, I fully trust my child's judgment, respect their choices, and sincerely wish them well. At the same time, I hope everyone can give them space."
