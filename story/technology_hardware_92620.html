Fur accessories highlight the five fashion elements of fur.

by violet0807 on 2008-09-17 16:09:20

This season, the short fur coat with an unrestrained flavor has become the main clothing item for fashion girls. Its coolness and wildness fully display the tenderness and personality of women.

According to consumer feedback, short fur outfits are not only gorgeous but also elegant and charming. They highlight a woman's personality and luxury when paired with various leather boots. Whether it's a black fur-trimmed short outfit or a small fur combined with a wide waist design, they all display a national style in luxury, showcasing the grace and charm of women.

Colorful fur continues to be popular

Color coordination still plays the leading role this season, but it's not necessarily true that colorful fur clothing suits everyone. Consumers are more aware of what suits them this season, and reasonable matching with clothing and skin tone helps women no longer get confused by too many choices. This season, the trend of colored fur continues from last season, especially since consumers focus on fur around the face area, so color selection is based on one's complexion. Of course, purple becomes the main color of the season.

Shawls display individual attire

Fur can bring out the noble, elegant, and luxurious aristocratic temperament of women. Fur shawls as accents on clothing are also an important fashion focus that cannot be ignored.

In recent years, with the popularity of fur clothing, personalized dressing attracts more attention. Women wearing fur shawls understand themselves better and know the art of wearing fur well. This season, small and casual fur shawls have more approachability. Mixing and matching with different materials and styles of clothes, the ultimate charm of fur spreads from the shoulders to the neckline.

Special decorations brighten up fur

There are many highlights in the decorative design of fur this season, which have won the approval of many consumers.

Laser engraving, computer spray painting, and diamond-encrusted designs on collars, cuffs, lapels, hems, and sleeve sides have become the trend in style design. The use of metallic accessories further highlights the noble quality of fur, showing the unique lifestyle taste of women. Moreover, various decorative belts worn at the waist add much unique flavor to fur clothing.

Fur accessories are gradually improving

Not all women like exaggerated luxury. The decoration of partial fur brings unique charm for women to showcase their elegance in winter.

Fur scarves that combine warmth and style, long boots adorned with soft fur, fur handbags hanging on the waist, and fur bows draped around the neck will all add the finishing touch for ladies who understand how to match their outfits. Why not buy some of these fur accessories this season to make your attire stand out?
