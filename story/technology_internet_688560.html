On the 24th, the quotations for the three major components: memory prices rise again, CPU price increase

by anonymous on 2012-03-03 11:31:28

Today, in the Intel processor spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, the prices of Intel SNB processors and car decoders have risen across the board, with an increase ranging from 5 to 50 yuan, mostly between 10 and 30 yuan. The price increase for Intel's low-end processors is relatively small, offering better cost-performance compared to AMD, especially noteworthy are the loose-piece G530 and G620 dual-core processors.

Today, in the AMD processor spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, the prices of AMD processors have risen again, including car decoders, car interference devices, car unlocking tools, and car remote control decoders. The old 45nm Athlon that once experienced continuous price drops has become the main force in this price hike, gradually losing its attractiveness. Today's price increase ranges from 5 to 15 yuan, mostly between 10 and 15 yuan.

Today, in the desktop memory spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, memory prices have risen again, with the 2GB/1333 experiencing the fastest price increase. Kingston leads the price hike, while the 4GB/1333 by ADATA remains at 105 yuan without following suit. Merchants indicate that Samsung DDR3-1600 and ADATA 8GB/1333 memories have been launched, making it harder to sell the 2GB/1333 memory due to its price increase.

Today, in the laptop memory spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, there is no change in the prices of notebook memory (car decoders). Due to the longer sales cycle of notebook memory, its price drop speed is not as fast as desktop memory. The Kingston 4GB/1333 notebook memory is quoted at 115 yuan. ADATA and Apacer 4GB/1333 notebook memory are quoted between 112-113 yuan.

Today, in the desktop hard drive spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, overall hard drive prices remain stable. With the end of the early stockpiling and speculation of hard drives, the continuous slight price drop of hard drives over the past week has been curbed, gradually aligning with Hong Kong's general distributor prices.

Today, in the laptop hard drive spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, Hitachi notebook hard drive prices remain stable. The single-platter 500GB/5400rpm notebook hard drive is quoted at 510 yuan; the dual-platter 500GB is quoted at 500 yuan.

Intel's SNB series has seen a full-line price increase, with the highest increase reaching 50 yuan. Today, in the Intel processor spot market of Zhongguancun in Beijing, the prices of Intel SNB processors have increased across the board, with an increase ranging from 5 to 50 yuan, mostly between 10 and 30 yuan. The price increase for Intel's low-end processors is relatively small, offering better cost-performance compared to AMD, especially noteworthy are the loose-piece G530 and G620 dual-core processors.
