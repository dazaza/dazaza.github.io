In order to upgrade the tourism level of Laizhou, expand its fame and brand influence, the city accelerates the development of the natural scenery of "one zone, one river, three mountains" in the South Sea area, Wulong River, Jingqi Mountain, Laozhai Mountain, and Niangniang Mountain. It has launched an eco-leisure tour as a fine tourist route. They have seized the major opportunity of the provincial planning and construction of the Dingziwan Seaside New City. In the southern coastal areas, they successively launched a number of fine tourist projects such as the Jinshan Tourist Resort Area, Wetland Park, Ancient Town Film Base, etc., improving the supporting service facilities such as tourist service centers, resort hotels, catering and entertainment, tourist transportation, etc. Since the official launch of the "South Sea Tourism Experience Journey" in early October 2009, it has attracted a large number of tourists for leisure vacations. The Niangniang Mountain Tourist Area highlights the characteristics of Taoist culture. At the beginning of this year, it launched the theme tourism activity of "Happy New Year Celebration, Praying for Blessings at Niangniang Mountain". It attracts numerous believers through various forms such as temple fairs, bell ringing, incense burning, praying for blessings, and welcoming auspiciousness.

Laizhou's rural folk custom tours are the vanguard of Laizhou tourism. The city has fully utilized its unique "Pear Dragon Fist" cultural resources to develop the tourism industry and raise the banner of Laizhou tourism. Pear culture, as the most regionally distinctive cultural resource of Laizhou, has been carefully packaged into a tourism product. The city has taken advantage of the first Laizhou Pear Culture Festival to increase the overall packaging and promotion of the Pear Country Folklore Tourism Area, continuously upgrading the construction standards of the scenic area, making this tourism area become the first fine cultural tourism project. During the first Laizhou Pear Culture Festival alone, the Pear Country Folklore Tourism Area received more than 80 tourist groups and over 80,000 visitors, generating over 5 million yuan in tourism revenue. Laizhou is rich in dinosaur fossil resources. The city cleverly utilizes the reputation of "China's Dinosaur Hometown" and its precious dinosaur fossil resources to carefully package the Dinosaur Geological Park Tourism Development Project, which is currently being promoted nationwide for investment. Laizhou is the birthplace of the Chinese intangible cultural heritage, the Mantis Fist. To better inherit the mantis fist cultural heritage and promote the development of Laizhou tourism, the city has deeply explored and effectively integrated the mantis fist culture. Laizhou Mantis Fist has attracted many domestic and foreign martial arts enthusiasts to come and learn.

Rural folk custom tours are another fine tourism project launched by Laizhou based on its unique local customs and conditions. The most famous ones are the characteristic new rural tours of Zhuo Village and the pear country folklore tours of Xitaozhang Village. Zhuo Village focuses on the theme of "Four Seasons Flower Sea New Countryside" for sightseeing, creating the Zhuo Village Characteristic Agriculture Tourism Area. It has successively established more than ten scenic spots such as the Hundred Flowers Garden Scenic Area and the Butterfly Orchid Cultivation Base, receiving up to 40,000 tourists annually. Xitaozhang, a rising star, relies on the unparalleled natural resource of a ten-thousand-mu pear orchard, carefully cultivating a tourist sightseeing scenic area. By adopting a free-to-charge model, it quickly expands its fame and becomes a great place for people to enjoy pears, pick pears, taste pears, and evaluate pears during their leisure time. Both Zhuo Village and Xitaozhang were awarded the title of "Shandong Province's Characteristic Tourism Village" in 2009. In just one year in 2009, Laizhou's tourism saw both the number of visitors and tourism income grow by over 100%, almost surprising everyone. How did Laizhou tourism achieve leapfrog development? This should be attributed to the strong promotion of the "three major fine tours" that the city has created: characteristic cultural tours, eco-leisure tours, and rural folk custom tours.
