Hubei Provincial Agricultural Science and Technology People's Benefit Golden-headed Centipede Breeding Farm was personally founded by Mr. Luo Yonglin in 1996, specializing in the breeding of medicinal animals (centipedes). The farm integrates scientific research, breeding, reproduction, processing, and sales into a large-scale professional breeding farm. It offers free centipede breeding technology training classes all year round. Our farm adopts its own invented three-dimensional high-efficiency breeding method, an exclusive domestic first-of-its-kind innovation that integrates breeding and planting, gaining widespread recognition from many breeders.

The centipede is a traditional and precious Chinese medicine in our country, with the functions of dispelling wind, relieving pain, unblocking meridians, and detoxification. In recent years, due to changes in the ecological environment, wild resources have been decreasing year by year, while the annual export volume has increased, leading to a very tight supply of centipedes, with prices rising year by year. This provides a very good opportunity for artificial centipede breeding. Centipedes are carnivorous and omnivorous animals, with a wide range of feed options, strong adaptability, and relatively simple artificial breeding methods. Men, women, young and old can breed them indoors or outdoors during their spare time, without polluting the environment or affecting the relationship with neighbors. It is currently a good project for getting rich.

After more than ten years of development, the annual production of commercial centipedes at the Agricultural Science and Technology People's Benefit Centipede Breeding Farm has exceeded ten million. We now have branch factories in Nanjing, Jiangsu, Nanning, Guangxi, Hangzhou, Zhejiang, and Shijiazhuang, Hebei. We welcome you to visit and inspect.

Supported by Huazhong Agricultural University: A key university under the national "211 Project" - Huazhong Agricultural University, together with Wuhan Agricultural Science and Technology People's Benefit Bioengineering Co., Ltd., jointly studies and is committed to scientific farming and enriching farmers through centipede breeding technology: People's Benefit No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede.

Advanced Technical Advantages: Wuhan Agricultural Science and Technology People's Benefit Bioengineering Co., Ltd., along with multiple biological experts and bioengineering professors from Huazhong Agricultural University, after years of breeding observation, using advanced purification techniques and biological genetic improvement engineering, has cultivated a new generation of varieties - People's Benefit No. 2 Golden-headed Centipede. Through feeding observations, this variety is easy to raise, gentle in nature, strong in adaptability, fast-growing, highly reproductive, with better medicinal value and even more significant economic value, making it an ideal investment breeding project for many breeders.

Complete Breeding Facilities: With agricultural science as an advantage, we expand our vision. With life sciences as a characteristic, relying on experts and scholars from the Animal Science and Technology College, focusing on modern bioengineering, special breeding, and insect resource development, we are a high-tech enterprise integrating scientific research, breeding, teaching, promotion, technical services, production, sales, and import-export trade. We have large ecological breeding areas, solar greenhouse breeding areas, greenhouse breeding areas, etc., and separate technical training centers, seedling breeding centers, seed source rooms, incubation rooms, gene transfer rooms, etc., with excellent foundations and complete systems.

Wuhan Agricultural Science and Technology People's Benefit Company: Upholding the tradition of diligent reading and hardworking farming, and establishing oneself to help others, our company takes scientific farming as its purpose, and promotes the industrial development of centipede resources with the business philosophy of integrity, dedication, pragmatism, innovation, and win-win. We will be dedicated to providing broader rural friends with higher-quality and higher-yield seedlings, more advanced technology, and more perfect services, allowing us to keep pace with the times and jointly create a more brilliant future.
