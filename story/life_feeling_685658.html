Cherish the Love in Front of You

In fact, the sky is very blue, dark clouds will always disperse. In fact, the sea is not wide, from this shore to the other shore. In fact, dreams are shallow. Everything follows its natural course. In fact, life can be sweet, but things don't always go as I wish. Life doesn't last more than a few decades; success and failure, honor and disgrace are all up to heaven. Since ancient times, there has been endless suffering in the world. If you have a broad perspective, your realm will be like that of an immortal.

I once deeply loved a girl who was not what you would call a great beauty. She didn't have an extraordinary appearance. Her hair wasn't black and smooth, her skin wasn't very white, she wasn't very tall, and her figure wasn't perfect. In short, she was just an ordinary girl.

But in my eyes, she was the most beautiful. Our relationship lasted for a very short time, like a fleeting dream. Due to our busy lives, we didn't meet many times, mostly communicating over the phone. However, her every smile and gesture deeply attracted me, especially her sweet smile which I will never forget.

Perhaps this could be called love at first sight. Perhaps it's a bit absurd. Perhaps, it's all because I'm too infatuated.

I remember falling in love with her during our first date. I wanted to give her something, but she refused everything. Eventually, after my persistent insistence, she liked a big teddy bear. To please her, I chose a large and beautiful one for her. But she didn't want it, instead choosing a smaller and cheaper one. She said the bigger one was too expensive and she liked this one better. Maybe my expectations weren't high. Right at that moment, I decided that I would cherish her with all my life's love because she really was a good girl.

What is the most beautiful thing in this world?

Perhaps it is the things that no matter how hard you try, you can never obtain them, making them the most precious.

She was the first girl I fell in love with. It might be due to my lack of experience in love or maybe because I was too foolish. Although I had made up my mind, I didn't know how to cherish or how to love.

It seems that fate really favored me. He didn't want me to easily get this love. Not long after we confirmed our relationship, she said, "Let's just be ordinary friends." At that time, I was still immersed in the ocean of love. This was like a bolt of lightning to me. Her reason was that we lacked common language.

[Common Language] What precious four words! I locked myself in the room, lay on the bed for three days and nights, without eating a grain of rice or drinking a drop of water [this is a true story]. The result was still unclear. During these three days, my mobile phone had 27 missed calls. How much I hoped one of them was hers, but none were.

Why? Why did I sincerely and earnestly love someone only to receive nothing but pain and sorrow in return? What exactly did I do wrong? Why am I being punished so severely? Am I not good enough? Not handsome enough? Or not rich enough?

If you say I'm not good enough, I'd really like to show you my heart. If I'm not handsome enough, I ask myself if I'm acceptable. If you say I'm not rich enough, then I'm working on it. I'm not afraid of hardship or fatigue, and I have ambition. Is this not enough? Is money really that important?

Time always flows silently. Before I knew it, a year had passed. I thought with the passage of time, I would forget. But in my mind, your shadow, your sweet smile, remains as clear as ever.

Since she left, I, who never smoked, have taken cigarettes as my friend. They are indispensable every day. Whenever I think of you, I want to get drunk. But after getting drunk, I wake up again. After reading the following story, "The Story of Aquarius,"

A long, long time ago, a bottle drifted in the vast ocean. This bottle had been drifting in the boundless sea for a very long time. Its only wish was to encounter someone it could fall in love with during its limited life. Many years passed, and finally one day, the bottle met a little goldfish. The goldfish was very innocent and cute.

The bottle asked her, "Can we be friends? I think we'll be happy together."

The goldfish readily agreed, "Sure! I can be your friend!"

The bottle said, "I will definitely protect you well. I won't let the wind blow you, nor will I let the water hit you. Come inside my belly."

After saying that, the goldfish swam into the bottle's belly. That's how they met. They were happy every day, having surprises and excitement every day. The bottle was also very happy. For so many years, the bottle had been alone. Now, with the goldfish by his side, he became very happy. He thought, "I must protect her!" The bottle took the goldfish to see the sunrise and sunset at the end of the sea and brought the goldfish to the bottom of the mountain to see the water's shockwaves. Day by day, they were happy. The bottle felt very content. He had no more requests. He just didn't know if he could take care of the goldfish for a lifetime. He thought, "If we could be this happy forever, it would be great."

They lived like this for a very long time, going through storms and thunderstorms together. However, the goldfish gradually grew up, becoming a beautiful goldfish. One day, the bottle noticed the sadness on the goldfish's face and saw tears in her cheeks.

The bottle asked the goldfish sadly, "What's wrong? Why are you suddenly unhappy today?"

The goldfish cried, "I want to go out and play in the outside world. I want to explore the outside world by myself. I want to find my own life."

The worst thing the bottle didn't want to happen finally occurred. The bottle knew this day would come. Calmly, the bottle replied to the goldfish, "If you really want to go out, I will fulfill your wish. I just hope you can live happily and happily after you leave."

After saying that, the bottle violently collided with the ruthless coral reef. The bottle shattered completely. The goldfish finally returned to her own world. She swam happily towards the depths of the sea. Only the head of the bottle remained, slowly sinking to the depths of the sea with the current.

The bottle was thus mercilessly left deep in the sea, but he had no regrets. He thought he should be satisfied. Although the goldfish was no longer by his side now, he was already very happy when he thought about the past, even though he had nothing now.

However, he remained calm. He knew he shouldn't indulge in any more luxuries. After all, he had owned her before, he had given her happiness, and he himself had been happy. Even though it had all become the past, even though he couldn't do anything now and might have to stay silently in the cold seawater for the rest of his life, his heart still felt warm at this moment. He was very grateful to heaven for giving him this opportunity.

Day by day, time passed. Finally, one day, the bottle suddenly felt very sad. He suddenly remembered the goldfish, wondering if she was happy now. But he knew that he could never see her again now. Yet, his feelings for the goldfish had deeply infected the seawater.

The bottle's affection for the goldfish deepened day by day. The bottle was worried about the goldfish's present happiness. The bottle always thought: Is she doing well? Is she still happy? Is she troubled now? Does she still remember me?

After a very long time, the bottle still missed the goldfish every day. His sincerity finally moved the Sea God. One day, the Sea God came to see the bottle.

The Sea God asked him, "Do you really want to see your goldfish?"

"Yes! I really, really want to!! But now I can't possibly go out and look for her anymore." The bottle was very sad.

"Are you willing to pay everything to see her?"

Hearing that he could see his beloved goldfish, the bottle seemed too excited. The many years of waiting had been painful, and the bottle's waiting had been fruitless. But when he realized he could see the goldfish again, he didn't care how much he had to pay.

"I can! As long as I can see her, I'm willing to pay anything." The bottle was very determined.

"Even if it means disappearing forever?"

The bottle hesitated for a while, but his longing for the goldfish seemed to surpass the meaning of life.

"Even if I give up my life, I must see her one last time. I just want to know if she's happy or not." The bottle didn't want to wait any longer. He had already given up his life.

After saying that, the Sea God took the bottle to see his goldfish. From afar, the bottle saw the goldfish swimming slowly with another goldfish. The bottle could tell that the goldfish was very happy, and the goldfish had found her true happiness. At that moment, the bottle realized that he seemed superfluous. Nevertheless, he was still very happy. He had no regrets. He was already very satisfied, extremely satisfied.

A bolt of lightning from the sky struck the last part of the bottle, and this time the bottle truly died. In the instant of his death, the bottle shed a tear. The bottle's tear, along with this tear, slowly sank to the bottom of the sea. With the bottle's longing for the goldfish and the love he once possessed, slowly, slowly, this was the first and last tear of the bottle's life. This is the tear of Aquarius, the tear at the mouth of the bottle is the tear of true love.

This is the destiny of Aquarius, to give up their true love, hoping that the person they love will be happy and joyful forever.

"Although many years have passed, I cannot hide my sadness. I cannot forget the day we met. In the sea, I searched for a long time, wanting to find your shadow, even if I could just see you once. I will never forget the image of you leaving. There are things I want to say to you but cannot express. I don't know if you're happy now.

I will slowly watch your shadow, then forget you, and tightly close my eyes. I know someone else has replaced me now, and I have no regrets. Although our separation was the start of my pain, I don't blame you. My little goldfish, although my life is about to end, I can never protect you anymore."

This is perhaps what the bottle wanted to say in his last moments.

Let us cherish our love now. Love is unique for each of us.

Afterward, I finally understood that what I felt for her was not true love. True love is selfless giving. I knew that I couldn't reach the level of the bottle in the story back then.

Now, I have matured a lot and understand what love is and how to cherish it.

But in the vast sea, I can no longer find her shadow. Although she hurt me, I also have responsibility. I didn't know how to cherish or how to love. If possible, come back, my love. This time, I will surely hold your hand tightly. Even if it's not my choice, it doesn't matter [because success and failure, honor and disgrace are all up to heaven]. I have never hated her. On the contrary, I sincerely hope she finds a good man like me now, to cherish her, care for her, and wish her happiness.
