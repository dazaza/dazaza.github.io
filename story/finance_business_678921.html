Interactions between colleagues are something everyone cannot avoid, and this has long been part of professional life. Whether working alone or busy with team members, you inevitably have to deal with colleagues or clients from time to time. Even if you are easy-going and approachable, it is not easy to build a good and harmonious cooperative relationship with colleagues in a short period of time. You need to respect them and treat them with the attitude of building a harmonious team. Only then can they give you equal trust and respect in return, thereby establishing a harmonious working relationship. Below are four secrets of interacting with colleagues, don’t miss them.

1. Invite colleagues to lunch or dinner

During regular times, you both have your own affairs. During work hours, you have your own tasks, and during rest hours, you also have your own matters to handle. Relatively speaking, there is less time to handle private issues with colleagues. If you want good interpersonal relationships, then you need enough time to deal with private issues among colleagues. Therefore, you need to find time to establish and strengthen this relationship. You should spend some time with your colleagues from time to time to show that you value their presence in the workplace and that you have excellent teamwork spirit. Usually, exchanges between colleagues in the workplace are often limited by deadlines and other factors. Therefore, having lunch or dinner together is a very good opportunity for communication. In this relatively relaxed atmosphere, you can relax and talk about some light-hearted topics, deepen the understanding between colleagues, and thereby improve the relationship.

2. Creative ideas for giving colleagues small gifts

We all know that only heartfelt things can be done well. This relates to creativity; the better the creativity, the higher the acceptance rate. Record down your colleagues' birthdays and the most important anniversaries for them. If you have the idea of buying them a birthday gift, then you can start preparing. The colleague who receives the gift will definitely be very moved and will always remember your thoughtfulness. You can also appropriately cultivate relationships with "non-colleagues."

3. Not every colleague is so terrible

Many people have a bad habit, easily seeing the other party as their "enemy," or as a "hypothetical enemy." They think the other party may harm them or damage their interests, so they guard against everything. As the saying goes, "It’s wrong to intend to harm others, but it’s necessary to guard against them." However, we don’t need to guard against everyone, as this will only make us live tired lives. To interact with colleagues, focusing on etiquette and emotions is enough. Colleagues don’t expect too much from you. Not everyone is thinking of harming you or taking what belongs to you.
