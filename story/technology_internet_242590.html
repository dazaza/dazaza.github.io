Experts from Beijing and Shanghai are consulting at the No. 163 Hospital in Changsha for liver disease treatment - the latest achievements in liver disease technology of our army are first entering clinical application.

by digme on 2009-09-25 11:34:42

In celebration of the 60th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the Mid-Autumn Festival, the PLA 163 Hospital will present a gift to the republic and the Chinese people by applying its latest and most advanced liver disease treatment technology - the "T-cell - Peptide Therapy" - to clinical practice, benefiting a large number of liver disease patients in the region. Starting from September 19th, the 163 Hospital will invite renowned liver disease experts from prestigious hospitals in Beijing and Shanghai to hold clinics in Changsha, Hunan Province, utilizing the latest scientific research technologies for liver diseases and offering joint consultations by these renowned experts to serve the liver disease patients in our province.

It is reported that during the period of expert consultation from September 19, 2009, to October 18, 2009, all liver disease patients visiting the PLA 163 Hospital can enjoy full waivers on the 200 RMB expert consultation fee and free detection of hepatitis B surface antigen and antibodies (HBsAg, HBsAb, HBeAg, HBeAb, HBcAb).

The PLA 163 Hospital located in Changsha, Hunan Province, is the only designated hospital in Hunan for this expert consultation event involving renowned doctors from Beijing and Shanghai. The aim of this activity is to promote the latest liver disease research results from the military to benefit the local residents, ensuring that services for the people are genuinely delivered, further addressing the issue of difficulty in accessing medical care, striving to focus liver disease medical work at the grassroots level, and allocating high-quality medical resources at the front line so that liver disease patients in our province can receive the highest level of medical services right at their doorstep.

The renowned liver disease experts participating in the expert consultation event at the PLA 163 Hospital include: Professor Peng Xiaojun, liver disease expert from the General Hospital of the Beijing Military Region; Professor Teng Benrong, Deputy Director of the Beijing Liver Disease New Technology Treatment Research Center; Professor Wang Zhenbiao, infectious disease expert from Beijing You'an Hospital; Professor Tang Wenchán, expert from the Shanghai Chronic Hepatitis Prevention and Treatment Research Center and researcher at the Chinese Medical Association Infectious Diseases Society; Professor Liu Wenxia, director of the Department of Infectious Diseases at Beijing Shijitan Hospital; Professor Chen Yuqing, former member of the Shanghai Branch Committee of the Chinese Medical Association and member of the Chinese Preventive Medicine Association; Professor Zhong Xiaozhen, liver disease diagnosis and treatment expert from the PLA 163 Hospital; and Professor Chen Huaqing, outstanding contributor to the latest liver disease research technology, among more than ten nationally renowned liver disease experts and professors.

For more details about the event, please refer to the official website of the Changsha 163 Hospital.
