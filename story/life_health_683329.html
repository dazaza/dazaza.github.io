2. Rice vinegar radish

Ingredients: 250 grams of white radish, appropriate amount of rice vinegar, Sichuan pepper, salt, sesame oil.

Method: Wash the radish and cut it into thin slices, add a little Sichuan pepper and salt, soak in rice vinegar for 4 hours, drizzle with sesame oil before serving.

3. Clear water radish soup

Ingredients: 250 grams of white radish each, 10 grams of ginger, appropriate amount of salt and monosodium glutamate.

Method: Cut the radishes into chunks, add clear water and cook with ginger, after thoroughly cooked, add salt and monosodium glutamate to taste before serving.

4. Millet silver ear radish soup

Ingredients: 250 grams of millet, 25 grams of silver ear, 30 grams of radish, pork according to personal taste.
