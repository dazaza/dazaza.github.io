Unveiling the identity of Yao Ming within World of Warcraft: The troll is quite fitting and has already reached the maximum level.

by ed528960 on 2009-08-02 23:38:02

It is well known to all that Yao Ming is a player of Warcraft, but perhaps people do not know clearly what kind of character Yao Ming is in Warcraft. Below, Titan Network will uncover the secret with you.

Q: How long has Yao Ming played "World of Warcraft"?

A: It is reported that Yao Ming has been playing Warcraft World for nearly 4 years.

Q: Is Yao Ming playing on the American server or the Chinese server now?

A: Yao Ming and his "best buddy", teammate of Shanghai Oriental Team Liu Wei, are on the same server and zone. The two often go online at the same time and do tasks together. Considering that Liu Wei is a player of the Shanghai Oriental Team and that Yao Ming has been playing Warcraft for more than 4 years, it is relatively more likely that Yao Ming plays in domestic Zone One or Three.

Q: What race and profession does Yao Ming choose?

A: According to comprehensive reports, we can say that Yao Ming chooses the Troll race of the Horde, and the profession is Rogue. According to media reports in Shanghai, Yao Ming chose a role whose height is very consistent with his - the Troll. In terms of physique, Trolls belong to the small giant category among game races, somewhat similar to Yao Ming's towering presence on the court; from the background story, the Troll race was once saved by Orc Chief Thrall and thus became loyal, which in some ways is similar to Yao Ming's feelings towards the Rockets.

According to the Liberation Daily, Yao Ming chose the "Rogue" profession, where sneak attacks are the most commonly used method of attack. This seems quite different from the image of a center who needs to stand firm under the basket, but Yao Ming enjoys it immensely. Actually, there are many professions to choose from in this game: Warrior, Mage, Priest, Hunter, etc., but Yao Ming specifically chose the Rogue. Sometimes, when Liu Wei, who chooses to be a Hunter, goes online, the two work together seamlessly, able to take down quite a few players from the opposing faction.

Q: "What level and equipment has Yao Ming reached?"

A: Yao Ming's level should have already reached level 70. However, due to work reasons, he probably doesn't have much time, so his equipment is relatively poor.
