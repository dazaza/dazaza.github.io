Enrollment for the part-time graduate program in project management under the enterprise management major at Renmin University in 2011.

by shuoshi on 2011-08-25 17:08:23

The Business School of Renmin University of China is the first-class discipline point of business management nationwide. It is the only school in China that has three national key disciplines simultaneously, namely enterprise management, accounting and industrial economics. The Business School enjoys extremely high academic status in the field of economic management in China. Scientific research is an important part of the development and construction of the College. The college emphasizes both theoretical research and management practice, always linking academic scientific research with national progress and reform and opening up development, providing strong theoretical support and practical help for the development of Chinese enterprises.

In order to meet the needs of high-level specialized talents for social and economic development, the Business School of Renmin University of China will continue to offer a postgraduate course program in project management direction of enterprise management major in Beijing area in 2011.

Training methods

1. Combining face-to-face teaching with self-study, the course teaching time lasts for two years, taught during leisure time;

2. Students who apply for a master's degree need to obtain a qualification card for degree course examination, which is valid for four years.

Fee standards

Tuition fee: 28,000 yuan (including material fees). The costs for students applying for a degree to take degree course exams, master's thesis guidance and defense, etc., shall be implemented according to the relevant regulations of Renmin University of China. After the advanced study class officially starts, if a student cannot continue the advanced study due to certain reasons, it will be regarded as automatically giving up learning, and the advanced study fee will not be refunded.

Issuance of advanced study certificates and application for a master's degree

1. Those who have completed the prescribed courses of the advanced study class will be issued a completion certificate after being reviewed by the Graduate School of Renmin University of China.

2. Those who have obtained a bachelor's degree for more than three years, passed the assessment of 18 courses in the major they have studied and the national unified level examination organized by the state, and publicly published a related paper, can enter the stage of applying for a master's degree.

3. After passing the school's thesis defense and degree approval, one can obtain a master's degree in management in project management direction from Renmin University of China.
