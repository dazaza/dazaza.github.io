Mid-Autumn Festival is coming, and the home appliance promotion war is about to begin.

by lzxliyas on 2011-08-12 16:47:21

As the Spring Festival approaches, large shopping malls and supermarkets are bustling with people buying New Year's goods, creating an increasingly festive atmosphere. With the improvement of living standards, the concept of "New Year's goods" is no longer limited to traditional items like dried fruits and snacks. Electronics such as computers, cameras, mobile phones, televisions, and induction cookers have also entered the category of New Year's goods. Sales of home appliances soar before and after the Spring Festival, and as expected, a wave of price reductions has swept through the home appliance market in Beijing.

According to reports from Dazhong Appliances, starting from January 21st, Dazhong has already launched its grand New Year's goods sale event. During this period, all electrical appliances are being sold at rock-bottom prices, with both the scale of discounts and the level of savings reaching the highest point of the year so far. Starting this weekend, Dazhong’s New Year's goods fair will reach another climax. During the Spring Festival, Dazhong will present a major promotional event across its more than 50 stores throughout the city.

A massive Spring Festival discount peak is set to occur this week.

This Dazhong New Year's goods fair gathers resources from global home appliance brands, totaling 20 billion yuan, offering low-price greetings and generous New Year gifts to consumers in Beijing, conveying Dazhong's heartfelt wishes for the Spring Festival. All stores citywide will offer low-priced sales across all categories.

For traditional home appliances, spend 1000 yuan and receive a 1000-yuan gift card. Mobile phones, cameras, and computers will see direct price drops starting from 300 yuan, along with a New Year gift package. This heavy promotion will trigger a peak in Spring Festival discounts, with ample inventory ensuring smooth sales during this round of promotions.

Price remains Dazhong's sharpest weapon in home appliance promotions. Its strategy of selling at thin profit margins but high volume has become the key to its success. For this Spring Festival home appliance promotion, Dazhong has prepared special-priced goods worth over ten billion yuan, covering categories such as televisions, mobile phones, computers, refrigerators, washing machines, small appliances, and digital products.

A citywide Dazhong discount storm will passionately unfold from this weekend until the Spring Festival.

100% chance to win! Zhongta引爆 final carnival night

On January 28th, the last weekend before the Spring Festival, Dazhong's first store in Beijing, Zhongta Xinhuo Pavilion, will kick off the final night market from 2 PM, presenting the ultimate promotional gala. In this year-end finale carnival sleepless night, both traditional home appliances and mobile digital computer categories will experience their ultimate madness.

Customers purchasing any product in the categories of televisions, air conditioners, refrigerators, washing machines, kitchen and bathroom appliances, or audio equipment and small appliances priced over 800 yuan can participate in a lottery (one invoice per draw). The grand prize is a 47-inch Skyworth LCD TV, the first prize is a 24K gold bar, the second prize is an LG French door refrigerator, the third prize is a Sanyo washing machine, and the fourth prize includes practical household appliances. Every participant has a 100% chance to win, with big prizes waiting for you.

Although mobile phones, digital products, and computers do not participate in the lottery, they will still offer 12 hours of crazy giveaways. Every mobile phone purchase comes with a gift, with additional reductions of 100-2000 yuan on top of the Spring Festival activity prices that night. Purchasing computers or photographic equipment comes with a 600-yuan gift (300-yuan direct reduction + 300-yuan gift), while DSLR purchases include an additional 500G portable hard drive. Apple computer buyers receive a free notebook, and Sony computer buyers get a 320G portable hard drive. On top of the Spring Festival activity prices, there will be at least an additional 100-yuan reduction during the night market.
