Summary of Common Techniques for Google AdSense

by prisco on 2006-09-09 18:21:42

Google AdSense is one of the main sources of website revenue. Here, I will introduce some commonly used techniques to help increase your Google AdSense income and prevent your account from being suspended.

**Ad Click Tracking Software**

This, in my opinion, is the most important step. Install an ad click tracking software to monitor which users clicked on which ads from which IP addresses. This provides excellent results for future data analysis. Additionally, it allows you to track malicious clicks by specific IPs so you can promptly report them to Google.

Currently, there are many ad click tracking programs available. A quick search will yield numerous options. I recommend choosing a more comprehensive software that can analyze at least the following data: user IP, the URL of the clicked ad, the ad's address, and the time of the click. This will provide a solid foundation for further analysis.

**Ad Placement, Coloring, and Quantity**

You can place multiple AdSense ads on a single page to increase ad exposure. However, don't overdo it—place no more than 3 content ad units and 1 link unit per page. Ads should be placed at the top of the page to achieve higher click-through rates. The background color and text color of the ads should match the overall color scheme of the page. This enhances the user experience and can also improve the click-through rate.
