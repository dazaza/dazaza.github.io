A policeman was stabbed while fighting with an escaped prisoner who was armed with a knife.

by dehua900 on 2011-05-08 09:42:20

At the time, Zhao Guojun's convenience store had more than 20 civilians present. When Deputy Chief Qiao Zhihong and police officer Hou Shujun attempted to identify and question Zhao Guojun, he suddenly pulled a dagger from his chest and began to wildly slash at the civilians and officers. In this critical moment, Officer Hou Shujun, in order to prevent Zhao Guojun from injuring the surrounding civilians and fellow officers, and to avoid a hostage situation, engaged Zhao Guojun in an intense fight with a baton.

Liaoyuan News - Around 19:30 on November 24th, the police officers of the Shuhe Police Station at Dongfeng County were arresting the fugitive suspect Zhao Guojun when they encountered fierce resistance as he wielded a knife. During the struggle with Zhao Guojun, Officer Hou Shujun was unfortunately severely stabbed in the right hand by the knife, allowing the suspect Zhao Guojun to escape once again.

During this time, Deputy Chief Qiao Zhihong, Zhang Dewei, and Gao Guangwen of the Shuhe Police Station urgently evacuated the civilians present. In the fight with Zhao Guojun, Officer Hou Shujun was stabbed in the back of the right hand by Zhao Guojun's dagger, severing the tendon of the little finger. Zhao Guojun took the opportunity to escape. Immediately after, Officer Hou Shujun was sent to the Yitong County Hospital for treatment, while the civilians present were unharmed.

Currently, the pursuit of the criminal suspect Zhao Guojun is underway with great urgency.

■ Reported by Jiang Yubin and Journalist Gao Guangda

Yesterday, I learned from the Dongfeng County Public Security Bureau that around 19:30 on November 24, 2010, the Nandanbei Police Office of the Shuhe Police Station received information that the criminal suspect Zhao Guojun, who had fled during a September 2010 criminal detention for intentional harm, had secretly returned home and was currently in a small convenience store in Sijian Village, Yingchengzi Town, Yitong County. After receiving a tip-off from the public, Deputy Chief Qiao Zhihong led Officers Hou Shujun, Zhang Dewei, and Gao Guangwen, equipped with police gear and weapons, to drive to the convenience store for the arrest.
