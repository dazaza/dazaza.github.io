[New Mei Wall Advertising] In the future, Chongqing residents will be able to stay at home and check online which hospital offers cheaper drugs, allowing them to choose hospitals with lower drug prices for treatment. Recently, the Banan District Government signed a strategic cooperation agreement with the Chongqing Pharmaceutical Exchange to establish an integrated comprehensive information platform for drug transactions, supervision, and services in the district.

Chongqing Municipal Government Deputy Secretary General Tu Jingping, Party Secretary of the Municipal Health Bureau Xia Yongpeng, Deputy Director of the Municipal Food and Drug Administration Luo Li, Chairman of the Chongqing Pharmaceutical Exchange Liu Gaqing, and district leaders Li Jianchun, He Ping, Jin Ling, Qin Dingbo, Jiang Yong, Fu Xingqun, and others attended the signing ceremony. The signing ceremony was presided over by Vice District Chief Qin Dingbo.

The two parties will strengthen cooperation in five areas.

Establishing a strategic partnership, both sides will further promote exploration and innovation in the reform of the medical and health system in Chongqing, accelerating the electronic trading platform of the pharmaceutical exchange and the informatization construction of the medical and health system in Banan District. Liu Gaqing said that this would provide valuable experience for the construction of the pharmaceutical circulation and security system and the reform of the medical and health system in Chongqing.

According to the agreement, the two parties will strengthen cooperation in five areas, including establishing an integrated comprehensive information platform for drug transactions, supervision, and services; establishing a comprehensive drug supervision mechanism involving multiple government departments; exploring quality supervision methods and measures for electronic transactions of drugs from the Chongqing Pharmaceutical Exchange in Banan District; exploring mechanisms for retail pharmacies to purchase and deliver drugs through the Chongqing Pharmaceutical Trading Platform; and exploring the establishment of regional drug distribution centers within healthcare institutions in the district to address issues such as low purchasing volumes and high delivery costs at township health centers.

36 hospitals and 300 pharmacies under supervision

It is understood that in June last year, Banan District had already launched the construction of an electronic drug safety supervision project. This project uses modern information technology to implement electronic supervision on the procurement, sales, and inventory of drugs in medical institutions and other pharmaceutical units within the jurisdiction, ensuring drug safety. At the same time, the project also has the function of monitoring the purchase and sales prices of drugs.

A relevant person in charge of the Banan District Food and Drug Supervision Branch introduced that after signing the agreement with the pharmaceutical exchange, the pharmaceutical exchange would first pilot the establishment of an integrated comprehensive information platform for drug transactions, supervision, and services in the district. In the future, the general public can also query information such as the price of drugs purchased by hospitals, basic drugs available, and medical insurance reimbursement ratios through the integrated information platform.

Currently, 36 hospitals and 300 pharmacies and clinics in Banan District have been included in this informatization management project, making it the first district or county in the city to incorporate the drugs of medical and health institutions into informatized supervision. The supervised drugs account for 80% of the total drugs used in the district.

Vigorously promoting the construction of Safe Banan and Healthy Banan

Li Jianchun stated that Banan District will give full play to its advantages, attracting large domestic and international pharmaceutical enterprises to build industrial bases in Banan, promoting the rapid and good development of the pharmaceutical industry in the entire district; deeply exploring new orders and mechanisms in drug transactions, procurement, and circulation, providing experimental bases and practical experience for the Chongqing Pharmaceutical Exchange to quickly achieve its five functions, building a national-level pharmaceutical trading market, a national-level pharmaceutical trading information center, a national-level pharmaceutical trading settlement center, and a national-level pharmaceutical distribution center.

Actively conducting surveys, exploring the establishment of a comprehensive drug safety supervision mechanism involving multiple government departments, ensuring that the public equally enjoys affordable and high-quality medical security services; accelerating the informatization construction of grassroots medical and health supervision, continuously opening up new situations in the drug supervision cause of Banan District, and providing strong drug supervision guarantees for building a safe, harmonious, and stable social environment.
