Man Rapes and Kills 17-Year-Old Girl from the Same Village, then Buries Her by the Creek (Picture)

by mingdaolin on 2009-12-04 12:26:37

Zheng Mouyi was captured

According to reports, in Xiabalin Village of Yan厝 Town in Longhai, villagers still sigh when they talk about the 17-year-old girl Xiao Yan (a pseudonym) who was brutally murdered. On her way home from work, Xiao Yan was intercepted by Zheng Mouyi, a man from the same village, at a secluded stream 200 meters away from her home. She was then brutally killed and raped, and her body was buried in the grass near the stream.

Even more shocking is that Zheng Mouyi had dug the grave for burying the body a day in advance. Moreover, while police officers were digging for the body, he stood among the onlooking villagers.

Yesterday, the Longhai police of Zhangzhou announced that Zheng Mouyi has been arrested on suspicion of intentional homicide. At the same time, his brother has also been arrested on suspicion of harboring a criminal.

[Investigation Report]

The naked body of a teenage girl was buried by the roadside.

In Xiabalin Village, Xiao Yan and her younger sister were known as the "two golden flowers" of the village. Xiao Yan left a good impression on the villagers, being especially obedient.

Xiao Yan worked as a salesperson in Xiangcheng District and rode her bicycle home every day after work, arriving at her home in Xiabalin Village around 7 p.m. However, on the evening of September 19, she did not return home all night. The next day, Xiao Yan's family and relatives searched everywhere but could not find any trace of her, so they reported it to the police.

Xiao Yan had to pass by a small stream on her way home, which was surrounded by dense lychee trees. Her family suspected that she might have accidentally fallen into the stream. On the morning of September 21, the police officers from Yan厝 Police Station and Xiao Yan's family searched around the stream and even drained the water out of it. Unexpectedly, they found Xiao Yan's handbag, bra, sunglasses, and other items in the riverbed.

"The girl was probably killed nearby." The police immediately looked for clues in the grass near the stream. Sure enough, the grass beside a vegetable field looked different from the surrounding area. When they dug down about half a meter, they found Xiao Yan's naked body. After forensic examination, it was determined that Xiao Yan died of suffocation and had been sexually assaulted before her death.

Suspect suddenly disappeared

The burial site was only 200 meters away from Xiao Yan's house, leading the police to believe that the murderer was very familiar with the terrain and was likely a local villager. "That place is very secluded, rarely passed by people, and the dense forest blocks the view," said Deputy Chief Kang of the Longhai Criminal Investigation Brigade. Therefore, the police carried out a large-scale investigation in Xiabalin Village.

At this time, the police learned that Zheng Mouyi, a 19-year-old villager, suddenly went missing on the day of the incident. Prior to this, someone had seen Zheng Mouyi showing off Xiao Yan's mobile phone.

Zheng Mouyi became a major suspect, so the police placed his brother, Zheng Moulin, under full surveillance. Sure enough, early in the morning on September 22, Zheng Moulin hurriedly walked out of the house with a package and headed towards Jiaomei in Longhai. The police followed them to a cyber cafe outside Jiaomei Town, where Zheng Mouyi came out of the internet cafe. Both brothers were captured. After interrogation, Zheng Mouyi confessed to the murder of Xiao Yan.

[Confession of the Suspect]

Dug a pit in advance to prepare for covering up the crime

In the eyes of the local villagers, Zheng Mouyi was idle and often engaged in petty theft, relying on catching fish to sustain his living.

Zheng Mouyi confessed to the police that because his cousin was a close friend of Xiao Yan, he frequently saw Xiao Yan and developed wicked thoughts towards her. In addition, Zheng Mouyi knew that Xiao Yan passed by the stream every day after work, and the dirt road beside the stream was very secluded.

On the afternoon of September 18, Zheng Mouyi dug a pit in advance beside the stream, preparing to kill and cover up the crime. The next evening, at around 7 p.m., Zheng Mouyi brought the pre-prepared electric wire and hid in the lychee forest. When he saw Xiao Yan riding her bicycle coming, he rushed forward and forcibly pushed her into the forest. To prevent Xiao Yan from shouting, Zheng Mouyi strangled her with the electric wire, then committed the violent act, and subsequently buried her body.

Watching the excavation scene

During the interrogation, the police were astonished by Zheng Mouyi's audacity, repeatedly saying, "Unthinkable."
