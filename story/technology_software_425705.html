Pickled Vegetable Superman Part XIV - Pickled Vegetables and Pigs

by wenxue126 on 2011-05-06 17:59:49

The Fourteenth Superman of Pickled Vegetables and the Pig

Ever since he set the goal of surpassing heaven, he began to work hard again, resulting in extreme fatigue and little progress. He was getting further and further away from heaven. As he continued practicing, he gradually developed anxiety and depression. After falling ill, not only could he no longer think about surpassing heaven or roses, but he also couldn't be a superman anymore. Among the N kinds of men I have personally experienced, he couldn't even control his own emotions, often feeling inexplicably distressed and anxious.

One day, he saw a pig lying peacefully on the grass sunbathing. He envied it greatly and went up to ask for advice on happiness. The pig said something that greatly inspired him. Pickled vegetables decided to take the pig as his teacher to learn the secrets of happiness and joy.

If pickled vegetables were to take someone else as a teacher, they would have their own skills. Now, pickled vegetables are taking the pig as a teacher. What can the pig do besides eating and sleeping? Of course, the pig has one use: its name can be used to insult people. But is this useful for pickled vegetables?

After saying all this, this is just our understanding. Let's see how pickled vegetables understands it.

Pickled vegetables believed that although pigs were greedy and lazy, they had good tempers and personalities. People say: personality is destiny. Since the pig has a good personality, its fate isn't bad either. Although it only lives for one or two years, during this time, the pig doesn't have to guard homes like a dog every night, doesn't have to dig for food in the ground like a chicken every day, doesn't have to build its own nest like a swallow, and certainly doesn't have to worry about clothing, food, shelter, and transportation like us humans. The pig can be said to have no worries about eating, wearing, or living. So, the pig is carefree and plump, with a face resembling Buddha. Additionally, the pig has a good mindset and never compares itself with others. Even if you kick it a few times, it will only grunt a bit, unlike a dog that will definitely jump up and bite you if you kick it. The pig's only defect is that it lives too short a life. However, although life is short, there is no regret.

These above advantages of the pig are secondary. The key is what the pig said that day, which greatly impressed pickled vegetables.

That day, the pig was lying on the grass, basking in the sun, and stretching out to say to pickled vegetables: "Pickled vegetables, don't you see humans? They are very capable. They can fly in the sky like birds and dive into the deep sea like fish. They can eat all of us animals and push down mountains and fill in seas. But so what? They can do anything, but they aren't as happy as me, the pig. Look at how capable they are, but they are actually very pitiful because they themselves don't know. When they think, God laughs. They still claim to fight against heaven and earth, but everyone's life contains tears, setbacks, and pain. Everyone is pursuing happiness and joy, but for most people, happiness and joy are often visible but unattainable. They don't know that the more they chase after happiness, the more happiness drifts away from them like a soap bubble. Because they always set goals for themselves and rush towards them without hesitation. Countless people rush towards their defined happiness like moths rushing towards fire. They get burned, exhausted, and full of scars, then blame heaven for being unfair and complain about how hard it is to be human. They never wake up and realize that the happiness they pursue is just a soap bubble that looks like happiness, not true happiness. They always think they are smart and believe in themselves, so God often laughs while watching them stumble."

Because of these words from the pig, pickled vegetables insisted on taking the pig as his teacher.

After becoming the disciple, the pig began to teach him: "The mind should be calm, desires should be few, and contentment brings constant joy." It made him recite it continuously and let pickled vegetables practice yoga, Tai Chi, and other slow exercises.

After practicing for a while, when pickled vegetables slowly calmed down and gradually forgot about surpassing heaven and roses, the pig taught him: "Life is a dream, everything is empty. If you want to truly understand, let the wind pass through your head."

When pickled vegetables gradually became quiet and no longer troubled by the outside world, the pig taught him: "No self, no self, no self, no self." And told him that "no self" is a very high realm. Ordinary people think about "me" all day long. All troubles and joys are related to the word "me". They consider things from their own perspective all day, so it's difficult for them to reach "no self". Therefore, if a person can reach the state of "no self," they transcend humanity and get closer to heaven.

At the beginning of "no self," pickled vegetables felt lost, unable to find himself or the rose. But gradually, he entered the state of "no self." At this point, pickled vegetables often forgot his own existence and the existence of the world. This brought him another world, a transparent and wonderful world where the sky was bluer and the water clearer. Everything was serene, without any noise or greed. He existed like a wisp of smoke or a gentle breeze.

He felt that he had truly begun to enter the realm of a superman.

Then, the pig taught him: "Transcend self, transcend self, transcend self, transcend self." After transcending the self rather than heaven, pickled vegetables changed again. He began to perceive the beauty of this world but still forgot his own existence. This made him unconsciously do many things to benefit society and nature without expecting any return. Although there was no return, he felt extremely free and happy, floating like a speck of dust between heaven and earth. He could wander in ancient Greece five thousand years ago or travel in present-day China.

At this point, pickled vegetables was no longer just pickled vegetables but a true superman of pickled vegetables.

The superman of pickled vegetables was very grateful to his mentor, Mr. Pig, considering him the embodiment of wisdom and virtue.

Since then, some of his descendants stopped eating pork.
