Murder fugitive becomes a millionaire by selling dog meat

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-07 13:11:50

Chutian Golden Newspaper (Weibo) reported by special reporter Lingyun and correspondent Donggang: Recently, at a dog meat shop in Xialu District of Huangshi City, the shop owner was just about to take out the garbage at 2 PM when he was held down by several strange men. "I am a policeman from Xinzhou! You are the fugitive Wang!" After one of the men showed his police ID, the shop owner immediately lowered his head and surrendered without resistance.

Gambling Debt Dispute Leading to Death

At 8 PM on August 4, 2009, due to a gambling debt dispute, 33-year-old Wang from Zhangdu Lake Forest Farm in Xinzhou District of Wuhan invited more than ten people, armed with machetes and other weapons, to fight with another group, resulting in the death of one person. After the incident, the main suspect Wang secretly transferred his business, and fled with his wife and children. The local police subsequently tracked him to Guangzhou, Fujian, Xiamen and other places for arrest, but Wang managed to escape every time.

After the nationwide police "Clear Net Action" began in May this year, the Xinzhou police organized a special team to chase fugitives in 15 provinces and cities including Guangdong and Fujian, mainly focusing on setting up networks around more than ten relationship persons where Wang might be hiding, and investigating clues.

Fled to Huangshi and Got Rich Selling Dog Meat

In the middle of last month, the Xinzhou police publicly offered rewards for the capture of Wang and 17 other final fugitives, and the reward announcement was posted in each village.

On November 11, a villager informed the police that Wang might be in Huangshi. Subsequently, the special team of Xinzhou police rushed to Huangshi, and conducted covert investigations with the full cooperation of the Xialu Public Security Bureau, focusing on "Pan" who had close relations with Wang. Pan worked as a waitress in a mall, and her closely related person surnamed Cheng ran a night market snack shop locally.

Around 2 PM on November 12, after work, Pan came to the second floor of the dog meat night food shop opposite the old Xialu Jinhao Mall. The tracking police found that the store owner who personally chopped dog meat was Wang, and they immediately arrested him.

After being caught, Wang confessed that he now owns two dog meat shops in Huangshi, and had accumulated over a million yuan in two years. Currently, Wang has been taken back to Xinzhou for trial.
