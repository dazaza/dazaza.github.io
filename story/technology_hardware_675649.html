Two brothers were stabbed to death in the streets on January 7th, and the authorities have issued a case update.

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-25 11:19:03

Weibo recommendation | Weibo hot topics of the day. The county Party committee and county government required that, as this case was a matter of life and death and had an immense impact, all departments of public security, procuratorate, court, and justice must closely cooperate, strictly handle the case according to law, and resolutely severely punish the criminals.

At 2:15 AM on January 29, 2012 (the seventh day of the first lunar month), the on-duty police officer at Yanning Police Station received a phone call from a member of the public reporting: "There are two people lying on the ground below the County Finance Bureau on Zhongxing Street in Yanning Town, it seems like they have been beaten." After receiving the alarm, the on-duty police officers at Yanning Police Station immediately responded. Upon arrival at the scene, the responding police officers discovered that the two individuals lying on the ground had multiple stab wounds, which were caused by intentional harm, and were unconscious. The police officers immediately called 120 to send the two individuals to the county hospital for emergency treatment. Despite the hospital's best efforts to save them, both individuals died at around 4 AM due to severe injuries. After investigation by the police, it was determined that the two deceased individuals were named Xu XX and Xu X, who were brothers and lived in Yanning Town, Yanjing County.

Investigation revealed that at around 1:30 AM on January 29, 2012, after being drunk, suspects Zhang X, Mi X, Gao X (female), and Aniu (female) encountered victims Xu XX and Xu X on Zhongxing Street in Yanning Town, Yanjing County. A fight broke out between the two parties due to verbal altercations. Suspect Zhang X used a knife he was carrying to stab Xu XX and Xu X in the chest, back, and other areas, causing the two victims to die from hemorrhagic shock. After the incident, the four individuals fled in a taxi near the long-distance bus station in Yanning Town towards Weicheng. When the car reached Yangliu Bridge in Shuanghe Township, the four individuals took the driver's car keys and continued driving towards Xichang with Mi X driving the taxi. As they approached Xiaogao Mountain top, the four individuals pushed the driver out of the car and continued fleeing. The suspects were intercepted by police officers set up by the Yanjing County Public Security Bureau near the Coal Ravine Tunnel in Jinhe Township, and all were captured. Currently, the suspects have been criminally detained, and the county procuratorate has already intervened in the case.

After the incident occurred, the county Party committee and county government attached great importance to it, promptly establishing a special investigation team. Various police units, including criminal investigation and traffic police, carried out encirclement and pursuit operations against the suspects. Within four hours, by around 6 AM, the suspects Zhang X and others were all captured near the Coal Ravine Tunnel in Jinhe Township.
