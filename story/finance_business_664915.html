Here is the English translation of the provided Chinese text. The content appears to be a collection of romantic poems and messages for Valentine's Day. Below is an organized version with some enhancements for readability and flow:

---

**Love is more than a word, it says so much.

When I see these four letters, I almost feel your touch.

This only happens since I fell in love with you.

Why this word has such power, I haven’t got a clue.**

_Love is not just a word; it carries so much meaning. When I look at the letters that spell "love," I can almost feel your presence. This sensation began when I fell in love with you. Why this word affects me so deeply, I cannot explain._

---

**Thank you for comforting me when I’m sad,

Loving me when I’m mad,

Picking me up when I’m down.

Thank you for being my friend and always around,

Teaching me the meaning of love,

Encouraging me when I need a shove.

But most of all, thank you for loving me for who I am.**

_You are my comfort in sorrow, my anchor in anger, and my strength in weakness. You’ve been my friend through every moment, teaching me what love truly means and encouraging me when I falter. Above all else, I am grateful that you love me for exactly who I am._

---

**Sweetheart, my thoughts are deep into you,

From the moment I wake up,

And throughout the whole day.

Happy Valentine’s Day!**

_Dear one, you are never far from my mind. From the instant I open my eyes in the morning until the stars appear at night, my heart is with you. Wishing you a joyous and loving Valentine’s Day._

---

**Thank you for standing behind me,

In all that I do.

I hope you’re as happy with me,

As I am with you.**

_Thank you for your unwavering support in everything I pursue. My wish is that you find as much happiness in my presence as I do in yours._

---

**If I could save time in a bottle,

The first thing I’d like to do,

Is save every day until eternity passes away,

Just to spend them with you.

If I could make days last forever,

If words could make wishes come true,

I’d save every day like a treasure,

And then again, I would spend them with you.**

_Oh, if only I could preserve time within a bottle! The very first thing I’d do is store each day, waiting for eternity to pass, just so I could share them all with you. If I could stretch days into forever or turn my dreams into reality, I’d cherish every moment we have and relive them endlessly by your side._

---

**To sweetheart or friend, words can mean much.

Valentine heart to heart, conveys a loving touch.**

_To my beloved or cherished companion, words carry immense weight. Through the heartfelt exchange of Valentine’s greetings, we convey the warmth and tenderness of our affection._

---

**You’re always there for me,

When things tend to go wrong.

It’s that faith you have in me,

That makes our love strong.**

_No matter how life twists and turns, you remain steadfast beside me. Your belief in me strengthens the bond between us, making our love unshakable._

---

**It’s your loving and your caring,

And knowing that you’re near,

That gentle touch you have,

Makes my troubles disappear.**

_Your kindness and concern wrap around me like a warm embrace. Knowing you are close fills me with peace, and your tender gestures erase any worries that weigh on my soul._

---

**A better love I couldn’t ask for,

With your sweet and gentle way.

And knowing that your love for me,

Grows more every day.**

_I could never dream of a greater love than the one you give. Your sweetness and gentleness touch my heart deeply, and each passing day brings new growth to the love you hold for me._

---

**We share so much together,

And you always pull me through.

Thank you for standing behind me,

In all that I do.**

_Our lives intertwine in countless ways, and through it all, you guide me forward. Thank you for supporting me in everything I undertake, lifting me higher with your encouragement._

---
