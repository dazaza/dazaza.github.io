Date: February 14, 2007 January 24, 2007, a classmate known as "Die Extra" posted a special thread on the TuMao Forum, drawing high attention and importance from many TuMao brothers and sisters. Zongtai Ying Jianren!

An old friend of the forum, known as "Autumn's Other Nickname," encountered difficulties in her family; her one-year-old baby suffers from congenital ear disease and currently cannot hear any sound, requiring immediate surgery! According to expert introductions, the treatment costs will consist of three parts: one part is for purchasing a cochlear implant (approximately 150,000 yuan); another part is for the surgical fee (approximately 30,000 yuan); and the last part is for nursing care (specific costs unknown). For a young mother and a family with meager income, this amount is an astronomical figure that is difficult to bear.

Dear Baby:

Love life, love Zongtai Ying Jianren, love TuMao, and TuMao Forum is our home! Such words have always warmed every friend on the TuMao Forum! At such a moment, as a big family, we must bring hope to "Autumn's Other Nickname." Thus, a passionate and touching donation campaign began.

Various charity sales and donation activities were enthusiastically carried out both online and offline in the forum. Many friends sent their donations with blessings, and although the amounts varied, everyone's mood was the same: wishing the baby could recover soon!

To differentiate from our original donation account for stray cats and better record and manage the funds, we specially announced the account under the responsible person’s name. This account hadn't been used for a while, so the password needed to be recalled. We especially thank this responsible person for their strong support. On February 4th, I was finally able to check every relevant record. More reassuringly, after nearly ten days of joint efforts, we had received a total of 8275.89 yuan in donations and charity sale proceeds!

On the afternoon of February 8th, several of us, including Xiaoshijie and Pangmao, brought this donation and all the blessings from TuMao brothers and sisters to the hospital to visit "Autumn's Other Nickname's" adorable baby who was awaiting surgery!

When we stepped into the hospital ward, Autumn and her husband were worried because their child couldn't undergo surgery promptly due to symptoms after catching a cold. Seeing their helpless faces, our hearts felt equally anxious. Looking at the cute child gazing at us with innocent eyes, we wished more than anything to take the child's place and make them well immediately!

Helpless Mother and Baby Awaiting Surgery:

We've come:

We took out the money donated by everyone, placed in an envelope, and handed it to Autumn, saying: This amount is not enough to cover all the medical expenses for the child, but we are still doing our best to lend a helping hand. Although money is not omnipotent, we still hope that through the strength of all of us, Zongtai Ying Jianren, we can help Autumn and her husband strengthen their confidence in treating the child's illness and future life. Here, we sincerely hope that "Sister Autumn" and your husband can face this unexpected event strongly, Zongtai Ying Jianren. After all, the road ahead is long, may you give your child hope for tomorrow with today's courage!

Baby Preparing for Surgery:

Pangmao insisted on taking a photo with the baby first (a bit similar? Baby: "When I grow up, I'll be much cuter than him!")

Baby: "Take one more with my Aunt Xiaoshijie and Aunt Angel!"

All the love and blessings from TuMao brothers and sisters:

Postscript:

After leaving the hospital gate, Xiaoshijie said: It seems that Autumn and her husband are still relatively strong, not broken down, which is what comforts me most! I said: There is a home where there is love. May every member of our big family feel the warmth of mutual support and encouragement.

This donation activity has now ended, and the remaining 275.89 yuan will be delivered to them when Autumn comes to the hospital next time! But the blessing of love will never stop. Let's pray together for "Autumn's Baby!" Let's wait together for the day the baby recovers!!!

Come on, baby, we're looking forward to your quick recovery!
