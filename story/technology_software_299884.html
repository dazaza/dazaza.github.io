Three types of professionals enter the home decoration market and are accepted by citizens

by zry3276i0 on 2009-12-09 11:17:31

During renovation, property owners generally work with interior designers. However, recently three other types of professionals have started to participate in the renovation market and are being accepted by more and more citizens. They are home inspectors, home accessories designers, and indoor environmental treatment specialists.

Home Inspectors Prevent House Problems from Affecting Renovation

Miss Yang invited a home inspector before renovating her new house, not to find faults with the developer but to check if there were any problems that might affect the quality of the renovation. During the previous renovation of another house, she encountered a problem where the tiles cracked on a large scale. The renovation team claimed it was due to issues with the wall itself and refused to compensate. Miss Yang had no choice but to hire another team to remove and relay the tiles.

This time, Miss Yang hired a home inspector, even though it cost her a few hundred yuan. However, the home inspector could identify potential quality risks in the house and confirm with the renovation company that the house can be renovated without worry, avoiding unclear responsibilities later on.

Accessories Designers Cater to the Trend of Minimalist Renovation and Emphasizing Decoration

Mr. He fully embraced the concept of minimalistic renovation and emphasized decoration, so he did not even hire an interior designer for his renovation project. However, after completing the renovation, he was at a loss as to how to decorate his simple new home. Fortunately, a home accessories designer helped him solve the problem.

Compared to interior designers, home accessories designers focus more on soft renovation, also known as soft decoration. Designers use their overall grasp of color, texture, and style, along with their comprehensive aesthetic ability in art and fashion, to unify and coordinate seven categories of home products: furniture, lighting fixtures, hanging paintings, fabrics, floral arrangements, green plants, and display items. They then create an overall design and execute the details. Currently, home accessories designers charge according to the standard of 50-100 yuan per square meter.

Environmental Treatment Specialists Protect Homes from Toxic Gas

After finishing the renovation, Ms. Li felt that the smell in her new house was very strong. She negotiated with the renovation company, which believed this was a normal phenomenon. Helplessly, she invited professional personnel from an indoor environmental testing company to conduct an inspection. It turned out that the harmful gases were indeed above the safety limit. Finally, the renovation company agreed to pay for the services of environmental treatment personnel to handle the indoor environment.

After the indoor environmental treatment specialist arrived, they sprayed some agents. After some time, Ms. Li noticed that the smell had significantly decreased. The treatment specialist told her that she could move in with peace of mind but should keep the room ventilated regularly after moving in.
