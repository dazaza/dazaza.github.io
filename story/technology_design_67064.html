The sacred flame entered Sanya, and the Olympic tickets went on sale again. Like the enthusiastic and rising people across the country, the people of Tibet also put down their work and study, and flocked to the streets, rushing to the Tibet branch of the Bank of China in the urban area of Lhasa. They were willing to queue up for a long time to spend a fortune on a ticket. Normal people go through normal channels to the official website for ordering Olympic tickets, but some enterprising brothers and sisters have already stockpiled a considerable amount of tickets and started transferring Olympic tickets. People like Leesum who have some spare money may buy these high-priced tickets, while others can only sit in front of the TV on August 8th, resting their chins on their hands to watch the grand occasion of our country.
