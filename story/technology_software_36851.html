Top Ten Unions Apply for Batch Internal Test Numbers of "Xplay Roller Wind"

On November 20, 2007, the veteran Chinese game operator "China Game Center" www.chinagames.net finally unveiled the internal test of its latest Korean racing game - "Xplay Roller Wind." The initial 200 internal test numbers were quickly snapped up. Many gaming union alliances, upon learning that this Korean sports racing roller game was being distributed by China Games, have actively requested batch internal test numbers. A second round of large-scale internal test numbers for "Xplay Roller Wind" has already been swiftly distributed. Currently, more than 20 gaming unions have contacted China Games, hoping to participate in a one-month internal test, working together to identify issues and grow alongside the game.

China Gaming Guild Alliance

The China Gaming Guild Alliance consists of a coalition of 63 guilds of various sizes, led by the [Free World Guild] (ranked first among China's top ten guilds).
