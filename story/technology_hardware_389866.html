Let's explore traditional Chinese medicine beauty together, written by "The Taste of Whitening" in Intensive Care

Today, we shift our focus back to our traditional treasure - Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising ways to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that absolutely deserves our attention!

So, let's join the editor and delve into TCM beauty together~

"TCM beauty" carries a profound cultural heritage of China. After centuries of beauty practices, the time-tested folk methods of beauty and health preservation have gathered infinite Eastern wisdom.

Whether in ancient palaces or modern cities, women have always been thoughtful about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM beauty", with its holistic internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, has gained increasing admiration from people who value beauty and health.

However, now TCM beauty products come in various forms. What kind of method will be effective for you? Which product will benefit you? And how do you find a product that suits you? I believe these are the issues everyone is most concerned about, and also the headache-inducing problems.

For this reason, I've visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I discovered an issue: supermarkets and malls generally don't sell TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the advantages of their products, claiming what would happen after using them, and citing customer satisfaction. However, these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it hard for us to find actual users unless they're friends around us. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product surviving in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I place more emphasis on our traditional TCM beauty products.

Because of my work, I often browse online, so I decided to utilize the internet to look for solutions. Taobao is the largest shopping platform in China. Usually, I often buy clothes and international brand cosmetics there. But I haven't explored our own TCM beauty-related items online yet. A quick search revealed quite a few interesting options. I decided to choose a store with high credibility to inquire further.

I clicked into a TCM beauty shop named "Green Forest." Frankly, what initially attracted me was the image of an ancient beauty on their product packaging—indeed, the love for beauty transcends gender. Upon reading their introduction, it claimed to be a family-run TCM practice, with all the products formulated by their experienced TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity—I wondered if they could formulate a secret recipe specifically for me, turning me into a youthful, invincible young girl overnight. After checking several product introductions, I learned that the old TCM doctor in this shop was around 50 years old but looked like he was only 35! Truly impressive, filling me with admiration and envy. If I could look like I do now even at 50, that would be fantastic.

With this thought in mind, I decided to consult the shopkeeper as a potential customer to see how she introduced her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello. Are all your products purely made of TCM herbs?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Is there anything specific you need?

Editor: I'd like to learn more first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to know about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. Unlike general chemical or hormone-based beauty products, the effects aren’t immediate but address the root cause of the problem. Our products follow a one-month course. The speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some may see results after one course, while others might need two or more courses. Generally, the longer you use them, the better the results.

Editor: Have there been customers who didn't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers get impatient and stop using the products after just a few tries, claiming they’re ineffective. Our products aren't magic pills, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish complexions despite being young and free from other ailments. These customers generally suffer from qi and blood deficiency. We adopt a dual-supplement approach, focusing on both internal and external adjustments. Such customers usually require at least two months of treatment. Some give up halfway and then claim the products are ineffective. However, those who persevere always see results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM methods requires patience.

Shopkeeper: Indeed, because each person's condition is different, the time required for adjustment varies. Customers who persist always see significant improvements in their skin.

Editor: Thank you, shopkeeper. I have a basic understanding now. I'll come back to purchase when needed. Wishing you a booming business!

Shopkeeper: You're welcome. Feel free to visit anytime.

I believe the quality of a product lies in its reputation. The shop's credibility is good, with many repeat customers. I decided to investigate the feedback of their customers to verify the authenticity. From numerous online buyers, I selected one with a high credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she willingly chatted with me. Below is a summary of our main conversation (to protect her privacy, I’ll refer to her as Xiao A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this shop?

Xiao A: About eight months ago, due to prolonged sleep deprivation, my skin was in terrible condition. My complexion was dull, my skin was dry, fine lines appeared, and I even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products. Instead, I sought a gentle TCM spot-removal solution. I stumbled upon this shop by chance and started trying their products then. Seeing good results, I continued using them.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper tailored a whitening and spot-removal Yung Rong Pill, a full-effect whitening and spot-removal mask, and a skin-nourishing hydration and wrinkle-reducing mask for my skin.

Editor: Were they effective?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month, my complexion became much more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots slightly faded, and my skin lightened a bit. Although it wasn't extremely white, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin now?

Xiao A: Now, my skin is truly whiter and softer. The spots have significantly faded and are barely noticeable. With foundation, they're completely invisible. The fine lines have become much shallower, and my skin looks moist and radiant. In the morning, my skin even appears rosy. Honestly, I'm very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I've recommended it to some friends, and they've also reported great effects.

Editor: Did you experience any side effects?

Xiao A: No, since the shopkeeper inquired about my physical condition before recommending the products, I didn't notice any abnormalities. However, because TCM works slowly, it takes a longer time.

Editor: Are you still using them consistently?

Xiao A: Yes. I’ve been taking the Whitening and Spot-Removal Yung Rong Pills for six months. The full-effect mask and the skin-nourishing hydration and wrinkle-reducing mask, I've persisted in using twice a week, totaling eight months. The shopkeeper suggests that I take the pills again every two months since they also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is really excellent.

Xiao A: Yes. You can try it too. Talk to the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful aspiration of humanity, but all things in nature go through birth and death, prosperity and decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, with age, the human body will inevitably age.

TCM believes that although humans cannot stay perpetually young, by replenishing the deficiencies of organs and blood and qi, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the body indeed delays the arrival of aging. Today, with humanity's preference for natural and green concepts, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulation of strength, still holds unique charm in anti-aging, beauty, and health preservation.
