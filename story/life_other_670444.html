After many years, I still miss you!

by zhouzhou56 on 2012-02-20 21:20:52

Many years later, I still miss you!

Last night, the moon was bright and beautiful. I don't know if you were also quietly looking at it. When I looked at it, a smile involuntarily bloomed on my face, and deep in my heart, there was an indescribable joy. I like the moonlight, and I like to reminisce about that youthful era. But now, as a wife and mother, I no longer have the extra time to appreciate this unparalleled beauty. With reluctance and apology, I went to bed early to tell stories to my child.

At dawn, you appeared in my dreams. You took me to your home to play. You led the way ahead, and I followed for a long while but couldn't enter your house. I remember that you lived among the vegetable garden because I saw those tender little cucumbers, and I wanted to pick them to taste, but I was afraid you would disappear, so I kept following you towards your home. We walked around your house for a long time before finding your gate, but eventually, I still didn't go in.

I left and walked towards an orchard. Every step I took, I would look back. Looking back, I found that you were also watching me from not far away. Later, they told me that this was your family's orchard...

In my dream, you are still the same as when I saw you as a child. Why, after so many years, do I still think of you? I don't know! I thought our lives had changed drastically, and we were at a crossroads, never to intersect. I shouldn't have any lingering attachment to you anymore, but it wasn't like that. At least, tonight, I met you again in my dream. Just as you said, those memories are so beautiful, let them stay in the past! Just like that era, our feelings were pure and innocent.

Yes, having someone in my heart to miss is a kind of happiness! This happiness comes without reason, just like when I read two stories to my child, and she said to me: "Mom, I feel so happy!" I asked why, and she said she didn't know, she just felt happy! Happiness is indescribable, it's a faintly sweet joy!

Many years later, I still miss you! Memories are like a clear spring, nourishing my lonely and helpless soul! And no matter when, I will interpret this naive sentiment with a deep longing, forever...
