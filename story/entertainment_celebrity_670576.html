Profile of Bai Han:

Born in Yangzhou, Bai Han graduated from the Acting Department of the Central Academy of Drama. She was a first-class actress at the National Theatre Company of China. She once won the Outstanding Young Actor Award for drama by the Ministry of Culture. She won the "Magnolia Award" for Best Actress at the 16th Shanghai TV Festival for her role as "mother-in-law" Cao Xinmei in the series "The Wonderful Era of Daughter-in-Law". She passed away due to illness on February 19, 2012.

Awards:

Movie "Big and Small Madams" won an honorary award at the Bulgarian Comedy Festival.

Movie "Urban Love Stories" was nominated for Best Actress at the 14th Golden Rooster Awards.

TV series "The Wonderful Era of Daughter-in-Law" won the Best Actress Award at the 16th Shanghai TV Festival "Magnolia Award". Bai Han's portrayal of the mother-in-law in "The Wonderful Era of Daughter-in-Law" also received unanimous praise from the judges for breaking through her own image.
