Woman causes a stir by tearing off the dress of her husband's lover on the street (Photos)

by hj1718004 on 2011-10-22 20:26:22

In the circled area, a bare-chested man is trying to separate two women, drawing the attention of onlookers. (Photo provided by netizens) On Thursday at around 6 PM, a farce unfolded on the streets of Changzhou when a middle-aged woman caught her husband and his mistress near Cui Zhu Avenue. Enraged, the woman tore off the dress of the other woman, leaving her in her underwear as she hurriedly made her way into a car. The entanglement among the three drew a crowd of onlookers, with some citizens taking photos that were later posted on a local Changzhou forum. By the time the article was published, it had garnered over 30,000 clicks.

Netizen posts pictures of the original wife catching the mistress

The day before yesterday, a post titled "Original Wife Rips Mistress's Clothes on Cui Zhu Avenue Street - A Man and Two Women Engage in a Struggle" appeared on the Hualong Lane Forum. The post included several on-site photos, each accompanied by textual descriptions.

According to the post, the original wife caught her husband and his mistress, leading to an entanglement where the wife continuously cried out, accusing them of being shameless. She lamented how she and her husband had built their life from scratch, only for this woman to take her husband away. In the photos posted by the楼主 "White Sneakers," one can see the man, shirtless, pulling his wife while the so-called mistress inside the car is wearing only her underwear and has her head down. At the scene of the dispute, many citizens gathered around the car to watch, even drawing the attention of traffic police.

Stripped to Underwear and Hiding in the Car

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter contacted the poster, Ms. Chen. Ms. Chen stated that around 6 PM the previous day, while looking for her husband in the parking lot, she witnessed this scene and went up to check the situation.

Ms. Chen introduced that her husband had seen this incident first. When the roughly 30-year-old original wife tore off the clothes of the 20-something mistress, the man quickly removed his own shirt to give to the woman, but she didn't have time to put it on and instead held it to cover her chest as she ran to the car.

When Ms. Chen arrived, she saw the original wife already outside the car pulling the underwear of the mistress, yelling and cursing, accusing her of destroying her family. The onlookers watching also cursed the mistress, calling her someone who ruins families. The mistress dared not speak, only repeatedly saying not to look and asking the onlookers to leave her alone.

"Bystanders said that the original wife caught her husband and mistress near the square around Cuiyuan Shijia, then grabbed the mistress's clothes and tore them off. Subsequently, the original wife threw the clothes away. It's said that the car was bought by the man for the mistress," Ms. Chen said.

"Her husband tried to pull her away, but she refused to let go, resulting in him slapping her a few times." Ms. Chen indicated that the situation escalated, attracting more people. A nearby traffic police officer then arrived to maintain order. "The police told them to stop arguing and dispersed us. Later, when our car drove out, that car followed behind us," Ms. Chen said.

Neighbors Confirm the Incident

The reporter went to the parking lot in front of Cuiyuan Shijia, where the boss of a nearby newspaper stand said that on Thursday evening, indeed there was an incident of women fighting on the temporary parking lane of Cui Zhu Avenue, drawing a large crowd. However, since he had to watch the stand, he did not go to see, and thus was unclear about the specifics.

An owner of Cuiyuan Shijia informed the reporter that on that day, he saw a woman leaning on a Lexus car, using both hands to pull someone inside, crying and shouting. A bare-chested man was dragging that woman out of the car. The license plate number was auspicious, with two "9"s. "Listening to what the woman was yelling, it seemed like she was accusing the woman inside the car of seducing her husband."

Another resident also told the reporter that it was clear from the situation that it was a wife beating the mistress. The man was shirtless, a bit fat, with a string of beads around his neck, looking quite wealthy. "Everyone discussed how the couple had started from nothing, but after becoming rich, the man found a mistress and even hit his own wife in public. This man is really despicable."
