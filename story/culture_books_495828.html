"Du Lala's Grand Finale" - From Naive White-Collar Worker to Free Middle-Class

by man8737 on 2011-09-27 16:38:36

Du Lala finally got her wish, leaving her old company DB to join SH as the Compensation & Benefits Manager. Compared to being an administrative manager at DB, this role in salary and benefits management offered a much brighter career prospect. However, changing jobs often means that while it's hard enough to find a new one, the tough times are just beginning - especially for a compensation manager like Lala who has never dealt with payroll before. The existing compensation system at SH is overly complicated but lacks scientific rigor, leaving Lala constantly on the defensive. Without her subordinate George Shen, she wouldn't even feel confident setting salaries for new employees.

It was different for Li Weidong, the training manager who started at the same time as Lala. As an expert in his field, he made a great first impression right from the start. Not only that, he took the initiative to request overhauling the recruitment process, greatly pleasing his superior’s superior, David Mai.

Lala's confidence was repeatedly shaken by Lu Baby, who is both Wang Wei's cousin and boss, due to her remarkable looks. The casual body language between Lu Baby and Wang Wei seemed to remind everyone that she held at least partial privileges over Wang Wei. Lala felt upset inside, but couldn't express it.

An unexpected accident casts uncertainty over the future relationship between Lala and Wang Wei, leaving them both deeply saddened... Life must go on. After experiencing extreme highs and lows, Lala gradually grows step by step.

For her ideals, she is always prepared.
