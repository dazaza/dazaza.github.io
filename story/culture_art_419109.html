College entrance exam has rules, and mental attitude is very important.

by dianxin8 on 2011-04-20 18:57:35

Before the exam, it is important not to have overly high expectations for physiological performance. Don't always think that you must be admitted to a prestigious university. This is the advice from ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network to all candidates. Remember, the college entrance exam is just one phase of life, not the entirety. As long as you treat the test with a calm mind and fully demonstrate your abilities, that is success. How you approach reviewing and testing has a significant impact on achieving optimal state. If you view reviewing and testing as a challenge, it will help you quickly enter the right mindset; if you see it as an exercise, you will engage with a peaceful mind; if you regard it as an opportunity, you will welcome it with a positive attitude.

From the questions raised by students, the writer of ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network observes that modern students are somewhat anxious, with excessive mental tension. Many hope to improve their grades in the shortest time possible. It can also be seen that after a period of intense review, students are eager to try out what they've learned. Additionally, there is some concern about the upcoming mock exam, with some students worrying that the questions might be difficult. This actually reflects a lack of self-confidence. Some students have opinions about English classes, so we let the class representative record them and communicate them to the English teacher in a timely manner.

Regarding the mock exam, we tell the students that this exam will differ somewhat from the previous semester's exams, with some new reforms in the test questions. However, no matter how things change, the core remains the same: it will test how well your recent review has gone, how well you have mastered knowledge points, and how effective your skills training has been. We should still adhere to the principle of stability amidst change, thoroughly consolidate our knowledge, carefully study methods, take scores lightly, and face pressure with a smile.

For the anxiety among students, the writer of ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network tells the students to trust in the efforts made during this period. Furthermore, recently, our entire class has been united and working hard together, and everyone's progress is evident to the teachers. Trust in your accumulated knowledge, which is certainly enough to withstand the exam. Trust in your instincts, act when the time is right. Trust in the methods and guidance provided by the teachers, and actively collaborate with them. Trust in your own capacity, being able to pick up and put down easily. Trust in your good habits, because good habits lead to good scores!

Secondly, abandon utilitarian thoughts. Do not worry excessively about gains or losses, do not focus solely on scores without considering methods; do not only look at your mistakes while ignoring your progress; do not care only about appearances and neglect effectiveness. Keep your goals and plans in mind.

We should appropriately follow some rules and cannot completely disregard them. We cannot resort to extreme measures or deceit. We need to strengthen supervision over private schools' tutoring processes and cultivation methods, ensuring that their courses comply with relevant regulations. Regarding economic matters, we believe market regulation is sufficient. We cannot stipulate that private schools spending 9.9 yuan out of every 10 earned is the norm, leaving only 0.1 yuan as profit; anything exceeding this would be considered profit-driven.

The writer of ks5u College Entrance Exam Resources Network believes that both levels 4 and 6 are highly regulated tests. Their question types remain consistently stable year after year. You need to become familiar with these question types and understand how much time each major type requires, which can significantly enhance your test efficiency. For instance, many people worry about the quick reading section, believing it to be impossible to complete. In reality, this part isn't difficult; it mainly tests speed. The distribution follows a clear pattern, with each paragraph generally corresponding to one question. By looking at the questions first and then reading the paragraphs to find answers, you can proceed much faster. For the last three word-filling questions, some require the original form, while others can be replaced with synonyms or similar words.
