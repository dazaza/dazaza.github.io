by bjseeprof on 2009-12-07 11:26:34

I always feel that the time at night should be cherished. There are quite a few trivial things at night, so it has been a long time since I last sat down quietly to listen to music. I don't follow popular music, nor do I know where to find music types that suit my taste. In the past, I liked heavy metal, so I would listen to Rammstein or Guns N'Roses from time to time. Especially Rammstein, they have such a tough spirit. When I searched for "German war chariot" on QQ Music, I found this song 《Stirb nicht vor mir》on the second screen, which I've never heard before. After trying it out, I think it's highly recommendable. As online introductions say: "This song is extremely tender, but still resolute. It comes from the 2005 album Rosenrot (Rose Red), and the female singer is Sharleen Spiteri from the British band Texas." Let's listen together. With a good sound system in the company's backdrop wall, it's a pity that there's no coffee prepared.
