According to the experts from the medical beauty surgery center, he once performed a rhinoplasty for a lady who was doing jade business and was called "not wealth-bringing" by her clients in Guangzhou. As for those who want to improve their husband's luck, there are even more cases. For example, special requirement surgeries such as padding the temples, lowering the cheekbones, raising the eyebrows, and making the nasolabial folds shallower are even more common.

Experts: A good mindset is more important than a good face

It is understood that the age of beauties seeking this kind of plastic surgery generally falls between 30 to 45 years old, mostly people who want to bring good fortune to their husbands or do business, with female beauties accounting for 80%, while male beauties have increased significantly compared to last year. Plastic surgery experts pointed out that the situation of these beauties seeking plastic surgery roughly falls into three categories: one is feeling that a flat nose affects financial luck, so they request a nose job; the second is mole removal, they believe that moles on the face or certain parts affect their career, love, etc., so they request removal; the third is feeling that "eyebrows not covering the eyes, loneliness dispersing wealth", so they request eyebrow shaping.

During interviews, plastic surgery experts unanimously believed that actually, the results of these facial feature-based plastic surgeries often aren't beautiful, but since the patients all have clear goals, the outcomes of the surgeries are mostly satisfactory. They have also tried to persuade these people from a professional perspective, but it has been ineffective because before coming for plastic surgery, these individuals have mostly experienced tremendous psychological pressure, unrelated to beauty, like liposuction for slimming.

Most people undergo plastic surgery to become more beautiful, but some people do it to bring good fortune to their husbands or wealth. The author recently learned from several major plastic surgery hospitals in Nanning that the trend of face-changing plastic surgery has risen again, with becoming wealth-bringing or husband-fortune-bringing being popular.

"Can you help me 'file' my high cheekbones lower and pad my nose higher to make me look more wealth-bringing?" Two weeks ago, Mr. Zhao told the doctor his plastic surgery requirements at a certain plastic surgery hospital. At 39 years old, Mr. Zhao told the author that he lives in Xixiangtang District, Nanning, and has been engaged in fruit wholesale business for a long time. Because he increasingly feels that his business is hard to manage, he always thinks it's due to his non-wealth-bringing face, so he had the idea of undergoing plastic surgery. Now, after having his "wealth-bringing" nose done, Mr. Zhao feels much more at ease. He said that although his business hasn't changed much after the plastic surgery, he still firmly believes that eventually, he will "turn his luck around."

People undergo plastic surgery for various reasons, some to correct inherent flaws, some to become more beautiful, and now there are also those who do it for face-changing purposes. Regarding this situation, experts believe that a good mindset is more important than a good face.

"Nowadays, among those coming for plastic surgery, there is a group of people who don't seek beauty, accounting for about 8%. They come because they have a 'husband-killing face' or a 'poverty face,'" said a plastic surgery expert from a certain clinic in Nanning. According to the introduction, one-fifth of the women here come for "husband-killing face" corrections.

The expert reminded that there is no scientific basis for determining whether someone is "wealth-bringing" or "wealth-draining." For beauties who believe in "husband-fortune-bringing faces," "wealth-bringing faces," and "official-promoting faces," the first thing to do is to adjust their own mindset and understand that a happy life can only be created through one's own efforts, and cannot be judged by appearance.
