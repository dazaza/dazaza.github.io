Difan Introduction:

The predecessor of Difan was established in October 1999. Over the course of three years, Difan has grown from an unknown small store into a well-known cosmetic chain store throughout Changzhou and even Jiangsu province.

In March 2005, Difan signed a cooperation contract with Shiseido (China) Investment Co., Ltd., the largest cosmetics company in Asia. The high-quality products and brand reputation have established a solid and stable market in the Changzhou area, and Difan has been rated as Shiseido's Gold Specialty Store every year.

Currently, Difan has over 300,000 active members.

In 2005, Difan was awarded "Trustworthy Unit".

All products passed quality inspections conducted by provincial and municipal administrations for industry and commerce with a 100% pass rate.
