China's first case of copyright infringement in the education industry: New Thinking sues New Starting Point, and New Starting Point loses the case!

Civil Judgment of Haidian District People's Court of Beijing Municipality

(2011) Hai Min Chu No. 24619

The plaintiff, Xin Si Lu Training School of Haidian District, Beijing (hereinafter referred to as "Xin Si Lu School"), sued the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "New Starting Point Company") for copyright infringement and unfair competition disputes. After accepting the case, our court lawfully assigned Judge Li Dongtao to preside over the trial alone, and a public hearing was held. The entrusted agents of the plaintiff, Xin Si Lu School, Xu Jingwu and Chen Jichun, and the entrusted agent of the defendant, New Starting Point Company, Feng Xiaoyu, attended the litigation. This case has now been concluded.

The plaintiff, Xin Si Lu School, stated that it was founded in 1999 and had received numerous honors such as [Ministry of Education Network Education Demonstration Institution], making it a modernized, standardized comprehensive private educational institution. In May 2011, in order to better promote itself, the school arranged for its online marketing department, computer professional teachers, and students to jointly create the website homepage (www.xsledu.com). The website took more than two months to be completed by more than ten staff members including planners, programmers, and graphic designers, featuring 221 basic images, 221 design layouts, over 100,000 edited words, with a total design capacity of over 1G bytes, at a production cost of about 240,000 yuan. In September 2011, the plaintiff discovered that the defendant's website homepage (http://bj.xqdedu.net) contained more than thirty instances of plagiarism and theft of the plaintiff’s website content, severely infringing on the plaintiff’s copyright. The defendant also misused the plaintiff’s website filing registration number. Furthermore, the defendant falsely claimed multiple exclusive qualifications and honors of the plaintiff, such as being the "[Designated Educational Assistance Institution of the Examination Center of the Ministry of Education]," clearly constituting fraudulent behavior and forming unfair competition against the plaintiff. Therefore, the plaintiff requests the court to rule the defendant:

1. Immediately cease infringement and delete the infringing content from its bj.xqdedu.net webpage;

2. Apologize to the plaintiff in the Beijing Times and on the bj.xqdedu.net webpage;

3. Compensate the plaintiff for economic losses amounting to 1 million yuan;

4. Compensate the plaintiff for reasonable expenses incurred due to this case;

5. Bear all litigation costs.

The defendant, New Starting Point Company, argued that the plaintiff did not submit evidence proving that the pages on its website existed prior to the notarization date of September 2, 2011, therefore even if there were identical contents between the plaintiff and defendant's websites, it could not prove that the defendant had "plagiarized" the plaintiff's website. The complained website www.xqdedu.net [http://www.xqdedu.net/] was registered under Liu Wenjing's personal documents, who is the owner and person in charge of the website. Therefore, the complained website is unrelated to the defendant. The defendant is not the owner or operator of the complained website, and whether the website infringed is irrelevant to the defendant. The plaintiff's claim for economic loss lacks a basis for calculation. The court is requested to dismiss all of the plaintiff's claims.

After investigation, it was found that the domain name of [Xin Si Lu School] is xsledu.com, the hosting unit is Xin Si Lu School, the filing number is Jing ICP Bei 07029102, and the website person in charge is Li Dongmei. The domain name of [New Starting Point] is xqdedu.net, the hosting unit and website person in charge are both Liu Wenjing, and the filing number is Jing ICP Bei 09044722.

On September 15, 2011, the Beijing Wen Zheng Notary Office issued Notarial Deed No. (2011) Jing Fang Zheng Nei Jing Zheng Zi Di 08699. This notarial deed shows that the agent of Xin Si Lu School used the notary office's computer to enter the website bj.xqdedu.net, which displayed Jing ICP Bei 07029102 Copyright@2010-2011 XQDedu.net, confirming the use of Xin Si Lu School's ICP filing number on the website but not acknowledging ownership of the website. [mailto:Jing ICP Bei 07029102 Copyright@2010-2011%20XQDedu.net]

During the trial, Xin Si Lu School submitted a CD containing the source code files of its website to prove that the pictures and other content on the involved website originated from its own website. After comparison, it was found that there were many similarities between Xin Si Lu School's website and New Starting Point Company's website in terms of filing numbers, certificates, pictures, and layout content. New Starting Point Company refused to acknowledge this, arguing that the generation time of Xin Si Lu School's website source code could not be determined to precede the notarization time.

On December 2, 2011, Li Dongmei issued a statement indicating that although she was the person in charge of Xin Si Lu School's website www.xsledu.com [http://www.xsledu.com/] (Jing ICP Bei 07029102), the actual operator and owner of the website was Xin Si Lu School. New Starting Point Company recognized that www.xsledu.com [http://www.xsledu.com/] belonged to Xin Si Lu School but questioned the authenticity of Li Dongmei's signature.

Xin Si Lu School submitted invoices for 2500 yuan in notary fees and 15000 yuan in legal fees.

The above facts are supported by the filing information, notarial deeds, statements, invoices provided by Xin Si Lu School, the filing information provided by New Starting Point Company, and the trial record of this court.

This court holds that as the owner of the Xin Si Lu School website, Xin Si Lu School legally enjoys copyright over the website content. The web page layout, design style, background color, animation, pictures, artwork, text, etc., of the website bj.xqdedu.net are similar to those of Xin Si Lu School's website, with identical content. Although the responsible person for the ICP registration of the website is Liu Wenjing, the content of the website pertains entirely to the business of New Starting Point Company. In the absence of contrary evidence from the company, it should be considered the actual user of the website. Without permission from the rights holder, the company used the content of Xin Si Lu School's website on its own website, including Xin Si Lu School's certificates, constituting copyright infringement and also unfair competition, and should bear legal responsibility for infringement. The company denies the infringement, but without submitting relevant evidence, this court does not accept it. Xin Si Lu School demands that New Starting Point Company apologize and compensate for economic losses of 1 million yuan, but the evidence is insufficient. This court will determine the method of responsibility based on factors such as the degree of infringement by New Starting Point Company, and will not fully support its claims.

Accordingly, in accordance with Article 48 (1) and Article 49 Paragraph 2 of the Copyright Law of the People's Republic of China, Article 2 Paragraph 1 and Article 14 of the Anti-Unfair Competition Law of the People's Republic of China, the judgment is as follows:

1. From the date this judgment takes effect, the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd., shall cease infringement;

2. Within thirty days from the date this judgment takes effect, the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd., shall publish a statement in the Beijing Times to eliminate the influence on the plaintiff, Xin Si Lu Training School of Haidian District, Beijing (the statement must be reviewed by this court; if the defendant fails to comply within the stipulated period, this court will publicly disclose the main content of the judgment in the Beijing Times, with the costs borne by the defendant);

3. Within ten days from the date this judgment takes effect, the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd., shall compensate the plaintiff, Xin Si Lu Training School of Haidian District, Beijing, for economic losses and reasonable expenses amounting to 150,000 yuan;

4. Dismiss the other claims of the plaintiff, Xin Si Lu Training School of Haidian District, Beijing.

If the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd., fails to perform the monetary obligation as specified in this judgment within the designated period, it shall, according to Article 229 of the Civil Procedure Law of the People's Republic of China, pay double interest on the debt during the delayed performance period.

The case acceptance fee is 6,900 yuan (prepaid by the plaintiff), which shall be borne by the defendant, Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (to be paid within seven days after this judgment takes effect).

If dissatisfied with this judgment, an appeal may be filed within fifteen days from the date of receipt of the judgment, along with copies according to the number of opposing parties, and the appeal case acceptance fee shall be paid, appealing to the First Intermediate People's Court of Beijing. If the appeal case acceptance fee is not paid within seven days after the expiration of the appeal period, it shall be deemed as automatically withdrawing the appeal.
