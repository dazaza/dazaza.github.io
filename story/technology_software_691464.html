Hongchang Group's Golden Merchant Stories

by hongchang81 on 2012-03-05 16:15:37

The Story of Jinshang [Attached with photos, General Manager Xiaoping Zhao (Jinshang Story)] Time traces back to 1993, which was already 18 years ago. A business elite from Zhejiang, possessing the typical qualities of Zhe merchants—courage, decisiveness, enthusiasm, generosity, meticulousness, and loyalty—had already been striving in Urumqi, the western hub of Xinjiang, for nearly a decade. At the age of forty, he chose to act as the agent for "Hongchang" paint. He is Mr. Xiaoping Zhao, the general manager of "Jiufeng Trading Co., Ltd." in Urumqi, Xinjiang (hereinafter referred to as Mr. Zhao).

Confucius once said: "At fifteen I set my heart upon learning; at thirty, I had planted my feet firmly on the ground; at forty, I no longer suffered from perplexities; at fifty, I knew what were the biddings of Heaven; at sixty, I heard them with docile ear; at seventy, I could follow what my heart desired without transgressing what was right." Indeed, forty is the age of no confusion. Perhaps it was a fortuitous coincidence or perhaps an destined affinity, but more importantly, it was a correct choice made by a wise businessman after seeing through disturbances and discerning right from wrong. Because of Hongchang Chemical's strength and vitality, the professionalism and dedication of Hongchang people, and the charm of Hongchang paint, Mr. Zhao has a special preference for Hongchang paint, thus forging an indissoluble bond with Hongchang.

By the year 2011, Mr. Zhao has been operating in the paint business for exactly 18 years, coinciding with the 20th anniversary of Hongchang Chemical's establishment. Hongchang has gone through twenty springs and autumns, enduring twenty years of wind, snow, frost, and rain, creating a brilliant miracle in China's private coating enterprises. For 18 of these years, Mr. Zhao has walked hand in hand with Hongchang, from starting from zero to achieving the current sales scale, becoming the mainstream furniture paint brand in Urumqi, firmly occupying the high-end furniture paint market share in Urumqi. This has been a process of hard work, continuous growth, and ups and downs.

In these 18 years of choosing Hongchang, Mr. Zhao has always adhered to the business philosophy of integrity, win-win, professionalism, service, and benevolence, taking stable development and sound operations as the core business strategy. When talking about these, Mr. Zhao always has endless topics to discuss. In the early stages of operation, Mr. Zhao did not have much knowledge about paint, product performance, and market operations. It was the pragmatic team of Hongchang that patiently and meticulously accompanied Mr. Zhao, conducting training session after session, assisting him in visiting furniture factories time and again, and continuously improving products according to the characteristics of weather and environment in Northern Xinjiang. These memories accurately reflect the style of Hongchang people, which has been infecting Mr. Zhao all along and continues to be the excellent quality of Hongchang people today.

Exclusive dealership, dealing exclusively, Mr. Zhao is the sole furniture paint agent of Hongchang Chemical in Urumqi, Xinjiang, and Hongchang paint is also Mr. Zhao's only agency brand. As Mr. Zhao puts it, since I've chosen it, I will continue down this path. I trust Hongchang. Over the years, Mr. Zhao has consistently sold Hongchang products as a complete package, from primer to topcoat, from curing agents to thinners, effectively ensuring the quality of the finished products in furniture factories. In the face of small profits, Mr. Zhao knows when to let go, saying that reputation is the most effective marketing communication channel.

After gaining a deeper understanding of Mr. Zhao, there is one incident that deeply moved me, truly embodying the benevolence and righteousness of this tough guy. The incident goes like this: Mr. Zhao's brother also ran a factory in Xinjiang. Due to an accidental fire, the entire factory was destroyed, and Mr. Zhao's brother ended up in prison. This was undoubtedly a huge disaster for his brother's family. However, at this critical moment, Mr. Zhao did not hesitate and stepped forward to take responsibility for handling the aftermath of the disaster. He also unhesitatingly took on the responsibility of caring for his brother's family, including the education and employment of two nephews and nieces, and even the compensation to employees after the fire, all of which Mr. Zhao bore entirely. Mr. Zhao said, blood is thicker than water, and these are things I should do as a younger brother. Regardless of how much money I have spent, I am at peace, and my wife supports and agrees with me. I thank my wife for her support. Indeed, the power of love is so strong!

Mr. Zhao is so benevolent and sentimental towards his family, and equally righteous towards his friends. Mr. Zhao often says, "A person without credibility cannot stand. If you promise something, you must deliver it; otherwise, don't make the promise." Mr. Zhao is very emotional towards his family, relatives, and friends, sometimes even letting emotions guide his actions. However, in work, Mr. Zhao is a person with very strong principles and is very rigorous. For example, during internal personnel position changes, both parties need to properly hand over relevant business matters with related units. Warehouse managers strictly adhere to the first-in-first-out principle to ensure fresh product dates. Any incorrect or omitted documents must be carefully checked, and responsibilities will be pursued without exception. Salespeople who are not diligent in promoting products will receive severe criticism and positive education. No detail in the internal business and external对接processes is overlooked. Mr. Zhao said that all of this is for our common progress, and we need this concept.

Looking to the future, Hongchang Chemical stands at a new starting point after 20 years, and our beloved motherland also welcomes the opening of the Twelfth Five-Year Plan. Society is harmonious and stable, and the economy is developing steadily. Mr. Zhao has deeply considered and planned strategically for the development of his "Jiufeng Trading Co., Ltd." In short, continuing to promote the correct business philosophy and strategy he has followed for 18 years. At the same time, keeping abreast of the times, following the steps of Hongchang Chemical, closely aligning with and leading the trend of coating, and deepening the market positioning of Hongchang's high-end furniture paint. Reasonably adjusting his operational team towards the direction of scientific and standardized, efficient, pragmatic, innovative, and sustainable development, writing new brilliant chapters together with Hongchang people!
