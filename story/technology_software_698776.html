Music makes cities more charming.

by yangliu435sh on 2012-03-09 11:09:29

The musician Xian Xinghai once said: "Music is the greatest happiness in life; music is a clear spring in life; music is the foundry of temperament." Indeed, for thousands of years, with its profound implication and indescribable melody, music has formed an ever-flowing, glittering river that makes people admire and indulge.

Firstly, it can cultivate one's temperament. The writer Ye Wenling said "Music will always be my soul's Eden." The philosopher Plato also believed that "Music education is much more important than other educations." Indeed, the beautiful melody and changing rhythm in music have an irreplaceable charm and power to cultivate people's spirit and temperament. Most of the music in daily life influences our morality, willpower, character and sentiment imperceptibly like "moistening things silently."

Secondly, it can develop intelligence. When we play the flute, pluck the guzheng, or perform piano pieces, our eyes need to look at the score, our brain needs to command both hands' ten fingers to sensitively play the notes arranged in the score into a melodious tune, and our ears need to fully function to sensitively correct and distinguish whether the sound matches the score. Thus, in the process of performance, performers actually unconsciously exercise their brain, which develops both the left and right brain over time, making people smarter.

Additionally, in music activities and dances, people can also coordinate the movement of both hands and reach the goal of exercising the left and right brain unintentionally.

Thirdly, it can decorate life. A philosopher once said: Music is an eternal theme in human life. In people's social life, music is almost everywhere. Especially now, when modern cultural media tools, such as tape recorders, televisions, karaoke machines, DVD players, etc., enter millions of ordinary families in urban and rural areas, music becomes even more colorful.

Music is a kind of cultural exchange suitable for all ages. It attracts numerous art lovers with its unique artistic charm and popular melodies. When you are happy, listening to a lyrical piece of music will bring you to a very beautiful realm, making you feel refreshed! When you are troubled and sad, listening to a gentle tune will make your mood calm and far away from troubles; when you are leisurely enjoying yourself, listening to an inspiring melody will make you feel your heart surging with excitement!

With its vast bosom and endless artistic charm, music infects countless heroes generation after generation, making them dedicate themselves and strive for it!

When walking on the street, the beautiful music played by roadside stores refreshes your spirit and forgets fatigue; when boating on the lake, the light music drifting from afar makes you feel relaxed and forget to return; when wandering in the fields, the sudden arrival of crisp and moving mountain songs will make you lose yourself; when lying in your mother's arms, the gentle and intimate lullaby will make your mind and body calm down, gradually falling asleep... Therefore, music has become an indispensable part of people's lives. We cannot imagine how silent and tasteless life would be without music.

Music knows no boundaries. It can purify people's souls, cultivate their sentiments, and enlighten their lives.

I like music. Listening to Bach's fugue is romantic and elegant; Berlioz's fantasy is unrestrained and free-spirited; Schumann's serenade is peaceful and leisurely; Pavarotti's high-pitched voice; Sarah Brightman's softness; Li Na's "Qingzang Plateau". Ah, that is the heavenly sound!

I like listening to the saxophone playing "Going Home". The low, winding, and deep melody stirs up my unforgettable deep longing for my hometown. The music, like a complaint, is like a returning sail, sending me back to my hometown to pick up dreams. The scenery of the Western Regions, the oases in the desert, the pearls on the Gobi Desert, and the grasslands at the foot of the Tianshan Mountains all make me dream day and night. This nostalgic music brings me a desolate realm and moves me to tears.

I also like to listen to the cheerful campus songs played by guitars, and I prefer to listen to the love-themed pop songs accompanied by electronic organs...

Whether it is Western music or national music, I like to listen to them all. Appreciating wonderful music is a beautiful enjoyment for me. Quietly listening to the "language of the soul" of music, letting the music stir up ripples of emotion in my innermost heart.

Especially when the snowflakes fall quietly outside the window, the pure and crystal snowflakes give me a quiet and sacred feeling. Only turning on a desk lamp, letting the golden halo warmly cover me, sipping a cup of fragrant tea quietly. Turning on the stereo, I enjoy wonderful music together with the dancing snowflakes outside the window and the fragrance in the tea!

Standing in the hall of national music, my heart seems to release the elegance of Yangchun Baixue and the grandeur of Huangzhong Dali...

Listening to the rhythm of high mountains and flowing water, the seven-stringed ancient qin made Bo Ya and Zi Qi become soulmates in life, searching for melancholy strings to play a wonderful melody, telling an old story.

Under the vast sky, by the Taihu Lake, a blind man plays the lute alone, "Reflections of the Moon on the Second Spring" makes Abing's sorrowful eyes burst with determination and strength!

Listening to the beautiful sadness in the violin concerto "Butterfly Lovers", one can't help but trace the tragic love story, yearn for the graceful butterfly dance, and linger on the tower filled with too much longing...

Wandering in the holy land of foreign music, I feel a kind of culture and spirit, permeated with a great love for music! Chopin uses his unique heart to express patriotic feelings; Beethoven uses his magical hands to compose pastoral tunes and collide with the door of "Fate"; Tchaikovsky stops by the tranquil lakeside to watch swans dance; Schubert leaves a lonely figure under the wandering linden tree...

Listening to music at dusk is a special kind of enjoyment. At that moment, the twilight is deep, everything in the house appears hazy and blurred, only the desk lamp flashes a soft glow. Quietly, nothing can disturb the brain, the soul lets the hearing lead like a dream-like drift into the temple of music.

The joy and solemnity of string music, the gentle and mournful lamentation, the singing and dreaming; the elegance and cheerfulness of plucked instruments, the loud big strings, the delicate small strings; the beauty of woodwind music; the brightness of brass music; the shock of percussion music; the splendor of keyboard music...

At this moment, my feeling is strong and strange, my heart is like being washed by this music and becomes sacred. I don't know if my heart is immersed in the music or the music is full in my heart, like a hidden stream twisting and turning. Then, I am moved, and the feeling of being touched gradually intensifies, I suddenly can't control myself, is it sweetness? Sadness? Missing? Grievance? Already failed expectations? Unattainable sweetness?...

This rhythm, this melody continuously collides with my soul, collides with my memory, suddenly, my eyes blur, tears roll down my cheeks, I lift my head, use a handkerchief to wipe my tears, completely unable to control myself. This abnormal feeling is so strong, so shocking!

Music purifies and cultivates my soul.

Entering the kingdom of music, I will find the home of my heart there, find peace and liberation. After receiving a baptism of music and reluctantly leaving its gate, my heart becomes light and happy.

The charm of music is infinite. Music has become the most intimate friend in my life, bringing me infinite happiness and happiness.
