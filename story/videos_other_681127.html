The new dress gives you the experience of a fashionable life.

by qushi888999 on 2012-02-28 11:22:08

Fashion is the inevitable requirement of the development of human social activities, the inevitable trend of social and economic development, and the inevitable result of the emergence of new needs in human life when the economy develops to a certain stage. Economic development and ideological openness provide objective conditions for the development of clothing. The historical origin of ancient costume culture in China has a long history. Looking at the development history of Chinese costumes, it is also one of the development histories of fashionable life, and an important branch of the development history of human social life that cannot be separated. We can easily find from the classical styles of dresses that costumes existed as early as the legendary period of the Three Sovereigns and Five Emperors. For example, during the Warring States period, the "Lüshi Chunqiu" recorded that during the time of the Yellow Emperor, "Hu Cao made clothes"; or it was said: "Bo Yu and the Yellow Emperor made clothes." This era, according to the cultural relics unearthed from archaeological excavations, should be around 5,000 to 6,000 years ago, during the prosperous period of the matrilineal clan society of the primitive society. China has long been known as the "Kingdom of Clothing," and starting from the Xia and Shang dynasties, new-style boot and crown clothing systems began to appear. What kind of boots are popular this year? The Zhou Dynasty was the historical era when China's crown and clothing system began to gradually develop and improve. During the Eastern Zhou and Warring States periods, due to the rise of the Hundred Schools of Thought and the active exchange of ideas, the development of clothing kept pace with the times; by the Western Zhou period, it had basically reached perfection, and at this time, there were already many written records about clothing. In the inscriptions on bronze vessels, there are records about men's fashion brands, such as "Tiger Crown Practice Inside" (Mao Gong Ding) and "Female Fur Treasure Palace" (Zhou, Bo Cai Wen Gui). With the emergence of the feudal hierarchy, distinctions between high and low, honorable and humble, various rituals also came into being accordingly. Clothing not only specifically reflected the lifestyle and living standards of the Chinese nation but also vividly reproduced the changes and sublimation of human thought consciousness and aesthetic concepts; their reflection in clothing mainly includes sacrificial attire, court assembly attire, military attire, mourning attire, and wedding attire, etc.; these clothes gradually adapted to emperors and commoners, and were even used for more than two thousand years since the Shang and Zhou dynasties in the feudal society of 2012's popular fashion. As times change, foreign cultures keep flowing into Chinese culture, thus the trends in popular clothing colors will, in turn, influence uniform colors, which is the result of the mutual interaction of these two color cultures. The process of the development of Chinese costume history is also the process of the development of Chinese national cultural history, where the true connotation of Chinese fashion lies, and it is also the root of people's pursuit of fashionable life.
