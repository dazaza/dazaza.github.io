Love for the disaster area: more than 200 Sany equipments participate in post-disaster reconstruction

by ccm168 on 2009-05-27 16:19:16

At this time last year, the Sany Group placed earthquake rescue and disaster relief as its top priority. More than 80 rescuers and 15 sets of equipment from Changsha, Chongqing, or Chengdu headed for one destination – Mianyang disaster area, starting that unforgettable period of earthquake resistance.

Afterwards, most of the rescuers returned, but some still remained in the disaster area to work, including the rescuers from the Sichuan branch company and donated equipment, until today.

On the morning of May 12th, Ma Lei boarded a bus from Mianyang to Beichuan County. A year ago, he was once a member of the Sany Group's earthquake rescue and disaster relief service team, participating in the rescue work in Mianyang disaster area. Subsequently, as a marketing representative of the Pumping Branch Company of Sichuan in the Mianyang region, he stayed behind to manage the pumping equipment here.

Provincial Road 105 is the inevitable route from Mianyang to Beichuan. A year ago, the sudden disaster made tents stretch for hundreds of miles along both sides of the road, with huge rocks occasionally rolling down from nearby mountaintops, adding unpredictable dangers to this provincial road.

Nowadays, Provincial Road 105 has taken on a new look, and the tents near the highway have quietly turned into distant prefabricated houses. On the anniversary of the earthquake, people with different thoughts rushed to Beichuan, making this road somewhat crowded. When passing by Beichuan Middle School outside the county town, seeing the parked Sany pump truck there, Ma Lei’s solemn face slightly relaxed.

Currently, throughout Mianyang City, nearly 40 units of Sany pumping equipment are involved in post-disaster reconstruction, including pump trucks, drag pumps, and mixing stations. As the landmark project of Beichuan's reconstruction - the construction of Beichuan Middle School has drawn much attention. Among them, four Sany pump trucks are involved in the construction.

As a service engineer for pumping equipment in the Mianyang region, Zhou Jian bears considerable pressure. The normal inspection system is at least twice a month, but the reconstruction work bearing the expectations of the entire nation makes Zhou Jian visit the construction site two or three times a week. When concrete undergoes continuous pumping, he simply stays there.

Currently, the construction projects of the new Beichuan city have not been fully launched, with the key focus being on the reconstruction of Beichuan Middle School, Leigu Town Primary School, Leigu Town Middle School, and some infrastructure such as the water plant outside the county.

Similar to Beichuan, the reconstruction work in An County mainly focuses on government office buildings, low-rent housing, hospitals, schools, and other civilian facilities. A year ago, facing the danger of landslides and possible collapses, Sany rescue team members drove excavators to work on the Xiaocha section in An County. Today, this road has become smooth and level. Three Sany concrete mixing stations stand by the roadside. Among them, the largest Tenghui mixing station in Mianyang is included. This mixing station produces four to five thousand cubic meters of concrete annually, owning three Sany drag pumps and two pump trucks, undertaking most of the reconstruction tasks. The X-shaped outriggers and single-side support functions of the Sany pump trucks provide more flexible construction methods in complex construction environments.

Ma Lei said that over the past year, he has remained steadfast in the Mianyang disaster area, feeling the optimistic and positive mindset of the people in Beichuan and An County. He deeply feels the joy of his work from the bottom of his heart. The participation of Sany equipment in the reconstruction is about participating in the process of building happiness.
