Golf Course Introductions:

Saujana Golf and Country Club

The supreme course in Malaysia for many years, PALM COURSE has consistently ranked first among Malaysian golf courses. It has long hosted the Malaysian Open and ranks 69th in the world according to the American Golf Masters' top 100 courses evaluation.

The Golf Club Datai Bay

The magnificent Datai Bay Golf Club offers a unique opportunity for golfers to experience playing on a course developed through primary forest, quite different from many domestic golf courses. To swing at Datai Bay requires skill due to narrow fairways where balls can easily be hit into the forest. In summary, it indeed provides an exhilarating golfing experience. The greens are covered with Bermuda grass, while the tees are planted with Tifdwarf grass. Datai Bay offers many challenges; to score well on some holes, extra effort is required. Do not get distracted by the beautiful scenery and surrounding environment of the golf course because you need full concentration to successfully putt the ball into the hole.

Gunung Raya Golf Resort

Nestled in the majestic hills of Gunung Raya, lies the most serene and picturesque golf resort on Langkawi Island. Spanning 300 acres, it was once a rubber plantation but is now an internationally acclaimed and breathtaking Gunung Raya Golf Resort designed by the internationally renowned golf course architect Max Wexler. In the vast green expanse, you will enjoy smooth greens, diverse tee boxes, and level fairways. The integration of sand and water hazards makes it even more spectacular.
