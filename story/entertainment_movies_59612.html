The Hollywood heroic legend epic "The Children of Huang Shi," jointly invested and filmed by China, Australia, and Germany, has now entered the submission review stage. From its inception to now, it has received significant attention from all sides. With a cast of handsome Hollywood actors, an international production team, grand war scenes, and a strong humanitarian spirit, it is seen as a strong contender for various awards at the 2008 Oscars.

Synopsis of The Children of Huang Shi:

In wartime China during the Anti-Japanese War, British journalist George Hogg delves deep into reporting on the Nanjing Massacre but is unfortunately captured by the Japanese army. He is later rescued by Jack Chen of the New Fourth Army. While recovering from his escape and treatment, he encounters over 60 orphans with different personalities who survived after a massacre by the Japanese invaders. As war draws near, to prevent these children from being harmed again, George and Jack take it upon themselves to lead these more than 60 orphans on a grueling journey along the ancient Silk Road.
