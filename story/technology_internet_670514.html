Bayern's greatest concern is the replay of last season, or even worse. Last season, Bayern was left behind by Dortmund from the start of the season. By the end of the first half of the season, they had basically lost hope for the championship. The team's goals kept decreasing - from runner-up all the way down to competing for a Champions League spot. In the end, they only managed to outpace Hannover by 5 points, finishing in third place.

Although Bayern no longer has competitors like Hannover this season, Dortmund's continued strength and Monchengladbach's rise have put Bayern once again at risk of landing in third place. While these two competitors are improving, Bayern is showing signs of decline. This season, there are four Champions League spots available in the Bundesliga. The top three teams directly enter the group stage, which will surely motivate Schalke, currently in fourth place, to strive further.

Bayern may not need to worry about securing a Champions League spot, but whether ranking third or fourth, it would be an unbearable pain for Bayern, who already experienced failure last season.
