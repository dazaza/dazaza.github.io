(Economic Frontier) Industrial Clusters: Grow Stronger and Larger in the Process of Upgrading

by dtjhsb119 on 2009-12-06 16:14:45

"Accelerate the transformation of economic development mode and promote the optimization and upgrading of industrial structure." In recent years, our city has been promoting the leading industries, improving special industries, seeking breakthroughs in emerging industries, fully accelerating the development of cluster economy, and actively organizing and implementing the development project of ten major industry clusters, making the cluster economy of the whole city further strengthened and expanded.

Host appearance: Friends, this is the equipment workshop of Jiangsu Xianzhong Petroleum Machinery Co., Ltd. Now, the workers are busy producing a series of high-pressure throttling manifolds and wellhead equipment worth 4 million US dollars for export to Russia. This year, the company has already completed sales of 150 million yuan, with 60% being exports.

As a key enterprise in the Jianhu petroleum machinery industry, Jiangsu Xianzhong Petroleum Machinery Co., Ltd., under the incentive of the city's accelerated promotion of the cluster industry policy, has expedited enterprise technological innovation and actively developed foreign markets, putting the enterprise on a fast track for rapid development. In Jianhu, only the two industry clusters of petroleum machinery and electronic energy-saving lamps and lanterns are expected to achieve sales of 8 billion yuan this year, among which enterprises with sales exceeding 100 million yuan reach 20.

The development of key industry clusters such as Yancheng Yancheng's craft glass products, Dafeng shot blasting machines, Dongtai Zhendong stainless steel products, Dagang shoe machines, etc., not only extends the industrial chain but also saves the production costs of enterprises and enhances the competitiveness of regional economy, including life jackets for ships.

同期声：江苏日月照明有限公司董事长 徐向阳 "The 'wall effect' has emerged, materials, market, technology, talents have all moved to the doorstep, reducing circulation costs while enhancing regional advantages and forming industrial reputation."

In 2007, the total main business income of the top ten key industry clusters in the city reached 58.8 billion yuan, an increase of 31% year-on-year, completing fixed asset investment of 13 billion yuan, increasing by 45%. In 2008, our city will further accelerate the implementation of the industry cluster development project. In terms of emerging industries, on one hand, we will make full use of the distinctive industries in our city to attract large projects, introduce strategic investors, and strengthen leading enterprises. On the other hand, we will seize the opportunity of large-scale coastal development and connecting with Shanghai, attracting major marine economy projects along the coast line, and at the same time actively responding to the industrial transfer from developed areas like Shanghai, planning specialized parks to accommodate related industrial investments.
