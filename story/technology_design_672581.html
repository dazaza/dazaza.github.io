Entangled childhood

by yrtu6849 on 2012-02-23 04:04:29

"Ice Tear, we will be together for a lifetime, right?" Xia Ling asked Ice Tear the same question again.

"Xia Ling, we are not lovers, how can we be together for a lifetime?" Ice Tear looked up at her cute appearance.

"No, I want to be with you forever, Ice Tear."

"Alright, Ice Tear and Xia Ling will be together forever."

Then Xia Ling smiled contently.

Time flowed slowly, Ice Tear and Xia Ling sang every day, singing through a long stretch of winding time. The days that Ice Tear and Xia Ling spent together were always joyful. Until one day, a boy named Mu Liangchen appeared.

That day, there was an impromptu small meeting in the store. The supervisor introduced a new colleague. It turned out the purpose of the small meeting was to introduce the new colleague.

"My name is Mu Liangchen." He said coldly.

Ice Tear always remembered the first time she saw Mu Liangchen. She saw his handsome face, his messy bangs, straight nose bridge, stern eyes, thin and tall figure. Ice Tear stared at him foolishly, wanting to see clearly everything about him and remember it.

Mu Liangchen also noticed a girl who kept staring at him. Her eyes were clear, her skin white, a common gentle girl. Mu Liangchen didn't smile at her. He stared at Ice Tear piercingly.

Ice Tear looked back at him, started feeling awkward, quickly lowered her head and avoided his gaze.

Thus, the small meeting ended while Ice Tear was lost in thought.

〈Two〉

It was a warm autumn afternoon, the sun was shining brightly. Ice Tear and Xia Ling walked on the shaded path, listening to the sound of leaves crunching underfoot, each lost in their own thoughts.

Suddenly, Xia Ling looked up, faintly smiling at Ice Tear: "Ice Tear, I like Liangchen, Mu Liangchen."

Everything seemed to stop. Ice Tear looked at her in surprise. She was very unexpected. She remained silent for a while. Then she hugged Xia Ling and said: "You will be happy, he will like you."

"Will Liangchen like me?"

"Yes, Xia Ling is Liangchen's princess!" Xia Ling didn't notice the strangeness in Ice Tear's words.

"Thank you, dear Ice Tear." Xia Ling smiled mischievously at Ice Tear.

Xia Ling began to actively approach and pursue Liangchen. But Liangchen showed no response to everything Xia Ling did. Xia Ling cried sadly, thinking he must not like her.

Ice Tear felt sorry for Xia Ling, but inexplicably felt relieved. She patted Xia Ling's back and said: "Dear Xia Ling, don't worry, keep trying. He will be moved. You should know, sincerity can move even the hardest stone."

Xia Ling sobbed softly, tears in her eyes like diamond light.

"I like him, I really like him."

Ice Tear's heart fell into a deep, bottomless pool. But she made no sound.

〈Three〉

Winter came.

Mu Liangchen finally accepted Xia Ling.

On a cold afternoon, Xia Ling's face was full of joy as she held Ice Tear's hand and said: "He agreed to be with me, he said he likes me too."

The quiet Ice Tear wanted to laugh because she thought she should be happy for her good friend's happiness, but her mouth was so stiff. At that moment, she wanted to cry, quickly biting her lips and holding back the tears that were about to fall.

The weather got colder and colder, Ice Tear became increasingly silent in the fierce cold wind. Xia Ling kept talking to her about everything about Mu Liangchen. Ice Tear listened, then smiled faintly, making no sound. She shared Xia Ling's happiness and joy with a sour heart, carefully hiding her own feelings that had slipped away before she could hold onto them.

〈Four〉

Xia Ling never asked Ice Tear if they would be together for a lifetime again. Perhaps they both knew: for a lifetime, it wouldn't be the two silly girls anymore. The one who should be with Xia Ling for a lifetime should be Mu Liangchen.

Xia Ling thought she was the happiest. Ice Tear also thought Xia Ling was the happiest. Because Xia Ling had Mu Liangchen. But things didn't seem to go as they both thought.

"Ice Tear, I... I... I have something I want to tell you." Before Mu Liangchen finished this sentence, Ice Tear already realized what Mu Liangchen wanted to say.

"Huh? Is there anything?" Obviously, Ice Tear was a little scared when she said this. She was looking forward to Mu Liangchen's next words, but also afraid to hear them.

"I... I like you! I've always liked you. The reason I didn't accept Xia Ling at first was because I was waiting for you. I thought you liked me, so I kept waiting. But after the whole autumn passed, I still couldn't wait for you... I..."

Ice Tear remained silent, she was happy, yet in pain, and also filled with hatred. She hated why he told her all these now. She wondered: If he had told herself that he liked her when Xia Ling first said she liked him, would the outcome be different?

"What about Xia Ling if you like me? What should she do?"

Yes. What should Xia Ling do? Mu Liangchen accepted her because he was moved, in other people's eyes, they were a perfect pair. Xia Ling took care of his life meticulously. Xia Ling couldn't be hurt. Xia Ling couldn't live without Mu Liangchen! So what should they do? Should they just miss their love?

The coffee shop was very quiet, playing soft music.

"Chen, thank you, thank you for giving me such a fairy tale. But once missed, it cannot come back! Although it will be painful, we should not destroy our current happiness. Let's just remember this fairy tale at dusk. Your happiness should be given by Xia Ling. You have been to my world, I remember, so I have nothing to regret. " Mu Liangchen, I like you too. Ice Tear added silently in her heart.

That evening, the sun was raining. Just like Ice Tear's heart.

〈Five〉

Life goes on, Ice Tear continues to smile silently.

Ice Tear knows: some things will eventually become the past, memories, not easily revisited. Whether it's herself or Xia Ling, or even Mu Liangchen, they will all complete their respective growths.

Winter has finally passed.

You have been here, I remember, so there is nothing to be sad about.

That beautiful and regrettable love, Ice Tear left it in the cold winter that has disappeared.
