The latest chapter of "Wdqk": Be willing to let go, be willing to laugh, be willing to throw away.

by icmin810 on 2012-03-07 15:46:51

Who would know just how resilient I truly am

Until one day we have no choice but to be strong

Everyone knows that even if life is monotonous and dull every day

It's still something we must keep living

Everyone has moments of pain they can't express

Helplessly finding no one to confide in, denying that they are alone

Whose promises truly come true

Reality forces us to exchange lies for vows

Who hasn't been unhappy at some point

It's just that the sweetness of the past was too deep

Who always claims they've let go

Yet tears fall because of a single sentence, a song, or a familiar scene

Who has many friends

But when their heart can barely handle their own pressure, they can't find anyone to confide in

Who feels they've given so much

Only to realize in the end they've never truly opened up to anyone

Who thinks time is the most terrifying thing

And then is grateful that time helps them forget what shouldn't be remembered

Dear who...

Find yourself, tell me who you are

Then, please remember:

I am me, someone no one should ever give up on

Please don't make strength a habit; it will exhaust both body and mind

Please continue smiling after being hurt, letting yourself grow mature. Even if tears well up in your eyes, keep smiling

Please live each day fully, willing to let go, willing to laugh, willing to discard. When you're free, find any reason or excuse to make yourself happier

Please don't fully believe vows, or only believe half of them

Please treat yourself as a friend, a friend who understands everything about you

If you can't find someone to open up to, talk to yourself and say, take good care of yourself

No one is destined to be lonely for a lifetime

Beloved self, there will always be the right person who gives me the right place in their heart
