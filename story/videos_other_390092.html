Online stores are not restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run the store full-time or part-time without needing to spend a lot of time watching the store.

How Exactly to Open an Online Store?

There are a wide variety of online stores, ranging from cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each project has great development potential and prospects. The key is how to proceed and what suits you best. This is the most important thing. Online stores also require skills. Below, I will teach you step-by-step how to open an online store.

1. What to Sell Online?

Like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of goods should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, as well as the nature of the goods and consumer demand.

Since I love shoes and have been in this industry for many years, I naturally started selling shoes.

2. Preparations Before Opening a Store

After choosing what to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verification is also required.

The reason I chose to open a store on Taobao is because it's completely free! Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. To open a store on Taobao, three conditions must be met: one, register as a member and pass verification; two, list 10 or more items; three, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, enter the username, password, email, etc., and click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link inside and confirm to complete the user registration.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction security, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification) need to be completed. Specific steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to "Want to sell items, first complete Alipay verification."

Step 2: On the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated. Click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-up page, input your real name, type of ID, ID number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step 3: After successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real Name Verification" button, read the Alipay Verification Service Terms, then click "I have read" to continue.

Step 4: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input bank card information, including bank, bank card number, province, city, etc. After completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Tip: If you upload copies of your ID online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC card ID, you also need to provide a backside picture.

Step 5: One day later, reopen "My Taobao", in the verification area click the corresponding link to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Transfer Amount", then input the amount of funds Alipay injected into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Procurement and Photography

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the procurement channel. For the same item, different procurement channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools to convert picture formats and sizes, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Publish Items

To open a store on Taobao, besides meeting the membership verification conditions, you also need to publish more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and pictures, you need to start publishing your first item.

Friendly Tip: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can publish items, but the items can only be published to "Items in Warehouse," where buyers cannot see them. Only after passing the verification can you put them on the shelf for sale.

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" publishing methods. Here, click "Fixed Price."

Friendly Tip: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a base price, allowing buyers to bid for purchases.

Step 2: Choose the category, select an appropriate category based on your item. For example, I chose women's shoes details. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step 3: Fill in item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, choose a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input the item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; then, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, freight, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as default use of Alipay payment, etc. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to publish the item.

If the publication is successful, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the published item page, click "Continue Publishing Items" to keep publishing items.

Friendly Tip: If buyers haven't made a bid yet, and you want to modify the published item information, you can edit and modify it under "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Items for Sale."

After the items are published, it's best to update and add them regularly to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

3. Obtain a Free Store

Taobao offers free store-opening opportunities for certified members. As long as you publish more than 10 items, you can own your own store with an independent URL. On this webpage, you can display all your items and arrange them according to your style.

To get my store up and running early, I initially published 10 items, spending almost half a day. After finishing, I couldn't wait to open "My Taobao - Free Store." Then, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes, My Show"; in the "Store Category," I could choose the item's category; in the "Store Introduction," I entered a brief introduction to the store. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it said "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." and provided the store's address.

4. Shop Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, it is necessary to decorate the shop appropriately, mainly including designing the shop logo, categorizing items, recommending items, and the shop style.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Manage My Store." In the left-hand "Shop Management," click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the shop name, shop category, shop introduction; the main business items need to be manually entered; in the "Shop Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to choose a pre-designed shop logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the shop announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Clicking the "Preview" button allows you to check the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items facilitates buyer searches. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left side. Then, input a new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), clicking the "Confirm" button adds it. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and through keyword search, add the published items for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

Taobao's "Recommended Items" function allows you to showcase your top 6 items prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left side; then, in the opened page, select the recommended items and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Shop Style

Different shop styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choose a light-colored, comfortable style. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, and the preview appears on the right side. Clicking the "Confirm" button applies this style.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears before you.

5. Promotion is Key to Success

The online small shop is open, items are listed, and features are established, but weeks have passed without any transactions, not even buyer messages. This is a common problem encountered by many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to promote my shop through four ways: forum promotion, link exchange, window recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums in various provinces or cities. If there is a section allowing advertisement posts, you can post advertisements. The content must be detailed, the item images must be exquisite, and regular updates and pinning are required.

The latter involves changing the signature line on forums to include the URL, logo, slogan, and name of your shop. Posting beautiful threads allows interested friends to visit your shop via your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the initial stages of opening a store, to increase popularity, you can exchange links with popular shops, thereby promoting your shop without spending money. For instance, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links. The method is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all shops and record the names of popular shop owners.

Then, log in to http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download Taobao's buyer-seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular shop owner names, and propose a link exchange request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the shop owner's name and click the "Add" button.

3. Window Recommendation

Taobao's "Window Recommendation" function is a special feature provided for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" according to categories, the window-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set the "Window Recommendation" function, open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Items for Sale," select the items to recommend in the window (items already recommended on the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

6. After Selling Items

After selling items, apart from receiving corresponding sale reminder information, you also need to proactively contact buyers, requesting them to pay for the goods, shipping, and evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases items, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang," emails, etc. Sellers can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer's ID and send internal site messages.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After contacting both parties, agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both buyers and sellers, it is recommended to choose Alipay payment.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent situations where goods arrive but payment isn't made, sellers generally adopt a "Payment Received Before Shipping" method when selling items.

First, request the buyer to pay, usually via Alipay. After paying, the seller can open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the remittance, and at this point, the seller can safely ship the goods to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction inquiries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao will transfer the funds to the seller's Alipay account. Thus, the transaction is completed. Don't forget to maintain contact with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your shop and purchasing items. Wangdianle specializes in creating a shopkeeper exchange platform, featuring main channels: Shop Experience, Ya Shop, and Taobao Shop, guiding you on how to open an online shop.

4. Evaluation, Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluation. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluation" on the sold items, choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual circumstances, and input text content.

Friendly Tip: How to become a highly-rated star shop owner on Taobao?

To become a star shop owner on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction. If the evaluation is "Positive," it adds one point; "Neutral" adds no points; "Negative" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star shop owner, remember to provide honest services. If issues like not buying after online deals or not paying after receiving goods occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - I Want to Report" for complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, and Wangwang screenshots must be collected.

Although online stores have zero cost and low risk, examples of failing to make a single deal and closing the store are also common. To allow your small store to survive online, the most important thing is "honesty." Only honesty can win the trust of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, enabling further development!
