[Course Introduction] Entrepreneurs are the backbone of modern society, and their management skills and qualities will have an important impact on enterprises and society. This seminar focuses on improving the quality of entrepreneurs in business ethics and management. Outstanding business ethics focus on enhancing entrepreneurs' ability to integrate resources, discover potential business opportunities, and better handle external relationships, thus laying a foundation for the leapfrog development of enterprises. Modern entrepreneurs compete on who can better integrate resources, who can faster discover business opportunities, and who can become the new generation of enterprise leaders. Innovative management aims to provide internal management system support for enterprises to grow stronger and bigger. Innovation in management allows enterprises to constantly realize system innovation, cultural innovation, and conceptual innovation according to their own actual situation and development stage. Let all software of the enterprise keep up with its high-speed development.

Tsinghua University, as one of China's highest institutions of learning, has always been a cradle for the growth of elite talents in business administration. The customized senior management courses for enterprise leaders with strategic development vision will allow students to experience a comprehensive leadership perspective and unique strategic development concepts, providing a thinking method based on strategic angles, enhancing entrepreneurs' strategic decision-making, management, and reform and innovation capabilities...

"Tsinghua University's Best Courses" combine long-term training experiences, starting from the actual problems and needs faced by enterprises and entrepreneurs, carefully designed, more targeted, timely, and innovative.

The course goal of "Tsinghua University's Best Courses" is to disseminate the latest management thoughts and methods, Tsinghua University Business Administration, cultivating outstanding international entrepreneurs for China.
