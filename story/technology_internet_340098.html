Related thematic articles: Untitled Document 　　Xiaowu gave Tang San a glare, and as soon as she thought about how she was hit by a stone last time, she became furious. "What are you waiting for? Who knows when you will be back. They have come to bully me. That damn Xiao leader said that I should be his pet rabbit. If I could bear it, I wouldn't be called Xiaowu." Tang San frowned, thinking in his heart that children of this age already had such dirty thoughts, so they really needed to be taught a lesson and let them know that students working part-time were not easy to bully. Thinking of this, he wiped his finger on his waist, and looked towards the battlefield. By now, Wang Sheng's body was already bruised in many places. His roars continued, but his soul power had become weaker and weaker. However, his opponent had not been hit even once, mocking him like a cat playing with a mouse. Wang Sheng's attack method was too single. Taking advantage of his soul power not yet being completely consumed, he launched the final wave of attacks. After running two steps, he rushed towards Liu Long again. This time, he had made up his mind that no matter how badly he would be beaten, he must leave some marks on his opponent. Liu Long had been dealing with Wang Sheng for quite a while, and naturally would not fail to see his intention. Seeing Wang Sheng rushing over, his expression became serious. He swung his long stick, hitting three times in succession towards Wang Sheng's head and both shoulders. As long as Wang Sheng, who was at the end of his strength, was driven back, he would not be able to launch another decent attack. But just at this moment, suddenly, Liu Long felt as if his stick-holding hands were bitten by mosquitoes, and his attack slowed down instantly. His attack had originally been calculated, but this slowing down immediately revealed a flaw. The first stick did hit Wang Sheng's head, but Wang Sheng still rushed forward, making his next two sticks miss their target. For a weapon soul master using a long weapon, being close to a battle soul master with a beast soul was absolutely a devastating blow. Even though Liu Long's strength was slightly higher than Wang Sheng's, the result was still the same. With a bang, Wang Sheng slapped Liu Long's head with his tiger claw, sending him flying. Following this, his body flipped over, and his right leg delivered a heavy whip-like blow to Liu Long's back, slamming him into the ground. Liu Long did not have Wang Sheng's good resistance ability. His soul power was all on his long stick, and his own defense was not outstanding. These two attacks from Wang Sheng were also full of effort. When Liu Long slammed heavily onto the ground, he immediately passed out. Wang Sheng excitedly roared towards the sky, as if trying to vent all the resentment in his heart these few years. Xiao Leader was originally leisurely watching Liu Long mock Wang Sheng. The sudden reversal of the situation on the field shocked him greatly. You must know that Liu Long was among the top three in strength among his subordinates. If he did not already possess the first soul ring, there would be no guarantee that he could defeat Liu Long. Xiaowu mocked, "Decayed flowers and broken willows are indeed decayed flowers and broken willows, truly unable to withstand a single strike. Xiao Leader, what do you have to say?" Xiao Leader waved his hand, letting the other subordinates pull Liu Long down for emergency treatment. They were only junior soul master academy students, and with that little soul power, they would not cause any fatalities. "The first round is ours lost. Lingfeng, it's your turn." The second person sent out by Xiao Leader was a tall senior student with a slim figure, small nose and small eyes, and a thin physique that did not show his twelve years of age at all. Instead, he seemed to be the same age as Xiaowu and Tang San. "Call Wang Sheng back. It's my turn." Tang San quietly said to Xiaowu's ear. Xiaowu's eyesight was also very good. Naturally, he could tell that the second person sent out by the opponent was an agile type of soul master, exactly countering Wang Sheng. Wang Sheng had already consumed too much soul power before, and would definitely lose. "Wang Sheng, come back. Let Xiaosan fight this round. You can rest." Wang Sheng was still not fully recovered from the excitement of defeating Liu Long earlier. Hearing Xiaowu's voice, he realized that Tang San had returned without him noticing. He knew his own condition. Although he defeated Liu Long, he indeed had no more fighting power left. How would he know that if Tang San had not secretly shot out three small stones to limit Liu Long's attack, how would he have achieved the previous victory? "Are you a part-time student?" Lingfeng looked at Tang San, doubt flashing in his eyes. Tang San just nodded at him, "Please make your move." Although Lingfeng was slim, he was still taller than Tang San, who was just in the first grade. This was a duel concerning who would become the student leader, so he did not think further. After taking another look at Tang San's belt, he leapt into the air, performing an agile somersault, spreading his arms wide, actually gliding two meters, rushing towards Tang San's head. Beast soul, Tang San immediately saw what kind of soul his opponent had. The teaching from Master these past few days was not wasted. This student named Lingfeng was an agile type of battle soul master. His beast soul should be a flying beast. Judging from his not fierce appearance and attack style, it should not be an eagle. Then, it must be a swallow or crow type of soul. Watching the opponent rush towards him, Tang San remained motionless, as if the opponent did not exist at all. Lingfeng's speed was very fast. In the sixth grade students, he was known as the fastest one, being Xiao Leader's best friend. Moreover, his movements were very agile. Just as he was about to reach Tang San, suddenly, his body turned again. Spreading his arms, he forcibly changed direction, arriving behind Tang San. Both arms swung simultaneously, chopping towards both sides of Tang San's neck. Until Lingfeng flipped over his head, Tang San did not move. Facing an opponent with unknown abilities, maintaining stability was the best choice.
