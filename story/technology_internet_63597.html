Some Views on the Development of Sichuan Internet and Individual Webmasters

by gallonwang on 2008-04-17 13:27:20

A friend from Ali Mama wrote to discuss about Sichuan Internet and personal website operators, which just sums up my own thoughts. I post it here for everyone to discuss together. Find out the real problems, treat them accordingly, and jointly develop and strengthen the Sichuan Internet.

After returning to Chengdu, I started discussing issues related to Sichuan Internet and website operators.

I also mentioned this on my blog before. Through exchanges with some local and non-local friends, I can roughly summarize my personal views.

About Sichuan Internet

1. Let's start with the city. Chengdu is indeed a leisure city as the media said. The commercial atmosphere of the entire city is very poor, which in turn leads to poor business awareness, and the pursuit of high technology is not as crazy as in coastal areas;

2. People from Sichuan who have ideas and abilities have gone to coastal cities like Shenzhen and Shanghai. Those who stay are mostly people who are content with their lives, having a house, buying a car, raising a child, and living without worries. The four words "content with a little wealth" fit best. The Sichuan phrase "qiao jiao lao ban" (literally 'foot-up boss') is very popular here;
