Jinsun Connect Machine Manual Jinsun Game Machine Manual

◆ The main unit displays real-time accounts, eliminating the need to stop the machine to check accounts. Just look at the account displayed on the main unit for shift handovers.

Part Two: Connection Usage Method

◆ After connecting the communication line in order, start from No. 8 machine, turn on the power supply one by one, finally turn on the power supply of No. 1 machine (the main unit is in No. 1 machine). Alternatively, you can turn on the power supply of each of the 8 machines and the main unit simultaneously. If coin insertion/return is not required, please disconnect the return key wire on the control panel and seal the coin acceptor.

Part Three: Main Unit Usage Method

◆ For normal use, just ensure that the communication line is connected and the power is turned on. During normal operation, the main unit displays the running status. During the betting process, the top line shows the remaining betting time before the light starts running, and the bottom line shows the total profit. During the light-running and jackpot processes, the top line shows the remaining running time (i.e., how many minutes left until coding, in minutes), it's best to code before the remaining 1000 minutes, to avoid blocking the machine during play. The bottom line also shows the total profit. During normal operation, when needing to hand over shifts, just record the total profit shown on the bottom line for clear account settlement. Since this machine adopts the most advanced and reliable method currently available, verification is extremely strict. Therefore, before handing over shifts, ensure that no points are added or deducted for two consecutive rounds so that the main unit can accurately record all sub-unit accounts.

◆ To check accounts, first enter the menu state. There are two ways to enter the menu: ① During machine operation, press the "function key", showing "waiting to return". After one round ends, it will enter the menu. ② In case of main unit failure, hold down the function key while turning on the power to directly enter the menu. After entering the menu, there are four options: "Check Accounts", "Report Accounts", "Parameters", "System". Press the "select" key to move the arrow. When the arrow points to the function you need, confirm to enter that function. (Note: To avoid unnecessary trouble, make sure all sub-units must have their power on and properly connected before entering the menu.)

◆ If the main unit LCD has backlight but no display, or has shadowing and the color is too dark to be clear, open the back cover of the main unit. On the main board, there is a color adjustment switch. Clockwise is lighter, counterclockwise is darker.

◆ After entering the account check, you can choose to check the main unit or sub-unit accounts. Use the "move" key to select between the main unit and sub-unit accounts. After confirmation, you can enter. After entering the main unit, you can check all accounts of the main unit. The first page shows the line number on top and the machine number below. Confirm to enter the total profit (the total profit of all sub-units), confirm again to enter the total coins inserted (all sub-units' coins inserted + previous ones), confirm again to enter the total coins returned (all sub-units' coins returned + deducted points), confirm again to enter the current profit (profit from the last barcode scan to now, i.e., the total profit on the second page - the total profit on the sixth page from the previous time), confirm again to enter the previous total profit (total profit of all sub-units at the last barcode scan), confirm again to enter the actual previous profit (actual profit at the last barcode scan), confirm again to enter the operating time (cumulative operating time from the last barcode scan to now), confirm again to enter the number of barcode scans (total number of times barcodes were scanned), confirm again to enter the machine number (for convenience in maintenance and reporting, each group of machines has a total payout (total number of points won by all sub-units)). The next six pages are backup pages, which are not currently used. Press confirm twice to return to the total profit, ending the entire account checking process of the main unit. Press return to exit the main unit account check. Note: There are many records here for more convenient account settlement during cooperation. If you cannot fully understand them, just record the total profit, which is sufficient.

◆ Press the move key to select sub-unit account check. After confirmation, you can enter. After entering the sub-unit, input the sub-unit number you want to check, and you can check the corresponding sub-unit accounts, including total profit of each sub-unit, total coins inserted for each sub-unit, and total coins returned for each sub-unit. Press return to exit.

◆ After entering the report, it shows 00 page total profit of all sub-units (from the last reset), 03 page current actual profit of all sub-units (from the last barcode scan), 07 page total number of barcode scans, 08 page machine number, 15 page verification code. Finally, it shows please input barcode. Inputting the correct barcode will successfully report the accounts. However, after entering the report, all sub-units also enter the reporting state, all sub-units display 76. At this point, pressing return to exit is not allowed. All sub-units and the main unit must be powered off to exit the reporting state.

◆ After entering parameters, you can set: Running Light Rate (first press delete to delete the original rate, then press the numeric keys to input the desired rate, minimum 00.00 maximum 99.9, default is 94.0, note: decimal points must not be omitted) Ratio of Card Issuance Rate (not used here), Coin Insertion Ratio (press delete key to select, options include 1 coin = 100 points, 1 coin = 1 point, 1 coin = 2 points, 1 coin = 5 points, 1 coin = 10 points, 1 coin = 20 points, 1 coin = 50 points, 1 coin = 80 points, 8 options in total, default is 1 coin = 10 points, note: the point ratio is 10 times the coin ratio) Maximum Betting Points (press delete key to select, options include 99 points, 50 points, 95 points, default is 99 points) Betting Point Ratio (how many points per bet, press delete key to select, options include 1 point, 2 points, 5 points, 10 points, default is 1 point), Startup Music (whether to sing after winning, press delete key to select, options include on and off, default is on, if singing after winning is not needed, choose off), Large/Small Multiplier (choose large multiplier 4 to 70 times or small multiplier 4 to 46 times, press delete key to select, default is small multiplier) Betting Time (minimum 15 seconds, maximum 60 seconds, first press delete to delete the original time, then press numeric keys to input the desired time, default is 30 seconds). Note: All default parameters are set according to the needs of the majority of the market by our company. If there is no special need, do not modify them. Because modifying any parameter requires the main unit to be connected to all sub-units, and reporting twice to reset (for methods of resetting, please refer to the reporting instructions).

◆ Entering the system requires the manufacturer's password, which will not be introduced here.

◆ Holding down the test key is inconvenient. Turn on the main unit power, input the sub-unit number, to test the communication status between the main unit and sub-units. The top shows the total number of communications, and the bottom shows the error count. If there are errors, check the communication connection line.

Part Four: Troubleshooting

◆ Main Unit Fault: Internal Error appears, please report twice and reset to eliminate most faults. 08 indicates reaching the highest account, please report twice and reset. 09 indicates time reached, please report once.

◆ Sub-Unit Fault: If fault codes starting with 4 appear, such as 40, 41, etc., it is a connection fault. Please first check if there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, if the connection line is disconnected, or if the communication on the computer board is not plugged in. If all of the above are normal, simply turn on the power for this sub-unit and all other sub-units, then report twice and reset. If there are duplicate sub-unit numbers, hold down K3 of this sub-unit, turn on the power, press the start key to flip to page 8, then press the return key to modify the sub-unit number. Finally, reconnect the main unit and all sub-units, report twice from the main unit, and reset (sub-units must be normally connected and powered on). For 10-player fishing machines, if the sub-unit or main unit is sent back to the factory for repair, upon reconnection, report twice from the main unit and reset (sub-units must be normally connected and powered on).

◆ Sub-Unit faults can be handled as follows: ① Check the coin insert/return switch for 30, 31 faults. ② If no problem, hold down K0+K3 or the point-up key+wash-point key, turn on the power, show 66, reset this sub-unit. ③ If still unresolved, check if the fault code starts with 4. If so, check if the communication connection line is faulty. ④ If confirmed no connection issue, check if there are duplicate sub-unit numbers. ⑤ If none, connect all sub-units and the main unit together, turn on the power, and continuously report twice from the main unit to reset. Note: For convenience in repairing sub-units, most sub-unit faults do not require power-off repairs. We have specially designed a hot-start switch K2. In case of sub-unit faults, just press K2 to restart the sub-unit in hot-start mode. However, K2 does not work if there is no fault in the sub-unit. Of course, you can also turn the machine off and on to resolve issues.

Part Five: Reporting Instructions

◆ First press the function key to enter the menu, use the move key to select reporting, confirm to enter the reporting state. Fully report via short message or phone call the contents of pages 00, 03, 07, 08, and 15. Finally, it will show "Input Barcode." After receiving the barcode, input the correct barcode to successfully complete the reporting. Note: Without proper reporting, the barcode cannot be calculated or entered.

◆ If zeroing is required, repeat the aforementioned reporting process to reset. Note: During zeroing, sub-units cannot add or deduct points.

◆ When the main unit cannot check accounts due to certain reasons, all sub-unit accounts can be checked. Method for checking sub-unit accounts: Hold down the K0 key or open the point-up lock, hold down the point-up key, turn on the power. Page 0 shows total profit, page 1 shows total coins inserted, page 2 shows total coins returned. Page 3 shows current actual profit. Press the start key to flip pages. Then add up the accounts of all sub-units, which equals the main unit's account.

◆ Special Note: For sub-unit account checking, if the JP light is on, it indicates a negative number. The JP light is located between the total score column and the score column.

◆ Additionally, when the main unit encounters faults like 06, 01, etc., the main unit-stored main unit and sub-unit accounts may be incorrect. In such cases, the accounts of each sub-unit must be taken as the standard.
