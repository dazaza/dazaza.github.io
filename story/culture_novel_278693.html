If he were to improve again, the floating sensation in his body would likely become the biggest obstacle to his next promotion!

A person with a floating internal state and uneven distribution of strength is never as good as someone with a solid internal structure and precise control over their strength. Xiao Yan was extremely clear about this. The three years of persistent practice during his waste period had established an extremely solid foundation for his cultivation. With this rock-solid base, Xiao Yan was able to build a towering edifice of strength that left others in awe. Between the two options, the choice was obvious. When it came to letting go, Xiao Yan was the most decisive.

"Pity, such a good opportunity does not come easily," Yao Lao chuckled as he watched Xiao Yan exit his cultivation state.

A wisp of turbid air was expelled from Xiao Yan's throat. He twisted his neck, hearing the crisp sound of bone contact. Feeling the surging flow of fighting spirit within his body like a river, a trace of joy appeared on his face. His palms lightly pressed against the lotus, and he gracefully landed on the ground. His fists suddenly shot out, creating fierce wind sounds.

"Enough. With the current ability, I can only perfectly control the sudden increase of these two star powers. If there were more, although it might seem like my strength increased in the short term, in the long run, it would not be beneficial for me." Slowly withdrawing his fists, Xiao Yan said. Yao Lao smiled and nodded. His hand gestured towards the lotus, retrieving it into his storage ring. Then, with a flick of his wrist, the previously broken jade fragment reappeared in his hand. He gently caressed it.

After improving his strength, the injuries inside his body were completely healed. Xiao Yan's face regained its rosy hue. Looking up at Yao Lao's actions and glancing at the broken jade fragment, he hesitated slightly, then moved forward cautiously asking, "Teacher, do you recognize this?"

"Hmm, I've seen this kind of peculiar storage device before," Yao Lao nodded and replied.

"Storage device? Can things actually be stored inside here? Like combat techniques or martial arts?" Xiao Yan first paused, then suddenly understood.

"Hehe, yes. If my guess is correct, this jade fragment storage device should be the work of the 'Blazing Valley' in the Dou Qi continent. Because they are the ones who specialize in this type of jade fragment storage technique. Moreover, after touching it for a while, it feels slightly hot, which is the mark of items made by the Blazing Valley," Yao Lao laughed.

Of course, in the eyes of this novice who had never left the Jia Ma Empire, he naturally had not heard of these names.

"It is a force on the Dou Qi continent. If we talk about strength, it is probably no weaker than the Yun Lam Sect. They are famous for cultivating fire-attribute martial arts on the continent and specialize in explosive attack and killing routes. Thus, they also stand out uniquely on the continent," Yao Lao said indifferently.

"Not weaker than the Yun Lam Sect?" Xiao Yan's mouth twitched slightly, and after a long pause, he couldn't help but bitterly smile. He didn't expect that the Yun Lam Sect, which dominated the Jia Ma country, was not so powerful on the Dou Qi continent.

"Hehe, don't think too highly of the Yun Lam Sect. That person named Ling Ying is not wrong. The Yun Lam Sect is merely a second-rate force on the Dou Qi continent. Even though they now have a Dou Zong level expert, they can only be at the bottom of the first-class forces," Yao Lao smirked, seemingly somewhat disdainful.

"Of course, even so, in front of your small Great Fighter, they naturally still count as a huge creature." Seeing Xiao Yan's bitter expression, Yao Lao laughed loudly: "Young man, the Dou Qi continent is vast. The world you have seen before is just a small corner of this continent. When you step onto the continent, I believe you will fall in love with that spectacular world. But you must also understand, wherever you are, your own strength is the most important thing to gain respect!"

"Understood, the same rules apply everywhere." Xiao Yan nodded slightly and smiled.

"If you understand, that's best." Yao Lao smiled, tossing the broken jade fragment in his hand and asked, "Do you want to know what's inside?"

Watching the jade fragment remain undamaged in the flames, Yao Lao's face also revealed a hint of surprise. Hearing this, Xiao Yan was startled. Glancing at Yao Lao, he saw a trace of joy in the other's eyes. At that moment, he also understood somewhat. Anything that could impress Yao Lao must be something truly valuable.

The black jade fragment, once it entered the green flames, fell silent again. However, Xiao Yan was not in a hurry. He slowly increased the temperature of the flames.

When the cave became unbearably hot, the quiet jade fragment finally showed some movement. The originally cracked and smooth surface began to ripple like water waves, as if it were about to melt.

"No problem, keep increasing the temperature," Yao Lao said calmly, showing no signs of panic.

Seeing Yao Lao's calm demeanor, Xiao Yan steadied his mind, nodded, took a deep breath, and the fighting spirit inside his body surged rapidly. Instantly, the temperature of the Green Lotus Core Fire soared!

As the temperature of the flames continued to rise, the pitch-black jade fragment finally melted completely. After melting, the jade fragment lost its black color and instead transformed into a pool of green, crystal-clear liquid. The liquid flowed slowly in the green flames, resembling a living creature, rather peculiar. At a certain moment, the flowing liquid suddenly stopped. Bubbles formed on the surface of the liquid, as if something was about to break through.

"Good things are coming," Yao Lao smiled, observing the movements of the liquid.

Hearing this, Xiao Yan's heart tightened, and his gaze fixed on the flames, almost unable to blink.

The bubbling of the green liquid lasted for about ten seconds. At a certain moment, a dazzling green light suddenly erupted from within. In an instant, it almost obscured all the light inside the cave.

However, despite the blinding green light, neither Xiao Yan nor Yao Lao closed their eyes because, in the instant the green light flashed, streams of text information suddenly surged out of the liquid. Then, they automatically arranged themselves in mid-air within the cave, forming a special font painting.

Scanning the scene in mid-air, Xiao Yan's gaze finally rested on the large characters at the top. His lips moved slightly, and a low voice emerged from between his teeth.
