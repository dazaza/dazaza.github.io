The 400 telephone service is a shared-cost service, with features and functions similar to the widely available 800 free call service. It is an upgraded version of the 800 free call service that perfectly combines traditional telecommunication resources with modern CTI technology and incorporates advanced customer service concepts. The 400 telephone only requires setting a call forwarding number to receive calls from all over the country.

Business advantages:

1. Interconnection, wide usage range

Fixed-line phones, wireless local loop, and internet phones can all make calls, and users from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, and international regions can also call.

2. Rapid networking, regional transfer, unified national number dialing

China Unicom provides business users with a unified national access number, making it easy for users to remember and enjoy standardized services, which also benefits corporate users in promoting their brand uniformly. For example, if there are call centers in Shanghai, Guangzhou, etc., all incoming calls (local and out-of-area numbers) can be automatically transferred to local reception, improving office efficiency.

3. Shared payment, effective cost control and malicious calls

Call charges are paid by both the caller and the callee. Business users can use the cost control function to effectively control costs, and callers will reduce malicious calls due to increased costs.

4. Low investment for enterprises, high efficiency

The called party can effectively utilize the resources of China Unicom and China Telecom, without needing to set up network equipment required for internal enterprise networks or lease digital dedicated lines, thus eliminating the need for network and equipment maintenance.

Service functions:

1. Corporate color ringback tones or welcome messages

Supports color ringback tone (or corporate PBX welcome message) functions, leaving a good impression on customers through the company's audible business card.

2. IVR voice navigation

Supports corporate PBX functions, combined with color ringback tone functions, to maximize the efficiency of your company’s phone handling. (e.g., Welcome to ** Company, press 1 for sales inquiries, press 2 for service acceptance... press 0 for directory assistance)

3. Call forwarding on busy/no answer

When the main calling user dials the 400 telephone number and encounters a busy line or no answer, the call can be forwarded to one or several pre-set numbers. Business clients can register up to 10 forwarding numbers by default, i.e., 10 forwarding numbers each for busy and no-answer forwarding.

4. Time-based destination selection

According to the needs of the enterprise, the 400 number can select the corresponding destination based on different time periods.

5. Call distribution

Distributes calls to the 400 number to different destination phone numbers according to a certain ratio.

6. Destination selection based on caller location

Selects the destination for incoming calls based on the geographical location of the caller.

7. Blacklist

Certain specific phone numbers can be placed on a blacklist, restricting them from dialing a particular 400 number.
