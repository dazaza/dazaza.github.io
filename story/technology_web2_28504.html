NOVOKING World - is committed to building the world's largest Chinese 3D virtual world

by cacard on 2007-08-30 09:06:03

2005 began to set up a team, today in April just set up a company, you can pay attention to this new project into the 3D virtual community, the official website is introduced as follows:

Novoking, founded in Beijing Shangdi Zhongguancun Science Park in November 2005, is one of the earliest 3D Internet explorers in China, committed to building the world's largest Chinese 3D virtual world.

In October 2005, Zha Weijiang and Zhang Hui, the founders of the company, began to launch the Novoking project and set up a research and development team to explore 3D Internet applications, try to build a new 3D virtual world for Chinese Internet users, and explore a new digital lifestyle.

In April 2007, Novoking company - Imagination Unlimited (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd. was officially registered in Beijing.

Novoking is a combination of the words Novo and King, Novo means "creation" and "innovation" in Latin, and Novoking is called "Kingdom of creativity" in Chinese. Novoking tries to provide users with a 3D virtual world that they can experience and actively create based on the 3D Internet Natural Human Computer Interaction (NHCI) platform. Here, users can obtain a unique virtual life experience through vivid audio-visual experience and magnificent imagination. They can roam, live, make friends, entertain, learn and create here, build a "dream society", and reshape their exciting life in the digital space where dreams and reality converge.

Novoking has brought together a group of Internet idealists in IT management, technology, planning and art, and is actively and rapidly pushing forward the Chinese application of 3D Internet with full enthusiasm and low-key pragmatic style.
