<p> Xiangzhou, Guangxi News - On June 21st, the case of a middle school student in Xiangzhou County stabbing a classmate to death has come to an end. The perpetrator, Zhang Mou, was sentenced to 13 years in prison by the Xiangzhou County Court for intentional injury. It is reported that Zhang's crime stemmed from the negative consequences of early romance. </p>

<p> According to the court's investigation, on the evening of January 28th this year, Zhang Mou, a second-year junior high school student in Xiangzhou County, was playing games at an arcade when he randomly pressed the buttons on his girlfriend Ahuan's game console, causing her dissatisfaction. She then informed five others who were also playing there, all of whom were middle school students. Upon hearing this, Acheng and his group decided to beat up Zhang. That night, Acheng and his companions went to a classmate's house where Zhang was staying temporarily, but Zhang did not come downstairs. Subsequently, Acheng and his friends engaged in heated arguments with Zhang via QQ chat, exchanging threats to fight each other. Zhang viewed Acheng as a "rival" and harbored resentment towards him. </p>

<p> The next afternoon at around 2 PM, Acheng and two others confronted Zhang again. They argued and got into a physical altercation. Zhang took out a small knife and stabbed it towards Acheng, hitting him in the left chest. Despite being rushed to the hospital, Acheng died from his injuries. At 4:50 PM that day, Zhang surrendered to the police accompanied by his mother. After the incident, Zhang's family compensated the victim's family with an economic loss of 3,000 yuan. (Chen Jingdong) </p>
