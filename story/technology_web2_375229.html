CheWen: A real-name system based car Q&A website

by ybcz0519 on 2010-12-19 12:12:55

This article is simultaneously published on Mobinode, business media please do not reprint! Chewen.com is a platform recently launched by the Oak Group, relying on genuine user relationships from Renren Network. Through real-person Q&A formats, it provides true and valuable information to car buyers while gathering users interested in the same topics into unique "interest circles." Chewen directly uses Renren accounts for login. After logging in, users will be asked to select car-related themes, models, and questions of interest. Subsequently, their personal homepage will display questions related to their chosen themes. Users can initiate questions, answer others' queries, or follow relevant users. Chewen claims to have 500 automotive industry experts covering various fields such as pre-sales, after-sales, customization, rights protection, and second-hand transactions, ready to address users' professional inquiries at any time. Users can follow experts in a particular field or their favorite models, interact with other users who share the same interests, and receive comments and information.

(Watsonxu's commentary: The launch of Chewen represents an attempt by the Oak Group to utilize Renren Network resources for vertical online communities. By using the Renren Passport, barriers between users are removed, and new vertical communities allow for separate display and organization of vertical content. This also serves as a filtering and reorganization of Renren Network's own content and information. Additionally, the direction of developing an expert-based community by gathering automotive experts within the community is also a commendable attempt.)
