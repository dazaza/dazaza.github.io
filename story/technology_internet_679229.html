& nbsp; www.szqpjc.com [http://www.szqpjc.com], Sunboard [http://www.szqpjc.com] is the commercial name for polycarbonate (PC), including hollow sheets and solid sheets (commonly known as durable sheets). It is a new type of lighting material widely used internationally for its high strength, light transmission, sound insulation, and energy saving. Due to its unique high quality, Sunboard rapidly entered the lighting material market in the mid-1980s. It has better performance than laminated glass, wired glass, tempered glass, and insulating glass in terms of being lightweight, weather-resistant, super-strong, flame-retardant, and soundproof, thus gaining wide recognition from architectural design, decoration projects, environmental engineering, and the advertising industry. According to statistics, since the late 1980s, the sales volume of Sunboard in the international market has been growing at an annual rate of 20%. Since 1995, the total sales volume worldwide has exceeded 100 million square meters annually. Ruicheng Sunboard: It has strong light transmission, impact resistance, heat insulation, weather resistance, dew prevention, flame retardancy, sound insulation, and good processing performance. The impact resistance of Sunboard is 100 times that of ordinary glass and 30 times that of acrylic glass; the surface of Sunboard has undergone UV protection treatment, possessing anti-aging properties, successfully solving the aging problems that other engineering plastics cannot resolve; Sunboard is classified as a national difficult-to-burn B1 grade engineering plastic material; Sunboard reduces noise by 18-20dB (10mm), making it the preferred material for highway sound barriers.

Currently, in China's modern greenhouse construction, materials such as glass and double-layer films are mainly used as covering and insulation materials for greenhouses. The main problem with modern greenhouses built using these materials is: high energy consumption of the greenhouse (although glass has good light transmission performance, its thermal insulation performance is poor), complicated work, and weak ability to withstand natural disasters. When encountering hail, heavy snow, and other natural disasters, the greenhouse itself will suffer serious damage, and the plants inside the greenhouse will also be greatly affected. The lifespan of plastic film is very short, and after use, it will cause white pollution to the environment.

As the fourth-generation greenhouse covering material, Sunboard fundamentally improves the condition of greenhouses. Double-layer and multi-layer Sunboards, which appeared in Europe in the 1970s, have been widely used in agricultural greenhouse construction. Its main characteristics are lightweight, weighing only one-fifteenth of glass of the same thickness; super-strong, with an impact strength 16 times that of glass; energy-saving, saving more than 40% energy compared to glass-covered greenhouses; durable, with a decrease in light transmission of no more than 7% after 10 years of use; cold and heat resistant, with no deformation between -40℃～120℃; transparent, with a light transmission rate of 79%～88% for colorless Sunboard; cold bending capability, with the minimum bending radius of Sunboard at room temperature being 175 times the board thickness.

In China's modern agricultural greenhouse construction, the use of Sunboard has just started, mainly in some cities in the north. In terms of greenhouse construction, there are greenhouses entirely built using Sunboard and high-end hotels used for leisure, viewing, and dining. In terms of greenhouse functions, there are production-oriented greenhouses, commerce-oriented greenhouses mainly as flower trading centers, and research-oriented greenhouses. These greenhouses partially or entirely use Sunboard as covering material, further improving the ecological environment of the greenhouse.
