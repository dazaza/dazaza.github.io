Baby Naming - Looking at the Promising Future of Babies through the Stroke Count of Names - China Naming Network Information

by chinaname on 2010-04-16 10:53:28

Expectant fathers and mothers all want to give their babies a meaningful name, embedding blessings and expectations within it. So, how can blessings and expectations be incorporated into a baby's name? The number of strokes in the name affects the baby's future; parents should pay attention to the number of strokes in the baby's name.

1. Follow-the-Noble Type:

Life Palace numbers 10, 12, 19, 21, 23, 32, 34, 43, 45, 54

People of the Follow-the-Noble Type don't need to go far for job opportunities in this life as chances are around them. Whether in public service, commerce, or as grassroots employees, they must follow large organizations or a noble person whose abilities are stronger than theirs. They should aim to surpass this noble person and then find the next target after surpassing them. Remember, those who help you are your nobles, and you must never offend them easily.

Taboo: If you dislike following others or only adhere to your own ideals and walk your own path in this life, it will be hard to have opportunities for wealth.

2. Down-to-Earth Type:

Life Palace numbers 13, 18, 20, 24, 29, 31, 35, 42, 46, 53

The Down-to-Earth Type's future completely depends on their strength to achieve success. Their professional knowledge must be sufficient to unleash their potential. To get rich in this life, they must work hard and be dedicated, accumulating experience step by step. Hard work will definitely lead to success if they persevere. Remember! Don't seek ease; finding joy in hard work is the key to happiness.

Taboo: The Down-to-Earth Type must avoid laziness at any time. If they wish for comfort and rest, losing their drive, then all previous efforts will be wasted, possibly leaving them with nothing.

3. Fortune and Luck Type:

Life Palace numbers 14, 17, 25, 28, 30, 36, 39, 41, 47, 52

The Fortune and Luck Type are the luckiest people. In this life, they have more opportunities than others and things tend to go smoother for them. This is due to the good karma accumulated from their past lives. Regardless of whether they are in academia, agriculture, industry, or commerce, they have great prospects and easily meet benefactors who offer help. Thus, they should appreciate and cherish their fortune. The quality of their work isn't the main point; what matters is that their work gains recognition from their superiors. Promotion and wealth won't be difficult then.

Taboo: The Fortune and Luck Type must avoid carelessness in their work, which could lose the trust of their superiors. Then, their good fortune may gradually fade away. Therefore, being diligent and cherishing one's fortune is what they should focus on most in this life.

4. Grand Ambition Type:

Life Palace numbers 15, 16, 26, 27, 37, 38, 40, 48, 49, 50, 51

For the Grand Ambition Type, the most important thing to stand out in this life is whether they themselves have grand ambitions and aspirations for their future. If they have the ambition to climb higher, they should steadily achieve each goal. As an employee, they should aspire to become a manager; as a manager, they should aim to become a director, thus moving up step by step. Remember! Do not aim too high without putting in the effort.

Taboo: The Grand Ambition Type fears having no great aspirations in life, wanting only a comfortable existence, or strongly devoting themselves to religion or spiritual pursuits, making it difficult to succeed.

5. Fame and Support Type:

Life Palace numbers 11, 22, 33, 44, 55

The Fame and Support Type has two paths to success in this life. The first is through fame. If they can establish their own renown in their specialized field, maintaining this fame rather than tarnishing it will lead to great wealth. The second is finding a stable support system. No matter what difficulties arise in their work, someone will always back them up, helping resolve all challenges, making success achievable.

Taboo: The Fame and Support Type must avoid offending their supporters. Thus, interpersonal relationships must be carefully maintained. If starting a business, they must find financial backers. As long as they proceed steadily, failure is not a concern.
