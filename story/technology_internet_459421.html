8-Year-Old Boy Dies After Being Electrocuted While Hanging on a Cable, Face Deformed by Electric Current

by hj1718004 on 2011-07-08 21:04:27

Yaxin.com news (intern reporter Ji Li) "Crack... Bang..." continuous sounds followed by a flash of fire. A man who was addicted to drugs and had a nightmare about killing someone turned himself in at the public security bureau. When the smoke cleared, Ms. Wang from Kuitun, Xinjiang, ran out of her house and was shocked by the scene in front of her: not far from her home, on top of a utility pole, a young boy was hanging in mid-air on a cable. His face was distorted and deformed from being struck by an electric current, and his clothes were burned. The boy cried out in agony: "Mom, save me..."

Ms. Wang lived in a bungalow in the Xihuali neighborhood of Kuitun City. She said that at around 8 p.m. on May 20, while she was cooking dinner at home, she suddenly heard a series of explosive sounds outside. At first, she thought it was someone setting off fireworks, but then she realized it was different. When she rushed out of her house, she saw a little boy hanging on a ten-meter-high utility pole not far from her home. "The child is too pitiful!" Ms. Wang, also a mother, shed tears.

Ms. Wang consoled the boy not to move around while asking for the names and phone numbers of his parents. She then dialed emergency numbers. A few minutes later, police officers from 110, doctors from 120, firefighters from 119, and a power repair vehicle all arrived one after another.

The boy was urgently sent to the intensive care unit of the Kuitun Hospital in Yili Prefecture. Around 9 p.m. on May 22, the boy, only eight years old, passed away after unsuccessful rescue attempts.

According to the Kuitun police, at the time of the incident, there was only the boy present at the scene. It is possible that his curiosity and playful nature led him to climb so high.

In the afternoon of May 23, I drove to the Xihuali neighborhood, three kilometers away from Kuitun City. Under the guidance of an informant, I saw the boy's mother standing alone on an open space, staring at the utility pole where the accident happened for a long time.

The boy's mother, Zhang Aizhen, 36 years old, came from Jiaozuo, Henan Province. In 2010, after getting divorced, she brought her child to Kuitun City to work. Her child was attending elementary school.

Zhang Aizhen said that on the day of the accident, she was at work. By the time she finished her shift, her child was already in the hospital.

"Despite two days of rescue efforts, the child still passed away. We've been here for less than a year, and such an incident has occurred. What should I do? How will I explain this to my family back home..." Zhang Aizhen sobbed as she spoke.

As of the time of this report, the cause of the accident is still under investigation by the police.
