Fann Wong and Mark Lee's big day

by isuo on 2009-05-19 14:43:18

On May 16, Fann Wong, known as "the first beauty of Singapore", and her long-time boyfriend Mark Lee entered the marriage hall. The two registered locally in a low-key manner that day. It was learned that on September 29 and October 11, the newlyweds will hold banquets in Singapore and Malaysia respectively. Friends revealed that the two plan to settle down in Beijing after their wedding.

Under the witness of both families and a few close friends who were present, the couple exchanged rings. After the simple ceremony ended, the newlyweds headed straight to the groom's hometown in Malaysia to pay respects to their ancestors. Mark Lee’s father passed away due to illness on February 10 this year. According to traditional customs, the two had to get married within 100 days, otherwise they would have to wait for three years.
