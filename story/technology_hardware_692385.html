Honey: Last night, my heart ached so much because of one sentence you said. Maybe when you said it, you were thinking of what's good for me, or maybe, you were just saying it casually. But you really hurt me. Is my affection for you really that fake to you? Can you say "I love you" casually and fall in love with others casually? You said I should find someone else who can take care of me, and you would still be nice to me like before. So do you think my feelings are worthless? Don't say I can't do it, I simply won't do it. I'm so attached to you, how could I do that? Is it because you won't come as often in the future? Should I give up on you and choose someone else? Although this is a virtual online romance, perhaps you don't even care about this relationship, but at least I think this is my love, a love that cannot resist reality. But I cherish you, although I don't know how long this virtual love will last, I will treasure every day with you. You said you won't come as often in the future, but now you're here quite often, right? Am I neglecting you because you don't come as often? Or am I blaming you for neglecting me?

Maybe, I take this online love between us too seriously. You didn't even care, but I fell into your affection. You can ignore me, but I crave you. When I saw that sentence of yours, my heart ached. I turned off my phone, left the UT room, and turned off all functions that could receive your messages. I thought, was I originally so indifferent to you?

But when I saw your announcement, I suddenly felt how capricious I was. Why couldn't I communicate with you? Why did I leave without saying a word? Making you worried. But I didn't know how to say it, I didn't know how to tell you my feelings. When your second round of announcements came up, I realized that you still wanted me. Before that, I was always waiting for you to say marry me again, but you only said that you would be busy in the future and come less often, making my heart restless.

I've already said that I understand you. I didn't capriciously ask you to be online with me every day. Men should have their own careers, that's the main thing. Games are just a way to relieve stress, a way to relax our tense nerves. Although I miss you and want to be with you every day, not seeing you makes me crazy, but I know we can't get addicted to it and neglect reality.

So, what I want is still your happiness and hope you achieve your ideals, realize your dreams. I hope to see the day you succeed so much. Even though it may not belong to me, I will be equally happy and feel equally blessed.

I love you so much. In the future, let's not argue, not get angry, not sulk, because it really hurts.

When I stopped crying and wiped away my tears, I went to the room to find you. I like being with you, in any form. I like talking to you, I like listening to you talk, I like letting you listen to my rambling, o(c_c)o... and I also like sitting quietly together without saying a word. Feeling like leaning on your side, quietly sensing your breathing and heartbeat.

I want to hear you sing, but I want to see you more, I want to see you smoking.

Honey, I know you care about me the most, you will satisfy me, right? Hehe hh

Kiss you honey, deep kiss.

Your beloved
