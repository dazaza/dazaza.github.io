Hotels in Rotterdam supporting sports enthusiasts: A testament to the strong sports following in Amsterdam is its 800-plus sports clubs. Amsterdam hotels cater to sports enthusiasts and athletes who come to Amsterdam to participate in various sporting events. Popular sports in Amsterdam include football, tennis, athletics, hockey, and ice-skating. Football is the national sport of the Netherlands and is strongly supported by the legion of fans in Amsterdam. The Dutch Football League features several strong teams, including the renowned football club Ajax Amsterdam. Among the major running events in Amsterdam are the Dam tot Damloop, the Amsterdam Marathon, the Vondelparkloop, and the Grachtenloop. Amsterdam also has facilities for playing basketball, baseball, and bowling. Sports like skateboarding, inline skating, ice skating, and roller skating are all played in indoor stadiums with top-of-the-line facilities. Many Amsterdam hotels have sports facilities and gyms where sports enthusiasts can continue their rigorous regimens.

Museum tours from Amsterdam hotels: Millions of visitors from around the world visit Amsterdam to see the more than fifty museums the city boasts. A comprehensive collection of scientific, historical, artistic, and miscellaneous artifacts can be seen in Amsterdam’s museums. During Museum Night, when people participate in a museum hop from 7 pm until 2 am every first Saturday of November, Amsterdam hotels are filled to capacity. People can enjoy museum exhibits that are generally closed to the public at these times by participating in this event unique to Amsterdam. If you visit Amsterdam with the intention of experiencing only the most prominent museums, you can visit the Museumplein or Museum Square, which includes the Rijksmuseum, the Van Gogh Museum, and Stedelijk Museum, the top three museums in Amsterdam, as well as the Diamond Museum. The Rembrandt House Museum, the Anne Frank House, and the Maritime Museum are also must-see museums of Amsterdam history. Amsterdam hotels in the Museum Quarter are ideal for visitors who want to stay close to all the museums.

Family holidays at Amsterdam hotels: The many family-friendly hotels in Amsterdam reflect the high regard that the Netherlands has for the family. Children’s suites and child minders are offered on request by many Star Amsterdam hotels. Entertainment areas and pool facilities are provided by them. Other Amsterdam hotels allow young children to share a room with their parents and even provide additional beds. You and your family can enjoy the tours around the city that many Amsterdam hotels arrange. Even if you have only a day or weekend to spend in Amsterdam, you must enjoy the tram and canal rides that give you an idea of the city’s charms. Families can enjoy many fascinating activities at The NEMO Science Centre, where children are allowed to play and interact with the exhibits as well. ARTIS is the oldest zoo in Europe, which is a must-see, along with the planetarium and aquarium in the same complex. Active children will find an outlet for their energy at the sprawling Vondelpark, where they can rent a bike or rollerblade. Many new hotels have been built to accommodate families that come to enjoy the beaches around Amsterdam as well.
