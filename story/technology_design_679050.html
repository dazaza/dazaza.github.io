Recently, producer Wang Lijun has been preparing relevant materials for the upcoming Chinese Wang Clan Research Association. As soon as he opened the Baidu webpage, a chilling and bloody headline caught his eye - "Online reports expose the tyrannical behavior of an 'official second generation' in Anhui province, who disfigures a young girl after their romance fails." Immediately, this news caused a huge stir...

According to revelations by Wang Lijun's assistant, Xiao Song, Director Wang has recently been busy preparing for the premiere of the movie "The First Crossing of the River," the start of filming for "Will You Marry Me?" and the approval process for the 30-episode TV series "War Flames Over Liaojil." The top priority at the moment is to select a stunning outdoor shooting location so that the storyline and visuals are both captivating and brilliant. Using harmonious tones, beautiful imagery, simple narratives, slow rhythms, emotional music, and textured lenses, they aim to tell a restrained love story set in the real world.

However, the report about "disfiguring a young girl after a failed romance" deeply disturbed Wang Lijun. That night, Wang immediately convened an emergency meeting with several key members including the screenwriters, directors, cinematographers, lighting crew, and production team, demanding immediate adjustments to the related plot of the movie "Will You Marry Me?"

Why was Producer Wang Lijun so concerned about this report? What connection does it have with the movie script?

After gathering information from various sources, the answer was finally revealed: It turns out that the courtship elements in the revised screenplay for "Will You Marry Me?" were eerily similar to the aforementioned incident. Unbelievably, reality had indeed unfolded in this manner. Xiao Yu is the local "playboy," whose narrow-minded rash actions and impure soul eventually led to condemnation and punishment. Under the close care of the Party and the responsible education and encouragement from prison and detention center staff, Xiao Yu regains his conscience and conducts a deep self-examination. His experience also teaches and transforms fellow inmates, guiding them toward the path of righteousness. Meanwhile, the beautiful Wen Jiao, disfigured by Xiao Yu, miraculously recovers her former good looks...
