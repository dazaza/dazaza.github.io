Meaning of wind chimes

If a girl sends a boy a wind chime, it means breaking up. Wind chimes also mean yearning and missing. However, it's best not to give wind chimes casually. After all, the meaning is not very good, but you can buy one for yourself to hang in a ventilated room for a melancholy sense of happiness.

Meaning of combs

① Combs represent longing, representing that the other person misses and cares about you very much!

② Combs comb hair daily, representing their closeness to you, symbolizing growing old together!

③ Combs also represent love, generally having the meaning of being pledged in ancient times!

④ Combs also represent health and happiness! Sweep away troubles, open up mental knots, and combing hair gives people spirit, brings confidence!
