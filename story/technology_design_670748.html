The total number of tourists in Anji during the Spring Festival reached 210,000 - Zhejiang Anji Farm Stay

by super1556 on 2012-02-21 10:59:09

During the Spring Festival holiday, the surrounding farmhouses of Zanglong Hundred Waterfalls were bustling. Yesterday, the reporter learned from the Tourism Committee of Anji County: from the first day of the lunar new year to the sixth day, the total number of tourists in Anji reached 210,000, with a tourism income of 158 million yuan and ticket revenue of 2.8 million yuan. Especially in the Jiangnan Tianchi Scenic Area, the daily average number of tourists reached 8,000 people. During the third and fourth days of the lunar new year, the number of tourists in Zhongnan Baiyuan and Bamboo Museum Park also gradually increased, reaching a maximum of more than 6,000 people per day.

Jiangnan Tianchi Tourists Went Wild

"Car after car drove into the Jiangnan Tianchi scenic area, sometimes even forming long queues." This has been a common scene on the main roads of Tiangang Town since the Spring Festival. On the first day of the lunar new year, many self-driving tourists came here to enjoy skiing and hot springs.

In order to do a good job in receiving tourists during the Spring Festival, Jiangnan Tianchi had made full preparations before the festival. Red balloons floated, festive couplets hung, and the staff greeted tourists with smiles, saying "Happy New Year, welcome to the Jiangnan Tianchi scenic area."

The staff told reporters that it snowed in Jiangnan Tianchi during the first month of the lunar year, making the conditions for skiing even better and creating a lively atmosphere. The Anji tour groups were all booked before the New Year, while many self-driving tourists came after seeing advertisements. "This part of the tourists already accounts for more than 70% of the total number of tourists," said the staff.

Baiyuan Bamboo Museum Park "Signature Performances" Attract People

Originally, during winter, places like Zhongnan Baiyuan and Bamboo Museum Park in Anji were relatively quiet. But during this Spring Festival, they showcased their "signature performances". On the first day of the lunar new year, there were over a hundred private cars parked at the entrance of the Zhongnan Baiyuan scenic area, all families coming together.

Staff introduced: "These days the scenic area is particularly lively, with ethnic performances every day. The bear riding bicycles, tiger shows, etc., are very popular among tourists. Also, many children like to come to Baiyuan to see animals. So although it's winter now, there's still a lot to do for tourists here."

At the Bamboo Museum Park, the newly launched 4D cinema was liked by many tourists. Although each movie lasts only 15 minutes, visitors like to go in and experience it. Additionally, many tourists from Shanghai and other places took advantage of the Spring Festival holiday to visit bamboo forests at the Bamboo Museum Park. Staff introduced: "There was a little snow during the first month of the lunar year, and the snowy bamboo forest scenery was very beautiful."

Rural Tourism Becomes Fashionable

On the third day of the lunar new year, Shangshu Tugan Village in Guishan Township welcomed many tourists from Hangzhou. "We learned about Shangshu Tugan Village in Anji through an Anji tourism recommendation event in Anji. Since we had time during the Spring Festival holiday, we brought our family here, and it feels really great," Mr. Wang from Hangzhou introduced.

Shangshu Tugan Village's Party Secretary Li Xiliang introduced: "During the Spring Festival, the village's tourism service center operated normally, receiving more than 500 individual tourists from the first day to the sixth day of the lunar new year."

In Huangdu Village of Xilong Township's White Tea Film Base, there were tourists visiting every day during the first month of the lunar year. A tourist from Shanghai introduced: "The tea gardens here undulate continuously, lush and verdant, very beautiful. The film base is located right in the middle of the tea garden, simple yet elegant. It's very comfortable to take a walk here."

Huangdu Village's Party Secretary Sheng Awei told reporters: "During the first month, many villagers brought their relatives and friends to the film base, took photos, and introduced them to the development of Huangdu Village. Now the villagers' concepts have changed, and so has the way they play."

In Lianpeng Town's Ligou Village, Xiaoyun Village, Xigang Village, etc., the creation of beautiful villages last year gave the villages a brand-new look. During the Spring Festival, many nearby villagers came here to see the new rural landscape.

Relevant responsible persons from the county's Tourism Committee introduced: "With the rise of rural tourism, the development of winter Anji tourism projects such as hot springs and skiing, as well as the active creation of new projects by Zhongnan Baiyuan and Bamboo Museum Park, Anji tourism reached another small peak during the Spring Festival. In the new year, the county's Tourism Committee will continue to strengthen external publicity for Anji tourism, further standardize the Anji tourism market, and improve the quality of Anji tourism."
