People who read this article will understand why spending a lot of time creating an original soft-sell article ultimately serves its purpose. Is it merely for the joy of helping others? Obviously, it's still about advertising. However, the reason soft-sell articles are called "soft" is that they must be different from hard advertisements. It's all about being subtle. The advertisement should be hidden and naturally brought up when mentioning a certain viewpoint, just passing over it so that people don't even realize it's an ad after reading it. Even if they later realize it, they won't feel repulsed. If I were to promote a product like "Beijing SEO Optimization Service," I could first talk a little about the internet, then a bit about the SEO industry, and then suddenly mention something related to Beijing SEO optimization services. And smart readers wouldn't find it difficult to discover that what they're actually reading is a soft-sell article about Beijing SEO optimization services—it’s about how you learn from it. In fact, as long as you put your heart into it, it's not difficult.

For those who don't know where to start writing soft-sell articles, I suggest going to large, authoritative professional websites and carefully reading the articles published by others. Basically, the quality of articles that can be published on these websites is good. Basically, they just wrote some praising words about Huaxia Mold Network. When publishing, whatever was submitted was deleted, with a very low pass rate. Although it has only four editors, they receive a large number of submissions every day, and each article is manually reviewed with strict conditions and high requirements for article quality. This might be the key to establishing the authority of this website. Frequently reading articles on these websites, such as http://www.seo-ee.com/Nanjing Baidu Optimization, allows you to learn many practical things and accumulate SEO optimization skills and techniques, which will greatly assist your writing. Once you have a certain level of professional competence, you won’t worry about lacking material to write about.

2. Mining gold in industry blogs.

According to Beijing SEO, there are many blogs that haven't been indexed but still contain a lot of valuable content worth reading. A faster way is to input keywords into Youdao and search for blogs, which will bring up a lot of industry-related blog posts. At this point, you can learn more or less knowledge from them. If you're doing Baidu promotion, many of the blogs found on Youdao aren't indexed by Baidu, but you can extract some content from them as your own, saving time and still being recognized by Baidu. 3. Refining from work practice.

In fact, many professional writers gradually become masters through accumulating experience from being novices. During the work process, you'll find many small details. Even now, seeing the vast amount of data in Beijing SEO is built up step by step from small codes. Not letting any detail go unnoticed during practice, you'll often discover tricks that others haven't yet discovered. Whenever you make even a small discovery, you can immediately record it and save it for future reference. Next time you write an article, you can take one of these points and share it with everyone. Even if your entire article has no real meaning, at least you've presented a useful viewpoint. Such articles, when reviewed by editors, will still be considered for inclusion.

2. How to write soft-sell articles

1. Catchy title

A catchy title is like a person's face. If the face is attractive, no matter the body shape, it will capture attention instantly and make people look twice. Titles can be suspenseful or explosive, as long as they are attractive. Don't underestimate these few words; there's a lot of learning involved.

2. Useful content

The primary value of a soft-sell article is sharing with others, and secondarily achieving advertising purposes. Nowadays, people's time is very limited. They won't waste time reading an article filled with advertisements. You must first let people learn something from the article, and it must be genuinely effective tips, before guiding the reader to further read and want to understand the author. That way, you have a chance to promote your product. People who know how to share are easily accepted by others. Remember this point: readers come first, advertisements come second.
