Character introductions for *The Devil Is By My Side*

**Jiang Meng / Played by He Junxiang**

"Give up on that!"

"There’s nothing I can’t obtain."

Male, 21 years old

A first-year university student in the Applied Arts Department known as the "Enchanting Prince." His father is the chairman of the school, and under his "rule," his classmates have become a notorious force on campus. Supported by his father, he behaves arrogantly, and both teachers and students alike obey him without question. Despite his fiery temper, Jiang Meng has a sick younger brother and thus lacked maternal care growing up. This background made him fear loneliness more than most people. Over time, he began to hide his emotions, treating those around him, including family members, with cold indifference. His domineering and reckless behavior became his protective color. However, after meeting Qi Yue, he gradually learns to truly understand the people around him. Although he remains stubborn, he begins to value hard-won friendships more.

**Qi Yue / Played by Yang Chaoling**

"Oh God! He must be a demon,

God, please take him away!"

Female, 22 years old

A second-year university student, Qi Yue dreams of experiencing pure love like all girls her age. At school, Qi Yue is low-key and doesn’t draw attention to herself, but her approachable personality has earned her many friends. Having never been in a real relationship, Qi Yue has secretly admired her classmate and basketball captain Shang Yuanyi. After mustering the courage to confess her feelings, she mistakenly gives her love letter to Jiang Meng, bringing this demon into her life and starting a series of nightmares. To make matters worse, it turns out Jiang Meng is the child of her mother's remarriage partner! This "younger brother" barges into her life, interfering with everything. She absolutely cannot let this demon ruin her life or her relationship with Shang Yuanyi!

**Shang Yuanyi / Played by Wang Chuan Yi**

"If you’ve got the guts,

Let’s settle this fairly on the court."

Male, 22 years old

The ideal prince charming in every woman’s heart! Skilled at basketball, academically gifted, and physically attractive, Yuanyi has always been idolized by everyone. He knows that women only notice his good looks and admire his perfect body lines and excellent ball skills when he plays. To avoid being seen as just a pretty face, Yuanyi works hard in his studies to achieve the image of a man who is both intelligent and physically fit. Busy with basketball practice, matches, and heavy coursework, he had no time for romance until Qi Yue came into his life…

**Xin Lixiang / Played by Fan Xiaofan**

"Only I understand the whole of you,

Including your dark past…"

Female, 20 years old

Lixiang appears sweet and lovely, but the serious look in her eyes makes it impossible to ignore her presence. A group of female college students led by her often gang up to bully any girl who shows affection for Jiang Meng. Qi Yue’s appearance captures all of Jiang Meng’s attention, which Lixiang refuses to allow. Determined to protect her love, she steps forward. Even though all men willingly fall at her feet, her gaze rests only on Jiang Meng, ignoring another pair of longing eyes…

**Jiang Youhui / Played by He Dubin**

"We must give them a happy family"

Male, 42 years old

Jiang Meng’s father. Though the prestigious chairman of the university, he likes to disguise himself as a janitor on campus or wear a cute pink apron while cooking. Born into a prominent family, Jiang Youhui is wealthy but not bound by traditional ideas about marriage. During one visit to a bento shop, he met the beautiful and graceful Xue Wei. Realizing that perhaps Jiang Meng needs a complete family, Xue Wei’s cuteness and clumsiness touched Jiang Youhui, making him decide that from now on, he, Xue Wei, Jiang Meng, and their daughter Qi Yue could enjoy the happiness of a four-person family.

**Huang Xuewei / Played by Ge Weiru**

"Fall in love! Only a woman carefully cherished by a man can bloom like a rose."

Female, 38 years old

Qi Yue’s mother. A romantic and dreamy young mother, her ex-husband tragically died in an accident, leaving her without support. However, she took on the responsibility alone and raised Qi Yue. Though seemingly scatterbrained and forgetful, often needing Qi Yue’s help with housework, Xue Wei, despite being Qi Yue’s mother, acts more like her sister, often crying and returning home for Qi Yue’s comfort… Yet her love and care for her daughter are greater than anyone else’s. She has always strived to provide Qi Yue with a complete family. Unexpectedly meeting the school chairman Jiang Youhui and deciding to marry him, Xue Wei does not know about the ambiguous relationship between Qi Yue and Jiang Meng, which quietly sows the seeds of future storms in their seemingly happy family…

**Yu Yangping / Played by Yuki (Masayuki Zeng)**

"When will you ever smile for me?"

Male, 21 years old

Kind and considerate, Yangping is easy-going and gets along well with others. Because of this, he manages to befriend the fiery-tempered Jiang Meng. The shy Yangping only reveals his playful side in front of Lixiang and Jiang Meng, enthusiastically clinging to Jiang Meng and following Lixiang, hoping Lixiang would treat him as attentively as she treats Jiang Meng. However, Yangping’s advances are always treated as jokes, and no one knows that Yangping actually likes Lixiang deep down. One of the reasons he stays close to Jiang Meng is because he can see Lixiang. For Yangping, this is a lonely and unreciprocated one-sided love…

"Hey, handsome. Want to go on a date with me?"

Female, 22 years old

One of Qi Yue’s good friends, Qing Zi is a standout beauty in the university. Bold in self-expression, she is very outgoing. Always ready to help others, Qing Zi is the first to step up when her friends are in trouble, making her a big sister figure.
