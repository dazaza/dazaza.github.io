The heart that waits is so tired.

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-27 15:49:17

The heart of waiting is so tired.

That day, you suddenly contacted me again. I was really surprised, but more excited.

Why did you quietly walk into my life and leave without even an explanation?

Do you know? Last winter, when you contacted me, I was really happy. Hearing the familiar voice on the other end of the phone, time pulled me back to four years ago. When we were in the second year of junior high school, you sat behind me. You often made some small movements, making me laugh out loud.

Once during a self-study class, you played with me and splashed water from the mineral water bottle onto me. At that time, I wasn't angry at all; I just thought it was interesting because I knew you didn't do it on purpose. But Principal X saw it, criticized you without hearing your explanation, and made you stand by the podium. You went up there holding the bottle (it was the teacher's deliberate punishment). At that moment, you stood in front, staring straight at me.

It wasn't a fierce look, just looking at me. The classmates below were all watching you as if it were a joke. At that time, my face turned red. I didn't dare to look at you because I felt guilty, thinking it was my fault that you had to go up there. I was very afraid then, afraid that you would never talk to me again or play with me anymore.

Later, you still stayed close to me. You continued to chat and laugh with me. I was really happy because I felt like you were my sunshine. Back then, I didn't know that what I felt at the time was actually what is now called "crush." I was naive and immature then and didn't understand these ambiguous things between men and women. When classmates talked about such things, I would even blush or avoid them. Thinking about those times, I was really silly. Do you know how you thought of me back then?

Later, because of the temporary reclassification in the third year of junior high school, we were separated. You were in Class XX, and I was still in the original Class XX. At that time, I had a slight sense of loss in my heart because you wouldn't be able to study with me anymore, you wouldn't be able to sit behind me and make me laugh, and I wouldn't be able to see your dimples and your cute smiling eyes as often. When your desk was moved away from mine, I felt like you were going to disappear forever.

Then came the tense life of the third year of junior high school. I was in this classroom, and you were in the next one. We rarely saw each other because you were mischievous and often skipped classes. Even when we saw each other, we just glanced at each other and then hurriedly went our separate ways. Time seemed to pass quickly, and graduation was approaching. Maybe many things would end here.

After graduating from junior high school, I didn't get into my ideal high school. I gave up the most beautiful high school life and chose to attend a junior college. That's how we lost contact.

In the past few years of studying outside, although I was having a great time here and the classmates cared about me, I would think of my junior high school life from time to time, more accurately, I would think of you from junior high school, the boy who often brought me laughter. I asked some old classmates about you, but they didn't know your situation. I was troubled, I thought, I must find you and retrieve those beautiful memories from the past. Sometimes, I would fantasize about meeting you on the street one day. How would I react, and what would you be like?

It was last year, by chance, I finally found out your QQ number. My heart was racing hard when I was searching for it. After adding you, I felt much more at ease. After all, the big stone in my heart finally fell at this moment. You politely asked who I was, and after I told you to guess from my online name, you sent over several names, and one of them was me. I was so happy then. I felt that you still remembered me, the innocent me from back then.

As we chatted on QQ, I learned that you had been studying at the school right across from mine for the past one or two years. And I hadn't known, even though I often went to that school to visit my cousin. I was stunned at that moment, realizing that you had been not far from me all along, but I didn't know. You told me that you were going to serve in the military in XX place for two years. I was very happy at that time because serving in the military can improve a person in many aspects, such as temperament and character. I was truly delighted by your wise choice.

We then exchanged phone numbers. You quickly got in touch with me. We had a great conversation, talking about our school days and our current lives. I felt like we had returned to our junior high school days. During that period, I was very happy because I was accompanied by your thoughtful phone greetings. I was completely immersed in it at that time.

X, I have always liked you. I'm going to serve in the military for two years. Can you wait for me to come back? You said it seriously at that time. At first, I was stunned, and then I was filled with joy. Isn't this what I've been wanting to hear from you? I didn't say anything at that moment, just kept silent. I had always suppressed my feelings for you. I told DD this good news. I originally thought she would bless us. Unexpectedly, she said you were an experienced player who liked to deceive others' feelings. She also mentioned some unpleasant things about you. I was very angry at that time, not at you, but at others' criticisms of you. Despite that, I still naively believed in you, believing that you were still the pure and simple boy I used to know. I ignored others' gossip and even prepared to be with you forever. Really, I was very brave at that time.

In the following days, I became more dependent on you. It felt like you had become my boyfriend. I paid attention to everything about you. I couldn't help but check your QQ profile, inquire about every bit of information about you, and whenever your QQ icon lit up, my heart would become tense. I transformed my longing for you into words and wrote them in my diary. I even fantasized about our future, walking hand in hand on the beach...

Beautiful days are always so short. Good times don't last long, and due to a misunderstanding, you suddenly stopped contacting me. At that time, I was distressed. I cried, frantically recalling whether I had done something wrong that upset you. I don't remember how many times I cried silently in my blanket every day. Whenever I heard the song "Not Worth It," I would involuntarily think of you and our past moments together. I was afraid that it was true, as written in the lyrics, that it wasn't worth my effort and tears for you.

Really, you left so heartlessly. Changed your phone number, went to XX place, and never contacted me. I felt very lost and angry. Why did you leave so quietly without telling me? Were the things you said to me before true, that you liked me and wanted me to wait for you for two years? Or were they just jokes because you were lonely? Do they count? Should I follow them?

During the time after you left, my mind was full of your voice, your laughter... Sometimes I listened to your favorite songs and carefully tasted the meaning of the lyrics. Listening to them, I couldn't help but cry. Tears are so salty. However, today, there is another taste—bitterness... Every day, I was listless, didn't want to do anything. I always suffered from insomnia. Even when I slept, I couldn't get rid of your shadow in my dreams. When I woke up, I found my pillow was wet.

Thus, I lived through a period of confusion. I was in great pain, wanting to forget everything about you. Every time I took out my mobile phone and wanted to delete your number, I hesitated when I was about to press the confirm button. Later, your number and all the messages you sent me remained in my phone. Whenever your QQ went online, I was really excited. I wanted to ask you, but I was afraid to disturb you. Why are you so stingy, not even giving a simple greeting? Giving up, I repeatedly looked at your QQ profile information, which I already knew by heart. I continued living like this...

I really want to forget you, forget those beautiful memories, and the familiar you in those memories. But why do you keep disturbing my life?

Last year, during the Dragon Boat Festival, you suddenly contacted me again, caring as usual. Your voice on the other end of the phone was still so familiar. I tried hard to control my excitement. I kept comforting myself, saying that the reason you didn't contact me last time was because you were too busy or forgot my phone number. Besides, when you first arrived there, the management was very strict. Therefore, there were various reasons why you didn't contact me. I originally thought that we could communicate well again, maintaining this complex relationship that was neither friends nor lovers.

However, good times didn't last long. In just a few days, you disappeared again. I was like a child who had lost their mother, crazily searching for you and secretly investigating information about you.

Until recently, due to an internship, the busyness of the internship diluted my endless thoughts of you. But when I stopped and relaxed, the image of you that I couldn't get rid of still appeared in my mind. I told myself not to think about him anymore, maybe he's doing well now and has forgotten about this little me.

Really, until now, I still can't forget you from back then. I don't know if I miss you or the feeling I had back then.

Maybe waiting for you was a mistake from the beginning, waiting for a result that will never come. But you don't know how sad I am alone.
