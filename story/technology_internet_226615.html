1> How to reduce abdominal fat

Many office workers, due to sitting in the office for a long time and lack of exercise, easily accumulate fat in the abdomen area, and abdominal fat belongs to deep fat, which requires multi-faceted cooperation to effectively solve.

1. Need to change dietary habits.

Don't sit down or lie down immediately after eating, it's better to stay standing, you can choose to take a walk or tidy up some things. This not only reduces fat accumulation but also helps digestion. Because within 30 minutes after eating, if you remain still, it's easiest to form abdominal fat.

2. Correct walking and sitting posture.

When walking, keep your head up, chest out, and swing your arms. Often hugging your arms across your chest, without using your abdominal muscles, can easily lead to a protruding belly. Moreover, walking while swinging your arms not only consumes more energy but also makes you look especially spirited. And when sitting, keep your back straight, don't bend over or protrude your abdomen, only then can you train your abdominal muscles, making them strong and not prone to sagging.

3. Need to combine with exercise.

Shaking a hula hoop or doing sit-ups or stretching whenever possible can gradually eliminate abdominal fat and make your abdominal muscles increasingly firm and less likely to accumulate fat again.

Aerobic exercise helps with weight loss

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pre-stored ATP energy can only last 15 seconds, after running 100 meters it's all used up. For the next 100 meters in a 200-meter race, blood sugar must rapidly synthesize new energy substances ATP under anaerobic conditions to provide energy, with lactic acid as the byproduct. In races like 200 meters or 400 meters, 100-meter swimming, tennis, and football, the energy comes from the anaerobic decomposition of blood sugar, so after the exercise, a large amount of lactic acid accumulates in the muscles, and lactic acid is the substance that causes muscle soreness after exercise. The blood sugar for this kind of exercise is provided by starch, G cannot burn fat, so it is not beneficial for weight loss.

The energy provided by the anaerobic decomposition of blood sugar (starch) can only last forty seconds, after running 400 meters it's all used up. For an 800-meter run, the next 400 meters must be powered by the synthesis of new energy substances ATP from blood sugar, blood fatty acids, and blood amino acids under aerobic conditions, where blood sugar is supplied after starch decomposition, blood fatty acids are supplied after fat decomposition, and blood amino acids are supplied after protein decomposition, and this entire process requires oxygen, meaning burning starch, fat, and protein with oxygen to produce energy substances ATP, supplying the heat needed for later-stage exercise, and this later stage of exercise is "aerobic exercise".

Running 800 meters or 1500 meters, 200 and 400 meters swimming, boxing, etc., all require starting to use oxygen to burn starch, fat, and protein, so the later stages of these exercises are all aerobic exercises. In the early stages of exercise, approximately the first five minutes burn starch, the longer the exercise continues, the more fat will be burned, as long as it lasts half an hour to an hour, fifty percent of the consumed calories will be supplied by burning fat, if you don't diet, even an hour of aerobic exercise can only burn the starch and fat in food, not the fat stored in the human body, so it is still not beneficial for weight loss; after dieting, an hour of aerobic exercise has the chance to burn the fat inside the body.

In short, doing aerobic exercise after dieting, and dieting after aerobic exercise, is how weight loss can be effective.

The most effective weight loss exercise

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

American exercise scientists believe that the most effective weight loss exercise is jogging first, followed by cycling, and thirdly playing handball, and these are all continuous aerobic exercises (exercises that use oxygen to burn body fat). The value of continuous exercise lies in maintaining the weight loss effects of dieting. After successful weight loss, dieting and exercise must become part of one's lifestyle to maintain a good figure for life. Stopping at any time makes it easy to gain weight again.
