A 70-cm-tall mother gave birth to three babies in three years.

by zzf000zxye on 2011-06-22 13:16:22

Gerard is holding her newly-born baby. Gerard and her husband are in the hospital.

According to local news, Stacy Gerard, called by British media as the world's smallest mother, is now enjoying the happiness brought by the birth of her third child. Doctors had warned Gerard, who is only 70 cm tall, multiple times not to get pregnant because it would put her life at risk. However, determined Gerard challenged medical authority despite the danger to her life for the sake of having a complete family. In three years, she has given birth to three healthy babies.

The baby weighs only 1 kilogram

Gerard gave birth to her third child, Malachi, prematurely on November 28th, but this little one only weighed 1 kilogram. Due to undergoing a cesarean section, Gerard's abdomen was stitched up with 34 stitches by the doctor, and she is still lying in bed.

Gerard's due date was originally set for just before Christmas, but doctors decided to perform the cesarean section earlier at the end of November. They were worried that if they didn't operate earlier, Gerard's life would be in danger. Gerard, who is recovering smoothly from the surgery, said: "I just want to stay by my child's side."

Gerard, who is already the mother of three children, comes from the United States. When she was very young, she was diagnosed by a doctor with osteogenesis imperfecta, a rare congenital disorder affecting bone development. Patients with this disease have especially fragile bones, underdeveloped heart and lung functions, and are all particularly short.

Malachi, who is over a week old, currently measures only 12 cm in height and weighs just over 1 kilogram. He can currently only stay in the neonatal intensive care unit of Cincinnati Hospital in the United States. The doctor said that Malachi's condition is still unstable, and he may need to stay in the hospital for several months at least. (Donglai)
