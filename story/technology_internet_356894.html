Father stabbed in clash after drunkenly reprimanding son (picture)

by yqwdfkmt on 2010-05-29 13:58:53

After the incident, Mr. Shi was taken to the hospital for emergency treatment. At around 2 PM on May 20th, he was wheeled into the operating room. His family and friends anxiously waited outside. At 5:20 PM, his surgery was finally completed. Subsequently, he was pushed into the intensive care unit.

That evening, the person in charge of handling the incident at Nanning's Nanhu Police Station stated that this matter involved issues related to women, with a relatively complex situation, and further investigation and handling were still ongoing.

The door was covered in blood, the outdoor area littered with broken wine bottles, shoes stained with blood, and an unfinished lunch... This was the scene left after a bloody incident involving not strangers, but a father and son – a 17-year-old son stabbing his 42-year-old father; the father lying in a pool of blood, his intestines spilling out. This shocking and thought-provoking event raises questions about whether it was due to the father’s educational methods or the son’s defiance.

"Little brother, quickly bandage your father's wound," urged a woman as she prompted Xiao Shi to treat Mr. Shi's injury. A nearby grocery store owner, Mr. Jiang, also arrived at the scene to see if he could help. Mr. Jiang's wife called the police.

Xiao Shi is 17 years old. For the past two years, he has neither studied nor worked, relying mainly on his father for support. "Xiao Shi likes to go online, often going out at night and only returning home when it's light outside," said a neighbor. Seeing Xiao Shi's addiction to the internet, Mr. Shi tried to discipline him, forbidding him from going online.

Mr. Shi's two younger brothers and three younger siblings also came to the hospital. One of them mentioned that Mr. Shi had poor academic performance and dropped out of school in the first year of junior high, beginning to indulge in gaming and hanging out with friends at that time.

The father's severe injuries caused great concern among those present. Blood continuously flowed from the wounds on Mr. Shi's right arm and abdomen, with some of his intestines protruding out of his body. His shoes were stained red with blood.

At around midnight on May 20th, in a residential area on Xiuxian Road in Nanning, many residents were having lunch. Suddenly, someone shouted, "Something happened! Someone got stabbed!" Hearing this, the residents immediately came out of their homes to witness a terrifying sight - Mr. Shi lying in front of his own door, covered in blood, while his son Xiao Shi sat beside him, seemingly in shock.

Some residents suggested that Xiao Shi, being young and easily impulsive, might have momentarily lost control and acted against his father. "Nowadays, some young people are hard to discipline. At the slightest provocation, they take it out on their parents," said citizen Mr. Wei, who believed that regardless of the circumstances, this incident deserves reflection: "Children need to think about how to manage the unruly youth of today."

Mr. Shi, 42 years old, is from Yongning District in Nanning. He lived with his son Xiao Shi and Xiao Shi's grandfather in a rather old house on Xiuxian Road. He had been living there for over ten years and currently worked as a gatekeeper at a company. Xiao Shi's father had been working in Guangxi for the past few years.

"Although he loves to play and go online, his personality is generally good. He isn't very rebellious and tends to remain silent and teary-eyed when scolded," said one of Mr. Shi's brothers. Recently, Xiao Shi had visited his uncle's home. "I once suggested taking him (referring to Mr. Shi) to farm, but he refused. I didn't know he frequently went online."

According to information gathered, because Xiao Shi was addicted to the internet, Mr. Shi would scold and even hit him. On that day at noon, after drinking, Mr. Shi tried to discipline Xiao Shi again, leading to a conflict where Xiao Shi took a knife and stabbed his father.

At the scene of the incident, an elderly man remarked that no matter how the father treated the son, the son should never use a knife to stab his father. "He is the dad, and the son using a knife on his dad is just unacceptable." Other residents also expressed that such behavior by children cannot be tolerated and should be punished.

"After the surgery, his condition remains serious and unstable, and he hasn't yet escaped life-threatening danger," said a doctor. Mr. Shi had experienced a period of shock, with internal bleeding in his abdominal cavity, and still required assistance from a ventilator to breathe. "He will need to spend the night (May 20th) in the intensive care unit."

Mr. Shi's youngest brother expressed that he didn't know much about Xiao Shi, "but he seemed like a decent person. It's unclear how he could do something like that."
