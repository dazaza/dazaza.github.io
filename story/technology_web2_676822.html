AVIC Harbin Aircraft Industry sold 6 Z-9 helicopters and 2 Y-12 aircraft in January.

by flajfj7427 on 2012-02-26 15:53:50

Z-9: Since 2011, AVIC Harbin Aircraft Industry has been continuously increasing its market development efforts domestically and internationally after signing a purchase and sales contract for 30 Z-9 series helicopters with AVIC International. The external sales of the Z-9 reached a record high, and the sales territory of the Y-12 expanded. In January alone, contracts were signed for the sale of 6 Z-9s and two Y-12s, bringing a strong start to market sales.

Employees in the Harbin final assembly workshop are conducting Z-9 assembly work. After signing the contract for the purchase and sale of 30 Z-9 helicopters last year, Harbin and AVIC International jointly formed a project team. Both parties, adhering to the principle of win-win cooperation, fully utilized opportunities such as airshows to actively increase their market expansion efforts both domestically and internationally. According to the purchase and sales contract, the sales of the 30 Z-9s will be completed by 2014. Under the active efforts of the project team, in 2011, contracts were signed for the export of 7 Z-9s to Zambia and 12 Z-9s to Cambodia, achieving commendable results in external sales of the Z-9.

This year, the project team continued to intensify market development efforts, successfully selling 6 Z-9s to Bolivia. As a result, out of the 30 Z-9 purchase and sales contracts, 25 have been completed, and the overall goal is expected to be achieved ahead of schedule.

The Y-12 aircraft is hailed as the proud flower of China's civil aviation, being the first Chinese aircraft to enter the international market, exporting to the most countries and having the highest sales volume among civil aircraft. Laos immediately put the two purchased Y-12s into operation, with each aircraft flying three flights per day, all fully booked, demonstrating high aircraft dispatch rates and good operational effects. It can take off and land on gravel runways, earning praise from Laos's civil aviation users. Excellent performance, good quality, superior service, and price advantages continue to attract repeat customers. To date, the Y-12 series aircraft has been exported to more than 20 countries and regions, with over 150 delivered to domestic and international users.

At the beginning of the new year, Harbin deeply explored potential Y-12 users while further improving the user revisit system to enhance after-sales service quality, adapting to market demands with the product’s superior performance and satisfying customers with honest services. In January, a purchase and sales contract was signed with Genhe City Government of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region for two Y-12E aircraft, and preliminary purchase intentions were reached with the State Oceanic Administration and other units, showing a promising outlook for the sales of the Y-12 series once again.
