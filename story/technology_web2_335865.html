You might probably get confused with most of Coach's designs since many of them look the same in check pattern. This time, Coach has incorporated bands to add richness and more interesting appeal to its rather classic and usual monochrome design.

The price of $795 is a bit too much though, but considering the enduring appeal of this black and white PVC purse could definitely make up for it. Coach's signature check print is designed on this tote, cheap bags, Monogram with pink Coach leather bag Adjustable pink color leather strap with gold color buckle Flat pocket on both side with gold color lock Pink leather lining on top Four gold color protective studs at the bottom Flat pocket and zip pocket inside. This coach bag 1443 looks large enough to fill with your daily necessities, which then makes this a practical and versatile purse.

Pairing this Coach purse with any types and colors of outfit should be very easy considering that it comes in monochrome shade. But so as not to look too dull, perhaps your black and white décolletage should complete your smart look.
