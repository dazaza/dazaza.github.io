YaWa: a domestic personalized home page service

by cacard on 2007-12-20 13:16:08

[Official AboutUs] YaWa (www.yaawa.com) is China's first personalized aggregation platform based on the Web3.0 concept, pioneering in search customization and modular aggregation, dedicated to allowing every user to freely aggregate massive amounts of information!

YaWa has uniquely created the WidgetRank aggregation technology and applied it to its services. Through continuous innovation and new applications, YaWa showcases the "RSS Aggregation + Search Customization Aggregation + Personalized Platform" model, enhancing the convenience for users to access information.

YaWa’s innovative WidgetRank aggregation technology and algorithms can automatically and effectively analyze, capture, and aggregate any RSS feed from blogs, forums, websites, etc., presenting them according to the reading method freely chosen by netizens. As a result, YaWa quickly became an important platform for netizens to read news, blogs, and personalize their experience.

YaWa will bring you a brand-new world and experience:

- Search Customization: Also known as keyword aggregation, you can input keywords of information you are interested in, and YaWa will instantly find the information you need from a vast amount of data. Simultaneously, you can aggregate and customize this information onto the YaWa platform. Every time you open YaWa, you don't have to input a search again to see the latest information you need.

- RSS Aggregation: When you find that a website or your blog friends provide good information but require you to visit their sites daily to know their updates, this wastes your precious time. In such cases, YaWa is your top choice. You only need to input a blog address or QQ number, and YaWa can automatically analyze, aggregate, and customize the blog information or news you want, categorizing and organizing it into one page, achieving "knowing everything in one page". It's just that simple.

- Making Frog Friends: More and more frog friends come to YaWa, showcasing their tastes, expressing their personalities, and making more unique frog friends, together creating a whole new world.

- Personalized Platform: Based on the Web3.0 concept, with a completely new interface modularization, you can freely create new modules and pages, dragging, editing module content and styles at will. If you register, YaWa will automatically save your personalized platform and generate a unique second-level domain name for you. You can freely display your personality and style, and YaWa will bring you a great user experience and surprises!

YaWa, similar to the exclamation "Wow!", wishes you a surprise every day!

YaWa, with a brand-new concept and a brand-new world, received favor and investment from Singapore's WR Investment Group during its early development stage. Meanwhile, multiple investors have proactively contacted us, giving the team immense encouragement and confidence. We wish to create a new world with you!

Are you ready? Let's discover the wonders and surprises of YaWa together!
