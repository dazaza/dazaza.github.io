One-year junior college and undergraduate recruitment of China Finance and Economics Management College

by px023 on 2007-06-28 11:34:54

Introduction of the Academy —— China Finance And Economic Management Institute is jointly invested and established by Hong Kong Huapeng Group and other domestic powerful educational service institutions such as Yijiahua and Xienen Education. It was approved and registered by the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China, with registration number: 35378339-005-03-07-5. The academy is a comprehensive open educational training institution integrating distance education, vocational skills, cultural exchange, educational investment, and research education.

Characteristic of Education —— Network education is a new teaching model that combines higher education with modern information technology, breaking through the limitations of time, space, and majors in traditional teaching and education. China Finance And Economic Management Institute has always adhered to the school-running guiding principle of "Quality is the lifeline of network education", establishing a teaching service system centered on courses and a student service system centered on students. While opening new majors, it provides students with a large number of online teaching resources such as online assignments, simulated test questions, and online answers, fully demonstrating the advantages of distance teaching. The academy also implements a dual-certificate parallel teaching model, allowing students to obtain multiple certificates and apply their knowledge in multiple ways, where academic qualifications + skills = high salary, truly solving the problem of student employment. After students complete the required credits with qualified academic performance, they will be awarded a graduation certificate from China Finance And Economic Management Institute (a realistic graduation certificate not belonging to national private education), which will be registered online on the official website of the academy: www.emiedu.cn.

Learning Characteristics —— Year-round enrollment, start learning upon registration, admission without examination, electronic registration, available online for inquiry, self-study as the main method.

Admission Requirements —— High school to junior college, high school to bachelor's degree (junior college and bachelor's degree consecutive reading): Admitting students and social individuals with high school, vocational high school, secondary vocational school, technical school graduation certificates or equivalent academic qualifications (those who graduated from junior high school, have worked for more than three years, and have excellent personal conditions can also enroll in some junior college majors). Junior college to bachelor's degree: Admitting students and social individuals with junior college or above graduation certificates or equivalent academic qualifications.

Learning Methods —— The innovation of the educational system has broadened the channels for students to receive higher-level education and training. To meet the needs of society, the academy has pioneered a variety of flexible study models: mainly part-time learning forms, adopting a CNCE teaching model primarily based on network education and self-study, supplemented by centralized network exchanges, namely: using (Correspondence + Network + Counseling + Exchange) "correspondence teaching materials + online course learning, online answers, online discussions, online tutoring, online course assignments, self-test exercises + examination evaluation, graduation assignments (graduation thesis) + regional networking and exchanges" teaching model.

Examination Methods —— After students finish each course in the online classroom (15 courses for junior college, 18 courses for bachelor's degree), there are six exams scheduled each year, with specific exam times to be notified separately (usually arranged on the 20th day of February, April, June, August, October, and December). Students can log in to take the open-book form exam online using their student ID and password. The academy will send the exam results to the students' email within an effective working period, please check your email or take the online exam directly online.

Major Settings —— Insurance, financial insurance, financial management, accounting computerization, e-commerce, law, real estate operation and management, real estate operation and appraisal, engineering cost estimation, business administration, industrial and civil building, public service management, advertising design and production, advertising, international economy and trade, international business, environmental engineering, mechatronics, mechanical engineering and automation, computer science and technology, computer network technology, computer application, architectural engineering technology, economic management, hotel management, tourism management, administrative management, chain operation management, enterprise management, automobile marketing, human resource management, marketing, civil engineering, secretarial management, logistics management, property management, project management, journalism, art design, applied psychology, English, nutrition, etc., more than 40 majors.

Enrollment Procedures —— Enrollees should carry the original and two copies of their ID card, standard blue-bottomed digital photos without headwear (Note: Photos need to be provided in electronic format, requiring JPG files no larger than 100K), and the original and two copies of the highest academic qualification graduation certificate, along with a personal resume and fill out the enrollment form. Enrollees can consult and enroll at the authorized enrollment office of the academy in the local area.

Charging Standards —— Fees are charged according to the regulations of relevant departments. Tuition fees are paid once, and no additional fees are charged.

1. Junior College (High School to Junior College) two-year tuition fee 2800 yuan, including enrollment fee, registration and file establishment fee, network tuition fee, examination fee, thesis defense fee, certificate electronic registration fee, etc.;

2. Bachelor's Degree (Junior College to Bachelor's Degree) two-year tuition fee 3800 yuan, including enrollment fee, registration and file establishment fee, network tuition fee, examination fee, thesis defense fee, certificate electronic registration fee, etc.;

3. Junior College and Bachelor's Degree Consecutive Reading four-year tuition fee 4800 yuan, including enrollment fee, registration and file establishment fee, network tuition fee, examination fee, thesis defense fee, certificate electronic registration fee, etc.

(Note: The above fees do not include textbook fees; charges vary by major, please contact the local teaching center for details.)

Nature of Certificate —— Students must complete all professional courses and all teaching links specified by the academy for this major, pass the exams or evaluations, meet the minimum total credit requirements for graduation, and have good moral character, and submit a graduation thesis to graduate. The graduation certificate will be stamped with steel seal, red seal, dean's seal (the certificate has four unique anti-counterfeiting features: 1. watermark; 2. hidden text; 3. bright red; 4. photo coding), and a study achievement certificate stamped by the teaching department of China Finance And Economic Management Institute will be issued. This certificate is electronically registered on the academy's website, and graduates will have an archive, score report, and graduation thesis appraisal.
