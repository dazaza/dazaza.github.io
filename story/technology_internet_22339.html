⊙ Seven Vertical Search Engines Affecting China's Development !!

by tame95 on 2007-06-12 14:49:23

Baidu's venture into Japan has an uncertain future, Google's entry into China met with accusations of plagiarism, and Yahoo! China underwent yet another transformation. By the time 2007 was only one-third over, the Chinese internet search field had already become highly eventful. Little known to many, amidst these internet giants lurked numerous potential threats. Various vertical search engines were quietly expanding and strengthening themselves, intending to snatch a share from Baidu, Google, and Yahoo! We can see that the development of these various vertical search engines will not only impact the market of the search giants but also influence the habits and development of the Chinese people.

1. Forum Search:

Representative: Qihoo www.qihoo.com

Founded in September 2005, it was developed by the core technical team from the former Yahoo! China’s 3721 R&D department. It received large amounts of venture capital within less than 200 days of its establishment and was hailed by all quarters as "the most investment-worthy enterprise," "the most innovative enterprise," and "the fastest-growing enterprise."

Function: Claimed to be the first portal for Chinese forums, it mainly collects and organizes a large amount of information stored on BBSs. Once a participant becomes a member of the Qihoo forum alliance, they are automatically included in Qihoo's indexing program for automatic searches and featured on the homepage or recommended as hot topics. Users clicking through still access the original forum pages, which helps promote good posts and forums, rapidly increasing site traffic (the more good posts, the higher the traffic). Additionally, qihoo.com promotes excellent forums using methods such as the "Global Chinese Forum Rankings" and "Forum Search Result Pages." The year 2006 marked the golden era for qihoo.com.

User Group: Web editors and BBS users

Popularity Index: ★★★★★

2. Lifestyle Search:

Representative: Kuxun www.kooxoo.com

Established at the end of 2005, this lifestyle information search website was jointly created by Chen Hua, who came from Microsoft, and Wu Shichun, who had been researching search engines. It is said that the website secured its first round of financing in just three hours in March 2006.

Function: Different from the web page search engines of Baidu and Google, kooxoo.com is a professional search engine that focuses on real-time lifestyle information. It provides services such as job hunting, renting/buying houses, purchasing train tickets, and covers various aspects of life including clothing, food, housing, transportation, work, friendship, and shopping. Kooxoo.com features an automatic update function that pushes the latest information matching the search criteria directly to the user. This technology earned Kuxun the title of "the world's first 'bubbling' search engine."

User Group: General public

Popularity Index: ★★★★☆

3. E-commerce Search:

Representative: Hengzhe www.hengzhe.com

Hengzhe is referred to as the most competitive indigenous Chinese informatization vertical comprehensive search engine, established by a young, diligent, and vibrant entrepreneurial team in a modest room near Yuelu Mountain. With a strong sense of responsibility, it examines the development of the network economy, integrating commercial opportunities, recruitment, real estate, shopping, yellow pages, and news information using advanced online search technology, promoting the development of e-commerce through high-quality trustworthy services, and creating the future with scientific market sensitivity, operating with people as the core. Hengzhe serves all visitors, providing them with comprehensive, professional, and timely information. The young Hengzhe adheres to the concept of creating infinite value for visitors, targeting all those seeking success and beautiful prospects, standing at the forefront of the market, capturing business information, linking network resources, mining commercial values, and actively practicing the historical mission of serving society. It provides users with better search experiences through leading retrieval technology.

User Group: Small and medium-sized enterprises and individual users

Popularity Index: ★★★☆☆

4. Travel Search:

Representative: Qunar www.qunar.com

Founded in May 2005 by Fred Dai, Iris Chang, and Douglas of the "Shark Team". Qunar focuses on providing deep information search services for consumers and establishes advantages in depth service in multiple aspects such as data search volume, search scope, response speed, price timeliness, data detail level, and usability of filtering and sorting functions.

Function: As the world's largest Chinese travel search engine, “Qunar” (Qunar.com) transforms the expectation of "Think search travel." into convenient and intuitive internet applications. By integrating and publishing all online travel product resources, it provides real-time, credible travel product price comparison and service comparison systems, helping consumers easily make sufficient choices and find the most suitable online travel products for their perfect journey. Hence, some people refer to using Qunar as "the smart person's choice."

User Group: Tourists and travelers

Popularity Index: ★★★★☆

5. Recruitment Search:

Representative: Souzhi www.globehr.com

Founded in May 2005, it claims to be the world's largest Chinese talent and job search engine. Using its independently developed precise search technology, it accurately searches all job information, providing users with a unified recruitment search entry point, saving users time and effort, and gaining widespread attention after its launch.

Function: Its independently developed Chinese job search engine technology can instantly search all human resources websites, forums (BBS), communities (SNS), and corporate HR channels nationwide. It allows users to spend the least amount of time finding the latest matching jobs from major talent websites across the country and simultaneously send out multiple resumes.

User Group: Job seekers

Popularity Index: ★★☆☆☆

6. Price Comparison Search:

Representative: Dingjiu www.ding9.com

Founded at the end of 2005 by Yang Zhifeng and Wei Jian. Dingjiu aims to create China's largest and most functional comparison shopping search engine, allowing consumers to search for any item they wish to purchase through Dingjiu and compare prices to become the starting point and necessary step for consumer shopping.

Function: Through product comparison, price comparison, and merchant comparison, it enables users to find the most suitable product, lowest price, and most reputable merchants. Dingjiu is committed to providing consumers with various shopping-related information, such as product information, merchant information, user reviews, expert reviews, discounts, and promotions, offering consumers comprehensive shopping guidance services and guiding them to make wise purchasing decisions.

User Group: Shoppers

Popularity Index: ★★☆☆☆

7. Entertainment Search:

Representative: Video Search www.openv.tv

Officially launched in April 2006, co-founded by Wu Xin and Chen Yanpeng. Currently, it is the largest video index library in China. OpenV.tv uses the world's most advanced Autonomy intelligent information processing technology and was awarded the "Video Search Gold Award" by the American professional online magazine Webuser. OpenV had angel investors from its inception, and Autonomy entered strategically.

Function: OpenV.tv owns globally leading, unique video processing technology. The pinnacle of this technology lies in "key frame search," and Open V leverages this core technology to aim at becoming China's largest video search engine. Frame search is an industrialized technology, reliable, stable, and easy to use. It turns the possibility of retrieving content, words, and frames into reality and can achieve precise targeted video advertising beyond television.

User Group: Video enthusiasts

Popularity Index: ★★☆☆☆
