Yirui and Anping, two half-sisters born on the same day in the same hospital, were mistakenly led into lives not meant for them due to an absurd error. The rich girl endured hardships while the poor girl enjoyed familial bliss. Li Guohao was the man Yirui once deeply loved. When their jointly operated Haorui Company was about to go bankrupt, Li Guohao attempted bank loan fraud to evade debts. At that time, Yirui assisted him for love. But when the truth came out, Yirui again believed in Li Guohao's repentance and promises, entering prison alone to atone for their sins.

Two years later, as Yirui faced life anew, everything had changed. Yirui searched for Li Guohao, and coincidentally, Shi Jiawei, the young general manager of An Group who lived in Li Guohao's former apartment, met her. Eventually, Yirui met Li Guohao under special circumstances. Li Guohao had abandoned Yirui to marry another woman and become the president of Northern Industry through his wealthy wife. Li Guohao did not expect Yirui to be released early and felt unprecedented fear because Yirui held evidence of his past bank loan fraud scheme. Li Guohao used all his tactics against women to retrieve the evidence. Meanwhile, Yirui's reappearance reignited Li Guohao's desire for her, not out of love but a strong sense of possession after losing her. Li Guohao decided either to reclaim Yirui or destroy her.

Through several encounters with Yirui, her kindness and tolerance deeply moved Jiawei. Her intelligence also made Jiawei realize Yirui was an invaluable talent. After much effort, Jiawei convinced his adoptive father, Andar, and Andar's daughter Anping to let Yirui join the company. Yirui cherished her job at An Group despite numerous trials. Anping, spoiled by her parents since childhood, thought there was nothing in this world she couldn't obtain. However, Jiawei was someone she could never have; he regarded Anping as his sister. Anping felt threatened by Yirui's appearance. No matter how she obstructed, she couldn't stop Jiawei and Yirui from falling in love. Jiawei's pure and passionate love finally melted Yirui's heart after many obstacles.

Yirui eventually understood why her mother so strongly opposed her joining An Group and being with Jiawei: An Group's chairman, Andar, was her biological father. Zhang Chuling could never forgive Andar for leaving her without a word, which caused her immense pain. Yirui thought her mother's hatred towards her stemmed from her resentment towards her father. But what Zhang Chuling kept hidden was not only her hatred for Andar but also for Andar's wife, Shuxian. Yirui was her tool to retaliate against Andar and Shuxian. Despite this, Yirui worked hard to resolve the grievances between her parents and even wanted Jiawei to return to Anping to fulfill her sister's feelings. However, the unknowing Anping hated Yirui even more.

Amidst all these changes, illness mercilessly consumed Yirui's life. She chose to bear it alone, still leaving the brightest smiles for everyone. Unable to bear the inner pain, Zhang Chuling left Yirui and went to find Andar in the village where he was exiled years ago. They arrived at the small house where they first met, but it had collapsed. Yirui used her life to protect Andar, and here, she finally called him "Dad." The twenty-year estranged father-daughter relationship smoothed over all grievances.

As Anping joyfully prepared to become Jiawei's bride, Jiawei couldn't suppress his longing for Yirui and left Anping. In a fit of madness, Anping stabbed Yirui, whose leukemia worsened, making her critically ill. When Yirui urgently needed blood transfusion, Zhang Chuling knelt before Shuxian and begged her to save Yirui. Zhang Chuling finally revealed the secret buried in her heart for over twenty years. All doubts were clarified. The two mothers reconnected with their familiar yet estranged daughters, filled with pain, but ultimately reconciled through Yirui's kindness and tolerance. Anping also understood her sister by Yirui's bedside and truly grasped the meaning of the ivy flower.

Andar, full of guilt towards both daughters, knelt beside Yirui's bed in repentance during her coma using his last strength. When people found him, he had already passed away, still kneeling but with a smile of happiness on his face because he finally found peace in his guilt.

Meanwhile, Li Guohao, to possess Meiying's assets, continued his plans with Yirui. However, he ultimately fell into the trap set by his most trusted brother. Yet, Yirui saved this man who had fallen into the abyss of the soul with the last brilliance of her life. Li Guohao finally understood the meaning of Yirui's words: "Being a good person makes you live comfortably; being an honest person makes you live courageously." Li Guohao ultimately made the right choice at the edge of losing humanity. He bravely turned himself in and bravely took responsibility for all his crimes.

Yirui and Jiawei could finally be together without reservation, though Yirui's time was limited. Jiawei used his wholehearted love to ignite Yirui's passion for life. They loved intensely, thoroughly, and tenaciously. Even as life fades, love remains eternal. Holding ivy in her hands, Yirui peacefully passed away in her lover's arms, completing their deeply touching love story.

This is a mainstream TV drama promoting truth, goodness, and beauty, aiming to make people feel long-lost emotions again and seek noble sentiments through tears. It is also a work exploring the roots of mainstream values. All precious and noble moral qualities in human society originate from the nature of kindness and tolerance.
