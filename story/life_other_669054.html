Tranquil Watch

Time flies, too fast, and busy days continue as usual. The changing of years and the cycle of the four seasons remain quietly watched. Alone, I walk, trace, sing, and chant, and everything tends to be ordinary.

The silent night, the cold moonlight, and the drifting starlight are all silent at this moment. The autumn wind is rustling, and the green has all faded away. The fallen leaves have piled up, leaving behind a profound silence. It can be said that "the bleak autumn wind turns the grass yellow, the bright autumn lamp makes the autumn night long, already feeling that the autumn window cannot contain all of autumn, how much more so when the wind and rain add to the desolation."

After the silence, the world, the melody of the autumn wind's dance, the silent lament of withered grass, the sad浅singing of falling flowers, and the affection of leaves embracing the earth are like one after another small serenade, seeping into the depths of the soul with either bitter or warm rhythms flowing for a long time in the heart. Everything from the past appears extremely insignificant in this long night.

"Having experienced the storms of life, having seen the truth and falsehood of emotions, having walked through confusion and helplessness... no longer deceived by false appearances, calmly facing everything. Not fond of boasting, not indulging in those fleeting romances, quietly apprecising every landscape in life, carefully savoring every touch of emotion." Far away from the secular world, discarding illusions, finding joy in wandering through music and literature...

Heavenly sounds, beautiful realms, are enough to let me linger in them for a long time. Especially the melancholy and touching music, though it may overflow the sadness and sorrow in my heart, I still can't suppress such affection. Wandering through words is also an addiction. Knowing well that the low chanting of words will disturb the mood, but still obstinately walking in the world of words, letting thoughts melt in music, and allowing moods to find an outlet in words.

"Being used to being alone, liking quiet reflection, silently chewing on the melancholy in my heart, reducing the heaviness in life, discarding the troubles in daily life, having less self-pity, and more magnanimity and openness." Walking into nature, listening to the voice of tranquility, experiencing the wonders of the world, interpreting the grandeur of life, and feeling the infinite fun given by life, "picking chrysanthemums under the eastern fence, leisurely seeing the southern mountain."

"Small happiness is right by your side, easy satisfaction is paradise." Tranquil watch, living a simple and peaceful life...
