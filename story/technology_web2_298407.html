eobd Auto C168 scanner

by eobdii553 on 2009-12-07 22:16:51

The Auto C168 scanner is the first wireless automotive fault diagnostic scanner in China. The C168 can be installed on most devices that have a Windows operating system, and it greatly enhances the compatibility between vehicle inspection and other automobile maintenance equipment. At the same time, using wireless technology, a technician in their office can directly examine a broken-down vehicle parked in the repair shop, effectively solving problems caused by space and distance. The C168, with its powerful functionality and practical technology, has garnered much attention and praise both domestically and internationally.

**Technical Parameters:**

- Chipset: ATMEL Chip

- Operating System: Windows

- Dimensions of COMBOX: 12*8*2.5CM

- Dimensions of RF box: 6*5*2.5CM

- Testing Port 1: Bluetooth Wireless

- Testing Port 2: Diagnostic Cable

- Power Supply for COMBOX: +12V

- Power Supply for RF box: USB

- Mainboard: Anti-electromagnetic industrial control motherboard

- Communication Mode: COM port and Bluetooth Wireless

**Features:**

- Easy to use, compact size, excellent performance, quick response

- Uses Bluetooth wireless technology and is based on the Windows operating system

- Full-range coverage of vehicle types including Mercedes Benz, BMW, VW/AUDI, Seat, Skoda, VAG COM, Landrover, Citroen, Renault, Fiat, Volvo, Peugeot, Opel, Chrysler, Ford, GM, OBDII/EOBDII CAN/OBD injection system, Toyota/Lexus, Honda, Mitsubishi, Nissan, Mazda, Suzuki, Daihatsu, Hyundai, Daewoo, Kia

- Equipped with CAN adapter to test auto models with CANBUS

- Displays data stream in waveform

- Performs functions such as reading DTC code, erasing DTC code, reading data stream tests on engines, AT, ABS, airbags, and other functions

- Internet online update, users can update software for individual vehicle models of their own choice via the internet

- Maintenance records management

- Standard USB port
