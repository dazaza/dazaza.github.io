Lijiang Grand Waterfall Hotel

by wszdw142 on 2008-05-12 19:29:16

The hotel features:

● The most eye-catching feature is a large artificial waterfall with a width of 72 meters at the top and 75 meters at the bottom, and a drop height of 45 meters. The waterfall flows down from the roof of the 12th floor of the main building (North Tower) of the hotel, which is the largest artificial waterfall in the world so far, and has received the certificate of the "Guinness World Records". During the day, the waterfall refracts tens of thousands of rainbow-like rays under the sunlight. From afar, it looks like the Milky Way falling from the sky, and up close, its overwhelming momentum is awe-inspiring. As night falls, under the illumination of silver-white spotlights, the glass cornice appears to be scattering pearls, crystal-clear droplets jump and fly on the diving board. The second-floor pool is shrouded in mist, resembling a fairyland. Visitors can't help but stop to admire it, reluctant to leave.

● 646 luxury guest rooms, decorated with great care, exuding luxury and grandeur. Among them are one presidential suite, 22 luxury VIP suites, 20 luxury VIP rooms, 62 business rooms, and 541 standard rooms. All rooms are fully equipped with facilities such as central air conditioning, international direct-dial telephones, bathroom TV and radio systems, wired and wireless broadband access and outlets, satellite closed-circuit TVs, etc. The hotel's environment is elegant, with unique scenery. Guests can enjoy the beautiful riverside views, look out at distant mountains, or overlook the lobby, enjoying the views while experiencing our attentive and considerate services.

● The hotel gathers an abundance of world delicacies, featuring Chinese, Western, Japanese, and other cuisines, with a total of 1800 seats. The "Silk Road on Water" Western restaurant and the "Sakura" Japanese restaurant offer unique exotic flavors that satisfy your appetite. The classical and elegant "Gui Shui Ge" Chinese restaurant, along with the "Li River Pavilion" which features 20 luxurious private dining rooms and is managed by renowned chefs from Guangdong, serves exquisite Cantonese cuisine and various delicious dishes. The "Wu Yue Chuan Xiang Guan" allows you to taste authentic Jiangsu-Zhejiang, Huaiyang, Sichuan, and Hunan regional cuisines right here in Guilin, making it an ideal place for business meetings and gatherings with friends.

● Modern conference facilities make it an ideal venue for hosting large-scale international conferences and various banquets and cocktail parties. The grand and magnificent Galaxy Conference Hall can accommodate 400 people for dining and 500 people for meetings. It is equipped with a six-channel simultaneous interpretation system, a 200-square-meter small stage, and a lift-up screen, with complete facilities and perfect audio-visual equipment. In addition, there are five different types of meeting rooms available for use by meetings of different sizes. A professional banquet team provides attentive and considerate service to ensure the success of your conference.

● Offers a large indoor constant-temperature swimming pool, hot and cold water massage bath, fully functional sauna room, SPA, beauty salon center, aerobics room, and a leisure area for hosting small cocktail parties, forming a large VIP fitness center integrating relaxation and fitness, covering an area of more than 2000 square meters. Fully equipped, high-end and luxurious, all fitness equipment uses the American well-known brand "Life Fitness", with professional fitness coaches providing you with full guidance throughout. Let you relieve the fatigue of the day, relax your mind and body, and enjoy a green and leisurely atmosphere.
