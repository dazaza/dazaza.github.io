6 Ways to Promote Your Patent for Free

by keyizhang1 on 2012-02-23 16:27:57

Patents face issues such as difficulty in invention and even greater difficulty in promotion, which leads to many personal patents being left unused at home. Moreover, due to the lack of effective patent agencies and self-protection awareness for patents, a significant number of individuals encounter the pain of deception by illegal elements during the promotion process. Personal inventions face the challenge of not finding an effective "platform," causing an intangible disconnection between patents and the market. As a result, a large number of practical patent products fail to find their rightful place in the market promptly.

This article addresses the issues of promotion and platforms, focusing on cost-free, extensive promotion, and proposes six effective free promotion pathways.

1. Choose a free promotion platform as a support system; here, we recommend Ke Yi Network www.1633.com [http://www.1633.com/], where you can freely post patent information. Relying on professional transfer websites not only provides inventors with a base but also brings in many clients specifically looking for inventions. Since this serves as your base, it is essential to ensure that the patent description is detailed and precise, avoiding exaggeration.

2. Utilize popular online sharing functions to share invention information with friends. Taking Ke Yi Network as an example, after successfully publishing a patent, there is a [Share To] function above the achievement introduction. Inventors can click to share, and the more places you share, the better. The more places you share, the more people will see and pay attention to your patent.

3. Use blogs for promotion. It is reported that many inventors have blogs, and posting some invention information on them is good but not the best way to utilize them. Unless the blog is followed by a large number of people, I still suggest using a patent promotion platform for publicity. Leveraging a strong platform makes things easier! You can use your own blog to promote the information on the patent transfer platform, making the information more convincing and facilitating the transfer due to the platform's characteristics.

4. Email promotion. Nowadays, obtaining emails from certain types of companies is not difficult. You can gather target transfer customer emails through searches and send emails using your email account. The language should be sincere and attractive, and include a link to your technology on the patent promotion platform. This allows interested parties to check it out without disturbing those who are not interested.

5. Leverage powerful social networking platforms for promotion. Free social platforms include QQ, Sina Weibo, Tencent Weibo, NetEase Weibo, Sohu Weibo, NetEase Blog, QQ Space, Sohu Blog, Renren Network, Alibaba Blog, Hexun Blog, etc., where you can post information for free.

6. Utilize free information release platforms. Information release platforms include 58同城 and Ganji.com. Inventors can post their patents on these platforms, gaining some attention.

Finally, a reminder: transferring patents is a long and tedious process. We hope inventors remain patient, trying various methods courageously.
