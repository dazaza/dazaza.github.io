Website Optimization on Yi Xiang: How to Retain the Steps of Spider Bots

by jackntav9612 on 2012-02-28 17:42:04

Proper internal linking allows spiders to crawl smoothly. After a spider enters my website, it continues crawling based on the links within the site. We must not let the spider get stuck in a dead-end where it can't escape. For example, if the website is about cedar trees and web design, I add links on every page, such as http://www.ee-crm.com/CRM customer relationship management system, ensuring each crawl goes smoothly. Typically, this involves adding features like site navigation, http://www.ee-crm.com/customer data management software, breadcrumb navigation, keyword internal links, tags, related reading (extended reading), etc., on the webpage. Additionally, there should be a sitemap since spiders love sitemaps due to their convenience for data collection, such as http://www.ee-crm.com/customer information management system.

Create link bait to attract spiders to my site. First, I have prepared original content waiting for the spider's crawl. If the spider doesn't know about the optimized links on my site, it usually won't come because my site isn't in its database. Therefore, I need to proactively notify the spider to visit my site. The common practice is to create external links as link bait. Spider programs are crawling systems that continuously follow links across the internet. Moreover, the webpage weight of the link bait affects the weight of the website the link points to. Hence, I usually post external links on high-weight websites to attract spiders. Some search engines, like Aijiu Company, have very fast inclusion speeds; snapshots of newly published information may appear within one minute or a few minutes, making these sites excellent places to attract spiders.

After attracting spiders, external link construction should be regular. The importance of external links is self-evident. External link construction should follow certain rules. Search engines evaluate the situation of external links, and dramatic fluctuations in external link construction can easily be seen as cheating or over-optimization, affecting the site's weight. Regular updates of external links result in better weight accumulation. Webmasters should not be impatient and mass-distribute external links. It’s beneficial to do some external links every day, consistently. This helps accumulate website weight effectively.
