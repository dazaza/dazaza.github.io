The police station chief was hacked by many people in front of the police station and got injured by 8 cuts

by zxyasda20 on 2011-07-28 14:48:53

E Jing News (reporter Ji Dong) - At 12th night, Captain Wang Wei of the Xiàlù traffic police team under Huángshí City's Traffic Police Detachment was attacked and slashed by several people in front of a police station. He suffered multiple stab wounds. After undergoing surgery, he regained consciousness only by yesterday morning.

Yesterday, I saw Wang Wei in the hospital ward. His head was heavily bandaged. Although conscious, he still appeared weak. His son, Wang Cheng, introduced that at 11 PM the previous evening, his father received a call from Manager Yi of the No. 7 Company of China Metallurgical Group Corporation Fifteenth Metallurgical Construction Company. Manager Yi reported that a worker at an old Xiàlù construction site had been hit and dragged by a driver, requesting Captain Wang’s assistance to mediate the situation. After calling 110, Wang, dressed in civilian clothes, went with Manager Yi to Luóqiáo Police Station. Wang recalled that as their taxi just arrived at the police station entrance, they noticed multiple cars driving towards them. Feeling something amiss, they rang the doorbell but no one answered. The two then ran towards the Xiàlù District direction. Within less than two minutes, a white minivan caught up with them. More than ten men armed with machetes jumped out of the van and started slashing at Manager Yi. Wang rushed to rescue him but was also brutally chased and slashed. Upon hearing the commotion, the police officers rushed out, causing the attackers to disperse.

It was reported that Wang Wei suffered eight stab wounds, three on his head which were deep enough to expose the bone, a large piece of flesh cut off from his left arm, severed tendons, fractured arm bones, and a knife wound on the back of his neck, fortunately missing the major arteries. Wang Wei is currently still under observation. Another injured person, Yi, suffered multiple stab wounds and remained unconscious as of the time this article was published.

Yesterday, another injured person, Liu GuiLin, told reporters that on the evening of the incident, he and his co-workers were working on road construction at the old Xiàlù water plant. Since the road was due to be paved the next day, the local traffic police had closed the nearby section of the road that evening. Around 9:20 PM, while the workers were clearing materials, a tow truck coming from the new Xiàlù direction attempted to forcibly pass through the construction area. The workers explained that forcing through would damage the materials and requested the driver wait until the materials were moved. However, ignoring Liu standing in front of the vehicle, the driver forcibly drove forward, pushing Liu several meters before Liu desperately jumped onto the front bumper to avoid falling off. He shouted for the driver to stop, but the driver ignored him and drove the tow truck all the way to the Huangshi Jinshan Development Zone before circling back to old Xiàlù, finally stopping in front of a pharmaceutical company in the Chengbei Development Zone of Daye City. This entire drive lasted about half an hour, covering approximately 20 kilometers.

Liu Guilin said that after the car stopped, the driver took out a stick and beat him to the ground. Later, six of his coworkers arrived by car, leading to another physical conflict between the two parties. Officers from Luóqiáo Police Station arrived and brought both sides to the station. After the opposing driver made a phone call, nearly 30-40 people arrived in close to 10 cars at the police station. Seeing the large number of people, the workers called Manager Yi for help. By around 11 PM, they learned that both Manager Yi and Traffic Policeman Wang Wei had been slashed.

According to reports, the injured traffic police captain, Wang Wei, is 48 years old. In 2005, while returning home late at night after work, he witnessed a thug attacking and stabbing a citizen. Wang stepped forward to intercept the thug and was stabbed twice. Over the course of his many years as a police officer, Wang has received numerous commendations.

Yesterday, I learned from the Daye Public Security Bureau that the bureau has formed a special task force to fully investigate this case. Due to the ongoing investigation, some details have not yet been disclosed.
