Luo Yonghao Reveals Hammer Phone to Be Released in Spring Next Year

August 30th News - Luo Yonghao, founder of Hammer Technology, seemingly disclosed during an interaction on Weibo with Li Yi, the Executive Deputy Chairman and Secretary-General of China Mobile Internet Industry Alliance, that the Hammer phone will be released in spring next year.

In the evening of August 29th, Li Yi informed Luo Yonghao via Weibo about a journalist wanting to conduct an interview. Luo Yonghao declined the interview, stating "I won't accept any media interviews before we release the phone hardware next spring." This statement appeared to be intentional, possibly as a way to mitigate the appearance of "deliberate leaks." Luo Yonghao also used his typical self-deprecating manner, saying "before the product is out, all the basic hype has already been done," indicating that he had previously hinted at the release time of the Hammer phone while interacting with netizens, though many did not believe him.

After Luo Yonghao once again revealed the release time of the Hammer phone, netizens still remained skeptical about his claims. Many believed that the release of the Hammer phone might be delayed. A netizen named "Jeridge" commented, "I don’t believe you'll have the phone hardware ready by next spring, even if I were beaten." Another concern from netizens was about the sales strategy after the phone's launch. A netizen named "Selling Senya's Xiao Shi" expressed worry, "Looking forward to the release of the Hammer phone next year, but hoping they won't do the same 'hunger marketing' tactics as Xiaomi."

On November 14, 2012, Luo Yonghao had revealed on Weibo that the release of the Hammer phone ROM would be postponed until the spring of 2013. The delay led to doubts about Luo and the Hammer phone from outside observers. On March 27, 2013, exactly one year after Luo Yonghao decided to make a phone, he and his company, Hammer Technology, officially launched the Smartisan OS operating system at the National Convention Center. On June 15, 2013, the official website for the Hammer ROM Smartisan OS opened for downloads, but it was only available for international version Samsung i9300 devices.
