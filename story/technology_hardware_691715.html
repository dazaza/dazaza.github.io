Top 10 advantages of studying Finance in Quebec, Canada?

by dingyi947279 on 2012-03-05 17:23:31

Students majoring in finance in China can choose to study at the University of Quebec in Canada if they want to study abroad. The cost is low, only 70,000-80,000 yuan RMB per year. The financial services industry is one of the important pillars of the economy of Quebec, Canada. The financial industry in Quebec directly employs 550,000 employees and pays a weekly salary of 420 million Canadian dollars. The University of Quebec offers a finance program that plays an irreplaceable role in our daily work and life. From banks, fund companies, insurance companies, securities companies to energy companies, the finance major is everywhere. No matter how many people choose this major, there has always been a shortage of talent in the financial sector in China, which is known as the "golden rice bowl" major. Therefore, studying finance at the University of Quebec in Canada is indeed a good choice for students going abroad.

The province of Quebec has a beautiful environment and comprehensive social security. There are fewer Chinese international students, and the strong academic atmosphere and pure foreign language environment can quickly improve language skills. Multiple campuses offer joint courses, with more than 85,000 students on campus and 5,500 professors, allowing each student to find their ideal professor and receive more help from them.

There are 550 majors closely linked to employment, providing better job prospects for students entering society. The cost of study is low, requiring only 70,000-80,000 yuan RMB per year to achieve your dream of studying abroad. Courses are taught in both English and French, enhancing future employment competitiveness. Some majors provide paid internship opportunities, ensuring good employment prospects. Many majors offer government scholarships, reducing your financial burden. Immigration policies are relaxed and complete, making it easy to change status after finishing studies. The Ministry of Education has signed a cooperation agreement with the province of Quebec in Canada to mutually recognize academic qualifications and degrees. The school has complete teaching facilities and advanced equipment. The campus is beautiful, transportation is developed, providing convenience for your first arrival.
