I have always admired people who love reading and those who love reading good books. They are like mirrors that can reflect my flaws and correct my life attitude and way of dealing with things. Whenever I meet someone who loves reading or a literature enthusiast who likes writing, I will talk more. Ten years ago, drinking a cup of tea or coffee at night, sitting under the desk lamp, reading books or writing, were all pleasant common occurrences. Now, with the gradual increase in age, staying up late has become a luxury. Occasionally, after one or two nights, I would get a headache the next day. Therefore, more often than not, I work or read quietly in the early morning. Although I often miss the tranquility and warmth of the night, the freshness and anticipation of the early morning is also a happy enjoyment. I sometimes admire my own perseverance. For so many years, I have experienced many storms and difficulties, but I have never given up on reading and writing. I have persisted until now, and it is still my beloved passion. I feel very happy! If a person spends their entire life persisting in doing one thing, I believe they will succeed. Unfortunately, there are fewer and fewer people who persist in doing one thing.
