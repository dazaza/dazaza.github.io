Starting from as low as 699 yuan, a buying guide for mainstream size LCD monitors.

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-04 10:23:29

In July, I introduced some of the more highly-anticipated monitors, a total of six models. Today, I will introduce another six products to everyone. The sizes range from 19 inches to 23 inches and include features such as CCFL backlighting, LED backlighting, TN, and wide viewing angles.

The focus of this issue's recommended products is on low-cost IPS and VA monitors, with an additional mention of the popular entry-level office monitor - the 19-inch. Let’s take a look right away.

Product Recommendation: ASUS ML239H-W

Reference Price: 1499-1799 RMB

Warranty Service: 3-year free warranty

Summary of Pros and Cons:

**Pros:**

1. Stylish ultra-thin appearance with a dual-ring base showcasing craftsmanship design

2. E-IPS wide-angle panel

3. Excellent power consumption performance

4. 3-year full warranty

**Cons:**

1. Prone to leaving fingerprints

2. Color temperature tends to be warm

ASUS ML239H-W

The ASUS ML239H-W continues the aesthetic appearance design of the ML series, featuring a glossy piano black finish that looks seamless overall. A narrow bezel around the edges paired with a broad lower frame makes the design thinner, giving the impression of a larger screen. The 23-inch screen uses an IPS ultra-wide angle panel, ensuring that even full HD does not result in excessively small pixel pitch.

ASUS ML239H-W

The monitor body doesn't have many labels. The ASUS ML239H-W supports HDMI input expansion and has Energy Star certification. The buttons are designed as touch-sensitive ones located at the bottom right corner, with a light-up prompt when touched.

ASUS ML239H-W

The back adopts a white piano gloss finish, combining the alluring high-gloss piano black finish with a pure white minimalist back, exuding elegance and fashion, making the ML239H-W a focal point in any living space.

ASUS ML239H-W

It employs an LED backlight method, where the internal heat generation of the monitor is minimal. The frame is filled with tiny heat dissipation holes, and in my opinion, this honeycomb-style heat dissipation hole design adds another level of fashionable appearance experience.

ASUS ML239H-W

In terms of interfaces, in addition to the mainstream VGA, the ML239H-W is equipped with an HDMI full HD 1080p interface, which can connect to high-definition entertainment devices such as PS3 and Xbox360. There is also a headphone output jack for transmitting HDMI audio signals.

ASUS ML239H-W

The adjustable angle of the monitor is approximately 35 degrees. For products using IPS wide viewing angles, the adjustable angle function isn't very significant. From the side view, the monitor still appears extremely thin.

ASUS ML239H-W

According to my measurements, the thinnest part of the monitor reaches 18.04 millimeters, and the overall thickness of the monitor is relatively uniform, meeting the market's pursuit of slim monitors.

**Specifications:**

- Brand: ASUS

- Model: ML239H-W

- Size: 23 inches

- Dot Pitch: 0.266mm

- Brightness: 250cd/m²

- Typical Contrast Ratio: 1000:1

- Dynamic Contrast Ratio: 50 million:1

- Resolution: 1920x1080

- Response Time: 5ms

- Screen Ratio: 16:9

- Panel Type: E-IPS

- Horizontal Viewing Angle: 178 degrees

- Vertical Viewing Angle: 178 degrees

- Interface Types: 15-pin D-Sub, HDMI, audio input

- IT Mall: Online purchase actual price

Review: Overall, this 23-inch E-IPS monitor is aimed at mainstream users, and its overall performance is quite good. Priced at 1799 RMB, it is a decent deal considering ASUS provides a 3-year warranty. Note: Based on feedback from some netizens, you can get it for around 1400 RMB either in-store or on Taobao. It might be worth trying.
