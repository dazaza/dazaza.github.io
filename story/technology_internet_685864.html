Learning makeup is a profound course, and it's impossible to master all the makeup techniques without attending relevant courses in Suzhou or seeking advice from others. For instance, by joining Suzhou makeup training, you can skillfully adjust foundations for different skin tones, making the makeup appear more natural. For example, for those with yellowish and darker skin tones, it's essential to purchase high-quality foundation and primer! Also, remember to touch up your makeup. The teachers at Suzhou makeup training schools are top-notch lecturers, like those at the Suqian Beauty and Hairdressing School [http://www.sqainisi.org]. Exchanging and learning with them will greatly advance your makeup knowledge.

Below, I'll simply teach you some steps. You need to learn and practice more to make your makeup look beautiful~

First is the primer. Primer smooths out pores, brightens the skin tone, and is the foundation of makeup.

Touch-up tips: Choose a concealer pen or cream that is slightly darker than your skin tone to cover any blemishes; if there are none, skip this step, hehe.

Next, you can choose a foundation. For darker skin, go for purple tones; white skin goes with white tones; yellowish skin should use green tones.

Then comes eyebrow drawing. Nowadays, brow powder is used more frequently.

Eyebrow color should be slightly lighter than hair color, avoid the situation where someone has yellow hair but black eyebrows.

Mascara can be chosen based on personal conditions, either lengthening or thickening, or both. For daily makeup, you can also choose natural false eyelashes.

You can appropriately draw eyeliner. Eyeliner should be applied before mascara according to Suqian Beauty and Hairdying Training [http://www.sqainisi.org].

Lipstick and eyeshadow can be chosen based on personal conditions and desired makeup effects.

Finally, lightly brush some blush and setting powder to make the makeup last longer.
