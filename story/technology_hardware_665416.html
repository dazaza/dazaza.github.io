Prospects for the Development Trend of Industrial Control Software during the "12th Five-Year Plan"

Introduction

2011 is the opening year and also a challenging year of China's "12th Five-Year Plan". The software and information technology service industry is a fundamental, leading, and strategic industry that promotes national economic and social development. Strengthening and expanding the software and information technology service industry is crucial for promoting the integration of informatization and industrialization in our country and enhancing national competitiveness. Currently, the software market is undergoing a major transformation, and the industrial automation software industry is no exception. Whether it is the emerging markets brought by strategic emerging industries such as the Internet of Things, cloud computing, e-commerce, convergence of three networks, high-end equipment manufacturing, or the domestic and international demand stimulation and national economic transformation, the software industry is experiencing significant tests.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council attach great importance to the development of the software and information technology service industry. In 2000, several policies were introduced to encourage the development of the software industry and integrated circuit industry, marking the beginning of the golden decade for the development of China's software industry. Over the past ten years, the innovation capability of China's software industry has continuously improved, and its application level and service capabilities have significantly enhanced. In 2010, the business revenue of the software industry reached 1.3364 trillion yuan, increasing from 6% in 2001 to 18% of the electronics information industry. New technologies, new forms of business, and new models have emerged in recent years, with trends towards networking, servicing, platformization, intelligence, and integration becoming increasingly evident, making it an increasingly important battlefield for global technological competition. In the past decade, China's software industry has maintained an average annual growth rate of 38%, referred to by the industry as the "golden decade" for software industry development. In the new period, the state has set goals for the software industry during the "12th Five-Year Plan", aiming for an average annual growth rate of over 20% in software service revenue by 2015, exceeding 20% of the electronics information industry income, and cultivating more than 10 enterprises with annual revenues exceeding 10 billion yuan. It is estimated that by the end of the "12th Five-Year Plan", the scale of China's software service industry will exceed 1 trillion yuan. Within the "12th Five-Year Plan" plan, the software service industry, as part of the "new generation of information technology", is an important link in aligning industry with the national high-tech industry. According to available information, the state’s preferential policies for the software industry will continue, and the new No. 18 document has already been submitted; the new policy is expected to provide key support in细分 fields.

The software industry is a knowledge-based production strategic industry that is pollution-free, low-energy-consuming, and highly employment-generating, being the core and soul of the information industry. Under the high regard and strong support of the state, China's software industry has developed rapidly, becoming a foundational, strategic, and leading industry in the national economy, playing a significant role in promoting the integration of informatization and industrialization and fostering new economic growth points. Guided by the National Economic and Social Development Twelfth Five-Year Plan Outline (hereinafter referred to as the "Plan Outline"), various industries are formulating their own development plans around their respective developments. It is reported that the "12th Five-Year Plan" development plan for the industrial automation software industry is currently being formulated.

This article discusses how the author believes industrial automation software should develop during the "12th Five-Year Plan", based on personal understanding of the trend of "demand-driven, technology-led" in industrial automation technology, proposing major issues that the current development of industrial automation software should focus on, for reference by industrial automation software companies.

1. Under the national backdrop of "integration of informatization and industrialization," the fusion of industry and software will undoubtedly become the soul of modern industrial development.

On January 12, 2011, Premier Wen Jiabao presided over a State Council executive meeting, where in the "Research Deployment Further Encouraging Software Industry Policy Measures," the integration of informatization and industrialization was still considered the driving force for industrial transformation and upgrading during the "12th Five-Year Plan." Experts believe that industrial software is the starting point, breakthrough, and important handle for the integration of informatization and industrialization, which plays a significant role in advancing industrial structure adjustment and upgrading, maintaining stable and rapid economic development. The essence of the integration of informatization and industrialization is industrial informatization, achieving the goal of building an information-based industry through the process of industrialization, with the information-based industry serving as the foundation of the information economy. The key to this integration lies in the demand traction of industrialization and the technical drive of informatization. New-type industrialization is the mutual fusion and common development of informatization and industrialization. The integration of informatization and industrialization refers to the gradual use of information and information technology in the industrialization process, permeating into relevant elements of the industrial field (industrial technology, industrial equipment, industrial links, industrial products), forming new types of industrial equipment, enhancing the quality and capability of industry.

Under the backdrop of national industrialization and informatization integration, in enterprise management, marketing, industrial design, production, safety, equipment, and products, the "soft power" formed by the integration of industry and software undoubtedly becomes the soul of modern industrial development in aspects such as optimized design, optimized scheduling, optimized processes, increased efficiency, reduced costs, energy conservation, emission reduction, and enhanced vitality. On June 28, 2009, Premier Wen Jiabao pointed out during his inspection of the software industry in Jinan: "From manufacturing to creation, software is the bridge!" highly evaluating the important role of industrial software in China's modern industry.

Currently, the integration of information technology and various industries is deepening, with industrial software playing an increasingly core and critical role in the transformation and upgrading of traditional industries such as major equipment, automobiles, steel, petrochemicals, smart grids, and civil aviation. Industrial software promotes the mechanization, electrification, and automation of equipment manufacturing, characterized by digitization, networking, and intelligence, becoming a key link in advancing the integration of informatization and industrialization. It plays an indispensable role and exerts irreplaceable key functions in the integration process. In recent years, China's industry has developed rapidly, becoming a globally influential manufacturing powerhouse. However, the problem of being large but not strong remains prominent. As the adhesive and engine of China's industry and informatization, industrial software is undoubtedly an important support for realizing the strategy of transforming from "Made in China" to "Created in China" and completing structural adjustments and upgrades. For China's industry, the need to upgrade traditional industries using information technology has become very urgent. Moreover, as the adhesive for the deep integration of industrialization and informatization, with the continuous growth and expansion of the industry, China's industrial software also has more development opportunities.

On June 10, 2009, the Chinese Academy of Sciences released the "Innovation 2050: Scientific Revolution and China's Future" series of reports, pointing out that the world today is at the eve of scientific and technological innovation breakthroughs and a new scientific and technological revolution. In the next 10 to 20 years, there is likely to be a new scientific and technological revolution and industrial revolution characterized by green, intelligent, and sustainable features. To achieve the strategic task of fully realizing a moderately prosperous society and modernization, China must prepare early in the face of possible new scientific and technological revolutions. The roadmap in the report also proposed the necessity of solving 22 strategic scientific problems affecting China's modernization process. Among these, six strategic scientific problems in the information field were highlighted: research and experimental network construction of new principles/technologies for "post-IP" networks, green preparation of high-quality basic raw materials, process engineering for efficient and clean recycling of resources, molecular design of agricultural plants and animals, ubiquitous perception information manufacturing systems, and E-level (10^18) supercomputing technology. Breakthroughs in these technologies will have a significant impact on China's informatization process.

On May 12, 2011, Chen Wei, Director of the Software Services Department of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, stated at the "15th China International Software Expo Forum" that China's software industry is still an immature industry, and during the "12th Five-Year Plan," five tasks would be promoted to make the software industry bigger and stronger. Chen Wei said that during the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, China's software and information technology services achieved leapfrog development, but overall, China's software and information technology services are still an immature industry with significant gaps compared to developed countries. During the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, which is a critical time for China's industrial transformation and upgrading, deep integration of informatization and industrialization, and transitioning from traditional industrialization to new-type industrialization, we will focus on promoting the compilation and implementation of the "12th Five-Year Plan," improving the core competitiveness of the industry, enhancing innovation capabilities, cultivating sustainable development capabilities, and perfecting industry norms among five key tasks.

Regarding the hotspots of the software industry in 2011, industry insiders believe that the development of the software industry will follow several keywords—service, green, intelligence, application, and high-end. It is reported that the "Ninth China Soft Fair" to be held in Dalian from June 16 to 19, 2011, will focus on "Green IT, Intelligent Application."

2. The Soul and Core of Software Automation Systems

In the process of China's equipment manufacturing development, high-end manufacturing necessarily requires the assistance and reinforcement of informatization. Industrial software plays an increasingly important role as the core control part of the equipment manufacturing industry. Its widespread application has greatly improved the level of production process control and product quality. With the expansion of production scale, intensifying competition, and higher environmental protection requirements, automation software has become a key factor in comprehensively enhancing the core competitiveness of enterprises.

As pointed out by Zhou Hongren, Standing Deputy Chairman of the National Informatization Expert Advisory Committee: "Industrial software is the integrating agent for informatization and industrialization, and also the embodiment of soft advantages. Without industrial software, it is impossible to form the soft advantage of informatization based on the hard advantage of industrialization, and there would be no integration of informatization and industrialization." Wu Cheng, Chairman of the Expert Committee of the China Industrial Software Industry Development Alliance and Academician of the Chinese Academy of Engineering, stated in an interview: "Nowadays, all modern equipment requires the use of industrial software, which is the core technology for enterprises to utilize information." The development of industrial software needs to balance high, medium, and low ends. Currently, the main difficulty in the development of China's industrial software lies in the high-end field.

Industrial automation systems cannot function without the support of software. Therefore, people believe: "Software is the soul and core of automation systems!" Whether it is system operation optimization, energy saving and consumption reduction in factories, improvement of product quality, or even corporate core competitiveness, all are closely related to software. Since the 1990s, IBM has taken actions such as acquisitions and selling its PC business to Lenovo, indicating that the era of hardware brilliance has become history, and the era of software climax has arrived. In the field of industrial control, the trend towards hardware-software integration is evident. For example, the emergence of embedded soft PLCs. The competitiveness of industrial automation enterprises is increasingly reflected in the competition level of software application and development!

3. The Wise Choice for the Leapfrog Development of China's Automation Industry - Innovation and Cooperation

Based on the internal and external constraints faced by China's industrialization, during the "12th Five-Year Plan," continuing to follow the path of new-type industrialization with Chinese characteristics and achieving the transformation from an industrial power to an industrial powerhouse is the fundamental task of this stage. To achieve this, efforts need to be made in enterprise innovation capabilities, industrial upgrading, institutional innovation, and international cooperation. Innovation (especially original innovation) is the key and decisive factor for China to truly "later surpass earlier." Cooperation, on the other hand, allows for "strength complementation and mutual benefit," making the development of China's automation industry more "vast seas for fish to leap." Because only through innovative cooperation can China's manufacturing enhance its advantageous position in the new round of international competition. Using innovation and cooperation can promote the leapfrog development and transformation of China's automation industry.

The development of industrial control software is also an important aspect of the development of automation technology. However, in some product areas, domestic software is at a disadvantage when competing with foreign software. Of course, domestic software technology is improving rapidly, government procurement is providing substantial support to domestic software, and the competitiveness of domestic software is continuously increasing. Due to the difficulty for newly emerging domestic software to compete equally with foreign software in terms of technical strength and application accumulation within a short time, specializing through division of labor, concentrating investment to strengthen, and then cooperating across product areas to form competitive synergy is the best competitive development strategy. Currently, new technologies and concepts are emerging one after another, and standards battles have begun in areas such as the convergence of three networks, the Internet of Things, and cloud computing. China's software enterprises must fully recognize and actively pay attention to the opportunities and challenges brought by new technologies, new models, new forms of business, and new concepts to the development of China's software industry. They should actively follow up in standard formulation and application implementation, seize the good opportunities contained therein, strengthen independent innovation, narrow the gap with world-renowned enterprises, enhance enterprise strength, and consolidate and improve their status in fierce market competition. "In the area of high-end industrial software for the informatization of equipment manufacturing, China has a significant gap compared to world advanced levels, resulting in over 90% of previous equipment development adopting large foreign software. In such a situation, developing domestically owned intellectual property industrial software has become the key to upgrading China's industrial sector."

To do well in "innovation and cooperation," it is necessary to closely monitor the movements of international software companies. Since the 1990s, IBM has successively acquired a series of middleware vendors, making middleware the core of corporate IT architecture and gradually making people realize the importance and core status of software.

Later, IBM continued to acquire some well-known software companies, such as Lotus and DB2. Software began to progress alongside hardware, and in 2004, IBM further sold its PC business to Lenovo, signaling that the glorious era of hardware had become history, and the era of software prominence had arrived. In the field of industrial control, the trend toward hardware-software integration is evident. For example, the emergence of embedded soft PLCs.

Currently, the latest version CoDeSys V3.4 software promoted by Germany's 3S Software Company (a soft PLC based on the CoDeSys platform for embedded systems) advocates the concept of "reusable," "open, reconfigurable automation." This software relies on the IEC61131 development environment, supporting several automatic control industrial standards languages such as ladder diagrams, flowcharts, structure charts, and high-level ST language. Besides the benefits of software reuse, CoDeSys software also has the characteristic of reconfigurable manufacturing. Reconfigurable manufacturing is a process that guides and manages the reconfiguration of manufacturing systems. It enables manufacturing systems to effectively respond to constantly changing environments. Reconfigurability refers to the ability of hardware modules or (and) software modules in a system to reconfigure (or reset) the system structure and algorithms according to changing data flows or control flows. It possesses organizational reconfigurability, business process reconfigurability, product reconfigurability, workshop processing system reconfigurability, and reconfigurable information platforms.

In the field of innovation and cooperation, Germany's 3S Software Company consistently and sincerely cares about their customers and partners, adapting promptly to changes in the market environment and customizing the most competitive products and solutions for customers to help them accelerate technological progress, enhance core competitiveness, reduce production costs, and find breakthrough opportunities in crises.

At the "2011 Annual Open, Reconfigurable Automation Development Platform Technology Forum," Germany's 3S Company focused on launching redundant (Redundancy) and safety (Safety) products, both of which are fully integrated into the CoDeSys automation software development platform that complies with the IEC61131-3 international standard. As a leading unified application programming, engineering configuration, and project management development platform in the automation industry, CoDeSys is suitable for various automation tasks, allowing users to quickly, intuitively develop, debug, and manage automation projects based on it. Compared to traditional methods, it eliminates the need to spend a lot of time transplanting and integrating various software packages, significantly reducing development costs and accelerating product time-to-market. We believe that Germany's 3S Software Company will certainly continue to add vitality to China's industrial development with their products and technology during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period and further create brilliant achievements for Germany's 3S Company in the Chinese market!

4. The Development of Automation Development Platform Software Must Be Given Due Attention

As everyone knows, the development of industrial automation systems cannot proceed without the support of software, so the development of automation development platform software must be given due attention. The 15th China International Software Expo (hereafter referred to as the "Soft Expo") opened at the Beijing Exhibition Hall on May 12, 2011. Experts and scholars jointly explored new trends in software development. This conference, themed "Grasp Opportunities, Innovative Development, Become Stronger and Bigger, Serve the Deep Integration of Two Industries," revolved around multiple information points including the "12th Five-Year Plan," strategic emerging industries, State Council Document [2011] No. 4, integration of two industries, independent innovation, software, services, and solutions, cloud computing, the Internet of Things, and a low-carbon economy, conducting comprehensive discussions. The forum focused on new technologies, new forms of business, new models, and new trends affecting the development of the software and information technology service industry during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period. The series of forums organized by the "Soft Expo" revolved around the key work priorities for the development of China's software and information technology service industry in 2011, focusing on the industrial development policy environment, new technologies, new forms of business, new business models, industrial application promotion, software services, integration of two industries, independent intellectual property rights, software transactions, software quality, and software service outsourcing.

The life cycle of software products is very short, product updates and upgrades are frequent, and the replacement speed is fast.

To maintain continuous competitive capabilities, large foreign software companies place great emphasis on enterprise technological innovation. Currently, the overall R&D investment level of China's software companies and even the entire IT industry is relatively low, restricting the development of China's software industry's technological innovation capabilities. To achieve sustainable enterprise development, companies must significantly increase their R&D investment ratio; they must actively adopt mature and advanced technologies as development and application platforms, expand R&D scale, conduct forward-looking research on software technologies and industry business models; they must strictly enforce quality inspection and control systems in every link of software design, coding, testing, joint debugging, installation, trial operation, etc., to reduce or avoid the generation of software errors or defects; additionally, they must further strengthen research on national policies and industry policies to enhance the targeting of new product R&D; they must also strengthen research on emerging technologies and seize new market opportunities.
