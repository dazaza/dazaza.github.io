Introduction to the plot of Naruto Episode 338:

This is a world of ninjas. In Naruto's world, being a ninja, like the hunters in Hunter X Hunter and pirates in One Piece, is the dream of young people! However, this time the protagonist Naruto is different from Gon who is self-taught and Luffy; he graduated from the Ninja Academy of Konoha Leaf Village (even if it was barely). Although he was called "the eternal dead-last" and considered a "bad student," since childhood, the evil Nine-Tailed Fox has been sealed within him, and without parents, Naruto has been neglected by the villagers. He doesn't know the reason, but he tries hard with various pranks to attract everyone's attention, and instead, people distance themselves from him even more. Fortunately, there is Iruka-sensei who cares for him, which prevents Naruto's personality from becoming distorted. He is always full of energy, cheerful, and extremely optimistic. To gain recognition from more people, Naruto's goal is - to become the Hokage! This is quite a high goal, as Hokage is one of the five strongest ninja titles in the world and also the leader of the Konoha Leaf Village. Naruto doesn't care; he is super confident that he will become the Hokage someday. Go for it, Naruto! Naruto's companions are classmates assigned by the teacher. At first, they didn't get along, but through shared battles, while still having surface conflicts, they eventually became good partners who recognize and trust each other. Among them are the cool handsome Sasuke (who bears the mission of revenge) and the smart and cute girl Sakura.

Kakashi leads the seventh team composed of Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura on missions and training. Their first C-level mission actually faced Zabuza, one of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen. Then a mysterious boy named Haku appeared... Later, Naruto and others participated in the Chunin exams. During the second round, they met Orochimaru, who left a cursed seal on Sasuke. Before the third round exam, Naruto trained under Jiraiya and benefited greatly. During the third round exam, Orochimaru led the Sound and Sand ninjas to initiate the destruction of Konoha. Orochimaru summoned the First and Second Hokages to battle the Third Hokage, while Naruto met Gaara, the container of the One-Tail...

The Third Hokage made a glorious sacrifice, and Jiraiya went to find Tsunade, another of the Three Great Sannins, to serve as the Fifth Hokage. Naruto learned Rasengan, and ultimately, Tsunade, Jiraiya, and Orochimaru engaged in a fierce battle... Sasuke, in order to kill Itachi, turned to Orochimaru, and despite Naruto and others' efforts, they ultimately failed... Naruto trained under Jiraiya...

The three years of training passed quickly, and a brand-new Naruto returned to Konoha Village. The charming Sakura also learned formidable ninja techniques from her mentor Tsunade. But behind this joyous reunion, an ultra S-rank mission awaited them... In the Sand Hidden Village, Gaara had become the new Kazekage.
