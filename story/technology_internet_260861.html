Common Questions for Beginners in Online Earning

by 41cash on 2009-11-17 19:22:27

1) Is online earning real? Can you really make money on the internet? What type of online earning does your site focus on, and what are the reasons for making money?

Online earning is definitely real. Many webmasters spend their time and energy promoting and training others for free, which obviously has its benefits! The essence of making money online: traffic -> money. Simply put, it's about making money through advertisements. There are at least 1.5 billion internet users worldwide, with 137 million in China alone. Therefore, this industry still has a promising future and is being taken more seriously by more people. This site mainly focuses on foreign click-based earnings. The principle of making money is that you spend some time (staying on an ad for 30 seconds) clicking on advertisers' ads, and the website will pay us according to the number of ads we click.

2) How long have you been doing online earning, and how much do you earn from it per month now?

I started doing online earning in August 2009. At the time, I saw others receiving US dollars, even earning hundreds or even thousands of US dollars per month, so I decided to try it out. It didn't cost anything, and I had a bit of spare time. Initially, I relied on commissions. In the first month, I only received a commission of over 10 RMB, which was so little that no one would be satisfied. Failure always has a reason, and success always has a method. After summarizing my experiences from the past month, I started by doing clicks, which didn't take much time—usually half an hour a day. Then I moved on to registration-based earnings, which had slightly higher requirements. Later, I also tried emails, PTP, Lose, surveys, etc. After practicing all these, I gained some basic knowledge. I later realized that it's hard to make money on general projects by working alone. Looking at others who earn over a thousand US dollars per month, they usually have their own websites and teams, cooperating with each other to achieve mutual benefits. I decided to create my own website and promote it to find friends interested in online earning. Now, I earn around 500 RMB per month, which is still very little, so I need to continue working hard.
