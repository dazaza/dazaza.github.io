She calls me mom

She has tender cheeks

She has soft hair

She loves watching Snow White

And Cinderella too

In front of her

Everything is pure

Everything is kind

My little baby girl

She will grow up slowly

Growing eyes like mine

A nose like her father's

Blossoming into a beautiful young lady

Finding a partner and bringing them home

Then calling me mom again

One day, when I am sixty

My baby girl will support me

Sitting under the sun

Baby girl will massage my legs

Baby girl will pat my back

Baby girl will accompany me to sleep

My life

Because of Baby Girl
