"Expensive but not elegant" crystal wedding dress

by mywlai on 2012-02-10 13:45:44

According to incomplete data statistics, designers of high-end wedding dress customization in Shanghai say that every lunar August is a higher season for marriage, called the Marriage Festival. A wedding dress for women can only be worn once in a lifetime and it is for their beloved one. Precisely because it is so precious, women regard it as a beautiful dream. Let's imagine, stepping on the red carpet, being watched, envied, and blessed by thousands, you are the happiest and the most beautiful. So how to achieve perfection for this important formal wear in life? There is no denying that gorgeous, dazzling, noble but not vulgar wedding dresses are girls' favorite. What kind of wedding dress has these characteristics? First of all, let's talk about crystal wedding dresses. According to the introduction of Shanghai high-end wedding dress custom designer, the 2011 wedding dress design continued the previous seasons' preference for crystals and lace, especially the crystal clear crystals. Under the exquisite and luxurious wedding dress made of lace spliced with crystals, the bride adds a few more touches of luxurious nobility. Use crystals as the main decoration on the top, then splice with lace, the shiny wedding dress looks see-through, very sexy. The lower part of the wedding dress uses the currently popular British silk gauze style crystal wedding dress. The wedding dress is not long, exposing the ankles, presenting a fluffy and sweet feeling. Like in Singapore, brides like Swarovski crystal decorated wedding dresses. Currently, the crystals used to decorate wedding dresses are larger and more colorful in shape, at the same time, some bright gemstones are also put to use. In terms of other decorative elements of the wedding dress, the visually sweet and cute bows can embellish the bride's coquettishness. Some wedding dresses are sewn with pink bows, not only enriching the three-dimensional sense of the wedding dress, but also adding a touch of fresh elegance to the bride. Both flat waist and high waist wedding dresses are popular, luxurious and princess-like wedding dresses are timeless styles: Speaking of the cutting and style of wedding dresses, Singaporean women are generally small in stature and do not like to wear too loose wedding dresses, but prefer body-hugging designs, especially those that can show the waistline. In recent years, such wedding dresses have been very popular among Singaporean brides. Low-waist cuts were popular in wedding dresses before, but now wedding dresses tend to be flat-waist or short-waist designs. Generally speaking, flat-waist wedding dresses make the bride look more lively and cute, and add a touch of charm to the bride. In addition, the color of the wedding dress, this year's wedding dress colors are still bold. Some wedding dresses dare to use black and white two colors, some use brighter colors such as champagne color. Strong color contrast with traditional white wedding dresses, pure white wedding dresses are no longer so outstanding. From the trend, two-tone wedding dresses are a good choice, they are not too avant-garde but very fashionable, and the popularity index will last relatively longer. Shanghai high-end wedding dress customization needs to remind: when choosing a wedding dress, the bride must take into account her own figure while customizing her preferred style of wedding dress. For example, brides troubled by their waistline should not design the waist too complicated and intricate, and brides who are not tall should not push too much fabric onto themselves.
