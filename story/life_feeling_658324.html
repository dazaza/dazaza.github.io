Love you! Irrelevant to romance

When I got on the highway in Jinan, the raindrops outside the car window slid down the glass in strings. The desolation of deep winter, compared with the south, has more barrenness and sadness. The temperature dropped rapidly, and the thin clothes could not withstand the cold during the hurried journey. I missed the warm taste of Jiangnan, the bright scenery of green mountains and clear waters, and that still warm and intimate reminder. The rain kept falling all the way from Pingyi to Wulian. Along the way, the withered branches and fluttering fallen leaves of the big poplar trees made me feel extremely sorrowful. After a decade apart, returning to the old place, the streets that were once in my memory are now full of tall buildings. All that is left is just the memory of prosperity.

The windshield wipers moved back and forth on the glass outside the car window tirelessly. The scenery rushed by and retreated bit by bit, getting further and further away. The comings and goings in life, the warmth of those touching moments in aging years, are so unforgettable. Life goes on day and night realistically. Coming from a bumpy road, when reaching the end of the road it's still a road. Should I turn left or right? Is it a choice or an abandonment? Hesitating at the crossroads, feeling helpless in the silent night, so powerless, yet so involuntary.

Just like feelings, joys and sorrows, we own its most real side, and what controls all of us is our own thoughts. Many things cannot be explained clearly, any explanation seems superfluous. A lot of things don't need reasons but can be done willingly. Is this love? Or liking? Faintly, deeply or shallowly, how many people can really understand. Feelings are our most glorious side. It can be silently without words, and it can also be grand and magnificent. No matter whether you are at the ends of the earth or within arm's reach, you can feel its ardor and its sadness. Actually, a person's life is very short, short enough that when looking back, all you see are memories. And those people who have accompanied you through the years have become as vast as the sea and fields. To me! Loving you is not about romance, but between the lines.

Goethe said "When you feel pain, please let go." Maybe the meaning of this sentence can only be understood and felt by those who have truly experienced pain. Life gives us rich connotations, but also brings us too much unspeakable hurt. While letting go of true feelings, is the warmth at hand gone for years? Does it hurt? Is it painful? Is it a wave of the hand, or a shake of the head? Youth is a child who loves to cry, every stroke records some past heartaches, the endless green mountains and clear waters, the heavy years become light. Can they be passed over with just a pen and a look back? Remember your initial appearance, your hands once tightly held mine, those deep feelings whispered in my ears. In the cycle of time, they have all become the punctuation marks of holding the pen and looking at each other, unable to gather the desire to say, weaving into the greatest pain of this life, picking up and putting down again and again, from spring flowers blooming to leaves falling everywhere, every unfolding and folding, quietly the flowers fall and bloom again.

Love you! Irrelevant to romance, only in the ordinary reality, there are winds and rains in four seasons, blue sky with white clouds and the earth, and I own the colorful and green memories. When the flowers bloom, their fragrance fills with longing layer by layer, your smile is like plum blossoms blooming, tasting not loneliness but the breath like orchids. Stretch out your hand and spread the dense palm lines, I will still remember the misty rain filling the building, writing down the most beautiful poems for you, enjoying the ancient path side, looking at each other with just a tip of the eyebrow, embracing the guitar years, endlessly singing, how much laughter, how much aging, these are the songs I wrote for you, your youth, my silver hair.

Whether you see me or not, I am here, whether you come or not, whether you love or not, the feelings are still there, neither increasing nor decreasing. Whether you think of me or not, I am here, neither sad nor happy. Let you come into my arms, or let me live in your heart. I like such faintly melancholy words, just like you like teary eyes, always turning its falling into a warm liquid. Facing the impermanence of human affairs, meeting and then parting, you give me a poetic chapter of youthful splendor, endless is the sorrow of departure, remembering all the people who have touched me in life, that burning heat and tacit growth, passing through countless sleepless nights of warm water and lights, from beginning to end, from the past to the present, every detail is still remembered in my heart. Someone said "Lonely is the heart, not lonely is the thought" That fleeting thought, unchanging concern, can withstand how many severe winters, tearful frost gradually melts in the warm March spring, the romance of willows brushing over the branches, another river of spring flowers and autumn moon, the hundred twists and turns of the southern rivers, calmly walking through thousands of years of vicissitudes, whose initial vow slipped on the cobblestones, which girl in the rain alley is dressed in red makeup gazing afar, waiting for frost leaves to cover the sky.

Love you! Irrelevant to romance, guarding an aged distance, this is a kind of tearful happiness, loving what cannot be loved, loving until finally scattering, smiling together in the dust of the world, I remember that I have loved, thousands of mountains sealed by ice and snow, the moon of the broken bridge in the West Lake, coagulating into thousand-year-old amber, my love for you! Still transparent and pure.
