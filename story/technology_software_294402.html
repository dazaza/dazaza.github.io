Management software is the core of enterprise informatization. In essence, management is the architecture, processes, and mechanisms established to achieve corporate goals. A model is an architecture; when it becomes concrete, it is a system, and after intervention, it becomes a mechanism. The role of management software lies in "solidifying" the management architecture and processes of enterprises, enhancing their execution capabilities. It is the combination of management and informatization, building enterprise management on information technology, establishing a simple and efficient modern enterprise management system.

System Overview

The GuaGuaPo Distribution ERP is an overall information management solution designed for medium-sized enterprises. It integrates inventory management, finance, distribution, CRM, and OA management into one system. It aims to help enterprises establish a concise and efficient real-time operation management platform, achieving online dynamic monitoring and management of logistics, capital flow, and information flow. It enables comprehensive control, transmission, and computerized records of information, providing data and speed for decision-making, ensuring the healthy operation of enterprises on a large scale.

GuaGuaPo Distribution ERP is developed on the .NET platform, using the Internet to allow headquarters and remote branches to collaborate on the same platform, achieving comprehensive real-time control over sales, inventory, expenses, and office information from remote branches.

20. IP Wizard: Currently, computers that can access each other on the Internet must obtain an IP address, but fixed IP addresses are very expensive, and quite a number of companies cannot afford them. Without a fixed IP, remote data communication can only be achieved through remote dial-up, which is expensive and has poor stability and maintainability. Alternatively, you could inform remote branches of the dynamic IP address via phone each time you log onto the Internet. To solve the increasing demand for fixed IP addresses from clients and the high cost of hosting servers, RenWoXing ERP Software Development Co., Ltd. invested substantial manpower and time to develop the IP Wizard product.

IP Wizard is a new IP address resolution technology built on the Internet network, using TCP/IP protocol for communication, connecting communications between any computers on the Internet. It does not require a fixed IP on the server side, and the client can connect to the server with IP Wizard installed on the Internet at any time, solving the problem of communication between computers without a fixed IP.

Advantages of IP Wizard: (1) No need for hosted servers or virtual hosts; (2) No need for a fixed IP address; (3) Saves on remote communication costs.
