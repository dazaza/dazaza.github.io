Man stabs and hijacks a car, assaults police and flees, collides with three cars, causing one death and several injuries

by zzf000zxye on 2011-07-13 13:18:48

Da Jiang Net reports by Ye Hongbing and reporter Zhang Yu: On December 1st, Zheng, a 27-year-old man from Youlan Town in Nanchang County of Jiangxi Province, robbed a green taxi with a knife on Jinggang Mountain Avenue in Nanchang and fled towards Liand Tang. During the escape, he stabbed a police officer and caused multiple traffic accidents, resulting in one death and several injuries. According to information obtained by Da Jiang Net from the Nanchang County Public Security Bureau, more than 70 police officers were deployed, and within an hour after the incident occurred, the suspect Zheng, who was suspected of endangering public safety, was captured at the Y三角 Toll Station. Zheng tested positive for drugs after the incident and is suspected of taking drugs. Currently, Zheng is in a coma, and the motive for the crime is still under further investigation. The other injured individuals are being treated in the hospital.

It is understood that at 13:14 on December 1st, the command center of the Nanchang County Public Security Bureau received a transferred alert from the city bureau stating that near the Toyota 4S store on Jinggang Mountain Avenue in Nanchang, a young male suspect had robbed a green taxi with a knife and fled towards Liand Tang. After receiving the alert, the Nanchang County Public Security Bureau immediately activated the emergency response plan for sudden incidents and mobilized criminal investigation, traffic police, special patrol police, and police forces from along the route to pursue and intercept the suspect on the road.

During the pursuit by the police, the suspect driving the stolen taxi collided with a BYD car driven by Zhao from Fengcheng City near the Lianxi intersection on Yingbin Avenue. Subsequently, the suspect abandoned the taxi on the side of the road, used a knife to rob a black New Bora car, and fled south along National Highway 105. At the Jiangsu Water Conservancy Construction Company section, the car driven by the suspect collided with three other vehicles, causing injury to a passerby during the process. After the accident, traffic on this section became paralyzed. The suspect then abandoned the Bora car on the road and attempted to stop a Santana 2000 and a Jetta taxi with a knife but failed. At this moment, Officer Fu Haitao from the Gangshang Police Station of the Nanchang County Public Security Bureau was passing by in a police car (license plate number 032M) with patrol officer Wu Guo while on duty. Initially, Fu Haitao thought it was just a traffic accident on the road, so he stopped to protect the scene and treat the injured. As soon as the car stopped, the suspect rushed to the driver's side of the police car, directly opened the door, and stabbed Fu Haitao in the left abdomen. Despite his severe pain, Fu Haitao immediately reported the specific location of the suspect to the county public security bureau command center and requested reinforcements. Due to excessive blood loss, Fu Haitao subsequently lost consciousness and was sent to a nearby hospital for emergency treatment by Wu Guo.

After receiving the specific location of the suspect, the Nanchang County Public Security Bureau immediately instructed the special patrol police, criminal investigators, and traffic police on the road to block the suspect heading towards the 105 National Highway direction. The suspect fled towards Xiangtang. During the escape, the suspect hit and killed one person and injured five others before reversing the car and fleeing towards Liand Tang. In response to this situation, the county public security bureau required the police on the road to strictly guard against the suspect escaping towards the old urban area of the county town to avoid greater harm. With the cooperation of multiple police units and personnel, the police gradually narrowed down the encirclement, trapping the suspect at the Y三角 Toll Station. The surrounding police officers bravely approached the vehicle where the suspect was forced to stop and subdued him.

Upon investigation, the suspect Zheng is a 27-year-old male from Zheng's natural village in Shaoc Cheng Village, Youlan Town, Nanchang County. The suspect tested positive in the urine test, raising suspicion of consuming K powder or ecstasy.
