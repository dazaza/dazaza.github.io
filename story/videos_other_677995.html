Course Target Audience: Regional managers, regional supervisors, market supervisors, and market personnel of footwear and apparel brands. Aimed at solving practical terminal marketing management problems for footwear and apparel brands, the teaching mode is systematic, practical, operationally strong, and highly interactive. The course is authoritative and systematic, skilled in using successful cases combined with theoretical explanations to address profound issues, allowing students to learn in a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere. With wonderful interactive segments and detailed practical actions, it comprehensively enhances the knowledge and skills of supervisors.

Training Benefits: Using real battle cases, this course addresses problems encountered by supervisors in their actual work. Through group discussions, role scenario simulations, case analyses, and management games, participants improve their management awareness and skills, reducing and eliminating unnecessary customer loss caused by personal mistakes, truly doubling store performance!
