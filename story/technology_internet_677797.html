Xinquan Company is dedicated to the research and development of gas-fired power generation units, and is one of the domestic manufacturers that masters the core technology. All its performance indicators are close to or reach international advanced levels. Gas-fired power generation can protect the environment, save energy, reduce costs, and has unlimited prospects.

Xinquan Company was the first to adopt innovative marketing strategies for gas engines both domestically and internationally, better solving problems related to funding and technology for customers, ensuring they have no worries. The following four cooperation methods are available for customers to refer to and choose from:

1. Rental Business

Xinquan Company's power generation units can be rented out for customer use, allowing customers to rent them anytime and anywhere. This method suits customers who need short-term use of generators. Xinquan Company is responsible for equipment installation, maintenance, repairs, taxation, insurance payments, and providing specialized technical services.

2. Installment Payment

Customers can purchase Xinquan power generation units through installment payments. Customers only need to pay a deposit and then make monthly installments thereafter to own the Xinquan power generation unit.

3. Trade-in for New

Customers can trade in old diesel engines for new gas engines, promoting recycling and mutual benefit.

4. Diesel to Gas Conversion

Customers can convert old diesel engines into gas engines at minimal cost for greater returns. Gas costs less than diesel, making power generation more profitable.

5. Free Gift, Electricity Sale Income

With this method, customers can own a Xinquan power generation unit without spending a penny. Customers first use the Xinquan power generation unit to generate electricity, and Xinquan Company collects monthly payments based on electricity fees. This achieves zero investment.

6. BOT, EPC, BT Models

BOT stands for [Build-Operate-Transfer], which is a method for infrastructure investment, construction, and operation, where Xinquan Company handles the project’s investment—management—operation—transfer processes. The EPC model includes design, procurement, construction, installation, commissioning until final handover as a full suite of services all handled by one company. The BT model is a variation of the BOT model, where a project is fully contracted to a company for financing and construction, and after successful acceptance, it is handed over to the owner, who pays the contractor the total investment plus a reasonable return. Currently, using BOT, EPC, and BT models to raise construction funds has become a new mode of project financing.
