[The Crime]

On the way to Baiqi Reservoir, Huang Shao Si stopped the motorcycle halfway up the mountain. As several people climbed down towards the reservoir, Huang Shao Si suddenly stopped and said, "That's the place."

15-year-old Xiao Qiang (a pseudonym) died at the hands of four friends. 17 hours after the incident, 16-year-old Geng Dou (a pseudonym) came to the public security bureau with blood on his hands to surrender. His father, who accompanied him, could not stop trembling. He never would have thought that his son would actually participate in a kidnapping and murder with three other companions...

Ah Qiang (a pseudonym), 15 years old, Huang Shao Si, 18 years old, Ma Zi (a pseudonym), 14 years old, and Geng Dou are all from Fenshi Village in Lanshan County, Yongzhou City. Due to their idleness, they all dropped out of school early and spent their days hanging around together. On the evening of October 14th, the four gathered together to watch TV and suddenly started talking about their ideals and futures. Without any particular skills, they all felt uncertain about their prospects.

At 3 PM on October 16th, the four teenagers gathered again to act: Ah Qiang invited Xiao Qiang under the pretext of celebrating his birthday, deceiving him out of the county town, then bringing him to the nearby Hongqi Reservoir. Huang Shao Si later picked up Geng Dou and Ma Zi by motorcycle, meeting them at the toilet near the Liu Tree Bridge gas station to wait for the ransom. They then drove together back to the reservoir to wait for the exchange.

Xiao Qiang, who was deceived under the pretense of attending a classmate's birthday party, did not realize until he reached the scene that it was a conspiracy. Behind him, Xiao Qiang said to one of the attackers, "Aren't we classmates?"

After killing Xiao Qiang, the two met up with Geng Dou and Ma Zi. Ah Qiang said, "Xiao Qiang is gone, no money can be obtained now, so let's deal with the aftermath immediately." After a brief discussion, the four buried the knives used in the crime in the rice fields behind Zongshi Middle School. The four took turns carrying Xiao Qiang's body for about three hundred meters and buried him in a relatively hidden corner before fleeing separately.

From the case-handling police officers, it was learned that apart from Ma Zi being orphaned at a young age, the others were all working at home. Due to their early dropout and involvement in society, their parents had difficulty managing them. All the teenagers expressed regret for their actions.

Huang Shao Si casually took out a pack of red cigarette paper, handing one to Xiao Qiang and one to Ah Qiang. Ah Qiang stood and smoked two puffs, then suddenly squatted down. Before Xiao Qiang could react, Huang Shao Si grabbed him upon seeing the signal. Seeing this, Ah Qiang quickly pulled out a plastic rope from his pocket and tied up Xiao Qiang's limbs.

Upon hearing the words "quite wealthy," the three immediately approved the plan. The four teenagers quickly gathered to plot and decided that on the evening of October 16, 2010, they would deceive Xiao Qiang out of his home in the county and then kidnap him, demanding a ransom of 50,000 yuan from Xiao Qiang's family. This criminal plan was quickly formed.

[Kidnapping]

After finishing the "preliminary" work, Huang Shao Si called Xiao Qiang's father in Mandarin and said, "Prepare 50,000 yuan within ten minutes, otherwise your son will die. Bring the money to the toilet entrance near the Liu Tree Bridge gas station, don't report it to the police!"

Xiao Qiang's father, very frightened, replied, "Who are you? I don't have the money!" In order to convince Xiao Qiang's father that his son was indeed kidnapped, Huang Shao Si used Xiao Qiang's phone to call his father, placing the phone next to Xiao Qiang's mouth. After Xiao Qiang called out "Father," Huang Shao Si hung up the phone after saying, "Prepare 50,000 yuan within ten minutes!"

[Investigation]

On October 15th, the four agreed to prepare tools separately. Huang Shao Si borrowed a red motorcycle from his uncle and bought a flashlight and a knife at a shop by the road in Zhuguan Temple Town. Geng Dou selected a sturdy plastic rope from home...

With one signal, they quickly subdued their classmate.

On the afternoon of October 17th at 2 PM, accompanied by his father, Geng Dou voluntarily confessed to the public security bureau about the incident involving himself, Ah Qiang, Huang Shao Si, and Ma Zi on the evening of the 16th. At that time, Huang Shao Si and Ma Zi had already been arrested along with Ah Qiang. Thus, a case where four post-90s teenagers plotted a kidnapping and brutally murdered the kidnapped person before disposing of the body was successfully solved, only 17 hours after the case occurred.
