The Yearning with Broken Wings

An eagle, coming from afar, flew to a carefree land in a distant country. But it broke its own wings, for it had lost restraint and the ability to control its thoughts. What it gained were merely the broken wings and yearning; the lesson made it realize how vast and far-reaching the space for flying should be...

Time makes us grow up, time makes it impossible for us to choose to escape. It gives you the seven emotions and six desires, but that doesn't mean you are omnipotent. We who walk through life, traverse the misty sands of the world, live carefree lives, but never think about survival; because we have our parents, we never think about pain, because we have our youthful prime to squander. We tightly hold onto life, desperately saying that we have firmly grasped life, yet life is like a sponge - the more you care, the tighter you grip, the faster and more water flows away. What is life? A vague concept appears in my mind. I think life is ceaseless walking, and one day, when the road ends, so does life.

We who survive in this life will eventually face reality on our own someday, handle difficulties and setbacks by ourselves, understand the fragility of life, learn to survive, control our thoughts, strive and explore. One day, you will bump into a wall and suddenly realize that the wall is hard and it's your nose that hurts. Only then will you know that life's journey is long, mistakes happen, and lessons are learned.

In life, we are insignificant. From another perspective, every leap and every success we achieve is a great accomplishment. We who hold life in our hands do many things: we become dispirited due to timidity, hesitate because of difficulties, and find it hard to move because of pain. Few people realize that the lessons from each event are the most precious wealth. Savoring these lessons carefully, they have a unique flavor - they arouse your appetite like honey and make you wary like rhubarb. Believe that lessons will take you further, for behind painful lessons lies a profound enlightenment from the heart. Think if these lessons have touched the depths of your soul, making you deeply engrave them in your heart, helping you tread each segment of your life path well, yearn for these lessons.
