Not only beginners face the risk of style selection. Due to the differences in local culture, clothes that are best-sellers in Hangzhou might not sell in Guizhou; styles that don't sell in Hangzhou might be best-sellers in Shenyang. Even experienced shopkeepers may make mistakes sometimes. The most perfect method to mitigate such risks is to "purchase several styles in larger quantities, but purchase less of each style," so even if you encounter unpopular styles, you'll only be stuck with one or two pieces; if you encounter a best-seller, you can quickly restock and make money.

However, traditional purchasing methods cannot achieve "fewer quantities but more frequent purchases," because the cost of purchasing goods is fixed. For example, traveling from Shanghai to Hangzhou, which is already very close, costs around 200 yuan per trip. If you only purchase 10 pieces of clothing, the cost per piece skyrockets to 20 yuan. If you're a shopkeeper from a more distant location, the cost of purchasing goods becomes even higher. This forces shopkeepers to purchase dozens or even hundreds of pieces at once, thereby increasing the risk.

By using Baokuan.com, since you only need to pay for courier fees, it saves a lot compared to traditional purchasing methods, enabling you to achieve "fewer quantities but more frequent purchases."
