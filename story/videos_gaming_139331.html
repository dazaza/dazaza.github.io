**Severed-Finger Xuanyuan**

The legendary Gambling God. This task has low difficulty and is recommended for beginners. The rewards include materials for the Gambling God set, which consists of: Gambling God Boots, Gambling God Ring, Gambling God Mask, and Gambling God Armor.

### Task and Rewards:

**Gambling with Money**, probabilities are as follows:

- 1/15 Tie

- 1/15 Win 60,000

- 1/15 Win 90,000

- 1/15 Win 120,000

- 1/15 Lose but get 10,000 out of pity

- 1/15 Gambling God Equipment

- 9/15 Lose

**Gambling with Wood**, probabilities are as follows:

- 1/15 Tie

- 1/15 Win 100 wood

- 1/15 Win 200 wood

- 1/15 Win 400 wood

- 1/15 Lose but get 10 wood out of pity

- 1/15 Gambling God Equipment

- 9/15 Lose...

**Gambling with Attributes**, probabilities are as follows:

- 1/12 Strength +10

- 1/12 Strength +30

- 1/12 Strength +50

- 1/12 Agility +10

- 1/12 Agility +30

- 1/12 Agility +50

- 1/12 Intelligence +10

- 1/12 Intelligence +30

- 1/12 Intelligence +50

- 1/12 All attributes +50

- 1/12 All attributes -30

- 1/12 Gambling God Equipment

**Mushroom Task**

Help Severed-Finger Xuanyuan collect 10 mushrooms.

Fixed Reward: 150 * (Wave + 1) Experience

Random Rewards:

- 1/3 Gambling God Equipment

- 1/3 Prestige +1

- 1/3 2000 * (Wave + 1) Gold

**Weird Chicken Task**

Help Severed-Finger Xuanyuan catch 9 chickens (the source of chickens is all chickens participating in the Rooster King Competition, including your own).

Fixed Reward: 150 * (Wave + 1) Experience

Random Rewards:

- 1/3 Gambling God Equipment

- 1/3 Prestige +1

- 1/3 30 * (Wave + 1) Wood

### **Woman and Blacksmith**

The woman's crab-killing task is not difficult in the early stages and has a chance to obtain the Crab Claw Halberd (a powerful tool for clearing monsters early on, even though it has been weakened, its power is still considerable, and selling it can also be a good resource), so it is a must-do task early on.

The blacksmith rescue task has average difficulty, and the reward is not too high, but you can smelt equipment and synthesize artifacts at the blacksmith, so it is also a must-do task.

**Crab Task**:

Help the woman kill 10 crabs (there are 8 crabs at a time, and the respawn time is 70 seconds...).

Fixed Reward: 150 * (Wave + 1) Experience

Random Rewards:

- 1/5 3000 * (Wave + 1) Gold

- 1/5 Superior Ring

- 1/5 20 * (Wave + 1) Wood

- 1/5 Crab Claw Halberd

- 1/5 Prestige +1

**Blacksmith Task**:

Rescue the blacksmith.

The blacksmith is in the cage at the top-left corner of the small forest on the left (near the bandits). Break the cage and protect the blacksmith back to Qi Xia Town to complete the task (the blacksmith is very fragile, one hit will make him need to be rescued again, so pay attention to protecting him).

Task Reward: 50,000 gold and 2 prestige points.

---

### **Fisherman**

The water snake task at the fisherman’s location has a chance to drop components of the ultimate artifact Taixu Divine Armor, and it can be chosen to do, with average difficulty. The fish-for-items task can also exchange for good items, and since fish are free, those who have time can try it out.

**Water Snake Task**

Help the fisherman kill 5 large water snakes (large water snakes are green and larger in size, there are 3 at a time, respawn time is 70 seconds...).

Fixed Reward: 100 * (Wave + 1) Experience

Random Rewards:

- 1/6 5000 * (Wave + 1) Experience

- 1/6 Prestige +1

- 1/6 Common Equipment

- 1/6 Fine Steel

- 1/6 30 * (Wave + 1) Wood

- 1/6 Snake Skin Gloves

**Fish-for-Items Task**

Fish are located below the water snake area, with a 10-second respawn time, and cleared every 5 minutes.

Specific Rewards:

- 0~9 Fish: No reward

- 10~19 Fish: Items dropped by monsters (Superior Ring, Magic Amulet, Attribute Book, Medals, etc.)

- 20~39 Fish: Straw Hat + Common Items

- 40~59 Fish: Cape + Advanced Items

- 60~79 Fish: Emerald Ring + Level 1 Artifact

- 80~99 Fish: Fishbone Necklace + Level 2 Artifact

- 100~119 Fish: Level 3 Artifact

- 120~149 Fish: Leather Belt + Level 3 Artifact + Taixu Component (randomly drops Dragon Pattern Component, Crab King Armor, Deer Leather Boots, Snake Skin Gloves, or Evil King Helmet)

- 150+ Fish: Fishing Spear + Level 3 Artifact + Taixu Component

### **Hunter and Elder**

Both the hunter and elder tasks have a chance to drop components of the ultimate artifact Taixu Divine Armor, but they are relatively more difficult and can be chosen based on preference.

**Hunter Task**:

Go to the forest and kill 8 deer (deer are invisible units and move at maximum speed by default).

Random Task Reward:

- 1/4 8000 * (Wave + 1) Gold

- 1/4 Prestige +3

- 1/4 Level 2 Artifact

- 1/4 Deer Leather Boots

**Cult Task**:

Help the elder convert the townsfolk brainwashed by the cult and help them escape.

Buy conversion potion from the elder (it's quite expensive, very unfair, I'm helping you here...) and go to the cult to convert the townsfolk. The success rate is only 1/3, so buy several potions for safety. Then protect them while they leave the cult (there's a small trick: after synthesizing the Magic Ring, use it to recruit the villager directly and take them away safely and quickly).

Fixed Task Reward: 2000 * (Wave + 1) Experience

Random Rewards:

- 1/4 Prestige +2

- 1/4 Level 3 Artifact

- 1/4 Mythril Iron
