Learning to let go is wonderful... Learning to give up is a harvest.

by qingchuyulan on 2010-04-16 22:44:44

Letting go is a choice of mindset; letting go is an art of the soul; letting go is a wisdom of life. The yellow leaves let go of the trunk in anticipation of lushness in spring; candles give up their perfect bodies to gain eternal light; and one's mood gives up worldly noise to embrace peace.

By letting go of pressure, we gain ease; by letting go of troubles, we gain happiness; by letting go of inferiority, we gain confidence; by letting go of laziness, we gain fulfillment; by letting go of negativity, we gain ambition; by letting go of complaints, we gain comfort; by letting go of hesitation, we gain grace; by letting go of narrow-mindedness, we gain freedom.

Life is about choices, and letting go is an art of choice, a required course in life. Without decisive abandonment, there would be no brilliant choices. Instead of struggling bitterly and ending up battered and bruised, it’s better to wave goodbye gracefully and bravely choose to let go. Goethe said: "The whole secret of life lies in giving up life for survival."

Why does life feel so weary? The problem lies in your inability to let go. Let go of past glories, old sufferings, and former romances. Unload all burdens. Once gone, time cannot be retraced, and lost emotions cannot return. Many things, once revisited, cannot be altered. Many people are destined to have fleeting connections. Perhaps only through such experiences do they become precious and cherished.

Letting go is a form of wisdom. "The wise see far ahead; the intelligent avoid danger while it is still invisible." Only by learning to let go can one become more tolerant and wise. Letting go is not waking from a nightmare, nor is it snow in June, neither indecision nor retreat, but rather a calm ascent and a tranquil stroll.

Learn to let go before tears fall, leaving behind a simple silhouette. Learn to let go, burying yesterday in your heart, keeping the best memories. Learn to let go, giving each other a fresh start, not clinging to painful love.

Letting go is a spiritual awakening, a manifestation of wisdom, like releasing birds back to the forest or fish into the water. Life is a difficult journey, rarely smooth sailing. When you must let go, do so decisively. By letting go, you can move further. Through abandonment, you can pursue new goals. Those who refuse to let go often lose what is most precious.

Only by letting go can you free your hands to seize true joy and happiness! Learn to let go to live a fulfilling life!

Abandon the pain brought by loss, the resentment left by humiliation, ceaseless arguments, endless explanations, unrealistic expectations of emotion, insatiable greed for money, and entanglement with vanity... Only by resolutely abandoning secondary, peripheral, and impractical things can your world become serene and clear. You will suddenly understand the truth that "small sacrifices bring small gains, great sacrifices bring great gains, and without sacrifice, there is no gain."

Living in this world is like being surrounded by thorns. If your heart remains calm, you won't move recklessly, and thus you won't get hurt. If your heart moves recklessly, you will harm yourself physically and mentally, experiencing all kinds of suffering...

Yes, you cannot change the weather, but you can change your mood; you cannot change your appearance, but you can choose your expression; you cannot control others, but you can control yourself; you cannot predict tomorrow, but you must make the most of today; you may not succeed in everything, but you can try your best in everything. In dealing with people and situations, be like water: turn with the mountain, turn with the shore, turn with the stone. If others don’t turn, then you turn... towards freedom!
