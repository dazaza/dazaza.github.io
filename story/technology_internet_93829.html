Wholesale Guidelines:

1. The wholesale process is: Order confirmation - > Payment - > Stock preparation - > Shipping.

(1) Brands marked as "in stock" can complete stock preparation and shipping on the same day.

(2) New brands marked as "next day shipping" will complete stock preparation on the same day and be shipped out the next day.

(3) If there are more than 3 styles temporarily out of stock, we will contact you to handle the situation before shipping; if less than 3 styles, in order to speed up the shipping process, we may first ship the available items and then transfer the corresponding amount to your membership prepaid wallet. This amount can be deducted from your next order. Due to the busy business, refunds cannot be requested.

2. Due to the nature of wholesale, profits are extremely low, all transactions on this website require payment upon receipt.

We have been doing business for a long time, and every daily transaction is successfully completed in this way. If you doubt or sincerely question the credibility of our website, please consider carefully before placing an order. Of course, you can start with the minimum quantity of five items for the first wholesale to check the quality and our service, and then cooperate long-term if satisfied. Finally, we warmly welcome your better suggestions, thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

Unified Product Description

Due to the large number of orders each day, customer service cannot verify with you one by one. Please check the quantity and style of the products carefully before clicking "payment". Issues caused by size discrepancies are not considered quality problems!

Please complete the inspection upon receiving the goods on the same day. If there are quality issues, you can refer to the after-sales terms in the help center to determine whether they fall under returnable/exchangeable quality issues. Once confirmed, please contact after-sales customer service for handling. If raised after the second day of receipt, due to the inability to determine responsibility, you will need to bear the costs yourself if the item cannot be returned or exchanged.

Exchange Instructions: Only limited to exchanging the same style but different colors. In cases where the style you want to exchange is out of stock, other styles can be replaced.

1. For product quality issues such as: torn clothes, large stains, detached embellishments, incorrect tailoring, straps sewn incorrectly, etc., obvious uncontested serious quality issues, as well as wrong or missing items. This site offers exchange services for such products. Claims must be made within 48 hours of receiving the goods and returned in the original packaging (the product has no damage or stains, unworn, unwashed). Photos must be provided before returning, otherwise, the request will not be handled. Beyond this period, no responsibility will be assumed. In this case, both parties share the shipping cost equally, with the buyer bearing the return shipping cost and this site covering the re-shipping cost. We do not accept any form of collect-on-delivery, otherwise, it will be considered abandoned. (Due to the nature of wholesale, profits are very low, and when quality issues arise, shipping costs often exceed the product's profit margin. We can only bear the one-way shipping cost, hoping for your understanding!)

2. Non-quality issues are not eligible for returns or exchanges, such as: minor needle holes or thread pulling, fading or small stains, some loose threads, cutting powder marks, human-caused flaws, loose buttons, uncut buttonholes, uncontrollable reasons. Color differences caused by computer display (different displays show colors differently), dislike, "not what I imagined", inappropriate size, fabric not meeting expectations, transparency, discomfort when worn, poor texture, coarse stitching, etc., which are subjective judgments by the buyer, are not considered quality issues. If the buyer refuses to sign for the delivery and directly returns it, it is considered the buyer voluntarily abandoning it, and this site assumes no responsibility.

3. Large physical store customers enjoy non-quality issue returns and exchanges: For non-quality issue exchanges, requests must be made to this site within 48 hours, overdue requests will not be accepted. Returns must be in the original packaging, with the product undamaged, unstained, unworn, and unwashed, otherwise, exchanges will not be allowed. Buyers bear the round-trip shipping costs for exchanged items, and a service fee of 15 yuan per item is charged for each exchange. No reserved goods service is provided. If the replacement item is out of stock upon receiving the returned item, this site does not assume responsibility, please replace with another item.

Note: Special-priced or discounted items, worn or washed items, regardless of any problem, will not be accepted for returns or exchanges!
