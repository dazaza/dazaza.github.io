Sample House Lease Contract (Template)

Lessor: (Hereinafter referred to as Party A) __________________

Lessee: (Hereinafter referred to as Party B) __________________

According to the "Contract Law of the People's Republic of China" and relevant regulations, in order to clarify the rights and obligations between Party A and Party B, both parties have agreed to sign this contract.

Chapter One: House Condition

1. House location: ____________________________________

Number of rooms: ____________________________________

House area: _______________________________________

House certificate: ___________________________________

2. House certificate number: ____________________________

Chapter Two: Purpose of Lease

3. Party B leases the above-mentioned house for the purpose of _____________________. Any change should be notified to Party A in written form.

Chapter Three: Lease Term

4. The lease term is _____ years _____ months. The lessor will deliver the leased house to the lessee from _________ year _____ month _____ day, and recover it on ___________ year _____ month _____ day.

If any of the following situations occur, the lessor can terminate the contract:

a. Party B sublets, transfers, or lends the house without authorization;

b. Party B uses the leased house for illegal activities that harm public interests;

c. Party B delays rent payment for more than ten days.

If Party B fails to move out on time, Party A has the right to sue in the People's Court, and all losses incurred by Party A shall be compensated by Party B.

After the contract expires, if Party A continues to lease the house, Party B has the priority right.
