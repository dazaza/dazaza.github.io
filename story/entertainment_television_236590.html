In the late Qing Dynasty Shanghai, Du Zhen, who presides over the Dasheng Money House, is determined to consolidate his position in the Shanghai market. Meanwhile, his only daughter Du Muxue, who has been studying in England, is called back to China. At the dock, a mistaken encounter causes William, who came to receive British business guests, and Muxue, who had just disembarked, to get into a fierce fight. Fortunately, Muxue's cousin Xiao Peiyuan arrives in time to help her escape... Back home, Muxue discovers that the real reason she was called back to China is to be married off to the Wei family. Having embraced Western ideas, she refuses to comply and decides to make the Wei family back off. However, it turns out that the person from the Wei family is none other than William, with whom she fought at the dock...

The Ge family of Huzhou operates in the silk industry and is immensely wealthy. The only one the Ge matriarch (played by Zhao Minfen) relies on is her eldest grandson Ge Jiahong (played by Chen Sihan). Unfortunately, Jiahong is playful, enjoys fighting, and is rather dense, often causing great worry for the matriarch. Fortunately, the gentle and meticulous personal maid Jin'er (played by Liu Tao) often helps ease the matriarch's concerns.

At a temple fair, Jin'er coincidentally meets Muxue, and they are both shocked upon seeing each other... It turns out that their appearances are identical! Inspired by the moment, Muxue suggests they swap clothes and take a photo as a souvenir. However, the flash startles a nearby horse, leading to chaos at the scene... When Muxue wakes up, she finds herself aboard the Ge family's boat returning to Huzhou. Realizing the situation is serious, yet touched by the matriarch’s care, Muxue decides not to reveal the truth and instead takes advantage of the situation to avoid her marriage arrangement with William. Meanwhile, Jin'er awakens at the Du residence but loses her memory! William believes that although Muxue no longer resists after being injured, he wants her true love, so he goes all out to please her. Under William's charm offensive, Jin'er eventually falls for him. And the gentle and considerate Jin'er makes Xiao Peiyuan, originally carefree and unrestrained, fall in love with this "cousin"...

Muxue, pretending to be Jin'er, retains her sunny personality, winning even more favor from the old lady. One day, Jiahong is severely beaten by his enemies, and Muxue carefully nurses him back to health, sparking feelings between them. As Jin'er's body gradually recovers, the Du family also decides to set a date for the wedding. Unexpectedly, Jin'er regains her memory at this critical moment... The Ge matriarch, eager to give Jin'er an official status, insists that Jiahong marries the fake Jin'er quickly. Reluctantly, Muxue and Jiahong agree to a sham marriage. But on the wedding day, the real Jin'er returns to Huzhou... Jin'er is discovered by the Du family on the wedding day for not being the real Muxue. However, Du Zhen persuades Jin'er to continue the pretense. By chance, William discovers that Ge Jiahong's wife is actually the Du Muxue he desires, so he secretly frames her, ultimately causing Jiahong to be imprisoned in Beijing for smuggling charges.

Desperate to save her husband, Muxue seeks William... Eventually, Jiahong gives in. On the way back to Huzhou, Jiahong meets Yi Renfeng (played by Wang Yixuan), the daughter of Magistrate Yi Zhengbang (played by Zhang Tielin), who is traveling dressed as a man. Jiahong hurriedly returns to Huzhou but only sees the graves of his grandmother and second uncle... Yi Zhengbang is displeased upon learning that his daughter has been associating with local people, so he cuts off Ge Jiahong's livelihood along with those of the people from the courtyard. Enraged, Ge Jiahong impulsively blocks Yi Zhengbang's sedan chair to argue, unexpectedly earning Yi Zhengbang's respect...

How will Ge Jiahong avenge the destruction of his family? How will Yi Zhengbang transform Ge Jiahong? How will Muxue uncover the mystery of Jin'er's life or death through Ru Yan? Can Jiahong and Muxue, the ill-fated lovers who have endured numerous setbacks, reunite? Can Ru Yan gain William's complete love? In this intricate web of love and hatred, who will truly find love... Stay tuned for "Destiny: It's You"...
