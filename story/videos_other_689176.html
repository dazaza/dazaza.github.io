Beloved for life, Xi Chen and Fan watch a comedy movie.

by hoouk260 on 2012-03-03 19:52:26

It was not easy to finish the meal. Ye Fan wanted to find an excuse not to go see a movie, but Lin Xichen seemed determined on this matter. He drove straight to the cinema where there were night shows, making it impossible for Ye Fan to come up with a reason not to watch. If she didn't like one movie, they could switch to another; if the timing wasn't suitable, they could change it to another time. In short, whether she liked it or not, she had to watch.

After buying drinks and popcorn, the two chose a foreign blockbuster - an intense disaster film that Ye Fan picked out. She didn't want to watch any romantic love story with Lin Xichen, who knows what kind of attentions he might show during the movie. In the dark cinema, sitting in adjacent seats, their breathing audible, shoulders touching, it was safer to watch such a non-romantic action-packed movie.

Lin Xichen did not raise any objections. He seemed only concerned about watching the movie together, rather than the type of movie. This made Ye Fan quietly relieved. If he insisted on watching a romance, she really wouldn't know how to refuse him. He always had a way of turning nonsense into sense.

When they entered, there were still three or four minutes left before the start, so their row was empty. They sat down in the middle, a good position to enjoy the movie clearly. Lin Xichen leaned comfortably against the backrest, seemingly not intending to disturb Ye Fan, waiting for the movie to start. Ye Fan felt tense all over, unsure of what this person might do.

"Am I more terrifying than a criminal?" Lin Xichen suddenly joked.

"I'm not sure. A criminal is my enemy to face, but you are not. You're neither an enemy nor a friend, it feels strange, I can't guess your next move," Ye Fan frankly said.

"The next move?" Lin Xichen grinned, "Of course, we wait for the movie to start."

Ye Fan was momentarily speechless, unable to say anything. This guy was truly annoying!

As people trickled in, finding their seats, most were couples, whispering and giggling as they nestled close to each other.

"Are you mad?" At this moment, the movie began, the hall immediately darkened. The people talking stopped and started watching the screen. Lin Xichen lightly reached out his arm around Ye Fan's shoulder, almost seamlessly sitting beside her, softly speaking by her ear.

Ye Fan was startled by him, turning her head to look at him, unaware of how close they were. Her cheek accidentally brushed against his lips, feeling a current-like heat, soft and warm. Her face turned red instantly, wanting to pull away, but Lin Xichen's arm held her shoulder. It was in the cinema, she couldn't scream out loud. Moreover, the couples around them were all cuddling closely. If she shouted, everyone in the hall would probably stop watching the movie and just stare at them.

"Lin Xichen, don't take advantage of the situation," Ye Fan whispered angrily, "Take your hand off."

"I didn't, I can't shout loudly at you, right?" Lin Xichen teased with a mischievous expression. In the dim light of the hall, it was unclear, but occasionally when the screen flashed bright images, his smile could be seen at the corner of his lips. "It was you who accidentally touched my lips. I didn't deliberately take advantage of you, but you smell really nice."

"Lin Xichen, I am really going to get angry!" Ye Fan's tone carried a hint of annoyance.

"Alright." Lin Xichen withdrew his arm, sat upright, and stared at the screen intently without looking at Ye Fan again.

Ye Fan sighed in relief, yet cold sweat broke out all over her body. The cinema should have central air conditioning, keeping the temperature moderate, but she felt a bit cold. She secretly glanced around, and no one paid attention to their words and actions. This was a cinema, and all the people here to watch movies were couples, none of them cared about others' affairs.

She looked ahead, and the images on the screen appeared exquisite and realistic. Gradually, she got absorbed in the movie and no longer paid attention to Lin Xichen who remained silent beside her.

The movie was really fascinating, quickly drawing her into the plot. But when she was fully focused, suddenly a scene appeared, a scene that made her immediately nervous. The protagonist fell into a hole filled with countless rats. She knew it was a movie, she also knew that the protagonist who fell into the hole was safe, but those vivid, scary, crawling rats made her whole body tense, and a scream almost escaped from her throat.

Not just her, the scene scared everyone around her too. Especially when a rat crawled over the protagonist, the hall suddenly echoed with many screams. Ye Fan's body stiffened from being scared by the scene. Then, the scream of the girl sitting next to her, which changed pitch, startled her, and she instinctively leaned towards Lin Xichen, tightly grabbing his hand. She felt as if the rat that had crawled over her foot earlier was now walking on her foot again. She let out a soft scream instinctively and looked down at her feet, but there was nothing, only her own tension.

"What's wrong?" Lin Xichen felt Ye Fan's icy, trembling hand. The images on the screen were indeed vivid and terrifying, but this was just a movie. Why was Ye Fan scared to this extent? "Are you okay? If you're uncomfortable, we can stop watching."

Ye Fan felt her breathing difficult. She closed her eyes but refused to let go of Lin Xichen's hand. She didn't realize that her entire body was leaning on Lin Xichen, and Lin Xichen naturally wrapped his other arm around her shoulder.

In the following scenes, Ye Fan closed her eyes and refused to watch anymore. All she heard were the squeaks of the rats. Those fat, ugly creatures had occupied her nights for a long time, always giving her nightmares.

"It's alright, nothing to worry about, Xiao Fan, everything is fine." Lin Xichen didn't know why, but he knew Ye Fan was very afraid of rats. He knew she was afraid of animals, but he never expected that what she feared the most were rats. "It's alright, really nothing to worry about, it's already over. Are you feeling better?"

Lin Xichen held her and kept comforting her gently. Occasionally, in the flickering light, Ye Fan's pale face could be seen, with tears at the corners of her eyes, refusing to open them, looking like a frightened child, helpless and sad.

Until the movie ended, Ye Fan refused to open her eyes. She quietly stayed in Lin Xichen's arms, listening to his steady heartbeat. Lin Xichen's hot breath blew onto her face, making her feel chilly all over, but this warmth made her feel comfortable.

Suddenly, the lights turned on. It was a comedy movie. Ye Fan opened her eyes, feeling a bit unaccustomed. After a long while, when everyone else had left, she managed to stand up and followed Lin Xichen out of the cinema. She felt as if the rat was still around her, its tiny green eyes staring at her. She was truly afraid, so afraid that even now, she had to sleep with the lights on, the room must be clean, furniture must be minimal, items cannot be piled up. She hoped to live in a place with a wide-open view, never wanting to see filth again, and never wanting to face crowded spaces again.

Lin Xichen held Ye Fan's waist, taking her home. Although he was reluctant, he half-supported and half-carried her out of the cinema and into the car. Outside, it was already late. He helped Ye Fan sit in the car seat, her body quietly leaning against the backrest, not very comfortable, as if avoiding something. She watched the crowd slowly leaving outside, a new group of moviegoers entering the cinema, neon lights flashing. She suddenly felt like crying.

"What's wrong? Are you uncomfortable? Should I take you to see a doctor?" Lin Xichen's gentle voice was not loud, fearing that a loud sound would scare Ye Fan. She looked really scared.

Ye Fan shook her head, not saying anything, her face still pale.

"Then I'll send you back, rest early." Lin Xichen said softly.

Ye Fan nodded blankly, still not speaking.

The car smoothly parked in the neighborhood where Ye Fan rented her apartment. It was pitch black everywhere. "Xiao Fan, is there a power outage here? Why are all the buildings dark, and there are many people outside cooling off. Let me go ask." Lin Xichen opened the car door and went out, then returned quickly, laughing and saying, "There's really a power outage, starting from noon today until tomorrow afternoon."

Ye Fan felt like she was about to break down. How unlucky! No lights, no illumination, she couldn't fall asleep alone!

"Is there a place that's open all night where I can stay?" she asked not quite clearly.

Lin Xichen thought Ye Fan must have something troubling her, she must have recalled something. He sat down in the car, leaning towards Ye Fan and looking at her, "Is there something wrong? Can you tell me?"

"I don't want to stay in the dark," Ye Fan pitifully looked at Lin Xichen. Actually, she didn't want to say it, but it slipped out unconsciously, "I'm really afraid of the dark, and also afraid of rats."

Lin Xichen thought for a moment, looking at the stifling and anxious night outside, and suddenly said, "If you don't mind, I'll take you to my place. I promise I won't do anything, I just hope you can rest well. It's too hot outside, and apart from bars, there seems to be nowhere else open all night."

Ye Fan blankly nodded, actually she didn't hear clearly what Lin Xichen said. She just wanted to get rid of the fear brought by the movie scenes as soon as possible. Not until the car parked in the garage, Lin Xichen got out of the car and led her into a large house did she blankly ask, "Where are we?"

"My home," Lin Xichen smiled gently, said, and the lights turned on, immediately illuminating the entire living room brightly. The view was very spacious and bright, the furniture lines were sleek and simple, but it was clear that they were works of renowned designers.

Ye Fan slightly furrowed her brows, sat down on the sofa, which was very comfortable, allowing her to hide herself entirely in it. She sighed deeply, looking at Lin Xichen, "Can you give me something that will keep me awake?"

Lin Xichen laughed and said, "Had I known you'd come, I would have prepared some tea. Here, except for me, few people come, so there's only coffee. Or, I can heat you a cup of milk?"

"No, I don't want to sleep," Ye Fan immediately said. She was afraid to close her eyes, those rats would appear in her mind, making her relive the terror of that night.

"Alright, don't, I'll make you a cup of coffee, with extra sugar," Lin Xichen immediately laughed and got up to make coffee. The temperature in the room was moderate. The air conditioner was set through his phone before he even arrived home, so upon entering, a refreshing coolness was immediately felt. There was also a faint fragrance in the air, probably because the pot of jasmine on the windowsill had bloomed.

Taking the coffee Lin Xichen handed her, after drinking a sip, she wrinkled her nose, somewhat puzzled and said, "No wonder Lu Rui said your coffee tastes worse than Chinese medicine, it's really bitter."

"Haha, I haven't added sugar yet. The sugar cubes are on the plate. Add milk and sugar yourself, I don't know your taste," Lin Xichen laughed, sitting down beside Ye Fan instead of across from her. He could tell Ye Fan was tense, but this tension wasn't due to him, but rather her past experiences that made her feel fearful.

"Am I very out of control?" Ye Fan tiredly leaned against the sofa, not letting Lin Xichen leave her side. She knew Lin Xichen wouldn't do anything improper. Though Lin Xichen was a flirtatious person, he wasn't vulgar or despicable, just confident and spoiled.

"Not at all," Lin Xichen grinned, "This makes me feel I still have a chance. At least you trust me enough to follow me here and be alone with me."

"I'm a police officer, you should know, if you really try anything, you may not be my match," Ye Fan casually smiled, "My martial arts skills are quite good in the serious crimes unit, handling someone like you, three or four absolutely won't be a problem."

"I believe you," Lin Xichen gently smiled, took the coffee back from Ye Fan, "Too bitter, don't drink it. I'll buy some good new tea tomorrow."

"No, it's fine, I'll drink it slowly. It can keep me alert," Ye Fan sighed softly, "I'm really sorry, but I was genuinely scared. Those images keep appearing in my mind, and today's movie replayed these scenes. I really don't want to fall asleep, I'm afraid if I do, those scenes will reappear in front of my eyes."

"What happened?" Lin Xichen looked at Ye Fan gently, his soft voice saying, "Keeping it bottled up inside isn't good. Saying it out loud might make it seem less of a problem."

Ye Fan was silent for a long time before slowly saying, "It was ten years ago, I jumped off a cliff and was saved by Uncle Jiang. After dinner, he and Aunt Qian sent me home. My parents, if they can be called my parents, took your money. They were counting sixteen thousand yuan. They didn't want to return this money to your family because of my return. However, please rest assured, although they greedily took the money and didn't return it to your family after I came back alive, after I started working, I've been donating part of my salary every month under the name of your family to help some elderly people and children. By now, I've repaid everything. Every recipient and the date are recorded, it's like I'm repaying it on their behalf."

Lin Xichen was stunned for a moment, softly saying, "So, you had to reduce your own expenses."

"I don't need much money," Ye Fan lightly smiled, saying, "Normally, my expenses are simple, like rent and such. When I was in the serious crimes unit, I didn't need a lot of clothes, and my salary was good enough for me to live comfortably. Now, with these things over, I plan to buy my own apartment later, so I don't have to live in someone else's house."

Lin Xichen was silent, this was unexpected for him.

"Well, where was I? Yes, I talked about how they were unhappy about me coming back alive," Ye Fan said calmly, "They said they would marry me off to a man named Li Doutu. My brother disagreed. I think I mentioned this to you before. He said that man was widowed and over fifty, and he absolutely couldn't let me marry him. But they said, I couldn't, absolutely couldn't go back to school. However, they were a little afraid of my brother, because he was a construction worker who had gone out to work and support the family since he was young, physically strong and with a fiery temper. At this time, my younger brother suddenly attacked my brother from behind, and then they locked me in a small room."

Ye Fan paused, as if gathering courage to face this memory. Lin Xichen didn't interrupt. He guessed Ye Fan's unease must be related to this small room.

"Then, they locked me in a tiny room, very small, filled with clutter, all sorts of useless things and things temporarily not needed. You know, for people living in such conditions, usable and unusable things are basically all trash that can be thrown away," Ye Fan exhaled deeply, slowly saying, "There was no light, it was very dark, with only a very weak beam of light seeping in through the cracks. There was a small window above, no glass, just some randomly nailed wooden strips. Streetlights shone in from outside, very dim. Then, I felt something crawling on my foot. I looked down and saw a fat rat on my foot. It stared at me, with its beady eyes blinking, and then, I suddenly realized there were many, many rats in there—"

Ye Fan's speech suddenly sped up, as if seeing those detestable rats again! Lin Xichen's heart tightened immediately. He quickly reached out and grabbed her nervous hand, feeling her hand ice-cold, trembling, her whole body stiff, her eyes full of fear.

"No one cared about me, they ignored me. My brother had been knocked unconscious by my younger brother, so I could only keep jumping. But the space there was too small, so small that besides the clutter, there was only a tiny spot for me to stand. At that time, I was so scared I couldn't scream, my brain was completely blank, and all I could hear was the squeaking of the rats and the fattest rats I had never seen before. I don't know how I survived that night. That's when I realized sometimes a minute could feel as long as a year. My memory seemed frozen at that moment, I felt like I could never escape it, so much so that even now, I still can't bear to be alone in a dark room, I can't turn off the lights and sleep, and I often suffer from nightmares. Luckily, just before dawn, my brother woke up. He secretly carried me out behind our parents' backs, then sent me to Uncle Jiang's house. Uncle Jiang and Aunt Qian took me in and arranged for me to study in another city. I lived with them ever since, and they treated me very well." Ye Fan tried to calm herself down, but she couldn't control the trembling of her body, "The first time I saw Uncle Jiang being held hostage, I thought that person looked very much like a rat, sneaky and rat-like. He threatened hostages with a sharp weapon, Uncle Jiang exchanged himself for the hostages, but he actually used the weapon to attack Uncle Jiang. I don't know where I got the courage, I just thought he was a rat, I wanted to kill him, I wanted to make him disappear in front of my eyes, so I shot. But, I was still too compassionate. At that time, I was studying medicine, I knew the position of the heart. In the end, I thought, this person is still a person, not a rat, so he was saved, but from that moment on, I suddenly decided to become a police officer, because for many people, some people are just like rats."

Lin Xichen kept warming Ye Fan's body with his own, she looked pale and helpless, with an indelible sadness in her eyes. All this was caused by him, if he hadn't played games back then, Ye Fan might have been living happily and peacefully as an ordinary woman now. And now, though she lives well, she has unforgettable and terrible memories. He swore to take good care of Ye Fan, never letting her be hurt in any way again!
