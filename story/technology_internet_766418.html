Baidu to Launch Three Hardware Products: Little Du WiFi, Router, and TV

by anonymous on 2013-09-12 12:04:04

The term "cloud computing" is no longer unfamiliar to today's internet users, and the increasingly diverse and convenient personal cloud services are gradually entering people's daily lives. However, when netizens have just become accustomed to storing vast amounts of data and entertainment resources in the cloud space, they find that due to network conditions, device compatibility, and other objective factors, they can only look at the large number of audio-visual resources in the cloud space with regret. Without WiFi, "enjoying cloud resources and synchronizing data on mobile devices" has become a "luxurious" experience that only the wealthy can enjoy.

However, these issues may soon be resolved, thanks to Baidu Cloud making significant contributions to solving this problem. As China’s leading personal cloud service brand, Baidu Cloud not only closely follows international trends in cloud computing development but also actively researches new models for personal cloud services, leading the industry in implementing comprehensive personal cloud services. Moreover, it studies how to allow users to conveniently connect different platform devices anytime and anywhere, easily using cloud data and services. The realization of this concept comes from its planned launch of innovative hardware devices — the "Xiaodu Trio," which is expected to be released soon.

It was revealed that the "Xiaodu Trio" includes: Xiaodu WiFi, Xiaodu Router, and Xiaodu TV. These devices can meet users' needs to "freely access, arbitrarily transfer, and casually watch" cloud resources and services. The entire set costs less than 300 yuan, and with this equipment, users can achieve internet connectivity anytime and anywhere, cross-terminal synchronization and sharing of quality resources, and convenient enjoyment of high-quality entertainment resources in the cloud, solving the issue of perfect cloud service enjoyment under different scenarios for Baidu Cloud users.

Insiders close to Baidu say that users will be able to pre-order the "Xiaodu Trio" as early as mid-September, with the Xiaodu WiFi possibly being the first product to hit the market. Xiaodu WiFi is a portable USB router that enables easy cross-terminal internet connectivity and free indoor WiFi coverage when carried around. Xiaodu Router is positioned as a tool for transferring cloud data at will, supporting remote downloading of audio-visual resources. Users can link their Baidu accounts to achieve simple remote multi-device operation (via smartphone or tablet control). Xiaodu TV not only supports DLNA and Airplay but is also the first TV in the industry to support Miracast mirror projection technology. Its most distinctive feature is leveraging Baidu's unique multi-screen interactive technology to project PC web flash videos onto the TV.

Notably, the three hardware devices launched by Baidu were all developed based on Baidu Cloud technology, representing the implementation of cloud-end technology in hardware terminals. This reflects the trend in the internet market where providers aim to capture users by grounding cloud services into hardware, making hardware the entry point for services. Clearly, Baidu hopes these terminal devices will serve as bridges enabling users to connect across terminals anytime and anywhere, enjoying Baidu Cloud services.
