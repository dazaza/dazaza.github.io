Online Music Listening:

Click the PLAY button in the middle of the player to play the pre-set song list.

Change Channels: In playback mode, click the drop-down arrow in the upper left corner to enter "My Channels". The channel songs are automatically recommended by the system based on music style. The large thumb icon on the right is the music box (carefully selected good songs).
