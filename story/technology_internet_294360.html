The real estate market maintained its heat throughout the year. Sales in all major cities remained high. The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you want to buy or sell, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. And this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when placing a buy/sell order. At the same time, there are certain rules regarding the quantity of stocks bought and sold: the quantity of stocks commissioned for purchase must be an integral multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks commissioned for sale does not have to be an integral multiple of 100.

There are four types of commissions: counter submission, telephone automatic, computer automatic, and remote terminal commission.
