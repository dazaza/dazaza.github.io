Central air conditioning disinfection and purification device

by nclep on 2008-11-14 11:27:50

NCL Environmental Protection Company first adopted the "dust removal method, neutralization method, and sterilization method" to install a dynamic air dust removal and sterilization system for the central air conditioning ventilation system, without ultraviolet rays or electrostatic dust removal devices. The specific principle is as follows: washing liquid enters the nasal cavity of the equipment to wash the inhaled polluted air, washing the dust into the washing liquid. Through the action of the pressure pump, the dust is input into the automatic dust removal filter for dust removal and sewage discharge, achieving the effect of automatically removing dust and purifying the air. This process has low and constant wind resistance (15-20P), can replace medium and high-efficiency filters, does not cause secondary pollution, and is called the dust removal method; according to customer needs, biological enzymes, ozone water, alkali water and other chemical reagents are configured in the washing liquid, absorbing the heat emitted by the motor unit to maintain a constant temperature, quickly neutralizing and decomposing radioactive substances and gaseous pollutants in the air, which is called the neutralization method; using aluminum honeycomb as the carrier, with a certain amount of nanoscale nickel, titanium dioxide material, photocatalytic material attached to its surface, synthesizing a metal photocatalyst mesh. Under specific light source irradiation, it continuously adsorbs-catalyzes-decomposes, removing bacteria, viruses, and harmful gases from the air, which is called the sterilization method.

This system can be customized and modified according to different air quality requirements of different environments for customers. It makes the indoor air quality meet the requirements of the Ministry of Health's "Hygiene Specification for Central Air Conditioning Ventilation Systems in Public Places" and "Disinfection Technical Specification."
