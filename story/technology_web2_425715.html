Uncle Big Belly

There was an uncle whose belly was very, very big, as if there was a pot hidden inside his clothes. Once, Uncle Big Belly was taking a walk on the playground where a group of children were playing football. The football hit Uncle Big Belly and then rolled into the grass, disappearing from sight. After the children couldn't find the ball, they noticed Uncle Big Belly's big belly. Little Brother with sharp eyes asked Uncle Big Belly if he had hidden the ball under his clothes. Uncle Big Belly said no, but they didn't believe him until he lifted his shirt to reveal his white belly. Then the children laughed and walked away.

Because of his big belly, when Uncle Big Belly entered a room, it was often the case that his belly arrived before he did. One summer, he went to someone's office to handle some business. When he reached the door, he remembered that he had forgotten something and hesitated for a moment. As a result, the people inside only saw his belly come in but not his face. They wondered, "How come this kid's face is so round and big, and why is there a T-shirt covering the face?"

Because of his big belly, Uncle Big Belly often made jokes. Once, he rushed to catch a bus. Because there were many people and the door was narrow, everyone pushed, and Uncle Big Belly got stuck there. People around had to exert great effort to pull him onto the bus. There were also a lot of people on the bus, all squeezed tightly like sugarcane in the carriage. Just as they were squeezing, suddenly, an old lady shouted, "Don't squeeze, don't squeeze! There's a pregnant woman here, be careful or it might be dangerous!" Upon hearing this, everyone hurriedly moved aside, leaving Uncle Big Belly standing there with his big belly. Everyone laughed. Just as Uncle Big Belly felt embarrassed, a nearby child, tired from standing, started crying and demanded to be held by his mother. Seeing this, Uncle Big Belly patted his big belly and said, "Child, don't cry. Come here, there's a real leather sofa available." Then he picked up the child and placed him on his belly. Everyone burst into laughter again, but this time they laughed even more joyfully and happily.
