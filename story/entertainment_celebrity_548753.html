[Famous person] Supermodel He Sui gains popularity overseas; her sexy and beautiful image also aligns with Chinese aesthetic preferences.

by mlwang on 2011-11-11 11:23:49

There are few domestic models who can become popular internationally. For example, Lu Yan and Liu Wen. However, there has always been a considerable difference between the aesthetic of Chinese people and foreigners. Those supermodels considered "extremely beautiful" by foreigners often have an ordinary appearance in the eyes of Chinese people. But this time, He Sui, the new generation international supermodel, conforms to the aesthetic standards of both the East and the West.

He Sui, from Wenzhou, is only 23 years old. She became famous overnight after walking in 11 shows at the Milan Fashion Week in September 2010, and was rated as the most potential new supermodel face globally by Italian version of嘉人 (Marie Claire).

In September 2010, when many Chinese supermodels were eliminated from the Milan Fashion Week, He Sui surprisingly rose to fame, consecutively walking in 17 fashion shows including Les Copains, Aigner, Massimo Rebecchi, Stephan Janson, Anteprima, Byblos, Miss Bikini, Carlo Tivioli etc. In all big and small events in Milan, you could see the figure of this young woman from Wenzhou, China.

In February 2011, He Sui once again stepped onto the international runway and amazed New York with her performance in 12 shows. She shone brightly in the following Milan Fashion Week. As the opening model for Ralph Lauren's 2011 autumn/winter fashion show, He Sui captivated the entire audience with her three-dimensional facial features and elegant, confident demeanor, gaining attention and favor from NY Daily Fashion and WWD, and becoming one of the most popular new supermodel stars in the fashion world.

In fact, 2011 was the second time He Sui entered the vision of the international fashion world. After winning the championship of the 12th China Model Star Competition in 2006, she smoothly became the corporate image spokesperson for Japan's Asahi Kasei Corporation in 2007. In 2008, due to skin allergy to makeup, she appeared less frequently and nearly faded out.

Recently, on the stage of the 2012 Victoria's Secret annual fashion show held in the United States, He Sui became the second Chinese angel supermodel after Liu Wen. Compared to her naive appearance in 2009 (left picture), He Sui now radiates the momentum and charm of an international model.
