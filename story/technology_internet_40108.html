Netizens change sides, QQ Coral Bug incident undergoes a huge change overnight! Tencent suspected to be the biggest mastermind behind the scenes.

by wivan2006 on 2007-12-22 13:50:45

Netizens who once cared about the Coral Bug (a QQ software plugin), after the December 19th trial media disclosed that Soff had bundled interest-generating plugins with Coral Bug, it seemed like public opinion changed drastically overnight. Netizens who were originally supporting and crying out for justice for Soff suddenly turned against him, even the dedicated website for this event, helpsoff.com, underwent a significant change in attitude - by disabling comments! Here, we can't help but admire Tencent's PR strategy.

helpsoff.com closure statement:

Haha, the brothers who were cursing can now shut up (please allow me to look down on you too, and greet your family). For Soff who dared to challenge Tencent, I have infinite admiration and wish him peace and prosperity in his business. Although I've never used Coral Bug, I appreciate the convenience it brought to netizens. I sincerely hope that our little penguin can grow smoothly, bringing us more surprises instead of finding ways to take money from our pockets. And I also look down on the plagiarism and exploitation nature of Tencent and its penguin family's original sin. This site will be closed for a limited time...

In fact, if everyone could calm down and analyze the entire event, we can clearly see the technical traces of Tencent's behind-the-scenes operations. From the statement released by Soff's lawyer, we can clearly see that Soff's so-called commercial bundling is completely different from the traditional operation methods of rogue plugins. A simple truth: if Soff really wanted to commercialize through QQ Coral Bug, with his technical strength, he could have forcibly bundled rogue software without letting users discover it. The real truth is that QQ Coral Bug has seriously affected Tencent's commercial interests. Since QQ Coral Bug removed the commercial elements of Tencent QQ, the user base is rapidly decreasing, and the trend is getting worse. Therefore, Pony Ma took drastic measures after multiple unsuccessful attempts to resolve the issue! Any netizen with some thinking ability, if not influenced by public opinion at this moment and carefully reflecting, can see the truth of the matter just from one reason: imagine, if it was for commercial interests, with Soff's mind and connections, wouldn't QQ Coral Bug have many better choices? Why didn't he accept Pony Ma's recruitment, when even working for Tencent wouldn't result in less income?

In summary, the current situation is entirely planned by Tencent's PR, aiming to fundamentally solve the QQ Coral Bug problem and eliminate Soff! For Tencent Corporation, this despicable PR tactic completely goes against the basic business ethics that a regular commercial company should possess!

I just want to say that Soff is guilty, but the crime does not deserve punishment!

Leaving someone a way out is also cultivating blessings for oneself!
