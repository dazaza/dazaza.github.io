For Gemini, the New Moon is born within your realm of influence. This New Moon is interesting for two reasons: one, it coincides with Mercury Retrograde, but the initiating force of the New Moon will allow you, who are clever and think calmly, to increase your efforts in various aspects. Two, shortly after the New Moon occurs, it forms a sextile with Uranus, but this soon dissipates. It's somewhat regrettable, as the idea of using Uranus' power to "stir things up" may not come true. Although there are other negative factors present, this New Moon is quite favorable for Geminis due to its special position. Firstly, it presents an opportunity to improve health issues and bad lifestyle habits. Secondly, it involves establishing and launching new life and work plans. Specifically, this new plan isn't something you suddenly thought of, but rather old plans that have been brought into the execution phase due to various reasons.

For Leo, the New Moon appears in the social palace. Naturally, this weekend is suitable for social activities, and you may receive invitations from friends or colleagues. It's important to maintain existing relationships while focusing on building new ones. Next Monday and the entire next week will be suitable for expanding social circles related to your career and work. Whether for personal interests or professional needs, the reasons for expanding your social circle are endless. The appearance and effect of the New Moon bring you more opportunities and venues, such as business gatherings, joining organizations, or summer activity groups. Regarding exercise, if you do have an invitation this weekend, pay special attention to protecting your legs, knees, and joints.

For Cancer, similarly, the New Moon gives you the starting power to change bad living habits and introduce new healthy habits. More importantly, the New Moon's power allows you to uncover and resolve hidden problems and potential issues in your life, especially at work. This most secretive and painful area isn't easy to tackle directly. Additionally, Cancers often overlook blind spots that can be resolved under this celestial influence. Identify what people and situations you're subconsciously avoiding, and find solutions on a conscious level. You can relax at home or go out over the weekend.

For Aries, the appearance of the New Moon makes it likely that you'll have special outings or trips that occupy work time this weekend, next Monday, and the entire next week. Don't worry; even if it's a business trip, you'll be pleased. The environmental change will alleviate your recent anxiety and unease. Furthermore, the initiating power of the New Moon might bring you "contract" matters. For instance, this weekend and next week, you might encounter life or work-related events like renting or buying a house, signing new work project contracts, etc. Considering Mercury Retrograde, these should be avoided if possible, or delegated to others like roommates or project team members. You might hear from relatives recently. Whether staying home or going out over the weekend, remember to stay away from alcohol.

For Taurus, the New Moon brings financial improvements. One, because of a previous case or reason, you start "reaping" rewards. Another, the New Moon means that a situation in work or life can change your current financial state and is ready to be executed or about to start. The key is the second point. In a sense, this reflects your current work status, such as a raise, which might be difficult to achieve in the current economic environment of layoffs and wage cuts. Alternatively, you might receive a new job offer with a good salary. This is a great New Moon for Taureans to take advantage of, but I believe the most important thing is to create a new plan for existing financial issues. Similarly, if there are contracts to sign involving financial matters, proceed carefully. I trust that Taurus eyes are sharper than Aries eyes.

For Virgo, this is a surprising New Moon because it appears in your career palace. Under the influence of the New Moon, this weekend and the entire next week, even the week after, new job opportunities may arise. If you're sending resumes, unemployed, or dissatisfied with your current job, you'll get responses. If not, you might receive a call from a headhunter or even get introduced to a job opportunity during a casual conversation with a friend. This isn't the key point. The changes brought by the new job and countless real or fake "indirect strategies" in life paths make it hard to determine if they're good or bad. For Virgos currently working hard in their careers, the New Moon not only brings new work projects and promotion opportunities but also provides chances to solve current work difficulties. Thus, you gain qualifications and reputation in your field. Your daily work still looks messy, so handle it cautiously. If there are contracts to sign at work, review the terms carefully and have a few people help you check.

For Libra, the appearance of the New Moon will make you travel or go on business trips over the weekend or next week. It could be purely for entertainment or sudden work assignments. It's unclear which one, and you might not leave your city at all. What else can the New Moon bring? Learning power. If you're a student, this New Moon is a good opportunity to start a new study plan or prepare for exams. If you've already started working and don't have time or energy to "return to school," focus on relevant career learning. Don't think "learning" only involves picking up books. A case study at work, or asking friends for PPTs or planning documents on MSN to modify unfamiliar cases, are all learning opportunities. With Mercury Retrograde, it's a good time for self-study and reflection. The weekend is suitable for spending time with your loved one.

For Scorpio, the New Moon brings investment opportunities or finding investment entry points. Of course, it's more about your current investment business or ideas and decisions regarding certain investments, such as real estate matters. Also, loan-related matters. Over the weekend and next week, you might see numbers increase in the most practical financial areas. Those who owe you money (if they're not proactive, chase them). If it's a loan, considering Mercury Retrograde, I suggest postponing any financial signings. Given Scorpios' intelligence and spiritual power, you can handle it. Lastly, for those currently involved in lawsuits, the New Moon brings positive energy, not necessarily winning in court after Sunday or Monday, but the process of gathering evidence and preparation will be aided by the New Moon. Both days are suitable for Scorpios to stay home or socialize.

For Sagittarius, the New Moon appears in the cooperation palace, marriage palace, contract palace—whatever you call it. Over the weekend, next Monday, and the entire next week, first, you might form new work partnerships, i.e., new clients. Congratulations, especially for those in sales. However, with Mercury Retrograde, contractual matters need careful handling in daily work. Therefore, establishing contracts might face obstacles, but they won't ruin things. Second, in the emotional domain, one of your relationships might develop into a serious relationship. For Sagittarians, this question has two perspectives: Is the formation of this serious relationship or being pushed into it led by your subjective consciousness, or is it "watercourse action" without your awareness? With communication and thinking somewhat chaotic, the New Moon's role in the emotional field isn't clear. The last possibility, and the least probable based on headcount and proportion, is getting married or engaged. However, this requires further consideration, and if truly deciding, you need to choose an auspicious date through divination. The weekend is suitable for gathering with friends, and ambiguous matters seem predictable.

Capricorn is the third zodiac sign affected by the New Moon concerning health issues. This is a good thing. You can start exercising since summer is coming, and you'll need to wear tighter clothes, so you should prepare accordingly. Of course, this is as difficult as quitting smoking. If you've already prepared a list of excuses, like not having time, then you can change some bad habits in your current life or work. Additionally, another force brought by the New Moon is the push and initiation in daily work. This is the most delightful part. Compared to Virgo, the word "career" is too grandiose, but the New Moon in Capricorn's daily affairs is practical. Whether you're employed or a small boss, you'll find new cases and businesses arriving at work. Similarly, for those who own a business, the New Moon gives you the opportunity to develop new products and markets. For employees, there might be new job opportunities or positions. Don't just see the good side; the New Moon also means that as you start or accept "new things," you also accept "new resistance and new enemies." The weekend is suitable for spending time with family.

For Aquarius, the New Moon is equally delightful. While your mood is being swelled by Jupiter and Neptune like a fat sea cucumber, the New Moon pushes you further in romantic matters. It's hard to rein yourself in. If you're currently single or feel one isn't enough, seize the moment this weekend and next week. In fact, whenever you have a "social" event, you're likely to meet someone you like. For those content with a two-person world, the New Moon makes you more romantic in ways different from before. Additionally, for creative workers, the New Moon brings many good ideas. Don't think "creativity" only exists in the art field—does programming not count? As long as you need "ideas," the New Moon will assist you. Moreover, next week, Aquarians will have a lighter work mindset and a stronger desire for entertainment. Taking a proper break is fine, depending on individual circumstances. If you want children, you can plan accordingly. If the timing isn't right, just let it be—I'm merely presenting possibilities. You can stay home or go out over the weekend.

If you're planning to move, next week is a good time to start looking for a new place or pack your belongings.
