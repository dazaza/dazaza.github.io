Huangshan 3D map appears on the Internet

by yinyin on 2008-08-04 21:14:16

If you have never been to the urban area of Huangshan, can you find the specific location of hotels and restaurants, traffic routes in the city, what shops are around, where there are parking lots nearby, and which restaurants are offering discounts through a simple search? Now, you just need to click your mouse and log in to www.online-hs.com. Through the 3D Huangshan urban map, you can conveniently learn about this information. This map not only fully presents the appearance of the Huangshan city but also provides road guidance, life, and tourism-related information.

This 3D map is officially called "Metropolitan Circle". It is a three-dimensional simulation digital city system developed by technical personnel based on GIS and virtual reality technology. It divides the city into several blocks and, according to the requirements of intuitive simulation, topologically networks the entire city for major streets, commercial areas, residential areas, stores, scenic spots, etc. At the same time, according to people's real-life behaviors and various needs, it develops corresponding information function modules. After citizens log on to the website, they can freely obtain information services such as urban electronic maps, e-government, intra-city e-commerce, bus driving route queries, leisure and entertainment, and department store retail.

As part of the construction of Digital Huangshan, the three-dimensional simulation of Huangshan created using the latest scientific and technological techniques strengthens the promotion and marketing of Huangshan through modern means of communication, allowing people to more intuitively understand the new Huangshan and accelerating Huangshan's pace in building a "modern international tourist city."
