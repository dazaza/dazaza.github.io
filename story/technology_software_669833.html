Man marries girlfriend suffering from leukemia in ward (in photos)

by ming1980k on 2012-02-20 11:45:32

The two young men in the ward, the groom puts the ring on the bride, and a deep kiss in the ward. Reporter Hu Hao / Wen Ji Chengjun / Picture "Where you are hospitalized is our new house!" After a leukemia girl from Quanjiao got sick, her boyfriend never left her side. Yesterday was the girl's 20th birthday. Accompanied by relatives and friends, the boyfriend went to Nanjing to hold a "ward wedding" with the girl. The ward filled with the smell of medicine was decorated with flowers, the character for double happiness, and the newlyweds, creating an unusual warmth and moving atmosphere. Facing his girlfriend dressed in a white wedding dress, the boyfriend Li Yafeng said: "In this life, you are my only bride!" A meeting: love locks on the destined person [High-definition pictures: boyfriend holds wedding in the ward for his girlfriend suffering from leukemia] Their meeting was also quite "dramatic". The handsome young man Li Yafeng came from Hengyang, Hunan. After graduating from junior high school at the age of more than ten, he left his hometown to work in Shanghai. The young man could endure a lot of hardship. Waiter, salesperson, any hard work he could do. Soon, Li Yafeng had his own barber shop in the rented neighborhood. With his excellent skills, his business was pretty good. One day in March last year, Huang Yan, a girl from Quanjiao, Anhui, working in Shanghai, accompanied her classmate to get her hair cut at Li Yafeng's shop. Unexpectedly, their first meeting made Li Yafeng very "regretful." "She has big eyes and is very beautiful," Li Yafeng said that the first time he saw Huang Yan, it made him have a feeling of excitement. Now recalling it, he still feels very sweet. But what made him "regretful" was that he didn't get Huang Yan's phone number or QQ number, not knowing when they would meet again! What Li Yafeng didn't expect was that perhaps destiny was meant to be, and during their first meeting, Huang Yan also "chose" Li Yafeng. Not long after, in order to meet Li Yafeng again, Huang Yan accompanied another friend to "get her hair cut", and Li Yafeng took the opportunity, blushing as he asked for Huang Yan's QQ number. Disease: fate playing tricks on the lovers Meeting too late, the two quickly fell into a sweet love affair! After both sets of parents found out about this matter, they both conducted "investigations," and both sets of parents were very satisfied. Urged by their parents, 23-year-old Li Yafeng and 20-year-old Huang Yan worked while preparing for their wedding, filling their lives with warmth. However, fate played a huge joke on them. In March this year, Huang Yan felt like she might be pregnant, so she went to the hospital for a check-up. The doctor told them that there was something wrong with Huang Yan's blood, and suggested transferring to another hospital for further examination. The two went to another large hospital in Shanghai, and the results of the examination left the two speechless: Huang Yan had leukemia! In the following days, Li Yafeng took Huang Yan, traveling between the major hospitals in Shanghai and Hefei, but the treatment effects were not obvious. "I also struggled inside," Li Yafeng said, leukemia was not a small illness, the treatment costs were bottomless, relatives and friends all advised him to give up, "But I will not give up, I love her too much!" What gave the two some comfort was that not long ago, Huang Yan smoothly gave birth to their precious son. Watching the adorable child cry at birth, Li Yafeng and Huang Yan were full of tears, tightly embracing each other. Wedding: "Today, you are my most beautiful bride" Due to the worsening condition, the family quickly sent Huang Yan to the Hematology Department of Jiangsu Province People's Hospital for continued treatment, and Li Yafeng also rushed from Shanghai to Nanjing. After Huang Yan fell ill, the originally promised wedding date was delayed. "But we agreed that November 9th is Huang Yan's 20th birthday. If she was discharged from the hospital today, we would hold the wedding at home; if she was still in the hospital, we would hold the wedding in the ward." During the gap in Huang Yan's chemotherapy, the two spent 298 yuan to take a few simple wedding photos. Huang Yan's condition also attracted attention. With the help of netizens and government departments, the couple not only received 90,000 yuan in donations, but netizens also spontaneously organized to help prepare the wedding for this loving couple. Red double happiness characters, festive balloons, white wedding dresses, rings—everything was prepared. Yesterday, surrounded by many relatives and friends, the originally quiet, medicine-smelling ward became lively and full of joy! Dr. Qiu Hongxia, the head physician of the Hematology Department of Jiangsu Province People's Hospital and Huang Yan's attending physician, presided over the wedding for the newlyweds. Nurses presented their flowers one after another to express blessings. When Li Yafeng put the wedding ring on Huang Yan lying in bed and kissed her cheek, saying, "In this life, you are my most beautiful bride," Huang Yan could no longer hold back her tears of happiness... Doctor: His unwavering devotion gave her the strength to recover "Huang Yan's condition has now entered the remission phase," the attending physician told reporters that Huang Yan's stable condition was closely related to her boyfriend's unwavering devotion. Two months ago, when Huang Yan first entered the hospital, her condition was very serious: intracranial bleeding, high tumor cells, high hemoglobin, fever, pulmonary infection. If not treated in time, death awaited her. "To achieve the current treatment effect, it has a great deal to do with her boyfriend's unwavering devotion and Huang Yan's own optimism." Doctors introduced that for leukemia, stem cell transplantation is the radical cure method, and the cost of transplantation alone would require at least 200,000 yuan. If complications occur during surgery, the treatment costs would be even higher. Moreover, before bone marrow transplantation, routine treatments cannot stop. To treat Huang Yan, Li Yafeng had already incurred a debt of 30,000 yuan, and the hundreds of thousands of yuan needed for later treatment is currently the biggest difficulty facing this newlywed couple. The experiences of the two moved people and also touched the doctors and those around them. Currently, the doctors are doing their utmost to treat Huang Yan, and they also appeal, hoping more people will lend a helping hand, sending "love" hope to this devoted couple!
