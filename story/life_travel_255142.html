Ski resort entrance fee, unlimited skiing time (including skis, boots, poles, rope tows, cable car).

Procedure:

1. The ski resort uses an all-in-one card system for consumption. Consumable items include skiing (boots, skis, poles), skiwear (jacket, pants), gloves, lockers (keys), snow tubes, food and beverage, hiring instructors, etc.

2. Guests go through the card collection process at the cashier, paying a deposit of 200 RMB per person for ski equipment and consumption. Each guest receives one consumption card, which can be used to collect and return ski equipment, as well as for other consumptions.

3. When guests use their consumption cards for consumption, the cost will automatically be deducted from the card. If the balance is insufficient, recharge at the cashier to continue consumption.

4. A consumption card can only rent any equipment once. After returning, it cannot be rented again.

5. Guests must return all rented equipment before checking out. If there is any equipment not returned, checkout will not be allowed.

6. Guests must use their own consumption cards to collect and return equipment, no mixing allowed. Otherwise, if unable to check out or the consumption amount does not match, guests are responsible for themselves.

7. Skiing starts timing when the first piece of ski equipment (boot or ski) is rented, and ends when the last piece of ski equipment (boot or ski) is returned. The ski resort gives guests 15 minutes of free consumption time. The skiing fee is automatically calculated based on the consumption time recorded on the guest's consumption card. After exceeding one billing unit, the next billing unit will be charged.

8. Skiwear, lockers, etc., are free of charge if returned within 5 minutes of rental. After 5 minutes, fees will be automatically deducted.

9. Hiring an instructor begins timing 5 minutes after the designated instructor.

10. Guests who damage ski resort equipment must compensate according to the ski resort's compensation regulations.

11. If a guest loses their card, they must get a replacement card. After getting a new card, all information from the original card will be transferred to the new card. The replacement card costs 20 RMB. Please take good care of your card.

Beijing Lianhua Mountain Ski Resort is located in Zhang Town, Shunyi District, Beijing, covering more than one million square meters, built and opened for business on December 19, 2003. Through the construction of the second phase project this year, it has now become the largest ski resort in Beijing by land area.

Lianhua Mountain Ski Resort is only 30 kilometers away from the Capital Airport. The grand and simple wooden imitation sculpture gate of the ski resort is only 600 meters away from the Liangshan station of the 918 bus route on the Shunping Road (Shunyi-Pinggu), with extremely smooth and convenient transportation.

Lianhua Mountain Ski Resort currently has seven ski runs for beginners, intermediate, and advanced skiers, one high mountain four-person chairlift, five rope tows, as well as a snow tube run, snowmobile track, snow football field, tennis court, and horse racing track, summer swimming and water play area, etc.

It also has a super-large parking lot of 60,000 square meters and a ski equipment rental hall of 12,000 square meters, green ecological garden restaurant. Green landscape hall, and uniquely styled Russian villas and other supporting facilities, which can provide visitors with super-valued accommodation and quality services. All ski boards are brand-new imported name-brand ski boards, which stands alone in the academic community of the capital city, unparalleled.

The spacious and bright green ecological restaurant and green landscape hall are surrounded by lush bamboo and willows, with babbling brooks, cascading waterfalls, and melodious bird songs, resembling a picturesque southern landscape painting; strolling among the greenery, looking out through the glass curtain wall, outside are the snow-covered mountains and the white whirlwinds stirred up on the ski runs. At Lianhua Mountain Ski Resort, the south meets the north, green meets white, achieving perfect harmony, and the white whirlwinds and green ecological rhythms jointly compose the most beautiful and strongest chapter of winter.
