"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode Plot Introduction - Film and Television Information - Xuntuwang

by yuliangnet on 2009-11-14 23:21:30

Episode 1

The story takes place in the early 1970s, in an northern mountain village called Shangliang.

The mother of the male protagonist Feng Jiachang was on her deathbed, and he was with his three younger brothers Tie Dan, Gou Dan, and Mao Dan by her bedside. With her last breath, she entrusted the family to him. His father was a son-in-law who had moved into his wife's home. After becoming disabled, he was even more looked down upon by the villagers.

The female protagonist Liu Hanxiang is the daughter of the village party secretary. One day, Hanxiang was stopped by a gang leader named Benzi at the school gate. Feng Jiachang came to her rescue like a hero, winning Hanxiang's heart. Pure love began to sprout in the hearts of these two young people from vastly different backgrounds.

Episode 2

After returning to the village, Feng Jiachang was determined to become a smart new farmer to change the face of his hometown. However, he was seen as an oddity by Liu Guodou, who found an excuse to send him to a study class. Hanxiang persuaded her father to let Feng Jiachang work as a part-time worker at a fertilizer factory in the city. At the fertilizer factory, Feng Jiachang performed excellently and even risked his life to fight a fire, earning merit. But his rural household registration completely shattered his dream of being a worker. To change his fate and escape the discrimination, and to become a true urban resident, he told Hanxiang that only joining the army could be the way out of the rural predicament. Hanxiang sacrificed her own innocence to help Feng Jiachang achieve his ideal.

Episode 3

In the new soldier training camp, Feng Jiachang formed a rivalry with urban soldier Tan Jianhua. Eager to show off, Feng Jiachang hid a broom in his bed to do extra labor first. To speak up for rural soldier Shi Xinuo, the two fought, all of which caught the attention of Commander Hu. Commander Hu sensed that Feng Jiachang was a good soldier but lacked experience, so he paid close attention and pushed him.

Hanxiang's classmate Bai Zhu came to her house in a car to propose marriage. Feng Jiachang's second brother Tie Dan was very angry and secretly punctured the car's tires. Afraid of getting into trouble, Tie Dan didn't dare to return home after dark. Hanxiang found Tie Dan, and Tie Dan begged Sister Hanxiang not to marry others or leave the village.
