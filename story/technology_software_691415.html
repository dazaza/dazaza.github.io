17-Year-Old Girl Traveled to Meet Internet Friend, Was Raped and Murdered (Photo)

by xue15jwsh on 2012-03-05 16:00:24

The suspect, Mr. Jin, accused of killing the teenage girl was apprehended (Photo by Zhou Mingjia). A 17-year-old girl named Wenwen (a pseudonym) came to Kunming to meet an internet friend but was raped and subsequently smothered to death in a hotel room. Yesterday, the Wuhua police provided answers: after the internet friend left for work, the lonely girl invited the friend's older brother over for a chat, which unexpectedly led to her demise. In another incident, an armed man driving while transporting drugs encountered police interception, resulting in him holding five people hostage while attempting to break through the checkpoint.

According to the police, last November 14th at noon, it was already past the check-out time, yet the teenage girl staying on the second floor of a certain hotel on Jiaoling Road had not come to check out. After knocking multiple times with no response, the staff opened the door with a spare key and found the girl still lying in bed. "I'm sorry, it's past the check-out time!" When the staff reminded her, there was no response. Upon closer inspection, the staff noticed the girl’s pale face and realized she had already ceased breathing.

After receiving the alarm, the Wuhua police arrived at the scene and discovered that the deceased was fully clothed, and the crime scene had been tidied up. The forensic doctor determined the cause of death as asphyxiation, with signs of sexual assault prior to death. Her clothes were put back on after she had died. Investigations revealed that the deceased was only 17 years old and from Tengchong, Yunnan Province.

Police investigations learned that the deceased worked as a waitress at a certain hotel in Yingjiang, Dehong, and on November 12th of last year, she traveled from Yingjiang to Kunming, telling her friends that she was coming to meet an internet friend.

Further investigation revealed that on the day the deceased met the internet friend, she also met the friend's older brother, Mr. Jin. Officers stated that during questioning, Mr. Jin appeared very calm, repeatedly claiming he had never been to the crime scene. However, evidence doesn't lie; Mr. Jin's DNA matched the DNA collected from the scene.

Faced with overwhelming evidence, Mr. Jin confessed to smothering her to death. It turns out that when Wenwen arrived in Kunming on November 12th, the internet friend intended to pick her up at the station but found his phone was dead, so he borrowed his older brother Jin's phone to contact Wenwen. They later ate together, during which Wenwen got to know Jin.

Jin confessed that he suddenly received a call from Wenwen inviting him to the hotel for a chat. They chatted until around two o'clock in the morning when the hotel had already closed. He then lay down with Wenwen fully clothed. An hour later, Wenwen fell asleep, but he couldn’t. Taking advantage of her lack of vigilance, he raped Wenwen and then suffocated her with a blanket.

Jin said that he did this because he feared Wenwen would tell his younger brother about the incident, leaving him unable to explain to his sibling. Since only the two of them knew about his visit to Wenwen, he believed that once she was dead, no one else would find out. This was his motive for murder.

Currently, Jin has been formally arrested on suspicion of intentional homicide.
