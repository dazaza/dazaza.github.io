Villager's 20-year search for wife, proving his innocence in her murder: Police to conduct DNA test

by zzfandsyb on 2011-07-11 13:03:15

The "undercover" successfully solved the case, and Yao Liangjun felt pretty good about it. It was this phone number that helped find Li Peixiang.

Tracking the story: Lei波's "Zhao Zuohai" spent 20 years seeking his wife to clear his name.

● Latest developments:

Lei波 police: Li Xingfa has been found, and DNA identification will be made soon.

At around 7 PM last night, Huaxi Metropolis Daily reporter got in touch with Ye Jianhua (phonetic), the director of Lei波 County Public Security Bureau. According to Ye Jianhua, after multiple investigations by the police officers, they found Li Xingfa, who had been "missing" for several days, in Yunnan yesterday. They also extracted blood samples from Li Xingfa.

According to Ye Jianhua, in order to clarify whether Li Xingfa and Li Fang are father and daughter, and to avoid wrongful convictions, they decided to conduct a DNA test on "Li Fang" and Li Xingfa. In mid-December, they sent police officers to Tianjin to extract the blood sample of "Li Fang." However, when the police were ready to extract Li Xingfa's blood sample, he "disappeared." Out of necessity, they dispatched multiple police officers to look for Li Xingfa. After sorting through Li Xingfa's social relationships layer by layer, the police finally found him at his daughter's home in Yunnan, where he had married into, and extracted Li Xingfa's blood sample. "Next, we will send these two blood samples to the state for authoritative DNA comparison, and then make relevant decisions based on the comparison results," said Ye Jianhua.

After persistently "seeking his wife and solving the case" for 20 years, Luo Kaiyou claimed that he had spent over a million yuan, but most of the attempts ended in failure. A turning point appeared in November this year. Within the following month, Luo Kaiyou not only got Li Peixiang's father to admit that "Li Peixiang is still alive," but also successfully found his ex-wife Li Peixiang, who was supposedly dead for 21 years, in Tianjin. "What people call a turning point is actually me hiring an 'undercover' agent to help me investigate the case," Luo Kaiyou said.

The person referred to as "undercover" by Luo Kaiyou is named Yao Liangjun, 47 years old, from Qingcheng Township, Yongshan County, Zhaotong City, Yunnan Province. Yesterday, Yao Liangjun first appeared at his home in Xiaochangtianba Community, Minsheng Village, Qingcheng Township. Through the accounts of both Luo Kaiyou and Yao Liangjun, the process of Luo Kaiyou's 20-year journey of "seeking his wife and investigating the case" to clear his name was recounted again, with stories full of legendary color.

Recruiting "Undercover"

Luo Kaiyou's encounter was truly unjust.

In April this year, Luo Kaiyou's eighth sister accidentally met Yao Liangjun, who later introduced him to Luo Kaiyou. After learning about Luo Kaiyou's situation, Yao Liangjun felt that Luo Kaiyou "was really wronged."

Later, Luo Kaiyou learned through acquaintances that although Yao Liangjun had served two sentences in labor reform during his youth and was just released, he turned himself in. Luo Kaiyou believed that for this reason, Yao Liangjun was still a "man who dared to take responsibility." Thus, Luo Kaiyou asked Yao Liangjun to help him investigate.

In June this year, Luo Kaiyou received a clue: Li Peixiang was deceived and taken away by two siblings, Luo Zhonghui (phonetic) and Luo Zhonghua. Now, Luo Zhonghui works in Beijing with her husband, while Luo Zhonghua was sentenced to 15 years in prison for abducting women and children and is still serving time.

Who should go to bring Luo Zhonghui back to Lei波? Yao Liangjun volunteered for the task. After more than a week, Yao Liangjun indeed brought Luo Zhonghui and her husband back from Beijing. After multiple negotiations and communications, although Luo Zhonghui refused to admit that she and her brother "sold" Li Peixiang, she expressed willingness to help Luo Kaiyou ask the Li family if "Li Peixiang is still alive." Regrettably, when Luo Zhonghui found Li Peixiang's mother, the mother was alerted by her family. When Lei波 police investigated Li Peixiang's mother, she excused herself with stomach pain and hid in the cornfield, never returning. However, Luo Kaiyou still learned from Luo Zhonghui's sister-in-law that Li Peixiang went to Tianjin and changed her name to Li Fang.

Breaking the deadlock again

A blank card lures Li Father

With the line to Li Mother cut off, Luo Kaiyou had to seek another breakthrough. After inquiries, despite being already 67 years old, Li Peixiang's father, Li Xingfa, was eager to get rich and had always been involved in konjac business.

Who should reach out to Li Xingfa? After repeated deliberations, the soon-to-be-released Luo Zhonghua came into focus. In November this year, Luo Zhonghua was released from prison, and Luo Kaiyou immediately found Luo Zhonghua. Similarly, although Luo Zhonghua refused to admit that he "sold" Li Peixiang, he agreed to help Luo Kaiyou.

In mid-November, Luo Zhonghua invited Li Xingfa to Huixi Town, Yongshan County, Yunnan Province. Prior to this, Yao Liangjun pretended to be a konjac boss and waited early in the town. After the three met, during their conversation, Yao Liangjun first mentioned a man named Yi Jialu (phonetic), asking him to help find Li Peixiang. If successful, he would receive 3 million yuan. "Finding Li Peixiang would yield so much money, why?" Seeing Li Xingfa interested, Yao Liangjun quickly used his skillful "boasting" ability, claiming that the conflict between Li Peixiang and Luo Kaiyou was actually unrelated to Li Peixiang. If Li Peixiang were found, she could receive "7 million yuan compensation from the state."

When the topic reached this point, Yao Liangjun suddenly changed the subject, claiming he was doing konjac business and pulled out a blank bank card from his wallet, saying: "There is one million yuan in here. Elder Li, you can take it first and help me collect konjac." Seeing Yao Liangjun's straightforwardness, Li Xingfa decided to visit Yao Liangjun's home for an "inspection."

On November 17th, Li Xingfa arrived at Yao Liangjun's house, and Yao Liangjun warmly entertained him. Simultaneously, Yao Liangjun immediately called Luo Kaiyou, informing him that Li Xingfa had arrived at his home. Upon receiving the call, Luo Kaiyou rushed to Yao Liangjun's house overnight. He hid in another room and directed Yao Liangjun to interrogate Li Xingfa.

"My daughter Li Peixiang is still alive now. After leaving the Luo family, my nephew Tong Guogui (phonetic), who worked at the Xuzhou Railway Bureau, introduced her to someone in Tianjin. I don't know where they live now..." That evening, Li Xingfa finally revealed the secret that had been buried for 21 years. Hearing these words in the adjacent room, Luo Kaiyou couldn't stop the tears from streaming down.

Number "Zou Da"

Proving Li Peixiang is in Tianjin

Although Li Xingfa opened up, he hadn't seen the real person yet. Luo Kaiyou decided to seize the opportunity and let Yao Liangjun take Li Xingfa to search for Li Peixiang.

On November 23rd, Yao Liangjun took Li Xingfa to Xuzhou to look for Tong Guogui, but they didn't find him, breaking the trail again. Yao Liangjun then incited Li Xingfa to call his seventh sister living in Yibin, saying he wanted to go to Tianjin to see Li Peixiang and his grandson. After the call was connected, the seventh sister of the Li family was very cautious, not only unwilling to talk but also repeatedly urging Li Xingfa to return to Sichuan quickly.

During the process of Li Xingfa taking out the address book to make calls, Yao Liangjun noticed that the first number in the address book was a landline with the area code 022, labeled as "Zou Da". He informed Luo Kaiyou at home about this situation. Luo Kaiyou found out that this number was from Tianjin. Considering that Li Xingfa had mentioned Li Peixiang being introduced to someone in Tianjin, Luo Kaiyou immediately required: "You go straight to Tianjin, then seek help from Tianjin police. Li Peixiang must be in Tianjin."

After arriving in Tianjin, the Tianjin police extended a helping hand and quickly traced the number to find a person named Shen Feng'ao, discovering that his wife was named Li Fang. It was later proven that this "Li Fang" was the former Li Peixiang. Afterwards, Lei波 police sent officers to Tianjin and successfully extracted the blood sample of "Li Fang."

Luo Kaiyou's 20-year journey of "seeking his wife and solving the case" to clear his name finally came to an end on this day.

What do the public security officers who participated in capturing the suspects of the "Luo Kaiyou killed his wife" case 20 years ago think today? Huaxi Metropolis Daily reporter will continue to reveal through interviews.
