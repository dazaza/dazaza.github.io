The heart of the Chinese people, the summer capital of the world.

by dalierhai on 2011-09-29 14:30:07

22.3 degrees, the average summer temperature. How many coastal towns in the world are so pleasant?

The average summer temperature in Dali is 22.3 degrees Celsius, and such a refreshing and pleasant temperature is very rare in all the cities with sea. It's almost the only one in the world! The climate is usually like early spring. Real estate in Dali, cold stops at coolness, and heat stops at warmth. When it is scorching, you can hide between Cang Mountain and Erhai Lake to enjoy the cool breeze. Dali deserves the reputation of the world's summer capital!

7 million tourists linger in Dali, and hundreds of thousands choose to live permanently between Cang Mountain and Erhai Lake.

Dali not only has a cultural heritage nurtured by thousands of years, but also has lake water as clear as the Lake District in England, and peaks as quiet as the Swiss Alps. The charm of Dali's health resort makes 7 million tourists come to Dali for vacation every year like migratory birds. Even more people choose to settle down and fall in love with Dali life.

100 trillion oxygen ions, how many peninsulas in the world are so healthy?

On the health peninsula by Erhai Lake, tens of thousands of negative oxygen ions per cubic centimeter keep your lungs comfortable. A 400-mu ecological wetland and an average humidity of 56% make your body and mind do aerobic yoga every day. The completely unpolluted green soil on the peninsula maximally activates life functions and relieves stress for the elderly and children.

The first of the top 10 livable small towns, can we find more such charming utopias in China?

Dali was selected as the champion among the top 10 livable small towns in China by authoritative Chinese media. Due to the humid nourishment of the sea water of the treasure Erhai Lake in Dali and the blocking of the continuous Cang Mountain against the cold air flow, this peninsula bay is warm and harmonious all year round. It is a utopia for living, enjoying the pure health of Cang Mountain and Erhai Lake.
