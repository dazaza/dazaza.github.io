Yuzhou Zunhai: Some Villas in the Project Have Been Capped, Special Price Units on Sale. During the Spring Festival, most projects chose to take a short break. However, the Yuzhou Zunhai project in Haicang District remained open to welcome customers for convenience. Reporters found during their visit to the construction site of the Yuzhou Zunhai project that some of the villas had already been capped.

Part of the villas have been capped. It is reported that the Yuzhou Zunhai project in Haicang District remained open to welcome customers during the Spring Festival and launched 15 special price units to thank its customers. The second group of Yuzhou Zunhai units are currently on sale, including buildings No.2 and No.3 with 90-120 square meters three-bedroom and four-bedroom units. Yuzhou Property has launched eight projects with gifts for viewing houses during the New Year and a car lottery for purchasing houses, with the Yuzhou Zunhai project also included in this event list.

[Project Link] Yuzhou Zunhai is located by the Future Lake next to the Sports Center on Xinggang Road in Haicang Bay. It is close to the World Trade Mall, large shopping plaza, and the 100,000 square meter Aluohai City Plaza. With the grandeur of a 500,000 square meter world-class seaside city, it enjoys the rare 24-kilometer coastline while possessing world-class seaside facilities such as the international west coast, Yundang Lake, the island road, and Hubei North. The project plans for duplexes, townhouses, sea-facing, and lakefront villas ranging from 280 to 380 square meters. Multiple high-rise buildings with 30-39 floors are built along the "bay," offering panoramic views of the sea from 75 to 135 square meter high-rise apartments. These include two-bedroom, three-bedroom, and four-bedroom duplexes, each enjoying a private expansive 400-meter wide view of the sea. Facing the sea, the lake, the park, and the sports center, Haicang Bay will always be the first row of landscapes, with permanent building distances and infinite views. The project boasts an uncommon 80,000 square meter Roman Byzantine-style garden with a green coverage rate of up to 76%. Customers can enjoy the luxurious European noble welcoming hall with a height of 5.9 meters, ultra-luxurious five-star double swimming pools, and a 3,500 square meter Roman royal sunken square. There's also a Roman imperial palace-level club offering a tailor-made luxurious life for the nobility.
