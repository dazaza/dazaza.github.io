Introduction to Season 1 of The Sarah Connor Chronicles

The story is set between the second and third movies. The main plot follows the Connor mother and son as they continue to evade robot assassins. After being transformed by the baptism of fire in the second movie, Sarah has become a thoroughly strong woman. In Episode 6 of Season 1 of The Sarah Connor Chronicles, due to her having destroyed the embryonic "Skynet" with the help of T800, it seems like they can lead a peaceful life. But how could the screenwriters let them rest? Aren't they two living people? They are clearly a huge gold mine waiting to be exploited! The killer robots quickly find the whereabouts of the mother and son, and the protective robots quickly take their positions, making the start of the series bustling with activity. However, if we look beyond the temporary "excitement" and examine the background of the series, The Sarah Connor Chronicles hides enormous crises.

The story of Terminator 6 takes place in 2011, while the second movie happened in 1997. The series starts from 1999 and through the help of robots time-travels to 2007 (American TV shows generally run parallel to real time). Terminator 6 is undoubtedly the biggest obstacle for The Sarah Connor Chronicles. The essence of American TV dramas lies entirely in the script, and the key to the script is suspense. The existence of Terminator 6 eliminates this suspense, so in a way, The Sarah Connor Chronicles has already ended. FOX Network has currently only purchased the broadcasting rights for 16 episodes. Once these 16 episodes are over, whether the series will continue remains uncertain.
