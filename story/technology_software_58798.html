Real life is already filled with danger and intrigue, and the online world is also rife with overt grabs for power and covert arrows, worms, trojans, pigeons, pandas... leaving you constantly on guard. The battle between hackers and "meat chickens" (vulnerable users) has never ceased. However, today's "meat chickens" have grown stronger, no longer as easily defeated as they once were, without any ability to fight back. If you ask what their secret weapons are, the "Seven Weapons" will accompany you through this "jianghu"!

1 Eternal Life Sword

Encryption software - laughter. No matter how great the difficulty, always laugh!

"A person who knows how to use their own strengths does not need martial arts to knock someone down." The strength of encryption software is "laughter" - no matter how powerful the virus opponent, it cannot match that captivating "laugh".

The "laugh" of encryption software is its unmatched "encryption algorithm". No matter how cunning or strong the hacker may be, I leave no gaps. Even if encryption software cannot counteract viruses or trojans, it can still bring down intruders. The currently popular international "RSA" algorithm has been recognized as an unbreakable code. Both the American PGP encryption software and the domestic "Big Wolf Dog Encryption Expert" use this algorithm to encrypt data. Encrypted ciphertext using the "RSA" algorithm cannot be deciphered by thieves even if they spend their entire lives trying. Therefore, I list encryption software as the first of the seven weapons.
