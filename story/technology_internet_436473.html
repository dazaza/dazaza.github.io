The newly enlightened Tianjin Station

by fkflr641 on 2011-05-24 16:57:26

A real and true local group buying guide in Zhengzhou. Keep it as a treasure, take a look at it when you have nothing to do every day, it can save you a lot of money.

Meituan: Really only one deal per day. Miss it and it's gone.

Duitang: A group buying site started by a former Google manager. Recently, they offered a hot deal on 20 yuan movie tickets for China Film Group Cinemas. Now they're offering IMAX Harry Potter tickets, worth keeping an eye on.

Lashou: Seems to be the website with the highest national traffic among group-buying sites. Please reply to me about weight loss products. The stuff from the Tianjin station is relatively ordinary. After raffling off houses, now it's laptops. I wonder what they'll raffle off in a few days.

58 Group Buying: A local information website. Mainly various food options. Currently offering grilled fish, Thai cuisine, hairy crabs, and even sphygmomanometers and other daily necessities.

Dianping Group Buying: Everyone knows Dianping, right? But since its group-buying channel opened, it has been continuously selling movie tickets for StarCinema. They've already sold over 50,000 tickets.

24 Coupon: Started a bit late but has already launched its Zhengzhou station.

RV Group: Newly discovered, quite cost-effective, suitable for all skin types, makeup methods, merchants grouped and services are very good.

Nuomi: From the same owner as Xiaonei. The Tianjin station quietly opened. Movie tickets for 9 yuan at Left Bank Cinema, performance tickets for 39 yuan. Worth checking out.

Redog Group: Should be a local company. The customer service phone number has finally been made available. Group buying is still quite suitable, worth paying attention to. Flash sales have stopped. There are 5-6 group buys each day.

Zantai Group Buying: This group buying updates about once a week. The items are all sorts of strange things.

Bali Group: More lottery than actual goods. Today they're even selling WSJ. Usually just small household items.

Fanti Group, Fantong Group: Under the Fantong network, previously used for restaurant reservations, now turned to group buying, under observation.

Ayao Group: Thought their previous group buys were pretty good. Now the items are average.

Belief Group: Don't know if it's related to Belief Bar. Mostly home and cosmetics.

Like Group: Cosmetics, home items, little gadgets, all kinds of creative stuff.

Tuan: Should be a small company. Occasionally releases some new items. Give it a try.

First Group Buying Network: Hmm. Not sure how to describe this network. Always selling discounts and coupons for websites. Now selling car rental coupons. Might come in handy someday... Later found out that it's actually quite popular nationwide. The Tianjin branch is pretty normal.

Qingtuan: Hmm. As I mentioned earlier, these few have new goods every day. Mostly cosmetics.

Joint Purchase Network: Has always been group buying cosmetics and discount coupons. The current Illy coffee beans are not bad.

Jintuan Network: Saw it once at Nankai University. At that time, group-buying websites weren't so popular yet. It was comforting to suddenly find one in Tianjin. However, since it opened, I haven't seen anything too suitable.

Longfeng Group: Seen by chance, talking about something called a "bourgeois life".

Juqi Network: Newly seen today, seems to have just opened on December 6th.

Hotehui: Newly launched Tianjin station.

Food Group: A few days ago, they were selling cups ranging from 24 to 105 yuan. Unfortunately, I didn't manage to grab one.
