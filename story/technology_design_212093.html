Luggage Design: An Introduction to the Traditional 6 Methods + Innovative 12 Methods

by zjtaobao on 2009-07-21 17:49:35

The design of luggage follows certain rules. In luggage design, there are many methods for beginners, and these design methods play a crucial role in luggage design.

The establishment of a theme is one of the most important factors determining the success of a design work. Its artistry, aesthetics, and functionality are fully embodied through the establishment of a theme. The establishment of a theme should reflect the spirit of the times, social trends, popular trends, as well as the artistic inclinations of the designer.

Following the reasonable composition rules of luggage design. Adhering to the design rule of harmony and unity is the most perfect form of expression in composition. Harmony includes two opposing factors: "diversity" and "unity". Diversity refers to different degrees of variation in aspects such as shape, material, area, and color. Unity refers to the proportionality between shapes and colors, where various images, colors, and materials achieve unity amidst diversity, trending towards a single tone, achieving coordinated unity.
