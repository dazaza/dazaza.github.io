The refurbishing of iPhone 4 is getting more and more serious, with buy-back prices as high as 4400 yuan.

by anonymous on 2012-03-03 11:41:43

The iPhone 4 is still one of the hottest phones on the market.

"Ifans Club" is a small stall at E-Kaogao Flagship Plaza specializing in Apple products. The owner, Yang Bo, has been selling digital products for decades and became an "Apple fan" (guo fen) several years ago, switching to selling Apple products exclusively. In Hangzhou's "Apple fan" community, his little shop is quite well-known. "If you're in a rush to buy an Apple phone, I suggest you buy the 8GB iPhone 4. Don't buy the 16GB or 32GB versions anymore," Yang revealed some market "inside information" after chatting with the reporter: "Recently, the iPhone 4 market has been chaotic. Not only are prices ridiculously high, but there's also a significant chance that what you buy could be a second-hand device."

The refurbishment of iPhone 4s is becoming increasingly rampant.

After walking around the digital market, the reporter noticed many counters displaying signs for "high-price回收 (huishou - buy back) iPhone 4" and "high-price回收 (huishou - buy back) old phones." When the reporter took out their own iPhone 4, it wasn't long before merchants approached asking if they wanted to sell their phone. "High-price回收 (huishou - buy back)" was offered.

"A while ago, a customer bought an iPhone 4 from me that had some issues with the power port. After using it for two months, they returned it to me. I kept it here after getting it back from the Shanghai Apple Service Center until yesterday when I sold it to someone who buys used iPhones. Guess how much I sold it for?" Mr. Yang mysteriously told the reporter, "4400 yuan! I made a few hundred more than my purchase price!"

It is understood that these recovered phones are taken to be refurbished into "refurbished units." So-called refurbished units involve replacing the front glass and rear shell of old Apple phones, repackaging them, and selling them as new units. "They can afford to pay high prices for them, which means the profit must be substantial," Mr. Yang informed the reporter. Typically, recovery merchants will sell the phones to professional refurbishers at a price 200-300 yuan higher than the recovery price. These professional refurbishers then bulk-buy original or high-quality imitation Apple parts, including front glass costing about 100 yuan per piece and rear shells costing around 80 yuan each, followed by simple packaging procedures. Refurbished units can then re-enter the market for sale. "Refurbishers can make at least 500 yuan per unit, sometimes even over a thousand," he said.

There are generally two types of sales channels for refurbished units: online sales and market sales. Online sales of refurbished units may include advance notices of "simple handling," with prices lower than new units; however, those sold through social channels are directly marketed as new units, leaving consumers unaware.

"The reason refurbished units are so prevalent is due to the massive demand for Apple phones," Mr. Yang explained. Since the release of the new iPhone 4S, Apple officially adjusted the price of the 16GB version of the iPhone 4 to 4548 yuan. However, with the discontinuation of the 16GB and 32GB versions of the iPhone 4, the legitimate channel began experiencing shortages, causing gray-market prices to soar, leading to a situation where "one unit is hard to find." "In September, the price of the iPhone 4 dropped to its lowest point at 3900 yuan, then rose back to 4200 yuan in October, and continued to climb, reaching a high of over 5000 yuan. Even now, the price of a refurbished unit is still at 4800 yuan!"

Currently, purchasing the 8GB version of the iPhone 4 is relatively cost-effective.

Given the severe issues with iPhone 4 refurbished units, how does the newly released iPhone 4S fare? Upon inquiry, the reporter learned that the official domestic version of the iPhone 4S has not yet hit the market, with only gray-market versions available, typically priced around 5600 yuan, which is a bit expensive.

Mr. Yang said that based on his experience, the price of the iPhone 4S won't drop anytime soon, likely not until March next year when it might reach a normal level. "If you're really in a hurry to buy an Apple phone, the relatively affordable option would be the 8GB version of the iPhone 4. Currently, the domestic market price is around 4200 yuan." It is understood that the memory of the 8GB version of the iPhone 4 remains at 512MB. Compared to the 16GB and 32GB versions of the iPhone 4, there isn't much difference in appearance or configuration, and it runs the latest iOS 5 operating system.
