Part One: About the Movie

Concept Explanation: "Golden Ceremony" is based on the Chinese Management Talent Forum, selecting five outstanding enterprise management representatives who have been honored as Chinese management talents nationwide as the main characters, telling their different paths of growth. From the initial ideals, the journey of struggle, the belief in success, personal growth and transformation... using actual locations, real names, and real scenes for a comprehensive 360° shoot, perfectly combining the virtual movie world with reality. Let everyone understand the survival and development strategies of successful people, allowing hundreds of millions of viewers across the country to jointly feel the hardships, difficulties, joys, and happiness behind successful individuals, truly experiencing the profound truth that "a sword's sharpness comes from honing, and plum blossoms' fragrance emerges after enduring bitter cold." Focusing on showcasing the glorious deeds of Chinese enterprise managers, demonstrating their innovative management concepts and tenacious spirit of struggle, integrating corporate brands and corporate culture into the plot using the method of "Big Shots," inviting numerous winners of the Chinese Management Talent Forum to participate in performances, adopting the approach of "The Founding of a Republic" to depict the brilliant lives of enterprise management elites, thus reflecting the development trajectory of Chinese-style management science.

Artistic Expression: Using a movie within a movie, combining realistic images with fictional ones, leaving space between reality and dreams; embedding stories within stories, where prototype characters watch their own stories, cleverly designing suspense, leading everyone into the story... Using the Paris I Love You and All City Hot Love style block structure storytelling method, each block focuses on a theme: great love, integrity, friendship, responsibility, and the belief in struggle, then interpreting it from the perspective of the female protagonist, presenting a unique world of successful individuals...

Plot Summary: To celebrate its tenth anniversary Golden Ceremony, the Chinese Management Talent Forum widely recruits talented individuals from society. Du Xiran, a student of the art department, was exceptionally admitted due to her outstanding creative talent. After entering, Du Xiran receives a "mysterious task," which she gladly accepts and begins pondering deeply... She draws some character sketches and contemplates them, wondering what the core of their success is. At this moment, there is a knock on the door: "Xiran, how are the materials for the hundred Chinese management talent figures coming along? 'He' wants to see you." (Who is he? Setting up suspense.)

Xiran takes out her data folder and enters his office. She sees a torn map and squats down to examine the front and back of the map before quickly piecing it together. At this point, a door opens slightly, and a very magnetic male baritone voice can be heard: "When the person is right, your world is right." Xiran ponders the meaning of these words. The reverse side of the map turns out to be a portrait, possibly indicating that while it may be difficult to piece together the fragmented world map, finding the correct person to assemble the portrait makes the world map come together as well. Xiran looks at this somewhat mysterious figure and smiles firmly and as if she has found a treasure.

The magnificent Golden Ceremony event scene on TV, a grand piano concerto sounds, and Xiran squeezes through the crowd to see the smiles of several real-life individuals whose portraits hang in her room. Xiran approaches one of the successful individuals and then hands him a mysterious box; the successful person elegantly opens it, initially shocked, then thoughtfully leads Xiran forward (filmed realistically but abstractly, representing a natural path, green symbolizing vitality and struggle, gold symbolizing ideals)... This successful individual essentially brings Xiran into his own world of struggle.

Xiran picks up another differently colored mysterious box and walks into the world of another person we aim to portray...

Xiran finally finds the key to completing the task: the gaze of successful people - resilient, perceptive, never giving up, daring to lead, persistently pursuing, grounded yet humble and kind... As Xiran looks at the completed one hundred portraits of Chinese management talent figures, the faces of the real individuals appear before her eyes... (The mysterious mastermind appears, satisfied, and tells Xiran, "You've done an excellent job!")

After the ceremony, Xiran sees the golden sunshine in her life, believing more firmly that having a dream means pursuing it, not getting stuck outside of reality... Facing love, she breaks down the walls she had set up in her heart and moves forward bravely. Believing: every day the sun is new, through struggle, everything is possible.
