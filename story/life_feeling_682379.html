Cherish the people around you

This matter has been buried in my heart for a long time. Over the years, besides regret and hatred, I feel more difficult to speak out loud, feeling that as a man, I used to be so insignificant...

At that time, I was in my sophomore year. Not long after enrollment, Hua from my class started to have good feelings towards me. Maybe it was because I was relatively handsome, or maybe it was because I liked literature and often published articles in the community. Hua's affection towards me was completely unreserved. She invited me to watch movies, found me to talk nonsense, almost all the classmates could feel it.

Perhaps it is the common ailment of all literary youths, what I pursued was love at first sight, and Hua, she looked too ordinary, couldn't arouse any interest in me at all. And I didn't directly reject her, kept this kind of ambiguous relationship with her, because there was still a bit of hypocrisy in my subconscious, I liked the feeling of being pursued and admired.

When I entered my sophomore year, several buddies in the dormitory had girlfriends. Every weekend, I was often the only one left, bored to death. At every nightly meeting, they would talk about the various gentleness and benefits of their respective girlfriends, making me feel itchy inside. The girlfriend of the dorm leader could make cold dishes, though it was just ordinary cold dishes, but the dorm leader ate them with relish and endless aftertaste, saying that they were much better than the canteen food in our school. And the third dorm mate's girlfriend, used the money she saved by eating frugally to buy him a windbreaker. The third dorm mate said: "The weather in the north is windy and big, a girl, not rich at home, rather than dress herself, bought me a windbreaker, what else should I be dissatisfied with?" Listening to their words, I couldn't interject, I could only listen. At this moment, the fifth dorm mate spoke up: "What you guys said are not real. In high school, my girlfriend let me kiss her, and we've already...". Everyone teased: "Already what? What does that mean?" The fifth dorm mate loudly said: "We went to bed together, are you satisfied now? But then the fifth dorm mate smirked, "That's impossible. How could I let my beloved take risks!" It turned out to be a false alarm.

At that moment, I also wanted to find a girlfriend to comfort each other! Especially when I noticed that my parents' feelings had problems, my heart was filled with bitterness.

Days continued to pass slowly, however, one day, a news broke out in the college: A female student from the third grade of the foreign language department got pregnant accidentally, her boyfriend cut off their relationship and fell in love with another person. The girl felt ashamed and jumped from the dormitory building!

Just before going to sleep, everyone talked about this incident. Some said that the boy was worse than beasts, without any sense of responsibility; others said that the girl was too foolish, didn't know how to protect herself. The fifth dorm mate sat up on his bed: "Today, I will give you something, quite a few people in our dormitory have girlfriends. Remember, no matter when, you must pay attention to the safety of the other party." We thought he was going to give us something, and everyone got up from the bed waiting, unexpectedly, it was condoms! "If the boys could take more responsibility, the incident like the one in the foreign language department wouldn't happen. If anyone in us gets into trouble in this aspect, I will never forgive him..." Originally, the brothers wanted to tease the fifth dorm mate with these condoms, after hearing what the fifth dorm mate said, everyone fell silent, the condom in their hands suddenly became heavy.

Afterwards, the second dorm mate also had a girlfriend, while I became a loner. Sometimes I couldn't even find someone to talk to. That day was my birthday, I didn't tell anyone. I went to class as usual, and Hua suddenly stopped me: "Happy Birthday!" and handed me a gift, an exquisite diary book. I only remembered mentioning my birthday casually when chatting with her, and she remembered it, a sense of moved filled my whole body.

Maybe, Hua's care came at the right time, maybe, my lonely feelings wanted to find a port to anchor. From then on, I seemed to change my view on Hua. Although she wasn't pretty, she was considerate. Although she couldn't write poetic articles, she could sing songs that made people drunk. More importantly, Hua actively pursued me. I once read such a sentence in a book: "If a girl takes the initiative, a boy can save a lot of trouble." I admit, a large part of the reason I could accept Hua was my emptiness. I am a weak hunter.

At that time, there was a "Couple Video Hall" not far from the school. Students often went there, and I also went with the second dorm mate. The video hall was divided into individual "private rooms", because the sound insulation effect was not very good, adjacent private rooms could hear each other's voices. Most of the time, the video hall played romance films, sometimes some adult films, those films needed to wait until late at night when it was quiet and someone requested them. And at this time, there would always be a pair of men and women in the adjacent private room making sounds that seemed to match the picture, making people feel restless all over.

On weekends, when Hua and I passed by that video hall, looking at Hua's face full of infatuation, I suddenly wanted to watch videos with her, to test her feelings towards me. Moreover, the condom given by the fifth dorm mate I never had the chance to use. When I hesitantly asked Hua if she wanted to watch videos, I didn't expect that she agreed without hesitation. Because almost all the classmates believed that as long as you go there, the relationship must be special.

Next, we went to the video hall, and during the video watching, I remembered safety at any cost. And in my mind, even if we broke up in the future, there would be no aftermath, no too much responsibility. On the way, she appeared shy and cute, which further aroused a certain desire in me. In the video hall, when the screen showed the scene of a naked couple embracing, I and her couldn't control ourselves either... Afterward, I wanted to throw away the condom, but she didn't allow it. She said it was the evidence of her first time, and she had finally been with the boy she adored.

However, real life is full of variables. At a social gathering, I met a girl named Xin from another department. We shared similar interests, and I quickly couldn't extricate myself. In front of Xin, I no longer had that primitive desire, but only respect for each other and yearning for literature...

But, what about her? We had done so many intimate things between us, how could Hua accept this reality all at once? But I truly loved Xin, that feeling was what I didn't have when I was with her, young me felt the difficulty of choice for the first time.

That night, when I took her out and hesitatingly told her the whole story, she first froze, then stared directly into my eyes: "Since you don't love me, why did you do that with me?" Her voice wasn't loud, but each word hit my heart like a heavy hammer. I didn't dare to meet her gaze, only feeling that I was a faithless person, unworthy of her true feelings.

Hua soon became thin, classmates only knew that we broke up, but wouldn't think that Hua and I had already had that kind of relationship. Because we were in the same class, we couldn't avoid seeing each other. Every time Hua saw me, she pretended not to see me, and I never dared to look her in the eye. But I could deeply feel that under her seemingly strong exterior, there was almost a desperate sadness... Because of a temporary loneliness, I hurt an innocent person, and I even used the condom given by the fifth dorm mate! I couldn't help but strongly blame myself deep down. Thinking of how Hua had once so infatuatedly preserved the condom we used, I had an indescribable taste in my heart...

And me and Xin, after experiencing a passionate love, our romance also faded with the end of university life. Even though it has been many years since graduation, I still can't forget her matters. She was not the main theme of my life, but she was an indispensable interlude in my life. Even when I got married, had children, and became a real man, I still couldn't let go of that experience, and had a kind of hatred deep in my heart that needed to be compensated.

Originally, condoms represented sincerity in love, but I, thinking back to how despicable I was using it as a tool to vent my frustration, superficially to protect the other party, under the guise of "love", actually for an unspeakable purpose, it was my game in sexual issues, a kind of irresponsibility. Hua's forgiveness towards me still makes me sigh and feel ashamed today. She and my love were irrelevant, but she was always tied to my conscience.

In the youthful years, that sad condom, because of a temporary loneliness, it has always been a knot in my heart, it made me understand how to love, cherish, take care of, protect, and treasure.
