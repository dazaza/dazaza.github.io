I've already cherished you, but you...

by mimi456 on 2012-02-10 00:06:57

I already cherish you, but you……

Actually, I really value you.

Why do I feel that you are indifferent to everything I do for you? Nowadays, I really don't know what to do to make you sincere to me. Maybe, we shouldn't have met in the first place. We are completely two different personalities of people, and we won't be happy together. You are the kind of person who can easily get close to others, while I, except for a few intimate friends, the rest are just ordinary friends.

The type of girl I like is the pure one. Do you know why I like snow? Because it has this quality.

Christmas is my favorite holiday. It might be because it usually snows during Christmas! I like the atmosphere of Christmas, many stores are decorated with white ornaments, couples exchange gifts, wearing tall Christmas hats, and they are very sweet together...

A long time ago, I told you that I like Christmas, but did you take it to heart? Do you know why I told you that I like Christmas? I just want to be with you, even if sometimes there's nothing to do when I'm with you, but as long as I'm with you, I am very happy. But now, I don't dare to find you anymore, because in your heart, I am even less important than an ordinary friend. Sometimes when I want to see you, I ask you to have dinner with me. At first, you went with me a few times, then you said you wanted to have dinner with your roommate, and finally, you said: "Just go have dinner by yourself, why call me?" I was silent, I didn't know what to say...

Today is Christmas Eve, I thought you would spend it with me, because before, I said I really like Christmas Eve, because the reason I like Christmas is because of Christmas Eve. But, I really didn't expect that you would hang out with your classmates, who haven't even graduated yet. I have nothing to say, in your heart, a small gathering is more important than my favorite holiday. What I want to say is, if you like a certain holiday the most, even if I have a gathering or other things on that day, I will refuse them to spend the holiday with you, this is the difference between us.

At noon today, after I finished class, I went to buy a gift, I wanted to make you happy, because when I'm with you, if you're happy, I'm happy. It seems like fate played a joke on me, it just happens that you have a classmate gathering today.

I guess I've been holding you back! Although a kite needs the restriction of a string to fly longer, without the string, at least it can fly freely...

I think, I can't let you go now! In fact, there's nothing that can't be let go. When it hurts, you naturally let go. I think, I'm not hurt too deeply now, otherwise how could I not let go?

I love you, you are my whole world; I don't love you, I am me, and you are still you...
