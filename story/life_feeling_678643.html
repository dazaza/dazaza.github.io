Only after loving and getting hurt, do you know the pain...

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-27 15:53:22

Loved, hurt, and then realized the pain........

Once upon a time, I thought you loved me and I loved you so much, but I don't know why our love also passed its expiration date......

The love in the world is countless. But only true love is the only one. Always believe that true love knows no age. Praying for the day when true love will come to me. With great expectation, that day finally came.

Silly, I think I'm the happiest girl in the world because I have someone I can rely on. As long as I can snuggle up to you, no matter how hard life is, I feel very happy. Love spreads little by little, so I bathe in the river of love.

Happiness overflows, and the air is filled with the taste of love. I wish it could continue forever.

Maybe I was too naive, really thought our love could last forever. As time goes by, we gradually mature and have a deeper understanding of love. Gradually, I no longer feel our love, and there's no longer the taste of love around me. I feel that the current love makes me so tired that I can't breathe. And you, you don't have the courage to accept love.

I feel that our love is on the verge of death, and I can feel the smell of death. I really want to go back to the past, back to our childish days, that innocent love, pure liking, warm happiness. But I know that day will never come...

Slowly, I started to like listening to sad love songs, feeling lost in life. Our love is like melting ice cream, it's sweet, but it doesn't have that clarity anymore. I don't know if this kind of love should continue or be immediately cut off. I've given a lot, but all I get is disappointment after disappointment. I feel you are gradually moving away from me, the distance between us is getting farther and farther. Your love means nothing to me, so stop making promises and saying "I love you, I love you, I love you...".

What exactly made me end up in this worse-than-death situation? Can you set aside your so-called face, dignity, and cowardice, use your courage, wholeheartedly, and unwaveringly love me, without caring about others' opinions? The former you, on QQ, would be the last one to log off because you wanted to see my avatar turn gray before you felt at ease.

Today's you, disappears inexplicably, no longer giving me the same sense of security as before. In this relationship, how much real love is left? Can you give me a definite look, make me feel that loving you is meaningful? Don't give me a cold expression, an indifferent behavior.

I can change for you, accept your strengths and weaknesses. But why can't you change yourself well for me? Don't just take my efforts towards you and pretend to be uninvolved. I am not a beggar in love, I don't need your generosity towards love, I just need your sincerity, your good love and cherish me.

Is it my repeated tolerance towards you that has turned into indulgence, making you ignore my existence and treat me as a passerby in your life? No, this is not the result I want. I don't want my efforts and waiting to end up with this heartbreaking result.

Now, what can I believe in to affirm that there is still true love in this world? Although I cannot confirm that you don't love me, although you still confidently tell me you love me, I feel that your love is like a bright rose, slowly wilting.

Loved, hurt, and then realized the pain........
