Precautions for buying cross stitch

There are now many types of embroidery cloth. If you're buying cross stitch on Taobao, relatively speaking, you only need to check the quality of the cloth. However, I want to draw everyone's attention to what to note when purchasing embroidery cloth separately.

Typically, we feel that as long as we measure the size well before buying, we can purchase suitable embroidery cloth. Actually, this is not correct. We also need to pay attention to the distance between each small grid. Some grids are larger, in which case we need to choose a correspondingly larger cloth. Some grids are smaller, in which case we need to make sure the size of the cloth is reduced accordingly.

This is based on my personal experience. Last time when I bought the dripping rose cross stitch, I didn't pay attention to the grid size, and after starting to embroider a few stitches, I realized it was wrong. Luckily, I hadn't embroidered too much; otherwise, all my efforts would have been wasted. I hope everyone will also pay attention to this issue in the future.

Additionally, when pairing the color of the embroidery cloth, it's best to choose a cloth with a color difference from the embroidery thread. This makes the finished embroidery stand out more. For example, if most of the threads are red, pairing it with a red cloth makes it hard to see. My first finished cross stitch suffered from this problem because I didn't notice at the time. Now looking back, I should have paid more attention. After all, the thread and cloth form an integral part of the finished product, both directly presented to us.

When selecting, we should pay attention to the following points:

1. Clarify the purpose (for personal use or as a gift)

2. Finished cross stitch products or semi-finished ones awaiting your own embroidery

If you want to embroider yourself, apart from choosing what you like, you should also consider whether you can complete it. Some pictures look good but are very troublesome to embroider, with many thread colors, taking a long time to finish! Such tasks can be exhausting, and if you can't complete them, you've wasted time. Meanwhile, others may have already completed the product, although without the fun, lazy people can still own a good treasure!

3. Pay attention to the details of the item (specifications and the type of embroidery thread used)

There are two types: domestic and imported threads. Domestic threads tend to break easily during embroidery (they are more fragile), and the back of the finished embroidery will appear with many joints and overlapping parts, increasing the thickness of the embroidery piece and making it uneven, affecting the overall effect!

Compared to domestic threads, imported threads have more luster and almost no quality issues. Therefore, the back of the finished embroidery looks neat (this also depends on the skills of the person doing the embroidery!). When stored for a long time and needs washing, there won't be any problems with the threads falling off.

Therefore, there appears a significant price difference among similar goods. You get what you pay for!

Actually, the embroidery cloth is also crucial. Poor quality cloth can affect the overall beauty of the embroidery piece!

4. Picture frame accessories

Some sellers include picture frames when selling, bringing convenience to buyers. However, remember that picture frames are heavier than a piece of embroidery cloth. If the frame isn't good, it's not worth spending extra money. Negotiate with the seller to only sell the embroidery piece, or choose your own preferred frame.

5. Reality vs. illusion of cross stitch patterns

Make sure the posted photos are of the original finished embroidery, not just the product pattern! The actual result might differ significantly!

6. Personally, I love embroidering cross stitch. There are also sales in small shops, but I noticed that generally, the prices of finished cross stitch sold by common sellers are quite high. Apart from the distinction between domestic and imported threads, some may aim to make a large profit (some aren't personally made but acquired from the market, which is pure profiteering since they pay embroiderers very little). Some sell their painstakingly crafted pieces for too low a price, which is also regrettable. Therefore, appropriate pricing is better. Otherwise, the seller sets a higher price than what's available on the market, losing its significance. Friends coming to Taobao all want to buy cheap, practical, and high-quality items while enjoying the pleasure of treasure hunting. Therefore, buyers of cross stitch must have keen eyesight!
