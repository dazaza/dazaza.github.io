The last words of Hu Wenhai, the murderer from Shanxi: If you are Chinese, please forward.

by yang5230 on 2009-12-04 17:19:14

Police: Do you know why we arrested you?  ,知己(To those who know how to cherish);   Hu Baihai: I know, I killed someone.     Police: Killed one person? You killed fourteen!     Hu Wenhai: Not fourteen, right?     Police: Then how many did you kill?     Hu Wenhai: I forgot, it was seventeen.     Police: Fourteen people died!     Hu Wenhai: The ones I forgot were still alive. I checked them all, if they looked like they weren't dead yet, I gave them two more shots. —— That's not killing cleanly.     Police: Do you know the consequences?     Hu Wenhai: (with a face full of flattery) I know, I know, I'll pay with my life.     Police: Do you regret it?     Hu Wenhai: How could I not regret it? There was a kid who didn't seem like a killer, but when you said it, I realized that person was just visiting. Also, I didn't kill everyone I should have.     Police: Who else do you want to kill?     Hu Wenhai: Just the women from those few families.     Police: Why do you want to kill them?     Hu Wenhai: When the village collected funds and exempted fees, three years of embezzlement and corruption amounted to at least five million yuan. They sold three coal mines, but only handed over two. When I reported this in town, no one cared. They hated me so much that they wanted to kill me. On June 19th, 1999, I was watering fields outside, and XXX brothers (another family was slaughtered) even argued with me, striking me on the head with an iron shovel three times. I had to get dozens of stitches. If my head wasn't hard, they would have chopped me to death by then. XXX (village accountant) sent someone to find me, wanting to settle with money, but I refused. From that point on, I decided to start killing, originally planning to act on New Year's Eve, as everyone would be watching the Spring Festival Gala, making it easier to kill cleanly. On June 19th, I called XXX (the accountant) and the village bookkeeper to write down how much they had embezzled, but they didn't cooperate. At that time, there was no sound of police sirens (indicating police cars), XX (accountant) became arrogant, pointing at his own forehead saying: "Baihai, if you have the guts, shoot here." So I shot him in the forehead, killing him. He thought I wouldn't dare. With no other choice, I had to act early... When the reporter asked again if he regretted it, he firmly replied: "No regrets, nothing to regret! It's just regrettable that I didn't kill everyone I should have." The phrase about regrets and not killing cleanly was mentioned multiple times by Hu Wenhai in different settings. It is analyzed that he was worried about leaving behind troubles for his daughter-in-law and grandchildren.

When asked why he also killed children, he reasonably explained: "If I didn't kill them, they would grow up to bully my child..."

During the court trial of Hu Wenhai, he sat upright, quietly reading his self-defense statement, just like a rural model citizen giving a speech. Another co-defendant accused of assisting in murder was also being tried alone. During the cross-examination, Hu defended by saying his friend did not kill anyone and had been trying to persuade him not to. At that time, the prosecution pointed out that one of the victims (who faked his death) testified that his friend had struck him with an axe. Hu countered: "I shot him and he fell to the ground, he never turned back to look. I picked up the axe and struck him." The prosecution further pointed out that his friend used pliers on the victim. Hu Wenhai immediately defended: "I forced him with a gun to do it, if he hadn't, I would have beaten him severely." Boldly taking all responsibility.

Before sentencing Hu Baihai to death, as he was leaving the courtroom, he grabbed the hand of a police officer guarding him, repeatedly saying: "Step back, step back." Despite the number of police present, they couldn't avoid him, truly humorous.

On December 25, 2001, which also happened to be Christmas day for Westerners, the Shanxi Jinzhong court legally held a public trial for the three defendants involved in the mass shooting case where 14 people were killed. In the end, two were sentenced to death, and one received a fixed-term imprisonment. During the final statement according to legal procedures, the first defendant Hu Wenhai stated: "I was born in the old society, grew up under the red flag... I hope to become a decent and kind person, constantly striving to achieve my ideals. My straightforward personality leads me to speak the truth and take responsibility for my actions. The corrupt village officials and I clashed frequently, sometimes turning me into their pawn. However, in recent years, the embezzlement and bribery by village cadres, their oppression of the common folk, resulted in the division of over 40 million yuan from enterprises like large coal mines (where villagers risked their lives). Over four years, I repeatedly reported these issues to relevant departments, but received no response. Officials at various levels, including maternal and child health, discipline inspection, prosecution, counties, cities, and districts, gave us cold shoulders and disdain... Where can we go to seek justice? Who will stand up for us? When I reported to the maternal and child health department, those civil servants who only cared about agricultural supplies drove around in big vehicles worth over 300,000 yuan, too busy to handle cases, even colluding with villagers to oppress ordinary people... I had no choice but to resort to violence, I had to protect the interests of the common folk myself... In fact, every year my income was 40,000 to 50,000 yuan, I could have completely ignored these matters! But I cannot! My conscience tells me I cannot ignore this. The people are oppressed, I cannot let these pests continue to oppress others... I know I will die soon, if my death can draw attention from the authorities, if it can lead to the investigation of those corrupt officials, I will die without regret... Otherwise, I will become a vengeful ghost and not let them off..." Applause erupted in the audience, and the presiding judge hurriedly stopped...
