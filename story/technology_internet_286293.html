Tencent's Starlight Action Sets Sail Again: Many Stars Care for Disabled Students (Photos)

by olpolooo8k on 2009-11-30 10:48:19

至上励合、朱梓骁、李媛希与孩子们跳舞  ；  ；  ； Tencent Entertainment News - On November 24, 2009, the Starlight Love Action set sail again. The group "Zhi Shang Li He", actor Zhu Zi Xiao, and singer Li Yuan Xi followed Tencent.com with carefully prepared gifts and warm greetings to go to Beijing Zhiguang Special Education Training School located in Changping District to console disabled students there and spent an unforgettable afternoon with them. Principal Wang Lijuan helped these mentally challenged children by forsaking superior living conditions and bidding farewell to her family. In the spring of '98, she started "Beijing Zhiguang Special Education Training School" which filled the gap in vocational education for mentally challenged adults in China. Under the meticulous care and education from the principal, each special student at Beijing Zhiguang Special Education Training School has mastered a special skill, some are good at performing, others can play the electronic organ, or make handicrafts. They also showed their skills to the stars, making the stars marvel endlessly.

The talent show was hosted by Wang Ke and Kiki, and the Starlight Action was officially launched, polo shirts. The disabled students first performed excerpts from a musical. A mentally challenged child's memory lasts only about 10 minutes, but the children here successfully performed a 70-minute musical, which is nothing short of a miracle. As a thank you, Zhi Shang Li He sang their representative work "Candy" acapella for them. This was definitely a special performance. Although it was acapella, the emotions were abundant. Subsequently, the children took the hands of the stars and danced Mongolian dance together. The entire performance ended with the children's sign language performance of "Grateful Heart". The children's sincere smiles and hardworking performances moved us, and the wonderful programs received enthusiastic applause from the entire audience.

It happened to be the birthday of two children today, and the stars brought a cake to celebrate for them. Li Yuan Xi put on the birthday hats for the children, Zhi Shang Li He and Zhu Zi Xiao sang the birthday song, accompanied by a mentally challenged student named Xiao Fei on the electronic organ. Xiao Fei sat in front of the electronic organ all along, as long as the music played, he would start accompanying, showing his love for the electronic organ. Before the performance, Zhi Shang Li He, Zhu Zi Xiao, and Li Yuan Xi were always around Xiao Fei, listening to him play songs, and even had a small chorus.

After the party, Zhi Shang Li He, Zhu Zi Xiao, and Li Yuan Xi followed the children to the canteen. Zhiguang School's concept is to let mentally challenged children take care of themselves, so they specially opened a cooking class. Today, our stars made dinner with the children - making dumplings.

Although the stars were full of confidence on stage, when they came to the cooking class, their performances were slightly inferior. Zhu Zi Xiao rushed to the workbench first and made the first dumpling, but all the leeks were exposed. Jin Eun-Sung from the Zhi Shang Li He group came from South Korea, and he specially taught the children here how to make Korean-style dumplings. However, he revealed that apart from the shape being different, actually Chinese and Korean dumplings taste the same. Another member Liu Zhou Cheng perhaps didn't know how to make dumplings, so he suddenly thought of making a siu mai for the children, but it was confiscated by the principal. On the contrary, the dumplings made by the mentally challenged children were thin-skinned and big-filled, just looking at the appearance was already very tempting.

Perhaps because it was dinner time, when steaming hot plates of dumplings were served, everyone gathered around the table and ate heartily. Eating the fruits of one's labor, the feeling must surely be different.

In the skills classroom, the children shared their labor skills with Zhi Shang Li He, Zhu Zi Xiao, and Li Yuan Xi. In the handicraft classroom, cheap polo shirts, the walls were covered with the children's works: wool gloves, paper-cutting works, Fuwa clay sculptures... The principal introduced that these works have all won awards from international institutions such as the United Nations. At this moment, there were children around us sewing cross-stitch embroidery, making blessing cards, and the proficient techniques made the stars praise endlessly.

Zhiguang School has its own costume-making studio, and most of the costumes and props displayed here were made by the children. Seeing the evening gowns and wedding dresses on the models, Li Yuan Xi expressed that the children here have great design talent, and the clothes showed elements of fashion trends. And Zhi Shang Li He couldn't help but love the cartoon headgear, competing with the children here to wear the headgear and show their cute side. They praised the children's exquisite handicrafts endlessly.

Good times together are always too short, but Tencent.com's Starlight Action sending love is infinite. Although it was only a short time together, Zhi Shang Li He, Zhu Zi Xiao, and Li Yuan Xi all expressed that they will actively participate in such public welfare activities in the future and call on friends around them to help these mentally challenged children together.
