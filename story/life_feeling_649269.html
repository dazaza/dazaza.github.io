The sadness of waiting for the quiet flow of years

Many dreams are waiting to happen, and I can no longer tell if you are a friend, or someone I've missed, or even someone I haven't met yet.

— Preface

A broken-winged flight, how can it gain strength? It's not a fairy tale, unable to depict such a colorful perfection. The sinking sorrow, quietly awaits the passage of time. Holding hands without being lovers, ultimately buries that calm helplessness. It's not that I don't want to love, but I know I shouldn't. Knowing what the result will be, I'm afraid that we can't even be friends. It's not fear of having no ending, but the fear of you disappearing forever from my world.

My heart is barren, filled with mediocrity, numb to the feeling of holding hands in love. Sinking in another illusory world, I don't know what I should do, weary, slow, cold, and repulsed. Seemingly carefree on the surface, lost and dim inside. Unwilling to be destitute, but lacking the drive to fight. The past that I don't want to mention, the future that I dare not look forward to, the current state of disarray. Searching for a direction, seeking a whiff of fragrance, wanting a tranquil place in this noisy reality. You are the寄托I seek. Fortunately, in my lifetime, I have encountered someone like you.

You are just like the gorgeous chrysanthemum that blooms in autumn, pure and true, also like the elegant君子lan blooming fiercely in warm winter, gentle and polite, talented but not arrogant, successful but not proud, humble though living in a valley. Your purity drifts in this mundane world's clamor. Meeting you is the greatest blessing in this cycle of life.

Color is the eternal hue in your life, becoming strong in trials. A solitary thinker, amidst the bustling city, still remains alone. In you, one can taste the tranquility that time has condensed, peacefully dignified in your harmony, stopping the world at that moment. Only then do I understand the word "freedom", and thus experience the word "composure".

Waiting while guarding a promise, burying deeply the love once spoken in the season when flowers bloom. Flowers bloom and fade, clouds roll and stretch, let the words once spoken be engraved as eternity, let the past smiles become permanent beauty. Not hoping that you will clearly remember me when reminiscing in the future. Living in the present, using the deepest memories to record this most splendid encounter in life.

A thought arises, even across thousands of mountains and rivers, only hoping this affection remains unchanged; a thought fades, despite the changes of沧海桑田, only wishing my heart stays peaceful. Without too many words, finally, I say the selfish words others have said: after many years, if you are not married, and I am not wed, could we be together?
