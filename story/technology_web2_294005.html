The three kingdoms of Wei, Shu, and Wu have directly transferred their battles from the mortal world to the netherworld, causing the entire netherworld to be filled with resentment, making life unbearable for ghosts. The netherworld is the foundation of the entire human realm. When the netherworld thrives, so does the mortal world. If the netherworld perishes, the mortal world perishes as well. Witness how Cao Tianchou, the 601st eldest grandson of Cao Cao, a pure Yang individual, ventures alone into the netherworld and restores order amidst its internal and external crises, utilizing the ghost path for cultivation.
