It is circulating inside Xiefei United: General Manager Xu said in Shenyang that everyone should do their own job well.

by yinding999 on 2009-12-17 16:19:44

Inside Sheffield United: Chairman Xu's words from Shenyang! Everyone should do their own jobs well.

Among the management of Chengdu Sheffield United Club, there is a saying: "Chairman Xu has spoken in Shenyang, everyone needs to do their own jobs and do them well."

Common sense tells us that this is impossible. However, the management seems to think that fabricating such a story could better inspire employees' enthusiasm and reduce their insecurity. This club, as well as the football park and sports park standing in Shuangliu, were almost all established by Xu Hongtao. With his gentle personality and strong sense of humanity, he has been deified in many people's hearts. Every employee that the reporter met would refer to "Chairman Xu" with great respect. The reporter joked with Ma Mingyu, "Will we need Chairman Xu to remote control in the future?" Ma Mingyu became serious, "I haven't thought that far ahead."

Currently, all arrangements of the club and the team are carried out according to Xu Hongtao's established policies. A director specially sent by Sheffield United Holding Company announced the new personnel arrangement at 11 a.m. on the 14th: In order to better manage the club and the team, a General Manager's Office was set up, with Ma Mingyu and Wang Maojun among others entering this office as part of the management team. Yao Xia was appointed as vice president of the club and also the team leader and player of Chengdu Sheffield United Football Team. Subsequently, at 3 p.m., the club held a press briefing to officially announce this news.

In fact, this personnel arrangement was also determined by Xu Hongtao before. Now, the personnel deployment of the club is fully carried out according to the plan determined by Xu Hongtao before.

It is said that Xu Hongtao once boldly proposed to the British side that next year's task is to strive for AFC Champions League qualification. Of course, now the goal is still untouchable. However, the club has indicated that the list of players for sale will be published within this week. And the transfer-in personnel are still under negotiation. According to Ma Mingyu, head coach Wang Baoshan and others have already identified several players when they went to Brazil to examine foreign players, and the negotiations are ongoing.
