Journal Introduction

"China Water Transport" was first published in 1979. It is a comprehensive monthly journal of the water transport industry, supervised by the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China and publicly issued both domestically and internationally. It is also an excellent national science and technology journal, included in the Chinese Academic Journal CD Edition and selected into the national journal array.

The journal aims to "serve the reform and development of China's water transport industry and serve the employees of China's water transport industry." It vigorously promotes the policies and guidelines of the state and the Ministry of Communications for the development of water transport, timely disseminates economic information on domestic and international water transport, objectively reports on the innovation and reform of the water transport industry, interprets domestic and international water transport laws and regulations, and popularizes and promotes scientific and technological achievements in water transport. It conducts discussions around hotspots, difficulties, focal points in domestic and international water transport as well as issues of concern to readers.

The journal targets decision-makers, managers, and technical personnel in enterprises and institutions in the water transport and related industries, including colleges and universities and research institutes. It has distinctive columns such as "Shipping", "Ports", "Economic Management", "Law", "Information", "Mechanical & Electrical", "Hydraulics", "Geology", and "Engineering". It is an important window for understanding the development trends of China's water transport and international shipping information; it is a must-read publication for those engaged in water transport and reference material for professionals in related fields.

Domestic Serial Number: CN 42-1395/U

International Serial Number: ISSN 1006-7973

Submission Requirements

1. Overall manuscript requirements: The topic should be novel, and the theme and content of the article must align with the spirit of the three representatives of the Party (i.e., its line, policy, and strategy); they must comply with national laws and regulations. The manuscript should present unique insights, rigorous arguments, fluent language, proper punctuation, and ideally be between 2000-6000 words. For a few topics with large capacity, rich materials, and complex layers, the limit is 8000 words.

2. According to the requirements of the "Norms for Retrieval and Evaluation Data of Chinese Academic Journals (CD Edition)" issued by the National Press and Publication Administration, please provide the following relevant information simultaneously: ① Name, gender, year of birth, ethnicity, and native place; ② Workplace (including secondary unit) and detailed communication address (including postal code); ③ Degree, title, and main research direction; ④ Content summary of the article (100～200 words), keywords (3～8), and English translation of the article title (including subtitle).

3. Citations in the manuscript must be accurate. Use sequential coding, and encode consecutively in Arabic numerals at the citation point according to the order of appearance of references in the paper. Enclose the sequence number in square brackets. If a reference is repeatedly cited in the same paper, use the same sequence number. To indicate the specific source of the citation, add the page number in parentheses after the sequence number. The reference list items must be complete, and the order must be standardized.

4. Authors are responsible for their manuscripts, and we will quickly reply within three days whether the manuscript is accepted. Editors have the right to make deletions and modifications regarding content structure, language text, and editorial norms. If you do not agree with the deletions and modifications, please specifically note this when submitting your manuscript. Authors should keep a copy of their manuscript.
