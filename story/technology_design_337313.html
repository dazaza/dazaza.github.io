Xu Jinglei's "Du Lala" breaks through her own limits, with passionate performance alongside Huang Li-xing.

by qwwqreeru on 2010-03-31 10:43:22

Sina Entertainment News - Directed by Xu Jinglei and starring herself, produced by Zhang Yibai, and co-starring Huang Lihang, Karen Mok, Ruby Wu, and Li Ai, the workplace fashion blockbuster *Du Lala's Promotion* is set to officially premiere on April 15th. A set of passionate bed photos jointly contributed by Xu Jinglei and Huang Lihang were exposed yesterday. Xu Jinglei, who has always been known for her literary and artistic image, seems this time determined to push the limits of passion. Not only does she passionately kiss Huang Lihang in the film, but she also sacrifices her "image" by appearing topless, marking the most daring breakthrough in her acting career so far.

Xu Jinglei and Huang Lihang's Bedroom Passion: *Du Lala's Promotion* Pushes Passion to the Limit

*Du Lala's Promotion* will be released on April 15th. Although topics such as workplace and fashion in the film have sparked heated discussions, they are far less explosive than Xu Jinglei's groundbreaking performance in the movie. Previously, a revealing photo of Xu Jinglei wearing a bikini on the beach from the film went viral online, and yesterday, the production team released another set of even bolder passionate scenes.

The set of stills features fiery and passionate scenes where both Xu Jinglei and Huang Lihang appear bare-chested and engage in an intense embrace. Xu Jinglei sacrifices her "image" by going topless, exuding sensuality. However, one nude back still has raised suspicions about whether it was a body double. In response, Xu Jinglei did not make any direct comments, but the staff revealed that the scene was filmed with the set cleared out. Apart from the two main actors, only a few crew members, including the cinematographer and lighting team, were present. As both director and lead actress, Xu Jinglei only stopped filming when satisfied. Regardless, Xu Jinglei's performance in this film absolutely breaks all previous boundaries, representing her most daring role in *Du Lala's Promotion*!

Xu Jinglei Re-creates the Plot: The Lead Couple Spends a Romantic Night in Thailand

*Du Lala's Promotion* is adapted from the namesake novel, but Xu Jinglei revealed that the film only selects one-fourth of the original content. This explosive and fiery passionate scene does not exist in the original novel. According to staff members who have seen the film, this passionate scene between Xu Jinglei and Huang Lihang was shot in Thailand. Xu Jinglei's character Du Lala and Huang Lihang's character Wang Wei receive a company reward for a vacation, during which their relationship advances significantly, culminating in the most explosive moment during a company gathering night.

The enchanting foreign scenery, coupled with the stimulation of love hormones, leads Du Lala and Wang Wei to spend a pivotal night together, directly propelling the plot forward. It is reported that this heavily featured bedroom scene is part of Xu Jinglei's re-creation. A staff member involved in the shooting revealed that Xu Jinglei and Huang Lihang frequently broke into laughter while filming the kissing scenes. Huang Lihang, at the "rookie" level in movies, appeared quite awkward facing Xu Jinglei and mostly completed the entire shoot under her guidance. On the other hand, Xu Jinglei was well-prepared. She not only instructed Huang Lihang on his positioning beforehand as the director but also guided him patiently during the filming, eventually capturing a breathtakingly intense segment.
