Disposable USB flash drive: Flashkus

by gadgetoflife on 2011-02-28 14:55:18

By 2011/02/21 Published: A USB drive that you use once and throw away? Isn't that too wasteful? However, considering the incidents over the past few years caused by improper handling of USB drives, this single-use USB drive called Flashkus does indeed have its market demand.

The Flashkus conceptual USB drive introduced by the ArtLebedev studio places the storage medium inside a piece of cardboard. An entire row of USB drives is on one card, and you can simply tear off a piece when needed. You can also write the name of the stored files on the surface of the cardboard or draw funny pictures!
