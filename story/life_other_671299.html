The Unassuming Autumn

Time flies, it seems like just yesterday we passed through early autumn, and now we are already deep into late autumn. Perhaps the many years of experiences have given us a bit more sensitivity, making us fall in love with expressing ourselves, and subsequently, writing.

I am not sure if it's the sentimental autumn that has made me think too much, or if it's my overthinking that makes me feel the sentimentality of autumn? In any case, this year, I seem to have an unusual feeling towards autumn. The bleak autumn winds, the falling flowers, autumn leaves, autumn rain, autumn moon, autumn thoughts... all these form the absolutely beautiful scenery of this season, and each scene touches my heartstrings, making me exclaim and reminisce. As the leaves fall in the clear autumn, countless beauties and sorrows, prosperity and loneliness unfold. The ancients once said: "Since ancient times, people encounter autumn and lament solitude; I say the autumn day surpasses the spring morning." Yes, for thousands of years, people have not hesitated to lavish praises on autumn, grieving for autumn without being hurt by it, sighing at autumn without mourning it.

I really want to thank this autumn, thank this autumn for bringing me into life, thank this autumn for changing my attitude towards life, making me realize that as long as one is "single-minded", there will be "perfect completeness". Life is originally so simple and ordinary, we don't need to be restless, we only need to wait with a calm mind for what we are waiting for, persist in our persistence, believe in our beliefs, and there will always be a day when we get what we are looking for, whether it is career, love, or family. With time, everything will be fine, slowly, take it lightly, and you will have what you should have.

"Some beauty comes from accidental encounters, and should be cherished even more; some waits, though long, are very worthwhile." Because the wind of late autumn really took away my little sadness, and brought me my wait, making me feel that some things that have passed, we should turn the page, because wonderful moments are behind us. As long as we face every day ahead with a calm attitude, everything is so simple, so beautiful!

As the wind rises and the leaves fall, the clear autumn does not seem lonely. Because of sensitivity, it is full of artistic conception; because of love, it is filled with poetic sentiment. A song of autumn, ten thousand volumes of emotion. I have fallen in love with autumn, fallen in love with life, and even more so with the surprise that this late autumn has given me this year.
