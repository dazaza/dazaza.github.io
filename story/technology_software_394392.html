Must-read for new stores -- How to comprehensively promote your store (Online Store Entrepreneurship Edition)

First, we need to come up with a nice and memorable member name and Wangwang nickname because a good name is easy for everyone to remember. My view on naming is either go completely local or go completely trendy and novel. In short, it's about going to extremes and forging your unique path. Talking about promotion, it's not only applicable to beginners but even diamond-level sellers need promotion. However, the methods and techniques used by diamond-level sellers are more experienced and mature than those of beginner sellers. For novice sellers, talking about promotion can be quite a headache. I am also a novice seller and have summarized my experience over the past few months to share with everyone. Due to personal limitations, if it's not well written, please criticize and correct me!

1. Persistently visit various forums and communities, read posts, reply to posts, and post threads. First, get familiar with the faces so that people will recognize you. When replying to posts, make sure they are meaningful and express your own opinions. If you see a thread asking for help and you can assist the original poster, take the time to respond and help! Helping others is helping yourself. For discussion threads, when replying, express your own insights. If the original poster or passers-by see it and find common topics, they might become friends with you. More friends make life easier, and more friends mean more opportunities! This is both a free and effective method, and you can learn a lot of knowledge.

2. Make good use of the showcase recommendation spots. Don't underestimate the showcase recommendation spots. If set up properly, traffic will increase, and with more traffic, the conversion rate will be higher. I suggest setting the delisting time of your items so that during peak hours every day, there are products ready to be delisted. Then, set three showcase recommendations daily. That means during the three peak hours, set all the items ready to be delisted as showcase recommendations. This is my personal summary and is very effective.

3. Set the item delisting time to 7 days. This way, the chance of your items being seen will double. At the same time, try to stagger your listing times. Your store frequently has new arrivals and items ending soon. Not only does this make buyers feel comfortable, but it also increases your exposure.

4. Set the title of your items well. The maximum length for an item title is 60 characters. Try to use all the words related to your items without wasting these 60 characters. This way, when buyers search for items, the chances of finding them will increase several times. This is also very useful.

5. Do a good job with friendly links. This step is also very important, and should not be overlooked. It's not necessary to only link with diamond-level sellers to bring in traffic. Diamonds start from scratch too. As long as the other party has confidence and potential, they could quickly become diamonds. So don't reject friendly links from new sellers. If they are in the same industry, it might bring unexpected surprises! Friendly links must be fully utilized, and doing so will also bring traffic.

6. After spending time in the community, if your posts are of high quality, there is a possibility of getting featured, which allows you to earn some silver coins. Using silver coins, you can grab ad spaces. Currently, there are three known ad spaces: 1) Community homepage image recommendation spot. 50 silver coins per position, quantity 8. Grabbing time: 1:00 PM. 2) Forum left-side text recommendation spot. 20 silver coins per position, quantity 20. Grabbing time: 4:00 PM. 3) In-site message recommendation spot. 30 silver coins per position, quantity 10. Grabbing time: 9:00 PM. Also, we can use silver coins to buy ad spaces on Ali Mama. In short, any method that lets others know about our store, we must spare no effort.

7. Another great place to promote your store is Taobao Inquiry. This section is in the Consumer Community. Based on the characteristics of your product, post questions or answer questions. Answering questions should be sincere and not like advertising. Ensure answering at least 100 questions daily. Note the precautions. In the inquiry section, you should answer questions with the mindset of helping others, professionally and convincingly. Only by helping others will they have a good impression of you. If you directly post store advertisements, it may cause the questioner to report you, which would be disastrous. So everyone should maintain the right attitude and approach this with the mindset that "helping others is helping yourself." You can also ask questions. The effect will be noticeable within a week!

8. Cleverly promote using Wangwang: Set a link to your store on Wangwang. You can also set some ads about the features of your store.

9. Use QQ groups for promotion and omnipresent publicity, but make sure to control the degree appropriately.

10. Strive for more promotional activities.

12. Promote your forum on commonly used forum promotion service websites.

For example: www.haobbs.net We believe in your creativity and hands-on ability. Encourage active exchange of promotion experiences and enthusiastic participation in forum promotion.

13. Post hot topics of your forum in chat rooms of major websites.

Such as: NetEase, Sina, Tom, etc., portal website chat rooms. If you cannot post URLs, break them apart before posting. There will be people who will look at your posts.

14. Post threads in forums with high traffic and popularity to attract attention.

For example: Mop (http://www.mop.com)

15. Forums of various universities. These places have high popularity, and the people here tend to like forums. Place your signature on these forums, and when posting or replying, appropriately include a small link or advertisement for your forum. This will bring a lot of traffic to your forum.
