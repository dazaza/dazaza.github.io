7 Details of Black and White Men's Fashion Style

by shenzhen123f on 2012-03-06 09:26:53

1. Black and white shirt and tie? No! Unless today is your best friend's wedding day, wearing a white shirt with a black tie is too stiff. Instead, choose a plaid print shirt or tie for better results. Rolling up the sleeves will add a touch of carefree elegance and naturalness. Of course, if you're confident enough, pairing black pants with a small hat will make you stand out completely.

2. Distressed black? Yes! Marky Ramone may prefer his black jeans, but if you're wearing a sports vest coat or casual sweater, your jeans should have the same elegant texture. We recommend choosing a charcoal gray, slightly faded distressed effect jean, paired with a white T-shirt and sneakers.

3. Black is universal? No! Replace black with navy blue to prevent accidentally turning a beautiful sailor-style coat into a house uniform. Pairing with a white V-neck sportswear and fine black stripe charcoal gray jeans, or an asymmetrical cut cardigan in the same color series, are all great choices!

4. All white? Yes! Wearing all white from head to toe is usually the best choice for waiters by the pool, but you can also develop this into everyday wear. The trick is to add some shiny black accessories. For example, a watch, or a nice piece of jewelry, even a T-shirt with a slightly changed neckline. Unexpected effects will help you get rid of the low-class concept of all white, thus entering a new level of refinement and elegance.

5. White jeans at night? Yes! Taobao men's clothing analysis: Want to make white jeans suitable for nighttime wear? Then, you need to be absolutely careful about the rest. It needs to be not only simple and generous, but also full and natural. A black thin high neck cashmere sweater and a well-fitted jacket are good choices. Skip the sneakers and choose black retro leather shoes instead, which is the most suitable equipment for you.

6. Choose black and white for social occasions? Yes! This combination is enough to deal with general social occasions. But want to be more fashionable? Then replace it with a black and white striped or checkered shirt. Pairing with white pants, adding a classic Hollywood-style watch, what else do you need? Go out now!

7. Striped jacket? Yes! Worried that your striped suit can't be worn? Indeed, nowadays, probably only four-year-old children and people without fashion taste would choose such light-colored bubble纱 jackets as their "appearance" clothes. But here's a method: just simply add black and white, and this combination becomes quite stylish and mature. Paired with a white shirt, black tie, and a pocket handkerchief, whether it's daily office work or a small party gathering, it's very suitable!
