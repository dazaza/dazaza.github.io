**HI93700 (Low Range Ammonia Nitrogen)**

- Range: 0.00 to 3.00 mg/L (NH3-N)

- Unit conversion: [NH3] = [NH3-N] x 1.214

- Light detector: Silicon photodiode and light-emitting diode

- Wavelength setting: 470 nm

- Measurement method: ASTM D1426-92, Nessler reagent colorimetry, carbon monoxide analyzer. Ammonia nitrogen reacts with reagent to show a pale orange-yellow color.
