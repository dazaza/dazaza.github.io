National Mourning Day: Front pages of newspapers across the country

by leesum on 2008-05-20 20:54:02

May 19th was the first day of mourning for the victims of the Wenchuan earthquake. Major websites and newspapers across the country announced this news on their homepages or front pages in bold black font, mourning for those who lost their lives in the Wenchuan earthquake. In the morning, when leesum went out, he saw that the covers of newspapers such as Nanjing Morning Post, Jinling Evening News, Modern Express, Yangtze Evening News, and Oriental Evening News at major newspaper kiosks in Nanjing were all in solid black, with prominent black words announcing the mourning day, evoking silent remembrance for the deceased. Upon arriving at the company, a similar solemnity was witnessed again: major websites across the country turned their pages into black and white to mourn the victims. May the deceased rest in peace, and may the living be strong. Let us stand together with the disaster victims and rebuild a beautiful home. Let foreigners witness the unity and strength of the Chinese people.
