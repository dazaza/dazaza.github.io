SEO optimization is not a mechanical and repetitive job.

by seo17516 on 2012-02-21 22:25:09

In the eyes of most people, SEO is just about updating content and adding external links. This is merely the most basic work of SEO, and also the common work content for many who are new to SEO. In fact, SEO is a scientific and systematic project, rather than repetitive mechanized labor.

1. SEO Should Learn Keyword Analysis

Nowadays, many SEO practitioners jump straight into it without much thought, picking a keyword randomly and starting on it. As a result, although many websites have decent rankings and traffic, they don't receive many orders. This is because many people choose keywords based on assumptions, leading to situations where some keywords are too competitive, and despite a long period of optimization, there's no improvement. In other cases, due to keyword selection bias, while traffic is brought to the website, these users cannot be retained, among other situations. Therefore, as a qualified SEO professional, one must learn to analyze various keyword data. Sources of keyword data include Baidu related searches, keyword tracking tools, Baidu Promotion Assistant, various Q&A platforms, etc. We accumulate data and then carefully select the most suitable keywords for optimization.

2. SEO Should Start Considering from Before Website Construction

Many friends always think that SEO is just about doing rankings, similar to bidding, except it costs less money than bidding. Indeed, as rumored on the web, external links plus content can achieve good rankings in a short time when keyword competition is not intense, but this ranking cannot be stable. Because our website construction may not necessarily meet user needs. So, we should consider SEO from the start of website construction. Nowadays, many websites are quite typical, especially medical sites, which look like mini versions of 39 Health Network, with plenty of content. However, though there is much content, much of it isn't what users want, and even more pop-up windows are added. How can such sites retain users? Such websites are useless even if they have good rankings.

3. Pursuing External Links Frenetically

Many external link personnel complain that their workload is too large, requiring them to post a large number of links every day. Some even purchase links for short-term benefits. By looking at the workload of external link personnel and the prosperity of the link market, it shows how crazy SEOs are about external links. But have you ever thought about why you do SEO? It's to optimize the website after improving its search engine ranking, thus gaining traffic and converting it into orders, right? However, if you only pursue external links, it might improve your ranking, but as mentioned in point two, if your website itself is bad, what use is it?

4. How to Make SEO No Longer Repetitive and Mechanical

Step One: Do a good job of keyword mining and screening.

Step Two: If building a new site, first consider the website layout, including keyword placement, etc. If taking over an old site, diagnose the site first. Generally speaking, when taking over an old site, a subtraction operation should be performed. Note that if the target keywords for small and medium-sized websites are too many, they shouldn't exceed three. The number of long-tail keywords can be unlimited.

Step Three: Add appropriate content to the website. What is appropriate content? Many websites, especially corporate websites, add articles for optimization, such as industry news, company news, etc. Can such articles provide great value to users? Definitely not! So we need to add appropriate content to our website! For example, let's say we run an underwear business website. There are two types of articles. One is "How to Choose Suitable Underwear," and the other is "Underwear Industry Sales Growth by 60%." Which article do you think would interest users? Clearly, the former.

Step Four: Perhaps this step will interest everyone, which is reasonable layout of the website's links. First is internal links. How should internal links be laid out? Currently, the most popular method is perhaps adding internal link anchor texts on keywords within the text. However, the consequence of this method is that it greatly affects the user's reading experience. We can add hot articles on the sidebar of the website, and related articles at the bottom of the article, so as not to affect readability while adding internal links.

Next is external links. Although with changes in search engine algorithms, the importance of external links in ranking algorithms has gradually decreased. Even so, external links remain an indispensable part of our work. How should we handle external links? Is it just sitting in front of the computer all day sending out links? Of course not!

External links can be divided into general weight external links and high weight external links. General weight external links include forum signature anchor texts, common phenomena indicating increased Baidu weight, blogs, and other common external link methods. Taking forum signatures as an example, many people say forum signatures are useless. Is this really true? Of course not! It's because we haven't mastered the technique well. After going to a forum, instead of just blindly posting after adding a personal signature, we should first analyze what kind of posts attract user attention on that forum. For example, if a post has over 150 clicks and over 60 replies, we record such posts, find their common points, and extract the relevant keywords. Then, we write posts around these keywords, thereby attracting user attention and increasing the likelihood of users clicking on our signature anchor text, thus enhancing the value of our external links.

High-weight external links include cross-linking. The main purpose of cross-linking is to obtain one-way links, generally between the main site and our resource site. Cross-linking operations are relatively complex and won't be detailed here. Another type is friendship links. Friendship links are double-edged swords. Since they involve mutual linking, we must be cautious when handling them. How to get friendship links? You can use some friendship link platforms, such as GO9GO platform, where you can directly find suitable websites, inquire with the site owner whether they are willing to exchange, or even post our friendship link information on the platform. From my usual experience using this platform, the quality of friendship links obtained from this platform is quite good, and those interested can try it out. Another point to note is, assuming we have 10 friendship links, and 4 of them have been penalized, we shouldn't remove all these sites at once, because it would cause too many external links to be lost, resulting in our site being penalized.

Step Five: Do a good job of data segmentation and accumulation. This is extremely important for SEO, and I hope every friend engaged in SEO work takes it seriously. During the SEO process, we should record daily external link publishing situations, content updates, inclusion changes, ranking changes, etc. Of course, in many companies, the person responsible for SEO is often alone. In such cases, we can use some webmaster tools to facilitate our work, such as Aizhan.com, the first to develop a Baidu weight query function. Many SEOs know this website, through which we can clearly see changes in our Baidu weight, PR value changes, inclusion changes, etc., with quite detailed data, greatly facilitating our SEO work.
