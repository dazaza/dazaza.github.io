How to operate sustainably and continue to develop is the top priority for all Zuoliyou Silver Jewelry stores:

1. How to deal with the increase in the number of silver jewelry stores

In the past two years, silver jewelry stores have sprung up like mushrooms after rain. When you are proud of the excitement of opening, within half a year or even a month, more followers and imitators appear, and the silver jewelry war is played out every day.

The glory of opening is gone, replaced by fierce competition. Customers always seek novelty. The opening of each new store means you lose part of the market and some customers. When you are still laughing at the reduction in foot traffic at the silver jewelry store across the street due to your arrival, another new store's opening will make fun of you. This "the mantis stalks the cicada, unaware of the oriole behind" scenario plays out almost every day in the business world. The best way is to regroup, renovate, and present yourself as a new store.

2. How to deal with the decrease in the number of visitors

No matter how much effort you put in, you may have organized some small events, distributed flyers, or even posted posters. However, the reality is that these are outdated games. Customers have become accustomed to them and are indifferent. Then you worry, lose confidence, and start thinking about giving up. The number of visitors to a retail store is determined from the start by the location on this street, so site selection becomes very important. Two adjacent silver jewelry stores basically have the same number of visitors. People entering one store will also enter another. Women's shopping randomness far exceeds purposefulness; some people browse from beginning to end, entering every store, making it a kind of pleasure. The number of visitors is relatively fixed, and the success rate mainly depends on the sales ability of employees, which will also become relatively fixed after a period of time. Pure silver jewelry, through cross-selling, encourages customers to buy several items at once.
