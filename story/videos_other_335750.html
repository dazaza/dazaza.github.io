I. How to correctly understand methanol?

Methanol, also known as wood alcohol, methyl alcohol, or methyl hydroxide, is the simplest saturated alcohol. Its chemical molecular formula is CH3OH. At room temperature, methanol is a colorless liquid with a slight smell of alcohol, melting point -93.9℃, boiling point 64.7℃, density 0.7914 g/cm3, and can dissolve in water and many organic solvents. It's part of Chuandao environmental protection fuel. Methanol is toxic, and large consumption can lead to poisoning and blindness.

Methanol has a wide range of uses and is a basic organic chemical raw material, mainly having the following three major uses: ① used as industrial solvent; ② used as basic raw material to produce formaldehyde, dimethyl ether, acetic acid, protein, and various other organic products; ③ since methanol belongs to monohydric alcohol, it burns very thoroughly, and the combustion product is carbon dioxide and water (combustion equation: 2CH3OH+3O2=2CO2+4H2O), so emissions are less, there is no pollution, and it has greater environmental superiority. More importantly, due to methanol's extremely low production cost, in recent years it has been widely used in deep processing to produce new clean fuels for civilian or vehicle use.
