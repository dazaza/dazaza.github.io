Top Ten Benefits of Registering a Hong Kong Company

1. Free choice of company name, allowing names containing words such as Academy, Publishing, Association, International, Group, Holding, Industry, etc.;

2. Minimal restrictions on the scope of business operations, for example: finance, accounting, import-export, pharmaceuticals, cultural publishing, information networks, trade, shipping, etc., can all become our businesses;

3. It is extremely convenient to apply for licenses under the name of a Hong Kong company, including Hong Kong trademarks, publishers, international book numbers, periodical numbers, foundations, societies, associations, etc.;

4. Hong Kong does not require capital verification, and registered capital can be increased at will, but there is no limit to the amount of capital in place; this lays the foundation for us to establish an international group company;

5. Hong Kong companies are allowed to have no business operations, and shell companies are permitted to exist. Establishing a Hong Kong shell company can become a powerful advertising promotion;

6. Joint ventures or cooperative material processing plants between Hong Kong companies and mainland companies enjoy various policy benefits in the mainland;

7. Hong Kong has low tax rates and few tax categories; no profit, no tax; use Hong Kong's low tax rate and tax planning to achieve reasonable tax avoidance;

8. Control accounts opened in Hong Kong banks through electronic banking services, including current, savings, foreign currency, and letter of credit accounts;

9. Use Hong Kong's reputation to create a Hong Kong brand, enhancing the international image of the company;

10. A Hong Kong company can serve as a window company for domestic enterprises to gain the trust and cooperation of foreign enterprises easily. Hong Kong is also one of the freest and most prosperous commercial ports in the world, with good infrastructure that provides a favorable environment for business operators. You don't have to go to Hong Kong personally to enjoy prices and services synchronized with Hong Kong.
