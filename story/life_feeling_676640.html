I'm sorry, I love you.

There was an ordinary high school. One day, a boy transferred in. He was tall and handsome, dressed in non-mainstream style. There was always an air of nobility and arrogance about him. Of course, for such an ordinary high school, such an excellent boy would definitely make waves throughout the school. Sure enough, overnight, he became famous. Every day, he received love letters and gifts, and it was almost commonplace for girls to "accidentally" bump into him. However, the boy never accepted any girl.

It wasn't that they weren't good enough; he just didn't feel anything for them. Until one day, when the boy saw a cute girl, he clearly remembered how fast his heart beat at the moment he saw her. The boy knew he had fallen in love with the girl. Although the girl wasn't outstanding, he only felt this way about her.

Afterwards, the boy asked a friend to tell the girl that he planned to pursue her. Upon hearing the news, the girl was first surprised, then she said, "I won't agree!" and turned away.

Later, the girl's friend told the boy's friend that the girl had been hurt before. She once treated a boy very sincerely, but that boy only used her as a toy, so she didn't dare to easily accept feelings anymore. She was just afraid of getting hurt again, especially since the boy was so excellent. The girl thought there was no way the boy would be sincere towards her. The friend passed these words on verbatim to the boy. The boy was hurt inside, realizing that the girl had been hurt before. He swore in his heart that he would love the girl well and not let her get hurt. The next day, the boy announced to the world that he liked the girl, which once again made waves in the school.

Every day, the boy would go find the girl, help her with homework, buy her food, take her to the dormitory, hold an umbrella for her in the rain while getting himself soaked. He never called the girl by her name; he always called her "little fool" because the boy said calling her "little fool" made it more intimate...

Gradually, the girl believed that the boy truly liked her. One night, while walking through the small forest path to the girl's dormitory, the boy confessed to the girl again: "Little fool, I like you, I really like you so much. Will you be my girlfriend? After such a long time, can't you see that I really like you? I promise I will never let you get hurt. I will cherish you all my life. Little fool, I will take good care of you. Trust me, okay?"

The girl shyly lowered her head: "Hmm." The boy leaned down and kissed the girl. The hazy moonlight shone on the boy's face, making him look incredibly handsome. The girl tightly closed her eyes. For a long time, the two became the envy of everyone in the school. The girl felt like Cinderella, finally finding her prince. She was happy and content every day.

But good times don't last forever. The girl noticed that the boy had become increasingly distant to her recently. He rarely came to see her, and even phone calls and messages were scarce. The girl panicked, feeling like her happiness was slipping away. Finally, she couldn't bear it anymore and went to the boy's classroom to find him.

When the girl arrived, the boy was sleeping with his head on the desk. The girl was angry that the boy would rather sleep than pay attention to her. Her classmate pushed the boy when he saw the girl, and the boy lazily got up, walked out of the classroom, and came to the girl. The girl looked up and asked the boy: "Honey, why are you ignoring me? Are you going to leave me?" A trace of sadness flashed in the boy's cold eyes, then quickly returned to indifference. He looked down at the girl and said: "Yes, so don't come find me anymore! We're over!"

These words were like thunderclaps on a clear day, shocking the girl. Tears immediately streamed down her face. She grabbed the boy's hand: "Honey, you're lying, right? You promised to cherish me all my life. You promised not to let me get hurt, didn't you?! You said I was your little fool, and you would take good care of me... How could you forget all of this? Honey, you must be lying, right?" The boy turned his face away from the girl, closed his eyes, and harshly shook off the girl's hand with such force that she fell to the ground. He said to the girl: "Isn't what I said clear enough?! I don't want you anymore! I don't like you anymore! Don't come find me again!"

A crowd gathered around, amused. Everyone was shocked by the boy's reaction. Heartbroken, the girl looked up at the boy and asked: "Why?" The boy put his arm around the shoulder of a pretty girl nearby: "This is the reason." The pretty girl might not have expected the boy to like her. She initially froze, then regained her composure and deliberately leaned closer to the boy. Watching this scene, the girl's heart ached so much she could hardly breathe. She covered her chest. Is that right?

So everything was a lie, he was just playing with me... If that's the case, why should I act like a clown and let everyone laugh at me? But what can I do? My heart hurts so much... The girl wiped away her tears and calmly said to the boy: "I understand. I won't bother you anymore." Then she turned and left. The boy didn't see how sad the girl was when she turned away. Similarly, the girl didn't see that after she left, the boy disgustingly pushed away the pretty girl and went back to sleep. No one saw that when the boy laid his head on the table, his eyes were red.

The boy couldn't hold back anymore. His heart hurt so much he felt like dying, even breathing caused pain. He really didn't want to hurt her. He never forgot the promises he gave her, and he wanted to keep those promises too! He really loved his "little fool" and wanted to cherish her all his life! But since receiving the cancer diagnosis from the hospital, none of that was possible... Dear little fool, my cute wife, without me by your side in the future, you must live happily. Your husband will love you in heaven. Wife, forgive my selfishness. I'd rather hurt you than let you watch me die. Even if you hate me for the rest of your life, I can't let you endure such great pain. Wife, I'm sorry, I love you.

The next day, the boy transferred schools. No one knew where he went.

The girl returned to the dormitory, turned the stereo up to full volume, and cried alone under the covers for a long time. For two whole days, she neither ate nor drank, and no matter who tried to persuade her, it was useless. She just lay there for two days. On the morning of the third day, she finally got up, brushed her teeth normally, washed her face normally, ate normally, and went to school normally, as if nothing happened. Friends concernedly asked her if she was okay, but she just smiled and passed by: "Do you think I look like something's wrong?"

Friends told the girl that the boy had transferred schools. The girl indifferently shrugged: "Oh." Seeing the girl's indifferent attitude, everyone thought the girl had forgotten the boy. In fact, only the girl herself knew that she would never forget him in her lifetime, even though he had deceived her, she still couldn't help but remember him...

After the boy left, the girl never dated anyone else again, focusing all her thoughts on studying. Six months later, the girl was admitted to Shanghai Jiaotong University. In college, the girl also didn't date anyone because her heart still held someone else. Although that person was cruel to her, she could never forget the boy. And she didn't want to forget, those memories were beautiful, she didn't want them to fade with time, even though these memories were tainted with deception...

Six years later, one day, the girl returned to this high school. The school was still the same as before, not a single blade of grass or tree had changed. As soon as she stepped into the school gate, memories flooded back. Every detail of her and the boy's relationship was replayed like a movie in her mind. Each step she took, her heart ached more. In the small forest behind the school, she still vividly remembered how they once held hands and strolled together, and he told her: "Silly wife, I will love you forever." At that time, she blushed and her heart was sweet. These scenes seemed like they happened yesterday, and she remembered them so clearly.

Suddenly, her gaze fell on a tombstone, and she wondered, how could there be a tombstone in the small forest of the school? She approached to take a look: "Ah!" The girl collapsed on the ground when she saw the name on the tombstone. The boy's photo on the tombstone was yellowed, but it couldn't hide his former good looks. The girl was stunned. Wasn't he supposed to have transferred schools?

How could he possibly be dead? But the photo on the tombstone was clearly his! What exactly happened? An uncle sweeping the forest passed by and, seeing the girl frightened and sitting on the ground, hurriedly helped her up and said: "Young lady, don't be scared. This boy died of lung cancer. I heard he was dating a girl from our school, and they both loved each other deeply. Later, after learning he had cancer, the boy intentionally hurt the girl and left the school. Not long after, the girl went to university, and the boy died. Before he died, he bought this small forest with money and said he wanted to be buried here after death. He said it was because he once made a promise to the girl here to love her forever, so he wanted to fulfill that promise here. Sigh, what a pitiful child..."

As the uncle swept the ground and walked away, the girl's mind went blank, and she collapsed again. It wasn't until the uncle had been gone for a long time that she finally couldn't hold back her sobs and screamed heart-wrenchingly: "You bastard! How could you deceive me for so long! How could you make me hate you for so long! You bad guy! How could you do this to me?! Why did you hide it from me and make me hate you when you were dying?! I don't want it!!"

The girl's desolate voice echoed through the small forest, and eventually her throat was hoarse. She cried and crawled to the boy's grave, touching his photo, muttering: "Husband, you must have been lonely being here all alone for so long? Husband, you always said I was foolish, but actually, you were the foolish one. Why did you deceive your wife? Making your wife hate you for so long. Husband, it's so cold and lonely here. Can I stay with you?"

The next day, the sweeping uncle discovered a female corpse. She had committed suicide with a fruit knife and looked peaceful, lying beside the boy's grave. There was a message written in blood on the ground: "Please bury me with him, thank you."

I wrote this to tell everyone that we must cherish our other halves! People always fail to treasure what they have when they possess it. We should all love each other properly, don't always wait until the last moment to realize.
