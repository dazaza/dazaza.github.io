A member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) led security guards to beat up villagers due to conflicts arising from soliciting customers.

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-07-28 16:30:18

The brawl occurred right in front of the Kangxi Nightless City. This photo was taken by our reporter Pu Dongfeng.

Yesterday, I learned from the police that Wang Huijie, a member of the Seventh Committee of the Yanqing County Political Consultative Conference, led the security guards of the resort he operates to beat several villagers. On July 26, Wang Huijie was administratively detained for 20 days by the Yanqing police for assaulting others and damaging property.

It is understood that the beating incident was caused by competition for business.

A conflict arose as both parties were soliciting customers at the same location.

At around 7 p.m. on July 24, a brawl broke out in front of Kangxi Nightless Valley near Kangxi Grassland in Kangzhuang Town, Yanqing County. Wang Huijie, head of Kangxi Nightless Valley, along with security guards Di Mou, Liu Mou, and two others, assaulted Gao Yongshan, Wang Runling, and other villagers from Sijie Village in Kangzhuang Town using wooden clubs and iron rods. Gao Yongshan, nearly 50 years old, sustained injuries to his left leg and waist. During the fight, Wang Huijie also smashed a Chery QQ car with a stick.

Gao Yongshan and others live in Sijie Village, not far from Kangxi Grassland. They raise horses and make a living by renting them out for tourists to ride. Kangxi Nightless Valley also offers horseback riding services for tourists, and both sides solicit customers almost at the same spot. It is understood that this brawl was caused by disputes over soliciting customers.

After being beaten, more than 20 neighbors and relatives of Gao Yongshan rushed to the gate of Kangxi Nightless Valley to block the entrance and demand an explanation. After local police arrived, they mediated for nearly three hours until about 11 p.m., when people gradually dispersed.

On the day of the incident, Di Mou was administratively detained for 10 days and fined 500 yuan by the Yanqing police for assaulting others; the other two security guards were each administratively detained for 13 days and fined 500 yuan for assaulting others. On July 26, Wang Huijie was administratively detained for 20 days and fined 800 yuan by the Yanqing police for assaulting others and damaging property.

Yesterday, when I called the Yanqing police to inquire about the above situation, they confirmed it to be true.

A mysterious woman went to the hospital for mediation.

On the afternoon of July 28, I met Gao Ming, Gao Yongshan's son, in Sijie Village of Kangzhuang Town. Gao Ming said that after his father was beaten, he had to take leave from work to return home to take care of the horses. I noticed that Gao's stable housed two chestnut-colored horses, which Gao Yongshan usually led to the front of Kangxi Grassland to solicit customers.

At 2 p.m., I rushed to Yanqing County Hospital, but Gao Yongshan was not in the ward. An hour and a half later, when I returned to the ward, a middle-aged woman dressed in black appeared in the ward. The woman claimed to be a "common friend" of both Wang Huijie and Gao Yongshan and told reporters that everything was fine now, as the two parties had reached a settlement, and all compensation money had been paid.

When asked about her identity, the middle-aged woman smiled without answering. She told reporters that Gao Yongshan's injuries were not serious, and he could be discharged in the afternoon. When I requested to see Gao Yongshan, the middle-aged woman informed me that Gao Yongshan had gone for a check-up. Not long after, the middle-aged woman returned to the ward with a young man. This young man claimed to be Gao Yongshan's nephew and stated that Gao Yongshan had already recovered and been discharged from the hospital.

Yesterday, I called Gao Ming, who said he was unaware of his father's discharge. I then called the admissions office of Yanqing County Hospital and learned that Gao Yongshan had not yet been discharged.

After the incident, Kangxi Nightless Valley continued to operate normally. When I called the duty staff to ask if they knew about Wang Huijie's detention, the staff said Wang Huijie was not there, seemingly "on a business trip." The horse-riding service for tourists was still going on as usual, at "50 yuan per hour."

■ Profile of Wang Huijie

Special invitee of the Political Consultative Conference

Wang Huijie is 37 years old and is from Yanqing. Wang Huijie is a member of the Seventh Committee of the Yanqing County Political Consultative Conference. Yesterday, I saw on the official website of the Yanqing Political Consultative Conference that Wang Huijie is one of the 30 "specially invited individuals" among Yanqing's political advisors.

According to information provided by the Beijing Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce's Enterprise Credit Website, Beijing Kangxi Nightless Valley Conference Service Co., Ltd. has a registered capital of 1 million yuan, mainly engaged in catering services and conference services, with Wang Huijie as the legal representative.

In 2009, China Broadcasting Network published an article titled "The Son of the Mountain - A Record of Wang Huijie, Pioneer Entrepreneur of Yanqing, Beijing," which stated: "Wang Huijie was born into a poor peasant family in Heihanling Village, Sihaizhen Town, Yanqing, Beijing. His childhood experiences made him determined to study hard for the prosperity of his hometown. In July 1993, he went to Beijing to start a business, and in early 2000, he founded a landscaping company. Currently, the company owns fixed assets exceeding 15 million yuan, employs 126 workers, and has entered a virtuous cycle of development."

The report mentioned that in 2005, Wang Huijie signed a sponsorship agreement worth 40,000 yuan with the Yanqing County Education Commission's "Sunshine Love Scholarship Fund" and sponsored a college student to complete their education, according to the blog of zzf000zxyr2.
