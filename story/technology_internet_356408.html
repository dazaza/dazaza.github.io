Day 1: Beijing/Kunming

Depart from your city by flight to the beautiful southern city of Yunnan. The first stop is Kunming. Upon arrival, you will be met by a private car and transferred to the Kunming Spring City Lakeside Resort. Located 38 kilometers east of Kunming on the shore of the clear and charming Yangzonghai Lake, surrounded by mountains with clear water, it enjoys a pleasant climate all year round. It is considered one of the most beautiful golf resorts in the world and is known as the golf paradise of Asia. After checking in at the resort, enjoy a rich and distinctive dinner within the resort.

Day 2: Kunming (Spring City Lakeside Golf Resort Lakeside Course) Pictures of Spring City Course

In the morning, after enjoying a rich breakfast at the hotel, play 18 holes at the Lakeside Course of Spring City Lakeside Resort. Designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., the course is renowned for its narrow fairways, ravines, strong lake winds, terraced greens, and picturesque waterside scenery. It is regarded by golf enthusiasts as one of the most challenging courses. The designer himself praised the Lakeside Course highly: "Spring City in the Asia-Pacific region integrates closely with the natural environment, making full use of the originally enchanting scenery, undulating terrain, and vast views to create an unforgettable and enjoyable golf experience."

After the game, free time.

Day 3: Kunming/Lijiang (Spring City Lakeside Golf Resort Mountain Course)

In the morning, after enjoying a rich breakfast at the hotel, play 18 holes at the Mountain Course of Spring City Lakeside Resort. Designed by Jack Nicklaus, the course is located on a high slope beside the Yangzonghai Lake in Kunming. It combines the open and poetic waterside scenery with the quiet and profound mountain landscape. The ingenious design of the 18 distinct holes caters to players of all levels, making them linger. The designer himself claimed: "The fairways are wide, making tee shots relatively easy, but the greens are surrounded by scattered bunkers and grass traps, requiring precise calculations when approaching the greens. Putting on the greens planted with Bentgrass is more challenging."

After the golf game, a private car will transfer you to the airport for your flight to Lijiang. Upon arrival in Lijiang, you will be met by a private car and transferred to the Lijiang Ancient Town Resort to check in.

Day 4: Lijiang (Yulong Snow Mountain Golf Course) Yulong Snow Mountain Golf Course

In the morning, after enjoying a rich breakfast at the hotel, take a private car to the Yulong Snow Mountain Golf Course to play 18 holes. Situated at an altitude of 3100 meters on the plateau, the course was designed by the famous course designer Neil Haworth. Due to the high altitude, thin air, and low gravity, the specially designed eighteen-hole 72-par 8548-yard course length has been proven to be a world record. It is also the only snow mountain course in Asia. After the golf game, you can visit the beautiful scenery of Yulong Snow Mountain.

Day 5: Lijiang/Beijing (Lijiang Ancient Town Golf Course) Lijiang Ancient Town Golf Course

In the morning, after enjoying a rich breakfast at the hotel, play 18 holes at the Lijiang Ancient Town Golf Course. Located at the foot of Wenhua Peak, which echoes with the distant Yulong Snow Mountain, just 8 kilometers away from the ancient town of Lijiang. Following the Wenhua Sea, under the reflection of the Yulong Snow Mountain, the course stretches and rises majestically, integrating mountainous and flat land, mountain scenery and water scenery, grandeur and beauty into one, fully displaying the beauty of nature. To the east lies the new city of Yulong County, to the south is Wenhua Peak, to the north overlooks the Yulong Snow Mountain and the ancient town of Lijiang, and to the west are the ancient Naxi villages. In the middle, it embraces the emerald Wenhua Sea, forming a typical back-mountain-front-water layout. After the game, a private car will transfer you to the airport for your return flight, concluding a pleasant journey.
