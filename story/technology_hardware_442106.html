Tarot Divination - August 2009 Zodiac Horoscope

by kdje0816 on 2011-06-06 14:37:53

Aries (March 21 - April 20)

This Month's Tarot: The Emperor

The appearance of the "Emperor" card indicates that there will be events this month that will affect Aries' future, and Aries needs to make their own decisions. However, having the initiative does not necessarily lead to good results as Mars in retrograde affects you, so your plans may not go smoothly.

Love: August is not a good time for single Aries to seek love. If you take the initiative to confess, the chances of success are small. Participating in social activities in the middle of the month may bring some romantic opportunities through Venus, but these connections are relatively short-term and unsuitable for developing into a serious relationship. If you can keep a low profile this month, the following months will see smoother love luck.

Career: Mars has an unfavorable trajectory in the career palace recently, so it would be better for Aries to observe changes rather than actively pursue actions in the workplace during this period. Work frustrations may make you irritable and prone to arguments. Try not to overly insist on your views during discussions and listen more, speak less.

Wealth: Your budget is insufficient, so large expenditures should be avoided temporarily, and travel or entertainment expenses that are considered luxurious for your wallet should also be avoided.

Tips: Aries should avoid sitting for long periods as it can affect the spine and internal organs. Massages can be beneficial. Walking to work if possible is recommended.

Taurus (April 21 - May 21)

This Month's Tarot: Judgment

August presents a good opportunity for promotion.

Taurus will soon achieve extraordinary success by fully utilizing their potential or obtain a higher position. This is the fortune presented by the "Judgment" card. Taurus' chart for August is filled with the energy of the Sun, and Scorpio is the lucky zodiac sign that brings financial fortune.

Love: There are opportunities for outdoor gatherings this month. Single Taurus might consider inviting their crush directly. Taking the first step can lead to a chance to hold hands openly. The early to mid-month is a good time to enhance intimacy, and daytime dates are more fortunate than nighttime ones.

Career: Opportunities to showcase your talents are nearby. Starting from the second week, this trend will become increasingly strong. Preparing beforehand can enhance your performance. Seizing this opportunity can make promotion not difficult. Luck seems to be on your side, so there’s no need to resort to shortcuts; relying on positive efforts is sufficient.

Wealth: Following your own judgment in investments will yield profits. Past failed investments or lost items might be recovered or compensated for this month.

Tips: You may have to entertain guests from afar. It’s a good time to reconnect with partners who haven't been in touch for a while.

Gemini (May 22 - June 21)

This Month's Tarot: The Wheel of Fortune

Gemini's tarot card for this month is "The Wheel of Fortune," indicating that the Moon will continue to interfere with your future, unfortunately still mainly negatively. Female Geminis are particularly affected physically, especially beware of gynecological diseases.

Love: August could be called Gemini's "Breakup Month." Lovers might initiate separation. Going out to relax offers chances to meet interesting members of the opposite sex, but for single Geminis, this month only brings short-term romantic luck. New acquaintances don't have much potential for development!

Career: Recently, your luck with cooperation isn't good, making it unsuitable for job hunting, investment, or negotiations. The worst interpersonal luck occurs in the first and fourth weeks, so be cautious in the office. Beware of betrayal from allies more than enemies.

Wealth: Stocks or other assets in your possession might depreciate, but after the Moon's influence, there is still a chance for your property value to rise. Do not rush to sell.

Tips: Traveling is fine, but it's best not to stay near water.

Cancer (June 22 - July 22)

This Month's Tarot: The Magician

Cancer received the upright "Magician" card in August, indicating the appearance of opportunities or benefactors with great potential, but success comes with risks. You might buy potential stocks, but also risk unprofitable businesses.

Love: Cancer will encounter someone of the opposite sex with compatible temperament this month. Conversations will be harmonious, and further interaction may lead to a closer relationship. However, for Cancers already in relationships, close opposite-sex friends may become a threat to existing feelings. Avoid meeting other members of the opposite sex alone on weekends of the second and third weeks.

Career & Wealth: If someone brings you better prospects, seize the opportunity, but maintain distance from powerful benefactors. For job changes, consider technical industries or media units. There is a chance this month to acquire stocks or other assets with good future potential. Real estate can also be considered. For female Cancers, shopping while traveling in August offers the best chances to find bargains.

Tips: Be cautious of falling from heights, so stay away from dangerous high-rise building terraces or cliffs.

Leo (July 23 - August 23)

This Month's Tarot: The World

"The World" card appears inverted, predicting significant obstacles in Leo's fate in August. The third week is the peak of bad luck, and doing nothing except being low-key and cautious is the safest course of action.

Love: August is not favorable for Leos in relationships—obstacles to union and involuntary breakups are possible. For single Leos, avoiding rigid and overly cautious women and men with explosive tempers due to insufficient abilities is advisable when attending matchmaking events.

Career & Wealth: Being noticeable in the workplace in August will be worse than being ignored. Both supervisors and conservatives view you unfavorably, creating many stumbling blocks in your career path. Pretending to be foolish is the best strategy.

Keep your wealth private, be careful with your wallet outside, and do not casually disclose financial details such as your actual income. Additionally, investing alongside others yields little profit for you, so if you have spare money, it's better to save it.

Tips: Avoid moving house if possible recently. If you must move, do not publicize it widely or invite people over too soon.

Virgo (August 24 - September 22)

This Month's Tarot: The Sun

Virgos will experience a relatively low period, as the "Sun" card appears inverted, indicating negative energy around you. During this time, you may become more sensitive, and scenarios from dreams might occur in reality, so pay attention to any warnings your dreams provide.

Love: Current romances may end without resolution, though you may not clearly see this yet. Therefore, this month you should remain in a state of observation regarding love, avoiding hasty engagements or intimate relationships. Virgos tend to split with lovers due to unreliability on the male side, so relationships between same-sign individuals may be even worse.

Career: Unpleasant events will occur in August, especially noticeable in the last two weeks. Endurance is essential, and revealing too much emotion in the office should be avoided. This month might feel somewhat discouraging, but enduring this period will eventually bring better fortune.

Wealth: Income levels are not high, so avoid spending on expensive items. Consider cheaper alternatives with similar utility. Also, do not let emotions dictate your wallet—spend rationally!

Tips: Virgos will engage in frequent outdoor activities recently, with ample exposure to sunlight. Bring sunscreen and umbrellas.

Libra (September 23 - October 22)

This Month's Tarot: The World

Libra drew the inverted "World" card in August, indicating a challenging road ahead. Influenced by Pluto in the horoscope, this month's success comes at a cost, requiring sacrifices. Libras resonate well with fire signs currently, but interactions with earth signs may be less harmonious, so minimal contact is advised.

Love: Married Libras may notice flaws in their relationships, mostly due to work-related reasons. Initiating care, such as preparing a meal or giving a gift, can ease tensions. Single Libras should avoid romantic entanglements in the second week, and black obsidian can help sever ambiguous relationships.

Career: While opportunities for success exist, they require equal effort. Your hard work deserves more compensation, but the last few days of August are the best time to request a raise from your boss.

Wealth: Libra's recent fortune favors saving over speculation. Any gains will likely be matched by losses, so keeping money in the bank is advisable.

Tips: Overseas travel often leads to losing money. Always carry a flashlight when going out at night. Additionally, avoid breaking ground this month, meaning postponing moves, renovations, or office relocations.

Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)

This Month's Tarot: The Magician

Scorpios also drew the upright "Magician" card, indicating upcoming opportunities to stand out. However, with a strong negative energy field around, health indices may decline. If feeling unwell, a medical check-up is necessary.

Love: With Leo entering the love palace, Scorpios in unrequited love benefit as the closeness with the person you've been waiting for increases. Acting promptly can lead to forming a pair!

Career: Those involved in research or working independently enjoy the most favorable fortunes recently. Your hard work has the potential to bring both fame and fortune. However, be aware that excellent performances may attract those above you attempting to suppress your growth. At such times, assert your rights firmly.

Wealth: Scorpio's best financial period is in the first part of August. Focus on increasing income within your main profession, and delay side jobs or investments.

Tips: Scorpios may easily feel fatigued recently. It's recommended to consume kidney-nourishing foods. If considering a pet, cats bring more blessings than dogs.

Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)

This Month's Tarot: Strength

In August, Sagittarius displays an uncontrollable desire to win and control. The "Strength" card warns Sagittarians that "self-restraint" is more necessary than progress. Watch out for Aries competitors, as they are likely to become your strong rivals.

Love: In the first two weeks, an irresistible member of the opposite sex may appear nearby. However, partnered Sagittarians should avoid infidelity to prevent trouble by the end of the month, including dangers from past loves. For single Sagittarians, most encounters this month won't result in good matches, so don't get too deeply involved. Additionally, if arguing with friends or lovers, take the first step to apologize.

Career: Recent physical strength may not support excessive work, so avoid overtime unless necessary, as it increases the likelihood of unfortunate events. Participating in too many colleague gatherings is not advisable; simple, single-colored casual clothing is recommended.

Wealth: Your financial luck is poor in the short term. Avoid gambling, investments, buying lottery tickets, and traveling long distances to other cities for consumption. Financial planning should focus on preserving current income.

Tips: Long-distance travel in the wild is still not recommended this month. Horseback riding can easily lead to accidents.

Capricorn (December 22 - January 20)

This Month's Tarot: The Star

Capricorn's tarot card for this month is the inverted "Star," serving as a warning: do not easily trust opportunities that come your way. Whether in love or career, matters that need to conclude should be finalized within this month.

Love: Aquarius enters the love palace, bringing relationships without results. If you realize you and your partner aren't suited, break up sooner rather than later, and avoid clandestine affairs or lingering attachments. The first, third, and fourth weeks of the month offer no good matches from blind dates or social gatherings, so participation is unnecessary.

Career: New tasks assigned by superiors may seem like opportunities but are actually traps, and accepting them under pressure is not advisable. If you're considering resignation, August is a good time. However, accepting new positions immediately is not recommended; use this month to clean up previously messy documents and interpersonal relations.

Wealth: Income levels are average this month, possibly involving hard work without rewards. Don't force yourself where luck isn't on your side, and ignore tempting lottery tickets, drawings, or get-rich-quick schemes.

Tips: Cleaning the room may result in breaking glass items.

Aquarius (January 21 - February 18)

This Month's Tarot: Strength

Aquarius enjoys strong career luck in August, while love luck is slightly weaker. Uranus entering the career palace, combined with the appearance of the "Strength" card, enhances your professional aura, promoting rational stability in the workplace. On the other hand, Pisces entering the love palace causes emotional complications.

Love: Aquarian relationships lack fairness—you invest more, but the other party's stance is uncertain. Female Aquarians fare better than males, while those without attachments face more misfortune and can focus on career advancement. If you wish to enjoy life in the short term, don't rush to end your single status.

Career: Recently, Aquarians reliant on mental labor have seen enhanced capabilities, with plans or designs having market potential. Aquarians who frequently travel on business are better suited to act independently, with potential lucky surprises along the way. Days that are multiples of three (e.g., the 3rd, 6th, 9th) increase your luck.

End-of-month savings are average, but not in deficit, allowing for moderate spending. Mid-range products suit you better than high-end goods.

Tips: Traveling is fine, but it's best not to head west.

Pisces (February 19 - March 20)

This Month's Tarot: The Empress

The inverted "Empress" card appearing in Pisces' future destiny signifies that both wealth and love will yield minimal returns in August. However, Jupiter's presence at the edge of the horoscope indicates that someone will support Pisces in critical moments, though a full reversal of fortune is unlikely.

Love: Pisces' romantic life is often entangled in unhealthy relationships, and this month is filled with flirtations. The simultaneous influence of the Moon and Uranus in the love palace makes disputes with the opposite sex common. Avoid excessive drinking in entertainment venues or gatherings to prevent attracting undesirable admirers. Pisces women's charm decreases, and participating in social events or parties will only invite harassment from inferior men, so it's better not to attend.

Career: Jupiter interferes with your rationality, leading to efforts in the wrong direction and no visible results. If you have new plans or ideas, do not submit them to your superior this month; wait for a better time to implement them for greater success.

Wealth: The inverted Empress card predicts that Pisces will have no luck with wealth in August. Do not expect high profits from your investments. Most importantly, stay away from shopping malls and other spending places, and avoid purchasing luxury goods entirely!

Tips: Pisces should avoid getting married in August. Even if you insist on maintaining your wedding date, various reasons may cause delays.
