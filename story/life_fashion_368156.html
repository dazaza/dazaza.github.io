How Zhou Dongyu Removes Dark Circles

How to remove dark circles http://www.0536seo.net/ Reprinted from Beijing News, October 16, 2010, 07:28

Wearing a T-shirt and jeans, with crescent-shaped eyes when she smiles, Zhou Dongyu made her debut as the only contracted "Moyoulang" (a term used by director Zhang Yimou for his chosen actresses). She has made countless people who dream of becoming stars envious, and even Zhou Dongyu herself said, "Before, I could only see such plots in books, such good luck only exists in novels, right?" This girl who experienced an accident in her childhood and lost her biological father, encountered a dramatic life at the age of nearly 18. In fact, her story is more like a play than Jingqiu's life.

Zhou Dongyu, born in 1992, Hometown: Shijiazhuang, Representative work: "Love Under the Hawthorn Tree"

Beijing News: Meeting you in person feels quite lively, unlike Jingqiu in the movie who seems a bit suppressed?

Zhou Dongyu: Because I read the script, before shooting, I let myself get closer and closer to Jingqiu, so I wouldn't be as open-minded as now, allowing me to portray the true psychological state and condition of the character.

Beijing News: Was this something you figured out yourself, or was it told to you by someone else?

Zhou Dongyu: I figured it out myself because I had never been exposed to acting before. I have to make an effort, so I decided to start early like a slow bird.

Beijing News: Did you find it difficult to start filming? After all, there were a group of people watching on the side.

Zhou Dongyu: Yes, I wasn't used to it at first. Thinking about having a big lens pointed at you, following you around, anyone who hasn't been exposed to it would be curious - where is it turning now? Which angle is it shooting me from? So I kept sneaking peeks at the lens, then the assistant director said, Dongyu, don't look at the lens, so I restrained myself, pretending it didn't exist. Eventually, I slowly adapted, entered the plot, stopped thinking about other things, and focused my attention.

Beijing News: During the filming process, some reports came out. I heard that you were very upset at the time?

Zhou Dongyu: At first, when I saw everyone saying that the new Moyoulang was worse off than Wei Minzhi, and also saw some secretly taken photos of me, saying I was ugly, I found it very hard to accept. Originally, I wasn't confident, I didn't know if I could act, and I didn't know if I could handle it, I had no certainty at all. At that time, there were many reports, which really hit me hard. After all, I am still a child. My previous life was very happy and ordinary, just like everyone else. Later, the director told me not to go online, the director asked me to trust him completely. I analyzed every scene I acted in very hard and seriously, and gradually, I didn't care so much anymore.

Beijing News: Do you have any particular favorite mass idols now?

Zhou Dongyu: Current idols... actually, I admire the director a lot. The director has extensive reading, regardless of which aspect, he can talk a lot. Moreover, the director is very good to actors, never giving us pressure. As a newcomer, we will feel a lot of pressure, but he has never scolded us, I think this is particularly precious. And he is very patient, protecting us well. I remember the scene where Dou Xiao went into the river, where he gave me money to buy sports clothes. The riverbed was paved very flat, ensuring there would be no danger, making me feel that we could live healthily and happily in this crew.
