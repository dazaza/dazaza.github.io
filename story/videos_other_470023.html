Buying a Car and a House within 5 Years - Financial Management Section

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-03 13:22:00

Assuming your monthly income is only 2000 yuan, you can still live well. I will help you divide the money into five portions. The first portion is 600 yuan, the second portion is 400 yuan, the third portion is 300 yuan, the fourth portion is 200 yuan, and the fifth portion is 500 yuan.

The first portion is for living expenses. With such a small amount of living expenses, you can only get about ten yuan per day. Breakfast consists of a bowl of rice noodles, an egg, and a cup of soy milk. Lunch includes a fast food meal and a fruit. Dinner involves cooking a small meal at home, making some rice with two dishes, and drinking a glass of milk before sleeping. This way, the monthly food expense would be around 500-600 yuan. However, if you are young and do not have too many health issues yet, this kind of diet should keep you healthy for several years.

The second portion is for socializing and expanding your network. This gives you more flexibility. You can spend 100 yuan on phone bills. Every month, you can treat guests twice, spending 150 yuan each time. Who should you invite? Remember to invite people who are more thoughtful than you, wealthier than you, and those whom you need to express gratitude towards.

By consistently treating guests every month, after a year, your social circle should start generating value for you. Your reputation, influence, and added value will increase, and you'll be seen as someone with a good image and generous nature.

The third portion is for learning. You can allocate 50 to 100 yuan per month to buy books. Although it's not much money, you should carefully read the books you purchase and apply what you've learned. After reading each book, try to explain its content in your own words to others, which will enhance your credibility and improve your approachability. Additionally, save another 200 yuan to participate in training sessions annually without interruption. When your income increases or you have extra savings, you can join higher-level trainings. Participating in quality trainings allows you to meet like-minded friends for free and learn principles that are hard to understand otherwise.

The fourth portion is for traveling. Reward yourself with at least one trip per year. Personal growth comes from continuous experiences. Go on self-guided tours and stay in youth hostels. The world isn't that big. If you travel every year, after a few years, you can mark many places on the map with red flags. These wonderful memories become the driving force of life, giving you more passion and energy to invest in work.

The fifth portion is for investment. Save it first, then you can invest in the stock market or use it as capital for purchasing goods. Small-scale businesses are relatively safe. Open a Taobao account, buy some things in bulk to sell, and even if you lose money, it won't be much. But if you make a profit, you earn not only money but also confidence and courage, along with experience in handling matters. Once you make more money, you can start buying long-term investment plans early, ensuring that you and your family have sufficient funds to take care of yourselves in the future, maintaining a good standard of living regardless of any circumstances.

After enduring this for a year, if you're still earning only 2000 yuan the following year, it's your fault for not improving. It's embarrassing to live so unambitiously. Find out which brand of tofu is the hardest, buy some, and bang your head against it.

For those earning less than 3000 yuan per month, you must find part-time jobs to earn extra money. Don't be picky or overly individualistic, refusing certain tasks or showing no interest. With low income, you must be very diligent and try to find sales-related jobs, such as real estate agents, which are excellent part-time opportunities. You can meet many valuable people and exercise your information-gathering and marketing skills.

This year, you should try to buy as few clothes and shoes as possible. Preferably, buy them using the money earned from your part-time job as a reward for yourself. When you earn extra money, remember to buy a gift for your loved ones, thanking them for supporting your financial plan. Be honest with them about why you are so frugal and share your dreams and goals with them.

There are always businesspeople who need help. Do part-time jobs for them to hone your willpower, eloquence, and work ability. Combine this with your financial management skills, and by the second year, your income should increase to at least 5000 yuan. At the very least, it should reach 3000 yuan; otherwise, your income growth won't even keep up with inflation.

Regardless of how much you earn, remember to divide it into five portions. Increase investments in your physical health to keep your body functioning well, increase investments in socializing to expand your network, increase investments in learning to boost your confidence, increase investments in traveling to broaden your horizons, and increase investments in the future to enhance your returns.

Maintaining this balance, you will gradually accumulate substantial surplus. This is a virtuous cycle of life planning. Your body will get better with more nutrition and care. You'll gain more friends and build valuable networks. Meanwhile, you'll have the means to attend high-end trainings, enriching your abilities in various aspects, broadening your thinking, expanding your vision, and harmonizing your character. In this way, you can gradually achieve your various dreams, buy the houses and cars you need, and prepare a sufficient education fund for your future children.

Life can be designed, careers can be planned, and happiness can be prepared. You can start now. When you are poor, spend less time at home and more outside. When you are wealthy, spend more time at home and less outside. This is the art of living. When you are poor, spend money on others; when you are rich, spend money on yourself. Many people do the opposite.

When you are poor, don't be stingy; be kind to others. When you are rich, learn to let others be good to you and be better to yourself. When you are poor, contribute yourself and let others utilize you as much as possible. When you are rich, preserve yourself and avoid letting others casually use you. These subtle ways of living are understood by few.

When you are poor, spend money for others to see. When you are rich, spend money for your own enjoyment. When you are poor, be generous; when you are rich, don't flaunt wealth. Life has returned to simplicity and tranquility.

Being young is not a mistake, and poverty doesn't need to be feared. Learn to cultivate yourself, understand what valuable items are, know where to invest, and know where to save. This is the key throughout the process. Don't randomly buy clothes; buy fewer but choose a few pieces with good taste. Eat out less often; if you do, treat others to meals, inviting people who have more dreams, thoughts, and efforts than you.

Once the money needed for living is enough, the biggest expenditure should be using your income to fulfill your dreams, spreading your wings boldly to dream, and letting life experience different journeys.
