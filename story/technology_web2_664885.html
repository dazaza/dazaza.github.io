Method One: Hydronic Heating - Free Boiler with Registration at Meijing Comfort Home

The principle of hydronic heating is to use natural gas as energy to heat the circulating water in the wall-hung boiler, which then circulates through closed pipes in the house. The hot water emits warmth, and the heat conversion process is very natural. Generally, there are two ways of heat dissipation for hydronic heating: one is installing flexible pipes under the floor covering materials (floor or tiles) to deliver heat via underfloor heating; the other is through radiators installed on walls or in corners. Due to the relatively complex system and brand integration involved in hydronic heating, it's best for citizens to choose well-known brands for more reliable construction techniques and after-sales service.

Method Two: Self-Heating Flooring

City-wide recruitment for 10 households to experience self-heating flooring for free

Flooring is a relatively mature building material product that has long been widespread in households. Last year, self-heating floors appeared on the market. Taking this year's popular Dewei Picasso self-heating floor as an example, its heating principle is to implant high-tech heating materials in the floor, which converts electricity into heat, making the floor warm.

According to the person in charge of Dewei Picasso self-heating flooring, installation is extremely convenient, similar to laying a carpet. If there are air conditioning wires in the room, it only takes a few hours to lay the floor without needing to route wires or install pipes. Simply plug it in and it heats up within minutes.

At the showroom located in Guannan Shangyu in the southern part of the city, two elderly people touched the intelligent thermostat on the wall and set the temperature to 28 degrees Celsius like controlling central air conditioning. Within a short few minutes, heat was transmitted from the floor. Ten minutes later, the two elderly people took off their shoes and coats and comfortably sat on the floor, asking about the safety of the product and other issues.

Meanwhile, Dewei Picasso self-heating flooring is also conducting a "step" warming campaign. Flexible purchasing options, ultra-safe, rapid heating

According to the person in charge, Dewei Picasso flooring has 15 patents, 3C certification, and a 15-year quality guarantee, suitable for both second-hand homes and new houses.
