If the moon has feelings, it will be entrusted to paradise.

by mimi456 on 2012-01-27 21:25:38

If the moon has feelings, it will be sent to heaven.

Thinking of this, Xiao Xi cried.

She still remembered clearly the scene when An left.

That evening, Xiao Xi had a quarrel with An. She shook off An's hand and stepped out of the crossroads that was still flashing with red lights.

Suddenly, a big hand pulled her over, but unexpectedly a truck ran over that familiar body.

Blood, noise, cries...

"Xi, I'm sorry, I made you angry again, it's my fault. I love you, I will always love you! I hurt so much, I may not be able to accompany you anymore. When I'm not there, take good care of yourself. Don't let me worry, I will always protect you from there. Xi, don't cry, don't be sad. Find a boy who loves you as much as I do, complete our dream with him, go to the place I said I would take you. Promise me, you will be happy."

"An, don't leave me, I won't be capricious anymore, I will listen to you, An..."

Crying and shouting could no longer call back the boy who loved her the most.

Xiao Xi felt that the world was dark, the sky was gray. An was gone, he left completely. He went to a place called heaven, is it cold there? Is there anyone to keep him company? An, Xiao Xi will live well for you, carrying our dreams...

Xiao Xi always remembers what An said in his last moment.

Xiao Xi entered the university that both she and An longed to enter. She was not alone, she knew An would always be with her.

Xiao Xi met many new friends, life was very happy. And there were also many suitors.

But, she didn't agree to any of them, because An once said: "Xi, find a boy who loves you as much as I do, complete our dream."

It was another evening, that figure was so familiar, but Xiao Xi knew it would never be him again.

This boy, as tall as An at 180cm, with the same smile as An, just as shy. Especially the eyes were very similar to An, typical double eyelids. Even the gaze was so alike, and even the blue framed glasses on the bridge of his nose.

An, is that you? Why are you so similar to him?

Later, Xiao Xi met Yu, the boy who looked so much like An.

Every movement, every smile, every look of his reminded Xiao Xi of An.

She thought that it must be An sending Yu to this university to keep her company, because An knew how much Xiao Xi feared loneliness.

An, do you know? Yu likes basketball as much as you, likes singing as much as you, likes watching the sea as much as you, is as gentle and considerate as you, even the number of strokes in your names are the same.

An, Yu. Even the names are so alike.

Xiao Xi was stunned, looking alike on the outside, even the personality and hobbies were so similar. How could it be?

The feeling of An. The smell of An.

Soon, Xiao Xi realized that she seemed to have fallen in love with Yu. That kind of love was not a substitute for An, but a real and true love for Yu just like she loved An.

Yu was so popular, with high popularity. Especially among female fans.

Thinking about the looks, gazes, and proud faces of those female fans, Xiao Xi chose silence, quietly keeping a friend's distance with Yu while carrying An's love.

She thought that only by not caring could she avoid getting hurt. She was afraid of suffering the pain of losing another boy as good as An again.

Xiao Xi transferred schools without telling Yu.

If it's destined, then no matter where in the world, it will not be missed.

After graduation, Xiao Xi chose to work in a coastal city, she wanted to see the sea.

So coincidentally, she met Yu again in that strange city. The cold winter began to feel warm.

On that street, Yu happily pulled Xiao Xi: "Girl, I finally found you, why did you transfer schools without saying a word? Luckily, I still remember you said you also like the sea." "Ah?"

Yu hugged Xiao Xi, "Fool, I like you! I liked you since the first time I saw you. This time I can't miss you again."

Xiao Xi couldn't help but shed tears, at this moment, she also hugged Yu.

An, did you see that? You must be fine in heaven, don't worry about me. Now Yu is here, we will be very happy. We will strive to complete the dreams of the three of us, we will take you to the places you want to go. Xi...
