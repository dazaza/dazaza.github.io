Three students from Melbourne's prestigious high school failed to dissuade their old classmate from drinking, leading to his death from a bicycle fall. They have been sentenced to pay compensation.

by samas49333 on 2011-05-26 10:36:24

A group of old classmates gathered to drink and chat. Unexpectedly, one of them died after falling off a motorcycle while drunk. The deceased's family sued the classmates for not stopping him from drinking. On May 11th, the Jiulongpo District Court made its first-instance ruling: several old classmates and the deceased's lover would each compensate more than 12,000 yuan.

Hu Xiao, aged 39, was from Dadukou. Yan Mo, Zhu Xiao Hua, and Liu Qiang were all his classmates. On the afternoon of September 11th, 2010, Yan Mo, Zhu Xiao Hua, and Liu Qiang invited Hu Xiao to have dinner together, "We haven't seen each other for a long time, let's drink tonight."

At around 6 PM that day, they had dinner and beer at a hot pot restaurant in Dadukou District. After the meal, Zhu Xiao Hua and Hu Xiao had something to take care of, so the rest went to a karaoke bar to sing. Not long after, Hu Xiao returned.

Around midnight, everyone left the karaoke bar. Hu Xiao then rode his motorcycle to pick up his lover Zhang Mou. Hu Xiao was carrying Zhang Mou from Matwang Township towards Shuinan direction. When the vehicle reached the Shuinan overpass bridge section, Hu Xiao lost control of the motorcycle, causing both the vehicle and people to flip into the roadside greenbelt. Hu Xiao died, and Zhang Mou was injured.

The recognition statement from the Jiulongpo District Traffic Police Brigade stated that Hu Xiao's blood alcohol content at the time was 204.1mg/100ml. Additionally, he did not have a motor vehicle driver's license and took improper measures when encountering unexpected situations, thus shouldering full responsibility for the accident.

"If they hadn't let him drink then, none of this would have happened... My child is only 6 years old, how will we mother and son live on?" Hu Xiao's wife, Ms. Xiang, believed that her husband's accidental death was due to the irresponsibility of his old classmates, so she filed a lawsuit against Yan Mo, Zhu Xiao Hua, Liu Qiang, and Zhang Mou at the Jiulongpo District Court. Zhang Mou did not appear in court.

Yan Mo and others felt very wronged and regretful —— they never expected that this drinking session would cause such a huge disaster. They claimed that during the meal, they had advised Hu Xiao not to drink, but he insisted; after the meal, they also advised Hu Xiao not to ride the motorcycle, but he still didn’t listen. After drinking, Hu Xiao rode the motorcycle to pick up his lover, and due to emotional issues, an argument occurred between them, leading to the accident.

Several old classmates expressed that seeing their close buddy pass away like this was heart-wrenching, but they found it hard to accept the demand for compensation as they had already fulfilled their duty of reminding and advising.

The deceased

bears primary responsibility

After reviewing the case, the Jiulongpo District Court believed that the deceased, Hu Xiao, being a fully capable civil actor, should have known that driving a motorcycle required a driver’s license, and should have been able to foresee the dangers of driving under the influence. However, without a motor vehicle driver's license and being heavily intoxicated, he drove a motor vehicle and took inappropriate measures when faced with an emergency situation, resulting in an accident and his death. Therefore, Hu Xiao should bear primary responsibility for these consequences.

Classmates

had faults due to ineffective persuasion

The court believed that Yan Mo, Zhu Xiao Hua, and Liu Qiang invited Hu Xiao to eat and drink, knowing the risks of driving under the influence, failed to fulfill their duties of reminding and advising, and thus bore some fault, hence the ruling to compensate 12,000 yuan each. Zhang Mou, knowing the risks of riding with a drunk driver, still chose to board Hu Xiao's motorcycle, failing to fulfill her duty of reminding and advising, and thus also bore some fault.

Chongqing Evening News Tang Zhongming
