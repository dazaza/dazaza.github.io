Company Profile

Pioneer of Enterprise Management (PEM for short), BANGYUAN Management Consulting Company, is one of the most powerful professional management consulting companies in China. It owns a large-scale professional outdoor expansion training base and is a strategic partner of Malaysia International Training Institution, Beijing Times Guanghua, Beijing Modern Central European Management Science Research Institute, and Gaoyao Guangxin Agricultural Ecology Park.

BANGYUAN Consultants adhere to the service philosophy of "Customer interests above all", following the principle of "Our success is built on the basis of customer success", providing various types of enterprises with management consulting services in the following areas: outdoor expansion training, sandbox simulation series management courses, corporate strategic management, organizational transformation, corporate image design, business process reengineering, human resources management system design, modern distribution, brand management, corporate mergers and acquisitions and integration, marketing management, financial management system, etc., offering professional high-quality services.

Through years of management consulting and training practice, BANGYUAN Consultants have formed their unique consulting and training capabilities and core advantages. They possess a large number of senior international lecturers both domestically and internationally, professional outdoor expansion instructors, forging a highly competitive team of lecturers. They have provided professional services to more than 300 large and medium-sized enterprises at home and abroad, including Jingxin South China Branch, China Unicom Guangzhou Branch, China Netcom, HP China, Construction Bank of China Guangdong Branch, Jizhida Supermarket in Guangdong, Tianmao Department Store, and other well-known large enterprises at home and abroad.

For production, R&D, procurement, and engineering personnel, the course design focuses on:

1. The planning and rigor of work

2. Cross-departmental team communication awareness and ability

3. Grasping information and reasonable use of resources

4. Cost control, quality assurance

5. Courage to innovate, constantly expanding

For administrative, human resources, and financial personnel, the course design focuses on:

1. Good service awareness during team activities

2. Comprehensive communication, coordination, and cooperation

3. Meticulous and rigorous working style

4. Understanding the profound meaning of the team, understanding one's own value and importance in the team

5. Analyzing and solving problems

For sales personnel, the course design focuses on:

1. Salespeople's perseverance, intelligence, fearlessness, and courage to move forward

2. Salespeople's self-transcendence, positive mindset

3. Responsibility and sense of honor of salespeople

4. Proactive communication spirit

5. Comprehensive teamwork awareness

For all employees of the company, the course design focuses on:

1. Self-breakthrough, transcending oneself

2. Efficient teams, efficient operations

3. Improving communication, mutual cooperation

4. People-oriented, common progress

5. Pursuing innovation, actively seeking change

For newly hired employees, the course design focuses on:

1. Identifying with the enterprise, being proactive

2. Improving oneself, continuous learning

3. Integrating into the team, jointly improving

4. Strengthening communication, respecting others

5. Highly responsible, meticulous

For middle-level leaders, the course design focuses on:

1. Outstanding leadership skills
