Speech cloud drives SAIC's pioneering iVoka

by juyaqufa004 on 2011-12-14 16:26:02

How to make the car a part of the Internet of Things, internet-connected vehicles, SAIC iVoka, this is an innovation with very strategic significance. At the 2011 Guangzhou Auto Show, the inkaNet intelligent network driving system, which successfully pioneered the 3G era of the automotive industry, developed new functions again. By using the globally innovative car voice interaction technology - voice cloud driving iVoka, it was the first to bring a tailor-made human-machine interaction driving experience for Chinese consumers. With the four major features of "innovation, insight, interaction, and smart technology", it meets the needs of the changing consumption behavior of the new generation, redefines the relationship between "people and cars", and once again leads the future trend of automotive information innovation technology. The inkaNet-iVoka was the first to truly integrate intelligent voice technology into automotive applications, completely transforming the traditional driving experience of consumers through information innovation technology.

**Innovation: Completely transform the traditional driving experience**

Whether it's the Siri on iPhone4S or inkaNet-iVoka, they are both revolutionary breakthroughs based on Internet voice technology, occupying the forefront of global information technology, and innovating trends leading industrial changes without prior agreement. inkaNet-iVoka was the first to truly integrate intelligent voice technology into automotive applications, thoroughly transforming the traditional driving experience of consumers through information innovation technology.

**Insight: The need for driving automatic safety**

inkaNet -iVoka can fully achieve "talking without touching", providing consumers with more comprehensive automatic safety protection. It helps drivers focus all their attention on driving, keeping their hands on the steering wheel and eyes on the road, without needing to touch the touch screen. The iVoka human-machine intelligent interaction system eliminates the need to pick up the phone to browse, and even sending text messages while driving becomes unnecessary, thus automatically reducing the probability of accidents.

**Interaction: The simplest interaction**

The method of information interaction continues to evolve, from pen and paper, typewriters, keyboards, to computers, mice, Win menus, and the slide-touch control methods of smartphones. Ultimately, these complex communication methods will return to the origin - language. All functions can be achieved through the most relaxed and natural dialogue, entering an era of voice interaction where "people talk to cars". Only in this way can hands and eyes be freed to do more important things; therefore, the simplest interaction is the best interaction!

**Smart Technology: Know what you want, think what you think**

iVoka is actually smarter and more considerate than people expect. Since everyone's voice is unique, in the future, iVoka may even function as a voiceprint key. Just one sentence can adjust the door, lights, steering wheel position, seat position, and safety belt height. People can also tell iVoka, "Check our fuel level and take us to the nearest gas station." These functions are not far away. In the future, iVoka will definitely bring more surprises to people. With the deepening of humanized technology, it may even make people forget that it is just a program. It can be like the most loyal and wise partner, guiding the way all along. Know what you want, think what you think.
