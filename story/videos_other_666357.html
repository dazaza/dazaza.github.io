[Thanksgiving] A Letter to Parents from College Students - Six Star Hotel

by teb25u6n1 on 2012-02-15 16:50:56

Dear Mom and Dad:

Since I left home for school, I have been negligent in contacting you, and I feel really sorry about that. Now, I want to report my current situation to you. But before you continue reading, please sit down first. Don't read past this point until you've calmed down, and be sure to finish reading, okay?

I'm doing fine overall. The fracture of my skull and the concussion are almost healed. This happened shortly after I arrived here when I jumped out of the window during a dormitory fire and got injured. I was in the hospital for only two weeks, and now I am basically back to normal. The headaches come only once a day now.

Fortunately, after the fire, a guy from the nearby gas station saw it and promptly called the fire department and ambulance. He also visited me in the hospital. Since I had nowhere to live after the fire, he kindly invited me to his apartment, which is actually just a basement, but it's still quite nice. He is a very good young man. We fell deeply in love later, and we even planned to get married! We haven't set the exact date yet, but it will definitely be before anyone notices that I'm pregnant!

Yes, Mom and Dad, I'm pregnant. I know it's not ideal, but I think you would also like to become grandparents. And I know you will warmly welcome the baby's arrival! Our wedding may have to be postponed because he accidentally contracted something, and we can't pass the pre-wedding blood test since I also got infected from him. I know... He is a good person, though lacking in education, but he has ambition... Please don't be angry...

Alright, alright, let me tell you the real truth now! I didn't break any bones, I didn't have a concussion, I wasn't hospitalized, I'm not pregnant, I'm not engaged, I'm not infected, and I don't even have a boyfriend. However, I do have some bad news to tell you: I failed six out of eight subjects this semester—mathematics, English, computer science, etc.! I hope you can look at this matter from the right perspective.

Thank you, my dearest parents!

With all my love,

XXX

November 25, 2010

Editor's Postscript: XXX might not be great at math, but his psychology skills are quite good—six-star hotel quality!

Wishing everyone a happy Thanksgiving! May all who share this achieve their wishes and stay healthy and happy!
