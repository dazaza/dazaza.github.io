Is automotive beauty technology easy to learn? Is it better to learn automotive beauty in a store or in a school?

In fact, learning anything depends on your comprehension and self-study ability. Generally, it takes about a week to learn the basics of automotive beauty technology. However, to become proficient, it may take half a year! I suggest a combination of theory and practice is better. This includes waxing, polishing, glazing, cleaning interior, and washing cars. If you want to master automotive beauty thoroughly, there is still a lot to learn. The simpler ones involve both interior and exterior decoration, including leather seat covers, floor mats, and hanging decorations. More complex ones include car washing, interior cleaning, and car paint waxing. Higher technical skills involve car modification.

If you really want to learn, you can first visit the Lai Bao Automotive Beauty shop, or call the Lai Bao Automotive Beauty School to learn more, then go to the bookstore to read some books on this subject for a simple understanding. Generally speaking, if you want to earn more than 2800 yuan per month, it will take 3-7 months to learn, depending on individual comprehension ability. Of course, after doing it for a long time, you can become a senior technician or a store manager, earning over 6200 yuan is quite normal. The learning content includes automotive beauty, sun film, anti-explosion film, film application, electrical appliances, audio-visual equipment, sales, and store operation, covering a wide range of aspects. It depends on which area you focus on, as mastering each part is not easy. Therefore, it's best to undergo specialized training.

There are many people who want to learn automotive beauty technology but don't know how exactly to learn it or where to go. Should they find an automotive beauty shop to be an apprentice or work as a car washer, slowly learning, or should they directly find an automotive beauty school to study both theory and practice together? This question troubles many people. It can be said that each method has its own advantages. If you want to learn quickly and effectively acquire useful skills, it’s better to go to an automotive beauty school. At Lai Bao Automotive Beauty School, students learn both theory and practice. They study a portion of theory, then go to the direct-operated store to practice until they have no problems with actual operations. This makes up for the shortcomings of either pure theory or pure practice, allowing students to quickly integrate knowledge and be able to do things themselves until they become proficient. Techniques like film application, waxing, and glazing mainly rely on practice and countless repetitions. The direct-operated store provides cars specifically for students to practice on. Only in this way can one comprehensively grasp automotive beauty and decoration skills, laying a good foundation for future job hunting and opening a store.
