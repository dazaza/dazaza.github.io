Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:48:45

The school adopts a combination of work and study, practical training facilities that rank first in the province, a simple, fast and accurate teaching model, fully closed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Municipal Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shi Huo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking diligently for change", continuously carried out teaching and management reforms, firmly established the awareness of "market competition", taken the market as the guide, and taken the enterprise demand as the standard. We have explored new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulated rich teaching and management experience, and students have received widespread praise from employers after employment.

First, with enterprise standards as the guide, we have equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, built a numerical control training workshop equipped with more than 50 CNC lathes, CNC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines and other equipment, and a general lathe training workshop. The school has also built communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shaping rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

Second, with job positions as the guide, we have created an enterprise-style teaching model.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, in the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job postings, students hold positions such as team leaders, line leaders, and employees in their "work". And through regular evaluations and examination results, outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative in learning.

There are quality monitoring indicators for students' daily "work" situations, which are statistically analyzed monthly. According to the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated. The composition of wages includes: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively stimulates students' initiative in learning and competitive awareness, allowing them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned faster and better.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their schooling, meet the needs of job positions, and initially reach the enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

Third, with enterprise demands as the guide, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students and formulated a daily standard for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training.

Fully learn the enterprise's employee management mode, cultivate qualified talents welcomed by enterprises. Enterprises not only need skilled workers with solid technical skills but also pay more attention to employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall quality require the efforts and cooperation of all aspects of the school. Quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance the overall quality of our school's students, our school mainly highlights two characteristics in management:

Characteristic Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," hiring retired military officers dedicated to quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, starting from dressing, behavior, manners, and housekeeping, let students develop good living habits, cultivate students' excellent organizational discipline style, and make them quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Characteristic Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, vocational quality courses are offered. These courses are divided into theoretical lectures and practical exercises, complementing and enhancing each other. The theoretical lectures focus on practical approaches to transform students' thoughts, while practical exercises use the "personal experiences" of students to consolidate teaching outcomes, thus making these vocational quality requirements the voluntary actions of students.

For vocational quality expansion training, our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before truly interning and working, entering the formal enterprise employee state in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to job positions.

Fourth, with employment as the guide, we have properly completed the employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's numerical control major is mainly automobile parts manufacturing. All student internship units are mechanical manufacturing enterprises listed in the world's top 500. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the satisfaction rate of student employment exceeds 95%.

Fifth, thoughtful后勤services, pioneering the "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students in our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On the one hand, it reduces the burden on parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the needs of students' physical development, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adapting to intensive study, effectively avoiding the adverse consequences caused by students eating outside the school.

The school has complete service facilities such as student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms. The logistics department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to be proactive, thoughtful, close, and convenient.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of three years of tuition and accommodation fees.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from society and giving back to society, relying on strong educational strength and forward-looking educational concepts, we save maximum costs for parents. Students only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees. The three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through the combination of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specially established a "Hongzhi Class" to fully or partially exempt all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly comprehensive quality students from humble backgrounds. Students in our school enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

Seventh, cultivating talents with advanced school culture and shaping a strong brand.

In the process of running the school, our school constantly summarizes experiences and lessons, creatively extracting our school culture.

Our Spirit (Our Team): Consensus, Learning, Collaboration, Innovation, Inclusion

Our Mission (Achieving Mission): Laying the Foundation for Corporate Wealth.

The extraction of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and direction of struggle for our school. The practice process of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.

Small victories depend on intelligence, great victories depend on virtue; small success relies on friends, great success relies on opponents. We are willing to co-develop with all vocational education schools in Hebei, providing more and better learning environments and employment opportunities for young people with aspirations.
