Common ways to eat natto:

First, place it in a bowl and stir evenly with chopsticks. Then add prepared condiments like chopped green onions, seaweed flakes, sesame seeds, raw eggs, dried small sardines, etc., and mix thoroughly. Finally, mix in mustard and soy sauce and place it on hot rice to eat. Avoid overheating when cooking with natto to prevent the death of natto bacteria.

With the advancement of food processing technology, natto is now available in many different flavors, allowing individuals to choose based on taste and need.

Japanese-style eating method:

Natto Rice

Ingredients: one box of natto, two packets of seasoning, one bowl of rice.

Preparation: Open the seasoning packets and add them to the natto, stirring until long sticky threads appear. Mix it with freshly steamed rice to make a fragrant bowl of natto rice.

Characteristics: A convenient and quick food, the most common way to eat natto.

Regular eating method:

Take one box of natto, open the sealed membrane, tear open the two seasoning packets, squeeze them onto the natto, and stir evenly until sticky threads form before eating.

Chinese-style eating method:

Spicy Vegetable Natto

Stir the natto until sticky threads form, then add the contents of the two seasoning packets and an appropriate amount of spicy sauce, continuing to stir. Serve with tender cucumber slices or cilantro and other vegetables.

Fruit Natto

Reveal the natto's sealed membrane, do not add any seasoning, stir evenly to form sticky threads, and set aside. Wash and cut your favorite fruits (such as apples, pineapples, etc.) into small cubes and place them in a bowl. Pour the mixed natto over the fruit, stir evenly, and you have a fragrant and refreshing fruit natto.

Different style natto:

After adding the contents of the two seasoning packets to the natto and stirring evenly to form sticky threads, add chopped green onions and continue stirring to create a scallion-flavored natto. Those who dislike scallions can substitute ginger shreds for another unique flavor.

Cold dish eating method:

Natto can be eaten with various cold dishes, combined according to personal preference. It is convenient to eat and nutritionally rich.

Additionally, natto can be used in various dishes. It can be stir-fried, deep-fried, wrapped in rice with seaweed as natto rolls. It can also be added to fried rice, congee, buns, dumplings, noodles, and soups.
