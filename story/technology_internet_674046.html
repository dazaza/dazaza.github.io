Lotus Cars introduced British Lotus technology with quality control by British Lotus. The official website of Lotus Cars positions the Lotus L3 as a trendy sports car for players, focusing on trends and sports. The Lotus L3 automatic is equipped with the F4A41 automatic transmission. Coupled with the Campro engine designed and developed by British Lotus Engineering, its performance is beyond doubt, making it the most driving-friendly automatic car in the same class domestically. This is also a structure that comes standard in many high-end cars currently.

The brother-in-arms Lotus L5 has a larger body size than the L3, with a wheelbase reaching 2670mm. Meanwhile, the L5 adopts the popular five-door design and short-tail sloping back shape, with a large-mouth air intake grille that has great impact. Both models are equipped with the "Lotus Campro S4PH 16V DOHCCPS" engine, applying two core technologies: "CPS Variable Valve Lift System + VIM Variable Intake Manifold System", which are the high-tuning versions.
