The Mobile Phone Triggered a War

Main actors: Little Brother Lou, Sister-in-law Lou

Special guests: Sister Jiao, Sister Lou

Battlefield: The Lou Family Courtyard. Brother Lou's corner of the kang (a traditional Chinese bed). Sister-in-law Lou's head of the kang. Sister Jiao and Sister Lou in the middle of the ground.

Cause of the war: Brother Lou bought two mobile phones.

Content of the war: Little Brother Lou impulsively bought two mobile phones, one for 200 and the other for 450. Happily, he brought them home, expecting to receive a smile like flowers from his wife. But unexpectedly, Sister-in-law Lou became furious and started cursing: "Who asked you to buy two? Didn't I tell you to buy just one? You never listen to me, treating my words as if they were farts. How can we continue living like this?" Little Brother Lou timidly lowered his head and said, "Don't I have you in my heart?" Sister-in-law Lou jumped up on the kang. "You still have the nerve to say you have me in your heart? Why did you buy the 450 one for yourself and only the 200 one for me? If you had bought me a 250 one, it would show that you value me. Do you think I'm worth only 200 in your eyes? Tell me!" Little Brother Lou stole a glance at his sister-in-law and said, "Then I'll go give the person who sold me the phone another 100, making it as if I spent three times 250 on the two phones, okay?" Sister Lou gave Brother Lou a dirty look, jumped to his side, and shouted, "Who are you calling 250? Today, I won't let this go with you." With that, she rushed into the kitchen, grabbed a kitchen knife, and swung it at Brother Lou. Little Brother Lou didn't back down; he picked up a broom and fought back. They both fought fiercely. Sister-in-law Lou screamed, "You ungrateful wretch, how dare you fight back! I'll take you down!" Brother Lou also got angry, "Don't get too presumptuous. I've endured you for long enough. Today, we will not stop until we determine who is superior." Both filled with energy, their battle was as intense as the swordplay on Mount Hua or the territorial battles in Shanghai. Observing Sister-in-law Lou squint her eyes and pout her lips, while Brother Lou clenched his teeth and bulged his cheeks. Suddenly, Brother Lou wasn't paying attention and his broom was chopped in half by Sister-in-law Lou. Furious, she pointed at him and scolded, "Do you even have any ability left? You can't even beat me in a fight. Let's get divorced right now!" Brother Lou's eyes glared menacingly, "Divorce then, I've put up with you for years, it's time for me to be free." As they spoke, they walked towards the door. Seeing the situation escalating, Sister Jiao and Sister Lou knew they had to intervene. Sister Jiao quickly stepped forward, pulled Sister-in-law Lou back, slapped her twice, and pummeled her with her fists, leaving her with black eyes. Sister Lou didn't hesitate either, kicking Sister-in-law Lou in the nose and stomach. Beaten, Sister-in-law Lou rolled on the ground. Sister Jiao and Sister Lou dragged her to the police station, urgently reporting a case of domestic violence. "Look what the husband has done to his wife!" This event caused quite a stir. The police chief personally took the case and recorded statements. The chief, extremely concerned, drank his hot tea in one gulp, ignoring the blisters forming on his mouth. He sat down and asked, "Who is the victim? Let me see." Sister Jiao and Sister Lou pointed behind them, "It's her, our beloved older sister." The chief frowned after seeing her, enraged, "You mean her? Not only is she not injured, but even if she were dead, I wouldn't care. She disgusts me." Sister Jiao and Sister Lou were confused. Turning around, they realized they had mistakenly dragged Fan Bingbing instead of their sister-in-law. They stood there dumbfounded. At that moment, Sister-in-law Lou spoke, "Jiao, Juan, it's me, your older sister." Hearing the pitiful voice, it was indeed their beautiful sister-in-law. But why did her face transform into the ugly version of Fan Bingbing? What went wrong? Suddenly, both screamed and dialed 120. The hospital was swift; within five minutes, the ambulance arrived at the police station. The three said goodbye to the police and rushed to the renowned Xinzhai Central Hospital, where they sought emergency care. After examining her, the expert sighed, "Not good. The young lady has been disfigured. Our hospital doesn't have the proper medicine to treat her. Accept the loss." Upon seeing herself in the mirror, Sister-in-law Lou burst into tears. "Oh my heavens, how could I turn into Fan Bingbing? How can I possibly show my face in public? I don't want to live anymore." Meanwhile, Brother Lou also arrived at the hospital. Bursting in, he cried out, "Wife, my wife! I don't want to divorce you. That was just something I said in anger. How could I ever let you go? Where else can I find such a good wife?" But when he looked down and saw the old sister sitting on the ground, he vomited. "How could that ugly Fan Bingbing be here? Meeting her is truly bad luck." Sister-in-law Lou became even more heartbroken, her tears flowing like the Yangtze River, flooding the hospital corridors. Sister Jiao and Sister Lou felt uneasy too. Steeling themselves, they told Brother Lou, "You must be strong. The one on the ground is Sister-in-law. It's our fault she's disfigured." With a despondent look, Brother Lou stared at Sister-in-law Lou and said, "Let's go home, no matter what you become, I will never abandon you. In my heart, you will always be the most beautiful. At home, you can use the 450 phone, and I'll use the 200 one. From now on, I will never make you angry again." Moved, Sister-in-law Lou cried and ran her nose. The two embraced and returned home. From then on, the handsome Brother Lou and Sister-in-law Lou, who had turned into the ugly likeness of Fan Bingbing, lived happily as before. Thus ended the conflict triggered by the mobile phone, concluding with a relatively happy ending. However, Sisters Jiao and Lou were deeply regretful. They had disfigured Sister-in-law Lou, and Shangpinrongzhi Wuhan Investment Management Co., Ltd. would bear responsibility for life. Henceforth, the two dared not throw punches recklessly again. Actually, they only wanted to prevent Brother and Sister Lou from divorcing and thought going to the police station would solve everything. Unknowingly, they caused a major disaster.
