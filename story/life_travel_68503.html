The Two Rivers and Four Lakes Project is a large-scale environmental protection project, urban infrastructure construction project, and tourist attraction construction project in Guilin City. The first phase of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes Project has fundamentally improved the ecological environment of Guilin City, perfected urban functions, opened up a new pattern of central city tourism, inherited and promoted Guilin's long-standing history and culture, and enhanced the level and quality of the city.

In May 2002, Guilin Ring City Water System Construction and Development Co., Ltd. established the Water Amusement Branch, mainly operating water amusement projects on the ring city water system of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes. The company has six departments: Office, Finance Department, Cruise Operation Management Department, Sales Department, Business Department, and Dock Management Department. It has more than 200 employees and over 40 cruise ships, with the ability to receive more than 6,000 people per day.

The current tourism routes operated by the company, such as the Two Rivers and Four Lakes Ring City Water System Tour and the Inner Lake Fishing Eagle Fishing Ecological Tour, have achieved good economic and social benefits. In particular, the night tour of the Two Rivers and Four Lakes has become a new brand representative of Guilin's night tour market. Tourists can not only visit three main scenic areas with distinct characteristics: Banyan Cypress Lake Scenic Area - Chinese classical garden, Gui Lake Scenic Area - ecological garden in harmony with nature, and Mulong Lake Scenic Area - Song Dynasty historical and cultural garden, and appreciate the newly built famous bridge exhibition garden, famous flowers, trees, and grass exhibition garden, pavilion exhibition garden, and sculpture exhibition garden within the scenic area, but also enjoy more than ten traditional famous mountains around the water system, such as Elephant Mountain, Fubo Mountain, Diecai Mountain, Yao Mountain, Baoji Mountain, and Old Man Mountain from the boat. Different from the natural mountain-water tour of the Li River, the Two Rivers and Four Lakes Ring City Water System Tour highlights the profound historical accumulation and cultural connotation of Guilin as a famous historical and cultural city in China. Through the restoration and communication of the ring city water system, a number of important cultural relics and ancient sites that were previously submerged or forgotten, such as the Sarira Pagoda (Tang Dynasty), Song Dynasty city wall, Li Jishen's former residence, and the place where General Ye Ting was imprisoned, have been repaired, protected, and excavated. Guilin's profound history and culture, beautiful natural landscape, and good ecological environment are perfectly and harmoniously unified in the Two Rivers and Four Lakes Ring City Water System.

In the near future, Guilin's Two Rivers and Four Lakes Ring City Water System will become another golden waterway as famous as the Li River.

"Tourists come by reputation and return satisfied" is the motto of our company.
