I. Product Introduction:

This machine uses aluminum alloy as its material, cast in one piece and assembled together with brass slope. By using appropriate rubber rollers, it is equipped with a roller tension adjustment device for different sizes of rice and an air volume adjustment device, allowing users to easily adjust the machine to achieve over 99% de-husking rate in one pass for various types of rice. The grains are cleanly separated from the husks, while maintaining the integrity of the rice without breakage, fuzziness, or discoloration.

The machine features an attractive design, durability, small size, light weight, simple operation, portability, easy part replacement, stable mechanical performance, low noise, fast de-husking speed, and meets the national new standard requirements specified in GB1350-1999 and GB/T17891-1999 for classifying rice indicators. It also utilizes 16MnDG low-temperature pipes.

The JLGJ-45 type testing huller is widely used in grain, seed, agricultural colleges, commercial inspection, and scientific research departments for testing rice de-husking rate, quality of rice, seed vitality, and rapid germination experiments. It is an essential instrument for these purposes.
