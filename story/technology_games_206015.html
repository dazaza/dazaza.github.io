Some experiences and insights about farming gold in games

by lk0gamej on 2009-07-19 06:21:43

Hello everyone, let me introduce myself first. I'm from Sichuan Zone 2, Level 54 Paladin, ID: noob_god. With the principle that the experience of resetting stats twice should never mislead newcomers, I'd like to briefly talk about paladin map grinding.

Here, I will only discuss the map grinding of the Paladin class, and some experiences and insights regarding completely level 54 PK (Paladins were nerfed in Act 2 to an unimaginable extent).

Firstly, you need to be clear that the Paladin is absolutely not suitable for solo grinding.

Secondly, the Paladin is definitely not just a healer. The reason why Paladins are powerful is because of their various imba buffs and their ability to achieve effects other classes cannot without needing much equipment.

Lastly, the Paladin's cute appearance...

So all my experience, whether it's stat allocation or equipment choice, starts from this basis.

Stat Allocation: Slow Healing. There are some differing opinions on this. I think leveling it to 5 is enough. Of course, if you want to max it out, that's fine too, because this skill is quite useful in the early game.

However, based on my experience, after reaching HZ (later maps), this skill is basically useless. I'll explain the reasons for abandoning it later.

Leveling to 5 is due to the fast prerequisite skills. Buying a level 5 Blackwood Cross can add +1 more Slow Healing, saving where we can.

Weapon Blessing: Must be maxed, no explanation needed.

Angel Blessing: This one deserves a mention. Some might add it as a prerequisite skill (because Honor Blessing requires +5). In reality, this is an even more imba buff than Weapon Blessing... When added to elemental ammo or mechanical rogues, it clears faster than g. So, it's also maxed.

Guardian Badge: Can be maxed or not. It's up to you. Personally, I find it more useful when grinding ruins, fighting bulls, and against HZ bosses, so I've maxed it. This is a personal decision.

Honor Blessing: Must be maxed, no explanation needed. Just a side note, if buying costumes, clothes enhance this skill. Now I regret not enhancing my main skill on my clothes...

Quick Healing: If you're not adding points here, why play a Paladin?

Holy Light Guardian: Level 1 is enough. When菠萝垂地(everyone jumps), you don't have to stand on the ground and smirk.

Light Revenge: This should be maxed too. I'm still researching the cost-effectiveness of this skill. However, I haven't seen any Paladins who didn't max this skill, hoho.

Soul Sacrifice: 5 points as a prerequisite, there's no way around it. To use resurrection and show off at level 45, it has to be added.
