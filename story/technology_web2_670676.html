Oblivious to the passage of time

by icvjas83 on 2012-02-21 09:58:23

Always drifting with a blank. The plain face washed clean can no longer return to the once youthful years, bearing memories that cannot be shaken off, the heart gradually calms down. Turning around, there's a lush green prosperity that never belonged to oneself, how could one bear the wind singing and the fleeting shadow of life... Once obsessed with the distant emerald green, harboring affection for the out-of-tune pink memories of the mundane world, infatuated with the intoxicating beauty after a reluctant farewell, always fond of the faint taste brought by sadness, liking the soft black hair caressed by the gentle breeze, occasionally glistening at the corner of the eyes, a cluster of thought marks between the eyebrows in a snap, a moment of paleness among the black hair, negating the perfect choice, confirming the interpretation of life.

The traces left by time intoxicated the bewildered eyes, the scenery ahead seemed poetic and melodious, yet very faint, the cycle of misty rain seemed like a dream and clouds, yet very deep, the interwoven heart was like water and moon, yet very painful. The yearning feeling enveloped the sorrow of the mundane world, the solo dance of tears avoided the crazy reality, disturbing the strings of life.

The passing clouds of simple years sealed the memories of the heart, silent fragments continuously played, aging whose withered appearance, the sorrow of the quiet night wrote the current rhythm, wanting to borrow tea to express the overflowing river water in the heart, but the pen tip hesitated hard to describe. The confused flow of years forever locked the memory outside of time and space, thousands of words couldn't express the buried mood, not knowing when this contemplation took place, several flavors, drunkenly circling around the heart.

Is life too short? Unable to finish painting the beauty of a blooming heart, ultimately walking away silently in regret; Is time too hasty? A frown already consumed all the splendor, petals filling the courtyard, saddened the once painted youth. If it were possible, please borrow three lifetimes to fill in the completeness left by life; if allowed, wish to let time flow backwards, solely deciphering the blurred blank pages in the heart. However, life has no "if", everything is predestined just to perfectly draw imperfection, just to add flaws to purity.

Half a lifetime of cycles, never recalling the light dancing in laughter, never lamenting the broken-winged dirge after grievances, never lingering in the dazzling luxury of virtuality. Because, there was no pure waterside pavilion or stunning red carpet in the past stage; because, the dirges of the past always chilled someone's heart, always dissolved happiness beyond sadness; because, before the luxurious life filled with a cold world, it froze the smiles under the sunlight.

Love and hate always dissolve in entanglement, joy and sorrow are always performed in dramas, complexity always spreads during hesitation. Without the twists given by life, there would be no splendid life. Every growth will have different accompaniments. Life in this world is always tangled like roots. Love is similar, suitable people rarely appear at the right time to love. Stories always intertwine mistakenly. Two imperfect halves meet while the other half still wanders elsewhere, arriving late. Such delay inevitably leads to a tragic ending.

Even the best compass can't draw a perfect life, even the most perfect love won't have so-called eternal vows, even the best times won't last forever, even the most beautiful scenery is merely a coordinate in life. After experiencing vicissitudes, gradually becoming naive, looking back at the roads walked, ignorance will disappear into clarity, memories like dreams will be deeply buried in the heart. There's no need to waste a lifetime guarding a certain moment; there's no need to wait a lifetime for a myth. What should come will eventually appear, what should go will eventually leave, because all sensations in life aren't electromagnetic induction, they come and go as they please.

In plain times and brocade years, reminiscing about the past always lightly plucks the strings of sadness. Lost beauties are always beautiful. If one tries to hold onto them according to one's will, beauty will fade. Listen! The ignorance of childhood, the recklessness of youth, the wild imagination of adolescence, the foolish excitement of youth, softly sing the freedom, courage, dreams, and trials of life. Life is full of busyness, steps can't stop for any single moment of beauty, pain can't spread for any segment of sadness.

The disappearance of flowing years, turning around and gazing, looking back on the once unparalleled grace, drunk eyes facing each other. Reality and dreams are clearly divided, don't reminisce about the plum fragrance of another year. Ultimately, sadness is a kind of beauty, pain is a kind of happiness, longing is a kind of test. Whether beautiful or tragic, they all belong to every fragment of life.

When parting with Wang, I swore, from then on we would be mortal enemies, I would never forgive him, I would hate him for the rest of my life, not only myself but also making my future children hate him.

Actually, calming down, there wasn't really deep hatred between me and Wang. In those five years together, the two of us went through so much, loved so deeply, and when we broke up, it was so complicated. So the deeper the love, the deeper the hatred. That hatred was like a seed planted in the heart, sprouting, growing tips, forming ears whenever spring came, gradually overshadowing reason, making an originally elegant mood complex and painful.

The deeper the hatred, the deeper the sadness. Hatred slowly devoured happiness until one day, looking in the mirror, the reflection showed eyes full of resentment and a face full of weariness. This self made me feel sad.

I realized then, that hatred was just torturing oneself until one became repulsive.

Still remember that year, Wang fell in love with me at first sight. Wang had a good handwriting, writing a passionate love letter every day, quickly captivating me. Seeing his words was like seeing the person, someone who writes well must be delicate. At that time, I was too young, didn't think too much, and just fell into the carefully woven love net, unable to extricate myself.

After several years of intimate living, gradually I became capricious and violent, these traits slowly revealed themselves. At first, because of love, we tolerated each other, but over time, conflicts often erupted like volcanoes, causing fatigue and distress at the least, and thunderstorms at the worst. When the two of us argued, it was as if we had enmity from a previous life, needing to vent before we could resolve it.
