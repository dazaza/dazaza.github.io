The old revolutionary base areas promotion associations at all levels in my city actively do practical things for the people in the old revolutionary base areas.

by hongsesiyuan on 2010-03-31 15:33:37

Since last year, the old revolutionary base areas promotion associations at all levels in our city have actively utilized their advantages and sincerely served the masses in the old revolutionary base areas, making new contributions to the construction of these areas.

The promotion association has actively acted as a bridge to help the masses in the old revolutionary base areas improve production and living conditions. Huangtupo Village in Yanggao County was the first village in the county to establish a Party organization in rural areas. After relocation, the village had always suffered from difficulties in obtaining drinking water for both people and livestock. Last year, the comrades from the municipal and county old revolutionary base areas promotion associations discovered this problem during a survey in the village. Then, these elderly people actively contacted the water conservancy department, securing 200,000 yuan in funds to help the village drill a mechanized well, not only solving the drinking water problem for people and livestock but also turning 160 mu of dry land into irrigated land. Dawang Village in Datong County was identified as a key old revolutionary base area village for assistance throughout the province. To help this village solve its road travel problems, the Datong County Old Revolutionary Base Areas Promotion Association actively coordinated with the county transportation bureau, securing an investment of 500,000 yuan to pave the main 3-kilometer-long road in the village; it also secured an investment of 500,000 yuan from the county water resources bureau to help the village restore its water interception project.

Securing help from relevant departments and actively assisting old revolutionary base area villages in broadening paths to prosperity. Last year, the municipal old revolutionary base areas promotion association secured 30,000 yuan in funds from the municipal agriculture bureau, helping Lingqiu County and Xinrong District develop 37 mu of grape planting, achieving increased income for farmers. The municipal old revolutionary base areas promotion association submitted a feasibility report on preventing frost damage during the flowering period of apricot trees to the municipal forestry bureau, securing 100,000 yuan in special funds from the provincial forestry department to purchase automatic smoke fog frost preventers, helping Hunyuan County and Yanggao County solve the frost prevention problem during the flowering period of apricot trees.
