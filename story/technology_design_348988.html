What is SEO technology? SEO is the abbreviation of Search Engine Optimization, which is translated into Chinese as "Search Engine Optimization", Baidu Optimization. It can generally be simply referred to as search optimization. Related search knowledge also includes Search Engine Positioning (search engine positioning) and Search Engine Ranking (search engine ranking). The main work of SEO is to understand how various search engines crawl Internet pages, how they index them, and how they determine their rankings for specific keywords, thereby optimizing web pages to improve their search engine rankings, increasing website traffic, and ultimately enhancing the sales or promotional capabilities of websites.

Which companies need website optimization?

One: Websites that require traffic to sustain operations need SEO. Traffic is fundamental for a website's survival. If no one visits your site, it is like admiring oneself in solitude with no value whatsoever. Traffic can be converted into value, such as turning traffic into advertising click income, converting traffic into membership fees for registered users, converting traffic into news article revenue, etc. All these are examples of turning traffic into money.

Two: Websites that need to promote products require SEO. What if your product doesn't sell? Online marketing is undoubtedly the best way, but among so many corporate websites, can you secure the top position on Google or other platforms? SEO can solve this problem by improving your website's ranking in search engines, making visitors more likely to have purchasing desires when staying on your website, thus enhancing the website's marketing capability.

Three: Brand enterprises also need SEO. SEO can significantly reduce the cost of opening up enterprise marketing markets, increase brand exposure in search engines to enhance corporate reputation, and genuinely help enterprises become industry brands. I look forward to more enterprises achieving success through SEO.

It is necessary to determine the purpose of your SEO. Especially when you want to cooperate with a professional SEO consultant or SEO company, you can clearly tell them your needs so that they can better implement SEO planning, while you will also have an evaluation standard for SEO consultants or SEO companies.
