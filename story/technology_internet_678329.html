16-Year-Old Junior High Student Stabbed to Death by Roommate in the Middle of the Night

On June 5th, our newspaper reported from Shan County (Shandong Province) (by intern reporter Jing Jia): On the evening of the 4th, a campus murder case occurred in Shan County, Shandong Province. A 16-year-old first-year junior high school boy was stabbed to death by a roommate in the school dormitory. Currently, the youth who committed the crime has been taken away by the police.

The father of the deceased, Mr. Wang Wanmin, informed the reporter that his son's name was Wang Chaunjun, 16 years old, studying in Class One, Grade Seven of the middle school department at Dawn School. At around 3 o'clock in the morning on the 5th, he received a call from his son’s homeroom teacher, saying that Wang Chaunjun had been urgently sent to the Shan County Central Hospital due to some urgent matters. Subsequently, Wang Wanmin and his wife were picked up by a school-provided vehicle and taken to the hospital. Wang Wanmin said that on the way to the hospital, the school staff still told him that his son got injured in a fight with someone else, but when they arrived at the hospital, they discovered that his son was already lying in the morgue.

"Such a good child, how could he suddenly be gone?" Wang Wanmin said that early on the 5th, he and several relatives came to the school for an explanation. However, all day long, they did not receive any formal response from the school side. "We didn’t see the school principal, nor did we see the homeroom teacher, Mr. Zhai."

Local police officers gave Wang Wanmin the following reply: "At around 11 p.m. on the 4th, Wang Chaunjun was stabbed in the left chest by a fellow dormitory member Zhou Mou and subsequently died."

"I most want to know the reason why Zhou Mou killed my son, but the police did not tell me," Wang Wanmin said. That day, he learned from a school staff member that his son had a grudge against Zhou Mou because he tried to prevent Zhou Mou from going out to surf the Internet.

The incident occurred at Dawn School, located east of Caozhuang Police Station in Caozhuang Town, Shan County. It is a boarding private school approved by the Shan County Education Bureau, integrating kindergarten, primary school, and middle school departments. According to nearby villagers, this school has more than 1,000 students and enjoys a good reputation locally. In the morning of the 5th, when the reporter arrived at this school, the campus gate was tightly closed, refusing entry to people from outside.

The reporter attempted to learn related information from Wang Chaunjun's homeroom teacher, but from 15:00 to 18:00 on the 5th, the reporter dialed his phone six times consecutively, with no one answering. The reporter called the school office number posted at the school gate, and a school staff member surnamed Hu said that the police had intervened in the matter, so the school side was not convenient to disclose relevant situations, then hung up the phone.

In the afternoon of the 5th, the reporter learned from the Shan County Public Security Bureau that the youth who committed the crime had been taken away by the police, and the case was still under further investigation. Around 18:30, Wang Wanmin called the reporter and said that local police officers had already taken him to the county town to conduct a forensic examination on his son's body. After the examination, it was determined that Wang Chaunjun was killed by a single stab wound, "This stab pierced through the left chest, piercing the heart."
