Comedy film "Laughing Out Loud" begins filming: Zhao Benshan says that it's not enough for Xiao Shenyang to fly solo - Chinese film

by liteemjt on 2011-06-05 23:17:09

I want to find a good doctor, not a fake one. I want to take good medicine, not poison. This made Mr. Zeng very disappointed: since it is literary criticism, we should be scientific. We should talk about both the strengths and weaknesses of comedy movies. If there are faults, correct them; if not, strive to maintain. Let's consider what was said earlier as idle chatter from someone who likes to meddle! Yesterday, when asked again, Zhao Benshan sarcastically criticized the experts with evident anger: Criticism is normal. If there were no doubts, that would be strange. Having doubts is normal and should be understood because there is a group of people who specifically criticize others; without criticism, they wouldn't have food to eat.

Zhu Yanping introduced that in "Big Laughing World," Xiao Xiangyang performs as a shoemaker who sells soy sauce and has legendary experiences wandering through the jianghu (martial arts world), while Zhao Benshan plays an otherworldly master who passes on all his martial arts skills to Xiao Xiangyang. This seems to confirm the hope and confidence in real life for a master to promote their apprentice. It appears these two teacher-student roles will be the main characters. Zhao Benshan introduced himself saying he might still continue speaking Northeastern dialect in the movie: I can't speak anything else anyway. There's no need for any breakthroughs. Our collaboration itself is a breakthrough. As for Xiao Xiangyang...

A star-studded stage featured Chen Zhipeng, Xiao Xiangyang, Cheng Ye forming a new Little Tiger Band for a medley performance, creating quite a lively atmosphere. The event also played a video of Zhao Benshan participating in various film performances over the years. The host jokingly mentioned eating Zhao Benshan's hotpot brings good fortune and crossing the Shanhai Pass leads you to Zhao Benshan, highlighting his prominent position. Zhao Benshan took the stage and said: Today, there are so many media outlets here, it scared me! Zhao revealed that he has been recuperating recently. According to doctors, it will take another year to determine if he has fully recovered. Therefore, some intense fighting scenes in the movie, he cannot perform. Recently, netizens revealed that Zhao Benshan spent over 200 million yuan buying a private business jet named 'Benshan No.' which arrived at Shenyang Airport. At the scene, Zeng Zhiewei joked that his only condition for acting in "Big Laughing World" was that he had to travel back and forth on Zhao Benshan's private plane. With such attention, Zhao Benshan openly confirmed: Yes, my plane has indeed arrived at the (Benshan) Media Base. Currently, the procedures are still being processed and it hasn't been activated yet!

The plane has landed at the base.

Yesterday, the开机 announcement for the movie "Big Laughing World" co-produced by Ben Shan Media and Bonne International Film Group was held in Beijing. Producers Zhao Benshan and Yu Dong, director Zhu Yanping, screenwriter Ning Caishen, action choreographer Cheng Xiaodong, and main cast members Xiao Xiangyang, Lin Xilai, Zeng Zhiewei, Chen Zhipeng, Cheng Ye, Xiao Xiao Bin, and Wu Zongxian (who couldn't attend due to scheduling conflicts) all appeared together. Such a lineup of comedy stars from mainland China, Taiwan, and Hong Kong, Zhao Benshan, Yu Dong, and Zhu Yanping all expressed that the three of them respectively represent the films "Three Guns", "October Siege", and "Chill" from the 2010 New Year season. They are collaboratively producing this year's 2011 New Year season movie, with box office expectations exceeding 300 million yuan. Zhu Yanping stated: My "Chill" first got besieged by the city and then got shot three times, dying quite disgracefully. Zhao Benshan confidently introduced that "Big Laughing World" is a joint effort with classmate Yu Dong from the Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business, allowing Director Zhu to turn things around: We came together after disagreements, and I went from being a stock buyer to becoming a shareholder. Regarding Zhao Benshan's remuneration, Zhu Yanping laughed and said: Teacher Benshan is putting in both money and effort this time, and the salary can be calculated however he wants since it's essentially him paying himself.

Salary, paying oneself.

Appearing at the press conference for the new New Year film "Big Laughing World", Zhao Benshan first declared that he would not discuss matters unrelated to the movie before beginning to respond to various topics.

Recently, at the Beijing seminar for "Urban Love Story", Professor Zeng Qingrui from Communication University of China criticized Zhao Benshan, stating that he has been blinded by the flowers and applause brought by ratings. "Rural Love Story" is actually a form of pseudo-realism. Zhao Benshan immediately became angry: I dare say, none of you seated here know rural life better than I do. If someone doesn't care about ratings, then they deny the existence value of ratings. Television isn't made for elegance but for ordinary people. I'm here to find medicine that benefits me, not something that kills me when consumed.

He revealed that his complete recovery still requires time, understanding that professors criticize to make a living, and that his private plane has reached his media base. As for Xiao Xiangyang, Zhao Benshan's attitude can be summarized in one sentence: He's not mature enough to fly solo yet.

Since shooting movies, Zhao Benshan has always collaborated. When asked about Xiao Xiangyang flying solo, Zhao Benshan feigned ignorance and countered: Fly? Where to? He seriously responded: Yes, every movie we've done is a collaboration, not just one person...
