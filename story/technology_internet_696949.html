Citizens riding horses on the street were taken away by the traffic police, saying that it was too difficult to take a taxi.

by abcdfdhehe on 2012-03-08 11:40:10

Citizens are afraid, what if the horses collide with pedestrians?

These two horses are actually mounts of warriors in the movie "Feast at Hong Gate".

Yesterday afternoon, a man and a woman rode tall horses along Zhongyuan Road, and after seeing them, passers-by quickly moved out of the way.

After citizens reported the incident, the police officers from the Second Detachment who arrived on the scene stopped the two horses and took both the riders and the horses to the police station. Chief reporter Xifu Ying, text and photos.

Riding tall horses on the road, citizens have reported it

At 5 pm yesterday, I rushed to the Second Traffic Police Detachment. As soon as I entered the courtyard, I saw two large red horses.

A man and a woman were leading the horses and were being questioned by officer Li Bing.

The two horses kept making low whinnying sounds. A closer look revealed that there were white stripe-shaped patterns on their noses, making them appear majestic and beautiful.

Li Bing introduced that at 4:40 pm, citizens reported that a man and a woman were riding big horses from east to west on Zhongyuan Road, walking within the pedestrian paths. Passers-by felt frightened upon seeing them and quickly moved away, affecting normal traffic. Both horses had iron horseshoes, which made a clacking sound when they walked.

[The citizens complained that the horses might kick people, and if the two horses got spooked, what would happen if they hurt someone?]

After receiving the report, the police chased down the horse riders 200 meters west of the intersection of Huashan Road and Zhongyuan Road, bringing both the people and the horses to the detachment's courtyard.

It's hard to hail a taxi, so we decided to ride our horses to the appointment

The woman rider occasionally touched her horse proudly and with a sense of pride.

[Riding a horse is much better than sitting in a car. The high-up feeling is awesome!]

Mr. Jia, the male rider, introduced that they train horses near Changzhuang Reservoir.

[These two horses are the mounts of warriors in the movie "Feast at Hong Gate". They are currently being trained here to make them more obedient for other movies.]

He told the police the reason why the two of them rode into the city: In the afternoon, a friend on Qinling Road invited them over. It was difficult to get a taxi at the time, so the two decided to ride their horses to his place. When they reached Wanda Plaza, they noticed an increasing number of people and more onlookers, so they dared not move forward anymore and turned back.

Officer Li Bing introduced that after checking relevant contents of the "Traffic Safety Law", there was no specific law regarding animals like horses walking around in bustling areas.

[However, their riding has already affected others' normal travel. The horse droppings pollute the roads, and once the horses get startled, they might injure people.] Just as he was speaking, one of the horses started urinating.

After being educated, the two nodded repeatedly. The police let them lead the horses out of the Third Ring Road area before continuing to ride in less crowded areas.
