Forum Promotion

The forum promotion mentioned here absolutely does not mean posting ads one by one in forums, nor does it mean adding the online store address to the signature and then spamming wildly. That consumes energy and the effect is not good either. Forum administrators can delete all your posts with just a few clicks of the mouse, block your ID, and the impact brought by such actions is negative, just like sending spam emails which is annoying. I once saw a website promoting method on a certain forum that was much more sophisticated.

It was a small website providing job hunting, employment, and learning materials. Their promotional staff turned various articles on the website into link forms, categorized them properly, and posted them on different forums at once. This way, it could provide convenience to those who need it, while not being deleted due to overly explicit advertisements. The promotional effect was undoubtedly better. If these materials were posted in the corresponding sections of forums that match their own positioning, they would surely bring a lot of clicks.

Search Engine Promotion Method

Search engine promotion refers to the method of promoting online stores using search engines, classified directories, and other network tools with online information retrieval functions. Since the basic form of search engines can be divided into web spider-type search engines (simply called search engines) and search engines based on manual classification directories (simply called classification directories), the forms of search engine promotion also correspondingly include methods based on search engines and methods based on classification directories. The former includes search engine optimization, keyword advertising, bidding ranking, fixed ranking, content-based positioning advertising, and many other forms, while the latter mainly involves website registration in appropriate categories of classification directories. With the further development and changes in the form of search engines, some other forms of search engines have also appeared, but most of them are based on these two forms.

The methods of search engine promotion can be divided into multiple different forms, common ones include: registering in free classified directories, registering in paid classified directories, search engine optimization, keyword advertising, keyword bidding ranking, webpage content positioning advertising, etc.

From the current development trend, the status of search engines in online marketing remains important and is recognized by more and more enterprises. The ways of search engine marketing are also continuously evolving, so suitable methods of search engine marketing should be chosen according to environmental changes.

Email Promotion Method

Using email as the main promotional means, common methods include electronic publications, member newsletters, professional service providers' email advertising, etc.

Permission-based Email marketing differs from spam. Permission marketing has obvious advantages over traditional promotional methods or unsolicited Email marketing, such as reducing ad annoyance for users, increasing the accuracy of potential customer targeting, enhancing customer relationships, improving brand loyalty, etc. You can let your customers leave their email addresses and, after obtaining their consent, send them some product information, discount information, etc.

Resource Cooperation Promotion Method

Through exchanging links, advertisements, content, user resources, etc., achieve mutual promotion between online stores with similar goals. The most commonly used resource cooperation method is the online store link strategy, utilizing the traffic resources between partners to promote each other.

Each online store can have its own resources, which can be expressed as a certain amount of traffic, valuable content and functions, online advertising space, etc. Using the resources of the website to cooperate with partners to achieve the purpose of sharing resources and jointly expanding profits. Among these resource cooperation forms, exchanging links is the simplest form of cooperation. Surveys show that it is also one of the effective ways to promote new websites.

Exchange links, also known as reciprocal links, are a simple form of cooperation between online stores with certain complementary advantages, i.e., placing the logo or name of the partner's online store on their own online store and setting up a hyperlink to the partner's online store, allowing users to discover their own online store from the partner's online store, achieving the purpose of mutual promotion.

The role of exchange links mainly manifests in several aspects: gaining traffic, increasing user browsing impressions, gaining advantages in search engine rankings, increasing the credibility of visitors through recommendations from partner online stores, etc. Exchange links have a deeper meaning than whether they can achieve direct results. Generally speaking, each online store tends to link to other online stores with high link value, so getting links from other online stores also means gaining recognition from partners and similar online stores in the field.

Information Release Promotion Method

Releasing relevant online store promotion information on other websites that potential users may visit, utilizing the opportunity for users to obtain information on these websites to achieve the purpose of website promotion. Websites suitable for information release include online yellow pages, classified ads, forums, blog websites, supply and demand platforms, industry websites, etc.

Information release is one of the commonly used methods for free promotion, especially in the early stages of Internet development when the amount of online information was relatively small. Often, satisfactory results could be achieved through information release methods. However, with the explosive growth of online information, the role played by this method relying on free information release has gradually decreased. At the same time, with the emergence of more effective online store promotion methods, the importance of information release among commonly used online store promotion methods has also significantly declined. Therefore, relying solely on mass sending of free information no longer holds much value. However, some targeted and specialized information can still attract great attention, especially when these information releases are placed in highly relevant contexts.

Online Advertising Promotion Method

Online advertising is one of the commonly used online marketing strategies, playing a significant role in online brand building, product promotion, and website promotion. Common forms of online advertising include: Banner ads, keyword ads, classified ads, sponsored ads, Email ads, etc. BANNER ads rely on web pages, keyword ads belong to a form of search engine marketing, and Email ads are a form of permission-based Email marketing. It can be seen that online advertising itself cannot exist independently and needs to be combined with various online tools to achieve the function of information transmission. Therefore, it can also be considered that online advertising exists in various online marketing tools, just in different specific forms.

Using online advertising for online store promotion has advantages such as a wide range of selectable online media, diverse forms, strong applicability, and timely placement, suitable for any stage of the online store's launch and operation.

Friendship Links

Strive to link with popular online stores, thereby increasing visit rates and search engine weight.

Regularly offer special price items, similar to daily specials in restaurants.

For example, from Monday to Wednesday, sell an item originally priced at 150 yuan for 88 yuan, limited to the first 20 buyers (the number of spots is determined by yourself, it's just a gimmick to stimulate your purchasing desire).

Gunmen, Soft Articles, and Excessive Hype Posting on Various Forums and Websites

Soft articles are soft advertisements that attract attention.

Flash Promotion

For example, when a new MP3 or sketch comes out, make it into a flash animation, add a small strip of advertisement at the bottom, because now beautifully designed flashes spread widely. Of course, you can also contact existing flash creators to place advertisements and pay a small fee.

Link Promotion

Regardless of the method (QQ, MSN, forum, MAIL, etc.), publicize the link to your online store, or hire someone to help promote it. As long as someone clicks on this link and visits your online store, give them a certain amount of commission or coupons from your online store. People referred to your online store for shopping will also receive corresponding discounts.

Keyword Optimization

When people buy things, they usually start by searching for related products. When setting product names, design commonly used keywords that allow buyers to search for your store and products multiple times, or use terms like lowest price and special offers. Describe the goods as detailed as possible.

Data Promotion

If your online store sells certain products, you can write product data and little knowledge-related content. In the article, you can attach your online store address, or set it in the signature for promotion.

In-store Event Promotion

Analyze the buyers of your online store

Based on past buyers, analyze the characteristics of these customers, their age, occupation, interests, etc. You can conduct corresponding promotions in the forums and places they frequently visit, promoting according to the different characteristics of the buyers. At the same time, actively seek business opportunities and target inventory accordingly. Success or failure is secondary; the key is to have an active awareness.

Also, always keep an eye on "Buyer Requests" information, perhaps there are buyers who need something from your store. Even if our store doesn't have what the buyer is looking for, we can find many potential customers. Add them as friends and recommend products similar to what they're seeking; it's easy to make a sale.

Promotion and Publicity

Make good use of every holiday for promotion and publicity. During holidays, there are large amounts of holiday-related searches. Therefore, you can work on keywords and also take advantage of holidays to carry out promotional activities, bringing more customers. Additionally, during each shipment, include some personal business cards or promotional materials.

Print your online store's Logo and address on T-shirts and give them as gifts to your online customers, which is also a good way to promote. Remember, the T-shirts must be of medium or higher quality, otherwise, they'll become rags after one wear, not worth it! Of course, with the weather becoming cooler, it's suggested to make hats, cups, or other promotional gifts.

Joint Promotion

Pure friendship links have limited effects. However, if several sellers collaborate on joint promotions, it can produce different results. Try to find complementary stores. For example, a photography book specialty store collaborating with a digital camera specialty store.

Reasonable Use of Auctions

Auctions are divided into single-item auctions and multi-item auctions (Dutch auctions). Taobao specifically sets up a dedicated section for auctions on the homepage, visible to buyers as soon as they open the homepage. Choosing the auction method means buyers can easily find us, and since auction prices are generally low, they are more likely to attract buyers, making auctions very helpful in increasing store popularity.

Have you noticed that Taobao actually takes good care of sellers in many aspects, launching many promotional activities? Some sellers occasionally put out one or two items for one-yuan auctions. This should be done within your capabilities. Once you do a one-yuan auction, prepare to lose money. After the buyer successfully bids and completes the transaction, ensure the quality of the item even if it costs you money. A one-yuan auction is for earning good reviews and popularity, avoid getting bad reviews, which would be counterproductive.

Offline Promotion

The offline promotion methods introduced in this article all require some cost. Online store owners should act according to their capabilities.

Gift Advertising

The following gifts are distributed for free, in busy areas with high foot traffic. Eco-bags, as their usage frequency is relatively high now, and production costs are low, print online store information on the bags. Calendars, print online store information on them. Mouse pads, print online store information on them and distribute them for free, in internet cafes, offices, etc.

Campus Agents

Nowadays, many college students have spare time, no need for a base salary, just give them a commission, let them promote, unlimited means, both online and offline. Provide them with free business cards (with titles like campus general agent for the online store), a set of materials, let them promote and sell, earning money while improving their marketing skills, spreading word-of-mouth among student councils.

Street Questionnaire Survey

Hire part-timers for surveys, primarily for promotion.

Conduct an online survey before the promotional phase

Targeted, for instance, try piloting on Xici, conducting surveys and answering questions on products and usage issues, safety concerns that people care about. During the Q&A process, subtly promote your online store.

Roadside Billboard Advertising

Primarily bus stop billboards, bus advertisements, body advertisements, internal cross-advertisements, etc.

Hire a few students to dress up as widely recognized cartoon characters like Superman, Ultraman, Pleasant Goat, Big Big Wolf, etc., in bustling areas.

Of course, they should have online store advertisements on them, and tourists might want to take photos together, the promotional effect should be good. Key is that media outlets are always looking for news, they love stories like this, once it hits the newspaper, the traffic will come.

Internet Cafe Collaboration

Collaborate with various internet cafes, pay a small fee, set the default start page of browsers to the online store homepage, find 30-50 internet cafes, when more people go online, it will drive some traffic.

Place a Small Sign at the Front Desk or Cash Register of Restaurants, Hotels, Leisure Venues

This small sign should have a brief introduction of the online store and its URL, guests may glance at it while consulting or paying.

Purified Water Advertising

The customer base for purified water is extensive, including office buildings, hospitals, schools, government agencies, families, etc. It's not hard to see an ad while pouring water, we can collaborate with manufacturers, stick ads on the barrels, and pay a small fee.

Business Card Printing for Wide Distribution

You might say, since most promotion is online, people never meet, isn't making business cards a waste of cost? Wrong! Although the store is online, you live in real life, and mailing packages happens in reality. Design a unique business card, print your contact information and store address on it, and include a few in the mail. Due to the exquisite design, buyers won't discard it, and if there's an opportunity, they will certainly show it to others, effectively promoting your store.

Distribute Business Cards or Flyers

After printing business cards, it's time to distribute them. Now printing flyers is cheap, a few cents per piece, print enough and distribute them in places where potential customers gather, such as near schools, etc. Different products face different customers, so apply this strategy appropriately.

Tell Your Family and Friends

Do your family know you have an online store? Do your classmates and friends know? Be sure to tell them right away. Leave messages on alumni sites, share the joy of opening a store with your classmates.

Organize Relatives and Friends to Help Promote

Since relatives and friends already know you have an online store, ask them to help promote it.

Growing up, you've met many friends, from elementary school to junior high, to high school and university, you've had many classmates. So mobilize them, ask them to help us promote.
