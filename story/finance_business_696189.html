According to the report by intern Li Yuxian and reporter Wei Na, what should be done if a child keeps talking in class despite repeated teachings? Netizen "Qingshan Rengjiu Zai" shared his tough method on the Parents 100 Forum - inviting his son to eat chili peppers. Some parents disagreed: If pepper water doesn't work, does it mean we should use tiger stools next?

Yesterday, the reporter contacted the poster, Mr. Xiong Hui from Wangjiabian Community in Hankou. He introduced that his son is 8 years and a half old and will enter the third grade this semester. In the past few months, not only did the son fail to complete his homework, but he also talked during class. Repeated education proved ineffective.

After the punishment, Mr. Xiong did a lot of work. After his son ate the chili, Mr. Xiong specially took him for a walk at night and told him the story of Zhuge Liang shedding tears while executing Ma Su.

Is it too "cruel"? Mr. Xiong thought: "Eating chili, one can recover in a few minutes, and it's harmless, better than spanking."

After Mr. Xiong posted his "tough method" online, parents were divided into two camps. "Ke'ai De Xiao Shitou" said: "This is like a person being sick; some diseases need slow treatment, while others require drastic measures." However, netizen "Qingluo Fuyi" questioned: "What's the difference between corporal punishment and harm? If we can use chili water, why not try the tiger stool?"

Mr. Xiong came up with a "wonderful idea": punish him by making him eat chili. He prepared a genuine chili, broke it in half, and placed it in his son's mouth, then sat across from him to supervise until he finished eating it. Estimating the spiciness had passed, he put the other half of the chili in his son's mouth, the entire process lasting about 15 minutes.

According to the introduction, last semester, Mr. Xiong "invited" his son to eat chili twice, with significant results. His son became much more focused in class and ranked third in the final exam of the class.
