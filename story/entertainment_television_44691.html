War Gaze Complete Collection Plot Introduction

As a long TV series with realistic military themes, its biggest highlight is the first panoramic presentation to the audience of the most real form of information warfare. It rectifies the misleading audio-visual experience from previous similar TV programs. Through vivid portrayal of soldiers and thrilling storylines, it tells the audience how information determines the success or failure of future wars. Meanwhile, it raises the profound proposition that for China to become an economic powerhouse, it must rebuild national defense involving all citizens.

The Jianghai Military Region organized a large-scale red-blue army confrontation exercise to test the information warfare equipment Tielma system of the Phoenix Mountain troops. However, the exercise deviated from its original purpose right from the start. Troops openly gathered on a large scale, disregarding practical combat requirements, creating momentum, staging scenes, and manufacturing an atmosphere of smoke and fire, turning a practical combat exercise into a performance. At the same time, during the exercise, due to fatal flaws in the Tielma system, it was detected, interfered with, and monitored by an unknown nationality satellite from space... At this critical moment, the protagonist of this drama, researcher of the military region Jiang Yongliang, stepped forward and proposed the suggestion to terminate the exercise. He sharply criticized the exercise at the evaluation meeting.

He pointed out that modern war is a high-altitude, smoke-free confrontation in space. Whoever can first achieve the occupation, interference, deception, and even control of the opponent's information will win the war. We must break free from traditional war thinking, recognize the situation of the new global military transformation, and establish a grand space mindset! Jiang Yongliang's words caused reflection among people like Luo Huaizhong, the chief of staff of the military region, and also triggered conflicts between him and Guo Changqing. Jiang Yongliang is an orphan who, under the promotion and cultivation of Guo Changqing, after studying at the military academy, became a modern soldier. He has a strong sense of crisis and foresight, regarding Phoenix Mountain as his home and Guo Changqing as his elder brother and benefactor.

When Jiang Yongliang returned to Phoenix Mountain to serve as the chief of staff with the Thousand-Armed Guanyin system he designed, the contradictions triggered by the exercise, along with the various conflicts arising from the deep feelings and differences in consciousness and vision between him and Guo Changqing gradually unfolded. Especially when Jiang Yongliang insisted on abolishing the training department against the opposition of Guo Changqing and others, the contradiction reached a boiling point. Taking advantage of this opportunity, Zhang Yulin, the head of the training department, suddenly made a move, proposing a combination of actual troop exercises where the Thousand-Armed Guanyin system and the Tielma system would engage in an electronic countermeasure showdown, aiming to defeat Jiang Yongliang.

The result of the countermeasure showdown was unexpected. Due to the intervention of Tian Menghong, vice president of Xinghua Group, the partner of the Tielma system, the Thousand-Armed Guanyin system was paralyzed; however, because Jiang Yongliang had prepared an emergency plan in advance, Xu Guanghui and Zhou Xiaohong, two staunch followers of Jiang Yongliang, led a vegetable-growing company using electronic deception methods to lure the opponent's elite company into a missile strike zone, resulting in their complete annihilation! One would think that Guo Changqing, Zhang Yulin, and others should have realized something in the face of these facts, but they did not...

In his confusion, Jiang Yongliang finally broke free from emotional constraints after being struck by the sacrifice of Bei Mingzhu and the dying advice of his mentor Wu Zheng. For the future of Phoenix Mountain, he submitted a report to the military region requesting to replace Guo Changqing as the commander! This independent action caused the conflict between both sides to reach its climax.

Just when Jiang Yongliang was isolated, his archenemy Tian Menghong developed feelings for him and began cooperating with him; at the same time, under Luo Huaizhong's guidance, Jiang Yongliang gained new insights into how to cooperate with private enterprises in the new era and how to rebuild a comprehensive national defense involving all citizens. Once, while Tian Menghong was abroad purchasing equipment for the Thousand-Armed Guanyin, she encountered the Ashkab uprising and was stranded in a hotel awaiting rescue. With the assistance of the embassy, Jiang Yongliang successfully implemented a remote rescue using his military intelligence, abilities, and courage, causing a subtle change in their emotions.

The conclusion of the entire drama is a brilliantly staged, realistically simulated urban offensive battle: A future war under information warfare conditions erupted, with one of our coastal cities suddenly invaded. The enemy used electronic technology to cut off water supply, power supply, and communication systems, instantly paralyzing the entire city, facing precise missile strikes... However, just as the citizens were at a loss, a People's Army that had already acquired a new mindset and established a rapid response mechanism swiftly organized a full-scale counterattack, ultimately crushing the invading enemy! Guo Changqing finally realized his mistakes, voluntarily applied to resign from his position as commander to study at the military academy, and solemnly recommended to the military region that Jiang Yongliang take over his position.
