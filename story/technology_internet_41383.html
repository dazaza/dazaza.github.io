### I. Urgently Recruiting Software Development Engineers for Japan (Focus on PM, PG)

1. **Urgently Recruiting Japanese-bound PG:**

- **Requirements:** Proficient in at least one of the following: .net, C, C++, Java, Cobol, C#.

- **Job Content:** Software development and programming work.

- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree or higher in computer-related fields.

- **Experience:** Both experienced and inexperienced candidates are welcome.

- **Language:** Japanese Language Test Level 2 or above. Candidates with insufficient Japanese skills can attend our professional business Japanese training.

- **Age:** 22-35 years old.

- **Compensation:** Monthly salary of 250,000 to 350,000 yen. Housing provided, dedicated living support staff, full life insurance coverage.

2. **Urgently Recruiting Japanese-bound PM:**

- **Requirements:** Bachelor’s degree in a computer-related field, Japanese Language Test Level 1 or equivalent. Minimum five years of experience required, with at least one year of working experience in Japan.

- **Compensation:** Monthly salary ranging from 500,000 to 700,000 yen.

### II. Urgently Recruiting CAD (2D, 3D) Designers for Japan (Priority Recruitment)

- **Requirements:** Formal bachelor's degree or higher in engineering or related fields such as mechanical, electrical, architectural, or civil engineering.

- **Language:** Japanese Language Test Level 2 or above. Those with insufficient Japanese skills can attend our training program before proceeding with interviews and formalities.

- **Experience:** Both experienced and inexperienced candidates are welcome. Proficiency in using one or more of the following software: AUTOCAD, I-DEAS, Pro-E, CATIA, SOLIDWORKS, 3D.

- **Job Content:** Product design, device structure design, equipment design, toy design, game machine design, automotive design, electrical appliance design, sheet metal design, etc.

- **Compensation:** Full-time employee benefits, monthly salary ranging from 200,000 to 450,000 yen (adjusted based on experience).

### III. Urgently Recruiting Electrical/Electronic Design Talent for Japan

- **Job Content:** Electrical/electronic circuit design, semiconductor equipment circuit design, control circuit design, motherboard design.

- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree or higher in a relevant field.

- **Experience:** Both experienced and inexperienced candidates are welcome (preferably with over one year of work experience).

- **Language:** Japanese Language Test Level 2 or above. Candidates with insufficient Japanese skills can attend our professional business Japanese training.

- **Age:** 22-35 years old.

### IV. Urgently Recruiting Control System Designers for Japan

- **Experience:** Both experienced and inexperienced candidates are welcome.

- **Education:** Bachelor’s degree or higher from a recognized university in a related field.

- **Job Content:** My computer control system, communication control system, management system.

- **Japanese Language:** Basic conversational and reading/writing skills, equivalent to Japanese Language Test Level 2 or above.

- **Compensation:** Housing benefits included, comprehensive insurance coverage, transportation expenses provided, residential allowance, full-time employment contract with our company, annual bonus (usually equivalent to two months' salary).
