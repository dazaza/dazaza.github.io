I guarantee you all know this person! The kids from Beijing

by fkflr028 on 2011-06-09 14:17:14

What is the relationship between Shinichi Kudo, Timon from The Lion King, and Takumi Fujiwara from Initial D? No, I am talking about a Chinese person. Speaking of Liu Jie, you might say you don't know him, but if you've watched some of his works, maybe some of you will recognize him. I dare to say that it might be a case of homonymy. However, his voice, I guarantee, is one that you are extremely familiar with. If you were born in the 80s and have a soft spot for the cartoons we watched as children, you should have a strong impression of his voice. He is the male dubbing artist with a very pleasant voice in animated series or TV dramas, known as a "seiyuu" in Japan. Perhaps the one you are most familiar with is the Mandarin version of Detective Conan. That's right, it's because of Shinichi Kudo that I remember him. It's the kind where once you see Shinichi, you immediately think of his voice. Similarly, when hearing Liu Jie's dubbing in other movies, I would mistakenly think it was Conan and get excited.

Today, after reading Liu Jie's resume, I suddenly overlapped Shinichi's handsome face with the adorable Timon.
