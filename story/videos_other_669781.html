Hospital vice president runs 8 kilometers to chase a thief, and they fight for nearly 4 hours

by anonymous on 2012-02-20 11:03:01

Zhejiang Online, April 30th - The drug thief who escaped looks around 30 years old, about 168 cm tall, with an accent from out of town, medium build, neither fat nor thin, wearing a black jacket, navy blue pants, and black rubber-soled cloth shoes. These were the physical characteristics of the suspect repeatedly emphasized by Vice President Xu Yingzhan of the Yuhang Gouzhuang Health Center to the police officers. Yesterday (April 29th) at dawn, this drug thief was stealing drugs in the pharmacy of the health center. Vice President Xu, who runs 5000 meters every morning, chased him for 8 kilometers and grappled with him for nearly 4 hours. Currently, the Yuhang police are making every effort to capture him, and we also ask everyone to provide clues.

At 1:50 AM yesterday, Xu Yingzhan was on duty at the Yuhang Gouzhuang Health Center when he heard "creaking" sounds coming from the first floor, like the sound of sawing metal. That was where the pharmacy was located. Xu Yingzhan called two colleagues and took a flashlight to investigate.

When they opened the door to the pharmacy, Xu Yingzhan immediately saw a dark figure moving a large bag to the window. "Don't move," Xu Yingzhan shouted as he rushed forward, but the drug thief quickly climbed through the window trying to escape. Xu Yingzhan grabbed a wooden rack used for hanging IVs and hit the thief, although the thief was still able to climb out of the window.

Xu Yingzhan rushed to the window, where the thief was throwing two luggage bags over the wall outside. Two bars of the security window had been sawed off. He immediately climbed out of the window and snatched one of the luggage bags filled with drugs from the thief's hand. The thief then scaled the wall and fled. Xu Yingzhan followed suit, climbing over the wall barehanded, and soon both of them disappeared from sight.

After the health center reported the incident, the Liangzhu Police Station officers and detectives immediately arrived. Half an hour later, the police found a yellow luggage bag near the old street in Gouzhuang Town. The bag was covered in bloodstains and contained Domperidone, human blood protein needles, and other drugs that were stolen from the Gouzhuang Health Center. However, what worried everyone greatly was that neither the thief nor Xu Yingzhan could be seen anywhere. Where had they gone?

The police mobilized more than 40 officers to search the entire Gouzhuang Town, but even after daybreak, there was no trace of their whereabouts.

At 7:00 AM, it was already bright. Finally, the Gouzhuang Health Center received a call from Xu Yingzhan: he had chased the thief all the way to Sandun. Unfortunately, just as both Xu Yingzhan and the thief were exhausted, the thief managed to ambush him and escape. While chasing the thief barehanded, Xu Yingzhan had lost his sense of direction and asked passersby for directions. He learned he was on a village road in Group 8 of Wuxing Village in Sandun, where he borrowed a phone to seek help.

Upon seeing the police officers, Xu Yingzhan's first words were: "Unfortunately, that guy got away!" Fortunately, Xu Yingzhan was fine, except for some bruising on his face and a bloody mark on his scalp.

"That thief moved slowly with the luggage bag, so I followed him. Near the old street, I got close enough to grab his luggage bag, and then he fought me. Maybe he was also afraid of being caught, so he dropped the bag and ran. At the time, I didn't think much about it and continued to chase him, wanting to catch him. I didn't realize how far I'd run."

Xu Yingzhan himself didn't know that he had run nearly 8 kilometers in one breath. Once he got close, the thief turned around and begged him: "Please don't chase me anymore, let me go!" But Xu Yingzhan refused and kept running forward. Thus, the two of them ran from night until dawn. After dawn, Xu Yingzhan seized the opportunity to tackle the thief, preparing to take him to the police station when the thief picked up a stone and smashed it onto his head. Xu Yingzhan became dizzy, and the thief escaped.

The Gouzhuang Health Center lost drugs worth more than 10,000 yuan. Everyone in the hospital knew about Xu Yingzhan's pursuit of the thief. They also revealed a secret: Vice President Xu is in such good shape for a reason; he often runs 5000 meters every morning.

(Source: Zhejiang Online - Qianjiang Evening News)
