The bilingual Spanish-English courses offered by universities such as Churchman University are highly favored by DuPont. DuPont is a globally-based scientific enterprise that provides scientific solutions to enhance the quality of life in areas such as food and nutrition, health care, clothing, home and building, electronics, and transportation. Founded in 1802, DuPont operates in 70 countries worldwide with over 79,000 employees.

This traditional industry company, which originally started with gunpowder production, has not stopped advancing. In the United States, DuPont is making efforts to enter the Hispanic market. It is reported that the number of Hispanics in the U.S. is rapidly increasing. A spokesperson for DuPont pointed out that any strategy that ignores rapidly growing populations such as Asians and Hispanics would be unwise.

Every year, DuPont arranges for a large number of middle and upper-level managers to study the bilingual Spanish-English business program at Churchman University, with DuPont covering most of the tuition fees. Many European-American employees express strong approval.

A sales manager responsible for LYCRA fibers on the U.S. West Coast named Gerard stated: "Previously, I was most worried about communicating with Hispanic distributors, but now I actually hope to encounter Hispanic clients." The volume of business in his responsible sales area has increased by 9% compared to the same period last year.

Data shows that sales managers within DuPont who have undergone bilingual Spanish-English training perform on average 7.6% better than those in the same sales regions who have not participated in such training.

A spokesperson for DuPont summarized that this on-the-job training translates into over $300 million in direct annual business growth for the company's performance across the entire U.S. market.

Churchman University’s distance learning business degree programs allow mid-to-upper level managers around the world to study online anytime and anywhere without affecting their current work, including taking exams and submitting papers online. The degrees obtained are exactly the same as those earned through campus-based studies and are the preferred educational model for mid-to-upper level managers worldwide, including in Europe, America, and China.

Churchman University's bilingual Spanish-English and Chinese-English courses are highly popular among multinational corporations, offering MBA, undergraduate, master's, and doctoral degrees. The financial aid provided by the school allows successful applicants to complete their degrees for only a few thousand dollars.

Churchman University has campuses in Pennsylvania, California, Texas, and other locations in the U.S. The earliest established branch, Churchman Business School, is a renowned American church university known for its rigorous academic atmosphere. Founded in 1911, it has nearly a hundred years of history and is one of the oldest business schools in the U.S., specializing in cultivating high-end talent for the American financial industry. In the "National Ranking of MBA Programs," this century-old prestigious university ranks 17th.

In addition to Churchman University, several other century-old prestigious universities in the U.S., such as Boston University and Brown University, are also widely recognized for their teaching quality by white-collar professionals in China.
