Have you ever been embarrassed? You must have experienced embarrassment at least once. Even if it never happened to you, you must have heard of something like that~! (I don't believe that you've never done anything embarrassing. You're probably just afraid of being laughed at.) Haha, even the "Awkward Moments Encyclopedia" has come out now. These days... I firmly believe that you must have had an awkward moment. So, go to the Awkward Moments Encyclopedia and share it so everyone can have a laugh. Don't worry, it's anonymous~ no one will know who the person behind the awkward moment is... As their slogan says: "Don't keep awkward moments bottled up inside, it's very unhealthy for you!" It's a very interesting website. You can find all sorts of awkward stories here. Some of them might even be similar to your own awkward moments... This site also incorporates the Digg model, where each awkward moment has 'upvote' and 'downvote' links. By clicking these links, you can influence the ranking of an awkward moment. You can also click "[report]" to file a complaint about a particular awkward story.
