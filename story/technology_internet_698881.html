Post-match ratings: Jeonbuk Hyundai 1-5 Guangzhou Evergrande - The Chinese Super League champions show no fear against Korean teams

by abcdfdhehe on 2012-03-09 11:38:33

Yang Jun - 7.0: Perfectly blocked the one-on-one shot by the opposing foreign player, Enio, with his fingertips when Feng Xiaoting made a mistake. Just before halftime, he also accurately deflected Enio's free kick. Although the goal was breached once in the second half, he does not deserve any blame.

Zhang Linpeng - 6.0: Known as "China Ramos," responsible for defending the right flank, his performance was commendable. However, similar to the characteristics of Real Madrid star Ramos during his early days, his mind wasn't calm enough when the game became intense. He received a yellow card after committing consecutive fouls on Lee Dong-guk and Cho Seung-hwan within two minutes, which also forced Cho Seung-hwan to leave the field due to injury. In the second half, while leading 3-0, he missed out on Lee Dong-guk, resulting in a goal being conceded.

Zheng Zhi - 7.5: The on-field captain of Evergrande made a timely return from injury, boosting the morale of the entire team and resolving the urgent needs of the defense. On the pitch, Zheng Zhi performed calmly, maximizing the restriction on "Lion King" Lee Dong-guk's play.

Feng Xiaoting - 6.0: His physical condition seemed less than perfect, but his performance was acceptable. However, a fatal defensive error in the 31st minute almost led to a goal, fortunately, Yang Jun brilliantly blocked Enio's certain-goal shot with a single hand; his performance in the second half was significantly better than in the first half.

Sun Xiang - 7.0: Performed exceptionally steady, actively advancing to assist when there was little pressure on the left flank defense, leaving a deep impression.

Zhao Xuri - 7.5: After receiving a red card in the national team, Zhao Xuri seems to have changed a lot, playing actively without being rough. He helped Evergrande take control of the match away from home, cleverly assisting Conca in scoring a goal through strategic positioning for a free kick. In the second half, he delivered a smart assist, helping Muriqui score successfully.

Zhao Yuanxi - 8.0: The former South Korean international player knows everything about Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors, and his relentless style left a deep impression, known as the "perpetual motion machine" in midfield, a typical unsung hero like Makelele.

Muriqui - 8.0: Except for wasting one one-on-one opportunity, his overall performance was nearly flawless, showcasing personal abilities clearly surpassing the Asian arena standard. Facing fierce Korean opponents, he remained calm while controlling the ball in the front court. At the 76th minute, he finally used his skills to break through Jeonbuk Hyundai's entire defense line, scoring a well-deserved goal.

Conca - 9.0: Neatly organized Guangzhou Evergrande's frontline, scoring a direct free kick in the first half with perfect personal ability; in the second half, taking advantage of Jeonbuk Hyundai's anxiousness to attack, he organized more effective counterattacks in the front court, completely tilting the game situation towards Evergrande. A shot inside the penalty area at the 69th minute unexpectedly turned into an assist; four minutes later, he scored a stunning world-class goal from the edge of the goal area, completing a double.

Gao Lin - 7.0: Active on the right wing, motivated by the foreigners, showing positive efforts. In the first half, he intercepted Jeonbuk Hyundai's back pass while lying on the ground, then continuously cooperated with Cleo, assisting the Brazilian in scoring the opening goal. In the second half, Gao Lin originally had a chance to score with a one-on-one opportunity, but failed due to poor footwork, wasting a golden opportunity.

Cleo - 9.0: Continued the excellent form from the Super Cup, scoring a goal each in the first and second halves, showcasing the personal abilities expected of a "Champions League-level killer," making Jeonbuk Hyundai, the double champion of the K-League, helpless. With such a perfect Cleo, who needs Barrios?

Guangzhou Fans - 10 points

They occupied Jeonbuk Hyundai's home ground, creating the best possible match environment for the Guangzhou Evergrande players. From Cleo's goal onwards, the chants of "Guangzhou, come on!", "2-0", "3-0" consistently became the main melody of the whole stadium, touching everyone. Their performance fully deserves tonight's perfect score of 5-1.
