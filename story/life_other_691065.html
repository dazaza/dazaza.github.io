In addition, it also set up relevant departments such as internal medicine and surgery, dedicated to scientific research and clinical practice of the urinary system, as well as professional solutions for various common diseases. The main department introductions are as follows:

Sexual Dysfunction Clinic

With increasing social competition, sexual dysfunction in men such as Erectile Dysfunction (ED), premature ejaculation, and impotence has become more prevalent, severely threatening the physical and mental health and family happiness of men. In response to this situation, Nanning Jiangling Hospital Urology Department was the first to establish the first professional medical specialty in Nanning for diagnosing and treating male sexual dysfunction. It uses a comprehensive set of internationally recognized treatments for sexual dysfunction, combining unique Chinese and Western medicine adjustments to comprehensively regulate bodily endocrine functions, balance physiological functions, achieving both internal and external treatment effects. Under full computer monitoring, safe and rapid sterilization is performed, enhancing the body's immunity, effectively relieving sexual dysfunction caused by prostatitis, and activating bodily energy. High-pressure dialysis technology is used for consolidation treatment along with experienced experts' unique psychological therapy, providing overall comprehensive treatment to achieve dual regulation of mind and body, treating both symptoms and root causes. Individualized treatment plans are formulated based on each patient’s condition, truly helping male patients overcome obstacles and regain "sexual happiness."

Treatment Scope: Reduced libido, ejaculation disorders, impotence, premature ejaculation, etc.

Prostate Specialty Clinic

The Prostate Characteristic Department of Nanning Jiangling Hospital Urology Department introduced the latest generation of authoritative prostate rehabilitation treatment systems from around the world, combined with high-precision diagnostic equipment such as the internal electric field workstation treatment system. Scientific detection and standardized treatment are provided, while gathering renowned senior prostate experts in the field of urology medicine domestically. This forms a comprehensive scientific and systematic treatment plan for prostate diseases, elevating the treatment level of prostate diseases to a new height, allowing many patients suffering from prostate diseases to receive more scientifically reasonable and complete treatment.

Treatment Scope: Prostate pain, prostate stones, prostate hyperplasia, prostatitis, etc.

Urogenital Infection Clinic

The Reproductive Infection Specialty Department is one of the hospital's key departments. It possesses a top-tier laboratory center domestically and internationally leading inspection instruments and treatment equipment. The city's first gene chip detection system and French Meril VITEK2 fully automatic bacterial identification sensitivity instrument were introduced, conducting routine inspections related to sperm processing, microbiology, biochemistry, immunology, endocrinology, pathology, and other special examinations. A full suite of technologies including bacterial culture, pathological testing, viral quantification, drug sensitivity tests, etc., provide strong guarantees for clinical diagnosis and treatment of urogenital infections and sexually transmitted diseases caused by various reasons. The combination of advanced equipment and experienced experts, along with personalized treatment plans, effectively resolves urogenital infection diseases.

Treatment Scope: Balanitis, acute and chronic urethritis, orchitis, cystitis, epididymitis, testicular epididymis inflammation, etc.

Reproductive Plastic Surgery Department

The Reproductive Plastic Surgery Characteristic Department of Nanning Jiangling Hospital Urology Department has top-level ultra-clean operating rooms, personally operated by authoritative experts in urological surgery, equipped with a full set of minimally invasive surgical equipment, Korean-style reproductive plastic surgery techniques, performing various circumcision surgeries, penile elongation surgeries, varicocele local excision surgeries, undescended testicle surgeries, etc., without pain and minimal invasiveness. There is no bleeding after surgery, no need for stitches, and quick recovery. It is the preferred therapy for many men to shape perfect genital organs.

Treatment Scope: Phimosis, redundant prepuce, penile elongation, etc.

Urinary Calculus Specialty Department

Based on high standards and high-quality modern minimally invasive new technologies, the core department of Nanning Jiangling Hospital Urology Department Urinary System Stone Minimally Invasive Center owns standard operating rooms at the ten-thousand level, introducing world-class minimally invasive equipment; the expert team has years of experience in minimally invasive surgery, using internationally advanced detection and surgical equipment, advanced laser lithotripsy technology, and experienced experts' unique physical therapies; tracking the latest dynamics in the domestic and international stone disease treatment field, committed to clinical research on stone diseases, striving to pursue the excellent quality of minimally invasive new technologies, truly achieving alignment with international stone disease diagnosis and treatment technologies. According to the patient's condition, targeted treatment plans are formulated, reducing the pain of diagnosis and treatment, ensuring treatment effectiveness, effectively solving the problem of long-term unresolved and recurrent urinary calculi.

Treatment Scope: Kidney stones, urethral stones, bladder stones, gallbladder stones, etc.
