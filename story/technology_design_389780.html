Let's explore traditional Chinese medicine beauty together, written by "The Taste of Whitening" in intensive care.

Today, we change our approach and return to our traditional treasure - TCM (Traditional Chinese Medicine) adjustment. TCM not only focuses on curing diseases but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising methods to enhance the beauty of palace women, leaving behind a wealth of experience that deserves our attention!

So, let's follow the editor into the world of TCM beauty together.

"TCM beauty" carries a profound cultural heritage of China. Through centuries of practice, these time-tested folk beauty and health preservation methods embody boundless Eastern wisdom.

Whether it’s ancient palaces or modern cities, women have always been preoccupied with how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM beauty," with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, has become increasingly favored by those who value both beauty and health.

However, now there are numerous TCM beauty products on the market. What kind of methods will be effective for me? Which products will benefit me? And how can I find products suitable for myself? I think these are the issues everyone cares about most, and they're also the most headache-inducing problems.

For this reason, I visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons. Later, I found an issue: Supermarkets and malls generally don't sell pure TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the merits of their products, claiming what they could do after use, and citing satisfied customers. However, these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it difficult to find actual users unless they are friends around us. I'm not saying their products are bad; each product that survives in the market must have its own customer base. Personally, I still prefer our traditional TCM beauty products.

Because of work, I often surf the internet, so I decided to look online. Taobao is China's largest shopping platform, where I usually buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics. But I've never looked into our own TCM beauty products online before. After searching, I found quite a few items. I decided to choose a store with relatively high credibility to inquire.

I clicked into a TCM beauty shop called "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me was the image of ancient beauties on their products—appealing to both men and women alike. After reading their introductions, they claimed to be from a family of TCM practitioners, and the products in their small shop were exclusively formulated by the family's old TCM doctor. This piqued my curiosity—I wondered if he could also formulate a secret recipe for me, transforming me into a youthful beauty overnight. After reading more product descriptions, I discovered that the old TCM doctor was in his 50s but looked like he was only 35 in the photos. Truly impressive, filling me with admiration and envy. I thought, if I looked like this at 50, I'd be happy too.

With that in mind, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she would introduce her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello. Are all your products made purely from TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, all are purely TCM. Do you need anything specific?

Editor: I want to learn first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to know about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. Unlike general chemical or hormone-based beauty products that show quick results, TCM treats the root cause, fundamentally solving the problem. A course of treatment lasts a month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people see results after one course, while others need two or more courses. Generally, the longer you use them, the better the results.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they didn’t see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient and stop using the products after just a few tries, claiming they don’t work. Our products aren’t miraculous drugs, so it’s impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish complexions despite being young and free of other ailments. These customers generally have qi and blood deficiencies, so we use a dual-supplement method of qi and blood, combined with internal and external adjustments. Such customers usually need at least two months of adjustment. Some give up halfway and then claim the product doesn’t work. As long as they persist, they will see results.

Editor: It seems that beautifying through TCM requires patience.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's situation is different, the time required for adjustment will vary. Customers who persevere will see significant improvements in their skin.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I’ve learned a lot. If I need anything next time, I’ll come back to buy. Wishing you a booming business!

Shopkeeper: Sure, you're welcome. Feel free to visit anytime.

An editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The shop has good credibility, with many repeat customers. The editor decided to investigate the opinions of their customers to verify the claims. Among many online buyers, I chose one with a good credit rating (Yellow Diamond). After explaining my intentions, she was happy to chat with me. Below is a segment of our main conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll call her Xiao A).

Editor: How long have you been using this shop's TCM beauty products?

Xiao A: About 8 months ago, due to prolonged sleep deprivation, my skin was in terrible condition, my complexion was dull, my skin was dry with fine lines, and I even developed spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I dared not use random spot-removal products and wanted something gentle, so I stumbled upon this shop and started trying their products. Seeing good results, I continued using them.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper recommended a Whitening Spot Removal Beauty Pill and an All-in-One Whitening Spot Removal Mask, along with a Skin Nourishing Moisturizing Anti-Wrinkle Mask based on my skin condition.

Editor: Did they really work?

Xiao A: Yes, after one month, my complexion became more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin lightened a bit. Although I'm not extremely fair yet, I'm no longer as sallow as before.

Editor: How has your skin improved now?

Xiao A: Now, my skin is genuinely whiter and smoother, the spots are much lighter and less noticeable, almost invisible under foundation. The fine lines have become very shallow, and my skin looks hydrated and radiant. In the morning, my skin even appears rosy. Honestly, I'm very satisfied with the current results. I've also introduced some friends to use them, and they all say the effects are great.

Editor: Have you experienced any side effects?

Xiao A: No, the shopkeeper inquired about my physical condition before recommending the products, so I haven't noticed any abnormalities. However, since TCM works slowly, it took some time.

Editor: Are you still using them consistently?

Xiao A: Yes, I've been taking the Whitening Spot Removal Beauty Pills for six months, and I've been using the All-in-One Mask and Skin Nourishing Moisturizing Anti-Wrinkle Mask twice a week for eight months. The shopkeeper suggested that I take the pills every two months now, as it also helps prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is truly excellent.

Xiao A: Yes, you should try it and discuss with the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful aspiration for humanity, but natural things always go through life and death, prosperity and decline. As part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, with age, the human body will also age.

In TCM theory, although it's impossible to stay forever young, replenishing the deficiency of vital organs and qi-blood can maintain balance in the body's yin and yang, indeed delaying the onset of aging. With today's trend towards nature and green living, TCM, with its five-thousand-year accumulation of strength, still holds unique charm in anti-aging, beauty, and health preservation.
