What are the specific treatment methods for cervical erosion?

by ciaihoude on 2011-08-30 08:32:20

Cervical erosion is a common female disease, but if it is not treated in time, the condition will continue to deteriorate and cause serious consequences. So, what are the specific treatment methods for cervical erosion? Regarding this issue, we will invite experts from Xi'an Cai Ai Gynecological Hospital to introduce the treatment methods for cervical erosion in detail.

1. Local drug therapy: This is mainly applicable to women with smaller erosion areas and milder inflammation infiltration. However, because it is difficult to operate and the treatment effect is not good, this method is no longer used. What are the symptoms of severe cervical erosion?

2. Laser treatment: This was initially a popular method. It does not cause bleeding and the patient feels no pain, which was a great advancement in treating cervical diseases. But this method also has limitations, as laser directly acts on the lesion, causing the diseased tissue to dehydrate due to high temperature, and even leading to the closure of local tubes, bringing a lot of trouble later on. Therefore, this method is no longer recommended.

3. Endoscopic surgery: Minimally invasive surgery that combines hysteroscopy and laparoscopy. The doctor's operating field of vision is clearer, and it will not harm the patient. Examination and treatment can be carried out simultaneously, and patients receiving treatment feel no pain and see results quickly. This is an ideal treatment method for everyone and one of the treatments for cervical erosion. Improper cleaning can lead to cervical erosion.

4. Physical therapy: Mainly uses physical methods to cover the eroded single-layer columnar epithelium. Regular check-ups are required after treatment, and this method can easily cause bleeding and infection issues. This is also a treatment method for cervical erosion.

The above are introductions to the treatment methods for cervical erosion, hoping they can help you.
