Tencent QQ 2008 Beta 1 is officially released.

by yinyin on 2008-04-09 10:23:51

Introduction to the new version features:

1. Optimized status reply prompts, automatic replies no longer repeat

Automatic reply is a very considerate feature. In previous versions of QQ, when a friend was away or busy, every message sent would receive a repeated automatic reply. Now, QQ2008 Beta1 has optimized the custom reply function, so that custom replies no longer repeat.

2. All-new QQ voice and video, efficient, smooth, and clear

The quality of QQ video and audio has been optimized. Super video is even clearer, and can run relatively smoothly even in poor network conditions. The call quality of super voice has also been further optimized. Want to give it a try?

3. Beyond network limitations, customizable signatures at your discretion

A new way to change your personalized signature has been added. Whether your QQ is online or not, as long as you are a Taotao user, you can update your personalized signature anytime by posting "&quot;qm+content&quot;" on Taotao (www.taotao.com).

4. Group shopping zone, an exclusive ultra-convenient and safe point card purchase channel

You can go shopping with your group friends! Currently, QQ groups focused on online games have their own shopping areas, making it convenient for you to stay updated with the latest dynamics of online games while communicating with your group friends, and also allowing you to easily purchase relevant game cards!

5. Optimized QQ VIP doodle emoticons, DIY and emoticon templates easily selectable

QQ VIP users can more easily use the doodle emoticon function. Whether it's highly customizable DIY or quick and easy emoticon templates, switching between them is effortless with just a click of a button. QQ VIP exclusive emoticon privileges, of course my QQ emoticons speak for me!

6. Quick costume change, let's change QQ Show outfits while chatting

No need to repeatedly log into the QQ Show Mall, simply open the chat panel and click the quick costume change button to change outfits while chatting on QQ! Here we provide you with the latest popular items from the QQ Show Mall, along with free items such as moods, expressions, and current events, giving you a different look and mood every day!~

7. Added game avatars like Dungeon Fighter, freely choose personalized avatars

Added four sets of game avatars including Dungeon Fighter, CrossFire, QQ Speed, and QQ Three Kingdoms, and optimized the QQ Xia avatar. You can choose an avatar that suits you based on your preferences, showcasing your unique side on the QQ interface.
