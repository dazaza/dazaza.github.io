Golf course surrounds municipal park for 10 years (picture)

by czkiigci on 2012-02-22 11:01:10

Share to: Welcome to post comments I want to comment. After many twists and turns, the reporter found the Urban Management Department of Xixiang Subdistrict Office. However, the explanation from the person in charge seemed too brief: "The park was not handed over to the management of the golf course, but rather because the park was not fully completed, it has been delayed for ten years without opening."

Mr. He, who was visiting the park, lives nearby. He said that a few years ago, it was not open here, "Inside here is a golf course, you can only enter by car, pedestrians cannot enter."

According to reports, before the official opening of the park in 2010, public facilities were improved, including clearing weeds and drainage ditches on 26 mountain trails and winding roads; renovating leisure facilities such as pavilions; isolating the park from the golf course fairways to prevent golf balls from injuring tourists; catching snakes and removing beehives. Director Yu said that now the park is managed normally, with 16 security guards patrolling the garden, and property management responsible for maintaining the park.

A responsible person from the Bihaigolf Golf Club said, "The golf course is at the foot of the mountain, we must ensure the safety of visitors, after all, they might be hit by golf balls." Therefore, the golf course stipulates that only customers playing golf or driving are allowed to enter, pedestrians are not permitted.

Why did the Bihaigolf Park remain hidden for ten years like a "Shangri-La"?

According to previous media reports, the Bihaigolf Park is located in the core area of Xixiang's Bihai, covering more than 200,000 square meters. The Xixiang Subdistrict Office invested over 30 million yuan, "After the park opens to the public, it will benefit 600,000 residents from five communities: Yantian, Gongle, Lequn, Gusu, and Nanchang, becoming the best park under the jurisdiction of Xixiang."

Director Yu of the park management office said that although the park has officially opened, there is only one way to enter the park, "The main gate has not yet been officially transferred to the park, it is jointly used by the park and the golf course." At the same time, he revealed that in the future, the main gate of the park will definitely display the nameplate of the Bihaigolf Park, while the golf course will select another entrance as the course entrance.

The golf course side claims that due to concerns about visitor "safety issues," pedestrians are not allowed to enter.

Bihaigolf Park is located on two small hills within the Bihaigolf Golf Club. After entering the gate marked "Bihaigolf Golf Club," walking along a slightly sloping road for hundreds of meters, you can see the Bihaigolf Park. Climbing up the mountain steps, the trees on the mountain are lush, the scenery is pleasant, and occasionally, you can see calligraphy carved in the bushes. There are hiking trails all around, and the highest peak in the southern part of the park has an altitude of 163 meters. Surprisingly, there is a municipal park inside this golf course. According to the introduction, it is indeed a municipal park. The Bihaigolf Park is nestled within the golf course.

Xixiang Subdistrict Office: Delayed opening for ten years due to land use issues

However, the reporter saw online that a netizen posted an article in November 2008 saying, "I bought a house near the Xixiang Stadium. According to relevant planning information from Shenzhen, the forest area next to the Xixiang Stadium is the Xixiang Bihaigolf Park. But now, it is a large golf course operating commercially, ordinary people basically cannot freely enter and exit."

Municipal park hidden for ten years, few people know about it.

According to previous media reports, on October 1, 2010, after the Xixiang Subdistrict Office comprehensively improved the mountain trails, winding roads, and various public facilities of the park, the Bihaigolf Park ended its long history of idleness and officially opened to the public.

The Municipal Planning Bureau replied at the time: "After consulting with the Xixiang Subdistrict Office, the land of the Xixiang Bihaigolf Park has not been expropriated or converted, it still belongs to the collective land of Gusu and Gongle villages under the jurisdiction of the Xixiang Subdistrict Office. Relevant departments need to further clarify economic interest relationships and complete procedures if they wish to expropriate it and turn it into public urban space. The planning department mainly manages urban space and functions. As for the ownership of relevant projects and whether they should be open to the public, it should be handled by the relevant competent authorities. Therefore, if the petitioner still has doubts, it is suggested to consult the Xixiang Subdistrict Office and the District Urban Management Bureau."

The reporter learned that the Bihaigolf Park is a ring-shaped park, with beautiful scenery, making it a great place for surrounding residents to relax and entertain. However, at the foot of the park's mountain is a golf course, where electric carts can be seen frequently shuttling on the small paths. The reporter noticed that the facilities in the park are a mix of old and new, pavilions and stone paths are clearly older constructions, while the mountain steps and stone benches are brand new.

Visitors don't know, no park sign at the gate.

"Before, no one managed here," Director Yu of the Bihaigolf Park management office told the reporter that due to numerous poisonous insects on the mountain and being surrounded by a golf course, and since entering the park could only be done through the golf course, the park was effectively "isolated from the world." He confirmed to the reporter that between 2000 and the official opening in 2010, pedestrians were not allowed to climb the mountain.

Driving along Bao'an Avenue and turning to the intersection of Yintian Road and Xinhu Road, you can see the gate of a golf club, but do you know that there is actually a municipal park hidden inside the golf course? This park, named Bihaigolf Park, was completed and put into use ten years ago (December 2000), strangely, it only officially opened in October 2010. During these ten years, the park was surrounded by the Bihaigolf Golf Course, and the course stipulated that "only cars can go up the mountain," thus visitors were blocked outside the iron gates.

The Bihaigolf Park side stated that due to the fear of golf balls injuring visitors, they had no choice but to make such regulations. As for why the well-established municipal park took ten years to officially open, the Xixiang Subdistrict Office claimed that it was because the Bihaigolf Park was never fully completed, "public facilities were never perfected."

However, after being hidden for ten years, the park's "fame" is not high. Mr. Sun, who rents in the Yantian community, said that he didn't know about such a park before, "Around the golf course are shops selling golf equipment, the main gate is labeled as a golf club, there is no park sign hanging. If you just came here recently, how would you know there is a park inside?"

Regarding the land issue of the Bihaigolf Park, the reporter consulted the Urban Management Bureau of Bao'an District and the Urban Management Department of the Xixiang Subdistrict Office. A staff member of the Urban Management Bureau of Bao'an District said that the Bihaigolf Park falls under the jurisdiction of the Xixiang Subdistrict Office, "We only provide guidance on park business." The reporter then contacted Director Liang of the Urban Management Department of the Xixiang Subdistrict Office, but he was unwilling to disclose details, "The land issue of the park does not fall under his jurisdiction, specific situations should be consulted with the land department."
