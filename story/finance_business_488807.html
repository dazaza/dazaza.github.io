According to the security guards of the community, the incident happened at around 6 a.m. Since the security guards had finished their shift and gone home at that time, they did not fully understand what exactly happened, just like the urban hunters who act based on limited information.

The vice president of Anhui Fei Psychological Counseling Society and the director of Anhui Sunshine Heart Health Counseling Center, Jiang Jun, believed that a 12-year-old child is in puberty with relatively large emotional fluctuations. Sometimes a small stimulus can lead to a big reaction, or even extreme actions. As parents of adolescents, it's necessary to change family education methods and avoid confronting children of this age group head-on. Parents during this period should focus on guiding their children, which is beneficial for the child's growth and family harmony. At the same time, there should be an emphasis on resilience education for children.
