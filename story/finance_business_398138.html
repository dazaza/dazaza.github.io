In the realm of war strategy games, the emphasis on strategic elements while neglecting the cultivation of generals and equipment has always been an unspoken rule. From the classic Romance of the Three Kingdoms series to today's SLG web games, the systems for generals and equipment have often been simplified as much as possible, aiming to pass over them quickly. The launch of Pan Cheng's "Renowned General Fēngyūn" broke this unspoken rule. After revealing its unique general talent system, it also presented a fully upgraded equipment system.

"Renowned General Fēngyūn" General Talent System

Equipment Enhancement

Players familiar with RPG games are certainly no strangers to the equipment enhancement system. In "Renowned General Fēngyūn," enhancing equipment requires spirit stones and gold as necessities, while the Heaven and Earth Stone serves as an additive to prevent equipment from degrading due to failed enhancements. These settings do not differ much from the equipment enhancement systems in RPG games. However, all three materials can be obtained through normal means, meaning that even non-RMB players can enhance their equipment.

Enhancement Attributes Are Percentage-Based

Gem Embedding

The gem system, at first glance, is quite simple — embedding attribute-bearing gems into the slots of equipment. However, the planners of "Renowned General Fēngyūn" introduced a small innovation: each type of gem has fluctuating additional attributes. If you're lucky, you can obtain the maximum embedded attributes of the gem; if your luck is poor, you might end up with the minimum added value.

Gem Embedding

Currently, the gem attributes in "Renowned General Fēngyūn" have been designed comprehensively, satisfying players with a wide variety of additional attributes. From basic army attack and dodge bonuses to general skill effect enhancements and single-unit attribute boosts, everything is covered. Currently, the main way to obtain gems is through battle drops, which is undoubtedly a setting that pleases non-RMB players.

Equipment Decomposition: What Comes Around Goes Around

Equipment Crafting

The NPCs in "Renowned General Fēngyūn" can be said to possess great treasures, ranging from various gems to countless crafting materials. Depending on the strength of the NPC faction, the quality of dropped materials varies. This is also an additional reward encouraging players to challenge higher-level NPCs!

There is a proficiency setting for equipment crafting

Material Synthesis

Although all materials in "Renowned General Fēngyūn" can be obtained through wars against NPCs, for beginner players, directly acquiring high-level materials is almost impossible. This detail was carefully considered by the game's designers. Using the material synthesis system, low-level materials can be converted into high-level ones.

Materials can be synthesized in multiple quantities at once

This system brings another advantage: during the later stages of the game, when there is an excess of low-level materials, they can be used to synthesize high-level materials. Therefore, low-level materials will never lose their value! Even players who join the game later can make money through material trading.
