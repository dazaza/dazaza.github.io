College girl hesitates between two boyfriends; one boyfriend stabs romantic rival to death

by fhh451dfg on 2012-03-09 16:45:17

On the day of Zhang Baojuan's birthday, Wei Junyong specially bought a set of brand-name clothes for Zhang Baojuan. Wei Junyong's generosity made Zhang Baojuan very happy. Wei Junyong also carefully prepared a candlelight dinner, which made Zhang Baojuan even more overwhelmed with emotion. In the twinkling candlelight, she excitedly said to Wei Junyong: "I have never been as happy as I am today. You are really good to me!"

The campus romance evolved into a tragedy.

At around 10 p.m. on January 2, 2010, the sky was covered with a somber cloud, and students of a certain university were walking around the campus in groups of three or two. Suddenly, a scream tore through the quiet night, and the students immediately ran towards the source of the scream and quickly called the police.

In others' eyes, Zhang Baojuan had a boyfriend who doted on her, which was indeed enviable. But in Zhang Baojuan's eyes, there should be a sense of life when two people are together. She liked romance, but Zhu Guoliang thought that was wasteful; she loved shopping, but Zhu Guoliang only bought cheap market goods. In Zhang Baojuan's view, he didn't satisfy her wishes many times, plus the difference in their values, they often quarreled over small things.

Wei Junyong pulled out a fruit knife and stabbed towards Zhu Guoliang's head. After being stabbed, Zhu Guoliang fought with Wei Junyong. During the struggle, Wei Junyong continued to stab Zhu Guoliang several times on the neck, chest, and shoulder, and blood spurted out. Zhu Guoliang fell on the ground with his breath almost gone, and Wei Junyong kicked him hard on the head and then fled.

At 6 p.m. on January 2, Zhang Baojuan received a text message from Zhu Guoliang: "I'm coming to see you. Can we meet?" She replied: "After the evening study session, wait for me at the school library." She was afraid of being discovered by Wei Junyong, so she deleted the text message.

Zhu Guoliang came from a poor family, and both of his parents were farmers. Although Zhang Baojuan did not ask for too much from him, he did not want her to live a hard life with him in the future. Besides studying, Zhu Guoliang became a promoter in a mall near the school, using part of the money earned for his own living expenses, and sending the rest to Zhang Baojuan. Zhu Guoliang was diligent and studious, especially excelling in mathematics, mechanics, and other subjects, earning the respect of the department leaders and being elected as the class monitor. According to a responsible person of his college, Zhu Guoliang had already been identified as an object for cultivation as teaching staff for the school.

After chatting for more than ten minutes in the second-floor reading room, Zhang Baojuan and Zhu Guoliang walked out together. When they reached the stairs downstairs, a familiar figure passed by them. Zhang Baojuan turned back and saw it was Wei Junyong! At this moment, Wei Junyong's body was filled with anger. He thought that if he didn't give Zhu Guoliang a lesson, that guy would not stay away from Zhang Baojuan.

For five years, their relationship gradually developed cracks.

Soon, police cars and ambulances roared to the scene, and the situation was horrifying. By the side of the road in the campus lay a disheveled young man. His hair was messy, his complexion pale, and his entire body was soaked in large and small bloodstains. About three to four meters away from the young man, a fruit knife about twenty centimeters long with a milky white handle was particularly eye-catching, and the blade was stained with blood. After examination by medical personnel, the young man had been stabbed more than twenty times in the brain, chest, neck, back, and upper limbs, with his heart and lungs pierced.

Their love story started when Zhang Baojuan entered high school in 2005 and became classmates with Zhu Guoliang. They admired each other, encouraged each other, and three years later successively entered universities, moving from Hunan to Shanghai.

Unlike Zhu Guoliang, Zhang Baojuan's university classmate Wei Junyong was eloquent and capable, quite popular among girls. When Zhang Baojuan was feeling sorrowful about her past relationship, Wei Junyong appeared and launched an intense romantic pursuit.

Zhang Baojuan's words made Wei Junyong extremely happy. He held Zhang Baojuan's hand and swore: "If you are willing, I can ensure that I will give you surprises every day!" "But me and Zhu Guoliang..." Before she could finish, Wei Junyong interrupted her: "Are you happy with him? Why torture yourself?"

Although Zhu Guoliang was very good to Zhang Baojuan, he was too boring. Moreover, since they were not in the same school, the distance often left Zhang Baojuan feeling lonely and isolated. Wei Junyong took advantage of the opportunity and used every excuse to invite Zhang Baojuan to eat, drink tea, and chat. As their interactions increased, their feelings deepened, and Zhang Baojuan's heart gradually leaned towards Wei Junyong. On December 20 of last year, Zhang Baojuan formally proposed breaking up with Zhu Guoliang. Despite Zhu Guoliang's repeated pleas for her to change her mind, Zhang Baojuan resolutely turned her back on him.

On December 1 of 2009, after five years of love, cracks began to appear. Zhu Guoliang was unwilling to let go of this relationship, feeling great pain inside, and his emotions were unstable.

At the crime scene, the police found a female youth standing there blankly. Among the scattered books, there were a few photos stained with blood, which were exactly her and the injured male youth's合影s. It was not difficult to guess that she and the male youth had an unusual relationship.

Zhang Baojuan and Wei Junyong's shoulders touched each other, which dealt a fatal blow to Zhu Guoliang. Zhu Guoliang heard that Wei Junyong had a complicated social circle. To win back his girlfriend's heart, he found Zhang Baojuan and warned her not to stay with such a person. This message reached Wei Junyong's ears, and he confronted Zhu Guoliang several times to "settle accounts."
