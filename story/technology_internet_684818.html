Relevant to me and not related to anyone else.

by wangquan1986 on 2012-03-01 10:48:49

Relevant to oneself, unrelated to others. Perhaps at certain moments, it conceals one's state of mind and also masks one's true feelings. This can be a good way to protect oneself and others. Recently, I saw some netizens going through online romances with great passion, continuously expressing their love. It looked quite amusing. Even if it is deepening true love, there's no need to be so ostentatious.

Suddenly, I realized that many people are not mature. Though they have reached the age, their hearts are still small. A sixty-year-old person with a teenager's heart, such a posture cannot fail to make people shudder.

Maturity refers to a person who can keep things hidden and restrained. One must speak when necessary, and not reveal what shouldn't be shown. This is the true sign of maturity. A person openly expressing their sincere emotions might just be revealing a personality trait, but many people are laughing and making fun of them. The pitiable ones often have something detestable, not understanding what true nature is and thus reaping nothing genuine.

It's hard to be mature as a person, and even harder to be mature in love. When it comes to love, it's fundamentally a matter between two people and doesn't necessarily need to be flaunted or boasted about. Everyone has experienced love in different ways, and even those who haven't experienced it surely understand it to some extent. Very mature love may not seem mature, and overly mature love cannot bring happiness... this is a hard balance to achieve, and also a situation that makes it difficult for people to face calmly.

Truly mature love should be famous and mutually supportive. Do you know that all timeless love stories are tragedies? Do you know that those unforgettable loves end with tears and snot? You must know, so if you love, let the love stay between the two of you. There's no need to boast and prove how sincere and deeply in love you are...

Consider hiding your feelings, and also hiding your love. This is not a bad thing. Don't laugh at me for being so humble; in fact, your love story may not necessarily interest others. If it does, it will only be used as a joke. Afterward, like the wind, it will pass by quietly... without a sound. So when a relationship starts, there's no need for others' celebration or knowledge, and when it ends, there's no need for others' concern or pity. Because love is only relevant to oneself, unrelated to others!

When the wind rises at night, writers call it a moonless and windy night. And when love is intense or ordinary, writers refer to it as "you and me" or "helplessly watching the flowers fall." These phrases can only be spoken by those who are in the midst of it. If it's not oneself speaking, who would know?

Pausing in someone else's love, expressing one's own feelings feels somewhat despicable. Well, though I try to persuade others to hide their feelings, I can't help but express my thoughts. Remembering the original intention of this writing, I still can't help but join the common crowd.

Think about it, look at it. Hiding one's feelings not only benefits love but is also suitable for any occasion. In this society where everyone's hearts are restless and insincere, why bother revealing one's true feelings? In fact, it means nothing. It might be better to follow the saying: "Retreat into a small building and form a unity, regardless of spring, summer, autumn, or winter." That way, one might find peace. At the same time, there won't be any big waves. Very stable. People who can reach this state can be considered mature, and also won't irritate or disgust others...

Are you ready, dear friends? Let yourself mature, hide your feelings. Can you do it? If you have concerns about others' motives, I believe everyone can do it. You will certainly be able to do it. How peaceful it is when it's quiet. How beautiful it is when you're with your true love in a shocking moment...

All worldly matters can be viewed this way. Love, more than anything, is simply relevant to oneself and unrelated to others!
