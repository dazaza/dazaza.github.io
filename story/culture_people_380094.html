Reflections after watching the Three Kingdoms

by waterblue on 2011-01-27 16:11:27

1. Liu Bei's experience tells us: the president of a group can start from street vending.

2. Zhuge Liang's experience tells us: entering a private enterprise may actually have more development space than entering a state-owned enterprise.

3. Lü Bu's experience tells us: frequent job-hopping directly leads to no boss daring to hire you.

4. Pang Tong's experience tells us: being too ugly may affect your job application results.

5. Ma Su's experience tells us: no matter how well you study professional courses, they are basically not useful at work.

6. Yang Xiu's experience tells us: in the workplace, always making yourself seem smarter than your leader will lead to a terrible end.

7. Gan Ning's experience tells us: having a bad record does not affect your ability to take the civil service exam. Heroes don't ask about origins.

8. Yuan Shao's experience tells us: if the market is freely competitive, state-owned enterprises may not be able to compete with private enterprises.

9. Cao Cao's experience tells us: if you want to make great achievements in the market, you must first hold up national policies.

10. Guan Yu's experience tells us: even if you are an MBA (Master of Business Administration), a temporary marketing mistake may cause you to lose to someone from another major.

11. Xiahou Dun's experience tells us: self-mutilation indeed has a great deterrent effect. When facing such a person, I am also scared.

12. Jiang Gan's experience tells us: in the securities market, the good news released by major players is generally for luring you.

13. Gongsun Zan's experience tells us: those who are good swimmers will surely choke on water, and those who play with fire will surely get burned; there are risks in the stock market, and one must enter cautiously.

14. Da Qiao and Xiao Qiao's experiences tell us: talented, wealthy, and handsome men usually cannot stay with you until the end.

15. Liu Shan's experience tells us: when a large enterprise is acquired, senior managers will definitely pay attention to employees' psychological dynamics.

16. Jiang Wei's experience tells us: double degrees sometimes are more competitive than postgraduate entrance examination.

17. Huang Yueying's experience tells us: studying well is not as good as marrying well.

18. Liu Biao's experience tells us: health is the capital of revolution. No matter how great you are, once you die, someone will sleep with your wife, spend your money, and beat your children.

19. Zhao Yun's experience tells us: no matter how strong an individual's strength is, if you only think about high salary, the result will be only one: having a career but no cause.

20. The Sima family's experience tells us: working for others is not as good as starting your own business.
