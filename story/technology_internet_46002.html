I. You can still do online marketing without a website

1. What is marketing without a website?

No need to establish a commercial marketing website, but instead fully utilize network resources by creating online homepages, online stores, or directly using online marketing software for online business operations.

This is an important, convenient, cost-saving, and popular method of online marketing that encourages many small and medium-sized enterprises to engage in e-commerce and online marketing.

I. You can still do online marketing without a website

2. The feasibility of marketing without a website

A. The Internet provides the realistic possibility for buyers and sellers to connect through the shortest path and fastest speed transactions.

B. The development of online stores, the rise of online marketing software, and the improvement of mass email functions all provide realistic possibilities for operating without a website in online marketing.

C. A company with just one computer can conduct marketing, provided their sales personnel have some basic computer knowledge.

II. Benefits of Marketing Without a Website

1. Saves a significant amount of costs related to equipment purchases, space usage, and long-term website maintenance fees.

2. Can save a large amount of manpower such as web designers, website builders, maintenance staff, and website managers.

3. Due to the absence of complex website construction and maintenance issues, it makes it easier to popularize online marketing knowledge and facilitate large-scale promotion and dissemination.
