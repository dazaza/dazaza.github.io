"Rising Star of Domestic Laboratories: An Interview with Suzhou Greent Lab System Co., Ltd." Special Report, China Laboratory Information Network, February 11, 2012. Reporter: Su Da, Photographer: San De.

Suzhou Greent Lab System Engineering Co., Ltd. is a modern scientific research and production enterprise specializing in the overall planning, design, production, installation of laboratories, and after-sales service for laboratory products. It is a provider of comprehensive laboratory solutions. Greent has long focused on technological innovation, relying on its professional accumulation in the field of laboratory equipment over the years, continuously absorbing advanced technology and design concepts from Europe, America, Japan, and other countries, combined with the development trends of the domestic market, to develop a series of laboratory furniture and supporting equipment with leading domestic levels, which have been highly praised by many enterprises...

In 2009, Suzhou Greent Lab System Engineering Co., Ltd. participated in the "China Third-Party Testing Laboratory Development Forum" organized by the China Academy of Inspection and Quarantine since 2009. To date, it has successfully held two sessions continuously, with an increasing number of participants and exhibition scale. Nearly 300 professional representatives attended the "Second Forum," and there were news reports from Xinhua News Agency, Beijing Television, China Food Quality Newspaper, China Quality Newspaper, and multiple websites and journals.

From May 24-26, 2010, Suzhou Greent Lab System Engineering Co., Ltd. participated in the "CECIA2010 and South China International Laboratory Equipment Exhibition" held at the Jinhan Exhibition Center in Guangzhou and achieved great success. This was our company's first participation in an exhibition. Through the active cooperation of all departments of Suzhou Greent Lab System Company, this exhibition achieved good social benefits and a good brand display effect.

In September 2011, Suzhou Greent Lab System Engineering Co., Ltd. participated in the first high-level forum of China's third-party testing industry and one of the most influential annual important activities in the testing field —— the "Third China Third-Party Testing Laboratory Development Forum and Laboratory Exhibition" with the theme of "Fair Testing, Serving Livelihood" held in Chengde, Hebei. The forum mainly consisted of a series of activities including the opening ceremony, forum keynote speeches, roundtable discussions, product exhibitions, laboratory displays, paper exchanges, etc. Government authorities, regulatory certification agencies, national quality inspection systems, technical directors, quality directors, laboratory managers, senior engineers from domestic and foreign testing institutions, analysis and testing centers, laboratories, and enterprise quality control departments were invited to jointly participate in and discuss the forum.

In various major forums and exhibitions, Greent Lab System Engineering Company always maintained a peaceful, low-key, pragmatic, and sincere business strategy, not deliberately exposing itself in the media, not intentionally allowing media interviews, and not casually accepting interviews. Focusing on making good products is the starting point of work for Greent people. We believe that the market is the best judge!

Suzhou Greent Lab System Engineering Co., Ltd. is a modern enterprise specializing in the overall planning, design, production, installation, and integrated after-sales service of laboratories, providing comprehensive laboratory solutions. The company has long focused on technological innovation, relying on its professional accumulation in the field of laboratory equipment over the years, continuously absorbing advanced technology and design concepts from Europe, America, Japan, and other countries, combined with the development trends of the domestic market, to develop a series of laboratory furniture and supporting equipment with leading domestic levels. Our designed products have a steady and generous appearance, flexible and diverse structures, and simple and convenient operations. While maintaining the product style unchanged, we have developed steel, aluminum, wood, and other high and medium-end series products for different customer groups. Through these products, we have upgraded the hardware facilities of modern laboratories for many excellent domestic enterprises, government agencies, scientific research colleges, and universities to a top-notch level.

"Leading the trend of the times, pursuing excellence in quality," we will always focus on professionalism and expertise in this field, aiming to be one of the top laboratory engineering service providers in China. We continue to guide new concepts, explore new technologies, and apply new materials to provide the healthiest, safest, and most environmentally friendly laboratory equipment for a wide range of enterprises and institutions, thereby creating an elegant, comfortable, aesthetically modern, and highly effective scientific research working environment. "Great-Lab," your best laboratory partner. (Written by Su Da)
