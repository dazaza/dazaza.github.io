4. Even if you cannot be as huge as the sun, you can still shine like the moon.

People's endowments, knowledge, wisdom, opportunities, and abilities etc., always have differences. So not everyone can be as great as Bill Gates. However, as long as we keep making efforts, find our own positioning, and never give up, even if we cannot be as dazzling as the sun, we may still emit light like the moon.

5. With health, there is a future! Happiness Index = Health + Career + Love + Money + Family + Friendship + Power...

Life is rich and colorful, many goals are worth pursuing, but health is always the first. Just like health is 1, career, love, money, family, friendship, power etc., are the zeros behind 1. Therefore, happiness with just 1 is far from enough, but without 1 (health), no matter how many zeros follow, they mean nothing to you. As the saying goes, peace is a blessing. Thus, the premise of happiness is to care for and cherish your life, and strive to create and share the values of career, love, wealth, power etc., in life. People always bring their own limitations or even prejudices in terms of concepts, knowledge, experience, interests, regions, etc., to treat, explain, and transform the world. The world itself has no right or wrong; what is wrong is merely human concepts and behaviors. So when your views and behaviors are correct, your world is also correct, and your dissatisfaction, troubles, hatreds, and conflicts will greatly decrease, and the happiness index will greatly increase. Please remember:

1. How we view life, life treats us accordingly.

2. How we treat others, others treat us accordingly.

3. A correct life value concept is more important than any other factor, it is the cornerstone of happiness.

2. Excellence is a habit.

This was said by the ancient Greek philosopher Aristotle. If excellence is a habit, then mediocrity is also a habit. We cannot choose the era of our birth, nor can we choose the environment of our birth, nor can we choose our parents, but we can cultivate a correct life value concept through later efforts, form good nature and outstanding skills, and create value for ourselves, our families, companies, and society. Habits are the result of accumulated cultivation over time. Remember: thoughts determine actions, actions determine habits, habits determine character, and character determines fate. Therefore, if we want to become excellent, from now on we must make the virtues necessary for excellence into a habit, so that our excellent actions become commonplace and turn into our second nature. For example, if you want to become an efficient person, you should develop the following seven habits: being proactive, beginning with the end in mind, putting first things first, thinking win-win, seeking first to understand, synergizing, and continuously renewing yourself.

3. Knowledge changes destiny, dreams achieve the future.

The history of humanity is essentially the history of acquiring knowledge, applying knowledge to transform the world, and realizing dreams. Therefore, knowledge, wisdom, and experience are undoubtedly crucial to your growth. It is not enough to acquire and possess knowledge like a bookworm; the ability to apply knowledge, wisdom, and experience is the most important. So those who read books should not look down on those without education, as their ability to apply knowledge and realize dreams may not be worse than yours; and those without education should not look down on the weak-looking scholars, aren't the world's richest man Gates, China's richest man Ding Lei, and Chen Tianqiao all knowledge heroes? Having dreams alone is not enough, the key is to put dreams into practice. In order to realize tomorrow's dreams, let us forget today's pain and hardships.

1. If you are correct, your world is correct.
