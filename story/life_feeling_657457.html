Thank you for walking through my life.

by meimei234 on 2012-01-08 22:47:55

Thank you for being part of my life.

I said I would grow up slowly on my own, but when I turned around, I still shed tears of weakness. I'm not lying, it's just that I'm not used to it yet. Please forgive me for not being able to fulfill my promise right away. As you said, time is the best ointment for healing wounds, and I will gradually get used to living without you.

In your yearbook, you said that my sunny smile drove away your gloom. That was a lie, wasn't it! Since we met, you have always given me too much protection. You told me not to watch TV all the time and reminded me to study hard. Even though I broke my promises many times, you just pretended to ignore me. Sitting behind you, I always liked to play with your hat during class, but you just smiled and let it go. I remember how lazy I was back then, often being late and getting punished by the teacher to clean the classroom. On my birthday, you gave me a Mickey Mouse alarm clock and pulled the corners of my mouth saying, "Now what excuse do you have for being late?" I rolled my eyes and ran off. I cried in a place where no one could see me because you were always so good to me. After that, I wasn't late as often. Even though the alarm clock broke the next year, I never bought a new one. When we were separated into different classes in the second year of junior high, I couldn't sit behind you anymore. I was afraid that distance would dilute our friendship, and that you, being so excellent and having so many friends, would forget about me. But you continued to be as kind to me as ever. Although we haven't seen each other since then, I still remember your kindness, the dolls and banners you gave me, and I will cherish them forever.

Xiaofeng is like a teacher. For my faults and flaws, she criticizes me outright. It made me want to kneel before Buddha and repent many times. Thanks to her, I am no longer blindly arrogant. If I tell her something sad, she gives me an encouraging pat on the shoulder, making me cry with gratitude. But don't underestimate her either. If you accidentally irritate her, you'll be singing "Regret" in hell for eternity.

Shanshan, you are actually the friend who values me the most. You have been quietly by my side all along. I said I like wish bottles and stars. You said you would give me a wish bottle filled with 1001 stars as a birthday gift. You also like stars, and those 1001 stars were folded by you in your spare time over several years, but you gave them all to me. You looked up lyrics for me online and copied notes for me. Math has always been my weak point, so you took time out to teach me math problems. But my dull brain still made you grind your teeth in frustration. I remember you once commented on me, saying that I am a coward who only shouts loudly in front of friends and then hides behind them. At the time, I laughed until tears came out. But you're right, I only receive your protection and have never brought you happiness. I am indeed a coward! I also wanted to fulfill what the teacher said: whoever can be desk mates for all three years of junior high school are the most fated people. Unfortunately, I can't be with you till the end.

Now I am far from all of you, but I have never forgotten any of the little moments we shared. Thank you for being part of my life. Maybe you will slowly forget about me, but I will always keep you in my heart! Dear friends, meeting you has been my lifelong happiness.
