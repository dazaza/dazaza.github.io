2011 Analysis Report of China's Automotive Accessories Industry

Analysis Report of China's Automotive Accessories Industry:

According to official automobile predictions, in 2011 alone, the sales revenue of the automotive products market mainly focused on car beauty will reach RMB 113 billion. By 2012, it will double to reach RMB 50 billion. If related supporting services are also taken into account, the market space will further expand. According to official estimates, by 2012, the scale of the entire automobile aftermarket will reach RMB 880 billion, second only to Japan in Asia, ranking second in Asia.

Through a calm review of the bustling scene of many automobile manufacturers entering the automotive accessories industry, we find that although China's automotive accessories industry has developed beyond previous levels, there are still objective problems that cannot be ignored.

The analysis report of China's automotive accessories industry believes:

1. The price of China's automotive accessories market is very transparent and open, which may also be another defect currently existing in the automotive accessories industry. You don't have to walk around the market much to discover: the same brand of car audio sells for more than RMB 300 in the wholesale market, but when it reaches a high-end store, it costs at least over RMB 1,000 or even several thousand yuan. The same branded seat cushion only sells for dozens of yuan in the wholesale market, but in a high-end branded store, it costs over RMB 100; this huge price difference confuses not only consumers but also professionals.

2. The threshold for the automotive accessories industry is relatively low while the profit margin is quite large. Numerous investors join the production and sales army of automotive accessories without standardized guidance. There is a certain scale in sales, but there are not many well-known brands. The awareness of automotive accessories is not high, there are too many counterfeit goods flooding the market.

3. Most enterprises in the industry neglect corporate culture, leading to a lack of soul in the enterprise. In today's corporate competition, the ultimate competition is about corporate culture. Compared with the successful corporate culture of whole vehicle enterprises, the shaping of corporate culture in automotive accessories enterprises lacks strength, resulting in a lack of soul in the enterprise.

4. The homogenization of automotive accessories is very serious. Without innovation, they only talk about profits. Currently, among the automotive accessories produced and sold in the market, there are not many products that clearly reflect individuality. The design is simple, and the production is not delicate enough. Monotonous products obviously cannot meet the needs of consumers. With the sharp increase in the number of private cars, consumers' demand for automotive accessories shows a strong trend towards personalization, requiring more personalized automotive decorations that can better reflect the owner's individuality.

5. The car beauty industry is not professional enough, and beauty talents have become a bottleneck constraining the development of the industry. Taking car beauty as an example, car beauty technology has already experienced the development of early technologies such as waxing, coating, and glazing.

After several years of development, the pattern and development trends of the automotive accessories industry have undergone significant changes. "Intensive" service and "one-stop" sales chain operation system will become the mainstream of the domestic automotive accessories market. Chinese and foreign-funded enterprises are all focusing on this point, using large and medium-sized cities such as Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Beijing as bases, accelerating resource integration and domestic layout. The automotive accessories industry harbors business opportunities. With the continuous fulfillment of China's commitment to joining the World Trade Organization, China's automobile aftermarket has achieved remarkable development, presenting ten business opportunities. After-sales repair services. Automobile advertising. Intelligent transportation. Automobile entertainment. Automobile clubs. Automobile maintenance. Parts and premium retail. Automobile insurance. Automobile financing. Automobile information.

The newly issued Article 33 of the "Automobile Industry Development Policy" explicitly states: "The State encourages automobile, motorcycle, parts manufacturing enterprises and financial, service trade enterprises to learn from international mature automobile marketing methods, management experience, and service trade concepts, and actively develop automobile service trade."

The next two years are important for the development of the automotive accessories industry. The automotive accessories industry is transitioning from the previous "dirty, messy, poor" state towards scaling up, standardization, and specialization. Opportunities and challenges coexist. Only by recognizing the situation can one seize opportunities in development and achieve greater success.
