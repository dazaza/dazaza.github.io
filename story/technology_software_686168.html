Nursing Home Director Attends All Funerals of 30 Elderly People Passed Away in 4 Years

by xue23swsh on 2012-03-01 22:48:31

Wang Guiying is feeding water to an elderly person who has been bedridden for a long time. Photo by Bai Shi, reporter of our newspaper.

In the Wutong Garden residential area in Changchun City, a plaque with the words "Love Elderly Apartment" hangs on the wall, not very eye-catching.

Wang Guiying, 49 years old, is the director of this elderly apartment. But everyone says she doesn't look like a director because she takes care of everything for the elderly, including cutting hair, taking baths, washing feet, and cleaning up feces and urine... She said that these elderly people are her own family members, and if she treats them well, they treat her like their daughter.

The elder who scolds her can't live without her

At the age of 36, Wang Guiying came to Changchun to drive a taxi after divorcing her ex-husband. One day, she met a passenger, "That woman runs a nursing home." They both talked about running a nursing home during the ride, which inspired her. "I will also open a nursing home." She said it and did it, rented a house, and the elderly apartment was opened. On New Year's Day in 2008, a 92-year-old man came to take a look, and she quickly went to his home to pick him up. The man was bedridden, and when she saw him for the first time, he was lying in bed. She hesitantly tidied up the man's bed, washed dirty things in the bathroom, and silently shed tears.

The third elderly person she took in was an old lady with cerebellar atrophy. When she had an episode, she loved to scold people, disturbing several elderly people so much that they wanted to leave due to symptoms of rhinitis. She quickly found the elderly woman and told her: "If you want to scold someone, find me, I am the director." This sentence stuck with the elderly woman, and each time, the elderly woman would come to scold her. Until one day, she couldn't help herself anymore, called the elderly woman's family, and kicked the elderly woman out for the first time. Three months later, the family came back and said: "My mother said she misses you very much." With just this one sentence, she welcomed the elderly woman back.

Attended the funeral of 30 elderly people

Watching the elderly pass away is something Wang Guiying often experiences. The first elderly person she sent off was a 93-year-old grandfather.

"He surnamed Yang, a particularly kind elderly man." The elderly man came with severe heart disease and lived in the apartment for more than a year. The elderly man called her "my dear daughter," and in the last moments before passing away in front of his children, the elderly man pulled out a gold coin from a small pocket in his chest. This gold coin was the elderly man's lifelong treasure. The elderly man gave the gold coin to her. Later, she attended the elderly man's funeral and returned the gold coin to the family. "I cannot accept anything from the elderly; what I do for them is what I should do."

In four years, Wang Guiying sent off one elderly person after another, and many of them peacefully passed away in her arms. After each elderly person passed away, Wang Guiying attended their funerals, a total of 30 times.

Elderly people can't live without her even for a moment

Because of her sincere kindness towards the elderly, the reputation of the nursing home spread. Many elderly people were referred through connections.

A family member's words made her unforgettable for life: "If you only take in easy-to-care-for elderly people, your nursing home will eventually close down." From then on, no matter what kind of elderly people she received, she took care of them wholeheartedly.

"The elderly are also like children, when they come here, it's like entering kindergarten, they don't adapt," Wang Guiying said. She often treated the elderly as children. Sometimes, when she encountered troublemakers, she would sleep with them and hold them in her own quilt. She held six or seven elderly people in this way.

Since opening the nursing home, Wang Guiying hasn't gone shopping at malls or celebrated the Spring Festival with her family.

Dai Yongfa, an elderly resident here, said that everyone couldn't live without the director, they couldn't bear not seeing her for a moment. Bathing, washing hair, cutting hair, feeding... The elderly were willing to seek her help for everything, and other caregivers were not allowed to do these tasks. Wang Guiying said that sometimes she felt tired. Over the years, she hadn't slept a full night, setting alarms every two hours on her phone.

Due to chronic sleep deprivation, she fainted five or six times and often collapsed while working, losing consciousness.

Her husband asked her, "You take care of these elderly people like this, what do you gain?" Wang Guiying didn't know what she gained either. She said, initially it was for making a living, but now it's not. She likes this career. In the nursing home, almost every elderly person's bedside hangs a banner given by their family.

Her wish is that in the future, when she has money, she will build a big house to accommodate more elderly people. She will continue doing this until she grows old herself...

By Feng Yan, reporter of our newspaper.
