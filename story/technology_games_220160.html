Happy Baby Game Guide

by jaykong on 2009-08-08 16:58:32

The objectives of this game are only two. The first is to keep increasing the score of your pet. The second is to complete the goals set by the game, thereby claiming a title or championship. This article will introduce methods for boosting scores.

When you start "Happy Pet," you begin with one room. As your level changes, more rooms will be added continuously. There are a total of nine rooms divided into 40 levels. Initially, your pet just needs to exceed 500 points to advance a level. As your pet's level increases, it will need to exceed 1000 points to upgrade. Each time you level up, the experience required doubles in order to reach the next level. Every time you upgrade, your pet will receive rewards of varying degrees, such as new houses, soap, brushes, balls, frisbees, and jump ropes, etc.

When Happy Pet reaches level 40, its title will change to "All Time Great." At the same time, the space in the pet's house will expand due to changing houses. Brushes, balls, frisbees, and jump ropes will all turn golden. The soap will become a pink heart shape. Since Happy Pet can only reach up to level 40, after reaching level 40, no matter how much the pet’s score continues to increase, it will always remain at the Happy Pet level.

However, at that point, the pet seems like it can start opening land to plant flowers and grass. This is quite fun.

In Happy Pet, players can not only earn points by feeding and washing their friends' pets, but they can also choose either the "World" or "Hong Kong" option to feed, wash, and play interactive games with unfamiliar players' pets to earn points. The key to the scoring method mentioned above is to visit different players' homes, find a pet that hasn't been fully fed yet, and continuously feed it. In fact, if you spend some time trying, you will find that there are always some pets that can eat endlessly every day (even if the red line of Happy Pet has already reached the top). The method for earning points through washing is to prepare a large amount of five-dollar fruit and search for the aforementioned pets. Since each time you feed another player's Happy Pet a piece of fruit, you can earn 2 points. Therefore, feeding 10 fruits will give you 20 points. Generally speaking, after a player uses this method to level up once, the corresponding Happy Pet will stop eating.

The simplest way to earn money and level up~ click nationwide~ click on avatars, visit friends~ randomly click~ and complete interactions with friends. Currently, I see that completing an interaction adds +5 experience and +20 coins. It appears that each friend can interact twice a day this way; any more won't add coins or experience. The specifics are still under study. Bai He believes that the main appeal of this game lies in the process of leveling up your pet. Money earned can be saved without buying DD immediately.
