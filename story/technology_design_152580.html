Recently planned a courier company website

by longersing on 2009-05-23 14:44:18

Domestic express companies are gradually replacing China Post, which has super poor service quality. I'm not particularly emotional or targeting China Post specifically; it's just that the people around me all talk about this. Across the country, there are a few places with good service attitudes, but they are too few. Most of the postal offices still think they are the dominant ones and others are begging them for services, so they act arrogantly. My Gu Baiyou planning was also initiated due to these reasons.

Nowadays, I no longer use China Post for my own packages. Because several times their couriers said they delivered the package but no one was there to receive it, but in fact, it was all lies - I was at home waiting the whole time. One time was even funnier; they deliberately said they had delivered it, and I said I wasn't home (testing him), and he said, "Then never mind." He also said that he left it right by my door, and when no one received it, he scolded me. Later, when I asked him if he could be more polite, he immediately changed his tone and said he was leaving. I was speechless. Isn't that funny? What a deceitful trick. After that, I stopped using their services.

To show my strong support for domestic express companies and logistics enterprises, I made a small plan for my friend's company opening. It's an express company. My friend is great, and he is also my idol. Of course, this plan cannot be compared with the leading domestic companies like Shentong or YTO, but at least it can compare with that "China Post" worm. In my opinion, if we all boycott them together, they will definitely go bankrupt within 3 years.

Let's talk about the content of the plan: In this website planning I did for him, the website mainly presents itself to everyone in the form of a brand. The layout is simple and the functions are concise, mainly considering the distribution of the user group: they are from different levels, and their familiarity with computer usage varies. Let alone asking them to open a website and browse it. This point is completely different from wedding planning and wedding celebration website planning. Therefore, the main functions on the website are focused on the main business of express delivery. So initially, it was decided as: reservation, consultation, and inquiry. Among these, inquiry is an indispensable function because it allows customers to track their goods online and always understand the status of their goods.

For graphic design, I chose colors that are as consistent as possible with the enterprise trademark logo, trying to avoid any disharmony that might lead to the loss of first impression sensory experience. In terms of layout structure, it is designed so that all the site can be accessed without scrolling or pulling down the scroll bar within one window, providing convenience. Finally, regarding the sections, they can be set by themselves, but remember not to miss out on things that customers care about: contact information, customer cases, and cooperating companies. Try to have as many as possible. Although we know that no one will look at these things, at least it's a matter of face, and having them on the site represents authority.

That's all for today.
