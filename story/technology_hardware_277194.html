The specific management method is: Telecommunications enterprises should provide users with at least one tariff scheme whose structure and billing unit are the same as the current standard tariff, and whose fees do not exceed the current standard tariff. The current standard tariff refers to the basic monthly rental fee and intra-district call fee of fixed local telephones determined during the structural adjustment of telecommunications tariffs in 2001 in each province, autonomous region, and municipality, as well as the current standard tariffs for the basic monthly rental fee and intra-local network call fee of wireless access telephones determined in each province, autonomous region, and municipality. Ministry of Industry and Information Technology

Regarding the adjustment of tariffs for fixed local telephone and other services

The following is the full text of the notice:

Two, upper limit management will be implemented for the long-term rental fees of leased circuits. The upper limit standard will temporarily follow the provisions in the "Notice on Structural Adjustment of Telecommunications Tariffs" (Xinbu Lianqing [2000] No. 1255, hereinafter referred to as "Document No. 1255"). For the tariff standards of leased circuit services not specified in Document No. 1255, the group companies of various telecommunications enterprises may independently determine them by referring to the tariff standards of already listed rate services, reporting to the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission for record.

National Development and Reform Commission

Sina Tech News, November 24th afternoon message, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission jointly issued the "Notice on Adjusting the Management Method of Tariffs for Fixed Local Telephone and Other Services", reforming part of the telecommunications tariff management method, implementing upper limit management for fixed local telephone and other service tariffs.

The "Notice" stipulates that the basic monthly rental fee and intra-district call fee of fixed local telephone services, as well as the basic monthly rental fee and intra-local network call fee of wireless access telephone services (commonly known as "wireless landline" or "Little Smart"), will change from the current government pricing to upper limit management.

Telecommunications enterprises should further strengthen research and internal management of telecommunications tariffs, gradually reducing costs through technological, service, and management innovations, striving to steadily decrease the overall level of telecommunications tariffs, allowing the general public to share in the achievements of telecommunications development and reform.

When the group companies of telecommunications enterprises formulate or approve the tariff schemes of their subordinate provincial enterprises, they should file their tariff schemes or approval opinions with the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission.

For domestic inter-provincial (autonomous region, municipality) as well as international, Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan leased circuit long-term rental business tariff standards, the group companies of telecommunications enterprises should file them with the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the National Development and Reform Commission, and provincial telecommunications enterprises should also file them with the local communications administration bureau and the same-level price regulatory authorities before execution.
