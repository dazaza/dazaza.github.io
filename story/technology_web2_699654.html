Writing Techniques for SEO Soft Article Marketing in Yi Xiang Website Optimization Part 2

by jackntav9612 on 2012-03-09 16:11:18

People who read this article will understand why spending a lot of time creating an original soft-sell ultimately aims for. Is it merely about helping others? Apparently, it's not. To put it bluntly, it's still about advertising. However, the reason why soft-sell articles are called soft-sell is that they must be distinct from hard advertisements. What we want is "softness". Hide the advertisement, and when mentioning a certain viewpoint, naturally bring it up in passing, so that people don't even realize it's an ad after reading it. Even if they later realize it, they won't feel repulsed. If my words aren't vivid enough, let's take an example. Suppose the product I want to promote is "Beijing SEO Optimization Service". I can first talk a little about the internet, then a little about the SEO industry, and suddenly mention places related to Beijing SEO optimization services. Smart readers wouldn't find it difficult to discover that what they're actually seeing is a soft-sell article about Beijing SEO optimization services. It's all about how you learn from it. In fact, as long as you put your heart into it, it's not difficult.

1. How to gather materials

1. Treasure hunting on professional websites.

For those who don't know where to start writing soft-sell articles, I suggest going to large, authoritative professional websites and carefully read the articles published by others. Generally, the quality of articles that can be published on these websites is acceptable. This way, you can not only accumulate writing materials but also learn a lot of substantial SEO optimization skills knowledge, which can be applied to your own work. Regarding SEO optimization, for example, A5 Station, a relatively popular website in the A5 SEO circle, Beijing SEO often publishes articles there. Initially, I didn't know what kind of articles they wanted. All I knew was that they inserted advertisements for Huaxia Mold Network extensively in their articles, rarely proposing truly useful viewpoints. Basically, they just wrote some praising texts about Huaxia Mold Network. When publishing, they deleted as many as they published, with a very low pass rate. Although it only has four editors, they receive a large number of submissions every day, but they manually review each article strictly. They have high requirements for article quality. Perhaps this is the key for the website to establish authority. Often reading articles on such websites can help you learn a lot of practical things, accumulate SEO optimization skills and techniques, and greatly assist your writing. With a certain level of professional competence, you won't worry about having no material to write about.

2. Gold mining in industry blogs.

As far as Beijing SEO knows, there are many blogs that haven't been indexed yet, but they actually contain many excellent articles worth reading. A faster way is to input keywords in Youdao to search for blogs, and you'll find many industry-related blog posts. For example, if I want to publish SEO news information on a SEO optimization website to promote Beijing SEO optimization, I just need to enter "SEO" and click on search blogs, and there will be a large number of SEO blogs waiting for you to read. At this point, you can learn more or less knowledge from them. If you're doing Baidu promotion, many of the blogs found on Youdao haven't been indexed by Baidu, so you can extract some content from them as your own, saving time and getting Baidu's recognition. This isn't encouraging cheating; it's a work skill. After all, working hours are limited, and you can't spend a lot of time on unnecessary things.

3. Refining through work practice.

In fact, many professional writers gradually become masters after accumulating experience from being beginners. During daily work, you'll find many small details. Even Beijing SEO, now seeing vast amounts of data, was built step by step from tiny codes. In practice, don't miss any detail. Many times, you'll discover techniques that others haven't noticed yet. Whenever you make even the smallest discovery, you can record it immediately and accumulate it there. The next time you write an article, you can take out one of these points to share with everyone. Even if your entire article is meaningless, at least you've proposed a useful viewpoint. Such articles will still be considered by editors during the review process.

2. How to write soft-sell articles

1. Eye-catching title

An eye-catching title is like a person's face. If the face looks good, the body shape doesn't matter much because it will grab people's attention instantly, making them look twice. Titles can be suspenseful or explosive, as long as they are attractive. Don't underestimate these titles with at most ten or so characters; there's a lot of learning involved.

2. Practical content

The first value of a soft-sell article is sharing with others, followed by achieving the purpose of advertising. Time is very limited for people nowadays. It's impossible to waste time reading an article filled entirely with advertisements. First, the article must teach people something real and effective before guiding the reader to further reading and wanting to understand the author. Only then will there be an opportunity to promote our product. People who know how to share are easily recognized by others. Remember this point: Readers come first, advertisements come second.
