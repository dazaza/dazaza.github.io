With the continuous development of the Chinese citizens' economy, housing construction and abstract gradually move towards the track of commoditization. People's living environment will have increasingly higher requirements. These requirements demand that we put efforts into constructing a natural environment and residential environment with cultural soul. Green building is the harmony built on the basis of the natural ecological system as the fundamental principle of a benign cycle between construction and environment. It combines factors such as environmental design, architectural law, appropriate techniques, and design.

Key words: Residential Environment, Green, Design and Social and Economic Development

As people's lifestyles improve and continuously enhance living environments, more and more individuals are emphasizing landscape environments in their new housing choices. Whether it's green or dynamic, the natural and cultural landscapes in residential areas and their surroundings are becoming harmonious. This modern lifestyle injects new content into neighborhood environmental design and also sets higher demands for this design concept. Buildings without flowers are incomplete; flowers should accompany construction. Isn't it true that excellent foreign residential area greening is an essential part of urban green spaces? It is the most widely distributed, closest to the people, and most frequently used environmental space by residents. The green ecology, aesthetics, psychology, and economic benefits in residential areas all play significant roles.

Not only must it be aesthetically pleasing, but it also needs to balance the environment, making the air fresh, cool, and humid, preventing dust pollution, regulating light, temperature, humidity, and airflow, improving weather and wind conditions. Moreover, the beautiful scenery and environmental impacts bring joy while eliminating urban noise, relieving physical and mental fatigue, relaxing the mind, creating tranquility, which is beneficial for life in gardening and promoting residents' health. It plays an important regulatory and exemplary role in improving urban ecological quality. Green housing can also be called sustainable ecological environment, meaning not destroying the fundamental conditions of the ecological environment, thus building spatial environments to meet the needs of social and economic development activities over a long period for human health. Greening areas not only relate to reducing energy consumption but also involve reducing water usage, minimizing material and resource utilization, decreasing waste, improving air quality, lighting, rainwater treatment and retention to replenish groundwater, restoring the natural environment, and reducing vehicle dependency.

Green residential buildings promote economic development and social progress. More green space development promotes the sustainable development of the economy and society. To create a good living environment that gives people aesthetic enjoyment and cultivates moral sentiments, providing society with a stable foundation, planners, designers, and architects must have a strong sense of social responsibility. From an ecological perspective, green areas seek harmony and interactive development with the natural environment. Planners, designers, and architects must understand and respect specific geographical environments, topography, climate, and unique cultural characteristics of a particular area to minimize existing ecological and cultural environmental damage. In green residential design, energy and other resources' consumption should be minimized, and the construction process should avoid generating waste and pollutants as much as possible. Maximizing recycling, reusable materials' use reduces non-renewable energy consumption, utilizing solar, wind, geothermal, and other green, clean energy sources. Green residential design optimizes the primary factors in outdoor space. Green residential areas consist of land planted with trees, flowers, and greenery. Green residential areas have two functions: one is to establish outdoor living spaces to meet various leisure environment needs.

The other is to create a natural environment using landscape features to produce beautiful outdoor facilities. Besides necessary embellishments, in the outdoor environment of residential areas, settings like sculptures, water features, lights, tables, chairs, benches, ladders, railings, flowers, etc., cannot be neglected. They not only bring convenience to people's lives but also enrich the taste of outdoor spaces. In green residential environmental design, special attention should be given to ecological protection: suppressing the use of toxic and harmful substances, conserving natural resources, protecting the environment through design work, and beautifying the environment with green plants. This itself is a form of natural resource conservation, as green residential areas expand the scope of natural resources through planting. Residential area greening design starts from residents' safety, health, hygiene, and improvement of the local microclimate in the city. Through high-quality green space environment construction, it creates a natural, quiet, beautiful, hygienic, and fully-environmental urban living atmosphere. When designing modern residential areas, attention should be paid to the following issues: Avoiding single grasslands and focusing solely on large-scale grasslands for residential area greening. Large-scale grassland construction is unscientific under relative community conditions, where grasslands are strong in appreciation but weak in practicality. Establishing regional natural and geographical conditions, structurally configuring reasonably, enriching levels, coordinating species relationships, and landscaping garden plants to make them naturally harmonious, making full use of space, and ensuring that light, water, nutrients, air, and other resources are fully and reasonably utilized.
