Nutritional benefits of common vegetables for women

by longersing on 2009-10-13 21:48:14

Common vegetables have many nutritional effects for women: garlic makes hair shiny, cilantro prevents osteoporosis, mustard makes complexion rosy, and toona sinensis can aid in conception.

Garlic makes hair shiny

Many women worry that eating garlic will cause bad breath. In fact, it is a "health guardian" for women.

Extensive epidemiological surveys show that the incidence of cancer is significantly lower in garlic-producing areas and among people who eat garlic regularly. Professor Li Huiming said that eating half a head of raw garlic every day can inhibit breast cancer and ovarian cancer.

Modern medical research has proven that allicin has strong antibacterial properties and is effective against various pathogenic microorganisms such as vaginal trichomoniasis and amoeba. Eating one head of raw garlic daily can effectively prevent and treat vaginitis.

Dr. Uzun, a gastroenterologist from Turkey, said that eating garlic regularly not only helps prevent cancer and blood clots but also keeps hair black and shiny. If you massage your scalp with garlic juice, it can not only reduce hair loss but also turn gray hair back to black.

Cilantro Prevents Osteoporosis

Medical studies have shown that after the age of 40, bone formation decreases, and the cortical bone becomes thinner, especially in women. At this time, a woman's bones are like an egg, with the cortical bone thin like an eggshell, fragile and easy to break. Experts remind that women entering middle age should eat more boron-rich foods to help the body absorb minerals and protect the bones, and cilantro contains a lot of boron.

In addition, cilantro is rich in iron, calcium, potassium, zinc, vitamin A, and vitamin C. It can also promote urination, helping maintain stable blood sugar levels and prevent cancer.

Mustard Makes Complexion Rosy

Professor Yang Li from the Chinese Academy of Chinese Medical Sciences told reporters that mustard has a strong spicy flavor and a strong stimulating effect. It can regulate female endocrine, enhance sexual function, stimulate vasodilation, improve blood circulation in the face, and make women's complexions rosier.

Professor Li Huiming said that the main component causing the pungent smell of mustard is isothiocyanate. This component not only prevents cavities but also has certain effects on preventing cancer, preventing vascular plaque deposition, and assisting in asthma treatment. Additionally, mustard has the effects of preventing high blood lipids, high blood pressure, coronary heart disease, and reducing blood viscosity.

Toona Sinensis Can Aid in Conception

Studies have shown that toona sinensis contains vitamin E and sex hormones, which have anti-aging and tonifying yang effects and can be effective in treating infertility, hence its nickname as "conception aid."

Professor Li Huiming introduced that toona sinensis contains volatile aromatic organic compounds such as toosendanin, which can strengthen the spleen and stomach, increase appetite. It has the effects of clearing heat and dampness, promoting urination, and detoxifying, making it a good auxiliary treatment for enteritis, dysentery, and urinary system infections.

The nutritionists from Guilin Nutrition Society wish all women beautiful days, happiness, and health every day.
