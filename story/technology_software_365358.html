Lao Du released Pole Wubi version 7.13 (Standard edition/Learning edition) today, nearly five months after the release of version 7.12 on 2010-4-29.

This version mainly fixed two issues:

Fixed: Illegal entry of 4 codes after prolonged use of poles

Fixed: Switch out pole only out of English, can not input Chinese characters

Attached by Lao Du:

I wanted to make this version a stable version of 7.1X, but due to the limitations of the test environment, energy and other aspects, this version still has known problems: when the pole is opened, ICBC U shield can not log in normally; Some games, such as Need for Speed 12, may have problems when the pole is set to the default input method. Therefore, please decide whether to choose the pole according to your situation.

Compared with the 7.12 version of the setting window, there are two obvious differences: one is to increase the [common/all] setting item display switch, convenient for novices and toss lovers (the following May 29 improvement), the second is the egg is still, but the photo of Xiao Du changed.
