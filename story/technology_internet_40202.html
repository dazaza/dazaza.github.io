Business opportunities are everywhere.

by impark on 2007-12-23 18:36:25

It's been a long time since I last visited an electronics market, mainly because there was nothing to buy. Today, I went to the Olympic Electronics City because I wanted to buy something and was lucky enough to finally see the iPod Touch and iPhone. After buying what I needed at the electronics city, I took a random stroll and arrived at the Apple store, where I found the legendary iPod Touch. So, I pretended to be interested in buying one and took a look; it was indeed very cool. The floating screen was quite beautiful, but unfortunately, there was no wireless network available then, so I couldn't test its internet speed. In Dalian, the 8GB version is sold for 2600 yuan, which is a bit expensive. After circling around and planning to go home, I saw another Apple store. I casually asked if they had the iPhone, and they said they did have stock. I requested to see the real item, an unjailbroken 8GB iPhone, and they quoted over 5000 yuan. Expensive! The saleswoman told me that they imported it from Shenzhen, with middlemen involved multiple times, so it was bound to be pricey. She even suggested that I ask a friend to bring one back from the US, which would be much cheaper. These are smart merchants. They said that the latest version of jailbreaking costs between 300~600 yuan, which is still cheaper than buying domestically. They can also do the jailbreaking, and she even gave me her business card, mentioning that her boyfriend is the one who does the jailbreaking. Talking about all this, I just want to express that whether it’s the good attitude of this saleswoman that brings business opportunities, or if I were to start such a business myself, opportunities are everywhere. I particularly admire江南春from Focus Media, who started doing advertising business in university and now is the boss of several advertising companies, having even acquired iResearch. Every time I see Focus Media's TV ads at the entrances of office buildings, I admire his abilities and diligence.
