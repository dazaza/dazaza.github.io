Solid wood stairs are the kind with the largest market share at present.

by lovea6o4 on 2012-02-24 15:06:12

The styles of stairs on the market today come in all shapes and sizes, with materials constantly innovating and changing in countless ways. Chasing after the latest trends when one is dazzled by so many choices is unnecessary; instead, finding a stair that suits oneself is the most practical approach. Solid wood stairs have the largest market share at present. Whether it's a Chinese-style or Western-style home, Ningbo stairs can be seamlessly matched. During the production process of solid wood stairs, there are multiple quality checks at every stage, from conceptual design, precise drawing, material selection, surface treatment, to oil processing, color correction, and final comprehensive inspection. Each step embodies an unwavering pursuit of product quality, providing customers with the most complete assurance through exquisite craftsmanship. Installing solid wood stairs can make efficient use of space and cleverly choose decorations. Besides connecting upper and lower spaces, they can also produce the best decorative art effect for the home, satisfying functional needs while offering aesthetic enjoyment. When choosing solid wood stairs, attention should not only be paid to the product itself but also to the after-sales service provided by the merchant. The most important aspect of the merchant's service initially is the installation of the solid wood stairs. When selecting solid wood stairs, one must consider the method of installation. Quick and convenient installation is better for home renovation as it minimizes noise and dust during the process. Wooden furniture in China has a long history, with many well-preserved halls being wooden structures. History has proven that solid wood products last the longest, especially original wood stairs, which are the most environmentally friendly and beneficial to human life. Before production, all dimensions of solid wood stairs are precisely measured, minimizing noise and dust pollution during installation compared to other types of stairs. In terms of material, Ningbo stairs have the most significant sound-dampening effect. The sound produced when stepping on them is quieter than steel or cement stairs. For safety, the tread of solid wood stairs should undergo rounded corner treatment to avoid foot injuries. In this regard, solid wood stairs have an absolute advantage over steel stairs, which are more dangerous due to their hardness and sharp edges.
