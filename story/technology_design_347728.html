However, Yang Wei's fighting spirit seemed even more tenacious than when he was tutoring. Every new school year, he would repeat the same story. Seven or eight years passed, and the student who had once broken into a girl's dormitory at night had already married that girl and could legally touch any part of her body day and night. But Yang Wei still did not know what it felt like to be with a woman. The teachers at Shenqi Middle School had already gotten used to seeing him every October drunk on five-yuan-a-bottle cheap liquor.

The principal at the time, seeing this skilled teacher unable to resolve his personal issues, finally couldn't help but have a long talk with him. He directly told him that men must sometimes act like beasts. The principal even admitted that his wife only agreed to establish their relationship due to her own infidelity after drinking, proving the importance and necessity of "fait accompli." The principal also reminded Yang Wei that he was no longer young, and if he didn't seize the opportunity, he might end up being single for life.

This conversation enlightened Yang Wei, and he decided to go all out in the new semester. However, what he hadn't expected was that none of the four newly assigned teachers were women, which made Yang Wei feel a sense of frustration akin to "dying before success." Therefore, he never had a good impression of this group of teachers, especially Chen Yiyang, who already had a girlfriend, making Yang Wei grind his teeth in hatred.

From the second year onward, the assignment of college students stopped, and most of the new teachers were older ones, either married or with boyfriends. That golden age of blooming youth was gone forever. In despair, Yang Wei's aversion and hatred towards people like Chen Yiyang increased.

A few years later, Yang Wei became the head of the teaching department. From then on, these four individuals, including Chen Yiyang, would annually be given "important" tasks by the school. At first, they couldn't understand why Director Yang always seemed to pick on them, but later they found out it was because they indirectly hindered Director Yang from finding a wife. Later, Yang Wei married a restaurant waitress through an introduction and began living a happy life where he could enjoy physical intimacy daily. But the workload of Chen Yiyang and others didn't decrease because of this, as Shenqi Middle School was already short-staffed.

After two days of political lectures, it was time for student registration. Since Chen Yiyang wasn't a head teacher, there was nothing for him to do. When he got home, it was dinner time. Sitting down, he picked up his chopsticks and started eating voraciously. As he ate, he sensed something was amiss. Everyone was silent, as if something had happened, especially his father, whose face was dark, as if he had fallen into the clutches of the traffic police again.

"What's wrong with you guys? Why is everyone so quiet? Dad, did the traffic cops bother you again today?" Seeing that no one was speaking, Chen Yiyang broke the silence.

Chen Buyu glared at his son and shouted, "Isn't it because of you, you beast, making me worry?"

"What did I do?" Chen Yiyang was confused.

"Where did you go a few days ago?"

"I went to a friend's house in the county to play for a few days. Didn't I tell you?"

Chen Buyu slammed the table hard, making the bowls and chopsticks jump, just like Chen Yiyang's heart.
