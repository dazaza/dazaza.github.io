This student has now returned to Changchun and is preparing for his wedding next year. I still remember the new residence in the Zhonghai community with the Dui Palace as the entrance, and its flying star pattern was 1, 1, 6, a precious metal-water configuration; the combination of 1 and 6 in the same palace represents the mutual generation of metal and water, symbolizing literary success and celebrations; within a year after the family moved in, they quickly found good fortune in marriage, truly bringing joyous peach blossoms through the comings and goings.
