If you meet a man like this, you can marry him.

by shfengyi on 2007-06-02 19:47:47

A little shy, but once on the street, loudly said "I love you."

Went to the temple with you to draw lots, gently held your hand and knelt down together.

Kept his promises.

Never late — if you were late, he wouldn't get angry.

Hugged for a long time, very tightly — every time you got up, you almost had to slowly push him away.

Went to bed a little later than you, woke up a little earlier.

Muttered your name when waking up groggily — never got it wrong.

Remembered your birthday, shoe size, password, and what scared you the most.

You were afraid of bugs, and when you screamed upon seeing them, he wouldn't laugh at you.

In the morning after the rain, when you went to the garden and used a small branch to return the earthworms crawling onto the road back to the grass — he helped you.

Laughed like a bad guy — but he wasn't.

When you weren't feeling well, he asked for leave, took you to see a doctor, and bought ice cream as a reward on the way back.

Never drank alcohol before driving, made sure you wore your seatbelt.

Helped with housework every day, chatting while doing it.

Often helped others without any reason.

Promised you forever not, then never did.

Whistled while fixing the toilet.

Said: "I hope you could be my daughter."

Carried you through puddles during a walk in the rain, saying: "You can gain some more weight, you know."

Wouldn't just walk away when arguing.

Admitted his mistakes when he was wrong.

Read the instructions for the lady's hair removal device and taught you how to use it.

Laughed at your jokes.
