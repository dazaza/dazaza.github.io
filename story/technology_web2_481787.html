Qingdao Municipal Agricultural Machinery Bureau: Organizing personnel to observe the construction achievements of the regional agricultural machinery maintenance center in Laixi City -

by maling29 on 2011-08-26 09:53:46

On May 11, Mr. Xu Wei, Deputy Director of the Qingdao Municipal Agricultural Machinery Bureau, and a group of more than 30 people including the directors of various district and city supervision stations and repair stations, conducted an observation of the construction status of the regional agricultural machinery repair center in Laixi City. They listened to reports on the condition of Laixi's agricultural machinery repair work and visited the repair workshops, equipment, and facilities at the Laixi Yuanxiang Agricultural Machinery Repair Center. The group expressed admiration for our city's approach of relying on the three-package service network of Foton Lovol Heavy Industries to create a regional agricultural machinery repair service center with broad coverage and strong radiation and driving capabilities. They also affirmed the achievements made by our city.

In recent years, under the attention and policy promotion of the state towards agriculture, rural areas, and farmers (the "three rurals"), the mechanization of agriculture in our city has developed rapidly. A large number of high-performance and high-quality agricultural machinery products have entered our city. However, the existing agricultural machinery repair service outlets are far from meeting the needs of agricultural machinery users. To address issues such as the scattered, disordered, and substandard nature of current agricultural machinery repair service outlets, rudimentary equipment, weak technical strength, and difficulty in ensuring repair quality, and to truly ensure that agricultural machinery owners no longer worry about repairs, the Laixi Agricultural Machinery Bureau took the opportunity to implement the "Opinions of the State Council on Promoting the Sound and Rapid Development of Agricultural Mechanization and Agricultural Machinery Industry." In combination with our city's actual situation, they meticulously planned and scientifically laid out investments of 1.5 million yuan to support the construction of one regional agricultural machinery repair center in each of the southern half, central city, and northern half of the city.

Among these centers, a first-class standard agricultural machinery repair service center (agricultural machinery 4S store) was built in the central city with Haotu Agricultural Machinery Company as the leader. In the southern part of the city, the Yuanshang Town, and in the northern part, the Malianzhuang Town, two second-class standard regional agricultural machinery repair service centers were constructed based on the three-package service stations of agricultural machinery manufacturers. On this basis, each town and street will establish a third-class standard agricultural machinery repair service center, initially forming a municipal-level leading, township-level main body agricultural machinery repair service network covering the entire city to solve the worries of agricultural machinery users. Currently, the Yuanxiang Agricultural Machinery Repair Service Center has been completed and is about to be put into use. This regional repair service center is built around the Foton Agricultural Machinery three-package service station, with repair services radiating to six surrounding towns in the city and cities like Jiaozhou and Jiaonan. (Meng Fanjun)
