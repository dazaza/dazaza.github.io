Reflections on the Local Promotion and Publicity of Foshan Ceramics

by dknfkkgfed1 on 2012-02-09 08:46:48

Reflections on the Local Promotion of Foshan Ceramics - Building Materials Knowledge - Industry News - Building Materials Network. In China, when it comes to ceramics, people think of Foshan, and Foshan ceramics has become a bright calling card for Foshan. The birthplace of Foshan ceramics is the Nanfeng Ancient Kiln in Shiwan, Foshan.

The past of Foshan ceramics was glorious, largely due to effective promotion. By stepping out of its home, looking across the country, and even going global, Foshan ceramics has grown and developed significantly. It could be said that Foshan ceramics occupies half of the national ceramics market.

In recent years, Foshan ceramics brands have extended into inland areas such as East China, North China, and even Northeast China. On the other hand, with adjustments in production capacity layout, Foshan's ceramic production bases have also moved inland, such as Qingyuan in Guangdong, Ga'an in Jiangxi, Yueyang in Hunan, Dangyang in Hubei, and even Sichuan and Shandong provinces, where the presence of Foshan ceramic factories can be seen. The number of existing ceramic enterprises in the towns of Shiwan and Nanzhuang in Foshan has decreased from over 300 to just 20-30 now. Currently, only marketing centers remain in Huaxia Ceramic City, China Ceramic City, and the ceramic industry base.

Recently, I had the honor of being invited to Foshan Radio's 2010 cultural and sports activities promotional event. There were not many people from the ceramic and sanitary ware industries present: Romalio, New Century Source, Dongpeng Sanitary Ware, and a few others. During the event, I discussed this issue with some colleagues: Is it necessary for Foshan ceramics to promote itself locally? I have thought deeply about this question. As one person put it, Foshan ceramics has already taken root in people's hearts, so there's no need for further promotion. I agree with this statement to some extent but reserve judgment on the rest. While it's true that Foshan ceramics has become ingrained in people's minds—just walking around these ceramic cities allows you to buy what you need—it pertains more to Foshan ceramics as a whole. However, whether or not to promote depends on individual enterprise brands. I believe that local ceramic brands in Foshan should continue promoting themselves, both in the past, present, and future.

As a radio station staff member mentioned, during an event organized by Foshan Radio in 2009, a Foshan-based antique tile brand sponsored the event, resulting in a retail increase of over 70% in Foshan and its surrounding areas that year. The reason lies in the process of upgrading the brand. With numerous outstanding Foshan ceramic brands, consumers often feel overwhelmed. Consumers need guidance in their purchasing decisions, and sometimes, it’s just a matter of one simple suggestion. Another overlooked issue is that the number of newcomers settling in Foshan has been increasing annually, and they may have limited knowledge of Foshan ceramics. Therefore, promoting and advertising in this area is crucial.

In fact, Foshan ceramics is a calling card for Foshan, and promoting Foshan ceramic brands is essential both domestically and internationally, especially within Foshan itself. Doing a good job of promoting locally will inevitably radiate nationwide. Therefore, promoting Foshan ceramic brands is necessary in the past, present, and future, and it is an issue that deserves deep reflection from the CEOs of Foshan ceramic brands.
