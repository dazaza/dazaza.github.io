Taobao Section

For beginners, many friends who want to open an online store are not studying e-commerce, nor do they have any computer technology foundation, and some may not even have a high level of education. But can these things stop us from opening an online store and block our path to success? The following process is a foolproof Taobao store opening tutorial that you can definitely learn step by step.

Why is online store opening so attractive?

Nowadays, opening an online store is not just about entrepreneurship; many people use it as a way to expand their capabilities and for leisure. What exactly is the charm of online store opening that attracts everyone? Summarized, there are mainly the following points:

1. Zero cost, low risk

Compared to traditional stores, online stores don't require renting storefronts, paying taxes, or electricity/water fees. Only minimal product listing fees and transaction fees are charged, and sometimes even those are free. Moreover, you can order goods according to demand without worrying about stockpiling.

2. Flexible operating methods

Not restricted by traditional business hours or locations, operators can run the store full-time or part-time without spending a lot of time watching the store.

3. Wide consumer base:

Because online stores are on the internet, they face all possible consumers or netizens who might see the products, which can be nationwide or even global.

How exactly do you open an online store?

Online stores offer a wide variety of items such as cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each project has great development potential and prospects. The key is how to go about it and what suits you best. This is most important. Below, I will teach you step by step how to open an online store.

One: What to sell online?

Just like traditional stores, the first step in opening an online store is to consider what to sell. The choice of products should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, and also the product attributes and consumer demands.

I enjoy watching Korean dramas and because Korean-style women's clothing has been quite popular among Chinese women in recent years, naturally, I started doing business in Korean-style women's clothing.

Two: Preparations before opening the store

After choosing the products to sell, before opening the online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, you also need to undergo corresponding identity and payment method verification.

The reason I chose to open a store on Taobao is because it's completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass certification; two, post 10 or more (including 10) items; three, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to Taobao and click on "Free Registration" at the top of the page. On the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., and click on the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation message. Open the link inside and confirm to complete user registration.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction security, note that the password should not be set too simply. It's recommended to use a combination password of "English letters + numbers + symbols."

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, you also need to undergo corresponding verification (including two processes: real-name verification and Alipay verification). Specific operation steps are as follows:

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click on "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click on "Please click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, undergo Alipay verification."

Step Two: On the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated yet. Click on "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click on the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step Three: After successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page, press the F5 key to refresh the page. Click on the "Apply for Alipay personal real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, and then click on the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step Four: First, fill in personal information according to the prompts and click on "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Upload online" or "Mail" copies of your ID, and click on "Next"; then, input bank card information including the bank branch, bank card number, province, city, etc. After completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Reminder: If uploading ID copies online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide the back picture.

Step Five: After one day, re-open "My Taobao," click on the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click on "Confirm the amount transferred," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click on the "Confirm" button.

3. Purchase goods, take pictures

A key factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the purchasing channel. For the same item, different purchasing channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To display the items for sale more intuitively in front of consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools for image format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Post Items

To open a store online, besides meeting the membership certification conditions, you also need to post more than 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you need to start posting your first item.

Friendly Reminder: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but they can only be posted to "Items in the Warehouse," and buyers cannot see them. Only after passing verification can they be listed for sale.

Step One: Log in to Taobao, click on "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. On the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" posting methods. Here, click on "Fixed Price."

Friendly Reminder: "Fixed Price" has a set price, and buyers can purchase immediately; "Auction" starts without a bottom price, allowing buyers to bid.

Step Two: Choose the category, select the appropriate category based on your item. For example, I chose the details of women's shoes. Click on the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step Three: Fill in item information, this step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" section, create a good title, click on the "Browse" button to upload the item image, input the item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; next, in the "Transaction Conditions" section, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc. Other information can remain at default settings, such as using Alipay by default. Finally, click on the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to post the item.

If posted successfully, a success page will appear below. Click "Here" to view the posted item page, and click "Continue Posting Items" to post more items.

Friendly Reminder: Before buyers place bids, if you need to modify the posted item information, you can edit and modify it under "My Taobao - I am Seller - Items on Sale."

After posting items, it's best to regularly update and add them to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Three: Obtain a Free Store

Taobao offers certified members the opportunity to open a free store. As long as you post more than 10 items, you can own a store with your own independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and arrange them according to your style.

To make my store open sooner, I initially posted 10 items in one breath, spending nearly half a day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Next, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes My Show"; in the "Store Category," I could choose the item category; in the "Store Introduction," I input the store's brief introduction content. Finally, clicking on the "Submit" button, a page appeared saying "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." and provided the store's address.

Four: Store Decoration is Important

After free store opening, buyers can obtain their own space. Just like traditional stores, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decoration," mainly including store logo design, item classification, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am Seller - Manage My Store." In the "Store Management" on the left side, click on "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; the main business items need to be manually entered; in the "Logo" section, click on the "Browse" button to choose the pre-designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" section, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Clicking on the "Preview" button allows you to view the effect.

2. Item Classification

Classifying items makes it easier for buyers to search. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click on "Item Classification" on the left side. Next, input the new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), then click on the "Confirm" button to add. Clicking on the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification allows you to add posted items through keyword searches for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" feature provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best 6 items, showcasing them prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Store" page, click on "Recommended Items" on the left side, then choose the items to recommend in the opened page, and click on the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items, giving buyers different feelings. Generally, choosing a light-colored and comfortable style suffices. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, and the preview appears on the right side. Clicking on the "Confirm" button applies this style.

After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears in front of you.

Five: Promotion is the Key to Success

The online store is open, items are listed, and there are unique features, but after several weeks, there are still no transactions, not even buyer comments—this is a common problem many novice sellers encounter. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to advertise my store through forum promotion, link exchange, showcase recommendations, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting ads and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums in various provinces or large cities. If there's a section allowing ad postings, you can post ads with detailed content, beautiful item pictures, and keep regular updates and pinning.

The latter involves changing your signature line on the forum to include your store's URL, logo, slogan, and store name. Posting some beautiful threads allows interested friends to visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening the store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus promoting your store for free. For instance, Taobao offers up to 35 friendly links. Adding methods are simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all stores and record the names of popular store owners.

Then, log in and download Taobao's buyer-seller communication tool—"Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and propose a link exchange request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao—I am Seller—Manage My Store," click on "Friendly Links" on the left side, input the store owner name, and click on the "Add" button.

3. Showcase Recommendation

The "Showcase Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers choose to search or click on "I Want to Buy" and search by category, the showcase-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set up the "Showcase Recommendation" feature, you can open "My Taobao—I am Seller—Items on Sale," choose the items to recommend to the showcase (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be showcased again, i.e., marked with "Recommend"), and click on the "Showcase Recommendation" button.

Six: After Selling Items

After selling items, besides receiving corresponding sales reminders, you need to actively contact the buyer, requesting payment for the goods, shipping, and evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases items, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang" or email. Sellers can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao—I am Seller—Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click on "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer through "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also record the buyer's ID and send internal messages.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties communicate, agree on payment methods and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both buyers and sellers, it's recommended to use Alipay for payment.

3. Payment, Shipping

To prevent situations where the goods arrive but the payment isn't made, sellers generally adopt a "payment received before shipping" method.

First, require the buyer to pay, usually through Alipay. After the buyer pays, the seller can open "My Taobao—I am Seller—Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and at this point, the seller can confidently ship the goods to the buyer.

Friendly Reminder: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao—Alipay Zone—Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction inquiries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and finally, Taobao transfers the payment to the seller's Alipay account. Thus, the transaction is completed. Don't forget to maintain contact with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation, Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluations. Sellers can open "My Taobao—I am Seller—Sold Items," click on "Evaluate" for sold items, and choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual circumstances, with the ability to input textual content.

Friendly Reminder: How to become a Taobao seller with relatively high credit ratings?

To become a star-rated Taobao seller, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction. A "positive review" adds one point, a "neutral review" adds no points, and a "negative review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star-rated seller, remember to provide honest service. If there are cases of online transactions not being completed, refusal to pay upon receipt of goods, etc., sellers can open "My Taobao—Credit Management—I Want to Report" to file complaints and reports, requiring collection of evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer signatures, and screenshots of Wangwang conversations.

Editor's Note: Although online store openings are zero-cost and low-risk, examples of failed transactions leading to "store closures" are also numerous. To ensure the survival of your small store online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the hearts of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, ensuring a steady flow of wealth.

Blog Section

It is commonly believed that popularity is crucial for selling things on blogs. The browsing volume of online stores centers around products, whereas blog browsing volume forms around the blogger's fame and written content. Generally speaking, the latter's browsing volume is much larger. For instance, Xu Jinglei's blog has tens of millions of views, which is hard to imagine for an online store.

Many bloggers have established fixed readerships, varying in number but painstakingly built through article by article. This group often shares consistent ideas, concepts, emotions, and styles. Love extends beyond the blogger's preferences, making the blogger's favorite products often favored by fans. Products upgraded by the blogger also frequently catch the attention of fans.

The power of blog writing effectively helps products form impact. Product descriptions in stores, no matter how good the content, are seen as product specifications. However, in blogs, product descriptions are considered literature. Bloggers fully display their personalities and expertise in describing products, going beyond direct descriptions. They often write novels, essays, or poems in the form of mood stories, resonating with appreciators. Blogs also become a new means for bloggers and friends to establish emotional connections through product sales.

Moreover, the interactivity of blogs is more pronounced than online stores. Different perspectives on a product lead to discussions and hot topics on blogs, and regardless of whether there are more praises or criticisms, hot products always lead to high sales. I once saw a blog selling Western pastry-making tools, filled with responses to Western pastry-making consultations, introductions to Western dining etiquette, import cream group-buying news, and club activity announcements. The more guidance provided by the blogger, the more potential customers looking for this seller. In such a blog, what you see is not a store atmosphere but rather a club where friends integrate their passion, interest, wisdom, and needs.

The tagging function of blogs is better than ordinary online stores, not only highlighting keywords in products but also helping products appear in network search systems through many keywords in articles. Blog links also expand the influence of products. Friends access more people through links.
