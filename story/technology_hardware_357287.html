Frequently Asked Questions

by kai48nocp on 2010-06-01 11:23:46

1. What are the conditions for joining the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program? Our company's YBC (Youth Business International) support project, while adhering to the YBC motto: "Helping Young People to Help Themselves" (Support a youth, achieve a future entrepreneur)! However, success depends on people. To become a member of the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program, you need to have the passion for entrepreneurship and be willing to work hard for your own business!

2. Does joining the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program require a fee? Yumingzhai does not charge an agency fee. As long as you make an effort, the 598 yuan you pay will be returned to you after one year!

3. Since there is no agency fee, why do I still need to pay 598 yuan? Collecting 598 yuan is not our ultimate goal. Online shopping is very convenient nowadays, and many people want to set up a shopping website just for fun, putting products online without caring whether they sell or not. This would lower the product price and cause headaches for those who really want to specialize in shopping websites. Now we collect 598 yuan, but for those young entrepreneurs who truly make efforts, we will return it after one year. This is mainly to raise the threshold of entrepreneurship, filter out these people, and let those who truly want to join the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program friends be in a very pure and fair entrepreneurial environment.

4. After one year, how much will be returned? Joining the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program requires effort. Following the methods taught by Yumingzhai, if you achieve annual tea sales below 48,888 yuan, 198 yuan can be refunded; if you achieve annual tea sales between 48,888 yuan and 88,888 yuan, 398 yuan can be refunded; if you achieve annual tea sales above 88,888 yuan, 598 yuan can be refunded!

5. How are your commissions calculated? For monthly tea sales below 15,000 yuan, the commission is 30%; for sales exceeding 15,000 yuan, the commission is 35%, which is 5,250 yuan; for sales exceeding 20,000 yuan, the commission is 40%, which is 8,000 yuan. And for each additional agent added, you can earn 100 yuan in commission.

6. Besides earning profits from tea sales, do we have other ways to generate income? Yes, there is another way to help you earn more income. Promote your website according to the methods taught by Yumingzhai. If someone becomes a member through your website and eventually joins the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program, Yumingzhai will also give you 100 yuan as a reward, thanking you for discovering another future entrepreneur.

7. I've never been involved in online sales before. If I don't sell more than 88,888 yuan after one year, won't I have wasted the 598 yuan? Actually, you can rest assured. We have two measures that can truly let you experience the feeling of being a boss. a) As long as you become a member of the Yumingzhai Youth Entrepreneurship Program, we will provide you with a set of materials that introduce the latest online promotion techniques in detail. As long as you study carefully and practice, selling tea will be easy. Even if you don't plan to engage in tea sales in the future and want to sell your own products, the promotion knowledge you have learned can easily make you a standout in the products you sell. We will also update this material according to the changes on the Internet and notify you. b) Yumingzhai will invest in advertising on various well-known websites for the main site. At that time, we will regularly change links in the "Online Mall" and "Internet Entrepreneurship Program" sections of the main site. The main site neither sells tea nor allows membership registration. When customers click on the above two sections, they will automatically jump to any young entrepreneur's website.
