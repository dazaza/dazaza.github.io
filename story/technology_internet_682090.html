The fabric market for home fashion curtains welcomes a price increase trend in the "pleasant" month of March.

by bery2012045 on 2012-02-28 17:20:39

Speaking of price increases, industries like fashionable home decoration [http://www.beryhome.com/] and curtain fabrics [http://www.beryhome.com/] are greatly influenced by market conditions, and several objective factors must be mentioned. Data shows that in 2011, the overall level of national resident consumption prices increased by 5.4% compared to the previous year, among which the price of construction and renovation materials increased by 2.4%. Although home building materials do not have as large price fluctuations as agricultural products and other daily necessities, on the basis of various increasing rigid costs, the slow price adjustment of fashionable home curtains and fabric markets has become an undeniable fact.

Recently, after visiting some fashionable home curtain fabric malls and home decoration companies, I found that although the post-holiday sales peak has not yet arrived, many enterprises and brands are already preparing for the upcoming March home consumption peak season in various ways. How to set prices is also a key issue they need to consider.

Carpenters earning 400 yuan per day, rigid costs drive price increases

At a certain furniture mall, I met the sales supervisor of a furniture brand, who told me that the industry price increase did not actually start this year, and the company was also making plans to adjust prices in the new year. He said: "From the current situation, a slight price adjustment is almost inevitable, but the extent of the increase and the pricing method are still under discussion within the company." Regarding the topic of price increases, merchants seem relatively cautious; in the increasingly competitive home market, some merchants dare not make rash moves in pricing in order to maintain their existing market share.

However, from a long-term perspective, the influence of objective factors is inevitable. Taking the furniture industry as an example, the prices of raw materials such as wood and hardware, as well as labor, energy consumption, logistics, and other costs, are continuously rising. Data shows that in the past two years, the overall price increase of domestic wood raw materials has reached over 10%. The prices of Lushuihe panels, imported Egger panels, and Odeson panels have been adjusted several times, with each increase around 10%. Enterprises need to bear the enormous cost pressures brought about by these changes, so the prices of home building materials should rise in 2012. Meanwhile, since 2005, the labor fees of renovation workers have been increasing at a rate of 20% annually. Now, the daily wage of a skilled mason averages around 150 yuan, with some reaching more than 200 yuan. It's rumored that carpenter wages can reach up to 400 yuan/day.

March price increases are customary; seize the price advantage and act calmly

Therefore, based on past market experiences, the home renovation market usually sees about two price increases in a new year. One is expected to be a 10-15% overall increase at the beginning of the year, while another might be a 5% minor adjustment mid-year. Additionally, in the new year, due to designers gaining more experience, the design fees of the same designer may increase correspondingly because of their rank promotion. Therefore, the increase in home renovation market costs can be anticipated. Industry experts analyzed that the next round of price increases in the home industry will form in February or March after the Spring Festival, with an estimated furniture price increase of 10%-20%, and a home renovation price increase of 5%-10%.

Network survey data shows that 75% of the surveyed netizens are not sensitive to price increases in the home industry, as this industry is not as closely related to people's daily lives as the food industry. When consumers have not truly delved into the market, they may not feel the impact of price factors. However, when I visited some consumers currently undergoing home renovations, they all generally realized this point. Some consumers told me that before starting home renovations, due to insufficient experience, they did not predict the price trend well, didn't know when to make a move, nor understood the market's increase amplitude. This left them facing already increased prices without enough original budget for their home renovations.

During my interview at a certain fashionable home mall, I met Mr. Chen, who was customizing sofa covers [http://www.berhome.com/]. He told me that his new house renovation was nearing completion, and he was mainly looking at furniture now. He said that although the home renovation investment had already exceeded the original budget, furniture affects future living comfort, so this expense cannot just be about saving money. Currently, he was only observing the overall market trend, comparing prices of different brands, hoping to seize the right moment to make a purchase. Subsequently, I interviewed some industry insiders, who indicated that China's home market, especially the furniture market, is a supply-exceeds-demand market structure, and it is difficult for price manipulation to occur. Only due to cost pressures does the upward trend exist. Therefore, during the time curve of overall price increases, consumers only need to choose the lower moments to make purchases, which won't cause too much financial burden.

In conclusion, price increases in home renovation, building materials, and furniture are inevitable, just with differences in specific increase amplitudes and timing. Based on past market experiences, price increases in home building materials around March have become an industry norm. Choosing this node to raise prices is because the home industry has a longer Spring Festival holiday every year, and the period before March is the off-season. At this time, raising prices is less likely to touch the consumer's price sensitivity. After the price adjustment enters March, traditional promotional seasons such as '3.15' make it easier to conduct promotional activities. The rise in building material prices will also trigger price increases in the home renovation market, and from March to April, the furniture industry may enter a price increase channel. Therefore, some industry insiders expressed that this is currently a favorable time for fashionable home consumption, and seizing the opportunity before the overall increase in the curtain fabric industry is undoubtedly a wise choice.
