Guanglian Times creates pan-media revenue sharing and wants to change the traditional marketing model

by zrq827871vxe on 2012-02-15 15:57:26

Since its establishment, the three systems have been launched successively. In 2007, Guanglian Times (formerly Jiuying Spacetime) was founded with a great dream. The difficulties of starting a business made this team experience all the pains that entrepreneurial teams must go through, and "坚持分账到底" became the driving force for the team to continue their entrepreneurship. Revenue-sharing marketing is a marketing model that has emerged to meet the needs of the times. It reduces the risk of advertising investment in traditional marketing models and also provides a solution for media to achieve continuous profitability. Just as Taobao can change people's shopping habits, Guanglian Times believes that pan-media revenue sharing can also change the marketing models of more and more enterprises and media.

As the number of customers continues to increase, customer demands become increasingly detailed. "Making changes according to market changes and customer needs is correct and inevitable." This statement has been proven true in the development and growth of Guanglian Times. Guanglian Times has successively developed three pan-media revenue-sharing system software products: BIZPROUD, BIZORDER, and BIZWINDOW based on market demand. These three software products target different types of customers and solve business problems in e-commerce operations through technical means.

Guanglian Times integrates revenue-sharing marketing into various industries. Guanglian Times' customers range from the health product industry to the clothing industry, from jewelry to wine. Each customer and even each industry has its operational characteristics, and the degree of e-commerce development and enterprise scale are different. Given these issues, to better serve customers, Guanglian Times has introduced three different systems: BIZPROUD, BIZORDER, and BIZWINDOW. We often hear about "revenue-sharing," an easily understood verb, but how does it operate between enterprises and media through technical means?

The BIZPROUD system runs on the company's own server and provides a revenue-sharing marketing system that integrates media order recognition technology at its core, along with an e-commerce online mall, sales process management, call center management, customer relationship management (CRM), and instant sales statistics functions.

The BIZORDER platform is an incubator for direct marketing companies, providing a one-stop solution for start-up direct marketing companies to implement media revenue-sharing marketing models.

The BIZWINDOW platform is aimed at enterprises with mature e-commerce platforms, call centers, and CRM systems. Without changing or affecting the enterprise's business processes, it realizes media identification and monitoring, thereby achieving advertising revenue-sharing cooperation between enterprises and media.

Of course, regardless of what kind of enterprise adopts which system, they are all participating in pan-media revenue-sharing marketing and enjoying a series of benefits brought by this new marketing model, such as resource sharing and continuous profitability. Encouraging more and more enterprises and media to participate in the revenue-sharing camp is the key to the success of revenue-sharing marketing. The systems developed by Guanglian Times allow various types of media and enterprises to participate in revenue-sharing with their most optimized resources. Whether it is a start-up enterprise or a mature e-commerce company, finding the right way to participate is the first step to success.

Guanglian Times helps enterprises achieve win-win results. The success of Guanglian Times does not lie in how large it is or how many employees it has, but in how many enterprises and media have achieved maximum benefits on the road of revenue-sharing marketing.

In traditional marketing models, we cannot see the specific effects after advertising placements, nor can we verify which media advertisements generated orders. Therefore, it is impossible to quickly and effectively adjust advertising or marketing strategies. However, based on the innovation of the pan-media revenue-sharing marketing model, the self-developed media revenue-sharing marketing system of Guanglian Times has enabled many enterprises to find breakthroughs in marketing, making every cent of advertising fees generate sales. It also solidifies their customer base, facilitates consumer management for enterprises, and enhances the service awareness of enterprises.
