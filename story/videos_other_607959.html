One of the reasons for the persistently high complaint rate in group buying is that

by beetgk2958 on 2011-12-07 10:47:55

However, in the process of group buying website adjusting city sub-stations, due to the lack of timely and effective communication between merchants and users, the situation that group buying coupons cannot be used happens from time to time, which makes group buying users quite disappointed. Netizen crystallyt bought a coupon for local cuisine on a website on October 28. When he was preparing to consume in the store, he found through media reports that the website had withdrawn its local sub-station and the merchant had terminated cooperation with the website. When he tried to contact the merchant and the website by phone, he couldn't get through. He believed that the behavior of the merchant and the website seriously violated the principle of honest operation. The delivery efficiency of online shopping premium products and product quality problems are one of the reasons for the high complaint rate of group buying; while group buying websites adjust the setting of city sub-stations, they should properly deal with user refund and other aftermath issues. According to the statistics of Tuan800 information channel, so far, a total of 288 complaints were received during the period of November 28-December 3, a week, a decrease of 7.1% over the previous period. Of these, 99 group-buying complaints have been resolved, with a resolution rate of 34.4%. Netizen chengyu0932 posted on Tuan that on November 3, he ordered 2 wallets on a group-buying website, but did not receive them until November 21. During the inspection process, he found that one of the wallets had obvious quality problems, such as scratches on the surface and malfunctioning zippers. However, after multiple calls to the website customer service, he was always evaded and delayed for various reasons, which he could not accept. In this case, it also exposed the efficiency of online shopping premium delivery and product quality problems of group-buying websites. Therefore, while some websites increase their investment in B2C malls, they must also ensure that product and service quality improve simultaneously. Due to the problems of merchant qualification review and logistics links, physical group-buying is still short of strength. The editor suggests that consumers carefully check the merchant qualifications and delivery efficiency of courier companies before placing an order, and choose well-known brands with good reputation and high credibility.
