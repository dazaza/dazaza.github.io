Chongqing Garden Expo Park determines fare for 80 electric sightseeing vehicles

According to the operation information, Chongqing Garden Expo Park covers an area of 3,300 mu (about 220 hectares), including an 800-mu Longjing Lake. To enable visitors to tour the park efficiently, during the eighth Garden Expo exhibition, more than 80 electric sightseeing vehicles and dozens of boats will be available for transportation.

This ticket is valid for all three electric sightseeing vehicle routes within the park, allowing passengers to board and alight freely at various stops along each route. After reaching a stop, the driver will punch a hole in the ticket of passengers who get off; after four holes have been punched, the ride is considered complete.

According to the previously announced passenger guidelines by the Garden Expo Park management office, there are three electric sightseeing vehicle routes set up (Main Entrance Lakeside Route, East Entrance Chinese Gardens Route, and West Entrance Route), with tickets priced at 10 yuan.

Today, a reporter from Huaxi Metropolis Daily visited the Garden Expo Park management office. It was learned that the fare for the electric sightseeing vehicles has been determined as 10 yuan per person for four trips; the preliminary price for the boat rides has been set but is awaiting approval from the relevant authorities. The final confirmed prices will be announced soon.

In addition to touring the park by vehicle, visitors can also choose water transportation. According to Yang Xiaojun, the sole operations manager of the Garden Expo Park's boats, after the opening, it is expected that ferry boats, speedboats, electric boats, and traditional-style barges will be put into service. A conservative estimate suggests that the capacity of the electric sightseeing vehicles could accommodate nearly a thousand people per hour.
