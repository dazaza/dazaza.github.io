Top Ten Dating Techniques

Bold Advance Method

Many girls have some degree of caution towards male friends. They always worry that they might accidentally, emotionally, stray off the path and regret it for life. In fact, this artificially narrows one's social circle.

After experiencing many ups and downs, many people have sighed that there are more good people in this world. With your life experience, you should still be able to quickly discern the merits and demerits of the people around you. If you want to make male friends, you shouldn't have an overly cautious mindset. You must first open your closed heart. The ancients once reminded us: "When you see fire, prevent fire; when you see people, prevent theft." Some people infer from this ancient teaching that "when you see the opposite sex, beware of hooligans." Having such a nerve-wracking notion in your mind will make you hesitant and think, "I can't possibly..." Then you will never have any friends of the opposite sex. Didn't the ancients also have the saying, "Without entering the tiger's den, how can you catch the cub?" which encourages people to be bold?

Semi-Hinting Method

The characteristic of the "semi-hinting method" is that although the suitor does not explicitly say "I love you," the courtship message is clearer than the "hinting style" and requires the recipient to respond relatively quickly.

For example, under the current situation where interactions between men and women in China are not entirely casual, specifically buying movie tickets, concert tickets, etc., and inviting the other person to go out together; inviting the other person to go for a walk or outing alone, generally implies courting the other person.

In life, when one party is infatuated with another but unsure whether the other party feels the same way, it is advisable to use this semi-hinting method. It can serve as a trial balloon and also express one's attachment to the other person first. A pair of intimate people who originally dare not declare their feelings can thus enter a relationship in a relaxed manner.

Emotional Harmony Method

Between lovers, their conversations are no longer just tools for conveying information. The content of what they say is not important to lovers. What is more important is the act of speaking itself, accompanied by the laughter and glances during their sweet talk. All of this gives the other person pleasure and joy.

This feature is precisely the special communication function of sweet talk. It values emotions rather than the meaning of words, it values the soul rather than language. For lovers, hearing the beautiful voice of the person they like echoing softly in their ears is itself a pleasure, a happiness.

Everyone likes people who agree with their opinions. When you tell the other person about your shared experiences or thoughts, the other person will naturally feel closer to you. Topics bring the two people closer together, showing emotional harmony.

Truth in Conversation Method

The art of conversation plays an important role in any setting, especially in romantic relationships. To understand each other, there must be more conversations. This is called the "truth in conversation" method.

The process of young people falling in love is sweet and poetic. Moreover, once young people fall in love, they often idealize and idolize their partner, thinking that he (she) is as brilliant as the sun and as perfect as the full moon. This is the "halo" effect that often appears during the process of falling in love.

However, dating and marriage are ultimately real-life situations. Marriages based on love at first sight and rushed weddings mostly end in failure. Only through mutual understanding during the dating process can one achieve a harmonious relationship and have a happy and fulfilling life.

Love at First Sight Method

Simply put, love at first sight is the immediate romance generated by a favorable impression based on intuition. During this "first meeting," what plays a decisive role is the image and impression of both parties, such as appearance, demeanor, and speech.

According to Greek mythology, men and women were originally one, and it was God who divided humanity into male and female. Later, everyone has their beautiful original form and searches for their counterpart in this vast world. This search is often completed by a strong impact of the heart. Thus, love at first sight often produces the strongest happiness.

Actually, the conversation of first love is a complex art. There is no "prescribed" way for conversations of love at first sight. It's hard to say what should be said, and people of different levels, professions, cultural backgrounds, and temperaments express themselves differently.

Letter Writing Method

Love letters are a way to exchange feelings and express admiration. One could say they are the "messengers of emotion" and "angels of love."

Although lovers may write long or short letters, not every love letter can communicate emotions, deepen understanding, or build a bridge of love. Those who aren't skilled at writing love letters may start writing without much thought, even filling pages with kisses, sometimes scaring away their beloved. Love letters are valuable for their sincere emotions and should avoid being flashy and insubstantial.

Love letters, meant to convey feelings, need to move the heart. "Nothing moves the heart more than emotion." Reading a novel once may suffice, but a love letter can be read countless times without tiring, its key being the genuine emotion within.

Expressing feelings requires subtlety and timing. The first "trial balloon" letter should be extremely implicit, considering the recipient's emotional capacity.

The fleeting youth leaves only hollow memories and regrets, but love letters remain a testament to enduring youth, a glorious page in your life history. Cherish your youth, save it for old age, and revisit it as leisure reading. That flavor will surely bring you supreme sweetness.

Object-Bearing Affection Method

If love is the most beautiful flower in the world, then the tender words exchanged between passionate young lovers are like dewdrops on the petals of this beautiful flower of love. The language of love, like the colorful and enchanting love lives of young people, is filled with infinite charm.

Indeed, tender words have charm and are inexhaustible. However, sometimes lovers feel a deep pain because they cannot find the right words to express their feelings for each other. They realize that compared to the rich emotions surging in their hearts, words seem too impoverished and limited.

At such moments, lovers seek another language, another way, to pour out their passions.

This method of expressing love is particularly suitable for introverted and shy lovers. Although they have feelings, they dare not openly express them. In such cases, they can think creatively and find an unusual way to convey their affection without saying passionate words like "I love you," while still allowing the other person to understand their intentions in a relaxed atmosphere. Why not embrace this enjoyable approach?

Affectionate Nickname Method

Nicknames are often conduits for emotional expression, and every couple hopes to hear terms of endearment or affectionate nicknames from their partner.

A simple nickname serves as a measure of interpersonal distance. The romantic relationship between members of the opposite sex is the most natural and intimate human relationship. The nicknames used by lovers reflect the subtle dynamics of their relationship.

First, changes in nicknames signify changes in the intensity of love. As young men and women progress from acquaintances to confidants and eventually to lovers, this natural progression can be seen not only from the hints in their glances but also from the evolution of their nicknames, revealing the secrets of their love.

Some young people fail to notice this. They shorten their partner's nickname over time. Initially calling someone "Comrade Wang Xiaoli," then "Wang Xiaoli" as they become familiar, "Xiaoli" as friends, and finally just "Li" after marriage. Such behavior can be uncomfortable and may affect the relationship.

"Empty-handedly Catching Mr."

Even though there are wolves everywhere and strong rivals surrounding, it's not easy for young ladies to get their ideal partner. Once a good target appears, competition becomes fierce, and simple tactics won't work.

First, control your eyes. Don't send signals recklessly when you see someone you fancy. Be careful not to go too far, ending up with unrequited love and damaging your reputation.

Next, observe his heart. Girls are usually very perceptive and can sense if a boy is good or bad.

Boys can be timid, and boys can be hurt. The phrase "boys should take the initiative" has caused many sisters trouble. Being a bit more proactive and confessing your feelings to him isn't a big deal. However: Avoid being too aggressive. Don't care too much about him. Caring too much takes away your composure; caring too much diminishes your confidence; caring too much makes you lose restraint. People are always like that – the more someone cares about them, the prouder they feel. Saying things like "I'll never marry anyone else" sounds unbelievable even to yourself. Boys chasing girls spend a lot of effort; girls chasing boys go through a lot of twists and turns. Once the barrier is broken, everyone is happy, but heartbreaks are unpredictable.

Praising Your Partner

"Praise" is very useful in romantic situations. A single compliment can make the person you admire develop feelings for you, turning secret love into public affection. If you're already dating, regularly praising your partner can significantly enhance your relationship – it's a great method.

However, praise should be skillful; otherwise, it might backfire. The worst is praising something the other person considers a flaw. For example, if the person is overweight and you say they have a good figure, or if their handwriting is poor and you say they write beautifully, they might think you're mocking them. So, think carefully before praising someone and base it on facts.
