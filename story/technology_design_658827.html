Disaster Sudden Fire Turns Huge Savings to Ashes

Si San said that none of the three brothers have started families yet, and they live together with their over 80-year-old mother in these three tiled rooms without a courtyard. Over these years, they earned 130,000 yuan from raising pigs and working, all of which was kept at home, http://www.zangling.cn/chi/, and never deposited in the bank. The mother lived in the west room, while Si San and his brothers lived in the east room, where this sum of money was placed inside a motorcycle toolbox.

Appraisal: A Box of Money Basically Becomes Worthless

"Hard-earned savings, just like that burned away, it's truly regrettable." The villagers all sighed in sorrow. Si San also cried with grief: "In the future, if we have more money, we shouldn't keep it at home; it must be saved."

"Pity, pity!" Upon seeing this box of burnt money, relevant personnel from the issuance department of the Zhoukou branch of the People's Bank of China expressed deep sympathy. After inspection, most of this money had become worthless, with very little that could be exchanged, http://www.zangling.cn/chi/, and appraising it would be quite difficult. The bank staff finally stated that they would try their best to help Si San verify and exchange the money, helping him recover some of the loss.

According to reports, in 2004, the People's Bank of China announced the 'Exchange Methods for Defective and Damaged Renminbi' stipulating: If the remaining face value is three-quarters (including three-quarters) or more and meets the specified characteristics, it can be fully exchanged; if the remaining face value is one-half (including one-half) to less than three-quarters and meets the specified characteristics, it can be exchanged at half value.
