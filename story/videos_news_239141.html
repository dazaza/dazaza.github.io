A 6-minute and 33-second video clip has recently caused a huge stir in Shunde. The male and female protagonists in the video look like high school students, and nearly 90% of the video's scenes are explicit sexual content. On the largest local forum in Shunde, there are over 100 pages of replies.

Incident: Student sex video appears on the internet

On December 12 at 11:24 AM, a netizen with the ID "Aide Jishi" posted a thread titled "Dongcun Video" on the Xintan section of the Shunde BBS, attaching a segment of a sex video. This video was online for more than a day until December 13 at 11:49 PM when "Aide Jishi" edited the post content, making the video unable to be linked.

From the video sent by the informant to our reporter, both the male and female protagonists appear to be around 16-17 years old. The female protagonist is also wearing a school uniform. During their encounter, the male protagonist adjusts the position and angle of the filming equipment multiple times. Judging from their accents, both young people are from Shunde.

With such a sensational video existing for over 24 hours, it triggered a frenzy of downloads among netizens. Some even conducted human flesh searches, claiming that the "male and female protagonists" are from Dongcun Village in Xintan. They attended Xianlian Middle School together during junior high school. The male protagonist surnamed Huang is the son of a teacher at Dongcun Primary School, living opposite the school, and he is currently not studying anymore. The female protagonist is a student at Xianlian Middle School and just entered high school this year.

The search by netizens sparked an even bigger storm, as the name of the school was revealed publicly. The implicated middle school carried out large-scale investigations in recent days. Eventually, the Shunde police also got involved in the investigation.

Yesterday, we learned from the police that the identities uncovered by netizens were not real. It is now confirmed that the two young people are not students from Xintan Town.

School: To seize the opportunity to carry out adolescent education

The principal of Xintan Xianlian Middle School admitted to our reporter yesterday that the pressure endured these past few days was "indescribable." Before clarifying the truth, Xianlian Middle School decided to conduct a thorough investigation first and let teachers educate the students accordingly.

Yesterday, officers from the Xintan Police Station of the Shunde Public Security Bureau notified Xianlian Middle School that they are investigating the identities of the young people in the video. It has been confirmed that the male and female protagonists in the video are neither current students nor graduates of Xianlian Middle School. After receiving the notification, the principal of the school said: "Although our school has cleared its name, this incident has already had very negative effects on the students. We will take this opportunity to carry out ideological education work for students in their adolescence."

Mr. Chen, the director of the Xintan Town Education Group, revealed that a town-wide school moral education meeting would be held today to educate all students in the town regarding this incident.

Meanwhile, a female netizen who claimed to be well-informed about the video incident stated yesterday that the male protagonist divided the video into upper and lower parts. "The lower part was originally also going to be uploaded to the internet. Now that it has caused a huge stir, he probably dares not do so."

Regarding the claim that the sex video has a sequel, the Shunde police said that they are investigating. If there is indeed a follow-up to the video, they will make every effort to intercept it to avoid further adverse effects. (Source: Guangzhou Daily, Reporter Zeng Yi)
