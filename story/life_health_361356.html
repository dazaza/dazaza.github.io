Health and Wellness - Sun exposure can help improve sleep quality. For insomnia caused by stress nowadays, experts point out that one should get more sun exposure. This is because there is a hormone in the human body that controls sleep - melatonin. Getting more sun exposure during the day aims to suppress the secretion of this hormone through light exposure. In the evening, it will be fully released to quickly enter a state of sleep.

Health and Wellness - Going to bed early helps protect liver health. Medical evidence shows that aside from genetic and infectious factors, most liver diseases are related to "staying up late." When you stay up late, the blood in your body supplies the brain, reducing the blood supply to internal organs, causing oxygen deficiency in the liver. Over time, this can damage the liver. The period between 11 PM and 3 AM is the optimal time for liver detoxification, so one should go to bed before 11 PM.

Health and Wellness - Liver disease patients eating bamboo shoots can prevent liver cancer. For hepatitis B, liver cirrhosis, and other liver disease patients, daily meals can often include lettuce, bamboo shoots, and asparagus. These three foods have significant liver-protecting and anti-cancer effects, with functions such as eliminating phlegm, promoting urination, detoxifying, and helping excrete waste from the body through feces.

Health and Wellness - Ten fingers can help you remove illness and strengthen your body. The ten fingers on a person's hands are actually ten acupoints in acupuncture, called well points. Exercising them has the effect of refreshing, awakening the mind, and opening the senses. So how should one exercise them? The method is actually very simple: just slap the palms of both hands together. That is, use the fingers of the left hand to slap the palm of the right hand, slapping both sides of the fingers once each; then do the opposite.

Health and Wellness - Taking medicine in the correct posture can better exert the efficacy of the drug. Taking medicine requires proper posture to better exert the efficacy of the drug and avoid adverse reactions. So how should one do it? 1. Bedridden patients should preferably take medicine in a sitting position, using 60 milliliters of warm water to swallow the medicine. 2. When taking medicine, a person should drink at least 100 milliliters of warm water and maintain a standing position for one and a half minutes to achieve good efficacy.

Health and Wellness - Women eating red meat can replenish iron and combat fatigue. Research proves that eating more red meat rich in heme iron is crucial for maintaining women's energy. Iron is an important raw material for producing hemoglobin and is responsible for oxygen transport and metabolism within the body. After women supplement with iron, their physical fitness, mood, and concentration improve. Therefore, women must pay special attention to the supply of iron in their regular diet.

Health and Wellness - If you are frequently exposed to smoke, you need to supplement more Vitamin C. If you often live in a smoky environment, don't forget to supplement Vitamin C! Because cigarettes contain high concentrations of reactive oxygen and free radicals, which can cause great harm to the lungs. Persistently taking Vitamin C can decompose nicotine toxins and effectively reduce the incidence of lung diseases.
