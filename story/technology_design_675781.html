If attacked by wasps, the best measure is to protect your head and neck with clothing, run in the opposite direction or lie down on the spot. Never try to drive them away with your hands, otherwise it will only lead to more attacks. If accidentally stung by wasps, first check if there is a sting left in the wound. It usually appears as a small lump at the swollen area, about 1-2 millimeters in size. You can use a needle or tweezers to remove it (do not squeeze the wound to avoid residual toxins entering the body). Then apply ammonia water, soda water or urine on the wound to neutralize the toxin and disinfect with iodine tincture. For severe pain, take painkillers and apply a cold-soaked towel on the wound to reduce swelling and pain. If the patient has difficulty breathing, hoarse voice, and wheezing, seek emergency medical treatment immediately.

At the time of the incident, villager Tang Shenglan (phonetic) was picking tea nearby when she suddenly heard her neighbor Qin Binrong's cries for help from a distance, so she quickly called her neighbor Qin Biping (phonetic) to rush over and provide assistance. According to Qin Biping's recollection, at that time, a swarm of wasps were fiercely attacking Qin Binrong and her son. Qin Binrong's body was covered with many wasps, and her head and body were full of stingers. However, Yao Yao's whole body was tightly wrapped in Qin Binrong's clothes, and there were no wasps on him. Tang Shenglan said that after being attacked by wasps, it was Qin Binrong who, in a moment of panic, took off her own clothes and covered her son Yao Yao tightly, which prevented Yao Yao from being stung by the wasps.

According to CCTV reports on October 17th, in Guanshang Village (phonetic), Suo Street Township, Shimen County, Changde, Qin Binrong (phonetic) and her one-year-old son Yao Yao were suddenly attacked by a swarm of wasps on the road. In a panic, this mother took off her own clothes and firmly wrapped her son up. Ultimately, her son was safe, but Qin Binrong died from the injuries caused by the wasp stings. Even before her death, she maintained the posture of hugging her child.
