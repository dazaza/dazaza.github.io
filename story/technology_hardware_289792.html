News Living Room: New Reporting Techniques

by bjjdwxgs on 2009-12-03 09:45:41

Note: Our channel's attentive audience for the "Two Sessions" coverage will certainly notice that this year, our news interview segment for the Two Sessions has changed a bit and become more innovative compared to previous years. Reporter Zhou Zhijun says, "Dear viewers, during the 08 Two Sessions reporting, our Yancheng Radio and Television Station News Channel not only aims to timely and accurately convey information about the Two Sessions, but also strives to deliver more information to citizens through innovative methods. At the scene of the Two Sessions, our innovation starts with the news interview segment."

Indeed, if you look at the recording site for the news interview segment, everything is fully prepared – lighting, sound systems, cameras – it looks like a small live broadcast studio. We have hosts, cameramen, and directors all present. People are adjusting the equipment, testing the sound system, preparing interview questions, and everyone is working in an orderly manner. For our innovations in Two Sessions reporting, the participating members of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) have the most say.

同期声：政协委员、盐城重啤集团总经理赵亚洲说：“以前都是单独会谈，现在通过一种创新的方式把三个人组合在一起，不同层次不同层面的人进行会谈。”

CPPCC member and General Manager of Yancheng Chongqing Beer Group, Zhao Yazhou, said: "Previously, it was always individual meetings, but now through an innovative approach, three people are combined together, with people from different levels and layers engaging in discussions."

政协委员、市科技局副局长邹毅实也表示：“克服了过去的那种呆板的单向交流，而是一种座谈式、互动式的交流，通过这种形式肯定会产生许多有效的火花，对我们新的一年的工作产生很大的启发。”

CPPCC member and Deputy Director of the Municipal Science and Technology Bureau, Zou Yishi, also stated: "It overcomes the past rigid one-way communication and adopts a seminar-style, interactive exchange. Through this format, it will surely generate many effective ideas and provide great inspiration for our work in the new year."

夏丽晖主持《交流》栏目见长，并多次主持新闻会客厅节目，对此感触尤深。

Host Xia Lihui, who is well-known for hosting the 'Exchange' program and has hosted the news interview segment multiple times, feels deeply about this.

主持人夏丽晖说：“我们邀请了好几位委员、代表一起来，共同来聊一个话题，这样我们在他们的交谈当中就可以发现更多的精彩的东西，还有他们的一些真实的见解和想法。这不仅是可以让我们为观众朋友奉献出更好的节目，更重要的是可以让大家通过我们的节目了解到我们身边的生活的方方面面，了解到我们盐城发展的点点滴滴，让我们更加热爱我们的盐城。”

Host Xia Lihui said: "We invited several committee members and representatives to come together to discuss a topic. In their conversations, we can discover more interesting things, as well as their genuine insights and ideas. This not only allows us to offer better programs for our audience, but more importantly, it enables everyone to understand various aspects of life around us through our programs, and to learn about every detail of Yancheng's development, making us love our Yancheng even more."
