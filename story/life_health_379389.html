Colorful Shrimp Paste

The best food for calcium supplementation for pregnant women is through diet! Nutritional commentary: Shrimp are nutrient-rich, containing proteins several to dozens of times higher than fish, eggs, and milk. Their calcium and magnesium content is also very abundant, and the ratio is optimal for human calcium absorption; shrimp contain vitamin D, the top seafood source, which promotes calcium absorption along with magnesium; they also contain potassium, selenium, and other trace elements and vitamin A. The meat is soft and easy to digest, benefiting the baby's brain development.

Ingredients:

100 grams of shrimp, 50 grams of tofu, 50 grams of cauliflower, half a carrot.

Steps to prepare:

1. Wash the tofu, press it into tofu paste.

2. Grind the shrimp into shrimp paste and set aside.

3. Wash the carrots, peel them, and cut them into small pieces.

4. Cook the cauliflower until tender.

5. Heat oil in a wok and stir-fry the carrots until fragrant.

6. Stir-fry the tofu paste and shrimp paste until half-cooked.

7. Continue to stir-fry the cauliflower until fully cooked.

8. Season with salt and continue to stir-fry until done.
