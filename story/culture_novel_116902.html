Novel Introduction

Tomb raiding is not like inviting guests for dinner, or writing an article, or painting embroidery, it can't be that elegant, that calm and collected, that polite and modest, tomb raiding is a technical skill, a technical skill for destruction. When ancient aristocrats built their tombs, they would certainly go to great lengths to prevent theft, so they used every trick in the book, setting up various traps and hidden weapons in the tomb, such as boulders, quicksand, poison arrows, venomous insects, pitfalls, etc., countless. By the Ming Dynasty, influenced by Western curiosities, some large tombs even used Western eight-treasure rotating heart mechanisms. Especially the imperial tombs of the Qing Dynasty, which could be considered masterpieces integrating thousands of years of anti-theft technology. Warlord Sun Dianying wanted to dig up the Eastern Tombs to use the treasures inside as military pay, mobilizing a large number of troops, using both digging and explosives, it took him five or six days to succeed, its sturdiness is imaginable. The subject of tomb raiders is to crack these traps by all means and enter the tomb to explore for treasure. However, in modern times, compared to how to dig up ancient tombs, finding them is more difficult. Tombs with obvious structures on the ground like earth mounds and stone steles have already been excavated almost completely. If you want to find those deeply buried underground tombs without any above-ground markers, then you need certain skills and special tools. Tools like iron stakes, Luoyang shovels, bamboo nails, drill dragons, probe yin claws, black folders, etc., were all developed accordingly. There are also some experts who don't rely on tools, some find clues in ancient documents to locate tombs, and a very few people master secret techniques, interpreting the pulse of mountains and rivers, using feng shui skills to find graves. I belong to this last category, during my tomb raiding career, I've traveled all over, experiencing many strange and mysterious events. If I were to tell them one by one, it would be enough to shock the viewers and make the listeners gasp. After all, those actions like dragon-shaped tiger hiding, revealing heaven and removing the earth, overturning the sea and turning over the river, are all extraordinary.

All these stories start from a damaged book left by my grandfather called "The Secret Techniques of Yin-Yang Feng Shui in Sixteen Characters". This damaged book, for unknown reasons, had its lower half torn off forcibly, leaving only the upper part about Feng Shui secrets.
