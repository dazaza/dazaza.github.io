JZ2 Trane air conditioning, starts heating as soon as it is turned on - Summary of experience

by m147mf81 on 2011-11-15 18:01:16

Fault phenomenon: The air conditioner starts heating immediately after being turned on.

Cause analysis: Upon checking, it was found that the machine started blowing hot air as soon as it was turned on. Since this is a newly installed unit, first check the wiring to ensure there are no mistakes. Suspecting an issue with the four-way valve, we tested the coil resistance of the four-way valve and found it to be normal with proper power supply. It was analyzed that the four-way valve might be stuck. For newly installed units, the possibility of a faulty four-way valve is low; more often, it's due to slight jamming. We tried repeatedly tapping the valve with a wooden stick to test the machine, but the fault persisted, confirming that the four-way valve was indeed faulty.

Repair measures: Replace the four-way valve.

Experience summary: When repairing the four-way valve, it is important not to replace it lightly. Slight jamming can often be fixed using simple physical methods. Many service centers use this physical method to repair four-way valves, especially for machines that have been used for a short time.
