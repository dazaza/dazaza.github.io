The era of diversified applications has arrived, and the internet is gradually moving towards prosperity. Everyone says that gold is everywhere on the network. To pick up gold on the network, the infrastructure of domain space IDC business holds great potential, and many ambitious individuals also want to join in agency for domain space to earn network gold.

So, what are the benefits of joining a domain space agency? In summary: small investment, low risk, strong support, no need for advanced technical skills yourself, experience the journey of entrepreneurship as your own boss, suitable for both individuals and enterprises, the more you work, the more you earn, with no upper limit to income.

When doing domain space agency, we all need to be clever. Many companies have chosen an agency because they initially thought the price was low or due to a single low price for their products. Later, they found many problems arose such as unstable space, unstable DNS for domains, inability to resolve IP addresses, or slow speeds, etc. Therefore, finding a service provider to act as an agent is very important. How do you find one? What kind of service provider should you look for? Below, I will summarize my experiences as a reference for everyone.

First, whether the service provider is trustworthy. This is a prerequisite. A service provider without credibility must not be considered. How do you determine if a service provider is credible? It's simple; use their company name to search on Baidu, etc., and check the online reviews of the company, whether there are any complaints, how good their reputation is, etc. If there are none, then you can consider it.

Second, profit margin. Doing agency work is for profit, so this must be paid attention to. If there is no profit or the profit is too low, then acting as their agent is meaningless, wasting effort. Therefore, getting the best discount possible is ideal, meaning the lower the discount, the higher your profit margin.

Third, product quality and diversity uniqueness. Good quality products lead to good sales. Who would want to promote a bad product? You face clients, so quality-advantaged products that are easy to manage can help agents achieve twice the result with half the effort. Diverse and unique products can make you stand out in the same industry.

Fourth, whether technology and customer service are in place, and whether customer service support can be found at any time. Generally, good service providers offer 7*24-hour service. You can check at different times, such as morning, noon, evening, late night, to see if there are people on duty on their company website, if there is a 24-hour service hotline, and even try calling to confirm their service quality. Without 7*24-hour service, it is difficult to get help if problems arise. So, please take this into serious consideration.

Fifth, compare three options, no fear of loss. Some say, some service providers offer free agency, while others require tens of thousands of advance payments for agency. Is the free one the best? Wrong! There is no such thing as a free lunch, many have heard this saying. Think from another angle, if everything is free, then what funds do they have to support their development? If they don't develop, how can they support your agency? Therefore, advance payment is the preliminary investment for doing agency, and according to understanding, many service providers' advance payments can be consumed. Once you have a batch of customers, that batch of money becomes entirely your income, making it zero cost. So, once you have a potential service provider, it's best to thoroughly understand their company operations, preferably ask friends who have used their services, and refer to their opinions.
