10.1.1 Small Mule/Italian Hybrid Knot

When using the Italian half knot to descend along the rope or protect the climber, pull the rope forward first to brake. Then make two mule ears as shown in the figure, pass the second one through the first one and tighten it. Note: Only then can you let go of the brake hand. Then tie another half knot at the end.

If only a momentary pause is needed when lowering along the rope, sports news, there is no need to tie a knot, just wrap the brake rope around the thigh 3-4 times. The thigh is flat.
