Supermodel Eunis Chan Loves Soup for Natural Beauty

Every time I see Eunis Chan, she makes you feel comfortable: clean face, well-behaved personality, no airs. Over the past two years, she took time to rest behind the scenes and wrote a book called "The Supermodel's Battle by Herself," teaching beauty tips, of course, as a gimmick. In addition, Eunis also hopes to impart domestic beauty knowledge.

Eunis believes that care must start from the inside to affect the outside. Getting better internally, external maintenance will be much simpler. She uses anti-wrinkle cream and deep cleans once a month to keep her delicate skin youthful.

She likes bronze gold makeup

As a professional model, Eunis often wears heavy makeup, so when she’s not working, she seldom applies makeup, allowing her skin to breathe fully. If attending formal occasions, she first applies a base before makeup, then liquid foundation followed by sunscreen, coated with transparent powder, brushes on a little blush, paints her eyelids with mascara, and finally applies lip gloss.

Asked if she could only carry one cosmetic, what would it be? She answers that it must be a pencil for makeup but still enjoys the smoky eye (smoky eyes) look that has been popular in recent years. She feels her skin is whiter, so she quite likes the beach bronze shape, making her full of sunshine. She captured their shape using a mobile phone.

With flowing hair, supermodel Eunis Chan

Getting that jet-black hair

With flowing hair, Eunis, due to work needs, often creates different hairstyles, which hurts her hair badly. To help promote blood circulation and make her hair more shiny, she dries it last and then coats it with water without conditioner, such as L'Oreal or Elseve line conditioner. Every two weeks, she gets hair oil treatment at the hair salon. Additionally, Eunis eats walnuts to make her hair grow healthier, as walnuts benefit hair, nails, and kidneys. Her hair stylist boyfriend recommends that she eat a teaspoon of black sesame seeds, which can make her hair more beautifully black.

Opening Eunis' makeup bag

1. Simple skincare products

With natural beauty, Eunis takes very good care of her skin, so she doesn't use too many skincare products. In the morning, before leaving, she only applies Lancome's Absolue Sun Cream. At night, she cleanses with La Mer Cleanser, then applies Glycel Revitalizing Eye Cream and Sisley anti-wrinkle cream.

2. Standing cosmetics

Eunis does not like heavy makeup, so she only uses a few cosmetics. Laura Mercier makeup base, Lancome's SPF15 sun protection liquid foundation, Laura Mercier transparent powder, Lancome Mascara (upper eyelid), Maybelline Mascara (lower eyelid), and Shu Uemura's lip gloss are commonly used cosmetics by Eunis recently.
