Mao Doudou, an emergency room nurse, and Yu Wei, a photographer at a portrait studio, went on a blind date, but met Mao Doudou's first love, Li Ruqiu, and the two parted ways unhappily after the blind date.

The couple had no idea what to do with their son, and the wedding was held as scheduled. However, on the second day of the wedding, the groom announced his fear of marriage and disappeared on his own, and proposed divorce. In her helplessness, Su Su proposed to leave her 7-year-old daughter with Mao Doudou's family to take care of as a condition for divorce. A day of marriage ended like this.

Mao Doudou began to go on blind dates one after another. It was incredible that every object introduced by different people was Yu Wei. After accepting their fate, Mao Doudou and Yu Wei couldn't help but seriously examine each other. Yu Wei frankly explained the purpose of consecutive blind dates. It turned out that he had been pretending to be his buddy who drowned and died, sending his photos to his grandmother for many years. The two gradually fell in love and quickly reached the stage of meeting both sets of parents. Yu Wei told Mao Doudou in advance about the complicated family situation of "two fathers, two mothers, and a sister who lost her father on the second day of her wedding". He said that his father and mother divorced and remarried respectively, and the two mothers were irreconcilable. After much hesitation, Mao Doudou finally mustered up the courage and asked Yu Wei to arrange a meeting between both sets of parents. The scene was predictable, and Mao Doudou was caught in the middle, at a loss.

Yu Wei and Mao Doudou finally got married. After the wedding, when they returned home, Yu Wei's sister who lost her father informed them that she would live with the couple. While complaining, the two had to force a smile and welcome her.

Life after marriage was relatively peaceful, although Yu Wei's sister, Yu Hao, constantly picked faults and caused trouble due to her eccentric personality, but they all passed smoothly because of Mao Doudou's tolerance and carefree attitude. Soon, all the "original ecological" simple behaviors of Pan Meili before marriage gradually became intolerable to Mao Feng, and his flirtatious nature made him entangled with Long Jin again. However, Pan Meili continued to treat him with simple love.

Yang Yifan was worried about Yu Hao returning by train alone, so he sent her off for another journey. Yang Yifan didn't want to drag Yu Hao down because he had been stationed on the island for a long time. However, Yu Hao was exceptionally determined at this point. The two met at the train station because Yang Yifan was going to military school. As the train started moving, Yu Hao jumped onto the train again. She was determined to marry this soldier.

At Yang Yifan and Yu Hao's wedding, Mao Doudou's two mothers-in-law also, encouraged by a group of young people, reconciled all past grievances.

The three daughters-in-law faced the future life with confidence...
