Circular Magnetic Power Generating LED Desk Lamp

Now let me introduce a more special magnetic energy lamp, designed for underground garage lighting. This is an LED lamp that generates electricity using magnetic power, designed by Andrej Čverha from HANGAR Design Forge. This product has no wires, plugs, or external power sources. The circular device on top continuously rotates back and forth, incorporating a built-in magnetic generator that generates electricity based on the principle of cutting magnetic flux lines. The low-power consumption LED lights use the generated electricity sufficiently for normal operation. The overall design of the lamp exudes a sci-fi and alternative style, resembling a desk lamp design from the future.

Designer: Andrej Čverha

An LED Desk Lamp That Can Be Given Like a Flower

Some people give flowers, others give lamps, and some treat lamps like flowers to give away. This is the flower lamp from Milan, meticulously designed by Modoloco Design Workshop in Milan, the world's fashion capital. The Ramo lamp combines the elements of flowers, vases, and lamps into one beautiful design filled with a sense of spirituality and natural beauty. What’s most touching is that it can be given away like a flower, making your LED desk lamp a frequently used item on her desk. She'll often think of you when she uses it, making this one of the most suitable desk lamps for gifts.

Silhouette LED Desk Lamp

Designed by two Italian designers, Flavio Mazzone and Claudio Larcher, from Modoloco in Italy, the Malena Silhouette LED desk lamp uses aluminum plates folded into rounded corners as the main body, cleverly crafted into a book-like shape. The edges of the cover are carefully trimmed into the outline of a desk lamp. The LED light source is installed inside, and the surface is specially treated to allow sufficient light transmission, forming a bright silhouette of the desk lamp. There's beauty in books, as well as light and shadow. The simple combination of light and shadow vividly presents the warmth of books, desk lamps, and home.

Gardening and Floral LED Pendant Lamp

LED lights once again elevate gardening to new heights, combining fashionable plant flowers with green LED lighting, resulting in both a potted plant and a decorative LED pendant lamp. Two types of LED lights are installed in the circular lamp plate: one as the main downward light source and the other as the upward auxiliary light source. These aesthetically pleasing and practical ecological lighting fixtures are very suitable for indoor home environments to get closer to green plants. The low-temperature waterproof LED design allows plants to grow well, the light makes the flowers look even more beautiful, and the plants make the light more vibrant. Let the green lighting of LEDs have a bit more natural green.

Designer: Enzo Berti
