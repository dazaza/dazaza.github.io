The sharp teeth of time can consume everything.

by bantaozap on 2011-09-15 14:48:09

The waves that have gone will not flow back, and the time that has passed will not come again.

All economy eventually boils down to time economy.

Time is the standard for testing truth.

Time is like a river, it can only flow forward and never return.

If you long for the laurel of victory, don't waste a single day.

A man of ambition values years, a wise man values days, and a sage values every moment.

The sharp teeth of time can consume everything, except for the truth which it cannot destroy.

You may fool with time, but it takes you seriously.

Do you love life? Then do not waste time, for time is the stuff life is made of, as in the classic quotes from Jane Eyre.

Seize the opportunity without hesitation, for time lost will not come again.

We must remember that our time for learning is limited. Time is limited, not only because life is short, but also due to the many distractions we face.

For those who possess no other property than wisdom, time is their sole capital.

Time can acquire gold, but gold cannot buy time.

Fill your time with joyous pursuits rather than binding time to accompany happiness.

Saving time makes one's finite life more effective, which is equivalent to extending one's life.

Once youth is gone, it will never return; once a career opportunity is missed, success will be hard to achieve.

Life is measured in units of time. Wasting others' time is akin to theft, and wasting one's own time is like slow suicide.

Prime years do not come twice, nor does a day bring two mornings. Seize the moment and encourage yourself, for time waits for no one.

Only by closely following the pace of time will luck always be on your side.

Making profits from business always requires at least three years.

Among all critics, time is the greatest, most accurate, and most talented one.

You cannot enjoy two mornings within one day.

Time is public property that cannot be monopolized, and with its passage, truth will increasingly reveal itself.

He who loses time is deserted by time.

Time is a substance, while wealth is a weight.

Time in the hands of a lazy man remains forever a blank sheet of paper.

Among all natural gifts that come with birth, time is the most precious.
