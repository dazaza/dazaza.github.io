How to win back lost love

by lwf225 on 2009-09-02 00:25:03

Tags: Emotional Diary, QQ Zone Diary, Classic QQ Zone Diary, Touching QQ Zone Diary

Love, what exactly is it? Why is breaking up so painful? It's been a long time... A long time ago, I thought I wouldn't fall in love with anyone. It seemed like I had already seen through everything in this world. But now, I am two souls that cannot be together. In fact, the person who loves you doesn't necessarily have to be very elegant or ambitious. Love only needs someone who can get along well in life and care for each other! Everything in life is so ordinary!

Fate made me meet her, and before I realized it, I understood that I shouldn't fall in love. Once you fall in love, it's hard to let go, hard not to think about her! Even if you convince yourself mentally, emotionally, you just can't break away from her! I wish I could stop loving her like a mischievous child, but this web of affection is so strong! Can lost love not be revived? Thinking about it, I was too idealistic and naive. Who could accept this kind of love I have? We broke up, but I can't find a reason to hate her. Besides the bitter laughter in my heart and self-numbing, what else do I have left? Who would want to be with someone who only seeks these empty thoughts? And who would want to share their soul with you and take on a portion of the melancholy from life?

In the pursuit of happiness, I lost it! Those who don't pursue anything mock me from the side! Can I have the same broad-mindedness as Zhuangzi? Can I have the courage to continue smiling in the face of loneliness? Can a separated soul last long? I once yearned for freedom, but no matter how much I wander, I can't escape! The higher the ideal, the harder the fall!

The heart can be lost. No matter how sorrowful it was in the past, the heart would always adjust itself. Now, however, it's just a painful smile.

I can no longer live without love. Is it a desire that comes from the depths of the universe and cannot be escaped, or is it the care of love! Not loving and having loved are different things. Once you've loved, when you separate, it feels like your life becomes incomplete! But when the dream wakes up, the pain is still there!

I can't see the night sky, I spent the whole night in the internet cafe! Not good at writing, I just say what I think.

Can lost love be found again? How to retrieve lost love?
