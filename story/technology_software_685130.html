Wife takes care of her husband, who was paralyzed in an accident, for 19 years: "This is how couples should be"

by fghgbd492 on 2012-03-01 13:49:12

Chutian Golden Newspaper - Liao Hesheng from Chibi City was paralyzed for 19 years due to an accidental work injury, and his wife Peng Guoan has been taking care of him with no complaints. Recently, when reporters interviewed Peng Guoan, she said very calmly: "It's nothing, couples should be like this!"

On the morning of February 28, 1992 at around 8 o'clock, Liao Hesheng had just stepped into the company's big iron gate when it suddenly collapsed and heavily hit his lower body.

After emergency rescue, Liao Hesheng regained consciousness, but the doctor told him he would never be able to stand again. Could a paralyzed person bring happiness to his wife? At this point, Liao Hesheng fell into despair. It was then that Peng Guoan held her husband's hand with her delicate hands and said, "Whatever happens, we face it together. I believe there is no hurdle we cannot overcome!"

That year, Liao Hesheng was 37 years old, and Peng Guoan was 35.

In order to take care of her husband full-time, Peng Guoan resigned and went home, where she learned massage techniques. Every day in the morning, afternoon, and evening, she gave her husband a massage; day after day, she washed the soiled bedding caused by her husband’s incontinence; to ensure smooth blood circulation throughout his body, Peng Guoan even custom-made a piece of fitness equipment. Every day, she would crawl under her husband's legs, supporting his two legs with her hands to help him exercise his upper limbs using the fitness equipment.

It is understood that Peng Guoan and her husband were high school classmates and spent eight years together as educated youth, making them close friends through hardship. In order to treat her husband, Peng Guoan sold two houses and once accumulated a lot of debt, which she only managed to pay off a few years ago. Peng Guoan told reporters: "The hardest times are over, now I need to take care of him even better and accompany him for the rest of his life!"

(Special correspondent Lin Yongjun, special reporter Fu Zhilin, communicator Yuan Lixin, Chen Zhiru)
