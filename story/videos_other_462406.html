Guiyang Channel, Xinhua News Agency, July 9th (reporters Li Jingya, Li Jinfeng) - The "Harmonious Cities and Livable Environment" forum opened on the 9th at the Guiyang International Eco-Conference Center.

According to reports, the topics of this forum include "Developing green buildings, creating ecological communities, constructing ecological cities", "Commonalities and development trends of sustainable human settlement examples", "Construction of new urban areas and urban regeneration", "New concepts, new developments, and new practices in human settlement science", "Application of circular economy technology in large real estate projects", "Green building development trends", "Energy conservation and emission reduction and harmonious human settlements", "Main development of real estate and human settlements", "Market transformation of sustainable buildings", "Future Ark": New practice of Zhongtian City Investment (000540, stock bar) human settlement example".

This forum invited more than a thousand well-known domestic experts and scholars, political figures from provinces and cities, industry leaders and insiders from home and abroad, including Mao Qizhi, deputy dean of the School of Architecture at Tsinghua University, Zhang Yuanduan, vice president and secretary general of the China Real Estate Research Association, Gu Yunchang, vice president and secretary general of the China Real Estate Association, Lijiaxiang, doctoral supervisor of the School of Architecture at Tsinghua University, and Qin Hong, deputy director of the Policy Research Center of the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, to jointly discuss the development trends and practical issues of "harmonious cities and livable environments."

Yang Yueguang, deputy director of the Department of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of Guizhou Province, said that Guizhou's ecological environment is beautiful. In urban environmental construction, we will adhere to people-oriented principles, focus on comprehensive systematic protection of the environment, coordinate the relationship between people and the environment, and promote the improvement of residents' living standards.

Guiyang Vice Mayor Ji Hong said that ecological advantages are Guiyang's greatest advantage. Therefore, Guiyang determined to take the scientific development concept as the overall leader, implement the development strategy of building an ecological civilization city, and proposed to build an ecological civilization city with "mountains in the city, mountains in the city, cities in the forest, forests in the city, lakes accompanied, green belts embrace". Guiyang enjoys the reputation of being the summer capital of China. It has compiled plans for an ecological civilization city and strives to achieve the urban development goals of being livable, suitable for work, and tourism.

Zhang Zhi, vice president of one of the event organizers, Zhongtian City Investment Group, said that Guiyang's real estate market is steadily entering the ranks of second-tier cities nationwide. In the future, Guiyang's urban primacy will further increase. The "Future Ark" project created by Zhongtian City Investment is an important part of the "Greater Guiyang" strategy. After the completion of the project, it will become a Guiyang urban sub-center integrating a world-class tourism engine, a comprehensive livable new city, and a landmark ecological corridor, and will be a major landmark engine project for Guiyang to realize the reconstruction of urban functional space and the upgrading of urban image.
