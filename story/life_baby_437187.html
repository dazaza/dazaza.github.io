Giraffe seeing a doctor

by fengtie on 2011-05-26 07:14:35

One morning, the giraffe woke up feeling very uncomfortable. "I need to see a doctor!" He hurriedly ran towards the Forest Hospital.

However, when he arrived at the hospital entrance, the giraffe was stumped - his head was even higher than the fourth floor! "How am I supposed to go inside and see the doctor?" The giraffe became so anxious that tears started streaming down his face.

"Don't worry, don't worry," Dean Panda rushed out and said, "Could you please stand by the window? I'll have the doctors check you right away."

The window on the first floor opened, and Dr. Rabbit poked his head out: "Let me feel your feet."

The window on the second floor opened, and Dr. Goat poked his head out: "Let me tap your stomach."

The window on the third floor opened, and Dr. Frog poked his head out: "Let me touch your neck."

The window on the fourth floor opened, and Dr. Mouse poked his head out: "Open your mouth, let me take a look at your tongue."

After the examination, the four doctors conferred briefly. Then, they brought out a large plate of leaf cake and a big cup of water, and told the giraffe: "This is your medicine, please eat it right away!"

The giraffe munched on the cake with an "Ah-um Ah-um," and gulped down the water with a "Gulping Gulping." Immediately, he felt much better. "Oh, I was just thirsty and hungry!" The giraffe was extremely happy. He wanted to thank the four doctors.

The giraffe smiled warmly and gave Dr. Mouse four kisses on his face. "One kiss is for you, and the other three please share with the other doctors!"

"Alright!" Dr. Mouse scurried up to the third floor and gave Dr. Frog three kisses on his face. "These are the giraffe's kisses, one is for you, and the other two please share with the other doctors!"

"Alright!" Dr. Frog hopped over to the second floor and gave Dr. Goat two kisses on his face. "These are the giraffe's kisses, one is for you, and the other please share with the other doctors!"

"Alright!" Dr. Goat trotted down to the first floor and gave Dr. Rabbit one kiss on his face, "This is the giraffe's kiss!"

"Smells delicious!" Dr. Rabbit happily said.
