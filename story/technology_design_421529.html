Will I take a loss?

by whboaiaa57jq on 2011-04-26 16:35:09

As soon as he walked out of the banquet hall, Ge Wei suddenly felt a chill down his spine. Only now did he truly realize that something was off about today's events. During the banquet, he thought he himself was acting strange, but because his attention had been diverted at the time, he didn't give it much thought. However, after regaining his composure, Ge Wei realized the seriousness of the issue.

"Could it be that the Love Guru did something to my limbs? My personality has even changed. I can play musical instruments that I've never touched before!"

With this realization, he no longer felt like teasing Hua Ningsnow and quickly drove her home. As soon as he got back, Ge Wei hurriedly turned on his computer.

Opening the Love Guru program, before Ge Wei could say anything, Ruobing spoke first: "Did you enjoy yourself tonight?"

Ge Wei furrowed his brows, "Did you tamper with me? Are you aware of what happened tonight?"

Ruobing, seemingly unfazed by her actions, maintained her usual cold demeanor, "I did make some modifications to your body. Training you into a playboy in the shortest time possible is extremely difficult. I couldn't possibly teach you step-by-step, which would take years. So, I adopted an extreme method. I injected playboy genes directly into your body. These genes will gradually transform your body to meet the standards of a super playboy."

Ge Wei noticed that Ruobing's conversation revolved around playboys, so he asked suspiciously, "Why do you keep saying that you want to turn me into a playboy? I remember your initial function was just to teach me about love, right?"

"That's true. However, at that point, my functionality was still in its early stages, so I could only aim to help you with love. But now things are different. As my capabilities grow stronger and the playboy genes inside you awaken, you are destined to become the ultimate enemy of all men—a super playboy! Without some extraordinary abilities, it's impossible to charm countless beauties from all over the world!"

The phrase "ultimate enemy of all men" startled Ge Wei. He imagined a scenario where people on the street pointed at him and shouted, "That's the bastard! After seeing his picture, my wife lost interest in tea and food and has been starving for half a year!"

"Why do you have to turn me into a spoiled rich kid?" Ge Wei continued, "And what is your purpose here? Why did those people give you to me instead of someone else?"

Despite not fully trusting that a computer program could harm him, Ge Wei couldn't ignore the evident changes in his body, though they were positive. He needed to seriously consider this situation.

Ruobing replied succinctly, "I don't know. It's not in my database, nor can I search for it."

It seemed that this futuristic technology wasn't something to be treated lightly. Although the changes were already happening—playboy genes injected into his body—Ge Wei had no intention of asking Ruobing to reverse them. For one, he wasn't sure if she'd agree; secondly, becoming a playboy didn't seem too bad; thirdly, making another change might carry risks, and he was feeling quite good physically as it was.

Still cautious, Ge Wei asked again, "Will these playboy genes have any side effects? Will I... lose sexual function?" Even saying those three words to a cute girl made him blush despite his thick skin.

"No, there won't be any side effects," Ruobing assured him.

"Good, good," Ge Wei said in relief. "Now let's discuss this: I've already turned into a playboy, and I'm interested in both Hua Ningsnow and Tang Jia. Can you tell me what to do next?"

After a brief pause, Ruobing finally responded, "You've already sparked a strong interest in Hua Ningsnow. Interest is the origin of affection. Your next move should be to seize the opportunity and pursue her, intensifying her interest."

Ge Wei nodded, "That makes sense, but how exactly should I do that?"

"You might consider developing your career. Hua Ningsnow knows your current situation, but today you showed exceptional talent, completely changing her perception of you. If you can amass significant wealth in a short period and continue treating her exceptionally well, she'll genuinely start considering a long-term relationship with you." Ruobing explained calmly.

This aligned perfectly with Ge Wei's own plans. If advancing his career also helped with dating, he was more than happy to proceed.

"So much for Hua Ningsnow, what about Tang Jia? What about her?" Ge Wei imagined having both beauties in his arms.

"Tang Jia? You don't need to actively pursue her. Just ensure that your relationship with her doesn't interfere with your pursuit of Hua Ningsnow!"

"Huh?" Ge Wei was confused. Was Tang Jia's affection for him so strong? He often heard that young people nowadays easily change their hearts—loving you passionately one day and ignoring you the next. Was Tang Jia, a wealthy young lady, really capable of genuine feelings? Even if she liked him a little, it wasn't to the extent of marrying no one but him, was it?

In fact, Ge Wei underestimated Tang Jia. Though she was a young lady, she had always yearned for an ordinary life. That's why she deliberately joined the Hua family's business as a regular employee and concealed her identity.

Coincidentally, Ge Wei was the kind of person who couldn't resist showing kindness to beautiful women while maintaining proper boundaries. This prevented Tang Jia from feeling threatened or disrespected, which paradoxically accelerated their relationship. Over several years, they grew closer, and Tang Jia repeatedly declined her family's requests to return and assume important responsibilities.

Through years of interaction, Tang Jia developed a dependency on Ge Wei, which eventually blossomed into deep affection. She didn't care about his wealth or whether he was extraordinarily talented. All she cared about was whether Ge Wei loved her alone.
