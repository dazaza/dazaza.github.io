Why postgraduate students while working are becoming more popular

by shuoshi_b on 2011-09-05 16:47:42

From the enrollment situation of part-time graduate students at Central University of Finance and Economics and other major universities, in the past three years, the number of part-time graduate students enrolled has been increasing year by year, and the tuition fees have also been rising year by year, showing an increasingly popular trend. Why are part-time graduate students so popular? A summary analysis reveals the following five reasons:

Reason one: Intensified competition in the talent market

Currently, the streets are full of junior college and undergraduate students. For a university graduate who has just graduated with a bachelor's degree, their salary may even be less than that of a migrant worker, and after working for a few years, they still cannot stand out. Therefore, after working for a few years after graduation, university graduates will realize the advantages of having a master's degree. At the same time, they cannot give up their jobs, and eventually discover that enrolling as a part-time graduate student is a good choice.

Reason two: The allure of attending prestigious universities without exams

The biggest difference from regular graduate students is that you don't need to take an exam to attend the part-time graduate programs of prestigious universities like Renmin University and Central University of Finance and Economics. The author once interviewed several part-time graduate students at Central University of Finance and Economics, and they all said that when ordinary people ask about their educational background, they can directly answer that they are graduate students at Central University of Finance and Economics, which gives them a sense of superiority.

Reason three: Gaining access to high-end networking opportunities

People who have worked for a few years will realize that in this society, success requires not only ability but also networking. With the same level of ability, those with wider networks can obtain more good development opportunities. Enrolling as a part-time graduate student can provide these resources. For example, Central University of Finance and Economics is among the best in China in fields such as accounting, finance, insurance, actuarial science, and management. These programs introduce advanced international learning systems and organize over 700 exchange activities each year with elites from various fields. Through these activities, part-time graduate students can meet many top figures, gain better exchange opportunities, and even have an industry top figure become their external mentor (Mentor).

Reason four: Flexible learning and lifestyle

The biggest feature of part-time graduate students is that they do not have to attend classes every day at school. They can work while studying, and the knowledge learned can be immediately applied at work, making learning simple, enjoyable, and practical. Moreover, through learning and exchanges, part-time graduate students can meet classmates, friends, and experts from different fields. With hundreds of events held each year, it can make their lives outside of work colorful and varied.

Reason five: Obtaining multiple degree qualification certificates

An increasing number of prestigious universities are beginning to cooperate with internationally renowned industry certification institutions, allowing part-time graduate students to not only obtain a graduate diploma and a master's degree but also receive industry certification practice qualification certificates. For example, Central University of Finance and Economics cooperates with the International Education Society, enabling part-time graduate students to obtain three certificates: a Central University of Finance and Economics part-time graduate certificate, a Central University of Finance and Economics master's degree, and an International Education Society industry certification. Students holding these qualification certificates can also obtain lifelong continuous professional development support from the International Education Society, helping them continuously improve their knowledge, network, and career.

With the rapid development of society and the constant changes in education methods, part-time graduate education is such an innovative educational method. As part-time graduate students become increasingly popular in different fields and job positions, the demand for part-time graduate education will inevitably continue to grow!
