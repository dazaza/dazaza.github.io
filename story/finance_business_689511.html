Therefore, what is the standard for evaluating whether a post in BBS forum marketing is successful?

Generally, companies specializing in BBS forum marketing determine the success of a post based on the following aspects:

- View count: You can check the total number of views on the post.

- Reply count: Check the total number of replies to the post.

- User participation statistics: From the post, you can know how many users participated.

- Valuable discussions in replies: In the replies, you can see where there are valuable discussions.

- Post triggers user discussion: Check if users are discussing within the post.

These five points are generally used by companies specializing in BBS forum marketing to evaluate the success of a post.
