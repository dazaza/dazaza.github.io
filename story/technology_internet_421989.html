**Yesmywine's Wine Fun Facts**

**Yesmywine's Wine Fun Fact 1: Waiting for Lafite?**

Yesmywine has discovered that there are so many good wines out there, but the one that truly suits you is the best. Whether you're a fan of New World or Old World wines, whether you love the rich aroma when the bottle is opened or the ever-changing sensations it brings, Yesmywine believes: We should fully enjoy this wonderful drink that brings us happiness.

Yesmywine also found that recently, people often ask me, "Do you have Lafite?" I would respond by asking, "Do you want the main label or the secondary label? Which vintage?" The answer I usually get is, "You know, the whole Lafite series. Do you have it? For around a hundred dollars?" When I heard this, I could only laugh bitterly and remain speechless. All I could say was, "I don't have the wine you're referring to." I wondered, has it come to the point where, besides Lafite, Chinese people no longer know how to choose other wines? Is Lafite the only way to show off your taste and status? Such a culturally rich wine, after being commercialized over and over again, how can we restore its original essence? Yesmywine noticed that Chinese wine consumption is becoming more and more sophisticated, with astonishing quantities. Merchants see this as a lucrative opportunity and keep playing on the edges. I ask these so-called "Lafites," how did you pass through the business registration checks? The area where Lafite is produced is limited, and France itself isn't that big. Think about it—how much Lafite can be produced in a year? It’s long been reserved by royalty or powerful wine enthusiasts via futures purchases. Very little is exported, and even if some make it to China, the price is unimaginable.
