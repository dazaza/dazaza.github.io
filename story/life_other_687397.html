Love, Unbearable

1

It has been a very long time since anyone asked about me or thought of me. I no longer expect that a crane will suddenly fly onto the green fields. I just silently guard the pure land in my heart and wonder if you can feel that I can only be a good friend - the words of Xiaocao (Little Grass).

This afternoon, Xiaocao came alone to clean up a corner of the playground again, sat down in the shade, and was lost in thought. Suddenly her thoughts returned to this morning.

Not long after getting up, she received a thick letter. At one glance at the handwriting, she knew it was from her boyfriend three years ago. She quickly opened it. There were three letters inside, one of which she had written to him before, but now he sent it back to her. What's going on?

Xiaocao quickly took out the letter he wrote, the content as follows:

Xiaocao: After receiving the last letter you sent, I am really complicated in feeling because I have been rejected by you again.

During the three years of high school, I have been rejected by you time and time again. At that time, I thought it was for your studies, but now it seems unsuitable for you. If I hadn't received this letter, I might have foolishly waited for you until you graduated... Regardless of whether you want to listen or not, I just want to tell you that during those days in high school, except for you, almost nothing is worth remembering. Thank you for giving me so many happy times. Let me say this for the last time: I love you forever, thank you for giving me so much happiness.

After finishing reading the letter, Xiaocao stood there in a daze, not knowing what to do? Her inner feelings were very contradictory and uneasy, thinking: Am I really too cold and ruthless?

She went to her bed, lay down, and reflected on the bits and pieces of these past three years... Three years? Is he really willing to wait for me for three years? A person who loves you so deeply, where else can you find such a one? Xiaocao, Xiaocao! What great fortune! What are you waiting for?

Xiaocao's heart was speaking to her, but all along, she just regarded him as an older brother. How can an older brother become a boyfriend?

The scenes of the morning flashed through Xiaocao's mind like lightning, straight into her heart...

2

Tonight, Xiaocao tossed and turned, unable to sleep. Her thoughts kept lingering on the letter she received this morning. She originally thought that regarding him as an older brother would mean he would never leave her, but now it seems he still has to leave her. So, why not try to accept him? It’s worth a try! Thus, Xiaocao got up from the bed, went to the phone, and dialed the number. The other side picked up the phone. The person answering must be his mom!

"Please ask Xueqiang to take the call."

"Just a moment." A gentle voice.

After waiting for a while, a familiar voice finally sounded: "Hello, who is this?"

This voice lacked the vitality it once had. After hesitating for a moment, she finally spoke: "Xiaocao."

The other side paused for a moment and asked: "What's the matter?"

"I like you."

The tone of your voice tells me you're surprised, so I gathered all my courage and said: "I'll only say this once. I've always rejected you just to test your patience, understand? If you have a girlfriend now, it doesn't matter. Give yourself three years to pursue her, then tell me the result after three years. Is that okay?"

"I will quietly wait for you for three years." An excited voice...

3

Just after hanging up the phone, Xiaocao began imagining how she would feel when she saw Xueqiang again in the future, but once she thought about it, she became afraid. She actually forgot that she could not accept him due to her aversion towards him.

Although she was moved by his perseverance, it was just for a moment, merely a fleeting moment! What to do? What to do? Will I go crazy? If I tell him the truth, will he go crazy? Will he?

4

Xiaocao's heart struck her again, telling her that there are many kinds of love in life that can be endured, but if endured out of sympathy, then this is the kind of love in life that cannot be endured.

But... Xiaocao's heart was in turmoil. After a long consideration, she picked up the phone again and decided to accept another storm in her life...
