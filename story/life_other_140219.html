Energy and environmental protection are the most important international issues of this century. People's energy consumption has become a key factor that increasingly affects their lives. But energy consumption is only a superficial problem that we have seen so far. What is more serious is that the global warming caused by thermal power generation has made the already burdened Earth worse. The "World Hour" activity launched by Sydney, Australia, is to call on the world to save energy and reduce emissions from thermal power generation. I don't know if you've ever thought about it: What can we do as civilians? Today JarryJarry introduces an energy saving and environmental protection software: Province Look.

image is the world's first Chinese public welfare environmental protection software, which aims to automatically help users manage power-consuming devices such as monitors and hard disks, save electricity and extend computer life. Look at the electricity you save can be numerically expressed, and converted into how many trees and oil saved, so that users realize that saving electricity can not only reduce energy consumption, but also contribute to ecological protection!

image

Log in to save to see the official website of http://www.shengshengkan.com to download the software. The first time you run it, the software will ask you to create an account and then log in. The main interface of the software will display your energy-saving achievements and the total energy-saving achievements of the energy-saving team you have joined.

image

The Provincial View toolbox provides a range of small tools that can easily adjust the display brightness; Easily turn off infrequently used hardware devices (Bluetooth, optical drive, etc.) to save power consumption; Let the computer start downloading or running specific software at a specified time, and automatically shut down at a scheduled time. More smart tools are being developed and will gradually be added to the toolbox.

After all, the power of individuals is weak, and more people participate in environmental protection can effectively slow down the impact of greenhouse effect on the earth. Now you can build a power-saving team, recruit members and let friends join your team to protect the environment. Team power saving results can be displayed in real time on their own client and website, power saving and team size can also participate in the ranking, get the honor of the team medal!

Through the Settings, the province can also insert energy saving public welfare information on your MSN, and promote energy conservation and environmental protection through social networks.
