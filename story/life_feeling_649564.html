The Beauty of Incompleteness

I always thought that some memories are painful, so I wanted time to consume all the painful memories. Finally, one day, my time had no memory of you. I thought this day would take a long time to come. But time is not as slow as we imagined, or perhaps it's not even an issue of time.

Perhaps what heals past wounds is not just time, but meeting someone who can truly care for you. It's not that we met at the wrong time and place, but rather we were never meant to be together. What we needed was simply to meet the right person, even though none of us know how far into the future that person might be. One day, you will understand why before you met her, you had so many regrets with others, and nothing ever worked out. Because your other half is your soul, and it won't let you easily fall in love with the wrong person. If you haven't experienced life and death together with your soul, you may forever carry a kind of inherent incompleteness, rather than so-called regret. Until later when you meet her, you realize that the world's previous incompleteness was for the sake of completeness. We still have a complete friendship, so you should thank that incomplete love.

Your lips gently rise as you say you will remember my kindness. I can accept your determination not to fall in love again. The heart affairs will eventually be forgotten; keeping the past in our hearts is for the sake of remembering the once-upon-a-time. That year's snowfall was truly beautiful. I wrote "forever loving you" on a thin tissue, then tore it apart and crushed it, letting it fly from the highest point of a tall building at the same speed as the falling snow, finally burying it in the snowy ground. Then it would melt along with the snow, allowing colorless remembrance to disappear gracefully in a colorless world.

Perhaps using affection is not anyone's fault, but no one should love wrongly. My heart is still tangled in loving you. Too often, we see happiness as a distance, a distant distance, and we don't even have the strength to play a secret role. So splendid, so fragile a dust-bound relationship, extinguishing too quickly, disappearing somewhat cruelly. The pain brought by loneliness often leaves nowhere to hide, helplessly accepting that sense of pain. If there were no encounters through the eyes, there wouldn't be such a loss due to an unnecessary attachment. How to choose, unwilling to decide, but finally understanding that choosing is also a kind of release. Without you, clutching memories makes it hard to sleep, only oneself knows why one must indulge in decadence.

In solitary monologue at night, the weary eyes are scorched by dim light, aimless emotions don't know where to rest. Can there be a little more courage to keep the memories, to remember them for a very long time without forgetting? What can we trade for the love we desire? Hard-to-solve beloved, who is still lingering over the already vanished plot? It turns out that I've been painting your outline so that I can still own these memories, and in lonely corners, I can still taste my own solitude. If those beautiful visions weren't just dreams, wouldn't worldly matters be less cold? What does one's heart represent? Unyielding attachments, it turns out I've never gotten used to being alone at night. I am silent, not talkative, you always tell me not to be too sentimental, and ask me not to be melancholic anymore. I understand my youth; my senses will ensure my liberation. In my world, you are like a beautiful firework that disappears instantly; your existence cannot be eternal.

The night sky is vast, the past so clear, but I really don't know who was right or wrong. I don't love because I can't, I don't hate because I can't, I feel pain but don't regret. The word 'love' is easy to write but hard to understand; the silence on the back of my hand doesn't know how to hide. In fact, anyone can easily make vows, but who can walk with whom until the end of time?

Stopping here in thought, I use the last second to think about your beauty, then my breath no longer carries your scent.

Guiyang's fleeting years, another season has passed, time finally sprinkles on the heart becoming parting sorrow. The rain is cool, often falling unusually, cold weather writes this chapter for both myself and you in this youthful era.

The previous incompleteness was for the sake of future beauty.
