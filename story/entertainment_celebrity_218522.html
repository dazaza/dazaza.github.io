Japanese actress Fujii Noriko disappeared with her son, and was rumored to commit suicide.

by ed528960 on 2009-08-05 16:53:02

The Japanese media has just reported that Gakuto Osaki was arrested for drug use, and on Monday morning, Noboru Takasaki, the husband of Noriko Sakai, was also arrested for possession of drugs. Yesterday, Noriko Sakai's family reported to the police that both Sakai and her son were missing. Later, it was reported that the police found a female body suspected to be Noriko Sakai near Lake Mashu, and they are currently verifying the identity.

In the early hours of August 3, the Shibuya police in Tokyo found Noboru Takasaki, the husband of Noriko Sakai, suspicious while patrolling on the street. After questioning, they found drugs on him and arrested him on suspicion of violating the "Stimulants Control Act".

Yesterday, the president of Noriko Sakai's management company "Sunmusic" told the media that he last met with Sakai in mid-July. Since then, whether calling her mobile phone or sending mobile phone emails, he could not reach Sakai, and her son was also nowhere to be found. Her relatives and friends are very worried and have already reported to the police. The president hopes that if Sakai sees the TV, she will contact them no matter what.

The 38-year-old Noriko Sakai married Noboru Takasaki, who runs a surf goods store, in 1998 and they have one son. It is reported that a local magazine claims Sakai cannot bear her husband's habit of infidelity, and they often argue, so they have been living separately. The report also says that Sakai once cried to her friends: "He has disappointed me so much, now I just want to cry whenever I see him."
