Happy Baby Player's Experience and Insights

by jaykong on 2009-08-05 21:09:45

Newbies who have just entered Happy Babies need to take a look at the big question mark in the upper right corner first. This is the game help. The other ones, in my opinion, are not very useful ~~~~~ Now let's talk about how to make money. To be honest, the game help has introduced it, and it's actually very simple. At the bottom, there are your friends. You can click on your friends, enter their room, then a dialog box will appear. This dialog box allows you to choose the item you want to interact with your friend. I usually select "kiss" for everything ~ hehe ~ Next, you'll see your money increase by 20, and your experience increase by 5. At this point, most people will leave.

Please pay attention. Actually, at this moment, you can check your friend's current cleanliness, happiness, and health levels! (Your friend's status is displayed on the left side.) If they're not full, you can open the box, take out your soap, and give your friend a bath. There's also a brush that you can use to scrub your friend. You'll find your money increasing continuously. Generally, if all of them are empty, you get 10-20 coins added, and around 10 experience points added. So, visiting your friends gives more experience than just interacting with them. Remember this! (Also remember, not only can you do this with your friends, but you can click "whole site" and visit everyone on the site. Although visiting non-friends doesn't give money or experience, you can still give them baths and scrub them, which gives a lot of money and experience!)

You can visit your friends once in the morning and once in the afternoon, and it will also increase your experience. As far as I estimate, you should be able to visit again after more than 8 hours.

Then there are interactive games with the babies. Personally, I think skipping rope earns money faster and is easier. Generally, jumping 3-7 times will give you 2 points of experience. If you jump close to your last count, it generally adds around 7 points, and exceeding it will add 10 points. So don't exceed your last count too much each time; one or two jumps are enough, leaving space for next time to earn more money!!

Ball games and disc games are similar, but they're harder to catch because they don't always follow a fixed route, so they're not that easy!

Next is earning money through competitions. Most people will participate in competitions when they start. The game help says there are 10 chances to participate, but my suggestion is to practice first. At the beginning, when your level is low, you have 20 practice opportunities per day. (When your level gets higher, there won't be any limit on the number of practices, but the experience will stop increasing after around 20 times.) Each time, you can get 5 points of experience. Remember, many people skip over watermelon peels, but I want to say that if you skip over a watermelon peel, your speed will slow down slightly.
