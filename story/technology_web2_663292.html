Mayflower Going Abroad Network Encyclopedia Brief Introduction to the Going Abroad Exchange Community

by gezidanya on 2012-02-11 14:15:20

Mayflower Abroad Network (5yhua.org) is the largest domestic vertical abroad portal site, providing free information and a platform for exchange of information for people going abroad. It also has a large number of professionals in the overseas industry as well as free guidance and exchange on immigration, studying abroad, overseas labor, international tourism, and overseas life. Mayflower Abroad Network consists of the Mayflower Abroad Network website and the Mayflower Abroad Network Forum. The Mayflower Abroad Network website includes the Mayflower Immigration Network, Mayflower Study Abroad Network, Mayflower Overseas Labor Network, Mayflower International Tourism Network, and Mayflower Overseas Real Estate Network.

The Mayflower Abroad Forum includes: Mayflower Immigration Forum, Mayflower Study Abroad Forum, and Mayflower Labor Forum.

The Mayflower Immigration Forum includes: Overseas Service Forum, Overseas Intermediary Discussion Forum, International Tickets | Moving | Visa Insurance Service Forum, Overseas Friendship and Romance Forum, Canadian Immigration Forum, Canadian Federal Skilled Immigration Forum, Quebec Provincial Skilled Immigration Forum, Business/Visa/Tourism Visas, US Commercial/Investment EB5 Immigration Forum, Australian Skilled Immigration, Investment Immigration, Swedish Immigration, British Immigration, German Immigration, Maltese Immigration, Hong Kong/Macau Immigration Forums, Overseas Tourism Forum, Overseas Real Estate Forum, Singapore Immigration, Quebec Study-Immigration Application Zone, US Tourism/Business/Work Visas, US Marriage Immigration Forum, Canadian Provincial Nomination Immigration Forum, Canadian Family Reunification Immigration Forum, Canadian Experience Class Immigration Forum, Australian Investment Immigration, Canadian Life Forum - Living in Canada, USA, Australia, French Learning Exchange Forum, Overseas News.

The Mayflower Study Abroad Forum includes: US Study Abroad Forum, UK Study Abroad Forum, Study Abroad Group Buying Zone, Study Abroad Lecture Gathering Zone, Hong Kong Study Abroad, Malaysia Study Abroad, TOEFL IELTS GRE GMAT Exam Forum, Overseas Returnees Forum.

The Mayflower Labor Forum includes: Labor Intermediary Discussion Area, World Cruise Work Forum, Singapore Labor Forum, Japan Labor Forum, Malaysia Labor Forum, Canadian Labor Forum, US Labor Forum, Australia Forum, Philippines Labor Forum.

If you have any questions about going abroad, you can directly post them on the Mayflower Immigration Forum or the Study Abroad Forum for consultation. The most authoritative and up-to-date information and the most rigorous and quick responses from moderators will answer your questions about emigrating and immigrating.
