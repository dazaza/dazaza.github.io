Brooks will make Hongyuan run faster.

by seo17519 on 2012-02-20 16:26:25

Finally, Brookes was signed by Dongguan New Century. Thus, the lineup of Dongguan New Century has been completed. It was Dongguan New Century's consistent style to sign players so quickly. They will not move until they have made a decision. Once they decide to take an action, they will hit the target in one shot. When we were still constantly guessing whether Dongguan New Century could invite Stakes from Pistons or not, they had already transferred the goal of introducing foreign aids to Brookes. With such a stunning speed, Dongguan New Century signed the NBA level star - Brookes. We do not know how Xinjiang, their old rival, feel about this. Because they had just lost Dobie who performed extremely well last season. Although the management of Xinjiang are working day and night to find new objects, for Xinjiang, the difficulty of competing for the championship in the new season will be upgraded because Dongguan New Century signed Brookes.

Speaking of Brookes, Chinese fans, especially the fans of Rockets, should be very familiar with him. He is Yao Ming's former teammate. Chinese fans are used to call him Little Black Bean. The playing style of Little Black Bean is familiar to Chinese fans. With an agile figure, he often relies on his lightning speed to break through the defense system of the opponent, and can easily get rid of any defender, posing a threat to the opponent's defense. In view of Brookes' lightning speed, some people have compared him with Wade, the famous Flash in NBA, and Allen Iverson, the Answer. But he only rivals with these two top stars of NBA in speed. He is far behind them in other aspects.

However, there must be disadvantages along with advantages. Any team will be scared of Brookes' speed, but at the same time, his shortcomings are also very obvious. Due to the limitation of height, Brookes' defense is not quite outstanding. Moreover, objectively speaking, Brookes is more like an excellent guard who plays between the organizing and scoring positions. He is greedy to shoot and often throws three-point shots recklessly. His organizing ability is not outstanding. This has made the media experts question Brooks, who is known as the organizing guard, in recent years. Although after being traded from Rockets to Suns, under the guidance of Nash, the master of passing, Brookes has matured a lot compared with the guy who only had eyes for the basket before. But his height cannot be changed. His defensive ability and whether he will monopolize the ball are what Dongguan New Century need to pay attention to.

There is a saying in the martial arts world: the fastest skill in the world cannot be broken. Setting aside those small disadvantages of Brookes, considering the overall situation of Dongguan New Century, it is relatively suitable for Guangdong's current system to introduce Brookes. Although Brookes' defensive ability is somewhat lacking, we must understand that this is CBA, not NBA where the confrontation is intense and muscles are flexed. Everyone knows the quality of defense in CBA. I believe these things cannot stop Brookes' steps. Moreover, Dongguan New Century has a lot of defensive tough guys. Yi Jianlian, Singleton, and Zhou Peng can make up for Brookes' omissions at any time.

I once heard a senior basketball player say that if you want to win in the game, then you need to run. Obviously, Brookes' arrival will surely make all the players of Dongguan New Century run faster. Moreover, speed is one of the key factors for Dongguan New Century to stand in the league except for defense. Look at the players of Dongguan New Century, each of them seems to be born for fast breaks. Wang Shipeng, Zhu Fangyu, and Zhou Peng are all top-notch players. Yi Jianlian, Singleton, and Dong Hanlin are the most agile big men in the domestic league. As for Chen Jianghua and Liu Xiaoyu, I think no one will forget what Chen Jianghua's forte is, right? Do you know that he was reputed to be faster than even Wade and Paul, the speed kings of NBA, in the World Championship many years ago?
