From the bottom of my heart, I say: Happy Birthday! Excessive hard work has turned your hair white and etched wrinkles on your forehead. You've worked so hard; today, take a break for yourself and let yourself rest.

A peaceful and tranquil old age, a time of rest, pleasure, and supreme enjoyment! Wishing you boundless blessings like the endless flow of the Eastern Sea and longevity like the timeless pine of the Southern Mountain!

Today is a joyful day. After all these decades of hard work, your descendants gather together to share in the joy of kinship. Wishing you a life as long as the heavens and a future filled with good fortune!

The beautiful years you've lived are like an ink-fragrant calendar. Year after year, on this very day of the calendar, I send my heartfelt thoughts to celebrate your birthday.

Time flies, and today is your birthday once more. May you have endless joy and happiness today, and may all the happiness of your birthday encompass everything wonderful! May you always be God's "chosen one."

Your birthday reminds me of your care for me and all that you've done for me. I only wish that the happiness you've given me, I can return to you in the same measure. Wishing you success in all things and boundless happiness!

The traces of time are revealed in the deep grooves of your wrinkles. Decades of brilliance, decades of dedication—people will never forget you. On this joyful day, wishing you health and longevity!

Blessings as vast as the Eastern Sea, longevity like the towering Southern Mountain.

Life is filled with prosperity and joy, days passing sweetly and happily, harmonious bliss filling your heart, and happiness shining brightly on your brow! (Connect the first word of each sentence)

With every passing birthday, your spirit and soul seem younger. We sincerely wish you health, longevity, happiness, and thriving success! On the occasion of your birthday, we offer our gift with heartfelt wishes for your daily happiness, lasting health, and infinite blessings!

In celebrating your birthday, it also stirs up countless memories: your care helped me through life's difficulties; your guidance led me to the shore of my ideal destiny. Sending my sincerest wishes, may you always be healthy and happy year after year!

Birthday wishes symbolize a golden key, opening three doors. What do the road signs point to? Happiness, health, and wealth.

Today, you have entered your seventies. For someone of your age, health is the greatest treasure. I sincerely wish you health and longevity!
