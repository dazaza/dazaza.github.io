At 19:30 Beijing time (12:30 local Italian time) on February 13, the 25th round of Serie A began its third match, with Florence overturning Palermo 4-2 in an away game, securing their first away victory of the season in the league and ending Palermo's seven-match winning streak at home in the league. In the following matches, Lazio will play away against Brescia.

However, Fiorentina turned the tide with three goals in the last twenty minutes. In the 74th minute, Montolivo struck from 30 meters out and hit the right post. Four minutes later, substitute Ljajic cut inside from the left flank into the penalty area and passed to Mutu, but Bouvou deflected the ball into his own net, making it 3-2. This was Fiorentina's first reverse victory of the season and also the first time they had scored three goals away from home.
