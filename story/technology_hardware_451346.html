We kindly ask the audience of [Weekly Film Review] to continue giving us more criticism and suggestions. This is something that Xiao Bei and I will keep doing.

Some friends have provided feedback to both Xiao Bei and me, mentioning that the time interval between the last two issues has been longer. I would like to clarify a few points here:

1. The release time of [Weekly Film Review] is generally chosen in the week following the weekend premiere of films;

2. [Weekly Film Review] does not aim to influence the box office performance of films during their opening weekends;

3. Many films cannot be previewed in advance and can only be watched upon their public release. Considering the production cycle of [Weekly Film Review], we can only release it the following week;

4. Recently, during the "off-season" of the film market, not every weekend sees the release of new films, or there are very few new releases. Therefore, [Weekly Film Review] does not currently insist on releasing an issue every week;

5. We hope that in the future, with the prosperity of the film market, Xiao Bei and I will have endless films to talk about every week.
