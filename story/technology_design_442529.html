Qilan, as the only fictional character in the movie *Guan Yunchang*, has drawn a lot of attention. Moreover, she even engages in a romantic relationship with Lord Guan. Regarding Guan Yu's feelings for Qilan, Wong Chun-Keung stated that if one were to boldly describe it in one sentence: Guan Yu is like the trolley boy of that era — someone who dares not confess his own emotions but silently stands by the person he loves. The heroine would have no idea at all. When asked about the intricate design of making Guan Yu fall in love with the elder brother's woman, Mak Siu-Fai explained, "We are filming *Guan Yunchang*, and his emotional world is something we must depict. But if it were someone completely unrelated to him, it wouldn't be interesting either."

Cooperation with Donnie Yen Again Proves Rewarding

Sun Li’s Version of a Flower Vase Is Anything But Simple

Sun Li and Donnie Yen have already collaborated for the second time, with Sun Li adding an emotional subplot in *Guan Yunchang*. When asked about their renewed collaboration and the highlights of the emotional scenes, Sun Li said, "Since I am a girl originally, my role in *Painted Skin* wasn’t my usual self. However, Big Brother Donnie met me when I was playing a male role, so in *Guan Yunchang*, when I became softer, he was actually unaccustomed to it." Regarding their emotional scenes, Sun Li joked that she didn't feel awkward at all. "I was originally worried about how I should act alongside such a great actor, but the director told us that one of us would be inside the door while the other outside." Regarding rumors of being sidelined earlier, Sun Li laughed it off saying, "With my good nature, how could I possibly be sidelined?"

Female actors in male-dominated films are often labeled as 'flower vases.' For a dual-hero film like *Guan Yunchang*, when asked if Sun Li might diminish the masculinity of the film, Mak Siu-Fai expressed, "Typically, when we hire female actors, it is because they can serve as flower vases. However, I believe that being a flower vase is the most basic requirement, and there are good and bad flower vases. Sun Li is a flower vase who enhances the acting quality." Sun Li also showed no concern about being called a flower vase, saying, "Even if I am a flower vase, Qilan is a complex one—she needs to kill people, smash things, and has a lot of cunning. The directors told me that the most important theme in *Guan Yunchang* is the righteousness between men, but actually, Qilan is also a very righteous person."

On April 13th, the epic hero film *Guan Yunchang*, co-directed by Mak Siu-Fai and Wong Chun-Keung, and starring Donnie Yen, Jiang Wen, and Sun Li, will be officially released on the mainland on April 26th. On April 13th, the film held a Hero·Beauty press conference where directors Mak Siu-Fai and Wong Chun-Keung appeared alongside actress Sun Li. Sun Li showed no fear regarding the label of being a 'flower vase,' calling herself a non-simple flower vase—a flower vase that can kill people and is full of cunning. In the final trailer, after various martial arts moves, weapons, horsemanship, battles, single combats, taking on a hundred enemies alone, and intense gun and arrow fights performed by Donnie Yen, Guan Yu transforms into a special forces soldier of the Three Kingdoms era.
