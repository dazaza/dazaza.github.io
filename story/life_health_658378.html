What to Do for Toothache During the Spring Festival

Author: Webmaster Source: Internet Date: January 22, 2012 Clicks: 3728

The Spring Festival is coming. It's our most important holiday, a time for family and friends to gather, eat, drink, and have fun. But if you get a toothache during this time, it can be quite troublesome. What should you do if your toothache is caused by internal heat? With doctors on vacation during the New Year, you'll likely have to deal with it yourself.

There’s no need to search for folk remedies for toothaches. Below are several dietary therapy methods that you can try:

1. Salted Duck Egg and Oyster Porridge: Use 2 salted duck eggs, 100 grams of oysters (dried oyster meat), and an appropriate amount of rice to make porridge. Eat it for 2-3 consecutive days. Suitable for those suffering from toothaches due to upward inflammation of deficient fire.

2. Century Egg, Tofu Skin, and Salted Lean Pork Porridge: Use 2 century eggs, 60 grams of rehydrated tofu skin, 100 grams of salted lean pork, and an appropriate amount of rice (or millet) to make porridge. Eat it for 2-3 consecutive days. Suitable for those with toothaches caused by deficient fire and cavities.

3. Oyster, Century Egg, and Salt-Cured Lean Pork Porridge: Use 100 grams of oysters, 2 century eggs, 100 grams of salt-cured lean pork, and an appropriate amount of rice to make porridge. Suitable for those with swollen teeth and sore throat due to yin deficiency.

4. Achyranthes Root, Fresh Rehmannia, and Black Bean Porridge for Toothache: Use 12 grams of achyranthes root, 15 grams each of fresh and cooked rehmannia, 60 grams of black beans, and 100 grams of glutinous rice. Wash all ingredients thoroughly, cut the rehmannia into small pieces, and add an appropriate amount of water to cook into porridge. Remove the medicinal residue of achyranthes root and rehmannia, season with a little salt, and consume freely. Suitable for elderly patients with physical weakness and insufficient vital energy.

5. Mussel and Cistanche Black Bean Soup:

Use 30 grams each of mussels (also known as dried scallops, a seafood product) and cistanche, and 150 grams of black beans. Clean the mussels to remove sand, wash the black beans, slice the cistanche, and place them in a pot with an appropriate amount of water. Simmer for over an hour, then strain the liquid and drink it all at once. Take one dose daily, continuing for several days until the toothache subsides. Mussels enter the kidney meridian, nourishing yin and reducing fire; black beans replenish the kidneys, eliminate chest heat and stiffness, and disperse accumulated heat in the five organs. Suitable for toothaches caused by cavities and gum swelling due to deficient fire.

6. Mung Bean, Egg, and Sugar Water: Use 100 grams of mung beans, 1 egg, and an appropriate amount of rock sugar. Crush the mung beans, wash them, and place them in a pot with an appropriate amount of water. Cook until the mung beans are soft, then crack the egg into the soup, stir well, let it cool slightly, and drink it all at once. Continue for 2-3 days. Suitable for toothaches caused by wind-heat, characterized by redness, swelling, and heat pain in the mouth.

7. Willow Root Boiled with Lean Pork: Use 50 grams of willow roots and 100 grams of lean pork. Wash both ingredients thoroughly, add an appropriate amount of water, and boil together. Season the broth, then drink the soup and eat the meat. Suitable for wind-fire toothaches characterized by swollen gums and red, swollen cheeks.

Now there's no need to worry about not being able to find a doctor during the New Year celebrations. You can still enjoy delicious food and drinks while treating your toothache. Not only will you not miss out on any good eats, but you'll also take care of your toothache!
