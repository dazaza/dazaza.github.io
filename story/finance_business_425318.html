Shijiazhuang takes seven measures to clean up the city and removes all street vending markets

by qiyouchax12 on 2011-05-06 07:40:20

The Shijiazhuang City Urban Management Bureau has recently issued the "Implementation Opinions on Further Strengthening the Sanitation and Hygiene Management Work in the Old Urban Area." Starting today, the provincial capital will increase its efforts to clean up all kinds of construction waste and domestic garbage that are dumped indiscriminately, strengthen dynamic monitoring of behaviors such as street vending and illegal parking, to achieve the effect of clean urban roads and good sanitation order.

This activity will last from March 25 to December 31. From April 1 to November 31 is the comprehensive rectification phase. The environmental sanitation rectification mainly includes cleaning up urban garbage, road washing and cleaning, and management of construction soil vehicles, while the urban appearance rectification is mainly divided into governance of street vending, rectification of street promotion, standardization of "two booths one vehicle one stall", and removal of street markets.

■ 190,000 cubic meters of garbage cleared out of the city

It is reported that there are currently 32 places in the urban area of Shijiazhuang with a total of 195,100 cubic meters of accumulated garbage. All districts will complete the cleaning of this accumulated garbage by April 15. At the same time, they will organize and coordinate relevant departments to increase investigation and cleaning efforts to prevent the phenomenon of cleaning first and accumulating later.

■ Main roads watered three times a day

Strengthen road flushing and watering, enhance 24-hour sweeping and cleaning, and strengthen the cleaning of small streets and alleys. Strictly implement the responsibility system for maintaining cleanliness in front of shops. All districts and the Municipal Sanitation Department will focus on doing a good job in cleaning and flushing key areas such as Yuhua Road, Zhongshan Road, Construction Street, Zhonghua Street, Weiming Street, Qingyuan Street, and other main roads, the surrounding areas of the Provincial Museum Square, People's Hall, Hebei Hall, Xiqing Park Square, two station squares, and highway entrances and exits. They will adhere to watering more than three times a day and flushing once every ten days. For the railway station, bus station, and the 300-meter sections outside each exit of the second ring road, they will organize a flush once a week. Other secondary roads will be watered once a day, and after flushing, the roads should appear their original color.

In addition, they will focus on doing a good job in cleaning and maintaining 53 streets that are cleaned for 24 hours and 55 streets that are cleaned for 16 hours. They will further expand the scope of 24-hour cleaning and maintenance, and by the end of the year, the 24-hour cleaning and maintenance area of each district will be increased to more than 60% of the total cleaning area of the district.

■ 24-hour monitoring of soil vehicles

To strengthen the management of construction soil vehicles, all districts will assign dedicated personnel to monitor 24 hours a day during the period when demolition, infrastructure construction sites, and urban street renovation projects transport construction soil, ensuring that transport vehicles are sealed and covered, keeping the body clean, washing the wheels clean, and maintaining the cleanliness and hygiene of the construction site entrance; strengthening patrols at other stages of construction and requiring soft or hard covering of exposed surplus soil and flying building materials at the construction site.

The Municipal Sanitation Department and the Urban Management Bureaus of all districts will jointly inspect with traffic management departments. Soil transport vehicles that do not meet the requirements for sealed transportation and installation of GPS positioning systems will not be allowed to engage in construction soil transportation.

In addition, all districts will organize joint law enforcement inspections involving traffic management departments and urban management departments 2-3 times a week to solve problems such as random dumping, transportation spillage, and unlicensed transportation of construction waste vehicles. Vehicles that do not follow the required routes and designated locations for dumping will be severely punished, even being expelled from the soil transportation market.

■ Dedicated personnel at intersections to monitor street vending

Relevant departments will strengthen the cleaning of key areas and main streets in the urban area, conducting uninterrupted patrols of key areas and sections, especially assigning dedicated personnel to monitor major intersections to prevent violations of street vending. Ensure the neatness of the main and secondary roads with high traffic flow, especially strengthening the management of mobile vendors. Strengthen the governance of illegal street vending during peak working hours, especially strengthening the governance of illegal street vending from 17:00 to 20:00. At the same time, they will also do a good job in managing open-air barbecue street vending to prevent it from forming scale.

■ Strict prohibition of unauthorized outdoor performances

They will focus on strengthening the management of street promotional activities in front of large shopping malls and supermarkets along Zhongshan Road (from Jianhua Mall to Weiming Street) and around the train station square, especially in places like Beiguo Mall, Guomei Electrical Appliances, Dongfang Shopping, Renbai Square, and Yoyoo Department Store. If it is indeed necessary for new openings or important political and economic activities at the provincial and municipal levels to conduct outdoor promotional activities, they can be appropriately considered based on management needs and the location of the streets. However, the content is limited to balloons and arches, and the quantity will be strictly controlled. Resolutely stop unauthorized outdoor performances, and if open-air performances are discovered and not effectively stopped, strict deductions will be made according to inspection and evaluation standards.

■ Breakfast carts must withdraw before 8:30 AM

Bicycle repair stalls must operate within designated areas, strictly prohibiting over-range operations. Stall owners must maintain the order and cleanliness of the urban environment, ensuring that the stall is removed and the area is clear. Newsstands and milk booths must operate at specified locations, not allowing hanging newspapers and other items on the outer side, not allowing over-range operations or operations outside the booth, and not moving without permission.

In addition, breakfast carts must withdraw before 8:30 AM every day, not allowing overtime operations.

■ All street markets to be removed by the end of September

In addition, all districts will combine with Shijiazhuang's market construction work to conduct a detailed survey of existing street markets in their jurisdiction and formulate a work plan to ensure that all street markets are completely withdrawn from the streets and moved indoors by the end of September this year, returning the roads to the people.
