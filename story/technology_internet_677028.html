Polyaluminium chloride [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html], abbreviated as: poly aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html] (PAC [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html])

Its molecular formula is: AL2(OH)nCL6-n〕m (n being 3-5, m≤10)

It is commonly used extensively as a water treatment agent.

Most of the polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html] products are widely applied in industrial sewage treatment, mainly in power plants, steel mills, papermaking enterprises, and coal mines, etc., which discharge large amounts of sewage. Another part is used for domestic water and tap water companies; due to its application field, the requirement indicators for this part will be slightly higher, mainly reflected in content, insoluble matter in water, and heavy metals. Some parts of the production process will also differ from those used in sewage treatment. The last application field is for daily necessities, where the indicator requirements would be even higher. Generally, spray-dried process produced polychloride aluminium is used, with higher standards in terms of water solubility speed, pH value control, and raw material standards, naturally leading to higher prices.

In summary, its application fields include:

1. Urban water purification: river water, reservoir water, groundwater.

2. Industrial water purification.

3. Urban water treatment.

4. Recovery of useful substances from industrial wastewater and residues, promoting the settling of coal powder in coal washing wastewater, starch recovery in starch production.

5. Treatment of various industrial wastewaters: printing and dyeing wastewater, leather wastewater, fluoride-containing wastewater, heavy metal wastewater, oil-containing wastewater, coal washing wastewater, mine wastewater, brewing wastewater, metallurgical wastewater, meat processing wastewater.

6. Wastewater treatment.

7. Paper sizing.

8. Sugar solution refining.

9. Casting molding.

10. Anti-wrinkle for fabrics.

11. Catalyst carrier.

12. Medicine refining.

13. Cement quick-setting.

14. Cosmetic raw materials.

The usage method of polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html]:

The dosage of polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html] is generally determined based on actual conditions. It is usually recommended to first conduct a small-scale experiment to determine the actual dosage. Repeat several times and test the results for comparison. Finally, obtain the optimal dosing result.

The general dosage is: 20-100 grams per ton for liquid product, 5-30 grams per ton for solid. If small-scale trials are conducted before dosing, the effect will be better.

Under normal circumstances, it is best to first dilute the solid polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html] with water and stir it into a liquid polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html], then apply it for better effectiveness and utilization rate!

Storage of polychloride aluminium [http://www.watercl.com/Apac.html]:

Liquid: It is recommended to prepare and use immediately, do not store for too long to avoid reducing its efficacy.

Solid: Store in a dry warehouse, prevent moisture, ventilate, and keep away from heat. Shelf life is one year. Longer storage periods may reduce its effective content.

Do not mix or store with toxic and harmful substances.
