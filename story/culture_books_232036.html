"Graceful Commoner's Daughter" - A Fierce Woman from the Modern World Starts a Family War in Ancient Times

by jqrxass on 2009-09-02 10:57:05

"Parents' command and matchmaker's words," even Yin Tianliang, who has traveled through time, must abide by this rule.

I can pretend to be silly because my brain was struck by lightning when I came through. I have to pretend to be silly because the owner of this body was originally a bit silly... Embarrassing. In order not to let the smart ones find out, in order to ensure my life of luxury in the Prince's mansion, I am adapting to the ancient life with a dull face but a cunning heart.

Until one day, I met him, who is as "cold as marble" despite our seemingly matched families... The nightmare began! I'm still young, I don't want to have too much contact with him; I'm still young, I don't want to have a baby so soon. But how could my fairy-tale-like mother-in-law let me go? Three tricks a day, every trick hits the mark, even if I am naturally tough, it's hard for me to escape from my mother-in-law's loving "clutches."

Finally, I still have to share my husband with other women. Following the principle that I don't want to use public toilets and I don't want to take public buses, I fight back fiercely to defend my status! The curtain rises for me, watch the continuous good show, I am striving for love and life in ancient times.

Graceful Concubine - Part One

Prince Zhan is suave and romantic, although deeply in love with his principal wife, his flirtatious nature hasn't changed, and he has taken several secondary wives. However, both of Prince Zhan's sons are born to the principal wife. The eldest son, Yin Tianling, is enfeoffed as heir according to regulations and will inherit the title. The second son, Yin Tianlin, is very similar to his father, passionate about music and poetry, disliking vulgar things. The elder daughter, Yin Tianjing, and the younger daughter, Yin Tianning, were both born to secondary wives and have lively personalities. The youngest daughter, Yin Tianliang, was also born to a secondary wife, but she lost her mother at birth and was raised by the principal wife. Since childhood, Yin Tianliang has been quiet and reserved. After being struck by lightning a few months ago and waking up from a coma, she became even quieter. For a while, Prince Zhan and his wife suspected that Yin Tianliang had been struck dumb by the lightning.

Graceful Concubine - Part Two

Time passed quickly, and it was said that there were only a few more days until the wedding. Princess Zhan confined her in the mansion, claiming that the nannies would teach her some things. The meaning of "things" was indeed broad, but she had a dirty thought - wanting to know how people in ancient times popularized "wedding night knowledge" before their daughters got married.

Unfortunately, no one paid attention to her dirty thoughts, and all they taught her were regular things, cultivating her towards the direction of a wealthy noblewoman.

Finally, it was the day before the wedding, and Princess Zhan mysteriously arrived, sitting across from Yin Tianliang and looking at her. Yin Tianliang swallowed her saliva secretly, the mysterious expression of Mother Princess was... unfathomable.

Graceful Concubine - Part Three

Thinking of Mulan, Yin Tianliang finally remembered an important question and asked her mother-in-law.

"Mother, can the women of our dynasty go to the battlefield?" What an enlightened dynasty.

The beautiful eyes of the princess consort looked at her for a while before smiling and asking: "Is Coolie (Tianliang's nickname) concerned about Miss Huo? Hehe..."

What a misunderstanding! Clearly, she was paying attention to state affairs, but here it became a private matter of men and women in her mother-in-law's eyes. Am I that narrow-minded? NO, of course not.
