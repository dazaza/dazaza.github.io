Details on the Precautions for Car Beauty and Decoration

The period before the Chinese New Year each year is the peak season for consumers to buy cars. Besides the necessary purchase tax, registration, and insurance, many car owners also like to decorate their new cars well. Tinting, laying down floor mats, seat covers... These have almost become "essential" actions for many new car owners these days.

However, this has its pros and cons. It's unclear how much benefit it can bring to your beloved car. What are the precautions regarding car washing, beauty care, and decoration?

According to industry insiders, if not done carefully, decorating a new car can actually harm it, especially in cold climates during winter, when it is even less suitable for car decoration. This is related to the car parts, as low winter temperatures can easily cause them to crack.

Laying down floor mats can damage plastic components.

Many new car owners worry that the original carpet of the car will get dirty and hard to clean, so after buying a car, they always rush to lay down a set of floor mats in their new car. In fact, this practice is completely unnecessary. Laying down floor mats requires dismantling all the seats and reinstalling them. However, the assembly skills of workers at ordinary auto accessory stores cannot compare with those of professional workers on the production line.

In winter, the lower temperature reduces the elasticity of plastic parts, making it easy to break or crack them during installation. If some small parts are damaged, it might not be easily noticed at first glance, but from a long-term perspective, it’s definitely not good for the vehicle. Additionally, since floor mats are waterproof but not breathable, once water gets between the car floor and the mat, the owner may find it difficult to detect promptly, which could lead to corrosion of the car floor. Moreover, some cheap floor mats, due to substandard quality, contain excessive toxic substances, which can easily cause air pollution inside the car.

The Worst DIY Decoration Methods for Car Owners Doing Their Own Car Beautification

Everyone wants their car to look more beautiful and unique. Most car owners like to work on car beautification. After a makeover, one wonders if their car has changed for the better or turned into something unrecognizable. Currently, there are four popular but terrible DIY methods we can review.

First DIY method for car beautification: Cheap undercarriage lights

Almost all undercarriage lights seen domestically are failures of modified cars. The more modifications, the worse they get.

Second DIY method for car beautification: Ugly body stickers

If you want to make your beloved car more personalized, beautiful, and fashionable, body stickers are one of the simplest and most effective ways. You can buy them online or at professional car decoration shops and directly stick them on the car body for an extraordinary look—cute, fashionable, and tasteful. But some people like to put outdated tattoo-style stickers on their cars, which lack any aesthetic appeal in color coordination. The gangster-like patterns on the car are truly incomprehensible. One can understand the desire of these car owners to look cool, but they lack any sense of aesthetics.

Third DIY method for car beautification: Using perfume in old or low-end cars

Choose authentic perfumes; though they may cost only a few dollars to produce, you might end up paying hundreds for them. You might think they look chic and great, but actually, the smell of such perfumes is quite unpleasant.

Fourth DIY method for car beautification: Placing a badge on the car hood

Whether it's a car worth 10 million yuan or just a few ten thousand, car owners treat their vehicles like treasures. This sentiment is understandable. Nowadays, many car owners like to imitate high-end cars by adding a badge to their own cars... I've personally seen rusted badges standing upright on car hoods. You might fool people who don't know much about cars, but if you encounter professionals, your image as a car owner will suffer greatly. Is your car like this? You should replace it immediately.
