On the 25th, Qi Na released another new sexy photo. The female protagonist is Zheng Rong.

by baoway on 2008-04-27 20:11:02

Ki Na reappeared on April 25 and released new nude photos. There were also rumors that Cheng Rong was involved in the Nude Photo Scandal. Analysts said that Ki Na did this to remind the public that the door of the Nude Photo Scandal would not be closed for a while. Although Ki Na reappeared, neither Edison Chen nor the Hong Kong police made any response.

Will Cheng Rong be implicated in the Nude Photo Scandal?

According to reports, on the Hong Kong Golden Forum where the nude photos were first posted, someone announced on the 25th that new nude photos would be leaked at 7 PM. Subsequently, two posts appeared indicating the release of nude photos, one of which specifically mentioned Hong Kong celebrity Cheng Rong. However, shortly afterward, the link address posted in the thread was deleted.

In fact, although the climax of the Nude Photo Scandal has passed, there are still ripples. On the morning of the 24th, Ki Na again posted several nude photos on the Hong Kong Golden Forum but deleted them within ten minutes. Netizens said that Ki Na this time released very few photos, and they were only of Chen Wenyuan. The content was very similar to what had been previously disclosed, and it could be seen at a glance that they were taken from a video. However, this batch of nude photos did not spread externally. A few netizens who claimed to have seen the photos later changed their statements, saying that this batch was exactly the same as before. Therefore, the authenticity of these photos and whether they had never been released is still in doubt.

Although Ki Na reappeared recently, neither Edison Chen nor the Hong Kong police made any response. It was reported that Hong Kong will hold a trial in October this year regarding the Nude Photo Scandal. The defendant is Shi Mou, who the police believe is the source of the photo releases, and Edison Chen will testify in court at that time. Thereafter, it was rumored that he would go to Africa for charity work.

Regarding the Nude Photo Scandal, the Hong Kong police believed that the source of the releases was computer company employee Shi Kejuan. He was suspected of "dishonestly using a computer." Currently, he has been granted bail and is scheduled to be examined on October 6. The prosecution has asked Edison Chen to return to Hong Kong to serve as a witness for the prosecution. It is understood that Edison Chen does not resist testifying in court but hopes to ensure his personal safety. The case is expected to be tried for four days starting from October 6. A spokesperson for the Hong Kong Red Hall agency where Edison Chen is based said: "If necessary, we are willing to consider helping contact Edison Chen himself to testify."

Edison Chen once stated in his last public appearance that he would do more charity work in the future. On the 25th, it was reported that he had already prepared under the arrangement of his American agent to fulfill his promise. By the end of the year, he would go to Africa to visit local poor people and experience the suffering of children lacking food. The person in charge of Edison Chen's Hong Kong agency said they were unaware of his charitable trip to Africa. He indicated that Edison Chen's decision could be implemented by himself, and there was no need to notify the Hong Kong company.

There were also reports that last week, Edison Chen personally went to the studio of his good friend Jeff Ng in New York. Jeff posted a set of photos of Edison Chen on his blog. The location was his studio in New York, and he wrote an article titled "The Truth" subtly mentioning the Nude Photo Scandal. Jeff said that when Edison Chen visited that day, the two discussed "the truth" for quite a while. He pointed out that actually, not many people had ever thought about the whole event and said, "No one really knows, and no one needs to know." In the photos, Edison Chen wore a cold hat. Although he held chocolate to cover his mouth, he was clearly smiling.
