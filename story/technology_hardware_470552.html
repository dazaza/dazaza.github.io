Galaxy GTS450 Transformers Edition

The Galaxy GTS450 Transformers Edition is based on the new 40nm GF106 display core, using a 4-phase fully solid-state power supply. It has 512MB DDR5 high-speed memory onboard, with a default factory frequency of up to 783/3608MHz. Both performance and stability are very ideal. The latest market price is only 669 yuan, making it suitable for students and home users. Recently, Galaxy has upgraded its products and launched the Transformers 3 Collector's Edition, which is worth paying attention to.

GTS450 Transformers Edition is based on the 40nm GF106 core, featuring 192 stream processors, with a core frequency of up to 783MHz. In terms of memory, it provides four Samsung 0.5ns GDDR5 particles, forming a 512MB/128bit specification. The core frequency is 783MHz, the stream processor frequency is 1566MHz, and the memory frequency is 3608MHz. The high-spec parameters achieve excellent performance.

In terms of power design, the GTS450 Transformers Edition adopts a mature 3+1 phase separated power design for the core/memory, all using fully enclosed inductors and Japanese Fujitsu solid capacitors. Each phase power supply is equipped with multiple low-temperature MOSFETs, providing pure and sufficient current. The graphics card also features a 6-pin external power interface, providing continuous and sufficient power to the card.

For heat dissipation design, the GTS450 Transformers Edition uses a new Black Whirlwind cooler, equipped with an oversized painted fan, offering more adequate airflow. The central Transformers label on the fan is particularly prominent. The red perforated brackets on both sides enhance the appearance significantly. The mesh structure provides good heat dissipation conditions for the graphics card, greatly improving heat dissipation efficiency.

Rich output interfaces make the Transformers Edition more versatile. Galaxy GTS450 Transformers Edition considers users comprehensively, equipping HDMI/DVI/VGA full series interfaces to meet the needs of users at different levels. Since the GTS450 already integrates a sound card unit and supports raw code output, it has significant advantages in building an HD gaming platform.
