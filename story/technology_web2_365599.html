Domestic online movie ticket booking service recommendation

by ybcz0519 on 2010-09-22 16:21:42

Currently, group-buying websites are very popular domestically, and with the hot screening of the movie "Inception," group-buying of movie tickets has also become a target for these websites. In my memory, group-buying websites like Meituan and Dianping have all launched group-buying deals for movie tickets. From my personal experience of group-buying movie tickets, I actually think that movie tickets are not suitable for group-buying. Firstly, for the cinema, while organizing group-buying for movie tickets, the tickets are still sold normally, so when you go to exchange your ticket, you will find it very hard to get tickets for Saturday or Sunday shows. Therefore, group-buying of movie tickets is more like selling tickets for non-peak times or non-popular movies (if you can't get tickets for peak times, you'll have to choose non-peak times or non-popular movies). Secondly, after group-buying, there is still quite a bit of time cost involved, as you first need to exchange for a voucher, then exchange for the movie ticket. So, is there a better way to buy movie tickets? Apparently, there is. Today, I will recommend some online movie ticket booking services that I personally think are good. Users can directly book seats for a specific theater and showtime online, without having to exchange tickets at the cinema, which is very convenient.

1. Wangpiao.com

Wangpiao.com is the first and only nationwide online platform for selecting seats and purchasing movie tickets. It fully realizes an all-self-service operation mode of viewing movie schedules online/mobile, self-selecting seats, online banking payment, and self-checking tickets. You can directly book movie tickets for a specified cinema and showtime within three days online. Afterwards, a digital string will be sent via mobile text message, and on the day of watching the movie, you can print the ticket at the equipment at the cinema entrance and enter the theater directly.

Maximum number of tickets per purchase: 5

Payment methods: Online banking, Wangpiao Movie Pass, Wangpiao Exchange Voucher

Supported cities: Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, Tianjin, Kunming, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Foshan

Mobile client: None

Recommendation rating: ★★★★★

Link: http://www.wangpiao.com/

2. Gewara

Gewara Life Network (Gewara) is dedicated to using digital networks to complement real life, providing consumers with wonderful city life services beyond clothing, food, housing, and transportation. It is the first domestic life network platform that integrates information, user community, and merchant interaction. Currently, Gewara includes life platforms such as cinemas, KTVs, gyms, bars, and sports venues. It not only provides basic information about merchants' names, addresses, phone numbers, pictures, etc., but also integrates dynamic information such as cinema schedules, gym course tables, KTV price lists, bar activities, etc. Users can also comment and evaluate merchants or movies, events, etc., organize activities, and arrange their lives. The movie ticket booking method is the same as Wangpiao.com (detailed introduction).

Maximum number of tickets per purchase: 5

Payment methods: Account balance, Alipay, online banking

Supported cities: Shanghai

Mobile client: None

Recommendation rating: ★★★★★

Link: http://www.gewara.com/

3. Hipiao.com

Hipiao.com is an e-commerce website for online ticket purchases. Currently, it mainly provides online movie ticket sales services for many cinemas. There are many listed supported cities on the website, but after trying personally, I found that there is no booking information for many cities. However, this should be the only online booking service that currently provides a smartphone app.

Maximum number of tickets per purchase: 8

Payment methods: Alipay, UnionPay

Supported cities: Some cities in Shanghai, Tianjin, Chongqing, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Henan, Hubei, etc.

Mobile client: iPhone app (download)

Recommendation rating: ★★★☆☆

Link: http://www.hipiao.com/

4. Himovie.net

Himovie.net is currently an online movie ticket booking service for Shanghai. Its service model is online booking plus offline ticket collection. After purchasing online, you can complete ticket collection by entering your mobile phone number + ticket collection password at the ticket terminal machine.

Maximum number of tickets per purchase: 4

Payment methods: Balance payment, online banking, Alipay

Supported cities: Shanghai

Mobile client: None

Recommendation rating: ★★★★☆

Link: http://www.himovie.net/Index.aspx

5. Ruzuo.com

Ruzuo.com may not belong to the same category of websites as the four mentioned above, but I personally think it could be a very good way to "group-buy" movie tickets. On Ruzuo.com, users can initiate a specific movie session at a certain time (currently generally on Saturdays and Sundays). Users can select a movie and initiate a group to watch the movie, join someone else's group, or invite friends to join. Once the specified number of people is reached, the corresponding session will be arranged. I think this kind of "group-buy" way might be a better one.

Cooperating cinemas: Dongsi Workers' Cultural Palace, China Film Archive, JinDian Garden Cinema, French Cultural Center Cinema Hall, Bona YouTang Cinema

Recommendation rating: ★★★★☆

Link: http://www.ruzuo.com/
