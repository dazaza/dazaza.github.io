Germany Sunel - Suggests that entrepreneurs examine more about the market influence of chain brands.

by sunny467 on 2009-11-26 14:24:54

Franchising is a type of business operation model that has become popular worldwide. In recent years, the development of franchise chain alliances has been rapid. In the United States, in recent years, more than half of the total social retail sales have been achieved through the franchise operation method. There is a new franchise opening every 8 minutes with a success rate of 95%, which is several times higher than other operating methods. In China, this business model has also gained market recognition. The booming scene of the 2009 Seventh Nanjing International Franchise Chain and Large-scale Entrepreneurship Project Exhibition held on November 15 in Nanjing can be seen from the participation of over a hundred enterprises across the country in goods wholesale, home appliances, catering, food, finance, education, and other sectors. Among these, the water purifier industry, which has received much attention in recent years as a sunrise industry, is favored by entrepreneurs and investors.

The water purifier industry has great market potential. Human production and living activities result in large amounts of industrial, commercial, and domestic waste being discharged into water bodies, contaminating water sources. Currently, about 420 billion cubic meters of sewage are discharged into rivers, lakes, and seas worldwide each year, contaminating 5.5 billion cubic meters of fresh water, equivalent to more than 14% of the global river runoff. The situation in our country is even less optimistic. Water pollution not only exacerbates the shortage of water resources but also seriously threatens human health due to water quality deterioration.

As water pollution becomes increasingly severe and consumers pay more attention to drinking water hygiene issues, huge market demand will inevitably bring great business opportunities. The advantage of water purifiers lies in: they are cheaper than bottled water, and before installation, only a large amount of water saving and electricity saving is required, making them safer and healthier compared to bottled water. In recent years, with the rise of water purifiers, they are taking over the market share of water dispensers and becoming a traditional household water appliance. Choosing a powerful brand with good product quality is the key to investment. Flexible and diverse business cooperation models, systematic and comprehensive market support plans, these are the guarantees for obtaining rich profits. According to market research by German Aqua-Pur, the success rate of individual entrepreneurs is less than 5%, while joining a sunrise industry for chain operation can achieve a success rate of 95%. For those who want to start their own business, choosing a sunrise industry is equal to being halfway successful. Furthermore, choosing a well-known and reputable brand will bring success within reach.

In recent years, the German water purifier market has developed rapidly, growing at almost double the speed each year. Whoever enters this industry early will have the opportunity to gain substantial profits in the market. German Aqua-Pur provides various business cooperation models such as regional agency, industry agency, retail stores, consignment points, trade competitors, and part-time marketing for individuals, offering suitable cooperation support for more business partners. Additionally, German Aqua-Pur, through long-term exploration, offers advertising support, store construction support, standardized channel management, and systematic training, providing investors with a systematic and comprehensive market support plan.

Finally, experts also advise investors to avoid fraud risks in project selection. They should focus on evaluating projects from a commercial perspective rather than acting impulsively. It is suggested that entrepreneurs examine more aspects of chain brands, including market influence, management level, corporate reputation, and product technology, comprehensively understand industry information, and carefully choose the franchise brand.
