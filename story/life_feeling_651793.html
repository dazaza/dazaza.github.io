The Coolness of Autumn, Listening to the Lute under the Moonlit Night

Stop Online Submission

Wandering along the way, bumping into each other, still lingering in this season of autumn.

Listening to the rain, so light, soft like cotton, as if it doesn't want to fall on the ground, this ground with its riot of flowers and worldly distractions.

As the seasons change, a gust of wind, a shower of rain, a full moon, all deeply herald the arrival of autumn. An autumn night is always a time for reminiscence. With the bright moon, the night is no longer so dim. Do you know, is it sorrow, is it pain, is it emotion, or is it parting?

In life, there are always a few moments when we often reminisce about the past点滴. In life, there are always a few moments when we shed tears while singing and walking. Perhaps I am not strong enough, perhaps I am too sentimental. When I face the bright moon alone, I can't help but reminisce, my heart rising and falling with past events. Maybe a smile that appears in the picture will make me feel beautiful for a while.

If the encounters in life are blessings from past lives, then I don't ask that we preserve the beauty of our first meeting. I only wish that every time we meet, your smile will be deeply etched in my eyes.

Autumn has come, the weather has cooled, the moon is bright, have people changed?

Heaven! Just tonight, this night with the bright moon hanging high, melt away my longing for my friends! So many worries, so much sorrow, so much laughter, so many tears, I want to tell you one by one, but can you still hear my cries?

I always thought "friends walk together all their lives," I always misunderstood this phrase, I always helplessly picked up and put down again, now I realize I've never let go, and I can't let go anymore.

Yes, I admit I am a person who tends to reminisce, and the past always seems most beautiful to me.

Yes, I admit I am someone whose heart refuses to accept reality, yet sees reality so clearly.

Yes, I admit that everything about me is not so beautiful, and my body is always occupied by sadness.

But oh moon, can you listen to my sincere heart? Listen, it is gently playing the lute for those vanished times, it is singing for those gradually distant friends, so gentle, yet so unrestrained, so passionate, yet so cold.

When the noise subsides, at some unknown turn, I can no longer see your hearts, or even your figures. I haven't had time to play the "High Mountains and Flowing Water" for you, but I hear the distant, melodious songs from afar. Are you bidding me farewell? If so, why didn't you sing "Friends, Don't Cry" to my face? If not, why does the song sound so familiar?

Autumn has turned cool, the sky is almost bright, the moon has bid farewell, but people are drifting further and further away!

Postscript:

Moon, I say it implicitly,

Please register my tears of warmth and cold,

Please record them in the ledger of sorrowful debts.

Moon, I say it choked with sobs,

Please check the drops of clear tears in the annual records,

Should they be new accounts or old debts?
