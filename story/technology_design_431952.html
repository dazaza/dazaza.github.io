The Most "Amazing" Driver on Jingzhu North: Slept soundly while driving, woke up the next day after a collision with no knowledge of the accident

The emergency call said the driver was "life or death unknown"

According to reports, the police officers of the Jingzhu North Detachment of Shaoguan Traffic Police recently received an alarm about a rear-end car accident. The driver was lying unconscious in the driver's seat, "unconscious", with "life or death unknown". It was already around 9:21 PM when two police officers immediately drove quickly to the scene. Upon arrival at the incident location, they saw that an unlicensed small car had collided with another normally traveling car ahead and then turned its head to crash into construction materials on the shoulder.

After getting out of the car, the police immediately ran to the severely damaged unlicensed small car. When they took a closer look, they were stunned: a man was leaning against the driver's seat, breathing heavily, without any visible injuries. There was also a pile of hundred-yuan bills scattered all over the driver's cabin. Could it be that the owner had suffered serious internal injuries? After carefully checking his breathing, the police found everything normal, with no signs of internal injuries. When the man began snoring loudly, the police realized with both laughter and bewilderment that the man was not injured in the accident at all - he was sleeping soundly!

No matter how the two police officers slapped or shouted, the owner just wouldn't open his eyes, peacefully sleeping sweetly in the severely damaged car.

With great effort, the police transferred the soundly sleeping driver to the police car and returned to the traffic police station for investigation. From the accident scene to the station, this involved driver remained sound asleep, and no matter what methods the police used, they couldn't wake him up to understand the situation.

In the end, the police had no choice but to send the driver to the hospital for blood tests overnight and conducted a comprehensive clearance and storage of the vehicle and money. The examination ruled out the possibility of drunk driving. The doctor told the police that he was just asleep, deeply asleep! At this point, the investigating police had no other options but to let the driver sleep fully.

The next day, the driver woke up groggily and was greatly surprised: "Why am I lying in the hospital? Wasn't I on my way home?" Completely unaware that he had been in a car accident.

It turns out that driver Liu X. was driving alone from Hunan to Dongguan that day. Due to not resting well the previous night, when he drove to the Shaoguan section of the Jinggangao Expressway, he slowly started dozing off. In the darkness, he nodded off. While deeply asleep, the car bumped into the car in front and then crashed into the shoulder. However, the impact of these serious collisions didn't wake him up, and he continued to sleep soundly with his head drooping.

After ruling out the possibility of drunk driving, the police fined Liu X. for "driving fatigue, failing to install license plates as required, and being fully responsible for the accident," and educated him on safe driving. They also returned the 58,100 yuan scattered in the car.
