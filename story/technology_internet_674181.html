Ranking of luxury carpet brands in 2012

by luomadt on 2012-02-24 11:04:33

8th Place: ITMILAN Italian Milan Carpet

The ITMILAN Italian Milan brand embraces the most avant-garde and popular styles of Milan in the design and decoration world. It showcases Milan's consistent sensuality, luxury, retro style, and wild beauty through bold color lines and elegant floral patterns. Luxurious yet simple, it combines rationality with sensibility. Through the combination of indoor carpets, it expresses the sentiment for Milan, being half classic and half noble from ancient Europe. It fully interprets modern urban home life with its exquisite and delicate designs. (Official Website: www.milanb.com[http://www.milanb.com/])

9th Place: ITRUOME Italian Rome Carpet

ITRUOME carpet integrates Italian design inspiration and color culture into Chinese residential concepts, achieving the charm of Chinese homes with unique craftsmanship and aesthetic personality. ITRUOME carpets prioritize environmental protection. Whether modern and fashionable or classic and traditional, different styles of ITRUOME carpets always make living environments lively, showcasing artistic taste and winning widespread praise from consumers, earning the title of "the most influential brand in China." (Official Website: www.luomab.com[http://www.luomab.com/])

10th Place: British Royal Elizabeth Carpet

Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Carpets and Wall Coverings (a world-class carpet and fabric brand) let art bloom and enjoy refined tastes. Royal Elizabeth completely integrates royal court design inspiration and color culture into home life concepts, achieving royal charm with unique craftsmanship and aesthetic individuality. The allure of art and the beauty of life are fully displayed by Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Carpets and Wall Coverings, showcasing noble artistic taste. (Official Website: www.elidt.com[http://www.elidt.com/])

1st Place: German ASTRA, WISSENBACH Carpet

Renowned in the international industry as "Easy care," it is one of the easiest-to-clean carpet materials, favored by "lazy people" and very suitable for home use. Just rinse with lukewarm water to wash away dust. (Official Website: www.astracarpets.co.uk[http://www.astracarpets.co.uk/])

2nd Place: Dutch ARTE ESPINA Carpet

Arte Espina is a Dutch company that both produces and designs carpets, established in 2000. Most of their carpets feature fashionable and bold styles. The company has a young and talented design team that continuously follows home life trends to create the latest carpet designs. (Official Website: www.arteespina.com[http://www.arteespina.com/])

3rd Place: Belgian Debomat Carpet

Boltcate Factory initially focused on producing velvet furniture decoration materials, mainly exporting to France and the UK. After years of growth and export experience, in the mid-1980s, Boltcate transformed into a factory producing imitation silk rugs by machine. Later, they increased investment in looms, acquiring electric jacquard machinery and combining CAD drawing design, enabling today’s Boltcate products to have rich patterns. (Official Website: www.debomat.be[http://www.debomat.be/])

4th Place: American KAS Carpet

KAS Carpet, well-known in the U.S., is headquartered in New Jersey and is a famous importer of handcrafted and machine-woven area rugs, as well as one of the main suppliers in the industry. Founded in 1981 by Rao Yarlagadda and his wife Kas, the company started as a small importer mainly selling Indian hand-spun cotton rugs. It quickly became a pioneer in carpet colors and design, maintaining a harmonious family business that many customers admire. (Official Website: www.kascarpet.net[http://www.kascarpet.net/])

5th Place: German INFLOOR Carpet

Modern interior design factors are increasingly limited by the size of indoor spaces. How can limited space be decorated properly without feeling crowded? The pattern design and color coordination of carpets can greatly change the spatial visual effect indoors. German infloor carpet has always focused on carpet design, making full carpet patterns fashionable. Infloor was founded by Ulrich Dresing in 1977, initially as a small family business with only three employees and one tufted carpet production machine. After many years of effort, infloor has become well-known in the European market for its expertise and knowledge in carpets, producing 300 million square meters of full carpet annually for global sales. (Official Website: www.infloor.de[http://www.infloor.de/])

6th Place: Iranian SETAREH KAVIR Group

Setareh Kavir Group is a well-known carpet producer in Iran, owning five companies: Setareh Kavir Yazd and Khatereh Kavir Yazd (mainly producing carpets), Alyaf Yazd (mainly producing yarn), Nassaji Yazd (mainly producing textiles on the back of carpets), and Rangrazi Yazd (mainly responsible for dyeing). (Official Website: www.setarehkavir.com[http://www.setarehkavir.com/])

7th Place: English Ryalux Carpet

Ryalux is the leader in the UK's carpet production reform. Located in England, Ryalux was created by William Lomas in 1970 and primarily focuses on producing high-quality broadloom carpets. The types of carpets offered include loop pile, twisted yarn, velvet, tight-twist fine velvet, and tight-twist long hair series. (Official Website: www.ryalux.com[http://www.ryalux.com/])
