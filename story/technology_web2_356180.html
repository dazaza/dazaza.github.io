A Change in Temperament: Zhang Bai Zhi Converts to Christianity in 2004

As the new year begins, Zhang Bai Zhi has shown a change from her previously irritable temperament. She has openly expressed to the media her recent conversion to Christianity and is already arranging for baptism. She hopes to completely rid herself of her old habit of driving too fast.

Before the New Year, Zhang Bai Zhi participated in the "Hong Kong-Macau Full of Joy" countdown event, with her mother, three younger brothers, and many relatives present in support, creating a lively atmosphere. However, she was dressed all in black as a tribute to Anita Mui. Zhang Bai Zhi said, "Sister Mei was very good to me and cared for me deeply. I believe she is now in a happy place."

The sudden loss of a beloved senior figure made Zhang Bai Zhi realize that she should cherish those around her. Therefore, her New Year's wish, besides living a simple life, is for the people around her to remain by her side. If possible, she also hopes to meet a boyfriend who truly loves her. She said, "In 2003, many unhappy things happened, but I managed to get through them. I really don't want to see more people who care about me leave or become unhealthy. I hope they can all be happy for a thousand years, ten thousand years."

Anita Mui's funeral will be held on the 12th day, and Zhang Bai Zhi said that even if she has work that day, she would postpone it to send off this beloved senior one last time. Under the guidance of her hairdresser, Zhang Bai Zhi has converted to Christianity and is currently arranging the date for her baptism. She says that since believing in Jesus, she has not only learned to love and embrace others but also reads the Bible every night before sleeping. After having her driver's license suspended due to speeding, she has decided to quit this bad habit after getting it back. With twelve cars at home, she plans to buy a new car worth 600,000 HKD as a gift for regaining her license. This time, she will install a speedometer to remind herself not to drive too fast.

No one is perfect. Romans 7:18 says, "For I know that good does not dwell in me, that is, in my flesh. The desire to do what is good is within me, but I cannot carry it out." We know that Zhang Bai Zhi, like all of us, has taken wrong paths and sinned. Another Christian artist, Cherie Cheung, once said, "Christians are not saints." Yes, the most important thing for Christians is not strict adherence to rules, but the ability to recognize and correct mistakes! Psalm 51:17 says, "God, a broken and contrite heart you will not despise." In Luke 15:4, Jesus says, "What man among you, having a hundred sheep, loses one of them and does not leave the ninety-nine in the wilderness to go after the one that is lost until he finds it?" And in Luke 15:10, Jesus says, "I tell you, there is joy before the angels of God over one sinner who repents."

We believe that Zhang Bai Zhi is now a reborn true Christian, not just a nominal one. We believe that she can stand up and glorify the name of God, which means that God is starting to use His vessel. Let us pray more for Sister Zhang Bai Zhi and other Christian artists who have committed their lives to Christ. The entertainment industry is a place where Satan runs rampant and is a vortex of temptation. It is not easy for Christian artists to remain holy and separate, which requires our prayers and the courage of more Christian artists to stand up and declare, "Jesus is the victorious King!" Satan will surely fail!

May God further protect His children in these end times, and may He greatly bless Zhang Bai Zhi, her husband, and her children! Amen!

Zhang Bai Zhi's Career Entry:

In mid-1998, after returning from high school graduation in Australia, Zhang Bai Zhi, under the arrangement of her agent, filmed the advertisement for "Sunshine Lemon Tea". When different versions of the ad were broadcast, various magazines, film companies, and advertising agencies contacted her agent. Among them, Stephen Chow firmly chose Zhang Bai Zhi as the lead actress for his 1999 Lunar New Year film "King of Comedy", which eventually became the box office champion of the New Year films, grossing 30 million Hong Kong dollars. Later, Zhang Bai Zhi starred in the romantic drama "Eternity" produced by Golden Harvest Film Company and directed by Marco Mak, alongside Richie Jen and Aaron Su. This film was released during the summer vacation in August last year and its box office exceeded 10 million. This solidified Cecilia's status in the film industry as both a singer and an actress.

In terms of music, Zhang Bai Zhi officially signed with Universal Music on June 8, 1999. Her first EP "Any Weather" was launched on July 26, 1999, and received an enthusiastic response, quickly reaching platinum status in just a few weeks. In terms of advertisements, Bai Zhi became the spokesperson for "Marks & Spencer", "French Wedding Gown", and others, and during the release period of her first EP, she filmed the Japanese "Kanebo Cotore" skincare series advertisement, achieving very ideal results. Believing in her continued multi-faceted development, her future is boundless.
