Hospital's original dean was exposed for indecent photos and then being put under double regulation (Photo)

by aia7892c on 2012-02-10 16:59:03

The former president of the Second People's Hospital of Liaocheng was involved in a "sex photo scandal." Pictures from Huasheng Online. On November 27, the event involving the former president of the Second People's Hospital of Liaocheng (hereinafter referred to as the Second Hospital) has continued to attract attention. This website learned that the former president Zhou某某 has been placed under "shuanggui" (a form of investigative custody).

Regarding this "sex photo scandal" in Liaocheng, this website repeatedly called Zhou某某's mobile phone and home phone, but no one answered.

Subsequently, we contacted the Discipline Inspection Commission of Liaocheng City for updates. Director Li of the commission office stated that the city's Discipline Inspection Commission indeed intervened in the investigation, but specific details were not convenient to disclose, and when there would be a conclusion was also "uncertain."

Since the address of the Second Hospital is in Linqing, we then called the Publicity Department of the Linqing Municipal Committee to attempt an interview. A deputy minister of the publicity department provided the phone number of a person in charge of online affairs surnamed Yuan. After explaining our intentions, the person in charge said they were unaware of the "sex photo scandal" and the leadership had not instructed them on it. When asked for the publicity department's view on the matter, the person in charge said that personally they thought it was a personal issue of Zhou某某, and the publicity department would not comment.

We then contacted the current president of the Second Hospital, President Wang. President Wang informed us that the Liaocheng City Discipline Inspection Commission had already made a decision and announced that Zhou某某 was under shuanggui. The hospital will comply with the instructions and arrangements of the higher-level departments and actively cooperate with the investigation work of the disciplinary inspection department. We subsequently recontacted the Liaocheng City Discipline Inspection Commission, where a staff member confirmed to us that Zhou某某 was indeed under shuanggui.

Later, we called the head of the Publicity Section of the Liaocheng City Health Bureau to understand the health department's views on the matter, but the responsible person did not answer the call. (Yin Zilong, Qingquan)

[News Review]

[Exposure] Former President of a Hospital in Shandong Gets Involved in a "Sex Photo Scandal"

At 10:02 am on November 22, a poster named "Leak Bar" posted a thread titled "Shandong Again Exposes Sex Photo Scandal; Passionate Photos of the President of the Second People's Hospital of Liaocheng Leaked," accompanied by five pictures.

In the pictures, a shirtless elderly man embraces a bespectacled woman for a selfie. The poster claimed that after identification by informed individuals, the man in the picture is the former president of the Second People's Hospital of Liaocheng, Mr. Zhou. It was also revealed that years ago, a video of Zhou with certain young women in hotel rooms was made into a disc and spread, which was later smoothed over by Zhou.

Netizens found a front-facing photo of Zhou that was once published in news reports, and upon comparison, discovered a high degree of consistency between the image in the "sex photos" and the elderly man in question. "This time, for unknown reasons, similar content has leaked again. It is also unclear whether it is related to the disc incident?" The poster raised questions in the post.

[Investigation] Posting Netizens Refuse Interview

Zhou's "sex photo scandal" immediately drew the attention of many netizens, who claimed "this post will go viral." Some netizens questioned the authenticity of the post and the purpose of posting it, but an anonymous netizen revealed that "heard there is something even more explosive, Tarceva B."

Through a search engine, we found that the first to post the thread was netizen "zqx921." At around 21:16 pm on November 21, the netizen successively posted the pictures to the "Linqing Bar." Once the post was out, some netizens suspected that the poster intended to "extort money," to which "zqx921" responded, "I take full responsibility for the text. If it involves extortion or is inaccurate, welcome to report it."

We learned that netizen "zqx921" was once a bar manager who was stripped of management privileges on May 27, 2011, for failing to properly manage the bar duties.

In the morning of November 22, the posts with images published by the netizen could no longer be viewed, showing "deleted." Subsequently, when Huasheng Online journalists sent an interview request to the netizen, they replied that they would not accept interviews and stated that the post was also reposted.

[Response] The Party Involved Has Already Retired Last Year

In the afternoon of November 22, Huasheng Online reporters called the Second People's Hospital of Liaocheng City. Director Zhang of the hospital’s publicity center said that President Zhou retired in September last year and hadn't been seen for a long time.

"This is a very meaningless act." Regarding the "sex photo scandal" circulating online, Director Zhang refused to verify online if the man in the photos was President Zhou due to being busy with year-end work, only stating that President Zhou's work at the hospital over the years has been remarkable.

Huasheng Online reporters searched online for relevant information about Zhou. He is a chief physician specializing in cardiovascular medicine, serving as the president of the hospital since 2002 and was named an outstanding president of Shandong Province in 2006. (According to Huasheng Online)
