"The DVD clear version of Iron Man"

by fenxiangkuaile on 2008-05-13 23:24:07

The movie tells the story of Tony Stark (played by Robert Downey Jr.), who was born into a wealthy family in New York. Unlike many spoiled rich kids who only know how to enjoy themselves and cause trouble, Tony Stark is exceptionally talented. At the age of 15, he entered the Department of Electrical Engineering at MIT and, with his outstanding performance, successfully found his place in society — as the new boss of his family business, "Stark Arms Company." The unfortunate death of his parents instead spurred Tony's motivation for advancing his career. At the age of 21, he took control of billions of dollars in assets and gradually established Stark Company as the top arms supplier to the U.S. military. However, Tony's arrogant and self-important personality, along with his willingness to use any means necessary to achieve his goals, often caused him a lot of trouble. Fortunately, he had a smart, capable, and charming female assistant, Virginia Potts (played by Gwyneth Paltrow), who always provided him with meticulous care and help.

At the same time, Obadiah Stane (played by Jeff Bridges), a villain who has long coveted Stark's wealth and lifestyle, could no longer contain himself. Taking advantage of Tony's kidnapping, he caused chaos within Stark Arms Company, employing every trick in the book and temporarily isolating Tony Stark from his friends and allies. With his cunning, Stane finally seized power and even took possession of the "Iron" armor that belonged to Stark, who had become addicted to alcohol, and gave it to his good friend Jim Rhodes. With the help of a group of scientists, Stane eventually used the "Iron" armor on himself. Thus, the main villain of the story now had the reasons and confidence to do as he pleased. What he never expected was that Tony Stark had secretly developed a more advanced and powerful new generation of "Iron" armor. And thus, the true "Iron Man" of the movie was born.

The movie then recounts how a subsequent kidnapping incident completely changed the life of Tony Stark. When Tony led a group of subordinates and military observers to test his latest invention in a desolate area, they were unexpectedly attacked by a group of extremely fierce terrorists. In the ensuing chaos, Tony was hit in the heart by shrapnel from an explosion. Upon waking up, he discovered a strange device implanted in his chest, which was keeping him alive. Naturally, the terrorists who saved him weren't just interested in capturing this weapons design expert; they had deliberately sought him out due to his fame. The terrorists then began using both persuasion and coercion, demanding that Tony Stark create even more powerful weapons for them. A wise man doesn't fight a losing battle, and the clever Stark certainly wouldn't sit idly by. Using the crude equipment and materials provided by the terrorists, he built himself a suit of invincible iron armor in the dark, sunless underground base, which possessed strong combat capabilities. In the fierce battle against the terrorists, he successfully escaped from their base and met an injured U.S. Army helicopter pilot, Jim Rhodes (played by Terrence Howard), along the way. Together, they managed to escape. After returning home, Tony Stark and Jim Rhodes became close friends. And he himself acquired a new identity — Iron Man!
