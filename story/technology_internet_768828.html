Meitu Kiss Phone New Model Released, to be Revealed on November 26th

by anonymous on 2013-11-25 11:23:07

At 9:30 this morning, the official Weibo account of Meitu Mobile released a new announcement: "Stay tuned for November 26th!" Judging from the accompanying image, it is undoubtedly a new smartphone model. Will it be the Meitu Kiss 1S or the Meitu Kiss 2? Based on the slogan in the picture, "The king of selfies, perfectly evolved!", there may be another breakthrough in pixel technology.

The phone's specifications and price are still unclear, so let's first revisit the first-generation Meitu Kiss smartphone:

On May 16th this year, the long-rumored Meitu XiuXiu smartphone —— Meitu Kiss —— was officially launched. The standout feature of this device is its front-facing 8-megapixel camera, making it the world's first smartphone with an 8-megapixel front-facing camera.

As a phone specifically designed for beauty-enhanced selfies, it uses a smart beautification technology developed exclusively for Asian women (with adjustable 7-level beautification effects for selfies). It can instantly whiten the skin, reduce dark circles, brighten eyes, moisturize lips, and refine facial features. Through deep optimization at the system level, it achieves driver-level rather than application-level development. This not only allows for intelligent photo optimization and facial recognition-based beautification but also enables real-time previewing of beautification effects.

In terms of other configurations, the device is powered by a 1.2GHz MTK6589 quad-core processor, with 1GB of RAM and 8GB of internal storage. The rear camera also has an 8-megapixel sensor. It runs on Android 4.2, features a removable 1800mAh battery, and is priced at 2199 yuan.
