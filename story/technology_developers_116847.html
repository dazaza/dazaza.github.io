Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd.

by 313754597 on 2009-01-05 10:51:04

Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd. is registered with the Huaihua Administration for Industry and Commerce. It is a specialized company that undertakes the design, production, and installation of large-scale steel structure factories, warehouses, box girders, multi-story steel structures, trusses, overpasses, crane projects, and mobile activity houses on a general contracting basis. The company is located at No. Wuling South Road, Huaihua (within Huaihua Railway Design Institute).

Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd. has a team of veteran engineers specializing in steel building design as well as a group of experienced construction technicians who can provide customers with a complete quality assurance system from design and production to construction and installation. Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd. also owns its own large-scale steel material processing plant capable of producing various types of H-beams, C-beams, box beams, color plates, floor slabs, PU foam partition boards, colored corrugated sheets, sandwich panels, ERP light-transmitting panels, and other steel structure materials. This forms an integrated service system combining steel structure engineering design, production, and on-site installation.

Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd. always adheres to the service principle of "integrity as the foundation", establishing its image through quality and seeking development through credibility. We sincerely cooperate with friends from all sectors of society, providing high-quality products and reliable reputation to join hands with old and new clients in making progress together and achieving brilliance.

Huaihua Asia Airlines Steel Structure Co., Ltd. - Steel structure buildings not only have short construction periods, spacious interiors, large usable areas, and novel appearances but also offer commercialized production, standardized installation, and flexible interior layouts with minimal impact and damage to energy and the environment. Due to their light weight, they require simpler civil foundation designs, reducing civil foundation construction costs and lowering overall expenses. Additionally, since the primary material used in light steel structures is steel, which is inherently robust and non-combustible, after fireproofing treatment, these structures can withstand terrible fires, earthquakes, and typhoons. Moreover, steel is recyclable, greatly saving on later maintenance costs, relocation fees, expansion fees, and factory construction expenses.
