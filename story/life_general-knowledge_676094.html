Car maintenance

by lili1 on 2012-02-25 17:38:04

As the saying goes, "three points for repair and seven points for maintenance," it is very important to maintain a car. The following six systems should be given special attention when maintaining a car:

I. Deep maintenance of the lubrication system

The main function of the lubrication system is to effectively lubricate various components of the car engine to prevent excessive wear. Under normal circumstances, every time the car travels 5000km-10000km, it needs to be cleaned and maintained once. When the engine noise is too loud, acceleration is powerless, and the water temperature is too high, it also needs to be cleaned and maintained once. Clean the oil sludge and other deposits inside the engine to avoid oxidation and thickening of the engine oil at high temperatures, reduce the wear of engine components, extend the life of the engine, and increase the power of the engine.

II. Cleaning and maintenance of the fuel system (i.e., cleaning nozzles and valve carbon deposits)

Under normal circumstances, the car needs to be cleaned and maintained once every 10,000 km - 15,000 km. Or when you find that the engine stutters, hesitates, accelerates poorly, emits black smoke, lacks power, and consumes more fuel, clean and maintain it once. Remove gum and carbon deposits from the system to prevent harmful corrosion, avoid and stop leaks in seals and radiators, and completely replace the old coolant.

III. Cleaning and maintenance of the cooling system

In general, the car should be cleaned and maintained once when the seasons change between winter and summer. During normal driving, it should be cleaned and maintained every 6 to 8 months, or when the water temperature is too high, there is leakage, or the engine boils over, clean and maintain it once. Remove traces and scale that cause the engine to overheat, prevent harmful corrosion, avoid and stop leaks in seals and radiators, and completely replace the old coolant.

IV. Cleaning and maintenance of the transmission (automatic transmission)

Under normal circumstances, the car needs to be cleaned and maintained once every 20,000 km - 25,000 km, or when the transmission slips, the water temperature is too high, the gear shifting is delayed, or the system leaks, clean and maintain it once. Remove harmful oil sludge and paint film deposits, restore the elasticity of gaskets and O-rings, make the transmission shift smoothly, improve power output, and completely replace the old automatic transmission fluid.

V. Cleaning and maintenance of the power steering system

The car needs to be cleaned and maintained once every 40,000 km - 45,000 km, or when steering is difficult, the system leaks, or after replacing power steering components, it also needs to be cleaned and maintained once. Remove harmful oil sludge and paint film from the system, clear the difficulty of steering at low temperatures, stop and prevent the leakage of power steering fluid, remove steering noise, and completely replace the old brake steering fluid.

VI. Cleaning and maintenance of the braking system

The car needs to be cleaned and maintained once every 50,000 km, or when the ABS reacts too early or too slowly, clean and maintain it once. Remove harmful oil sludge and paint film from the system, eliminate the danger of malfunction at ultra-high or ultra-low temperatures, effectively prevent the brake fluid from deteriorating and expiring, and completely replace the old brake fluid.
