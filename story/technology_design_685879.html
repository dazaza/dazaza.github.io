Woman rescuing car accident victims has her leg broken in a collision (photo group)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-01 17:27:35

The husband of the injured Wang Yuanyuan received the flowers. In the hospital, Diao Na who had a leg injury. Jiaodong Online (Weibo) reported on November 10th by reporter Jia Chuhang and correspondent Qu Shuqiang: A month ago, the incident involving Xiao Yueyue in Foshan, Guangdong province made people sigh with regret. A month later, regardless of how much time has passed, we still remember it. However, the touching and heroic event that happened in Yantai, Shandong province where a "most beautiful girl" saved an injured person amidst traffic moved people deeply and left a lasting impression. Currently, the rescuer, the rescued, and the perpetrator have formed a bond through this misfortune, using the kindness and brilliance of humanity to write a legendary and moving story on the land of the civilized city of Yantai.

Video: The girl was praised for saving an injured person lying on the ground but got her leg broken by a car. Source: Qilu Network's "Civilian Express".

-News Review: Encountering an injured person in a traffic accident, the girl got out of the car to rescue and got her leg broken.

Diao Na, 24 years old, is an employee of the Tourism Company of Nanshan Group. At around 6 p.m. on October 23rd, she and her husband were driving home after work through a narrow section of Tonghai Road in Longkou City, Yantai when they discovered a woman lying in a pool of blood in the middle of the road. It was drizzling and it was rush hour; this woman was at risk of being hit again at any moment.

To prevent the injured person from being run over again, Diao Na and her husband dialed 122 (emergency services) and 120 (medical emergency services), while standing in front of the woman and waving their hands to direct traffic. In a few minutes, more than ten vehicles avoided the scene under Diao Na's direction.

As the sky grew darker, Diao Na’s husband went back to the car to get a warning sign for more noticeable alerts. At this very moment, another unfortunate event occurred: a speeding car couldn't avoid her in time, and the car's side mirror knocked Diao Na down.

After diagnosis at the hospital, Diao Na had two fractures in her right calf, one of which was comminuted. A leg for a life, facing gratitude and praise, Diao Na said, "I just didn't want the tragedy of Xiao Yueyue to happen again, I just hope there will be no more Xiao Yueyues in the world."

-Diao Na, the rescuer: "A leg for a life, worth it!"

On November 9th, Diao Na sat on her hospital bed with a light makeup on her face, looking much more spirited. On Diao Na's bedside, a plush toy named "Little Grey" lay there with wide-open eyes. "This is a gift a friend just sent," saying this, Diao Na held "Little Grey" in her arms like a mother holding her baby.

Diao Na's mother standing nearby said, "The young couple got married in May, originally planning to have a child this month, aiming for a child born in the Year of the Dragon next year. Now they can't have the child."

When asked why she stopped to save someone on October 23rd, Diao Na paused for a moment and said: "At that moment, I saw a woman lying across the road covered in blood, immediately thinking of Xiao Yueyue, so without hesitation, I told my husband to stop the car. I felt that this injured woman needed my help."

Upon hearing that she was called "the most beautiful girl" by netizens, she shyly lowered her head, "I'm not the most beautiful girl. I just thought, if Xiao Yueyue had met me at that time, I would certainly have stepped forward to take her to the hospital. This is just to prevent the tragedy of Xiao Yueyue from happening again."

After more than half a month of treatment, Diao Na's right leg that was broken in the collision is gradually healing, but it still looks heavily scarred. She said, now she can't use much strength and can only slowly exercise with crutches, however, "a leg for a life, worth it!"

-The Rescued, Wang Yuanyuan: "You are my forever good younger sister"

The woman saved by Diao Na is named Wang Yuanyuan, 29 years old.

"She was more seriously injured, with ten broken ribs," Diao Na's father-in-law Uncle Sui said, "Yuanyuan also has a four-year-old child at home, it's really pitiful." Saying this, Uncle Sui picked up the flower basket, "She just woke up yesterday and could talk, I'll go see her again."

In the neurosurgery ward on the fourth floor, Dai Yongye, Wang Yuanyuan's husband, received the flower basket with both hands, his eyes filled with gratitude. The families of the two injured parties, one being the rescuer and the other the rescued, silently encouraged each other through the silent yet blooming flowers being passed around. "As long as we're alive, everything will be fine!"

Wang Yuanyuan lay on the hospital bed, unable to turn over, only able to slightly move her head. "We've all heard about Xiao Yueyue's case, I encountered Diao Na, this is truly lucky, it's the luck of our whole family!" Wang Yuanyuan said, although they are in the same hospital ward, she and Diao Na haven't met yet, "After I recover and can get out of bed, the first thing I want to do is to visit Diao Na, thank my savior, she is my forever good younger sister..."

Dai Yongye has already taken a long leave from his company to fully care for his wife. He said, "At first, I didn't know that Diao Na used her body to protect my wife, saving her life, it was only later when the family of the driver who hit Diao Na found me and told me about Diao Na's selfless act that I learned about it." He said, these days the three families often meet, and on the 8th, they gave Diao Na a banner.

-The Perpetrator's Mother: "The kind-hearted girl is my son's savior"

In Diao Na's ward, apart from the family members, friends, colleagues, and citizens who came to visit, there was always a middle-aged woman quietly waiting by the bedside. She is Du, 57 years old, the mother of the driver who hit Diao Na.

"Diao Na not only saved Yuanyuan's life, but also saved my son's life. If it weren't for this kind-hearted girl, how many drivers would have committed heinous crimes..." Mrs. Du said with red and swollen eyes, if it weren't for Diao Na, her son would definitely have hit the injured person on the road, the consequences would have been unimaginable, and if that happened, her son's life would have been ruined.

On the morning of October 24th, the day after the incident, Mrs. Du and her husband took a small bus into the city, waited until 8:30 at the bank entrance, withdrew fifteen thousand yuan, and rushed to the hospital to take care of Diao Na ever since. Her son and husband also visited and apologized multiple times, and all medical expenses were fully paid in advance.

"Diao Na is a good person, my son did something wrong, our entire family must unconditionally compensate her. If possible, I would willingly exchange my leg for hers." Mrs. Du didn't care about her pregnant daughter-in-law who was having severe pregnancy reactions, "This girl is a kind-hearted person, I need to take care of her well. Even after the girl is discharged and goes home, we will continue to take care of her at home."

The sincere attitude of the Du family instead made Diao Na and her mother Ms. Wang feel somewhat uncomfortable. Ms. Wang said, "My daughter was born with perfect limbs, now her leg will have scars forever. Initially, I harbored some resentment, but the actions of the perpetrator's mother moved me, this mother isn't easy either..."

On the 8th, Wang Yuanyuan's family gave Diao Na a banner; on the 9th, Diao Na's family gave Wang Yuanyuan flowers; every day, Mrs. Du, the perpetrator's mother, waits by the bedside... The three families often meet, hoping that the kind-hearted girl recovers soon is their common wish.

They used their actions, their conscience, their honesty and kindness, helping others joyfully, writing a touching story about love and justice, public morality and responsibility in civilized Yantai, on Chinese soil.
