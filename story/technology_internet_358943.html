<p> BEIJING - (Reported by Wang Qiushi) By simply taking the temperature and listening to the heartbeat, a black clinic diagnosed a child named Xiao Tong (pseudonym), who had pneumonia, as having tracheitis and treated him for 4 days. The result was that this 5-month-old boy was sent on a one-way trip. Yesterday, I learned from the Tongzhou Court that the couple Zhang and Song who ran the black clinic were prosecuted for suspected illegal medical practice.</p>

<p> Mr. Zhang and his wife operated an unlicensed individual clinic in Nanjie of Liuhe Village, Songzhuang Town, Tongzhou District. They had been administratively penalized twice by the Tongzhou Health Bureau. Prosecutors charged that at around 2:00 PM on December 4th last year, 23-year-old Ms. Wang brought her 5-month-old son Xiao Tong for treatment. Without adopting any auxiliary examination measures, Mr. Zhang simply took the temperature and listened to the heart sounds, incorrectly diagnosing Xiao Tong with "bronchitis" and giving him daily injections of antibiotics and "traditional Chinese medicine preparation Shuanghuanglian powder injection" for intravenous therapy.</p><a href='http://www.xingnvlang.cn' target='_blank'>Welcome to log in</a>

<p> After continuous treatment for 4 days, Xiao Tong suddenly exhibited symptoms such as convulsions and upward rolling of the eyes. The clinic only performed mouth-to-mouth suction of phlegm and pressed the Renzhong acupoint. Later, Xiao Tong was sent to the Luhe Hospital of Tongzhou District and died despite resuscitation efforts. It was determined that Xiao Tong died due to respiratory failure caused by interstitial pneumonia in both lungs. Mr. Zhang and his wife were arrested.</p> <div style="clear:both;height:0;visibility:hidden;overflow:hidden;"><a href='http://www.xingnvlang.cn' target='_blank'>Hundred Boxes</a></div>
