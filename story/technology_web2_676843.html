Liu Quan, who had never seen so much money, stayed calm. He held his wife's hand for courage and together they went to the bus division office. The police discovered that the Hong Kong dollars were all in 1000 dollar bills, 100 thousand per bundle, a total of ten bundles, exactly one million Hong Kong dollars. At 11am, Mr Fu arrived at the flexible team of the bus division. When he found that his lost one million Hong Kong dollars was there in full, he tightly held Liu Quan's hand and shakily said "thank you" repeatedly, "it feels like a dream!" He almost cried several times. Mr Fu said that after losing the money, his mind went blank and everyone was dumbfounded. This money was his life savings, prepared for his child who studies medicine to go to Hong Kong for further studies. A citizen in Wuhan lost a full one million Hong Kong dollars in a taxi. More than an hour later, just when he was extremely worried, he learned that the taxi driver had already handed the money over to the police.

Liu Quan said: "If so much money is lost, the owner must be very anxious, it would ruin him." While the police were verifying the information, the owner Mr Fu's report came in.

After being questioned by the police, 42-year-old Liu Quan recalled that at around 8 o'clock on the 12th, the owner got into the car and stuffed a convenience bag under the seat, forgetting it when he got out of the car. He didn't notice it at the time. Later, two more passengers got into the car, and it wasn't until the third passenger tried to take the bag away when getting out of the car that he suddenly remembered, and immediately stopped that passenger from taking it.

At 9 o'clock on the 12th, the flexible team of the Wuhan Public Security Bureau Bus Division received a situation report from taxi driver Liu Quan of Wuhan Hengchang Gongmao Taxi Company, stating that he had found one million Hong Kong dollars in cash left by a customer in the car.

After the story of taxi driver Liu Quan returning lost property spread, it was warmly praised by citizens and netizens. Many netizens left messages: "Good driver, I admire you!" In the afternoon of the 13th, Wuhan City held a commendation meeting, where the title of "Role Model Driver who Returns Lost Property" was awarded to Liu Quan on the spot, along with a reward of 2000 yuan.
