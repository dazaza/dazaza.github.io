Baidu Tieba new version is online

by yinyin on 2008-08-04 21:12:07

The homepage of the new version of Tieba is grand and beautiful, and the most eye-catching area is "Today's Activities". This section is closely integrated with the calendar function that was launched not long ago, and it displays major events within Tieba in a timely manner. In addition, the "Tiebas You Should Pay Attention to Today" function has also been added.

The 2008 Beijing Olympics is about to begin, and we can also see many elements prepared for Olympic coverage on the Tieba homepage: Olympic activities, Olympic Games Tieba, Olympic life relay, and more.
