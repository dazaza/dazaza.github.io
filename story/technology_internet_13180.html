My experience with using Launch IBP 8 for search engine optimization (SEO)

by ciwork on 2007-02-11 06:02:04

Recently, I haven't done anything and put the construction of the China International Labor Information Network website on hold first, and spent a few days on website SEO. I don't know about others, but my IE favorites are all filled with links to SEO websites.

Let me talk about a small matter first. I heard that GOOGLE and DuHugu reached an agreement, making DuHugu the only SEO software authorized by Google. I was so excited that I searched everywhere for the DuHugu software, but in the end, I only found one answer - there was no such thing!

I have read all of ZAC's "Search Engine Optimization Ranking SEO One Post Per Day". I really have to admit that he is an expert in search engine optimization! I also read other articles with relatively high rankings on the web, learning some things about optimizing websites for GOOGLE and Baidu.

So let's get started.

Since the main keyword for the China International Labor Information Network is "labor export", I used the Top 10 optimizer provided by Launch IBP 8 to compare the top 10 ranked websites on the current major search engines with my existing pages. I didn't know until I compared them, and was shocked to find out that some websites were far ahead of mine in terms of page optimization.
