Let's Step into Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Beauty Written by Taste of Whitening in Intensive Care

Today, we switch flavors and return to our traditional treasure - TCM adjustment. TCM not only focuses on treating diseases and saving lives but also excels in beauty adjustments. Throughout history, countless TCM practitioners have spent their entire lives devising ways to enhance the beauty of court ladies, leaving behind a wealth of experience that is absolutely worth our attention!

So, let's follow the editor and step into the world of TCM beauty together~

"TCM Beauty" has deep cultural roots in China. Through thousands of years of beauty practices, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have gathered infinite Eastern wisdom.

Whether it's the ancient royal court or modern cities, women have always been meticulous about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "TCM Beauty" with its comprehensive internal and external adjustments, and the concept of achieving health and beauty from within, has gained increasing admiration from people who value beauty and health.

However, now TCM beauty products come in all shapes and sizes. What kind of method will be effective for me? Which product will benefit me? How do I find the right product for myself? I think these are the issues everyone is most concerned about, and also the most headache-inducing problems.

For this reason, I've visited supermarkets, malls, and beauty salons; later, I found an issue: Supermarkets and malls generally don't sell pure TCM products, while some beauty salons do use them. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduced the merits of their products, telling me what would happen after using them, and customers saying they were great. But these are ultimately one-sided opinions from merchants, making it hard for us to find actual users to understand unless we have friends nearby using them. I'm not saying their products are bad; every product that survives in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still value our traditional TCM beauty products.

Because of work, I often surf the web, so I decided to use the internet to look around. Taobao is the largest shopping platform in China. Usually, I often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics there. But I haven't yet explored our own TCM beauty products online. After searching, I found quite a few items. I decided to choose a store with relatively high credibility to ask questions.

I clicked into a TCM beauty shop called "Green Forest." Honestly, what initially attracted me was the images of ancient beauties on their products. Beauty is something both men and women care about. After reading their introductions, it turned out they were from a family of TCM practitioners, and all the products in the small shop were specially formulated by the old TCM doctor in the family. This piqued my curiosity. Wow, could he possibly give me a secret formula, turning me into a youthful beauty overnight? After looking at a few more product introductions, I discovered that the old TCM doctor in the shop was around 50 years old but looked like he was only 35. He was truly amazing, filling me with admiration and envy. If I could look as young as I do now when I'm 50, that would be fine.

Thinking about this, I decided to consult the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she introduces her products.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello. Are your products purely TCM?

Shopkeeper: Hello, yes, they are all pure TCM. Is there anything you need?

Editor: I want to learn first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect would you like to learn about?

Editor: Your products say they're formulated by an old TCM doctor. How long does it take to see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old TCM doctor. TCM emphasizes internal and external adjustments. Unlike general chemical or hormone-containing beauty products which show immediate effects, TCM can treat the root cause and fundamentally solve the problem. Our product course lasts a month, and the speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people see results after one course, while others need two courses or more. Generally, the longer you use it, the better the effect.

Editor: Have any customers said they didn't see results?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are impatient, use it a few times, and then stop, claiming it's ineffective. Our products are not magic pills, so it's impossible to achieve satisfactory results after just a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellowish skin but are still young and have no other illnesses. These customers generally suffer from blood and qi deficiency, so we adopt dual supplementation of blood and qi, along with internal and external adjustments. For such customers, adjustments usually take at least two months. Some customers give up halfway and claim the product is ineffective. However, those who persist will definitely see results.

Editor: It seems that using TCM methods for beauty requires patience.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because each person's condition is different, the time needed for adjustments will vary. As long as customers persist, their skin will improve significantly compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I have a basic understanding now. I'll come back to buy if I need anything. Wishing you booming business!

Shopkeeper: Sure, no need to be polite. Welcome to visit anytime.

The editor believes that the quality of a product depends on its reputation. The shop's credibility is pretty good, with many repeat customers. The editor decided to probe the opinions of their customers to verify the truth. Among the numerous online buyers, I chose one with a high credit rating. After explaining my intentions, she willingly chatted with me. Below is a main segment of our conversation (to protect her privacy, I'll refer to her as A).

Editor: How long have you been using the TCM beauty products from this shop?

A: Around eight months ago, due to staying up late frequently, my skin was in bad condition. My complexion was dull, dry, with fine lines, and even spots. Since I have sensitive skin, I didn't dare to use random spot-removal products. I wanted a gentle TCM spot-removal product. I accidentally found this shop and started trying it then. Seeing good results, I continued using it.

Editor: What products did you use?

A: The shopkeeper helped me select a whitening and spot-removal Yung Rong Pill, an all-in-one whitening and spot-removal mask, and a moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask.

Editor: Did it really work?

A: Yes, after one month, my skin tone became more even, my face wasn't as dry, the spots faded slightly, and my skin lightened a bit. Although it wasn't very white, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin improved now?

A: Now, my skin is much whiter and softer, the spots have become much lighter and less noticeable. With foundation, they're invisible. The fine lines have also become very shallow, and my skin looks hydrated and radiant. Even in the morning after waking up, my skin has a rosy glow. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. Moreover, I've recommended some friends to try it, and they also say it works well.

Editor: Have you experienced any side effects?

A: No, because the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, I haven't noticed any abnormalities. However, since TCM takes time, it was a longer process.

Editor: Are you still using it consistently?

A: Yes. I've been taking the whitening and beauty Yung Rong Pill for six months. The all-in-one mask and the moisturizing anti-wrinkle mask, I've been using twice a week consistently for eight months. The shopkeeper suggests I take the Yung Rong Pill again every two months because it can also prevent aging. Women fear aging the most, haha.

Editor: It seems our long-standing beauty treasure is really excellent.

A: Yes. You should give it a try and discuss it with the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful wish of humanity, but all natural things go through life and death, prosperity and decline. As part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective law. Therefore, as age increases, the human body will also age.

TCM believes that although it's impossible to stay forever young, by replenishing the deficiencies of the organs' blood and qi, keeping the yin and yang of the human body balanced, it can indeed delay the arrival of aging. We believe that in today's world where people value nature and green concepts, TCM, with its strength accumulated over five thousand years, still holds unique charm in preventing aging and promoting beauty and health.
