Xixi International Information Science and Technology Park moves into the Yuhang Innovation Base

by dragonsoft on 2010-07-02 19:09:54

Core Tip: On April 15, the signing ceremony of Xixi International Information and Technology Industry Park project led by Longrun Group (China) to enter Hangzhou Yuhang Innovation Base was held by the West Lake. Both parties want to make it a high-end IT industry park.

One side is the leading figure in Shanghai's IT industry, the other side is Hangzhou's unique high-tech industry entrepreneurial platform. On April 15, the signing ceremony of Xixi International Information and Technology Industry Park project led by Longrun Group (China) entering Hangzhou Yuhang Innovation Base was held by the West Lake with the spring atmosphere. Both parties will work together to make it a high-end IT industry park.

It is reported that the Xixi International Information and Technology Industry Park project covers about 150 mu and aims to build a cluster base including software development, information service outsourcing, internet commerce, data processing, ICT product R&D sales and services as its main business forms, with features of service outsourcing, business process outsourcing and high-end knowledge service outsourcing facing Shanghai and overseas markets. More than 20 IT enterprises under the Youth Talent Association of Informationization in Shanghai led by Longrun Group will be the first batch to enter the park.

The entry of this project will greatly promote the construction of public service platforms including public data network, data center, and technical support center of the innovation base, thus greatly promoting the great improvement and development of the high-tech industry in the innovation base. The location, transportation, environment and industrial advantages of the innovation base will open up a whole new world for the development of Xixi International Information and Technology Industry Park.

Hangzhou Yuhang Innovation Base is an extension area of Hangzhou National Information Industry Base, positioned as a top-class high-tech industrial park in China. The planned total area of the base reaches 98.9 square kilometers, which is currently the largest continuous plot for high-tech industry development in Hangzhou urban area. Built and managed by the Management Committee of Yuhang Group (Innovation Base), the base focuses on building a harmonious demonstration area that integrates industry, science and technology, ecology, human settlement and modern city elements, is the most innovative, has the most investment potential and the most ecological charm, and is livable, suitable for employment, tourism and study. It plans to spend five to eight years to build the innovation base into a low-carbon satellite city integrating the beauty of nature, humanity, science and technology, and art.

Since its establishment, Yuhang Group (Innovation Base) has been committed to "building a top-class park, introducing large good high merchants, constructing public platforms, efficiently advancing projects, and cultivating high-tech industries" to promote the construction of "large platforms, large industries, large projects, large enterprises". In 2010, the innovation base will complete basic investments of 2.5 billion yuan, with a total investment exceeding 6.9 billion yuan, continuously optimizing the soft and hard environments for enterprise development and supporting facilities for livable and employment life. With superior entrepreneurial environment, it has welcomed the entry of a number of top domestic IT enterprises such as Alibaba and Hengsheng Electronics, praised by Jack Ma, chairman of Alibaba's board of directors, as the "paradise of entrepreneurship in dreams".
