To understand the future development prospects and employment situation of the mold design profession, one must first understand the important position of molds in industrial production. Simply put, molds are models used for mass production of products. For example, if you want to produce cups or TV casings, you must have molds for cups or TV casings to produce qualified cups or TV casing products. Hence, molds are often referred to as the "mother of production"...

In modern manufacturing, more than 60% of parts require mold forming. As a core industry and efficient production tool in modern manufacturing, mold technology and industry development is an important measure of a country's manufacturing level.

In Germany, a developed manufacturing country, the mold industry is regarded as a "key industry"; in Japan, which rose through manufacturing, the mold industry is listed as "the secret behind the development of the entire industrial foundation"; in the United States, the mold industry is seen as the "foundation of industry."

Qin Ke introduced to the reporter of Outlook News Weekly that currently, in terms of scale, China's mold industry ranks third in the world after Japan and Germany. It can be said that in recent years, the international competitiveness of China's mold industry has improved rapidly, but most of it is concentrated in mid-to-low-end fields, with low technical levels and low added value. Overall, the technical level lags behind developed countries by 15 to 20 years.

Within the domestic market, there is a shortage of mold talents, whether they are front-line mold workers, mold designers, or even senior mold management talents.

According to relevant human resources market statistics, mold design has been ranked among the top ten gray-collar positions, becoming the hottest position in the mechanical workplace currently, with an average monthly salary ranging from 3,500 yuan to 5,000 yuan. Professionals in mold design and manufacturing mainly engage in mold design and manufacture, stamping forming, plastic forming, die-casting forming, installation, debugging, maintenance of molds, and technical operations in mold production. Currently, there is a severe shortage of mold design talents, especially in important cities such as Jiangsu-Zhejiang regions, Shanghai, Shenzhen, etc. It can be said that the urgent demand for mechanical mold design talents in most southern regions is extremely pressing!
