Rice Bond: The best online takeaway ordering service

by watsonxu on 2009-08-05 10:42:01

Rice bond is a website for Hangzhou to provide online booking services (accurately speaking is mainly for white-collar takeout booking comprehensive service website), although before also understand some online booking service website, but personally feel rice bond is undoubtedly better in many aspects, the following are some of the main features and functions of rice Bond booking service:

* 1. Fandbond provides online meal booking service for enterprises and their employees (currently only available in Hangzhou);

* 2. Simple interface, you can directly order food by logging in to the home page;

* 3. Provide "system booking order" and "self-ordered telephone order" two ways of ordering food, for "self-ordered telephone order" without registration can also be ordered directly;

* 4. You can easily see which restaurants offer online ordering near your location, and you can see other people's reviews of the restaurant;

* 5. According to the style of the cuisine, the price range of the restaurant can be selected;

* 6. Provide food ordering service together, you can send an address to the company's employees, everyone together to choose what they like to eat, and then unified order;

* 7. Meal to pay and online payment (Alipay) are available;

* 8. You can redeem gifts by ordering food, recommending restaurants, etc.
