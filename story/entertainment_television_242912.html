Yi Shiqing, due to helping his brother, had been in arrears on his mortgage payments for a long time and was taken to court by the bank. His house will be forcibly auctioned off by the court. In order to save his home, Yi Shiqing started borrowing money and collecting debts without telling his wife Ding Jiayan, but all efforts were in vain. Not only that, he was ridiculed by an old classmate and rival - Wang Pingshi, who is now a real estate tycoon. In a panic, Yi Shiqing used public funds to repay the loan, putting him at risk of imprisonment.

In order not to let Yi Shiqing fall into prison, Ding Jiayan reluctantly sought help from Wang Pingshi. This deeply hurt Yi Shiqing's pride and caused cracks in their already strained marriage due to life pressures. Disasters continued to pile up as Yi Shiqing was fired from his company and became a middle-aged unemployed man. To cope with the sudden changes in life, Ding Jiayan ended her life as a full-time housewife and took a job at Wang Pingshi's company, causing an extreme crisis in her relationship with Yi Shiqing. Ultimately, they both signed divorce papers, plunging Yi Shiqing's life into a deep valley.

Yi Shiqing's childhood friend Shi Tianlei learned that Yi Shiqing had fallen this far because of helping him. Already overwhelmed by a web of debt, Shi Tianlei decided to borrow high-interest loans again. Unexpectedly, the lender relentlessly pressured him and even took his fiancée Ju Xiaomei hostage. In a panic, Shi Tianlei accidentally killed someone and went on the run.

Ding Jiayan returned to her parents' home, which caused resentment in her mother who had endured much hardship during the house-buying process. Her mother urged her to remarry Wang Pingshi. However, Ding Jiayan's younger sister Ding Jiaxin showed the greatest sympathy towards her "former brother-in-law."

Yi Shiqing's situation left Fang Yizhou in a difficult position. As a lawyer, she must fulfill her duties, but as a single "overseas-educated" woman, her emotional world was tumultuous. Her boyfriend only valued her wealth and used underhanded methods to transfer her house to his name and sell it. Yi Shiqing stepped in to help Fang Yizhou, tilting her emotions towards him. What Yi Shiqing never expected was that the "moonlight clan" tenant living in his house, Xie Qianli, turned out to be Fang Yizhou's half-brother with a different surname. Moreover, Xie Qianli was pursuing Ding Jiaxin, and their relationship was interfered with by Ding’s mother due to housing issues, leading to tragic consequences for Ding Jiaxin. At this critical moment, Xie Qianli's sincere devotion moved Ding’s mother, who then offered an unexpected condition to Xie Qianli despite being stingy.

The lives and emotions of several families became complicated. Only the young couple Jiang Hongling, a primary school teacher, and Feng Dajun, a bartender, seemed to live relatively peacefully. However, the arrival of Grandpa Feng disrupted this tranquility. After learning that his daughter-in-law was pregnant, Grandpa Feng secretly sold his old house and paid the down payment for their new house without telling his grandson. However, unforeseen disasters kept coming one after another.

Shi Tianlei quietly sneaked back into the city, intending to pay back the money he owed to Yi Shiqing before turning himself in. However, a traffic accident claimed his life. Ju Xiaomei was heartbroken. After fulfilling Shi Tianlei's last wish, she walked into the prison gate.

Yi Shiqing rekindled his spirit and made astonishing achievements. He worked hard to mend the emotional wounds with Ding Jiayan and wanted to reunite the family. Just then, Ding Jiayan was hospitalized, her life hanging by a thread. In order to save Ding Jiayan's life, Yi Shiqing resolutely sold the house that still had an outstanding loan.

However, the lack of a kidney source to save Ding Jiayan became a critical obstacle. Wang Pingshi, Xie Qianli, and the Ding family were all anxious. At this time, Yi Shiqing lay calmly on the operating table, donating his kidney to his ex-wife. This made both of them realize that family ties are their true home, and they are each other's "house" in each other's hearts.
