China Special Steel Market Forecast and Industry Investment Consulting Report

by geiliyou on 2009-08-28 17:46:25

With the in-depth development of China's heavy industrialization process, the demand for special steel is entering a golden age. Unlike common steel which is mainly used in construction, bridges, roads and steel structure construction, special steel products are mainly applied in military industry, aviation, machinery, automobile, railway, petroleum, coal and other industries, also being the key supported industry of the new steel industry policy including mold laser welding. With the increase in automobile consumption, overall investment upgrade in train and railway system, rise of advanced machinery manufacturing industry, aerospace and military industry entering a new round of rapid development period, the demand for special steel will enter its golden age. In 2008, China's special steel industry and special steel market maintained a good development trend. The demand for various kinds of alloys, especially for special alloys such as molybdenum, nickel, vanadium, tungsten for mold laser welding was still very strong, which was mainly reflected in: domestic high-quality special steel demand will continue to show a growth trend. The development of China's equipment manufacturing industry provides a historic development opportunity for domestic rapid development of special steel. In 2009, the special steel industry will present a steady development trend.

However, there are also problems in the development of China's special steel industry at this stage. The main factors restricting the development of China's special steel are the serious imbalance of industrial structure in the special steel industry; low concentration of special steel industry, insufficient competitiveness and vicious competition caused by repeated construction at low level of mold laser welding. In 2009, under the global economic background of financial storm attack, opportunities for the development of China's special steel enterprises are greater than challenges, transforming from rapid growth in output to rapid technological progress; and the demand for variety and quality of special steels is developing from intermediate to advanced. There is a broad market space in China's automobile industry, shipbuilding industry (including marine diesel engines and marine equipment), high-speed railway EMU, hydroelectric, thermal power, nuclear power generation equipment and transmission and transformation equipment, large-scale metallurgical and mining machinery equipment, etc., and the demand for quantity and quality of varieties of special steels is developing from intermediate to advanced. Enterprises should seize the opportunity, determine their market positioning, and make strategic arrangements as early as possible.

At present, China has completed the primary stage of industrialization. The next step is the heavy chemical industry stage. The automobile, machinery, petrochemicals, aviation, military industry, railways and other industries will welcome rapid development, and the demand for special steel will increase significantly. During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, China's special steel industry should strictly control the total amount, increase the varieties and quantities of high value-added and high-tech alloy steel to meet market demands; resolutely eliminate backward processes and equipment including argon arc welding, shut down small electric furnaces and small rolling mills; focus on key points, develop special steel with characteristics and market; optimize industrial structure and reorganization, realize specialized production, avoid repeated construction; strengthen national defense awareness, enhance the ability of special steel military supporting; strengthen environmental protection, improve the environment; ensure that during the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, the total amount of special steel is strictly controlled at 10 million tons, of which 3.5 million tons are alloy steel. During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, the country will focus on supporting the development of 9 series of products, mainly including alloy spring steel, alloy structural steel (including gear, valve steel), bearing steel, high-speed tool steel, stainless steel, heat-resistant steel, etc.

Further development of China's heavy industrialization process, special steel entering the golden age, it can be seen that the development of special steel is bright!
