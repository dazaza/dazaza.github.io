With 40% of Guangdong's children's clothing failing inspections, many experts say that children's skin is very delicate, making it more prone to skin allergies due to excessive formaldehyde and pH levels. Excessive formaldehyde and pH levels in clothing can be harmful to human health; short-term exposure may cause contact dermatitis, with symptoms including redness of the skin, rashes, itching, or a needle-like sensation. Severe cases can lead to eye and respiratory tract irritation, and repeated exposure can cause localized pigmentation of the skin. Additionally, formaldehyde has potential teratogenic and carcinogenic effects. The quality of clothing is also a source of worry for mothers!
