**Ingredients:**

- 300g of green sprouts

- 1 tomato

- 1 egg

**Seasonings:**

- 1 tablespoon of oil

- 1/4 teaspoon of salt

- 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

**Preparation Process:**

1. Dice the tomato into small cubes, wash and drain the green sprouts, and beat the egg in a bowl until uniform.

2. Heat a small amount of oil in a pot, add the tomato and stir-fry until red oil appears, then add enough water and bring to a boil.

3. Add the green sprouts, cook until the water boils again, then add salt and soy sauce for seasoning. Pour the beaten egg evenly into the pot.
