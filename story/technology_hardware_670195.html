The Largest Specialized Cable City Settles in Houjie, Radiating to the Pearl River Delta

Investing over 100 million yuan, the largest and highest-class cable city in China will settle in Xitou Community of Houjie Town. Currently, this cable city is recruiting merchants and is expected to open for business in April next year. At that time, it will provide one-stop services including finance, logistics, office, catering, leisure and entertainment.

Industry insiders believe that the establishment and construction of this cable city will keep Houjie's related industries ahead in domestic peers, effectively integrate industry advantageous resources, and promote the healthy development of the industrial chain.

Establish a One-Stop Trading Platform

As one of the important supporting industries for national economic construction, the wire and cable industry accounts for a quarter of the output value of China's electrician industry, and is the second largest industry in the machinery industry after the automobile industry.

Dongguan, as a representative of advanced manufacturing in South China and even nationwide, and a world-renowned manufacturing city, has formed a complete industrial cluster in terms of cable production, cable equipment, cable materials, and cable testing, playing a pivotal role in the development of the economy in South China and the Pearl River Delta.

According to reports, Haojing Cable City plans to create convenient supporting facilities for merchants in many aspects, including market operation center, customer service center, warehousing and logistics center, business office center, electronic information center, exhibition and display center, commercial food street, etc., providing comprehensive services such as finance, logistics, office, catering, leisure and entertainment, and realizing a one-stop trading platform.

Settling in Houjie, Radiating to the Pearl River Delta

Haojing Cable City, settled in Houjie, enjoys unique geographical and transportation advantages. It is located at the intersection of roads such as Provincial Road 256 and Huanwan Expressway. After the opening of the Houjie station of Light Rail Line R2, it can reach all parts of Dongguan within 30 minutes and Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhongshan and other places within 60 minutes, making it the core transportation hub of the 1-hour economic circle of the Pearl River Delta.

It was also learned that after achieving the first phase goal, the cable city will have a second phase expansion of 300 mu on the current basis. It will also consider promoting in frontier cities such as Chengdu, Beijing, and Shanghai to provide more convenient and fast services for the industry.

Haojing Cable City

Dongguan Haojing Cable City is a professional market integrating product sales, technical exchange, and talent services for the wire and cable industry. The total investment exceeds 100 million yuan. The market covers an area of more than 130 mu with a total construction area of nearly 200,000 square meters. More than 800 shops are available for upstream and downstream merchants in the national wire and cable industry to choose freely, and more than 1000 parking spaces allow merchants to enter and exit conveniently.

As the largest wire and cable specialized market in the country, Haojing Cable City involves various types of wire and cable products used in power grids, households, and industrial factories. It is a comprehensive service center integrating industry production equipment and raw materials.
