Installation and Selection of Glass Wool Products in Steel Structures

by anonymous on 2012-02-24 16:24:27

* When laying the glass wool roll felt, the faced side should face the indoor side, perpendicular to the purlins. At one end of the eaves, leave about 20 cm extra roll felt and fix it on the outermost purlin with special clamps or double-sided tape;

* Two rolls of wool can be connected by stapling along the fly edge of the facing;

* According to the engineering needs, in order to avoid cold bridges, you can consider placing some rigid insulation materials such as XPS on the purlins;

* The faced side should face the indoor side, unroll from the eaves to the wall foot, use double-sided tape to fix the glass wool roll felt on the lowermost purlin, and leave an extra 20 cm;

* Cut the glass wool roll felt 20 cm above the top purlin of the wall, and fix it with double-sided tape;

* During unrolling, ensure alignment and tension, and lay the glass wool roll felt at the other end of the eaves, leaving an extra 20 cm roll felt, fixing it on the outermost purlin with special clamps or double-sided tape;

Two, Wall construction

* Install the roof color steel plate, remove the clamps at both ends of the eaves, and use the reserved 20 cm facing to finish the edges of the glass wool;

One, Roof construction

* Pay attention to maintaining the tension, alignment, and tight seams between rolls of the glass wool roll felt. If longitudinal overlaps are needed, they should be arranged at the purlins;

* According to JBJ26-95 "Energy-saving Design Standard for Civil Buildings" for the same region, the selection standard for glass wool is different. In areas with low temperatures, high-density and thick glass wool is generally selected. In areas with high temperatures, low-density and thin glass wool is used. Some hypertensive patients may need hospitalization.

* Finish the edges of the glass wool and install the color steel plate;
