Mobile phone data backup device, ensuring that your mobile data will not get lost.

by faxian1 on 2009-09-22 16:31:31

Almost everyone has thought about this: if I lose my phone, it gets stolen or snatched, or the SIM card gets corrupted for no reason, the financial loss is minor, but all the phone numbers of friends and contacts that I've recorded over time will be lost forever, unrecoverable. Although I've thought countless times about recording them, the hustle of life and inherent laziness prevent me from accomplishing this task. Moreover, there are always new friends and clients whose numbers need to be recorded— who has the patience to do all that?

In fact, if your phone is really lost or the SIM card gets damaged, aside from the economic loss, losing all contact information at once results in a severe and irretrievable loss. Nowadays, thefts and robberies of mobile phones are very common, and who can guarantee that a SIM card, being just a piece of plastic, will always be readable? In reality, by spending just a few dozen yuan, you can get a 100% insurance solution for this hidden risk that modern people face. This is the latest product introduced and demonstrated by the host on CCTV Channel 2's "For Your Service" program — the Mobile Phone Card Data Backup Device. It provides insurance for people's social networks and brings stability and security to busy lives.

The mobile phone data backup device can quickly back up and copy the contact information stored on GSM or CDMA SIM cards. This device operates independently and does not require a connection to a computer. After backing up the data, even if the phone is lost, there's no need to worry about losing the contact information. If users need to use different SIM cards due to traveling to different cities or countries, they can transfer the backed-up data to any SIM card. Even if the device runs out of power, the already stored data will not be lost.

After backing up the data, it can be preserved for a long time, with new data regularly added and ready for use at any time. For those who own two phones, or for those switching to new cards or networks, it allows for quickly copying the old phone's data onto a new card in one go, eliminating the hassle of re-entering the data manually. The current retail price is 30 yuan.
