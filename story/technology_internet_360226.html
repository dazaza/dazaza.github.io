Event Content:

I.

10 Yuan Screening Projects:

Single-item 10 yuan screening, free registration fee, consultation fee

 

l        

10 Yuan Unplanned Pregnancy Checkup

Project: Routine urine test, ultrasound, early pregnancy detection

Suitable for: Menstruation delayed by more than a week, suspecting pregnancy

l        

10 Yuan Gynecological Inflammation Checkup

Project: Gynecological examination, ultrasound, routine vaginal discharge test

Suitable for: Individuals with unusual odor, abnormal vaginal discharge, itching, burning pain, frequent urination, etc.

l        

10 Yuan Cervical Lesion Checkup

Project: Gynecological examination, colposcopy, routine vaginal discharge test

Suitable for: Increased vaginal discharge or mixed with blood streaks, burning, itching, lower back pain, pelvic heaviness, etc.

l        

10 Yuan Female Infertility Checkup

Project: Colposcopy, routine vaginal discharge test, follicle detection analysis once

Suitable for: Married for over a year with normal sexual life but not yet pregnant

l        

10 Yuan Breast Disease Checkup

Project: Palpation, breast ultrasound

Object: Breast pain, lumps, redness, swelling, heat, etc.

III.        

Discounted Treatment Projects

Half-price Assistance Full-price Assistance

In response to the call from the National Ministry of Health, following the spirit of assistance from the “Golden Lily” Women's Health Promotion Project, allowing love to warm hearts. The only designated hospital in Suzhou for the “Golden Lily” Women's Health Promotion Project — Kangli Hospital — has specially established a special public welfare fund. To ensure that the assistance work is standardized, open, and transparent, the relevant matters are announced as follows:

I. Assistance Targets

1. 20 female patients who have fallen into poverty due to illness;

2. 10 extremely impoverished women, municipal model workers, and municipal March 8th Red Banner Award-winning female patients.

II. Surgical Assistance Scope

Gynecological tumors, ovarian cysts, uterine fibroids, infertility, family planning, etc., hysteroscopic and laparoscopic minimally invasive surgeries.

III. Assistance Content

1. For 20 female patients who have fallen into poverty due to illness, half-price reduction on surgical fees, treatment fees, and hospitalization fees;

2. For 10 extremely impoverished women, municipal model workers, and municipal March 8th Red Banner Award-winning female patients, full-price assistance on all surgical expenses at our hospital;

3. Provision of one free “Golden Lily” female assistance ward (3 beds), until fully occupied.
