The process by which Ma Yulin obtained her role in the TV series "Struggle" was somewhat dramatic. On the evening of December 21st, Tencent exclusively initiated the "Intimate Enemy Main Creator Relay in Six Major Cities," with the second event held in Shanghai. The main actors Xu Jinglei (via Weibo), Huang Li Xing, Zhong Liti, and Lawrence Wong attended an event at Yonghua Cinema to meet fans. A pregnant He Liya and her partner Sun Minggang became another lucky couple blessed by "Intimate Enemy." At the scene, a fan asked Xu Jinglei and Huang Li Xing: if they first cooperated as lovers and then got married on screen, would their third collaboration involve having a child? Huang Li Xing straightforwardly replied, "Yes!"

At that time, some of the first confirmed actors for "Struggle," such as Tong Dawei, gathered with Zhao Baogang. During this gathering, Ma Yulin met her schoolmate Hu Lan Biotech by chance, who invited her to meet the director.

When Zhao Baogang saw Ma Yulin, he immediately invited her to play the lead female role in "Struggle," while slightly drunk. This caught Ma Yulin off guard, prompting her to tell Zhao Baogang that he must be tipsy and suggested discussing casting decisions after sobering up. Unexpectedly, the next day www.gzhl78.com instructed her to confirm the role with the director via phone call. Thus, Ma Yulin happily joined the production team.

In the drama, Ma Yulin and Tong Dawei portrayed a romantic couple. Off-screen, Tong Dawei also took good care of Ma Yulin. On the thirteenth day of filming, Ma Yulin suffered a fracture due to a sudden car passing by, causing her to lose balance and twist her foot, swelling it up like a bun. Tong Dawei quickly fetched red flower oil to rub on her injury. After enduring through the day's shoot, she went to the hospital for examination, confirming a fracture in the fifth metatarsal bone of her right foot. Subsequently, Tong Dawei helped her go to the bathroom and entertained her with jokes, leading rumors about their relationship to spread widely.

In reality, although Ma Yulin and Tong Dawei were deeply in love on-screen, and Wen Zhang and Li Xiaolu appeared as a couple, the truly loving couple in real life were Ma Yulin and Wen Zhang. They had met while filming "Brocade Guard" in 2005 but kept their relationship hidden due to concerns about its impact on their budding careers.

Entertainment relationships are often short-lived due to excessive rumors and lack of trust. To protect their love, Ma Yulin bought a pair of crystal necklaces, one for each of them, flaunting their affection publicly. Upon seeing this, Zhao Baogang realized, "If I had known you two were dating, I wouldn't have let you act together; it would definitely affect your characters' emotions!" Ma Yulin jokingly stuck out her tongue at the director.

After their romance was made public, both individuals loved freely, engaging in activities like singing karaoke and drinking coffee during breaks from filming.

Ma Yulin, despite being a Shanghai girl, is quite carefree and sometimes forgetful. Her negligence worsened after being with Wen Zhang. Before leaving home, Wen Zhang would remind her what to bring; when it turned cold, he would advise her to wear more clothes to prevent catching a cold. Enjoying Wen Zhang's care without worries, Ma Yulin said contentedly, "Sometimes, Wen Zhang feels like an older brother."

Ma Yulin views fame lightly. Even after winning the "Most Popular Female Actor Award" for her role in "Struggle," she remained humble, eating wherever convenient and maintaining close friendships. She enjoys traveling extensively, though unplanned and prone to forgetting things, luckily now having Wen Zhang as her loyal attendant.

Once in France, upon realizing she had no cash for a taxi fare, Wen Zhang promptly produced change, saying, "I've been prepared." Returning home, she discovered she'd forgotten her keys, but Wen Zhang magically produced them, explaining, "You often forget your keys, so I took them!"

As an actress, Ma Yulin frequently suffers from stomach issues due to fluctuating dietary needs for roles. Wen Zhang cleverly encouraged her, "This is like a battle; you need to defeat a bowl of rice to win a victory!"

Though only a few years younger than Ma Yulin, graduating in 2002, Wen Zhang occasionally exhibits childish behavior. The more mature Ma Yulin understands the importance of emotional adjustment, inviting him for coffee or golf to relax. She often says, "In love, we tend to be self-centered, but making concessions for love signifies maturity. Many women fail to understand this, losing their true loves and regretting it forever. In 'Struggle,' Xia Lin returning from France for her lover demonstrates emotional maturity, something I've learned."

On days spent at home, Ma Yulin enjoys riding motorcycles with Wen Zhang. Each time his silhouette appears around the street corner, she eagerly runs downstairs. Riding behind him on his motorcycle represents the most romantic moments for her, despite the thrill and danger. Concerned for his safety, she gifted him her newly purchased Ford SUV with the license plate ending in "06." She humorously remarked, "Your motorcycle should retire; don't keep roaring around, making me anxious all the time!"

In December 2007, while filming "Let's See" in the Qinling Mountains of Shaanxi, Wen Zhang had an accident, falling from a mountain and severely injuring his waist. Fearing Ma Yulin's worry, he concealed the incident until she learned about it from a friend. Rushing to his bedside, she tearfully scolded him, "Why didn't you tell me? How could you be so careless? Why refuse using a stunt double? And why not go to the hospital immediately after getting injured?"

Following her tirade, Wen Zhang smiled brightly, "It's just a minor injury! Look how worried you are! Didn't you say 'Minor injuries don't stop soldiers'?" Ma Yulin couldn't help but laugh through her tears, asking, "What do you want to eat? I'll cook it for you!" Without hesitation, Wen Zhang replied, "Anything you cook, I'll enjoy!" Ma Yulin looked at him tenderly, tears welling up in her eyes...
