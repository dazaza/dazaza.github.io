Strive to face the world with a smile and understand the true meaning of life.

by cvgf3289 on 2010-05-29 09:26:04

Life is like an incomplete scripture, everything is not perfect, at least far from perfect! This incompleteness is the very reason for change! Going with the flow is the path people must choose. Don't blindly pursue a simple life, it will only result in self-deception, and in the end, you'll be left with nothing but scars.

Everyone knows that roses are beautiful, but have you noticed their sharp thorns? Lotus flowers are also beautiful, but they grow in water, can you admire them up close? Good things are not without flaws, all things in the world have their own imperfections. Nature has given them their noble qualities, but also their shortcomings.

If you want to live freely and happily, you must learn not to always calculate what you gain! As long as you don't lose anything, you should be content. Anything you do will inevitably bring some form of gain, it's just a matter of how much...

When flowers bloom, don't think about when they fall; when they fall, think about them making room for the next generation to thrive better! Do not worry about external things or human affairs! Remember these profound truths of life, sorrow brings nothing, happiness brings mental clarity and lightness. It may even prolong your life! Why not choose happiness? If something happens, can your sadness or pain change it? Can crying alter the current situation? Why not treat it as a life experience, a stepping stone for growth? That way, you can walk more steadily on the road ahead, isn't that right?

Always face the world with a smile. Life is like a mirror, if you cry at it, it cries back at you. If you shout at it, you know the outcome already!

Rather than saying "I'm so sad" while crying, it's better to say "It doesn't matter!" Letting go means being able to truly let go...
