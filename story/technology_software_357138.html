Dedicating Love and Brightness: The Great Ideal Staircase of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital

In our youth, we all had some wonderful, splendid, yet somewhat naive and unrealistic ideals. It is a desirable, shining word that people praise, yearn for, and pursue because it brings hope and strength. The great writer Tolstoy once said, "An ideal is a guiding beacon; without an ideal, there is no firm direction; without direction, there is no life."

Dedicating brightness, empowering the wings of ideals

A great enterprise must have lofty ideals. A company with grand ideals surely has a praiseworthy bright future! From ancient times to the present, every successful person has their own story. Chairman Chen Huojun of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital believes that success lies in ideals and beliefs. Since childhood, he has aspired to be a useful person, a good person, doing good deeds, dedicating love to society. This belief has always influenced his later life and work. To empower his ideal, he never misses any opportunity to contribute to society.

There is an old Chinese saying, "The poor cultivate themselves alone, while the prosperous benefit the world." After years of relentless effort, Chen Huojun not only achieved his own career development but also elevated his personal value.

The establishment of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital originated from incidents involving friends around Chairman Chen Huojun. Many of his friends, in order to treat glaucoma, myopia, cataracts, etc., traveled through numerous hospitals, enduring difficult treatments. This made Chen Huojun acutely aware of the need for advanced equipment and high-tech specialized ophthalmology hospitals. Thus, the idea of founding one was born. This concept quickly received support from Soochow University, leading to the creation of the first non-profit tertiary ophthalmology specialty hospital in Suzhou — Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital — by Hong Kong Asia Medical Investment Group and Soochow University. The hospital aims to be one of the top ophthalmology hospitals in East China, based in Suzhou and serving the entire country. It is an internationalized ophthalmology center integrating medical care, research, teaching, health care, and blindness prevention and treatment. It collaborates deeply with Soochow University in medical technology, discipline construction, talent introduction, teaching, and scientific research to better serve eye disease patients across Suzhou and even the whole nation.

Dedicating love, ideals and responsibilities after disasters

At 7:49 am on April 14, 2010, a 7.1-magnitude earthquake struck Yushu, Qinghai, the homeland of King Gesar, preserving some of the purest, most beautiful, and precious landscapes and memories in China. It is the place where many people's dreams begin, nurturing many people's ideals. The strong earthquake severely affected Yushu County, causing significant cracks in the ground and severe damage to water infrastructure. Throughout Yushu County, ruins and collapsed buildings were everywhere, and thousands of lives were lost instantly, shattering the ideals of disaster-affected people.

The eyes of the world focused on this event. Faced with the helplessness of the elderly, the innocence of children, and the suffering of the injured, what should we do? At this moment, we gradually understood: "A person truly lives not only for ideals but also for responsibilities. Use your ideals to bring hope to others, achieve their ideals, be responsible for yourself, your family, and more importantly, society."

New campuses will come! New homes will come!

Rescuing and caring for children should be a priority in earthquake relief efforts!

Children cannot suffer hardships, and education cannot be neglected.

To quickly raise the national flag at schools and let the sound of reading return, the nation is taking action, Qinghai Yushu is taking action, and Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital is taking action...

On April 20, the large-scale fundraising program "Ties to Yushu - Boundless Love" hosted by the Publicity Department of the CPC Central Committee, the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television, the Ministry of Civil Affairs, and the Red Cross Society of China, and organized by CCTV, was broadcast live on CCTV. Among the donors, Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital's donation of 1 million yuan was prominently recorded in history, fully funding the construction of the "Ideal Hope Primary School" project in the disaster-stricken area of Yushu, Qinghai.

Chairman Chen Huojun of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital stated: "After the earthquake in Yushu, Qinghai, the disaster situation touched the hearts of all employees of the soon-to-open Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital. The board decided to find ways to assist the disaster area. Since Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital is jointly established by medical institutions and educational institutions, teaching and nurturing students are also part of its responsibility. Therefore, all personnel involved in the hospital's establishment conceived the idea of allocating 1 million yuan from the hospital's construction funds to build a 'Ideal Hope Primary School' with a school building area of 1000 square meters for the disaster-stricken area of Yushu. Although we cannot go to the scene to help those in distress, we can extend a helping hand, bringing them hope and restoring their ideals. This action will inspire more individuals and enterprises to deeply integrate into the vitality, creativity, and cohesion of this ancient nation, shining brighter than a thousand suns!"

What drives us to strive is our ideals, but what we must follow to realize them is responsibility. His words moved us and made us understand that having ambitious ideals is important, but more crucial is action, perseverance, and unwavering belief in action. Action must adhere to the principle of assuming the responsibilities you should bear, and whether ideals and responsibilities are well combined.

This is an era that demands the demonstration of responsibility, requiring a generation of corporate citizens who dare to take responsibility. Daring to assume historical responsibilities and dedicate to society is not only a posture but also a commitment. As the General Secretary hoped, "Strengthening a sense of social responsibility and historical mission, integrating personal growth into the tide of promoting national development and ethnic revival, striving tirelessly for the realization of grand ideals."

The Yushu earthquake reshaped the worldview and values of corporate citizens in a disastrous way, catalyzing the boundless passion of the soon-to-open Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital, elevating its dedication to society during the disaster. This passion includes respect for life, love for compatriots, and more significantly, responsibility to the nation, the state, and society.

Chen Huojun, chairman of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital, stated: "Balancing societal contribution with self-development" is the purpose of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital. Contributing to society includes offering excellent technology, first-class service, and sincere love. The fundraising of 1 million yuan for the "Ideal Hope Primary School" project is merely the beginning of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital's public welfare endeavors. In the future, Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital has the responsibility and obligation to bring light to all eye disease patients, continue to promote the spirit of love, track leading domestic and international ophthalmic medical technologies, exchange the latest global ophthalmic medical achievements, realize the vision of synchronizing Suzhou ophthalmic medical development with international standards, allowing local residents to enjoy cutting-edge domestic and international ophthalmic medical levels, keeping various eye diseases and visual impairments away, and promoting the development of nationwide blindness prevention and treatment.

He further expressed: "Ophthalmology is a noble and beautiful cause that brings light to all humanity. Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital has the confidence and determination to become a flagship in the domestic and even international ophthalmology field, creating an international hospital branded by dedication."

His words moved us, and we firmly believe that whether it's dedicating brightness, love, or contributing to society, when Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital opens, we will cheer for it with enthusiastic applause! Together, we will add bricks to the great ideal staircase of Soochow University Affiliated Ideal Eye Hospital's tomorrow.

A harmonious ideal eye hospital will make all eye disease patients' eyes suddenly shine!

A developing ideal eye hospital will undoubtedly win the trust and admiration of the world!
