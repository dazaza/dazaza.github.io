An 85-year-old man survived after his heart stopped for 15 minutes.

by hcjahk89 on 2012-02-13 13:13:02

iyaxin.com (by reporter Fajun Gou) - "If it weren't for the emergency rescue by the medical staff, my father might not be alive today." On the afternoon of the 2nd day, in the ICU of the 120 Emergency Center at Yanqi County People's Hospital in Xinjiang, Junping Guo said emotionally while looking at his father, Zhilian Guo, who had regained consciousness.

After news spread that the elderly man's heart had stopped, relatives and friends rushed to Yanqi County, and the family also prepared to arrange his funeral. However, what they didn't expect was that the elderly man miraculously survived. The entire family presented a banner to the hospital to express their deep gratitude.

Director Xuejiang Yu of the ICU said that when the elderly man was first brought in, his heartbeat and breathing had stopped, and his pupils were dilated. He immediately organized the medical staff to perform cardiopulmonary resuscitation. After 40 minutes of emergency rescue, the elderly man was out of life-threatening danger; his heartbeat resumed autonomous movement, and he had weak breathing, but the patient was in a deep coma. 28 days later, the elderly man regained consciousness. Director Xuejiang Yu said that the elderly man had suddenly experienced a myocardial infarction at the time.

Junping Guo said that his father is 85 years old this year. On the afternoon of April 14th, his 78-year-old mother, Xin Zhang, was riding an electric scooter with her husband to enjoy a walk at the square. On the way home, she noticed that her husband had tightly closed eyes, was foaming at the mouth, and was unconscious. This scared Xin Zhang very much. She touched her husband's nostrils and found that he had already stopped breathing. She quickly called for help, and a young man nearby immediately dialed 120. Just then, an ambulance from Yanqi County Maternal and Child Health Hospital passed by, and within 15 minutes, the elderly man was sent to the emergency department of Yanqi County People's Hospital.
