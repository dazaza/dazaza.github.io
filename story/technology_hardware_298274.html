"The Son of Light" (Fantasy category) - Completed

A lazy teenager chooses to study the neglected light magic due to his temperament and unintentionally steps into the wheel of fate, gradually becoming a legendary grand wizard. Through his efforts, he stops the division between the eastern and western continents, unifying the entire continent so there is no longer any racial distinction, becoming the revered "Son of Light" by all races.

Key words of the novel: Son of Light, Changgong Wei

"The Kind Death God" (Fantasy category) - Completed

Originally a destitute youth on the streets who earns a living through "fishing," he accidentally embarks on a long journey to avenge his master's death. His ancestral master imparts all his knowledge, but will A Dai turn into a demon from being around assassins? When the blood sun rises, monsters will appear; when blood rain falls upon the world, disaster will ensue. As a millennium-long calamity is about to hit the continent, who is the savior? The union of kindness and evil, the unification of light and darkness. With the blood of the phoenix as the guide, crossing numerous barriers, and with the blood of the divine dragon as the conclusion, love becomes eternal?

Key words of the novel: Death God

"Raging God" (Fantasy category) - Completed

This book is another masterpiece after "The Son of Light." This story takes place in a world filled with battles. On the Jin Yuan continent, many races coexist, among which humans, demons, and beastmen are the most powerful. To fight for more land and resources, wars have never ceased for hundreds of years. In this chaotic era, our protagonist is born as a half-human, half-demon, half-beastman hybrid. What experiences will he undergo? What will be the final outcome? Please continue to follow this book.

Key words of the novel: Raging God

"Sky Speeding Star Trace" (Urban Supernatural) - Completed

Dark supernatural abilities are full of devouring power, while space supernatural abilities are full of mystery.

Two different powers simultaneously appear in one person.

Can eyes also kill? That is the power of space.

Can men also be raped by beautiful women? That is the destiny of the protagonist.

Bewitching vampire beauties, holy supernormal saintesses, mysterious soul sacrificer witches, they will all appear around the protagonist and change his fate.

Key words of the novel: Tian Hen, lily, supernatural ability, darkness, space

"Only I Am Immortal" (Eastern Xianxia) - Completed

Heaven and earth, only I am immortal (the protagonist's favorite saying, also his dream, and of course, the dream comes true, he is truly a god, or can even say the god of gods, only he deserves the title of god, I can only describe him with one word: Only I Am Immortal.)

In the great land of China, at the western border, because of mockery from his best friend, he walks on a path he has never imagined.

Lianyun Immortal Sect, the "last" of the seven major righteous sects of China, once every five years, receives disciples, welcoming the only beggar-like fifth-generation disciple.

From this point on, the door to cultivation opens, can the mischievous and smart child grow up to become a god that dominates the heavens? Read this article - Only I Am Immortal.

Main characters: Hai Long, Piao Miao, Tian Qin, Hong Zhi, Ying, Li Feng

Key words of the novel: Hai Long, Piao Miao, Tian Qin

"Guardian of the Zodiac" (Urban category) - Completed

Twelve zodiac signs symbolize twelve months, but no one knows that they also symbolize twelve guardians.

Twelve guardians inheriting the bloodline of the zodiac.

They possess the abilities of their own zodiac signs, silently protecting the East.

Qilin, the auspiciousness of the East. When the king with Qilin blood arrives, he will command the twelve zodiacs to dedicate all their strength to protect the East.

Rogue Qilin, roaming the human world, urban mythology, twelve zodiacs, all within this book.

Key words of the novel: zodiac, Qilin, East, West, Ox King

"Emperor of Strings" (Fantasy category) - Completed

Pioneering music magic, overturning previous settings, red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet, the rainbow level will become the standard for all martial arts and magic.

Dragon, no longer an invincible creature, this will be a high dragon novel.

Psychic sound master, a branch of mental magicians, the noblest and most useless profession on the Long Qin Nu Si continent. But is it really completely useless?

An unprecedentedly pure heart of a stringed instrument, a generation of string magic master, quietly born in the Blue Sky Sea. This will be the story of a simple youth gradually becoming the emperor of strings. But is he really just the emperor of strings?

Key words of the novel: Ye Yin Zhu, String, Magic, Combat Qi, Beastman, Dragon

"Spirit Land" (Otherworldly Continent)

Tang San, an outer disciple of the Tang Sect, was expelled for secretly learning the inner sect’s ultimate techniques and jumped off a cliff to prove his integrity, only to arrive in another world, a world belonging to spirit souls, named Spirit Land.

There is no magic, no combat qi, no martial arts here, but there are miraculous spirit souls. Everyone in this world, at the age of six, will awaken their spirit soul in the Spirit Soul Temple. Spirit souls can be animals, plants, objects, helping people in their daily lives. Some particularly outstanding spirit souls can be used for cultivation, and this profession is the most powerful and important one on Spirit Land - Spirit Master.

When the secret weapons of the Tang Sect arrive in Spirit Land, when Tang San awakens his spirit soul, can he restore the glory of the Tang Sect in this world of spirit souls?

Professional levels: Spirit Scholar, Spirit Master, Great Spirit Master, Spirit Respected One, Spirit Patriarch, Spirit King, Spirit Emperor, Spirit Saint, Spirit Warlord, Titled Spirit Warlord.

Key words of the novel: Secret Weapons, Spirit Master, Spirit Power, Spirit Ring, Spirit Bone, Spirit Land Tang San
