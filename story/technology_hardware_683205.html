[Editor's Recommendation]

Why are some people able to work effortlessly and earn a lot of money, while you cannot? Why do some people earn hundreds of thousands of yuan annually from the same job, while you struggle to make ends meet? What is the reason? If you only think about working for others and focus solely on your tasks without caring about anything else, you will never earn much money. Only by treating the company’s affairs as your own can you stand out at work, become an excellent employee, and earn a lot of money in your job.

Chapter Five: Building Workplace Relationships, Networking for Personal Connections

In workplace interactions, there exists a contradiction between competition and cooperation, whether with bosses or colleagues. Chapter Six: Adhering to Professional Ethics, Being Responsible for Oneself

As ancient wisdom states: "In one's position, perform one's duties." Here, "duties" not only refer to doing one's assigned tasks well but also encompass serious responsibility, loyalty, trustworthiness, enthusiasm, and positivity as professional ethics. Adhering to professional ethics benefits the company, colleagues, and fosters healthy industry development. More importantly, it holds oneself accountable and maintains integrity.
