Urban development experience shows that the government relocation to achieve new city development and drive large development groups and enterprises to enter is the fastest way to the prosperity of a new city. With the Dali Prefecture Government's relocation to the Dali Haidong Administrative New Area, neighboring the Erhai International Eco-City, the prosperity and development of this area are just around the corner.

Golden location, sharing the value of Dali's new administrative center, the future core of the city!

The Dali Prefecture Government has officially moved in, and the municipal government’s eastward relocation plan is under conception. The Erhai International Eco-City was born in the administrative center area, condensing the brand-new vitality of the city's future development, integrating residential, business, shopping, and entertainment!

The first row of Cangshan and Erhai, Dali's only peninsula surrounded by water on three sides!

Erhai has only one peninsula surrounded by water on three sides, like a diamond on the crown of Erhai, only here can the unparalleled scenery of Cangshan and Erhai be collected, facing the nineteen peaks of Cangshan directly, embraced by sea views on three sides, a place unique in the world.

A construction scale of 42 billion yuan, achieving the golden value of the new urban area!

Real estate giants such as Yunnan Urban Investment, Huabin Group, and Wuzhou International Group have an investment scale exceeding 42 billion yuan, creating a bustling commercial core area, and the quality life of a big city is like living in the new center of the city.

Seize the sea-view property, enjoy the global value asset

Sanya, wintering like migratory birds, with the main landscape resources being coastal scenery!

The beautiful beaches and blue seas of Sanya are very attractive, and its warm and humid climate in winter attracts people like migrating birds to spend the winter in Sanya! However, it is unbearably hot in summer, making it unsuitable for vacations, and most of the landscape resources are concentrated along the coastline!

Sanya vacation properties, with a price increase of over 200% in three years, but high investment costs and slowing growth!

In 2008, the price of Sanya vacation properties did not exceed ten thousand yuan, but by 2010, after the approval of the international tourist island, the price of Sanya sea-view properties quickly rose to 28,000-40,000 yuan per square meter, with an increase of more than 200%. However, the potential value has been overdrawn, not only with high investment costs, but also possibly slower future growth!

Hainan can only be lived in during winter, while Dali is suitable for living all year round.

Dali, suitable for living all year round, has a mild spring-like climate throughout the four seasons, with breathtaking scenery of Cangshan and Erhai!

Dali boasts no less impressive tourism resources than Hainan. Dali's thousand-year-old culture is brilliant, with enchanting and rich ethnic customs. There are eighteen lively streams, nineteen snow-capped peaks, and the most famous Erhai scenery!

Dali sea-view property, increased by 4,000 yuan in three years, hurry up to seize the value lowland of sea-view vacation property!

With its mountain-water livable climate, Dali has gained increasing recognition. In 2008, sea-view properties were about 3,800 yuan per square meter. Now, the Erhai International Eco-City has reached 7,000-8,000 yuan per square meter! But compared to Sanya, the seafront properties along Dali's coastline, which also boast magnificent sea views, are still in a value lowland, worthy of investment!
