1. Bedroom in the bridal suite should not place flowers, water plants, fish tanks, etc. Elders have said that things with too much "water" energy like flowers, water plants and fish tanks should not be placed in the bedroom. If they are placed in the bedroom, it's easy to extinguish the fire of love between the couple. You wouldn't want the passion to disappear soon after the wedding, so it's better to be careful.

2. Mirrors in the bridal suite bedroom should not face the bed. Many people know this taboo, but do you know why? The answer is that people usually look ugly when they wake up in the morning. Maybe your wife next to you, after a night of sleep, looks almost like Miss贞子. For the safety of both of you, it's better to move the mirror far away. It's not good to scare your beloved one.

3. Bridal suite decoration needs "bridal paint". Nowadays, it's not easy to marry a wife, at least ten thousand yuan is needed. Everything is ready except for the decoration of the bridal suite, which is very important and cannot be taken lightly. The choice of coatings is particularly important. Otherwise, the whole indoor air pollution will harm health, cause incurable diseases, and even affect the next generation. We remind you to be cautious again and again. However, some manufacturers now offer "bridal paint", which is suitable for new house decoration and environmentally friendly without odor.

4. The color of the bridal suite decoration should not be too bright. Although it's a bridal suite, colors shouldn't only pursue festivity. Try to use less dark red and more soft colors with simple decoration. Red is passionate, but the couple is living life together. Too bright colors can easily make people feel tired, making it hard to rest well.

5. Do not pile up clutter under the bed in the bridal suite bedroom. Under the bed is often a poorly ventilated and dark place. Placing clutter there can easily lead to dampness, mold or bacteria growth. Also, it's difficult to clean regularly, causing hygiene dead zones, which troubles the young wife when cleaning. Moreover, from the perspective of qi field, piling up clutter under the bed can easily affect personal qi field, leading to nightmares and poor sleep quality.

6. Bedroom should not have too many electrical appliances, especially TV facing the foot of the bed. There should not be too many electrical appliances in the bridal suite, one television is enough. In Feng Shui, too many electrical appliances in the bedroom is called "fire house", which affects health and causes quarrels between couples. For the sweetness and happiness of the couple, it's better to pay attention. Modern medical theory points out that radiation from electrical appliances indeed harms human health. Feet are the second heart of human body. A TV in standby state facing the foot of the bed can easily affect the meridian operation and blood circulation of the feet, easily causing diseases.
