3. CCTV Weather Forecast Theme Song - Reportedly the only unaltered background music ever used by a television station, continuously used by the Weather Forecast program. Adapted from the traditional piece "Fishing Boats Sing at Dusk" by Shanghai-based electronic keyboardist Pu Qizhang. She was once a solo performer with the Shanghai Ensemble and graduated from the "Little Star" Art Troupe, collaborating with the Shanghai Light Music Orchestra on many popular tunes such as "Happy Dai Countryside." Composer Tu Bahai often collaborated with her. After this piece was completed, Pu Qizhang retired from the music scene. However, the piece gained widespread popularity in China due to its adoption as the background music for the prime-time program "Weather Forecast." When Pu Qizhang adapted this piece using a Yamaha concert electronic keyboard with three rows of keys, she did not anticipate that it would become the golden background music for a prime-time show or influence so many people.

Link: http://www.cqcdbs.com/ertong/ysxss/yy/MUSIC/Yzcw.mp3
