My pain is something only I can understand.

by lili1 on 2011-12-31 00:43:04

My pain is only understood by myself.

I always like to reminisce about the past during such lonely nights - those memories deeply buried in my heart, what I've gained, possessed, and lost, all feel like a fleeting dream. I've always known that I shouldn't be obsessed with the past; forgetting what I shouldn't remember, letting go of everything, can bring me brief relief. Actually, I fear the deep night because an endless loneliness will envelop me; yet, I also love the night because only when surrounded by pitch darkness are my tears and I safe.

I recall a promise I once made, but now I understand that feelings aren't something we can control. No one knows what tomorrow will bring. Since we possess it now, let's learn to enjoy it. Perhaps there's hope as long as there's existence. Don't wait until the day love loses its original color and the promise becomes worthless. Everything in this world has a beginning and an end, and so do feelings. When the game is over, whether it's destruction or liberation, it can only be endured alone.

Someone once said, "With love, there's no harm;" another said, "With love, there's no separation." I only know that when I think of these words, my heart aches. Heaven knows how much I need the warmth of such love. If I could, I'd wish to still possess it peacefully at the moment of my life's end. But in your cold words, I realize all this has been buried in so-called happiness, lost completely. I once buried love deep within my heart, thinking it was the safest place, not knowing that this distance hurt me the most.

Love is the bond of two people's feelings, and it's also the sincere coming together of two hearts. In life, we can't live without love. A life without love is meaningless, just like two people living together without any feelings - it's not only a physical torment, but also a spiritual decay and mental torture. Too materialistic things won't last long, too realistic issues are merely illusions. Loving someone who doesn't love you back is just a burden for the other person, and trusting someone's promises too much will only make you lose yourself even more! In the end, you can only hide in a corner alone, crying with a weary body.

Vows of eternal love may just be fantasies in today's real life. Most relationships exist between money and rationality, and often money prevails. Someone once told me, in today's society, having money means having feelings; sometimes, feelings are worth nothing before money. Although this statement is too extreme, it truly reflects people's yearning for life and material desires. It's not to say that those choosing such lives are greedy, because everyone chooses their way of living differently. Looking at a problem from different perspectives reveals different meanings, but don't let reality cloud your mind!

Referring to the emotions around me, I once thought I was happy. But suddenly I find both my palms and backs of hands are icy cold, and I can't find anything to warm me up. Am I still happy? Have I been deceiving myself all along? Maybe in this world, no one really belongs to anyone else. I can only compromise, bowing to loneliness in the face of love. It turns out, whether loving or not loving, both lead to loneliness. Loving is lonelier than not loving.

Sometimes I think my quietness is indeed terrifying, so cold that it's hard for people to approach, even capable of breaking someone down. In fact, this is just a form of self-protection, a true reflection of inner fragility. If the heart is already cold, what can still be warm? Suddenly, many faces flash through my mind, but they vanish like fireworks. Trying to hold my right hand with my left, I give myself the simplest warmth. No longer expecting others' offerings, I start learning to give myself what I need.

Friends, please cherish the ones you love or who love you nearby. The Buddha said: 500 years brings a mere passing glance between two people. So why can't we cherish the happiness we already have around us?
