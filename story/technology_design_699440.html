The fruit tea aroma training program

by chandadada on 2012-03-09 15:04:20

The Beijing expansion training project "Fruit Tea Fragrance" is a new expansion project exclusively developed by Beijing Baili Expansion Company. This project mainly exercises the spirit of maximizing the overall interests of the team, informing us that each person must make the greatest effort to work in order to welcome the ease of the team and share the fruits of victory. The content of this project is very interesting. Last year, a beauty salon unit in Xiaoxihu Scenic Area did this expansion project, which was particularly popular with everyone. As long as everyone takes it seriously, they can enjoy a fragrant fruit tea! Drinking such healthy drinks while strolling in the green natural oxygen bar makes one feel that life is too beautiful, so just enjoy it at this moment! The spirit of expansion lies in developing divergent thinking and creating the greatest value. The expansion project "Fruit Tea Fragrance" is a large-scale expansion project for cultivating team spirit, and also interprets this spirit in Beijing expansion training. In the picturesque Xiaoxihu Scenic Area, the entire team is divided into three groups trekking on the road of Xiaoxihu. Each team member holds handbags of various sizes, inside which are our project supplies. There are Job's tears, dried dates, rock sugar, fresh milk, bottled mineral water, raisins, outdoor stove, drinking cups and other supplies. Before long, we arrived at the destination, an open-air barbecue area covering more than 20 meters in diameter. First, according to the coach's arrangement, everyone sat down in three parts by small teams. The coach placed all the supplies in a fixed position, with dedicated personnel managing these supplies and distributing them according to the coach's instructions. The amount of supplies each small team can receive is determined by the accuracy rate of the team members' answers, which also affects whether the fruit tea made tastes delicious. It turns out that the rules are as follows: the coach will ask many questions about the daily work of the beauty salon based on its leadership. We divide these questions into three major parts: mandatory questions, where each correct answer allows you to arbitrarily pick one supply, while wrong answers give no chance to pick supplies;抢answer questions, where each correct answer allows you to pick two supplies, while wrong answers give nothing; optional questions, where each correct answer allows you to pick five supplies, while wrong answers deduct five supplies. The project began with the sound of the coach's whistle, and the competition was fierce! Everyone brainstormed, vying for opportunities, after a round of competition, one small team made a really authentic fruit tea, the faint rice fragrance mixed with milk aroma, sweet and very tasty, while the fruit teas made by the other two teams were just average, only drinkable. Seeing everyone enjoying the deliciousness they made themselves, feeling the joyous atmosphere, and being surrounded by the picturesque scenery, it was truly heartwarming. Only happiness after labor is the most blissful. I am truly inspired by these excellent team members, they are so lively, united, and smart, I am proud of them!
