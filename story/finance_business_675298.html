A woman's life cannot be trampled on.

"Do you understand?"

"There are two kinds of beauty in the world: external beauty and internal beauty. External beauty may satisfy your vanity but doesn't necessarily bring lifelong happiness. Internal beauty may not give you enough face-saving, but it certainly brings lifelong happiness. Women...is face-saving important...or happiness important..."

Woman: Can we be friends?

Man: No, I don't need female friends other than my girlfriend!

Man: First, we don't share the same language, girls don't like the things I usually play with; second, you can't meet the needs that my girlfriend can give me; third, I don't have time to shop and eat with you, those are things I do with my girlfriend; fourth, my girlfriend would be jealous. So why bother?

The proudest thing for a man is not sleeping with many women, but having one woman willing to let him sleep with her for a lifetime.

The proudest thing for a woman is not having many men, but having her man willing to refuse so many other women.

Men, must resist temptation. Women, must endure loneliness.
