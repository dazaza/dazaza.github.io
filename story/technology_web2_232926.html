Alena is a beautiful Russian girl. She is not only an expert in human history and prehistoric civilization but also a skilled martial arts expert and rock climbing enthusiast, often hired by various foundations to explore and search for prehistoric relics around the world. Green is an so-called archaeologist who conducts tomb raiding and cultural relics smuggling under the guise of archaeology. In an accident, he discovered an ancient ruin and unraveled the mystery of the end of the world from it. In the distant past, there was already a highly developed prehistoric civilization on Earth. They predicted that one-tenth of future humans would die due to an apocalypse caused by solar storms. For this reason, the seven civilizations of prehistoric Earth each created a talisman with miraculous powers. As long as one talisman eventually survives to the future, all humanity can escape the disaster. However, the ancients underestimated the greed and darkness of human nature. Professor Green sold his research results on prehistoric ruins and apocalyptic disasters to the world's largest arms organization. In the eyes of this organization, humanity's disaster is a great opportunity to make money in chaos and further control the world. They dispatched mercenaries led by Professor Green to begin searching worldwide for the origins of the other six prehistoric civilizations.

When Alena learned that someone was willing to fund her to look for prehistoric ruins in the Middle East, she accepted happily and brought along her boyfriend, with whom she had some minor conflicts, hoping to resolve their differences through this easy exploration trip. But the beautiful Middle Eastern coast could not untie the knots in her lover's heart. Eventually, her boyfriend and Alena parted ways unhappily, leaving her alone to continue searching for traces of prehistoric civilization on the cliffs by the coast. Hard work pays off. In an accident, Alena discovered the remains of the origin of prehistoric culture - the last uncontrolled ruins by the arms organization. Unaware of the truth, Alena reported the news to Professor Green. Before waiting for the organization's subsequent personnel, the heartbroken Alena wandered around the ruins and picked up a pretty little stone - the talisman of prehistoric civilization as a souvenir. She didn't realize that danger was coming to her. Professor Green quickly arrived at the ruins site, accompanied by several armed helicopters, amphibious tanks, and a large number of fully armed international mercenaries. Seeing this grand scene, sensitive Alena instinctively realized that something must be amiss. She immediately found an opportunity to escape from the excavation site, which made the arms organization's plan to eliminate her fail. The arms organization, finding nothing after searching the ruins thoroughly, was extremely angry and sent out large forces to search for Alena's whereabouts in the Middle East region. Relying on her excellent skills and the protection of the prehistoric talisman, Alena narrowly escaped multiple times. Back in Russia, Alena, with the help of her friends, unraveled the secrets recorded in the prehistoric ruins and mastered the use of the talisman. At this point, Alena began to plan a counterattack.
