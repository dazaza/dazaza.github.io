Web2.0Share Weekly: aihow.com, Jifutong, Ruobin, Maerhutong, etc.

by ybcz0519 on 2011-09-07 15:18:17

Web2.0Share Weekly is a featured column of Tianya Haige, every week we carefully select the latest outstanding startups in China to share with everyone. Startups can submit their information through Weibo: @web20share or email: watsonxu{at}web20share.com.

AiHou website adopts the latest voice recognition evaluation system developed by iFlytek, providing online singing, online karaoke, network karaoke, and singing evaluation services. It can also provide real-time singing evaluation and scoring, becoming your personal online KTV. You can invite friends to sing together.

Link: http://www.ihou.com/

YouYan is a social comment plugin provided for webmasters. Users can log in using seven social networks (Sina Weibo, Renren, Tencent Weibo, QQ Space, Sohu Weibo, Netease Weibo, Kaixin), and comments will be automatically forwarded to social networks.

Link: http://uyan.cc/

JiFuTong (@JiFuTong): Using XunSound technology, ultrasonic signal transmission distance is controllable. JiFuTong is as simple as typical NFC applications: just wave at the payment terminal, information transmission begins immediately, and payment transactions are completed swiftly. Supports iPhone, iPad, Android and other mobile terminals. Video introduction available.

Link: http://www.gifront.com/

TuKeQuan: A website that provides travel solutions for the social era!

Link: http://tukeq.com/

Ruobin (English name: Zorpia) is a social networking service site. The main functions include photos, albums, blogs, social networks, customized homepages, comment systems, discussion areas, music and video sharing and storage. The company headquarters is located in Hong Kong, China.

Link: http://www.zorpia.cn/

MaiMang is a transaction application based on geographical location, providing users with fast and convenient buying and selling experiences. Currently offers an Android client.

Link: http://maimang.co/

Douban Alpha Town has launched its domain (alphatown.com) and will officially open to the public at the end of August. Douban Alpha Town currently has 6 Fangs, each Fang has 7 streets, and each street has a maximum limit of 800 people. People entering the city can post in all street discussion areas. Each street's facilities include: bulletin board, voting, public discussion area, activities popular among residents (online), public photo album, message board, etc. For more details, you can go inside and explore.

Link: http://alphatown.com/

Discovery: A location-based photo interaction software. You can discover photos within 300 meters, browse, comment, or share them on Weibo. You can also make friends and follow their fresh pictures from other places. Currently offers iPhone and Android clients.

Link: http://www.faxian.cn/

FanFanLe: A platform where Taobao sellers provide prizes and Taobao buyers play the FanFanLe game to win prizes.

Link: http://www.fanfanle.com/

MaoEr Hutong is an online mall under Beijing Tulundingye Clothing Co., Ltd., focusing on caps and aiming to create a professional and authoritative cap direct sales website in China. Will there be more vertical websites in e-commerce in the future?

Link: http://www.maoer360.com/
