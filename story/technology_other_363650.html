12 Hidden Uses of Coca-Cola

by geekzhang on 2010-09-01 20:19:42

Coca-Cola is not only good to drink, but also has many household uses!

The experiments done to make this list were based on plain, plain Coca-Cola; It's not Diet Coke, it's not some new breed.

1. Pour cola into a hot saucepan and bring to a boil to remove all stains.

2. To give a modern photo the dark brown look of an old one, it's easy to just gently brush Coca-Cola onto the surface of the photo and let it dry quickly. Be careful not to get it too wet or the photo will be distorted. Similarly, black and white photos will look more like "ashes" if they are treated with Coke. Maps can look classically chic in the same way.

3. If you dye your hair and find it too dark, a flat Coke can help lighten it.

4. Dipping old coins into Coke makes them shiny, collectable and more decorative.

5. Pour Coca-Cola into a pot and let it sit for a day to remove dirt and keep the inside spotless.

6. Pour a can of Coke into the toilet and use it as a cleaner. The acid in the coke will take effect immediately.

7. Mixing cola and ketchup one by one makes a really delicious barbecue sauce, and spreading it on chicken and beef just before grilling is delicious and mouthwatering.

8. Flat Coke is a great conditioner. Pour it over your head, rinse and dry it, and your hair will look new.

9. Placing a plate full of coke in the garden can repel bugs from plants. These bugs love the sweet taste, and once they get their hands on coke, they're stuck.

10. If it is difficult to twist a rusty bolt, we can use a cloth soaked in coke to wrap the bolt, and wait for a few hours, the screw will naturally turn easily.

11. You wash your jewelry with a glass of Coke, gently rub it on the surface with a toothbrush and rinse it off. (It is recommended not to wash valuables or things inlaid with gems with cola)

12. Flat Coca-Cola can cure stomach upset, but do not drink fresh Coke, because this will aggravate the condition. (If needed, add a little sugar to get the gas out of the cola.)
