The new session of the property fair is about to open, and the Erhai International Eco-city (forum house type sample album review map search) has launched marketing activities frequently. The previous activity for Teachers' Day allowed teachers to have the opportunity to experience Dali for free. It is reported that customers who newly purchased the subscription card this month can also receive such an invitation.

In addition, the launch of the Happy Organic Farm allows both new and old customers to be given vegetable plots according to the size of their housing area. This allows guests to enjoy the fun of personal farming and harvest. Not only that, special preparations were made for a tourism and vacation fund specifically targeting Kunming customers. With an annual quota of 5000 yuan for 5 years, the total amount is 25,000 yuan, ecological large-scale development. This fund will serve as a subsidy for oil expenses and other items for Kunming customers going to Dali. All customers who purchased housing can enjoy this subsidy. At the same time, new customers can also choose to offset this amount against the house price.

It is understood that the project has been launched multiple times, but due to phased development, the properties are also being launched in batches, with many more high-quality property types to come. This month, 67 sea-view apartments of the final phase of the First Bay are being launched. Because the project is surrounded by Erhai Lake on three sides, the undulating coastline forms a bay, so the location of buildings 1 and 2 is called the First Bay. The buildings located at the First Bay face the sea without obstruction, offering a broad view and being closer to Erhai Lake.

Project Introduction:

Located in Haidong, Dali, the Erhai International Eco-city may be the most beautiful peninsula in the world. It is located next to the Dali State and Municipal Governments, west of the coastal avenue, 3 kilometers from the city center, 7 kilometers from Dali Airport, adjacent to the state and municipal government office areas. Its unique position has become the vanguard of the new development zone in Dali, making it the first station to enter and develop Haidong.

On this peninsula surrounded by sea on three sides, there is a 400-mu ecological wetland, a 680-mu ecological health complex, a clear water surface of 260 square kilometers, a 3.3-kilometer peninsula coastline, a historical and cultural heritage of over 1200 years, nearly ten thousand negative oxygen ions per cubic centimeter keep the lungs comfortable, an average humidity of 56% keeps the body doing aerobic yoga... ... Most importantly, the price of such luxury sea-view apartments is only one-fourth of similar properties in Hainan, making it a new emerging investment wealth hotspot.

The Erhai International Eco-city is backed by Long Mountain and the administrative office area of the Dali State Government, neighboring the Binhe Avenue food street. It takes 2 minutes by car to the Dali Port ferry terminal, about 3 minutes to Walmart, about 5 minutes to the train station and bus station, and about 7 minutes to the airport and the future intercity train station. Rarely does such a great natural ecological environment coexist with convenient living facilities, having both fish and bear's paw. Under the call of the Dali State Government for two protections and two developments, the original northwest urban area of Hai and the old urban area of Xiaguan can only wait for urban renewal, and there will be no more residential projects right by the Erhai Lake. Therefore, the Erhai International Eco-city can be said to possess all comprehensive advantages such as location, environment, landscape, and supporting facilities, gathering various resources into one, making it irreplaceable. The wise find joy in mountains, the intelligent find joy in water; the Erhai International Eco-city, residing in a place of superior mountains and waters, aims to create a model of lakeside resort leisure living.

Within the district, property planning mainly includes eco-island landscape townhouses, peninsula sea-view apartments, and peninsula courtyard villas, surrounded by eight community landscape parks.
