US Netizens Use iPhone to Record the Entire Process of Purchasing an iPad

by heimeirem on 2010-04-08 02:16:19

On April 3 in the U.S., Nokia phones and Apple will officially start selling iPads in 221 Apple retail stores across the U.S. and related vendors, with the pre-scheduled release time being 9 a.m. local time in the U.S. However, due to the large number of time zones spanning across the U.S., this has caused the iPad release in different time zones across the U.S. to occur at different times, allowing members within our network who are based in the U.S. to be fortunate enough to purchase the long-awaited iPad relatively quickly at retail stores. Now, let's take a look at the purchasing situation of American members who have already bought the iPad.

Kron2000 is from Connecticut. To buy the iPad, kron2000 had to drive for 50 minutes to get to the Mall, where mobile phone themes can be downloaded. Kron2000 used their first-generation iPhone to document the entire process of purchasing the iPad. However, since it was a first-generation iPhone, the video quality wasn't very clear, but you can still download mobile phone pictures.
