Cost-performance Ratio First: Recommended Popular 880G Motherboards in the Mainstream Market

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-04 10:19:45

The launch of AMD's latest Llano processor has finally turned the integration of CPU and GPU from a concept into reality. Motherboards based on the A75 chipset will gradually replace the current mainstream status of the 880G and 870, but the 880G motherboard, having developed to this point, offers an excellent balance of maturity and price. Therefore, the 880G remains the best choice for many consumers at present. Below, I will recommend several outstandingly cost-effective 880G models for your reference.

Product Information: Yington A880GT War God

Reference Price: 399 yuan

Recommendation Reason: The Yington A880GT War God motherboard is the first in its class priced at 399 yuan, with 128M of onboard video memory, and equipped with a gigabit independent network card, offering remarkable value for money.

Yington A880GT War God

The Yington A880GT War God uses the AMD 880G+SB710 chipset, supporting AMD Socket AM3 Series CPUs and the core-unlocking function. It also supports the new 6-core CPUs and various memory specifications such as DDR3 1333(OC)/1066.

Power Supply Section

The Yington A880GT War God features a luxurious 4-phase power supply, all using 1 upper bridge 2 lower bridge MOSFETs, high-quality low-temperature enclosed inductors, and ultra-long-life solid-state capacitors. The components are sufficient and reliable, fully meeting the power needs of a 6-core CPU.

Memory Slots

The Yington A880GT War God provides 2 DDR3 DIMM memory slots, supporting dual-channel DDR3 1333(OC)/1066 memory specifications, with a maximum support of 8G capacity. It also offers 1 PCI-E X16 graphics card slot, 1 PCI-E X1 slot, and 1 PCI slot, demonstrating strong expansion capabilities.

I/O Section

In the I/O section, the Yington A880GT War God provides one PS/2 interface, full display interfaces including HDMI+D-sub+DVI, 4 USB interfaces, a gigabit network card, and audio interfaces. It is equipped with the Realtek RTL811E network chip and the Realtek ALC662 six-channel sound card chip.
