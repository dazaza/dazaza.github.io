In the midstream of the war, technology is the first combat capability.

by 56963tg on 2008-03-14 10:23:50

Science and technology is the primary productive force - this simple truth is one that all friends who have experienced junior high school life must understand. However, in the context of midstream warfare, science and technology represents the primary combat capability! One could say that the strength of a guild largely depends on the progress of its scientific research. Only by possessing formidable technological power can a guild enhance its overall strength and secure a dominant position in the fiercely competitive and intense world of warfare. So, let's take a brief look at the crucial role that science and technology play in midstream warfare.

Firstly, we know that enhancing the technological strength of a guild can increase its level, as well as its defensive capabilities. Moreover, the advancement of guild technology can also strengthen the abilities of its members, which is closely related to every member. So, how can we accelerate the improvement of guild technology to achieve the goal of comprehensively enhancing the abilities of all members? Let’s first examine some very critical factors.

We know that to enhance the technological strength of a guild, we need to conduct scientific research. Conducting this research requires substantial financial and material resources, as well as various types of research materials and items. These items are divided into three categories: random drops, fixed drops, and those sold by NPCs.

Random drop items: These items are familiar to players even at low levels. For example, "Blood of the Fallen" can be randomly dropped by any monster with the prefix "Fallen." Therefore, collecting these items can either contribute to the guild or be sold. Since the drop rate isn't very high, it is recommended to prioritize providing them for guild use. Any excess obtained while leveling up can be considered for sale later. The characteristic of these items is that there is a certain chance of obtaining them by killing a specific type of monster. Thus, they are relatively numerous and easier to obtain. There are a total of 17 such items, including shredded cloth, blood of the fallen, fine fabric, cotton velvet, feathered spirit shards, fly slime, flying dragon scales, hard beetle scales, fire energy crocodile hearts, spider slime, gamma insect eggs, giant ape fur, strange crocodile skin, carrion beast innards, fly eggs, rhino turtle shells, and terrain dragon tails.

Fixed drop items: These items can only be obtained from specific monsters at specific locations. Currently, there are only six such items: bio-dog chips, spiny beast chips, desert flower chips, energy cards, test leader medals, and Mamer's memory card. These six items are produced in the virtual training ground and laboratory. Only by defeating designated monsters in these two dungeons can you obtain these six items. Among them, bio-dog chips drop from simulated bio-dogs; spiny beast chips drop from simulated iron-armored spiny beasts; desert flower chips drop from simulated desert flowers; energy cards drop from small monsters in the laboratory; test leader medals only drop from the three test leaders in the laboratory—Weapon Test Leader Perco, Armor Test Leader Naggada, and Energy Test Leader Tama. These are large bosses, making the medal a rather difficult item to obtain. After all, currently, there aren't many teams capable of smoothly clearing the dungeon and defeating all the bosses, given the high difficulty and time constraints of the dungeon. Another item, Mamer's memory card, only drops from the laboratory supervisor Mamer. This item is the hardest to obtain among all fixed items, as many teams don't even know what Mamer looks like. Therefore, to quickly improve the guild's technology, it is necessary to form a powerful team to collect these chips.

NPC-sold items

At the general store merchant, there are fewer items available for purchase, only three. However, these little gadgets aren’t cheap—they require quite a bit of effort to acquire. Nevertheless, these items are high-grade goods in the process of upgrading technology, especially after reaching higher tech levels! These three items are experimental apparatus, testing instruments, and precision testing instruments. Their prices are 100, 1000, and 1000 Warfire Coins respectively.

We know that upgrading the technology level brings numerous benefits to guild members, including increases in strength, agility, intelligence attributes, and even health recovery speed. However, Rome wasn't built in a day. How can we reasonably upgrade our technology level to achieve maximum effect? First, we need to look at the structure of the tech tree to determine the most optimal upgrade method.

In scientific research, there will be three fundamental technologies that are frequently used, which are also the main branches. Only by researching these fundamental technologies can we unlock the research functions of the next level of technology. They appear when the guild reaches levels 1, 3, and 5. When the guild reaches level 1, the fundamental technology research is called Chemical Harmonizer; when the guild reaches level 3, the fundamental technology is called Chemical Synthesizer; and when the guild reaches level 5, the fundamental technology research is called Chemical Catalyst.

These fundamental technology research projects follow one another. The success of one project serves as the prerequisite for starting the next project. Only by completing the previous technology research project do we have the opportunity to work on the next technology research project. In the multitude of technology projects within guild technology, whether it's improving constitution or intelligence, developing defensive forces or increasing movement speed, all guild technologies must proceed step by step. Similar to these fundamental technology projects, each one has another as its prerequisite.

Therefore, when enhancing the technological capabilities of a guild, managers must consider which direction to develop—whether it's improving the abilities of members or enhancing the guild's defense, etc. Only through reasonable combinations and substantial resource preparation can we quickly enhance the overall strength of the guild to achieve the grand goal of dominating the world!
