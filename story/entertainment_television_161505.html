Masked Rider Decade Episode 21 Plot Summary and Broadcast Information

Masked Rider Decade Episode 21 Plot:

The seventh installment of the Heisei Kamen Rider series, also marking the 35th anniversary of the birth of Kamen Rider. As a commemorative work, insect designs are once again utilized. The protagonist, Tenjo Souji, transforms into Kabuto, known as the strongest rider. The story begins with Shibuya being destroyed by a falling cosmic meteorite. Seven years later, alien creatures known as WORMs appear and start killing humans. Even the efforts of Japan's special organization ZECT prove futile. Member Kagami Shin knows that only the developed belt can match the power of the alien creatures, and it falls into the hands of Tenjo Souji, who possesses a calm mind and agile skills. He gains control of the Kabuto Zector in its beetle form and transforms into Kamen Rider Kabuto to defeat the WORMs.

Seven years prior, along with the meteorite that destroyed Shibuya, came the space creatures WORMs, whose basic form is a green "pupa." They have a uniform appearance with hands covering their faces and can mimic the appearance, behavior, and voice of any human they encounter, engaging in murder and invasion within human society. Even ZECT's military weapons cannot harm the WORMs. When the "pupa" evolves and sheds its skin to become an adult insect, its appearance changes, and its activity and destructive capabilities are greatly enhanced. They can also perform accelerated movements beyond human perception, a phenomenon known as "CLOCK UP."
