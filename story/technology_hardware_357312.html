A Visitor in the Dead of Night

by nlqo9709 on 2010-06-01 15:12:10

Last night, just as I was about to go to bed, I suddenly heard the doorbell ringing loudly. Very unwillingly, I got up, "shuffling" to the door, "Who is it?" The bell continued to ring outside, but no one answered. Who's playing a trick on me? It's so late, I thought it shouldn't be a bad person, so I skipped the step of peeping out through the cat's eye and opened the door directly. "Auntie Xuelian mm", Liu Zhe looked at me pitifully. I looked at the two people standing outside the door curiously. Yun Nan had an invoice in her hand, Kang was carrying a bag, Hunan also had an invoice, she didn't pay attention to me and entered directly. "What's going on? It's so late, and you're holding a bag, aren't you running away from home?" I looked at Kang suspiciously. "What running away from home? It's having a home but can't return!" She has always been extremely casual with me, "Hurry up and give me slippers, I'm exhausted!" I dared not ask any questions and quickly handed over the slippers. Kang is my former colleague and roommate, a very interesting person. She once cried for me because she was worried about my heavy burden when I bought a house. Later, under my persuasion, she also bought a house in our community. During the crazy rise in housing prices, she would mock her own behavior of crying hard for me at that time. She is a nice person, but doesn't have many friends. I am the kind of friend who talks to her without reservation, but I often "criticize" her. She doesn't mind at all. Not only does she not mind, but she often sends me delicious food, making me feel that persuading her to settle down in our community was a wise decision of mine. This time her behavior was too strange. Her couple has always respected each other like guests, living in harmony. How could they come to my house at midnight? And with their child? One question after another came to my mind. After she sat down on the sofa, I asked what happened. Originally, her husband went on a trip out of town, and she took her child to attend extracurricular classes. When they returned home, she found that she forgot to bring the keys. Helplessly, she called her husband hoping he would come back soon, but even after the phone ran out of power, there was no answer. So, she took her child out to eat and then sat in the corridor. Coldness could be endured, but the urge to go to the WC couldn't be resisted. In the end, she had no choice but to come to my place. I was so angry that I scolded her. She should not make herself suffer, let alone make her child suffer. What if the child caught a cold? I remembered that she once left a key at my place, but after she took it, she never put it back. I searched for a long time but couldn't find it, so I had to arrange for them to sleep at my place. Back in the bedroom, my husband asked me two questions: 1. What if I didn't bring the key; 2. If this situation happened, would I blame him. My answers: 1. I definitely wouldn't sit in the corridor with my child. The keys to my house are in the hands of six people. I could just make a call and get the key, but I would absolutely not let my husband out of town know about this matter. Even if I couldn't get the key, I would take my child to relatives or friends' homes, I wouldn't make myself suffer, nor would I pass on negative emotions to my child. 2. If such a situation really happened, I would definitely blame him. The reason is simple, because he takes good care of me every day, making me gradually lose my ability to live independently. Even things like mobile phones and keys are prepared by him, reminding me to take them. If he goes on a business trip and no longer reminds me, then it's his dereliction of duty, not blaming him would be strange! (For this, he severely despised me.) In the morning, I found that they had already gotten up. Given that Liu Zhe didn't wear his school uniform and couldn't carry his schoolbag, today would be a holiday for Children's Day. Kang herself also took a break, taking the child out to play. I warned her not to lose herself, and instructed Liu Zhe to watch over his mother, which made Kang snort and curse me. I don't know where that mother-and-son pair is wandering now. Kang is not a person who likes to move around. Her leg is injured, which further hinders her movement. Today, she will probably not be very comfortable. Her husband will be back in the afternoon. I hope she won't really blame him, and more importantly, don't let a war happen because of this. I will call her tonight, asking her to put a key at my house or Fan's house (one of the keys to my house is at her house) to avoid similar situations happening again. Even if a key is placed at her parents' house, it wouldn't take long to come back by taxi. It's great to have friends, but that person actually felt embarrassed to bother me at first. I wouldn't be that silly. I wouldn't let my child and myself sit in the corridor for half a night. I would fully utilize the relationship with my friends, using them when necessary since we are friends. Therefore, friends, if you have anything, don't think it's a bother to tell Xuelian. To exert effort for friends, Xuelian would consider it an honor!
