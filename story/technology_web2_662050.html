Continued: A girl was run over by two vehicles - Her mother reproached herself for not taking good care of the child

by nbtie357 on 2012-02-09 16:48:16

The visiting hours at the hospital had not yet arrived, and Ms. Qu, Xiao Yueyue's mother, remained stationed by the door of the ICU. Photo taken by Jiaobin Cai from the Yangcheng Evening News. Report by Huang Bochun, Xu Chen, Zhong Chuanfang, Zhou Song, and Lin Yuan for the Yangcheng Evening News: These days, Ms. Qu, Xiao Yueyue’s mother, has been dreaming about her daughter every night, recalling the warm moments they shared, she quietly sheds tears; talking about her daughter's suffering, her face is full of regret. At 3 PM on the afternoon of the 17th, it was once again time to visit Xiao Yueyue, and Ms. Qu looked at her unconscious daughter and called out deeply, “Daughter, please give your mom another chance!”

At around 11:30 AM on the 17th, our reporter met Ms. Qu again outside the ICU at the General Hospital of the Guangzhou Military Region. She had just arrived from Foshan. When the reporter informed her of Xiao Yueyue's latest condition, a faint smile appeared on her weary face. She told the reporter, "Xiao Yueyue is very strong, she will wake up!"

"Xiao Yueyue was very clever, she liked dancing and watching the animated series 'Qiao Hu'. She would constantly imitate the actions inside." Recalling the details of her daughter's life, Ms. Qu started tearing up again, "It's all my fault for not taking good care of my child. As a mother, I feel very guilty and have a psychological shadow. These few days, I've been dreaming about Xiao Yueyue non-stop. I dreamt of her wearing a yellow dress dancing, I dreamt of her telling me 'haha' all the time."

At 3 PM, it was once again time to visit Xiao Yueyue. Xiao Yueyue's parents, grandparents all appeared in the visiting corridor. Looking at their unconscious daughter lying in bed, Ms. Qu kept shouting, "Yueyue, you must be strong!" "Yueyue, please give your mom a chance" "Yueyue, mom didn't take good care of you, you need to breathe hard" "Yueyue, mom loves you the most."

Xiao Yueyue's father, who had been cooperating with the police investigation and hadn't seen his daughter for two days, opened his mouth and shouted as soon as he saw her, "Daughter, daddy is here to see you!" Afterwards, he hid in a corner and quietly shed tears. Mr. Wang said that apart from relying on the hospital's treatment, he also found some folk remedies in books, saying that every day he needs to tell Xiao Yueyue the thing she wanted to do most before she got hurt. "What Xiao Yueyue wanted to do most was to ride the electric plane at the amusement park with her brother."

Although Xiao Yueyue's parents continuously announced to outsiders that "donations are temporarily not needed," people coming to the hospital to visit Xiao Yueyue were still coming in an endless stream. Some school teachers even brought students and their donations. A lady who did not wish to disclose her name sent a donation, and Xiao Yueyue's parents repeatedly asked her to leave her name and contact information, but she declined.

Although some people questioned whether Aunt Chen Xianmei, who helped Xiao Yueyue, was "seeking fame," Ms. Qu insisted that Aunt Chen was absolutely not seeking fame. "Aunt Chen's heart is very kind. Seeing some cardboard in front of the stall, she would always politely ask if we still needed them?" Regarding those more than a dozen indifferent passers-by, Ms. Qu frankly said, "I don't want to judge them. There are many enthusiastic people in society helping us, and I believe that everyone's hearts are very kind."

At 4 PM on the 17th, when the reporter informed Xiao Yueyue's father that the police had basically determined that the Zhao surname man who contacted him in the morning of the 16th was not the hit-and-run driver, after listening, his face turned angry: "I really hate this kind of person, it's too disgraceful, I'm very angry!" Then, he sighed deeply: "Don't joke around with other people's pain!"
