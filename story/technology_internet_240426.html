Comprehensive Guide to Turning QQ Icons On and Off

by wodezzz on 2009-09-20 09:09:51

Tencent Customer Service has released a comprehensive navigation page for canceling services, turning off, and lighting up icons. All self-cancellable services are included here; it seems only QQ numbers cannot be self-cancelled. Now I am sharing this with everyone.

1. Diamond Category Icons

[QQ Membership]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Red Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Yellow Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Blue Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[Q Tang Purple Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[Sound Speed Purple Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Speed Purple Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Pink Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Green Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

[QQ Black Diamond]: Service cancellation will turn off the icon.

2. Mobile Category Icons

[Mobile Binding]: Cancel mobile binding to turn off the icon.

[Mobile QQ]: Close Mobile QQ to turn off the icon.

[Cellular QQ]: Click here to apply for turning off the icon.

[SMS Superhero]: Turn off SMS Superhero service to extinguish the icon.

[WAPQQ]: WAPQQ icon validity is up to three months; it automatically turns off after expiration.

[Mobile Tencent Website]: Log in via your mobile phone to the mobile Tencent website (3g.qq.com), sequentially enter customer service—>customer service consultation—>I want—>icon settings (requires logging into your QQ number; if not logged in, follow prompts to log in first). Select "hide" and submit.

3. Product Category Icons

[Membership Ringtones]: After membership service closure or deleting all friends' ringtones, it will automatically turn off within 72 hours.

[Q Song Q Charm]: The lit-up QGQM icons began to be turned off in batches starting January 12, 2009.

[QQ Friend-making]: For advanced users, log in to friend-making, click “personal management”—“super stealth”—“hide QQ red heart” to hide. Regular users can log in to friend-making, click “personal management”—“cancel account” to turn off the icon.

[Video Symbol]: Revoke video device to turn off the icon.

[Super Cyclone]: Currently, the Super Cyclone icon can be turned off. If your points exceed 2000, log into x.soso.com to see the button for turning off the icon.

[Tencent Books]: Close VIP book service to turn off the icon.

[QQTalk]: After the service goes offline, the system will automatically turn off all lit-up icons.

[Ask]: Click here to log in to Ask, input your QQ number to log in. On the top right corner of the webpage, select “My Ask”, then enter and choose “My Settings” on the left column. Finally, in the option “Display Ask icon and my questions on QQ,” select “Close.”

Note: After selecting close, the Ask icon and your question information will no longer be displayed on QQ avatars in QQ2006 official version and later versions.

[PaiPai Seller]: Log in to PaiPai, enter My PaiPai, click “Basic Settings”—“Display on QQ,” manually set to hide.

[PaiPai Buyer]: The illumination duration of the “blue Pai character” icon changes according to your shopping behavior on PaiPai. When your remaining illumination duration is zero, the “blue Pai character” icon will temporarily turn off. Click here for details.

[QQ Album]: Click here to apply for album cancellation, which will turn off the icon after cancellation.

[QQ Podcast]: Log in to QQ Podcast, enter, click “Edit Personal Information,” then click “Turn Off” to extinguish.

[QQ Space]: Click here to apply for space cancellation, which will turn off the icon after cancellation.

[QQ Mail]: Log in to QQ Mail, click “Settings,” uncheck “Light up mail icon on QQ” in regular settings to turn off the icon.

[Q Bar]: Q Bar points deducted to negative or no login/reading of Q Bar posts for 14 consecutive days. Meeting either condition will turn off the icon.

[WealthPay]: Log in to WealthPay, enter Account Settings, select “Close Icon” to hide.

[QQlive]: Log in to QQ client, click the “QQlive Advanced User” icon in your TIPS window, enter the related page, and you can set to hide.

[QQ Bookmarks]: If the user's collection count is less than 10, the icon will be closed.

[QQ Home]: Log in to QQ Space—Show World—Mini House, click More Functions, select “Cancel Client Icon” to set successfully.

[TaoTao]: Log in to TaoTao, click Settings—Basic Settings—Icon Settings—Turn off my TaoTao icon.

[QQ Love Wall]: The QQ Love Wall icon is valid for 2 months; it will automatically turn off after expiration.

[QQ Sound Messages]: If the activity index is less than 10, the sound message icon will automatically turn off.

[QQ Schoolmates]: Log in to the official QQ Schoolmates website, select “My Information” from the top right corner—“Basic Information,” then click “How to have schoolmate icon on QQ?” at the bottom right corner of the page. Follow the prompt to select “Hide” the icon.

[Search Bar]: You can log in to Search Bar and cancel the lit-up icon in the personal center.

[QQ Music]: Automatically turns off after not logging in for 15 consecutive days.

[QQ Pinyin]: Automatically turns off after not using QQ Pinyin for 14 consecutive days. Manual operation method: Click the QQ Pinyin icon on the QQ client, enter the “My QQ Pinyin” page, and click “Turn Off Icon.”

4. Game Category Icons

[QQ Pet]: After the pet dies and is buried, the icon will turn off.

[Free Fantasy]: The icon automatically turns off after one week of not logging into Free Fantasy game.

[QQ Tang]: Click here to set the icon to turn off.

[QQ Speed]: Click here to set the icon to turn off.

[CrossFire]: Click here to set the icon to turn off.

[QQ Dance]: The icon automatically turns off after not logging into QQ Dance for 14 days.

[DNF]: The icon automatically turns off after not logging into Dungeon & Fighter for 14 days.

[Flying Island]: The icon automatically turns off after not logging into QQ Flying Island for 14 days, or you can use the self-service icon extinction system to turn off the icon. Click here to turn off the icon.

[QQ Audition]: The icon automatically turns off after not logging into QQ Audition game within a month, or you can turn off the icon yourself. Please click here.

[QQ Fantasy]: Click here to set the icon to turn off.

[QQ Three Kingdoms]: Click here for self-service icon extinction.

[QQ Xia]: Temporarily does not support turning off.

[CFVIP Users]: Turning off CFVIP service will turn off the icon.

[Xunxian]: Click here to set the icon to turn off.
