Annoying Brain Teasers

1. Why isn't Matsushita as strong as Sony? Answer: Panasonic (Afraid of Brother Sony)

2. Which is higher, A or C? Answer: C is higher (Because in ABCD, A is lower than C)

3. Which flower is the weakest among Jasmine, Sunflower, and Rose? Answer: Jasmine (What a weak jasmine flower)

4. What kind of line do gorillas hate the most? Answer: Parallel lines. (Parallel lines never intersect [banana])

5. Which one is the worst among eraser, tiger skin, and lion skin? Answer: Eraser (Worst quality)

6. What does cloth fear and what does paper fear? Answer: Cloth fears "ten thousand," paper fears "just in case." (Better safe than sorry)

7. Is Dandan the name of a puppy or a little tiger? Answer: Little tiger (Tiger's fierce gaze)

8. Which animal has the worst sense of direction? Answer: Milu deer (Lost)

9. Who among the wolf, tiger, and lion will definitely be eliminated in a game? Answer: Wolf (Peach Boy)

10. A mother gave birth to Siamese twins; the sister is named Mary. What’s the name of her twin sister? Answer: Monroe (Marilyn Monroe)

11. The pig escaped from the pigpen. What should you do? {Guess a celebrity} Answer: Leehom Wang

12. The pig escaped from the pigpen again. What should you do? {Guess a celebrity} Answer: Han Hong

13. The pig escaped from the pigpen for the third time. What should you do? {Guess a celebrity} Answer: Jay Chou

14. What kind of sword is transparent? Answer: Invisible

15. Who cries when they think of their mother? Answer: Grandpa (Every night I think of my mother's words, shining with tears, lupin flowers)

16. Why do they put two spoons of salt on the basketball board? Answer: Hard to explain

17. Why is Xiaobai very similar to his brother? Answer: The truth comes out

18. Which fish is the most foolish? Answer: Shark (Silly shark)

19. Which fish is the smartest? Answer: Whale (Smart whale)

20. Which song features three people in its first line? Answer: I Am Not Huang Rong (I, Bush, Jack Jones, Huang Rong)

21. Which sect's followers don’t lie? Answer: Quanzhen Sect

22. Which chat tool is the slowest? Answer: MSN (So slow it's dying)

23. Where do you hide your pigeon? Answer: Rooftop (Singing your song on the rooftop)

24. Why does the forest always send lions to handle communication matters? Answer: Lost contact

25. Baozi died, and his father came to take revenge on Potato. Knowing he couldn't win, Potato ran away. A river blocked Potato's way. {Guess a vegetable} Answer: Snow pea

26. Whose sea is it? Answer: Pineapple (Baltic Sea)

27. Why doesn't the princess need a mosquito net after she gets married? Answer: Because she has a frog prince

28. A white horse is called a white horse, a black horse is called a black horse, and a black-and-white striped horse is called a zebra. What about a horse that has black, white, and red stripes? Answer: A shy zebra

29. Who wore glasses during the Tang Dynasty? Answer: Li Bai (The bright moonlight before the bed, hmm! It's frost on the ground)

30. Which historical figure got bullied the most? Answer: Su Wu. Reason: Su Wu herded sheep by the North Sea (Got beaten up by the sea).
