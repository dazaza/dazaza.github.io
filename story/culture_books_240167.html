"Official High Rank" is a record of the experiences and observations of municipal party committee and municipal government secretaries in the officialdom.

The mayor of Chuhe City has gone missing. On the second day of the interim mayor, Guo Gang's term, an overseas businessman was murdered. Guo Gang was determined to solve the case and make a strong start. However, the city's discipline inspection commission secretary, Zhou Motong, said that this wasn't a simple murder case and advised against rash actions.

Guo Gang suspected that the powerful executive vice-mayor Yang Zijian and the public security bureau chief Wu Xiong were both umbrellas for the black forces, so he proceeded cautiously, without making a sound. Yang Zijian designed a plan to make Guo Gang be on the same side as him, together "tying up and going public". While Guo Gang ordered his "five-person team" to secretly investigate the murder case, he also got entangled with Yang Zijian.

Yang Zijian realized that Guo Gang had a divided heart and designed a car accident to kill Guo Gang. Guo Gang narrowly escaped death. But Zhou Motong and Mayor's secretary Ouyang Guang fell into the hands of the black forces. The "five-person team" was led astray by someone. When Zhou Motong was about to be tortured to death by the black forces, they finally solved the murder case and revealed the mastermind behind it.

Unbelievably, just as the higher-ups were about to announce Guo Gang's promotion, the anti-corruption hero hailed by the common people committed suicide...

Part One of "One Rank Higher"

This mayor of Chuhe City, the number one figure in people's eyes who could command wind and rain, suddenly and inexplicably vanished! For a time, the whole city was in an uproar. Some said that Li Yaoyong, this big corrupt official, had fled abroad with a huge amount of money; some said that Li Yaoyong had been "double specified" by the higher-ups, the organization would not let any corrupt individuals go, and they were currently investigating; some said that Li Yaoyong could not face the people's trial and had cut himself off from the party and the people. Some spoke vividly, some hated with righteous indignation, and some cursed with great satisfaction. In short, in the street talks and alley discussions, the voices of blame were incessant, while sighs of regret and sympathy for possible misfortunes were few and far between.

"I'll ask again, the last time I'm asking! You tell me, has Mayor Li contacted you yet? It's been three days..." Yang Zijian, the deputy secretary of the Chuhe Municipal Committee and executive vice-mayor, was viciously yelling at Ouyang Guang over the phone.

Part Two of "One Rank Higher"

"Ouyang, if you have something to say, just say it," Guo Gang saw that Ouyang Guang had something on his mind.

"Mayor Guo, this car is hard to ride," Ouyang Guang hinted. But after saying it, he regretted a bit, why did his mouth move so fast, what's the point of saying these things, isn't it asking for trouble? But then again, he felt that he must tell Guo Gang, only when Guo Gang goes smoothly, can he go smoothly.

"How is it hard to ride? I want to hear."

"Do you know the origin of this car?" Ouyang Guang gathered all his courage.

Part Three of "One Rank Higher"

Yang Zijian thought they had both left, but when he opened his eyes, he was surprised to find Qiu Qiong sitting by his bed. He was startled, thinking he was confused, but upon looking again, it was indeed Qiu Qiong, so he said, "Didn't you leave?"

"Yuan Shiling went back, I was worried about you, so I came back to accompany you."

"Thank you. Go home, I'm fine." Yang Zijian slowly waved his hand.

Qiu Qiong didn't say anything, she lowered her head, bit her teeth, and pulled down the zipper of her dress from behind, pressing her body towards Yang Zijian. When Yang Zijian saw Qiu Qiong's exposed peaks, he couldn't resist anymore, breaking through his own defenses...

Part Four of "One Rank Higher"

He couldn't go.

Nor could he really clash with Yang Zijian. If that happened, once Yang Zijian went to Liu Fuming to stir up trouble, then Guo Gang might even offend Liu Fuming, the party secretary he had never met. How could he continue to get by then?

He also couldn't let Yang Zijian think that he, the mayor, was insincere. He couldn't touch any of Yang Zijian's sensitive nerves. Bold moves wouldn't work, he had to hide first, letting Yang Zijian lower his guard against him. Thus, every single thing Guo Gang wanted to do, he first communicated with Yang Zijian, even making Yang Zijian feel that he, Guo Gang, respected him very much. In daily work, Guo Gang never interacted too much with any department head, achieving the smart person's idea of interacting with everyone being the same as not interacting with anyone, being good with anyone being the same as not being good with anyone. Letting Yang Zijian realize that he hadn't cultivated cronies.
