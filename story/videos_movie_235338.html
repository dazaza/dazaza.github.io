"Founding of a Republic" tells a series of stories that happened from the end of the War of Resistance against Japan in 1945 to the eve of the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949. The film mainly describes the preparation for the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, reflecting the turbulent years before the founding of New China. Director Jianxin Huang said that compared with previous mainstream films, this film has made new breakthroughs and uncovered and presented some little-known stories. It includes an in-depth description of the relationship between Chiang Kai-shek and his son Chiang Ching-kuo.

"Founding of a Republic" is directed by Sanping Han, chairman of the China Film Group Corporation. This is his return to directing after an 18-year absence. Jianxin Huang and Xingdong Wang respectively serve as director and screenwriter. The chief planner of the film is Heping Zhang. Xiaoshi Zhao is responsible for cinematography, Jing Zhao for art direction, Xizhong Wang for makeup, and Danrong Wang for sound recording. They are all top talents in the industry.

The cast of the film is unprecedentedly strong. Guoliang Zhang and Guoqiang Tang respectively play the two main characters of the film, Chiang Kai-shek and Mao Zedong. Other main actors include Jin Liu, Sha Liu, Xueqi Wang, Kun Chen, Zongdi Xiu, Wu Fu Wang, Qing Xu, Jian Wang, Junmei Wu, Bing Wang, and Xin Wang. Wen Jiang, You Ge, Honglei Sun, Dehua Liu, Kaige Chen, Xiaogang Feng, Daoming Chen, Baoguo Chen, Yong You, Yuanzheng Feng, Hao Chen, Yong Hou, and Shan Jiang will also make cameo appearances in the film, playing important political figures. It is reported that the cast of the film is still being expanded and adjusted.

"Founding of a Republic" began shooting on February 2 at the National China Film Digital Production Base in Huairou, Beijing. It is planned to be completed by early May. After post-production is completed, it will be released on the market on September 10 this year and will have a nationwide large-scale public release during the National Day holiday.
