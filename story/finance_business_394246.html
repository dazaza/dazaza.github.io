50 Practical Methods for Online Store Promotion

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 09:45:22

We have compiled 50 common promotional ideas. Everyone can choose the methods they like based on their own capabilities and external environment, and we hope it will be helpful to all online merchants.

1. SNS Store Soft Article Promotion Method: Nowadays, SNS-type stores are very popular. When going to an SNS store, mainly add hundreds or even thousands of users in a targeted manner from some popular SNS stores. Then select appropriate soft articles and reasonably attach the store link, publishing them in your diary. Let some of your friends share it, so that many people pay attention to your article and your store.

2. SNS Store API Cooperation Promotion Method: If you or your employees have certain technical strength, you can develop some small plug-ins related to your store's products and insert them into platforms like Renren (formerly Xiaonei) or HaiNei. As long as there are open APIs for SNS stores, go ahead and insert them. Many stores are doing this now. Check out some applications on Renren; many are provided by small stores, and they more or less advertise their own stores. This market is better to enter early because many SNS stores are just starting to try developing APIs, and the review requirements are not particularly strict yet. In the future, when there are more stores, it will be harder to insert into SNS stores.

3. Bookmark Promotion Method: Add pages with excellent content to QQ Bookmarks, Baidu Collections, Guangzhou Vipshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., Yahoo Favorites, etc., allowing internet users who like this content to read and collect it.

4. Moderator Alliance Promotion Method: This method is suitable for promoting forum community stores. First, join some moderator alliances, like Daqi Moderator Alliance, which is quite good. Submit some interesting content from forums every day, waiting to be accepted by relevant channels. Once accepted, it will bring you a lot of IP traffic. The most I've had was bringing over 30,000 IPs to my store in two days.

5. Reply Top Promotion Method: Actually, I don't know what to call this method. Its limitation is very small. As far as I know, it only applies to Mopu Big Mixed Bag, so let's temporarily call it reply top. I discovered this method accidentally while checking the IP sources of my store. One day, I found over 200 IPs coming from a post on Mopu, but I knew we hadn't posted any promotional posts there, so it seemed strange. I checked the post and found that someone had placed a link to our store and replied to it, pinning it to the top, thus bringing us over 200 IPs.

6. Reward Registration Promotion Method: A promotion method aimed at increasing member registration. Generally, everyone promotes through reward registration or some incentive registration methods. Nowadays, people are very pragmatic; they won't do anything that doesn't benefit them.

7. Free Game Promotion Method: Free games mean hanging a free game on your store, but it must be related to the theme of the store and preferably a rewarded game. Their store hung a completely free "Dream Lottery" football score guessing game, which was entirely consistent with their store content. The promotion effect was also very good.

8. QQ Group Promotion Method: Use dozens of QQ accounts, each joining dozens of groups, and post about the store daily. On average, 50 QQ accounts * each QQ account joins 50 groups * each group has 50 people = 75,000 people viewing. If you have more QQ accounts, join more groups, especially large groups with more members per group, then the promotional effect will be better. Even excluding offline QQ users, the effect should not be underestimated. Be patient and unafraid of being kicked out. Additionally, you can send group emails in QQ groups, for example, recommending the exciting content on your store www.yourstore.com via email.

9. Search Engine Promotion Method: Nowadays, new webmasters are most enthusiastic about this short, flat, and fast method. The function of search engines is to integrate network resources, find the most suitable content for netizens, and bring traffic to other stores as an unintended side effect. It seems that the promotion columns of many webmaster-type stores have become SEO zones. The best SEO method is to have standardized webpage code, fast access speed, unique and less repetitive content, useful to netizens, and with uniqueness.

10. Soft Article Promotion Method: Write articles or quote good articles, cleverly inserting your own website URL.

11. Blog Promotion Method: Soft articles need a place to be published. One way is to post them on forums and article sites, another is to post them on blogs. Posting on blogs has the advantage that they won't be deleted randomly.

12. Media Promotion Method: Let traditional media such as newspapers, TV, radio, etc., report on it.

13. Mobile Promotion Method: WAP stores, mass SMS, ringtone prompts, etc. The functions of mobile phones are getting stronger, compact and easy to carry. In the future, the number of mobile phone users online will definitely exceed the number of computer users.

14. Web Summary Promotion Method: Submit high-quality webpages, images, posts to summary websites and aggregation-type stores. If recommended, it can also bring considerable traffic.

15. Forum Promotion Method: Register dozens or hundreds of forums, each registering dozens of accounts, setting your signature as your store. Post hot content, and promote your own posts. Pay attention to changing personas and posting controversial titles. A good title is 100% of the success or failure of forum promotion. Here, the term forum refers to a broad range, including guestbooks, forums, Tieba, etc., all places where netizens may gather.

16. Third Pole Promotion Method: While promoting your store, append your wealth third pole number. At the end of each webpage's content, add your wealth third pole number to increase visibility and bring additional wealth income.

17. QQ and MSN Nickname Promotion Method: Change your nickname to your website URL, such as free prize games.

18. Email List Promotion Method: Regularly or irregularly send electronic magazines to netizens.

19. Unexpected Email Promotion Method: Use professional email bulk sending tools, collect email addresses in batches online, and then send them in bulk. Many stores today use email addresses as usernames, so collecting email addresses is much easier than before, and these email addresses are active ones frequently used by netizens rather than inactive ones, so the effect is good. The email title should be "standardized", avoiding making netizens mistakenly think it's spam.

20. Word-of-Mouth Promotion Method: Do good content well, let netizens spread your store voluntarily.

21. * Access Content Promotion Method: Divide the store's content into access levels and set access conditions. Give netizens a promotional link, and netizens can only access the content after recommending a certain number of visitors or registered members. Non-mainstream love stores often use this method, with excellent results.

22. Store Ranking Voting Promotion Method: Join rankings launched by other stores or rank other stores' rankings, effectively increasing the store's exposure rate.

23. Aggregation Link Promotion Method: Aggregate excellent content together to attract netizens.

24. BT Download Resource Promotion Method: You can embed your store in audio-video files, especially in files requiring certificates to watch, even popping up the store. Some webmasters use this method, leveraging the release of the latest movies, releasing a video file to get hundreds of thousands of IPs, with absolutely first-class effects.

25. Picture Watermark Promotion Method: Prevent other stores from referencing pictures without being friendly or effective, and blocking search engine indexing. From a certain perspective, other stores are also special netizens of your store. If you watermark your store, put the store address, actively allow other sites to reference the pictures, increasing the exposure rate of the URL, then the promotional effect is good. Watermarking includes picture watermarking, video watermarking, webpage watermarking...

26. Video Source Flash Promotion Method: Nowadays, video stores all provide external access interfaces. When other stores or diaries reference these videos, they directly promote the store, expanding its influence.

27. Submission to URL Station Promotion Method: Store navigation stations, store directory stations.

28. Email Auto-Reply Promotion Method: Set the store address and store introduction as auto-reply content in your mailbox. When you receive any email, the mailbox will automatically reply.

29. Unpaid One-Way Link Promotion Method: Unpaid linking to other stores enriches your store's information, and when netizens search for other sites, your webpage results may also appear.

30. Mutual Exchange Link Promotion Method: This was the most popular store promotion method a few years ago, and now there are very few existing exchange link stores.

31. Batch Submission Exchange Link Promotion Method: Use automatic submission tools to submit your store to other stores' friendship link sections. When webmasters review whether to pass your site, they contribute one IP. The development of human society benefits from the improvement of tool automation. Fully utilize the advantages of Internet information transmission automation, digitization, and batch processing, skillfully using batch publicity tools to save human repetitive labor.

32. Store Promotion Alliance Promotion Method: Lone wolves cannot become heroes, and one person's energy, time, and intelligence are ultimately limited. Therefore, you need to know how to borrow external forces. Several webmasters unite, reaching consensus on promotion. While promoting your own store, casually take along other stores. With the same effort, gain more returns.

33. Traffic Statistics Promotion Method: Place an iframe page reference code on all pages of the store, put other stores' counters on the referenced page, and control the change of statistical IDs with program code. When other webmasters check statistics, browsing visit sources, your store appears. Most webmasters will check, and this publicity method works well, belonging to a closely guarded secret. Many people know the power of Baidu and Google from the store visit sources.

34. Mutual Channel Exchange Promotion Method: Exchange channels with other stores. That is, promote the other store as one of your channels on your store. Thus mutually supporting each other, helping each other, creating great power.

35. Free Resource Promotion Method: Provide free services to other stores, such as free statistics, free evaluations, free weather query codes, free blogs, free IP query codes, free stock query codes, free search codes, free online customer service systems, free guestbooks, free domains, free spaces, free PR evaluations, free e-books, free queries, free QQ hangouts, free certifications, free materials, etc. Make the services you provide become standard configurations for other stores. Over time, traffic naturally increases.

36. Content Alliance Promotion Method: This method suits webmasters with server resources. Provide a platform that can bind other stores' domains, give other webmasters ad space at the top and bottom of the store, attracting other stores to join. Although it seems like you're making clothes for other stores, you're actually promoting your own store, expanding its recognition and influence. The place with the largest traffic on the network is not search stores, but other stores. Search stores have large traffic, but averaged across each store, it's just a few dozen, with little actual significance to yourself. Pulling in one webmaster is better than pulling in thousands of netizens. This method is long-term and lasting.

37. Threatening Compulsory Promotion Method: For example, stores **miibeian, various public security bureaus, can force stores to place links. Ordinary people can't use this. However, it can be indirectly used, such as the hacker link method.

38. Get Big Promotion Method: For example, portal sites. Let small stores consider adding links to your store an honor. Despite few stores receiving any benefits from portal stores, the natural admiration for strength still makes countless webmasters voluntarily offer links and consider it an honor. Whether others notice or appreciate it is another matter.

39. Client-Side Software Plugin Promotion Method: Webmasters with technical skills can use this method. Develop popular client-side software commonly used by netizens, such as chat tools like QQ, online gaming tools, download tools like Xunlei, etc., which can pop up windows, directly popping the store homepage, or some event pages. The effect is excellent.

40. Paid Resource Free Promotion Method: For example, providing software registration codes, registration numbers, serial numbers, non-serial number versions, improving store stickiness. For example, ghost versions of operating systems have kept several stores alive with daily visits of hundreds of thousands of IPs.

41. Group Promotion Method: Nowadays, web2.0 social networking stores all have this feature. Send messages widely in groups to attract attention.

42. Task Publishing Promotion Method: Publish tasks on Weike stores, not only attracting those accepting the tasks, but also a large number of onlookers. Make full use of this one-to-many transmission advantage.

43. Offline Leaflet Distribution Promotion Method: You can either distribute leaflets yourself or cooperate with other traditional enterprises, using their existing channels. For example, cooperating with supermarkets, printing your store URL on shopping receipts. You can exchange resources from your store with the supermarket.

44. Traffic Exchange Alliance Promotion Method: Join a traffic exchange alliance, use professional traffic exchange system platforms, directly exchange traffic with other stores, mutually beneficial, jointly developing.

45. Encourage Visitors to Promote Method: How to move netizens to promote by word of mouth depends on the store content, flexibly arranged. Generally, novel, interesting, interactive content works better. For example, test types, evaluation types, holiday blessing types, quirky types, etc., content webpages are easily strongly spread. Consider how your store can uncover this type of material.

46. Business Card Promotion Method: On business cards, manuals, everything paper you can control, print URLs, write URLs.

47. Email Signature Promotion Method: Set it in the email settings. Add the store URL and store introduction. Afterward, writing letters will automatically include it, saving you a lot of trouble.

48. Online Competition Promotion Method: Such as beauty contests, store contests, and various qualification contests.

49. Paid Promotion Method: If free methods don't work. As long as the cost-effectiveness is appropriate, spending one to recover two, paid promotion is not bad. Giving up means gaining; what you give up will come back.

50. Division of Labor Promotion Method: The improvement of social efficiency comes from the improvement of tool automation and the value of division of labor. Your ability is limited, good at integrating external forces, hiring others to promote, letting professionals handle professional matters, achieving twice the result with half the effort.
