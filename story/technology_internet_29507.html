With the increasing economic and trade exchanges between the two places, the HK domain name continues to heat up.

by now on 2007-09-11 09:47:41

In the ten years since Hong Kong's return, economic and trade cooperation between mainland China and Hong Kong has become increasingly close. Especially after the signing and implementation of CEPA, economic and trade exchanges between mainland China and Hong Kong have greatly increased, and talent exchanges have become more frequent: universities in mainland China and Hong Kong are open to each other and exchange with each other; performers from both places cooperate together and perform on the same stage; additionally, the "Hong Kong Stock Connect" will soon be open, giving individuals in mainland China the opportunity to invest in Hong Kong's stock market. The interactions between the two places have reached an inseparable level. Meanwhile, developments in the internet have made communication even more convenient and fast, and HK domain names, as Hong Kong's online identifier, continue to gain popularity, becoming one of the domain types that enterprises in mainland China and Hong Kong highly value.

It is reported that HK is the English abbreviation for Hong Kong. The .HK domain name is a distinctive online identifier of Hong Kong. Hong Kong's commerce has long entered mainland China, and Hong Kong talents have long flowed into mainland China. With the Internet Domain Name Registration (HKDNR) certifying Times Internet (www.now.cn) as the first authorized registrar on the mainland, the .HK domain name has fully entered the mainland internet market. Many domestic enterprises registered the .HK domain name at the first opportunity, especially numerous enterprises in the Greater Pearl River Delta region. According to data from HKDNR, by May 1, 2007, the number of registered .HK domain names had reached 138,952, increasing by 30% compared to the same period in 2006. After May, due to the opening of Chinese .HK domain name registration, the growth rate surpassed 30%.

The .HK English domain name has developed rapidly domestically, with the number of registrations continuously increasing. Under this favorable situation, at the beginning of this year, the Hong Kong Registry also opened the registration service for Chinese .HK domain names in mainland China. The Chinese .HK domain name uses Chinese as its carrier, conforming better to the language habits of the Chinese people. In terms of domain name resolution, it supports both simplified and traditional Chinese characters. As the first authorized registrar, Times Internet further reduced the price to its lowest, with a market price of only 160 yuan. Just upon its launch, there was a heated rush to register, with well-known domestic enterprises such as Haier, Lenovo, Konka, and Chery quickly protecting their Chinese .HK domains.

In recent years, the number of Hong Kong-funded companies investing in mainland China has been increasing, especially in Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Shanghai where there are many Hong Kong-funded companies. As Hong Kong serves as Asia's financial center and holds significant weight globally, Hong Kong enterprises naturally want to reflect this unique advantage and identity. Therefore, choosing the HK domain name as their main company domain has become a trend. The mutual recognition and emphasis placed on HK by mainland enterprises and Hong Kong-funded enterprises have further promoted a substantial increase in the registration of HK domain names.

In the ten years since the return, under the support of the government, Hong Kong has overcome the impact and influence of the Asian financial crisis and the SARS epidemic, continuing to maintain its characteristics as a free port and international metropolis, as well as its status as an international financial, trade, and shipping center. The rising popularity of the .HK domain name is also excellent proof of the increased exchanges between Hong Kong and mainland China.
