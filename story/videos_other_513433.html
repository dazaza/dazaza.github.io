A touching love story

by doudouqaj on 2011-10-21 21:39:08

Today's episode of "Search for Love" tells a truly moving love story: about being born too late to meet the one you're destined for, or being born when they are already too old! But this is a comedy. It takes place in Yueyang, where a 21-year-old adopted daughter falls in love with her 43-year-old adoptive father. Breaking through countless obstacles just to spend the rest of their lives together. The father's face has been burned and disfigured, but the girl is wildly persistent. She says: only this man can give her a sense of security, only this man will never abandon her! She loves him! So no matter if he gives her cold looks, or hides from her, she chases after him head-on. In the end, she moves him, she breaks down his reservations, and they finally end up together. The girl in the program is very sunny and has a very healthy mindset, so I believe this is a story about love, and not just gratitude! At the end of the show, the two of them read the words on their marriage certificate over and over again in the sunlight... This reminded me of the movie "The Favorites" I watched a while ago. It depicts two AIDS patients' infinite longing for love and warmth as they approach life and death! In the film, De Yi races against life and trains on the train tracks; even their affair between two worlds; De Yi calls Qin Qin "Mom"; Qin Qin cools her body over and over again to help De Yi reduce his fever; and at the end of the film, De Yi leaves behind a pool of red blood... Especially that heart-wrenching and chilling line "Let's get married, while we're still alive."... These two stories are of course not similar, but reading the marriage certificate made me think of "The Favorites" once again. Life is precious, love is precious! I cannot waste my life, so I discard my impatience! In fact, happiness is a very simple thing...
