Sewage Sucker Truck -- Precautions for purchasing Hubei Chengli sewage sucker truck

Published Time: 2011-11-8 14:51:52 Views: 97

Precautions for Purchase:

1. Check if the trademark identification is complete. Genuine products have good quality outer packaging, with clear text on the packaging box and vivid color printing. The packaging box/bag should be marked with product name, specifications model, quantity, registered trademark, factory name and address, and telephone number, etc. Some manufacturers also mark the parts. Important components such as generators, distributors, fuel injection pumps, etc., are accompanied by user manuals, certificates of conformity, and inspectors' seals to guide users in correct use and maintenance. When selecting, carefully recognize to avoid buying counterfeit and inferior products. Hubei Chengli Group registered trademark "Chengliwei" brand (China Famous Trademark). For more details, please check the company website.

2. Check if there is any deformation in geometric dimensions. Some parts may deform due to improper manufacturing, transportation, or storage. During inspection, you can roll the shaft-type part along a glass plate for a circle and check if there is any light leakage at the contact point between the part and the glass plate to determine whether it is bent; when selecting clutch driven disc steel plates or friction plates, you can hold the steel plate or friction plate in front of your eyes to observe if it is warped; when selecting oil seals, the end face of the skeleton oil seal should be a perfect circle and can fit tightly with the flat glass without distortion; for non-skeleton oil seals, the outer edge should be regular, and when you squeeze it with your hand to make it deformed, it should recover its original shape after releasing. When selecting various gaskets, attention should also be paid to checking the geometric dimensions and shape.

3. Check if the mating parts are smooth. Due to vibration and collision during handling and storage, burrs, pressure marks, damage, or cracks often occur at the mating parts of spare parts, affecting the use of the parts. Pay attention to check when selecting.

4. Check if there is any rust on the surface of the parts. The surface of qualified spare parts has a certain degree of precision and a bright gloss. The higher the precision of the spare parts, the stricter the packaging for rust prevention and corrosion prevention. Pay attention to check when selecting. If rust spots, mold spots, or cracking and loss of elasticity in rubber parts, or obvious lathe tool marks on the journal surface are found, replacement should be considered.

5. Check if the protective layer is intact. Most parts are coated with a protective layer upon leaving the factory. For example, piston pins and bearing shells are protected with paraffin; piston rings and cylinder sleeves are coated with rust preventive oil and wrapped with packaging paper; valves and pistons are immersed in rust preventive oil and sealed with plastic bags. If the sealing sleeve is damaged, the packaging paper is lost, or the rust preventive oil or paraffin is lost when selecting, return and exchange should be made.

6. Check if the bonded parts are loose. Components composed of two or more parts are connected through press fitting, bonding, or welding. There should be no looseness between them. For example, the plunger of the oil pump and the adjusting arm are combined through press fitting; the driven wheel of the clutch and the steel plate are riveted together; the friction plate and the steel plate are riveted or bonded; the paper filter element frame and the filter paper are bonded; the electrical equipment wires are welded. If looseness is found during selection, replacement should be made.

7. Check if the rotating parts are flexible. When selecting total assemblies like oil pumps, manually rotate the pump shaft, which should feel flexible without jamming; when selecting rolling bearings, one hand supports the inner ring of the bearing, while the other hand rotates the outer ring, which should be able to rotate freely and quickly, then gradually stop. If the rotating parts do not turn smoothly, it indicates internal rusting or deformation, so don't buy.

8. Check if the assembly components are missing any parts. Regular assembly components must be complete and intact to ensure smooth assembly and normal operation. Missing individual small parts from some assembly components will make the assembly components unable to work or even scrap.
