On a night when the air is cool like water

by bquxiaoyanp on 2011-09-23 16:03:02

On a night with cool breeze, I turned on my computer and a handsome man's avatar popped up on QQ. I opened his personal profile to check. It was "Boating in the Sea of Emotions", 32 years old, from Southeast Guizhou, a national civil servant, married, passionate about life, advocates simplicity, candid in presenting himself, abides by sincerity. My intuition told me that this would be a man of cultivation and taste, also a man with stories.

At that moment, through the cold computer screen, strangers as we were, we felt the true feelings and warmth in each other's hearts. Ah, it was the surge of true feelings and the ripple of love. The man sighed deeply and smiled contentedly. His smile was so beautiful, his figure stood in my heart as a charming landscape, even more majestic. Thereafter, "Boating in the Sea of Emotions" appeared and disappeared in my world. I believe he still lives in his wife's love, still lives a fairy tale-like happy life with his wife. He is like a trickling stream, gently flowing through my sea of emotions without disturbing my dreams, but making my dreams fresh and beautiful. In this world, there will be many stars meeting, many people intersecting, but only once, no need to leave any trace, gradually passing by, soundless and unadorned, just like the water flow passing by a stone, leaving washing or mud behind, those who have seen are gone, those who haven't seen can never see. Think about it, we dress up every day, but how many people really look at us in the hustle and bustle? But it's good this way, just passing by is fine.

Sure enough, during our conversation, he spoke modestly, had extraordinary eloquence, was courteous, proud but not arrogant, which left a deep impression on me. He said he appreciated my works, the writing reflects the person, he judged that I am a kind-hearted and understanding person, so he wanted to tell me a feeling that he couldn't let go. These two days his wife was on a business trip and not at home, his pent-up worries were related to her. This worry was like the rain in March, coming continuously. He and his wife always had a good relationship, they were college classmates and fell in love then. Their married life was calm and lovingly happy, now they already have a lovely son. But the gloom in his heart always lingered, whenever he thought of it, he felt sighs and pain accompanying him. His wife had an experience of cohabitation with others before marriage. Facing this sincere man, it seemed that I peeked into his pure heart. Yes, he was not wrong, the traditional Chinese chastity concept restricted people's understanding and views on women's pre-marital sexual behavior. It also made countless temperamental men sigh and worry, abandon but cannot bear to let go. But my feeling at that time was that he and his wife also had a cohabitation experience before marriage, otherwise they wouldn't be in this living situation now. So I tactfully asked: Did you know before marriage? Why did you agree to marry your wife? His straightforwardness this time made me respect him even more, the admiration and respect were spontaneous. He said: I knew before marriage, but our love was good, we also lived together before getting married. Ah, I finally understood, this is a man with a sense of responsibility, love, and thoughtfulness. He used his sense of responsibility and love to build a warm little house for his wife like Snow White's in a big forest, where it was warm and sweet, romantic and affectionate. I asked him: Do you still love your wife? Yes, very much. That's enough. In the process of emotion, you have an extraordinary experience, a touching and unique beauty, your life has thus become like a brilliant poem. Your wife's experience is her lifelong pain and heartbreak. For many years, she has paid wholeheartedly, all poured onto you to love you. What else do we seek? Her pain has already passed through her beautiful heart, her heartbreak has also passed through under the washing of your concentrated love, this is already very good.
