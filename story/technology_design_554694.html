Signing of BAIC Foton Chengde Engine Manufacturing Center

by ftleisa on 2011-11-14 13:11:11

On June 1, 2011, the Chengde Engine Components Manufacturing Center of Beijing Foton Motor and the second phase technical renovation project of Xuangong Factory of Foton Rayson Pumping Machinery officially signed an agreement. It was introduced that the Chengde Engine Components Manufacturing Center of Beijing Foton Motor has a registered capital of 5 billion yuan, among which Shanghai Foton Company invests 2.55 billion yuan, owning 51% of the shares, Beijing Jianlong Heavy Industry Group Co., Ltd. invests 1.65 billion yuan, owning 33% of the shares, and Beijing Automotive Group Co., Ltd. invests 800 million yuan, owning 16% of the shares. The second phase technical renovation project of Foton Rayson Pumping Machinery Xuangong Factory injects nearly 1.8 billion yuan, including the construction of a new pump truck joint workshop, expansion of existing workshops, and factory area engineering renovation.

The Chengde Engine Components Manufacturing Center of Beijing Foton mainly engages in the smelting business of core components such as automotive starters. In order to meet the asset upgrade needs of Foton Company, the Shanghai Foton Chengde Starter Element Manufacturing Center was established jointly by Beijing Automotive Group Co., Ltd., Shanghai Foton Automobile Stock Co., Ltd., and Beijing Jianlong Heavy Industry Group Co., Ltd. The company is located in Chengde City, Hebei Province. According to the plan, this starter element manufacturing center will produce 150,000 tons of automotive starter castings and 150,000 tons of automotive chassis castings annually, including two parts: starter casting production and automotive chassis casting production, implemented in three phases. Phases one and two are for starter casting production with a capacity of 150,000 tons; phase three is for automotive chassis casting production with a capacity of 150,000 tons, totaling 300,000 tons of capacity. The starter castings mainly satisfy the casting needs of Aoling starters, Leiwo starters, Foton Cummins starters, Chien heavy-duty truck starters, and SAIC Group starters. At the same time, the second phase technical renovation project of Foton Rayson Pumping Machinery Xuangong Factory will include the construction of a pump truck joint workshop, expansion of existing teams, and factory area engineering renovation. After the completion of the project renovation, it will achieve an annual standard production capacity of 9,000 special vehicles and a minimum annual production capacity of 10,600 vehicles. Among them, the annual standard production capacity of pump trucks is 500 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 600 vehicles; the annual standard production capacity of mixer trucks is 6,000 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 7,000 vehicles; the annual standard production capacity of self-loading mining trucks is 2,500 vehicles, with a minimum annual production capacity of 3,000 vehicles.

According to relevant responsible persons of Foton Motor, as the project continues to progress, it will bring a large amount of tax revenue and thousands of job opportunities to the local area, playing a promoting role in improving the local economic stagnation pattern and industrial structure. Moreover, it can also contribute a large amount of tax revenue to the local finances and promote local construction. More importantly, with this key high-end manufacturing industry as the lead, a large number of related industries will also flourish, generating direct or indirect employment and tax revenue in multiples. It is reported that in 2010, Foton sold nearly 700,000 whole vehicles, achieving sales revenue of 53.5 billion yuan, with a total profit of 1.9 billion yuan. Currently, Foton Automobile has entire vehicle and component sectors in 10 provincial-level regions such as Beijing, Tianjin, and Shandong, and research and development branch organizations are distributed in countries and regions such as China, Japan, and Germany.
