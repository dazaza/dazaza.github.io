French tourists can expect to study French in France. Whether you are on a business or leisure trip, one of the most attractive cities in France offers the opportunity to register yourself to study French abroad. The language school in Nice, α-B, is a well-known language school. It is located in the center of the capital of the French Riviera, just a stone's throw away from shops, beaches, and the old town. The language school provides 16 classrooms, equipped with modern facilities, free internet access, as well as a WiFi zone and a library. To accommodate more students, the school has started Alpha B with 12 classrooms and a second facility similar to the language school's center from July to September. It is situated near the construction of the language school.

The Alpha B Language School offers flexible courses for students:

The Standard Course lasts from 2 to 48 weeks. This course provides lessons from beginner to advanced levels, each lasting 45 minutes, totaling 20. The minimum age requirement for this course is 16 years, and it accommodates only up to 10 people.

The Intensive Course provides experience lessons totaling 30 of 45-minute sessions. For more information, you can refer to or compare it with the standard process.

Preparation for Digital Exams (Levels A1, A2, B1, and B2). It offers a course lasting 4 weeks and levels ranging from intermediate to advanced. Lessons are provided totaling 30 of 45-minute sessions. To obtain more detailed information, please refer to or compare it with the standard process.
