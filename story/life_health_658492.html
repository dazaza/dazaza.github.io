Twelve food therapy methods for toothache - a must-read

What can be done about toothache? There are many ways to treat toothache, but the best method is still food therapy. You can solve your toothache by doing it at home, which is great!

Toothache can generally be divided into three types: cavities toothache, wind-heat toothache, and deficient-fire toothache. Here, we introduce nine food therapy recipes for treating toothache. If you think this way, then you need to carefully read the following content:

1. Take 100 grams of regular white wine, put it in a teacup, add 10 grams of salt; stir, and once the salt dissolves, boil it on the stove. Hold a mouthful at the painful place, making sure not to swallow it, and the toothache will stop immediately.

2. Salted Duck Egg Oyster Porridge: 2 salted duck eggs, 100 grams of oysters (dried oyster meat), an appropriate amount of rice cooked as porridge, eaten continuously for 2-3 days. Suitable for those with toothache due to deficient fire inflammation.

3. Century Egg Tofu and Salted Lean Meat Porridge: 2 century eggs, 60 grams of rehydrated tofu skin, 100 grams of salted lean pork, an appropriate amount of rice (or millet) cooked as porridge, eaten continuously for 2-3 days. Suitable for those with toothache due to deficient fire and cavities.

4. Mussel Cistanche Black Bean Soup: 30 grams each of mussels (also known as dried scallops, a seafood product) and cistanche, 150 grams of black beans. Wash off the sand from the mussels, clean the black beans, slice the cistanche, and put them all in a pot with an appropriate amount of water to cook for over an hour. Then remove the ingredients and take the juice, drinking it all at once. One dose per day, continue for several days until the toothache is cured. Mussels enter the kidney meridian, nourishing yin and reducing fire; black beans tonify the kidneys, removing chest heat and dispersing accumulated heat in the five organs. Suitable for those with toothache due to cavities or deficient fire causing gum swelling and pain.

5. Dog Liver Vegetable Tofu Soup: 250 grams of dog liver vegetable (herb), 250 grams of tofu. Wash both ingredients, add an appropriate amount of water and cook until done. Remove the dog liver vegetable, add seasonings, drink the soup and eat the tofu. Suitable for those with liver fire wind-heat causing redness, swelling, and heat pain in cavities. (Avoid for those with deficient fire.)

6. Crush garlic, warm it up, and apply it to the painful area to treat pulpitis, periodontitis, and toothache symptoms. This toothache treatment method is the easiest to operate, as garlic is available in every household.

7. Dissolve monosodium glutamate at a 1:50 concentration in lukewarm water, hold the MSG solution in your mouth for a while, then spit it out. Repeat this several times, and after two days of persistence, the toothache will improve.

8. Mung Bean Egg Sugar Water: 100 grams of mung beans, 1 egg, an appropriate amount of rock sugar. Crush the mung beans, wash them with water, place them in a pot with an appropriate amount of water, and cook until the mung beans are soft. Crack the egg into the mung bean soup, stir evenly, cool slightly, and drink all at once. Continue for 2-3 days. Suitable for those with wind-heat toothache and oral redness, swelling, and heat pain.

9. Tiger Fall Fire Decoction: 30 grams of raw gypsum, 9 grams of Huai Achyranthes root, 6 grams of peony bark, 100 grams of mung beans, an appropriate amount of rice. First crush the gypsum and mung beans, then place all the herbs in a cheesecloth bag, sew it shut, and place it in a clay pot with an appropriate amount of water and rice to cook porridge. Once the porridge is ready, remove the herb bag and drink the porridge. Gypsum is extremely cold and acts as an anti-inflammatory and fire-reducing agent; Achyranthes root guides heat downward to disperse stagnant heat; peony bark promotes blood circulation and dispels blood stasis; mung beans clear heat, detoxify, and reduce inflammation. Suitable for robust patients with excessive real heat. Avoid for those with weak constitution and gastric qi deficiency. Be cautious!

10. When experiencing toothache, cut a small piece of ginger and bite down on the painful area, repeating if necessary. It's also fine to keep it in your mouth while sleeping. This is a safe and reliable empirical formula.

11. Willow Root Lean Meat Soup: 50 grams of willow tree roots, 100 grams of lean pork. Clean both ingredients, add an appropriate amount of water, and cook together. Season, then drink the soup and eat the meat. Suitable for those with wind-fire toothache characterized by swollen gums and redness in the cheeks.

After reading the above content, you may find it very enlightening, but there is one thing to note: toothaches are often caused by cavities or pulpitis, so it's still necessary to visit the dental department for root canal examination and treatment. The key to quickly relieving toothache is finding the cause, which is the fastest and most effective way to solve toothache problems. Don't be overly confident and waste time without results, as suffering unnecessarily is not worth it.
