**Structure of the Pure Water Machine - Five-stage Filtration**

Firstly, a pure water machine is a device that uses RO membrane filtration technology to produce purified water. Simply put, an RO membrane is like a filter net with extremely tiny pores. These pores are so small that only water molecules and substances smaller than water molecules can pass through, while other larger molecular substances are isolated.

The main components of a pure water machine include: PP cotton filter core, granular activated carbon filter core, sintered activated carbon filter core, RO membrane filter core, post-activated carbon filter core, as well as the housings for these filter cores, transformer, booster pump, high-pressure electromagnetic valve, low-pressure electromagnetic valve, wastewater ratio controller, faucet, and pipeline components, etc.

**● Working Principle of the Pure Water Machine: Producing Pure Water Generates Wastewater**

A complete pure water machine is controlled by various electromagnetic valves to manage the inflow and outflow of water, achieving automation. When the pressure tank lacks water or when the faucet is opened, the low-pressure electromagnetic valve senses insufficient water pressure and activates the water pump to start producing purified water. When the pressure tank is full or the faucet is closed, the low-pressure electromagnetic valve senses increased water pressure and shuts off the water pump, stopping the production of purified water.

When there is a water outage or the tap water inlet valve is closed, the high-pressure electromagnetic valve will sense insufficient water pressure and stop the water pump during the water-making process to prevent it from running dry and getting damaged.

In general kitchens, there is usually an extra hole next to the sink faucet, often used for installing a spray gun or detergent hydraulic device. This hole can be used to install the faucet for the pure water machine. When you don't need pure water, you can open the regular faucet, which provides untreated tap water. When you open the faucet of the pure water machine, it delivers directly drinkable purified water. Moreover, wastewater is only generated during the production of purified water; using regular tap water does not produce any wastewater.

**● Is the Pure Water Produced by the Pure Water Machine Good for Human Health?**

Water directly produced by the RO membrane is pure water, and long-term consumption may not be good for health. The water that the human body can effectively absorb is weakly alkaline. Therefore, most post-filtration systems add mineral particles to make the water coming out of the faucet weakly alkaline and increase the minerals in the water, making it beneficial for health after drinking. This weakly alkaline water can also be directly used for fish and aquatic plants.
