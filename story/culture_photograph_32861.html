Ten Often Overlooked Techniques in Landscape Photography

This impression comes from a speech by a teacher, and it is very practical. It has an immediate guiding significance for photography enthusiasts.

We live in an age of reading images. Many places we may never go to in our lifetime, but with beautiful landscape photos, we can recognize these places through looking at the photos. Therefore, landscape photography allows us to understand nature and share the grandeur and beauty of nature. There is an interesting phenomenon in China's photography circle: professional photographers who make a living from photography mostly like to take pictures of people, while amateur photographers who take photography as entertainment mostly like to take landscape photos. Therefore, China has the largest group of amateur landscape photographers. However, compared with other types of photography, landscape photography is easy to enter but difficult to improve. It is not easy to take breathtaking and fascinating landscape photos. I have been communicating with grassroots amateur photography enthusiasts for a long time and have summarized some technical issues in landscape photography that are often overlooked. I would like to share them with you here.
