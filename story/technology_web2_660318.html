"The Sunflower Garden" - this ecological park themed around sunflowers also boasts two world-firsts: First, it covers an area of 66.7 hectares, making it the largest sunflower garden in the country; Second, it is the only place in the world where sunflowers bloom all year round. [Attraction Introduction] The Million Sunflower Garden has introduced 18 varieties of ornamental sunflowers from Europe and Japan, and cultivated a batch of varieties suitable for planting in subtropical regions. Through gene introduction, red, purple and other special colored sunflowers with strong fragrance have been cultivated. With scientific planning and planting, the Million Sunflower Garden keeps one million sunflowers blooming all year round.

With the development of tourism and people's preference for eco-tourism, the Sunflower Garden has evolved from a single sunflower garden into a theme flower city with famous flowers from all over the world. Sunflower Chicken 95% of the feed for Sunflower Chickens consists of fresh sunflower components. They have a fragrant aroma, crispy bones, and Vitamin E levels ten times higher than market chickens. In June 2004, during the provincial governor-level banquet at the Baiyun Hotel, Sunflower Chicken was the only chicken selected. This chicken has won numerous culinary awards and is hailed by food connoisseurs as the top chicken in Panyu, Valentine's Day ticket event at the Million Sunflower Garden.

Newken Lotus Root Panyu is a famous land of fish and rice, with abundant agricultural products. The local delicacies made from fresh local agricultural products carry a strong regional flavor. Panyu Newken lotus roots are large and starchy, making them a major local specialty, Valentine's Day ticket event at the Million Sunflower Garden. Many restaurants use them to create signature dishes, attracting many diners from Guangzhou and Hong Kong-Macau. Among them, braised pork belly with lotus root, braised duck meat with lotus root, and fried lotus slices with fermented bean curd are all local specialties. A particularly unique dish is green bean stuffed lotus root. Chefs first boil the green beans until they are skinless, then stuff them into the holes of the lotus root, add special ingredients, and stew until ready to serve. This dish is both fragrant and delicious.

Shawan Ginger Milk Dessert: Sweet and Beneficial If you're looking for food in Panyu, don't miss the traditional dessert of Shawan ginger milk, which has been passed down since the 1930s and suits modern tastes.

At the Shawan Qinfangyuan Dessert Shop in Panyu, according to Zeng Huizhuang, the successor of the famous Shawan ginger milk master "Niunai Dang," making ginger milk must use yellow, high-starch quality ginger, and select concentrated fresh buffalo milk.

When making, place the ginger juice at the bottom of the bowl and pour milk heated to about 80°C into the bowl and mix evenly. If the milk surface can support a spoon without sinking, then the ginger milk is successfully made.
