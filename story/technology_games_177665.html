The Last Letter to the Dearest Person

by lk0gamee on 2009-07-16 13:43:21

Please forgive my abrupt departure, I am so tired. We never open up to each other, and we hide things from each other that hurt both of us, which makes me very uncomfortable. Thinking about our past, it was really good, I miss our previous life so much. The pressure on me is overwhelming, making me feel so distressed. You seem to think your friends are more important than me, which makes it hard for me to balance. Many things you do, I keep inside without saying anything, but actually, I know everything. The reason you don't insist on divorce is only because...

I feel so bad when you don't care about me. In the past, even if I slightly ignored you, you would mind a lot, but now you don't. I don't know what you're thinking, but I'm in so much pain. These days, I've thought about a lot at night. I believe you won't change for me anymore. You need your friends, entertainment, and your personal space, there's no longer any room for me. But I need you so much. For example, tonight, I really wanted to be with you, but you refused. You think I can accompany you tomorrow or the day after, but as for your friends, they just cannot be left alone.

I hate why I care about you so much, why I pay attention to you so much. Why have I given so much for you, yet all I get is your increasing indulgence in yourself? Now I realize I was wrong, terribly wrong. Everyone is selfish, including me. I only require myself to do the right thing, never considering whether you can do the same. Because I thought as long as I did, you would too. It seems I still don't know you well enough.

Just like what I said today, as long as you don't betray me, I won't say anything. Actually, I understand in my heart that even if you betrayed me, I still wouldn't let go of you, I would accept you again because I can't bear to lose you. Falling in love with you has made it so hard for me to let go. Giving up feels like having a piece of my heart torn out, that painful. It's the first time I mentioned divorce, and I deeply regret it. I just wanted you to hold onto me more, to satisfy my vanity a little. But you often mention divorce, every time you're unhappy you bring it up, which causes me immense pain. I've never thought about divorcing you, I need you, I need you spiritually and physically.

I also hate you, because you made me fall in love with you, but hurt me deeply. To be honest, I can't live without you now. This is caused by you. You may understand or maybe not. I guess you don't. Falling in love with someone who keeps hurting you is extremely painful, because you know you can't live without them, but facing their repeated harm is unbearable. How much I wish he wouldn't hurt me, how much I wish...

I am full of contradictions. I know what I need in my heart, but I always care about face and don't act accordingly. I obviously care about you, but pretend not to. I clearly need you so much, but pretend not to. Seeing you send hundreds of text messages to other men makes me feel terrible, I want to say something but can't because you don't like it. Since I interfere with you, I can only secretly check your phone messages, or urgently stop your service. I just want to secretly care about you, but I'm afraid you'll get angry and say I don't give you enough space. But when you care, it's like this - you can't tolerate even a speck of dust in your eyes, you can't stand seeing him being close or familiar with other men, he should only be good and familiar with me.
