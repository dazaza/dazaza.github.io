Green paint, an environmentally friendly life!   In 2010, the economy gradually recovered, and there was a surge in demand for talent. The competition for talent in the paint sector became increasingly fierce. The demand and importance that paint companies placed on paint technology, sales, and management talent reached unprecedented levels. To meet the growing demand of paint companies for paint talent, a leading domestic paint industry recruitment website —— Paint Talent Network, in response to the requirements of many new and old clients, specially hosted the first large-scale online job fair of 2010: "Green Paint" enterprises.   According to statistics, talents participating in this job fair came from 30 provinces and cities including Guangdong, Jiangsu, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shandong, Beijing, Zhejiang, Fujian, Hunan, Hubei, Liaoning, and Sichuan. A total of 21,300 people participated, with most having work experience of around 3-5 years or 5-8 years. The job fair provided 10 distinctive positions such as paint engineers, color adjustment engineers, and wood lacquer engineers, along with approximately 100 regular recruitment positions. Annual members of the Paint Talent Network could participate in this job fair for free. As of now, about 200 qualified companies nationwide have been counted as participants.   The launch of the first "Green Paint" enterprise large-scale online job fair by the Paint Talent Network in 2010 pioneered the recruitment of technical engineers, sales engineers, and senior management talent in China's paint field. The success of previous job fairs helped nearly 2,000 paint companies fill their urgent position needs, earning praise and support from industry professionals. It provided and uncovered more specialized talents, promoting extensive exchanges among industry talents.
