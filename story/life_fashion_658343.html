Eat Your Way to Fair and Moisturized Skin, Say Goodbye to Spots

Date: 2012-2-1 22:33:30 Views: 9

Key tip: Everyone has a desire for beauty. Have you tried many whitening methods to deal with those pesky spots on your face? However, when many of these methods don't work, it can make you lose faith in whitening. In fact, a few simple methods can help you "drink" away the spots! If you've tried every method but still haven't seen results, why not give this simplest method a try?

Fair and Moisturized Skin

1. Drink a cup of tomato juice daily to prevent freckles

Drinking a cup of tomato juice or eating tomatoes regularly can be effective in preventing freckles. This is because tomatoes are rich in vitamin C, often referred to as a "vitamin C treasure trove." Vitamin C can inhibit the activity of tyrosinase in the skin, effectively reducing the formation of melanin, thus making the skin whiter and softer while fading dark spots.

2. Rice and cucumber porridge for spot removal

Take 100 grams of rice, 300 grams of fresh cucumber, 2 grams of salt, and 10 grams of ginger. Wash and peel the cucumber, remove its core, and slice it thinly. Rinse the rice and crush the ginger after washing it. Add about 1000 milliliters of water to the pot, place the rice and ginger over heat, boil on high heat, then switch to low heat until the rice is cooked. Add the cucumber slices and continue cooking until the porridge thickens. Season with salt and enjoy twice a day while warm. This helps moisturize the skin, remove spots, and aid in weight loss.

3. Regularly drinking lemon juice can make the skin whiter and softer

Blend lemons into juice and add an appropriate amount of rock sugar for consumption. Lemons are rich in vitamin C, with 100 grams of lemon juice containing up to 50 milligrams of vitamin C. They also contain calcium, phosphorus, iron, and B vitamins. Drinking lemon juice regularly not only makes the skin whiter and softer but also prevents skin blood vessel aging and eliminates facial pigmentation. Additionally, it helps prevent arteriosclerosis.

4. Black fungus removes black spots

Take 30 grams of black fungus and 20 jujubes. Clean the black fungus, remove the seeds from the jujubes, add an appropriate amount of water, and simmer for about half an hour. Consume once after breakfast and dinner daily. Regular intake can beautify the complexion, remove spots, enhance skin elasticity, and treat facial black spots and slimness. The black fungus mentioned in this recipe is recorded in the "Compendium of Materia Medica" as being able to remove black spots on the face. Black fungus can moisturize the skin and prevent skin aging. Jujube harmonizes the middle energizer, strengthens the spleen, and moisturizes the skin, aiding black fungus in removing spots.

5. After washing your face morning and evening, pat your face with fresh carrot juice

Grind fresh carrots and squeeze out the juice, taking 10-30 milliliters. After washing your face morning and evening, pat your face with fresh carrot juice. Once dry, gently pat your face with hands coated in plant oil. Additionally, drinking a cup of carrot juice daily also helps remove spots. This is because carrots are rich in vitamin A precursors. Vitamin A precursors can convert into vitamin A within the body. Vitamin A has the effect of smoothing and strengthening the skin, and can also prevent rough skin and freckles.

In fact, whitening and spot removal can be very simple, and beauty that comes from within through food is the best.
