1. Earn traffic points by staying online.

2. Exchange traffic points for website traffic.

The method to increase website traffic is: Log in to the Virtualvisit website, submit your website (or webpage) address in the traffic system, and set up information such as page click-through rate, source, and time control, and you will receive stable traffic.

The method to sell website traffic is: When the user's available points exceed 20,000, log in to the Virtualvisit website, and in the points trading system, submit the number of points you wish to sell. The official party will directly purchase them at a price of 2 yuan per 10,000 points.
