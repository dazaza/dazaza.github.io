Heart-wrenching Pain

My heart has been aching all along, and I've been forcing myself to forget the hidden sorrow in my heart. But alas, the unambitious me calls out his name in my dreams, only to wake up and realize it was just another empty dream. Tears flow uncontrollably as I regret falling in love with someone I shouldn't have, leaving me battered and bruised. I hate myself for thinking that if I could just learn to be cold-hearted like him, I would live carefree; if I could learn to despise, I would forget him completely.

But after so much effort, I realized the foolish me could never learn to be indifferent towards him or forget him thoroughly. I couldn't bear to delete all his messages. In every sleepless night, I reread the warmth he once gave me, still feeling the lingering warmth. With this faint warmth under my pillow, I can finally drift off to sleep.

I've gotten used to him texting me repeatedly asking what I'm doing, used to sweetly sleeping after his goodnight wishes, used to his playful teasing, which sometimes even annoyed me but was a sweet annoyance that I enjoy - he gives me sweet burdens.

I've also gotten used to hiding things from him. I know once some truths are revealed, I'll be hurt even more. He will never know that I couldn't forget him since the first sight of him, and he will never know that even though I knew he kept testing me, I let him uncover everything step by step because I couldn't bear to deceive him anymore, even if no one can clean up the mess in the end.

He set three rules for our relationship, and I accepted them all. I wanted to appear strong and indifferent, but I couldn't escape my own feelings. I could only secretly cry where he couldn't see me.

I think probably for the rest of my life, I will never feel such deep love again. If in this life he is just a passerby who brushes past me, I will try hard to remember his appearance and his scent, while I still can, imprinting his image more clearly in my mind. Although I fell in love with someone I shouldn't have in this life, I still want to thank him for the warmth he once gave me. If there's another life, I will definitely not drink the heart-wrenching soup of oblivion, and I will wait for him in the most beautiful time of my next life.
