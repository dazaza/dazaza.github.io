### Integration of Work and Study, Practical Teaching, and Military-style Management: A Comprehensive Introduction to Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School

#### **Teaching and Management Model**

Our school adopts an integration of work and study model, emphasizing practical training and frugal learning. We possess first-class facilities for practical training in Hebei Province, a simple, fast, and accurate teaching mode, full closed quasi-military student management, comprehensive employment placement services, and the lowest tuition fees in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and registered with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shi Huo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road. It has excellent geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school adheres to the development philosophy of "liberating thoughts, being pragmatic and innovative, and thinking diligently for change." We continuously reform our teaching and management methods, firmly establish a "market competition" awareness, and take market demand as our orientation. Based on the needs of enterprises, we explore new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich experience in both teaching and management. Our students have received widespread praise from employers after graduation.

---

#### **1. First-Class Teaching Facilities Oriented by Enterprise Standards**

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for conducting quality vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested over 10 million yuan, building a numerical control (NC) practice workshop equipped with more than 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, conventional lathes, and conventional milling machines. Additionally, we have constructed communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape training rooms, and new teaching buildings as supporting facilities.

---

#### **2. Enterprise-Oriented Teaching Mode**

We have established a teaching mode based on enterprise positions. In the teaching process, all students are managed as if they were enterprise employees. Through competitive job assignments, students take on roles such as team leaders, line supervisors, or regular workers during their "work." Their performance is evaluated through daily assessments and exams, and outstanding individuals are recognized as excellent team leaders or skilled technicians. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day, students' "work" is monitored for quality, and statistics are compiled monthly. Based on these scores, wages are calculated, which include components such as workload, performance evaluation, work discipline, appearance, safe operation, and bonuses. This approach significantly boosts students' motivation and competitiveness, enabling them to master knowledge and skills more quickly and effectively.

Through enterprise-oriented teaching reforms, students gradually adapt to enterprise management models while still in school, meeting job requirements and eventually surpassing enterprise standards.

---

#### **3. Quasi-Military Student Management Oriented by Enterprise Needs**

We implement a fully enclosed quasi-military management system, cultivating students who meet the comprehensive requirements of enterprises. Enterprises not only need technically skilled workers but also emphasize overall qualities such as teamwork, obedience, execution ability, and competitive awareness. To improve these qualities, efforts are made across all aspects of the school, integrating comprehensive quality education throughout the students' learning process.

**Key Features of Management:**

- **Quasi-Military Management:** Drawing on the principle of "becoming a responsible adult before becoming talented," we hire retired military officers to conduct specialized quasi-military management. From daily routines, dress codes, behavior, etiquette, to housekeeping, students develop good living habits, organizational discipline, and become disciplined talents with a strong military ethos.

- **Vocational Quality Education Management:** During the learning process, we offer vocational quality courses divided into theoretical instruction and practical training. These two aspects complement each other, enhancing students' understanding and transforming vocational quality requirements into voluntary behaviors. Additionally, we collaborate with enterprises to provide internship opportunities, allowing students to experience real-world work environments under the guidance of school leaders and teachers, laying a solid foundation for future employment.

---

#### **4. Employment-Oriented Placement Services**

As the quality of our students improves and employment channels expand, we have established numerous internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi region, and the Jiaodong Peninsula.

The primary employment direction for our CNC specialty graduates is the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All internship units are world-renowned mechanical manufacturing enterprises listed among the Fortune Global 500. The professional alignment rate for internships and employment reaches 90%, and student satisfaction exceeds 95%.

---

#### **5. Comprehensive后勤Services and Free Dining System**

In Hebei Province, our school uniquely implements a "free dining system" for all enrolled students. This alleviates the financial burden on parents and ensures proper nutrition for students' physical development and academic demands. It also avoids potential health issues caused by eating outside the school.

The school's dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms are well-equipped. The logistics department always prioritizes "service" and strives to be proactive, considerate, efficient, and convenient.

---

#### **6. Tuition-Free Policy**

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational institution that upholds a strong sense of social responsibility—sourcing from society and giving back to society. Leveraging our robust educational strength and forward-thinking concepts, we aim to maximize cost savings for students' families. Students only pay miscellaneous fees for three years, while tuition and accommodation expenses are covered through work-study programs and vocational quality training.

We also established a "Hongzhi Class" to fully or partially exempt all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly qualified students from low-income families. All students receive a national scholarship of 1,500 yuan per year.
