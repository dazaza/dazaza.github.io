My Leading Lady is You

A long, long time ago, he and she were in the same class.

He was interested in physics, mathematics, and chemistry, but unfortunately, English was his weak point. No matter how hard he tried to memorize it, he just couldn't remember! On the other hand, her physics, mathematics, and chemistry weren't very good. Although he was talkative, he always found himself tongue-tied when facing girls! She, however, was lively and outgoing, always having endless things to say.

At the beginning of the school term, they were strangers to each other, and she even called him by the wrong name.

Later, when their seats were rearranged, although they sat far apart, her seatmate happened to be his former seatmate. Thus, they gradually started simple conversations.

One ordinary morning, she said to him, "Can you help me with my studies? Physics, math, chemistry... I'm about to die!" (What a childish thing to say!)

This unexpected request shocked him; it was the first time someone had asked him for tutoring, and he agreed. At that time, he didn't have any particular feelings for her.

Later, she learned from him that his English grades were terrible. She encouraged him to work hard on it and not give up. She even offered to tutor him in English. He was deeply moved because this was the first time at school that someone had encouraged him.

He tutored her for two days, and in the final exam a few days later, she finally passed, excitedly showing him her test paper. At that moment, he smiled too, feeling that his efforts hadn't been wasted. However, his own English score remained as usual – failing. Out of four subjects, he advanced in one but regressed in three. Feeling like a failure, he wondered why his English was so bad!!

After the tutoring incident, some classmates began to say that they were a couple!! Despite his attempts to keep it a secret that he was tutoring her, rumors spread quickly. He couldn't explain and didn't bother to. The truth would clear itself. It's just tutoring, after all; what's the big deal? In fact, sometimes he even liked these rumors, though he didn't know why.

Another ordinary morning, they were chatting casually, and then she invited him to go to McDonald's with her and his younger sister, AA system. He accepted.

It was his first time going there. They had to decide what to eat beforehand. He always accommodated her, saying he could eat anything. That day, they ordered a bit of food, but he ate very little, perhaps because he wasn't used to it! As they talked about their own stories, laughing and eating, the atmosphere felt great. He just listened quietly, watching her complex and ever-changing expressions, feeling inexplicable sweetness inside.

Unconsciously, whenever he saw her laughing and joking around with other classmates, he always felt a strange kind of jealousy. He realized he had fallen for her, enjoying her chatter nearby and her cute, childish smile. The feeling of liking someone was strangely wonderful. But he didn't know if she liked him back. He wanted to confess, hesitating but lacking the courage. What if she rejected him? Then he wouldn't be able to remain friends with her, and he feared returning to square one. So he silently paid attention to her joys and sorrows.

Suddenly, he remembered a song, "My Leading Lady is You." The lyrics perfectly described them.

"It was you who suddenly entered my world that day, changing everything. I willingly bid farewell to my past for you..." For her, he grew tired of online games. A person who used to go online every day stopped doing so for her. Of course, he would still go online with his classmates during weekends. Compared to before, he had changed a lot for her.

A semester passed, and he ultimately didn't say anything to her, nor did he hear anything from her. He was disappointed.

In fact, he really wanted to tell her: "I love you. Will you be my leading lady?"

...

He hoped so much that she could read this article and understand his heart! Perhaps this was an ending without results.........
