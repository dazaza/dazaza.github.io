[Game Introduction]

NBA LIVE 08 will start from the overall concept of basketball, with the slogan "back to basics" coming out from EA Canada - the main creative studio of one of EA SPORTS' popular game series, NBA LIVE. In recent years, the NBA LIVE series has not achieved the success that everyone expected in the market, especially on the Xbox 360 platform. The development team behind the game began to accept criticism about this game from all sides. Based on what we saw yesterday at EA Canada regarding NBA LIVE 08, it can be affirmed that they are indeed making efforts to improve this time.

Maybe, the disappointment in the recent NBA LIVE games is not surprising. The team focused more on the control system of the game, such as the free superstar control in NBA LIVE 06 and the complete freedom operation system in NBA LIVE 07. It seems that the team was moving further away from solving basic problems affecting gameplay, which even led to a decline in the quality of other game content. In LIVE 08, the production team will overcome all difficulties and focus on the fundamental essence of the game before adding new content.

The simplest way to distinguish between NBA LIVE 07 and the upcoming LIVE 08 is to compare them in all aspects, as introduced by Ullrich, the creator of LIVE 08. First, let's review LIVE 07: sluggish players, poor basketball physics effects, and unrealistic shooting actions. When Ullrich demonstrated NBA LIVE 08 on TV, the differences immediately became apparent. When players accelerate, they indeed appear to be playing on hardwood floors rather than skating as in previous versions. This improvement includes passing and shooting actions, while the contact between players on the court also feels more realistic. Of course, the most exciting part is that the frame rate of NBA LIVE 08 has increased. Ullrich informed everyone that the game will run at 60 frames per second.
