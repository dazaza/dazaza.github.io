Introduction to the World's Renowned Exhibition Centers: Guilin International Convention and Exhibition Center

by longersing on 2009-01-15 16:04:43

Introduction to the International Convention and Exhibition Center:

The Guilin International Convention and Exhibition Center is located in the heart of Guilin, known for its unparalleled natural scenery, and is a modern exhibition center. Covering a construction area of 51,000 square meters, it is divided into six exhibition halls with a total of 2,000 international standard booths. The exhibition halls are column-free, creating large spaces with spans ranging from 50 to 78 meters, and clear heights of 9 meters, 12 meters, 15 meters, and 28 meters respectively. The floor load capacity is 3 tons per square meter, and the center is fully equipped with water, electricity, and air conditioning facilities. There are 16 entrances and exits, with freight entrances measuring 6 meters high and 5 meters wide, allowing freight vehicles direct access to the booths. Additionally, there is a 25,000 square meter outdoor exhibition area that can accommodate various exhibition needs. The business building, covering an area of 9,000 square meters, provides delegation offices, meeting rooms, negotiation rooms, business rooms, press and information release rooms, as well as corresponding banking, postal, communication, warehousing, freight forwarding, customs clearance, tourism, catering, and entertainment services.

The exhibition center is situated on the 100,000 square meter Jia Tianxia Square, where large-scale celebratory events can be held. It also features a large parking lot capable of accommodating 1,000 cars simultaneously.

According to current statistics, whether in terms of venue scale or the level of facilities and equipment, the Guilin International Convention and Exhibition Center ranks among the top 10 convention venues nationwide.

Guilin Conference Venues:

1. International Conference Hall: Located on the first floor, covering an area of 1,296 square meters with wool carpet flooring, it can accommodate 1,000 people (theater-style). Equipped with a large projection screen, a 6-channel simultaneous interpretation system, sound system, and voting system.

2. Multi-function Hall: Located on the first floor, covering an area of 455 square meters with sandalwood solid wood flooring, it can accommodate 1,000 people (theater-style). Equipped with a large projection screen, a 6-channel simultaneous interpretation system, sound system, and voting system.

3. First Meeting Room (Round Table Meeting Room): Located on the second floor, covering an area of 178 square meters with sandalwood solid wood flooring, it can accommodate 60 people.

4. Third Meeting Room: Located on the first floor, covering an area of 158 square meters with wool carpet flooring, it can accommodate 40 people.

5. Fourth Meeting Room: Located on the first floor, covering an area of 108 square meters with sandalwood solid wood flooring, it can accommodate 30 people.

6. Electric Classroom: Located on the second floor, covering an area of 178 square meters with sandalwood solid wood flooring, it can accommodate 60 people.

Fee Explanation:

1. The above fees include rental fees, cleaning of public areas, lighting, and necessary security.

2. The minimum rental area shall not be less than the area of a single exhibition hall.

3. The aforementioned daily rate refers to normal working hours from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM. Overtime charges apply for any time exceeding this period.

4. The duration of international and domestic mixed exhibitions shall not exceed 8 days (including setup and dismantling). For exhibitions exceeding 8 days, additional rental fees for overseas booths will apply.

5. The location of the registration reception desk and the opening ceremony site for the exhibition organizer will be uniformly arranged by the convention and exhibition center. In principle, the corresponding outdoor space will be used as the opening ceremony venue based on the rented exhibition hall area.
