Which one do you want, a cyber wife or a real-life wife?

by gege on 2012-03-07 13:55:58

Network wife or real-life wife, which one would you choose?

The girl stood dumbly with a lunchbox in her hands behind the boy. She saw him happily typing on the keyboard, telling his online wife about their endless love. The girl's heart twitched, but she still told herself that it was just the internet. She placed the lunchbox on the edge of the boy's desk and said, "Eat quickly, it won't be tasty if it gets cold." The boy grunted without even looking up, continuing to pour out sweet words. The girl walked away silently.

It was already 2 AM. The girl sighed. She no longer expected the boy to see her off, but he didn’t even say a word of concern. She smiled tolerantly and went home. Several months later--

The girl said: "Look me in the eyes and tell me you betrayed our love, go ahead!"

The boy said: "No, I haven't. I just said she wants to visit me, that's all. I said no, but she insists on coming. She knows I have a girlfriend. She will come to this internet cafe tomorrow afternoon. She said she just wants to see me!"

The girl said: "Just to see you? Have you replaced our five-year relationship with your online romance? Can I talk to her?"

The boy said: "I haven't, I haven't... She also said she wants to meet you! I told her I wouldn't pick her up because you're here, so she'll come by herself!"

At exactly 3 PM the next afternoon, the boy's online wife appeared at the internet cafe. The boy called her Snow. Snow was beautiful, full of vitality, only 18 years old, five years younger than the girl. Snow's gaze passed over the boy and fell on the girl's weary face. They smiled at each other, without any hostility. The girl signaled for the boy to step aside. The boy wisely sat back in front of the computer and continued his online world. The girl took Snow's hand and sat down on the chair next to her. After a long silence, Snow spoke boldly: "I love him, not just in the network."

The girl: "Love is not passion, not impulse. Do you understand him? Or do you even understand yourself?"

Snow: "There is no more love between you two, right? Why not break up amicably?"

The girl: "Haha, let me tell you ten things first!"

Snow: "Go ahead. I'm listening."

The girl aligned her gaze with the boy, and the boy immediately retracted his ears. The girl smiled, pulled back her gaze, and said:

First, he is a person who loves sleeping late. Every morning, you have to call his name at least twenty times before he wakes up obediently. After leaving the house, he never buys breakfast himself. So, every day you have to prepare breakfast for him and make sure he finishes eating. He is such a careless person, unable to take care of himself.

Second, he is obsessed with the virtual world and can't pull himself away. Every night when he plays at home, he shouts loudly, never considering others' sleep. And the next day, you always have to face the neighbors' ridicule and apologize repeatedly.

Third, he has many friends. Whenever they eat together, drinking is the first priority. His body isn't strong. His alcohol tolerance is seven liang of white wine or four bottles of beer. Exceeding this limit will get him drunk. When drunk, everyone drinks tea, but he vomits endlessly after drinking tea, which hurts his stomach.

Fourth, he is a football fan. Every four years there is the World Cup, every two years the Champions League or European Championship, and every week Serie A, Football World, Premier League, etc. He never misses them. In the middle of the night, he gets up to watch matches. After he finishes watching, you must get up to clean the trash beside the TV and the kitchen, where the food he rummaged through is in disarray.

Fifth, every autumn, his eyes start blinking non-stop and tearing up when exposed to wind because his eyes are sensitive to it. Do you know what medicine he should take? For five years, I've given him that medicine every year, but he doesn't even know what it is or its name.

Sixth, his hands crack every winter, not from chilblains, but due to a lack of a certain vitamin in his body. It cannot be cured fundamentally, only alleviated. Do you know what ointment or pills he needs to apply and take? He doesn't either, because he never takes medicine by himself. You have to put it in his hand for him to take it!

Seventh, he is a person who values his face immensely. Among friends, someone inevitably likes to joke about him or his girlfriend. No matter how wronged or excessive, he cannot show it in front of his friends. Friends are the most important thing to him.

Eighth, sometimes he doesn't come home, or stays up all night in the internet cafe indulging in his virtual world. PK (player vs player) is his hobby. Sometimes he chats with real-life friends until dawn, or knocks on the door in the middle of the night to tell you he's "safely home."

Ninth, I believe he is absolutely not a flirtatious person. Even if his friends fool around with other girls outside their girlfriends' knowledge, he would never do such a thing, because he values the feeling of being together with someone, not satisfying his so-called male desires.

Tenth, overall, he is a good man, just too careless and unaware of taking care of himself, like a big boy. Haha, when he opens the cabinet to take clothes, he will definitely forget to close it; when he opens the drawer to take a lighter, he will surely forget to close it. If you don't follow him, he might even forget to lock the door when he goes out.

Do you understand these things? Can you do all of them? How do you think love should be expressed?" Snow said: "Point by point, love leaves traces on anyone. You have penetrated like water dripping on stone. Every word, every sentence, leaves me nowhere to hide."

By this time, the boy had exited his virtual world. Every word the girl said struck his heart. Yes, five years of feelings. She knew him too well—his flaws, his strengths, his preferences, everything. For five years, why hadn't he realized how muddled he was? If the girl hadn't been by his side all along, what a mess his life would have become. Looking at his clothes, pants, shoes, socks, he even didn't know why they were always so neat and clean. Why had he never held a spatula yet could eat delicious meals every day? Why did he feel so healthy despite his previous complaints of discomfort? For five years, he had always eaten what the girl prepared for him. Why were his friends always envious of him? Why did he feel the love between him and the girl had become so faint? Why did he release his emotions to the online beauties? Because it was too peaceful. They almost never argued because the girl always gave in, quietly loving him through the trivialities of life. Suddenly, he understood why some people say true love is not "until the mountains lose their peaks and the earth merges with the sky," but rather understanding and tolerance in shared hardships. The boy turned and stared at the girl. For many years, he had never carefully looked at the girl's face for more than five seconds. For the first time, he noticed that the lively, beautiful girl from before had now become a mature, elegant woman.

The boy stood up and walked to the girl, saying, "Why have you gotten older? I just noticed--hehe! Wife, let's accompany Snow and play well. She rarely comes." Snow smiled knowingly, "No need. I'm leaving. You're a good man, and you have a great wife. I believe someday I will love someone like her. But the man I love will definitely not be you, because you are not mine." With that, Snow turned and left. Her tears formed a beautiful rainbow, gracefully curving as she turned away!

The girl maintained a polite smile throughout. Perhaps her heart had been deeply wounded, but her calmness and wisdom saved their painstakingly nurtured five-year relationship. The boy gently hugged the girl, smelling her hair. For five years, the fragrance of the girl's hair had never changed, just like her unwavering love for him. Her hair was still as fine and smooth as ever, just like the harmonious and gentle love she cultivated. The boy laughed and asked the girl:

"Wife, what medicine did you give me? I really don't know till now."

"I don't want to tell you, because only I know. If you leave me, you might not survive, haha."

"Ah, that's terrifying. So, am I going to be imprisoned by you for life?"

"How did you just figure that out? Who made you so restless? Even Guanyin gave Tang Monk a secret formula to control the monkey. So, heaven helped me since I'm pitiful."

"Right, wife, you never watch football matches, right? Why do you know so clearly about events happening every four years, every two years, even every week?"

"Alas... near ink turns black. Who told you to set your account password as something like Batistuta?"

"Ah--you even know that? Don't steal my equipment!"

"What decision?"

"Tomorrow is Sunday. I wonder if there will be staff at the Civil Affairs Bureau. To secure my equipment, I decide to get a marriage certificate tomorrow, making our property joint. That way, you will cherish it well, ha!"

"Do you have to propose in such a fancy way? I don't even want to marry you."

Suddenly, the boy picked up the girl and kissed her fiercely. The girl pushed him away quickly, her face red, embarrassed by the stares of the people around them, who all focused on them. The boy loudly questioned the girl: "Will you marry me? If not, I will perform a long kiss here today."

After speaking, the boy hugged the girl again. The girl laughed happily and said, "Yes, yes! I've waited for five years. Fool!"
