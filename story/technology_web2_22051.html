Top Ten Windows Live Spaces Elite

by dutmcse on 2007-06-09 09:24:05

Someone summarized the elites in Baidu Space, why is it that Windows Live Space always lags behind Baidu? The display of elites in Baidu Space is mostly due to the efforts of Baidu's popular figures. However, Windows Live seems yet to fully integrate into Chinese culture, and the concept of popular promotion has not been established. Perhaps due to their wealth and arrogance, they neglect this aspect, so I will take on this task. Hopefully, this can be of some help to the netizens of Live Space. This elite list is entirely based on a summary of the Live Spaces homepage and several major Live Spaces links. Also, only spaces with a Google PageRank > 4 are included. There might be some excellent spaces that were not included, but those that are included are or were absolutely the elites of Live Spaces.

1、Live Space Alliance (PageRank=4): An official space alliance organization that played a significant role in promoting the initial phase of MSN Space.

2、Easy Start To The Day (PageRank=4): An officially recommended personal Live Space tutorial space, with relatively timely updates to the tutorials.

3、Mom and Dad Club (PageRank=4): One of the earliest and more active Live Space organizations, dedicated to early childhood education.

4、Little Cape's Imaginary World (PageRank=4): The works of painter Ji Mi are displayed here quite adequately.

5、Green Bean Frog Tribe (PageRank=4): A well-known frog online, consistently offering free emoticon avatars for users.

6、Fat Rabbit Zhouzhou (PageRank=4): A network comic artist with many fans.

7、Poison (PageRank=4): A blog that started with unconventional photos and remains a photo blog to this day.

8、MSN Toolbox (PageRank=4): One of the pioneers in early MSN Space tutorials, uniquely also aggregating a large amount of material and resources.

9、Guoguo Materials (PageRank=4): One of the famous material sites in Live Space, as renowned as You Qi, though You no longer updates his space.

10、Exploring the Secrets of Msn Spaces (PageRank=4): A tutorial space as famous as Baiwu Jinji and Technique Summary, included here because it has consistently updated.

In fact, there are many other outstanding spaces, and it's impossible to include all with just 10 spots. Unfortunately, none of the spaces in Live Space have a PageRank greater than 5! Let's wait and see.
