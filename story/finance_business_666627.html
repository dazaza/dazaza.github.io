Yuzhou Properties: Eight Projects Welcome New Year with Great Discounts, Three Projects Rank in the Top Ten

Last week, the total number of new home transactions across the city was 409 units, with residential units accounting for 218. This represents a 40.76% decrease compared to the previous week, showing unsatisfactory sales performance. However, it's worth noting that three projects from Yuzhou Properties stood out significantly. The three projects—Yuzhou Central Coast, Yuzhou City on City, and Yuzhou Huan Dong International—all ranked in the top ten for residential unit transactions. They respectively secured third, fourth, and sixth place in last week's residential transaction rankings.

In a challenging market environment, these three Yuzhou projects managed to achieve remarkable sales results due to their superior quality and high cost-effectiveness. Yuzhou Central Coast, offering prime sea-view apartments starting at just 6,888 yuan per square meter, was warmly received by the entire city upon its launch, defying market trends. Meanwhile, Yuzhou Huan Dong International, a large community spanning 500,000 square meters, leveraged advantages such as lower total prices to claim the top spot in Tong'an District's residential transactions in 2011. With a total of 975 units sold throughout the year, it also firmly held the fourth position in the top ten residential projects across the city in 2011. Additionally, as a central project within Xiamen Island featuring zero common area deductions and excellent value-for-money apartments, Yuzhou City on City Phase II launched a special promotional campaign during the Chinese New Year period, with prices starting at only 13,666 yuan per square meter, attracting numerous buyers.

Furthermore, Yuzhou Properties has eight projects shining brightly in Xiamen. In addition to the aforementioned three top-ranked projects, they include Yuzhou Yunding International, Yuzhou Golf Manor, Yuzhou Xitishop City, and Yuzhou Sunshine Flower City. It is reported that from January 12, 2012, to March 3, 2012, all eight Yuzhou projects will offer significant New Year discounts, along with viewing gifts and car prize draws for property purchases.

[Yuzhou Xidishop City]

In 2011, Yuzhou Properties, with the perspective of an urban operator and innovative development models, deeply cultivated Lujiazui Island, leveraging the inherent advantages of the Haixi Economic Zone's core to create an international urban resort complex—Xidishop City—that lives up to the expectations of the entire city.

North of the Film City, International Urban Resort Center

The project is located north of the Tong'an Film City, boasting unparalleled natural resources, a strong cultural atmosphere, and superior resource integration, crafting an unprecedented super-complex in Haixi—a world-class urban resort center. The area where Xidishop City is located belongs to the emerging northern urban area focused on sightseeing tourism. Surrounded by unique scenic resources such as Dongxi River, Tingxi River, Meishan Mountain, Dalun Mountain, Beichen Mountain, and Sanxiu Mountain, it also boasts a national 4A-level scenic area, numerous tourist attractions, and five-star hot spring hotels. Thousand-Mu Urban Resort Complex
