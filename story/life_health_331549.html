Yesterday I introduced to everyone the disease that men should be most alert to: prostate disease, and also explained its importance in detail. Today I will introduce how to take care of the prostate in daily life.

1. Avoid sitting for long periods

The location of the prostate means that men are largely sitting on their prostates, so men who sit for long periods often have a heavier burden on their prostates. The seated position slows down blood circulation, leading to chronic congestion of the prostate, accumulation of local metabolic products, blockage of the prostate ducts, and impaired drainage of prostatic fluid, which can lead to chronic prostatitis.

2. Be careful not to get cold

The prostate has abundant adrenergic receptors, and when it gets cold, it is extremely easy to cause sympathetic nerve excitement, leading to contraction of the adrenal glands, increasing pressure in the urethra, affecting urination, and difficulty in urination, which can have adverse effects on the prostate.

3. Prevent long-term constipation

People with constipation accumulate a large amount of feces in the rectum, which can exacerbate the congestion of the nearby prostate. At the same time, people with constipation often strain during bowel movements, increasing abdominal pressure, compressing the prostate, narrowing the urethra, obstructing urination, and being detrimental to prostate health.

4. Maintain regular sexual activity

People who engage in frequent sexual activity within a short period have an incidence rate of acute prostatitis as high as 89.7%. On the other hand, excessive abstinence can lead to excessive accumulation of prostate secretions, causing over-expansion and congestion of the prostate, which can also trigger inflammation. In addition, practices such as coitus interruptus or external ejaculation can also cause swelling and inflammation of the prostate.

5. Be cautious about hormone supplements

Some advertisements claim that testosterone therapy can increase male libido, but the fact that high doses of testosterone are often associated with prostate enlargement and tumors is rarely mentioned. If men are already suffering from prostate problems, they should stay away from these hormone supplements.

6. Exercise under the sun

Life stress increases the likelihood of prostate diseases, and when life stress decreases, prostate symptoms can be alleviated. So try to maintain a relaxed state, such as going outdoors with your family to exercise. Moderate physical activity helps enhance the body's resistance and can improve local blood circulation in the prostate.
