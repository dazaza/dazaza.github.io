Love at first sight

I forgot how many times I have sat in this café. The Capuchin in front of me was almost finished, Lan Yi lowered her head, her fingers tapping on the table from time to time, and her eyes occasionally stealing a glance at that cold and tall figure. What should I do? My face is all crumpled. Recalling a few weeks ago:

"Xiao Yi, do you think it will snow in Beijing this winter?"

"Of course it will, it's only November."

"I don't think so, or we make a bet." Xiao Qi's eyes kept flashing.

"Alright. What's the bet?" Lanyi didn't notice anything strange and agreed at once. It will definitely snow. That's too sure.

"If it snows, I'll do one thing for you. Otherwise..."

"I'll do one thing for you, right." Lanyi continued.

"That's right."

Thoughts returned, and it turned out that Beijing really didn't snow in 2010. The thing Xiao Qi asked her to do was confessing to someone in this café. Sounds incredible, but no matter how she begged Xiao Qi to change it, Xiao Qi was determined. There's nothing can be done, she has to accept the loss, but she really doesn't have the courage.

Once again, her gaze fell on the tall figure behind the counter who was methodically brewing coffee. Her heart was beating a little fast, her face slightly flushed. What should I do? Lowered her head. No, Xiao Qi had already given her an ultimatum.

Calm down, take a deep breath, isn't it just confessing, say something then flash away, never come to this café again... constantly comforting herself, she looked up ready to look at that figure...

"Ah.." Lanyi suddenly screamed, feeling the stares of the crowd, she quickly covered her mouth, then blankly stared at the tall figure in front of her.

The corner of the man's mouth slightly rose, "Do you have something to say to me?"

Lanyi's face instantly turned red as if it could match the color of her skin. How did he know I have something to say to him? Am I that obvious?

"Hmm...that...that..." stammered for a long time without saying anything.

Someone lightly smirked, "Wait for me a moment." He gently turned and left. Leaving obviously stunned one...

Soon, someone (named Ye Chen) came back, holding a cup of fragrant coffee and sat down.

Looking at the magnified handsome face in front of her, Lanyi was so nervous that she didn't know what to say, her thoughts in her brain had paused.

"You can say it now," Ye Chen said calmly, glancing at her from the corner of his eye.

".............that, that..." Just say it, he came himself, no need to find him, if not now, when? Lanyi gritted her teeth...determined...

"I...I...you...you..." lowering her head..."Can you go out with me..." in one breath. Then she felt so shy that she wanted to hide her head under the table...then waited for some sarcastic words from him.

"Alright."

"Alright??" Lanyi surprisingly raised her head and then quickly lowered it again when she saw his seemingly-not-really smile...

And just like that, Lanyi started dating without fully understanding the situation.

Much later, at her and Ye Chen's wedding, she learned that her best friend Xiao Qi had been bribed by a certain boss-level person long ago.

Later she asked him: "If it had snowed..."

He embraced her, gently stroking her hairline and said softly, "Then I would have confessed to you." Mentally added a sentence, of course, I prefer to see your cute confession, this sentence couldn't be said, otherwise she would beat him.

Later, it was a very sweet story. Originally, love at first sight had already happened.
