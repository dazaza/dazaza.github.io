What are the dietary therapy methods for leukemia?

Food is the most basic guarantee for human survival and can meet the energy needs for survival. At the same time, most foods can also play a role in preventing or treating diseases, which is what people often hear as food therapy. Below we introduce the dietary therapy methods for treating leukemia for your reference.

Garlic chives stir-fried river clam meat

[Ingredients] 250 grams of garlic chives, 250 grams of river clam meat, 2 cloves of garlic and appropriate seasonings. [Preparation method] Wash the garlic chives and cut them into segments 2-3 cm long. Loosen the river clam meat with the back of a knife, slightly blanch in boiling water, then slice and marinate with rice wine and salt. Heat the vegetable oil, cool it down for a moment, fry minced garlic and ginger until fragrant, add the garlic chives and stir-fry until half-cooked, then add the clam meat, season with fine salt and sugar, boil for about 4 minutes, and add monosodium glutamate to complete. [Effect] Clear heat and detoxify, fight cancer and promote urination. [Main treatment] Can be used as an adjuvant treatment for all kinds of malignant tumors and leukemia.

Garlic tofu

[Ingredients] 400 grams of soft tofu, 100 grams of green garlic, and appropriate seasonings. [Preparation method] Heat the vegetable oil, lower the temperature to six-tenths hot, put in the garlic sections and stir-fry until soft, then add the tofu cubes, stir while adding appropriate amounts of rice wine, soy sauce, fine salt, and sugar as seasoning, add a little water and bring to a boil, thicken with cornstarch, and adjust with monosodium glutamate. [Effect] Tonify deficiency and detoxify. [Main treatment] Suitable for patients with all kinds of malignant tumors and leukemia.

Fresh mushroom cabbage dumplings

[Ingredients] 500 grams of flour, 500 grams of cabbage, 100 grams of fresh mushrooms, and appropriate seasonings. [Preparation method] Mix the flour with a trace amount of fine salt and 200 grams of cold water, knead repeatedly into a smooth and soft dough, cover and let rest for 15 minutes. Blanch the cabbage in boiling water until soft, chop finely, mix with chopped fresh mushrooms, add minced ginger, green onions, rice wine, fine salt, sesame oil, and monosodium glutamate to make the filling. Divide the dough into 60 portions, roll out the wrappers, fill and form into dumplings, cook in boiling water until done. [Effect] Detoxify and fight cancer. [Main treatment] Suitable for leukemia, uterine cancer, skin cancer, sarcoma, etc.

Oral mushroom braised tofu

[Ingredients] 15 grams of oral mushrooms, one small piece of tofu, 10 grams of ham bits, 10 grams of peas, and appropriate seasonings. [Preparation method] Soak the oral mushrooms, wash clean, and clarify the soaking water for later use. Cut the tofu into strips, blanch in boiling water, then drain. Bring fresh soup and the soaking water to a boil in a pot, add the oral mushrooms, tofu, ham bits, and peas, add salt, simmer for about 10 minutes, thicken with cornstarch, adjust with monosodium glutamate, and drizzle with a little sesame oil. [Usage] Eat with meals. [Effect] Tonify qi, strengthen the spleen, and benefit the stomach. [Main treatment] Suitable for lung disease, leukemia, anemia, infantile calcium deficiency, iron deficiency, etc.

Garlic-braised eggplant

[Ingredients] 25 grams of garlic, 500 grams of eggplant, 2 grams of salt, 5 grams of sugar, 10 grams of soy sauce, 1 gram of monosodium glutamate, 5 grams of ginger, 10 grams of green onions, 10 grams of dry starch, 50 grams of vegetable oil, and 200 grams of clear soup. [Preparation method] Remove the stem from the eggplant, wash clean, cut in half, and on each half's surface, make crosshatch cuts about 1 cm wide, then cut into rectangular pieces approximately 4 cm long and 2 cm wide (deep cuts but not severed). Cut each clove of garlic in half. Heat the pot with vegetable oil until the bubbles dissipate and blue smoke appears, then remove from heat. Once the oil has cooled slightly, add the eggplant to the pot and stir-fry, then add minced ginger, soy sauce, salt, garlic, and clear soup, bring to a boil, then simmer over low heat for 10 minutes, mix evenly, sprinkle with green onion, thicken with a mixture of sugar and starch diluted with water, and adjust with monosodium glutamate. [Effect] Clear blood heat, relieve qi stagnation, promote diuresis, and detoxify evil toxins. [Main treatment] Suitable for treating purpura, leukemia, etc.

Toad boiled eggs

[Ingredients] One live toad, one egg. [Preparation method] Clean the toad, remove the internal organs, place one egg inside the abdomen, sew it up, and boil for 30-40 minutes. [Usage] Eat the egg daily, one course lasts 7 days, observe symptoms and blood conditions, if no adverse reactions occur, continue taking. [Effect] Detoxify and fight cancer, support the righteous and expel evil. [Main treatment] Acute leukemia.

Winter melon Job's tears soup

[Ingredients] 300 grams of winter melon, 30 grams of Job's tears. [Preparation method] Cook both together for 1 hour. [Usage] Drink the soup after adding salt or sugar for flavoring, once daily or every other day. [Effect] Strengthen the spleen, eliminate dampness, detoxify and clear heat. [Main treatment] Promotes cell immunity and humoral immunity, has a certain anti-cancer effect, used as an adjuvant treatment for leukemia and other tumors, also suitable for diseases caused by low immune function in children.
