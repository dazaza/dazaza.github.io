Three male workers implement the AA system and hire prostitutes in the bathhouse

by jeffbcl5644 on 2010-06-23 21:07:00

Four people were caught

The Morning Post reports that with the increased police efforts to crack down on prostitution, gambling, and drugs, two brothels in Pingdu have gone underground. Prostitutes contact clients through pimps and then carry out their activities in rented apartments or hotels, playing guerrilla warfare with the police. The day before yesterday, the Pingdu police successfully busted these two dens of iniquity based on clues provided by netizens after plainclothes officers conducted covert investigations.

Netizens post threads providing clues

On the morning of June 20th, while browsing a website forum, two netizens successively posted threads titled "Prostitution still exists in Pingdu," which drew the attention of the police officers from the Public Security Brigade of the Pingdu Public Security Bureau. One netizen claimed that Ms. Li, the owner of a certain bathhouse inn in Huibu Town, Pingdu City, to evade police checks, adopted a method of contacting clients first and then bringing in women for prostitution, playing guerrilla warfare with the police; another netizen stated that there was a rented apartment on Qingdao Road in Pingdu City where mysterious men and women frequently entered and exited, and he often saw a woman standing in front of the door waving at passing men and flirting, suspected of engaging in prostitution. The officers immediately reported the situation to Captain Wang of the Public Security Brigade of the Pingdu Public Security Bureau. After carefully reading the posts, Captain Wang immediately arranged for officers to stake out the two suspicious locations mentioned by the netizens.

Divide into two teams to bust the dens of iniquity

After a day and night of surveillance, the investigating officers discovered that the two locations mentioned by the netizens indeed had prostitution activities, and the prostitutes were very cautious. To avoid alarming them prematurely, early yesterday morning, an officer changed into plain clothes and walked into this rented apartment on Qingdao Road. Seeing the uninvited guest, the boss's wife, Ms. Shang, proactively approached to chat, asking the plainclothes officer if he wanted a massage and claiming she had some pretty girls who could provide special services. After probing, the plainclothes officer learned that there was a hidden room inside where a woman was currently engaged in prostitution. The plainclothes officer immediately revealed his identity, catching the male and female engaged in prostitution red-handed. While interrogating Ms. Shang, a prostitute called her, saying she had just received a job and requested a room from Ms. Shang. The officers followed the lead and caught another pair of individuals engaged in prostitution.

At around 10 a.m. yesterday, another team of investigating officers arrived at a certain bathhouse inn in Huibu Town, monitoring the men and women entering and exiting the inn from a mobile police vehicle parked far away. At noon that day, the officers noticed that three men entered the bathhouse inn, followed shortly by a woman dressed provocatively also entering the inn. The investigating officers quickly rushed into the inn to conduct a search, catching three men and one woman in the act of prostitution as well as Ms. Li, the boss's wife who was specifically responsible for pimping.

Three men hired a prostitute in the bathhouse

According to the confessions of the client Mr. Yu, he is from Chongqing and works in Huibu Town, Pingdu City with two fellow townspeople. Due to not being able to return home for a long time, feeling lonely and unable to afford much money, the three of them discussed finding a woman to play with together. When they told this idea to Ms. Li, the owner of a certain bathhouse inn in the town, Ms. Li immediately contacted a 38-year-old woman via phone and negotiated a price of 120 RMB for them to "bundle" and take turns engaging in prostitution. Afterwards, the three agreed to implement an AA system, each contributing 40 RMB to Ms. Li. Just as the prostitute was having relations with them, the police suddenly rushed in and caught them red-handed.

(Reported by Zhao Yuxun, Lan Changyun, Zhang Dapeng)
