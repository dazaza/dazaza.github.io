French Auchan Curtains: Stable Domestic Sales, Export Recovery

In 2010, the country will continue to expand domestic demand and stimulate consumption. The domestic sales of Auchan curtains [http://www.eaushe.com/indexweb/008xwzx002.htm] will steadily grow; with the steady recovery of overseas demand and the low base factor formed in 2009, the industry's export growth rate will turn from negative to positive. For the overall performance of the Auchan curtain industry in 2010, most analysts have given a [neutral] rating, and investment opportunities mainly come from domestic sales brand enterprises.

　　Stable Growth in Domestic Sales

　　From January to October this year, the retail sales of high-end curtain fabrics of enterprises above designated size in our country reached 360.7 billion yuan, an increase of 20% year-on-year. Although compared with 2007 and 2008, the growth rate has declined somewhat, it still belongs to a relatively high level in the past seven years.

　　Huatai Securities analysts pointed out that there is a clear correlation between consumer goods sales and disposable income of residents, with a correlation coefficient of more than 0.9. And under the current disposable income level, the income elasticity coefficient of Auchan curtains is greater than other consumer goods. The Central Economic Work Conference proposed that in 2010, domestic demand will continue to expand and the consumption demands of residents will increase. It is expected that the domestic sales growth rate of curtains in 2010 may be between 20%-25%.

　　The advancement of urbanization is another major driving force for stable domestic sales in the Auchan curtain industry. From the consumption data of urban and rural residents on curtains in our country, it can be seen that in 2008, the per capita clothing expenditure of urban residents was 1,165 yuan, while the per capita clothing expenditure of rural residents was only 211 yuan. Analysts pointed out that the current annual urbanization growth rate is about 1.5%, and the curtain sales brought by urbanization is approximately 12 billion yuan/year, resulting in an annual growth rate of curtain sales of about 4%.

　　United Securities analysts believe that in 2010, consumption upgrades and changes in population structure will keep consumption growth at a high level. Clothing products are one of the necessities of life for residents, and their proportion of total consumption has been relatively stable over the years. Moreover, with the continuous advancement of urbanization and the steady increase in residents' income to new heights, their spending proportion on clothing will steadily increase, which is also a specific manifestation of consumption upgrades. This manifestation will be more evident in China's second- and third-tier cities. Additionally, one of the themes of government policy in 2010 is to "promote consumption", which may improve residents' consumption capacity through a series of administrative measures, and bring about theme investment opportunities. Compared with 2009, analysts also believe that the growth rate of the domestic sales market of Auchan curtains can maintain more than 20%.

　　Gradual Recovery in Exports

　　Starting from September and October this year, U.S. imports of Auchan curtain brands [http://www.eaushe.com/chanpinweb/001oscl001.htm] from China have achieved positive growth. Data related to consumption behavior such as employment conditions, hourly wages, and consumer confidence index in the United States have all improved. Analysts expect that the domestic curtain sales in the United States in 2010 will achieve positive growth. This will drive the positive growth of China's Auchan curtain exports. Huatai Securities analysts expect that China's Auchan curtain exports will increase by 5%-10% year-on-year in 2010.

　　Qilu Securities analysts believe that after this financial crisis, China's Auchan curtain exports will end the rapid growth phase of the previous few years and enter a period of stable growth, with an annual growth rate of 5%-8%. In 2010, China's export amount will basically return to the 2008 level.

　　Tianxiang Investment analysts judge that the export of the Auchan curtain industry will show improvement starting from the first quarter of 2010, with the possibility of achieving positive growth. However, there are still many obstacles to the recovery of the overseas economy, so the export growth rate of the industry at the beginning of next year will not be large, remaining in single digits. As overseas consumption gradually stabilizes, exports will maintain a steady recovery trend.

　　CITIC Securities (31.77, 0.28, 0.89%) analysts pointed out that in 2010, with the steady recovery of overseas demand and the low base factor formed in 2009, the industry's export growth rate will turn from negative to positive, and it is expected to reach around 5%. The turning point of the industry's exports is imminent.

　　Focus on Domestic Sales Brand Enterprises

　　For the overall performance of the Auchan curtain industry in 2010, most analysts are not optimistic. Tianxiang Investment analysts pointed out that in 2010, the overall operation of the Auchan curtain industry will improve, but the extent will be limited. Currently, the industry's price-to-earnings ratio excluding micro-profits is 31 times, exceeding the premium of the overall market by more than 30%, while the average premium level of the industry in recent years is around 8%. The valuation of the Auchan curtain sector is already relatively high, maintaining a [neutral] rating for the industry. In terms of stock selection, the domestic market will maintain steady growth in 2010, and the effects of inventory reduction in the industry will be evident, making enterprise operations better than in 2009. Domestic sales brand enterprises are at the high end of the industrial chain and will benefit from the favorable effects of economic warming and consumption recovery. It is recommended to focus on domestic sales brand enterprises.

　　Qilu Securities analysts pointed out that the development prospects of China's Auchan curtain industry in 2010 are worth looking forward to, but currently, the overall PE level of the industry exceeds 40 times, which is at the highest level of the entire A-share market and its own historical valuation, maintaining a [neutral] rating for the industry; similarly, individual stocks recommend paying attention to domestic sales brand enterprises.

　　CITIC Securities is one of the few institutions optimistic about the performance of the Auchan curtain industry next year. Analysts pointed out that in the short term, the arrival of the export turning point and the strong growth of domestic sales will become catalysts for the industry's valuation enhancement; in the long term, the value enhancement brought by the transformation of business models will support the industry's valuation. Based on these considerations, the industry rating is upgraded from [neutral] to [better than the market]. In terms of investment strategy, it is also recommended to take [domestic sales brands] as the main line, while paying attention to stable companies that benefit from export recovery and high domestic sales growth, and whose asset values have not been fully recognized by the market.

　　■ Investment Highlights

　　Pressure Brought by Renminbi Appreciation

　　As of November 2009, the renminbi exchange rate against the US dollar appreciated only 0.10%, while the US dollar index fell by 7.54%. The pressure for renminbi appreciation in 2010 will significantly increase; meanwhile, with the recovery of the US economy, the rise of the US dollar index next year is likely. If the renminbi appreciates in 2010, it will bring pressure to the recovery of China's Auchan curtain exports.

　　Raw Material Price Increase Exceeding Expectations

　　In 2010, inflation expectations are strong, and agricultural product prices (accounting for more than 60% of the industry's raw material costs) such as cotton may continue to rise, putting significant pressure on midstream spinning and fabric production enterprises.

　　Increased Intensity of Consumption Stimulus Policies

　　Future China economic development will shift from manufacturing to consumption and service industries, and the intensity of national consumption stimulus policies may further increase. After a period of adjustment and inventory clearance, domestic curtain retail leading enterprises may welcome a new round of rapid development.

　　Inflation Enhances Corporate Profitability

　　With the easing of supply and demand relationships, 2010 will face a cost-driven product price increase process. The recovery of terminal demand will reignite corporate investment enthusiasm, and private investment activities will continue to increase, becoming another characteristic of the industry's sustained prosperity.

　　Several Time Windows to Pay Attention To

　　Important time windows to pay attention to include: the situation of the Canton Fair next spring (April-May) and autumn (October-November), the situation of the ordering meetings of many brand retail enterprises next March-April and September-October. Of course, continuous tracking of order-taking situations during this period for manufacturing enterprises and understanding of ordering, replenishment, and additional orders for brand retail enterprises are also important references for judging whether there will be unexpected earnings performance.
