Devouring the Stars, Journey to the Past Nostalgic Novel - "The Flower Mummy"

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-24 15:00:14

Flowers, in fact, have souls. You must admit, there is no one in this world who still likes to discuss the word "emotion", but I am still stubbornly going to tell you this story, because I always believe it exists somewhere, such as in memories. If I hadn't met the flower soul, I would still consider myself a mummy, without flesh, without life, without passion, without hope. Of course, I am even less kind. Every person entering Shanqian has naked desire in their eyes. They attempt to kill me and then take my wealth for themselves. Of course, this is a naive thought. The ultimate destination of those ignorant mountain people who try to intrude into Shanqian is to die miserably at my hands, and then their corpses pile up in the cave where I live and slowly rot. I like this feeling, being ruthless, selfish, and cold. I admire their despair more when they are about to die, because my loneliness will slowly sink in this corrupt air.

Yes, I am a mountain king. An old mountain king. To be exact, my heart is very old. My inner self has been longing for purity. That feeling is like sitting by my mother when I was little, looking at the stars in the summer night, with fireflies accompanying, and a faint fragrance of night-blooming jasmine. Then, my mother would tell me stories about our family, those legendary stories that make people yearn.

If you give me a wish, I would hope time stops before there are any memories, everything returns to peace, and everything is like a newly born thing.

This day, I was organizing my chaotic thoughts in Shanqian when I suddenly smelled the breath of a stranger.

I laughed happily. I always laugh happily before killing someone.

Following the scent, I found the intruder, loosened my hand bones, and went forward with an evil smile on my face.

"Uh, hello, I got lost, can you take me out?"

I was stunned, looked up and met a pair of clear eyes, like a quiet spring, like a bright moon, like the stars in the summer night when I was little.

"My name is Flower Soul, can you take me out?" The other party asked carefully.

"I..." For a long time without conversation, my words were somewhat awkward and slightly hoarse. But I don't understand, if others see my appearance, they must scream and run away. Why isn't she afraid of me?

"Can I?" She persisted in asking.

"Follow me." I tried hard to express my language. I didn't know why I suddenly wanted to help her, was it because she didn't run away like others? Or was it because her clear eyes touched something inside me.

"Ah?" She seemed not to understand, because of too much silence, my voice had become terribly low. Without thinking much, I took her hand and walked towards the entrance of Shanqian.

Her hands seemed to struggle a little in my palm, but soon stopped moving.

She followed me silently for a long time, and I became curious, asking her: "You - not - afraid - of - me?"

When I asked this question, I almost bit my tongue. One, my speaking function was slow; two, I regretted saying this, making her scared wasn't what I wanted.

"No, I trust you."

"Why?"

"The bottom of your eyes, so deep loneliness, you must be a good person, just that you've been lonely for too long, right?" Her voice was very soft, but the last two words were said with great strength, as if she wasn't asking, but giving the best answer to my life.

"You, nonsense." I threw her hand off, the airflow behind me suddenly heated up, which was a sign of my anger.

"Do you know? I am also..."

She lowered her head like a child who made a mistake, her hands rubbing the edges of her clothes.

I smelled the same airflow, this airflow is called loneliness.

I couldn't bear to mock her naivety and ignorance, or her presumptuousness.

"I - have - killed - many - people."

"That's not what you want, right?"

She showed no fear, looked up and stared directly at me.

"I... I..."

"Everyone, go! Kill them and we'll get rich."

"Kill them, block the door."

"There is still one here, everyone be careful, don't let anyone escape, cut the grass and root out the roots."

"We're getting rich, ha ha..."

The corners of my mouth started to twitch violently, it was an unbearable anger.

The memory from twenty years ago replayed in my mind like a movie. I clearly remember every greedy face, every shameless laugh, every groan of my family's tragic death, every scene unbearable to look at.

My father knocked me unconscious while I was crying loudly, stuffed me into a pile of weeds. When I woke up, my home was like a bloody hell.

A big fire, although it didn't endanger my life, gave me a devilish face.

So, I vowed to kill all the people in the world.

"Killing me, can you really forget?"

Flower Soul's voice woke me up. I discovered that my hand was actually pinching her delicate neck. Her face was already losing its color.

I let go, throwing her on the ground.

She was innocent, an innocent person, a person as lonely as me. I silently repeated this in my heart, avoiding her starry eyes.

"The cool night is like water, the cold moon is like frost, loneliness is unknown by anyone. Laughing on the swing, the flower soul is like a dream, loneliness is always sorrowful."

"Take me out... Take us both out..."

Her voice was like a spell, driving away my anger.

"Go, I don't want to see you."

I pulled her up again, almost running to send her to the door.

"Get out..."

She turned around, her tender hand touching the face I considered scarred.

"Go out, you are a good person, go out, you should own sunshine."

"Huh? What's this?"

She drew back her hand, on it was something crystal clear.

After calming down, I watched her figure gradually disappear.

I promised her, as long as she left, I would leave Shanqian.

Outside Shanqian, the sky was clear, the breeze gentle, peach blossoms bloomed in patches, unrestrained and vibrant. The sunlight shone, spreading a fresh fragrance.

I returned to Shanqian because I still had unfinished business.

Someone learned that I had removed the defense of Shanqian, spread the news throughout the martial arts world, knowing that killing me would get them what they wanted. More people rushed into Shanqian, and I always smiled as I fell under their knives, then my soul watched them return with satisfied smiles.

(Because I wanted revenge, I learned the art of resurrection from a worldly high-level expert outside, which is why I always killed people but never got killed.)

I always remembered her words: Go out.

Go out...

Take your heart out...
