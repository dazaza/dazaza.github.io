Starting a business on Taobao is not a dream: Sharing my Taobao journey.

by ecduomall123 on 2012-02-24 09:16:24

This is a relatively long story, so I'd like to thank each and every one of you who take the time to read it carefully!

My Taobao online store was created on March 26, 2011, but I didn't really start working on it until October of that year. Now, my credit rating has already reached three diamonds.

In the early days of opening the store, like everyone else, I faced many difficulties. At the time, I thought about whether it would be better to act as an agent or to go to the wholesale market to buy goods myself. I think many of you have agonized over this point. Regardless, from the moment I conceived the idea of opening an online store, I firmly believed in one thing: finding good sources of goods. In the following text, I will detail this ——

I first came into contact with Taobao in 2009. I remember the first time I went on Taobao, I was captivated by the colorful, beautiful clothes and bags. That day, I spent two whole hours browsing. The first thing I bought online was a pair of Ecool Wolf jeans, which was a gift to myself. It was precisely this pair of jeans that made me think about starting my own store. By the end of 2010, I had a conflict with my company manager, leading to my forced resignation.

During that period, my health wasn't great, and I didn't want to waste my life in the office dealing with trivial matters. I also didn't want to stay at home wasting my precious youth. So, I made up my mind to open an online store and was determined to make a name for myself in this field.

But what project should I choose? If I can't find a competitive and well-positioned project, it's better not to start at all because it's bound to be a road to failure. Especially with women's clothing, there are so many crown stores now. If the product quality isn't solid and lacks uniqueness, my store will surely be lost in the vast sea of Taobao.

In February of 2011, just before the Lunar New Year when it was time to return home for the Spring Festival, I was still racking my brain over what project to do. On the day I was about to head home, I browsed online specifically to buy a pair of pants for my sister. Who knew that this seemingly casual gift would open the door to my Taobao entrepreneurial journey.

When my sister saw the pants, she exclaimed: "How much did these jeans cost?" When I told her the price, her eyes widened in disbelief: "At home, you wouldn't be able to get these pants for less than 200 yuan! What beautiful pants, and they're even branded!"

Next, my cousin and my sister's colleagues all asked me to help buy these pants.

Later, I bought and sent back more than ten pairs for them. At this point, I thought: Why not use this project for Taobao? Go directly to Duoshang.com to find manufacturers in bulk, no need to run around looking for quality sources of goods, most importantly, the prices are very low!

So, without hesitation, that day I started looking for quality manufacturers and sources of goods on Duoshang.com (www.ecduo.cn). I found that many manufacturers from the Pearl River Delta were selling directly on the platform. These prices are equivalent to factory prices. Compared to other Taobao sellers, this gives me a significant price advantage. However, I still had some doubts, so I chose a manufacturer with a good balance of various factors for a site visit.

After finding the address and transportation route of the manufacturer online, the next day, I went there with a nervous heart.

Upon arrival, I discovered that the factory was large and relatively scaled. The quality and styles of the products were basically consistent with the descriptions on Duoshang.com. At that moment, I was excited. Good quality sources of goods are something that many C-store owners dream of. This time, heaven helped me, and I finally found suitable sources of goods. After talking with the boss, she said that on Duoshang.com, I could place orders directly for goods, saving me from running around everywhere. The quality of these sources, as you've seen, is definitely reliable!

After returning, I immediately selected some clothes that I thought had good styles and reasonable prices to decorate my store and modify the item pictures. But at the beginning, the results were far from satisfactory—either too dark or too bright, nothing was usable. My decorating skills and PS skills were poor. I was anxious like an ant on a hot pot, ignoring the pain of my gastritis, staying up night after night.

No matter how pretty my clothes were, if the pictures weren't nice, the hard work of finding those sources of goods would become a pile of wasted fabric. I realized I must calm down and find a solution. I slowly searched online for PS techniques, store decoration techniques, and learned simple PS techniques such as cutting out images and adding watermarks. After nearly half a month of exploration, my store and pictures became passable.

On May 1st, I finally welcomed my first order! That feeling of excitement is something only those who have worked hard and paid their dues can truly understand.
