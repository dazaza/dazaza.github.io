2: The quality of backlinks far outweighs quantity. We often see websites in the same industry where those with fewer backlinks rank better than those with more. This shows that the quality of backlinks is much more important than quantity. One high-quality backlink is more effective than dozens or even hundreds of spammy backlinks. Search engines frequently update website rankings, each update deleting some links that the search engine deems useless, which is why when we check our website's backlinks, we find that many have disappeared.

5: Valuable content sometimes is the root of high-quality backlinks. Links cannot produce content for a website, but valuable content can bring high-quality backlinks to a website. If a website can provide very valuable content to users, satisfying user needs, and other webmasters cannot offer enough content on their own sites, they will naturally link to your site if they believe it can solve users' needs and satisfy them.

4: Links should be broad but not excessive, and should prioritize relevance. When building links, whether the content is relevant is one of the most important considerations. If external links can relate to the website's theme, that is best. Sometimes, a page may not be related to the entire site's theme, but if the page's theme is related to the linked page, it carries more weight than unrelated links. For example, if a restaurant says how good our company's website construction is, it may not be entirely credible; however, if it comes from a peer or a design company, the credibility is much higher.

Links are one of the essential characteristics of the internet and also its core. A link refers to the connection relationship from one webpage to a target. The target can be another webpage, different positions on the same webpage, images, email addresses, files, or even applications.

1: Website backlinks should also follow a natural logic. Search engines tend to focus on natural patterns. The establishment of backlinks should avoid sudden surges; obvious over-optimization is very likely to result in penalties from search engines. Once penalized, all previous efforts become wasted. Some people increase a large number of backlinks within a short period and then stop, which is harmful to website optimization. Even if the website is not penalized by the search engine, the keyword ranking will fluctuate. Building backlinks requires steady growth over time. Instead of adding hundreds of backlinks in a month and then doing nothing afterward, it would be better to distribute this time and effort over several months, gradually and steadily increasing, persistently.
