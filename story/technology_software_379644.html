InstaFetch – Sync your Android phone with InstaPaper [Android]

by xdash on 2011-01-24 10:25:20

InstaFetch is an application that runs on Android smartphones, allowing Android users to access Instapaper, the most popular offline reading tool available on iOS.

As everyone knows, Instapaper is a highly sought-after reading tool on iOS. Not only does it save web pages as bookmarks, but it also captures and stores the original content of these pages for later offline reading. Moreover, its reading experience is quite excellent. Instapaper has gained strong popularity among information workers and self-learning enthusiasts. (Similar services recommendation: Read It Later)

Unfortunately, currently, Instapaper can only run on iPhone and iPad mobile devices, with no official Android application available yet. Thankfully, through InstaFetch, Android users can also achieve synchronization between their phones and Instapaper.

Key features of InstaFetch:

- Directly save content from your Android phone to Instapaper;

- Sync with your Instapaper account and download the complete saved content to your phone;

- Convert web page content into a left-right flipping format, adjust font size, and improve the reading experience;

The premium paid version will also include the following features:

- Support for Instapaper folders;

- Full-screen reading;

- Automatically record pages that have been read;

- Night mode (brightness text display on a dark background);

- Add star ratings;

- Sync with Evernote;

- Support for Wifi-only under automatic sync mode;

- Article reading priority;

If you are an Android phone user who loves synchronized reading, this application is highly recommended. Wishing you a pleasant reading experience.
