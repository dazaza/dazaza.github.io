Otherwise, making empty promises could lead to trouble if they cannot fulfill them (some reckless men often say "I love you" without caring whether they can live up to it).

Because they seek positive reinforcement, repeatedly asking the question becomes necessary for them. Of course, when a man hesitates or refuses to answer, women usually feel hurt and assume that the other party does not love them.

However, let's flip the perspective: why must women force their boyfriends to blurt out "I love you" without much thought? Isn't observing his actions far more reliable?
