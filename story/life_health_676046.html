Some practical methods of moxibustion for health preservation

by nyguagnyi on 2012-02-25 16:46:46

In modern society, people are paying more and more attention to their health. Among the recently popular health-preserving methods, moxibustion should be quite practical. When we use the moxibustion health-preserving formula with Nanyang mugwort sticks, we can choose the best option according to the condition of the disease, human physique, and the excess or deficiency of the functions of the internal organs, following the principle of correcting bias and rescuing flaws. Here are some examples of formulas:

① Respiratory System Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Fengmen (BL12), Shenzhu (GV12), Feishu (BL13), Zusanli (ST36).

② Cardiovascular System Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Hypertension: Fengmen (BL12), Quchi (LI11), Zusanli (ST36), Yanglingquan (GB34).

Coronary Heart Disease: Shenzhu (GV12), Ximen (PC7), Sanyinjiao (SP6), or also moxibustion on Zhongwan (CV12).

③ Digestive System Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Pishu (BL20), Weishu (BL21), Zhongwan (CV12), Zusanli (ST36), Yanglingquan (GB34).

④ Nervous System Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Dazhui (GV14), Shenzhu (GV12), Shenshu (BL23), Zusanli (ST36).

⑤ Urinary and Reproductive System Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Shenshu (BL23), Guanyuan (CV4), Sanyinjiao (SP6), Zusanli (ST36).

⑥ Central Qi-Supplementing Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Baihui (GV20), Zhongwan (CV12), Qihai (CV6), Zusanli (ST36), Sanyinjiao (SP6), Taiyuan (LU9).

Supplement central qi, promote blood circulation, unblock meridians, supplement central qi, lift yang, strengthen spleen and harmonize stomach, warm the middle and dispel cold, tonify both spleen and kidney, diuretic and remove dampness, regulate ying and wei.

This can be used as a basic moxibustion formula for sub-health moxibustion health care, and can also treat qi deficiency and blood stasis caused by stroke, stomach pain, abdominal distension, diarrhea, shortness of breath, fatigue, weakness in waist and legs, edema, etc.

⑦ Five-Organ-Supplementing Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

Feishu (BL13), Xinshu (BL15), Geshu (BL17), Ganshu (BL18), Pishu (BL20), Shenshu (BL23).

Tonify five organs, regulate qi and blood, calm spirit, unblock meridians.

These are bladder meridian acupoints on the back, which can be used for strengthening the body, preventing sub-health, and treating physical weakness. It can also treat palpitations, shortness of breath, lower back soreness, fatigue, post-stroke syndrome, and all kinds of chronic diseases and injuries.

⑧ General Strengthening Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula:

This method has been popular since ancient times. As a regular healthy moxibustion, you can take Zusanli (ST36), Zhongwan (CV12), Guanyuan (CV4), or just moxibustion on Zusanli (ST36), which also has a good fitness effect. The implementation of fitness moxibustion takes only ten minutes each time, not more than 30 minutes, without much pain, and is economical and saving. Long-term adherence to moxibustion can improve physical fitness, enhance health, and its effect is absolutely no less than taking ointment, making it a highly recommended health preservation method.

⑨ Solely Guanyuan Moxibustion Health-Preserving Formula using regular moxibustion sticks or smokeless moxibustion sticks:

Guanyuan. Supplement primary qi, warm kidney and strengthen spleen, tonify kidney and prevent slipping, restore yang and rescue reverse, warm the middle and disperse cold, regulate qi and relieve pain, tonify kidney and absorb qi, warm yang and promote urination, tonify blood and adjust menstruation, warm the lower abdomen, tonify deficiency and purge excess, prolong life and preserve health.

It can be used for urinary and reproductive system diseases such as impotence, premature ejaculation, nocturnal emission, enuresis, difficulty in urination, frequent urination, menstrual disorders, amenorrhea, dysmenorrhea, metrorrhagia and leucorrhea, postpartum bleeding and other common gynecological diseases, gastrointestinal system diseases such as abdominal distension and pain, diarrhea, prolapse of anus, constipation, stroke and all kinds of chronic diseases and injuries. Guanyuan is one of the important tonifying points in the human body. Women can combine it with moxibustion at Sanyinjiao (SP6), and men can combine it with moxibustion at Zusanli (ST36).

Guanyuan is as important as Zusanli as a commonly used health-preserving point. In ancient times, there was a saying that "moxibustion at Qihai in spring and Guanyuan in autumn will produce saliva". If after typhoid fever, or long-term cough in middle age does not stop, it may lead to asthenia, moxibustion at Guanyuan 300 times. For people over middle age, if they have joint pain in the waist and legs, it is due to kidney qi deficiency and wind evil invasion, moxibustion at Guanyuan 300 times. Elderly people who suffer from shortness of breath, it is due to kidney deficiency and qi not returning to the sea, moxibustion at Guanyuan 200 times. According to "Leijing Tuyi", "Guanyuan treats various deficiencies and damages... But for cold accumulation and deficiency, moxibustion is suitable, up to more than a thousand times, and at least two or three hundred times. Many lives have been saved. However, repeated moxibustion for deficiency, combined with Zusanli below, hence it is said: 'If you want your Dan Tian to be safe, Zusanli should never dry'." Dr. Dou Cai of the Song Dynasty said, "...Deficiency of true qi leads to disease, and loss of true qi leads to death. The method to save life is moxibustion first, dan medicine second, and Fu Zi third. People over thirty years old can moxibustion 300 times under the navel every three years; people over fifty can moxibustion 500 times under the navel every two years; people over sixty can moxibustion 300 times under the navel every year; this makes people live long and stay young. I often moxibustion Guanyuan 500 times... thus obtaining health in old age." From this, we can see that Guanyuan not only treats various deficiencies and damages, but also preserves health and prolongs life.
