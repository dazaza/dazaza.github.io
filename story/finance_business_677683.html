Man swallowed a nail clipper to show how hearty he was in drinking

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-27 09:05:32

It took the doctor 70 minutes to remove a nail clipper about 8 cm in length and 2 cm in width. The Weibo post of top ten beautiful lakes in domestic universities was well received by netizens. Reported by journalist Hou Tie with photos. At around 11:30 yesterday morning, in the endoscopy room of Dalian Central Hospital, Director Dr. Li Jinghua first used a lasso to loop around the nail clipper, then slowly pulled it upwards, trying to drag it out through the esophagus. However, when the nail clipper reached the first narrowing point of the esophagus, it got stuck and fell off from the lasso. The doctor had to start again. After trying six or seven times, they had no choice but to abandon this method. The doctor then used a stone retrieval net basket. This tool is similar to a whisk, consisting of four metal wires, making it more convenient for fixation. Sure enough, Dr. Li Jinghua quickly fixed the nail clipper with the stone retrieval net basket and continued to pull it up. However, when the nail clipper was pulled to the first narrowing point of the esophagus, it fell off again. After seven attempts, the nurses helped Dr. Li Jinghua make the patient tilt his head back as much as possible, increasing the angle near the narrowing point of the esophagus. In the end, it took the doctor and nurses 70 minutes to remove the nail clipper which was about 8 cm long and 2 cm wide. "Such a situation is actually very dangerous. If it cannot be timely removed through the esophagus, then an open surgery would be required to cut open the stomach to remove the nail clipper." Dr. Li Jinghua introduced that fortunately it was successfully removed in time, and it would not cause too much adverse effects on the patient for now. Reported by journalist Hou Tie. During a friend gathering, a man made the other person drink two glasses of alcohol as a bet, and astonishingly swallowed a nail clipper. Two days later, after the doctors' efforts, it took 70 minutes to successfully remove the nail clipper from his stomach. Originally, 44-year-old Li Feng (pseudonym) drank alcohol with a good friend two days ago. During the meal, both of them drank quite a lot. Regardless of how long it has been, I still remember... They started betting to persuade each other to drink more while they were in high spirits. Li Feng's friend picked up a nail clipper and said to Li Feng, "If you can swallow this thing, I will drink two glasses!" When Li Feng heard this, he didn't think too much and actually picked up the nail clipper, put it in his mouth, and swallowed it. Afterwards, the two of them didn't pay much attention and did not seek medical attention immediately. On the afternoon of the previous day, Li Feng felt unwell and rushed to the hospital. At this point, the doctor learned that he had already eaten lunch and could not perform a gastroscopy. Unfortunately, they could only advise him to closely observe his condition and wait until the next day when his stomach was empty to carry out the removal surgery.
