The Mizuno MX-560 Driver has a unique geometric 460cc head-shape solution that delivers extreme forgiveness without sacrificing distance. Mizuno discovered that the geometry of previous square and geometrically influenced drivers sacrificed distance in their quest for maximum MOI (moment of inertia) for extreme forgiveness while maintaining a low spin rate and high launch angle for an ideal trajectory. A study of geometry proved that an optimal design could both maximize up/down and toe/heel MOI without a reduction in ball speeds and driving distance.

The MX-560 has a re-engineered 'bulge and roll' design to the face, which improves consistency from off-center strikes. The MX 560 can also be fitted with a Fujikura 360 shaft, which looks great and feels like it would make this club explode at impact.

A Power Bar construction, reminiscent of Mizuno’s original T-ZOID design, reinforces the crown and provides a distinct alignment aid. A CNC-milled, plasma-welded Cortech design utilizes multiple face thicknesses for extreme distance.
