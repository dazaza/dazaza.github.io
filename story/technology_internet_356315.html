Try not to use detergents containing bleaching agents;

Avoid soaking the T-shirt for a long time;

Try not to wash with a washing machine. If using a washing machine, turn the pattern side inside out;

After washing, it's best not to spin dry with a washing machine, but let it air dry naturally.

T-shirt design differs from other forms of flat design and has its own uniqueness. Patterns have life, lively jumping on the T-shirts, looking spirited. However, due to the limitations of the printing process, versioned T-shirt patterns also have their different characteristics.

1. The design style should mainly be simple, able to directly reflect the design intention of the versioned T-shirt.

2. Patterns are preferably represented by color blocks. Unlike paper, T-shirts have textures on their surfaces, so overly detailed details may not print well; if the pattern area is too large, it will affect the breathability of the entire garment, making it feel a bit greasy when worn.

3. A pattern with several colors means several versions. If there are patterns on both the front and back, then the number of versions for both patterns must be added together for calculation; the more versions, the higher the price. This is something that price-sensitive friends need to pay attention to.

4. Generally, screen printing is used for versioned T-shirt printing. Therefore, when designing patterns, printing standards should be followed. If Photoshop is used (it is recommended to use the CS version), it must be adjusted to 300 pixels (dpi), at a 1:1 original size (the size of the pattern on the T-shirt is exactly the same as the design size); there are no restrictions for vector software.

Choosing the right base shirt:

Simply put, a base shirt is a blank cultural shirt. There is a price difference depending on the quality, ranging from 10 yuan to over 30 yuan; choose according to your actual needs.
