Abstract: With the continuous development of society, the material and cultural life of the nation has undergone tremendous changes. Many urban residents have entered a moderately prosperous life, and they have also put forward corresponding environmental quality requirements for their lives, hoping to live in an environment closely connected with nature. Beautiful landscapes and residential area environmental designs need to develop certain artistic expressions, thereby conveying high standards of residential living environments.

Keywords: Community; Landscape Environment, Design

Residential landscape design and environmental design are extensions of material life. They are not simply about implementing spatial environmental settings from aesthetics and functionality, but also require integrating design intentions and promoting the formation of landscape elements. For example, through unique social and cultural trends, or highlighting the real essence of the residential area itself and its surrounding environment. The use of advanced design intentions adds picturesque scenery, enhancing the living environment. Through each residential neighborhood, the landscape has become an attractive, distinctive, and iconic carrier.

The method of landscape design is no longer like it was in the past, where one would wait for the main design construction to be completed before adding and repairing the environment. Instead, the planning and design of the environment are integrated into the entire process of residential design to ensure major planning and construction coordination, combining natural terrain and natural resources as much as possible to make overall planning and fully reflect the design concept. Residential area landscape design should not be limited to one type of style but should be based on different design ideas and specific decisions, ultimately achieving good visual effects. Landscape design expands lines and shapes and smoothly integrates into the environment using lively and flexible methods, improving and expanding existing garden design techniques.

In the design of community landscapes, the concept of "people-oriented" should be established, making the meaning of establishing this concept clear. This makes the transition from simple aggregation of greenery and facilities in residential environment design completely meet the needs of residents at different levels. The idea of being people-oriented has already catered to the delicate emotional needs of people (such as various garden landscape small items), with human-centered design further introducing the profound connotations of successful living space design for relevant individuals. Integrating ecological design into environmental design transforms the residential environment into part of the city's ecosystem, creating a residential environment that moves from single-function space planning to systematic design planning, resulting in a man-made natural environment, a green-living area.

For instance, not only does it require a high green coverage rate, but it also considers scientifically reasonable characteristics of plants, trees, shrubs, flowers, and vegetation; the water environment in residential areas needs to consider water circulation and utilization. As lifestyles and income levels improve, the practices of residents' housing have fundamentally changed, especially during leisure time after work. Therefore, there are more residential areas offering various fitness opportunities, conversations, meetings, and other activities. Thus, living needs are more reflected in various landscape elements such as garden landscape small items and fitness equipment, which are not only used in daily habits but also require a certain cultural aspect to enrich citizens' lifestyles during their leisure time.

Attention should be paid to dynamic landscape composition and visually pleasing results in static landscape design, rather than just focusing on linear tracking of landscape elements in most cases. The main axis line in community landscapes often forms a continuous, dynamic landscape sequence, such as green and blue axes of different types. Various factors lead to different landscape spaces, providing people with sufficient space to feel and experience the atmosphere, creating a cultural living ambiance and enhancing its signature effect. Interactive residential environment planning aims not only to satisfy people's visual aesthetic effects but ultimately serves the purpose of resident usage. Resident interaction with natural and human-made residential environments allows residents to participate in integrating human-nature interactive spaces into their homes.

Two aspects of residential environments—appreciation and functionality—should form a landscape that is both appreciable and functional, with green grass embellishing an open layout. Small gatherings and layouts are more conducive to promoting interpersonal relationships, allowing residents to easily integrate into nature, enabling each member to enjoy beautiful natural scenery and fresh air, fostering harmonious neighborhoods.
