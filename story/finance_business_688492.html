According to the report by Yangcheng Evening News (Weibo), on the morning of the 20th, at Shiling Park in Buji, Longgang, Shenzhen, a 65-year-old grandfather accidentally rolled down while descending the mountain, breaking his leg. Four teenagers were prosecuted for killing a 16-year-old boy due to emotional issues.

Hanshufang Novel Network www.hanshufang.com has more chapters with faster updates. In urban novels, over 20 kind-hearted people voluntarily participated in the rescue and assisted the 120 emergency personnel in carrying the elderly man down the mountain to the hospital for treatment.

When the reporter climbed to the top of the park mountain after receiving a tip-off from readers, the elderly man had already been sent to the hospital for treatment. Aunt Li, who participated in the rescue, said that after this white-haired elder finished his morning exercise and was descending from the top of the mountain along the stone path steps, when he reached the middle position, there was a garbage bag on the steps. The bag was wet with water, making it very slippery. The elder didn't notice and stepped on the garbage bag, causing him to slip and roll down.

Aunt Li introduced that upon seeing the elder fall, many passersby began shouting for help. Hearing the cries for help, those dancing halfway up the slope, practicing swordsmanship at the waist level, and climbing the mountain all rushed over. There were about 20 people, including white-haired elders, young men in their thirties, and children over ten years old. The crowd carried the elder to the rest pavilion on the mountaintop. Some called 110 for police assistance, while others, noticing the blood still flowing from the elder's foot, used towels to bandage the wound. Worried about the elder's head lying directly on the ground, one young man surnamed Lei let the elder rest his head on his own legs. Someone even tried calling the elder’s son's phone number.

Due to the elder's son and daughter-in-law working night shifts and having their phones turned off, they then called the management office of the elder's residential complex, asking them to help contact other family members.

About 20 minutes later, the nearby Zhonghai Hospital's 120 ambulance arrived, and the kind-hearted passersby once again helped the 120 medical staff carry the elder onto the stretcher and down the mountain. They only dispersed after seeing the elder smoothly loaded onto the 120 ambulance.

The reporter subsequently went to the hospital and met the injured elder, Mr. Fan. By then, his family had also received the news and arrived at the hospital to take care of him.

The reporter learned that the elder is from Hubei Province and is 65 years old. According to the doctor, the elder suffered a comminuted fracture of the right ankle and needed immediate surgery.

Mr. Fan introduced that he fell and rolled after stepping on a garbage bag, injuring himself. At the time, many passersby gathered around to help. These good-hearted people assisted the doctors in carrying him down the mountain without even leaving their names before leaving. From the bottom of his heart, he was grateful: "I wish them well for their kindness!" The reporter noticed that as he said this, the elder wiped away tears from his eyes.
