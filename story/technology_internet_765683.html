College student falls in love with a WeChat 'girlfriend', only to find out a year later that she is actually a man

by anonymous on 2013-08-16 18:42:47

Xiao Chen, a university student in Hangzhou, had been in a nearly year-long relationship with his girlfriend. They maintained their relationship through phone calls and WeChat and had never met in person. His girlfriend sounded gentle on the phone. Entrusted by his girlfriend, Xiao Chen took care of her cousin. He could never have imagined that his girlfriend and her cousin were actually the same person!

Meeting the "goddess" on WeChat

Last September, Xiao Chen, a senior year male student, added a netizen named "Yoki" on WeChat, and they often chatted enthusiastically. "Yoki" said her real name was "Guo Jiajing", a senior year female student majoring in Economics and Management at a university in Ningbo. She was originally from Wen Cheng and lived in the urban area of Wenzhou city. Her parents resided abroad for a long time.

After Xiao Chen's request, the other party sent him a picture. Xiao Chen found the girl in the photo fresh and lovable, completely fitting the image of his ideal goddess. Having never been in love before, Xiao Chen believed this was a fated encounter.

After confirming their lover relationship, they often talked on the phone for hours. Xiao Chen thought his girlfriend's voice was somewhat neutral but sounded very gentle. Xiao Chen felt it wasn't easy for his girlfriend to study alone outside, so he complied with all her requests.

Girlfriend asked him to take care of her cousin

The girlfriend said she had a cousin named Bai Mou studying high-level review courses in Hangzhou. "My younger brother is not used to living in the school dormitory and wants to move out. However, the school requires a guardian or adult relative to come and provide proof." The girlfriend suggested that Xiao Chen pretend to be Bai Mou's older brother to help provide the proof.

Xiao Chen met Bai Mou and went to the school to get the proof. Afterwards, the girlfriend asked him to help take care of Bai Mou. As the person being taken care of, Bai Mou and Xiao Chen met seven or eight times, even eating together and watching movies.

Once, the girlfriend called and said she was in Wenzhou, her grandmother had passed away, and she needed to go to Wuxi, Jiangsu, wanting Xiao Chen to help cover the travel expenses. Xiao Chen immediately prepared to transfer money to her, but the girlfriend said her card was lost and she only had a Bank of Industry and Commerce card under her cousin Bai Mou's name. Thus, Xiao Chen transferred 340 yuan to Bai Mou's card.

In nearly a year's time, the girlfriend repeatedly asked Xiao Chen for money under various reasons such as buying skincare products, clothes, living expenses, and phone recharge fees. To please his girlfriend, Xiao Chen sent money dozens of times to Bai Mou's card, totaling 19,000 yuan.

"Guo Jiajing" did not exist

It wasn't until this summer, after graduating and returning to his hometown of Shuitou Town, Pingyang County to work, that Xiao Chen mentioned his girlfriend to his friends and was reminded that he must have been scammed.

So Xiao Chen ran to the school where Bai Mou was located in Hangzhou and obtained Bai Mou's father's phone number from the teacher. On the phone, Bai Mou's father said he didn't know Guo Jiajing, and there was no such person among his relatives. Bai Mou was his only son.

Realizing he had been deceived, Xiao Chen reported the incident to the Shuitou Town police station.

On August 11th, Bai Mou was arrested. It was understood that Bai Mou, 20 years old, was from Wen Cheng.

When asked why he pretended to be a girl, Bai Mou confessed that he was introverted and also interested in feminine things, enjoying painting his nails, and his behavior and gestures were "a bit girly". He said he had homosexual tendencies. Initially, he just pretended to be a girl for fun, but later discovered that Xiao Chen was very generous towards him, so he began intentionally scamming for money.

Currently, Bai Mou is suspected of fraud and has been criminally detained by the Pingyang County Public Security Bureau.
