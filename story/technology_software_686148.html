Report from Dongguan (reporter Lüpu photography report) -- Recently, on a fully loaded passenger bus traveling from Huaiji to Dongguan Central Station, the driver performed a "stunt" on the highway. Instead of tightly gripping the steering wheel, both hands were busy cracking sunflower seeds, leaving a mess of sunflower seed shells around the driver's seat. This scene was just captured by a passenger with a mobile phone video.

The passenger who provided this video segment, named Alight, told reporters that this trip started at 11:30 AM on November 26 from Huaiji, with the final stop being Dongguan Bus Terminal. After getting on the highway, the driver began cracking sunflower seeds, continuing until reaching Sanshui where he stopped due to seeing police at the toll station.

The fleet manager stated that the fleet supervisor was currently unavailable, but with the video and license plate number, the matter would be easy to handle. Such drivers who disregard passenger safety will certainly be dealt with severely.

Highest speed reached 110 kilometers per hour?

Upon hearing about the bus driver cracking sunflower seeds while driving, the management personnel of the 807 inspection team of the fleet also expressed surprise. He said, "Such leisurely behavior as cracking sunflower seeds is something I'm hearing about for the first time."

Recently, a shocking scene occurred on a bus carrying more than 40 people traveling from Huaiji to Dongguan Central Station: the driver remained "calm" while driving over 100 km/h and continued to crack sunflower seeds.

According to the license plate number provided by Aiming, the investigation confirmed that the bus belonged to the Dongguan City Automobile Transportation Co., Ltd.'s 807 fleet. A management staff member surnamed Li from the 807 fleet inspection team said that according to company regulations, the bus speed under normal circumstances should not exceed 100 kilometers per hour. The exact speed afterward would need to be checked through the onboard speed measurement device.
