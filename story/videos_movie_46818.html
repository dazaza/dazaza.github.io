The annual most popular domestic sitcom in the mainland "I Have a Family"

"Home with Kids" starts from the realities of China, with启发式教育as the main line, and the family platform it builds is more tolerant. It not only has the common characteristics of tens of millions of ordinary families, but also has the unique personality characteristics of reconstituted families. "Home with Kids" is a situational comedy that uses a reconstituted family as a story platform and focuses on adolescent education topics. Single father Xia Donghai returned to China with his 7-year-old son Xia Yu from the United States, and reunited with his daughter Xia Xue who grew up in China. Later, he married head nurse Liu Mei, who was also divorced and had a son named Liu Xing. The five people living under the same roof inevitably have arguments and conflicts due to their different personalities and habits, not only between children and parents, but also among the children themselves and between father and mother. In addition, the frequent involvement of their grandfather, grandmother, biological father and biological mother adds more fun and interesting stories. Besides making people laugh, the plot also reflects the warmth, harmony, care and wisdom shown in resolving conflicts in the family, and has knowledgeability and educational significance. The whole drama advocates scientific and enlightening educational methods for minors.

Since its premiere, "Home with Kids" has been deeply loved by a wide audience. During winter and summer vacation seasons, the ratings have remained high. All TV stations have broadcast it successively and received widespread praise. It has been hailed by the media as the Chinese version of "Growing Pains". The first two parts of "Home with Kids" (each part has 100 episodes) have gained excellent reputation among teenagers and parents. Many families even regard watching this drama together as a way to enhance emotions and learn communication.
