About the Human-Centered Design Salon

The Human-Centered Design Salon will revolve around Web products and operations, and we aim to build it into a top-tier Web product forum in China. Attendees can release new sites or features, explain the application of a technical solution, share their product design or operational experience, or even seek answers to certain questions.

Invited Guests:

Du Mu Heng - Product Manager at Tencent

Worked for Apple for 6 years in Worldwide Developer Relations, Asia Education & Strategy Development departments, and as an independent consultant responsible for the joint project on social responsibility education between Apple and Foxconn. He has unique insights into Apple's ideology and product design philosophy. He has given speeches on product design in various public activities and shared his product design experience with companies including Tencent, Sohu, and Douban. Currently focused on product design work for iOS/Android platforms.

Guo Jiayue - Founder of Mamamiya Website

An ordinary person who had the good fortune to be the founder of Meikong.com and now runs Mamamiya website.

Gu Ying - Poster Fashion Network

Currently employed by Poster Fashion Network. Bridging fashion and the internet, focusing on fashion, internet products, and e-commerce. Previously worked as a director at a fashion TV channel, client product project manager, and portal site video program product manager. Graduated from the Chinese University of Hong Kong with a master’s degree in Global Communication.

Xu Wei - Product Manager at Google

Enthusiast of travel, photography, marathons, and electronic toys. The first product manager at Google China. Responsible for the development of several products such as Hot List/ Input Method/ Google金山词霸/iGoogle/ Mobile Maps. Currently working on the R&D of e-commerce related products.

Wang Shoukun - Chief Scientist at Douban

Graduated from Tsinghua University with a bachelor’s and master’s degree in automation engineering. Currently serving as the chief scientist at Douban. Focused on internet information architecture and algorithms, personalized recommendation systems, web2.0 network model innovation, digital music, and online listening applications.

Wang Junyu - Co-founder of豌豆实验室 (Pea Lab)

Graduated high school as the top scorer of Guangdong Province that year. Graduated from Peking University's "Yuanpei Plan" experimental class with a bachelor’s degree in physics. Previously worked in the user experience team at Google, participating in the design and development of localized products. In early 2010, left Google to join the founding team of Pea Lab as a product manager to create Wandoujia Phone Spirit, aiming to serve emerging smartphone users in China with simple, easy-to-use, yet powerful products.

Yang Yuancheng - Co-founder and CPO of Jiepang Network

Born in 1986 in Yongchuan, Chongqing. Master’s degree in software engineering from Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Joined Hai Nei Network and Fanfou Network in 2007 as a product manager. Joined Baidu in late 2008 for user experience research. Co-founded Jiepang Network in February 2010.
