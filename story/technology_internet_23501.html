Yahoo Assistant hosts "Make Search Love" campaign

by yinyin on 2007-06-27 21:42:02

Reach out your hand and give yourself a chance to show your love to underprivileged children! Starting June 27, 2007, if you use the Yahoo Assistant Toolbar to search, you can donate to a child in an underprivileged area and participate in a field education program.

Link: http://cn.zs.yahoo.com/active/0705/index.htm

Brief introduction of the event:

Childhood is not only a beautiful and pure memory for everyone, but also the starting point of all our hopes and fantasies in life. However, in some poor areas, we see dilapidated school buildings, simple teaching facilities, dim libraries, where students in the entire six years of primary school life have not seen a single book, in their childhood, no "Pipilu", "Snow White", "Thumbelina", and "The Emperor's New Clothes". Think of how terrible and gray a childhood without imagination is.

Now, you can do your part by searching with the Yahoo Assistant Toolbar.

Yahoo Assistant "Let Search full of Love" activity, we will donate books to the poor primary schools in the areas with the highest points in the activity, solve the problem that some primary school students do not have extracurricular reading materials, so that more children go to swim in the beautiful fairy tale world, understand that in this world, far from poverty, there is a more beautiful place, that is, the dream of hope!

C 'mon! We're together! Make search full of love!

Activity Time:

June 27, 2007 - July 27, 2007 Assistant Search Charity event

September 2007 field action

Steps to participate:

1. By searching in the search box of the Yahoo Assistant Toolbar, each search will add 10 points to the donor of your choice. Finally, we will donate to the children in the areas with the highest points! On the event homepage, you can find your current points and total points for each region, and the details page shows the points of the top five search users in each region.

2. When the voting phase of the activity is over, we will set up book shelves for 20 schools in the regions with the highest scores and provide 10,000 books. We will invite the top 5 users who have contributed the most points to the region in the search for Love to walk into the mountain, bring the love of all users who have participated in this activity, and personally pass this warm love to the hands of children!

Note: ◆ In order to ensure the accuracy of your points, it is recommended that you check the user name and points on the event homepage before searching. If there is no user name and points displayed, please click "Join Love Search now" to participate in the activity. ◆ One Email can only be credited for one region. ◆ The ranking of the same integral user is the first user to reach the integral.
