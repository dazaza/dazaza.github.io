Tweegee: web2.0 portal for kids

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-10 08:56:31

In the web2.0 era, the most neglected user group is children. And includes children between the ages of 7 and 15. This part of the population is directly ignored by web2.0. Usually what we think of as Web 2.0 product users, more are office families, at least high school and college students. In this regard, perhaps we should learn from foreign service providers. Tweegee is a web2.0 portal. The site is targeted at children aged 7-15. Which provides them with a complete global Internet resource to create and share fun as a website theme. With Tweegee, kids can take full control of their online activities, build their own complete personal websites, play rich multiplayer games via IM, email and groups, read the news most relevant to them, write their own articles and do more. imageTweegee believes that children should be properly guided online so that they can experience the "real Internet". Children in this high-tech age should receive early education and awareness of the Internet, especially the concepts of Web 2.0, in order to guide them to adopt appropriate online behaviors and freely explore the World Wide Web in the future. Therefore, Tweegee does its best to give children the right tools to guide and educate them about different Internet applications, while protecting their privacy and keeping them safe. Instead of thinking purely about bringing fun to the children. Tweegee is a unique web2.0 service that encourages you to discover, explore and experience Tweegee with your children!
