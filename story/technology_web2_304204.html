Round 16 of the Italian Serie A match records: AC Milan has only one home loss, Inter Milan has a small advantage.

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-12 18:39:51

Round 16 of the Italian Serie A: AC Milan only lost once at home, Inter Milan has a small advantage. According to Sina Sports, at 12:00 AM Beijing Time on December 13, the 16th round of the 2009-2010 season of the Italian Serie A will be held. This weekend, the defending champion Inter Milan will play away against Atalanta. After securing their place in the Champions League group stage in the middle of the week, the Nerazzurri have shifted their focus back to the domestic league. Although they lost in the Italian Derby and the point gap was narrowed, it is difficult to surpass the Nerazzurri's advantage. AC Milan will host an inconsistent Palermo at home. If the Rossoneri can start with a dream beginning again, they should be able to win consecutively. Additionally, Juventus will play against mid-table team Bari away, Fiorentina will play against relegation-threatened Chievo away, and Roma will also play against Sampdoria, who were very offensive at the beginning of the season, away.

Below are the specific historical head-to-head records for each match (home team listed first):

Atalanta vs Inter Milan:

The two teams have met 98 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Atalanta winning 19, drawing 23, and losing 56, scoring 89 goals and conceding 176; among which, at home, Atalanta has won 12, drawn 16, and lost 21, scoring 52 goals and conceding 81.

AC Milan vs Palermo:

The two teams have only met 44 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with AC Milan winning 27, drawing 9, and losing 8, scoring 77 goals and conceding 29; among which, at home, AC Milan has won 19, drawn 2, and lost 1, scoring 56 goals and conceding 13.

Bari vs Juventus:

The two teams have only met 56 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Bari winning 5, drawing 16, and losing 35, scoring 97 goals and conceding 122; among which, at home, Bari has won 5, drawn 11, and lost 12, scoring 25 goals and conceding 43.

Chievo vs Fiorentina:

The two teams have only met 10 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Chievo winning 1, drawing 1, and losing 8, scoring 7 goals and conceding 16; among which, at home, Chievo has drawn 1 and lost 4 without any victories, scoring 3 goals and conceding 9.

Sampdoria vs Roma:

The two teams have met 104 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Sampdoria winning 32, drawing 30, and losing 42, scoring 107 goals and conceding 136; among which, at home, Sampdoria has won 25, drawn 19, and lost 8, scoring 67 goals and conceding 40.

Lazio vs Genoa:

The two teams have met 78 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Lazio winning 28, drawing 23, and losing 27, scoring 117 goals and conceding 110; among which, at home, Lazio has won 20, drawn 16, and lost 3, scoring 70 goals and conceding 29.

Parma vs Bologna:

The two teams have only met 20 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Parma winning 6, drawing 7, and losing 7, scoring 21 goals and conceding 21; among which, at home, Parma has won 3, drawn 5, and lost 2, scoring 10 goals and conceding 8.

Cagliari vs Napoli:

The two teams have only met 48 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Cagliari winning 13, drawing 19, and losing 16, scoring 37 goals and conceding 46; among which, at home, Cagliari has won 9, drawn 12, and lost 3, scoring 22 goals and conceding 14.

Siena vs Udinese:

The two teams have only met 12 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Siena winning 2, drawing 4, and losing 6, scoring 13 goals and conceding 20; among which, at home, Siena has won 1, drawn 3, and lost 2, scoring 9 goals and conceding 10.

Catania vs Livorno:

The two teams have only met 4 times in the history of the Italian Serie A, with Catania winning 2 and losing 2, evenly matched, scoring 5 goals and conceding 7; among which, at home, Catania has won both matches, scoring 4 goals and conceding 2.
