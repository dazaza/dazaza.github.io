It acts on the terminals of cholinergic motor nerves, antagonizing calcium ion action in some way and interfering with the release of acetylcholine from the terminals of motor nerves, causing muscle fibers not to contract, leading to muscle relaxation for wrinkle removal and beauty purposes. Demonstration of the effect of injecting botulinum toxin for frown line removal: In facial beauty, moderate to severe frown lines can be improved by injecting botulinum toxin. Thus, injecting botulinum toxin is an effective minimally invasive cosmetic method for removing facial wrinkles, worthy of choice by those seeking beauty. However, plastic surgery experts point out that although the effect of botulinum toxin for wrinkle removal is relatively ideal, not every beauty seeker can choose this method. It is necessary to consider whether the beauty seeker's physical condition allows for botulinum toxin injection to ensure the safety of beautification. For example, if the injection site for botulinum toxin exceeds the "safe zone," it may cause drooping eyebrows, ptosis, protruding eyelids, and in severe cases, fever, fatigue, and breathing difficulties may occur.
