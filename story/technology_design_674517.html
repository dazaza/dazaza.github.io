Traffic police said that the slogan against drunk driving in liquor advertisements was hung without permission.

by nim203pqms on 2012-02-24 14:22:25

The Greatest Product Placement in History for Liquor Advertisement (Picture from the Internet) ——"The Greatest Product Placement" has been removed, and Yibin traffic police will sue the advertisement company. Due to the refusal of drunk driving publicity slogans by the traffic police being implanted with a liquor advertisement, it caused a heated online debate. Surrounding this post, netizens have jokingly referred to the banner as "the greatest product placement." In the afternoon of the 3rd, the Yibin Municipal Public Security Traffic Police Brigade publicly responded, indicating that the banner was hung without the permission of the brigade by an advertisement company. They also expressed their intention to sue the advertisement company and use legal means to protect the rights and interests of the traffic police brigade.

Online Post: Liquor Advertisement Planted in Traffic Police Propaganda

On the 31st of last month, Yibin netizen "Xiao Yu" posted on a local online forum, stating that two traffic police propaganda slogans appeared on the outer wall of the road at the original satellite observation station in Nan'an, Yibin. The slogans were implanted with advertisements for a certain brand of liquor.

From the pictures posted, there were two red banners with white letters hanging on the outer wall of the road, respectively printed with the slogans "Traffic Police Warm Reminder: Refuse Drunk Driving" and "Set Out with Peace, Return with Happiness." To the left of both banners were advertisements for a certain brand of liquor, and the lower right corner of the banners both printed: Announced by the Yibin Municipal Public Security Bureau Traffic Police Brigade.

After the post was made, it attracted numerous netizens' attention and was quickly forwarded to major online forums and communities such as Dacheng Network. Some netizens, with a mocking attitude, thought, "On one side is refusing drunk driving, and on the other side is a liquor advertisement, too perfect!" Other netizens were confused about the embedded advertisements in the slogans, questioning whether the traffic police had received sponsorship fees, nicknaming it as "the greatest product placement."

In the afternoon of the 3rd, reporters went to the original satellite observation station in Nan'an mentioned in the online post and found that the banners on the outer wall had already been removed. "It's been removed for several days," said a nearby citizen.

The Traffic Police Have Already Sued the Advertisement Company

"We are completely unaware of what's going on." Xiao Yong from the Yibin Municipal Traffic Police Brigade's Publicity Department introduced that he accidentally saw the post and pictures online. Subsequently, the traffic police brigade investigated the pictures in the post.

Upon investigation, it was found that the banners mentioned in the online post were made by a certain advertisement company in Yibin without prior oral or written commission or permission from the traffic police brigade and its subordinate second detachment. "We don't even know which advertisement company it is," said Xiao Yong.

Subsequently, the reporter contacted the person in charge of the advertisement company, who confirmed that the banner was indeed hung by the company without prior approval from the traffic police brigade. Xiao Yong stated that in this incident, the traffic police were falsely accused by the advertisement company.

In the afternoon of the 3rd, the Yibin Municipal Traffic Police Brigade publicly responded to this matter, indicating that they have filed a lawsuit against the advertisement company to the Cuiping District People's Court of Yibin City and will protect the rights and interests of the brigade through legal channels.

Huaxi Metropolis Daily reporter He Wenzong Pu Kanglin
