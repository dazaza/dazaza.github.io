Rage Dragon Battle 2 Ultra-Rich Warcraft System

by youxiruanjian88 on 2009-03-23 08:46:12

[Basic Introduction]

A single-player game of the magical online game genre, featuring an extremely rich pet and divine beast system, dazzling glowing artifacts, a character second transfer system, fishing grounds system, package drop system, BOSS challenge system, gambling system, and teleportation system.

[Update Log]

1. The female protagonist system has been added; you can bring one female protagonist. After bringing a female protagonist, you will only be able to carry two pets instead of three.

2. A second transfer job system has been added, including three professions: Dragon Knight, Magic Master, and Magic Warrior.

3. An event package system has been added. There is a chance to obtain mid-level artifacts and mid-level divine beasts from it. High-level monsters and BOSSes have a chance to drop event packages. However, fighting Event Messengers will almost always yield event packages. Event Messengers may appear in medium to high-level adventure areas. They also have a chance to be caught at high-level and top-tier fishing spots. Defeating them will allow you to obtain the package.

4. A gambling system has been added, requiring 200,000G per attempt. There is a chance to win low-level artifacts, low-level divine beasts, and event packages.

5. A fishing ground system has been added, including low, medium, high, and top-tier fishing grounds. The items fished differ depending on the tier of the fishing ground; higher-tier fishing grounds yield more valuable items. The fishing ground in Brave Village is a low-tier fishing ground, Gadanzhen is a medium-tier fishing ground, Sand City is a high-tier fishing ground, and Slon Royal City is a top-tier fishing ground. You can enter through the Fishing Administrator (available in every town and village). In high and top-tier fishing grounds, there is also a chance to catch Event Messengers. There is a high probability of catching money at each fishing ground, and the amount increases with the tier of the fishing ground.

6. A location name system has been added this time.

7. Advanced divine beasts and top-tier divine beasts (both come with ultra-cool skills) have been added, as well as advanced artifacts and top-tier artifacts (all come with stunning attacks, sleep attacks, paralysis attacks, or critical hits), all of which are very cool and powerful.

8. A large number of town maps and high-level adventure areas have been added.

9. A monster avoidance charm system has been added. Use it when you don't want to fight monsters but want to explore the map. Available for purchase at various shops. Regular avoidance charms prevent encounters for 10 seconds, while larger ones prevent encounters for 30 seconds.

10. Additionally, a teleportation stone system for towns and adventure areas has been added. Note: High-level and top-tier artifacts, high-level and top-tier divine beast eggs can only be obtained by defeating monsters. Gambling can only yield low-level divine beast eggs and low-level artifacts. Event packages have a very low chance of yielding high-level artifacts and high-level divine beast eggs.

11. Additionally, there is a professional guild in Slon Royal City where both first and second transfers can be made. However, second transfers can also be done in Filishuizhen.

12. Additionally, experience pills can be fished out. If you're tired of fighting monsters, you can go fishing. Fishing earns money quickly, gives items, and helps level up.

13. Additionally, entry tickets for challenging Event Messengers have been added. Using them allows you to enter the area where Event Messengers appear. There is a chance to encounter Event Messengers there, and their numbers are unlimited. Spend some time exploring if you have time (be patient). Some packages can be beaten, with entry tickets costing 5 million G each. Available for purchase at shops in Sand City, Slon Royal City, and Filishuizhen.

14. Additionally, entry tickets for challenging BOSSes have been added. It's best to farm BOSS pets and artifacts inside. BOSS entry tickets come in two types: one for challenging the first five BOSSes, and another for challenging the final BOSS. Each type costs 9 million G, available for purchase at shops in Slon Royal City and Filishuizhen.

15. Additionally, high-level equipment and high-level pet eggs have been divided into star ratings.

16. Additionally, exploring the map will bring many unexpected surprises. Please take your time to explore any map. ^_^ Also, note that all pets have had skills added, and they are divided into nine categories. Besides the low-level common pet category, all other categories have different skills.

17. Additionally, all pets can wear equipment, and there are several types of special pet equipment. Pets include Angel, Earth, Wind, Ice, Fire, Thunder, Demon, Warrior Beast, Small Demon, and Common Pet across nine categories, with Common Pet being the lowest rank and not possessing skills.

Correction Content:

1. The drop rates for all equipment, items, and pet eggs have been tripled.

2. Some bugs have been fixed.

[Usage Method]

Game operation instructions: F1 key Sound toggle

Shift key Accelerated character movement

Alt key + Enter key Toggle between full-screen and windowed game mode
