Directed by renowned director 尔 Dongsheng, starring Gu Tianle, Nicholas Tse, Li Bingbing, and 阿 Sa, the suspenseful gun battle blockbuster "King of Gunmen" has seen its box office steadily rising since its preview on the evening of July 1st. Upon learning of this success, 尔 Dongsheng expressed a slight sense of surprise: "After all, we encountered the once-in-four-years World Cup, so winning this battle was not easy." "King of Gunmen" breaks the silence of the summer season as Yu Dong analyzes the behind-the-scenes story.

Moreover, Gu Tianle, who delivered a brilliant performance on screen, also actively promoted "King of Gunmen" off-screen. There are dozens of blog posts related to "King of Gunmen" on his blog. This also shows his personal interest in the film. Regarding "King of Gunmen", he had some different insights: "Apart from the action, 'King of Gunmen' pays more attention to the era's background. Just like the current social environment where international economic order becomes more complex every day, the director devoted a large portion of the film to describe social issues—financial crisis, economic bubble, Euro crash, even revealing the behind-the-scenes operations of financial regulators. Many reviews suggest that 'King of Gunmen' is more like a psychological drama, which is quite reasonable because the film reveals the crisis of elite spirit, which is also a problem caused by the heavy pressure in many big cities' societies."

Since June, the mainland film market has continued to be sluggish, without any film exceeding 100 million yuan in over a month. The emergence of "King of Gunmen" broke this quiet situation. From the evening preview on July 1st to July 18th, the box office of "King of Gunmen" has exceeded 110 million yuan in total. This achievement not only makes it the first Chinese film this summer to exceed 100 million yuan but also lets Director 尔 Dongsheng enter the hundred-million-yuan club. Although the hundred-million-yuan club is not significant for 尔 Dongsheng, he stated: "I have said before, box office is what the distributors care about, I focus more on input and output. However, the fact that 'King of Gunmen' exceeded 100 million yuan allows me to see the inclusiveness of mainland audiences. Films like 'King of Gunmen' with serious themes are never big sellers. Achieving such good results on the mainland, I am somewhat surprised overall. Here, I would like to thank the excellent performances of all actors and the support of the investors, and more importantly, thank the audience for their support, whether it is praise or criticism, I accept them all."

Regarding the box office success of "King of Gunmen", Yu Dong, President of the production and distribution company Bona International Pictures Group, said: "Firstly, thank the main creators of the film for contributing such a masterpiece, secondly, thank the joint efforts of several production companies including Emperor Entertainment Group, SMG, and Silver Metropolis Organization, as well as friends who cooperated in promotion. In terms of release timing, 'King of Gunmen' took a risky move by choosing to premiere during the elimination round of the World Cup's quarterfinals, which brought significant early pressure. However, our targeted publicity strategies were precise and steady. Moreover, in terms of promoting to target audiences, we made meticulous plans. Most crucially, we aimed to make the audience feel satisfied; they are our God. Ultimately, both the film's reputation and box office have proven that our efforts have not been wasted, and we have won this battle."
