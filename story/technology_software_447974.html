Based on the spirit of the "Notice from the xx County Insurance Production Committee on Issuing the Plan for the 2011 'Safe Production Month' in xx County" (Document No. 10 [2011] of the Safety Bureau), and carefully implementing the "Implementation Plan for the 2011 'Safety Education Month' Activity" issued by the County Education and Sports Bureau, and combining with the actual situation of school development, this plan is formulated.

I. Overall Requirements

Implement the guiding principles of scientific development and safety development, with the goal of "effectively curbing major incidents, resolutely preventing extremely serious incidents, and ensuring continuous stability in the safety situation of schools throughout the county." Focus closely on the requirements of the "Year of Grassroots Foundation for Safe Production" and the construction of star-rated safe campuses. Seriously implement the spirit of relevant safety production meetings at provincial, municipal, and county levels. Taking the development of the "Eight Ones" activities as the key point, further strengthen the responsibility of school safety management, enhance the safety awareness and quality of teachers and students, promote safety culture construction, achieving the goals of promoting work through activities and promoting the year through a month, thereby facilitating the stable and continuous healthy development of the entire county's education sector.

II. Event Timeframe and Theme

Event Time: June 1 to June 30.

Event Theme: Safe Development, Prevention-Oriented.

III. Implementing Entity

xx Middle School

IV. Event Content

Actively carry out the "Eight Ones" activities, namely: publishing a special column for safety education and publicity, mobilizing teachers and students to conduct an activity to identify safety hazards around them, organize a major discussion themed class meeting titled "Safety Accompanies Me," host a competition on school safety prevention knowledge, organize an emergency response drill, distribute a "Safety Reminder Letter to Parents," organize an expert lecture, and conduct a signature campaign with the theme of "Safe Development, Prevention-Oriented" to ensure that the concept of "Safety First" deeply penetrates all students and their parents. The main contents include:

1. Publish a special column for safety education and publicity. Make full use of bulletin boards, blackboards, campus broadcasts, and speeches under the national flag to strengthen student safety knowledge education. Focus particularly on legal education and fire, traffic, flood control, food hygiene safety education, as well as drowning prevention and epidemic disease prevention education. Through hanging banners, setting up promotional booths, writing slogans, and distributing brochures, widely publicize school safety work to society, forming a good atmosphere where the whole society cares about students, education, and student safety.

2. Conduct an activity to identify safety hazards around them. Before June 20th, the school should actively organize personnel to comprehensively inspect potential safety hazards in the townships and schools. Inspections must be thorough and meticulous, whoever inspects signs off, and whoever signs off takes responsibility. Any identified hazards should be promptly rectified according to the principle of four implementations: corrective measures, rectification funds, responsible personnel, and deadlines. Fill out the hazard inspection form. On this basis, widely mobilize teachers and students to report safety hazards within the school and its surroundings, rewarding students who participate actively and achieve outstanding results.

3. Conduct a major discussion themed class meeting titled "Safety Accompanies Me." Organize each class to hold a themed class meeting titled "Safety Accompanies Me." Regarding flood season safety, food safety, traffic safety, etc., violent harm incidents, conduct safety education through various forms such as teacher-student interaction, everyone participating in safety education reports, etc., popularizing safety knowledge and enhancing safety prevention capabilities.

4. Host a competition on school safety prevention knowledge. In conjunction with the national safety production emergency knowledge competition, conduct a school safety laws and regulations and emergency rescue knowledge answer session across the entire school. The school publishes questions from websites such as the Municipal Work Safety Supervision Bureau via various media, adopting flexible and diverse formats to mobilize teachers and students to participate in the answer session.

5. Organize an emergency response drill. Further revise and improve all emergency response plans, achieving scientific, practical, guiding, and standardized plans. According to relevant requirements, organize all teachers and students for a seismic avoidance and emergency evacuation drill during the event month, practicing students' escape techniques and self-rescue awareness in the drills.

6. Distribute a "Safety Reminder Letter to Parents." Through the form of a "Safety Reminder Letter to Parents," teach parents knowledge and skills regarding safety prevention, striving to achieve the excellent situation of "student safety, jointly managed by home and school."

7. Organize an expert lecture. Actively carry out the "Safety Knowledge Enters Campus" activity, inviting experts in traffic police, fire, and earthquake fields to give lectures on traffic, fire, and earthquake knowledge at the school, enhancing students' self-protection awareness and accident prevention capabilities.

8. Conduct a signature campaign with the theme of "Safe Development, Prevention-Oriented." Organize all teachers and students to sign on a banner themed "Safe Development, Prevention-Oriented," enhancing teachers' and students' safety awareness and self-protection awareness.

V. Work Requirements

(1) Each class should consider strengthening student safety education as one of the main contents of deeply carrying out the study and practice of the scientific development view activity. Realize that student safety education relates to the progress and improvement of students, and the future development of the country. Ensure that the rate of students receiving education reaches 100%.

(2) Through self-inspection and self-correction of campus safety hazards, create a safe campus environment. By organizing students to find safety hazards, create a good atmosphere in families and society where "everyone participates in school safety, and everyone cares about school safety," not only improving children's safety awareness but also enhancing societal safety awareness through the transmission path of student-family-society.

(3) Stand on innovation, seek practical effects. Carefully summarize school safety work experiences, adhere to being close to reality, combine with own characteristics, innovate forms and content, highlight features, and enhance effectiveness. Actively explore long-term mechanisms for promoting work through activities, ensuring safety through activities, and doing a good job in safety publicity and education work, creating a good atmosphere for the smooth hosting of the 11th Games.

(4) After the event, the school will seriously summarize the situation of conducting the "Safety Education Month" activity in this unit, archive it for reference, and send the school safety month report, activity correspondence, and typical pictures to the safety office public email before June 28th.
