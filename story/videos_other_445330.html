Taxi driver suffered a sudden cardiac death on the way, and saved passengers by pulling the handbrake before he died.

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-06-13 15:35:33

At around 4 p.m. yesterday afternoon, taxi driver Master Ji was carrying a passenger near Meitehao on Liaoyang West Road when he suddenly felt unwell. In the emergency, he hurriedly pulled the handbrake and parked the car by the side of the road, saving the life of the passenger. However, he collapsed on the steering wheel and never woke up. According to the doctors at Haici Hospital, Master Ji was suspected of sudden cardiac arrest.

The driver carried a passenger and suddenly fell ill.

At around 4:30 p.m. yesterday afternoon, I rushed to Haici Hospital upon hearing the news. Master Ji had already died despite resuscitation efforts. Outside the emergency room, Mr. Liu, Master Ji's colleague, told me that at around 4 p.m., he suddenly received a call on Master Ji’s phone, but the caller was not Master Ji.

"He told me he was a passenger of Old Ji, who fainted while driving and was sent to Haici Hospital," Mr. Liu said to the reporter, "This passenger told me that at 4 p.m. today, he got into Old Ji's taxi. He noticed Old Ji's face was purple as soon as he got in, but Old Ji kept saying there was nothing wrong." The vehicle drove near Meitehao on Liaoyang West Road when Old Ji suddenly turned pale and said he didn't feel well. After driving for several hundred meters, he suddenly braked urgently and parked the car by the roadside, then collapsed on the steering wheel and became motionless.

The passenger thanked for being saved.

Mr. Liu said that the passenger quickly dialed 120 and contacted his family and colleagues through Master Ji's phone, accompanying the 120 rescue personnel to send Master Ji to the hospital. When they arrived at the hospital, the passenger repeatedly expressed gratitude to Master Ji's colleagues. "At that time, the traffic volume was very high. Fortunately, Master Ji pulled the handbrake in time, otherwise the car would have rushed into the traffic flow causing an accident, and I might have lost my life." After thanking Old Ji's colleagues repeatedly, the passenger left. Upon learning of Master Ji's misfortune, his colleagues rushed to the hospital to visit him and comforted his family. I later went to the scene of the incident and saw that Master Ji's taxi was still quietly parked by the roadside, but it was empty.

There were signs before death.

I learned that Master Ji was only 37 years old. Mr. Liu told me that on the evening of December 31, 2009, he drank with Master Ji together, "When we were halfway through drinking, he said his chest hurt, but none of us paid attention at the time, then we went home separately. This morning when we met, he said his chest was still tight, so I advised him to go to the hospital, but he kept waving his hand and said it was fine." At around 6 p.m. yesterday, Master Ji's family rushed to the hospital, and the emergency room was filled with heart-wrenching cries. Because the family was in deep sorrow, I really couldn't bear to disturb them.

According to medical staff, Master Ji died suddenly from cardiac arrest, "The patient may have had a history of heart disease, but it was not discovered and not taken seriously." The doctor told the reporter that due to untimely first aid during the sudden onset of illness, Master Ji died suddenly. (Reported by Li Deyin)
