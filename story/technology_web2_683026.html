Symbol of Strength: The Seven Nations' Rulers Make a Shocking Debut

In the latest version of "Struggle of Seven Heroes," the rulers of the seven nations - Qin, Qi, Chu, Zhao, Wei, Han, and Yan - make a stunning appearance. These supreme kings will enjoy unparalleled glory and unify the world! After the annual version "Ruling the World" is online, the leader of the alliance that occupies the capital in the county city battle, in this QQ web game, will be gloriously crowned as the ruler. Alongside them, 1 General, 3 Commanders, and 6 Marshals will also be born, receiving special privileges upon their coronation. This is not only the player's ultimate honor but also a symbol of the alliance's extraordinary strength.

Privilege Coronation: Prestigious Official Positions for the Ruler

In the new version of "Struggle of Seven Heroes," the ruler enjoys more privileges. When the ruler logs on, all national players are informed to come and wait respectfully. The ruler’s castle exudes dominance at all times. They receive at least a 10% bonus to troop attack and defense, along with eight supreme privileges specific to the ruler such as "National Megaphone," "National Announcement," and "National Mute." Every day, they can also collect rich official salaries! The rewards for the ruler's privileges have never been so generous before, absolutely shocking everyone's nerves! It can be foreseen that the most thrilling and blood-pumping county battles will soon begin.

Official Position Enhancements: Super Privileges May Double

In the new version of "Struggle of Seven Heroes," official privileges may double. Whether it is the ruler, the general, the commander, or the marshal, the four types of players who receive promotions in the alliance may enjoy doubled privileges in the new version. The position coefficient will also affect "troop attack and defense increase." In other words, the higher the player's official position, the higher the proportion of troop attack and defense bonuses will be, potentially giving them an unprecedentedly strong combat force to march forward fearlessly. Moreover, the large amount of prestige points and Seven-Star Sand from official salaries will be enjoyed daily by the four types of promoted players. Fame and fortune start from the official system of "Struggle of Seven Heroes!"

The new version of "Struggle of Seven Heroes," "Ruling the World," is officially online, allowing you to look down on the world and unify the land!
