Big Background: The Shanda Innovation Institute is one of the two important innovation engines of the Shanda Group. Unlike Microsoft or Lenovo Research Institutes, our focus on innovation doesn’t just stop at the technical level but also includes business models and product innovations. The Innovation Institute has a highly free and open working environment with many "legendary" tech experts. Here, all employees can fully participate in projects they are truly interested in without too many institutional restrictions.

Above is a general overview of our Innovation Institute; now let's talk about our project specifically: Through internal recruitment (mainly) + external recruitment, we are forming a small entrepreneurial team of about 10 people (yes, you didn't read wrong. Except for the inability to bear and enjoy some risks of solo entrepreneurship, everything else can be regarded as an entrepreneurial experience in your life. No cumbersome meetings, redundant requirement documents—just quick product iterations, a fast-paced rhythm, and together influencing Shanda and changing the world). Our broad direction is locked on SoLoMo (Social, Local, Mobile). This is a project I initiated and participated in throughout. I believe we have the courage to redefine the concept of social networking within the industry and have confidence in creating the best mobile social product domestically. Product, design, interaction, and performance…

Job Type: Full-time employment and interns

Location: Pudong New Area, Shanghai

Salary: There will be a dedicated HR representative who will discuss with you. If you're reliable, I think it will basically meet all your reasonable requirements. However, we expect you to join us because you're dissatisfied with the status quo and want to change the world.

Number of Hires: 2

Requirements:

- Perfectionists, obsessive-compulsives preferred;

- Enthusiasts and users of smartphones;

- Unique insights and understanding of terminal product UI/UE, pursuing good user experience;

- Proficient in Objective-C, familiar with iOS SDK, familiar with XCode development environment;

- Over six months of iOS development experience, able to independently develop iPhone apps, those with successfully published apps will be given priority;

- Strong sense of responsibility towards software products, good communication skills and excellent team collaboration ability;

Functions:

- Fully participate in the definition of mobile app product requirements and features;

- Undertake the development tasks of the app product, able to independently complete the development of the app;

- Design & code review of other team products;

- Participate in team technical exchanges and sharing activities to promote common progress;
