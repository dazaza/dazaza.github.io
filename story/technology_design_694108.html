Rescue Action: Gentle Cleansing

Cleansing Keywords: Cleansing gel, massage, nighttime

Compared to creamy cleansers, cleansing gels are more effective but not as harsh as cleansing soaps. They might be a good choice. After being tortured by the city air all day, the skin is already gray and fragile at night. Use the cleansing gel to clean up all these pollutants. Don't forget to gently massage in circles for better results!
