Female driver who violated traffic regulations claimed to be the wife of the Industry and Commerce Bureau chief and scolded the traffic police

by zzf000zxyn7 on 2011-06-16 13:52:25

Traffic police were interviewed, and the driver who violated traffic rules claimed to be the wife of a certain Industry and Commerce Bureau director in Changsha and scolded the traffic police.

According to reports by Hunan TV, on May 18th at a crossroads in the bustling area of Changsha, a woman was driving a white Nissan car with the license plate number湘A・W5778 on the busy streets of Changsha. She was stopped and penalized by the on-duty traffic police because her vehicle's windows did not have inspection or mandatory insurance stickers. Unexpectedly, after getting out of the car, the female driver, who claimed to be the wife of a certain Industry and Commerce Bureau director in Changsha, started causing a scene on the street against the on-duty traffic police.

Video: The female driver claiming to be the director's wife curses and insults the traffic police. Source: Oriental Satellite TV's "Look East".

Chen Xinzhang, the instructor of the Eighth Detachment of the Yuhua Traffic Police Brigade in Changsha, introduced to reporters that when the driver heard that the on-duty traffic police intended to fine her two hundred yuan, she immediately became furious and started yelling at the on-duty traffic police. Not only did she rip off the duty badge from the traffic policeman's uniform, but she also openly insulted the traffic police. This female driver claimed to be the wife of a certain Industry and Commerce Bureau director in Changsha, insisting that she had not violated any laws and refused to accept the penalty. After several minutes of deadlock with the on-duty traffic police, she eventually drove away from the scene at an accelerated speed, ignoring the ticket issued by the traffic police. According to the traffic police, if the driver refuses to accept the ticket issued by the traffic police on the spot, legally speaking, as long as the evidence provided by the traffic police is solid and the facts are clear, the penalty will still be valid. After checking the vehicle information, the traffic police confirmed that the owner of the car was Ms. Zhang, aged fifty years old.
