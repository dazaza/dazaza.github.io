Nine Precautions for Camping:

Prevent drowning: After dark, swimming is prohibited. Non-swimmers can only be in shallow water areas (water depth not exceeding 1.3 meters).

Prevent darkness: Bring a household appliance - flashlight, haha, it will be just as convenient even when it gets dark.

Prevent mosquitoes: Make sure to bring mosquito repellent, otherwise you'll be feeding mosquitoes at night.

Prevent rain: Please bring an umbrella or raincoat in case of unexpected weather.

Prevent noise: From midnight 12 to early 7 am, loud noises are prohibited near the camping area to avoid disturbing others' rest.

Prevent sunburn: Especially for women, prepare sunscreen items and preferably bring a towel for wiping sweat.

Prevent dirt: It's best to bring a few newspapers to sit on during meals or bonfire parties.

Prevent loss: Bring fewer valuable items and don't leave them inside the tent.

Prevent insomnia: It's best to bring an inflatable pillow for a comfortable sleep.

[Event Introduction]

The world's first themed rafting, national AAAA-level tourist area, Hubei's top ten tourist attractions, Yangjiaxi Rafting with its thrilling and mysterious military theme is filled with a strong military color. It has functions such as rafting adventure, military training, and aquatic leisure. The rafting river section is divided into dynamic water rafting and static water rafting. Here, visitors wear camouflage clothing, paddle camouflage kayaks, are organized into military units led by elected commanders, singing military songs, battling rapids, crossing dangerous shoals, feeling like undergoing military training on water, with the scenic views flying past on both sides, endless splashing waves before their eyes. The beautiful valley hides its charm, people feel like they're traveling in a painting, experiencing the thrill of wave-battling shoals, or enjoying the leisure of drifting on calm waters.

Rafting down Yangjiaxi, battling waves and crossing shoals, thrilling yet safe, is an experience; while paddling boats on still water, forgetting worries in the secluded valley, is another kind of joy... All of these experiences will leave unforgettable memories.

Here is the only culturally-themed rafting project among over 150 rafting scenic areas nationwide.

Here is the only AAAA-level rafting scenic area in the province.

During the rafting here, there's interaction between the water and the shore, various entertainment projects ensuring that you won't feel monotonous or bored. The entire rafting journey lasts about 3 hours!

For more information about Yangjiaxi Rafting from Dayu Network: http://hb.qq.com/a/20100602/001533.htm
