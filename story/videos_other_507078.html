Teachers and students: Just now, the reports given by the students were touching and inspiring, expressing deep affection for *** , the motherland, and the people. They demonstrated an optimistic and positive attitude towards life, a spirit of daring to strive, and revealed the vigorous and upward spirit of the youth in the new century. It showcased the lively and positive spirit of middle school students in the new era. ** pointed out that we should guide young people to establish a correct socialist sense of honor and disgrace. The first point is: to take loving the motherland as an honor, and endangering the motherland as a shame. To love the motherland, one must have a patriotic spirit. The Chinese nation is a great nation, and patriotism is the most beautiful flower of our nation. Patriotism is a sacred word; throughout the twists and turns of historical development, patriotism has always been cherished by the Chinese people. Entering the 21st century, our great motherland is becoming increasingly prosperous and strong, and patriotism should become the strongest sound of this era! Patriotism is a brilliant banner uniting all ethnic groups in China in struggle, a powerful force driving the progress of Chinese society and history, and patriotic education is undoubtedly the most important education! Students, as the youth of the new century, you are the hope of the motherland, and the future of the motherland will surely belong to you. Therefore, you must carry forward the noble spirit of patriotism. To bear and carry forward the spirit of patriotism, it must be reflected in actions. If the motherland needs us to sacrifice our lives, every upright Chinese person will hesitate not at all to die for the country. However, we live in a peaceful era now, and the motherland does not need us to go to the battlefield. So how should we show our patriotism? As students, your primary task is to love your own school. Can a student who doesn't even love his own school possibly love his own country? Loving the school is a very specific and close action. Wen Yang Middle School provides you with a good learning environment, teachers work hard to cultivate you into talents, here is where you grow. As a student of Wen Yang Middle School, you have no reason not to love your own school. Every student should cherish the image and reputation of the school like they do their own eyes; we should sincerely respect our teachers; we should consciously protect the plants and trees on campus, creating a harmonious learning environment; you should also love every classmate in the school like your own brothers and sisters, mutually loving and learning, growing up happily and healthily together! You should also cherish time, study hard, strive for excellent results, and repay the school and our respected teachers! Patriotism is the highest morality, the soul of the nation. Dear students, for the glory of the school, for the heavy responsibility on our shoulders, for the rise of the Chinese nation, you must study hard and become qualified middle school students with patriotism, practicing the socialist sense of honor and disgrace! As students of Wen Yang Middle School, bring glory to Wen Yang Middle School.
