Guo Jingming said Han Han does not represent the post-80s writers, fans掀起another cursing battle

by mlwang on 2011-11-21 11:15:32

On November 15, Han Han once again mentioned Guo Jingming under the media's pursuit of questioning when he attended a brand launching ceremony which he newly represented. His short words were interpreted as hinting that Guo Jingming put too much emphasis on the propaganda unlike his own low-key style. Yesterday, Guo Jingming "hit back" and said in an exclusive interview with his magazine that Han Han could not represent the post-80s writers. The two "mutual appraisals" within a short term immediately triggered a war of abuse on the Internet. Fans of Han Han and Guo Jingming swore at each other, making the situation full of gunpowder smell for a time. As representatives of the post-80s writers, Han Han and Guo Jingming are often compared by people. How Han Han appraises Guo Jingming and how Guo Jingming looks at Han Han have become indispensable topics in media interviews. Both of them from "New Concept Composition Contest" won numerous fans because of their unique talent and personality. In the blog era, they both ranked top ten in the Sina Blog Popularity List. Counting young writers who can reach one million copies sales nowadays, they are also benchmarks. However, unfortunately, fans of the two have been in a state of enmity and cannot be compatible with each other.

Perhaps this is because Han Han and Guo Jingming do not like each other either, at least Han Han has clearly expressed his dissatisfaction to Guo Jingming before. On the afternoon of November 15, when Han Han attended the launching ceremony of a certain brand he had just represented, he once again "spoke against" Guo Jingming: "There are many readers who like him, but of course we cannot judge the taste of these readers. But I personally think that I do not particularly like to publicize my new book." Such meaningful words were interpreted by the media as Han Han's sneer at Guo Jingming for being not as low-key as himself and liking publicity too much.

If you don't return a gift after receiving one, it's against propriety. On November 18, when Guo Jingming was interviewed exclusively by the magazine "Literary Wind Enjoyment" produced under his company, he was asked who could better represent the post-80s writers when compared with Han Han. Guo Jingming said, "Han Han cannot represent the post-80s writers, neither can I. We actually seem to be less like writers." After the news came out, "Guo Jingming talks about Han Han" became Baidu hot search terms today immediately.

As long as Han Han and Guo Jingming are involved at the same time, strong reactions will be triggered on the Internet no matter what the specific events are. This morning, more than 7,000 netizens on a well-known portal website forum discussed this matter. After reading nearly 10 pages of posts, the reporter found that most of them were quarrels and abuses between fans of the two. Some attacked idols of each other, some directly made personal attacks on reply messages, all filled with gunpowder smell.

Some netizens were relatively "calm", replying that Han Han and Guo Jingming were more like entertainment stars consumed excessively rather than writers. "We should focus our attention on more works of the two. If we have different preferences, then take what we need. There is no need to quarrel."
