In the group buying business, attention should be paid to choosing some cost-effective or mid-to-high-end catering and hotels.

by beetgk2958 on 2011-11-17 10:52:59

If you want to continue to develop, both need to be improved. What kind of profit can the relatively low price of food and beverage bring to the website? Liu Changhua said that compared with other commodities, catering merchants have better cost control, so even if the price is low, it can still bring certain profits to the group-buying website. The profit of Taobao Group Buying JuHuaSuan business is around 10% - 15%. Wei Zijie also said that the price and combination method of dishes on the group-buying platform are generally determined by merchants, so they can still control the cost when combining meal packages, bringing a certain profit margin to the website. Currently, Youhuo's profit is about 5%-10%.

"Catering belongs to rigid consumption in daily life, eating takes up a large proportion in consumption expenditure, so this item has a wide customer base and relatively considerable sales revenue, which is more attractive to group-buying websites." Wei Zijie, founder of Youhuo Network, said in an interview with reporters. He indicated that as the price of meals continues to rise, currently most Chinese food group-buying offers are at 50% off, while Western food is at about 30% off. Especially for those restaurants that people usually avoid due to high prices, consumers can try them at a relatively lower price through group-buying, thus a large number of consumers can quickly be attracted to pay attention to the website when there are many catering projects. Zhang Lei, General Manager of "Taobao Jinan" and one of the person-in-charge of Taobao Group Buying JuHuaSuan business in Jinan area, also told reporters that among the current projects of major group-buying websites, necessities are quite popular, and food belongs to daily consumption with a vast consumer base. This undoubtedly has appeal for both the website and the catering store.

Necessities are popular and catering fits right in

Zhang Lei also expressed that compared with other industries, the biggest feature of the catering industry lies in its strong repetitive consumption, and the best way to promote is experiential marketing. Currently, most small and medium-sized catering stores in our city provide community-type services, and their service scope is often a business district or a community. For group-buying, it itself has the distinct characteristics of low price and nearby consumption, often facing a city or even a smaller area, so the two fit perfectly. Regarding this, Zhang Lei, Vice President of the Jinan City Catering Industry Association, said in an interview with reporters that there are currently two or three thousand big and small restaurants in our city, and the competition is very fierce. For some newly opened restaurants or those launching new dishes, they urgently need promotion. On the one hand, some small and medium-sized restaurants, due to insufficient economic strength, find it not worthwhile to pay high advertising fees for TV and other media; on the other hand, for the catering industry, no type of promotion is as direct as letting consumers try the food, which leads to some small and medium-sized catering stores attracting consumers through group-buying. Regarding costs and profits, he gave an example where a merchant sets aside 50,000 yuan, "The effect of promoting this price on TV and other media is often not ideal. Now some merchants put these 50,000 yuan into the group-buying discount, attracting consumers with low prices and gaining a stable group of consumers through direct consumer experience, thus laying the foundation for future profitability."

Zhang Lei also pointed out that for catering merchants, group-buying can gather temporary popularity, but from a long-term perspective, it is not conducive to healthy development. Currently, young consumers make up the majority of the group-buying crowd, whose consumption patterns are hard to fix, and with so many selectable items online now, the probability of repeated consumption is not very high. Catering needs to focus on improving its own quality to do well.

Liu Changhua mentioned that with the increase in group-buying websites and intense competition, some websites once adopted zero-profit or even negative-profit ways to attract high-quality catering chain stores, leading to frequent vicious competitions, causing the average profit of catering group-buying to once fall below 5%. Currently, group-buying websites are undergoing reshuffling. According to data from China E-commerce Research Center, by the end of September this year, more than 1,000 group-buying websites nationwide have closed down or exited the group-buying market in fierce competition, accounting for 18% of all operating group-buying websites. He suggested that those group-buying websites that truly want to take action should enhance their operation mechanism while increasing the screening intensity of customers, paying attention to choosing some high-quality and reasonably priced or mid-to-high-end catering hotels in the group-buying ecosystem, and establishing a cooperative relationship with them, allowing the website to gain an advantage in the competition.

In the interviews, multiple consumers revealed to reporters that they encountered issues such as less food quantity and poor service attitude during the consumption of group-buying catering projects more than once. Some consumers even expressed feeling like "second-class citizens" during consumption. Therefore, they suggest that group-buying websites strengthen the approval process for catering stores, and catering stores should improve the quality of dishes and services in group-buying projects.
