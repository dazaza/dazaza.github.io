After the recording of the third album was over

by qqaaq4o7y on 2012-03-07 15:12:54

It was time for intense dance practice. Han Geng felt a bit dizzy and disoriented, not because his body couldn't handle it, but because the recent situation had become too overwhelming.

First, during a call, Changmin said that TVXQ would be back next month. It should have been a happy thing, but hearing Changmin's voice, Han Geng sensed heavy-heartedness. When asked what was wrong, Changmin wouldn’t say, nor did Yoochun provide any answers. Han Geng knew Yoochun all too well; if Yoochun didn’t want to talk, even prying with a wrench wouldn’t get him to open up. Therefore, Han Geng worried more about Yoochun bottling things up inside. Moreover, Yoochun had asked him a question…

“Han Geng, this is just a hypothetical… purely hypothetical… what if one day Kyuhyun tells you that the person who has always liked you is actually Siwon, what would you do?”

“Eh? What kind of question is that?”

“Just assume for a moment. What would you do? Would you tell Heechul?”

“No... probably not.”

“But wouldn’t you worry Heechul might find out the truth someday and get hurt?”

“Even if I were in that position, I wouldn’t say anything. Maybe I’d find Siwon and tell him I don’t feel that way about him and ask him to treat Heechul well.”

“What about facing Kyuhyun then?”

“Kyuhyun… I’d explain to Kyuhyun that I don’t have those feelings for Siwon. And, I believe Siwon was sincere when he was with Kyuhyun.”

“Han Geng…”

“What’s wrong? Why so many strange questions today?”

“If I were as brave as you, I wouldn’t mind. If something like this happened to me, I doubt I could say a word.”

“Why?”

“Because saying it would only increase the number of people getting hurt.”

“Getting hurt is better than being kept in the dark, otherwise, it would hurt even more later.”

“Han Geng… Do you feel like Heechul has kept you in the dark?”

“How did we suddenly start talking about me?”

“… Nevermind, wait for me to come back. I’m a little tired lately.”

“Alright, I’ll cook you something nice when you come back.”

…… ……

From this conversation alone, it was clear that Yoochun was encountering some issues. Though the exact nature remained unknown, whatever it was must have been significant enough for the usually patient Yoochun to speak up. In addition, Eunhyuk’s state had suddenly become very low. These days, Donghae avoided him, and Eunhyuk didn’t protest, simply coming home to sleep with his head buried. Junsu called several times a day, and Han Geng heard Eunhyuk on the phone with Junsu on the balcony, his voice breaking into sobs. But as an older brother, Han Geng didn’t know how to console Eunhyuk because, despite all these years, there was still a language barrier between them. Eunhyuk struggled to fully understand what Han Geng said, a fact Eunhyuk once joked about on a show, but it was true. There are real issues with accepting sound waves and tones between people, and someone like Yesung, who from the beginning could understand without words, was rare...

"Hey."

"Hmm?" Han Geng, exhausted from practice, lay sprawled on the couch, eyes closed, unwilling to move.

"Did you see Hitan?"

"Ah..." Han Geng realized it was Heechul speaking and sat up awkwardly, "Hitan...” He peered under the couch and pulled out a dirty cat face, "Here... I saw it crawl in, but I was too tired to stop it."

"Really..." Heechul unexpectedly sat beside Han Geng after locking Hitan in its cage, "You can stay sprawled like a dog."

"...Heechul talks like that, toxic words, yet his concern comes out sounding like scolding. But Han Geng understood him, so he just smiled and obediently lay down again.

"What are you smiling at? You can't even recognize an insult?"

"That's exactly why I like you, Heechul..."

"So you're asking me to leave?"

Heechul was picking a fight...

"Do I bother you?"

He wanted to argue...

"Yeah... sometimes I think I'm bothersome..."

Hmm? This tone... Han Geng opened his eyes to surprisingly see a sorrowful Heechul. His heart sank instantly.

"Who said you were bothersome? Aren't you the incomparable Kim Heechul? Who dares to say you’re bothersome?"

"Don't you think I'm bothersome?"

Heechul bitterly smiled looking at Han Geng.

"When did I ever..."

"You didn't say it, but isn't it true that the great Kim Heechul got dumped by you?"

"..." Han Geng was momentarily speechless. The one who initiated the breakup was indeed himself... but he only did it to salvage his last bit of dignity...

"Isn't this topic too serious? I hate serious topics... hahaha..." Heechul laughed to himself, "But I still want to know, why did you break up with me? For Yoochun? How is he better than me? Ah... of course, he's better at everything, cooking, caring for others, isn't he the 'mom' of TVXQ... speaks Japanese, sings, dances..."

"Heechul..." Attempting to interrupt Heechul's monologue.

"What, am I talking too much? After being with Yoochun, I've become nothing but flaws, right? In your eyes, Kim Heechul is a bothersome guy full of flaws, right?"

"Heechul, I don't understand why you belittle yourself like this... I've never thought that way..."

"But you dumped me!"

Heechul finally became agitated, throwing the toy in his hand far away.

"I don't want to argue with you." Standing up, Han Geng walked around Heechul, but Heechul grabbed his sleeve. Looking down, Han Geng found tears in Heechul's eyes.

"After you broke up with me, I thought about it for a long time, even now while being with Siwon, I keep thinking, why did you break up with me." Slowly standing up, unable to control the tears streaming down his face, "I didn't believe that Han Geng would change his heart. Even when I heard Yunho say Yoochun often visited you, I didn't believe it until you called me to break up, until after Yoochun's incident when you asked the company to let Yoochun stay with you, only then did I realize..."

"Heechul..." Reaching out to grab his hand, feeling the coldness, Han Geng finally embraced Heechul tightly, unwilling to see his tears fall on the floor anymore, "Heechul, please forgive me... because I was in pain, being with you caused me pain... I've never been the good person you think I am, I've never wanted to be that great person. Seeing you with someone else made me uncomfortable, but I told myself that if I loved you, I should accept all of you. Later, I realized I'm just a petty ordinary person, I can't bear seeing you repeatedly with others... I'm not as good as Yeongdong... and you know better than anyone that Yeongdong and I cannot coexist..."

Lifting Heechul's face gently wiping away his tears, "I don't want to hold you back, I never wanted to, so I let you go..."

Looking into Han Geng's sorrowful eyes, Heechul suddenly understood everything. All this time, he thought he was the only one enduring the pain, but he didn't realize the source of this pain was himself.

"Because I was in pain, being with you caused me pain..."

He remembered Han Geng's appearance, the Han Geng who appeared before him and Yeongdong, smiling while holding his hand and telling Yeongdong 'thank you for taking care of him'. That Han Geng was already in pain back then... but now their positions were completely reversed. They no longer had the time or opportunity to fix these mistakes. They could only... let each other go...

"Do... you love Yoochun?"

"Maybe, if he can let me rest." Wrapping his arms around Heechul's waist, feeling the familiar scent.

"Well... okay then..."

Enduring the stabbing pain in his heart, "I let you go, Han Geng..."

"Make a proper choice between Yeongdong and Siwon. They both care about you, I have nothing to worry about..."

"I'm not a kid, stop worrying about me..."

"If you have troubles, I'll still drink with you."

"I really stopped drinking for Siwon."

"Oh... good, it's bad for your health."

"Are we the best friends, Han Geng?"

"Yes, the best... friends..."

-Silver Tears

Super Junior finally came back on Music Bank. The new songs on the Best Selection album totaled five tracks, with the main song featuring the common SMP (SM Performance) style alongside a ballad about the unity of the 13 members continuing together. However, due to this song, problems arose...

When Sungmin sang the second line of his part, he cried... and the crying grew louder, causing him to curl up. Fans screamed below, but the other members could only stand there helplessly because the stage setup consisted of 13 gradually ascending small columns, each member stuck on their own column unable to move. Furthermore, since Sungmin was at the lowest point, even nearby members like Kangin and Han Geng could only look down at him, unable to bend over and help. If that wasn't enough, someone rushed up, wrapped Sungmin in a blanket, and carried him offstage. And that person was none other than Kim Junsu, recognizable even to the fans.

In the waiting room, Junsu tightly held onto the sobbing Sungmin, saying nothing. Yoochun paced anxiously nearby. Big news was about to break, and on such an important comeback stage. The company's dissatisfaction with them had been building for some time, which was why Super Junior hadn't released a new full album, and now Sungmin had messed up the performance...

"Feeling better?" After a while, Junsu pulled out a tissue to wipe Sungmin's face.

"Mm." Sungmin adjusted his breathing; his face was swollen from crying for too long.

"What happened?" Junsu showed an unusual calm.

"Nothing." Sungmin shook his head and started searching through his bag. A moment later, he pulled out a pack of cigarettes.

"You...!" Grinding his teeth halfway through a curse, Junsu snatched the cigarettes, threw them on the ground, and stomped on them twice, "Are you crazy?! Why are you crying for no reason?! Since when have you become so useless, Lee Haechan?!" Pushing Sungmin, "What's going on with you?!"

"I saw the Her Sea light sign." Sungmin sat lifelessly in the chair, letting Junsu grip his collar.

"You..." Junsu's expression immediately turned sour, and his grip softened.

"We're done. Not just me and Donghae, all of us are done."

"What are you talking about?"

"Standing on the stage, singing the song about our eternal togetherness, I suddenly understood what 'never returning' means." Sungmin spoke, his eyes reddening again, "I'm really a slow-witted idiot, everyone probably knew except me. Our hearts have scattered, irretrievably. We're just maintaining the facade of Super Junior to fool ourselves into believing we're still one."

"There's no such thing." Junsu glanced at Yoochun; Sungmin's words chilled them both.

"Shouldn't I find an opportunity to tell the Her Sea fans that we've truly broken up?" Laughing bitterly, Sungmin looked at Junsu.

"It's all about Donghae, isn't it." Junsu sighed softly, lifting his head after a pause and staring directly into Sungmin's eyes, "Haechan, it's not impossible."

"..." Sungmin didn't ask, just stared back into Junsu's eyes. As he stared, he understood what Junsu was about to say.

"It's impossible, I won't do that." Shaking his head, Sungmin stood up and hugged Junsu, "I won't give you up, I can't do that."

"Haechan, leaving me won't be as painful as leaving Donghae." Held by Sungmin, Junsu suddenly felt exhausted. If he lost even this shoulder, what should he do...

"Don't say that, Junsu, it's impossible." Clinging tightly to Junsu, "No, I've already chosen..."

"Is Haechan alright?" Urgent footsteps interrupted their conversation, Ryeowook's voice leading the charge. Then all the members entered. Donghae stood at the back, calmly watching Junsu and Sungmin embrace.

"I..." Sungmin lowered his head and approached everyone, "I'm sorry."

A few seconds of silence.

"It's fine," Seungmin was the first to step forward and pat Sungmin's shoulder, "You just gave us a scare."

"Yeah, I didn't know what to do either." Han Geng nodded, turning his head to notice Yoochun sitting in the corner, his face lighting up with surprise. "How did you guys come back so fast? Weren't you supposed to return in a few weeks?"

"Where are Yunho and Changmin?" Kangin looked around the room.

"Just the two of us came back." Yoochun stood up from his seat, "We'll continue staying in Japan for a while, so we won't have the chance to return next month." Taking a few steps towards Han Geng, noticing Keyth quietly staring at him, Yoochun hesitated slightly.

"Han Geng, you and Yoochun go ahead, Haechan and Junsu should also go back." Unexpectedly, Keyth spoke up, surprising everyone in the room.

"Brother..." Siwon thought Keyth was angry and tugged at his sleeve.

"What? Do you think I'm joking?" Keyth glared at him, "If I guessed right, these two idiots ran back from Japan and need to go back soon, right? So don't waste time. And the rest of us will stay behind to figure out how to clean up after Haechan. That dimwit probably can't think of anything beyond saying 'sorry.'"

"Keyth-nim..." Sungmin touched Keyth's hand, moved by his kindness.

"Go away, troublemaker, Junsu take him away." Keyth pretended to be annoyed and shoved his hand away, "And that one..." Glancing past the crowd at the emotionless Donghae. "You should go too."

"Why?" Donghae glanced at Sungmin, quickly averting his gaze.

"I'm not letting you slack off, but look at how pale Hyun looks... Has he been to the bathroom again? Donghae, just take him home to rest. Ugh, when did I become such a gossip like Jungsoo?" Keyth helplessly slapped his forehead, "Today, I'm not acting like myself, right Siwon?" Turning to rub Siwon's chin.

"But Brother feels so warm." Siwon smiled and embraced Keyth from behind.

Warmth... Warm people attract affection... Keyth chuckled bitterly, watching Yoochun helping Han Geng with his coat. They suited each other well... Two warm people together would create even more warmth...

"Brother, can I take Jungsoo home too?" Yesung suddenly stood up.

"No, no way. I'm the leader, I need to deal with this too." Leeteuk waved his hands frantically, but his sore back reminded him of the effort put into the comeback stage...

"How about I be the leader for a day?" Keyth felt incredibly generous, "Let me experience it too. Take your sore back home, we don't need the elderly here."

"But..."

"Alright, the rest follow me, we're going to the bar to solve Haechan's problem." Without giving Leeteuk a chance to speak, Keyth led the way, and the others followed in unison.

"Thank you." Just as he was leaving, Keyth clearly heard Han Geng's voice behind him, but he didn't turn around.

"Did you really escape back? What did the manager say?" Han Geng and Yoochun walked side by side on the street, wearing hats and masks due to the winter weather. Fans didn't expect them to be so bold, so they weren't noticed.

"Mm... We just informed Changmin, and he said he'd work out a plan with Yunho and Changmin, then we sneaked our passports and came back." Yoochun turned to look at Han Geng, and despite the mask covering half his face, Han Geng could imagine the mischievous grin from his curved eyes.

"You..." Gently nudging Yoochun's head, Han Geng tucked Yoochun's hand into his pocket, "How's your body? Did flying hurt? Why rush back?"

"In fact, Junsu insisted on coming back, saying Haechan's condition wasn't good. I was afraid something might happen to him, so... and I missed you, Han Geng."

"I missed you too." Gripping Yoochun's hand tighter, "How's it going with Yunho?"

"Mm, Yunho and I talked it out, decided to be good friends again."

"Coincidentally, I also talked it out with Keyth..." Han Geng laughed bitterly.

"No wonder Keyth earlier... I thought Keyth would forever resent me, making things difficult for me, because of me..."

"It's not like that, Keyth isn't that kind of person. He just isn't used to people ignoring him, or people willingly leaving like me," Han Geng continued laughing, but his eyes clouded over, "After explaining, he understands. My feelings and my presence may have initially troubled him, but over time, he'll forget, someone will fill my place, always will, you shouldn't forget he's Kim Keyth."

"But who will fill Keyth's place?" Yoochun looked at Han Geng with sorrow, "Don't say it's me, Han Geng, I don't believe it. During my time in Japan, some unexpected events occurred, leaving me quite confused. I often imagine how this misalignment began, whether it was wrong from the start or went wrong somewhere along the way. Now I realize many things aren't as they seem, nor do they progress in the direction we hope. Like when I loved Yunho, my emotions spiraled out of control, wanting to destroy together with him, but in the end, we parted ways. In my heart, no one can replace Yunho, because I don't want to try loving uncontrollably again, so Yunho is destined to be my deepest love... Han Geng, do you understand what I'm saying? I broke up with Yunho because I couldn't bear myself, Yunho also distanced himself for the same reason. But Keyth is different, one day he will settle down, and on that day, the person by his side should be you, I don't want to take that position..."

"Are you trying to persuade me to go back to Keyth now?"

"You need to know, over the years, I've never seen Keyth make concessions, but he conceded to me for you..."

"He's not petty."

"He is." Yoochun firmly gazed at Han Geng, "He gets mad when I snatch a burger from him, let alone when I took someone as important as you."

"Keyth can change."

"Then tell me, has your heart for Keyth truly changed?"

"..." Han Geng stopped walking, suddenly turning around and tightly embracing Yoochun, "Why push me back? We agreed..."

"Han Geng..." Slowly wrapping his arms around Han Geng's back, "Because I realized I've caused too much pain to too many people... Yunho is pained, Changmin is pained, Keyth is pained..." And Uta and Junsu too...

"What merits do I, Kim Yoochun, possess to receive so much love, only to let it shatter one by one in my hands... Han Geng, I don't want to lose you as the last one... I truly don't know what other variables the future holds..."

"Fool..." Gently stroking Yoochun's hair, "If I didn't have you by my side now, I might truly be suffering alone."

"Then promise me, if one day you want to turn back, don't hesitate because of me, alright?"

"Yoochun..."

"Promise me, please."

"Alright, but if the one wanting to turn back is you, please follow your heart too."
