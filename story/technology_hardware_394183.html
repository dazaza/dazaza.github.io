After discussing the promotion of online stores, let's now discuss the operation of online stores. Operation is the foundation of online stores and something that store owners must master.

Let's first discuss the topic everyone cares about most: how to increase store sales? Here, we'll discuss it from three aspects.

First: Product Reputation

A beautiful product picture doesn't necessarily mean a good product. Now, "Rayli Fashion" products look nice, all modeled by beauties. Prices aren't high either, but do you know that many buyers end up getting "cheap street-market clothes"? I believe many people have had this experience. Although the pictures look appealing, seeing the actual product is heartbreaking. If you're the store owner, you're in trouble: "bad review"!

So, when finding products, don't just focus on appearances; focus more on the product's real reputation. Good products don't necessarily require lots of beautiful models taking pictures. On the contrary, successful online stores often have relatively plain products.

Second: Product Price

Note—lower prices aren't always better; they should be proportional to product quality. Generally, people think lower-priced products sell better. Not necessarily true. What's most important is whether the price matches the product quality.

For example: one pair of women's shoes costs 15 yuan, another store charges 45 yuan. The pictures look similar. If you're the buyer, which would you choose? Customers' psychology is crucial—"I'd rather buy the more expensive one than worry." Can 15 yuan buy good shoes? Such cases are countless.

The product price is determined by the store owner, but requires careful consideration. For example, popular clothing online usually ranges between 50-200 yuan, so your range could be 70-150 yuan. Higher pricing might affect sales. Why not lower pricing? Because the main group buying online includes office workers, private computer users, company secretaries, retail salespeople, etc. These groups generally prefer fashionable and elegant or branded items. Products under 50 yuan are hard for them to even mention. For example, 15-yuan shoes might seem like rural market stall shoes to them, completely ignored!

Therefore, product pricing is determined by the store owner, and isn't necessarily better at lower prices.

Third: Product Variety

Many newly opened online stores want to sell a wide variety of products. Handbags, shoes, clothing, cosmetics—all included, making the new store seem chaotic and lively. This is actually wrong. It's recommended that product categories don't exceed two, or even just one. The reason is simple: too many product types make it seem like an unprofessional "general store." Buyers would rather go to specialized stores. No matter how good your product quality or low your price, it might still be ignored due to customer doubts. In fact, online stores are sometimes similar to physical stores. Look around at the physical stores near you, and you'll understand why specialty stores and chain stores are so popular.

These three basic aspects are fundamental. Now, let's further elaborate and specify. All these are cumulative experiences, and to deeply understand the art of operation, you still need to explore and discover more in practice. Below are 14 points summarized, hoping they will be helpful to everyone.

1. When selling goods online, most of the time buyers cannot see the physical item, so clear and attractive product photos and detailed descriptions are needed to attract buyers. If the product photos in your online store aren't clear and the descriptions are simple, it's hard to win buyers' favor.

2. The variety of goods in the online store should be complete, and updates should be quick. Here, completeness refers not to selling various goods, but to a single category of goods. For example, if we only sell women's clothing, then tops, pants, skirts, and blazers should all be available, allowing customers to mix and match freely. Also, new arrivals should be prominently displayed, but quantity shouldn't come at the expense of quality. Don't stop restocking because of poor sales. If the products in the online store are always the same old faces, it won't bring freshness, and buyers won't return after a few visits.

3. After-sales service should be thorough. After selling a product, contact the buyer immediately. Taking Taobao as an example, fill in the shipping order number as soon as possible after shipping. This allows the buyer to track logistics immediately when checking the order, giving them peace of mind right away. Alternatively, send the buyer a shipping notification email, ideally including a photo of the package slip, showing the buyer clear handwriting and specific tracking numbers, making them feel more secure and closer. This is important for attracting repeat customers.

4. Pay attention to the "credit rating" evaluation of the online store. Different platforms may have different credit rating evaluations, but they are generally similar. Here, we still take Taobao as an example: after completing a transaction, both parties have the obligation to evaluate each other's credit. High credit ratings are crucial for operating an online store, as all buyers will use credit ratings to decide whether to buy from you. Additionally, measures like "seven-day no-questions-asked returns" and "advance compensation" related to credit, if feasible, should be implemented. Sometimes these are more effective than so-called credit levels.

5. Honesty first. When operating online, honesty is the most important. Online, every seller has a record of honesty visible to buyers, showing previous sales records and evaluations from other buyers. Online records document the honesty of any seller, making it difficult for dishonest sellers to continue operating. Honest sellers can still find buyers even if their prices are higher.

6. In the early stages of opening a store, increase popularity. Consider setting up one-yuan auctions or selling at low prices to complete transactions. With more transaction records and good reviews, more buyers will trust your store and shop there. One-yuan auctions can quickly gather popularity, but be prepared to sell the item for one yuan. While conducting one-yuan auctions, it's best to run some advertisements to let everyone know about your "discount" policy here. The purpose of the one-yuan auction is to attract buyers to browse your other products while purchasing this item.

7. Implement some effective promotional strategies, offering regular discounts or gifts. Give returning customers a certain discount, or offer gifts or discounts when spending a certain amount, or provide free shipping, etc. In conjunction with events, change your signature to introduce the event, and announce this "promotion message" elsewhere. 8. Include small gifts. To increase positive evaluations, don't let the buyer know beforehand. When they receive the item and the gift, they will certainly be happy. The gift doesn't have to be valuable; it's a token of appreciation. Regardless of whether your buyer will shop again, treat them as a friend, face them sincerely, and you will definitely get a good return.

9. Calmly handle buyers who bid but don't purchase. It's normal to encounter such buyers when running an online store. As a seller, maintain a peaceful mindset and avoid conflicts with buyers. Sellers operate long-term, while buyers come and go freely. After real conflicts, the passive party is always the seller. All issues can be resolved through proper channels, only then can you face increasingly fierce online store competition.

10. Calmly handle bad reviews. Occasionally, due to your own reasons or other factors, bad reviews may appear in your credit record. Face it calmly, take corresponding remedial measures, or explain the real situation online. At the same time, strive to gain more good reviews to create a positive impression among buyers.

11. To determine whether a particular item will sell well, first search for it on the website. See how many people sell similar items, including identical or similar ones, their sales situation, product pricing, store design, product introduction, etc. These research and market analysis tasks are essential, ensuring "knowing yourself and knowing your enemy ensures victory in every battle."

12. Running an online store should be gradual. The first three months are typically for adapting to online store operations and gathering popularity. During this time, learn from experienced sellers, adjust your operations according to the market promptly, and accumulate some customers. These customers are your friends and the most connected people, and you must serve them well. Satisfied old customers will continuously introduce new customers. Gradually, the store's operations will improve positively.

13. "Online bidding" attracts more browsers than "fixed-price" sales. However, bidding might result in being sold at a low price. In such situations, some store owners invite friends to participate in the bidding. This can raise the price to an acceptable amount for the seller, and if the buyer bids too low, the friend can win the bid. This behavior is non-credible, and it's recommended not to adopt it. As long as you operate honestly, the store's popularity will increase.

14. When running an online store, you should be observant and well-informed, accurately grasping market trends. While capturing information online, take time to visit physical markets occasionally and don't limit your perspective to the internet alone.

Women's love for beauty has been unchanged from ancient times to the present and is becoming increasingly intense. Therefore, the prospects of the cosmetics market are extremely broad! From the current consumer base and sales volume, cosmetics undoubtedly rank first. If you're asking what to sell in an online store that sells well and makes money, this is the top choice!

Disadvantages: 1. Expiry and spoilage issues; 2. Glass bottles are not very convenient for transportation.

Second Category: Women's clothing and shoes

Compared to cosmetics, women's clothing and shoes have both advantages and disadvantages. The advantage is that they don't have as many concerns as cosmetics: shelf life, transportation, etc. Women's clothing and shoes are extremely convenient to transport and don't have expiry issues. Their disadvantage lies in the limitations of sizes and models, and they are more particular about styles, which makes it harder for women's clothing and shoes to retain customers compared to cosmetics. Women's clothing and shoes come in a wide variety of styles, and no matter how big your store is, it's impossible to include all styles and designs. If customers browse and don't find something they particularly like, they will go to other stores.

Advantages: 1. Extremely large consumer base; 2. High demand; 3. No expiry or spoilage issues.

Disadvantages: 1. Size issues; 2. High requirements for styles and brands, requiring sellers to have a discerning eye.

Nowadays, almost everyone has a mobile phone, most people have an MP4, and many people have a computer, illustrating the vastness of the electronic market. The disadvantage is the issue of after-sales service and repairs, which is quite troublesome, and the buyer may be reluctant to bear the round-trip postage.
