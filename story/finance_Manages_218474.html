The "revolution" of supply and demand informatization will be carried out to the end!

by uandm on 2009-08-05 15:46:47

The informatization of supply and demand has become "pervasive," affecting almost every field such as government affairs, communications, urban emergency response, public security, education, culture, health, labor insurance, human resources, fire protection, finance and taxation, transportation, financial insurance... Almost any field has embarked on a path of categorized informatization and is becoming increasingly reliant on it.

Informatization has greatly promoted the development of economy and society, making this world richer and more fascinating.

For industrial enterprises' development, informatization is particularly important. It plays a practical and profound role in enhancing enterprise management levels, promoting modernized management, transforming business mechanisms, establishing modern enterprise systems, effectively reducing costs, accelerating technological progress, strengthening market competitiveness, and improving economic efficiency.

Informatization helps enterprises find "gold."

In recent years, the Kaiping municipal Party committee and municipal government have attached great importance to advancing enterprise informatization. Currently, among Kaiping's large-scale industrial enterprises, most have their own websites. More than 80% of these enterprises can widely apply information technology in office automation, computer-aided design/manufacturing (CAD/CAM), financial management, inventory management, e-commerce, product data management, etc., with the degree of informatization increasing year by year.

Kaiping's enterprises that widely apply informatization use computer technology in all aspects of production, supply, sales, personnel, finance, materials, product research and development, and the entire production process. They have completely摆脱ed traditional craft methods, thereby maximizing improvements in enterprise management level, quality level, and product competitiveness, while also significantly shortening the product design and manufacturing cycle. This has led to significant improvements in the enterprise's economic benefits, technical innovation capabilities, market competition capabilities, and risk resistance capabilities.

Mr. Li Weiqiao, Director of the Information Industry Bureau of Kaiping City, said in an interview with reporters that to promote Kaiping's enterprise informatization work, besides establishing exemplary enterprises and upgrading advantageous traditional industries, there should also be a focus on developing modern information services industry. This will help promote industrial optimization and upgrading, transform the mode of economic growth, and follow the guidelines issued last year by Jiangmen City regarding the acceleration of the development of modern information services industry. Combining this with Kaiping's situation, we should organize excellent projects from Kaiping to participate in the bidding for special funds for the development of modern information service industry across the province. Furthermore, using the enterprise services of the three major industry innovation centers in Kaiping as engines, we should vigorously promote the construction of specialized industrial bases (parks) and projects with high competitiveness and agglomeration effects, such as modern information service parks, "digital family" service bases, and public information service platforms, and cultivate a number of key enterprises in the modern information service industry.
