Beauty and Hairdressing: Long-lasting curls after perming

With winter approaching, can parties be far behind? The exciting Halloween, the warm and romantic Christmas, New Year's Day, and various company year-end parties...just thinking about it is thrilling and full of anticipation. Besides starting to search for stunning outfits, the process of pre-party beauty maintenance is also urgent. To make a dazzling appearance, beauty and hair care definitely have their secrets!

1. Method to Eliminate Skin Fatigue

These days, with busy work schedules and lack of sleep, your complexion may appear dull or even slightly swollen. Here’s an exclusive 10-minute face mask recipe from Lianyungang Beauty and Hairdressing School [http://www.lygainisi.org], which can immediately restore your skin's vitality.

Ingredients: 1/4 avocado, 1/4 banana, 1 tablespoon of pure yogurt

Method: Mix the avocado, banana, and yogurt into a paste, apply it on a damp face, and gently massage to promote blood circulation. For better results, after massaging for five minutes, place a sheet mask on your face, leave it for another five minutes, then wash off. Follow up with a moisturizing cream.

Since the oleic acid in avocados has excellent hydrating properties, bananas are rich in vitamins A, B, C, and E, and lactic acid in yogurt promotes cell metabolism, restoring skin elasticity. Combining these three ingredients, within 10 minutes, you can see miraculous effects—fatigue fades away, and your skin regains its radiant glow!

2. Acne Emergency Treatment

Annoying pimples seem glaringly obvious in the mirror, but don't worry! Using erythromycin eye ointment or toothpaste can quickly remove redness from pimples. Steps include: Squeeze a little toothpaste onto a cotton swab, press it on the pimple for 10-15 seconds, or until the redness and swelling subside. Then apply concealer, and your skin will instantly feel smooth again. Even if it's just a temporary illusion, it's the best way to fix things before the party.

3. Quick Fresh Breath Method

After beautifying your lips, if you're experiencing bad breath due to poor body metabolism recently, it's quite simple—you don’t necessarily need to carry breath fresheners all the time. Drinking a glass of lemon water, chewing some tea leaves dry, or eating an apple can instantly refresh your mouth.

4. Instant Silky Hair Method

In winter, sometimes hair becomes oily due to dehydration. If there's a sudden party and no time to wash your hair, and your hair looks greasy and limp, ruining your overall look, don't worry. Spray a clean conditioner on your hair, carefully wipe it clean with a towel, and comb it out. Then lower your head, let your hair fall naturally, carefully spray styling gel only at the roots, and blow-dry it. Yes, you'll immediately feel your hairstyle restored to a soft and smooth, flowing state.

5. Nail Polish Repair Method

Doing a detailed manicure really takes a lot of time, and if you simply can't spare two hours but your nails have chipped at the tips, take a cotton swab, dip it in nail polish remover and rub it on the chipped area, carefully smoothing out the edges. Then apply the same color nail polish on the peeled area, rinse quickly with cold water to speed up drying. To prevent the nail polish from peeling easily, you can also apply a layer of topcoat after finishing.

6. Full-body Care Method

Yes, even in the coldest weather, who can resist the feeling of skirts fluttering? If you've completed the above five emergency measures and still have an hour left, it's enough time for a full-body care session!

Fill a bathtub with water at the most comfortable 40 degrees Celsius for your skin, as too high a temperature will dry out surface cells, and too low will affect skin relaxation. Add 200 grams of salt, 200 grams of white wine, 2 tablespoons of honey, and a bag of milk to the water, stirring evenly. Salt helps quickly restore energy and reduce skin swelling; white wine makes the skin smooth, soft, and elastic; honey helps tighten pores and eliminate fine wrinkles; milk makes the skin white and delicate.

After 20 minutes, rinse thoroughly. Now, from head to toe, you’re flawless and ready to step out confidently, becoming the most dazzling queen of the party!
