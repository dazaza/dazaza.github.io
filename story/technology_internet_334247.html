Although drought disasters do not cause immediate huge losses like floods and earthquakes, they can still result in very significant economic losses and many social problems. Let's take a look at the following pictures; these are all our fellow countrymen who have also extended helping hands during earthquakes and snowstorms. Now that they are suffering from disasters, what should we do?

Use of donations (tentative, subject to adjustment based on actual site assessment; if adjustments are made, we will release them immediately): According to our communication with local areas in Yunnan and based on our experience, a donation of 200 yuan can sponsor a poor family (calculated as four people) to purchase basic drinking water for two months. Ten percent of the total donation amount will be used as project execution and management fees.

1+1 Pairing Sponsorship Standards:

A donation of 200 yuan can sponsor one poor family in Yunnan to purchase drinking water for two months (donors can obtain the contact information of the assisted family).

Donation Process:

Please fill in the recipient's (donor's) name, address, postal code, telephone number, and mobile phone number completely so that we can issue and mail donation invoices and other materials to you.

Donation Usage Process:

1. The China Poverty Relief Foundation signs a project implementation cooperation agreement with the poverty alleviation office of the project county (to be determined next week) and transfers the sponsorship funds to the bank account of the county poverty alleviation office;

2. The county poverty alleviation office reports the list of project villages to the China Poverty Relief Foundation;

3. Villagers' democratic evaluation meetings organized by the village committee or villager groups democratically select the sponsored objects;

4. Publicize the proposed list of sponsored objects;

5. After the public announcement period ends, distribute sponsorship funds to eligible poor families;

6. The head of the poor family signs the receipt for the sponsorship funds;

7. The county poverty alleviation office stamps the receipt for the sponsorship funds with the unit seal and mails it to the China Poverty Relief Foundation;

8. After the project is completed, the China Poverty Relief Foundation releases a project execution report (list of donors, list of beneficiaries, related pictures, related receipts, etc.).

Services Provided by the China Poverty Relief Foundation for Donors:

1. For donations over 50 yuan, issue and mail donation invoices;

2. For donations over 200 yuan, donors can help one poor family in Yunnan buy drinking water or food for two months, and donors can obtain the contact information of the assisted family;

3. For donations over 10,000 yuan, donors can sponsor one poor village;

4. For donations over 100,000 yuan, donors can sponsor one poor township, invite donors to visit the project area for an on-site inspection, meet and exchange with beneficiaries;

For donations over 500,000 yuan, hold a donation ceremony for donors, invite donors to visit the project area for an on-site inspection, meet and exchange with beneficiaries.

How to Ensure Donations Are Used Reasonably, Effectively, and Transparently:

1. One-to-One Pairing Model

A donor donating 200 yuan can sponsor one poor family, and the donor can obtain the contact information of the assisted person and actively contact them. This is the most effective supervision of the China Poverty Relief Foundation by donors;

2. Actively and Real-Time Publish Project Progress and Donation Dynamics, Actively Accept Supervision:

The China Poverty Relief Foundation will actively and real-time publish project progress and donation dynamics through the official website of the organization and the official website of the event, actively accepting supervision from donors, society, and media.
