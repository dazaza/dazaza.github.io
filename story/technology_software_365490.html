[Special Topic] Synchronous Weekly Report 20100920: Dropbox, Cloud Disk, WebQQ2.0, etc.

by xdash on 2010-09-21 17:58:27

1. **Dropbox Launches New Mobile Client, Increases User Storage Limits**

This week, Dropbox launched new clients for iPhone, Android, and iPad, with a BlackBerry client currently in the works (official announcement). Additionally, the storage limit obtainable through inviting friends has been significantly increased from 5GB to 10GB. By inviting friends to register for Dropbox, both parties can receive an additional 250MB of storage until the cap is reached. (My referral link)

Website: [https://www.dropbox.com] (Requires proxy)

2. **360 Acquires "Cloud Disk" Related Domains, Enters Value-Added Cloud Storage Market**

September 14 News: According to insiders, 360 Company recently successfully acquired three double-pinyin domains related to "cloud disk": yunpan.com, yunpan.cn, and yunpan.com.cn. A spokesperson for 360 stated that Chairman Zhou Hongyi had previously promised to launch two value-added services by the end of the year, one of which will be cloud storage.

News Link: [http://www.techweb.com.cn/news/2010-09-14/681668.shtml]

---

3. **Tencent Launches WebQQ 2.0, Challenges WebOS**

This week, Tencent quietly launched its browser-based client product—WebQQ 2.0.

"Different from the previous version, WebQQ 2.0 emphasizes customizable desktop experiences and one-stop service concepts. Besides offering instant messaging functionality, it integrates various Tencent products and applications such as QQ client, Soso search, Tencent Browser, QQ Space, QQ Music, email, Weibo, and input methods. WebQQ 2.0 comes pre-installed with over 40 applications and five desktop themes. Users can add, manage applications, batch-manage contacts, use Tencent Cloud Pinyin Input Method, and edit personal profiles." (via)

While people are amazed by the powerful integration capabilities of WebQQ 2.0, its Gmail application was suspected of being a "phishing program" that logs into and accesses users' Gmail information without permission. Tencent promptly removed this feature.

Website: [http://web2.qq.com/]

4. **Data Bank (DBank) Sponsors YYeTs Subtitle Group with Unlimited Cloud Storage**

This week, Data Bank, a well-known domestic synchronization tool, officially provided unlimited space and bandwidth sponsorship for YYeTs Subtitle Group. YYeTs plans to upload over 4,000 episodes of foreign TV series, more than 600 foreign films, 12 English course series, and 1 German course series.

Detailed Report: [http://www.yyets.net/thread-300617-1-1.html]

5. **Titanium Backup v.3.4.6**

Titanium Backup is a tool for backing up Android apps and their settings, supporting automatic backups. Reviewed by Gfan.com.

Related Link: [http://www.gfan.com/app/tools/201009163590.html]

6. **Sina Weibo RSS Auto-Sync Assistant**

A third-party developed tool for Sina Weibo that automatically forwards customized RSS updates to Sina Weibo, supporting keyword filtering. After using this, you are no longer limited to binding only one blog to Sina Weibo.

Website: [http://sinaapp.csdn.net/rss]

7. **Personal Cloud Data Search Engine: Greplin**

Greplin can be used to search common cloud services. After registration and authorization of your cloud services, you can use it to search across platforms like Facebook, Dropbox, Twitter, and Google Voice.

Website: [https://www.greplin.com/]

---

8. **A Heap of Microblogs**

AHeapOfMicroblogs is a tool developed by an individual that supports real-time synchronization to 17 mainstream domestic microblogging platforms, using OAuth authentication. Official detailed help and FAQs are available.

Website: [http://t.yunmengze.net/]
