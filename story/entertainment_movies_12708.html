2006 China's Top Ten Free Movie Websites * Places to Watch Free Movies

by happy1022 on 2007-02-04 23:14:15

First place: Ultimate Audio and Video (http://www.tk4479.com)

It might be the pioneer of free movie websites in China. Although the domain name is a bit hard to remember, its good service has earned a solid reputation among many free movie websites. You can watch the fastest and latest free movies here. However, you often need to "grab lines" as there are limits on the number of people who can watch online.

Second place: Cool Entertainment Broadband (http://www.91q.com)

Simple registration allows you to freely watch hundreds of classic movies. If you recommend three friends to visit this website, you can get free weekly subscription (accumulative) services. The website has sufficient resources, updates quickly, and provides quality usually seen in paid services, so it's highly recommended!

Third place: Kamun Free Movies (http://www.kamun.com)

Kamun Free Movies, all resources are shared by netizens, and watched through P2P download and on-demand. The speed is very fast, but you need to install the client.

Fourth place: Yu Ruyi (http://vod.ruyi.com)

Unicom video, requires relatively high configuration, otherwise it cannot be played. Also, registration is needed to watch.

Fifth place: Century Frontline (http://www.avl.com.cn)

A comprehensive entertainment website run by Guangzhou Telecom. It has been providing free movies for a long time with pretty good speed, but registration is required and you also need to "grab lines" to watch. Registration will also get you a nice video email!

Sixth place: POCO Movies (http://movie.poco.cn)

Poco Free Movies, a site for free movie exchanges. The website itself provides free movie downloads, and you can also download movies from other people's computers via P2P, but first you need to download and install their dedicated software.

Seventh place: KAN51 Online Mini Movies (http://www.kan51.net)

China's largest free online mini-movie website. The movie playback speed is good. If you want to relax and watch mini-movies or variety show movies online, KAN51 is your best choice!

Eighth place: BTChina Union (http://www.btchina.net)

China's most complete and largest free BT movie download website. There are many movie torrent links, and the download speed is fast. This is the top recommended website for free BT movie downloads.

Ninth place: eMule·China (http://www.emule.org.cn)

eMule is a P2P software developed by foreigners. Connecting to different servers allows you to download different movie files, and anyone can permanently install and use it for free. Moreover, you can download many movies that cannot be downloaded domestically, hehe!

Tenth place: Super VOD (http://www.powervod.com)

A fairly good online movie website. Registration is required to watch, and after registration, a password will be sent to your email. The speed is quite good.
