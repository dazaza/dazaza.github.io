It's true, they are in a relationship.

Although the parties involved deny it every time they are asked, Zhang Han and Zheng Shuang's relationship has actually become an open secret in the industry. Perhaps because of this, producer Li Xiang of the hit drama "Accidentally in Love" accidentally confirmed this matter during an interview in Changsha on the night of the 26th: "No matter who Zhang Han collaborates with, one thing will not change, and that is, he and Zheng Shuang are the ones in love."

Actually, since their collaboration in the two parts of "Meteor Rain," rumors about Zhang Han and Zheng Shuang have been circulating. When they appeared together on entertainment programs, they were often teased. In Hunan TV's New Year drama "Accidentally in Love," newcomer Jiang Kaitung pairs up with Zhang Han as lovers, which was harshly criticized by the fans of Zhang Han and Zheng Shuang.

Regarding this, Li Xiang naturally supports Jiang Kaitung: "She is a newly graduated actress, and the pressure of participating in this drama was quite significant. When I discussed the script with the actors, Zhang Han and others were very confident, but she was the only one who cried, crying very sadly." She believes that Jiang Kaitung successfully completed her role: "She exceeded my expectations, and this is all due to her own efforts."

As for Jiang Kaitung and Zhang Han being attacked by fans, Li Xiang couldn't help but defend her protégé: "I feel that sometimes fans are too cruel. The reason why Zheng Shuang is compared is certainly because of Zhang Han. But think about it, Zhang Han is an actor, today he acts with Jiang Kaitung, and in the future, he will act with other actresses." She joked that choosing different heroines was also Zhang Han's idea: "Unless the fans hope that Zhang Han forever collaborates with Zheng Shuang, this would require them to open their own production company, which is not realistic, right?" Subsequently, she made a shocking statement: "In fact, there is one thing that will not change, and that is, he and Zheng Shuang are the ones in love. This will not change because he acts with others. Life is life, work is work, they should be separated."
