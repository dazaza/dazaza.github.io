Key points for autumn skin care

by yuyu2319 on 2011-08-29 16:10:24

In the autumn, due to the dry weather, Swiss sheep placenta can cause skin dryness and peeling. In fact, the small trick of skincare in autumn is moisturizing plus whitening.

For oily skin, the first thing to do is to hydrate the skin. Experts suggest that people with oily skin might as well use oil-removing astringent water, which can not only shrink pores but also hydrate the skin. Meanwhile, for oily skin with excessive sebum secretion, large pores, and prone to clogging, it has the effect of inhibiting sebum secretion and anti-inflammation.

The autumn care of neutral skin also needs the correct method: after cleansing, use oil-free moisturizing lotion to refresh the skin, then apply nourishing astringent milk containing olive oil components to form an oxygen-permeable protective film on the surface of the skin. This layer does not allow moisture to evaporate, both moisturizes the skin and resists pollution.

Compared with oily and neutral skin, dry skin requires more careful care. In autumn, dry skin is suitable for using moisturizing liquid to replenish water because the pH balance of dry skin is easily destroyed, and moisturizing liquid can repair the skin's pH value. At the same time, match with appropriate astringent cream to supplement nutrition to the skin, making it moist and not dry. In addition, experts also specially remind people with dry skin that due to frowning, talking, and other facial muscle movements, people with dry skin are prone to false wrinkles when the skin becomes dry in autumn. Therefore, it is necessary to drink more water, eat more vegetables and fruits, and eat less spicy food.

Although the ultraviolet rays in autumn are not as intense as in midsummer, they can increase melanin in the skin, darken the complexion, penetrate deeper into the skin, damage the structure of the skin, and make the skin age. Therefore, the selected skincare products should ideally have both whitening, moisturizing, and nutritional functions to meet the various needs of the skin in autumn.

Top1 Popular - Collagen

Collagen, also known as collagen, is the most abundant and widely distributed protein in animals. Its main function is to maintain the shape and structure of the skin and tissues, and it is also an important raw material for repairing damaged tissues. Therefore, we can say without exaggeration that collagen is the scaffold for all life existence.

Beauty effects: Prevents skin wrinkles and maintains smooth, elastic skin; effectively eliminates skin spots; promotes skin regeneration, restores elasticity, improves hair quality.

Product forms: Collagen oral solution, collagen powder, collagen capsules

Top2 Popular - Vitamin E

Vitamin E is also known as tocopherol. It consists of four types of tocopherols: α-tocopherol, β-tocopherol, γ-tocopherol, and δ-tocopherol. Speaking of vitamin E, I believe everyone must be familiar with it. In daily life, middle-aged and elderly people often take vitamin E to delay aging, while women prefer cosmetics with added vitamin E for beauty and tender skin.

Beauty effects: Has antioxidant properties, delays cell aging, reduces lipofuscin formation, and improves skin elasticity; after injury, taking vitamin E internally can effectively reduce scar residue (it can also be used externally).

Product forms: Vitamin E capsules

Top3 Popular - Grape Seed

Grape seed is a natural powerful free radical scavenger that removes free radicals from the body; fights oxidation, with an antioxidant effect 20 times stronger than vitamin C and 50 times stronger than vitamin E; has anti-tumor and anti-mutation effects; anti-aging; skin health and beauty effects.

Beauty effects: Prevents skin wrinkles, prevents melanin deposition, effectively whitens the skin, and reduces scar formation.

Product forms: Grape seed capsules, grape seed beverages

Top4 Popular - Propolis

Propolis is a resin (gum) collected by bees from plant buds or tree trunks, mixed with secretions from the bees' mouthparts, and processed together with pollen and beeswax to form a gel-like substance, acting as an umbrella for the beehive. Propolis is a natural antibiotic that contains rich and unique bioactive substances.

Beauty effects: Propolis has excellent antioxidant effects and detoxification, regulates endocrine, etc.; it can eliminate acne, decompose pigmentation spots, reduce wrinkles, and delay aging.

Product forms: Propolis capsules, propolis liquid

Top5 Popular - Aloe Vera

According to scientific research, it has been found that aloe vera contains many components that are beneficial for human skin, with good nutritional and moisturizing effects, few irritations, and comfort after use. It has certain therapeutic effects on rough skin, facial wrinkles, scars, freckles, acne, etc. Therefore, its extracts can be used as cosmetic additives to formulate sunscreens, shower gels, etc.

Beauty effects: Improves rough skin, accelerates wound healing, soothes sunburn and mild burns, improves sunspots, pigmentation, and endocrine imbalance; regulates gastrointestinal function, lubricates intestines, promotes bowel movements, and facilitates the elimination of toxins within the body; helps the skin produce and retain moisture, soothing dry skin.

Product forms: Aloe vera capsules, aloe vera juice

Top6 Popular - Pearl Powder

The effective components in pearl powder, such as shellfish protein amino acids (collagen), glycine, natural methionine, and taurine, after being absorbed by the human body, can accelerate skin metabolism, perform overall conditioning and maintenance of the entire body's skin. It promotes the synthesis of new cells, continuously supplements nutrients to the skin's surface, and maintains the normal secretion of female estrogen.

Beauty effects: Makes the skin smooth, delicate, elastic, and delays aging. Regulates endocrine and removes pigmentation and brown spots in women.

Product forms: Pearl powder, pearl powder capsules
