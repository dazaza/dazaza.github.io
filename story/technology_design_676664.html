In the producer price index, the production materials prices decreased by 0.1% month-over-month, among which, the mining industry prices dropped by 0.8%, raw material industry prices increased by 0.2%, and processing industry prices fell by 0.2%. The living materials prices increased by 0.1%, among which, food prices went up by 0.2%, clothing prices increased by 0.1%, regular daily necessities prices remained stable, and durable consumer goods prices rose by 0.2%.

The industrial producer purchase price index reflects the change trend and variation amplitude of purchase prices of input products for industrial enterprises.

In the producer price index, production materials prices increased by 0.3% year-over-year, among which, mining industry prices rose by 6.1%, raw material industry prices increased by 1.7%, and processing industry prices dropped by 0.9%. Living materials prices increased by 2.1%, among which, food prices rose by 3.5%, clothing prices increased by 3.1%, regular daily necessities prices went up by 1.6%, and durable consumer goods prices fell by 0.2%.

In the industrial producer purchase prices, black metal material prices decreased by 1.1% year-over-year, non-ferrous metal material prices dropped by 2.5%, fuel power category prices increased by 6.6%, agricultural by-products category prices went up by 3.9%, textile raw material category prices increased by 2.6%, building materials and non-metal category prices rose by 2.5%.

The industrial producer purchase price index reflects the change trend and variation amplitude of purchase prices of input products for industrial enterprises.

In January 2012, the national industrial producer price increased by 0.7% year-over-year (Note: a 26-month low), and decreased by 0.1% month-over-month. The industrial producer purchase price increased by 2.0% year-over-year and dropped by 0.3% month-over-month.

Statistical Scope: The industrial producer price statistical survey covers the prices of more than 11,000 kinds of industrial products in 1,702 basic classifications; the industrial producer purchase price statistical survey covers the prices of more than 6,000 kinds of industrial products in over 900 basic classifications.

In the industrial producer purchase prices, fuel and energy category prices increased by 0.2% month-over-month, agricultural by-products category prices remained unchanged, black metal material category prices dropped by 1.1%, non-ferrous metal material category prices fell by 0.8%, and chemical raw material category prices decreased by 0.5%.

The industrial producer price includes both the industrial producer factory price and the industrial producer purchase price.

3. Survey Method: The industrial producer price survey adopts a combined method of key surveys and typical surveys. Enterprises with annual main business revenue over 20 million yuan use the key survey method; enterprises with annual main business revenue below 20 million yuan use the typical survey method. The industrial producer price survey involves nearly 60,000 industrial enterprises in more than 400 cities nationwide.
