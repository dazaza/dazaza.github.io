Fortune 500 Delegation Focuses on Low-carbon Development in Anhui Province

by ditan360 on 2010-06-28 16:36:55

Low Carbon Network exclusive Recently, the International Energy Conservation Association with the theme of "promoting the development of regional ecological economy, and allowing low carbon and market innovation to achieve a win-win situation", led a delegation composed of Fortune Global 500 companies and multinational corporations to visit Anhui for regional economic project cooperation and dialogue. The delegates also attended the opening ceremony of the sixth China International Huishang Conference. The delegation members that visited Anhui this time included senior executives from ABB Group, General Electric (GE), Dell, Dryvit, Pfizer Animal Health Group, Cohen Group and other enterprises.

During the seminars held separately with the Anhui Provincial Government, Hefei Municipal Government, Wuhu Municipal Government, and the National High-tech Zone Management Committee of Hefei, the main topics discussed were: First, the green development of Anhui Province and the promotion of industrial upgrading and transformation. How to effectively attract Fortune Global 500 companies and multinational corporations under the new circumstances, and jointly build the sustainable development of regional economy and environment; Second, strengthening the cooperation between Anhui Province and non-governmental organizations, leveraging professional strength and international resource platforms, implementing effective government guidance measures and industrial support, promoting the improvement of market mechanisms related to energy conservation and environmental protection industries and low-carbon economy from multiple aspects and angles, forming a soft environment and soft power; Third, how Fortune Global 500 companies and multinational corporations can marry ecology with the economy while achieving market investment benefits, making environmental protection and development win-win, and the layout plans of various delegation member enterprises in Anhui Province, etc., a series of fruitful exchanges and discussions were carried out.

During the enterprise and project inspections, the responsible persons for the inspections from ABB Group and General Electric (GE) both expressed that they had already found cooperative projects, and the next step would be to accelerate the implementation of results. The Asia-Pacific region head of Pfizer Animal Health Group expressed the intention to invest in projects in Anhui, while the vice president of Dryvit (China) and the chief representative of Cohen Group in China also stated that the results of this visit to Anhui with the International Energy Conservation Association were abundant, laying the groundwork for the enterprise's future development work in Anhui.

A thirteen-member delegation led by Secretary-General and CEO Junyang Li of the International Energy Conservation Association, consisting of Fortune Global 500 companies, multinational corporations, association project officials, expert representatives, etc., also inspected Hefei City, Wuhu City, Chery Automobile Company, Hefei Natural Thermal Power Co., Ltd., Guozhen Environmental Protection Company, etc. During the three-day itinerary, they were warmly received and welcomed by the Anhui Provincial Government, Hefei Municipal Government, Wuhu Municipal Government, and the National High-tech Zone Management Committee of Hefei. The delegation members also felt the potential of Anhui Province's regional economic development and the high regard of all levels of government for the energy conservation and environmental protection industry.

During the seminars with leaders at all levels in Anhui Province, Secretary-General Junyang Li expressed: This visit to Anhui and the inspection of relevant cities saw Anhui Province adhere to an innovative development strategy, clearly realizing the transformation and upgrading in承接industrial transfer. As a national strategy, the Wanjiang Economic Belt as a demonstration area for industrial transfer, establishing dual-core and regional division mechanisms, realizing industrial upgrading and then transferring during the process of accepting transfers, fully demonstrating the foresight and wisdom of the leadership of the Anhui Provincial Government.

Secretary-General Junyang Li also provided corresponding suggestions:

First, transform the Wanjiang Economic Belt into an engine for the development of central regional economies, arranging different cities in combinations to complement each other's strengths, form a united force, and design it into a regional competitive advantage entity that connects internally and externally on China's economic map;

Second, increase scientific and technological investment, promote low-carbon technology innovation and establish an independent intellectual property rights R&D system, while building a regional low-carbon technology R&D, cooperation, and transfer platform to realize regional economic and technological resource sharing;

Third, mobilize the enthusiasm of private capital investment in green industries, using market means to give birth to new economic entities, and support the development of energy-saving and environmental protection technology enterprises;

Fourth, establish government-enterprise linkage innovation, construct an information sharing mechanism, and realize the network integration of the Wanjiang Economic Belt region;

Fifth, strengthen the government-led economic cooperation and coordination functions among enterprises, improve the talent supply system at all levels, effectively reduce development costs, and enhance the competitiveness of landing enterprises;

Sixth, take a group of leading enterprises as the backbone to develop, deepen division of labor on a professional basis, effectively integrate and extend the industrial chain, form a specialized supporting system, and drive the adjustment of the regional economic industrial structure.

Secretary-General Junyang Li also expressed that the International Energy Conservation Association will rely on its advantages in domestic and international resources, experts, and professional organizational platforms to fully support the construction of the Wanjiang urban belt as a demonstration area for industrial transfer, as well as make the energy conservation and environmental protection industry a pillar industry for Anhui Province's economic development.

Members of the delegation composed of Fortune Global 500 companies and multinational corporations indicated that they will further combine the professional and international advantages of the development organization platform of the International Energy Conservation Association, establish a long-term mechanism, and with closer relationships, achieve win-win outcomes in the process of promoting the sustainable development of China's economy and environment.
