Invisible orthodontics in Nanjing without braces.

by 454kq on 2011-05-27 10:59:44

Nanjing invisible orthodontics without braces, the advantage of no ligature bracket of invisible orthodontic treatment is that it is not affecting aesthetic in the traditional orthodontic process without wires and brackets, etc., which is favored by those who beautify teeth. The orthodontic expert of stomatology department of Nanjing PLA 454 Hospital introduced that this invisible orthodontic technology inherits the traditional concept of orthodontic treatment for dental arch malocclusion. It is a perfect combination of modern dentistry, computer aided three-dimensional diagnosis, personalized design and digital molding technology.

Compared with the traditional orthodontic appliance for dental arch malocclusion, invisible orthodontic technology does not need brackets and wires but adopts a series of invisible orthodontic appliances which are made of safe elastic transparent polymer materials so that the orthodontic process is almost completed without being noticed by others, not affecting normal life and social intercourse. Meanwhile, without the trouble of bonding brackets and adjusting archwires, clinical operation is greatly simplified, saving time and effort in the whole orthodontic process.

Invisible orthodontics is a new high technology in the field of international orthodontics. Although invisible orthodontics has good effect and prominent advantages, it is not suitable for everyone and is currently mainly used for mild to moderate tooth malocclusion.

Advantages of ligature-free invisible transparent orthodontics:

Aesthetic: almost completely invisible. You will complete your orthodontic treatment without others knowing, solving many patients' aesthetic concerns about orthodontic treatment.

Comfortable: without traditional orthodontic devices such as brackets and wires, the orthodontic process is no longer painful. Minimal stimulation and discomfort to periodontal tissues.

Convenient: self-removable, not affecting socializing, eating, exercising, etc.. Fewer follow-up visits, saving your valuable time.

Clean: easy maintenance of oral hygiene, no need to worry about gingivitis, tooth demineralization, discoloration and other problems.

Convenience: short and effective treatment course, generally about 9-12 months of treatment can be completed. Traditional methods take about 8 hours of treatment time, while invisible orthodontics only takes 3 hours.

Ligature-free invisible orthodontics is suitable for people with the following problems, including individual tooth malposition, crowded teeth, crooked teeth, tooth gaps, etc. Those who wish to have personalized design are more suitable for ligature-free invisible orthodontics.
