CCTV exposes Samsung mobile phone repair profiteering: RMB 8 cost, RMB 1650 repair fee

by anonymous on 2013-10-11 19:12:53

Recently, CCTV News exposed the内幕 of excessive profits in mobile phone outer screen repairs. Xiao Huang's Samsung Note2 screen in Beijing was cracked, with only the outer glass damaged, yet the Samsung after-sales service quoted 1650 yuan, requiring the undamaged LCD screen beneath to be replaced as well. A small repair shop could replace the glass for a minimum of 390 yuan. A screen manufacturer revealed that good quality outer screen glass sells for just 7 or 8 yuan, indicating astonishing profits in mobile phone repairs.

The mobile phone touch screen is mainly divided into two parts: the top layer is the cover plate, which primarily serves a protective function; the bottom is the core component, the liquid crystal touch screen. At Samsung's authorized after-sales service center, even though only the outer glass cover was cracked, customers were still required to replace the entire liquid crystal touch screen, naturally at the consumer's expense.

Below is the text version of the report:

For the same mobile phone, with a cracked LCD screen, the cost at Samsung's after-sales service center is so high. So, how much does a mobile phone LCD screen actually cost? Does the after-sales service repair of Samsung mobile phones' LCD touch screens involve excessive profits? To answer this question, reporters secretly visited Guangdong, a production hub for mobile phone LCD displays.

This factory in Dongguan is producing LCD display glass cover plates. With a factory area of nearly three hundred square meters, it isn't large in this industry. So, what is the ex-factory price of the LCD screen surface glass cover plate here?

Reporter: How much does a glass screen like this sell for?

A certain mobile phone LCD screen producer: Good ones? We sell them for seven or eight yuan.

Reporter: What about in bulk?

A certain mobile phone LCD screen producer: The price is the same for bulk purchases. We supply tens of thousands of pieces at a time.

Reporter: Can your hardness reach the hardness of an original Apple screen?

A certain mobile phone LCD screen producer: It can reach it! How couldn't it? It can even exceed it, it's just a matter of production time.

Glass cover plates costing seven or eight yuan, including shipping fees, amount to around ten yuan in costs. Even at the cheapest mobile phone repair shop in Beijing, replacing a glass cover plate costs 390 yuan. This means the profit is astonishingly over thirty times.

The reporter secretly visited a listed company in Shenzhen producing LCD screen cover plates. Its cover plate price is twelve yuan per piece, and the price of screens with added etched circuit boards on the cover plate was similarly unexpected.

A sales manager from a certain listed company: If I calculate the brushed film at twenty yuan, plus the original piece at around ten yuan, it doesn't even cost ten yuan because we produce these industrially, so it's more than twenty yuan.

Reporter: How much does one of your genuine screens cost?

A certain mobile phone LCD screen producer: Genuine ones? They sell for forty to fifty yuan. For example, the original Samsung factory ones sell for forty-five to fifty yuan per piece. We sell them, like this white one, at ten yuan per piece.

Based on the prices of these two companies' glass cover plates, the profit margin for replacing cover plates is at least ten times. Reporters found in Beijing that the crack screen repair fee for Samsung phones is mostly around 600 yuan at most ordinary mobile phone repair shops, sometimes exceeding a thousand yuan, clearly exposing excessive profits.

Since it's only the phone cover plate that's cracked, and technically it's possible to replace only the cracked glass cover plate, why does Samsung insist on replacing the entire touch screen, making a fault that could be fixed for a few hundred yuan cost consumers nearly two thousand yuan?

Xiao Huang's friend: Isn't it just the outer screen broken? Why not just replace the glass?

Samsung mobile phone repair staff: All our products come from the factory as a set. Individual replacements are done at small stalls, where they dismantle the screen and replace the part.

Samsung mobile phone repair staff: All screens we get from the factory include both inner and outer screens.

So, what is the market price for Samsung's liquid crystal touch screens? Reporters haggled in Huaqiangbei, Shenzhen, one of the largest electronic product distribution centers in China, and eventually got the lowest wholesale price for the Samsung Note2 touch screen at 890 yuan, and the S4 touch screen at 1020 yuan. Compared to the S4 touch screen repair price of 1850 yuan at Samsung's after-sales service center, this price is already more than 800 yuan cheaper. An insider told reporters that it's difficult to get screens directly from Samsung's original factory. These touch screens are basically recovered from defective screens by mobile phone repair enterprises, replacing the cracked cover glass with new ones. In the industry, such screens are called "original pressed screens."

Salesperson: There are no assembled screens for Samsung, all are original.

Reporter: Are they all original screens?

Salesperson: Yes.

Reporter: Isn't the original screen strictly controlled by Samsung? How did you get it?

Salesperson: She has connections, there are people inside Samsung's factory.

Salesperson: The outside is the cover plate. We reinstalled it. The inside is original.

Reporters learned from informed sources that the Samsung Note2 and S4 use AMOLED light-emitting diode display technology, offering more sensitive touch, richer colors, and thinner screens. The touch screens are produced in South Korea, currently priced internationally at around 100 US dollars, equivalent to about six hundred yuan RMB. Calculating based on this price, Samsung's own after-sales service repair profit is between one to two times. The mainstream LCD panel is TFT thin-film transistor liquid crystal display device, a mature technology for over ten years. The international market price is around 50 US dollars, equivalent to three or four hundred yuan RMB. However, regardless of the price, Samsung's LCD screen after-sales repair prices are extremely high.

Reporters purchased the entire iPhone4s LCD screen in Huaqiangbei, Shenzhen, for a wholesale price of only 120 yuan, less than the fraction of the repair price of 1590 yuan at Apple's after-sales service center.
