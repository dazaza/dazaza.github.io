Episode introductions of "Next Life, Be Your Woman"

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 1

The story takes place in the early 1970s in an Upper Liang village in the north. The male protagonist Feng Jiachang's mother was on her deathbed, leaving him to take care of his three younger brothers Tiezhan, Gouzhan, and Maozhan. Their father was a son-in-law who had become disabled and was looked down upon by the villagers.

The female protagonist Liu Hansiang is the daughter of the village party secretary. Once, Hansiang was stopped by a gang leader named Banzhi at the school gate. Feng Jiachang came to her rescue, winning her heart. Pure love blossomed between these two young people from different backgrounds.

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 2

After returning to the village, Feng Jiachang decided to become a smart new farmer to change the face of his hometown but was seen as an oddity by Liu Guodou, who used excuses to send him to a study class. Hansiang persuaded her father, allowing Feng Jiachang to work as a part-time worker at the fertilizer factory in the city. At the fertilizer factory, Feng Jiachang performed excellently and bravely risked his life during a fire, earning merit. However, his rural household registration cut off his dream of becoming a worker. To change his fate and escape discrimination, he told Hansiang that only by joining the army could he truly leave the rural困境and become a real urban resident. Hansiang sacrificed her innocence to fulfill Feng Jiachang's ideal.

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 3

In the new recruit squad, Feng Jiachang and the city soldier Tan Jianhua formed a rivalry. Feng Jiachang was eager to perform, hiding a broom in his bed to be the first to labor. He fought with another soldier to stand up for the rural soldier Shi Xinuo, drawing attention from Commander Hu. Commander Hu sensed that Feng Jiachang was a good soldier but lacked experience, so he secretly observed and encouraged him.

Hansiang's classmate Bai Zhu came to propose marriage with a car. Feng Jiachang's second brother Tiezhan was very angry and secretly punctured the car's tires. After causing trouble, Tiezhan dared not return home after dark. Hansiang found Tiezhan, and Tiezhan begged Sister Hansiang not to marry anyone else or leave the village.

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 4

In the new recruit dormitory, there was another conflict between rural and city soldiers. Feng Jiachang hit someone. During training, Feng Jiachang got distracted thinking about the "four-pocket" and was punished to practice alone. Commander Hu educated Feng Jiachang on how to become an excellent soldier. When assigning the new recruits, both Feng Jiachang and Tan Jianhua were assigned to the communication camp.

Bai Zhu proposed again, and Hansiang rejected him. Feng Jiachang's third brother Gouzhan beat a chicken belonging to Tongchui's family. Hansiang protected Feng's children with her wisdom and broad-mindedness.

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 5

During the new recruit training, the city soldiers in the second squad performed flexibly and received praise. Feng Jiachang trained hard with Shi Xinuo late into the night. Shi Xinuo got injured and was treated by Nurse Li Dongdong, who seemed rough but effective.

Hansiang went to Tongchui's house to compensate for the chicken and retrieved the long-overdue debt owed to the Feng family. Tiezhan and Maozhan were caught stealing eggplants by the commune and taken to the police station. Hansiang rushed to save them once again.

"Next Life, Be Your Woman" Episode 6

After the communication camp training, Feng Jiachang and Tan Jianhua made rapid progress and became the squad leaders of Squad Six and Squad Two respectively.

Liu Hansiang received a letter and a certificate of excellence as a model soldier from Feng Jiachang.

On Sunday, the army conducted Lei Feng activities. Feng Jiachang led a team to the train station to help the elderly and carry children. A ticket inspector bullied an old rural man, prompting Feng Jiachang to stand up for justice, leading to a fight with the ticket inspector. Nurse Li Dongdong helped Feng Jiachang treat his wounds.
