Synopsis of "Good Fortune and Peace":

Sixty years ago, a mountain girl named Huang Chunxiang (played by Tan Aizhen) accidentally saved Yan Wangcai (played by Ding Qiang), who had collapsed while gathering medicine in the mountains due to being caught in a trap for wild animals. They fell in love at first sight, but unfortunately, they parted ways under the beautiful Paulownia rain. Chunxiang kept the watch that Wangcai left her, waiting for him for sixty years, like a dream. Chunxiang always hoped that one day Wangcai would come back to find her, and this deep affection moved her granddaughter Xie Fuan (played by Ruby Lin).

"If only I could have such a beautiful first love..."

Yan Yungao, chairman of the world's largest traditional Chinese medicine group 'Babao Hall Pharmaceutical Group', was unconscious after a car accident and was lying weakly in the ambulance, rushing along the country roads to be sent to the hospital for emergency treatment. Not long after, his descendants, who were like wolves and tigers waiting for him to die so they could inherit his estate, arrived one after another. Seeing Yungao critically ill, apart from his grandson Yan Yang (played by Sean Xiao), who genuinely felt sad, everyone else was happy. Even when the doctor tried to resuscitate Yungao, they all strongly opposed it. "Let dad (grandpa) go peacefully!" This statement reached Fuan's ears, who happened to be secretly treating patients with athlete's foot for extra income in the hospital, making her feel as if she had heard something from outer space. With a strong sense of justice, Fuan decided not to let Yungao's descendants get their way, so she sneaked into the emergency room and used Eucommia leaves to save Yungao. Smelling the familiar scent, Yungao vaguely woke up and dimly saw Fuan's face, recalling a familiar face. He faintly called out:

"Chunxiang?"

Yungao's eldest grandson Dafeng (played by Blue Lan), upon seeing Yungao unexpectedly saved, immediately scolded Fuan for meddling, leaving a bad impression on both sides, unaware that this was just the beginning of their fateful encounter.

Apart from being very righteous, Fuan also deeply cares for her family, thoughtfully taking care of her frail grandmother Chunxiang and her young brother Pidan. Since Fuan managed to snatch a bit more time from death, after Yungao woke up, he was very disappointed with these inhumane grandchildren. However, Yungao knew this was his own retribution for nurturing their vulture-like nature. But this wasn't what Yungao regretted the most; what he regretted the most was lying to and deceiving Chunxiang sixty years ago!

It turned out that the Wangcai mentioned by Chunxiang was actually him. Back then, he approached Chunxiang to deceive her into giving him a secret prescription for treating gastrointestinal discomfort. After descending the mountain, he changed his name to Yan Yungao and used the prescription to open Babao Hall, becoming wealthy as a result. Ironically, Chunxiang steadfastly guarded this first love, and it wasn't until Yungao met Fuan that he suddenly remembered...

Yungao decided to make amends to Chunxiang and revealed that he would announce his successor on his birthday. This news caused great tension among the Yan family members, each unsure of themselves. Following the suggestion of his "underground girlfriend" reporter Zhenzhen (played by Wang Yiren), Dafeng personally came to Manfu Mountain to look for the super tonic "Niu Zhang Zhi" for longevity.

Upon arriving at Manfu Mountain, Dafeng unexpectedly learned that Fuan was actually the granddaughter of an herbal expert. When Fuan heard that Dafeng was looking for Niu Zhang Zhi, she sternly lectured him. Due to an unexpected incident, Fuan's first kiss was stolen by Dafeng through deceit, and at that moment, her home caught fire in an "accident"...

Heartbroken, Fuan bid farewell to Chunxiang and, following her grandmother's last wish, left Manfu Village to descend the mountain and search for the "Grandpa Wangcai" her grandmother spoke of. Just as Fuan descended the mountain, Yungao arrived at her home, looking at the ruins and listening to the old village chief recounting the many hardships of Chunxiang's life over the years. Yungao decided to spare no effort in finding the "Fulan" and "Pidan" mentioned by the village chief.

On his eightieth birthday, Yungao announced: "I will give the inheritance rights of Babao Hall, worth 80 billion, to the person who can marry the descendant of Xie Chunxiang—Xie Fulan!"

As expected by Yan Yungao, everyone hurriedly searched, but Dafeng wanted to find "Fulan" first and kill her to end everything.

The real Fuan, while searching for Grandpa Wangcai, mistakenly saved Dongjie (played by Joe Chou), who had been ambushed. To make up for it, Dongjie ordered his subordinates to secretly protect Fuan and her brother.

Fuan and Pidan arrived in an unfamiliar city with nowhere to go and had to spend the night in the park. It rained during the night, and Pidan developed a high fever the next day. With no money, Fuan remembered Dafeng and took Pidan to find him. Moved by compassion, Dafeng helped take Pidan to see a doctor and arranged accommodation for Fuan and her brother. After Dafeng left, Dongjie visited, leaving his business card because he owed Fuan a favor, asking her to contact him if she needed help. Unexpectedly, subtle relationships developed among these few people...
