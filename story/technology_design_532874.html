Stylish and Personalized, Leading the Trend -- MG 3Xross Makes an Extremely Cool Appearance

by danihhi890 on 2011-11-04 09:19:10

On October 29, the MG3 Xross appraisal event personally planned and executed by Shanghai Automotive took place at the SAIC Jinan Dongyue MG 4S store. The event was extraordinarily lively. During the event, comprehensive introductions were given about the related information of the upgraded flagship model MG3 Xross, which is newly launched by SAIC as a "legendary British premium compact car". Additionally, the guests on-site were treated to brilliant performances and interactive games. Customers who placed orders on-site received limited edition gifts provided by SAIC, with the first customer to place an order winning a grand prize, bringing surprises to the ordering customers. So what are the heart-stirring configurations that make this much-anticipated MG3 Xross version so special: Unlimited creativity, MG3 Xross challenges the "hundred changes" possibility. Continuing the top-tier team from the world design capital of the UK's creation of the "Faceted Flow" cut surface art design, it has a strong British individual style; the sharp front face's large-mouth shape carries a bit of arrogance; the outwardly expanded large-sized petal-style rims paired with widened wheel arches are full of sportsmanship. The silver metallic front and rear bumpers perfectly blend with the large side skirts extending to the doors, outlining sturdy and powerful sharp lines. The load-bearing capacity of the dazzling silver aluminum-magnesium alloy roof luggage rack reaches up to 60KG, interpreting the crossover concept while also delivering a powerful visual impact. Even more eye-catching are the in-car British dynamic orange-black interwoven leather seats paired with the i-Style white minimalist classic interior design, mixing Apple elements with avant-garde style, undoubtedly becoming essential tools for this most IN trendsetting vehicle. Of course, the MG3 Xross isn't just about appearance: the latest calibrated high chassis design increases the ground clearance to 164mm, pushing the off-road performance of A0-class small cars to the extreme; the leading constant speed cruise control system in A0-class small cars improves fuel economy during long-distance highway driving; the 5-speed manual transmission accelerates 100 kilometers in only 11.5 seconds, allowing adventurous and challenging young people to fully experience the exhilaration and excitement of driving control. The dazzling "transformation" of the MG3 Xross once again ignites young owners' enthusiasm for modifications, eager to turn their beloved cars into "hundred change X-warriors" that overturn tradition yet express themselves boldly. The crowd surrounding the display stands and countless iPhone 4s held high for photos sufficiently prove the trendy charm of this "legendary British premium compact car." Perfect quality, UK Design achieves the legend of the MG British small car. In less than four months since its launch, it has already garnered over 30,000 orders, with tight availability causing many dealers to anxiously wait... In this year's bleak car market environment, the outstanding sales performance of an A0-class small car is unexpected or expected? Investigating the reasons, the personalized fashionable British design and the superior oversized space compared to peers are enough to stir the hearts of young owners "excited by love." More importantly, in the latest announced 2011 C-NCAP third batch evaluation results, the MG3 Xross achieved an excellent score of 47.1 points, surpassing German series vehicles renowned for safety and prudence, earning the five-star safety rating. Worth noting is that the MG3 scored 16 points in the crucial deformable moving barrier side collision test for occupant protection, making it one of the few A0-class two-door small cars to achieve a perfect score since the establishment of C-NCAP, rising to become the "small car safety king." Innovative reinforced USD body structure, SRS six-surround airbags, European pedestrian protection design, unique active sensing headlights in its class... To ensure the MG3 has safety quality comparable to mid-to-high-end models, Shanghai Automotive spares no effort in R&D and design, exceeding safety cost investments in peer vehicles, referencing "European standards" Euro-NCAP and C-NACP dual five-star collision design requirements, all demonstrating Shanghai Automotive's strength and confidence in "big factory making small cars."
