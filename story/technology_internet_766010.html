iPhone 5C manual and packaging exposed; domestic models may be first to launch

by anonymous on 2013-09-04 14:01:50

Perhaps the launch of a low-cost version of the iPhone holds special significance for Apple, as the exposure rate of this new model even has a tendency to surpass that of the iPhone 5S. Recently, on the internet, the latest manual and packaging box of the so-called low-cost iPhone 5C were exposed again, and domestic media revealed that the device will also hit the domestic market at the same time as the iPhone 5S.

Exposure of the iPhone 5C Manual

This time, the iPhone 5C manual exposed on the internet does not seem to have many special features, and it also comes with a "SIM card ejector tool." However, from the pictures in the manual, it is confirmed that the iPhone 5C indeed uses a black front panel, which looks better when paired with other color options.

Not only that, but from the latest exposure of the iPhone 5C packaging box by netizens, the packaging is somewhat similar to the previous iPod Touch and similarly shows that the front panel is black. In addition, netizens also disclosed that the packaging of the iPhone 5S is not as good-looking as that of the iPhone 5C.

Possible Simultaneous Domestic Launch

Worth noting is that the domestic media ifanr previously revealed in an article that Chinese carriers will sell the Apple iPhone 5C and iPhone 5S simultaneously worldwide, and Apple specialty stores will also offer them at the same time. Therefore, if the rumors are true, then domestic consumers will be able to purchase the iPhone 5S and iPhone 5C around September 20th. Additionally, according to insiders, both models of the iPhone have passed the MIIT equipment certification and will be sold simultaneously by China Unicom and China Telecom, but the exact prices are still unclear.

Previously, there were reports that if everything goes smoothly, the Apple iPhone 5C and iPhone 5S will go on sale domestically around November 28th, with the price of the iPhone 5S set at 5599 yuan, while the iPhone 5C will be around 3399 yuan.

Rumored Price Starting at Approximately 2448 Yuan

According to exclusive information released by the Dutch website Simonlyradar, the main hardware specifications of the Apple iPhone 5C are very similar to those of the iPhone 5, featuring a 4.0-inch Retina display, equipped with a dual-core processor and 1GB of RAM, offering three storage options: 16/32/64GB. In addition, the device will run on iOS7.0 and continue to support the Siri function, with dual-camera configuration, featuring a 1.2-megapixel front camera and an 8-megapixel rear camera with LED flash.

It is reported that Apple may officially release the new iPhone in the United States on September 10th. Regarding the price that everyone is more concerned about, the Dutch website Simonlyradar reported that the price of the 16GB version of the iPhone 5C is 400 US dollars, approximately 2448 yuan RMB, and it is expected to be officially launched at the end of September.

The iPhone event will be held on September 10th.
