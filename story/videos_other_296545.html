Chen Guanxi's Trendy Store Opening Turns Chaotic; Mentions of "Comeback" Cause Immediate Backlash

by fjhutjhid on 2009-12-06 12:38:33

Chen Guanxi's trendy store opening turns chaotic; mentions of a comeback cause him to turn sour. Yesterday afternoon, Chen Guanxi's brand new trendy store, JUICE, officially opened in a three-story villa on Julu Road in Shanghai. The not-so-wide road was packed with fans who had come to wait and the media covering the event, causing traffic to come to a standstill. As the owner, Chen Guanxi personally arranged the entire opening ceremony, with friends like Li Cansen, Xu Haoying, and Japanese trendsetter Hiroshi Fujiwara specially coming to support him. However, when the media brought up the topic of a comeback, the originally cheerful Chen Guanxi immediately turned sour.

Chen Guanxi (left) performs the "dotting the eyes" ritual for the lion dance. Photo by Liu Xingzhe of the Morning Post.

Focus on business acumen; Chen Guanxi loses temper at mention of comeback

After declaring that he would "forever exit Hong Kong's entertainment industry," Chen Guanxi made a return in the fashion world. In May this year, his clothing store on Chang乐 Road closed its doors, but it seems that Chen Guanxi has not given up on his beloved fashion industry. After receiving 20 million yuan in funding from a wealthy woman in Hong Kong, the new JUICE store on Julu Road in Shanghai became Chen Guanxi's first move in his return. Yesterday, during the opening of JUICE, the owner, Chen Guanxi, appeared wearing a deep blue hat and sunglasses, dressed in a gray coat. He invited his friends Li Cansen, Xu Haoying, and Hiroshi Fujiwara to help celebrate, and even arranged for a lion dance team to perform, personally completing the "dotting the eyes" ritual. Subsequently, he also posed generously with a group of guests for photos with the media. However, Chen Guanxi's appearance outside the store lasted only seven minutes.

During the interview, Chen Guanxi talked extensively about business strategies, showing his ambition in the fashion industry. He also revealed that a Beijing store would open soon. Originally in a good mood, Chen Guanxi finally lost his temper after being repeatedly asked by the media when he would make a comeback. Despite repeated attempts by his staff to deflect the questions, Chen Guanxi angrily countered the media: "Why do you always ask me when I will make a comeback? I am very busy and have many things to do."

Fans chase "Xi" across great distances; scene descends into chaos

The scheduled opening and ribbon-cutting ceremony at 3 PM was delayed by half an hour before Chen Guanxi finally appeared. By then, the scene was already difficult to control. In front of the store entrance, hundreds of Chen Guanxi's fans held banners, their faces plastered with JUICE stickers, shouting Chen Guanxi's English name "Edison" as soon as they saw him. According to the staff, many fans had arrived the night before to secure a good spot. After the ribbon-cutting, many fans who hadn't yet seen Chen Guanxi rushed towards him, causing continuous screams and resulting in a loss of order. Back inside the store, Chen Guanxi went to the second floor's floor-to-ceiling glass window to wave at the fans. Seeing the "impressive" crowd, Chen Guanxi smiled slightly. Fans shouted: "Chen Guanxi, we will always remember you. Do you still remember us?" Chen Guanxi raised his hand, forming the letter "C." The fans outside also quickly raised their hands in response. According to reporters' interviews on-site, these fans came from all over the country, many of them students who spent over a thousand yuan on travel expenses just for the occasion. Some fans were so moved upon seeing their idol that they cried, saying excitedly: "We are just so excited to see him. We hope he knows that we will always support him. If we can, we will also buy clothes from the store."
