108 Most Comprehensive Methods for Online Store Promotion

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 09:38:43

1. SNS Store Soft Article Promotion Method: Nowadays, SNS-type online stores are very popular. When visiting SNS stores, it's mainly about joining hundreds or even thousands of users on a few popular SNS stores. Then select appropriate soft articles and reasonably attach the store link, publishing them in your diary. Have some of your friends share it, so many people will pay attention to your article and your store.

2. SNS Store API Cooperation Promotion Method: If you or your employees have certain technical capabilities, you can develop some small plug-ins related to your store products and insert them into platforms like Xiaonei (Renren) and HaiNei. Any SNS store with an open API can be targeted. Many stores are doing this now. Look at the open applications on Xiaonei; many are provided by small online stores, and they all have more or less advertising for their own stores. This market is better to enter early as many SNS stores are just starting to try developing APIs, and the review requirements are not yet particularly strict. As more stores come online, it will become harder to get your plug-ins accepted.

3. Bookmark Promotion Method: Add pages with great content to QQ bookmarks, Baidu Collections, Yahoo Favorites, etc., allowing web users who enjoy such content to read and save them.

4. Moderator Alliance Promotion Method: This method is suitable for forum community store promotion. First, join some moderator alliances, like Daqi Moderator Alliance, which is quite good. Submit interesting content from forums daily and wait for it to be accepted by relevant channels. Once accepted, it will bring you quite a bit of traffic. I once brought 30,000+ IP clicks to my store in two days.

5. Reply Sticky Promotion Method: Actually, I don't know what to call this method either, but its scope is very limited. As far as I know, it only applies to Mopu Dazahui, so let's temporarily call it reply sticky. I discovered this method accidentally while checking the source of my store's IP traffic. One day, I noticed over 200 IPs came from a post on Mopu, but I knew we hadn't posted any promotional messages there, so it seemed strange. Checking the post, I found someone had placed a link to our store and made it sticky through a reply. That’s how we got over 200 IPs. Later, I learned that a tool called "Yuemingfu" could be used to stick replies. Moderators cannot delete these posts, only administrators have that authority, so generally, it can stay sticky for a period of time.

6. Prize Registration Promotion Method: A promotion method aimed at increasing member registrations. Generally, everyone uses prize registration or incentive registration methods for promotion. People today are very pragmatic, and they won’t do anything without benefit.

7. Free Game Promotion Method: Free games involve hanging a free game on your store, but it should align with the theme of the store and be fun...

8. QQ Group Promotion Method: Use dozens of QQ accounts, each joining dozens of groups, and promote the store daily. On average, 50 QQ accounts * 50 groups per QQ * 50 people per group = 75,000 people viewing. Even if some QQ users are offline, the effect shouldn't be underestimated. Be prepared to endure rejection and not fear being kicked out. Additionally, you can send group emails in QQ groups. For example, you can recommend the exciting content from your store ***.com in the email.

9. Search Engine Promotion Method: Today's novice webmasters are most enthusiastic about this quick and effective method. The function of search engines is to integrate network resources, helping netizens find the most suitable store content, bringing traffic to other stores as an unexpected side effect. Many webmaster-type store promotion columns seem to have turned into SEO zones. The best SEO method involves standard webpage code, fast access speed, unique and non-repetitive content useful to netizens, and having exclusivity.

10. Soft Article Promotion Method: Write articles or quote good ones, cleverly inserting your URL within them.

11. Blog Promotion Method: Soft articles need a place to be published. One option is to post them on forums or article sites, another is to post them on blogs. Posting on blogs has the advantage of not being easily deleted.

12. Media Promotion Method: Let traditional media, newspapers, TV, radio, etc., report on your store.

13. Mobile Promotion Method: WAP stores, mass SMS, ringtone prompts, etc. Mobile phones are becoming increasingly powerful, compact, and easy to carry. In the future, the number of mobile internet users will definitely exceed that of computer users.

14. Web Clip Promotion Method: Submit high-quality webpages, images, posts to clipping sites and aggregator stores. If recommended, it can also bring considerable traffic.

15. Forum Promotion Method: Register dozens or hundreds of forums, register dozens of accounts on each forum, set your signature to your store. Post popular content and self-top your posts. Remember to switch aliases and post controversial titles. A good title is 100% of the success or failure of forum promotion. Here, forums refer to broad forums, including guestbooks, forums, tieba, and any other places where netizens might gather.
