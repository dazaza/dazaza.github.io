Luo Furen panicked and scrambled up the stairs. Inadvertently, he stumbled and lost one of his thousands-of-yuan branded leather shoes. This man, who was usually calm and composed, no longer cared about his shoes. He stumbled and kept climbing upwards. Meanwhile, voices were continuously heard from below the stairs, as if dozens of people were chasing him up the stairs. As Luo Furen ran, he constantly looked down the stairs. This was the emergency exit of the building, not a wide corridor but just an emergency rescue route. People usually entered and exited the building via the elevator. However, Luo Furen dared not take the elevator because... because he had failed, and the other party already knew his identity and location. If he dared to stop, the other side would definitely kill him instantly! "I haven't failed! I haven't failed! I was almost able to find out your identity... I was so close to knowing who you are! 'Doctor', you go against the current while I follow it. How could space-time let me fail... I refuse to accept this!"

Luo Furen ran while loudly screaming. His voice echoed in the building's passage. The voices downstairs paused for a moment, then quickly became even more noisy. Thus, as if unaware of fatigue, Luo Furen reached the top floor of the thirty-story building. At the end of the stairs was an iron-locked door. Without hesitation, Luo Furen pulled out his gun from his waistband. Two gunshots rang out, shattering the lock into pieces. Then Luo Furen kicked the door open and rushed onto the rooftop like a madman.

"Doctor! I know you're watching me, or you will be able to watch me in the future..."

After shouting these two sentences, Luo Furen realized how disheveled he was. This handsome young man in his late twenties began tidying himself up. While smoothing his hair and brushing off dust from his clothes, he noticed that he only had one shoe left. Wrinkling his brows, he kicked off the remaining shoe. He then shouted at the void: "I've lost, lost without any regrets. I didn't expect you to use such a method to make me appear. Indeed, this is my only weakness, and you have grasped it. So I lost without any regrets... But I refuse to accept this outcome! You are clearly going against the current, so why did space-time let me lose? I refuse to accept this!"

"So... this can only mean one thing: I am not the person destined by space-time to defeat you. The person or group of people destined to smooth out the fluctuations in space-time is not me!"

As Luo Furen said this, he slowly pulled out half a newspaper, front and back pages, from his pocket. The edges of the newspaper were torn, indicating there was another half.

"Desire it greatly, don't you?" Luo Furen laughed loudly while holding the half-newspaper: "If you get it, you will have four complete pages. Then, there will be no force in this world that can restrict you. The power to smooth out space-time fluctuations will be useless. Tell me, don't you want it desperately? Hahaha..."

While speaking, Luo Furen suddenly tore the newspaper twice, splitting the two-page section into four parts. Each part contained a quarter of the front and back pages, meaning they added up to half a page. After doing this, Luo Furen held the four fragments in his hand and said, "Human desires are truly disgusting things... Doctor, do you know? This thing gave me everything, and also destroyed my world. If... if the person who originally obtained it wasn't me, perhaps I could still live quietly with her..."

While speaking, Luo Furen had already walked to the edge of the roof. He held the four fragments in his hand, then pointed his palm towards outside the roof. Once he opened his palm, the four fragments would fly away. Being on the roof dozens of floors high, once these four fragments flew away, no one would be able to find them again.

"Doctor, now the power of space-time flowing smoothly is still protecting me, but once I let go, this power will leave with it... I've already lost, so nothing matters anymore. But Doctor, this time your opponent is just me alone, next time your opponents might be four people... Hahaha..."

Luo Furen slowly loosened his palm, and the newspaper fragment in the center of his palm was blown away by the wind. These four fragments, each having half a page, disappeared into the night with the gentle breeze. When Luo Furen could no longer see these four fragments, suddenly a bolt of lightning struck his forehead from the sky, and his vision turned completely black...

Memories of past events reverberated in Luo Furen's consciousness. From childhood to adulthood, over two decades of his life trajectory played repeatedly... He was a child from the countryside, whose father was the heir of a certain commercial family, belonging to the typical second-generation rich kid type. Because he got into trouble in the city, he fled to the countryside and hid for two years. During these two years, his father, with his handsome appearance and fashionable dress, gold and style, pursued the most beautiful girl in the village. However, his father was a beastly man, impregnating the girl, and when the two years were up, he left without leaving anything behind except his name. The girl went to the city to look for him a few times, but how could a country girl possibly find this playboy? In the end, the girl gave birth to Luo Furen in her own home, crying blood...

Luo Furen was born in such an environment. No one in his family liked him, including his mother. And because of having him as baggage, his mother had to marry a thirty-something hick from the village. Therefore, his childhood was spent being humiliated and abused until he broke his arm at the age of five. Realizing that continuing to stay would surely lead to death, this naturally intelligent boy decided at the age of five to climb aboard a train heading south with a group of vagrant children, arriving in Shenzhen...

The life of a vagrant child was filled with bitterness and pain. Luo Furen, with his clever mind, flexible means, and good looks, managed to become a small leader among the vagrant children by the time he was twelve or thirteen, though still controlled by some big thugs, forced to be a pickpocket every day. Nevertheless, he secretly saved a sum of money. At fifteen, Luo Furen seized an opportunity to steal a large sum of money from the big thugs and immediately left Shenzhen, settling down in a small town in the south. This sum of money was protection fees offered by the big thugs to higher-level gangsters, totaling more than 300,000 yuan, plus Luo Furen's own few ten thousand yuan. He rented a very simple single room in the small town, spending less than 800 yuan a month on food and necessities, and used the extra money to buy books - textbooks starting from elementary school, various types of knowledge books, and many extracurricular knowledge or social information. Never having read a book before, Luo Furen started learning knowledge in this simple little house, studying for five whole years...

In five years, Luo Furen started from learning characters until mastering the knowledge required for a Ph.D. exam. He had completely absorbed all this into his brain. He finally realized that he was the genius described in books, and without needing to test his IQ, he knew his IQ was at least 180 or more. He was a genius...

"Talents given by heaven must have their uses! I have survived countless hardships since childhood, and heaven has certainly chosen me to accomplish great deeds. Yes, I am the one recognized by heaven, those mentioned in historical ancient books... I am a person with destiny!" Luo Furen firmly believed in his life at this moment. He was born to create miracles. His existence was destiny!

Although it sounds absurd, Luo Furen really believed this. He left the small town with the more than ten thousand yuan he still had, and came to another prosperous seaside town. Besides packaging himself, he bribed several tax officials in the town. Then, he claimed to be the son of a director of a major company, coming to this town to negotiate business. With his clever arrangements and financial attacks, he quickly gained the trust of several medium and small companies locally, and defrauded more than a million yuan in funds. Luo Furen then fled far away, and after paying more than 200,000 yuan in bribes, successfully arrived in Hong Kong from mainland China...

It must be admitted that Luo Furen was indeed a genius. In his five years of self-study, law, taxation, finance, these knowledge were mastered by him thoroughly. And Luo Furen, who firmly believed himself to be a person with destiny, did not worry at all about failing or being caught, extremely bold with more than a million yuan in funds as backup, and indeed succeeded in continuous frauds in Hong Kong. Moreover, he adopted the method of borrowing chickens to lay eggs, using the money defrauded to defraud others, using defrauded business partners as proof to defraud other business partners. Such rolling snowballs, when his funds reached tens of millions, he quietly left Hong Kong, and it was not until several months later that the major fraud case in Hong Kong erupted...

At this time, Luo Furen was 27 years old. In just seven short years, he had become a millionaire, but he was still not satisfied. He felt that the preparatory work in the early stage was sufficient, and the next step should start his so-called plan of persons with destiny. So he came to Shanghai, intending to start his commercial empire legend here, no longer fraud, no longer accumulation of funds, but a real great cause...

However, at this point, half a newspaper with two pages changed him...

Luo Furen accidentally obtained this half newspaper. Initially, he did not know the power of this newspaper. If it weren't for an accident, these two newspapers might have been thrown into the trash can by him. But due to an accident, he suddenly found that the date on this newspaper was wrong. Yes, the date shown on this newspaper was the date of the next day! Luo Furen finally became interested in this newspaper. After carefully studying the mystery of this newspaper, he indeed discovered the difference of this newspaper. It showed the information of the next day, and with the change of the holder's intention, this newspaper could show the information of up to seven days in the future, that is, all the information within a week in the future. In other words, owning this newspaper meant that the holder had the ability to perfectly predict the future for seven days...

Luo Furen was ecstatic. He thought that this was the sharp weapon given by heaven to him, the person with destiny, allowing him to change the world, change the whole world... He suddenly wanted to be an emperor. As long as he had this thing in his hand, with the perfect ability to predict the future, then he would be the emperor of the future world, the god of the future world! But then, he found out that this newspaper was not something from this world, or rather, this newspaper was not something from this era. It belonged to the future, belonging to someone who returned from the future, or someone who came from another dimension, and the secrets of this newspaper were much more than he knew...

But when he realized this, it was already too late, too late...

(Am I dying? Am I dying? So dark, I can't see anything anymore. Can the doctor control the weather? I wonder if my newspaper can... Damn, I failed, I failed... I...)

Luo Furen's body was completely charred black. Lying on the roof, he silently stared at the sky. His eyes remained wide open. He died with unfulfilled wishes! And the four fragments of the newspaper continued to float in the night sky, rising and falling, unknowing where they would fly to in the future...
