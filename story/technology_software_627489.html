A traffic police officer was sentenced to 3 months of criminal detention for causing a traffic accident while driving under the influence of alcohol.

by supperskye on 2011-12-16 13:38:46

Our newspaper recently reported an incident involving Hu, the deputy head of the Wenzhou Detachment of the Zhejiang Provincial Highway Traffic Police Corps, who was involved in a car accident on Chezhan Avenue in the urban area of Wenzhou after driving under the influence of alcohol, resulting in damage to two vehicles (for more details, see A8 edition of our newspaper on June 13). Yesterday afternoon, the Lucheng Court of Wenzhou publicly tried this drunk driving case. The court's first-instance verdict: Hu is guilty of dangerous driving, sentenced to three months of criminal detention, and fined 3000 RMB. It is understood that this is also the first traffic police officer in Zhejiang to be sentenced for drunk driving since the implementation of the criminalization of drunk driving on May 1 this year.

[Case Replay] Traffic Police Drunk Drives and Hits a Taxi

In the early hours of June 11, after drinking, Hu drove a yellow Ford sedan along Chezhan Avenue from north to south.

At around 12:32 AM, when the car passed through the intersection of Jinxiu Road and Chezhan Avenue, it rear-ended a taxi waiting for the red light ahead in the same lane, causing damage to both vehicles. According to the staff responsible for towing the cars that day, the rear of the taxi was severely damaged, the hood of the yellow Ford driven by Hu was lifted, and the front of the car was heavily damaged with both airbags deployed.

After the accident, one of the taxi drivers at the scene called the police. Subsequently, Hu was taken away for investigation. The forensic department of the Wenzhou Public Security Bureau tested the blood sample extracted from Hu suspected of drunk driving, detecting an alcohol content of 127.8mg/100ml in his blood, confirming that he was driving under the influence of alcohol. Consequently, Hu was placed under criminal detention by the police.

On June 16, the Lucheng Procuratorate prosecuted Hu for dangerous driving.

[Court Verdict] Three Months of Criminal Detention and a Fine of 3000 RMB

Yesterday in court, Hu confessed without reservation to the facts of drunk driving.

In his final statement, Hu said: "I apologize to the parties harmed by my foolish behavior, especially to the car owner. I have also paid a price, being dismissed from public office, and it has affected my family. I am sorry to my leaders, and I am sorry for their guidance and cultivation. This case marks the end of my career as a police officer. I hope everyone can take me as a warning!"

After deliberation, the court believed that Hu violated road transportation management regulations by driving a motor vehicle while drunk, constituting the crime of dangerous driving. Considering that Hu truthfully confessed his crimes after being apprehended, voluntarily pleaded guilty, and compensated for the economic losses of the victims, he could be legally given a lighter punishment. Therefore, the court made the above-mentioned ruling against Hu according to law.
