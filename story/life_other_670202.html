Love has been suspended due to unpaid fees.

Looking back, I always have countless feelings. Why did you appear in my world and stay so carelessly?

In that season when summer flowers were in full bloom, it was my most innocent season. As the sun set, you said I was a flower in the school's back garden. To others, I might be ordinary, and among countless flowers, I might be very common, but you thought I was the most special one. No one knew that at that time, I was surprised and honored, and my heart was filled with joy.

Every evening, I would watch you shoot one basketball after another on that lawn. Each ball always moved people, each ball always brought cheers and applause. I handed you a bottle of water and wiped the sweat off your face with a tissue. Pride and confidence filled your face and eyes. I smiled, and so did you. I said you were great, and you said it was because I was there.

Do you still remember - watching fireworks together?

On that cold night with drizzling winter rain, you held my hand by the quiet Longtan River. You casually told me about what you saw and heard at another school, and I quietly listened. You said you liked my quietness. My hands were icy cold, and you tenderly held them. I liked your warm palms. The drizzle was enough to wet us, and I held an umbrella while we stood and watched the fireworks on the opposite bank. My heart was warm, as if the fireworks were burning and flickering in my heart.

You leaned down and told me, that wasn't fireworks, it was my smile - warm and bright. The cold wind blew, but your embrace was warm. We counted the days we had spent together with our hands - three hundred and sixty-five days plus seventy-seven days.

You remembered more clearly than me, and I thought you cared about me. Thinking back to when we were still in the same school, we went to class together, took exams together, ate together, shopped together on weekends, and...

But we were separated in two different cities because of our dreams. In the first year of our separation, over the phone, I said I missed you, and you said you missed me even more.

You said that no matter what, in different cities, we always had to learn to be alone. When it rained, we had to hold our own umbrellas, and when we were sad, we had to wipe our own tears. During the National Day holiday, you came to see me, and I was happy for a long time, touched for a long time. You gave me a watch, and I said how could you give me "the end". You said you just wanted to express the time we had spent together, and I said okay. Later, you started your internship, and I was still working hard for my college entrance exam.

You called me and told me how strict your boss was, and you could only answer my calls at night, but you promised you would keep going. When you were tired, you could feel my presence far away comforting you. Honestly, I was moved. Before 10 PM, your phone was always off.

After 10 PM, you said you felt so tired from work, and you really wished summer vacation would come soon so we could play together, crazily. So you believed distance could create beauty. But I thought distance could create beauty, yet it couldn't provide a fortress for love.

When we were close, there was no room in your heart for anyone else but me, and I was your only one. Therefore, you said you would protect me for a lifetime, and I believed you without hesitation.

I made a mistake on my college entrance exam and didn't get into a key university, which made me very sad. You consoled me, and I wanted to retake the exam, and you said you would wait for me. However, at this point, I received an acceptance letter from a second-tier university in the north, and you congratulated me. You also said four years would pass quickly, and we would still be together then. So I went to university with peace of mind. On the day I left, you said you couldn't come to see me off.

I was extremely disappointed. There was only an hour left before boarding, and in the crowded crowd, I couldn't find you. It started raining, and seeing others holding umbrellas, I wanted to cry.

Suddenly, someone hugged me tightly from behind, scaring me. Haha, you scared me again, lying that you couldn't come to see me off. This time, I really cried, and you could say I was silly. When entering security check, you said you would wait for me to come back.

The long-awaited winter vacation finally arrived, and I returned. I thought you would be waiting for me at the train station early, but who knew I ended up waiting for you at the train station for over an hour. You said there was a traffic jam, and I got angry. You said I should understand you.

Haha, alright then. We went online together, you logged into QQ, and then went to the bathroom. I casually opened your QQ main page, and I was stunned... The group exclusively for me no longer had my name. I also felt sorry for you because I liked a senior in university who was good to me, but how was I supposed to know it was for me?

Without saying a word, I went home alone. When you called me and asked why I left, I only thought you were hypocritical. I asked who Xiaoting was, and you were silent. Maybe you were also stunned on the other end of the phone. You faintly said you originally wanted to tell me, but you were afraid it would affect my college entrance exam. Later, you were afraid I would enter university with sadness, and since I was a girl going so far away, you were also afraid I would feel helpless, so you continued to hide it. You explained, you apologized, you hesitated, you were confused. You also said you didn't want to become strangers, hoping we could remain friends after breaking up.

I only thought you were deceiving me, you had too many reasons. I wanted to forgive you, but why was I so heartbroken? I couldn't accept someone loving two people at the same time, so I didn't like Chiung Yao's novels, where there was always betrayal of spirit and flesh. All along, I had already recognized you, and regarded your matters as mine. When you said your monthly living expenses were overdrawn, I saved money and sent part of my living expenses to you. You said you were moved, and said I was truly the person who cared about you, and I didn't deny it.

In the busy high school life, I squeezed out time to learn how to knit gloves. I wanted to finish knitting them before winter came and send them to you. I lied to you that I bought them. In university, I also learned how to knit scarves. Originally, I planned to give it to you as a birthday gift during the winter vacation. Even if there was no love anymore, there could still be friendship. But now, I can't bring myself to give it to you. The harm and deception you gave me were so deep, so painful.

You said you would return the money I sent you, and I asked why not all at once. You said you didn't want to stop contacting me. I really thought you were being unreasonable, too unreasonable to me.

All along, I thought as long as I loved you, you would definitely feel it, and you would definitely love me. Later, I realized that love isn't determined by one person, it's not just one person's persistence or one person's contribution that counts. The watch you gave me stopped at 23:43. Maybe at that moment, you stopped focusing on me and started with that girl. I have been worrying about you for four years, and you have become cheap to me.

Now, what I see is real sunshine, full of warmth and floral fragrance. Actually, without you, my world can still be beautiful.

In the past, I was just used to having you in my heart, but now, new feelings are like cells in my body, truly alive. Why wouldn't I cherish them? I just want to tell you, I am very happy now, and you have long been a passing cloud in my past. Our love has been suspended due to unpaid fees.
