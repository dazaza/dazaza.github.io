Spring plowing and spring sowing are coming again, be alert to fake and inferior agricultural supplies.

by cgccg001 on 2009-12-20 03:38:41

[Verbal Introduction] They say, "A person can mistakenly use land for a moment, but the land can mistakenly affect a person for a year." To have a good harvest, besides relying on favorable weather and geographical advantages, the type of agricultural supplies used is truly very important. Think about it, the whole family works hard in the fields for a year, looking forward to the harvest, only to end up with nothing due to fake seeds or fertilizers! If farmers buy counterfeit agricultural supplies, it's heart-wrenching. Now, as we enter the season of preparing for farming, how can our farmer friends ensure they purchase reliable agricultural supplies? And what should they do if they encounter fakes? Don't worry, let me tell you a story first.

[Voice-over] The man in front of you is named Chen Rentai, a native farmer from Magou Town in Yandu District. A few years ago, he was growing watermelons at Dafeng Farm. Last year, after learning techniques and making money elsewhere, he returned home with over ten thousand yuan earned through hard work.

[On-site Soundbite] Villager of Magou Town, Chen Rentai: At that time, I planted all watermelons, 25 greenhouses, and 15 mu of open-field melons.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of Xinmin Village, Magou Town, Chen Zhenghai: Successfully planting watermelons can play a leading role, increasing their income, guiding other farmers to develop this industry.

[Voice-over] Right after last year's Spring Festival, Chen Rentai enthusiastically went into the city and bought back 20 cans of watermelon seeds, ready for a big effort. However, when the watermelons were supposed to hit the market, problems arose.

[On-site Soundbite] Villager of Magou Town, Chen Rentai: When customers came to load the truck, if a melon fell off the truck and didn't crack, there was definitely something wrong. When cut with a knife, the melon had thick skin.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of Xinmin Village, Magou Town, Chen Zhenghai: Watermelons were piled everywhere on the northern cement road and by the river. Even worse, the wholesale watermelons were returned by the truckload, people refusing his melons, everyone feeling deeply pained and angry!

[Voice-over] Chen Rentai was indeed severely harmed by these seeds. Not only did he fail to fulfill the promises he made to his fellow villagers, but also the tens of thousands of yuan he had painstakingly accumulated went down the drain. Who wouldn't be angry in such a situation? In order to get an explanation, Chen Rentai frequently visited the seed distributor, but they refused to take responsibility.

[On-site Soundbite] Villager of Magou Town, Chen Rentai: They completely ignored me. After a brief look, they said it wasn't a problem with their variety but rather my cultivation method.

[Voice-over] Whether it was the watermelon seeds that had issues or if Chen Rentai himself made mistakes during planting couldn't be determined immediately because several months had passed, leaving him unable to find evidence. Helpless, Chen Rentai turned to the Consumer Association of Yandu District for assistance.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of the Consumer Association of Yandu District, Cheng Liqian: Upon receiving this complaint, we found it significant, with severe damages.

[Voice-over] To help Chen Rentai seek justice, the Consumer Association of Yandu District conducted multiple investigations on his farmland. Two months later, with sufficient evidence, the seed distributor finally reached an agreement with Chen Rentai.

[On-site Soundbite] Villager of Magou Town, Chen Rentai: He was compensated 10,000 yuan at the time, and the surrounding people still doubted it.

[Voice-over] The reason why Chen Rentai could successfully protect his rights and receive compensation was partly due to the investigation and assistance provided by the Consumer Association and partly due to his own efforts.

[On-site Soundbite] Villager of Magou Town, Chen Rentai: Of course, one should fight for rights. After working hard for a year, whether small households or large ones, according to my personal view, all should fight for their rights.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of the Consumer Association of Yandu District, Cheng Liqian: First, Chen Rentai kept the invoice for purchasing seeds, providing evidence for protecting rights. Second, Chen Rentai persisted with confidence and determination in protecting his rights.

[Voice-over] Although Chen Rentai received compensation, due to his initial oversight, the road to protecting his rights wasn't easy.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of the Consumer Association of Yandu District, Cheng Liqian: His oversight was not keeping a sample of the seeds.

[Voice-over] Now, as we approach the spring plowing season, how should we choose safe and reliable fertilizers and seeds when buying agricultural supplies? Let's listen to what the experts have to say.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of the Consumer Association of Yandu District, Cheng Liqian: First, purchase seeds from dealers with relevant qualifications. Second, pay attention to identifying and understanding various descriptions and performances of the seeds during the purchasing process. Third, keep purchase contracts and invoices. Fourth, it's best to retain seed samples to prevent losing evidence for protecting rights if there are problems with the seeds.

[Voice-over] Additionally, experts suggest that farmers who suffer harm from counterfeit agricultural supplies should dare to sue and protect their legitimate rights and interests through legal means.

[On-site Soundbite] Secretary of the Consumer Association of Yandu District, Cheng Liqian: There are many ways to protect rights, and when necessary, complaints can be filed with arbitration judicial authorities to obtain legal assistance.

[Editorial Note] "One deceitful act harms farmers for a year!" Agricultural supplies differ from ordinary industrial products. Consumers who buy a pair of poor-quality leather shoes can stop wearing them after a few days. However, if farmers plant fake seeds in their fields, the consequences are unimaginable. "Plant a single grain of millet in spring, and harvest ten thousand grains in autumn." This is the earth's gift and reward to farmers, and also their hope for the year. If they encounter counterfeit agricultural supplies, all their hard work and sweat will go to waste. Therefore, farmers should not easily believe exaggerated claims or seek small profits, but must remain vigilant to avoid being deceived.
