Seoul Semiconductor, for the first time in Korea, has commenced mass production of LED light sources for car headlights. Seoul Semiconductor’s LED light source is being used by the world’s fourth-largest car manufacturer in their main models as of September 6, 2011, according to China News. The world’s leading LED supplier, Seoul Semiconductor (Legal Representative: Lee Jung-hoon, www.seoulsemicon.com), announced today that it has successfully applied its R&D efforts into the LED car headlights light source, achieving the first mass production in Korea. According to this announcement, Seoul Semiconductor's production of LED light sources for car headlights is now being utilized by the fourth largest car manufacturer in the world for their best-selling van cars in Korea. Last year in May, Seoul Semiconductor successfully developed and supplied the market with LED light sources for car headlights DRL (daytime running lights). Through professional auto parts manufacturing companies via Commerce and SL channels, Seoul Semiconductor introduced its brand new Z-Power LED products to the market. The technology required for LED car lights was previously very high. In this context, Seoul Semiconductor developed its own LED light source for car headlights and achieved mass production for the first time in Korea, which can be described as significant.

Seoul Semiconductor's Z-Power products are the result of many years of focused technological development, ultimately obtaining a certificate issued by the AEC (Automotive Electronics Council AEC-Q101). Meanwhile, Seoul Semiconductor began producing LED light sources for car headlights in DRL for European manufacturers last year. Compared to the current HID lights, the new LED light source for car headlights offers high performance, high luminous intensity, and high reliability. Additionally, the product features stable performance and fashionable design. Currently, with the increasing demand for hybrid electric vehicles, there has also been a rapid growth in market demand for LED car headlights. Last year, the global LED car lamp market reached $986 million, and the market is expected to grow at an average annual rate of 22%, reaching $2.16 billion by 2014. Notably, the LED car headlights market is expected to grow rapidly at an annual rate of 36%, much higher than the overall development pace of LED automotive lighting.

Seoul Semiconductor’s product line covers the LED automotive lighting field, including indoor and outdoor LED automotive lighting products, aligning with current market trends. Besides the Korean market, Seoul Semiconductor supplies a variety of automotive LED product lines to car manufacturers in the U.S. and European markets. Seoul Semiconductor’s Vice President John S. Bae stated: "We trust in the use of high-tech products from Seoul Semiconductor across all automotive LED product portfolios. As a result, we can meet domestic demand and also expand into the global market."
