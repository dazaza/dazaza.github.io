The scope of this product's protection: within the waiting period, if suffering from 18 types of children's critical illnesses, the premium will be returned; after the waiting period, if suffering from 18 types of children's critical illnesses, the insured can receive the critical illness insurance money (waiting period is 90 days, no waiting period for continuous subscription). In addition, insurance products with financial management functions such as dividend insurance, universal insurance, and accident insurance with relatively simple designs are often sold on some group-buying websites at discounted rates compared to traditional channels, where they can enjoy an 80% discount, and some can even reach a 40% discount.

The reporter saw that the first premium of this product could be paid through online banking or authorized transfer payment. Authorized transfer payment requires identity verification, and only after passing the verification can the transfer be made. When transferring, customers must ensure that the bank account information is filled out correctly and that the deduction account has sufficient balance. For renewal premiums, a 60-day grace period for payment is provided (within 60 days from the next day of the premium payment date stated in the policy). If the premium is not paid by the end of the grace period, the effectiveness of the insurance contract will be suspended.

"100,000 yuan coverage, only 0.55 yuan per day! Covers 18 types of common children's critical illnesses, specially designed for children, complete the subscription in 2 minutes, no physical examination required!" On March 1st, Ms. Zhou saw this information on a group-buying website in Xi'an and developed a desire to purchase.

Although group-buying insurance can enjoy rate discounts compared to traditional investment channels, it must be pointed out that insurance products differ from general consumer goods. Moreover, group-buying activities often carry hidden risks, so consumers need to be cautious before purchasing group-buying insurance. Besides choosing reliable group-buying websites, it is also necessary to choose powerful insurance companies. Before placing an order, consumers should consult and verify through the customer service hotline of the insurance company.

Ms. Zhou's son is just over two months old, and she has been planning to buy an insurance product for her child. This kind of group-buying form of insurance allowed her to purchase it easily without leaving home. Of course, another reason that attracted Ms. Zhou was the low price.

Currently, existing group-buying insurance products mainly include health insurance, accidental injury insurance, and automobile insurance, which are varieties with small amounts and simple procedures. The process of group-buying insurance is not complicated. A group-buying website launched a car insurance group-buying product, and the entire process includes determining the expiration time of car insurance, comparing prices, registering to participate in group-buying, uploading valid documents, choosing insurance companies and coverage range, picking up and delivering policies, and querying policies, all clearly visible.

Besides being relatively simple in procedure, price is the most important factor attracting consumers to group-buying insurance. For example, on a certain insurance group-buying website, an 80,000 yuan Santana sedan subscribing to 500,000 yuan vehicle damage insurance, third-party liability insurance, passenger insurance for each seat at 10,000 yuan, plus theft, glass scratch insurance, the total premium is 2369.01 yuan, which is nearly 20% cheaper than the market price. Some group-buying websites attract consumers by giving away or providing supplementary products at ultra-low prices.

With the rapid development of e-commerce in China, network group-buying has become a prevailing trend in recent years and has already targeted the "fatty meat" of insurance. In addition to group-buying websites launching insurance products, some insurance companies have also followed the trend and opened group-buying channels on their official websites to facilitate collective single-policy subscriptions for consumers.
