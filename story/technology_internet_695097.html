Using face masks in the nursing process is one of the most effective methods for nourishing the skin. It can make dry and rough skin become moist and smooth, and oily and dark skin become white and tender. Therefore, applying a face mask is an important step.

I. The role of face masks

First, regular application will promote the softening of keratin, dilate pores, and facilitate the absorption of nutrients by the skin.

Second, the gradually tightening face mask will enhance the surface tension of the skin, which helps to improve human blood circulation.

Third, the adsorption effect of the face mask can clear deep-seated dirt from the pores. [http://www.shainisi.org Shanghai Beauty Training School]

II. Classification of Face Masks

There are many types of face masks with various states.

Divided by function: whitening, freckle-removal, wrinkle-removal, anti-inflammation, acne removal, etc.

Divided by state: ointment-like, gel-like, powder-like, etc.

1. Soft Mask

A powdered semi-finished product that needs water to be added when used. There are many varieties of soft masks, they are easy to operate, and easy to preserve. When using, first clean the skin, then take an appropriate amount of mask powder and add water to form a paste. Then use a brush or spatula to apply the mask on the face. [http://www.shainisi.org Shanghai Beauty Training School] After a few minutes, the paste-like mask powder will naturally solidify. After about 15-20 minutes, remove the solidified mask from bottom to top, and wash the skin.

Note: High-quality soft masks are non-irritating and do not need to avoid the eyelid area when used, but ordinary commercial soft masks should best avoid the eye and lip areas.

2. Hard Mask

A powdered semi-finished product made from high-quality medical plaster, kaolin, and other substances. Compared with soft masks, hard masks have relatively more complex functions. [http://www.shainisi.org Shanghai Beauty Training] The unique penetration power of hard masks and the effective substances in the mask are easily absorbed, so whether it is nutrition, whitening, or anti-inflammatory treatment, there are obvious effects.

Note: When using, cleanse the skin, apply water and base cream, cover eyebrows, eyes, mouth, male beards, and hairier areas with paper. When removing the mask, let the customer move their mouth slightly to loosen the mask powder, then remove the mask from bottom to top.

3. Honey Wax Mask

After melting, it becomes semi-fluid. The main components are paraffin oil, beeswax, and mineral oil. When applied to the skin, the honey wax mask forms a closed state, with strong penetration of effective ingredients, promoting the secretion of sebaceous glands and sweat glands, replenishing the skin's moisture and nutrients, making the skin moisturized and supple, enhancing elasticity. Suitable for dry skin, normal skin, aging skin, and can also be used for care on all parts of the body, but acne inflamed skin and sensitive skin should not use it.

4. Dryable Gel Mask

Appears as a translucent viscous substance. The main components are film-forming agents, soluble agents, moisturizers, and ethanol. After drying, it forms a closed film with strong affinity to the skin. It has a significant effect on shrinking pores and cleaning the skin. Suitable for cleansing and tightening normal and oily skin.

5. Hydrating Gel Mask

Appears as a translucent viscous substance. The main components are moisturizers and nutritional additives, with a mild nature and strong hydration properties. Suitable for dry skin, aging skin, or for eye care.
