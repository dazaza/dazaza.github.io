"The 15-second trailer of the theatrical version of 'A Chinese Ghost Story' was accidentally leaked and went viral online."

by mlwang on 2011-09-28 16:47:34

Recently, the 15-second trailer of the theatrical version of the online game "A Chinese Ghost Story" starring Liu Yifei was accidentally leaked and went viral on the Internet, becoming one of the hottest videos recently. It has attracted a lot of attention from fans and players, once again pushing "A Chinese Ghost Story" to new heights.

Creative! The big online game promotes Liu Yifei's stunning transformation. According to insiders, this film is produced by the Rayfire Studio of NetEase's "A Chinese Ghost Story" game. As the spokesperson, Liu Yifei makes her first appearance in an online game movie with heartfelt performance. In the trailer, although Liu Yifei's dual persona as the "black and white Xiaojian" flashes by quickly, it still excites the players, showing them a completely different Liu Yifei. Portraying Xiaojian with two contrasting personalities - black and white - is a significant transformation in her career. Liu Yifei also expressed that she believes this will be a high-quality theatrical version, with thrilling battles and rich emotions intertwined, offering players a very rich experience. It is definitely worth looking forward to.

The talented director of "Storm Riders 2": Must personally experience the game. Known as a talented director, Pang Shun has directed many fantasy action blockbusters. This time, he will personally take charge as the chief director of the theatrical version of "A Chinese Ghost Story". Pang Shun frankly stated that "A Chinese Ghost Story" is a masterpiece in terms of visuals, audio, storyline, and gameplay. Many scenes and characters in the game are highly creative. The most special aspect is that you can choose your desired role and immerse yourself emotionally into this computer world. Only by personally trying out the game can you experience its wonderful and unique effects and feelings. This theatrical version is expected to become a milestone in the history of Chinese online games.
