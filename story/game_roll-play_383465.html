The Legend of Sacred Path: Kingsoft's mysterious post-modern wuxia game of 2011, as a transformation work of Kingsoft's wuxia series, the unique character designs and grand 3D visuals complement each other, creating a distinctive visual effect. The game will officially meet players in March 2011, stay tuned!

Anger of the Sky: Kingsoft's new 2D Western fantasy MMORPG masterpiece of 2011. In the rifts between foreign lands and the central continent, the game centers on the opposition between humans, demons, beasts, and mecha, highlighting the conflict between magic and mecha civilizations. The rich and varied transformation system is the game's biggest feature, with dazzling light effects giving players an immersive fantasy experience. The grand game world, over a hundred transformations, and dreamlike game scenes, along with thoughtful and detailed design concepts, Kingsoft's 2D fantasy online game Anger of the Sky is set to explode in 2011!

Tale of the Red Mansion (tentative name): Whether it's TV dramas, movies, or games, time-travel themes have been extremely popular this year. Kingsoft's 2DQ version of the fantastical turn-based MMORPG Tale of the Red Mansion uses the main storyline and era background of one of the four great classical novels, Dream of the Red Chamber, as its basis. Players travel through time to the fantastical world of the Red Mansion, helping famous characters complete tasks and fighting evil forces like demons and bandits. The game appropriately incorporates elements of humor, parody, and time travel, and innovatively introduces eight new turn-based gameplay mechanics, giving turn-based battles a whole new meaning. In the first half of 2011, players can enter Tale of the Red Mansion and traverse through time.

Moonlight Legend: Adapted from Kingsoft's classic single-player game of the same name, developed by Xishanju Studio, the online version of Moonlight Legend will reunite with you in 2011 to create the pinnacle of extreme action online games. The game allows players to relive the classics of single-player wuxia games through its infinite role story mode and original family cultivation gameplay; its full-screen lock-free attack mode that gets the adrenaline pumping, the evolved characteristic combo system, as well as the Three Alliances War and special dungeons perfectly showcase the essence of swords and chivalry, providing an extreme action experience.
