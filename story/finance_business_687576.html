When a class of 48 third-grade students has 18 wearing glasses, the merchants start to secretly rejoice. Once nearsightedness is confirmed, it's time to get glasses — and the "glasses economy" seems to have a long spring. Be it high-tech full-featured frame glasses or fashionable contact lenses that enhance one’s appearance, glasses are also narrating a commercial myth of an era...

1. Radiation-proof, titanium material glasses play with "high technology"

After integrating many fashion elements, traditional glasses have been regarded by people as "facial costumes," becoming an indispensable item for urbanites.

Behind the strong sense of fashion, another trend is sweeping through the entire glasses market — the "high technology" embedded within glasses. The frames are made from titanium materials used in airplanes, the lenses not only prevent radiation and UV rays but also reduce eye fatigue and change color... These "technological elements" enrich the functions of glasses while also significantly increasing their value.

"Nowadays, frames are mainly divided into titanium and plastic types. Titanium frames are more expensive, plastic ones are relatively cheaper, but it still depends on the brand," Ms. Wang, the store manager, said. The price differences among different levels of frames are also significant. "There are over a hundred varieties of frames in the store. The cheapest frames cost only a hundred or two hundred yuan, while the most expensive ones can reach over twenty thousand yuan."

"The current lenses integrate various functions such as reducing eye fatigue, preventing radiation, and blocking UV rays," Tong Min introduced. However, these lenses are also much more expensive than traditional ones. "A pair of anti-fatigue lenses usually costs around a thousand yuan, about four hundred yuan more expensive than traditional lenses."

2. Fashionable faces: Young people favor colored contact lenses

Spring is coming, and Yingzi wants to take wedding photos during the blooming season. But she is worried because she is nearsighted and her eyes seem lifeless due to long-term use of glasses. Since wedding photos are a once-in-a-lifetime event, she fears her dull eyes will ruin the photos. Fortunately, her friend Tingting recommended a solution — black colored contact lenses, which apparently make eyes appear bigger and more spirited.

Don't want to wear framed glasses? No worries, there are contact lenses. Want your eyes to be even more enchanting? There's a trick — wear colored contact lenses (commonly known as "beautifying lenses"). As the saying goes, eyes are the windows to the soul. To make this "window" brighter and more charming, girls spare no effort. After applying eyeliner, eyeshadow, and false lashes, they now focus on changing the color of their irises, and thus, beautifying lenses naturally came into being.
