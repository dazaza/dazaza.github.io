Be careful to avoid getting "Q" for ten thousand years: Tips to prevent your QQ password from being cracked

by cntans on 2009-10-23 03:49:39

Generally, those who want to steal our QQ passwords will use various password cracking software. When our QQ is targeted by others, how can we protect our password? Here we introduce several common and effective methods.

1. "Q" without a trace - Preventing local cracking

Local cracking usually involves cracking the information files saved on the local hard drive after QQ login to obtain the password. Obviously, to block this loophole, we must start with "login" and "logout", trying to protect your information files (including chat record files, as these could also lead to password leakage), or simply delete them. Besides, for those using brute force software to steal accounts, the setting of the "QQ owner's" password itself is also very crucial.

1. Add a "password lock" to the information

For friends at home or with fixed internet access, QQ's "message protection mode" is a good choice. Choosing this method to log in, even if someone gets your chat record file, they cannot do anything without the password (it is recommended not to use QQ's login password as the message protection password, otherwise this function would lose its meaning). When enabling the "message protection" function, it is recommended to enable "password hint" simultaneously. This way, if you forget the message protection password, you won't be locked out yourself. Specific settings are: click "menu" > "system settings", then switch to "local security", check "enable local message", then check "enable local message encryption password hint". After filling in the prompt question and answer, click "complete". When you forget the message protection password, you can enter through the "password hint". Security and inconvenience seem to be a pair of enemies. For safety, please endure a little operational inconvenience.

2. Let the messages completely "disappear"

Particularly emphasized is the situation of using the internet in an internet cafe or non-fixed place. Due to the special nature of internet cafes, when online, we should choose "internet cafe mode" in the "login mode" of the login interface. This way, when closing QQ, QQ will automatically remind us whether to delete the login records of that number. Clicking "yes" ensures that our QQ information will not remain on the computer at the internet cafe. This method is simple and effective, providing good protection for your privacy!

Besides using QQ's own "internet cafe mode", we can also manually clear the passwords, chat records, etc., left behind after using QQ with other software, thereby protecting the QQ password. Here we recommend the small tool "QQ Bodyguard", hoping it will be useful to everyone.

The use of "QQ Bodyguard" is very simple: fill in the QQ number in the "settings" section, select the installation path of QQ, and click "start cleaning" to completely clear the information left on the computer.

3. Patience test - Use randomness to counter brute force

Using exhaustive methods to crack QQ passwords theoretically can certainly succeed, but its efficiency is extremely low and very time-consuming. Therefore, as long as our QQ password is set more complexly, the cracking will be very difficult.

2. A miss is as good as a mile - Prevent remote cracking

Although local cracking is relatively simple, because it has strong limitations in terms of time and space, it determines that the hackers adopting this cracking method are not common. Relatively speaking, due to the complexity and uncertainty of the network, the situation of QQ passwords being stolen on the network is more likely to occur. Then, for the dazzling array of remote cracking methods, how should we guard against them? Below, we will explain one by one.

1. Guard against online password cracking

Remote online cracking of QQ passwords still uses the principle of exhaustive technology to guess all possible inputs on the keyboard. The only difference is that it can automatically verify the password with Tencent servers. Therefore, guarding against it requires Tencent Company to make security settings on the server to prevent online cracking, and users need to set complex passwords. This way, online theft of passwords can be effectively prevented.

2. Guard against cracking

Actually, it is quite normal for our computers to be infected with Trojan horses. But how can we safely use QQ even after being infected with Trojan viruses? I think the first thing to do is to "renew". Hmm, don't understand? Haha! It means to use the latest version of QQ! The reason is simple: the new version of QQ is an improvement based on the old version, so there will definitely be significant improvements in functionality, and there will definitely be a lot of work done on security. Using the latest version of QQ is like having applied countless patches. Of course, using QQ this way feels "Q cool".

But relying solely on the latest version of QQ is not enough. Let's take a look at how to securely set up QQ. First, when entering the password during login, use the soft keyboard provided by QQ, because most of the "QQ" stealing software relies on recording your keystrokes in the background to steal QQ passwords. Using the soft keyboard to input passwords, even if the "Q" stealing software can record mouse actions, it is not easy to get your real password.

3. Be careful to get ten thousand years of "Q" - Do a good job of password protection

Below, we provide some common techniques and protective measures. As long as you apply these techniques while chatting on QQ, you can reduce the occurrence of QQ password theft to a certain extent.

1. Of course, the first thing is to stay alert. Many friends set the same password for their email, QQ, and even bank accounts for convenience. This is a very bad practice. Once someone else gets any one of the passwords for your email or QQ, it is equivalent to knowing all your privacy on the internet. Therefore, categorizing and setting passwords properly and keeping them well protected is the inevitable choice for every friend who values security. Besides, please do not randomly click on URLs sent by others, and do not open unfamiliar images or programs. This can effectively prevent remote password theft.

2. If your QQ password is stolen and you want to retrieve it, the best method is through password protection. So remember to apply for password protection for your QQ account and keep track of the ID number, password protection questions, and answers used during registration. Otherwise, if your number is stolen, it will be difficult to retrieve it! Applying for password protection is actually very simple. After opening QQ, click on "apply for password protection" in the lower right corner of the login panel to enter the application page. I don't need to say more about the next steps, right?

3. Use excellent antivirus software and firewalls, and update the virus database in time. Turn on the virus monitoring of the antivirus software and the firewall when surfing the internet. Because many malicious software that steal QQ passwords send emails to notify the "QQ" thieves after obtaining the QQ password. If a firewall is installed, it can effectively intercept such emails. If the "Q" thief does not receive the email, then even if the password is recorded, there is no danger.

4. Change your password frequently. It is best to change it once a month. When changing, you can set the password according to a rule familiar to you (for example, set the last few digits according to different months), so as not to forget your own password after frequent changes.

5. Reverse input of the password. Suppose your QQ password is "4008". When entering it, do not input it sequentially, because if you are infected with a Trojan horse, the keyboard operation will be recorded. You can first input "408", then move the cursor behind "4" and input "0". In this way, the password you input is still "4008", but from the perspective of the "Trojan horse", what you input is "4080", thus protecting your password.
