About APC:

APC, under Schneider Electric, is a global leader in critical power and cooling services, providing advanced products, software, and systems for home users, office spaces, data centers, and production manufacturing application environments. With its strength, experience, and extensive Schneider Critical Power and Cooling Service network, APC offers comprehensive planning, seamless installation, and maintenance solutions throughout the entire service lifecycle. Through its unique relentless innovation, APC provides pioneering energy efficiency solutions for critical technology and industrial applications. In 2007, APC was acquired by Schneider Electric and successfully merged with MGE UPS to become a subsidiary of Schneider Electric, providing critical power and cooling services. In 2008, APC's revenue reached 2.6 billion euros (including APC-MGE sales of $3.6 billion), with 12,000 employees globally. APC solutions include uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), precision cooling products, racks, physical security, and planning and management software, including the industry’s most comprehensive integrated power, cooling, and management solution, the APC InfraStruXure? architecture. Schneider Electric now has 114,000 employees, operating in 100 countries worldwide, with a 2008 turnover of 18.3 billion euros. To learn more about APC, please visit www.apc.com.
