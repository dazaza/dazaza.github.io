The difference between Fuding old white tea and new tea

by shulin730715 on 2012-02-13 20:57:51

Due to the healthy trend advocated by tea, and the government's vigorous promotion of tea culture and continuous promotion of local tea industry, Fuding white tea is no longer a fresh term for tea lovers and tea vendors across the country. Fuding white tea is favored by the tea industry and the business circle because of its excellent taste, extraordinary efficacy, and better preservation in a suitable environment. Fuding old white tea can be used as a drug guide in modern Chinese medicine prescriptions, and the efficacy of old white tea becomes more significant over time, which cannot be compared with new tea. In order to enable everyone to better distinguish between Fuding old white tea and new tea, we will briefly introduce the distinguishing methods.

1. Distinction of appearance and tea fragrance

From the appearance, Fuding old white tea has an overall sensory black-brown dullness, but it is still possible to identify some white down on the tea leaves. Grab a handful of tea leaves and smell them, the aged aroma is arrayed, the downy aroma is heavy but not turbid, which can immediately refresh people; New tea is like the White Peony that can be seen on the market. The appearance of the tea leaves is brown-green needle white and covered with white down. Especially the White Peony made from tea leaves during the spring tea season has a thicker white down at the bottom of the leaves and the top buds than those produced in other seasons. The relatively fine and good white tea must have a downy aroma in its aroma, and has a hint of sweetness.

2. Distinction of tea water and tea aroma

Use 90 to 95 degree boiling water to brew Fuding old white tea and new tea leaves, pour the tea water into porcelain small tea cups respectively, and then smell the aroma and taste the tea. The aroma of old white tea is clear and slightly downy, and the first steeping has a faint herbal aroma. Smell it for a few seconds and it can refresh you. The taste is mellow with sweetness filling the mouth; The aroma of new tea is faintly downy, with a fresh and fresh atmosphere, the taste is relatively light and tender, without other miscellaneous tastes, only the sweet taste of tea, making people feel comfortable after drinking.

3. Tea steeping endurance

Old white tea is quite durable, it can dispel wind-cold, and under normal brewing conditions, it can reach more than twenty infusions, and the taste is still good later on. If possible, it can be boiled, because boiling old white tea can fully reveal its essence; New tea can be brewed according to personal habits, but if tea has seven infusions and the taste is still good, it is already considered good white tea.

I hope the above three points can help friends who love Fuding white tea. Also, I would like to remind tea lovers that drinking tea in moderation is good for health, and over-drinking is counterproductive. I also hope that senior experts in the tea industry can provide more guidance and corrections on the above content so that everyone can understand more about tea knowledge.
