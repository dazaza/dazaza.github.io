Kunming Railway Public Security Office at Kunming railway station police station in time and properly handled a mentally disturbed passenger waving a knife self-mutilation incident to ensure the safety of the lives and property of the passengers. At 7:35 on February 2, ticketing duty police officer Zhou Wei found No. 25 window (ticketing hall outside the day of the window) before the queue ticket buyers suddenly began shouting and running away. A man holding a kitchen knife in his right hand, waving shouting suicide. The situation is critical! Zhou Wei immediately reported to the on-duty team leader Wang Hongcai Political Commissar. Chen Hongyue, director of the Department of Public Security, who was checking the work on the second floor platform, rushed to the scene in front of the No. 25 window to command the disposal work personally. According to the deployment of the disposal plan, one is that four elite police forces quickly use anti-stab vests, anti-explosion forks and other treatment tools to prepare for treatment on site; two is to notify the monitoring of the No. 25 window for the whole process of recording; three is to organize two police forces around the No. 25 window near the queue waiting for the ticket buyers to be dispersed, and the area will be sealed off and no personnel will be allowed to pass through. From the abnormal behavior of the man with the knife and his muttering, it was preliminarily judged that the man should be mentally disturbed. According to the experience of dealing with mentally disturbed persons in the past, Chen Hongyue arranged for a police force to talk with him face to face to attract his attention, find breakthroughs in his words and try to ease his emotions and put down the knife voluntarily; he arranged two special police officers and two police officers behind him. Ready to制服him at any time. Through dialogue, the man kept saying "I am going to Guangzhou, I want to buy train tickets", through this information, the treatment personnel comforted the man's emotions on the one hand "the train tickets you need have been bought for you", on the other hand, they found a train ticket not far from the man on the ground so that he could put down the knife and take the train ticket. After hesitating for a moment, the man slowly put down the knife in his hand and bent down to take the train ticket on the ground. Chen Hongyue seized this opportunity and immediately signaled the handling police officers to act with gestures. Four policemen wearing anti-stab vests attacked from the rear and side of the man at the same time and instantly制服the man. Subsequently, the man was taken to the police station for proper control. After checking the man's belongings, an ID card and a hard seat train ticket from Kunming to Changsha on the same day were found. The ID card shows: the man surnamed Yang (26 years old, Yi nationality, from Dali, Yunnan). Preliminary understanding, the man took the knife from a restaurant on Beijing Road and rushed to the train station ticket office. Since the man has no companion, whether he has mental illness, travel reasons and other situations can not be verified temporarily. At present, the police station has contacted 120 to send the man to the hospital for treatment, and further investigation will be carried out when his spirit recovers.
