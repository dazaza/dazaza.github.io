Who owes who?

by anonymous on 2012-03-09 02:37:32

(I) Once again, it's raining outside the window. Whose tears are these? You said that if I exposed my sadness without reservation to those around me, the only ones who would feel pain are those who care about me. As for the person causing my sadness, they would feel no guilt at all. After hearing this, I secretly started changing myself, trying to appear as a carefree and silly girl in front of those who love me. Was it because time moved too quickly or was it because I moved too slowly? Was it because I didn't do enough or was it because you were already tired of me? You left, leaving nothing behind...

(II) It's still a starless night sky, and I'm alone gazing up at it without shedding a tear. You said that this world doesn't believe in tears, and when facing problems, one must learn to confront them fearlessly. Crying is merely a sign of weakness; tactical knives. Tears solve nothing, and on the contrary, your crying is a form of surrender. After hearing this, I no longer cried easily, and even if I wanted to cry, I eventually chose to smile. Now, everything has changed unexpectedly, taking away everything and leaving behind only sorrow.

(III) That familiar melody drifted by my window once more. I fell silent. You said that it was your favorite song. This song was very sad, revealing just how much sorrow and warmth you needed deep down inside. I remained silent. Quietly, I downloaded your favorite song onto my phone and listened to it constantly, hoping to explore the sorrow in your heart and offer whatever warmth I could provide. I thought this would move you, that we could quietly walk together. But alas, things did not go as planned! It turns out I only moved myself.

(IV) You said, if we have the fate in this life, we'll hold hands and walk together. And you also said, if we meet again in the next life, we'll never part. These ethereal dialogues easily stirred my heartstrings. Your words were deeply imprinted in my mind, never forgotten. Silently, I paid attention and waited in ways you didn't know. Even though time had passed and everything had changed, I still stood where you left, waiting!

(V) You said that life is like a train, passing through many beautiful landscapes. No matter how reluctant, we must wave goodbye to those beautiful scenes that don't belong to us. In life, separation is inevitable, whether it be friendships or family ties. When it's time to say goodbye, we must learn to let go gracefully and not stay stuck in the past. Under the guidance of time and your teachings, I experienced much and gradually learned to let go. Despite this, there are some separations that still leave me heartbroken. Before I fully emerged from the shadow of separation, you disappeared. After turning around, we became strangers, forever apart. You no longer belonged to me, and I no longer belonged to you!

(VI) You said our friendship will last forever, and we will be happier and more joyful than anyone else. You said meeting me was the most beautiful encounter in your life. You said my world was too sorrowful, and you felt pity and concern for me. I never dared to believe I would encounter such profound friendship, unseen before yet so deep. I worried it might be a dream, fearing I couldn't bear losing again, and even more afraid that my sorrow would affect you. I kept pushing away, filled with contradictions, and finally decided to hurry up and get better, so I could give you happiness and joy. Gains and losses, meetings and departures, are all too common, leaving me at a loss. This is also good, leaving this place of sorrow is also my blessing for you.

(VII) If one day I die, please scatter my ashes into the sea where we promised to meet in the next life. If there is a next life, please let me transform into a pink dandelion. In this life, I owe her too much, so in the next life, let me become a dandelion who gives up everything to wait for the love in her heart. This is also my vow to her in the next life (Vow: Love you for eternity). In this life, you are my sister, and in the next life, I will wait for you. This life is destined to make me think of him for a lifetime, so in the next life, let him think of me for a lifetime. It's what he owes me in this life.
