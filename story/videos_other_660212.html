Winter clothing on clearance, spring clothing now available

by shenzhen129r on 2012-02-07 14:46:46

The bosses of clothing stores who open their doors for business have taken down the winter coats and put up bright spring outfits. Keywords such as retro and chiffon are quietly setting the trend for this spring's fashion, receiving much favor from consumers. In recent days, the retail business in the clothing market has been quite booming, but it is hard to see any wholesalers around. The market trend shows winter wear clearance sales and spring wear on display. Walking into any clothing market in Lusong, one can feel a sense of spring warmth. The cotton-padded jackets that filled the shops before the Spring Festival are mostly off the shelves, replaced by some spring outfits. Flowing chiffon is now prominently displayed by merchants. "Before the Spring Festival, I had nearly 500 cotton-padded jackets in stock, but now I need to hurry to sell the spring wear," Mr. Wang, who does women's clothing business at Ruiyuan Clothing Wholesale Market, said helplessly, "On February 4th, it will be the beginning of spring, and it is impossible for wholesalers to come back for more cotton-padded jackets. I can only sell them at a low price." Mr. Wang has been in the clothing business for 12 years. He said, "This year, chiffon shirts will become mainstream, they can be worn as outerwear or layered under other clothes. Whether you're a girl who likes cute styles or a professional woman who prefers a neat look, you should own such a shirt." In Chen Guo's store, which sells women's clothing on the second floor of Zhichao Fashion Plaza, there are many retro round-necked shirts and collarless knit cardigans. Chen Guo said: "This year, the retro style will still be popular. At least, currently, the mainstream in the market is still retro." Ms. Chen Xiaoli, the color image director of a personal image design company in Zhuzhou, expressed: "A chiffon shirt with a touch of palace style and wooden ruffles is absolutely a must-have item in a woman's wardrobe this year."
