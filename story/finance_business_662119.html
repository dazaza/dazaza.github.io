What issues should male infertility patients pay attention to? A child is the source of joy and laughter in a family, and a family without children cannot be considered a complete family. Therefore, in daily social life, it is important to prevent infertility. Below, we will introduce what issues male infertility patients should pay attention to.

What are the taboos for infertile patients in their daily lives?

1. Avoid smoking and alcohol, as nicotine in cigarettes can cause chromosomal abnormalities, and alcohol can directly damage the testicles.

2. Avoid spicy foods, as they can induce infections and inflammation of the reproductive system.

3. Celery and garlic have spermicidal effects and should be consumed in moderation.

4. Eat more fresh vegetables and seafood; consume fruits rich in vitamin C, such as bananas, tomatoes, and oranges.

5. Tea is rich in vitamins and antioxidants, so it can be consumed more often.

6. Avoid high-temperature environments such as saunas and boiler rooms.

7. Avoid radiation, such as X-rays, computers, and radio communications.

8. Avoid toxic substance contamination, such as pesticides, paint, and batteries.

9. Avoid taking drugs harmful to fertility: chemotherapy drugs, some antihypertensive drugs, some antibiotics such as neomycin, erythromycin, gentamicin, urea, metronidazole, etc.

10. Avoid prolonged sitting, avoid tight underwear; avoid overwork, avoid decreased immunity.

11. Maintain a good psychological state; understand what causes blood in semen; identify the female ovulation period to establish the optimal pregnancy period.

Finally, male infertility experts believe that infertility caused by sexual problems is often overlooked. There is no exact calculation of how much infertility is caused by sexual problems, which involves many aspects such as sexual knowledge, sexual love, and sexual function.
