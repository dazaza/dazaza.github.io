Wu Qilong's marriage with Ma Yashu is in trouble, and the broker discloses that they have agreed to divorce.

by zhlnjxvr80 on 2011-12-20 16:02:15

Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu

Previously, rumors of the crisis in Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu's three-year marriage were rampant. Although the parties involved had firmly denied these rumors, Ma Yashu's agent, Ruan Qianzhi, finally admitted on the previous day that Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu had agreed to divorce at the beginning of the year but had not yet found the time to complete the procedures, keeping the divorce news confidential. Additionally, Ma Yashu's meeting with a so-called new love interest, an Australian handsome man, at a hotel in Hengdian was witnessed. Afterwards, Ma Yashu’s response regarding her new boyfriend left much room for interpretation. From various clues, it seems their marriage might indeed be a thing of the past.

Barbecue with Foreign Handsome Man

Recently, there have been continuous reports of Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu's divorce, with claims that the male party gave 6 houses and a million yuan as alimony. In July, while filming "Who Knows Women's Hearts" in Hengdian, Ma Yashu once stated that they were not divorced, saying, “If I had six houses, why would I need to work hard shooting?” However, just days later, intimate photos of Ma Yashu and a foreign handsome man barbecuing at a restaurant in Hengdian were exposed. It was reported that this gentleman owns a family business in Beijing, is young, handsome, and wealthy, and has been acquainted with Ma Yashu for over a year.

According to the reporter who captured these scenes: “They were indeed together in the room for three days and nights without leaving. When Ma Yashu came out to eat barbecue with him, she had no makeup on, looking somewhat weary. The two were inseparable like chewing gum.” Afterwards, according to the Morning News report, Ma Yashu admitted in an interview that the foreign handsome man was her new boyfriend, claiming he was just a good friend. But when asked if such intimacy would make Wu Qilong jealous, Ma Yashu only said, “I can tell you that after he (referring to the foreign friend) learned about my situation, he started pursuing me. Please believe me, I will not do anything to harm Qilong.”

Few Meetings After Marriage

After a brief honeymoon period, Wu Qilong and Ma Yashu seemed to enter a difficult phase. Last August, Wu Qilong revealed that they met only five times a year. Ma Yashu indeed had many jobs after marriage, and Wu Qilong had complained that she took more roles than he did. This foreign handsome man chased from Beijing to Hengdian, and although Ma Yashu claimed he came just out of friendship, curiously, her agent also disclosed that Ma Yashu had mentioned someone was pursuing her.

Unwilling to Clarify Things Clearly

The Agent's Ambiguous Statement

The so-called agreed divorce indicates both parties have intentions to divorce, but the delay in formalizing the procedures suggests some lingering hope. Previously, the event of agreeing to divorce was confirmed by Ma Yashu's agent. Subsequently, our reporter again contacted both agents. Ruan Qianzhi admitted that Ma and Wu had agreed to divorce, while Ren still insisted that the divorce matter was unclear and refused to respond.

Ma Yashu's Hesitant Answer

When questioned about her rendezvous with the foreign handsome man, Ma Yashu's statement left much room for imagination: “After he learned about my situation, he began to pursue me, and I won't hurt Qilong.” What exactly does “my situation” refer to? Already divorced? A marriage in name only? Using a third party to regain Wu Qilong's attention?

Weak Foundations in Three-Year Marriage

In March 2006, Wu Qilong acknowledged having registered his marriage with Ma Yashu in Kunming.

In May 2007, Wu Qilong claimed that the couple rarely met, and there were no immediate plans for children.

In July 2008, Wu Qilong was seen having midnight snacks with Yang Jinhua and then driving her home.

In September 2008, Wu Qilong and Yang Jinhua were spotted partying all night, leading to rumors of marital trouble.

In September 2008, Ma Yashu told the media that she "trusted her husband," and Wu Qilong stated, “I am not someone who falls in love at first sight.”

In May 2009, rumors of Wu Qilong's marital changes surfaced, confirmed by insiders, but Ma Yashu only said that the two were fine.

In July 2009, marital change rumors resurfaced, with claims that Wu Qilong exchanged freedom for NT$1 billion, but Ma Yashu denied it.

In August 2009, the agent announced their divorce, coinciding with photos of Ma Yashu living with a foreigner being released.
