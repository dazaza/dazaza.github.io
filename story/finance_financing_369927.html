2. How to Make Money (Key Point)

Making money through mass emails:

a. Add friends, as many as possible: (Sometimes due to network delays, adding friends might fail. In such cases, skip this step and proceed to the next.)

In the search bar, type any word, preferably something simple so that more people appear, like searching for "sky"

Click Browse, and the following results will appear:

Click "Add Friend."

b. Join groups, choose those with more members:

Click Groups, select All:

Choose groups with more members:

After joining a group, you can also add friends within the group. Our goal is to increase the number of friends as much as possible.

Because there's a built-in function to send mass emails to friends.

Sometimes due to network delays, adding friends might fail, but try again later.

c. Key: Mass Email Sending

All the work done before was preparation for this step.

Click Mail:

Then click, a new dialog box will pop up, click "Send Mass Mail," which means sending emails in bulk:

It will display as follows, click Select All, then follow the instructions below, and finally click Send.

Other Ways to Earn Money:

Referring others can also earn you money: Under My Cash, you can see referral links. If others register through this link, you will also earn points, which will automatically be converted into US dollars (key point).

Uploading pictures that get viewed can also earn you points.

Others viewing your homepage or the groups you created can also earn you points.

The official has not defined the exchange rate, but based on my own observation, about 100 IPs roughly equal 0.6 US dollars.

Points need to reach the minimum limit to be converted into US dollars; otherwise, they will be reset to zero the next day.
