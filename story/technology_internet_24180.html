Sohu blog small note function officially launched

by stephenzhai on 2007-07-06 23:11:59

Speaking of a small note, can make people cause infinite association, school to the back seat of the girl secretly passed a small note, the exam when cheating with a small note... ... Now, the romantic delivery has made its way to blogs.

Recently, the small slip function of Sohu blog was officially launched. On the one hand, the small note realizes the online communication between the two strange blogs within the Sohu blog, which is completely different from the communication methods of QQ and MSN, adding greater fun to the blog. Some people say that the Internet will enter the age of blog chat, and there is some truth in it.

On the other hand, it solves the problem of privacy. In the past, although the whisper function of Sohu blog provides a more private way of communication for bloggers, after all, it can not be completely invisible, people can still see that some day, some time, so-and-so left a whisper to so-and-so. However, the small note function, which allows bloggers to send information to each other, makes up for this deficiency.

Benefits of Sohu blog note:

1, the realization of online blog "instant communication" within the blog.

2, information confidentiality, one-to-one communication between blogs, fast and convenient.

3. Connect Sohu blog, ChinaRen campus, ChinaRen alumni record, ChinaRen community, etc., to communicate with friends in different places more convenient and fast.
