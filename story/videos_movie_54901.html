Introduction to the Plot of Turns Out I'm Not Handsome

Since he can remember, "Zhuang" has always thought of himself as a handsome guy, and this belief is not baseless. From childhood to adulthood, whether relatives, elders, snack stall owners, girls filling out questionnaires, ticket scalpers, or classmates borrowing notes, everyone always sincerely calls him "Handsome Guy." So, naturally, he believed he was indeed handsome... The person who woke Zhuang up from this handsome dream was none other than his two classmates and best friends—Si Si, a roommate full of strange ideas, brimming with humor, and as the vice president of the Pigeon Club, whose only soulmate is the pigeon "Lao Wang." And A Kang, a self-proclaimed playboy and founder/president of the Pigeon Research Club, who talks about love experiences but whose advice always leads to failure when followed.

The tangled relationship between Cindy and Zhuang becomes even more complicated with the addition of the beautiful Qiaoping, forming a triangle. And A Kang encounters the toughest love triangle of his life, unable to help Zhuang because he's dealing with his own romantic entanglement with Mannan.
