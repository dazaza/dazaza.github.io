After work, I love exercising the most. In addition to going to the gym three times a week, I also often play badminton with friends, or climb mountains near Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces on weekends. While exercising, I can also breathe fresh air and enjoy the beautiful scenery. Why not do it!

What I particularly like is running. Every morning, I jog 8 laps in the neighborhood, or on Wednesdays, I join my friends from the outdoor club for running training at Yuanshen Forest Park. After running, we have dinner together and chat about recent events, which is quite enjoyable.

If you ask me about sports health supplements, apart from consuming protein powder for muscle definition during fitness workouts, I usually just drink purified water during other sports activities. However, last month, a company called US Nature Sunshine collaborated with our club for an event and sponsored us with a chlorophyll beverage for use during exercise. They set up a table every time we ran, with a large bucket of purified water and dark green chlorophyll concentrate. After mixing it, they provided it to everyone for free, claiming that it could increase the oxygen content of hemoglobin and help improve athletic performance... but I didn't take it too seriously...

The first time I drank it, I thought the taste was pretty good, cool and refreshing, with a hint of mint. It was very satisfying.

Every time I went running, I would mix a bottle with purified water to drink. After drinking it for a few days, I felt that it seemed more comfortable than before, even though it was so hot this summer. Although I sweated a lot, I didn't feel particularly stuffy or uncomfortable. After running about ten laps, my heart wasn't as tired.

Then I bought two bottles myself to drink while exercising.

It happened that Nature Sunshine was promoting the product for the club. For 148 yuan, you get one bottle and get another for free. Each bottle is 467ml, and each time I only need to mix 5-10ml with a bottle of purified water, so it's not expensive. But it really does feel good when you drink it.

Especially since I sometimes smoke when I'm busy with work, I found that after smoking, drinking this minty cool beverage makes my throat feel better. It's said to help detoxify, which is great.

A few days ago, I participated in the Yangzhou Marathon with my club friends and brought it along. The result was great! Although I didn't win any prizes, my performance was much better than last year's race. On the same course, I finished 35 minutes faster than last year!! I am very satisfied with my personal achievement! Of course, this is also related to my year-long commitment to training...

However, the liquid chlorophyll from Nature Sunshine is indeed pretty good, and I recommend it to friends who like exercising!
