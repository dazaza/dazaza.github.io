The question now is, some people hope so and some do not.

The competition for the "Happy Girl" from 4 to 3 is about to begin. Last week, my premonition was correct: although it is already autumn, it is not yet time for the petals to fall. That night, Huang Ying saved herself with her determination and passion while singing "Having Nothing." She stood barefoot on a high platform, and at that moment, I felt she was like a blade warrior.

Currently, Yu KeWei and Jiang YingRong are the hottest contenders for the championship. If Huang Ying continues to perform strongly over the weekend and takes it up a notch, then this year's top three "Happy Girls" may very well be swept by three Chengdu girls. However, this obviously does not align with the broad representativeness that a nationwide talent show should have. Therefore, the possibility of the three Chengdu "Happy Girls" sweeping the top three seems unlikely this week.

But just like stocks, love, football, and other unreliable things, if everyone thinks this way, then it's hard to say...

Alright, let's get to the point! Who will leave among the four contestants this weekend!?

"The autumn waves reflect beauty, under the rain moon the soul flies to heaven; who will prevail, the fiery hero ascends the golden throne."
