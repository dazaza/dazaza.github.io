With long nails on ten fingers, will you miss me?

by lili1 on 2012-02-22 22:54:05

With long nails on ten fingers, would you think of me?

The night is quiet and cool wind blows, messing up my hair.

I am used to walking alone in the campus, and used to listening to sentimental music with earphones plugged in, " ... If life ends here, without me from now on, I will find an angel to take my place to love you... "

"Quietly sitting by your side, how much time like this will we still have? I will face the light outside the window and firmly remember your smiling profile; I said farewell wouldn't be sad, this is my only deception to you, because I like your eyes the most, so beautiful that they don't suit crying..."

Whenever I hear touching parts, I always look up at the bright crescent moon in the sky. Only in this way can I prevent tears from falling unintentionally...

You always say I'm too fragile, not at all like a man. Haha, without experiencing the helplessness of love, how could you understand its sourness; maybe, it's because I love too deeply; or maybe, it's because I feel reluctant about my love.

What word should be used to describe my current life? Desolate? Empty? Or more like decadent? I don't know, I really don't know...

I don't know when it started, but I fell in love with letting my nails grow long. Looking at my long nails on ten fingers, I would unintentionally think of you. Remembering how you once covered your long fingers in front of my eyes and proudly said: "How about that, isn't it very nice looking, haha~" "Yeah, it really looks good."

I sincerely praised, "In the future, I will also let my nails grow long, let's see whose are better~". "What would a guy like you do with such long nails? And they must be ugly as hell!", perhaps it was from that time on, that I gradually developed the habit of growing my nails. Until now, every time a nail breaks, I still feel heartbroken.

I remember you liked to dye your nails; one time, your black long nails reached out to me, which scared me quite a bit. Seeing your excited expression, I couldn't help but be influenced, saying that your dyed nails were also a different kind of scenery; just, you spent a lot of energy treating your fungal nails before, it seems like the chemical components of nail polish can easily cause fungal nails right? Besides, I still prefer the simpler you.

So after that, I mentioned to you not to dye your nails anymore. I remember you reluctantly agreed then, and indeed rarely did I see you with dyed nails in front of me afterwards; actually, I know that you still secretly dyed your nails, but every time you saw me, you would carefully remove the color. But thinking of your excitement over your dyed nails, I didn't want to say anything else. Maybe, girls in their blooming years all like to be like that.

Just, I wonder if the present you would still continue to dye your nails? Would you still proudly show off your nails in front of others?

I once thought, as long as I cover my eyes, I won't be able to see this world

I once thought, as long as I cover my ears, I won't be able to hear all troubles

I once thought, as long as I stop my steps, my heart won't travel far

I once thought, the love I need is just a hug

Smiling with slightly curled lips, huh~ what's wrong with me now? Why am I so melancholy and sentimental? Why do I often recall various things in the past due to some trivial matters?

Time and again, memories draw life into a circle; and I, have turned countless times in the same place, singing a monologue on the stage, lonely and unable to be free; I always hope to return to the original place where we first met, the rainy season in early summer, the noisy street corner, the pale purple umbrella; if I could choose again, I hope I can love more purely....
