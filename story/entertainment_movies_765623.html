Synopsis:

Adam, played by John Cusack, is about to propose when his girlfriend dumps him. To recover from this major life setback, Adam decides to take a vacation with his buddies at a ski resort. However, he soon realizes that his friends come with their own set of problems: Lou (Rob Corddry) loves spending his days at various parties but can neither find good ones nor gather enough people for them; Nick (Craig Robinson) suffers from constant micromanagement by his wife wherever he goes; and Jacob (Clark Duke) is so much of a homebody that he's still obsessed with video games despite his age. Watching these chaotic lives unfold around him, Adam feels utterly disappointed with how his midlife has turned out.

At the resort, the four discover an open-air hot tub that seems somewhat strange. They decide to try it out, drinking heavily while soaking in it. Suddenly, a miraculous event occurs—they travel from 2010 back to 1986! Adam sees this as a chance to change his life, aiming to correct all the mistakes he made in the past through this incredible journey. However, Adam cannot control his other friends' actions, who are going absolutely wild in the past. Soon, they realize that continuing like this will mess up the future, prompting them to devise ways to clean up the mess they've created...
