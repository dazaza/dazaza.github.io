Children's podcast - Eagle Net

by huahuaniu on 2007-08-23 08:00:07

In the middle of August 2007, China's first children's "podcast channel" is about to land on China's Young Eagle network, which is another first after the first children's "blog channel" was created by the end of 2004. At present, the number of blog members of the Young Eagle network has exceeded 1 million, and it is still increasing. Therefore, this fresh way of showing podcasts will also be loved by children.

With the continuous innovation and progress of network technology, "blog" text and pictures can no longer meet the needs of children who are full of curiosity on the Internet, and the "podcast" which is quietly rising has become an interactive form pursued by children. The "blog channel" of Foal Eagle is the embodiment of the service purpose of conforming to the trend of The Times and insisting on building a diversified interactive platform for Chinese children.

From August 15th, all you need to do is log on to the new homepage of www.61cks.com and www.chinakids.net.cn to access the podcast channel, where young netizens can share their happiness and sorrows with their partners through video and audio, or discuss topics to express their unique views. Or stage a PK singing show to show the voice, or show the campus style to make friends...... The country's small Internet users really have a free voice, spontaneous expression, show themselves on the stage, the harvest of unexpected wonderful!

For a long time, the Young Ying network has assumed the important responsibility of providing a display window and communication platform for the national grassroots young pioneers, and promoting the development of the national Young Pioneers informatization cause. Therefore, another feature of the upcoming "podcast channel" is to serve grassroots schools and provide a video display window for campus activities. From the beginning of the new semester in September, the "campus life theme DV show" will be broadcast one after another, such as "The most of the semester" reflecting the orientation of the new semester, and "We go to school!" reflecting the first-year freshmen. "Our happy Home" reflecting the happy squadron, "Common festival" reflecting the celebration of the Olympic Games, etc., wonderful content will be simultaneously broadcast on IPTV and achieve instant interaction.

In addition, along with the "podcast channel" also launched the "Young Eagle exchange channel", which is the first domestic children's "barter" network platform independently developed by the young Eagle network to serve children's Internet users, it breaks the original trading method, the "red scarf flea market" to the Internet, online release idle items, exchange information, in exchange for expected items, enjoy the fun of exchange. Test personal integrity, establish sincerity and trust, harvest surprises and friendship!

About China young eagle net

China Young Eagle website www.chinakids.net.cn is a website commissioned by Beijing Young Eagle Computer Network Information Co., LTD., authorized by the Office of the National Youth Working Committee and the China Young Pioneers Career Development Center. It is the largest online platform for young people in China. It mainly serves children between the ages of 7 and 15. It is the first and only online platform in China that provides learning, entertainment and community services for children and adolescents throughout the country and promotes the development of children's informatization.
