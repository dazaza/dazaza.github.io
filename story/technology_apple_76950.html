The iPhone 3G will go on sale Friday at 8 a.m.

by mifia on 2008-07-09 10:39:07

According to the introduction on Apple's homepage, the iPhone 3G will be available for sale in all Apple specialty stores starting at 8:00 AM on July 11th. You can register your personal information in the specialty store, which will help you select a phone and allow you to test it: make a call, browse web pages, or receive emails.

Shopping Tips from the Apple Store

Tip 1. Shop early. Shop late. The store tends to be busiest in the middle of the day. Shopping early in the morning or late in the evening helps you avoid the crowds.

Tip 2. Make an appointment. Test-drive new products and learn all about them with Personal Shopping.

Tip 3. Check out anywhere. When you use your credit card for purchases, any Specialist or Concierge can check you out on the spot.

Tip 4. Don’t be afraid to ask for help. If you have any questions, speak to a Concierge (in the orange shirt). The Concierge is your guide to the Apple Retail Store and will always point you in the right direction.

The Apple Retail Store is the perfect place to buy your iPhone 3G. With our free Personal Setup, a Specialist will help you choose your phone, select a rate plan, and—best of all—have your new iPhone 3G ready to make calls, browse the web, and receive email right on the spot.
