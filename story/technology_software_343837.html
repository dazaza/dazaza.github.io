The first time, I was heading home after a night shift, already quite late, when I heard some noise at the door while washing up in the bathroom. It sounded like someone was trying to pick my lock. So, I shouted loudly: "Who's there? What are you doing?" Surprisingly, the thief responded from the door: "It's so late and you're still not sleeping, what are you doing?" Then there was silence. I was at a loss for a moment...

The second time, I was resting at home during the day, browsing the internet when I suddenly heard some noise coming from the kitchen. I quietly walked over and saw a thief trying to pry open my security window and door. I grabbed a kitchen knife and said to him: "What are you doing? If you don't leave, I'll call the police." The thief calmly packed up his tools and said to me: "Are you crazy? There's someone at home, why didn't you say anything? I've wasted my time for nothing." He then turned around and left. I couldn't help but laugh and cry...

The third time, I was walking alone on the street when a boy of about 15 or 16 tried to reach into my pocket. I turned to him and said: "Kid, what are you reaching for?" "Of course, it's money," the kid replied. Seeing he was just a child, I scared him by saying: "I don't have any money, stop reaching or I'll send you to the police station." The kid glared at me and said: "If you don't have money, why are you being so mean?" He then stormed off angrily, leaving me speechless with anger.

The fourth time, on the bus, I felt an itch around my waist, as if the strap of my underwear had broken. But I didn't pay much attention to it. When I got off the bus, I heard someone say: "What's this? Sewing banknotes so firmly and even attaching them inside underwear, how am I supposed to take it out at the mall?"

The fifth time, after returning from a business trip, I just got off the train and found that the zipper of my bag had been pulled open. Upon checking, the documents were still there, but there were additional rows of words written by the thief on the blank spaces: "Such a beautiful bag, but no money inside, what a waste! You pretend to be rich but actually have no money, wasting my feelings."

Regarding related thematic articles: As basic clothing, do you always feel that although they are easy to wear, they lack a strong sense of shape and are too monotonous? In fact, while being versatile, they can also be changeable. Now let's use three popular styles of jeans to learn multi-wear matching courses.

Ripped Jeans (left) Sweet Temptation: Ripped brand + lace top + woven high heels

Style Decode: Ripped jeans usually give off a cool vibe, but if you want to achieve a sweet look, pairing it with a delicate lace top will create a good balance. Meanwhile, stilettos greatly enhance femininity.

(center) Soft Vacation: Ripped jeans + two-piece printed top

Style Decode: Resort outfits should be paired based on the principle of utmost relaxation and comfort. A flowing and soft two-piece top with bright patterns makes one feel cheerful upon wearing it. Paired with ripped jeans, it enhances comfort and elevates mood.

(right) Glamorous Rock: Ripped brand + black top + BIGSIZE necklace

Style Decode: Ripped jeans already carry a strong rock vibe. Pairing with a black graphic T-shirt and adding a large abstract geometric necklace creates a glamorous look. Paired with ultra-hot Roman heels, who says rock can't be dazzling?

Straight-leg Jeans Company (left) Lively Campus: Straight-leg brand + plaid shirt + black jacket

Style Decode: Once autumn arrives, plaid shirts bring a faint warmth. Wearing straight-leg jeans along with a black suit jacket creates a lively schoolgirl style. Paired with a black handbag and red Mary Jane shoes, it's filled with intellectual campus charm, making one want to get closer.

(center) Leisurely Countryside: Straight-leg jeans + green wide-printed top

Style Decode: Wide countryside-style printed tops are very bohemian. Paired with exaggerated accessories and bracelets, straight-leg jeans immediately exhibit a very sweet exotic style.

(right) Energetic Sports: Straight-leg brand + bright sports jacket + colorful geometric high heels

Style Decode: Brand jeans and sportswear are naturally perfect pairs. Paired with a bright yellow short jacket, it's full of vitality. The key is the black-pink striped shoes which instantly boost fashion index, distinguishing it from ordinary sportswear combinations!

Overalls Brand (left) Fresh Boy: Overalls company + plain T-shirt + polka dot scarf

Style Decode: The handsome boy style has always been a STYLE many girls want to challenge. In fact, using overalls is a good item, just needing a simple T-shirt to immediately transform into a handsome little boy. Adding a very hip-hop style scarf gives it its own unique style!

(center) Retro Aristocrat: Overalls franchise + leopard print shirt + retro small handbag

Style Decode: Leopard print shirts are difficult to handle items, especially if wanting to appear elegant. Choosing wide-leg overalls, plus a same-color retro handbag, immediately transforms into a 70s aristocratic style.

(right) Slow Living Leisure: Overalls franchise + layered vest + Panama hat

Style Decode: Slow living is the most desirable lifestyle. Paired with layered vests, it's very artistic. Adding a Panama hat makes one feel elegant and comfortable, full of positive energy every day.
