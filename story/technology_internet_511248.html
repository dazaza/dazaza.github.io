Li Ruijie, CEO of China Youth International Business (CYIB) (Weibo), issued a ban on employment, angrily criticizing his former employees Wu Peng and Xu Jian.

by edivista on 2011-10-19 08:47:18

Click to enter Wu Peng's Weibo

On the evening of October 15, CEO of Zhongqing Bao, Li Ruijie, issued a non-compete order to two former employees on RTX (internal instant messaging tool). He bluntly said, let the former project manager Xu Jian die, and let the former president of Zhongqingbao Wangfu and General Manager of Overseas Division, Wu Peng, go home to accompany his wife giving birth to twins, and help change diapers for kids. (Click the picture to enter Li Ruijie's Weibo)

Li Ruijie mentioned on RTX that please let the corporate culture construction and employee development center of Zhongqingbao Wangfu company issue formal non-compete orders on Monday. The first person to be banned from industry is Xu Jian, the original project manager of Liangjian under Zhongqingbao Wangfu; the second one is Wu Peng, the former president of Zhongqingbao Wangfu and General Manager of Overseas Division. Let Xu Jian die, and let Wu Peng go home to accompany his wife giving birth to twins, and help change diapers for kids. Non-competition restriction shall not be less than one year, but no more than three years. Please let Zhongqingbao’s lawyer Li Dongbo keep an eye on these two people. If they violate the non-compete restrictions, sue them immediately, and require them and their companies who dare to hire them to pay huge compensation.

Announce throughout the industry, any company that dares to hire these two people during the restricted period will be ready to litigate with Zhongqingbao Wangfu. Li Ruijie said, at the same time, cancel the due shares of Zhongqingbao Wangfu options for these two people. Starting from today (September 15), Wu Peng is no longer a member of AT group of Zhongqingbao.

In response, last night, the former president of Zhongqingbao Wangfu and General Manager of Overseas Division, Wu Peng, posted "This Is Your Ship" on Weibo, implying that there are always two kinds of people on the ship of enterprise: the owner and passengers. Passengers who think themselves as owners had better be deceived. But no matter what kind of crew members, they have to listen to the captain's call. When the captain deceives the crew, he must show his dominant aura.

Recently, Li Ruijie's radical words and deeds such as eliminating money-losing products and National Day military training events made Zhongqingbao become the focus of public opinion. Later, Zhongqingbao's marriage and childbirth employee welfare plan was exposed, making many insiders doubt it as a marketing event.

Looking at Zhongqingbao's product line, Zhongqingbao still mainly relies on red online games. Although Zhongqingbao is striving to develop new theme products besides red online games, such as Xuanwu, Menghui Shanhai, and Tianchao, data from public beta tests and internal beta tests showed that these products were lukewarm. In a recent interview with TechWeb during the release of Resistance War 2, Li Ruijie clearly expressed dissatisfaction with the current financial report. At that time, he told TechWeb that in the future, Zhongqingbao should become increasingly diversified, and the percentage of revenue from red games in the company should gradually decrease. Through a series of recent radical statements, many people familiar with Li Ruijie said that this was unlike his usual style. Li Ruijie usually acted straightforwardly, but now he seemed to be driven crazy.
