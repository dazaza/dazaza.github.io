This article is not for boasting about my baking skills, but sincerely hopes that mothers who love their children, as long as they have time to be with their children: I hope you can make some daily snacks for your family and children by yourself. First of all, the ingredients and production process of Western pastries are very fresh, the whole process is a very good parent-child game, and children can also understand the value of food through labor. Speaking of baking

First of all, compared with cooking, stir-frying and frying, baking belongs to the category of smoke-free, low-temperature barbecue. The temperature comes from quartz tubes, which is a relatively healthy method of food preparation.

Secondly, there are many varieties and weights of baked goods, and the controllability of DIY is good. Many small ideas can be played out according to imagination.

Thirdly, beginners don't need to be afraid of difficulties. Good ingredients and careful adjustment can definitely make delicious and beautiful pastries.

Generally speaking, cakes in Western pastries can be baked once they are put into the mold, which is relatively simple. Only some cakes have a little trick when beating, but the total time consumption is short; cookies only need to knead dough, but shaping usually needs to be done after cooling, and the time consumption is medium; bread is not recommended for beginners to try because it requires too much skill and experience (those who have bread machines and are familiar with yeast can try).

My personal experience started with cakes. Experience sharing: as long as the ingredients are selected well, there is no bad taste. Sponge cake is a good attempt to learn baking. Brownie does not need to be beaten or leavening agent, simple to make, completely depends on the selection of materials. Muffin is also an entry-level cake, using a little bit of leavening agent. The dosage can be well controlled at home, while the pastry shop needs to ensure the quantity and general quality of the product, and the dosage is not good. The success rate of cakes is high, which can not only learn knowledge, but also cultivate and increase confidence. As for cookies, I won't list them here. The cookies I failed most were butter cookies, and others didn't fail much.

Essential tools

1. Electric egg beater, Qihe for more than 50 yuan is enough

2. Kitchen scale, electronic or small platform scale is fine

3. Flour sieve

4. Egg-beating basin, a deep stainless steel basin is good, mainly to prevent splashing when beating eggs

5. Measuring spoon, generally a 5-piece set is enough, this is better than a measuring cup

6. Rubber spatula, just use San Neng, one search will do

7. A few bottomless cake molds you like

The above are essential tools. In addition, tin foil paper, oil cloth, cake flower paper, silicone molds can be appropriately selected. Those who need to decorate cream cakes can buy a decorating knife, those who need to make cookies can buy a decorating bag and cookie nozzle.

Essential materials

1. Cake flour, it is an essential component of cakes. Also called "cake powder". Self-rising flour and regular daily-use flour belong to medium gluten flour. High gluten flour is used for making bread. Medium gluten flour is enough for pizza dough.

2. Powdered sugar Do not equate it with fine granulated sugar or soft brown sugar. For example, the patterns of cookies can only be made with powdered sugar, replacing them with others will result in failure.

3. Butter Generally divided into salted and unsalted. It is a pale yellow solid and needs to be refrigerated. Unsalted is used for Western pastries, and salted is used for cooking.

4. Whipping cream, mainly used for decorating cakes. Although some recipes do not require it, let me explain it here. Butter is sometimes also called cream, but it must be solid. On the other hand, whipping cream must be liquid. These two items, I strongly suggest parents not to save such a tiny amount of money and buy pseudo-healthy "vegetable butter" or "vegetable whipping cream". These two items are the choices of pastry shops or hotels, even six-star hotels. Of course, black workshops use butter or cream essence, which is more terrible than "plant-based". Plants do not produce cream, either solid or liquid. Adding the plant title is because they are all genuine man-made creams, which are hydrogenated vegetable oils, belonging to "trans fats" that the human body cannot metabolize. They are undoubtedly bad things. Moreover, their taste must be inferior to natural animal cream or butter.

5. Baking soda, although it also belongs to leavening agents, you can prepare a little.

6. Other materials can be configured according to different production requirements.

Common terms

1. Preheat Equivalent to heating the pan for stir-frying, turn the oven temperature to the required baking temperature about 5 minutes in advance. This step cannot be skipped, therefore any microwave oven concept of discounting barbecues is impossible. Do not use microwave ovens instead of ovens

2. Beating Generally, when beating, it is often required to be oil-free and water-free, and the utensils must be clean. Take egg white beating as an example, it is divided into wet beating and dry beating. Finally, search for relevant pictures to compare and learn.

3. Sieving To make pastries delicate, this step cannot be skipped. When sieving in flour, if it is cake flour, it should not be stirred in circles to prevent the flour from becoming tough. If the flour becomes tough, the taste will be worse.
