The Blank Diary of the Lonely Demon Castle

Qingsu, this seems to be a long and tedious letter, with a slightly heartbroken and helpless tone. But whether you can see it or not is not important to me.

As for the ending between you and me, if this is not the best, then what would be the best?

It's simply that you live by the tranquil riverside, and I live in the lonely castle.

A,

After the long May Day holiday, all students must return to school. We finally met again in our long-missed classroom. You were wearing the Jack & Jones stand-up collar T-shirt I gave you before. Your hair was messy, covering your eyebrows and eyes, and the strange earring on your left ear matched the one on my right ear. I walked over, and when you looked up and saw me, an evil smile appeared on your face. When I looked down, I saw the cotton slippers on your feet and immediately felt disheartened. I asked you, aren't you hot at all? You widened your eyes innocently and said, how could I be hot with such a big opening at the back?

Look, we're so stupid, always missing the point. The focus should be on how you dare to come to class in slippers, rather than whether you are hot or not. Yet my attention is always focused solely on you.

Sitting next to you, I sneakily look at you. You still have that unruly aura that I crave, like a little rascal, along with the unique noble demeanor of young masters. When I first saw you, I thought, you must be one of those very rich boys. Rich people might be nouveaux riches, but true nobles are melancholic. Qingsu, you are a true noble.

I quietly told you, I've fallen for a boy. As I said these words, my eyes stayed fixed on your face, so sharp to catch any hint of unusual expression from you. However, your eyes remained calm, your expression always teasing, without showing what I wanted to see - that look of restlessness and panic - which has never appeared on your face because of me.

You clapped your hands and laughed, "That's great! Finally, I can get rid of your harassment."

I pinched your arm hard. Your skin is so good, even a slight pinch leaves a mark. I said, "Am I harassing you? Where am I doing it? Tell me, tell me!" You pointed at me and laughed, looking completely self-evident. My hand fell limply, and countless grievances welled up in my heart. Yes, Qingsu, I've actually been bothering you all along, just unwilling to admit it.

You asked me, how did you meet that guy? I honestly answered, I went shopping, bought a lot of clothes, and finally wanted to buy a pair of shoes, so I went to the newly opened Nike store. I liked a pair of very pretty shoes. A handsome salesperson came over and asked if I wanted to try them on. When I looked up and saw his fangs as he smiled, my heart seemed to melt into a puddle in the slightly warm air of May. He looked so clean-cut, so I acted like a lovesick fool without shame.

In the end, I maliciously told him, let's exchange phone numbers. If there are any pretty shoes, call me. He smiled shyly, as if seeing through my intentions, but what does it matter? The important thing is that I got his number.

After hearing this, you looked full of disdain and said, "So it turns out you're such a flirt, falling for every man you see." I smirked smugly, turned my head, rested it on the desk, and closed my eyes to rest. My phone was playing an old song by Yang Qianhua: "If love can lose, then I'm definitely the loser, and you don't need to mind my sacrifices."

Completely mismatched with the soothing rhythm was my irregular heartbeat.

B,

I think I will remember that day forever in my life, when the moonlight was gentle. Scooped in my palms, it shattered into shimmering ripples. I stood in the sunlight in the corridor, with my drunken father screaming curses behind me, "Get lost, just like your ungrateful mother, traitorous bastard."

I stubbornly raised my head, seemingly walking down the stairs leisurely. In reality, the humiliation inside me was like a tsunami, swallowing the entire world.

Since my father's business investment failed, my mother left with the family's few remaining savings. Before she left, she cried and told me, "Xiaodu, Mom had no choice, Mom has her own hardships."

I blankly stared at the beautiful woman in front of me, and gradually, a mocking smile appeared on my face. What hardship could make her abandon her husband and child? What unavoidable reason made her make such a decision when the family needed unity the most? I turned around and coldly said, "I can understand you, but I cannot forgive you."

I didn't cry, nor did I try to stop her. A family upheaval revealed the warmth and coldness of human relationships. It also made me realize that people in this world only like adding flowers to brocade, and as for offering help in times of need, they are all self-deceptive fairy tales. Since they are fairy tales, it means they are not trustworthy, they do not exist.

I wandered hungrily on the road, from the dim twilight until the desolate midnight, not knowing how long I had walked, only seeing the warm sun set behind the mountains, as if it would never be bright again. At midnight, on the street, there were middle-aged men with lecherous eyes, and women with sallow faces whose professions were unclear. I took off my shoes, tied the laces together and hung them around my neck, walking barefoot on the cold cement ground. Small pebbles embedded themselves in the soles of my feet, and the pain made me forget my hunger and sadness.

When that group of small ruffians with garish dyed blonde hair surrounded me, I realized I had made a serious mistake. Quiet streets at night are the best places for crimes to happen. They grinned at me and said, "Beautiful lady, why don't we play together?"

I was already panicking inside, but I put on a strong front. I only said one word, "Get lost."

They didn't get angry, instead laughing more vulgarly. Someone whistled long and loud, "Wow, the beauty has quite a temper." Then they started dragging me.

I couldn't help but scream, and the screams in the quiet night sounded particularly shrill. For a moment, I thought I was done. I would surely be severely mistreated by those ruffians. When they reached out to drag me, I almost felt despair.

You appeared when my voice was already hoarse and unable to make a sound. They slapped me several times, but I wasn't an easy target either. I bit one of their arms and wouldn't let go, no matter how they pulled, hit, or kicked me. My teeth were like roots, biting with all my strength. I sorrowfully thought, might as well die together.

In the chaos, I only heard a resounding voice say, "Let her go."

A long time later, when I recalled the scene of that day, it was as if the weather changed dramatically, heaven and earth collapsed, and at the last moment, you arrived like a knight with an imperial decree, commanding "Spare the execution" with authority.

You gave them a few red banknotes, and the group cursed and left. I squatted on the ground, hugging my knees with both hands, burying my head in my arms and not daring to look at my savior. You sat beside me, and after a while, you suddenly laughed, saying, "I didn't expect someone as frail as you to have such explosive strength." I angrily replied, "You shouldn't have meddled." You sighed deeply, "We're friends, aren't we?"

I don't know how long we sat on the road. When the sky gradually turned white, you pulled me up, coaxing me like a child, "Alright, let's go back to school."

I threw my shoes on the ground and shuffled them on carelessly. You looked at me deeply, and unexpectedly, bent down to tie my shoelaces for me.

Long ago, I heard that if a boy ties shoelaces for a girl, then he is worth marrying. You must think I'm crazy, which is why I suddenly collapsed onto you and sobbed uncontrollably on the misty and dewy morning street.

Qingsu, I'm not crazy, I just... like you.

C,

Later, we became familiar with each other. Every day, I would go early to reserve a seat for you in class. During class, I would take selfies crazily with your phone that has a 3 million pixel camera. Sometimes you would joke, "Stop taking pictures, no matter how many you take, you won't become a beauty, don't waste my memory."

I admit, everything you said is true, but isn't having rose-colored dreams also not a sin? Qingsu, I have a bit of a sinister thought. I hope others who see so many pictures of me on your phone would mistakenly think there's something going on between us. But do you have to be so cruel? I know I'm not pretty, and there's no need for you to remind me, even if it's in jest.

You won't understand, girls in this world who are not pretty all have sensitive hearts.

Yes, I really like you, but you are so good that all I can do is stay silently by your side, feeling content every day just seeing your smile. This is probably our limit; being greedy won't help.

On weekends, we went out to play. Crossing the street, I tightly held onto your sleeve, my palm constantly becoming damp. You looked at me and gently held my hand, crossing the zebra crossing. You told me, walk on the white part of the zebra crossing, that way you'll be happy for life. I widened my eyes, not expecting you to have such a childish side.

In the crowded streets, you kept holding my hand, not letting go for a moment, making me feel at ease. To passersby, we were just an ordinary couple, no different from those loving couples in the world. But why do my eyes seem like they want to tear up? Is it because this moment is too rare, even being misunderstood makes me feel precious?

A little girl with a basket of roses blocked our way, sweetly saying, "Brother, buy a flower for your girlfriend, she's so pretty."

You laughed loudly, rubbing my hair that was neither short nor long, saying, "Little sister, you're not honest. This sister isn't pretty at all." Moreover, you added, "She's my sister." I was so angry that I threw your hand away and ran forward, ignoring your calls. Soon, you caught up, frowning and asking, "What's wrong with you? I was just joking, do you really need to get mad?"

I suddenly shouted loudly, "How does it concern you that I'm not pretty? Do you have to humiliate me in front of strangers?" "What's so great about being handsome? Just because you're handsome, you can bully people? Let me tell you, ugly girls have dignity too."

Your expression paused for a moment, and I could tell you were originally trying to laugh, but then you hastily consoled me, "Stop crying, stop crying, it's embarrassing, do you know?"

But my tears wouldn't stop. These tears weren't just from your insult about my looks, but also from the sadness of not being able to be your girlfriend, and the gratitude of you holding my hand across the street. More and more people on the road glanced at us sideways, and you anxiously patted your chest, "Miss, would you like to go to Pizza Hut?"

I pitifully said, "Going to KFC is fine, anyway, it's all the same company."

You shook your head helplessly, but one Pizza Hut pizza is worth six KFC hamburgers, so if we're eating, we should eat the best.

Actually, aside from your sharp tongue, you are quite good to me.

D,

Because I like you, I want to possess you. So I miss you in all my free time, and whenever I see something good, the first person I want to share it with is you. Therefore, I keep giving you Jack & Jones stuff for no reason, just because I myself like ONLY so much.

Qingsu, I used to think that every piece of my ONLY could match perfectly with your Jack & Jones. But when I finally mustered the courage to tell you that I like you, you froze, and after what felt like an eternity, you finally lowered your head and said, "Don't be like this, let's just be friends."

I don't know why you didn't accept me, but this is my first love, and nothing can stop me. In my craziest moments, I carved your name on my arm with a knife, then smeared ink on it. After it healed, it was like a life symbol. I proudly rolled up my sleeves in front of you, but in the next second, I saw your face turn pale instantly. You scolded me, "Are you insane? Are you insane?"

That night, we drank on the playground. You asked me drunkenly, "Have you loved before? Have you truly loved before?"

I don't know how you define "love". Does it mean being hurt, suffering greatly, and learning cruel insights, to be considered as having loved?

If your definition is this, then perhaps I haven't truly loved yet. Because I haven't learned those cruel insights.

On that cool night, the drunk you finally opened up and told me about the person in your heart, that girl, so wonderful, making you still miss her in another city far away. You said, "Xiaodu, if you've seen her, you'd like her too."

I stubbornly said, "I wouldn't."

Qingsu, because I like you, I consider every girl around you as my rival, and how could I possibly like the person you value so much. I clenched my fist and swung it at the wine bottle in my hand. After a dull sound, blood dripped down from my fingers.

You were scared sober, and dragged me to the medical room to bandage my wound. After we came out, unlike last time, you didn't call me crazy, but instead looked at me, your eyes filled with broken light. Your voice trembled, "Xiaodu, this kind of emotion you have is not love."

I laughed, then large tears streamed down. Qingsu, the weather is getting hotter, so my eyes sweat a lot. If this isn't love, then maybe I'll never fall in love again.

I've known for a long time how ordinary I am, like raindrops falling from the sky, once they merge into the sea, they disappear without a trace. But in the distance between the sky and the ocean, there is a process called life. Tu Xiaonian, I encountered you in life, and thus began a vivid dream. Like every girl in adolescence, I fantasized about a boy coming to end my loneliness, and I thought that boy was you.

You reached out to hug me, softly saying, "Xiaodu, I'm sorry." I cried my voice hoarse, "Qingsu, what I really want isn't these three words."

Anyway, you will never say the three words I want to hear, so whatever else you say doesn't matter.

After that, we decided to be friends without any grudges. As someone like me to you, there is a term that sounds beautiful but is actually quite sad, called "bosom friend."

Your bosom friend has found someone she likes, and you urged me to find him and confess to him. Seeing your joyful expression made my heart heavy. Do you really want to get rid of me that much? Am I such a burden to you?

If that's the case, then I'll do as you wish. If you want me to be happy, then I'll show you happiness.

I started texting that guy, and you insisted on seeing the content of our messages. You said, "I'm a guy, I understand guys' thoughts, let me teach you how to reply." But I just smiled quietly and said, "No, I'll handle my own love affairs."

Her long burgundy hair, skin white like a vampire, and most strikingly, she has a nose piercing, wears short skirts even in the cold, and has a prominent rose tattoo on her exposed collarbone.

Everything about her is breathtaking. I think not just you, but any guy would find it hard to forget a girl like her.

She doesn't talk about regret, nor does she lower her head and cry. She proudly placed her hand in yours, her expression calm as if you two had never broken up.

You stubbornly said, "Jin Yan, I need to think about it for two days." She smiled calmly, "Alright, two days."

On the third day, she appeared full of energy, holding a string of prayer beads and cheerfully saying, "Qingsu, happy birthday." Originally, she had specially gone to Emei Mountain in Sichuan to pray for your lifelong safety. After struggling for a few seconds, you finally embraced her solemnly and sighed deeply, "Jin Yan, let's start over."
