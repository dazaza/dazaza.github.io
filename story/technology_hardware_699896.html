The Maokang operation platform is becoming increasingly mature.

by maikaolin0111 on 2012-03-09 16:53:26

In the recent year, Mecox Lane has continuously improved the infrastructure services of warehouse, logistics and customer service, etc., providing consumers with an increasingly better online shopping experience. Currently, Mecox Lane women's clothing has set up warehouses in Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou and Chengdu, and also built an operation center covering more than 110,000 square meters in Wujinag, Jiangsu Province, which is expected to be put into use in the spring of 2012 for discounted women's clothing. After the operation center is started, Mecox Lane's warehouse will be able to accommodate 600,000 SKUs, and the maximum dispatch volume per day can reach 130,000 orders. By strengthening management, currently the order fulfillment rate of Mecox Lane's warehouse is 99.98%, the complaint rate is less than 0.08% for children's shoes and clothing, the same-day shipping rate exceeds 99%, and the inventory location accuracy rate exceeds 99.8%. In order to deliver goods to customers as soon as possible, Mecox Lane has carried out multiple speed-up actions in 2011, starting air transport, land flights, twice-a-day delivery and other measures. Currently, from placing an order to delivering to the hands of customers, next-day delivery can be achieved in Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou, provincial capital cities within 2.3 days, and the national average delivery time is 2.8 days for sweaters. In 2011, Mecox Lane also realized cash-on-delivery in 1,050 cities nationwide, and provided door-to-door returns and cash refunds. Through the online query and SMS notification system developed by Mecox Lane, customers can check the delivery progress of products on Mecox Lane's website. Mecox Lane will also inform customers via SMS of the arrival time at each delivery node. Now Mecox Lane has also launched night delivery, POS machine card swiping, and convenience store self-pickup services are also under planning. Besides the improvement in delivery speed, Mecox Lane has also formed a unique delivery culture. Customers can score delivery personnel, ensuring the quality of the last kilometer of delivery; in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and Shenzhen, cartoon delivery personnel have been introduced to bring surprises to customers while delivering goods; giving customers eco-friendly bags, taking out the garbage for customers, calling for joint efforts to protect the Earth, etc., fulfilling the company's social public welfare responsibilities. Mecox Lane's customer service answers customers' questions about products and services through various ways such as phone calls, emails, and online replies. In order to improve service efficiency and quality, Mecox Lane has established multiple evaluation criteria to score service personnel, with key indicators including connection rate, effectiveness rate, processing time per business transaction, service attitude, service awareness, problem-solving ability, correct execution rate of processes, etc. When shopping online, customers are most concerned about the inconsistency between pictures and actual products. In order to truly represent product information to the greatest extent, Mecox Lane has established a team including more than 20 top photographers, hundreds of Chinese and foreign models resources, as well as professional photo editing, graphic design, planning, and copywriting teams. They provide multiple photos of real people shooting, detail shots, fabric shots, etc., fully presenting the features of the products. In the process of e-commerce transitioning from marketing wars and price wars to focusing on products and services, Mecox Lane has already taken the lead in this transition through its investment and improvements in infrastructure.
