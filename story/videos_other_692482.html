My father worked in the transportation team. Because of his straightforward personality and penchant for standing up for justice, when some drivers on the team died in car accidents leaving behind family members struggling to make ends meet, my father couldn't stand by and watch. He stood up for those widows and orphans, seeking reasonable solutions and even got into arguments with the leaders for them. As a result, compared to his colleagues who had served in the army for the same duration, my father's salary was two or three levels lower.

In this vast provincial capital, finding someone who is deliberately hiding is no easy task. Mom said that eating an apple can reveal one's life stance. On his 28-year-long bumpy teaching journey, there remain footprints soaked in blood, sweat stains on the blackboard, tears in both eyes... I understand all these; I comprehend my father, and my father feels proud of me. You are the greatest parents in the world, the most respected people in my heart. Therefore, you two are a beautiful scenery in our home.

When I already had memory, I found how difficult it was to start over. As for me, it was like enjoying an extremely sumptuous meal, making me very happy. At this moment, Chen Hong's "Often Come Home and Visit" echoed in my ears again. Yes, I owe my parents too much, whether there is something or not, I should often come home and visit. But mom just glanced at her and didn't show any agreement. Seeing her daughter's innocent look, mom smiled contentedly while stroking her hair.

Mother is an ordinary person. She has never said any profound truths, nor has she seen much of the world, but in my heart, she is a warm home, a pleasant song, a simple and unadorned prose! Since Mom passed away, I have returned home much less frequently, only making symbolic phone calls daily, asking about their well-being, then busy with my own affairs. My parents' hair has turned white significantly. The fast-spinning gyroscope in Mom's hands couldn't catch up with their hurried steps. Even though we keep telling the same few stories, I am already very satisfied.

The two female protagonists, Gu Yi Luo and Chen Xiang Nan, come from vastly different family backgrounds. The Gus are prominent and wealthy, owning luxurious residences in Shanghai, while the Chens live in a remote countryside. I truly love this kind of life, being with my beloved mother, wherever we are, there are the most beautiful landscapes. Therefore, as parents grow older, celebrating their birthdays is also a good day to make them happy and bring everyone together. Love belongs to me, but it also belongs to everyone.

I realized: he actually wore the watch on his right arm! So I teased him: who wears a watch on their right hand? He smiled innocently with a face full of chrysanthemums: I don't know which hand it should be worn on. But for Father to help Feng leave the farming community, he painfully sold the honey peach trees that he had painstakingly planted for two years, sending Feng through the school gates.

Persuading her to get up, she insisted that I forgive her first. The night was silent. That day, my father's appearance was terrifying; his hair and body were covered in dirt, dirty and disheveled.

During the Spring Festival when I returned home, you said you had connected a card reader. A grand project lies ahead, and this year you must achieve great success. You need to rebuild our small courtyard into a new-style small building, and send the children to elite schools for the best education.
