It narrates the touching stories of three generations in northern China battling sandstorms, struggling against nature, eventually achieving harmony with it, and sharing in the fruits of ecological civilization. The series encapsulates the fates and setbacks experienced by rural areas and farmers over the past fifty years since the founding of the People's Republic of China, depicting several generations of leaders at the county, township, and village levels leading the masses in transforming nature, building ecosystems, and collectively embarking on a path to prosperity. The story is moving, the characters vivid, the language lively, and the regional characteristics distinct, with folk customs, regional culture woven throughout. The series showcases the fates and emotions of many characters, painting a grand picture of various historical periods since the 1950s, including cooperativization, the People's Communes, the three years of natural disasters, the Cultural Revolution, and the reform and opening-up era.
