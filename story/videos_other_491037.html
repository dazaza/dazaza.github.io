Large luxury hotels, guesthouses, and public entertainment facilities all adopt comfort air conditioning. In order to ensure the normal operation of air conditioning and refrigeration equipment and its ancillary components within buildings and to save energy, strict scientific management of the air conditioning and refrigeration equipment must be carried out.

This management work should be conducted by professionals. Corresponding management institutions must be established in the organizational structure of hotels and guesthouses, various management systems should be built, regulations should be formulated, and several specialized groups should be set up. These management institutions are responsible for establishing various management systems, formulating regulations, and compiling plans for equipment operation, maintenance, and repair, etc.

For medium-sized buildings (such as hotels with 500 guest rooms), the management institution should have a chief engineer in charge of equipment, along with 1-2 deputy chief engineers or engineering department managers, under whom several professional groups are set up.

Among the management personnel, the proportion of staff responsible for equipment operation and maintenance should account for 5%~10% of the total number of employees, or approximately 10% if calculated based on the number of guest rooms. A certain proportion of these personnel should be dedicated to air conditioning and refrigeration technology maintenance.
