The classic series continues from Six Gods Flower Water to Six Gods Bath Gel.

Change also symbolizes constancy. After years of trust, this Six God Ice Purifying Moisturizing Shower Cream will continue the classic tradition of Six God Flower Water, becoming an indelible memory for the next generation.

During summer, mosquitoes and pests run rampant, and heat rashes drive people crazy. Placing a bottle of Six God Flower Water on the desk, we fight them uniquely together. A hint of fragrance always unconsciously reminds me of my childhood life related to Six God, as if I can still feel the warmth and gentleness of my mother's large hands from those ten or twenty years ago, making it seem like yesterday.

Nowadays, I still pursue the familiar scent of Six God Flower Water, preparing a bottle in advance every summer; otherwise, I cannot spend the summer peacefully. In nearby supermarkets, you will surprisingly find that every summer, while many brands of flower water appear and disappear, Six God Flower Water remains there, waiting for you to take it home. I also notice that the packaging of Six God Flower Water has improved from the original glass bottle to today’s Six God spray flower water, attracting more young eyes. That green transparency seems to touch the heartstrings even more.

Starting with liking Six God Flower Water, by this summer, I immediately fell in love with the newly launched Chinese herbal soap and shower cream by Six God. Especially the mugwort soap and shower cream by Six God, the two words "mugwort" are enough to evoke memories of taking mugwort baths and eating mugwort cakes when we were little kids.

During the Dragon Boat Festival, the children at home would be grabbed by their grandmothers and mothers to take mugwort baths, while they muttered words about health and driving away snakes, insects, rats, etc. It was only when I grew up that I learned mugwort has been an ancient remedy for disease prevention, recorded in medical classics such as "The Yellow Emperor's Inner Canon" and "Compendium of Materia Medica". When chemical additives harm our health, the new mugwort essential oil bathing series and mugwort soap developed by Six God caught the attention of consumers.

While lingering at the daily chemical product counters of various supermarkets this summer, I discovered that Six God had launched a new shower cream - Six God Ice Pure Moisturizing Shower Cream. From its name alone, I could guess the secret of this shower cream: both cooling and moisturizing.

In most of our minds, cooling and moisturizing are two functions that go in opposite directions. The shower products on the market either emphasize cooling or promote moisturizing. This one, which can balance both cooling and moisturizing, immediately made me eager to try it. Especially its unique formula label - Qinglu Cooling Moisture Formula, which originates from an ancient prescription in "Compendium of Materia Medica."

The leaf of hibiscus mutabilis, also known as Qinglu Leaf, just looking at the flower of hibiscus makes one imagine the effect of using this shower cream. There are also lilies, clematis, etc. Not to mention Chinese herbs, even flowers and grasses are considered kings and sages among plants. Especially lilies, my memory is that every summer when I was a child, my mother would cook mung bean and lily soup, saying it could cool down and reduce summer heat. Every summer, I remember the mung bean and lily soup cooked by my mother.
