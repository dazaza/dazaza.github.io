3. Go out and actively promote

Create a signature or text link with your website URL and related information, or make it into a picture. When sending emails or posting on forums (BBS), place it below your posts. 4. Friend links

Increasing traffic through friend links is a good way to promote your website currently. Whether it's text or LOGO links, as long as they are linked reasonably, they can bring considerable traffic. Note that you should find websites with larger traffic to partner with, preferably requiring them to place the link in a prominent position. Also, find websites with content related or similar to yours for linking, which is more beneficial for improving rankings. A reminder: placing too many LOGO links on the homepage can slow down the loading speed of your website. Therefore, it's best to stop at a suitable point.

5. Swap ads with other websites

Since you've started operating an online store, you should pay attention to competitors' websites. Many online stores sell products different from yours. At this time, you can contact them to swap ads, increasing your customer base. Additionally, placing ads can enrich the content of your homepage and serve as a beautification tool.
