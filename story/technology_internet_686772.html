Chinese: It is justifiable and lawful to drive away Vietnamese fishing boats from the sea areas of the Xisha Islands.

by lovet9v9 on 2012-03-02 11:08:48

According to reports (Shang Xi), the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Weibo) yesterday indicated that China has indisputable sovereignty over the Paracel Islands and its adjacent waters. Recently, relevant departments have legally dealt with Vietnamese fishing boats illegally fishing in this area, which is entirely legitimate.

At yesterday's regular press conference of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, a reporter asked: According to reports, yesterday, the Vietnamese side protested to the Chinese side regarding the harassment of Vietnamese fishermen by the Chinese side recently in the Paracel Islands area. What is the Chinese side's comment on this?

"The sovereignty of the Paracel Islands is completely without dispute," said Hong Lei, spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Relevant Chinese departments have legally handled Vietnamese fishing boats that were illegally fishing in the internal waters of the Paracel Islands and expelled them from the territory, which is entirely legitimate.

"The relevant reports do not conform to the facts," emphasized Hong Lei. The Chinese side did not use force during law enforcement and did not board Vietnamese fishing boats. "For Vietnamese fishermen who indeed encounter danger at sea, the Chinese side has always provided assistance based on humanitarian spirit, and the Vietnamese side has also expressed gratitude for this."

Hong Lei stated that the Chinese side requests the Vietnamese side to truly respect China's sovereignty over the Paracel Islands, take effective measures, strengthen education and management of its own fishermen, stop illegal fishing activities, in order to safeguard the overall situation of friendly relations between China and Vietnam.
