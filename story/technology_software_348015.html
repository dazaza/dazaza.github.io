So let's just kill this project.

by nealge5 on 2010-04-19 11:35:19

On July 2, the US General Motors Corporation and Sichuan Tengzhong Heavy Industrial Machinery Co., Ltd of China reached a preliminary agreement on the latter's purchase of Hummer, a well-known off-road vehicle brand under General Motors. It seems that from now on, the Chinese automobile industry will really go to the world, bringing honor to the Chinese automobile industry. The media rushed to report it. Everyone looked forward to it, as if the world's most powerful off-road vehicle Hummer would be made in China soon. I can only say that this is an illusion. There is nothing wrong with this company in Sichuan going out by this method. From the perspective of the enterprise, as long as it meets its own purpose and can achieve profit targets, such purely trade-based investment freedom is more worthy of encouragement and learning. However, the problem lies in that the "Hummer" brand and its products actually symbolize the declining domineering industrial culture of the United States. Behind it is a set of backward design concepts abandoned by the times. Not only can we not learn any advanced technology, but it is also impossible to enhance the R&D capabilities of China's off-road vehicle production. The acquisition of such an American obsolete car brand by Chinese enterprises is not something to be proud of. On the contrary, from the perspective of national industrial policy promotion and enhancement of China's responsible national image, I think the Chinese government should oppose this acquisition and prohibit Chinese enterprises from participating in the manufacture of such high-energy consumption products. According to the preliminary agreement reached by both parties, the headquarters and production department of the Hummer Company will still be located in the United States. The company plans to expand its sales network globally, including the undeveloped Chinese market. For Sichuan Tengzhong Heavy Industry in China, what they look forward to most is naturally the Chinese market. Because according to reports from the Detroit News Network, Sichuan Tengzhong Heavy Industrial Machinery Co., Ltd will also sign a long-term contract with General Motors regarding vehicle assembly and raw material supply. As everyone knows, Hummer has actually come to an end in the United States, becoming a huge burden for General Motors. It is increasingly unpopular not only in the US market but also globally without any development space. Hummer's high chassis, strong power, and large interior space are accompanied by high fuel consumption, which is an insurmountable flaw brought about by its unique design concept. In China, there won't be many real off-road enthusiasts who buy it. Instead, it satisfies those who do not truly love off-road driving but only fulfill the psychological needs of showing off wealth and power. The cultural foundation behind the Hummer brand is actually the arrogance and hegemony of the world superpower United States. It completely violates the current mainstream trend of environmental protection and energy saving in the global industry. In the new century, "Hummer" basically represents the arrogance and hegemony that goes against the tide of the world. It also contradicts China's traditional values of kindness, humility, self-restraint, and moderation, as well as the mainstream value of advocating harmony towards the world. Only a superpower like the United States, relying on its powerful military force and control over world oil, can afford such a "wasteful consumption method" of consuming world energy and flaunting wealth. Hummer is a typical representative of the American automotive design philosophy. The American automotive design philosophy represented by Hummer has been proven not to fit the human living concept and development direction of the Earth. This financial storm has thoroughly shattered the "wasteful" thinking of Americans in the automotive field. The individual heroism in the traditional manufacturing industry of the United States has already been replaced by the emerging industry culture of the United States that advocates team spirit and organizational values. More and more people are abandoning this individualistic, arrogant, and dominant consumption psychology. More and more Americans are beginning to pay attention to climate change, global environmental protection, and accept the concept of energy-saving consumption. "Hummer" rose with the process of the United States becoming the strongest country in the world, strengthened and consolidated its brand image with the background of national machinery, police, and military equipment. But in the new century, the world began to develop multi-polarly. Balance and peace, green and environmental protection, communication and dialogue, cooperation and harmony have increasingly become the consensus of countries around the world. Brands like "Hummer" have lost their cultural basis. Without a cultural basis, brands will inevitably lose their market. Even a developing country like China, with a large number of nouveau riche consumers, cannot save this brand because its cultural basis has expired, and the values it represents have been updated by the times. As a post-developing country attracting worldwide attention, China is striving to establish the image of a responsible major country and strengthen or export values such as "harmony between heaven and man," "harmonious nature," "peaceful coexistence," and "energy conservation and environmental protection" that conform to the trend of the world. Therefore, it should not encourage domestic companies to take over brands like "Hummer" that have lost their cultural basis, nor should it encourage consumers to try such "wasteful" and "show-off" style of consumption. China is also facing a serious energy crisis, and energy conservation and emission reduction have become the direction of national industrial development. "Green and environmental protection" should become the consumption direction for all citizens. Since "Hummer" has been abandoned by the United States, China should not take it over either. We cannot wait for Chinese companies to completely change the cultural values that the "Hummer" brand depends on. On the contrary, after Chinese companies take over "Hummer," it will make people associate that China will dominate the world like the United States. The "Hummer," once regarded as a hero in the hearts of Americans, will also bring Americans exaggerated imaginations that China wants to defeat the United States. If the goal of Chinese companies taking over is to turn "Hummer" into a gentle "lamb" image, then it might as well directly acquire it, dismantle it, and raise a new "lamb." But this is obviously not what companies should do. Moreover, Chinese private enterprises may not have the strength to take over a company like "Hummer" because its management, corporate culture, and legal environment may not adapt to Chinese private enterprises. Chinese companies actually don't need to pick up such a hot potato. Therefore, the Ministry of Commerce of China, together with the Environmental Protection Bureau and even the National Development and Reform Commission, should intervene legally after evaluation. For the sake of national image, future generations, and genuine innovation and development of enterprises, it is better to reject this project.
