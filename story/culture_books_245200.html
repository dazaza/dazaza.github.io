by jqrxass on 2009-10-04 21:12:33

A love that transcends a thousand years, how can it possibly end well?

A modern university campus beauty finds herself in an unknown era due to a peculiar sign. In a society where male dominance is supreme, she goes through countless setbacks and blows, eventually shedding her innocence and naivety to enjoy the freedom of the skies.

Everything seems to be on a normal track; after crossing over, she becomes the daughter of the prime minister, extraordinarily beautiful, dressed in fine clothes, surrounded by servants, with nothing amiss. However, every person and event around her seem to foreshadow that she will have extraordinary experiences. Finally, at the emperor's selection feast for concubines, her fate begins to reverse. To avoid entering the palace and becoming one of the emperor's women, she ultimately falls into a much larger conspiracy and trap...

**Part 1 of "This Heart Boundless" (Upper Volume)**

Chen Qingyu was startled when he saw me suddenly holding the sign in my hand. I mumbled, "Heart... Li..." As soon as the words were out of my mouth, a familiar wave of heat instantly surged forth. The scenery around me suddenly changed again. This time, what appeared before my eyes wasn't a magnificent palace but a huge tomb. I was horrified, my limbs powerless, unable to speak. All I could do was silently hope that Qingyu would quickly take the sign from my hand. When I looked down, the sign was no longer in my hand. Shocked and overwhelmed with pain, all I could hear was a voice getting closer and closer, "Heart Li, Heart Li, don't leave..." With all my strength, I tried to pinch myself, hoping the physical pain would make me wake up. But the massive tomb in front of me began to grow larger, seemingly right in front of me. The waves of heat grew higher and higher. I felt like I couldn’t breathe anymore, about to die. Suddenly, the tomb door burst open, darkness swallowed me whole, and everything went black as I finally fainted.
