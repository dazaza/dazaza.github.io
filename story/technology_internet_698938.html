Shanxi Huimin Hospital authoritative dermatology expert Yang Qiaomei reminds that the cause of psoriasis is complex, and treatment must be standardized.

Psoriasis is a complex, chronic, recurrent inflammatory skin disease. Medical researchers have gradually discovered many triggers of psoriasis through studying its causative mechanisms and treatments. They believe that the occurrence and development of psoriasis are related to genetics, immune dysfunction, metabolic disorders, endocrine disorders, mental stimulation, and external injuries, among other factors, and vary from person to person. Therefore, treating it is relatively difficult.

Many patients, after being diagnosed with psoriasis, are eager for quick results, neglecting long-term effects. If they do not promptly recognize this and correct it, it will be detrimental to the patient. Due to the complex causative mechanism of psoriasis, there are numerous types of internal medications used in its treatment. There are significant differences in medication experience and habits between countries, medical institutions, and doctors, and the treatment methods and means also vary greatly. Therefore, many patients undergo multiple treatments and fail repeatedly. The main reason is that the cause has not been identified, the medication lacks specificity, long-term medication treats symptoms rather than root causes, and the body may develop drug resistance.
