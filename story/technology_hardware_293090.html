Where are the advantages of American Resin soft water over other brands?

The founder of the company, Mr. John Grayson, has been dedicated to water treatment since his youth. In 1953, after working as an engineer in the water treatment industry for many years, he started his entrepreneurial career in Illinois, USA, with two partners and only $700, producing the first ion exchange water treatment equipment. At that year's Chicago Product Exhibition, Runso's appearance caused a huge stir in the industry. Every drop of water produced is fresh, clean, and sweet. The Runso brand is positioned as a high-end product for the home market, with its excellent quality coming from forward-looking technology and top-notch materials. The products have received quality certification from numerous international authoritative institutions. Not only are all components made from the best materials, but the product performance and pricing are also comprehensively considered, allowing users to obtain products with excellent material and performance, i.e., higher cost-performance, while advocating premium service to achieve a 100% customer satisfaction rate.

1. The controller of the Runso water softener is made of NORYL (used in aviation) material, stronger than steel, resistant to pressure and temperature changes, with panel material comparable to bulletproof glass.

2. The Runso water softener comes in two models: gear-type mechanical control and computer control. Gear-type mechanical control is time-based, meaning the regeneration days are set based on your home's approximate water usage and water hardness; the computer control type is flow-based, meaning the regeneration tons are set based on your home's approximate water usage and water hardness.

3. The Runso water softener is modular, compact, and easy to place. The suction tube connecting the salt tank and the resin tank uses quick-connect fittings to ensure no leaks. It also facilitates salt tank cleaning, ensuring more thorough salt dissolution. Integral models are slightly larger and require a dedicated placement position. Since the tank is stored in the salt tank, cleaning the salt tank will be more difficult.

4. Due to the uniformity of Runso's resin, the required regeneration time is about one and a half hours, saving more salt; ordinary water softeners regenerate for two hours, also saving more salt than other products.

5. Runso water softener resin manufacturers offer lifetime warranty; certified by national MA certification and issued approval documents by the Ministry of Health.

6. Runso water softeners hire the current Mrs. America as their spokesperson each year and come with original machine serial numbers.

A device is designed and produced based on various technical parameters. The design of the water softener revolves around its main working component—the softening resin. The resin's exchange capacity limits the size of the water flow speed, so small water softeners can only be tall and narrow rather than flat. If it is too short, the water flows too fast, not having enough time to exchange, making filtration impossible. Due to the excellent performance of Runso resin, it can be designed for high flow rates and small volumes. From the attached technical parameter table, with water pressure drop as the horizontal coordinate and water flow speed as the vertical coordinate, it is evident that Runso products have the advantage of being twice the flow rate of similar products under the same pressure drop.

Runso control valves are made of composite materials, stronger than steel. A 1-inch valve opening meets the maximum water requirements for a family.

Most other processor control valves use plastic materials, which can introduce unnecessary debris into the water. The valve opening is 3/4 inch, limiting water flow.

Other components of Runso are also durable and adaptable to a wide range of environmental temperature changes. The cover material is the same as that used in bulletproof glass.

Runso resin, particularly durable and highly efficient, comes with a lifetime quality guarantee.

Other brands' industrial standard resins are easily affected by chlorine and temperature, prone to cracking and failure. They only come with a one-year quality guarantee.

Runso treatment tanks are wound with three layers of fiberglass, extremely durable, ensuring no chemical substances or odors enter the water.

Other brands' two-layer tanks are welded or glued together, with 90% of leaks occurring at the joint.

The flushing process is accelerated, reducing hardness leakage and effectively preventing iron poisoning.

Runso water softeners entered the Chinese market in 1997 and have been well-received and loved by many users.

The Amazon I water softener launched by Runso Company is specially designed according to Runso's 50 years of production experience, combined with domestic water conditions.

-- Hard water, large capacity

In northern China, the water quality is relatively hard. If the capacity of the water softener is too small, it would cause inconvenience. For this reason, the Amazon I model specifically uses a filter tank with a diameter of 28 centimeters, equipped with Runso's exclusive super-efficient resin, increasing the processing capacity by 1.9 times.

-- Small space, more compact

Domestic housing space is limited, especially in already renovated homes where the main water line is mostly inside the cabinet (minimum 80 centimeters). The Amazon I model is only 72 centimeters high, allowing for easy installation of the central home water softener even after renovation, making it practical and elegant.

-- Long life, better price

As mentioned above, the processing capacity of the Amazon I model is equivalent to that of ordinary products 1.1 meters high, with increased capacity without increased price, keeping the price unchanged. Meanwhile, Runso manufacturers offer a free lifetime warranty for the filtering resin, greatly reducing user costs. Compared with domestic or imported products, Runso water softeners have the best performance-to-price ratio.

-- More humanized design

The water softener uses large-grained raw salt as a regenerator. After a period of use, there will be sand and other impurities accumulating in the salt tank, requiring cleaning. The Amazon water softener has a modular salt tank design, making it convenient to clean and install flexibly.

Runso water softeners also come with a bypass device. When the water source changes or during water outage maintenance, the bypass device can be rotated to separate the equipment from the water line, ensuring water supply without affecting the equipment. Every detail reflects Runso's people-oriented design concept.

Installation location and dimensions

Since 2000, Runso has been custom-made for Chinese families, highlighting humanized design and adopting a modular structure with flexible installation and easy cleaning of the salt tank. Fully considering the high domestic water hardness standards and water pressure factors, a special 11-inch filter tank is used, increasing the capacity by 1.9 times, making it smaller in volume, only 72 centimeters high, fitting in cabinets without affecting your home's water pressure, thus better meeting domestic user needs.

Operating costs

Water softeners need a 220-volt power supply, passing through a transformer, using an actual voltage of 24 volts (safe voltage for humans), with a power of 3 watts, at very low cost. The salt used for cleaning the water softener is ordinary coarse salt, very cheap, costing only a few yuan per month for a family, solving all your daily water usage problems and significantly improving your quality of life.
