Online Store Promotion (2) Method One: Techniques for Listing Items

1. It is recommended that you do not list all your items at the same time, but rather in three separate batches! Why? Let me explain. Everyone knows that on Taobao, items closer to their end time are ranked higher. If you list all your items at once, then only one day this week will have your items ranked highest. By listing them in three separate batches, you give yourself three chances in a week! This is why the listing period is 7 days instead of 14. The shorter the listing time, the better. When searching for products, the default sorting is by time remaining. So check if this works for you. However, we need to add something more!

2. It’s best to release your items within the same time frame! Do you know when online transactions peak? It's from 9 AM to 12 PM, 2 PM to 5 PM, and 8 PM to 10 PM. Therefore, I suggest releasing your items at 12 PM, 5 PM, or 10 PM. This way, during peak times, your items will be ranked higher, increasing their views and thus sales.

Method Two: Use of Window Recommendation Slots

1. There are tricks even in recommending items! Since you've divided your items into three batches, remember to recommend the second batch after the first, the third batch after the second, and the first batch after the third! (Explanation: wait until the first batch automatically delists before recommending the second batch). This is how I manage my items! Hehe! This can increase the view count for each item! For example, when the first batch is about to end, they are ranked higher, and so are the recommended items. In an exaggerated sense, all your items can be ranked high for 7 days!

2. Dividing items into three batches has another advantage! Taobao gives every seller a few recommendation slots, usually 5 window positions for beginners. But with so many items, who should get the slots? Here's the thing: window recommendations are only effective within 2 days of the item's end time. So naturally, the slots should go to the items nearing their end time. I think 5 to 20 slots are enough for sellers below 4 hearts. Recommendations are hard to come by, but when my two items got recommended, their view counts significantly increased. However, most buyers prefer lower-priced items, so price advantages are important.

3. Items that are both cheap and unique should be placed in the store's recommendation slots. This is where posting and replying in forums helps.

I often see some sellers' personal signatures and click into their stores, even if I don't buy, it at least increases their view count. Time, price, and popularity are always the keywords people search for.

The front-to-back order of items on Taobao:

1. Items with window recommendations are ranked higher than those without (in cases where both have window recommendations, the ranking follows the remaining time).

2. Without window recommendations, items are ranked based on remaining time, with shorter times being ranked higher.

Everyone agrees that window recommendation slots are important (duh, you already knew that). Sellers participating in the "Consumer Protection Plan" can gain 5 permanent recommendation slots.

Techniques for Using Item Titles

Fully utilize the item title. Taobao stipulates that titles cannot exceed 30 characters. Make full use of these 30 keywords, aside from the item name, leaving around 20 characters for common search keywords, or product functions and promotions. For example, "Narisu Shuzhang Capsules for Constipation Detox Buy Two Get Value 10 Yuan 30 Aloe Vera Bottle." Actually, "constipation" and "treatment for constipation" mean the same thing, but using either keyword increases the chance of finding the item, thus increasing its view count.

3. Item Names:

There are many excellent posts on Taobao about item name composition. Here are my thoughts:

First, look at Taobao's guidelines for item names:

Product Release Management Rules

Rule Eight: Misuse of Keywords Product Management Rule

Definition: Sellers misuse brand names or unrelated terms in their item titles to attract attention or increase searchability, disrupting Taobao's normal operation order. Such items are deemed as misusing keywords.

Details:

1. Sellers use irrelevant terms in item titles (e.g., "MISSHA Apricot Exfoliating Mask Weight Loss Fitness Products Hot Sale").

2. Sellers intentionally use Taobao's hot keywords in item titles with no direct relation.

3. Sellers use other brands' names in item titles.

4. Sellers compare their items with other brands (e.g., "Comparable to LV Genuine Leather Bag").

5. Sellers use superlative statements like "biggest," "highest," "best."

6. No descriptions of gifts or prizes are allowed in item titles; such information should be included in the item description.

7. Sports category items cannot use multiple attribute keywords to interfere with searches.

8. Sellers without exclusive dealership rights cannot claim such in their item information.

9. In the QQ section, item titles cannot contain unrelated category terms.

These are the rules for keyword management in the "Product Release Management Rules." Shopkeepers who haven't carefully read them should post a reply for self-reflection.

1. Composition of Item Names: Item names are the most direct way for buyers to search for items. Therefore, item names should include the following elements: Brand Name, Place of Origin (or Style), Attribute, Keywords (cold or hot keywords), Model (Color, etc.), as well as Reputation, Events, and Characteristics.

Example: Four-diamond reputation. 09 Spring Fashion Latest Korean Version Petal-shaped Collar Ruffled Edge Long-sleeved Dress 184 meters

This item name includes: Reputation (four diamonds), Keywords (09 Spring Fashion Latest), Style (Korean Version), Characteristics (Petal-shaped Collar, Ruffled Edge Long-sleeve), Attribute (Dress), Model (184 meters).

This is an item name that complies with Taobao's rules and is effective for searches. Any combination of two words in this name can be found on Taobao, making it a successful item name.
