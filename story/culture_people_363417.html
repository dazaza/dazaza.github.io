China's inventions that influence the world

by geekzhang on 2010-08-30 09:53:30

Cambridge University Press has spent more than half a century (from 1954 to the present) on the 27-volume Science and Civilisation in China, mainly led by renowned Sinologist Joseph Needham, to discuss China's inventions that have influenced the world. Check out the Needham Institute website sometime and order some books. http://www.nri.org.uk/science.html

If you think that volume 27 is too tiring to read, you can take a look at The British scholar Robert Temple in The West's Debt to China, Robert Temple: The Genius of China's summary of the world-influential inventions in the 27 volumes is as follows
