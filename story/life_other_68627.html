Summary of earthquake relief donation methods

by geekzhang on 2008-05-14 11:39:56

Due to the current lack of smooth transportation channels in the disaster area, we suggest that netizens temporarily focus on cash donations. According to media reports from the front lines, the disaster area is currently gathering necessary food supplies, especially biscuits, milk, powdered milk, bread, and mineral water. Additionally, tents and medical supplies are also crucial for disaster relief efforts. With numerous injured individuals, hospitals urgently need blood supplies for patient treatment; thus, donating blood is a feasible method of assistance if conditions allow.

3. China Foundation for Poverty Alleviation

On May 12, 2008, the Wenchuan earthquake shocked the whole country. This 7.8-magnitude earthquake caused enormous trauma and suffering to the people in the disaster area. The sudden disaster touched everyone's heart deeply. After experiencing the snowstorm disaster at the beginning of the year, the Chinese people are now facing another severe test!

To help our compatriots overcome the disaster, the "We Are Together in Heart - Wenchuan Earthquake Emergency Rescue Action" of the China Foundation for Poverty Alleviation was immediately initiated. A donation of food worth 1 million yuan was made to the earthquake-stricken area.

Natural disasters are sudden and unstoppable, but the strong confidence, courage, and unity of all sectors of society in the face of sudden disasters are our guarantees to overcome all difficulties. Earthquakes may be merciless, but great love knows no boundaries. All the public, enterprises, and individuals who care about the disaster situation and pay attention to the people in the disaster area, please lend a helping hand and contribute your small strength to help the people in the disaster area get through the living difficulties. Let us unite our hearts and work together to overcome the difficulties with our compatriots in the disaster area and win the victory of earthquake relief as soon as possible!

Facing the disaster, we are together in heart!
