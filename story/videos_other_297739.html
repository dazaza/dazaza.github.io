Hobo Handbags: A Popular Trend for Fall and Winter

by heop234iud on 2009-12-07 13:52:14

The great thing about these bags is the variety of options available. Hobo style bags are made by some of the most affordable brands as well as some of the most expensive ones. For a bag that won’t break the bank, check out the Jessica Simpson Aero Medium Hobo. This bag comes in black, grape, and camel, and can be yours for under $100. It is crafted from high-quality synthetic leather and features gold tone hardware. Another affordable option is the Melie Bianco Ramona bag, a hobo style available in purple, red, saddle, gray, and black. It is also made from fine synthetic leather. If you prefer a hobo bag made from genuine leather, you should definitely take a look at what the American company Coach has to offer. They might just be the leader when it comes to designer label leather hobo bags. Their prices are higher, but not comparable to some of the European design houses. What you’ll really love is the selection. Coach offers everything from the Groovy small hobo bag to the large New Cambridge style hobo bag. Coach also sells leather coin purses, wallets, and wristlets so you’ll have something to go in your new designer bag. Although introduced a few years ago, hobo handbags are here to stay. Unlike fleeting trends, the hobo bag is a classic because of its versatility. The slouchy shape gives it a casual flair, but with the right size, color, and style, you can carry a hobo bag with an evening gown. The traditional large hobo is perfect for running errands, work, and everyday tasks. In short, the hobo bag is a style that will remain in your collection for many years to come. You may even want to invest in several, perhaps one in a classic color like brown or black muted leather, and another playful and trendy option like patent leather pink. In the spring and summer months, women prefer lighter purses in shades that reflect blooming flowers and the lightheartedness of the season. However, as fall and winter approach, smaller, lighter purses will be replaced by multifunctional, classic hobo handbags. This handbag style is coveted by fashionistas everywhere and is just as popular in a small town as it is in Hollywood. The style is casual yet not sloppy, and the design offers ample storage space.
