The onlookers quickly moved to stop the "long-haired man" upon seeing the situation, by which time Wang Furong had already been beaten to the ground, with blood streaming from a wound above his left eyebrow, his shirt torn, and his body covered in dirt.

"At the time, I tried to persuade the BMW driver. It's not easy for people who collect counterfeits, so let it go, 400 yuan is fine." A citizen who was present at the scene said, referring to legal rights protection.

After the accident, the BMW driver got out of the car to negotiate compensation with Wang Furong. Since Wang Furong had just collected a load of scrap goods, he didn't have much cash on him, but the BMW driver accepted Wang Furong's offer of a 500-yuan compensation.

An angry crowd surrounded the "long-haired man" and the BMW car, dialing 110 (police) and 120 (emergency medical services).

Wang Furong was still shocked and hadn't reacted when the "long-haired man" suddenly opened the door and got out of the car. Without giving Wang Furong a chance to explain, he started punching and kicking him in the head, armpit, and knees, and even picked up a 30 cm long, 20 cm wide cement slab from the roadside to prepare to smash it onto Wang Furong.

"A long-haired man in black on the passenger seat suddenly said to me, 'I'll give you 5 million yuan for your life.'" Wang Furong recalled. The merchants who witnessed this scene confirmed Wang Furong's account: "That guy said he would pay 5 million yuan for the tricycle owner's life, it was terrifying!"

Wang Furong said that after borrowing money from all sides, he could only manage to borrow 400 yuan. "I'm just a scrap collector, I really don't have that much money. Can 400 yuan be enough? If not, I'll think of another way," Wang Furong said to the BMW driver.

Henan Business Daily (Weibo) reports (Intern reporter Shen Wei): At 1:50 PM yesterday, near the intersection of Nanfeng Street and Weisheng Road in Zhengzhou City, a manually-powered tricycle loaded with scrap paper boxes, newspapers, etc., collided with a BMW car. A black-clad, long-haired man sitting in the BMW arrogantly proclaimed, "I'll give you 5 million yuan for your life!"

As of 7 PM last night when the journalist went to press, the case was still under investigation.

"The behavior of the long-haired man was too despicable! The money has already been compensated, yet he still beat someone, it's unacceptable!" An eyewitness merchant angrily said.

The police officers from the Wenhuai Road Police Station who arrived at the scene took away the "long-haired man", and the 120 emergency medical staff sent Wang Furong to the hospital. Later, after being treated by doctors, Wang Furong's 1.5 cm long wound on his left brow bone required seven stitches.

Afterward, the driver took the 400 yuan and prepared to leave. Everyone thought the matter had been privately resolved, but what happened next came as a great shock to Wang Furong.

"I was riding westward on the BMW, I didn't brake in time, and I scratched the BMW, leaving a one-foot-long scratch on the right rear door." Wang Furong said.

Yesterday afternoon, 55-year-old Wang Furong was riding a tricycle fully loaded with scrap items such as cardboard boxes and newspapers along Weisheng Road from north to south. When he reached the intersection of Weisheng Road and Nanfeng Street, his tricycle collided with a BMW.
