There are many methods for treating sinusitis, how to choose? Experts from the Guangxi Ear, Nose, Throat, and Ophthalmology Hospital introduced that the conventional treatment method for sinusitis is drug treatment. However, due to thickened nasal mucosa, degeneration, polyp growth, fibrosis or fibrous changes, and the resulting ventilation and drainage obstacles, drugs often fail to achieve the desired therapeutic effect, and long-term use can cause drug-induced rhinitis, making treatment more difficult.
