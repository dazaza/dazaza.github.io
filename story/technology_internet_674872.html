Chinese construction machinery sounds the clarion call for international strategy

by dfasd98fh on 2012-02-24 16:15:55

The self-sufficiency rate of China's construction machinery rose from around 70% during the Tenth Five-Year Plan period to over 80% currently. The industry has gradually realized a leap from manufacturing to creation, with both sales volume and sales revenue surpassing those of the United States, Japan, and Germany to rank first in the world. In fact, in recent years, China's construction machinery industry has shone brightly in the global construction machinery industry with a high-speed development trend. At the beginning of the New Year in 2012, the domestic brands of China's construction machinery industry presented a thriving scene of successive exports overseas.

Recently, two sets of Sany 180 environmental protection mixing stations were installed and debugged for 88 days, and they have now been established in Doha New Port, Qatar. The successful bidder, Sany Environmental Protection Mixing Station, made its first export overseas.

On January 8th, the first 40-meter boom pump truck of Zoomlion Concrete Machinery Company appeared in Myanmar and was officially delivered to the customer. This delivery of Zoomlion's 40-meter boom pump truck is the first 40-meter boom pump truck sold by Zoomlion in Myanmar and also the longest boom pump truck currently in Myanmar.

On the evening of January 10th, the first export rotary drilling rig [Shantui SER22] of Shantui Machinery Company was loaded and shipped at Tianjin Xingang Port and set sail for Singapore.

On January 18th, on the eve of the arrival of the Chinese Dragon Year, six [Shu River] models 900, [Shu River] 904, and [Shu River] 1004 four-wheel tractors, carrying the deep friendship of Changlin people, set off for Madagascar, marking the start of the large-scale wheel-type tractor [Shu River]'s export to foreign markets in 2012.

Enhanced internal strength heralds the internationalization strategy

After China joined the WTO, with advanced technology, management, and capital from developed countries entering the Chinese market, competition in the industry intensified. From the overall development trend, the introduction of foreign advanced resources has greatly promoted the innovation of China's construction machinery product technology, accelerated the adjustment of the industrial structure, product structure, and enterprise operation mechanism of the construction machinery industry, pushing enterprises further into the international market. It was precisely in 2002 that China's construction machinery enterprises welcomed the first year of internationalization. Sany Group was the first to sell products overseas, pioneering the export of China's construction machinery. Subsequently, the Chinese construction machinery army launched a large-scale offensive towards overseas markets.

In the ten years since joining the WTO, the export mode of China's construction machinery has shifted from foreign trade companies to mainly enterprise self-operated exports; from private labels to mainly enterprise autonomous brand exports; from mainly exporting to third-world countries to developed and sub-developed countries; export regions from guerrilla warfare to position warfare; sales models from direct sales to agency systems. Meanwhile, during the process of developing overseas markets, product performance and quality have continuously improved, intellectual property awareness and independent innovation capabilities have continuously strengthened, overseas sales channels and service networks have become increasingly complete, and overseas investment and capital operations have increased. The overall internationalization strategic layout of China's construction machinery industry has begun to take shape.

The driving force behind China's construction machinery industry going global can be summarized as follows: First, integrate quality resources; Second, reduce costs and minimize trade frictions in target markets. In terms of core resources, compared to international counterparts, the advantages of China's construction machinery industry lie in the market, scale, production capacity, and human resources, with cost-effective products; the disadvantages lie in concepts, systems, product technology development, and enterprise management, with significant gaps compared to developed countries' same industries. In developed countries, integrating quality resources (mainly technical resources, market sales resources, and key supporting resources) is an important content. A powerful Chinese construction machinery industry has set its sights globally, aiming to make a name internationally and let the world feel the power of Made in China.

An inevitable trend, the international environment needs "Made in China"

In June 2011, the total value of the XCMG Group-Venezuela project was 4.842 billion yuan (equivalent to 744.6 million US dollars), making it the largest single order of China's construction machinery exports to date.

In October 2011, 24 units of high-drive bulldozers from Xuangong completed assembly, debugging, and testing, preparing to be sold to Angola in Africa, with a contract value of more than 20 million RMB, which was the largest order for Xuangong's high-end product exports in 2011.

Even though Venezuela's large-scale affordable housing construction and Africa's infrastructure construction provided a vast market for the large-scale export of China's construction machinery, from another perspective, the reliable quality and advanced technology of China's construction machinery products are also the main factors in winning overseas markets.

Since the economic crisis in 2008, the European and American construction machinery markets have shown a declining trend, with product sales and market share gradually being caught up or even surpassed by "Made in China." In March 2010, CSR Corporation signed a supply contract for 12 Type Zhongbai Freight 1 AC drive electric locomotives with Belarusian National Railway Union, achieving a breakthrough in the zero export of major railway technical equipment from China to Europe.

In August 2011, XCMG Group successfully exported its XCMG QUY80E (CE) crawler crane to Western Europe for the first time. Currently, the equipment has been smoothly dispatched to Antwerp Port in Europe. The successful export of XCMG's large machinery to Western Europe proves that XCMG products' quality and overseas after-sales service levels have qualified to enter the high-end market, giving great confidence to local XCMG dealers and marking a new step forward for XCMG's crawler crane products. In December 2011, Taiyuan Heavy Industry held a successful signing ceremony for the export of 4 WK-35 large mining excavators to Russia through AVIC International Gongmao Co., Ltd.

In the global top 50 construction machinery manufacturers ranking released in 2011, the number of Chinese enterprises listed set a historical record, breaking through 10; among them, three enterprises entered the top 10. Such a rapid development momentum showcased China's speed to the world. One could say that the large-scale export of China's construction machinery is an inevitable trend, and the world needs "Made in China."

The state adopts policies and measures to encourage exports

Mainly including: Ministry of Commerce's guidance on the work of national outbound investment and economic cooperation in 2010; comprehensively using means such as export tax rebates to support the export of products with independent intellectual property rights, own brands, large complete sets, high added value, and labor-intensive products; supporting key enterprises in conducting key technology and common technology research and development; providing services throughout the process for enterprises with strength and orders in receiving orders, fulfilling contracts, after-sales services, and safe collection of foreign exchange; providing support for the construction of platforms such as technological research and development, experimental testing, product certification, information services, and training for export bases, scientific and trade innovation bases, etc.; supporting enterprises to participate in exhibitions in emerging markets, conduct trademark registration and quality certification; supporting enterprises to develop overseas marketing networks and open after-sales service outlets; alleviating the difficulties of financing for small and medium-sized enterprises' exports, guiding financing guarantee institutions to actively provide financing guarantees for small and medium-sized export enterprises; implementing a 42.1 billion US dollar export credit insurance amount, promoting the going-out of large complete equipment sets, etc.

Strengthening internal capabilities, steadily advancing and gaining fragrance overseas

Entering 2012, there have been constant major events and good news in China's construction machinery industry, with increasing export projects. Sany's acquisition of Putzmeister and Liugong's acquisition of HSW in Poland signify the steady progress of China's construction machinery industry towards overseas markets. However, how to walk this bright path steadily and well is also something the Chinese construction machinery industry needs to consider.

Experts suggest that only by strengthening internal capabilities, improving the use reliability of export products, accelerating international standard certifications, doing well in autonomous intellectual property searches, increasing scientific research efforts on autonomous intellectual property projects, fully utilizing various export foreign trade methods and policies, effectively expanding international markets, and focusing on developing localized agents can Chinese enterprises go further and better on the export road.
