Chongqing Boosted Suining's Development - Liu Yishou

Xie Daiyin, besides being the Director of the Suining Development and Reform Commission, is also a lecturer at Southwest University and a master's degree supervisor. Every month, he travels back and forth between Suining and Chongqing. The distance is very close; it only takes over an hour to get there.

Xie Daiyin said that traveling to Chongqing from Suining for business matters takes about the same amount of time as going to Chengdu, making them equidistant. From the map, Suining is perfectly positioned next to both Chongqing and Chengdu.

In fact, Suining’s integration with Chongqing began many years ago. Xie Daiyin mentioned that not long ago, he attended a strategic development forum organized by the Chongqing Development and Reform Commission, where he was one of the few invited development and reform commission directors from other provinces and cities. He has already participated in such forums many times.

Chongqing is a significant direction for Suining's economy. In recent years, Chongqing's economic boom has also driven Suining's economic growth, according to Xie Daiyin.

Aiming at Chongqing's Bonded Port Area

Yesterday, within Suining's Western Modern Logistics Port, the first project of Huayuan in the west was completed and put into operation. In the middle, numerous construction sites were bustling with activity.

With Chongqing having established the Cuntan Airport Bonded Port Area, Suining will connect with it through logistics. Xie Daiyin stated that this logistics port under construction has been included in the provincial government's guidelines for accelerating the development of the Chengyu Economic Zone. Relying on this logistics platform, Suining will vigorously develop specialized trading markets for electronic components, agricultural products, medicinal herbs, and textiles, actively absorbing the logistics radiation from Chengdu and Chongqing, and striving to build a secondary logistics center within the Chengyu Economic Zone.

Accepting Chongqing's Industrial Transfer

Xie Daiyin said that Suining's current plan is to prepare for accepting the transfer of Chongqing's automotive and motorcycle industries. In fact, this cooperation has already reached a considerable scale. Currently, some enterprises in Suining are providing support for Chongqing's automotive and motorcycle sectors.

Suining is an ideal location for accepting Chongqing's industrial transfer due to its unique geographical advantages, convenient transportation, solid industrial foundation, and various professional parks that have already reached a certain scale. This lays a solid foundation for accepting Chongqing's industrial transfer, Xie Daiyin added.

Moreover, Suining also has good conditions for accepting the transfer of Chongqing's electronics industry. The industrial chains of electronic molds, diodes, transistors, integrated circuits, LEDs, and electrodeless lamps are gradually becoming complete. The three production bases of microelectronics, circuit boards, and light sources are gradually achieving high levels of specialization and product sophistication in the electronics industry.

Becoming Chongqing's Vegetable Basket and Backyard

There used to be a saying among the people of Suining: large vehicles go to Chongqing, small vehicles go to Chengdu. The former refers to trade, while the latter refers to meetings. For Suining, Chongqing is a big market. Xie Daiyin said that Suining's five key products—high-quality pigs, beef cattle, poultry, specialty fruits, and pollution-free vegetables—are quite influential nationwide.

Next, Suining will establish a number of characteristic bases, strengthen the construction of circulation systems focused on agricultural product trading markets and cold chain logistics, and build Suining into a green food supply base for Chongqing.

After the completion of the Suining-Chongqing railway and expressway, the time distance has shortened. Xie Daiyin said that apart from integrating into Chongqing's metropolitan circle, Suining will also integrate into the lives of Chongqing residents. Besides becoming Chongqing's supporting player economically, Suining will also become Chongqing's backyard—a great place for weekend relaxation for Chongqing citizens.

The cultural heritage of Suining is deep-rooted, with the hometown of Guanyin and the national treasure Song Dynasty ceramics. Along the Fujiang River, Suining has presented itself as a lakeside city. Xie Daiyin said that from the past two golden weeks, half of the tourists visiting Suining were from Chongqing.

Focus on Sichuan-Chongqing Economic Cooperation Forum

Chongqing Businessmen in Suining

Suining has a new market, somewhat similar to Chongqing's Chaotianmen wholesale market, where various goods can be purchased in bulk.

"We have many Chongqing people doing business here," said Chen Dengqiong from Tongnan, who has been in Suining for 18 years. He knows hundreds of fellow townspeople who are all Chongqing people doing business in Suining.

According to incomplete statistics, there are tens of thousands of Chongqing businessmen working hard in Suining, which is more than 100 kilometers away from Chongqing, with some operating projects worth hundreds of millions of yuan.

Now, Chen Dengqiong has bought a house over 100 square meters in Suining, a storefront, and even a Ford commercial vehicle last year. In the market, there are many Chongqing people, including wholesale bosses from Tongnan, Tongliang, and Dazu, nearly 200 of them.

Chongqing Elements on Suining Streets

Chongqing Vehicles

Among the vehicles coming and going on the Suining expressway, many have license plates starting with "Yu," transporting goods up and down. Many cars on the streets, besides Chongqing-produced Changan Fords, also have "Yu" license plates.

Chongqing People

It can be said that if you ask someone in Suining if they know any Chongqing people, most would say yes, specifying what they do. There are tens of thousands of Chongqing people in Suining, some doing business, some working, and some settling down here.

Chongqing Hot Pot

"Chongqing hot pot, excellent!" On the streets of Suining, if you randomly ask a citizen where you can eat hot pot, they would point you to Jinjiang Road, the commercial core area of Suining. There are many hot pot restaurants with Chongqing dining banners on this road, such as Dezhao, Liu Yishou, Qinma, and Kongliang.
