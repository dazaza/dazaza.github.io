Lotus启动 drought-resistant charity sale with naked donation

by ygheojhsdrgm on 2012-03-09 10:03:05

The shocking image of people descending hundred meters underground to fetch water has been tugging at the hearts of many readers. This is a microcosm of Yunnan's once-in-a-century drought that has persisted for three years, and also reflects the plight of many simple and honest villagers who rely on the weather for their livelihoods. Upon release of this report, the headquarters of Lotus Cars in Hangzhou immediately responded. Following their previous collaboration with the Yunnan Youth Development Foundation (referred to as the Youth Foundation) to aid Xiangyun County's Qinghai Lake, they took action again to contribute their modest efforts as a socially responsible enterprise to Yunnan's drought relief efforts. Online reservation for test drives of the Lotus L5 is available.

### Investigation of Drought Relief Feasibility Hundred Meters Underground

Lotus Cars promptly dispatched Yunnan Bi Hai Yuan Company, its local dealership, for an on-site investigation, requiring them to provide a feasible assistance plan. Two colleagues from Yunnan Bi Hai Yuan descended alongside villagers through a mountain cave with a slope exceeding 70 degrees, which only allowed one person to pass at a time, reaching the side of an underground river. After a simple measurement, it was found to be tens of meters deep, and the underground river could continuously provide water, meeting the requirements for water extraction and drinking water projects.

### Donation of Lotus Sedans to Solve Water Extraction Difficulties

With the cooperation of our newspaper, after detailed discussions with local villagers and budgeting for the feasibility and cost of water extraction, the first phase of the project to bring water to the surface required an 11.5-kilowatt pump, a generator of more than 22 kilowatts, as well as high-strength water tanks, pipes, fuel, etc., with a budget of approximately 100,000 yuan. Upon receiving the message back at Lotus headquarters, the company immediately decided to donate a Lotus L3 coupe as the initial fund for water extraction and drought relief. With the agreement of the Lotus headquarters, our newspaper collaborated with the Youth Foundation, Qujing Municipal Publicity Department, Communist Youth League Committee of Qujing, and the Publicity Department of Fumin County Committee to conduct a bottom-price charity auction on March 10th at the Zhujiang Source Square in Qujing City. The reserve price was set at 50,000 yuan, and the highest bidder by 15:30 would win the car. All proceeds would be donated to the Youth Foundation specifically for the village's drought relief drinking water project, with a tax-free donation invoice issued by the Youth Foundation. Any surplus funds would also be used for drought relief in other areas of Qujing. The Youth Foundation also accepts targeted donations from all sectors of society for "Qujing Drought Relief" and has set up a 24-hour donation consultation hotline: 13700691177.

### Yunnan Bi Hai Yuan, a Loving Enterprise, Steps Up Again

As a local Lotus Cars dealer, Yunnan Bi Hai Yuan is also a public-spirited enterprise. They have previously organized car club events in collaboration with the Youth Foundation, donating over 110,000 yuan to Baiyan Village in Dali and nearly 100,000 yuan for water delivery in Bailu Township. With strong support from the manufacturer, they decided to make another effort for drought relief. For instance, if you place an order for a Lotus car at the event site, not only can you enjoy the group-buying public welfare price, but you can also donate the full 5,000-yuan deposit to the Youth Foundation specifically for drought relief in Qujing disaster areas. It is reported that the group-buying public welfare price of the Lotus L3 has dropped below 70,000 yuan, while the Lotus L5 offers a discount of 10,000 yuan from its original market price of 99,800 yuan. We hope that car buyers who are eager to help can pay attention to and support this cause or send text messages to 13888099955 to participate in the charity auction, noting the bidding price.

### Explanation of the Love Car

Lotus Cars is a project introduced by Youngman Automotive Group with British Lotus brand and technology. The company adopts a unique production and management philosophy of "independent enterprises, joint venture model," differing from most automobile manufacturers. The Lotus L3 series models were meticulously crafted over three years by British Lotus, adhering to design standards, process standards, and quality control systems of British Lotus. It embodies the three top-notch technologies of British Lotus: "chassis technology, exterior technology, safety technology," integrating the technical characteristics of British Lotus sports cars.
