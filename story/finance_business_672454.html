The True Meaning of the Black Belt

At a ceremony, a martial arts master knelt before his grandmaster as he was about to receive the hard-earned black belt. After years of rigorous training, this disciple’s skills had greatly improved, and finally, he was about to achieve recognition in this art.

"Before I award you the black belt, you must pass one more test," said the grandmaster.

"I'm ready," the disciple replied, assuming it would be the final round of martial arts examination.

"You must answer one basic question: What is the true meaning of the black belt?"

"It's the end of my martial arts journey," the disciple answered without hesitation, "It's the reward I deserve after all my hard work."

The grandmaster paused for a while, clearly dissatisfied with the disciple's answer. Finally, he spoke, "You are not yet ready to receive the black belt. Come back in a year."

A year later, the disciple knelt before the grandmaster again.

"What is the true meaning of the black belt?" the grandmaster asked.

"It's a symbol of excellence and the highest achievement in our martial arts," the disciple said.

The grandmaster remained silent for several minutes, still unsatisfied. Finally, he said, "You are not yet ready to receive the black belt. Come back in a year."

Another year passed, and the disciple knelt before the grandmaster once more.

"What is the true meaning of the black belt?"

"The black belt represents a beginning, the start of an endless journey of discipline, struggle, and pursuit of higher standards," the disciple answered.

"Good, you are now ready to accept the black belt and begin your journey," the grandmaster replied with satisfaction.

In this fiercely competitive environment, the establishment of a business represents a new milestone, a starting point. A business may have a temporary advantage in technical expertise at its inception, but running a business requires more than just technology. It demands teamwork, a vision that inspires dedication, management systems, and most importantly, a leader who plans to learn how to manage and operate effectively. Every achievement in a business is also a new beginning. Only by maintaining personal and team learning can a business sustain long-term success and healthy development.

For a smart person, every day is a new beginning. Life is like a ceaseless river, never staying in one place or stage. It needs constant transcendence. Transcendence is elevation, mutation, and an indispensable phase of life. It is this transcendence that has led humanity from ancient ignorance to today's civilization and prosperity.

Transcending oneself is a requirement of life. Nietzsche said, "Life seeks to build its own ladder... It yearns to look far into the distance, longing for the most intoxicating beauty... because it demands upward movement!" "Life seeks to rise, to rise and transcend itself." The place where life yearns is the goal and ideal each of us has chosen for ourselves. The process of transcending oneself is the process of creation. Living in this world, we should not merely seek comfort and enjoyment. Lazy and selfish people will never experience the true joy of life. Only through effort, full commitment, and continuous transcendence can one secure a position in fierce competition, making life's collisions spark brilliant flashes.

Those who view art as life and science as their soul never stop moving forward. The extraordinary creations of great mentors are exemplary of transcending life. Copernicus' heliocentric theory, Napoleon's grand achievements, Shakespeare and Balzac's immortal works, Marx's revolutionary theories... They have surpassed time and space, erecting natural monuments that conquer and influence generation after generation of people around the world.

To transcend oneself means to pursue relentlessly and fight tenaciously; it means walking paths untraveled by predecessors and finding new beginnings in the work you do.

There was a lonely young painter who possessed nothing but his ideals. For his ideals, he set off on a distant journey. Initially, he applied to a newspaper in Kansas City. The positive atmosphere there was exactly what he needed, but the editor-in-chief found his works lacked originality and rejected him, leaving him to taste the bitterness of failure.

Later, he painted for churches. Due to low pay, he couldn't afford a studio and had to borrow a disused garage. One day, under dim lighting, he saw a pair of shiny little eyes — it was a mouse. He smiled as he watched it, and it slipped away like a shadow. Later, the little mouse appeared repeatedly. He never harmed it, not even scared it. It performed various movements on the floor, doing acrobatics, and he rewarded it with crumbs of bread. Gradually, they trusted each other and built a friendship.

Soon, the young painter was introduced to Hollywood to produce an animal-themed cartoon. This was a rare opportunity, but he failed again.

In the darkness of night, he pondered his future, even beginning to doubt his talent. At that moment, he suddenly remembered the little mouse in the garage, and inspiration flashed in the dark. He quickly sketched the outline of a mouse.

The greatest cartoon character in history — Mickey Mouse — was born, and Walt Disney became world-famous.

Exploration and innovation have changed the entire world. Therefore, we say humanity progresses. Without innovation, we would merely be monkeys! Scientific discoveries and human progress come from humanity's self-sacrificing exploration of nature. Progress stems from innovation.

Life's Adage: Only those who transcend themselves create one brilliance after another. Each of your successes is a new beginning.
