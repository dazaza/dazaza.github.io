The rule of "no refund" has been a trading rule for years in the Witkey tasks. Once employers use the Witkey task trading method, they cannot cancel the task and get their money back regardless of whether they are satisfied with the final result or not. However, rules are always meant to be broken. On August 28, 2008, Task China launched a new feature called "Super Challenge," which broke the no-refund rule in Witkey tasks.

What is Super Challenge?

The Super Challenge is a special trading method in Witkey tasks used to solve more difficult Witkey tasks. When employers post Witkey tasks and choose to use the Super Challenge option, if there is no satisfactory winning entry after the submission deadline and they do not wish to extend the task by adding more funds, they can get 70% of the task amount refunded.

For example, Mr. Li spent 2000 yuan to collect a business plan but was dissatisfied with the results. He could only continue to collect by extending the task and increasing the task amount by 20%. However, with Witkey tasks that have the Super Challenge option, if Mr. Li is dissatisfied with the collected business plan, he can choose to end the task as a failed challenge instead of just extending it.

Why does Witkey need to add Super Challenge?

Some Witkey tasks are challenging and difficult to complete, such as tool development, studying abroad, script dubbing, and song creation. These challenging tasks are suitable for using the Super Challenge when posting Witkey tasks. A challenge may succeed or fail. If it fails, the task amount will be refunded.

Can all Witkey tasks choose the Super Challenge method?

Not all Witkey tasks can choose the Super Challenge method. Only tasks that meet the following two requirements can be set as Super Challenge tasks when posted.

First: Tasks that require high standards and involve a large workload, where employers may not necessarily find satisfying works, such as 3D animation production. There might be relevant talents on the website, but it's uncertain.

Second: Tasks whose completion has a certain degree of randomness and whose completion status is easy to judge, such as finding childhood playmates. Everyone knows whether they were found or not.

If a successfully posted Super Challenge task is ultimately not completed, the task amount can be refunded under the supervision of customer service and workers after verification. The Super Challenge emerged based on the long-standing no-refund rule in Witkey tasks. Now, let's summarize the Super Challenge with four keywords.

Keyword One: Original Witkey Tasks - No Refunds

There is no absolute standard for evaluating task transactions, and there are no quantifiable indicators. Therefore, after employers post tasks on Witkey websites, they must accept the transaction rule of paying 100% of the task amount in advance and never getting refunds, regardless of whether they are satisfied with the results or not. Of course, from successful cases, most employers are satisfied with the task results.

Keyword Two: What if the collection results are unsatisfactory?

A small portion of the tasks posted by employers, such as business plans, tool development, studying abroad, script dubbing, and song creation, are highly difficult and challenging. If the final results are unsatisfactory, employers still have to select a winner from the unsatisfactory results according to the original Witkey task trading rules.

Keyword Three: Super Challenge Witkey Tasks

Employers on Task China can choose some challenging Witkey tasks as Super Challenge types. They also require 100% prepayment of the task amount, but if the final results are unsatisfactory, they can apply for the task challenge to be deemed a failure. The Super Challenge applies to Witkey tasks with a certain level of difficulty and a strong challenge for workers. Since it's a challenge, there is success and failure. If the challenge fails, 70% of the task amount will be refunded to the employer, and 10% will be paid to participating workers according to certain rules.

Keyword Four: Reduce Consumption Risk

Being able to get a refund if the Witkey task results are unsatisfactory helps reduce the consumption risk for employers and allows workers to participate in different levels of difficulty based on their own abilities. Some tasks are highly difficult and challenging, and although employers are willing to offer high task amounts, they worry about not being able to get satisfactory results and having to take significant risks. After the introduction of Super Challenge Witkey tasks, even if the results are unsatisfactory, they can still get their task amount refunded, which is fairer to employers.

The Super Challenge is suitable for publishing Witkey tasks with high difficulty and strong challenges. For some employers, it provides an additional choice that reduces consumption risk. However, the Super Challenge Witkey tasks may be less attractive to excellent workers. For most employers, it is still recommended to use the default Witkey task trading method.
