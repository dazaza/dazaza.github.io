Jiangmen Leather Goods Enterprises Achieve Breakthrough: Stable Exports Become the Breakthrough Point

Under the situation where domestic sales are difficult to expand in a short time, how to ensure that Jiangmen's "main force - home goods and leather goods enterprises" achieve a breakthrough, stable exports have become the breakthrough point. "The transformation and upgrading of foreign trade and economic enterprises is inevitable. Transformation is the only way that cannot be avoided or bypassed," said Li Hangao, deputy director of Jiangmen Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Bureau. Vigorously cultivating national-level export bases is one of the aids for Jiangmen to promote the transformation of foreign trade and economic enterprises.

Recently, the signing and unveiling ceremony of the China Home Goods Export Base and the China Bags and Leather Goods Export Base co-built by the China National Light Industry and Arts & Crafts Import and Export Chamber of Commerce and the Jiangmen Municipal Government was grandly held in Jiangmen. The landing of these two national-level export bases in Jiangmen marks a key step on the path of transformation of foreign trade and economy in Jiangmen.

Why Jiangmen Can Obtain Two National-Level Bases

The rooting of the two national-level export bases in Jiangmen is an affirmation and encouragement of the achievements made by Jiangmen in this work, as well as a kind of support. How did Jiangmen win such an honor?

According to the introduction of the Jiangmen Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Bureau, the production and export of home goods and bags and leather goods industries in Jiangmen have obvious regional advantages. Applying for the China Home Goods Export Base is conducive to enhancing the competitive advantage of this industry.

According to incomplete statistics, in 2009, the output value of Jiangmen's home goods industry exceeded 40 billion yuan, with an export value of 6.11 billion US dollars that year; in 2010, the export value of Jiangmen's home goods industry was 7.794 billion US dollars, an increase of 27.47% year-on-year; in 2010, the output value of Jiangmen's bags and leather goods industry exceeded 5.5 billion yuan, the export of bags and leather goods to the United States increased by 41.12% year-on-year, and the export of bags and leather goods to the Middle East increased by 47.89% year-on-year... For many years, Jiangmen's home goods and bags and leather goods industries have maintained a relative advantage in foreign exports. Therefore, someone likened Jiangmen's home goods export to a "big tree."

Li Zhuping, president of the Jiangmen Bags and Leather Goods Industry Association, introduced that Jiangmen's bags and leather goods manufacturing industry started in the 1950s. Currently, there are nearly 500 enterprises engaged in the production and sales of bags, leather, and leather goods, making it an important production base for travel cases, men's briefcases, and trendy handbags in South China. "Export regions are concentrated in Europe, Hong Kong, and the United States, and the three major markets account for about 62.65% of the city's total export value of bags and leather goods."

In addition to the bags and leather goods industry, the home goods industry is also one of the pillar industries in Jiangmen. Among them, the hardware stainless steel products industry alone has a history of nearly 40 years, with more than 1,000 hardware stainless steel tableware manufacturing enterprises, forming a relatively complete industrial chain with strong supporting capabilities. The proportion of mid-to-high-end products exceeds 50%, and the export value exceeds 950 million US dollars.

In addition to large-scale industries, the independent innovation capabilities of these two industries also stand out among various industries, producing brands like "Vinda" and "Jianwei", which are recognized as Chinese famous brands and well-known trademarks. Due to the early start of these two industries, they not only possess advanced production equipment and manufacturing processes but also gather a large number of industry talents. Their product design, production management, technical talents, and skilled workers in the home goods and bags and leather goods sectors enjoy a good reputation within the industry.
