I owed a bad saying at the beginning.

by ypvrit11 on 2011-07-05 10:05:43

The after-sales service for sold products is a bit "troublesome"

Yesterday, the reporter exclusively learned that Tosyo Electric (air conditioning brand "TOYO"), which was praised as the dark horse of air conditioners in the industry in the past two years, has now ceased all production. The reporter called Tosyo Electric Zhongshan Co., Ltd., and the staff member surnamed Xin from the Overseas Marketing Department confirmed to the reporter that the original management team of Tosyo Electric has mostly dispersed. Currently, Tosyo Electric Co., Ltd. is merely in name.

Today, the reporter learned from chain electrical appliance retailers such as Suning and Gome that Tosyo's cooperation with channel providers has already terminated this year. Currently, Gome no longer sells Tosyo air conditioners, and Suning has also cleared its inventory of Tosyo air conditioners. The last Tosyo booth at the Beigang store will be removed in the next few days. Tosyo Electric (Zhongshan) Co., Ltd. was established in March 2001 as a manufacturer of air conditioners jointly invested by Chinese, Japanese, and Hong Kong parties.

"Dealers really can't continue anymore"

A responsible person from Suning Electrical Appliances revealed to the reporter yesterday that Suning gradually stopped selling Tosyo air conditioners starting from the second half of last year, and both parties have officially parted ways this year. The responsible person stated, "Tosyo announced last year that it would shift all domestic sales to overseas sales. It's said that Tosyo air conditioners have recently been liquidated."

The reporter then called Li Weiqiang, the former general manager of Tosyo Electric Sales Co., Ltd. Li Weiqiang quietly told the reporter, "I am no longer in this position. Currently, a Mr. Guo is in charge of Tosyo's sales. Regarding the issues with Tosyo, I cannot say much now." When Vice President Mai Decheng of Tosyo Electric Sales Co., Ltd. received the reporter's call, he said, "I am on a business trip out of town, and I don't know about this matter." The mobile phone of Cao Desheng, the China market director of Tosyo Electric, remained unreachable.

Subsequently, the reporter called Tosyo Electric Zhongshan Co., Ltd., and the staff member surnamed Xin stated, "Our company will no longer engage in the production and sales of air conditioners, and there will no longer be any 'Tosyo' branded air conditioners in the future." When asked about the reason, the staff member said, "Regarding this issue, we are also helpless. I am also looking for a new job."

The after-sales service for sold products is a bit "troublesome"

It is understood that Tosyo air conditioners have a total annual production capacity of 2.8 million units and were once reputed to be one of the largest air conditioner manufacturers in the world two years ago. According to information obtained from channel providers, although Tosyo air conditioners' sales do not match those of first-tier brands, they still sell tens of thousands of units annually in Guangzhou. If Tosyo air conditioners cease production, Tosyo Electric will no longer be able to provide after-sales services for Tosyo air conditioners.

A responsible person from Suning Electrical Appliances stated, "Last year, Tosyo air conditioners sold quite well in Suning. They also invested a considerable amount in advertising and promotion costs. We don't know why Tosyo unilaterally terminated our cooperation this year without providing any products. We tried several times to negotiate with Tosyo personnel but couldn't find anyone."

This responsible person further stated that for all Tosyo air conditioners sold through Suning, Suning will continue to provide after-sales repair services, but spare parts replacement will not be available. A responsible person from Gome Electrical Appliances also expressed similar views, stating that they will continue to provide after-sales services for Tosyo. However, it was simultaneously learned that a specific component in Tosyo split-type air conditioners produced after 2003 is very complex and can only be replaced with an original unit. If this part malfunctions, it will cause the air conditioner to stop working and become irreparable. (Reported by Jiang Wen)

Seized by court, Tosyo Electric has ceased all production

China Air Conditioning Information Network / Xu Shi

By the end of May, Tosyo Electric (Zhongshan) Co., Ltd. fell into a state of complete production halt due to its assets being seized by component suppliers through court proceedings.

It was learned that on May 23, a Zhejiang-based component supplier, unable to recover payment, had the finished goods warehouse of Tosyo Electric sealed through the court. After this incident occurred, multiple other component suppliers of Tosyo Electric came upon hearing the news and proceeded to seal other production equipment and warehouses of Tosyo Electric; subsequently, the main creditor bank of Tosyo Electric also joined the debt collection efforts, leading to the complete cessation of production at Tosyo Electric.

It was learned that at the time of the incident, Luo Junzhang, the president of Tosyo Electric (Zhongshan) Co., Ltd., was not in Zhongshan but attending a meeting in India. After the incident, Luo Junzhang returned to Zhongshan to handle the matter.

It was learned that the total amount owed by Tosyo Electric to various component suppliers and banks is nearly 1 billion RMB yuan.
