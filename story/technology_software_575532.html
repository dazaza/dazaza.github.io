Man drags neighbor girl home, beats and threatens her before kneeling down to propose

by zhangdoniw0 on 2011-11-21 16:11:52

Our reporter Yang Xiaoming - After encountering a female college student from the neighboring house in the corridor, he forcibly pulled her into his own home. While threatening and beating her, he proposed to her. After being taken back to the police station by the police, this 43-year-old man said that his behavior was due to a momentary impulse.

In the morning, just after leaving home, the female college student encountered a horrifying scene. At around 6:50 am on the 24th, 23-year-old female college student Li Xin (a pseudonym) left her home to go to school. She turned around when she closed the door and met a man who lived in the next room. Although they had met before, she did not know him, so she hurriedly apologized to him. What Li Xin did not expect was that the man suddenly grabbed her hair and pulled her towards his house. Li Xin resisted with all her strength by kicking against the burglar-proof door, but it was futile. Li Xin was forcibly dragged into the man's house. She tried hard to resist and escape, but the man beat her while pulling her into the bedroom and took out a steel pipe to threaten her not to shout. Then, the man knelt down in front of the bed and complained to Li Xin, saying that because he had been in prison, he could not find a job or a wife. He demanded that Li Xin marry him. Hearing these words, Li Xin was completely baffled. She hurriedly said that her family situation was poor, she couldn't be late for school, and begged the man to let her go. At this point, the man's emotions gradually calmed down. He gave Li Xin 100 yuan and some apples, and said, "Then you can go. When will you come back?" Li Xin dared not answer and quickly ran out of the man's house. Due to her injuries not being serious, Li Xin did not go to the hospital but called her family to tell them about the incident, and then her family reported it to the police.

After receiving the alarm call, the Hongmiaopo Police Station of the Lianhu Public Security Bureau immediately arranged for the police to rush to the scene. At this time, Li Xin was still waiting downstairs, looking shaken. After asking about the situation, the police immediately went upstairs and arrested the man.

Upon interrogation, the man surnamed Cao, 43 years old, is from Xi'an. In 2002, Cao was sentenced to 10 years in prison by the Lianhu District People's Court for robbery and was released from prison in July this year. He currently works as a security guard at a company.

According to Cao's confession, at 6:50 am on the 24th, he came out of his house to buy breakfast. In the corridor, he saw a girl living on the same floor as him. At that moment, he didn't know what happened, his brain got heated up, and he pulled the girl into his house. The girl shouted "Help!" so he took an iron rod to threaten her not to shout. Later, he asked the girl to be friends with him, but she refused. He also threatened the girl, saying, "If you don't agree, we will die together." Later, the girl said that she was a student, needed to attend class, and couldn't be late, so he stopped dragging her and let her go.

The investigating officer of the Hongmiaopo Police Station told the reporter that Cao's behavior made people feel somewhat incomprehensible. When they asked Cao, "What did you intend to do when you forcibly pulled the girl into your house?" Cao said, "Because I have always been single and haven't married yet, I wanted her to be my friend and get married."

Currently, Cao has been criminally detained by the Lianhu police on suspicion of provoking trouble.
