Crown International, Jiangsu Shuntian's new season goal is to ensure a top six finish and strive for a top three position.

by nn8b9n1s on 2012-03-05 21:26:34

At the press conference held by Jiangsu Shuntian (Weibo) Football Club for the new season, it was learned that a total of four enterprises would sponsor the club in the new season. The target for the new season of Jiangsu Guoxin Shuntian Team, which ranked fourth last season, was set as "maintaining sixth place and striving for third". The five newly introduced players also made their appearances.

At the press conference, besides Deng Zhuoxiang, Xu Youzhi, and Wang Yulong (Weibo) who had already appeared before, the fifth foreign player Milean Madakovic and the Olympic team young player Jiang Jiajun (Weibo) signed at the end of the transfer deadline also made their debuts. The team's head coach Dragan expressed his gratitude to the club for introducing these players, believing that these players could quickly integrate into the team and make the team's tactical play richer.

Xiecheng (Group) Holding Co., Ltd., which sponsored Shuntian last season, continued to support the team this season. Meanwhile, three other "provincial-level" enterprises also sponsored the team, including Jiangsu Real Estate Investment Co., Ltd., Jiangsu International Trust Co., Ltd., and Jiangsu Natural Gas Co., Ltd. Xu Zujian, Vice President of Jiangsu Guoxin Group and Honorary Chairman of Jiangsu Shuntian Football Club, said that the investment of the club in the new season would be higher than last season. Although the team achieved good results last season by ranking fourth, everything should start anew for the new season. Xu Zujian also specifically mentioned the situation where Shuntian performed well at home but poorly away last season, hoping that the team could change its image as an "away bug", otherwise, no matter how well it performed at home, it would be useless.

In the first round of the new season, Jiangsu Guoxin Shuntian Team would face Shanghai Shenhua (Weibo) Team away, the latter becoming one of the most eye-catching teams in the Chinese Super League (Weibo) due to the introduction of veteran star Anelka.
