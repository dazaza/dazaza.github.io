The life philosophy told by the mini-game: mistakes will accumulate.

by mlwang on 2011-09-28 17:00:55

With the continuous acceleration of life pace, easy and leisurely mini-games have become people's first choice for daily stress relief. From the "ancient" Tetris to the currently popular Angry Birds, fishing, and fruit slicing, simple mini-games bring more than just simple happiness to people, but also profound philosophical implications about life.

Tetris: Mistakes accumulate while successes disappear.

If you make a mistake, your error will remain forever and keep accumulating until it drives you into a desperate situation. Only by finding the right way to solve the existing mistakes will the situation improve, and the success thereby brought will not stay long in your life. Avoiding mistakes and constantly pursuing one success after another is the essence of your life.

Plants vs Zombies: You must adjust your state regularly to deal with different challenges.

There is more than one kind of enemy, and the environment will not always be advantageous to you, so you must constantly adjust your state, using the most appropriate method to repel the toughest enemies while preserving your strength to face even more difficult challenges. Also, every weapon needs a cooldown time, the best solution is to prepare several options.

Angry Birds: Sometimes lowering your heart and mind is to fly higher.

Never lose confidence because you are in a low point. What you need to do before flying higher is to endure and gather strength, and take longer time to adjust the direction of flight. There is no one who is always on top in this world, only those who know how to rest can fly higher, provided that they focus their eyes on the distance rather than the feet.

Fruit Ninja: Fruits and bombs coexist, opportunities and challenges go hand in hand.

Opportunities always come suddenly and vanish in a flash, you must always be ready and seize the best opportunity. Sometimes danger comes with opportunity, how to accurately identify the two and make the correct response is a skill you must master, otherwise you may face the situation of failing at the last moment.

Fishing Joy: Never think that you have a lot of capital.

Whether it is time, energy or youth, all will be consumed as capital in repeated efforts, cherish everything you can use, never think that you have plenty, and never waste them. The only thing you can do is to hit the target every time, and exchange the least capital for the most harvest.
