Chunhuiyuan Hot Spring Introduction: Chunhuiyuan Hot Spring Resort, located by the beautiful Wenyu River in Shunyi District, Beijing, covers an area of more than 600 mu. It is a comprehensive resort featuring hot springs, integrating business meetings, leisure vacations, entertainment, and catering. The resort's design is magnificent, reaching four-star standards, with a serene and elegant Zen atmosphere everywhere. The rich and varied hot spring baths, warm and comfortable guest rooms, fully equipped meeting rooms, carefully prepared delicious dishes, and the attentive service provided by well-trained professional staff make Chunhuiyuan Hot Spring Resort the favorite destination for urban fashionistas this winter.

The hot spring of Chunhuiyuan Hot Spring Resort is sourced from a depth of 1800 meters underground, sharing the same origin as the famous Xiaotangshan Hot Spring in Beijing. The spring water temperature remains at 60 degrees Celsius all year round, belonging to weakly acidic sodium bicarbonate thermal mineral spring. Chunhuiyuan Sunshine International Hot Spring Club is Beijing's first integrated SPA leisure health center that combines hot spring beauty therapy and living water therapy. It is meticulously crafted, exuding nobility and elegance, creating a five-star "soaking" environment. Diverse modern hot spring bathing methods include: characteristic Chinese herbal hot spring baths, far-infrared light wave baths, Basque showers, Japanese large wooden bucket medicinal baths, hot spring bubble massage baths, noblewoman fragrant body baths, milk baths, lingzhi ginseng baths, etc.

The hot springs within the resort are unique, sourced from a depth of 1800 meters underground, sharing the same origin as the famous Xiaotangshan Hot Spring in Beijing. Cleverly integrating meetings with leisure, coupled with the surrounding fresh and elegant natural environment, it embodies comfort and high-class humanistic ambiance everywhere, inspiring boundless imagination and wisdom among the intellectual elites from all walks of life who come here to attend conferences.

Chunhuiyuan is the first to bring outdoor hot spring pools into villas. The lakeside villas and hot spring villas, rich in personalized design, are built around the lake, offering beautiful scenery. Moreover, the courtyard outdoor soaking pools built inside the villas allow guests to enjoy the gushing hot springs from 1800 meters underground without leaving home, whether it's the cold winter or scorching summer, sunny or rainy days, breathing in the fresh air of nature within their personal space.
