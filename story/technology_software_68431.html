Independent innovation has forged Haochen's glory - display of independent innovation achievements of domestic CAD software

by haochenruanjian on 2008-05-13 16:02:36

On May 7th, the domestically produced platform design software with independent intellectual property rights - HiCAD 2008i was grandly launched in Beijing and a user liaison conference was convened. Deputy Director Xu Chao of the National Copyright Administration, Director Zhao Xiuling of the Copyright Department of the National Copyright Administration, Deputy Director Ma Yanmin of the Torch Center of the Ministry of Science and Technology, Director Song Shaogang of the High-tech Industry Development Center of the Torch Center of the Ministry of Science and Technology, Chairman Wu Yiliang of the China Investigation and Design Association, Standing Secretary Wang Ziniu of the China Investigation and Design Association, Chairman Wang Yanmei of the Computer Application Committee of the China Investigation and Design Association, Academician Ni Guangnan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Director Lu Xingsheng of the Copyright Department of the Jiangsu Provincial Copyright Bureau, Chairman Lu Genfa of the Jiangsu Provincial Investigation and Design Association, Director Zhou Xudong of the Suzhou Municipal Science and Technology Bureau, Deputy Director Su Bo of the Administrative Committee of the Suzhou Industrial Park, Deputy Director Xu Wenqing of the Science and Technology Bureau of the Suzhou Industrial Park, Director Wu Liangqing of the Suzhou Software Industry Office, General Manager Mao Busheng of Suzhou Science and Technology Venture Capital Company and other leaders attended this conference. They gathered together with representatives from design units across the country, CAD software development units, and software agents to jointly witness the excellent achievements of domestic CAD software.

As a typical representative of domestic CAD software, Haochen Software is one of the first explorers of domestic CAD technology. For sixteen years, it has been committed to the research and promotion of CAD software and has introduced the Haochen ICAD platform series software with independent intellectual property rights through independent innovation. Haochen actively explores internationalization and continuously adheres to independent innovation, independent intellectual property rights, and an independent brand. With a focused product strategy, innovative technology, world-class quality, and considerate service, it has won the trust of a wide range of international users and partners. With full compatibility, supporting features, high quality, low price, and precise grasp of local design needs, HiCAD 2008i has not only significantly improved in function and performance but also emphasized practicality, continuity, and convenience in actual operations. Based on the principle of "smooth throughout, worry-free use," HiCAD 2008i aims to make users worry-free in file input, file operation, and printing.

The development of domestic software requires joint efforts from all parties. In his speech, Chairman Wu Yiliang of the China Investigation and Design Association reviewed the promotion work of domestic software. The "Domestic Software Application Exchange Forum" and the "Domestic Two-dimensional CAD Software Exhibition Activity" have achieved good results, generating a large number of domestic software users within the industry. Based on the good application of Haochen Software within the industry, the Haochen Software User Liaison Conference has been approved by the association for establishment. "The association will continue to strongly support the development of domestic software, push forward the key work of promoting domestic two-dimensional CAD software, and encourage more investigation and design units to actively use domestic CAD platform software."

Deputy Director Xu Chao of the National Copyright Administration detailed the policies regarding intellectual property protection in his speech, mentioning the improving copyright environment in China, "A favorable copyright environment has promoted the revitalization of our country's software industry. Under such circumstances, domestic software has grown rapidly."

Director Lu Xingsheng of the Copyright Department of the Jiangsu Provincial Copyright Bureau specifically mentioned that Haochen Software has made great contributions in the work of legalization, and expressed pride in having a company like Haochen in Jiangsu.

The software industry, as a fundamental and strategic industry, is an important support for enhancing the overall scientific and technological competitiveness of the country, a key link in informatization construction, and the pinnacle of international competition in the 21st century. Deputy Director Ma Yanmin of the Torch Center of the Ministry of Science and Technology attended this conference and delivered an important speech, expressing high attention and recognition for the independent innovation work of domestic software.

A positive environment encompasses more than just policy and government support; strong public opinion also truly supports and encourages domestic manufacturers to innovate independently. Academician Ni Guangnan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences came to the venue and has helped software manufacturers like Haochen who are dedicated to the true revitalization of domestic software.

The development of domestic software requires a favorable climate. Corporate innovation cannot be separated from a suitable soil. Haochen's success and brilliance benefit from independent innovation and even more from the innovative atmosphere carefully cultivated by the Suzhou government over the long term and the innovation platforms it has painstakingly built.

"In recent years, due to a batch of software enterprises like Suzhou Haochen, the software industry in Suzhou has developed rapidly," said Director Zhou Xudong of the Suzhou Municipal Science and Technology Bureau: "Therefore, in future development, we will certainly provide support and assistance in all aspects for Haochen's innovative development, promoting the progress of the software industry in Suzhou and even Jiangsu Province."

Due to the increasingly strong innovative atmosphere and constantly enhanced innovation capabilities, the Suzhou Industrial Park has been rated as the top "domestically most investment-worthy development zone" in the eyes of multinational companies. Deputy Director Su Bo of the Administrative Committee of the Suzhou Industrial Park stated: "We will further increase the focus on scientific and technological innovation, gather advantageous resources, and fully support the strengthening and expansion of typical enterprises of independent innovation represented by Haochen Company, striving to achieve new major breakthroughs in the cluster development of high-tech industries as soon as possible."

"Innovation comes from application, finding experience in application," this is the profound realization of Haochen Software during its development process. In December 2006, Beijing Urban Construction Design Research Institute comprehensively launched the application of 900 sets of Haochen ICAD platform and professional software. Chief Engineer Yang Xiuren personally led the team, determined the implementation plan, and supported the transplantation of other professional software (such as Explorer Software) onto the Haochen platform. In addition, successful procurement and design cases from many large enterprises such as Xiamen Municipal Institute, Siemens, Haier, Lenovo, etc., have enabled Haochen Software to find R&D ideas and market expansion strategies through sufficient communication with customers, "It is the support of users that allows us to develop better and faster." President Lu Xiang of Haochen Software said so.

Based on the initial success in the domestic market, Haochen Software began to explore foreign markets. With its solid product quality, Haochen Software quickly established a distribution network in many regions including the United States, Canada, Brazil, Chile, Finland, the United Kingdom, France, Germany, Russia, Ukraine, Romania, Croatia, Japan, Australia, India, Singapore, Indonesia, etc., exporting products to 45 countries and regions, achieving a sales growth rate of 300%.

In addition to creating solid products, Haochen Software places 360-degree comprehensive service at the same level of importance as R&D. This preparation meeting for the Haochen Software User Liaison Conference is a concentrated manifestation of Haochen Software's quality service. The Haochen Software User Liaison Conference is a non-profit organization that provides technical, legal, policy support, and other services, discussions, and training activities for Haochen users. The Liaison Conference will leverage various high-quality resources owned by Haochen Company, such as premium customers, professional technology, specialized talents, etc., to provide members with a platform for exchanging management and technology, experience and knowledge, theory and practice.

To grow and strengthen China's software enterprises, they must continuously improve and perfect the industrial chain and integrate existing resources. In the integration of the entire CAD/CAM/CAE industrial chain, our innovation capability is still hovering at a low level. Various systems bring about more fragmented management, and information islands are becoming increasingly severe. Haochen has always been committed to cooperating with partners and continuously providing complete solutions for CAD/CAM/CAE, perfectly solving the integration problem of multiple information resources, greatly saving user costs and improving user work efficiency.

The development of domestic software requires a positive environment, which includes government support, association promotion, corporate efforts, research institute results transformation, user feedback, and other factors. Only when various forces work together to promote the cause of domestic software can the software industry in China truly be revitalized.

"To open the door of hope for domestic software"

"Only through innovation can there be development, and only by persisting in innovation can greater glory be achieved. And innovation not only includes technological innovation but also a comprehensive embodiment of market, service, and other aspects." President Lu Xiang of Haochen Software said: "In future development, Haochen Software will continue to adhere to independent innovation, strengthen exchanges and cooperation with the government, users, and other software manufacturers, and push the development of domestic software to a new height."

To accelerate the legalization process in the surveying and design industry and solve the legalization problems of small and medium-sized design units, Haochen Company cooperated with the Surveying and Design Association to jointly carry out the 'Hope Donation Project' for domestic CAD software and awarded products and copyright certificates to the first batch of donated enterprises at the conference. Through concrete actions, Haochen further promoted the legalization work of software in the surveying and design industry.
