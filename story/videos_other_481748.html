Jiangsu Changfa Group is a large diversified industrial group integrating agricultural equipment, refrigeration appliances, and real estate. It owns three main companies: Changzhou Changfa Agricultural Equipment Co., Ltd., Jiangsu Changfa Refrigeration Co., Ltd., and Jiangsu Changfa Real Estate Co., Ltd. The group currently employs more than 10,000 people, including over 1,500 professional technical personnel and more than 200 senior engineers. In 2009, the group's revenue exceeded 7.5 billion yuan, and it continues to grow at an annual rate of 30%, specializing in disc-type series.

In 2002, the Changfa trademark was recognized by the National Trademark Office as a China Famous Brand. The same year, Changfa Group became one of the first 520 enterprises in China to be honored for its emphasis on contracts and creditworthiness by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce. The Changfa single-cylinder diesel engine and walk-behind tractor were respectively awarded China's Renowned Product in 2005 and 2006.

Changzhou Changfa Agricultural Equipment Co., Ltd. is a large-scale agricultural machinery manufacturing enterprise that integrates R&D, production, and sales. Through mergers and acquisitions, it has formed a product chain consisting of tractors, combine harvesters, rice transplanters, generator sets, and diesel engines. The company has four major business units: wheeled tractors, rice transplanters, electromechanical products, casting, as well as eight subsidiaries: Power Company, Research Institute, Marketing Company, Import and Export Company, Material Company, Fengling Agricultural Equipment, Benhang Gears, and Jialian Harvesting Machinery, covering a total area of more than 2,000 mu. As a leading enterprise in China’s agricultural machinery industry, the company produces many top-notch agricultural machinery products both domestically and internationally with strong technical capabilities and advanced process designs. For example, the CF series of medium and large wheeled tractors have been rated as national consumer satisfaction brands for consecutive years; the 2ZS-4 type hand-guided rice transplanter has quickly gained popularity in the domestic market; the full-feed and semi-feed rice-wheat combine harvester has reached international leading standards in overall performance; various types of generator sets are exported to more than 60 countries, including Europe and America, accounting for 90%; Changfa brand diesel engines and hand-guided tractors rank among the top producers and sellers in the domestic industry. In recent years, the company has strengthened cooperation with top research institutions at home and abroad, developing a series of high-quality agricultural machinery products that lead industry trends, such as new biogas generator sets, vertical direct-connected single-cylinder diesel engines, Golden Crown Series premium diesel engines, CF4108 multi-cylinder diesel engines, and 150 horsepower wheeled tractors, all of which possess excellent power and economic performance.

Jiangsu Changfa Refrigeration Co., Ltd. (shortened as Changfa Stock, stock code: 002413) mainly produces evaporators and condensers for refrigerators and air conditioners, with an annual production capacity of 21.85 million sets, ranking first globally in terms of product variety and production scale. Its products are sold worldwide to major manufacturers of refrigerators, freezers, and air conditioners, becoming designated suppliers for well-known enterprises such as Electrolux, Siemens, GE, Panasonic, Samsung, LG, Haier, etc. It is the largest two-appliance production base in China, with Changfa brand evaporators and condensers consistently ranking first domestically in market share for multiple years. The aluminum foil project invested in by the company at a cost of 1.8 billion yuan can produce 30,000 tons of aluminum foil annually, further extending the industrial chain and enhancing comprehensive competitiveness, laying a solid foundation for the company's new strategic layout.

Jiangsu Changfa Real Estate Co., Ltd. was founded in 2003, with real estate development and operation as its main business, possessing Grade One qualifications for real estate development enterprises. Since its establishment, the company has adhered to a regional strategy of standing firm along the Shanghai-Nanjing line and developing in the Yangtze River Delta, establishing a rigorous and efficient team, upholding forward-looking and specialized development principles, and striving to become a leading real estate enterprise at the forefront of the market. Currently, the company owns more than 3,000 mu of land reserves and develops projects with an annual construction area of 1 million square meters. Over the years, the Changfa people have upheld the cultural theme of being open and honest in their dealings and diligent in their work, striving together to make Changfa Real Estate a comprehensive real estate operator integrating property development, commercial operations, and property management. Its development footprint has spread across six major areas in the Yangtze River Delta—Kunshan, Suzhou, Wuxi, Changzhou, Zhenjiang, and Shanghai—and is gradually expanding into broader national markets.

From 2003 to 2010, Changfa Real Estate cumulatively developed 17 projects, with investment amounts exceeding 9.8 billion yuan, sales area reaching 1.05 million square meters, development area reaching 2.92 million square meters, achieving sales revenue of 10 billion yuan, cumulative delivered area reaching 1.477 million square meters, and cumulative delivered households reaching 11,374. In 2011, the total construction area under construction exceeded 1.9 million square meters.

The pragmatic and innovative Changfa people will continue to adhere to sustainable, energy-saving, low-carbon, and green development directions. By relying on culture, management, R&D, services, and talent as five key pillars, they aim to form a comprehensive competitive ability for sustainable development. They will persist in taking on social responsibilities as outstanding corporate citizens, actively participating in various public welfare causes and urban renovation developments, striving to grow into giants in China's future real estate industry.

Currently, Changfa Real Estate has a development volume of nearly 10 million square meters in Wuxi, where the "Qingyang Yuting" project in the Nanchang District and the "Ou Feng Xintiandi" urban complex project in the deputy central district are under development and on sale.
