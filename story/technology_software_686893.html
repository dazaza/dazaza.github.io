by bestmen1988 on 2012-03-02 11:42:06

December 30, 2011, Friday, Overcast. Today's weather is extremely gloomy! It's hazy, as if covered by a thin mist, and it feels like snow might fall at any moment. The transparency is quite poor! After dawn, seeing such weather conditions, Xiaocao wore a down jacket while exercising! When walking in the yard, she felt much warmer, but as a result, her legs felt a bit cold! Therefore, in the afternoon when going out, she wore thick long skirts and new short boots, but since she wasn't used to them, she felt her feet were very cumbersome, making Xiaocao seem less agile, though indeed much warmer. To reduce the heat, Xiaocao wore something thicker... and thus her feet didn't freeze, and she returned home feeling warm all over! However, there was one thing: after walking for a long time, her feet felt heavy! Haha! Xiaocao originally intended to persist until tomorrow, which would complete a month. January is too cold, and her grandson's kindergarten is also on break, so she decided not to go out and just stay at home to move around! Haha! So wearing thicker clothes, even a slight jump would make her feel very hot. Fortunately, today at 9 o'clock, she had to go to the office, where she would be active for only about tens of minutes, and even then she didn't dare to jump vigorously. In summary, it was very hot!

In the morning, Xiaocao went to the office with the old leader "Jiejie You Chongyang". They received a supplementary payment of 200 yuan per person due to this year's heating price increase, along with a ticket for collecting grain and oil! Regardless of the quantity, it represents the unit's goodwill towards retired comrades for the New Year and Spring Festival! During the lunch break, Xiaocao set the alarm to 3:40 PM, rushing to the office at 2:30 PM, where "Jiejie You Chongyang" was already waiting in the office. Then, "Jiejie You Chongyang" told Xiaocao that the materials were written by Xiao * , but he couldn't fully understand the relevant intentions expressed, nor could he accurately put into words what he wanted to say. Therefore, the key points, difficulties, conventions, and existing problems were divided into four aspects, and Xiaocao made a simple record. Since Xiaocao hasn't been working this year, she isn't very familiar with many new policies, their actual work situations, and the measures they've taken, as well as the implementation status of each link in their work. "Jiejie You Chongyang" also let colleagues copy some auxiliary materials and required them to send the written materials to Xiaocao! These are materials for the bureau's assessment report, requiring a length of about ten minutes, so the difficulty should not be great. The bigger challenge is that many details discussed today may not be fully remembered when writing. No problem, timely contact with "Jiejie You Chongyang" and colleagues can help. Xiaocao must send the draft to them as soon as they start work on the 4th, and then let "Jiejie You Chongyang" review it, indicating where it is inappropriate and guiding colleagues to modify it accordingly! Therefore, from tomorrow, Xiaocao has to focus on this task, but tomorrow is the last day before the holiday, and there will be no time to write blogs in the morning. During the New Year's holiday, when her grandson comes back, she definitely won't have a peaceful mind to write either. Thus, the crucial time is tomorrow afternoon, and we'll see based on the progress of the writing! Therefore, tonight Xiaocao will give a simple summary of her situation here on Sina Blog Garden! Haha!
