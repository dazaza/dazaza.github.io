42 million SMEs, 10% corporate email usage rate

by now on 2008-09-26 09:16:19

In recent years, the development of China's small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) has been rapid. By 2008, the number of domestic SMEs had exceeded 42 million households, accounting for 99.8% of the total number of enterprises. The construction of enterprise informatization has become crucial.

According to iResearch Consulting based on data from the Radicati Group, it is predicted that by 2011, the number of corporate users will grow at an average annual rate of about 20%, reaching 16.45 billion emails per day. However, according to a relevant person in charge of Times Network (www.now.cn), most of China's SMEs are still in the growth stage, with relatively lagging informatization levels. Only about 10% of SME users use corporate email services, which forms a stark contrast with the large number of SMEs.

Corporate email services allow enterprises to quickly and efficiently exchange information or data, improving work efficiency. Times Network reminds its customers that enterprises can combine their own characteristics and choose suitable corporate email services and value-added functions, achieving a tailored fit.

Anti-spam function

Currently, China has become the world's second-largest spam manufacturing country after the United States. The increasingly rampant spam has seriously affected the online marketing activities of enterprises. Corporate email services use professional antivirus engines to achieve real-time online antivirus functions and have a powerful anti-spam system, ensuring green email communication services for enterprises. For example, Times Network's intelligent spam filter uses internationally leading artificial intelligence simulation technology, simulating the human brain's "fuzzy judgment" process to identify spam and employs four-layer spam filtering technology. The success rate of anti-spam reaches over 99.8%, comprehensively rejecting spam and allowing you to no longer be troubled by spam!

Global email sending ensures smooth international trade

When mainland email users send emails to overseas mailboxes, they frequently receive notifications of overseas rejections. This not only causes serious losses for enterprises engaged in international trade but also severely damages the company's credibility and image. Global email sending ensures smooth communication between the mainland and overseas, without geographical restrictions, enhancing the international competitiveness of enterprises.

High-efficiency mobile office

In this information-driven society where changes occur rapidly, "efficiency" has become a catchphrase for some executives. However, for people who frequently travel on business, sending and receiving emails can be a challenge. Nowadays, many service providers offer products with mobile email support. You can simply log into your VIP email via a mobile browser and use it as conveniently and quickly as on a computer.

The business world is like a battlefield, where details determine success or failure! Using the enterprise's independent domain name as the suffix for employee emails is crucial. It helps establish the corporate brand image and enhances credibility. Various signs indicate that with the promotion of corporate email services and the deepening construction of enterprise informatization, corporate email services will inevitably become a standard configuration for SMEs, with a bright future ahead.
