by fenqu on 2007-11-12 15:25:44

A website without the support of its members will never succeed!

Let me tell you a story about me and the website's members!

Starting a business is actually a very arduous task. It’s now 1:30 in the morning, and I still can't sleep peacefully. There are still some things that need to be handled. Today's tasks must be finished today and cannot be delayed until tomorrow. Moreover, I have to handle everything myself; there is no one who can help me, not even an assistant.

Although starting a business is a tough endeavor, most entrepreneurs find some joy in the monotonous process of entrepreneurship, maintaining a positive attitude, and regulating their emotions. Whenever I have thought about giving up, I always find reasons in my mind that prevent me from doing so, seeking spiritual pillars and encouragement. The online accounting site I currently operate—Caike Online (www.coko365.com)—does not have any dazzling success halo. Instead, like many ordinary entrepreneurs, I repeat the same path of having no members, no fame, and no funds, and I persistently struggle. Because there are no members, I need to work harder on publicity and attracting members. Because there is no fame, I promote it vigorously to increase exposure. And because there is no money, I can't hire anyone, so all tasks must be personally executed by myself. Many people reading this article might think in their hearts that I am just another advertisement soft-sell. Yes, this article is indeed for promoting my Caike Online, aiming to enhance its fame and increase member registration. Why? Because these are what I currently lack the most. If I had money, would I still sit here late at night writing such an article? Definitely not. Although everyone may feel repulsed while reading this article, amidst the repulsion, there are also some gains. At least, you can see the persistence and effort of an entrepreneur, as well as their fighting spirit.

After saying so much in the introduction, I haven’t yet touched on the main point. I’ve been doing Caike Online for a long time and have gained many insights. Among them, the most important is "a website without the support of its members will never succeed." Sometimes I really want to give up, but every time I think about the members, especially knowing that Caike Online has so many enthusiastic members supporting and caring about it, I can't give up. I should persevere. Only by persevering and making Caike Online better can I repay these enthusiastic members. Sometimes, entrepreneurship isn't entirely for oneself. After getting familiar with the website's members, there develops a sense of emotion. When fully devoted to running the website, there is emotion involved, possibly for the members or for the website itself, rather than solely for one's own future and prospects.

In the process of operating Caike, I naturally encountered many enthusiastic members, and here I want to tell a story about some of the interactions between me and these members.

One day, an old friend called me to chat. He is currently in Hangzhou, responsible for a foreign e-commerce website. We chatted a lot, and before we knew it, the conversation turned to Caike Online. Then he smiled and said, "Little Tian, a colleague introduced me to use an online accounting website. When I opened the website, it turned out to be your Caike Online!" After hearing that, I was extremely happy because Caike's development and its current state are not just due to my hard work alone, but also due to the silent support of many members. Many enthusiastic members find Caike Online great and valuable, so they recommend it to their classmates, friends, or colleagues.

Last Wednesday, I met an old friend at Zhongshan Park, also a friend in the internet circle. We agreed to meet at Starbucks at the No.7 entrance of Zhongshan Park. We agreed on 3 PM, and he arrived early, so I did too. Not long after meeting, we talked about our respective work and lives, and I briefly introduced the situation of Caike Online to him. After listening, he told me very mysteriously, "Little Tian, your website is really good. It made my wife use your website daily for accounting, but I rarely use it because I don't have much time." After hearing that, I was happy all afternoon! Actually, this happiness is the result of my hard entrepreneurial efforts, and it represents hope. I believe this kind of happiness is comparable to earning 100,000 yuan. The information provided by my friend shows that my website is valuable, and my Caike Online is gaining more and more support from friends, forming a good reputation.

Tonight, a stranger added me as a friend. At first, I thought it was something else, maybe just chatting with another friend in the internet circle. Why did he add me? Because he found my Caike Online accounting website interesting, so he added me. Why did he know about Caike Online? It was through his wife. His wife wanted to keep accounts, so she searched online for accounting tools. Initially, she found Caizhi Accounting Software, but since it was a standalone version, it wasn't convenient. Later, she found our Caike Online and decided it was convenient as an online accounting software, didn't require downloading, could be used anywhere, and was powerful and free. So, she happily decided to use our Caike Online. After the friend explained the reason, it made me happy again!

Although the entrepreneurial process is arduous, this arduous journey contains unexpected joy and fun. Only entrepreneurs can truly understand it! Therefore, whenever I feel like giving up, thinking about these members makes me reluctant to abandon my efforts. I tell myself that I must persevere and make Caike Online (www.coko365.com) better, launching more powerful features and providing higher quality online accounting services to repay the love our members have shown towards Caike Online.
