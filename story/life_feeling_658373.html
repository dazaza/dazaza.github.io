You can stay away if you want.

I recognize her as your current lover. I had a premonition when she showed up at my door, that she was here for you.

I stood at the classroom door, expressionless, staring at her with an apparent calmness though in reality, I was already sweating in my palms. It wasn't because I felt guilty; I had nothing to feel guilty about. But since she's related to you, I have to be on guard.

After standing there for a while, she left with a female companion. My friend told me that she said you were my ex-boyfriend, so she came to take a look. I instantly felt repulsed. What kind of girl is she? For what reason did she bring up our nearly year-old relationship out of nowhere? Does seeing me, the past protagonist, make her happy? Or is she trying to compare whether she, the new person, is better than the old?

Ever since she appeared that day, I haven't been able to feel happy.

The first thing I did after getting home after the last class was to open QQ to see if you were online. Your avatar was gray. I opened the chat window and sent you a message, then quietly sat aside reading the newspaper. After a while, your avatar started blinking, you were online, and you quickly replied to me. You said: She has already told you what happened today. You said: Let her take a look, isn't it troubling?

In the end, you said: Sorry, I don't want to bother you either.

At this point, my nose became sour, and tears uncontrollably fell onto my hands. With trembling hands from crying, I struggled to type "Is the ex-girlfriend more humble?" on the keyboard and pressed "Enter+Ctrl" to send. You quickly replied, saying "No, I didn't think that way."

My red eyes tightly stared at the words you sent on the display screen, each word tugging at my heartstrings, the feeling of heartache becoming increasingly profound. I closed the chat window and cried even more heartbreakingly, leaving large water stains on the blue bedsheet.

Actually, what she did was very normal. Her purpose was simple; she just unnecessarily brought up this past event, she just wanted to see the main characters of the story, she was just full of curiosity, it's not a big deal. But I just didn't want, couldn't let her step into this mess.

Even though I've chosen to stay away, why do you still leave marks in my heart?

It's almost forgotten now, but slowly, bit by bit, I'm starting to remember.

When you first transferred to this class, I barely noticed you. Your ordinariness didn't cause any ripples in this class. Occasionally, when someone mentioned you, they would just say "What's the name of the new student who transferred to our class?" But one afternoon a few weeks later, your figure in a black T-shirt standing in the corridor reminded me of someone I missed, and then you began to enter my line of sight. I started paying attention to you, to your affairs.

For a long time, we had no interaction. My personality isn't timid or fidgety, but I didn't dare to say a single word to you. Later, I forgot how it started, but suddenly our relationship improved, our interactions increased, and we talked more. Even later, our ordinary friendship began to undergo subtle changes. But neither you nor I broke this layer of relationship.

Actually, you're quite a decent guy, with a bronze face that's somewhat handsome. The girls in the class began to pay attention to you, and some even fell for you. I remember LXT asked you before pursuing you if you liked me. I was both looking forward to and afraid of your answer. You didn't answer her because you said it was none of her business, and I am your classmate. I guessed she admired you, her various behaviors made people suspicious. It was around this time that rumors started circulating in the class saying "You like someone." That someone was me, and I was indeed surprised, but everything seemed reasonable. The ambiguous feelings between you and me not only made others suspicious but also made me start doubting myself.

Graduation was approaching, and we lived peacefully and happily, happiness spreading to the hearts of our few close friends. At the time, I could imagine that we would continue to get along like this in the future, without the troubles of others. Even the little secret between you and me, I hoped to keep forever. But ultimately, this layer of relationship was broken. Just when OYZ taught me to seize the good opportunity with you, she found you again and asked you about your feelings for me. Perhaps you thought all this was my plan, you thought this was the answer I wanted, so you didn't tell her, you only left it for me. When you prepared to tell me everything, I fled at the last minute because I was scared, I retreated, I feared your suddenness, I feared whether your next action was going to confess? Because even I had guessed your thoughts.

But I eventually couldn't escape. The next day, your letter clearly confirmed that my guess was correct. Amidst the joy, I blamed OYZ's gossip for putting us in such an awkward situation.

My small reserve quickly gave in under your attack. You are a very proactive and scheming person because you directly hugged me and said "If you give me a clear answer, I won't let you go." You didn't even give me enough time to consider thoroughly. To avoid other people's prying eyes, I agreed. Actually, I didn't tell you, I had already silently agreed.

However, I didn't expect our newly budding relationship to wither so quickly. Of course, this was something that happened later.

After being with you, I suddenly became afraid of interacting with you, afraid of facing you, I always avoided you. We didn't get closer but instead became more distant. I remember when we were still ordinary friends, you treated me better than others, the things you said to me were more ambiguous than to others. Every word you said back then made me feel warm inside. Even my best friend Xiao Ou sighed to me, saying how lucky I am. I don't know if you can still recall those words, but I can vaguely remember them now because the sweetness they brought me, I can still feel now. You were the first person to say such domineering yet satisfying words to me.

Do you remember? Later, you said I was a very face-conscious person. So until then, I never once said "I love you" to you, not even "I like you" could I say. At the time, I thought you would understand, you would understand me as I understood you, but you didn't. What you needed was a clear and explicit expression, what you wanted was heartfelt words spoken openly. But at that time, due to the hurt from my first love, I stubbornly refused to say "I love you" to anyone again. People young and bold yet stubborn, love young and passionate yet impulsive, just like me, just like you.

After many small arguments, you began to waver in this relationship, you began to doubt my sincerity. I admit that my heart wasn't 100% yours, the feelings I gave you were indeed mixed with impurities. But my lingering thoughts for the old person had already been abandoned before I started with you. There was just his shadow in my heart. But when your figure became clearer in my heart, you decided to leave me. You proposed breaking up, you said, you hope I am happy. You also told me, if you still love him, then go find him.

I didn't tell you, actually at that time my heart was full of you, my feelings for him had returned to the position of a friend. But I didn't tell you these things because you also said I was a very face-conscious person, so I didn't want to say it and didn't want to ask you to stay again. Then, you told others that we broke up, then, you told others that I hurt your heart, my words hurt you too much, I didn't consider your feelings. After hearing these words from others, my love for you began to devalue. Then, I started to hate your nonsense, dislike your unguarded and ruthless words, we started arguing, we started blaming each other, and then, we went back to being "ten years ago, I didn't know you, you didn't belong to me, ten years later, we are friends who can still greet each other."
