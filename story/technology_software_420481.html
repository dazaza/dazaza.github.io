Reading Prompt

Talent competitions, intelligence quizzes, and balloon battles. On the afternoon of April 9th, the "Community Grand Mobilization Show My Family" competition co-organized by Dahan Newspaper and Zhengzhou TV Station was held in the Dihu community of the Hanghai West Road Office in Zhongyuan District. Many readers and residents experienced the joy brought by the competition, and the children were especially delighted, spending a happy afternoon with their families.

[Performances Here Made Everyone Happy]

Drumming, fast clapping, model walking, drawing applause continuously

Singing, dancing, drumming, storytelling, fast clapping, model walking... In the talent competition segment, the young contestants demonstrated a wide range of skills.

The little boy Pei Junxian from the No. 8 participating family, dressed in a white shirt and jeans, looked very handsome. Although only five years old, his hip movements were quite impressive. His rhythmic and powerful dance immediately ignited the passion on site, even the staff providing convenience services paused their work to watch.

"Morning I stand on the green pasture..." Amidst almost all the performances by children representing their families, Ma Zelin, a 60-year-old man representing the No. 4 participating family, sang Han Hong's "Heavenly Road," eliciting applause from the audience.

"What bucket can never be filled?" "Rice bucket." "No, it's toilet bowl."

In the intelligence quiz segment, various brain teasers indeed stumped many participating families. The hasty answers and diverse responses made everyone laugh. However, when answering questions related to Dahan Newspaper and the Shangdu Channel of Zhengzhou TV Station, the families who drew these questions immediately gave correct answers, proving that they are indeed our loyal readers and viewers.

The balloon battle not only tests physical strength but also technique. To win the championship, the children put forth all their efforts, trying every method to burst the balloons as quickly as possible: The little girl from the No. 3 participating family jumped up forcefully and then sat down hard; the little boy from the No. 5 participating family sat on the balloon and rubbed it with his buttocks...

At the competition site, there were also many familiar hardcore fans. The little girl wearing a white vest walking the model step from the No. 9 participating family was particularly eye-catching. Isn't she Cheng Xiyuan, the "most active little contestant" from last year's Family Fun Sports Meeting, who is now in second grade at Zhengshang Road Primary School?

Ms. Ding, Cheng Xiyuan's grandmother, laughed and said that after her granddaughter participated in four family fun sports meetings last year and was very happy, she signed up again this time.

[Stories Here Full of Love]

Full family support, the boy playing drums won second place

Ding Yixun, the little boy from the No. 6 participating family showcasing his talents on stage, is 7.5 years old, not tall, but the towering drum set he just arranged formed a huge contrast with him. Despite being young, his skill level was high, performing effortlessly, with the drum beats sometimes urgent, sometimes slow, passionate and unrestrained or gentle and delicate...

Ding Yixun coming to perform on the drum set could be described as "a grand mobilization." Relatives joined in full force, helping move instruments before the mall, someone helping hold the microphone during the performance, and others guiding from below... "We are fully mobilized as a family. The person moving the instruments earlier was the child's uncle, the one encouraging from below was the mother, and I'm holding the microphone," Ding Yixun's father smiled and said that bringing the child to participate in such activities makes the whole family very happy.

The child played the drums well! How did he learn? Facing inquiries, Ding Yixun's mother said that originally, they wanted the child to play the piano, but by chance, the child came into contact with the drum set and liked it. From the age of 5, he started practicing until now. The child's uncle also likes drum sets, so Ms. Chen bought a set for her son. "Initially, I thought he might only be interested for three minutes, but unexpectedly, he still has enthusiasm now." She said that currently, after dinner every day, the child practices voluntarily, even with blisters on his hands, refusing to stop, and has become a little star at school.

"I am also an amateur enthusiast. I usually download some video tutorials on the computer and let him practice along." Ding Yixun's uncle said that in the future, they plan to send Yixun to a professional school to study. "Look! I got six smiley faces, I am the runner-up!" After the event ended, holding a pile of prizes, Ding Yixun made a funny face to the reporter and happily said.

[Scenery Here Worth Watching]

Dihu Community, a lake of clear water enriches residents' lives

In the bright spring sunshine, willows sway gently by the shores of Dihu Lake, ripples glittering on the surface.

Accompanied by the clear waters, men and women of all ages stroll and chat leisurely, while a speedboat patrols the lake... "I really like living here, especially this lake." A resident of Dihu Garden said that this lake enriches everyone's life.

A walk by the lake brings a more genuine sense of comfort!

The vast expanse of the lake shimmers with waves, the sound of the water lapping against the shore heard in the spring breeze. An introduction to Dihu is carved on the stone at the main gate, known to many "old Zhengzhou" residents, Dihu was originally named Jinhai Reservoir, with a clean water area of 160,000 square meters, the source of Jinshui River running through the city center, etc.; not far away, a 500-year-old August Osmanthus tree stands quietly by the lake, adding a touch of dignity and elegance; under the Dyed Heart Pavilion, beside the water scenery platform, people chat and sing operas; looking closely at the water surface, you can see a group of small fish swimming back and forth...

Living in a beautiful environment, quality service and a good cultural atmosphere make residents feel more comfortable. Currently, Dihu Garden, where more than 4,000 households have moved in, is already the largest community in the southwestern part of Zhengzhou, and will be built into a super-large mature livable community with complete educational, medical, commercial, and recreational facilities.

With a large community, detailed services are particularly important. The "one-stop joint service center" at the southern end of Wangfu Street is a distinctive service under the concept of "large property management, small development" of Dihu Garden. The service hall gathers four major service platforms including the development company, property management company, police station, and community, forming a comprehensive service center integrating pre-sales, mid-sales, and after-sales services, providing convenience for residents' lives.

Meanwhile, to facilitate travel for residents, especially middle-aged and elderly residents, Dihu Garden has specially purchased professional vehicles and established free community buses. In addition, Dihu Property has launched the "unit little manager" intimate service, standing by 24 hours a day to promptly solve sudden problems in residents' lives, and there are sprinkler trucks and cleaning vehicles to help improve the community environment.

Now, the water of Dihu has come alive! Water flows noisily under the bridges at both ends of the lake. With the launch of the "Zhengzhou Ecological Water System Planning Project," Dihu connects upstream to Guojiazui Reservoir and downstream to Jinshui River, becoming a true living water system.

Shangdu Channel (ZZTV2) of Zhengzhou TV Station

Zhengzhou TV Station is the most influential TV station in the provincial capital Zhengzhou, recently moved into the new CBD area, with the largest broadcasting room in Central Plains region in terms of area and technology. Shangdu Channel of Zhengzhou TV Station is a channel much loved by everyone. Since its revamp on May 21, 2007, it underwent another complete revamp in 2010, positioning itself as an economic and lifestyle channel, broadcasting daily from 6:00 AM to 1:30 AM the next morning.

Several famous programs of Shangdu Channel of Zhengzhou TV Station are almost household names, such as "Shangdu Report", "Tonight I Am Moved", "Everyday Cuisine", "Beautiful Parents", "Shangdu Real Estate", "Shangdu Home", "Colorful Life", "Happy Anime Bar", "Happy Life Handy Tips", etc., all popular programs.

[Services Here Are Very Thoughtful]

Setting up a scribble corner for children, repairing remote controls for residents

At the event site, the "scribble corner" became a paradise for many children, while free repair services for remote controls and other convenient services made many residents feel very useful.

"I want to paint a little rabbit!" Before the event began, four-year-old Lu Entong, who came to participate in the competition, couldn't resist the attraction of the "scribble corner" and excitedly started painting.

It turned out that the "scribble corner" was specifically prepared by our newspaper for the children attending the activity, giving them more entertainment options. It is reported that in the future, the event site will add mobile library stations and other service facilities to enrich the content of the event.

That afternoon, the Dahan Newspaper 96211 Convenient Service Team also arrived on site to repair remote controls for residents free of charge. Many residents with needs saw the event preview and came specifically. Elder Liu held a "sick" TV remote control and said that after using it for five or six years, the contact was not good, sometimes working and sometimes not. After the repairman checked, he found a lot of dust inside. After cleaning, it worked! "Thank you so much. I originally planned to buy another one, but now I don't need to." The elder smiled and said happily. Besides repairing remote controls for residents free of charge, the repairmen also taught everyone how to maintain home appliances on site, and the residents present said it was "very helpful".
