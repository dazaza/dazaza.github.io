Full Plot Summary:

Guan Yu and Zhang Fei are high school students, but due to their love for standing up for justice, they have been expelled from twenty-four schools, known as the Twenty-Four Schools! Just when the two were feeling desperate after being expelled again, a person suddenly appears. This person introduces himself as Liu Bei, styled Xuande, a fallen noble, who often changes schools because he can't afford the tuition. Liu Bei proposes a plan, stating that since he has noble blood, he can attend the best noble school in the country [Donghan Academy], but can't afford the extremely expensive tuition. Liu Bei says: As long as the money is settled, everything can be solved. He suggests the three of them swear brotherhood, making everyone royal nobility! Then they can all enroll! So the three come to a beautiful and meaningful place to swear brotherhood～But just as the three finish the last sentence: "We don't seek to be born on the same year, month, and day, but we wish to die on the same year, month, and day～" four people—Xiu, Wang Dadaong, Ding Xiaoyu, and Wang Yashuo—travel through a time portal from the Jin timeline to the Yin timeline. Because Dadaong accidentally drops a coin, the coin knocks down a stick, the stick knocks down an empty can, the can knocks down a stone, creating a chain reaction... The falling rock created by Dadaong hits Liu Bei and knocks him unconscious! Guan Yu, committed to promises, wants to follow his elder brother to death on the same year, month, and day. Zhang Fei tries to stop him but fails, so he knocks Guan Yu unconscious first. They then discover that Liu Bei is not dead, only severely injured and unconscious. After discussing with Dadaong, Xiaoyu, and Yashuo, they decide to leave Xiu in the Yin timeline as a substitute (because Xiu's counterpart in the Yin timeline is Liu Bei). Reluctantly, Xiu agrees to maintain the order of time. Meanwhile, the other three take the seriously injured Liu Bei back to the Jin timeline for treatment. When Guan Yu wakes up, he finds out that Xiu, pretending to be Liu Bei, is not dead and becomes very happy. Zhang Fei is also glad that they don't have to die together. Thus, the three finally arrive at the [Donghan Academy]. Guan Yu and Zhang Fei both feel that they can definitely make a name for themselves here!

The three hope to study well and graduate from Donghan Academy, but because Donghan Academy is big and attracts attention, it incurs attacks from the Yellow Turban High School and River East High School (where Dong Zhuo is the principal), putting Donghan Academy in constant crisis. Even a "Eight Doors Golden Lock Array" designed by Master Zuo Ci was set up at the school gate to block students from attending classes. Naturally, the three cannot sit idly by and must break the array. However, breaking the Eight Doors Golden Lock Array is not easy, and even Elder Brother Liu Bei Xiu and Xiao Qiao are trapped inside, facing life-threatening danger. Therefore, Guan Yu and Zhang Fei accept the suggestion of Student Union President Cao Cao, finding an extremely resilient and cheerful youth 'Ma Chao', and Ma Chao's good friend, a marksman who never misses 'Huang Zhong' to help break the array. Plus, there is always the super handsome youth 'Zhao Yun' who doesn't get along with them. Five people work together to break the camp and rescue Liu Bei (Xiu) and Xiao Qiao. In the process of breaking the array, because Guan Yu saves Zhao Yun's life by blocking an arrow with his arm in a critical moment, resolving the grudges between Guan and Zhang against Zhao Yun, they become sworn brothers, share livers and gallbladders, and successfully break the Eight Doors Golden Lock Array.

After breaking the array, the group makes new brothers and originally thought they could smoothly finish high school, but on one hand, the Yellow Turban High School is lurking nearby, wanting to plunder Donghan Academy's resources, and on the other hand, Dong Zhuo, the principal of River East High School, has ambitions for the position of principal of Donghan Academy, which owns the "Alliance Leader". Thus, the Five Tigers and Liu Bei (Xiu) are destined not to live peaceful campus lives, and a battle for supremacy is about to unfold...

Episodes Summary

★Season 1★

【Episode 1】

Since childhood, Xiaozhang Fei and Xiaoguan Yu have been dismissed from kindergarten for protecting female classmates. The two continue to be expelled for similar reasons. One day, they encounter a poor noble - Liu Bei outside a high school entrance blocked by a fake eight gates. Liu Bei, intending to use the two, proposes a peach garden oath, becoming sworn brothers to gain noble status and attend the noble school “Donghan Academy”. During the swearing-in ceremony, when mentioning “same year, same month, same day of death”, Liu Bei is knocked unconscious by a flying boulder. Believing Liu Bei is dead, Guan Yu attempts suicide to honor his commitment, but Zhang Fei knocks him unconscious. The culprit turns out to be Dadaong traveling from the Jin timeline to the Yin timeline.

The group discovers that Xiu looks exactly like Liu Bei, so they switch the two, letting Xiu stay in the Yin timeline as Liu Bei while Dadaong and others take the real Liu Bei back to the Jin timeline for treatment.

Liu, Guan, and Zhang start attending the “Donghan Academy” where the “Alliance Leader” resides. On the first day, Liu Bei Xiu discovers the level of education in the Yin timeline is abysmal, not even knowing the multiplication table. Liu Bei Xiu becomes the smartest person there. Guan Yu sees Diaochan for the first time and falls deeply in love, blushing immediately.

“River East High School’s” Hua Xiong challenges Zhao Yun to a duel outside the school, but Zhao Yun ignores him. Cao Cao recommends Guan Yu and Zhang Fei to accept the challenge. Principal Wang Yun agrees that if they drive away Hua Xiong, Liu, Guan, and Zhang will never be expelled. Guan Yu agrees to fight. At this point, Zhao Yun also agrees to fight upon Diaochan's request, leading to two groups competing to fight. Guan Yu's kick makes Zhao Yun realize Guan Yu's depth of skill, causing mutual respect between the two...

【Episode 2】

Seeing Guan Yu's remarkable skills, Cao Cao invites Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei to live in his mansion to conquer the world together! The three enthusiastically agree...

Cao Cao takes the three to a secret room filled with maps showing the military distribution of various schools. Cao Cao explains that to conquer the world, they must eliminate Gold High School first. However, Donghan Academy's biggest enemy is actually River East High School, whose principal Dong Zhuo only pretends to submit due to the pressure of the “Alliance Leader” being the common ruler, maintaining superficial peace.

Although Guan Yu defeated Hua Xiong, it caused Dong Zhuo more dissatisfaction, sending people to seal off Donghan School's entrance with the hardest and strongest "Eight Gates Golden Lock Array." Cao Cao suggests strengthening the ability to break the array for success. Thus, Guan Yu and others search for Ma Chao and Huang Zhong, convincing them to join with martial arts and sincerity. The five work together to break the array.

【Episode 3】

After successfully breaking the "Eight Gates Golden Lock Array," Principal Wang Yun titles the five as the "Five Tiger Generals," ensuring they will never be expelled or put on probation, bringing comfort to Guan Yu. The group holds a victory banquet at Cao Cao's house, discovering the poison in Guan Yu's arm worsening. Despite frantic efforts to find medical help, no cure exists. Fortunately, upon learning of the secret doctor "Famous Doctor" on Mount Tiangang, the group immediately decides to take Guan Yu there for treatment!

The group boards Zhang Fei's private yacht, heading towards Mount Tiangang. Along the way, they find Guan Yu poisoned and unconscious. The arrow poison is "Seven Days Cheap Bones," causing extreme debasement within seven days before fatal poisoning...

【Episode 4】

Diaochan awakens in "Dingjun Mountain Farm," realizing she was kidnapped by Li Ru and Meiniang, implanted with a "Beautiful Girl Series" chip. Li Ru and Meiniang manipulate Diaochan using the chip, making her freeze, sing backward, enlarge her head, etc., completely controlling her!

Principal Wang Yun receives a message from Gold High School stating that if Wang Yun resigns and allows Gold High School to take over Donghan Academy, they will release Diaochan. Wang Yun initially plans to resign immediately but is dissuaded by the Five Tigers, who promise to rescue Diaochan within the deadline. Anxious Wang Yun announces that whoever rescues Diaochan can become her personal bodyguard and earn great merit, driving all male students crazy to save Diaochan.

Cao Cao is appointed as the "CEO of Rescuing Diaochan," giving lectures to explain the strategy for attacking the mountain. The Five Tigers ascend the mountain top but succumb to the poison "Muggle Muggle You Fool" in the air. Only Huang Zhong, with slower natural breathing, can barely withstand it, holding back to resist enemies. Guan Yu leads the group into the mountain-top hut. However, Diaochan has already been rescued by Lü Bu, who transfers to Donghan Academy and becomes Diaochan's personal bodyguard. Liu Bei Xiu understands that Diaochan and Lü Bu will eventually be together, curious whether Guan Yu can change history.

【Episode 5】

Lü Bu appears, immediately clashing with the Five Tigers. Cao Cao reports his concerns about Lü Bu to Wang Yun, who believes it is merely Cao Cao's paranoia.

Lü Bu and Diaochan are inseparable, angering Zhang Fei, Ma Chao, and Huang Zhong. While Lü Bu and Diaochan eat roasted meat together, Huang Zhong uses "Ice Soul Silver Needles" to sneak-attack Lü Bu but is discovered and exposed by the highly skilled Lü Bu. Diaochan is upset by their behavior.

Liu Bei Xiu tells Zhang Fei that Lü Bu and Diaochan will ultimately be together, believing true love might change history. Zhang Fei decides to humble Lü Bu and help his second brother win back the love of his life.

Zhang, Ma, and Huang challenge Lü Bu on their way to school. Lü Bu provokes them to fight together, resulting in a long struggle without resolution. Zhao Yun arrives late, ready to "blindly attack" Lü Bu, but is stopped by Guan Yu who learns of the situation later. Diaochan and Xiao Qiao also arrive, finding the Three Tigers ganging up unfairly, making them angry.

【Episode 6】

With great ambition, Lü Bu announces his candidacy for student union president against Cao Cao, persuading Diaochan and Xiao Qiao to assist in his campaign, pressuring Cao Cao greatly. With the slogan "Choose Lü Bu as president, and Diaochan will serve," Lü Bu attracts 95% of Donghan Academy's male students, making the election fiercely competitive. Cao Cao aims to secure the presidency, fully supported by the Five Tigers. The group devises the slogan "When Cao Cao speaks, Cao Cao arrives..."

Guan Yu learns from Xiao Qiao that Diaochan plans to cook despite her poor culinary skills. To make her happy, Guan Yu decides to cook for Diaochan, delivered by Zhang Fei in disguise.

After delivering the meal outside Diaochan's house, Zhang Fei and Xiao Qiao excitedly watch everything happening inside. Guan Yu wishes to leave quickly, but Zhang Fei tries every method to stay, witnessing Diaochan and Lü Bu's happiness, leaving Guan Yu utterly heartbroken yet forcing a smile.

The previously defeated Yellow Turbans High School decides to challenge the Five Tigers. Unexpectedly, they choose field hockey for the competition. If the Five Tigers lose, Wang Yun must resign and hand over the Alliance Leader's jade seal to the principal of Gold High School, Zhang Jue. Wang Yun initially refuses to accept the challenge, but Cao Cao analyzes that the Yellow Turbans are growing stronger. If the challenge isn’t accepted, the Yellow Turbans may directly attack. Given the Five Tigers' strength, defeating the Yellow Turbans should not be a problem, leading both to persuade the young Alliance Leader. Can this battle ensure the Five Tigers retain Wang Yun's position as the Alliance Leader?

【Episode 7】

Wang Yun is placed under house arrest by Dong Zhuo, accessible only by Diaochan. Therefore, Hua Tuo disguises as Diaochan to examine Wang Yun. On the way, encountering Lü Bu, Hua Tuo argues to avoid exposure, making Lü Bu believe Diaochan guessed his mission. Observers Cao Cao and Liu Bei Xiu increasingly suspect Lü Bu and Diaochan's relationship has ulterior motives. Examining Wang Yun, Hua Tuo discovers reversed meridians but unknown causes, returning to research at Mount Tiangang Laboratory.

Important members of Donghan Academy are mysteriously attacked; Zhao Yun is knocked unconscious by Meiniang. The Four Tigers rush to rescue, but Liu Bei Xiu and Cao Cao remain at the entrance, finding illusions. Surrounded by overwhelming numbers, can the Five Tigers escape safely?

【Episode 8】

Though rescuing Zhao Yun from Fengming Temple, the group discovers Zhao Yun has lost his martial arts skills. While secretly protecting Zhao Yun's safety and seeking treatment, all methods by Hua Tuo fail. Eventually, Ma Chao finds the reason: Zhao Yun was afflicted with "Lock Maria," equivalent to a terminal illness. Ma Chao's mother further investigates, uncovering Dong Zhuo's conspiracy.

School tyranny intensifies; Dong Zhuo announces replacing the student union president with Lü Bu instead of Cao Cao, declaring Lü Bu his adopted son and rightful president, shocking the entire school. Publicizing Lü Bu's identity changes his relationship with Diaochan.

To save Zhao Yun, Cao Cao sends Jiang Gan to steal the decoder, but his large figure gets stuck in the window, failing. The group then sends the petite Xiao Qiao, leaving her battered. Will she succeed in stealing the decoder? Can Zhao Yun's martial arts skills be restored?

【Episode 9】

Using the decoder, Zhao Yun becomes irritable, unlike his calm self. Xiao Qiao is saddened that the decoder she painstakingly stole was fake. Cao Cao reassures the group, explaining his plan. Cao Cao manipulates Lü Bu, suggesting winning Diaochan's heart by helping steal the real decoder.

Lü Bu uses a decoy tactic, inviting Dong Zhuo to dinner at Hongmen Roast Meat Shop and placing the real decoder in a conspicuous spot for Cao Cao to easily obtain entering the principal's office. However, unaware of the decoding phrase, the group wildly guesses, complicating Zhao Yun's rescue process.

【Episode 10】

Cao Cao points acupressure on Dong Zhuo's heart, but Dong Zhuo does not faint and instead nearly awakens, revealing his heart is on the right side. Failing, Cao Cao escapes hastily, having one shoe pulled off by still confused Dong Zhuo. Subsequently, Meiniang and Li Ru publicly announce a grand search for the shoe's owner like searching for Cinderella, vowing to find the perpetrator. The Five Tigers devise ways for Cao Cao to avoid trying on the shoe and flee the school, but Dong Zhuo still accuses Cao Cao of attempting assassination, sealing his home and issuing a nationwide warrant for Cao Cao's capture.

Fleeing, Cao Cao and Guan Yu seek refuge with Lü Bo She. Unfamiliar with the area and people, the journey is perilous. Facing Dong Zhuo's vast evil forces, can they escape danger safely?

【Episode 11】

Viewing Cao Cao and the Five Tigers as a grave threat, Dong Zhuo attempts to use the Campus Autonomy Law's Heaven's Will clause to execute Cao Cao and Guan Yu. Li Ru and Meiniang devise a treacherous plan, swapping the Heaven's Will sign {Life} with {Death}, ensuring their deaths. Although Sun Shangxiang comes from Jiangdong to rescue, she can only save one. Who among Guan Yu and Cao Cao will be saved? How will the Five Tigers face this Heaven Sacrifice Ceremony of life and death?

Seeing Dong Zhuo sitting on the principal's throne of Donghan Academy, Zhang Bao demands rewards but quarrels with Dong Zhuo, leading Dong Zhuo to attempt killing Zhang Bao with the Soul Transfer technique. Unexpectedly, Zhang Bao's golden retriever leaps to protect its master, scaring Dong Zhuo unconscious. The golden retriever is struck by Dong Zhuo's palm wind and faints. Upon waking, Dong Zhuo and the golden retriever have swapped bodies, initiating a fierce human-dog battle.

【Episode 12】

The captured Dong Xiaozhu (Dong Zhuo's nickname when turned into a dog) unexpectedly has the soul transfer broken by Li Ru rolling down a hill 99 times, disappearing both Dong Zhuo and the dog...
