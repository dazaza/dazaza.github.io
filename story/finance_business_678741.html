Apply soon! \r\n \r\n 1. Introduction to the School \r\n Konkuk University was established in 1946. It currently has over 30,000 students and more than 400 professors. The university features an International Cooperation Center and a Foreign Language Education Institute, which promote globalization. \r\n Konkuk University has two campuses: Seoul and Chungju. Chungju is an inland city located in the center of South Korea, about 100 kilometers from the capital Seoul, covering an area of 984 square kilometers with a population of approximately 220,000. It is the central city and transportation hub of the central inland economic zone in South Korea, where industry and agriculture develop comprehensively. It takes about 2 hours by bus from Chungju to Seoul. Centered around Chungju Lake, surrounded by Jigyeongsan, Namsan, and Wolaksan mountains, Chungju not only preserves historical relics from the Three Kingdoms era but also boasts many natural scenic spots, making it a tourist city for rest and recuperation. Chungju has warm seasons all year round, and even in winter, it does not feel cold. Chungju specialties include apples, peaches, chestnuts, and maepansori stones. \r\n The Chungju campus has more than 8,000 students. It has a faculty of over 400 excellent teachers who cultivate social talents over four years. It has six colleges and unique majors that cannot be found anywhere else in the world, including here at the Chungju campus. There are large-scale dormitories that can accommodate more than 2,400 students living together. There are also well-equipped large-scale gymnasiums, bright outdoor sports fields where students can run freely at night and share team spirit and friendship. \r\n Graduates of the Chungju campus are adaptable anywhere and have good humanity. They are highly praised for possessing rich knowledge and leadership skills with team spirit, compared to graduates from any country in the world. \r\n \r\n 2. Admission Standards and Criteria \r\n 1) Selection based on applicant's grades (Preliminary Exam: Material Review, Final Exam: Interview). \r\n 2) Applicants must process necessary procedures such as obtaining a Republic of Korea entry visa. If the materials submitted by admitted students do not meet the requirements of the entry procedure agency, thus unable to obtain a visa, the applicant bears all responsibilities. \r\n 3) The following situations are not within the recruitment scope: \r\n A. Incomplete required application materials. \r\n B. False information on the application form or enrollment materials. \r\n C. Parents or applicant are illegal overstayers. \r\n D. Do not meet the lecture attendance or economic capacity requirements of this university. \r\n 4) After admission, if it is found through verification of domestic and international school records and residence facts that the application materials are inconsistent with the facts, fraudulent, or obtained through improper means, regardless of whether they have entered the country, their admission qualifications or study permits will be revoked, and relevant departments will be notified. \r\n \r\n 5.
