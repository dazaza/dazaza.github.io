16. Flavorful Pig Skin Jelly

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Spicy and fragrant

Main ingredients: 250g cucumber

Auxiliary ingredients: 15g shrimp

Condiments: 10g soy sauce, 10g vinegar, 5g garlic, 5g ginger, 5g sesame oil, 3g salt

1. Peel and wash the garlic and ginger, mash the garlic into a paste, and mince the ginger.

2. Wash the cucumber and slice it into coin-shaped pieces or smash and chop it into 3cm long segments, mix with salt to bring out the flavor.

3. Soak the dried shrimp in warm water until soft.

4. Mix the dried shrimp with the cucumber, add soy sauce, vinegar, garlic paste, minced ginger, and sesame oil, mix well, serve on a plate.

10. Cold Mixed Black and White Ears

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Fresh and fragrant

Main ingredients: 500g green pepper, 150g cauliflower

Auxiliary ingredients: 5g shrimp

Condiments: 5g garlic, 5g green onion, 3g salt, 2g monosodium glutamate, 10g soy sauce, 10g vinegar, 5g sesame oil

14. Green bean sprouts mixed with assorted mushrooms

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Sweet

Main ingredients: 100g apple, 100g pear, 100g pineapple, 100g orange

Auxiliary ingredients: 50g cherries

Condiments: 20g white sugar

1. Add water to the pot, add sugar and boil, cook until the syrup thickens, let cool.

2. Peel and core the apples and pears, wash.

3. Wash the pineapple and peel, all cut into small pieces.

4. Peel the oranges and break into small segments.

5. Mix the apples, pears, pineapple, oranges with the syrup evenly, garnish with red and green cherries.

7. Bean sprout mixed with egg thread

Main ingredients: Appropriate amount of pig skin

Auxiliary ingredients: Scallion, ginger, Sichuan peppercorns, star anise, salt, garlic, sugar, soy sauce, cooking wine, vinegar

Production process:

1. Blanch the pig skin in water, remove the fat. Then cut into strips.

2. Add appropriate water to the pressure cooker, add scallion, ginger, Sichuan peppercorns, star anise.

3. Heat the oil in the pan, add sugar for coloring the pig skin. Then add a little soy sauce and cooking wine, stir-fry.

4. Put the pig skin in the pressure cooker, add appropriate water. The ratio of pig skin to water should be 1:2 or 1:3.

5. Cook for 15-20 minutes in the pressure cooker. Pour the cooked broth into a container and cool, then refrigerate.

6. After taking it out of the refrigerator, cut into blocks, add minced garlic, vinegar, mix well.

17. Cold mixed assorted vegetables

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Numb and spicy

Main ingredients: 500g lean beef

Auxiliary ingredients: 20g coriander

Condiments: 50g chili oil, 25g Sichuan peppercorn powder, 4g salt, 2g monosodium glutamate, 10g green onion

1. Steam the beef until cooked and slice.

2. Wash the coriander, trim and cut into sections.

3. Slice the green onion for later use.

4. Place the beef slices in a dish, add salt, MSG, chili oil, Sichuan peppercorn powder, green onion strips, and coriander sections, mix well.

9. Cucumber mixed with dried shrimp

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Salty and fresh

Main ingredients: 260g eggs, 150g vermicelli

Auxiliary ingredients: 200g spinach

Condiments: 10g sesame oil, 10g vinegar, 10g dried shrimp, 3g salt, 2g monosodium glutamate, 5g garlic

1. Soak the vermicelli and dried shrimp in water until softened, set aside.

2. Peel the garlic and mince.

3. Wash the spinach, cut into 5cm long segments, blanch in boiling water, remove and rinse with cold water, squeeze out the moisture.

4. Cut the soaked vermicelli into 8cm long segments.

5. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add a little salt and mix well.

6. Heat the wok, add sesame oil until five parts hot, pour in the egg mixture, spread into a thin omelet, flip and fry briefly, remove and cut into strips.

7. Combine the spinach, vermicelli, egg strips, vinegar, salt, monosodium glutamate, minced garlic, and sesame oil in a basin, mix well, arrange on a plate and sprinkle with soaked dried shrimp.

3. Sweet and sour radish threads

Ingredients: Black fungus, silver ear fungus, 1 dried red pepper, 1 clove of garlic

Condiments: Vegetable oil, salt, sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, chicken essence

Method:

1. Clean the black fungus and silver ear fungus separately, soak until soft, tear by hand into small pieces.

2. Boil the black fungus and silver ear fungus in boiling water for 1-2 minutes, remove and rinse with ice water, drain the moisture.

3. Cut the dried red pepper into strips, crush the garlic into fine pieces, quickly sauté in a hot oil pan, turn off the heat.

4. Place the black and white fungus in a bowl, add salt, sugar, chicken essence, a little soy sauce, vinegar, and pour in the sautéed oil, mix well.

5. Can also be prepared in advance and stored in the refrigerator for better taste when chilled.

11. Cold mixed onion threads

Ingredients: Green and red peppers, fungus

Method:

1. Soak the fungus, remove the roots, tear into small pieces, blanch in boiling water for two minutes until cooked, remove and chill, set aside.

2. Cut the green and red peppers into small pieces for later use.

3. Crush three or four cloves of garlic into garlic paste, add fragrant vinegar, excellent freshness, salt, sugar, chicken essence, sesame oil to make a seasoning sauce.

4. Mix the seasoning sauce into the fungus and pepper pieces.

19. Cold mixed spicy chicken threads

Production technique: Mix

Flavor: Fresh and fragrant

Main ingredients: 260g eggs, 400g mung bean sprouts

Auxiliary ingredients:

Condiments: 20g soybean oil, 5g soy sauce, 3g salt, 2g monosodium glutamate, 5g sesame oil

1. Wash the mung bean sprouts, blanch in boiling water, remove and drain.

2. Crack the eggs into a bowl, beat into a batter, pour into a heated oil pan to make an egg skin, cut into thin strips.

3. Place the mung bean sprouts in a basin, add egg skin strips, soy sauce, salt, monosodium glutamate, and sesame oil, mix well.

8. Coriander mixed with beef

1. Wash 1-2 onions, cut into onion threads.

2. Take a basin, fill with clear water, add some salt.

3. Place the cut onion threads in the basin, let sit for about half an hour. (The goal is: soak the onion threads in salt water so they are not too spicy, so you can add more salt appropriately.)

4. After half an hour, take out the onions, place in a dish.

5. Now you can mix, add some vinegar, sugar, appropriate sesame oil, a little salt, mix well before eating.

6. Honey juice fresh fruit

15. Delicious preserved egg tofu

Ingredients: Mushrooms, bean sprouts, spinach, coriander, red pepper threads

Seasonings: Cold-mixed vinegar, excellent freshness, salt, sugar, chicken essence, spicy oil, fragrant oil

Method:

1. Wash the mushrooms, blanch in boiling water until cooked, drain and tear into thin strips.

2. Blanche the bean sprouts and spinach, chill with cold water, cut the spinach into sections.

3. Wash the coriander, cut into pieces; dry red pepper cut into threads.

4. Place the mushrooms, bean sprouts, spinach, coriander, red pepper threads in a large bowl, prepare the seasonings in another small bowl.

5. Mix well, serve on a plate.
