Corporate SMS caters to mobile commerce, with monthly usage in the financial industry alone reaching 500 million messages.

by now on 2009-08-26 12:27:07

The application of corporate SMS in mobile office and marketing is nothing new. It has been deeply integrated with various aspects of business operations, including production, services, and internal office work. With the advent of the 3G era, corporate SMS will keep pace with 3G technology and find even more outstanding applications in mobile commerce, helping businesses improve service quality.

According to relevant data, financial institutions such as banks send as many as 500 million text messages per month. This figure indicates that corporate SMS is widely used in business development. Banks can convey their service items to customers via SMS for a minimal cost, and mobile phone users also consider this an indispensable service method. With SMS notifications from the bank, they can stay updated on their financial information at any time, making them feel cared for and reassured. When users are satisfied, the service quality of the bank naturally improves.

Corporate SMS is not only applied in the financial industry but also extensively used in other public services, life services, e-commerce, and other industries. So why does this ordinary SMS get so frequently applied by enterprises? In this regard, a relevant person in charge of Now.cn, a corporate SMS provider (www.now.cn), introduced: "Corporate SMS is simple and convenient to use, flexible and efficient. It can help businesses achieve mobile office operations and facilitate communication between businesses and customers, achieving effective marketing results. Therefore, corporate SMS has gained widespread application among many enterprises." The author also learned that compared to regular SMS, corporate SMS has the following functions and characteristics:

**Multi-channel options, efficient and flexible**

Corporate SMS can be sent through multiple channels, whether it's immediate sending, scheduled sending, individual sending, or group sending, all can be accurately and timely delivered; its mass SMS function can send information to many users simultaneously, which is both efficient and fast, suitable for enterprise use in notifications, data collection, latest activities, industry information, meeting convening, entertainment, and other communications. To provide users with multi-channel options, the Now.cn SMS platform offers tri-net services (mobile, Unicom, telecom) to meet the comprehensive needs of terminal users. Multiple ports can ensure smooth batch sending without congestion, precisely reaching the target.

**Account inquiry, batch import**

Detailed SMS sending records and consumption recharge records make user consumption clear at a glance. If the sending fails, no fee will be charged. It also has a powerful contact management function, allowing easy batch import of large numbers of customer or employee phone numbers, as well as group management for convenient moving in and out of groups, and sending SMS according to groups. Compared to ordinary mobile phone SMS mass sending, this method is more time-saving and labor-saving.

**Low price, low-cost operation**

It was learned that Now.cn's corporate SMS costs as low as 0.059 yuan per message, and bulk purchasing further reduces costs. Regular SMS generally costs around 0.1 yuan per message, enabling businesses to achieve high operation at a low cost and reduce communication expenses.

SMS has unique advantages that play an important role in the process of enterprise informatization. Under the impetus of 3G services, mobile commerce becomes increasingly important, and corporate SMS will undergo transformation in mobile commerce, becoming an important factor in the multi-faceted development of enterprises.
