At 3:45 AM Beijing Time on December 5, the 15th round of Serie A began, with AC Milan hosting Brescia at San Siro. During the game, Boateng, Robinho, and Ibrahimovic each scored a goal, leading Milan to a 3-0 victory over Brescia, successfully securing all three points at home and maintaining their lead at the top of the standings with 33 points.

In the 3rd minute, Milan took the lead; after receiving a return pass from Ibrahimovic, Ambrosini knocked the ball back, and the advancing Ibrahimovic sent the ball to the front of the goal, where Boateng followed up with a successful shot, giving Milan a 1-0 lead! In the 11th minute, after receiving a pass from Ibrahimovic, Robinho moved to the center and attempted a long-range shot that went high. In the 16th minute, Vaas crossed from the right flank, and Carciolo jumped high in the center to head the ball towards the goal, but Abbiati reacted quickly to dive sideways and block the ball. In the 19th minute, Diamanti's direct free kick hit the wall and went out of bounds, earning Brescia a corner kick opportunity.

In the 20th minute, Diamanti's corner kick reached the front of the goal, and Abbiati came out to punch the ball away. In the 27th minute, Robinho took a small-angle shot from the left side of the penalty area, hitting the side netting. In the 28th minute, taking advantage of a mistake in Brescia's back pass, Robinho pushed the ball into the net, making it 2-0 for Milan! In the 31st minute, Pirlo sent a through ball into the penalty area, and after dribbling past Martinez, Ibrahimovic powerfully shot the ball into the net, making it 3-0 for Milan! Today, Brescia's defense was powerless against Milan's aggressive attack, conceding 3 goals within just 31 minutes of the first half.

Ibrahimovic assisted and scored, directing Milan's great victory, proving himself as the best player in Serie A with 8 goals and 6 assists. Ronaldinho's gesture symbolized position inheritance, and the "Cyclist" locked down a starting spot in Milan with this performance.
