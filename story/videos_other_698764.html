Lazy Dragon Legend [2] Nine Wings Arrive

by yano702cxue on 2012-03-09 11:06:04

Xiaobai carried Jiuyi and flew all the way. Soon, they rushed to the foot of Duanlong Mountain. After crossing it and passing through General Ping, they would reach the destination - Lazhong Longtan (Lazy Dragon Pond). The terrain of Duanlong Mountain was perilous, with venomous snakes and fierce beasts everywhere. Since the privatization of Nanba Kindergarten a year ago, the ambitious principal had already designated Duanlong Mountain as the kindergarten's outdoor special training base, where various activities such as hunting, archery, and fighting were frequently held, completely turning the children into little wild beasts. During every winter and summer vacation, all taxi drivers in Nanba Town below the mountain stayed at home, and all catering and entertainment venues dared not open their doors. They could only use this method to avoid the little demons from Nanba Kindergarten.

Ordinary dogs dare not go up Duanlong Mountain alone, but there are no ordinary dogs in Nanba Kindergarten. Although Xiaobai was fat, black, smelly, and lazy, he was definitely not an ordinary dog. In just half a year since Xiaobai came to Nanba Kindergarten with Jiuyi, he had grown into the fiercest dog in the school. Even the principal’s pair of bulldogs had to give way when they saw him. Xiaobai was loyal to Jiuyi, polite to classmates, and had only one word for enemies: kill!

Xiaobai didn't stop carrying Jiuyi and quickly climbed the mountain. Xiao Chou opened the way in front while broadcasting with his small mouth wide open:

"Xiaobai is here~~~ Quickly hide the food~~~"

"Jiuyi is here~~~ Don't hide the food, run for your life first~~~"

It was like a pot exploded in the forest. Elephant King "One-eyed Dragon" led the herd to escape first, and the forest was filled with dust rolling around. Elephants, bears, wild boars, monkeys, tigers, lions... all kinds of birds and beasts fled away in a flash.

Elephant King "One-eyed Dragon" felt a headache whenever he saw Jiuyi. He lost his eye in a bet with her. Later, he had to revise the first rule of the "Ten Commandments of the Elephant Herd" to remember that painful lesson.

The first rule of the "Ten Commandments of the Elephant Herd" was stipulated as follows:

Prohibition on betting with Yan Jiuyi from Tiny Class Three of Nanba Kindergarten. No matter what, upon seeing Yan Jiuyi, do only one thing: Run!

The forest was abnormally quiet, with only Xiaobai panting heavily with his red tongue sticking out as he ran. Jiuyi pinched her nose, pulled Xiaobai's ear, and began to teach him a lesson:

"How many times have I told you? Eat less meat, exercise more, and take a bath occasionally. Look at how smelly you are, exhausted like a bear after only running 90 kilometers... When we were back home, you carried me for 300 kilometers without panting like this!"

Xiao Chou also shook his head and said:

"From the first day Xiaobai entered Nanshan Kindergarten, I was completely captivated by him! But it's been half a year! I've never seen him take a bath once..."

Jiuyi looked at Xiao Chou curiously and asked her:

"How do you know he hasn't taken a bath?"

Xiao Chou glanced at Xiaobai shyly and said:

"Because I look at him with admiration all the time..."

Suddenly, Xiaobai roared: "Little brat! I have privacy rights too! Go away! Otherwise, I'll pluck all your hair and throw you into the microwave to serve as breakfast for the principal!!!"

Jiuyi patted Xiaobai's neck and said to him:

"Hey, Xiao Chou is deeply infatuated with you. Besides, she's a girl, please watch your language. Look how sad she is, flying back."

Xiaobai was speechless. His young master cursed all day, yet still found time to educate him about civilized language. It was truly thoughtful and very touching for Xiaobai.

Deeply moved, Xiaobai moved his claws like the wind and quickly crossed Duanlong Mountain, reaching General Ping at the foot of the mountain. Xiao Chou caught up from behind and reminded them whether they should rest. The other children and the pack of dogs were just rushing to the top of Duanlong Mountain at this moment.

Jiuyi signaled Xiaobai to stop and took out a water bottle to let him drink. Xiaobai drank water with a bang, which was quite intimidating. After drinking, he lay down on the spot and closed his eyes for a short rest. Xiao Chou came over and flapped her wings enthusiastically to fan him.
