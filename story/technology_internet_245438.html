How to select wooden doors

by longersing on 2009-10-07 21:48:50

Since September, the home decoration market has gradually entered the peak season for purchasing and sales. As the major part of decoration expenses, building materials naturally become the focus of new home owners. Due to competitive pressure, building material merchants spare no efforts to compete for market share, and various promotional tactics such as discount, buy-gift, increasing service items, lottery etc. are emerging one after another in the bustling building material market.

However, some merchants play the low-price card, and some product prices are so low that they are unbelievable. Is it really cheap or just a gimmick? Or is it a sleight of hand? The sales price of products is determined by market competition status and cost. No merchant will do a losing business, and low price often indicates the shrinkage of product quality and services. The editor specially consulted senior professionals from well-known integrated home companies abroad, and simply discussed the topic of wardrobe/sliding door selection.

Firstly, look at the price. Price is always what people care most about. Currently, there are big differences in the selling prices of sliding doors and wardrobes in the market. For big brands such as KD and Sofea and other first-line domestic brands, the retail price of sliding door products is generally between 400 yuan/? and 1000 yuan/? or so, and the retail price of the main board materials used in wardrobes and custom furniture is around 200-220 yuan. Even during manufacturer promotions, the discount usually does not exceed 20%, and is generally around 15%. The price of non-brand products is much lower, with sliding doors priced at around 130 yuan/? to 200 yuan/?, and wardrobes priced around 130 yuan/?. These products have no guarantee in terms of product quality, environmental friendliness, and services. Sliding doors and wardrobes are categories with high usage frequency in daily home life. During the selection process, sliding door prices around 300 yuan/? is a line, and wardrobe main board prices at 160 yuan/? is another line. Below these two lines, I suggest being cautious when purchasing (except for special-priced items).

Secondly, look at the materials. In the market, sliding door profiles are mostly made of silicon, titanium, and magnesium alloys. When selecting, first look at the wall thickness of the profiles. The thickness of branded sliding doors can all reach 1.2mm, for example, KD's high-quality profile wall thickness can reach 1.8mm, greatly ensuring the sturdiness and safety of the sliding doors during use. Non-branded sliding door profiles have a wall thickness of around 1.0mm, and even as low as 0.5mm. The safety of such sliding doors can be imagined; secondly, look at the cross-section of the profiles. High-quality profiles have flat and uniform cross-sections, while inferior profiles, due to reduced alloy content, have insufficient hardness, easily deform, and have rough and uneven cross-sections. When selecting sliding doors, the wheels are one of the key components. High-quality sliding door wheels have wheel bodies made of hard glass fiber, with high strength and hardness, smooth cooperation with the wheel core, and silent operation when pushed and pulled. Brands like KD can ensure smooth pushing and pulling for 100,000 times; while non-branded sliding door wheels do not fit tightly with the upper and lower tracks, have loose movements, insufficient wheel hardness and strength, easily damage and deform, and produce noise when pushed and pulled. Then, you need to look at the door panel materials. Branded door panels have rich and realistic colors, strong texture, no voids or edge burrs, etc., and the materials for glass and density boards are all supplied by well-known domestic suppliers, such as KD's glass supplier "Xiangshi" and density door panel supplier "Green". For wardrobes, bookshelves, and other customized home products, the main board material is the most critical. Its environmental performance directly affects home health. Major brands in the integrated home field generally have strict material selection standards, and their boards are procured from large manufacturers such as Jilin Sen Gong Lu Shui He and Daya, ensuring reliable quality, environmental friendliness, and structural strength; hardware and auxiliary material accessories also cooperate with well-known domestic and international suppliers.

Thirdly, look at environmental protection. Brand overall home enterprises have strict internal control standards, and their enterprise standards are generally higher than national standards, including environmental protection levels. At present, the formaldehyde emission level of furniture products is E1 grade, i.e., formaldehyde emission ≤1.5mg/L, which is basically the safe environmental protection level for living. A small number of excellent enterprises have already reached and exceeded the EO grade environmental protection standard. EO grade is the highest and safest formaldehyde emission level, such as Qu Mei Home, KD Home, Yi Feng Furniture and other leading enterprises in the national home industry whose product environmental protection grades have entered the EO grade era. Now, many brands/merchants on the market claim to use "Jilin Sen Gong Lu Shui He" boards (Lu Shui He boards are considered the highest representative of "high-quality environmental protection" in the industry), but in fact, many enterprises cannot provide evidence proving that they use Lu Shui He boards. Actually, identifying the "true Li Kui" is quite simple. Netizens just need to log on to the official website of Jilin Sen Gong Lu Shui He. The company has already published a list of partners on its website. If the XX brand is listed in the "partners" list, then it is Li Kui; if not, then it is Li Gui.

Fourthly, look at the craftsmanship. Excellent home brands are meticulous in the product processing technology phase, with strict internal audit standards, and will never sacrifice product quality to compress costs. For example, KD Company ensures product quality by requiring very high processing accuracy, which must be precise to 0.1 millimeters. Products are strictly produced according to procedures, and processes and technologies will never be simplified to shorten the construction period. KD's product delivery cycle is generally 3~7 days longer than that of peers. KD's production capacity and production management are actually among the best in the same industry, but in order to ensure quality, KD adheres to its own technological standards.

Fifthly, look at design and service. Excellent design gives products a soul. The matching and design of sliding doors and wardrobes are not as "simple" as rumored in the market. If home design and matching ignore the reasonable combination of these two types of products, it is difficult to achieve an overall style effect. The product design of leading brands takes into account many factors such as fashionability, practicality, and economy, focusing on the overall style and reflecting the humanization of homes. Service is also a topic that everyone is very concerned about. Three parts product, seven parts installation, the quality of installation directly affects the practical effect and even the service life of the product. Customized home products especially require the rigor of dimensions. Installation errors, brand companies have strict execution and acceptance standards, while non-brand companies often neglect the installation phase. In addition, the completeness of the after-sales service system is also a reference point; additionally, some personalized services, such as KD Home providing free disassembly and assembly services for all home products, meaning that after the home products are installed, if the customer requests to move the position (including moving), KD Company will provide free disassembly and reinstallation for the customer.
