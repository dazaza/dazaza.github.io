1. What is the holiday function?

Answer: The holiday function allows the refrigerator's chilling compartment to be independently turned off, so when you are traveling, only the lower freezing chamber can be used to preserve food. This saves electricity and is practical. In many brands of refrigerators, the chilling and variable temperature zones can be freely turned off.

2. Dual Dynamic Cooling System

Answer: The freezing zone automatically delivers cold air quickly to every level of the chilling and 0-degree preservation chambers, making the temperature more uniform and improving cooling efficiency. It reduces power consumption, thereby compensating for any discrepancies in performance. Relatively speaking, the chilling and variable temperature zones can reduce their workload.

3. What is the silicon filter membrane humidification system?

Answer: Primarily applied in the variable temperature zone, generally the upper layer of the 0-degree preservation chamber is a low-humidity area suitable for fresh fish and raw meat, preserving freshness. The lower layer is a high-humidity area ideal for fruits and vegetables, equipped with a silicon filter membrane humidifying partition that breathes but does not lose water, reaching 90% humidity, ensuring fruit and vegetable moisture and luster. The silicon filter membrane separates the low-humidity and high-humidity areas within the variable temperature zone, enhancing the preservation effect.

4. How to introduce the overall bending effect and overall foaming of the refrigerator to customers.

Answer: The advantages of overall bending include greater strength and rigidity from a single molding process, less deformation, strong sealing performance, and better preservation effects. Overall foaming uses German Hennecke foaming machines for fully enclosed integral foaming, providing excellent insulation and higher strength, making it less prone to deformation, energy-saving, quiet, and maintaining a constant temperature.

5. What is the function of the soft freezing zone?

Answer: We refer to 0°C-7°C as the soft freezing zone. Due to different foods requiring different preservation temperatures, the soft freezing zone eliminates the need for thawing, offering convenience to customers and avoiding nutrient loss during thawing of meats. Suitable for meats and liquid foods intended for consumption within a week. No defrosting required, cutting instantly without losing nutrients.

6. What is linear inverter technology?

Answer: Imported second-generation inverter compressors from Korea will detect internal storage conditions and adjust piston movement distance and speed using a straight-line operation method. Energy-saving, intelligent temperature control, ensuring the storage room remains constant. Keeps food fresh, with low energy consumption achieving excellent cooling, enjoying both freshness and energy savings.

7. What is the "newborn dual circulation cooling system"?

Answer: The newborn dual circulation cooling system is an upgrade of existing technologies. The chilling and freezing compartments each have their own independent evaporator and cooling fan, controlled by a computer for separate cooling. Unlike other dual circulation cooling modes, this system allows both compartments to cool simultaneously, preventing flavor changes in meats due to alternating cooling.

8. How many ways does the refrigerator have for automatic ice-making?

Answer: There are several forms of automatic ice-making currently available on the market: 1) Traditional ice-making using ice trays; 2) Door-mounted water addition for ice-making; 3) Pipe-connected filtered ice-making by connecting a water pipe at the back of the refrigerator (generally used in bar-style refrigerators).

9. Benefits of the smart variable temperature room?

Answer: The smart variable temperature room achieves free temperature variation in three stages (-3°C, -1°C, 4°C) and closed storage, keeping food at optimal temperature states, ensuring better insulation and preservation effects, ensuring no nutrient loss, and lasting freshness.

10. What are the advantages of the door-mounted ice-making system? (Check the structure first when learning.)

Answer: The door-mounted ice-making system has four advantages: 1) Increased extra space for storing more food; 2) Easy-to-move ice tray for convenient cleaning; 3) Adjustable ice cube size at the touch of a button; 4) Transparent ice tray for clear visibility of ice cubes.

11. What is the 360-degree stereoscopic circulation system, and what advantages does it have in cooling?

Answer: The 360-degree stereoscopic circulation system is an exclusive side-end cooling output design for opening refrigerators, achieving ultra-fast and ultra-uniform cooling in the chilling compartment, creating the latest stereoscopic cooling system. Cooling outlets are present on the sides of the chilling compartment, achieving 360-degree stereoscopic circulation. Advantages include: 1) Uniform cooling; 2) Front and rear simultaneous airflow systems; 3) Cooling speed; 4) Strong odor removal.

Chapter Two: Washing Machine Product Knowledge

12. Principle of washing in agitator washing machines:

Answer: When the agitator rotates in both directions under the drive of the motor, the washing liquid inside the drum experiences horizontal and vertical forces. Due to friction between the washing liquid and clothes and between the drum wall and clothes, the direction and magnitude of these forces change continuously, generating two vortices horizontally and vertically. Near the agitator, the vortex is stronger, while around the bucket walls, the vortex is gentler. Their combined effect creates strong turbulence of clothes inside the drum, resulting in friction and impact between clothes themselves and between clothes and the drum wall. This repeated mechanical motion simulates hand-washing effects like rubbing and beating, achieving cleaning purposes.

13. Types of washing machines based on washing types and their characteristics?

Answer: Agitator-type, front-loading drum-type, and top-loading stirrer-type. Most available models are either agitator-type or front-loading drum-type.

Agitator-type washing machine: Lower wash quality compared to others, shorter washing time, but higher wear and tear, and higher water consumption.

Front-loading drum-type washing machine: Washes clothes without wear and tangle, more water-efficient, and provides higher wash quality under the same conditions as agitator-type washing machines.

Stirrer-type washing machine: Good overall performance, but larger in size, complex structure, difficult manufacturing technology, and higher cost.

14. Types of washing machines currently available on the market?

Answer: Twin-tub semi-automatic agitator washing machines, fully automatic agitator washing machines, fully automatic front-loading drum washing machines.

15. What do X, P, B, G, Q, J in washing machine model numbers represent?

Answer: X: Washing machine, P: Twin-tub, B: Agitator, G: Drum, Q: Fully automatic, J: Stirrer.

16. Drainage methods for agitator and front-loading drum washing machines?

Answer: Agitator washing machines generally have bilateral bottom drainage, with a drainage height not exceeding 20 cm; front-loading drum washing machines usually have top drainage, with a drainage height not less than 80 cm.

17. Types of washing machines based on structural form?

Answer: Single-tub, double-tub, multi-tub.

18. What is the "three-in-one" in washing machines?

Answer: Combines washing, spinning, and drying functions.

19. Process of washing clothes?

Answer: Soaking, washing, rinsing, dehydration. Generally, only the last three steps are used for washing clothes.

20. Main construction of agitator washing machines?

Answer: Inlet pipe, outlet pipe, inner tub, outer tub and casing, computer control panel and motor, inlet valve, outlet valve, etc.

21. Types of washing machine casings and their features?

Answer: Generally divided into galvanized steel plate casings and plastic casings.

Galvanized steel plates have anti-vibration and corrosion-resistant features; plastic casings are corrosion-resistant, do not rust, and can be placed in damp places.

22. Are there top-drain agitator washing machines?

Answer: Yes, but only a few models are available on the market and they are relatively expensive.

23. What is the noise standard for washing machines?

Answer: Noise during washing should not exceed 62 decibels, and during dehydration, it should not exceed 72 decibels.

24. Principle of washing in front-loading drum washing machines?

Answer: Clothes and water are lifted up and dropped down through clockwise and counterclockwise rotations of the inner drum, heated for washing. Detergents fully penetrate clothes through squeezing and patting movements and slight friction from the perforated inner drum wall, effectively cleaning clothes.

25. Three conditions necessary for washing clothes?

Answer: External force, activation of detergent, and the role of water.

26. Three key considerations when purchasing a front-loading drum washing machine?

Answer: Capacity, dehydration speed, motor.

27. Structure of fully automatic front-loading drum washing machines?

Answer: Includes a fully automatic washing machine computer board, water inlet solenoid valve, water level sensor, traction motor, safety switches, and other control components.

28. What is the highest spin speed of front-loading drum washing machines currently available on the market?

Answer: 1600 rpm. For those without drying, about 800 rpm is common. For those with drying, it ranges from 1000-1200 rpm.

29. How much can the drain hose of a front-loading drum washing machine be extended?

Answer: Both inlet and outlet hoses can be extended, up to 2.5 meters.

30. How to determine the appropriate laundry load for a front-loading drum washing machine?

Answer: Taking a 5 kg front-loading drum washing machine as an example:

Mildly soiled cotton clothing------Maximum 4.5 to 5.0 kilograms;

Very dirty clothing------Maximum 4.0 kilograms;

Easy-to-handle materials------Maximum 2 to 2.5 kilograms;

Delicate fabrics------Maximum 1.5 kilograms;

Wool clothing------Maximum 1.0 kilogram.

31. How to ensure the cleaning efficiency of a front-loading drum washing machine?

Answer: To ensure the cleaning efficiency of a front-loading drum washing machine, two points are crucial: the amount and quality of detergent used: it is advisable to use a small amount of highly effective low-foam detergent powder. This is because if there are too many bubbles during washing, it will affect the free fall of the laundry in the drum, reducing the impact force and mechanical force, thus affecting the cleaning effect; Heating washing: the action of the detergent depends on the activity of the enzymes, scientific experiments have proven that enzymes are most active at around 40 degrees Celsius, therefore heating washing can greatly improve the cleaning efficiency of clothes.

32. Why does a front-loading drum washing machine require professional installation?

Answer: Because the rear of the front-loading drum washing machine has fixed bolts that need to be removed and the high-speed rotation requires professional personnel to adjust the balance, professional personnel are needed for debugging and installation.

33. How to reduce the washing time of a front-loading drum washing machine?

Answer: (1) Select different washing programs based on the material of the clothes: cotton, synthetic fiber, wool cashmere programs.

(2) Choose different starting washing programs based on the dirtiness of the clothes. For clothes that are not very dirty, select quick washing, which can save water, electricity, and time.

34. Does a front-loading drum washing machine consume a lot of electricity?

Answer: The washing power of a front-loading drum washing machine is generally around 200 watts, and the dehydration speed is proportional to the rotational speed. If the water temperature is increased to 60 degrees Celsius, it generally takes over 100 minutes to wash a batch of clothes, consuming about 1.5 kWh of electricity. If drying is included, the time depends on the fabric type, with a minimum of 40 minutes.

35. Why does the washing machine shake during washing?

Answer: This is normal. Shaking during washing often occurs due to insufficient water levels or the washing machine not being placed evenly or on a weak floor. Increasing the water level, placing the washing machine evenly, or moving it to a sufficiently strong surface will resolve the issue.

36. What is the power-off memory function?

Answer: In the event of a sudden power outage, the washing machine will automatically remember the completed washing program. When power is restored, there is no need to reset the program, and the washing machine can continue to complete the entire washing process.

37. Why does the washing machine not start even though it already has half a bucket of water?

Answer: Fully automatic washing machines must reach the set water level before washing clothes.

38. What is the general temperature for washing water?

Answer: Generally around 40 degrees Celsius, with a maximum not exceeding 60 degrees Celsius (except for high-temperature disinfection front-loading drum washing machines), to avoid damaging the washing machine drum or causing plastic aging and deformation.

Answer: Refers to setting the washing time in advance to the designated time (within 24 hours), after which the washing machine will automatically start and complete the washing process.

40. What is "air washing"?

Answer: Air passes through the heating chamber to form a powerful hot air flow that penetrates clothing fibers, breaking down odor particles, effectively killing bacteria deep in the fabric. The high-temperature air flow carrying odor particles and bacterial residues passes through the condenser, condenses into water, and is discharged outside the machine. This function is currently available only in some front-loading drum washing machines.

41. What is the water retention rinse function of washing machines?

Answer: Refers to stopping water intake once the water level reaches a certain height and performing agitation washing.

42. What is the mini water level?

Answer: Designed for washing small amounts of summer clothing, the mini water level works with corresponding washing programs to significantly save time and energy while cleaning clothes.

43. What is three-dimensional water flow?

Answer: During washing, water flows from three directions toward the center, forming a three-dimensional vortex that disperses clothing, combining with an innovatively designed anti-tangling wave disc to effectively reduce tangling and accelerate dissolution and penetration of detergents.

44. What is dual power?

Answer: Driven by both the agitator and the inner drum, rotating in both directions, producing strong omnidirectional boiling water flow that penetrates clothing fibers, thoroughly removing stains, strongly cleaning clothes, while minimizing wear and preventing tangling.

45. What is the "spray waterfall" in washing machines?

Answer: Through circulating three-dimensional water flow, like a waterfall, impacting clothes, increasing tumbling, reducing tangling, improving cleaning efficiency, even untangling knotted towels.

46. What is "frequency conversion" washing?

Answer: Using frequency conversion technology and intelligent program control, achieving soft start and soft stop, reducing noise by about 10DB.

The computer control system automatically perceives the balance of clothes to determine the best spin speed for the motor, fundamentally solving the problem of vibration and noise, requiring high precision in speed adjustment motors.

47. What is fuzzy logic water level control?

Answer: The fuzzy logic water level control system can quickly and intelligently sense the weight of clothes and automatically set a reasonable water level, improving cleaning efficiency while saving water.

48. Why does the washing machine switch to rinsing during the dehydration process?

Answer: This happens because clothes are unbalanced during dehydration, switching to rinsing and automatically adjusting the imbalance, then resuming dehydration.

49. What is self-check for imbalance?

Answer: Automatically adjusts load balance, ensuring smooth operation of the washing machine during high-speed dehydration, saving energy and reducing noise, not disturbing rest.

50. What is the difference in energy efficiency between agitator washing machines and front-loading drum washing machines?

Answer: Compared to agitator washing machines, front-loading drum washing machines use about 40-50% of the water. In terms of electricity usage, agitator machines are 50-80% more energy-efficient than front-loading drum machines.

51. How should washing machines be maintained if they are not used for a long time?

Answer: Drain any standing water, keep the inner drum dry and clean with no residual water, and place the washing machine in a dry, non-corrosive gas-free, sun-shielded, acid-free, and alkali-free environment to prevent rusting of metal parts and degradation of electrical components' insulation performance.

55. What are the benefits of the direct spray rapid cleaning system?
