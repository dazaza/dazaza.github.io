Man drives black car with inspector hanging on for nearly 2000 meters then escapes (picture)

by yqwdfkmt on 2010-05-29 14:40:10

After the outline, Yang You Tan fled through hard labor, and the police took a step further to investigate the matter. Driver's ID: According to the staff of the taxi management station at Mudanjiang City Transport Management Office, around 9 PM that night, inspectors found that the black Toyota car with license plate Hei C28H98 had no passengers, and the driver did not have valid operating credentials or professional qualification certificates, making it an illegal operation vehicle. The inspectors immediately impounded the car to the parking lot.

Northeast Network news on May 4th at night: On the night of April 30th, Lang Xiaojun, an inspector from the Taxi Management Station of the Mudanjiang City Transport Management Office, was dragged for over 2000 meters by an unlicensed "black car" while enforcing the law. "If it weren't for Lang Xiaojun's rich experience and having undergone special training, he might have lost his life," said the leader of the Mudanjiang Transport Management Office.

When making records, the driver presented his motor vehicle driver's license, which showed his real name as Yang You Tan. Earlier, Yang You Tan claimed he needed to get a phone call from the car. However, once inside the car, he quickly locked the doors and started the engine to escape. At that moment, Inspector Lang Xiaojun, who was sitting near the entrance of the parking lot behind the car, signaled him to stop. But instead of stopping, Yang You Tan accelerated directly toward Lang Xiaojun. Not having time to dodge, Lang Xiaojun managed to jump onto the hood of the car, grabbing the windshield wipers with both hands.

For nearly half an hour and traveling almost 2000 meters, Lang Xiaojun suffered injuries to his neck, hands, waist, knees, and other areas, and was sent to the hospital for treatment. According to Dr. Lu Cong from the orthopedic department of the emergency room, Lang Xiaojun sustained multiple soft tissue contusions, suspected left lumbar vertebra fracture, and required hospitalization for further examination and treatment.

Upon seeing this, the on-site inspectors Jiang Tao and Chi Rifeng quickly hailed a taxi to chase the fleeing Toyota car. During this time, the speeding Toyota car attempted several times to throw off Lang Xiaojun but failed. The two inspectors in the taxi also tried to block the Toyota car. Eventually, the Toyota car collided with the chasing taxi. The strong impact ejected Lang Xiaojun from under the car, causing him to heavily fall by the roadside. Subsequently, the Toyota car sped away.
