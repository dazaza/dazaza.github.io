After struggling for 7 days, the condition of 2-year-old Yueyue suddenly deteriorated yesterday. Guangzhou General Hospital of Guangzhou Military Region stated that they would spare no manpower and resources to treat Yueyue, but they didn't reveal more about her condition. Yesterday afternoon, Yueyue's family said that after the doctor's rescue, Yueyue's injury has become stable, and the family didn't receive any critical notice. While Aunt Chen couldn't bear the repeated media siege and various condolences, indicating that she felt a lot of pressure and wanted to return to a peaceful life. Yueyue's condition kept deteriorating

Yesterday, the hospital held a routine briefing at the ICU entrance, stating that Yueyue's condition had been deteriorating since October 18th, and the doctors made every effort to save her. However, the press conference did not have a question-and-answer session. Director Su Lei of the ICU said: all medical resources have been mobilized, and the final treatment result will be announced on the 20th. In addition, the hospital stated that they would do their utmost, try every method, and spare no expense to rescue her. The family didn't receive any critical notice.

Upon hearing that Yueyue's condition worsened, Ms. Qu, Yueyue's mother, burst into tears of grief, and during the interview, Ms. Qu kept blaming herself for not taking good care of the child.

According to Director Wang Weimin of the Neurosurgery Department, Yueyue showed signs of deterioration in heart and lung function. Informed medical staff also revealed that Yueyue was rescued twice since the 18th, and the patient's condition was still very serious. She continued to undergo EEG tests and brainstem evoked potentials. At 4 PM yesterday, Yueyue's grandfather could no longer contain his anxiety and came to the ICU visiting channel to see his granddaughter. The doctor did not disclose the child's condition to the family, but he heard that Yueyue was urgently rescued at 9 AM, and when he saw his granddaughter, he found that her bed hadn't moved, so it should be temporarily fine. Moreover, Yueyue's grandfather also disclosed that as of 4:30 PM yesterday, the family had not received any critical notice. Acting Mayor of Foshan

The incident indeed tarnished Foshan's reputation

At the quarterly work analysis meeting held yesterday in Foshan City, Liu Yuelun, Deputy Secretary of the Foshan Municipal Committee and Acting Mayor, mentioned the Yueyue incident while talking about urban management, and believed that the incident indeed tarnished Foshan's efforts to create a national civilized city, but the 18 passersby could not represent the overall level of civility of Foshan people.

Foshan people are not so bad! When Liu Yuelun mentioned at the meeting that Foshan passed the provincial inspection and obtained the nomination qualification for a national civilized city, he suddenly shifted the topic, mentioning that now the online public opinion focus links the Yueyue incident with Foshan, which is very troubling. This is an accidental event, (the 18 passersby) of course reflects the quality issue and lack of love of citizens, and the low moral level, but it cannot represent the overall level of civility and ideological realm of Foshan people, and the incident has a great negative impact on Foshan's creation of a civilized city. Liu Yuelun also said that after the incident, many people sent him text messages, saying that no matter how well Foshan's economy develops, if its image is not good, it is useless.

Liu Yuelun stated that the Foshan municipal party and government departments need to seriously study again and improve the civility and moral level of citizens.

Aunt Chen

Unable to bear the disturbance, hoping to restore peace

It was learned that Aunt Chen took her grandson and got into her son Tang Xiaobing's car and left the hardware city residence at dusk on the 18th. The scene media all thought that Aunt Chen's family could not withstand the harassment of the media and condolence delegations for several days and returned to their hometown Yangshan. After the outside world learned this, they sent blessings one after another and expressed understanding of her actions.

Aunt Chen was not as busy as before yesterday, and her spirit improved a bit, except for occasional incoming calls. Because she saw the townspeople, Aunt Chen spoke her native language and chatted particularly warmly with the townsfolk.

Tang Xiaobing told reporters that after the Yueyue incident, his mother had not eaten or slept well, facing the media and condolence delegations every day. She recounted the horrific circumstances of the incident countless times and had to endure criticism, the pressure was unimaginable.

Not only that, the landlord also had opinions on their family. They couldn't receive visitors at home and had to go downstairs or to the shop where they worked, affecting the business of the shop.

Doing good deeds, I think it's hard. Aunt Chen repeatedly said that she just did what anyone would do, her biggest wish is for Yueyue to recover soon, and she also hopes that life can return to normal.

Don't interview her anymore, she's really tired. Tang Xiaobing hasn't been able to work these days and has been by his mother's side, hoping that everything will end soon, Tang Xiaobing said.

Boundless Love

Uncle Ou visits Yueyue and brings chicken soup

I came over when I heard on Weibo that Yueyue's condition had worsened. Yueyue's condition touched the hearts of countless Guangzhou residents, including Uncle Ou. At noon yesterday, originally planning to visit his elderly mother, Uncle Ou appeared at the hospital to visit Yueyue.

I cried three times while watching the Yueyue incident five times. Those 18 passersby were too cold-hearted, and I've been feeling very upset inside. Uncle Ou couldn't hide his excitement and handed over the chicken soup he originally intended for his mother to Yueyue's mom.

After seeing Yueyue, my mind became much calmer. Ms. Feng from Liuzhou, Guangxi, said that she came overnight, hoping to do something for Yueyue.

Ms. Feng said that she learned about Yueyue's plight through the media and had always wanted to come see her. Her husband and neighbors strongly supported her, and she even brought 5,000 yuan as a condolence payment.

Yesterday, a man who wished to remain anonymous rushed from Panyu to the hospital. He prepared a red envelope for Yueyue's parents, containing three 100-yuan bills, each with the tail number 333. In Cantonese, '3' symbolizes 'life', implying that Yueyue recovers quickly.

Reward

Aunt Chen is the pride of her hometown

Aunt Chen is undoubtedly the pride of the working people of Yangshan County! Early yesterday morning, leaders from Aunt Chen's hometown of Yangshan County visited her and presented her with a condolence payment of 17,800 yuan.

Zhu Yanqun, member of the Standing Committee of Yangshan County, told Xin Kuai Bao reporter that Chen Xianmei's actions caused a sensation in her hometown, and Aunt Chen is not only the pride of Yangshan County but also the pride of Qingyuan.

The whole village was buzzing, and villagers kept talking about this when they saw me; of course, I support her. Mr. Tang Yifu, Aunt Chen's husband, said that more than ten years ago, when he was still working outside, Aunt Chen single-handedly supported the entire family, not only farming several acres of land but also taking care of two children. She has endured a lot of hardship at her age, but such (rescue) incidents are normal for us rural people.
