Second, Preparation Steps for Opening an Online Store

After choosing the product to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, corresponding identity and payment method verification is also required.

The reason I chose to open a store on Taobao is because it is completely free. To open a store on Taobao, three conditions must be met: First, register as a member and pass certification; Second, list at least 10 items (including 10); Third, for convenient and secure transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., then click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Afterward, a confirmation email will be sent to the registered email address. Open the link inside, confirm, and the user registration is completed.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction safety, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is suggested to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., corresponding verifications must be conducted (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification). Specific operational steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to the text "If you want to sell items, please complete Alipay verification first."

Step 2: In the opened page, there will be a prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated yet. Click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., then click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step 3: After successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page and press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real-name Verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read" button to continue.

Step 4: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts and click "Next"; then select ID document verification. You can choose "Upload Online" or "Mail" copies of your ID documents, then click "Next"; next, input bank card information including bank, bank card number, province, city, etc., after completion, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Reminder: If uploading copies of your ID documents online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC card ID, you also need to provide the backside picture.

Step 5: One day later, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Transfer Amount," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and finally click the "Confirm" button.

Fourth, Shop Decoration is Very Important After Free Store Opening

After opening a free store, buyers can obtain their own space. Just like traditional shops, to operate normally and attract customers, it is necessary to decorate the store appropriately. This mainly includes designing the store logo, categorizing items, recommending items, and choosing the store style.

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao - I am a Seller - Manage My Store." Under "Store Management" on the left, click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; main projects need to be manually entered; under the "Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to select the pre-designed store logo image; under the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Clicking the "Preview" button allows you to check the effect.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, selecting a light-colored and comfortable style is fine. I chose the "Green Fairyland" style template, and the preview image appears on the right side. Click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

Friendly Reminder: How to Become a High-Credibility Star Seller on Taobao?

To become a star seller on Taobao, your credibility score must be at least four points. The basis for the seller's credibility score is the evaluation from buyers after each successful transaction via Alipay. "Positive Reviews" add one point, "Neutral Reviews" add no points, and "Negative Reviews" deduct one point. Therefore, to become a star seller, remember to provide honest services. If situations like online transactions without buying or receiving goods without payment occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - I Want to Report" to complain and report, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer's signature receipt, and Wangwang screenshots must be collected.
