Ten Essential Tips for Buying a Laptop

by mylovehisa on 2007-11-13 12:34:20

Recently, I've seen a few netizens who, due to unfamiliarity with market prices, ended up purchasing laptops at prices significantly higher than the retail value. They expressed their regret on forums, and I deeply sympathize with them. However, what I see more of are the successful stories of our fellow laptop enthusiasts outsmarting the merchants when buying laptops. It suddenly occurred to me, does buying a laptop have to be this complicated? Do we need to have an in-depth understanding before making a move? It's not that we aren't clever, but rather that the merchants are too cunning. To help everyone buy laptops more easily, I've written this guide for your reference.

1. Graceful Steps

Before purchasing a laptop, the most effective and time-saving method is to check the ZOL Laptop Section and the Laptop Enthusiast Forum. Search for the laptop you want to buy and look at other netizens' purchasing and usage experiences. Generally speaking, for laptops that aren't very obscure, there will be users on the forum. For popular laptops, discussions can be seen almost every day. You can refer to the purchase prices of other netizens; media and website quotes are often higher than the final market price. As the experts say, "Come and take a look at IT168."
