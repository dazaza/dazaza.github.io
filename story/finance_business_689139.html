To be a QQ member requires 10 yuan per month, it's too expensive! At first, there was such a cheap deal, one Q Coin for QQ membership!! But your QQ account must have only 1 QB left; more or less, Tencent will not process it! First, go to http://pay.qq.com/index.shtml and log in with your QQ number, then go to the account spending page! (Click on purchase membership) The system will redirect you to your MYQQ page! Choose to activate membership now. Then select QQ individual account monthly subscription payment. Finally, click next step. The webpage will display the following message: Congratulations, operation successful,www.gzfp2012.com. Your personal account has only 1 Q Coin, this time you are given a membership experience (the insufficient amount will not be charged again, please rest assured), if you want to continue using the membership service next month, please recharge here as soon as possible, thank you! If successful, your QQ inbox will receive an email from Tencent! Next month it will cost 10 QBs,www.gzfp2012.com, but don't worry! Next month you can also use 1 QB!! Method: For example, if you apply for membership on the 17th of this month, then you need to enter MYQQ on the 16th of next month to cancel the membership server! Then you stop using it for 1-2 days, after 2 days, follow the above method to reapply! This way, you can long-term use 1 QB to apply for membership!! ^_^
