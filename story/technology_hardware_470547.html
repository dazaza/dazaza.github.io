April 4th's major components quotes: memory prices rebound, Hitachi 2TB drops below 500

by tuoqing1188 on 2011-08-04 10:17:40

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, the overall price trend is weak, with more inquiries from buyers, mainly for entry-level i3 or i5 models. Currently, a small amount of AMD Llano APUs have begun to impact Intel's market, although not enough to pose a threat to Intel processors, they have weakened the buying sentiment in Intel's market.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for AMD processors, the overall prices remain stable for now. With the arrival of loose processors, the upward trend in boxed processor prices has been curbed, and even some low-end models have seen slight price drops. The market refuses high-priced boxed Athlon processors.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop memory spot market, Kingston memory prices have risen, driving a small increase in some branded memory prices. Due to upstream memory distributors of certain brands colluding to slightly raise prices at the source, this has caused memory prices to rise. However, foreign memory prices are currently falling. Merchants reveal that all levels of distributors face inventory pressure, with daily price declines leading to basic loss-making operations, necessitating price increases to clear part of the inventory and recover funds. Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook memory spot market, strip prices have not yet been adjusted. Currently, the average price for 4GB/1333 notebook strips is 145 yuan, and the average price for 2GB/1333 notebook strips is 80 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop hard drive spot market, there is no significant fluctuation in overall prices. This week, Seagate and Hitachi 2TB hard drive prices have fallen below 500 yuan, with Hitachi Cool Drive quoted at 498 yuan. According to merchants, Seagate 2TB hard drives still have room for further decline. Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook hard drive spot market, market demand is weak, and Seagate/Hitachi notebook disk prices remain unchanged. The author recommends Hitachi 500GB/5400 RPM notebook disks, which are quoted at only 375 yuan, offering good value for money.

Intel Prices Weakened, More Buyers Inquiring

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, the overall price trend is weak, with more inquiries from buyers, mainly for entry-level i3 or i5 models. Currently, a small amount of AMD Llano APUs have begun to impact Intel's market, although not enough to pose a threat to Intel processors, they have weakened the buying sentiment in Intel's market.
