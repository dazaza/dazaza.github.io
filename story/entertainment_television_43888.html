Introduction to the Subplot of Episode 40 of "Crossing the Pass"

Episode 40

In the newly renovated house in their hometown, Chuanwen suggests that the elders evaluate the three sons. The couple hopes that in the future, Chuanwen will take on the role of the eldest son at home.

Chuanjie and Shaoying go to the Mining Bureau to inquire about the approval of coal mine extraction rights, where they encounter Vice President Ishikawa of Morita Industries. They then realize that Japan's Morita Industries is also competing for the extraction rights.

Zhukai Shan has returned from Shandong and firmly opposes participating in the coal mine operation, forcing Chuanjie and others to conceal the fact that they had mortgaged the restaurant. Chuanwen goes to check the accounts and finds that the money hasn't been touched, which puts his mind at ease.

Chuanwu returns home for the first time as a regiment commander, and the whole family celebrates with wine.

Shaoying investigates the situation of Morita Industries and analyzes with Chuanjie that the key to the approval of extraction rights lies with Director Yao of the Mining Bureau.

Chuanwu goes to pay respects to Zhen Sanjiang and encounters Xian'er. Xian'er forces Chuanwu to forget her and be kinder to Xiuer.

During Zhukai Shan's 66th birthday banquet, a tipsy Shaoying almost reveals the matter of the mortgaged restaurant. Luckily, Chuanjie reacts quickly and manages the situation. Chuanwen feels there's something amiss with Chuanjie, but gets no answer from Na Wen, so he discusses it with his parents, eventually pinpointing Xiuer as the breakthrough point.

Episode 41

Yushu takes Xiuer to school early in the morning, and Wen's mother waits until evening to catch Xiuer. Sure enough, upon raising her voice, Xiuer reveals everything about the mortgaged restaurant.

Zhukai Shan becomes furious and insists on expelling Chuanjie and the Yushu couple from the house, ignoring the entire family’s pleading on their knees.

Ichiro visits the Zhu family again, meeting the couple with joy, but ends up drinking too much. So Wen's mother instructs Xiuer to return the pocket watch he left behind.

Na Wen visits Chuanjie and Yushu's temporarily rented place and, seeing Chuanjie severely ill, quickly returns home to call Chuanwen. By the time they arrive, Chuanjie and his wife are nowhere to be found.

Xiuer goes to return the pocket watch to Ichiro, who invites her to have Western cuisine. Happy, Xiuer drinks too much, and Ichiro helps her rest in his room.

Episode 42

Unable to find Chuanjie, Wen's mother is about to confront Zhukai Shan when Zhukai Shan mentions Shaoying. Chuanwen and Na Wen rush to Shaoying's house, finding Chuanjie and Yushu there, putting their minds at ease.

Before leaving, Ichiro gives Xiuer one of his clothes to wear.

A stranger comes to the Shandong restaurant demanding to see Zhukai Shan. It turns out this person is from the Mining Bureau, coming regarding the coal mine extraction rights. Zhukai Shan expresses his disapproval of mining.

While Na Wen sends an errand boy to summon Chuanjie from Shaoying's house, she keeps the Mining Bureau official waiting.

When Zhukai Shan learns that Chuanjie and others are competing with the Japanese for the extraction rights, his attitude completely changes, fully supporting the coal mine venture and even helping Chuanjie come up with ideas for funding. The official is moved by Zhukai Shan and reveals his identity as Director Yao.

The Harbin branch of Ichiro's trading company opens, sending invitations to both Zhukai Shan and Morita. Morita uses the opportunity to instill expansionist thoughts into Ichiro, learning during their conversation about Ichiro's connection to the Shandong restaurant.

Xiuer gifts Ichiro a shirt, and Ichiro expresses his true feelings to Xiuer. From then on, Xiuer undergoes a change, which Yushu notices.

Episode 43

Chuanjie and Shaoying come to Shandong to borrow 600,000 yuan from Mr. Huang for the coal mine.

Ishikawa, led by Kohei from the Kakumei Society, visits Director Yao's house, discovering that Yao has suffered a stroke. A secretary from the Mining Bureau comes to the Shandong restaurant, asking on behalf of Director Yao how the borrowing trip to Shandong went.

Upon learning about Director Yao's stroke, Kaishan, Chuanjie, and Shaoying rush to his house, only to find him perfectly healthy. It turns out Director Yao feigned illness to wait for Chuanjie's return with the borrowed funds.

The extraction rights are granted to the Chinese, leaving Morita and Ishikawa extremely angry. Morita calls in Major Tailaki from the Army, requesting the Army General Staff occupy the mine under the guise of military exercises.

Chuanwen is very dissatisfied that the coal mine doesn't have a place for him, so at the founding ceremony of the Shanhe Coal Mine, he not only gets drunk but also makes a big fuss.

As it turns out, the Japanese Army really occupies Jiazigou, and the first load of coal from the Shanhe Coal Mine is blocked halfway. Before leading troops to Jiazigou, Chuanwu returns home once, informing the family that the Japanese might just be testing them.
