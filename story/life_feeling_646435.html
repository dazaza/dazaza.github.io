When We Grow Old

When we grow old, the sunlight will still warmly shine on the tree branches. At that time, will we be able to sit peacefully under the tree and listen to the sound of flowers blooming?

When we grow old, the world will be much more beautiful than it is now. Walking on the roads where we once rushed about in our youth, gazing at the cities we built with our sweat. Perhaps, what rises in our hearts then will not just be a sense of pride, but even more so, a feeling of relief and contentment.

When we grow old, our children will have grown up, and our mindset will no longer be young. Bathed in the sunlight, we can quietly wander through the tunnel of time. At that moment, you may recall a familiar figure or a heart-wrenching memory that has been etched into your mind.

Time changes everything, including our appearance and our posture.

When we grow old, our steps will become slow and unsteady. Perhaps what we yearn for most then will be to have someone who has accompanied you all along, standing by your side, never tiring of listening to the stories from your life that you never seem to finish telling.

When we grow old, our focus will no longer be on people, but on nature. By that time, all our love and emotions will be merged into an infinite admiration for nature. Only then will we have the peace of mind and the time to quietly savor the beauty and harmony of nature.

When we grow old, our hearts will be like still lake water, no longer stirring any ripples. At that time, we will gaze calmly at the blue sky and let our thoughts fly back to the past years, allowing the kites of our youth to drift freely across the fertile fields of our hearts.

When we grow old, sipping a cup of fragrant tea and listening to an old song of the times, we will cherish love even more, as well as family and friendship, treasuring every dawn and dusk. Life is like a spinning top, constantly rotating, and we will eventually move from the prime of our lives to the day of old age.

When we grow old, looking back on each sweet and bitter moment of today, we will look back with a calm smile. No matter how many pavilions shrouded in misty rain, no matter how many sorrows and helplessness, they will all dissipate in the wind with that calm smile.

Every cycle of life is a process of flowers blooming and falling. When flowers bloom, they do so fully; when they fall, they create a colorful carpet on the ground, leaving us with a youth without regrets.
