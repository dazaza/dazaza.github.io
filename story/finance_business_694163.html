After some time, Wang grew increasingly dissatisfied with Grandma Wang always going in and out with the "Old Immortal," as there is a distinction between men and women, and others might gossip. But Grandma Wang didn't think much of it. The two often quarreled over this, eventually leading to their divorce. Wang was extremely upset and for nearly ten years, he had been looking for this "Old Immortal" to settle accounts but did not know where he was, only having one photo of him. Wang deeply imprinted the face in the photo into his mind, fearing that if he met him someday, he wouldn't recognize him.

Grandma Wang from Gongyi was quite scientific and often went to burn incense and pray for blessings. This was also for the safety of her family, and her husband Old Wang fully supported her.

Later, Grandma Wang heard that there was an "Old Immortal" Zhang Zhengming in the neighboring village who was almost 20 years older than her. He would always get what he prayed for when burning incense. Thus, Grandma Wang became friends with the "Old Immortal" and often let him take her along to burn incense. After returning home, Grandma Wang would excitedly show Old Wang pictures of the "Old Immortal."

Realizing Mistaken Identity in Court

It wasn't until Yang Grandpa took Old Wang to court that Old Wang realized he had mistaken the person, feeling regretful. Wang's relatives have already compensated Yang Grandpa for his economic losses. Yesterday, the Gongyi People's Court sentenced Wang to one year in prison for intentional injury, with a two-year probation period. The "Old Immortal" Becomes a Rival

Seeing his wife always going to burn incense with the "Old Immortal" from the neighboring village, Old Wang angrily divorced her. For nearly ten years, Wang has deeply engraved the appearance of the "Old Immortal" in his heart. Fate brought them together unexpectedly.

Realizing Mistaken Identity in Court

After being beaten, the "Old Immortal" cried out in injustice: the injustice wasn't because he had any relationship with Wang's ex-wife, but rather that Wang had mistaken the person, and the one beaten was merely a passerby who looked very similar to the "Old Immortal"...

On August 11, 2011, at 6 p.m., Wang was loitering outside his home and saw Yang Grandpa, who was over 80 years old. Wang's heart tightened: with such an appearance, who else could it be but the "Old Immortal"? Wang approached Yang Grandpa and asked, "Are you Zhang Zhengming?" Yang Grandpa was puzzled and replied, "I'm not, you've mistaken me for someone else."

However, Wang believed that the person in front of him was his "enemy" and started hitting him. Yang Grandpa, being elderly, was knocked down with just two slaps from Wang, and after falling, Wang kicked him several times. At this point, many people gathered to watch, and someone called the police, which made Wang leave. According to the forensic science laboratory of the Gongyi Public Security Bureau, Yang Grandpa suffered a fracture in his left ulna due to external injuries, constituting a minor injury.
