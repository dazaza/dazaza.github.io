E-commerce has permeated all walks of life, and its speed and low cost are attracting more and more enterprises and institutions to join. E-commerce platforms are becoming increasingly perfect and developing towards cloud computing and public computing models. This article, through the combination with the Jiangsu Provincial Science and Technology Department's provincial science and technology innovation and transformation special project (Family Online E-commerce Website), has built a preliminary cloud computing infrastructure, and further discussed the key components of e-commerce on the cloud platform and their compositional relationships. Cloud computing technology makes future e-commerce more flexible and energy-saving, promoting the application of public computing and green computing in the field of e-commerce, reducing the cost of online transactions.

1. Construction of E-commerce Cloud Computing Infrastructure

Cloud computing, as the future network application architecture, is emerging in development. Cloud computing is a method of sharing IT infrastructure that can connect huge system pools to provide services. Cloud computing allows enterprise data centers to operate more like the Internet, allowing computing resources to be accessed and shared in a secure and scalable way, like virtual resources.

Virtualization is an abstraction layer that separates physical hardware from the operating system. On one hand, it can cut and re-integrate the computing and storage capabilities of existing servers into smaller units, thereby providing higher IT resource utilization and flexibility; on the other hand, it also provides universal interface standards for large-scale cloud computing integration, thus making computing public.

2. Key Components of E-commerce on the Cloud Platform and Their Compositional Relationships

In the design of e-commerce systems, Linux servers built on virtual hardware resources are used as basic system units, and LVS (Linux Virtual Server) and NAT (Network Address Translation) technologies are used to construct a practical cluster system tending towards cloud computing. Between broader data centers, intelligent content routing technology realizes flexible management and allocation of application services, incorporating high availability, high security, high performance, low cost, and high scalability into the cloud infrastructure.

In practice, we deeply explore the integration process of virtualization and LVS in constructing cloud computing infrastructure. Virtual computing technology well segments the computing and storage capabilities of hardware and provides standard interfaces for smaller computational units; LVS is a clustering construction technology based on load balancing under Linux. Through segmentation and reconstruction, LVS-based load balancing scheduling is achieved, making the distribution of computing power within the cloud more reasonable.

On the cloud-oriented computing cluster, further using the "Family Online" e-commerce website platform as an example, various e-commerce businesses, processes, and applications are packaged and integrated into cloud services, with SOA at its core. The business layer service integration is completed through the Enterprise Service Bus (ESB). SOA is a specification for designing, developing, applying, and managing dispersed logical (service) units in a computing environment. By analyzing the mutual calls between services, SOA enables company managers to easily obtain data information on when, why, and which business logic is executed, thereby helping enterprise managers or application architects iteratively optimize their enterprise business processes and application systems.

In the detailed design process of the web service of the e-commerce system, by integrating and packaging e-commerce processes under Windows and Linux platforms and publishing them as services, heterogeneous loose coupling is realized. This allows business logic within the cloud to be uniformly scheduled, building a more flexible system.

To solve the fluidity problem of wide area networks within the cloud, reduce the repetitiveness of network transmission, and improve bandwidth utilization, content routing technology, intelligent DNS resolution technology, and remote incremental replication technology are used in storage management. The combined use of these technologies will greatly enhance the fluidity of data within the cloud. Dynamic configuration of operating systems, applications, or services can be transmitted to any corner of the cloud. In a complete ubiquitous environment, high-quality computing services can be obtained using any device and any network at any time. The focus of ubiquitous computing is to provide a customer-oriented, omnipresent adaptive computing environment. In the fused space established by ubiquitous computing, people can "anytime, anywhere" and "transparently" obtain digital services, including e-commerce services. After the maturity of the ubiquitous computing environment, users can naturally obtain the required network and computing services at any location in living and working places. Since the devices providing computation and communication have been integrated into this environment, users do not need to consciously choose to use a certain device or network. Due to the omnipresence of computing power, the information space will be integrated with the physical space of people's lives and work. Ubiquitous information appliances, sensor networks, embedded system devices will be the carriers of future e-commerce. The growth of "Family Online" technology should adapt to this change and better integrate into the next generation of e-commerce systems.

3. Significance of Cloud Computing Research in the Construction of E-commerce Platforms

The purpose and significance of cloud computing research are significant. Just as the power system was transformed into a public utility production and consumption model, the future human race will enter a public computing era, i.e., the cloud computing era. People will rent dynamically configured computing resources worldwide. This change will surely improve the overall utilization efficiency of computing resources, reduce energy consumption and costs, achieving green computing. It solves the problems of inflexible enterprise computing resource allocation, high energy consumption, and unreasonable demand-based configuration of computing and storage resources in the past. Based on the digestion and absorption of grid computing, cloud computing ideas, combined with service-oriented (SOA) and web-oriented (WOA) methodologies, a series of software engineering development practices are carried out. Through the integration and transformation of several data centers' cloud computing, the service quality and user experience of the "Nantong Family Online E-commerce Co., Ltd." website platform have been effectively improved, reducing operating costs and energy consumption, achieving the effect of green computing. This research project has been officially approved and funded by the Jiangsu Provincial Science and Technology Department, and the developed website platform is currently in healthy commercial operation.
