This article comes from Taobao 8848 http://www.8848.cm Chapter Five: Oh God, please send me a prince on a white horse! In the quiet night, the autumn moon was alluring. A petite figure was struggling to walk, possibly having walked too far, her steps were unsteady, like a drunkard's. Her slender and fair hands dragged a black suitcase; its smooth lines indicating its extraordinary value. However, compared to this suitcase, its owner seemed somewhat disheveled. Her once clean beige sportswear looked dirty, probably due to being soaked. Her Anta sneakers had patches of darker colors. The long hair that should have been flowing freely now hung limply against her back, with droplets sliding down to leave streaks of dark yellow watermarks.

Sigh...

In the quiet road, a sigh slowly echoed, then after lingering melodiously in the air, it returned to silence, accompanied by hi-tec outdoor shoes. The struggling figure suddenly stopped, she raised her head dazedly, looking at the half-moon in the sky, her delicate eyebrows knitted together.

It seems almost time for the Mid-Autumn Festival.

I wonder if my parents have noticed that I've left home?

Hmm, perhaps, even if I disappeared for a year or two, they wouldn't know!

Feeling somewhat discouraged and helpless, Su Yixuan shook her head and continued walking, dragging the suitcase slowly forward. The 'lala' sound of the wheels touching the ground sounded again in the silent night. After taking a few more steps, she stopped again and pointed arrogantly towards the sky.

"Hmph, don't think you can defeat me this way! I, Su Yixuan, am not someone who gives up easily!" Her voice was sweet and ethereal, her clear eyes shining with stubbornness and brilliance. But as soon as these passionate and determined words were spoken, she deflated like a punctured balloon, losing all energy.

It wasn't surprising that she felt so negative. Because anyone facing what she did today, wearing Columbia outdoor shoes, would probably feel an urge to bash their head against a wall. Yet, Su Yixuan could still challenge the heavens, which fully demonstrated how strong she was. She was probably as tough as weeds in the wild, equally resilient and tenacious.

With a dejected sigh, Su Yixuan pulled her suitcase slowly forward again. In truth, she didn't know where she was going? Penniless, she didn't even have a place to stay. But surely, she couldn't sleep on the streets.

"What should I do?" Su Yixuan opened her big eyes and murmured to herself. She was already very tired, and all she wanted right now was to lie down on a comfortable bed and sleep. But all of this was just a longing for her.

Without money or identification, she simply couldn't afford to stay in a hotel or find work. Regular workplaces generally required an ID card, but hers had disappeared somewhere unknown.

"Damn rotten man. If I ever see you again, I won't let you off." Thinking of Lan Jialuo, Su Yixuan felt an indescribable hatred. Because all these misfortunes started because of Lan Jialuo, wearing Ba Ge sports shoes.

Taking a deep breath, Su Yixuan told herself to stay calm. She looked up at the moon, its gentle light bringing her a sense of peace and serenity.

Oh God, please grant me a prince on a white horse!

A prince who can rescue Cinderella!

Su Yixuan faced the moon, devoutly clasping her hands to make a wish. Although she didn't believe in the existence of fairy tales about Cinderella and princes in this world, in such a desperate situation, she still hoped that this kind of fairy tale could give her some hope.
