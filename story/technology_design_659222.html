Sina Entertainment News: The theme of the 2012 CCTV Lantern Festival evening party has been determined as "auspicious and如意make merry on Lantern Festival", which is expected to return to the basic cultural connotation of the Lantern Festival. "Make merry on Lantern Festival" is a special positioning of Chinese culture for the Lantern Festival that has been formed over thousands of years. Only this festival should be celebrated in the form of "making merry". Therefore, the 2012 Lantern Festival evening party will fully render and highlight the festive atmosphere of "making merry" through multiple links such as host language status, linking methods, and program design.

Firstly, in the design of the hosts, it will conform to the theme of "auspicious and如意make merry on Lantern Festival", dividing the six hosts into "auspicious" and "如意" two teams, with the form of "three-on-three" competition to send blessings throughout the entire evening party. In terms of host language, a relaxed and natural tone will continue to be maintained, while at the same time, the popular phrases left by the 2012 Spring Festival Gala programs will be integrated throughout, embodying Jiangxi culture.

Secondly, the evening party will retain the family-style celebration format of the 2012 Spring Festival Gala. The entire program still starts with the joint singing of celebrity families from all over the country, including the Dai Yuqiang family, Hu Yue and Lu Jianmin couples, Liu Hegang couples, etc., thus creating a warm and festive atmosphere.

Thirdly, in terms of program design, the program creativity of the Lantern Festival evening party adopts many forms of "cross-border" and "mix-and-match", striving to give the audience a refreshing viewing experience, while increasing the joyfulness of the entire evening party. For example, the song "Because of Love" will sound again on the Lantern Festival evening party after the Spring Festival Gala; however, the song retains the stage packaging mode of Wang Fei and Eason Chan from the Spring Festival Gala, but the singers are replaced by Cai Ming and Liao Changyong, bringing new fun to the same song work. However, this time, he and his golden partner Dong Qing did not perform magic, but sang songs, which is bound to bring new expectations to the audience. In addition, Li Yundi, the piano prince from the Spring Festival Gala, will this time play the piano solo with Sa Yi's saxophone, accompanied by the duet dance of Li Qian and Zhang Aoyue, the lead dancers of "Dragon and Phoenix Present Auspiciousness" from the Spring Festival Gala, forming a lush and coherent mixed program "Colorful Clouds Chase the Moon".

In addition to the innovative interpretation of classic works, the Lantern Festival evening party will also extensively introduce new people and new works, hoping to send a blessing of reunion and perfection to all TV viewers across the country and Chinese people around the world during the lively and festive Lantern Festival.
