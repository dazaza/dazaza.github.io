For the new house with European style decoration, there are only SIMON switches in the market that match.

by simon_blog_1987 on 2012-02-09 13:35:44

My husband and I met while studying in Europe and lived together there for over a decade. This time, his company sent him back to China to manage a long-term project, so we decided to settle down in China. For our new home's renovation, we chose a European style to try to get as close as possible to the style of where we used to live. However, choosing switches became a big problem. After visiting several building materials markets, most of the options were square, white styles that simply couldn’t match our home’s decoration style. By chance, we saw the V8 series switches at a SIMON switch specialty store. To my great surprise, these were exactly the same model of switches we had in our home in Barcelona!

When we lived in Spain, SIMON switches were everywhere around us, and I never expected to be able to buy them here in China. Due to SIMON's quality and design, it enjoys high honors in Europe and even worldwide. With nearly a hundred patents, 12 quality assurance steps, over 40,000 switch operations, and a normal usage life of 12 years, SIMON provides excellent guarantees.

The V8 series of SIMON electrical switch products emphasize nobility and stability in their color tone positioning. Available colors include Pearl Red, Zitan, Subsilver, Brushed Copper, Black Crystal, Glossy White, Bright Gold, Champagne, and Brushed Silver. The metallic sheen of the Brushed Copper has an extremely strong sense of power, but SIMON's special process removes the coldness of metal, uncovering its warm inner qualities. The Black Crystal is presented in a "mirror style," with the only downside being that it easily leaves fingerprints. The color of the switch is clearly marked on the barcode seal of the outer packaging, and the triangular transparent window on the panel packaging allows consumers to conveniently check before purchasing. The middle toggle button, which comes into contact with the fingers, uses a rubber paint process, softening the overall distance and coldness given by the metal frame, making it soft and elastic.

Being able to purchase SIMON switches and other SIMON Electrical products domestically makes me very happy. Finally, I can have a perfect home.
