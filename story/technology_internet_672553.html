"Xishi Secret History" is a hot broadcast, with three key figures supporting Wu Jingjing as "Xishi"_ Mainland Drama _ Entertainment - Yixiu

by kuailaira2 on 2012-02-23 00:44:28

"Xi Shi Secret History" is currently airing on satellite TV, and the three main actors are protecting Wu Jingjing, who plays "Xi Shi". Wu Jingjing's portrayal of Xi Shi has attracted a lot of attention. In this drama, which is directed by You Xiaogang, all female roles have been given to new faces, making it quite bold. The reason why Director You was so confident in his choice was not only because Wu Jingjing was an outstanding student at Communication University of China Nanguang College, selected from 6000 candidates, but also because he had three ace actors in his hands - Ma Jingtai, Ma Dezhong, and Chen Haomin. These three actors were entrusted with the task of supporting the role of "Xi Shi", creating a story of heroes and beauties that is sure to be captivating.

Ma Jingtai plays King Fuchai of Wu in "Xi Shi Secret History". This is his comeback work after a 3-year hiatus and his fourth collaboration with Director You in the "Secret History" series. Ma Jingtai expressed his willingness to play a supporting role to Wu Jingjing, as he was impressed by her portrayal of Xi Shi. He even joked about poking the director sitting next to the monitor during filming, saying that the selection of these "post-90s" actors was spot on. They looked good and had solid acting skills.

Ma Dezhong, a popular young actor from TVB, recently shifted his focus to mainland China. "Xi Shi Secret History" is his first work after leaving TVB, and he values it highly. Ma Dezhong also praised the newcomer Wu Jingjing, particularly appreciating the eyes of "Xi Shi". Her performance was calm and impressive. Ma Dezhong revealed that the "post-90s" generation, represented by Wu Jingjing, often comes up with unexpected ways of acting that can inspire experienced actors like himself.

Chen Haomin has recently become very popular due to the "Ji Gong" series. In "Xi Shi Secret History", he portrays Fan Li, the first strategist of the Spring and Autumn period, showcasing the deep affection he once displayed as "Duan Yu". Regarding his counterpart Wu Jingjing, who plays "Xi Shi", Chen Haomin also gave high praise. He described her as exceptionally pure, coming from a good family background without any setbacks, resulting in a simple character and pure eyes. This is exactly the feeling of Xi Shi required in the drama. Only such a Xi Shi could engage in a timeless love affair that would move the audience.
