During the interview, Sun Chaoyang didn't say much. But when he heard his father's words, tears streamed down from his eyes. Now 36 years old, his movements are still not free. After the car accident, his wife divorced him, and his daughter is being taken care of by his sister. All these have been tormenting his heart. However, his parents, with their selfless love and firm belief, have helped him recover little by little, both physically and mentally.

To awaken their son, Sun Kaihe and Ren Chunying spoke to him every day and gave him massages. The attending doctor, seeing this determined couple, kindly advised them, "Sun Chaoyang's condition is not good. You should prepare yourselves."

Sun Kaihe couldn't believe that his son would never wake up. He and his wife stayed steadfast by the bedside, calling out his name over and over.

Every morning at 9 o'clock and in the afternoon at 3 o'clock, on a street in Dawang Village, Chengguan Township, Xingyang City, the figures of Sun Kaihe, Ren Chunying, and their son Sun Chaoyang could be seen - Father Sun Kaihe holding Sun Chaoyang from the left side, Sun Chaoyang's entire body leaning heavily on his father, walking slowly, each step almost requiring all his strength; Ren Chunying holding a folding wheelchair in her right hand and pulling Sun Chaoyang's right arm with her left...

Upon receiving the call, Sun Kaihe and Ren Chunying immediately went to Hebei. The sight of their son lying unconscious on the hospital bed shocked them: unable to speak, no sensation, and unconscious...

Sun Kaihe and Ren Chunying learned some massage techniques from the local doctors and massaged Sun Chaoyang daily, speaking to him, feeding him, helping him turn over...

Sun Kaihe still feels pain when recalling that day: "My son was capable before the accident. He worked away from home, built a house within a few years, got married, and had a beautiful daughter. He was filial too, always sending money home. I didn't expect someone so lively to just collapse..."

Eight years ago, on November 19, 2003, Sun Kaihe remembers it clearly. From that day on, everything changed for their family. After receiving the call, he was speechless for half a day - his 28-year-old son had been hospitalized due to a car accident, critically ill, possibly becoming a vegetative state patient.

In March 2004, Sun Kaihe brought Sun Chaoyang back to their home in Xingyang from Hebei. At this point, Sun Chaoyang's eyes and hands occasionally moved, but the rest of his body remained without sensation.

"As long as I am alive, as long as I can move, as long as we have breath, we will not give up on our son." Sun Kaihe's words, though simple, were forceful.

A miracle occurred after more than 100 days - Sun Chaoyang's hands began to move!

During these eight years, through over 3000 days and nights, Sun Kaihe and Ren Chunying became Sun Chaoyang's personal doctor, nurse, and crutch... Every morning at 9 o'clock and in the afternoon at 3 o'clock, they wheeled Sun Chaoyang out of the house, walking repeatedly on the village paths, measuring this long path with their love and persistence.
