Summary of 10 Common Phenomena and Analysis of Reasons for Keyword Ranking Decline

by xiaoke369 on 2012-02-16 15:34:47

The ranking of website keywords rises and falls. Below, I will summarize these issues:

1. Baidu snapshot regression

This may be a phenomenon of demotion in online promotion. However, there is also a claim on the internet that this is due to problems with Baidu's database. Regardless of the reason, it is important to check if the website has any operations that search engines dislike.

2. Snapshots and indexing are still present, but keyword rankings have dropped significantly

A significant drop in keyword rankings could be due to changes in search engine algorithms. In most cases, if the entire website’s keywords (including long-tail keywords) drop as a whole, it might indicate being penalized.

3. Articles indexed on the same day, with rankings appearing on the same day, but disappearing the next day.

4. The entire site is banned, with no traffic from search engines. Searching "site:domain" yields no results.

This phenomenon can be considered one of the strictest punishments by search engines. If you encounter this situation, it means your website must have done something that search engines hate the most. If the site remains banned for a long time without recovery, the domain may need to be abandoned.

5. The homepage is banned, with no results when searching directly by URL.

This is also a common form of punishment by search engines. However, compared to the first type, the severity of punishment is less severe. As long as some improper operations are corrected, things should return to normal after some time.

6. Homepage occasionally gets banned and then released, or the entire site occasionally gets banned and then released.

In such situations, it might be due to server problems.

7. Baidu 11th position phenomenon

This is a unique demotion phenomenon specific to Baidu. It means that regardless of how other websites' rankings change, most of your website’s keywords will always be at the first position on the second page of Baidu search results, making it very stable.

8. A significant reduction in the number of indexed pages

This situation is usually caused by duplicate content on the website, though there could be other reasons as well.

9. Only the homepage is indexed

This means that after using "site:domain" in a search engine, only the homepage remains. This situation may occur after the website has been penalized.

10. Snapshot stagnation

Snapshot stagnation is relatively a milder form of penalty among demotion phenomena. If the snapshot is still within a week, there shouldn't be any major issues. However, if the snapshot hasn't been updated for more than 7 days, it is necessary to identify the cause of the demotion and correct the errors promptly.
