A good mood will not fall from the sky. Every day, we must adjust ourselves to maintain a joyful attitude. Apart from ourselves, no one else will come and make us happy for no reason. Therefore, if you want to keep a full energy status all day long, you need to learn how to entertain yourself. Start from the moment you get up and begin your full day! Here are ten ways to get a good mood and relieve stress.

1. Bed stretching exercises: Perhaps you don't believe it, but just a few simple steps can get rid of the habit of staying in bed too long. Before getting dressed, try sitting on the bed and doing some simple stretches. Loosen the tense muscles and shoulders, slowly turn your head and neck, take a deep breath, and then get up. You'll feel a sense of happiness.

2. Music awakening: The loud alarm clock may hurt your spirit. A relaxing wake-up ritual is necessary. For example, choose a favorite CD and set a music timer. Beautiful music will gently awaken you, bringing a good mood for the whole day.

3. Try homemade pastries: Research shows that eating sweets helps soothe depression. Actually, tasting homemade snacks not only brings the joy of success, but also unexpected fun during the cooking process.

4. Make breakfast for yourself: Some people would rather sleep half an hour more than have a delicious breakfast. In fact, breakfast is the most important meal of the day. Breakfast is the source of vitality for the day. Skipping breakfast just to sleep a little longer is not worth it. It affects health and misses the beautiful feeling of enjoying a peaceful breakfast. Decide to get up half an hour earlier tomorrow and make a delicious breakfast for yourself! This will give you a refreshing day.

5. Take a soothing bath: Whether taking a shower or soaking in the bathtub depends on your time availability. If soaking, the water temperature should not be too high, and the time should not be too long. Choose some citrus-scented bathing products, which are the best for boosting spirits. If taking a shower, here's a small secret to relieve shoulder muscle soreness: put a towel on your shoulders and use the showerhead water column at a tolerable heat to hit both shoulders for ten minutes each time. Do this three times a week or more for excellent results.

6. Dusting and vacuuming: The dishes in the kitchen are piling up, there's a layer of dust on the windows, and dirty clothes are scattered everywhere. Instead of letting these things bother you, spend some time vacuuming, dusting, and tidying up. When you look around, you'll feel infinitely refreshed.

7. Stay away from TV: Studies show that people who center their lives around watching TV tend to be less happy. Yes, sometimes lying on the sofa and staring at the TV all day leads to feeling like you've seen nothing and remembered nothing. Then you start worrying and regretting, thinking you shouldn't have let TV take up so much time.

8. Go for a walk outside: On sunny, breezy days, it's perfect to go out and shake off the moldy feeling of being cooped up indoors or in the city.

9. Sit quietly and read a book: Do you remember the strong ink smell of books? Do you remember the gentle touch of your fingers flipping through pages? Do you remember when you were last deeply moved by a plot in a book? Find some time, brew a cup of coffee, and relive that feeling again!

10. Buy a gift for yourself: It could be a bouquet of flowers, a scarf, a pair of expensive yet very comfortable shoes, or even a sumptuous, delicious meal. Occasionally indulging yourself is enough to cure bad moods caused by high pressure and tension.
