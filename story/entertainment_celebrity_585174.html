Netizens passed on that a certain middle school hired Zhang Qian'er, known as the "first beautiful bosom in China," as the honorary principal.

by mlwang on 2011-11-25 10:31:50

Recently, some netizens on some online forums have posted to expose that the No. 12 Middle School of Yulin City has hired a bosomy model as the honorary principal, and attached a photo of the principal and the model holding the letter of appointment together. Is it true or false? Is it proper for the middle school to hire a bosomy model as the honorary principal? For a time, netizens talked about it in groups.

The title of the post put online is "My Alma Mater hires China's first bosomy model as the honorary principal, and I am very complicated."

The reporter saw that the content of the post was divided into two parts: the upper part is the microblog picture of bosomy model Zhang Qian, which reads: "Hired as the honorary principal of No. 12 Middle School of Yulin City, I hope to make a little contribution to the cause of education! Thank my good friends for their support and trust." The lower part is a bespectacled man tightly gripping Zhang Qian's right hand with his right hand, and the left hands of the two people together hold a red "letter of appointment". It was rumored that a certain middle school had hired Zhang Qian'er, known as China's first bosomy model, as the honorary principal.

After this post was issued, the click rate and reply rate were very high. Some netizens said: "The bespectacled man is Principal Tan Moumou." Some netizens said: "The school is a place for teaching and educating people, is it appropriate to hire a bosomy model as the honorary principal? What can she do for the school?" However, there are also netizens who questioned the authenticity of the post, hoping that relevant departments would find out the truth and announce it to the public.

Who exactly is Zhang Qian? The reporter learned through an online search that she is from Chengdu, Sichuan, and is called "China's first bosomy model", and there are many large-scale sexy and fiery photos of her wearing cool clothes online.

Her microblog name is "Zhang Qian'er", after the reporter logged into the microblog and added her as a follower, it was found that regarding the content of "being hired as the honorary principal of No. 12 Middle School of Yulin City", her microblog showed "this microblog has been deleted by the original author", the original microblog content showed the release time was "21:48 on October 28".

Principal claims the photo is PSed during the interview

In the afternoon of November 8, the reporter saw at No. 12 Middle School of Yulin that the structure, framework, and stone column colors of a teaching building and various scenes were exactly the same as the background in the online post photo. Some students said in interviews that they had never heard of Zhang Qian being hired as the honorary principal, while some students said they had heard of it, but did not know the specific situation.

The reporter then came to the principal's office and saw a man who looked extremely similar to the man in the online post photo, but he claimed to be the head of the school office, and said that Principal Tan was away on business these few days.

Is the content of the online post true? The man said that he knew it had already spread wildly online, and as long as someone with a brain thought about it slightly, they would know that this was an impossible thing, the school could not possibly hire such a person as the honorary principal, the content of the online post was purely a rumor, and the photo was PSed by netizens for hype.

At around 10 o'clock in the morning on the 9th, the reporter again came to the principal's office and still saw this man, at this time he admitted that he was Principal Tan. He said, first, he had no intention of hiring Zhang Qian as the honorary principal; second, he didn't take a photo with Zhang Qian; third, what photo the online post photo was PSed from, he wouldn't explain.

However, he laughed and said that No. 12 Middle School of Yulin is a public school, originally called Mount Ming High School, since it was renamed in 2008, it has been three years, but not many people in Yulin know about this school, "after such a stir online, it's not a bad thing for the school, the netizens' hype hit the mark, my purpose has already been achieved, regardless of how the netizens comment, just let them make a fuss."

China's first bosomy model Zhang Qian'er

Multiple parties confirm the photo is real

Is the online post photo really PSed as the principal said? The reporter transmitted the photo to professional photographers of the Yulin Photography Association and art editors of multiple media outlets, and they all determined after identification: the photo is real, absolutely not PSed.

The reporter left a message for Zhang Qian via Weibo to seek the truth, and she sent over her agent's mobile phone number and asked the reporter to contact him. At around 5 p.m. on the 9th, her agent Jack (English name) said to the reporter over the phone: "Our top model doesn't need hype, if this matter (referring to Zhang Qian being hired as the honorary principal of No. 12 Middle School of Yulin) didn't happen, she wouldn't randomly post information on Weibo."

"Is there such a matter?" In the afternoon of the 9th, when relevant responsible persons of the Secretariat of Yulin Education Bureau heard this situation, they were extremely surprised. The responsible person stated that No. 12 Middle School of Yulin is a public school, hiring an honorary principal must be approved by the Education Bureau, and so far, they haven't heard about this school hiring a bosomy model as the honorary principal, and will immediately report to the bureau leader.

At around 4 p.m. on the 15th, a female staff member of the bureau's secretariat telephoned to consult the bureau leader and told the reporter that after investigation, the online post photo was not PSed, it was indeed a real-life photo of Principal Tan and the bosomy model, but the school did not hire the bosomy model as the honorary principal.

Introduction

On the morning of the 10th, Principal Tan went to the bureau leader's office with the letter of appointment and some photos for investigation, saying that not long ago, Zhang Qian followed a certain association to the school to promote "Disciple's Rules", at that time, he didn't know she was a bosomy model, the association gave the school some books, and hired him as a publicity volunteer, the letter of appointment in the online post was the one issued by the association to him, there was nothing much inside, equivalent to a kind of honorary certificate, just with the words "letter of appointment" written on the shell. To leave a memory, he took a photo with each person in the association holding the letter of appointment, and unexpectedly, his photo with the model was posted on Weibo by her for hype.

The content of the letter of appointment remains a "secret"

The reporter proposed to see the letter of appointment, but the Education Bureau asked the reporter to find Principal Tan himself, and Principal Tan asked the reporter to find the bureau leader, both sides pushed back and forth, making the letter of appointment apparently become a "confidential document".

On the 17th, the reporter contacted Zhang Qian's agent Jack again, and he still claimed that the letter of appointment in the online post was the one issued by the principal to Zhang Qian as the honorary principal, and said that after meeting the principal, they had become friends, and didn't want this matter to affect the principal's job position, so he didn't want to take a photo of the content of the letter of appointment and send it to the reporter.
