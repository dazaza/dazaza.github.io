**Title: I'm in Kenting, Sunny Weather - Episode 8**

**Introduction to the Plot of "I'm in Kenting, Sunny Weather":**

Hanwen and Liangliang, two childhood friends who grew up in Kenting, are huge fans of the online pure love writer "Rain Never Stops." Using the nickname "Sunny Weather," they frequently interact with "Rain Never Stops" on her blog. Hanwen, who has never been in love, even falls for "Rain Never Stops," fantasizing that she is as pure and innocent as the characters in her stories. In reality, "Rain Never Stops," whose real name is Xiaowei, is a girl with intense emotions but a history of failed relationships. After being heartlessly treated by her boyfriend, she abandons her soon-to-be-published novel and everything in Taipei, secretly retreating to Kenting.

Annan, the illustrator for "Rain Never Stops'" novels, comes to Kenting after facing difficulties collaborating with the publisher and dealing with financial troubles in his own studio with his girlfriend. He arrives in Kenting to collect a debt.

By chance, Annan meets Hanwen and Liangliang, and the three become good friends. The frequent interactions between Annan and Liangliang make Azuo, who has a crush on Liangliang, extremely jealous. After meeting Annan, Hanwen, who shares a love for surfing and the ocean, decides to open a surf shop with him.

Xiaowei keeps her distance, only socializing with a foreign friend who came to Kenting with her. Her cold and distant attitude frustrates Hanwen, but her aloofness and sadness also attract him, making him question his feelings for "Rain Never Stops."

The foreign friend who accompanied Xiaowei to Kenting turns out to have an ulterior motive. After Liangliang enthusiastically gives them a tour, the foreigner writes a paper criticizing the management issues and contradictions within Kenting National Park, suggesting that Kenting be removed from the list of national parks. This action causes a stir and inspires Liangliang to propose a BOT bidding system to manage jet ski operators, which sparks strong opposition from local businesses. During a heated protest, Azuo is severely injured while protecting Liangliang.

Xiaowei returns to Taipei to resolve her contractual issues with the publisher. Her ex-boyfriend, now facing a major career crisis, tries to use her fame to promote his latest real estate project, using this opportunity to get close to her again.

Hanwen, separated from Xiaowei in another city, wants to help her solve her contract problems but feels helpless due to his lack of legal knowledge.

After meeting Brother Chu, Annan gradually understands his deep affection for Kenting and his grand vision for the tourism industry. He hopes to collaborate with Brother Chu to start a business. A group of people join Brother Chu's plan, but just as their business is about to take off, Brother Chu disappears. Where did Brother Chu go? How will they continue their dream for Kenting?...

This episode continues to explore the intertwined lives, dreams, and challenges faced by the characters in Kenting, where love, friendship, and aspirations collide amidst the beautiful coastal scenery.
