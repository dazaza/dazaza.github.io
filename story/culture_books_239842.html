She is a fox fairy with a thousand years of cultivation; he is the prince with prestigious status in the human world. A millennium fox demon who has never been tainted by human affairs falls into the red dust, and also into the heart of a gentleman as warm as jade. When a naive and innocent young fox girl encounters Prince Zhao Ming, who is deeply affectionate and of noble character, a love story between a human and a fox begins. That poem "Lanling Xiangsi Fu" which fully expresses innocence and deep affection, describes infinite sweetness and intimacy, and melancholy!
