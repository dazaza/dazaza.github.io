Depend on the mountain and the mountain may collapse, depend on people and they may run away, only oneself is the most reliable. Jize Enterprise

by g969lgjy on 2012-02-18 13:07:15

1. Relying on the mountain, the mountain may fall; relying on people, they may run away; only oneself is the most reliable.

2. No one will accompany you for a lifetime, so you have to adapt to loneliness; no one will help you for a lifetime, so you have to fight all your life.

3. Rather than regret today with tears, it's better to struggle today with sweat.

4. When the tears are gone, what remains should be strength.

5. Life is like a cup of coffee without sugar, it tastes bitter when you drink it, but when you savor it, there is a lasting aftertaste that never fades.

6. There is a kind of bond that becomes a landscape after letting go, and a heart that reveals sincerity when kept calm.

7. Choose what you love, and love what you choose.

8. If you don't give up in this second, there will be hope in the next second.

9. Remember what you've had, cherish what is hard to get, don't give up what belongs to you, don't force what doesn't belong to you, and keep lost things as memories.

10. If you like it, treasure it; if you treasure it, don't give it up.

11. Life is a journey, not caring about the destination, but rather the scenery along the way and the mood to enjoy it.

12. Don't compare with others, just surpass yourself; if you cry, let it be tears of passion; if you laugh, let it reflect growth.

13. Those who are happy about your sadness are enemies; those who are happy about your happiness are friends; those who are sad about your sadness are your conscience.

14. If you love, love deeply; if you don't love, leave.

The first sentence: "People are forced into action." Everyone has potential, living comfortably can lead to complacency, and dying in worry can lead to trouble. So, when facing pressure, don't be anxious; perhaps it's just a small test from life. Believe in yourself, everything can be handled well. Urgency can push heroes to extraordinary deeds, times create heroes, the poor seek change, and people only gain motivation under pressure. As long as there is hope in your heart, there is happiness.

The second sentence: "If you are simple, the world will be simple to you." A simple life leads to a happy life. People should be content, tolerant, and not overthink things. The heavier the mental load, the more likely one is to complain. Periodically clear out unpleasant memories and discard unhappy people and events.

The third sentence: "Life has no rehearsals, every day is live broadcasting." Sometimes I think, if life were like a video game where you could start over, what would life be like? Because time flows irreversibly, every day lost cannot be retrieved, so we must cherish every moment, respect our parents, care for our children, treat our loved ones kindly, and be good to our friends.

The fourth sentence: "Having talent is like being pregnant, eventually people will notice." People should not be arrogant; the Earth will turn without anyone. Throughout history, those who flaunt their talents often end up badly. So, even if you're talented, stay humble and cautious, do your own thing, and gold will shine eventually.

The fifth sentence: "In the past, meeting a true friend meant drinking a thousand cups wasn't enough; now, drinking a thousand cups means few true friends remain." Not being much of a drinker, I don't appreciate the taste of alcohol, but I understand the value of true friends. A life without friends is lonely and incomplete. Due to busy lives, contact diminishes, and friendships grow distant. Spend some time contacting friends to chat, let friendship flourish through laughter. When friends face difficulties, remember to stand by them, even if you can't help, offering comfort is the greatest support.

The sixth sentence: "If life takes a wrong direction, stopping is progress." It's hard for people to correct their own mistakes or discover their errors. Sometimes, knowing they're wrong, they still can't back down and repeat their mistakes. Maintain the right direction, adhere to your principles. There are many temptations in the world, don't expect free lunches, and don't sacrifice happiness for temporary pleasure. If you realize you're wrong, stop immediately.

The seventh sentence: "The two great tragedies of life: one is losing all hope, the other is being overly confident." People in modern times seem particularly fragile, newspapers daily report numerous cases of depression among celebrities. These people must swing from one extreme to another. Overconfidence leads them to believe they are perfect and capable of anything. A little setback can make them extremely self-deprecating, even losing the courage to continue living. Find the right place for yourself and enjoy life.

The eighth sentence: "Life and love are alike; missing love is missing life." What is love? It confuses and fascinates people. Facing love, be brave and express your feelings. If there are flowers to pick, pick them now, don't wait until there are none left. People will age and die, however they live, it's a lifetime. Missing love is missing the brilliance of life.

The ninth sentence: "Rich people always end up together." Modern society is becoming increasingly materialistic, and love is becoming less and less essential. Thus, I have to believe that "rich people always end up together" is the most accurate description of modern love.

The tenth sentence: "To succeed, you need friends; to achieve great success, you need enemies." Competition drives development. Because of the existence of rivals and the determination not to lose, one strives to do their best. Sometimes, enemies have more power than friends. The world doesn't always have eternal enemies, but it does have eternal friends. Enemies can sometimes become friends.
