The state strictly investigates the formaldehyde content of home decoration materials.

by longersing on 2009-08-17 16:50:36

Article Title: The State Strictly Inspects Formaldehyde Content in Home Decoration Materials of Decoration Companies

In early August, the General Administration of Quality Supervision, Inspection and Quarantine (GAQSIQ) announced the results of a recent law enforcement inspection on eight categories of indoor decoration and renovation products, including wooden furniture, plywood, interior wall coatings, and fireproof boards. The results showed that the problem of excessive release of formaldehyde and other harmful substances in indoor decoration, interior renovation, and renovation products remains prominent. Subsequently, reporters visited various decoration markets in Beijing and found that the supervision work in regular large shopping malls was significantly better than that in remote malls.

The release of formaldehyde has become a "chronic problem" in indoor decoration and renovation.

It is reported that from July 25 to 31 over a one-week period, a concentrated inspection campaign targeting decoration and renovation products was carried out nationwide. During the "Concentrated Action Week," law enforcement officers inspected 7,242 production enterprises of decoration and renovation products nationwide and handled 106 cases.

According to Yan Fengmin, deputy director of the Law Enforcement Department of the GAQSIQ, it was found during inspections that unlicensed production problems still exist; the issue of excessive release of formaldehyde and other harmful substances in products produced by illegal enterprises is relatively prominent; and the illegal act of counterfeiting well-known brand enterprises still exists. Yan Fengmin introduced that this concentrated action is an important part of the "Fresh Living Quarters" special law enforcement action, which was jointly launched by the GAQSIQ, the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development, and the State Administration for Industry and Commerce. During the activity period, the three departments will inspect the illegal acts of producing, selling, and using counterfeit and substandard decoration and renovation materials in key areas, key markets, and key construction sites.
