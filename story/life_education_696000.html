Team-building activities -- Noah's Ark

by boe868 on 2012-03-07 18:41:31

A game that can be used during interviews, through which you can understand the personalities of team members and find the talent you want.

The Legend of Noah's Ark:

It narrates the story of when the earth was struck by a flood, only Noah's family was saved by boarding the boat.

Now please imagine, if you were on this Noah's Ark, in addition to Noah's family, what animals would you also like to board this ark? How would you make your choice?

Before your eyes, there are only six kinds of animals left. Due to the limited capacity of the ark, only three kinds of animals can be allowed to board.

Which animals would you let board?

First, please indicate the animal you most want to board in the first place, then the second, and finally the third.

A. Horse B. Rabbit C. Deer D. Sheep E. Chicken F. Pig

Analysis and Answer

In mental games, the test of Noah's Ark is often cited.

Animals symbolize our respective hidden desires.

The animal you most want to board represents the self presented on the surface, which is the self-expectation of the "self", and this "self" is also recognized by people around you.

The animal you secondly prioritize to board represents the current you, which is the self-expectation.

The last animal you want to board represents the dream deemed impossible by yourself, which means the future self.

A. Horse

Strongly craves others' attention and hopes to get recognition from those around him.

Has the courage to try and accept challenges, with great dreams and ambitions.

The horse symbolizes "self and vanity", and horses with traits of intelligence and endurance have powerful vitality to gallop wildly.

People of this type like regularity and order, and at the same time, they will require others to follow and maintain them.

Sometimes, it's easy to forget one's own shortcomings and naggingly criticize lovers or partners, or endlessly complain to others.

You have quite personal insights and judgment in dressing, always attracting admiring glances without following trends blindly.

Currently, you strongly crave love and also want to lead your lover [or wife, husband].

Once your love is not satisfied, you want to complain or get angry as a result.

B. Rabbit

People who choose rabbits are basically gentle, kind, and homesick.

They do not unreasonably demand from others, and if they encounter someone they like, they will not hesitate to give themselves completely.

In dealing with matters and treating people, they dislike ostentation and seek changes, always diligently staying on their posts.

Currently, you are surrounded by many good friends who can comfort and encourage you when you fail or feel lonely.

If you are female, probably there are some paternalistic men around you who can help you.

Now, the thing you hate the most is probably arguing with others!

Your major shortcoming is not being able to cleverly convey your feelings to the person you like.

C. Deer

Not caring about surrounding matters, being self-centered, clearly distinguishing between oneself and others.

In life, actively planning for oneself and striving to achieve goals.

Deer represent the animals of "effort and humility", so you will tirelessly work hard within possible limits.

And hope to gain recognition from people you care about, not seeking to stand out, but hoping to receive applause.

Similarly, you don't expect others to treat you too formally.

When treating others, you always have clear likes and dislikes, and once the first impression is bad, it is difficult to win your favor again.

Currently, it is the time for you to determine your goal and take immediate action.

D. Sheep

Sheep symbolize "obedience and kindness".

You always respect elders and trustworthy people, and for those you trust, you will go through fire and water without hesitation.

Of course, such a kind person as you will absolutely never betray allies.

Due to a gentle and mild personality, sometimes you may sacrifice yourself too much, leading to failure because of serving others.

Currently, paying attention to socializing is urgent for you, otherwise, you may easily fall into the trap of infatuation.

Participating more in reading clubs or recreational groups should help you find suitable opposite-sex partners.

E. Chicken

From the outside, you appear to be a person with a good attitude towards others, very family-oriented and gentle,

Actually, you often judge others based on preconceived notions: "This person is not good enough", "That type is the most ideal".

To be honest, currently, you must adjust your attitude and try to deal with various types of men ["women"].

The reason is that although you want to further engage in friendly conversations with men ["women"], you always keep others at arm's length.

Trying to broaden your horizons and increase knowledge, you will discover opportunities around you.

Relying solely on waiting for the opposite sex to take the initiative is not enough when interacting with them.

Your thoughts and actions should be combined, and taking the initiative is the fundamental way to solve the problem.

In addition, you are very likely to be stubborn due to emotional impulses.

F. Pig

You are a humorous and fun-loving person.

Drinking and having fun with friends, singing and making noise, is one of your great hobbies.

However, sometimes you may feel that you are not good enough, falling into a state of inferiority and self-pity.

I believe you often reflect on yourself and regret certain words after the fact.

Perhaps people who don't know you well might think you are shallow, reckless, or overly eager to please others.

Even facing such criticism, it seems you don't take it seriously.

After a slight reflection, you won't be troubled by meaningless slander, and this is precisely your greatest charm.
