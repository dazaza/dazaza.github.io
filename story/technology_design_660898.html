Dongyang's garment industry makes a stunning transformation

by xuezhen5556g on 2012-02-08 12:41:19

However, since 2003, the Dongyang garment industry has fallen into a development low, with the number of enterprises plummeting from thousands at its peak to more than a hundred. Yesterday, the Dongyang Fashion Women's Apparel Industry Association reported that after several years of hardship and perseverance, "Dongyang Brand" apparel has made a beautiful V-shaped reversal, welcoming new development opportunities. In the late 1990s, a street filled with 500 garment factories had hotels packed with procurement agents from all over. Kangliya, Chaolong, Dirshen, Haonan, Luo Hao, Hai Zhi Lan... "Dongyang Brand" women's wear once made many people in Dongyang proud. In the late 1990s, the clothing market was swept by a wave of imitation wool suits. The astute people of Dongyang immediately smelled this business opportunity. For a time, clothing enterprises surged in Dongyang. "At the time, there were thousands of large and small garment factories in Dongyang, and each factory basically had its own brand," said Vice President of the Dongyang Garment Industry Association and Chairman of Luo Hao Garments Company, Luo Xiuliang. He said that Zhhenxing Road, which was awarded as one of the "Top Ten Specialty Commercial Streets in the Province" in recent years, also began to flourish from then on. At that time, Zhhenxing Road gathered a large number of suit production enterprises, reaching up to 500 at its peak. These enterprises adopted the "shop downstairs, factory upstairs" model, producing and selling their respective suits. From then on, the Dongyang garment industry established its position in the national clothing field. Clothing procurement agents from all over came upon hearing the news, filling up large and small hotels and inns in Dongyang. "Back then, as long as you sat in the office every day, you would constantly receive calls requesting franchise stores," recalled Luo Xiuliang. In 2003, the garment industry underwent a dramatic change, bringing a life-and-death test for enterprises. For Dongyang's garment enterprises, 2003 was a year of life or death. That year, "personalization" became the keyword in the garment industry. The popular suits were impacted by jackets, leading to a sharp decline in market demand. Once, walking down the street in a suit was something to be proud of, but that year it became a symbol of being outdated. Whether young people or successful middle-aged individuals, they started experiencing aesthetic fatigue and began to dislike "colliding clothes." Soon, a batch of jacket brands such as Jinqiabang and Septwolves emerged in Fujian, shaking Dongyang's position in the garment industry. Affected by this, Dongyang's garment factories either shut down or transformed, causing the number of enterprises to sharply decrease to more than a hundred. "This was both a filtration and a test," reflected Luo Xiuliang, who "escaped death" during those years. This might be the "cost of growth" for Dongyang's garment enterprises, and precisely because of this round of reshuffling, Dongyang's garment enterprises learned resilience and how to think. After the current pain, the strong returned, and the industry walked out of a V-shaped reversal. After a period of silence, the Dongyang garment industry successfully achieved a beautiful V-shaped reversal. Always excelling in foreign trade, "Dirshen" balanced internal and external operations while extending its reach into the popular redwood industry; "Haonan" continuously strengthened internal management, known within the industry for its management advantages; Tongli Garment Company, Xinya Industrial Company, and other enterprises maintained close ties with well-known domestic and international brands, accepting orders for processing and production. Most of their products are mid-to-high-end, sold to Western Europe, Northern Europe, and the United States... Especially "Luo Hao", which locked onto its target audience, accurately positioned itself, hired top design teams, tracked fashion trends starting from details, and adjusted product structures...
