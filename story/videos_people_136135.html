Minister Yin Weimin came to Wenzhou to supervise employment work

by 304885068 on 2009-03-31 08:52:47

From March 25 to 26, a group led by Yin Weimin, the Minister of Human Resources and Social Security, came to Wenzhou to supervise employment work. Shi Limin, Deputy Secretary General of the Provincial Government, Chen Xiaoen, Director of the Provincial Labor Security Department, and others accompanied them to Wenzhou.

Wenzhou Secretary Shao Zhanwei, Wenzhou Deputy Secretary Zhu Xianliang, Wenzhou Vice Mayor Meng Jianxin, Director of Wenzhou Labor Security Bureau Chen Hehe, Director of Wenzhou Personnel Bureau Wang Yiqi, and others accompanied the research or attended the briefing meeting.

In the morning of the 26th, Minister Yin Weimin and his group first went to Kangnai Group for investigation and research. They also held a symposium with the responsible persons of Delixi, Jindi, Angu, Xuanda and other enterprises in the company. During the symposium, Minister Yin Weimin carefully learned about the impact of the financial crisis on Wenzhou enterprises from several entrepreneurs from different industries. He encouraged the enterprises to be confident, treat employees well, and continue to maintain and develop good development trends.

Afterwards, Minister Yin Weimin and his group went to Wenzhou Vocational Introduction and Guidance Service Center. On the first floor of the vocational introduction hall, Minister Yin Weimin walked into the Xin Village and Wuma Vocational Introduction Office, had a cordial conversation with the office staff, and carefully understood the employers' job information, vocational introduction, migrant workers' job hunting situation. Subsequently, Minister Yin Weimin also chatted in detail with several migrant workers who were looking for jobs, asked about their job hunting wishes in detail, and encouraged them to find their ideal jobs as soon as possible.

At the working report meeting held in the afternoon, Minister Yin Weimin fully affirmed the series of measures taken by the Wenzhou Party Committee and the government to stabilize the employment of migrant workers. He pointed out that the improvement of the employment situation depends on economic development and enterprise development. Now the economy has shown some signs of warming up, but whether it can continue still needs to be observed. He emphasized that the government and enterprises must keep a clear head, closely monitor the situation, combine the actual situation of Wenzhou, creatively implement various policies and measures of the central committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council, maintain stable and rapid economic development, and keep the stability of the employment situation, making new contributions to national development.
