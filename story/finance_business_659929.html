2. Our Analysis and Judgment

(1) Entering the resource industry, taking a new step in diversified development

After the overall listing in 2007, the company actively adjusted its industrial structure, focusing on the core business of water conservancy and hydropower engineering construction. It formed a pattern of common development across six major business sectors: engineering construction, cement production, civil explosives, highway operation, real estate, and hydropower development, achieving an upgrade from pure engineering construction to integrated engineering contracting and development.

The continuous growth of investment and operational businesses, along with their increasing share of revenue, helps the company better improve its gross profit margin, thereby enhancing the profitability of the enterprise. This time, entering the coal industry marks another step forward for the company's diversified business into upstream resource industries.

(2) Early layout in the Xinjiang region, seizing the opportunity

In November 2007, the company passed resolutions to establish Xinjiang Gezhouba Engineering Construction Co., Ltd., Xinjiang Gezhouba Investment Development Co., Ltd., and Xinjiang Kizir River Basin Water Conservancy and Hydropower Development Co., Ltd. We believe that the company has secured relatively abundant orders for hydropower construction in Xinjiang. Xinjiang is rich in resources, but infrastructure development lags relatively behind. The company's entry into Xinjiang not only promotes the growth of infrastructure and other construction businesses but also benefits the development of the company's investment and resource businesses.

(3) The company maintains good growth in newly signed contracts

From January to March 2010, the cumulative amount of newly signed contracts by the company reached RMB 16.756 billion, an increase of 43.27% year-on-year. Among them, newly signed international engineering contracts accounted for approximately 42.38% of the total newly signed contract value; newly signed domestic and international hydropower projects accounted for about 67.05% of the total newly signed contract value, showing remarkable performance.

We believe that 2010-2012 will be the main period for the release of performance in the company's investment business, with cement, civil explosives, real estate, and other businesses successively entering the return period, providing strong momentum for the company's sustained business growth.

3. Investment Recommendations

We estimate that the earnings per share of the company from 2010 to 2012 will reach RMB 0.79, RMB 1.01, and RMB 1.19, respectively, corresponding to price-to-earnings ratios of 13.7 times, 10.7 times, and 9.1 times. We maintain our recommendation rating for the company's investment.
