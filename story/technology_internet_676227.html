Mr. Tang said yesterday: "Thinking that the person who lost the bag must come back to look for it, so I kept it in the store. If no one claims it, I plan to hand it over to the police this afternoon."

Yesterday, at the "Feishengyu Yangrou Pao" store, customers were coming and going in an unceasing stream in this store of less than 30 square meters. Mr. Tang said that right where he picked up Mr. Zeng's leather bag, two customers left a package of documents. He chased after them for quite a distance before catching up with them and returning it. There are also incidents where customers leave their mobile phones or wallets here. Each time someone loses something, they express their gratitude to Mr. Tang, but he always declines. "These are all ordinary things. As a businessman, I should always do business honestly. I won't take money that doesn't belong to me, even if it seems like easy money."

Therefore, Mr. Zeng specially made a plaque to thank him.

This happened on December 8th. Around 9 PM that day, while tidying up the tableware, Mr. Tang found a brown leather bag on a chair. He immediately picked it up and shouted, "Whose bag is this? Whose bag is this?" But none of the diners responded.

When Mr. Zeng came to thank him, Mr. Tang (on the left) said, "This is just an ordinary thing." Photo by Tian Yuanye of Lijin Daily.

As for what was inside the bag, Mr. Tang said, "It's someone else's bag. I didn't open it. Whoever owns it can come and claim it."

At around 10 PM last night, outside the "Changshengyu Yangrou Pao" store on Shidi Street in Jiankang City, 40-year-old owner Tang Yujun was standing aside, puzzled when a group of people suddenly returned to the store holding a plaque. It turned out that it was because of his act of picking up the bag.

That afternoon, a friend of Mr. Zeng decided to try visiting the "Changshengyu Yangrou Pao" store, only to find the bag had been kept there all along. Mr. Zeng immediately asked his friend to take out the cash from the bag to reward Mr. Tang, but Mr. Tang firmly refused.

"I immediately noticed that the bag contained 50,000 yuan in cash, but more importantly, it also contained securities worth over 8 million yuan, as well as business accounts and a driver's license," Mr. Zeng said, making phone calls everywhere trying to locate the bag without success. "At the time, I told my friends that whoever could help me find the bag would get the 50,000 yuan inside."

Mr. Zeng, who does mining business, recounted the process of losing the bag yesterday. On the morning of the 8th, after eating at "Changshengyu Yangrou Pao", he hurriedly returned to Dongwei. In the rush to pay quickly and save time, he only realized later that he had lost his bag.
