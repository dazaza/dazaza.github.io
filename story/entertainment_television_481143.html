"The New Story of a Return to Zhujie" Episode 71 is popular, with shocking lines. Netizens: Too revealing._

by nemo on 2011-08-24 16:04:12

The new version of "The Story of A Return to Pearl" has drawn ridicule from all sides after the horse-riding confession scene of Er Kang and Zi Wei. The other night, this masterpiece of the 21st century exploded with another melodramatic moment. When Wu Ge Yong Qi was explaining the meaning of "consummating a marriage" to two women in the drama, he used phrases like "no intention and no strength" and "with all one's heart and strength", which made people imagine all sorts of things.

The plot on the night of Yong Qi and Xin Rong's wedding was described as follows: Gui Ma told the two to consummate their marriage. At this time, Yong Qi, who had only Xia Yan Zi in his heart, said to Xin Rong: "Let me tell you, some men have the intention but no strength, some men have no intention but have strength, the best is those who have both intention and strength, and I am the worst kind, having no intention and no strength!" Then he ran to Xia Yan Zi early in the morning to explain that he and Xin Rong did not consummate their marriage. Xia Yan Zi curiously asked: "What is consummating a marriage? Does it mean that the house becomes round instead of square when you get married?" Yong Qi then said: "When we consummate our marriage, I will explain, and when we consummate our marriage, I will definitely have the intention and strength, and will do my best!"

The popular TV series "The New Story of A Return to Pearl" currently airing on Hunan TV, last night played out the stunning wedding scene of Yong Qi and Princess Xin Rong, pushing the emotional entanglement between Yong Qi, Xin Rong, Benjamin, and Xia Yan Zi in the palace to a climax. Regarding this wedding scene where some are happy and others are sad, Kan Qingzi, who plays Princess Xin Rong, frankly admitted that if she encountered similar situations in real life, she would definitely choose someone who loves her.

Thirteen years ago, in "The Story of A Return to Pearl", the love stories of Yong Qi and Xia Yan Zi, and Er Kang and Zi Wei shook the screen and repeatedly set viewing records. Thirteen years later, in "The New Story of A Return to Pearl" written by Qiong Yao herself, three new characters were added - Xin Rong, Benjamin, and Yu Fei, testing the feelings between Yong Qi and Xia Yan Zi. Especially Princess Xin Rong played by Kan Qingzi, as predicted by Qiong Yao, "She will definitely grab your heart," countless viewers have repeatedly exclaimed "It's really too heartbreaking."

In order to shoot this grand wedding scene well, the production team of "The New Story of A Return to Pearl" put in a lot of effort, shooting several nights continuously to achieve satisfactory results. Now, as an ordinary audience member watching her own performance, Kan Qingzi can't help but feel conflicted, "A painful marriage is about to begin." She said, "In such a society, actually Xin Rong is also a victim. She grew up learning feudal rites and teachings, in her concept there is no 'love', only the commands of parents and arranged marriages. What she needs to do is follow the arrangements of her elders and be a filial daughter."

Talking about her views on love, Kan Qingzi frankly said that if she were Xin Rong, she wouldn't make that choice. "If it were me, I would choose someone who loves me. Girls all yearn for romantic and passionate love, but when it happens, it just happens, not necessarily with great external conditions, as long as the two people's temperaments match, and they can live happily and peacefully together."
