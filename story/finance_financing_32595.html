Column introduction: This year, have you really felt the continuous rise in the price of daily necessities? As the year draws to a close, let's summarize the price increases of this year and predict the trends for next year.

When prices rise

By: Cai Nv

Introduction: The topic of price hikes has been discussed for more than half a year now, from the initial increase in daily food items to utility fees and mooncakes, then to rent, land prices, and wedding banquet costs. Although the word "price increase" has been widely discussed, have you really noticed the price increases in your daily life? As we approach the end of the year, let's take stock of this year's accounts together, calculate ways to manage finances amidst rising prices, and prepare adequately for next year's potential price increases.

Calculating the cost of price increases

This year's wave of price increases began around April, with a chain reaction of rising prices in pork, cooking oil, eggs, and other food ingredients like dominoes falling one after another. This was followed by media questioning, price drops after increases, and price rises in other types of goods.

This topic has been discussed for over half a year now, and the list of items that have increased in price has grown longer. However, surprisingly, when Cai Nv chatted with some office ladies about "price increases," they gave an unexpected response: "Price increases? It doesn't affect me much, I just noticed that the boxed meals downstairs at the company have gone up in price." Despite the aggressive price increases, they only seem to have left a slight ripple on the surface of the water for these white-collar women, which Cai Nv finds very interesting.

The reason why young women feel little impact from price increases is not because the prices haven't risen enough, but because the items affected are related to household essentials such as firewood, rice, oil, and salt. For working women who are busy with their jobs, the prices of clothing and cosmetics are far more important than the prices of items affected by the price increases. Cai Nv invited a newly married young housewife and a white-collar employee living with her parents to separately write down the items whose price increases have impacted their lives, hoping that readers can objectively understand the different impacts of price increases on different women from these two tables.
