Course Introduction

Saujana Golf and Country Club

Among the numerous golf courses in Kuala Lumpur, the most famous is undoubtedly Saujana Golf and Country Club. It is only a half-hour drive from the city center and features two 18-hole championship courses: Palm Course and Bunga Raya Course. The former is also known as Cobra, while the latter is known as Crocodile. As suggested by their names, both courses present extremely challenging "killing trenches"! Among the two, the Cobra course is the most renowned, having been selected by Asian Golf Monthly as the best championship course in Asia. The annual Malaysian Open is held here.

Awana Genting Highlands Golf & Country Club

Located at an altitude of 3,100 feet, Awana Genting Highlands Golf & Country Club offers players the exhilarating experience of swinging through the clouds. This 18-hole international standard course features undulating terrain with sand traps, ponds, and streams, presenting a challenge to those seeking to break par. The dry cold air and cool environment provide a unique golfing experience at Awana.
