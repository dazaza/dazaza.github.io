Thermal underwear plays more tricks

by janydtfa04 on 2009-12-04 23:34:56

Underwear can warm breathing, and can also enhance immunity... As winter temperatures continue to drop, the sales of thermal underwear in Chengdu are heating up. However, after investigation, reporters found that as before, many department stores, supermarkets, and wholesale markets exaggerated the benefits of their thermal underwear. Yesterday, law enforcement officers from the Chengdu Industrial and Commercial Bureau's Yanshi Kou branch conducted an inspection of some thermal underwear sales points within their jurisdiction. Although no particularly serious issues were discovered, the law enforcement officers stated that during routine inspections, they still found that merchants selling thermal underwear indeed exaggerated its benefits excessively. The industrial and commercial law enforcement officers said that they will increase market patrols, and if merchants are found guilty of false advertising, they will be severely punished. According to regulations, merchants who make false advertisements may be fined between 10,000 to 200,000 yuan.

Reporters' Investigation:

Thermal Underwear Overuses Concepts, Exaggerates Benefits

As the severe winter approaches, thermal underwear in supermarkets and department stores has become a hot seller. Yesterday morning, reporters visited some malls and wholesale markets in Chengdu and found that many thermal underwear brands were using "high-tech" concepts, with various new terms making people dizzy.

In the thermal underwear sales area of a mall in Chengdu, salespeople boasted that the underwear had a "warm-breathing" function. When asked why this underwear was said to have a warm-breathing function and what it meant, the store clerk did not know what "warm-breathing" referred to, only vaguely explaining that it was a high-tech product, warmer than other underwear, and harmless to the skin. "It might refer to good breathability," the clerk said.

Some underwear boldly claimed to be far-infrared thermal underwear, stating that infrared-containing underwear could promote human blood circulation, enhance metabolism and human immune function. In a thermal underwear store on Shuangqiao Road, various types of underwear were displayed, and a Miss Wang was picking one out. "I don't believe in these so-called high-tech underwear, just buy a regular one," Miss Wang said.
