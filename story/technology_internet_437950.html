Naked Marriage: Feng Xiaogang appears at the premiere of "The Great Earthquake", pays tribute to the citizens of Tangshan with three cups of wine - Chinese film

by letaoehv on 2011-05-27 11:10:04

Feng Xiaogang shows up to "hand in his homework"

Speaking plainly about the movie, Feng Xiaogang said, "I've said all I want to say in the movie. Today I am here to hand in my homework. The era of Naked Marriage is over and the homework is complete. Whether or not this movie hits home is up to the audience to decide." Feng Xiaogang also said, "I especially want to thank the friends around me who helped me finish this movie. I also especially want to thank those who gave us tremendous support."

Subsequently, Feng Xiaogang suggested that everyone should drink three cups of wine. Feng Xiaogang said, "The first cup is dedicated to the 240,000 Tangshan compatriots who perished in the earthquake. May they rest in peace. The second cup is dedicated to the witnesses and survivors of the earthquake and the heroes who reached out to help during the Tangshan earthquake. The third cup of wine specially thanks Feng Xiaogang for his contributions to the movie, as noted by the Tangshan city leaders."

Chen Daoming said, "This is a commemorative movie made so we can forget. Everyone worked very hard, it was very cold, and our hearts were very weary. I hope everyone goes home and has a good dream." Zhang Jingchu said emotionally, "There's one line from the script that really stuck with me: 'It's not that I don't remember, it's that I can't forget.' I must honor my promise to my loved ones and live on well. Playing a daughter of Tangshan in this movie was a great comfort to me." Li Chen then told Zhang Jingchu in Tangshan dialect, "Do you know what Mom said? 'You don't know what you have until it's gone.' After watching the movie, go home and visit your parents often."

On the evening of July 12th, the premiere of the movie "Tangshan Earthquake" was held in Tangshan. After the film finished playing, director Feng Xiaogang appeared with the entire cast and bowed deeply to the audience. Finally, Feng Xiaogang toasted everyone with three cups of water wine.

Xu Fan full of gratitude

Xu Fan, who plays the mother in the movie, received thunderous applause from the audience upon her appearance. She said, "During the filming of this movie, I was always full of gratitude. I thank my mother for raising me and I thank my children. All I want to say is, I love you!"
