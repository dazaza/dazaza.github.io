Scribbls: Infuse your associations into the brush

by diwowo on 2008-06-13 00:40:02

Scribbls should be classified as a drawing community, different from drawing game sharing platforms like Xsketch. Here, all the drawings are full of association and inspiration. By "drawing", it doesn't mean artistic masterpieces like the "Mona Lisa". Instead, they are very simple pencil sketches, even a bit "crude". Scribbls advocates associative creative drawing. Based on the two provided pictures, make associations, unleash your creativity, and draw a related third picture to form a set. Here you can see many inspiration-filled associative drawings, and you can't help but admire these people's associative abilities. If you like, you can offer your "love" and cast your vote.
