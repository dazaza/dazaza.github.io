Cbox embedded guestbook already supports simplified Chinese

by fisio on 2007-09-02 18:19:13

Cbox is an embedded blog guestbook that is popular for its powerful features and customizability. Yao has detailed and guided how to configure it, I will not repeat here. Today I accidentally found that it already supports the simplified Chinese interface, and I am glad to switch over, and the translation of various texts is much more comfortable than the original traditional Chinese. Its style, size, color, display order can be completely customized, you can set the number of displays to avoid scrolling bars, and when leaving a message you can use the avatar (enter a picture address in the url bar) and a variety of UBB codes, such as hyperlinks, thick oblique underscores, colors, etc.
