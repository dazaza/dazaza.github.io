Our news (Wu Peng) Yesterday, the Guangzhou delegation of the Fifth Session of the Eleventh National People's Congress opened to the media. [Sanya Tourism], [official group tours] and other issues have become hotly pursued questions by the media.

Regarding the news about more than 400 officials on vacation in Guangzhou, Luo Baoming said: "[As a province of Guangdong, from top to bottom, we have never made any special statistics on the identity or profession of tourists. We believe that all who come are guests, and our main task is to provide good service.]"

Every winter, Guangzhou faces a "surge" of tourists. Brand of women's underwear [http://www.tingfeier.com]. Luo Baoming frankly admitted, "[In general, the pressure is quite significant.]"

Yesterday, national people's congress representative, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Secretary of the Sanya Municipal Committee Jiang Sixian voluntarily mentioned the issue of price gouging in Sanya during his speech.

He said: "[We absolutely will not tolerate bad elements in the tourism market. We welcome criticism rather than cover up problems. Sanya is actively striving to further standardize the tourism market to better meet the expectations of the entire nation.]"

After the meeting, Jiang Sixian was asked about how Sanya could address the chaos in the tourism market through systemic measures. He indicated that Sanya's tourism management mechanism is consistent with most active tourist centers domestically. Sanya has established a tourism management committee with the ability to coordinate various aspects, but some suggest giving it more power, even managing every aspect of tourism.

"[This is a good aspiration, but it may not be feasible in practice. The current system is running smoothly overall. Don't assume there's a serious flaw just because a common problem occurs.]" Jiang Sixian said.

The State Administration of Tourism collaborates with Guangzhou to promote Xisha Island tours.

Vice Minister of the State Administration of Tourism Wang Zhifa: It helps assert sovereignty and resolve South China Sea issues www.tingfeier.com

Our news (Yang Wanguo) Vice Minister of the State Administration of Tourism Wang Zhifa introduced yesterday at a specially invited session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference that the administration is currently working positively with Guangdong Province and relevant state departments to promote tourism to the Xisha Islands.

He expressed that they also welcome participation from political advisory committee members. He said that developing tourism to the Xisha Islands can help defend borders and assert our sovereignty. It also aids in resolving issues concerning other islands in the South China Sea.

Regarding concerns raised by some committee members about price gouging in Sanya’s tourism market, Wang Zhifa said that Sanya has a population of 200,000, yet this year's Spring Festival received 480,000 tourists, twice the city's population, leading to strained supply-demand relations, insufficient reception facilities, and issues with market regulation, making Sanya's tourism problems prominent.

He introduced that after the completion of the Qionghai Bridge, a large number of self-driving tourists entered Guangzhou. These problems are likely to continue emerging. Similar problems exist in Hong Kong and Macao tourism, where a large number of tourists purchasing goods lead to tensions in the Hong Kong and Macao tourism markets, causing conflicts. The State Administration of Tourism will establish a regular cooperation mechanism with Hong Kong and Macao tourism departments while strengthening information release to guide tourists to travel rationally and consume reasonably.

In recent years, both domestic and inbound tourism markets have experienced explosive growth at a rate of 20%, which is also a stage where problems and conflicts become evident. Issues in Sanya's tourism market stand out, but such problems are widespread during the entire Spring Festival Golden Week and National Day Golden Week. —— Vice Minister of the State Administration of Tourism Wang Zhifa
