Currently, the neighborhood committee of the community and the Xinhua Property Management Company both claimed that they were unaware of the construction party responsible for the wall but stated that they would help inquire and investigate.

■ The passage of Xinyuan Community was blocked, causing "deadlocks" even outside of rush hours

Near the north fence gate of Xinyuan Community in Shijiazhuang, a cement wall about 7 meters wide stood in the middle of the road, with only a gap of less than 1 meter left for residents to pass through. It was learned that this wall was built on the night of February 25th.

Between this cement wall and the north fence gate of the community, there was a kindergarten and a foreign language training school. Upon discovering the new wall, many parents picking up their children gathered here.

"Why build a wall on such a good road? Just looking at it makes me feel upset. During rush hours and when children are dismissed from school, it gets terribly congested here," said Ms. Xie, an owner in the community. She added that they had never received any notice regarding the construction of the wall before. "Even if it's inconvenient for travel, what if there's a fire or something? With the wall here, the fire truck can't get in at all." Some residents worriedly said.

A staff member surnamed Xie from the Xinyuan Community Neighborhood Committee said that they did not know who built the wall. "However, we will help find out."

Mr. Jia, the manager of Xinhua Property Management Company, stated that the property management did not build the wall, and they also did not know who the construction party was. "Previously, there were cases of car thefts in Xinyuan Community. Among the five gates of the community, only this gate did not have security personnel on duty." Manager Jia speculated that someone might have built the wall to prevent car thefts.
