The Healthiest Whole Grain Weight Loss Method: In your regular white rice, add a small amount of whole grains rich in dietary fiber, such as wheat, millet, or sorghum. You can cook this using a regular electric rice cooker, which helps with weight loss. Even if you eat the same amount as usual, the components in whole grains can regulate gastrointestinal function, promote cell activity, and enhance metabolism. What types of whole grains are there generally? Whole grains mainly include corn, millet, black rice, oats, barley, buckwheat, bran, and various dried beans like soybeans, green beans, red beans, and mung beans. There are typically three categories:

1. Cereal Grains: Corn, millet, red rice, black rice, purple rice, sorghum, barley, oats, buckwheat/bran, etc.

2. Miscellaneous Beans: Soybeans, green beans, red beans, black beans, broad beans, cowpeas, peas, etc.

3. Tubers: Sweet potatoes, yams, potatoes, etc.
