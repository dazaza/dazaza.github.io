News Follow-up

Two nights ago, little Wu cried while writing an article, which Mr. Wu then forwarded to the reporter. The article reads: "After the teacher praised me that day, I didn't dare to step out of the classroom. I heard some classmates mocking and discussing it, and I felt very hurt... Actually, this is a very instinctive thing. Since I was young, I've heard my parents say: my grandma, grandmother-in-law, and grandfather-in-law all fell down once and were kindly helped up by good Samaritans. However, the second time my grandma fainted on the ground and no one helped her up, she passed away right there, leaving my father's family in great pain and regret. Although I did not witness these events, they are deeply imprinted in my mind... Everyone's home has elderly people, everyone will grow old, and everyone should do this (saving lives)."

Mr. Wu stated that at the time, little Wu's uncle was driving her past. He learned from little Wu's uncle that he sent the unconscious lady to the entrance of Guangzhou Red Cross Hospital, where she had already regained consciousness. Her uncle wanted to take her to the hospital, but she refused to see a doctor at all costs. In the end, little Wu's uncle could only help her hail a taxi and let her leave. "My sister scolded my brother-in-law at the time, saying he might have been deceived."

Classmates Laughed at Little Wu for "Showing Off Wealth" and She Didn't Want to Go to School

"The Second Middle School Girl's" father called the reporter, stating that their family education emphasizes helping others. The driver who saved the person was actually the girl's uncle.

Yesterday, the father of the "Second Middle School Girl," Mr. Wu, contacted our newspaper and reflected that after his daughter did a good deed, the teacher praised her actions. The school authorities also expressed that they are considering praising her again at the semester-end graduation ceremony. But at the same time, some of her friends did not support her. Little Wu therefore endured a certain amount of psychological pressure, feeling emotionally low and not wanting to go to school.

"No matter how rich we are, the car doesn't belong to our family," he explained, saying that the car was actually newly purchased by his sister, and the person who helped save someone that day was actually his brother-in-law, Little Wu's uncle. He indicated that because his sister is in business, none of them want to come forward.

The money was given by Little Wu herself.

"A few days ago, I read the report, and my brother-in-law said he didn't give money to the unconscious lady. We asked our daughter again, and we found out that she gave the 500 yuan meant for buying clothes to the woman herself." The reporter reviewed the video, and indeed, it was Little Wu who, after the unconscious lady got into the car, took the money out of her own pocket, handed it to the woman's clothing pocket, and then closed the car door. Mr. Wu expressed concern that classmates might resent his daughter more since she gave 500 yuan to a stranger, so much so that he hoped it could be considered as money from her uncle.

Dad Never Expected Such Great Love from His Daughter

Yesterday afternoon, a man claiming to be the parent of the "Second Middle School Girl" contacted our newspaper. After verification by the school teacher, it was indeed Little Wu's father, Mr. Wu. Mr. Wu mentioned over the phone that saving lives was originally a small matter, although it received high praise from public opinion, these few days have troubled their family, especially his daughter Little Wu.

According to Mr. Wu's introduction, the reason why his daughter got out of the car to save someone was not without cause. Before this, three elders in Little Wu's family had fallen ill suddenly on the street and collapsed, fortunately being helped by kind-hearted people and thus saved. However, later on, one of the relatives, aged 56, due to coronary heart disease, fainted again on the road without receiving timely assistance and ultimately passed away unfortunately. Therefore, in the Wu family's education, there is a strong emphasis on cultivating the habit of helping others, especially helping those who fall on the ground, to prevent them from inhaling "earth qi." Talking about his daughter, Mr. Wu said she is usually very loving, but seeing her actually help the unconscious woman on Bao Ye Road was unexpected even for him.

Facing Passersby with a Bit of Helplessness, Carrying the Woman onto the Car

Mr. Wu said that his daughter usually doesn't talk much at home, has an introverted personality, but gets along well with others, and others' evaluations of her are also good. However, his daughter has another side: she likes dressing up, wears fashionable clothes, and also likes showcasing herself on Weibo and communicating with netizens. According to what Mr. Wu knows, in reality, there are boys who express affection for Little Wu, so he disciplines his daughter strictly, picking her up and dropping her off every day, and not allowing her to leave home after returning, which unconsciously makes her feel very suppressed. Nevertheless, Mr. Wu said that his daughter is also quite hardworking in practicing Taekwondo, can get injured, and is actually persevering and capable of enduring hardship.

Mr. Wu also said that after the incident occurred, his daughter had some misunderstandings about the informant Ms. Lei. With the help of our newspaper, Ms. Lei interacted and communicated with Little Wu. "Ms. Lei told my daughter, those who criticize you, they may say such things verbally, but deep down, they still admire you." This sentence made my daughter feel much more relieved. Ms. Lei also confirmed that: "After chatting with Little Wu, the five hundred yuan she brought back, and the three thousand yuan reward I received, she still wants to donate."

Mr. Wu claimed that the reason his daughter reached out to help was due to a previous incident where a family member fainted on the road and did not receive timely assistance, resulting in death. Thus, when she encountered similar situations, she would act proactively, never expecting to be misunderstood by people. At the same time, he also expressed that the Porsche does not belong to their family but to his sister. The person who helped save someone that day was actually Little Wu's uncle. However, his sister and brother-in-law, due to business reasons, do not wish to reveal themselves publicly and prefer not to comment much on this matter.
