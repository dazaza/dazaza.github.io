"Narcissus" tea is absolutely a purely natural organic tea. This is not just a gimmick; it is determined by its own growing environment and conditions. Any Zhangping person knows that Narcissus tea is extremely easy to manage. Why not use chemical fertilizers? This is not only due to the demand for green quality but also decided by the inherent quality of Narcissus tea itself: once it comes into contact with chemical fertilizers and pesticides, the tea color turns yellow, the taste becomes bitter, and in severe cases, the tree may wither and die.

It can be said that the organic components of Narcissus tea cannot be artificially manipulated; they are determined by its inherent nature. This ensures the pure natural organic components of Narcissus tea, which is the fundamental reason why Narcissus tea can maintain excellent sweetness and emit the fragrance of orchids.
