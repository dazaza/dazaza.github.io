Meanwhile, the amount of social donations for little Sun Kai has also been increasing. Liu Wenfang said that during the May Day holiday, a father and daughter from Hejian specifically rushed to Cangzhou to visit Sun Kai. The girl was 12 years old, encouraging Sun Kai to get well soon and recover early. She left behind 600 yuan. Liu Wenfang earnestly asked the child's father to leave his name and contact information, but the other party insisted on not doing so, only revealing that their surname was Miao.

The executive vice president of the Chamber of Commerce, Jin Shujuan, told reporters that Vice President Wu Lanzhi was deeply moved after reading the report about little Sun Kai, thus initiating a donation campaign for Sun Kai. Everyone responded enthusiastically, raising 1800 yuan in one day from several vice presidents of the chamber, including Zhang Jihong, Jin Shujuan, Wu Lanzhi, Xu Shequn, and others. Subsequently, everyone contributed generously, with some giving 300 yuan and others 500 yuan. Ms. Wu Yanfang, who was on a business trip in Guangzhou, specially flew back after receiving the text message from the chamber presidents calling for love and joined the fundraising campaign.

On the eve of Mother's Day, the condition of 11-year-old "aplastic anemia" boy Sun Kai from Cangzhou touched the hearts of 22 women far away in the provincial capital. Representatives of the members of the Qiaodong Women Entrepreneurs Chamber of Commerce delivered 9000 yuan donated by 22 sisters to the newspaper office. They expressed that little Sun Kai was a pitiable yet adorable child. Moved by the reports, they were too busy to visit him personally, so they entrusted the newspaper to deliver this heartfelt gift to Sun Kai's family, hoping it could help with his treatment.

On April 26, Yandu Group sent out the first donation of 5000 yuan to little Sun Kai, and on April 29, another 24000 yuan was transferred into Sun Kai's account. By 18:41 on May 5, Yandu Group netizens had raised a total of 38639 yuan for Sun Kai, each donation filled with blessings and expectations from loving netizens. Everyone hopes that through their own small efforts, they can help Sun Kai change his fate and overcome the illness.

"Thank you all so much. Now there is a total of 252000 yuan in my bank account, plus over 50000 yuan from kind-hearted people and partial cash donations, making the total amount of love donations exceed 300000 yuan," Liu Wenfang said emotionally.

Since our newspaper issued the call to "save little Sun Kai" and initiated the "1 yuan group-buying" charity activity on Yanzhao Urban Network Yandu Group, it has received enthusiastic responses from a large number of netizens and readers.

The amount of donations received by little Sun Kai has exceeded 300000 yuan.

Female entrepreneurs "flew back" to participate in the donation.

During the May Day holiday, a letter from Zhangjiakou arrived at our Cangzhou workstation, expressing concern and wishes for little Sun Kai.

This letter was sent by all the students of Class 15, Accounting Department, Zhangjiakou Vocational Education Center. In the letter, the students wrote: "Little Sun Kai, are you feeling better now? Although the disease has caused you mental suffering, we believe that deep down, you should be an optimistic and strong little boy, right? Don't feel lonely because we are always watching over you from afar. We wish to accompany you through difficulties and listen to your growth stories."

The envelope also contained a letter written by the head teacher of Class 15. She wrote: "Sun Kai, when you get better, let your parents take you to Zhangjiakou to play. When we have holidays, we will go find you, bringing Zhangjiakou specialties and many gifts. This is our promise. I want to tell you, you must be strong. Believe that all of us will help you. What we all hope to see most is your bright smile. Behind you, there are countless uncles, aunts, brothers, and sisters supporting and encouraging you. Be ambitious!"
