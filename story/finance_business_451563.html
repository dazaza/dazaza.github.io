Beijing Sharp TV Repair Center 29S21-A1 TV Maintenance Case 1

TV Model: Sharp 29S21-A1 TV

Fault Symptoms: After turning on the power, there is no raster on the screen, no image, no sound, and neither the red power operation indicator light nor the yellow standby indicator light is lit.

Analysis and Maintenance: When this machine works normally, the red power indicator D1102 should always be lit. At this time, +15V voltage acts on the base of the PNP transistor Q1014 through R1063, causing Q1014 to cut off, and the yellow standby indicator D1101 goes out. If the yellow standby indicator lights up, then Q1014 conducts, the +15V voltage disappears, the Zener diode D726 cuts off, and the relay RY201 releases its contacts to open, cutting off the AC supply of the main switch power supply, and the line output stage stops working. Due to the disappearance of the reverse line pulse of Q753, D751 cuts off, the relay RY751 releases its contacts to open, cutting off the AC supply of the secondary switch power supply, and the red power operation indicator goes out. The entire power system only has the microprocessor IC1O01 control power circuit working (power consumption about 3W), and the TV enters the set standby working state. Since neither the red nor the yellow indicator lights of this machine are lit, it indicates that the main switch power supply and the secondary switch power supply have not worked, and the microprocessor control dedicated power circuit is also abnormal. Measuring the emitter of the standby controlled tube Q1014 reveals no +12V operating voltage, leading to the conclusion that the fault is caused by an anomaly in the remote control dedicated stabilized power supply. Checking the adjustment tube Q750 reveals a DC voltage of 18V at its collector (normal), but measuring its base shows no 12.7V reference voltage. Further checking R706, D754, C754 reveals that resistor R706 has changed value and opened.

Handling Method: After replacing R706, the fault was eliminated.
