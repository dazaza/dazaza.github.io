Synopsis:

Neil McCauley and Chris Shiherlis lead a team that robs a truck full of bonds. Rookie Wayne Tippett kills a guard during the heist. McCauley tries to kill Tippett to silence him but fails as Tippett escapes.

Detective Vincent Hanna, who is working on the case, is also facing another marital crisis. Despite this, he throws himself into solving the case. However, McCauley's meticulous actions leave few clues for investigation.

As the police conduct their investigation, McCauley and his gang are planning to sell the stolen bonds back to the original owner, Van Zant. However, they fall into Van Zant's pre-set trap and only manage to escape after a fierce battle.

Through an informant's tip-off, Hanna discovers that Chris is one of the robbers. The police keep them under surveillance and soon uncover the next robbery target of McCauley and his gang - a metal warehouse. Meanwhile, McCauley meets a female designer named Eady, who knows nothing about his criminal activities. They fall in love at first sight, and McCauley decides to move to Australia with Eady after the warehouse job.

The police set up an ambush around the metal warehouse, but the experienced McCauley notices something unusual and hastily cancels the operation. Undeterred, the gang decides to rob a bank instead.

The next day, Hanna finds McCauley and hopes he will turn over a new leaf. That night, the gang manages to shake off the pursuit and disappear, but loses their inside man Trejo during the escape. When the police arrive after the robbery, a fierce gunfight ensues, resulting in heavy casualties for McCauley's gang.

McCauley, having narrowly survived, resolves to thoroughly investigate the leak. He captures Trejo and learns that Wayne Tippett and Van Zant were responsible for the betrayal. McCauley kills Van Zant. But just as he is preparing to leave with Eady overnight, he discovers Tippett's hiding place. Knowing the location is heavily guarded, McCauley still resolutely moves to assassinate Tippett. As he exits the hotel, Hanna intercepts him. After a chase, McCauley is shot and falls.
