On September 1st, a reporter met Li Teqiang at a hospital in Xiangtan City. The doctor introduced that Li currently had multiple soft tissue contusions and was also experiencing hematuria (blood in urine).

"Truly, what you think of comes to pass," riding his motorcycle to the field, Chen Jian discovered a black goat lying on its side without breathing. Looking up, not far away, a car was parked, and Chen immediately chased after it. "The car didn't have a license plate; the emblem was 'four circles,' and there was a very long gun mounted on the passenger seat." Chen Jian estimated the weight of the gun, "It weighs at least 30 kilograms, and it's equipped with infrared and a silencer. The bullets are steel pellets."

"The air rifle was bought to kill rats"

Villagers took advantage of the situation to flip out his work ID, "Li Teqiang, station master of the Xiangtan toll station on the Tan-Shao Expressway." This made everyone even angrier, shouting, "Beat the sheep thief to death." Seeing he was outnumbered, the car driver called the police at 110. After the police arrived at the scene, they found two pigeons killed by the air rifle and two sets of license plates inside the Audi car, one of which was a military plate.

Upon hearing about the capture of the sheep thief, hundreds of villagers rushed over. Some villagers said that the car was an Audi A6, "very expensive." Hearing that the driver was using a luxury car and carrying an air rifle to steal sheep, the villagers' resentment turned into a barrage of punches and kicks, causing the driver to fall to the ground.

The Station Master Received Three Punishments

Zhaotan Village in Yuhu District of Xiangtan City is adjacent to the Xiang River, with lush water grass, and almost every household raises a group of black goats. However, in the past six months, as more people have come to "hang around" in the village, the villagers' goats have either become lame or died. "They were all shot by air rifles," the villagers said. Each time they encountered someone carrying an air rifle, they would ask them questions, "They always say they're here to hunt birds."

After Li was taken away by the police, several people claiming to be Li's colleagues came to "resolve the issue." "They compensated the Chen family with over 2000 yuan. If it wasn't his sheep, why would he compensate?" For this, the villagers raised such doubts.

On the morning of September 1st, the management office of the Tan-Shao Expressway presented a meeting summary from the director of the Jiuhua Branch of the Xiangtan Public Security Bureau, He Haiqiang, and the chief of the Jiuhua Police Station, Wang Zhenqiu, who met with the office on August 31st. The summary showed: the investigating officers did not find any evidence that Li Teqiang used a gun to shoot sheep. Moreover, when the officers returned to the villagers to collect witness testimony, the dead sheep had already been cooked by the villagers. Currently, Li is defined as "illegally carrying controlled instruments," and has been fined 500 yuan. Due to Li's injuries, he has not yet been detained.

Suspension, Self-examination, Fine

Currently, the incident is still under further investigation.

On August 28th, people driving cars to "hunt birds" in the village increased again. Around 4 PM, Chen Jian, worried that his own sheep might be shot, got up from his nap and went to check the fields.

Punishment

"The car had a very long gun mounted on it"

The Director of the Toll Collection Inspection Department of the Tan-Shao Expressway Management Office, Mr. Gao, said that the management office has already made a disciplinary decision regarding Li's illegal carrying of controlled instruments: suspension, self-examination throughout the entire system, and a cash fine of 2000 yuan.

When asked about the purpose of buying the gun, Li said that there were too many rats at the toll station, so he spent over 2000 yuan to buy an air rifle near the Shuifumiao Service Area specifically to kill rats. Regarding the compensation paid by his colleagues to the Chen family and the military license plate in the car, Li said, "That was to prevent the situation from escalating. The military plate was confiscated and placed in the car but never submitted."

Li believed that his experience on August 28th was entirely due to the villagers' misunderstanding. "I've been in a bad mood recently, so I borrowed a friend's car to go to the riverbank for relaxation. I only shot two birds and absolutely did not harm the villagers' sheep. The location where I was surrounded by the villagers was one kilometer away from where the sheep fell, and the gun couldn't reach that far. Furthermore, there was a tranquilizer needle in the body of the dead sheep, and I don't have such equipment."
