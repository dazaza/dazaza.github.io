This article comes from Taobao 8848 http://www.8848.cm Chapter Eight Sir, can you take me home? Ou Jingze turned around, his warm and handsome face tinged with a hint of drunkenness. "What is it, little beggar?" His voice was slightly hoarse but melodious. Especially in the quiet autumn night, it carried an indescribable charm.

But Su Yixuan didn't find it pleasant at all. All her attention was on the word 'little beggar'. Do I look like a beggar? She looked down at her somewhat dirty tracksuit, her hair sticking together due to being wet, and the traces of sewage still on her hands.

Alright, she admitted that she did look a bit disheveled, but even so, how could she be compared to a beggar! Annoyed, she glared at him and opened her mouth to retort, but remembering her purpose, she quickly put on a smile. "Sir, can you take me home?"

Ou Jingze cast her a suspicious glance. Although he was indeed drunk, his mind was very clear. "I remember there are only three villas up the mountain; are you from one of them?" He looked at Su Yixuan's dirty appearance, his eyes flashing with disbelief.

Rich and arrogant! Su Yixuan muttered under her breath, but for the sake of her safety, she decided to ignore Ou Jingze's gaze.

"Uh, that's not what I mean," she hesitated for a while before muttering, "I mean, take me back to your place."

"Back to my place?" Ou Jingze repeated blankly, his gentle yet confused eyes quickly flashing with a cold gleam. "I don't need special services."

Special services?

What nonsense?

Uh, wouldn't he mean... At the thought of the implication behind his words, both shock and anger surged in Su Yixuan's heart. "Hey, what do you mean by that? I'm not the kind of woman you think I am. I just lost my home, so I had no choice but to come up with this plan. If you don't agree, fine, I'll sleep on the streets tonight. Anyway, I'm a black belt expert, I'm not afraid!"

Ou Jingze stared at her for a while, then turned around, opened the car door, and prepared to get in. His movements were graceful and efficient, without any hesitation.

Seeing him turn away without a second thought, Su Yixuan gritted her teeth in anger. What's so great about driving a sports car!

So arrogant, with not a shred of compassion!

Although she despised Ou Jingze's behavior in her heart, she also felt ashamed of herself for clinging onto him like this. But given her current situation, she could only try to latch onto Ou Jingze, this super important figure. Yes, she was a black belt expert, but after all, she was just a girl. If she encountered several thugs, wouldn't she be in serious trouble!

Therefore, when she saw Ou Jingze opening the car door and preparing to leave, she immediately ran to the car door and pressed her hand against the closing door. "Sir, are you really going to leave like this?"

Ou Jingze raised his eyebrows slightly and quietly looked at Su Yixuan. Although his gaze was gentle, it inexplicably made Su Yixuan feel a sense of authority. Yes, authority. As someone who practiced martial arts, she valued authority the most and was also the most sensitive to it. So, when Ou Jingze looked at her like this, Su Yixuan actually felt somewhat uneasy. But for the sake of having a place to stay, she decided to cling to him.

"I really have nowhere to go. All my money has been stolen. Sir, consider it as doing charity, let me stay over for one night. After tonight, I won't bother you anymore." Su Yixuan said pathetically, "You know, I'm just a fragile girl, wandering on the street so late at night. It's easy to get into trouble. The society today is so unsafe, with thugs, ruffians, perverts everywhere. Sir, can you bear to see such a beautiful girl get harmed?"

Ou Jingze furrowed his brows lightly, seemingly digesting Su Yixuan's long speech. After a long pause, he finally spoke. "Why should I let you stay?"
