30 Life Tips To Make You A Better Person

by ib9b8x3m8m1 on 2012-02-26 09:36:20

In life, there are many small methods that can make you more beautiful, perhaps you haven't noticed them before. However, today we will have a lesson on popularizing daily beautiful living methods. The following thirty lifestyle beauty tips will make you more beautiful day by day.

1. Become a butt genius

Want to restore elasticity to your sagging buttocks? Apply sea salt shower cream evenly to your buttocks, pinch the excess flesh with your fingers and massage from bottom to top for 20 times, then rinse with warm water. Doing this regularly will yield quite good results!

2. Overnight tea can lengthen eyelashes

Cool down the leftover tea water, and before bedtime or early in the morning, use a cotton swab to moisten your eyelashes, which can help lengthen them. Try it out! It's guaranteed to be the most cost-effective and economical "eyelash growth serum". Also, applying a used teabag hot on your eyes can reduce dark circles!

3. Rubber bands are great helpers for nail trimming

Nails easily fall all over the floor when trimming. If you wrap a toothpick with a rubber band and insert it into the opening of the nail clipper, the nails will stick to the rubber band and won't fall everywhere.

4. Shoe maintenance techniques

When you need to store shoes for a season without wearing them, first brush the shoes clean with a shoe brush, apply plenty of leather care oil or shoe polish evenly, then stuff newspapers inside the shoes, put them in shoe boxes, and add a few packets of desiccant. This way, your cute shoes won't become hard or moldy.

5. Maintenance technique for blowing balloons to lose weight

When it comes to losing weight, everyone knows the most effective method is continuous exercise. However, most girls are too lazy to move. In fact, just blowing balloons such a simple action can also help lose weight! Blowing 50 balloons a day consumes as much body fat as running for 10 minutes! Amazing, right? Try it every day if you want to lose weight!

6. Secret recipe for gaining weight

Too thin and want to gain weight? Mix milk with Yakult in a ratio of about 3:2, and drink it immediately! Remember, don't leave it too long, otherwise, it will ferment and produce thick sediment. Drinking a few times a day will bring unexpected successful weight gain experiences!

7. No more smelly feet

Do your feet smell terrible after school or work? Cut off a section of old pantyhose, place some baking soda inside, and it can eliminate shoe odors. Your socks and feet won't smell bad anymore!

8. Dehumidification with charcoal

Do your clothes in the wardrobe often feel damp? You can wrap charcoal in newspaper and place it in the wardrobe to serve as a dehumidifier.

9. Become a flat-bellied beauty

Stop complaining about the unsightly flab on your belly! Quickly apply sea salt shower cream to your abdomen and massage vigorously until it heats up. Then wrap it with cling film for 30 minutes, and rinse with warm water. Doing this frequently will give birth to the most charming tummy ever!

10. Weight loss tips

First, mix one teaspoon of green tea powder with warm water, then pour in Yakult or yogurt and consume the mixture. Eating it after each meal is especially effective. Not only does it beautify and aid digestion, but it also helps with weight loss!

11. Become a beautiful-legged girl

Lift your legs high, apply sea salt shower cream evenly on your calves, clench both hands into fists and rub upwards from the heels for 20 times. Continue rubbing until it heats up, then wrap it with cling film. After 10 minutes, rinse with warm water. Keep doing this persistently, and having a pair of beautiful legs will no longer be a dream!

12. Trick to slim down your calves

Don't be lazy. Every night before sleeping, rest your legs against the wall for 30 minutes at a 90-degree angle to your body. Over time, you're guaranteed to have a pair of beautiful legs!

13. Eating yams benefits the skin

Yams are rich in diosgenin (natural DHEA), which contains various hormone base materials and has the effect of promoting endocrine hormone synthesis. When applied to the skin, it can promote cell metabolism, enhance skin hydration, and improve physical constitution.

14. Cherry beauty method

The Classic of Materia Medica records: eating cherries makes people look good and improves their complexion. Cherries are high in iron content, and regular consumption can increase hemoglobin levels, making people look rosy. Carotene and vitamin C help whiten and refine the skin, giving it elasticity.

15. Bruises disappear

Falling down may result in large bruises, which are unsightly and make it difficult to wear short skirts. What to do? Roll a boiled egg over the bruise, and it will soon start to fade. You can try it!

16. Chalk keeps jewelry shiny

Does your jewelry lose its luster over time? Don't worry! Just place a small piece of chalk in your jewelry box, and it will keep your jewelry shiny. What are you waiting for? Try it now!

17. Simple acne treatment

Do you have acne troubles? Go to a Chinese medicine store and buy Cassia seeds. Drink them after dinner every day, and the pimples will disappear the next day. It can also flatten your stomach and reduce your weight!

18. Method to prevent boot shafts from deforming

When storing boots for a season, they often deform because the shafts are too long and lack support at the bottom, making them unshapely the next year. At this point, you can collect the cardboard tubes from toilet paper rolls and use them to prop up short boots. For tall boots, you can use the long paper towel rolls from the kitchen, which are taller! This way, your boots will still look stylish the next year!

19. Toast removes dirt

Stains on collars and cuffs make your clothes unwearable? Coats worn every day easily accumulate dirt on the collars and cuffs. Use thick slices of toast to scrub vigorously, and the stains will come off.

20. Razor removes pills

Are you bothered by pilling on old sweaters? Simply use an ordinary razor to remove the pills from the sweater without damaging it!

21. Expired lotion or whipped cream polishes shoes to shine like new

What use are expired lotions or whipped cream? It's a pity to throw them away, and you're hesitant to use them again. Actually, using them as shoe polish works pretty well! Shoes that have been worn for years will still shine like new after being polished!

22. Tricks to relieve buttock pain

Many office workers or students feel buttock pain after sitting all day, making it uncomfortable to sit. Actually, just stepping on high-heeled shoes with the soles of your feet can improve the situation, or stepping on empty beverage cans. This reduces venous obstruction and promotes blood circulation, making work or study easier!

23. Versatile uses of shampoo

Don't throw away shampoos you don't like, as they are similar to collar cleaners. White socks that can't be washed clean can be entrusted to shampoo. If you have oily hair, add some shampoo when washing pillowcases for better results.

24. Helps soft and smooth hands

Doing housework or washing dishes can cause redness and wrinkles on your hands. Take 3 teaspoons of fine salt and dissolve them in a basin of warm water. Soak your hands for about 5 minutes to help restore soft and smooth hands. To relieve foot fatigue, soak your feet in warm salty water for a few minutes, then rinse with cold water.

25. Removes dead skin

After bathing while your body is still wet, use an appropriate amount of salt to exfoliate the aged skin. This can remove dead keratinized skin (but remember to gently rub only, otherwise it may damage your skin) and promote blood circulation.

26. DIY lip gloss method

Materials: lipstick (unused, remaining, existing), alcohol lamp (or hairdryer), stirring rod, iron spoon, lip balm (or lip oil, petroleum jelly).

Method:

1. First, scoop the lipstick and lip balm onto the spoon using the stirring rod.

2. Heat the spoon over the alcohol lamp or with a hairdryer, and stir evenly with the stirring rod. Remember not to heat for too long.

3. Pour the liquid lip balm into a container while it's still hot, and let it cool and solidify.

27. Silk scarf prevents hair deformation

Every girl might have this concern: after painstakingly styling her hair at the salon, she wakes up the next morning to find it deformed! Actually, this situation is preventable. Just lay a smooth silk scarf on your pillow before sleeping, and this way, your hair won't get messy, and your beautiful hairstyle will be maintained!

28. Lemon bath makes skin whiter

When lemons are in season, they are inexpensive. Buy them often, cut them in half, squeeze out the juice, and add cold water and sugar to drink! But don't discard the squeezed lemon peels; place them in bathroom water, and whether washing your face or taking a bath, your skin will become softer, smoother, and whiter!

29. Solution for menstrual cramps

Boil about 3 to 4 pieces of angelica root, add red dates and brown sugar, and cook for about 30 minutes with an appropriate amount of water. Drinking this not only alleviates menstrual cramps but also helps clean out menstrual blood more thoroughly!

30. Quick removal of dark circles

After waking up, wash your face, and use both hands to perform clockwise circular massages around your eyes for about 5 minutes. This promotes blood circulation under the eyes, and dark circles instantly turn rosy.
