Two post-90s night robbers were identified and killed to cover up the crime

by uelu6c6b on 2012-03-06 08:55:33

Suspect Zhong pointed out the crime scene.

In this case, a teenage girl was found dead with her hands and feet bound, her neck stabbed three times. The body was discovered in the washroom of a training school in Xinfeng County. After receiving the report, the Xinfeng County Public Security Bureau quickly identified the suspects through investigation and interviews. Within three days of the incident, law enforcement officers captured two suspects in a rented house in Dongguan, Guangdong Province.

According to suspect Zhong's confession, they were recognized by the victim during a robbery. He then stabbed the victim three times in the neck with a knife, killing her.

**Incident**

A teenage girl was stabbed three times in the washroom and died.

The victim, Zeng, 20 years old, was a student at a computer training school in Xinfeng County. Recalling what she saw on February 26th still makes her uneasy.

At around 9:00 AM that day, after getting up, Zeng went to the washroom on the fourth floor of the school and found her roommate Lu had been killed. There was a lot of blood on the ground. Panicked, she immediately ran back to the dormitory to report to the school and call the police.

After receiving the report, the Xinfeng County Public Security Bureau attached great importance to the case, forming a special task force and dispatching experienced police officers who quickly arrived at the scene to conduct an investigation. Liu informed the investigating police officers that on the night before the incident, her roommate Lu went to the washroom to brush her teeth and did not return to the dormitory all night.

The police conducted an on-site investigation and found that the school was located in a bustling area of the county. The school was a four-story office building, with classrooms on the second floor, student dormitories on the third floor, and a student washroom on the rooftop of the fourth floor. There were some spattered bloodstains on the walls at the crime scene. The deceased’s neck had been stabbed three times with a sharp object and showed obvious signs of strangulation. Her hands and feet were tied with ropes, but her clothes were relatively neat.

After forensic examination, it was determined that Lu had died from mechanical asphyxiation and hemorrhagic shock, with the time of death estimated to be between 9:30 PM and 10:00 PM on February 25th. Following discussions, members of the special task force believed that the suspects were very likely premeditated criminals familiar with the victim's daily routine.

**Investigation**

Two "post-90s" men were locked down after ruling out sexual assault.

To solve the case as soon as possible, the special task force split into two groups. One group continued to investigate the scene carefully checking every corner; the other investigated the social relationships of the victim.

During their visits and investigations, the special task force learned that the victim was loyal and honest, had no conflicts or disputes with surrounding students, and had relatively simple interpersonal relationships. This virtually ruled out the possibility of retaliatory murder.

The investigating police officers told reporters that the victim's mobile phone and cash were missing. The special task force initially judged that the victim might have been killed during a robbery or sexual assault.

After further investigation, the forensic medical examination results returned to the special task force, confirming that the victim had not been sexually assaulted before her death. "What kind of person would choose a school student as a target? What kind of person would choose the washroom, a place the victim must visit, as the crime scene? What kind of person could be so cruel?" the investigating police officer pondered repeatedly.

After investigation, two men from Ganxian County eventually came into the police's line of sight. It was learned that before the incident, these two men often visited the school and were familiar with the students' schedules there. Further tracking revealed that before the incident, the two men lived in a rented house in Xinfeng County. According to surrounding residents, both men were about 20 years old, sleeping during the day and going out at night, loafing around idly. The police found that after the incident, the two men hadn't checked out of their room but had disappeared, significantly increasing their suspicion for committing the crime.

**Arrest**

Xinfeng police rushed to Dongguan to capture the suspects.

With the assistance of relevant departments of the Ganzhou Municipal Public Security Bureau, the special task force quickly identified the two suspected men as Liu, born in 1991, and Zhong, born in 1992. Both resided in Wangmudu Town, Ganxian County.

It was learned that Liu and Zhong had known each other since childhood. Zhong's parents worked outside, so he lived with his grandparents. Because the elderly couldn't discipline him, he dropped out of school early and wandered around in society. Liu, on the other hand, lost his father at an early age, and his mother couldn't manage him either. After finishing junior high school, he began to hang around in society. Due to similar life experiences, their relationship became even closer. Previously, Liu and Zhong had worked in Guangdong.

On February 27th, after investigation and interviews, the special task force learned that Liu and Zhong might be hiding in Dongguan, Guangdong Province. The police immediately rushed to Dongguan to arrest the suspects. With the cooperation of local police, the special task force quickly located Liu and the other person hiding in a neighborhood in Houjie District, Dongguan. At 3:00 PM on February 29th, the police stormed into a rented house and arrested Liu and Zhong while they were sound asleep.

**Confession**

Killed the victim upon being recognized during a robbery.

Upon interrogation, the suspects Liu and Zhong confessed that in October 2011, after quitting his job and returning to Ganxian, Zhong met the unemployed Liu. During this period, Zhong frequently invited Liu to the involved training school to see his girlfriend Xiao Xiao. Soon, Liu and Zhong became familiar with the environment of the school and the living habits of the female boarding students, and had even stolen students' belongings twice.

On February 2nd this year, the two came to Xinfeng County and rented a house in Jiading Town. They slept in the rented house during the day and went online at night, quickly spending all their money.

On February 25th, broke and penniless, Liu and Zhong decided to rob the boarding students at a training school in Xinfeng County.

At around 4:00 PM that day, Zhong and the other person carried tools such as a dagger and cloth strips and hid on the fourth floor of the school. At around 8:30 PM, they heard the footsteps of two girls returning, and later saw them go back to their dormitory on the third floor. Liu suggested robbing them in the dormitory, but Zhong, due to fear, didn't dare to proceed.

Half an hour later, they noticed one of the girls go downstairs. Liu again proposed robbing her, but Zhong still dared not to go. Around 9:30 PM, Zhong and the other person noticed a girl walking from the third floor to the fourth floor. They quickly used cloth strips and belts to control her. The girl shouted at Zhong, "I know you; you once treated me to dinner." Zhong then stuffed a pair of cotton pants into the victim's mouth and took away over 2510 yuan and a mobile phone. Fearing exposure, Zhong and Liu dragged the victim to the washroom, strangled her neck with their hands. Zhong realized she was still alive, so he stabbed her neck three times with a knife. Subsequently, the two immediately fled the scene.

Zhong also confessed that at around 8:00 AM on February 26th, fearing capture by the police, they took multiple buses to Dongguan, Guangdong, but were caught by Xinfeng police within three days.

Currently, the two suspects Zhong and Liu have been criminally detained, and the case is under further review.
