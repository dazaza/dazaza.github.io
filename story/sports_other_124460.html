Security gates applied in factories

by daikuan on 2009-02-13 11:56:19

1. Background Overview:

With the continuous development of the social economy, raw material prices have soared, making the management of factory production products and raw materials increasingly important. Relying solely on internal factory management, employee self-discipline, and security personnel is no longer sufficient to meet the needs of enterprise development. Using scientific management methods to prevent the loss of products and raw materials has become urgent. Without infringing on the personal rights of each employee, using technological means such as security gates and metal detectors for production management effectively combines human and technical prevention, thus becoming the true guardian of corporate assets.

2. Purpose of Installing Security Gates:

The main function of security gates is to inspect factory employees as they enter and exit the workshop or factory, preventing employees from carrying the company's products or metal raw materials out of the factory. This eliminates the loss of products and raw materials, ensuring the safety of the company's property.

3. Environmental Requirements for Installing Security Gates:

Security gates are best installed at workshop entrances and exits, stairwell passages, or factory gateways, not suitable for outdoor installation. Protection from wind and rain is necessary. If the security gate is installed outdoors, such as at the factory gateway, there should be a corresponding canopy for protection. Ideally, the installation site should be about 5 cm above the ground level, and within a 1-meter radius, there should be no large metal objects to avoid unnecessary external metal interference during operation, thereby improving the inspection accuracy and precision of the security gate.

4. Selection of Installation Location for Security Gates:

- Inside the entrance and exit of the production workshop;

- In the passage from the workshop exit to the employee lockers;

- At the gatekeeper’s channel (with rain protection facilities);

- Other designated locations.

5. Inspection Scope and Practical Operations of Security Gates:

Metal products with metal content no smaller than that of a one-yuan coin can all be accurately detected for their position on the human body. According to customer needs, sensitivity can be adjusted to achieve optimal alarm detection effects.

- When detecting larger metal contents, the sensitivity can be adjusted to shield metals in clothing such as buttons, zippers, belts, keys, coins, and even mobile phones. Alarms will sound only when metal exceeds the specified range. For example, in small appliance production enterprises or large metal item manufacturing companies.

- When the metal product or raw material being inspected is similar in size to the metal on clothing or carried items, employees can be asked to place their metal objects in lockers. Alternatively, long tables, item baskets, or conveyor belts can be placed next to the security gate for employees to retrieve their metal objects before passing through the gate, reducing possible alarm areas. In case of an alarm, security personnel can use handheld metal detectors for further checks, increasing passage speed and improving work efficiency and supervision. Suitable for IT enterprises (mobile phones and accessories, mp3 players, USB drives, etc.), circuit board manufacturers, precision instrument component companies, rare metal processing enterprises, etc.

6. Recommended Security Gate Models:

- XT-LED type security gate or XT-III type security gate;

- XT-LED Precision Type Security Gate is also appropriate.
