by boanzjm5y on 2010-04-11 12:33:47

On January 25, a notorious incident involving the principal of Square Road Elementary School in Wenzhou being caught for soliciting prostitution sparked widespread public attention. The idea of a role model as a teacher engaging in such behavior was shocking and disgraceful. Just as the aftershocks of this event were gradually subsiding, it was revealed that within the Yiwu education system, there was another scandal where a principal led multiple teachers who were also caught in the act of soliciting prostitution. Yesterday, the Publicity Department of the Yiwu Municipal Committee and the Yiwu Education Bureau confirmed this incident. The time when these teachers engaged in prostitution was only four days apart from when the principal in Wenzhou was caught.

● Event Details

Alcohol-induced misconduct, booking rooms and calling for escorts

Both the Publicity Department of the Yiwu Municipal Committee and the Yiwu Education Bureau attributed the teachers' acts of soliciting prostitution to one reason—alcohol-induced misconduct.

According to official statements, on the evening of January 21, the principal and a teacher from a junior high school in Yiwu engaged in prostitution after having their fill of food and drink, and were subsequently caught by the police. A teacher from Yiwu who wished to remain anonymous provided more details. This teacher said that after the prostitution incident came to light, the local education bureau specially organized a meeting with all school principals to report on the incident, so the internal education system gradually became aware of it. From his school's principal, he learned that on January 21, the street where the school was located held a meeting which ended late, after which the street organized the teachers for dinner. During the meal, there was no shortage of toasting. After the meal, the principal first booked a room at the Mingjing Boutique Hotel in Yiwu, while other teachers went outside to find escorts and brought them back to the hotel. Later developments resulted in them being caught red-handed by undercover police officers during room inspections.

"Probably due to alcohol-induced misconduct," the teacher speculated.

It is rumored that those involved were all school leaders

The teachers involved in the prostitution were from Yiwu Donghe Junior High School, an ordinary junior high school in Yiwu, established on September 1, 1970, located in Qiyi Village, Donghe Township, Chengxi Town, Yiwu City. The leading principal was named Jin Chengmin.

Rumors spread that a total of four teachers were involved in the prostitution incident, all of whom were part of Donghe School's leadership. Besides Principal Jin Chengmin, there were also the head of the Political and Educational Affairs Office, the head of the Academic Affairs Office, and the head of the General Affairs Office. Two were responsible for finding escorts, while two directly engaged in relations with the escorts. The unnamed teacher said that according to what he heard, the school’s head of the General Affairs Office and a third-grade homeroom teacher "arranged girls" for the principal and the head of the Academic Affairs Office but did not participate in the prostitution themselves. After the incident, two people were detained, and two were released on bail.

Regarding how many teachers were involved and whether they were all school leaders, the Publicity Department of the Yiwu Municipal Committee stated that the official notification had already explained the matter and would not repeat it. The Yiwu Education Bureau emphasized repeatedly that those penalized were the school principal and another teacher.

● Handling Results

Principal dismissed, other teachers return to teaching

In the village where the school was located, these teachers generally had a good reputation, so the impact on parents was significant, and they often discussed it while dropping off or picking up their children. Especially before the winter break, parents spread the news widely, and the "teacher prostitution" incident almost became common knowledge locally. "Teachers have too little quality. They are supposed to be educating students but end up doing such things. It's hard to trust them with our children," many parents commented.

Director Gong of the Yiwu Education Bureau's office told reporters that after the incident, Principal Jin Chengmin was administratively demoted and dismissed from the school. "This punishment means Jin Chengmin has to seek his own way out," Director Gong said. As Jin Chengmin is a party member, the Yiwu Discipline Inspection Commission also gave him disciplinary and administrative penalties. The security guard of Donghe Junior High School confirmed this, saying that Jin Chengmin had been transferred from the school before the New Year.

As for the other teachers involved in the prostitution, Director Gong said they had received corresponding administrative demotions and have returned to teaching. "That's how the education system works—one person per position."

● Police Reaction

Yiwu police try to avoid discussing the matter

Contrary to the attitude of the Propaganda Department and the Education Bureau acknowledging the teachers' involvement in prostitution, the Yiwu police, who were directly involved in investigating and catching those engaged in prostitution, avoided discussing the matter entirely.

When reporters sought information from the Beiyuan Police Station in Yiwu responsible for the investigation, Chief Xu Jianghong only responded with one sentence: "It is true, but we cannot disclose the specifics or details." Afterwards, reporters called the Political Department of the Yiwu Public Security Bureau for further details, and Director Chen Zhengming, after understanding the purpose of the interview, immediately refused to comment further, only telling reporters that all unified notifications regarding the matter would come from the Publicity Department of the Municipal Committee. When reporters tried to learn more, Chen Zhengming even questioned the reporters: "Don't you understand the official statement?" and then hung up the phone.

Reporters also noticed that previously, discussions about the prostitution incident involving teachers from Donghe Junior High School appeared on local forums in Yiwu, but yesterday, all related posts had disappeared, with the page showing as non-existent.

● News Link

Wenzhou Famous School Principal Engaged in Prostitution

Dismissed from Position and Expelled from Party

At the beginning of this month, a post appeared online claiming that the principal of a well-known school in Wenzhou city, along with an employee from a certain bureau and a third person, was caught by the police engaging in prostitution with two escorts at an entertainment venue in the city center.

In the post, it was also mentioned that "according to authoritative sources, the details disclosed by the escorts were unspeakable. Currently, under the mediation of senior officials, the principal has been released on bail, but informed sources say that the principal will definitely face both expulsion from the party and dismissal from his position."

Subsequently, some netizens commented that the principal was referred to as 'Tang', the principal of a famous elementary school in Wenzhou city. Other netizens posted comments saying, "Square Road Elementary School, hold on!"

On the afternoon of February 4, the spokesperson for the Wenzhou Public Security Bureau introduced that around 11 p.m. on January 25, 2010, officers from the SWAT team of the Wenzhou Public Security Bureau, actively participating in the "Winter Action", found multiple cases of prostitution during a routine inspection at Hua Tianchi Water Therapy Massage Center adjacent to No. 84 Xiaonan Road in Lucheng District. At the scene, 14 suspects, including Tang Moumou, were arrested for suspected prostitution activities.

After questioning, it was determined that Tang Moumou (principal of Square Road Elementary School in Lucheng District, Wenzhou), Fu Moumou (deputy director of the enforcement team of a bureau in Lucheng District, Wenzhou), Lu Moumou (driver for Lucheng Security Company), and two others were engaging in obscene activities inside the massage center. Their actions met the criteria defined by the Ministry of Public Security for prostitution.

On February 7, relevant departments informed reporters that Tang Mou, the principal of Wenzhou Square Road Elementary School, had been expelled from the Communist Party and administratively dismissed. Simultaneously, the Standing Committee of the Lucheng District People's Congress made a decision to accept Tang's resignation from his position as a representative of the Wenzhou Municipal People's Congress.
