Gossip Girl's Warm-hearted Strategy

by baiyasin2s on 2012-02-27 20:31:40

After the winter solstice comes the harsh cold, and this is when scarves and hats become essential items for women to protect themselves from the wind and stay warm! Hollywood actresses are no exception, and their stylish combinations of scarves and hats can leave you dazzled. Here's a selection of recent street snaps of celebrities to help us learn from Blake, Whitely, and Alba, among other street style queens, about clever ways to stay warm and stylish this winter!

### Winter Stylish Scarf and Hat Combinations: Solid Color Scarves

**Jessica Alba: Leather Jacket + Solid Color Scarves**

Alba’s outing with her family was picture-perfect happiness. Wearing a black leather jacket over a black T-shirt, paired with black pants and shoes, she created a slimming effect that works wonders! Her little trick for staying warm yet stylish lies in the scarves—she layered two solid color scarves, one darker and one lighter, adding a fashionable touch. The contrasting colors against her all-black ensemble make her stand out even more! A small green chain bag adds a vintage flair to the look.

- Outerwear: Rick Owens

- Bag: Bvlgari

- Pants: Habitual

- Necklace: Jessica Elliot

**Rooney Mara: Brown Coat + Solid Color Scarf**

Mara's outfit exudes a casual neighborhood girl vibe. Her brown wool coat with rounded hemlines makes her look more demure and adorable, while simple blue jeans elongate her legs subtly. The scarf, although warm, blends too much with the coat’s tone. Switching to a lighter shade or choosing a slightly lighter coat would enhance the look! A black Balenciaga moto bag adds sophistication to the overall ensemble.

- Bag: Balenciaga

**Catherine Zeta-Jones: Black Fur Coat + Solid Color Scarf**

The stunning Catherine Zeta-Jones opted for an understated all-black ensemble wrapped tightly for warmth. However, her choice of a black fur coat paired with a solid color scarf remains both cozy and luxurious! Adding a newsboy cap with shiny black edges gives her a youthful and trendy feel. A glossy black bucket bag maintains the chic monochrome theme but could benefit from a pop of color like red accents on the bag or scarf.

**Camilla Belle: White Wool Coat + Solid Color Scarf**

The mixed-race beauty Camilla Belle wore a white double-breasted wool coat with blue jeans, black boots, and a black scarf—a simple yet striking combination perfectly aligned with her usual fashion sense! For a clean and comfortable winter look, pairing a white wool coat with a black scarf is ideal; the classic black-and-white contrast will make you shine!

**Camilla Belle: Black Leather Jacket + Solid Color Scarf**

Belle's angle here accentuates her exotic allure. This time, shopping with friends, she chose a black leather jacket over a multicolored sweater, making the black fringe scarf even more prominent and harmonious with her black knit hat—warm and sweet! Blue jeans, black boots, and a crossbody bag complete the look—stylish, sweet, and warm, perfect for current weather conditions.

### Winter Stylish Scarf and Hat Combinations: Patterned Scarves

**Melissa Joan Hart: Green Sweater + Heart Patterned Scarf**

Actress Melissa Joan Hart's casual street style during shopping features a vibrant green sweater—a standout piece for winter attention-grabbing! She paired it with a white heart-patterned scarf, creating a striking contrast. Additionally, her bright light brown handbag complements the sweater and scarf, making her noticeable even during a low-key outing.

**Nicole Richie: Black Fur Coat + Floral Patterned Scarf**

Street style queen Nicole Richie stepped out in a black fur coat—elegant and luxurious! To avoid monotony and elevate her look, Nicole added a beige floral patterned scarf with subtle fringes, making her outfit eye-catching. Paired with blue jeans and a Celine tote, it’s a warm yet chic winter look!
