If you have seen the dazzling 3D effects of Ubuntu 8.04, if you have heard about the spirit of Ubuntu in terms of freedom, sharing, and mutual assistance, and if you want to give it a try but are afraid of complicated operations or affecting your precious Windows system, Wubi is perfect for you. Just like a Live CD, Wubi will not make any changes to your XP system. However, unlike a Live CD, it provides full hardware access, and you can download, install, and use applications just as you would in Ubuntu. Wubi stores most of its files within a folder in Windows, and you can uninstall it anytime.
