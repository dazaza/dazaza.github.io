Defeat: Classic Novel Recommendations

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-25 19:59:53

"God's Grave" is the representative work of the famous online novelist, Chen Dong. It is also a sequel to his debut work, "Immortal and Immortal". The book follows the protagonist Chen Nan as he searches for his lover Yu Xin from ten thousand years ago and investigates the secrets of the gods and demons' extinction. This leads to the introduction of the vast six paths and the chessboard of heaven and earth, creating a legendary tale filled with passion, heroism, and melancholy beauty. It narrates the legends of countless heroes and beauties and conducts a deep exploration of elements such as humanity, society, and life. After reading it, one not only feels moved and inspired but also finds it thought-provoking and endlessly intriguing, making it an excellent piece among online novels.

"Gods have died... Demons have perished... I still live... Why? Why am I revived from the ancient tomb of gods? Where should I go?"

The cemetery of gods and demons, besides burying the strongest beings of each generation and the top cultivators of alien species, has every other grave housing an ancient god or demon. This is a resting place for gods and demons.

An ordinary youth resurrects after ten thousand years from the ancient tomb of gods. The countless tombstones of gods and demons leave him in awe.

Through ten thousand years, the sea has turned into mulberry fields. The originally separated fairyland continent and magic continent are now connected, where Daoist techniques coexist with magic, true energy with combat energy, and Eastern divine dragons with Western giant dragons dance together...

Characters Introduction:

Chen Nan: The reincarnation of Defeat Heaven's son, Gu Du Xiaobai, who absorbed the spiritual awareness of all living beings for the final battle. His life can be divided into two parts. Ten thousand years ago, his identity was simple, being the son of Chen Zhan. Due to Dantai Xuan's interference, his martial arts regressed. Later, due to Yu Xin almost perishing, he wanted to end his life and engaged in a fatal duel with Eastern Changming. After dying in the duel, he was buried in the Cemetery of Gods and Demons for ten thousand years by Chen Zhan and Gu Du Beatheaven through an array. His spiritual awareness combined with his body and the soul power of Gu Du Xiaobai to be reborn. Finally, one day, he resurrected from the Cemetery of Gods and Demons. Through ten thousand years, the sea has turned into mulberry fields. The former red faces and family members are no longer there. To survive and seek his beloved in his heart, he gradually embarked on a path that defied heaven.

Yu Xin: The reincarnation of the Human King. The most beloved woman of Chen Nan, pure and kind-hearted. She appears delicate but has a strong heart. However, for love, she would rather die without regret. Ten thousand years ago, the old monster Eastern Xiaotian night-attacked the Chen residence, and Chen Zhan was "tricked." Yu Xin almost perished to protect Chen Nan. Later, Chen Zhan reversed her fate against heaven, eventually keeping her alive. She entered the Hundred Flowers Valley to sit in a deathly meditation. When she emerged from the valley, she heard the news of Chen Nan's death. Subsequently, she followed Chen Zhan into the Heavenly Realm. After Chen Zhan and his wife suddenly left, she obtained the Taishang Forgetful Record and had to cultivate it for survival, becoming the well-known Heartless Fairy in the Heavenly Realm. Later, she was forced to split into Ling Shi Yu Xin and Little Chenxi. Eventually, in the Tree of Life, she split into the Elven Holy Maiden Catherine. Under Chen Nan's efforts, the spiritual awareness of the four women merged, restoring her to the Yu Xin from ten thousand years ago. She recuperated in the Divine Map using the Spring of Life. Before the Great Annihilation, she was thrown out of the Divine Map. After the Great Annihilation, she recuperated with a Spring of Life, preserving her spiritual awareness. She was saved by her previous incarnation, the skeleton of the Human King. Finally, her spiritual awareness merged with her previous incarnation, the Human King, becoming the Human King. But at this time, Chen Nan already had a wife and children. Before advancing towards the Heavenly Way, they could only briefly recall the past hand-in-hand.
