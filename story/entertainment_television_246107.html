Liu Qianjin, who had gone through countless battles, was straightforward, shrewd and a bit cunning. He looked forward to the day when his old superior, Minister Cheng, would send him back to the battlefield to lead troops into battle again. When the appointment as the captain of the vanguard team of the reformed public security forces was placed before Liu Qianjin, he did not happily accept the task of escorting prisoners westward. Another reason why Liu Qianjin was unwilling to take the command was that Minister Cheng assigned Peng Hao, his fellow townsman whom he looked down upon as an "intellectual", as the political commissar of the detachment.

Before the troops set out, Miss Zhou Yuan, who was cheerful and loved to talk and laugh, was recruited into the army. Liu Qianjin did not hide his affection for Zhou Yuan and made her the confidential clerk of the detachment.

The convoy escorting the prisoners set off. Our army broke the secret code of the enemy agents and knew that there were spies hidden among the escorting teams. Minister Cheng drove to catch up with the convoy and informed Liu Qianjin of this situation, asking him to guard against internal traitors causing chaos.

Tang Jingyin, the "First Guerrilla Army Commander of Western Sichuan" of the Kuomintang, followed the westward-bound convoy because her husband, Ning Jiahé, the "Southwest Region Provincial Guerrilla Area Commander-in-Chief" of the Kuomintang, was in custody. She almost rescued her husband at Wofosi Temple. In order to stop the westward march and rescue Ning Jiahé, Tang Jingyin blew up the plank bridge and burned the grain station, causing heavy losses to the westward-bound convoy. With the help of the hidden spies, Tang Jingyin finally hijacked Ning Jiahé.

Liu Qianjin investigated the internal traitor. Various signs indicated that Peng Hao was a major suspect, which made Liu Qianjin very distressed. However, he firmly believed that his good friend would not be a traitor. Minister Cheng came to the front line to understand the situation of the troops. Liu Qianjin told Minister Cheng about his troubles, and Minister Cheng decided to investigate Peng Hao's history.

During the journey westward, the prisoners often sought opportunities to escape. If they failed to escape, they would plan to poison others. Doctor Ling Ruobing, who was sentenced for misappropriating medical supplies intended for Tibet, saved everyone. Peng Hao moved Ling Ruobing with respect for his personality. She wrote an encouraging song for the prisoners to encourage their reform and helped deliver Wen Jie when she went into labor.

The female prisoner Wu Xiaofeng, who was released early, discovered that her husband's murderer, Gou Jingtang, was in custody, so she deliberately attacked the police to return to prison and planned revenge with Liu Yan Yan, a murderer from a prostitute background. They almost beat Gou Jingtang to death. Liu Qianjin admired and was helpless towards this woman who hated evil and caused trouble repeatedly but still took care of her everywhere.

The activities of the internal traitor became increasingly rampant. First, he killed Xiao Li who was delivering a letter for help to the Lianshan Military Subdivision, then he killed two soldiers who had already discovered the identity of Tang Jingyin as the leader of the bandits, allowing Tang Jingyin, who was already like a fish in a jar, to escape.

Liu Qianjin and Wen Jie expedited the investigation of the internal traitor and found the cartridge case beside the fallen soldiers. Liu Qianjin asked Minister Cheng to conduct a technical identification on the cartridge case.

Peng Hao also noticed the unusual behavior of his comrades. Through understanding, he knew that his comrades suspected him and the organization was investigating him, which made him very distressed. He voluntarily handed over his pistol to Minister Cheng and requested the superior to conduct a technical identification.

During the journey, the detachment clerk Guan Xiaoyu gradually developed feelings for the steady and serious warden Hou Zhongwen, and their feelings continued to deepen. By the time they reached Xinjinpíng, the two had basically established a romantic relationship. The logistics director Zhen Shicheng joined the westward-bound team to win Guan Xiaoyu's heart. Seeing his ideal lover getting closer to Hou Zhongwen, Zhen Shicheng felt extremely disheartened.

As the westward-bound team was about to reach Xinjinpíng, the communicator Xiao Yu, who had been responsible for archive protection, suddenly seized all the archives and burned them. Hou Zhongwen chased after him on horseback, but the spy Xiao Yu committed suicide. It seemed that the internal traitor problem was resolved.

Minister Cheng arrived at Xinjinpíng and told everyone that the technical identification results showed that the cartridge cases that killed Xiao Li and the two soldiers were fired from Peng Hao's pistol, so Peng Hao was still suspected of being an internal traitor.

Peng Hao was very distressed. He even thought of committing suicide. Finally, he decided to redeem himself by investigating the bigger spy behind Xiao Yu and exposing the real internal traitor.

Well-behaved prisoners participated in the construction of the Xinjinpíng farm, cutting wood, building houses, splitting mountains, and repairing roads. Lu Zhengshan, who participated in the Taierzhuang Campaign, and Xiao Yuzi, who was imprisoned for theft, became good friends. The repeated attempts of prisoners to escape failed. Prisoners Fu Mingde and Qiu Shuangxi suspected that it was Xiao Yuzi who reported them, and they tried several times to kill Xiao Yuzi, but Xiao Yuzi narrowly escaped death every time...

Doctor Ling Ruobing, Lu Zhengshan, and Liu Yan Yan opened a clinic in Xinxing Town, which became the contact station for the Xinjinpíng farm...

Under the arrangement of the special agent group leader Aunt Zhou Dagū in Dachedian, Ah Feng seduced the logistics director Zhen Shicheng. Zhen Shicheng, who fell into corruption and became a traitor and spy, sent intelligence about Xinjinpíng multiple times, causing significant losses to the farm. Old Squad Leader and others sacrificed themselves for this...

After Old Squad Leader's sacrifice, Peng Hao reviewed his notebook and found a record: Xiao Yu once searched for cartridge cases at the Laolongkou grain station where Peng Hao had fired his gun. Peng Hao realized that this was the internal traitor instructing Xiao Yu to collect his cartridge cases and frame him. Therefore, he cleverly dropped the cartridge case when Liu Qianjin shot the rock sheep, claiming that he shot the rock sheep, with the cartridge case as proof. Liu Qianjin and Wen Jie suddenly understood that Peng Hao becoming a suspect was entirely due to the design and framing of the hidden enemy agents. Peng Hao wrote a report to the superior and sent Old Squad Leader's notebook, requesting handwriting identification. Minister Cheng replied that after identification, Old Squad Leader's handwriting was confirmed, agreeing with Peng Hao's opinion. The superior removed the suspicion on Peng Hao, and Liu Qianjin and Peng Hao reconciled as before...

Minister Cheng secretly arrived at Xinxing Town and narrowly avoided assassination. Liu Qianjin and Peng Hao investigated the internal traitor and traced it back to Zhou Yuan. Liu Qianjin was unwilling to accept this reality. He wanted to win Zhou Yuan over. Zhou Yuan frequently received instructions from the code-named "Wild Wolf". She refused to be controlled anymore and finally confessed her identity. Liu Qianjin decided to use her as bait to uncover the larger conspiracy behind her...

More and more doubts surrounded Hou Zhongwen, but there was still insufficient evidence to confirm and solve the mystery of whether he was the "internal traitor". Hou Zhongwu urgently telegraphed Tang Jingyin...
