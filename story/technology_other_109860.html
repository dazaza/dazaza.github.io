China Telecom section 189 on the 20th release number auspicious number 8888 and other appointments completed

by hexiaoyao on 2008-12-08 23:11:48

December 7 news, China Telecom relevant people confirmed that the attention of China Telecom 189 number will be on December 20, the national number, at the same time, China Telecom to start the appointment of 189 number selection, China Telecom C network's new offensive began to enter the early stage.

The number will be released on December 20

Since December 4, China Telecom's advertisements and information about the reservation number of section 189 have been overwhelming. Recently, entering many commercial buildings in Beijing, the "Telecom 189 segment will be released this month" endorsed by film and television star Deng Chao oncoming, showing that the 189 segment is about to begin a comprehensive number release.

People's Daily Online:

Beijing Telecom has recently started the reservation of the number 189 segment, "8888", "6666", "9999" and other four of the same number as the reporter's press yesterday has been all booked.

China Telecom reminds users that the opening is the user's reservation for the number 189 segment, and the official number release time is expected to be in mid to late December, users need to go to the business hall with their personal ID card within 7 days of the official number release, if the deadline is exceeded, it will be considered that the reservation number will be automatically abandoned.

Segment 189 will be the exclusive segment of the "Tianyi" brand launched by Telecom. So far, China Telecom has not officially announced the official schedule of number allocation.
