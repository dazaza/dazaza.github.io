Three years, are you okay?

The heart is so contradictory. Actually, there's no need to be so troubled; I have already become indifferent deep in my heart. Three years, three years, such a short time that it's not enough to forget someone. I don't like those sorrowful things because sorrow is putting one's own pain onto others. Those who have experienced pain will fall into sadness again when they see it. Maybe others would say that kind of person is a coward, not daring to face it, but only those who have experienced it would understand that kind of pain. I think the most heartbreaking thing is revisiting old places. Those things that happened, those words spoken, those promises made, will all come back. Maybe I am a coward, and those burdens are so hard for me to bear. Zhanxue, I know you carry as much burden as I do, but at this moment, I can no longer suppress my feelings. I have only loved once in my life, but why has it turned out this way? Is it really because your mother interfered? No, no, I realize if I had persisted, then everything would not have been like this. But I gave up, I made the biggest choice of my life. I don't understand whether it was right or wrong. You must hate me to the bone, but I want to say, my love for you is no less than your love for me. At that time, I really felt powerless. You should understand the difficulties I faced. I believed we could make it, but I could do nothing. I know you blame my mother, but every parent thinks for their children. They love you, and I love you too. I don't want to put you in a difficult situation. You're so smart, you should know.

Zhanxue, I'm sorry, I'm so sorry. Three years, I've forgotten how to get through each day. It's really hard to survive, without a soul. I don't know when it will end. I'm so tired, so uncomfortable. I want to stop and leave quietly, resolving all the love and hatred of this life. But when I see my parents, my friends, and people still suffering in the world, I remember our agreement, our dream country. Because of this agreement, I'm still holding on. I don't know when I'll achieve it, but it's my only goal now. Even if it means going through fire and brimstone, I will make it happen.

Three years, are you doing okay? Did you get into your ideal university? Are you still passionate about writing? I know nothing about you these years, lost contact. A few years ago, I saw a sentence: loving someone means hoping they live happily and joyfully, even if you can't be together. I understood a lot. Sometimes, I miss you so much that my mood suddenly becomes gloomy. But I'm doing well, at least I haven't let my friends and family share my pain.

I've finished my college entrance exam, just as bad as I expected, because I didn't want to pass this year. It would be best to repeat a few years, stay in university for seven or eight years, enter society a bit later. Hehe, you should understand why I did it this way. Although sometimes I feel emotional due to certain scenes, I'm still doing okay. In short, I'm fine now. Zhanxue, the next time we meet will be the day we achieve our goal.

I know, you won't see what I write here. I can't contact you, but I still fantasize that one day you might accidentally see it. I publish it on my diary, it's all about our fate. Zhanxue, I love you, only you.
