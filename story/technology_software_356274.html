Central University of Finance and Economics Double Certificate Program New Great Wall Central University of Finance and Economics Self-strengthening Double Certificate Program

by peiweis65 on 2010-05-27 10:27:23

At 9:00 PM on the night of April 25, 2010, the "New Great Wall Central University of Finance and Economics Self-Strengthening Society" was officially established in the Central Official Clan University Auditorium. Simultaneously establishing the "Self-Strengthening Society" were four other universities including Central Official Clan University, Nanjing Forestry University, Nanjing Agricultural Science University, and Nanjing Normal University. All members of our university's Self-Strengthening Society attended the founding conference. Assistant to the President of our university, Liang Qie, and Director of the Student Affairs Office, Ye Fei, also attended the main conference.

The event was organized by the China Poverty Alleviation Foundation. During the establishment conference, the head of the New Great Wall Program Department of the China Poverty Alleviation Foundation introduced the project overview and development plan for the New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society. Our university’s Vice President, Li Qiaosheng, was appointed as the honorary president of the "New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society," with Wang Yuzhao, president of the China Poverty Alleviation Foundation, presenting the appointment letter and flagging the New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society. Assistant to the President, Liang Qie, presented the society's plaque to our university's New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society. Representatives from the leadership teachers of each school’s Self-Strengthening Society, assisted student representatives, presidents' representatives of the Self-Strengthening Society, and representatives from New Great Wall partner organizations all delivered speeches. The conference concluded with all members reciting the "New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Declaration."

The New Great Wall Project is a social welfare initiative launched and implemented by the China Poverty Alleviation Foundation aimed at assisting extremely impoverished elementary school students through both economic support and talent development services. The New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society at our university is a student organization formed under the guidance of the Student Affairs Office, led by the project office of the China Poverty Alleviation Foundation, and registered with the university's Youth League Committee. Its members are primarily composed of students receiving aid from the New Great Wall program, while also welcoming other financially aided university students.

The mission of the New Great Wall Self-Strengthening Society is to convey societal care and cultivate self-reliant talents. Its goal is to foster social responsibility among its members, instill a spirit of self-esteem, confidence, self-discipline, and self-strengthening, nurture virtues such as integrity, selflessness, responsibility, diligence, discernment, flexibility, and decisiveness, and enhance their comprehensive abilities in organization, planning, communication, foreign languages, and computer application. This ensures that members become well-rounded and excellent talents.

In 2003, 60 students from our university received assistance from the New Great Wall Special Hardship Elementary School Student Program. Among them, 40 students received an annual subsidy of 2000 yuan for four years, totaling 320,000 yuan; another 20 students received a one-time subsidy of 1840 yuan each, totaling 36,800 yuan, making the total amount of subsidies 356,800 yuan.
