Lanzhou netizens send microblog: Love desks and chairs arrive in Yushu

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-03 17:16:12

Lanzhou, October 14th, at around 11 a.m., a large truck filled with tables and chairs, cotton coats, workbooks, water heaters, and other daily necessities and school supplies slowly drove out of the Tianshui Road highway exit in Lanzhou City, speeding towards Yushu in Qinghai.

This is the "Grassland Dining Table Plan" initiated by Lanzhou netizens through Weibo. This event raised a batch of living and learning supplies such as dining tables and chairs for more than 120 boarding students of a primary school in Yushu. Two village cadres cycled 3,000 kilometers to raise money for road construction for their villagers.

On September 14th, netizen "Yixieyizheng" received a call from volunteer Pan Yuqi who has been engaged in volunteer activities in Yushu for a long time. Pan Yuqi said that in the place where he volunteers, there is a school with about 120 boarding students who eat their three meals a day outside the tent. He wanted to help solve the problem of dining tables for the children, so he asked "Yixieyizheng" to inquire about the price of tables and chairs in Lanzhou and planned to raise funds nationwide. "I'll take care of this." "Yixieyizheng" readily agreed. Subsequently, "Yixieyizheng" immediately went to various markets in Lanzhou to learn about the prices of dining tables and chairs. Starting from September 15th, "Yixieyizheng" began posting on Sina Weibo to raise donations and named the activity the "Grassland Dining Table Plan". To make netizens believe that this activity is public welfare, "Yixieyizheng" published his bank account under a real-name certified ID on Weibo.

After the Weibo post was sent out, it received a positive response from Lanzhou netizens. Soon, the Weibo post was widely forwarded.

The small acts of kindness from netizens gathered from all directions. A total of 72 netizens and groups from Lanzhou, Jiuquan, Beijing, Nanjing, and other places contributed their love to the "Grassland Dining Table Plan". The "Grassland Dining Table Plan" activity raised 32 dining tables, 128 chairs, 88 cotton coats, 1 water heater, and more than 1,000 workbooks.

It is understood that the love materials of the "Grassland Dining Table Plan" were delivered to Batang Grassland Primary School in Yushu on the morning of October 15th. On October 16th, the children were already eating on the brand new tables and chairs.
