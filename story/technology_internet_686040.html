2. Hair Loss due to Kidney Deficiency

Many male patients often assume that hair loss is caused by kidney deficiency. Severe cases may spend money on kidney tonics [http://www.toufa5.com]. According to Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), kidney deficiency causing hair loss is one type of diagnosis, but hair loss isn't always due to kidney issues. Experts believe that indiscriminately consuming supplements doesn't help much. It's crucial to get a proper medical diagnosis to identify the cause and treat accordingly for effective hair loss management.

3. Reducing or Increasing Hair Washing Frequency

Hair loss patients might think this way. Due to excessive scalp oil secretion, they increase their hair washing frequency. Doctors suggest that washing your hair every 3-4 days in winter and every 2-3 days in summer is normal. Washing too frequently can irritate the hair follicles, while washing too infrequently can lead to the proliferation of Malassezia fungi, worsening hair loss. For those who experience frequent hair shedding, appropriate hair washing can also help prevent hair loss.
