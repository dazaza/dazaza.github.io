Introduction:

Since she was young, Ellie Arroway (played by Jodie Foster) has had a deep passion for radio communication. As an adult, she becomes an astronomer whose project involves receiving signals from outer space. She hopes to use this to find evidence of other civilizations in the universe. Unfortunately, her brilliant ideals are met with disdain from investors, but luckily, a mysterious person helps Ellie continue her work.

By chance, Ellie receives frequency signals from Vega, seemingly proving that her long-term work has been correct. However, this does not bring personal success to her career, but rather conflicts and discussions from political, social, religious, and national perspectives. At the center of all these storms, Ellie must also deal with her emotional entanglement with the clergyman Palmer Joss (played by Matthew McConaughey).

Facing challenges far beyond academia, Ellie struggles with personal and global issues, as well as truth and reality.
