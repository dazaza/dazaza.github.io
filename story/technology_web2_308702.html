Ronaldo: Real Madrid has a promising future if they keep playing like this, and both Cristiano Ronaldo and Messi are the best in the world.

by qinglai888 on 2009-12-15 12:12:14

Ronaldo: Real Madrid has a bright future if they continue like this, CR7 and Messi are the best in the world

According to Sina Sports, Ronaldo watched the match between Real Madrid and Valencia at Kaka's house. The two Brazilian superstars discussed various topics including Real Madrid, Cristiano Ronaldo (CR7), Florentino Perez, and their families. The two stars will meet again during the Christmas period.

El Marca revealed that last Saturday, Kaka was accompanied by superstar Ronaldo as they watched the live broadcast of Real Madrid versus Valencia from Kaka's home in Madrid. The "Alien" Ronaldo has been staying in Madrid these few days, and Kaka, being his former teammate in the Brazilian national team and AC Milan, would keep him company whenever he had time.

It is reported that the friendship between Ronaldo and Kaka dates back more than 10 years. In recent years, it's not uncommon to see these two superstars together, whether in Madrid or Sao Paulo. This summer, when Kaka's son Lucas turned one year old, Ronaldo attended the birthday celebration.

Because of their close relationship, as soon as Ronaldo arrived in Madrid, he immediately contacted the No. 8 player of Real Madrid. On Saturday evening, the two had dinner together, then sat in front of the TV to watch Real Madrid's away game against Valencia.

Interestingly, the apartment in Velazquez, Madrid where Kaka lives was sold to him by Ronaldo, so the Alien is quite familiar with Kaka's home. Both were pleased with Real Madrid's victory. In this match, both Kaka and CR7 were absent for Real Madrid, but the team still showed a good image. Although they were twice equalized by Valencia, Real Madrid eventually won the game. The two superstars both expressed that if Real Madrid continues playing like this, they have a bright future.

Kaka and Ronaldo also spent a long time discussing CR7's situation. In the end, both agreed that the Portuguese player and Messi are currently the best players in the world.

Ronaldo came to Madrid to visit his son and also to resolve some personal issues. After finishing these matters, he will return to Brazil. Currently, Ronaldo resides in Sao Paulo, Brazil. He and Kaka agreed to gather again during the Christmas period. Due to a groin injury, Kaka hasn't played since the El Clasico. Even if he returns to Sao Paulo during Christmas, he will undergo rehabilitation training under the guidance of a physiotherapist. According to El Marca and Cadena Ser, Kaka may only return to the field in February next year.
