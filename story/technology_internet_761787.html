Alibaba is reported to have made a strategic investment in AutoNavi, continuing to lay out local life services

by geekzhang on 2013-05-10 11:22:16

Just after Alibaba's stake in Sina Weibo was settled, news again broke out in the industry that the matter of Alibaba’s strategic investment in AutoNavi has also been settled and the announcement will be made soon. It is reported that this time around, Alibaba will hold about 20% to 30% of AutoNavi shares, becoming its largest shareholder, with an amount exceeding 1 billion US dollars.The news of Alibaba's investment in AutoNavi has been around for a long time, and the most core content revolves around Alibaba's O2O layout. In October last year, Taobao quietly launched a map search service in its local life channel, further exploring O2O. This year, Xing Dian, the head of the Local Life Division under Juhuasuan, once stated that they will launch a revolutionary local life product that can change the current state of the industry in June. At the Global Internet Conference a few days ago, an insider from Tmall confirmed to 36Kr that Alibaba's revolutionary O2O product has been basically completed, and will subsequently be promoted to merchants, with plans to go online in the second half of this year.Similarly, AutoNavi is also continuously exploring in the O2O field. Last year, AutoNavi and Sina Weibo reached a strategic cooperation agreement, collaborating on multiple levels including products, data, cloud platforms, and APIs. Users can directly share and interact with friends on AutoNavi. After opening a specific location, users can see their Sina Weibo friends' check-ins and comments, and can also share information about eating, drinking, playing, and entertainment with nearby friends. After Alibaba strategically invested in Sina Weibo and AutoNavi, all the data that these parties cooperated and shared previously may eventually be utilized by Alibaba.In January this year, AutoNavi's mobile users had already exceeded 100 million. According to the "2012 Q3 China Mobile Map Client Market Quarterly Monitoring Report" from Analysys International, AutoNavi holds a 25.9% market share, ranking first, while Baidu Maps and Tuoba Maps rank second and third with shares of 19.1% and 9.5%, respectively.The local service field is like a newly discovered gold mine. Although not many people have yet mined real gold, a large number of people are rushing here, and most of them are quite aggressive. With simultaneous investments in Sina Weibo and AutoNavi, combined with its own layout in the O2O field, Alibaba's SoLoMo era may soon arrive.How will Baidu and Dianping break through?
