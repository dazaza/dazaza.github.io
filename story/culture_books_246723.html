"Green Mountains Still Smile in the Spring Breeze" - Hilarious Embarrassing Stories of Girls Chasing Boys

by jqrxass on 2009-10-12 16:18:42

The handsome youth with flowing black robes stood by the purple bamboo, his hands behind his back, looking up at the sky, clouds, wind, rain, moon, and stars. This upward gaze lasted for hundreds of years... The purple bamboo transformed into a spirit, becoming our naturally dense female protagonist, Xiaochunfeng. To cover up her embarrassment from being distracted, the handsome youth invited Xiaochunfeng to cultivate together into demons, but she did not agree.

Her refusal instead ignited the youth's persistence. He appeared by Xiaochunfeng every day, saying he could live as long as heaven and earth, protecting her for thousands upon thousands of years. But a great calamity in the six realms turned the purple bamboo forest into a sea of fire, leaving her as the sole survivor, and the handsome youth disappeared. It was only then that the extremely slow-witted Xiaochunfeng began to feel the responsibility of the female lead - without the male lead, the female lead might be out of a job anytime! Thus, Xiaochunfeng went to seek the Buddha.

"Green Mountains Still Smile at Spring Wind Part One"

"Cough... What's your name?" "Xiao Chunfeng."

"Gender?" "...Female."

"Hobbies?" "Men!"

Stay calm! A leader should remain calm at all times: "Age?" "One thousand, one thousand five hundred, oh no, one thousand four hundred sixty... Uh, Big Brother Dangjia, I forgot."

"Green Mountains Still Smile at Spring Wind Part Two"

"Aren't you the young master's attendant? Or else, tell him, let's go together..." That was a bad idea, Xiaoguang sneered disdainfully, continuing to jest: "Are you so reluctant to part with me?" "There is a bit of unease." Thinking about Xiaoguang's previous episodes, though not frequent, there was a trend of worsening each time, she felt worried about leaving him alone. "Then tonight I'll keep you company properly, making it unforgettable for you." And it would also be unforgettable for the young master. Unforgettable, this was a proposition hard to fathom.

"Green Mountains Still Smile at Spring Wind Part Three"

"Spring Wind," he propped himself up half, his smile frivolous, his eyelids blinking an alluring curve, "have you forgotten my impotence? We've already had the beautiful memory of sharing a bed, so what are you still hesitating about? Who do you want to preserve your chastity for this time?"

"Shh! Can you be quieter? What if someone finds out?" Xiaochunfeng squatted halfway on the ground, nervously making a hushing gesture, her voice very low, almost like a mosquito's hum, merely speaking with breath, "If we sleep separately, if someone breaks in at midnight, you can conveniently use your light kung fu."

"Green Mountains Still Smile at Spring Wind Part Four"

Tch, for a woman who calls out 'Xiaoguang' repeatedly, he should be grateful that he didn't shoot the dart directly at her, and yet she actually expected him to appear? To appear for what? To let her see clearly that those calls were more lethal than the darts he threw?

"I, I... I thought it would be him..." Xiaochunfeng spoke softly, almost daring not to look at Mingyueguang. Thought? Ha! Mingyueguang couldn't help but let out a trace of a cold laugh: "When you were sick, you thought it was him by your side; when you encountered danger, you thought it was him who saved you. He is better than me, so no matter how much I do, I'm just paving the way for him, right?"
