"Black smoke!" "The glass was falling down with a splash, crackling," "The rescued person could hardly walk steadily"... At around 9 o'clock yesterday morning, a fire broke out on the 17th floor of the residential building in Beihu Impression Community in Longtan Temple. When the fire happened, a 45-year-old man was still trapped inside the house, but fortunately he was rescued by the firefighters. Currently, the man is still under observation in the ICU ward, and the cause of the fire is still under investigation.

Narrative: "All the people in the building have run out"

The fire started in one of the apartments on the 17th floor of Building 5, Unit 2, Phase 4 of Beihu Impression. The windows facing the corridor were burned to bare frames, the white burglar-proof door had already deformed, and a large area of the wall was blackened by smoke. On the corridor, the accumulated water left from firefighting had not dried yet, and the air was filled with a strong smell of burnt odor.

Ms. Zhang, the first person to call 119, recalled that at that time she was chatting with her friend on the vacant land in front of the building, "Suddenly I saw a big cloud of smoke rising upstairs. Later, the smoke grew bigger and bigger, and I thought it must be on fire."

Ms. Zhang quickly called the police, and then the melted glass began to fall down, "scaring all the people in the building to run out."

Rescue: "The firefighter supported him but he still couldn't walk steadily"

The Chenghua District Government Professional Fire Brigade rushed to rescue with two fire trucks. After entering the burning room, the firefighters found that there was still a man inside. The firefighters recalled that when they found the man, he had already lost consciousness.

The firefighter carried the man on his back and rushed out of the fire scene in no more than ten seconds.

After emergency treatment, the man woke up five minutes later. "The firefighter supported him, and he still couldn't walk steadily, his body kept shaking, I guess he was scared badly," said Ms. Li, a resident. The man was then sent to Chengdu Sixth People's Hospital for treatment.

The doctor found a mobile phone and a wallet on the man. His ID card showed that the man surnamed Sun, 45 years old. Through the mobile phone, the hospital has contacted Mr. Sun's wife. Due to inhaling thick smoke, his respiratory tract was damaged, and Mr. Sun still needs to be observed in the ICU ward for some time.

Firefighters introduced that the fire point was in the bedroom, and it was preliminarily estimated that the fire was caused by improper use of household appliances. The specific reason is still under investigation.

News extension:

Cars downstairs were affected by the fire upstairs

Mr. Zhang parked his car under the burning residential building, and the glass of the left rear window of the car was smashed into pieces. "It costs two or three hundred yuan to install a glass window, who will take responsibility?" Mr. Zhang hoped that the property management of the community could give a reasonable explanation.

The reporter of Tianfu Morning Post contacted the property management company of the community, and its security captain Mr. Lai expressed that they would assist Mr. Zhang in doing a good job in post-accident handling.

Lawyer Sun Shunfa of Sichuan Haoda Law Firm said that cars generally have insurance, and in case of unexpected situations, the insurance company should bear the compensation liability. Since this vehicle damage was caused by the fire, after the cause of the fire is investigated clearly, the party at fault for causing the fire shall also bear certain joint liability for compensation.
