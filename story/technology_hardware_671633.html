The hospital has already stated that all medical expenses will be waived, so why do you still insist on taking them away?" the reporter could not understand Li Mou's actions.

At 3 PM on December 18th, shortly after Li Mou admitted lying, he voluntarily came to the office and said he wanted to immediately process the discharge procedures to take the mother and child away.

At 4 PM on December 19th, the reporter also specially went to the hospital to try to dissuade Li Mou.

On the afternoon of December 20th, considering the patient was moderately anemic, the nurse was preparing to give her another 400 ml of blood when Li Mou again indicated they would still choose to leave.

Despite everyone's attempts to persuade him, Li Mou still rushed to the director's office to process the discharge procedures. Soon, after a simple tidy-up, Li Mou forcibly pulled his wife out of the hospital, tears streaming down Peng's face. However, looking at the family's retreating backs, Director Yang couldn't help but sigh: "Is saving face more important than two fresh lives?"

Seeing this situation, Director Yang Guichang immediately helped the patient with a blood transfusion and arranged for a doctor to specifically observe the baby in case of any emergency.

He insisted on taking the mother and child home

Just because he couldn't let go of "a man's dignity", disregarding the health of his wife and child -

"Actually, I wasn't deliberately lying, but I couldn't help myself." Facing everyone's doubts, Li Mou frankly admitted that because the family opposed their union, for four years, they had never been to his mother-in-law's house.

But surprisingly, an hour later, Li Mou still insisted on taking the mother and child away from the hospital. Seeing this, the head nurse personally took out one hundred yuan and gave it to Li Mou to buy milk powder. Later, another kind-hearted person gave fifty yuan. However, everything everyone did seemed unable to shake Li Mou's determination to leave.

After learning about the situation, the hospital immediately studied and decided not to consider economic issues first, but to treat the adult and child well before discussing anything else. Subsequently, Director Yang told Li Mou to take the 300 yuan first to buy milk powder for the baby. If there were real economic difficulties, the hospital would reduce or waive fees as appropriate, hoping that Li Mou's family would stay until both the adult and child were safe before leaving. Then, the hospital immediately provided the patient with a free 400 ml blood transfusion.

Li Mou said that as a man, even if his wife gave birth, he had no money for hospitalization, which made him feel very guilty. Receiving help from kind-hearted people made him both happy and sad. He was happy because he met good people along the way, but he was sad because accepting others' kindness made him feel like he owed them for the rest of his life, making his heart restless.

At this moment, Li Mou lowered his head, silent for a long time. However, soon, he said again, "I cannot answer your request, I need to see if my wife agrees." Subsequently, when everyone went to the bedside to ask the patient Peng Mou, she replied, "I don't want to leave the hospital." After seeing Peng Mou's statement, Li Mou then said, "Then let's stay for two more days."

Refusing everyone's help,

The man still chose to leave.

His wife was about to give birth,

Just because he had no money, he pretended to be a neighbor.

Having lost his source of income returning home in glory,

On the morning of December 18th, at 11 AM, a taxi hastily stopped in front of the Guizhou Retired Physicians Hospital. The door opened, and a young couple rushed into the hospital. Upon seeing the doctors, the man anxiously said, "Doctor, please help her. Her water broke, she's about to give birth."

Upon learning about the situation, the obstetrics department immediately examined the pregnant woman and found that her cervix was fully open and needed to be immediately sent to the operating room for rescue. In about five minutes, a boy was born, and the mother and child were safe. However, the doctors found that the child was premature, weighing only three pounds and four ounces. After simple treatment, the doctor immediately placed the child in an incubator for observation. Meanwhile, the mother had mild anemia before childbirth, with only 6 grams (6g-L) of blood at the time, and after childbirth, she lost some blood, leaving her with only 4.3 grams of blood.

Rumor behind,

The hospital offered support,

Knowing the news, the chief of the obstetrics department, Mr. Sun Ronggang, once again came to the ward to persuade Li Mou to stay and continue treatment. Director Sun informed that since the baby was premature, the internal organs might not have developed completely, with very weak resistance. Once outside the incubator and exposed to cold, it might lead to lung infections, digestive tract, and respiratory infections, which would be quite detrimental to the baby's growth. Additionally, the mother remained anemic. If blood was not replenished in time, late postpartum hemorrhage might occur, posing significant safety hazards.
