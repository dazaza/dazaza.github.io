The Weibo of Baixia Branch of Nanjing Public Security Bureau issued a wanted notice in the style of "Sanguosha", which ignited the network.

by edivista on 2011-12-13 09:22:30

Click to enter the Weibo of the Baima Branch of the Nanjing Public Security Bureau

Wanted notices can also be used to play the currently popular card game "Sanguosha" (Three Kingdoms Kill). The Baima District police in Nanjing City have pioneered a Sanguosha version of the wanted notice nationwide. Photos of six local fugitives have been made into Sanguosha character cards for netizens to download. Shen Yuan, deputy director of the Political Work Office of the Baima Branch, stated that the original intention of the Baima police was very simple: the wider the wanted notice is spread, the more people see it, the more effective it will be. Meanwhile, the Baima police stated that providing clues before December 15th could lead to rewards, with a total reward amount of 280,000 yuan for the six fugitives. (Click on the image to enter the Weibo of the Baima Branch of the Nanjing Public Security Bureau)

All the wanted criminals are on the run after committing murder.

It is understood that Sanguosha is a card game set against the backdrop of the Three Kingdoms era and is currently extremely popular with high enthusiasm. In addition to role cards, there are also equipment cards and strategy cards in Sanguosha. This time, the Baima police also designed various equipment cards and strategy cards such as police cars, prisoner transport vehicles, and surrender in the Sanguosha version of the wanted notice.

The reporter saw that the Sanguosha wanted notice published by the Baima police not only set up Sanguosha-style skills and different numbers of blood grids for these six fugitives but also wrote the photos of the six wanted fugitives and their crimes on the cards.

The Sanguosha version of the wanted notice shows that all six fugitives are involved in murder cases. For example, on the Sanguosha wanted notice card of Xu Qun, it is written that on the afternoon of July 20, 2003, Chen Mou, who was temporarily residing in the Baima District, was found dead at home. It was determined to be a case of intentional homicide. After investigation, her boyfriend Xu Qun (male, 37 years old, from Yanshan County, Shangrao City, Jiangxi Province) was found to have major suspicion and is now on the run. Among the six, Wang Huachun and Wang Shenjun committed the same case. On March 2000, Shi Mou in the Jianning District of Nanjing City was stabbed with a sharp weapon and died despite medical efforts. Investigations revealed that the two were suspects and have been on the run ever since.

The wanted notice advises fugitives: "It's cold outside, don't hide anymore."

Along with this Sanguosha wanted notice, the Baima police also posted another set of wanted notices on their official Weibo account. The style and design of these wanted notices bear some resemblance to the popular Japanese anime "One Piece." In this version of the wanted notice, it also writes: "It's cold outside, don't hide anymore, surrender voluntarily and there will be policies."

This time, the Baima police also set up a tip-off reward system for the six fugitives. Before December 15th, providing clues leading to the capture of the fugitives would result in rewards. Except for Xu Qun, whose bounty is 30,000 yuan, the other five each have a bounty of 50,000 yuan. Yesterday afternoon, when the reporter called the tip-off hotline number provided by the Baima police, the officer answering the phone stated that no valuable clues had been received yet.

Previously launched a roaring Tom Cat anti-fraud video.

After the Baima police in Nanjing released the Sanguosha version of the wanted notice, most netizens gave positive reviews. A netizen named Xian Hu Jian Tian said: "Too talented, support! The uncles of the Nanjing police are really cute and V5 (magnificent)." Another netizen named Zuo Yin You Jian commented: "Very novel and creative, attracting attention while also narrowing the distance between the police and the public." However, some netizens still prefer the seriousness of the regular version of the wanted notice.

Shen Yuan is one of the designers of this Sanguosha version of the wanted notice. He stated that Sanguosha is a very popular game now, and the police-designed fugitive-catching version of Sanguosha can be integrated into this game. Netizens can remember the appearance of the fugitives while playing the game, so as to provide clues. He indicated that these wanted notices have already attracted a lot of attention. Not only have they been widely forwarded online, but many media outlets have also expressed interest in publishing them. He said: "Issuing wanted notices is to catch the fugitives, allowing more people to remember and see them." These fugitives have been on the run for a certain period of time, and pursuing them has cost a lot of effort and manpower. Adopting this form is also to let everyone see it and pay attention to safety.

This is not the first innovation of the Sanguosha version of the wanted notice by the Baima police. Earlier this year, the Baima police also launched a series of anti-fraud and theft prevention videos featuring the roaring Tom Cat, which also received a large number of forwards. The voice actor for Tom Cat was none other than Shen Yuan himself, who stated that traditional methods are sometimes boring and rigid, leaving little impression after viewing. Regarding netizens' comments about the frequent cuteness of the Baima police, Shen Yuan said: "We mainly hope to do more for everyone, and please focus your attention on these individuals, providing clues."
