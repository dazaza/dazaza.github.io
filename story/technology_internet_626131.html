A Sina V-certified user named Wuhan Patapong released a recruitment information at around 4 o'clock in the morning yesterday, claiming to recruit a live-in maid for an introverted otaku.

Base salary of 3000 yuan, no housework required, but need to accompany watching anime.

In this recruitment notice, the reporter saw that it was marked that the applicant should wear a maid costume, live in the master's house, call the employer "master", accompany the master to watch anime, not wash clothes or cook, just make tea and serve tea, and take care of the master's daily life. And it is claimed that absolutely no sexual relationship is involved. The treatment of the maid is also clearly marked, with contract, three insurances, weekend double rest, base salary above 3000 yuan, and various bonuses according to performance. (Click on the picture to enter Wuhan Patapong Weibo)

No housework but need to accompany watching anime, what is this private maid? The reporter learned that this is actually related to the maid culture popular among ACG fans in recent years.

Maid originally comes from Europe, its English name is maid, so many anime fans like to call it "maid". In Japanese anime and game related works, "maid" is regarded as a profession with "moé" attributes, usually young women wearing aprons (that is, the so-called "maid skirts"), and there are also strict requirements in terms of address, such as must call the other party "master".

In recent years, the "maid" trend has also spread to China, and different themed "maid" cafes have emerged in many cities in mainland China. Some board game stores and video game stores also use "maids" as a means to attract customers. Including this user who posted the recruitment information for private maids, their profile is also marked as "Wuhan Maid Video Game" and they have been recruiting part-time and full-time maids for the store. But recruiting private live-in maids, according to the reporter's understanding, there has been no such situation before.

Best to speak both Chinese and English, personal safety guaranteed by contract

The reporter then called the recruitment contact number provided by the Weibo.

After the phone was connected, a man asked about the reporter's identity. When the reporter said he was consulting for a friend, the man admitted that the information was indeed released by them.

According to the man, this recruitment of a private maid was the requirement of a 17-year-old boy, that is, the introverted otaku mentioned in the Weibo, but he did not agree to disclose the detailed private information and contact method of the recruiter.

The man told the reporter that for this recruitment of a private maid, it would be best if she could speak both Chinese and English, because the employer likes British-style maids, so they hope the applicant can speak English. The job content is as published on Weibo, does not include heavy housework, and absolutely does not involve body transactions, which is commonly known as sexual transaction behavior, so the safety of girls does not need to worry.

When the reporter asked how they could guarantee that this promise would definitely be fulfilled, the man said that they would provide a detailed contract for the girl to decide after reading it. If the employer breaches the contract, the maid can use the contract to seek legal action.

When the reporter raised again, although the contract only mentions accompanying the master to watch anime, if the anime watched is adult eighteen-plus animation; or if it involves privacy and embarrassing services under the name of serving the master's daily life, whether it constitutes sexual harassment to the female; the man claimed that the acceptance degree of anime scale varies from person to person, which should be privately negotiated between the applicant maid and the employer. We just post this information on behalf of the parties involved, and do not conduct more work in between.

Psychologist's Perspective <<<

Why are maids so popular

Dr. Yuan Yonggui, director of the Department of Psychology and Psychiatry at Zhongda Hospital, told reporters that maid culture ultimately satisfies modern people's need for respect. Our needs are divided into multiple levels, when one need is met, new, higher-level needs will arise.

In fact, every day in our work and life, we often have to be in a low position, facing our superiors and elders, we all have to behave very humbly. However, in front of a maid, a person first feels treated with absolute respect, which can greatly satisfy a person's self-esteem needs, and produce a sense of pleasure as "I am the master, I am the authority." Similar consumption places like maid cafes, which focus on maid culture, will make consumers feel "I am not a customer here, I am the master," this feeling of returning to one's own kingdom will make people more relaxed, relieving stress.

Moreover, the status of men and women in modern society is constantly changing, and it is normal for men to feel some loss today as women become stronger. And maids can also help them regain that sense of superiority.

Yuan Yonggui stated that moderate maid culture has no problem in relieving stress, and as a pop culture phenomenon, it is unnecessary to look askance at it, but any matter has its limits, if the parties involved are overly obsessed with such games, it will not be beneficial to maintaining good mental health levels. Lawyer's Perspective <<<

Non-sexual bottom line should be marked first

Zhang Liang, a lawyer from Jiangsu Tiangan Law Firm, said that although "maids" are still a new term in China, from this recruitment notice, it is essentially similar to finding a nanny, and also belongs to an employment relationship.

As for the content of the service, although maids and nannies are different, the key is still whether it involves pornographic content. If it involves pornographic content, it is considered illegal, if it really does not involve, it is just personal preference, the law will not interfere.

Mr. Zhang also reminded young women who plan to apply for such jobs to specify their acceptable non-pornographic bottom line in the contract when negotiating with the master, so as to avoid disputes where they cannot explain themselves clearly.
