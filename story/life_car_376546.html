Continuous progress never stops, Changan Automobile's quality is a cut above the rest.

by ymsdlfs on 2010-12-30 22:34:19

Changan Automobile originated in 1862, grew up in the People's Republic of China, and thrived during the reform and opening-up period. It is a pioneer of modern Chinese industry. Changan Automobile belongs to China Ordnance Industries Group Corporation Limited. In 1996, it separated from its original parent company to become an automobile joint-stock limited company. In 1997, it was listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange. It is a specialized automobile company integrating automobile R&D, manufacturing, sales, and services. Domestically, it owns two listed companies and four stocks.

Changan Automobile is an open company that actively strengthens joint ventures and cooperation with world-class automobile companies. It has established strategic partnerships with multinational enterprises such as Ford, Suzuki, Volvo, Mazda, and Visteon. The products under its brand, including Mondeo-Zhisheng, Focus, Fiesta, Mazda3, Mazda2, Volvo S80L, Volvo S40, Suzuki Tianyu, and Swift, are deeply loved by a wide range of users.

Quality is the golden signboard of Changan Automobile. All consumers of Changan Automobile can feel their dedication to product quality. The company is committed to driving industrial harmony development through technology and providing global consumers with energy-saving, safe, environmentally friendly, and high-quality products. High quality also significantly enhances the safety of Changan Automobile's products, making them very popular among customers. An increasing number of people choose Changan Automobile as their partner for work and travel.

Changan people say: there are no trivial matters in quality work. Quality work requires perseverance and the full play of all employees' talents. Doing one's job well is just the foundation; treating work with enthusiasm and striving for perfection is the way to make continuous progress. As a quality worker, revitalizing product quality is our duty and responsibility. We must not shirk our responsibilities. Each team focuses on quality control, adheres to strict measures for quality, emphasizes the entire process of quality control, and fully implements refined management of quality work. Based on the principle that "the next process is the customer," we take practical actions to do a good job in quality inspection and prevention.

On October 31, Changan Automobile released four brand logos at the Water Cube in Beijing. Its products will be divided into two series in the future: passenger cars and commercial vehicles, each using its own brand logo. At the same time, Changan Automobile's corporate name logo and corporate public welfare logo made their first appearance. After the release, Xu Liuping, Vice President of China Ordnance Industries Group Corporation Limited, Chairman and President of China Changan Automobile Group, and Chairman of Changan Automobile Co., Ltd.; Zhang Baolin, President of Changan Automobile Co., Ltd.; Song Jia, Vice President; and Wang Jun, Assistant to the President, accepted media interviews.

According to Xu Liuping, the passenger car brand logo launched this time will mainly target the mainstream passenger car market, or the mass consumption market. In the future, Changan Automobile will launch a premium brand to form a high-low combination similar to Toyota and Lexus. Xu Liuping also revealed that by the end of this year, Changan Automobile will announce its strategic plan for 2020. By then, Changan Automobile's annual production and sales volume target will be 6 million vehicles, aiming to become a world-class automobile enterprise.

Changan Automobile is constantly improving. Now, based on superior quality, it is moving towards the premium market. There is no best, only better. In the future, Changan will adhere to the scientific development guideline of "goodness first, fast progress in goodness," hold high the banner of independent innovation, and advance towards the goal of becoming an international excellent enterprise with revenue exceeding 10 billion yuan and ranking among the world's top 500!
