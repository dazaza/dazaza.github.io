The social order of the online promoter industry is above "benevolence"

by jiashengyuan on 2012-02-16 01:11:42

In fact, the online promoter is a new type of job under the new media of the network. The online promoter company is a collection of social development loopholes. The marketers, advertisers and planners of traditional media keep pace with the times, grasp the opportunities of the new media platform and transform themselves by relying on their own advantages. To be blunt, online promoters and online promoter companies are all products of the pre-network marketing era. They just follow the trend of the development of new media.

The negative effects brewed by the constantly rising grassroots voices, the noisy sounds that keep disturbing this, the drawbacks of information fragmentation and discourse degradation, and many online hypes that have been criticized and condemned by netizens come from online promoters who lack quality, professional ethics and values. Every time they break the bottom line and morality seriously declines in the face of interests, they are repeatedly accused by a large number of netizens for their shameless behavior.

These people think that as long as the motive is pure and the intention is good, they can occupy the spiritual high ground, hold the truth and always be right, regardless of the damage or even sacrifice of public interests. What they don't know is that talking only about substantive justice and ignoring procedural justice is a kind of violent thinking and violent behavior that is extremely harmful to society, especially an obstacle to the establishment of a good network order.

Experience and a great deal of facts tell us that no matter how pure the motivation is, how noble the wish is, and how grand the goal is, almost all actions that stop at nothing to achieve their goals are often the root of man-made disasters and the source of calamities. Here Jia Shengyuan Online Promoter suggests that in all public fields, we should reduce the dominance of purpose, see more whether each link of the incident is reasonable and legal, and see more whether people's every move is within the provisions of law and social ethics. In a civilized legal society, the truth of facts and the beauty of public order are more important than doing good deeds.
