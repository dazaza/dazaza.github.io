Guangzhou Patent Application: Routes and Procedures for Recognizing Guangzhou Famous Trademarks

by gddingtian7c on 2012-02-15 15:33:30

The Road and Procedures for Identifying Famous Guangzhou Trademarks

(I) The evaluation of famous Guangzhou trademarks is handled by the Guangzhou Famous Trademark Recognition Committee, which is organized and established by the Guangzhou Administration for Industry and Commerce. After the recognition committee office receives the application materials for famous trademarks submitted by the industrial and commercial branches in various jurisdictions, it reviews them according to the "Measures" and this "Implementation Opinion", conducts on-site inspections of the business premises of the applicant and licensee; and seeks opinions from relevant departments regarding matters in the application materials, including the Publicity Department of the Municipal Committee, the Municipal Economic and Trade Commission, the Municipal Science and Technology Information Bureau, the Municipal Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Cooperation Bureau, the Municipal Agriculture Bureau, the Municipal Tourism Bureau, Guangzhou Entry-Exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau, the Municipal Quality Supervision Bureau, the Municipal Drug Supervision Bureau, the Municipal Health Bureau, the Municipal Environmental Protection Bureau, the Municipal Intellectual Property Office, the Municipal State Taxation Bureau, the Municipal Local Taxation Bureau, the Municipal Human Resources and Social Security Bureau, Guangzhou Customs, Huangpu Customs, relevant industry associations, and relevant business offices of the Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce. After comprehensive summarization, it submits written preliminary review opinions to the recognition committee for evaluation.

(II) Procedures

1. Applicants conduct online declarations through the Red Shield Network and submit application materials to the trademark advertising department of the district industrial and commercial branch within 5 working days after online declaration. When submitting application materials, the original trademark registration certificate, audit report, association proof, and tax department proof must be carried for verification.

2. After proposing recommendation opinions on the application materials, each industrial and commercial branch should submit the materials to the Trademark Management Division of the Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce within the specified time limit. Within 10 working days from the receipt of the application materials, the Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce makes a decision on whether to accept the application and notifies the applicant.

3. The Trademark Management Division of the Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce is responsible for conducting preliminary reviews, on-site inspections, and situation verifications for accepted famous trademark recognition applications and proposes preliminary review opinions. A decision is made within 90 working days from the date of acceptance.

4. Solicit opinions from relevant units such as the Municipal Economic and Trade Commission, the Municipal Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Bureau, the Municipal Quality Supervision Bureau, the Municipal Food Supervision Bureau, the Municipal Intellectual Property Office, Guangzhou Customs, and Huangpu Customs.

5. The Municipal Administration for Industry and Commerce convenes a Guangzhou Famous Trademark Recognition Meeting to review and recognize famous Guangzhou trademarks.

6. Publish the results of the recognition of famous Guangzhou trademarks in newspapers to solicit opinions from all sectors of society.

7. If there are no objections during the public announcement period, the trademark will be recognized as a famous Guangzhou trademark, an announcement will be issued, and certificates and plaques will be awarded; if any non-conformities with the conditions for recognizing famous trademarks are found during the public announcement period, after verification, the trademark will not be recognized as a famous Guangzhou trademark, and the applicant will be notified in writing and the application materials will be returned.
