Prologue: This was a legendary era with more handsome men than locusts...

Scene 1: Zhou Yu's tent

Characters: Zhou Yu, mirror, Lu Su, Cao's envoy

(Opening, spotlight on two people)

Zhou: (to the audience) Everyone, (pointing to the mirror) this is the legendary mirror I swiped from Snow White of "The Enchanted Land." It is said to know everything in the world and understand all truths. Now let me ask it a question. (turns head) Mirror, oh mirror, who is the most handsome man in the Three Kingdoms?

Mirror: Of course it's the super invincible mighty man Zhang Fei!

Zhou: (searching around) Hammer, where's the hammer?

Mirror: (serious) That was just a joke, the correct answer should be Xu Chu, the thunderous hero unmatched in strength.

Zhou: (pulls out the hammer, smirking) Hmm, found it.

Mirror: (strikes a pause pose) Since that's the case, then I don't need to hide it anymore. The most handsome man in the Three Kingdoms is the charming, elegant, witty, both scholarly and martial, loved by all - (pauses, Zhou Yu looking smug) - me, the mirror!! No need to thank me, everyone, praise me! Hahahahaha...

Zhou: Million-ton Steam Hammer!!! (beats up the mirror, rolling off stage)(Lu Su enters the tent)

Lu: Commander, there's an envoy from Cao Cao seeking an audience.

Zhou: (flicks his hair) Let him in. (Cao's envoy enters the tent, holding a letter)

Envoy: Commander Zhou, our lord has sent this letter.

Zhou: Lu Su, take the letter. (Lu Su takes the letter)

Envoy: (gesturing for a tip) Um... this... you know what I mean...

Zhou: (realizing suddenly) Ah, so you want...

Envoy: (delighted, nodding) Yes, yes.

Zhou: Servants, drag him out and behead him.

Envoy: Why?! Why are you killing me?

Zhou: Because - you're too ugly!

Envoy: In war, envoys aren't killed, Zhou Yu, you have no humanity!(Lushun, Lü Meng enter, dragging the envoy off stage. "Ahhh!")

Zhou: (strikes a cool pose) Don't say I have no humanity, I just lack some.

Lu: Commander, you're truly too cool! You deserve to be the first handsome man of the Three Kingdoms!

Zhou: (laughs loudly) Hahaha, needless to say...(the mirror vomits) Hmm? (glances at the letter, tears it up in a few swift motions, throws it into the trash can.)

Lu: Commander, why tear up the letter?

Zhou: Cao Cao's writing is nonsensical, ten words with eight mistakes, who would read it.

Lu: But...

Zhou: (impatient) If you want to read it, go ahead yourself.

Lu: (takes the letter from the trash can, sees it's been cut into little figures) Who would've thought the commander's mastery of traditional Chinese paper-cutting art is so profound!!

Zhou: Genius! I am indeed a genius!! Hmm... hehe... hahahahaha! Lu Su, quickly mobilize the entire army, I will personally lead the team to humble Cao Cao's arrogance!

Lu: But... Commander, we've heard that Cao Cao recently hired two Central Plains officers to train their navy, they are now very powerful.

Zhou: Oh? That must not be underestimated... Lu Su, gather all the junior generals in the tent, we'll set out for reconnaissance.

Lu: Yes. (Zhou, Lu exit, mirror exits)

Wu Kingdom dock > Yangtze River > A small road by the Yangtze River

Characters: Zhou Yu, Taishi Ci, Lu Xun, Lü Meng, Cao Cao, Cai Mao, Zhang Yun, Jiang Gan, mirror (cameo)

Zhou: (counting heads) All here... hmm? Where's Lu Su?

Lu Xun: He suddenly said he had a stomachache...

Zhou: Baka Yarou!!! Fine, let's go.

(A group of them sing loudly: "Let us row our boats...")

Lü Meng: Commander, the Cao army is ahead.

Zhou: Stop. (whispering) Quietly go in, no shooting. (Everyone pulls out binoculars to look. A group of Cao Cao's men are on the ship)

Cao Cao: (wearing a white windbreaker, sunglasses, buzz cut) Follow me. Amo Gen! ...
