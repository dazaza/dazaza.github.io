I am willing to stand behind you, invincible.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:24:11

I said: dear, I am willing to stand behind you.

I start to miss you, think about the fragments of our acquaintance until now.

Think about your status in my heart, that status without suspense.

Think about what I would do without you.

I start to overthink, feel very sad and panic.

A girl sang "The Departure Station" in her space.

I just feel very sad.

Listening to this song reminds me of the stations in Changsha, Wuchang, Xiaogan.

Since then, I have come to hate the stations thoroughly, because the stations always foretell departure.

Later, I listened to another song, I can't remember its name.

I only remember these four words in the lyrics - depend on each other for survival.

Then it occurred to me: depend on each other for survival, in order not to leave or abandon.

Feelings are always like this, need to be honed, need to be polished by time, in order to last long.

Those fleeting feelings must be tasteless, so people don't linger on them.

Long-lasting feelings must go through some unforgettable experiences.

In the past, I was a person with strong dependence, always using others' opinions to verify my own value.

Now, I am still a person with strong dependence, but I have learned to distinguish clearly.

I always tend to miss someone when singing.

I remember after we got together, we sang songs together.

At that time, I said: This is the only time I don't have to send text messages to tell you I miss you when singing.

Because you were right beside me, hugging me.

I also always tend to reminisce about the past when singing.

Overthinking, my brain can't stop for a moment. In the end, I even forget what I thought about.

Dear, I miss you so much.

I look forward to seeing you, wanting the holiday to come soon, so I can see you earlier.

I hope everything can settle down, no longer living in constant fear.

I want to see you standing steadily in that place, where I can see you as soon as I turn around.

You wait for me in that city.

We live together. So beautiful.

You are like the sky, I just need to look up to own everything.

This is something I saw yesterday in a Baidu blog, a sentence a girl said to the then-young blogger in the old times.

Because of its brilliance and dazzlingness, the blogger remembers it until today.

And me, how long has it been since I could straightforwardly express such sincere and heartfelt words.

Actually, who is whose sky, or even everything.

In the years before, at the age when love was regarded as faith, after being abandoned by love, the sky didn't collapse because of it, and I didn't lose everything either.

Now, love is no longer my daily required course, but my longing for you has never found a proper place to be placed.

You have become the main character of my life, participating in all my joys and sorrows.

You said no matter what I will become in the future, you will like me, you said you will always love me and not leave me.

I know you are sincere, I believe you, thus I believe in love.

I always use age and experience as an excuse, because I really don't know where my current mentality comes from, I can't find the root.

In fact, I have always been happy to be loved by you, even without too many words and dazzling romance.

I have such a good man who is willing to give me a promise, I should be lucky, I should cherish it.

I love you, because I know you love me just as much, undistracted.

I miss you so much.

Dear, I want to watch TV with you, chat, then hug you.

I miss the smell on your body so much, it's like having warm sunlight shining on me.

You said you want to eat the noodles I cook, dear, I will definitely cook for you, the kind that tastes really good.

I always want to dress you up stylishly, even if I can't see you every day, I am willing to.

In the future, when I make money, I will buy a lot of good clothes for you, I said I don't want my man to wear inferior products, I can't bear to neglect you.

In how long, we can live together, can stay together forever, I can't wait.

I miss our short time together, the sunlight shines on our eyelashes, dazzlingly brilliant.

Even the dust sparkles with dazzling light, all the particles in the air are happily jumping.

With you, plain water can taste sweet.

Dear, are you secretly laughing at my sentimentality?

I miss you so much, I don't know what to do.

I just miss you, I don't know how to express this longing with words, it's so huge, my heart can hardly bear it.

I just miss you day by day, counting the days until we can meet again with my fingers.

Dear, do you miss me as much as I miss you, or even more?

Whatever, I just miss you.

Missing, floods everywhere.

Because of love, I run madly towards you.
