Real estate companies slow down their hiring pace.

by bhhgsyfk4143 on 2012-02-13 15:59:47

This year, the participation of real estate companies in more than 10 job fairs held by the three major talent markets in Zhuzhou has significantly decreased compared to last year. However, local group-buying websites in Zhuzhou are aggressively recruiting talents. As an emerging occupation, the annual salary of management personnel from group-buying websites has exceeded 100,000 yuan. The reporter did not find any recruitment information for real estate companies in the Zhuzhou area through Zhitong Talent Network and Zhuzhou Talent Network. Similarly, Sany Heavy Industry, a large enterprise, did not achieve the expected results in its recruitment in Zhuzhou. "On February 4th, only 30 people submitted resumes, and only two people came for the interview. Although the salary and benefits we offered were not low, the monthly salary for painters was over 5,000 yuan." Gao Song, the recruitment manager of the Pumping Division of Sany Heavy Industry, analyzed the reasons: the preparation was very rushed, there was no publicity work done beforehand, and they chose a regular booth, so the booths with good locations were already booked, which led to fewer applicants. ■ Career Dynamics The reporter also noticed that there is no recruitment information posted on the Zhuzhou stations of all the major group-buying websites nationwide. Lashou.com posted 17 recruitment messages on its official website, all recruiting network technicians, and 16 of them were located in Beijing. According to the customer service staff of the website, there are currently no recruitment plans in Zhuzhou. "Before the end of May this year, we plan to recruit 1,000 new employees. On February 3rd, 400 people have been hired, and on the 9th, more than 200 people entered the second round of interviews, and it is estimated that more than 100 people will be hired." Su Shijun, the director of the Talent Development Department of the Human Resources Department of BAIC Motor Corporation, said. At the job fair on February 3rd, 28 companies simultaneously recruited talents at the Zhuzhou Human Resources Market. Although they received 9,508 resumes that day, 9,270 of them were directed to BAIC Motor, and it is worth noting that the matching success rate (the preliminary intention reached between job seekers and enterprises) for the entire job fair was only 7.2%. Emerging Occupation: Local Group-buying Websites Aggressively Recruiting Talents "We will definitely come to Zhuzhou to recruit talents again. Not just Zhuzhou, but Xiangtan and Changsha as well, we will hold job fairs. Currently, we plan to hire 1,000 new employees." Gao Song said. BAIC Holding has already employed more than 500 people. Last year at the beginning of the year, group-buying websites began to enter the public eye. Some group-buying websites expanded rapidly within a few months before closing down. After the "clean-up" of this industry, the prospects remain unclear. However, the local "newcomer" Gezha Group took the opportunity to expand its business and attract some talents to join. Subsequent investigations revealed that many job seekers indeed did not know that Sany Heavy Industry had recruited in Zhuzhou. "If I had known that Sany Heavy Industry was recruiting in Zhuzhou, I would definitely apply. Regardless of how the salary and benefits are, working in such a large company would definitely help my own development." Job seeker Li Zhuming said. New Phenomenon: Real Estate Companies Slow Down Recruitment Pace Compared to last year, real estate companies have clearly faded out of this year's recruitment wave. "Last year, 6 or 7 real estate companies participated in the recruitment, but this year, only one did." Li Lan, the client manager of Zhuzhou Private Enterprises Talent Market, said. "We expect to hire more than 60 employees, and we have already received nearly 300 resumes. We will start screening tomorrow." Wang Shuai, the head of Zhuzhou's local group-buying website Gezha Group, said. He also revealed that his company has received many resumes from staff members of the Zhuzhou stations of national large group-buying websites. "The salaries we offer are relatively high, the annual salaries of the three supervisors are all over 100,000 yuan, and salespeople with good performance can earn between 6,000 to 8,000 yuan per month." Wang Shuai said. Currently, each recruitment fair held by the three major talent markets in Zhuzhou allows 300-600 job seekers to successfully enter companies, with a matching success rate of around 50% (except for the recruitment fair held by the human resources market on February 3rd), which means there are still 300-600 positions left unfilled. "Enterprises and job seekers are choosing each other. Job seekers prefer to join state-owned enterprises, large private enterprises, or companies with better welfare benefits, while companies tend to favor experienced talents." Yi Xiaoling, the director of the municipal human resources market, said. Sany Heavy Industry will return to Zhuzhou for recruitment With the cooling down of the real estate market, most real estate companies have stopped their recruitment process, and some have even started laying off employees. "In January this year, the company originally had six property consultants, but one was laid off, one was transferred, and I chose to resign." Yang Li (a pseudonym), an employee who used to work in a large housing complex in Zhuzhou, said.
