Home

Dreaming of home, both career and business will take a turn for the better. Dreaming of building a home is an auspicious omen. Dreaming that the doors and windows of your home are tightly closed, relatives or friends may pass away. Dreaming of a dazzling, luxuriously decorated home, you can become very wealthy. Dreaming that your home catches fire, you will suffer serious injuries. Dreaming of selling your house, your business may fail or you might get fired. Dreaming that part of your home is damaged, most of your property will be lost. Dreaming of a white home, you will suffer theft or loss. Dreaming that the front door or house catches fire, male relatives may pass away. Dreaming that the back door catches fire, female relatives or friends may pass away. Dreaming of renting a house, you will work abroad. Dreaming of renting out rooms in your home means more income but also more expenses. Women dreaming of entering their home with another man will betray trust. Dreaming of the roof, you will endure hunger. Dreaming of the house being destroyed, enemies will triumph. Dreaming of standing on the roof shouting loudly, an epidemic will occur in your area.
