New Apple products exposed in advance: two Macs + iPad 5

by anonymous on 2013-11-16 11:51:24

According to an inventory information from a non-direct channel, Apple will unveil a new Retina Display MacBook Pro, a 9.7-inch screen 5th generation iPad, and a redesigned Mac Pro desktop computer at the press conference next Tuesday.

This inventory information shows that the current stock of Apple TV set-top boxes and iPad minis is very sufficient. This non-direct channel mainly supports large retailers and distributors rather than serving Apple's online and offline retail stores.

Apple will hold a press conference on October 22. Inventory information indicates that products such as the 13 and 15 inch Retina Display MacBook Pros, Retina Display 5th generation iPads, and Mac Pros will be upgraded, but the prospects for upgrading the iPad mini and Apple TV remain uncertain.

Sources said that the inventory levels of the $329 16GB WiFi version of the iPad mini and the $429 32GB WiFi version of the iPad mini are still high. However, it is generally felt that the supply of iPad minis is tight. This may indicate that Apple plans to keep several non-Retina Display iPad minis in the market, which will be sold at lower prices after the launch of the new Retina Display iPad mini, thus expanding into the mid-to-low-end market.

In addition, before the press conference next week, Apple may be transferring iPad mini inventory from direct channels to non-direct channels. This practice is also common when the company clears its inventory in the past. Sources also said that the inventory of the current generation of Apple TVs is reaching "the highest level in months." (Li Li)
