(Weibo Information Network) Recently, a secretly filmed video circulating online about mainland singer Ma Ruila allegedly engaging in escort services has sparked heated discussions among netizens. On April 12, NetEase Entertainment contacted Ma Ruila's agent Jiang Xinxin, who confirmed that the woman in the video is indeed Ma Ruila but denied that the artist had engaged in escort services. The "Bingbing" mentioned in the video is not Fan Bingbing. Jiang Xinxin further stated that there are many details of the escort video still under investigation and will be announced at an appropriate time.

Escort Video: (Ma Ruila Escort Video)

Recently, a secretly filmed video circulating online about mainland singer Ma Ruila allegedly engaging in escort services has been widely discussed. The video not only shows a woman resembling Ma Ruila bargaining with a man, undressing, and showing her body naked, but also exposes insider information such as first-line to third-line actresses being involved in escort services, including Bingbing’s escort price being around 300,000 to 500,000 yuan. Once the video was released, it immediately caused a frenzy among netizens, and the female protagonist Ma Ruila quickly became an internet celebrity. However, after the video was leaked, neither Ma Ruila nor her agency explained the reason for the leak, and she only left a message online saying "the innocent have nothing to fear," leaving people puzzled.

Ma Ruila Weibo Screenshot: (Ma Ruila Weibo claims "the innocent have nothing to fear", click on the picture to enter Ma Ruila's Weibo)

The woman in the video is indeed Ma Ruila, and she has never been arranged to engage in escort services.

After two days of contact, on April 12, NetEase Entertainment finally got in touch with Ma Ruila's agent Jiang Xinxin. Jiang Xinxin frankly admitted to having seen the related video and confirmed that the woman in the video is one hundred percent his artist Ma Ruila. In the video, the woman skillfully bargains with the client; however, Jiang Xinxin firmly denies that Ma Ruila engaged in escort services: the company did not arrange such activities for her, and Ma Ruila herself did not engage in escort services either. Regarding the insider information revealed by the woman in the video about Bingbing's escort price being 300,000 to 500,000 yuan, Jiang Xinxin said he believes it is a misunderstanding by everyone: the video never specified that Bingbing refers to Fan Bingbing.

As for why the woman in the video made such bold statements without engaging in escort services and whether the leak of the video was a deliberate trap, Jiang Xinxin hesitated to speak: regarding the occurrence of this incident, it involves many unknown details, and we are still investigating further, such as how this video leaked out. At an appropriate time, I will hold a press conference to fully explain the entire event to everyone.

Denial of Artist Hype: Her Career Has Been Affected

After the escort video went viral on the internet, Ma Ruila herself never personally appeared to explain the reasons. Jiang Xinxin stated that the incident greatly affected the artist's career, but with the company handling the situation, Ma Ruila's emotions are currently stable. Originally, she was starring in a movie "Mainland Version of Chiu Sau-Chung: Brotherhood Above All," playing the lead role, but due to this incident, the movie had to be temporarily halted. After checking the data, NetEase Entertainment discovered that the movie "Mainland Version of Chiu Sau-Chung: Brotherhood Above All" starred by Ma Ruila is directed and produced by Jiang Xinxin himself.
