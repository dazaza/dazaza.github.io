The Professional Managers Branch of the Apparel Industry Association was officially established.

by wuren451t on 2012-02-11 14:58:25

At the establishment conference, Liu Yeping, president of Guangdong Apparel & Attire Industry Association introduced that as an important talent pool for the construction of Guangdong Province as a strong province of women's clothing brands, professional managers have played a key role in the operation of fashion women's clothing brands. The fact that Guangdong apparel brands start to generally set up professional manager posts is an important sign that brand management is developing from pure family management to executive professionalization and also a sign that Guangdong apparel brands are constantly growing mature. It is precisely against such background that Guangdong Apparel Association (GDA) considered that the timing for establishing a professional manager branch institution was ripe now. He also put forward four expectations to the professional manager branch in his speech: First, it is expected that the professional manager branch can operate in a standardized manner, and abide by laws and disciplines; Second, it is expected that the professional manager branch can make efforts on self-discipline of the industry, especially standardizing and harmonizing the cooperative relationship between professional managers and service brand enterprises; Third, it is expected that the professional manager branch can give full play to its own resource advantages and high knowledge level advantages, actively take the lead in exploring the construction and promotion application of new business models; Fourth, it is expected that the professional manager branch can actively support and participate in the industry activities of the association, jointly contributing ideas for promoting the healthy development of the industry.
