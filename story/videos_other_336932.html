I. How to correctly understand methanol?

Methanol, also known as wood alcohol, methyl alcohol, and methyl hydroxide, is the simplest saturated alcohol. Its chemical molecular formula is CH3OH. At normal temperature, Chuan Island environmental protection fuel, methanol is a colorless liquid with a slight alcohol smell, freezing point -93.9℃, boiling point 64.7℃, density 0.7914 g/cm3, soluble in water and many organic solvents. Methanol is toxic, and excessive drinking can lead to poisoning and blindness.

Methanol has a wide range of uses and is a basic organic chemical raw material. It mainly has the following three major uses: ① used as an industrial solvent; ② used as a basic raw material to produce formaldehyde, dimethyl ether, acetic acid, protein, and various other organic products; ③ Since methanol belongs to monohydric alcohols, it burns very fully, and its combustion products are carbon dioxide and water (combustion equation: 2CH3OH+3O2=2CO2+4H2O), so the emissions are less, no pollution, and it has greater environmental advantages. Moreover, due to the extremely low production cost of methanol, it has been widely used in recent years for deep processing to produce new clean fuels, which can be used as civil fuel or vehicle fuel.
