Elpo Watch 155th Anniversary Celebration: The "Romance" Sets Sail

(October 20, 2011, Shanghai) To celebrate the 155th anniversary of the Swiss watch brand Elpo, the "Romance" set sail romantically at Hongbang Shanghai, a creative and fashionable landmark. Elegant Angel, Ms. Amy Chow, Romantic Goddess, Ms. Kelly Chen, and Handsome Young Actor, Mr. Feng Lin gathered in Shanghai with Mr. Raphael Boillat, President of Elpo Watches, Ms. Nathalie Boillat, General Manager of Elpo Watches, and Mr. Su Da, CEO of Elpo Watches Asia Pacific region. Together, they turned the gyroscopic disc to start a romantic journey for the guests of Elpo present at the scene.

Accompanied by the sound of departure whistle, the Elpo "Romance" set sail. The beautiful scenery of Switzerland came into full view, flags flying high in the magnificent sky, and boats rocking steadily against the backdrop of the long and tranquil Alps. Dressed in an elegant court ball gown, the elegant angel Amy Chow danced slowly and solemnly with her partner on the bow of the ship. At that moment, the click of the photographer's camera captured what seemed like the romantic and beautiful encounter symbolized by the Elpo logo. This moment was also recorded on the official website of Elpo watches.

As the "Romance" sailed into the city, the silhouette of tall buildings echoed with vibrant music. Leading a group of dancers between the ship and the scenery, handsome young actor Feng Lin demonstrated through interactive imagery and precise coordination how Elpo has evolved from traditional watchmaking craftsmanship towards innovation and modernity, becoming increasingly diverse and accurate.

Gradually, the ship reached a deserted island where a group of adorable flower fairies were gracefully dancing in the emerald paradise. Holding flowers in her hands, the romantic goddess Kelly Chen appeared among the fairies. Suddenly, birdsong and floral fragrance filled the air. A closer look revealed none other than the Elpo Romance series watches blooming beautifully among the flowers!

When describing their impression of Elpo watches, the three endorsers expressed: "The long history and romantic stories of Elpo Watches particularly attract us. It not only inherits the classics but also evolves with the times, becoming more diverse and precise. And the eternal theme of romance is precisely the charm that makes it irresistible!" With its generations-spanning romantic charm, Swiss Elpo Watches adds an extra touch of surprise to the classic heritage with its outstanding quality. For nearly two centuries, Swiss Elpo Watches have been widely embraced around the world for their romantic and elegant brand values. Their couple watches have become the must-have choice for modern romantic couples due to their innovative and lively characteristics, turning into a lifetime classic to flaunt!
