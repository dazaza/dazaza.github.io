Hubei Agricultural Science惠民Centipede Breeding Base Address: Room 318, Tianhui Building, Huazhong Agricultural University, Wuchang District, Wuhan City. Breeding centipedes is a new emerging industry with low input and high output, with a broad prospect and is a very promising good project.

Firstly, centipedes are valuable medicinal herbs with huge market demand. Due to the widespread use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides, as well as mountain excavation and mining, the survival environment of centipedes has been severely damaged, leading to a sharp decline in the wild population. The market supply cannot meet the demand, and prices have skyrocketed. The acquisition price of medicinal departments has risen from 0.1~0.2 yuan per piece in 1990 to over 0.6 yuan per piece, yet it still cannot satisfy market needs, and further price increases are inevitable. These phenomena indicate that the market prospects for centipede breeding are broad.

Secondly, centipedes are omnivorous animals that eat various animal meats, insects, fish and shrimp, as well as various fruits, vegetables, and grains. They never picky eaters, making it easy to prepare sufficient and delicious food for them anytime and anywhere. This lays a solid material foundation for their large-scale breeding.

Thirdly, centipede breeding equipment is simple. Indoors or outdoors, rooftops, ground surfaces, water tanks, water basins, etc., can all be used for breeding.

Fourthly, the artificially bred less-prickly centipedes (also known as golden head centipedes) are large, robust, disease-resistant, have no infectious diseases, reproduce strongly, give birth twice a year, with about 50 offspring per litter, and grow up in eight months. Introducing one centipede per year yields at least 30 yuan net profit. The economic benefits are very considerable.

The most critical equipment for temperature-controlled breeding is a warm room with a certain area that can be heated, temperature controlled, and has good insulation conditions. These warm rooms can be newly built or existing ordinary houses or plastic greenhouses can be renovated. Regardless of the type of warm room, it must meet the following four principles: first, economical and practical; second, equipped with heating and insulation conditions; third, able to maintain good ventilation; fourth, scientifically and reasonably structured for easy management.

In daily management, temperature-controlled breeding is basically the same as normal temperature breeding, differing in the following three aspects: first, attention must be paid to controlling the temperature, keeping it between 20℃ and 36℃; second, water evaporation accelerates, requiring timely replenishment of adequate moisture; third, due to the high temperature in the warm room and relatively poor ventilation, leftover feed from centipedes must be cleared in time to prevent decay and odor formation.

There are many methods for heating, such as coal stoves, wood shavings stoves, rice husk stoves, electric heating tubes, etc.
