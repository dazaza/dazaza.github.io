Let's walk into Chinese herbal beauty together Written by Whitening Taste in Intensive Maintenance

Today, we change our taste and return to our traditional treasure - Chinese medicine adjustment. Chinese medicine not only focuses on treating and saving lives, but also excels in beauty adjustment. Throughout history, countless Chinese doctors have spent their whole lives thinking of ways to make palace women beautiful, leaving behind rich experience, which is absolutely worth our attention!

So, let's walk into Chinese herbal beauty with the editor~

"Chinese herbal beauty" has a profound cultural heritage of China, through thousands of years of people's beauty practice selection, generation after generation of folk beauty and health preservation methods have gathered infinite oriental wisdom.

Whether it is the ancient palace or modern city, women always think hard about how to make themselves more beautiful.

And "Chinese herbal beauty" with its internal and external adjustment, and the concept of making people healthy and beautiful from inside to outside, is getting more and more people who value beauty and health to admire.

However, now the products of Chinese herbal beauty are varied, what kind of way will be effective for oneself? What kind of product will be beneficial to oneself? Through what kind of way to find the product suitable for oneself? I think this is the most concerned and headache problem for everyone.

For this reason, I have been to supermarkets, shopping malls, and beauty salons; then I found a problem, supermarkets and shopping malls basically do not sell Chinese herbal products, some beauty salons use Chinese herbs. When asking merchants, without exception, they all introduce me the advantages of their products, how they can be used, and customers say they are good. But these are all one-sided statements from merchants, it is difficult for us to find actual users to understand unless our friends around us are using them. I am not saying that their products are not good, each product that can survive in the market must have a certain customer base. Personally, I still value our traditional Chinese herbal beauty products.

Because of work, I often surf online, so I decided to use the Internet to look for it. Taobao is the largest shopping and trading platform in China. Usually, I also often buy clothes and various international brand cosmetics on it. I just haven't understood our own Chinese herbal beauty stuff online. After searching, there are really quite a lot of treasures here. I decided to find a store with higher credit to ask.

I clicked into a Chinese herbal beauty shop named "Green Forest". To be honest, what attracted me to click in at first was the image of ancient beauties on their products, hehe, the love of beauty is common to both men and women. After reading their introduction, it is said that they are a family of Chinese medicine, and the products in the small shop are all uniquely formulated by the old Chinese doctor in the family. This aroused my curiosity, wow, imagine if they could give me a secret formula, and I would become a young and invincible beauty tomorrow. After reading several product introductions, I found that the old Chinese doctor in this shop is about 50 years old, but looks like 35 in the photo; it's really amazing, making me envy and admire him. Imagine if I look like this at 50, it's also acceptable.

Thinking of this, I consulted the shopkeeper as a customer to see how she introduced her products to me.

Editor: Shopkeeper, hello, are your products all pure Chinese herbs?

Shopkeeper: Hello, they are all pure Chinese herbs. Is there anything you need?

Editor: I want to learn first.

Shopkeeper: Sure, no problem. What aspect do you want to learn about?

Editor: Your products are said to be formulated by an old Chinese doctor. How long will it take to see effects?

Shopkeeper: Yes, they are formulated by an old Chinese doctor. Chinese medicine emphasizes internal and external adjustment. It is different from general beauty products containing chemical substances or hormones. The effect will not be immediate, but it can treat the symptoms and root causes, fundamentally solving the problems. A course of our products lasts for a month. The speed of effectiveness varies from person to person. Some people can see some effects after one course, while others need more than two courses to see effects. Generally, the longer you use, the better the effect.

Editor: Are there any customers who say they don't see any effect?

Shopkeeper: Yes, some customers are too eager and stop using after a few times, so they say there is no effect. Our products are not miraculous pills, so it's impossible to get satisfactory results after a few uses. For example, some customers have very yellow faces, are still young, and have no other diseases. These customers generally have qi and blood deficiency. We adopt qi and blood supplementation and internal and external adjustment. For such customers, the adjustment takes at least two months. Some customers give up halfway, and then say the product is not effective. As long as they persist, there will be effects.

Editor: It seems that beauty through Chinese medicine cannot be too eager.

Shopkeeper: Yes, because everyone's situation is different, so the time needed for adjustment will be different. As long as customers can persist, their skin will be greatly improved compared to before.

Editor: Okay, thank you, shopkeeper. I have a rough understanding. I will come to buy next time when I need. Wish your business prosperous!

Shopkeeper: Okay, don't mention it. Welcome to visit often.

The editor thinks that the reputation of a product determines its quality. The credibility of the shop is still good, with many repeat customers. The editor decided to probe the opinions of their customers to see if it is true. Among many online buyers, I chose a buyer with a high credit rating. After explaining my intention, she was happy to chat with me. Below is the main part of our conversation (for her privacy, I call her Xiao A here).

Editor: How long have you been using the Chinese herbal beauty products from this shop?

Xiao A: About 8 months ago, at that time, due to staying up late for a long time, my skin was very bad, my complexion was very dull, my skin was dry, there were some fine lines, and I also had spots. Because I have sensitive skin, I didn't dare to use random spot-removing products. I wanted to find a gentle Chinese herbal spot-removing product. I discovered this shop unintentionally, so I started trying it from then on. Later, seeing good results, I continued to use it.

Editor: What products did you use?

Xiao A: The shopkeeper helped me formulate a whitening and spot-removing beauty pill and an all-in-one whitening and spot-removing mask, and also a tender skin hydrating anti-wrinkle mask.

Editor: Are they really effective?

Xiao A: Yeah, after using for a month, my skin tone became very even, my face wasn't so dry, the spots faded a bit, and my skin became whiter. Although it wasn't very white, it wasn't as sallow as before.

Editor: How is your skin improved now?

Xiao A: Now my skin has really become whiter and softer, the spots have become much lighter, they are almost unnoticeable, and they can't be seen after applying foundation. The fine lines have also become very shallow. My skin looks very hydrated and radiant, and it still looks rosy in the morning. Honestly, I am very satisfied with the current results. And I also recommended some friends to use them, and they also said the effects are good.

Editor: Have there been any side effects?

Xiao A: No, because the shopkeeper asked about my physical condition before recommending the products, so I didn't notice any abnormalities. But since the efficacy of Chinese herbs is slow, it took a relatively long time.

Editor: Are you still using them now?

Xiao A: Yes. I have been taking the whitening and beauty spot-removing beauty pills for 6 months, and I have been using the all-in-one mask and tender skin hydrating anti-wrinkle mask twice a week consistently for 8 months. The shopkeeper suggested me to take the beauty pills every 2 months for 2 months because it can also prevent aging. Women are most afraid of aging, haha.

It seems that our long-standing beauty treasure is really great.

Xiao A: Yes. You can try it, talk with the shopkeeper.

Eternal youth is a beautiful wish of human beings, but natural things always go from birth to death, from prosperity to decline. As a part of nature, humans cannot escape this objective rule. Therefore, with the increase of age, the human body will also age.

In Chinese medicine, although it is impossible for people to stay young forever, by replenishing the deficiency of zang-fu organs and qi-blood, maintaining the balance of yin and yang in the human body, it is indeed possible to delay the arrival of aging. Believe that in today's world where people advocate nature and green concepts, Chinese medicine with its five thousand years of accumulated strength, still has its unique charm in anti-aging, beauty, and health care.
