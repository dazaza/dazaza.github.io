Several Taobao stores trial Bitcoin payment

by geekzhang on 2013-05-03 21:02:55

From obscurity to fame, from the initial virtual currency to investment goods and then to donations, Bitcoin has gradually become popular in China step by step.Recently, the reporter found that as a commodity and investment product, Bitcoin already had more than 10 Taobao sellers using Bitcoin as settlement currency. The payment function of Bitcoin has gradually appeared.Can Bitcoin be used as money in reality? A survey showed that more than 1,000 merchants around the world said they would accept Bitcoin as a currency for settlement. However, in China, due to the credibility issue between the two parties in the transaction, the success rate of Bitcoin payment in the aforementioned Taobao stores is not high. Industry insiders said that Bitcoin is far from meeting the basic conditions to become a currency.More than 10 online stores accept Bitcoin paymentsCurrently, Bitcoin in China is showing an explosive growth trend. On the Taobao search page, there are more than 400 items related to Bitcoin. The reporter found on Taobao that in addition to the hot-selling Bitcoin, online stores also sell supporting products for Bitcoin, such as 4-core Bitcoin mining machines and other projects.Moreover, some Taobao stores have also appeared with Bitcoin as the official settlement currency. The reporter found that more than 10 Taobao stores on the Taobao website support Bitcoin payments. The products that support Bitcoin payments include physical items such as energy-saving lamps, mobile phones, cross-stitch embroidery, antiques, and virtual items such as I Ching services.In a store that supports Bitcoin payments for energy-saving lamps, under one energy-saving lamp item, it was written in large letters: Accept Bitcoin payments, buy at the market price. The reporter contacted the store owner of the above-mentioned store, who said that the main reason for supporting Bitcoin is to facilitate some emerging Bitcoin enthusiasts.How to calculate with Bitcoin? The store owner gave an example: An energy-saving lamp costs 58.8 yuan, and the current price of Bitcoin is 726 yuan/Bitcoin, so the price of this lamp can be converted into 0.08 Bitcoins, plus a difference of 0.003 Bitcoins, which is 0.083 Bitcoins. Adding the difference of 0.003 is mainly because the price of Bitcoin is unstable.According to the store owner, his store has completed 3 transactions using Bitcoin payments, equivalent to more than 2,000 yuan RMB. To conduct a transaction, you need to first transfer the Bitcoins to my Bitcoin wallet. Bitcoin transactions are mainly based on trust. If not, use RMB. He said.Besides some physical items, the reporter also saw a notice supporting Bitcoin payments in a Taobao store providing I Ching services. Its Bitcoin pricing converted to RMB is slightly higher than the pricing for RMB payments.Bitcoin settlement raises concerns about credit issuesCurrently, the main payment method for Taobao stores is centered around Alipay. The buyer first transfers the money to the third-party Alipay, the seller ships the goods, and after the buyer confirms, the third party transfers the money to the seller. If paying with Bitcoin, there is no such third party.According to the aforementioned energy-saving lamp store owner, only some of the products in his store support Bitcoin payments. Products with higher prices will not adopt Bitcoin payments, mainly due to the concerns of buyers.Although more than 10 Taobao stores already support Bitcoin payments, for most Taobao sellers, Bitcoin is still a novelty. The reporter called several Taobao stores, and many store owners said they had never heard of Bitcoin.Perhaps because Bitcoin payments are currently not mature, the success rate of Bitcoin payments in some of these stores is not high. The aforementioned energy-saving lamp store owner believes that the main issue is still the credit problem, requiring both buyers and sellers to understand this new type of currency and have confidence in its future direction.A Bitcoin collector enthusiast believes that currently using Bitcoin payments is more like a hype, used as a gimmick to attract buyers. If Bitcoin is used, then the relationship and credit between buyers and sellers need to be established by Taobao. Currently, Bitcoin transactions are difficult to gain momentum.The reporter recently contacted a responsible person from Taobao's PR department, who said they haven't figured out how Bitcoin works yet, and Taobao has not made a public statement regarding Bitcoin as a commodity transaction or currency settlement. Yesterday, the reporter tried calling Taobao again but was unable to get through.Over a thousand foreign merchants accept Bitcoin settlementsIt is understood that abroad, more and more institutions are accepting Bitcoin. In October 2012, according to a report published by BitPay, more than 1,000 merchants have been receiving Bitcoin payments through their payment system. These merchants come in various types, ranging from Mazzeo Barbecue in New York City to the Howard Johnson Hotel in California; from the Fullerton Conference Center to wedding cake stores in Oklahoma that accept Bitcoin payments, including both online and offline suppliers.Since Bitcoin is not issued in unlimited quantities, its issuance is controlled by software algorithms. There are currently approximately 8 million existing Bitcoins. According to the algorithm, the more existing Bitcoins there are, the harder it becomes to produce new ones. It is calculated that by 2140, the total number of Bitcoins will reach its upper limit of 21 million.Chen Sijin, senior advisor at the Royal Bank of Canada, said in an interview that the popularity of Bitcoin, just like the previous surge in gold prices, reflects from another side how dire the financial situation in Europe and America is and how panicked cash holders are.Regarding the current situation in China where no merchants are willing to settle in Bitcoin, Chen Sijin said that historically, the development of physical currency into today's credit currency hinges on two key factors: First, there must be sufficient currency supply in the market; Second, it should be easily divisible and accepted by everyone in a region; Third, it must be guaranteed and issued by the government (or an authoritative institution similar to the government). Currently, Bitcoin is far from meeting the basic conditions to become a currency.Chen Sijin believes that the value of Bitcoin depends on how many people, goods, and services are willing to accept Bitcoin payments. If the number of people accepting Bitcoin increases, the market trading of Bitcoin will further flourish, and Bitcoin will have a huge appreciation space. Since the number of people currently owning Bitcoin is only hundreds of thousands, compared to the user base of over a billion internet users, its growth potential is enormous. This is also an important reason why most Bitcoin holders have strong confidence. However, if the number of Bitcoin users decreases, its value may very likely fall, or even become worthless.
