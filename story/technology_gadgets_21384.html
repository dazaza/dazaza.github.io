SONY Ericsson PSP gaming mobile phone is about to arrive sketch exposure

by yinyin on 2007-06-03 19:43:56

The news that SONY Ericsson will push the psp mobile phone has been rumored, the news at the time said that SONY's mobile phone can play games through an external game controller, but according to a patent document recently exposed, the previous talk is not reliable, because the sketch shows that this is a real sense of the PSP mobile phone.

According to the report, in normal mode, the phone can be used for normal communication, but when the screen is rotated 180 degrees, the phone becomes a game console, as shown in the picture, and the user can move it horizontally. A closer look at the sketch reveals more details, such as buttons that turn into controls for the console.

Of course, the current SONY PSP mobile phone may still be in the research and development stage, and there may be a long way to go from the real market, which can be entered into SONY Ericsson.
