You are my destiny

They all say that God is fair. When he opens a window for you, he will also close a door.

Mo Yan is a beauty, which is an evaluation of her face. She has an angelic face, but unfortunately she didn't have a devilish figure, so others' evaluation of her was: Mo Yan is very cute, if she could be thinner, then she would be a great beauty. Mo Yan also thought about losing weight, but she couldn't stick with it. She knew that although she was a little fat, her physical condition wasn't particularly good, so she always consoled herself. There are many beauties in this world, but as long as I'm not ugly, it's fine. Even so, there were still many people who liked Mo Yan. But Mo Yan thought that love without feelings would hurt each other, so she stayed single, and those who liked her became her brothers or remained friends.

People who know Mo Yan all say that Mo Yan is gentle, speaking softly and laughing softly. But they didn't know the rebelliousness in Mo Yan's bones. In the year Mo Yan turned 18 and left home to study in another city for the first time, she changed. Smoking, drinking, fighting, she thought this kind of life was very unrestrained. At the age of 19, she suddenly realized that such a life was a waste of time, so she decided to study hard and stop going out to play. Mo Yan thought that with her intelligence, she could make up for everything she had lost before and carve out her own place in this school full of hidden dragons and crouching tigers.

However, she forgot one thing, fate likes to joke with people. Mo Yan's cousin knew that Mo Yan was studying at a nursing school, where most of the students were girls. One day, Mo Yan's cousin sent her a picture message saying that the handsome guy in the picture was his good friend, asking Mo Yan to introduce a boyfriend to his good friend. At the first sight of the picture message, Mo Yan's heart moved a bit. But Mo Yan quickly stopped her thoughts and agreed to introduce her best friend to her cousin's good friend. For some unknown reason, there was no conversation between them, not even basic chats. The matter ended there. Mo Yan thought she wouldn't have any intersection with him.

On March 15, 2011, Mo Yan's beloved grandfather was hospitalized. The cousin working elsewhere somehow knew this news and called Mo Yan. At that time, Mo Yan was interning at the People's Hospital where her grandfather was staying. She cried uncontrollably and couldn't clearly explain her grandfather's condition. That night, Mo Yan and her friends went to the internet cafe. She entered her cousin's YY guild and told her cousin the specific situation. Because Mo Yan rarely played YY, she didn't know that someone was listening while she was talking to her cousin.

That was the first time Mo Yan and her cousin's good friend, Chu Xi, had their first real interaction.

After the cousin left for business, Chu Xi kept chatting with Mo Yan, dispersing her depressed mood. Chu Xi was a very domineering man. Knowing that Mo Yan didn't have a boyfriend, he directly told Mo Yan that he would pursue her. Actually, Mo Yan knew that she liked him from the start, so she directly agreed.

Some people said Mo Yan was too forward, agreeing to be Chu Xi's girlfriend just after seeing a picture message. But from Mo Yan's perspective, liking is liking, there's no need to pretend to be coy. Sometimes, when the feeling is right, you don't want to lose it. Finding the right feeling is really not easy. In the following days, Chu Xi called and messaged Mo Yan every day.

Mo Yan also experienced the feeling of dressing up for the person she loved for the first time. She started learning how to dress up and stopped showing her plain face. Mo Yan with a plain face could be described as a clean and pure angel. But Mo Yan with a touch of light makeup carried a hint of allure, making her even more refreshing. Mo Yan wanted to wait for Chu Xi to come back and meet him like this.

She waited for two months, and Mo Yan's cousin came back, telling Mo Yan that Chu Xi would be back in a few days.

But Chu Xi didn't come back, and his calls and messages became fewer and fewer until they basically stopped. Sometimes, when Mo Yan initiated contact, Chu Xi didn't respond much. Finally, one day, Chu Xi called Mo Yan and told her not to send messages to his QQ anymore, claiming that his QQ had been hacked. Smart as Mo Yan was, how could she believe Chu Xi's excuse? By the end of June, her cousin came back to get engaged. Seeing her cousin and future sister-in-law's happy faces, Mo Yan finally couldn't help but send a message to Chu Xi, asking if he had another girlfriend? Chu Xi only replied that he was conflicted and needed time to think.

Her cousin, seeing Mo Yan's sad face, couldn't help but say: Sister, you and him won't have a result. In my group, everyone knows that a woman named 'Gao Diao' is his girlfriend of six years, and they are getting married during the New Year. Mo Yan gave a self-mocking laugh. Actually, she knew, but since Chu Xi hadn't told her personally, she still didn't want to give up hope.

But deep down, Mo Yan was already persuading herself to let go. Mo Yan wasn't a noisy person. She understood clearly in her heart. She would rather be sad alone. Maybe because of the accumulated emotions. One night, Mo Yan went to a bar she hadn't stepped into for three years. With a group of strangers, Mo Yan was given a lot of alcohol. Perhaps God still favored Mo Yan, she didn't get drunk, but the man who had improper intentions towards Mo Yan did. That man kept pulling Mo Yan, not letting her go back. In Mo Yan's mind, unless it was someone she liked or unless there was a marriage guarantee, Mo Yan wouldn't do anything that would make her regret it for life.

Maybe because of her mood, Mo Yan drank while staring at her phone. Until 1:30 AM, when Chu Xi's QQ went dark, Mo Yan finally couldn't help but call Chu Xi. On the phone, Mo Yan cried very sadly. This was the first time Mo Yan cried so much for a man. Hearing Chu Xi's worried voice, Mo Yan thought that Chu Xi still cared about her, right! Mo Yan decided to go back. The next day, after Mo Yan finished work at noon, Chu Xi proactively called Mo Yan, asking if she was okay and telling her not to do this again. Mo Yan gathered her courage and asked Chu Xi for a clear answer, knowing that the answer would break her heart, but Mo Yan still held onto that last bit of hope.

The Chu Xi on the other side of the phone was silent for a long time, saying he couldn't forget his first love. Mo Yan laughed and simply said: "I understand. Wishing you happiness." Then she deleted all contacts related to Chu Xi and buried the memories of Chu Xi. Mo Yan told her friends that if she ever mentioned Chu Xi's name again, her friends should hit her, hitting her once for every mention.

Perhaps time is really the best remedy. Under Mo Yan's deliberate forgetting, slowly, Chu Xi's name and things about Chu Xi were sealed away by Mo Yan. Sometimes Mo Yan thought, Chu Xi should be very happy now.

But fate joked with Mo Yan again.

On the evening of Mo Yan's 20th birthday, Chu Xi sent a message to Mo Yan saying: "Mo Yan, how are you recently? Sorry. I've been waiting for you to come online, just to say sorry."

Mo Yan said: "Chu Xi, there's no need to say sorry. I'm doing well now. You... should be very happy now, right?"

Chu Xi paused for a moment and replied: "Yan Yan, I've broken up. Can you give me another chance?"

Mo Yan wanted to refuse, because Chu Xi was the first person she fell in love with at the age of 20, and someone she had never met face-to-face. But her feelings were still towards Chu Xi. The next day, Mo Yan agreed to Chu Xi, and Chu Xi explained that he owed his ex-girlfriend something, and his ex-girlfriend forced him to leave Mo Yan using extreme methods. Although Mo Yan knew these explanations were just excuses, her heart still couldn't help but leap a little. After all, wasn't Chu Xi back by her side?

Chu Xi told Mo Yan that he would definitely treat her well from now on and would call her every day.

Mo Yan's birthday is October 20th. At the beginning, Chu Xi indeed called Mo Yan every day. They often chatted on QQ. No matter how tired or sleepy Mo Yan was, she would wait for Chu Xi to say he was sleepy and go to bed first before logging off.

However, recently Mo Yan found that those sweet moments were disappearing again. Chu Xi's messages were becoming less and less. Mo Yan thought that if Chu Xi didn't send messages, she would call him. As long as she could hold on until after New Year's Day, Chu Xi would come back and meet her. Then he would surely discover her good qualities.

Many people asked Mo Yan, aren't you tired of this? Chu Xi has nothing, you are a nurse with a stable job, you don't have to worry about not finding a good boyfriend, why do you like Chu Xi so much? Mo Yan had no answer. She just firmly believed in destiny. Some things are indeed destined. So, falling in love with Chu Xi was Mo Yan's destiny.

But Mo Yan had no answer to whether her relationship with Chu Xi could last until he came back? Would she be hurt again?
