Is the iPhone 5C a bargain? Is the iPhone 5S worth it?

by anonymous on 2013-09-12 12:14:34

Yesterday morning, Apple made a colorful appearance, but its stock price fell by 2.28%.

At the highly anticipated Apple product launch, two new iPhone models —— the iPhone 5C (hereafter referred to as 5C) and the iPhone 5S (hereafter referred to as 5S) were unveiled. The confidentiality of the new products was once again non-existent, with earlier speculations being completely accurate. However, most people guessed the beginning but not the end; the 5C is not the rumored "cheap version of the iPhone." As for the 5S, its biggest selling point is the fingerprint recognition function.

For a time, heated debates filled the public sphere. Critics believed that the starting domestic price of the 5C at 4488 yuan (16GB) was disappointing and unattractive; the innovation in the 5S was lackluster. Supporters, however, argued that the 5C broke away from the metal + glass style established by the iPhone 4, and the processor and fingerprint functions of the 5S make it the strongest phone available without question.

On one hand, Apple is holding firm, while on the other, it's making changes, seemingly trying to take a path that balances both approaches. However, domestic Apple-related stocks seemed low in spirits yesterday, with many experiencing a trading limit down.

Is the 5C a replacement version of the iPhone 5?

The times have changed, as has the competitive environment. Nowadays, it's difficult for Apple to receive the almost unanimous praise it did when the iPhone 4 was released, and this launch of the 5C and 5S was no different. Intense debates about whether they are "good or bad" filled the public sphere. But one undeniable fact is that the iPhone is still the most attention-grabbing smartphone. Among these debates, the fiercest argument was undoubtedly about the price of the 5C. Regarding this new model, people only guessed the beginning, not the end.

According to official Apple data, the 5C uses a one-piece plastic shell, with dimensions of 124.4 * 59.2 * 8.97 mm and weighs 132 grams. It comes in five colors: green, white, blue, pink, and yellow. The prices for the 16GB and 32GB versions are 4488 yuan and 5288 yuan respectively, with no 64GB version available.

This is a rather controversial price. Prior to this, there were rumors within the industry that Apple would introduce a cheaper version of the iPhone to further increase sales. Subsequently, spy photos of the iPhone 5C were leaked, and due to its plastic shell, people naturally assumed it was the cheap version of the iPhone. However, an Apple insider told the Southern Metropolis Daily that regarding the so-called "cheap" claims, the company felt helpless internally. From start to finish, Apple has never officially acknowledged the existence of any so-called "cheap version." You can find this awareness in CEO Tim Cook's statements. "There won't be a low-priced iPhone this year," Cook said before the unveiling of the iPhone 5C.

In fact, the 5C should be more accurately defined as a replacement version of the iPhone 5. Apart from differences in external design and iOS system versions, everything else is identical. Currently, the Apple China official website has announced that the future iPhone lineup will retain the 8GB version of the iPhone 4S, along with the iPhone 5C and iPhone 5S. The iPhone 5 will exit the stage, facing discontinuation, becoming the shortest-lived generation of iPhones in history.

Fingerprint technology not yet open to developers

Besides the 5C, Apple also simultaneously launched the true upgrade version of the iPhone 5, the iPhone 5S, with prices for the 16GB/32GB/64GB versions at 5288 yuan, 6088 yuan, and 6888 yuan respectively. Like the iPhone 5, the 5S still uses a 4-inch display screen and is equipped with a 64-bit architecture A7 processor. Additionally, the 5S includes a co-processor M7, specifically used to measure data from the accelerometer, gyroscope, and compass. Fitness applications can directly access data processed by the M7 and monitor your motion state.

An Apple insider informed the Southern Metropolis Daily that in some sports applications, the M7, which consumes less energy than the A7, will play a role in saving power. Moreover, compared to the previous generation, although the camera of the 5S remains at 8 million pixels, the aperture has increased to 2.2, and an additional white flash light has been added alongside the existing yellow flash light.

For ordinary users, in terms of identification, the biggest difference between the 5S and the iPhone 5 lies in the color options. Three colors replace the original black and white: space gray, gold paired with white, and silver paired with white. Furthermore, the 5S features a new fingerprint recognition security function called "Touch ID," which adds a stainless steel ring around the HOME button, eliminates the small square in the traditional HOME button, and makes the inside of the button flat instead of concave.

An Apple insider stated that "Touch ID" will currently only be used for various security applications within iOS 7, such as unlocking and App Store payments, and will not be opened to developers, mainly considering issues like privacy leakage. As for Touch ID itself, the fingerprint information will be stored locally on the phone and will not be uploaded to Apple's servers.

Additionally, mainland China and Hong Kong are among the first sales regions for the 5C and 5S, with sales starting on the 20th of this month, a first in history.

Supports 4G, but does not support China Mobile's 3G

Still using a 4-inch screen, apart from the fingerprint technology and 64-bit processor of the iPhone 5S, the almost predictable product appearance changes and configuration upgrades, plus the unrealized expectations of a cheap version, have led to various loud criticisms of Apple. Some opinions even suggest that the performance of Apple and its domestic concept stocks are related to this. On the day of the launch, Apple's share price on NASDAQ fell by 2.28%, and the next day, domestic A-share Apple concept stocks continued to experience low volatility. By the close of trading, the sector fell by 4%, with Changxin Technology, Unisem (China), Desay Battery, GoerTek, and Anjie Technology all hitting the lower limit.

Compared to the reasons related to the products themselves, a bigger issue is that during the announcement of the new products, Apple did not announce cooperation with China Mobile, the largest telecommunications operator in the country. Due to China Mobile adopting the special 3G standard TD-SCDMA, Apple has never been able to reach a cooperation agreement with it. With China Mobile leading the testing of TD-LTE services, cooperation between the two parties in 4G has become possible. According to reports, the 5C and 5S released by Apple support 13 LTE frequency bands, including TD-LTE, theoretically supporting China Mobile's 4G network.

According to information queried by the Southern Metropolis Daily on the MIIT website, the TD-LTE versions of the 5S and 5C that support China Mobile have already obtained network access permits, proving that the long-term "tortured relationship" between the two parties is about to bear fruit. However, it is worth noting that this news cannot immediately benefit Apple suppliers because the 5S and 5C only support TD-LTE and do not support the current 3G standard TD-SCDMA used by China Mobile. In other words, as long as the 4G license is not issued domestically, the China Mobile version of the iPhone cannot be officially sold.

Moreover, the TD-LTE 4G network of China Mobile has not yet been fully rolled out nationwide. Based on the test situation conducted by the Southern Metropolis Daily in Guangzhou, the signal coverage is not particularly ideal, insufficient to support full commercial use. This way, the practicality of the 5S and 5C that do not support TD-SCDMA is also lacking.

Apple's Situation: On the Edge of Love and Pain

The new iPhone has been released, still sticking to the 4-inch screen, taking the high-end route, with a starting price above 4000 yuan that is still unaffordable for most people. These are Apple's steadfast commitments, but they also reveal obvious elements of change, such as replacing the iPhone 5 with the iPhone 5C, making the three currently available iPhones (4S, 5C, 5S) appear different to create a clear visual impact, solving the problem of similar appearances in previous products. Another major change is the attitude towards the huge market of China —— holding the first press conference in China and listing mainland China as one of the first batch of sales regions.

However, due to the simultaneous presence of "commitment" and "change", I always feel somewhat awkward, as if Apple is missing something. For example, the domestic press conference only broadcasted the live video of the U.S. event without any executives appearing; the 5C is not a cheap version, continuing to pursue high-end positioning. This makes my feelings towards Apple always linger on the edge of love and pain —— having extremely high expectations, but no matter how it changes, it always seems not to be the Apple or the iPhone I want in my heart.

This sentiment also spreads widely among Chinese consumers, who always hope that Apple can bring something unexpected, but when those things really come, they are hard to satisfy everyone. I once imagined, if the new iPhone had a large screen, or if the price dropped below 3000 yuan, would it be what I wanted? Or would I still be on the edge of love and pain, thinking that Apple pushing low-cost products degrades its brand, and pushing large screens is a compromise to the market?

Tracing back to the root cause, Apple was originally a niche brand targeting personalized individuals, only attracting users who liked Apple. However, after years of rapid development, it has become a brand with a very wide user base, with more attention and thus more diverse tastes.

An Apple insider told me that the new product line structure does not specify which product is particularly aimed at which type of user, just hoping to attract more users. This indicates that Apple has gone back, and it is difficult to return to the past personalized and niche Apple. This is the need for company development, and also the need for the market, the struggle between persistence and compromise.
