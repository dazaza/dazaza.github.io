Unveiling the Secrets of Car Cool Pads: "Defeating Heat, Comfort, and Safety"

Starting from July 14th,伏天 (dog days) officially began, with this year's lasting a lengthy 40 days. Drivers may have a deep understanding that if a car is left under the sun for an extended period, not only will the inside temperature soar, but the seat temperature can also become unbearable. As a result, various types of car cool pads have started appearing on the market. Through investigations, it was learned that more than half of car owners opt to purchase cool seat pads during the summer, yet the safety and hygiene performance of these pads should not be overlooked.

1. More Than Half of Car Owners Choose Cool Pads

During scorching summers, after leaving your beloved car outside for some time, the temperature inside will skyrocket. Turning on the air conditioning is just one solution, and many people choose cool pads as another means of cooling down. Surveys conducted by reporters revealed that over half of the individuals prefer cool pads.

"I am someone who gets hot easily. Although my car has air conditioning, I always feel much more comfortable sitting on a cool pad. Therefore, every summer I buy a new set of cool pads, usually made of linen or ice silk. However, I have noticed one issue; after installing the cool pad, the car seat becomes quite slippery, so it must be secured properly; otherwise, it could lead to danger," Mr. Zhang, a citizen, told the reporter. However, some citizens are indifferent about this, "In summer, having air conditioning in the car is sufficient. I once used a cool pad with a slight massage function, which made me too relaxed and drowsy while driving. Hence, I haven't used any cool pads since then."

2. The Hygiene and Safety of Cool Pads Cannot Be Ignored

In summer, due to high humidity, cars tend to get damp, leading to mold growth, especially in the sponge cushion materials inside seats, which absorb moisture and get contaminated. Similarly, purchasing cool pads might also pose issues of dampness and contamination. Therefore, to maintain cleanliness inside the car, replacing cool pads regularly is indispensable. Moreover, as most cool pads have smooth surfaces without enough friction, sitting on them can cause the body to slide, affecting normal driving operations. Hence, cool pads must be securely fixed to the seats and not simply placed loosely. Otherwise, if the pad moves around, in emergency braking situations, the forward inertia could cause one to slip off the seat, resulting in danger.

As reflected by many citizens, many cool pads are designed according to human body shapes and come with massage functions, making drivers overly comfortable, which isn't necessarily good. In such overly comfortable environments, people are more likely to fall asleep, posing potential hazards to safe summer driving. Therefore, car owners should choose based on their own conditions. It is recommended that overweight or physically unwell car owners consider choosing massage cool pads, while other drivers should avoid overly comfortable massage cushions. The hygiene and safety of cool pads should be the primary consideration for citizens.

3. Comparing Several Types of Cool Pad Performances

Facing the wide variety of cool pads available in the market, how to choose becomes a headache for many car owners. For this reason, reporters specially visited several auto parts stores to understand the performances and comparisons of common cool pads.

Currently, many car owners choose linen cool pads. They emit a faint aromatic scent detectable by humans, which can kill bacteria and inhibit parasite growth. Linen seat pads use natural linen fibers as raw materials, featuring breathability, coolness, heat insulation, anti-static properties, environmental friendliness, and resistance to dust accumulation. However, it's difficult to distinguish genuine from fake linen pads, as some merchants deceive consumers by passing off cotton thread pads as linen ones. Therefore, extra caution is needed when purchasing. Generally speaking, linen pads are heavier than cotton thread ones; linen has better strength and feels harder than cotton thread. Touching a linen pad under the sun, you can feel a temperature drop of 3 to 5 degrees Celsius; additionally, linen pads have a certain luster.

Some car owners also choose bamboo-woven or grass cool pads. The main advantage of bamboo pads is their coolness. Nowadays, designers generally incorporate intricate patterns into cool pads, enhancing their aesthetic appeal. However, bamboo pads are not breathable and can easily damage leather seats if used without air conditioning.

Jade seat pads have emerged in recent years, handmade from jade pieces and natural ice silk threads. This type of seat pad becomes icy cold when cool and absorbs heat when warm. If the car is parked in the sun for a long time, this seat pad can become extremely hot. After prolonged air conditioning use, it becomes very cold. Therefore, careful selection is advised.
