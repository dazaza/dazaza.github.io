The age of the skin is like the tree rings of a big tree. When the tree rings increase by one circle, the age grows by one year. With the growth of age, the skin will naturally enter an aging state. At what age does our skin start to age? The appearance of eye wrinkles means that you should start to face the problem of skin aging. Here I not only tell you those causes of eye wrinkles that are often overlooked by you, but also teach you simple and practical ways to remove eye wrinkles.

1, Causes of the formation of fine lines around the eyes - bad living habits

These bad habits include:

Using face cream instead of eye cream indiscriminately.

It's best not to apply facial cream around the eyes, as overly nourishing cream can cause fat granules on the skin around the eyes. It is recommended to switch to a refreshing gel eye cream and massage it to inhibit the continued generation of fat granules.

Squinting to see things

Looking at things by squinting for a long time is incorrect and can easily keep the eyelid muscles in a tense state, causing the formation of fine lines.

Inappropriate height of pillows

Poor quality sleep affects the blood circulation of the skin around the eyes. Choosing a pillow of appropriate height can ensure good sleep. It is suggested that the pillow height be 8 centimeters.

These seemingly normal details of daily life actually accumulate more and more fine lines around your eyes. Now let's talk about how to effectively eliminate fine lines around the eyes:

In a dry office, be sure to drink plenty of water. Especially in air-conditioned rooms, you need to be extra considerate to yourself. You can place a cup of water on your desk to create a humid environment for your skin.

Weight loss should be gradual. Sudden weight loss does not give the skin enough time to adapt to the reduction of fat, which can also cause wrinkles.

When you see small wrinkles at the corners of your eyes, few women don't feel anxious. But they just don't know how gentle care can drive away the small wrinkles. Those hyped skincare products seem ineffective on their own faces. Don't worry, try these simplest methods.

Rice ball wrinkle removal

After the fragrant rice at home is cooked, pick some soft, warm rice (not too hot) and knead it into a ball. Gently rub it on your face to draw out the oil and dirt from the pores of the skin until the rice ball becomes oily and dirty. Then wash it off with clear water. This allows the skin to breathe freely and reduces wrinkles.

Chicken bone wrinkle removal

Most of the dermal tissue of the skin is made up of elastic fibers. Without them, the skin loses elasticity and wrinkles gather together. Chicken skin and chicken cartilage contain large amounts of chondroitin sulfate, which is the most important component of elastic fibers. Wash the leftover chicken bones and cook them with chicken skin to make soup. Not only is it rich in nutrition, drinking it regularly can also reduce wrinkles and make the skin delicate.

Pig trotter wrinkle removal

Take several old sow pig trotters (if unavailable, regular pig trotters can be used), wash them clean, and boil them into a paste. Apply it to your face before sleeping at night and wash it off in the morning the next day. Persisting for half a month will show significant wrinkle-removal effects.

Fruits and vegetables wrinkle removal

Vegetables and fruits such as loofah, banana, orange, watermelon peel, tomato, strawberry, etc., have the most natural moisturizing and wrinkle-removing effects on the skin. They can also be made into masks for facial application, making the face smooth and reducing wrinkles. Tea wrinkle removal

Beer has low alcohol content, and the tannic acid and bitter acid it contains stimulate appetite, help digestion, and have a cooling effect. Beer also contains a large amount of vitamin B, sugar, and protein. Drinking beer in moderation can strengthen the body and reduce facial wrinkles.

Tea contains more than 400 kinds of rich chemical components, mainly including tea polyphenols, aromatic oil compounds, carbohydrates, proteins, multiple amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and pectin, making it a natural health drink. Besides improving health, it can also maintain smooth skin, delay the appearance of facial wrinkles, and reduce wrinkles. It can also prevent various skin diseases, but attention should be paid to avoid drinking strong tea.

Chewing wrinkle removal

Chewing gum for 5 to 20 minutes every day can reduce facial wrinkles and make the complexion rosy. This is because chewing exercises the facial muscles, changes the facial blood circulation, and enhances the metabolic function of facial cells.
