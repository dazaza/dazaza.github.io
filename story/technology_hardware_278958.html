Champions League - Ronaldo Returns, Key Forward Scores as Real Madrid Wins 1-0 to Top the Group

At 26th November Beijing Time, in the fifth round of group C of the UEFA Champions League, Real Madrid hosted Swiss team FC Zurich at Santiago Bernabéu Stadium. In the first half, Kaka assisted Higuaín's opening goal, Marcelo's powerful shot hit the post, and in the second half, Ronaldo came on as a substitute for warm-up. Real Madrid narrowly defeated their opponent 1-0, accumulating 10 points to top the group.

The two teams have met three times historically, with Real Madrid winning all matches. For this game, Pellegrini made slight adjustments to the lineup. Albiol returned to the starting eleven, Lass Diarra recovered from injury to play, Raúl and Higuaín formed the forward duo, Guti and Van Nistelrooy were absent due to injuries, Ronaldo and Benzema were substitutes, and Gago was not included in the roster. This game also marked Iker Casillas' 100th Champions League appearance.

In the 4th minute, Alonso passed the ball, Kaka weaved through two defenders and then took a left-footed shot which was saved by the goalkeeper to a corner kick. Ramos' header from the right-side corner kick went high. A minute later, Alfonso cut inside from the right flank, took a step back, and delivered a left-footed long-range shot that was securely caught by Casillas. In the 9th minute, Marcelo's cross from the left was cleared, and Alonso's long-range shot posed little threat.

In the 10th minute, Zurich goalkeeper Leoni made a mistake on his goal-kick, allowing Higuaín to intercept the ball, break past defenders, and take a low left-footed shot that was saved by the goalkeeper. In the 20th minute, Kaka dribbled past a defender centrally and sent a clever through pass. Higuaín let the ball bypass Thijsen on the left flank, broke into the box, and scored easily with a low shot, making it 1-0. The Argentine forward scored his second goal in four Champions League games.

Real Madrid comfortably dominated the game. In the 26th minute, Diarra combined with Kaka to distribute the ball, and Alonso unleashed a right-footed long-range shot from 20 meters out, which Leoni dived to tip over the bar. A minute later, Margaras delivered a diagonal pass, and Alfonso's long-range shot was secured by Casillas.

In the 33rd minute, Ramos skillfully dribbled past two players on the right flank before delivering a cross that crossed the entire penalty area, but Marcelo's angled shot was blocked by a defender and went out for a corner. In the 34th minute, Higuaín's corner kick was directed outside the box, and Marcelo followed up with a powerful volley that struck the left post and rebounded.

In the 39th minute, Alfonso's powerful shot grazed the left post and went wide, creating a dangerous moment. A minute later, Kaka distributed the ball, and Marcelo's hurried long-range shot went high. In the 43rd minute, Higuaín broke down the right flank to the baseline and delivered a cross to the front of the goal, but no one was there to connect. Real Madrid led 1-0 at halftime.

In the 47th minute, Alfonso distributed the ball, and Egeter's shot was blocked. In the 56th minute, Alonso's free-kick was headed by Ramos but cleared by Leoni just in time. In the 59th minute, Kaka broke into the box but failed to shoot under pressure, falling to the ground but receiving no call from the referee.

In the 60th minute, Rocha's direct free-kick was blocked by the wall. Two minutes later, Kaka was brought down by Duric, who received a yellow card. Van der Vaart's attempt near the goal was neither a pass nor a shot and went wide.

In the 66th minute, Marcelo's cross from the left was met by Van der Vaart's side-volley, which slightly missed the target.

In the 69th minute, Ronaldo came on as a substitute for Raúl, greeted by cheers from the crowd. In the 77th minute, Van der Vaart was pushed over in the box while trying to turn, but the referee gave no indication. Ronaldo's long-range shot hit a defender and deflected. Pepe's cross from the left was intercepted before Kaka could shoot.

In the 82nd minute, Alonso was cautioned with a yellow card for fouling Margaras during a back chase.

In the 86th minute, Ronaldo's powerful shot from close range was blocked, and his follow-up attempt went wide. In the 89th minute, Kaka delivered a beautiful pass, but Van der Vaart missed an unmarked chance inside the box, shooting high and missing a golden opportunity.

In the 90th minute, Granero sent a through pass, Ronaldo's angled shot from the left side of the box was saved by Leoni onto the corner flag, almost scoring.

Real Madrid (4-4-2): Casillas; Ramos, Albiol, Pepe, Arbeloa (57’ Van der Vaart); Lassana Diarra, Alonso, Kaka (89’ Granero), Marcelo; Raúl (69’ Cristiano Ronaldo), Higuaín

Zurich (4-5-1): Leoni; Stahel, Balmer, Thijsen, Koch; Gajic, Egeter, Rocha, Duric, Margaras; Alfonso
