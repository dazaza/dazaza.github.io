More than ten citizens helped rescue an octogenarian who fainted on the street

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-29 12:29:09

When Mr. Liu Wen, a citizen, was passing by, Grandpa Lai Guojiang fell down. Seeing someone went up to help Lai Guojiang, he quickly took out his DV and shot the whole rescue process. Mr. Liu Wen said that more than ten warm-hearted citizens surrounded at that time to check on the elder's condition. A shopkeeper nearby saw the elder's head lying on the ground and felt it was uncomfortable, so he hurriedly took newspapers and put them under his head. A passer-by took out his mobile phone and prepared to call 120. At this time, an aunt proposed that the hospital was just across the road, so going directly to the hospital to call a doctor would be faster. Before her words were finished, this aunt ran to the hospital. Medical staff are examining Grandpa Lai Guojiang. Video screenshot. Yesterday morning, I saw Grandpa Lai Guojiang in the bed of the Department of Cerebrovascular Disease in the Inpatient Department of the People's Hospital of Banan District. The 80-year-old Lai Guojiang said that at about 8:00 on the morning of December 26th, he went to see a doctor at Banan District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital. After seeing the doctor, when he returned home and passed by a lottery sales point at Xinstreet, he suddenly felt dizzy and then lost consciousness. "When I woke up, I found myself lying on the bed in Banan District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital receiving oxygen".

According to the report from the correspondent Han Zheng, at around 9:00 on the morning of December 26th, Grandpa Lai Guojiang from Yudong Town, Banan District, fell down unconscious on the street. More than ten warm-hearted citizens nearby reached out to help, allowing him to receive timely treatment. A passer-by used a DV (digital camcorder) to record the rescue scene at that time. Yesterday, Grandpa Lai's family wanted to use our newspaper to thank those warm-hearted citizens who reached out to help.

Doctor Liu Yao from the Emergency Department of Banan District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital said that she was seeing a patient at that time, and a 60-year-old aunt suddenly rushed in and said, "An old man has fallen down and is lying on the ground, hurry up and save him." Doctor Liu immediately went to the scene with the nurses carrying medical equipment and stretchers. About 100 meters away from the hospital, she found Lai Guojiang lying on the ground. Doctor Liu and the medical staff carried him back to the hospital with a stretcher and contacted his family.

After seeing the medical staff arrive, the warm-hearted aunt left. Before leaving, when Mr. Liu Wen asked for her name, this aunt shook her hands and said that what she did was only a small thing, not worth mentioning, and anyone who saw it would do the same.

In the afternoon yesterday, Ms. Lai Qingmei, the daughter of the elder, said that her father went out alone that day. If it weren't for the timely help of so many warm-hearted citizens, her father's life might have been in danger. She and her family really thank those warm-hearted citizens who "do good deeds without leaving their names".
