My movie 2012 movie

by hoouk260 on 2012-03-03 20:04:54

People live in this world, but for what? Some people indulge in revelry and debauchery, while others just play around. Life is like a movie, with different plots, but it will eventually come to an end. What kind of protagonist are we playing in our own movies?

My online space name is "Calm Heart", and some people have asked me why I chose such a name. Today, I will give an answer: I hope to face all things calmly and view every life movie peacefully.

I love. Because I have love, I work hard. For more than ten years, I haven't even bought myself a decent necklace. I am a woman without any jewelry. The money he gave me when we got married was all invested in the business.

For me, no matter how hard I work, seeing my child sleep soundly and laugh happily is more important than anything else. Many of her excellent essays were polished by me, although she always writes her Chinese teacher's name in the guidance teacher column. But seeing her writing level improve day by day and being top in all subjects still makes me very happy. Sometimes, I know there is work left undone, but no matter how busy I am, if she needs me, I will smile and discuss with her gently about how to continue writing or doing something. Although every time after discussing with her, I have to work overtime late into the night...

Regarding love, I won't fall in love easily. Not even for business, not even for casual encounters would I do so. Earning money is for living, but life should make oneself happy. If that money doesn't make me feel justified enough, I'd rather give it up. This point, I will also educate my daughter in the future: don't forget you're a person for the sake of earning money.

Sometimes, because of someone, something, or some scenery, my heart hurts. When I'm in pain, I know I can't yet reach the state of "calm heart", although I can control my facial expression to remain calm. Being an adult is really a tough thing, and being able to adapt well physically, psychologically, and socially is even harder.

Every day, I see many people, each playing their different movies:

Someone works quietly at their job and takes a walk with his wife in the evening.

Someone works on a construction site, going out early and coming back late. With the weather getting cooler, he says it's cold and tiring to ride his bike back and forth, but he still wants to go home.

Someone contracts engineering projects and stays on the construction site all day, unable to return home, but earns a lot of money.

Someone's house has been demolished, and they have become rich, but they lost all the compensation money.

A certain woman became the head of her unit, and rumors say she has a relationship with a certain leader.

A certain woman stays at home as a full-time housewife and keeps the house very clean.

A certain woman often gambles outside and doesn't come home, complaining that her husband doesn't earn enough money for her to spend...

Me? Sometimes I also wonder, how do outsiders evaluate me? In front of me, they all say nice things, but behind my back, what kind of words are they saying? To me, those are just small movies that have already played. Once they are over, it doesn't matter anymore. Shoes are worn on my feet, not for others to see. As long as I feel comfortable, that's fine.

If I can make my family happy and make those who love me happy, that is my happiness.

Sometimes I sit alone in the room, looking through the window at the sunset. As the twilight gradually enters the window, slowly expands, and then slowly fades away, until my window returns from loneliness to brilliance and then back to tranquility, isn't it like the nearly half-life movie I've performed? A woman without any jewelry, ordinary like a low-budget movie, the happiness inside me cannot be seen by those watching the movie, because this movie is read by myself.
