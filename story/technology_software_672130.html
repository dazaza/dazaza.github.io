The ultimate goal of optimizing a website is to allow visitors to easily find your site through search engines. If a website's keyword ranking falls to the third page or beyond in search engine results, it will essentially receive no traffic. So, how can you rank higher? Although I haven't been doing website optimization for very long (about one and a half years), all six of the websites I currently optimize are on the first page of Baidu and within the first two pages of Google for English SEO. Here, I'll share some of my personal experiences with building sites on our A5 platform for everyone's reference. Everyone is welcome to criticize and correct me!

1. First, clearly define the positioning of your website, understanding what SEM services it provides and to which audience. This requires us to carefully consider keyword selection, which is the primary factor in rankings. For example: The Chinese Vulvar Leukoplakia Network (without domain name to avoid appearing as an AD) has chosen keywords such as "vulvar leukoplakia" and "vulvar white spots." It's worth noting that commonly used misspellings can also be very important.

2. Properly manage the content, directory, and file structure of your website. A good website structure makes it easier for search engines to crawl and index your site. Avoid placing files too deeply within folders; ideally, keep them within 2-3 layers.

3. When creating a webpage, consider which keywords to use. Long-tail keywords are often used more frequently on internal pages. Currently, half of the traffic on the Chinese Vulvar Leukoplakia Network comes from these long-tail keywords.

4. Ensure interconnectivity between webpages through internal linking within the site, and try to avoid broken links.

5. Use static pages whenever possible. Search engines generally prefer static pages. Regardless of the program you use (for instance, I usually use ASP + Access database), you can generate static HTML pages.

6. Promote actively by exchanging links and increasing external links. This includes exchanging links with others, posting on Baidu Tieba and Zhihu, networking with other webmasters (sometimes including your website in conversations), and posting on A5 with a link in your signature (although this doesn't encourage spamming on A5, try to help others).

7. Your website should have substantial content, truly original material that provides value to visitors. Only then will search engines favor your site.

8. Do not attempt to deceive search engines using cheating methods. Remember this point well — if you cheat, you will eventually pay the price.
