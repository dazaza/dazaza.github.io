Zhejiang Taizhou boss drove BMW and hit 8 cars, 4 deaths and 6 injuries caused by drunk driving

by xia2oo007 on 2010-04-06 14:46:55

ECNS, Taizhou, April 6th (reported by Jiang Yun) - On the evening of April 5th, a traffic accident occurred in the Guan area of Sanmen County, Zhejiang Province. The suspect Yang Shuzhong was driving a BMW car under the influence of alcohol and collided with multiple vehicles successively, resulting in 4 deaths and 6 injuries. Currently, the injured have received effective treatment, and the suspect Yang Shuzhong has been controlled by the police.

At 20:17 last night, the 110 Command Center of the Sanmen County Public Security Bureau received an accident report. According to the emergency response plan, they immediately instructed the Traffic Police Brigade Accident Team, Criminal Investigation Brigade, Haiyou Police Station, Fire Brigade, and other units to rush to the scene for handling, quickly rescuing the injured and controlling the suspect. Corresponding groups were also formed, including on-site cordon group, casualty treatment group, on-site investigation group, and evidence collection group. They set up cordons on the incident road section, implemented traffic control, visited eyewitnesses, retrieved video surveillance, promptly conducted on-site investigations, and collected and fixed evidence.

During the treatment of the injured, three people had no vital signs when they arrived at the Sanmen County People's Hospital. After examination by doctors, it was confirmed that they had died. One person died after ineffective hospital rescue at 23:00 that night. The remaining six people were in stable condition.

According to the police investigation: At 20:12 last night, Yang Shuzhong drove a black BMW car with the license plate number Zhe JA0333. When passing through the People's Road Telecom Bureau entrance section, he collided with a Buick car ahead. The Buick car was squeezed forward and collided with an oncoming electric scooter. Then the BMW car hit another two-wheeled electric scooter ahead, causing injury to the driver.

The BMW continued moving forward and reached the Mian Niu Mountain Road intersection where it hit a second two-wheeled electric scooter, injuring two people on the vehicle. The BMW continued moving forward and at the Nanshan Road branch of the Sanmen Branch of the Agricultural Bank of China, it slightly grazed a third two-wheeled electric scooter. Afterwards, the BMW car rushed forward more than ten meters and consecutively collided with a rickshaw, a fourth two-wheeled electric scooter, and a minivan. This resulted in the death of the rickshaw driver Yang, passenger Mei, and the two-wheeled electric scooter driver Zhou, as well as the side-flipping of the minivan and injuries to three passengers inside. The BMW finally stopped after colliding with the steps of the sidewalk on the right side.

The entire accident scene extended approximately 193 meters. The BMW driven by Yang Shuzhong collided with two motor vehicles, five electric scooters, and one rickshaw, causing four deaths and six injuries.

After the accident, the police immediately controlled the offending driver Yang Shuzhong, took him to the Haiyou Police Station for interrogation, extracted blood samples, and sent them to the Taizhou Municipal Public Security Bureau Forensic Appraisal Institute for testing.

Upon investigation, Yang Shuzhong, male, born on July 26, 1962, is the chairman and general manager of Sanmen Huali Pharmaceutical Chemical Co., Ltd., currently serving as a representative of the Taizhou Municipal People’s Congress, a representative of the Sanmen County People’s Congress, and a standing committee member of the Sanmen County People’s Congress. Test results showed that on the night of the incident, Yang Shuzhong's blood alcohol content was 3.3 mg/ml, which qualifies as drunk driving.

Yang Shuzhong, while drunk-driving, failed to take timely and effective measures after colliding with the first vehicle, ultimately leading to an accident resulting in four deaths, six injuries, and damage to multiple vehicles. His actions violated Article 115, Paragraph 1 of the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China, suspected of endangering public safety by other dangerous methods. The Sanmen County Public Security Bureau has already launched an investigation into this case on the evening of April 5th. Given that Yang Shuzhong is a representative of both the Taizhou Municipal and Sanmen County People’s Congresses, the Taizhou Municipal Public Security Bureau and the Sanmen County Public Security Bureau separately submitted reports to the municipal and county-level People’s Congress Standing Committees seeking permission to take criminal compulsory measures against the suspect Yang Shuzhong.

After the accident, all levels of leadership attached great importance to the matter. Secretary of the Taizhou Municipal Committee Chen Tiesong and Deputy Secretary Wu Weirong made important instructions regarding the accident handling work, requiring city and county levels to fully treat the injured, handle the accident according to law, swiftly, and fairly. Member of the Standing Committee of the Taizhou Municipal Committee and Director of the Public Security Bureau Chen Mianquan immediately led relevant personnel to Sanmen to direct the accident handling work. The main county leaders, such as Secretary of the Sanmen County Party Committee Li Hao and County Magistrate Dong Fubiao, promptly went to the hospital to visit the injured, console the families of the deceased and injured, and activate the major traffic accident emergency response plan. They established the "April 5th" Traffic Accident Handling Leading Group, led by the deputy head of the county, mobilizing relevant department personnel to conduct work.

Currently, this case is still under further investigation and handling. (End)
