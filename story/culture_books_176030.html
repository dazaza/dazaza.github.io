"Thriller Legend" The Undying Soul of Michael Jackson, Authors: Wang Hairong, Wei Shuangshuang, and Hou Yanli. TXT E-book Content Summary

Relevant individuals from the Xinhua Publishing House informed reporters that the three editors of "Michael Jackson: The Legend of the Thriller," Wang Hairong, Wei Shuangshuang, and Hou Yanli, absorbed first-hand information provided by Qi Heng and Cao Weiguo, journalists from the Los Angeles branch of Xinhua News Agency while writing this book. Qi Heng and Cao Weiguo have been tracking and interviewing Michael Jackson in the United States for years. Some of the photographs in the book were taken by Qi Heng. Cao Weiguo had also tracked major events involving Michael Jackson, including his child molestation case, financial disputes, and the closure of Neverland Ranch, and even conducted on-site interviews at Neverland Ranch. According to relevant individuals from Xinhua Publishing House, from the initial compilation and editing to the later typesetting and distribution of "Michael Jackson: The Legend of the Thriller," it only took a week. Additionally, the book includes nearly 200 photos sourced from Xinhua News Agency, Reuters, Agence France-Presse, and other national news agencies, including childhood pictures of Michael Jackson, performance photos, stage snapshots, and precious photos of him attending various activities.

Chapter One: The DNA of Art

On the eve of Michael's London concert launch, all media outlets covering music or lovers of music could not suppress their excitement. People from the 1990s and earlier said: "Not having worshipped Michael Jackson is equivalent to not having listened to music." However, today's E-era might consider this merely a "brand effect," as they do not truly understand Michael Jackson's personal charm. Younger fans do not recognize Michael Jackson as the "King of Pop," but rather view him as a master of plastic surgery or a fairy tale about a black man turning white.

Chapter Two: Growth and Transformation

"Since we took control of our own destiny and started making our own music, every album of mine or our band has carried the words 'Dedicated to our mother.'" -- Michael Jackson. Michael's father was the object of his hatred, while his mother left another impression in Michael's heart. Katherine brought warmth and tranquility to the home. The parents' natural love for music deeply influenced the children around them. The siblings' "secret artistry" received support from their mother and eventually won recognition from their father.

Chapter Four: A Dream Disturbed by Many Troubles

The "whitening" incident was actually a misunderstanding by people. In 1993, at the peak of his career, he accepted an exclusive interview with Oprah Winfrey, one of the most renowned TV program hosts in America, reviewing his life and career comprehensively. In the interview, he discussed his family, his lonely and painful childhood, conflicts with his siblings and father, media slander against him, good deeds after being burned during an advertisement shoot, rumors about skin whitening, feelings about his black identity, rumors about plastic surgery, the classic crotch grab in his dance moves, the origin of the title "King of Pop," his private life with the opposite sex, family values, his relationship with Elizabeth Taylor, a guided tour of his Neverland mansion, kindness towards children, sources of inspiration for his dance steps, and his attitude as a pioneer in pop music.

Chapter Five: Lonely Peter Pan, Who Will Keep Him Company?

In Neverland, Michael sculpted a statue of Peter Pan; he wanted to preserve a never-growing-up childlike heart but couldn't escape the world's insistence on seeing him as a normal man. Yes, as a person, he had all the characteristics that males should have, so he should have love, a family, and children. But in Michael's heart, he was still just a big boy who hadn't grown up yet. Faced with the pursuit of the opposite sex, he would retreat in fear, so he wouldn't understand the allure of Boz Scaggs, would push away Madonna's passion, and maintain a shy infatuation with Princess Diana. Only with Taylor could he stop pretending to be a mature man and simply be a child.
