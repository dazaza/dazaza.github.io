First, an investment and entrepreneurship plan for a car beauty shop is a written summary of the entrepreneur's intended business. It describes the internal and external environmental premises and element characteristics related to the proposed business, providing a roadmap for business development and a benchmark for measuring the progress of the business. Typically, a business plan integrates functional plans such as marketing, finance, production, and human resources. Key considerations when writing a good business plan include: 1) focus on the product, 2) a compelling plan summary, 3) the courage to compete, 4) indication of action guidelines, 5) showcasing your management team, and 6) understanding the market.

Third, Store Renovation

When renovating the beauty workshop, it is best to consider convenience for future use and take various situations into account. Arrange water storage (water tank), drainage (sewage), filtration (sewage), and electrical outlets, considering moisture-proofing and short-circuit prevention. Workshop design should be simple, wiring and piping should be reasonable, and it's best to have the workshop's electricity controlled by a separate switch to prevent affecting the normal power supply of the entire store due to short circuits. Additionally, consider future expansion needs and reserve space for new businesses, such as film application rooms and lift positions. Here are some suggestions for friends preparing for renovation:

(1) Ideally, the car washing area should be designed with at least three positions. If the area is insufficient, there should be at least two positions, otherwise, after rainy days, it would be impossible to keep up with the workload during sunny weather.

(2) Install a lift machine if possible, whether for oil changes or underbody armor spraying, it’s the best way to showcase your professional image to customers.

(3) Retain a film application room, preferably near the entrance rather than tucked away inside the store. During film application, close the glass doors so that passersby can see the process.
