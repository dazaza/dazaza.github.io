Nine eye creams in four steps will make your eyes irresistible (Part One)

by liu514113345 on 2012-02-26 22:47:25

Eyes are not only the windows to the soul, but also the source of a woman's vitality. A beautiful woman always has a pair of charming eyes. However, in reality, we are often troubled by issues such as fine lines at the corners of the eyes, dark circles, eye bags, and dryness. To have bright and captivating eyes, follow us...

● Step One: Say goodbye to fine lines for an electric gaze

Wrinkles around the eyes can reveal your age without a doubt. Therefore, don't wait until the first wrinkle appears before you start using eye cream. Young people need to learn to "prevent problems before they occur." If you already have wrinkles around your eyes, while anti-aging products may not have the magic to completely eliminate them, they can moderately reduce fine lines, restore skin elasticity, and smooth out the traces of time to some extent.

Anti-wrinkle Eye Cream Recommendations:

CHANEL Perfect Repair Cream

Highlights: Combats aging around the eyes, strengthens the connection between skin tissues, smooths fine lines and wrinkles, makes the skin around the eyes softer, firmer, and more elastic, and softens spots around the eyes.

LANCOME Anti-Wrinkle Firming Eye Cream

Highlights: Rich in special active skin compounds that enhance the compactness of the skin around the eyes, improve skin elasticity, prevent drooping eyelids and the formation of under-eye bags.

Avon New Active Elasticity Eye Cream

Highlights: The triple tightening formula works together with anti-fatigue factors to deeply nourish the skin around the eyes, strengthen the cellular structure of the eye area, effectively tighten loose skin, and improve the texture of the skin around the eyes.

● Step Two: Bright Eyes Without Dryness or Swelling

Hydrating the skin around the eyes is a universal way to combat fine lines, effectively preventing small dry lines caused by dehydration. Especially for white-collar women who work in air-conditioned environments all year round, choosing a refreshing moisturizing eye cream is a smart choice. It won’t add extra burden to the delicate skin around the eyes and can prevent fine lines caused by dry environmental conditions.

Moisturizing Hydration Eye Cream Recommendations:

Estée Lauder Rapid Hydration Eye Cream

Highlights: Besides its powerful hydration function, it also contains natural mica essence and mineral particles, which can instantly smooth the skin around the eyes and cover up dark circles.

Clinique Moisture Eye Cream

Highlights: An essential daily step for eye care, making the skin around the eyes finer and firmer, reducing swelling, with a fresh formula that feels non-greasy.

Vichy Aqualia Thermal Deep Hydration Eye Gel Cream

Highlights: Contains African plant extract Filladyn, which improves dehydration lines and also contains de-swelling factors that promote lymph circulation, relieving under-eye bags and upper eyelid swelling.
