In 2008, the scale of China's IDC market will reach 5.3 billion. How should the IDC market compete?

by now on 2008-04-25 14:29:37

According to the report of the advisory firm at the beginning of the year, the scale of China's IDC market in 2008 will reach 5.3 billion yuan, nearly doubling compared to the previous year. In theory, the IDC market should have formed an explosive situation under this stimulus. However, recent observers pointed out that looking at the trend of the domestic IDC industry in the first quarter of this year, the smoke of price wars is beginning to dissipate. Service providers are paying more attention to improving hardware levels and service quality. The IDC market is undergoing severe tests from both the market and consumers.

A major transformation in the Chinese IDC market has quietly occurred. As the basic service system of the Internet industry, the IDC industry is undergoing a reshuffle. Smaller-scale and inferior-service IDC providers are gradually being eliminated by the market, and large service providers integrating the IDC market has become a trend. What caused this change? A journalist interviewed a person in charge of Now.cn, one of the largest IDC service providers in South China. He analyzed that after the chaotic price competition in 2007, consumers have begun to realize that low prices are often accompanied by poor-quality products. Many IDC service providers participating in the price war reduced technical personnel, server maintenance and development investment, or even used PCs instead of data centers to save operating costs. This often led to unstable lines, poor security, frequent server crashes, and significant losses for consumers. Therefore, the market focus is gradually shifting towards large service providers with strong technical capabilities, good data centers, and excellent service systems.

Since the beginning of 2008, internet investment hotspots such as mobile 3G have been exceptionally fiery. Enterprise websites, mobile WAP sites, and various aspects have seen explosive growth from details to technology, and the IDC industry, as the fundamental business of the Internet, has also gained momentum. Currently, there are many types of IDC services, including server hosting, leasing, sales, and various value-added services, and the technological level is developing rapidly, providing large-scale, high-quality, safe, and reliable services for enterprises going online, e-commerce, mobile 3G, and other applications.

How to seize this opportunity to grow bigger and stronger and then enter the national market? It is understood that some large service providers have already taken action. For example, Now.cn has telecom/unicom/Hong Kong and domestically leading dual-line data centers in terms of server hosting and leasing, generally adopting Intel Core2 dual-core processors, G-level dual-channel memory, integrated high-performance gigabit network cards, and other industry-leading configurations, striving to give users a perfect and ultra-fast experience. At the beginning of this year, the company launched the "Quality Improvement, Service First" 2008 Quality Service Year campaign, setting an example in the industry and receiving widespread praise from the industry and customers.

How to enhance the competitiveness of IDC service providers? Having substantial strength and excellent product design concepts is the premise, but service is more important for users, and ensuring the continuous operation of servers as much as possible is the key. Because small domestic service providers can no longer meet competitive needs, among domestic large IDC providers, there is not much difference in hardware levels such as bandwidth, data centers, and value-added services like CDN. The more comprehensive and timely the service, the more easily it gains consumer trust, bringing huge benefits to the enterprise. In the long term, it is also easy to cultivate a stable customer base.

Analysts believe that in the battle for the IDC industry, neither hardware nor service can be missing. Only by organically combining the needs and interests of consumers with the company's development philosophy and establishing a harmonious, stable, and developmental partnership with users can a win-win situation be achieved, realizing the healthy development of the IDC industry.
