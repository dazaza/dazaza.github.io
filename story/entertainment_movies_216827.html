Movie [Time-Travel Treasure Box] Plot Introduction:

Lau Ching-wai's new film "Time-Travel Treasure Box", with an investment of over 100 million yuan, was probed by domestic media for the first time yesterday at a photography studio in Guangzhou. Director Lau Ching-wai appeared with main actors Eric Tsang, Fiona Choi, Pearl Ka, Guo Degang, Wang Xuebing, Louis Tan and others. However, in this work which is said to gather "50 actors with market appeal", there are still many people who have not yet made their appearance —— Sun Li, Zeng Zhwei, Sandra Ng, Anthony Chan, Leung Siu-lung, Yuen Biao, Yuen Wah, Kong Yiu-ching, Tin Kai-man, Huang Bo, Guo Tao, Gillian Chung, Huang Yi...

Listening to it sounds like a "knock-off" version of "A Chinese Odyssey Part One: Pandora's Box", the "Time-Travel Treasure Box" was once speculated to be a sequel to the former. Meanwhile, many fans worried that the work which had been regarded as a classic by countless people could not be surpassed even by its creator Lau Ching-wai himself. Would he repeat or subvert? Yesterday, Lau Ching-wai revealed the answer with a plea: "Don't put pressure on me, I just want to look forward."

[Character Revelation] This time, Purple Fairy has four incarnations

Eric Tsang

Role: Qing Yise

Appearance: His appearance comes from Zhao Yun, wearing armor, with a topknot on his head, not handsome —— because Lau Ching-wai wanted him to look "stupid".

Plot: Qing Yise is a mountain bandit, kind-hearted but conceited. He kindly rescues the distressed woman Sun Li, but can't help the "professional habit" of taking advantage of the situation, resulting in a huge trouble. With no choice, he flees in panic. In the confusion, he is inexplicably sent to another space-time by the Moonlight Treasure Box, where he encounters the Battle of Red Cliffs and an unexpected wonderful marriage.

Self-narration: My character travels through time four times in total. Once I turned into a woman and was mistakenly recognized as Purple Fairy by the Bull Demon King. This part is very interesting because it parodies "King Kong", my clothes were torn apart sexily, and the Bull Demon King grabbed me like King Kong did. (Lau Ching-wai added: You wouldn't believe by looking at Eric Tsang's face, but his legs are so beautiful, just like a beauty's legs, straight and even.) The other three transformations are Zhao Yun, Japanese ronin, and ordinary civilian respectively.

"A Chinese Odyssey" Complex: I really like this play, but it doesn't have much to do with me playing in "Time-Travel Treasure Box".

Sun Li

Role: Rose Fairy

Appearance: Unknown

Plot: Although Rose Fairy ranks among the immortals, she only envies mandarin ducks. After meeting Qing Yise, she fell in love with him and chased after him relentlessly, all the way to five hundred years later. She used every means possible, her heart was broken, but in the end, she was still willing to lend a helping hand to the man who had repeatedly betrayed her...
