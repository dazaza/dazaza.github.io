Wedding Countdown, 12-Month Pre-Wedding Checklist

The most dazzling moment of your life, the most unforgettable day, and the most colorful instant. To host a perfect wedding, every detail matters, from venue decoration to table centerpiece. No corner should be overlooked. Therefore, preparation is crucial before the big day. Below is a 12-month pre-wedding countdown checklist to help ensure you're on track.

### Wedding Countdown: 12 Months Before the Big Day

#### 9-12 Months Before

- **Announce Engagement:** Inform friends and family about your engagement so they can prepare for the wedding.

- **Meet Parents:** Arrange a meeting between both sets of parents to initiate wedding planning. Seek their opinions on guest lists and other details.

- **Set Wedding Date:** Finalize the wedding date early as it serves as the foundation for subsequent preparations.

- **Draft Budget:** Prepare a "wedding fund" or draft a phased budget based on household income and expenses, ensuring sufficient funds for the event.

- **Choose Venue:** Select a unique, appropriately sized, and conveniently located venue. Research online or consult experienced friends before visiting potential sites.

- **Guest List:** Confirm the number of guests with both families and your partner, which will determine the venue size and help balance the budget.

- **Select Attire:** Stay updated on bridal fashion trends and try on dresses to understand what suits you best.

#### 6-9 Months Before

- **Order Attire:** After six months of research and trials, finalize your choices for the wedding gown and formal wear.

- **Pick Attendants' Attire:** Choose bridesmaids' and groomsmen's outfits based on the wedding theme and color scheme.

- **Photographer/Videographer:** Hire skilled professionals who can capture both candid moments and grand scenes.

- **Florist:** Discuss floral arrangements with a florist to match the wedding theme.

- **Venue Decorator:** Meet with decorators to confirm the final design plan that reflects your personal style.

- **Entertainment:** Book a band or DJ at least three months in advance to secure their availability.

- **Honeymoon Plans:** Decide on your honeymoon destination to avoid last-minute issues like unavailable flights or accommodations.

- **Finalize Guest List:** Ensure all invitees are confirmed.

- **Notify Out-of-Town Guests:** If your wedding is in a remote location, inform distant guests early.

- **Plan Party Details:** Organize a pre-or post-wedding party near the main event.

- **Catering:** Arrange food suppliers if necessary.

#### 4-6 Months Before

- **Stationery Orders:** Book invitations, RSVP cards, and table cards.

- **Cake Selection:** Gather information on cake makers and envision your ideal cake design.

- **Transportation:** Reserve vehicles for transporting wedding supplies.

- **Hair & Makeup Artists:** Book stylists for hair and makeup services.

- **Accommodations:** Arrange lodging for out-of-town guests.

- **Book Honeymoon Flights/Accommodations:** Secure travel plans for your honeymoon.

#### 2-4 Months Before

- **Purchase Attendants' Attire:** Have bridesmaids and groomsmen try on their outfits together.

- **Finalize Menu:** Meet with caterers to discuss menu options, service styles, and wine selections.

- **Order Cake:** Finalize cake design, size, and flavors after thorough research.

- **Background Music:** Select ceremony background music.

- **Favors:** Choose gifts for guests that align with the season and wedding theme.

- **Wedding Rings:** Purchase rings not too early to avoid outdated styles but allow time for engraving.

#### 4-8 Weeks Before

- **Send Invitations:** Distribute invitations early, especially to distant or busy guests.

- **Final Fittings:** Conduct final fittings for all attire, including hair and makeup trials.

#### 2-4 Weeks Before

- **Arrange Seating:** Plan seating arrangements by grouping families, classmates, friends, and colleagues while considering age differences and familiarity.

- **Confirm Details:** Finalize specifics with florists, photographers, bands, and hosts.

- **Modify Attire:** Make any last-minute adjustments to the wedding gown and finalize all clothing.

#### 1 Week Before

- **Gratuities:** Prepare envelopes with thank-you money.

- **Delegate Responsibility:** Trust someone reliable to manage important items like rings, guest lists, and supplier contacts.

- **Final Preparations:** Focus on grooming, such as eyebrow shaping, manicures, and hair care.

- **Pack Luggage:** Prepare luggage for the honeymoon to avoid leaving anything behind.
