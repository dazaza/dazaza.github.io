Six levels, no limits to being afraid of one's wife

Second level: Detailed annotation of call records

The requirement at this level is that the monthly call records should be printed on time. For calls lasting more than 1 minute or incoming calls during non-working hours, the reasons should be clearly noted for the wife's review.

Third level: Anniversary reminder + itinerary report

The requirement at this level is that the wife's matters are always the most important. Wedding anniversaries, Women's Day, wife's physical examination, etc., should be detailed reminders. At least two colleague numbers should be left for the wife to check.

Fourth level: Only following the wife on Weibo

The screen of this male protagonist's mobile phone shows that apart from his wife's Weibo, everything else is daily English learning, workplace classics, etc.

Fifth level: Always ready to group-buy for the wife

Which woman wouldn't be delighted upon grabbing something desirable and cheap? To ensure both spouses have happy faces, this male protagonist willingly becomes a perceptive secretary. Group-buying enthusiasts know that updates don't happen before midnight! So this man stays up every night, always seeking to please his wife and giving her surprises.

Sixth level: Kissing the wife's photo after work

In the mobile phone, there are only photos of the wife. Firmly believing that the wife is the most beautiful! Reviewing the photos before work ends every day, kissing each one.
