Since my online store has just opened, there are many things I don't understand and still need to learn. Many things that I know have already been mentioned by seniors on the forum, so what I can share is only some small psychological experiences that I have summarized during the process of running my store.

Because I believe that mentality is as important as technical experience. Only with a good mentality can one do business well and expand it. Here, I hope that those who have just opened their stores or are hesitating at the beginner level will keep going.

As long as you work hard, there will be rewards, and I wish all Taobao store owners a prosperous business and abundant wealth!

The ten indispensable mindsets for running an online store:

1. A mindset of persistence

No matter what you do, persistence is one of the keys to success. Once we have set our goals, we must strive step by step with the most committed spirit. Opening a store on Taobao is the same as in reality; it's not just about getting the goods and putting them on the shelf to make money. We also need to do various tasks related to service and promotion. Sometimes, even after initial investment, there might be a period where business is slow. If we lack the spirit of persistence, we will never see the day of success.

There have been reports that many people searching for water sources in the desert died very close to the water source. The reason was that they didn't persist. If they had given themselves more hope mentally and persisted in their beliefs, they would have found the hope.

Opening a store is the same principle.

2. A mindset of continuous learning

Continuous learning is not only about learning from the experiences of seniors but also about learning various skills. There are many experiences from seniors on the forum, so I won't elaborate on them here. There are many other things that need to be learned, such as visiting well-performing stores to see what good practices you can borrow—this is the simplest way of "stealing skills."

Also, if you sell clothes, learn more about clothing coordination, color matching, fabric differentiation, and characteristics, etc. Make sure everything is clear in your mind so that customers asking questions can immediately see that you are professional.

If you don't know how to edit pictures, you can take time to learn. Practice taking photos until you get better. Don't be afraid of trouble or tiredness. You'll only know what you need when you try doing it yourself. Only through continuous learning can you make progress. Remember, a person's life is spent in continuous learning.

3. A pragmatic and patient mindset

Work should be done sincerely. Ask questions if you don't understand. Don't pretend to know everything.

Don't expect to become successful overnight. Successful people start from every small task. Only by being down-to-earth and doing small things well can you prepare for bigger ones.

When running an online store, there are many repetitive daily tasks: managing the store, promoting items, posting and replying on forums... Also dealing with customers, some customers may be troublesome. Sometimes you spend half the day talking to them, and finally, they tell you they've decided to buy something. But when it's time to pay, they change their minds and want something else, or maybe after all your efforts, they end up buying nothing.

Another troublesome situation is misunderstandings, which require a lot of patience to explain. Without enough patience, you will lose many customers and fail to do business well.

4. An optimistic and open-minded attitude

I think many people have heard this fable: "Two people each have a glass of water, and both drank half. One said, 'I only have half a glass left,' while another said, 'I still have half a glass.' This story tells us that when facing the same situation, some show helplessness and bitterness, extinguishing their own motivation. Others are very optimistic, always encouraging themselves to work hard.

Which of these two attitudes is more likely to succeed?

5. A mindset of willingness to give

There's a saying: Giving doesn't guarantee receiving, but not giving guarantees no receiving. Sometimes we work hard and end up with no reward.

But during the process of effort, we gain a lot unconsciously, such as learning how to work hard and cherish things.

Don't overly focus on short-term gains and losses. As long as you truly work hard and give, one day you will receive much more than you expected. A life that serves others is the most valuable!

6. A mindset of innovation

This society changes too fast. We must stay at the forefront of the times to avoid being eliminated.

Read more news, learn more. Observe the changes in the world and the markets around you. Always keep your thoughts aligned with the times and adaptable to society. Change bad habits and methods, and add more of your own innovations.

With this mindset, you will continuously change and not fall behind others.

7. A mindset of mutual assistance

In this world, everyone needs help from others, and no one can survive alone without others' help.

When you see someone seeking help, try your best to assist them, without thinking it's too much trouble to write a few words. Helping others makes them grateful, and the feeling is even happier than selling products.

Sometimes, when a customer likes something in someone else's store and asks you for advice while hesitating, you should also do your best to help them, even if they're not buying from your store. They already consider you a friend, so they open up to you. Isn't it a happy thing when someone trusts you? By helping others, they know your kindness and indirectly promote your store. Why wouldn't you do it?

8. A mindset of treating customers as friends

Even if you can't treat customers as gods, you should treat them as your friends.

Only by sincerely treating someone can you sustain and develop your business. If you always think about taking advantage of others, then your store cannot develop further.

9. A mindset of courage to take responsibility

Once there's a problem with your goods or other issues, when customers come to you, never look for excuses or shirk responsibility.

Take responsibility bravely. Apologize when necessary, even if you feel wronged.

When the customer says harsh words out of anger, you cannot argue with them.

Having such courage, others will trust you. Misunderstandings will be resolved. With mutual trust, business won't be a problem.

10. A boss's mindset

A boss's mindset means standing high and looking far ahead.

He will put a lot of energy and passion into everything he does and make decisions decisively. He treats his work as a lifelong career to strive for.

Without a boss's mindset, if you only think about making big money and focus solely on immediate interests, and hesitate to work hard, then your progress will not be far-reaching. A single bread can become a globally famous hamburger, so what is impossible?

Therefore, everyone, let's cheer up and work hardest!
