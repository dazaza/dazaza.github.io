The recruitment requirements of the County Finance Bureau are strange and accused of being predetermined, the Personnel Bureau will investigate

by zzf0zxyx80 on 2011-07-14 13:08:09

The public display provided by netizens at the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County. A screenshot of the recruitment page on the website of the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County. Southeast Network, November 24 (reporter Huang Jian) After our website reported "Recruitment for subordinate units of the Finance Bureau of Pingnan, Ningde requires overseas degrees and has been questioned," about the public recruitment of staff for the charge ticket management office under the Finance Bureau of Pingnan County, it required: "1 recruitment quota. Full-time undergraduate graduates from regular colleges and universities, with a foreign bachelor's degree, majoring in international accounting, with College English Test Band 4, registered permanent residents of Pingnan, female, and under 25 years old." The strange recruitment requirements have caused heated discussions online and strong reactions from a wide range of netizens and readers. Most netizens expressed that they hope there will be a satisfactory response to this matter. This morning, the reporter immediately started tracking this event.

Ningde Personnel Bureau: Leadership attaches great importance, ordering the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County to conduct an investigation

The reporter interviewed a surnamed Huang staff member from the office of the Personnel Bureau of Ningde City, asking if they knew about this matter. She responded that they also learned about it last night through the report on Southeast Network. They were completely unaware of this matter before. Moreover, the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County did not report this recruitment plan to the Personnel Bureau of the city. She said that the recruitment of county-level public institutions is handled by the Allocation Department of the Personnel Bureau.

The reporter then contacted Director Chen Xingu of the Allocation Department of the Personnel Bureau of Ningde City. She said that the bureau already knows about this matter, has reported it to the leadership, and the leadership attaches great importance to this matter, already ordering the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County to investigate and implement this matter. The bureau will soon hold a special meeting to discuss this matter. When the reporter asked if the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County had any feedback, she said there was none yet.

Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County: Three unknowns when asked

The reporter telephoned the Personnel Bureau of Pingnan County, where a male staff member answered the call. When the reporter asked if the recruited staff members had already arrived, he said he didn't know; when the reporter asked whether the recruited staff member obtained their foreign degree from a domestic university or returned from studying abroad, he said he didn't know; when the reporter asked if it was reasonable to require both domestic graduation and a foreign bachelor's degree, he said he didn't know; when the reporter asked if he could find someone who knows to answer the question, he still replied that he didn't know, then hung up the phone.

Finance Bureau of Pingnan County: I'm here to handle affairs outside, others are out

The reporter telephoned the Finance Bureau of Pingnan County, where a male answered the call. When the reporter asked him if he knew about this matter, he said he was here to handle affairs outside, and the office staff went out for a meeting. When the reporter asked if he could help find another office's telephone number or ask other office personnel to take the call, he said it was difficult, then hung up the phone.

Our website will continue to follow up on this matter.
