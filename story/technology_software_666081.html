Technical Characteristics and Future Development Direction of Domestic Aluminum Plate Cold Rolling Mills

1. Technical characteristics of domestic aluminum plate cold rolling mills

1. Compact arrangement of equipment in the unit, complete overall functions, improved automation level of the entire machine. Modern aluminum plate four-roll cold rolling mills all adopt irreversible rolling, equipped with automatic coil transport devices. In the units designed from the late 20th century to the early 21st century, they were also equipped with automatic measurement and automatic centering devices for coils, enabling automated operations for loading and unloading coils, gradually reducing operational intensity, improving yield, and enhancing competitiveness.

2. Increased rolling speed, increased single-machine production capacity

In recent years, the maximum rolling speed of domestic aluminum plate cold rolling mills has increased from the original 300m/min to nearly 800m/min. With the introduction of automatic control systems for plate shape, the highest rolling speed continues to increase, reaching 1200m/min. However, due to thicker incoming material, the single-machine output is relatively low. The single-machine production capacity has increased from an initial 7.5kt/a to the current 40kt/a, but compared to foreign countries, there is still a certain gap in both rolling speed and single-machine production capacity.

3. Improved domestication level of the entire machine, convenient maintenance

With the development of the domestic equipment industry, domestic supporting capabilities have further improved. Previously, domestically produced cold rolling mills mainly relied on imported detection elements or control systems, which are now gradually being replaced by high-quality and cost-effective domestic elements or systems, such as the thickness automatic control system (AGC), the automatic bias correction control system for strip (EPC), X-ray thickness gauges, and the automatic control system for plate shape (AFC). At the same time, due to the improvement in domestication, the cost of equipment maintenance has become more economical, making products more competitive.

4. More perfect and standardized hydraulic and lubrication systems

The hydraulic system of domestic aluminum plate cold rolling mills mainly adopts an integrated pump station and decentralized valve table layout, with the rolling mill control trending towards full hydraulization. Servo hydraulic systems for alignment, bending roll servo hydraulic control, thickness servo hydraulic control, as well as the measurement and hydraulic control systems for loading and unloading coils, are widely used in rolling mill control.

5. Significant development in electrical drive systems

The control systems for unwinding machines, winding machines, and main transmission motors generally use fully digital DC drive units. Compared to AC drive units, the advantages of fully digital DC drive units include lower costs, simpler and more reliable control, and easier maintenance. AC drive units, compared to DC drive units, offer advantages such as compact structure, less maintenance, and excellent dynamic control characteristics.

2. Future development direction of domestic aluminum plate rolling equipment manufacturers

Observing the design, research, and production trends of modern aluminum plate foil rolling equipment worldwide, if China wants to further improve the overall level of its domestically produced aluminum rolling equipment technology, domestic aluminum plate foil rolling equipment manufacturers should adapt to global trends, establish a modern aluminum rolling equipment research, design, manufacturing, production, and maintenance system, organically combining independent concepts from different fields to produce more modern rolling mills.

1. Emphasize integrated design and service for aluminum plate cold rolling mills

Integrated design of rolling mills is very important; all components of the mechanical, hydraulic, and automation systems must be effectively coordinated. The selection of sensors and the positioning of measuring devices need to be considered.

2. Focus on scaled and specialized production, consciously becoming a system provider

Future aluminum plate markets require stricter finished product quality, along with higher flexibility and adaptability of their production equipment. As developers of aluminum plate cold rolling mills, it is necessary to enhance the functionality of the process system through collaboration across mechanical engineering, hydraulic systems, electrical engineering, and automation systems, closely collaborating with users to form a systematic partnership that promotes sustainable development for both parties.

3. Emphasize safety and reliability of aluminum plate cold rolling mills

Factory equipment design adheres to high standards in safety, requiring safe and reliable mechanical and electrical design structures; requiring the best balanced optimal safety and protective measures; requiring safe operation through close cooperation between mechanical, hydraulic, and electrical automation; requiring safe and reliable operation of equipment and production processes, and requiring safe and comfortable operation during maintenance and service stages.
