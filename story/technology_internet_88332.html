The Myth of Tang Jun's Taobao Store Opening Entrepreneurship: From 6800 Yuan to 2 Million Yuan

by longyi on 2008-08-31 14:19:19

Tang Jun, the founder of Ripai LCD and the general manager of Guangzhou Ripai Digital Co., Ltd. In 1999, he started his working life in Guangzhou alone from Hunan, starting as a technician, then an engineer, and later a department manager. With greater ambitions, he moved to Shenzhen in 2003 and worked in the security industry for two years, creating a profit of 2 million yuan for his company in just two short years. As he approached thirty, he began his first entrepreneurship venture, resolutely taking his half-year salary of 6800 yuan to start his entrepreneurial journey.

Tang Jun has conducted research on both hardware and software of electronic computers, and LCD products were the emerging products at that time. After careful study and market analysis, he identified LCD monitors as a product with a broad future, thus establishing a connection with LCD monitors.

In the old Yigao Computer City in Guangzhou, Tang Jun rented a small counter of 7 square meters for 2500 yuan to start selling LCD monitors. At the beginning, due to being a first-time entrepreneur without experience or sales skills, he only sold one or two LCD monitors in a week! This posed a survival crisis. However, at this time, by chance, he saw a friend selling items on Taobao, so he attempted to start his online store career. Unexpectedly, Mr. Jack Ma's phrase "there is no difficult business in the world" created a miracle for his small online store.

Tang Jun sells high-value LCD monitors on Taobao. Initially, increasing credibility was slow because, in online business, good reviews provide the greatest persuasiveness to customers. Thus, he started selling some computer accessories (such as mice, headphones, etc.) at cost price, making no profit, but over time, the online store gradually accumulated a lot of credit. By June 2006, it became a diamond-credit seller. For stores specializing in LCDs on Taobao, having diamond credit is rare, and its reputation among buyers steadily improved.

Being an honest online merchant and providing heartfelt service have always been Tang Jun's service principles! When the online store initially started, since LCD products are prone to damage, a 20-inch LCD TV shipped to Tongling was accidentally damaged during transportation. The customer unknowingly signed for it, only to find out the product was damaged after opening it. They immediately contacted Tang Jun. Tang Jun arranged for them to return the product and claimed compensation from the courier company, but since the customer had already signed for it, the courier company refused to compensate. The customer helplessly complained about their bad luck, frankly speaking, the customer should bear the responsibility. However, at this moment, Tang Jun made an important decision. He told the customer that he would unconditionally send them a brand-new identical product! This loss-making action earned him credibility. But learning from this experience, Tang Jun now has a dedicated transportation company partner, and before the customer signs for the product, they must check if the product is damaged. Success grows stronger with each accumulation. Entrepreneurial success is truly built through drops of sweat and drops of dedication!

After accumulating a certain amount of customer resources, Tang Jun considered strengthening and expanding the enterprise, choosing to go the brand route, as only brands can be the entry tickets in modern market competition. By the end of 2005, Tang Jun registered the "Ripai (RIPAI)" trademark for the products and began production, transitioning from retail to bulk and then to scale production. All current product lines have passed the country's 3C certification, as well as CE and FC safety certifications!

When the online store slowly accumulated a certain amount of credibility, and considering that the old Yigao Computer City itself positioned itself as low-quality products, he abandoned the physical store to focus on building a flagship online store! Usually, two people are specifically responsible for Aliwangwang, while another is responsible for designing the corporate website. Professional services, honest transactions, and superior quality ultimately achieved the myth of 300,000 yuan monthly sales on Taobao.

From the northernmost "Mohe", the southernmost "Hainan", to the westernmost "Korla in Xinjiang", all permeate the bloodline of "Ripai (RIPAI)"! From shipping two units a week, one unit a day, to 100 units a month, and then developing to 250-300 units per month; from starting capital of 6880 yuan to today's assets of 2 million yuan after three years! Tang Jun completed his first pot of gold in entrepreneurship, and new higher goals are being planned.

The reason Tang Jun succeeded is because he chose to try and change, choosing to surpass his former self! Today's actions will determine your future tomorrow. Today, Tang Jun has become a successful online entrepreneur, entering the top 50 in the Guangzhou region of the Fifth Online Business Competition. His motto is "step by step, dreams become reality." Believe that in the days to come, Tang Jun and his Ripai will achieve better results.
