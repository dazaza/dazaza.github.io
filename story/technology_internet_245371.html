Most importantly, promoting on Kaixin001 doesn't cost money. Moreover, Kaixin001 has developed unique features like friend-to-friend transmission and invitation functions that you can take full advantage of.

First: Register an account on Kaixin001. If you're male, I suggest using a female identity. Your profile picture should preferably be of a beautiful woman, but not one who's too generic or famous, so users won't easily recognize it as a fake account and ignore you. You can add your website, online store, or product information to your profile, but not excessively.

Second: Use Kaixin001's invitation feature to invite all your MSN and email contacts to join. By doing so, you gain free audiences and promoters. Once these friends join, they'll likely invite their own friends, significantly increasing your reach.

Third: After inviting friends, use Kaixin001's "Find People" function, such as "Possible Friends" or "Friends of Friends". Using this function allows you to quickly add friends of friends, making them your own friends. Since adding friends of friends is more likely to succeed and less likely to be rejected, your number of friends will increase. Don't rush into promotion at this stage, as unfamiliar friends might easily delete you if you start promoting right away.

Many people spend millions building websites and accumulate members over several years, yet may not reach 80,000 members. If you send a mass message with a 20% activity rate, you'll have nearly 20,000 direct target audiences. Through their further transmission, the potential audience could reach hundreds of thousands.

Fourth: After accumulating a certain number of friends, you need to disguise yourself. How? For example, regarding your product or the website you want to promote, post relevant blogs. If I'm promoting an online bookkeeping software for individuals and families called Caakee Online, I wouldn't immediately post soft promotional articles about Caakee Online because unfamiliar friends would easily block or delete me, wasting all my previous efforts. At this point, I would definitely forward some good financial management articles, letting everyone know I provide quality financial content and share knowledge with everyone. This approach is less advertising-heavy, allowing me to make an impression in front of friends while leaving a good impression on others.

Fifth: With these foundations laid, there's still something missing. What is it? You need to add some particularly active and famous grassroots Kaixin001 members. Due to their efforts and positivity, they've gathered a large following on Kaixin001, with popularity, many friends, and high credibility. You need to befriend them. According to the 2:8 rule, 80% of your friends are silent or contribute little to you. You need to focus on the few very valuable friends.
