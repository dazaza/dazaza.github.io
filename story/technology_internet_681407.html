IFM Product Applications

Euchner-Ifm electronic products are applicable in various industrial fields.

Robotics Technology

Robots are fundamental components for improving production and logistics. Due to their new modular design and advanced standardization, they offer a wide range of flexible applications.

Sensors are the soul of robots. Ifm offers a wide selection of sensors that provide compact, high-efficiency structural models and meet demanding application requirements.

Assembly automation demands increasingly shorter production cycles and higher quality standards, while it is necessary to control, monitor, and record more quality and functional characteristics of products during the assembly process.

Ifm's innovative sensors and fastening components can be precisely applied within narrow installation spaces.

Logistics and production development are closely linked. The processing of raw materials and products in production processes imposes increasingly strict cycle-based and time-specific logistics requirements.

Automotive Industry

Ifm Electronic GmbH, as a leading sensor manufacturer, provides application solutions for all critical areas of the automotive industry.

Ifm offers you extensive experience in vehicle body manufacturing, welding, engine and transmission production, paint equipment, final assembly, and all transmission technologies.

Transmission Technology

Transmission technology can be applied in almost all industries. Whether pallets, boxes, workpiece fixtures, granules, stones, or pipelines;

Ifm Electronic can provide suitable sensors, evaluation systems, and cost-effective AS-Interface (AS-i) connection systems for almost all areas.

Food Industry

As one of the world-renowned sensor manufacturers, Ifm Electronic provides application solutions for all critical areas of the food industry.

Due to the heavy load on sensors used in this market, they are prone to damage. Therefore, the requirements for applying sensor technology are also very high accordingly.

Machine Tool Industry

Ifm Electronic offers a series of durable and reliable sensors specifically tailored to the needs of the machine tool and metalworking industries.

Ifm products are easy to operate and possess strong resistance to impact, vibration, and oil corrosion.
