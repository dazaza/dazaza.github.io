Bidding is about to start, Liaoning Tieling City is tendering for the procurement of 9 fire trucks 18671079399

by xfxybk on 2012-02-18 16:25:29

Recently, Tieling Hengxin Bidding Co., Ltd. was commissioned by the Tieling Public Security Fire Brigade to conduct a domestic invitation-only bidding process for the procurement of fire trucks and firefighting equipment for the Tieling Public Security Fire Brigade.

According to reports, this project is divided into four packages for procurement, with a total of 9 fire trucks to be procured. Specifically: Package 1 involves the procurement of 2 18-meter high-reach aerial fire trucks; Package 2 involves the procurement of 1 fire truck; Package 3 involves the procurement of 2 water tanker fire trucks; Package 4 involves the procurement of 4 water tanker fire trucks. All vehicle types must include accompanying equipment. The project requires suppliers to bid on all items, otherwise their bids will be rejected as non-responsive. Additionally, joint venture bids are not allowed for this project.

It is reported that the tender opening for this project will take place at 1:30 PM on February 23rd in the meeting room on the 6th floor of the Tieling Public Resources Trading Center in Liaoning Province.
