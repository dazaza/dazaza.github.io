Xinhed County Liba Water Conservancy Machinery Factory specializes in producing various specifications and models of screw hoists, winch hoists, cast iron gates, steel plate gates, cast iron copper-lined gates, flap gates, round gates, square gates... Our products are reliable in quality, reasonable in price, and widely used in water conservancy irrigation, reservoirs, power stations, aquaculture, and are ideal mechanical equipment for water conservancy projects.

1. LQ type screw hoist: This product comes in manual, electric, hand-electric dual-use, enclosed, single-lift double-lift types, ranging from 0.5 tons to 100 tons with over 20 varieties.

- Hand-pushed lock type: 0.5T-3T

- Side-shaking lock type: 0.3T-5T

- Hand-cranked type: 3T-20T

- Hand-electric dual-use type: 3T-100T (single-lift point, double-lift point)

2. QPQ, QPK (quick-closing door), QPG, QHQ type winch hoist series: Available in single-lift point, double-lift point, and equipped with electric drive mobile type. Products below 25 tons are hand-electric dual-use, above 40 tons are electric with safety overload protection devices, all equipped with upper and lower limit devices. Additionally, our factory can produce various tonnage adjustable stroke winches and mobile hoists according to user requirements.

3. Steel gates: Our factory produces according to national standards based on user design requirements. We can also adopt new processes for surface zinc spraying treatment according to user design requirements. Rubber sealing is used.

4. Cast iron plane sliding gate: This gate has good sealing effect, corrosion resistance, easy installation and maintenance, suitable for single and double directional sealing in projects.

5. Sealing rubber and expansion joint rubber strips: All items tested by the Rubber Research Institute of the Chemical Industry Department meet national standard requirements.
