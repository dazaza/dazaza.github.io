Scutellaria Barbata (commonly known as四方马兰, 并头草, or 狭叶韩信草) [Medicine] is the whole herb of the plant Scutellaria barbata D.Don from the Lamiaceae family. It is produced in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Shanxi, Shaanxi, Yunnan and other places. It is harvested during the flowering period in summer, dried in the sun, used raw, or also used fresh.

[Pharmacology] Contains alkaloids, flavonoid glycosides, phenolic compounds, steroidal compounds, etc.

[Pharmacodynamics]

(1) The alcohol preparation (200 mg/ml) has an anti-cancer effect on cancer tissues such as colon cancer obtained from human bodies when placed in tumor tissue culture fluid; the decoction has a destructive effect on HeLa cells separated from a stable cervical cancer cell line.

(2) Experimental results using the cell respiration method show that it has more than 75% inhibition rate on leukemia cells.

(3) The alcohol preparation inhibits the growth of transplantable mouse sarcoma S-180, Ehrlich ascites carcinoma, brain tumor-22, etc.

(4) The macerate has a blood pressure lowering effect when administered intravenously to animals; oral administration does not produce the same effect.

(5) The ether extract of the macerate has a diuretic effect in animal experiments.

(6) The decoction has antibacterial effects on Staphylococcus aureus, dysentery bacillus, typhoid bacillus, Pseudomonas aeruginosa, Escherichia coli, etc.

[Drug Properties] Pungent, slightly bitter, cool. Enters the lung, liver, spleen, stomach, and large intestine meridians.

[Therapeutic Effects] Detoxifying and anti-cancer, reducing swelling and dispersing nodules, removing blood stasis and stopping bleeding.
