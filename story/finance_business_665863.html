Enteritis dietary therapy - Anji white tea price congee

[Source]: Folk medicinal food recipe

[Ingredients]: 15 grams of Anji white tea at its price, 100 grams of japonica rice, and an appropriate amount of sugar.

[Preparation]: Boil Anji white tea for 15 minutes first to get about 500 grams of concentrated juice. Remove the white tea, add japonica rice and sugar into the concentrated juice of Anji white tea, then add about 400 grams of water, and cook them together as porridge.

[Usage]: Divide into two servings, eat warm.

[Function]: Resolve phlegm, promote digestion, diuretic and reduce swelling, invigorate qi and refresh spirit. Applicable to acute and chronic dysentery, enteritis.

[Comment]: White tea has an invigorating effect, so it should not be consumed before sleep. The price of Anji white tea contains tannins, habitual constipation sufferers should avoid eating it, and breastfeeding women should abstain from it.
