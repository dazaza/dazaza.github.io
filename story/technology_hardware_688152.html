The girl is emotionally agitated at the edge of the rooftop.

by yingsi7943 on 2012-03-03 01:52:09

Today, a girl called her boyfriend later in the morning. The two had a fierce argument again and the boyfriend said some angry words like "you die". The girl replied: "Alright, I'll die for you to see..." After the call, the girl indeed climbed to the second-floor terrace.

■ Chongqing Evening News reporters Fan Yongjin, Yang Fan, intern Yin Yi, photography report

Witness Mr. Han introduced that at around 9 o'clock last night, he saw on his way home from Nanba Road that there was a girl lying on the edge of a commercial-residential building, with her hands hanging in the air, crying continuously. Many residents gathered nearby. Mr. Han felt something was wrong, and some residents recognized that the girl seemed to live on the third floor. He immediately reported it to the police.

Subsequently, the two were taken away by the local police station officers for investigation.

At the same time, the surrounding traffic patrol police also arrived at the scene. Due to the girl's intense emotions, she refused anyone to approach, making the rescue work unable to proceed.

Soon, Kong Lingcen, a female traffic patrol officer in her 20s from the Jiangbei District Beicheng Tiandi platform, and her colleagues arrived at the scene. Through interviews, they learned that the girl was about 16 years old, from a remote family in Weixin County, Sichuan Province, and followed her working mother to Chongqing, renting a house on the third floor of this small building. At around 9 o'clock yesterday morning, taking advantage of her relatives being out, the girl climbed out from her third-floor balcony and jumped onto the second-floor terrace. This building has an underground garage, so the second-floor terrace is more than 10 meters above the ground.

After the door of the second-floor terrace was unlocked, since other rescue personnel could not approach, as the only female, Kong Lingcen decided to use the advantage of gender to get closer. She stopped at a distance of 7-8 meters from the girl just in case. At this point, the girl was sitting at the edge of the terrace corner, with both legs inside, extremely dangerous. Seeing a woman approaching, the girl did not refuse.

After hearing these, Kong Lingcen immediately offered to help experience her boyfriend, got her boyfriend's phone number from the girl, and immediately handed it over to her colleague, asking the boy to come quickly. Before the police officer dialed the girl's boyfriend's phone, the other side didn't expect that one of his angry words would make the girl take it seriously, indicating that he lived in Sha Area and would take a taxi over immediately.

After receiving the alarm, the local fire department immediately dispatched a fire truck and set up a rescue air cushion below the girl.

At around 10:50 AM, the girl's boyfriend finally arrived. To the surprise of the police officers and rescuers, after seeing her boyfriend, the girl didn't blame or cry, but smiled instead. Within two minutes, the boyfriend helped the girl up.

During the waiting period for the girl's boyfriend to arrive, Kong Lingcen continued talking to the girl. Hearing that the girl liked surfing the Internet, Kong Lingcen immediately suggested that they mutually follow each other on Weibo. The girl agreed, and her mood improved significantly. However, when the girl was about to hand over her phone to Kong Lingcen to add Weibo, she suddenly pulled her hand back.
