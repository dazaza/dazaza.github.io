This product is mainly aimed at high-end clients, requiring substantial capital, typically over 2 million RMB, to participate in such transactions. ETF is a basket of stocks. Therefore, even during a sharp drop in stock prices, we can make profits through this price difference by purchasing a basket of stocks at a low price, converting them into ETFs, and selling them at a higher price on the market.
