How to Apply to Churchman University?

Churchman University, with its bilingual programs in Chinese and English, is currently accepting international students. So, how can one successfully complete the application process?

By carefully reading the requirements for applicants on the university's official website: "We are particularly interested in students who demonstrate the following abilities:

1) Showing creativity, especially adaptable to new concepts and new technologies.

2) Knowing what their career goal is and showing determination to achieve their goal.

3) Demonstrating self-discipline and following all application requirements in a timely manner."

The admissions department of the university pays special attention to the following student capabilities:

1) Creativity and adaptability to new concepts and technologies.

2) Clear academic and career goals.

3) Demonstrating self-discipline and completing all application requirements accurately and promptly.

It can be seen that the university values the student's ability to grasp new knowledge and concepts, their determination to further their studies, and their self-discipline. Therefore, when preparing application materials, one should focus on enhancing themselves in these three main areas. For example, you should introduce any cutting-edge business activities you've participated in, as well as your courage and perseverance in work and life.

Churchman University's distance learning business degree courses allow middle and senior managers from all over the world to study online at any time and place without affecting their current jobs, take exams online, and submit papers online. The degrees obtained are exactly the same as those earned through on-campus study, making it the preferred educational model for middle and senior managers worldwide, including in Europe, America, and China.

Churchman University's bilingual (Spanish-English and Chinese-English) courses are highly popular among multinational corporations. The university offers MBA, undergraduate, master’s, and doctoral degrees. Once successfully applied for, the low-income scholarship provided by the school requires only a few thousand dollars to complete the degree.

Churchman University has campuses in Pennsylvania, California, Texas, and other locations in the United States. Among them, the earliest established branch, Churchman Business School, is a renowned American church university known for its rigorous academic atmosphere, founded in 1911 with nearly a hundred years of history. It is also one of the oldest business schools in the United States, specifically cultivating high-end talent for the American financial industry. In the "National MBA Rankings for Business Schools," this century-old prestigious university ranks 17th.

In addition to Churchman University, other century-old universities in the United States such as Boston University and Brown University have teaching qualities that are consistently recognized by white-collar professionals in China.
