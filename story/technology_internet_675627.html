**Electrical Worker Certification Training in Suzhou (High and Low Voltage Electrical Work)**

**Training Prospects**

To prevent casualties and promote safe production, it is essential to enhance the safety technical skills of electrical workers and standardize their training, assessment, certification, and supervision. According to the "People's Republic of China Law on Safety in Production," the "People's Republic of China Administrative Licensing Law," and other relevant laws and regulations, all personnel engaged in electrical work must receive safety technology training legally organized and guided by the competent authority for safety production supervision. After passing the examination and obtaining the qualification certificate for electrical work operations, they can then work with a certificate to carry out corresponding tasks and operations. **Qualification Certification**

After passing the examination, the provincial department of safety production supervision or the municipal safety production management department will issue a special operation qualification certificate made uniformly by the National Administration of Safety Production Supervision. Only after obtaining the qualification certificate can electricians be allowed to work independently.

The special operation qualification certificate is the only valid document for special operation personnel to engage in special operations. The certificate is valid nationwide (query website: http://www.guangl.cn/zgzscx.aspx). Special operation personnel must carry this certificate when performing special operations.

**Eligibility Requirements for Low Voltage Electrical Work:**

1. Electricians must be at least 18 years old, have completed junior high school or above, and pass a physical examination at a county-level hospital or higher, ensuring good health, normal mental state, and no conditions that would hinder electrical work. Physical examinations must be repeated every two years thereafter.

2. Possess necessary electrical knowledge and business skills and have at least two years of work experience in related industries.

**Eligibility Requirements for High Voltage Electrical Work:**

Must hold the original certificate issued by the Safety Supervision Bureau for "Low Voltage Electrical Work."
