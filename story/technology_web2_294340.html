Han Shu Choi Ji-yoo signs with Han Shu brand, makes her own cucumber and pearl powder masks

by hanshu2009 on 2009-12-05 20:43:38

Hansu Invests Heavily in Inviting Choi Ji-woo to Create a Radiant Skin Miracle

Korean film and television star Choi Ji-woo has officially signed with the Hansu brand, becoming the spokesperson for the Hansu brand. The advertisement series featuring Choi Ji-woo promoting Hansu's super energy products received an overwhelming response from society as soon as it was aired. Her elegant performance in a red dress left the audience in awe. In the commercial, Choi Ji-woo's white and fine skin texture made many female viewers envious. The advertising slogan "Whiter than you, smoother than you" has now become a popular internet phrase, showing that Choi Ji-woo's image of flawless whiteness is deeply ingrained in the minds of consumers.

When asked why she chose to endorse the Hansu brand, Choi Ji-woo expressed: "I have been using Hansu products in Korea, and this time being able to endorse my favorite brand makes me very happy. I also hope to share my skincare experiences and insights with Chinese consumers."

Choi Ji-woo Reveals Her Beauty Secrets

In "Winter Sonata," Choi Ji-woo portrayed a frail yet resilient character with a detached but deeply emotional style, leaving a deep impression on audiences. Later, in "Stairway to Heaven," she vividly depicted kindness and tenderness intertwined with heart-wrenching emotions. Choi Ji-woo's popularity soared due to her excellent acting skills, and she has now become one of the top actresses in Korean dramas. Choi Ji-woo herself has extremely white and delicate skin, serving as a typical representative of Asian female artists. She has her own set of methods for skincare. During her student days, she often made her own fruit and vegetable masks, such as cucumber sheet masks, to hydrate her skin and improve dull complexion. After starting her career, she frequently used pearl powder masks to whiten her skin while ensuring its smoothness. When asked about her beauty secrets, Choi Ji-woo said: "Skincare is a lifelong course for women because the nutritional needs of the skin vary at different ages. But the most important thing is to choose what suits you best and go for natural options."

Choi Ji-woo Falls Deeply in Love with Hansu, Working Tirelessly on Advertisements

During the filming process, Choi Ji-woo's gentle and elegant demeanor and friendly attitude were often praised by the staff as angelic. However, once she started working, she became a "devil," setting extremely high standards for her performances, leaving everyone present speechless. Before the shoot, she actively participated in discussions about the shooting script, and many creative ideas for the shots incorporated Choi Ji-woo's input. During the filming, she continued to provide numerous suggestions and insisted that the director conduct comparative shoots. After each shot, Choi Ji-woo would rush to the camera to review it with the director, not tolerating any flaws in her performance or visual angles. A simple shot was repeated many times. During breaks, Choi Ji-woo politely declined the meals arranged by the staff, insisting on eating only after all the shots were completed. She said: "I am already a 'fan' of Hansu in Korea, and now having the opportunity to be its spokesperson makes me very happy. I want to ensure that the super energy advertisement is perfect without any flaws."

After a day of shooting, Choi Ji-woo bid farewell to each member of the staff, and her kind attitude and dedication deeply moved everyone present. Even the director exclaimed that Choi Ji-woo was the most dedicated and professional actor he had encountered while shooting advertisements.
