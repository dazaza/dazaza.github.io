Below, let us analyze the reasons why babies are picky eaters for all mothers:

One, indulging in stubbornness. Spoiling and not having fixed meal times leads to a lack of routine, allowing them to indulge in their preferences, favoring snacks over proper meals, causing spleen and stomach damage.

Two, inappropriate feeding. Providing only high-nutrition or nourishing foods, encouraging or even forcing children to eat exceeds their digestive capacity, leading to a gradual weakening of digestive abilities over time.

Three, insufficient from birth. Such as premature twins, low birth weight infants, etc., who are born with weak constitutions and insufficient spleen and stomach functions.

Four, post-illness imbalance. Especially diarrhea, pneumonia, repeated respiratory infections, which deplete qi and yin, causing spleen and stomach injuries and abnormal digestive function.

Five, psychological factors. Some parents often scold their children at the dinner table, leading to an unpleasant eating mood for the child. Other children may fear becoming overweight and thus lose their appetite and refuse to eat.

In response to the problem of children being picky eaters, parents have tried various solutions. Let us teach parents a few treatment methods:

One, find the reason for the child’s loss of appetite. Parents should adopt accurate feeding methods, providing a variety of easily digestible foods according to the child’s age, correcting bad eating habits, reducing intake of rich and fatty foods, not forcing the child to eat, paying attention to emotional care, ensuring the child has a pleasant mood, strengthening the desire to eat, and not scolding the child at the dinner table.

Two, supplementing what the stomach likes. First use the child’s favorite food to stimulate their appetite, then supplement with reasonable foods based on growth and development needs.
