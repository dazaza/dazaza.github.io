A Brief Analysis of the Online Shopping Situation of Urban Residents

by anonymous on 2011-04-01 12:16:01

[Abstract] With the vigorous development of Internet business, online shopping has gained increasing popularity among more and more Chinese people due to its convenience and cost-effectiveness. It is also a future trend. During the past year's "May Day" and "National Day" Golden Week holidays, online shopping made a big splash. Chinese consumers proved the growth of online shopping by spending nearly 100 million yuan daily on the Internet. In order to gain a deeper understanding of the online shopping situation of urban residents, we conducted an online survey by creating a questionnaire titled "Survey on the Online Shopping Situation of Urban Residents."

[Keywords]

[Main Body]

Currently, there are mainly two service models for online shopping: C2C platforms, which refer to e-commerce between individuals, or the model of individual merchants to consumers; B2C platforms, which refer to e-commerce between businesses and individuals, or the model of enterprises (or units) to consumers. Online shopping is safe, convenient, and fast, and it is also a future trend. Therefore, with the popularization of the Internet, online shopping is gradually becoming one of the ways people consume.

1. Analysis from the results of 61 randomly selected respondents shows the following characteristics:

1) The demographic of online shoppers in urban areas tends to be younger with higher overall education levels.

From the age composition of online shoppers, the group tends to be younger. Users aged 20 to 30 are the main force in online shopping, accounting for 88.5% in this survey. As the Internet becomes more widespread, more young people will start shopping online, and this data will continue to grow. Users under 20 years old account for 9.8%. Due to their lack of economic independence and mostly no income, their purchasing power is weak. Users over 30 years old account for 1.6%. Although this group has a fixed income, their understanding of the Internet decreases with age, combined with relatively traditional consumption concepts and more psychological barriers, resulting in weaker motivation for online shopping.

From the educational background of the respondents, most are high school or vocational high school graduates, junior college or undergraduate graduates, accounting for 6.8%, 18%, and 73.8%, respectively. In the next few years, as the educational level of Internet users changes, online shopping users will gradually penetrate into lower educational levels and shift towards serving the general public.

2) Online shopping consumption will continue to grow.

As the online shopping market continues to develop, urban residents' acceptance of online shopping deepens, and more and more people are shifting their shopping focus to the Internet. Although online shopping is still relatively backward, its development potential is enormous.

Have you ever bought anything online? ()

In the survey, 63.9% of respondents have shopped multiple times online, 21.3% twice, 6.6% once, and only 8.2% have never shopped online. Generally speaking, after having a successful online shopping experience, there will be a second and third time. This is because the online shopping market attracts consumers with low prices and convenient shopping methods.

3) Male online shoppers predominate, and clothing and hats are the main items purchased online.

From the analysis of online shoppers, the proportion of males is significantly greater than females. This is mainly due to the fashionability, convenience, and entertainment of online shopping combining with male shopping habits. Female Internet users are also gradually becoming active in online shopping. Among the surveyed online shoppers, females accounted for 57.4%, and male users accounted for 42.6%.

Currently, the variety of goods available in the online shopping market is very extensive, covering a wide range. The range of products consumers buy through online shopping platforms continues to expand, from mobile phones and computers to clothing, cosmetics, and household items. Online shopping product preferences are ranked as clothing and footwear, digital products, and book/audio-visual products, accounting for 72.1%, 36.1%, and 26.2%, respectively. The trend of online shopping becoming a part of daily life is quite evident.

The popularity of clothing and cosmetic sales is closely related to the product, channel, and user characteristics. First, the consumption of clothing and cosmetic products combines well with online fashion trends and product diversity, effectively leveraging the advantages of online shopping. Second, clothing items have small amounts, are easy to preserve, and take up little space, making them preferred products for merchants. Third, fashion elements are infiltrating the online shopping market, corresponding to the enthusiasm of men for digital products and women's strong purchasing power for clothing accessories.

4) Taobao has the highest recognition, and online payment is the most commonly used method.

The survey data shows that Taobao is the most recognized and has the largest number of users among shopping websites. Out of 61 respondents, 54 people frequently shop on Taobao, accounting for 88.5% of the total, with most users having a high dependence on Taobao. Secondly, 22 people often shop on Paipai.com, accounting for 36.1%. This is because the website gathers various sellers, offering a wide range of products, ensuring that users can meet their purchase needs on one shopping site.

On which websites have you done online shopping? ()

In the selection of payment methods, 44 users chose online payment, accounting for 72.1% of the total, followed by bank transfers, chosen by 14 users, accounting for 14.8% of the total, and postal money orders accounting for 1.6%. This indicates that with the popularization of the Internet, traditional payment methods are declining.

Two, Problems Existing in the Online Shopping Market

Although the online shopping market is continuously developing and has advantages such as convenience and speed, there are some problems that need to be solved urgently.

1) The quality of goods in online shopping cannot be guaranteed. In the survey, 81% of respondents believed that the most pressing issue to be addressed in online shopping is the quality of goods. Now, whether it's Taobao or Paipai, they are filled with a large amount of counterfeit and inferior products, affecting consumers psychologically. Therefore, many people hesitate to shop online, which restricts the development of online shopping to a certain extent.

2) Merchants lack integrity. All major shopping websites have issues such as inaccurate merchant descriptions and false advertisements, misleading consumers, thereby leading to a decline in the credibility of some online shopping websites and merchants.

3) Lack of regulation and guidance for the online shopping market.

4) Severe insufficiency in delivery capabilities.

Three, Suggestions and Countermeasures

1) Quickly formulate online shopping laws to standardize online business operations and combat fraudulent activities using online shopping. Through legislation, curb fraudulent behavior in the online shopping market, and hope that it can become a concern for all parties involved this year, including the government, websites, buyers and sellers, banks, third-party payment platforms, and consumer associations.

2) Increase publicity efforts so that Internet users are familiar with and actively use the regulations already in place. To standardize online transactions, increase publicity efforts, reminding participants in online transactions to pay attention to risk avoidance. Before transactions, all parties should try to learn as much as possible about the other party's true identity, credit status, and ability to fulfill contracts. Pay attention to payment security and keep records of online transactions as evidence for dispute resolution.

3) Strengthen supervision of online transactions and establish and improve the online transaction credit certification system. Large online trading platforms should take real responsibility, strengthen the qualification review of operators, ensure the safety of online transactions, and promote the healthy and orderly development of the online trading industry.

4) Vigorously develop the logistics industry. The development of online shopping depends on the support of the logistics industry, and the two are interdependent. Therefore, to develop online shopping, the logistics industry must also be developed.
