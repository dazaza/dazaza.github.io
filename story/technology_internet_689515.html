Gift boxes are a type of outer packaging custom-made to make gifts more attractive, thus adding value to the product.

Generally speaking, gift boxes can be classified by material into paper gift boxes, wooden gift boxes, plastic gift boxes, aluminum gift boxes, and tinplate gift boxes. According to product type, they mainly include wine gift boxes, tea gift boxes, shoe gift boxes, mooncake gift boxes, and craft gift boxes, among others.

With the development of the economy and society, and the continuous improvement of people's living standards, the requirements for products are also changing. As the saying goes, "People rely on clothing, things rely on packaging." Various goods not only need good quality but also require exquisite and tempting outer packaging. Therefore, as packaging boxes, gift boxes and various outer packaging boxes not only have the function of storage, freshness preservation, and moisture prevention but also play a role in aesthetics, enhancing brand recognition, and added value. For example, in products suitable for gifting like tea, local specialties, bird's nest, caterpillar fungus, etc., a customized, tasteful, eye-catching product packaging gift box can not only make consumers like it and increase sales but also make the product appreciate several times in value. Gift packaging boxes can achieve the purpose of enhancing the brand awareness of tea and increasing the added value of products.

Gift boxes are also an art form, a perfect representation of the value of goods. Especially in today's rapidly developing knowledge economy and information science, the fusion and collision of national culture, regional culture, and East-West culture mean that gift paper printing represents not only stunning works of art but also increasingly highlights its multiple functions of being close to the market, catering to the market, guiding consumption, and meeting people's material needs and spiritual enjoyment in product packaging.

To a large extent, gift boxes beautify and promote products with their exquisite shapes and decoration, enhancing the competitiveness of goods. They are one of the important means of converting products into commodities and become a crucial link connecting products with consumers and sellers with buyers.
