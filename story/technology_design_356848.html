Rescued 12 years after being abducted, boy's biological father had searched across 23 provincial-level regions

by yqwdfkmt on 2010-05-29 11:39:37

The police who received the report from Gu Keli's wife also abandoned the case themselves, until they finally obtained a key clue in their old age and helped to find the child. When he saw his daughter, tears streamed down Gu Keli's face, who had been on the road for years and looked haggard.

On March 28, Gu Hongliang was brought to Xinmi by the police and handed over to Gu Keli. Father and son met, and Gu Keli burst into tears again, while Gu Hongliang, who almost had no memory of his biological father, was very confused. At this time, Gu Keli was unkempt and haggard. Watching him walking with difficulty with crutches, everyone present was deeply moved.

The police said that considering the child's educational issues, if the two families could reconcile, it would not be a bad thing for both sides. This suggestion was agreed upon by Gu Keli, who was worried about life, and Wang Shuqing, who was about to lose her "son". After negotiation, the two families reconciled first, Gu Hongming continued to go to school, and the two families could visit each other's homes at any time.

According to his wife He Xiu Ying, the people who abducted his son were actually women, one fat and one relatively kind-looking. Gu Keli and his wife reported the case to the Baizhai police station, and he chased after them according to the mother's description, but found nothing until dawn.

In order to save money, he worked while looking for his son. Over the years, he spent nearly 160,000 yuan. Not long after his son was abducted, his wife became mentally confused due to excessive grief and left home, remaining untraceable to this day.

In 1998, after receiving He Xiu Ying's report, the Xinmi police launched an investigation. According to He Xiu Ying's response at the time, the perpetrators were three or four people, one of whom was somewhat familiar and spoke Sichuan dialect.

On December 11, 1998, in the afternoon, Gu Keli, who had just finished coal mining, suddenly received a call from his neighbor, saying that his young public had been kidnapped and his son had been taken away.

After taking Gu Hongliang away, the Xinmi police also brought his adoptive parents, Wang Shuqing and Liu Yuchaoxia, as well as their rural relatives to Xinmi. They were unable to sever the nearly 12-year emotional bond. Gu Hongming was also reluctant to part with his adoptive parents, tightly hugging Liu Yuchaoxia.

On April 17, another criminal, Liang Yingqiong, was also arrested in Shaanxi.

After returning to Bazi, Gu Keli saw his terrified wife and 4-year-old son. The neighbor said that at around 8 a.m., when he passed by, he heard a "wailing" sound outside the house. When he came out to take a look, he saw that Gu Keli's son and wife had both been tied up, and the son, Gu Hongliang, was nowhere to be seen.

To find his son, Gu Keli ran through 23 provinces, cities, and regions across the country, including Xinjiang, Sichuan, and Guizhou.

After spending more than 30,000 yuan, he borrowed another 40,000 yuan. After using up the loan money, he sold his family's house for 30,000 yuan.

Through investigation at a local coal mine, the Sichuanese workers Liang Yingfu, Dong Guoxue, Liang Yingcheng, etc., came into the police's line of sight, but these people had vanished without a trace, and their real addresses and identities could not be verified.

In 2006, Gu Keli encountered a car accident while looking for his son in Guizhou, resulting in a comminuted fracture of the left hip.

On March 15, 2010, Captain Su Hongpeng of the third responsibility zone criminal investigation team of the Xinmi Municipal Public Security Bureau led the police back to Dong Guoxue's hometown in Sichuan for investigation and learned that Dong Guoxue was still working in a coal mine in Xinmi. On the evening of March 19, Dong Guoxue was captured at a coal mine in Chaohua Town.

After intense interrogation, Dong Guoxue confessed: he and Xiao Cheng Liang Yingbai, Li Dayun, Liang Yingqiong, etc., abducted Gu Hongliang. Subsequently, they sold the child to Bohou City, Henan Province for 7,000 yuan.

On October 30, 2009, the police uncovered a clue during their investigation: Dong Guoxue once rented a house in Qian Mou's home in Bo Ji Zhang Village during his farming period in Ping Mo Town. Through photo identification, Qian Mou confirmed that the person who had lived in his house was indeed Dong Guoxue.

Dong Shuying said that the child was adopted by Wang Shuqing and Liu Yuchaoxia. The police from Henan and Hebei quickly went to Wang Shuqing's home and took the girl to the Bohou City Public Security Bureau. At that time, Gu Hongming had changed his name to Wang Ganggang and was 13 years old.

Three criminals are still at large in Xinmi.
