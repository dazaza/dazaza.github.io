Three-day culinary cultural theme tour in Tianjin

by afkqxoyh on 2010-05-26 18:10:15

Preparations:

Book tickets for the celebrity teahouse crosstalk performance, first-class tickets, 30 yuan per person. Phone: 022-27116382

Book a room at Home Inn Music Academy Store (Daguangming Bridge Store, Shiyijing Road Store)

Prepare loose change. Most Tianjin buses cost 1.5 yuan per person, so prepare plenty of loose change.

Day 1:

1. Depart at 5:30 am, take bus 916 to Dongzhimen.

2. Arrive at Dongzhimen at 7:00 am, take Line 2 and transfer to Line 5, get off at Puhuangyu Station, then take a taxi to Beijing South Railway Station.

3. Buy intercity high-speed train tickets, departure at 9:00 am.

4. Arrive in Tianjin at 9:30 am, buy return train tickets and a Tianjin map (don't buy inside the station, go outside and buy), then take a taxi to Home Inn Music Academy Store (Daguangming Bridge Store, Shiyijing Road Store), about 10 yuan. Settle down and rest for a while.

5. Depart at 10:00 am, take bus 651 from Daguangming Bridge Station (opposite RT-Mart supermarket) to Gulou Station, visit Gulou for free.

6. At 11:30 am, walk eastward for one kilometer to Ancient Culture Street, where there are small restaurants offering cheap and practical lunch options, visit Ancient Culture Street, Heavenly King Temple, Ni Ren Zhang, Benge Zhang, Guoren Zhang, enjoy local snacks, and buy small souvenirs.

7. At 15:00 pm, leave the street, take a taxi to Chifeng Road to visit Porcelain House (this house is built with porcelain), admission fee 20 yuan. At 16:00 pm, collect tickets at the Mingliu Teahouse located at the crossroads of Harbin Road and Xinhua Road, listen to crosstalk for two and a half hours.

8. At 18:30 pm, eat potato vermicelli or potato knife-cut noodles mixed at Jiede Restaurant on Harbin Road, potato vermicelli costs 5 yuan, or mixed potato knife-cut noodles cost 7 yuan, soda costs 1 yuan per bottle.

9. At 19:00 pm, take a taxi to Xiaowa Fang Honey Bar, have a drink (cost depends on what alcohol you choose, generally half a case of beer is enough). Taxi fare does not exceed 20 yuan.

10. At 22:00 pm, return early to take a shower and sleep. Tomorrow will be tiring. A taxi fare of 8 yuan is sufficient.

Day 2:

1. Get up at 8:00 am, leave Home Inn, walk south and turn west at the first intersection for 50 meters, there is a Kun Cheng Snack Shop (opposite old Tianjin fried sauce noodles), have breakfast, recommended food: tofu pudding, egg cloud dumplings, Gabacai, fried dough sticks, box-packed eggs (note, it's "box" not "egg", it's box-packed eggs). Buy freshly fried fried dough sticks and box-packed eggs from the small stall next to Kun Cheng, don't buy fried dough sticks inside Kun Cheng.

2. Depart at 9:00 am, buy a small bottle of liquor at the supermarket near the entrance, remember not to open it, otherwise it won't pass security checks. Wait for the double-decker bus No. 643 at Daguangming Bridge Station next to Home Inn, get off at Zhongshanmen Station, take the light rail to Tanggu Station, 5 yuan per person. Follow the crowd after getting off, visit the foreign goods market, which is not very interesting, like an enlarged version of Tianyi Market. But it's quite famous, so visit anyway.

3. At 11:30 am, take bus No. 103 from the Foreign Goods Market Station to Beitan Station, cross the road to buy seafood (note to walk further inside, there is a huge market in a small alley, don't assume there are only a couple of shops by the roadside, walk more). After buying seafood, process it in a roadside shop, drink some white wine, it's refreshing and sterilizing.

4. At 14:00 pm, nearby is the terminal station of bus No. 103, take the original route back to the Foreign Goods Market. If you wish, it's better to take the light rail from the first stop of the Foreign Goods Market, TEDA Station, where you can find seats, as the Foreign Goods Market is extremely crowded and you won't find any seats. If you don't want to or don't care about standing, just take the subway back from the Foreign Goods Market.

5. Return to Tianjin at 16:00 pm, take the same bus you came with, go to the Kun Cheng Restaurant behind the hotel to eat baba fish for 24 yuan and hotpot tofu, baba fish is very delicious and strong, generally two people should not order too much, baba fish is very big.

6. At 18:00 pm, finish eating and stroll around the supermarket near the hotel entrance, buy necessary items. If not tired, you can take a milk bath or foot massage. You can also go to Haihe River near Liberation Bridge to see the night view.

Day 3:

1. Get up at 8:00 am, check out, if your luggage is heavy, you can store it at the hotel and come back to pick it up when leaving in the afternoon. Breakfast is the same as above.

2. Take the double-decker bus No. 643 to Nankai University, admire the prestigious university, remember to enter from the East Gate and exit from the South Gate, where there are statues.

3. At 10:30 am, take a taxi to Quanyechang Pedestrian Street, no more than 20 yuan. Go shopping, women will be happy.

4. At noon 12:00 pm, at the hutong opposite Jiangbin Building in Quanyechang Pedestrian Street, there is the main store of Goubuli. It's quite expensive. It's recommended to try it. Buy a set meal on the first floor, never listen to the server to go upstairs, upstairs is for out-of-town people, very overpriced, a beef bun costs 9 yuan, note, it's correct, one bun costs 9 yuan. A pork bun costs 36 yuan for eight pieces. One bun isn't even as big as my eyeball. The set meal on the first floor costs more than 20 yuan, just have a taste.

5. After finishing the meal, continue shopping until your feet blister, then take a taxi to Xianjiao Corner. It costs about 10 yuan. Experience the Tianjin subway, fewer people than in Beijing, quite advanced. Subway ticket costs 3 yuan per person.

6. Exit at Xiawafang Station, take Exit D, across the road is Jinmen Pancake with a red signboard, 5 yuan per set, two pancakes and two eggs, experience what real pancake with filling is like. While eating the pancake, walk north for about 300 meters, there is a Xiaoli Roast Chicken, buy two, pay attention to the server's calculation and the scale. Servers there often miscalculate, and each time they make a mistake, they charge more. After buying roast chicken, do a major task, take a taxi from Xiaoli Roast Chicken to Guilifang Sesame Twist Shop at Limin Road Intersection to buy the famous Eighteen Streets Big Sesame Twists, note that only this one is genuine, all others are fake. Ask the driver to wait outside, after buying sesame twists, directly get back in the car and go back to Home Inn to pick up your luggage, let the driver wait, pick up the luggage and head straight to the train station to end your Tianjin trip. Car fare between 20~30 yuan. Note, Tianjin taxis charge for waiting time, how much depends on how fast you act.

Notes:

1. Taxi drivers are very interesting, you can chat more, but most of them will say your choices are not good, the scenic spots are not fun, Tianjin daily rental network, but traveling is about going to places you haven't been to, they live in Tianjin all the time, so they naturally think it's not good. Don't believe these, we don't always go to Mutianyu either.

2. If you don't know the way, ask whenever necessary, Tianjin people are like Zhang Damin, very funny and very热情, especially easy-going.

3. Wudadao and Italian Style Street are optional scenic spots, actually there's really nothing much to see, but taking photos there looks pretty good. If you really want to visit, it might be disappointing. If you really want to see, it's suggested to take a taxi to the railway station and detour around there, it's a bit far, but spending an extra 10 or 20 yuan to visit two scenic spots is actually worth it.
