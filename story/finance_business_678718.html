There are so many stars at Kyung Hee University, but what about Central University? Can you give us an overview?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Famous actors like Hyun Bin, Kim Hee-sun, and Zhang Nara graduated from the Theater Department of Korea's Central University.

Central University, renowned for its cultural and sports programs, was founded in 1918 and is one of Korea’s most prestigious institutions with a long history and tradition. It has campuses in Seoul and Anseong. With nearly 30,000 students and faculty members and over 180,000 graduates, it currently offers 14 graduate schools, 18 undergraduate majors, and 72 subjects. The university has established friendly sister relationships with prestigious Chinese universities such as Tsinghua University, Fudan University, Renmin University, Liaoning University, and Yanbian University.

The campuses are divided into the Tongjeong District in Seoul's southern metropolitan sub-center and the second campus in Anseong, Gyeonggi Province. Graduates from Korea who choose to study abroad find that Central University offers 18 undergraduate majors and 14 graduate schools. Central University has 30,000 undergraduates, 4,700 master's and doctoral students, 700 professors, and 500 staff members. Art, journalism, and film performance are traditional strong disciplines of the school. Central University has established a national art education system with its own art museum, digital resource center, large multimedia center, and government-funded design management center. Its ultimate goal is to rank among the top 100 universities worldwide by 2018.

To enhance competition among Korean universities, the Korean Ministry of Education and media agencies conduct an annual comprehensive evaluation of each university. Central University has been rated as the best university for six consecutive years. In the evaluation conducted by the Korea JoongAng Daily of 204 Korean universities, Central University ranked first in theater, film, and photography departments, while the pharmacy school ranked fourth.

Can you talk specifically about the famous Film Department at Central University?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Both the Drama and Film Departments rank first nationwide. Lee Young-ae (the actress who played Dae Jang Geum) and Kim Hee-sun (from the Film Academy) both studied at Central University.

Main course content: The Film Department provides a rich method of studying films combining theory with practice. From historical and social perspectives, it explores and researches contemporary plays, television, novels, documentaries, advertisements, and experimental films.

Based on the belief of mastering professional background and technical skills, it combines historical and critical studies applied to film, video, and multimedia. As one of the best film academies in Korea, this major cultivates talented film experts who can develop films as both an art form and a vital element of the 21st century.

Foundation courses include: Film Analysis, Film History, Film Basics

Elective courses include: Korean Film History, Basic Film Production, Origins and Development

What does the Drama Department cover?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Committed to performing arts based on knowledge and artistic skills and talents, the Drama Department combines history, theory, drama criticism, and practical courses. This departmental drama education provides a perfect foundation for combining theory with practice. As an independent drama education center with proud achievements, a long history, and advanced functions, our department provides students with extensive knowledge and theoretical foundations, enabling them to excel in employment in stage design, performance, direction, technical production, and playwriting both domestically and internationally.

What are the foundation and elective courses?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: Foundation courses include: Acting Basics, Introduction to Dramatic Arts, Drama Analysis.

Elective courses include: Ancient Western Drama History, Acting Methods, Drama Practice Courses, Advanced Acting, Ancient Drama History.

Are there any successful application cases?

Korean Study Abroad Expert: A student named Guo took a train from Datong, Shanxi to Qingdao for consultation. Initially, he expressed unfamiliarity with studying in Korea, but after observation, I found him to be a very diligent student. He had already communicated with Central University officials during the autumn international education exhibition. His study abroad purpose and goals were very clear. However, due to his less-than-ideal college entrance exam scores, applying to Pusan National University, ranked tenth in Korea, posed significant challenges. Despite this, the student and his parents insisted on applying only to this university, so I devised a detailed study abroad plan based on his situation.

Given Guo's excellent family background, we showcased substantial financial strength in terms of deposits, which greatly benefited the visa application process. During the university's interview process in China, we provided the student with detailed interview training tailored to his situation, especially regarding sensitive issues like unsatisfactory college entrance exam results. This helped the student pass the interview smoothly, laying the foundation for the successful application to the university.

Through this case, we can see that study abroad regulations are not rigid. By carefully analyzing and understanding the essence of the rules, we can successfully obtain admission letters for students.
