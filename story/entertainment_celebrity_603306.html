At 10:30 am on December 3, Gan Lulu (Weibo), wearing a black deep V-neck mini skirt and bright red high heels, appeared at the scene of the Fourth China-ASEAN Auto Show. The exhibition center immediately sparked a climax as journalists and photography enthusiasts scrambled to take pictures. The organizers had to deploy dozens of security guards to maintain order at the scene. Accompanying her was Gan Lulu's mother, also dressed fashionably, holding a compact camera, taking pictures while walking, smiling all the way.

The Time and Space Network specially invited Gan Lulu and Mother Lulu to the Time and Space Network booth for an exclusive interview. (Click on the picture to enter)

At the interview site, the Time and Space editor presented a pair of Time and Space Network mascots Qiele Niu to Gan Lulu. Belonging to the zodiac sign of Ox, she exclaimed how cute and much she loved them. "You can't make flowers bloom when you try hard, but sometimes things happen unexpectedly."

Time and Space Editor: What made everyone instantly recognize you should be that marriage video on February 14 this year. Can you talk to the Time and Space netizens about how you originally thought of shooting this video?

Gan Lulu: Actually, I've shot many film and television works before. But sometimes when chatting with my mom, she would tell me: Lulu, why are you so sad? You're only this popular because of the bathroom marriage gate. I think making this video is like trying hard to make flowers bloom but they don't, yet something unexpected happens. Shooting videos for marriage has already happened in foreign countries, but it didn't cause much of a stir. I'm glad the marriage video has caught everyone's attention.

Unimpressed by being called the sexy Dragon Maiden and unwilling to compare with Yang Mi

Time and Space Editor: Among so many sexy photo shoots and artistic photos, which set do you like the most?

Gan Lulu: I like the recent Golden Phoenix Hot Spring set more. Therefore, many netizens call me the sexy Dragon Maiden. Although I don't understand why netizens want to call me that. Because of the title of the sexy Dragon Maiden, netizens compare me with Yang Mi, saying I look like Yang Mi. Why can't Yang Mi look like me? Is Yang Mi as sexy as me?
