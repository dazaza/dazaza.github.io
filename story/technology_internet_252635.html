20 Effective Prevention and Treatment Tips for Breast Cancer

Examinations Can Save Lives

1) Clinical breast examinations and mammograms are equally important, especially for inflammatory breast cancer.

2) Regular self-examinations are very important.

3) Don't worry if you're asked to recheck; breast calcification deposits are normal.

A Balanced Diet and Exercise Can Reduce the Risk of Breast Cancer

4) Regular exercise can reduce the incidence of breast cancer.

5) Regularly consume green plants, especially those rich in folic acid or lutein.

6) Post-menopause, obesity can increase the incidence of breast cancer.

7) Consume fewer soy products or legume foods. Also, eat less meat containing higher levels of hormones, such as chicken and pork.

What Normal Breasts Should Look Like

8) Lumpy breasts do not necessarily mean a higher risk of cancer, especially within the menstrual cycle.

9) Pain is usually not a sign of breast cancer.

10) Women with denser breast tissue have four times the incidence of breast cancer compared to others. When breast tissue density is high, cell growth accelerates. Asian women tend to have denser breast tissue, so they need to be especially careful.

Breast Cancer Incidence Rates

11) The incidence rate of breast cancer is not as high as reported (one in eight), and this number needs to be well understood.

12) Even if there's a family history of cancer, it does not necessarily mean an increased probability of breast cancer.

13) If breast cancer is detected early, the cure rate in the U.S. reaches 90%.

14) Many factors influence the incidence of breast cancer.

15) If your father or brother has had colon cancer, the incidence of breast cancer increases because these two diseases are genetically linked.

High-Risk Group for Breast Cancer

16) Regular MRI or mammogram checks.

17) If there is a BRCA1 or 2 mutation, removing the ovaries can reduce the risk by 50%.

18) Taking drugs similar to Tamoxifen or raloxifene, which inhibit estrogen, can reduce the incidence of breast cancer by 50%.

Breast Cancer Treatment

19) Breast cancer is not just one type, so treatment methods vary.

20) Chemotherapy is not the only treatment method.
