Is the early pregnancy test accurate?

by betty2012 on 2012-02-27 17:19:33

Due to its convenience, simplicity, and ease of operation, the early pregnancy test strip is often used by sexually active women as the most common method for testing pregnancy. However, many women often doubt the accuracy of these test strips. So, how accurate are early pregnancy test strips? Experts in gynecology from Xi'an Abortion Hospital provide a detailed introduction. These experts tell us that, although early pregnancy test strips claim to have a 99% accuracy rate, one should not overly trust their self-test results. Especially when women conduct pregnancy self-tests at home without any external guidance, the accuracy rate is generally only between 50% and 75%. If tested under the guidance of professional doctors in a hospital, the accuracy rate can approach 99%. When using early pregnancy test strips, women must strictly follow the instructions to ensure reliable and accurate test results. However, correctly following the instructions during actual operation is far from easy.

Below, the experts will explain the specific testing steps:

1. Pay attention to the production date on the packaging box, and do not use expired test cards because the chemical reagents may lose efficacy over time.

2. To reduce the probability of inaccurate tests, carefully read the instructions for the test card before going to the bathroom to perform the test, and then carefully follow the instructions.

3. If you are unsure about the test result, it's best to call a doctor for advice and complete the test under their guidance. For example, if you drink too much water and dilute your urine, the doctor will tell you whether you need to retake the test. If the test result is positive but not very clear, you should assume you're pregnant and go to the hospital for a check-up.

4. If the self-test result is negative, and your period has not arrived after one week, you should take another self-test.

5. Most importantly, trust your body. If your symptoms indicate pregnancy regardless of the self-test result, consider that the self-test might be wrong and it’s best to go to the hospital for a check-up. Common early pregnancy symptoms include nausea, vomiting, fatigue, frequent urination, breast swelling, cravings, and drowsiness. The experts from Xi'an Abortion Hospital suggest that women who don't plan to get pregnant soon should take proper contraception measures. If an unintended pregnancy is discovered, it's important to visit a professional gynecological hospital to choose an appropriate abortion method.

3. Endoscopic visible painless abortion surgery is performed under ultrasound monitoring, avoiding harm and aftereffects caused by excessive scraping of the uterus.

The above is a detailed introduction about the accuracy of early pregnancy test strips. Hopefully, women now have a better understanding. If menstruation is delayed and the early pregnancy test shows a positive result, it is crucial for women to promptly visit a professional and regular hospital for an early pregnancy examination. For those who experience unintended pregnancies, early detection of pregnancy helps in timely termination of the unintended pregnancy. Xi'an Sanqin Hospital wishes you good health!
