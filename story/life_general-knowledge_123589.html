Shijiazhuang Orient Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:46:18

Integration of work and study, part-time work for financial aid, the best practical training facilities in the province, simple, fast and accurate teaching model, full enclosed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest charge in the whole province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihe Road South, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road. It has superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development concept of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, thinking hard and seeking change", continuously carried out teaching and management reforms, firmly established the awareness of "market competition", taken the market as the guide, and taken the enterprise demand as the standard. We explored new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulated rich teaching and management experience, and after employment, students received widespread praise from employers.

First, taking enterprise standards as the guidance, we have equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Sufficient and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the basis and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, and built a numerical control training workshop equipped with more than 50 CNC lathes, CNC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines and other equipment, as well as an ordinary lathe training workshop. The school has also built communication electronic laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, shape rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

Second, taking employment positions as the guidance, we have created an enterprise-style teaching model.

Establishing a teaching model mainly based on practice, in the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job postings, students hold positions such as team leaders, line leaders, and employees in their "work". And through regular evaluations and exam results, outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day's "work" situation of the students has quality monitoring indicators, which are statistically analyzed monthly. According to the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, composed of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonus, etc. This effectively motivates students' initiative to learn and competitive awareness, allowing them to better and faster master the knowledge and skills they have learned.

Through enterprise-style teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to the enterprise management model during their school years, meet the needs of employment positions, and initially reach the enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-style teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

Third, taking enterprise demands as the guidance, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students and formulated a daily norm for students and a practical manual for vocational quality training.

Fully learning from the enterprise administrator employee model, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises welcome. Enterprises not only need skilled workers with solid technical skills but also pay more attention to the comprehensive qualities of employees, such as team spirit, obedience, execution ability, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of comprehensive qualities require the efforts and cooperation of all aspects of the school, and comprehensive quality education runs through the entire learning process of students. To enhance the comprehensive quality of our students, our school mainly highlights two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," and hires retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, starting from dressing, behavior, etiquette, and tidying up the quarters, students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined talents with good military temperament.

Distinctive Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

In the learning process of students, we offer vocational quality courses, which are divided into theoretical teaching and extension practice, complementing and enhancing each other. The theoretical teaching of vocational quality mainly focuses on practical combat, guiding students to transform their thoughts, while extension training adopts the method of "personal experience" to consolidate teaching results, thereby making these vocational quality requirements become the spontaneous behavior of students.

For vocational quality extension training, our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends batches of our students to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before actual internship employment, enter the formal enterprise employee state in advance, and lay a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, taking employment as the guidance, we have successfully completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's numerical control major is mainly automobile parts manufacturing. All student internship units are mechanical manufacturing enterprises ranked in the world's top 500. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the employment satisfaction rate of students exceeds 95%.

Fifth, considerate后勤services, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for students in the province.

All students of our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the needs of students' physical development, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adaptation to intensive study, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students eating outside the school.

The school has complete service facilities such as student dormitory buildings, canteens, supermarkets, phone booths, and bathrooms. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service" and strives to make services proactive, thoughtful, intimate, and convenient.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years for students.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from and returning to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-looking educational concepts, we aim to save costs for parents as much as possible. Students coming to our school only need to pay three years of miscellaneous fees, and the three years of tuition and accommodation fees are paid through the integration of work and study, i.e., vocational quality training. The school has also specifically established a "Hongzhi Class" to fully or partially exempt all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly comprehensive quality students from poor families. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

Our mission (achieving mission): Laying the foundation for corporate wealth.

The distillation of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and direction of struggle for our school. The practice of school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.

Small victories rely on intelligence, great victories rely on virtue; small success relies on friends, great success relies on opponents.
