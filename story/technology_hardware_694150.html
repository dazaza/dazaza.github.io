In the eyes of the elderly couple, although He Hua was a little introverted, she did not seem like an impulsive or heartless person. When they saw what was in front of them, he and his son were shocked. They immediately shook Yunyun, but there was no response, though her body still had some warmth. He quickly picked up the child and ran to the hospital in the town. In the end, the doctor told him that the child had already died.

3 Neighbors and Family Members - No One Witnessed the Incident

Regarding this matter, the elderly couple felt a bit angry, but also found it strange. The father said that there were two daughters in the family, He Hua being the younger daughter, who was very introverted and didn't like talking from a young age. She dropped out of school after finishing the second grade of elementary school and at 19, married a man in Shaanxi through an introduction. Later, due to her husband's poor treatment of her, she divorced and returned to Sichuan. Losing her granddaughter, the grandfather was deeply saddened.

How could Yunyun suddenly be bound and die at home? Jin Shunming and his son returned home with their granddaughter and saw He Hua still watching TV. "Was the child bound by you?" Jin Shunming thought that since there was no one else in the house at the time and no strangers had been in the house, and since the child was fine when he left in the morning after eating a steamed bun, the person most likely to have bound the granddaughter would be He Hua.

Currently, the local police station in Dujiangyan is investigating the matter.

Subsequently, from Jin Shunming's eldest daughter, Jin Lin, we learned that He Hua was usually a quiet person who didn't greet people when she saw them, showing a very introverted personality. Once, she saw her younger brother Jin Qiang arguing with He Hua, and He Hua impulsively scratched her younger brother's face. She also heard that the couple often argued.

2 Grandfather: Daughter-in-law Admitted to Tying Up the Child and Covering Her with a Quilt

Yesterday afternoon, Yunyun's grandfather, Jin Shunming, recounted the incident. At around 8 o'clock in the morning, he went to Daguan Town for errands and returned home at around 11 o'clock to cook lunch. At that time, his daughter-in-law He Hua and the child were in the bedroom, his son Jin Qiang was working outside, and he was busy cooking in the kitchen. Unexpectedly, the power went out, so he decided to go to the town to buy something to eat.

The reporter noticed that this neighborhood was newly built housing for resettlement after the earthquake. Jin Shunming's house was on the first floor, and next to the iron railing was a long grass rope left by Jin Qiang to tie his wife.

Facing questioning from the family, He Hua remained silent. In anger, Jin Qiang tied He Hua to the neighbor's iron railing and loudly asked her what happened, but she still didn't say anything. "Later, she admitted that she tied up Yunyun and then covered her with a quilt." Jin Shunming said that later his daughter-in-law admitted it, but never explained why she tied up her own daughter.

Yunyun's family suspected that it was her biological mother, He Hua, who tied up the child and covered her with a quilt, causing the child to suffocate and die, pointing out that He Hua personally admitted to this matter.

She Was Introverted But Not Heartless

Yesterday, the reporter tried to understand the situation from the Qingcheng Mountain Police Station, but they stated that the case was under investigation and it was inconvenient to disclose any information.

Jin Shunming still remembered the situation at the time: two quilts were tightly covering the bed. When they were lifted, they saw Yunyun lying on the bed, bound from head to toe with ropes. One leg was bent in a struggling posture, her whole body had turned black, and there were wounds near her forehead.

At around 12 o'clock in the afternoon, when he returned home, he saw He Hua watching TV. Soon after, Jin Qiang also came back. "He couldn't find his granddaughter anywhere and realized something had happened," Jin Shunming said. Jin Qiang shouted for his daughter everywhere, but there was no response. When he questioned He Hua, she didn't answer either. Finally, they found Yunyun on the bed in the bedroom.

Yesterday, the reporter met He Hua's parents in Shuangle Village, Daguan Town. Both elderly people were over 70 years old.

Ms. Wang, who lived nearby, said that at the time of the incident, they didn't hear any sounds, and no one witnessed the entire process. All they knew was what others had told them later. Jin Shunming also said that no one saw Yunyun being suffocated, nor did anyone else witness it.

In 2007, He Hua married Jin Qiang.

Subsequently, the local police officers arrived at the scene and took He Hua away for investigation.

Jin Shunming suspected that because Yunyun was not obedient, He Hua first tied her up with a rope and then covered her with a quilt to prevent her from making noise, resulting in the child suffocating to death.

1 Lifting the Quilt Revealed the Fully Bound Granddaughter Lying on the Bed

At around 12 o'clock yesterday noon, inside the Xinhe New Residential Area in Daguan Town, Dujiangyan, the 2-year-old girl Yunyun was found dead on her own bed, fully bound with ropes and covered from head to toe with a quilt.
