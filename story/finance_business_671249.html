UTC Experts to Appear at Beijing ISPO 2012

From February 22 to 25, the 8th edition of the ISPO BEIJING Asia Outdoor and Fashion Exhibition will be held at the National Convention Center in Beijing. As a global benchmark for the development of the outdoor industry, the ISPO exhibition has always been a hub for new trends, ideas, and brands in the outdoor sector. This year's event has attracted participation from over 400 brands both domestically and internationally. UTC Experts, as a professional travel equipment operator, will also make an appearance with its agency brands: Eagle Creek, Timbuk2, Wenger, and ExOfficio.

UTC Experts has consistently focused on introducing professional, high-quality international travel equipment brands, aiming to create China's most distinctive travel concept platform, providing practical travel equipment solutions for travelers. It is understood that the four products featured by UTC Experts this time are all classic brands that are rare gems, sure to bring pleasant surprises to outdoor enthusiasts.

As the top American outdoor travel brand, Eagle Creek (Yike) currently boasts the most comprehensive product line, ranging from large suitcases, travel bags, hiking packs, specialized packing organizers for various clothing items during trips, to travel accessories like neck pillows, eye masks, waist packs, and various lightweight daily-use bags, fully meeting travelers' all-around travel needs. Notably, Eagle Creek (Yike) products use premium materials and advanced craftsmanship, ensuring superior quality. Their commitment to "Global Warranty, Lifetime Repair" without conditions is a promise made to travelers. It is understood that at this ISPO exhibition, Eagle Creek (Yike) will offer outdoor enthusiasts a brand-new experience, including exploration series suitcases made entirely of eco-friendly materials and the latest Pack-it Specter packing organizers crafted from ultra-light, transparent, waterproof, and easy-to-clean fabrics.

If Eagle Creek is the "darling" of outdoor enthusiasts, then Timbuk2 is also the perfect partner for cycling lovers. Timbuk2 originated in the United States and holds a leading position in the American messenger bag market. As the pioneer of messenger bags, Timbuk2 consistently adheres to the design philosophy of "attractive design, powerful functionality, never out of style," using the best materials and finest craftsmanship to produce bags that can last a lifetime. According to the UTC Experts exhibition responsible person, this marks the first appearance of the Timbuk2 brand at a Chinese exhibition. For this occasion, UTC Experts has carefully selected multiple series of products, including Timbuk2 classic and luxury messenger bags, bicycle bags, and DSLR camera bags.

A century of Swiss heritage, continuing classics. It is understood that the Swiss centenary classic brand Wenger (Wei Ge) will also make an appearance at this ISPO exhibition. Several high-quality, multi-functional Wenger backpacks will be showcased, including dedicated bicycle backpacks and urban white-collar tailored light casual travel backpacks. In addition to multi-functionality, Wenger backpacks emphasize the design of the carrying system, providing users with perfect body fit and maximizing pressure distribution.

Moreover, UTC Experts brings a "new friend" to the exhibition - the leading American clothing brand ExOfficio. This marks the first entry of the ExOfficio brand into the Chinese market. At that time, UTC Experts will present their quick-dry underwear series. As one of the world's two major quick-dry underwear brands, ExOfficio features fast-drying, odor prevention, comfort, and is of extremely high quality.

On February 22, over 400 brands from home and abroad will gather at the ISPO BEIJING 2012 exhibition site, showcasing their latest products and technologies, displaying the latest fashion trends in the integration of China's outdoor sports industry and fashion. For those who love outdoor activities and pursue a fashionable lifestyle, this will be a must-attend event.
