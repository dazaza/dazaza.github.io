Reasonable nutrition promotes physical health.

by longersing on 2009-05-18 23:36:20

Gu Baiyou Life - A reasonable nutritional combination promotes physical health. There are dozens of types of food we consume daily, and each type of food contains different nutrients. If a few foods with different nutritional components are cleverly combined in the diet, not only can the nutrients be absorbed well, but the side effects can also be reduced, making the body healthier. Just as I mentioned before about Gu Baiyou's seasonal vegetable consumption, there are also rules for our regular meat dishes.

Pork + Garlic: Increase vitamin absorption

"Eating meat without garlic reduces nutrition by half." The content of vitamin B1 in pork is on average 9 times higher than that of other meats, but this vitamin is unstable and stays in the body for a short time. If you eat garlic at the same time, the allicin in garlic combines with vitamin B1, changing its water solubility to fat solubility, which greatly increases the body's absorption and utilization.

Mutton + Onion: Lower cholesterol

The onion is currently known as the only vegetable containing prostaglandin A. Prostaglandin A expands blood vessels, thereby helping to lower blood pressure. Mutton stir-fried with onions is popular; adding some onions reaches an upgraded nutritional combination. Because mutton has high protein and saturated fat content, eating too much mutton during dry autumn and winter seasons has the risk of increasing cholesterol levels. Adding some onions when cooking mutton can prevent excessive absorption of cholesterol and fat from mutton.

Eggs + Bread: Improve protein utilization

Eggs are rich in comprehensive nutrition. If eggs are eaten together with bread (carbohydrates), it can improve the utilization of proteins. Some people only eat an egg and drink a cup of milk for breakfast, causing the protein in the egg to be wasted. If eaten with bread or steamed buns, the protein can be maximally absorbed by the human body. Eggs are best steamed or boiled. When eating boiled eggs, they should be cooked tenderly, meaning boiling them for five to six minutes after the water boils. At this point, the yolk just solidifies, and the absorption rate of protein by the human body is the highest. But fried eggs are best avoided because frying eggs until yellow or burnt causes protein denaturation.

Fish + Tofu: Enhance calcium absorption

Both fish and tofu are high-protein foods, but the proteins and amino acids they contain are not reasonably composed. For example, tofu protein lacks methionine and lysine, while fish meat protein lacks phenylalanine. Nutritionists call these incomplete proteins. If two foods are eaten together, they can complement each other, making the composition of proteins more reasonable. For example, tofu stewed with fish head is not only delicious but also the vitamin D in the fish head enhances the absorption and utilization rate of calcium in tofu. It is worth noting that old tofu is better when making fish stewed with tofu, and adding some vegetables and mushrooms makes both the flavor and nutrition excellent.

Black and White Fungus Combination: Nourish Yin and Moisturize Lungs

White fungus is usually made into sweet soup for consumption, while black fungus can be used to make dishes or as a garnish. From a dietary therapy perspective, both black and white fungus have the effect of preventing lipofuscin formation, belonging to longevity-enhancing and anti-aging foods. At the same time, both types of fungus contain polysaccharides, which enhance immunity and have antiviral effects. However, due to differences in nutrient content, their effects on the human body after consumption still vary.

Fungus is rich in vitamin D, which prevents calcium loss and is very beneficial for growth and development. The phospholipids it contains also have brain-nourishing and calming effects. In traditional Chinese medicine, white fungus has the effects of moistening the lungs, generating body fluids, nourishing qi and blood, and benefiting the kidneys, suitable for people with weaker respiratory systems. Black fungus contains the most iron among various vegetarian foods, and eating it regularly can nourish the blood and maintain youthful looks, and prevent iron-deficiency anemia.

In addition, the gelatin contained in fungus has extremely strong adsorption capabilities, reducing dust damage to the lungs. Black fungus also contains a nucleic acid-like substance that can lower cholesterol levels in the blood, benefiting patients with coronary heart disease and arteriosclerosis. If black and white fungus are eaten together, the nutrients they contain will complement each other, having the effects of nourishing yin, moisturizing the lungs, tonifying the kidneys, and enhancing brain function.

Corn + Peas: Protein Complementarity

Corn contains carotene, which, after being absorbed by the human body, can be converted into vitamin A, having a cancer-preventing effect. Peas have the effect of harmonizing the spleen and stomach, promoting intestinal health and diuresis, and are also rich in carotene and vitamin C, making the skin soft and smooth. The coarse fiber in peas can also prevent colorectal cancer. Corn and peas combined can increase the human body's utilization value of protein. Since the amino acids that make up the proteins in the two are different, mixed consumption allows for protein complementarity, thus enhancing the nutritional value of the food.

Seafood + Vegetables: Neutralizing Purines

Seafood is high-purine and highly acidic food. Consuming too much can cause metabolic disorders, increase uric acid concentration in the blood, and trigger gout. If you eat more vegetables when eating seafood, as alkaline foods, vegetables can neutralize uric acid salt concentrations, facilitating uric acid excretion and greatly reducing the risk of developing gout.

Regularly eating healthy foods from Gu Baiyou can help us become stronger.
