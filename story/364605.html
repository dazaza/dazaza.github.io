Traffic Tips:

There are many ways to get to Xinduqiao. Here are some suggestions from U&V:

Tip one: Drive yourself. The road conditions from Chengdu to Kangding are good, just pay attention to the turns. The road conditions to Xinduqiao are average, so drive slowly.

Tip two: Take a bus from the Xinanmen Tourist Bus Terminal in Chengdu to Kangding County Town (buses roll out every hour before 2 PM daily), then hire a car to Xinduqiao.

Chengdu to Kangding: 5~6 hours, ticket price 120 yuan/person (very good road conditions); Kangding to Xinduqiao 2~4 hours, 50 yuan/person (average road conditions).

Tip three: There is a direct bus from the Xinanmen Tourist Bus Terminal in the center of Chengdu to Ya'an (the next station after Xinduqiao Town), which departs only once a day at 8:30 AM, ticket price 170 yuan, journey time 8~10 hours.

Tip four: Direct flights from Chengdu to Kangding Zheduo Mountain Airport have been opened, ticket price 600 yuan/person. After arriving at the mountain airport, it's only 40 kilometers to Xinduqiao, but the road conditions are average.
