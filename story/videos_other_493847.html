by vlocko07 on 2011-09-23 11:33:46

First of all, you need to respond to the work performance. As the common saying goes, every effort has its reward. When your contract expires, the company will ask if you are willing to renew the contract with the unit urgently. Alternatively, they may already have a renewal contract for you to sign. At this point, you can inquire about the salary and benefits from the company and express your opinions if they are not satisfactory.

Example 1

Application Letter

Dear Leader,

Since graduation, I have been working in XXX, and now it's already halfway through the XXXth year. After careful consideration, I am making a request for a raise, and the reasons are as follows:

1. Currently, my responsibilities include: sales follow-up assistant, external auxiliary material operator, and helping with office clerical work. As an assistant follow-up person, I go wherever needed. Once one task is finished, I move on to another. This multitude of tasks makes my current work situation such that: when the salespeople have free time, I don't, and when they're busy, I'm even busier.

2. The Hangzhou Office of XXX is one of the larger foreign enterprises in Hangzhou, but my current salary is only XXX (a bit more than XXX after tax), far below the average monthly salary in Hangzhou. Meanwhile, the external orders that could bring me extra income are getting fewer and fewer, and the average monthly commission this year can only reach RMB XXX/month. Even when added together, it is still below the average monthly salary level in Hangzhou.

3. Comparing internally within XXX, either I earn the same salary while handling more job content; or I handle the same amount of work content but receive a relatively lower salary.

Based on the above actual circumstances, I sincerely request that the leadership review and approve my request for a raise.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Example 2

Salary Increase Application Form

Dear Manager,

As our company continues to grow, my personal abilities are also constantly improving and advancing. During this period of shared growth, my colleagues have given me a lot. I am grateful to the company leaders for their cultivation and assistance.

I joined the company in March 2007. First of all, I would like to thank the company leaders for their care and emphasis, providing me with such a good working environment. This enabled me to quickly integrate into the work and get along well with my colleagues. Since joining the XXXX company, I have always invested in work and learning with a happy and full attitude, striving to do my best to make the work as satisfactory as possible, fulfilling my due responsibilities for the company’s growth. Since you are my direct leader, you should understand these things, and you might even obtain evaluations about me from my colleagues.

I believe that as long as there is effort, there will be rewards. Therefore, I am making a request for a raise, hoping that the leadership can give importance to my application.

If the company leadership believes that my current work content and quality have not yet reached the requirements for a raise, I sincerely hope that you can provide valuable opinions or suggestions so that I have a direction and goal to strive for. In enhancing my own abilities, I can also perform better in my work and move towards higher goals.

Also, please rest assured. If the company does not consider my request, I will still approach every task with a positive and responsible attitude, and I will not slack off because of this. This is not my only pursuit.

Sincerely,

Applicant: XXX

April 2008

(Other parts can be modified according to personal needs)
