Al, a modern hereditary aristocrat, was transported to ancient times during an archaeological expedition. She arrived in the era when Hittites unified Mitanni. With its turmoil and chaos, Al was captured by the Mitanni army but was later made their leader due to her modern medical skills and intelligence. Thus, she led the Mitanni forces in battle against King Saru of the Hittites. After Mitanni's defeat, Al was captured by Saru and taken back to the palace. In the palace, Al engaged in intellectual battles with Saru, and despite this, they fell deeply in love. However, Al unexpectedly discovers that her connection with Saru has spanned thousands of lifetimes. As the reincarnation of the Moon Goddess and Saru as the Storm God, will their tragic love story end happily in this era? How will Al choose between her love and her inner hesitations?
