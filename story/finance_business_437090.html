Centipedes have been used as traditional animal-based Chinese medicine for thousands of years. The government has always called for a vigorous capture of centipedes every year, and in some main producing areas of centipedes, the original seasonal capture and seasonal purchase have gradually evolved into cross-seasonal capture and four-season purchase. In all the Chinese herbal medicine markets, the purchase price of each centipede has soared from a few cents to more than 1.5 yuan, with even higher export prices and increasing demand. However, due to the destruction of ecological environment such as indiscriminate deforestation of mountain forests and pollution by pesticides and fertilizers, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, leading to a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, a once-in-a-century opportunity is presenting itself. In the coming years, centipede farming will surely become a shining highlight in special breeding industry, with significant economic and social benefits. International pharmaceutical institutions no longer require Chinese medicine products to be pure products with known structures but can be mixtures with fixed components, stable efficacy, and reliable safety, providing favorable conditions for Chinese medicine to go global. ● Policy Support:

After the 16th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, especially in the 11th Five-Year Plan, the CPC Central Committee attaches great importance to the three rural issues with farmers' income increase at its core. Developing centipede farming industry, entering rural areas, getting close to farmers, not only can earn foreign exchange for the country but also meet the material life needs of the people; therefore, it is the preferred project for adjusting the rural industrial structure, providing farmers with another practical way to increase their income and help them get rid of poverty. At the same time, it opens up a rare path to wealth for laid-off workers and those seeking re-employment.
