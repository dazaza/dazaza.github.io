Jilin's heavy snowfall begins to weaken today - Weather Forecast - News Center - Sina.com

by psyzems5 on 2012-03-08 23:46:52

On the 8th, the whole province will be cloudy. In Tianzhu, Gansu Province, the weather forecast for the next 30 days predicts local areas in Changchun, Jilin, and Yanbian will have light snow showers.

Meteorological experts analyzed that this snowfall process would bring both positive and negative impacts on agriculture, transportation, and daily life in Jilin Province. The positive effects include improving soil and environmental drought conditions, increasing water resources, and reducing fire risks in forests and rural residential areas. However, it has adverse effects on transportation, agricultural production, and agricultural facilities.

On the 7th, the whole province will see partly cloudy skies turning to clear, with most regions experiencing a northwest wind of level 2-3. Light snow is expected in Changchun, Tonghua, Baishan, Yanbian, and the ChiBei District of Changbai Mountain. The highest temperature in Tonghua during the day will be 3℃, while other areas range from -1℃ to 2℃. The lowest temperature at night in Tonghua will be -8℃, and other areas will range between -10℃ to -14℃.

According to China Weather Network, Jilin Province recently experienced significant snowfall, prompting the provincial meteorological bureau to conduct artificial snow enhancement operations. This snowfall brought both advantages and disadvantages to Jilin. It is predicted that the snowfall will begin to weaken today (the 7th).

The Jilin Meteorological Observatory forecasts that snowfall will start to weaken today, followed by a drop in temperature. Specific forecasts are as follows:

It is recommended that municipal departments promptly organize snow removal to improve urban traffic conditions. Urban traffic management departments should timely direct traffic to prevent serious traffic congestion. Grain enterprises and farmers should also promptly remove accumulated snow from open-air grain piles, reinforce and repair damaged greenhouses, dry out snow-soaked grains after the weather clears to prevent future mold, and continue preparations for spring drought prevention.

On the 9th, the whole province will be sunny with occasional clouds.

Under the joint influence of cold and warm air masses, starting from the 5th, Jilin Province experienced snowfall. By 20:00 on the 6th, Changchun, Tonghua, Baishan, Dunhua, and 20 other counties (cities) had heavy snow. The Jilin Meteorological Bureau conducted artificial snow enhancement operations during this snowfall, firing 323 rockets. The average precipitation in the operation area was 7.2 millimeters, and the province's cumulative water increase reached 130 million cubic meters.
