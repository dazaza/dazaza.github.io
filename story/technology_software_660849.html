The entire renovation industry, from its inception to maturity, has only gone through a short span of 15 years. The renovation industry has consistently adapted to meet market demands, aligning with the gradually increasing requirements for decoration services by homeowners. Service companies in the renovation industry have continuously explored better renovation models to serve homeowners. Modern people are increasingly busy, and the division of labor is becoming more detailed, requiring modern decoration companies to take on more service projects. Renovations have entered the "precision renovation era." With 10 years of experience in the decoration industry, I believe that the era of precision renovation is an inevitable trend. Just as we inevitably accepted the semi-package form before, we will also gradually accept the precision renovation model, and be willing to entrust all the work that the decoration company needs to help us complete to them.

Currently, some leading enterprises in the domestic decoration industry are leading the development of this renovation model. I am also constantly exploring the development model of this industry. In the future, it will further develop to include soft furnishings, appliances, and furniture, achieving true "move-in ready" living.
