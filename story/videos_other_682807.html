An old man was hospitalized from the cold after jumping into an icy river to rescue a drowning child.

by wjwf8179 on 2012-02-29 09:35:29

Froze himself sick and hospitalized, declined financial assistance from the family of the person he saved

□ Reporter: Yu Yang

Zhoukou News - Seeing a child fall into a fast-flowing river, 63-year-old Zhang Guisheng from the North City Office of Shenqiu County didn't hesitate due to his age. He jumped into the icy river to rescue a drowning child who couldn’t afford the 600 yuan tuition. In recent days, Zhang Guisheng's act of bravery has been widely praised by the local residents.

At around 15:00 on January 30th, while taking his granddaughter to an art tutoring class, Zhang Guisheng vaguely heard someone calling for help. He ran towards the sound and arrived at the Sha River where he found two children struggling in the water and another one shouting "help" on the shore. Without much thought, Zhang Guisheng took off his coat and jumped into the river. In the river, he grabbed one child with each hand, trying to bring them to the shore. At this moment, the child on the shore reached out to pull Zhang Guisheng, but due to the steep and slippery slope, the child also slipped into the river. Faced with the urgent situation of three drowning children, Zhang Guisheng, who was almost out of strength, quickly shouted for help.

Nearby people who heard the call rushed over, and a young man jumped into the water without hesitation, taking over the children from Zhang Guisheng’s hands. Others formed a protective circle by holding hands and rescued the remaining two drowning children out of danger. Subsequently, Zhang Guisheng was also helped out of the water by enthusiastic bystanders. After getting ashore, everyone immediately removed the wet clothes from the children, dried their bodies, and nearby residents quickly brought quilts and warm clothes from their homes to keep the children warm.

After Zhang Guisheng and the three drowning children were rescued, they were quickly taken to the Shenqiu County People's Hospital for emergency treatment. Due to inhaling water after falling into the river, the children developed symptoms of pulmonary edema and were then transferred to Zhoukou Central Hospital. The doctors there said about Zhang Guisheng's condition when he was brought in: “Because the patient is old and physically weak, and had stayed in the water for a longer period of time, he showed symptoms of high blood pressure and rapid heart rate. The emergency room immediately organized doctors for treatment, and after intense care, Zhang Guisheng's vital signs gradually stabilized.”

Yesterday, I interviewed Zhang Guisheng, who is still recovering. Zhang Guisheng said: “When I was rescuing them, I didn’t think too much. I just knew that I am over 60 years old, and even if it means risking my life, I must save these children.” After Zhang Guisheng was hospitalized, the parents of the drowning children came to the hospital and gave him nutritional supplements and 1000 yuan, but he firmly refused.

“This old man is truly brave, showing no hesitation in the face of life and death...” Local residents, upon hearing about Zhang Guisheng's heroic act of saving the drowning children, expressed admiration: “It’s so cold outside, yet this old man managed to pull the drowning children back from the brink of death. Such a selfless spirit deserves praise.”

As the interview was coming to an end, Elder Zhang Guisheng, lying in bed, still held a trace of regret in his eyes. That regret was about the young man who jumped into the water with him to save the children. After ensuring the safety of the children, the young man quietly left without even leaving his name.
