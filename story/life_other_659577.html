Next life, I will love you well

While writing this article, my tears also flowed down unconsciously! Everyone has a true love that belongs to themselves! When facing the person who loves you, will you question whether you really love her? I hope you can all seriously consider it and not regret until you lose her, because at that point, it will be useless. Here, I want to loudly tell you: "Wife, in this life, I only love you, until I grow old and die."

Once, by chance, the boy met the girl and fell deeply in love with her! The girl didn't have much impression of the boy!

That night, the boy was sleepless all night. He knew he had completely fallen in love with the girl. Her image kept flashing through his mind.

The boy knew that he and the girl were impossible, so he tried to forget her, but no matter how hard he tried, he just couldn't forget. In the end, he gave in and had to forget the girl.

A few days later, the boy saw the girl again. His heart was very excited, very scared, and he kept telling himself: "Forget her, forget her." "Hi, we meet again." The girl's voice broke the boy's warning. At this moment, the boy's face was very hot, his heart kept beating fast, at this moment, the boy felt time stopped. The sound disappeared, leaving only the sound of his own heartbeat.

"What's wrong with you? Why are your cheeks red?" The girl asked with concern on her face.

"No...nothing, it's hot, I'm fine. I didn't expect to see you here." The boy answered while looking down, his face as red as the rising sun.

"Oh, haha... What a coincidence. Are you alone?" The girl smiled innocently at the boy.

"Huh? Yes, haha, are you?" The boy replied incoherently.

"Yes! It's so boring shopping alone. Hey! How about you accompany me for a walk!" The girl looked at the boy and smiled.

"Is this... Am I hearing things!" The boy's heart was pounding, as if it would jump out.

"What, is it not okay?" The girl looked disappointed.

"No..no. I'd be delighted." The boy was so excited he couldn't speak. He threw away the warnings he had given himself earlier.

The boy left the girl's phone number. Every week, the girl would invite the boy to go out and play. The boy was also very happy to oblige.

Every time they went out, the girl's friends would joke: "Wow, not bad, found yourself a boyfriend already? Will you forget us sisters once you get a husband?" "He's my good friend, stop guessing!" The girl's face showed a blush. The boy heard that he was the girl's good friend and wanted to shout with excitement, but he held back. But his face turned very red. Over time, the boy and the girl slowly fell in love. The boy spent every day with the girl, and the girl also became very dependent on the boy. The boy treated the girl so well that people around them envied him. They joked that if they had a husband like him, they would be happy. The girl's face flushed with a blush as she leaned against the boy's chest.

One day, the boy and the girl were walking hand in hand on the street when suddenly they saw a few people robbing an old woman. The boy immediately rushed forward, and the girl also ran up to help. Unexpectedly, the girl was so capable! A robber was preparing to sneak attack the girl from behind, and the boy quickly rushed to the girl's side and hugged her. The wooden stick struck the boy's head with force, blood flowed down instantly. Seeing this, the robbers immediately fled. The boy smiled at the girl and then collapsed straight down. After a while, the ambulance came and took the boy to the hospital.

After a few days, the boy recovered, but his personality changed drastically. Every day he indulged in wine and women, even bringing women to the girl's side. Unable to bear it, the girl broke up with the boy. The girl cried her heart out. She walked alone on the streets, visiting every place where she had memories with the boy. A few days later, the girl who was with the boy ran up and handed the girl a letter before leaving.

The girl found a place and opened the letter.

Dear, Wife:

Dear wife, this is the last time I call you this. By the time you read this letter, I will have gone to a very faraway place. Do you know, wife? From the first time I saw you, I knew I had fallen in love with you, love that I could not control. I forced myself not to like you, but I couldn't help it, every day your image kept appearing in my mind.

Ha! Who would have thought I would finally become your boyfriend! I swear, I will love you well, treat you well, but fate did not allow me to stay by your side; I cannot give you love anymore. That day, when we bravely fought with the thugs, I was gloriously wounded. The doctor discovered that I have cancer, and it's already in its late stages. They said I don't have much time left. Alas! I really didn't want to know, because I wanted to stay with you.

Hehe! Wife, from now on, I cannot stay with you, I cannot give you love, but you must remember my heart will always be with you.

Wife, I am no longer here. You must take care of yourself, love yourself, eat well, sleep well. This life, I cannot stay with you, find someone who loves you and live happily ever after. This life, I cannot give you happiness, but next life, I will definitely give you the most beautiful happiness.

Alas! There are still so many words I want to say to you.

Wife, I want to shout loudly to you:

"Next life, marry me! I will love you well, wife, I love you forever."

The girl finished reading, and by this time, she was sobbing uncontrollably, shouting to the sky:

"Husband, I don't want you to marry me next life, I want you in this life................."
