Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hotspot: After being interrogated, the man confessed that his surname is Hu. He had just pried open the door of room 802 and stolen gold earrings, a gold ring, and other jewelry, along with over 3000 yuan in cash. While observing from the rooftop if anyone was below, he spotted a patrolling officer and returned to hide, but was still caught. Hu confessed that since August 2011, he had climbed the external drainpipes at Lanhe Mingyuan residential area to the rooftops and committed over 20 cases of home burglary. The melanoma orphan's life is critical, and hundreds of netizens have been helping him search for relatives for five months. The total amount involved in the case is over 200,000 yuan. Currently, Hu has been criminally detained. At around 6 pm on the 14th, when the Hulan police were patrolling at building 7 of Lanhe Mingyuan residential area, they found a man standing on the roof of the 8th floor. Upon seeing the patrolling officers, the man immediately hid inside the building. The police immediately guarded all the unit doors of building 7 and then searched each corridor one by one. At around 7 pm, when the police searched to room 802 of unit 2 of building 7, they found a gap left on the door with pry marks. The police pushed the door open, and it was pitch black inside. When searching to the small room on the north side, a man suddenly jumped out from under the bed, waving a hammer to prevent the police from getting close. The police rushed forward and pinned the man down. The picture shows the handwriting left by the suspect. Source: Northeast Network. Recently, the Hulan police caught a suspect of home burglary during a night patrol, solving over 20 cases of home burglary series. It is understood that the suspect used his slim figure to climb into homes. If there was no money or valuables to steal, he would write messages like "Your family is so poor, try harder" on the walls of the victims' homes using soy sauce or other condiments. Among the over 20 households that were burglarized, he left messages for about five or six families.
