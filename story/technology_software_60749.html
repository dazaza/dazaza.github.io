"Yutong Learn Characters" is a children's character recognition software. It is suitable for bilingual Chinese-English picture-based character learning for children aged 3 to 7.

"Yutong Learn Characters" was developed based on the author's years of experience in teaching character recognition and right-brain development practices. It fully utilizes information technology to overcome the drawbacks of traditional character learning methods, such as monotony and boredom, which make it difficult to capture the interest of young children. Each Chinese character is paired with a beautiful real photograph, and there is standard voice pronunciation by real people. Additionally, large red characters dynamically demonstrate the process of writing Chinese characters in a grid, stimulating both the left and right brain of children through images, text, sound, and animation. This not only increases children's interest in characters and enhances memory but also aids in the intellectual development of children, promoting balanced development of both hemispheres of the brain.

The carefully selected Chinese characters include the 284 most commonly used characters for children as statistically determined by the "Children's Pictorial" Publishing House, as well as the 400 most commonly used characters that children are required to recognize in the first volume of the Elementary School Chinese textbook (People's Education Press New Curriculum Standard Edition). By mastering these characters, children can understand eighty percent of the content in ordinary children’s reading materials, meaning they can start independently reading simple children's books and explore the ocean of knowledge. With this foundation, your child can easily get ahead from the very start of school.

All words and phrases in the software are pronounced by real people with standard voices. The Chinese pronunciation is recorded by national-level announcers, while the English pronunciation is recorded by renowned American English teachers.
