Is there an age limit for hair transplant surgery? Hair transplant surgery uses precision instruments to extract healthy follicle tissue from the patient's own occipital region, which after professional cultivation and processing, is then transplanted to the area of hair loss. Once the follicles survive, they can achieve the purpose of treating hair loss. Since it is implemented through surgery, many people worry about whether there is an age limit for hair transplant surgery. Hair transplant surgery is just a minimally invasive surgical procedure. The surgical process is very relaxed and comfortable, so as long as the patient is over 8 years old, they can undergo hair transplant surgery. Is hair transplant surgery affected by the four seasons? In all four seasons, the climate varies with different seasons. Many friends worry that hair transplantation at different seasons may affect the effect of hair transplantation. In fact, hair transplant surgery is not affected by the climate. Although it is easy to sweat in summer, it may affect the health of implanted hair, but due to the high temperature in summer and the increased blood flow speed, it also helps the growth of follicles; although the temperature is low in winter, it affects the recovery of wounds, but it is not easy to sweat, and methods such as using hats can be used to cover up. Therefore, there is no seasonal difference in hair transplantation. What is the golden period for hair transplant surgery? In fact, the golden period for hair transplantation is the stable phase of hair loss. Generally, after patients find themselves losing hair, they will use various drugs and folk remedies to treat hair loss. Not only are these methods ineffective, but they also delay the best treatment opportunity. After discovering hair loss, one should promptly go to a professional hair transplant hospital for detailed follicle testing to determine the cause of hair loss and treat accordingly.
