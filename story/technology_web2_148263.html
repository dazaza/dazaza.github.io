China Unicom 3G test commercial Sunday, October large-scale number release

by diglog2008 on 2009-05-12 15:40:39

Following the release of the full service brand on April 28, China Unicom WCDMA 3G network has now entered the later stage of network debugging and optimization. Yesterday, the reporter learned from Guangdong Unicom that Guangdong Unicom 3G business will be officially unveiled on May 17, 3G netbooks and network card business, as well as a number of customized mobile phone terminals will be unveiled in Unicom 3G brand store.

It is reported that due to "5· 17 "is Unicom 3G network trial commercial, large-scale number allocation will wait until October, so the first six Guangdong Unicom cities - Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Dongguan, Foshan, Zhongshan, Zhuhai will be limited number allocation, limited development of friendly users. In the trial commercial phase, only the 186 number segment is opened, only the 186 users who choose 3G basic access network, and only the postpaid service is launched for ordinary users.

The reporter learned from other channels that the basic tariff of Unicom's 3G network is 50 yuan/month, and the call is 0.36 yuan/minute, and the answer is free; Roaming, SMS, Internet access, MMS and other costs are basically the same as 2G network. In addition, Unicom has planned a total of 7 package rates, which are 186 yuan, 226 yuan, 286 yuan, 386 yuan, 586 yuan, 886 yuan and 1686 yuan, and reduced the tariff through the package. For example, 186 yuan includes 510 minutes, which is equivalent to 0.364 yuan per minute, and the excess part is calculated by 0.36 yuan per minute, but it also includes a certain domestic call time, video TV time and corresponding value-added service content.
