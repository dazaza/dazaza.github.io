Bedroom decoration secrets: Win in five steps against decoration companies

by longersing on 2009-10-23 13:37:14

The Bedroom Decoration Secret Manual will teach you how to create a home fashion that surpasses decoration companies by focusing on the overall tone of the bedroom, the softness and warmth of lighting, curtains, and bedding.

1. The overall tone of the bedroom decoration should be soft, preferably using elegant and warm colors such as light yellow, light green, light purple, and light blue. The main theme should be tranquil, comfortable, and cozy, with the decorative color not being too complicated or intense. Strong colors can stimulate nerves, causing excessive excitement, which is not conducive to quickly entering deep sleep. Overly cold colors like navy blue, gray, and black used over large areas can prevent an already tense mood from relaxing. Over time, this can lead to depression.

2. The lighting in the bedroom decoration should be warm. Sources of light in the bedroom include natural light and artificial lighting. Natural light mainly comes from the sun, while artificial lighting involves using lamps for illumination. Typically, bedroom lighting includes general lighting, local lighting, and decorative lighting. General lighting is used for rest and daily activities, while activities like grooming, changing clothes, and reading in bed require local lighting. Decorative lighting is aimed at setting the mood. For general lighting in the bedroom, attention should be paid to ensuring the light is soft, warm, and variable. Avoid using only one large central lamp indoors, as strong or overly white light can make the room appear dull and lifeless. If choosing a ceiling-hung lamp, it's necessary to use a lamp with a warm light and an appropriate lampshade; otherwise, hanging a heavy lamp poorly illuminated can significantly reduce the indoor atmosphere. Types of lighting fixtures for bedrooms include recessed lights, flush-mounted lights, wall lights, and portable lights. Lighting should also be flexible, warm, and relaxed. Activities like changing clothes, grooming, and reading all require suitable lighting. Through perfect and effective lighting design, the individuality of indoor space decoration can be fully expressed.

3. It’s best if the bedroom decoration has two layers of curtains. The outer layer should be made of thicker linen or cotton fabric to block out light, dust, and noise, creating a good resting environment. The inner layer can use thin materials like gauze or lace, which are transparent or semi-transparent, mainly for decoration. The color should match the overall style of the room and can be changed according to the season for different colors and textures.

4. In winter, bedroom decoration must choose comfortable and warm bedding for decoration, possibly colorful ones that can add some vitality to monotonous days.

5. Skillfully use bed canopies for bedroom decoration. With a long history, bed canopies have become a spiritual symbol entering modern households. They are not only a good choice for bedroom decoration but also increase the privacy of sleep. Whether sleeping alone or with a partner, a sense of security is desired, and bed canopies can provide a romantic and private space for you and your partner.
