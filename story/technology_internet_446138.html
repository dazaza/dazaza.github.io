New Express, June 14th Report: According to Hong Kong Weekly, the final straw that broke the camel's back of Tse and Cheung was Cecilia Cheung still keeping in touch with her ex-boyfriend Edison Chen. After meeting happily on a plane last month during Mother's Day, they secretly met again recently, making Nicholas Tse unable to bear it anymore, deciding to part ways with Cecilia Cheung. Another Hong Kong media reported that Nicholas Tse has sent a divorce agreement through his lawyer to Cecilia Cheung, believing that Cheung must have been greatly shocked upon hearing this news. The recent mysterious whereabouts of Cheung have made people around her extremely worried.

According to the weekly report, Nicholas Tse hoped to calmly think about their marriage issues while filming in Malaysia, but unexpectedly, Cecilia Cheung lost control emotionally and pressed him step by step. Not only did she send threatening text messages, but also secretly moved out of Yangming Mountain with their two sons, going missing. However, what truly caused Nicholas Tse to lose hope was when he discovered that after the incident of Cecilia Cheung and Edison Chen taking selfies on a plane, they continued to communicate and even arranged a secret meeting. This made Nicholas Tse finally realize and firmly decide to get divorced.

It is rumored that Cecilia Cheung avoided the media by recently staying at the Four Seasons Hotel in Hong Kong with her two sons. Interestingly, the Four Seasons Hotel is also one of Edison Chen's usual haunts in Hong Kong. They reportedly secretly met there when Chen returned to Hong Kong a few days ago. An insider revealed that Cecilia Cheung had planned to sell her independent house before renting a place at Yangming Mountain. Nicholas Tse thought it didn't matter where they lived and even helped her pay a year’s rent at Yangming Mountain. Unexpectedly, Cecilia Cheung suddenly left Yangming Mountain with her two sons, making it impossible for anyone to find her. She even prohibited the maid from answering calls, fearing that the maid might reveal her whereabouts to La Goo (Tse's mother).

However, you can't wrap fire in paper. Someone saw her at the Four Seasons Hotel, meeting with Edison Chen. The purpose of the meeting was apparently that Cecilia Cheung requested Edison Chen, who once destroyed her family and career due to the photo scandal, to help sign her up with Lin Jian Yue.

Edison Chen helped Cecilia Cheung sign a binding contract. Seeing Nicholas Tse's resolute attitude, Cecilia Cheung met with lawyers under the arrangement of her mother and sister. After consulting legal advice, she planned to fight for custody rights while plotting a full comeback to restore her image and quickly sign with a major company. After being rejected by mainland companies, she offered a HK$100 million deal for three years to Lin Jian Yue, the tycoon of Hong Kong film and television industry and owner of Universe Entertainment.

An insider revealed: She calculated well - if she could successfully sign the contract, she could receive HK$100 million at once. First, it would prove her economic ability to raise her two sons. Second, she could say that she could make money herself and not rely on Nicholas Tse's money. Third, she could repay the huge debt she owed from real estate speculation. In addition, Cecilia Cheung would use the fact that Nicholas Tse is busy shooting films and her two sons have always been taken care of by her as reasons to demand custody of both sons.

After the photo scandal, Edison Chen struggled to make a comeback while having to support his family. His father even applied for bankruptcy. It was rare that Cecilia Cheung needed something from him, so they agreed to collaborate and stir up publicity, each benefiting from the other. Therefore, it is said that Edison Chen has actively lobbied boss Lin Jian Yue to sign Cecilia Cheung. If they become label mates in the future, there will naturally be plenty of opportunities for cooperation and earning money together. When asked about this matter, Lin Jian Yue admitted that Cecilia Cheung indeed visited his company, but it was just to discuss scripts.

On the other hand, regarding Cecilia Cheung's mysterious whereabouts, different media reports have various versions. Some Hong Kong paparazzi waited in vain at the airport for a week, while Malaysian media reported that after finishing the movie "The Shadow Lover", Cecilia Cheung flew to Kuala Lumpur with her two sons, lying in wait at the hotel where Nicholas Tse stayed, hoping to beg for forgiveness face-to-face. She even brought her father Hu Xuyong, who has connections with organized crime, and stayed at his residence in Malaysia, quietly waiting for Nicholas Tse to calm down. Hu Xuyong is known for his low profile and is the result of Cecilia Cheung's mother's second marriage. However, her parents divorced when she was nine years old, and then she went to Australia to study in high school, later returning to Hong Kong to enter the entertainment industry. Due to her father's complex background, in 1999, an organized crime group issued a gang rape order against her, forcing her to hold a press conference. Fortunately, the police intervened in the investigation and resolved the situation. In recent years, it was rumored that Zhang's father often resides in Malaysia, enjoying financial support from his daughter and living leisurely.

Regarding custody disputes, Hong Kong media pointed out that Nicholas Tse has already replied to Cecilia Cheung's request for divorce through a lawyer's letter. A Hong Kong lawyer stated: This letter has no legal effect. A lawyer's letter cannot be used as court evidence. It is merely a routine reply indicating that both parties agree to apply for divorce, which would expedite the process. When asked if Tse and Cheung need to meet after applying for divorce, the lawyer said: They do not need to meet, everything can be handled by their respective lawyers. Furthermore, discussions about the custody of the two sons can also be fully entrusted to the lawyers without either party needing to meet. However, if both parties cannot reach a consensus on custody, they will have to go to court.

●Tse's side: La Goo may testify against Cheung

Originally, Nicholas Tse did not want to take the custody issue to court, but because he hopes Lucas can attend school and lead a normal life like any other child, he decided to fight for the custody of his two sons regardless. Regarding Cheung's claim that Tse is too busy working and rarely takes care of his sons, it was reported that La Goo might consider testifying in court. An insider revealed: La Goo couldn't stand seeing her daughter-in-law drain her son's wealth and using her two grandsons as bargaining chips. She considered personally testifying that Cecilia Cheung took Lucas away from school to shoot movies and travel, affecting his schooling and elementary school entrance exams. She would also expose how Cecilia Cheung frequently loses emotional control over money and small matters, making her unsuitable to take care of the two grandsons. However, La Goo is still considering this move because Nicholas Tse doesn't want to air too much dirty laundry publicly, fearing it might anger Cecilia Cheung, ultimately affecting their two grandsons.

●Cheung's side: Lucas is not close to his father

Although Cecilia Cheung once claimed on her friend Xiaoxi's show that she signed a prenuptial agreement, stating that if they ever divorced, the two children would be hers, the lawyer has already stated that the prenuptial agreement has no legal effect. Moreover, La Goo encouraged Tse to fight for the custody of their two sons. However, Nicholas Tse's chances in this battle for custody are not very high. According to the law, after the divorce of both parents, children under two years old generally live with their mother, while those over two years old have priority if they have lived longer with one parent. It is known that Lucas has always lived with Cecilia Cheung, and Nicholas Tse meets with his son very rarely. Additionally, Tse is rather strict with his son, always feeling that Cheung spoils her son too much. An insider revealed that Lucas even hides behind his mother out of fear when he sees Nicholas Tse. If Tse and Cheung really end up in court, the judge will consider the opinions of the children themselves, which would obviously be disadvantageous to Nicholas Tse.
