Edicy's free website building service launches a Chinese version

by xjyzhenai on 2009-04-14 10:55:33

JarryJarry wrote in a blog post on November 20, 2008: Edicy: making it possible for anyone to create a website. Recently, Edicy's free website building service has launched a Chinese version, allowing us to better utilize its free services and build a CMS website.

Edicy was created to provide direct online services for businesses and individuals. Using these services, you can quickly create a professionally designed website without any technical knowledge. Nowadays, online tools are too complicated, and often require you to hire expensive designers and programmers to create and maintain websites.

The creation of Edicy simplifies the entire process. The use of Edicy is quite simple; you can establish a website for your business or organization within minutes and make it available on the internet. All online tools can be used without additional software when the computer is connected to the network. The process involves choosing one of our well-designed plans, entering your text, adding your ideas, and then publishing your URL on your customized website. It's that simple.

Who is Edicy suitable for? Edicy is very flexible and convenient to use, so anyone who wants to create a website can use it. However, our main target users are those who are not familiar with the programming languages of website creation and do not wish to learn them. If you want to build a website to promote your small business or local group, or if you want to quickly and simply create a URL, then Edicy's online system is your best choice.

Registering for Edicy is very simple; just fill in your email address. Then, you will receive an email containing your initial password and related information. You can log in using the password from the email. After logging into Edicy with the password from the email, you can start your website-building journey.
