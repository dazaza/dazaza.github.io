Personal Housing Loan Procedures_Golden Location

by ww0976393 on 2010-04-04 10:50:06

I. Applicants (borrowers) should fill in the loan application form at the Construction Bank's handling agency or at a developer who has signed a "cooperation agreement" with the Construction Bank in prime locations, and provide the following materials:

1. Personal household registration book, ID card, or other valid residency documents;

2. Proof of occupation and income;

3. Relevant proof materials such as a purchase contract or letter of intent, etc., related to office buildings;

4. Other materials required by the Construction Bank’s handling agency.

II. Investigation: The Construction Bank’s handling agency or a law firm (or notary office) entrusted by the Construction Bank will conduct an investigation into the materials provided by the borrower.

III. Approval: The Construction Bank’s handling agency will review the borrower's application according to standards.

IV. Borrower opens an account, receives a savings card, and signs the loan contract.

V. Processing of relevant guarantee procedures for mortgage, guarantee, pledge, and insurance.

VI. The loan contract takes effect, and funds are transferred to the developer's account.

- Office Building - Introduction

Currently, there are two popular grading standards in the office building market: Grade A office buildings and 5A office buildings. Grade A office buildings are a commonly used term without a fixed standard because no one wants their building to be called Grade B. As a result, almost any building with glass facades, elevators, and some height tends to call itself Grade A. In contrast, 5A office buildings do have a relatively set standard, similar to the star-rating method for hotels.
