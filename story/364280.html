September 9 to October 10 - Free wedding certificate photo shoot for newlyweds in Shenzhen

by cain205228 on 2010-09-11 12:47:58

Carnival Impressions · Shenzhen Wedding Photography Studio is offering free marriage registration photos for all newlyweds who register their marriage from September 9 to October 10 in Shenzhen, as a special gift for the auspicious day of October 10, 2010, which symbolizes perfect completeness (the number '10' multiplied ten times).

This event is open to all newlyweds registering during this period and is completely free! If you are an existing Carnival customer getting engaged on October 10, you can enjoy an even richer festival gift – a super-sized 1.2-meter Korean hugging bear!

Event Details:

a、All marriage registration photos will be shot with the same quality standards as wedding photos:

b、Free makeup service once for both the groom and bride

c、Free gifting of four official marriage registration certificate photos as per the requirements of the Ministry of Civil Affairs

d、Photographed using a Canon full-frame 5D II professional DSLR camera

e、Shot using studio lights specifically designed for wedding photography studios

 

2. Free Marriage Registration Photo Shooting Process:

a、To ensure a smooth experience for your free marriage registration photo session, please make a phone reservation at least two days in advance.

b、After the shoot, if you need to retrieve your photos immediately, please bring a USB drive or we can send them via email.

c、To achieve professional portrait-level ID photo quality, the photos will be processed by Carnival Impressions’ design department before being professionally printed (not printed on-site at the photo booth or registration office).
