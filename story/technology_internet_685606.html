China's First Copyright Infringement Case in the Education Industry:

New Thinking sues New Start, and New Start loses!

The Civil Judgment of the Haidian District People's Court of Beijing

(2011) Hai Min Chu Zi No. 24619

Plaintiff: Beijing Haidian New Thinking Training School (hereinafter referred to as "New Thinking School") accused defendant Weilai Xin Qidian Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "Xin Qidian Company") of copyright infringement and unfair competition disputes. After accepting this case, our court assigned Judge Dongtao Li to preside over the trial, and a public hearing was conducted. The plaintiff’s agents Xujingwu and Chenjichun, and the defendant’s agent Fengxiaoyu attended the litigation. This case has now been reviewed and concluded.

Plaintiff New Thinking School claimed: Established in 1999, the plaintiff has received many honors including [Demonstration Institution for Network Education by Ministry of Education], and is a modernized and standardized comprehensive private educational institution. In May 2011, for better promotion, the plaintiff arranged its school online marketing department and computer professional teachers and students to jointly create the website homepage (www.xsledu.com). This website took more than two months to complete with the efforts of more than ten staff members including planners, programmers, and art designers. It includes 221 basic pictures, 221 design drafts, and over 100,000 words of edited text, with a total design capacity of more than 1G bytes and production costs of about 240,000 yuan. In September 2011, the plaintiff discovered that there were more than thirty instances where the defendant had plagiarized and stolen the plaintiff’s website content such as images, texts, and drafts, seriously infringing on the plaintiff’s copyright. The defendant also misused the plaintiff’s website filing registration number. The defendant falsely claims multiple exclusive qualifications and honors from the [Designated Educational Assistance Institution by Examination Center of Ministry of Education] in several places on their website, which is clearly deceptive behavior and constitutes unfair competition against the plaintiff. Therefore, the plaintiff requests the court to order the defendant:

1. Immediately cease infringement and delete the infringing content on the webpage bj.xqdedu.net;

2. Apologize to the plaintiff in the Jinghua Times and on the webpage bj.xqdedu.net;

3. Compensate the plaintiff for economic losses amounting to 1 million yuan;

4. Compensate the plaintiff for reasonable expenses incurred due to this case;

5. Bear all litigation costs.

Defendant New Starting Point Company defended: The plaintiff did not submit evidence proving that the pages on its website existed earlier than the notarization time of September 2, 2011. Therefore, even if there are identical contents on both the plaintiff and defendant's websites, it cannot be proven that the defendant plagiarized from the plaintiff's website. The complained website www.xqdedu.net was registered under Liu Wenjing's personal credentials, who is the owner and responsible person of the website. Thus, the complained website has no relation to the defendant. The defendant is neither the owner nor the operator of the complained website, and whether the website infringes or not has nothing to do with the defendant. The plaintiff's claim for economic loss has no basis for calculation. The court is requested to dismiss all of the plaintiff's claims.

Upon investigation, it was found:

[New Thinking School]'s domain name is xsledu.com, the hosting organization is New Thinking School, the filing number is Jing ICP Bei 07029102, and the website responsible person is Lidongmei. [New Starting Point]'s domain name is xqdedu.net, the hosting organization and website responsible person are both Liu Wenjing, and the filing number is Jing ICP Bei 09044722.

On September 15, 2011, the Beijing Wenzheng Notary Office issued the (2011) Jing Fangzheng Nei Jing Zhengzi No. 08699 notarial certificate. This notarial certificate shows that the agent of New Thinking School used the notary's computer to enter the website bj.xqdedu.net. The website displayed Jing ICP Bei 07029102 Copyright@2010-2011 XQDedu.net and confirmed the ICP filing number of New Thinking School but did not recognize the ownership of the website. [mailto: Jing ICP Bei 07029102 Copyright@2010-2011%20XQDedu.net]

During the trial process, New Thinking School submitted a source code file CD of its website to prove that the images and other content on the involved website originated from its own website. Upon comparison, it was found that there were many similarities between New Thinking School's website and New Starting Point Company's website in terms of filing numbers, certificates, images, and drafts. New Starting Point Company did not acknowledge this due to the inability to confirm that the source code generation time of New Thinking School's website was earlier than the notarization time.

On December 2, 2011, Lidongmei issued a statement indicating that although she is the responsible person for the New Thinking School website www.xsledu.com (Jing ICP Bei 07029102), the operator and owner of the website are New Thinking School. New Starting Point Company recognized that www.xsledu.com belongs to New Thinking School but did not acknowledge the authenticity of Lidongmei's signature.

New Thinking School submitted invoices for 2,500 yuan in notary fees and 15,000 yuan in legal fees.

The above facts are supported by the filing information, notarial certificate, proof, invoices provided by New Thinking School, the filing information provided by New Starting Point Company, and the court's trial record.

This court believes: As the website owner of New Thinking School, New Thinking School legally enjoys the copyright of the website content. The webpage layout, design style, background color, animation, image, art, and text of the website bj.xqdedu.net are similar to those of New Thinking School's website, with identical content. Although the ICP registration responsible person is Liu Wenjing, the website content is all related to New Starting Point Company's business. In the absence of contrary evidence from the company, the company should be regarded as the actual user of the website. Without permission from the rights holder, the company uses the web content of New Thinking School's website, including New Thinking School's certificates, constituting copyright infringement and unfair competition. Legally, they should bear the responsibility for infringement. The company denies infringement without submitting relevant evidence, and thus, this court does not accept their defense. New Thinking School demands an apology from New Starting Point Company and compensation for economic losses of 1 million yuan, etc., but lacks sufficient evidence. This court will determine the way to assume responsibility based on factors such as the degree of infringement by New Starting Point Company and will not fully support its litigation request.

Accordingly, in accordance with Article 48 (1) and Article 49 (2) of the "Copyright Law of the People's Republic of China" and Article 2 (1) and Article 14 of the "Anti-Unfair Competition Law of the People's Republic of China", the judgment is as follows:

1. From the date of the effectiveness of this judgment, the defendant Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. shall cease infringement;

2. Within thirty days from the date of the effectiveness of this judgment, the defendant Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. shall publish a statement in the Beijing Morning Post to apologize to the plaintiff Beijing Haidian New Thinking Training School and eliminate influence (the statement must be reviewed by this court. If the defendant fails to perform within the deadline, this court will publicly disclose the main content of the judgment in the Beijing Morning Post, with the costs borne by the defendant);

3. Within ten days from the date of the effectiveness of this judgment, the defendant Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. shall compensate the plaintiff Beijing Haidian New Thinking Training School for economic losses and reasonable expenses totaling 150,000 yuan;

4. Dismiss the other litigation requests of the plaintiff Beijing Haidian New Thinking Training School.

If the defendant Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. fails to perform the monetary obligations specified in this judgment within the designated period, they shall pay double interest for the delayed performance period according to Article 229 of the "Civil Procedure Law of the People's Republic of China".

Case acceptance fee of 6,900 yuan (prepaid by the plaintiff), to be borne by the defendant Future New Starting Point Education Consulting (Beijing) Co., Ltd. (to be paid within seven days after the effectiveness of this judgment).

If dissatisfied with this judgment, one may appeal to this court within fifteen days from the date of receipt of the judgment, submit an appeal petition, and submit copies according to the number of opposing parties, pay the appeal case acceptance fee, and appeal to the First Intermediate People's Court of Beijing. If the appeal fee is not paid within seven days after the expiration of the appeal period, it will be treated as automatically withdrawing the appeal.
