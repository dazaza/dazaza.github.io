You will always be in my heart.

by ccwill1p on 2009-12-04 13:12:29

Author: Yuchun (??) From afar, I love to look for songs of twilight years, standing in the quiet evening, listening quietly and high. So beautiful, so fragrant, as if it has precipitated a thousand dreams of old age! When not speaking, sadness uses white characters to express feelings on the heart, and when the heart opens, one doesn't know which corner they have gone mad to? Women are really sentimental! It's hard to erase the past with one stroke. In the melody that is clearly understood, thoughts of you arise again! Just one person, without any particular feeling, can people truly forget? Recently, a song has been popular: In this world, we will never meet again. Standing in the dim light, repeating it over and over. Suddenly, there's a desire to cry, suddenly there's a desire to know, You----- Have you ever missed me? Have you ever had the same feelings alone? Have you ever thought about me? If only fate could give me a chance to forget, what would I choose to forget? Your voice, your heart that has left, the good times that have passed! In the shimmering water, what lingers in the heart is always the most unforgettable emotion. Growing up seems like waking but is still asleep! Many seem drunk but still sober! Growing up is full of confusion and bewilderment! How I wish I could return to the beginning where we first met! This place where emotions blend together......... Ah! I really miss you! However, all things are gradually destroying hopes that people cannot reach! Perhaps in the nights of the future, I will slowly forget you, or perhaps I will deeply engrave you in my memory forever! Then please allow me on some afternoon to quietly think about you. Silently-------Wishing you happiness! Some people, some events live in my emotional thoughts forever! Even if I forget your voice, your smile, your appearance. However, the feeling I get every time I think of you is something I can never forget!
