Love Watching Art Map 2008

by yinyin on 2008-08-23 23:57:36

Shanghai will soon see an art event guide map tailor-made for art lovers. With this "I Watch Art Map" in hand, you can find your favorite art venues and see the artworks that you have only seen in books or albums.

Iwatch Media, which launched this map, said that the map would be updated every three months and distributed for free, aiming to fully present the city's art events and new art venues of the season in a simple and intuitive way as soon as possible.

This edition of the "I Watch Art Map" presents three major local art events in September: Shanghai Biennale, Shanghai Contemporary Art Exhibition, and Shanghai Art Fair. In addition to providing basic information such as exhibition period, address, transportation, and contact number, the "I Watch Art Map" also highlights the key topics of each exhibition.
