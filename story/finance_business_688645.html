Family of Yao Jiaxin's Victim: A Dispute and Conflict in the Stairwell

Xian, February 8 (Zhang Yuchen, Lie Wei) - At around 11 a.m. on the 8th, six people including Zhang Xian, Wang Hui, Zhang Pingxuan, Zhang Pingxuan’s second daughter Zhang Lang, and fellow villagers arrived at Yao Qingwei's home to "demand" a 200,000 yuan donation. Due to a disagreement in words, they had an argument and physical conflict with Ma Yanming, Yao Qingwei's proxy waiting downstairs at Yao's building. Subsequently, they were taken by the local police to the police station for questioning.

Video: The scene lost control when Zhang Miao's family and Zhang Xian went to Yao's place to demand compensation - Source: Huashang Video

On the morning of the 7th, Zhang Miaoxuan, father of Yao Jiaxin's victim Zhang Miao, his mother Liu Xiaoqian, and others announced through their agent Zhang Xian's blog that they are willing to accept the "200,000 yuan gift" offered by Yao Qingwei during the trial of the Yao case, and would "go to Yao Qingwei's place on the morning of February 8, 2012, to receive this amount." Some students picking up trash on the street sparked controversy as some parents worried about bacterial infections. Previously, Zhang Miao's family had rejected this donation.

At 12:30 p.m., Zhang Xian, Wang Hui, and Zhang Pingxuan were taken to the Changlezong Road Police Station in Xi'an for mediation. A reporter from China News Service rushed to the scene for an interview.

Amidst the noise, Zhang Pingxuan appeared somewhat reserved. He stated that during the death penalty review period of the Yao Jiaxin case, Yao Qingwei had once delivered 200,000 yuan to his home, but he had mailed it back because these 200,000 yuan came with conditions, which was an attempt to seek a lighter sentence for Yao Jiaxin. Now, Zhang Miao's mother Liu Xiaoqian is suffering from illness and urgently needs money, so they are willing to accept this 200,000 yuan donation.

Ma Yanming, Yao Qingwei's proxy, told reporters that the conflict arose due to contradictory statements made by the Zhang family regarding the 200,000 yuan donation. According to Yao Qingwei, those 200,000 yuan were personally handed over by him and his wife to Zhang Pingxuan, who accepted them after their conversation, but later sent the money back for unknown reasons. If the Zhang family can provide sufficient evidence proving that donations from society have been exhausted, leading to their inability to continue living, then he himself could offer a certain level of assistance to the Zhang family, and continue donating until his passing, methods for treating hemorrhoids.

Ma Yanming emphasized that Yao Qingwei is in poor health, suffering from heart disease, and has fully entrusted him to handle this matter. To prevent conflicts arising from both parties meeting, he waited downstairs early. Legally speaking, the Yao family no longer has the obligation to pay the 200,000 yuan. From the aspects of sentiment, reason, and law, he does not wish for any conflict between the two sides.

At around 14:00 in the afternoon, after completing their statements, Zhang Xian and others quickly left the police station. (End)
