J-10 takes off

by h112015755 on 2009-11-20 15:20:06

On the first day of his appointment as the commander of a certain aviation regiment of the Air Force, Senior Colonel Yue Tianlong was confronted with an unknown nationality reconnaissance aircraft flying into our sea area. Fighter jets were ordered to drive it away. Deputy Commander Yin Shuanghu, who served as the field commander, worried about safety and hastily withdrew the troops. Based on practical combat needs, Yue Tianlong proposed that Deputy Commander Yin, with superb flight skills, should organize a Blue Team for Red-Blue air combat training within the regiment. During the training, Yue Tianlong's aircraft collided with a bird in the airspace above the airport. He calmly responded and successfully landed with a single engine. The deputy commander responsible for bird control at the airport was disciplined. In the aerial confrontation training, Deputy Commander Yin Shuanghu turned defeat into victory with the highly skilled maneuver known as the cobra maneuver, defeating Commander Yue Tianlong. Yue Tianlong intruded into the Blue Team headquarters and asked Deputy Commander Yin to teach him the cobra maneuver. Unexpectedly, during the training, Yue Tianlong's aircraft entered a spin and lost control. Just before a catastrophic accident occurred, Yue Tianlong successfully recovered control with his superior skills and good psychological quality, but then faced an empty fuel tank and made a forced landing at an abandoned old airport. Yue Tianlong continued to practice hard and finally completed the high-difficulty, high-risk aerial tactical maneuver known as the cobra maneuver. When higher-level agency officers conducted evaluations, Commander Yue Tianlong firmly recommended Deputy Commander Yin Shuanghu for promotion. During the Sino-Russian joint military exercise, Russian pilot Ivan engaged in aerial confrontation with Yue Tianlong during training. Yue Tianlong avoided using the cobra maneuver, but unexpectedly, Ivan used the hook maneuver tactic to turn the disadvantage into an advantage, making Commander Yue Tianlong see new gaps. During the exercise, Commander Yue Tianlong led the pilots to successfully complete the high-difficulty subject of aerial refueling. On the return flight, they received orders to drive away reconnaissance aircraft of unknown nationality. Yue Tianlong and Yin Shuanghu, with their superior skills and tactical maneuvers, drove the opposing fighter jets away from our exercise area, successfully completing the exercise mission. This script focuses on shaping the image of a unique character in the Air Force pilot. As the commander of an aviation regiment, he is both a commander and a combatant, eager and daring to fight. He competes with Yin Shuanghu and foreign pilots in martial arts, even appearing quite unconventional in his words and deeds. Adding the young charm of pilots, the display of modern aircraft, the excitement of high-difficulty, high-risk aerial stunts such as the cobra and hook maneuvers, will make the future film visually appealing and uplifting.
