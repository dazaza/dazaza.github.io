Online store sellers use the Spring Festival Gala tags to attract customers

by lhltckfrtg on 2012-03-07 10:10:33

Weibo recommendation | Today's Weibo hotspot (Editor: SN017) Related thematic articles: On January 25th, the reporter Sun Yong reported that taking advantage of the ongoing heated discussion about this year's Spring Festival Gala, many online merchants could not wait to label their products with the "Spring Festival Gala" tag. Today, when I entered the phrase "2012 Spring Festival Gala" in the search bar on the Taobao homepage, more than 1400 related items were displayed, including e-book downloads.

In the opening segment of the Long Year Spring Festival Gala, which was themed around family, actress Hu Ke made an appearance wearing a red off-the-shoulder dress. The simple ready-to-wear design made this evening gown very suitable for everyday wear. The same style of evening gown is priced between 130 and 170 yuan on Taobao. One shop owner said, "This gown is a new model that we originally had in stock. Unexpectedly, Hu Ke also chose this piece for the Spring Festival Gala, so I immediately changed its name to be relevant to the gala." In recent years, Liu Qian has become a regular guest at the Spring Festival Gala. In response to his magic show this year, some Taobao stores have launched high-quality magic props. A set of Liu Qian's super-sized metal magnetic cup (transformation cup) props from one store includes an aluminum super-sized magnetic cup, a magnetic red ball, and a regular red ball, along with foreign video teaching procedures for TXT novel downloads.

Besides Hu Ke's dress, other celebrity outfits from the Long Year Spring Festival Gala quickly appeared on Taobao, such as Sha Yi's snow boots, Wang Fei's red dress, Dong Qing's two evening gowns, and Zhang Mingmin's red scarf. However, some styles are currently only available for "pre-order." Many shop owners stated that due to the Spring Festival holiday, their Taobao shops only accept orders but do not ship them.
