Recruitment research direction of sociology major for on-duty graduate students of Renmin University

by shuoshi on 2011-08-29 17:27:02

Direction One: Human Resource Management

Human resource management is a vigorously developing discipline with extensive application fields. With the advent of the knowledge economy era and the influx of Western human resources, this core resource has led us to a frenzy of blind learning, neglecting the demands of social economic development. How can we fully utilize the advantages of human resource management under the socialist system to ensure harmonious operation of social development and market economy? We need to deeply explore the fundamental knowledge in the field of sociology and comprehensively apply management methods to better leverage the role of human resource management.

Direction Two: Social Psychology

Social psychology is an interdisciplinary subject between psychology and sociology, influenced by both disciplines. It is a branch of psychology that studies the social psychological phenomena of individuals and groups. It reveals functional psychology and enriches the soul of our society. In a social context, through exploring how dyes and the environment interact to produce meaningful thoughts, feelings, and behaviors, social psychologists help weave a colorful tapestry of humanity. Combined with our research wisdom, it can be applied to interpersonal relationships, applied management, and other practical applications, benefiting us for life.

Direction Three: Management Sociology

This direction is suitable for high-level public management workers in government, military agencies, science, education, culture, health, sports, and large administrative departments to study and research. By applying social science and management science methods, they can master comprehensive coordination management skills, drawing on the successful experiences of many developed countries in cultivating administrative management talents. This fosters senior specialized talents with firm and correct political orientation, good ideological and moral cultivation, who can engage in policy analysis and research, management planning research, organizational and personnel management, administrative legislation and law enforcement, administrative supervision, and agency affairs management.

Direction Four: Social Work

This direction cultivates senior specialized talents through the study of the main discipline - sociology. These talents possess basic social work theories and knowledge, relatively proficient social survey research skills, and social work abilities. They can work in civil affairs, labor, social security, and health departments, as well as trade unions, youth, women's organizations, and other social welfare, service, and public welfare institutions, engaging in social security, social policy research, social administrative management, community development and management, social services, evaluation, and operations.
