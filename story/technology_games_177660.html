Treasure artifacts, mounts, instances, high-level equipment designs, Zhuzi Country map, Mo Family Village storyline, stocks, duel system! You've pushed players into the abyss time and time again!

Treasure artifacts, 99% of them are useless, and high-level ones can't even be traded! To obtain a school treasure artifact, it requires 5000 school contributions. Mounts, the cost to raise them is completely disproportionate to their utility. They're purely decorative items for players with more money than sense.

High-level equipment designs, how can you have the nerve to release something so ugly? I don't know if it's the public's aesthetic judgment that's the problem or if there's something wrong with your eyes! Just look at the equipment design; it's about the same size as the treasure artifacts. Truly miniature!

The Zhuzi Country map, aside from allowing faster travel to certain areas, I haven't found any other use. It feels completely out of place with the entire game!
