Travelogue of Guangzhou Tower: Graduation Trip

by guangzt324 on 2011-09-21 10:18:40

The beginning of the story... Guangzhou New TV Tower tickets

Elementary school students have graduated from elementary school..

Junior high school students have graduated from junior high school..

College students have graduated from college..

Therefore.. Graduation trips have begun..

Guangzhou..

Guangzhou in childhood.. Guangzhou in my memory.. The buildings are tall, the Pearl River is beautiful, and things are expensive.. Later, I realized that there is always a gap between ideals and reality.. Maybe it's because I've grown taller, Guilin is very beautiful, Litan things are very expensive, so the distance has shortened.. People from Guangxi living in Guangzhou have no pressure.. Regardless of height, appearance, body type, diet, sleep schedule, accent.. You can turn Binyang dialect into non-standard Cantonese.. There are many authentic Guilin rice noodles.. Guangzhou肠粉 (Cheung Fun) and rolled rice noodles are similar but don't have the yellow skin sauce.. People in Guangzhou also have nightlife..

Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street.. Stayed two nights at the Xiaoxiang Hotel on Pedestrian Street.. Can make free long-distance calls.. Pedestrian streets all look the same.. Nearby, there are a few places similar to the old streets of Litan.. The names sound nice.. Cats all look noble.. During the day, you can find a map to go to Shunji, Nanxin for desserts.. Eight yuan double-layer milk pudding tastes richly delicious.. You can walk to the beautiful Stone Chamber Cathedral.. In the evening, you can walk along the Pearl River and feel the breeze.. A 38-yuan ticket can buy you a seat on the first floor of a ferry.. Look at skyscrapers decorated with lights..

Shamian Island.. A place I like very much, like a painting.. Many countries' former embassies in Guangzhou are here.. The architectural styles are beautiful.. People taking wedding photos, foreign car models, and white Lincoln stretch limousines are all beautiful..

Baiyun Mountain Park.. Went to a remote place with nowhere to stay.. Could only check into the reception area of a certain campus of Guangdong University of Foreign Trade and Foreign Languages.. The result was expensive, shabby, and broken.. The only advantage was that the TV could receive many channels.. You could watch good cartoons.. Halfway up Baiyun Mountain, you could see the entire view of Guangzhou.. All were tall buildings.. Only tall buildings..

Huangpu Military Academy.. On the day of departure after checking out, there was still half a day left.. After leaving the luggage at the train station, we entered the subway station.. Since the subway to Huangpu Military Academy was cheaper, we decided to go to Huangpu Military Academy temporarily.. As a result, after getting off the subway station, we had to squeeze onto a bus whose doors almost couldn't close and almost got stuck at the entrance, then went to the dock and took a ferry across the river to reach Changzhou.. Walked to Huangpu Military Academy on the way.. Liked there very much.. A very quiet, clean, and comfortable place.. People who studied at Huangpu Military Academy were really lucky.. There are handsome pictures of Zhou Enlai in the Huangpu Military Academy exhibition hall..

Xiamen..

A place for young people with spare money to pretend to be cultured.. Family hotels on the Ring Island Road in Zengcuo An need to be booked many days in advance.. Prices generally increase during summer vacation.. Many tourists.. Many stores with an artistic atmosphere.. Prices are generally higher.. Almost everything here cannot be bargained over.. Even if the price is high, you cannot bargain.. If you bargain, it means you do not respect the boss pursuing artistry and elegance, which means you do not respect artistry.. So if you want to travel to Xiamen and pretend to be cultured, don't talk about money.. Everyone going to Gulangyu Island must drink coffee.. Drinking bubble tea requires courage.. Therefore, pretentious things are best separated from money.. Otherwise, it feels strange..

Waves, beaches, sunshine, and swimsuits.. The feeling of seeing the sea right after getting off the bus is great.. The seawater here is very clean without any fishy smell.. Love cities with seas.. Feel like the sea is your own home.. On the afternoon I just arrived in Xiamen, I changed into swimwear but didn't swim.. Because there were no girls in the sea.. Only children, foreigners, and men.. Don't know if the local girls aren't open enough or can't swim.. So I had to put on a skirt and return to where I was staying.. Staying in Zengcuo An.. A village gathering cultured youth.. Just across the road, you can see the sea.. Wanted to see the sunrise, woke up at 5:30 am, and found it was already bright outside.. There's a time-honored brand called "Under the Banyan Tree" in the village.. The Satay noodles are very delicious.. Sea clams look nothing like sunflower seeds.. The cheapest bike you can rent is fifty.. Unfortunately, the chain fell off, and I had to pay to fix it myself.. Renting a bike for a day costs fifteen yuan in Guilin but cost one hundred forty yuan in Xiamen.. It could have bought a bike.. However, riding along the sea and along the Li River feels different.. There are many special shops in the village.. Family hotels are beautiful.. Things are a bit pricey.. The elders are very kind.. Mangoes are big..

Xiamen University and Nanputuo Temple are together.. The key point is that they are also next to the sea.. People in Xiamen University are very happy.. Hardly saw any students from Xiamen University.. Inside, they were all tourists.. There's a Lu Xun Memorial Hall.. Elders in Nanputuo Temple give you cups and pour tea water.. The plain cakes inside the temple cost twenty-two yuan.. Outside the gate, identical vendors sell them for ten yuan.. My Buddha is compassionate..

Gulangyu Island.. Going to Gulangyu Island from the pier is free.. Coming back costs eight yuan.. Meaning you come but never return.. Very crowded.. Just after arriving on the island, it feels very much like reaching a bustling commercial street in some city.. You can find the strongly recommended stores mentioned in the travel guides.. Average taste.. Noisy and chaotic.. Tour guides leading groups rushing through sightseeing.. Tourists are all taking photos.. People coming and going.. But on the way to the reserved room, after passing through a cave, you arrive at a completely different place.. Here, it's very quiet, simple, comfortable, and full of cats.. A life isolated from the world.. By seven o'clock in the evening, there's no one on the road.. Only local residents take walks, enjoy the breeze, and chat.. Watch the sky slowly darken.. Time disappears on Gulangyu Island.. Many years later, I will still remember that night many years ago.. I quietly waited for the darkness to fall until late at night in the courtyard.. The scent of jasmine flowers.. A well.. Quiet cats.. The cat's name is Wukong.. Quiet wind.. Light rain after the typhoon.. A smoking woman.. The innkeeper talks to me about relevant and irrelevant matters.. Pictures of a boy.. A stranger sleeping in the bunk below me..

Yingtan..

Didn't know what Yingtan was before.. Went to Jiangxi Yingtan because I didn't want to return the original way from Xiamen.. Also because there's a Longhu Mountain in Yingtan.. The birthplace of Taoism.. Result is, except for stew soup, I didn't like it there.. No reason.. Just didn't like it..

The end of the story..

Elementary school students became junior high school students..

Junior high school students became college students..

College students became graduate students..

Therefore.. The journey continues..
