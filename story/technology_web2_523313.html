Girl, do you understand the boy's predicament?

by fanqimm on 2011-10-27 10:30:38

We are police academy students. We don't have handsome hair; everyone has a buzz cut. We can't do many romantic things, but we truly want a sincere relationship and the chance to be good to one girl...

One of my dorm mates has been pursuing a girl recently. He is always on edge, carefully considering her text messages, worried about making any mistakes. Though he clearly likes her, he doesn't dare to say it out loud. Every day, he saves money by eating frugally just so he can buy her a satisfying gift. Every night, he chats with her until late into the evening, not daring to take a nap. To protect her, he has signed up for the SWAT team and the Sanshou (kickboxing) team... yet they haven't achieved success in their relationship. Watching him every day really moves me deeply. I want to help him share some of the burden, but I realize that I am truly powerless to do so. Perhaps these hardships are something everyone must go through...

Sometimes, you really like someone and are willing to do anything for them. You wish you could declare your feelings loudly, but most times, all we can do is secretly keep it in our hearts because if we say it out loud, we might just get three words in return: "You're crazy." So we can only quietly look at your face from a corner, bold but restrained, not daring to get too close...

So girls, when you encounter a boy who likes you, don't easily say it's impossible. He may not be the prince on a white horse you imagined, but he could be the one who silently protects you, the one who secretly wipes away tears for you. Therefore, cherish the person in front of you. And I hope all boys can find the girl they like...
