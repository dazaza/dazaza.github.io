Xixi International Information Science and Technology Park moves into the Yuhang Innovation Base

by dragonsoft on 2010-07-02 18:09:33

Powerful alliance to create a high-end IT industrial park; Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park moves into the Innovation Base

In the afternoon of the 15th, the signing ceremony for the Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park project led by Longsoft Group moving into the Yuhang Innovation Base in Hangzhou was held on the beautiful West Lake. Standing Committee Member of the District Party Committee and Executive Deputy District Chief Shen Yu delivered an enthusiastic speech at the ceremony. Standing Committee Member of the Hangzhou Municipal Party Committee and Secretary of the Yuhang District Party Committee, Zhu Jinkun attended the celebration meeting held afterward.

Standing Committee Member of the Yuhang District Party Committee and Secretary of the Party Working Committee of the Yuhang Group (Innovation Base), Sun Bingsong presided over the signing ceremony. Assistant District Chief and Director of the Yuhang Innovation Base Management Committee, Shen Wunan, and President of the Shanghai Youth Informatics Talent Association and Chairman of Longsoft Group (China) Co., Ltd., Dai Jianbiao, respectively signed the investment agreement.

Relevant leaders and department heads from Hangzhou Municipality and Yuhang District, responsible persons from towns under the Yuhang Group, and relevant enterprise responsible persons from the Shanghai Youth Informatics Talent Association participated in the signing ceremony, jointly witnessing this important moment in the history of the development of Yuhang's high-tech industry.

The Innovation Base is currently the most promising area with development potential and space for the high-tech industry in Yuhang District and even Hangzhou Municipality. The Yuhang District has positioned it as the main platform to achieve sustainable development and a strategic highland for developing the high-tech industry. Since the establishment of the Innovation Base Management Committee at the end of 2008, around the goal of "a city demonstration zone characterized by higher education, high technology, and noble living standards, reflecting the beauty of nature, humanity, science and technology, and new cities," efforts have been made in aspects such as "building a first-class park, attracting large enterprises with good prospects, constructing public platforms, efficiently promoting projects, and cultivating high-tech industries" to solidly advance the construction of "large platforms, large industries, large projects, and large enterprises."

The Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park project covers about 150 mu (approx. 10 hectares) and includes various business formats such as software development, information service outsourcing, internet commerce, data processing, ICT product research, sales, and services. It features service outsourcing targeting Shanghai and overseas markets, business process outsourcing, and high-end knowledge-based service outsourcing. The park will host more than 3,000 high-tech talents. The leading group of 20 Shanghai IT companies headed by Longsoft Group will be the first batch to move into the park. Established in 2001, Longsoft Group has gradually achieved success and progress in areas such as software outsourcing, financial industry software, network and data integration, software and foreign language education, science and technology park construction and management after nearly seven years of development. In 2009, the total annual output value of the group is expected to reach close to 1 billion yuan. This undoubtedly adds a bold stroke to the early realization of the blueprint planned for the Innovation Base. Because this project will strongly promote the construction of public service platforms including the public data network, data center, and technical support center of the Innovation Base, thereby greatly promoting the significant enhancement and development of the high-tech industry at the Innovation Base. Meanwhile, the geographical location, transportation, environment, and industrial advantages of the Innovation Base will open up a whole new world for the development of the Xixi International Information Technology Industrial Park.

Chairman of Longsoft Group, Mr. Dai Jianbiao, expressed heartfelt gratitude to the Yuhang District for its active support of Longsoft's project investment in the Innovation Base of the Yuhang Group. He said that the construction of the park would play a very positive role in the development of industrial forms in this field in Hangzhou. By relying on the interactive model of domestic and international cooperation and Shanghai-Hangzhou interaction, forming an international industrial chain, signing the investment agreement is just the first step of a long journey. There are still many more detailed tasks ahead. Mr. Dai Jianbiao hopes to work closely and sincerely with the working team of the Yuhang Group to jointly build Hangzhou's information service base.
