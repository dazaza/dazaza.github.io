The "21st Century Middle School Student Composition" magazine is soliciting manuscripts.

by ewfdx4r8 on 2010-04-07 16:53:53

"21st Century Middle School Student Composition" (Teacher's Edition) is a core publication in the national education category and a professional academic journal. Supervisory authority: Hebei Provincial Press and Publication Bureau; Host: Hebei Education Newspaper Office; International Serial Number: ISSN 1672-8327; Domestic Serial Number: CN 13-1347/G4; Postal Distribution Code: 18-219 (Junior High School Teacher's Edition), 18-220 (Senior High School Teacher's Edition), 18-221 (Comprehensive Edition).

To facilitate teachers' publication of educational teaching papers, at the request of many authors, the Teacher's Edition of "21st Century Middle School Student Composition" was changed to a ten-day periodical starting from January 2009. It is divided into three versions: Junior High School Teacher's Edition, Senior High School Teacher's Edition, and Comprehensive Edition. Each version is in 16K format with 80 pages, priced at 6.00 yuan each.

This journal focuses on middle school "Chinese language teaching", highlighting new concepts, viewpoints, experiences, and insights demonstrated by middle school Chinese language teachers in their teaching and nurturing process. It explores new ideas, methods, and characteristics in Chinese language teaching, providing a platform for Chinese language teachers to publish and showcase their achievements, thereby helping them pass the title evaluation smoothly and successfully advance their ranks. (This journal also publishes papers on school management and student management.)

Columns: Textbook Research, Teaching Reform, Examination Guide, Reading Guidance, Writing Analysis, Comprehensive Learning (Oral Communication) Exploration, Poetic Prose Interpretation, Multimedia Application, School Management, Student Management, etc. (Not all columns may be available in each issue, and they can be increased or decreased based on specific circumstances.)

Manuscript Content:

1. Articles by junior high and senior high school (including vocational high schools, technical schools, and colleges) Chinese language teachers regarding research on Chinese textbooks and Chinese teaching.

2. Articles by junior high and senior high school (including vocational high schools, technical schools, and colleges) school leaders and head teachers on school management and student management.
