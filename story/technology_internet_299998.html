Product Description:

[Editor's Recommendation]

The Shinco GM-702C uses the latest Bluetooth technology to perfectly pair with Bluetooth phones, enabling hands-free calls which avoid distractions while driving and improve safety. It can also be used for conference calls, requiring only a Bluetooth phone. It offers an extended call time and clear audio quality, enhancing business efficiency at any time.

[Product Introduction]

1. GPS satellite precise positioning: Equipped with SiRF third-generation 20-channel high-sensitivity receiving chip, with positioning accuracy within 15 meters.

2. Touchscreen LCD: A 7-inch wide-angle, high-definition touchscreen LCD in a 16:9 aspect ratio, with simple Chinese and English menus that can be learned in 5 minutes.

3. Nationwide navigation maps for 1984 cities: Includes professional navigation maps for 1984 cities, with a large database covering road networks, gas stations, hotels, restaurants, shopping, entertainment, traffic lights, and more.

4. FM sound frequency transmission: The audio signal of the navigator is transmitted to the car radio’s FM frequency band, allowing playback through the car audio system for a private cinema experience.

5. Chinese/English voice prompts: During navigation, Chinese/English voice prompts guide the driver without distracting attention, ensuring safe navigation.

6. Multiple destination input methods: Supports pinyin, telephone numbers, points of interest, addresses, crossroads, address books, and historical records as basic destination input methods, with handwriting input and special point advanced quick search modes.

7. Four route search modes: Offers recommended routes, highway priority, distance priority, and general route modes, automatically calculating and navigating based on the set route mode.

8. Detailed route planning: After setting the destination, touch the "road information" button (or "direction icon" during navigation) to display a list of upcoming road names, entrances, and exits.

9. Trip information display: Includes trip information such as vehicle position, target direction, arrival distance, and estimated time, displayed on the LCD screen for easy reference.

10. MP4/MP3 internet audio/video playback: Easily play audio/video files stored on an SD card, transforming the GM-702C into an MP4/MP3 player when not in use for navigation, offering smooth and vivid playback.

11. Hands-free wireless calling: Calling while driving can be dangerous; the GM-702C connects easily with Bluetooth phones for a free and secure hands-free conversation space.

12. Conference calls: Whether indoors, at a construction site, or in the wild, just connect an external power source and pair with a Bluetooth phone to turn the GM-702C into a teleconference system with long call times and clear audio quality.

13. Phone number memory redial: The GM-702C has a memory function for dialed and entered phone numbers. In Bluetooth connection mode, find numbers to redial from the memory list without needing to operate the phone.

14. Audio/Video Input: The GM-702C can also serve as a display, connecting TV, DVD, backup video, and other devices via AV input terminals for expanded functionality.

[Operation Method]

Simple three steps for easy navigation. Example: From "Tiananmen" to "Beijing Station"

Step 1: Set the starting point: After turning on the device, it automatically locates your current position (Tiananmen) as the starting point.

Step 2: Set the destination: Enter the main menu interface, click on the "destination" item, choose "point of interest" (or use handwriting input or other methods to query the destination), find "Beijing Station" from the category menu and click it, then click on the "destination" item again to successfully set the destination.

Step 3: Start navigation: Click on the "navigation" item to complete the process, and the Shinco satellite navigator will guide you smoothly to your destination.

Stabilized power supply, car power connection cable, audio/video connection cable, sandbag, plastic mounting bracket, and touch pen.
