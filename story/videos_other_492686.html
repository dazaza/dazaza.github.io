I. Implementation of Staff Education Plan

1. Retraining on the Administrative Licensing Law from January to March.

2. Safety knowledge training class and "three virtues" education campaign from April to July, celebrating the "May Day" cultural and sports activities, studying the Trade Union Law.

3. Technical training from August to December. Organizing discussions on the "three outlooks" and "three virtues", integrity learning training classes.

4. A systematic study every Friday throughout the year focusing on safety management, engineering quality management, democratic evaluation of conduct, business knowledge training, computer skills training, etc.

II. Specific Measures for Staff Education Work

1. Establishing organizations, improving systems, formulating plans, and compiling programs.

Focusing tightly around the important thought of "Three Represents" and the implementation outline of civic morality, actively developing education in "civic morality" and current political affairs.

To ensure the implementation of staff team building, our station established a staff education leadership group, with one leader among the station's leadership team responsible for this work. A study system was formulated, setting aside every Friday afternoon as study day, which has been consistently maintained. Our station issued the "Provisional Regulations on Staff Education Management" and the "Staff Education Plan", and compiled the "Staff Education Study Plan". We earnestly carried out education in the "Three Represents" and civic morality, organized relevant knowledge tests or exchanges of study insights, striving to improve the ideological education of the staff team. There have been countless good deeds, with no cases of illegal or disciplinary violations by any staff member.

2. Strengthening the technical training of employees, carefully organizing employee technical training.

Employee technical training arrangements focus on selecting young employees, with the goal of obtaining higher academic degrees. For this purpose, our station established a special educational guidance group, with one station leader responsible for this work. On November 4th of this year, we specially organized nine employees to participate in administrative law enforcement training and pre-job training organized by provincial and municipal authorities, receiving unanimous praise from provincial and municipal leaders.

3. Carefully organizing the "Four-Five" legal education plan, implementing the annual legal education plan, further enhancing employees' legal awareness.

Regarding the "Four-Five" legal education work throughout the year, our station specifically established a "Four-Five" legal education leadership group, formulated the "Four-Five Legal Education Plan", compiled an annual legal education plan, and carefully organized its implementation. First, we seriously studied Comrade Jiang Zemin's strategy of governing the country according to law, carefully studied channel laws and regulations and related business knowledge, enhancing the improvement of business theoretical levels. Second, we conducted publicity and education on basic legal knowledge centered on the constitution, educating employees to exercise rights, fulfill obligations, maintain social stability, and protect their own legitimate rights according to law. Through the "Two-Five" and "Three-Five" legal education, the legal knowledge of all employees in the station has generally improved, achieving excellent social benefits.

III. Significant Achievements in Staff Education Work

Due to our station's emphasis on vocational education work, strengthening leadership over this work, effective measures, and proper implementation, certain achievements have been made in vocational education work, providing guarantees for the completion of this year's "two civilizations" construction tasks.

1. The overall quality of the employee team has improved;

2. The anti-corruption work has shown significant results, with no occurrence of illegal or disciplinary violations by employees;

3. Employees have enhanced democratic and legal awareness, with no cases of illegal or criminal behavior;

4. The socialist spiritual appearance of employees has been demonstrated, with countless good deeds such as donations to poor villages.

Our station has done a large amount of work in vocational education, achieving certain results, but there is still a gap compared to higher-level requirements. In the new year, we will build on our achievements, overcome shortcomings, take some practical measures, and continue to focus on employee education work, striving to elevate our station's employee education work to a new level.
