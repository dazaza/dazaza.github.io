1 Anhui Energy Power Non-public Issuance of A Shares Stock Prospectus

Special Notes

1. Relevant matters of this non-public issuance have been reviewed and approved by the 25th temporary meeting of the sixth board of directors.

2. The issue objects for this issuance shall not exceed ten specific investors, including the controlling shareholder of the company, Waneng Group, as well as securities investment fund management companies, securities firms, insurance institutional investors, trust investment companies, financial companies, qualified foreign institutional investors, etc., which meet the regulations of the CSRC and the company's recognized conditions for legal persons, natural persons, or other eligible investors.

Except for Waneng Group, after obtaining the approval document for this issuance, the company's board will determine other issue objects according to the authorization of the shareholders' meeting and the relevant regulations of the CSRC, in consultation with the lead underwriter.

Waneng Group plans to subscribe to the company's shares issued this time in cash, with the number of shares it intends to subscribe being not less than 10% (including 10%) of the total number of shares issued this time by the company, and not exceeding 30% (including 30%) of the total number of shares issued this time by the company.

The shares subscribed by Waneng Group in this issuance shall not be transferred within 36 months from the end of the issuance; the shares subscribed by other investors in this issuance shall not be transferred within 12 months from the end of the issuance.

3. The pricing benchmark date for this non-public issuance of stocks is the announcement date of the resolution of the 25th temporary meeting of the sixth board of directors, namely February 24, 2012. The issuance price shall be no less than 90% of the average trading price of the company's stocks for the 20 trading days prior to the pricing benchmark date, i.e., 4.74 yuan per share. If the company issues dividends, sends bonus shares, or increases capital during the period from the pricing benchmark date to the issuance date, the lower limit of the issuance price will also be adjusted accordingly.

4. The number of shares issued this time by the company shall not exceed 380 million shares. The board will request the shareholders' meeting to authorize the board to determine the final issuance price and issuance quantity in consultation with the lead underwriter based on relevant laws and regulations and regulatory requirements, considering the bidding results and actual circumstances at the time of issuance. If the company issues dividends, sends bonus shares, or increases capital during the period from the pricing benchmark date to the issuance date, the upper limit of the number of shares issued this time will be adjusted accordingly.

As of the date of issuance of this prospectus, the audit and evaluation work involved in the proposed investment projects for the funds raised in this non-public issuance are ongoing. The audited historical financial data, asset evaluation results, and verified profit forecast data of the target assets will be disclosed in the supplementary announcement of the issuance prospectus. The company will convene another board meeting to deliberate the supplementary announcement of the issuance prospectus and other related matters, and submit them for review at the shareholders' meeting.

6. This non-public issuance plan for stocks still needs to be approved by the company's shareholders' meeting, the Anhui Provincial SASAC, and the CSRC before it can be implemented.

Two, Background and Purpose of This Non-public Issuance

(One) Background of This Non-public Issuance

1. Achieving energy saving and emission reduction is one of the important goals of China's power industry development.

China has become a major global energy consumer, with carbon emissions ranking first in the world. The Chinese government committed before the Copenhagen Climate Summit to reduce carbon emissions per unit of GDP by 40%-45% compared to 2005 levels by 2020. On the other hand, China's power industry currently remains dominated by thermal power, with coal as the main raw material for thermal power industries. Therefore, whether it is energy consumption or gas pollutant emissions, the power industry occupies an important position and is a key area for China's energy saving and emission reduction efforts. To achieve the energy saving and emission reduction plan, China's power industry vigorously develops green and low-carbon energy and implements environmental protection policies such as "shutting down small units and building large ones." Through this non-public issuance, the company will effectively increase the proportion of large-capacity thermal power units and possess nuclear energy investments, actively responding to the national call for energy saving, emission reduction, and green environmental protection.

2. Improve fuel procurement channels and adapt to the requirements of the capital market for overall listing.

In recent years, the tight supply and demand situation for electric coal has spread from local areas to the whole country. Currently, part of the coal used by the company's subsidiaries Waneng Tongling Company and Waneng Hefei Company is entrusted to the associated enterprise Electric Fuel Company for procurement and supply. Through the acquisition behavior of this non-public issuance fundraising, the Electric Fuel Company will be included in the listed company scope, thereby effectively ensuring fuel procurement and further controlling procurement costs, while also meeting the requirements of the capital market for overall listing.

1. Improve asset structure and enhance the company's operating strength.
