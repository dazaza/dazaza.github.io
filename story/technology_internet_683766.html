Acrylic short fiber 1.5D×38mm, acrylic tow 3D×102mm prices were both adjusted, CPL prices temporarily stable, nylon 6 chips prices trend increased. The market for polyester filament was still sluggish, with average transactions. Market prices remained stable, but the sales of different varieties were uneven. In Shengze, Tongxiang, Taicang, polyester filament quotations were all temporarily stable. For example, a mainstream factory's POY 75D/72F quoted at 13,450 RMB/T locally, however, in other places, the actual bulk transaction of semi-dull FDY was mainly based on negotiation between both parties, with a discount of 50-100 RMB/T from the quoted price. Currently, downstream enterprises are still very cautious in purchasing, lacking purchasing power support, the polyester filament market is not optimistic. Spinning mills' sales situation varies greatly. Coupled with the volatile adjustment of upstream raw materials, spinning mills have high polyester filament inventory. To strive for market share, the promotional sale will slowly extend. Most people believe that the overall polyester filament market will continue to show a weak trend, and it cannot be ruled out that filament prices may have a small downward process. The trading volume of 100% cotton yarns is not large, but the variety price trend continues to rise. From the perspective of product sales, there is a good demand for 32s cotton yarns, mainly used for jet-spun nylon-cotton series. Other specifications have relatively ordinary transactions. In blended yarn products, individual varieties of polyester-cotton yarn prices continue to adjust upwards; polyester-viscose yarn series products prices remain stable with some increase, but the transaction volume is not large. Pure polyester yarn prices show an upward trend, but the transaction volume is not large. Polyester short fiber prices temporarily stabilize and consolidate. Rayon yarn market continues to rise, prices show a comprehensive upward trend. Viscose short fiber prices in the market continue to be firm and upward. It is expected that the rayon yarn market will have an upward adjustment trend in the future.
