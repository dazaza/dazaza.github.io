[Sohu Auto Suzhou Station] On February 25, 2012, the much-anticipated launch event for the urban compact car - Kia K2 hatchback - was grandly held at Suzhou Impression City. The atmosphere at the event clearly demonstrated the popularity of the K2 hatchback as many enthusiasts gathered to witness its superior quality. Clients checked in, and the band delivered an energetic performance. A leader from Kia's East China region gave opening remarks.

The newly launched K2 hatchback offers two engine displacements - 1.4L and 1.6L - across four models, with prices ranging from RMB 72,900 to RMB 99,900. There are eight color options available, including champagne gold and diamond silver. For vehicle violation inquiries in Jiuquan, please visit http://www.biyaode.com/jiuquan/.

A lively dance performance preceded the unveiling of the new car. As a punctual person, the editor arrived early at the event venue. Even before the event began, customers were already showing great interest in the new car. At 2 PM, the band's passionate performance drew a large crowd to the stage. The event officially commenced with an invigorating dance that highlighted the youthful and vibrant characteristics of the Kia K2, pushing the activity to its climax. At this moment, our star, the K2 hatchback, revealed its mysterious veil.

A top sales consultant provided a detailed analysis of the K2 hatchback. In response to customer requests, the new car's price and special on-site promotional policies were announced, leading some potential customers to place orders on the spot. Besides the captivating performances, the event also included a lottery segment with generous prizes such as car models, electronic photo albums, and mobile phones, ensuring every guest had a delightful experience.

The top sales consultant conducted a thorough presentation of the new K2 hatchback. The first owner of the K2 hatchback in Suzhou received their car at the event.

As the first two-box model in the K series, the K2 hatchback not only inherits the refined, tasteful, and sporty style of the "K" series but also fully showcases the inherent fashion and vitality of a two-box car. With its dynamic appearance, spacious and versatile interior, agile and convenient handling, and advanced high-tech features, the K2 hatchback further meets the differentiated needs of young consumers for automobile use, providing a high-quality driving experience for urban travel.
