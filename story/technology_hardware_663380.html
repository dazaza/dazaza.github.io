Underfloor heating in the scorching summer

by aincemissn on 2012-02-11 15:54:11

Our station news: "As long as you have a remote control or remote control software, you can easily control the lights, temperature, and music in your home, enjoying a safer, more comfortable, and more energy-efficient living environment...". The much-anticipated "thinking residence" - Taiping Baodi (community) intelligent model room was officially opened to the public on September 20th. The 143 square meter intelligent model room gave homeowners a new understanding of smart homes.

On the morning of September 20th, the Taiping Baodi (community) smart home press conference and model room opening ceremony was held at the project sales office. Representatives from the developer and homeowner representatives gathered together to witness this special moment. At the press conference, the relevant representatives of the project introduced in detail to the homeowners the sales situation, engineering progress, and engineering material parameters of Taiping Baodi. They also invited homeowner representatives to visit the carefully crafted 143 square meter smart home model room.

"Previously, I didn't understand what intelligence meant, but today I finally experienced it. It's almost too convenient. With just a light touch of a button, our lives can become simple and easy. Moreover, it can be remotely controlled through a network, allowing us to set up a comfortable environment before even entering the door. Coming home is pure enjoyment..." Mr. Zhang wanted to buy a house for his parents. After experiencing the "intelligent home" presented by the Taiping Baodi model room, he became even more determined to make the purchase. "Especially the intelligent alarm system; having our parents live in such an environment gives us greater peace of mind."

Mr. Yin Hui, the marketing vice president of Taiping Baodi, introduced the project situation, photographed by HOUSE365.

The on-site engineering technician explained: "Traditional residences focus on spatial structure as the hard living function of the residence, while the 'thinking residence' emphasizes soft living functions based on the hard living functions of the residence. Simply put, it creates a safer, more comfortable, and more energy-efficient living environment. We install a set of 'smart home management systems' in the residence. Through this intelligent residential management system, you can control all the lights in your home with just one remote control, achieving one-touch switches, full-on/full-off, and one-button guest/dining/cinema/entertainment lighting scenes. Appliances can be remotely turned on and universally controlled. In the hot summer, you can make a call to turn on the air conditioner in advance. In the cold winter, you can turn on the underfloor heating or water heater in advance. This includes remote control and timed control of curtains. If there is a break-in, the system will detect the danger and call the homeowner to alert them. You can also choose different audio sources and volume levels in different rooms based on the preferences of family members, enjoying different music programs such as light music, opera, and popular songs."

The entire Taiping Baodi project offers main unit sizes ranging from 90 square meters to 400 square meters. On the opening day, the smaller units were almost sold out, leaving only the mainstream large units over 144 square meters available for buyers to choose from. Each household has an entrance garden, all are pure slab-style layouts, ensuring the best sun exposure for each unit. Currently, the average price is around 5,300 yuan per square meter, which is basically in line with the prices of ordinary housing in North Bridge, further highlighting the high cost-effectiveness of the technology-based residences.

In addition, Taiping Baodi is located in the core area of Taishan New Village, known as the "Xinjiekou" of North Jiangsu. It is surrounded by four major banks: Industrial and Commercial Bank, Agricultural Bank, Construction Bank, and Communications Bank. Nanjing No. 17 Middle School, Nanjing University, Southeast University, and other universities are nearby. Taishan Hospital, Suguo Supermarket, and farmers' markets are within close proximity. It is also adjacent to Huadong Mall, offering comprehensive living amenities. In terms of transportation, it takes only a half-hour drive to reach the Xinjiekou business district. There are 12 bus routes distributed nearby, providing convenient access in all directions. Additionally, the planned Torch South Road Station of Metro Line 3 connects tranquility and prosperity.

Besides its excellent location advantages, Taiping Baodi boasts a unique natural environment that deserves praise. Backed by the Laoshan Mountains, it is surrounded by Bota Mountain Park, Daping Mountain, and Xiaoping Mountain. It is also adjacent to the "incense and blessing land" of Taishan Temple. Therefore, the location of Taiping Baodi can be called a "mountain-water blessing land," with an undeniable living environment.

Mr. Yin Hui, the marketing vice president of the Taiping Baidi project, introduced that "people-oriented" is the design concept of the Taiping Baidi project. It aims to help homeowners transition from "having a place to live" to "living well." Currently, the main units for sale at Taiping Baidi are three-bedroom units over 140 square meters, with an average price of 5,500 yuan per square meter, offering extremely high cost-effectiveness in the current North Bridge market. Previously, homebuyers could not directly understand the true meaning of "intelligent residences." With the introduction of the model room, homebuyers can experience the future comfortable life in advance. (HOUSE365 He Yipei)
