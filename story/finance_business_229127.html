Introduction to the full plot of "Queen Seondeok":

"Queen Seondeok" tells the story of Princess Dae Man (played by Lee Yo Won), who was abandoned as a child due to an ominous prophecy. Through her relentless efforts and navigating power struggles within the palace, she grows to become the first queen of Silla - Queen Seondeok.

Under the control of Consort Mishil (played by Go Hyun Jung) in the harem, King Jinpyeong and Lady Maya gave birth to twin daughters. However, for the king, giving birth to twins is considered extremely unlucky, symbolizing the omen of "the end of the royal male line." Out of necessity, King Jinpyeong sent someone to take the younger daughter Dae Man away, but Mishil dispatched her people to secretly pursue and kill her. At a critical moment, Dae Man was rescued by the legendary Hwarang figure, Venu, through his clever tactics. He then handed Dae Man over to be raised by King Jinpyeong's maid, Cho Hwa. Cho Hwa took Princess Dae Man to the desert regions of China and raised her in a small inn.

Princess Dae Man's greatest rival, Mishil, used her extraordinary beauty to gain the favor of King Jinheung and thus controlled the power in the palace. Filled with ambition, after the death of King Jinheung, Mishil installed the young King Jinpyeong on the throne. When the wife of King Jinpyeong gave birth to twin daughters, Mishil planned to use the lives of these twin daughters to threaten King Jinpyeong and directly seize royal authority. However, Venu and Cho Hwa saved Princess Dae Man, thwarting her plan. Despite this, Mishil, holding great power, did not abandon her pursuit of the throne and conspired to have her husband ascend it. She viewed Dae Man's twin sister, Princess Cheonmyeong (played by Park Yi Jin), as a thorn in her side.

Under Mishil's rule, Princess Cheonmyeong displayed extraordinary wisdom and quick judgment but rarely revealed her true nature. However, she would always showcase her leadership abilities at crucial moments. Although Princess Cheonmyeong married Kim Yongsu (played by Park Jung Cheol) and bore a son, Kim Chunsu (played by Yoo Seung Ho), she developed feelings for Kim Yu-shin (played by Um Tae-woong), who had once saved her life. Kim Yu-shin, a renowned general of Silla, possessed exceptional martial arts skills. After accidentally rescuing Princess Cheonmyeong from an assassination attempt orchestrated by Mishil's confidants, he met Princess Dae Man under her arrangements. While assisting Princess Dae Man in realizing her grand dream of unifying the Three Kingdoms, Kim Yu-shin developed affection for Dae Man and became her loyal supporter. With the help of Kim Yu-shin, Princess Dae Man gradually dismantled Mishil's influence and ascended to the throne as the first queen of Silla.
