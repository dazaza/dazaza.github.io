My poor brother!

by sgsf0759u on 2010-04-11 12:53:30

I have a brother, he changed my fate! So in 2003 I was admitted to Suzhou Medical College and stood at the starting line again. In that summer vacation, Bu Duo Jian, my dear brother had an accident and fell off the building! I still remember, that night the telephone rang shrilly breaking the quiet night, we were woken up, because there are four elders in our family so we were most afraid of the midnight phone calls, fearing that they might have some accidents! Mother hurriedly answered the call, it was from auntie. I quietly listened on the bed, but only knew that my brother had something happened. I thought he had a car accident. Mother went to the hospital, I still lay on the bed, not sleeping, kept thinking, if my brother became disabled, what can I do for him? If he became disabled and no one wanted him, I would take him with me and take care of him! Fortunately, the fact was not so bad. He fell from the second floor and just got some flesh wounds! I think my silly brother does not know how much I care about him, because we still insult each other when we meet! After the internship ended, I applied for a job in Xuzhou. Mother, auntie, and my brother as the relatives group cheered for me. That day I felt very happy! My mother did not give me a biological brother, but my auntie gave me a biological brother!
