Palai's (Noon) parents died in a car accident, but Palai believes that her aunt and the doctor, who is her aunt's friend, conspired to kill them in order to get hold of the family property. Palai has been studying overseas for a long time. She came back for the funeral and decided to take revenge.

She decided to start with her cousin's boyfriend Siva (Paul). Since her property had been seized by her aunt, she was treated as a servant at home. Therefore, she asked Siva to marry her. The reason she gave was that if he didn't marry her, she would hand over the evidence of the doctor — Siva's father — being involved in the crime to the police. Palai had already placed the evidence on a website she created herself, and only she knew the password. Helpless, Siva agreed.

Palai moved into one of Siva's annexes. They made an agreement that no one should know about it. If Siva could make her satisfied, he would get the password to delete the incriminating evidence. Thus began their comedic yet heartwarming drama of sparring partners.

Of course, Siva was not willing to be manipulated by Palai. He always tried to outsmart her and gradually fell in love with her during their verbal spars. One day, he kidnapped Palai to a villa and raped her (a classic Thai drama plot, alas~). Later, Palai got pregnant. She was also moved by Siva and fell in love with him. In the end, the aunt's crimes were exposed, Siva's father committed suicide by gunshot, and Siva left in sorrow. Palai found a love website left by Siva: www.palaimylove.com (this is truly classic and very touching), which moved her to tears. Siva specially arranged a unique "password" reunion, and finally, the lovers were united!
