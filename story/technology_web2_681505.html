Mission County "four or five" year work summary: The # # League Committee has always attached great importance to adolescent legal education according to law, and has seriously implemented the county's "four or five" year plan. The Mission Committee developed a "# # County Juvenile Legal Publicity and Education in the Fourth Five-Year Plan", organizing, planning, and step by step implementing the entire legal system and governance according to law, effectively ensuring that the content of the "four or five" year plan is realized. In 5 years, we have followed the legal work key points and implementation details as requested, working along two lines, focusing on cadre and juvenile legal education, playing various round organizational work advantages, organizing a series of rich and colorful activities with very fruitful results.

One, Improve knowledge, strengthen leadership, coordinate arrangements for the county juvenile legal education according to law. The level of youth legal concept and legal knowledge directly determines the progress of ruling the county policy implementation. # # Mission County regards the entire county's youth leadership, strengthens the legal education of young people and the administration of jobs lawfully, placing significant emphasis on this work, overall planning, firmly catching up with fulfillment.

One is the formation of mechanism, strengthening leadership. Mission County, under the organization of the "under four or five" year plan, formed a delegation headed by the Secretary of the County Party Committee in charge, the deputy head of the office, responsible for daily work, strengthening leadership significantly. The League Secretary of the County Party Committee Office listens to a special report on the administration of jobs lawfully each quarter, summarizing the first phase of work and deploying the next phase of work.

Two is overall planning, unified deployment. The Mission County Party Committee placed the legal system according to law, joined the work focus, included in the committee work report and annual working points, making clear the county regiment of youth organizations' focus, key points, and basic points in law and governance according to law, providing overall planning and unified deployment for the entire legal system according to law.

Three sound system, perfect mechanism. We established a monthly report, quarterly report information feedback mechanism, timely finding and summarizing good experiences and typical cases from the grassroots units conducting legal work according to law, and promoting them throughout the county through the "# # Team Monthly", timely implementing ranking requirements and relevant spirits to various round organizations. We set up an end-of-year supervision and inspection mechanism, and organized grassroots groups to sign responsibility forms on juvenile legal education according to law, assessing at the end, effectively mobilizing the base to carry out work enthusiasm.

Two, Strengthening legal knowledge popularization education, enhancing legal ideas, improving teenagers' legal quality. First, dividing the youth levels, determine different contents of legal education. The Mission County Party Committee issued a "two 00 four years working point", taking legal knowledge and Deng Xiaoping Theory as five key elements for youth in the county, and identified constitutional knowledge, civil work, and life closely related basic legal knowledge, and legal knowledge related to maintaining social stability for education among Youth League members, enhancing young people's awareness to maintain the dignity of the law and use legal weapons to fight against illegal and criminal acts; for the vast number of children, educating them on the prevention of juvenile crime law and other relevant legal knowledge, helping them cultivate law-abiding good habits, enhancing their ability to distinguish right from wrong and protect their legitimate rights and interests according to law.
