How to Get Rid of Elephant Legs After Childbirth

by ciaifuke on 2012-03-05 16:44:25

Many women after childbirth either develop "elephant legs" or become very slim. So, how can one get rid of those unwanted elephant legs post-delivery? Thick calves make skirts or boots look unappealing. How can we achieve more even calf lines?

Let's take a look at different ways to slim down various types of thick calves:

I. Slimming Solution for Fatty Legs:

Judgment Criteria: Pinching the leg and being able to grab a lump of fat. The legs appear chubby and are quite disproportionate to the actual body weight.

Slimming Points: Besides exercise, there is nothing more effective than avoiding high sugar and high oil foods. Eat more low-calorie seafood and vegetables, eat fewer snacks, and do more aerobic exercises like running, swimming, cycling, which can eliminate excess fat on the legs.

Slimming Method One: Postpartum Leg Calisthenics

Start doing leg calisthenics post-delivery from the fifth day till the end of the month. This will help in exercising leg muscles and improving the return flow of venous blood in the lower limbs. Sit on the ground with both legs stretched out and together, back straight, arms extended behind you, fingers spread supporting the ground. When inhaling, try to curl your toes as much as possible; when exhaling, stretch them out completely. Then lie on your back, stretch both legs slightly apart, place your arms at your sides. Inhale while bending your left leg towards your chest with your toes stretched out; exhale while stretching your left leg straight, making it perpendicular to your upper body, with your toes curled up. Alternate between both legs.

Calisthenics are suitable for mothers who have had normal deliveries. Since new mothers tend to be weak, it's important to exercise according to your specific situation, not rushing into things. Each set of exercises should last 2-3 minutes, twice a day in the morning and evening. Pay special attention to coordinating your breathing with the movements. After the first month, you can engage in various muscle group exercises to restore the strength and elasticity of thigh muscles. Suitable exercises include slow jogging, leg extension and flexion exercises, swimming, etc.

Slimming Method Two: Gua Sha (Scraping) Leg Slimming Technique

After bathing every night, apply baby oil or olive oil with lubricating properties on your calves. Use a gua sha board (or the back of a wooden comb, a smooth spoon, etc.) to vigorously scrape the calf muscles from the ankle all the way up to the knee bend. Do this 100 times on each leg.

This should be done after bathing. It's best to drink a little warm water after scraping. If you can also do some air bicycle riding or scissor leg exercises (lying in bed, lifting your legs, stretching them out, opening them as wide as possible, pausing for a few seconds, then closing them like scissors), if you're not aiming to lose weight across your whole body, there's no special dietary restriction. Just eat less greasy and sugary food. If you can reduce your dinner intake, you can slim down faster.

Effect: Generally, people will notice their leg circumference reduced by about one size after persisting for two weeks to a month.

Plan Three: Liposuction for Legs

To solve the problem of fatty accumulation in the calves, you can also opt for leg liposuction, though this process may be somewhat painful.

The common method involves making a 0.5 cm incision inside the ankle or at the popliteal fossa, injecting swelling anesthesia, using ultrasound outside the body to break down and dissolve fat cells, then sucking them out with negative pressure. The surgery time is controlled based on the amount of fat in the calves, generally requiring around 1.5 hours for both calves.

The surgery removes fat cells from the body, compared to methods such as exercise, dieting, and medication for weight loss, it significantly reduces the number of fat cells, so there won't be any rebound effect after the operation.

The cost is approximately 3000-4000 yuan.

II. Slimming Solution for Varicose Veins or "Edema" Legs

Judgment Criteria: Pressing the calf muscles with your hand results in poor and slow skin rebound. Such thick legs belong to this type.

Slimming Points: Avoid fast food, vacuum-packed food, pastries, and salty snacks. Don't drink carbonated beverages like cola or Sprite. Don't stay in one position for more than an hour. Doing simple muscle-strengthening exercises can improve edema conditions.

Plan One: 10-Minute Quick Slimming Method

Thick calves are often due to edema. To make your legs look more perfect, massage them quickly 10 minutes before a date. After massaging, allow the body to expel accumulated waste products, then use slimming cream, lift your legs high and rest for a moment, and your leg size can shrink by 2-3 cm.

1. Alternate hands for stroking. Place both palms entirely around the calf area and alternate stroking upwards with both hands. Perform this 10 times.

2. Twist and rub with both hands. Tighten both palms around the calf muscles and twist to rub continuously until the entire calf becomes warm.

Plan Two: High-Leg Lift Method

After sitting for a long time, place your legs higher than your heart. Before sleeping at night, you can lean your legs against the wall or elevate them 20-30 cm while sleeping. Lie on your side, supporting your upper body with your hand. Bend one leg and keep the other straight. Then repeatedly lift the bent leg, perform this 10 times, turn to the other side, and do the same action with the other leg.

Plan Three: Slapping and Massage Method

Slap and massage along the lymph nodes of the leg to make the lymph nodes and blood circulation smoother. Moderate massage can eliminate the heavy feeling and edema in the legs.

From the ankle, start slapping rhythmically upwards towards the back of the knee with both hands. After finishing the right leg, do the same with the left leg.

When deciding to choose this leg-slimming method, you should massage at least twice a week, and each massage session should last over an hour to achieve the desired effect. You can do this while watching TV at night.
