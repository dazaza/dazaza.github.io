In "The King of Wars", a hero doesn't need to have a distinguished origin, and a rascal can also become a general.

by jqrxass on 2009-09-08 20:51:27

You Fei, a street thug, roamed in the羊city (Guangzhou), with six brothers under his command. They made their living by collecting "loan shark money". On August 8, 1937, he forcibly married a young and innocent girl named Xie Pei Shan. However, on their wedding night, he himself became someone else's prey - forcibly conscripted into the National Army... After the Battle of Songhu, the main forces of the 259th Brigade and the 137th Regiment were almost wiped out, and both the brigade and regiment commanders also died heroically. Following the last wishes of Brigade Commander Yi, the remaining troops formed an independent company...

Part One

The thunder roared like an angry beast, its sound far surpassing that of the cannons at Humen. The continuous thunder caused people’s ears to ring. A strong wind arose, sand and stones flew everywhere, and people hurriedly sought shelter under eaves. The vendors scrambled to pack up their stalls, and the streets were in chaos. Bean-sized raindrops "pattered" onto the ground. Perhaps at this moment, heaven was looking down upon the earth, smugly watching the pitiful humans struggle helplessly against its mighty power. Soon, the city of Guangzhou was draped in silver curtains of water. The once very noisy streets suddenly fell silent, except for one thing – the rain was laughing smugly.

Part Two

Ding Mao chuckled heartily, patted You Fei on the shoulder without saying anything more, and began cleaning his precious gun again. When Ding Mao cleaned his machine gun, it was as if he was treating a beloved lover, gentle and meticulous, completely unlike You Fei who clenched his teeth in frustration. Seeing Ding Mao's demeanor, You Fei beside him felt uneasy inside. Frowning, he said, "What kind of expression is that, Ding Mao? Acting like a woman, I feel like throwing up just looking at you!"

Part Three

The Japanese reinforcements arrived quite quickly, but the Chinese army seemed to have suddenly taken some kind of stimulant. Small groups of troops were everywhere harassing the Japanese soldiers. The Japanese going to reinforce what You Fei called the No.1 Sentry Post would occasionally be picked off by sniper fire from the roadside. By the time they rushed there yelling, the place was always deserted. If they ignored these harassments, the marching Japanese soldiers would fall down, and almost none of them could get back up. Such meaningless losses were unacceptable to the Japanese officers.

Part Four

Wang Lie also frowned and said, "This problem is quite tricky. I think we won't be able to solve it in the short term. It's not easy to make so many uniforms quickly; where are we going to get the fabric from? But providing each person with a cap should be no problem. I'll arrange it as soon as possible, trying to make the clothing of various companies as uniform as possible. Everyone will have a chest badge sewn on their chest, and the number of the 259th Brigade will surely make them feel proud."

Part Five

Xu Zhiwei was muttering something incomprehensible. Finally, he waved to indicate that the guards could leave. The little guard ran off as if fleeing, and Xu Zhiwei became tense after hearing that You Fei was coming. As long as You Fei was around, he always felt constrained in whatever he did. It seemed that tomorrow, a good fight was necessary; otherwise, once the older brother came, things wouldn't go according to his own plans anymore. Xu Zhiwei secretly resolved in his mind.
