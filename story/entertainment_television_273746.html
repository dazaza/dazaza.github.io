Introduction to the TV drama "Promise" - Episode-by-episode summary (Episodes 1-26, complete series) and the grand finale (original name: True Lies)

TV Drama "Promise"

Original Name: "True Lies"

Episodes: 26

Main Cast: Wang Ting, Sui Junbo, Yu Zhen

The TV drama "Promise" is adapted from the salvage event of the "Nanhai No. 1". The two male leads are portrayed by Wang Ting, known for his tough guy roles in "Major Case Squad 6" and "Warrior," and Yu Zhen, who has gained popularity for his versatile spy roles in "Special Agent Group 5" and "Black Rose."

Plot Introduction of "Promise":

The South China Sea underwater archaeology team's "06" survey ship sets out to the South China Sea to conduct the final reconnaissance and positioning of a sunken ship loaded with Yuan Dynasty porcelain.

That night, while the "06" survey ship was anchored in the target sea area, it was rammed by an unidentified nationality cargo ship, resulting in its destruction. Of the 12 team members, only Wang Ci and Peng Hu were rescued after drifting at sea for 72 hours, while the rest of the crew tragically perished.
