Wu Dong Qian Kun, Chapter 127: Fierce Battle

by fanyete on 2011-09-15 22:34:58

Wei Tong's ferocious shout did not affect Lin Dong in the slightest, as he was fully focused on controlling his powerful spiritual energy to coalesce before him. Under his urging, the fluctuations emitted by the "Pointed Spiral Wave" became increasingly fierce.

"Whoo!"

The "Pointed Spiral Wave" spun like a storm at high speed, shaking the air and emitting a whistling sound that pierced the ears and reverberated around.

On the field, Wei Tong also glared at Lin Dong with a cold expression. Robust Yuan Qi swirled around him, and the momentum of the Yuan Dan realm reached its full potential under his control.

Wei Tong could sense the strength of Lin Dong's current attack, so he held nothing back either. His fists slowly spread apart, and a formidable Pure Yuan Gang Qi rapidly condensed in his palms. In the blink of an eye, his palms became dazzling with an extremely aggressive fluctuation spreading out!

"Thousand Cleave Palm!"

In the stands, many who were familiar with Wei Tong were surprised by his actions, their gazes turning astonished as they looked towards Lin Dong, clearly not expecting him to be able to push Wei Tong this far.

Lin Zhen Tian and others also furrowed their brows at this moment, worry showing on their faces. Although they didn't know how powerful this so-called "Thousand Cleave Palm" was, they could feel how terrifying the gathered palm wind was.

"The Thousand Cleave Palm is only a fourth-rank martial art, but it is extremely aggressive, considered one of Wei Tong's strongest killing moves. With this move, he once killed three experts of the Yuan Dan realm," Xuan Su said beside them, her tone somewhat serious.

Upon hearing these words, Lin Zhen Tian and others, already quite worried, felt their hearts jump again, their fists slowly clenching.

"Young man, die!"

Amidst the numerous startled exclamations, Wei Tong's palms became even more dazzling. The Yuan Qi fluctuations were so formidable they made one's heart tremble. Just as the Yuan Qi fluctuations reached their peak, he gave a sinister smile and brought his palms together forcefully, then swung down as if splitting a mountain!

"Hoong!"

As Wei Tong's palms came down like an axe, the brilliant light instantly transformed into a beam several yards wide, shooting out violently!

This beam contained extremely strong Pure Yuan Gang Qi, stretching and contracting unpredictably, resembling a giant blade that split mountains, sharp enough to make one's heart chill.

"Pointed Spiral Wave!"

When Wei Tong's fierce beam shot out from his palms, Lin Dong's eyes turned cold instantly. His finger pointed out abruptly, causing the "Pointed Spiral Wave" before him, which had been prepared, to spin wildly. Then, like the tip of a storm, it broke through the sky with a thunderous whistling sound, charging head-on towards Wei Tong's fierce and aggressive beam.

On the viewing platform, countless pairs of eyes watched tensely as the two exceptionally fierce attacks clashed. This was a collision between spiritual energy and Yuan Qi!

The speed of both attacks was horrifyingly fast. Many could only see a flash of light before the two fierce attacks collided like meteors.

"Bang!"

At the moment of impact, a loud boom resounded across the arena. Powerful Yuan Qi and spiritual energy shockwaves surged out in rings, finally causing the invisible spiritual barrier covering the arena to ripple continuously.

Countless people squinted their eyes, watching the brilliant light sphere in the middle of the collision, which emitted an extremely fierce wind.

The light sphere was distinct. Robust spiritual energy and fierce Pure Yuan Gang Qi eroded and dissolved each other upon contact, as if trying to swallow the other whole.

In the initial confrontation, the "Pointed Spiral Wave" did not achieve much success, instead retreating step by step under the fierce Pure Yuan Gang Qi attack, continuously being pushed back towards Lin Dong.

Judging by this appearance, it seemed that Lin Dong's spiritual energy had not achieved much effect.

In the stands, Lin Zhen Tian and others' expressions changed slightly due to this scene. Lin Dong prided himself on his spiritual energy; could it be that even his spiritual energy couldn't withstand Wei Tong now?

"Spiritual energy is inherently not as fierce or aggressive as Pure Yuan Gang Qi, so this situation isn't surprising. Everything depends on the outcome," Master Yan said with a faint smile.

Hearing this from someone considered a master-level expert, Lin Zhen Tian and others felt somewhat reassured.

Meanwhile, as they spoke, Lin Dong's "Pointed Spiral Wave" continued to retreat step by step, almost about to be directly struck by the beam. However, Lin Dong did not retreat. His face remained solemn as he extended a finger again!

With Lin Dong's finger pointing out, the "Pointed Spiral Wave," which had been retreating step by step, suddenly paused. Then, the hidden formidable power within it erupted completely!

"Hoong!"

Invisible ripples spread across the sky. Then, to everyone's astonishment, the previously weak and powerless spiritual energy shockwave exploded with an intense counterattack force. In the blink of an eye, it dispersed one-third of Wei Tong's fierce beam. The shockwave's momentum did not diminish, continuing at an extremely fast speed toward the retreating Wei Tong below.

Seeing that the Thousand Cleave Palm force not only failed to have any effect but was instead reflected back by Lin Dong, Wei Tong's expression changed. He bent his knees, and his figure suddenly retreated explosively.

"Pei!"

Under Lin Dong's control, the Pointed Spiral Wave carried part of Wei Tong's Pure Yuan Gang Qi, drawing a flexible arc in the field, relentlessly chasing after the retreating Wei Tong.

"Bao!"
