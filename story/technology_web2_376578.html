Baidu launches the Music Anytime channel, aiming at the market of personalized music recommendations

by ybcz0519 on 2010-12-31 09:32:41

This article is simultaneously published on Mobinode, business media please do not reprint! Baidu recently launched the Listen to Music as You Wish channel, where users can directly listen to music through its service, collect their favorite songs, and it will recommend music based on what the user has listened to. Baidu's Listen to Music as You Wish channel is currently divided into two parts: personalized radio and public radio. Users can switch freely between personalized and public radio by "changing channels." Public radio is currently divided into 14 categories, allowing users to choose their preferred category for music listening. Additionally, Baidu’s Listen to Music as You Wish channel provides three appearance options for users to switch at will. Currently, the music used in Baidu's Listen to Music as You Wish channel should all come from third-party websites. Baidu has already promoted its Listen to Music as You Wish channel on the Baidu Music homepage. Baidu Music remains the preferred source of music for many people domestically, and with the support of Baidu Music, Baidu Music's Listen to Music as You Wish channel should be able to attract a significant number of users.

Currently, domestic services that offer similar features include:

Douban Radio: Currently offers iPhone and Android clients. It can arguably be considered the best personalized music recommendation site domestically. Supported by the Douban community, this also makes its recommendation mechanism more extensible.

Link: http://douban.fm/

Xiami Radio: A high-quality music sharing community that also provides radio services. Users can add excellent music content from the community to the radio station. Furthermore, it offers numerous public radio stations. Its mechanism for splitting revenue from paid music downloads is personally considered a very valuable attempt.

Link: http://xiami.fm/

Sogou Music Box: A personalized music network space. Through it, you can conveniently collect your favorite songs and share your favorite music with friends. It allows you to listen to private music wherever you can access the internet, while also providing personal display space.

Link: http://mbox.sogou.com/

Watsonxu Comment:

When discussing domestic music websites, whether they are music-sharing sites or radio sites, one unavoidable issue is the problem of music copyright. The issue of music copyright has never been properly resolved domestically. Foreign websites such as Last.fm, Spotify, Pandora, Rdio have excellent music subscription payment models, but currently, such models seem not to be widely accepted in China. However, it is encouraging that some domestic music community websites are supporting legitimate versions while seeking appropriate profit-making methods (including Kugou's cooperation with multiple record companies nationwide, promoting songs for record companies in exchange for music copyrights; Xiami's mechanism of user download revenue sharing through Xiami coins). From the perspective of user needs, personalized music recommendations certainly have a large market. Of course, such a market requires a focus on the issue of music copyright and exploration of profitable models suitable for domestic conditions. Hopefully, Baidu can also become an important force in driving the development of domestic digital music.

Link: http://fm.mp3.baidu.com
