How to increase experience points: Tilling +3&#8226;&#8226; Planting +3 Weeding +2 (including weeding for yourself and friends) Watering +2 (including watering for yourself and friends) Harvesting starting at +20 Purchasing decorations from the store (the amount of experience gained depends on the item purchased). Note: To prevent experience grinding, weeding and pest removal are capped at 300 points per day. As your experience increases, you will level up. Leveling up rewards you with decorations for your farm and allows you to purchase more expensive and varied crops to plant. Placing weeds and pests to cause mischief: ● You can place up to 3 weeds and 3 pests on each of your friend's plots. ● You can place weeds and pests on your friend's farms up to 50 times per day. On Kaixin Network, it's best to keep a low profile~ How to level up quickly: ● Use "fertilizer". Purchase fertilizer to shorten crop growth time, allowing for earlier harvests and preventing theft. ● (Internet suggestion: At first glance, each plot seems to have an extra cost of 50 yuan, but as everyone levels up, more people are planting tomatoes and strawberries. Ignoring tomatoes for now, just considering strawberries, losing one costs 76 yuan. Spending 50 to protect 76 is simple math, isn't it? Additionally, unless someone refreshes and waits right before my crops mature, I can choose to fertilize at 59 minutes or 19 minutes. Combining the earnings table, the loss is still minimal.) Internet suggestion: (A classic strategy) &#8226; (1) Only plant white radishes Use fertilizer to rapidly grow Quickly increase experience and level up The advantage of this method is fast sowing and harvesting rates, leading to quick experience gains. The disadvantage is that monetary gains are minimal, with almost no income increase (since most of the income is offset by fertilizer costs), and it requires longer online time to care for crops. (2) Mixed crop planting Choose several crops with high price differences to plant together. The advantage of this method is achieving a balance between experience and money. Both experience and money increase at a balanced rate. The disadvantage is that since different crops are planted, their growth times vary. More frequent attention is needed to care for the crops, and they are easily stolen by other farmers if not watched closely. &nbsp;
