Comfortable bathing for weight loss - a secret recipe for losing weight

by hkypt on 2010-03-17 13:38:53

Losing weight is truly a daunting project for many women. They are afraid of the side effects of weight-loss drugs, they can't stick to exercising, and fasting simply goes against their cravings. So now there's an excellent way for you to have a slim figure easily: use the function of perfume to "slim" yourself down.

4 Methods of Weight Loss Bath

Soaking in a steaming bathtub for a while is one of life's great pleasures. You who are fighting obesity must seize this good opportunity for weight loss.

Healthy Green Tea Bath

Green tea is the vanguard of the weight loss army. Surprising, right?

1. Wrap the used green tea dregs (the amount of 3 times) or used tea bags (3-5 pieces) with gauze.

2. Put the bag into the bath water and soak for about 20 minutes.

Effect: Eliminate fatigue, accelerate blood circulation and fat burning, also has whitening effect.

Note: Do not use leftover tea from the night before.

Graceful Sake Bath

In Japan, sake is used as a material for weight loss and beauty, said to be extremely effective, worth a try.

1. Mix 500ml of sake with half a tub of hot water, wipe the chest, arms and neck area for about half an hour until sweating occurs.

2. After 5 minutes, use the same method to wipe the waist and legs.

Effect: Promote blood circulation, speed up metabolism in the body, also deeply clean dirt in pores, making skin smooth and shiny.

Note: Not suitable for people with sensitive skin.

Curvy Coarse Salt Bath

Using naturally coarse salt that hasn't been artificially processed for bathing, slimming down unknowingly, is a beauty secret of many Japanese girls.

1. Add about 2 spoons of coarse salt to warm water at 40 degrees Celsius.

2. Soak for about 5 minutes, get out for about 30 seconds, then soak again, repeat 2-3 times.

3. Finally, clean your whole body with shower gel.

Effect: Accelerate the excretion of waste in the body, and the minerals in the salt will adhere to the skin, increasing moisturizing effect, promoting blood circulation and metabolism. Besides slimming, it also has certain therapeutic effect on headaches and shoulder soreness.

Note: Shower salt can be used instead, but table salt cannot be used.

Refreshing Lemon Bath

1. Slice the lemon thinly.

2. Wrap with gauze and place in the bathtub, soak for about 30 minutes.

Effect: Lemon contains abundant vitamin C, which can stimulate capillaries in the skin and promote blood circulation.

Note: Sunscreen is necessary.
