Professional logistics express: Huana City holds a logistics news conference

by myztky32 on 2009-12-02 20:42:35

On the morning of July 17, 2007, just as the curtain rose on the first China (Shenzhen) International Logistics Express Expo at Shenzhen International Exhibition and Convention Center, the Hong Kong South China City Group jointly held a press conference here with the People’s Government of Tieling City in Liaoning Province regarding the construction project of Tieling Northeast Logistics Express City (in short, "Northeast City"). Attending this press conference were nearly 200 people including relevant leaders from Shenzhen, Mayor Zhang Jingqiang of Tieling City in Liaoning Province, other relevant provincial and municipal leaders, Chairman Zheng Songxing and Vice Chairman Liang Manlin of South China City Group, logistics express industry associations and press reporters. Renowned performing artist Mr. Zhao Benshan and famous singer Ms. Song Zuying were also invited to attend the press conference.

According to information obtained by the reporter, the "Northeast City" project that Hong Kong South China City Group is investing in and constructing in Tieling City, Liaoning Province will have a total investment exceeding RMB 5 billion. The "Northeast City" project is located in Tieling City, Liaoning Province and aims to build a modern business center integrating product display, transaction, warehousing, distribution, information service and living facilities support, mainly based on industrial raw materials and supplemented by other production and living materials, serving Northeast China, facing the whole country, and radiating to Northeast Asia.

The operation of "Northeast City" will adhere to the management concept of Shenzhen South China City - "building nests to attract phoenixes, releasing water to raise fish, prioritizing merchants, and achieving win-win for all". It will consist of commodity display and trading areas, China Railway Express warehousing and distribution areas, business office areas, and living facilities areas. It will mainly operate production materials and living materials such as building materials and decoration, furniture and home furnishings, textiles and clothing, leather goods, hardware tools and machinery, plumbing supplies, chemical plastics, printing paper packaging, electronics, and small commodities. It will integrate product display, transaction, warehousing, distribution, freight, e-commerce, financial settlement, providing one-stop services for procurement, office work, leisure, and entertainment.
