How to Light Up the QQ Xunxian Icon

Log in to Xunxian via QQ, and once your game character reaches level 25, the system will automatically light up the Xunxian icon for you. The icon display: Icon Upgrade Rules (Source: Bi Yun Xuan):

Note: You need QQ 2008 or a higher version of QQ to display the Xunxian icon. Due to inconsistent QQ versions, some QQ versions temporarily do not support icon upgrades, so it uniformly displays as a red gourd icon.

Precautions:

If the player's level meets the requirements for lighting up subsequent icons, the update to the corresponding current level icon will be automatic within 3 days.

Icon display rules: Take the information of the highest-level character from the current QQ number to display the icon.

The process of lighting up the icon is irreversible; once a higher-level icon has been lit, it cannot revert to a lower-level icon or be turned off.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Why should I light up the Xunxian icon?

A: Lighting up the Xunxian icon is the right of every Xunxian user. It allows you to showcase that you are a Xunxian player on the Xunxian-related data panel on QQ, showing your uniqueness!

Q: How do I light up the Xunxian icon?

A: You just need to have a character in Xunxian and reach level 25, and the system will automatically light up the icon for you.

Q: Why does it require level 25 to light up the icon?

A: This is to make the Xunxian icon more valuable and represent a higher pursuit! At the same time, this is also a test!

Q: How do I upgrade the Xunxian icon?

A: Reaching level 40 will upgrade the Xunxian icon to a level 2 icon, and reaching level 60 will upgrade it to a level 3 icon. The upgrade process requires no action from the user; the system will automatically assist players in completing the upgrade.

Q: Why hasn't my icon lit up/ upgraded even though I've reached the required level?

A: Please use the QQ version provided by the official website (Chuan Mei QQ, Piao Yun QQ, or Shan Hu Chong are all unofficial versions) and upgrade your version to QQ 2008 Official Edition or higher. Versions below QQ 2008 Official Edition will not be able to display the Xunxian icon.

Q: Why can't I light up/upgrade the icon even though I've reached the required level and my version meets the requirement (not lower than QQ 2007 II Beta1)?

A: First, we apologize for any inconvenience caused. The automatic icon upgrade will be completed within 3 days. If after 3 days the icon still cannot be lit up/upgraded, we recommend contacting Xunxian online customer service and selecting "Other - Game Complaints" to report your issue. We appreciate your understanding for any inconvenience caused.
