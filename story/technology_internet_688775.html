Rule two, Slim Waist Eating Method

One, eat more high-fiber foods

Fiber can slow down the release of energy from food, thereby reducing the accumulation of fat in the body. Daily fiber intake should be 20-25 grams. Fruits, vegetables, and grains are good choices.

Two, eat more soy products

Soy products are also good low-fat foods. They are rich in vitamins and proteins. Every day, you should consume appropriate amounts of soy products, such as tofu, soy milk, soy milk, etc.

Three, eat more protein and less fat

Protein can increase your metabolic rate because your body needs to expend energy when digesting protein. For every 100 grams of protein consumed, 25 grams are burned, leaving an actual intake of 75 grams.
