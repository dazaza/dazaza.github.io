Market activity continues to increase, focus on individual stock opportunities＿stocks

by dx0957095 on 2009-12-03 18:28:25

From the market conditions, although the market fluctuated violently and the blue chip stocks pulled down the index, the market sentiment was high, and the hot spots were still very active. In the morning, the Disney sector strengthened significantly due to the news stimulation. Then the automobile stocks also performed outstandingly under the stimulation of An凯 Bus's limit-up. The bridge road construction and chemical fiber stocks were also collectively active in the afternoon. These stocks continued to boost market enthusiasm. In addition, the GEM sector ranked first in the industry in terms of increase, with six stocks such as Dayu Water Conservation and Lemei Pharmaceutical reaching the limit-up. The remaining stocks also received enthusiastic pursuit of funds. Due to their smaller circulation, as new things, they are easy to attract investors' attention, and the GEM is prone to follow the trend.

The agricultural sector performed the most stable and outstanding today. Denhai Seed's early limit-up in the morning has already formed a great stimulus for agricultural stocks. Dunhuang Seed's strong attack and limit-up in the afternoon once again stimulated the overall activity of the agricultural sector. Fengle Seed and Xinjiang Development also reached the limit-up, while Xinwu Abundant, Fucheng Wu Abundant, Xin Sai Shares, and Longping Hi-tech also gained significantly. In terms of messages, the expectation of recent rise in agricultural product prices has become increasingly strong, and the agricultural subsidy policies of some provinces have emerged frequently, directly stimulating agriculture. While the overall agricultural sector lags behind the market, it also has a certain valley effect, triggering the attention of funds.

Yesterday we proposed that currently, with the market sentiment restored and investment enthusiasm rising again, investors should appropriately ignore the index in the short term and focus mainly on individual stock operations. Today's market performance in this aspect was very obvious. The market fluctuated sharply or even dived several times, but it did not affect the speculation of individual stocks. On the contrary, with the stop of the index falling, the hot spot effect quickly spread throughout the market. This characteristic of inactive indices but active individual stocks is actually the key moment for investors to seize opportunities. The rise of weighted stocks will greatly push up the index, which may trigger technical adjustments, while the pressure or inactivity of weighted stocks is more conducive to creating a profitable atmosphere.

Technically speaking, the回调 amplitude of the stock index supported by the moving average line is limited, but at the same time, there is also a requirement for further technical adjustment. Therefore, the market may still maintain a consolidation tug-of-war trend in the short term, and continue to pay attention to the active opportunities of individual stocks.

The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows: When trading stocks, you must specify whether you want to buy or sell, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. Moreover, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the order includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to trade, the quantity, and the price for buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. The stock codes and abbreviations must be consistent when placing an order. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks being traded: the quantity of stocks being purchased must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks being sold does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four methods of commission: counter submission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
