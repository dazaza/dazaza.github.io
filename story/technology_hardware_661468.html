The location of our team's practice is China Tianxing Group. Tianxing Group is located in Yongkang, Zhejiang, known as the "Chinese Science and Technology Hardware City". Tianxing Group, approved by the State Administration for Industry and Commerce, is an enterprise group without regional restrictions, with Tianxing Group Co., Ltd. at its core, consisting of seven subsidiaries. It is one of the few enterprises in Yongkang that have been major taxpayers for 15 consecutive years. Copper, chemical, door, real estate development, and cement are the five major basic industries of the group. The company has total assets of 800 million yuan, a factory area covering 204,000 square meters, and a building area of 108,000 square meters. It has an annual production capacity of 55,000 tons of copper plates, copper strips, copper rods, copper pipes, and oxygen-free copper rods, 600,000 sets of metal door series products, 40,000 tons of hydrogen fluoride, and 800,000 tons of cement. The enterprise has passed ISO9001:2000 quality management system certification and ISO10012:2003 metrology inspection system certification. Its products have obtained CE certification. The "Tianxing" trademark has won the title of China's well-known trademark and Zhejiang Province's famous trademark. "Tianxing" business name has received Zhejiang Province's well-known business name, and its products have been rated as Zhejiang Province's famous brand products and Jinhua City's famous brand products. There are more than 800 employees.

The predecessor of the group was a family workshop engaged in small hardware processing - Yongkang Mold Electrical Factory. In the past twenty years, it has been market-oriented and quality-dependent, continuously developing new products. It once pioneered Yongkang hardware famous products such as the "Koufu Brand" copper hot pot, electric heat cup, three-in-one copper hot pot, energy-saving iron pot, etc., laying the foundation for the development of Yongkang cookware industry and achieving its own capital accumulation. In 1992, the company began to enter the copper material processing industry. After three technical renovations in 1992, 1996, and 2000, the enterprise development entered the fast lane, and the construction of "three civilizations" reached a new level. In 2006, it achieved sales revenue of 1.03 billion yuan and paid 21.5 million yuan in taxes to the state, both setting new historical records. In the past ten years, the industrial output value, tax payment, and increase in social employment of the company have respectively increased by 22.4, 14.5, and 15.5 times, showcasing the impressive achievements of "a decade of change, great changes in Tianxing."

The location of Tianxing Group, Zhejiang Yongkang, has attracted widespread attention from all sectors of society, especially the economics community, with its "tenfold growth in ten years" development speed and experience; as a dynamic economic model, Jinhua Yongkang economy indeed deserves research.

In the past dozen years, Jinhua Yongkang economy has developed rapidly, mainly due to the fact that Jinhua Yongkang economy is mainly driven by private and private-owned forces, which is a spontaneous and stable sustainable economic order. History shows that under a spontaneous order, the practice samples of pioneers appear, generating inducement effects, and through mutual competition, they can quickly and continuously expand, forming new systems and mechanisms. The enterprising spirit (or a kind of competitive psychology) of the people of Jinhua Yongkang has largely promoted mutual competition.

Yongkang currently has nearly 10,000 various types of industrial enterprises, producing more than 10,000 kinds of products, among which there are over 40 national, departmental, and provincial excellent products. There are 23 enterprises with sales revenue exceeding 100 million yuan, and 4 non-regional enterprise groups (Yingpeng, Xingyue, Wangli, Buyang). Among the eight major hardware industries in Yongkang, the total production volume and export volume of power tools account for 1/4 and 1/3 of the national total, respectively, ranking alongside Japan and Germany as the world's top three power tool manufacturers. The export volume of small appliances and kitchenware such as shavers ranks first nationwide. The production volume of non-ferrous metals accounts for more than 70% of the national total, and the market share of anti-theft doors also exceeds 70%, making Yongkang the "Capital of Chinese Doors".

"Small commodities, big market" has attracted more and more investment entities. After the reform and opening up, the market orientation was realized, and individual private enterprises thus gained great development, resulting in the formation of a large group of small and medium-sized enterprises. In terms of characteristics, it is a spontaneous and expansionary economic order based on individual economies. The formation of this group has reduced the transaction costs of operations, achieved agglomeration effects, and further promoted development.

Zhejiang Yongkang has long enjoyed the reputation of "Hardware craftsmen traveling all over, no matter which prefecture or county, they are inseparable from Kang." Generation after generation of Yongkang hardware craftsmen have jointly nurtured a rich tradition and history of hardware culture, earning Yongkang's fame for small hardware. The Internet is a virtual network, but the people of Jinhua Yongkang have established a practical mode network of the people of Jinhua Yongkang.

At the same time, the development of modern inter-networks has played a guiding role in Yongkang's economy. For the same person, leaving the network will lose many opportunities to gain benefits, which also proves that the network has strong cohesion. Meanwhile, being realistic, working hard, and daring to innovate are good traditions of Jinhua Yongkang, and also the main reasons for Yongkang's success.

One of the notable aspects of Yongkang's success today is the transformation and simplification of local government functions. The government does not directly intervene in microeconomic activities and daily operations, but instead focuses its main efforts on improving the macro environment, doing a good job in construction planning, formulating game rules, and doing a good job in infrastructure construction.

After the economic crisis, under the background of internationalization of export competition and globalization of trade, Yongkang has achieved victory in past competitions, but it cannot use the past successful model to operate the future. Someone once said that the essence of a market economy is a credit economy, and the competition in a market economy is the competition for talent. How to gain new competitive advantages under the new market economy background is something enterprises must consider. Integrity is an essential element for enterprises to engage in commercial activities, and talent is the foundation for enterprises to maintain continuous development. Conducting honest economic behavior under a transparent framework and cultivating and utilizing talents in a suitable talent growth environment are urgent tasks for the development of private enterprises. Our team stayed at Tianxing Group for 3 days. Although the practice was short, we learned a lot and gained much, gaining a new understanding of each other.

On July 11, our small team arrived at the practice destination, Yongkang City. After a brief preparation, the team members, filled with excitement, arrived at Tianxing Group at 2:30 PM. Under the guidance of the staff, we visited the production workshops and production lines of the headquarters of Tianxing Group. The staff introduced us to the production process of the factory, the work and functions of various departments, and particularly introduced us to the product development department, i.e., the new technology development department, making us realize that Tianxing Group has always been market-oriented and quality-dependent, continuously developing new products over the past two decades. Tianxing Group once pioneered Yongkang hardware famous products such as the "Koufu Brand" copper hot pot, electric heat cup, three-in-one copper hot pot, energy-saving iron pot, etc., laying the foundation for the development of Yongkang cookware industry and achieving its own capital accumulation.

Through half a day of visits and understanding, we realized that the high-speed operation of a company requires close cooperation and collaboration among various departments. Especially in today's highly competitive social background, a company needs a core technical department to support its existence and development. This made us deeply feel that innovation is the soul of a nation and even more so the survival force of an enterprise. We need to adhere to keeping pace with the times and pioneering innovations.

On the morning of July 12, our small team arrived at the headquarters of Tianxing Group. First, we contacted Mr. Peng Feifeng, the general manager of the human resources department of the group, and had a face-to-face interview and exchange with him. We discussed and researched a series of issues regarding the company's talent recruitment status and the difficulties of university graduates' employment. At noon, we were warmly entertained by the group, enjoying a sumptuous lunch. During the lunch, we had a simple exchange with the senior staff of the company. In the afternoon, after a brief rest, we interviewed the president of the company, Mr. Ying Tianxing. The interview content mainly focused on leadership qualities and business operation models.

Through this short yet long day, we benefited greatly and had a clear goal for our future lives. On July 13, our small team conducted the last day of practice, and our three-day summer social practice was successfully completed. In the morning, we brought the questionnaires we had prepared in advance to our practice site - the headquarters of Tianxing Group, and conducted a sample survey of its employees, asking them to answer the questionnaire content. The employees of Tianxing were very enthusiastic and actively cooperated with us, greatly facilitating our work. In the afternoon, we returned to our accommodation, statistically analyzed the data of the questionnaires, and specifically analyzed the results. Our questionnaire mainly investigated the overall quality and education level of the employees of Tianxing Group. The investigation results and detailed data are as follows: ordinary workers account for 62% of the total employees of the company, mainly with junior high school or lower education; technical workers account for 25% of the total employees of the company, mainly graduated from high schools, colleges, and technical institutions; managers account for 13% of the total employees of the company, mainly with bachelor's degree or higher education. Other detailed data are not listed here.
