Recommendation for Popular Card Cameras on the Market

by deguendg on 2009-12-03 21:22:59

With the development of digital cameras, people are no longer satisfied with just taking clear pictures; the appearance design of the camera has gradually become a factor considered in purchasing decisions. This is especially true for consumer-grade compact digital cameras, where some products' designs attract more attention than their photography performance. Below, we will recommend several fashionable and highly regarded compact digital camera models.

The Canon IXUS 200 IS features a 24mm ultra-wide-angle lens and a 3.0-inch "PureColor II" touch LCD screen, marking the first time such specifications have appeared in the history of Canon's digital cameras. The IXUS 200, which boasts excellent performance, has a rounded exterior design without sharp edges, complemented by a sandblasted metal body that provides a good grip.

The IXUS 200 IS is Canon's first compact digital camera to feature a 24mm ultra-wide-angle IS optical image stabilization zoom lens. Combined with its new 5x optical zoom, the lens focal length is equivalent to 24-120mm on a 35mm camera, with a maximum aperture of F2.8 (wide-angle) / F5.9 (telephoto). Its focal range covers from ultra-wide-angle to medium telephoto, making it suitable for group photos or landscape shots. Whether capturing close-ups or distant views, the IXUS 200 IS can include all the beauty within its frame. Additionally, the 12.1 million effective pixel CCD and DIGIC 4 image processor ensure outstanding image quality.

The IXUS 200 IS is the first Canon digital camera to adopt a touch LCD screen. The new design integrates clear browsing with elegant touch control, fully achieving "touch to shoot, touch to view." The easy and convenient touch operation makes shooting and viewing simpler and more intuitive. With the new 3.0-inch "PureColor II" technology, the IXUS 200 IS touch LCD screen offers a wider color gamut, high resolution, high contrast, and a wide viewing angle, effectively enhancing visibility. At the same time, it retains the classic mode dial and menu/review button functions of Canon, allowing users to choose their preferred operating method based on habit.

The advanced touch autofocus can select and track focusing objects. Regardless of whether you're photographing people or other moving objects, simply touch the subject you want to capture to start automatic tracking focus. After half-pressing the shutter, it can automatically track focus and exposure, especially useful when photographing moving faces, allowing you to easily capture clear images with accurate brightness and facial tones.

**Canon IXUS 200 (SD980) Specifications:**

- Sensor Type: CCD

- Sensor Size: (1/2.3) inches

- Maximum Resolution: 4000*3000

- Image Processor: DIGIC 4

- Equivalent 35mm Focal Length: 24-120mm

- Aperture Range: F2.8-5.9

- Display Size: 3.0 inches

- Pixel Count: 230k pixels

- Aspect Ratio: 16:9

- Sensitivity: Auto, ISO80/100/200/400/800/1600

- Anti-Shake: Optical

- Video Recording: 1280x720 pixels (30 frames/sec)

- Dimensions: 99.9x53.4x22.9 mm

- Weight: 130g

**Editor's Opinion:**

Whether it’s the 24mm wide-angle lens or the 3-inch touch screen, both are firsts for Canon's digital cameras. Therefore, the Canon IXUS 200 IS can be considered an innovative product. Besides these two firsts, the IXUS 200 IS also excels in other performance aspects, inheriting the advantages of Canon's IXUS series.
