Opera is currently the best performing browser on the Linux platform, and the Opera China team itself is located in Opera's research and development center, mainly responsible for the development of global Linux platform projects. This release initially solves the problem of the Chinese font display on Linux for many years, and you can see most of the improvements and features of Opera China on Windows:

Detailed list of improvements:

* Greatly improved fonts, using the font.ini solution

* New Chinese style skin

* Support double click the TAB to close, open by default

* Fast search, integration of domestic popular search engine

* Quick translation, you can translate directly by typing "fy word" in the address bar, such as "fy opera"; Randomly select the text, right-click menu translation is also available.

* Optimized translation, proofread tens of thousands of words

* Fixed browser compatibility issues for dozens of websites in China

* Optimize the fast forward string, automatically identify the page "next page", space or mouse gesture quick page

* Email client built-in popular domestic mail service provider, just fill in the account password can directly send and receive emails

* More details change, please experience for yourself
