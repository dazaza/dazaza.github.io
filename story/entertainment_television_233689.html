Early in the morning, Chen Bo stood in front of the bulletin board, looking at his own promotion to Executive Vice President of Lixiang Development starting today. He was overwhelmed with emotion, never imagining that the General Manager would have such foresight to promote him after only three months. However, the colleagues were whispering behind his back, and most of them were sneering.

Su Xin, dressed as a caddy, happily received a call from Chen Bo while she was in the preparation area of the golf course hall. She worried that he couldn't get through this month again and would come to borrow money. Chen Bo told her the good news about his promotion and asked her to come to Taipei during the summer vacation, so he could treat her well. Su Xin said she wasn't familiar with Taipei, and Chen Bo said that if she arrived in Taipei, she could call him on his mobile phone, and he would pick her up.

The date between the female model and Chen Bo was at a newly decorated house. The female model said that this was the new house she rented for Chen Bo, which would be their love nest. As he had become a vice president, he should live better. Chen Bo, who had been fed sweet talk, also felt it was reasonable. The female model also took care of some of Chen Bo's outfits. That night, they painted a picture of the future in their new love nest and enjoyed the present moment...

Late at night, Ying Jie was in the group office talking with the general manager of a subsidiary company about company affairs. The general manager reported to Ying Jie that an employee without baggage had already been promoted as the leader to streamline the personnel of the entire development company. Ying Jie conveyed the president's hope for the process to be done as soon as possible and without any aftermath, and requested a meeting with him tomorrow morning. After the general manager left, a tired Ying Jie stood by the floor-to-ceiling window watching the moonlight, thinking about Su Xin.

Sheng Shu's son, Guo Zhi, waited at the school gate for Su Xin's graduation ceremony to end. He was going to take her to Taitung to play and take photos, which was also work, as they needed to check the roads for any damage along the way. He and Su Xin had made an appointment a month ago, and they had been eagerly awaiting this day for a whole month. Su Xin saw Guo Zhi and remembered their appointment. Su Xin apologized to Guo Zhi, saying her older brother had something in Taipei that needed his help. Guo Zhi said he could drive him to Taipei. Su Xin said she had already booked the train tickets and asked Guo Zhi to go to work. Disappointed, Guo Zhi said at least let him send her to the train station?

Chen Bo went to the office and was taken by the general manager to meet Ying Jie at a high-end restaurant. Ying Jie met Chen Bo for the first time and smiled when he saw his hair a bit messy and his suit size too small. He thought to himself that finding such a person was right. Ying Jie told Chen Bo to do his best, and all the benefits would be given to him - a car, a secretary, housing allowance, and his salary tripled. Chen Bo wanted to ask what exactly was so good about him that deserved such cultivation from the company, but he swallowed his words. He thought that many supervisors in the world got where they are because of good luck, even the president was elected due to good luck. So he was the same; it must be just good luck. Don't ask too many questions, lest the higher-ups really think about why they promoted him, which wouldn't be good. And Ying Jie's inner thoughts were that layoffs should be fast and without any aftermath, so he reminded the general manager of Lixiang Development to satisfy all the needs of this big head during this period, making him willing to be loyal to the company, and not show any misplaced sympathy for employees who will soon be laid off...
