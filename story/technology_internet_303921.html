Shanghai Taio Travel International Resort Club Safety Tips: Treatment for ankle sprains during journeys. In the event of an ankle sprain, elevate the affected limb and apply an ice pack or cold towel first to reduce pain and subcutaneous bleeding. Then apply a topical ointment designed to promote blood circulation and dissipate bruising. If you are traveling without medication or adhesive tape, you can also grasp the injured ankle with one hand and hold the toes with the other, shaking them from outside to inside. Then let the toes bend downward as much as possible, followed by bending the toes upward as much as possible, repeating this multiple times, which should improve the condition. For severe sprains, such as complete ligament tears or unstable ankles, avoid pressing or rubbing randomly and immediately go to the hospital.

Hainan's First Yacht Themed Hotel Tops Out, Pirate Rooms to Appear in Sanya

The ultra-luxury yacht member-exclusive hotel invested and built by Hongzhou Group - Sanya Hongzhou Edrey Yacht Hotel topped out today and will be put into use in six months. This is Hainan's first yacht-themed hotel, marking the entry of Sanya into a brand new "yacht era."

Hongzhou Group held a grand topping-out ceremony for the hotel today. The hotel is located near the estuary of the Sanya River, with a total construction area of approximately 36,000 square meters. However, it only has 88 rooms, including garden view rooms, panoramic view rooms, sea view rooms, sky-connected moonlight rooms, pirate characteristic rooms, Caribbean suites, and Hawk Captain suites among seven different room types, fully embodying the yacht marine lifestyle. A responsible person from Hongzhou Group stated that the hotel cost about 500 million yuan, featuring a 1000-square-meter lobby, captain's lounge, aquarium, distinctive restaurants, and other ocean-themed supporting facilities making the hotel's "yacht nature" more prominent.

It is understood that this hotel is a yacht member-exclusive hotel, positioned as the top yacht hotel in the South China Sea, integrating accommodation, navigation, sports, and other functions, mainly including housing, catering, entertainment and leisure, yachts, docks, navigation, infrastructure, and activity project facilities, meeting the needs of high-end tourists.

Sanya City’s "Eleventh Five-Year" plan points out: actively encouraging the development of private yachts and corporate clubs, building China's top-class yacht base and exhibition base. Hongzhou Group has set yacht industry as one of its development goals, establishing strategic partnerships with companies such as Italy's Ferretti, America's Brunswick, France's Beneteau, and Sunseeker, introducing multiple yachts, including the super luxury yacht "Pershing 72" purchased for 50 million yuan which has now settled in Sanya. Facilities at the yacht dock at the estuary of the Sanya River are also being improved.

It is understood that Sanya's proposed yacht industry development plan has attracted many domestic yacht enthusiasts who either purchase property or play at sea in Sanya, forming a unique seascape.

Author: Wu Zhongbin

Source: Hainan Daily
