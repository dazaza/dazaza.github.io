Daughter-in-law avenges herself by making a spear to stab her mother-in-law

by anonymous on 2011-08-12 14:35:07

Xingtai, Handan - Hebei Urban Network (Correspondents Mingqiang Yang and Huiwu Zhang) - The mother-in-law and daughter-in-law relationship has always been a major issue in most families. If the man doesn't handle it well, not only will the family atmosphere become disharmonious, but significant problems may also arise. Recently, the Weixian County Public Security Bureau acted swiftly and conducted thorough investigations to quickly solve an intentional injury case where the daughter-in-law harmed her mother-in-law. The suspect, Liu Qiaoqiao, was apprehended.

At around 12 noon on June 3rd, the Zhangtai police station received a report from a villager stating that someone in the village had been stabbed seriously that morning. The victim was currently being treated at the hospital while the suspect had fled. After receiving the report, the police station informed the criminal investigation team No. 3 while heading to the crime scene. The criminal investigation team divided into two groups: one group went to the hospital for investigation, and the other joined forces with the Zhangtai police station officers to examine the scene. Through their efforts, the investigating officers preliminarily grasped the basic details of the case and identified the suspect as Liu Qiaoqiao from the same village. Subsequently, the police officers set up blockades at various checkpoints to prevent the suspect from fleeing while conducting searches within the village and its vicinity. On the morning of June 4th, the police captured Liu Qiaoqiao who had returned home to retrieve supplies for her escape.

Upon investigation, it was revealed that the suspect Liu Qiaoqiao and the victim, Junzhi Zhang, were mother-in-law and daughter-in-law. They had long-term disputes over household matters, leading to strained relations. The man, caught between them, dared not criticize either his wife or his mother, so he chose to ignore both, watching helplessly as the two most important women in his life fought, while he became passive. At around 8 o'clock on June 3rd, when Liu Qiaoqiao handed over her son, who was less than two years old, to her mother-in-law, Zhang Junzhi, for babysitting, they again had a verbal altercation. Filled with anger after returning home, Liu Qiaoqiao decided to give her mother-in-law a lesson. She made a spear out of a radiator pipe and then went to her mother-in-law's house, stabbing Zhang Junzhi before fleeing.

Currently, the suspect Liu Qiaoqiao has been criminally detained by the Weixian County Public Security Bureau, and the case is under further review. (All names used are pseudonyms.)
