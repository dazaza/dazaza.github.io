6. What are agents, direct stores, franchise stores, and self-operated stores?

Answer: Agent: A regional general distributor authorized by the headquarters, who has exclusive operating rights, sales rights, and development rights of the [Style of Life] brand and products within the regions and fields specified in the contract.

Direct Store: A chain store directly operated by the [Style of Life] headquarters, which is the business form where the headquarters directly operates, invests, and manages each retail point.

Franchise Store: A cooperative way where the franchisee can use the image, brand, trade name, and products of the [Style of Life] headquarters to operate autonomously in a certain region.

Self-operated Store: A terminal retail shop opened and operated by the [Style of Life] agent within their own agency area, which is the business form where the agent directly operates, invests, and manages each retail point.
