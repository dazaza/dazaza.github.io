Introduction:

Fong Sai Yuk, a skilled martial artist and righteous hero, is highly favored by Chen Jialuo, the leader of the anti-Qing organization, the Red Flower Society. However, Yu Zhenhai, the ruthless second-in-command of the Red Flower Society, sees Fong Sai Yuk as an obstacle to his ambition of becoming the leader and plans to eliminate him. The members of the Red Flower Society are unaware that their leader is actually the elder brother of Emperor Qianlong. Deceitful Japanese ronin acquire Chen Jialuo's "Origin Book" and plan to hand it over to Emperor Qianlong. Knowing that revealing his origins would risk splitting the Red Flower Society, Chen Jialuo sends Fong Sai Yuk to intercept the Japanese ronin. Unaware of the importance of the item he is tasked with retrieving, Fong Sai Yuk fails to complete his mission after rescuing a kidnapped girl along the way. Soon, informants report to Chen Jialuo that the "Origin Book" has fallen into the hands of Sun Shiyi, the Viceroy of Guangdong and Guangxi, whose daughter, Sun Anyer, is the very girl Fong Sai Yuk had saved. Chen Jialuo then sends Fong Sai Yuk to cleverly retrieve the "Origin Book" using a "handsome man strategy." Sun Anyer has already fallen in love with Fong Sai Yuk secretly, and Sun Shiyi knows he is part of the Red Flower Society. To subdue Fong Sai Yuk, Sun Shiyi devises a plan for a martial arts contest disguised as a marriage proposal. With remarkable skills, Fong Sai Yuk easily wins Sun Anyer's affection. Despite knowing Fong Sai Yuk's true intentions, Sun Anyer wholeheartedly helps her beloved. Upon opening the "Origin Book," Fong Sai Yuk realizes the truth. In a hurry to return home, he finds himself surrounded by soldiers but is rescued by Sun Anyer. Fong Sai Yuk keeps Chen Jialuo's origins a secret, claiming he failed to retrieve the book. The cunning Yu Zhenhai forces Chen Jialuo to disable Fong Sai Yuk's martial arts abilities, leaving him severely injured. He is taken back to a cottage by his mother and elder brother to recuperate. Meanwhile, Yu Zhenhai plots to kidnap Chen Jialuo and seizes the leadership position. Determined to consolidate his power, Yu Zhenhai decides to hunt down Fong Sai Yuk. Fong Sai Yuk's elder brother is killed while protecting him, and his mother is captured by Yu Zhenhai. To save his mother, Fong Sai Yuk painfully fights his way back to the Red Flower Society to confront Yu Zhenhai. Unexpectedly, Yu Zhenhai discovers that Fong Sai Yuk's martial arts were not truly disabled, leading to his demise at Fong Sai Yuk's hands. Chen Jialuo regains power and invites Fong Sai Yuk to join him in establishing a great cause. However, Fong Sai Yuk chooses to retire from the江湖 (jianghu - martial world), returning to his hometown with his mother and fiancée to live a reclusive life.
