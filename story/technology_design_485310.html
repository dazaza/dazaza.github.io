Who can partner with others to seek wealth

by dd26huiuygui on 2011-09-05 09:36:49

When starting a business, one person's strength is often too small and needs to partner with others. However, some people are not destined to do business with partners, which may lead to financial loss or eventual failure; while others are suitable for doing business with partners, and often start their path to wealth due to partnership. In fact, there is a very simple way to see if a person is suitable for doing business with partners - those whose Bazi (eight characters) has the "Bi Jian" and "Jie Cai" as the useful gods, can seek wealth through partnership when they are in the luck cycle of these useful gods, and will receive more help from friends.

There was a friend like this, I met him in 2004. At that time, he was driving for a company, already 34 years old, and had just bought a house but owed a lot of debt. If we were to describe his situation at the time, it could be called being almost penniless. We met in the first month of 2004 by chance, and I divined a hexagram for him. I remember it was the "Ze Huo Ge" hexagram, symbolizing a person undergoing a great change to renew themselves and move towards good fortune. By the end of the year, this friend partnered with a relative to invest in a business. At that time, he didn't have much money, but thanks to the many friends he had made over the years, he managed to borrow around 70,000-80,000 yuan, adding loans, making a total of less than 200,000 yuan for the two of them, and thus began their entrepreneurial journey (later I went to adjust the feng shui of his office). In just a few short years, he changed houses, bought cars, and entered the ranks of millionaires. Through this, we developed a deep friendship. Sometimes, when we sit together drinking, reminiscing about the tough times of the past, we can't help but marvel at how far we've come.

This person's Bazi astrology is a typical example of someone who is suitable for seeking wealth through partnership. His Bazi is: Gan Zao: Xin Hai, Xin Mao, Gui Chou, Jia Chen. The major cycles start from 1978, respectively: Geng Yin, Ji Chou, Wu Zi, Ding Hai, Bing Xu... This Gui Water born in the Mao month, although there is Xin Metal supporting in the heavenly stems, unfortunately, the earthly branch Chou Earth sitting underneath harms the body, and the Jia Wood in the hour branch severely leaks energy, making the Gui Water weak. When weak, it likes support. Starting from the Wu Zi major cycle, he entered a water luck cycle that supports his body. In 2004, during the Jia Shen flow year, Shen Metal supports water, and the conflict between Shen and Chen occurred that year when he started a business with a partner. He then walked through the Hai Zi water flow years, making a large fortune in just a few short years.

On the contrary, if a person's destiny has the "Bi Jian" and "Jie Cai" as taboo gods, then they will often harm themselves by seeking wealth through partnerships, resulting in losses greater than gains. Not only will they not make money, but good friends may become enemies, and may even end up in court. A word of advice to friends - there are too many unknowns on the road to wealth, please proceed with caution when seeking wealth through partnerships.
