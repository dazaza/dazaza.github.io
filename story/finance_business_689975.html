What are the causes that make women become uglier as they age? The following ten aspects are the root causes.

1. Being overly worried and self-satisfied.

Frowning will only deprive skin cells of nutrients, causing the facial skin to dry out and lose its luster, resulting in wrinkles. It also deepens the "worry lines" on the face. As the saying goes, a smile can make you look ten years younger. To possess beauty, one should first have a calm and tolerant heart.

2. Staying up all night, disregarding day and night.

Lack of sleep is the biggest enemy of skin care. Insufficient sleep disrupts the regulatory activities of skin cells, affecting the vitality of the epidermal cells. This easily manifests on the skin. For women who want to be beautiful, getting enough sleep is crucial. Therefore, one should sleep at least 8 hours daily. Only by sleeping well can one eliminate skin fatigue and normalize the regulatory activities of skin cells, delaying skin aging.

3. Frequent exposure to the sun without protection.

Direct sunlight and excessive ultraviolet rays directly damage the deep elastic fibers and collagen protein of the skin, causing the facial skin to darken, become rough, slack, and lose its luster, leading to wrinkles and even skin cancer. Therefore, it is important to develop the habit of using quality sunscreen to prevent skin aging.

4. Relieving worries by smoking and drinking.

Nicotine constricts skin blood vessels; thus, smokers develop wrinkles 10 years earlier than non-smokers. If you are a smoker, you will appear 10 years older than your peers. Drinking reduces the oil content in the skin, causing dehydration and indirectly affecting the normal function of the skin.

5. Exaggerated expressions of joy, anger, sorrow, and happiness.

Exaggerated actions and extreme expressions increase wrinkles on the face. Therefore, frequent laughing, frowning, squinting, and making faces are harmful to beauty.

6. Drinking water sparingly and consuming only beverages.

Insufficient water intake leads to inadequate oil secretion, causing the skin to easily dehydrate. Ensuring the skin gets sufficient hydration is key to skincare. Therefore, one must force themselves to drink 6-8 glasses of water daily instead of caffeine-rich beverages.

7. Lack of exercise, leading to flabby muscles.

Moderate exercise accelerates overall blood circulation, making the body's movements appropriately relaxed, enhancing skin smoothness. It also provides the skin with ample opportunities to sweat, achieving healthy balance and significantly reducing the chances of skin aging. Thus, a beautiful woman is also a healthy woman who exercises regularly.

8. Applying makeup meticulously but removing it carelessly.

Many people know that applying makeup should be meticulous. However, incomplete makeup removal is a common mistake many commit. To thoroughly remove the dirt from pores that hinders normal skin breathing, merely using cleanser isn't enough. Deep cleansing work must be done periodically.

9. Using skincare products indiscriminately.

Many women do not understand their own skin. They use skincare products without considering whether they suit them or not. If given the chance, it might be worthwhile to take some time to conduct a skin test, understand your skin better, and then tailor your skincare routine accordingly.

10. Pursuing stimulation and ignoring fruits and vegetables.

Spicy, fried, and other stimulating foods are like large-scale destructive weapons to the skin. To become more beautiful with age, one should moderately reduce consumption of these foods, which helps maintain endocrine balance in the skin and reduces the occurrence of acne and oily skin issues. Vitamins in fruits and vegetables benefit the skin greatly, whether consumed as food or made into natural masks, adding much charm to life while yielding excellent results.
