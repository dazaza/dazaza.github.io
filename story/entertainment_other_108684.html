Host of Hunan TV

At the recording scene, he slammed the table and threw the chair

He quarreled and cursed with his female partner

Report by reporter Ping Li: Yesterday, a video named "Conflict between Hunan TV hosts during the program, Li Xiang throws things and storms out" spread on the Internet. Through the video, we can see that host Li Xiang lost his composure in front of the camera. He loudly argued with his female co-host Xinran, throwing chairs and slamming the table. In the end, they both angrily left, forcing the ongoing recording of the program "I Disagree" to be interrupted.

Scene: The two hosts cursed each other

This video lasted more than 6 minutes. At the beginning, Li Xiang started arguing with his partner. Both were very angry and neither gave way. Li Xiang first said that girls who go to bars these days don't respect themselves much, and used his partner as an example, which made the female partner unhappy. But Li Xiang didn't think it was inappropriate. He said that since they were doing the program together, if not using the other party as an example, then who should be used? However, the female host thought that broadcasting like this would make the audience think she was someone who loved to go to bars, which would damage her reputation.

It seemed to be a simple misunderstanding, but the two wouldn't let it go. Although there were no coarse words in their arguments, they had already torn off their masks. The picture showed multiple times that the staff came forward to mediate, but Li Xiang pushed them away by saying "it's none of your business". After about 3 minutes of argument, Li Xiang became even more excited. He heavily slapped the table in front of him, jumped up and said that he couldn't communicate with his partner anymore, and the program couldn't continue.

A few seconds after he left the lens, the sound of a chair being thrown could still be heard. Then he returned to the lens and continued to argue with the female host until both angrily left the scene.

Reaction: Netizens accuse of hype

Regarding this incident, many netizens expressed their opinions. Most of them believed that this was a deliberately planned hype. "They are arguing like this, but the cameraman is still recording enthusiastically, and there are even close-up shots of the two hosts, it's too fake!" "Before this, how many of you knew there was a program called 'I Disagree'? Now after all this trouble, the program and the hosts have become famous, clever hype!" Many netizens also pointed out, "This video clip is controlled by the TV station itself, how could it spread online?"

Some people also expressed their views on the low quality shown by the two hosts, believing that hosts should be people with quality and cultivation, but what was seen in the video was really disappointing.

Li Xiang: I think I am sick

The reporter immediately contacted the host Li Xiang. He said: "Now I am about to record the program, I can't accept your interview. If you have any questions, please contact our PR department." Then he hung up the phone hurriedly.

The reporter then interviewed Miss Liu from the PR department of Hunan Entertainment Channel. She told the reporter, "I just learned about this matter from the Internet. We have already communicated with Li Xiang. He will explain to the audience through his blog at an appropriate time in the next few days." She also revealed that the two hosts have already reconciled, and that episode has been re-recorded. However, for the question of "What will the station do to the two hosts?", Miss Liu avoided answering.

When the reporter asked, "Who spread the recording? Is this a hype behavior of the production team?" Miss Liu said, "The station is not yet fully aware of this matter. I am not clear about the inside story either. I wasn't at the scene, so I can't give you a definite answer."

Yesterday afternoon, Li Xiang updated his blog to make an "explanation". In this blog post titled "I Think I Am Sick...", Li Xiang wrote: "Today I recorded two episodes of 'I Disagree' in one breath, and an unprecedented collapse suddenly hit me... I am originally not a person who likes to quarrel with others like a fishwife. I don't know if I am too into the role, now whenever I see something slightly wrong with the people around me, I shout at them. And I argue with them over trivial matters... My temper has been so bad recently, I don't know why I have become like this."
