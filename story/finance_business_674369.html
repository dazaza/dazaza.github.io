Due to fierce competition and great social pressure, people are long-term in a nervous environment. Therefore, there are more and more people suffering from some chronic diseases or sub-health conditions. Some chronic diseases will have serious side effects if they are treated with drugs or surgery. TCM experts recommend trying the method of TCM acupoint massage for the treatment of some chronic diseases, which can not only effectively relieve or cure various diseases but also have no side effects. Here are some introductions.

1. Headache: Baihui acupoint can relieve most headaches.

Massage method: Outdoor high temperature and heat make people dizzy. When entering an indoor air-conditioned room with low temperature, the originally expanded blood vessels and muscles contract instantly, making the headache unbearable. At this time, you can press the Baihui acupoint (the intersection of two ears upwards to the top of the head) on the top of the head, which can make the whole body's qi and blood flow smoothly. The Shenmen acupoint (on the depression of the wrist transverse crease near the little finger side when looking up the palm and clenching the fist) has a calming and relaxing effect. The well-known Taiyang acupoint (in the depression between the tail of the eyebrow and the corner of the eye) can relieve the trouble of lateral headache. If the headache is biased towards the forehead, try massaging Hegu acupoint (place the transverse crease of the first joint of the thumb on the other hand's tiger mouth, and press down at the tip of the thumb, which is Hegu acupoint). If the back of the head hurts, you can embrace your head with both hands and press "Fengchi acupoint" (located on both sides of the posterior neck, at the junction of the hairline and the external side of the spine, which is the starting point of the neck muscle) to relieve symptoms.

2. Eye fatigue: Massage around the eyes along the orbital bone until feeling soreness and distension.

Eye fatigue

Staring at the computer, TV, reading books, or documents for a long time makes the eyes feel sour and uncomfortable. You can relieve it by pressing the acupoints around the eyes.

Massage method: Following the orbital bone, from the inner upper corner of both eyes outward, gently press the攒竹acupoint inside the eyebrows, the睛明acupoint inside the eyes, the承泣acupoint below the eyes where tears fall, and the Taiyang acupoint on both sides of the forehead, until feeling soreness and distension. This can improve the blood circulation of the eyes and stimulate the surrounding muscles of the entire eye to relieve discomfort.

Reminder: People with high eye pressure, glaucoma, hypertension, or redness, swelling, heat, and pain in the eyes should avoid massaging the acupoints.

3. Allergic rhinitis, cough: Yingxiang treats rhinitis, Shaoshang relieves cough.

Allergic Rhinitis

People with allergic constitutions will have more obvious symptoms when staying in an air-conditioned room in summer. Low temperatures will stimulate the nasal cavity and trachea, especially the dirty air conditioner filter that accumulates a large amount of dust mites, easily triggering respiratory problems, causing continuous runny nose, sneezing, and coughing.

Massage method for allergic rhinitis symptoms relief: Stimulate Yingxiang and Yintang acupoints. The Yingxiang acupoint is located at the nasolabial fold on the edge of the nostril, and the Yintang acupoint is in the middle of the two eyebrows. Both acupoints can disperse the heat and stagnation in the nose, and pressing until feeling warm can solve the problem. For sore throat or cough, try the Tianfu acupoint and Shaoshang acupoint. The Tianfu acupoint is located slightly concave in the middle of the upper edge of the manubrium sterni, which is the gateway for the intake and output of breath, and can relieve asthma, dissolve phlegm, and regulate qi. The Shaoshang acupoint belongs to the lung meridian and is located on the inner edge of the root of the thumb nail. It is a special acupoint for treating throat symptoms and can alleviate throat pain.

In addition, Fengchi acupoint can be pressed for daily health care. Fengchi acupoint is located at the depression where the neck meets the back of the head, about above the hairline. Fengchi acupoint follows the gallbladder meridian, which helps clear heat.
