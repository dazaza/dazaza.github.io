The Legend of Sword and Fairy 4 (Hard Drive Version)

by youxiruanjian88 on 2008-12-29 08:49:24

The scenes of the game world in "Chinese Paladin 4" (hereinafter referred to as "CP4") will make a great breakthrough compared with those in previous games. Since CP4 character images use real human proportions, the overall scene is completely made according to the real proportion, such as Xianggetai where Confucius was once stranded, and the bell and drum tower lane in the ancient city of Shouyang, etc., which perfectly present the Chinese classical style to all players.

The starting point of the story journey in CP4 is at the top of the Qingluan Peak of Mount Huangshan, galloping across the神州大地, traveling between heaven and earth, eastward to the Bohai Sea, southward to Bashu, northward to the legendary "Arctic Land", and westward to the Kunlun Mountain fairyland of the ancient sage. There are both prosperous and bustling places in Central Plains, and small stations on the Silk Road in the desert frontier passes. The architecture, grass and trees in each town, and even the food sold in the shops, all have their own characteristics.

The maze in CP4 also strives to reflect the unique landscape and architectural style of ancient China. As the protagonist's journey progresses, players can truly feel the magnificent scenery of rivers and mountains, the grandeur of ancient tombs, and the Taoist and immortal style of the blessed land.
