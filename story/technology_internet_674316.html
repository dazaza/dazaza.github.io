Top 10 Excellent Carpet Brands Ranking in 2012

by balidt on 2012-02-24 11:44:19

The 2012 China Brand Influence Summit was held at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing. Entrepreneurs’ brands were evaluated based on ten criteria: quality, service, credibility, social image, popularity, reputation, customer loyalty, economic benefits to the enterprise, influence on the industry, and impact on people's lives and the national economy. Based on these evaluations, the [Top Ten Excellent Carpet Brands of 2012] list was ultimately selected.

1st Place: Italian Milan Carpet (Brand website: www.milanb.com [http://www.milanb.com/])

Editor's Comment: The ITMILAN Italian Milan brand embodies the most avant-garde and popular styles in design and decoration, showcasing Milan's consistent sensuality, luxury, retro, and wild beauty through bold colors, lines, and beautiful floral patterns. It is both luxurious and simple, combining rationality with sensibility. Through the combination of indoor carpets, it expresses an emotional attachment to Milan, being half elegant and half noble from ancient Europe, perfectly interpreting modern urban home life with its delicate beauty.

2nd Place: British Royal Elizabeth Carpet (Brand website: www.elidt.com [http://www.elidt.com/])

Editor's Comment: Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Carpets and Wallpapers (a world-class carpet and fabric brand) let art bloom and fully enjoy taste. Royal Elizabeth completely integrates royal court design inspiration and color culture into the concept of home life, achieving a royal charm through unique craftsmanship and aesthetic personality. The charm of art and the beauty of life are fully displayed by Royal Elizabeth EILISHA Carpets and Wallpapers, showing a prestigious artistic taste.

3rd Place: Italian Rome Carpet (Brand website: www.luomab.com [http://www.luomab.com/])

Editor's Comment: ITRUOME Carpet completely incorporates Italian design inspiration and color culture into Chinese home concepts, achieving the charm of Chinese homes through unique craftsmanship and aesthetic personality. ITRUOME Carpet insists on environmental protection as a premise, whether modern, fashionable, classic, or traditional, different style trends of ITRUOME Carpet always make living environments lively, fully displaying artistic taste and winning widespread praise from consumers, being hailed as [China's Most Influential Brand].

4th Place: French Auchan Carpet (Brand website: www.asditan.com [http://www.asditan.com/])

Editor's Comment: French Auchan Carpet (China’s No. 1 carpet brand), the French Auchan Carpet brand was born in the late 1980s in France, just when the lifestyle carpet brands were challenging the aristocratic Parisian high-end carpet tradition. Auchan Carpet created a sense of luxury. Auchan Carpet is a chameleon in the world of Parisian high-end carpets; although it frequently hires renowned designers from various countries, the brand's style framework has not changed due to the change of designers, consistently maintaining the color characteristics and elegance of French style. The designer's personal involvement, along with the guarantee of Auchan Carpet's production and operation system, keeps the Auchan Carpet brand style in sync with the latest trends.

5th Place: German ASTRA, WISSENBACH Carpet (Brand website: www.astracarpets.co.uk [http://www.astracarpets.co.uk/])

Editor's Comment: Praised internationally in the industry as [Easy care], it is one of the easiest carpet materials to clean, favored by "lazy people," very suitable for home use, requiring only a low-temperature water rinse to wash away dust.

6th Place: Dutch ARTE ESPINA Carpet (Brand website: www.arteespina.com [http://www.arteespina.com/])

Editor's Comment: Arte Espina is a Dutch carpet company that integrates production and design, established in 2000. Most carpets are characterized by fashion and individual expression. The company owns a young and capable design team that continuously follows home living trend designs to create the latest carpet styles.

7th Place: Belgian Debo Carpet (Brand website: www.debomat.be [http://http://www.debomat.be/])

Editor's Comment: Initially, the Bolcart factory mainly produced velvet furniture decorative materials, exporting primarily to France and the UK. After years of growth and export experience, in the mid-1980s, Bolcart transformed into a machine-woven silk-like rug factory. Later, investments were increased in looms, acquiring electric jacquard machines combined with CAD design, enabling today's Bolcart products to have rich patterns.

8th Place: American KAS Carpet (Brand website: www.kascarpet.net [http://www.kascarpet.net/])

Editor's Comment: The famous American KAS Carpet, headquartered in New Jersey, USA, is a well-known importer of handmade and machine-woven rugs and also one of the main suppliers in the industry. KAS was founded in 1981 by Rao, Yarlagadda, and his wife Kas, initially serving as a small importer mainly selling Indian hand-spun cotton rugs. It quickly became a pioneer in carpet color and design. KAS has maintained a harmonious family business, which many customers appreciate.

9th Place: LOUVRE French Louvre Carpet (Brand website: www.lufugo.com [http://www.lufugo.com/])

Editor's Comment: LOUVRE French Louvre Carpet (the leading enterprise in the domestic and even Asian carpet industry, producing over 20 million rolls of various carpet fabrics annually). The “Louvreg” series of carpets has been ranked first in production, sales, and exports nationwide for consecutive years. The company's products are exported to 108 countries and regions including America, Europe, Australia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

10th Place: French Paris Carpet (Brand website: www.balidt.com [http://www.balidt.com/])

Editor's Comment: French Paris Carpet (one of the largest carpet manufacturing enterprises in Europe, and one of the top brands in the carpet industry this century). The French Paris Carpet Company is one of the largest carpet manufacturing enterprises in Europe, with advanced production equipment, stable product quality, and nearly thirty years of carpet production experience. The company has established a retail, wholesale sales network and franchise system centered in Guangzhou, covering the whole country and expanding overseas, developing into a diversified global company trusted by consumers at home and abroad, and one of the top brands in the carpet industry this century.

I believe the above rankings are relatively objective, but they are provided for reference only!
