(Old Affairs Touching)

【ONE】

Our love, it felt like opium enticing one's senses—no barrier of time, no restriction of place. We could indulge in our revelry whenever we wanted. Su Yixian was lying on the couch watching a soap opera on TV, munching on an apple, and she said this to me. My face immediately flushed with embarrassment. Such shameless words coming from a seventeen-year-old girl hit my ears like those of a woman from Loulan. It seemed Su Yixian didn't notice the change in my expression. She leered at the kissing scene between the male and female protagonists on TV, her eyes glued without blinking. I couldn't help but feel disheartened by today's generation of post-90s kids. Fortunately, I myself was born in '89 and wasn’t categorized as part of the post-90s group. I gave her a disdainful look, signaling her to mind her image. However, she instead scolded me furiously. "Xiang Xiaomi, stop acting so naive all the time. Seeing you old spinsters pretending to be pure makes me sick. So what if people kiss or have sex? These are natural needs for everyone." She glared at me while taking a big bite out of her apple.

Such vulgar thoughts that others might find disgusting were spoken so shamelessly by Su Yixian. She claimed being a mistress was a shortcut to success. No one would expect a seventeen-year-old girl in her prime to hang out in bars every day. She was the type of girl who thrived amidst cigarette smoke. Only I didn't despise her. Only I gave her a place to stay, cooked for her, washed her clothes. I did everything for her, leaving no stone unturned. Yet, she still refused to give me any respect. Su Yixian said, "Your mom stole my dad and even caused my mom's death. This is an unsettled debt forever hanging over us." Xiang Xiaomi was born to repay my mom's debts. I had to take care of Su Yixian and endure her haughty attitude. From initial resistance to eventual gradual acceptance, I learned to let her wild and rebellious words pass through one ear and out the other.

【TWO】

Whenever Xiao Dian came to my house, he avoided seeing Su Yixian. Generally, he tried to steer clear of her, saying, "Su Yixian is a demoness, a despicable and vulgar demoness." I always thought Xiao Dian compared Su Yixian this way because of her background and job. After all, Xiao Dian and I had been dating for over three years, and I understood him to some extent. He detested the kind of rich young ladies who expected everything handed to them, and also looked down on lowly bar girls like Su Yixian, considering such women as incapable as disabled people.

Later, I realized that my previous belief was just self-deception. One morning, Su Yixian returned home as usual, exhausted. Early in the morning, the house reeked of foul odors mixed with a pungent alcohol smell. When I woke up, I saw Su Yixian vomiting messily outside my room door. I knew she did it intentionally. I pinched my nose, patting her back to help her breathe. Normally, Su Yixian didn't drink in the morning, and even if she did, she never drank enough to vomit. Su Yixian laughed maniacally, saying, "Xiang Xiaomi, you're beautiful, just like Song Hye-kyo in the Korean drama I'm watching now. But Dian Ge still slept with me. To him, you're just a decoration. Hahaha..."

I looked at her helplessly. Despite her pale face, Su Yixian's smile was stunningly beautiful, truly enchanting. No wonder those men seeking pleasure were so captivated by her. Even Xiao Dian, whom I loved passionately, fell under her spell. Actually, I should have realized it earlier. Every time Xiao Dian saw Su Yixian in front of me, he avoided her gaze, as if Su Yixian were a devouring monster that made him afraid to look at her.

【THREE】

When Xiao Dian came to explain, it was night, and Su Yixian had already gone to work. In the dark outline of the night, I sat on the floor, smoking cigars lethally. I didn't let Xiao Dian turn on the light. Three years ago, I was also smoking cigars like crazy in such a dark night at the cliffside west of the city. I smoked pack after pack until I got a bloody nose, almost fainting. Xiao Dian saved me that night, carrying me horizontally to the hospital just before I lost consciousness. Eventually, he took full responsibility and sent me home.

The last time I smoked cigars recklessly was when my father, on his way to prison, entrusted me to take care of his young daughter, Su Yixian. Su Yixian's mother was my father's lover. My mother said Su Yixian's mother was seductive, just like Su Yixian herself, stealing my father away and giving birth to a daughter as bewitching as her. At that moment, my father personally told me about having a illegitimate daughter. He also mentioned that my mother knew about it. In that instant, I felt immense hatred towards the man I once admired. I wanted to slap him hard, but he was already being escorted by the police.

Xiao Dian silently watched as I threw cigarette butts one after another in front of him. He was like a gloomy owl hiding in the darkness, staring at me with sharp eyes filled with tangled emotions. When I finished my eleventh cigar, I suddenly ran out of the door. Covering my mouth, I ran as fast as I could, tears finally streaming down my face, falling onto the ground and being absorbed by the long-awaited dust. Unexpectedly, Xiao Dian followed me. By the time I reached Phoenix Cliff in the west of the city, I was drenched in sweat. Just as I sat down to rest, Xiao Dian appeared panting heavily in front of me. He said, "Mimi, I love you more than I love Su Yixian." I buried my head in my knees, secretly licking my wounds. At a moment like this, I wouldn't let him see me cry. He said he loved me more than Su Yixian, which meant that in his heart, I was merely the first choice for marriage. If I weren't around, he might have chosen Su Yixian as his girlfriend. Subconsciously, I remained silent, and he quietly stood behind me.

【THREE】

I love the bone-chilling wind of winter; it penetrates my skin, cools my marrow, and gives me strength to struggle after being intensely stimulated. Late at night, I lay on the cold stone ground, letting the freezing wind envelop me. Xiao Dian took off his coat and covered me with it. I grabbed his coat and threw it over the edge of the cliff, completely disregarding his shocked expression. It was probably around 2:00 AM. After the security personnel at Phoenix Cliff turned off the lights and rested, the entire cliff became eerily quiet, devoid of any living soul. I lay there looking at the dark, swirling sky until my eyes grew sore before blinking. Meanwhile, Xiao Dian was snoring loudly like a dead pig. I turned my face to look at him, gazing for a long time... recalling the sunny boy from three years ago.

Back then, my mother killed Su Qianfang in Dongjie People's Alley. Afterwards, my father, in a fit of rage, pushed my mother from the 17th floor. After my mother's death, my father was arrested by the police. At eighteen, standing alone on the edge of Phoenix Cliff, wanting to escape this world, it was Xiao Dian who gave me hope to live. He called me Mimi, a nickname only my parents used. Everyone else called me Xiaomi. I remember Su Yixian once said, "Xiang Xiaomi, your parents really gave you a fitting name. You're like honey, always tempting others." That was how everyone mocked me.

Xiao Dian was sound asleep, not waking up even though I cried loudly in my consciousness. Towards him, I felt both love and hatred. A classmate once said, "If a man is constantly chasing after other women, it's better to let go early." I wondered, if I hadn't met Xiao Dian at eighteen, or even if I had never fallen in love with him, what would my life be like now? Maybe I would have died already, or maybe I would be living a life that isn't human-like and ghost-like. Squatting beside Xiao Dian, I silently asked myself in my heart: Xiao Dian, how exactly should I treat you? You've done so much for me, like the sweet spring water I long for in life. You awakened my world, saved my life, taught me love, and later supported me in taking care of Su Yixian. You deceived me mercilessly and found comfort in someone else. Should I repay you or seek revenge on you? I know Su Yixian deliberately let Xiao Dian sleep with her. She knew how much I loved Xiao Dian. Because she once said, her purpose in life was to see me suffer.

At three in the morning, I saw Xiao Dian turning over and licking the saliva drooling from his mouth. Slowly, he rolled over, and in that instant, a terrifying idea popped into my mind. Without hesitation, I acted. I extended my delicate hands from behind him and applied force on his back, helping him flip over. In that brief moment, he plummeted to his death. I watched Xiao Dian fall like a meteor into the fortress below the cliff. The bottom was a patch of green stones, occasionally with a thin stream of water flowing through. I liked the moss on that slope, which reminded me of the oasis in life, decorating the continent humans had long sought after.

【FOUR】

Xiao Dian was dead, killed by my own hands. Am I not great? At eight in the morning, I sat in the living room of my house waiting for Su Yixian to finish her shift. As soon as she walked in, I told her this. Her expression changed from shock to astonishment, all captured in my eyes. Su Yixian said, "Dian has no sin. He himself is a good man. It was me who seduced him. Hahaha... Xiang Xiaomi, you're naturally a woman who's cheap to the core. You can even kill the Dian who loves you so much." Su Yixian was extremely happy, yet sadness lingered in her eyes. She smiled seductively, but inside, her heart was tearing apart. Looking at her, I felt a bit of compassion.

【FIVE】

Xiao Dian wasn't the only man for me. I'd rather he die than share him with anyone else. Therefore, if he dies, I must die too. But from now on, Su Yixian will have to carry on alone. Taking care of her for three years has tired me out. Three years ago, I promised my father to take good care of her. But by the end of last year, she started working as a bar hostess, drinking and sleeping with different men every day. A flower of youth choosing such a self-destructive path is beyond my control. Starting from noon, I was busy non-stop, dressing myself like a fairy. Wearing a white wedding dress, I stood at the edge of Phoenix Cliff. Oh, Phoenix Cliff, the place that fascinates me. Since childhood, every time I cried, I would come here and wail. Feeling a lump in my throat, I glanced one last time at the setting sun in the west of the city, its beauty appearing seductive. But no matter how beautiful the sunset, it couldn't surpass my own beauty.

I gently floated down from Phoenix Cliff, my pure white wedding dress and veil fluttering in the wind, dazzlingly surrounding me. I felt like an angel slowly descending. I vaguely saw Xiao Dian standing on the green stones below, looking up at me with a smile, arms open wide, waiting for me to slowly fall into his embrace.
