As an online store, if you want to develop and make long-term progress, the first thing that will bother you is the source of goods. How to find a competitive and reliable source of goods? I believe this issue causes headaches and sadness for countless store owners. However, for large sellers and big companies, this situation is not too difficult to solve. Based on their conditions and qualifications, finding a source of goods is an easy task.

In Taobao, although there are many store owners, very few have become large sellers or big companies. Even though we all know that Lemon Green Tea has grown significantly, how many Lemon Green Teas can Taobao produce? By saying this, I am not trying to discourage you or belittle e-commerce, but this industry still needs adjustment, accumulation, sedimentation, and time. However, the prospect is infinitely bright. As it says:

Thirty years ago, physical stores changed destinies; thirty years later, online stores change lives.

Since online stores have such a promising future, we should strive to do well, seek good sources of goods, and compete with other friends. During my days as a checker in the Store Owner Special Zone, I noticed many questions about sourcing goods while on duty. Below, I will summarize these issues to hopefully be of help to you. If it's not well written, consider it as a brick to draw jade.

1. What should beginners do, and which industry should they enter?

Explanation: There is no standard for this. The most crucial point is to base it on your own situation.

1) If you are an office worker and don't have much time, it is suggested to sell products with lower added value (for example, clothing, accessories, and other small items) or do some agency work (currently, Taobao does not allow agency work, so when doing agency work, follow the relevant rules. There is a lot of agency information on Alibaba, and friends can take a look). The reason for doing this is to minimize risks because running a Taobao store is not an easy task. Don't think that sitting in front of a computer praying is enough. It requires a lot of energy and time, testing one's willpower and perseverance. Without a certain level of endurance, it's hard to survive. If you purchase a large amount of goods, as a beginner, you haven't learned many things yet, lack the ability to quickly sell the products, and the products may depreciate and become outdated, causing losses and severely affecting motivation. Therefore, friends need to consider potential problems in the future.

2) If you have idle money, can afford losses, and don't worry about having no food to eat, then it's recommended to go into industries that interest you and that you are familiar with. Nothing can lead to success more easily than interest.

3) Regardless of the situation, always base it on reality. Although the store may be small, it is still part of entrepreneurship, so plan and prepare carefully.

2. Where to buy goods and how to buy them?

Explanation: Currently, store owners mainly use these methods to buy goods:

1. Manufacturers

2. Wholesale markets

3. Online

Below, I will explain in detail:

1. Manufacturer Goods

Because manufacturers offer low prices, we all want to get goods directly from the manufacturer, but in reality, how many people can actually obtain goods directly from the manufacturer? Manufacturers usually have volume requirements, and if you don't meet certain conditions, even if you get the goods, they won't be cheap. Of course, if you know someone, it's different. Here, you shouldn't simply blame or complain about the manufacturer because they also have their difficulties.

2. Buying from wholesale markets. (Taking clothing as an example)

1) Look like a buyer. For instance, your clothing and appearance should resemble someone buying goods. Don't dress more stylishly than a star, thinking you're in a fashion show. Who else would they overcharge but you? Also, buying goods is physically demanding, so simplicity is best. Having a small cart or a black plastic bag is better, almost equivalent to the symbol of clothing purchasing. This method is quite effective, as I've personally tried it. When I go shopping, I carry a bag and pull a small cart, rushing to the market. Wow, as soon as I arrive, those cute little sisters look at me with lustful eyes, calling me boss left and right, making me feel great inside. They offer discounts and minimum quantities, making me laugh awake that night.

2) Have determination when purchasing goods. Plan before leaving, determine what goods to buy and how much money to bring. Especially for beginners, pay extra attention. In the wholesale market, money is never enough, and those beautiful sisters won't care if you have bus fare back home. Don't let them confuse you, and don't go crazy buying just because you like something. Remember, the goods aren't for you, they're for your customers, and you don't represent their tastes. When I buy goods in the wholesale market, the sisters don't let me leave, holding the catalog in my hand and refusing to let me put it down. Looking at the sisters' hopeful eyes, I could only grit my teeth and turn around and walk away, even though I was reluctant. But I knew how much money I had left. You can refer to my experience and know when to say no.

3) Don't rush to buy goods. First, visit several shops, check the general price of clothes, and don't be deceived. In the morning, wholesale is the main business, so shopkeepers can go earlier to get more favorable prices. When buying goods, ask clearly how to purchase and how to wholesale. Ask anything you don't understand, don't be shy about what others might say. The market is so large, there's more than one shop. If they don't treat you well, move on to other friendly shops.

In summary, visiting wholesale markets isn't an easy task, especially for beginners. Due to lack of experience, they can't get very low-priced goods and must meticulously plan and calculate, wasting a lot of effort. Why? It's like a newly graduated university student sitting at the same table as a leader. The leader handles situations effortlessly, while you are nervous and far from being their match. Moreover, many cities don't have such wholesale markets. So what do we, who run online stores, do?

Don't worry, we have another option—online sourcing!

3. Online Sourcing

Personally, I believe that with the development of e-commerce, online sourcing will become a mainstream way. Currently, for beginners, online sourcing is undoubtedly the preferred way to source goods because the threshold is relatively low, requiring no connections with manufacturers or visits to wholesale markets. And for experienced sellers, even crown-level sellers, it remains a good channel to enrich their shops. Regarding online sourcing, there are many websites now claiming to offer this service, but the quality varies greatly. Here, I only recommend Alibaba, not for any other reason, but first, it's at least under the same company as Taobao, and Alipay is available.

Previously, people might have had the impression that although there are many items on Alibaba, the listed prices are not low, and even when finding low-priced bulk purchases, the quantity required often doesn't match, with constant concerns about quality issues. However, this situation is improving. Alibaba has specifically created a sourcing platform for Taobao store owners—the Small Order Wholesale Platform.

From what I understand, suppliers joining this Small Order Wholesale Platform must meet three conditions:

1. Support Alipay Escrow Transactions;

2. Support small-quantity wholesaling;

3. Prices are the most cost-effective among similar sources.

Currently, many suppliers have joined the Small Order Wholesale and collaborate with Alibaba to conduct activities, offering very low prices. Additionally, participating merchants provide actual product images, allowing direct viewing of the quality of the sources. Thus, compared to other online sources, the new Alibaba offers absolute advantages in both quality and price.

Due to the improved transaction security and image quality after the reform, the new Alibaba is most suitable for beginners. You can search through a variety of products to find ones that suit your budget and interests to serve as your store's sources.

Here are the key points I've summarized for sourcing small orders on Alibaba:

1. Must choose an Integrity Pass user;

2. Must choose one that supports Alipay Escrow Transactions;

Do not be tempted by items that do not support it.

3. Among similar sources, choose suppliers with actual product photos;

This allows for a direct view of product quality. Meanwhile, pay attention to other details such as return policies and logistics. Start with a small order to confirm the quality before deciding whether to add more products.

4. Pay attention to activities on the Small Order Wholesale Platform;

Current common activities include "Direct Supply from Factories," "Special Supply for Drilling," and "Premium Street." These are negotiated by Alibaba staff with suppliers, generally offering high cost-effectiveness, and most come with actual photos.

5. Compare and contrast;

How to do it? Besides using search and filters, here’s another method: Observe the update speed of the store. Check how often the products in their store are updated and whether they can keep up with market trends. This helps determine if the supplier is dedicated to managing their online channels, and typically such web merchants offer good services. Through comparing similar products, you can also identify which store updates fastest, as only factories or large wholesalers can access the latest and trendiest products, making it easier to find the source of the products.

How to obtain cheap and high-quality goods is an important link in opening a store. The choice of sources will directly affect the entrepreneur's profit and income status, so it is essential to be cautious in choosing sources. From the perspective of the universality of online operations, sources can be sought from three aspects: manufacturers, primary wholesalers, secondary or even tertiary wholesalers. Generally speaking, the closer the procurement method is to the source, the greater the profit margin. However, each level of wholesaler has its pros and cons, and entrepreneurs with different strengths should carefully choose based on their real situations.

There are not many commodities that can be sourced directly from manufacturers because most manufacturers disdain dealing with small-scale sellers. Typically, manufacturers require very high initial quantities, making them unsuitable for small-scale wholesalers.

Large wholesalers or primary wholesalers are relatively easy to find. Usually, search engines like Baidu or Google can find many of them, and there are also specialized source websites with rich and comprehensive information. Some e-commerce websites can find various sizes of wholesalers for different sources, and some services and reputations are relatively good. They generally supply goods directly from the factory, ensuring stable sources. However, due to the large number of orders, services inevitably lag behind. Large wholesalers have slow shipping times, which is understandable, but the biggest challenge lies in returns, where requests for returns are often rejected or the returned goods are unsatisfactory, and refunds are impossible. Experienced individuals must clarify product details and service methods before ordering. If the order amount is large, it is best to sign a standardized sales contract to avoid disputes later.

When encountering difficulties working with large wholesalers, you can turn to secondary wholesalers. Secondary wholesalers are usually those who have just transitioned from retail to wholesale, understanding the sources and customer demands of this industry better. Since they are just starting out, without fixed wholesale clients or recognition, they require smaller initial quantities, and prices are generally no higher, or sometimes even lower than large wholesalers. Entrepreneurs can negotiate terms with them based on their purchasing experience, such as price and return policies. To attract repeat customers, their after-sales service is generally good. However, the obvious drawback of secondary wholesalers is the issue of integrity. Therefore, it is best to test the waters with small-scale cooperation before engaging in large-scale collaborations after understanding their practices and service attitudes.

The reason why online stores have space is that low costs are an important factor. Mastering cost-effective sources is the key to operating e-commerce successfully. Taking clothing items as an example, many well-known brands have uniform national prices, and in regular physical stores, the lowest discount is 85%. However, on the internet, clothing items are sold at 20% to 70% of mall prices.

So, how can you find low-cost sources?

1. Become a Market Hunter: Closely monitor market changes and fully utilize discounted goods to find affordable sources. For example, sellers often find high-quality branded clothing during seasonal changes or at special sale outlets and resell them online, utilizing regional or temporal price differences to gain sufficient profits. Some cosmetics sellers, after becoming familiar with the managers of high-end cosmetics counters, can obtain goods at a discount of up to 70% before new products hit the market and sell them online at 90% of the counter price, yielding higher profits due to the high prices of cosmetics.

2. Focus on Foreign Trade Products: Foreign trade products have always been popular due to their quality, style, fabric, and price advantages. Many internationally renowned products priced at hundreds of dollars abroad are sold for only a few hundred yuan RMB online, attracting numerous buyers. New online entrepreneurs with familiar foreign trade manufacturers can directly source goods from the factory. Among the remaining products from foreign trade orders, there are many good items, mostly limited to 1-3 pieces, often featuring next year's or currently trending styles, priced at only 40%-70% of mall prices, making them highly marketable.

3. Purchase Brand Surplus Inventory: Some brand-name goods have substantial inventory surpluses, and some merchants simply sell all their inventory to professional online sellers. Brand-name goods are one of the most watched categories online, with many buyers searching directly for their favorite brands. Moreover, although some brands may be surplus goods in one region, the broad coverage of the internet can make them bestsellers in other regions. If you have excellent bargaining skills and can acquire their surplus inventory at low prices, you can achieve substantial profits.

4. Obtain Overseas Discounted Goods: World-class brands abroad offer very cheap prices during seasonal changes or before holidays. If sellers have relatives or friends overseas, they can ask them to help obtain attractive discounts for online sales. Even if the selling price is 40%-70% of traditional mall prices, there is still a 10%-40% profit margin. This sales method is gaining attention from some international students. For example, the store operated by the Japanese student "Peach Boy" sells Japan's latest cosmetics and beauty nutritional supplements, transported by air to domestic and even other countries. Because its cosmetics are fresh, launched faster than domestic counters, and cheaper, they are widely pursued. Additionally, some American and European students also sell top-tier brands like "Victoria's Secret" and "LV" apparel and luggage online, with profits exceeding 30%.

5. Bulk Purchases: It is essential to frequently visit regional wholesale markets to familiarize yourself with the market and obtain very cheap wholesale prices. After finding a source, you can initially try selling a small quantity. If sales are good, consider increasing your purchase volume. Online, some sellers have good relationships with suppliers and only purchase goods after they are sold, thus avoiding capital occupation and product stockpiling. In short, regardless of the channel used to find sources, low prices are a critical factor. Finding cost-effective sources lays the foundation for the success of your online store.

Comparison of Several Store Sourcing Methods

1. Find the local wholesale market and select goods purposefully.

[Advantages] Sources are self-selected, products are self-picked, ensuring quality.

[Disadvantages] Transportation is a consideration; weather is a consideration; after-sales service needs consideration; pricing needs consideration; risk of overstocking needs consideration; handling pictures oneself...

2. If there's a wholesale market nearby, just go out and look for whatever goods you need.

[Advantages] No risk of overstocking, no transportation issues, no weather concerns, and personal assurance of product quality.

[Disadvantages] Perhaps only the price issue since you buy less... Also, handling pictures yourself. If there's anything else, it might be that the place where you live is lively.

3. Operate as a proxy store for a physical store.

[Advantages] No worries about shipping or product quality (of course, quality issues still need to be considered, preferably visiting the store and checking its scale), no need for significant capital, fixed purchase price, no need to handle pictures.

[Disadvantages] Suppliers have already determined the courier company, cannot change it; if the provided pictures are poor, you cannot handle them; if customers need more product information, you may not be able to answer or provide additional pictures.

4. Operate as a proxy store for a large online store.

[Advantages] No worries about shipping or pictures, no need for significant capital, fixed purchase price.

[Disadvantages] Suppliers have already determined the courier company, cannot change it; if the provided pictures are poor, you cannot handle them; if customers need more product information, you may not be able to answer or provide additional pictures. Another important point is that you cannot accurately judge the product quality.

5. Operate as a proxy store for a distant wholesaler without a physical or online store.

[Advantages] None.

[Disadvantages] You cannot correctly verify the authenticity of the product information provided by the supplier; usually, orders are placed through online communication via QQ, MSN, or a phone number. They will require payment before shipping. Everyone knows that network information resources are abundant, and pictures can be obtained from anywhere.

6. Locate a dense sales area for a particular product and place orders online for bulk purchases. Generally, these sales areas are far from your location.

[Advantages] Online prices may be very cheap, with ample sources, and suppliers may provide some pictures.

[Disadvantages] You may still need to handle some pictures yourself; product quality cannot be guaranteed until you receive the goods; there's a risk of overstocking; funds may not circulate smoothly.

7. Already have a physical store and simultaneously open a network market.

[Advantages] Without mentioning, there are definitely many advantages, benefits, and profits. After all, businesses need to be promoted through different channels to earn from multiple avenues. Otherwise, why would so many manufacturers choose to advertise and spend so much on advertising fees?

[Disadvantages] May sometimes be too busy to attend to online customers.

However, manufacturers usually require a high minimum order quantity, which is unsuitable for small wholesalers. If you have sufficient capital reserves and no risk of overstocking or don't fear overstocking, then go to the manufacturer for sourcing.

2. Large Wholesalers:

Advantages: Relatively easy to find, generally using Baidu can find many. They usually get supplies directly from the manufacturer, with Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., providing relatively stable sources.

Disadvantages: Because they are large, they have many orders, and services inevitably lag behind. Moreover, they usually have fixed repeat customers and don't fear lacking wholesalers, making it difficult to negotiate terms unless you place orders many times and become one of their major customers, potentially earning special discounts or preferences. The real problem lies in returns. Sometimes the received items may have defects, especially jewelry. These items were given to friends to wear casually; selling them would invite complaints.
