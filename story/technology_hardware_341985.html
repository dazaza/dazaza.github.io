Only by being content with what we have can we make ourselves happy-bm

by liyuner345 on 2010-04-08 09:51:46

Is there such a thing as eternal love? There certainly is! So why do the majority of people not believe it exists? Because they haven't found the one most suited for them on their journey through life. That is, the one destined for them. Why can't they find this person? In the vast sea of humanity, life is fleeting, like dew, and finding the one most suited for you is no simple task.

Romance is originally a gamble without a final win or loss. It stays with us until the end of our lives, and only then does the conclusion of love come about. Regardless of what awaits us in the end, having love in our hearts is enough. I think cherishing the present moment is more practical! Life is long, and who can keep the one they love by their side forever? And who can say that losing them means losing all meaning? The outcome is not the value; the worth lies with the true masters of love themselves—no one has the right to judge! People, when will you wake up? When will you be content? When will you consider someone like me?

To live, one must be content! Only through contentment can one find satisfaction. People, everything I do is for the sake of my family, for all of you. But when have you ever considered me? What surprises me is that three years ago, I watched them meet, get engaged, and marry, feeling that everything between them was so natural. If it's really as she says, that they don't possess the kind of love they once had, then what were all those things before? They once proudly displayed their love right in front of me. Wasn't that love? I think it isn't as she says, that they don't possess love anymore. To respect and understand others more, one should always harbor tolerance and gratitude. Tolerance is a virtue, a wisdom. By embracing rivers, the sea becomes vast. Thank your enemies, for they give you help; thank your friends, for they make you stronger.

But over the years, the trivialities and pressures of life have eroded some of the brilliance of love, even making her unable to see the morning light of love. Sometimes, I too feel lost in marriage, unable to see the light of love. People, if you just pay attention every day, everything will belong to you. Have you thought about it this way? No... Every day you do nothing but ask your family for money. Why don't you consider how hard it is to even make a profit? People live every day alternating between happiness and sorrow. If your body is healthy and free from disease, your quality of life improves. If in life you avoid the dangers of war, the loneliness of prison, the suffering of torture, and the taste of hunger, then you are living a happy and sorrowful life. If your home enjoys four generations under one roof, a happy marriage, and harmonious living, then you are enjoying worldly pleasures. If you have both spiritual abundance and material wealth, life becomes even more beautiful. People, now you have the responsibility to prepare to be a boss. Why aren't you satisfied?

Don't get engaged out of loneliness. Time is a wolf. Over the long term, if you're an emotional person, even without love for the other party, feelings may develop over time. What will you do then? Friends are an unsolvable mystery. As we grow up, we make more friends, expanding our social circles. While undergoing qualitative changes, they also experience quantitative changes. Some friends might be lifelong treasures, while others could become graves that bury us. The degree of interaction should always maintain a safe distance. Too far and you risk estrangement or even loss; too close and conflicts or wounds may arise, leading to loss as well.

Life is long, and who can keep the one they love by their side forever? And who can say that losing them means losing all meaning? The outcome is not the value; the worth lies with the true masters of love themselves—no one has the right to judge!
