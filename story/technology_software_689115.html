Yesterday, the weddings of two couples attracted the attention of many citizens. What should the groom wear when getting married? A black evening gown or a white suit? No, that's not fashionable. Yesterday, at the wedding of Senquan Yang, he was dressed in a green dinosaur costume, holding a large inflatable hammer, "roaring" as he smashed open the door to the bride's room, completely taking control of the scene. Yesterday was also a great day for the marriage of Zhou Jie and Yong Nie. Unlike the usual wedding convoys, their wedding car was a four-wheel drive electric car. Although the short journey from Xiangfu International Hotel to Wangfu Restaurant took nearly two hours, Zhou Jie said this wasn't done for showing off or seeking attention, but for romance and environmental protection.
