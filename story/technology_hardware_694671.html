May 5th quotation for the three major components: divergence in DRAM rise and fall, HDD price eases

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-07 11:04:40

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, Intel processor prices remained stable with no changes to mainstream processors.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for AMD processors, AMD processor prices dropped significantly. The FX8120/FX8150 eight-core processors fell by hundreds of yuan, quoted at 1420 yuan and 1720 yuan respectively. The boxed X4 641 saw a small drop of 5 yuan, quoted at 440 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop memory spot market, memory prices continued to fluctuate. Samsung memory prices rose against the trend, while other brands remained flat or slightly decreased.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook memory spot market, memory prices increased again. Samsung 4GB DDR3-1600 rose by 10 yuan, and Kingston 4GB DDR3-1600 increased by 5 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop hard drive spot market, hard drive prices fell for five consecutive days. 2TB hard drives dropped to 770 yuan, but the decline in 500GB/1TB hard drives slowed down. If there are any changes in hard drive price trends, ZOL will inform everyone as soon as possible.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook hard drive spot market, hard drive prices saw a slight drop. Hitachi's 500GB/5400 RPM hard drives fell by 10 yuan, quoted at 485 yuan.

Intel processors experienced minor fluctuations today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market, with SNB processor prices rising and falling (the following prices were obtained by editors from counters in Zhongguancun, provided by certified agents, and are subject to change, for reference only).

Boxed i7-3930K: Boxed i7-3930K fell by a hundred yuan, quoted at 4500 yuan.
