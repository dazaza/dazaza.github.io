Corporate Portal: Building a Network Home for Enterprises

by topxfcjf on 2010-07-10 11:40:15

In the 21st century information society, failing to digitize a company's products or not having a website that centrally showcases the company’s products and image is undoubtedly like continuing manual labor in an era of mechanized production... An individual may sustain their livelihood by farming a few acres of land, but as a company, it must strive for external expansion and capture more market share. Therefore, companies need to extend their reach as much as possible to meet the needs of people in different places, thereby allowing the company to grow.

 

Website as the Best Extension (Corporate Companion)

In the Internet age, information allows corporate reach to extend worldwide; a website serves as an ideal platform for centralized display of various corporate information. Different companies may have varying requirements for their corporate websites. The basic requirement is to showcase all product information and present a good corporate image. A higher-level requirement is to enable online order management and transactions. The highest level requirement is to seamlessly integrate all corporate activities into the website... Thus, the concept of a corporate portal begins to take shape.

 

Encyclopedia Explanation of Corporate Portal

A corporate portal is a website that connects the internal and external aspects of a company. It can provide a single entry point for accessing various corporate information resources. Employees, customers, partners, suppliers, etc., can all obtain personalized information and services through this portal. A corporate portal can seamlessly integrate a company's content, commerce, and community: first, through the corporate portal, a company can dynamically release various information stored internally and externally; secondly, the corporate portal can complete online transactions; additionally, the corporate portal can support online virtual communities where users can discuss and exchange information.

 

The Launch of the Corporate Portal Concept
