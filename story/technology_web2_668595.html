I gently approached him, and he seemed fearful and tried to retreat. After washing and dressing, I hastily ate a bowl of white rice before being led to the young master's room. When I arrived, I saw two tree spirits standing outside with a large lock hanging there. I found it somewhat amusing that a young master as frail and scholarly as this one would need such elaborate precautions, clearly Mother Wang was truly worried about the young master running away.

When I pushed open the door, my vision went dark for a moment. The room was exceptionally dim, the windows were tightly locked, making it both stifling and hot. As soon as I stepped in, the door behind me was closed again. Not having time to consider these details, I wanted to quickly find the young master. Once my eyes adjusted, I realized the room was almost empty.

Searching for the young master, I noticed a shadow crouched in the corner. As I slowly moved closer, I was startled by what I saw. There sat a disheveled figure, clothes in tatters, arms tightly hugging their own body, head buried in their chest, cowering in the corner as if trying to make themselves invisible.

"Is that you, Young Master?" I asked softly, afraid that even the slightest sound might startle the person in front of me.

The head lifted slowly, and I could see his eyes. My heart suddenly ached. What had once been clear, water-like eyes were now clouded, soulless, devoid of any spirit.

It seems I underestimated the situation. Clearly, something more must have happened to the young master. How did he become like this?

I gently knelt closer to him. He looked at me warily as he retreated. "Don't be afraid, Young Master, it's me, A-san!"

He paused, lifting his head slightly, examining me carefully. I leaned down so he could recognize me clearly. Suddenly, the young master leapt onto me, losing balance and pushing us both to the ground. His head landed on my chest, and his arms tightly embraced me as he cried and laughed, shouting: "A-san! A-san! It really is you! You're not dead! Haha, you're alive!"

"Yes, yes! I'm alive! I'm alive!" Unable to push away the emotional young man above me, I gave up, thinking that if this could comfort his heart, then even if my hands were crushed, it would be worth it.

After a long while, the young master calmed down and sat up, leaning against me. His icy body gradually warmed up. We looked at each other silently, deeply sighing over our almost lost reunion.

But as time passed, this position started to feel somewhat ambiguous. Lying on the ground with one person pressing on top of another, our bodies closely pressed together, our faces so close that our breaths almost intertwined. His cracked lips were just near mine, requiring only a slight movement to touch.

Looking into the young master's eyes, which once held a lifeless void but now showed signs of clarity, I thought he must have regained his senses.

"Eh, Young Master! Can you get off me? I can't breathe," I whispered.

Realizing this, the young master turned red in the face. "I-I'm sorry!! I'll get up immediately!"

Unfortunately, moving too abruptly, he stumbled, resulting in me unintentionally receiving my first kiss in my life!

The young master immediately widened his eyes, jumping off me. His face turned bright red like a boiled shrimp. "I-I'm sorry! I..."

For a long time, he couldn't say anything. Climbing back up, I felt reassured seeing the young master's spirit improved, showing some vitality, unlike earlier when he seemed lifeless. I gently knelt closer to him, but he still retreated fearfully.

"It's fine, Young Master! Accidents happen! Just forget about it!" I smiled nervously. "After all, we're modern people! One kiss... and anyway, you're so beautiful, how can I lose in this?"

Seeing that I wasn't upset, the young master just lowered his head without saying anything, standing there lost in thought. But I noticed his long hair draped over his shoulders, making his already thin frame seem even more delicate. Thinking about the hardships he must have endured during our escape, and the fact that he hadn't eaten or drunk anything for days, how could he not be thinner? With such a weak body, how could he possibly escape from this silver prison? No, I needed to wait until the young master fully recovered his strength before attempting any escape plans.

"Young Master, you've suffered!" My voice trembled.

The young master's whole body shook, immediately enveloped in deep sorrow. "A-san, do you know? That day, you shouldn't have saved me..."

I sighed. "If I had the chance to turn back time, I would still choose to save you!"

"That day, saving me resulted in this worse-than-death outcome. It would have been better if you hadn't saved me at all!"

Such self-deprecating words infuriated me. "Young Master, are you so foolish? If you die like this, do you think your parents in the netherworld would be proud of you?"

The young master immediately looked up at me, anger flashing in his eyes. "Living like this now, how can I talk about pride? My father and mother must surely be ashamed of me and reluctant to see me."

"Is this how you repay your parents?" I took a step forward, closing the distance between us, calling out his full name directly, causing him to pause in surprise and look at me curiously.

"Think carefully. Why did your family suffer such calamities, leaving only you alive? And why did A-san risk his life to protect you? Furthermore, why did I stop you from committing suicide? Don't you understand the deeper meaning behind all this?"

The young master stared at me intently, no longer filled with sadness but instead with suspicion. "What deeper meaning? What secrets?"

"This is destiny guiding your path." I explained earnestly. "Young Master, as someone who understands, you should know that when heaven bestows great responsibilities upon someone, it will first test their resolve, strengthen their will, tire their muscles, starve their bodies, and place them in difficult situations, all to challenge their hearts and develop their abilities."

Hearing this, the young master bowed his head, muttering my words softly. The frown on his face gradually smoothed out as if he began to understand. Then he looked up, his eyes brighter and clearer. "A-san, are you saying this is a test for me?"

I nodded slightly, but the young master shook his head bitterly. "The price is too high. Losing everything, now trapped in a brothel, even if I achieve fame and success later, how can I ever hold my head high after such a past?"

"You misunderstand. Let's not talk about future achievements. Tell me, what is your greatest wish right now?"

"My greatest wish is to clear my family's name, restore justice for my father and son, and tear apart those who slandered us. If I can avenge this, dying would be worth it."

"The reason you want to live is revenge. I don't want to do this, but it's the only way to give you a reason to keep living."

"Do you know Han Xin?"

The young master naturally shook his head and listened attentively. "Han Xin lost his parents at a young age and relied on fishing and selling lines to survive, often receiving help from an old woman washing silk. He faced much hostility from others around him."

"One day, a group of ruffians publicly humiliated Han Xin. A butcher said to him: 'You may look big and strong, carrying swords and knives, but inside you're a coward! If you dare, use your sword to stab me. If not, crawl under my crotch.'"

"Han Xin knew that fighting alone would lead to his downfall. So, he chose the easier path, crawling under the butcher's crotch in front of many onlookers. This event is known in history as 'the humiliation under the crotch.'"

"The humiliation under the crotch?" The young master repeated this idiom, his eyes lighting up instantly.

"Why do you know so much, A-san?" The young master looked at me incredulously, as if seeing a familiar stranger.

I realized I had spoken too much for a servant's identity, let alone someone who grew up alongside him.

"Master, don't dwell on it now. To move forward, we must focus on survival!" To change the subject, I proposed a new idea: "Given our current situation, ending up in this brothel might actually be a blessing in disguise."

"How so?" The young master became more interested, his tone urgent.

"The good part aside, this brothel serves as our hiding place and also a great location for gathering intelligence. People from all walks of life, including nobles and officials, come here seeking pleasure. This makes it an excellent place to gather information. By expanding our reputation here, we may encounter corrupt officials and collect evidence. Moreover, we might meet righteous heroes among the江湖and potentially recruit them."

As I continued explaining the benefits of staying in the brothel, the young master listened with growing excitement, gripping my hand tighter and breathing heavily, as if enlightened.

"After hearing your words, I won't seek death anymore, A-san. You've opened my eyes to truths I never understood. From now on, I promise not to consider death lightly!"

Seeing the young master finally realize the value of life, I breathed a sigh of relief and smiled at him.

Suddenly, the young master paused, then glanced away awkwardly, smiling at me. Although I was curious, I didn't pursue it further.

Now that the young master was convinced, another issue loomed—how to deal with Mother Wang. My smile faded instantly. How to pass through this poisoness's scrutiny and preserve the young master's honor remained a pressing concern.

The young master stood up resolutely, shouting loudly: "Since this is the case, I will endure like Han Xin, waiting for the right moment to avenge my family. If they want this body, let them have it!"

His righteous stance made me chuckle inwardly.

"Young Master, it doesn't have to go this far. A-san will ensure your honor remains intact. I will make sure you achieve fame and fortune and can walk proudly again."

The young master naturally didn't believe me, thinking I was merely comforting him. However, he still smiled, shaking his head as he knelt down and looked into my eyes: "A-san, your wisdom fills me with admiration. Even now, despite everything, you haven't abandoned me. I will remember this kindness forever. As a servant, I should have been protecting you, but I only thought of myself, never considering your innocence. Now it's time for me to repay you."

Bathed in the glow of pure light, the young master's gaze stole my breath away. His fragile appearance suddenly shone brightly like the sun, stirring my heart profoundly.
