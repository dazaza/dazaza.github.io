穿越! Crossing! Transformation! Another world is fine, but why does she have to turn into a dragon?

The enchanted forest lake reflects her snowy and tender body. The golden-haired swordsman bound by past ties gathers the brilliance of the sun's fragments. The beautiful youth imprisoned in the tall tower reveals an innocent smile. Under the moonlit night, the one with the red cap dances and sings blessings, gently kissing. This is an unimaginably wondrous otherworld...

Accompanied by an unlucky thief and a memory-less handsome youth, Yi Longlong sets off on her journey under the name of a dragon.

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Upper) Part One

Yi Longlong found the source of this fragrance to be the eggshell from which she was born. The eggshell fragments, as white as jade, lay quietly on the ground covered with white stones, their surfaces gleaming with a translucent luster like glass, emitting an inviting aroma. Looking at the appearance of the eggshell, Yi Longlong couldn't help but doubt: Can this thing really be eaten? Moreover, she didn't even have teeth yet.

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Upper) Part Two

The person who threw the hand axe stood up from the corner table of the tavern and took off the cloak that concealed her face and body. She was a graceful and tall red-haired woman about twenty-five or six years old, wearing a well-fitted dark red soft armor. Her long red wavy hair was tied into a ponytail with a black band at the back of her head, exuding a strikingly masculine charm. The five people sitting with her at the corner table were all wearing cloaks identical to hers.

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Upper) Part Three

Unlike her previous all-white body, for some unknown reason, the young dragon's body now exhibited a soft pink hue. From head to tail, from tail to claws, even the tiny sharp nails were tinged with a captivating pink glow. This color, for some reason, seemed to make her look incredibly appetizing. Lin Qi stared at Yi Longlong, recalling the tender fawn loin, the delicious squab meat, and rabbit meat he had just enjoyed on the dining table. The delicate texture of the meat still lingered in his mouth. Although he was already full, he suddenly felt an impulse to take a bite.

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Middle) Part Four

With her mind elsewhere, Lin Qi did not notice that the hand continuously stroking the young dragon gradually emitted a gentle silver light. Accompanying his movements, the silver light gradually enveloped Yi Longlong's petite body. By the time Yi Longlong realized something was amiss after crying enough, Lin Qi also came to his senses. The young dragon's body was completely encased in a silvery glow resembling flowing water, forming a glowing silver cocoon-like container. Let's just call it a "light cocoon."

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Middle) Part Five

Even the domestic science department, which holds the lowest social status and mass-produces professional maids, butlers, and housekeepers, has its share of extraordinary stories. There was once a classmate with a queenly demeanor. Despite wearing a maid's uniform, her outstanding beauty and elegant bearing captivated more than half the school — including a smaller portion of female students. Consequently, before graduation, she was pursued by a male student from the same school and became a formal wife immediately upon graduation, hardly getting the chance to practice the knowledge she learned at the academy.

Dragon Dragon Dragon (Middle) Part Six

Not expecting anything serious to happen, Yi Longlong had no intention of prying into the youth's privacy. Anyway, such a feeling wasn't bad. In these pleasant and relaxed days, the two were like intimate family members, holding hands, feeling the touch and warmth of each other's fingers, occasionally swaying slightly, like a small boat floating on a stream, so peaceful and gentle.
