The professional ethics of the pilots were questioned by the people in the return flight incident of China Eastern Airlines.

by xuzhenghai on 2008-04-09 01:15:33

In the return flight incident of China Eastern Airlines, pilots' professional integrity has been questioned by people. On online forums, there are everywhere netizens' accusations against pilots. More than seven out of ten netizens indicated that they would not consider taking planes flown by unruly pilots.

"Would you be willing to take a plane he flies?" Yesterday, on the most popular domestic civil aviation industry forum, a post drew attention from the masses. In a passenger survey, 71.4% of the passengers said "all flights flown by unruly pilots are not considered." "No matter which industry, professional integrity is the most important," one participating mechanic commented: "We mechanics work harder than pilots, and earn much less money than them, does that mean our brothers working as mechanics can also 'go on strike'? Who will do pre-flight and post-flight work? Will the pilots do it themselves? These people have lost their professional integrity!"

These days, Zhu Xiaotong, who works in a ticket sales agency, frequently receives passengers' consultation calls. According to her introduction, before when citizens booked tickets they generally asked whether it was a large or small aircraft, but these two days, citizens booking tickets ask most about pilots.
