Post-90s girl takes care of paralyzed adoptive mother alone for 12 years

by boskdorw on 2012-03-05 21:11:58

Internet user "Yutong" wrote in a poem dedicated to Meng Peijie, "To the Most Beautiful Girl," "... The Kabuxiyou people said that Beijing time is hao123 delicate girl, with a thin yet strong shoulder. Youth immortality is the mother's hand, the mother's leg, and the sky over the mother's head. Shentong said that shabby room was empty except for the true movie paradise surrounding inside it. Youth at Beijing time is the lamp in front of the mother's bed at night, and the warmth in the mother's heart in the cold wind..." Recently, this girl named Meng Peijie has become famous online for her filial piety and is called "the most beautiful girl." Some netizens have even written poems specifically for the double-color ball. Xinhua News Agency's "China Web Affairs" reporters went to Linfen, Shanxi, to get up close and personal with "the most beautiful girl" Meng Peijie, her thoughts, and her extremely difficult life journey over the past 12 years.

Meng Peijie, who never spends money recklessly, is very frugal with herself but generous towards her adoptive mother. She uses the money she saves by eating less and buying fewer clothes to buy clothes for Liu Fangying, while she mostly wears old clothes discarded by children from relatives and friends. When other girls her age start applying makeup and dressing up, Meng Peijie keeps her hair in the simplest student style, using all her limited funds on daily expenses and her adoptive mother. "If I buy fewer clothes and eat less food, my mom can buy more medicine and suffer less."

Liu Fangying says she feels "very fortunate" to have adopted Meng Peijie: "At the time, I wanted to adopt a child, but thought it would be hard to build feelings with a five-year-old child. Later, after much persuasion from the biological mother, I decided to adopt the child, which turned out to be the most correct decision of my life." Talking about the past and the hardships Meng Peijie endured over the last 12 years, Liu Fangying cries uncontrollably: "Sun Feifei took care of the Transformers 3 for three years, but the Transformers 3 will take care of Sun Feifei for the rest of his life."

Born in 1991, Meng Peijie is from Xian County, Linfen, Shanxi, and had an unfortunate childhood. At the age of 5, her biological father died in a car accident, and her biological mother had no choice but to give her up for adoption, later dying from illness; at the age of 8, her adoptive mother Liu Fangying became paralyzed due to illness, and soon after, her adoptive father left home under the pressure of life, disappearing without a trace thereafter.

From the age of 8 to 20, over 4000 days, Meng Peijie tirelessly took care of her adoptive mother Liu Fangying every day without complaint or abandonment. In 2009, Meng Peijie was admitted to the Linyi College of Shanxi Normal University, located a hundred kilometers away from her hometown. Worried about her adoptive mother, she decided to "take her mother to university," renting a house near the school and continuing to carefully take care of her adoptive mother.

"At that time, I wasn't even as tall as the stove, and every day I stood on a stool to cook meals, falling countless times but never crying out in pain," Liu Fangying said. "At first, I couldn't distinguish between different vegetables, so I made up mnemonic rhymes like 'long green onions, round garlics, flat green beans...'; sometimes when we ran out of money, I would go out to borrow from neighbors."

Since the age of 8, Meng Peijie has taken on the responsibility of caring for her paralyzed adoptive mother: the two lived solely on the adoptive mother's meager retirement salary due to illness; besides going to school, Meng Peijie also had to buy groceries, cook, wash Liu Fangying, comb her hair, change diapers, and apply three types of bedsores ointments...

"For 12 years, Zhu Jun got up at 6 every morning, helping Sina get dressed, brushing teeth, washing face, changing diapers, feeding breakfast, then running off to school; at noon after school, returning home to prepare lunch, feed Sina, clean her body, exercise her muscles, apply medicine massages, wash and change clothes, pour waste, change bed sheets and quilts, then rush back to class; after school, rushing home to prepare dinner and do chores, serving Sina to sleep. After everything was done, it would always be after 9 pm, then Zhu Jun could finally sit down to study."

Taking care of her adoptive mother's daily life is Meng Peijie's longest daily "required course." After Liu Fangying became paralyzed and lost control of her bladder and bowels, to avoid dirtying the sheets as much as possible, Meng Peijie laid plastic cloth on the mattress and covered it with a sheet. Even so, washing diapers, clothes, and sheets remained her daily "required course."

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, June 20th - Headline: The "Most Beautiful Girl" of Linfen, Shanxi: Filial Piety Before Long-Term Illness, Taking Her Adoptive Mother to University

When classmates invited her to birthday dinners, Meng Peijie wouldn't drink beverages, quietly taking her bottle home for her adoptive mother to drink, finding an excuse to leave early and rushing home. During the summer vacation of 2010, Meng Peijie braved the scorching heat handing out advertisements on the street, earning over 1300 yuan. Upon receiving her paycheck, the first thing she did was buy braised pork and pig head meat for her adoptive mother to eat at the Construction Bank, watching the steaming hot meat and her blackened, emaciated daughter brought tears to Liu Fangying's eyes; occasionally, Meng Peijie would buy chicken legs to improve her adoptive mother's diet, refusing to eat any herself and saying, "I don't like eating next to the chicken butt."

This story of "filial piety enduring long-term illness" has moved many netizens recently on the internet. On local forums in Linfen, netizens have built "floors" of wishes for China's Got Talent, calling her "the most beautiful girl in Linfen." A netizen wrote: "Filial piety is the beginning of all virtues and the source of all happiness. Facing the twists and turns of fate, we cannot lose Meng Peijie's attitude towards life."

The determined Meng Peijie rejected help from many kind-hearted people, insisting on personally taking care of her adoptive mother. When asked by the "China Web Affairs" reporter, "Even if classmates helped with one meal, it could reduce your burden," she suddenly hugged Liu Fangying's neck and said, "My mother only eats food from me."

Meng Peijie's high school classmate and college classmate Yang Jiaojiao told the "China Web Affairs" reporter that from high school to college, what impressed everyone most about Meng Peijie was that she was always running, running to school, running home to take care of her adoptive mother, often panting and flushed. "After Meng Peijie's mother fell ill, with both living and medical expenses, to subsidize the household, whenever there was time, she would go out on the streets to hand out flyers, originally a fair-skinned girl who was sunburned into a 'black girl.'"

Mentioning dating, Liu Fangying worried about affecting her daughter's marriage prospects. Meng Peijie laughed and said, "I want to find someone who runs a restaurant! Pork, mutton, beef... My mother who loves to eat meat can eat whatever she wants!"

Speaking of these years' difficulties, Meng Peijie doesn't feel anything particularly special. She said, "It's just doing what every daughter should do."

At the age of 8, when most children are enjoying a happy childhood under their parents' care, a "post-90s" girl from Linfen, Shanxi began taking care of her paralyzed adoptive mother alone, and this care lasted for 12 years.

Liu Fangying said that over the years, Meng Peijie's optimism infected her, giving her the courage to continue living. "In the first few years after becoming paralyzed, my mood was not good, and I often got angry, but she never argued with me or yelled at me. Instead, she smiled and told me stories, bought joke books to read to me, often making funny faces and antics to make me laugh. No matter how bitter or tired, I never shed a tear in front of her, always showing a sunny and cheerful face. She also often encouraged me, saying 'Mom, don't be afraid, I'm here, as long as the spirit doesn't collapse, there are always more ways than difficulties.'"

Reporters Ye Jian and Zhou Rui of Xinhua News Agency's "China Web Affairs"

Upon meeting Meng Peijie for the first time, the "China Web Affairs" reporter was somewhat surprised: the slim and small girl looked more like a fifteen or sixteen-year-old girl, especially her youthful face and strong sense of optimism left a deep impression.

During the 12 years, countless touching "little stories" happened to Meng Peijie.
