Smart toilet - "sitting" on the high-tech frontier

by zry3276i0 on 2009-12-09 12:03:30

The development of technology has made life more comfortable, and this is reflected in all aspects of life, even the innovation of toilet bowls has never stopped. With the continuous progress of science and technology, various kinds of toilets with different functions have emerged, such as toilets with blow function, toilet seats with temperature control devices...etc. These amazing functions allow us to enjoy the joy and warmth brought by high technology even when "going to the toilet".

Bidet

The idea of having a medical examination while using the toilet is really strange. Toto, Japan's largest bathroom equipment manufacturer, has developed a new product. The new toilet comes with a small cup that extends as soon as someone sits down, collecting 5 cubic centimeters of urine; then, the metal tube supporting the cup delivers the urine to the hidden instrument to measure the sugar level in the urine. While waiting for the urine test results for one minute, you can also open the nearby small box, take out the blow-up band inside, tie it around your wrist to measure whether your blood pressure is normal.

Navigation Toilet

Arkon Equipment Company located in Arcadia, Southern California, USA, launched a high-tech device that can be installed on the toilet seat. This small and exquisite bathroom device called LavNav (the abbreviation of "toilet" and "navigation") is equipped with sensitive sensors, which use colored signal lights to guide the owner to quickly and accurately find the position of the toilet at night. Moreover, this device will not make you wake up others who are sound asleep in the confusion of being in urgent need of going to the toilet but unable to find the light switch.

MP3 Toilet

Listening to music while using the toilet is a kind of luxury. A famous Japanese sanitary ware manufacturer launched an automatic toilet with MP3 function and "sterilizing ion" technology. This toilet eliminates the water tank in its design, using direct flush. It itself has the "Relax Music" function of playing music, with speakers equipped on both sides corresponding to stereo playback, making it the world's first toilet with music playback function. There is a human body sensor at the front end of the toilet, which can automatically play music as long as the toilet lid is opened. The toilet itself has built-in songs. Users can also use SD cards to play MP3 files.

Fragrant Toilet

It has been a habit to pinch your nose while using the toilet, but with a toilet that can suck away bad smell and release fragrance, this habit will change. A domestic enterprise invented a deodorizing device that can be installed on a sitting flush toilet. This deodorizer connected to the flush toilet mainly includes a gas collection seat cushion and a deodorizing device. The gas collection seat cushion is installed on the sitting toilet; the deodorizing device consists of a negative pressure gas chamber, a fan, and a regeneration chamber with absorbent, containing activated carbon fiber cotton and perfume storage device. This deodorizer can first absorb and remove bad smells, then release fragrances. With it, people no longer have to pinch their noses while using the toilet.

Automatic Sensor Toilet

Is it possible for a toilet without a water tank? The Santou fully automatic sensor toilet does not have a water tank. This toilet has a very high degree of intelligence. After a person approaches the toilet, the toilet lid opens and closes automatically, then detects the person leaving and initiates automatic flushing and cleaning. This toilet also has functions such as automatic strong deodorization. Due to the lack of a water tank and direct connection to water pipes, the bathroom space becomes more spacious.

Heating Toilet

In the cold winter, warmth should be omnipresent, including the toilet that has the most intimate contact with your skin. A company's researched toilet has a heat preservation function, so even in winter, your buttocks can feel warm. Secondly, there is warm air drying, comfortable warm air makes the buttocks dry and fresh after use, allowing you to choose the required warm air temperature between 40-59 degrees Celsius, giving you a five-star luxurious experience. Such a considerate design is beyond the reach of traditional toilets.

This toilet appeared very early, similar products were sold domestically quite early, and users have formed a certain scale. Today, it is still one of the most successful designs.

"Smart" Toilet

The development of technology has made toilets "smart". A "smart toilet" produced by Uster Environmental Protection Company in Sweden has the special feature that the toilet is divided into two areas: the front area for urination and the back area for defecation. The water tank of the toilet is specially designed, and only a little water is needed to flush the urine. When flushing feces, the size of the water flow is completely controlled by the user, there is no need to use all the water in the water tank every time. According to the staff, this toilet saves more than 60% water compared to general products. Although the control of flushing water volume is already very common, the "zoning" of the toilet is indeed relatively novel.

Internet Toilet

For net worms, going online is their entire life, even when they are in the toilet. A company under Microsoft developed a toilet with internet access function. This toilet is equipped with a high-performance computer, waterproof wireless keyboard, computer LCD monitor, and six-channel surround sound system below the water tank. Users can sit on the toilet and surf the internet through a wireless network while using the toilet. Although the toilet is good, there were later rumors that Microsoft denied this matter. Regardless of the authenticity of the incident, this novel idea indeed caught the attention of net worms.

Composting Toilet

Turning the toilet into a small fertilizer factory is truly a genius idea. An electronic toilet introduced by a British company not only saves water but also provides compost for vegetable gardens and flower beds. The toilet is equipped with blades that mix excrement and peat moss after the seat cover is closed, eventually producing high-quality compost. Since this fertilizer has been sterilized at high temperatures, it is safe and hygienic to use. During processing, an electric fan inside the toilet can channel any odor outside through the pipe. Using this toilet in rural areas can ensure hygiene and effectively fertilize, isn't it a win-win situation?

Toilets with Core Processors

Don't think processors can only be installed in computers, but also in toilets. Using Intel chips to check morning excretions and analyze symptoms like blood. Once the smart toilet detects suspicious phenomena, it can automatically contact the doctor and check your online calendar to arrange an appropriate meeting time. By now, you might already find this incredible, but it doesn't stop here! Smart toilets can even contact ambulances! This toilet should be very useful for elderly people who are frail.
