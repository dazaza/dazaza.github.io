Footprint enables individual website owners or bloggers to view visitor information in real time.

by zzzffff on 2011-04-10 19:37:54

Footprint allows webmasters or bloggers to view in real-time the visitor information for their websites or blogs. This includes information such as the visitors' country of origin, visit status (green indicates online and active, orange indicates users who were active within the last 10 minutes, red indicates offline), duration of stay, number of visits, page views generated, and the current page they are on. Additionally, when you hover your mouse over a specific visitor's information, a small pop-up window will appear showing even more detailed information about the user's visit, such as their IP address, browser, operating system, and ISP network provider, making it very convenient. Footprint makes it easy for you to manage and search through data. Moreover, to use Footprint, you don't need to download any software, which is extremely convenient.
