The Fifth Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea Climbing Festival launches the Beauty's Journey. As the ancients said, a mountain does not need to be high, having immortals makes it spiritual. The bamboo in the South Mountain always makes one believe in the existence of immortals. Drifting on a bamboo raft, facing the quiet lake with bamboo groves on both banks, the sound of bamboo leaves enters the ears, white egrets fly lightly, mandarin ducks play in the water, in such scenery, without many words, just quietly meditating, one can understand the tranquility and beauty of the South Mountain. Along the waterside corridor, winding upwards, everywhere is a scene, every step is filled with emotion. In the search, suddenly, a white-bearded immortal old man appears chanting in the mountains...

Slowly moving cableways take you through the air to overlook the vastness of the bamboo sea, like rippling green waves, swaying with the wind, the ingenious craftsmanship of nature is breathtaking. Retracing the ancient official road, pondering the artistic conception of the ancients, watching bamboo carvings, appreciating the exquisite skills of painting. The activities of the Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea Climbing Festival run from March 6th to March 28th. Different from previous climbing festivals, this year's festival not only continues the popular spring climbing competition in the South Mountain Bamboo Sea but also integrates the concept of health through hot springs. The Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea scenic area collaborates with the Yu Water Hot Spring to create a healthy journey, allowing people who have hibernated in the city all winter to stretch their muscles and bones in the early spring South Mountain Bamboo Sea, soak in the hot spring at Yu Water for health, and enjoy the most comfortable and fashionable spring health journey.

Tianmu Lake always makes one involuntarily emotional, the blue lake flows gently, the willows sway gracefully, whispering the tenderness of spring. In such a wonderful place, there are always couples leaning against each other, softly murmuring sweet words in the landscape painting of Tianmu Lake.

Spring is a good time for climbing, the Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea climbing challenge becomes the classic event of this spring. The winding mountain road follows the stream, extending from the front of the long-lived elder in the South Mountain towards the depths of the bamboo sea. The Wu-Yue 'Brother', at an altitude of 508 meters in the South Mountain Bamboo Sea, is the endpoint of the climbing competition. A mountain path of more than a thousand meters is perfect for friends, classmates, and colleagues to have a climbing race together. What matters is not who reaches the top first, but the scenery along the way and the good mood of appreciating the scenery.

To celebrate Women's Day, this year's Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea Climbing Festival grandly launched the "Beauty's Journey". Online media from Shanghai, Suzhou, Hangzhou, Nanjing, etc., jointly recruited "professional beauties" for self-driving tours in the South Mountain Bamboo Sea, igniting the enthusiasm of numerous netizens to sign up. After repeated screening, professional beauties who stood out became important guests of this climbing festival activity. During the festival, they could not only freely tour the Tianmu South Mountain Bamboo Sea scenic area but also receive a free ticket to the Yu Water Hot Spring. These youthful and attractive professional beauties, after appreciating the ten-thousand-acre bamboo sea on the summit of the South Mountain, would then soak in the Yu Water Hot Spring for relaxation and enjoy the comfort of aromatic SPA, eliminating fatigue and promoting health and wellness.
