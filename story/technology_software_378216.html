I. iFanr (ifanr) Shanghai Station Small Gathering Event

On December 26, I was honored to be invited by iFanr to participate in the #ifanrevent# small fan meet-up held at Kathleen's 5 on the 5th floor of the Shanghai Art Museum (live streaming post). At the request of the organizers, I gave a presentation titled "Beginner's Guide to Syncing" for the guests present.

Since this was an introductory-level sharing session, the content covered was not particularly profound, mainly focusing on industry analysis and tool recommendations, which can largely be found in shadows of related articles previously published.

The PPT presented on-site has been uploaded slide by slide to Flickr (I'm glad that some friends on-site and on Weibo liked my PPT; I spent over half an hour agonizing over the placement of a few pixels the night before...). You can view it directly as an album or in slideshow mode. Additionally, there is a cloud storage download link available.

Lastly, special thanks to HP for sponsoring this event. As a keepsake, I received a very powerful 360GB external hard drive.

II. Sync Control 2010 X'MAS Christmas Prize Giveaway Event

The Christmas prize giveaway event released on Christmas Eve received enthusiastic support and forwarding from netizens, with a large number of participants, so much so that the codes were distributed until my hand cramped. By the end of the event, a total of 527 friends participated. Except for a few friends who submitted invalid email addresses, gifts were basically sent out to everyone else.

Due to the high demand for some invitation codes, we even applied for an increase in the number of prizes, but there were still not enough. Fortunately, we had prepared a primary/backup gift distribution plan.

Some netizens reported that after registering with the VIP qualification invitation code they received from TAP, their account was still a regular one. Regarding this issue, we have contacted TAP to confirm, and it might be a bug in the invitation system. You can contact me or TAP to switch invitations (other invitations will also be sent out as compensation).

Currently, there are still a few remaining invitations available. Interested friends can apply via email. Additionally, I will also be distributing some on Weibo recently.
