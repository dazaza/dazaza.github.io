Five Accounting Websites Teach You to Be a Good Housewife - Financial Weekly Report

By Luo Shuai

In the era of network information, how can one be a good housewife? The traditional concept of "graceful in the hall, capable in the kitchen" still holds some power, but you need some new IT knowledge to arm yourself. With the halo of new technology on your head, it will add more grace and charm to your gestures.

Below are introductions to some domestic accounting websites. The selection criteria are based on the ranking order of these accounting websites on ALEXA as of September 1, 2007. Once you are familiar with them, you can discard the cumbersome paper and calculator. Just move your fingers, and all the accounts big and small in the family, all numerical statistics will be within your grasp.

www.coko365.com, Caike Online Website

This is also a free online accounting website. After users register and log in, they will see sections such as "accounting", "statistical analysis", "plan arrangement", "system settings", and there are some smaller sections under each section. After you input relevant numbers, the system can automatically generate related statistical data and tables, reminding you of your income and expenditure at any time.

One unique feature of this website is the establishment of the "data export" function. You can download the day's accounting situation in EXCEL format to your computer hard drive at any time for backup purposes.

Website Operations Director Tian Keshan introduced to the Financial Weekly reporter that this website differs from other accounting websites in that it is a website completely based on the "online software" concept. Both technical language and website architecture adopt online software standards, so it has advantages in terms of security, stability, and scalability. Other accounting websites often base themselves on the "online community" concept, which uses different technical languages. Tian Keshan said the website is expanding some functions, such as cooperation with banks, and the upcoming "mobile phone accounting" function, where consumers can simply send a text message after consumption to automatically record accounts online.

MYMONEY Family Finance Accounting Website

This is a website where you can record daily expenses anytime. After users register and log in, they can enter the "accounting" channel. Here, the system sets up sub-sections such as "income and expenditure subject management", "income and expenditure plan management", "daily income and expenditure flow", and "income and expenditure statistical analysis". You can make detailed settings for these sections, and the system will automatically generate some statistical conclusions for your reference.

In addition, the website also has sections such as "family information management" and "financial diary management" for you to modify family member information and record financial diaries. You can also enter the "advanced finance" channel, which provides introductions to some financial products and basic financial accounts. In the financial forum, you can read others' financial experiences.

Kaipulan Personal Affairs Processing Network

Strictly speaking, this is a personal information service website. Besides the accounting function, it also provides other functions such as online diaries and interpersonal relationship reminders. After logging in, users can record relevant account content according to prompts.

Interestingly, the accounting on this website is entirely set up according to corporate subjects, including "balance sheet", "cash flow statement", "cash flow analysis", "debt ratio", "liquidity ratio", etc. Although it may seem overwhelming, it is not without its own characteristics.

Wallet Network

The interface of this website is relatively friendly, and the settings are also quite simple. After logging in, users can see three options: "simple mode", "standard mode", and "couple mode". Click on the mode you want, and you can start recording accounts. The categories of accounts on this website are also very simple, divided into four categories: "earned", "spent", "want to earn", "want to spend", and there is also a "leisure chat" function.

On the website's navigation page, there are records of netizens earning and spending money, as well as monthly rankings for the most earning and spending. Besides accounting, you can also exchange experiences about spending or earning money with people who share similar interests.

Online Accounting Website

This is also an accounting website specifically for personal use. After users log in, they record their personal daily income and expenses. The system automatically lists the accounting information of the last seven days and automatically tallies deposits and debts and bonds on the right side of the webpage, as well as the income and expenditure comparison of the past six months.

The founder of the Online Accounting Website, Liang Feineng, told the Financial Weekly reporter that he was originally a "moonlight clan" member. To better control his financial income and expenditure, he founded this website. As the number of members increased, his interest in this website grew stronger, and later he resigned to fully dedicate himself to the operation of this website.

"Hoping this website can become a financial assistant for white-collar workers and homemakers," said Liang Feineng.

After acquiring the knowledge of using these financial websites, you need to pay attention to the following issues. One is the safety of accounting. No housewife would want to disclose her family's detailed accounts to others, so it is best to register anonymously when signing up.

At the same time, to ensure the safety and completeness of accounting, it is best to copy a backup on your computer hard drive for future reference. Additionally, most of these accounting websites are currently operated by studios or individuals. Due to insufficient income channels, most websites are still operating at a loss, so it is important to back up accounting data in a timely manner.

However, good news has already come: The recently published American magazine Fortune selected 15 companies that will change the world, and among them, the accounting website expensr is prominently listed. This quickly drew the attention of venture capitalists to domestic accounting websites, and the media began to notice and report on the profit model and investment opportunities of accounting websites. Therefore, finding a convenient-to-use, highly potential accounting website, while enjoying the ease and comfort of accounting, you might also witness the growth process of a website that could change the world.

Link

ALEXA ranking is a commonly cited indicator used to evaluate the traffic of a particular website. In fact, ALEXA ranking is based on statistics of the websites visited by users who have downloaded and installed the Alexa Tools Bar embedded in browsers like IE. Therefore, its ranking data does not have absolute authority. However, since it provides multiple evaluation indicators such as overall ranking, visitor volume ranking, and page visit volume ranking, and there is currently no more scientific and reasonable evaluation reference, most people still consider it as the current authoritative website traffic evaluation indicator.
