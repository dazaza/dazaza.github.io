Matsumoto Intelligence: Product is the root, carefully crafting high-end products to safeguard industry development.

by erbai558 on 2012-03-03 09:29:37

("18 Companies that Influenced China's Smart Home Industry in 2011" Special Feature Brand Recommendation Article) Matsumoto, a brand spanning multiple industries, has established itself in the field of switchgear, intercom systems for buildings, and theft alarm series products. Matsumoto Intelligent focuses on creating high-quality product positioning, aiming to perfect every detail. Transitioning from diversification to meticulous craftsmanship, Matsumoto Smart Home has created strong competitiveness.

Founded in 1998, "Matsumoto Intelligent," as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Guangdong Wei Xiong Group, is currently one of the largest, most technologically advanced, and strongest production capacity professional integrated manufacturers in the domestic security and intelligent building fields. After years of development, Matsumoto Intelligent has established a complete sales system in major cities across the country, with sales branches and offices in Shenzhen, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Nanchang, Zhengzhou, Chengdu, Qingdao, Hefei, and more.

Technological Leadership: Possesses a National-Level Postdoctoral Workstation

Technology is one of the core aspects of enterprise progress. As a leading company, Matsumoto Intelligent views talent and technology as the first elements of corporate competition, introducing several postdoctoral researchers to conduct forward-looking technological research and product reserves, ensuring the core competitiveness of its products.

Matsumoto Intelligent boasts a team of elite professionals who were among the earliest in the country engaged in the research and development of security products and system design and integration. They have always been at the forefront of advanced technology and possess many years of practical experience. Matsumoto Intelligent owns a national-level "Postdoctoral Workstation," which serves as a strong backing for its high-end product R&D. High-caliber human resources enable Matsumoto Intelligent to grasp many forward-looking theories and technologies, and could be said to guide the development trends of intelligent buildings to some extent. The information release technology of the building intercom system was first proposed by Matsumoto Intelligent and received a national invention patent. Now, many regional developers recognize this function and technology, making it a new selling point for real estate projects. As a result, many mainstream manufacturers have also started adding this feature to their respective products.

First in the Industry to Launch IP Systems

In recent years, the application of TCP/IP technology in building intercoms and the security industry has been like injecting a "stimulant." The use of TCP/IP technology has broken through the "bottleneck" of signal transmission distance, enabling more innovative applications of building intercom functions, such as alarm linkage, monitoring linkage, IP video telephones, remote control, and more.

Matsumoto Intelligent was the first in the industry to launch an IP system. Compared with bus-type intercoms, the biggest highlight of TCP/IP intercoms lies in their rich functionality, extending various home entertainment functions through digital, networked, and intelligent characteristics. It uses digital transmission for voice, data, and video, integrating with the Internet to make the smart home system more user-friendly.

Defending Industry Development with Products

Smart homes are products that combine low-voltage and high-voltage systems. In promoting smart home systems, Matsumoto Intelligent leverages its philosophy of product-centered business operations, gaining an absolute advantage in the smart home market, which other competitors cannot match. Relying on a comprehensive market service system, its launched IP digital home control products have been widely applied in numerous projects both domestically and internationally. Over the past dozen or so years, Matsumoto Intelligent has seamlessly integrated into people's lives, becoming an indispensable part of daily existence.
