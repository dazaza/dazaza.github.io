Hong Kong Broadway | South China Morning Post reporter Huang Zouhui

by wan1jincai on 2012-03-06 14:12:31

Yangcheng Evening News reporter Huang Zhouhui, correspondent Ye Zichuan and Li Mingliang reported: Recently, passenger Miss Zhang was taking the subway in Guangzhou and accidentally left 9 million RMB worth of bills on a large subway platform. Fortunately, these huge bills were carefully picked up by the staff at Linhexi Station of Guangzhou Metro an hour later and returned to the owner. At 21:50 on January 17th, Wang Chunrui, the duty station master of Linhexi Station, found a file bag lying in the middle of the station while checking. After picking up the file bag, he waited for a long time at the same place, but no one came to claim it. Wang Chunrui then took the file bag back to the station with other employees to inventory its contents. They were amazed! Even after multiple checks, the file bag contained business entrustment letters with the company's financial seal and authorized signature stamps, as well as checks totaling over 9 million RMB, along with business licenses and ID card copies. Considering the huge amount of money involved, Wang Ruojun repeatedly broadcasted to find the owner of the lost items along all the stations. However, after more than 10 minutes, there was still no news from the owner. At around 22:05, another employee suggested calling 114 to check the business telephone number of the lost owner, but they were unable to contact the company due to confidentiality of the contact information by 114. Just when they couldn't do anything, at 22:20, Linhexi Station received a call from the metro service hotline saying that Miss Zhang Xing had lost a file bag containing very important documents and a large amount of cash. After contacting the passenger, the service unit at Linhexi Station confirmed that the lost item belonged to Miss Zhang, thus allowing her smooth access. At 23:05, an anxious Miss Zhang came to claim her lost item. Facing the recovered file bag and huge bills, she tightly held Wang Chunrui's hand and said: "My heart has calmed down. Thank you so much!"
