by fwaaojxp on 2011-09-27 15:23:10

The world has been deceived by the United States! Chairman Mao once said: "rely on our own efforts and start from scratch." Chairman Mao also said: "All American imperialism are paper tigers." As a sovereign country, it should follow this basic principle.

In recent decades, however, many countries in the world have gradually abandoned this principle. Politically, economically, culturally, and militarily, they have been more or less deceived by the United States.

Politically, there were originally more than 60 socialist countries in the world. Due to not adhering to the socialist path, they were deceived by the United States, changed their political and national systems, but did not become prosperous nations.

Economically, many countries relied on U.S. economic aid, which not only failed to make their national economies take off, but instead plunged them into a quagmire. Originally, developing countries should have been creditors, but now they have become debtors.

Culturally, many countries did not continue to carry forward and improve their own excellent cultures, but blindly pursued American culture, "Western democracy." Not only did they fail to achieve true democracy, but their democratization became even worse.

Militarily, quite a few countries relied on the United States as the world's guardian, only to turn their own territories into colonies of others.

In conclusion, the world has been deceived by the United States. Now should be a time for awakening. If American politics don't work in their own country, if the U.S. economy collapses, if American culture doesn't adapt to its own soil, and if the United States can't act as a guardian, can it still be depended upon? Sovereign nations must have their own ideas. They must be independent, start from scratch, and follow a path with their own national characteristics.
