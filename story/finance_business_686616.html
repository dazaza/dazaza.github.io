One of the biggest highlights of the Lotus L5 Sportback is undoubtedly its Campro 1.6L CPS engine designed by the UK-based Lotus. In addition to excellent power and response at high speeds, this engine can effectively reduce fuel consumption and increase torque at low speeds, achieving perfect fuel economy at lower speeds and high-end power all within one engine. Moreover, the Lotus Campro 1.6L CPS engine with core technology provides smoother acceleration, less vibration, and quieter noise, enhancing driving comfort for the L5.
