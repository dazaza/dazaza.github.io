Male youth beats girl to fracture on the street after being rejected for love declaration

by bi1t8083ha on 2012-02-05 09:35:16

On a chance encounter on the street with a good-looking young woman, He Mou, a young man of society, suddenly developed feelings for her and declared his love on the spot. However, the woman did not reciprocate. In an instant, He's demeanor changed as he proceeded to violently beat the woman in the middle of the street, causing her fractures. After identifying himself, he left with arrogance.

Recently, the Yunyang County Court in Chongqing sentenced He Mou to three years in prison for disturbing public order.

Flirtatious: Declaring love upon seeing a beautiful woman

The 19-year-old defendant, He Mou, nicknamed "He'er," resides in Shilou Village, Panstone Town, Yunyang County. With only a primary school education, he dropped out of school and has since been unemployed. In 2008, he was sentenced to eight months in prison for theft. However, instead of reforming, He regarded his criminal record as capital for his life in the underworld.

On the evening of January 18th, He Mou was taking a stroll near Fengming Middle School in Yunyang with Ran Mou when suddenly, two attractive young women caught his eye as they exited a restaurant. He Mou took particular notice of one of them, Tan Mou, who was wearing a hoodie, and instinctively quickened his pace.

Ran Mou, understanding He's thoughts, encouraged him to pursue her. He approached Tan Mou and pulled her hood from behind.

Unprovoked harassment caused Tan Mou to turn around, glare at He Mou, and utter an insult.

Face-off: Violent beating on the street

Failing to charm her and receiving an insult, He Mou felt humiliated and became enraged, slapping Tan Mou across the face. Out of instinct, Tan Mou resisted, but this only resulted in even more brutal attacks. He Mou's fists rained down on Tan Mou's beautiful face. As he later described it, "I don't even know how many punches I landed on Tan Mou." During the attack, another individual who tried to intervene was also beaten by He Mou.

When He finally stopped, Tan Mou's face was a bloody mess. "If you have any guts, leave your name..." Despite being severely injured, Tan Mou still attempted to document the attacker’s information. "My name is 'He'er,' come find me if you dare," He announced before walking away arrogantly. The police were able to quickly identify him based on the nickname "He'er."

Outcome: Sentenced to three years for provoking trouble

As the assailant disappeared into the crowd, Tan Mou immediately called the police. At 8 PM on January 20th, local police officers apprehended He Mou at his home. Subsequent evaluations revealed that Tan Mou had suffered nasal bone fractures, classified as minor injuries.

Upon review, the Yunyang County Procuratorate determined that He Mou sought excitement and arbitrarily assaulted others in public places, resulting in minor injuries, constituting severe circumstances. His actions violated the crime of disturbing public order. Additionally, the prosecution noted that since He Mou had previously served an eight-month sentence for theft in 2008 and now committed another offense punishable by imprisonment, he qualifies as a recidivist and should be punished more severely according to the law. Consequently, the procuratorate prosecuted He Mou for disturbing public order.
