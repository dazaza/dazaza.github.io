Woman forces two daughters to have sex with a quack

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-03 12:14:56

Taihai.com, February 4 -- A mother in Miaoli County was addicted to religion and often went north to Taoyuan to conduct "Yin-Yang dual cultivation" with her master brothers. She even threatened her twin eldest daughter to join her in "cultivation", and asked her sister to take turns. The mother also told her eldest daughter that "you two must give your first time to the master." Afterward, the eldest daughter informed her grandmother and sought help from a social worker. The mother once threatened to "jump off a building and commit suicide". After being arrested by the police, the Taoyuan District Prosecutors Office prosecuted the man and this mother on February 3 for aggravated forced sexual intercourse.

According to Today's News Network report, this shocking case of a mother forcing her two daughters to conduct "Yin-Yang dual cultivation" with her master brother, and threatening to jump off a building if they did not comply, happened in Miaoli County. A Lin surnamed woman in her 40s raised three children alone, two of whom are twin daughters. Due to financial difficulties, Lin hoped to change her luck through religious power, and often went to Taoyuan to attend religious gatherings. For convenience, Lin even rented a house locally in Taoyuan.

On November 20 last year, Lin's eldest daughter went to her mother's rented house to find her. Seeing her eldest daughter had poor development, Lin asked Master Yan to treat her. Using the excuse of needing to conduct "Yin-Yang dual cultivation," he took the opportunity to molest the girl's chest and private parts. The eldest daughter resisted, but Lin did not stop him, instead telling her daughter "you and your sister's first time must be given to the master," and even helped undress the girl. Master Yan succeeded in penetrating the girl's private part with his finger. Afterwards, the mother threatened her, saying that if she did not comply, she would jump off a building and commit suicide.

Afterwards, the mother threatened the eldest daughter not to tell anyone, and required her sister to also go for "treatment," making the two sisters take turns. The eldest daughter then told her sister and sought help from their grandmother. Although the grandmother tried to make Lin stop, she insisted that the two must receive treatment, and thus the sisters' plight was exposed. The Miaoli court first placed the two girls in custody. The police later arrested Lin and Master Yan, and prosecuted them for aggravated forced sexual intercourse and violating the Child and Youth Welfare Law.
