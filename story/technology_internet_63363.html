3G mobile new tool breaks through the bottleneck of WAP site takeoff

by now on 2008-04-16 16:39:06

Recently, dotMobi Company announced that it will soon update the DeviceAtlas mobile device database, which leads globally. This global mobile development tool is custom-made for mobile Internet developers worldwide in the 3G era. Currently, the company is expanding the coverage of DeviceAtlas for mobile devices in China to promote the application of mobile Internet in China. For countless people engaged in WAP development and operation, as well as those who are concerned about the development of the mobile 3G industry, this is undoubtedly a great piece of news. "This indicates that the bottleneck where '.mobi' sites established through WAP cannot be accessed by all mobile Internet users due to the variety of phone types in the country can hopefully be broken, and the .mobi domain name can be comprehensively promoted," said the person in charge of Times Interconnection. Establishing an efficient and usable mobile website is crucial for the development and promotion of mobile Internet. With the help of DeviceAtlas, WAP developers can create specific mobile website experiences for a series of different devices by accessing the database.

It is reported that within six weeks after the launch of DeviceAtlas, tens of thousands of mobile developers quickly used the content provided by this tool to create 'device-recognizable' mobile website content for a wide variety of mobile devices.

Influenced by this news, domestic WAP developers and operators have already taken action, leading to an increase in mobile website construction. As WAP has become the main direction for future mobile phone research and development, WAP-supported virtual hosts are highly favored in the market. It is learned that recently, inquiries about WAP website construction at Times Interconnection (www.now.cn) have increased compared to before. The person in charge indicated that all virtual hosts of the company support WAP and can meet the needs of different types of mobile website constructions.

The DeviceAtlas development tool is cost-effective, so it means that the equipment data provided can well meet the requirements of each user, from individual users to large-scale industrial giants, all can benefit from it. Therefore, the influence of DeviceAtlas on the 3G industry, especially WAP, is extensive and profound. Mobile developers and the entire industry face a special development opportunity, which will greatly promote the successful development of future mobile Internet.
