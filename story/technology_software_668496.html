Lian Town: Enjoy more discounts on the launch day with the "Happiness Passport" _1521 Lian Town

by xmnqhqzi on 2012-02-18 01:46:14

The Lantern Festival is approaching, and the Xiang'an district's real estate development, Te Fang · Li An Town, is also about to welcome some good news. The sales center will open on the day after the Lantern Festival. According to the property consultant, the project is currently undergoing a "Happiness Passport" application event. Before the opening of the second group of buildings, customers can go there for registration. At the time of the opening, they can enjoy more discounts. It is reported that the new products in the second group, ranging from 38-87 square meters, will debut in March. Afterward, shops with areas ranging from 30-200 square meters may be launched. There are not many units left in the currently sold Building No. 2, which includes two-bedroom units of 80-87 square meters, single apartments of 38-42 square meters, and three-bedroom units of 120 square meters, with an average price of 6800 yuan/square meter. Purchasing a unit of 80 square meters comes with the gift of three Xiamen household registrations. For those purchasing now, a one-time payment enjoys a 9.8% discount, and mortgage payments enjoy a 9.9% discount.

[Project Link] Te Fang · Li An Town is another major work by the state-owned enterprise Te Fang Group in the Xiang'an District in 2011, following Mei Di Ya Deng and Jin Xiu Xiang An. Li An Town is located in the mature Malang Town area, adjacent to Xiang'an No.1 Middle School, Malang Middle School, Malang Central Primary School, and other educational institutions. Nearby are shopping centers like Xin Hua Du Plaza, Da Qian Department Store, Malang Commercial Street, Xiamen First Hospital Tong Min Branch, various banks, postal services, and more. The education, commerce, medical, and all kinds of living facilities are exceptionally superior. The Li An Town project is divided into four plots: A, B, C, and D, with a community scale of 400,000 square meters, including nearly 50,000 square meters of commercial space within the community itself, making it a genuinely large community and an ideal place for young people's perfect lives.
