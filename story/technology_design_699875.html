Internet posts said that the traffic bureau specially set up a water tank for the director, and in response it said that it was for better use.

by lhltckfrtg on 2012-03-09 16:50:26

, International专线 On the afternoon of December 20th, a staff member of the office of the Tangshan Lubei Transportation Bureau informed the reporter that the building of the transportation bureau had two floors, with five directors' offices on the second floor. There was only one small water heater in the corridor, while there were two water heaters on the first floor. The water heater on the second floor had been damaged several times due to too many people drawing water and burning it dry. This staff member stated that the original intention of posting this notice was "to better utilize the electric water heater." The staff member also mentioned that there were no water dispensers in the director's office of the bureau, "I don't understand why there are so many comments online."

Response: The purpose of the notice was to better use the water heater.

Forum post: "This is exclusively for directors to drink water."

Netizen "Guazhoud River Fisherman" exclaimed repeatedly, "Too amazing! Only for the leaders' service, the service is too considerate"; Netizen "Yuelang Fengqing" pointed out sharply that the worship of privilege has penetrated into every corner of the officialdom; Some netizens questioned, "Nowadays, offices all have water dispensers, so why is this notice posted?"

The forum poster claimed that the photo was taken at the entrance of the Lubei Transportation Bureau Director's Office Building, pointing out that "the office was concerned that the directors might not have boiling water to drink, so they arranged a special water heater for them."

On December 20th, Huasheng Online and many other forums featured a post titled "The Director of the Tangshan Lubei Transportation Bureau is really amazing," where an electric water heater with a notice stating "This place is exclusively for directors to drink water" pushed the Tangshan Lubei Transportation Bureau to the forefront of public opinion.

Reporters found that the post initially appeared on a local Tangshan forum and was then widely reprinted by various forums across the country. By the time the reporter finished writing, dozens of online forums had forwarded the post, and netizens were discussing it extensively.

"This place is exclusively for directors to drink water" - In the pictures provided in the forum post, reporters saw such a notice. The notice also reminded relevant personnel to pay attention, "Other comrades should go elsewhere to draw water."
