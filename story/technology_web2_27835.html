Let's go back to the topic from the scenes: Is your website healthy? It's very important to you!

It relates to your money, your friends and emotions in the virtual world, etc... So, what is a healthy website?

Definition of a Healthy Website

Basically speaking, whether a website is healthy must satisfy two conditions: one is whether it can be accessed by users, which we commonly refer to as accessibility; for example, within a day, whether its pages can always be seen by remote users, and whether there have been any prompts indicating that the page cannot be accessed; secondly, if all the pages of your website are visible to users, how fast are these pages accessed? When we switch pages, is it unbearably slow or does it respond quickly? If your website experiences multiple periods of inaccessibility within a day, or if it takes as long as making a cup of tea for users to open your webpage, then please note, your website has already largely deviated from the definition of being healthy. The red light is on, and you need to find ways to solve these troubles.

"The problem is, how do I know if my website is normal from the user's perspective?" When you innocently ask this, don't worry, let's move on to the next topic "Website Monitoring Protects Your Personal Website."

How do you know if your website is healthy?

Now you should know what you need, and what needs to be done for your website. That's right, you need to know at all times whether your website is healthily alive, and if it gets sick, you need to be notified immediately. With this understanding, you will realize how crucial website monitoring services are for your online business or emotional satisfaction.

What website monitoring services basically do is monitor the availability and response time of your website from the perspective of terminal users, at a certain frequency and across numerous locations. Once an anomaly occurs, the monitoring service will promptly and accurately alert you; for example, it will tell you at a specific date and time, from a specific location, that there was a problem accessing your website. Through such timely alerts and continuous checks, it helps you quickly identify and resolve issues, thereby reducing user loss and enhancing the cohesiveness of your website.

The role of website monitoring services goes far beyond just monitoring availability and response time. Through it, you can also learn how healthy your website is compared to competitors in the same industry; through it, you can determine whether the network service provider (ISP) you chose has provided quality service; through it, you can customize personalized website monitoring parameters and data reports. All of this exceeds your imagination of how wonderful it can be.
