The State Council's Several Opinions on Further Promoting the Economic and Social Development of Guangxi

State Council Document [2009] No. Central Government Portal Website Source: General Office of the State Council

To the People's Governments of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, all commissions and ministries of the State Council, and all subordinate institutions:

Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region is located in the convergence area of South China and Southwest China. It is an important gateway and frontier area for our country facing ASEAN, a convenient major channel for the southwestern region, and plays an important strategic role in promoting regional coordinated development, deepening opening-up and cooperation with ASEAN, maintaining national security, and stabilizing the southwestern border areas. Since the reform and opening-up, especially the implementation of the Western Development Strategy, cadres and masses of all ethnic groups in Guangxi have united and strived to develop, achieving significant economic and social progress. However, due to special historical reasons, Guangxi still faces many difficulties in its development. To further promote the rapid and good development of Guangxi's economy and society, the following opinions are now put forward.

I. Overall Requirements for Promoting the Economic and Social Development of Guangxi

(1) Significance. Guangxi is the autonomous region with the largest population of ethnic minorities in our country, as well as an old revolutionary base area and a border area. There are both areas along the Beibu Gulf coast with good development conditions and great potential, and large rocky mountainous areas that are concentrated and contiguous impoverished regions; there are abundant resources such as mineral products, tourism, and specialty agriculture, but also bottlenecks such as transportation, energy, and talent that restrict the conversion of resource advantages into economic advantages; there are favorable location conditions for participating in international and domestic regional cooperation, but also institutional and systemic obstacles to deepening open cooperation; currently, there are rare opportunities for accelerated development, but also serious challenges posed by the international financial crisis. In the new situation where domestic and international economic environments are undergoing profound changes, further promoting Guangxi's economic and social development will help form a new growth pole for the coastal economic development of our country, promote the development of western regions and interaction between eastern, central, and western regions; fully implement the national peripheral foreign strategy, deepen the strategic partnership between our country and ASEAN; further consolidate ethnic unity and border stability, achieve common development and prosperity of all ethnic groups; overcome the impact of the current international financial crisis, turn crises into opportunities, and promote the economy and society to take new steps.

(2) Guiding Thought. Hold high the great banner of socialism with Chinese characteristics, guided by Deng Xiaoping Theory and the important thought of "Three Represents," comprehensively implement the scientific outlook on development, adhere to and improve the system of regional autonomy for ethnic minorities, deeply implement the Western Development Strategy, further emancipate thinking, deepen reform and opening up, transform development methods, focus on advancing new industrialization and optimizing the industrial structure, focus on promoting coordinated urban-rural and regional development, focus on improving comprehensive utilization of resources and ecological environment protection levels, focus on developing social undertakings and solving prominent livelihood issues, focus on expanding openness and strengthening international regional cooperation, fully mobilize and play the enthusiasm of all parties, strive to achieve scientific development, leapfrog development, and lead the way in building a moderately prosperous society in all respects in the western region.

(3) Basic Principles. Adhere to scientific development and people-oriented approaches, promoting the coordination of economic and social development with population, resources, and the environment, ensuring that officials and masses of all ethnic groups share the fruits of reform and development; adhere to reform and opening up, innovate mechanisms, promote key field reforms and all-round openness, providing impetus for good and fast development; adhere to addressing current issues while planning for long-term development, not only effectively solving outstanding problems currently faced by economic and social development but also planning for long-term development from a global and strategic perspective; adhere to overall planning and focusing on key areas, not only grasping the overall picture and planning comprehensively but also seizing critical links to achieve breakthroughs; adhere to self-reliance and national support, fully utilizing the initiative and creativity of localities while increasing central government support.

() Strategic Tasks.

-- Build regional modern commerce, logistics, advanced manufacturing, specialty agriculture, and information flow centers. Fully leverage the aggregation, radiation, and leading roles of the Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone and the Xijiang Economic Belt, improve industries, accelerate the development of advanced manufacturing, high-tech industries, and modern services, vigorously develop specialty agriculture, and build a modern industrial system characterized by distinct features, cluster development, harmonious complementation, and strong competitiveness.

-- Establish a new high ground for international regional economic cooperation. Accelerate the construction and improvement of platforms for cooperation with ASEAN, expand cooperative fields, increase the scope of cooperation, innovate cooperative mechanisms, play a greater role in the China-ASEAN Free Trade Area, enhance the ability to participate in international economic cooperation and competition, upgrade the level of economic internationalization, and build an open economy system characterized by internal and external linkage, mutual benefit and win-win outcomes, safety, and efficiency.

-- Cultivate a new growth pole for coastal economic development in our country. Relying on coastal ports, further strengthen the construction of major channels in southwestern China, build international channels connecting multiple regions, actively develop coastal modern industries, optimize coastal economies, give full play to late-mover advantages, and form a new growth pole for coastal economic development in our country.

-- Build a culturally rich ethnic region. Uphold the theme of all ethnic groups uniting in struggle and jointly developing and prospering, conscientiously implement various ethnic policies, cherish and maintain socialist new ethnic relations, accelerate the economic and social development of ethnic regions, consolidate and develop a good situation of economic prosperity, social progress, ethnic unity, and border stability.

(5) Development Goals.

In the current period and for some time to come, it is essential to respond to the impact of the international financial crisis and maintain steady and relatively fast economic development. Starting from reality, earnestly implement all decisions and arrangements made by the central government, ensure growth, ensure people's livelihood, ensure stability, and continue to maintain the good momentum of Guangxi's development.

By 2015, the per capita regional GDP will reach the medium level of western regions, basic public service capabilities will be further improved; the industrial structure will be further optimized, and comprehensive economic strength will be significantly enhanced; new progress will be made in institutional and mechanism innovation, and the pattern of an open economy will basically take shape; the income levels of urban and rural residents will significantly increase, and the social security system will basically cover both urban and rural areas; production and living conditions in special type poverty-stricken areas will be significantly improved, and the number of poor people will continue to decrease; the energy consumption per unit of regional GDP will significantly drop, and the total amount of major pollutants discharged will be effectively controlled.

By 2020, the goal of building a moderately prosperous society in all respects will be achieved; the per capita regional GDP will reach the medium level of the whole country; basic public service capabilities will reach the national average level; a reasonable, optimized, resource-saving, and competitive modern industrial system will basically take shape; a new growth pole for the coastal economic development of our country and a more attractive new high ground for cooperation with ASEAN will be built; the income of urban and rural residents will basically reach the national average level, and the income gap between urban and rural residents will tend to narrow; the quality of life, health levels, and cultural qualities of all ethnic groups will significantly improve; the Eight Provinces will remain green and water clear, the sea blue and the sky bright, with good ecology and beautiful environment, and the ability for sustainable development will significantly enhance.

II. Promote Coastal and Riverside Leading Development, Improve Regional Development Layout

() Give Full Play to the Leading Role of the Beibu Gulf Economic Zone. In accordance with the requirements of high starting point, high level, and high standards, accelerate the implementation of the Guangxi Beibu Gulf Economic Zone Development Plan, adhere to rational and orderly development, quickly transform Guangxi's coastal areas into a strategic highland for the Western Development Strategy and an important international regional economic cooperation zone. Make full use of the port advantages, actively introduce large enterprises from home and abroad, focus on developing petrochemicals, steel, forest pulp paper, shipbuilding and repair, electronics information, grain and oil processing, new energy and other industries, cultivate and strengthen port-related industry clusters, and accelerate the formation of coastal advanced manufacturing and modern logistics. Currently, it is necessary to speed up the elimination of outdated production capacity, actively promote corporate mergers and acquisitions, further adjust the industrial structure, and timely construct key projects such as Fangchenggang Steel Premium Products, Qinzhou Refinery Phase II, and Beihai Tieshan Port Petrochemicals. Accelerate the construction of a one-hour city circle of Nanning (Nanning), Beihai (Beihai), Qinzhou (Qinzhou), and Fangchenggang (Fangchenggang), strengthen the connection and co-construction of infrastructure such as railways, highways, and aviation with surrounding areas, enhance the agglomeration effect of urban groups, and form an important passage hub connecting multiple regions. Encourage pilot trials in administrative management, finance, banking, investment and financing, land, and foreign-related economic reforms. Implement preferential tax policies for encouraged industries, conduct pilot programs linking the increase of urban construction land use with the reduction of rural construction land. For projects already included in the plan, accelerate approval and verification, and improve administrative efficiency.

(VII) Actively Create the Agglomeration Advantage of the Xijiang Economic Belt. The Xijiang River region in Eastern Guangdong, Central Guangxi, and Northern Guangxi, facing the Pearl River Delta, backed by the Southwest hinterland, with convenient transportation and a good industrial foundation, needs to further integrate resources, gather momentum, and accelerate the formation of the Xijiang Economic Belt. Accelerate the development of the Xijiang Golden Waterway, improve navigation capacity, form a comprehensive transportation system where railways, highways, and waterways connect and complement each other, effectively reduce comprehensive logistics costs, and provide strong support for industrial expansion, upgrading, and agglomeration. Use key cities within the region as nodes, and industrial parks as carriers, improve spatial layouts, and form industrial belts with clear divisions of labor, obvious advantages, and collaborative support. Liuzhou should increase efforts in adjusting its industrial structure, strengthen the automotive, machinery, metallurgy, and chemical industries, and accelerate the construction of advanced manufacturing. Guilin should make full use of its tourism resource advantages, create an international tourist destination, promote the upgrading of machinery, auto parts, rubber, medicine, and deep processing of specialty agricultural and forestry products, and further develop the national high-tech industrial development zone. Laibin should improve the comprehensive processing and utilization level of sugarcane, actively develop aluminum and manganese deep processing, and cultivate and strengthen emerging resource-processing industries. Wuzhou, Yulin, Guigang, Hezhou, and other places should accelerate market alignment with the Pearl River Delta, improve the investment environment, enhance supporting capabilities, actively承接eastern industrial transfers, expand the scale of industries, and improve development levels. Hasten research and formulation of the Xijiang Economic Belt Development Plan.

(VIII) Enhance the Self-development Capability of Resource-rich Areas in Western Guangxi. Western Guangxi has abundant mineral, hydropower, and tourism resources. It should actively implement the resource development strategy, vigorously develop characteristic industries, and actively explore new paths for accelerating development in old, minority, border, and poor mountainous areas. Baise should focus on creating a nationally important aluminum industry and red tourism destination, and accelerate the development of coal, power, and agricultural product processing industries. Hechi should focus on non-ferrous metals, hydropower, and eco-tourism, and accelerate the development of specialty food and silkworm industries. Chongzuo should focus on sugar and manganese deep processing, and accelerate the development of tourism, cement, sisal deep processing, and other industries. Chongzuo and Baise should utilize their border advantages to accelerate the development of border trade logistics and export processing industries.

III. Accelerate the Development of Modern Agriculture, Consolidate the Foundation for Economic and Social Development

(IX) Strengthen Grain Production in Reality. Ensure the balance of grain production and sales, strive to increase the self-sufficiency rate of grain. Implement the strictest farmland protection system, stabilize the area of grain cultivation. Increase the intensity of land consolidation and low-yield field transformation, strengthen agricultural infrastructure construction, actively promote good seeds and farming practices, and build a number of advantageous production areas for rice, corn, and winter-planted potatoes. Implement national policies for increasing grain production capacity, establish a long-term mechanism to promote grain yield and efficiency, implement supportive policies for major grain-producing counties, and effectively guarantee reasonable returns for grain farmers. Strengthen the construction and renovation of grain circulation facilities, develop regional grain modern logistics. Establish an emergency grain processing and supply system, and further improve the grain reserve system.

(X) Vigorously Develop Specialty Agriculture. Leverage the advantages of specialty agricultural resources, actively develop famous, unique, and new agricultural products, and build an important production area for subtropical agricultural products in our country. Stabilize and reasonably control the planting area of sugarcane, improve the interest-sharing and risk-sharing mechanism between sugar enterprises and sugarcane farmers. Strengthen the research, breeding, and promotion of sugarcane superior varieties, support main producing counties (cities, districts) in improving production conditions, steadily promote the mechanization of sugarcane production, and increase single yield and sucrose content. While continuing to develop sericulture, subtropical fruits, and cassava production, focus on strengthening the construction of processing and storage capacities, extend the industrial chain, consolidate and improve the leading position nationwide. Increase support for the development of advantageous specialty agriculture such as sugarcane and sericulture. Make full use of mountainous resources, develop fast-growing forests, economic forests, woody oil crops, flowers, and forest tree seedling industries based on local conditions. Develop ecological agriculture and sightseeing agriculture. Actively develop livestock and aquaculture industries, focusing on dairy water buffalo and grass-eating livestock and poultry, support the standardized transformation of healthy aquaculture ponds, and expand the scale of advantageous products such as shrimp, tilapia, and pearls. Support the construction of the Beihai National Agricultural Science and Technology Park. Improve the animal and plant disease prevention and control system, and construct a border major animal disease prevention and control interception belt. Fully leverage the leading role of Guangxi's agricultural reclamation, and promote the industrialization of specialty agriculture.

(XI) Expand and Strengthen County Economies. Aimed at enriching the people, strengthening counties, and striving for a moderately prosperous society, actively develop township secondary and tertiary industries, and increase farmers' income through multiple channels. Focus on the processing of specialty agricultural products and resource-based industries, accelerate the development of industrial clusters complementary to large and medium-sized cities and large enterprise groups, promote cooperation between small and medium-sized enterprises and large enterprises, and create a group of industrially strong counties. Encourage urban industrial and commercial enterprises to establish raw material production and processing bases in rural areas, reasonably plan industrial concentration zones, and promote the flow of urban capital, technology, talents, and management factors to county areas. Promote the restructuring and reform of county enterprises, foster strong township enterprises, and actively develop rural commerce, logistics, tourism, and other service industries. Vigorously promote agricultural industrialized operations, support the establishment of relatively complete processing systems in major agricultural product production areas, and use fiscal interest subsidies and subsidies to support the development of leading enterprises and specialized cooperative economic organizations. Accelerate the development of county towns and key towns, promote household registration system reforms, further relax the conditions for settling in small and medium-sized cities, and enable farmers who work and live stably in towns to become urban residents in an orderly manner. Trial the direct management of counties by the Autonomous Region's financial management system, and enhance the financial integration capabilities of counties.

(XII) Strengthen Rural Infrastructure and Public Service Capacity Construction. Place the focus of infrastructure construction and public services in rural areas, and genuinely improve rural production and living conditions. Strengthen the construction of small-scale farmland water conservancy, accelerate the supporting transformation of irrigation areas, and expand the effective irrigated area of farmland. Implement rural drinking water safety projects and increase the popularization rate of tap water in rural areas. Accelerate the construction of county and township roads, promote the "unobstructed" project for townships and the "accessible" project for villages in rural roads, conduct pilot projects for the construction of asphalt (cement) roads to registered villages, and increase the density of road networks and the accessibility rates of counties, towns, villages, and natural villages. Construct rural household biogas pits, rural biogas rural service outlets, large and medium-sized biogas projects, livestock farms, and joint household biogas projects. Accelerate the construction of rural electrification counties and small hydropower fuel replacement projects, and improve the rural power grid. Gradually incorporate the infrastructure construction of overseas Chinese agricultural and forestry farms, state-owned farms, and state-owned forest farms into local plans. Extend public services such as medical care, education and training, agricultural science and technology, news publishing, radio and television, and sports fitness to rural areas. Actively carry out pilot projects to expand rural dilapidated house renovations. Appropriately reduce municipal matching funds for public welfare construction projects deployed in Hechi and Baise by central investment.

(Thirteen) Deepen Rural Reforms. Stabilize and improve the basic rural operation system, establish a rigorous and standardized rural land management system, grant farmers more adequate and guaranteed rights to contract and operate land, and keep the existing land contracting relationship stable and unchanged for a long term. According to the principles of legality, voluntariness, and compensation, allow farmers to adopt various ways to transfer the right to contract and operate land, and explore the establishment of a service market for the transfer of the right to contract and operate land. Strictly manage homesteads and protect the usufructuary rights of households over homesteads according to law. Establish a modern rural financial system. Carry out pilot reforms of provincial rural credit cooperatives, and accelerate the establishment of rural commercial banks while maintaining the stability of the county-level positions of rural credit cooperatives. Cultivate and develop new financial institutions such as village and town banks, loan companies, and rural capital mutual aid cooperatives, and establish and improve systems for farmer small credit loans and joint guarantee loans for farmers. Accelerate the construction of rural credit guarantee systems, increase the variety of effective guarantees in rural areas, and allow qualified farmers' professional cooperatives to conduct credit cooperation. Expand the scope of postal savings bank services related to agriculture, and increase credit support for "agriculture, rural areas, and farmers." Explore the establishment of a mechanism for compensating losses from agricultural loans. Promote collective forest tenure reforms, and basically complete the tasks of clarifying property rights and contracting to households within about five years. Promote the construction of mechanisms for promoting agriculture through industry and bringing cities to the countryside. According to the directions of integrating systems into localities, managing into society, and economically integrating into the market, promote the reform and development of overseas Chinese agricultural and forestry farms, and currently focus on accelerating the construction of infrastructure such as roads, water supply, and electricity supply.

Persist in the Direction of Industrialization Dominance, Promote Structural Optimization and Upgrading

(Ten) Vigorously Develop Advanced Manufacturing. Actively cultivate advantageous industrial clusters, optimize the mode of resource development and utilization, adhere to the path of new-type industrialization, and accelerate the transformation of resource advantages into economic advantages. Vigorously develop pillar industries such as non-ferrous metals mainly aluminum, automobiles mainly economical cars, food mainly sugar-making, petrochemicals mainly oil refining, metallurgy mainly steel, and machinery manufacturing mainly engineering machinery, extend the industrial chain towards refined processing, create famous brands, and build a nationally important sugar industry, non-ferrous metals, petrochemicals, and steel. Accelerate the development of advantageous industries such as building materials, papermaking, shipbuilding, silk, clothing, timber processing, forest chemical industry, and pharmaceuticals, expand the scale of industries, and upgrade the level of industries. Develop ethnic pharmaceutical industries mainly focused on Zhuang medicine. Promote the adjustment of industrial structures, eliminate backward production capacities in industries such as metallurgy, papermaking, and chemicals. Encourage large enterprise groups to combine and reorganize, and accelerate the cultivation of a batch of enterprises with sales revenue exceeding 10 billion yuan. Support the construction of a circular economy demonstration province for the sugar industry, improve the national and local two-level sugar reserve regulation and control mechanism, and study including the production of fuel ethanol from sugarcane in the scope of central fiscal bio-energy and bio-chemical non-grain guidance reward special funds. For state-owned enterprises in old industrial cities like Liuzhou, Guilin, Nanning, Wuzhou, and Yulin, implement revitalization policies for old industrial areas in Northeast China, etc., in terms of handling bad assets and technical transformation upgrades. Appropriately lower the minimum price standard for industrial land, and support existing mining exploration work in the deep parts and peripheries of mines. Currently, earnestly implement the adjustment and revitalization plans for key industries, continue implementing the agricultural machinery purchase subsidy policy, implement policies for rural cars, motorcycles, and home appliances, as well as technical transformation interest subsidies, tax incentives, important industrial product reserves, and increased issuance of corporate bonds, to help enterprises get through difficulties.
