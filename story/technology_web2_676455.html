Crystal columns in the living room, glass column vestibule, crystal curtain maintenance: 1. Do not hang crystal curtains in harsh environments such as attics or basements, and also avoid strong sunlight, otherwise they will easily discolor, affecting color and aesthetics. 2. In daily life, pay attention to keeping your hands clean before touching crystal curtains, and do not touch them with greasy dirt, which can easily leave stains and damage the appearance of the crystal curtains. 3. Try to keep crystal curtains away from water or corrosive liquids, especially in summer, and also avoid letting crystal curtains stay in a humid environment to maintain their dryness and prevent unattractive "spots". 4. Since the nature of crystals is relatively fragile, be sure to avoid heavy pressure, prevent dropping and high temperatures, and do not let them come into contact with hard objects to avoid scratches. Be careful when pulling the curtains to avoid breaking the crystal strings on the curtains. 5. When maintaining the artificial crystals on the crystal curtains, do not easily use jewelry cleaning agents or ultrasonic cleaners sold on the market to avoid fading and oxidation.

Crystal Curtain Cleaning: 1. Try not to wash or wipe the crystal with water. If you need to clean the crystal curtain, use a soft, non-fluffy dry cloth to wipe it, and be careful not to apply too much force. 2. When dust accumulates on the surface of the crystal curtain, use blowing rather than stroking or wiping with external objects to remove the dust. 3. If the crystal curtain is very dirty, it can be washed with water. After mixing regular detergent with water, place the removed crystal curtain in it, clean it with a soft-bristled brush or by hand, then rinse it with clean water, take it out to air dry, and when it is half dry, wipe it clean with a soft towel.
