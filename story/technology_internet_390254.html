Female college students earn 300,000 yuan in 5 years through their self-initiated online store entrepreneurship project.

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 14:51:02

The female college student's self-employment project began with opening an online shop in her second year of university in 2005. She sold Shenyang specialty foods, Korean and Japanese leather bags, intimate clothing, etc. In five years, the initial capital of 500 yuan for the student's self-employment project has rapidly grown to over 300,000 yuan.

Among more than 2 million online shops, most people are still striving for a few more diamonds (a rating system on Taobao). However, Suddenly's Shop has developed from a single red heart to the current double crown, which is indeed a miracle. A 26-year-old girl, without hiring anyone to boost her ratings or advertising on Taobao, almost entirely by herself, built up a small shop bit by bit, which has now become a well-known specialty store on Taobao. How did she succeed? What experiences can she share with readers? Yesterday, this reporter went to Suddenly's small shop for a face-to-face interview with her.

Self-employment Project for College Students: Starting a Business in the Second Year

Suddenly's small shop is located in a residential neighborhood near the Shenyang Real Estate Building, in a 60-square-meter apartment on the first floor. The room is filled with many plastic mannequins, wearing down jackets that are newly stocked.

In her second year of university, Suddenly registered this shop online called "Suddenly's Special Search Store."

"At the time, I just randomly came up with a name; it didn't have any special meaning. Initially, the shop only resold Shenyang specialties such as Bulaolin candy and other local delicacies. I bought samples myself, took photos, uploaded them to the online shop, and when out-of-town netizens placed orders, I would buy the goods from Carrefour, at a cost of 7.8 yuan per bag, selling them for 8.8 yuan per bag, then mailing them to customers. In the first month, I actually made over 100 yuan, and I was particularly excited. Later, I increased the variety of products in the shop, including foreign trade bags, clothing, etc., and the number of customers grew. After half a year, I could earn over 1,000 yuan per month. At that time, I offered free delivery within Shenyang, taking public transportation to deliver goods to customers. Although it was busy, my heart was full of joy," Suddenly said.

Self-employment Project for College Students: Living Expenses for Opening an Online Shop

Between studying, she only invested 20% of her energy and could earn over 1,000 yuan per month. If she devoted herself fully after graduation, she would definitely earn more. As graduation approached, many classmates were busy looking for jobs, but Suddenly had resolved to focus on running her online shop after graduation. Unexpectedly, this idea was rejected by her parents first.

"Both of my parents thought that running an online shop wasn't sustainable, especially for a girl, it would be even harder. Finding a stable job and working regularly would be much better. But I am the type of person who is very stubborn; once I make a decision, I won't easily change it," Suddenly said. Her parents wouldn't provide funds, so she saved money from her living expenses. She reinvested the money earned from her online shop back into it. By the time Suddenly graduated from university in 2006, the level of her online shop had reached three diamonds, with monthly sales exceeding ten thousand yuan, and her monthly income had also reached 2,000 yuan.

Self-employment Project for College Students: A "Homebody" with a Monthly Salary Over Ten Thousand Yuan

Suddenly said, "Opening an online shop has low costs, currently no taxes need to be paid temporarily, there's no need to rent a shop on bustling commercial streets; finding a house on a regular residential area's first floor is sufficient. You can buy a computer to go online and open a shop, and it can also serve as a warehouse. I handle purchasing goods, taking photos, editing and uploading posts, chatting with customers, and delivering packages myself. Every day, I work for more than 10 hours, and currently, I send out over 50 items per day."

She specializes in unique products, sometimes personally going to Qingdao and Hangzhou to stock up on goods, ensuring quality and authenticity. Last November, her monthly sales even exceeded 60,000 yuan.

Effort pays off, and Currently, Suddenly's business is thriving, with a monthly salary exceeding ten thousand yuan. The initial investment of 500 yuan has snowballed to over 300,000 yuan. She has just become the Shenyang agent for a clothing company in Shandong, stocking up on winter clothes, preparing for a good run during the autumn and winter seasons.
