Currently, wall coverings suitable for home decoration on the market can roughly be divided into three categories. To master them, one must first understand them. Below, Italian Roma experts will introduce them one by one.

1. Plastic Wallpaper

Made from PVC plastic, varieties and colors are very rich, making it the most widely used type of wallpaper for home window top-end decoration. It is made through coating, printing processes, flexible and wear-resistant, washable, acid and alkali resistant, with sound-absorbing and heat-insulating functions. Plastic wallpapers are further divided into regular wallpaper and foamed wallpaper. Regular wallpaper includes single-color embossed, printed embossed, glossy embossed, and matte embossed types, which are currently the most commonly used wallpapers; Foamed wallpaper comes in high-foam printed, low-foam printed, and embossed printed varieties. High-foam wallpaper has elastic embossed patterns, serving as both decorative and sound-absorbing multi-functional wallpaper. Low-foam wallpaper features embossed patterns in the same color, with effects like wood grain, mosaic, and imitation tiles, realistic patterns, strong three-dimensional sense, good decorative effect, suitable for indoor wainscoting, living rooms, and indoor corridors decoration.

2. Plant Fiber Wallpaper

Made from plant fibers such as hemp and grass, it is a high-end decorative material with strong texture, non-toxic, breathable, sound-absorbing, naturally harmonious, and aesthetically pleasing but complex in production process. The tensile strength of plant fiber wallpaper is five times that of regular wallpaper. If stains appear, they can not only be wiped clean with water but also brushed off.

3. Textile Wallpaper

Made from silk, wool, cotton, linen, etc., fibers, it has a good texture and breathability. It creates a sense of sophistication, elegance, softness, and comfort. However, the surface of the wallpaper easily accumulates dust and is hard to clean, requiring dust-cleaning equipment when in use. Fabric wallpaper is a new type of wallpaper, also known as wall fabric. It is a type of textile wallpaper, used in combination with coatings, its color can be arbitrarily adjusted according to the color of the coating itself.

Determine your needs and purposefully select wallpaper. The sole principle is to coordinate with the overall decoration style. For spacious rooms, choose large flowers or wider stripe patterns to make the room appear fuller. For smaller rooms with more furniture, choose finer patterns and lighter-colored wallpaper to expand the space. For dimly lit rooms, it is advisable to use light warm tones; for well-lit, bright rooms, deep-toned wallpaper can easily highlight the indoor atmosphere. Living rooms are ideal for wallpapers with light, elegant, and simple patterns; bedrooms should have warm colors and strong decorative pattern wallpapers to create a sweet, intimate atmosphere. Kitchens and bathrooms should have bright-colored, washable wallpapers.
