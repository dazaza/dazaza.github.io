III. Discounted Treatment Projects

Half-price Assistance Full Assistance

In response to the call of the National Health Department, following the rescue spirit of the "Golden Lily" Women's Health Promotion Project, to let love warm hearts. The only designated hospital in the Suzhou area for the "Golden Lily" Women's Health Promotion Project - Kangli Hospital - has specially established a special public welfare fund. To ensure that the sponsorship work is standardized, open, and transparent, the relevant matters are announced as follows:

I. Assistance Targets

1. 20 female patients who have fallen into poverty due to illness;

2. 10 extremely impoverished women, city-level labor models, city-level March 8th Red Banners female patients.

II. Surgical Assistance Scope

Minimally invasive surgeries including gynecological tumors, ovarian cysts, uterine fibroids, infertility, family planning, etc., performed via hysteroscopy or laparoscopy.

III. Assistance Content

1. For 20 female patients who have fallen into poverty due to illness, provide half-price reduction on surgical fees, treatment fees, and hospitalization fees;

2. For 10 extremely impoverished women, city-level labor models, city-level March 8th Red Banners female patients, provide full financial assistance for all surgical expenses at our hospital;

3. Provide a free "Golden Lily" Women's Assistance Ward (3 beds), until fully occupied.

IV. Assistance Conditions

1. For 20 female patients who have fallen into poverty due to illness, provide half-price reduction on surgical fees, treatment fees, and hospitalization fees, conditions include:

(1) Need to provide identity proof, township-level government or county-level civil affairs department issued proof of household economic difficulties;

2. For 10 extremely impoverished women, city-level labor models, city-level March 8th Red Banners female patients, provide full financial assistance for all surgical expenses at our hospital, conditions include:

(1) Need to provide identity proof, county-level (or above) issued proof of extreme household economic difficulties;

(2) City-level labor model, city-level March 8th Red Banner female patients need to provide honor certificates issued by city-level government or organizations;

(3) Meeting any one of the above two conditions is sufficient.

3. To enjoy the above assistance, it must be reviewed by the Organizing Committee of the Suzhou "Golden Lily" Women's Health Promotion Project, and only after passing the review can corresponding women's health assistance be obtained. Limited slots available.
