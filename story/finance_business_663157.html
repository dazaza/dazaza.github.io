Divorced man solicits votes online to regain his ex-wife

by haojing12 on 2012-02-11 11:04:36

Xiao Yi and his ex-wife's合影. "I want to win back my ex-wife, please help me everyone...." With Valentine's Day approaching, a recent post on Xici Hutong has caught much attention. The man known online as "Romantic Element," Xiao Yi, says that in the six months since their divorce, he has been constantly thinking about the woman with whom he spent 12 years of his life. As a brand is organizing a Valentine's Day confession contest, he hopes to use the power of netizens to make his heartfelt message appear on the Xinjiekou electronic screen on Valentine's evening, thereby making a sincere confession to his ex-wife who will "happen to pass by"...

Reporter Zhang Lei

That year, he was 22, she was 19.

Once, they loved each other deeply.

Their love left footprints everywhere they went together.

Recently, at his home in Nanjing’s Maigaoqiao area, Xiao Yi told reporters the story of him and his ex-wife.

In 2000, Xiao Yi was 22 years old, studying in his fourth year at a university in Xi'an, full of tension and anticipation for stepping into society. That summer, 19-year-old Yan Hong also entered this university; like Xiao Yi, she was from Jiangsu’s Sihong County, naturally getting to know this fellow townsman senior. Eating, chatting, shopping...the two young hearts drew closer and fell in love soon after.

In Xiao Yi's computer, there are all the photos taken from the start of their romance through the years, Terracotta Warriors, Famen Temple, Guangdong, Hangzhou... or even just city streets, campus corners, every photo is filled with sweetness and happiness.

After graduation, Xiao Yi chose to stay in Xi'an, starting a business while accompanying the girl he loved, until Yan Hong graduated in 2004. That year, they came to Nanjing together, “As long as the two of us are together, whether the city is strange, whether the future is uncertain, finding jobs, renting houses, daily necessities... these really aren't issues.” Thus, the two leaned on each other, building their own tomorrow.

Once, they were inseparable.

Even after many years of marriage, they often cuddled and chatted till dawn.

>> On that night, he made the biggest mistake of his life.

The pressure of life led him to drown his sorrows in alcohol, and one drunken bout where he raised his hand changed everything.

“You say, what else could separate us?” Such jokes have appeared more than once in Xiao Yi and Yan Hong's conversations.

In June 2010, Yan Hong gave birth to a lovely daughter, "Big eyes, very much like her." Yesterday, Xiao Yi showed reporters picture after picture of his daughter, with the clear-featured Yan Hong often appearing as the main character, mother and daughter indeed looking like they were cut from the same mold. "Everything went smoothly and naturally, the birth of my daughter made me feel that my life was complete up to this point." Xiao Yi sighed, stroking the adorable face far away in Sihong’s hometown on his computer screen.

He never imagined that life's turning point would come so suddenly. "New fatherhood, no experience. Working during the day, taking care of wife and child at night, plus the frequent quarrels between my wife and mother over the child, being stuck in the middle is truly uncomfortable, mentally exhausted every day..." And that period coincided with an economic crisis, his business wasn't doing well either, "I already had a habit of drinking a little, when my mind was troubled and my body was tired, it was easier to get drunk." Because of this, Yan Hong had argued with him countless times, thus the atmosphere of conflict in the house gradually became thicker.

Finally, on the evening of National Day, the cries of the child, the nagging of his mother, the client’s order cancellations, and his wife's scolding...all these elements drove the already thoroughly drunk Xiao Yi crazy, "I hit her..." Xiao Yi doesn't remember how he hit her, only knowing that when he sobered up, he found himself sitting blankly on the sofa, his elderly mother weeping beside him, his wife and daughter gone, the house filled with the smell of alcohol, the taste of home drifting further and further away.

His wife calmly brought back the divorce agreement, and in anger, he really signed it.

A few days after staying at her parents' house, Yan Hong returned. Life seemed normal again, but Xiao Yi told reporters yesterday that from then on, his wife looked at him with much more estrangement in her eyes, "We can never go back to before." This sentence was spoken much later by Yan Hong, now his ex-wife, "Some mistakes can be made many times, like drinking; but some mistakes, one time is enough."

Cold war, arguments, leaving home, returning home...and repeating this cycle, "From that night to the divorce, exactly eight months, I couldn't make up for my mistake, instead, I used stubbornness and irritability to personally destroy our marriage." Xiao Yi painfully recalled. One day in mid-June last year, just over ten days before their daughter's first birthday, "She brought back a divorce agreement, very calmly asking me to sign. I asked her if she thought it through, signing means no turning back. She looked at me without saying a word. I got angry, so fine, let's sign..." Minutes later, Yan Hong left with a simple suitcase, leaving behind only one sentence, "I don't want anything, leave it all for you and the daughter." A few days later, the two exchanged their marriage certificate for a divorce certificate.

On the night of the formal divorce, Xiao Yi returned home, his mother and daughter were in the hometown, the empty house left only him alone. "Started regretting uncontrollably, regretting drinking, regretting hitting her, regretting arguing with her later, regretting signing that agreement..." All night, Xiao Yi said he sat blankly staring at the divorce certificate, crying like a fool.

In 2006, Xiao Yi and Yan Hong had already established themselves in Nanjing and owned their love nest near Maigaoqiao. Reporters saw yesterday that the house wasn't big, and the decoration was rather simple, but it was enough to make the two young people who had long recognized each other consider marrying. "No proposal, just told her let's go get the certificate tomorrow, so the next day we went to the Civil Affairs Bureau." That night, they went to a restaurant, ordered a few dishes, drank a little wine, and then went home to chat under the covers until dawn.

Xiao Yi will always remember the intimacy he once shared with Yan Hong. "A year after marriage, because of work, I had to be away on business for several months, but I didn't want to leave her for such a long time, finally managed to introduce her to this work team, telling others she was a classmate of mine." During those months, most of the time they could only "stop at propriety", yet they could always convey their love through a glance or a smile, "That feeling was really sweet and unforgettable."

And until the second decade of the new century, the two could still often cuddle and chat all night, "It seems there's endless talk, the so-called seven-year itch and aesthetic fatigue do not exist between us. If there's really a kind of love that leaves an indelible mark, I think it should be like ours."

>> This time, he wants to win back lost love.

Going home after the divorce became the greatest torment, for her, he quit drinking.

"Many things only become clear when you truly lose them, and realize the cost is something you simply can't bear." Saying this, Xiao Yi was trying hard to hold back tears.

Life after the divorce, every night going home became the most difficult and painful thing for Xiao Yi. "The sofa was picked out together with her; the flowers on the balcony were planted by her; the graffiti on the wall was drawn by her to amuse the child; everything at home reminds me of the beautiful moments we experienced here together..." Xiao Yi quickly moved into the guest room, and even dared not enter the master bedroom on weekdays, "In the morning, instinctively hugging the side, the feeling of missing her is really unbearable."

"Can love be won back?" After viewing countless photos of Yan Hong on his computer countless times, Xiao Yi asked himself. He almost immediately jumped up and called Yan Hong. Due to the child, he and his ex-wife had maintained contact after the divorce. On the phone, Xiao Yi stammered and asked Yan Hong if she could give him another chance? After a long silence, Yan Hong said, "Do you know? If that night you hadn't signed the agreement but tried to keep me, maybe we wouldn't have come to this point. But now, it's too late." Then she hung up.

"Because of the 12 years we've walked together, I don't think it's too late." Xiao Yi told reporters yesterday. He did not give up because of Yan Hong's decisiveness, repeatedly expressing his remorse and longing for her. Although Yan Hong kept telling him that the two could never be together again, her words gradually softened, like one time she asked, "Can you change yourself?"

"I can, I really can." Xiao Yi told reporters that shortly after the divorce, he quit drinking, "For half a year, I haven't touched alcohol. Actually, my temper isn't bad, we didn't argue much for so many years, it was all caused by alcohol..." Meanwhile, he actively planned for the future, the responsibility of raising the child and the hope of winning back his lover made him full of energy, his business also improved significantly. "Responsibility, commitment, and respect and care for her." Xiao Yi understood the "change" Yan Hong needed in this way.

During this time, their daughter grew bigger and cuter, Yan Hong almost called every day to hear the child call her "Mommy" a few times, and rejoiced at the new words the baby learned. Through the child as a bridge, her and Xiao Yi's contact increased. Inevitably, Xiao Yi took the opportunity to ask for a "chance". After one time Yan Hong suggested he "find someone", he seriously answered, "If I find someone, it will be someone who truly loves the daughter, and that person is you."

Voting on various forums, striving to rekindle the relationship with his ex-wife on Valentine's Day.

Xiao Yi said that although Yan Hong still hasn't agreed to his "pursuit", their relationship has greatly improved. "A few days ago, we lit a bonfire at an open space at the entrance of the community, sat on the ground and talked for a long time, and finally both cried. I firmly believe that she still has feelings for me."

By chance, Xiao Yi saw online that a certain brand was holding a Valentine's Day confession contest, "After uploading the confession, if it gets high click popularity, it will appear on the Xinjiekou electronic screen on Valentine's evening, this is a chance given by heaven."

"Yan Hong, after half a year of divorce, I haven't stopped thinking about you every day, I can't live without you, and the child can't live without a mother, give me one more chance." This confession, paired with a few photos from their romantic past, was uploaded online by Xiao Yi, then he started voting on various forums, requesting netizens to help him give his ex-wife a surprise on Valentine's Day. "I've already arranged with her to take the child to Xinjiekou to shop on Valentine's evening, she agreed..." Yesterday, reporters saw that with the help of netizens, Xiao Yi's confession ranked at the top.

At the end of the article, the reporter wants to send a netizen's blessing to Xiao Yi, and also to Yan Hong who declined the interview. "You've already agreed to spend Valentine's Day with him, success is within sight. How to cherish the recovered happiness, you know."
