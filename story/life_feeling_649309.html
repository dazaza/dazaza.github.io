Wife's Diary: Recording Our Happiness

In the middle of the night, I woke up and felt my husband tightly hugging me. I was secretly pleased! I thought: this guy usually seems pretty cool, but who would've thought that while sleeping he accidentally reveals his true feelings. So, I was deeply moved and just as I was about to enjoy his embrace, I heard him mumble in a daze: "Honey, it's so cold!" At that moment, I wanted to kick him out of bed.

One day, while watching TV with my husband, an actress was dancing ballet on the screen. My husband said to me: "Dear, you're also suitable for ballet." I was secretly happy! I thought: my husband must think I have a good figure. But I wanted him to be more straightforward with his compliments, so I held my breath and asked him: "Why do you say I'm suitable for ballet?" My husband seriously and professionally replied: "People who dance ballet can't have too big chests." I almost fell off my chair.

On a weekend morning after getting up, I talked to my husband about recent expenses, feeling that we often spend money recklessly, which is not good. So, we decided to get rid of the habit of spending money carelessly. In the evening, my husband accompanied me to the supermarket. I saw my favorite Sachi Ma (a type of Chinese pastry), but I didn't know which brand to buy. So, I randomly picked one, priced at 4 kuai 8. Just as I was about to pick it up, I heard my husband calling out incessantly beside me: "4 kuai 6, 4 kuai 6." After hearing that, I couldn't stop laughing, realizing how serious he was about our saving plan.

One morning, I was resting while my husband was going to work. I sent him to the elevator entrance. The elevator door opened, I turned around to go home, then I heard my husband calling me from behind. I turned around and saw him standing at the elevator entrance, one foot raised blocking the elevator door, leaning forward playfully saying: "Honey, there's no one inside, give me a kiss!" I was both angry and amused!

Once, while combing my hair in front of the mirror, I said to my husband: "Imagine if your husband came home every day after work to cook and wash clothes, and I don't have to do anything, just go to work, wouldn't that be great?" My husband walked to me and kept shaking me, saying: "Honey, wake up, wake up, it's not early anymore." I was completely defeated by my husband.

My husband and I like to watch DVDs together, but every time we need to change discs, it's very painful, especially in winter when we don't want to leave the warmth of our bed. So, every time the picture stopped, I immediately turned my head and pretended to sleep, even snoring. Seeing this, my husband had no choice but to get up and change the disc himself. As soon as the disc was loaded, I woke up immediately, pretending to be half-asleep and said: "What happened? What happened? Do we need to change the disc? Let me do it, let me do it." My husband said I was too naughty. A few days later, I forgot about this incident. When it was time to change the disc, I was about to call him, but he had already turned his head and fallen asleep. Naturally, I did the same thing to him, laughing myself to death.

After washing the dishes, I also cleaned the stainless steel pot with great effort, finally making it brighter than when it was new. So, I was extremely excited! My husband was standing on a stool on the balcony drying clothes, I eagerly held up the pot and showed it to him. He looked at the pot, tilting his head left and right carefully examining it, but he didn't compliment me. Just as I was about to ask him, he nonchalantly brushed his hair with his hand, "Hmm, this young man is still quite handsome..."

At first, my wife said she couldn't cook. I said: "Not possible, I can." As a result, now I do all the cooking! Haha.

When he came to pick me up after work, I was shouting that I wanted to buy bananas. We arrived and found two girls from my company also buying them. I knew them well, but he didn't at all. I called out to them: "Great! I don't have to buy them, right?" The girl generously handed over a bunch of bananas: "Take them! Why are you being so polite?" I only took one, and the girl said: "Take more! Why are you being so polite?" He followed suit and said: "Take two!" The colleague hesitated for a moment and quickly agreed: "Take more! Take more!" He said no no, two are enough. Then I broke off another one, surprised at how he could embarrass me like this, he handed the net bag to me and gave the two bananas to the colleague, seriously saying: "Thank you!" Everyone laughed wildly when they remembered this the next day at work...

My husband likes to hide in the house and make me find him, but our house is too small, so I always find him easily. One night before sleeping, he went to turn off the light (the switch is a certain distance from the bed). After turning it off, I saw him quickly squat down on the ground. Even though I could see him clearly (my night vision is very good), I stayed silent. I saw him squat for a while, then crawl towards the edge of the bed. I held back my laughter, waiting for him to cautiously crawl to the side of the bed, peek his head out, and I suddenly pounced on him, scaring him! Haha, laughing wildly!

In my husband's eyes, I am a famous nearsighted person with low IQ. However, sometimes, he falls for my tricks. The other day on the street, in a bustling mall entrance, we got separated, but I quickly spotted him. I saw him anxiously looking behind him. I walked behind him, loudly calling his name. He suddenly turned around, and I acted as if I didn't see him, still shouting loudly and showing how scared and anxious I was. He happily laughed and hugged me, saying "Ah, silly!" Ah, so sweet!

Another memory comes to mind: last night after dinner, while taking a walk in the yard with my husband, we suddenly saw a cockroach on the road. I screamed "Husband, step on it, step on it, kill it!" Then I also stretched my foot to prepare to step on it. My husband said "Ah, it's Xiaqiang, let it go." This made me feel like I was being cruel and lacking compassion.

My husband takes the bus home, but the road was jammed. He sent me a text message telling me to take a detour home. I replied to him saying that if the traffic is blocked, he should just take a nap on the bus. He replied: No! If I dream of you, it'll be scary!

One day, while watching a sports match on TV where the Chinese team lost again, I confidently declared: "In the future, I will let my child practice sports to bring glory to the country!!" My husband looked up from his book and glanced at me, saying: "Then let him practice weightlifting, judging by his mother's build, he can do it!" Wuuu....

One day, my husband and I discussed the dumb topic that everyone talks about: "Would you rather be a man or a woman in the next life?" I thought for a long time and said, "In my next life, I want to be a man, and let you be a woman to serve me!" My husband turned his face and looked at me, saying "In the previous life, you also said the same thing"...

Yesterday, my husband and I were fighting rats at home. My husband was very brave and stepped on the rat to death. I praised his bravery, but he sadly said: "Ah, I remembered watching 'Shuke and Beta' when I was a child, and I feel so sad!"

The first time I cooked for my husband, my skills were really not up to par, and the taste, aroma, and appearance of the dishes were nowhere close. My husband cutely buried his head and ate while comforting me, saying: "Dear, it's okay, just providing me with food and shelter is enough, I don't require prosperity."
