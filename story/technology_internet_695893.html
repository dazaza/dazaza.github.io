by lavender04 on 2012-03-07 17:41:13

I remember in high school, while watching Meteor Garden, Todo Jing Shan Cai said that everyone should have a good pair of shoes, and this pair of good shoes will take you to the best place. Usually when going out, I don't like wearing high heels because they are for special occasions. Instead, I prefer items such as Alexander Wang bags, Tiffany rings, Longchamp backpacks, and Devil novice cards. The chance of me wearing high heels is slim since it causes foot pain. Therefore, here are some personal insights about shoes:

1. Don't simply pursue bad-fitting shoes just because they're trendy. Now I own a pair of shoes that are short-heeled, but my mother says the heel is too short to make an impact, so it needs to be appropriate. Since my feet are thin and long, I prefer strappy shoes.

2. Must buy leather shoes. Daphne shoes or similar ones might not be made of real leather, Gucci sale shoes may wear out quickly and become uncomfortable due to their tightness.

3. They will certainly not be flat. Alexander McQueen shoes, for example, are completely different from flat shoes in terms of comfort and style. Wearing flat shoes can be tiring because the foot position is different compared to wearing heels.

4. A preliminary decision has been made: a pair of flat-soled shoes with peace soles is often worn to go out to buy bread for convenience occasionally.

5. Cloth shoes: Until I heard about old Beijing cloth shoes, I didn’t realize how good they were until one day my father took a pair of old Beijing cloth shoes for a try. They turned out to be very thick and soft, very comfortable. Later, I found that some brands of old Beijing cloth shoes also produce high-heeled models, which feel very comfortable unlike ordinary shoe soles that are hard. It seems there's a layer of cloth in them, so a preliminary decision was made to buy a dual-old Beijing cloth shoes next time.

6. Some night markets offer very nice shoes that are easy to wear. Remember, if they may cause foot skin damage, do not listen to the boss’ assurance. Heels and fronts might not align well with Mom’s preferences, causing the foot to slip forward, especially sandals, making it look ugly.

That’s all for now.
