Beijing hair transplant hospital, burn hair transplant, how is the effect of hair transplant?

by yonghe2013 on 2012-03-06 14:46:06

Hair transplantation is one of the ways for every balding patient to solve their troubles nowadays, and it is also an ideal choice for many beauty enthusiasts. Although there are people around us who have undergone the surgery, it is after all only a small proportion of the population. Many balding patients still question the effectiveness of hair transplant surgery and how to judge the quality of such surgeries. Regarding the issue of the effectiveness of hair transplant surgery raised by hair friends, doctors say that we need to look at three aspects to determine the quality of the surgery: hair density, naturalness of hair, and the direction of hair growth.

First, let's judge the effectiveness of hair transplantation from the perspective of hair density. Many people choose hair transplantation to solve the problem of hair density, aiming to evenly distribute the hair and achieve a naturally dense effect. Therefore, a basic requirement of good hair transplantation results is reasonable planting density.

Secondly, we can judge the quality of hair transplantation by the naturalness of the hair. The naturalness of the hair refers to whether the new hair grown after the transplantation surgery looks the same as the original hair, whether it appears natural, and whether it blends seamlessly so that others cannot tell that the patient has undergone hair transplantation. If such an effect can be achieved, it indicates that the hair transplantation surgery was very successful.

Finally, we can judge the effectiveness of hair transplantation based on the direction of hair growth in Beijing. Hair transplantation is not just a simple technical issue; the doctor also needs to consider the patient's needs and ensure that the new hair grown after transplantation has the same growth direction as the original hair. Only in this way can the new hair blend with the existing hair to achieve a perfect result.
