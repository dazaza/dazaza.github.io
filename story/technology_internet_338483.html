According to the British Sun and Daily Express reports on the 29th, 74-year-old Gary Box from Louth, Lincolnshire in the UK not only married a wife 34 years younger than him but also made her pregnant. On March 12, his wife Dawn underwent a cesarean section and gave birth to their second child. Gary, at 74, became a father again and thus entered the Guinness World Records as the oldest "newborn father" in the UK.

When Gary and his wife Dawn went out for a walk, they were often mistaken by strangers as a father and daughter pair because Dawn is only 40 years old, 34 years younger than Gary. Gary and Dawn knew each other since 1994 when they lived in the same community. Due to health issues, Dawn rarely went out on dates with boys her age. Gary, who has three adult children from his previous marriage, was very concerned about Dawn's health as a neighbor and often showed her care and warmth. About six years ago, Dawn realized that she had fallen deeply in love with Gary and boldly expressed her feelings to him.

In 2002, Gary, then 66, married Dawn, who was 32 at the time. Not long after, Dawn gave birth to their first child - their now 6-year-old Daniel.

Last year, Dawn was delighted to find out that she was pregnant again. When Gary accompanied Dawn to the hospital for prenatal checkups, all the doctors mistakenly assumed that Gary was Dawn's father rather than the father of the baby in her womb. However, Gary didn't mind at all. On March 12, Dawn underwent a cesarean section and gave birth to her and Gary's second son - Ryan, who weighed 2.45 kilograms. Ryan faced some breathing problems at birth, but he eventually recovered after receiving special care in the neonatal intensive care unit.

The birth of Ryan allowed 74-year-old Gary to enter the Guinness World Records and become the oldest newborn father in the UK. In an interview, Gary excitedly said: "I am extremely proud. I feel like the happiest man in the world. I think we won't have more children, but who knows? People may talk about my age, but I will strive to stay healthy so that I have enough energy to take care of the children."

Gary said: "I still feel young at heart. I never smoke or drink alcohol. Even though I'm 74, I can do anything that young people can do."

Gary also attributed his good health to eating bananas regularly. Gary said: "I eat bananas every day and don't like salty food. I believe this dietary habit keeps me healthy. Also, love is one of the reasons that keep me young."

According to reports, the world's oldest newborn father is Naru Ram Chaugule, a 90-year-old man from Rajasthan, India, who in 2007 still managed to make his current wife Sabli pregnant, giving him another child. Chaugule claimed he was confident of living to be 100 and wanted to have more children with his wife.
