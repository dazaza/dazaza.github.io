by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-02-18 20:39:23

Why do we part?

There will be separation when there is love. Among the men and women in this world, who has not been entangled in the纠葛of love and hate? Sometimes we are nourished by love, and sometimes we are also destroyed by it. Those who love you may not necessarily bring you happiness, and those you don't love may not be bad either. Love is like a mirror - to the benevolent it appears benevolent, to the wise it appears wise. Whether you love or leave, we have no right to make the other person cry. The hurt that exists in love is actually due to our ignorance and shallowness.

In the realm of love, there should only be two voices: love or not love. But because we often have selfishness in love, we cannot be magnanimous when we don't love, and some loves are given to the wrong people at the wrong time, which has already predetermined a sad ending from the very beginning. Thus, "broken-hearted people" can no longer find space in the horizon, and tears of departure are everywhere around us. Separation is always painful. Even if there is no love, it is still heavy when you turn away. What we cannot bear to part with is the deep affection we once gave, or the time wasted for it. As for that person, they have already become unimportant at the moment of decision to leave. Otherwise, why bother to separate? If you love, don't talk about leaving; if you leave, don't talk about love anymore.

The parasol tree, the drizzle of autumn rain, leaf by leaf, sound by sound, drops on the empty steps until dawn. What kind of sadness is this? Pain is the shuffling figure under the tree, the desolate helplessness in the rain, and the sleepless tossing and turning at night. However, in love and marriage, some pain comes from our own pickiness and pride, suspicion and vanity. Solving such pain with breakups and divorces will only add more pain. Do not misinterpret pain and cover up your own weakness with unnecessary moans. Separation is used to end pain, not to torture each other. If you still look back frequently after separation or grieve every day after divorce, then your departure was either rash or naive. Some pain is prolonged by ourselves because you live in regret and unwillingness.

Some separations come with regrets, perhaps because we didn't love well enough when we could, or because there were words left unsaid when we parted, leaving regrets in the days to come. Then cherish the person in front of you. What is more important than the present life? No matter how beautiful the memories are, they have already passed. True understanding is to hold on to the hand in front of you. Regret, a little is沧桑, too much is the sadness of our existence. Even if we don't become butterflies flying over the sea, we should still hibernate in this winter and give birth to the hope of spring.

Why do we part? It's just that this love has walked away, and we did not retain it. When leaving, cry if you want, then watch the dawn light gradually drive away the darkness. Another love will still smile comfortably under the sunshine. Pain only proves that we once loved so sincerely, it is not fragility or failure.
