Snipd: Internet scissors hands

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-19 08:41:41

People who visit the Internet often will find many good resources and content on the website. Then there is the urge to save them all. So we need to have a unified place to manage these pieces of content. If it exists in the local hard disk, it is necessary to manually classify and organize the relationship of the content, and the hard disk becomes messy and inconvenient to carry around for a long time. So JarryJarry recommends a new tool for everyone: Snipd: Internet scissor hands.

Snipd is a browser button plugin. There is no need to add a file plugin to your browser. Snipd supports common browsers such as Firefox and IE. If you're using Firefox, drag and drop the Snip It button onto the Bookmarks toolbar. If you use Internet Explorer, add Snip It to your favorites. Detailed installation instructions are available on Snipd's website. Registered Snipd after, you will get an exclusive directory, such as: http://www.snipd.com/xtit/. In this way, you can easily share the content of your collection with your friends, and it is also convenient to visit the treasures of your friends' collection. It has the characteristics of SNS.

Now, you can start to bookmark the content of the web. When you go to a website, there are your favorite pictures, videos, texts and other favorite content, use Snipd to cut it out and save it in your treasure box.

For example, if you go to Sweet IT.Net, see some pictures you like, click the Snip It button from the bookmark toolbar or favorites, and a Snipd Ajax favorites bar will emerge. At this time, your mouse hover over any picture, the scissors logo will appear. Click on this logo to save this content to Snipd. If it is text content, you just need to drag and select the text, and the text is favorites! Very convenient and simple.

In your collection box, all the contents of your collection are classified according to the source website and content category, which is very easy to organize and summarize later. Snipd is like the Internet scissors hands, for you to surf the net to add a little fun!
