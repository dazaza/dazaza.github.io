[Website Building Exchange] Ten Essential Blogs for External Links

1. Baidu Space: Everyone can do without it, but not Baidu Space. The exact reasons are well known to all.

2. Tianya Blog: This blog has high authority, and Google highly trusts it. Its Q&A section on Tianya also performs exceptionally well.

3. NetEase Blog: I've always been very interested in NetEase because many of my blog external links have been indexed quickly through NetEase.

4. Sina Blog: As everyone knows, Baidu values Sina a lot, so this blog must not be neglected.

5. Sohu Blog: To be honest, I originally planned to give up on Sohu Blog, but recently Sohu Blog started accepting spider crawls again, and the effect is still very good.

6. Phoenix Blog: This blog's external link resources are quite valuable, but its management is strict, and excessive advertising external links are not allowed.

7. Windows Live: For new sites to be indexed by Google, it's often due to this. My new site wasn't indexed for several days, but after being stimulated by it, it was indexed within 2 hours.

8. Hexun Blog: This blog has helped me a lot in the past. When other blogs weren't being indexed, Hexun provided a lot of support.

9. Wangshang Blog (Alibaba Blog): This blog is extremely favored by Google, and your articles get indexed by Google very quickly, usually within a few minutes.

10. China Gold Online: This is a finance-related blog. If your article is outstanding, Google News will feature it. One of my articles once generated traffic from Google News here, significantly improving its ranking.
