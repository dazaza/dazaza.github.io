The most classic summary of love

by 9966683269 on 2007-06-05 19:33:32

To all friends,

Regardless of whether you are married or unmarried,

This is a very good article.

If you don't have time to savor it right now,

please take the time later to calm your heart,

and read it with dedication.

There aren't many good articles anymore...

Being single isn't always being elite. Being single might offer more freedom,

but freedom has a synonym, called loneliness. Because people don't always like being alone;

sometimes we need to share good things with others,

sometimes we need comfort when we're sad. The reason for the rise of the "single elite"

is due to economic independence, personal independence, and emotional independence. What is independence?

Independence means needing without depending,

an independent person needs the opposite sex but does not depend on them.

Before becoming lovers, she should first be a friend. After she becomes your friend and enters your life,

she then has the possibility to know you, understand you, recognize your strengths,

develop feelings for you, and further develop a relationship,

becoming your lover, your partner. The world's colors are not just white and black,

there is a long gray area between black and white. As long as you spend more time together,

you can discover each other's merits, develop feelings,

this is the natural process of developing emotions.

"Love at first sight" and "faithful love till death" are unrealistic,

what we need is not this kind of impractical and illusory emotion. Some describe relationships with the opposite sex

as picking stones by the seaside; everyone picks the one they like. Once you pick the stone you like the most,

take it home and treat it well because it's your only stone.

And remember, from then on, don't go back to the beach.

Always believe,

I've already found the biggest, most beautiful, and most suitable stone for me.

The most important thing in a relationship isn't how good he is,

but how good he is to you. A person may have excellent conditions, scoring 100 points,

but out of these 100 points, he only gives you 30-40 points, or even 10-20 points;

on the contrary, another person may only have 70-80 points,

but he treats you wholeheartedly,

so which one should you choose? In fact, everyone's conditions are the same.

No matter how good you are, there will always be someone better than you.

Although you may not be able to be the "best person",

you can certainly be the "best person for the other party". Every boy can say:

"Although I'm not the best man in the world, I am the best man in the world for you."

Likewise for girls, this is something everyone can achieve.

The most important thing in a relationship is how good he is to you,

not how good he himself is.

But if someone is already very good,

and also sincerely loves you,

then you can really entrust your life to him.

Nowadays, the only condition women should consider for marriage

should be whether you love him, whether he loves you,

whether he is sincere towards you,

whether you feel pressured or happy with him,

rather than what he has!

True love in this world is rare. In one's lifetime,

it is hard to find someone

you truly love and can spend your entire life with.

If you are afraid to express yourself,

or fear that something might happen,

and miss the possibly once-in-a-lifetime true love,

that would be too regrettable,

so you must take the initiative and speak your mind.

If a boy looks down on a girl just because she took the initiative,

then that boy is not a man, but an animal. Moreover, happiness is more important than face,

if sacrificing a little face can bring lifelong happiness,

it is absolutely worth it. Be brave and speak your heart,

don't hide your true feelings.

Never say that destiny hasn't arrived, in fact, destiny is everywhere,

but it is fleeting. If "destiny" is not seized in time,

then there will be no "fate". Most women value spirit in relationships,

while men tend to value material aspects. Besides being attentive and considerate to girls,

boys also need to learn to take responsibility for girls,

to direct all their attentiveness and consideration for all girls

towards one girl.

Also,

being silent and rigid cannot win a girl's favor,

so learn to sweet-talk and compliment girls often.

Men love for sex; women have sex for love.

A relationship that is easy and pleasant to maintain tends to last longer! A relationship that is difficult and painful to maintain is unlikely to last long,

at which point a choice should be made. We are all ordinary mortals,

seeking simple, happy, and loving relationships. For all relationships, the process is far more important than the result. Why?

Because all relationships are without results.

What is the result? Marriage? Does marriage mean living happily ever after?

Clearly, we do not judge the value of a relationship by its outcome,

nor by its duration.

For relationships, whatever has happened exists,

and whatever has existed has value. Every moment of every relationship in the world

is worth cherishing. Marriage is the biggest gamble in life.

In this infinite amount of time,

we still have to show our ugliest side to each other.

Marriage differs from romance: romance allows spending two hours getting dressed up,

energetically doting on and being considerate to the other, easily completing the task.

But marriage cannot always remain in such a high state. Therefore, marriage is a big gamble,

requiring full preparation, detailed planning, and complete faith,

and then taking the risk. Even so, there's still a chance of losing. Thus, if before gambling, you know you

aren't willing, don't love him deeply, and don't want to spend your life with him,

then this gamble is bound to fail.

Absolutely do not marry for any reason other than love.

The value of a relationship doesn't lie in what you can gain from the other person,

but partly in the feeling of being depended on and needed. Someone depends on me, needs me,

and I will feel fulfilled.

Facing relationships, we should adopt a three-no policy: First, don't rush: don't hurry into marriage.

Marriage is a wonderful thing, but don't rush, what's yours will be yours. Second, don't be afraid: don't fear giving.

Both parties must work their entire lives to maintain a good relationship. You can't 'get' a good relationship,

you can only find someone you love,

and together cultivate, create, and complete a good relationship.

If you don't have the resolve to be a good man, then you won't get a good woman.

There is no bad man who has a good woman,

a bad man only has a pitiful woman; similarly, there is no bad woman who has a good man.

Moreover, in a relationship, there is someone else working alongside us,

trying to make it better. The success of this partnership is highly likely,

the key lies in whether you are willing to put in effort and give.

Third, don't give up. When you suffer setbacks, frustrations, injuries,

when you feel disheartened and disappointed,

there is someone who unconditionally and forever stands by your side,

supports you, encourages you, comforts you,

allowing you to stand up again and face the world. This strength,

is something even the closest parents cannot give us,

something even the best friends cannot give us,

only the beloved partner found in life's journey can give us.

Actually, love is the only truly precious thing in life;

also the only truly worth pursuing thing. As long as you have someone you love,

you have the motivation,

you can face the whole world.
