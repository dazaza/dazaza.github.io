Memory is for some people, recollection is for sometimes.

Perhaps it was a bit deliberate, deliberately avoiding those moments that should have been commemorated with sorrowful tears, deliberately refraining from recalling those days when even breathing turned into suffocation. I apologize that these more than five hundred days and nights could not wash away my regret and bitterness, nor could they build the strongest wall in my heart. In the face of certain pain, I am still just a failed deserter, helplessly watching my courage collapse before reality, yet not forgetting to turn indifference into the strongest weapon.

I don't know if it's you I can't forgive, or myself. There are still so many unspoken words at the corner of my mouth, how could you bear to leave without a trace of reluctance? Once, I proudly said you were the sun that gave me warmth and daylight, but never expected it would turn into a prophecy, ultimately becoming a distant sun indeed. How regrettable, the sun will never know that without it, even the most splendid world would lose its color.

The other day, while tidying up things at home, I suddenly came across old photos from childhood. The two kids in the photos were laughing uncontrollably without a care in the world, making one involuntarily smile along. But as I laughed, a tear fell onto the smiling faces of the past. I think I must be an unqualified younger sister, because I didn't manage to see you one last time in your final moment. If every childhood protected by an older brother is a happy one, then I was no exception in my happiness, thinking it would last forever. You told me not to be afraid, that you would protect me. After you left, I grew up a lot, seemingly deciding one morning to start being sensible, without any process, like a innate talent, they all said I've matured.

There are always such people in life that even if all memories about them are wiped clean, there will still be pain quietly surging at some unexpected moment. A friend said, if the burdens we carry are too heavy, the road ahead will only become increasingly tiring. I probably lack the firm mindset to forget, nor do I have the most serene smile to face it, so I could only choose the most awkward way, forcing myself to deliberately ignore the hardest times, fantasizing that we've just been apart for a long time.

Big Brother, I've already forgiven the person who destroyed you with their own hands, I'm no longer the child waiting to be protected. And, I truly miss you, but unfortunately, heaven gives me no grace for you to hear. So, let's put them together with tears in the sorrowful past, and wait for time to dry everything.

If it's farewell, after seasons pass, let these words express my feelings, let this article convey my thoughts, wishing you peace and well-being.
