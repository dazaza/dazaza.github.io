The "good girl" me fell in love with a married man

by lk0lifea on 2009-07-21 23:50:13

Keywords: Love, married man

He said I was a good girl. In April, when the spring scenery was just right, Qin Zheng and I were in a dimly lit private room at a coffee shop. He tightly embraced me and gently kissed my forehead, "Sigh, you're really a good girl." And indeed, I am very well-behaved. After graduating from university, I came to this advertising company to work as an ordinary clerk. My life is extremely regular: go home after work, eat, watch TV, and rest; I never go out to play. I don't even touch entertainment venues. I only go shopping with my sisters or stay at home with Lei.

Lei is my boyfriend who feels like a chicken rib - not feeling happy when possessing him, but also feeling a bit regretful when losing him. We've been together for four years. He treats me well, so well that he places the foot-washing water in front of me before sleeping. Buying groceries and cooking, he does it all by himself. What reasons do I have to be dissatisfied? We plan to get married next year. Although there's no passion, neither of us wants to go through any more troubles.

We live together, but we haven't crossed the line. Friends can't believe that after two years of living together, a single man and woman haven't had a relationship! However, that's the truth. My traditional thoughts and strict education from my parents since childhood prevent me from accepting cohabitation before marriage. So, when Lei's kisses and caresses come around, I persist... even feeling uncomfortable and uneasy.

I admit, Lei is a good marriage prospect. But he's not the one I like. The man I like requires me to look up to him with admiration, having respect and passion for him. He should be talented, have a broad mind, mature and steady with a big man's demeanor. I will always feel under his spiritual guidance that there's nothing difficult in life.

Such a man exists beside me, and he is Qin Zheng.

Willing to be your confidante

In the entire advertising company, every colleague admires Qin Zheng's talent, especially me. His slim figure, tall stature, taciturn and cool appearance.

Qin Zheng and I often deal with each other due to work. Once during a planning activity, I was working overtime alone in a mess. He walked over, bent down, frowned and stared at the computer. A few minutes later, he gave me an idea that greatly helped me. The mature charm he exuded almost made me dizzy.

After a decision-making meeting, I was promoted by the boss to be his personal secretary. Everyone was clamoring for me to treat them, "To get such an important position, you must have met a benefactor!" I also wondered inside, this favor made me uneasy. It can be confirmed that Qin Zheng attended that meeting, and his opinion was very important.

I asked him on QQ why he promoted me? He sent a smiling face, "If you treat me to dinner, I'll tell you."

That meal, we enjoyed it very much. I never knew he could be so witty and talkative, graceful and charming. This man is simply too perfect.

Later in QQ chats, Qin Zheng sometimes sent pictures of roses or affectionately teased me. "I like you, I want to be with you." He semi-probed and sent it over. "Really?" "When the false becomes true, the true also becomes false." "Then, I take it seriously?" I couldn't help but take it seriously, I've always had a good impression of him.

Qin Zheng still sent a warm smile, does this count as a hint? Everyone in the company knows that he is already married, and his wife works in Yichang.

I consoled myself, I haven't done anything with him, I just have a good impression of him, this impression won't deteriorate into anything else.

We met again at the restaurant. With the glittering lights, our gazes were particularly gentle towards each other. But I declared first, "I just want to be friends with you, closer than ordinary friends, confidants. But you have to promise me not to do anything out of bounds, okay?" When Qin Zheng heard me finish telling him about my pure love with Lei, he was amazed and sighed, "Oh, you're really a good girl. I won't hurt you, please believe I'm a gentleman."

The game rules have already been broken

Qin Zheng is deliberately creating our romantic moments, although these moments are brief and hidden. He, who has always been serious and unsmiling, actually...
