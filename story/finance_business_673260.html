According to the report of Business World by Voice of Economy, Lin Shuhao who is now red-hot in NBA is becoming the next commercial king in the minds of merchants. The latest news is that Chinese sport brands such as Peak and Li Ning have formed a team to go to America to sign a contract with Lin Shuhao at any time. It is estimated by professionals that the price of Lin Shuhao's current endorsement contract can exceed 4 million US dollars and his total value has reached 20 million US dollars, ranking fifth among all NBA stars. Lin Shuhao who studied economics at Harvard University also understands very well how to protect his own commercial value. After paying 1625 US dollars, Lin Shuhao has applied for the trademark right of "Lin-sanity". The right covers the use right of various clothing as well as activity dolls, drinks and backpacks etc.. It is said that Lin Shuhao submitted an application for the trademark right of "Lin-sanity" on February 13 local time in the United States. Before Lin Shuhao's application, two Californians had applied for the trademark right of "Lin-sanity". However, a local lawyer in Washington indicated that the actions of the two Californians would not work and were just a waste of time and money. But, the issue surrounding Lin Shuhao's "domain name" is far from over. In China, the competition for Lin Shuhao's domain name has become fierce. Some netizens found that the domain name of the pinyin of Lin Shuhao www.linshuhao.com has been registered in advance and was auctioned online. The auction price has exceeded 260 thousand yuan. So, who has the foresight to register the domain name of the Chinese pinyin of Lin Shuhao? According to the information provided online, the reporter logged onto the website www.linshuhao.com and found that there was no specific website information on the page. There were some advertisements around the page and an introduction about Lin Shuhao in the middle position. It was written on it: according to Forbes website reports, "Lin Shuhao" has become the brand name whose value increases fastest in the world today and its value is about 14 million US dollars. Forbes also said that this "brand value" of Lin Shuhao was the same as Kobe Bryant of Lakers Team and ranked sixth highest among all athletes in the world. There was a selling advertisement below the introduction. It was written on it: the domain name you are visiting can be transferred! And the contact email box, QQ number and mobile phone number were marked. Following the telephone number left on the website, the reporter called the telephone and expressed the intention to purchase the domain name and made an inquiry about the domain name matter. The person who answered the telephone was a man from Guangdong. He told the reporter that he surnamed Liu and was a software engineer and the reason why he registered the domain name was related to his interest. Mr. Liu: This industry has developed for more than ten years and I am considered as a latecomer. Many predecessors in this aspect have already sold their domain names at high prices while we only eat a little porridge. This mysterious Mr. Liu also told the reporter that many people consulted the matter of the domain name every day and the prices offered were from several hundred thousand yuan to over one million yuan but they only asked without buying. Mr. Liu was also waiting for a good offer to sell the domain name. As for the specific price, Mr. Liu did not want to talk much about it. He only told the reporter that the price of Yi Jianlian's domain name could be referred to. Regarding various doubts online, Mr. Liu also made a response. He told me that the phenomenon of selling domain names was already very common abroad and he only did the domain name business in his spare time and was not professionally engaged in it. If the price was appropriate, he would sell it; otherwise, he could keep it for fun. Yi Jianlian's domain name was sold for 168 thousand US dollars.
