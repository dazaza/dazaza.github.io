Lying requires skills and talent, which some people just can't achieve. Among the 12 zodiac signs, who can lie without batting an eye and turn lies into truths? Below, we will use our keen eyesight to help you identify one by one those zodiac signs whose truths and lies are hard to distinguish.

**Top 1 for Lying: Scorpio**

Mysterious and enigmatic Scorpios can easily see through others' thoughts; it's impossible to deceive them. However, they are super liars themselves. Once a Scorpio decides not to tell the truth, it’s harder than climbing to the heavens to figure out what they're thinking. Since they lack security, as soon as your questions slightly touch on their privacy, they will instantly erect a protective barrier and use all sorts of rumors to placate you. Moreover, their lies sound truer than the truth, with no flaws for you to exploit. Don’t probe into their secrets or try to harm them, otherwise, you'll never hear their true words!

**Top 2 for Lying: Gemini**

Geminis are smooth talkers, speaking more melodiously than singing. As soon as they open their mouths, they can charm you. At first, it will be impossible for you to differentiate between what is true and what is false, but as they keep talking, they often find it difficult to keep their stories consistent, and their lies eventually fall apart. Although their lies are quickly uncovered, they still enjoy lying, continuing non-stop to "lie" until the end. However, these are usually harmless lies, without malice or intention to hurt you. You can simply go along with them and laugh it off.

**Top 3 for Lying: Sagittarius**

Sagittarius belongs to the optimistic type, always smiling and cracking jokes. They are also top-notch liars. When they weave stories, they do so vividly, captivating you without you even realizing it. They will make you nod repeatedly, praising their tales. This is their common method of lying - to enhance the atmosphere and create laughter by describing things in such a way that makes you willingly "fall for it." Although they love to lie and joke around, they are rare humorous geniuses and worth befriending.

**Top 4 for Lying: Aquarius**

Aquarians are changeable, quirky, and love sudden bursts of creativity, making them great storytellers. Their speech is often vague, saying half while hiding the other half, leaving people confused. Watching you get anxious and impatient gives them a sense of satisfaction, almost as if they’re deliberately challenging your intelligence and patience. In reality, they have no intention to deceive but simply want to build suspense and use lying to spark your curiosity, playing hide-and-seek with you to show off their wit.

**Top 5 for Lying: Libra**

Libras possess a charming allure, drawing attention with every word and action. With their pleasant demeanor, how could they make this list? It's because they love to engage in grand discussions, often unintentionally lying to liven up the atmosphere. "Lying" is also one of their social tactics. To enliven the mood and stimulate the emotions of those around them, exaggerating slightly has become second nature to them. Although they enjoy telling small lies, they are harmless and beyond reproach.
