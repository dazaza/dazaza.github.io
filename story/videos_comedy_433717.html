2. When Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei first started their quest for power, they needed to recruit soldiers and buy horses. They bought many horses and built a large cage to keep them inside. The next day, they discovered that all the horses had escaped from the cage, so they decided to increase the height of the cage from 5 feet to 10 feet. The following day, they again found all the horses had escaped, so they decided to make the cage 20 feet high. Then, the three of them went back to sleep. At this time, one horse asked another horse: "Do you think these idiots will still make the cage higher?" The other horse replied: "It's hard to say! If they continue to forget to close the gate..."

3. It is said that Cao Cao and Liu Bei were discussing heroes over wine. While eating and drinking and talking, a soldier serving the dishes came up, and Liu Bei noticed his thumb in the dish, so he angrily said: "What the hell are you doing?!" The soldier quickly explained: "My thumb is injured, and the military doctor said it needs to be kept warm." Liu Bei scolded again: "If it needs warmth, why don't you put your thumb in your ass?!" The soldier replied: "When I'm not serving the dishes, my thumb is in my ass."

4. It is said that Zhang Fei got married. Zhang Fei was a bit foolish and didn't understand how to have sex, so he went to ask Zhuge Liang. Zhuge Liang thought: "If I explain too deeply, he won't understand, and if I explain too simply, it'll seem too vulgar. What should I do?" At this moment, two horses were mating by the river, and Zhuge Liang suddenly had an idea. He said to Zhang Fei: "Do you see those two horses? Do you see what they're doing? Zhuge Liang asked: "But what?" Zhang Fei said: "It's just that sometimes it's too cold by the river, and there are people watching."
