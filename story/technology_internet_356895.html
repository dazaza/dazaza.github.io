"I am a teacher at school, also a rural teacher, my economic condition is still okay, but when Sharp Brother returned home, I had a car accident myself, spent a lot of money on treatment, and borrowed a big vehicle from a friend." At 9:30 PM last night, a plane flying from Nanchang to Guangzhou slowly descended at Baiyun Airport. The passengers arriving were none other than the internet celebrity 'Sharp Brother' Cheng Bangrong and his family. This Sharp Brother's family was invited by the Country Garden Farm, later to serve as the manager of the costume acting department. Regarding this new appointment, Teacher Cheng, who accompanied Sharp Brother, also expressed: "We know that netizens care a lot about 'Sharp Brother', and we also predict that Country Garden Farm inviting him to serve as a manager is for promotion, but for our family, the most important thing is to see him become self-reliant and reintegrate into society, which is the result we all want to see." According to Teacher Cheng's introduction, Sharp Brother's current economic situation is still very difficult. After returning home, he received some help, but the money was basically spent on repaying debts, medical treatment, and the living expenses of the whole family. His brother works in the countryside, his income barely maintains his own family's livelihood, and cannot provide much assistance to his younger brother. Uncle Cheng Housheng frankly admitted that what impressed Country Garden Farm was Sharp Brother's sincerity. Sharp Brother is not afraid of netizens misunderstanding him for fame "to make money through hype," "For Sharp Brother, earning money with his own ability and finding his own life value is very important, so our family fully supports him, and hopes netizens will understand."
