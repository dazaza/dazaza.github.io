The annual salary of the "new lineup" exceeds 40 million yuan, Tie Ning: as the chairman, I am humbled. Martin's agent Andy Miller has already expressed to the media that joining a CBA team is a great opportunity for Martin to maintain his physique and keep a fresh feeling towards basketball. It also provides him with a brand-new chance to expand his fame globally. Not only did Martin's agent confirm that Martin will join the CBA league next season, but he himself finally came out and confirmed on his personal Weibo that he would join Xinjiang: "Yes, the news that I'm going to China to play a season is true. After the CBA league ends, I will return to the NBA."

In order to renew Doby's contract, it is said that the Xinjiang team increased Doby's annual salary from $1.35 million to $2 million. This time they were able to convince Martin to play in the CBA because they offered him an astronomical post-tax annual salary of $2.65 million. If you consider the taxes in the US and the length of the season, this CBA contract is equivalent to an NBA contract worth $7 million annually.

As for Meng Duo, the "free agent" signed by新世纪 (New Century), rumors suggest his annual salary is between 1.5 million and 2 million yuan. Last season, veteran Zhao Yonggang, who played for the Foshan team, will receive an annual salary of 900,000 yuan from the Xinjiang team.

After Martin arrives, along with the previously confirmed renewal of Doby, and domestic players Tang Zhengdong, Meng Duo, Zhao Yonggang, etc., the Xinjiang team's strength in inside and outside lines has caught up with the reigning champion Guangdong Southern Tigers. The combination of Meng Duo and Doby at the guard position would make most CBA teams step back. Zhao Yonggang will stabilize Xinjiang's external firepower. Tang Zhengdong is also working hard to integrate into the Xinjiang team. After experiencing last season's loss of the championship despite having superior strength, the Xinjiang team did not retreat but instead increased their investment. Faced with such a luxurious lineup, can the reigning champion Southern Tigers still "HOLD" on?

Last season, foreign player Doby represented the Xinjiang team in 43 games, averaging 35.7 minutes per game and scoring 32 points, grabbing 4.2 rebounds, making 4.6 assists, and stealing the ball 3.7 times per game, making him the best-performing foreign player in the CBA last season.

Chinese football has gained much attention due to Evergrande, and now Chinese basketball has also welcomed a sponsor willing to spend lavishly — the Xinjiang Guanghui team, which will not stop until they win the championship. This summer, they have already spent over 40 million yuan on signing players and coaches.

Even more surprisingly, Xinjiang has even poached from Yao Ming's Shanghai team. It is widely reported that Xinjiang offered head coach Deng Huaide, who coached Shanghai last season and is currently leading the Chinese men's basketball team to compete for the London Olympics, an annual salary of $1 million.

At the age of 34, Martin was the absolute mainstay of the Nuggets' interior last season. Affected by injuries, he only played 48 games for the team last season, but started every game, averaging 8.6 points, 6.2 rebounds, and 2.3 assists per game. Martin was the No. 1 pick in 2000, and during his 11 years in the NBA, he has played in 654 games, with average stats of 13.5 points, 7.2 rebounds, 2.1 assists, and 1.2 blocks per game. This time, Martin joining Xinjiang Guanghui broke the previous record set by J.R. Smith, making him the highest-paid foreign player in CBA history.

In terms of local players, Xinjiang also made every effort to poach talent. It is understood that Tang Zhengdong signed a "3+2" contract with Xinjiang, with a five-year annual salary contract worth 18 million yuan.

According to American sports expert Adrian Wojnarowski, the Xinjiang Guanghui, who are spending lavishly for the CBA championship, offered Martin a one-year post-tax salary of $2.65 million. The Denver Post also stated that Martin's contract does not include a "tyrannical clause" that would terminate the contract if the NBA lockout ends, meaning Martin will play a full CBA season with Xinjiang. Additionally, Xinjiang will pay a salary to Martin's personal business assistant, who will accompany Martin to China.

Until now, the former NBA No. 1 draft pick has never tasted the joy of winning a championship. In the year he came closest to a championship, Martin faced the peak-period Duncan and the extremely stable Spurs. However, after joining Xinjiang, Martin may very well lift the championship trophy in a foreign land. With the addition of power forward Kenyon Martin, Xinjiang's internal and external recruitment is basically complete. Martin's arrival signifies that Xinjiang has found the last piece of the puzzle in their quest for another championship challenge.

Just from the data Martin produced in his 11 seasons in the NBA, he can already be considered the top power forward in the CBA next season. Moreover, Martin is physically strong and has formidable inside strength. Even if Battier and Tang Zhengdong fail to perform up to expectations, Martin absolutely has the ability to lead the Buyulan brothers and carve out a sky for Xinjiang's inside game.
