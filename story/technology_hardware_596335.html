Introduction to Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring Scenic Area

Facing the vast Taihu Lake, surrounded by lush green forests, basking in abundant sunshine and oxygen, the Lingshan Yuan Yi Lixing Hot Spring offers rich natural mineral water treatments. Its unique South Asian themed architecture and meticulous service concepts cater to guests seeking luxurious and romantic vacation experiences. The hot spring water originates from deep within the earth's crustal fissures, evolving over decades through underground thermal magma before surfacing. The well bottom temperature reaches 70°C, with the wellhead at 50°C. The water has a pH of 7.49, containing abundant minerals, trace elements, and special chemical components. Not only effective for treating various diseases but also beneficial for health maintenance, beauty, skincare, and recuperation, relieving pain and fatigue, rejuvenating youthful vitality.
