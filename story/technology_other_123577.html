Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:40:57

Integration of work and study, part-time work for tuition waiver, the province's first-class practical training facilities, simple, fast, and accurate teaching mode, fully enclosed quasi-military student management, complete employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and filed with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and a beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development philosophy of "liberating thinking, being pragmatic and innovative, and striving for change," continuously carrying out teaching and management reforms, firmly establishing the "market competition" awareness, taking the market as the guide, and taking enterprise needs as the standard, exploring new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich teaching and management experience, and students have received widespread praise from employers after employment.

1. Taking enterprise standards as the guide, we have equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the province.

Adequate and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the foundation and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, building a numerical control (NC) practice workshop equipped with over 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines, etc., and an ordinary lathe practice workshop. The school has also built communication electronics laboratories, piano rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shape rooms, and new teaching buildings, among other supporting facilities.

2. Taking employment positions as the guide, we have created an enterprise-oriented teaching model.

Establishing a practice-based teaching model, during the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students take on roles such as team leaders, line supervisors, and employees in their "work." They are evaluated through regular assessments and exams, and outstanding team leaders and technical experts are selected from the students. This effectively promotes students' enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each day, students' "work" situations are monitored by quality indicators, which are tallied monthly. Based on the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, composed of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonuses, etc. This effectively stimulates students' initiative in learning and competitive awareness, allowing them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned more quickly and better.

Through enterprise-oriented teaching reforms, students can gradually experience and adapt to enterprise management models during their schooling, meeting the needs of employment positions and preliminarily reaching enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-oriented teaching is to gradually surpass enterprise employment standards.

3. Taking enterprise demands as the guide, we have created a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students and formulated a daily standard for students and a manual for practical career quality training.

Comprehensively learning from enterprise employee management models, cultivating qualified talents welcomed by enterprises. Enterprises need not only skilled workers but also emphasize employees' overall qualities, such as teamwork, obedience, execution, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall qualities require efforts and collaboration from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs throughout the students' entire learning process. To enhance our students' overall qualities, our school mainly highlights two distinctive management features:

Distinctive Management Feature One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a responsible adult before becoming talented," hiring retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from daily life systems, from dressing appropriately, behavior, manners, and tidying up quarters, students develop good living habits, cultivate excellent organizational discipline, and quickly become disciplined individuals with a good soldier-like demeanor.

Distinctive Management Feature Two: Career Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, career quality courses are offered, divided into theoretical lectures and expansion practices. These two aspects complement each other and improve mutually. Theoretical lectures on career quality focus primarily on practical applications, guiding students in ideological transformation, while expansion training consolidates teaching results through students' "personal experiences," making these career quality requirements the students' voluntary behaviors.

Career quality expansion training involves signing agreements with enterprises, sending our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students understand society and enterprises before actual internships, entering the formal enterprise employee state early, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

4. Taking employment as the guide, we have successfully completed employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' qualities and employment channels, we have widely established student internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi area, and the Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's numerical control specialty mainly focuses on the automobile parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are mechanical manufacturing enterprises ranked among the world's top 500. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

5. Thoughtful后勤services, uniquely creating the "free meal system" for enrolled students in the province.

All enrolled students in our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus based on students' physical development needs, fully ensuring the nutritional needs for students' physical development and adapting to intensive studies, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by dining outside the school.

The school's student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, phone booth, and bathhouse service facilities are complete. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service," striving to provide proactive, thoughtful, considerate, and convenient services.

6. Only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition and accommodation fees for three years.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school, with a strong sense of social responsibility originating from society and giving back to society. Relying on powerful educational strength and forward-thinking educational concepts, we aim to save costs for students' parents to the maximum extent possible. That is, students coming to our school only need to pay miscellaneous fees for three years. The tuition and accommodation fees for three years are paid through the integration of work and study, i.e., through career quality training. The school has also specially established the "Hongzhi Class," providing full or partial exemptions of all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly comprehensive quality students from humble backgrounds. Students in our school enjoy the national-provided scholarship of 1500 yuan per student per year.
