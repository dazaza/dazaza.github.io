Youxian Meidi homeware farmer discovered the knee joint cupping apparatus for treating his father, which has received a national patent.

by n7ssd2mlp on 2012-02-07 11:04:37

The original intention was to cure his father's disease. Li Zhibin demonstrated the use of a cupping device. Photo by reporter Zhang Haiqiang.

Li Zhibin said that the outcome of the cupping device he manufactured was very good. After a period of use, his father’s arthritis became increasingly mild, and he walked more easily. Li Zhibin immediately found a friend, borrowed money, and started formal research and development. His family also changed from opposition to full support, encouraging him to continue.

"At the time, my whole family opposed my research on cupping devices, thinking it would be better to spend more time doing odd jobs to solve household expenses," Li Zhibin said. In the face of family opposition, he did not retreat. He found a friend who worked in the hospital and seriously studied professional knowledge about human knee joints. Subsequently, Li Zhibin, who had no foundation, first made a prototype of the cupping device with wood and persuaded his father to experiment on the elder's legs.

Obtained national patents

Stubborn young man persists in research

After the sample of the cupping device was made, Li Zhibin applied for a patent with relevant departments at the end of March 2010. In early 2011, Li Zhibin received the utility patent certificate issued by the National Intellectual Property Administration and the medical device registration certificate approved by the Shijiazhuang Food and Drug Administration.

A year later, after countless experiments, he finally determined to make the cupping device with transparent benzene material.

To treat his elderly father's leg ailment, Linghou farmer Li Zhibin conducted painstaking research. In one year, he invented a knee joint cupping device and obtained a national patent, winning the praise of his fellow villagers.

The knee joint cupping device developed by Li Zhibin consists of a transparent polycarbonate jar and sealing materials. It uses traditional Chinese cupping therapy, which works through vacuum negative pressure principles to promote the flow of meridians and blood circulation. Its biggest feature is transforming common local and acupoint cupping into overall joint cupping. A farmer who only knew how to farm and do small businesses has earned the admiration of friends around him.

Li Zhibin is 33 years old and lives in Naji Cheng Village, Sanyuan Township, Linghou County. Since graduating from technical secondary school in 2001, Li Zhibin went out to do automobile parts business and worked in places like Liupanshui, Guizhou for seven or eight years. Not only did he fail to make money, but he also accumulated debts of over 200,000 yuan. After this "personal Waterloo", Li Zhibin returned home. "It was the cold winter of 2009 at that time, I felt very regretful, not knowing what else I could do," Li Zhibin said. Rising early and retiring late, he continued farming while seeking odd jobs outside to make ends meet.
