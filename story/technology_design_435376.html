Movie featuring Cecilia Cheung: At 30, there are many things women don't need to overthink - China Film Network

by letaoeoe on 2011-05-22 21:53:35

Not long ago, Er Dongsheng said that he had tailored a role for Zhang Baizhi and was waiting for her comeback. However, since Stephen Chow said that everyone would like to cooperate with her in shooting films and he was also queuing up, though at the back, Zhang Baizhi could not directly deny it. There are indeed many plans. However, I think women after 30 should not think too much or be anxious about something and just follow the fate's arrangement. Many people still remember that she once said on "Kangsi Coming" that she must sign an agreement with her husband before getting married. In case they divorce one day, children must be given to her. Zhang Baizhi naturally remembers what she said before but has a different attitude now. She thinks that there is no need to plan so far ahead and cherishes the present more.

Lucas made an appearance at the Shanghai Film Festival and everyone thought that his acting talent was really strong and it was a pity if he did not enter the entertainment circle. In response to this, Zhang Baizhi said that everything depended on Lucas' willingness. He was playing very happily now. Maybe when he was eight years old, he would not want to be photographed by everyone every day and have other interests. She would obey his will and not force him.

The other day, the new film conference of Stephen Chow's animated blockbuster "长江七号爱地球" was held in Beijing and Zhang Baizhi showed up again to support Stephen Chow. However, Lucas would not come this time because he had something else to do. After discussing the issues concerned by everyone such as her comeback, Zhang Baizhi said that she had not thought too much and would obey the fate's arrangement.

Zhang Baizhi appeared on the opening red carpet of the Shanghai Film Festival. Her shape that had not slimmed down yet surprised everyone. She who loved beauty could not bear it and showed up the next day with a hairstyle and clothes that made her look slimmer. This time when she showed up, Zhang Baizhi had full confidence. White stockings and high heels matched with a red skirt and a wide belt around the waist displayed her good figure. Many women inquired about her secret of slimming down quickly. There was nothing special. I exercised every day. Stephen Chow added that she got up at more than 6 o'clock in the morning and swam in the hotel swimming pool until more than 9 o'clock. It was from 7 o'clock to 9 o'clock. I swam for 70 minutes every day according to my plan.

As for why she chose Stephen Chow's film as the first step of her comeback, Zhang Baizhi said that apart from revenge, she actually hoped to see more good cartoons made by Chinese themselves. One day when I went home, Lucas suddenly said to me: Hey man, not again! I did not teach him this. It turned out that this was the favorite line of the protagonist in his favorite American cartoon. I suddenly realized that cartoons had great influence on children. So I decided to support Stephen (Stephen Chow)'s animated film and hoped that he would continue to make it in the future. I hope that more directors will make good-looking animated films for my children to watch because I will give birth to many children.

The other day, the theme song of "长江七号爱地球" was officially announced. However, everyone found that the main singer was not Lucas but Zhang Baizhi. In response to this, Zhang Baizhi laughed and said that if you guessed it, we would not be Zhang Baizhi and Stephen Chow. After finishing the joke, Zhang Baizhi defended her son. It was not easy for him. A three-year-old child received English education all the time and could hardly speak Cantonese. His Mandarin was sung well. Actually, he sang but in a lower voice. Stephen Chow added that he should call their family's Filipina maid to sing because Zhang Baizhi taught the song to the Filipina maid in her family and let her repeatedly teach Lucas to sing the song. At last, Lucas learned it poorly while the Filipina maid sang it proficiently.

Whether entering the entertainment circle or not depends on Lucas' own opinion.
