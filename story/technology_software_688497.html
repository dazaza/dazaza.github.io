ECNS, Enshi, December 21 - Title: A 60-year-old single man in Hubei adopts an abandoned baby for two years; the child calls him "Mom"

Author: Mou Lianxiang, Hu Junjie, Tang Xu

Two years ago, a destitute single man found and adopted an abandoned infant by the roadside. For the past two years, this man has acted as both father and mother, carefully nurturing the boy's growth. They rely on each other for survival, showing true human kindness amidst their poverty. This man is Zhao Xianxiang, a farmer from Group One, Lianghui Village, Boyang Town, Lichuan City, Hubei Province.

Single Man, Impoverished Home

Zhao Xianxiang, who is now 60 years old, is a simple and honest farmer. Since childhood, he has been frail and sickly, with little education or special skills. As an adult, he continued to work the land at home. Due to his family's impoverished situation, he never married.

Zhao Xianxiang's home is very modest. The house is built of stone blocks, with broken walls and covered with cracked tiles. Looking up indoors feels like being outdoors, dotted with light through the holes. His home lacks a proper stove, using only the traditional "fire pit" and blackened "cauldron" for cooking.

Despite his poverty, he does not have the bad habit of laziness. He often helps villagers with farming and odd jobs, and in everyone's eyes, he is a warm-hearted person.

Kind-Hearted, Adopting an Abandoned Baby

One cold winter morning in 2009, it was snowing heavily, making the weather unusually harsh. Zhao Xianxiang still went to work in the fields as usual. Around noon, while carrying his load and struggling home, the sound of a baby crying stopped him in his tracks. He put down his burden and followed the sound. What he saw shocked him: a baby wrapped in swaddling clothes lay there, its face frozen purple, with only weak cries echoing in the icy valley.

Whose child was this? Zhao Xianxiang called out everywhere but received no response, confirming that the baby had been abandoned by its parents. Without hesitation, Zhao immediately took off his own clothes, wrapped the child, and rushed home.

Upon arriving home, he quickly warmed the baby by the fire and searched for formula to feed it. He also gave the child a name—Zhao Anyue. "This child is really lucky; I didn't think I would be able to raise him, but it's a miracle," Zhao Xianxiang said reflectively.

Tough Raising, Never Giving Up

After bringing the child home, many people advised Zhao Xianxiang to give up adopting him. However, Zhao firmly and resolutely said, "No matter whose child it is, it's still a life. As long as I am alive one day, I will take care of him for one day, raising him until he grows up."

For more than two years, despite his weak body, Zhao Xianxiang carried the child with him every day whether working the fields or cooking. He took care of the child like both a father and a mother, feeding him, washing his clothes, bathing him, and putting him to sleep. Now, the child is already two years old, healthy and well-behaved. During the interview, the child kept calling Zhao Xianxiang "Mom." Zhao Xianxiang often held the child affectionately and kissed him. "As long as I am still alive, I will take good care of him and try my best to let him go to school in the future," Zhao Xianxiang said with determination and hope. (End)
