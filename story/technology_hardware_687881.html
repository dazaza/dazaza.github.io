Legend of the Sky: A man was criminally detained for falsely selling gold, knocking out a jewelry store owner and robbing more than 10,000 yuan

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 19:59:51

The criminal suspect was identifying the crime scene under cover of darkness.

Xu, who operated a jewelry shop in Fude Village, thought he could make a profit when he heard that a "regular customer" was going to sell gold and silver ornaments again. Unbeknownst to him, instead of a deal, he was met with a frenzied beating from an electric baton. Nearly 10,000 yuan in cash and a gold ring were stolen from him. After the incident, the Fude police station took it very seriously. On February 24th, three suspects including Wang were arrested. Wang confessed that it was he who had invited Xu under the pretense of selling gold and silver ornaments, and it was also he who had electrocuted Xu. Yesterday, during the identification of the crime scene, he tried to justify himself by claiming that he had been led astray by his friends.

At around 24:00 on February 16th, a man supported by his friend came to report the case at the Fude police station. The skin on a large part of the back of the complainant, Xu, had already been "burned". Xu told the police that he operated a gold and silver jewelry store in Fude Village. During business transactions, Wang had once sold gold and silver ornaments at his store, and thus they exchanged phone numbers and became familiar with each other. On the day of the incident, he received a call from Wang, saying that he would sell gold and silver ornaments and agreed to conduct the transaction that evening. Xu waited for Wang inside the store. Around 23:30, Wang and two other men walked into the jewelry shop. Without any warning, Xu felt an electric shock on his neck and then fell to the ground after being electrocuted. When Xu woke up, nearly 10,000 yuan in cash and a gold ring worth more than 5,000 yuan had been stolen from him. With his remaining consciousness, he called his friend.

After receiving the alarm, Zhang and Chen immediately rushed to the scene. With the assistance of the criminal investigation team's police officers, the Guandu District Public Security Bureau Fude Police Station quickly formed a special investigation team to carry out investigative work. On February 24th, based on clues, the police successively arrested three suspects. Currently, the three suspects have been criminally detained.
