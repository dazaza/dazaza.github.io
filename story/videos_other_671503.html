Recommendation of good-looking novels

by zhaobin888 on 2012-02-22 09:48:07

Romance Category:

1. Tang Qi Gongzi (Representative work: Three Lives, Ten Miles of Peach Blossoms) - An introductory reading material for Xianxia romance. Although I started reading novels (romance category) a long time ago, it was undoubtedly this one that moved me the most. What are the twists and turns of fate? What is unrequited love? What is the cycle of life? What is reuniting after separation? I can only say, Master, your emotions run too deep. The heroine's level of thought hasn't reached yours, so doesn't this benefit her younger brother instead? Other works include "Years Are Two Lifelong Flowers" and "Hua Xu Yin". Personally, I feel that the author's other works don't reach the height of "Three Lives", but I've collected all of the author's books.

2. Shu Ke ("The Strange Tales of Wulin"/"One Thunderbolt"/"Chong Zi") - This author should be one of the representative authors of new martial arts literature. The first novel I read on Jinjiang Literature Network was his/her work. By the way, my personal favorite is actually "The First Husband" and my favorite character is Great Lord Shangguan. Haha, although I also feel sorry for Murong in "The Strange Tales". This author excels at torturing male supporting characters and even more so at making unexpected plot twists to change the male lead, causing me to often root for the wrong pairings.

3. Fresh Guoguo (Representative work: "Xianxia Qiyuan: Hua Qian Gu") - This is the book I consider to be the best Xianxia tragedy. The master in the story is one of our ideal husband candidates. However, if only the master could express a bit more emotion...

4. Siye Linglan ("Besides Me, Who Else Can You Love") - (Hehe, this is such a heartwarming book.)

5. Ying Zhao ("Wumen (JIONG) Affairs") - Alright, this is an author who doesn't produce much work and always leaves stories unfinished for years, but they truly write well. Once I fall into their stories, I can't climb out.

6. Zhuang Zhuang ("Eternal Night") - Apparently, this author is from Chengdu and works as a journalist. I'm currently following her new divine story. It's said that it will be published in May and then she won't update anymore. This sister's works on Jinjiang aren't VIP. She's a good person! By the way, I still read her VIP works.
