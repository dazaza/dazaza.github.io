Established in 1990, the Wanhang Textile and Apparel Co., Ltd. of Shishi City has been committed to the development of children's clothing industry, created the *(Wanmei)* apparel brand. After more than ten years of steady and progressive efforts, it has now developed into a professional children's clothing company integrating research, design, production, and sales. The company owns over 20,000 square meters of modern standard workshops, has introduced more than 600 sets of first-class advanced equipment from Europe and Japan, adopted advanced production processes, and established a complete quality assurance system.

Through the joint efforts of all employees for more than ten years, *(Wanmei)* has vigorously promoted the brand upgrade strategy and has grown into a well-known and reputable mid-to-high-end children's clothing brand! With healthy and environmentally friendly comfortable fabrics, vibrant colors, simple, fashionable designs full of childlike fun, Wanmei has a large number of loyal consumers. For many years, Wanhang Company has always earnestly implemented the enterprise philosophy of "customer first" and "service first", with integrity cooperation and mutual benefit as its goal, pursuing a perfect and trustworthy brand image, striving to make Wanmei a century-old classic children's clothing brand!
