A Brief Discussion on the Highlights of the First 3D Gaming Extravaganza - By Wang Yijian

Following the continuous emergence of 3D films, 3D TVs are becoming increasingly popular. According to industry insiders, 3D TVs will become a mainstream home appliance in households across the country within the next few years. At that time, 3D visual enjoyment will be widely known, and watching 3D movies will become an extremely important part of family entertainment. However, the changes brought about by 3D TVs are not only reflected in watching 3D movies but also have significant implications for 3D gaming.

It is reported that China's first 3D E-sports Extravaganza will kick off at the National Aquatics Center (Water Cube) on September 3, 2011. This grand event aims to provide gamers with a 3D-based visual experience. Among the games we have encountered, 2D games dominate, but the advent of 3D games is an unstoppable trend. So, how can players' competitive experience and visual sensation in 3D games be upgraded? How can the dynamism and impact of 3D game graphics be demonstrated? How can the rich color elements of 3D games be expressed? These are the questions that the first 3D gaming extravaganza needs to answer. Next, we will briefly introduce the situation of this 3D gaming extravaganza and see what highlights this "pioneering" 3D gaming event has to offer.

One, Event Location: Water Cube, Free Entry

The event is scheduled from September 3, 2011 (Saturday) to September 4 (Sunday), from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. It must be said that the timing is well chosen, utilizing the weekend opportunity so that many interested friends can participate in this unique event. The location is also very special—the National Aquatics Center (Water Cube), which was the venue for swimming competitions during the 2008 Beijing Olympics. Selecting such a grand and impressive location is undoubtedly to highlight the influence of the entire event. Moreover, you don't need to worry about tickets because the event is completely free, making it a free feast of 3D gaming. For those who work tirelessly over weekends, why not take the chance to witness the mystery of 3D gaming?

Two, Event Content: 3D E-sports

E-sports is something everyone is familiar with. Simply put, it's playing games. Various e-sports tournaments emerge constantly in the industry, but this is the first time for a 3D e-sports tournament. In this special e-sports competition, top players from China and South Korea will engage in a peak battle with the 3D version of StarCraft II. The participating players are as follows:

China: Fly (Lu Weiliang), infi (Wang Xuwén), TH000 (Huang Xiang), Loner (Dai Yi)

South Korea: MOON (Jang Jae-ho), NaDa (Lee Yun-yeol), lyn (Park Jun), oGsMC (Jang Min-cheol)

These are all top-notch experts, so the exciting spectacle of the competition can be imagined. It should be noted that September 3 is the quarterfinals advancing to the semifinals, and September 4 is the semifinals and finals, so don't miss them. Besides the official matches, this extravaganza also features a signing session with e-sports celebrities. Additionally, the audience can compete against Sky (Li Xiaofeng), and there will be generous gifts for everyone.

Three, Strong Support from Flicker-Free 3D Technology

It is worth mentioning that this event is organized by LG Display. To ensure the smooth running of the event, manufacturers from the flicker-free 3D TV alliance, including Skyworth, Konka, Haier, Hisense, Changhong, TCL, LG, Acer, and AOC, have all brought their most competitive products.

With strong support from numerous manufacturers, this grand event provides comprehensive experience opportunities for the attendees. All they need to do is wear a pair of 3D glasses to easily tour the entire venue and fully experience all the 3D cinemas, 3D TVs, and 3D computer products. Additionally, through spectating, attendees can understand the unique advantages of flicker-free 3D TVs in 3D gaming.

In summary, this grand event has quite a lot of highlights. Since that is the case, what are you waiting for? Why not spend a weekend to experience it thoroughly and genuinely feel the charm of 3D gaming and 3D TVs?
