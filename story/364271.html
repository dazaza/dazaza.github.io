Ten sentences full of philosophical meaning.

by rentouma on 2010-09-11 11:18:31

        The first sentence: "People are all forced into action." Everyone has potential. Born in comfort, die in adversity. Therefore, when facing pressure, don't be anxious. Perhaps this is just a small test from life. Believe in yourself, and you can handle everything well. When pushed to the limit, even a good man can join the Liangshan outlaws. Times create heroes, the poor seek change, people only have motivation under pressure.

     The second sentence: "If you are simple, the world will be simple to you." A simple life leads to a happy life. People should be content, be broad-minded, and not overcomplicate things. When the mental burden becomes too heavy, one tends to blame heaven and worry excessively. Regularly clear your memory of unpleasant people and events. Life is short, wealth and status are secondary, you don't bring anything with you when you're born, nor take it with you when you die. A simple life is a happy life.

     The third sentence: "Life has no rehearsal, every day is live broadcasting." Occasionally, I think if life were really like a video game, where you could start over if you messed up, what would life be like? Precisely because time flows on and never returns, each day lost cannot be retrieved, so cherish every moment. Respect your parents, love your children, care for your partner, and be kind to your friends.

     The fourth sentence: "Talent is like pregnancy, after some time, others will notice." People, don't be arrogant. The earth will continue to spin without anyone. Throughout history, those who flaunted their talent and acted recklessly have had bad endings. Therefore, even if you are very capable, always remain humble, focus on your own affairs, and gold will eventually shine.

     The fifth sentence: "In the past, meeting a friend meant drinking endlessly, now, drinking endlessly means few true friends." Not being much of a drinker, I may not appreciate the taste of alcohol, but I can feel the charm of friendship. A life without friends is lonely and incomplete. However, due to the busyness of life, we gradually lose touch, and friendships grow faint. So, take some time to reconnect with friends and let the camaraderie flourish in laughter. When a friend encounters difficulties, remember to stand up and support them, even if you can't help, offering comfort is the greatest support.

     The sixth sentence: "If life takes a wrong turn, stopping is progress." People always find it hard to correct their flaws or discover their mistakes. Sometimes, knowing something is wrong but still unable to stop, making the same mistake repeatedly. Grasp the right direction, adhere to your principles. There are many temptations in the world, and there's no such thing as a free lunch. Don't sacrifice happiness for fleeting pleasures. If you realize you're wrong, stop immediately.

     The seventh sentence: "The two great tragedies of life: one is despair, the other is overconfidence." Modern people seem especially fragile, with reports daily about celebrities suffering from depression. These individuals must have gone from one extreme to another. Because they are overconfident, they believe they are perfect and omnipotent. If faced with setbacks, they become extremely self-deprecating, even losing the courage to continue living. Find an accurate place for yourself and enjoy life.

     The eighth sentence: "Life is like love, missing love means missing life." What is love? It makes people confused and infatuated. In the face of love, be brave and express your feelings openly. If there's a flower worth picking, pick it immediately, don't wait until there's none left. People will inevitably experience birth, aging, sickness, and death. No matter how you live, it’s a lifetime. Missing love means missing the brilliance of life.

     The ninth sentence: "All rich people end up together." People in modern society have become increasingly materialistic, and love has become less significant. Thus, I have to believe that "All rich people end up together" is the most accurate description of modern love.

     The tenth sentence: "To succeed, you need friends; to achieve great success, you need enemies." Competition drives development. Because enemies exist, and because of the determination not to lose, one strives to do one's best. Therefore, sometimes, enemies can be more powerful than friends. There are no eternal enemies in the world, but there are eternal friends. Sometimes, enemies can also become friends.
