The cold winter. The remnants of snow fall. In her palm, lies that red leaf quietly...

-Preface

Written by Ru'er

Gazing at the horizon, how far is the distance?

In a dim corner, she is all alone. In this desolate winter, you chose to leave, without any lingering.

You didn't see, she was still foolishly chasing that unfulfilled nightmare. You didn't see, she was still stubbornly guarding that once beautiful sky. The petals of her heart shattered into pieces, falling amidst the swirling snow.

Only after loving do you understand the depth of affection; only after being drunk do you know the strength of wine. Only after being hurt do you realize that choosing the wrong direction at the crossroads can be painful.

In that rainy season, she was indeed drunk, deeply and heavily so. The price of awakening was her repeated offerings of tears.

The past road was so difficult, so profoundly memorable. Why did it break so easily? She thought this would be the purest beauty in her life, but you threw her off the clouds, shattering her completely...

She realized that you were also severely wounded. She no longer had the strength to struggle or the tears to cry. The cold air made her almost suffocate. What is pain? What is bitterness? Perhaps, she has already gotten used to it...

The vastness of time permeates her thin figure, the drunken lonely shadow embedded in the fragments of memory. Beneath the thousand-year-old green bamboo, the unspoken vows are buried, along with the spoken lies...

Thus, abandoned in the ruins behind the fierce wind, she counts the desolation alone. Her disordered thoughts knock on the door of her heart, but the sorrow of those days has faded away. She tried to ignite memories several times, but the happiness of those days has ended...

When words become superfluous, the shallow sorrow of parting gradually fades in solitude, yet in that season, she really cried...

In the paradise of love, who waits for you? Whose petals under the thousand-year-old green bamboo are buried... A pair of slender swallows land on the window frame, bringing the sighs of a thousand years: This feeling can be cherished as a memory, but at the time, it was already lost...

Lonely snow falls in the night. Along a path, she walks further and further, with no end in sight. Her search seems to have been covered in dust long ago. Fantasy remains that initial purple.

Where is the distant place you spoke of? Perhaps, it's the beautiful mirage. Or perhaps, as Hai Zi said: In the distance, there is nothing but darkness...

A red leaf falls into her palm. How could there be red leaves left in such a desolate winter? Perhaps, someone deliberately left it...

Faint ink sketches deep impressions: Living together in hardship is not as good as forgetting each other in the world...
