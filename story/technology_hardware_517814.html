2011 Roewe Concert Lands in Suzhou, Discounts Available for Same-day Car Orders

by rabindr130 on 2011-10-24 14:00:48

At 14:00 on October 22nd, there will be a great promotion for Roewe cars at the Huagui showroom in Suzhou. All models of Roewe will be sold at their lowest prices with various low-cost options available. The value you get is greater than your wait! This is the 2011 Roewe Digital Concert. When you reserve a Roewe car, not only will you receive cash discounts and gift packages, but also tickets to the superstar concert at the Gusu Sports Center on November 5th. You'll even have the chance to perform on stage with the stars. Are you excited yet? In October, Huagui Suzhou is bound to be dazzling and highly anticipated. On November 5th, there will be a superstar concert at the Suzhou Sports Center featuring stars such as Wu Bai, China Blue, Lin Chih-hsuan, Huang Pin-yuan, and Yu Kewei. With so many stars shining brightly, this Roewe concert and music gala is an event you can't miss in Suzhou! Both the Huagui Suzhou and Huhong Suzhou showrooms are hosting a Roewe Singing Contest where outstanding contestants will have the opportunity to perform on stage with the stars.

The 2011 Roewe All-Digital Concert features not only superstars but also a strong lineup from SAIC Motor's Roewe brand. The "Cross-domain Professional SUV" Roewe W5, known for its comfort, sportiness, and safety, will be showcased at the Suzhou concert. Its P-4WD professional all-mode drive system, HFA high-strength off-road body structure, dual-arm front suspension + five-link rear suspension, imported off-road tires, and SCS intelligent automatic safety control system make it a powerhouse in the off-road category. The overall design of the Roewe 550 exudes British charm with distinct characteristics. Its interior is dominated by simple and elegant straight lines and arcs, making the layout clear and the design straightforward and lively. The Roewe 350's inkaNet 2.0 version has globally pioneered key technical challenges in the development of "Internet of Vehicles" technology.

This is an innovative concert where, besides the lineup of superstars, there will be a dazzling appearance of Roewe's star models. The "Cross-domain Professional SUV" Roewe W5, which combines quietness, sportiness, and safety; the Roewe 750 that continues the British classic; the Roewe 550 with British-style exterior design and a simple and lively interior; and the Roewe 350 equipped with inkaNet 2.0 that has globally broken through key technical challenges in "Internet of Vehicles" technology development. These models represent Roewe's journey in China and will all be showcased on stage.
