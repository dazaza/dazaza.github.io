14.74-Billion-Pixel Photo of Obama's Inauguration

by bago on 2009-03-01 10:48:20

An American company GigaPan Systems has just released a device called GigaPan Epic, which can produce digital photos with hundreds of millions of pixels. This Gigapan Epic technology was used at US President Obama's inauguration.

The GigaPan Epic took a 1.474 billion pixel photo at the Obama inauguration, in which you can almost see everyone's face. It is worth noting that the photo was not completed in one shot, but rather it was stitched together after the GigaPan Epic precisely calculated the shooting delay.
