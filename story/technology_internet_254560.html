Reasons why the Baidu homepage www.baidu.com cannot be opened and solutions

by wangzhq0618 on 2009-11-01 14:03:36

Recently, the network in the teaching and research office often fails to open the Baidu homepage, but other pages of Baidu such as Baidu Zhidao and Baidu Tieba can all be opened normally. Of course, other web pages like Google China and some blogs can also be opened. I believe many friends have encountered this situation~ By the way, my browser is IE8~

There seem to be many reasons for this issue:

1. The local area network problem, which leads to the inability to access the Baidu homepage. A proxy software like Wingate can be used. (This might be the reason)

2. The hosts file on the computer has issues and needs repair. Search for the hosts file on your computer, open it with a text editor, and check if there is a record item for www.baidu.com. If so, please delete the record and restart the browser. (I checked this point, and it seems there isn't any) ; or download an IE repair tool to fix it.
