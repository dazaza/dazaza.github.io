Best Answer: Glad to answer! Suggestions for opening an online store: Opening a store on Taobao is still a pretty good choice. Once you build up your sales volume and customer base, the profit can be quite substantial. However, competition now is fierce. When opening a store, you must do extensive promotion. Your products need to have certain advantages over similar ones, such as price advantage, good product quality, and excellent after-sales service. Honest operation is fundamental. Here are some recommended high-sales-volume products: 1. Virtual game points cards, recharge cards, QQ services, etc. 2. Women's clothing, accessories, and shoes.

Operating an online store also requires a deep understanding of business strategies and mastering some operational secrets. 1) When selling goods online, buyers mostly cannot see the physical items, so clear and attractive product photos need to be taken along with detailed descriptions to attract buyers. If the product photos in your online store are not clear and the descriptions are too simple, it will be hard to gain buyer favor. 2) The variety of products in your online store should be as complete as possible, and updates should be swift. Place new arrivals in prominent positions, but don't increase quantity at the expense of quality. Don't stop restocking because of poor business; if the same few items always appear in your store without offering freshness, buyers will lose interest. 3) After-sales service needs to be thorough. After selling a product, contact the buyer promptly, and after shipping, send them a shipping notification email with preferably a photo of the package receipt showing clear writing and specific tracking numbers to make the buyer feel more secure and welcomed. This is crucial for attracting repeat customers. 4) Pay attention to the 'credit rating' evaluation of your online store. When products are sold online, the platform website charges a certain transaction fee based on the proportion of the product's sale price. After completing a transaction, both parties have the obligation to provide credit evaluations for each other. High credit ratings are crucial for the operation of your online store, as all buyers use credit ratings to decide whether to purchase from you. 5) Honesty comes first. Operating on the internet mainly relies on honesty. Online, every seller has a record of their honesty that buyers can see, including previous sales records and evaluations by other buyers. The internet records the honesty of any seller, and those who are dishonest find it difficult to continue operating online. Honest sellers can sell products at higher prices and still find buyers. 6) In the early stages of opening a store, you can consider using one-yuan bids or low-price sales to increase popularity. Having more transaction records and positive reviews will lead to more buyers trusting your store and purchasing from you. A one-yuan bid can quickly gather attention, but be prepared to sell the item for one yuan. While conducting one-yuan bids, it's best to combine this with some advertising to let everyone know about your 'discounts'. The purpose of the one-yuan bid is to attract buyers to browse other products in your store while bidding on this item. 7) Adopt effective promotional strategies, regular discounts, or gifts. Give returning customers certain discounts or offer gifts or discounts when they spend a certain amount. Free shipping could also be offered. During promotions, change your signature to introduce the activity and promote it elsewhere, fully utilizing all resources to advertise your online store. 8) Include small free gifts. To gain more positive reviews, you can include a gift without letting the buyer know beforehand. When the buyer receives the item and the unexpected gift, they will be pleased. The gift does not need to be expensive but shows thoughtfulness. Regardless of whether the buyer will shop again, treat them as a friend and approach them sincerely. There will be good returns. 9) Stay calm with buyers who place orders but do not complete the purchase. Encountering such buyers is normal when running an online store. As a seller, maintaining a peaceful mindset is important for facing future competitive challenges. 10) Accept negative reviews calmly. Sometimes due to personal reasons or others, negative reviews may appear in your credit record. Face them calmly, take appropriate remedial measures, or explain the real situation online. At the same time, strive to earn more positive reviews to create a good impression among buyers. 11) To determine whether a particular product sells well, search the website first. Check how many people are selling similar products, including identical or similar ones, and analyze their sales conditions, product pricing, store design, and product introductions, etc. This market research allows you to "know yourself and know your enemy." 12) Running an online store should be gradual. The first three months of opening a store are typically for adapting to online operations and gaining popularity. During this time, learn from experienced sellers and adjust according to the market. Accumulate some customers, treating them as friends and the most connected people. Serving these customers well will continuously bring you new clients. Afterward, the store's operation will develop positively. 13) Auction-style pricing attracts more browsers than fixed-price offers. However, auctions might sometimes result in low-price wins. In such cases, some store owners ask their friends to participate in the auction to bid up to an acceptable amount or, if all bidders offer very low prices, the friend can win the auction, resulting in only minor transaction fees lost. This practice is non-credible and thus not recommended. 14) For online stores, it is important to "look around" and accurately grasp market conditions. If possible, frequently visit markets and not just focus on online opportunities. Fourth Section: Precautions for Opening an Online Store. Opening an online store is a business activity and comes with certain precautions: 1) An online store is not omnipotent. Among countless online stores, only a few truly achieve profitability and success. Unrealistic ideas about online stores should be discarded, and the store should be operated diligently for its rewards. 2) Be a law-abiding citizen and avoid selling products prohibited by national laws and regulations in your online store. 3) Operate your online store honestly and do not deceive consumers or seek unjust gains. 4) Adhere to national laws and policies. Currently, there are no specific regulations governing online stores, but there have been cases where thriving online stores were penalized by local administrations for unlicensed operations. Therefore, when necessary, registration should be applied for, and relevant taxes should be paid. 5) Operating an online store carries risks, and sufficient mental preparation is required. Among China's online stores, the proportion that truly makes money is not very high. Before opening a store, careful analysis and comparison are needed. If setbacks occur during operations, face them calmly.

Special Note: Not everyone makes money from opening an online store. According to my knowledge, the proportion of profitable online stores might not exceed 5%, and some even engage in illegal activities like selling counterfeit goods. Everyone should carefully decide, and this article serves only as a reference.
