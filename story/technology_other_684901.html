No one was injured after flipping into a ditch to avoid a dog 18671079366

by xfjndf on 2012-03-01 11:25:12

The sedan flipped into the ditch and caught fire, soon burning into an empty shell. Photo by Wang Zhanbing.

On February 28th, our newspaper reported (by reporter Lü Xiugan and correspondent Wang Zhanbing) that on the evening of the 27th, on the coastal road in Zeku Town, Wendeng City, Weihai, a young couple was injured in a car accident while avoiding a dog. Fortunately, they were discovered by nearby villagers, and the driver was rescued in time from the self-igniting car.

Mr. Hou from Wendeng and Ms. Lu are a couple. At around 9 p.m. on the 27th, the two were driving their private car along the coastal road from west to east when Ms. Lu suddenly noticed a large dog darting in front of the car, causing her to instinctively step on the brakes. After the tires locked, the car skidded diagonally for more than 40 meters towards the north side of the road, flipped three times in the ditch on the north side of the road, crossed a small stone bridge, and eventually overturned in the ditch. During the rolling of the car, Mr. Hou was thrown out of the car and suffered chest injuries due to compression. Ms. Lu suffered a cervical vertebra fracture and was trapped inside the car.

Yu Xixing, a villager from Xinwangzhuang Village, went out of his house to use the restroom and heard the sound of car brakes and a series of collisions. "An accident happened!" Yu Xixing realized that there had been a car accident on the road north of the village, so he quickly got dressed and rushed to the north of the village. On the way, he also contacted two households north of the village to rush to the scene for rescue. About 500 meters away on the road north of the village, six villagers including Yu Xixing found the overturned accident vehicle in the ditch. Ms. Lu was trying to crawl out from the driver's seat but failed due to her injuries. Yu Xixing and others quickly pulled Ms. Lu out of the car. At this time, the car engine had already caught fire, emitting sparks and black smoke. After being rescued, Ms. Lu kept shouting: "There is still someone in the car!" Yu Xixing and others quickly checked the already burning car compartment but did not find any passengers. Later, they discovered Mr. Hou groaning in pain about 20 meters west of the car.

During the rescue, Yu Xixing called 110. The traffic police, fire department, and Songcun Hospital of Wendeng immediately dispatched police forces, fire trucks, and ambulances to the scene for rescue. Mr. Hou and Ms. Lu were quickly sent to the Wendeng Central Hospital for treatment. Although the fire-fighting officers worked hard to put out the fire on the burning vehicle, due to the large fire, the car finally left only a frame.
