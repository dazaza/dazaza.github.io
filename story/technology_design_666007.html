Clever women must learn: five economical whitening remedies

by wswen156 on 2012-02-15 12:56:15

There are also cost-effective and practical remedies for skin whitening!

Strategy One: Chrysanthemum Beauty

Chrysanthemum contains rich essential oil and chrysanthemum pigment, which can effectively inhibit the production of melanin in the skin and soften the epidermal cells.

Method One: Chrysanthemum can be made into porridge for internal use;

Method Two: Mash it up with egg white and apply on face, which can whiten the skin.

Method Three: Put the chrysanthemum petals into a bottle, pour in medical alcohol and seal it. After one month, dilute it with cold water at a ratio of 2 times, which has a whitening effect on the skin, especially for oily skin.

Strategy Two: Cucumber Skin Whitening Mask

1. Put the cucumber into the juicer to extract juice and pour it into a small bowl.

2. Then add egg white, pearl powder, and an appropriate amount of flour to form a paste that doesn't drip.

3. Wash your face, apply the prepared paste on your face, wash it off after it dries, then pat on softening water and skincare products. This can be done once or twice a week.

Strategy Three: Beer Refreshing Skin

1. Take a clean small bowl, pour in beer. Soak the medical cotton gauze in beer for about 3 minutes.

2. Take out the gauze, wring it slightly, and place it on your face so that your facial skin gets thoroughly relaxed. Keep it on for about half an hour.

3. If the moisture in the gauze is absorbed, repeat the first two steps to soak it again and then apply.

Strategy Four: Tea Water Whitening

Method One: After washing your face, apply tea water on your face and gently pat it with your hands.

Method Two: Attach the defatted cotton dipped in tea water on your face for 2-3 minutes, then rinse with clear water, which has the effect of removing spots and whitening.

Tips: Sometimes the color of tea water on the face cannot be washed off immediately, but it will naturally disappear overnight.

Strategy Five: Potato Whitening Mask

Potatoes contain abundant vitamins, which can promote the growth of skin cells, maintain skin luster, bleach subcutaneous melanin, not only whiten and soften the skin, but also fade summer sunspots.

1. Wash the potatoes, peel them, cut them into pieces, and put them into the juicer to extract juice.

2. Pour one-third cup of fresh milk into the container, mix in flour to make a paste, which can be used as a mask.

3. Apply it on your face, wash it off after 20 minutes.
