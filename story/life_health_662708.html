The Strange Phenomenon of Hair Transplantation Effects and Prices

by spring77 on 2012-02-10 15:28:12

If you heard the word "hair transplantation" a few years ago, many people would feel strange, and some people might even not understand the meaning of this word. In the past, there were only a few large hair transplant hospitals in our country. Now, hair transplant hospitals are as numerous as cattle hairs. Almost all the people with hair loss know this technology. Hair transplantation is gradually becoming "a household name". The rapid development of the hair transplant industry has brought blessings to many patients with hair loss.

Due to the rapid development of the hair transplant industry, hair transplant hospitals have not been standardized, so a lot of problems have emerged. For example, some hair transplant institutions disrupt the market by low price to attract patients for hair transplantation. However, the effects are often not satisfactory. When you question them about poor hair transplantation effects, they often ignore you, refusing consultation and refund. A reporter learned that a patient once went to a self-claimed largest hair transplant institution in Beijing for hair transplantation. Because of the scars left on his neck, he negotiated with the hospital, but the hospital claimed that it was not within the compensation scope. Their attitude was extremely terrible compared with their pre-surgery attitude. The patient had no choice but to pay out-of-pocket to go to another hair transplant hospital for hair transplantation. According to the hair transplant hospital, they often receive patients who failed hair transplantation in other hospitals. They need to transplant hair for these patients and repair the scalp scars of these patients.

Afterwards, the patient found that the previous hair transplant hospital was actually not cheap. Due to technical or other reasons, the number of units they expected to implant had a big difference. Finally, the price of hair transplantation in this hospital was almost the same as the price of hair transplantation in other relatively higher-priced hospitals. It is not said that a low price can definitely do a bad job in hair transplantation surgery. But while attracting patients at a low price, they should ensure quality. We all know that hair transplantation is a very patience-demanding surgery. The postoperative effect largely depends on the hospital technology and the carefulness of all doctors and medical staff. If the hospital has such problems, it will undoubtedly bring great trouble to the patient. Patients with hair loss originally care about their hair. Hair transplantation is for making themselves free from the troubles of hair loss and make themselves more confident. Unexpectedly, the surgery left greater hidden dangers for them. After the surgery failed, the behavior of ignoring and not caring about the patients is really unforgivable.

This kind of thing is not the first time for the reporter to hear. Nowadays, the competition in the hair transplant industry is very fierce, and marketing methods keep emerging. Some profit-maximizing hospitals blindly and exaggerate their own strengths, claiming to be the "largest domestic hospital", thinking that they can thus stand "invincible" and become the overlord of the hair transplant industry. They even compile some "non-existent" articles to attack their peers, confuse the patients' hearing, and mislead the patients. If speaking of facts is "revealing", then speaking of lies is "deception". Will the patients choose such hospitals?

The author thinks that the hair transplant industry should not fall into such a vicious circle, but needs a "healthy and transparent" environment. The effect should speak for itself. High technology and good effect are what the patients care about most. Instead of wasting time on these meaningless behaviors, it would be better to spend money to study abroad, bring good hair transplant technology into the country, and promote the better and faster development of the hair transplant industry.
