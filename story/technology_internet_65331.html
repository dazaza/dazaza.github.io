WanJie hospital shapes an immortal myth

by xiaozi163 on 2008-04-25 21:16:34

The first encounter with Wanjie Hospital, I was attracted by her sophisticated technology, which made me realize that Wanjie Hospital is marching towards a new peak with a brand-new posture.

Beijing Wanjie Hospital, located in Chaoyang District of Beijing, is a newly rising modern high-tech general hospital and a designated hospital for basic medical insurance in Beijing. Wanjie Hospital has strong strength and prominent high-tech advantages, equipped with advanced medical equipment such as Gamma Knife, Photon Knife, X-Knife, linear accelerator, DET, DSA, MRI, CT, etc., the net asset value of equipment ranks among the top hospitals in China. In recent years, Wanjie Hospital has treated nearly 1 million patients. In non-invasive treatment, Wanjie Hospital has reached world-advanced level in treating brain tumors, body tumors, and epilepsy, writing a new chapter in the history of Chinese medicine.

Wanjie Hospital has introduced four world-class large instruments at a huge cost, filling the gap in domestic modern medicine four times, and opening the curtain of non-invasive therapy without surgery in China. Gamma Knife - no craniotomy, no bleeding, one-time treatment for tumors and epilepsy, known as the "miracle knife", is a milestone in non-invasive therapy in neurosurgery. The X-Knife and Gamma Knife are known as the "twin treasures", especially in the treatment of complex brain tumors or brain tumors larger than 3.5 cm, Wanjie Hospital has more advantages in treating brain diseases. DET - the most advanced diagnostic equipment in the world, accurately determines the epileptic focus, detects tumors early and accurately. Precise diagnosis before treatment, rapid verification after treatment, it is known as the "magic mirror of tumors". Photon Knife - non-surgical, bloodless treatment for cancer and body tumors, "denying the traditional concept that cancer cannot be treated", giving modern treatment methods to tumor treatment.

Beijing Wanjie Hospital has a powerful group of experts, currently having 15 professor-level experts, 2 doctoral supervisors, 11 master's supervisors, and 1 chief researcher. They come from famous hospitals and research institutions such as Peking University Medical School, Cancer Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, and Xuanwu Hospital. Gamma Knife Center - China's first Gamma Knife and X-Knife treatment research center, Wanjie Hospital is also the only treatment center in China that has both Gamma Knife and X-Knife simultaneously.

Since its establishment, the Wanjie Hospital Center has used Gamma Knife and X-Knife to treat various benign and malignant brain tumors and brain diseases such as epilepsy in over 15,000 cases without surgery, no bleeding, no pain, no danger, and one-time treatment. The total number of treatments ranks first in the world, accounting for 1/9 of the total number of treatments worldwide using Gamma Knife and X-Knife. Wanjie Hospital has rich experience and remarkable achievements. Over the years, the center has trained and delivered more than 30 professional talents to more than 10 Gamma Knife hospitals in China, including Hangzhou, Chongqing, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Shijiazhuang, Taiyuan, and Harbin, making it the cradle of Gamma Knife talent in China. Photon Knife Center - equipped with comprehensive treatment methods such as surgery, conventional radiotherapy, and chemotherapy. Meanwhile, as the first Photon Knife treatment center in Beijing, Wanjie Hospital was the first to use the Photon Knife, filling the gap in Beijing - under the control of an intelligent computer, the Photon Knife uses high-energy photon beams to perform multi-field, non-coplanar, conformal treatment according to the shape of the lesion. Non-surgical, bloodless, painless, and risk-free, it is more accurate and reliable than manual surgery.

The Wanjie Hospital Center has China's first batch of Photon Knife experts with rich experience and academic authority. The combination of Photon Knife with high-level surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy, and traditional Chinese medicine forms a comprehensive system for tumor treatment, capable of treating nearly ten thousand patients per year.

As a modern general hospital, Wanjie Hospital relies on the great advantages of high-tech equipment to create conditions for other departments to carry out high-level medical business, gradually aligning with international leading technologies, and moving forward to a new starting point like a shining star, shaping an immortal myth in the medical field.
