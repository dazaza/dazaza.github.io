Characteristics and structure composition of the ball press machine, production process of corporate special films

The ball press machine is also called: strong ball press machine, ore powder ball press machine, double roller ball press machine, etc. The ball press machine is mainly used for - making balls and blocks of colored and black metal ores, toxic and harmful gas detectors, direct furnace smelting, to increase added value. Any waste materials and additives in the metallurgical industry that need to go into the furnace must be achieved through a ball press machine. For example: dust removal ash, pond mud, oxide scale, steel slag, iron concentrate, aluminum ash powder, silicon-manganese ore powder, etc.

The ball press machine is a device that presses powdered material into balls. The use of the ball press machine is extremely extensive, it can press the following materials into lumps, granules or balls, such as aluminum shavings, activated carbon, alumina, bauxite, caustic soda, charcoal, clay, coke shavings, coal, cryolite, fertilizers, plastics, limestone, pigments, urea, potassium chloride... etc. The purpose is to reduce dust, control bulk density, return to use, improve transportation characteristics, etc. The design capacity is mainly used for pressing dry powder into pellets for the Pidgeon process magnesium smelting technology, fireproof coatings.

The structure of the ball press machine consists of three parts: feeding part; transmission part; forming part.

1. Feeding part, mainly to achieve quantitative feeding to ensure that the material enters evenly between the rollers. The screw feeding device is driven by an electromagnetic speed-regulated motor, passing through pulleys and worm gear reducers to rotate, forcing the material to be pressed into the main feed port. Due to the constant torque characteristic of the electromagnetic speed-regulated motor, when the amount of material fed by the screw feeder matches the amount required by the host, a stable feeding pressure can be maintained to ensure the quality of the ball. If the feeding amount is too large, then the overload protection will activate on the feeder; if the feeding amount is too small, then the ball cannot form. Therefore, proficient operation skills are an important condition to ensure the normal operation of the ball press.

2. Transmission part, the main drive system is: motor -- triangular belt --- reducer --- open gear --- roller. The main engine is powered by an electromagnetic speed-regulated motor, which passes through pulleys and cylindrical gear reducers, and is transmitted to the active shaft via a bar pin coupling. The active shaft and passive shaft are synchronized through open gears. A hydraulic device is installed behind the passive bearing housing. The hydraulic protection device is composed of a hydraulic pump injecting high-pressure oil into the hydraulic cylinder, causing the piston to produce axial displacement. The front end of the piston rod connects with the bearing seat to meet the production pressure requirements.

3. Forming part, mainly refers to the main part, the core part is the roller. When excessive material enters between the two rollers or a metal block enters, the piston rod of the hydraulic cylinder is overloaded, the hydraulic pump will stop, the accumulator regulates the pressure changes, the relief valve opens to return oil, the piston rod moves to widen the gap between the rollers allowing the hard object to pass through the rollers, after which the system pressure returns to normal, protecting the rollers from damage, titanium equipment. Hua Wei Machinery Manufacturing adjusts the pressure according to the requirement of ball density, ensuring flexible and adaptable production.
