August 8th's top three: Hard drive prices drop, X4-631 falls to 400 yuan

by yanh203exue on 2012-03-08 15:59:51

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for Intel processors, Intel prices have risen against the trend, with SNB processors seeing an upward trend. Four mainstream SNB processors have slightly increased by 5-10 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun spot market for AMD processors, AMD processor prices have seen slight fluctuations, with only two entry-level processors experiencing a small price drop. The AMD X4-631 loose piece has dropped to 400 yuan.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop memory spot market, memory prices have stopped falling, and wholesale prices in Hong Kong/Beijing have been synchronized. Memory prices are expected to continue to fall slightly in the future.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook memory spot market, memory strip prices remain stable for now. The price increase of 2GB DDR3-1333 memory strips is limited, while the price of 4GB DDR3-1333 memory strips is virtually high and faces pressure to decrease.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun desktop hard drive spot market, hard drive prices overall continue to fall, with different degrees of price drops for 1TB/2TB/3TB hard drives. The 3TB hard drive has fallen sharply by 40 yuan today, approaching 1100 yuan. Demand for 500GB hard drives has increased, leading to tight supply, and prices have started to rise against the trend.

Today in Beijing's Zhongguancun notebook hard drive spot market, hard drive prices remain stable, with no changes for Seagate/Hitachi products.

Intel prices have risen against the trend, with SNB processors showing an upward trend.
