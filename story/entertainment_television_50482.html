Single Dad in Love Episode Summaries

Episode 1

Every day, the busy single dad Kang Bong-ho is a pest control specialist by day and turns into a boxer at night. In order not to make his son San late for the kindergarten visit activity, he drives recklessly and collides with Hee-lee's bicycle as she was moving a human model. The human model breaks, and Hee-lee stamps her feet anxiously as Bong-ho's car disappears. When Hee-lee hears the news that her good friend Hyun-gi has had hemorrhoid surgery, she sneaks into Hyun-gi's ward and picks up a needle to stab Hyun-gi, only to find out that the owner of the buttocks is actually Bong-ho.

Episode 2

Hee-lee, who falls asleep as soon as she sits down, leaves important documents on the subway. Bong-ho, who happens to be in the same carriage as Hee-lee, unexpectedly picks up Hee-lee's file. To find the owner of the file, after some twists and turns, Bong-ho finally reaches the university hospital. Under Hee-lee's pleading and the temptation of a considerable trial fee, Bong-ho becomes a subject in the clinical trial and even rescues a patient who faints during blood sampling.

Hee-lee, seeing Bong-ho taking care of others, changes her initial bad impression of him and gradually becomes attracted to Bong-ho's sense of responsibility.

Episode 3

After six years without seeing each other, Bong-ho and So-eui reunite. Perhaps due to the deep wounds they left each other, the two pretend to be strangers and pass each other by. Bong-ho endures his complex feelings and wins in the match. Meanwhile, Hee-lee, seeing Gi-seok and So-eui being intimate, feels like an outsider and leaves the welcome party alone. That evening, when Hee-lee hears that Gi-seok is in the apartment, she asks Bong-ho to let her stay over for the night. Can Bong-ho, San, and Hee-lee get through this night smoothly at Bong-ho's house?

Episode 4

After a series of events including spending the first night at Bong-ho’s home, cleaning the gym together, and breaking Bong-ho's phone, Hee-lee and Bong-ho’s relationship grows closer. However, Su-eui, watching the two from the sidelines, increasingly feels uneasy and ultimately decides to meet with Bong-ho. Bong-ho decides to participate in the Korean selection competition for the Asian Boxing Championship. Hee-lee insists on filming Bong-ho but ends up catching a cold and being sent to the emergency room. Despite muttering about how he always seems to lose money whenever he sees Hee-lee, Bong-ho can't help but show concern for her.
