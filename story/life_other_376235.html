Steps for Synthetic Fiber Carpet Cleaning:

1. Use a vacuum cleaner to thoroughly vacuum.

2. Dilute the cleaning agent or inject it into the water tank.

3. Spray the cleaning agent evenly over the carpet.

4. Allow 10-15 minutes for the stains to detach from the fibers.

5. Use the carpet washer to extract dirt, moving backward to ensure partial overlap in each pass, with at least two extraction passes.

6. Simultaneously, use the water extractor to remove moisture from the cleaned carpet.

7. Let the carpet dry completely; to speed up drying, use a carpet dryer.
