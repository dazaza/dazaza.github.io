by linderuk616 on 2012-02-09 16:41:33

"This time I finally found my savior, and now I can rest easy. I must thank him in person, or I would feel guilty for the rest of my life," said Gao Jiandong, a villager from Taiping Township in Tongling County on November 16th. He appeared unusually relaxed because he had seen the kind driver by chance on the street several days ago.

Three years ago, his daughter was hit by a car at the gate of their home and her life hung by a thread. A passing taxi driver persuaded the passengers in his car to get out and took them to the hospital without asking for any reward. But due to his own oversight, he forgot to say "thank you" to the driver.

Although the incident happened more than three years ago, Gao Jiandong said, "I will never forget what happened on September 17th, 2008."

At around 11 that day, Gao Jiandong went to school as usual to pick up his daughter Feifei (a pseudonym). There were not many vehicles on the rural roads, but occasionally some cars passed by. Worried about his daughter's safety, Gao Jiandong kept holding her hand until they reached the gate of their home. But just this casual release of his daughter's hand led to a tragedy. A farm vehicle sped by, knocking down Feifei and dragging her for more than ten meters.

When he caught up, his originally lively daughter was already unconscious. Seeing his blood-covered daughter, Gao Jiandong was at a loss and only held his daughter crying on the spot, shouting for help to passers-by. Several vehicles passed by, but none of them stopped.

To his relief, a few minutes later, a green-shelled taxi happened to pass by and stopped in front of him. He heard the driver say to the several passengers in the car, "You guys get out quickly, I won't charge you, saving lives is urgent." Gao Jiandong then carried his daughter onto the taxi.

After sending Feifei to the hospital, Gao Jiandong did not expect that his parents were also quickly sent to the hospital by this kind driver.

Gao Jiandong recalled that when they arrived at the county hospital, the doctor said that his daughter's condition was very critical and arranged an ambulance to send them to the city hospital for treatment. "The doctor told me that if we had been a little late, my daughter might have died." The driver left after sending them to the hospital, and Gao Jiandong didn't even have the chance to say thank you.

Three months later, Feifei recovered and was discharged from the hospital. Talking about the driver who helped him back then, Gao Jiandong said, "His kindness will never be forgotten." In these three years, they have been looking for this kind person who helped them. But with so many people in the world, there has been no progress.

A turning point occurred on November 14th this year. That morning, Gao Jiandong took his daughter to the city to buy school supplies and inadvertently saw this kind person on Chángjiāng Road in Tongling. At that time, the person was driving a taxi past them.

"This person is my daughter's savior, it's definitely him," Gao Jiandong thought, "Finally, I've found him." But because the taxi drove quite fast, Gao Jiandong couldn't catch up, but fortunately, he remembered the license plate number.

"Please help me thank this kind taxi driver. Thank him for giving me such a happy and complete family again..." On the afternoon of November 14th, Gao Jiandong wrote a thank-you letter to the Tongling City Transport Management Office, looking for the kind driver.

He never expected them to thank him

After inquiry, the transport management office quickly identified the kind person Gao Jiandong was looking for. His name is Ding Guanghua, and he has been driving a taxi for six years.

"If he hadn't mentioned this, I almost forgot about it," said Ding Guanghua when talking about the experience of saving someone three years ago on the afternoon of November 16th. Talking about what he did, Ding Guanghua said, "It's all trivial; as long as that child could be saved."

Ding Guanghua recalled that at the time, he saw Gao Jiandong holding his blood-covered daughter and crying incessantly, while several vehicles passed in front of him without stopping, which made him feel uneasy. "Saving lives is urgent! So I negotiated with the three passengers in my car, asking them to get out first and take another ride, or wait for me to come back and pick them up."

"I didn't accept any money for taking them to the hospital, and I didn't think they would thank me afterward because the situation was so urgent at the time." Ding Guanghua calmly said that in the years he has driven a taxi, he often encounters situations where others need help, and he always steps in to help, "Who doesn't have tough times?"

"If I met Gao Jiandong on the road today, I probably wouldn't recognize him." Hearing that the little girl Feifei who was saved at the time is now in the fourth grade of elementary school and has good grades, Ding Guanghua seemed very pleased.

Lu, a responsible official from the Taxi Management Office of Tongling City Transport Management Office, revealed that the office is considering commending Ding Guanghua and arranging for Gao Jiandong and Ding Guanghua to meet on the morning of the 17th. This day, Gao Jiandong has waited for three years.

Our newspaper will continue to follow up on this matter.
