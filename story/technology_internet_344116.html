Keywords: Jordan shoes, Nike shoes, Air Jordan 17: Michael Jordan deliberately created a new pair of shoes showcasing their favorite performance. The design of the shoe takes inspiration from jazz-themed sturdy soles that provide a stable foundation, while the upper material is covered with a dynamic pattern, fully embodying Michael Jordan's pursuit of perfection in his style of play.

2002 Key Technology: Blown rubber (heel), MBT shoes (formerly palm).

Advantages: Comfortable fit, good cushioning, high quality, excellent stability and ankle protection.

Jordan 18 Generation:

It features an excellent texture with full-grain leather and suede upper, chrome details emerging in embossed patterns on the body of the shoe, providing a perfect sense of value. Ventilation holes are specially designed between the soles at the heel and forefoot to increase ventilation. The shoelace department has been specifically reinforced to ensure the laces do not easily loosen during intense basketball games. The collar is designed with Far Foam and Lycra artificial elastic fibers to perfectly wrap around the wearer’s foot, ensuring great comfort. High-tech carbon fiber is specially installed in the heels to provide additional stability for ankle support. A full-foot piece is directly located under the insole cushion, with another piece added under the heel for a two-tier thin air-cushion design, which will surely provide an excellent shock absorber for the rear. The upper is covered with lightweight midsole, and the upper and midsole are hand-stitched with strong sutures, offering minimalist styling, good durability, and excellent foot control. The sole features fishbone-patterned notches that offer both horizontal and linear movement grip. Michael's logo is chrome-plated, separating the front and rear foot pieces made of hard rubber outsoles. The extension covers the heels with hard rubber, providing a stable film and additional protection for the heel and ankle. The design inspiration is derived from the traditional design of racing shoes. The exaggerated heel features nine dots echoing with the fine toe, representing the eternal spirit of Jordan.
