"Cao County's 'Five Measures in Place' Ensure the Smooth Progress of Cross-regional Operations during the 'Three Summers' -- Industry Information --"

by maling29 on 2011-08-26 09:55:40

The three-summer cross-regional operation has begun. The Caoxian agricultural machinery staff combined with the actual situation of this county, went deep into the grassroots, carried out five high-quality services to ensure everything is in place, gathered their hearts and efforts, and did their best to do a good job in this year's cross-regional operation.

First, the inspection of machinery is in place. Technical personnel were organized to go deep into the fields to help machine operators debug, inspect, and maintain machinery, ensuring that wheat harvesters are in good technical condition for cross-regional operations, making the equipment integrity rate reach above 95%.

Second, information service is in place. The mobile phones of agricultural machinery intermediaries, cooperative directors, team leaders, and cross-regional operation machine operators were included in a unified SMS system, which facilitates providing dynamic weather conditions and wheat harvest information to cross-regional operation machine operators, offering objective data for rational selection of cross-regional operation areas by machine operators; and contacting surrounding counties and cities in advance to understand the demand for machinery, promptly passing these information through mobile phone text messages to a wide range of machine operators, reducing blind mobility among machine operators, guiding tractors to orderly operations.

Third, technical training is in place. To ensure that summer cross-regional operations achieve good economic and social benefits, the County Agricultural Machinery School, Technology Promotion Station, and manufacturers work together to continue safety and operational technology training for cross-regional operation machine operators. Adhering to the principle of learning for use, focusing on detailed explanations of the installation and debugging, operation and use, maintenance and repair, and troubleshooting of combine harvesters. The school uses practical textbooks and multimedia forms for teaching, with manufacturer technicians providing face-to-face, hands-on on-site guidance and practical operations, allowing them to more systematically and intuitively understand operational techniques.

Fourth, safety implementation is in place. Refining the cross-regional operation work plan, each responsible leader personally takes charge, decomposing and implementing tasks layer by layer, and formulating emergency response plans for various situations such as adverse weather, insufficient machinery, and emergencies, ensuring everything goes without a hitch. Personnel are dispatched to form an agricultural machinery safety task force for inspections, strengthening the supervision of agricultural machinery, strictly investigating unlicensed driving, drunk driving, and violations of regulations, eliminating agricultural machinery accident hazards, ensuring safe production of cross-regional operation agricultural machinery.

Fifth, logistics support is in place. The Caoxian Agricultural Machinery Bureau jointly arranged and deployed the rectification work of the agricultural machinery market with the Administration for Industry and Commerce, vigorously investigating counterfeit and substandard agricultural machinery products, cleaning up fake subsidy machinery定点 enterprises, and cracking down on illegal acts that harm farmers' interests, including straight hook knives series. Special rectifications were conducted on repair outlets and parts stores throughout the city, effectively purifying the agricultural machinery market. Meanwhile, agricultural machinery repair and parts outlets with high credibility, good technology, and strong service capabilities were selected as comprehensive service stations for three-summer agricultural machinery, fully stocked with supplies, providing machine repair, technical guidance, and parts supply services 24 hours a day for machine operators. Additionally, actively coordinating with oil companies to ensure sufficient fuel supply, facilitating the smooth progress of the three-summer cross-regional operations.

(An Han)
