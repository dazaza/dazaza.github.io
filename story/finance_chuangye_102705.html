Let's see what kind of wealth-building process suits you.

Title: If there really is reincarnation, and King Yama lets you choose, what animal would you choose to be reborn as?

1. Poodle

2. Persian Cat

3. Eagle

4. Elephant

1. Choose "Poodle"

Your work ability is steady and solid. Although you don't have much luck in speculative fortune, your advantage lies in knowing how to save. In your work, you are also the type who steadily builds a foundation, not easily achieving great accomplishments but also striving not to make mistakes. You will be a loyal and hardworking employee in the eyes of your boss, but you wouldn't make a good boss. By working hard and doing your part, combined with your naturally diligent and frugal nature, you can also accumulate a substantial amount of wealth.

2. Choose "Persian Cat"

You have good luck with speculative fortune. In your work, you tend to take unconventional approaches, always thinking about how to become rich overnight. Your mind is very agile and faster than others, unfortunately, most of your ideas are overly ambitious and impractical. Moreover, you usually only think about it without taking action. With a personality like yours, you also have a fearless heart, so why not boldly invest? Investment should not be just for fun; manage it carefully, and quickly accumulating wealth is not a dream.

3. Choose "Eagle"

You have strong analytical and logical abilities, along with sharp intuition, making it easy for you to grasp market trends and create miracles. However, you may currently be hindered by insufficient information, preventing you from fully utilizing your talents. Besides being a basketball genius, Jordan also had unseen basic training and effort. Your talent needs continuous honing. Read more related books, seek advice from experienced peers in the industry, and when your foundational skills are sufficient, there will be no worry about not rising to prominence and soaring high!

4. Choose "Elephant"

You don't like to work and are somewhat lazy and fond of eating without working. You often lack enthusiasm for work, live lazily, and only want a peaceful and comfortable life. Unless you were born with a silver spoon in your mouth and never have to worry about food and clothing, your life will be filled with many regrets. Wanting a comfortable life is not impossible, but while you're young, work hard and earn money with all your might. Your potential is not much worse than others. It's not too late to enjoy after securing a prominent position.
