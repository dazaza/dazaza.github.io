Never possessed, never lost

Online submission

In the depth of night, let thoughts drift, at this midnight when no one whispers and no one listens, muttering to oneself.

Hard to part with, just because it's not time to separate yet. I've been thinking all along: the rise and fall of fate, the blooming and falling of flowers, are all predetermined. There will be a day when we end what should end and start what should start. No one can force it, no one can resist it.

There are always some opportunities in life that cannot be judged as fair or unfair. Some people, no matter how much you think about them, you can't enter their inner world, even if you get closer, you can't hear their heart melody; some people, no matter how far away they are, you still occupy their whole heart.

Listen to some sad songs, like the sorrowful wind gently brushing past your ears. Everything becomes blurry, hazy as if from another world, unclear to see. Then you have to admit, some things have already gone out of sight, but there are still some things occupying our hearts, full, blocking the heart gate, no one can get in.

There have been too many expectations and desires, also tasting countless disappointments and sorrows, then realizing the more hope there is, the deeper the disappointment. I no longer think about the distant future, no longer design so-called beauty, too illusory, really, I just want every present moment. And I believe, in the space-time of life, there will always be some feelings and people that belong to oneself.

These increasingly aging years have lost the interest in applying makeup. Words have been criticized too much by others, losing the instinct to pick up the pen, really, this gradually lost inspiration was once full of passion for a person, and also due to this person's criticism, all elegance disappeared.

When old enough to no longer write words about love, then realize love ages easily, along with the gradually desolate mood...

At midnight, the silence is often broken by some uneasiness, some anxiety, even some people, yet no ripples or inspirations arise. Words in one's own hands have never matured, but have grown old too early.

Never thought about leaving a word or two for anyone, just this fleeting confusion, scribbling randomly, letting it flow high and low, ruthless through the years...
