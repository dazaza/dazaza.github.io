The Agricultural Committee and the Agricultural Machinery Center of Fushun City visited the agricultural machinery market in Tieling City -- Industry Information

by maling29 on 2011-08-26 09:56:18

In order to build a good Fushun agricultural machinery exhibition hall and better serve the vast number of agricultural machinery users and agricultural machinery manufacturing enterprises, and build a platform for the exchange, display, and transaction of agricultural machinery products, recently, Mr. Tang Yanchen, Director of the Agricultural Machinery Department of Fushun's Agriculture Committee, and Mr. Huang Wenzhong, Director of Fushun Agricultural Machinery Technology Promotion Center, along with two other colleagues, made an inspection tour to Tieling City’s large agricultural machinery market. Mr. Liu Dan, Manager of Tieling Agricultural Machinery Market, Mr. Yang Zhiguang, Director of Management Department of Tieling Agricultural Machinery Bureau, Ms. Zhao Guorong, also a Director, and Mr. Wang Gang, Director of Tieling Agricultural Machinery Center accompanied them during the investigation and research.

Manager Liu Dan gave a detailed introduction about the operation status and future development direction of Tieling's large agricultural machinery market. Covering an area of 50,000 square meters, this market has 22 agricultural machinery manufacturers currently settled in, selling most of the agricultural machinery products subsidized by the state and province, including disc-type rotary tiller series. On average, more than 400 farmers come every day to purchase agricultural machinery, with daily sales reaching over 200 units (sets), generating sales exceeding 5 million yuan. It has become the largest professional market in Liaoning Province's agricultural machinery industry. Director Yang specifically discussed Tieling's experiences and practices regarding agricultural machinery purchase subsidies and large agricultural machinery markets. Director Huang from Fushun Agricultural Machinery Center introduced the implementation plan for Fushun's agricultural machinery exhibition hall to his Tieling counterparts. The exhibition hall is an integrated place for displaying and selling national subsidy agricultural machinery tools, providing after-sales service, spare parts, and information services. It adopts a business model centered on four-in-one operations, including agricultural machinery tool sales, spare parts, after-sales service, and information feedback. It only operates national subsidy agricultural machinery tools, making it a tangible market with distinct characteristics, allowing farmers to see and touch when purchasing national subsidy agricultural machinery tools. Additionally, it offers on-site training, processing of purchase procedures, registration, insurance, etc. It has obvious advantages in promoting new and applicable agricultural machinery tools, enhancing the brand of national subsidy agricultural machinery tools, and the image of agricultural machinery manufacturing enterprises. It aims to create a platform for direct communication between agricultural machinery manufacturers and farmers, exploring new ways to demonstrate and promote new tools and technologies.

Director Tang from Fushun's Agricultural Machinery Department expressed gratitude for the warm reception by Tieling's Agricultural Machinery Bureau and congratulated on the many honors obtained at the provincial and municipal levels and the outstanding achievements in agricultural mechanization work. Director Huang from Fushun Agricultural Machinery Center stated that this visit to Tieling's large agricultural machinery market was enlightening and broadened their horizons. There are many highlights in Tieling's agricultural machinery work, especially the successful construction and operation of the large agricultural machinery market, which is worthy of learning and reference by Fushun's Agriculture Committee and Fushun's Agricultural Machinery Technology Promotion Center. He believed it would play a positive role in accelerating the development of agricultural mechanization and the construction of the agricultural machinery market in Fushun.

(From Zhi Rong)
