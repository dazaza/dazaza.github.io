Video: Outgoing female college student, great pole dance on the playground equipment

by shootero on 2011-06-15 13:31:29

China Jiangsu Network, June 14 (reported by Yuan Cheng) - When it comes to pole dancing, what is the first thing that comes to your mind? Many netizens might associate it with the sexy dances performed in nightclubs or adult clubs. But have you ever thought about seeing a sexy and hot pole dance show on a school playground? A screenshot of Sister Pole Dancer video.

On June 12, a video named "Outgoing female university students perform pole dance on the playground equipment" was uploaded online. This video, which was filmed by a mobile phone and lasted for 13 minutes and 58 seconds, has been viewed more than one million times and was wildly shared by netizens. The reporter found this video through search engines. From the video footage, we can see two girls walking back and forth near the parallel bars on the playground. They are rhythmically twisting their bodies as if they are dancing to the music. One girl wearing a white T-shirt and a purple short skirt used the vertical column of the parallel bars to make some sexy poses, which stirred up excitement among the boys filming the video. In the video, the girl in the short skirt and the girl in the floral dress kept exchanging ideas. The girl in the short skirt kept demonstrating to the girl in the floral dress, as if teaching her how to practice. During the dance, the large-scale dance moves of the two girls made the male onlookers from the dormitory very excited. Some of them even gave comments and explanations, and even triggered whistles from other dormitory boys. After a sexy and passionate dance, the two girls left the scene with bags in hand.

Netizens who watched the video left their impressions. Netizen Jie23 said: "There are still people like this, disgusting!" Another netizen said: "The boys shooting the video aren't good people either." Some netizens analyzed from a professional perspective and concluded: "They danced pretty well."

In the past, pole dancing similar to erotic dances could still be seen in some dance halls with heavy erotic elements. Dancers use their flexibility and sexiness to perform suggestive movements around the pole. Therefore, in many people's minds, pole dancing is a form of entertainment in Western adult venues exclusively for adults to enjoy. Nowadays, pole dancing has evolved into a fashionable fitness and weight loss exercise, especially popular among female white-collar workers in Europe and America. The modernized version of pole dancing has gradually been accepted by people amidst controversy. As for whether the two pole dancers who appeared on campus are professional dancers or students, it remains unknown.
