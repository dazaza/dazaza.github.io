Mu Hanqiu’s lecture today > How to treat rhinitis:

1. Timely Treatment

When an acute upper respiratory infection occurs, pay attention to thorough treatment as early as possible. If you suspect you have rhinitis, you can go to the ENT department of the hospital for examination and confirmation. Additionally, break bad habits such as picking your nose or trimming nasal hair. Eat fewer spicy and stimulating foods, and avoid blowing your nose too forcefully.

2. Strengthening Constitution

In spring, go to bed early and get up early, exercise appropriately. Keep the room well-ventilated. At the same time, pay attention to increasing the intake of nutritious substances, eat more vegetables and fruits appropriately.

3. Cold Water Nasal Rinse

First, gently blow out the mucus from your nose, then rinse your nose with cold water. This not only enhances the disease resistance of the nasal mucosa but also helps clear bacteria and viruses hidden in the nose. In practice, rinsing your nose several times a day is very effective in preventing and treating rhinitis.
