Uncontrollable longing

I thought I could get used to living alone; I thought I could take good care of myself; I thought that being free from family ties would allow me to be carefree. I was wrong. The uncontrollable longing, like the floating catkins outside the door, tells the mood of the sky. Despite being busy at work, I still don't want to do it. When my mind is idle, I can't help but think about all the good things in the past...

When we fought, and I didn't eat, you cooked for me in different ways; even when I refused, you fed me bite by bite, letting me be capricious while insisting on filling my stomach. I said, don't be so good to me, otherwise I will not be able to let you go. Yes, I have already been unable to give up your goodness. You are not excellent at all, and sometimes if it weren't for life's pressures, you could stop striving. More often, I feel frustrated with you, but once a choice is made, one has to pay all the costs for their choice. Of course, many people regret their choices, because during painful times, they forget all the happiness they once had. We've known each other for years, and I don't regret rejecting others because of you; I also don't regret excluding others from my heart because of you. I fell in love, unreasonably and baselessly, despite most people's misunderstanding. Only Xiao Xiao, when I told her: "I think we can't separate, I'm doomed by him," she replied: "That's good!" In the past, I looked forward to a beautiful wedding, but now, I only hope that if we get together, we receive sincere blessings from those around us. I don't know whether the future holds fortune or misfortune, but if we don't dare to explore, no one will ever know, as the future is intangible and invisible.

I don't know if I can truly trust anyone anymore. Some small words can trigger a lot of suspicion in me. I dislike this version of myself. I dislike not trusting people, doubting the beauty and ugliness, good and evil of this world. Experience always makes one lose and gain, slowly losing oneself in the ups and downs, wrapping oneself tightly, not allowing anyone to hurt me, nor allowing myself to trust anyone. I don't care about the future, I just want to follow my heart, regardless of right or wrong, truth or falsehood. Neither you nor I will regret anything that happens in the future, okay?

I should congratulate you on resuming your original position, hoping that everything goes as you wish, rising step by step! I will keep waiting for the end of this job here. I dislike giving up halfway on promises made to others. There are still many responsibilities on my shoulders. I've told you, when I am free of burdens, I want to enjoy life with you effortlessly. This day won't take too long. I don't want to understand thoroughly, in fact, I shouldn't ask too much. Everyone has their own secrets deep in their hearts, and these secrets can only be known by oneself. I am like this, so I have no right to dig out the secrets hidden in your heart, right? Let all the past be erased by wind and rain, let life continue, let life shine, let you and me be brilliant!

The uncontrollable longing, the uncontrollable tears streaming down my face, the uncontrollable urge to call you back, just to hear your voice, just to hear you ask: "Have you eaten?" Without you, half of my circle is missing, and my life has lost its rhythm. I've gotten used to everything about you, but now, it's a city for one person. Both you and I need to re-adapt to a life without you or me forming a complete circle. We both take good care of ourselves. When we have time and miss each other, we send greetings through the wireless waves, listen to the sound of missing each other, and listen to the greetings hanging in our hearts, okay?

I think, one day, if you read these words of mine, you will surely cry. There are too many heartfelt words here. But, will there be such a day? I think, no matter how time changes, even if we grow old together, you probably won't come to read these heartfelt words of mine. Only heaven and earth know how strong the bond between you and me is. We just feel that the two of us are quietly savoring the taste of missing each other.
