I don't know if you have the same experience as JarryJarry, you suddenly want to listen to a song, but you don't know the name of the song, you don't even remember the lyrics, you just vaguely remember, or you have heard people humming the melody of one or two sentences. So we can't go on Google or Baidu to search for MP3 downloads of songs to listen to. What should I do?

Try the service Jarry is going to tell you about today: Midomi.

image

midomi is a tool that searches for music based on your voice. Sing, hum, or whistle to instantly find your favorite tunes and connect to communities that share your musical interests. You can contribute songs to the database by singing in any language or in any genre within midomi's online recording studio. The next time anyone searches for this song, your performance could be the first result! You can also create your own profile on midomi, sing your favorite songs, share them with your friends, and let other midomi users know you. You can listen to others sing, rate them, view their pictures, send them messages, buy original music, and more.

The core of midomi is built on MARS (multimodal adaptive recognition system = Composite Adaptive Recognition System), which enables functional applications that we have never achieved before. Even Google, Baidu music search does not have such a function. There is never a shortage of new applications on the Internet, and there are more applications that you can't think of, or even applications that you can't think of, waiting for you to discover...
