If she is a fish, she can love him for 7 seconds.

by meimei234 on 2012-03-06 15:12:04

If she were a fish, she could love him for seven seconds.

Her mood has been bad recently. She smiles at everyone during the day, but when she goes home at night, she just wants to cry. It's not that she wants to complain about anything; it's just that she doesn't get understood, and her whole heart feels shattered. She feels like the love in her heart is drifting further and further away. She doesn't know if it's her who has changed or if it's society that has changed. There are so many differences between the past and now...

She really wants to find a place, lock herself up, and feel so disheartened. Things aren't going well at work either. Maybe she feels like she's really stupid and can't do anything right. She has tried so hard, but she always makes mistakes. Why is she so dumb?

She wants to become someone who forgets everything and isn't so troubled. She wants to cry but doesn't know why. She just feels so suppressed that she wants to cry. It's drizzling outside, as if her heart is shedding tears. She wants to cry but doesn't know why. In this unfamiliar city, she feels helpless, like the world might accidentally swallow her up... She has to bear so many things she doesn't want to on her own. The loneliness in her heart is something she can't express to anyone because she has no friends here. Her husband's love died when she was coldly placed on the operating table. No one knows how much pain she feels...

When she was feeling helpless, he appeared and started chatting with her. Maybe it was coincidental, but their relationship became a bit strange. When she didn't see him, she missed him. When she had free time, she wanted to send him a text message. Her mood improved a bit.

Women are easily satisfied. As long as you treat them well, they will treat you well too and lean on you. If you give them a little more care, they'll lose themselves completely...

He sent her text messages every day, saying he missed her. Her gloomy mood brightened up. Every day, she looked forward to his call. Does he know that she misses him?

In this unfamiliar city, they met without any beginning. Even though they knew there wouldn't be a result, she still missed him and wanted to be with him. She didn't care about the future; she only cared about having him now.

Maybe it was loneliness. When she saw him, she wanted him to hold her. Gradually, she started to depend on him a bit. Because they were together, she was happy. She thought happiness meant being together...

When she missed him, she put him in her heart. Memories are like a river, continuous and unbroken...

She knows their relationship is not transparent and would vanish instantly under the sunlight...

But liking someone really has no reason... Ignoring everything... Just like this... Like a drug addiction, unable to quit... Continuously spreading...

Today, her mood was really bad. Right in front of her, he chatted with a young girl. She wanted to scold him. Was he trampling on her last bit of self-respect? Thinking about it made her heart ache...

She didn't want to talk to him online at night, she was too heartbroken. But then she thought, what does it matter? She's not his wife. Why should she care? He talked to her, but she didn't want to talk back. When she was angry, she couldn't pretend not to be. It seemed like she really started to care about him. She became a bit selfish, a bit jealous. He told her, "I just want to make you angry so I can see that you care."

She left for a week, and she missed him a lot. He kept calling her, saying he missed her. She knew she wasn't his wife, just someone he liked to have an ambiguous relationship with... A passionate kind of impulse. When this passion fades, that's probably the end of their relationship. In the end, they might not even be friends anymore.

Women are emotional beings. They always fall in love and can't let go. For love, sometimes they even lose their self-respect. Why? This kind of love makes her so fragile...

Neither of them dared to make any promises because they both knew there was no future.

One day, he came to her store. She was chatting online with one of his buddies, and he got upset. He didn't respond when she talked to him and checked her chat records. He started interfering in her life, but actually, she wasn't mad. This showed that he cared about her...

She doesn't know how many women he has been with before. She only knows that she's not the last one. In this month, she regarded herself as his wife. She only hoped that during the time they spent together, there would be no regrets, only happiness.

She loves him, even though he's bad, even though he's just playing around. She doesn't care. She doesn't expect to be with him for long; she just hopes that she's the one by his side right now.

She loves so humbly. Being like this really makes her feel sorry towards her husband, but she can't control herself... All those feelings are left with him... She's not the third party because she doesn't want to destroy his family. She just wants the warmth of being with him...

She likes his understanding nature. She values him a lot and regards this man as her confidant and the only person she can rely on.

Even if they separate in the future, it means the edge of their fate has ended. She won't regret it, never in her life. Learn to slowly let go. They are each other's passersby. Once the passion fades, they should part ways... Whenever she hears that song, "Be My Woman, Don't Regret," she remembers the sweet feeling when he first sang it to her. She wrote all this because it hasn't ended yet, so there's no conclusion yet...

I think every woman yearns for a man who cares about her and has a passionate romance. Although they may appear reserved, their hearts are fervent... Once true love comes, it becomes uncontrollable...

They went out with a few friends. The two of them were together, owning each other. Their love was so genuine that day and night, they held hands and embraced each other, very sweet. She just wanted to tell him that loving him was not a regret...

The arrival of dawn means parting. Even if she doesn't want to, she won't say it. She really wishes she could love him like a fish for seven seconds. Someone told her that a fish's memory lasts only seven seconds. After seven seconds, it forgets everything from the past, and everything becomes new again.

So in that small fish tank, it never gets bored because after seven seconds, every place it swims through becomes new again. It can forever live in freshness.

She'd rather be a fish. After seven seconds, she'd forget everything, including the people she met and the things she did, all disappearing like smoke. But she's not a fish, so she can't forget the person she loves, the bitterness of longing, or the pain of missing him...

A fish can't see its loved one crying, but it can feel the other's heartache. In this life, we can never be free like a fish. So he can't feel her heartache when he leaves her, just like he can't sense her love for him...

How long can you love someone?

If she were a fish, she could love him for seven seconds...
