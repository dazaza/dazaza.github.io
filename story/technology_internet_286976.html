Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 30th - (Reporter Yongsheng Wang) Today, a certain evening paper and several portal websites reported that the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC) has grounded all MD-11 cargo aircraft nationwide. In the morning, a reporter from the Legal Evening News confirmed with relevant authorities at the CAAC. The official stated that the MD-11 cargo planes have not been grounded, nor has the CAAC issued such a notice. The news report is inaccurate.

The "Black Box" Has Been Found, Cause Under Investigation

This official informed the reporter that once there are any new developments or investigation results, they will be announced to the public in a timely manner.

Tracking the crashed plane at Pudong: The black box has been found, the cause of the accident is still under investigation.

Today, some media reported that the CAAC has grounded all MD-11 cargo aircraft across the country. Regarding this claim, an official from the CAAC clearly stated that the CAAC has never issued such a notice; it's impossible for the planes to be grounded without reason.

An official from the CAAC told reporters that the black box of the Zimbabwean cargo plane that broke apart and caught fire at Shanghai Pudong Airport has been found. However, the decoding of the black box data cannot yet definitively determine the cause of the accident.

Background Link: At 8:14 AM on November 28th, a Zimbabwe Airlines MD-11 cargo plane overran the runway during takeoff at Shanghai Pudong Airport. The plane disintegrated and caught fire. There were seven crew members on board. Currently, four are injured, and three have died.
