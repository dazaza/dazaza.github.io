An epileptic seizure (epileptic seizure) refers to the clinical phenomenon caused by abnormal and excessive hyper-synchronous discharges of brain neurons. Its characteristic is sudden and transient symptoms. Since the location of abnormally discharging neurons in the brain varies, there are various manifestations. It can be motor, sensory or autonomic nerve with or without changes in consciousness or alertness.
