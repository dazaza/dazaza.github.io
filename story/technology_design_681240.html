The man refused to pay maintenance and was detained for beating his 76-year-old mother in the street.

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-02-28 12:05:52

The photo shows: Speaking of unfilial son, Mrs. Ming's left eye was so swollen that it was difficult to open, and she was heartbroken.

Chutian Metropolis Daily (Weibo) report (reporter Guan Qianyu): Dr. Li from a public hospital in Shiyan City ignored his parents for 12 years. When the parents came to ask for maintenance fees, he actually beat up his 76-year-old mother on the street in front of his workplace. It is reported that the 39-year-old Dr. Li has been put on hold by his unit. The police administratively detained him for 12 days and fined him 500 yuan.

Shock: Unfilial son slapped his old mother in the street

"He was too cruel. As soon as we met, before we could say two words, he slapped me twice in the face..." On the morning of July 17, at Wudang Road Police Station in Shiyan City, 76-year-old Mrs. Ming cried painfully.

I saw Mrs. Ming's left face was red and swollen, and her left eye was so swollen that it was difficult to open. Her husband looked serious and kept wiping her tears.

Mrs. Ming introduced that she and her husband were both 76 years old and lived in Zaoyuan Village, Leigu Town, Zhushan County. On July 13, the elderly couple went to Shiyan to find their son Li Mou. They wanted to see their 8-year-old grandson whom they had never met, and also asked their son for 300 yuan per month maintenance fee for each person. When contacting their son, their son didn't want to meet them. At around 7 p.m. on July 16, her son entrusted a colleague to tell her to come to the gate of the hospital. She met her son on the road at the gate of the hospital. After just saying two words, her son suddenly slapped her twice on the left face, knocking her down to the ground. Before she could get up, her son hit her again. She immediately shouted for help.

The surrounding people quickly called the police. Li Mou fled the scene. Officers from Wudang Road Police Station arrived at the scene, took care of the two elderly people at the station, and intervened in the investigation.

Cause: A marriage caused resentment between mother and son

Why did the biological son hit his mother so hard? "This is all because of his marriage." Mrs. Ming introduced that she had three children, the elder and second were girls, and Li Mou was the youngest son, who was regarded as a treasure since childhood and was relatively sensible when he was young. He used to do everything at home like finding pig grass and chopping wood. After graduating from university and getting a job, he dated a girlfriend from Shiyan. His family was dissatisfied with his girlfriend and advised them to break up. Li Mou insisted on marrying her. "Since then, he has changed." Mrs. Ming said that in the past 12 years after marriage, Li Mou has never seen his parents and has not given them a penny. His grandson is 8 years old, and the elders have never seen him once. "During festivals, when he called us, he always cursed us."

Progress: The rebellious son was detained, and the elderly will sue

After the incident, the police first mediated the matter, hoping to resolve family conflicts. Since Li Mou showed no remorse, and Mrs. Ming disagreed with the mediation, the mediation ultimately failed.

On July 24, the Shiyan police made an administrative penalty decision, administratively detaining Li Mou for 12 days and fining him 500 yuan.

After this incident happened, the hospital decided after research to put him on standby.

I learned that Mrs. Ming is currently receiving treatment at a hospital in Shiyan. After examination, the doctor believed that Mrs. Ming suffered retinal damage, posterior dislocation of the lens, and severe vision loss. The vision of her injured left eye has dropped to 0.05, and there is soft tissue damage on her face. After examination, Mrs. Ming had a hematoma in the left brain, and the hospital was organizing experts for consultation.

Mrs. Ming expressed that after she recovered and was discharged from the hospital, she would file a lawsuit through the court to make her son bear the legal obligation of support.

On July 27, Zhang Xingzhong, deputy director of the Office of the Aging Committee of Shiyan City, learned about this matter and said that if Mrs. Ming legally defends her rights through litigation, the office will apply for legal aid for the elderly.

Postscript:

As the saying goes, family harmony leads to prosperity in everything, and among all virtues, filial piety comes first. Parents' interference in their children's marriage was certainly wrong, but the son used this as an excuse to fail to fulfill his duty of support for more than ten years, and even beat up his white-haired mother who came to ask for maintenance fees on the street, which is retaliatory. A few slaps on his mother's face hurt his mother's heart, not only breaking the bond of affection, but also damaging the social reputation of a chief physician, leading to his punishment and casting a shadow over his career. We hope that the son can untie the knot as soon as possible, let affection return, after all, blood ties are thicker than water.
