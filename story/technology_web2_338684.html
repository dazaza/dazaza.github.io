There are tricks to choosing a wedding dress_ Shenyang Photography Studio

by fj0914738 on 2010-04-01 18:11:09

In order to become the center of attention on their wedding day, many brides-to-be are very cautious when choosing a wedding dress, hoping to carefully select one that best highlights their beauty. However, when selecting, one must have a deep understanding of their body's strengths and weaknesses, aiming primarily at amplifying the strengths and modifying the flaws.

★ For those with poor lower body lines (large hips, thick legs, etc...), choose mainly puffy skirt styles to conceal these areas; avoid fish-tail designed skirts.

★ For those with a protruding belly or wide waist, consider gowns with inverted triangular designs at the waist or high-waisted doll-style dresses, which can shift and blur visual focus.

★ For those with fleshy arms, consider dresses with matching shawl designs or find a coordinating shawl to serve as an elegant accessory while covering the arms. This will not look out of place in cooler autumn weather. Avoid strapless or too thin-strapped dresses to prevent accentuating flaws through strong contrasts.

★ For those who are too slim, it’s best to avoid low-cut or highly revealing styles. Opt for high-necked or long-sleeved Victorian-style gowns to enhance grace and presence.

★ For those with overly full upper bodies, choose simple, understated elegant cuts. Overly intricate designs may give off a bulky impression, especially if the skirt is fitted, which could emphasize an unbalanced appearance.

The above methods for selecting a gown based on body type actually just require paying more attention to personal characteristics, seeking advice from friends and professional stylists, usually resulting in finding a beautiful gown that suits oneself. Do not blindly adhere to a specific design based on personal dreams while completely ignoring how it complements your figure.

★ For those with fair skin, there aren’t many issues with dress color coordination, making it more convenient with no significant problems.

★ For those with darker skin tones, consider bright-colored dresses that can create a contrast, highlighting a healthy sensuality. Like how black people often stand out in vibrant streetwear, this can emphasize a striking and eye-catching beauty. However, the prerequisite is having evenly healthy and glossy dark skin, allowing such effects to perfectly complement one’s captivating traits.

★ For those with sallow yellowish skin tones, avoid colors like purple, navy blue, cinnamon, lotus, and copper gold. Purple and navy blue can make already non-white skin appear duller and more waxen. Cinnamon, lotus, and copper tones, unless paired with fair skin, can blend into the naturally yellow undertones of East Asian skin, failing to highlight any focal points and even causing difficulty distinguishing between skin and dress from a distance.

Besides carefully selecting a pristine white wedding gown for happiness, the formal wear chosen for banquets should not be overlooked. Even if the combination of dress design and one's figure is perfect, picking an unsuitable color can prevent one from shining brightly at the wedding banquet, which would be regrettable.

When wearing formal attire, never overlook small details. After selecting an elegant romantic princess-like gown, remember that the gown requires various accessories. Typically, the veil matches the gown, so there won't be any significant jarring differences.

For accessories, I suggest focusing on key accents. In recent years, fashion trends lean towards understated luxury emphasizing elegance. Therefore, if the in-laws hope the bride wears all the jewelry gifted by relatives and friends, good communication is essential. Quantity doesn't equate to beauty; too many dazzling ornaments mixed together—gold, silver, K-gold, pearls—can be overwhelming and fail to shine individually.

As for headpieces worn during the banquet, try to match them with the gown's material or color scheme. The concept here is similar to jewelry pairing: quality over quantity, presenting a graceful, serene, and sweet demeanor. Therefore, don't neglect the final details of jewelry and headpieces, as they could undo all the effort put into selecting the gown and wedding dress.

Generally speaking, these details are crucial for a bride to leave a lasting impression on her wedding day. If you wish to be a radiant and beautiful bride, take the time to thoroughly research suitable gown styles for yourself, leaving behind the most perfect romantic memories.
