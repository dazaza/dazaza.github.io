Forum Promotion

The forum promotion mentioned here absolutely does not mean posting advertisements one by one in forums or adding the online store address to the signature and then spamming wildly. That wastes energy and the effect isn't good either. Forum administrators can delete all your posts with just a few mouse clicks, block your ID, and the impact brought by such actions is negative, like sending junk emails which people detest. Once, I saw a website promotion method on a certain forum that was much more sophisticated.

It was a small website providing job hunting, employment, and learning materials. Their publicity personnel turned various articles on the website into link forms, categorized them well, and posted them on different forums. This way, it could provide convenience to some people who need it while not being deleted due to overly direct advertisements. The promotional effect was undoubtedly better. If these materials were posted in sections of forums that match their positioning, they would definitely attract many clicks.

Search Engine Promotion Methods

Search engine promotion refers to the method of promoting online stores using search engines, classified directories, and other network tools with online information retrieval functions. Since the basic form of search engines can be divided into web crawler-based search engines (simply called search engines) and manually classified directory-based search engines (simply called classified directories), the forms of search engine promotion also correspondingly include methods based on search engines and methods based on classified directories. The former includes search engine optimization, keyword advertising, bidding rankings, fixed rankings, content-based positioning advertisements, and many other forms, while the latter mainly involves website registration in appropriate categories of classified directories. With the further development and changes in the form of search engines, other forms of search engines have also appeared, but most are based on these two forms.

Search engine promotion methods can be divided into many different forms, common ones include: registering in free classified directories, registering in paid classified directories, search engine optimization, keyword advertising, keyword bidding rankings, content-based positioning advertisements, etc.

From the current development trend, the position of search engines in online marketing remains important and is recognized by more and more enterprises. The ways of search engine marketing are also continuously evolving, so it is necessary to choose suitable ways of search engine marketing according to environmental changes.

Email Promotion Method

Using email as the main promotion means, common methods include e-publications, member newsletters, professional service providers' email advertisements, etc.

User-permitted Email marketing differs from spam. Permission-based marketing has obvious advantages over traditional promotion methods or non-permission-based Email marketing, such as reducing ad disturbances to users, increasing the accuracy of potential customer targeting, enhancing customer relationships, and improving brand loyalty. You can let your customers leave their email addresses and, after obtaining their consent, send them some product information, discount information, etc.

Resource Cooperation Promotion Method

Through exchanging links, advertisements, content cooperation, user resource cooperation, etc., mutual promotion goals are achieved between online stores with similar objectives. The most commonly used resource cooperation method is the online store link strategy, utilizing partner resources to promote each other through website visits.

Each online store can own its own resources, which can be expressed as a certain amount of traffic, valuable content and functions, online advertising space, etc. Utilizing website resources to cooperate with partners achieves the purpose of shared resources and jointly expanding benefits. Among these resource cooperation forms, exchanging links is the simplest form of cooperation. Surveys show that it is also one of the effective ways to promote new websites.

Exchanging links, also known as reciprocal links, is a simple form of cooperation between online stores with certain complementary advantages. That is, both parties place the other party's online store LOGO or online store name on their own online store and set a hyperlink to the other party's online store, allowing users to discover their own online store from the cooperative online store, achieving the goal of mutual promotion.

The role of exchanging links is mainly reflected in several aspects: gaining traffic, increasing user impressions during browsing, gaining advantages in search engine rankings, and increasing the credibility of visitors through recommendations from cooperative online stores. Exchanging links also has a deeper meaning than whether direct effects can be achieved. Generally speaking, each online store tends to link to other high-value online stores. Therefore, gaining links from other online stores means gaining recognition from partners and similar online stores in the same field.

Information Release Promotion Method

Releasing relevant online store promotion information on other websites where potential users may visit, utilizing the opportunity for users to obtain information on these websites to achieve the purpose of website promotion. Websites suitable for these information releases include online yellow pages, classified ads, forums, blog websites, supply-demand platforms, industry websites, etc.

Information release is one of the commonly used methods for free promotion, especially in the early days of Internet development when the volume of online information was relatively small. Often, satisfactory results could be achieved through information release. However, with the explosive growth of online information, the role that this method relying on free information release can play has been gradually decreasing. At the same time, with the emergence of more effective online store promotion methods, the importance of information release in the commonly used methods of online store promotion has also significantly decreased. Therefore, relying solely on mass-sending free information has little value. However, some targeted and specialized information can still attract significant attention, especially when these pieces of information are released on highly relevant platforms.

Online Advertising Promotion Method

Online advertising is one of the commonly used online marketing strategies, playing a significant role in online brand promotion, product promotion, and website promotion. Common forms of online advertising include: Banner ads, keyword ads, classified ads, sponsored ads, Email ads, etc. BANNER ads rely on web pages as media; keyword ads belong to a form of search engine marketing, and Email ads are a type of permission-based Email marketing. Clearly, online advertising itself cannot exist independently and needs to be combined with various online tools to realize the function of information transmission. Therefore, it can also be considered that online advertising exists within various online marketing tools, just in different specific forms.

Using online advertising for online store promotion has advantages such as a wide range of selectable online media, diverse forms, strong applicability, and timely placement, making it suitable for any stage of online store launch and operation.

Strive to establish links with thriving online stores to increase traffic and enhance search engine weight.

Regularly offer special price items, similar to daily specials in restaurants.

For example, from Monday to Wednesday, sell an item originally priced at 150 yuan for 88 yuan, limited to the first 20 buyers (the quota is up to you, it's just a gimmick to stimulate purchasing desire).

Gunmen, soft articles, stir up posts on major forums and websites.

Soft articles are essentially soft advertisements, attracting attention.

Flash Promotion

For example, when a new MP3 or sketch comes out, turn it into a Flash animation and add a small advertisement at the bottom. Given the wide circulation of well-designed Flash animations, this method can be very effective. Alternatively, you can contact existing Flash creators to advertise and pay a small fee.

Link Promotion

Regardless of the method (QQ, MSN, forum, MAIL, etc.), publicize the link to your online store. Alternatively, hire someone to help promote it. For every click on this link that leads to a visit to your online store, give them a certain commission or store coupons. Shoppers referred to your online store will also receive corresponding discounts.

Keyword Optimization

When people shop, they usually start by searching for related products. When setting product names, design them with commonly used keywords to allow buyers to easily find your store and products through multiple searches. Use terms like "lowest price" or "special offer" and describe products as detailed as possible.

Data Promotion

If your online store sells certain products, write related product data and trivia. You can include your online store address in the article or set it in your signature for promotion.

In-store Event Promotion

Analyze the buyers of your online store.

Based on previous buyers, analyze the characteristics of these customers, such as their age, occupation, interests, etc. You can conduct corresponding promotions in forums and places they frequently visit. Promote according to the different characteristics of buyers. Moreover, actively seek business opportunities and stock up accordingly. Success is secondary; the key is to have an active mindset.

Additionally, always keep an eye on "buyer purchase request" information, as there might be buyers looking for what you sell. Even if we don’t have what the buyer is looking for, we can still find many potential customers. Add them as friends and recommend products similar to what they’re seeking; it’s easy to make sales.

Promotion and Publicity

Make full use of each holiday for promotion and publicity. During holidays, there are large numbers of searches related to the holiday. Therefore, everyone can focus on keywords and also take advantage of holidays to carry out promotional activities, which will bring more customers. Additionally, when shipping goods, include some personal business cards or promotional materials.

Making Promotional Gifts

Print your online store's logo and URL on T-shirts and give them away as gifts to your online customers. This is also a great way to promote. Remember, T-shirts should be made of medium to high-quality material; otherwise, they might end up as rags after just one wear, which wouldn't be worth it! As the weather gets cooler, consider making hats or cups as other promotional gifts.

Joint Promotion

Pure friend links have limited effects. However, if several sellers collaborate and conduct joint promotions, they can achieve different results. Try to find stores with complementary natures. For example, a photography book specialty store collaborating with a digital camera specialty store.

Reasonable Use of Auctions

Auctions are divided into single-item auctions and multi-item auctions (Dutch auctions). Taobao specifically sets up a dedicated section for auctions on the homepage, where buyers can see it immediately upon opening the homepage. Choosing the auction method means buyers can easily find us. Also, since auction prices are generally low, they tend to attract buyers easily, making auctions very helpful for increasing store popularity.

Have you noticed that Taobao actually takes care of sellers in many ways? They introduce many promotional activities. Occasionally, some merchants may offer one or two items for a one-yuan auction. This should be done according to your capabilities. Furthermore, once you do a one-yuan auction, prepare for losses. After a successful bid, ensure quality delivery regardless of the loss. A one-yuan auction aims to gain positive reviews and popularity, so avoid bad reviews, which would be counterproductive.

Use Reviews to Promote Your Store

Sometimes, we see very simple reviews from sellers, either saying "Good buyer" or nothing at all. Actually, we can fully utilize this opportunity to speak. Many buyers check the seller's credit before purchasing. So, we should seize this chance to advertise. All my reviews say: "Great buyer, hope to see you again at Youyou Toy Shop. Regular customers will surely get discounts." Or "New items will be added regularly, come and take a look."

Participate in Activities

Many C2C websites regularly hold promotional events. Always stay updated with these activities, keep an eye on the latest dynamics, and strive to utilize these events well, such as essay contests, charity sales. You can sponsor prizes or donate to let more people know about you.

Offline Promotion

The offline promotion methods introduced in this article require some cost. Online store owners should act within their capabilities.

Gift Advertisement

The following gifts are distributed for free in bustling areas with high foot traffic. Eco-friendly bags, because they are now used frequently, with low production costs, and printed with online store-related information. Calendars and wall calendars, printed with online store information. Mouse pads, printed with online store information and distributed for free, given out freely in internet cafes and office buildings.

Campus Agents

Nowadays, many college students have spare time, no base salary required, only commissions, letting them promote without restriction, both online and offline. Give them free business cards (with titles like Campus General Agent for the online store) and a set of materials, letting them promote and sell, earning money while also training their marketing skills effectively. Word spreads among student unions.

Street Questionnaire Surveys

Hire part-timers for surveys, with promotion being the main goal.

Before the official promotion, conduct an online survey.

Targeted surveys, like piloting on Xici, focusing on products and usage issues, safety concerns, conducting surveys and answering questions. In the process of answering questions, subtly promote your online store.

Roadside Lightbox Ads

Primarily station lightboxes, bus ads, bus body ads, internal cross-banner small ads, etc.

Hire a few students to dress up as popular cartoon characters like Superman, Ultraman, Happy Sheep, Gray Wolf, etc., in busy downtown areas.

Of course, they should have online store ads on them, and tourists may take photos with them, resulting in good promotional effects. Media outlets are always looking for news, and they love stories like this. Once it's on the newspaper, the traffic will come.

Internet Cafe Collaboration

Collaborate with internet cafes, paying a small fee to set the default homepage of browsers to the online store homepage. Find 30-50 internet cafes, and with more people surfing the net there, the traffic will increase.

Place a Small Standing Sign at the Front Desk or Cashier Counter of Restaurants, Hotels, Leisure Venues

This small standing sign should include a brief introduction of the online store and the URL. Guests may glance at it while consulting or paying.

Purified Water Ads

The customer base for purified water is extensive, including office buildings, hospitals, schools, government agencies, families, etc. It's not hard to check out an ad while pouring water, right? We can collaborate with manufacturers, sticking ads on the barrels and paying a small fee.

Print Business Cards for Wide Distribution

You might say, since most promotions are online and people never meet face-to-face, isn't making business cards a waste of cost? Wrong! Although the store is online, you are still you in real life, and mailing packages happens in reality. Design a unique business card, print your contact information and the store's address on it, and include a few in your mailings. Due to its exquisite design, buyers won't discard it easily. If there's an opportunity, they will definitely show it to others, which is equivalent to helping you promote.

Distribute Business Cards or Flyers

After printing business cards, it's time to distribute them. Printing flyers is now cheap, costing just a few cents per piece. Print them and distribute them in places where potential customers gather, such as near schools. Different products target different customers, so this strategy should be applied in the right place.

Tell Your Family and Friends

You've opened an online store. Do your family members know? Do your classmates and friends know? Be sure to tell them as soon as possible. Leave messages on alumni records and share the joy of opening a store with your classmates.

Organize Relatives and Friends to Help Promote

Since your relatives and friends already know about your online store, ask them to help promote it.

Having grown up, you've met quite a few friends, haven't you? From elementary school to junior high, then to high school and university, you've had many classmates, haven't you? So, mobilize them and ask them to help us promote...
