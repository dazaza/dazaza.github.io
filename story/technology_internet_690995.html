[Qingdao News] How to apply for the use of house repair funds procedures

by qssqwe215 on 2012-03-05 13:42:23

The housing maintenance fund in Qingdao has been levied for many days. Currently, 43 outlets of Qingdao Bank in the urban area have simultaneously opened green channels. [The housing maintenance fund should be paid, but the problem is, if there are really issues with the house in the future, how do you apply to use these funds?] Ms. Wang, a citizen, asked. After paying the housing maintenance fund, how exactly do you apply to use it? For this, the reporter conducted an investigation and interview.

43 outlets have opened green channels

Starting from March 1st, the "Qingdao Housing Special Maintenance Fund Management Measures" (Trial) was officially implemented. Residential and non-residential houses within the seven districts of the city should pay the special housing maintenance fund, except for single-owner properties that do not share common areas or facilities with other houses. On March 1st, the city's housing maintenance fund payment system was successfully networked and debugged, and citizens began paying the housing maintenance fund.

In addition, Qingdao Bank has opened green channels for paying the housing maintenance fund at 43 outlets in the urban area, making it convenient for citizens to deposit, withdraw, and use the fund. It is understood that the real estate transaction counters of Qingdao Bank in various districts are currently the only one-stop counters in the city that can handle all 11 types of property certificate fees at the same time.

[We knew about this news before the Spring Festival, it's a good thing. But the amount is not small. The standard for paying the high-rise housing special maintenance fund is 133 yuan per square meter, and the standard for paying the multi-story housing special maintenance fund is 51 yuan per square meter. Calculating this way, we need to pay more than 8000 yuan for our house. We've owned the house for many years, all second-hand homes, and I'm unsure when it's appropriate to pay this fee. All I worry about now is how to use this money. Is the procedure very troublesome?] Ms. Wang, who lives in Sifang District, reflected to the reporter, [In the past, using the housing maintenance fund was quite troublesome.]

Four necessary procedures to follow

On March 2nd, regarding the issue of how to use the housing maintenance fund, the reporter conducted an investigation and interview. According to the introduction, based on the "Qingdao Housing Special Maintenance Fund Management Measures" (Trial), if the housing special maintenance fund is under the autonomous management of the owners, four procedures need to be followed: First, the owners' committee needs to formulate a usage plan; Second, the usage plan should be displayed prominently in the community for no less than 7 days and must be agreed upon by more than two-thirds of the owners involved, both in terms of area and number of households; Third, the owners' committee submits the usage plan to the housing special maintenance fund management institution for record-keeping, then the owners' committee holds the record-keeping proof to handle the transfer procedures at the designated bank account management bank; Finally, the owners' committee organizes and implements the usage plan.

(If the fund management institution manages the housing special maintenance fund, the process is more complicated), requiring seven procedures. The first two procedures are the same as those under the owners' autonomous management. In the third step, the property service enterprise submits the usage plan to the housing special maintenance fund management institution for review. The last procedure is, after the inspection is passed, the housing special maintenance fund management institution notifies the designated bank account management bank to disburse the remaining amount of payable fees.

Of course, in case of emergency situations, the procedure can be simplified, with five cases qualifying. Elevator malfunctions determined by relevant departments to endanger personal safety; falling exterior walls, roof leaks, etc., severely affecting the use and safety of the house; clogged drainage pipes, leaks affecting normal use; damaged fire protection facilities, with rectification notices issued by the fire department; other urgent situations endangering the safe use of the house and affecting the normal life of the owners.

Four types of expenses cannot be covered by the maintenance fund

In addition, the reporter learned through interviews that not all housing repair situations use the housing maintenance fund. Four types of expenses are excluded: legally required repairs and updates of shared parts and facilities of the house that should be borne by the construction unit or the construction contractor; legally required repairs and maintenance of water supply, electricity, gas, heating, communication, cable TV, broadband data transmission pipelines and facilities that should be borne by related professional operating units; repairs and maintenance costs that should be borne by the property service enterprise according to the property service contract; restoration costs due to human damage and other reasons that should be borne by the responsible parties.

To ensure the owners' right to know about the maintenance funds and enhance the transparency of their use, the fund management institution and the owners' committee should reconcile the accounts of the housing special maintenance fund with the designated bank account management bank at least once a year and announce it to the owners. It is also required that the fund management institution and the designated bank account management bank establish a housing special maintenance fund information system, allowing owners to check the use, value-added income, and balance of their individual accounts at any time.

For those who violate regulations and misappropriate the housing special maintenance fund, the municipal housing administrative department will recover the misappropriated housing special maintenance fund, confiscate illegal gains, and may impose a fine of up to twice the amount misappropriated; if a crime is constituted, criminal responsibility will be pursued against the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel. If a property service enterprise misappropriates the housing special maintenance fund and the circumstances are serious, in addition to being punished according to regulations, its qualification certificate will also be legally revoked.
