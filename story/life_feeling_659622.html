14 Gift-Giving Tips to Make Your Girlfriend Happy on Valentine's Day Without Sending Flowers

by liwuyou on 2012-02-06 14:40:13

Actually, sending flowers on Valentine's Day is a common practice. If you want to have a unique Valentine's Day, you might as well try the following 14 ways. Even if you don't send flowers, there are 14 romantic tricks for Valentine's Day:

1. Light candles or cover the lamp with a red gauze scarf while chatting. The advantage lies in the atmosphere. When the lighting is not good, both of you can only see each other, hehe!

2. Make an appointment at a flower shop and ask the flower sender to wait at the place where you and your partner are dating at 7 pm.

3. Meet on an uninhabited beach or beach and light a bonfire to roast potatoes. This method mainly focuses on the atmosphere.

4. Walk in the drizzle without an umbrella, nodding and smiling to people who are taking shelter from the rain on the side of the road. This is very romantic, I suggest you try it.

5. Memorize the constellations, invite him (her) to the top of the mountain and point out his (her) constellation. Then look at the starry sky and imagine, of course, even if you don't memorize it, you can randomly point to a beautiful star and make up a moving story. In this situation, girls are usually not too smart and have a certain tendency to follow blindly. In more cases, whatever you say, he will agree.

6. Sing old songs, the older the better. Let everyone feel familiar with each other. When everyone feels funny, the distance is slowly closing at this moment.

7. If you are going to profess your love to the other party, you can say like this: "I heard that there is a way to profess your love by drawing a heart on your palm. If the other party agrees, they also draw one." While saying this, use a red pen to draw one on your palm and put the red pen aside.

8. Prepare a wide cloth strip in advance and write words on it. When you are chatting happily in the coffee shop, suddenly become silent, take out the cloth strip from your pocket and shake it open. It can be written: "I am so happy to be with you!"

9. When you go on a date with him (her), stop the watch. When you part, look at the watch and say: "My time stops when I am with you, I hope the time is still 7 o'clock."

10. Use long matchsticks and insert them one by one vertically on the cake to spell out the other party's name, light them from the beginning and let them flicker to the end.

11. Bribe a restaurant and pay in advance. When you are chatting, the lights suddenly dim, and the waiter pushes out a birthday cake and sings the Happy Birthday song together.

12. Go to the wild for a lover's barbecue picnic.

13. Buy a book of Traditional Crosstalk Selections, and when you are lonely, read it together and often get unexpected happiness.

14. Learn from the ancients, seek plum blossoms in the snow.
