Promotion of Precious Metal Trader Training Course - Shenzhen Area

by bxgold147t on 2012-03-08 14:16:25

For thousands of years, gold has not only been a global currency, but also a commodity, an investment vehicle, and an adornment. Gold investment comes in various forms, ranging from gold coins to complex structured financial products. Investors can choose the most suitable investment channel according to their own needs and return forecasts. With the increasing prevalence of precious metal trading and investment, the market's requirements for the qualifications of industry professionals are also getting higher. In order to thoroughly implement the "State Council's Decision on Vigorously Developing Vocational Education", the Ministry of Labor's "Opinions on Further Doing a Good Job in Vocational Training Work", and the "Several Opinions on Promoting the Development of the Gold Market" [Yin Fa [2010] No. 211] issued by six national ministries, in response to the current lack of professional precious metal investment and trading talent in our country, the Precious Metal Industry Committee of the Chinese Vocational Education Society has proposed the establishment of the "Precious Metal Trader (Intermediate Level)" profession. This training course aims to help current employees and aspiring precious metal professionals understand the professional knowledge and ethics of the precious metal market, thereby promoting and standardizing the professional conduct of precious metal practitioners. During the vocational training process, in addition to theoretical and analytical knowledge, there is a greater emphasis on cultivating the quality and practical skills of professional talents, enabling students to truly master the skills of actual trading. The National Human Resources and Social Security Department's CETTIC has included the Precious Metal Trader in the national new vocational skills talent demonstration training project. It has now authorized the Xiamen Liang Gold Trading Co., Ltd., under its subsidiary Siming District Manager Training Center in Xiamen, to carry out CETTIC "Precious Metal Trader" talent training work in Fujian, Shanghai, and Shenzhen.

◆Training Location: Luohu District Talent Training Center, Shenzhen

◆Training Time: April 21, 22, 28, 29, 2012, with the exam on the evening of April 29.

◆Enrollment Conditions:

1. Possessing a junior college degree or above and having worked in related fields for more than 4 years

2. Possessing a bachelor's degree or above and having worked in related fields for more than 3 years

3. Possessing a secondary school diploma and having worked in related fields for more than 8 years

4. No academic qualification, but having worked in related fields for more than 10 years (Those who meet any of the above conditions can participate in vocational training)

◆Course Content: Formation mechanism of precious metal prices, basic knowledge of precious metal investment, information management technology for precious metal investment, chart pattern analysis of precious metals, domestic and international regulations and trading rules related to precious metal transactions, sales management, customer management, and maintenance in the financial services industry.

◆Certificate Issuance: A CETTIC "Precious Metal Trader (Intermediate Level)" vocational training certificate will be awarded to trainees who attend the training and pass the examination. This certificate can be queried on the China Vocational Training and Skills Appraisal Service Network and the Precious Metal Industry Network. According to relevant provisions of our country's "Vocational Education Law": this certificate serves as proof of employment for the trained personnel.
