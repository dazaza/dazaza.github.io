Ma Yun: Laiwang and WeChat are like ants against an elephant. Let's see how it goes.

by anonymous on 2013-11-16 16:10:04

Ma Yun, on the 25th in Hangzhou, when discussing the relationship between Laiwang and WeChat, said that the active competition between Alibaba and Tencent is beneficial to the prosperity of mobile internet.

"As for the game between Laiwang and WeChat, I want to see what Ma Huateng does next. I'm very happy to see his brows furrow, competition is a kind of fun," Ma Yun said.

A month ago, Alibaba launched its social tool "Laiwang", and the direct competition with WeChat became a hot topic.

WeChat is currently China's most active mobile internet social tool, many people believe its market leading position cannot be shaken, and the industry even predicts that WeChat will become the main entry point for mobile internet in the future.

Previously, Ma Yun sent a letter via WeChat, claiming that he would permanently close his WeChat account to show support for Laiwang.

In this open letter written to Ma Huateng, Ma Yun said that his "karma with WeChat has run out, and he will now fully support Laiwang, this immature child".

"Laiwang and WeChat, it's like ants gnawing at an elephant, let's see how it goes."

A week ago, Ma Yun required all employees to have a hundred friends on Laiwang, otherwise they wouldn't receive their year-end bonus. This internal incentive mechanism is said to have brought at least 5 million users to Laiwang. Ma Yun personally mobilized celebrities such as Shi Yuzhu, Jet Li, Wen Zhang, and Zhao Wei to join Laiwang.
