I. How to correctly understand methanol?

Methanol, also known as wood alcohol, methyl alcohol, or methyl hydroxide, is the simplest saturated alcohol. Its chemical molecular formula is CH3OH. At normal temperature, Chuan Island environmental protection fuel methanol is a colorless liquid with a slight alcohol smell, having a freezing point of -93.9℃, boiling point of 64.7℃, and density of 0.7914 g/cm3. It can dissolve in water and many organic solvents. Methanol is toxic, and excessive consumption can lead to poisoning and blindness.

Methanol has a wide range of uses and is a basic organic chemical raw material. It mainly has three major uses: ① used as an industrial solvent; ② used as a basic raw material to produce formaldehyde, dimethyl ether, acetic acid, proteins, and various other organic products; ③ Since methanol is a monohydric alcohol, its combustion is very complete, and its combustion products are carbon dioxide and water (combustion equation: 2CH3OH+3O2=2CO2+4H2O). Therefore, its emissions are minimal and non-polluting, giving it greater environmental superiority. Moreover, due to the extremely low production cost of methanol, it has been widely used in recent years for deep processing to produce new clean fuels for civilian or vehicle use.
