Now, hair loss is increasingly becoming younger, and more and more people between the ages of 20 to 30 are experiencing hair loss. How can we avoid the peak period of hair loss? Hair loss not only causes great distress to one's appearance but also negatively affects various aspects such as career and love. The pain of hair loss is unimaginable for ordinary people.

The first method to avoid hair loss is to change unhealthy lifestyles. Smoking and drinking cause significant harm to the human body, especially damaging organs like the liver, which indirectly affects hair growth. Sleep time: To avoid hair loss, ensure 6-8 hours of sleep daily. This not only reduces personal stress but also relaxes the mind. Studies show that from 11 PM to 3 AM, a deep sleep state should be maintained. Between 11 PM to 1 AM, the gallbladder produces blood, and from 1 AM to 3 AM, the blood detoxifies. Additionally, outdoor exercise can promote blood circulation in the skin and scalp, promoting hair growth.

The second method to avoid hair loss is to change poor eating habits. Most nutrients required for hair growth are obtained from external sources, so ensuring a diversified intake of nutrients is essential for healthy hair growth. Avoid developing picky eating habits. For those with oily scalps, frequent washing is necessary, and the diet should consist mainly of light foods, avoiding greasy or fried foods.

The third method to avoid hair loss is to reduce physical damage to hair. Sometimes, we notice thinning hair in the parting area due to consistently keeping the hair parted in the same direction, which exposes it to prolonged sunlight. Therefore, it is recommended to occasionally change the direction of the parting to prevent hair loss.

The fourth method to avoid hair loss is to develop the habit of massaging the scalp. Massaging promotes local blood circulation in the scalp, which is crucial for healthy hair growth. Developing this habit not only prevents hair loss but also provides relaxation.

Clearly, to have healthy hair, one must start by improving their lifestyle. It is hoped that through the aforementioned methods, everyone can lead a more colorful life and stay away from hair loss.
