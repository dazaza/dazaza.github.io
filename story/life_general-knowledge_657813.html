The people who love you and the people you love, love and career

Today is August 1st. Besides being Army Day, it seems to have no special significance, but for Shuhan, it is extraordinary. In a strange big city, who would remember one's birthday? So Shuhan decided to quietly enjoy his own happiness alone. It happened to be his day off today. After lunch, just after wiping the oil off his mouth with a napkin, his mobile phone rang. He picked it up; it was Qiong. "Hey, Shuhan, do you need to work today?" Shuhan: "No."

Qiong: "Then come see me."

Shuhan felt around in his pocket and only found twenty yuan. He said, "I'm not familiar with where you are, I'm afraid I'll get lost. If I get lost, there won't be anywhere to compensate for my loss."

Qiong: "Then I'll come find you. I took leave today."

Shuhan didn't take it to heart and planned to go swimming with Xiao Li. In the afternoon, he received another call from Qiong: "Hey, I've arrived. Come pick me up. I'm at the entrance of ** Hotel."

Shuhan noticed that Qiong had brought along a friend. After asking around, he learned that this was her colleague and close friend. The scorching sun of the dog days of summer beat down on the earth. Qiong placed one hand on her forehead to shield herself from the sun while fanning herself with the other hand, saying, "It's so hot, let's go drink milk tea at the ※※ store. We can cool off with the air conditioning and chat." On the way, each of them bought an ice cream. Qiong suddenly asked Shuhan, "Why don't you go into real estate?"

This was something Shuhan hadn't even dared to dream about. He didn't know when he'd ever own a house of his own. Shuhan: "Ah, real estate? Without money, it's all talk." Qiong: "You can still do it without money. Like you, selling houses as a salesperson. Go to a real estate agency company, and once you have money, you can start your own business... "

Qiong works at an Australian foreign trade company. She is a very capable strong woman. They say that behind every successful man, there is a silent supporting strong woman. Naturally, her husband will become an extraordinary person under her guidance in the future. From the beginning, Shuhan knew that Qiong wasn't an ordinary girl. She had great foresight and ambition. She graduated from high school and started working, never attending university, but her social experience surpasses Shuhan's. While Shuhan was in school, Qiong often advised him to study English well, so that he could achieve success in the future and not suffer losses in business.

In the past, when Shuhan said he wanted to be a salesman, Qiong said, "I know you're someone with ideals and ambitions. You won't be satisfied just being a salesperson. That's good. Once you expand your capabilities and have a group of clients who belong to you, you can start your own company." This was what Qiong taught Shuhan during their first meeting - to have ambition.

(Continued) After drinking the milk tea, Qiong said it was too hot. There were houses everywhere in this big city, nothing fun to do, so they decided to go shopping at the supermarket. These two were really like sisters. In the supermarket, they looked at this and that, laughing happily, "If you keep talking, watch out, I'll be restless in my sleep and kick you in my dreams." "Keep talking, you pig, either pressing your legs on me or pushing me off the bed," "Hahahahahaha..."

Shuhan pushed the shopping cart behind (he didn't have much money, so he kept a low profile), finished buying food, then asked Shuhan if he needed any daily necessities or clothes. Shuhan hesitated for a moment. When he came to this city, he only brought a few pieces of clothing. His salary hadn't been paid yet, so naturally, he lacked many things. He didn't feel comfortable letting others spend money on him. This was the second time Qiong visited Shuhan. The first time, they ate at a high-end restaurant. She said since it was the first time she treated Shuhan to a meal, it had to be abundant. The second time, Qiong returned from a business trip and passed through the city where Shuhan lived. She met him despite him being at work. With only fifty yuan in his pocket, it was still Qiong who paid for their KFC meal. This time again, she paid, which made him feel truly sorry... When he has money, he must not forget this good friend.

Qiong's friend was very similar to Shuhan's first love - willow leaf eyebrows, a pointed face, a bamboo-like figure, and a cherry red mouth.

On the way back, Shuhan couldn't help but ask for her friend's QQ number. His voice was as soft as a mosquito's, not daring to look at their faces. Before they could answer, he turned and left. He didn't know how Qiong would feel, nor did he dare to know how Qiong would feel. Even he himself didn't know if Qiong liked him or not. Their complicated relationship, neither completely love nor friendship, was slightly less intense than love but slightly deeper than friendship. Shuhan was very clear in his mind that he didn't like Qiong, so every time Qiong was nice to Shuhan, he always said, "I'm glad to meet a friend like you."

This time Qiong was really angry. Not long after she went home, she called Shuhan and joked, "What do you want my friend's QQ for? Do you like her?"

Shuhan: "No."

Qiong said seriously, "Do you or not?" Shuhan: "No." Qiong: "Then why do you want her QQ?" "I..."

He was momentarily speechless, and the story ends here.

Now it's time to ask you some questions. 1. Would you like a girl who treats you well, also likes you, but isn't the type you like?

2. When you encounter someone who looks very much like your dream lover, but your dream lover already has someone they like, would you replace your old dream lover?
