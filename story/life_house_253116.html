Negotiation skills with装修 companies: How to discuss decoration

by longersing on 2009-10-28 18:31:29

There are many negotiation skills when dealing with decoration companies. You cannot experience the atmosphere of such negotiations without going through a face-to-face discussion, even if it is with a friend. How to negotiate for home renovation? Here are a few tips that I hope can provide references and help everyone find the best decoration company.

1. Understand the construction qualifications of the decoration company

When negotiating with a decoration company, you need to understand their construction qualifications. The decoration company must have the qualification to carry out decoration projects, and you should also check their business license. Moreover, whether the company has a regular office location and can issue qualified invoices are all aspects that need to be carefully examined. The level and experience of interior designers directly affect the quality of design, so you should thoroughly inquire about the designer's background from the company. If it is a referral from a friend, you can understand the situation of the company through acquaintances. However, sometimes friends and family may not be "experts" in this field, which could lead to "misguidance". Therefore, it is still necessary to take personal responsibility.

2. Initial contact with the home decoration company

Currently, there are two ways for decoration companies to quote: one is where the homeowner reports how much they want to spend, and the decoration company designs and quotes according to the homeowner's requirements; the other is where the homeowner states the specific requirements for home renovation, and the decoration company quotes how much it will cost to meet those requirements.

Many consumers prefer the second negotiation method, aiming to avoid letting the decoration company know their budget bottom line. Consumers generally want the decoration company to quote first so that they have room to compare and bargain.

In fact, all formal decoration companies have similar profit margins. The differences lie in the strength of their design teams and the quality of after-sales services. By doing this, homeowners are only wasting their own time and delaying the construction progress. Therefore, when initially contacting a decoration company, it is best to directly express how much money you are prepared to spend and what kind of effect you want to achieve. Only if the decoration company agrees to undertake your home renovation project can you enter the specific design, quotation, and negotiation stages.

3. Confirm the design and haggle

After the homeowner and the decoration company reach a preliminary agreement, the decoration company needs to understand the homeowner's specific requirements and conduct on-site measurements. Subsequently, the company will hand over the design drawings and a detailed quotation list to the homeowner, listing very specific materials and construction quantities.

After receiving this material, the homeowner should first check whether the design meets their requirements. Of course, the homeowner can ask the designer to explain the design plan, such as the treatment of some spaces and the application of materials. After confirming the design plan, the homeowner should carefully examine whether each item's price and quantity in the quotation list are reasonable.

4. Eliminate contract loopholes in the decoration contract

After recognizing the quotation, a formal decoration company will sign a construction contract or agreement with the homeowner. In this contract, the homeowner should pay attention to the following three issues:

1. The contract must clearly state the specific requirements for decoration and the completion date. Some consumers do not notice these points when signing the contract, leaving room for some decoration companies to produce shoddy work and delay the schedule.

2. The contract must specify the exact brand or model of the decorative materials used to prevent the decoration company from substituting inferior products.

3. Warranty clauses in the contract are indispensable, and responsibilities must be clarified: if it is a problem related to construction or material quality, the decoration company should bear full responsibility; if it is due to improper use by the user, both parties can negotiate a solution.

5. Investigate the construction team - Personally, I think this is the most important point, as the construction team is ultimately the group involved in your renovation process.

The quality of the renovation largely depends on the quality of the construction team. Therefore, before starting the renovation, the homeowner should also investigate the construction team used by the decoration company.

From the current situation, in major cities across the country, most of the construction teams engaged in home renovation are outsiders. Currently, the degree of electrification in decoration engineering is very high. Generally, capable construction teams improve quality and efficiency by using tools like electric pumps and nail guns. Therefore, homeowners can judge whether the construction team has the corresponding quality just by looking at the tools used by the workers.
