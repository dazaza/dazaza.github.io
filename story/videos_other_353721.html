The Dragon's Valley Assistant for "Eternal Tower 2.0" is about to be released, sparking big speculations on the update content.

by sdhyxcu5h on 2010-04-30 20:55:32

It has been half a year since the Korean server of "Eternal Tower" was updated to version 1.5. At the beginning of this year, with the successive release of anticipated titles such as "Dragon's Valley", "TERA", "Starcraft II", and "Age of Empires", "Eternal Tower" also faces challenges from all sides. In early February, NC Soft announced that "Eternal Tower" would undergo a large-scale update in the first half of this year, and held a press conference for version 2.0 on March 3rd. Level cap increase, new regions, new skills: Currently, the highest level in "Eternal Tower" is 50, and through the previous content updates, many players have reached saturation. This time, the increase in the level cap will naturally lead to the addition of new regions, new skills, and new instances. The recent double experience event on the Korean server is very likely preparation for the update. Before the update, more players will reach the maximum level to experience the content of the next update. It is estimated that raising the maximum level to 60 will not be a problem. At the same time, "Eternal Tower" will reveal new regions. With more players leveling up, revealing new regions becomes necessary. Where these new regions are and how they appear are focal points of player attention. New skills are another major focus of player interest, with the primary concern being balance issues between classes due to new skills learned from leveling up. Adjustments to the cap on equipment and item levels, and of course, the appearance of high-level equipment. It is expected that hero-grade armor, which does not currently exist in the game, will be revealed, along with possible appearances of higher-tier abyssal gear and Deybaniwen sets. With the introduction of new equipment and items, high-level magic stones will also be revealed. Whether the much-anticipated "Attack Power +6" magic stone will be added remains to be seen. Adding underwater regions: There is also a possibility of adding underwater maps to the game. Underwater cities and swimming were previewed in a prior video. Will the dragon race unveil its mysterious veil? Is the content of the dragon race in "Eternal Tower" coming soon? Many tasks and instance materials related to the dragon race are no longer secrets. Besides the divine and demonic races, the appearance of a new neutral race and the addition of neutral content will come with the expansion of the map. Looking at the opposition between the divine and demonic races and their battles against the dragon race, the "Eternal Tower" worldview centered around AION depicts an infinite battle. At this point, the appearance of the dragon race already has ample possibility, especially since the subtitle of the last update was "The Shadow of the Dragon Race". The arrival of ground-based abysses: The appearance of new abyssal regions is also one of the major contents that players are looking forward to. The existing abyss already has a lot of content like fortress battles and Dredgion, but due to the limitations of flight time in aerial combat, freedom is greatly reduced. To meet player needs, it is expected that this update will add "ground-based abysses" that do not require flying. Thus, rich new content such as fortress battles and RvR instances, as well as large-scale PvP battles, will be presented to players. Adding new PvP content: Many players have reflected that the current PvP content is insufficient, and this issue may be addressed by adding new battlegrounds through this update. Adding customization features: Additionally, it is speculated that this update may add new customization features. In each previous update, new character customization features were added, and following tradition, this update will also include additions to this content.
