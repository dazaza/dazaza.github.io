However, the interference of Xilan Cheng's Basketball Association in the league cannot be completely denied.

Wishing the team to secure the championship earlier this season. However, an economical and practical draw delighted both sides, performing the bunny hop. Although Levante, playing at home, is currently ranked in the middle to lower part of La Liga, they are only a few points away from the top of the table. Being able to force a draw against the mighty Barcelona undoubtedly injected a strong dose of confidence into Levante's quest for successful relegation survival this season.

Barcelona's title win this season marks their third consecutive La Liga championship. Their impressive achievement of three consecutive league titles is enough to make their biggest rival, Real Madrid, envious. For Barcelona, securing the league title isn't enough to satisfy the ambitious club. Facing Manchester United in the final Champions League match, Barcelona is already eager to act. Judging by the current momentum, it may only be a matter of time before Barcelona ascends to the peak of Europe once again.

In the eyes of most fans, Barcelona is now virtually unsolvable. They established a solid foundation for winning the league early on, and have been playing exceptionally well in the Champions League, smoothly advancing to the final. However, the seemingly flawless Barcelona does have some vulnerabilities. For instance, aside from Messi who can consistently score efficiently, the other two members of the media-lauded new Barcelona trio, Villa and Pedro, lack the consistent performance that Messi displays. They can maintain high efficiency in scoring for a period, but will inexplicably fall into a scoring drought for a while. This is most evident with Villa. To be fair, as the rookie season for Villa (nicknamed El Guaje) at Barcelona, his overall performance has been relatively satisfactory for the fans. However, Villa's frequent long-term scoring droughts have indeed made the club and fans somewhat uneasy about his condition.

Pedro's situation is not much better than Villa's. Nowadays, Pedro can no longer be measured as just Barcelona's super substitute. If we still evaluate Pedro based on this standard, his performance this season would still be considered impressive. Now, Pedro is gradually establishing his position as a starter, and he once firmly occupied one spot in the trio. However, due to inconsistent form and other factors, Pedro still habitually plays the role of the familiar super substitute. With only Messi as a stable and efficient scoring point, it's no wonder that Barcelona began searching for suitable players for the forward line even before the end of this season.

Barcelona's biggest gain in the second half of the season was the gradual improvement of Argentine player Mascherano within the team. Little Brother (Mascherano's nickname) had once become a dispensable player due to overlapping positions with Busquets and the coach's tactical arrangements. Despite arriving at Barcelona with a high transfer fee, he could only spend his time on the bench, almost becoming another failed signing. Fortunately, Little Brother did not succumb. In the second half of Barcelona's season, Little Brother gradually found his rhythm. Sometimes, when the team needed him, he could also play as a center-back and perform admirably. The three consecutive championships have brought Barcelona to the first peak of this season. For Barcelona fans, the question is: with the league championship secured, can the Champions League trophy be far behind?
