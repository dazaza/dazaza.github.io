It was claimed that a male student was admitted after talking with the former president of Peking University for 5 minutes.

by trgfh70wdf on 2012-03-02 09:32:44

How could Wang Chenzhou tame the examiner in just a few minutes of chatting? Wang Chenzhou, the sports minister of the Student Union and first prize winner of the National High School Mathematics League, thus obtained the qualification for direct admission. This sunny young man is good at summarizing and reflecting during his regular study, and in his spare time, he is most passionate about sports activities. He was lucky to be a representative of Nanjing students and went to Beijing to watch the Olympics. It was precisely because of his love for sports that he later had an excellent exchange with Academician Xu.

Academician Xu Zhihong and his team also admitted science experts Ji Jing and Li Fan. Both students won major awards in science competitions to obtain direct admission qualifications. Being in a foreign language school, they both have excellent English proficiency and a strong foundation in humanities, and are poised and confident.

The Beijing trip was originally not scheduled to include the Olympic Table Tennis Venue at Peking University, but Wang Chenzhou, who has a deep affection for PKU, specially visited it. At that moment, he associated that Peking University must have done more than just building a venue for the Olympics. He had always wanted to find out the answer. This question opened up Academician Xu's conversation. Xu Zhihong said that the table tennis venue was built by Peking University raising over 200 million yuan on its own, and the process was very challenging. More importantly, over 3600 student volunteers from Peking University served the Olympics. Academician Xu left a deep impression on this sunny boy, and after reviewing his outstanding resume, he decided on the spot to admit him. Wang Chenzhou never would have thought that his direct admission test would be so short.

"In the meeting with former Peking University President Xu Zhihong, I asked a question: about Peking University's classical education, then..." This sentence appears in a small article written by Li Junjie, who was directly admitted to Peking University's Geography Department. Because there was no subsequent examination, she could only put ellipses after the word "then." This girl who loves reading Zhuangzi and asking why is the first prize winner of the national geography competition and has a special fondness for classical literature. In her dialogue with Academician Xu, she did not rush to "sell" herself, but instead wanted to understand whether Peking University's classical education suited her. Academician Xu Zhihong said that Peking University indeed places great emphasis on literature and history, and even specifically established the Institute of Classical Studies. However, he frankly admitted that he is not satisfied with the current level of classical studies among university students, so all students entering Peking University, regardless of their major, must undergo classical education. Talking about classical studies, he believed that classics do not become outdated; for example, the concept of sustainable development is derived from classical studies. Clearly, Academician Xu also believed that the girl who raised such a question was very suitable for Peking University, and thus, she was fortunate to be directly admitted.

Reporter Huang Yan

"What contributions has Peking University made for the Olympics?" Last December, Academician Xu Zhihong, former president of Peking University, came to Nanjing Foreign Language School to converse with students, and student Wang Chenzhou boldly asked a question. Unexpectedly, within less than five minutes of dialogue, Wang Chenzhou was named and "taken away," directly admitted without needing to take Peking University's direct admission written or oral exams. Like him, male student Li Fan, and female students Li Junjie and Ji Jing were also directly admitted by Peking University. It is understood that at the start of the new semester, apart from 76 students being accepted by foreign prestigious universities, Nanjing Foreign Language School also received the good news of admitting 165 students through direct admissions from 26 domestic top universities.
