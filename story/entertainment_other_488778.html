Heartwarming: 9 touching micro-novels | Micro Records

by vinuu on 2011-09-13 17:47:59

1. She came home from work, and he was sitting on the sofa. With a blank expression, he was smoking, and suddenly he spoke: "Hey, I'm saying, we've been cohabiting like this for two years now. Don't you feel tired of it?" She stopped in the middle of changing into her slippers and turned around, stunned as she looked at him: "What did you say?" He still had the same blank expression: "I'm saying, I'm tired of it." Tears streamed down her face as she helplessly turned away to wipe them. Suddenly, a warm body hugged her: "How about we get married instead?"

2. Whenever he had free time, he would fold heart-shaped origami with paper and give them to her whenever they met.

How long had this habit lasted? Even he couldn't remember clearly.

One day, she said over the phone: "Today, someone came to collect waste paper, so I asked the price, then sold all the heart-shaped origami you gave me."

She paused for a moment, "It was exactly nine yuan. Why don't you dress up a bit? We can go to the Civil Affairs Bureau and register our marriage."

3. The bus arrived at the station.

The broadcast sounded: Please take your valuable items with you when you get off.

He held her hand and said to her: "Let's go, my valuable item."

4. They had broken up two years ago.

For two years, he had been used to opening her blog after work every day to see her mood of the day.

Sometimes she was happy, sometimes sad, sometimes lost...

He just quietly watched, without making any comments, even deleting his browsing records.

Until one day, her blog was filled with her wedding photos.

There was a line of small text below: "I'm getting married, I won't wait for you anymore, and I won't update anymore."

5. A couple who had broken up for a long time, accidentally met in a foreign country.

He asked: Are you okay?

She answered: I'm fine.

He asked: Is he okay?

She answered: He is fine.

She asked: Are you okay?

He answered: I'm fine.

She asked: Is she okay?

He answered: She just told me that she is fine.

6. His office was on the floor above hers across the street.

They were separated by a street, and every day she secretly looked up at him, guessing his emotions, recording them on Weibo.

One day, he was gone.

Her Weibo update read: "He's gone, I'm missing him."

The next second, there was a comment immediately: "Silly, I moved to the floor opposite yours, so I don't have to look down at you anymore."

7. Snowy night.

He knelt down on one knee, his right hand touching her favorite red rose, and his left hand holding a ring box.

She was full of happiness and said, "Stop fooling around, there are so many people."

He smiled and said, "Will you agree or not?"

She nodded. The crowd cheered.

On the way home, she looked at his slightly hunched back and said, "You've proposed for more than ten years. Next year, let's change the way we celebrate our wedding anniversary."

8. In the subway.

Dad was holding a large-sized Doraemon plush toy, and the son was holding a smaller one.

The son looked enviously at the one in Dad's hands.

Dad said, "Be good, don't make a fuss, this one is for Mom."

9. The husband was taking care of his wife, who was about to give birth, by the bedside.

The wife asked the husband: "Do you hope for a boy or a girl?"

The husband replied: "If it's a boy, we will protect you; if it's a girl, I will protect both of you."

A girl who is willing to hold your hand for life is your angel. Angels are meant to be cherished.
