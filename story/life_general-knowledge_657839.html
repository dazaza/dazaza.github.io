Cinderella's Tears

When we were very young, each of us loved fairy tales and dreamed of being the princess in the story, living in a harmonious kingdom, waiting for the prince to come on a white horse and say to the princess, "Dear and kind little princess, you are my only love in this life, and I will give you happiness for the rest of your life." From then on, the prince and princess lived happily ever after.

Most fairy tales end this way, even Cinderella in the fairy tale can receive the prince's lifelong care. How much more so for the beautiful and kind princess? Perhaps the author of the story does not want to see the disappointment of children, so every story has a happy ending. The author may hope that we should be confident in life and love, so there is a princess dream hidden in the heart of every little girl. They hope that one day the prince will come on a white horse to pick her up.

I had such a dream when I was young too. However, I did not believe in the love between the prince and Cinderella until I met JASON. Since I got to know this melancholy prince, I have been looking forward to being his princess. Although he always called me "little princess," I knew I was just a Cinderella living at the bottom of society. He was a nobleman with one-quarter British blood who had been active in high society for 29 years, attending famous universities, tasting fine wines, and having a refined artistic temperament. My life, however, was worrying about whether I earned enough money to pay rent and utility bills each month. This is how common people live. It's not that we don't strive, but birth is something we cannot choose. I grew up in such a family, so I am destined to be just a Cinderella.

When I met JASON, he had just arrived in China for a week. He said he came to find his princess. We chatted happily online, and my sunshine and happiness infected him. I could feel the melancholy in his heart gradually dissipating due to my joy. I was genuinely happy for him. We even made an appointment to take photos together. He said that from childhood to now, he had only taken pictures with his mother. I was the second woman in his life who would take pictures with him. When these words appeared on the computer screen, my whole heart became tense. I wasn't sure if he liked me, but I was vaguely attracted by his gentlemanly demeanor and melancholy. Every day, I hung QQ waiting for him until one day, we had agreed to go online, which happened to be his birthday. I decided to give him a surprise and video chat with him, but he broke the date. There was no news from him for two days, and I was very worried and uncomfortable. I tried to reason with myself, but my thoughts of him never diminished. Two days later, one night, he came online. Although I was a little unhappy, I knew he had just arrived in China and must be very busy, so I forgave his breaking the date long ago.

He still affectionately called me "little princess." I said, "The little princess can video chat with you." He cautiously asked, "Really?" I said, "Of course."

Later, he told me that his father's company had problems, and he might have to marry a woman he didn't love because marrying her could help his father's business out of difficulties. Such a plot seemed like it was in a novel or a TV drama, why did it happen to me! He continued, "In fact, this time coming to China, I've already found the princess I love, but I haven't confessed yet, or maybe I have confessed but she doesn't know." Then he asked me, "Little princess, if one day a boy you like confesses to you, but you find out later, what would you think?" After reading these sentences, I couldn't control my feelings anymore, tears streamed down my face, and I said, "I don't know, I just know I feel very bad right now!" At this moment, I knew he loved me, but so what? In this society, the love between the prince and Cinderella cannot have a good result.

He left quietly without realizing that promise, taking my thoughts to England, and even didn't have time to ask my name, just like Cinderella wearing a beautiful glass slipper going to the prince's ball, at the stroke of twelve, Cinderella left the prince behind. The stories in the fairy tales are all perfect, but life is different. However, having the prince's love in this life, I am already very satisfied. Thank you for fulfilling my childhood princess dream. Although this dream is not perfect, I can own your love, and I have no regrets in this life!
