Tram Wolf 3 Chinese Version Strategy Guide: Thanks to Lin Jun on the Entrepreneurship Road

by dsfjd75zw on 2011-06-10 16:35:55

1. Thanks to Lin Jun, who wrote a long blog as a congratulatory message for my entrepreneurship. I thought it was too long to be edited, so he wrote a short one. I thought it was a bit negative, so he revised it again. After several rounds of折腾 (trouble), I felt deeply ashamed, but he was still as patient as when he was an editor.

2. Lin Jun used a lot of flattering words in his blog, such as "famous in the江湖 (jianghu - circles)" and "extremely smart", which I really don't feel comfortable accepting. I enjoy writing articles and even think that my articles are not too bad, but objectively speaking, I am not as good as Lin Jun. Maybe Lin Jun's articles are still not the most skillful or best ones I've ever seen (for example, whether in praising people or criticizing them, he is still not as good as Liu Ren), but since he is better than me, it is enough for me to praise him.

3. Lin Jun is a straightforward person, and sometimes he suffers because of this straightforwardness. Ten years of experience in the industry is enough to change many people, but he still sticks to his true self. This is rare. When I am about to act against my will in the future, I will remind myself to "learn from Lin Jun". It is difficult to stick to principles and make money, and it is even more difficult to choose between principles and money.

4. Lin Jun initiated an "IT Veteran Group", which includes many successful people, and invited me to be an honorary moderator. I support him. Although I may have less time to be a moderator in the future, I cannot refuse this honorary title, and it is an honor to be on par with those successful people. I will try my best to communicate in the veteran group in the future, even though I do not accept the word "old".

5. Thank Lin Jun again.
