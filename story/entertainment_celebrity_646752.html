In the afternoon of December 18, Ma Jingtao held a fan meeting in Beijing to celebrate his Sina Weibo followers breaking through the one million mark. Ma Jingtao, who returned after three years of seclusion, still maintained his roaring style, leading the fans below the stage to shout several times. Not only did he take off his clothes on the scene to show off his chest muscles, but he also forcibly kissed director You Xiaogang who came to support him. In addition, his eldest daughter Ma Shiyan unexpectedly appeared to support her father. Ma Jingtao also expressed intimate love to his wife Wu Jiani from afar and looked forward to having another son. (Click to enter Ma Jingtao's Weibo)

Sina Weibo fans break one million, forcibly kiss You Xiaogang, daughter supports

Ma Jingtao opened a Sina Weibo account, and it took only 222 days for the number of fans to easily exceed one million. For this, Ma Jingtao, known as "the Cook," gathered 100 fans from all over the country. On that day, Ma Jingtao wore a T-shirt with the words "The sound of waves remains the same, the king returns, roaring in all directions, unmatched by ten thousand men," and wearing a Peking Opera face mask, roared as he made his entrance. After a roar, he revealed his true identity, which immediately caused fans to scream. That day, Ma Jingtao was in a great mood, responding to all the fans' requests. Not only did he give big bear hugs, but he also spun fans around in place. Many fans were moved to tears on the spot.

Director You Xiaogang, who has cooperated with Ma Jingtao in many dramas, also attended to send his blessings. Ma Jingtao joked that he and You Xiaogang were the best partners in this life and intimate couples in the past life. He not only personally sent a warm scarf but also forcibly kissed Director You Xiaogang, leaving the latter somewhat at a loss. Ma Jingtao's eldest daughter Ma Shiyan, who is currently studying at the Central Academy of Drama, also specially appeared to support her father. In addition, the event site played highlights from the films and TV dramas Ma Jingtao had shot over the past 26 years, and there were also heartfelt voice messages recorded by Ma's fans. The highly spirited Ma Jingtao exclaimed: Seeing your passion, I am really excited, I would willingly give myself to you. After finishing speaking, amid the fans' cheers, he took off his outer T-shirt, revealing a tight-fitting short-sleeved shirt, showing off his muscles, causing the fans below the stage to scream continuously.

Weibo becomes an important part of life, Roaring Body is a heartfelt touch

Ma Jingtao, who self-deprecatingly calls himself a computer illiterate, said that he used to be accustomed to expressing emotions through handwritten texts. But since opening a Weibo account, his horizons have broadened greatly, and it has built a bridge for communication with fans. Whether the fans tease or seek happiness or care, I accept everything. I hope to use Weibo to gather a kind of strength, sharing the joy of roaring with everyone. Ma Jingtao revealed that Weibo has already become an important part of his life, just like family. He takes out fixed time every day to read comments and communicate with fans.

Browsing through Ma Jingtao's Sina Weibo, you will find many popular online terms today, such as: "Cook" (Master), "Hu Ren" (Madam), "Bei Bei" (Baby), and sometimes even a series of exclamation marks, performing the Weibo version of the Roaring Body. Regarding roaring, Ma Jingtao has his own understanding: It is not a form, but a heartfelt touch every minute and second, simple and direct, foolishly cute. It is my way of expressing inner longing, loneliness, and instability.

After being dormant for three years, not idle, grateful to wife, looking forward to having another son

Three years ago, Ma Jingtao stepped back from the spotlight when his career was thriving, inspired by a phrase from Director You Xiaogang: Art needs solitude, if art creators blindly pursue fame and fortune, they will definitely become restless. Deeply inspired, Ma Jingtao decided to retire from the screen for three years to carefully沉淀himself. However, these three years were not idle for Ma Jingtao. "I had two sons at once, wrote novels and scripts, and also started my own company. I deliberately changed the rhythm of my life, calmed down, and thought about some things."

For his wife Wu Jiani, who silently supported him, Ma Jingtao expressed his love from afar: "Jianyi, you gave me a lot of strength, allowing me to have this state of readiness today. Thank you for giving me two treasures, and I look forward to the arrival of the third baby, hoping we can make our dreams come true." Ma Jingtao also thanked his ex-wife for her contributions.

With new talents emerging in the entertainment circle, when asked whether he was worried about becoming outdated after being dormant for three years, Ma Jingtao refused to admit old age: "I love sports very much, like horse riding, basketball, football - these sports that consume calories instantly. Although it is true that the later generations push the previous ones, the previous ones will always remain irreplaceable. I will work hard, harder, and harder again to set a good example. Let everyone know that it is not easy to be the previous generation."
