For environmental protection in shopping, choose the "green product"

by fashioninchina on 2008-05-10 12:50:38

Preface: Green Living, Sula Industry.

Green Products

So-called green products refer to products that are beneficial for the improvement of society and environment. These products or production processes themselves save energy, conserve water, have low pollution, low toxicity, can be regenerated and recycled, or also known as pollution-free products. Compared with traditional similar products, green products at least have the following characteristics:

(1) The core function of the product must not only meet consumers' traditional needs and conform to corresponding technical and quality standards, but more importantly, it must meet the green demands that are beneficial to society, natural environment, and human physical and mental health, and comply with relevant environmental protection and safety hygiene standards.

(2) The physical part of the product should reduce resource consumption and make full use of renewable resources as much as possible. Harmful raw materials and auxiliary materials to the environment and human health should not be added in the product entity. During the manufacturing process of the product, "three wastes" pollution to the environment should be eliminated or reduced.

(3) The packaging of the product should reduce resource consumption. The waste from packaging and the residue after the product is scrapped should become new resources as much as possible. Therefore, product manufacturers should adopt green packaging when packaging their products. Green packaging refers to packaging that saves resources, reduces waste, is easy to recycle or regenerate after use, is easy to decompose naturally, and does not pollute the environment, such as easily decomposable non-woven bags and eco-friendly bags. Green packaging has already widely spread in developed countries, but in our country, it is just getting started. There are still many shortcomings. Therefore, Sula Non-woven Bag Experts hope to work together with everyone during this period to enhance the environmental awareness of all citizens. Taking shirts as an example, according to statistics, the total number of shirts produced nationwide each year is 1.2 billion pieces, of which at least 800 million pieces use cardboard packaging. Converted into paper usage, it amounts to approximately 24 tons per year, equivalent to the paper output of 1.68 million trees with a breast diameter of 10 centimeters. If we calculate the total amount of paper used for all commodity packaging and the trees that need to be cut down, it is undoubtedly an astronomical figure. It can be said that the more cardboard boxes are used, the faster the green forests disappear. Moreover, the plastic products used for shirt packaging take over 200 years to be absorbed by the soil, and these chemical wastes cause very serious environmental pollution. In fact, many consumers strongly support the use of simple packaging for commodities. When purchasing goods for personal or family use, whether there is attractive packaging doesn't matter; if it is given as a gift, the packaging indeed needs to be exquisite, but the most important thing about the product is its intrinsic quality. External packaging should be "icing on the cake," rather than "stealing the limelight." In this regard, the non-woven bag experts had earlier launched a series of exquisite gift eco-bags for environmentally conscious consumers, which received great recognition. For the vast majority of consumers, genuine goods at reasonable prices remain the most important standard for choosing products. This requires Chinese enterprises to both strive to reduce packaging costs during the packaging of goods and consider the degree of environmental pollution caused by packaging waste, continuously researching and developing new types of green packaging materials. In this aspect, Sula Industry Co., Ltd. is willing and sincerely committed to cooperating with all enterprises that support environmental protection, creating a new world of green marketing together. In short, in the field of promoting environmental protection, non-woven bag experts will always go hand in hand with everyone, moving forward with environmental protection.

(4) The focus of product production and sales is not on guiding consumers to consume excessively leading to mass production, but on guiding consumers to consume correctly and produce moderately, establishing a brand-new aesthetic concept of production.
