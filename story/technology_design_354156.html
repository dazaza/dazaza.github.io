Chapter Thirteen: The Academy Ball

After the duel with Bono, Cit had been recuperating in his small cabin.

In fact, Cit's injuries were not severe; the main issue was exhaustion and depletion of magic after using the martial arts technique "Sword and Thunder." He had no choice but to take a long time to recover.

Because he won the duel with Bono, Cit became an idol across the entire Olym Magic and Martial Arts Academy. The young ladies went crazy for him, the boys wanted to befriend this outstanding disciple of the deans, and various groups tried to recruit Cit. This situation troubled Cit greatly. Lily couldn't help either, so she called upon Arnold • Night for assistance.

What surprised Lily was that Night, who usually avoided trouble, readily agreed to help this time. But this confusion was soon resolved.

"Stop right there!" came Cit's angry shout.

"Don't be like that. It's just a few dates, and these girls are all beauties I've carefully selected, one in a hundred." Someone dodged Cit's flying kick while pitifully defending themselves.

"If you want to go, go yourself! I know you must have made a lot of money from this. Hand it over!" Cit was even more annoyed.

"Am I that kind of person? Am I? I just let them repay my kindness by inviting me to eat, that's all." Someone continued to act shamelessly.

"Big Brother Cit hits well." Lily cheered on the side.

"Hey, hey, how can you say that too, Lily? I'm so sad, wu wu wu wu~"

"Hmph, who asked you to do such things. And it was me who sought your help, your actions make me lose face in front of Big Brother Cit. So you must be punished." Lily scolded.

"Is it because I made West attract so many admirers that you're jealous and upset?" Someone wore an expression as if to say, "I'm a thug, what are you going to do about it?"

"You... I... It's not that!" Her secret exposed, Lily's face flushed red.

"Haha... Ow!"

It turned out that Arnold • Night was so preoccupied teasing Lily that he ignored Cit's pursuit, and was hit squarely on the head by a punch. Fortunately, Cit was only joking and didn't use full force, otherwise Night would have had to lie in bed for a few days.

"Damn, here comes my move!"

"Come on, I'm ready."

"A wise man doesn't fight when he knows he'll lose, I'm outta here~" Before finishing, he darted out of the hut, shouting as he ran, "Remember, you have a date tomorrow! Dress nicely, don't embarrass your buddy~"

"Go die already~!" Shouting at his retreating figure, Cit couldn't help but smile faintly. However, his expression quickly turned serious again.

Lily was puzzled when Cit turned around and asked, "Lily, do you know Night's identity?"

"I'm not sure. What's up?"

"Nothing. I always feel he's extraordinary. Just now, although we were fooling around, it was also a test from me. Every one of my attacks, he casually dodged, but it wasn't simple."

"Could it be that he's very strong?"

"I can't say for sure, but I can guarantee that he is certainly no less than me. Arnold • Night, who exactly are you?"

...

Spring passed, autumn arrived, and before they knew it, Cit had already been at the Olym Magic and Martial Arts Academy for half a year.

During his time at the academy, Cit learned the basic knowledge of mages like a thirsty desert traveler, especially in earth magic. His understanding and strength were continuously improving. Mieu would also timely offer some of her own experiences and techniques, helping Cit absorb better. In terms of martial arts, through the personal instruction of Dean Fenno, his combat skills became more perfect, and his control and use of battle qi also became more complete.

On this day, the weather was clear and bright.

"Cit, we're going to attend a ball this afternoon. Are you coming or not?" Chris chuckled and said.

Chris was someone who was very enthusiastic about public activities, skilled at gathering information, and good at making friends with students. Although he had only been enrolled for half a year, he was already considered a notable figure among the new students.

"No," Cit replied decisively.

Just as Chris was trying every means to persuade Cit without success, Arnold • Night poked his head in from the door and said to Cit, "Hey, the dean wants to see you, go now."

After seeing Cit leave, Night immediately signaled to Chris with a look, and Chris understood. Both of them looked at Lily who was peacefully reading with sinister smiles.

Feeling the cold atmosphere, Lily looked up and asked, "Why are you looking at me like that?"

"Lily, don't be afraid. We have no ill intentions, we just want to tell you something..."

When Cit returned from the dean's office, Chris and Night had already left, leaving only Lily sitting alone staring at the table absent-mindedly.

"Lily, what's on your mind? Where did they go? Lily?"

"Oh, Big Brother Cit has come back, they've already gone."

"Then what are you thinking about?"

"Nothing, hehe. By the way, what did the dean want with you?"

"Nothing much, just wanted to test my recent cultivation results."

"Oh, I see."

"Big Brother Cit, can I ask you something?"

"What is it? Go ahead." Lily's hesitant manner made Cit quite curious.

"About that ball, what do you think? Are you really not going to participate?"

"Ah, it's about that. What's up, Lily, do you want to go?"

"I... I'll follow Big Brother Cit's decision."

Seeing the expectant look in Lily's eyes, Cit, who originally decided not to attend the ball, couldn't help but be somewhat moved.

"Alright then, let's go. But Lily, you have to dress up properly. Ladies must wear beautifully when attending balls."

"Okay!" Lily said excitedly.

At this moment, Chris and Night, who believed their plan would surely succeed, were shopping for Cit and Lily's ball dresses and accessories...

Carrying a pile of clothes and small gift boxes, the two returned to Cit's hut.

"I'm exhausted. So, have you decided to go?" After drinking two cups of water, Night spoke up.

"I knew it was you two who told Lily to attend the ball." It seemed Cit had already seen through their trick.

"Hehe," Night laughed, "We're doing it for both of you. Look at you, in the academy, all you do is practice, everything else is simplified. You might not find it boring, but Sister Lily will. If you neglect her, what will you do?"

"Smooth talker." Though he said it this way, Cit still looked at Lily with an apologetic gaze, and she smiled back.

"Anyway, since you've decided to go, our efforts in spending so much time and money buying these things for you haven't gone to waste." Chris stepped in to mediate.

"So beautiful clothes!" Lily exclaimed after flipping through the items the two brought.

"Of course, the clothes for the most beautiful Lily can't be chosen carelessly. Try them on quickly."

Cit and Lily didn't hesitate, each taking their clothes into their respective rooms.

The first one out was Cit. The white suit fit perfectly, with a row of buttons on the right side just the right size, paired with silver thread edges, making the main color white not monotonous. The silver sash connecting from the left shoulder to the fifth button on the right added a touch of masculinity, paired with white gloves also edged with silver thread, making Cit look like a noble gentleman.

As Chris and Night joked with Cit, Lily also came out.

The door opened slightly, revealing a delicate foot stepping into the hall. Under the embellishment of black high heels with fine straps, it emitted a gentle white light. Everyone involuntarily drew in a breath. Shoulders slightly exposed, a pure deep blue gemstone hung from her chest, emitting a soft glow, and the matching long earrings swayed gently with her steps, making her skin appear as smooth as congealed fat. The elegant corset accentuated her slim waist, which seemed almost too delicate to hold. Her hair was tied up high in a black bun, and the fitted purple dress outlined her perfect curves. The delicate pleats at the bottom of the long skirt rippled lightly with her steps, resembling a fairy gliding gracefully.

All three men couldn't help but stare in amazement.

Lily appeared flustered, as this was the first time she wore such clothing, and the three gentlemen were looking at her with such expressions. Hesitating for a moment, she asked, "Do... do I look bad?"

"Lily is the most beautiful young lady in the world right now!" This phrase from Chris was spoken without thought, instinctively.

"Chris is right, Lily looks incredibly beautiful now." Cit realized this, walked to Lily's side, slightly bowed, and extended his right hand, saying, "May I have the honor of inviting you to the ball, beautiful lady?"

"Delighted, sir." Lily responded in kind.

"Haha, as long as you two like it, Chris, let's go prepare our things too. Everyone, see you at the ball!"

The location of the ball was set in the college auditorium, the largest building in the school. How large? Well, it could accommodate everyone in the school for a ball.

When Cit and Lily arrived at the auditorium, it was already filled with people dressed in formal attire. Lily's appearance caused a slight commotion.

Night and Chris pushed through the crowd to reach Cit and Lily.

"How late you two are." Night was wearing a full black suit, simple in style but naturally handsome.

"It's fine, the ball hasn't started yet. Now everyone is busy chatting." Chris was wearing a brown suit, with a special emblem embroidered on the left chest.

"It should start soon."

At this moment, someone who was probably the host stood in the center of the dance floor, signaling the crowd to quiet down.

"The academy life is colorful, because we are in the prime of our lives. We diligently study knowledge and skills, but we should also enjoy dancing freely! Now I declare, the academy ball officially begins!"

The band played music appropriately. Pairs of dancers entered the dance floor hand in hand.

Cit and Lily began to dance to the music. As for Night and Chris, they disappeared somewhere after the host announced the beginning of the ball.

Lily placed one hand on Cit's shoulder and held the other lightly, spinning with the music. At this moment, she felt the whole world had stopped, leaving only her and Cit dancing. It was such a happy feeling, she hoped they could keep spinning forever...
