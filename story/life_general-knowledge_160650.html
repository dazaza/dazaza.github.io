Anxi Tieguanyin Tea Factory

by kwanyintea on 2009-06-14 18:59:33

Zhen Tiange Tea Factory in Anxi County is located in Anxi, the hometown of China's Tieguanyin tea. With the corporate spirit of "honesty, progress, development, and innovation" and the business philosophy of "excellent value for money," we sincerely welcome inquiries and visits from friends all over. We offer direct sales from the origin, specializing in Minnan Oolong teas including Tieguanyin, Huangjinguì, Benshan, Maoxie, and other famous teas. Additionally, we also sell various exquisite tea sets and tea packaging machinery. In December 2007, our factory was designated as a quality tracking unit for Fujian industrial and commercial enterprises by the Fujian Industry and Commerce Times on March 15th. Our brand is promoted by one hundred tea cooperatives nationwide.

Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory inherits the traditional techniques of tea-making families, combining modern technology with strict adherence to the principle of "quality drives brand." We have established green tea gardens in multiple well-known tea-producing towns within Anxi, the homeland of Tieguanyin, ensuring the supply of high-quality products. The factory has now become a specialized tea company integrating research, cultivation, production, and processing. Our products are stable in quality, superior in grade, and unique in culture. All varieties of tea undergo rigorous scrutiny and careful selection by national senior tea masters to ensure that each product highlights its essence in terms of shape, color, aroma, and taste, making them enjoyable to consume repeatedly. In recent years, our products have won numerous awards, earning widespread praise from tea enthusiasts and consumers at home and abroad.

Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory consistently upholds the business philosophy of "quality determines brand," adheres to the service concept of "guests are welcomed, meeting equals friendship," and persists in the development model of "profit sharing, common development." We widely connect with tea enthusiasts across the country and gather talented individuals. Currently, we have developed dozens of direct-operated stores and franchise stores nationwide. We sincerely welcome tea enthusiasts and merchants from all regions to visit, guide, and negotiate business, working together to prosper the tea market.

In 2004, under the leadership of the General Manager of Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory, the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative was established in Changkeng Township, Anxi County. There are 116 tea farming households and 14 tea merchants participating in the cooperative, accounting for 91% of the total number of households. Among the members of the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative, 88 people have obtained national vocational skill qualification certificates, including 2 senior technicians, 8 technicians, 50 senior workers, 12 intermediate-level workers, and 16初级level workers. The entire village of Zhen Tian has an arable land area of 392 mu, a mountainous area of 3709 mu, and a tea garden area of 1008 mu. In 2006, the per capita income of farmers was 5154 yuan, with more than 3100 yuan coming from the tea industry. The establishment of the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative has become a benchmark and window for external promotion for Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory. As the largest production base for the factory, the cooperative not only owns large-scale production and processing facilities but also possesses a group of skilled talents trained through practice and training, which is crucial for ensuring the quality and optimization of Tieguanyin tea products.

Additionally, since its establishment, the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative has hired experts and professors as advisors; founded the first farmer research institution in the country - the "Anxi Zhen Tian Farmer Tea Research Institute" to serve tea farmers; organized technical training and exchanges, implemented the "five unifications" method of tea garden management, and established 10 direct-sales websites for tea farmers to conduct online transactions, achieving significant benefits. First, the safety and quality levels of tea have significantly improved, with all tea produced by members meeting national tea health and quality safety standards. Second, the cost of tea garden management has clearly decreased, saving the cooperative 100,500 yuan in 2006. Third, tea-making skills have significantly improved, increasing the average selling price of tea in the cooperative by 30 yuan per kilogram in 2006, representing a 25% increase compared to the same period in 2005. Fourth, the collaboration awareness of members has increased, with the mechanism of shared interests and shared risks fundamentally transforming the overall awareness of the cooperative. Fifth, 10 direct-sales counters for cooperative tea products have been established in major and medium-sized cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Chongqing, Xi'an, Hainan, Fuzhou, and Quanzhou, with annual sales reaching 33 million yuan.

"Inheriting tea culture, sharing Zhen Tian tea," Zhen Tian Tea connects through tea. Since the Ming and Qing dynasties, Anxi Tieguanyin has undergone over 200 years of meticulous nurturing, becoming a top-tier tea with its unique charm, fragrant both domestically and internationally, and exported to Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Southeast Asia, Europe, and America. Anxi, the birthplace of "Tieguanyin," is China's largest original place for Oolong tea. Zhen Tian Tea is rooted in Anxi, allowing pure Anxi Tieguanyin to spread its fragrance far and wide! A cup of clear tea, a segment of life, a trace of reminiscence! Good tea is simply good tea.

The General Manager of Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory is a retired soldier trained by a certain unit of the Beijing Military Region. Returning home in 2000, he demonstrated the great military spirit of perseverance, fearlessness, and courage to fight, along with a strong revolutionary style. Despite his family's poverty, he started as a tea farmer and wrote a legendary story in Anxi, the hometown of Tieguanyin tea: In 2005, he began his entrepreneurial journey with the official opening of Zhen Tian Tiange Tea Company, writing the first page of his future entrepreneurial blueprint. In 2006, under the advocacy of Su Xingmao, President of the Anxi Tea Association, he established Fujian's first tea cooperative - Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative and served as Deputy Director of the cooperative. In August of the same year, to enrich his tea knowledge and enhance his business philosophy, the General Manager successfully passed the dual certification exams for Senior Tea Taster and Senior Tea Processing Technician. He was also honored to be appointed as a judge for the 2006 Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative Technical Evaluation Competition.

2007 was an extraordinary year. On March 12th, accompanied by Anxi County CPPCC Chairman Su Yulin, former Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial CPPCC, Mr. Su Ming visited our factory and the cooperative, leaving an inscription, infusing boundless vitality into the enterprise. From leaders to employees, everyone showed immense enthusiasm and vitality for the future of the company. To better promote the company's products in the market, General Manager Su Zhiyong attended advanced training for tea tasters. The autumn harvest was abundant, and this year not only produced high-quality autumn tea, but Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory also achieved remarkable success, winning the Gold Award at the Fifth International Agricultural Products Exhibition held in Jinan, Shandong Province from October 12th to 16th under the guidance of the Fujian Provincial Department of Agriculture as the sole representative of Anxi Tieguanyin tea. Regarding the development of the enterprise, it is not only necessary to analyze and respond to the current market economy but also cannot be separated from online promotion. Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory had already preemptively created its own corporate website in 2005, bringing new life to the company. The General Manager of Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory tasted the sweetness of the internet, but as a retired soldier, he did not forget his roots. On November 27th, relevant leaders from the Fujian Provincial Labor Department and Quanzhou City Labor Bureau attended and launched the online trading ceremony of the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative, which was even reported live by CCTV's "Focus on Agriculture," adding luster to Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory and the loving Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative.

Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory always strives for the realm of "zero defects in quality," strictly adhering to the requirements of the national food quality and safety market access (QS) system to continuously improve quality management systems, measurement and testing systems, and product testing systems, ensuring the quality and safety of tea. Whether it is the intrinsic quality of the tea or the external packaging, efforts are made to achieve perfection, constantly surpassing oneself and others. In terms of raw material selection, priority is given to self-owned tea gardens and high-altitude pollution-free premium rough tea purchased from designated locations. Advanced processing equipment is introduced, and strict hygiene standards are maintained in processing areas. Standardized operations are carried out for tea screening, picking, blending, baking, and packaging, with specific operating procedures and technical essentials established for each link, and operations are strictly conducted according to the procedures. Meanwhile, a complete product quality traceability system is established, and once quality issues arise, they are promptly traced and corrected, forming a mechanism and positive atmosphere where "everyone cares about quality issues, and all participate in quality management." The factory passed QS certification in 2008. In 2009, the General Manager of Anxi Zhen Tiange Tea Factory, Su Zhiyong, was also evaluated as a Senior Tea Taster and Tea Processing Technician, making him currently the youngest Senior Tea Taster in Fujian Province.

On November 27, 2007, the online trading launch ceremony of Zhen Tian Tea Farmers' Tea Cooperative and the 2007 Autumn Tieguanyin Initial Processing Technology Evaluation Conference were held in Zhen Tian Village, Changkeng Township, Anxi County. Dong Diwei from Fujian Provincial Agricultural Research Institute, He Changlong from Quanzhou Municipal Agricultural Bureau, and Li Wentong from Anxi Tea Association participated in the launch ceremony.

On March 2, 2006, the Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative was established in Zhen Tian Village, Changkeng Township, Anxi County, marking the establishment of Quanzhou's first tea cooperative. The cooperative is voluntarily formed by tea farmers, tea merchants, and tea enterprises. It is a win-win model solving problems related to dispersed tea production and operation, promoting advanced technologies, expanding sales markets, and safeguarding the interests of tea farmers.

In March 2006, Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative signed a supply and safe use contract with the "Minnan Oolong Tea Pollution-Free Tea Garden Production Materials Distribution Center." The supplier provided pesticides and fertilizers to the cooperative at wholesale prices, unified scientific application guidance for tea farmers, and registered pesticide usage for 101 member households, achieving unified supply of pesticides and fertilizers. This ensured the "source" of tea production met standards, providing reliable guarantees for tea quality.

On April 30, 2006, the "2006 Spring Anxi Tieguanyin Initial Processing Technology Competition" was held at Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative.

In October 2006, Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative held an autumn tea production technology training exchange meeting, inviting senior agricultural technician Lin Huoliang, tea production technology expert Chen Guorong, and Chen Lianban to give lectures on autumn tea production technology.

On March 2, 2007, the Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Farmers Tea Research Institute was established.

From October 12th to 16th, 2007, the Fifth China International Agricultural Products Fair with the theme of "Green Agriculture, Harmonious Rural Areas" was held at the Shandong Jinan International Convention and Exhibition Center. Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative participated in the exhibition area of Fujian's one-village-one-product specialized cooperatives.

On April 20, 2008, Anxi Zhen Tiange Tieguanyin Tea Factory was officially established, marking the opening of the external sales network of Anxi Zhen Tian Tea Cooperative, starting a brand-new marketing system. On the basis of controlling pesticide residues, reducing costs, and improving quality, it allows a wide range of tea dealers and tea enthusiasts to obtain more affordable tea, enabling everyone to buy with confidence and drink with joy.

On August 6, 2008, Anxi Zhen Tiange Tieguanyin Tea Factory officially passed the national food quality and safety certification - QS quality certification.
