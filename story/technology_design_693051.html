Beautiful scenery and new times, Hongji Yongchang. Congratulations on the 20th anniversary of Guangdong Hongchang Chemical Co., Ltd. from "Coatings Business News" newspaper.

In the golden autumn of October, osmanthus fragrance fills the air; at the age of twenty, happiness overflows in Hongchang. For twenty long years, time flows like a song. For twenty years of struggle, there has been brilliance all along. In twenty years of ambition, great achievements have been created, with one triumphant song after another forming a grand symphony.

Back then, you were full of youthful vigor and lofty aspirations, starting to build magnificent dreams with just a few machines and a tile-roofed house. Remember those days when you danced with the wind, seizing opportunities and persistently planning strategies, achieving miraculous growth in the blink of an eye.

Do you remember when you first researched curing agents, enduring countless setbacks before finally obtaining the secret formula? Do you remember your first battle in the Southwest, overcoming difficulties and making a name in Chongqing? Do you remember expanding further into the market, capturing thirty cities? Do you remember exporting overseas in 2004, spreading the good name of Hongchang paint far and wide? Do you remember entering the US market in 2005, leading the industry in innovation...

In just a few years, the product became popular nationwide and sold well overseas, earning the reputation of "Hongchang Double Dragon, the Light of Furniture"! Step by step, achieving remarkable growth; both production and sales thriving, taking flight: The administrative building and technical research center have just been completed, while the water-based paint base and oil-based paint base are opening one after another... The old tile-roofed house has transformed into a ten-thousand-square-meter chemical city; a few old machines have hatched a multi-development international group.

Over twenty years, achievements have been remarkable, indeed impressive! For twenty years, Hongji has supported the pillars, with its "paint craftsmanship" fame spreading far and wide. The concept of technology triumphing has deeply rooted in the company's soul, constantly striving for innovation highs; twenty years of professional paint-making experience has accumulated rich heritage, achieving many firsts within the industry.

With visionary leadership, establishing the first national-level postdoctoral scientific research workstation in the coatings industry; prioritizing strength, shouldering the national 863 Torch Program mission; the plaque of a national high-tech enterprise shines brightly, and the environmental-friendly coating R&D center settles in Hongchang...

With these three unparalleled scientific research platforms, ten core patented technologies independently developed have been born; adhering to the "paint craftsmanship" forged by technology, it has risen to become one of the "Four Great Kings" of China's coatings industry!

For twenty years, achievements have been extraordinary, indeed glorious! For twenty years, vitality remains undiminished, and the torch burns brightly. Pursuing excellence, but also sharing; yearning for success, but more so for growth. People-oriented, a team of elite soldiers has been established; customer-respecting, superior products and quality are offered.

One set after another of exquisite Hongchang and Lishi paints allow consumers to appreciate the charm of science and technology, adding color and brilliance to thousands of homes; carefully organized training sessions and boot camps help dealers expand Hongchang's business, assisting partners in realizing their life dreams; heart-lifting commendation evenings and recreational trips make employees feel warmth and pride, igniting self-confident power...

For twenty years, loyalty has remained unwavering, trust steadfast, and vitality brimming, all because they share one name: China Hongchang!

With great ambition creating great enterprises, Hongji supports the pillars. On the banks of the Xijiang River, Hongchang's brilliant cause shines brightly, growing stronger every day; under the Qipan Mountain, the extraordinary Hongchang people march forward with high morale!

We pay our enthusiastic tribute, we sincerely cheer. May Hongchang's cause soar higher, wider, and farther! May Hongchang's products become better, more beautiful, and stronger!

Let us deeply bless Hongchang: Beautiful scenery and new times, Hongji forever prosperous!
