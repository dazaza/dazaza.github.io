Episode 29

Hong Guk-young devises a plan and writes a letter to Jeong Hou-hyun. After receiving the letter, Jeong Hou-hyun sends the Wu Zhenghao and Bak Chor party to Cheonjangsan, but they are arrested by guards lying in wait there. Realizing his plans have been exposed, Jeong Hou-hyun goes to Hong Guk-young and says he was merely a pawn for others.

Episode 30

Jeong Soon visits Kim Gwi-joo who is imprisoned at Uigunsabu and learns about what has happened. She feels intense anger towards Princess Hwawan. As Jeong Soon leaves Uigunsabu, she encounters Jeong Hou-hyun and angrily slaps him. Jeong Hou-hyun tells Jeong Soon that there is no way to resolve the current situation, making Jeong Soon even angrier. Filled with fear, Jeong Soon rushes to the throne room to see the king, but finds it eerily silent inside.

Episode 31

Princess Hwawan tells Jeong Hou-hyun that the current Yeongjo is nothing more than a puppet, and the time to implement her plan has come. Jeong Hou-hyun asks her what exactly the plan is. Princess Hwawan replies that Jeong Hou-hyun can also ascend the dragon throne.

In despair, Lee Seon gathers his good friends Da-soo and Sung Yeon for a drink. After getting thoroughly drunk, he returns to the palace. Hui-bin summons Sung Yeon after learning about this.

Episode 32

Hui-bin tells Sung Yeon that she will be sent to Qing to study painting. Upon hearing that she might stay in Qing for five years or even longer, Sung Yeon cannot help but shed tears. However, Hui-bin indicates that given the precarious position of the Crown Prince, he cannot afford even the smallest mistake. If Sung Yeon does not want to become a burden to the Crown Prince, she should quietly leave.

Sung Yeon meets Seon in the palace; Seon approaches her happily, but Sung Yeon turns away with tears in her eyes.

Episode 33

Seon learns that Hong Pong-han recommended Sung Yeon to go to Qing before Hui-bin. He questions Hui-bin for the reason. Hui-bin replies that ever since she knew about the connection between Sung Yeon and Seon, she decided to support Sung Yeon's dream of painting.

Upon hearing that Sung Yeon accepted Hui-bin’s suggestion, Lee Seon feels disheartened.

Unexpectedly, Qing officials state that they cannot educate women in painting and thus reject Sung Yeon.

Episode 34

Seon meets Choi Suk-ju alone. Choi Suk-ju tells Seon to halt the ongoing investigation into power factions. Seon counters by asking what Choi Suk-ju intends to offer in return if he accepts his advice.

Jeong Soon suspects Yeongjo's health condition, so she secretly brings a royal physician into Yeongjo's quarters. The physician informs her that Yeongjo may have dementia, and using pueraria root could reveal accurate symptoms. Jeong Soon orders the selection of reliable children from within the royal kitchen.
