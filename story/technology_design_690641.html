Is adult undergraduate education useful? Private Education Service Network

by sudaa4t5 on 2012-03-05 11:20:40

Of course it is useful. After graduation from adult education, the national recognized academic diploma certificate will be issued. The social recognition is relatively lower than that of ordinary undergraduate. However, after you obtain an adult undergraduate degree, when you apply for postgraduate study or other qualification certificate exams, this academic qualification is recognized. Therefore, choose carefully according to your own situation. There are four types of continuing adult education: self-study examination, adult college entrance examination, distance learning, and radio and television university. Among them, the social recognition of self-study examination is the highest, followed by the adult college entrance examination. Here we provide five major types of learning for your reference, hoping it will be helpful to you. Adult College Entrance Examination is one of the ways in continuing adult education. Continuing adult education also includes self-study examination, distance education, and radio and television education. As long as these four types are offered by the school and you meet the application requirements, you can apply. When applying specifically, you can first choose a major you like, or you can first choose a school you like. Here we provide core information of five major types of academic qualifications for your reference, hoping to solve your problem.

Name of Academic Qualification | Admission Threshold | Learning Method | Recruitment Object | Study Period | Study Cost | Certificate Value

Self-Study Examination: There is no admission exam for self-study examination. Candidates take single subject exams, and after all subjects are passed, an academic qualification certificate is issued. There are two to four exams per year. Self-study examination mainly relies on self-study, but candidates can also participate in self-study tutoring classes organized by other social training forces or host schools. It is not restricted by gender, age, nationality, race, and educational level already received, belonging to completely open education. Self-study examination adopts a method of subject-by-subject exam, credit accumulation, and finally obtaining a diploma, without being restricted by semester or academic year system. Only examination fees and textbook purchase fees need to be paid. The cost for graduation from specialized or undergraduate courses does not exceed 1000 yuan. Obtain a higher education self-study examination undergraduate or specialized academic qualification diploma recognized by the state. The academic qualification certificate is the hardest to obtain, with the highest recognition among continuing education.

Adult College Entrance Examination: Adult College Entrance Examination is a nationwide unified recruitment exam, where schools select students based on the exam results. The unified exam is held once a year in October. There are mainly three learning methods for adult college entrance examination: full-time study, part-time study, and correspondence study. Adult College Entrance Examination mainly recruits local residents, but non-local students can also study through correspondence. The study period for Adult College Entrance Examination varies depending on the recruitment level and the major studied, taking as short as 2 years, and as long as 5-6 years. The tuition fee is about 3500 yuan per academic year. Obtain a higher education undergraduate or specialized academic qualification diploma recognized by the state, with slightly less recognition compared to self-study examination.

Network College: Network University sets its own questions and organizes exams. Only those who pass can enter. There are two teaching methods: one is online teaching through the network, and the other is concentrated face-to-face teaching. This applies to on-the-job personnel, employees, and other members of society. There is no geographical restriction on the recruitment scope. Network education generally implements flexible academic systems, mostly ranging from 2 to 6 years. The tuition fee is around 5000 yuan per academic year. Obtain a specialized or undergraduate academic qualification diploma from a network college recognized by the state.

Private Colleges: No examination is required, and registration can be done upon payment of tuition fees. Students at private colleges have full-time classes arranged by teachers. This applies to on-the-job personnel, employees, etc., with no geographical restrictions on the recruitment scope. Private colleges offer 2-year specialized programs and 4-year undergraduate programs. The tuition fee is about 6000 yuan per academic year. Upon completion of the study period, graduates receive a graduation certificate issued by the private college.

Radio and Television University: No examination is required, and registration can be done as long as the relevant academic qualifications are met. Radio and Television University also offers two methods: online classrooms and face-to-face teaching. This applies to on-the-job personnel, employees, etc., with no geographical restrictions on the recruitment scope. Radio and Television University education implements a credit system, with a 2-year academic system and an 8-year valid student status. The tuition fee is about 2000 yuan per academic year. Obtain a specialized or undergraduate academic qualification diploma from the Radio and Television University recognized by the state.
