As the year-end approaches, all kinds of star rankings burst forth, dazzling to behold. Just as some sexy male rankings are released, the sexy female rankings follow suit in waves, with a profusion of rosy faces that evoke the breath of spring.

Following the recent selection of the "25 Sexiest Men in the World," the American entertainment channel "E!" has now selected the "Sexiest Woman in the World." Topping the list is Czech supermodel Karolína Kurková. The dazzling beauty of Karolína and other angels at the "Victoria's Secret" event was breathtaking, propelling several of them onto the list. Without further ado, let's delve into the rankings.

No.1 Supermodel Karolína Kurková

Karolína Kurková’s healthy and radiant appearance captivates people deeply. Many say she is "simply a miracle:" hardworking, upright, and amiable. Kurková comes from a good family background, and when she decided to pursue modeling, her parents gave her full support.

At 15, after arriving in New York, Kurková was quickly discovered by the renowned DNA Model Management and soon became the cover girl for American Vogue magazine. She is now an exclusive model for top brands such as Victoria's Secret, Tommy Hilfiger, Chanel, etc., earning six figures daily. In the highly corrosive fashion industry, Kurková remains pure-hearted and highly ambitious.

No.2 Supermodel Bar Rafaeli

Israeli supermodel Bar Rafaeli is also one of the "Victoria's Secret" angels. Her devilish figure and angelic face make her a favorite among many lingerie and swimsuit brands.

No.3 Actress Angelina Jolie

No introduction needed; she is the best combination of sensuality and maternity in today's Hollywood.

A fierce battle in Hollywood recently took place between two women: Angelina Jolie and Jennifer Aniston, all because of one man, Brad Pitt. However, this incident did not affect people's affection for her.

Everything about Angelina Jolie is a topic in Hollywood: her estrangement from her father, her romantic relationships and marriages surrounded by endless gossip, and her charitable endeavors. Every move of Angelina Jolie draws Hollywood's attention.

No.4 Supermodel Gisele Bündchen

Born on July 20, 1980, in a small village in southern Brazil, Gisele began her modeling career in 1995. With five sisters, each blessed with a similar physique and beauty, they are all supermodels on the runway. Gisele was nicknamed "Oliver" (the girlfriend of the cartoon character Popeye) due to her tall and slim build during school days.

Gisele has modeled for all major fashion stores and appeared on numerous world-famous magazine covers. She sports a navel ring and a star tattoo on her left wrist. This young, wealthy, and famous model maintains a natural and charming personality. Her breasts are world-renowned, earning her nicknames like "The Beauty Warrior from Brazil."

In 2004, Gisele made her film debut in "Taxi Queen." In 2006, she starred in "The Devil Wears Prada," where her stunning beauty and perfect figure added much to the movie. Gisele has also been a lingerie model for "Victoria's Secret."

No.5 Actress Scarlett Johansson

Born in 1984, Scarlett Johansson is one of the most promising teenage stars in Hollywood today. Born in New York City, Scarlett is a fraternal twin with her brother.

At just 12 years old, Scarlett Johansson won the American Independent Spirit Award. In 1998, at only 14, she starred in "The Horse Whisperer," which earned her a New Star Award.

In 2000, Scarlett appeared in films like "Ghost World" and "American Rhapsody," mostly independent productions. However, her outstanding performances caught the attention of Hollywood studios, leading to her role in the sci-fi horror film "Eight Legged Freaks" in 2002. That same year, she starred in Sofia Coppola's "Lost in Translation," receiving widespread acclaim. Subsequently, she starred in "Girl with a Pearl Earring." These two films simultaneously earned her nominations for Best Actress in both drama and comedy categories at the 2004 Golden Globe Awards, making her one of the hottest newcomers in Hollywood.

No.6 Supermodel Adriana Lima

Another "Victoria's Secret" lingerie angel, Adriana Lima from Brazil, possesses a golden figure that captivates the heart.

Adriana Lima was born on June 12, 1981, in Salvador, Brazil. Unlike her aspirations to become a model, it was an accidental opportunity that changed her life, leading her away from school to appear in fashion magazines and on runways, becoming the focus of millions.

No.7 Supermodel Heidi Klum

From Germany, Heidi Klum is another "Victoria's Secret" angel.

In the winter of 1991, encouraged by a friend, Heidi entered a model competition while still in school. Everything changed from there.

In 1992, she stood out in a strict model selection contest co-organized by Petra magazine and a New York model agency, entering the top three to participate in the Gottschalk Saturday Show.

She started modeling for Petrol catalogues and became one of the top models in Paris and Milan, advancing towards the big city of New York.

Soon, she became the image spokesperson for more and more large advertisements, such as Bonne Bell, Finesse, Gerry Weber, Givenchy’s Amerige, I-N-C, etc.

Appearing in "Victoria's Secret," she joined the ranks of world-renowned lingerie models.

She undertook cover assignments for various publications and when her photo appeared in the swimsuit edition of Sports Illustrated, she instantly became one of the top figures in the modeling world.

She continued to develop in the film and television industries, playing roles in the comedic series "Spin City" and investing in "54."

Beautiful and humorous, she eventually married hairstylist Ric Pipino.

No.8 Actress Penélope Cruz

The influence of Spanish rose Penélope Cruz in Hollywood is growing stronger. Her exotic beauty feels fresh and full.

English name: Penélope Cruz

Other names: Penelope, Pani Lopu - Cruz

Birthday: April 28, 1974

Country: Spain

Awarded: 1998 Spanish Film Queen ("The Girl of Your Dreams")

1993 "Belle Epoque" won the Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film

1999 "All About My Mother" won the Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film

Born on April 28, 1974, in Madrid, Spain, Penélope Cruz first entered the entertainment industry through collaborations with musician Nacho Cano on music videos. In 1991, she acted in her first film "Greek Labyrinth" directed by Rafael Alcázar, followed by "Hamlet, Hamlet" directed by Bigas Luna in 1992, establishing her place in the Spanish film industry. Subsequently, she received numerous film offers, including "Beautiful Season," "Rebels," and "Because of Love" in the same year.

In 1994, Cruz moved to the U.S. to develop her career. In 1995, she collaborated with Nick Hamm in "Angel's Kiss." In 1997, she worked with the famous director Pedro Almodóvar for the first time, playing a minor role in his film "Live Flesh." In 1998, her lead role in "The Girl of Your Dreams" earned unanimous praise from fans and propelled her to international stardom. In 1999, she once again collaborated with Almodóvar in "All About My Mother," bringing her great honor. Her presence graced the award stages of Cannes, Berlin, Venice, and the Oscars, making Penélope Cruz the second Spaniard, after Antonio Banderas, to successfully break into Hollywood. In 2006, she collaborated with Almodóvar again in "Volver," marking her return to her homeland after years of working in Hollywood.

Cruz's beauty has also provided plenty of gossip material. Rumors have linked her with Matt Damon, Nicolas Cage, Tom Cruise (with whom she was reportedly close to marriage before suddenly splitting), Matthew McConaughey (from "Sahara"), and most recently, her compatriot Javier Bardem.

No.9 Advertising Star, Actress Manuela Arcuri

Arcuri is a household name in Italy, also known as the top football babe due to her associations with almost all the handsome men in Italian sports. As Italy's top advertising star, Arcuri is also called the Juventus Godmother. However, her interests extend beyond Juventus, having been linked with "Rome Prince" Francesco Totti, Inter Milan's Cristian Chivu, AC Milan's Filippo Inzaghi, and even Olympic fencing champion Aldo Montano.

No.10 Singer Shakira

Latin queen Shakira radiates wild passion, her mesmerizing hips charm cannot be matched, and her voice is filled with infectious energy.

Is she a witch or an angel? That’s the question. For Shakira, this question may never find an answer. She is certainly not purely innocent but always evokes a sense of sanctity. She is definitely not unrestrained, yet often wildly passionate and fiery. Perhaps she is the spirit lingering between witch and angel...

Shakira was born on February 2, 1977, in Barranquilla, Colombia, with the full name Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll. She has Lebanese and Colombian dual heritage, perfectly blending Arab and Latin qualities. She is a naturally gifted artist who combines songwriting and performance talents, sensuality, passion, and elegance.
