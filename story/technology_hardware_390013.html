【Original】11 Types of People Suitable for Running an Online Store

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 13:15:11

Hello everyone, I am wangzi, which means prince in Chinese. Please don't take it the wrong way, I'm not saying I am a prince, it's just my nickname. I hope that in the future we can all learn from each other and exchange more ideas about running online stores!

Alright, let's discuss who is most suitable for running an online store! If you're still a beginner, you can also see which type you belong to! (This is purely my personal opinion)

The first type of person: Good mentality

These people have a positive and optimistic attitude. Having a good mindset is a prerequisite for success. Although mindset is not the only condition for success, it is a necessary condition. Otherwise, many large companies and enterprises would not be constantly emphasizing the issue of mindset. (Let me tell you a secret, when I used to work at a large shoe company, that company held morning meetings every day, and every day they emphasized the same issue - mindset. At the time, I didn't pay much attention to it, but after I left, I reflected on it and found it quite meaningful. This is actually part of the company culture, and the company does this to improve employee quality. The company invests a lot of resources in this work, but very few truly understand it.) Hehe, sorry for digressing. I just wanted to give you an example to let you know how important having the right mindset is. As long as we have the right mindset, no problem will be too difficult, and all obstacles can be overcome. Is your mindset correct?

The second type of person: Very smart people

These people are quick-witted and agile in thinking. They often act faster than others and put their thoughts into action immediately. However, this is not meant to belittle those who are less intelligent. I myself belong to the less intelligent type, but that doesn't mean we can't succeed. I firmly believe that as long as we are not intellectually disabled, anything is possible. That brings us to the next type of person. I must remind those smart people not to be overconfident like in the "Tortoise and the Hare" story. I'm sure there are quite a few such people in reality. Here, I'm referring to only some individuals. Don't shoot me! Actually, whether or not someone is like that, only they themselves know best. For instance, some people think they have better conditions than others, so they look down on them and think they are superior. I'm sure such people do exist. Reflect on yourself and strive to become a perfect smart person!

The third type of person: Hardworking and active people

These people are willing to work hard, endure hardships, and are proactive. So even if they are not very smart, as long as they are willing to make efforts, think, observe, and invest more time and energy than others, they will eventually succeed. I suddenly remembered a phrase: The secret to success is to make a little more effort every day than others. Yes, have you done that?

The fourth type of person: People with strong organizational skills and efficiency

These people have strong execution capabilities. No matter how difficult the task, they can always complete it in the end. This shows that they have a good plan before they start doing things. Of course, this plan includes many things. Simply put, it is the process of analyzing a task thoroughly, formulating a plan, and implementing it. Therefore, we can also become such people, but we need to practice to reach that level. Although it is a bit challenging, we must have perseverance, persistence, and determination to continue until the end! Can you do that?

The fifth type of person: People who are not content with the status quo and keep striving

These people are never satisfied with their current state and are always striving to do better. This is like someone getting paid. The first time they get 1000 yuan and see others getting 2000, they feel that others have much more. But when they get 2000, they don't feel it's enough anymore and want even more. These are people who are not content with the status quo. As the saying goes, people aim high while water flows low. Some might say, isn't this person too greedy? Yes, instead of calling it greed, let's call it ambition. Starting a business precisely requires such ambition, but one should know when to stop, otherwise it could backfire. So, if you were the one getting paid, what would you think?

The sixth type of person: People with life wealth goals

Actually, these people are similar to the ones mentioned earlier. They have high expectations for their lives and dare to pursue them. Basically, they are also not content with the status quo.

The seventh type of person: People who can let go of face-saving

This means that if you want to get rich, you need to have a thick skin. Take handing out flyers for example, many people might find it embarrassing to run around the streets handing out flyers because they might be shy or afraid of meeting acquaintances. Hehe, I once had such an experience. It was the first time I did that, helping a friend. At first, I was really shy, fearing that people wouldn't accept them and also being afraid of meeting acquaintances. I hesitated and couldn't even stretch out my hand, so obviously, no one took them. Later, when I saw my friend handing them out confidently, I decided to do the same. Surprisingly, people actually accepted them. Back then, I was young and inexperienced, and to finish the task quickly, I handed out several at a time. Now, looking back, it seems quite amusing. So, if it were you, could you do that?

The eighth type of person: The most innovative people

These people have unique insights and always stay ahead of others. They always let others chase after them. This corresponds to the saying: When others don't have it, I do; when others have it, I excel; when others excel, I change. Can you dare to innovate?

The ninth type of person: Very confident people

These people, no matter what they do, always have confidence in themselves first. They believe they can achieve, believe they can definitely do it, and believe they are the best. With confidence, they tackle tasks with full assurance. Do you have confidence? Or are you constantly blaming yourself and complaining?

The tenth type of person: People who can endure hardship

These people are not afraid of hard work and will not give up no matter how tough or exhausting it gets. Many people may not be able to endure hardship; once they feel tired, they develop the thought of giving up. This only shows that their willpower is not strong enough, and they cannot endure for the sake of their goals. If you want to succeed, you must not fear hardship and must also be patient. Success is not achieved overnight. Can you endure loneliness?

The eleventh type of person: People who cherish time

This is also very important. No matter what you do, efficiency is measured by time. Only those who cherish time can succeed faster than others. People often say that cherishing time is equivalent to cherishing life, and time is money. Are you making good use of your time? Don't let others get up at 7 in the morning while you're still lying in bed dreaming comfortably. Don't say you have nothing to do and can sleep more. Tasks are something you seek out to do, not something that comes to you. If you wait for things to come to you, they will likely be troubles, and good things may never come your way.

Before I knew it, I've said so much. These are all my personal views. If we apply these to online stores, which ones do you need to achieve? In short, remember that having a good mindset is the most important thing. Combine it with actual actions, and you won't be far from success. Keep going!

Alright, it's late. I also hope everyone can share their own opinions and ideas. Let's exchange and discuss together! Prince wishes everyone a booming business and abundant wealth!
