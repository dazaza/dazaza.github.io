In order to get a unit reward house and fake divorce, do you still remember Huang Hong's sketch "Smart Husband" in this year's Spring Festival Gala? In real life, more and more couples join the ranks of divorce for houses.

Too high divorce rate, hurt the hearts of unmarried people

Before the 2011 Spring Festival, Yao Chen and Ling Xiaosu, a model couple who had always shown off their love in front of people and microblogs, finally failed to survive the "seven-year itch", and got divorced in Xi'an. Subsequently, they also exposed their "new lovers", which made many fans feel that their concept of love was destroyed. Similarly, marriage is also like this for many ordinary people. It costs 9 yuan to get two books to get married, and the same 9 yuan can also exchange two divorce certificates, sentencing the end of a marriage.

"Now getting married, I really can't guarantee whether I will end up getting divorced one day, and if so, I have to do a property notarization first." Mr. Wang, who is not yet married, said that affected by the increasingly high divorce rate now, he would consider all possibilities of divorce before getting married and make preparations in advance.

To 8192 pairs in 2010

I learned from the Marriage Registration Office of the Tiqiao District Civil Affairs Bureau that shortly after the introduction of the purchase restrictions, the number of people getting divorced has significantly increased. "There are usually 5 pairs of divorces a day, and recently there are an average of 9 pairs a day, with the most being more than ten pairs in a day." The staff speculated that some people were exactly going through divorce to avoid the "purchase restrictions". The age of the "fake divorce" couples is normally between 40 and 60 years old, and most already own two properties. During the divorce procedures, the properties will be attributed to one of the spouses or the child’s name. Those who have not specified the method of property distribution in the divorce agreement mostly complete the property transfer procedures before the divorce procedures.

The statistics of the number of divorces in various districts of the city from 2005 to 2010 show that the number of divorces in Shizhong District in 2007 was higher than in 2008, which does not conform to the general rule of annual increase. Compared with 2006, it increased by 340 pairs, and this growth rate is much higher than the annual increase of more than 100 pairs in other years. "2007 was the year when the Wei Jiazhuang shantytown renovation began." Sun Ai Jun, director of the Marriage Registration Office of the Shizhong District Civil Affairs Bureau explained that the emergence of this sudden large increase phenomenon was also related to the demolition and relocation in relevant areas.

Beware of "real acting" in fake divorce and losing both person and property

According to Jinan City's "purchase restriction order" issued this year, starting from March 1, 2011, families of residents with local hukou who already own one housing unit will be restricted to purchasing one housing unit (including new commercial housing and second-hand housing) within the urban area of the city. For families of residents with local hukou who already own two or more housing units in the city, and families of non-local hukou residents who already own one or more housing units, the sale of housing to them in the urban area of the city will be suspended.

The phenomenon of a sharp increase in divorce due to demolition also occurred in Lixia District. "When Yaojia relocated, the divorces were particularly concentrated, with more than 30 pairs of divorced couples handled in a single day," said the staff at the Lixia District Civil Affairs Bureau's marriage registration office.

Whether it is the previous demolition or the newly introduced purchase restriction order, there have been people consulting about the issue of "fake divorce." Lawyer Li Lingxiu of Shandong Haocai Law Firm said that under such circumstances, lawyers would warn the parties involved that "fake divorce" carries great risks, and not to obtain apparent benefits by exploiting loopholes, ending up losing both person and property.

Can anyone see through the authenticity of divorce?

In the movie "If You Are the One 2," it was said, "No matter how you choose marriage, it is wrong, and a long-lasting marriage is about making the wrong choice." When conflicts arising from daily trivial matters and kitchenware become increasingly irreconcilable, more and more people are unwilling to continue tolerating like people in the past, but instead join the "divorce army."

"Others may think I value his success and wealth, but it's not that simple." Xiao Gao (a pseudonym), who was originally engaged to her boyfriend of similar age, made a decision that everyone around her couldn't understand: she broke up with her boyfriend and accepted a middle-aged man who had been pursuing her and was fifteen years older than her.

Fake divorce is no longer a novelty

On March 21, at the Lixia District Civil Affairs Bureau's marriage registration office, in just half an hour, four or five pairs of couples came to handle divorce procedures, all around thirty or forty years old. One pair assigned all three properties they currently owned to the male's name. Another pair drew particular attention; during the process, the two were constantly laughing and talking. When the staff asked about the custody of the child, the woman smiled and asked the man: "With you or with me? How old should we raise him? You fill it out as you see fit."

According to the statistical data from the marriage registration offices of the five urban districts of Lixia, Shizhong, Huaiyin, Tianqiao, and Lic城 in Jinan City, the number of divorces in each district basically shows a yearly increasing trend. The total number of divorces in the five districts has increased from 4503 pairs in 2005 to 8192 pairs in 2010, almost doubling in six years.

Regarding the reasons for the continuous increase in the number of divorces, Fu Qiang, a representative of the Jinan Municipal People's Congress and the director of Shandong Pengfei Law Firm, believes that overall, it is due to social changes leading to significant changes in people's values and views on marriage and love. Traditional marriage concepts have been greatly impacted, and people have become more casual about love and life.

"Fake divorce" properties generally will be registered under one person's name. For families of residents with local hukou who already own two or more housing units in the city, and families of non-local hukou residents who already own one or more housing units, the sale of housing to them in the urban area of the city will be suspended.

Fu Qiang, a representative of the Jinan Municipal People's Congress and the director of Shandong Pengfei Law Firm, also stated that some couples who are "fake divorcing" will privately sign other agreements regarding property distribution, etc., but these secretly signed agreements, if only one party denies, will not have legal effect, and will still be allocated according to the divorce agreement.

From 4503 pairs in 2005

After the purchase restriction order was issued, it made many investors rack their brains, thinking of ways to bypass it. Thus, the trick of "fake divorce" emerged, and love and marriage, in the face of housing, became so worthless and fragile. In the face of such a situation, it seems worth pondering where we should go from here. ― Editor

Li Lingxiu stated that those who "fake divorce" for housing generally assign multiple properties to one person's name in the divorce agreement. Once this agreement is signed, it becomes legally binding. Even if the two parties remarry, the properties have already become one party's private property, not jointly owned by the couple.

Actually, "fake divorce" for housing issues is not a new product after the introduction of purchase restrictions. "In the past, this often happened during demolition and when purchasing affordable housing," said the staff at the Tianqiao District Civil Affairs Bureau's marriage registration office.

"In fact, it is taking advantage of policy loopholes, but don't say we can't distinguish, even if we know clearly that it is for another purpose, we cannot refuse to handle it." Regarding the situation of "fake divorce" for demolition compensation or purchase restrictions, relevant staff of the civil affairs department said that proposing divorce requests for reasons such as "emotional breakdown" is people's freedom and not illegal.
