How to buy fertilizer in the Happy Farm on QQ Alumni?

by zhangling on 2009-11-23 22:19:16

"Happy Farm" helps with crop planting in the following ways:

1. Go to the store to buy seeds, then plant them in already cultivated land, crops will grow automatically;

2. Need to weed, water, and exterminate pests in a timely manner, otherwise the health value of the crops will drop which will affect the yield of the crops; (You can also help your friends weed, water, and exterminate pests. Besides gaining experience, you can also get coin rewards.)

3. After crops mature, harvest them into the warehouse promptly. Each purchase of a background or decorative item will give corresponding experience points.

4. Each player starts with only six plots of land.

5. Cultivation: To cultivate each plot of land requires spending a certain amount of coins and having the corresponding level restrictions:

- First plot: 10,000 coins (level 5 required)

- Second plot: 20,000 coins (level 7 required)

- Third plot: 30,000 coins (level 9 required)

- Fourth plot: 50,000 coins (level 11 required)

- Fifth plot: 70,000 coins (level 13 required)

- Sixth plot: 90,000 coins (level 15 required)

- Seventh plot: 120,000 coins (level required...)

- Eleventh plot: 300,000 coins (level 25 required)

- Twelfth plot: 500,000 coins (level 27 required)

Common Questions:

6. What is the meaning of multi-season crops? It means that after planting once, the crop can be harvested multiple times.

7. What does "produce xx remain xx" mean? "Produce xx remain xx" indicates that the crop has matured. Produce xx refers to the quantity produced by the crop, while remain xx refers to the remaining quantity after the crop has been stolen.

8. Why can't I steal from my friend's mature crops? After each crop matures, not only can each person only steal once, but the crop can only be stolen up to 40% of its output...

9. How do I leave messages for my friends and view various messages? On the left side of the game, why can't I see my friend in the farm's friend list after they have activated Happy Farm? In the upper right corner of our Happy Farm friend list, there is a refresh button. Clicking this button will show all currently active Happy Farm friends~ PS: If it takes a long time to display, please right-click on the Happy Farm interface, select "Settings" from the pop-up menu, go to "Local Storage", and increase the storage size, usually 1M is enough.

10. Why does it keep saying no watering is needed? Normally, land does not need watering unless it dries out, i.e., when the color of the land changes, then watering is necessary.

11. Why have two of my plots changed color? This means your land has dried out and needs watering. Click on the watering can tool in the toolbar below and sprinkle water on the dry land. If you don't water, the health value of your crops will gradually decrease, slowing down their maturity.

12. What should I do if weeds and pests grow on my land? When weeds and pests grow automatically on our land, or when our friends come to our land to cause trouble—planting weeds and releasing pests—we must use weeding agents or pesticides to remove weeds and kill pests respectively, otherwise it will seriously affect the growth of our crops.

13. How do I harvest mature fruits? Once our fruits are mature, we can click on the hand-shaped tool to collect the fruits, which will be automatically stored in our warehouse.

14. How do I take care of the backgrounds and decorations I already own? Just click on what you want to use to DIY. After purchasing decorations, if they do not immediately appear, it is recommended to log back into the game after some time to refresh.

15. How can I gain more experience points? Actions such as buying decorations, planting, pest control, weeding, harvesting, and plowing will increase experience points.

16. Why don't I gain experience points when I kill pests, weed, or water? For increasing experience points, actions like killing pests, weeding, and watering are limited. When the total number of pest-killing and weeding actions exceeds 150 times, experience points will no longer increase, only coins will.

17. What determines the ranking in the friend leaderboard? The friend leaderboard ranks players based on each player's level.

18. How can I see the cash amount of my friends? Enter your friend's farm, click on the house inside your friend's farm or your friend's avatar, and you can see your friend's cash assets, as well as the historical total quantity of fruits obtained from each type of crop.

19. What should I do if a blank page appears or doesn't display properly? Check if your flash player is the latest version. The current version is 10. Download address: ADOBE FLASH PLAYER. The current version is Adobe Flash Player version 10.0.22.87.

20. Where are the seeds and fertilizers I purchased placed? Just click on the items you've already purchased! !
