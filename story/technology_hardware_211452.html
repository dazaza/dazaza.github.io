HKC mythos adopts the design of the straight board touch screen, and the whole shape is simple and atmospheric; It uses the Windows Mobile 6.1 professional operating system and uses a 3.2-inch 65536-color TFT screen with a resolution of 240×. 400, the display effect is excellent, and the machine also supports GPS function and TV-OUT. HKC Mythos boldly follows the design style of Korean models, and the 3.2-inch fashion mirror screen occupies most of the area of the front. The screen is surrounded by a smooth, mirrored plastic panel that makes it easy to collect fingerprints and dust. Below the panel is the main keyboard area, the use of sub-black plastic material, the work is a little rough. The sides of Mythos are surrounded by a subsilver band, made of plastic but with a metallic feel. The back is also black in tone, and a lot of pinstripes are added to increase the feel and anti-slip. The overall design model is cuboid, and the four corners are rounded to make it look small and thin, which is in line with Asian aesthetics.

image

The body size of HKC Mythos is only 105× 56. 12.9mm, you can see it's not very thick. Mythos is paired with a 3.2-inch 16:9 65536 color TFT color screen with a resolution of 240× 400, the use of resistive touch screen technology, the actual display effect is good. The distribution of buttons on the side of Mythos is relatively simple, the camera design on the back is handsome, and the logo in the Microsoft window below indicates that Mythos is a Mobile phone using the Windows Mobile system.

image image

Mythos mobile phone adopts the mainstream Windows Mobile6.1 operating system, the interface, function menu and other similar mobile phones are much the same, but in the preset Today theme, there is a Smart Today small plug-in can be used, which has a certain 3D effect. It makes it easier to check the time, adjust the alarm, open programs, and turn on Settings. In essence, is a shortcut small plug-in bar, you can set their favorite or commonly used programs on the above, practical good.

At the same time, because it is a 3G mobile phone customized by Tianyi, 3G application functions such as Internet and number Bestong are set up in advance on the standby interface. In addition, there is a small detail, in the lower right corner of the screen preset task manager, very easy to manage background programs.
