Big Exposure of Stars' Gossip: On January 18, NetEase Entertainment reported that the audience who have watched the TV version of "Martial Brothers" must certainly know that Yan Ni's portrayal of Mrs. Tong is undoubtedly the most charming woman in the Jianghu (ancient Chinese martial world). In the upcoming movie version, Mrs. Tong remains as charming as ever but has transformed into a professional ancient career manager, meticulously calculating and leading her team across the Jianghu.

The boss's style is authoritative

As the head of the Tongfu Inn, being just charming is not enough. In the movie version, Yan Ni fully displays her big sister charm, portraying a decisive and authoritative innkeeper to the fullest extent. Although these heroes have a certain status in the Jianghu, once they enter the inn, they all become subordinates of "Mrs. Tong". As a leader, one naturally needs to have authority, so in the new play, whether it's managing the inn or leading the heroes to cleverly retreat from real estate villains, Yan Ni is always full of presence and takes charge. Everyone also listens to her obediently, like stars surrounding the moon.

Team spirit with righteousness first

To say that as a manager, one can develop their career successfully, naturally cannot be separated from a loyal team, so team awareness is particularly important. On one hand, "Mrs. Tong" needs to be the backbone, but on the other hand, she still needs to care about her team. In life, they are inseparable friends who share happiness and difficulties. Whether it's daily life or inner emotions, she needs to take care of everything meticulously. In the movie, "Mrs. Tong" fully displays her righteous chivalry: sincerely inviting "Big Mouth" back to the team, after sending away "Guo Fucong", she quietly cares about everything in her life behind the scenes, which deeply touches the sisterhood feelings. "Wushuang" may be doing things outside, but "Mrs. Tong" has always been concerned about her marriage issues. It's with this sincerity that she moves everyone around her, so no matter how difficult it is, everyone will stay by Mrs. Tong's side, never leaving.

Bold and Strategic in the Business World

As everyone knows, the new version of "Martial Brothers" uses the hottest topic of property speculation today, telling a financial crisis story of the Tongfu Inn. In order to cleverly retreat from the real estate developers and protect the Seven Heroes Town, making everyone clearly aware of the real estate bubble issue, "Mrs. Tong" leads everyone to strategize, using various strategies such as the "beauty strategy", "bitter meat strategy", and "empty city strategy" in front of the real estate developers. Facing the main villain "Ji Wuli", Mrs. Tong remains calm and collected, impeaching and deploying troops like a military strategist, engaging in intellectual battles. Finally, under everyone's united efforts, they not only win the victory but also help the Tongfu Inn get out of the economic crisis.

Although it is a costume comedy, the movie version of "Martial Brothers" depicts a modern business storm, integrating the topic of property speculation into the film as one of its highlights. The main actors also reflect various roles in the modern workplace, especially Mrs. Tong, this career manager. Besides bringing joy to everyone, imparting the way of career managers and survival rules in the workplace has become one of the hidden themes of the movie.
