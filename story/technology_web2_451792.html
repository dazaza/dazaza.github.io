This year, the legal work of our bureau followed the requirements of the State Council's "Comprehensive Promotion of the Implementation Outline for Governing the Country According to Law" and the five-year plan of the provincial, municipal, and district governments for comprehensively promoting governance according to law. We established an administrative law enforcement post responsibility system, specifically implementing statutory duties prescribed by laws, regulations, and rules to law enforcement posts and personnel; formulated supervisory inspection and investigation measures, layer-by-layer implementing responsibilities; built a clear accountability mechanism for lawful administration, ensuring that the administrative actions of our bureau were legal and effective. Below is a summary of the legal work throughout the year:

### I. Deeply Carrying Out Socialist Rule of Law Concept Education

In accordance with the arrangements and requirements of the central, provincial, municipal, and district Political and Legal Affairs Commissions and the Municipal Justice Bureau regarding socialist rule of law concept education, our bureau carried out all relevant tasks.

1. **Strengthening Learning, Enhancing Awareness**

We organized cadres and employees to seriously study the relevant documents, materials, and "Socialist Rule of Law Concept Education Reader" issued by the central, provincial, municipal, and district Political and Legal Affairs Commissions and the Municipal Justice Bureau. Through learning, we enhanced the awareness of our cadres and employees regarding the concept of governing the country according to law and strengthened their self-discipline in performing specific tasks.

2. **Establishing Law Enforcement Thinking, Firmly Upholding the People-Oriented Concept**

We educated judicial administrative police officers and law enforcement service personnel to firmly establish the thoughts of being close to the people, loving the people, and serving the people, always adhering to the principle of safeguarding and realizing the fundamental interests of the broadest masses as the starting point and ultimate goal of judicial administrative work. We shifted perspectives, establishing the subject status of the masses, ensuring rights, humanized law enforcement, and serving the people's consciousness. This further deepened the feelings between the judiciary and the people, placing the warmth and safety of the masses in our hearts, truly delving into the masses, getting closer to them, listening carefully to public appeals, caring about their hardships, transforming our deep feelings for the masses into the driving force for doing well in judicial administrative work and legal service work. We devoted ourselves wholeheartedly to doing practical things for the people, thinking what the masses think, acting swiftly when the people are in urgent need, enforcing the law strictly and fairly, upholding fairness and justice, and effectively safeguarding judicial equity.

3. **Firmly Establishing the Concept of Serving the Big Picture, Strengthening Legal Services**

Judicial administrative organs and legal service institutions such as law firms, notary offices, and grassroots legal service offices actively serve the core work of the District Party Committee and District Government, deeply engaging in the main battlefield of New Capital economic construction, continuously meeting societal needs for law, providing judicial guarantees and legal services for the economic development and social stability of the entire district.

### II. Implementing Administrative Law Enforcement Responsibility System

To comprehensively promote the construction of standardized service-oriented agencies, improve administrative efficiency, and standardize administrative law enforcement actions, our bureau established and implemented the administrative law enforcement responsibility system.

1. **Established a Qualification Certification System for Law Enforcement Personnel**

The qualification certificate acquisition rate and on-the-job certification rate for administrative law enforcement personnel reached 100%.

2. **Established a Learning and Training System**

Training was provided to administrative law enforcement personnel applying for the "Administrative Law Enforcement Certificate" on basic and professional laws and regulations such as the "Administrative Punishment Law," "State Compensation Law," "Administrative Review Law," "Administrative Litigation Law," and "Administrative Licensing Law." The training coverage and pass rate reached 100%.

3. **Established an Administrative Law Enforcement Publicity System**

Statutory law enforcement scope, responsibilities, standards, conditions, time limits, procedures, and "Department Standardized Service Details" along with operational processes were publicly displayed, achieving a 100% publicity rate.

4. **Established Complaint, Reporting, and Case Follow-Up Systems**

The legally accepted complaint and reporting rate from parties involved reached 100%.

5. **Established and Implemented the 'Bureau Chief Reception Day' System**

Enhanced duty shifts, including weekends, holidays, with specific responsibilities assigned to bureau leaders, middle-level cadres, and individual staff members.

6. **Lawyers Participated in Major Decisions of the District Party Committee and District Government**

Provided legal advice for normative documents, promoting the scientific and legal decision-making of leadership.

### III. Governing According to Law, Enforcing Strictly

Our bureau combined socialist rule of law concept education activities with administrative law enforcement supervision and inspection work. Strictly following the provisions of laws and regulations such as the "Lawyers Law," "Notary Law," and "Grassroots Judicial Services Regulations," we strengthened the professional ethics and practice discipline education of agency personnel, lawyers, notaries, and grassroots legal service workers; established and improved internal management systems for the bureau, law firms, notary offices, and grassroots legal service offices; achieved governance according to law and standardized management. We strengthened the management and supervision of the notary service market, increased administrative law enforcement efforts, investigated and banned illegal cross-regional practices by certain notary offices in our area, maintaining the order of the notary service market. Among legal service personnel, we established the idea of "loyalty to the law, upholding justice, honesty and integrity, diligence and responsibility, fair competition, and strict self-discipline," fully leveraging the important role of agency cadres, lawyers, notaries, and grassroots legal service workers in democratic legal construction and building a harmonious society.

Through socialist rule of law concept education activities and law enforcement supervision and inspection, we further strengthened the legal construction of judicial administration in our district, improving the level of strict and impartial law enforcement. Throughout the year, there were no cases of administrative reconsideration, administrative litigation, or administrative compensation, fully completing the objectives and tasks of legal work, promoting various judicial administrative businesses to new heights.
