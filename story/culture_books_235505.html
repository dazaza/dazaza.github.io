"The butterfly lovers" literal translation does not fully convey the meaning. A better translation might be "We do not have the wings of a colorful phoenix to fly together, yet our hearts are connected by a single point of spiritual understanding." What ancient Chinese referred to as 'spiritual understanding', is now known as the 'third consciousness'. This book is an authentic reasoning book that explains the third consciousness, distinct from the suspense and horror books in the market which use terror, bloodshed, and violence as selling points. The entire book is divided into three volumes, with this volume's story set in a university campus.

A prank among friends led to Lin Xier, the 'campus flower' of Northern College, meeting an unnatural death. Subsequently, a series of inexplicable 'paranormal events' occurred on the Northern campus: the 'hair-combing woman' in the midnight girls' dormitory building, piano sounds from Room 307, and a male student suddenly dying on the school playground...

Through investigation, the police uncovered a neurological research institution and learned about the incredible 'third consciousness' theory, and all mysteries were about to be revealed...

Shen Youlan was right; when they entered the haunted house in the afternoon, it was still bright outside, but now night had completely fallen.
