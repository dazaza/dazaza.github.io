by togpyb27 on 2011-10-28 16:37:23

"Hidden", "Who's in Charge of My Youth" were successively put into practice by white-collar workers this year. At the beginning of the year, white-collar workers started to draw "career nourishment" from film and television works. First, "Hidden" became popular, and "Yu Zecheng" sparked heated discussions on office philosophy. Then, "Who's in Charge of My Youth" became a hit, with the workplace stories of seven young men and women deeply touching a group of young white-collar workers. In late July, Xu Jinglei's film version of "Du LaLa's Promotion Diary" will begin filming, and the film will be made into China's first fashionable workplace blockbuster themed around the workplace experiences of urban white-collar workers.

Different film and television works bring different career guides. In "Hidden," Yu Zecheng skillfully handled his complex relationships with station chief Wu Jingzhong, "colleagues" Ma Kui, Lu Qiaoshan, and Li Ya. The survival philosophy it conveys can be seen in various "analysis" articles, and even "be like Yu Zecheng" has become a new workplace buzzword.

In "Who's in Charge of My Youth," Zhao Qingchu, Qian Xiaoyang, Li Pilei, Lei Lei, Zhou Jin, Fang Yu, Gao Qi represent seven different career paths. Behind the characters, white-collar workers can find their own shadows, and some even use it as a "practical" career textbook.

From elephants to ants

Main character: Qian Xiaoyang, a nursing school graduate with a junior college degree

Qian Xiaoyang originally worked as a nurse in Ningxia, thinking she was an ambitious youth. Later, she ran away from home to Beijing. But how could such a big city as Beijing easily accommodate a penniless, diploma-less, unskilled girl with no prominent family background? At the job market, Qian Xiaoyang received a shock.

Later, with the help of friends, she became a salesperson. She used her own methods to sell two houses, creating revenue for the company, but without considering that she was seriously violating the company's rules during her probation period (entertaining owners without permission and signing purchase contracts under false names), and refused to accept criticism. Therefore, the company decided to terminate her employment contract. After a series of life incidents, Qian Xiaoyang matured day by day. Finally, she became the general manager's secretary, the hardest-working employee, and mastered computer shorthand.

Commentary: Qian Xiaoyang initially overestimated herself and aimed too high. Later, when she found work as a salesperson, she persisted in her individuality and broke the rules, leading to her dismissal. These all indicate that the workplace is never a place where you can do as you please. Personality must yield to society. After paying a price, Qian Xiaoyang realized that she couldn't be an elephant; she was just an ant, and she had to work very hard as an ant.

Workplace "White Bone Essence"

Main character: Zhao Qingchu, lawyer at a famous law firm

After graduating from Peking University's Law program, Zhao Qingchu successfully joined a renowned law firm in Beijing. On her path as a lawyer, she pursued social status, psychological satisfaction, fame, and profit all at once. She seized the opportunities she should seize, let go of the ones she shouldn't, always stayed ahead, but refused to rush forward.

Regarding her relationship with Zhou Jin, she believed that no matter how crazy one is about love, there are necessary principles and attitudes that need to be defended. Regarding temptation, she immediately suppressed any greed in its infancy and insisted on starting from scratch to achieve a well-off life.

Commentary: Zhao Qingchu is a typical example of a workplace "White Bone Essence" (white-collar worker + backbone + elite). Her professional growth has been smooth sailing, full of vitality, and she rationally controls her life. In fact, in Zhao Qingchu's field of law, the polarization is quite significant. Successful lawyers can earn millions annually, while less successful ones may not earn a single penny. For newcomers to the workplace, having an appropriate platform is crucial.
