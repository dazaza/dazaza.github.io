■ "Wudi" Generates Plenty of Discussion

Director Reveals How Song Zuying's Sister Joined the Crew

Due to the popularity of the first two seasons, "Ugly Girl Season 3" has also captured plenty of attention. To provide viewers with a completely new visual experience, apart from incorporating a large number of musical elements throughout the season, a new batch of actors has been added, including Song Zuying's younger sister, Song Jialing. Director Zhang Feng revealed some behind-the-scenes anecdotes about the "Little Dragon Girl".

Was She Selected Through Connections? "Because of Her Contrasting Appearance and Role"

The biggest difference between "Ugly Girl Season 3" and the previous two seasons is the addition of a filming location called "Surprisingly Enough Bar." Song Jialing plays "Little Dragon Girl," the bouncer of the bar, skilled in martial arts, serving as the protector of this trouble-prone bar. "Little Dragon Girl" is not tall, usually wearing work clothes, but her numerous braids add a touch of exotic flair. Why would such a skilled person willingly work as a waiter or bouncer in a bar? There must be a mysterious backstory…

Director Zhang Feng introduced that Song Jialing joined the show not only because the producer and Song Jialing's brother-in-law, who is also Song Zuying's husband Luo Hao, are long-time friends, but more importantly, she possesses many traits that do not align with the typical martial arts characteristics. Generally, warriors in films and TV series are robust and muscular. However, Song Jialing has a sweet and beautiful appearance. Moreover, all the male actors were selected based on Lin Wudi's height, standing over 1.80 meters tall, and the female actors were mostly over 1.70 meters. Standing at 1.65 meters, "Little Dragon Girl" appears particularly petite in the crew, with delicate arms, contrasting greatly with the profession of a bouncer. In the show, Song Jialing effortlessly lifts and throws out troublemakers from "Surprisingly Enough Bar." After editing, everyone recognized the effect of this contrast.

Did She Receive Any Special Treatment While Filming? "Almost Always Refused Stunt Doubles"

Director Zhang Feng stated that he did not know Song Jialing was Song Zuying's sister when the third season began shooting. It wasn't until near the end of filming that he heard about it from the choreographer in the crew. When Zhang Feng met Song Jialing, he asked her, "Are you Song Zuying's sister? You don't look very similar!" Song Jialing briefly replied, "It's true."

It is reported that although Song Jialing lacks acting experience and speaks Mandarin with a Hunan accent, her efforts gained recognition from everyone. Zhang Feng mentioned that initially, Song Jialing was not accustomed to acting, but each time she came to the set, she had her lines memorized thoroughly. Watching the drama, one can see that as a bouncer, she engages in many fight scenes with male actors like Lei Jiong and Lin Wuqing, who are much taller and heavier than her, and she performs them excellently. The director also said, "Every time I filmed Song Jialing's fight scenes, I would ask her if she was scared and if she wanted a stunt double. But she almost always refused to use one. Once, she jumped from a height of over 20 meters herself. Apart from her trust in me, it mainly reflects her dedication to her work."

How Did She Get Along with Others? "Never Mentioned That She Was Song Zuying's Sister"

Since she didn't initially know the other actors and found acting challenging, she didn't actively communicate with others in the crew and often stayed alone. It wasn't until she became familiar with the crew members that she gradually became more lively. However, she never made any requests to the crew and never mentioned that she was Song Zuying's sister.

Additionally, Song Jialing endured a lot of hardship in the crew. For example, her hairstyle in the drama consists of multiple braids, which takes the hairstylist about four hours to create each time. Sometimes, to keep up with the schedule, she was not allowed to untie her braids before sleeping or wash her hair. The filming period coincided with the peak summer heat in Changsha, and the shooting studio lacked air conditioning, making her uncomfortable. However, she raised no objections. She is a girl of few words, and everyone in the crew likes her. It is reported that during filming, Song Jialing missed the zongzi her mother made and the water from her hometown. She also invited all the crew members to her home several times to taste the water from her hometown.
