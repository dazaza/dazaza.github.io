Make good use of the "power outage day"

by hunanshida on 2012-02-08 11:44:31

The self-study examination office of Hunan Normal University found that "power-off day" is actually a good way to solve the problem of lack of communication in many families due to the fast pace of life, which leads to the estrangement of family affection. In fact, for children, the experience of "power-off day" is very precious. Because they can experience a warm exchange with their parents and feel the love between each other. Nothing is more beneficial to a child's growth than family love. Children need to communicate emotionally with their parents, and parents also need to strengthen their emotional ties with their children.

The teachers from the Hunan Normal University Self-Study Examination Network suggest that parents try setting a family rule: the whole family chooses one day without turning on the TV, one day without using computers, and instead spend time together for entertainment and communication. The important thing is that we can jump out of the world of TV and computers and enjoy a colorful life again. It can also let children feel the warmth of the family.

The teachers of Hunan Normal University's self-study examination major think that on the "power-off day", you can also teach children some safe electricity usage knowledge. For example, let children know the location of the main power switch and learn how to cut off the main power in an emergency; do not use hands or conductive objects (such as wires, nails, etc.) to touch the power source and socket; do not touch appliances with wet hands or clean them with a wet cloth; if someone gets electrocuted, try to turn off the power in time or use a wooden stick to separate the electrocuted person from the electrified appliance, never save people by hand to prevent yourself from getting electrocuted; do not disassemble or install power lines, sockets, plugs, etc. at will; when installing light bulbs, make sure to turn off the power and do it under the guidance of adults.
