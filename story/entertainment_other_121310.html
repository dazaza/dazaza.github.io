New Focus - Mining Netizens' Concerns, Tracking Hot Global Events

by rickywen on 2009-01-25 01:29:56

The Internet era allows people to stay at home and know what's happening around the world. However, the overwhelming abundance of information has made it inconvenient for many people to keep up with global events. The limited free time after 8 hours of work leaves people at a loss, getting lost in the vast ocean of information. New Focus tracks the popular global events by mining the attention points of netizens. The daily provided information is not much, but it is definitely the focus of public attention; although it is not news, it is certainly the recent hotspot; although there is no lengthy report on the focal event, it will surely allow you to understand the ins and outs of the event from concise content and provide more entry points for detailed understanding. Through "New Focus", you can know the attention points of netizens around the world with very low time cost; since netizens are actually a reflection of the real-life crowd, you will have rich topics to talk about during your leisure time and broaden your horizons.

The team behind New Focus is currently studying how to mine the attention points of netizens and let the masses become the filter of global information; aggregated from the masses, feedback to all! This model has been officially launched as a Demo on January 1, 2009. We hope everyone will like New Focus and continue to pay attention to its growth!
