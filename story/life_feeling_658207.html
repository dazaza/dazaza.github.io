Quitting is not forgetting.

by meimei234 on 2012-02-02 22:42:58

Abandonment is not forgetting.

Are you okay? You, at this very moment?

Even though you have disappeared from my world, I still sit quietly in such deep nights. It's not waiting, because there's no longer you in the wait. I still wander in such long nights. It's not lingering, because there's no longer you in the lingering. I am just used to facing myself in such deep nights, used to reminiscing about the past in such deep nights. I've heard that a person's life is built up by memories, and beautiful memories can build a beautiful life.

I really thought that as long as I don't try to remember, as long as I don't touch it, as long as I move forward with my head down, I could truly forget, truly delete you completely from my memory. In the days without you, I learned to let myself escape, wanting to escape to a corner where even thoughts cannot find me, then hide myself. I worked so hard, trying to comfort myself, everything will pass. It really will pass, time will heal my wounds, and also make me forget all the happiness and joy you gave me. After losing you, I realized that beauty isn't just about possession; sometimes losing is also a kind of beauty. Like now, only beauty remains in my memory. If one can embrace such beauty and grow old slowly, it might be the happiest thing, even if there are a few sighs in happiness. At least we didn't let go until we were both hurt beyond repair, at least after parting we still hold gratitude in our hearts. So, sometimes losing may also be a kind of beauty, just a little melancholy and regret.

You said for me to take care of myself. On the road ahead, you won't accompany me anymore, you'll let me walk alone. I am really trying, trying to learn how to move forward slowly, trying to live life with my eyes closed. Days without your appearance seem like nothing has changed. Time still flows second by second, days still pass day by day, I am still listless, but unexpectedly strong. I try hard not to let my thoughts turn back to you. The past is thin like silkworm wings for me, just one look back and memories pierce through, then cracks appear in my heart, then tears overflow. I don't dare to turn around easily, for fear that once I do, I will break the agreement, and will use every method to find you again. For fear that once I do, all the pain will flood, and become uncontrollable.

But sometimes, I still feel lost and don't know what to do, still wake up in such nights, because in such nights, I still sigh, I still stay in the dream you wove, I still float in the memories you gave me, I still let my thoughts follow the original path, habitually finding you. Perhaps, I haven't really been making an effort to escape. But what night is it tonight? Exhausting my entire life, I can no longer find you. Only because I have promised, agreed to give up like that, no attachment, no worry, no longing. Agreed to let go of each other calmly like that, no crying, no sadness, no searching.

Life, actually, is very long, so long that no matter how hard I try to move forward, I still can't reach the end as quickly as possible. Life is really very short, short to the point it feels like just a moment. I really don't know, is it better to experience or not to experience? Is it better to forget or to hold on tightly? Getting close to you warmed me. Moving away from you froze me. But not all encounters have beautiful endings, not all efforts are rewarded. I agreed so readily to you, to part well, letting memories freeze at the most beautiful first encounter.

After losing, I realize the heart really hurts. But, I don't dare to cry. If I cry, it proves I haven't taken good care of myself. If I cry, I'm afraid weakness will become a habit. Because, on the road ahead, it's really just me moving forward alone, just me starting alone. You are no longer my destination, because you were never my starting point, but just an emotional stopover.

I can only set off again, the road ahead is long and unpredictable. But I will always be grateful, saying goodbye at the most beautiful time, allowing my life to embrace such beautiful memories and age slowly.

Abandonment is only unavoidable, and abandonment is not forgetting.
