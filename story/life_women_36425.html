Women in cheongsam - charm that could topple a city, graceful and charming. Is it suitable for you?

by ynskycomcn on 2007-11-24 15:25:06

Women in cheongsam (旗袍) naturally reveal an elegant and gentle temperament. The small standing collar makes the woman's white neck more slender, and the soft fabric lightly wraps the woman's body. The smooth and brisk lines make the woman's figure graceful and charming. This kind of tangible delicacy and feminine sweetness is enough to make a woman alluring. Moreover, the incomparable grace and calmness are flavors that Western women cannot achieve. Therefore, cheongsam is most suitable for oriental women.
