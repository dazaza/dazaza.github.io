How to Chase a Girl

1. Send her a text message every day. It doesn't have to say "I love you," but it must convey this meaning. Ideally, send it when you estimate she has turned off her phone and gone to sleep. This way, as soon as she turns on her phone in the morning, she will read your heartfelt intentions, and her heart will feel like honey. Never think that sweet words are unnecessary; women are the animals that lack security the most. Direct words are the information that can make her feel secure the most.

2. Occasionally buy her some small gifts, and you can attach your confession of feelings. For example, you can buy her a plush dog and tell her, "I am just like a puppy, always faithfully guarding you!" Or give her a beautiful doll and whisper in her ear, "I hope our future baby is this cute." Her heart will definitely soften bit by bit. When her bed and desk are filled with all kinds of small gifts you've given her, she won't be able to stop thinking about you.

3. A rose represents your heart. Women like roses not because roses are particularly beautiful, but because roses represent love. Once you understand this point, don't get stuck on silly questions like "Does she like roses?" When she holds a big bunch of roses and hears her friends and colleagues envy her saying, "Wow, your partner really loves you!" The pride in her heart is indescribable.

4. Go shopping with her. When she eagerly hopes that you'll go shopping with her, don't put on a sad face and run away at the last moment. She actually understands that all men dislike going shopping. Precisely because of this, she will appreciate it if you go shopping with her.

5. Let her hang up the phone first. After each call, gently tell her, "You hang up first." After being affectionate and still wanting more, the person who hangs up last will feel somewhat disappointed hearing the busy tone. So, let her hang up first, allowing her to fall asleep reminiscing about the warmth you brought her.

6. Travel. You can take her to a completely unfamiliar place. In a bustling crowd where there isn't a single familiar face, only your hand is her sole reliance. You can also take her to an uninhabited suburb. With vast skies and earth, only you and her exist; two hearts will become more sincere and closer in nature.

7. Watch movies. The dim atmosphere of a cinema and its superior visual effects are incomparable to TVs or computers. You can choose a comedy with a happy reunion theme. When the protagonists are extremely sweet, she will unconsciously grip your hand tightly. You can also try horror films. However, when she's scared and hides her face from the screen and buries herself in your arms, you absolutely cannot bury yourself in her arms first. If you're also scared, the best method is to hug her and close your eyes.
