US online accounting website expensr obtains US$5 million financing

DoNews, April 28th news (reported by Wang Le) According to insider information, the US personal accounting website expensr will announce this Tuesday that it has obtained financing. Currently, various details are not yet known.

According to this person, the scale of expensr's financing is around US$5 million, with a share transfer of no more than 20%. It is currently unclear who the investors are. Previously, there were rumors in the industry that Facebook might acquire expensr.

According to insiders, expensr was once rated as one of the "Top Ten Websites That Can Change Human Future Lives" by Forbes, with approximately 200,000 users. Currently, there are several domestic websites similar to expensr, such as Wallet Network and Caike Online (www.coko365.com). Among them, Wallet Network claims to have 450,000 registered users.
