Director Reveals How Song Zuoying's Sister Got Into the Production

Given the popularity of the first two seasons, the third season of "Ugly Betty" has garnered plenty of attention. To bring viewers a completely new visual experience, in addition to adding a large amount of musical elements throughout the season, a batch of new actors has been added, including Song Zuoying's real-life sister, Song Jialing. This season's director, Zhang Feng, reveals some behind-the-scenes anecdotes about "Little Dragon Girl."

Was she chosen because of connections? "Because of the contrast between her appearance and the role"

The biggest difference between the third season of "Ugly Betty" and the previous two seasons is the addition of a filming location called "Enough Thunder." Song Jialing plays "Little Dragon Girl" in the show, who is the bouncer of the bar, skilled in martial arts, and the protector of this trouble-prone bar. "Little Dragon Girl" is not tall, usually wearing work clothes, but her braided hair gives her a bit of an exotic style. Why would such a master be willing to work as a server and bouncer in a bar? Presumably, there is a mystery about her background...

Director Zhang Feng introduced that Song Jialing was able to join the production partly because the producer and Song Jialing's brother-in-law, who is also Song Zuoying's husband Luo Hao, are long-time friends, but more importantly, she has many traits that do not fit the martial arts atmosphere. Generally, heroes in films and TV dramas are robust and muscular. However, Song Jialing has a sweet and beautiful appearance. Moreover, the cast originally selected actors based on Lin Wudi's height, so male actors were all over 180 cm tall, and female actors were all over 170 cm tall. Standing at 165 cm, "Little Dragon Girl" appears particularly petite in the cast, with thin arms, creating a stark contrast with the profession of a bouncer. Yet, in the show, Song Jialing effortlessly lifts and throws people causing trouble out of "Enough Thunder." After the film was edited, everyone recognized this contrasting effect.

Did she receive any special treatment during filming? "Almost always refused to use a stunt double"

Director Zhang Feng stated that he did not know Song Jialing was Song Zuoying's sister when the third season started shooting. It wasn't until near the end of the shoot that he heard the choreographer mention it. When Zhang Feng met Song Jialing, he asked her, "Are you Song Zuoying's sister? You don't look much alike!" Song Jialing simply replied, "It's true."

It is reported that despite lacking much acting experience and having a Hunan accent in her Mandarin, her efforts were recognized by everyone. Zhang Feng mentioned that Song Jialing was not accustomed to the filming process initially, but each time she came to the set, her lines were memorized thoroughly. Watching the TV series, viewers will find that as a bouncer, she has many fight scenes with male actors like Lei Jiong and Lin Wuqing, and she performed them excellently. The director also said, "Every time I filmed Song Jialing's fight scenes, I would ask her if she was afraid and if she wanted a stunt double, but she almost always refused to use one. Once, she jumped from a height of over 20 meters herself. Besides trusting me, it was more about her dedication."

How did she get along with others? "Never mentioned being Song Zuoying's sister"

Since she didn't know the other actors at first and found acting a bit challenging, she didn't actively communicate with others in the crew and usually stayed by herself. Only after becoming familiar with the crew members did she gradually become more lively, but she never made any requests to the crew or mentioned that she was Song Zuoying's sister.

Additionally, Song Jialing endured a lot of hardship in the crew. For example, her hairstyle in the show consists of small braids, and it generally takes the hairstylist about four hours to style her hair. Sometimes, to keep up with the schedule, she wasn't allowed to untie her braids at night or wash her hair. The filming period coincided with the scorching summer in Changsha, and the shooting studio had no air conditioning, making her uncomfortable, but she had no objections. She is a girl of few words, and everyone in the crew likes her. It is reported that during filming, Song Jialing greatly missed her mother's zongzi and the water from her hometown, and she invited the entire crew multiple times to taste the water from her hometown.
