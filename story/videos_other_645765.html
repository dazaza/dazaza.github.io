Yiling Rock National Ethnic Culture Yiling Rock, known as the treasure house of natural art, is a famous scenic spot in Nanning, the capital city of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Located only 21 kilometers away from the urban area of Nanning, Yiling Rock is a typical karst cave and has long been praised as the "Palace Underground". It is also called Gan Palace (in Zhuang language, meaning a beautiful cave like a palace). According to geological scientists' estimation, Yiling Rock was formed one million years ago.

In addition, the Yiling Rock scenic area also includes the Nine Dragons Fountain, Children's Paradise, Leisure Corridor, Circular Mountain Path, Poets' Inscription, elegant Liangshan Hotel, Dafuhao Restaurant and other facilities. On the forested circular mountain path in the scenic area, monkeys gather in groups, providing an abundance of wild interest that will make you feel the natural and harmonious emotional exchange between humans and animals. Climbing up for a distant view, the magnificent scenery of the fields and nature can be fully appreciated. The complete one-stop service for traveling, touring, accommodation, food, entertainment, and shopping makes Yiling Rock an excellent place for tourism, vacation, summer retreat, and conferences.

The Yiling Village displays the simple national customs, ancient architecture, and artistically reproduces the profound Zhuang ethnic culture from multiple angles and perspectives. The attractions in Yiling Village include: Suona welcome, Bamboo Dance, Mountain Song duet, tasting ethnic snacks, Zhuang family workshop, Zhuang family wine shop, watching the Zhuang pole dance, etc.

According to geological scientists, the Yiling Rock cave was formed over a million years ago. The cave is 45 meters deep and contains eight major scenic areas and over a hundred attractions. Inside the cave, stalactites, stone pillars, stone curtains, and stone flowers form vivid images, lifelike and breathtakingly beautiful. Touring inside the cave feels like being in a fairyland, with too much beauty to take in all at once. The Zhuang ethnic风情in the Yiling Rock scenic area is very fascinating, influenced by local customs and Zhuang culture, forming unique historical culture and humanistic landscapes during its development.

Yiling Rock is located in the belly of Liangshan Mountain. The entire cave resembles a sea snail, covering an area of 2400 square meters, with a tour distance of 1100 meters. The cave is divided into three layers, with winding and twisting paths. Everywhere there are dense clusters of stalactites, stacked stone pillars and curtains, and labyrinthine gorges and holes, creating a confusing and mysterious atmosphere. Some places resemble a dome hall that can accommodate thousands of people, while others are narrow alleys where only one person can pass through. The cave already has eight major scenic areas and over a hundred large and small attractions, including "Two Lions Welcoming Guests", "Sky Bridge", "Feast in Yao Pool", "By the Red Water River", "Seaside Park", "New Look of Zhuang Villages", "Beautiful Country", and "Scenic North" etc. People entering the cave sometimes climb upwards as if roaming among clouds, sometimes descend down slopes as if stepping into deep valleys, and sometimes turn corners, feeling like they are in a magical realm. Every year it attracts hundreds of thousands of domestic and foreign tourists for sightseeing tours.
