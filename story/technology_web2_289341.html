Decoration If one day

by hartyqekb on 2009-12-02 17:23:07

When you love, let him be free; when you don't love, let love be free.

Care about what you once had, and also care about lasting forever.

Rather be proud and single than force yourself into unhappiness.

Don't wait for the wrong person, and don't hurt the heart that shouldn't be hurt.

Sometimes, love can also be a kind of harm.

Cruel people choose to hurt others;

Kind people choose to hurt themselves.

Secret love is the best silent drama, and speaking out may turn it into a tragedy.

Crying doesn't mean I surrender;

Stepping back doesn't mean I admit defeat;

Letting go doesn't mean I give up;

Just as my smile doesn't mean I'm happy.

Only abandonment, no forgetting.

The more indifferent you are, the less you get hurt, and time will make things fade away.

Time makes deep things deeper and shallow things shallower.

Buddha says that fate is like a piece of ice that takes five hundred years to cross.

Buddha asks if it's bitter? I say it's not. So Buddha grants me a bit of fate. If I gain it, I am lucky; if I don't, it's my destiny. That's all.

Five hundred glances in the past life exchange for one passing encounter in this life.

I'd rather exchange a passing encounter in the next life for five hundred glances in this life.

When I laugh, the whole world laughs with me; when I cry, I cry alone.

Loneliness doesn't necessarily mean unhappiness;

Getting something doesn't necessarily mean it will last long.

Losing something doesn't necessarily mean you won't have it again.

You might feel sad because of some reason,

But you can find a reason to make yourself happy.

When you fall in love with someone, you always feel a little scared, afraid of getting them, afraid of losing them.

People who don't have to wait are happy.

We really need a very long time to understand,

Who we truly miss, and what kind of thing.

Everything can become a habit, like parting and longing.

Love and being loved aren't necessarily proportional.

Meeting the right person at the right time is a kind of happiness;

Meeting the wrong person at the right time is a kind of sadness;

Meeting the right person at the wrong time is a sigh;

Meeting the wrong person at the wrong time is a kind of helplessness.

Don't easily give up, or you'll regret it.

If one day I put on a wedding dress for someone else,

It might be because of age, or tiredness,

Or because I really want to walk hand in hand with that person...

Let him be free when you love.
