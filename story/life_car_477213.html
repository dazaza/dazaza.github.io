Top Ten Secrets of Car Maintenance?

How to keep your car looking brand new? This topic is already well-known in the automotive world, and there are many answers. Summarizing experiences, China Auto Beauty Recruitment Network www.qcmrrc.com recommends the top ten secrets of car maintenance:

1. **Engine Exterior**

Clean the exterior of the engine. Pay attention to waterproofing for electrical components during cleaning. If the electrical parts have high waterproofing requirements, avoid using high-pressure, high-temperature water guns to rinse the engine. Instead, use a brush dipped in cleaning agent to clean the exterior of the engine.

2. **Distributor**

Use a clean cloth to wipe away dirt inside the distributor cap, remove dirt from the distributor contact points, eliminate burn marks on the contact points, check the high-speed contact point clearance or magnetic pole clearance of the electronic ignition system, and lubricate all lubrication points of the distributor.

3. **Three Filters and Engine Oil**

Use compressed air to blow off dust from the air filter; replace the fuel filter at appropriate times and clean the filter nets at pipe joints; replace engine oil and the oil filter. For domestically produced vehicles, also clean the coarse oil filter, fuel pre-filter, and centrifugal fine filter.

4. **Battery**

Check if there is any corrosion on the battery terminals, wash the exterior of the battery with hot water, and remove corrosion from the battery terminals. Measure and adjust the specific gravity of the battery's electrolyte.

5. **Coolant**

Check and replenish the coolant, clean the exterior of the radiator.

6. **Tires and Wheel Hubs**

Check the wear condition of the tires and rotate them as needed. Check the pre-tightening condition of the wheel hubs and bearings, adjust the pre-tightness if there is any gap.

7. **Braking System**

Check and adjust the working stroke of the handbrake lever; check and adjust the shoe gap of the drum handbrake; check and adjust the free stroke of the foot brake pedal; check the wear condition of the wheel brake shoes, replace the brake shoes if they reach the wear mark; check and adjust the shoe gap of the wheel brake; check and replenish the brake fluid, etc.

8. **Fastening**

Check the tightening condition of important bolts or screws on the chassis, especially the important bolts and screws of the steering system. If looseness or damage is found, replace and tighten them.

9. **Chassis**

Check the condition of all parts of the chassis piping, look for leaks, tighten all metal connecting rods, and check if the rubber bushings are damaged. Perform grease lubrication on all lubrication points of the chassis.

10. **Lights**

Check and repair car lights, check and maintain cooling and heating devices, clean the sound system, etc.
