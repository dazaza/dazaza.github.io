Samsung S8000C: Full Touchscreen "Jiet" Masterpiece Arrives in China at Lightning Speed

by budfu39q on 2009-12-04 11:09:54

After the global launches in London, Dubai, and Singapore, the Samsung groundbreaking full-touchscreen phone "Jet" - the S8000C (Forum) - has arrived in China.

The Samsung S8000C is called "Jet" overseas and retains the same name in Chinese. As a strategic full-touchscreen smartphone launched globally by Samsung, the S8000C offers users swift and smooth touch experiences, stunning visual enjoyment, and powerful extended applications. Below are images of the Samsung S8000C.

Just like a jet rapidly crossing the sky, an all-new era of mobile touchscreen technology has been born!

### No "Jet" Without Speed

The full-touchscreen era is one of high speed. The Samsung S8000C features an 800MHz high-speed processor, a configuration that not only sets a precedent in the industry but also lays a solid foundation for the smooth operation of various programs and fluid touch experiences.

To ensure users have greater freedom during touch-based applications, the Samsung S8000C incorporates the newly upgraded TouchWiz 2.0 user interface, along with an innovative 3D touch interface. Users simply need to press a button to enter this mode. A true 3D cube will appear on the screen, allowing users to freely rotate the cube and access pre-set application shortcuts. This revolutionary redesign of the interface marks the beginning of the 3D full-touchscreen era.

The Samsung S8000C also supports multitasking, enabling users to switch between other running applications at will. The new interface has significantly improved web browsing speed and touch command response compared to previous versions.

Additionally, the S8000C features a unique one-finger zoom function, allowing single-handed control for enlarging or shrinking images and documents, making mobile internet use quick and simple.

### No "Jet" Without Glamour

The full-touchscreen era is one of multimedia. To provide users with a perfect visual experience, the Samsung S8000C is equipped with a 3.1-inch WVGA resolution AMOLED touchscreen display featuring 16 million colors. With a resolution of 480x800, its image clarity is four times that of a regular WQVGA screen! Due to the use of AMOLED innovative material, the S8000C screen excels in color reproduction, brightness, and contrast, with a 180-degree viewing angle allowing users to watch from any angle.

In terms of audio-visual performance, the Samsung S8000C delivers equally impressive results. It features a 5-megapixel digital camera with dual LED flash, supporting professional functions such as smile mode, panoramic shooting, and photo geotagging, just like a digital camera. The S8000C also supports D1-level video playback and D1 @30fps video recording. For Divx and Xvid format videos, they can be played directly upon download without conversion. In music, the S8000C applies excellent DNSe & SRS sound effect technology. All these high-performance features give users an extraordinary sensory experience.

In terms of design, the S8000C impresses with its ultra-glamorous overall shape and meticulously crafted details. The entire body is ultra-thin and rounded, with a thickness of just 11.9mm, complemented by ergonomic curve handling, making the S8000C look cool and feel great in hand. The shell is made of matte material, which pairs beautifully with the colorful AMOLED screen, appearing delicate and refined. The back of the device features red-and-black interwoven patterns that subtly hint at elegance and luxury while resisting fingerprints. Additionally, a unique crystal stereo button is located centrally below the body, echoing the 3D touch interface inside and out, enabling quick access to function menus for easy and flexible use.

### No "Jet" Without Capability

The full-touchscreen era is one of versatility. Besides daily communication functions, phones have become indispensable tools for business work and leisure entertainment. The Samsung S8000C also features Samsung's self-developed motion recognition engine "motion gate," allowing users to complete common operations such as accessing multimedia files and quick dialing through simple actions (shaking, double-tapping, etc.).

To enhance the practicality of the full-touchscreen, the Samsung S8000C also includes an "intelligent unlock" function. This feature allows users to set corresponding characters for unlocking the phone and menu operations, enabling users to use the phone according to their preferences, greatly enhancing personalization. Moreover, the S8000C also installs an A-GPS application, providing real-time navigation services and convenient point-of-interest query functions. This ensures users can operate effortlessly even in "driving" mode.

High-speed processing capabilities, high-quality audio-visual presentation, and highly efficient comprehensive application functions – the Samsung S8000C not only brings users an "unparalleled touch" but also redefines full-touchscreen smartphones.
