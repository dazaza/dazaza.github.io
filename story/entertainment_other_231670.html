　　The green small phone is an exclusive prestigious icon for WAPQQ users. Users who log in via WAPQQ can light up the green small phone for free. Once a user logs into WAPQQ service, they become a prestigious WAPQQ user, and the small icon will remain lit for the next three months. This icon is displayed in the new version of the QQ client, QQ2008 Beta2, and subsequent versions; it is not applicable to versions prior to QQ2008 Beta2.

1. What is WAP?

Answer: WAP is mobile internet access. "Wow Pu" is its Chinese pronunciation. To use WAP services, first confirm that your phone supports WAP services! Then, by visiting Mobile Dream Network or Interactive World, you can browse content as rich and colorful as the Internet.

2. What specific services does WAP offer?

Answer: WAP services are numerous, covering chatting, entertainment, and lifestyle. For example, WAPQQ online chatting, mobile ringtones download, mobile movies, etc.

3. How are WAP services charged?

Answer: WAP service fees will be deducted from your monthly bill. Fees are divided into: information fees and traffic fees. Information fees are collected by the service provider, and traffic fees are charged according to local operator policies.

4. What is WAPQQ?

Answer: WAPQQ is Tencent's official chat product based on WAP. WAPQQ can not only communicate with mobile friends but also chat freely with QQ client users anytime.

5. Where can I find and use WAPQQ?

Answer: Mobile users: Log in to Mobile Dream Network -> Socializing -> QQ Chat

Unicom C-network users: Enter Interactive World -> Instant Messaging -> QQ Chat

Unicom G-network users: Enter Interactive World -> Community -> Chat & Dating -> QQ Chat

(Suggest adding QQ Chat as a bookmark for easy access next time)

6. Does using WAPQQ require downloading?

Answer: No. As long as your mobile phone can access the internet, no download is necessary, and there are no restrictions based on phone brand.

7. How do I use WAPQQ without a QQ number?

Answer: If you're a new user, please click on "New User Guide (Apply for QQ Number)" on the QQ login page, and our system will assign you a QQ number.

8. What privileges does WAPQQ have?

Answer: WAPQQ offers basic functions such as online QQ chatting, applying for QQ numbers, finding new friends, and mobile buddy lists. It also provides special privileges like leveling up, QQ Show, forums, families, and personal space. Additionally, WAPQQ users enjoy an exclusive green VIP mobile icon in the new QQ client.

9. After entering my QQ number and password and confirming the charge, the page prompts me with "Password Incorrect." What should I do?

Answer: If prompted with "password incorrect," simply re-enter and confirm your QQ number and password, then choose to log in again. This step will not incur any additional charges.

All billing information for WAP services will pop up a reminder page, and charges will only occur after the user clicks confirm.

10. Will the activated icon turn off? Can it be canceled?

Answer: If the user has not logged into WAPQQ within the last three months, the icon will automatically turn off.

Once a user logs into WAPQQ, they become a prestigious WAPQQ user, and the small icon will remain lit for the next three months.

This icon cannot be canceled.

For example: User A activates the icon on June 1, 2008. If User A does not log into WAPQQ within the next three months, the icon will automatically turn off on September 1, 2008. If the user logs in again on July 12, 2008, the validity period of the illuminated icon will be extended until October 12, 2008.

If you don't want to wait three months or if the icon hasn't turned off after three months, you can call customer service to manually turn off the WAPQQ green small phone icon.

Phone number: 8008309138 (free for members) or 0755-83765566. After connecting, select the Super QQ category, then choose manual assistance, and inform them that it has been three months and why the WAPQQ icon hasn't turned off, and they will manually extinguish it for you.
