Cure the "bad habits" and have a relaxed summer vacation!

by blitseo on 2010-08-10 11:06:54

In the blazing sun of July, reporters learned from pediatricians of various hospitals that every summer vacation, the number of children coming to the hospital for treatment of ADHD and Tourette's syndrome suddenly increases. Experts from the Hunan Consultation Center for ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome told reporters that they now receive more than a dozen children on average every day, mostly aged between 6 and 18 years old. The sharp increase in the number of children with ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome cannot but raise the vigilance of parents.

ADHD in children is extremely harmful, yet parents know very little about it.

The reporter asked many parents who came for consultation, but most of them were not very familiar with ADHD, assuming that their children were just mischievous. When asked if they knew about the harm of ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome, many parents showed indifference. Most parents thought their child’s condition was temporary and would naturally improve as they grew up, without causing any serious harm. However, experts told reporters that ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome are caused by an imbalance of monoamine neurotransmitters in the brain due to various reasons before or after birth. According to authoritative surveys, among the 400 million children nationwide, over 20 million children with ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome need help! These children may be excluded by other peers due to ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome, leading to issues such as poor self-esteem, lack of confidence, poor academic performance, difficulty in advancing education. In adulthood, they may have poor work ability, severe emotional instability, depression, schizophrenia, behavioral disorders, antisocial personality, substance dependence or abuse (such as alcoholism, drug addiction, or drug use), and even embark on a path of criminal behavior!

With the increasing number of patients, take advantage of the summer vacation to seek prompt treatment!

When the reporter asked about the reasons for the increase in the number of treatments, the expert analyzed that the fundamental cause of ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome is the imbalance of neurotransmitters in the brain. The causes of this situation are complex, such as minor brain injuries, environmental pollution, improper food intake, long-term mental stimulation, all of which can easily lead to the occurrence of ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome! It is heartening that now, with the in-depth publicity of the hazards of ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome in society, many parents have realized the impact of ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome on their children's learning, life, and growth. Therefore, taking advantage of the summer vacation when children have lighter study tasks and less mental pressure, they seek systematic and effective treatment promptly! Experts also appeal through the reporter that parents must pay sufficient attention to ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome in children, and try to create a warm and relaxed environment so that children can enjoy a pleasant childhood. Do not blindly expect your children to become dragons.

How to treat leads to a brand-new medication!

When the reporter asked about the treatment methods for ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome, the expert said that the side effects of traditional treatments for ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome limit their widespread use, which troubles many parents who don't know how to safely and effectively treat their children's conditions. However, now the Sun Yat-sen University Medical School Drug Development Center has developed the world's first transdermal controlled-release formulation for treating ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome - "Jian'er Patch." It is currently the only type of new transdermal controlled-release formulation available on the market that does not require injections or oral medication, being safe and free of side effects. Many parents using it report good results without any side effects. This novel formulation is expected to become the preferred treatment option for many ADHD and Tourette's Syndrome patients!
