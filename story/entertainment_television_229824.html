"Family Outing" is a segment of the large-scale weekly Sunday variety show "Happy Sunday" on SBS TV in South Korea, hosted by Yoo Jae-suk with several regular guests and special guests.

Each episode, the artists form a family and choose a rural household in the countryside where they spend two days and one night. During this time, the family must complete various farming tasks left by the owners, participate in team-based competitive games, cooperate in preparing meals, and engage in family competitions for better sleeping positions, offering you an originally comedic program featuring the artists' natural humor.
