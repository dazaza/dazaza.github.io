Hubei Kailong Shengshi Electric Vehicle Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is one of the earliest modern high-tech enterprises in Central China region that engages in electric vehicle R&D, manufacturing, sales and after-sales service. The company has advanced production equipment, strong technical force, fashionable development capability, perfect testing methods, and a sound after-sales service system.

Kailong electric vehicles adopt American electric vehicle manufacturing technology, designed and manufactured to automobile standards under the guidance of experts in automobiles, steel structures, mechanics, and other disciplines. It is currently the most advanced and complete off-road electric vehicle in China. The imported motor and electronic control systems are used, while the rest of the key components are specially developed by domestic specialized manufacturers. Therefore, the product not only has a series of advantages such as elegant and simple appearance, low noise, strong endurance, simple and economical maintenance, but also has the quality of similar imported vehicles, yet at a price much lower than imported vehicles. It is especially suitable for golf courses, tourist attractions, real estate (garden communities), large enterprises, government agencies, parks, entertainment venues, sports arenas, colleges and universities, hospitals, nursing homes, stations, airports, docks, and other fields.

In terms of talent absorption, the company continuously and systematically absorbs outstanding graduates from various universities and has established a complete employee welfare system and common development platform, making the management team increasingly powerful. In personnel training, multiple training forms including internal and external training are adopted. This enables Kailong people to continuously improve and enhance their service levels, establishing a solid customer-first and service-first concept. The main purpose of internal training is to repeatedly reinforce and consolidate the knowledge learned in external training while strengthening education on corporate culture.

Kailong people hope for "bluer skies, greener lands, and clearer waters." With the spirit of hard entrepreneurship, Kailong people will embrace new challenges with full enthusiasm.
