**Exhibition Introduction:**

Founded in 1963, SEMA (Specialty Equipment Market Association) is a professional automotive association specializing in automotive equipment and parts. It has 6,466 member companies and controls $31 billion worth of global automotive parts sales annually. Since the SEMA SHOW moved to Las Vegas in 1977, it has grown over 30 years into one of the most influential custom vehicle exhibitions in North America and globally. Each year, it attracts more than 2,000 enterprises from over 100 countries worldwide, with over 100,000 professional buyers, wholesalers, senior corporate decision-makers, technicians, and import/export agents attending to observe and negotiate deals.

In 2011, the SEMA Show attracted 2,200 exhibitors from dozens of countries around the world, with at least 100,000 industry leaders and 50,000 professional buyers attending. The exhibition was divided into 12 sections based on the nature of the products showcased. Over 1,400 new products were displayed in the New Product Launch Area. The annual SEMA Show, supported by SEMA's extensive trade network, provides domestic companies aiming to make significant strides in the North American market with an opportunity to directly interact with North American professional customization and parts buyers. By participating in the SEMA Show, companies can obtain first-hand market information, fully understand the dynamics of the North American market, secure orders, and gradually penetrate the North American market.

**Market Introduction:**

Participating in the SEMA Show is a must for Chinese businesses entering the North American auto parts market. SEMA, also known as the Specialty Equipment Market Association, is the most powerful trade network for automotive and motorcycle accessories in North America. Over the course of more than 40 years, SEMA has accumulated a wealth of high-quality market resources. Member companies are spread across over 100 countries worldwide, numbering more than 6,400, with annual global sales exceeding $27 billion.
