General Secretary Hu Jintao delivered an important speech.

by iuxy0224 on 2011-06-25 14:33:18

Today, we convene the County Committee's People's Congress and Political Consultative Conference work meeting. The main objective is to deeply implement the spirit of the 4th Plenary Session of the 16th Central Committee of the Party and the provincial and municipal People's Congress and Political Consultative Conference work meetings. We aim to further enhance the governance capabilities and governance levels of all levels of party organizations, strengthen and improve the party's leadership over the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference, better play the important role of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in the economic and social development of our county, and promote the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in our county to new heights and create a new situation.

In recent years, the County Committee has always attached great importance to the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference, earnestly implementing the spirit of the 16th National Congress of the Party and the 4th Plenary Session of the 16th Central Committee, vigorously strengthening political civilization construction, widely soliciting opinions from the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference on major decisions, and emphasizing the role of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in promoting work, forming a united and cooperative, harmonious situation. Especially since the change of leadership, under the correct leadership of the County Committee, the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference have closely revolved around the core work of the County Committee, fully performed their own functions, raised the "establish, speak, seek" main theme, actively moved forward, worked hard, and played a significant role in promoting the economic and social development of our county, maintaining social stability, and other various works. The People's Congress truly fulfilled the responsibilities granted by the constitution and law. Whether in legal supervision, work supervision, personnel supervision, or exercising the decision-making power of major matters, handling representative work, and strengthening self-construction, new achievements and results have been achieved, presenting many new highlights, some of which have had a certain influence throughout the province and city. The Political Consultative Conference firmly grasps the two major themes of democracy and unity, fully plays the three major functions of political consultation, democratic supervision, and participation in government affairs, unites and mobilizes all aspects of strength, revolves around the center, serves the overall situation, and makes active contributions to promoting the coordinated development of material civilization, spiritual civilization, and political civilization in our county. Especially in major activities and issues such as combating SARS, agricultural tax collection, investment attraction, project construction, cultural ecological village construction, and resolving hot and core issues of public concern, the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference did not shy away from difficulties, risks, or being passive. They fully utilized their functional advantages, worked actively, made pioneering progress, achieved outstanding results, strongly supported the government's work, and ensured the smooth sailing and protection of the County Committee. Regarding this, the County Committee is satisfied, and the masses are satisfied. With the deep advancement of socialist political civilization construction, the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference also needs to keep pace with the times and continuously strengthen. This meeting of ours is an important step in strengthening and improving the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference.

Regarding holding this meeting well, the County Committee attaches great importance. At the meeting, the County Committee also issued the "Several Opinions on Strengthening and Improving the Work of the People's Congress" and the "Opinions on Further Strengthening and Improving the Work of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference." Before issuing them, opinions were repeatedly collected from all sides, and the County Committee Standing Committee conducted special studies. These documents serve as guiding documents for enhancing and improving the party's leadership over the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in the current and future periods. We need to seriously study, deeply understand, and comprehensively implement these documents. In a moment, Chairman Fuchiu, County Governor Zhichao, and Chairman Guodong will make important speeches. Below, I will represent the County Committee and share four points of opinion.

First, we must stand at the height of enhancing governance capability construction and political civilization construction, truly valuing the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference.

Strengthening and improving the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference is the fundamental path to developing socialist democratic politics and constructing socialist political civilization. It is also an important guarantee for consolidating the party's governing position, enhancing the party's governance capability construction, and promoting scientific governance, democratic governance, and lawful governance by our party. The 16th National Congress of the Party clearly proposed the construction of socialist political civilization; the 4th Plenary Session of the 16th Central Committee proposed the "scientific governance, democratic governance, lawful governance" governance strategy. Last year, at the 50th anniversary commemoration of the founding of the National People's Congress, General Secretary Hu Jintao delivered an important speech, emphasizing that all citizens, especially cadres at all levels, should seriously study the "Constitution," support the full play of the role of the People's Congress, and research and solve new situations and problems in strengthening and improving the work of the People's Congress. Since last year, the provincial and municipal committees have successively convened the People's Congress and Political Consultative Conference work meetings at all levels of the party committee, making specialized work arrangements for strengthening and improving the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference, and issuing dedicated documents. This fully demonstrates the degree of attention given by the central, provincial, and municipal authorities to the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference, providing theoretical basis for us to strengthen and improve the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference. Therefore, we must fully recognize the significance of strengthening and improving the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in the new era ideologically, laying a good ideological foundation for the smooth development of the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference. From the current reality of our county, we need to unify our thoughts in the following four aspects.

First, regarding all levels of the party committee, the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference is an important component of the party's work. Our party is the ruling party, and the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference are important ways for the party to exercise governance. All levels of the People's Congress are the highest organs of power at all levels, turning the will of the party into the will of the state through the People's Congress. The Political Consultative Conference is an important part of our country's political system, serving as an important organization for the united front and all classes to freely express opinions and conduct democratic consultations. How the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference work develops in a place and how their roles are played directly reflects the high and low governance level and the strong and weak governance capabilities of the same-level party committee. Only by attaching great importance to and fully playing the role of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference can we mobilize the enthusiasm of all classes and all aspects to participate in reform and construction, forming a harmonious and lively situation; we can open up channels for free expression, gather wisdom, and promote the scientificity and democracy of the party's governance, avoiding and reducing mistakes; we can make the party's propositions and decisions legally binding and enforceable, ensuring better implementation. Supporting the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference is strengthening the party's leadership; establishing the authority of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference is establishing the governing authority of the party; playing the role of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference is advancing scientific governance, democratic governance, and lawful governance. Therefore, all levels of the party committee must enhance awareness of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference, organically combining the party's leadership, people's ownership, rule of law, and democratic governance, placing the work of the People's Congress and the Political Consultative Conference in an important position in the overall work, including it in the overall layout, and making unified arrangements.
