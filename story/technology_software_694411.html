Maternity Evening Dresses Pregnancy does not necessarily mean that date nights or event functions stop, and it certainly doesn't mean the end of flattering wedding dresses, cute, and lovely evening gowns. For those special nights out, every expectant mother needs a go-to dress. Whether you prefer long to short or an LBD (little black dress) to a vibrant jewel tone, there is sure to be a perfect dress for you. Find an evening maternity dress that flatters your shape and suits your personal style, and you are guaranteed to look and feel great all night long.

One attractive option for a maternity evening dress selection is the maxi dress. A maxi dress is very flattering as it creates a long and lean appearance. While there are many casual maxi dresses, the evening options exemplify such features as richer fabrics, intricate necklines, and a distinctive gown quality. New for the season is the Gold Band Maxi Dress from Maternal America. This Grecian-inspired design combines rich gold and elegant black for the perfect evening look. The gold band emphasizes a beautiful v-neckline and empire waist, creating a flattering silhouette that allows the fabric to drape beautifully over your baby bump. Paired with gold strappy heels, you're guaranteed to be a showstopper.

The Maxi Maternity Dress from Olian Maternity is another lovely choice. This soft fabric stretches where you need it and creates a slimming appearance. The most beautiful aspect of this dress is its neckline, with criss-crossing straps forming a stunning pattern. Paired with heels or flats, this maxi dress is guaranteed to impress!

Another maternity evening dress style that becomes more popular during the fall and winter months is a dress accented by a jeweled neckline. Jeweled accents bring a rich, warm feel and create a beautiful frame for your face. They add unique detailing to whatever gown they adorn and elevate your look to the next level. One attractive option for an evening out with your special someone or a dinner extravaganza with your office is the Embellished Dress from Maternal America. Classic and elegant, the empire waist creates a wonderful silhouette, and the stretchy material ensures your utmost comfort. The neckline is the focal point of the dress, consisting of attractive beading in a mild scoop-neck style. The jewels, layered on top of the deep navy blue fabric, truly exemplify elegance.

Another great beaded option is the Beaded Neck Cocktail Dress from Olian Maternity. The stunning scoop-neck beading against the black creates a high-fashion feel, and the cut accentuates both the shoulders and neck beautifully.

A little black dress, more affectionately known as the LBD, is another amazing option for maternity evening wear. LBDs are timeless, pretty, and flattering. Ideal for cocktail parties, intimate dinners out, and even business events, they are versatile, and you are sure to get a lot of use from them. While all-black details vary, each LBD has its own unique features that allow you to choose the perfect dress for your personality and style. The Black Diamond Beaded Dress is a wonderful choice from Maternal America. With long sleeves perfect for the fall months, it has an intricate v-neckline that culminates with a beaded diamond design at the bust.

Another stylish LBD is the Satin Border Gown, also from Maternal America. Quickly dressed up with a great pair of heels, it features a scoop neck and flattering empire waist. The delicate pleating on the bodice and satin band at the hem give it a lovely feminine appearance. Wherever you decide to wear your dress, an LBD is the perfect go-to staple for a variety of occasions.

An evening gown is a crucial staple to have in one's maternity wardrobe, and there are a plethora of options available for your unique style. Find the design that best suits your body and taste, and you are sure to look and feel great all evening long.
