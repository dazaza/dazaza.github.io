by 86mjg2131 on 2010-04-20 14:00:11

A girl who loves to lose weight. Many people say: girls are to be loved, not to be understood. But I want to understand Mei Yun, and because of this, I fell in love with her. Mei Yun is the typist in the company, she is a very quiet girl. She is not pretty, but she is a girl with charm.

The first time I saw Mei Yun, she was sitting by the computer in the office, buying a third-generation weight loss product called "Xianzi Sleep Slim" on www.xzsss.com. At that moment, Director Li of the office, who was familiar with Mei Yun, introduced me to her: "This is the new business staff, and this is our little sister Mei Yun." I extended my hand in friendship, but Mei Yun just smiled and said, "Please take care of me in the future."

Sometimes, I have a lot of documents and materials to let Mei Yun type, which gives me more opportunities to approach her. She has an inner quality of a knowledgeable woman, reserved and not showy, pursuing quality of life without luxury. She refuses fashion but looks for weight loss products online. At that time, I thought, her figure is already quite good, why does she still need to buy weight loss products? Maybe she wants to improve herself further.

One day, when Mei Yun had nothing to do, she came to my office to chat. "I want to create a music website, can you help me?" I said, "Of course, I am also interested in music." We just started chatting when Manager Zhao called Mei Yun to a banquet. A few days later, I wrote a complete plan for building the website. Just as I was about to hand it over to Mei Yun, coincidentally, Manager Zhao wanted to entertain a client, so he invited both Mei Yun and me.

During the dinner, Mei Yun sat next to Manager Zhao. It was either Manager Zhao telling jokes or serving dishes to Mei Yun, or Mei Yun lighting cigarettes for Manager Zhao. Even the client envied Manager Zhao for having such a beautiful "young wife" with a good figure, and praised her openly. At that moment, Mei Yun's face just turned red.

The next day, Mei Yun asked me, "Didn't you promise to write a plan for building the website for me?" I said, "It's not finished yet, please wait a few more days." Mei Yun smiled playfully and said, "Then I will wait for you for ten thousand years." My heart felt sour, and I said, "Will you wait until the flowers wither?" It's really hard to understand why such a pretty girl would follow a manager who is more than ten years older than her? Thinking about it again and again, I couldn't forget Mei Yun's tall figure and her dimples when she smiled.

I handed over the website construction plan to Mei Yun. I saw her eating the third-generation Xianzi Sleep Slim weight loss product, and she said it was given to her by Manager Zhao. She said this weight loss product was really good. Looking at the beautiful Mei Yun by her side, I felt endless pity in my heart, wishing I could be a warm pool of water, allowing Mei Yun to hibernate peacefully inside. I really couldn't hold back, so I wrote a love poem for Mei Yun. That day, just as I was about to give this poem to Mei Yun, she went to Hainan for a vacation with Manager Zhao. When I saw Mei Yun again, despite the strong French perfume smell from her body almost making me choke, I still gave her the poem. After seeing the poem I wrote for her, Mei Yun was very surprised and said, "I already have a boyfriend, don't you know?"

In the evening, I sat alone by the river, feeling very sad. Am I, young and capable, really worse than Manager Zhao? What Mei Yun needs is just money. Girls like her, no matter how beautiful they are, I don't cherish them anymore. Whenever I see this fashionable beauty-loving girl in the unit, I always feel a bit annoyed. Why do modern girls all like money so much? In order not to hurt my self-esteem, I resigned from my current job this month and started running business in a food company. There is also a girl as pretty as Mei Yun there, staring blankly at the Xianzi Sleep Slim weight loss product online. Modern girls spend all their energy on losing weight. But I heard that this weight loss product is really effective. Later, this girl fell in love with me. Although my financial situation is not very good now, I still have enough money to buy her weight loss products.
