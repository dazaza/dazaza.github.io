2011 Cabinet Trends

by qwy854627 on 2011-05-23 12:57:36

After a millennium of development, human civilization has reached an unprecedented level of prosperity. Our living standards have also reached new heights. Especially modern kitchens, which offer not just functionality but also a sensory enjoyment. In 2011, the trend in cabinet development will become more professional and more humanized.

When it comes to home renovation, kitchen cabinets are a very important aspect. As consumer purchasing attitudes mature, ordinary design styles and simple functionalities can no longer meet the high demands for cabinets. To better understand the criteria consumers use when selecting cabinets and their latest purchasing needs, China Kitchen conducted an online survey.

According to the survey results, the importance consumers place on the environmental performance of cabinets and product reputation is increasing day by day. Furthermore, interest in buying kitchen items as a set is also rising. In light of these consumption trends, how to enhance brand recognition and how to gradually achieve the concept of an integrated kitchen has become a crucial topic that all merchants in the 2011 cabinet market cannot avoid.

Health and environmental protection are highly focused on

Environmental protection has always been one of the most concerning topics for consumers in the home furnishings market. Compared with other products, people tend to have stricter requirements regarding the environmental friendliness of cabinets. According to the survey results, compared to hidden dangers such as cutting corners and price traps in cabinet decoration, more consumers are still more concerned about environmental issues. Additionally, nearly forty percent of consumers prefer to buy solid wood cabinets. Besides having collection value, the primary reason is that the environmental coefficient of pure natural wood is higher.

Zhang Qing stated: Cabinets come into direct contact with food every day. If they don't meet environmental standards, villa kitchens could pose a threat to family health. Therefore, when choosing cabinets, I not only look at whether the brand has authoritative environmental inspection reports issued by relevant departments but also inquire in detail about whether the basic materials, accessories, and post-processing technologies of the cabinets comply with environmental standards. In short, one must do thorough research before making a choice. It is understood that as consumer attitudes mature, besides paying attention to environmental inspection reports when purchasing cabinets, whether the panels reach E0 standards and whether they truly achieve zero formaldehyde have all become topics of concern for consumers.

Brand reputation is the key to selection

When it comes to product reputation, there are two main keys: quality and service. Only by excelling in both aspects can a brand achieve greater development. Moreover, since cabinets belong to customized products, the service requirements for merchants are relatively high. Therefore, for the cabinet industry, meticulous and comprehensive services are essential conditions for capturing a larger market share. This service does not merely refer to pre-sale door-to-door measurements or mid-sale door-to-door installations; it also includes perfect after-sales services, such as timely problem resolution and regular follow-ups. Dai Wei, the manager of Debei Cabinets, mentioned quality service: We consistently adhere to a brand-oriented approach. No matter how we promote sales, our quality and service remain uncompromised. We offer a 10-year warranty on all our products, which is the highest in the industry because we have great confidence in our products. The importance merchants place on brand building and reputation accumulation is evident from this.

Facing increasingly stringent environmental requirements from consumers, major cabinet merchants in the home furnishing market are becoming more and more focused on the environmental performance of their products. Regarding green decoration, Debei Integrated Cabinets Chengdu area stated: Nowadays, people advocate a green, environmentally friendly, low-carbon lifestyle. Debei can be said to perform quite well in this aspect because the panels we use are among the best domestically, and we ensure four-sided sealing for all panels, effectively preventing formaldehyde exposure. Additionally, in terms of hardware components, we consider our consumers' needs and use damping hinges, effectively preventing noise pollution. Debei has been in business for over a decade, so we can take care of these details for our consumers.

Improving quality of life starts with the kitchen where you prepare your meals.
