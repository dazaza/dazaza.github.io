Hundred Million Christmas New Year Shopping Carnival

by baizhao2010 on 2010-09-13 16:21:21

Many people still remember this summer’s "2010 Baizhao Cool Summer Shopping Festival." At the time, the festival attracted hundreds of thousands of consumers with its diverse activities and great promotions on quality goods, sparking a shopping frenzy and earning widespread acclaim. Last month, Baizhao launched the "2010 Baizhao Christmas New Year Shopping Festival," once again leading the trend of winter shopping in 2010. It is reported that this Christmas New Year Shopping Festival will continue until February 12, 2011, just before the Spring Festival.

 "Where are you planning to go shopping for Christmas this year?" "Of course, Baizhao!" Recently, when discussing plans for "Christmas New Year Shopping," economically-minded citizens have mentioned the "Baizhao Christmas New Year Shopping Festival."

According to the head of this year's Baizhao shopping festival, the event fully integrates the resources of Baizhao merchants. Within a concentrated timeframe, they passionately offer the best products and services from top merchants. "This festival lasts long, spans a large period, has rich content, covers a wide range, and involves many merchants, touching almost every aspect of people's lives. It allows consumers to truly enjoy the fun of 'a colorful Christmas, dancing into the New Year, easy shopping, and happy consumption,' fully experiencing the benefits of 'saving money, good quality items, and generous rewards.'"

 

During the event, Baizhao Gift Mall will launch an annual super-low price mega-sale across various sections including plastic gifts, PVC gifts, gel injection molding, advertising promotional items, trademark rubber badges, auxiliary materials, accessories, toys, silicone bracelets, mobile phone charms, Christmas New Year gifts, mugs, refrigerator magnets, USB flash drive cases, shoe flowers, and shoe buckles. These items will be offered at even lower factory prices compared to wholesale rates as a way to thank their customers.

It is worth noting that during the event, customers who spend over 1000 yuan in a single day at Baizhao Gift Mall can receive a free exquisite gift with their purchase receipt.

 

 

The responsible official also revealed that just before Christmas, Baizhao will introduce a variety of interactive shopping segments such as "Santa Claus is Here! — Sweet Gifts Giveaway," allowing consumers to experience unique holiday shopping joy in a relaxed and lively atmosphere.

It is reported that Baizhao Gift Mall is located in Shijie Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, and opened in 2006. The mall specializes in advertising promotional gifts, business office gifts, and custom festival gifts.
