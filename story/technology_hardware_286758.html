Premier League - Liverpool 2-0 Everton: Mascherano Causes Own Goal, Kuyt Scores

At 21:30 on the evening of November 29th, Beijing time, a key match of the 14th round of the 2009-10 Premier League season took place at Goodison Park. In the derby of Merseyside, Liverpool faced Everton for their 212th encounter. In the first half, Yobo deflected Mascherano's long-range shot into his own net, and in the second half, Dirk Kuyt extended Liverpool's lead. Liverpool won 2-0 away from home, bringing them one step closer to returning to the top four.

In previous encounters between the two teams, Liverpool had an advantage with 82 wins, 64 draws, and 65 losses over 211 matches. In last season's fixture at this venue, Liverpool defeated Everton thanks to two goals from Fernando Torres. For this game, Liverpool was still plagued by injuries, with Torres absent due to injury and Babel not even on the substitutes' list. Pepe Reina continued in goal, with Glen Johnson at right back, Agger and Carragher forming the central defensive partnership, and Insua playing left back. In midfield, Lucas and Mascherano formed a double pivot, with Aurelio and Kuyt on the flanks and Steven Gerrard as the attacking midfielder. Ngog replaced Torres up front.

Despite being hailed by Benítez as the most classic local derby in England, both teams currently find themselves in somewhat desolate situations. Liverpool has been pushed out of the top four, while Everton has slipped to 16th place in the league table, just one step away from the relegation zone.

The game started with Glen Johnson sending a diagonal pass into the box in the 4th minute. Ngog chested the ball down and turned to shoot but lacked power, allowing Howard to collect the ball easily.

In the 12th minute, after Insua provided support from the left flank and returned the ball, Mascherano unleashed a powerful shot from outside the box from 25 yards out. The ball was deflected by Yobo’s foot and flew straight into the bottom-right corner of the net, leaving Howard no chance to react. Liverpool led 1-0. Since Yobo changed the trajectory of the ball, it was officially counted as an own goal.

In the 16th minute, Gerrard attempted a diagonal shot from the right side, which Howard held onto. In the 17th minute, after Everton threw a boundary ball from the right, following three consecutive headers inside the box, Bilyayevych found himself unmarked at the far post six yards out, but his shot went wide of the left post.

In the 23rd minute, Everton created continuous threats in front of Liverpool's penalty area. Subsequently, Bilyayevych executed a scissor kick shot that Reina spectacularly saved, with the defense clearing the danger. In the 27th minute, Pienaar's left-footed shot from the edge of the box missed the target on the left. In the 28th minute, Hibbert initiated an attack with a long pass, Fellaini headed the ball on, but Jo's follow-up shot lacked power and was collected by Reina.

By the 30th minute, Pienaar's diagonal shot from the right was gathered by Reina. In the 35th minute, Kuyt's powerful shot from the edge of the box went wide of the post. In the 41st minute, Glen Johnson sent a diagonal cross from the right, and Insua headed the ball powerfully from six yards out, only for Howard to make an incredible save to tip the ball over the bar. In the 43rd minute, Jo scored from close range, but he was offside, making the goal invalid. The first half ended with Liverpool leading 1-0.

In the 55th minute, Fellaini surged forward and took a shot that was blocked by the defender. In the 58th minute, Everton launched an attack through a long pass. Liverpool's defenders cleared the ball only a short distance, allowing Pienaar to attempt a left-footed shot from the edge of the box. Reina fumbled the ball, which almost trickled through his legs into the goal, but he quickly regained control.

In the 62nd minute, Heitinga's left-footed shot from the edge of the box sailed high over the bar. In the 64th minute, Liverpool's corner resulted in Agger's header going wide. In the 67th minute, Kuyt crossed from the right, and Ngog's close-range shot was blocked by Yobo's body. In the 69th minute, Saha's low-angle shot hit the side-netting.

In the 71st minute, Everton's free-kick saw Cahill rise to head the ball towards goal, but Reina made another remarkable diving save. Fellaini followed up with a close-range shot, which Reina again blocked with his body.

In the 80th minute, Yakubu's clearance error allowed Gerrard to retrieve the ball and pass it back to Riera, who then shot and caused Howard to drop the ball. Kuyt capitalized on the rebound opportunity and easily slotted the ball into the empty net, extending Liverpool's lead to 2-0.

In the 89th minute, Riera's left-sided shot was parried away by Howard.

Everton Starting XI (4-4-2):

24-Howard; 2-Hibbert, 4-Yobo (87' 23-Lucas-Nil), 15-Distin, 3-Baines; 20-Pienaar, 5-Heitinga, 25-Fellaini, 7-Bilyayevych; 17-Cahill (81' 22-Yakubu), 11-Jo (67' 8-Saha).

Liverpool Starting XI (4-5-1):

25-Reina; 2-Glen Johnson, 23-Carragher, 5-Agger, 22-Insua; 18-Kuyt, 21-Lucas, 20-Mascherano, 8-Gerrard, 12-Aurelio (78' 11-Riera); 24-Ngog (75' 15-Benayoun).
