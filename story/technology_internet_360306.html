Borrowing strength from the Pacific Gateway Network to seize the opportunity in the world's towel industry

by topxfcjf on 2010-07-09 11:30:04

       Our towel factory was established in 1980 and is a manufacturer mainly engaged in the production of various mid-to-high-end towel, bath towel series products, as well as bathrobes, cotton blankets, acrylic blankets, and bedding. Although it has been around for quite some time, due to information isolation, outdated marketing methods, and lack of e-commerce platforms, the development of the enterprise has been slow, making it an obscure local small factory. The real big development of the enterprise started after 2006. In just three to five years, our factory developed into a modern low-carbon environmental protection enterprise covering the entire industrial chain from spinning, weaving, bleaching, printing, dyeing, to finishing, with infrastructure such as its own thermal power plant, reservoir, and sewage treatment plant.

Online Business Promotes Enterprise Development

       Looking back at the rapid development of our enterprise, we cannot fail to mention Pacific Gateway www.topoyo.com. Pacific Gateway is a large comprehensive international e-commerce platform founded in Shenzhen in 2006, covering a wide range of categories and functions. It has Chinese, English, Canadian, and Russian language platforms and has opened up 15 logistics channels, integrating logistics freight centers and express customs clearance companies from 15 cities in the Greater China region, providing credit certification for more than 1,000 logistics companies. These characteristics of the website perfectly meet our enterprise's demand for transformation towards an outward-oriented model to seek benefits in the international market. At the beginning of the website's operation, we quickly established contact with them and, under their guidance, built our independent corporate website. Relying on our self-operated corporate website and the rich resources of Pacific Gateway, our e-commerce business has flourished.

       As the experts of Pacific Gateway said, owning your own website is equivalent to owning your own online property, which is the most basic prerequisite for corporate credibility and image and a strong guarantee for further development. An independently named corporate portal can effectively increase the success rate of e-commerce transactions while continuously expanding the influence of the enterprise.

Corporate Portal websites are the carefully crafted "online home" of enterprises by Pacific Gateway. Through this platform, we can not only release various promotional information about our latest products, company news, company photos, qualifications, honors, but also conduct comprehensive online promotions for the enterprise, such as managing orders online, publishing corporate blogs, building product trees, etc., and make more business partners (domestic and international buyers or procurement agents) through online services, thereby obtaining more valuable orders.

E-Commerce Opens Up Overseas Markets

       E-commerce has given wings to our enterprise. The enterprise has achieved remarkable results in implementing brand strategies and internationalization, successively obtaining honorary titles such as "China Famous Brand", "China Well-known Trademark", and "National Exempted Inspection Product". The "LOFTEX" brand has a high level of recognition. In 2007, the company's technical center was recognized by the National Development and Reform Commission and other five ministries as a nationally recognized enterprise technical center with strong comprehensive innovation capabilities. In 2008, the company's textile testing center received "National Accredited Laboratory" recognition (CNAS). Meanwhile, the company became the secretariat undertaking unit of the Towel Sub-Technical Committee of the National Home Textiles Standardization Technical Committee. The new fiber products produced by the company, such as bamboo fiber, maifan stone, pearl fiber, plant cashmere, bamboo charcoal fiber, etc., have entered the mainstream markets of developed countries like the United States and Australia.

       The vitality of e-commerce has prompted the company's management level to continuously reach new heights. After the rapid development in recent years hand in hand with Pacific Gateway, our enterprise has now become a national large-scale modern home textiles manufacturing enterprise, specializing in producing China Famous Brand towels, bath towels, bathrobes, towel quilts, blankets, and bedding. Covering an area of 4.3 million square meters, with 8,000 employees, producing over 100 million pieces of towel products annually. Exporting to more than thirty countries and regions including Japan, the United States, Europe, Australia, Canada, and Southeast Asia, deeply favored by users. The company owns 246 of the world's most advanced rapier and air-jet looms, 20 printing production lines, as well as cone dyeing machines, overflow dyeing machines, and advanced post-finishing equipment. Established good business relationships and cooperation with many internationally renowned large enterprises. In recent years, the group has adhered to the strategy of internationalization and branding. It has set up companies in countries such as the United States and Australia, registered brands such as "HOMETEX, ADORE, HOME TRENDS", successfully entering the mainstream markets of developed countries, gradually realizing the transition from private label production to having intellectual property rights.

       In future development, the company will continue to rely on e-commerce platforms such as Pacific Gateway, dedicated to creating an international enterprise and international brands, advancing boldly into terminal markets and mainstream markets, relying on scientific and technological innovation and solid quality, leading the trend of towel popularity, creating its own brand and green low-carbon products, deepening energy conservation and emission reduction, contributing its strength to the development of China's national industry.
