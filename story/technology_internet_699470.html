There is a type of house in the market that is highly sought after by buyers, and that is the duplex unit. Whether it's a true two-story high duplex or a smaller and more agile mini-duplex, both are best-selling unit types in the real estate market. In the decoration of these duplex units, a staircase connecting the upper and lower spaces is essential.

They are not only art for connection but also come with various emerging functions such as storage, entertainment, and artistic creativity. 1. Modern Style

The design of staircase decoration [http://www.dcuan.com] mainly depends on the combination of staircase materials and styles. Modern style staircases generally achieve their look through marble steps, glass steps paired with glass handrails. Many modern style staircases have relatively elegant colors; the color of marble steps should not be too dark, coupled with simple and transparent glass handrails, which better convey a sense of simplicity and freshness. Through the pairing of points and surfaces, straight lines and diagonals, modern style staircases break away from the traditional dullness and rigidity of stairs, making the entire indoor space clearer and more transparent.

2. European Style Solid Wood Staircase [http://www.china-kewen.com]

European style generally emphasizes lavish decoration, intense colors, and exquisite shapes to ultimately achieve an opulent decorative effect, and staircases are no exception. In some large duplex units or villa units, designers often choose European-style staircases made of marble steps paired with solid wood balustrades, and pure solid wood steps paired with solid wood balustrades are also a major trend. To reflect the luxury of European style, the balustrades of the staircase often use hand-carved wooden columns, and the steps should also focus on a glossy finish.

3. Chinese Style

In recent years, the return of Eastern-flavored residences has allowed the Chinese decorative style to win a larger market [staircase columns http://www.xx0.org]. In large duplex units and villa units, Chinese decoration can be fully utilized, and the Chinese-style staircase plays an important role. Nowadays, most Chinese-style staircase designs adopt a combination of solid wood steps and solid wood balustrades, showcasing the elegant culture of traditional China through various unique Chinese symbols. For example, adopting a full-package design as the main feature, side facades decorated with Chinese wood carvings, and balustrades can also adopt Chinese window lattice or hollow wood enclosures, creating a continuous romantic Chinese style.

4. Country Style

Country style is also one of the mainstream interior decoration styles, and many people love its natural and fresh feel. Therefore, designs that relax the mind and feature smooth curves are very suitable for this style. Country-style staircases mostly combine solid wood steps with ironwork balustrades. In many small duplex residences, spiral ironwork staircases are one of the common varieties.
