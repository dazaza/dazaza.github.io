Whether to choose satin or tulle for a wedding dress depends on the body shape _ Shenyang Photography Studio

by fj0926446 on 2010-04-01 18:02:03

Speaking of the international wedding gown brand VIVAN under "Mingmen Bridal", many girls would think of luxurious and elegant European palace gowns. Yesterday, there were quite a few brides-to-be consulting at the "Mingmen Bridal" store. Faced with so many styles, the girls all felt dazzled, why was every piece of wedding dress in VIVAN so beautiful?

The sales consultant of Mingmen smiled and asked these girls: "Where is the wedding held?" It turns out that whether the wedding gown matches the wedding venue is the first thing to know. If it's a grassland wedding, then a gown with a long train wouldn't be suitable.

Next, the sales consultant didn't rush to introduce the gowns but instead first explained "what kind of gown suits what body type."

A. Satin Type This kind of wedding gown requires the bride to have a full figure and an elegant and noble temperament. It is suggested that brides with petite or relatively slim figures should not try this style.

B. Tulle Type The tulle gown is light, lively, cute, and fresh, giving off a very princess-like vibe when worn.

C. Slim Fit Type This style has high requirements for body shape, requiring both height and slim yet curvy figure.

D. Floor-length Type The floor-length gown is more convenient to wear, suitable for petite brides. It has a body-shaping effect and can make the figure appear taller.

E. Train Type The gown with a train looks more luxurious and grand, suitable for church or large hotel weddings.

Soon, the girls had their own targets. The sales consultant said that now many weddings are becoming more and more grand, and many brides are no longer satisfied with wearing just one wedding gown during the ceremony. For example, some brides first choose a unique gown that is shorter in front and longer at the back, which is worn in the morning of the wedding day. Another gown might be a pure white floor-length gown worn during the evening banquet, making the occasion more formal and grand.

The tailor from Mingmen also particularly reminded the brides: don't lose weight before customizing your gown, and the underwear worn during the measurement should preferably be the same as the one worn on the wedding day, so that the gown size can be measured most accurately.
