Traditional Chinese Medicine Jitai Tablets 2010 Shanghai TCM Detoxification Academic Seminar

by bjjitaipian on 2012-02-29 21:25:02

Jitai tablets participate in the 2010 Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Detoxification Academic Seminar, celebrating the grand opening of the 2010 Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Detoxification Academic Seminar. To welcome the June 26th International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking, the 2010 Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Detoxification Academic Seminar was grandly held on June 23rd, 2010 at the Pudian Software Building in the Pudong New Area of Shanghai.

This seminar was co-organized by the Shanghai Center for Promoting Science and Technology Industry of Traditional Chinese Medicine, the Science and Technology Committee of Pudong New Area, the Pudong New Area Association for Science and Technology, the Office of Zhangjiang Hi-Tech Park, and the National Shanghai Biomedical Science and Technology Industrial Base. It was hosted by the National Engineering Research Center for Traditional Chinese Medicine Manufacturing Technology and the Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Manufacturing Technology Co., Ltd. More than 170 comrades attended the meeting, including leaders and experts from the Shanghai Narcotics Control Commission, the Shanghai Food and Drug Administration, the Medical Department of the Shanghai Science and Technology Commission, the Science and Technology Committee of Pudong New Area, the Association for Science and Technology, the Comprehensive Governance Office of the Political and Legal Affairs Commission of Pudong New Area, as well as important leaders from the Shanghai Ziqiang Social Service General Society and the Shanghai Zhongzhi Community Service Society, responsible persons of their respective district stations, and representatives of community social workers. The seminar consisted of three parts: the opening ceremony (leaders' speeches), academic research reports, and a drug control knowledge competition.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Deputy Secretary Pu Zemin of the Pudong New Area Association for Science and Technology. Director Huang Ying of the Shanghai Center for Promoting Science and Technology Industry of Traditional Chinese Medicine delivered a welcoming speech. Director Zhang Ping of the Zhongzhi Community Service Society reported on the progress of the pairing and joint construction work between the Shanghai Zhongzhi Society and the Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Manufacturing Technology Co., Ltd. Important speeches were made by General Manager Wang Lanzhong of the National Shanghai Biomedical Science and Technology Industrial Base, Deputy Director Fan Jincheng of the Pudong New Area Health Bureau, Deputy Director Shen Jianhua of the Pudong New Area Food and Drug Administration, Deputy Director Sun Dianwei of the Comprehensive Governance Office of the Political and Legal Affairs Commission of Pudong New Area, General Director Sun Anqing of the Ziqiang Social Service General Society, and Deputy Director Zheng Wei of the Shanghai Narcotics Control Office.

Director Shen Pingniang of the National Engineering Research Center for Traditional Chinese Medicine Manufacturing Technology presided over the academic research report session. At the report session, Dr. Deng Yanping of the China Drug Dependence Research Institute gave a presentation titled "Characteristics and Prospects of Traditional Chinese Medicine Detoxification," Professor Han Mei of Beijing Normal University presented on "Application of Molecular Imaging Evaluation Methods in Traditional Chinese Medicine Detoxification Research," Researcher Zhang Jianchen of the Shanghai Narcotics Control Office spoke on "Application and Development of Comprehensive Intervention Measures in Community-Based Detoxification and Rehabilitation," Doctor Zhuang Lingyun of the Zhangjiang Community Rehabilitation Center presented on "Exploration of a Community-Based Detoxification and Rehabilitation Model Combining Jitai Tablets with Psychological and Social Interventions," Professor Liu Runhui of the Second Military Medical University presented on "Research on the Pharmacological Substance Basis of the Detoxification Effect of Jitai Tablets," and Political Commissar Li Yu of the Shanghai Compulsory Isolation Detoxification Center gave an academic report titled "Reflections on the Work of Community-Based Anti-Drug Social Workers."

A hot topic at this seminar was the successful case of the Zhangjiang Community Rehabilitation Center using Jitai Tablets to treat patients addicted to methadone. A 42-year-old patient shared his personal experience, stating that he had been addicted to drugs for 17 years and had failed multiple attempts at detoxification. In February 2010, he began taking the traditional Chinese medicine "Jitai Tablets" for two months, supplemented by multiple psychological counseling sessions with Doctor Zhuang Lingyun. He has now successfully overcome his methadone addiction symptoms, truly returning to nature and society. This successful example fully demonstrates the significant effects of "Jitai Tablets" in detoxification and withdrawal treatment. Secretary Sun Anqing, General Director of the Shanghai Ziqiang Social Service General Society and the Shanghai Anti-Drug Volunteer Association, expressed great attention to this, especially after listening to the patient's own introduction. He thoroughly understood the entire process and hoped to timely summarize and promote it, benefiting more patients with addictions.

Deputy General Manager Du Chenjie of the Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Manufacturing Technology Co., Ltd. provided a detailed explanation of the "Project Flow Introduction of Efficacy Evaluation and Community-Based Detoxification and Rehabilitation Treatment Model Research Primarily Based on Interventional Treatment with Jitai Tablets" and answered and unified some key issues regarding the specific operations of Topic Three by social workers.

Finally, the anti-drug knowledge competition part aroused great interest among the delegates attending the conference, especially among the broad social workers of the Shanghai Ziqiang Society and the Zhongzhi Society. It not only increased anti-drug knowledge but also enlivened the conference atmosphere, truly achieving the effect of learning through fun.

The successful convening of the 2010 Shanghai Traditional Chinese Medicine Academic Seminar provided a platform for relevant experts, professional social worker organizations, and grassroots social workers to jointly participate and exchange experiences, truly achieving the goal of studying and exchanging community-based detoxification work experiences. It confirmed the efficacy of traditional Chinese medicine detoxification in community-based detoxification and rehabilitation, expanded the recognition of Jitai Tablets among patients with addictions, promoted the in-depth development of the National Science and Technology Support Program "Clinical Re-evaluation and Quality Improvement Research of Listed Detoxification Traditional Chinese Medicines," and played a catalytic role in realizing Topic Three of the project, "Efficacy Evaluation and Community-Based Detoxification and Rehabilitation Treatment Model Research Primarily Based on Interventional Treatment with Jitai Tablets."
