Undersea Guilin

by zengxr840824 on 2008-05-31 17:58:45

The Sea Bottom Guilin scenic spot was carefully built by the Jianrong Group Corporation of Malaysia with an investment of nearly 100 million yuan. It miniatures a batch of landscapes that feature Guilin's mountains and waters, such as the city emblem of Guilin - Elephant Hill, Seven-Star Hill which is shaped like the Plough, Diecai Hill which looks like piled brocade, Bright Hill where the Reed Flute Cave known as the "state guest cave" is located, Moon Hill in Yangshuo and the landscape along the Lijiang River, into more than 40 display tanks using the landscaping technique. It ingeniously combines marine life with Guilin's mountains and waters, reappearing the fascinating sea bottom fairyland from hundreds of millions of years ago.

The Sea Bottom Guilin scenic spot is located on the south side of the beautiful Nanxi Hill in the urban area. Inside it are 11 visiting areas including the Sea Bottom Guilin area, Guilin ancient biology area, tropical rainforest area, marine biology area and performing area, etc. It displays more than 8,000 rare fishes and strange marine creatures of over 800 varieties from rivers, lakes and seas all over the world. It is one of the largest oceanariums in Asia that has the most fish species.

Here you can see Nautilus which is a national treasure-level animal unique in China and appeared on the earth about 500 million years ago. The one you can see now is the only living Nautilus in the country at present; there are also Dipnoi which reproduced in large quantities in the ancient times and are called "living fossils"; rare blind fish whose eyes have degenerated and live in the dark water of karst caves in Guilin; weird and huge baby fish full of mountain-water spirit growing deep in Guilin's mountains; Limulus polyphemus (called blue-blooded animals) which is the offspring of Trilobite that became extinct long ago and dates back to about 100 million years ago in the late Mesozoic era. Top ten wonderful performances:

The performance of "Sea Bottom Guilin" is more wonderful. The sexy mermaid group will interpret the realm of the joy and beauty of human and fish with high-difficulty underwater dance skills, arousing your romantic reverie of the fairy-tale like sea kingdom. The man-shark co-dance performance is performed by experienced divers who dare to perform life-and-death acts with 3-meter big sharks, providing you with brand-new sensory stimulation. In addition, there are other top ten wonderful performances such as seal performance, bikini diving, and feeding of the big strange bird cow nose stingray (fen), underwater bomber giant stingray (hong), piranhas, century-old turtles, etc., adding more fun for you.

World's best:

"Sea Bottom Guilin" presents the rarest, newest, most beautiful, oldest, rarest, and most interesting marine creatures in the world in front of you. Here you can see the biggest plankton in the world - jellyfish, the fiercest fish - shark, the slowest swimming fish - golden seahorse, the most poisonous fish - lionfish...

This scenic spot is the science popularization education base for teenagers in Guilin City

Travel tips:

Ticket price: 80 yuan for adults, 30 yuan for children (1.2-1.4 meters), free for children under 1.2 meters

Opening hours: 8:30-18:30

Transportation to the scenic spot: Get off at Nanxi Hill Hospital station by bus No. 11, 12, 33, 56, 99, and walk for 50 meters to get there

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Distance in kilometers from the scenic spot to each main location:

Distance to Guilin urban area: 2 km

Distance to bus station: 1.5 km

Distance to railway station: 1 km

Distance to airport: 3.5 km
