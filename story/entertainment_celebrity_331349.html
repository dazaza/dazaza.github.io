Reflection on the Sharp Brother incident, our website administrator should pay attention to whether the pursuit of traffic will hurt the other party while doing so - Nanjing SEO Bar

by maomaochaoren on 2010-03-03 18:19:48

Today I read a post by Laochantao. I realized that during the time when the internet was abuzz with the popular image of "Xili Ge", we had been circulating his photo everywhere and even photoshopped it. Would these behaviors cause harm to the parties involved? Although it is unlikely that Xili Ge would be online, the internet has drawn more people's attention to him, and some meddlers have even gone to take pictures of him as if he were a prop. The videos later released online showing him being scared and crying made me regretful. An ancient saying goes: Do not do small evils because they are small, nor refrain from doing small good deeds because they are insignificant. How thought-provoking this sentence is! I hope that our webmaster's ability is not only used for generating traffic, but we can also use our abilities to spread the information of those in need all over the country. Help Xili Ge find his family.

At 1 p.m. on February 25, a post titled "The Handsome Beggar in Ningbo Is Just a Pitiful Person" on Tianyi Forum unraveled the mystery.

The poster is a well-known Ningbo netizen, "Laochantao". "Laochantao" began paying attention to "Xili Ge" as early as July 2008, and in early August 2008, he even lived with him for a while.

Yesterday evening, the reporter contacted "Laochantao". Speaking of "Xili Ge", "Laochantao's" first sentence was: "He is not a beggar, but a pitiful person."

In August 2008, "Laochantao" started paying attention to a group of mentally ill vagrants in Ningbo. They had no identity, no family, and even forgot their own names. "Xili Ge" was one of them. "Laochantao" called him "Xiaobai".

"When I first met him, he couldn't even speak clearly." "Laochantao" said that at that time, "Xiaobai" hadn't spoken for a long time.

At that time, "Xiaobai" was picking up cigarette butts. "Laochantao" asked him, "Are you hungry?" He replied, "Yes." "Laochantao" wanted to test his self-care ability, so he gave him 10 yuan and let him buy food. Unexpectedly, after receiving the money, "Xiaobai" turned around and bought a pack of cigarettes from a convenience store.

"Xili Ge" with various styles - "I asked him why he didn't buy food, and he pointed to the trash can. It took me a long time to understand what he meant: food can be picked up from the trash can," said "Laochantao".

"Xiaobai" also had moments of clarity. He once told "Laochantao" something like this: "Find a woman to love me." This is precisely the reason why he often wears women's clothing.

"He wants a wife and a home. He puts on women's clothes, imagines himself as a woman, thus becoming one with her, having a complete home, and feeling happy," said "Laochantao".

The last time "Laochantao" saw "Xiaobai" was at the end of last year, when he was wandering near Yuehu Lake. "I will go look for 'Xiaobai' again and see how he is now," said "Laochantao". He hopes to use this event to appeal to relevant departments to show more concern for these mentally ill people who cannot take care of themselves. Life should be fair, and it should be the same for everyone.
