Happy Baby Simple Guide (Instant Update)

by shomarjack on 2009-08-04 18:18:31

I went wrong when I played this game. When I first entered, I didn't fix my appearance and just picked a bad name and went in, then I had to spend money on plastic surgery and change my name. Be sure to pay attention that when you first enter the page, the rabbit's gender, color, ears, nose, eyes, brain shape, etc., can all be modified (it seems that you can turn the rabbit into a pig or panda depending on your own matching). You must first change it to what you like, otherwise if you change it later, you will have to spend money.

Firstly, when you first enter the game, wash yourself thoroughly and buy a big hamburger to eat, don't be afraid of wasting money, the more expensive the food, the more experience seems to be added.

After leveling up once, they give you a broken ball, go ahead and throw it around. If you catch it accurately, it adds money and experience, and you can even win trophies.

Ball catching secret: Try to stay close to the little rabbit (not too close, otherwise you won't have time to react), roughly find the direction after the ball is thrown, keep the mouse almost fixed. Of course, occasionally with the up and down movement of the ball, there will be slight changes. As the number of successful attempts increases, the ball speed will get faster and faster. Generally, every 15 times or so, the ball speed will significantly increase. We can appropriately move the mouse backward, mainly mastering the ball speed, quick reaction is needed. It should be noted that when catching the ball, you must not click twice on the ball. If you click twice, the success count will +1, but it counts as a failure (the same as the frisbee game, but the up and down movement of the frisbee will be smaller, making it easier).

As your level increases, you will get jump ropes, frisbees, and later upgrades for soap, balls, etc.

Regarding the running track at the sports field, you get 10 chances per day. Winning first place gives 30J and 20 experience. If you want to level up or earn money, skipping rope is more practical. Although it doesn't add much, compared to throwing balls or frisbees, the speed is faster and the success rate is higher.

Supplement: If you know how to play, the chance of winning the race is still quite high. Moreover, the watermelon peel can be clicked away with the mouse, whether it's on your track or someone else's. Move your mouse over and left-click, and the watermelon peel will disappear, and there's a chance to earn money by clicking it away. Now it has been updated so that pressing spacebar allows jumping over obstacles, so we can place the mouse in front waiting for the watermelon peel...

Skipping rope method: Pay attention to the rope, generally, when the rope passes under the chin, you can jump. After successfully jumping 50 times, when the rope passes the eyes, you can jump; after 60 times, when it passes the eyebrows. Similarly, the more successful attempts, the faster the speed. After 70 times, the speed remains almost unchanged, if you find the rhythm and don't get bored, you can jump as many times as you want. My highest score is 212, but I got distracted.

Oh well, just keep jumping until you're exhausted.

The more successful attempts with the ball, frisbee, and skipping rope, the more experience is added when it ends.

After each failure, check if you need to use soap. Whether using soap or washing, if done to full capacity, it also adds money and experience. Even if only one small grid is lost.

Another thing, this is currently a test version. So far, playing games like skipping rope, throwing balls, and frisbees adds less money and experience, but there is no upper limit. Regarding visiting friends' trophy, after entering someone else's house, click leave, go to town, then walk back into the house, visit again and click leave... Even if you've visited before, after coming out, you'll find that the number of visits still increases. However... This number seems to be a virtual number. After I visited 25 times, it showed that I won the trophy, but I actually didn't get it. And when I relogged into the game, I found that the number of visits was the same as the first strange visit, meaning that this trophy is hard to farm...

In short, that's all for now, wish everyone plays happily.

Supplement

About hitting trees, there's a chance to gain money from hitting trees. Moreover, after hitting all the trees, randomly entering a house or going home, then coming out, you can continue hitting them... Hitting trees also has a chance to get 30J at once.

If you can't understand this, you can first look at the customer service guide in the game.

Supplement

A very sneaky way to earn money and experience...

Go to someone else's house, when their baby's cleanliness value or heart value is not full, give them soap, wash them, and we earn money and experience... Of course, you can also throw food in others' houses, they gain health value, and we gain experience.

Among the trophies, there is an accumulated gift trophy. To farm this, you can go to the food store and buy the cheapest carrots, 7J each, 50 only cost 350J, very easy to obtain. There's another sneaky method, which is, two people agree, use only one carrot, exchange gifts back and forth, spending 7J can farm the Santa Claus trophy.
