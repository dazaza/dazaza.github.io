Start from zero - Become an excellent wedding planner

by longersing on 2009-04-24 14:37:39

Two years ago, Chen Chen graduated from the Chinese Department of Nanjing University. As a tall and beautiful girl, it was not hard for her to find a job. Her parents in Xuzhou hometown also arranged a stable job for her, but she was like a bird that had just gained freedom and was curious about the society.

"When I was in middle school, I saw a movie called 'Wedding Planner' starring Jennifer Lopez, which left a deep impression on me. She was an expert in planning romantic weddings for newlyweds." This movie had a great impact and shock on Chen Chen. She still remembers those romantic weddings, and being a wedding planner has become her ideal career. "I am a girl too, so I understand very well what newlyweds want for their own marriage, something they will remember for a lifetime."

Thus, when Chen Chen attended the job fair at Wutaishan Stadium with her resume after graduation, she still hoped to get such a job. "I don't know if there are such jobs in Nanjing that would allow me to become someone who deals with weddings and romance every day. I only hope to find a job that I like." At the job fair, Chen Chen saw a wedding ceremony company. "I don't know exactly what this company does, I just feel that the company image is good and it's related to weddings, so I paid attention to it."

Unexpectedly, this wedding company didn't hire undergraduates. "When I was about to go over, I happened to see a master's degree holder submitting his resume. The recruiter said earnestly: 'We don't need such high education as a master's degree, not even an undergraduate degree.'" After hearing this conversation, Chen Chen strangely hid her resume and shyly filled out a form, writing down 'Junior College' in the education section. "And I was really hired!" Chen Chen said.

The romantic job started with serving tea and water.

In the first month of work, due to having no knowledge of wedding ceremonies, Chen Chen's job was to open doors for guests, say "welcome", serve tea and water, and hand over ashtrays...

"I joined this industry because I liked wedding planning, but after all, I am also a graduate of Nanjing University. Regardless of the trial period salary of a few hundred yuan, doing these tasks every day made me feel extremely unbalanced," Chen Chen said. Moreover, her mother always hoped she would return to Xuzhou, and was completely dissatisfied with her current job in Nanjing. Every time she talked to her mother on the phone, her mother always reminded her: "If you don't do well, come home." Chen Chen said, at that time, she was very disheartened. "I often cried alone at night. I never wanted to be the tea sister who serves tea and water or hands over ashtrays anymore. I wondered if I had chosen the wrong industry."

It seemed that the romantic job started with tedious and simple tasks, and she felt like a deflated balloon. She stopped actively handing over ashtrays to customers. "I realized that if I didn't do these things, someone else would do them for me. I suddenly found out that even though I didn't pour water, the guests still had water to drink, our general manager personally poured water for the guests and handed over ashtrays." The boss didn't say a word to Chen Chen, he just did what Chen Chen should have done. Chen Chen, recalling this scene from long ago, still feels ashamed, "At that moment, my heart sank, and the psychological imbalance disappeared immediately."

From that day on, Chen Chen's "welcome" was more enthusiastic than anyone else's, and she served tea and water more diligently than anyone else. Within a month, due to her meticulous service attitude, she became a formal customer service staff member of Romantique Wedding.

Planning romantic weddings for unromantic grooms

Gradually, Chen Chen began to participate in some wedding planning. Due to her liberal arts background and past experience in writing children's literature, her ability in wedding planning improved quickly. After gaining recognition from the guests multiple times, the company assigned some special requests to her to handle. Now, Chen Chen is already a top-notch wedding planner comparable to Jennifer Lopez in the movie.

Not long ago, Chen Chen planned a romantic wedding for an unromantic groom. "When the groom came to me, he said that he deeply loved his fiancée, but he wasn't a romantic person, so he wanted to give her a surprise at their wedding. He hoped that his wedding would be romantic enough."

Chen Chen learned that the groom was the head of a company, so he usually appeared stern and serious. Although his feelings for the bride were sincere and passionate, he hardly gave her any surprises during their relationship. During their first communication, she confidently told the groom that she would definitely turn their previous relationship, which was steady but lacked romance, into one full of romance and surprises at their wedding. "The groom was half-believing, and actually, I myself was also a bit nervous," recalling this wedding, Chen Chen's mouth was filled with happiness, "I tossed and turned for several nights, finally coming up with a good idea."

"I tampered with their proposal segment, hiring a magician to teach the groom how to perform magic. Unexpectedly, the groom took it very seriously, often coming to our company to practice secretly without letting the bride know. He practiced for hours at a time, practicing for more than two months."

During the wedding, the groom and bride followed the arranged process until the proposal segment. "At that moment, the groom on stage suddenly pulled out a fiery red rose, stunning the bride who couldn't speak from excitement. In an instant, the rose turned into a shining wedding ring." Many guests, including Chen Chen, exclaimed in surprise, and the bride was speechless. When the groom put the ring on her finger, tears streamed down the bride's face.
