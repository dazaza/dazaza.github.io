On May 11, 2009, at the "Leading Brand, Embracing Brand" press conference of the underfloor heating industry, Zhang Yongjun, the standing vice president of the Underfloor Heating Branch of the Liaoning Decoration Association, announced the list of the first batch of construction enterprises to receive credit rating certificates and awarded the underfloor heating construction enterprises with credit rating certificates.

The healthy and orderly development of the underfloor heating industry requires accurate leadership and standardized management by industry organizations. After being passed at the second plenary session of the third meeting of the Liaoning Decoration Association and strictly reviewed by an expert group, 24 enterprises in our province received the first batch of credit rating certificates. Among the underfloor heating construction enterprises in our province, 12 enterprises including Liaoning Huayuan HVAC Engineering Co., Ltd. and Shenyang Fenghuolun Geothermal Engineering Co., Ltd. obtained Grade I credit rating certificates; 11 enterprises including Shenyang Jinfeng HVAC Technology Co., Ltd. obtained Grade II credit rating certificates; Huludao Jinhai Cold and Warm Equipment Installation Co., Ltd. obtained a Grade III credit rating certificate. This signifies that the credit rating management work of underfloor heating construction enterprises in our province has entered a substantive operational phase.

To strengthen the industry management of ground heating construction enterprises in our province, standardize the underfloor heating market order, ensure engineering quality, and protect the legitimate rights and interests of owners and construction enterprises, last June, the Underfloor Heating Industry Association of our province issued the "Interim Measures for the Management of Credit Rating of Ground Heating Construction Enterprises in Liaoning Province." The measures stipulate that the credit rating management of ground heating construction enterprises will be incorporated into the scope of "underfloor heating" construction industry management.

After the introduction of the measures, underfloor heating construction enterprises in various parts of our province quickly responded and actively applied, using this as an opportunity to further standardize their business practices, which played a powerful promoting role in the development of enterprises and the overall image improvement of the industry.

To ensure the seriousness and authority of the credit rating evaluation work, the approval process will be carried out in phases. The industry association requires that the first batch of enterprises receiving credit rating certificates should cherish the obtained qualifications and credibility, strengthen self-discipline, reinforce management, and play a positive leading role in industry development.

It is understood that the second batch of credit rating evaluation work is currently underway, and the list of enterprises receiving certificates will be announced after the evaluation is completed.
