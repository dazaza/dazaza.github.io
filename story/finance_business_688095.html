Contract plan 1491878921 will take effect from the next month after the network entry.

by wzcunzhangr on 2012-03-03 00:33:55

Netease Technology News, February 29: The details of China Telecom's iPhone 4S contract plan have been officially announced. Among them, the "pay for service and get the phone for free" contract price starts at 5780 yuan. The contract price for the 64GB version of the iPhone 4S is 7899 yuan, with a monthly fee threshold of 389 yuan for users who sign up for a 36-month zero-cost phone package; the monthly fee threshold for users who sign up for a 24-month zero-cost phone package is 589 yuan; there is no zero-cost phone option for users who sign up for a 12-month contract. According to the "pay for service and get the phone for free" contract plan, the contract price for the 16GB version of the iPhone 4S is 5780 yuan, with a monthly fee threshold of 289 yuan for users who sign up for a 36-month zero-cost phone package; the monthly fee threshold for users who sign up for a 24-month zero-cost phone package is 389 yuan; the monthly fee threshold for users who sign up for a 12-month zero-cost phone package is 889 yuan. The contract price for the 32GB version of the iPhone 4S is 6899 yuan, with a monthly fee threshold of 289 yuan for users who sign up for a 36-month zero-cost phone package; the monthly fee threshold for users who sign up for a 24-month zero-cost phone package is 489 yuan; the monthly fee threshold for users who sign up for a 12-month zero-cost phone package is 889 yuan. It is understood that the contract plan takes effect from the following month after the network access, but the new network access package takes effect in the same month. The monthly basic fee for the package in the current month is charged per day according to the number of days from the date of network access to the end of the month (including the day of network access). The content included in the package enjoyed in the current month (including voice, SMS, MMS, internet traffic, and WiFi duration) is also calculated per day (rounded to the nearest integer), and the excess part is charged according to the overage fee of the package. (By Chen Min)

According to the "buy phone and get service for free" contract plan, the purchase price is 4999 yuan for the 16GB version, 5999 yuan for the 32GB version, and 6999 yuan for the 64GB version. After purchasing the phone, users can choose any package between 49 yuan and 889 yuan and sign up for a 24-month contract. The returned phone credit is equivalent to 10 times the monthly package fee, ranging from 490 yuan to 8890 yuan.

It is understood that China Telecom has launched two types of contract plans for the iPhone 4S: "pay for service and get the phone for free" and "buy phone and get service for free". The monthly package fee is divided into 11 levels: 49 yuan, 69 yuan, 89 yuan, 129 yuan, 159 yuan, 189 yuan, 289 yuan, 389 yuan, 489 yuan, 589 yuan, and 889 yuan. (Source: Netease Technology Report)
