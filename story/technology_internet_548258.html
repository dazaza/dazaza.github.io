Weighing 4600 jin, the flower steamed bread can feed hundreds of people and will be registered for Guinness World Records

by gotdjijang on 2011-11-11 09:56:39

A flower cake jointly steamed by relevant departments of the county used 2800 jin of flour and 400 jin of red dates, making the total weight of the flower cake reach an astonishing 4600 jin! On February 20, our reporter Qiao Xianjia reported from Jinan that how big is the largest flower cake at the New Year Celebration? If nothing unexpected happens, this mystery will be unveiled in Liangshan County, Jining. A flower cake jointly steamed by relevant departments of the county used 2800 jin of flour and 400 jin of red dates, making the total weight of the flower cake reach an astonishing 4600 jin!

On February 18 (the fifth day of the first lunar month), the "Shandong Hospitality New Year Celebration - Liangshan Heroes Celebrating the New Year" series of activities, "Hu San娘 Martial Arts Marriage," was held at the Liangshan Scenic Area. This 4600-jin large flower cake immediately caught everyone's attention as soon as it appeared. The flower cake is 2010 millimeters high and has 36 layers from bottom to top; the bottom layer has a diameter of 2.4 meters with 108 petals, symbolizing the 108 heroes; the second-to-last layer has 72 petals. On the side of the flower cake, there are inscriptions such as "Shandong Hospitality, Liangshan New Year Celebration" and "Wind and Rain Smooth, Bumper Harvest."

The Liangshan giant flower cake started being steamed on February 9 (the 26th day of the twelfth lunar month). To steam the giant flower cake, a specially made steamer weighing 360 kilograms with a diameter of 2.5 meters and a matching large steamer were used. Finally, two cranes and five vehicles were mobilized to transport it to the venue. A relevant person in charge of the Liangshan County Tourism Bureau stated that apart from participating in the evaluation for the "2010 Shandong Hospitality New Year Celebration Greatest Achievements," they are currently contacting to apply for the Guinness World Records.

In addition, at noon on New Year's Eve, 82 small wooden tables were lined up in a row to form a 101-meter "Long Street Feast" inside the Jiudingta Chinese Folklore Happy Park, where hundreds of tourists sat down to eat simultaneously, creating a lively and enthusiastic atmosphere for celebrating the New Year. Taking advantage of the Spring Festival holiday, Yantai Changyu Wine Culture Museum made a giant wine bottle standing 3 meters tall with a diameter of 0.8 meters. In Binzhou Boxing, traditional skills in making cloth tigers were utilized to create the "largest" cloth tiger standing 1.8 meters tall and 2 meters long... With the help of the Spring Festival holiday, efforts across the province to vie for the "Greatest Achievements of the New Year Celebration" can be described as "firing on all cylinders." (Pictures provided by the Liangshan County Tourism Bureau)
