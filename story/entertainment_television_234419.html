The Birth of the Dark Warrior

Mai Tishuang has always been business-savvy since childhood and deeply understands that only through business can one achieve success. Thus, he diligently studied and completed his MBA at Stanford University in the United States, seeking opportunities abroad. After graduation, Tishuang saw potential in He Wentian’s company and proactively approached him, openly stating that he didn’t want to be an employee but rather a partner, even if it was just for 0.01% of the shares. Impressed by Tishuang’s uniqueness, He Wentian allowed him to join as a partner. Their relationship became both mentorship and friendship, seamlessly cooperating in acquiring companies and then selling them off, becoming renowned snipers in the American business world.

Later, Tishuang became known as the ruthless "Black Warrior" in the business world, showing no mercy. Gradually gaining power, his differences in opinion with Wentian also increased. For the benefit and development of the company, Tishuang decided to fully acquire Wentian’s company. Unable to bear the blow, Wentian drove drunk. Tishuang learned that Wentian had lost control of his car and fallen into the sea. After the incident, Wentian’s body could not be found, but Tishuang firmly believed that Wentian was dead, feeling immense guilt. Is the value of a business deal greater than friendship, or even life? Reflecting on everything, Tishuang resolved to no longer be the Black Warrior and withdrew from the business world.

Tishuang discovered through newspapers that Wentian's wife and child were living in hardship in Hong Kong. Believing the responsibility was his, Tishuang decided to return to Hong Kong to take care of Wentian’s family.

Lin Miaomiao Gets Involved in Debt Collection

Back in Hong Kong, Tishuang looked for Wentian’s wife, Shi Mulian, coincidentally staying at a hotel run by Tang Ji. He met Tang Ji, who was troubled by the hotel's operation issues. Tang Ji is a middle-aged playboy born into a wealthy commercial family, notorious outside but very filial to his father, surrounded by beautiful women.

By chance, Tishuang met Lin Miaomiao at a famous store, and thus their feud began. Miaomiao is a crazy shopping enthusiast, owing a large amount of credit card debt, forcing her to become a debt collector. With a spirit of not charging unless successful, she became the ace of the debt collection company.

Mulian owes Miaomiao's financial company a large sum due to her grandmother Zhou Xiaoqiao being scammed. Miaomiao is responsible for collecting the debt, leading to another encounter with Tishuang. Finding Mulian, Tishuang takes on Mulian's debt, causing Miaomiao to mistakenly believe that Tishuang is Mulian's ex-husband who abandoned his family, creating a poor impression of Tishuang. Mulian is unaware that her husband's death was indirectly caused by Tishuang and continues to believe that her husband Wentian is still alive. Mulian's grandmother often accuses Tishuang of being the murderer of her son Wentian. Initially shocked that Mulian discovered the truth, Tishuang later learns that the grandmother suffers from early-stage dementia, and everyone believes her words are unreliable...
