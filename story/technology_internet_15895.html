The value of youth domain names is recognized by 80% of parents favor CN domain names

by yinyin on 2007-03-30 18:02:29

As the basic resource of the Internet, the CN domain name is the entrance to various Internet applications in our country, and it is the "network identity card" of individuals on the Internet. For young people and children, to integrate into the trend of the Internet, starting from the CN domain name is the only way. This point has reached a consensus among far-sighted parents. In the CNNIC survey on the application status of CN domain names for children in China, up to 77% of people believe that they should start with CN domain names and let children integrate CN domain names at the starting line.
