Sun Li and Deng Chao wed in Shanghai upon having their child; many stars sing romantic songs www.seosoba.com

by hzseosoba on 2011-06-09 19:32:58

Despite the announcement of a ban on taking photos and posting on Weibo before the wedding, it clearly couldn't stop people's curiosity. Deng Chao and Sun Li's wedding was held at 6:30 PM last night at the Ritz-Carlton Hotel in Pudong. When "Happiness Like Flowers" director Gao Xixi asked Sun Li if she would marry Deng Chao as the officiant, Sun Li not only shouted "I do," but also admitted that she was already pregnant. Friends from the entertainment circle such as Han Hong sang songs like "The Moon Represents My Heart" to offer their blessings at the wedding scene.

Groom: The Wedding is the Biggest Gift

Since noon yesterday, media had already been waiting at the hotel where their wedding was being held. Despite being busy preparing for the wedding the day before, Deng Chao still looked spirited yesterday. At 18:18, just before the wedding officially began, Deng Chao in a black suit walked hand-in-hand with Sun Li in a white fish-scale mesh wedding dress to greet the media at the background board in the hotel lobby.

Deng Chao kept thanking the media for their hard work while protecting his bride. When asked what gifts and surprises he prepared for the bride today, he smiled and replied: "The wedding is the biggest gift." Sun Li affectionately leaned on Deng Chao, creating a warm scene. The media shouted for them to kiss, and the two complied with a passionate kiss.

Bride: Be Good to "Us"

Guests attending the wedding banquet live-streamed the moment when the couple exchanged rings on Weibo yesterday. The music played at the scene was the theme song "Love Like Air" from the TV series "Happiness Like Flowers" starring Sun Li and Deng Chao. Meanwhile, the big screen in the banquet hall reviewed the moments they shared over the years. Facing all this, Sun Li cried uncontrollably. Finally, when Gao Xixi asked Sun Li if she was willing to marry Deng Chao, Sun Li shouted: "I do."

An excited bride also admitted that she was pregnant. During their vows, Sun Li teased Deng Chao: "Be good to me! Oh, now there are two of us here, be good to us!" And Deng Chao made a commitment to his wife and child: "I will!"

Officiant・Host: The Newlyweds Cried Three Times Out of Joy

Around 7:30 PM last night, a red-faced Gao Xixi walked out of the banquet hall accompanied by his assistant. He told reporters that he was the officiant for Deng Chao and Sun Li, "Wishing this new couple a harmonious life." He also revealed that the wedding was touching, and both Deng Chao and Sun Li cried, almost making him cry too. Interestingly, despite the strict security, he happily said: "Go up and take a look, there are many empty seats upstairs, consider it my treat."

As the host, Cao Kefan also revealed that he cried three times. The first time was when the newlyweds appeared, Sun Li walked down the 36-meter T-stage towards Deng Chao at the end of the stage, "A happy Sun Li almost cried all the way." Another time was when the couple exchanged rings, Deng Chao said he would cherish and protect her, not only did Sun Li cry until her eyes were red, but even the guests' eyes turned red.

Gifts from Guests: Creativity Wins

Yesterday afternoon, guests began arriving one after another. When the reporter arrived at the hotel at 1 PM, stars had already started arriving. It was reported that the first star to arrive at the hotel was Hu Haiquan. And when the reporter arrived, coincidentally, Li Chen was just reaching the hotel. Due to strict security, without staff guidance, even stars couldn't enter, so Li Chen dressed in trendy leather attire waited in the hall. While blessing Deng Chao and Sun Li with happiness, Li Chen revealed that the gift he prepared was a specialty brought from Beijing.

Cao Kefan, who served as the host for the wedding, presented a couplet to the newlyweds on-site: "Beautiful woman marries, transcending the ordinary," with the horizontal scroll reading "Happiness Like Flowers," which was rated as the most creative gift. He said this was because this golden couple was truly "Super Beautiful." Mrs. Wong and her husband said they prepared a big red envelope, and some guests revealed that they gave a red envelope containing 9999 yuan.

Guest Performances: A Wedding Concert, Han Hong Recognizes a Godmother

Recently married Sha Yi was asked how this wedding differed from his own, laughing heartily, "Equal shares." Shang Wenjie said she would sing a song at the wedding. Even the ice skating couple Shen Xue and Zhao Hongbo attended the wedding yesterday. Zhao Hongbo revealed that they specially came to Shanghai from elsewhere.

Yesterday, through the safety channel, the reporter sneaked into the wedding kitchen and saw that the dishes served that day were not luxurious, including home-style dishes like oil-burst shrimp. Many guests sang at the wedding, from the kitchen you could hear Han Hong singing "The Moon Represents My Heart," and she recognized Sun Li's mother as her godmother.

Not Enough Red Envelopes Trigger Media Scramble

At 6:30 PM, Deng Chao specially arranged for staff to give red envelopes and gifts to the media, but perhaps due to not considering that it was the Shanghai Television Festival, many out-of-town media also arrived at the wedding site, resulting in insufficient red envelopes, causing a chaotic scene, and even verbal disputes among some people. The red envelopes printed with Deng Chao and Sun Li's English pinyin contained 500 yuan each. The gifts were cosmetics endorsed by Sun Li (AUPRES) and a box of chocolate from Godiva.

Regarding the live streaming on Weibo, Cao Kefan believed that guests should respect the new couple's agreement. He said he was invited six months ago but didn't tell anyone. However, he also frankly admitted that in today's Weibo era, being followed is hard to avoid, and as long as it's not malicious, he hoped the new couple wouldn't mind too much.
