How to book international discount flight tickets online

by anonymous on 2012-03-05 14:09:08

E-commerce is becoming increasingly developed, and the majority of people who travel abroad now choose to book their flight tickets online. So how do you book international discounted tickets online? What is the process for booking tickets online? How do you book international flight tickets online? This article will provide you with a satisfactory answer based on actual circumstances.

There are generally two situations when booking international flight tickets online:

1. Search for ticket information online, find suitable prices, then call the ticketing office for detailed inquiries (suitable for international flights, especially complex international routes).

2. Go to large flight ticket websites like Ctrip, Elong, Boyou Travel Network, etc., register as a member, search for tickets, pay for reservations, and complete the payment (this situation is suitable for domestic flights, point-to-point prices are timely and accurate).

Below is a detailed explanation of the first situation's ticket booking process:

1. After searching online for suitable ticket prices, call the staff (due to the larger fluctuations in international ticket prices compared to domestic tickets, online prices may not be updated in time and are only for reference).

2. Report your desired itinerary, date, number of passengers, and wait for the reservation agent to check for you.

3. The reservation agent will promptly report the lowest discount available for the day's flights, allowing you to choose according to your needs.

4. Once you have selected a flight, you need to inform the passenger's name, ID number, or other valid documents, ensuring accuracy as incorrect details can prevent boarding. Provide your mobile phone number so the reservation agent can send a text message to verify the passenger's information.

5. Finally, inform the delivery address, delivery time, and payment method. A ticket is thus reserved. If you book late in the day, the ticket might not arrive on the same day. To ensure your discounted seat, use Alipay or a guarantee to secure the ticket today for delivery tomorrow. If you are an existing customer, these issues are waived!

Below is an explanation of the second online ticket booking process:

**Step 1: Ticket Inquiry**

- **Step 1.1**: User Login: Use your username and password to log into large or small ticketing websites such as Ctrip, Elong, Boyou Travel Network, etc.

- **Step 1.2**: Enter the inquiry page by clicking on the ticket booking channel.

- **Step 1.3**: Choose your trip type: one-way, round-trip, multi-city (including connecting flights), e.g., Shanghai-Seoul tickets.

- **Step 1.4**: Input departure location, destination, departure date, and departure time (mandatory).

- **Step 1.5**: Other query conditions: airline, cabin class, ticket delivery city, aircraft type (optional).

- **Step 1.6**: View the list: If there are tickets that meet your needs, browse the ticket information and prepare to start the booking process. If not, change the query conditions and re-search for tickets that fit your criteria.

**Step 2: Ticket Booking**

- **Step 2.1**: Confirm ticket information: Click on the queried ticket, review the ticket introduction information, confirm again, and click "book."

- **Step 2.2**: Fill in passenger information: Passenger type, passenger information, contact person information, etc.

- **Step 2.3**: Fill in ticket issuance method: If choosing an electronic ticket, no additional issuance information is required. If choosing delivery, fill in detailed delivery or pick-up information.

- **Step 2.4**: Select payment method.

- **Step 2.5**: Select confirmation method and submit.

- **Step 2.6**: Confirm order information: Carefully review the order information to ensure successful booking. If confirmed correctly, submit the order.

- **Step 2.7**: Read the order submission information and remember the order number. Wait for the booking to be completed.
