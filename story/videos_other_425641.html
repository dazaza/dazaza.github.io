DLSD Series Ultra-fine High Gradient Magnetic Separator

DLSD Series Ultra-fine High Gradient Wet Magnetic Separator is independently developed by our company after combining the features of strong magnetic separators at home and abroad and the advantages of our standing ring high gradient magnetic separator. This is a new type of strong magnetic separator used for iron removal in non-metallic minerals (this series of products has obtained national patent, patent number: ZL200920065387.3). This series of products is currently the best and most advanced strong magnetic iron-removal equipment both domestically and internationally. It adapts well to fluctuations in feed particle size, concentration, and grade, operates reliably, and is easy to operate and maintain. It is currently the most ideal non-metallic iron-removal equipment.

Application Scope of Ultra-fine High Gradient Magnetic Separator

Iron removal and purification of non-metallic minerals. For example: feldspar, quartz, nepheline, fluorspar, sillimanite, spodumene, kaolin, etc.

Working Principle of Ultra-fine High Gradient Magnetic Separator

This machine generates a magnetic field by passing direct current through electromagnetic coils. The magnetic flux lines pass through the magnetic medium inside the chamber to produce a high gradient magnetic field. When slurry containing magnetic substances flows from bottom to top over the magnetic medium, magnetic particles are adsorbed onto the surface of the magnetic medium by its powerful magnetic field. Then, through PLC program control of various valves and electrical components' automatic operation, it achieves the purpose of automatic iron removal and iron shedding.

Main Features of Ultra-fine High Gradient Magnetic Separator

1. High background field strength, with the medium surface field strength reaching up to 31800 Gauss.

2. The raw ore enters the magnetic medium from bottom to top, and the pressurized flushing water flushes the tailings from top to bottom. Yueyang Dalishen. The DLSD series magnetic separator uses pressurized water to flush the tailings, with the flushing direction opposite to the feeding direction, making it easy to flush out coarse particles, effectively preventing the blockage of the magnetic medium and solving the technical problem of easy blockage of magnetic media in other strong magnetic separators.

3. Other high gradient magnetic separators at home and abroad have relatively strict requirements on the feed particle size. We have researched a unique magnetic system structure and optimized combination of magnetic media (the magnetic medium box has obtained national patent, patent number: ZL200920306697.X), allowing the upper limit of the feed particle size for the DLSD series magnetic separator to reach 20 mesh, simplifying the on-site grading operation, giving it broader adaptability.
