16-Year-Old Milk Delivery Worker Dies After Being Beaten by Father-and-Son Owners of Dairy Station for Smashing a Crate of Milk

by king2z9o on 2012-02-24 11:36:30

After the two defendants were taken away from the court, the parents of the victim still could not suppress their grief and wanted to confront the defendants. (Morning Post, Hao Xiaotian, photo)

A 16-year-old boy named Xiao Yu (a pseudonym) accidentally knocked over a box of milk during his delivery and had an argument with the milk station owner, Zhang Chengzhi. Later, Zhang Chengzhi and his son, Zhang Sheng, fought with Xiao Yu. During the fight, Zhang Sheng picked up an empty beer bottle and hit Xiao Yu on the head, causing his death. Yesterday, the Intermediate People's Court of Guangzhou City tried this case. The Zhang father and son were tried together. The mother of the deceased cried and said, "I demand the execution of the murderer!"

Carrying a portrait of her deceased son to court, she angrily cursed the murderer.

Yesterday morning, Xiao Yu's parents came from their home in Hebei Province. Before the trial, Xiao Yu's mother, wearing a red headscarf, walked into the courtroom with the support of her husband. She tightly held her son's portrait and couldn't help but shed tears. The judge advised them to hand over the portrait to relatives, but Xiao Yu's mother wouldn't let go of the photo. Xiao Yu's father said, "You don't know how we have lived these days." Under the persuasion of family members, they finally handed over the portrait to relatives in the audience. In the photo, Xiao Yu was smiling.

At half past ten, the defendants, Zhang Sheng and Zhang Chengzhi, were escorted into the courtroom. Upon seeing the murderers of her son, Xiao Yu's mother stood up and angrily cursed, "Even if you were cut a thousand times, it wouldn't be enough! Give me back my son's life!"

Hitting the head with a beer bottle caused death.

According to the prosecution of Guangzhou's first branch, at 10 PM on September 3, 2011, in the west side of the vegetable market courtyard of the Guangzhou Wireless Electric Plant dormitory in Haidian District's North Wa Road milk station, during a mutual beating over trivial matters, Zhang Chengzhi punched Xiao Yu in the face, and Zhang Sheng hit Xiao Yu on the head with an empty beer bottle, causing him to die from severe brain injuries.

During the trial, defendant Zhang Chengzhi recounted that on the night of the incident, Xiao Yu and his colleague delivered milk to the milk station. While unloading, Xiao Yu broke a box of milk. Xiao Yu said he would compensate, but Zhang Chengzhi told him, "It's not about the money. According to company rules, if one customer doesn't receive milk, 50 yuan will be deducted." This led to an argument, and they started hitting each other.

"He (referring to Xiao Yu) was the first to lay hands," Zhang Chengzhi's son, Zhang Sheng, said. He saw the victim kick his father and chase him with an iron rod. The victim also grabbed a beer bottle to hit his father. In anger, he picked up a beer bottle and smashed it on Xiao Yu's head. However, Zhang Chengzhi said, "I hit the victim, but I didn't see my son lay a hand."

The San Yuan Company is also a defendant.

Xiao Yu's parents filed a civil claim for compensation totaling 1.34 million yuan against the Zhang father and son, including 300,000 yuan for mental distress compensation. They also listed Guangzhou San Yuan Food Co., Ltd. and another scientific and trade company as defendants, demanding that both companies bear joint liability for compensation.

The representative lawyer for the family of the deceased, Mr. Duan, introduced that the Zhang father and son usually represented Guangzhou San Yuan Food Co., Ltd. (referred to as San Yuan Company) for door-to-door milk delivery. The dispute arose due to issues regarding milk loss compensation during work hours and in the workplace, which falls under official duties, so San Yuan Company should bear the compensation responsibility.

However, the agent for San Yuan Company stated that there was no substantial employment relationship between the Zhang father and son and the company, thus the company is not an appropriate defendant in this case. Another scientific and trade company did not express much opinion on the compensation request from the family of the deceased. "Xiao Yu was our only child," said Xiao Yu's mother. She and her husband might not want the money, but she demanded the execution of the murderer. Xiao Yu's father shouted, "My son died too unjustly!"

Emotional disturbance of the deceased's parents

The prosecutor handling the case believed that defendants Zhang Sheng and Zhang Chengzhi ignored national laws, jointly and intentionally harmed others' bodies, leading to death. They should be pursued for criminal responsibility for intentional harm. Considering Zhang Sheng's voluntary surrender, he can be legally given a lighter punishment. The prosecutor suggested sentencing Zhang Sheng to life imprisonment and Zhang Chengzhi to 6 to 9 years in prison.

In the final part of the trial, the Zhang father and son both said, "We are willing to compensate." When the court officers prepared to take the Zhang father and son out of the courtroom, Xiao Yu's parents suddenly rushed out from the defendant's bench. They swung their fists towards Zhang Sheng and Zhang Chengzhi and were eventually stopped by the court officers. The verdict was not announced in court.
