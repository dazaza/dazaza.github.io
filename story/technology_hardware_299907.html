Suzhou New Height Metal Equipment Manufacturing Co., Ltd. (formerly Suzhou SuXin Rare Metal Equipment Co., Ltd.) specializes in the production of titanium equipment, titanium heat exchangers, titanium reactors, and titanium coils. Our company is a manufacturing factory that specializes in producing various non-standard titanium material devices. Founded in 1989, we are a professional production enterprise mainly engaged in the production of titanium equipment, composite titanium equipment, and hardware machinery processing. Located in Suzhou, known as the "Paradise on Earth," under the care of friends from all walks of life and after years of hard work, we now present ourselves with a brand-new appearance.

We have strong technical strength, complete supporting facilities, perfect testing methods, a strict quality assurance system, and first-class management levels. The factory covers an area of 7200 square meters, with a workshop area of 3500 square meters and office space of 1300 square meters. In 2002, we passed the ISO9001:2000 quality system certification.

Our main products can be divided into three categories:

【One】: (Titanium) chemical equipment, (composite titanium) chemical equipment, and (zirconium) chemical equipment - design and manufacture.

【Two】: Production of various non-standard (titanium) hardware mechanical processing parts.

【Three】: Operation and sales of titanium materials (plates, tubes, rods, wires), marketing of metal composite materials such as titanium-copper composite rods and titanium composite plates, titanium standard parts, elbows, flanges, and other titanium pipeline products.

We manufacture various specifications of titanium equipment including: titanium equipment; titanium heat exchangers, titanium reactors, titanium towers, titanium storage tanks, titanium heat exchangers, titanium coils, titanium condensers, titanium seed plates, titanium baskets, and other non-standard equipment and titanium non-standard machined parts. Our products are widely used in the chemical industry, pharmaceutical industry, metallurgical industry, chlor-alkali industry, petroleum industry, power industry, environmental protection industry, and seawater desalination electroplating fields. Our customers cover more than twenty provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, and our products have received excellent reviews from users! At the same time, we are also actively committed to the development and promotion of new products (various non-standard titanium equipment can be designed and manufactured according to customer requirements).

"New Height's" management policy: "Quality is the accumulation of bit-by-bit experience; zero defects are an endless pursuit."

"New Height's" business policy: "Quality first, customer foremost."

"New Height Titanium" and "Quality New Height" represent our corporate development philosophy; "Credit and Quality" equals "New Height Titanium."
