Victoria Beckham

After spending Christmas in the UK, she hurried back to L.A. Her star clothing coordination is still so eye-catching. The choice of glossy fabric blazers and tight pants are enough to attract attention, reflecting Victoria's mature personality. In the dead of winter, fashionistas can't do without long scarves, which serve both decorative and warming purposes effectively. Worth recommending are waterproof platform super thick-soled high heels, which can make legs look longer while saving effort. Victoria is undoubtedly smart.

"Winter has come, can spring be far behind?" For this couple, spring is just around the corner. For their trip to Mexico, their clothing coordination is full of spring vitality. A plain low-cut V-neck mini skirt might seem a bit simple, but adding a military green jacket lets the spring feeling flow out.

Molly Sims

"Words reflect the person," or you could say "clothes reflect the person." Non-mainstream clothing, Molly pairs with many black elements - wool fur jackets, bags, boots, and sunglasses all reflect her tough personality. And the low-cut shirt shows her softer side.
