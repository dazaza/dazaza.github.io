Related thematic articles: Changsha hotel, Kaifeng hotel, Tongcheng website hotel reservation. Every year, the most important thing is to make plans to take the baby out for a walk. The happiest thing is that our baby grows up every day and we can go around with my husband. After several days of brewing, we finally decided to go to Tianjin. In the morning, after having breakfast, we leisurely took the subway to the train station. There were so many trains, and it was very convenient to get on the train. It felt like we just got on the train and arrived in Tianjin. The weather was pretty good and not too hot. We took a taxi to the Ancient Culture Street first. My daughter began to be less excited and insisted on buying toys. After being beaten, she became obedient. After buying a sugar figure, she calmed down and stopped making trouble. Holding the sugar figure, looking at the clay figurines by Zhang, touching the stone lions, when seeing the tank, my daughter was scared. Our baby really has a strong sense of security. The confused father actually lost the camera when getting off the car, but fortunately, the enthusiastic people of Tianjin picked it up and returned it to us, which made us very grateful. When taking a taxi, the baby said: Dad, be careful this time not to lose anything again.

Tianjin hotel     The sincerity of Tianjin people is fully displayed. As long as you ask for directions, the uncles will kindly point them out clearly, much more enthusiastic than the people of Shanghai. The baby wants to ride the Ferris wheel. Along the way, there are young people who give up their seats, and warm-hearted aunts. We also visited Dabeichan Temple for a while. My daughter seriously took three sticks of incense and burned them. Unfortunately, I wore a skirt and couldn't kneel down to worship. There were actually many young people there. After riding the Ferris wheel, my daughter wasn't afraid at all and climbed around calmly, shouting that Superman came and wanted to open the door. Chuckle, Tianjin has changed a lot and has a bit of a big city feel compared to a few years ago. At noon, we bought some Yonghe food and got on the train. After 30 hours, we arrived at the South Station and took the subway home, had a nice sleep, and ended our Tianjin trip. Amitabha Buddha, bless that my daughter doesn't get sick, otherwise, mother-in-law would blame me! Love the baby, and take the baby to Sichuan every year to hang out! A wonderful and happy time.

Tianjin hotel     Arrived at the Tianjin hotel and had a sound sleep. Strolling to the Five Avenues, there were big copper carriages and real horse-drawn carriages. On the horse-drawn carriage, facing the gentle breeze, the feeling along the street was quite pleasant. With the breeze, various small western-style buildings, the simplicity and honesty of Tianjin people always make you feel so harmonious. The funniest thing is that the horse stopped halfway through the carriage ride and let out a few farts and some droppings, which made my daughter laugh a lot. She kept saying there was the smell of horses wherever she went. Walking all the way to Go Believe Restaurant, the restaurant was really luxurious but the service was not that good. My daughter actually ate four buns and a bowl of rice porridge. After returning to the hotel, we were very tired and went to sleep. In her daze, my daughter fell off the bed twice but could crawl back onto the bed herself. In the morning, we hurried to eat a pancake roll. Breakfast in Tianjin is still simple, and we couldn't find stalls for rice porridge and fried dough sticks, only pancake rolls. I don't know why there were so few restaurants where we stayed, and fewer people eating. The streets were very tidy, and there weren't so many street vendors, which is better than Beijing.
