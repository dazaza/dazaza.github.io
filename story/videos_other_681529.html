The specific case reports are as follows:

1. Agricultural, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Land Use Planning Objectives and Policies:

The area appoints agricultural economic development targets. The district government has always adhered to promoting farmer income as the core, taking structural adjustment as the masterstroke, implementing agricultural industrialization energetically, paying close attention to conducting agricultural projects, focusing on structural adjustment such as the project of returning farmland to forest and animal husbandry strong area 219. This makes the production of our area agriculture present a unique characteristic of urban agriculture, and agriculture and rural economy present a good momentum of development.

At the same time, as all levels of party committees and governments in our area increase investment in agricultural infrastructure improvements, especially in water conservancy infrastructure perfection, farmers' production living conditions will be further improved, which will promote the development of the rural economy. In recent years, all levels of party committees and governments have placed improving the ecological environment in an increasingly important position, through projects such as returning farmland to forest and natural forest protection, making soil and water loss and natural disasters effectively controlled, thus promoting the region's economy to develop fast, healthy, and sustainably. Looking to the future, forestry and ecological environment construction will be strengthened.

Looking to the future, our area's agriculture total production value will grow by 6% annually, animal husbandry output value will account for more than 50% of the total agricultural output value, while animal husbandry increase value will maintain an annual rate of 9%. The 512 project is advancing comprehensively, water conservancy infrastructure will be fundamentally improved, forestry and ecological environment construction strength will be increased further, which will promote our area's rural economic and social development.
