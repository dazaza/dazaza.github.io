CDM cement plant pure low-temperature waste heat power generation CO2 emission allowances can be sold internationally.

by lklkkgfeg5 on 2012-02-07 08:41:40

,news.football-jersey.net cdm cement plant pure low temperature waste heat power generation co2 emissions can be sold internationally - enterprise dynamics - industry news - building materials network According to the clean development mechanism (CDM) based on market economy principles specified in the Kyoto Protocol, the electricity recovered by the pure low-temperature waste heat power generation device of the cement plant PC kiln actually reduces the C02 emissions generated by the coal-fired power plants in the power grid due to the need to generate these amounts of electricity. Therefore, these C02 emission reductions can be sold in international carbon emission trading. Calculated according to the current international market price, each ton of C02 is about 7.5 US dollars. The C02 emission reduction of a 5000 t/d PC kiln with low-temperature waste heat power generation is approximately between 35,000 to 40,000 tons per year, varying according to the recovery efficiency of the waste heat power generation device, which is equivalent to an annual increase in revenue for enterprises of 250,000 to 300,000 US dollars. Moreover, the shortest term for CDM sales contracts is 10 years, and the sale of C02 emission reduction quotas alone can roughly offset the investment costs of the waste heat power generation device. For this reason, we must first follow the CDM rules and apply for approval procedures according to many strict and complex domestic and international processes. At the same time, we must also find international buyers for CDM. It's not difficult to find international buyers at this stage. However, one point must be noted: CDM projects must be involved in the early stages of waste heat power generation engineering, the earlier the better, that is, when compiling the feasibility study report of waste heat power generation, it should include the content of applying for the sale of CO2 emission reduction quotas. The approval documents from central or provincial/municipal authorities for this feasibility study report should also include the approval opinions on CDM projects, which will facilitate the further implementation of CDM projects. Because according to CDM rules, once the waste heat power generation project has been completed or has started power generation debugging, it will be very difficult to get approval for the proposed sale of CO2 emission reduction quotas. Therefore, relevant cement enterprises must make early preparations and must proceed simultaneously with the waste heat power generation project. Nowadays, the international carbon emission trading market is quite interested in the C02 emission reduction trading of pure low-temperature waste heat power generation of China's cement enterprises, because its trading scope is clear, measurement is simple and accurate, and it is easy to check and implement. If it is a cement (group) company, with several PC kiln production lines in different regions, they can be bundled together to apply for CDM projects and implemented in phases, which will be more welcomed by international buyers. Generally speaking, CDM projects require clinker production line capacity of at least 2500 t/d, smaller capacity makes it hard to find buyers, directly affecting the selling price and reducing benefits. The Chinese government agency responsible for CDM projects is the National Climate Change Response Coordination Group, which was approved by the State Council in October 2003. The office of the group is located in the Department of Regional Economy of the National Development and Reform Commission, responsible for executing or approving the execution of domestic and international cooperation of CDM projects. The Development and Reform Commissions of provincial and municipal governments also have corresponding institutions to guide and execute this work. In addition, some domestic and foreign intermediary agencies can also undertake CDM project business.
