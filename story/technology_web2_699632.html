People who read this article will understand why it takes a lot of time to create an original promotional piece. Is it merely for the joy of helping others? Apparently, it's still for advertising. However, the reason why soft articles are called soft articles is that they must be distinct from hard advertisements; they need to be subtle. The advertisement should be hidden, and when mentioning a certain viewpoint, it should be naturally introduced, just passing by, making people unaware that it is an advertisement. Even if one realizes it later, there won't be any resentment. If I were to promote a product like "Beijing SEO Optimization Service," I could first talk about the internet, then about the SEO industry, and suddenly mention places related to Beijing SEO optimization services. Clever readers wouldn't find it difficult to discover that what they're actually seeing is a soft article about Beijing SEO optimization services. It all depends on how you learn from it. In fact, as long as you put your heart into it, it's not difficult.

1. How to collect materials

1. Treasure hunting on professional websites.

For those who don't know where to start writing soft articles, it is suggested to go to large and authoritative professional websites to carefully read the articles published by others. The quality of the articles that can be published on these websites is generally good. This not only accumulates writing materials but also allows one to learn many practical SEO optimization skills and knowledge, which can be applied to their own work. For example, in terms of SEO optimization, A5 station is a relatively popular website in the A5 SEO circle, and Beijing SEO often publishes articles on it. At the beginning, I didn't know what kind of articles they wanted, so I inserted a lot of advertisements for Huaxia Mold Network in my articles, rarely proposing truly useful viewpoints. Basically, I wrote some praising words about Huaxia Mold Network. When publishing, the more I submitted, the more got deleted, with a very low pass rate. Although it only has four editors, they receive a large number of submissions every day, and all articles are manually reviewed strictly. They have high requirements for article quality, which might be the key to establishing the authority of this website. Frequently reading articles on such websites can help one learn a lot of practical things and accumulate SEO optimization skills and techniques, which will greatly assist one's writing. Once you have a certain level of professional competence, how can you not have materials to write about?

2. Gold mining in industry blogs.

According to Beijing SEO, there are many blogs that haven't been indexed yet, but they still contain many valuable articles worth reading. A faster way is to input keywords in Youdao and search for blogs, which will bring up many industry-related blog posts. For example, if I want to publish an article about SEO news on an SEO optimization website to promote Beijing SEO optimization, I just need to input "SEO" and click on "Search Blogs," and there will be a lot of SEO blogs waiting for you to read. At this point, you can learn some knowledge from them. If you're doing Baidu promotion, many of the blogs found on Youdao haven't been indexed by Baidu, so you can extract some information from them as your own, saving time and being recognized by Baidu. This isn't encouraging cheating; it's a working technique. After all, working time is limited, and we can't spend a lot of time on unnecessary things.

3. Refining from work practice.

In fact, many professional writers have become masters through slowly accumulating experience from being novices. In daily work, one can discover many small details. Even now, the vast amount of data seen by Beijing SEO was built up step by step from small codes. In practice, one shouldn't overlook any detail. Many times, one can discover some tricks that others haven't yet discovered. Whenever there's even a small discovery, it can be immediately recorded and accumulated. Next time when writing an article, one can take out one of these points to share with everyone. Even if the whole article is meaningless, at least one useful viewpoint has been proposed. Such articles will still be considered by the editor during review.

2. How to write soft articles

1. Eye-catching title

An eye-catching title is like a person's face. A good-looking face will capture attention instantly, regardless of body shape, making people look twice. Titles can be suspenseful or explosive, as long as they are attractive. Don't underestimate these titles of just ten or so characters; there is a lot of learning involved.

2. Practical content

The first value of a soft article is sharing with others, followed by achieving advertising purposes. Nowadays, human time is very limited. It's impossible to waste time reading an article full of advertisements. One must first let the reader learn something from the article, and it must be real and effective techniques. Only then can one guide the reader to further reading and make them want to know more about the author. That way, there will be an opportunity to promote the product. People who know how to share are easily accepted by others. Remember this: Readers come first, advertisements come second.
