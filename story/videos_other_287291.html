The story of car washing - Oulai Li Jie

by bgvftybj4u on 2009-12-01 10:21:00

Yesterday was rainy all day. This morning, wow! The sun is out! I opened the window and the fresh air made me feel very comfortable. After washing up and looking at the clock, it was not even eight o'clock yet, so I decided to go wash my car. You have to go early, otherwise if you wait until the afternoon, you will definitely have to wait in a long line. I got a car wash card from an auto repair shop near my neighborhood. Usually, besides washing my car, when there are small problems with my car, I also go there for help, so after coming back and forth, I've gotten familiar with the mechanics and young workers there. Especially Master Nan, last year he completely cured my car's "asthma". Speaking of my car, I am still a little upset. Last year, for quite a while, whenever I encountered traffic or stopped at a red light, the idling speed would fluctuate and make a "woo h woo h" sound, which sounded like gasping for breath. But after Master Nan repeatedly used instruments to check and repair it, it was surprisingly completely "cured". Here, the car wash is in a very large and modern car wash room. Since it was early, there were only two cars ahead of me that had just finished washing and were waiting for the final cleaning and floor mat suction procedures. The car wash room was empty. According to the requirements of the guide, I retracted the antenna, shifted into neutral, released the handbrake, and the left front wheel slowly moved forward on the track. In the blink of an eye, water sprayed from both sides and above, soap water splashed, and instantly everything outside was invisible. I suddenly had a thought, if there was an automatic shower room for people to take a bath, wouldn't that be interesting? But people who are ticklish should avoid it, and how could my long hair be washed automatically? Anyway, I shouldn't let my imagination run wild anymore. Haha, soon the car was washed. Seeing that it was still early, I decided to use the free waxing service that came with the card. Xiao Qi, the young man responsible for car beauty care, took charge of the waxing. He skillfully wiped the surface of the car again with a dry cloth, picked up a thin sponge dipped in wax, and started circling on the outer paint. The white wax turned into neat white circles under Xiao Qi's hands, looking very beautiful against the green metallic paint. At this moment, Xiao Qi asked me: "Sister, your car's right side door (surface) isn't smooth, has it been repainted?" Hey, this young man has a good sense of touch! That was a problem left over from being hit by a motorcycle taxi N years ago. When I heard Xiao Qi speaking with an accent, I asked him: where is your hometown? Xiao Qi didn't answer, he just kept his head down and worked on his task. Then Master Nan joked beside him: "This is the only good person left from Henan!" Xiao Qi spoke up: "What's wrong with Henan people? I am from Henan!" I suddenly remembered a book called 'Who Did the People of Henan Offend?' So I semi-seriously said to Master Nan: "You can't have regional discrimination! Look, you've driven the honest person to desperation!" Actually, don't look at Xiao Qi as being quiet, but every time he speaks to me, it's about how the car should be maintained. A smart, studious young man, at least in my eyes, Henan native Xiao Qi is excellent! My mood was great in the morning, after thanking Xiao Qi and Master Nan, I need to hurry home and get online to meet my blog friends!
