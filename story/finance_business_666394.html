Polo shirts from Ralph Lauren can also be used as formal wear.

by filedyuiy7 on 2012-02-15 17:21:38

The strictness of formal wear in the office is nowadays much less, with men's polo vests being a popular choice, and many offices allowing their employees to wear semi-informal attire as long as they are properly dressed. This multi-purpose role is often fulfilled by polo shirts. Round neck t-shirts have an entirely informal look which is not suitable for the work environment. Wearing a suit and tie all day is not only stiffening but also quite uncomfortable. Keeping these factors in mind, brands like Ralph Lauren and Lacoste, among many others, have created a separate range of polo shirts for men. Ralph Lauren is one of the largest manufacturers of these shirts globally and is known for their sporty appearance. These shirts were initially produced in the late 19th century as sports gear to ensure polo players felt comfortable while playing. Over time, these clothes came to be used for other sports as well, such as golf and cricket.

Polo shirts offer excellent quality for outdoor work. I am 6'2" and wish the shirt was just a little longer for better tucked-in coverage, but it performs quite well considering it is not a "tall" size. The color holds up well to washing. The thicker material is good for all but the warmest summer days. The cut and material of these shirts make them appropriate for sports and outdoor activities.

However, the popularity of these clothes increased, and regular non-sports people started wearing them too. Until very recently, these shirts were not considered appropriate for the office, but the increasing liberality in office dress codes has helped facilitate the entry of polo shirts into the workplace as well.

Offices that have dress codes also have "casual dress" days, and on these days, employees can let go of their strictly formal attire and wear something more relaxed. But wearing round neck t-shirts would not be proper even on such days. Thus, polo shirts are the only sensible choice. The uniformity in color and material of these garments makes them ideal for semi-formal occasions.

Polo shirts are also a popular choice for uniforms in various stores and fast food joints. The comfort level in them is much higher than in complete formal shirts or suits. The breathable material of these t-shirts lets the wearer feel comfortable even when working in very high temperatures, such as in fast food joints or all day outdoors on a golf course or at outdoor restaurants. These are all formal workplaces that allow regular use of polo shirts for men.

Ralph Lauren is one of the leading brands in the world of fashion, and Ralph Lauren polo shirts make great formal attire for both men and women. Women find these shirts very comfortable and apt to pair with any style they desire. For men, RL t-shirts mean not just comfort but also style. The versatility of the RL collection and the colors in which they come—like orange, crème, red, white, black, navy blue, dark green, and white among others—make them a regular choice for semi-formal as well as formal attire. Ralph Lauren polo shirts are also a great option for small occasions like a date, a luncheon with colleagues, family gatherings, or even friend hangouts.

For men, formal wear can become a tedious monotony after a point in time. But with RL polo shirts, they can break away from this monotony while combining style with professionalism.
