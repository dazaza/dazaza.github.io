Website secretaries take a different path, online customer service proactively markets

by huangyi on 2007-06-08 11:37:13

According to statistics, 75% of small and medium-sized enterprises in our country have extremely low daily traffic on their corporate websites. In contrast to the relatively low traffic, another phenomenon is even more worrying: the role of the website in uncovering potential customers is quite limited. At this point, some online customer service systems that can directly engage in instant two-way communication with visitors are proving to be powerful tools for attracting and retaining customers.

Currently, many companies are keen on promoting their brands and conducting online marketing activities through methods such as registering on search engines and purchasing generic web names. These promotional measures indeed bring significant traffic to corporate websites. However, online marketing is a one-on-one, two-way interactive communication method. This approach is non-coercive and consumer-led, so for businesses, converting website visitors into users is also a crucial task. Corporate website online customer service systems can effectively attract and retain them by directly communicating with visitors, ultimately converting them into users and maximizing the benefits of online marketing.

An online customer service system is a web-based instant messaging tool that generally does not require any software installation. Real-time conversations can be conducted simply within a browser window. As a customer service and proactive marketing tool for corporate websites, the online customer service system has functions related to proactive marketing, customer support, and customer relationship management. It allows businesses to provide personalized services to each visitor of the webpage, achieving the marketing goal of turning visitors into customers. One of the more well-known online customer service systems in the industry is Nicenic's Website Secretary.

Some users report that Nicenic's (www.nicenic.com) Website Secretary is very powerful. Without needing to install any software, it enables instant online communication and voice conversations with clients. It allows companies to always know which visitors are on their site, understand the pages these visitors are interested in, actively invite clients to chat, and respond promptly to client inquiries. This helps uncover more potential customers, capture fleeting business opportunities, effectively reduce operating costs, improve work efficiency, obtain user consultations and feedback information, and enhance customer satisfaction. This online customer service system has already created tangible benefits for many companies.

Another survey indicates that over half of customers state they are unlikely to purchase again from websites with sluggish online service responses. Poor online customer service not only jeopardizes a company’s online business but also devalues the company's brand in the eyes of its most important customers. Therefore, choosing a good online customer service system can create maximum direct value for a company and effectively enhance its brand image.

Experts believe that in today's world where online marketing techniques are constantly emerging, the key lies in genuinely retaining website visitors. Uncovering potential users and maintaining existing customers can help achieve the ultimate goal of online marketing. The construction of online customer service systems should draw the attention of companies.
