Fan Bingbing's latest bizarre on-set photos are revealed, with a cigarette in her mouth and a knife in her hand, dressed as a butcher.

by ed528960 on 2009-08-23 13:28:08

On the 14th, "Perfectly Matched" was officially released nationwide. The creators of the film were also busy. After Zhang Ziyi and So Ji-sub flew to South Korea earlier to promote the film, director Jin Yimeng also held a publicity event in Beijing with Fan Bingbing and Wang Ji. The film attracted attention with its well-known all-star cast, and its gorgeous and beautiful scenes also had a Korean style. In an interview, director Jin Yimeng said that she never thought Fan Bingbing would act so crazy when playing an ugly role. "Fan Bingbing's interpretation of the character is very accurate," Jin Yimeng frankly stated that she hopes to have another opportunity to cooperate with Fan Bingbing.

In "Perfectly Matched," Fan Bingbing has a bold appearance. In a newly exposed set of stills, Fan Bingbing wears a leather apron, has an afro hairstyle, dark circles under her eyes, smokes, and wields a knife, playing a butcher. According to Tang Yi, the dedicated stylist for Zhang Ziyi, "In 'Perfectly Matched,' we adopted a one-on-one styling method. I was responsible for Ziyi, and Bingbing was handled by Bu Kewen, who has collaborated with her for many years. We each had independent makeup rooms, and many of the styles were unclear beforehand. I remember on the first day of filming, it was a scene where rivals meet and sparks fly. At first, I gave Ziyi a simple and elegant look, but when I took a break and went to check on Bingbing, I was shocked. Bingbing usually knows how to apply makeup well, and this look was extremely striking. She had black eye circles, black lips, an afro, and even dirt on her face. When I came back, I described it to Ziyi, and she said we needed to make some changes, which resulted in the landlady image you see in the trailers."

According to viewers who have seen the film, Fan Bingbing's appearance in the movie is very satisfying. "The arrogant, smart, and 'spicy' character of Wang Jingjing is portrayed vividly by Bingbing! I especially like the scene where she wields a kitchen knife and slashes at 'Sophie's' head. Her cold beauty persona left a deep impression on me, and I absolutely love it." Fan Bingbing's performance in this part is bold and unconventional, disregarding her image completely, portraying a vicious butcher character thoroughly, showing a sense of giving everything for the role.
