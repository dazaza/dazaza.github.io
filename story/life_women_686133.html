Eye Care: Four Tricks to Reduce Eye Aging and Eliminate Fine Lines

by liu514113345 on 2012-03-01 22:26:31

The first wrinkle we usually find on our face occurs around the eyes. The skin around the eyes is fragile and sensitive, making it the area where signs of aging appear most easily. Therefore, eye care naturally becomes the focus of our skincare routine to get rid of the "aging" follower. How to remove fine lines around the eyes? Here are four effective methods to rejuvenate your eye skin! Let's get started!

1. Keep the skin around the eyes hydrated

The appearance of fine lines around the eyes is almost always caused by dryness. Newly appeared dry lines can quickly disappear with proper hydration. However, if these dry lines are not carefully cared for at the beginning, they will gradually deepen into deeper wrinkles. At that point, completely removing eye wrinkles will be extremely difficult, and the inevitable result will be loose and aged eye skin. Therefore, keeping the skin around your eyes hydrated is the key to eye care. Recommended reading: A collection of hydrating eye creams to solve your winter troubles.

In addition to using hydrating eye cream, you should also adjust according to the dryness of the air environment, trying to keep your skin in a relatively moist environment. Adding one or two green plants to your workspace or rest area, or adding an air humidifier, can prevent dryness around your eyes and even all over your body.

2. Use green tea more often

Green tea has good antioxidant properties and is the best choice for eye care. It can promote blood circulation in the eye skin and help resist radiation. You should drink more green tea regularly. Besides drinking it, green tea can also be used as an eye mask. Not only can it relieve eye fatigue and replenish moisture to the eye skin, but it can also eliminate dark circles and eye puffiness. 3. Stick to doing eye exercises

A simple yet effective set of eye exercises can relieve symptoms of eye fatigue and dryness. Gently massaging the eyes promotes blood circulation in the skin, helps to expel metabolic waste and toxins from the skin, enhances the self-repair ability of the eye skin, and effectively combats aging problems.

4. Eat blueberries and yogurt often

There are absolutely no shortage of foods in life that can take care of the skin around the eyes. Blueberries and yogurt are excellent skincare products for eye care. They can be eaten directly or made into homemade eye masks and applied to the skin around the eyes. They can help promote metabolism, moisturize the skin around the eyes, reduce the formation of fine lines around the eyes, and make your eyes bright and radiant.
