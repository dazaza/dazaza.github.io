Introduction: Stretch marks are a testament to a woman becoming a great mother. However, while welcoming adorable babies, mothers pay a heavy price—interwoven stretch marks on their bodies, scars that itch and hurt during rainy weather, and loose, sagging abdomens—all of which rob once-beautiful mothers of their confidence and beauty. How can they be like celebrity moms such as Xiao S and Kelly Chen, staying away from stretch marks and being sexy and charming? Removing stretch marks is the first step towards becoming a sexy mom.

"After having my baby, I tried dieting and yoga, and my figure gradually recovered. But I had no solution for the stretch marks on my belly. I bought all kinds of creams, medicines, and cosmetics that were rumored to work, spending over ten thousand yuan with no effect. I visited many hospitals, but doctors said there was no particularly effective treatment. Now, I lack confidence when taking showers, fearing others might see my stomach. I even feel my husband's passion for me has diminished..." At the Oriental Plastic Surgery Medspa Non-surgical Skin Aesthetic Center, Ms. Feng voiced the shared sentiment of all mothers troubled by "stretch marks."

Director Yanju Yu of the Oriental Plastic Surgery Dermatology Aesthetic Center told reporters that stretch marks have long been a difficult issue in the medical field. When women are pregnant around eight or nine months, the abdominal skin stretches excessively, causing the breakdown of dermal fibers and resulting in stretch marks. The excessive expansion also causes the skin to lose resilience, leading to a loose and sagging abdomen, making stretch marks, cellulite, and scars from cesarean sections a major concern for all mothers!

The Luxe Star Fractional Laser is the latest computerized intelligent laser therapy technology introduced by Oriental Plastic Surgery, specifically targeting stretch marks and difficult-to-treat scars. Its unique 2940nm light wave has extremely strong penetration power. Through precise digital control, the fractional laser focuses on the areas with stretch marks and scars, concentrating energy deeply into the dermis layer, stimulating a large increase in collagen production, prompting deep cellular reordering in the stretch mark and scar regions, thus repairing scars and fading stretch marks.

After special processing, the laser's instant heat significantly promotes the activity of collagen and elastin fibers in the dermis layer, stimulating their rapid proliferation and reorganization, quickly tightening and lifting the skin.

As an experienced dermatologist, Director Yanju Yu has always been dedicated to research on stretch marks and difficult-to-treat scars. She found through years of clinical practice that targeted combination treatments often yield results greater than the sum of their parts: "For example, Ms. Feng, although her figure has recovered, her abdominal skin is still loose and sagging. In this case, she needs to undergo Fotona ST skin tightening treatment first to make the abdominal skin firm and elastic, then proceed with fractional laser treatment for stretch marks, which will produce better results!"

Currently, Ms. Feng has completed her first course of treatment and is about to start the second. She happily discovered that her previously dark red stretch marks have faded to a light yellow-brown; when touched, there are no longer the ridged scars she had a month ago. Now, unless looked at closely, the stretch marks are barely noticeable!
