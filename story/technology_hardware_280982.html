Company Profile

Pioneer of Enterprise Management (PEM for short), Bangyuan Management Consulting Company, is one of the most powerful professional management consulting companies in China, with a large-scale professional outdoor expansion training base. It is the strategic partner of Malaysian International Training Institute, Beijing Times Guanghua, Beijing Modern Central Europe Management Science Research Institute, and Gaoyao Guangxin Agricultural Ecology Park.

Bangyuan Consultant adheres to the service concept of "Customer interests above all," upholding the tenet of "Our success is built on the basis of customer success," providing various types of enterprises with management consulting services in the following areas: outdoor expansion training, sandbox simulation series management courses, corporate strategic management, organizational transformation, corporate image design, business process reengineering, human resource management system design, modern distribution, brand management, corporate mergers and acquisitions and integration, marketing management, financial management system, etc., offering specialized high-quality services.

After years of management consulting and training practice, Bangyuan Consultant has formed its unique consulting and training capabilities and core advantages, boasting a large number of domestic and international senior international lecturers, professional outdoor expansion instructors, forging a highly competitive team of lecturers. They have provided professional services to more than 300 large and medium-sized enterprises at home and abroad, including Jingxin South China Branch, China Unicom Guangzhou Branch, China Netcom, HP China, Construction Bank of China Guangdong Branch, Guangdong Jizhida, Tianmao Department Store, and other well-known large enterprises at home and abroad.

Service Philosophy

Bangyuan Consultant adheres to the service philosophy of: everything starts from customer satisfaction, helping customers solve management problems, helping customers learn how to solve management problems.

Purpose and Mission

To convey international management concepts and methods, provide practical solutions and promote their implementation, assist local enterprises in improving management, and cultivate core competitive abilities for sustainable development.
