The second chapter

If someone finds a pig named Song Qiaofeng at the rainy station in February, please take him away.

Since this pig doesn't want to get lost at the station where twilight flows, and it doesn't want to be found by Zhang Xiaoxiao, losing its soul and looking stunned and ugly. It also doesn't want to be her hobby later.

However, if there were any ifs. Hello, my name is Song Qiaofeng. But fate still favored me. At a downpouring station, I was picked up by a girl named Zhang Xiaoxiao. One day, she came to me and said softly, "Hello, my name is Zhang Xiaoxiao." I looked at her helplessly, and she smiled cruelly, making people feel like they were basking in the spring breeze. She said, "Song Qiaofeng, let's be friends." She was so straightforward. Her smile didn't seem forced, and from her eyes, you could tell that she was extremely serious. Thus, on a stormy night, Zhang Xiaoxiao took in a pig named Song Qiaofeng.

After I stayed with Zhang Xiaoxiao, I became the common enemy of all the boys in the school. They gave me dirty looks, swore at me, even wanted to beat me, or threw a sentence at me: "Song Qiaofeng, don't let me see you with Zhang Xiaoxiao again, go as far as you can." I knew they were不服 (unconvinced). Some boys were infatuated with her celestial couple image. Occasionally, some would start harboring ill intentions, began envying me, opposing me, seeing me as a thorn in their side, even tormenting me, choking my neck, beating and kicking me, saying, "Song Qiaofeng, please stay away from Zhang Xiaoxiao. She's so beautiful, being with you is like putting a fresh flower on cow dung." I wiped the blood from the corner of my mouth and laughed, saying, "Putting a fresh flower on cow dung, how does the flower feel? The flower should be happy, where can it find such good fertilizer? Besides, cow dung contributes its all for the blooming of the flower. We are quite suitable together." One day, Zhang Xiaoxiao invited me to walk in the rape flowers. She really liked flowers. Every time she smelled the fragrance of flowers, she would dance gracefully among them. She said, "Song Qiaofeng, look quickly, how beautiful those flowers are." Then, she suddenly took off her SpongeBob slippers, stood barefoot on the ground spinning around. Her straight hair fell on her shoulders, like a pure spirit. She smiled and asked me, "Song Qiaofeng, am I beautiful?" My eyes stared directly at her, watching and watching, I couldn't help but laugh out loud. Hearing my laughter, she pouted and said, "Song Qiaofeng, what are you laughing at?" Unexpectedly, she was so beautiful even when she was angry, her eyebrows bright like stars, her lips slightly turned upwards revealing two small dimples like budding flowers. I said, "Of course you're beautiful, those flowers aren't as beautiful as you." "Hmph, slick talker." Really, Zhang Xiaoxiao, in my heart, you will always be the most beautiful, an irreplaceable reality.

Suddenly, a fragrant butterfly flew by, seeking to sing with the birds in the spring breeze, gently spinning, lightly falling, so tranquil in the air, with such clear eyes, and such light wings. "Song Qiaofeng, can you catch those two butterflies for me?"

She looked at me blankly, under her long eyelashes was a pair of longing eyes, which made me very uncomfortable, my face turning red.

"Zhang Xiaoxiao, if you like, I can give you bigger butterflies. But do you know that these two butterflies might just be Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai who have transformed into butterflies out of love. Would you really bear to separate them?"

Finally, you gave up. You said, "Song Qiaofeng, thank you."

On the bright afternoon of the next day, I specially made two butterflies, one male and one female, and invited her to fly kites. Her hair was just washed, still slightly wet and retaining a bit of fragrance. Perhaps because I was in a hurry, I hadn't noticed that I was holding her hand when I invited her out. When I accidentally discovered that she was staring at our tightly clasped hands, I awkwardly let go. "Song Qiaofeng, you idiot, she hasn't officially become your girlfriend yet. So eager, even holding her hand, are you trying to die?"
