Echo: The Mystery of the Lost Caverns (A mysterious cavern world)

by youxiruanjian88 on 2009-02-01 08:39:55

【Basic Introduction】

This game tells the story of players struggling to survive during their adventurous journey of exploring ancient civilization ruins. It will surely give a big surprise to the fans who like traveling and adventure.

Since it is about exploring the mystery of caves, it is naturally that the game will create a mysterious secret cave world for players. In order to better create the world of lost caves, the production team carefully studied a large number of research reports from the Paleolithic era and recreated real game scenes. The game will also provide players with an accurate historical encyclopedia of that era so that they can further explore the truth according to historical facts during the game. This game is somewhat similar to "Lost in Blue" developed by KONAMI for NDS. Although other adventure games also set some ups and downs in the adventure plot for players, the purpose of this game is to let players learn how to survive in a desolate world and find the miracles forgotten by humans, excavating the lost ancient civilization. The concept of the game is highly creative. Besides the events often encountered in the adventure journey, there are also many more survival modes such as fishing, hunting, etc., all kinds of survival skills are available. You can even collect ancient fossils or other meaningful things. In the world of lost caves, players will see many fresh things they have never seen before, such as mysterious Shaman rituals, etc.

In the game, players play as Arok. You will explore the prehistoric civilization through Arok's eyes. You will encounter many fascinating plots. Surviving in the eerie caves will help you uncover the true secrets of the lost caves. In this work, players will enter a mysterious world full of puzzles and start strange adventures. Survive in a huge barren world through meticulous observation skills, while discovering the greatest forgotten wonders of mankind. Game developers not only continue the traditional gameplay of adventure games but also integrate the art of ancient civilizations into the game. For example, you can learn cave murals, various crafts, and appreciate the mysterious and weird Shaman culture during your adventure journey. Moreover, the convergence of these craft cultures will also play a certain role in helping players survive. For instance, Shamanism is a very spiritual primitive religion. Under the control of the belief that everything has a soul, they mainly worship the ancestral spirits of clans or tribes, hoping to have superhuman forces to save themselves. They invite spirits through prayer, sacrifice, or singing, allowing the spirits to possess the Shamans and grant humans powerful divine power. Players obtain the opportunity to survive by completing certain Shaman rituals. The bizarre scenes and crisscrossing cave passages in the game will make you feel thrilled when you shout loudly and hear the echo on the cave walls. How to successfully walk out of the deep caves and find the buried history depends entirely on the player's intelligence, observational skills, and proficient survival skills. Proper use of wisdom will enable you to survive in the vast wasteland journey.

【Software Features】

The game graphics are exquisitely crafted, and the scene layout is very realistic. Not only are there natural caves and穴dweller caves, but also vast wildernesses and lush green grasslands. The entire game picture will present a photo-level standard. There are lifelike animals carved by Cro-Magnon people on the cave rocks, such as goats and sika deer. Bone fragments are scattered on the ground, and fences made of bones, as well as hanging bones, will make you feel chills down your spine or arouse your curiosity about primitive civilization. During the time period when players survive in the mysterious caves in the game, they will also encounter changes in seasons and climate, and the game scene environment will change accordingly. When spring arrives, beautiful flowers bloom on the grassland, the babbling brook flows slowly, and the green meadow presents vibrant vitality. When winter comes, the surroundings are covered with beautiful silver snow, perhaps you can study the local geographical environment and climate change. When you enter this magical world that was once home to cave dwellers, hunters, fishermen, or monks, you will see the carcasses of primitive animals supported by wooden sticks or animal skulls. Between the rocks and trees, you may discover astonishing secrets. In the game, when you start to find one puzzle after another interesting, deeper research and exploration will spark greater interest. If you are afraid of losing direction, you can make some marks to avoid unnecessary blind exploration and inquiry. You can truly feel the shock and charm brought by ancient civilization.

This is a game aimed at teenagers. Due to its healthy theme and the characteristic of exploring and recalling the brilliant ancient civilization, "ECHO: Secrets of the Lost Cavern" has been rated as a "T" level game by ESRB. The ancient civilization carved by nature's windknife, long lost in the river of time, even if only a tip of the iceberg is discovered, the past era will reappear before our eyes today like an unstoppable tide.
