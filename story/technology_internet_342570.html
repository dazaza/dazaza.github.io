Introduction to the Journal: The "Rule of Law and Society" magazine is a large-scale media outlet that promotes the Party's strategy of governing the country according to law. It reflects on China’s democratic and legal system construction as well as spiritual civilization building, and serves as an important platform for popularizing legal knowledge and legal education. Over the years, the publishers of the "Rule of Law and Society" magazine have recognized the importance of their work from the perspective of promoting national legal system construction and governing the country by law, striving to publish this journal with diligence.

The "Rule of Law and Society" magazine is a publicly issued legal periodical nationwide, integrating scientific, ideological, readable, and documentary elements. It is committed to timely, vivid, comprehensive, profound, and full coverage of major domestic and international news details and inside stories. Its goal is to be close to the people, pay attention to society, advocate the rule of law, reflect life, provide readers with rich comprehensive information and legal knowledge, offer strong legal assistance, and protect legitimate rights and interests.

So far, the Rule of Law and Society magazine has established close cooperative relationships with many domestic media outlets and achieved interactive broadcasting with CCTV's Social and Legal Channel "Frontline".
