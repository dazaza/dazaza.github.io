Luneng intervenes in the battle for Rong Hao and Deng Zhuoxiang, Shuntian and Chanba at a disadvantage and may be eliminated

Rong Hao, who played for Shuntian last season, and Deng Zhuoxiang are the hottest figures in the market. In the previous stage, the Shaanxi Chanba Club had already come very close to signing Rong Hao, but from the current situation, both Shaanxi and Shuntian clubs may have to step aside because the strong team Shandong Luneng of the Chinese Super League has also joined the competition, and they are catching up later.

Attractiveness

Luneng is obviously stronger than Chanba and Shuntian

Although multiple clubs are competing, Rong Hao's destination has not been finally determined so far, but it can be肯定that Shandong Luneng, which has just joined the competition, is likely to be the biggest winner, signing Rong Hao at a high price and also bringing Deng Zhuoxiang along.

The ability shown by Rong Hao in the league and the national team made Luneng change its mind about not introducing domestic players on a large scale. It is reported that in terms of treatment, Luneng did not offer much higher conditions than other clubs, but basically satisfied the player and the agent. What attracts players more is Luneng's strong background and investment compared to other clubs. Luneng is an absolute strong team in the Chinese Super League, and for capable and ambitious players, joining such a team will surely make greater achievements. Therefore, in this regard, Luneng is definitely at an absolute advantage over other clubs.

Luneng's sudden appearance may hurt the Shaanxi Chanba Club the most. In fact, in terms of player introduction, Rong Hao is not the only focus of competition between Shaanxi and Shandong. The overseas player Zhou Beihai is also a common goal of the two teams. The Luneng side has been operating Zhou Beihai's return, but unexpectedly, the Shaanxi team came out halfway. "In order to exchange for Shaanxi not to negotiate with Zhou Beihai, Shandong previously expressed that they would let Rong Hao go to Shaanxi," a well-informed person revealed. However, introducing Zhou Beihai is definitely not as realistic as introducing Rong Hao. The annual salary proposed by Zhou Beihai and adding some clauses favorable to his re-overseas transfer in the contract are difficult for Shandong to accept. "In this situation, the Shandong side feels that introducing Rong Hao is more reliable," the well-informed person said.

Sign contracts

Contract signing order violates rules

For the scramble among Shaanxi, Luneng and other clubs, the Shuntian side appears helpless. Shuntian, which has just achieved first-year survival in the Chinese Super League, is still a newcomer. The club's goal in recent years is to stand firm and cultivate its own players with Chinese Super League capabilities.

In fact, for Rong Hao and Deng Zhuoxiang, the Shuntian club hopes more than anyone else that they can stay in Shuntian. However, reality is cruel, and Shuntian can only keep the bitterness in their hearts. Shuntian currently does not sign Rong and Deng, not because they were scared away by the high annual salary. A senior Shuntian official told reporters: "Even if it's tens of thousands more, we can afford it." The main reason why they cannot immediately renew ties with the two is that the Dutch agent of Rong Hao and Deng Zhuoxiang requires signing the two work contracts first, which is difficult for the Shuntian club. First, due to the delay in publishing the new transfer policy by the Football Association, Wuhan Club, the owner of Rong and Deng's ownership rights, is unwilling to negotiate with Shuntian about their transfer and even less willing to sign the transfer contract immediately. Therefore, even if Shuntian signs the work contract with the two now, without signing the transfer contract with Wuhan, there will be subsequent issues such as high transfer fees. Secondly, if the income of Rong and Deng is too high, far exceeding the level of other Chinese Super League players, it will bring instability factors to the team, which is what the Shuntian club worries about the most. After all, football is played by eleven or even more people.

Preparation

Shuntian plans to heavily use the 89, 90 youth teams

The harsh reality of the Chinese Super League is in front of Shuntian Club and head coach Pei Encai, who have made full mental preparations. Even if Rong Hao and Deng Zhuoxiang really transfer to other teams, Shuntian must find substitutes for them in the shortest time possible. Coach Pei Encai has his own methods in nurturing young talents, so Shuntian Club plans to heavily use the players from the 89, 90 youth teams to test Coach Pei Encai's ability to nurture young talents. Shuntian's 89, 90 youth team can be said to be the hope of Jiangsu football in the future, and the forward Ge Wei in the team has received affirmation from multiple parties. Senior officials of the Shuntian Club highly praised this forward whose height exceeds 2 meters, believing that if Ge Wei is used well, he will surely be a player who can enter the national team in the future. General Manager Zhang Yudao of Shuntian Club told reporters: "The club is actively contacting the agents of Rong and Deng, making every effort to keep the two, but has also prepared for their departure."
