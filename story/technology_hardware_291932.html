It has been six years since Ma Hongyun graduated from university. The young man has a diploma and good looks, but it is only because he is poor - unable to afford a house - that his relationships with three girlfriends have failed. Last week, a friend found him a cheap second-hand apartment, but even though it was cheap, it still cost more than 100,000 yuan. Even after emptying all his savings, Ma still fell far short of the amount needed. Out of desperation, Ma had no choice but to borrow money from relatives and friends. Just as Ma was about to wear out his legs running around, he unexpectedly received news from Er Niu, who said this guy had made a fortune in recent years and become the big boss of a real estate company.

Ma and Er Niu were not ordinary acquaintances; they had grown up together playing bare-bottomed. When Ma was a child, his family's conditions were better than Er Niu's. One year during the Spring Festival, Ma stole two yuan from his father to buy a string of firecrackers. Seeing Er Niu's envy, Ma generously tore off a long piece and gave it to him. Another time, while the two were playing by the river, Er Niu accidentally fell into the water. It was Ma who grabbed the riverside willow tree with one hand and risked his life to save Er Niu... Remembering how good he had been to Er Niu, Ma went to Er Niu's company full of hope.

But what Ma didn't expect was that as soon as they mentioned borrowing money, Er Niu revealed his true colors - it turned out that after becoming rich, this guy had become an unfeeling snob who had already forgotten the friendship of their childhood. As soon as Ma mentioned borrowing money, Er Niu started complaining about his own financial difficulties...

Ma was so angry he couldn't speak. After leaving Er Niu's company, he got on his bicycle and rushed back home in a huff. Along the way, Ma transferred all his resentment towards Er Niu onto the rich people. Looking at the villas by the roadside, he cursed: "All rich people have hearts of wolves and dogs! How come there isn't an earthquake to collapse these villas and crush you heartless bastards!" Seeing luxury cars driving by, he cursed again: "What's so great about driving a car? If you crash into the ditch and die, you bastard!"
