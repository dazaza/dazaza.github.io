Career thief sneaked into the wedding banquet to steal and was caught on the spot

by gy45tr3aop on 2012-03-09 16:51:14

Subsequently, when he attempted to steal at another wedding banquet, he was spotted by relatives of both the bride and groom who were more familiar with each other, leading to a prompt police report.

Around 1 PM on October 3rd, the Yiquanying Police Station of the Fengtai Public Security Bureau received a report that someone was stealing at a wedding banquet in a restaurant near Caqiao. Based on the testimony of the relatives of the newlyweds attending the banquet, the police apprehended a man surnamed Zhang with a northeastern accent. Two mobile phones were recovered from his person at the scene.

Zhang admitted that people at wedding banquets generally have low vigilance, and the two parties of relatives usually do not know each other well, making it easy for him to infiltrate. He noticed some relatives hung their coats with items inside directly on chair backs without using garment covers, allowing him to repeatedly commit theft successfully. Currently, Zhang has been criminally detained by the Fengtai police on suspicion of theft.

Upon investigation, it was revealed that Zhang, originally from Heilongjiang, had previously been dealt with for stealing at weddings in his hometown. He had also been handled in Anhui Province for drug use. This time, he was passing through Beijing via train on his way back to Heilongjiang from Anhui. During his stay in Beijing, on the morning of October 3rd, Zhang planned to sneak into a wedding in Daxing District, which he was more familiar with, to carry out thefts.

Zhang purchased a set of casual clothes for himself. While passing through the Caqiao area, he noticed a restaurant hosting multiple wedding banquets. He confidently entered the restaurant and stole two mobile phones from the pockets of coats hanging on chairs during two separate wedding banquets.

According to reports from the Fengtai Public Security Bureau, a criminal suspect specializing in infiltrating wedding venues to conduct thefts has been arrested by the police. Currently, the suspect Zhang has been placed under criminal detention by the police.
