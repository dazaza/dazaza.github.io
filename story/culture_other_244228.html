Netizens in Haimen, don't you really visit local Haimen websites?

by extra7 on 2009-09-29 21:44:00

I have never seen a local forum of a county or city so quiet, with only dozens of posts per day, and sometimes less than 15. Please don't doubt me. I am not just talking about the HaMen forum. You can check out several so-called big forums in HaMen, some of which even have zero posts for many days. Even if they occasionally break this perfect number, it is usually due to the contributions of posting robots. Then take a look at the QiDong forum, RuDong forum, RuGao forum, and HaiAn forum, all of which have more than 1000 posts per day. Is it because they have more local netizens? Is it because their regions attract more attention from netizens in other regions than HaMen? Is it because their forums are thousands of times better than HaMen's? Is it because they only have one local forum that gathers all the local popularity? After investigation, it was found that it is far from the case. Instead, it is clear that local netizens in HaMen do not think highly of local websites in HaMen. Local QQ groups in HaMen are also full of idle chatter. In the past, the most people from Nantong in the Nantong QQ chat room were from HaMen. People from HaMen prefer big websites from other places and do not care about the local situation in HaMen. They also lack unity within the region of HaMen. There are also divisions between people from the sand area and those from the north of the river, as well as differences between towns, making it difficult for them to come together. How many people actually say "I am from HaMen" when speaking to outsiders. I have been collecting information related to HaMen for a long time. People from HaMen have many strengths, but these are mostly from the past. As for current strengths, I think they only have a little wealth and a bit of cleverness. Without unity, without integration, and without positive energy.

The history of HaMen is not very long, and it is really hard to talk about any cultural heritage! I remember the HaMen spirit "Sea accepts hundreds of rivers, strong will and resolute action". The first time I heard it, I was greatly inspired. But when I looked at the HaMen online community, it was unimaginable. Take a look at the HaMen Tieba, HaMen UC community, HaMen Taobao community, and various HaMen QQ groups... I really don't know what to say!

How to unite netizens in HaMen and jointly care about our hometown HaMen, how to promote HaMen, and how to let everyone communicate happily on the Internet...
