Ticket booking process:

1. Book tickets by phone, the ticket fare can only be paid through bank transfer and not cash transactions. Customers must pay the ticket fare first before we can reserve the tickets for you. Folk tourism.

2. After the customer has completed the payment of the ticket fare, they must contact the booking staff to verify the customer's ticket fare and register the customer's valid ID.

3. Customers can take their valid ID and order number to the designated window by the staff to collect the tickets or choose door-to-door delivery.

Special Notice:

1. Please follow the rules and regulations of our company. All users who inquire about booking services must follow the procedures.

2. Please abide by the rules and regulations of electronic train tickets from the Railway Bureau Passenger Department.

3. All ticket booking users must provide real and valid IDs or valid documents of the passenger as well as contact information.

4. Once the staff successfully places your electronic ticket order, they will then provide you with a designated account from the Railway Bureau Passenger Department Ticket Office to make the payment.

5. After we receive the ticket fare, we will send you your electronic ticket order number via email or text message.

6. You can check your boarding information online using the electronic ticket order number sent to you.

7. Passengers should arrive at the station 30 minutes in advance with their valid ID to handle the boarding pass.

8. Passengers must pay the ticket fare within 120 minutes; otherwise, the system will freeze or cancel your reserved electronic ticket.

9. Payment must be made within the credit period. Payment methods include:

a. Bank counter

b. ATM transfer

Ticket collection methods:

1. Tickets can be delivered to the address provided by the booking customer free of charge.

2. Tickets can also be collected directly at the station with the valid ID and order number of the booking customer.

Hangzhou Daily news (reported by Zhe Dong, correspondent Wen Momo) - Reporters learned yesterday from the railway station that this year's Spring Festival travel rush will still adopt the method of telephone booking and on-site ticket collection for ticket sales. It is reported that the number of group tickets this year has increased from last year's 135,000 to no less than 160,000 this year. It is expected that 770,000 passengers will be sent this year, an increase of 4.6% over the same period last year. The number of city-wide ticket agency points has also increased from more than 30 last year to 74, among which Changping will have five agency points. It is understood that due to the recognition of the telephone booking and on-site ticket collection method used in the past two years by all sectors of society, therefore, this year's Spring Festival travel rush Dongguan East Station will still use the method of telephone booking and on-site ticket collection for ticket sales. In order to reflect the harmonious Spring Festival travel rush, the railway station will fully reflect the concept of people-oriented in terms of booking and collecting tickets. Deputy Station Master Liu Xiaoping told reporters that in terms of ticketing methods, the station will mainly adopt telephone booking, metro, and mainly group online booking. Apart from group bookings and online bookings, GPS quotations, all other quotas will be allocated to telephone bookings, allowing every ordinary person to fairly and justly book tickets by phone, ensuring that passengers return home to reunite with their families. To facilitate public ticket booking, Liu Xiaoping particularly reminds passengers: please use mobile phones when booking tickets because the booking process is relatively complex and may cause inconvenience to passengers themselves.
