UNIQLO Adorns Xiamen

The clothing of UNIQLO is precisely "creating garments for people." It transcends age, profession, and gender, surpassing all differences between individuals, made for you, me, and him. It is the kind of clothing that allows each person in every corner of the world to freely match according to their own style, comfortably and happily worn every day. It's innovative clothing that subtly transforms lifestyles while maintaining minimalist design and foundational versatility. Different from any brand in the world, UNIQLO will continue to think, imagine, and create, always making "clothes" for people.

Through high-quality selected fabrics, simple and versatile styles, and a colorful palette, comfortable and concise clothing enables consumers to achieve self-styled coordination, showcasing unique personal charm.

The Opening of Two New UNIQLO Stores in Xiamen

The upcoming openings of two new UNIQLO stores in Xiamen —— SM City Square Store (opening on November 5th) and California Plaza Store (opening on November 19th) —— demonstrate UNIQLO's emphasis on the Xiamen market. The SM City Square Store, located at the intersection of Jiahe Road and Xianyue Road in Huli District, is considered a landmark consumer destination in Xiamen. The third store, California Plaza Store, is located on Lianqian East Road in Siming District, also one of Xiamen's traditional important business districts.

A Spectacular Appearance of Autumn and Winter New Arrivals

With the opening of the two new UNIQLO stores in Xiamen, many popular autumn and winter items make a spectacular appearance, bringing a warm fashion experience to Xiamen consumers. UNIQLO's popular product, the HEATTECH series, which has cumulatively sold over 1 billion pieces globally, features numerous excellent properties such as moisture absorption, heat generation, insulation, moisturization (only available for women's wear), and elasticity. It provides good thermal retention and stretchability, making winter dressing easy and fashionable. At the same time, the premium lightweight down series offers an astonishingly light texture, bringing a renewed concept of clothing. Lightweight yet stylish and still warm, it uses premium down with up to 90% content. Besides jackets, there are vest options available for both men and women, offering convenient matching options based on different clothing needs throughout the entire winter. Please enjoy this warm experience through actual wearing.
