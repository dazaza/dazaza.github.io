I might never end up with Lu Jun, Shenzhen private detective.

by fengmi7v on 2012-02-24 23:25:03

The way people look at me is becoming increasingly ambiguous, and some无聊people even come to the hospital specifically to see me. The rumors about me shooting pornographic videos are getting more and more intense. My private life with my ex-husband, Lu Jun, has been secretly filmed! I bought a CD from a vendor selling pirated discs at a high price. Although the faces of the people in the video were blurred, the familiar scene still made me faint...

In order to save my parents' marriage, I met him (the characters in the article are pseudonyms). If it weren't for my father's request for divorce, perhaps I would never have ended up with Lu Jun. That year, I had just graduated from nursing school and worked as a nurse in a hospital in the county town. Just as I was secretly rejoicing at my independence, my father suddenly started arguing for divorce with my mother. He said he had always wanted a divorce, but because I was young, now that I am working, he can put his concerns aside.

Perhaps I should understand my father. From childhood, my mother has always been arguing with him, criticizing him for various reasons. For this, I often stood up for my father. But I can also understand my mother. As a young intellectual sent down to the countryside from a big city, when others packed their bags and returned to the city to live a prosperous urban life, she stayed in our backward coastal small city for the sake of my young self and my father. Her inner bitterness and dissatisfaction are also understandable. In the past, whenever they argued fiercely, they would both mention the word "divorce", but their marriage has always persisted stubbornly. But this time, my father seemed determined, he even moved out.

That day, I aimlessly walked on the street, troubled and puzzled by why my father decided to divorce, and also sad for my inability to do anything about my parents' marriage. Suddenly, a line of text caught my eye: This studio provides private detective services. To be honest, I only read about the term "private detective" in newspapers and magazines, never expecting that we would also have such services here! If I could find out the reason for my father's divorce, maybe I could save my parents' marriage. I was very excited by this idea.

When I hesitated and walked into the private detective studio, a handsome young man warmly received me. He said his name was Lu Jun, the founder of this studio. He was very talkative and good at catering to customers' psychology. In a short while, I was convinced, and we smoothly signed a service contract. He would investigate the reason for my father's divorce, and I would pay him 2000 yuan as service fee after the task was completed. To be honest, for a newly employed nurse like me, 2000 yuan is not a small amount, but in order to save my parents' marriage, I would willingly pay more.

Lu Jun began the intensive investigation. He was really dedicated and kept me informed of any situation in a timely manner. A week later, Lu Jun handed me a stack of photos. It turned out that my father had a new love interest, the third party being Ms. Wu, a divorced woman from my father's unit. When I threw the photos in front of Ms. Wu, my father surprisingly did not hide his relationship with her, showing a reckless attitude. And the most painful one was my mother. She originally thought that my father was proposing divorce due to her bad temper, but unexpectedly, the usually honest father also had a third party. Mother was deeply shocked, she lost her appetite, and finally fell ill. The situation became complicated all of a sudden.

Unexpectedly, my kindness led to such an outcome, I was really at a loss. But no matter what, I still need to pay Lu Jun's service fee. That day, I delivered the hard-earned 2000 yuan to Lu Jun's studio. Lu Jun asked me how things went, and I helplessly shook my head. Lu Jun consoled me, telling me to leave it to him and not to worry too much. Lu Jun's words touched me, tears uncontrollably streamed down from my eyes. To be honest, from childhood, I had never encountered such a thorny issue.

A few days later, my father, who had not returned home for a long time due to the divorce, suddenly came back home. He seemed to have abandoned the idea of divorce. Despite still sleeping separately from my mother, he did not mention divorce anymore. My father's behavior made me feel strange. After a period of time, I gradually heard people say that Ms. Wu's son, who was still in elementary school, was inexplicably beaten up, and on the same night, someone knocked on Ms. Wu's door, and a group of masked men broke in and threatened her. If she dared to seduce my father again, they would harm her son. Her son has always been Ms. Wu's beloved, after careful consideration, she decided to give up on my father and instead persuaded him to go home.

Needless to say, I knew it was Lu Jun's doing. When I took off the only valuable item I had on me, the ring given by my mother, and presented it to Lu Jun in gratitude, Lu Jun laughed and told me to put it back on.

"I want to personally put a ring on your finger! How could I take your ring?" Lu Jun said meaningfully.

Lu Jun's words made my face flush red instantly. However, if there was indeed such a capable person to act as my protector, I could really be worry-free, I thought.

And thus, Lu Jun became my protector, we fell in love.
