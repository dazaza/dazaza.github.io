### Integration of Work and Study, Practical Training Facilities, and Employment-Oriented Education

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and registered with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. Located at No. 29 Shi Huo South Road in Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, it boasts a prime geographical location, convenient transportation, and an elegant environment.

Our school consistently adheres to the development philosophy of "liberating thoughts, being pragmatic, and innovating." We continuously reform our teaching methods and management practices, firmly establishing a "market competition" awareness. Guided by market demands and enterprise needs, we explore new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulating rich experience in teaching and management. Our graduates have received widespread praise from employers.

1. **First-Class Teaching Facilities Based on Enterprise Standards**

Advanced teaching and experimental equipment are the foundation and guarantee for quality vocational education. Our school has invested over 10 million yuan, building a numerical control (NC) training workshop equipped with more than 50 NC lathes, NC milling machines, conventional lathes, and milling machines. Additionally, we have established communication electronics laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, physical training rooms, and new teaching buildings as supporting facilities.

2. **Enterprise-Oriented Teaching Mode**

We adopt a practice-based teaching model where all students are managed as enterprise employees during the teaching process. Through competitive job placements, students assume roles such as team leaders, line supervisors, and employees. Regular evaluations and exams determine outstanding team leaders and technical experts among the students, effectively promoting their enthusiasm and initiative for learning.

Each student's daily "work" performance is monitored by quality indicators. Monthly statistics determine salary distribution based on work volume, performance evaluation, work discipline, appearance, safe operation, and bonuses. This system enhances students' active participation and competitiveness, enabling them to master knowledge and skills more efficiently.

Through enterprise-oriented teaching reforms, students gradually adapt to corporate management models while studying, meeting job requirements, and eventually surpassing enterprise employment standards.

3. **Quasi-Military Closed Management System Oriented Towards Enterprise Needs**

We comprehensively adopt enterprise employee management models to cultivate talents favored by companies. Enterprises not only need skilled workers but also emphasize employees' overall qualities, such as teamwork, compliance, execution, and competitive awareness. The cultivation and improvement of these qualities require collaborative efforts from all aspects of the school, integrated into the entire learning process.

Two distinctive management features:

- **Strict Quasi-Military Management**: Inspired by the educational philosophy of "becoming a person first, then becoming talented," we hire retired military officers for specialized quasi-military management. From daily routines to attire, behavior, etiquette, and housekeeping, students develop good living habits and excellent organizational discipline, quickly becoming disciplined individuals with a strong military demeanor.

- **Vocational Quality Education Management**: During the learning process, we offer vocational quality courses divided into theoretical instruction and practical application. These two aspects complement each other, enhancing students' understanding and transforming vocational quality requirements into voluntary behaviors. In collaboration with enterprises, we send students in batches to real-world positions for pre-employment internships, allowing them to understand society and businesses before officially joining the workforce.

4. **Employment-Oriented Job Placement**

As the quality of our students continues to improve, we have established internship and employment bases in the Pearl River Delta, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region, and Jiaozhou Peninsula. The primary employment direction for our NC major students is the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All internship units are top 500 global mechanical manufacturing enterprises. The professional alignment rate for internships and employment reaches 90%, with a student satisfaction rate exceeding 95%.

5. **Comprehensive后勤Services Including Free Meals**

Our school implements a "free meal system" for all students, providing three meals a day free of charge. This alleviates the financial burden on parents and ensures proper nutrition for students' physical development and intensive study. It also avoids potential issues arising from eating outside the school premises.

The student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, telephone booth, and bathhouse are fully equipped. The logistics department always prioritizes "service," striving to be proactive, considerate, efficient, and convenient.

6. **Only Miscellaneous Fees Charged**

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational institution committed to social responsibility. Leveraging our strong educational capabilities and forward-thinking concepts, we aim to minimize costs for students' families. Students only pay miscellaneous fees for three years; tuition and accommodation fees are covered through work-study programs and vocational quality training. We also offer a "Hongzhi Class" that fully or partially exempts high-achieving students from low-income families from all miscellaneous fees. Every student receives a national scholarship of 1,500 yuan per year.

7. **Advanced School Culture for Brand Building**

Throughout our educational journey, we have summarized experiences and innovatively developed our school culture:

- **Philosophy (Educational Philosophy)**: Leading enterprise employment standards

- **Responsibility (Commitment)**: Creating a favorable environment for Hebei's vocational education

- **Spirit (Team Spirit)**: Consensus, learning, cooperation, innovation, inclusiveness

- **Culture (Core Culture)**: Cultivating blue-collar elites

- **Vision (Development Goal)**: Becoming China's leading brand in vocational education

- **Mission (Achievement)**: Laying the foundation for corporate wealth

The refinement of "school culture" outlines our grand blueprint and direction for struggle. Practicing this culture helps us build a strong brand and enhance our influence.

Small victories depend on intelligence; great victories depend on virtue. Small successes rely on friends; great successes rely on rivals. We look forward to collaborating with all vocational schools in Hebei to provide better learning environments and employment opportunities for aspiring youth.
