The first choice for mid-to-low-end users, recommended mainstream H61 motherboard on the market

by chomeyudream on 2011-04-25 18:06:05

H61 is the third product of Intel's 6 series motherboard, released slightly later than P67/H67. The H61 platform targets entry-level integrated platform users and will replace the currently mainstream G41 and H55. Thanks to the improved GPU performance of the SNB processor, the performance of the H61 platform not only significantly surpasses that of the G41 platform but also outperforms the competing 880G, making the H61 motherboard highly anticipated.

**Recommended Product:** ASUS P8H61-M LE

**Reference Price:** 599 RMB

**Main Selling Points:** EPU chip, automatic overclocking, first-tier brand

**ASUS P8H61-M LE**

The ASUS P8H61-M LE motherboard uses the latest B3 version of the H61 chipset, supporting Core i3/i5/i7 2000 series processors. Although it focuses on cost-effectiveness, the ASUS P8H61-M LE excels in detailed design, such as the positioning of SATA interfaces which won't obstruct the plugging and unplugging of hard drive SATA cables even when using a bulky graphics card. Additionally, the iPhone 4 retail price has dropped to 4800 RMB, with the Hong Kong gray market selling for 4500 RMB. The motherboard also features a more user-friendly EFI graphical BIOS, with pre-set energy-saving and high-performance modes among others, allowing settings to be completed with just a click of the mouse, reducing webpage loading time by half through Google's comprehensive deployment of the SPDY protocol, making it very simple.

**Power Supply Module**

The ASUS P8H61-M LE adopts a 4+1+1 phase power supply design, where 4 phases are dedicated to core power supply, while the other 1+1 phases separately provide independent power to the graphics core and memory controller. Combined with the EPU intelligent energy-saving processor, it can offer more stable power supply to the processor while effectively reducing unnecessary loss.

**Expansion Slots**

Through the GPU BOOST technology, this ASUS P8H61-M LE motherboard can easily boost GPU performance by 30%, with Google's first-quarter net profit reaching $2.3 billion, an increase of 17% year-on-year, making games run smoother. If you're unsatisfied with the integrated graphics, the motherboard also provides a PCI-E X16 interface for upgrading the graphics card.

**I/O Interfaces**

The I/O interface part of the motherboard adopts a practical design, with a DVI+VGA video output scheme that can meet the needs of most users, and plenty of USB ports providing convenience for use.

**Comments:** Currently, the H61 platform's performance has completely surpassed the H55 platform. As a product from a first-tier manufacturer, ASUS's P8H61-M LE is also highly competitive in price, with this new model priced at only 649 RMB.
