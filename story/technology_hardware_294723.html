Introduction to the Fifth Unit: When the country is broken, when the rivers and mountains are shattered, when fierce enemies invade, and when a nation faces life and death, what we need are bloody, unrestrained, ostentatious heroes who can ignite the indomitable and unbeatable spirit of every soldier! What we need is an iron-blooded army that is so strong it can make any formidable enemy hesitate - an army that fights with strength and overcomes strength! This is a novel about the legendary life of the founder of China's fifth special unit. It spans the Anti-Japanese War, the Korean War, and the self-defense counterattack war against India, recreating the fiery chapters of bloodshed. Let every Chinese soldier remember the two sentences left by the heroes of that era: "Rather be a fallen soul on the battlefield than a slave of a fallen nation!"
