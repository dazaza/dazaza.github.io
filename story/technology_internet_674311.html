Candidates who entered public institution recruitment examination and did not take the exam were judged as unqualified

by king2z9o on 2012-02-24 11:41:19

[I didn't even consider them as actors in a self-written and directed play.]

Speaking of the recruitment exam results announced on February 21 by Guizhou Radio and Television University and Guizhou Vocational Technical College, Lantiao Ao (a pseudonym) found it absurd —— after passing the qualification review on February 17, he had not received any notification from the recruiting unit regarding the time and location of the exam, nor did he participate in any relevant exams; but four days later, he unexpectedly discovered his [unqualified] result appearing on the score list of this exam, becoming an [accompanying name] for the candidates who advanced.

One of the recruiting units, the Personnel Department of Guizhou Radio and Television University, indicated through a responsible person surnamed Liang that the occurrence of being [accompanied] was due to a clerical error in the personnel department. In Lantiao Ao's view, this explanation was insufficient and unacceptable. The Youth Daily discovered that those who encountered [being accompanied] were not limited to Lantiao Ao alone.

An unexpected [qualification review passed]

Lantiao Ao, who just graduated with a master’s degree last December, is currently busy looking for a job. Searching for recruitment announcements, submitting resumes, participating in written tests and interviews —— these have all become part of his recent life routine.

On December 20 of last year, he saw a recruitment announcement for "Master's Degree Graduate Recruitment" by Guizhou Radio and Television University and Guizhou Vocational Technical College on a job-seeking website for fresh graduates.

Lantiao Ao quickly found the "Recruitment Position List" on the original announcement website, and at the same time, he also saw a similar recruitment notice on the official website of Guizhou Radio and Television University. He discovered that the position of [Business Enterprise Management Teacher] matched his major, and he also had work experience in both universities and enterprises. Therefore, Lantiao Ao followed the email address provided in the announcement and sent his resume, application form, and other materials.

The Youth Daily saw in the "Guizhou Radio and Television University, Guizhou Vocational Technical College Simplified Examination Procedure Master's Degree Graduate Recruitment Implementation Plan" published on the Guizhou Human Resources and Social Security Network that this public recruitment by the two institutions involved hiring a total of 61 various types of personnel, with the recruitment target being full-time general higher education graduate students with a master's degree or above.

The announcement clearly stated that candidates must go through six recruitment stages: registration, qualification review, examination, physical examination, evaluation, and handling of employment procedures.

In the nearly two months after sending his resume, despite checking the email account used for submitting resumes every day, Lantiao Ao never received any form of notification from the two schools in Guizhou. Due to applying to multiple units, Lantiao Ao gradually forgot that he had applied to these two schools until an accidental discovery on February 17.

"On February 17, I searched for my name online to see if there were any interview or test notifications, and accidentally found my name listed in one piece of information," Lantiao Ao told.

He opened the webpage and found out that he had already entered the "Guizhou Radio and Television University, Guizhou Vocational Technical College Simplified Procedure Master's Degree Graduate Qualification Review Qualified Personnel List." There were a total of 13 candidates applying for the same position as him, with 4 graduating from universities in Guizhou.

Seeing this qualification review result that he had found himself, Lantiao Ao felt somewhat relieved. "The announcement said that the time and location of the exam would be notified separately," he told, "In the following few days, I was waiting for an official phone call. I thought there should be a phone call, roughly telling me that I had made it to the interview, and informing me of the exam time and location. But there was no call."

Not informed to take the exam, yet already [unqualified]

Perhaps because this recruitment was a "simplified examination procedure recruitment," the content of the [examination] section in the announcement was only a few dozen words: "Examination Content: Professional Knowledge Test. Examination Results: Quantitative scoring will be conducted by the examination expert panel on the applicants. Those who fail to complete the examination or do not obtain valid results will be disqualified from entering the next stage."

But Lantiao Ao's experience wasn't [simplified], it was [neglected]. For four days after February 17, no one told him to take the exam.

An even more absurd event occurred on February 21.

"On February 21, I entered that webpage again and surprisingly found myself on the physical examination list," Lantiao Ao told, "At the end of the webpage, there was also a score table for this exam. I glanced at it, and the result shocked me —— I didn't even take the exam, so where did the scores come from?! Moreover, it was unqualified, with a score of 56."

The Youth Daily saw on the electronic document titled "Guizhou Radio and Television University, Guizhou Vocational Technical College Simplified Procedure Master's Degree Graduate Exam Scores and Personnel Entering the Next Stage List" that among the 204 candidates who passed the qualification review, 175 obtained exam scores, while 29 had their [exam scores] column marked as [absent].

Lantiao Ao's [exam score] was [56], out of a maximum of 100 points. In the previous qualification review announcement, Lantiao Ao's applied position [Business Enterprise Management Teacher] planned to recruit [1 person], with 13 people passing the qualification review; but in the score list, the number of applicants for this position became 12 people, another originally-applied candidate Ma Mou appeared on the promotion list for [Financial Category Teacher], and his graduation major also changed from [Business Management] to [Finance].

In addition, the corresponding items on the score list for the [Business Enterprise Management Teacher] position showed that 3 people were [absent], leaving 9 people with scores, of which 5 were qualified, and 4, including Lantiao Ao, were unqualified. The highest scorer was Peng Mou from a university in Guizhou, who advanced to the next stage.

"How did this score come about?" Lantiao Ao was very confused, "In terms of strength, the university I graduated from is one of the three '985' universities among the 13 people applying for the Business Enterprise Management Teacher position."

The Youth Daily learned that those who encountered [being unqualified] were not limited to Lantiao Ao alone. It was only when they were asked directly that another candidate participating in this recruitment realized that the recruitment exam had already been completed, and she too had been [accompanied].

On February 22, the day after the results were announced, Lantiao Ao called the supervision hotline left in the recruitment announcement to report the situation and explain his doubts about this recruitment. However, the staff member of the Supervision Room of Guizhou Vocational Technical College who answered the phone asked him to call the Personnel Department of Guizhou Radio and Television University. After calling the latter twice, no one answered.

In the afternoon of February 23, The Youth Daily called the contact number of the Personnel Department of Guizhou Radio and Television University and reported the relevant situation.

About 20 minutes later, a teacher surnamed Liang, who claimed to be the head of the Personnel Department of Guizhou Radio and Television University, returned the call and apologized to Lantiao Ao, indicating that such an error was due to a mistake in the statistical summary by the personnel department, and the school leaders of the two schools took this matter very seriously.

However, when asked about the specific cause of the error, Mr. Liang said that he still needed to understand the situation from the examination expert group and suggested that this issue could be resolved through separate negotiation with Lantiao Ao, but re-examination would probably not be arranged.
