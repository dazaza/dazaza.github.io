The secret to a sudden increase in external links to a website is "persistence".

by long78 on 2011-02-27 20:29:40

Baidu updated again last night, and most websites gained more external links in this update. Especially Qingdao SEO's wedding website increased its external links explosively from 4170 yesterday to 6630. The blog of Qingdao SEO also saw a surge in external links from 619 to 1990. This was a very powerful update, and other websites also saw an increase in external links. Such a powerful external link made the current ranking of the website steadily rise. It can be seen that strong external links are indispensable for website rankings.

How to add more external links to a website and let the external links keep surging? In fact, it's very simple, as long as you stick to it! The increase in external links follows the same principle as website updates, which requires us to add and update them regularly. We often find such an interesting phenomenon: when we add external links to the website, by monitoring the external links daily, we find that the external links have not increased but even decreased (as shown in the external link monitoring data of the two websites below), or sometimes more and sometimes less, even decreasing every day. Similarly, take the website as an example. We update the content every day, and the content is included on the same day, but the next day we find that the total number of inclusions of the website has decreased, sometimes increasing and sometimes decreasing. I don't know if everyone has noticed this problem. In fact, veteran webmasters basically understand what's going on, while new webmasters are more confused. Precisely because of this phenomenon, many new webmasters think that they are doing useless work. They spend a lot of time creating external links every day, but the result is that the more they do, the fewer external links there are, so they give up doing external links after just a few days.

Here, I want to tell the new webmasters that what you see is a short-term phenomenon, and you don't need to worry about whether your external links have been done. As long as you seriously do the work, the search engine will see your efforts and contributions. You can roughly examine the effect of external links in this way: persist in doing external links for a week or two weeks, and then observe the basic ranking of the website through Baidu's weekly update, and you can see the result. Generally speaking, as long as you do external links, the website ranking will definitely improve to a certain extent. However, the degree of ranking increase depends on how much and the quality of your external links. You don't need to check how many external links have increased through domain queries because some external links cannot be found through domain queries. We only need to look at the ranking effect. Of course, while doing external links, the website must keep updating synchronously, which is the basic point. The three-day external link data of the wedding website and the external link data of the Qingdao SEO blog.

The external links of these websites in the hands of Qingdao SEO are generally done by writing soft articles, so I want to tell the novice webmasters that the best way to do external links is to increase them by writing soft articles. The characteristics of doing external links with soft articles are easy to spread, high-quality external links, the external links made are relatively natural, and are less likely to be judged by search engines as artificial optimization. So the real website optimization is to make the search engine unable to see that you are doing optimization. But the biggest requirement of soft article external links is to test the basic writing skills of webmasters. Therefore, soft article external links are quite difficult to say, quite easy to say, and the key depends on individual writing level. The better you write, the more websites will reprint your soft articles, and the average ones will naturally be reprinted less. So my secret to explosive external links is good soft articles, of course, it is also about sticking to writing soft articles frequently, and then posting them on well-known webmaster networks or forums. If you persist for one or two months, the external links brought by soft articles will increase by thousands or even tens of thousands. With more external links, the website ranking will naturally go up. Believe me, just try it~!
