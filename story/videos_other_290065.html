Selection

Choose a reputable brand sold through normal distribution channels to avoid counterfeit or inferior products.

Look at the appearance: Packaging should be intact, sealed tightly without leakage, and the powder should be white (if it's colored powder, it should be uniform, but it's recommended to buy white powder). When shaking the detergent bag, the product inside should flow freely without lumps.

Read the instructions carefully: Do not believe in vague contents like magical particles or stain removers. Pay attention to specific components, especially those that enhance performance, such as biological enzyme preparations and bleaching agents. These enhancing components often determine the quality differences between detergents.
