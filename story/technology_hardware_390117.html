How to Increase Traffic to Your Online Store

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 13:51:54

1. Diligence

Diligence refers to our passion for opening a store. At the beginning, there are no regular customers and no fame. So, we first need to improve the internal aspects of our online store, promote it more, and not give up on any customer. Even if they just come to inquire, they might buy your product someday.

2. Brainstorming

Brainstorming means constantly finding problems during the operation of the online store. Our online store should make good use of search engines. Most internet users will find products through search engines. For example, if someone wants to buy [mobile phones], they may search for [mobile phones] on the search engine. You can check the ranking of your product names by searching them. If the ranking is not good, ask yourself why the keywords of your product rank so low. Here, it is recommended that the online store program should preferably generate HTML pages.

Every time you upload a product, you can query the keywords of your product through Baidu keyword search. Emphasize one point: don't think of single names. For example, [digital camera]. It's almost impossible to rank in the top 3 pages in the short term. So, we can try [Sony Digital Camera Latest Price]. In this way, this product introduction page might bring some sales volume. The more products you have, the better the effect will be because the more products you have, the more likely search engines will index them, bringing more traffic and increasing sales.

3. Persistence

Many people who open online stores can't continue after a few months or half a year, mainly because they can't earn much money at the beginning and can't persist. Usually, people start with idealistic thoughts when opening an online store, but once reality deviates from their ideals, they easily have the thought of giving up.

In fact, within these six months, you already have some customers. If your after-sales service is good and your product quality passes, you will have returning customers. Of course, customers will also introduce each other, which will significantly increase your sales. Opening a successful online store (www.mideso.com) cannot be achieved overnight or with a few words. Just look at real-world stores; most rely on returning customers or introductions between customers. They impress customers with their service, so we should treat our online store with a peaceful mindset, which often brings unexpected results.

4. Inform Family and Friends

If you have opened an online store, do your family know? Do your classmates and friends know? You must inform them immediately. Because if you tell 100 people, each person will tell more people, spreading the word rapidly. There will be countless people who will know about your store in a short time. You can leave messages in alumni records and let your family and classmates share the joy of opening an online store with you. Don't worry about not having friends; even a hitman has elementary school classmates.

5. Correctly Exchange Friend Links

After running your store for some time, you can privately contact others to exchange friend links. By exchanging store links, you can form a small network that enhances mutual influence. Try to choose stores that are not in the same category as yours. On one hand, there will be no competition, and on the other hand, it can mutually promote each other. If the other party's store name is too long, affecting the overall aesthetics of your store, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., you can appropriately modify the other party's display name. This method can bring a certain amount of traffic to your online store.

6. Reasonably Plan Product Release Time

Releasing products has its own set of rules. It can be exaggeratedly said that releasing products outside peak hours is equivalent to not releasing them at all. When is the peak hour? According to statistics, it is: 10:00-11:00, 14:00-17:30, 19:30-22:00.

Some people say that the number of products should vary from person to person, but I disagree. Isn't more quantity better! More is good as long as they are not repetitive. The advantage of more products lies in showcasing your strength. Let me explain; buyers find products by entering keywords. Taobao's natural sorting is based on remaining time. Generally, buyers only browse the first three pages. If there are many of your products on these three pages, I don't believe they won't visit your store.

Let me give an example: If you release many products during peak hours today, then a week later, during the peak hours of that day, many of your products will appear on the first three pages. Of course, the duration should be set to seven days, don't be lazy. If you do this every seven days, then during the peak hours every day, your products will be on the first three pages! (You need to release products over these seven days).

Pricing products also requires attention (cannot disrupt market order; Taobao is very proficient). Buyers often use 'price from low to high' sorting. If your product lacks price advantage, when buyers sort, you will be left out.

Product descriptions should not be overlooked.

Product descriptions are closely related to store decoration. I'll discuss both here. Product descriptions give customers their first impression. The name should be practical, not just nice-sounding. You have 30 characters; make sure you use them well; don't waste them.

Product descriptions should include as many physical pictures as possible, detailed features, and necessary precautions, reducing future trouble.

When describing products, pay attention to font size and color, making it easy for customers to see (if I can't see clearly, I'll mark it with an X).

Store decoration should be as aesthetically pleasing as possible, attracting customers and making them feel professional and secure.

If store owners engage in joint promotions, they can benefit mutually, not only increasing store traffic but also improving cash flow. If several sellers collaborate on joint promotions, different effects can be achieved. Try to find complementary stores. For example, a photography book specialty store and a digital camera specialty store could cooperate. Hang recommendations for each other's products on their respective product pages. 8. Take the Initiative, Seek Buyers

Business isn't waiting around. Often, we can seek buyers. Some buyers prefer to use purchase requests, so you can frequently check the purchase request marketplace to see if they're looking for items you sell. Visit relevant forums and learn about others' interests through their articles. Finding the right people means success is halfway there. Fully understand what you operate, then research who would need them. Avoid blindly posting advertisements (which is often against forum rules).

9. Participate in Activities, Generate Traffic

Some specialized forum communities serve as our stage. Read posts carefully and reply thoughtfully. Reading posts increases knowledge, and the benefits of replying don't need to be repeated. (Just a reminder: Don't post aimlessly or take too long to get to the point. Be detailed and clear. Don’t confuse readers.) Forums host various activities daily, so stay updated on the latest dynamics. www.mideso.com Essay contests, charity sales—you can sponsor prizes or donate. This is similar to manufacturers sponsoring sports events—a win-win situation. Be active, letting more people know you. (Be familiar with Taobao's rules.)

Join Business Alliances, Teamwork Progresses

Business Alliances are freely formed by sellers from the same city or category. First, apply to Taobao. Once approved, you can appear as a collective entity. Joining alliances offers many benefits—meeting many sellers, periodic gatherings or training exchanges organized by the alliance leader, which is the fastest growth method for newcomers. Taobao's homepage also has a dedicated Business Alliance page, providing more exposure opportunities.

10. Print Business Cards

Print your own business card. When mailing products to buyers, consider inserting a few cards. If they're appealing, they might be collected or recommended to others. If you have a physical store, place some cards there for others to take. When they leave your store, they might continue browsing online, and vice versa.

11. Use Forums and Blogs

Forums hide many potential buyers, so don't ignore their role. Remember to set your avatar and signature properly, making them attractive. Pair them with good posts, whether original or replies (mutual benefits!). Others will notice you. www.mideso.com Share your business experiences, the bittersweet moments of Taobao, the joys of reading and listening to music, etc. Regularly update your signature, keeping others informed of the latest policies in your store.

Nowadays, every website has forums, and anyone can apply for a blog—it’s free advertising!

13. Utilize Search Engines

Perhaps you've never thought that someone could find your store through a search engine. Some opportunities must be seized.

Frequent Baidu users might notice a section called "Tieba," which is essentially a forum. Unlike typical forums, Baidu Tiebas are mostly self-generated. Every word can become a Tieba.

When you enter terms like "8210", "No Thieves in the World", or "hand-drawn" in Baidu, corresponding Tiebas will appear on the right side. If none exists yet, you'll be prompted to create one. If it already exists, the latest three posts will be displayed.

14. Email Signatures

Email advertising is popular now, but avoid mass emails—they're easily blacklisted or reported. That's not a scare tactic.

Add a signature to your emails and change it regularly. Recipients (friends) might be unintentionally attracted. I once added signatures to text messages I sent to friends, nearly frustrating them (later I stopped).

15. Unique Strategies

During university, there was a small barbecue shop nearby with a savvy owner who knew how to operate and advertise. Each city has radio song-request programs, and their owner frequently called to request songs, always adding: "Welcome to Xiao Mao Barbecue, located at...; phone number is..." A call costs only a few cents, but everyone listening to the program hears this advertisement. Compared to those spending money on radio ads, this is cost-effective. Consider whether there are resources around you that can be utilized.

16. Fun Advertising

A friend from a neighboring school told me: There were two pancake vendors at their school gate. One always shouted slogans, while the other adopted a different strategy—he got a blackboard and wrote: "Pancake Fruit Silence is Golden"; around the New Year, his blackboard read: "Pancake Fruit Remember to Go Home"; for a while, he disappeared, and when he returned, his blackboard read: "Pancake Fruit Re-emerges..."

When selling something that doesn't differ fundamentally from others, you can advertise uniquely. If one day I see him and his blackboard on the street, I would definitely buy a pancake just to reminisce about the stories he's shared over the years.

17. Personal Space

The new version of personal space has many functions, so use it fully. Its role is second only to its community. This is your platform to showcase yourself, and many customers come because of your personal space.
