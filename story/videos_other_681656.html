Female worker developed mental illness after being beaten when asking for salary, but the boss is still nowhere to be found

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-28 14:44:28

In the Spring Festival of 2012, Xiaojuan's whole family spent their time at Xianyue Hospital. Every day, her mother would talk to Xiaojuan a lot. However, no matter how Xiaojuan's parents called out to her, she always shrunk in the corner of the bed, with a blank look, and showed no reaction at all.

However, in early January this year, Mr. Li, when he first accepted an interview, said that he had already covered a significant amount of medical expenses and was not "irresponsible."

As Xiaojuan's hospitalization period extended, the medical bills accumulated. Although Mr. Li continued to pay, his attitude was no longer "proactive". Moreover, Xiaojuan's condition did not improve at all. Nobody knew how long she would need to stay in the hospital. However, each day of Xiaojuan's hospitalization cost several hundred yuan.

Mr. Li's impulsive action changed not only his own fate but also Xiaojuan's. Now, he faces a substantial compensation claim. If Xiaojuan stops treatment, her condition will worsen.

Southeast Network - Strait Herald reported on February 4 (by reporter Chen Jie, text and pictures): "Save Xiaojuan! If this child does not receive timely treatment now, her life may be ruined." Yesterday, Xiaojuan's mother informed the reporter that before the Spring Festival, the boss who hit Xiaojuan stopped covering her medical fees.

So far, Xiaojuan has been hospitalized for over 100 days. However, Xiaojuan's mother said: "Now it seems like Mr. Li has disappeared. His mobile phone is off; when we go to his house, he's not there; when we go to the factory, we can't find him."

However, after paying the medical expenses several times, Mr. Li stopped covering the costs just before the Spring Festival. Currently, Xiaojuan has been hospitalized at Xianyue Hospital for over 100 days and still cannot recognize her parents.

Yesterday, the reporter repeatedly dialed Mr. Li's phone number, but it was always "off."

Currently, Xiaojuan owes more than 6,000 yuan in medical expenses, and her treatment situation is urgent. Previously, the Herald published an article titled "Female Worker Beaten into Mental Illness While Seeking Wage?" which introduced the case of Xiaojuan, an external female worker who developed mental illness after being beaten by her boss. After being hospitalized for over a hundred days, she could no longer recognize her parents. Xiaojuan's plight has drawn attention from many kind-hearted people. Many readers called the Herald and contacted Xiaojuan's family to express their care.

Currently, Xiaojuan's family has filed a lawsuit against Mr. Li. However, legal proceedings take a long time, while Xiaojuan's treatment cannot be delayed.

Xiaojuan's outstanding medical expenses have exceeded 6,000 yuan, and her treatment situation is urgent.
