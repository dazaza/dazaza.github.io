The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge brings business opportunities, HK domain names soar in value

by now on 2009-12-25 08:57:28

December 15th, the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge project started. This bridge spans 35.6 kilometers across the sea and is nearly 50 kilometers long. The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge connects Hong Kong, Macao with the western part of Guangdong Province, directly incorporated into the national expressway network, becoming an important transportation artery of Southwest China. In the future, it will take only half an hour to travel from Zhuhai to Hong Kong. At that time, it will present greater economic vitality between the mainland and Hong Kong and Macao, and make land travel between the mainland and Hong Kong and Macao more convenient.

The establishment of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge not only brings historical opportunities for development to the west shore of the Pearl River Delta but also opens up a broader prospect for the vast number of enterprises in the mainland to enter the Hong Kong market. Under the impetus of convenient transportation and policy promotion, in the near future, exchanges and cooperation between Hong Kong and Macao and the mainland will be more frequent, and the development and exchange of the three places will become a huge economic circle. As the Hong Kong domain name .HK, it will play an increasingly important role in the integration of this economic circle. With the establishment of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge and the further close ties between Hong Kong and the mainland, the value of the .HK domain name has soared.

Entering the Hong Kong market, HK domain leads the charge

The author learned from Times Internet (www.now.cn/domain), the best partner of HKDNR, that the .HK domain name is the unified positioning symbol of Hong Kong, with a distinct Hong Kong mark. In recent years, with the increase of Hong Kong-funded companies investing in the mainland and more and more mainland enterprises setting up offices in Hong Kong, the registration market of the .HK domain name has also expanded. A relevant person in charge of Times Internet said that generally speaking, when mainland enterprises want to enter the Hong Kong market, they will pay attention to the application of the .HK domain name. Because the .HK domain name can help mainland enterprises establish a Hong Kong image, which is beneficial for better enterprise development and trade promotion in business dealings.

The establishment of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge stirs up HK domain fever

Nowadays, the establishment of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge further promotes the close ties between the mainland and Hong Kong. Hong Kong, as an international financial center, will present more development opportunities to mainland enterprises, which will inevitably lead to a strong momentum of mainland enterprises entering the Hong Kong market, and the HK domain name will also become an object pursued by enterprises. If enterprises want to open up the Hong Kong market, they must first establish the brand image of Hong Kong. As the domain name of Hong Kong, .HK becomes the first choice of enterprises without any doubt.

Hong Kong and Macao occupy an important position in Asian and even international economy. Their driving force in the development of the Pearl River Delta economic circle is undeniable. With the establishment of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge, it will further promote the development of the Pearl River Delta economic circle, and the cooperation and exchange between Hong Kong and the mainland will be closer. The opportunity for mainland enterprises to use Hong Kong to open up overseas markets will also be more. As the unified positioning symbol of Hong Kong, the HK domain name will help enterprises open up a broader market in Hong Kong.
