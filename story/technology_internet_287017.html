Energy conservation and emission reduction are inevitable trends, and instant electric water heaters are expected to become widespread.

Recently, the General Office of the State Council issued a notice on the "Work Arrangement for Energy Conservation and Emission Reduction in 2009", requiring the launch of the "National Action for Energy Conservation and Emission Reduction" focusing on saving oil, electricity, and promoting energy-saving lifestyles among all citizens. Meng Xiangan, deputy director of the Renewable Energy Society of China, pointed out that domestic hot water accounts for approximately 4.5% of total energy consumption. The consumption of domestic hot water is closely related to bathing, and what appears to be an ordinary electric water heater is already tied to the major issue of "energy conservation and emission reduction". Eliminating high-energy-consuming electric water heaters and promoting energy-saving ones have become international consensus. Grimes has long focused on this major national issue. Its instant electric water heater products have abandoned the repeated heating and large-scale waste of hot water by storage-type electric water heaters. With its unique third-generation dynamic thermal circulation system, it offers three different heating modes - instant, rapid, and energy-saving - to meet your temperature needs in different seasons, avoiding unnecessary electrical energy waste.

Compared to instant electric water heaters which require high installation line standards, making them difficult for ordinary households to install, hard to popularize on a large scale, and having issues like low water flow and insufficient water temperature in winter, Grimes' instant electric water heater successfully overcomes these technical problems. A 2.5 square meter dedicated line can be installed in every household, making it easy for everyone to use. Moreover, with a preheating time of 3-8 minutes, it effectively overcomes the technical bottleneck of low water flow and insufficient water temperature in instant models, paving the way for large-scale promotion.

Meanwhile, on May 15, 2009, Grimes' instant electric water heater products (10L\5.5KW) achieved an energy efficiency rating of level 2. Since then, Grimes Electrical Appliances Co., Ltd. has become one of the first companies in the quick electric water heater industry to obtain this energy efficiency rating. It can be said that Grimes' instant electric water heater was born alongside the national plan for energy conservation and emission reduction, and in the future, it will inevitably take the path of popularization.
