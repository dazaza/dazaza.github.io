Weibo user Lu Junqing offers ten million yuan to solicit evidence of charitable fraud to prove his innocence

by edivista on 2011-12-10 11:14:21

Click to enter Lu Junqing's Weibo

On December 7, Lu Junqing, the chairman of the World Outstanding Chinese Entrepreneurs Association and the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Tianjiu Ruchang Investment Group, posted a reward of millions on his Weibo account. He stated that he wanted to gather evidence to clear the smear campaign accusing him of using charity for financial gain.

Since the exposure of the China-Africa Hope Project in mid-August, Mr. Lu and his daughter have faced various criticisms. The report by CCTV's "Economic Half Hour" at the end of November directly accused Lu Junqing of using charity as a pretext to amass wealth. To understand the impact of these events on Mr. Lu and his daughter, a reporter from Tianfu Morning Post interviewed them via email. (Click on the image to enter Lu Junqing's Weibo)

For more than three months, facing various doubts and accusations, Lu Junqing has been like a kung fu master, protecting his daughter who initially sparked the controversy, without revealing any weaknesses; even when facing heavyweight opponents such as CCTV and Southern Metropolis Daily, he still manages to counter each move, appearing calm and composed.

Now, Mr. Lu and his daughter frequently give interviews and almost daily update their Weibo accounts. Their skills in crisis public relations have rapidly improved. Regarding the troubles brought by Weibo, Lu Junqing frankly admitted: Weibo has indeed brought us many positive changes, making us work more rigorously and standardly. We should thank Weibo.

Original post on Lu Junqing's Weibo: [Lu Junqing offers a million-yuan reward to prove innocence!] After the corruption allegations of the China-Africa Hope Project and the false news about the World Chinese Entrepreneurs Association being illegal were exposed, recently another wave of smear campaigns emerged accusing us of using charity to amass wealth. To prove our innocence, we are offering a reward for evidence: anyone who can provide evidence of when and where we used which charity project to amass how much wealth, after being judged by legal departments, will be rewarded with 10 million yuan. My charity is full of sunshine.

Talk about Charity

No distinction between self and others, no national boundaries

After the Guo Meimei incident, the public detested charitable corruption even more. Netizens referred to Lu Xingyu as 'Lu Meimei,' based on their doubts about her involvement in the China-Africa Hope Project. Thus, Lu Junqing's charity became a hot topic on Weibo.

The World Outstanding Chinese Entrepreneurs Association and Tianjiu Ruchang Group are two major organizations under Lu Junqing's name. For the latter, Lu Junqing acknowledged that its main business is conference economics, and considered it a smoke-free industry, nothing wrong with it. The doubts from the outside world mainly focused on the nature of the Entrepreneurs Association and whether it had amassed wealth through the China-Africa Hope Project.

These doubts persisted despite Lu Junqing's multiple explanations. On the evening of November 26, CCTV's "Economic Half Hour" analyzed the operating model of the Entrepreneurs Association and Tianjiu Ruchang Group, interviewing a businessman surnamed Sun from Shandong Province who claimed to have been deceived. The report questioned Lu, accusing him of allegedly amassing wealth under the guise of charity.

In the email sent to Tianfu Morning Post, Lu Junqing stated that he had never served as an advisor to a member surnamed Sun from Shandong Province, and his statements were contradictory.

Apparently aiming to prove their innocence to the public, over the past three months, Mr. Lu and his daughter conducted several relatively high-profile domestic charity activities. A series of activities such as donating to impoverished students in Fenghuang, Hunan, building schoolhouses in Liangshan, Sichuan, and donating love buses were broadcast live on Weibo.

However, both father and daughter denied that this was due to public opinion pressure. Lu Junqing said: We did a lot of charity domestically before, just very low-key, without publicity. Lu Xingyu reiterated: In terms of charity, I do not distinguish between self and others, nor do I differentiate between national boundaries.

Talk about Doubts

Rumors amount to 68 items

Being questioned, denying, being questioned again, and denying again - for more than three months, this scene kept repeating. Lu Junqing posted numerous Weibos and announcements to refute and cry out for justice. Whether it is real or false remains inconclusive to this day.

Media involvement made Lu Junqing a controversial figure. From his education to his rise to fame, his background was thoroughly investigated. Facing the continuous doubts, Mr. Lu and his daughter responded through various means. Lu Junqing's Sina Weibo account, which he opened on August 19, has already been updated nearly 200 times. Most of the Weibo content consists of various clarifications and statements.

Lu Junqing once referred to his daughter as the third greatest injustice in the world after Dou E and Zhao Zuohai. In this interview via email with Tianfu Morning Post, both Mr. Lu and his daughter cried out for justice again. Lu Junqing even said: Historically, loyal ministers like Yue Fei and Yuan Chonghuan were wrongly executed, we may be temporarily unjustly accused, but we have the opportunity to clarify, and we can use the law to protect our rights. We are already very lucky.

Why are there so many grievances? Lu Junqing emphasized to the reporter that our charity is full of sunshine, denying amassing wealth through charity, and believing that all the storms were caused by black hands behind the scenes stirring up trouble. He said: In this storm, according to incomplete statistics, there are as many as 68 rumors about us, and countless media and netizens spread these rumors. More people are just seeking entertainment.

Regardless of whether crying out for justice is effective, having to take time out of work to give interviews makes Lu Xingyu feel exhausted.

Weibo makes us work more rigorously

Before the incident, Lu Xingyu showed off her wealth on Weibo and shared happiness, thus becoming famous overnight. Over the past three months, from frequent micro-interviews at first to now consciously enhancing Weibo interaction, Mr. Lu and his daughter have become increasingly adept at using Weibo.

Although Lu Xingyu frequently interacts with netizens on Weibo nowadays, she claimed during the Tianfu Morning Post interview that she had the idea of shutting down her Weibo account at first. How did a platform for sharing happiness turn into one for insults? But later, she thought that everything she does is good, why should she hide? It doesn't fit my Sichuan girl character. She believed that she was at most just a trigger, and external doubts were premeditated.

Similarly, for Lu Junqing, the storm caused by Weibo has not subsided. Many netizens represented by Zhou Xiaoyun continue to expose his inside stories. But today's Lu Junqing enjoys interacting with netizens, finding joy in communicating with them. Even if there are criticisms, they are not bad things. If there are faults, correct them; if not, improve further.

When talking about the relationship between the China-Africa Hope Project and Yang Lan, Lu Junqing admitted: There were some misunderstandings, we are willing to take related responsibilities, but there was no malicious misuse of others' names.

Speaking of the changes brought by the event, he frankly admitted that Weibo indeed brought us many positive changes, making us work more rigorously and standardly. We should thank Weibo.

Reporter's Notes

Facing the indestructible ones, we remain confused

In this annual revisit of Weibo hotspot events by Tianfu Morning Post, we originally intended to explore the impact and changes of the events on Mr. Lu and his daughter, without intending to continue discussing issues such as the so-called wealth accumulation model and the nature of charity. However, after completing the interview, we still felt confused.

On the surface, the father and daughter were caught in endless rumor debunking and cries for justice. The events might have brought negative effects to them, but in reality, their lives and careers seemed to have undergone no substantial changes, although this cannot prove that the doubts or accusations from the outside world were wrong.

Over the past three months, facing various doubts and accusations, Lu Junqing was like a kung fu master, protecting his daughter who initially sparked the controversy behind him, without revealing any flaws; even when facing heavyweight opponents such as CCTV and Southern Metropolis Daily, he still managed to counter each move, appearing calm and composed.

Some people likened Lu Junqing to an indestructible one, analyzing his extraordinary abilities, taking him as a successful case and learning object. Such voices, which were initially drowned out by curses and doubts, have increased now.

Although the legal verdict on Mr. Lu and his daughter and their career has yet to be determined, will the emergence of admirers mark the beginning of Lu Junqing proving his innocence at the age of 44?

Let us continue to observe.
