The sky remained gloomy. A man was walking on the road with a girl of about 5 or 6 years old. The color of the man's clothes was no longer distinguishable. The little girl's face was covered in sweat and dust, appearing grayish. Only her expressive eyes were looking at the man. "Dad, I'm hungry," the girl said to the man. With a bitter smile on his face, the man took out a hard biscuit from his pocket and gave it to the girl. "Be a good girl and eat it. Dad will buy you some water." The man walked to a small store in front. Standing at the door, he said to a plump boss lady, "Sister Xia, I'd like to buy a bottle of mineral water." The boss lady took out a few bottles of water and stuffed them into the man's arms, saying, "Take them. It's so hot, drink more." The man insisted on giving her a wrinkled five-yuan note. The boss lady sighed, pulled the girl over, and prepared a basin of water to wash the child carefully. Looking at the child, she said, "Nian'er, tell Auntie what you ate today?" The girl happily replied, "Auntie, dad gave me a biscuit and carried me this morning." The boss lady looked at the child and said to the man, "Take care of your health. Nian'er still needs your care." The man silently pulled the girl to his side and said, "Understood. Thank you, Sister Xia," then turned around and left with the girl. Watching the already bent back of the man and the child hopping behind him, the boss lady's eyes became moist. Inside the store, there was a table where a few women dressed provocatively said, "Oh, what's wrong today? Did the crocodile also shed tears?" The boss lady angrily said, "You fox spirits know nothing," and slowly began telling a story...

A young and beautiful woman lay on a hospital bed, her pale face looking at the handsome man in front of her. She said, "Husband, don't bother anymore. We have no money left." The man smiled and said, "It's okay. The doctor said you're almost well. It's about time for me to pick up Nian'er." Slowly turning around, as soon as he stepped out of the ward, this strong man burst into tears. Furniture, appliances, car, house - everything that could be sold was sold. Relatives and friends, all the money that could be borrowed was borrowed. Even his father's final savings for his coffin were given to the man, who told him, "Do your best. Don't let down those who followed you." Walking to the hospital's back garden, the man broke down in tears. "200,000 yuan," the doctor had said. Another 200,000 yuan would cure his wife's illness, but where could he find such an astronomical sum now? After shedding all his tears, he wiped them away and headed towards the kindergarten entrance.

While waiting for the children to be dismissed, an elderly woman asked another woman, "How is your husband doing?" The woman replied with a worried look, "The doctor said he needs a kidney transplant. But where can we find one? Although I can afford it, it's illegal to trade human organs." The elderly woman nodded and said, "Indeed, it's heart-wrenching." The man's eyes lit up, and he approached them, asking, "Big sister, may I discuss something with you?" The woman looked at the man suspiciously, "What do you want?" The man quickly replied, "Don't misunderstand. I'm here to pick up my child too. After hearing your situation, I believe I can help you solve the problem." The woman curiously asked, "What can you do?" The man asked, "Does your husband need a kidney? Can I donate mine?" The woman said, "That's not possible; it's illegal." The man said, "Big sister, let's talk somewhere else." They moved to the opposite side, where the man shared his own story with the woman, saying, "Big sister, let's help each other. Having only one kidney is fine." The woman hesitated for a long time and said, "Let me ask my husband first. Do you have a phone number to contact?" The man bitterly laughed and said, "I have nothing left, all sold clean. Give me your phone number, and I'll contact you tomorrow." The woman gave him her number and said, "Let's contact tomorrow," and they each returned home with their children. The man returned to the hospital with the child, seeing his wife's hope for recovery and the daughter by her bedside, finally showing a bit of a smile.

The next afternoon, the man called the woman. She told him to come to the hospital for a blood type check and then discuss the price. The man excitedly said, "Thank you, big sister. You've saved our family." The woman said, "If it succeeds, you've also saved our family." In the morning, the man met the woman at the agreed place at the hospital. After a series of complex tests and procedures, the diagnosis showed that it was feasible. They went to a coffee shop, where the woman asked, "Name your price." The man thought for a moment and said, "Big sister, my wife needs another 200,000 yuan to be cured. I have no money left. How much can you offer?" The woman smiled and said, "You're very honest. I've heard about your situation. Your willingness to do this for your wife deeply moves me. I'll give you 500,000 yuan. I hope you and your wife can buy a house and furniture when you return." The man tearfully said, "Thank you. I will repay you in the future." The woman said, "No, this price is very fair. We won't take advantage of others' misfortunes. I'll give you 300,000 yuan first, and after the surgery, I'll give you the remaining 200,000 yuan." The woman continued, "Man, you must promise me one thing: don't tell anyone about this matter." The man's face turned red, and the woman smiled and promised.

The surgery was successful, and the transfer was smooth. As agreed, the woman handed the man a 200,000-yuan check and said, "Rest assured and recover. I've arranged a nanny for your wife. Tell her you're going out to work now, and I'll also take care of your child." The man looked at the woman and sincerely thanked her. The man recovered quickly. When he returned to his wife's bedside, he found her complexion had almost returned to normal. He asked the doctor, "How is she now?" The doctor told him, "Good. She can go home to recuperate now. She should fully recover in half a year." The man's worries finally subsided. After asking some detailed precautions, he processed the discharge formalities. The man used the remaining money to buy a second-hand house, which was quite satisfactory. Bringing his wife and daughter to their new home, he thought, the nightmare was over. It was time to start anew.

The man found a job and worked diligently. His wife stayed at home to recuperate. To support his family's survival, the man often worked overtime. One day, the man caught a fever and felt cold all over, so he went to the hospital to get some medicine but didn't pay much attention. However, the medicine didn't work. The man went to the hospital for a check-up and learned that due to insufficient rest after the kidney removal, his wound had become inflamed. Upon hearing this, the man was shocked. He asked the doctor how much it would cost. The doctor said, "This is a medium-level surgery, not too expensive. But I must tell you one thing: your test report shows some unfavorable results." The man asked, "What is it?" The doctor said, "Your future sexual life will be affected." The man silently thought, "For the sake of my wife and family, I am worth it now." Returning home, he told his wife, "I need to go on a business trip out of town. I've hired a nanny to stay with you. Don't worry about anything. I'll be back soon." His wife tenderly looked at her husband and said, "Take care of yourself outside. Don't miss me too much." The man kissed his wife's forehead.

The man went to the hospital and had his father sign the surgery consent form. Two months later, the man was discharged from the hospital and returned home. Seeing his wife and daughter, he smiled contentedly. Something unexpected happened. The man could no longer endure the intimacy between him and his wife. As time passed, a barrier grew between them. The man's constant tolerance led to his wife eventually proposing a divorce. Shocked, the man stared at this familiar yet strange face.

Regarding the beauty benefits of olive oil, the man nodded in agreement. In the division of property and child custody, the man once again allowed his ex-wife to make the choice. Disappointed again, the wife chose the real estate and half of the current funds in the household. The man accepted it, looking at the woman he deeply loved and said, "Take care of yourself."

The man took the girl and the few ten thousand yuan he received, rented a house, and reflected on his wasted half-life while gazing at the child in front of him. Finally, the person who could sell his own organs without hesitation shed tears. The heart truly hurts, why does it hurt so much? It feels like even breathing is difficult. The chest feels like it's being torn open. After crying all his tears, the heart died. But the child still needed care. She was still young, needing warmth and schooling. "I will not put psychological pressure on my daughter," the man gritted his teeth and stood up. Under the sunset, the man's back appeared so towering.

In the blink of an eye, a year had passed. Nian'er started first grade. Watching her gradually grow up, the man finally felt a sense of relief. One day, he took his daughter to the mall to buy clothes. Just as they arrived at the entrance, they encountered his ex-wife. Nian'er happily shouted, "Dad, look! It's mom!" The man looked at the woman now adorned in gold and silver jewelry and said, "You... Are you doing well now?" A man of about fifty with a bald head got out of the car and stood beside her, asking, "Who is this?" The woman's eyes showed disdain and said, "This is my ex-husband." Then she added to the bald man, "Don't look at him; he's useless." The bald man leered and said, "Still, I'm better, right? Ha ha." This shameless couple laughed mockingly at the man as if no one else was around. Turning around, they got into a Honda Accord and drove off, leaving behind a trail of laughter.

"Dad, what's wrong with you?" Nian'er anxiously asked her father. The man's face turned blue, his lips purple, and his hands trembled. "Heaven, what have I done wrong? Why does fate punish me this way?" The man's already wounded heart was stabbed again by his wife.

After returning home, the man went crazy drinking and smoking, often muttering to himself. Gradually, people noticed that he was no longer quite normal. Only Nian'er's words could reach him and make him listen.

"What happened later?" Several women described as "fox spirits" by the boss lady were already in tears asking. Later...

One day, snowflakes were falling outside. Nian'er rubbed her frozen red hands and said to the man, "Dad, I'm cold and hungry." The man mechanically took out some money, bought a bottle of wine, a loaf of bread, and a pack of peanuts. On his way back, a minivan came around the corner. Despite braking, the ground was already covered in snow. With a bang, the man was hit and thrown aside. Fortunately, the car wasn't moving fast. The man fearfully watched the car while picking up the scattered bread and peanuts. Two tall men with buzz cuts got out of the car, looked at it, and said, "Damn, that was close. He should be fine." The one with a cigarette in his mouth even laughed and said, "Looks like he's fine." And just like that, they drove off confidently.

Back home, the man gave the bread to Nian'er. "Dad, your head is bleeding," Nian'er asked. The man touched it, saw the blood on his hand, and said nothing, lying down on the bed. Nian'er ate the bread while doing her homework. The teacher required the students to write a diary titled "My Parents." Finishing the other homework, Nian'er tilted her head, thinking, "What does mom look like?" Mom's image was already blurred in Nian'er's mind. Watching her coughing dad lying in bed, Nian'er brought in a basin of water, mixed it with hot water, and gently wiped her dad's face and hands. She tucked the blanket around him, washed herself, checked the doors and windows, turned off the lights, and obediently climbed into her small bed to sleep.

In the morning, Nian'er woke up early, pushed the man awake, and said, "Dad, I'm going to school." The man fumbled for a one-yuan note and gave it to his daughter, hoarsely saying, "Nian'er, buy something to eat." Nian'er bought two fried dough sticks and a cup of soy milk, placed one stick and the soy milk on the small cabinet by her dad's bedside, picked up her schoolbag, and ate while walking to school. "Auntie, good morning," Nian'er cheerfully greeted a woman she saw. The woman looked at Nian'er, dressed thinly, and asked, "Nian'er, are you going to school? It's so cold, why aren't you wearing more clothes?"

Nian'er happily said, "Dad promised to buy me new clothes in a few days." The woman called Nian'er over, combed her hair, and said, "Wait a minute, Auntie will find you a piece." The woman found a relatively new down jacket from her younger daughter's clothes and smiled, saying, "Nian'er, call Auntie again, and I'll dress you in pretty clothes." Nian'er joyfully jumped and called, "Auntie, Auntie!" The woman dressed Nian'er in the down jacket, took out a fifty-yuan note from her pocket, and gave it to Nian'er, saying, "Keep this money, take it home to your dad." Nian'er hesitantly said, "Auntie, dad doesn't allow me to accept money from others." The woman said, "Stupid child, we can't accept money from others, but I'm your aunt. Listen to me," and shoved the money into Nian'er's pocket. "Go to school, don't be late. Study hard, or Uncle Xiaogang will spank you." Nian'er said, "I understand, Auntie. I'm going."

Just as she reached the school gate, she saw the person she feared, Uncle Xiaogang. His eyes were sharp, and despite spending all day online, they were still keen. He didn't have much to do, just playing games to earn a living and helping collect utility fees in the neighborhood. But Nian'er's family's fees were all paid out of his own pocket. Nian'er, Uncle Xiaogang called her over because if he knew she wasn't studying well or was misbehaving, he would definitely pull her pigtails and spank her bottom. "Uncle Xiaogang, good morning," Nian'er flatteringly asked, "Have you eaten?" Uncle asked her, "Yeah, have you eaten?" "Yes," Nian'er replied. "Go to school. I'll buy a stove for your house and install it this afternoon," Uncle said, patting Nian'er's head before leaving. "Thank you, Uncle," Nian'er said. "Hehe, you know how to be polite now," Uncle happily said.

At school, Nian'er started collecting the classmates' homework. She was the class monitor. At noon, after school, she returned home and saw that the man hadn't gotten up yet. Nian'er asked, "Dad, I'm home from school." There was no response. Nian'er was puzzled. What's wrong with dad? Climbing onto the bed, she saw her dad holding a photo of her when she was little with her mom. Looking at her dad's face, the man's complexion had turned gray. His eyes were hollow and open, as if lost in worldly emotions, or perhaps lamenting his grievances. Blood had dried from his ears and mouth. "Dad!" Nian'er's scream attracted Uncle Xiaogang, who lived behind their house. Seeing the man like this, Xiaogang's heart sank. Touching the man's face, it was already cold. Shocked, he asked Nian'er, "Your dad... How did your dad bleed?" Nian'er cried and said, "I don't know. Dad was already bleeding when he came home last night." Xiaogang took out his phone and called the police. In a short while, the house was filled with uncles and aunts, all anxiously asking what happened. An aunt said, "I saw him picking things up on the ground last night. There was a car in front. Was he hit?" "Did you see the license plate number?" Lin Grandpa, who retired from public security, asked. "No, it was too dark and snowy. I didn't notice," she replied. Alas, the police arrived, took a few photos, and asked how it was discovered. The room was in chaos. At this moment, Nian'er sat inside the bed, tightly holding her dad's hand, asking, "Dad, where does it hurt? Why don't you speak?" A sentence echoed, and almost everyone in the room was in tears. Sister Xia hugged Nian'er, wiping her tears and said, "Nian'er will live with me from now on. I can't let this child suffer any longer." Everyone wanted to raise Nian'er. Actually, everyone had been taking care of the father and daughter pair all along. But seeing the current situation, everyone wanted to use their abilities to care for this extremely suffering child. Watching her father being carried out of the room wrapped up, Nian'er cried out, "Don't take my dad away! I'll be good from now on! Don't take my dad away!" That voice... really cut deeper than a knife. Lin Grandpa ran home and took out two thousand yuan, placing it in Sister Xia's hands, saying, "Niece, take good care of her. If you need more money, let me know. I'll sell my house to help take care of Nian'er." Sister Xia pushed the money away, sat on the ground, and cried out loud, "Even when my deadbeat husband passed away, I wasn't this heartbroken. Heaven, why don't you open your eyes and take a look?" In an instant, men and women of all ages in the neighborhood were in tears. The man was always nice in everyone's hearts, liked to help neighbors, and even sold his kidney to save his wife's life. Who would have thought that such a man would end up like this? At the time of his death, no one knew why he still held the photo. Was it because he couldn't let go of his wife? Or his daughter? Or both...?
