Shixiwa is a newly developed famous, special, and high-quality product that has attracted widespread attention from all sectors of society. It is gradually gaining popularity in our country. In recent years, its breeding technology has also made significant progress, especially the breakthroughs in pond domestication farming and factory seedling cultivation, which have laid a good foundation for promoting Shixiwa farming. To actively promote the development of Shixiwa farming, we have based our efforts on our own breeding experience and absorbed the latest research results of domestic peers. We currently hold a leading position in technologies such as artificial seedling cultivation, live bait culture, live frog transportation, and disease prevention and treatment. The farming technology of Shixiwa is just emerging, with a long farming cycle requiring more than two years before they can be sold. Additionally, it requires very strict conditions, and only a few people have such farming conditions. Many who want to farm are restricted by the lack of technology and farming conditions.

With the development of the economy and the improvement of people's living standards, people's demand for food has risen to the level of nutritional value. According to nutrition experts, loach has extremely high nutritional value and has been hailed as aquatic ginseng, increasingly appearing on dining tables. Shixiwa is China's largest edible frog, with fat legs, adult frogs weighing between 250-400 grams, and some reaching over 500 grams. Shixiwa meat is fresh and delicious, cooling and nourishing, rich in various vitamins and minerals, and contains high protein. Currently, most of the Shixiwa in the market still relies on wild capture for supply. With social development, people have higher requirements for material life, especially in terms of food. Chinese people know how to eat; eating creates emotions, and emotions arise from eating. Moreover, there is an increasing demand for good quality, original ecological, wild, gamey, rural, and green foods.

The development and utilization of Shixiwa resources have broad market prospects. Currently, Shixiwa on the market mainly relies on the capture of wild frogs, causing wild resources to decrease, and market prices continue to rise. If this continues, it will severely damage Shixiwa resources and ecological balance. Conducting artificial Shixiwa farming aims to reduce the capture of wild resources and restore wild resources, forming commercialized production to meet the market's growing demand for wild meat. Shixiwa primarily feeds on live insects such as mealworms, great wax moths, and locusts. Tadpoles mainly consume algae and decaying leaves. This is a highly efficient farming project with low investment, low cost, low risk, and high benefits. Developing Shixiwa resources is a new way for mountainous areas to get rid of poverty and revitalize economic growth, with important practical significance.
