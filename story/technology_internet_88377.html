He focuses his energy on refining his works, rather than wasting it on fame and profit. This is the key factor for him to write good works.

8. Realm - From Fo Ben to Heishan and then to Dragon Snake, it can be seen that Shen Ji's understanding of nature and the world is far beyond some children who pretend to be profound and mature after reading a few novels.

Ranking Reason: What is a fantasy novel? It is said that someone deeply addicted to fantasy novels might exhibit the following symptoms: wanting to light cigarettes with fire magic, trying to condense water elements while washing face, wishing they could use space magic while waiting for the bus, frequently posing to try to condense magical power, going to famous mountains not for tourism but to find legendary cultivators, and suspecting that there might be a ninth-level or higher phantom beast inside eggs...

Therefore, the first thought of every author is to make their novel more attractive, as if the reader were wandering within the story.

Science fiction writer Ye Yonglie once said that science fiction, magic fiction, and fantasy fiction are the three main types of speculative fiction. This classification method has gained widespread acceptance. Speculative fiction refers to a series of stories that occur under hypothetical conditions. The difference lies in: science fiction emphasizes scientific basis, with Mary Shelley's "Frankenstein" as the first representative work; magic fiction is based on mythology, with Western works like "The Lord of the Rings" and "Dragonlance" as representatives; fantasy fiction has recently become popular in mainland China, built on imagination, going further and freer than magic fiction, unrestricted by scientific evidence, allowing more room for creativity. Huang Yi can be considered a representative of modern Chinese fantasy fiction. Today, the number and variety of Chinese fantasy novels have already encompassed all these elements.

And the book "Coiling Dragon" is moving forward in this direction, deserving to be called a classic of fantasy novels, isn't it?
