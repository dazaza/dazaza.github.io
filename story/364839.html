Zhong Ao Da Chao: the task of sports work is very arduous

by ideas on 2010-09-17 10:14:04

According to news from the General Administration of Sport: recently, under the coordination of the General Administration of Sport, public security organs have legally initiated investigations into Xie Yalong, Li Dongsheng, and Wei Shaohui. This is a continuation of the thorough rectification of the football circle and the legal crackdown on fake matches, gambling, and corruption in football; it is also an important part of the General Administration of Sport's vigorous rectification of competition conduct and anti-doping comprehensive management work.

This action has been legally carried out since last year through close cooperation between the General Administration of Sport and the public security department. Meanwhile, the administration has carried out anti-corruption education and rectification work in the football industry, causing a significant stir in society.

The General Administration of Sport's attitude towards legally cracking down on criminal behavior involving match manipulation and gambling has always been very clear. It firmly opposes fake matches, black whistles, and gambling, actively promoting judicial intervention, striving to create a fair, just, and healthy football development environment. With a firm determination to punish corruption and rectify the football circle, the administration has made decisive decisions, acted swiftly, firmly supported, and actively cooperated with the public security department in investigating cases, achieving some positive results. At the same time, in response to the serious problems exposed in the football field, the administration has taken a series of measures to carry out special governance for education and rectification in the football world. Taking this as a warning, it applies lessons learned to other areas, further strengthening party conduct and clean government construction, continuously increasing the intensity of anti-corruption education and rectification work.

In this rectification work in the football circle, the General Administration of Sport has always focused on education and salvation for cadres who have encountered issues. It consistently adheres to the principle of striking against a minority while educating and saving the majority, explaining policies, making clear the consequences, with painstaking efforts and utmost sincerity. However, if after repeated education and salvation there is still no self-reflection or redemption, once the public security department determines illegal behavior, involved personnel will certainly be dealt with seriously, without shielding, without leniency, and without allowing lingering problems. Regardless of who is involved, all will be handled according to law and discipline.

In this special operation conducted closely in cooperation with the public security department to legally crack down on criminal behavior involving match manipulation and gambling, many staff members of the Football Sports Management Center and the Chinese Football Association have already been legally arrested or investigated. These individuals involve the main leaders, middle-level cadres, and staff of the center and association. This fully reflects that the internal environment and state of football have deteriorated to a shocking degree.

Match manipulation and gambling-related criminal behavior is a tumor in the body of Chinese football. It corrupts sports ethics, harms society, and violates criminal law, and must be resolutely eradicated. Its harm lies in: first, it severely affects the healthy development of Chinese football. Fake matches, gambling, and corruption in football harm the reputation of our football, restrict its development, constrain the improvement of the national team level, damage the image of Chinese football, and destroy the market and social environment for football development. Second, it tarnishes the image of Chinese sports. Chinese sportspeople have worked hard for decades, forming the spirit of Chinese sports, bringing great honor to the people and the country. The outstanding performance of our sports delegation at the Beijing Olympics and this year's Vancouver Winter Olympics gave the nation immense encouragement. As a highly watched sport, football not only has a very low level but also constantly encounters problems in the league, severely tarnishing the good image of Chinese sports and desecrating the efforts and achievements of several generations of Chinese sportspeople over decades. Third, it seriously hurts the feelings of the general public. Match manipulation and gambling hurt the feelings of hundreds of millions of fans and the general public who care about and love football. Some abnormal phenomena on the field have caused immense dissatisfaction and indignation among the people. Fourth, it corrupts social morals and affects social stability. Some football practitioners manipulate matches and even gamble in the league, fabricating falsehoods, illegally profiting, deceiving audiences—these behaviors seriously violate sports ethics, challenge healthy and just social values, and violate national laws, thereby causing a series of problems and adversely affecting social stability.

Currently, in accordance with the requirements of the central government, the administration is deeply carrying out the "Strive for Excellence and Innovation" activity within grassroots Party organizations and among the broad membership, combining the current realities of sports work to determine the theme of "Anti-corruption and Diligent Professionalism." The problems that have occurred in the football industry serve as vivid negative case studies, drawing high attention from Chinese sportspeople, touching the thoughts of every comrade, providing everyone with a heart-shaking warning education, serving as a cautionary tale, applying lessons learned to other areas, and sounding the alarm bells long-term.

The tasks of sports work are extremely arduous. In terms of mass sports, we are taking advantage of the major opportunity presented by implementing the "National Fitness Regulations," adopting breakthrough thinking and measures to strive for leapfrog development in mass sports. In competitive sports, the Chinese sports contingent is seeking long-term, sustainable development in competitive sports, fully preparing for the Guangzhou Asian Games and the London Olympics, and orderly conducting nationwide special governance work in sports ethics and anti-doping. Major problems have occurred in football, and advancing the further development of the football cause is equally important as exposing and solving these problems. While continuing to firmly support and actively cooperate with the public security department in legally cracking down on criminal behavior involving match manipulation and gambling, we are focusing on cultivating young reserve talents in football, rebuilding the youth reserve talent system, deeply researching, establishing, and improving policies and measures for managing professional football under the conditions of socialist market economy.

Under the high regard of the Party and the country, Chinese sports have achieved world-renowned accomplishments, which were created by several generations of sportspeople through struggle and hard work. Problems appearing in individual projects and individuals have tarnished Chinese sports, but this cannot negate the efforts paid over decades by several generations of sportspeople, nor can it shake the determination and confidence of the Chinese sports community to build a strong sports nation.
