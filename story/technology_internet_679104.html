Two men were caught for using carpooling as bait to rob and kill three women in three days

We pushed open the heavy iron gate of the detention center and walked into the fully enclosed interrogation room. We saw the afternoon sunlight passing through the iron bars, casting shadows on the slightly pale faces of the two criminal suspects. Their gazes were astonishingly similar — blank, hollow, yet filled with ignorance and bewilderment...

At the age of 30, they should have been in the midst of starting a business or enjoying the warmth of family life, but instead, they found themselves behind bars, falling into the abyss of evil. But who would have known that these two individuals had personally killed three innocent women.

The disappearance of three women caused panic among the townspeople.

At 15:30 on December 19, 2011, Na from Bayan Cagang Su Mu, Ewenki Banner, Hulunbuir City, Inner Mongolia came to Xianghua police station to report that her sister had left home at noon on December 17 and had not returned since. She could not be contacted, and her whereabouts were unknown.

At 11:00 on December 21, 2011, Sun from Beihexiang, Dazhaobeihe Police Station, Ewenki Banner reported that his mother Ma had left Beihexiang early in the morning on December 20 to go to Bayantohai Town for business and had not returned since. She could not be contacted.

At 15:15 on December 21, 2011, Han from Beihexiang Police Station again reported that his mother Shi had left Beihexiang early in the morning on December 19 to go to Bayantohai Town for business and had not returned since.

After receiving the report, the police officers from Xianghua and Beihexiang Police Stations immediately started work and entered the information about the three people into the missing persons information system at 6:10 PM on December 21, starting the search process.

Within three days, three cases of disappearance occurred one after another. The news was like a thunderclap on a clear day, causing the originally peaceful and tranquil small city to become uneasy. The ages of the three missing people ranged from 26 to 49 years old. Were they victims of revenge killing, or had they been lured away? Such bizarre disappearances were incomprehensible. As time went by, the surrounding residents began to become restless, rumors spread, and a sense of fear enveloped the hearts of the people.

Upon hearing the news, the police acted swiftly and carried out comprehensive investigations.

After investigation, it was found that the three missing people did not have any related social connections. Moreover, Na's last phone call with her friend Su sounded abnormal. Given that the residence areas of the missing people were all in Dayan Town, which is basically connected with Yakeshi City, and that the area is mainly dominated by mining enterprises with large population and logistics flows, this case might involve surrounding banners and cities. It would be difficult for the individual police officers of Ewenki Banner Public Security Bureau to handle the case alone. After consultation, relevant personnel from Hulunbuir Municipal Public Security Bureau went to Dayan Town to provide support and assistance.

At 23:15 on December 21, the [12·17] Series Missing Persons Command Center was established. At the same time, seven action groups were formed: the interview group, the search group, the video communication data retrieval group, the missing persons' various financial card withdrawal information retrieval group, the roadblock inspection group, the floating population and ex-convict (with driving skills) and other key personnel investigation group, and the taxi and other non-formal passenger transport vehicle investigation group. These groups conducted investigative interviews, set up roadblocks, retrieved video communications and various financial card information data in the Dayan Town area.

Based on the evidence in hand, the police conducted a large-scale screening of people aged between 20 and 40. In the first week of screening, the police screened more than 80,000 permanent residents, more than 10,000 migrant residents, and over 300 enterprises and individual businesses, but the case did not make any progress.

According to the arrangements of the command center, a group of police mobilized the local security committees and public security joint defense teams to set up strict controls at major intersections and channels within their jurisdiction, carefully checking and verifying vehicles and pedestrians passing by, blocking the escape routes of the suspects. Another group of police conducted comprehensive interviews and screenings of residents around the crime scene and key places such as hotels, bathhouses, rental houses, and internet cafes within their jurisdiction.

After the screening, relevant situations were continuously reflected back to the command center, but each was negated one by one.

Two suspects were arrested, and the truth came to light.

At this point, the special investigation team promptly adjusted its strategy, narrowing down the screening age to between 20 and 35. At 3:00 AM on December 22, based on various clues, the command center comprehensively analyzed the situation and decided to classify the three missing person incidents as the [12·17] series robbery and murder case, and moved the special investigation command center to the Dayan Branch of the Ewenki Banner Public Security Bureau, establishing the [12·17] special investigation team. After careful analysis and research, the special investigation team identified gaps and decided to focus the screening on people aged between 25 and 35.

On December 22, while conducting a screening at a toll station, the police learned that someone had seen the suspect vehicle. The police immediately started investigating the whereabouts of the two suspects. After several hours of work, Wang could not clearly explain his recent whereabouts, so the police took him back to the special investigation team for review.

At 17:00 on December 22, there was a significant breakthrough in the case. The suspects Wang and Zhao "surfaced" and were subsequently arrested.

After being brought to the review team, when faced with irrefutable evidence, Wang confessed to conspiring with Zhao to commit car robbery, murder, and rape.

It turned out that the suspects Wang and Zhao lured three female passengers under the pretense of ride-sharing on the roads near Dayan No.1 Well, robbed them, killed them, and then dumped two bodies in the cellar of two abandoned houses on Dongsheng Street south of the railway and another body in an underground sewage well in a residential neighborhood. They stole over two yuan in cash and four pieces of jewelry. After committing the crime, they sold the stolen items at a certain gold and silver jewelry processing and recycling point in Yakeshi City, obtaining a total of 9,700 yuan in illegal proceeds.

According to the police, both suspects were ex-convicts. Wang had been sentenced to seven years in prison for theft; Zhao had been sentenced to four years in prison for theft and nine years for theft and robbery. Years of imprisonment made them harbor vengeful feelings towards society, leading them to personally create a series of tragic cases. (Han Feng, Wang Ji Yang, Li Ping)
