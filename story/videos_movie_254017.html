Introduction to the Plot of "Palace Heart Strife":

The plot is set against the backdrop of the Tang Dynasty court and tells of the power struggles within the four departments responsible for preparing the Empress's attire. Set in the harem of the Tang Dynasty, it reveals the intricate heartaches and scheming of the women confined there. Charmaine Sheh plays the role of a palace maid, Liu Sanhao, who with her 'three goodness' spirit - having good intentions, speaking well, and doing good deeds - manages to rise above the complex power struggles in the harem. She also develops a romantic relationship with co-star Kenneth Ma that mirrors the romance between Dae Jang Geum and Minister Min.

These four are constantly embroiled in power struggles for higher positions, often dragging the innocent Sanhao and Jinling into their schemes, even making them scapegoats. Despite enduring countless hardships, Sanhao remains steadfast in her 'three goodness' spirit. However, Jinling, after being subjected to much bullying, decides to seek revenge. When both Sanhao and Jinling gain significant influence, their sisterly bond fractures due to their mutual affection for palace guard Gao Xianyang (played by Kenneth Ma).
