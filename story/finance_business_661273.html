In 2006, TVB brought a multitude of series to its avid television fans, including "Dancing in the Desert," "Hard to Be a Woman," "Men's Troubles," "Phoenix Quartet," and "Fat Field Joy." As we step into 2007, the shows that TVB plans to launch have become the focus of attention from all sides. So, take a look at the following program list; are there any actors or series you like?

"Years of风云"

Main cast: Liu Songren, Miao Qiaowei, Wu Weiguo, Lam Fung, Ma Dekui, Ng Cho Kei, Xuan Huan, Charmaine Sheh, Hu Xier, Tang Chui Man, etc.

Next year marks the tenth anniversary of Hong Kong's return. For the first time, Hong Kong Television Broadcasts Limited collaborates with CCTV to produce a 60-episode drama, "Years of风云," which takes the past ten years since Hong Kong's return as its backdrop. It offers a panoramic view of family joys and sorrows, telling the story of the development of China's automobile industry. Whether going global or returning to one's roots, blood ties will always remain unbroken. The series will be filmed in four locations: mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and Canada. It is a cross-regional, 60-episode super production celebrating the tenth anniversary of Hong Kong's return. It is expected to be broadcast simultaneously in mainland China and Hong Kong in October next year.

"True Heart Heroes"

Main cast: Lam Pak-yit, Charmaine Sheh, Lai Lok-yee, Wong Hop-hei, Lam Ka-hua, Tina Tang, etc.

The Immigration Department quietly takes care of every stage of the lives of Hong Kong citizens. This drama reveals the inseparable relationship between the Immigration Department and the general public, as well as the various troubles and joys experienced by its staff. The male protagonist is determined to serve the nation and decides to join the police force but unfortunately fails to do so. Later, he becomes an employee of the Immigration Department and realizes that it is equally important for the citizens.

During his time at the Immigration Department, the male protagonist experiences significant cases such as intercepting gambling syndicates, rescuing Hong Kong people in peril overseas, and searching for internationally wanted criminals. He also reunites with his childhood best friend, who is now a police officer. In their professional capacity, they are like the best partners, but privately, differing opinions test their friendship. Conflicts continue to arise, leaving the two questioning where they should go from here.

"Jade Face"

Main cast: Cecilia Choi, Heidi Kwan, Yeung Siu-kei, Liao Bik-lun, Gai Ming-wui, Ma Dekui, etc.

While the selection contest for Dream of the Red Chamber in Mainland China is in full swing, TVB also plans to launch a drama about Grand View Garden in 2007, titled "Jade Face." However, unlike the Mainland version, this "Jade Face" centers on the most controversial character in Grand View Garden, Wang Xifeng. As the manager of a large family, Wang Xifeng often uses the phrase "big families have big problems" as her motto. Despite being ruthless in dealing with matters yet adept at handling relationships, she gains favor with Grandma Jia while instilling fear in others. Still, she has the skills to maintain the Jia family's reputation and glory.

Cecilia Choi will portray Wang Xifeng. On the surface, Wang Xifeng appears noble and resourceful, supporting half of the Jia family, but behind the scenes, she faces compromises and sacrifices as a woman. Cecilia Choi will depict the rise and fall of Wang Xifeng's life alongside the changes in the Jia family. Female stars like Liao Bik-lun and Yeung Siu-kei will play the roles of the "sisters" in Grand View Garden. Among the many leading actresses, Cecilia Choi exudes the most commanding presence, and her ancient costume is the most striking, potentially repeating the success of "Dancing in the Desert."

"Heartstorm"

Main cast: Chris Tang, Huang Zongze, Lam Fung, Mona Man, Li Sik Chi, and Mi Xue, etc.

"Family ties" are the foundation that binds a family together, but modern people often focus on monetary benefits and undervalue family bonds. A dried seafood shop and a large family lead to a storm over inheritance due to financial interests.

The Tang family owns assets worth six billion dollars. Family members get along harmoniously, making it an enviable household. Tang Zian is the adopted son of the Tang family and shares a close bond with his adoptive father, Tang Renjia. He fully dedicates himself to managing the family business. Initially, the family was filled with filial piety and brotherly love, but after the secret of Zian being a bastard child is revealed, tensions begin to arise within the family. And Renjia's death ignites a battle over the inheritance.

"Glistening Pearl"

Main cast: Cecilia Choi, Ada Lau, Sally Yeh, Lam Pak-yit, Chris Tang, Huang Zongze, Chung Kar Yan, Wong Hee, Guan Lijie, etc.

The new drama "Glistening Pearl" explores the world of diamonds and jewelry, bringing out the beauty and brilliance of life. Cast member Huang Zongze expressed excitement about this drama. He said, "Because this series will be filmed in South Africa and Qingdao. I haven't been to South Africa yet, so it will be great to combine work with travel and enjoy the local scenery."

Set against the backdrop of the diamond and jewelry world, the drama conveys that whether life is brilliantly dazzling and eternal depends on whether a person possesses noble qualities. Even if they undergo several trials, they will eventually be refined like diamonds into a perfect and shining life. The drama will be filmed in South Africa and Qingdao, featuring beautiful people, stunning landscapes, and touching human stories to capture the hearts of the audience. An 80-episode long drama centered on commercial competition, it is one of the highlights of next year and is worth looking forward to.

"Breakout Action"

Main cast: Marcus Ma, Ng Cho Kei, Liao Bik-lun, Flora Xu, Yeung Siu-kei, Mi Xue, Yue Wah

Peripheral groups cause considerable harm, leading to illegal lending, smuggling, drug trafficking, and arms trading. The protagonists overcome these peripheral groups with indomitable spirit.

Gao originally had a good family, dedicated to upholding justice. However, when his father was exposed for betting on peripheral horse racing and subsequently committed suicide, Gao's friend began investigating these peripheral groups and mysteriously met an untimely death. Gao then worked at a fashion store, discovering that the higher-ups might be the masterminds behind the deaths of his father and friend. Gao provided information to the police, who requested him to infiltrate the group as an undercover agent. When Gao almost exposed his cover, he was helped by his ex-girlfriend Ying, only to find out that Ying, who had kept her distance from him, was actually an undercover police officer.

"The Most Beautiful Seven Days"

Main cast: Kenneth Ma, Huang Zongze, Zhou Liqi

The original cast of "Lovers Under the Sky" creates another romantic classic, accompanied by beautiful scenery, portraying a fresh, romantic, and deeply moving love story.

Every girl dreams of a prince charming, but can fairy tales come true? As the saying goes, "Love makes even water sweet." Faced with love and bread, how do we choose? A girl longing for romance finally meets her ideal partner but discovers his hidden secrets. A boy obsessed with wealth finds true love but is pursued by a wealthy woman, offering him a chance to rise rapidly. In the scenic Tuen Mun Bay, a romantic love story quietly unfolds.

"Police Academy Patrol"

Main cast: Joey Yung, Gigi Lai, Tony To, Ng Cho Kei, Chen Jianfeng, Flora Xu, Guan Zhibin, Shen Yingting, Ng Hok Kong, Yang Yi, Situ Ruiqi, Cai Zijian, Deng Zhifeng, Chen Guobang, Chen Kaiyi, Li Jiasheng, Guo Shaoyun, Qian Jiale, etc.

Continuing the story of "Police Academy Ambition," the trainees finally complete their training and officially become police officers. These rookie cops face numerous issues and challenges ahead that they need to overcome and resolve.

After twenty-seven weeks of training, Wen and Xiang, among others, officially become police officers. Xiang aspires to become a sergeant, strictly adhering to procedures, not seeking merit but avoiding mistakes, hoping to maintain a clean record to aid promotion. On the other hand, Wen believes a good police officer should wholeheartedly serve the citizens, even facing danger without retreating. Their differing approaches lead to conflicts.

"War Horses"

Main cast: Chiu Sau-chiu, Ada Lau, Chung King-fai, Wong Dak-bin, Guan Lijie

Audiences love heroes because their wisdom and bravery inspire us to face adversities in real life. Xin Qiji, skilled in both literary and military arts, his heroic deeds undoubtedly move every viewer.

Heroes emerge during turbulent times, as hardships test the heart. Xin Qiji never forgot his homeland and aimed to restore Central Plains. For this, he abandoned fame and fortune, forsaking personal feelings, leading his troops to surrender to Southern Song. Twice he crossed the river to spy on enemy forces and, driven by conscience and righteousness, ventured alone into enemy territory, killing foes, earning the title of "Flying General."

In literature, Xin Qiji expressed his grand aspirations through poetry, leaving famous verses that illuminate his legendary life.

"Divine Calculations"

Main cast: Marcus Ma, Chen Haomin, Yeung Siu-kei, Yuan Hua, Li Shiyun, etc.

Seeking good fortune and changing destiny is nearly everyone's dream. However, everything happens according to fate. Forcing change may require paying a heavy price.

Tian used the "Tui Bei Tu" to help Zhao Kuangyin ascend to the throne but consequently fell out with his wife, Xin. Yang used Feng Shui techniques to ascend to the imperial throne, resulting in betrayal from allies. Obsessing over divination focuses only on immediate results, causing one to miss the most precious people and moments in life, losing the finest joys, anger, sorrow, and happiness. The drama centers around the "Tui Bei Tu," revealing through the story of people competing for this mysterious book that "embracing fate and cherishing those before you" is the timeless righteous path.

"God Searching Chronicles"

Main cast: Alex Fong, Chung Kar Yen, Chen Haomin, Tam Siu Waan, Guan Duhui, Rebecca Ho

Based on the Chinese classic mythology "Search for Gods Records," combined with visually pleasing animation technology and fantastical artistic expressions, it forms a grand drama involving the realms of immortals, humans, and demons.

Zhi Leizhi accepts her fate, gaining divine energy from her mother, who transformed from a magical herb, becoming semi-divine. As a child, bullied by mischievous kids, she was saved by the Yellow Great Immortal, gaining immense strength. Xi was cursed to sleep uncontrollably but learned unparalleled swordsmanship due to the attachment of the soul of Ganjiang. Three individuals with divine connections encounter a generation of evil demons threatening calamity. How will they use their god-given abilities to destroy the demons and save humanity from suffering?

"Aunties' Brigade"

Main cast: Adenene Che, Tang Choi-man, Yeung Tong, Shaw Tin Ngo, Jiang Dawei, Tang Ning, Ma Guoming

Adenene Che and Tang Choi-man reunite after "Hard to Be a Woman," with Che's appearance drawing much attention. Rumor has it that Choi-man won't play a strongwoman role this time. The combination of Choi-man and Shaw recalls "I Have a Date with Spring," adding Yeung Tong, creating a drama of three women.

Growing up abroad, she maintained a close friendship with classmate Yoyo (played by Yin Ying). After graduating from university, Yoyo's extramarital affair led to her suicide. Xiao Man mistakenly believed Yoyo took her own life due to gossip from a few housewives in the building. Deeply affected, Xiao Man develops hatred towards gossips and returns abruptly from abroad to Hong Kong. She devises a revenge plan against the public housing housewives (Tang Choi-man, Yeung Tong, Shaw Tin Ngo), aiming to disrupt their peaceful lives. Amidst her scheme, she unexpectedly forms a bond with Yoyo's husband, Gan Chenghou (Ma Guoming), eventually developing into a romantic relationship.
