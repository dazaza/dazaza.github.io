"Grafted Marriage: The Complex Love Life of a Married Man"

by jqrxass on 2009-10-12 16:21:48

Gu Mei is a post-80s girl who met the married Qin Zhiyuan due to work relations. They fell in love at first sight. Gu Mei's youth and cuteness made Qin Zhiyuan dissatisfied with his wise but rigid wife and their increasingly bland marriage, so after getting divorced, he formed a new family with Gu Mei, expecting happiness to come as scheduled.

However, the age gap, differences in living habits, and divergent ways of thinking cannot be ignored. Will their marriage be harmonious? Paper marriage, perhaps named for the fragility of the first year of marriage which is like paper, easily broken. Even marriages that seem destined and naturally progressing are as fragile as paper, so what will become of their hastily arranged marriage?

"Grafted Marriage" is another marriage manual written by someone born in the 70s following "Seven Year Itch" and "Family Love". It uses delicate and calm writing to analyze China's subhealthy marriages, dissecting the essence of marriage through the trivialities of daily life and exploring ways to manage it.

Grafted Marriage Part One

The auspicious time slowly passed, nearing the start of the banquet. The host looked helplessly at Qin Zhiyuan. Qin Zhiyuan looked at the host, then at the guests below, and finally placed the microphone in his hand into Gu Mei's hand. He tore off the bowtie around his neck and said to Lin Xiaotian: "Xiaotian, I know you're fighting for your sister, and it seems you won't leave unless you make a big fuss today. Fine, I'll take responsibility for my actions. Whatever way you want to cause trouble, I'll accompany you. If you want to hit me, go ahead. I absolutely won't fight back. But there's one condition: once you've finished hitting me, you must leave immediately."

Grafted Marriage Part Two

Actually, just downstairs, she had pulled Mingming home out of spite towards Qin Zhiyuan. She couldn't face his indecisive look. He could have stayed here to be with the child legitimately, but she insisted on pushing him away. At the same time, she resented him. She never thought that their ten years of feelings were worth less than a single revelry with a young girl. Only two months after their divorce, he hastily remarried, making her feel both disappointed and furious.

Grafted Marriage Part Three

Some people say that men in their first marriage rely on love to face it, while after remarrying, they rely on technique. Qin Zhiyuan had to admit that after going through a divorce, he cared more about his current marriage now. Marriage is not a game. His divorce and remarriage have already made many people look at him differently. If this marriage doesn't work out either, it would be an irreparable flaw in his life.

Grafted Marriage Part Four

Gu Mei stood still, glaring at Qin Zhiyuan. She didn't expect that he would really confront her in front of the whole company. In the past, when there were delays in company meetings, everyone understood because Jinan city frequently experiences traffic jams. A simple explanation was enough, and Qin Zhiyuan usually wouldn't care too much. It seemed clear that he was deliberately making things difficult for her today. Enraged, she walked straight up to Qin Zhiyuan and asked coldly, "Qin Zhiyuan, what do you mean?"
