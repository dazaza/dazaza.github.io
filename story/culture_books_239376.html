"Heartbeat Designer" Encounters the Online "Colorful Ladies Gang"

by jqrxass on 2009-09-17 15:00:18

The protagonist of the novel, Wei Duqing, experiences a dramatic change in his life after his girlfriend of seven years leaves him following an argument. When his mother comes to visit and expects to see his girlfriend, he has to look for a substitute online, only to encounter a group of beautiful women known as the "Colorful Women Gang". In a series of designed encounters involving love and lack thereof, some are what he desires, some are not, and others are impossible for him to refuse... Between love and temptation, between fidelity and lies, the distance is both close and immeasurably far.

In fact, everyone in this world is a heartbeat designer - in designing and being designed, the one who ultimately gets hurt can only be oneself.

This is a baffling story that analyzes the most secret desires of all women;

This is a heart-breaking story that interprets the deepest nightmares of every man's soul.

Part One

Despite my great pain, I even thought about giving up at one point, but since love has ended, the only way is to forget. However, as Wong Kar-wai said, when you want to forget someone, you remember them even more. So the result of me trying to forget her was thinking about her every day. Thinking about it to a certain level made me want to change my environment. Leaving the place where we lived together for many years might bring a new horizon.

There's a saying, "When you reach the end of the water, sit and watch the clouds rise." I can't be as detached as a sage, so I could only escape in a vulgar way. An old advertisement once said, men are fragile too. And I admit that.

Part Two

Sun Datou got angry: "Can you have a bit of ambition? Get up quickly. I've just arranged with a few people to play the murder game at the Shelter near Xiba River. Come and make up the numbers. There are plenty of girls, if you see someone you like, take her home."

I wasn't very interested in such things. A bunch of foolish men and women pretending to be profound, "when it's dark, please close your eyes," and "dare games" and all that. Sun Datou likes to be the judge the most, always leering at the female players' chests. If he fancies anyone, he'll try every method after the game to take her home. Since people nowadays are quite open-minded, he succeeds eight or nine times out of ten, so he enjoys this game immensely, especially when there are strange girls involved, almost addicted.

Part Three

Sese looked around and turned on the bathroom light. I said, "This is the toilet." Just as I was about to remind her that the bedroom is another room, she said, "I want to take a shower. You do whatever you need to do. Your clothes are wet, go change quickly."

Now it was my turn to be surprised. A beauty runs to my house in the middle of the night and without a word wants to take a shower, what does that mean? Looks like I'm having good luck tonight. The feeling at this moment is indescribable, I felt like I was floating, distracted, almost unable to control myself.

Part Four

The more I thought about it, the more interesting it became, and I almost laughed out loud. My mother doesn't have such high awareness; for her, seeing a beauty means seeing a daughter-in-law, so she immediately picked up on Xiao Dan's words and said, "Girl, did I wake you up? Look at me..."

She seemed to want to slap her thigh but finally didn't. Xiao Dan smiled again and said, "Auntie, it's really okay."

I suddenly found that she looked quite innocent at this moment, still sleepy, last night's stars and last night's wind, even yawning makes her seem like a sleeping beauty.
