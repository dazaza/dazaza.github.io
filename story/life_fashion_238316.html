Home furnishings promotions are heating up, with unprecedented discounts

by longersing on 2009-09-15 14:32:48

Toilets originally priced at 2680 yuan are now sold for only 599 yuan, bathroom cabinets originally priced at 4780 yuan are now sold for only 1599 yuan, and showers originally priced at 2180 yuan are now sold for only 699 yuan... With the arrival of the "golden September and silver October", the promotional discount voices of various home furnishing markets have started again. All the marketing strategies adopted by merchants without exception focus on the two words "price". When I visited the market, I found that except for discounts, discounts, and more discounts, it seems that home furnishing promotions have run out of ideas. What kind of secrets are hidden behind these ultra-low prices?

Home furnishing promotions hear price cuts again

With the arrival of "golden September and silver October", the home furnishing market has entered the hot sales period. No matter which mall you enter, all kinds of promotional advertisements are very conspicuous, dazzling. I noticed that most of the content of these advertisements is about price cuts and discounts. Compared with the past, they are too old-fashioned and lack innovation.

At the Fu Anju Home Furnishing Mall, I saw such an advertisement. By purchasing a crazy benefit card for 20 yuan, you can obtain the qualification to participate in the auction activities of toilets and tiles for 1 yuan. At the same time, you can also purchase the products of the opening event. For each product (set) over 1000 yuan, each card can offset 100 yuan of goods payment. In addition, you can get the opportunity to participate in the lottery of special opening products (toilets for 499 yuan each). Not only that, on the opening day, the purchase of sanitary ware is discounted at 7.2%, tiles at 6.8%, additional 9.5% discount for purchases over 3000 yuan, additional 9% discount for purchases over 5000 yuan, and additional 8.5% discount for purchases over 10000 yuan. At the same time, you can draw big prizes and gifts... This is the price-cutting promotion activity launched by the three major brands of Fu Anju during the grand opening and the upcoming re-opening.

Similarly, promotional activities are also in full swing in other home furnishing malls. In places like Gui Zhong and Kuai Huan Building Materials Market, I saw that many brands, big and small, have launched promotional activities. In the Kuai Huan Building Materials Market, a brand of sanitary ware advertised as follows: "Home appliances go to the countryside, supported by the government. Water-saving households, decoration subsidies, replace old toilets, water-saving use ××". Upon entering the store, the discount幅度is staggering. For example, toilets originally priced at 2680 yuan are now sold for only 599 yuan, bathroom cabinets originally priced at 4280 yuan are now sold for only 1199 yuan, and showers originally priced at 1280 yuan are now sold for only 399 yuan. On the other hand, another sanitary ware store offers even greater promotional strength. For example, a four-piece set product (toilet + bathroom cabinet + faucet + shower) originally priced at 8440 yuan is now sold for only 2980 yuan, and so on, the higher the price, the greater the discount. Besides building materials, furniture promotions are also very lively. For example, a premium sofa originally priced at 6800 yuan is now sold for only 2500 yuan; a fashionable dining table originally priced at 4980 yuan is now sold for only 1980 yuan... The heated promotional activities of various home furnishings make the entire home furnishing market seem to be filled with smoke.

When visiting the market, I found that all promotional activities are no more than price cuts and discounts. It can be imagined that in the upcoming "National Day" Golden Week, there will undoubtedly be an unprecedented price competition among the major home furnishing enterprises in Nanning. And in this round of market competition, price cuts have become the trump card for enterprises to grab the market, and price has become the link between enterprises and consumers.

Is low-price promotion a bait?

On the surface, the preferential measures promoted by various home furnishing enterprises are tempting, but it is not easy for consumers to really get such benefits.

Take the bidding of a certain brand of tiles at 1 yuan per piece as an example. A total of 30 square meters of living room tiles are offered. First, consumers must spend 20 yuan to purchase a crazy benefit card to qualify for the bid. Even if they bid successfully within 10 yuan, it is still just the tile quantity for one living room. Then what about other areas? Consumers still need to buy the same brand of tiles at normal prices, which is too "strict" for those consumers who are very sensitive to prices, like to buy cheap products, and pay attention to corporate promotional advertisements.

A four-piece set product (toilet + bathroom cabinet + faucet + shower) originally priced at 8440 yuan is now sold for only 2980 yuan, and a bathtub originally priced at 4980 yuan is now sold for only 1799 yuan. Such large-scale price cuts are considered unimaginable in the industry. According to Mr. Zhang, an insider, in fact, the prices of these products marked at several thousand yuan are not that high. Due to reasons such as lack of product features, outdated styles, and single functions, their sales are not ideal. Moreover, similar products in the market cost only a few hundred yuan. Marking them high and then offering discounts creates an illusion of being very cheap and very worthwhile.

During the interview, I observed that some promotional products were not "conspicuous". Not only were their positions not prominent, but their packaging and promotion were also not as attractive as new products. A sanitary ware brand store advertised that a toilet originally priced at 2680 yuan was now sold for only 399 yuan. According to the address provided on the advertising flyer, I found this store and consulted the salesperson about this special offer toilet. The salesperson told me that consumers must spend more than 1000 yuan on sanitary ware products in the store to qualify for the purchase of this special offer toilet, and there was no stock available. If I really wanted to buy it, I would have to wait for more than a week. Similar situations are quite common. Mr. Zhang, an industry insider, told me that in fact, most promotional products are usually inventory products that have been stockpiled for a long time, and the quantities are not large. Enterprises only use these inventory products as bait to attract consumers to the store, using this marketing strategy to boost the popularity and sales of the store.
