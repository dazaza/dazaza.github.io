Development Prospects of Sunlight Boards

The application of sunlight boards has just begun, mainly in the construction of modern agricultural greenhouses. This is particularly important in Beijing and some cities in Northern China. In greenhouse construction, there are greenhouses entirely built with sunlight boards, greenhouses where only the gable walls are made of sunlight boards, and greenhouses where both the gable and side walls use sunlight boards. In terms of greenhouse functions, there are production-focused greenhouses, commerce-focused greenhouses mainly used as flower trading centers, and research-focused greenhouses. These greenhouses partially or entirely use sunlight boards as covering materials, further improving the ecological environment within the greenhouses.

In an annual market of 50 million square meters of sunlight boards, 20% is used for agricultural greenhouses; in North America and Europe, 70% of the 60 million square meters of sunlight boards annually is used for agricultural greenhouses. China is a major agricultural country with a large population and limited land resources, so developing modern agriculture and constructing factory-efficient agricultural greenhouses is a fundamental direction for agricultural development. As the fourth-generation covering material for agricultural greenhouse construction, sunlight boards will have very broad applications. The cross-shaped sunlight boards are widely used in curtain walls. [www.szqpjc.com]
