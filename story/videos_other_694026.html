Cold winter is coming, beware of the "static electricity" in your hair

by cshelive9520 on 2012-03-06 21:35:51

Beware of "electric" hair in the cold winter

In the cold and dry season, static electricity can happen at any time! It can interrupt an elegant handshake with others, but the most annoying thing is that your hair may "fly" due to static electricity. When static electricity has become the obstacle for you to become a handsome man or a beautiful woman, you must take up arms to fight back!

With the cold winter coming, beware of "electric" hair:

The root cause of static electricity in hair

In fact, the root cause of static electricity in hair is the lack of moisture in the hair strands, making them not smooth and hydrated enough.

In winter, the moisturizing factors in the hair will naturally decrease, and the cold wind will "blow away" the water and oil from the skin, leaving the delicate scalp unable to fully nourish the hair. When the moisture content in the hair drops below 10%, various signs of dryness will immediately appear - static electricity, falling hair strands, split ends. Even oily or neutral hair types can become dry and lose luster due to the decreased secretion of sebaceous glands, and even lead to increased hair loss. Therefore, hair care in autumn and winter should focus more on daily scalp and hair care, keeping the hair hydrated all the time is your top priority.

Rehydrating the scalp is the key

There are so many hair care products on the market, which one is more suitable for use now?

In fact, most hair care products can only hydrate the hair strands, neglecting the nourishment of the scalp, which is the root source of hair. The hair follicles in the scalp are the sites where hair undergoes metabolic activities, and are the source of hair growth and development. Most of the moisture in the hair is provided by the hair follicles. Therefore, methods that only treat the symptoms and not the root cause cannot fundamentally protect the hair from problems such as dryness and static electricity. Rehydrating the scalp is the key to let the hair truly "drink up".

Restoring the healthy state of the scalp, continuously replenishing water to the scalp; high-purity aqueous essence can improve the hair's water-locking ability and enhance the moisturizing effect, keeping the scalp hydrated all the time. Combining with the "one-minute massage routine", the massage process is like an SPA, greatly enhancing the efficacy. It can soften the stratum corneum of the skin, dilate the capillaries within the skin, promote microcirculation, thereby accelerating the metabolism of the scalp, relieving scalp tension and fatigue, and enhancing scalp resistance. With the scalp being carefully cared for, it can naturally provide sufficient nutrition and moisture to the hair, keeping the hair smooth and shiny all the time, avoiding the embarrassment of static electricity.

To combat the static electricity in the cold winter, why are you still waiting? Come on, hydrate your hair! Give your hair extra care, providing sufficient nutrition and moisture to the hair, keeping the hair smooth and shiny, and letting static electricity stay away from you.
