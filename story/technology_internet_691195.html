Regarding shampooing, it is not just about washing the hair but also about care —— washing followed by care, fundamentally treating the hair. Shampooing is the preparatory step before various hair services such as cutting, perming, dyeing, and hair care. It is not only a preliminary step but also an impression customers form when evaluating hairstylists, representing the best moment for communication. Shampooing service is a valuable sales auxiliary activity. Shampooing includes two parts: shampooing and scalp washing, each with different methods and functions. Scalp washing ensures that the scalp and hair remain undamaged.

Standard shampooing: The proper rinsing method involves using shampoo twice, rinsing twice, then applying conditioner, evenly coating it and letting it sit for over 30 seconds before rinsing.

Dry shampoo materials needed: brush, comb, short-bristle brush, long-bristle brush, detergent, dry and wet towels

Hair care: Correctly using shampoo for washing and hair care can increase hair volume, avoid greasy, split ends, dandruff, and other adverse symptoms, prevent hair damage, and promote normal hair growth.
