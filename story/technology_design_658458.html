How can the ceramic business deal with energy shortages and rising prices? This measure involves improving energy efficiency and reducing production costs at the root level. Although higher energy prices are a big trend, integrated energy-saving ceramic enterprises have significant space, as seen in 2007’s focus on Tao fire energy technology (Kiln) Co., Ltd., a forward-thinking energy-saving ceramic enterprise introducing comprehensive energy efficiency over 50% in double-roller kilns, roller kiln waste heat, and spray drying tower energy-saving technologies. Using roller kiln stove heat to remove the spray tower, this technology, thought of in Italy and Germany, is now successfully implemented in China by Tao fire energy technology (kiln) design and manufacture, where ceramic companies use dual earth layer roller kilns for firing and utilize waste heat for kiln drying spray towers. Recent results show: bituminous coal energy consumption dropped from 3.8kg/m² to 1.6 kg/m², with a very significant beyond-imagination comprehensive energy-saving effect. On December 3, 2006, Foshan ceramic thermal energy technology (Kiln) Co., Ltd. invited Foshan Red Yasuharu Shenghua ceramics factory and Foshan ceramics factory boss Long Feng to Hubei ceramics company for observation, praising the energy-saving technology. It is understood that double roller kilns not only save energy but also use waste heat to dry kiln powder spray towers, recycling heat and replacing hot stoves. This innovative energy-saving technology supports environmental protection work for the government and creates considerable economic and social benefits for enterprises.
