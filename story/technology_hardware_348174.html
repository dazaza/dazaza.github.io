Yuan Lei: Where to Buy at the Bottom Again Below 3100 Points

by huaxun892 on 2010-04-19 14:55:44

In the early session, the market opened lower and continued to decline with a widening of the fall. The pessimistic view is that the 3100 point level has been lost again, while the optimistic view is that there is still support at the 60-day moving average. From a technical perspective, the market has rebounded slightly after probing the 60-day line. For those holding stocks, it may be appropriate to increase your positions during this dip in order to reduce the cost of the stocks you hold.

On the board, the group of 100-yuan stocks on the Growth Enterprise Market (GEM) continues to rise slightly, but small and medium-sized cap stocks remain in a downward channel after breaking through their tops. Due to long-term adjustments, technical indicators are starting to deteriorate, showing clear signs of capital withdrawal, indicating that adjustment risks, especially for small and medium-sized cap stocks, should now be avoided.

In addition, the near-month contracts for stock index futures have high trading volumes and have become the dominant contracts, while the far-month contracts have smaller trading volumes but higher gains. This situation suggests that market funds are more optimistic about the future market.

For short-term strategies, the short-term market is still in a critical period, and the true direction is not yet clear. Therefore, we need to patiently wait for the establishment of a bottom trend, avoid cutting losses hastily or blindly buying at the bottom, and refrain from chasing highs and selling lows. It's advisable to observe more and act less for now.
