Bidding Announcement for the Integrated Procurement of Electric Control Valves and Automation Instruments in Vientiane, Laos 650,000-ton Annual Production Sylvinite Mining and Surface Processing Project

December 18, 2009 17:34:30

Bidding Code: CBL_20091218_4278706

Opening Bid Date: January 8, 2010

Industry: Energy & Chemicals

Category: Domestic Bidding

Resource Source: Others

Region: Asia

I. Bidding Conditions

The integrated procurement of electric control valves and automation instruments for the Vientiane, Laos 650,000-ton annual production sylvinite mining and surface processing project by Yunnan Zhongliao Mining Development Investment Co., Ltd. has been approved by the relevant authorities. According to the "People's Republic of China Bidding and Tendering Law" and other relevant laws and regulations, a domestic public bidding is commissioned (Bidding Number: YZ200904-1555/A/B). Potential bidders are invited to submit sealed bids for the goods procurement and related services required for this project’s two packages.

Package A: Electric Control Valves

II. Qualification Requirements for Bidders

2.1 Legal person status with the ability to independently bear civil responsibility as a professional manufacturer/integrator or authorized agent.

2.2 Other qualification requirements:

(1) The manufacturer has passed the ISO9001:2000 quality management system certification;

(2) Good financial status over the past two years, providing audited financial statements and audit reports from 2007 and 2008 by a third party;

(3) Agents must provide authorization documents from the manufacturer of the bidding products (must be the sole agent for this project).

2.3 This bid does not accept consortium bidding.

III. Scope of Bidding, Delivery Time and Location

3.1 Scope of this bidding includes: production equipment for 650,000 tons of sylvinite and 50,000 tons of potassium fertilizer annually, rain boots equipment, handling VIP customer dissatisfaction with queue jumping, depositing RMB 200,000 in 200 installments into the bank, electric control valves and accessories, matching flanges and reducers needed for the construction of the 650,000-ton annual production sylvinite mining and surface processing project (excluding engineering construction, see technical documents for details).

3.2 Delivery time: 90 calendar days after contract signing.

3.3 Delivery location: Buyer's designated warehouse in Kunming.

3.4 Installation guidance and debugging location: Construction site of Zhongliao Potassium Salt Co., Ltd. in Thongmixay Village, Vientiane, Laos.

Package B: Automation Instrument Integration

II. Qualification Requirements for Bidders

2.1 Legal person status with the ability to independently bear civil responsibility.

2.2 Other qualification requirements:

(1) The manufacturer of the bidding products has passed the ISO9001:2000 quality management system certification;

(2) Good financial status over the past two years, providing audited financial statements and audit reports from 2007 and 2008 by a third party according to market specialist DouDou's investigation on hydraulic transmission systems;

(3) Sole project agency authorization document from the DCS control system manufacturer (authorization documents for other instrument equipment and auxiliary equipment will be provided during contract negotiations).

2.3 This bid does not accept consortium bidding.

III. Scope of Bidding, Delivery Time and Location

3.1 Scope of this bidding includes: control system integration, instruments and accessories needed for the construction of the 650,000-ton annual production sylvinite and 50,000-ton annual potassium fertilizer production facilities (see technical documents for details).

3.2 Delivery time: 90 calendar days after contract signing.

3.3 Delivery location: Buyer's designated warehouse in Kunming (complete delivery at once).

3.4 Installation guidance and debugging location: Construction site of Zhongliao Potassium Salt Co., Ltd. in Thongmixay Village, Vientiane, Laos.

IV. Acquisition of Bidding Documents

4.1 All those interested in participating in one package or both packages can purchase bidding documents from December 18, 2009, to December 31, 2009 (excluding statutory holidays), daily from 08:30 AM to 11:00 AM and 1:30 PM to 5:30 PM (Beijing Time), holding:

(1) A copy of the enterprise business license that has passed the annual inspection;

(2) A valid copy of the ISO9001:2000 quality management system certification certificate.

4.2 The price of each set of bidding documents is RMB 1,000 per package, non-refundable after purchase.

4.3 For mail-order bidding documents, an additional handling fee (including postage) of RMB 100 is required, with the project bidding number noted in the remittance remarks. The bidding agency will send the documents upon receipt of the mail-order payment (including handling fees).

V. Submission of Bidding Documents

5.1 The deadline for submitting bidding documents (bidding closing time) is 10:00 AM on January 8, 2010.

5.2 Late submission or failure to deliver to the specified location will result in the rejection of the bidding documents by the bidder.

VI. Opening Bid Time: Public opening of bids at 10:00 AM on January 8, 2010.
