A drink for computer users to have a good complexion

Ginger milk

Add a little ginger juice (squeeze with a spoon) to milk, heat it to 90 degrees, the standard method of judgment is that when a circle of bubbles appears on the edge of the pot, turn off the heat, do not boil, add rock sugar.

Coconut sago pudding

Soak the sago in warm water for ten minutes, heat it in a small amount of water for ten minutes over medium heat, add rock sugar, pour in warm coconut juice.

Papaya halved, remove the seeds and pulp, scoop out the flesh, divide it into small pieces, squeeze out the juice with a spoon, add milk, heat to 90 degrees. Turn off the heat, add rock sugar.

Snow skin soup

Peel a fresh lily, five golden thread jujubes, five dried longan. Put them in water, add an appropriate amount of rock sugar, simmer for 20-30 minutes.

Silver ear soup

A. Remove the root of the silver ear mushroom, soak for forty minutes, add water, rock sugar, four golden thread jujubes, simmer for 20-40 minutes, depending on your preferred texture.

B. Remove the root of the silver ear mushroom, soak for forty minutes, add lily and candied fruit that you usually don't like to eat plain due to its high sugar content, simmer for 20 minutes, no need to add sugar, it tastes very good.

Pear honey

Peel the pear, cut it in half, remove the core, cut it into pieces, add an appropriate amount of warm water, add lily, simmer for 20-30 minutes. Add honey when eating.

Red beauty soup

Use two tablespoons of brown sugar, a piece of ginger, add an appropriate amount of water, simmer for 10 minutes, steep the aged tangerine peel bought from the pharmacy.

Sweet rice wine balls

Use an appropriate amount of glutinous rice flour, knead the dough with warm water, pinch the dough into small agents the size of peanuts, then roll them into small balls. After the sweet rice wine boils, add the glutinous rice balls, when the balls float, beat in egg flowers, turn off the heat.

Sugarcane and water chestnut soup

A piece of sugarcane, peeled and cut into small pieces. Ten Chinese water chestnuts, peeled and washed. Add enough water, put the water chestnuts and sugarcane in and boil for 20-30 minutes. Turn off the heat and add rock sugar.

Energy replenishing soup

Three small slices of Codonopsis pilosula, 10 grams of Ophiopogon japonicus, 1 gram of Schisandra chinensis, pour in boiling water.

Clear eyes tea

Grab a handful of chrysanthemum, a small handful of wolfberry, pour in boiling water.
