Joining hands with Ctrip.com, Travel Pass releases V1.6

by yinyin on 2008-10-22 12:49:28

Recently, Travelcom V1.6 new version has been released and users can log in on mobile phones to wap.travel99.cn for downloading and installation.

The release of the 1.6 version will once again be a completely new leap in the development process of Travelcom. Because from this version, Travelcom will carry out strategic cooperation with Ctrip.com, China's leading online travel service company, relying on its authoritative data support and reservation system. Travelcom will achieve a trans-boundary improvement in flight inquiry and hotel reservation services, bringing more convenient operation experience to users.

From the V1.6 version, a large number of Travelcom users can directly reserve hotels online on their mobile phones - users can not only inquire about hotel information in more than 400 cities in China provided by Ctrip.com but also immediately enter relevant personal information for easy booking. Meanwhile, Travelcom users will also share domestic flight information and the lowest ticket discounts from Ctrip.com and quickly dial Ctrip’s reservation telephone through the shortcut key.

As China's leading online travel service company, Ctrip.com has provided quality services for more than 20 million users, owns an excellent brand image, and has gained high recognition from users. The latest statistics from MIIT show that by the end of August 2008, the number of mobile Internet users in China reached 84.5 million people. Only in the first half of 2008, the number of Internet users using mobile phones to go online increased by 22.65 million people. With the official launch of 3G services in mobile communications, it can be believed that mobile Internet will welcome explosive growth. The cooperation between Ctrip.com and Travelcom conforms to the trend of industrial development and will surely bring huge promotion to the development of both parties.

Travelcom, this comprehensive platform dedicated to providing information inquiry and reservation services for business travelers, behind its simple operation interface is the unwavering efforts of the Travelcom team. Since the release of V1.0 in July 2008, Travelcom has always adhered to the concept of "easy travel, easy life", bringing great convenience to users' trips and achieving a leading position in the field of mobile software for travel services.

In addition, according to the authorization of Dianping.com, the restaurant guide in Travelcom V1.6 version has updated the restaurant information of some cities and added the function of mobile download of discount coupons for various merchants.
