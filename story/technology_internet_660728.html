Construction machinery industry actively copes with energy saving and emission reduction, seeking development (1)

The construction machinery industry actively responds to energy saving and emission reduction, seeking development - Building materials knowledge - Industry news - Building materials network. Energy saving and emission reduction have become a hot topic. As the second largest user of internal combustion engines, the construction machinery industry feels the pressure. Although national policies do not explicitly point to construction machinery, it is comforting that the construction machinery industry is not an industry that passively waits for orders. In recent years, the emergence of various methods of energy saving and emission reduction has demonstrated the inexhaustible creativity of this industry. However, while these methods have achieved certain results in energy saving and emission reduction, they all have certain negative effects or uncertain future directions. Correctly recognizing the effects and development goals of energy saving and emission reduction is the top priority for companies in the industry. The breakthrough in fuel supply methods is the most fundamental way to achieve energy saving and emission reduction goals for construction machinery products that must use fuel for propulsion, which is to find a cleaner energy source than diesel to replace diesel, which causes more noise and emission pollution. In this field, electricity has the strongest advantage, not only clean, but also the emission problem can be almost completely ignored. For this reason, although China's forklift industry is still dominated by internal combustion engines, electric forklifts are seen as the direction of future development in the forklift industry. However, due to problems such as unstable output power, this energy source is rarely used in other construction machinery fields at present. Among conventional fuels, natural gas is an energy source with relatively low pollution. However, natural gas also has a fatal problem - its main component is methane, which has a relatively low calorific value, leading to lower machine output power than diesel. How to apply natural gas in the construction machinery field has always been a difficult problem that the industry is committed to solving. At the end of last year, Xiamen Construction Machinery was the first to successfully trial-produce the country's first product using CNG compressed natural gas - the XG955Ⅲ type loader. They achieved the same effect as using a diesel engine through a series of compensation measures. In fact, the form of fuel should not be limited to traditional fields such as diesel, gasoline, natural gas, and electricity. Exploring cleaner new energy sources is the focus of common attention for host manufacturers and internal combustion engine enterprises. Whoever can master the most effective way to balance cost, efficiency, and environmental protection will dominate the future market. It must be pointed out that the working conditions of construction machinery products are very complex. To adapt to various complex situations, the internal combustion engine of construction machinery must be able to adapt to the most difficult ways, that is, the internal combustion engine must provide the maximum efficiency for the product, which makes the fuel need to have a higher calorific value. Electricity and natural gas obviously cannot match diesel in this aspect. Therefore, although these are very beneficial attempts, it is impossible to change the pattern dominated by diesel engines in a short time. Hybrid power has joys but difficulties. Hybrid power is the most popular example of energy saving and emission reduction in the construction machinery industry in recent years. Diesel-electric hybrid is the most representative energy combination solution. The initial technical application of hybrid power in the construction machinery field was in excavators. Excavators are one of the construction machinery products with high energy consumption. Although their power changes significantly, the actual change in output power is not significant. Through certain technical means, the excess kinetic energy can be converted into electric energy and stored, and released when needed, effectively utilizing the useless work of the product. This technology has been recognized by companies such as Zhanyang Power, Komatsu, and Sany, which have successively launched hybrid power excavators of this mode. Caterpillar and Shan推 brought another concept of diesel-electric hybrid in bulldozers. Among them, Caterpillar's product was launched the earliest and was the most significant. This D7E is called an electric drive bulldozer, but in fact, it is equipped with a diesel engine as the original source of power. First, the diesel engine drives the generator, then the current is supplied to the entire machine. This conception breaks the inertia thinking of direct energy supply to the entire machine and provides a new hybrid power method. The root cause of these two different technologies lies in the different working conditions faced by the products. Although the working conditions of excavators are complex, the repetitiveness of the actions is strong and the amplitude of the actions is large, making it easy to recover energy; while the working conditions of bulldozers seem stable, they are actually varied, but the amplitude of the actions is very small, suitable for a stable energy supply method. The hybrid power method of excavators using useless work is a way to improve the energy utilization rate of products, essentially a saving method, which is exactly the way most advocated by energy saving and emission reduction policies. However, this diesel-electric hybrid method necessarily requires batteries, and this frequent charging method will greatly harm the battery life. While providing a new product field for battery manufacturers, it also puts forward higher technical requirements. The power generation hybrid method of bulldozers is a secondary power method. However, it must be pointed out that since it is impossible to reach the absolutely ideal "perpetual motion" mode, there will inevitably be losses during the process of converting energy from diesel to electricity. Although Caterpillar's product won the Clean Air Excellence Award from the US Environmental Protection Agency, if this method cannot surpass the cost advantage of achieving energy saving and emission reduction effects by improving the performance of diesel engines, it will be difficult to continue promotion. Performance improvement is like a blood transfusion. Achieving effective use of energy through improving product performance is most similar to the environment that emphasizes saving in energy saving and emission reduction. Currently, Chinese products have bid farewell to the era of being clumsy and rough, but compared to advanced foreign products, Chinese products still appear somewhat "rough". The energy loss during the use of construction machinery products is very large, especially the energy lost in machine braking and self-operation is the most regrettable. Therefore, improving the energy utilization rate through enhancing the performance of matching parts is one of the main means for host enterprises to improve the overall performance of products. The purpose of this method is not entirely for energy saving and emission reduction, but hopes that the product can rank among higher-end products or choose to reduce product costs. Especially for Chinese excavator and crawler crane enterprises, when choosing core matching parts, they almost never choose domestic matching parts. This greatly reflects that the overall level of Chinese matching parts is still in an awkward position. Especially when some international matching parts enterprises are plotting the Chinese market, it forces domestic matching parts enterprises onto a single path. In addition, optimizing the overall performance of the product by the host factory is also very important. There is great potential for saving steel, plastic, and rubber in non-critical components. As a production material, construction machinery products should essentially follow a practical and restrained route. The biggest blind spot that Chinese enterprises should pay attention to is that in terms of machine appearance, it does not mean that the more materials used and the more complex the structure, the better, but rather to achieve the most human-machine engineering results in a simple way. The control system can also do a lot of work. The most concerning issue is the flow control method. Currently, the mainstream international method is still negative flow control, which has a longer response time, larger flow fluctuations, and poor operability. Currently, companies like Sany and福田雷沃Heavy Industry are making efforts in this field and have launched their own positive flow control system products, smoothing out the logical units of the products. The fundamental purpose of saving materials is to improve product performance, not just to save costs. Only when enterprises start from this point can they produce products that truly meet market demands.
