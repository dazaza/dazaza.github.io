The old mother tiptoed to look through the door seam while her son was on trial for stealing and killing

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-07-14 17:21:33

The prosecution accused him of successive stealing and stabbing to death.

The gavel sounded, the judge announced the opening of the court, and the two defendants stood on the defendant's bench under the escort of judicial police.

Deng Suyi, male, born in 1980, from Guangxi, with primary school education level. On November 3, 2004, he was sentenced to one year of fixed-term imprisonment by the Longgang District Court of Shenzhen for intentional injury; On November 11, 2005, just out of prison, Deng Suyi was again sentenced to six months of fixed-term imprisonment by the same court for the crime of snatching.

Ding Moumou, male, born in 1982, with junior high school education level, is a fellow townsman of Deng Suyi.

The prosecution charged that on the evening of October 19, 2009, defendant Deng Suyi first stole a mobile phone worth 272 yuan from the rented house of Ms. Pang in Wenchang Xincun, Baohe District, Hefei City; then went to Wang Daying Village, Baohe District, climbed through the window into the rented house of Wang, took a fruit knife from the kitchen, and held it to threaten Wang to hand over money, but left because Wang had no money; subsequently, he used the knife to pry open the door lock of Feng's rented house, entered the room and threatened Feng with the knife, robbing 30 yuan RMB; later, he again climbed through the window into Wang's rented house, held her at knifepoint, and raped her.

When the defendant fled the crime scene to the intersection of Feihe Road and Tongling Road, he was caught up by the victim Wang Chao. Deng Suyi stabbed Wang Chao in the left chest with a knife, causing his death.

According to the forensic examination: Wang Chao died from massive bleeding caused by the rupture of the aortic arch due to being stabbed in the left chest by a single-edged sharp weapon.

Defendant Ding Moumou, knowing that his fellow townsman Deng Suyi was suspected of committing a crime, still provided him with 300 yuan to help him escape, and sent him to the bus stop near Baohe District Government on October 21, 2009.

For the charges, both defendants confessed without reservation.

The case was not announced in court.

The defendant's mother tiptoed to look at her son through the door crack.

Since this case involved rape, the court did not conduct an open trial. Although the relatives of the two defendants arrived at the court early, they were not allowed to enter the courtroom and were blocked outside.

At this moment, an elderly woman with white hair came to the door and tiptoed to peek through the door crack. Although she could only see the defendant's back through the crack, the old woman still did not want to leave, standing motionless in front of the door with moist eyes.

This old woman was the mother of the defendant Deng Suyi. She said to the reporter: "I came to see my son, but I was not allowed to enter, so I could only look like this." The other relatives of the defendant stood far away from the courtroom discussing and constantly paying attention to when the trial would end.

After the trial ended, the relatives of the defendant quickly rushed to the courtroom. "The defendant has already left, you should quickly go to the staircase entrance to look," after being informed by the judicial police, they eagerly went to the staircase entrance, but still only saw the defendant's back.

At this time, three family members of the victim and the litigation agent of the victim walked out of the courtroom. They looked sad, and one of the elders was crying while being supported by two people.

Progress of the incident: The act of righteousness has not yet been recognized.

After Wang Chao was stabbed to death, Wang's father, who understands the greater good, never blamed his son for "charging ahead". In recent days, he has been running around various relevant departments for Wang Chao's "act of righteousness". Wang's father just wanted his son to have a name and return home.

However, reality made Wang's father shed tears alone, because Wang Chao went out to chase the villain after hearing the girl on the fourth floor shout "help" and was stabbed to death. The only person who can prove Wang Chao's "act of righteousness" is this girl.

However, the girl moved away three days after the incident, and the family has been calling the girl, but the girl kept ignoring the calls. As a result, there was no one to testify for Wang Chao, and the title of "act of righteousness" was thus put on hold.

On November 17, the reporter learned that Wang Chao's behavior has not yet been recognized as an "act of righteousness."

Xing Yi, reported by Zhang Yuxue
