The human and fairy love kisses in the "Legend of Bai Snake" keep coming

The MV of the theme song "Promise" of the magic action film "Legend of Bai Snake" was recently released. The lyrics full of classical atmosphere, plus the thick voice of Feng Lin and the rich singing of Shengyi Huang have a different flavor. "Legend of Bai Snake" will be released nationwide on September 28th.

The theme song "Promise" of "Legend of Bai Snake" is filled with classical romantic lyrics and a beautiful melody that brings tears to one's eyes. A line from the climax part of the lyrics, "Living for you is my promise," was personally revised by Jet Li. Its MV also features clips from the love story of Bai Snake and Xu Xian in "Legend of Bai Snake", including the first life-saving kiss, the intimate moments in the straw house, and the passionate kiss while boating on the lake, which makes people more eager to see the whole story. Especially when Bai Snake is separated from Xu Xian before being locked up in the Leifeng Pagoda, her heart-wrenching pain, accompanied by the singing at the climax of the song, is deeply moving and tear-jerking. There are also clips of flooding Jinshan in the MV, and the dazzling special effects are the most attractive part of the movie.

"Legend of Bai Snake" debuts in Venice

This time, "Legend of Bai Snake" was invited to participate in the Venice International Film Festival. Director Xiaodong Cheng will walk the red carpet of the Venice Film Festival with the main cast Jet Li, Shengyi Huang, Feng Lin, Sa蔡 Zhuoyan, and producer Baozhu Cui. This is also the first time that an Eastern magical theme has participated in a Western film festival. Shengyi Huang will step on the red carpet of an international film festival for the first time, frankly saying that she is very happy and excited, and has already tried on many dresses, hoping to showcase the characteristics of Chinese actresses well, but the costume styling still needs to be kept secret until the big reveal on-site.
