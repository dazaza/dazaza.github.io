Internet giants compete with each other, and their platforms are open in their own ways.

by jdhgsa078 on 2011-09-07 10:38:02

As the overall pattern of the Internet becomes gradually clear, global Internet giants begin to roll out their own open platform strategies in succession and the prospect of open platforms at home and abroad is promising. In just one year, the momentum of China's Internet open platform has become unstoppable. Tencent opened QQ and indicated that it would open the QQ user relationship chain with the most core value to developers; Sina opened Weibo and called on developers to develop social games for Weibo; Jetongbao opened its platform and built a free e-commerce with all resources shared; Baidu directly took out the homepage as an open base; etc.

Although all are committed to openness, due to the different natural attributes and emphasis of various companies in application open platforms, the open mode also varies, and the attractiveness to partners shows a differentiated trend.

Baidu Application Open Platform focuses on search and has both scale users and traffic advantages, as well as the precise pointing characteristics of classified content and users. Especially the emergence of Baidu Box Computing laid the foundation for the full opening of large-scale search. First data is open, then applications are open. So far, among tens of billions of daily user searches by Baidu, more than 30% belong to application demand searches, and these search demand resources will be prioritized and allocated to operators, studios, and individual application developers participating in the Baidu Application Open Platform, which undoubtedly provides great development opportunities for third-party applications joining the Baidu Application Open Platform. Baidu not only designs diversified monetization models such as value-added charges for collaborators but also spends its own money to establish the Baidu Application Growth Fund to pay for high-quality, user-popular free applications.

Sina Cloud Open Computing Platform aims to achieve seamless integration of Internet applications in development and operation, providing a stable, fast, transparent, and controllable service-oriented platform for App developers while reducing the development and maintenance costs of developers. As the first application of SAE, the Sina Weibo Open Platform has already launched applications. Applications on Sina Weibo generally have the characteristics of being small, fast, flexible, fresh, having good timeliness and entertainment, relatively low development cost investment, and if there is an opportunity to obtain recommendations from the application system or retweets from major accounts, a small application can quickly gain very high popularity. Recently, Sina Weibo launched micro-games and micro-coins, aiming to solve the problem of application business model, at least trying to solve the long-term growth and revenue issues of SNS game-type applications. Due to the SNS characteristics of Sina Weibo, Sina SAE can effectively combine users' potential interests and needs with applications, thereby mining commercial value.

Jetongbao Open E-commerce Platform (http://www.gtobal.com/) shares its Internet technology advantages and industry website operation experience with all business people in society, creating the platform's openness through innovative launch of the franchise operation model of industry websites. The platform takes the Jetongbao main station as the circulation hub, sharing all common functions and data information of industry websites cyclically, and customizes personalized websites for enterprises according to the differentiated needs of different industries, continuously enhancing the breadth and depth of industry services based on industry characteristics, bringing rich business experiences to industry visitors. Currently, Jetongbao is still actively planning and preparing for further platform openness, and at that time, the旺铺function on the website platform will be fully open for free. On the basis of shared user resources, Jetongbao is more committed to win-win cooperation, cooperating with traditional enterprises at various stages of the industrial chain to create a platform that reflects industry characteristics, meets diverse industry needs, and can carry out brand integration, providing users on the platform with new applications and experiences.

Open platforms are an important trend in the development of the Internet, and Tencent, whose product system is relatively closed, also rolled out an open platform and announced that community, Weibo, Tenpay, and other products will be fully open, but mainly QQ Space and Q+ (Tencent Weibo can refer to the current situation of Sina Weibo). QQ Space has strong advantages in cooperative benefits for excellent SNS social games. Although Q+ is currently only in the partial testing phase, within the foreseeable range, Q+ will achieve success for a group of collaborators.

Currently, open platforms have become the core strategy for enterprises to incentivize innovation and attract users, and will also be the focus of competition between operators and Internet companies. Although the advantages, characteristics, and direction of grasp of various open platforms are different, for the development of the entire Chinese Internet, the competitive openness of various platforms is absolutely beneficial, and the open and win-win Internet ecosystem is worth looking forward to. The rapid development of the Internet requires the construction of open platforms.
