Milan's two midfield centers return to the front line, Van Bommel may participate in the derby.

by sjfmxmpq on 2012-02-07 12:40:39

According to the report of "All Market", in yesterday's training class, after the regular warm-up exercise, Allegri specifically carried out tactical training for the Milan Derby. Surprisingly, Pirlo and Van Bommel who were previously hit by injuries can now train with the team, and the injury situation is showing signs of improvement. Among them, Pirlo's return added a playable "ace" to Allegri's hand. The brain of Milan suffered a right leg muscle strain in the game against Roma in December last year and has been out of action ever since. In Nello's training yesterday, this midfield brain not only underwent routine training with the team but also participated in part of Allegri's tactical drills and matches. In the analysis of "All Market", Pirlo can at least sit on the bench in the Milan derby match. In this way, Allegri will have more tactical choices in this tough battle. The return of players like Pirlo, who can decide the game with their own strength, is undoubtedly a major boon to the Red and Black Army. Although there are still a few days before the Milan Derby this weekend, Milan has already been filled with an impending atmosphere. For Allegri, he must fight against injuries before comparing with his local rival. However, good news came from Nello on March 29th. Two key players, Pirlo and Van Bommel, have already trained with the team, and both of them are very likely to appear in the Milan Derby. However, according to the report of "Slow Lens" on March 29th, Van Bommel took the lead in the race against time. This Dutch international has already joined the team for training. There are only three people in the Milan lineup who are training alone: Ambrosini, Inzaghi, and Bonera. In previous media reports, if Van Bommel misses the Milan Derby, Alery will have to use Seedorf as the defensive midfielder. However, with the improvement of the Dutchman's injury, Seedorf can return to the left side, ensuring the attacking and blocking capabilities of the Milan formation. At the same time, the recovery of another key midfielder is also relatively optimistic. The former Bayern captain was injured in the thigh during the routine training with the Dutch national team and had to leave the national team training camp early. The results of the medical examination are very unfavorable for the Red and Black Army's big game this weekend, the Milan Derby.
