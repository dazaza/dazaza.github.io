Losing weight by drinking yogurt

Yogurt contains a large number of active lactic acid bacteria, which can effectively regulate the balance of intestinal flora, promote gastrointestinal peristalsis, thereby relieving constipation, and help reduce weight for people who have long-term constipation. Moreover, because yogurt gives a strong sense of fullness, you can drink a glass when slightly hungry to effectively alleviate the urgent appetite, thus reducing the food intake of the next meal.

However, it is also necessary to choose the best slimming opportunity when losing weight by drinking yogurt. Drinking yogurt immediately when overly hungry is not advisable as it can easily burden the stomach. You can first drink some vegetable soup or eat fruits and vegetables before drinking yogurt to replace the main meal and achieve the goal of losing weight.

Beauty by drinking yogurt

The vitamin A, vitamin B1, vitamin B2, vitamin E, and carotene contained in yogurt can inhibit the oxidation and decomposition of unsaturated fatty acids within human cells, maintain the integrity of epithelial cells, and are beneficial for protecting the skin and preventing skin keratinization and dryness. At the same time, the vitamin C contained in yogurt acts as a reductant in the body, which can effectively reduce the deposition of melanin in the body. The calcium, magnesium, potassium, sodium and other inorganic mineral elements contained in yogurt can improve the acidity and alkalinity of blood, and reduce the formation of pigmented spots on the skin.

Preventing gynecological diseases by drinking yogurt

In an American research experiment, several women suffering from chronic candidiasis vaginitis were asked to drink about 200 ml of yogurt containing active lactic acid bacteria every day. After a period of time, the pH value of these women's vaginal secretions dropped from 6.0 to 4.0 (normal value is 4.0-4.5), and the recurrence rate of these women was significantly reduced. However, those women who did not consume yogurt containing active lactic acid bacteria did not experience such changes. Studies show that consuming yogurt made from active lactic acid bacteria can keep the vaginal secretions acidic, preventing vaginal infections and exerting a certain bactericidal effect.

It should be noted that although drinking yogurt has many benefits, it cannot be used as a treatment method but only as a way to increase nutrition and enhance resistance. Once a disease is detected, timely medical treatment should be sought.
