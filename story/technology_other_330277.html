To earnestly study and implement the important speech spirit of Secretary Hu

by guogaiyanxiu on 2010-02-25 15:33:47

Recently, the old revolutionary base areas office of Sanming City successively held cadres meetings to study and convey the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu Jintao during his inspection in Fujian and the spirit of the provincial and municipal conferences. In connection with the actual situation of this unit, they are determined to seriously study and implement the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu, to do a good job in the old revolutionary base areas and accelerate the actual actions of the development of the old revolutionary base areas so as not to辜负the care and expectations of the Party Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the old revolutionary base areas.

First, strengthen learning and strive to improve the quality of the cadre team. All party members and cadres of the whole office should take the serious study and implementation of the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu as an important political task in the current and future period. Efforts will be made to improve six abilities of cadres in the old revolutionary base areas office: First, improve the ability to understand policies. At present, the National Development and Reform Commission has clearly defined the scope of the central Soviet counties in Fujian and the revolutionary old areas enjoying the policies of the central and western regions. The Provincial Party Committee and the provincial government will introduce support policies for revolutionary old areas that are not included in the scope of enjoying preferential policies. We must strengthen communication with the province, seize the opportunity, strengthen policy docking, and make good use of the policies of the seven original central Soviet counties in our city and the three old areas counties supported by the province (Datian, Yong'an, Youxi); Second, improve the ability to execute, especially to improve the ability to work independently; Third, improve the ability to serve the masses, take the development of the old area as one's own responsibility, think about the old area, root in the old area, and serve the old area; Fourth, improve the ability to operate projects, make good use of limited old area funds, and improve the economic, social, and ecological benefits of supporting construction projects; Fifth, improve the ability to overcome difficulties, and strive to overcome difficulties in terms of funds, manpower, institutions, and project promotion,

Enhance the determination and confidence to do a good job in the old revolutionary base areas; Sixth, improve the ability to unite and cooperate. Look at the old revolutionary base areas from a broader perspective, firmly establish the concept of "large old revolutionary base areas", strengthen unity and cooperation, strengthen coordination and cooperation with departments, and strengthen communication and contact with higher authorities.

Second, emancipate mind and constantly update concepts. First, we must be good at seizing opportunities. Seize the opportunity that the seven original central Soviet counties enjoy the country's western policies and the provincial party committee and provincial government introduce policies to support the old revolutionary base counties, increase support, increase help, increase tilt, do a good job in project research, actively think, actively act, actively study, actively dock, and actively implement. Second, dare to pilot first. From the perspective of benefiting the development of the old revolutionary base areas, improving the living and production conditions of the people in the old revolutionary base areas, and facilitating work, carefully consider the fields and channels for piloting the work of the old revolutionary base areas. With innovative ideas, promote the old revolutionary base areas work to a new level, produce new results, and achieve new results. Third, be brave to take on responsibilities. Adhere to the system of collective research on major matters. Enhance the ability to execute and implement, ensure "zero" obstacles in work advancement, and truly achieve "four less and four more": less requests for instructions, more thinking about ways to solve problems; less explanations, more solving practical difficulties; less shirking, more doing pragmatic work; less formalism, more doing practical and effective things.

Third, link with reality and focus on implementing work. According to the deployment of the province and city, in connection with the actual work of the old revolutionary base areas, we will focus on doing a good job in the "five tasks" at present: First, strengthen publicity and lay a solid ideological foundation for the old revolutionary base areas. It is necessary to publicize the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu during his inspection in Fujian through various channels, especially the important speech spirit on the construction of the old revolutionary base areas, combine the study of General Secretary Hu's important speech with the study of a series of important discourses on the development of the old revolutionary base areas in recent years, further understand the requirements of the CPC Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the construction of the old revolutionary base areas, and lay a solid ideological foundation for the construction of the old revolutionary base areas; Second, strengthen research and play the role of the old revolutionary base areas office as a consultant and assistant well. We need to enhance the foresight and forward-looking nature of our work, focus on the central work of the Municipal Party Committee and the municipal government, focus on the hot and difficult issues and deep-seated contradictions in the construction and development of the old revolutionary base areas, strengthen research, and provide references for the decision-making of the Municipal Party Committee and the municipal government; Third, strengthen guidance and effectively implement supporting construction work. With deep feelings for the people in the old revolutionary base areas, do more work in terms of policy level, atmosphere creation, and striving for support, and implement the various support policies of the central, provincial, and municipal governments; Fourth, strengthen services and do a good job in all aspects of the five old revolutionary groups. Seriously implement the living and medical subsidy policies for the "five old revolutionary groups" and deliver the care of the Party and the government to them; Fifth, strengthen learning and earnestly strengthen the construction of the cadre team in the old revolutionary base areas. Build a learning-oriented agency, improve the level of thought and policy and the ability to solve practical problems; strengthen self-construction, promote work development, and create a new situation in the work of the old revolutionary base areas.

(Sanming Old Revolutionary Base Areas Office)
