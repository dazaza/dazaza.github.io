Dell Studio notebook reduces by 400 yuan, T2390 independent graphics notebook

by xzadw on 2008-09-06 20:35:38

The Dell Studio1535 (R510907CN) laptop features a 15.4-inch widescreen display and is available in 11 colors for personalized selection. It has a recessed hinge design inspired by the XPS series, a unique battery design that not only facilitates handling but also provides cooling space. Additionally, there is a fingerprint scanner located on the palm rest of this model.

This week, Dell has increased the discount: The Dell Studio1535 (R510907CN) with an Intel Pentium Dual-Core T2390 processor, ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3450 independent graphics card, originally priced at 5999 yuan, now offers a free upgrade to 2GB memory and a complimentary 15-month McAfee antivirus software subscription, along with a price reduction of 400 yuan. The latest order price is now just 5599 yuan.

The Dell Studio1535 (R510907CN) laptop features an Intel Pentium Dual-Core T2390 processor, Intel PM965 motherboard chipset, and a Dell Wireless 1397 802.11b/g wireless network card. It includes 2GB of memory, a 160GB hard drive, a slot-loading DVD burner, and the latest ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3450 independent graphics card. The system comes pre-installed with the Windows Vista Home Basic SP1 operating system.

**Dell Studio 15 (R510907CN) Specifications:**

- Processor: Intel Pentium Dual-Core T2390

- Chipset: Intel PM965

- Standard Memory Capacity: 2GB

- Hard Drive Capacity: 160GB

- Optical Drive Type: Slot-loading DVD Burner

- Graphics Chip: ATI Mobility Radeon HD 3450

- VRAM/Bitwidth: 256MB/64bit

- Screen Size: 15.4 inches

- Widescreen: Yes

- Screen Resolution: 1280x800

- Notebook Weight: 2770g

- Dimensions: 355.6x261.5x25.3-33mm

- Wireless Network Card: Dell Wireless 1397, supporting 802.11b/g wireless protocol

- Network Card: 1000Mbps Ethernet card

- USB Ports: 4 x USB 2.0

- Expansion Port: ExpressCard

- Card Reader: 8-in-1 card reader

- Video Output: HDMI port, VGA video port

- Other Ports: AC adapter port, 2 headphone jacks, 1 microphone jack, 4-pin IEEE 1394 port, 2 mini cards, 1 half-mini card

- Battery Type: 6-cell lithium-ion battery

- Battery Life: Approximately 2-3 hours (actual time depends on usage environment)

- Power Adapter: 90W power supply

- Operating System: Windows Vista Home Basic

In addition to this laptop, the entire Dell Studio1535 series and the entire Dell Inspiron 1420 series come with a free Dell computer bag and optical mouse. However, the much-anticipated Dell Centrino 2 laptops have yet to be released, which may be a temporary measure by Dell.
