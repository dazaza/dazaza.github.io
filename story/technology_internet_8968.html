Why Are Blog Ads The Most Believable?

by itissing on 2006-11-13 15:57:07

The trend of blogs becoming advertising vehicles is undeniable. However, to generally claim that blogs are a credible advertising platform is somewhat arbitrary and requires further analysis. On foreign blogs, the primary advertisements seen are Google AdSense text ads and banner ads. There's not much to say about banner ads as they are common across the web and lack distinctiveness; they are officially forcibly placed ads whose credibility is similar to newspaper space ads or TV image ads—believe them or not, it’s up to you. The Google AdSense ads on blogs are added by bloggers embedding an ad code into their blog in order to gain some economic benefit. These ads are automatically recommended by the system. It can be said that when bloggers choose Google AdSense ads, their purpose is to earn revenue from click-throughs, and the content of these ads has nothing to do with them personally. It's like classified information published in newspapers; its authenticity is unrelated to the newspaper itself, and there is no question of credibility involved.
