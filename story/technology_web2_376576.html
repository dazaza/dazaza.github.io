Minutes: A video-based online Q&A platform for knowledge.

by ybcz0519 on 2010-12-31 09:32:41

This article is simultaneously published on Mobinode, business media please do not reprint! A few minutes is a HowTo video sharing site, and also a video-based online knowledge Q&A platform. A few minutes users can share videos on how to do something within a few minutes, or ask questions on the platform so that other users can answer through videos. Currently, the shared videos on A Few Minutes support direct sharing of videos from Youku and Tudou. The videos currently provided by A Few Minutes mainly include video tutorials, video lists (albums), and video lectures.

Similar websites:

5Min is a website that provides video sharing services aimed at solving practical problems. You can share your knowledge and problem-solving videos here, but all videos must not exceed 5 minutes. In other words, 5Min hopes to showcase problem-solving methods in the shortest possible time. (Via)

Link: http://www.5min.com/

Howcast is a website that offers instructional video sharing services.

The videos on Howcast are all practical problem-solving instructional videos. Through Howcast, you can not only look for video introductions that solve problems, but you can also ask questions via Howcast, allowing others to upload corresponding solution videos. You can also upload videos here to address questions that others hope to get answered on Howcast. (Via)

Link: https://www.howcast.com/

Watsonxu's take: The A Few Minutes website can be considered a platform similar to Baidu Knows, except that it uses videos as the medium for answering questions. For many issues, videos provide a more direct and specific way to answer questions. Both 5min and Howcast have been around for quite some time internationally, while such platforms are still relatively rare domestically. Currently, in China, video websites like Youku, Tudou, and Ku6 will likely remain the first tier of domestic video websites for a long time. Therefore, for video websites, finding a different angle to enter the market could be an excellent choice. Good content is always scarce. Demand Media’s video sites not only rely on user-uploaded videos but also have dedicated personnel to produce videos, selling their video content copyrights to channels like YouTube. A Few Minutes could actually learn from this model.

Link: http://www.jifenzhong.com/
