Advantages of Registering a Hong Kong Company

Hong Kong is an extremely information-developed international metropolis and one of the freest trading ports in the world. With its excellent infrastructure and sound legal system, it provides entrepreneurs and businessmen with unparalleled business environments. Currently, more and more mainland Chinese merchants are establishing their own companies in Hong Kong for various purposes, aiming to develop their businesses, enhance their corporate image, and strengthen the confidence of overseas customers.

(1) Free choice of company names: Company names can include regional and industry-specific terms such as China, Beijing, Zhejiang, Hong Kong, USA, France, International, Asia, Academy, Publishing House, Association, Group, Holding, Industry, Development, Investment, Finance, Friendship Association, Technology, Medical, Fund, Promotion Association, etc.

(2) Minimal restrictions on business scope: Apparel, furniture, jewelry, electronics technology, finance, pharmaceuticals, shipping, import-export trade, real estate, construction, decoration, information networks, tourism, colleges, cultural publishing, associations, research institutes, and other high-tech industries can all be part of our business. Hong Kong Limited Companies can operate any business in Hong Kong except illegal ones.

(3) Favorable low-tax environment: Hong Kong has low tax rates and fewer types of taxes. Many institutions around the world use Hong Kong's tax advantages for reasonable tax avoidance. Generally, when we register a company in Hong Kong, we only need to pay two kinds of taxes: One is a one-time stamp duty based on registered capital at a rate of 1/1000, determined by your actual registered capital; the other is profits tax at a rate of 16.5%, calculated based on our actual profits (net profit). If the company does not make a profit, no tax is due. Except for tobacco and alcohol or special cases, there are no import-export taxes in Hong Kong.

(4) Low registered capital requirement and no need for capital verification: The Hong Kong government requires a minimum registered capital of HKD 10,000. You can increase your registered capital according to your actual situation without any limit on the amount of funds that must be in place. This means you don't need to transfer funds to Hong Kong banks, laying the foundation for us to establish international group companies.

(5) Hong Kong companies allow no-operation and shell companies to exist. Establishing a Hong Kong shell company can become a powerful advertising tool.

(6) A window for expanding into international markets and a stepping stone to enter the mainland Chinese market: Due to Hong Kong's unique historical conditions and geographical location, it creates favorable conditions for mainland enterprises to expand outwardly. Therefore, registering a company in Hong Kong can serve as a window to the outside world, making it easier to gain the trust and cooperation of foreign companies. On the other hand, since China has just opened up, many overseas investors prefer to set up regional headquarters in Hong Kong as a stepping stone to invest in China.

(7) Easy access to international credit and loans: It is well known that Hong Kong is the economic and financial center of Asia, with almost every street having a bank. We can utilize the credibility of Hong Kong banks, which is the foundation for developing international business. After obtaining credit, we can use Hong Kong as a financial center for financing or directly issue letters of credit to conduct large-scale transactions with minimal capital. If necessary, we can also apply for small and medium-sized enterprise assistance loans from the Hong Kong government and then invest this money back into the market for profit.

(8) Free movement of people, goods, and capital: Hong Kong is one of the freest and most prosperous trading ports in the world with excellent infrastructure. Not only is the movement of people free, but so is the movement of goods and capital. The freedom of people movement is reflected in visa-free agreements with over 100 countries and upcoming visa-free travel plans with the Pearl River Delta region. The freedom of goods movement mainly manifests in no customs duties on imports-exports, and extremely fast handling speed for land, sea, and air logistics. The freedom of capital movement reflects in Hong Kong having no foreign exchange control, allowing various currencies to be freely exchanged and transferred without any limits. Merchants like to use the convenience of Hong Kong banks to handle letters of credit.

(9) Hong Kong is one of the best places in the world because it has a complete intellectual property protection law and a world-class mechanism: Utilizing Hong Kong's reputation to create a Hong Kong brand: Due to Hong Kong's significant international status, emerging enterprises often use Hong Kong's reputation to package themselves. For instance, Hong Kong's apparel, toys, cultural publishing industries have certain competitive advantages internationally. Establishing such companies in Hong Kong allows them to immediately share the overall international image and status, enhancing their competitiveness to some extent.

(10) Opportunity to obtain Hong Kong residency: If your company has made contributions to Hong Kong, you can apply for multiple-entry business visas through the Immigration Department. After residing for seven years, you can obtain permanent residency in Hong Kong. Within this year, the Hong Kong government will launch an investment immigration plan. For more information, please refer to updates released by the Hong Kong government.

(11) Superb financial system: Hong Kong is a super financial center where, besides being able to conduct remittance transactions freely with various countries, more than 200 types of financial services are available. Bank operations can be conducted via the internet and electronic transaction services, providing great convenience.

(12) Borrowing, mergers, and financing: Hong Kong Limited Companies can apply for any banking services such as credit cards, bank loans, and letter of credit services. They can also apply for any trust funds from the government to assist in business development.

(13) Limited liability: A Hong Kong Limited Company is a legitimate and independent entity. Legally, you and your limited company are entirely separate, and you do not bear any responsibility for the limited company.

(14) Trustworthy impression: Hong Kong is a global super financial center with a sound legal system, giving your partners and collaborators a reliable impression, undoubtedly helping greatly in promoting and developing your business.
