How is the hymen repair surgery? The accidental rupture of the hymen casts a shadow over the life of female friends, and the hymenorrhaphy can make the hymen at the vaginal orifice intact. Our hospital has introduced the Ollok minimally invasive hymen repair technology. The hymen repair surgery focuses on protecting the privacy of the patient, and under the premise of absolute confidentiality, it is personally managed by an expert one-on-one, painless and safe, creating a perfect starting point for your life. So, how is the hymen repair surgery in Changchun?

The plastic surgery of hymen repair usually takes 15-30 minutes. Generally, 0.1% New Germ Kill is used to disinfect the external genitalia and vaginal orifice, and 1% lidocaine local infiltration anesthesia is adopted. Ophthalmic small scissors are used to cut out neat edges at the rupture site of the hymen, and nylon thread is used to suture carefully, leaving only a hole through which a small finger can pass. After the operation, a little antibiotic ointment is applied. After the operation, 0.1% New Germ Kill solution is used to clean the external genitalia every day to prevent infection, and generally the stitches can be removed after five days.

Precautions before hymen repair surgery:

Hymen repair surgery should avoid the menstrual period. It is better to schedule the surgery three days after the menstruation is clean to ten days before the menstrual period, that is, any day between three days after the menstruation is clean to ten days before the menstrual period can be scheduled for hymen repair surgery. Women with sexually transmitted diseases or vaginitis should be treated first, and then arrange for hymen repair surgery. Hymen repair surgery cannot be arranged during pregnancy.

The process of hymen repair:

The process of hymen repair is: trim the edges of the ruptured hymen into a wound, and suture the submucosal and inner and outer mucosa in position; or circularly suture the hymen to reduce the circular aperture of the hymen, so that it can pass through a small finger. For severe hymen rupture, it is advisable to strip part of the mucosa on both sides of the vaginal orifice and pull the hymen towards the center of the vaginal orifice for suturing.

Gynecological experts kindly remind: Hymen repair surgery is to restore the already damaged hymen or create a new hymen through cosmetic surgery methods. Before marriage, the integrity of the hymen represents the purity and flawlessness of women. If there is no blood from the hymen rupture during sexual intercourse after marriage, it may imply premarital infidelity. The effect of hymen repair surgery depends on the choice of hospital. Regular professional hospitals have guarantees for hymen repair surgery, and the results are good with no side effects. However, surgeries performed in some irregular hospitals may produce adverse effects.
