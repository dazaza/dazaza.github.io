Today, we still want to introduce a good WebOS: webbr.

image

webbr is an online operating system based on Flash technology. The UI style design is quite good, but also has a web2.0 flavor. The construction theory of webbr is based on the concept of SNS and socialization. Share their interests among contacts, their friends and their network. Social based discovery services that enable you to find new friends, groups and interests. On the webbr platform, you can use webbr chat rooms, profiles and webbs to communicate and share with friends. In addition, webbr offers a small number of online applications, but webbr feels like it is still in the early stages of beta, with fewer applications and content available. But judging from the foundation of webbr, it will be strong in the future. Of course, it also benefits from the generosity of investors.

webbr is also an exciting visual platform. Compared to Jooce, it's a fair fight. But they are not products of the same UI style.
