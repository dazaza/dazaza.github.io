In Adidas's collaboration with Kazuki, they considered that some fans enthusiastic about Superstar might develop a liking for this series. Therefore, they later decided to add a Superstar model. Of course, this shoe did not appear rushed despite being hastily initiated. Adidas teamed up with Kazuki Kashiwa and brought in Japan's renowned street fashion brand-whiz.

Today, I will introduce the only pair of Superstars in Kazuki's series (according to current data). There is no need for me to explain the status of Superstar. I originally thought O BｙO would 100% adopt new themes and create new shoes, but it seems I was wrong. In fact, upon careful consideration, the era of brainwashing consumers is completely over. Creating different styles to meet different consumer demands is the mainstream, and of course, all major companies are doing just that.

As the only Superstar in the O by O series, this pair may give everyone an impression of mediocrity in terms of color matching. However, due to its scarcity, this shoe has already become more valuable than the Kball low and Kball High. Moreover, whether it will be launched in China remains uncertain, so when you see a price tag of 2000 RMB in some shoe stores, don't be surprised.

The sneakers use suede as the material for the upper. Suede, which is unpopular in China, is actually the top choice for sneaker enthusiasts in Europe. This special preference is akin to how our parents always consider genuine leather as a reference for purchasing shoes. Personally, I also like suede very much, but the natural conditions in Beijing are not very suitable. The Comme des Garcons x Moncler 365 series made its debut in Tokyo, so...

The body of the shoe uses burgundy as the main color theme, a color choice that hasn't been seen for a very long time. From afar, it indeed looks very high-end. It is unclear whether this choice was Kazuki's personal requirement or the favorite of Whiz's responsible person.
