Through some channels, the suspects purchased the contact list of officials at or above the division level in Zhanjiang City, then used "arbitrary number display" software to impersonate the family members of two department-level city leaders for fraud.

A person familiar with the telecommunications industry told me that domestic and international numbers can be displayed arbitrarily. These so-called "mobile phone number changing" softwares are essentially typical IP softwares. When a person's mobile SIM card has activated the number-changing software service, both calls and text messages not only go through the mobile operator network of the mobile SIM card, but also through the IP cracking call network of the software. That is, when the mobile phone dials the other party's phone, it will definitely enter a server. Through the illegal gateway set up by the "number-changing" software, you can make calls with this "phone number".

Reporters learned that after going through the investigation, filing, trial, and other procedures by the public security organs, the case was recently announced in court. The court believed that the suspects Xie Zhi and Xie Hu worked together, impersonating state organ staff multiple times to carry out telephone fraud crimes, attempting to gain huge profits. Their actions violated Article 266 of the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China, which could be identified as fraud. At the same time, considering that the criminal acts carried out by the two defendants did not cause serious harm or influence to the victims, it was considered an uncompleted crime. Moreover, during the commission of the crime, Xie Hu was under 16 years old. Therefore, they were sentenced to 14 months and 10 months of fixed-term imprisonment respectively for the crime of fraud.
