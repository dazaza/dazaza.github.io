Below, I will introduce the most authoritative ranking of the top ten collagen brands. First, let me explain what collagen is!

Collagen is an extracellular protein and is the most abundant protein in the human body, accounting for more than 30% of total proteins. It is widely found in the skin, hair, bones, teeth, tendons, etc., with the highest content in the skin. It is the "key" that ensures smooth and delicate skin full of elasticity. There are 27 types of collagen in the human body, and the type of collagen in different parts varies. Collagen forms a fine elastic net in the skin, locking in moisture and supporting the skin like a scaffold.

In the skin, collagen acts as a "spring," determining the elasticity and firmness of the skin; it also acts as a "reservoir," determining the water content and storage capacity of the skin. Collagen directly determines the hydration, smoothness, tightness, and "skin age" of the skin. As Dr. Brandt, the father of global collagen, stated: "The process of skin aging is the process of collagen loss." Collagen is hailed as the best beauty product today and has already sparked a new storm in the beauty industry worldwide.
