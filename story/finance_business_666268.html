Valentine's Day: Cai Shaofen shows off pink roses, while Xiao S flirtatiously tweets with her husband (pic)

by chinamei0e6 on 2012-02-15 16:00:31

Introduction: Today is the romantic Valentine's Day. Will the stars' Valentine's Day be extraordinary? Today, many stars have posted their respective Valentine's Day snapshots on Sina Weibo. Fang Zuming (Weibo) and Chen Bolin (Weibo) have shared their sweet photos together; Xiao S (Weibo) and her husband bravely expressed their love on Weibo, engaging in sweet flirtation; Cai Shaofen (Weibo) has showcased the pink roses sent by her husband, while Leung Man-tat (Weibo) made exquisite cakes for her fans.

Fang Zuming posted a Weibo picture with Chen Bolin.

Fang Zuming and Chen Bolin made an appointment, Cai Shaofen showed off pink roses.

Yesterday at 1:46 PM, just before Valentine's Day, Fang Zuming happily but somewhat helplessly forecasted on Sina Weibo that he would spend tomorrow's Valentine's Day with Chen Bolin, and also posted a funny photo with Chen Bolin. Both of them squatted on the ground looking up at the lens, Fang Zuming was smiling happily, while Chen Bolin stared wide-eyed without laughing. This combination of one laughing and one serious was quite amusing. Chen Bolin also retweeted this Weibo post, jokingly asking if they should exchange gifts in the future. Fang Zuming's Weibo post sparked great interest among netizens, having been forwarded more than three thousand times and received over a thousand comments by the time of this article.

Cai Shaofen showed off roses on Weibo.

Cai Shaofen and her husband Zhang Jin (Weibo) have always been a very sweet couple. How could Zhang Jin not show his affection on such a special day as Valentine's Day? Early in the morning, Zhang Jin prepared a fresh bouquet of pink roses for his beloved wife Cai Shaofen along with a card. Cai Shaofen showcased the flowers sent by her husband, where each delicate pink rose must resemble the blush on Cai Shaofen's face at this moment. Cai Shaofen proudly declared her happiness: "I woke up early to receive the Valentine's Day flowers from my lover, I am so moved! My love... I love you so much."

Xiao S and her husband mutually express love via Weibo, Leung Man-tat showcases her homemade exquisite cake.

Xiao S and her husband Mike are considered a model couple in the entertainment circle. Xiao S, as a wife, has maintained the sweetness of their first love even after being married for many years. On Valentine's Day, they expressed their mutual love from afar, envying others. Mike posted a heartfelt message on Weibo at 7:46 AM today: "Even though I can't be by your side this Valentine's Day, I want to tell you: I love you more than I did the second before! Happy Valentine's Day!" Such bold and deep confession was touching. Xiao S responded to her husband's declaration of love: "I often think about you, look at you, and feel that loving you for a lifetime is not enough! Happy Valentine's Day~" Their sweet Weibo flirtations attracted many netizens' praise and forwarding. By the time of this article, their mutual expressions of love had been forwarded more than eight thousand times and received nearly four thousand comments.

Leung Man-tat's Weibo

Not long after her wedding, Leung Man-tat was highly spirited and made a very beautiful heart-shaped cake at home, brimming with love. Her husband Sergio must be exceptionally happy to have such a talented Chinese wife. Leung Man-tat wished all lovers to be sweet and loving on Weibo: "Happy Valentine's Day! I give everyone a handmade Valentine's Day cake, wishing you and your lover to be sweet and sticky every day! Every day is Valentine's Day! Feliz día de San Valentín!"
