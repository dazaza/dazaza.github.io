Fund companies open shops on Taobao, where funds can be bought for as low as 10 yuan

by anonymous on 2013-09-18 21:52:45

When it comes to buying funds, you may have thought about it but never taken action. There are many reasons: lack of knowledge, inconvenience, not having enough money...

However, by the time of "11·11", none of these will be an issue as fund companies are massively opening online stores on Taobao, making the purchase of funds increasingly convenient. If you're confused, there will be dedicated customer service available to assist you; if you don't have much money, you can buy funds specifically developed for Taobao customers.

With a minimum investment of 10 yuan, what are you waiting for? Pay attention now!

Journalist's Experience: 32 Fund Flagship Stores Land on Taobao

In the first half of this year, netizens suddenly noticed that fund company flagship stores were gradually appearing on Taobao, undergoing renovations. After June, the number of these fund flagship stores rapidly increased.

Last week, I searched for "fund company" in the Taobao store section and found that Fuguo, GF Fund, Yifangda, ChinaAMC, Guotai, Debang, China Merchants, Galaxy, Huaan, and Wanjia, among others, had already launched a total of 32 official flagship stores online. Just one week prior, only 28 could be found on Taobao. Among them, Boshi, Guotai, Yifangda, Huaan, and others still displayed messages like "Under renovation, please stay tuned, we'll see you soon."

Diverse Decor Styles of Online Stores

Browsing through Taobao's fund stores, I found that the completed fund online shops each had distinct styles. Fuguo Fund went with a cute theme, featuring colorful Q-version graphics while simultaneously providing the most comprehensive fund financial knowledge education. GF Fund and ICBC Ruian maintained consistency with their official website decor styles, with GF Fund opting for a bright orange theme and ICBC Ruian presenting itself in red and white.

I contacted some fund companies that currently do not have Taobao stores yet, and they all expressed that they are actively preparing to open stores on Taobao. Some fund companies stated that their Taobao stores might go live within two months.

Opening Time Continuously Delayed

Despite the enthusiastic trend of fund companies opening stores, these flagship fund stores are still "unemployed." Among them, the most developed Guangfa Fund and Guofu Fund online stores already have products on display, but purchases cannot be made yet.

When will the fund online stores officially open for business? "We originally expected to open on August 28th," said an e-commerce professional from a fund company. After missing the deadline at the end of August, rumors spread that the opening date was set for September 9th. However, the time has passed, and the fund stores on Taobao still haven't opened. An informed source revealed to me that once approved by regulatory authorities, these fund online stores will be ready to open. The latest arrangement suggests that the first batch of public mutual fund Taobao stores might officially open on November 11th of this year.

However, Taobao is extremely cautious about this. A related person from Alipay Finance clearly stated to the media: the launch time is still uncertain, and there is no product launch timeline.

Continuous Stream of Consultants

I noticed that even though the stores haven't opened yet, some online shops have already equipped themselves with a considerable number of customer service personnel who are already providing fund consultation services to investors.

In recent days, I logged into Taobao at various times daily, and during working hours, most fund company customer service icons showed as online. Even past 10 PM, some company online stores still had customer service staff available. I attempted to contact one of the fund customer services, and the response was both timely and polite. Interestingly, the fund store customer service rarely used popular Taobao phrases like "dear." This customer service representative told me that although several products listed on the company's Taobao page show transaction records, they are still in internal testing and not yet available for external sales.

Once the online store officially opens, Taobao-savvy netizens will find it very easy to buy funds: simply choose the desired fund, input the required shares, click "confirm" and make the payment, and you will quickly purchase the fund.

"Currently, there are more people consulting via Taobao online stores, mostly asking when the store will open. There is also a significant portion of customers inquiring about products and thresholds. Overall, these inquiries come from people who are unfamiliar with funds, and our main task is to provide fund-related information," said Xiao Zhao, a customer service representative from a fund company. Since the launch of the Taobao fund store, consultations have been continuous, giving him great confidence in the industry's future prospects.

The "Diao Si" Reversal Stuns the Industry

The fund companies' emphasis on online stores is closely related to the "Diao Si" reversal achieved by Alipay and Tianhong Fund through Taobao in June this year.

Unlike banks that hold large deposits, the amounts of money online can only be referred to as "Diao Si." However, it was these small funds that caused Tianhong Fund's scale to rapidly expand in just about three months. Reports suggest that Tianhong Fund's scale has already approached 50 billion yuan. This indeed made the fund industry realize the vast potential of accumulating small amounts of money through the internet.

Netizens Want to Know: Is There a Suitable Fund for Me With Limited Funds?

For the vast majority of investors, the establishment of fund company stores on Taobao is undoubtedly a good thing. Buying funds no longer requires searching all over the internet; everything can be handled on Taobao alone, and the purchasing process is simple. However, everyone is more concerned about whether buying funds on Taobao can truly bring real benefits to investors.

What New Products Are Available for Taobao Customers?

Tianhong Fund's collaboration with Taobao through Yu'e Bao demonstrated the enormous potential of accumulating scattered small amounts of money through the internet.

To cater to the smaller investment amounts typical of online investors, some fund companies have already taken action. Guotai and TEDA Macroview have specially designed funds exclusively for Taobao. Among them, Guotai Fund designed the "Guotai TAOJIN Internet Bond Fund," with a minimum investment threshold of only 10 yuan. Meanwhile, TEDA Macroview Fund submitted the "TAO LI Bond Fund" specifically targeting Taobao customers on May 21st. According to the CSRC's published information, both products have already been approved.

Are Transaction Fees Cheaper When Buying Funds on Taobao?

Currently, the advantages of the funds sold in Taobao stores in terms of transaction fees and other aspects remain unknown. However, from the initial inclusion of postal channels to the opening of third-party fund sales companies such as Haobuy Fund Network and Sumi Fund Network, there are now four major channels available for "fund citizens" to purchase funds: bank distribution, securities firm distribution, direct sales by fund companies, and third-party fund sales. What differences do these various channels present to fund investors?

The most direct difference lies in the comparison of transaction fees. Regardless of which channel is chosen to buy funds, investors need to pay three main types of fees: subscription (or initial offering) fees, redemption fees, and fund management fees. Currently, the lowest cost method is to purchase through the fund's official website. With the fund discount activities initiated by Huaxia, Shangtou Morgan, and others since August, the supplementary subscription fee for transferring out and into funds can be as low as 0 yuan.

Generally speaking, when redeeming through the fund's official website direct sales, money market funds can arrive on the same day, while stock funds that normally take around three days to arrive can also achieve same-day arrival if converted to money market funds on the same day. On the other hand, when redeeming through distributors, there are more verification processes, such as stock funds possibly taking 4-7 working days.

The sales fees of third-party sales institutions are slightly higher. For example, Zhonglu Fund Network offers a minimum 40% discount on its original 1.2%-1.5% subscription rate, maintaining a baseline of no less than 0.6%. Taking equity funds as an example, the discounted fees for subscribing through banks or securities firms range between 0.8%-1.2%, whereas the lowest possible fee through third-party fund sales can reach 0.6%. Therefore, in the "price war," the fund's official website offers the best deal, followed by third-party sales, with banks and securities firms lagging behind.
