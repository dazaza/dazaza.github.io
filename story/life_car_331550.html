Thank you for your trust and support in group buying of cars. The Beijing Yimai Group Buying Network works together with netizens to personally negotiate prices, completing the launch of 100che, which marks the beginning of standardization in the group car-buying industry. We use the advantages of interpersonal relationships, group efforts, and information technology to help car enthusiasts realize their dream of purchasing a car at a low price.

When buying a car at an automobile trading market, the market can provide you with the convenience of handling many necessary procedures and paying major taxes and fees —— that is, "one-stop" service.

When preparing for group car buying, you just need to bring all relevant documents and sufficient funds, and follow the steps below:

Step 1: First, go to the parking square within the market to select your favorite model. In front of each vehicle in the parking lot, there is a product label on the windshield indicating the model, price, selling unit, and trading seat (X area X number) as well as contact person or telephone number. Then, according to the label's indication, find the selling unit in the trading hall, locate the trading seat, and after negotiating the price with the seller, you can complete the transaction.

Step 2: After negotiating the price and selecting the vehicle, you can proceed to the on-site bank unit to pay the car payment.

Step 3: After paying the full amount, with the sales invoice issued by the selling unit, accompanied by the business personnel of the trading unit, go to the market's on-site industrial and commercial management department to handle the industrial and commercial verification and stamp the verification seal. Only after this, the invoice becomes effective. At the same time, the selling unit will deliver to you the delivery order of the commodity vehicle (which includes a handling fee of 150 yuan), car keys, vehicle qualification certificate (for imported vehicles, it is the commodity vehicle inspection certificate and customs import goods certificate). At this point, you officially own your beloved car.

Step 4: Go to the "Levy Inspection Office" to pay the vehicle purchase tax, which is 10% of the car price. That is, the selling price / 1.17 X 10% = levy amount (for imported vehicles, it is 10% of the selling price).

Step 5: Go to the market's on-site insurance institution to handle vehicle insurance procedures (paying the insurance premium). Please do not go to non-market insurance departments to handle insurance business; otherwise, if difficulties arise in claims during insurance after-sales service, the market will not be responsible.

Step 6: Handle the payment of vehicle and vessel usage tax and stamp duty at the on-site vehicle and vessel usage tax window. The usage tax amount is generally between 60-320 yuan/year/vehicle.

Step 7: Handle the vehicle inspection, license plate registration, and issue the vehicle moving permit. The market has a comprehensive service department that can handle vehicle inspection and license plate registration services for you.
