First sentence: "People are forced to change." Everyone has potential, living in peace leads to death in adversity, so when facing pressure, don't be anxious, maybe it's just life's little test for you. Believe in yourself, everything can be handled well. Urgency can drive heroes to Liangshan, times create heroes, poverty leads to change, people only gain power under pressure.

Second sentence: "If you are simple, the world will be simple for you." Simple living leads to a happy life, contentment brings joy, tolerance brings greatness. Nothing should be thought complicated, the mental burden is heavy, and you will blame heaven and earth. Periodically delete unpleasant memories.

Third sentence: "Life has no rehearsals, every day is live broadcasting." Sometimes I wonder, if life were like a video game, where you could restart if you messed up, what would life be like? Precisely because time flows irreversibly, each day cannot be retrieved, so cherish every moment, respect parents, care for children, be considerate to loved ones, be kind to friends.

Fourth sentence: "Having talent is like being pregnant, time will reveal it." People, don't be overly self-righteous, the Earth will continue to spin without you, throughout history, those who relied on talent ended up badly. So, even if talented, remain humble and diligent, focus on your own affairs, gold will shine eventually.

Fifth sentence: "In the past, meeting a thousand cups with a friend was common, now, a thousand cups without a friend is rare." Not a great drinker, I understand the taste of liquor, but I feel the charm of friendship. Life without friends is lonely and incomplete, but due to busy lives, contact diminishes, and friendship fades. So, take some time, chat with friends, let the camaraderie rise in laughter, and when friends are in trouble, stand up and support them, even if unable to help, comfort is the greatest support.

Sixth sentence: "If life takes a wrong turn, ending is progress." People always find it hard to correct their mistakes, they also find it hard to discover their errors. Sometimes, knowing the mistake, yet unable to stop, leading to repeated mistakes, grasp the right direction, adhere to principles, resist temptations, don't seek temporary pleasure at a heavy cost, if wrong, must stop.

Seventh sentence: "Two tragedies in life: one is complete despair, the other is overconfidence." Modern people seem particularly fragile, newspapers daily report numerous celebrities suffering from depression, these people must have moved from one extreme to another. Believing oneself to be perfect and omnipotent, a small setback can lead to extreme self-doubt, even losing the will to live. Find a proper position, enjoy life's pleasures.

Eighth sentence: "Life and love are the same, missing love is missing life." What is love? It confuses and captivates, facing love, be bold, express your love, seize the moment, don't wait until it's too late. People age and die, however one lives, it's a lifetime, missing love is missing life's brilliance.

Ninth sentence: "Rich people eventually become couples." Modern society becomes increasingly materialistic, love becomes dispensable, thus "rich people eventually become couples" is the most accurate description of modern love.

Tenth sentence: "To succeed, you need friends; to achieve great success, you need rivals." Competition leads to development, because of rivals' existence, because of the unwillingness to lose, efforts are made to do one's best. Thus, sometimes, rivals have more power than friends, there are no eternal rivals, but eternal friends, sometimes rivals can become friends.
