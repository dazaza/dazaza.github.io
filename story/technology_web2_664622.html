7 Classic and Trendy Spring Outfit Combinations for Stylish MMs

by shenli470v on 2012-02-13 17:12:06

The following are seven classic fashion trends for individualistic mms in spring, they are super IN, and fashionistas must not miss them.

In the spring clothing coordination pictures, the flower elements of the black lace undershirt echo with the embroidered coat, and the casually rolled-up cuffs make the Chinese elements more modern and fashionable. The improved version of the harem pants maximally creates a sense of verticality, and the tightened lower part of the pants balances the expanded shape and stretches the overall line ratio. The wide black waist sash at the waist becomes the eye-catching sign of the golden section.

In the spring clothing coordination picture, the white cashmere cardigan and white rain boots become the highlights of the upper and lower body respectively, and the different shades of black in the middle segment shuttle through the hollowed-out, leather, and satin. The elegant flower pot collar, the sexy hollowed-out waist, and the wild leather pants are tied together by a black belt to show the avant-garde fashion of women.

The classic blue and white stripes, the sailor sentiment that has been popular for several generations, the spacious and falling wide-leg pants are stylish and handsome, and can conceal small defects in the legs. Using belts as decorative embellishments enriches the layers of the upper body. Remember to choose a pair of high-heeled short boots to stretch the overall visual lines.

In the spring clothing pictures, the gray embossed suit, the wide shoulder pad design increases the strong womanly charm. The vintage color matching is wrapped under the gray crisp suit, the upright momentum of the shoulder pad and the dazzling colors create a retro taste. The purple tights not only respond in color but also become a sharp tool to neutralize the smooth lines.

In the spring clothing coordination picture, the vest combines the Chinese hemp fabric in style, and the ultra-long design makes the coordination more diversified. The black and white interlaced color matching is like Chinese ink painting, elegant and generous.

Red has always been one of the most oriental characteristic colors. The close-fitting base layer woven with black gold and silver threads flashes in the handsome red short suit, and the length over the buttocks forms a perfect proportional response with the high-waisted design of the short suit.

The white coat like armor is handsome, and the multi-layered design of the shoulders, chest, back, and collar makes the white rich and vivid. The hard armor-style coat is dotted with soft gold satin, and the layered ruffled collar spreads in the tough white fabric. The pleated shrinkage pants of the metallic color series, the falling texture and the heavy metal gloss create a powerful atmosphere.
