Fanduodi Network (http://www.fanduodi.com), established in December 2011, is a new type of Taobao shopping cashback platform in China. Its simple operation mode has been highly praised by users. Within two months of its launch, the user base reached 100,000 users.

Q1: How to use Fanduodi for Taobao cashback?

1. Select the desired item on Taobao and copy the product name (or link).

2. Enter Fanduodi, paste the product name (or link) into the search box, and click to check the discount.

3. If there is a hidden discount, click "Buy on Taobao" in the search results, then place and pay for the order on the newly opened Taobao page.

4. Return to Fanduodi and click "Cashback Now", then authorize it. (Important! You will only receive your cashback after completing this step!)

5. Monitor the order status to ensure the cashback is confirmed, and wait for us to make the payment to you.

PS: It is recommended to use IE or Google Chrome browsers for cashback shopping.

Q2: How is the cashback time calculated on Fanduodi?

1. Generally, orders will be credited within 1 working day after you complete the payment and return to Fanduodi to click "Cashback Now".

2. Some orders require you to confirm receipt of goods and return to Fanduodi to click "Cashback Now", after which the cashback will be credited within 3 working days.

3. The cashback time starts being calculated only after you update the order status by returning to Fanduodi and clicking "Cashback Now". Without updating the order, we cannot track your order status!

4. If you do not receive your cashback for an extended period, it may be due to abnormal reasons such as the inability to receive payments via Alipay account. Please contact customer service for assistance.

Q3: How to use the shopping cart?

1. If there are items already in your shopping cart, please clear them first.

2. Check each item you wish to purchase sequentially through the search results, enter the Taobao product details page, and add the item to the shopping cart. Repeat this process for all items.

3. After following the above steps to add all items to the shopping cart, complete the payment uniformly, then return to Fanduodi and click "Cashback Now". Be sure to check if the cashback for each item is successfully tracked.
