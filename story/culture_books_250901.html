"Crystal Clear" Ming Xiaoxi's complete debut work

by jqrxass on 2009-10-22 17:34:20

The lively girl, Ming Xiaoxi, on her first day at the academy,

accidentally annoyed three super troublesome handsome teenagers.

After several twists and turns, she gradually became a part of their lives.

However, after saving Bing, who was drunk, and bringing him back to the apartment,

this incident was reported by the school newspaper as a shocking scandal.

This drew the anger and challenge of Bing's "fiancée."

Bing then orders Xiaoxi to be his girlfriend

and moves into her apartment to start a "cohabitation" life.

Meanwhile, Xiaoxi also realizes

that the talented teenager Che has been covering deep loneliness with his elegant smile.

"The Crystal Clear" - Part One

This girl is naturally inclined to stand up for justice. She grew up in the most famous "Changsheng Martial Arts Hall," personally trained by her father, the hall master, who is known as the "First Person of Wulin," Ming Changhe. With her natural talent for martial arts, she has never lost a fight against hoodlums like A Fei from a young age. Her motto is "When you see injustice, draw your sword to help."

"The Crystal Clear" - Part Three

"Ah, are there really such beautiful female bodyguards in the world? Her eyes are icy cold, yet they deeply suppress passionate emotions! Hmm? In the novels I've read, cool female bodyguards always end up having breathtaking and shocking romances with the people they protect! But wait, why don't you choose her as your fake girlfriend, but instead pick me, this unfortunate person? Oh — I get it, it must be because she looks down on you, so you're using me to try and ignite her jealousy!"

"The Crystal Clear" - Part Three

Ming Xiaoxi sighs and looks at Dong Shuiyue: "I don't want to cause trouble either, but unless Mu Liubing is willing to voluntarily give himself to Tie Saxiang, she will never let me go. Now that things have developed to this point, we can only see how it goes. However... Mrs. Dong, you don't need to worry too much. I am the invincible Ming Xiaoxi. As long as I'm careful in the future, it should be fine!" She strikes a "Popeye" pose, making everyone chuckle lightly.

"The Crystal Clear" - Part Four

"My father runs the most famous 'Changsheng Martial Arts Hall,' and my aspiration is to help my father expand the martial arts hall in the future. I want 'Changsheng Martial Arts Hall' to be all over the world so that Chinese martial arts can be promoted. However, promoting martial arts cannot completely follow the old traditions anymore; advanced business philosophies and management methods must be adopted to make it more widely accepted by people around the world. Therefore, the professional direction I will choose to study in the future is — Business Management."
