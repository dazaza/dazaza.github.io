After observation on raising, this kind of species is easy to raise, tame in temperament, strong in adaptability, fast in growth, high in reproduction rate, better in medicinal value and more remarkable in economic value. Breeding facilities are perfect: The company takes agricultural science as its advantage and life science as its feature. From the domestic market perspective, in recent years, with the rapid development of the traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) industry, the TCM market is increasingly prosperous. Moreover, due to people's renewed trust in TCM and the great development of Chinese patent medicines, there is a situation of insufficient supply of valuable TCMs, leading to their market prices constantly rising. In the early 1980s, the market purchase price of centipedes was only about 4 jiao per piece, but now, it has risen to 2 yuan, and the supply is still extremely scarce. The market supply of centipede venom is even more inadequate.

The economic benefits of breeding No.2 Scorpion Centipede are significant. While providing valuable TCMs to society and benefiting humanity, those who breed centipedes can also gain high economic benefits. As a valuable medicine, centipedes have a large market demand. Artificial centipede breeding is gradually becoming a promising cause.

Supported by Huazhong Agricultural University: A key university under the national "211 Project" – Huazhong Agricultural University collaborates with Wuhan Nongke Humin Biological Engineering Co., Ltd. to jointly study and promote scientific farming and enrich farmers through the centipede breeding technology of Humin No.2 Golden Head Centipede.

As a high-tech enterprise integrating research, breeding, teaching, promotion, technical service, production, sales, and import-export trade with modern bioengineering, special breeding, and insect resource development at its core, it has excellent foundations and a complete system.
