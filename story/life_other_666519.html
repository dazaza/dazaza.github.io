Waiting for a person who is in love but cannot be together

I don't know why I am waiting for a person who is in love but cannot be together. I also can't understand why I am so stubbornly waiting for such a person who can only be in love but cannot be together. I clearly know that this was an irreparable mistake from the very beginning, yet I find myself sinking deeper and deeper uncontrollably, unable to extricate myself.

Is this a mistake of love? Is falling in love with someone else's partner a mistake? Is falling in love with someone you shouldn't a mistake? Of course, I wouldn't dare say it's entirely correct either. Love can be without calculation of gains or losses, and without consideration of success or failure. However, marriage cannot be like that. A married person has too many responsibilities. One cannot claim there is no longer any feeling, or that there was never any feeling to begin with. Yet, since a marriage has been established, one must take on the responsibility. We must all take responsibility for this, whether it brings happiness or sorrow. How could we console ourselves with such weak excuses?

Yet, letting go of this love is so unbearable, so painful. The love that was once fully devoted now has no result. I can only watch from afar, love silently. The pain in my heart, how can it ever be relieved? The only thing I can do is to love carefully, which brings two-sided suffering. Unable to let go yet unable to love freely, what a sorrowful matter this is!

Waiting for a person who can only be in love but cannot be together requires care. When I need your care and affection, you will not possibly be by my side. Perhaps you are with the person who belongs to you, and what am I then? I am nothing. All I can do is to let thoughts surround me, let my mind guide me, let my tears flow quietly, think of you silently, look at you attentively. All I can do is wait, wait for you to give me a call or some information about you when it's convenient for you.

Waiting for a person who is in love but cannot be together also requires a sincere and tolerant heart. Hide all grievances, hide all wounds because I fear seeing any dissatisfaction in your eyes. I fear hearing your faint sighs filled with complaints. I fear seeing any traces of pain on you. I can make myself suffer, but I don't want you to get hurt. That would be more painful than my own pain, making my guilty heart even more full of deep remorse.

Waiting for a person like you, who can only be in love but cannot be together, I have no regrets. Even if there are too many scars, too much dissatisfaction, I will try my best to smile it off. Even if one day our love comes to an end, I can proudly tell myself that I have loved, sincerely loved, and truly given my all. Even if in your heart there is actually no place for me, you don't care about me at all, I don't care. I won't let you see my wounded heart bleeding quietly, I won't let you see my silent tears. I will not blame you. I am willing for you to forget me. I am willing for you to suddenly remember me on a lonely night, to feel my gentle prayers and silent blessings.

But I don't want your sadness, your pain. Although you can only be the person I deeply love but cannot always be with, I hope that you are happy every year and every month, happy every moment, safe every minute and second. That's enough. Then I can rest assured. I don't pray for too much, nor do I dare to expect too much.

Waiting for you, a person who can only be in love but cannot be together, I do it willingly, without regret. But I don't understand why it has to be this way. Loved, hurt, pained, tired, yet I cannot reject your love, cannot let go of my love for you. I can only forever, silently, and quietly wait for you, a person who can only be in love but cannot be together.

Loving a person is not easy, forgetting a person is even harder. Yes, loving a person is a very bitter thing, but why can't I stop giving? Missing a person is a very tiring thing, but why can't I resist missing you? Waiting for a person is a very foolish thing, but why am I still obstinately doing it?

I wait for you wholeheartedly, wait for you, a person who can only be in love but cannot be together...
