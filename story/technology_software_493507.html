My sister's classmate from faraway Sichuan has returned.

by jzwfeifeic on 2011-09-22 18:06:44

Our car arrived at the foot of the Qinling Mountains, and I looked at the distant Qinling Mountain Wildlife Park. I suddenly envied those animals so much; they get to admire the beautiful Qinling Mountains and breathe in its air every day.

The dark mountain waist was covered with white snow, like a beautiful white silk ribbon falling from the sky onto the Qinling Mountains, making the mountains appear much gentler, just like the misty, cloud-surrounded Qinling Mountains I saw when I was little, equally lovable.

The car was speeding, and the Qinling Mountains were getting taller and more majestic. They connected with the high sky, and I felt we were getting smaller. I could clearly see the thin layer of snow on the mountain waist. In some places, the snow was thicker, covering the dark color of the mountain; in other places, the snow was lighter, blending with the dark mountain to form the colors of a black-and-white ink painting, very beautiful. I wanted to gently touch the always-distant Qinling Mountains with my hand, and I wanted to feel its existence. All of this was right before me, not in a dream.

As night began to fall, our car finally stopped outside a farmhouse. The Qinling Mountains in front of me became even darker and taller. The Qinling Mountains were right in front of me, but I could only quietly look at them, unable to touch them. I would have to enter the mountain to be able to touch its distant yet familiar rocks. The farmhouse yard hung with strings of fiery red peppers, and red persimmons hung outside the windows. After drinking a cup of tea inside, I came out again. I wanted to take another look at the Qinling Mountains, but when I came out, the winter's early darkness had already enveloped the Qinling Mountains. I could only see the black night outside. The Qinling Mountains that were just as high as the sky and stretched endlessly without beginning or end had suddenly disappeared. No matter how hard I tried to find them, I could only see the black sky and black surroundings. It was just like when I was little, and the endless beautiful Qinling Mountains would suddenly disappear into the clouds, while I waited for their reappearance.

I really wish I could stay and fully appreciate the beauty of the Qinling Mountains. I can feel it standing there in the black night, looking at me just like I look at it.

The Qinling Mountains were getting closer to me, and I was getting happier. I could clearly see each of its mountain ranges, towering and with sharp angles, colored in dark hues. Such sharply defined and continuous mountain ranges extended into the clouds on both sides, giving you the feeling that their continuous mountain ranges are boundless.

We took a classmate's car and went to eat together. The car was speeding on the road, and piles of white snow kept appearing by the roadside. The wild grass and winter trees flashed past. The little girl in the car fell asleep quickly, her hand still tightly holding her bright red dough figure rose.

After driving for a while, just after turning a corner, I saw the Qinling Mountains. Towering and continuous mountain ranges stretched endlessly in front of us. My heart was filled with surprise. When I was little, I once saw them faintly under cloudy weather, their mysterious ink-blue mountains stretching endlessly. Due to environmental pollution, I never saw such a sight again. Now, the Qinling Mountains were right in front of me, and I was both excited and happy.

In the afternoon, my sister's classmate who lived far away in Sichuan returned and called my sister to go play with classmates. My sister took me and my daughter along to play. We searched for a long time to find the place, and when we arrived, they had already gone boating and played at the South Lake. They all brought their children, and the kids were already happily playing together. The girls held dough figure roses, which were bright and beautiful. The water of the South Lake was clear, and the winter scenery along the shore was very beautiful.
