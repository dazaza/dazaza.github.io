Galaxy Securities: Weichai Power's main business is at the peak of prosperity, and the future lies in product line extension＿Stock

by dx0966632 on 2009-12-03 22:45:40

The trend of engine sales structure is improving. In the third quarter, the company's engine sales volume increased slightly by 2.4% compared to the previous quarter, but the sales revenue increased by 11%, reflecting the improvement in the sales structure of engine products. The proportion of heavy truck engines and high-pressure common rail engines in heavy truck engines has significantly increased, and these products have higher unit prices and gross profit margins than other engine products.

Production and sales have reached a historical peak of prosperity while the prices of major raw materials remain low, leading to a gradual increase in the company's gross margin level each quarter.

We predict that there will be neither a significant increase nor a decline in the sales volume of semi-trailer tractors in 2010. First, domestic logistics transportation demand will not experience a sharp drop; secondly, from the perspective of replacement demand, based on the current stock, calculated with an average replacement cycle of 5 years, the annual replacement volume of heavy trucks is approximately 500,000 units; thirdly, the sales volume of semi-trailer tractors in the first quarter of 2009 was at an absolute low point in recent years, so there will still be significant year-on-year growth in the first quarter or even the first half of next year.

Potential competitors are unlikely to pose heavy pressure on Weichai in the short term. The production and matching technology of heavy truck engines have high content. Due to scale and brand advantages, Weichai's industry position is difficult to shake. Moreover, Weichai will also establish its advantageous position in the general powertrain field by expanding into smaller displacement engines in the future.

We maintain our forecast for the company's earnings per share (EPS) for 2009-2011 at 4, 4.76, and 5.33 yuan respectively. The company's current valuation level is still relatively low, and we maintain our "cautious recommendation" investment rating for the company.
