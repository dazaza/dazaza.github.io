The highest stars of the Republic of Korea coalesce into the strongest family!!

"Family Outing" is a segment of the large weekly Sunday variety show "Sunday Sunday Night" on South Korea's SBS television station, hosted by MC Yoo Jae Suk with several regular guests and special guests.

Each episode, artists form a family and choose a rural household in the countryside to spend two days and one night. During this time, the family must complete various farming tasks left by the owners, inter-team competitive games, cooperative cooking to fill their stomachs, and family competitions for better sleeping positions, presenting an original ecology comedy program featuring the artists.
