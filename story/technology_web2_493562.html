Summary of Basic Services for Social Applications

by ybcz0519 on 2011-09-22 18:52:44

### I. What are Social Application Basic Services?

This year, we have seen an increasing number of social services. Originally, services that existed in the form of tools are now integrating social elements, such as relationships and sharing based on these relationships. We have also witnessed the emergence of many excellent social applications, like Zhihu for social Q&A services and Aikuwang for social bookmarking and sharing services. Meanwhile, there are also numerous services designed to serve these social applications, such as providing social login solutions, social comment solutions, and social feedback solutions. Here, I refer to these services, which can become standard components of social services, as social application basic services. Today, I will simply summarize them and share with you.

### II. Social Login Service

Denglu is a social login tool provider: By using Denglu's social login tool, users on your website can log in via Renren, Kaixin001, Sina Weibo, Tencent Weibo, Taobao, Douban, MSN, Google, and more than ten other social media accounts to connect to your site, enabling single sign-on across your network of sites. (Via) Compared to domestic services, foreign counterparts like Janrain are already relatively mature and widely adopted.

**Main Functions Include:**

- Use services from Renren, Kaixin001, Sina Weibo, Sohu Weibo, Tencent Weibo, Taobao, Douban, MSN, Google, etc., to log into your website.

- Social Sharing: Pop-up overlay, video preview support. Set screenshot and description text. Display source of the website.

- Data Reports: Analyze data on social login and social sharing for your website to help optimize user experience.

- Single Sign-On: If you own multiple websites or partner sites, users need only log in once to any site to access others seamlessly.

- User Information Configuration: Obtain real names, gender, age, work address, study address, interests, etc., to assist with marketing.

Link: [http://www.denglu.cc/index.html](http://www.denglu.cc/index.html)

### III. Social Comment Service

Youyan is a social comment plugin provided for webmasters. Users can log in with seven social networks (Sina Weibo, Renren, Tencent Weibo, QQ Space, Sohu Weibo, NetEase Weibo, Kaixin001), and comments will be automatically forwarded to their social networks. You can view the effect in the comment box below this site. (Via)

**Main Features:**

- Simple and Quick Deployment: Generate two lines of code through "Youyan" and add it to your webpage. Currently offers WordPress plugins for easy use by WordPress users.

- Customization: Box width, maximum comment length, number of comments per page, feedback style, text, and custom CSS.

- Rich Management and Statistics: Active users, popular articles, daily comment counts, return flow numbers, etc., easily managed and viewed by webmasters.

- Comprehensive Social Network Support: Supports login with Sina Weibo, Renren, Tencent Weibo, QQ Space, Sohu Weibo, NetEase Weibo, Kaixin001 accounts, with automatic synchronization of comments.

- Sync Service: Every article posted by the webmaster supports automatic posting to Weibo (requires binding of Weibo account). Comments within the article are synchronized by forwarding and replying to the webmaster's Weibo.

- Can coexist with existing comment boxes or replace them entirely.

Link: [http://uyan.cc/](http://uyan.cc/)

**Pinglun.la | Social Comment System:**

Pinglun.la is a simple and easy-to-use social comment platform. Website owners using Pinglun.la can fully understand the behavior of website users and improve user activity and revisit rates. Users using Pinglun.la can record their comments across the entire internet. (via)

Link: [http://www.pinglun.la/](http://www.pinglun.la/)

**Baye.me** is another social comment service, currently released in Beta2 version.

**Main Features:**

1. Email Reminders: Notifications for new site comments, pending review comments, daily reports of pending reviews, and reminders when users receive replies.

2. Weibo Sync Function: Syncs to Sina and Tencent Weibo.

3. Inherits Sina Comment Box Functionality: Users can choose whether to display the Sina comment box, showing real-time updates from Sina Weibo.

4. Directly Delete Comments on the Page (Webmasters can delete any comment; users can delete their own).

Link: [http://baye.me/](http://baye.me/)
