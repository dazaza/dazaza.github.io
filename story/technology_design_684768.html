Weibo repost

by teway2012 on 2012-03-01 10:28:19

I. Activity Purpose

To celebrate the opening of Tianwei's Taobao flagship store, boost popularity and sales, and also increase the popularity of Tianwei's official Weibo account to expand its influence, it is proposed to combine a Weibo retweet competition with the distribution of tens of thousands of Taobao discount coupons to benefit consumers.

II. Activity Content

1. Initiate a Weibo retweet competition titled "To celebrate the opening of Tianwei's Taobao flagship store, give away free Taobao shopping vouchers worth tens of thousands of yuan."

2. The retweet theme content is: "@Sichuan Tianwei Food, and retweet this Weibo post to participate in the draw and win a free 100-yuan shopping voucher. To celebrate the opening of Tianwei's Taobao flagship store, as long as your name contains one of the three characters 'Da Hong Pao', you can exchange for a 50-yuan shopping voucher for just 1 yuan through the official Tianwei Taobao flagship store. Each person is limited to one exchange only, and the deadline is March 20th. Hurry up!"

3. Activity Rules

(1) A total of 5 members will be randomly selected from the Weibo retweet competition winners. These members must be registered Taobao users to facilitate the issuance of discount coupons, without being restricted by having the characters 'Da Hong Pao' in their names. Winners will be notified by the system.

(2) As long as the member has retweeted this Weibo post via their personal Weibo account and their Taobao username contains one of the three characters 'Da Hong Pao', they can also contact customer service to exchange 1 yuan for a 50-yuan shopping voucher.

(3) Each member is allowed only one exchange opportunity, and repeated participation will be invalid.

(4) Members must be real-name authenticated Taobao Alipay users to confirm their real names. The first 20 successful exchanges each day will be valid, based on the designated Taobao exchange link (the retweet competition is not subject to this restriction).

(5) Shopping vouchers can only be used at the Tianwei Taobao flagship store to purchase any item in the store and can also offset shipping fees. Shopping vouchers cannot be exchanged for cash or split, and are valid until March 30, 2012. Expired vouchers will be void.

III. Notes

1) This activity is initiated on the Weibo event platform, and prizes will be randomly drawn by the Sina Weibo event platform;

2) The list of winners will be announced by @Sichuan Tianwei Food within 5 working days after the end of the event;

3) Winners must send their real name, Taobao username, ID number, phone number, and mailing address to @Sichuan Tianwei Food within 48 hours of the announcement of the winner list. Failure to provide the information within the time limit will be considered as automatic abandonment, and no replacement shopping vouchers will be issued;

4) The interpretation rights of this event belong to the official Sichuan Tianwei Food Co., Ltd.
