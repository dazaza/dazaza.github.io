On a day that was supposed to be filled with joy, Nick O'Bannon went to watch a car race with his friends. Suddenly, Nick had an ominous hallucination - several race cars would collide, burning debris would fly into the stands, killing his friends, causing the upper stands to collapse, and falling rocks would land right on him... After waking up from the nightmare, Nick frantically persuaded his girlfriend Lori and friends Janet and Hunt to leave quickly. Nick's terrible hallucination turned into reality, and the four of them narrowly escaped by a hair. They felt they had cheated death and decided to cherish life and live on. But for Nick and Lori, misfortune had only just begun. Nick's visions of death continued to appear, and the survivors of that event died one by one, their deaths extremely terrifying. Nick must find the final way to save himself before death arrives. This is the latest work of the classic horror film "Final Destination", and also the first 3D version of the series.
