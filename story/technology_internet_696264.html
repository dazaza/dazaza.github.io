What to do if your hair is dry and frizzy

by king2f4j on 2012-03-07 23:08:26

Here are some methods that are good for hair:

1) Washing your hair with beer works well. I've been doing it for over half a year - it's my personal experience! After frequently washing your hair with beer, it will become softer and smoother, and there will be a faint smell of beer. This is because the grains in beer are beneficial to hair and will promote faster hair growth! After long-term use, your hair color may change slightly, not as pure black anymore, with a slight yellow tinge. However, this is not noticeable unless you look closely! Usage: Every time I wash my hair, I pour an appropriate amount of beer into the water used for washing hair. Then I wash my hair! Do not pour beer directly onto your hair, as it can corrode the hair roots! Note: After buying beer, remember to open the cap a few days to let the alcohol evaporate before using it, otherwise the alcohol can damage your hair! I think the biggest benefit of washing my hair with beer is that my hair becomes shinier and more lustrous! And it grows faster than usual! The only downside is that after prolonged use, my hair has turned slightly brown!

2) Olive oil hair care: You can put olive oil on your comb before washing your hair, then use the comb to evenly apply the olive oil to your hair. Be careful not to get it on your scalp, otherwise it will become very oily. Then wrap your hair with a hot towel for a while, and then wash your hair. Alternatively, you can add olive oil to the water used for washing your hair. Remember not to add too much, otherwise it will be too oily and if not rinsed clean, others may think you haven't washed your hair for several days. You can also mix olive oil with your conditioner or hair mask or serum. After washing your hair with shampoo for the first time and drying it slightly with a towel, apply the mixture to your hair and wrap it with a hot towel. You can wait for about ten minutes, reheat the hot towel, continue wrapping, and then rinse it off. The benefits of using olive oil for hair care are that your hair becomes smoother and less frizzy. The effect is really good, just as good as beer. I really like using olive oil for hair care, and the effect is really great! It is especially suitable for dry, fluffy, and frizzy hair.

Another small method to make your hair straighter: After washing your hair, do not dry it completely. Comb your hair smoothly and let it dry naturally. This will make your hair straighter.

Also, you can carry a small comb with you for convenience. Frequent combing can help smooth and straighten your hair.

Dietary therapy: You can also improve through dietary therapy, which requires long-term persistence to be effective. Black beans have the reputation of being "the lady who turns hair black", and they are excellent for hair care. Daily consumption can effectively improve sleep and help reduce hair loss. They also have multiple effects such as promoting hair growth, turning hair black, and making hair shiny. They taste great too, so isn't it wonderful to enjoy delicious food while beautifying your hair?
