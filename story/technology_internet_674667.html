**Maintaining Solid Wood Stairs**

1. Proper use and maintenance of the stairs are essential to ensure their long-term durability and timeless beauty.

2. For daily cleaning, use a vacuum cleaner or soft broom. If there are stains, clean them with a wrung-out cloth.

3. Stubborn stains can be removed with alcohol or cleaning agents, but avoid using strong corrosive solvents like turpentine or thinner.

4. It is recommended to wear soft-soled shoes when stepping on the stairs to reduce wear.

5. Handrails provide dynamic support, and trembling is a normal phenomenon of elastic support.
