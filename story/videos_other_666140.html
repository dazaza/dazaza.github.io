Small facial plastic surgeries that make you prettier

Careful netizens often discover that some female stars become more beautiful after a period of absence. It is not obvious that they have had plastic surgery, but they become more attractive unconsciously. Are there such colleagues or friends around you? In fact, this is not magical at all - small facial plastic surgeries have made them prettier without others noticing. So what are these small facial plastic surgeries? Let's take a look together.

1. Double Eyelid Surgery

Among the five senses of the human face, the eyes are the most expressive. Drooping single eyelids often give people a sense of monotony, swelling, and lack of vitality. To make up for this deficiency, double eyelid surgery is the most common method. Currently, the commonly used methods are roughly divided into three categories: incision, suture, and Korean-style double eyelid surgery. The surgical method is usually determined based on the thickness of the customer's eyelids.

2. Eye Bag Removal

The aging and relaxation of the lower eyelid skin, the weakening of the connection between the skin and the orbicularis oculi muscle fibers, and the protrusion of excessive fat tissue in the eye socket - this leads to the appearance of eye bags. Although the appearance of eye bags is a normal phenomenon in the human body, eye bags make people look tired and lacking in vitality. In addition, severe cases may cause some complications, such as ectropion of the lower eyelid, entropion of the lower eyelid margin with inverted eyelashes, full-face facelift wrinkle removal, causing tearing, eye irritation, and other discomfort symptoms.

3. Brow Lift

The rise of brow lift is related to the popular eyebrow tattooing from a few years ago. Uneven eyebrow tattooing skills have made many people's eyebrows look like "sleeping dragon eyebrows", "earthworm eyebrows", "faded color eyebrows", which are outdated and old-fashioned. Brow lift removes a strip of skin above the eyebrows, fixes the eyebrows upward by suturing, removes the original eyebrow tattoo marks; at the same time, it can also play a role in lifting the eyebrows, making the facial contour look younger. Generally, during the brow lift, other cosmetic surgeries such as drooping corners of the eyes, upper eyelid laxity, and crow's feet can be performed simultaneously.

4. Buccal Fat Pad Removal (Slimming Face Surgery)

The buccal fat pad is located between the muscles on both sides of the cheeks, playing a role in smoothing and cushioning. It is an organization that can be "sacrificed". An enlarged fat pad can cause excessive fullness of the cheeks, making the facial contour unclear. Removing excess buccal fat pads can make the cheeks smoother, more立体(stereoscopic), and make people look younger.

5. Mandibular Angle Reduction

The increase in mandibular angle reduction surgeries is the most prominent feature of facial plastic surgeries in recent two years. The popularity of petite-faced beauties has made mandibular angle reduction surgery highly sought-after. Mandibular angle reduction involves sawing off and grinding down the protruding parts of the mandibular angle to create a petite-faced beauty effect in one step. There are various methods for mandibular osteotomy, and now mostly intraoral incisions are used, leaving no scars on the cheeks.
