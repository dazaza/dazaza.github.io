by anonymous on 2012-03-03 15:27:49

With an increasing number of merchants engaged in the wholesale of car beauty products, car maintenance products and car cleaning products, the competition in the car products market is becoming increasingly fierce. The operation of car beauty products, car maintenance products and car cleaning products also begins to focus on strategies. The boss of Kerry Car Beauty Shop Management Software Network made the following summary of the operating elements of Wenzhou car product stores, hoping to give some inspiration to merchants engaged in car product operations.

1. Operation Introduction: First, determine a specific parking lot, then negotiate with the property management department. The principle of car product dealers regarding site usage should be clear, and the site usage fee should be reduced to the minimum. Moreover, the operation of car product stores is beneficial to the parking lot. A wide variety of car beauty products, car maintenance products and car cleaning products can enhance the service features of the parking lot. Of course, it is also beneficial to the community.

2. Location Selection: The choice of parking lot is very important. If there are several parking lots around the car product store, then an investigation should be conducted first to see which parking lot has better operating conditions. First, choose a parking lot where cars are relatively concentrated. Second, interpersonal relationships are important. Many details of the operation of car product stores are related to the property management department of the parking lot. If good interpersonal relationships can be established, it will be very helpful for the operation of car products.

3. Project Confirmation: The car products operated in the parking lot include car beauty products, car maintenance products, car cleaning products, etc. However, each store has different operating capabilities and characteristics, so the selected car product operation projects are also different.

[Operation Know-how]

Although car beauty does not have as high a technological content as car production or car repair, it is still a technical job. From washing and waxing to repairing and decorating, every task must be done conscientiously. Technical level and service quality cannot be compromised. In addition, during the Spring Festival, some small promotional activities can be held, such as giving away free cleaning services or Chinese knot decorations, to increase the added value of services and accumulate customer resources at the same time.
