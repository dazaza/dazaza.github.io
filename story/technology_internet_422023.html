1. Objective

To standardize the workflow of attendance management to facilitate job development in this position and provide a basis for employee monthly salary settlement.

2. Scope of Application

This procedure applies to the attendance management position.

3. Key Responsibilities

3.1 Information management work for various forms such as employee leave application forms, rest adjustment forms, reward/punishment notices, personnel transfer forms, daily attendance reports, security & dormitory supervisor sign-in sheets, etc.

3.2 Daily attendance management work for employees.

3.3 Handling of employee resignation procedures.

4. Work Process

4.1 Information Management of Various Reports and Forms

4.1.1 Employee Leave Application Form

4.1.1.2 Leave application forms exceeding six days must be signed off by the HR Manager; work-related leaves exceeding three days must also be confirmed by the HR Manager; leave applications between three to six days are confirmed by the HR Section Chief.

4.1.1.3 After confirmation by the HR department, assign numbers to these forms and input them into the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Employee Leave Registration - New Entry. After completing the entry, click Save/Review sequentially.

The numbering format is year-month plus four-digit number. For example, if a leave form is received on April 1, 2010, write the number 10040001 in the top right corner of the leave form;

In the new entry, the start time of the leave can only be 0 o'clock or 12 o'clock, with half-day leave counted accordingly;

In the new entry, enter the leave form number in the Approval Opinion section.

If an error occurs during input, find the incorrect record in the filter, double-click to open it, click the inverted triangle next to Review and select Cancel Review, after successfully canceling the review, you can directly modify, then save/review; if the record is not needed, after successfully canceling the review, close the record, click the incorrect record, then click Delete in the menu.

4.1.1.4 Completed leave forms should be arranged in reverse order by number, bound into volumes, and archived with a cover attached. Note on the cover that these are employee leave forms and specify the number range.

4.1.1.5 Employee cancellation of leave is reported by the department assistant to the HR department, following the same method as 4.1.1.3. If the attendance has already been reviewed, then add a new leave record in the employee leave registration, set the leave time to the required cancellation time, then cancel the review, and delete the record.

4.1.2 Rest Adjustment Form

4.1.2.1 Rest adjustment forms submitted by various departments need to be approved by the department manager before being confirmed by the HR department. The original rest date and the current rest date on the rest adjustment form should not exceed 45 days apart, and the original rest day should be Sunday.

4.1.2.2 After confirmation by the HR department, enter the rest adjustment form into the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Employee Rest Adjustment Management - New Entry.

In the ERP system under Human Resources - Report Analysis - Employee Attendance Details, input the employee ID to check the employee's attendance. Only normal absences on public holidays can be adjusted for rest. Public holidays can be set in the leave settings.

If an error occurs during input, the modification method is the same as for leave forms.

4.1.2.3 Employee rest adjustment forms are archived together with leave forms.

4.1.3 Reward/Punishment Notice Form

4.1.3.1 Reward/punishment notice forms submitted by various departments need to be signed off by the department head for review and approval, and the recipient needs to sign for confirmation; those who receive a major demerit or above or a major merit or above need to be approved by the department manager, and the reward/punishment for team leaders and clerks and above need to be approved by the department manager.

4.1.3.2 Reward/punishment notice forms approved by the department are submitted to the HR department for signature confirmation.

4.1.3.3 After confirmation by the HR department, assign numbers to these forms and input them into the ERP system under Human Resources - Personnel Management - Reward/Punishment Management - Review/Unreview - New Entry.

The numbering method is the same as for leave forms, and the approval opinion in the new entry includes the reward/punishment notice form number;

If an error occurs during input, the modification method is the same as for leave forms.

4.1.3.4 Completed reward/punishment notice forms should be arranged in reverse order by number, bound into volumes, and archived with a cover attached. Note on the cover that these are reward/punishment notice forms and specify the number range.

4.1.3 Personnel Transfer Form

4.1.3.1 Piecework personnel transfer forms need to be signed off by the department head for review and approval, and timed work personnel transfer forms need to be signed off by the department manager for review and approval. Timed work personnel transfer forms are handled by the HR assistant. In the new entry, the effective date must be in the current month, otherwise it cannot be saved.

4.1.3.2 Personnel transfer forms approved by the department head are entered into the ERP system under Human Resources - Personnel Management - Personnel Change - Review/Unreview - New Entry, modifying the unit name, department/team, position, attendance shift, attendance location, effective date.

If an error occurs during input, the modification method is the same as for leave forms.

4.1.3.3 After entering, the personnel transfer forms are handed over to the archive administrator for archiving.

4.1.4 Daily Attendance Report

4.1.4.1 Daily attendance reports submitted by various departments need to be signed off by the department head for review and approval.

4.1.4.2 Absenteeism reported in the daily attendance report is entered in the leave section, with "Daily Attendance Report" entered in the approval opinion section.

4.1.4.3 Employees reported as self-departed in the daily attendance report are entered in the ERP system under Human Resources - Personnel Management - Resignation Application - Query - New Entry.

In the new entry, the resignation date is the last day of the employee's attendance, the reason for resignation is self-departure, the return card type for those returning their ID cards is normal return, and for those not returning their ID cards is resignation without return. If there are registered properties, notify the assistant to go to the finance department to transfer the properties.

4.1.4.4 Completed daily attendance reports are placed in the unit's daily attendance report folder. Monthly attendance reports are sorted by date, bound, and stored in the archive cabinet.

4.1.5 Security & Dormitory Supervisor Sign-in Sheet

4.1.5.1 Review and ensure that the security & dormitory supervisor sign-in sheet has been signed off by the department head.

4.1.5.2 Check the attendance of unsigned personnel, urging those on leave to submit leave forms and those adjusting rest to submit rest adjustment forms.

4.1.5.3 Place the sign-in sheet in the security group daily sign-in sheet folder, sort monthly sign-in sheets by date, bind, and archive.

4.2 Daily Attendance Management

4.2.1 Attendance Anomaly Card Replacement

4.2.1.1 Before 14:00 every afternoon, extract the previous day's attendance anomalies from the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Daily Attendance Acceptance - Employee Abnormal Attendance HR Department Announcement/Review - File - Export Excel, save it to My Computer - E Drive - Attendance Anomaly Announcement Folder and Attendance Anomaly Status Table on the file server (Fserver).

Departments that cannot use shared documents (such as the polishing group) need to print out the attendance anomalies and hand them to the department assistant.

4.2.1.2 Handle attendance anomalies based on the attendance management regulations and emails returned by various departments in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Daily Attendance Acceptance - HR Department Announcement/Review.

In the anomaly category, choose to display all abnormal punches, select the unit name for the unit requiring card replacement, select the attendance date for the card replacement date, select the department code for the department requiring card replacement, and click Search. Based on the attendance situation returned by various departments, select the corresponding category in the anomaly category, input the cause in the anomaly cause, and then click Publish/Supervisor/Review.

If the card replacement is incorrect and needs modification, select the corresponding unit name, attendance date, department code, or individual employee number/name, select to display all punch records in the anomaly category, click Search, locate the incorrect card replacement record, click the record, click the inverted triangle next to Review and select Cancel Review, click the inverted triangle next to Supervisor and select Cancel Supervisor Signature, then modify the anomaly category and anomaly cause, and then click Supervisor/Review.

Pending materials are entered in the leave section; leave must have a leave form to input leave.

4.2.1.3 Each department's attendance situation must be confirmed and reviewed by the respective department head via email.

4.2.1.4 Promptly urge departments that have not returned attendance situations within three days.

4.2.1.5 Personnel accompanying the card need to track the card punching situation, confirming whether they are normally attending work.

4.2.1.6 Responsible for daily checks and maintenance of the attendance card machines, ensuring employees can normally punch in for attendance. If there is a large amount of identical attendance anomalies for employees, notify the IT department to inspect and repair the corresponding card machines.

4.2.1.7 Responsible for handling special attendance issues for individual employees, such as modifying attendance shifts or attendance locations.

If the employee's profile was initially input incorrectly, in the ERP system under Human Resources - Personnel Management - Personnel Profile - Query, filter out the employee's profile, click Modify, and make direct modifications; if direct modifications cannot be made, filter out the employee's profile, click Edit - Batch Personnel Changes, the Personnel Profile Change dialog box appears, select the personnel change object and the changed value, click Confirm to make the modifications.

If the employee's shift is incorrect, first modify the profile, then in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Report Analysis - Employee Attendance Details, correct the erroneous attendance caused by the previous shift error. Select the shift to be modified, click the Schedule Shifts option in the menu, the Personal Attendance Adjust Shifts dialog box appears, modify the shift in the Post-Adjustment column, click Save. Click the Day Processing option in the menu, the Daily Attendance Data Processing dialog box appears, select the time of the modified shift, and click Process.

If the employee's shift is incorrect, first modify the profile, then in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Report Analysis - Employee Attendance Details, correct the erroneous attendance caused by the previous shift error. Select the shift to be modified, click the Schedule Shifts option in the menu, the Personal Attendance Adjust Shifts dialog box appears, modify the shift in the Post-Adjustment column, save. Click the Day Processing option in the menu, the Daily Attendance Data Processing dialog box appears, select the time of the modified shift, and click Process.

4.2.2 Video Surveillance Inspection

Every day in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Report Analysis - Employee Video Attendance Inspection Form, select the date, unit name, and department, click Search, check if there are any proxy punches among the video-punched personnel.

Select the abnormal punch photos, click Penalty Slip/Print, issue penalty notices for proxy-punched personnel. The penalty notice is submitted to the HR supervisor for signature confirmation, handed to the department assistant for confirmation and review by their department head, and then returned to the HR department. The processing method for penalty notices is the same as for reward/punishment notices.

4.2.3 Monthly Attendance Summary at the End of Month

Before the 5th of each month, complete the processing of the previous month's attendance anomalies, after completion, perform the end-of-month attendance data processing in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Attendance Data Processing - End-of-Month Attendance Data Processing. In the End-of-Month Attendance Data Processing dialog box, select the processing period, i.e., the month, select the processing object as all, and click Process. After the end-of-month attendance data processing is completed, before the 8th, check and review the previous month's attendance in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Report Analysis - Employee Attendance Summary Monthly Report.

Select the unit name, salary type, and summary month, click Confirm, search for the employee monthly attendance summary report. Check if the number of people in the monthly summary matches the current number of employed people, then check if the full attendance bonus, personal leave, sick leave, marriage leave, maternity leave, bereavement leave, work-related injury, official business, absenteeism, statutory holidays, tardiness/early departure, excessive own-card replacements, and attendance deductions are correct. You can check by descending or ascending order of a specific item.

Employees whose shifts are non-follow-the-card and where the scheduled attendance equals actual attendance receive a full attendance bonus of 30 yuan; click on the employee's summary record to view, click the Attendance option in the menu, check if the leave and attendance match, if the person's attendance shows leave or absenteeism but there is a punch record, then check the person's leave form, card replacement email, or daily attendance report, if it is an input error, modify it in the leave registration; if an employee repeatedly arrives late or departs early, check if the shift is wrong or if there was an error in the transfer.

For payroll errors caused by the previous month's employee attendance errors, write an internal contact form, specifying the amount or items to be supplemented, submit it to the HR supervisor for signature confirmation, then to the finance manager for signature approval, and then to the finance department's salary accountant to be added to the next month's salary.

4.2.4 Setting Public Holidays, Statutory Holidays, or Annual Leaves

When encountering public holidays, statutory holidays, and annual leaves, uniformly set them in the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Daily Attendance Acceptance - Employee Public Holiday/Annual Leave - New Entry.

Select the company, holiday type, date, in the batch public holiday setting, select the department to set the public holiday, for individuals in a department who do not need to set the public holiday, input the employee ID of those individuals who will be working overtime in the list below. For individual public holidays, input the employee ID of those who need to set the public holiday in the individual public holiday setting. After completion, click Save-Review. Public holidays, statutory holidays, and annual leaves can also be set in the employee leave registration.

4.2.5 Shift Scheduling Settings

In the ERP system under Human Resources - Basic Data - Shift Scheduling, you can modify and add shifts.

In the Shift Scheduling dialog box, click on the shift name to modify, click Edit in the menu, modify the shift punch times in the basic settings on the right. Allow up to 3 minutes of tardiness when starting work, the first punch time and the third punch time should be the shift's start time plus 3 minutes, the advance time is the start time for effective punching, and the post-shift time is the end time for effective punching.

If a new shift needs to be added, click New in the menu of the Shift Scheduling dialog box, input the new shift's code, name, daily working hours, daily card swipe times, and remarks indicating the department using this shift in the basic settings on the right, then set the punch times, and click Save in the menu.

4.2.6 Collection of Samples for Face Recognition Machines and Fingerprint Punching Machines

Piecework personnel use video card machines for punching, timed work personnel use face recognition or fingerprint punching.

Face recognition punching requires collecting face samples on the face recognition card machine. After collection, the face sample is stored on the face recognition machine, the attendance location in the personnel profile needs to be set on the corresponding face attendance machine, the card swipe type is face recognition, otherwise the attendance system server will automatically delete face samples inconsistent with the storage card machine. In the ERP system under Human Resources - Attendance Management - Attendance Data Processing - Face Attendance Machine Template Interaction - Face Attendance Machine Template Management, select the corresponding face attendance machine in the face attendance machine, click the personnel template to upload on the right side of the face machine, click Upload in the menu, the face sample is uploaded and saved on the attendance system server.

Fingerprint punching requires collecting fingerprint samples on the HR department's fingerprint card machine, after correctly setting the card swipe type and attendance location in the personnel profile and saving, click Upload Fingerprint Machine, after uploading is complete, click Save again. After collecting the fingerprint sample, the fingerprint sample will automatically be saved in the fingerprint library, if the employee changes the attendance location, just set the attendance location correctly and save, then click Upload Fingerprint Library and save again.

Personnel dining at the Golden Emperor Administrative Restaurant need to select the Golden Emperor Administrative Restaurant card machine in the Personnel Profile - Attendance Location - Dining Card Machine, then upload fingerprints from the fingerprint library and save.

4.3 Handling Employee Resignation Procedures

4.3.1 Resignation

4.3.1.1 Check and ensure that all resignation application forms submitted by various departments have been approved by the department manager.

4.3.1.2 Resignation application forms approved by the department manager are submitted to the HR department supervisor for confirmation.

4.3.1.3 Resignation application forms with dates not yet reached are kept in order by employee code.

4.3.1.4 When the resignation application date expires, recover the employee's work permit, issue a resignation handover form, and transfer it to the HR assistant for employee exit interviews.

For piecework employees, the resignation handover form needs to be handed over and signed in sequence by the department supervisor, the dormitory administrator of the management department, and the accommodation registration clerk. For timed work employees, the resignation handover form needs to be handed over and signed in sequence by the department supervisor, the dormitory administrator of the management department, the accommodation registration clerk, and the property administrator of the finance department.

External residents do not require the dormitory administrator's signature. The attendance clerk registers the virtual network short number provided by the company for the employee, and sends the registered virtual network short number via email to the general manager's office and the finance department every Saturday. For deputy section chief level and above employees leaving, a confidentiality commitment letter needs to be signed.

4.3.1.5 Resignation handover forms should be recovered. After the resignation handover form is fully signed, input it into the ERP system under Human Resources - Personnel Management - Resignation Application - Query - New Entry.

4.3.1.6 When an employee resigns, if they do not have a salary card, the department assistant can apply for cash settlement of salary on their behalf. The application must be signed off by the department manager and the finance manager.

4.3.1.7 After receiving the application for cash settlement of salary, fill out the salary settlement form, urge the department of piecework personnel to calculate their output and report
