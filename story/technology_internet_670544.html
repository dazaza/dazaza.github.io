I grabbed my schoolbag filled with snacks and happily ran to school - today was a spring outing! We boarded the bus at 8 o'clock and headed for our destination. On the way, we ate and drank a lot, as if we hadn't eaten for three days. After we were full, the tour guide started to speak. She first introduced us to the scenic spots and then talked about some precautions. Suddenly, the guide asked if there were any special students in our class, and everyone immediately shouted out my name, asking me to sing a song. Helplessly, I sang one. Carrying a busload of laughter, we arrived at our destination - Guangzhou Chimelong Happy World! From time to time, we heard the roaring sound of roller coasters and people screaming, which scared us so much that we didn't dare to ride the roller coaster. Zengzeng, Xiahxia, Shuyi, and I found a game called "Popeye", and we queued up. But the queue was so long that after waiting for ten minutes, it still wasn't our turn, and it was already time for lunch. We were quite annoyed and decided to eat first. To our surprise, eating was also fun. We ate while watching several foreigners chopping wood and sawing wood, and watched them dive and swim. All the unhappiness disappeared. After eating, we played the "Rainbow Flyer". The "Rainbow Flyer" is a machine for two people. You press a button, and the machine will swing us on the track, rushing down from above, which is very fun. We waited in line for ten minutes before it was our turn. Zengzeng and I rushed to a machine enthusiastically and sat down without hesitation. The game started, and we pressed the button hard. Unexpectedly, this was an old machine that moved slowly and couldn't swing high. Looking at Shuyi and Xiahxia who were almost swinging to the clouds, Zengzeng and I were still enjoying the "rocking bed" on the old machine. When the game ended, Zengzeng and I were quite disappointed because it wasn't fun at all, all because of the broken old machine! To prevent Zengzeng and I from being disappointed, Xiahxia and Shuyi joined us to play "Popeye". Finally, it was our turn, and we excitedly rushed in. Three of them shared a machine, and I shared another with two other classmates. The game began, and the machine first turned slowly in circles. Suddenly, it bounced up and down in the air, spinning around with great centrifugal force, which was very exciting! As it spun, the machine suddenly stopped, tilting us and the machine together with a "thud". Just when we thought the game was over, the machine suddenly bounced again, moving up and down in the air and rotating counterclockwise, super exciting! After playing "Popeye", we were still dizzy and disoriented as we went to play "Hurricane Flying Chair". The "Hurricane Flying Chair" is a chair suspended by ropes. People sit on it, and as the machine turns, we sit on the chairs and spin in the air, with many chairs spinning together. There weren't many people playing this game, so we quickly got to play. I chose a more outer "flying chair" and sat down excitedly. The machine started with a "dingling", lifting me into the air and beginning to spin. Wow, it was truly refreshing, cool and comfortable, and I could see the scenery inside the park! Moreover, when the chair reached its highest point, it felt like I could kick the roof with my feet, which was incredibly enjoyable! After getting off the "flying chair", I felt dizzy as if I was still flying in the air. Next, we played many more games. It was time to gather, and we hurried back to the entrance of the amusement park to assemble. We were reluctant to leave here. Today's sun was so bright, and our mood was the same.
