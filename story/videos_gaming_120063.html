Special Sniper 2 (扮演 a jungle warrior)

by youxiruanjian88 on 2009-01-19 09:16:02

In the game, players take on the role of a jungle warrior with very limited main weapons available. The primary arsenal consists mainly of three different types of sniper rifles. Notably, the game developers put significant effort into modeling and texturing these rifles, making them one of the highlights of the game! Secondary weapons include pistols and combat knives for close-range fighting. The gameplay is quite straightforward: a directional map in the lower-left corner indicates the next course of action, guiding players forward with arrows. Of course, along the way, players will encounter numerous enemies, most of whom are armed with AK47s. Snipers hidden deep within the jungle also pose a potential danger to players! During the journey, players are assisted by a brave mercenary teammate who must be strategically controlled for movement and attack methods. Players need to maneuver through the jungle and engage in combat with enemies. When faced with large-scale enemy confrontations, this invincible warrior truly unleashes his power!

Game Graphics:

Most of the game takes place in dense jungles and narrow mountain paths. The vegetation and grassy terrain are depicted with moderate detail, effectively portraying a tropical savanna's mountainous jungle in nearly realistic fashion! However, due to the engine's limitations, the game's lighting effects are rather poor. The clouds in the sky appear flat and static, like unmoving textures. There’s no ripple or light reflection on the water surface, and the waterfalls in the mountains only give off a misty impression. In an era where DX9.0 game special effects are rampant and GPUs are being pushed to their limits, it’s surprising that this game would go against the trend and use an outdated engine with no special effect optimizations. Perhaps there are other standout features?

Music and Sound Effects:

Each level in the game has its own background music accompanying the player during combat. These tracks are all somber and low-pitched, helping players remain highly alert in the dense jungle. Despite the repetitive melodies, they never feel tiresome! The sound of the firearms, however, feels somewhat hollow, lacking distinctiveness and falling far short of realism.

Gameplay and AI Design:

The game's controls are exceptionally simple. Besides the basic settings, F1 and F2 are used for teammate attack mode settings, and F6 for instant saving, which needs to be frequently utilized! The enemies' AI is not weak. When players snipe from a distance, the enemies immediately scatter upon hearing the gunfire and assume attack positions—either crouching or finding cover. They then shoot towards the player from different directions. In short, enemies do not stand still waiting to get hit. Moreover, the game offers four difficulty levels. On medium difficulty, I sometimes found myself scrambling to keep up with the enemies during tactical maneuvers, wishing I could grab their AK47s and unleash a barrage of fire! When encountering large squads of enemies, finding cover promptly is crucial. If you attempt a frontal assault, enemies will encircle you from various directions, potentially leading to your demise. It’s best to stay behind cover, attacking from the side while sending your invincible teammate ahead to handle the attacks independently, significantly reducing your own damage. After all, why wouldn’t you rely on a teammate with impenetrable defenses? One downside of the game is the limited variety of weapons. Those struggling with sniping should practice more as the AK47s on the ground seem permanently in the hands of the enemies!

Summary:

The quality of the game engine dictates the sales strategy of this game, and the gameplay mode is also quite simple. Although it may seem slightly monotonous at times, the game's entertainment value and ease of play are still quite appealing! Players don't have to deal with complex 3D mazes that might leave them disoriented. This team-based shooting experience on designated routes can serve as a pain reliever for those prone to motion sickness and provides good practice for fans of the sniper mode. Overall, it's a well-entertaining small-scale game!
