PeaPod: A fully-featured Android phone management software

by magictim on 2010-09-22 16:22:01

Wandoujia is a fully-featured Android phone management software, and the development team's goal is to turn it into a "iTunes" management tool for phones. Unfortunately, I am currently using an outdated Nokia 5800 and cannot personally experience this software. Therefore, I can only provide a simple introduction to its functions. If you are an Android user and interested in this software, I would recommend that you try it out yourself.

Project homepage: http://wandoujia.com/

Currently, Wandoujia has seven main features: screen capture, backup, contact list, text messages, applications, music, and videos. It can help you:

1. [Screen Capture] Capture Android phone screens and share them on Sina Weibo. (New) Now you can show off your screen captures with the phone case!

2. [Backup] Backup/restore important data such as text messages, contacts, and applications.

3. [Contacts] Manage contact lists, easily import/export contact lists (supports Nokia, Outlook, etc., contact list formats), manage groups, and add avatars to contacts.

4. [Text Messages] Send and receive text messages on your computer, supports group messaging and message export functions.

5. [Applications] One-click installation of Android applications to your phone.

6. [Music] One-click import of online or local music to your phone.

7. [Videos] One-click import of online or local videos to your phone.

For more stories about Wandoujia, please see ifanr's special interview report: "The Story of 'Wandoujia' and Its Team."
