Website of the Year: Sina News

News is infinite, Sina delivers. For ten years, it has been the first news portal of Chinese network; the world connected by its massive information is still the visual focus and topic source for Chinese people. It established the "Homepage Large Information Volume" and "Title Open New Window" Internet news standards, which have become netizen habits; while "bullying" traditional media, it also provides new standards and communication space for the latter.

Network Visionary of the Year: Hou Xiaoqiang

He is the promoter of Chinese celebrity blogs, a pioneer of the Web 2.0 era, and the leader of commercialized online literature. He understands the nature and possibilities of the network, is good at integrating high-quality social resources, and transforms the hidden original forces and personal interests among the people into more constructive and communicative positive energy through the network. He created the most valuable website of the year for Mandarin literary dream factory.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Information Category): Ye Yan

Nominees: Caijing.com, FT Chinese, Tencent News, Wikipedia, Ye Yan

In the last century, Liang Qichao once regarded translation as a way to save the country. In today's information age, translation is an important way to open up the world. Not everyone can read foreign information without obstacles, not all information is valuable, this is the value of discovering, translating, and spreading effective information by Ye Yan. It acts as the largest broker between literature and the Internet.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Business Finance Category): Taobao.com

Nominees: Anjuke, Eastmoney.com, JiZhang.com, JD.com, Taobao.com

This is the largest mall in the world, the largest online trading market in China, one end connects with the world factory of "Made in China", the other end connects with the largest consumer market in the world. It is the only "lipstick" in the financial cold wave. It gives young entrepreneurs dreams, gives hope to otaku men and women, encourages internet professionals, and gives confidence to government managers.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Speech Category): Tianya Community

Nominees: Baidu Tieba, Kaidi Community, Mop, Tianya Community, Sina Weibo

Aiming to be the global Chinese online home, its topic setting ability comes from the trust, participation, and expression awareness of network citizens. Here, the most grassroots stars, the most distinctive Tianya dramas, and the most straightforward talk venues are spontaneously generated. Criticism of current affairs has real insight, and romantic sentiments amuse countless people. It forms the unique "Tianya Sect" in the Internet industry: with its own idols, funny points, and awkward language.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Service Category): Dianping.com

Nominees: Dianping.com, Urban Circle, Kuxun Travel, Liba.com, Wo Ai Da Zhe Wang

Wine and meat pass through the intestines, all on Dianping.com. In a country rich in delicacies and gourmet eaters, it is China's Michelin Guide and Cha's Restaurant Review. It is a successful example of gourmetism + word-of-mouth economy + Web 2.0 era, only serving the taste buds, only serving breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late-night snacks. It is the scale in the hearts of thousands of foodies, making "eating well and doing nothing" a virtue rather than a vice.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Social Category): Kaixin001

Nominees: Facebook Chinese Version, Hainei Network, Kaixin001, Mole Garden, Renren Network

It uses components to form the adhesive of semi-acquainted society, uses games to mobilize the enthusiasm of screen couch potatoes, and gradually becomes popular during office garbage time. On the one hand, it helps workplace people expand their networks through interactive mini-games, on the other hand, it reveals the characteristics of media through functions such as reposting and voting. It is proficient in the methodology of Chinese-style interpersonal relationships and understands the values of network communication, turning imported electronic face masks into localized bridge-building guides.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Audio-Visual Category): Tudou.com

Nominees: Hao Ting Music Network, Ju Jing Music, Tudou.com, Yi Qi Ting Music Network, Youku.com

It is the hub of massive videos and also the image window of folk creativity. It thrives between big media and small tastes, building an image liveliness hall and starting a feast of audio-visual entertainment that eliminates depression. It tells you, everyone is the director of life, every frame on the Internet comes from your projection of life.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Literature and Art Category): Qidian Chinese Network

Nominees: Artbaba, Qidian Chinese Network, NetEase Reading Channel, Novel Reading Network, Artron Art Network

Literature has no threshold, today's readers can create their own school and become authors; literature can also enrich, the clicks of readers make authors win real money and become millionaires or even billionaires. This website with over 4 billion daily clicks and 38 million daily text updates has become the freest text experiment field and the largest reading supplier of contemporary Chinese literature.

Most Valuable Website of the Year (Fashion Category): Haibao Fashion Network

Nominees: FTD Guanchao Network, Haibao Fashion Network, Modern Night Gathering, Onlylady, Tencent Fashion

It is an international brand classroom before China enters the luxury era, a fashion version of "Hai Guo Tu Zhi", a fashion expert who observes constellations, admires stars, has the beauty of clothing externally, enjoys sports internally, excels in talking about love and has transactional abilities. Its information is interesting, pictures have ideas, street shooting has charm, dressing up has insights, updating elegant clothes' scenery, gathering fashion tension, and continuously providing fascinating lifestyle good taste.

Small Audience Website of the Year: Douban

Nominees: Douban, Scientific Squirrels Society, Kongfuzi Used Books Network, Colorful Photography, Small Audience Player Portal Network

"Find people who share your tastes from 12703825 registered users," although the number of registered users has exceeded tens of millions and increases by hundreds of thousands daily, Douban remains a niche website because it gathers too many non-mainstream people. They hide in various Douban groups, sharing tacitly understood feelings. "Douban" has even become an adjective, equivalent to "very artistic."

Blog of the Year: Li Chengpeng

Nominees: Han Han, Hong Huang, Huang Jiwei, Li Chengpeng, Lian Yue

He is a football commentator in the broadcasting studio, a novelist in his spare time, and a public intellectual on the Internet. His writing style is as spicy as Sichuan people's nature, reviewing this society in laughter, anger, and rationality. He wrote epitaphs for Chinese football due to its lack of striving, persistently pursued the truth of "Qi Shi Ma", and behind the words, there is the independent thinking of a social citizen.

2009 Network Life Value Chart - Outside the Main Chart

Aims to select characteristic new toys, interesting new forms, creative new software, and benchmark technologies that are difficult to include in the main chart.

Input Method with the Most Media Character: Sogou Input Method

It is not just an input method, but also an entertainment tool and a micro-media. It refreshes the latest vocabulary and events in its constantly updated cell dictionary and prompt window, adapts to the writing habits of netizens, creates personalized small pleasures, and is undoubtedly the most up-to-date input method so far.

Seamless Link Communication Platform: Feixin

It is the light rail of the Internet world and the network knight of the mobile phone world. It breaks the barriers between two personal communication terminals, combining the convenience of computer input and the always-on hand advantages of mobile phones. It extends the function of mobile phones for free and maximally meets the communication needs of modern people.

Most Efficient Information Beacon: RSS

It is the message tree of the Internet world and the filter in the era of information explosion. It is a blessing for information enthusiasts, eliminating the hassle of repeatedly clicking on web pages, and capturing the vast world with just one reader. It provides an outlet for content websites and cultivates a large number of online reading websites.

Chat Tool with the Highest Living Adhesion: WLM

The latest upgraded version of MSN, WLM, brings the comprehensive integration of the network and life. The "Shared Map" function allows friends to share traffic route queries and real-time road conditions services, while "Recent Updates" makes instant chat tools borrow the interpersonal adhesion fun of SNS websites.

The Most Personal Online TV Channel: PPlive

It creates a new model of online audio-visual, realizing dual functions of video on-demand and live broadcast. It is the kaleidoscope of images on the Internet, offering comprehensive audio-visual enjoyment for netizens, and the network TV service it represents will surely become the mainstream viewing method in the future.

The Most Idealistic Shared Space: Cloud Computing

Cloud computing connects individual systems together, forming a huge demand and supply pool. It will definitely be applied in various software and service systems. It is the God's hand for small and medium-sized enterprises and individual users, you cannot see it, but it is in the sky.

The Most Comprehensive P2P Download Tool: Xunlei

It aggregates the advantages of various download tools, allowing the sharing spirit of the Internet to spread. It integrates online servers and computer resources, forming the rapid transmission of data files. Its player and Lei Qu functions further transform P2P software into communities and multi-functional user experiences.

The Most Promising Mini Community: Sina Weibo

It is an extremely simplified speech space, possessing both media temperament and personal interest; it is your private news channel and portable bulletin board, leveraging the celebrity effect to create a newer, faster, anytime, anywhere mini community. It has clear goals and is more likely to grow into the Chinese version of Twitter.
