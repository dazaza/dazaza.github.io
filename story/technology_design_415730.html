Game Network's "Shan Hai" tells a story of obtaining love through wisdom, but this story is set in ancient times. Based on the "Classic of Mountains and Rivers," the game features the seven hegemon warlords with choices of generals, including stories related to ancient society figures. "Shan Hai" concept art

Like birds and fish, ice and fire are incompatible.

In the era of the Yellow Emperor, a war broke out between the Xuan Yuan clan and the Chi You clan. The Ying Long by the side of the Yellow Emperor was unable to counter Chi You's water-controlling artifact, so they sought the help of Nü Ba, successfully annihilating the Chi You tribe. Ying Long represents water, and Nü Ba represents fire; they are opposing yet complementary forces. On the battlefield, the beautiful and affectionate Nü Ba fell in love with the brave and skilled Ying Long. Their love was discovered by the Heavenly Emperor…

As punishment, Ying Long was chained to the divine tree, unable to meet his lover. The heartbroken Nü Ba came daily to the tree, comforting Ying Long with her beautiful singing.

Day after day, though ice and fire are incompatible, Nü Ba's proximity to Ying Long caused her once-beautiful appearance to become increasingly grotesque. Yet she persisted, willing to lose the most beautiful face in the world, continuing to sing each day, growing sadder with every song. Ying Long, can you hear what I am singing? Unaware that the beautiful-voiced but ugly woman was Nü Ba, Ying Long's heart slowly calmed upon hearing the songs.

With Nü Ba's covert assistance, Ying Long returned to the celestial realm. However, Ying Long remained oblivious to Nü Ba's sacrifices. Later, urged by people's resentment and the gods' persuasion, Ying Long was ordered to descend to the mortal realm to subdue the Nü Ba who had caused chaos among humans. They fought over the Yellow Spring Sea, and eventually, Ying Long killed Nü Ba. Before dying, Nü Ba restored her original appearance and voice. Realizing the truth, Ying Long was shocked! Watching the anguished Ying Long, Nü Ba comforted him with her final song before gradually disappearing into the Yellow Spring Sea, leaving behind only her silk veil and tears of relief…

The legendary Ying Long and Nü Ba

The story of Ying Long and Nü Ba from the "Classic of Mountains and Rivers" has moved countless players. But what about Nü Ba and Ying Long? Though Nü Ba used her wisdom to save Ying Long, she couldn't save their love…
