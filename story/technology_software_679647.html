Important Notice: The board of directors and all directors of our company guarantee that there is no false record, misleading statement or significant omission in the content of this announcement, and they take individual and joint responsibility for its truthfulness, accuracy, and completeness.

1. The number of restricted shares available for listing this time is 229,050,000 shares.

2. The date on which these restricted shares will be available for listing is February 29, 2012.

Approved by Document No. [2011]1802 of the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC), Jiangsu Phoenix Publishing & Media Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "our company") publicly issued RMB ordinary shares (A-shares) on the Shanghai Stock Exchange, with an issuance scale of 509 million shares, including 229,050,000 shares issued offline, accounting for 45% of the total issuance; and 279,950,000 shares issued online, accounting for 55% of the total issuance.

I. Time of Listing of Allotted Shares

The 229,050,000 shares allotted offline in the initial public offering of our company are locked for 3 months, i.e., from the first trading day of the stock's listing (November 30, 2011) to February 28, 2012, and will be listed and circulated on February 29, 2012.

II. Number of Listed Allotted Shares

The 229,050,000 shares allotted offline in the initial public offering of our company account for 9.00% of the company's total share capital, with a total of 110 allotment objects. III. Issue Price of Allotted Shares

The sponsor (lead underwriter) of our company's IPO issuance and listing conducted the allotment according to the allotment principle stated in the "Announcement of Offline Issuance", and the issue price of these allotted shares this time is 8.80 yuan per share.

IV. Historical Changes in Share Capital

Our company was established on June 30, 2009, with a total share capital of 1,500,000,000 shares and registered capital of 1,500,000,000 yuan upon establishment.

On December 24, 2010, the Publicity Department of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee and the Jiangsu Provincial Finance Department issued the "Approval Regarding the Agreement of Jiangsu Phoenix Publishing & Media Group Co., Ltd. to Increase Capital in Jiangsu Phoenix Xinhua Bookstore Co., Ltd. with the Main Business Assets of Publishing" (Su Cai Zi [2010] No. 10). Jiangsu Phoenix Publishing & Media Group Co., Ltd. increased capital in our company with the equity of 12 companies and the main business assets of publishing. After this increase in capital, the total share capital of our company increased to 2,035,900,000 shares, and the registered capital of the company increased to 2,035,900,000 yuan.

After the initial public offering on November 30, 2011, the total share capital of our company became 2,544,900,000 shares.
