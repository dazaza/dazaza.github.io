Preparation is needed before taking photos with Heart Corner Imaging.

by caiqiao122 on 2010-03-31 10:43:47

1. Please bring a USB flash drive or external hard drive larger than 512MB. Customers who want all the negatives can copy all the photos after taking pictures, and then email us the photo numbers of the selected photos for editing and inclusion in the album at home. Customers who do not need all the negatives, such as maternity photos, will select the fine-edited photos to be included in the album immediately after taking the photos, and we will send you the edited photos after two weeks. 2. Hair should be clean and tidy. Wash your hair the day before, but do not wash it on the day of the shoot with wet hair, and do not tie up your hair. Customers who haven't had a haircut for a long time should preferably go to a barber shop to trim their hair, especially the bangs. 3. Do not wear glasses during photography as they may reflect light. It is best to wear contact lenses, and colored lenses have the best effect. 4. Drink less water and rest well the night before the photo session to prevent swollen eyes and dark circles, especially for maternity photos in Beijing. If you have a face mask at home, using it will yield better results. If possible, please trim your eyebrows at home to save makeup time when you arrive. 5. If you have favorite necklaces, earrings, or other jewelry, you can bring them for use during the photo session. 6. Remember to shave your armpit hair. 7. If you have favorite plush toys or small clothes sewn for the baby, you can bring them as props for the shoot. 8. We also provide clothing for husbands during the photo shoot. Groom yourself beforehand, including trimming beards and hair; we do not provide makeup services for husbands. 9. Makeup takes about an hour, during which time the husband can watch TV or read newspapers in the living room. You can bring your own books, PSPs, or laptops (we can provide internet access) to pass the time. 10. After the photo session, we provide makeup remover (best if you bring your own), facial cleanser, and moisturizer. You need to bring a small towel to wipe your face. 11. This studio prohibits bringing your own camera for shooting.

Reminder: Most pregnant mothers come with their husbands, and the number of people should not exceed three. We do not provide family portrait shooting services here. If you have a severe cold, please reschedule the photo session and do not bring friends with colds, as our customers are all pregnant mothers who are very susceptible to infection. Our staff must also take extra precautions, so we hope for your understanding regarding this matter.

Shooting Time: The day before the shoot, we will confirm the arrival time for the photo session by phone. On the day of the shoot, please arrive on time and try not to be too late, as it may affect the normal shooting schedule for the next customer.

Also, payment in full cash is required on the day of the shoot. Credit cards and *** payments are not accepted.
