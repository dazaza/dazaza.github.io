Angels Don't Understand Dolphins' Pain

Today, the supermarket was doing good business. I didn't finish work until nine o'clock. Handsome Zhou Yichen was waiting for me at the door with a box of egg tarts.

We sat on a bench by the street. He smiled indulgently as he watched me wolf down the egg tarts. Occasionally, he would reach out to smooth my wind-tousled hair and softly say, "You don't have to eat so fast, I'm not going to take them from you."

I stuffed the last egg tart into my mouth and glared at him. I hadn't eaten properly all day; I had only drank a few bottles of water.

He furrowed his brows and wiped the crumbs off the corner of my mouth with a napkin. He said gently, "Yanyan, can we stop working? I'll give you pocket money."

As soon as his words were out, I sprang up from the chair. "Zhou Yichen, it's none of your business what I do. You should remember, who asked you for pocket money? I am Ji Xiyang, an independent modern woman who refuses men's money and strives to assert myself."

He was silent for a moment. "Yanyan, I just think you're working too hard."

I couldn't stand to see him looking pitiful, making me seem like a shrew bullying a poor scholar. My heart softened, and I sat back down, shaking his hand back and forth. "Alright, I know you care about me."

He smiled at me and patted my head. There was a feeling in my heart like a spilled glass of water, a vast dampness spreading. I stared at him blankly, forgetting how to speak and laughing foolishly.

A white puppy came over and licked the egg tart box I had placed on the ground. Zhou Yichen petted its head. In its pitch-black pupils, I saw his worried face. Then he ran across the street to the supermarket.

He carefully poured the milk he bought into the box and used his Swiss army knife to cut the ham sausage he bought into small pieces. Seeing the puppy eating with its head down, he said affectionately, "Don't rush, no one is going to take it from you."

I felt dizzy. Those words sounded familiar. "Zhou Yichen, are you putting me on the same level as it?"

His smile was so innocent. "You're not even as obedient as it. Look, it must be starving."

I suddenly felt deeply moved. I haven't seen boys around me or those self-proclaimed handsome hooligans on the streets in a long time that could move me.

When we returned to school, he naturally picked up the puppy. It lay quietly in his arms, very gentle. I took it in my arms and said, "Zhou Yichen, you take care of its food, and I'll take care of its shelter. But you have to remember to bring it food every day."

I gave it a bath, using a lot of shower gel. The whole bathroom was full of bubbles. I called out, "Zhou Yichen, hehe, if he treats me badly, I'll torture you, tit for tat."

The stinger is always the deadliest weapon of women.

Since we brought the little dog home, Zhou Yichen somehow got a motorcycle. He shouted loudly under the girls' dormitory building. After primping in front of the mirror for a long time, I reluctantly went downstairs. The roommates were curious and looked down. Then Qing burst out screaming. When I reached the bottom of the stairs, I saw all the girls on the floor sticking their heads out of the balcony.

I deliberately pouted and said to him, "Can you be quieter? Everyone is looking at me, how embarrassing."

He casually threw me a jelly without even looking at me, only feeding the little dog. I angrily bit into the jelly on the side, thinking wickedly, they really look like a father and son!

Finally, after feeding his son, he looked up and patted the car body. "Yanyan, get on the bike." I rolled my eyes at him, "Do you still remember you need to send me to work? I thought you only cared about it and not me."

He laughed patiently. "Even the dog gets jealous of you, what a silly girl."

Why did my face suddenly feel so hot?

I got on his bike. He handed me the dog and said, "I'll come pick you up after work tonight." The bike roared to life. Amidst the envious gazes of the girls, I proudly lifted my head.

When I got off the bike at the supermarket entrance, two colleagues asked me, "Xiyang, is he your boyfriend?"

I seriously answered, "Oh, no, he's just infatuated with me."

Zhou Yichen smiled tolerantly, seemingly already accustomed to my various mischievous deeds. His voice was still so gentle, "Yanyan, remember to eat."

In the evening, the roommates surrounded me and asked, "Xiyang, what's your relationship with Zhou Yichen? Is this dog a gift from him? Xiyang, how did you meet?"

I ate the chips he bought me absentmindedly. "You guys ask too many questions, it's annoying."

They fought to bathe the dog and even sprayed perfume on it. I thought smugly, "I am the empress, I am Cleopatra, they are my servants, they are my slaves, haha."

On Children's Day, I insisted that Zhou Yichen give me a present. He looked down on me disdainfully, "You're so old, why pretend to be young!" I clung to him stubbornly like a leech. "I don't care, I don't care, I want it anyway."

Unable to resist me, he finally gave in. "Alright, old woman, what do you want?"

I dragged him happily to the Lancome counter and pointed to a spiral bottle. "I want this, Lancome Hypnôse Star perfume, 680 yuan."

His eyeballs almost fell out. "50 ml for 680 yuan! Forget it, go ask your boyfriend for it, I'm broke. Pick something more childlike for Children's Day."

I sulked while he scolded me. Finally, we stopped in front of a store. He told me to wait, and when he came out, he was holding a huge dolphin plush toy and smiling at me.

I looked at the tag. It was more than four hundred yuan. The perfume wasn't more than two hundred yuan more expensive. "You don't need that money to make a fortune."

He had an innocent expression. "But this plush toy is much bigger than the perfume and cheaper. There's no reason not to choose it. Moreover, when I saw it in the window, I thought it looked just like you, very cute."

My face turned red instantly.

During the summer vacation, Zhou Yichen had to go to the suburbs with the school football team for training. He brought a lot of dog food to my house. He said, "Yanyan, please take care of it these days."

I nodded solemnly. "Just leave your son with me."

The night before he left, I insisted he take me out to play. He thought for a moment and said, "You're twenty-one years old, it's time for you to see the world."

The bar was dimly lit, and there was a girl playing the piano. Zhou Yichen explained, "This is a quiet bar, not as noisy as regular bars."

He drank alcohol himself but ordered me a Long Island Iced Tea. This idiot didn't know that Long Island Iced Tea isn't tea, it's alcohol. Before long, I felt dizzy.

The girl playing the piano came over and greeted him. He exclaimed in surprise, "An Jinrui, how are you here?"

I looked at her hazily. She had bright eyes and white teeth, long black hair, and a red dress. Even with the dim lighting, I knew she was beautiful.

I heard Zhou Yichen, the lecherous man, tell her, "This is Ji Xiyang, my sister."

I went to the restroom to wash my face. When I returned to my seat, they were already sitting at the piano, playing a duet seamlessly with high and low notes. While cursing Zhou Yichen as a hypocrite, I drank all the Long Island Iced Tea in front of me and passed out. A man came over, "Miss, are you alright?" He pointed at my face. I wanted to push him away but didn't have the strength. He got closer and closer, unaware of the impending danger.

Zhou Yichen saved me. Afterwards, Jinrui told me that he rushed over and punched the man, knocking him down. Then he was surrounded by a group of people who started hitting him. If it weren't for Jinrui calling her friends in the bar for help, things might have gotten serious.

After leaving the police station after recording our statements, I apologized and held his hand. "It's all my fault, I've caused trouble for you."

He sighed and patted my head. "No problem, it's the older brother's job to protect the younger sister."

The next afternoon, I carried a lunchbox and walked for several bus stops to deliver it to him. His teammates said, "Wow, your arranged bride has come to deliver food!"

I spent the whole morning preparing the bento. I shaped the rice into a bear, with black beans as its eyes and a round fried egg as its bib. There were also fresh red tomatoes and crisp green vegetables. I even used red beans to form a heart-shaped pattern on the bento. Worried that the pattern would be distorted due to the bumps in the bus ride, I chose to walk.

"I've come to thank you for saving me yesterday," I said. He rolled his eyes, "Do you think one bento can settle the score?" I looked at him seriously, "Zhou Yichen, will you accept my offer to repay you?" He stared at me dumbfounded, then I laughed loudly, "You fool, you actually believed that, ha ha."

He raised his eyebrows and scrutinized the bento, poking the red beans with a spoon. "Yanyan, you really put a lot of effort into this."

"Red beans grow in the southern land, this object symbolizes longing the most. Zhou Yichen, this is my heart, can you feel it?"

During the entire summer, my focus was on taking care of the puppy and working part-time. Under my meticulous care, the puppy gained a lot of weight. Every time I came home from work, it would waddle over excitedly, looking like a ball of yarn rolling towards me.

On the afternoon of Qixi Festival, I specially took a day off to find Zhou Yichen. His teammates teased me, "Child bride, you're here. We have a day off today. Didn't he come to find you?"

My heart sank, but I forced a smile and said, "Nothing, I'll go now."

In the evening, fireworks were set off in the square. I held the puppy and stood among a crowd of couples, feeling out of place. Many girls in lace dresses and boys in white T-shirts made the scene pleasant to the eye.

When the first firework shot up, I saw Zhou Yichen by the fountain with An Jinrui. They also noticed me and the chubby puppy.

Zhou Yichen picked up the puppy and played with it. "Long time no see, did you miss me?" An Jinrui smiled gracefully beside him. I felt increasingly superfluous. I took out a gift box and gave it to An Jinrui. "This is for you both. Wishing you boundless happiness and longevity."

Zhou Yichen chuckled and said to her, "Yanyan always talks nonsense, don't mind her."

I snatched the puppy and said, "Did I embarrass you?"

Jinrui said, "Yanyan, thank you. Let's play together, it's more fun with more people."

I couldn't lose my dignity, so I waved grandly. "No, there are a lot of boys waiting for me. Have fun, you two."

As soon as I turned around, I saw something even more embarrassing. I saw Xiang Qing staring at me wide-eyed. I hastily pulled her away, but she shook off my hand, stepped forward, and pointed at Zhou Yichen's face angrily, "How could you do this to our Xiyang!"

I tightly held onto her. "No, it's not like that, Qingqing. I'll explain later." Then I picked up the puppy with one hand and dragged Xiang Qing with the other, running away while repeatedly apologizing to Zhou Yichen and An Jinrui.

The consequence of this incident was that I didn't see Zhou Yichen again until school resumed. I didn't answer his calls or reply to his messages. I didn't have the face to see him.

If it hadn't been for the fire, I probably would have continued avoiding him.

Awoken by Xiang Qing's scream in the middle of the night, I found the whole floor filled with thick smoke. The fire had spread from the neighboring building, and people were rushing downstairs in panic. Suddenly, I remembered something and shoved the dog into Xiang Qing's arms before rushing into the flames without looking back.

Yes, I was there to retrieve the dolphin plush toy. It was the first gift he ever gave me, and I had to protect it no matter what. I took a few steps holding the large plush toy and was choked unconscious. I faintly saw his anxious face.

When I woke up, I was already in the school clinic. Xiang Qing and the others were crying around me. I weakly said, "Don't cry, I'm not dead yet." Xiang Qing scolded me harshly, "Is a plush toy worth risking your life for?" Then she pulled all the sisters out of the clinic, and only then did I see Zhou Yichen in the corner, looking at me with concern. I wanted to say something to him, but I lost my words as soon as I opened my mouth.

Silent for a long time, he said, "Yanyan, let me tell you a secret I've never told anyone else. The second automatic vending machine to the right of Gate 12 at the airport is broken. If you insert a coin, two cans of cola will pop out. I've tried it. Next time, you should try it too." Then he laughed.

I didn't laugh. I said, "Sorry, the misunderstanding on Qixi Festival was because you often came to feed the dog. My friends might have misunderstood."

His eyes sparkled. He said, "That dog belongs to you too, Yanyan." Then he sighed and said, "Rest well. On Christmas Eve, I'll take you to watch the fireworks."

I buried my face in the pillow and cried secretly.

On Christmas Eve, I put on my best clothes, combed my hair meticulously, and even sprayed some perfume. I checked myself in the mirror for over an hour, afraid of any flaws. I repeated his name in my heart countless times. Despite dressing up for so long, I still felt I wasn't pretty enough, not worthy of you, Zhou Yichen.

The Christmas Eve square was crowded with people. I saw a pendant of an angel hanging from his neck, but it only had half the wings. Of course, the other half was with Jinrui. This was the Qixi Festival gift I gave them. The pair of pendants was bought with the salary I earned from working all semester. But that night when I saw them together, I realized I would never be his other half.

Zhou Yichen held my hand and said, "Yanyan, take care of yourself from now on." Tears streamed down my face uncontrollably.

Zhou Yichen and I met when we were six. He won the first prize in the children's group at the piano competition while I was playing with mud with other kids. At ten, his family moved to the most luxurious Huayuan neighborhood, while mine lived in a small courtyard nearby. At fifteen, his math score was 98 points, while mine was 68. At eighteen, fourteen girls gave him chocolates on Valentine's Day, and I ate them all. At nineteen, when he entered university, the captain of the school football team approached him, while I was busy finding a part-time job to earn money. Today, at twenty-one, he watched fireworks with me, and two days later, he would fly to the famous St. Petersburg State Conservatory with his beautiful girlfriend, while I would continue to work and study here.

No matter how hard I try, I can never catch up to him. Like dolphins, they can never become angels, and angels can never understand the pain of dolphins.

When we parted, he said, "Yanyan, don't you want to hug me?" I shook my head. "Zhou Yichen, I'm afraid once I hug you, I won't be able to let go."
