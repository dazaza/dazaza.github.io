Man mistakenly boards the EMU 5 minutes after being late and is then rescued (Photo)

by anonymous on 2012-02-16 10:19:56

Huang Sigui was found clinging to the train window last night at 10:43 when he arrived at Hankou Railway Station. The photo was taken by reporter Chen Liang. Huang Sigui, originally from Guizhou, took a high-speed train to Wuhan and due to a video conference, mistakenly got off the train to smoke and missed the departure. Seeing the train moving, he clung to it and traveled along, creating a dangerous situation.

According to the morning post report (reporter Yin Qinbing), a migrant worker from Guizhou Province took a high-speed train from Shanghai to Wuhan. During a stop at Hefei station, he got off the train to smoke but unfortunately missed the departure time. When the train started moving, the man imitated Spider-Man, clinging onto the high-speed train and stayed outside for 5 minutes as the train sped down the tracks. Luckily, he was discovered by passengers inside the train, and after an emergency stop, he was safely rescued.

The incident happened at 8:27 PM last night. At that time, the "D3015" high-speed train traveling from Hongkou, Shanghai to Hankou Railway Station had already stopped at Hefei station for two minutes and was starting to move slowly.

A passenger, Mr. Chen, in carriage No. 5 said that two minutes after departure, he saw someone clinging to the outside of the door. Everyone thought they were seeing things, but upon closer inspection, they confirmed it was indeed a man wearing a yellow short-sleeved T-shirt, his face pressed against the glass almost deformed, with a very tense expression.

At this point, although the high-speed train had just left Hefei station, it had already accelerated to about 50 kilometers per hour.

"There's someone outside the door!" The news quickly reached the train conductor. After reporting, the dispatch center instructed an emergency stop. Subsequently, the man was rescued by the train police and attendants, leaving him completely exhausted.

The man's name is Huang Sigui, 40 years old, from Longgang Town, Kaiyang County, Guizhou Province. At 9 PM last night, when the reporter talked to him on the phone, he was being ordered to write a self-criticism.
