Bargain shopping tests your discernment

by wgyuvs8a on 2012-02-22 08:10:32

Hanzheng Street can provide all family clothing ● Hanzheng Street Brand Fashion Plaza: branded men's wear is at least 20% cheaper than in shopping malls. Not long ago, the online post "Hanzheng Street Cabbage Trip at 5:30 AM" attracted nearly 30,000 netizens to click. The poster purchased 10 beautiful clothes within two hours from 6:30 AM to 8:30 AM, spending less than 800 yuan. The most expensive wool coat was 145 yuan, and the cheapest base layer shirt was 30 yuan. Sweaters and hoodies were priced between 50-80 yuan. The location for shopping was precisely the Hanzheng Street Brand Fashion Plaza, a clothing market built only four years ago but has surpassed the old markets such as Wanshang Baima and Hanzheng Street Central Mall. A seasoned seller on Hanzheng Street revealed that some mall counters would take goods from Hanzheng Street, re-make the price tags, and increase the prices by at least 30%. Moreover, along the Dofu Road of Hanzheng Street, there are several wholesale markets with different positions. Dofu Mall focuses on mid-low priced children's wear, Guoti Mall focuses on sports wear, and if you want to buy underwear, you can go to the First Avenue. ● Liutong Alley, Rat Street: Trendy Women's Shopping Paradise Liutong Alley opposite Wanshang Baima and Rat Street adjacent to Zhongshan Avenue are regarded as "trendy women's treasure hunting spots" where styles are very fashionable. A seasoned "shopaholic" said she goes shopping in Liutong Alley every week and finds that many fashionable small shops in places like Xin Minzhong Leyuan and Guanggu Walking Street also come here for goods. There are also Taobao store owners who come to purchase goods here. If you know how to mix and match, you can spend a hundred or two hundred yuan to get all the trendy elements of the season. It should be noted that most merchants on Hanzheng Street only do wholesale business in the morning and have no time to deal with retail customers. However, the clothes directly from the warehouse in the wholesale market have not been detailed processed or ironed, so there may be some flaws such as wrinkles and excessive threads. Some merchants do not allow trying on, requiring accurate visual estimation, and one must be clear about their own clothing size. "Overseas Shopping": Big Brands at Cabbage Prices A pair of branded men's shoes costs 1500 yuan in domestic counters, but when purchased from an overseas shopping website, including shipping fees, it costs less than 400 yuan. On a well-known Wuhan lifestyle forum, netizen "Yixue Ningzi" introduced her shopping experience on an overseas shopping website: she bought seven pairs of branded shoes for 2400 yuan. The number of proficient English-speaking "overseas shopping tribes" is increasing. They shop on overseas websites and then transport the goods to China through forwarding companies. Overseas shopping experts have summarized tips: not being good at English is not scary, just install a browser that can translate English; currency conversion is not scary, just get a dual-currency credit card; international shipping fees of at least 100 yuan are not scary, reasonable shopping and group orders can help. Shopping websites regularly offer discount coupons. Netizens can log in through rebate websites and receive varying amounts of cashback. Outlets International Prestige Goods Discount Sales Specialty discount stores dealing with off-the-shelf products, out-of-season products, and inventory products are flourishing in Jiangcheng. Whether it is the Pacific Outlets Square specializing in international big brands or the Kuma Clothing Brand Trading Center focusing on affordable brands, as long as you know how to shop and search, you will always find unexpected surprises. In the shoe area of the second floor of the outlet, the original price of a full leather winter boot near two thousand yuan, after discount, some only cost three or four hundred yuan. The downside is that there are not many brands in the shoe area, and the sizes and colors are incomplete. At the Kuma Clothing Brand Trading Center in Hankou, Jordan, Converse, and other brand discount stores are no different from specialty stores in terms of decoration, and all products are generally 4-6 discounts, even as low as 1 discount. Affordable Malls Test Your Keen Eye Frequent shoppers like Ms. Liu suggest that when shopping here, one should look more, try more, and compare more. Sometimes, an unnoticeable counter "snow hides" some good items. In addition, Jian'er Mall in Qingshan is also a treasure trove for many netizens to shop. Many shopping gurus say that each mall in Wuhan has its own characteristics and frequently launches irregular promotional activities. Usually, one should browse and try more often, and during sales, they can make direct purchases. Also, there will be some stylish items with large discounts on flower carts. Internal Group Buying Relies on Timely Information Some brand manufacturers have taken alternative routes by frequently organizing internal group buying sessions through online posts, where clothing and shoes are sold cheaper than those on special sale carts. At the scene, wool sweaters start at a minimum of 70 yuan, generally priced between 100-200 yuan. Wool sweaters costing over a thousand yuan in malls are sold here at 3-4 discounts. Many citizens spend over a thousand yuan to buy one piece for each family member. A group buying session usually lasts two or three days, and netizens must enter with their registered ID and phone number. However, internal group buying only conducts small-scale publicity on forums, and participants are mostly regular forum users or those who hear about it through word-of-mouth recommendations from friends. Characteristic Small Shops Hidden in Streets and Alleys Many people have their own "favorite small shops." Netizen Shang Jing said that if you want to find cheap yet quality clothes, choose brands with their own physical stores. There is a dedicated "characteristic small shops" section on the "De Yi Life" website, summarizing hundreds of various unique shops. Some are located in streets and alleys, while others are hidden in office buildings, each having their own "exclusive skills." For example, focusing on a few niche brands, or the shop owner being particularly skilled at recommending combinations, etc., to attract stable repeat customers. Novice netizens can pay attention to the "expert order display" on some forums. Clothing experts will post pictures of the clothes they bought and their fitting photos online to share. If interested, you can ask the thread starter for the address. In addition, a batch of fast-fashion brands like ZARA and Uniqlo have entered Wuhan. Their styles change frequently, and prices are affordable. You can dress up in an "international style" for less than 1000 yuan. In Hanjie, Central Department Store, Lingjiahu Wanda, and other places, you can see the presence of these fast-fashion brands. Hankou North Leather City offers cheaper fur than Hai Ning Without traveling far to Zhejiang, you can buy authentic Hai Ning fur right at your doorstep. Since the opening of the Hankou North Hai Ning Leather City on September 28th, sales records have been continuously broken. Mink coats priced over ten thousand yuan on average can sell more than ten pieces in a day in some stores. A fur store owner said that stores usually sell at 6-8 discounts according to the marked price, while in the wholesale market, selling at 1.5 discount still makes a profit. Moreover, the rent for storefronts in Hankou North is lower than in Hai Ning, so the selling price in Wuhan may be even lower than in Hai Ning.
