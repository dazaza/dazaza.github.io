"Welcome~" Xiao Ling smiled sweetly at her reflection in the mirror, once again uttering the greeting she had practiced hundreds of times with a voice that was still somewhat childish. "I'm going to work soon, how relaxing," Xiao Ling muttered to herself. Having just graduated from university, she was lucky enough to find a job right away. Although it was just as a receptionist at a hotel, she was satisfied, after all not everyone is so fortunate to have a job waiting right after graduation.

The next day, at the entrance of XX Hotel.

"Welcome~" Xiao Ling greeted each guest entering with a sweet smile, making them feel her warmth. The guests couldn't help but look over, paying attention to this fresh and slightly petite girl.

Xiao Ling's joyful smile infected the guests, making their steps lighter. Unconsciously, the morning passed like this.

At noon, Xiao Ling was walking happily with her colleagues towards the dining hall. Deep in conversation with her colleagues, Xiao Ling barely noticed someone coming towards her. When she realized, it was already too late, and they had collided.

"Miss, why aren't you more careful?" Despite his words of reproach, the young man who had been bumped into didn't blame Xiao Ling at all.

"Ahhh! I'm sorry, I'm sorry! It wasn't intentional!" Xiao Ling lowered her head and apologized hurriedly. But the young man seemed not to be so easily "forgiving" Xiao Ling.

"Oh? If it wasn't intentional, does that mean it was on purpose?" The young man grinned mischievously, looking at Xiao Ling.

Helpless, Xiao Ling finally looked up. The young man in front of her had fair skin, almond-shaped eyes full of laughter, and thin lips that were sexy, making one want to bite. Xiao Ling couldn't help but stare dumbly.

Seeing Xiao Ling staring at him for a long time, the young man wasn't upset, instead the laughter in his eyes deepened.

"Miss?" The teasing voice of the young man rang in Xiao Ling's ears, soft and pleasant.

"Ah...? Oh... Uh..." Xiao Ling came back to her senses upon hearing someone call her. Seeing the deep laughter in the young man's eyes, she blushed and lowered her head, two suspicious red clouds rising on her face.

"Haha..." The young man couldn't help but laugh when he saw Xiao Ling's expression, the dimples on both sides of his mouth showing as he laughed, filled with joy. "My name is Xu Liang, what about you?"

"Ahhh...? Oh! My name is Xiao Ling." Xiao Ling quickly returned to normal, giving Xu Liang a sweet but formulaic smile.

"Isn't it fake? I want to see your most genuine smile, otherwise don't smile." Xu Liang looked deeply into Xiao Ling's eyes, speaking slowly.

Xiao Ling looked at Xu Liang. Looking into his profound eyes, she smiled genuinely without reason, so real, so adorable, yet with a touch of mischief.

"See you this afternoon." After saying this, Xu Liang gave Xiao Ling another mischievous smile before turning and leaving.

Xiao Ling stared dumbly at Xu Liang's retreating figure until it disappeared completely before snapping out of it. Touching her blushing cheeks, Xiao Ling sighed at herself for being such a fangirl as she walked towards the dining hall, completely forgetting about her colleagues who had come with her.

In the afternoon, when Xiao Ling was ready to continue her duties as a doorman, she unexpectedly saw Xu Liang again.

"Are you going to work again?" Xu Liang showed that mischievous smile again. Xiao Ling's face turned red involuntarily.

"Yes... Ugh, so tiring, standing for half a day, constantly saying 'welcome', with a false smile, exhausted, and can't even rest..."

Once Xiao Ling started talking, she couldn't stop. The initial excitement of getting a job had been replaced by fatigue.

Listening to Xiao Ling's complaints, Xu Liang's expression changed from a mischievous grin to a twitching corner of his mouth, three black lines appearing on his forehead at some point. And Xiao Ling's complaints seemed to have no end in sight. Finally, Xu Liang couldn't take it anymore.

"Cough cough, Xiao Ling ah, this... isn't it getting late? Don't you still have to work? Won't you be late?"

"Eh? Really!! I almost can't make it in time, thank you for listening to me talk so much. See you later when you have time."

As soon as these words were spoken, Xiao Ling vanished.

"Hehe, silly girl, how can you be so trusting towards someone you've just met, telling everything, you wouldn't even know if you were sold someday," Xu Liang muttered to himself where he stood.

In the blink of an eye, night fell.

"Oh~ Time to go home~" Xiao Ling cheered, "I'm exhausted, my feet are sore."

After changing out of her uniform, Xiao Ling bid farewell to her colleagues one by one. Walking to the door, she surprisingly found Xu Liang leaning casually against the entrance.

"Eh, how can I see you everywhere?" Xiao Ling looked curiously at Xu Liang, somewhat puzzled.

"Hehe, don't ask. Let's go, haven't eaten dinner yet, right? I'll take you, let's go." As he spoke, Xu Liang pointed to the silver-white XX car behind him (imagine any car brand).

On the road. The atmosphere inside the car was somewhat oppressive.

Xiao Ling glanced at Xu Liang driving seriously on her left. Under the faint light within the car, Xu Liang's side profile was bathed in a soft glow, highlighting his flawless fair skin, making him seem like an angel. Once again, Xiao Ling couldn't help but stare dumbly.

"Have you had enough? Had enough, then get out, we've arrived." Xu Liang parked the car and teased Xiao Ling mischievously.

"Yeah, enough... What?? Disgusting!"

Xiao Ling inadvertently exposed her fangirlish side, immediately turning red. Covering her bright red face, Xiao Ling hastily got out of the car.

Dinner was enjoyable, the atmosphere between the two very relaxed. Before they knew it, two hours had passed.

"Let me take you home, it's 9 o'clock already. It's not good for girls to go home too late."

"It's only 9 o'clock... No, anyway, I'll be alone at home, bored to death."

"What place do you want to go then? I'll treat you."

"Ah, Xu Liang, you're really nice! Hmm... How about we go sing karaoke?"

Excitedly rambling nonsense, Xiao Ling bit her lip thinking for a long time, looking incredibly cute.

In a certain KTV...

"There's nothing to say, we pretend with each other, your phone keeps ringing..."

"The strange voice in your phone sounds like a child, sweet and pleasant..."

Karaoke queen Xiao Ling sang song after song without tiring. Fortunately, her voice was sweet and charming, sparing Xu Liang the agony of ear-piercing noise.

An hour later...

"They all said we'd part ways at the next intersection, no one is allowed to turn back..."

"I'm used to you walking behind me, accepting all my requests regardless..."

Another hour later...

"I believe, I still love you baby..."

"Guest, you cannot, you're getting closer, your eyes are looking where, pretending to be so calm..."

"Cough cough, Xiao Ling, take a break will you? You've been singing for over 2 hours. If you keep going, your voice will give out, and you have work tomorrow anyway." Unable to bear it any longer, Xu Liang finally interrupted Xiao Ling. No matter how beautiful the melody or how sweet the voice, listening to the same few songs repeatedly would eventually become tiresome.

"Oh... Alright..." Dejected, Xiao Ling sat back down beside Xu Liang and picked up a beer on the coffee table and drank. By the time Xu Liang snapped out of it, there were already three or four empty cans in front of Xiao Ling (don't ask me why Xu Liang got distracted for so long, nor why Xiao Ling drank so much, let's keep it mysterious!).

"Hey! Xiao Ling, are you crazy!?" Xu Liang snatched the beer can from Xiao Ling's hand and slammed it onto the coffee table, "Do you plan to work tomorrow!"

By now, under the influence of alcohol, Xiao Ling, who couldn't hold her liquor well, was already disoriented and about to fall asleep.

"Mm... No... @#￥%&*..." Xiao Ling mumbled a few meaningless words, unable to hold on any longer, she slumped over and fell sound asleep in Xu Liang's arms.

"Hey, wake up! Where do you live..."

Xu Liang's voice gradually softened, "Goodnight, Xiao Ling." A gentle smile appeared on his face. Watching Xiao Ling sleep soundly in his arms without any guard, Xu Liang felt something tender in his heart stirred.

The next day.

"Mm... So comfortable..." Lazily stretching, Xiao Ling flipped over to hug a pillow and continue sleeping, only to...

"Cough cough, men and women should maintain proper distance...~" A mischievous voice sounded beside Xiao Ling. Xiao Ling's eyes shot open, wide-eyed staring at Xu Liang beside her.

"Aaa――――" Xiao Ling screamed, "How did you end up in my bed!! What did you do to me――――"

Xu Liang helplessly poked his nearly deafened ears, scolding, "Miss, last night you were drunk, clinging to me refusing to let go, and not telling me where you lived, so I could only bring you to a hotel. Moreover, I didn't even complain about you hogging the bed."

"That... Then why didn't you sleep on the sofa?!" Xiao Ling made her last attempt to argue.

"Sleep on the sofa when there's a bed? Am I crazy? Besides, there's enough room on the bed, and it's the room I booked, so why shouldn't I sleep on the bed?" Xu Liang humorously explained to Xiao Ling while wearing his trademark mischievous smile.

Xiao Ling was utterly defeated. Pouting adorably, she glared resentfully at Xu Liang. Looking at those crystal-clear jelly-like lips, Xu Liang suddenly felt a flutter in his heart, unable to resist leaning down to capture those tempting little lips (tada... kissing scene... there wasn't one in 'Red Dress'...)

"Mm...!!" Xiao Ling's eyes widened, staring fixedly at the person in front of her who had stolen a kiss.

"Your lips... are so sweet. Just don't sleep on your stomach next time. Airport." After stealing a kiss, the cat slowly whispered this sentence in Xiao Ling's ear with a seductive tone before calmly getting up and leaving.

Xiao Ling's face instantly heated up, then suddenly became angry: "Who sleeps on their stomach, that's not my back, missy, my body is naturally beautiful!!"

Touching her kissed lips, Xiao Ling felt the lingering warmth Xu Liang had left behind. Strangely, there wasn't a trace of dislike in her heart, instead, a wave of sweetness arose from the bottom of her heart. For no reason, Xiao Ling sat on the bed touching her lips and smiled foolishly.

A long time later, Xiao Ling remembered she had to work today. Hurriedly jumping off the bed, she saw a note on the bedside table: "Airport, came back very late last night, took leave for you today oh." There was no doubt who had left this note. Looking at the note, Xiao Ling's lips involuntarily curled upwards.

In the blink of an eye, a week passed.

In this week, Xu Liang hadn't shown up again. Xiao Ling tried her best to erase Xu Liang's image from her mind, constantly reminding herself not to think about Xu Liang anymore, but the longing was like a flood, uncontrollable, and Xiao Ling's thoughts of Xu Liang grew stronger.

A week, long or short. In these 7 days, Xiao Ling seriously reflected on her feelings for Xu Liang. Eventually, she determined that she - liked Xu Liang.

Just as Xiao Ling was daydreaming, a person she had longed for appeared beside her.

"Airport, what are you thinking about?" Xu Liang's mischievous smile reappeared.

"Xu Liang... ..." Xiao Ling blankly stared at the person in front of her, fearing it might be a mirage.

"Hey, snap out of it." Xu Liang waved his slender fingers in front of Xiao Ling.

"Li!!! Suddenly, Xiao Ling tightly hugged Xu Liang. "Li... I missed you so much... Why... Why did you stay away for so long... Why didn't you tell me you were leaving... Stupid... Do you know how much I missed you..." As she spoke, Xiao Ling choked up.

Xu Liang was initially stunned, then a warm, sweet, and happy smile bloomed on his lips. "Silly girl, I'm not not coming back, my booked room hasn't been checked out yet, how could I leave? Be a good girl, don't cry, I'm right here in front of you." Xu Liang wrapped his arms around Xiao Ling, gently patting her back, softly consoling the lovely person in his arms.

Xiao Ling lifted her tear-streaked face, her throat hoarse from crying, "You promise you won't leave again?"

Xu Liang tenderly wiped the tears from Xiao Ling's face, "Yes, I won't leave." Then he grinned mischievously, "Just a week, and you already missed me this much?"

Xiao Ling, whose face was already thin-skinned, immediately turned red, making a move to hit Xu Liang.

"Alright alright, I'm wrong, okay? Come on, I'll take you somewhere, as an apology." Xu Liang grabbed Xiao Ling's delicate and small hand, unhesitatingly pulling her into the car.

By the sea.

It was just sunset. The soft, fine sand shone golden under the sunset, emitting a faint halo. Xu Liang and Xiao Ling strolled along the beach holding hands, their shadows stretched long by the sunset, intertwined together.

"Do you know, my favorite time is when the sun sets. Very beautiful, very beautiful." After a moment of silence, Xu Liang opened his mouth lightly. Whether it was an illusion or not, Xiao Ling felt an indescribable sadness in Xu Liang at this moment. That sadness was so heavy it seemed like it could swallow him whole. Xiao Ling lovingly tightened her grip on Xu Liang's hand. She understood that comforting words wouldn't help much at this time, so she chose to remain silent.

Another period of silence followed. An oppressive silence. Both remained silent, just holding hands watching the sun slowly set, feeling this wordless happiness.

"It's dark, let me take you home?" Xu Liang turned to look at Xiao Ling. Under the dark night sky, Xiao Ling's tear-washed eyes appeared exceptionally clear.

"I... Okay, let's go." Xiao Ling seemed to want to say something, thought about it, and changed her words.

Inside the car.

"Li... What exactly did you do? Why did you leave for so many days without any news? I... I was worried about you."

"I... just wanted to give you some time to clarify your own feelings." Xu Liang resumed his previous mischievous appearance, as if the earlier tenderness and melancholy were just a dream, a beautiful but unrealistic dream.

"Are you mad? Fine, I'll tell you." Xu Liang sighed, "I make music, but no one appreciates me, so I came to this city. First, to clear my mind, second, to see if I could find inspiration. The past few days I went out searching for inspiration. I went to the suburbs. It was... very beautiful."

Having known Xu Liang for so long, this was the first time Xiao Ling heard him mention his identity. Recalling her own college days: despite having excellent grades, due to her proud nature, she refused to flatter her professors or bribe them, so the school never recognized her talent, and she had to find work through her own efforts. Luckily, she was good enough to be hired by this renowned hotel. Thinking about this, Xiao Ling suddenly understood Xu Liang's pain.

"It's fine, Li. I believe in you, knowing your outstanding talent is enough."

"It doesn't matter... Never mind, let's not talk about this. In a few days, I might leave again, but don't worry about me, I'll come back, relax."

Hearing that Xu Liang might leave again, Xiao Ling's eyes reddened, but hearing that he would return, she immediately turned from sorrow to joy. Such a child.

The following few days were Xiao Ling's happiest days. Every morning, Xu Liang would send her to work downstairs from her house, and every evening, he would pick her up after work to have dinner, then go to the beach, and finally send Xiao Ling home.

"It's cloudy today..." As soon as she got up in the morning, Xiao Ling saw the gloomy weather outside the window, which made her mood a bit worse. But just thinking about seeing Xu Liang's handsome face soon made ripples form in her heart.

"Strange, he's late today, this time he hasn't come yet... Anyway, I'll go first, or I'll be late." After waiting for a long time downstairs without seeing Xu Liang, Xiao Ling hurriedly ran to the bus stop to go to work herself, "Big deal, I'll just call Li later to tell him I've gone ahead."

Throughout the entire morning, Xiao Ling failed to get through to Xu Liang's phone. At first, no one answered, then it was directly switched off. Remembering what Xu Liang had said before about possibly going out again, Xiao Ling put her mind at ease and didn't think much about it.

Another whole week passed. During this week, Xiao Ling received no news from Xu Liang. Gradually, Xiao Ling began to worry. She hurriedly went to the front desk to inquire if Xu Liang had checked out of his room, and the answer was yes, and the check-out date was a week ago!

Xiao Ling helplessly dropped her hands. Could Xu Liang have left without saying goodbye to her? Xiao Ling painfully thought. Could their sweet love affair have lasted only a few days? Xiao Ling became increasingly sad and disappointed.

Their romance had already spread throughout the hotel, and Xu Liang's sudden departure caused dissatisfaction among many colleagues. Everyone consoled Xiao Ling, and the manager also humanely gave her a five-day vacation to let her sort out her emotions.

During these five days, Xiao Ling thought a lot. Every day she recalled the bits and pieces of her time with Xu Liang, missing everything about him, including his mischievous trademark smile.

These five days were painful for Xiao Ling, almost shedding tears every day. But she was, after all, the energetic girl Xiao Ling, and she quickly adjusted her attitude and perked up. She remembered Xu Liang had said he would come back, she believed him, so she was willing to wait.

Before they knew it, three years had passed.

During these three years, Xiao Ling had changed a lot. She had shed her naive and innocent demeanor and smoothly promoted from a receptionist to a supervisor of a department. Though matured greatly, she still couldn't forget the person named Xu Liang, and would secretly shed tears in the dead of night, reminiscing about her past happiness.

This morning, Xiao Ling went to work at the hotel as usual. When she warmly greeted her colleagues around her, she heard the voice that had haunted her dreams.

"Miss, is room 502 occupied?"

Xiao Ling stiffly turned around. It was him, the person she had thought about day and night.

Xu Liang also turned around, giving Xiao Ling a mischievous smile that was both unfamiliar and familiar to her.

"It's me, Xiao Ling. I'm back."

(The End)
