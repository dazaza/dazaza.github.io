Chasing the dull life _ 759

by eawdqrxbh on 2012-02-21 00:21:18

Chasing a dull life is a realm, a kind of vision, and an attitude towards life. Plainness is a realm. It has no stormy waves or dangerous situations, like a freehand landscape that isn't heavy on colors but lightly touches upon them - like yellow, not only blown by the wind, green ornaments, as if without any publicity, only revealing if there's no fragrance. Freehand landscape "can float, slowly sip wine and hum a tune." The Yellow Mountain surrounded by dense clouds builds its flat space, beautiful tuberose silently growing from the mundane, interpreting a carefree and happy life. In fact, facing everything in life, no matter who we are or what we do, it's all part of the mundane world, like passing clouds, Epiphyllum blooming momentarily, only eternal life remains insipid. Therefore, in life, one should not be confined to certain times or matters, not overly concerned with success and failure, gain and loss, but rather face life with a heart of tolerance, commonality, not seeking material happiness, not being saddened by losses, maintaining calmness and composure in a plain life. Only then can one enjoy relaxation and tranquility; only this way can life be simple and quiet. Plainness is a state of mind. Russell said: "When life is like a river, early on it's narrow, rushing down held between both sides, then the banks gradually widen, the river gradually slows down, eventually quietly flowing into the sea...". The journey of life, like a river, has both surging forward beautifully and standing still like plain water, both the passion of sublimity and the arduous and tortuous roughness, but will eventually flow into the sea. Human life begins with the first cry full of vigor and vitality, finally ending in silent helplessness, no matter how great the achievements in life, it will eventually become pale smoke dissipating, hard to remain in the annals of history. Only with a light heart, indifferent to fame and wealth, tranquility of mind, seeking something and nothing, can you set your heart at ease, calmly survive, live with freedom, chase the distillation of spirit, achieve the nirvana of the soul. Plainness is the attitude towards life. Life doesn't have to chase after the beauty of rain flowers, doesn't have to chase the tide falling instantly. On the road of life, only by climbing lofty mountains and steep hills can one enjoy the "artificially high mountain peaks" connotation, only by swimming across rivers and lakes can one taste bitterness turning sweet, only by trekking in a desert can one withstand insipid fleeting time, only by experiencing boundless cold and loneliness can one understand that time cannot be turned back for regret, regret cannot return. ... Along the road, no matter how light your steps are, there's always a piece of dust, two lines of footprints. No matter how you try various methods to erase the traces behind, you only care for this and lose that, leaving the years imprinted. Plainness is the joy of life. The impermanence of life, the trend of time, no past, no future, there's only this present life. In this present life, with one mind and a detached attitude, breaking away from the worldly pomp irritability, facing every storm in life, being tolerant, quietly cultivating oneself, regarding with equanimity, this is the joy of life, being indifferent to Mingzhi, quiet Zhi Yuan, no demands, no luxury, with little insipid, building the eternity of life, it is the true meaning of life, the joy of life.
