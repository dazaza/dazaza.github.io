Wong Kar-wai went to Zhao Jia's troupe to select actors, and Master Zhao advised his apprentices to be civilized in their performances.

At 19:00 on the evening of November 29, Wang Jia Wei arrived in Shenyang quietly to Liu Laogen's stage for selecting actors for his new film "The Grandmaster". This trip received all-out support from Ben Shan. All disciples of the Zhao Family Troupe were available for Wang Jia Wei's selection. For this purpose, Zhao Ben Shan specially held a meeting with his disciples, emphasizing that the performance must be quality and civilized, and the time should not exceed 20 minutes.

According to reports from ChinaFotoPress on November 30, after Zhang Yimou specially came to Shenyang to select actors for "Three Guns and a Surprise", famous director Wang Jia Wei also had his eyes on the talent-rich Ben Shan team. At 19:00 on the evening of November 29, Wang Jia Wei arrived at Liu Laogen's stage in Shenyang quietly to select actors for his new film "The Grandmaster". It was learned from Ben Shan Media that this trip received all-out support from Ben Shan. All disciples of the Zhao Family Troupe were available for Wang Jia Wei's selection.

Wang Jia Wei's new film "The Grandmaster" quietly started filming a week ago in Chikan, Kaiping, Guangdong. The main cast including Tony Leung, Zhang Ziyi, and Zhang Chen have all joined the production. The film will be shot in this film city from December 12 to April 18 next year. In order to cooperate with Wang Jia Wei's schedule, the film city will stop opening during the shooting period to ensure that the plot of the film does not leak out. Although the shooting time has been determined, Wang Jia Wei, who always strives for perfection, was reluctant to announce the list of actors. It turned out that since he visited Ben Shan and appreciated Northeastern Er Ren Zhuan (a form of traditional Chinese folk performance) this summer, he has been considering incorporating Northeastern style into his new film. For this reason, he communicated with Ben Shan many times, hoping to select actors from the Zhao Family Troupe. Due to Ben Shan being hospitalized, the matter was delayed again and again until after the start of the film's production.

It was learned from Ben Shan Media that when Wang Jia Wei came to Shenyang to select actors, Ben Shan paid great attention to it and specially returned to Shenyang from the "Rural Love 3" production crew in Kaiyuan, urging his disciples to perform well. The generous Ben Shan even promised Wang Jia Wei that any actor he liked could be taken away immediately, and the Zhao Family Troupe would fully cooperate. It is understood that mainly 3-4 male actors need to be selected. Da Long has already been selected, and the remaining three are still under consideration. Before the performance, Zhao Ben Shan specially held a meeting with his disciples, emphasizing that the performance must be quality and civilized, and the time should not exceed 20 minutes. During the performance, Wang Jia Wei approved the first pair of actors and kept exchanging opinions with Liu Shuangping, the president of Ben Shan Group. Afterwards, Ben Shan's daughter sat beside Wang Jia Wei to exchange ideas.

(Source: ChinaFotoPress, Author: Yi Zhe)
