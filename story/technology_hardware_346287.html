Advice from a 25-year-old entrepreneur to college students

by zhiyun0972 on 2010-04-15 19:18:41

1. What is the essence of attending university? The most important difference between college students and non-college students does not lie in whether they have mastered a professional skill... A person who maintains a wrong view after independent thinking is more worthy of affirmation than one who accepts a correct view without hesitation... Plants can grow year by year on campus, while we are destined to be quickly replaced by another group... Although each time only one net eye catches fish, to catch fish, it is necessary to weave a net...

2. Life Planning: Decisions at the Three-Fork Road Not taking detours is the shortcut... Politics, business, academia. At this three-fork road in life, where will you go... Instead of competing with a hundred people for five positions, it's better to compete with one person for one position...

3. There is no such thing as hot or cold majors, and there is no hierarchy among schools No employer will think that you represent your school or major... Since it is probability, there are more than one possibility... If choosing academia, it's easier to achieve success in a niche field than in a popular one... Switching majors has almost become a trend and fashion... The strength competition among universities in the postgraduate examination room and the talent market is actually insignificant...

4. Do not specialize in only one field, nor should you only specialize in one field Knowing a thousand tricks is not as good as being proficient in one... Ten times ten percent is not one hundred percent, but zero... In this realistic society, truly realizing personal value is the most decent, face-saving, and dignified thing... To know what to study, the best method is to pay attention to job advertisements... Many majors have become useless skills due to the lack of effective uniqueness... Why not apply the "buy one get one free" promotional mindset to the job application process...

5. Students who don't skip classes aren't good students Skipping no classes is just like skipping all classes... Attending university mainly involves learning how to think about problems... Skipping class is not wrong, but don't skip the wrong ones... The English corner is absolutely not the place to learn English... Sacrificing your major for English would be putting the cart before the horse... Recruitment agencies are places to use talent, not to cultivate it... You must both skip classes and make teachers give high scores...

6. The Dialectics of Working While Studying For underprivileged students, the first thing to do is not to earn money, but to save it... Most girls use computers as DVD players, and most boys use them as game consoles... In an era when even virginity can be easily fabricated, what is worth trusting so easily... Attitude determines everything... When studying takes a back seat, university students can only be considered part-time students...

7. Doing things is not as important as being a person; networking decides success Being academically excellent is not as good as being good at doing things, and being good at doing things is not as good as being good at being a person... Knowing how to speak can reduce thirty years of struggle... How much money a person has does not refer to their ownership of money, but rather their right to use it... Of the money a person earns, 12.5% comes from their own knowledge, and 87.5% comes from networking relationships... Earn money through your profession before the age of thirty, and through your network after the age of thirty... There are only four people separating you from anyone else in the world...

8. The Internet: The Relying Heaven Sword and the Sword of Damocles Writing a flawless and excellent graduation thesis in two hours... The technical threshold for entrepreneurship in the internet sector is not high; the key lies in market insight and marketing ability... Light dancing and flying has already come to an end, while Pizi Cai continues to have intimate encounters with other girls... Many college students have online friends all over the country, yet they never take the initiative to say hello and chat with the people around them...

9. Love: Pluck the flowers while they bloom Love comes unexpectedly. It can be awaited, but not manufactured... The lonelier you are, the more you should guard against love... If being single is shameful, shouldn't Ximen Qing be rated as one of the top outstanding youths of the Song Dynasty... Pluck the flowers while they bloom; do not let the flowers wither on the branches... A person with ten thousand dollars spending one hundred on you represents only one percent of his wealth; whereas a person with only ten dollars spending all ten on you makes you his entire world...

10. Sex: God is dead, the gods are corrupting Say love, act love... I only feel happiness in the repeated physical collisions. Afterward, there will be greater loneliness and emptiness... Why let someone else's vanity harm you... As she mechanically lies down and spreads her legs, her parents are looking forward to their daughter's future... When spring ends and beauty fades, flowers fall and people die unknown to each other...

11. Postgraduate Entrance Examination: Painful Euthanasia There is nothing more failed than wasting youth... The expansion rate of postgraduate admissions is 30%, which means the depreciation rate of master's degrees is 30%... With the same three years of effort, you can increase the value of E1 by 1, or increase the value of E2 by 2 or even 3... Completing a master's or doctoral degree does not necessarily mean stronger work abilities... Facing a cost of 135,400 yuan, would you still invest in pursuing a postgraduate degree without hesitation... Effort leads to results, but not necessarily good results...

12. Studying Abroad: "Sea Turtles" Become "Seaweed" A monthly salary of 2500 yuan could attract three postgraduate "sea returnees" to compete fiercely... For certain majors, going to study in the United States is no different from studying in Ethiopia... Since companies worldwide want to carve up the Chinese market, why do Chinese insist on studying abroad and working for foreigners...

13. Non-Traditional Education: Suffering or Advantages She set several records in China's information industry. She was called China's "Queen of Outsourcing." And she was merely a self-study junior college student... To win back what you once lost, you must make up for the efforts you fell behind others... Non-traditional education students not only need a certain level of strength, but also must master certain skills, achieving victory by playing to their strengths and outsmarting others... The road is under your feet. Easy to walk, walk well...

14. Graduation: Hidden Traps in All Directions The alma mater doesn't treat you as its child, so why should you treat it as your parent... Listening to tutoring classes is just buying peace of mind... The talent market is a minefield... Using various methods to job hunt is not wrong, but do not lower your standards... As soon as the employer asks you for money, turn around and leave... In these times, even written agreements are not enough evidence, let alone oral agreements...

15. Job Hunting: Don't Be Too Honest Your job resume must highlight your core competitiveness... During job hunting, there is no need to be completely straightforward like Yan Shouyi... Lying is not difficult, but continuing to lie and not revealing any flaws is hard... When filling out your specialties, be as specific as possible... A job resume only needs a table on an A4 sheet of paper... Interviews actually follow patterns; during interviews, just recite the standard answers...

16. Riding a Donkey to Find a Thousand-Mile Horse The standard distance between the two rails of American railways is 4 feet 8.5 inches, why? Because the width between two horses' hips is 4 feet 8.5 inches... Talents are misplaced resources... The biggest tragedy in the world is that too many young people never discover what they truly want to do... Small and medium-sized enterprises may allow you to receive more adequate training... Starting from the grassroots does not mean starting from every grassroots position... Want "prosperity," want even more prospects...

17. Office Politics: White-Collar Workers' Mandatory Course Large companies are about being a person, small companies are about doing things... Whether employees can be promoted largely depends not on whether they work hard, but on how much the boss appreciates them... Company matters and secrets are always more complex and profound than you imagine... Pretending to be confused at the right time is not only necessary but also wise... Just consider your colleagues as a group of strangers you can call by name...

18. Entrepreneurship: Becoming a Millionaire Before 29 Years Old A dead camel is still bigger than a horse... Overeating kills the bold, starvation kills the timid... It's no longer "big fish eat little fish," but "fast fish eat slow fish"... Grasping trends is the most important ability for an entrepreneur... The space left for graduates in high-tech industries is already very small... Haste makes waste. Accumulating experience by working for others before entrepreneurship is very necessary... The market is always more important than the product... Not enough money to spend, what to do? First, order meals according to your budget; second, borrow chickens to lay eggs...
