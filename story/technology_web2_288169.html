I don't understand.

by qaen4306 on 2009-12-02 01:36:38

I love you ---- I love you, no one knows me, and I can't let others know, otherwise I will fall into a abyss. I cannot get rid of my longing for you; the night is torturing my weak heart. I want your return, but you keep on destroying me again and again. I am very sad, and no one can untie this knot for me. Is what I am doing right or wrong? Maybe only I myself know clearly!

I love you, since the first time we met, I have been deeply attracted by you. I hope that you are my only one, and I hope that eventually we can walk into this holy hall together, relying on each other for life. Will you hate people? Perhaps worldly eyes have already been peeping at our fruitless love, or they have already been secretly laughing at us. From the beginning, it was destined to pay a price for this feeling, but I have no regrets because I love you.

I love you, no one can comfort me. I can only cry quietly by myself and pretend to be living happily. This truth covers all the pain. I endure, I hesitate, sometimes I wonder why I do this? Beautiful dreams are always broken after dawn comes.

I love you, your appearance made me lose control. I shyly look at you, but you turn a blind eye to me. You completely take away my freedom. The suppressed thoughts in my heart you cannot understand. I mistakenly think that you can understand my good intentions. The crazy longing gradually sublimates, it's hard work and very disappointing. The earth plays with me like this.

I love you, I heard that you already have a girlfriend. For me, this is almost a bad news. It heavily strikes my hopeful heart, everything collapses. Perhaps it is a punishment, or perhaps it is self-inflicted. Can't blame anyone. I start to lose the courage to love you, and decide to give up. The shackles under me should be shaken off. When you mercilessly turn around and go away, your figure gradually becomes blurred, I realize that everything was just a lie, I should forget you, but I can't!

I hate you, you know. Why do you still insist on doing things wrongly? What have I done to deserve this treatment from you? I don't understand. My heart feels like being pricked by needles, painful. The remaining sweetness is turning sour, rotting. All the persistence becomes meaningless. One glance, one word from you is enough to soften my determination. Why am I so useless? Knowing that there won't be a good ending, I still deeply fall into this pit. Tossing and turning, it's hard to sleep ----

I hate you, really! Fading love, like a kite that slips out of hand, just like that, there is no happiness anymore! Love always brings pain, but we are willing to bear that pain. Too many helplessness, too much uselessness, add burdens in the passing youth, it's tiring!

I love you, this is a secret that no one knows, and will never know?? Do you know? I am really hurt. At the moment I gave up on you, I cried!
