Government reports are inspiring, deputies and committee members are deeply encouraged

by dtjhsb119 on 2009-12-06 16:18:34

This morning, the first session of the sixth People's Congress of Yancheng City was grandly opened. More than 500 deputies from all walks of life in the city attended this meeting. All members of the Committee were present at the meeting. Li Qiang, Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee and Mayor, made a government work report on behalf of the municipal government.

At 8:30 am, the first session of the sixth People's Congress of Yancheng City was grandly opened with the majestic national anthem. The government work report by Mayor Li Qiang summarized the government work in various aspects over the past five years and made an overall plan for the development of our city in the next few years. Many contents have caught the eyes of the attending deputies and members.

Journalist Zhou Zhijun: Dear viewers, I am now at the meeting site of the first session of the sixth People's Congress of Yancheng City. The work report by Comrade Li Qiang is pragmatic and has triggered heated discussions among the representatives and members of the two sessions.

CPPCC Member: Overall, I think this report is very clear in its thinking, and the structure is also quite rigorous. The content is also substantial, making it a very good report.

People's Congress Deputies: In Mayor Li's work report, there are several aspects that stand out. First, it is fact-based, and second, all the figures in his report, especially those reflecting the development of Yancheng over the past five years, sound inspiring and encouraging.

CPPCC Member: The report mentioned many practical things, and the entire report has high value. We listened to it with great joy.

The report elaborated significantly on the deployment of 28 practical projects benefiting the citizens, addressing the concerns of the deputies and members about people's livelihood issues.

CPPCC Member: Regarding the aspect of people's livelihood, it fully reflects harmony in life, especially focusing on some impoverished people.

People's Congress Deputies: Neighborhood construction, flood prevention, these are issues that the residents in our urban area care deeply about. I believe these aspects are highlighted in the government work report, showing significant points.

CPPCC Member: As an educator, I can feel the strong support of the municipal government for education from the report. The report also points out, especially regarding the full exemption of compulsory education fees for primary and secondary schools in urban and rural areas, which has improved our teaching environment.

The report covers various aspects of economic development and social progress in our city. Members of the two sessions come from different levels of society. After listening to such a pragmatic and forward-looking government work report, they were all deeply encouraged.

CPPCC Member: For our service industry, we will better serve the general public and solve the employment problems of some young people.

CPPCC Member: As business operators, we should support the requirements of the government in this regard. After returning, we will carefully study the government work report and do well in this aspect of work.

CPPCC Member: As a representative of a minority group, I will focus on my own position, work hard, make my small contribution to the development of Yancheng, and hope that Yancheng's future will be even more brilliant.
