When you trust me, the miracle happens!

by shunyuan on 2012-02-25 10:51:21

In the United States, there is a young man named Henry with an unknown background. He is already over 30 years old but has achieved nothing, spending his days sitting in the public office making grandiose statements. One day, one of his close friends found him with great joy: "Henry, I saw a magazine with an article about Napoleon's illegitimate son who wandered to America. And the characteristics of his only son are exactly like you: very short, speaking English with a French accent..."

Henry was skeptical but willing to believe it was true. After taking up the magazine and pondering for a long time, he finally believed that he was Napoleon's grandson. Afterwards, his perspective on himself completely changed. In the past, he was proud of his short stature, but now he admired precisely this point: "What does being short matter! Back then, my grandfather commanded thousands of troops with this very image."

In the past, he always thought his English was poor, but now he was proud of speaking English with a French accent. Whenever he encountered difficulties, he would always say to himself: "There is no word 'difficult' in Napoleon's dictionary!" Thus, with the belief that he was Napoleon's grandson, he overcame one difficulty after another. In just three years, he became the president of a company. Later, when he sent someone to investigate his background, he received the opposite conclusion. However, he said: "Now, whether I am Napoleon's grandson or not doesn't matter anymore. The important thing is, I have understood the secret to success, which is: When I believe, it will happen!"

I read this story when I was in the second semester of my sophomore year in high school. I didn't have faith in going to college because I had been lost in the shadow of my physical flaws.

How powerful is belief? This is an immeasurable result. Perhaps from Henry's words after his success, we can understand a bit: "When I believe, it will happen!" You must have learned from TV, newspapers, and other media the dissemination of many real-life miracle stories created by humans. For example, people encountering danger in the desert and surviving under impossible conditions; challenging the limits of life while hungry and thirsty after an earthquake... These stories all share one common feature: the protagonist in the desperate situation had a belief supporting them, allowing them to survive.

Life is a boat, belief is the oar; life is a tree, belief is the root. Without the paddle's movement, the boat will stop moving forward; without the support of the root system, the tree cannot soar into the clouds. Perhaps you've seen the performance of China's disabled art troupe at the Kennedy Center on TV. Then you must remember those five one-legged dancers. They are disabled, yet they performed many high-difficulty moves on stage that even able-bodied people find hard to achieve. The whole dance was filled with sorrow yet inspiring, showcasing the resilience of life to the extreme. After the performance, journalists from multiple countries interviewed them on the spot, asking how they practiced these moves? After reaching a tacit agreement, they all said in unison: "The belief that disabled people also have their strengths has brought us to today!" Similar stories and examples are countless. Belief is undoubtedly the necessary path to success. Psychologists indicate: human behavior is governed by belief. What kind of achievements you want to make depends on your belief. Belief means what a person says; thought means today's heart. Combined, the two mean what I say to myself today in my heart. If a person always complains about themselves mentally, saying "I can't," it's hard to imagine they will achieve anything significant in their future life; conversely, if a person constantly encourages themselves, saying "I can," their chances of succeeding in life will be greater. Only by believing in oneself can one succeed.

When you're at the lowest point in your life, feeling proud of some defect of yours, why not tell yourself: "Believe in yourself, and you will accomplish something tomorrow! When I believe, it will happen!"

People without goals are helping those with goals achieve their goals. Looking back at your efforts over the past few years, are you satisfied with your current situation? If you look back three years from now and things remain the same, won't things be the same three years from now? But changing direction and putting in the same amount of effort towards your own career, wouldn't the returns be different? Only by knowing how to plan your life will you not be aimless forever. Some people always ask, why can't they get out of their current predicament? That's because you have never made a change in direction. Your efforts continue to maintain the status quo. Imagine, why are some people willing to borrow hundreds of thousands for investment to change their lives with time and opportunity? That's understanding the value of investing time. If you wait like this, what you lose is time, and what you gain is still nothing.

Welcome to exchange with me and create a brilliant career together! I will give you the most suitable career opportunity for you! What environment are you in? What kind of person are you? With just a computer, you can start a business at home without leaving the house.

In plane geometry, everyone knows the axiom that "the straight-line distance between two points is the shortest." In terms of life, however, it's clearly not that simple. Everyone's environmental background is different, especially due to psychological resistance and low spirits, many will take many detours due to temporary confusion and melancholy. This is like Mount Fuji, which has only one peak, but the number of paths to climb Mount Fuji is countless. As for how high you can climb or whether you can reach the summit, it completely depends on whether you have belief and how long you stick to it. Otherwise, when a person doesn't believe they can achieve something, even a molehill can seem like an insurmountable mountain. Faced with Mount Fuji, they may only wander at the foot of the mountain or retreat outright, leading to a wrong path.
