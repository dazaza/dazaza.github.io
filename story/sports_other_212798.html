Eco-friendly furniture will be the general trend of the future market.

by bodamn2 on 2009-07-23 09:37:59

It's no surprise that environmental protection has become an eternal topic in the furniture industry. The environmental friendliness of furniture has always been a matter of great concern to people, and eco-friendly furniture has always been favored by them. There are many reasons for this, but the main reason is that whether furniture is environmentally friendly or not is related to human health.

China suffers from resource shortages, especially in terms of forest resources. In recent years, China's furniture industry has developed rapidly. Due to improper protection policies and rampant deforestation, China has become the world's largest wood consumer. As a result, the government had to include furniture in the "List of Prohibited Goods for Processing Trade" to restrict the large-scale export of panels and furniture made from domestic wood as raw materials in order to protect scarce resources and the environment. Consequently, the price of wood has risen sharply, leading to the emergence of a large amount of inferior wood and artificial panels, deepening the issue of "furniture environmental protection." Therefore, environmental protection, energy saving, and greenness have always been topics of discussion within the industry. Meanwhile, with the improvement of people's living standards, when purchasing furniture, they now prioritize its environmental performance over the former consumption concept centered on practicality, quality, and price. With improved living standards comes enhanced quality of life and taste, and people are paying more attention to their health. Whether buying food or daily necessities, consumers first consider whether the product is harmful to their health, and the same applies to buying furniture. Environmental protection will always be a consideration. Furniture manufacturers must realize that while the style, color, material, and shape of furniture may change over time, environmental protection remains eternal. It's hard to imagine how happy we would still be if all our furniture were not environmentally friendly. Without environmental protection, furniture loses its value, regardless of how attractive its appearance, color, material, or design might be - such furniture is essentially waste. If the furniture is environmentally friendly, even if one or two aspects don't meet people's requirements, most people would still choose it.

For two consecutive years, furniture has ranked second in product complaints. Recently, complaints about furniture have been increasing, and tragic events caused by furniture pollution have also occurred frequently. Environmental inspections of furniture continue, yet this cannot stop the emergence of furniture with severe pollution. Although the country has issued a series of industry standards for this purpose, such as requiring furniture enterprises to use E1 grade panels with formaldehyde emissions ≤1.5mg/L or E2 grade panels with formaldehyde emissions ≤5.0mg/L that must undergo surface treatment before being used indoors, and has also authorized the State Environmental Protection Administration to issue the highest domestic standard green certification - the "Ten Rings Certification," problems still persist. Since the environmental standards of the national furniture industry are not yet fully unified, some companies lacking professional knowledge, with outdated production equipment, and insufficient funds have taken advantage of loopholes to produce fake environmental products or green product certificates through various channels. The increase in counterfeit green certifications makes it even harder for consumers to judge when purchasing green goods. Therefore, consumers require specific labels on certifications and test reports to identify them, but many counterfeit merchants and health risks still exist. Formaldehyde can be said to be the main hidden danger. Due to various factors and the growing environmental awareness of consumers, consumers place greater importance on environmental protection when purchasing furniture, making eco-friendly furniture once again the focus of the furniture industry.

Before purchasing furniture, consumers must first investigate which materials are non-polluting, which brands are environmentally friendly products, and which malls have better reputations. Because consumers' ability to discern is weak and there are numerous counterfeit marks and certificates, they often find it difficult to distinguish between genuine and fake ones. A large number of counterfeit green certifications in the current furniture market deceive many consumers. Faced with numerous "green products," consumers need to be more vigilant. Generally, solid wood furniture is the most environmentally friendly. Choosing branded furniture or buying from well-known malls is relatively safe. Nowadays, there are many companies specializing in solid wood furniture, with a wide variety of brands. For example, Ximengbao, Aixin Castle, Huarui, Noah's Home, etc., are all brands worthy of consumer trust.

In response to consumers' emphasis on environmental protection, if furniture companies want to win more consumers and increase sales, they must meet this demand of consumers. At the same time, they should strengthen their brand, using the brand to explain everything. To strengthen the brand, integrity is key. Companies must take integrity as their foundation and prioritize consumer interests. Any behavior that deceives consumers damages the brand image and ultimately prevents the establishment of a brand trusted by consumers.
