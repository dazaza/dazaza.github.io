Fengxing Video SNS Community launches public beta test

by yinyin on 2008-08-04 21:51:34

Online video-on-demand service Phoenix announced formally on July 28th the launch of its new film & television social network service (SNS) community zone. The new social community model used by the SNS allows users to develop their own circle of friends within the community. Members will be able to keep up-to-date with all recent activities of their friends in the community.

The new Phoenix client, after incorporating the SNS community, has created a typical vertical SNS community platform which takes the user as the convergence center and film & television content as the bond, allowing users to display themselves and make friends while appreciating movies.

In addition to continuing to provide the original video-on-demand services, it will also use ways such as mining user relationships, knowledge, and information so that all users can comment on, collect, and share content of interest with other community members.
