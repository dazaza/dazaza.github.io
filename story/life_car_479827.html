Car Beauty: New Car Decoration and Maintenance

Matters needing attention in car decoration:

A. The color of the decoration materials must be coordinated with the color of the car, and one should not blindly pursue high quality and high price, so as to avoid counterproductive results. For example, a black car body should be paired with light tea-colored sun film.

B. According to the size of the interior space, try to choose some small, beautiful, practical ornaments that can fully reflect the owner's personality, such as teacups, perfume bottles, and timers.

C. Cleanliness requires all decorative items inside the car to be clean, hygienic, and neatly arranged. A clean interior, fresh air, and no dirt will give people a sense of ease and comfort.

D. Safety mainly refers to the fact that interior decorations should not interfere with the driver's safe driving or the safety of the passengers.

E. Comfort means that the color and texture of the interior decorations should meet the owner's aesthetic view. Because only a comfortable environment can bring the owner and passengers a pleasant mood and a relaxed feeling, ensuring safe driving.

Commonly used decorations for new cars

Basic decorations for a new car include floor mats, foot pads, seat covers, handle covers, perfumes, alarms (gear locks), reversing radars, and explosion-proof films; high-end decorations also include leather seats, sound systems, wood veneer parts, car sunroofs, satellite cruise positioning systems, airbags. More practical options also include various cushions, backrests, cup holders, anti-static labels, debris trays, mudguards, car covers, walking racks, car refrigerators, air pumps, brake lights, clearance lights, rearview mirrors, parking signs, fire extinguishers, chamois, dusters, oxygen bars, electric windows, automatic antennas, car curtains, mechanical locks... and large enclosures, spoilers, fender flares, rain guards, decorative lights, stickers, steps... Below are introductions to several commonly used decoration products:

2. Alarm (gear lock): The security of a car is what people care about most, so alarm products are essential. Cars equipped with electronic alarms generally have remote-controlled doors, which are very convenient and practical. However, if thieves figure out how to crack them, they can easily steal the car. Therefore, many cars now also install gear locks. Gear locks are mechanical locks that lock the car's gear lever in neutral or reverse. Even if the thief starts the car, they cannot drive it away. The interior space is small, and the lock rod is hard and difficult to saw through. Electronic alarms may sometimes have issues due to use or product defects, and once installed, gear locks are not easy to remove. When choosing alarms and gear locks, consider product quality, installation technology, and after-sales service.

1. Floor mat: There is usually a carpet-like item under the car seats, which is pre-installed in the original car. Once dirt or grime accumulates on it, it is hard to clean. If a waterproof, easy-to-clean layer is placed on top, cleaning becomes much easier. This protective layer is called a floor mat. It comes in two types: hand-sewn and molded. A well-made floor mat can effectively prevent dust and dirt from seeping into the carpet but has poorer water resistance. Molded floor mats are made in one piece with no seams, offering better leak prevention, but when encountering uneven ground inside the car, their aesthetics suffer. Generally, floor mats are made from 3mm thick rubber and come in gray, beige, and black colors. Additionally, the installation level of the floor mat is also very important. Poor installation can cause the edges to curl and the middle to be uneven, resulting in an uncomfortable overall appearance. Since floor mats are rubber products, they have some odor and fewer color options, making some high-end cars feel lower-grade after installation. Now some cars use home carpets, which look great, show class, and are not too expensive.

4. Reversing radar: With more cars on the road and less usable space, there are more new drivers, more female drivers, more scratches, and more disputes caused by scratches. What was once not a problem—reversing—is gradually becoming one, and we face reversing multiple times a day. A reversing radar allows the vehicle to get as close as possible to obstacles while reversing without affecting performance even in the dark or foggy conditions, greatly enhancing safety during reversing.

3. Explosion-proof film: On a scorching summer day, driving on a road without shade, even with the air conditioning on, you cannot escape the sun's rays and UV radiation. The direct sunlight can make you restless. If you apply a good explosion-proof film, it will block 99.99% of UV rays, preventing direct sunlight from entering the car and giving you a sense of coolness. This is the explosion-proof film's heat insulation and UV protection function at work. Additionally, if the glass breaks, the shards won't fly around, reducing harm to people.

Issues related to new car modifications

1. Front bumper: Absolutely do not buy the common bumpers available on the market, which are installed on the front bumper of the original car. These bumpers do not provide crash protection and are merely decorative pieces.

2. Rear bumper: Generally, do not install one because the departure angle of the rear part of the car is already small, and installing a rear bumper further reduces the departure angle, making it unsuitable for off-road use.

3. Roof rack: Due to substandard materials or excessive fasteners, general roof racks tend to create noise and have low load-bearing capacity.

3. Off-road spotlights: There are two types—one installed at a lower position, such as on the front bumper or front guard, which falls under auxiliary lighting. The purpose is to increase the illumination angle and provide supplementary lighting for the main headlights.

4. Suspension lift: Professional suspension lift kits are expensive, like ARB's complete set of lift components, plus four professional shock absorbers costing up to 10,000 yuan, but they offer excellent performance. Alternatively, amateur lift kits can be used, such as heavy-duty suspension components for six-cylinder engines, including front springs, rear steel plates, and six-cylinder shock absorbers.

5. Larger tires: Generally, 235/75R15 tires are used, divided into highway type and dual-purpose highway/muddy road types. They are not cheap, each costing over 400 yuan. Changing tires is a significant modification point to improve the overall driving performance of the car. If funds permit, it can be considered.

6. Negative value wheel drums: For 235 tires, due to their width, they might hit the lower arm (a component of the front axle suspension) when the steering is locked. To solve this problem, replacing with negative value wheel drums is a better method.

7. Others: Modifications also involve other aspects. In terms of off-road vehicles, there are installations like high-position intake pipes, winches, and more professional differential locks.

Summer car usage: Pay attention to water temperature and air conditioning system

High water temperature

High water temperature is one of the major problems for summer engines. There are many reasons for high vehicle water temperatures, such as poor heat dissipation due to internal and external blockages in the radiator, malfunctioning electric fans, damaged fan couplers, and leaks in the cooling system.

Maintenance

In summer, daily vehicle maintenance becomes more important. Due to high summer temperatures and humidity, engine oil adsorbs moisture from the air, which easily causes oil degradation. Most users' vehicles are primarily used within urban areas, with short travel distances and long idle times.

Brake fluid itself is hydrophilic, so it absorbs water molecules over time and mileage. External temperature changes and frequent use of the braking system evaporate this moisture. Therefore, manufacturers specify a replacement cycle of 2 years or 40,000 kilometers for brake fluid. If not replaced in time, the boiling point of the brake fluid decreases, directly affecting braking performance and causing damage to brake system components due to brake fluid degradation.

The condition of tire usage is also very important in summer. We must adjust the tire pressure after the tire temperature has dropped, as high tire temperatures can affect the accuracy of tire pressure measurements.

Air Conditioning System Maintenance

Summer is the season when we frequently use air conditioning, and many problems arise, such as poor air conditioning performance and unpleasant odors. There are many reasons for these problems: poor heat dissipation, dirty radiator exterior, cleaning the condenser, malfunctioning electric fans, checking corresponding circuits and electric fan motors, and system pressure issues. High or low system pressure can prevent the air conditioning from cooling properly. For air conditioning odors, it could be due to a clogged air filter or foreign objects on the evaporator surface, requiring cleaning or replacement.

New car decoration: Be cautious about installing theft prevention first

After purchasing your dream car, here are three suggestions regarding decoration:

1. Install theft prevention first: Whether you plan to decorate your car or not, a theft prevention device is essential. Anti-theft devices can lock the engine in anti-theft mode, preventing the car from starting even with the original key, and can connect to the central locks of the four doors.

2. Do not neglect applying the film: Applying the film must be done indoors, and when selecting the film, try to choose imported explosion-proof films with warranty cards. The front and rear windshields should be covered with whole sheets. If sand spots or dead wrinkles appear after applying the film, the manufacturer is responsible for reapplying it.

3. Observe the working environment and require a formal invoice: Proper installation is based on caring for the customer's car. Regardless of whether the car is new or old, the installation personnel must lay down disposable foot mats and seat covers before getting into the car.
