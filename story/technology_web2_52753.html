“planbus! 是一个让你与你的好友实现互动的网络平台。在这里，你可以把自己在qq空间、新浪博客、搜狐博客、博客中国、饭否、叽歪、优酷视频、土豆、6房间、MSN Space以及facebook等其他网站上的原创展示给你的朋友。让你时刻抓住好友们的动态。他们最近在作什么？关注哪些话题。。等等。

planbus! 还能根据每个人同步的服务内容生成一个趋势图，这个功能很方便地让你一目了然到某个用户近期在哪个网站活动最频繁！”

The technology is based on analyzing the structure of a webpage contents using our auto filtering parser bot. At the time when a user delivered an URL the bot will automatically parse the document and extract the repeating contents from within that page. Those contents are marked ’valuable’ by our system and provide to the user for confirmation. Each confirmed result will generate a pulse event and ready for our system to check for future updates of the specific URL.

Our current work can nearly successfully parse up to 70% of whole internet webpages. We’re still working forward to improve our parser bot.

Planbus will serve Chinese internet users and syndicate most web2.0 websites. It will help users to check when the website has new contents updated and then push to users’ pulse page, shared with contact list or in-site friends.
