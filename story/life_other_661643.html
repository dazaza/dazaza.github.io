The Desolate Wishing Tree

It started to snow, how beautiful!

A boy was walking alone in the snow, then he stopped. This was a tree, a wishing tree. The boy stretched out his bony hand and touched the tree covered with snowflakes. Suddenly, the boy spat out a mouthful of blood, splashing it where he had touched. Instantly, a gorgeous blood flower appeared, gradually melting the snow. The tree trunk was exposed, and on it were carved the four words "Never Leave, Never Abandon." The boy remembered that it was last year's Valentine's Day when he carved "Never Leave" and his girlfriend carved "Never Abandon."

His tear-filled eyes gradually blurred, pulling back memories from last year. They met by coincidence, falling in love at first sight. So, they became lovers. The boy liked the girl very much; she wasn't very pretty, one could say quite ordinary! The only thing that caught attention was her smile, so innocent, so cute... Perhaps the boy just fell for her simple and adorable nature. The girl also liked the boy because he had deep, mysterious eyes that once seen, you didn't want to look away.

The boy always disliked smiling, no one knew why he always seemed cold. Only towards the girl did the boy reveal a long-lost smile! In his own words: his smile felt too forced. The girl said, "Will you change for me?" "Hehe! Of course, silly, who made you my little fool!" Whenever the girl heard the boy calling her "little fool," she would look extremely happy and call him "big idiot."

The girl once asked the boy, "If I leave, what will you do?" "I will try my best to keep you here, not letting you go," the boy said firmly. "What if I can't be held back? What will you do?" "If that happens, I think I will lose the courage to live..." "No, I won't let you... I will never leave you." The girl covered the boy's mouth with her hand, not letting him continue. The boy took her hand off and kissed her lips, as it was their first kiss, so it was awkward. The girl happily responded...

Hehe! Such a happy couple. Later, the girl left the city due to other matters. The boy and the girl were so reluctant to part, but there was nothing they could do. At first, the girl missed the boy very much, responding daily to his emails. The boy thought about the girl every day, sending messages without interruption. Until one day, the boy noticed that the girl wasn't responding enthusiastically anymore, always indifferent, always saying she was busy and had no time... In fact, the girl had fallen for another boy. Maybe because of time, the girl gradually lost feelings for the boy. Until one day, the girl said, "Let's break up, maybe we aren't suitable for each other." After hearing this, the boy felt as if the entire world had collapsed. He didn't know what to do, just helplessly asking, "Is there any possibility for us to continue?" "I said, we are not suitable, do you understand? Don't bother me anymore"...

The boy was desperate, he really couldn't understand why this happened, was it all his fault? He wanted to forget the girl, but every night he was accompanied by tears. He truly couldn't leave her; life without the girl meant the boy lost the meaning of living. ...

The boy died, under that wishing tree... The police found an unsent letter at his home, addressed to the girl.

Little Fool: By the time you receive this letter, I will be dead. Sorry! Please forgive my abrupt departure. Do you remember our "Never Leave, Never Abandon" from last year? You heartlessly abandoned me, losing you, I have no reason to live anymore. I don't blame you, maybe meeting you was my mistake, hehe! I don't regret loving you once. But in that world, no one will call me "Big Idiot" anymore, sigh! I miss, I miss hearing you call me "Big Idiot," but I know that's impossible now because you no longer belong to me. I hate, I hate that boy, why did he take you away from me, I can't accept it. If, if there is a next life, will you marry me? If there is a next life, please don't abandon me again, okay?

Farewell! Your loving Little Fool!

Idiot!

It started to snow, the snowflakes wrapped around the "Never Leave, Never Abandon" on the tree trunk again, taking away only a devoted corpse...

It's snowing, the snow is so beautiful, beautifully tragic. Beside the wishing tree, it seems like there's a boy with deep, mysterious eyes sitting there, silently sighing...
