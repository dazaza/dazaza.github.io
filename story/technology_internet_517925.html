Changfeng Ocean World Tour

by guangzt324 on 2011-10-24 14:48:13

This Ocean World is one of the earlier established underwater worlds. Many fish inside are quite interestingly shaped. Here, you can experience the beauty and wonder of nature. Occasionally, there are song and dance performances too, which are really great. There are also quite a number of species, and the jungle adventure is pretty good as well.

Another part of the Changfeng Ocean World - the aquarium, is China's first large-scale themed aquarium. The main building is located 13 meters beneath Yinchu Lake, housing more than 300 species and over 15,000 fish. A one-day tour of Shenzhen Ocean World is available. Inside the aquarium, there are different areas such as Jungle Adventure, Coral Reefs, Deep Sea Shipwreck, Shark Tunnel, and Penguin House. Visitors in the shark tunnel can feel the thrill of a close encounter with the sea's dominant creatures. The renovated Jungle Adventure area has a realistic rainforest environment that is very popular with children. In the summer of 2010, rare double-jawed sharks were introduced, marking the first time they were exhibited in a domestic oceanarium. To date, Changfeng Ocean World has received millions of visitors from home and abroad and is one of the national youth science popularization education bases. The aquarium also successfully launched numerous services to the public, including a diving club, membership club, underwater weddings, and overnight stays at the aquarium, gaining excellent brand recognition and commercial reputation.

The polar white whale performance hall of Changfeng Ocean World underwent two renovations and has become the largest in mainland China and the first marine animal performance hall in East China to have white whales, capable of accommodating 1,500 people simultaneously. Besides the star attraction, the little white whale, other marine mammals like California sea lions have been introduced. Soon, visitors will be able to watch their wonderful performances and personally experience the joy of harmonious coexistence between humans and animals. The animal performance stage plays "Little White Whale Finds Mommy," "The Secret of the Treasure Chest," "Take Me to the Moon," and "Shanghai Nong Hao" were successfully staged during the May Day and National Day Golden Weeks, receiving enthusiastic applause from the audience.

[About Ocean World]

Changfeng Ocean World is located within the picturesque Changfeng Park and belongs to the Australian Oceania Group, integrating large-scale marine animal performances with an aquarium fish exhibition into a comprehensive marine theme park. Oceania International Group is the world's largest group company in the aquarium industry, owning six aquariums globally, located in Australia's Sunshine Coast, Melbourne in Australia, Busan in South Korea, Shanghai in China, Bangkok in Thailand, and Dubai in the Middle East UAE.
