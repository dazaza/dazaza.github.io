Skillfully use colors to beautify your home environment.

by longersing on 2009-10-28 18:30:49

Skillfully using color to beautify the home environment: How can we use color in home decoration? The most direct way is of course through painting walls with paint, thus creating large areas of color blocks. However, the simplest method is to use some colorful decorations.

● Bathroom: Ceramic bottles and jars for a cool transformation

Decoration focus: The renovation focus in the bathroom lies in textiles. Just by changing to richly colored towels or foot mats, the style of the bathroom changes.

DIY: If you put your mind to it, towels can also be good helpers in decorating your home. For example, during holidays, friends often give fruit baskets as gifts. If you lay down a towel as a base, then add some small items like bottles and jars, placing them in a corner adds charm.

In the cool weather, naturally in subtropical Guangzhou, it makes one's mood particularly bright, and shouldn't our homes also change into autumn wear to welcome winter?

In home decoration, using color to express seasonality often achieves twice the result with half the effort. As long as you grasp the popular colors suitable for autumn and winter, and combine them with elements such as fabrics, decorations, and lighting, the refreshing, leisurely, and slightly warm atmosphere of autumn homes will appear.

How can we use color in home decoration? The most direct way is of course through painting walls with paint, thus creating large areas of color blocks. However, the simplest method is to use some colorful decorations.

● Living Room: Cushions and table lamps enhance layering

Decoration focus: Sofas, coffee tables, and other large pieces of furniture are hard to change, but don't forget that cushions have an embellishing effect. You can choose several cushions, focusing not only on color coordination but also on the use of materials, styles, patterns, sizes, and shapes, thereby adding layers to the living room space.

Additionally, you can choose to use throw blankets. Throw blankets can be paired with any type of furniture, whether it's bamboo and rattan furniture favored by people from Guangdong, or some simple plastic furniture. Throwing a colorful blanket over them can present a new look.

Lampshades are another focus. If you want to change to new lighting fixtures, main lights should preferably have the 18th-century style with hanging and cast iron designs, while side table lamps should follow simple and trendy styles. Alternatively, changing to a new lampshade is a cost-effective and eye-catching way; according to the autumn and winter color principles, you can choose shiny silk-like fabrics or stable and retro velvet fabrics, which under soft light emit a cozy home atmosphere.

DIY: To compensate for the understated tones of autumn and winter colors, why not try doing it yourself? Go to a decor store or market and select some glittery decorations like sequins and crystals, attach them with wire to chandeliers, or sew them onto the ends of long tablecloths, creating your own luxurious retro style.

● Dining Room: Tablecloths and curtains taste the seasons

Decoration focus: If you have a glass dining table, you can choose some table pads in autumn and winter trending colors. If it's a wooden table, consider using silk tablecloths with an oriental flair to refresh the table's appearance.

If there are windows near the dining area, the pairing of curtains becomes even more important. Currently, layered curtains are popular. You can choose dark-colored sheer curtains paired with light-colored fabric curtains, and remember to swap the positions of the fabric and sheer curtains.

DIY: Vegetables and fruits used in cooking can also be used to decorate living spaces. For example, putting bell peppers, persimmons, pears, peaches, apples, etc., all into a large bowl and placing it on the dining table, doesn't it give a sense of harvest?
