Tengyi C50 is currently accepting hot reservations, and those who place an order will be given a 3M full car window film as a gift.

by myc0n5o52 on 2012-02-06 16:59:31

Changan Automobile's new family sedan, the 1.5T turbocharged model, has made a stunning debut with prices ranging from 78,000 to 91,800 yuan. Simultaneously, a nationwide event offering free 3M full car tinting for pre-orders of the Tengyi C50 has been enthusiastically launched. It is understood that, in order to maximize gratitude towards consumers, from now until December 31st, during this month-long event, customers who visit Changan Automobile's 4S stores and place a deposit of 1000 yuan for the Tengyi C50 will receive genuine 3M full car tinting. For more details, please consult your local Changan Automobile 4S store or Deutz Germany.

Positioned as a full-size T-family sedan, the Tengyi C50 uses exclusive turbocharged technology and surpasses configurations of similar models, becoming a benchmark among domestic brands. Since its debut at the Chengdu Auto Show, it has attracted numerous consumers. This pre-order event not only expands market space but also brings more benefits to consumers.

In terms of appearance, the Tengyi C50 has an elegant and fashionable design, embodying the mature aura of a family sedan. The front features a curved honeycomb grille with a divided air intake. The "T" emblem on the left side of the grille stands out prominently, highlighting the T-powered attribute.

Regarding space, the Tengyi C50 adopts a simple linear aesthetic design, creating a dynamic and sharp visual sensation. The vehicle measures 4650mm in length, 1775mm in width, 1455mm in height, with a wheelbase reaching 2700mm, surpassing competitors in its class and introducing the concept of a full-size family sedan for the first time in the market.

For power, the Tengyi C50 is equipped with China's first mass-produced 1.5L exhaust turbocharged gasoline engine. The engine utilizes lightweight aluminum alloy cylinder block and head designs, reducing overall weight by 20% and lowering fuel consumption by 16%, with a comprehensive fuel consumption of 6.9L per 100km. It delivers 98kW at 5600rpm and a maximum torque of 188Nm between 2000-4500rpm, comparable to 1.8L or even 2.0L naturally aspirated engines.

In terms of configuration, the Tengyi C50 reaches heights difficult for other domestic models to achieve: GPS+DVD, dual airbags, ABS+EBD, smart tire pressure monitoring, and a whole-car anti-theft alarm system providing comprehensive driving protection! Additionally, the Tengyi C50 offers a service plan of 5 years or 150,000 km + 4 free maintenance services, setting a new record among similar models and offering more value to consumers.

The launch of the Tengyi C50 allows domestic brands to gain significant influence in the core area of engine technology, breaking the complete monopoly of foreign companies, further enriching the product line of the Tengyi sedans, and surely enhancing Changan Automobile's competitiveness in the A-class family sedan market.
