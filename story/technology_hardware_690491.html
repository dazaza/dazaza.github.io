PSB's official Weibo account sparks heated debate by seeking marriage for a policeman

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 10:32:58

The official Weibo account of Wuhou District Public Security Bureau of Chengdu, "Safe Wuhou", has officially launched its plan to save single police officers. Yesterday, the No.1 single police officer of the bureau appeared on Weibo: 178 cm tall, 70 kg in weight, with a postgraduate degree, attracting exclamations from female fans outside the city - "Why is he from Chengdu!"

In response to the enthusiastic female fans, Shi Yi, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the bureau and one of the administrators of the "Safe Wuhou" Weibo account, said that they hope to help single police officers in the bureau find their other halves through this platform. It is understood that this is the first police Weibo account in the country to launch an official matchmaking plan for police officers.

The appearance of the single police officer has made fans unable to hold back. "After much anticipation, the No.1 single police officer has finally been unveiled! Single female fans, if you have a thing for uniforms or police officers, don't hesitate to mark him down... " At 3:12 PM yesterday, the official Weibo account of Wuhou District Public Security Bureau of Chengdu, "Safe Wuhou", officially launched its "Save Single Police Officers Plan". The first police officer comes from the "Falcon" Special Operations Team of the Patrol Police Brigade of Wuhou District Public Security Bureau. After the man poured sulfuric acid on his wife and child, he went missing. Boys hope that their fathers will be brought to justice soon, symptoms of rhinitis.

Standing at 178 cm tall, weighing 70 kg, with a postgraduate degree, wearing a police uniform and holding a service pistol in real-life photos, the personal profile and real-life photos of the 29-year-old male police officer attracted a large number of fans when he "appeared". By 6:00 PM yesterday, the Weibo post had been forwarded more than 1500 times.

"Decisively rushing to Chengdu for a group blind date." "Single girls, take a look, it's encouraging!" ... Even some netizens asked, "Can male fans participate?" The official Weibo account of the "Jiangning Public Security Online" also sighed, "This is something we can't learn from. We can only decisively help forward it and advertise for our brothers in Chengdu."

While fans were enthusiastically watching, some netizens asked, "Does the Chengdu Public Security Bureau really need to act as a matchmaker like this?" "Is this a joke?"

In response, Shi Yi, one of the Weibo account administrators and Deputy Director of the Political Department of Wuhou District Public Security Bureau, said that the official Weibo account's launch of the "Save Single Police Officers Plan" is absolutely not a scam, but rather hopes to use this public platform to help single police officers in the bureau find their other halves as soon as possible.

Shi Yi said that in recent years, the bureau has recruited many new police officers, many of whom are young people from other places. They have no relatives in Chengdu, are busy with work, and do not have good channels for blind dates. "In the first phase, we plan to introduce five single police officers, and subsequent introductions will depend on the results of the blind dates."

As for the registration method, all you need to do is "mark them down", and later "Safe Wuhou" will announce the details of the get-togethers and registration methods. Shi Yi said that they will then "officially arrange a collective afternoon tea, chat, and enjoy the moon! Hopefully, good marriages can be arranged through Weibo!"
