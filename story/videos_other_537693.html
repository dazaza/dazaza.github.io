Schneider Electric debuts new products at the 2011 Security Expo.

by ijgdrlabj on 2011-11-07 09:42:54

Shenzhen, China, October 31, 2011 --- Schneider Electric, a global expert in energy efficiency management, recently made an appearance at the "2011 China International Public Safety Expo" held in Shenzhen with its brand Pelco. It comprehensively showcased to industry users and technical experts Schneider Electric's integrated digital security solutions for different application environments, combining building and security systems. They also grandly launched the new Sarix series of network cameras featuring innovative SureVision high-end imaging technology, fully demonstrating Schneider Electric's supply capabilities and leading advantages in overall digital security solutions.

Gao Qiwai, Vice President of Schneider Electric China and head of the Building Business Unit, expressed: "China is comprehensively implementing the construction of smart, safe cities, which brings continuous energy to the rapid development of the building and security industries. Schneider Electric deeply integrates the leading resources of its brand Pelco in the security field, providing one-stop services for customers in different industries. As the security industry gradually enters the digital application era represented by IP technology, Schneider Electric will use innovative digital security solutions to truly achieve highly reliable, highly available, intelligent security guarantees, addressing the challenges faced by building management and security management."

At this Security Expo, Schneider Electric exhibited the newly released Sarix series cameras featuring SureVision imaging technology, including the Sarix IX series of network fixed cameras and the Sarix IM series of fixed dome cameras. As a new advanced imaging technology, SureVision can integrate wide dynamic range, low-light performance, and glare suppression into a complete high-end technology, establishing a new standard for image quality in the video surveillance field and opening a new era for video image quality. The Sarix cameras using SureVision technology can almost process light like the human eye, automatically adjusting even under poor lighting conditions, presenting outstanding details and clear images.

In addition to showcasing integrated digital security solutions for environments such as airports and subways, Schneider Electric also exhibited security applications within modern smart home environments at this event. Access control systems, home camera monitoring, and fire and gas alarms represent indispensable components of home security in smart homes. Schneider Electric's smart home system Wiser truly integrates electrical, multimedia, and communication devices into a user-friendly interoperable solution, helping homeowners manage their homes intelligently and achieve a stylish, environmentally friendly lifestyle. Through this system, users can remotely monitor their home status via mobile devices like iPads or office computers, real-time controlling the status of home devices and security. Additionally, Wiser can help users maximize energy savings while enjoying a more comfortable life.

The China International Public Safety Exhibition has been continuously held for thirteen sessions so far, ranking alongside the famous Las Vegas Security Show in the United States and the Birmingham Security Show in the UK as the "World's Three Major Security Shows." It is also Asia's most authoritative and influential security exhibition. The thirteenth Security Expo will be held from October 29 to November 1, reaching a new high in scale, with over 1500 participating companies and an expected audience of 85,000 people.
