At 3:45 AM Beijing time on March 20th (8:45 PM local time in Italy on the 19th), the 30th round of the Serie A League kicked off, with league leaders AC Milan traveling to face Palermo away. Goyan scored the only goal of the match, helping Palermo secure a 1-0 home victory over Milan and ending their five-game losing streak. Despite the loss away from home, Milan still leads the league with 62 points.

In the 29th minute, Goyan brought down Pato who was breaking through with the ball, earning Milan a free kick just outside the penalty area. Seedorf's direct shot was blocked by the wall. In the 31st minute, Pinilla's long-range shot was stopped by Abbiati diving to his side. In the 32nd minute, Cassano's close-range shot in the box went high. In the 39th minute, Cassano's shot after dribbling sideways also went high. In the 45th minute, Seedorf made a through pass for Pato, but Sirigu intercepted the ball. During the first-half stoppage time, Van Bommel was shown a yellow card for a foul during midfield defense. At halftime, Milan temporarily trailed 0-1.
