Causes and treatment of male kidney Yang deficiency? Throughout history, medical practitioners have believed that kidney deficiency is one of the main reasons for illness and aging in the elderly. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) considers the essence of health to be "the balance of Yin and Yang, leading to a well-regulated spirit." This means that under normal circumstances, the functions of the five organs and six腑(fu-organs), the circulation of vital energy and blood, the limbs and bones, and the fourteen meridians all maintain a balance between Yin and Yang, achieving relative equilibrium. Balance is a symbol of health.

As people age and become elderly, their bodies experience: Qi and blood deficiency, depletion of essence, congenital insufficiency, lack of postnatal nourishment, kidney Yin deficiency, excessive liver fire, toxin accumulation, and blocked meridians. Someone once surveyed 235 people over the age of 20, finding that the rate of kidney deficiency was 58% at age 30, 71% at 40, 80% at 50, 90% at 60, and 95% at 70. From this, it can be seen that the percentage of kidney deficiency increases with age.

Causes and treatment of male kidney Yang deficiency? Therefore, effectively regulating the imbalance of Yin and Yang, Qi and blood, and organ functions in the early stage, and promptly eliminating toxins and correcting kidney deficiency, are key to delaying aging and preventing geriatric diseases. The *Inner Canon* states, "When Tian Gui (essence) is exhausted, there is less essence, and the kidneys decline," which can cause human aging. Renowned TCM practitioner Yue Meizhong believed, "Human aging starts with the kidneys drying up, affecting other organs." Zhu Danxi stated in *Gezhi Yulun*, "After reaching sixty or seventy years old, both essence and blood are depleted. Even without any issues, heat symptoms arise. Why? Dizziness, blurred vision, itchy skin, frequent urination, runny nose, tooth loss, excessive saliva, reduced sleep, weak legs, hearing loss, forgetfulness, dizziness, dry intestines, dirty face, hair loss, and eye strain... In the elderly state, these symptoms are all present... All the aforementioned symptoms indicate blood deficiency." This shows that the depletion of essence and blood, along with toxin accumulation and blocked meridians, are causes of human aging.

Kidney deficiency truly reflects the natural law of the depletion of kidney essence in the elderly. To promote a path to health and longevity for the elderly, it is necessary to tonify the kidneys, detoxify, and address kidney deficiency early on. Experts believe: Prevention of aging should start at age 30, anti-aging measures at 40, and extending life expectancy at 60. For kidney deficiency syndrome, timely medical consultation is recommended.
