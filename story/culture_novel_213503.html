Implementation Plan for the 2006 Target Responsibility Evaluation of the Urban Housing Ownership Supervision Office

In order to truly, objectively, and scientifically evaluate the moral integrity and work performance of all staff members, providing a basis for promotion, appointment, rewards and punishments, training, dismissal, and adjustment of salary benefits, according to the "Interim Regulations on the Evaluation of Staff Members of Public Institutions" issued by the Personnel Department of the State (Personnel Nuclear Training Release No. 153), and the "Notice on Printing and Distributing the Evaluation Regulations for Staff Members of Public Institutions in Hanzhong City" issued by the Municipal Personnel Bureau (Hanzhong Personnel Issue No. 221):

If an individual disagrees with the evaluation results after the review by the evaluation group, they can file an appeal to the Municipal Bureau within 7 days after the evaluation group submits the review opinion and it is approved by the department or office leadership.

Five, Use of Evaluation Results

(One) For staff members of this office who are evaluated as qualified or above in the annual evaluation, the following regulations apply:

1. Individuals who have been rated as excellent for three consecutive years or three times within six years may apply personally, with their unit submitting the application. After approval by the Municipal Bureau and verification by the Municipal Personnel Bureau, their salary level will be promoted according to relevant regulations;

2. Employees who have been evaluated as qualified or above for three consecutive years will have the qualification for promotion; those who have been rated as excellent for two consecutive years will have priority for promotion;

3. Professional technicians who are evaluated as qualified or above will have the qualification for reappointment;

4. Workers who are evaluated as excellent for two consecutive years will have priority for promotion to a higher technical level;

5. Individuals who are rated as excellent in the annual evaluation and have already received a salary increase as a reward will not receive another salary increase but can still be rated as excellent.

(Two) For individuals whose annual evaluation is determined as unqualified, the following measures will be taken:

1. Monthly evaluations will be summarized and announced. Those who fail will have 50%-80% of their monthly bonus withheld. If they fail for three consecutive months, they will lose the qualification for annual excellence;

2. Major mistakes at work that cause a certain impact will result in the withholding of monthly and annual bonuses by 50%, and the loss of the annual excellence qualification;

3. For those who are determined as unqualified in the current year's evaluation, no year-end bonus will be issued, and they will receive criticism and education;

4. For those who are determined as unqualified for two consecutive years, depending on the situation, they may be demoted, reassigned, downgraded, or dismissed;

5. For those who are determined as unqualified for two consecutive years and refuse organizational arrangements or are still unqualified after reassignment, they will be dismissed according to relevant regulations.

(Three) For those rated as "basically qualified", the evaluation results will not be implemented temporarily, and will be handled based on the final rating after the warning period.

(Four) The use of evaluation results should be closely integrated with the selection of advanced activities and reward recognition work within the office.

Six, Organizational Leadership

A target responsibility evaluation leadership group will be established within the office. Composed of office leaders as the group leader and deputy group leader, with the main responsible persons of each section as members, it will mainly be responsible for the implementation of the target responsibility system and mid-year checks and year-end evaluations.
