Notice on the Organization of the First Shenzhen E-commerce Talent School-Enterprise Networking Fair, Faculty Training  

To the responsible persons of all colleges and universities, employment office directors, e-commerce major directors, teaching and administrative managers:

Shenzhen is so far the only e-commerce demonstration city approved by the National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) and Ministry of Commerce (MOFCOM). On March 17, 2010, the 159th regular meeting of the Fourth Session of the Shenzhen Municipal Government reviewed and in principle passed the "Work Plan for the Construction of a National E-commerce Demonstration City in Shenzhen," establishing the overall goal of developing e-commerce in Shenzhen and specific goals for the next three years. The Work Plan proposes that after three years of efforts, by the end of 2012, more than 70% of enterprises will be engaged in e-commerce applications, and more than 50% of small and medium-sized enterprises will frequently use third-party e-commerce services. The total amount of e-commerce transactions will exceed 300 billion yuan. The e-commerce service industry will become an important emerging industry, and Shenzhen will become a developed area in the national e-commerce service sector. Shenzhen's first Internet industrial park, Luohu Internet Industrial Park, officially opened in 2010, with the first batch of 37 companies entering the park. The development of e-commerce and the Internet industry in Shenzhen is constrained by the lack of talent. To promote the cultivation and employment of e-commerce talents in domestic secondary and higher vocational colleges and universities, and to meet the demand for e-commerce and online marketing talents in Shenzhen, the Luohu Science and Technology Bureau of Shenzhen, together with the Certification Management Office of the China E-commerce Association's Professional Manager and the Online Marketing Committee of the China E-commerce Association, jointly organized the "First Shenzhen E-commerce Talent School-Enterprise Networking Fair." Relevant matters are now notified as follows:

I. Theme of the Networking Fair

Serving Shenzhen e-commerce enterprises and assisting IT talents from secondary and higher vocational colleges and universities in specialized employment.

II. Content of the Networking Fair

Organizing 37 outstanding e-commerce enterprises from the Shenzhen Internet Industrial Park, 60 IT-related enterprises and traditional enterprises from other districts in Shenzhen, to hold a negotiation conference with secondary and higher vocational colleges, technical schools within and outside the province, focusing on school-enterprise cooperation, order-based training, and the establishment of e-commerce talent training bases, building a platform for exchanges and cooperation between enterprises recruiting workers and schools arranging graduate employment (internships).

III. Organizers

This conference is co-organized by the Luohu Science and Technology Bureau of Shenzhen and the Online Marketing Committee of the China E-commerce Association, and hosted by Shenzhen Boyan Technology Co., Ltd., the designated talent training institution of the Shenzhen Internet Industrial Park.

IV. Participants

E-commerce enterprises and traditional enterprises from the Shenzhen Internet Industrial Park and other areas of Shenzhen; secondary and higher vocational colleges, technical schools within and outside the province.

V. Time, Location, and Fees

Conference time: April 30, 2010 (Friday), 9:00 am - 5:00 pm

Conference location: Xinmao Garden Hotel, Shenzhen

Participation fees: Accommodation, lunch, materials: 450 RMB per person per day

VI. Registration Time

(1) Accommodated representatives registration time: 2:00 pm - 6:00 pm, April 29, 2010

(2) Non-resident representatives registration time: 8:00 am - 9:00 am, April 30, 2010

VII. Registration Location

The first floor of Xinmao Garden Hotel, Shenzhen
