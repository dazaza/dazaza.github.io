Dragon Dream Class Setting

Four Schools and Class Settings

"Dragon Dream Online" features four schools: Shaolin, Wudang, Ming Sect, and Secret Palace. Each school offers four different types of classes, allowing players to choose their favorite class according to their character descriptions for cultivation. The setting of each school in the game is distinct, and the role positioning of each class also varies. For example, the Luohan Hall class of Shaolin possesses strong spirit and strength, making it very suitable as a physical attacker in a team; whereas the Dharma Institute leans more towards support roles. Similar settings exist within Wudang, Ming Sect, and Secret Palace, where various classes possess different attack methods and have their own advantages in different battles.

Schools and School Specialties

The professional positioning of the four schools shares some commonalities, but each school has unique specialties.

Shaolin: Specializes in boxing, staff techniques, and Luohan Heart Methods;

Wudang: Specializes in swordsmanship, internal family boxing, heart methods, and various array techniques;

Ming Sect: Specializes in knife techniques and various Asura Devil Arts;

Secret Palace: Specializes in double-ring weapons, lightness arts, and various incantation techniques.

The differences between the four schools are quite apparent, allowing players to choose based on their preferences. Notably, Shaolin features purely male characters while Secret Palace consists entirely of female characters.

Over 300 of these skills are realistically presented through motion capture technology, allowing players to master the classic martial arts of the four great schools and fulfill their aspirations to wander the jianghu (martial world).
