In spring, people should eat more ideal slimming foods.

by king0q8o on 2012-02-22 16:08:03

Below, Left Spin Right Reduce [http://www.965com.com/zuoxuanroujian/] will introduce several weight loss foods to everyone, which can be appropriately added to your own recipes for better slimming effects. So, let's take a look at what these foods are.

1、Carrot

Freshly squeezed carrot juice, rich in nutrition, when consumed daily with honey, not only helps slim the face but also brings about an overall feeling of lightness and change.

2、Honey Carrot Juice

Ingredients: half a carrot, half a cup of water, one spoonful of honey

Cut the carrot into strips and place them in the blender, add half a cup of water and honey, start the machine for about one and a half minutes, the carrot juice and honey have been fully mixed, it is both nutritious and delicious.

3、Apple Celery Salad

Ingredients: three apples, a bunch of celery, salad dressing

Left Spin Right Reduce [http://www.965com.com/zuoxuanroujian/] reminds you to wash fresh apples and celery, slice them and rinse with ice water to enhance their crisp texture, then mix with salad dressing for a delicious and healthy dish.

4、Bean Sprouts

Green bean sprout vegetables are very nutritious, and of course, they contain potassium which is beneficial for reducing edema. Moreover, bean sprouts can strengthen chewing effects, making them an excellent food that combines nutritional value with promoting oral activity.

5、Spinach

A small handful of spinach contains abundant potassium and vitamins A and C, but special attention needs to be paid to the cooking method as spinach nutrients can easily be lost.

6、Celery

Rich in high fiber and potassium, it can also promote oral activity, making it a rare healthy food for a smaller face.

7、Dried Persimmons

Dried persimmons, which can be eaten as snacks, not only do not worry about gaining weight, but their excellent chewing effect and ultra-high potassium content make your face smaller the more you eat.
