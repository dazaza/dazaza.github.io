Therapeutic functions of pomelo:

A southern fruit abundant in autumn. It is not only a delicacy but also a natural health food, rich in vitamins, especially Vitamin C, which is 3-5 times more than that in apples and pears.

It contains abundant dietary fiber, special fruit acid, and amino acids, which can regulate human metabolism, lower blood pressure and sugar, relieve phlegm, moisten the lungs, promote digestion, sober up, reduce fire, and promote urination. It has certain auxiliary curative effects on patients with spondylitis, abscess, constipation, and diabetes. It can also prevent arteriosclerosis and cancer.

There are many varieties of pomelo, such as grapefruit, golden thread pomelo, honey pomelo, and Satsuma pomelo. If you want to use it for weight loss, remember to choose according to the principle of "thick skin and much juice."

Pomelo is fragrant, sweet and sour, cool and nutritious, with high medicinal value, and is one of the precious fruits people like to eat. Traditional Chinese medicine believes that pomelo flesh is cold in nature, sweet and sour in taste, and has the medical effects of stopping cough, relieving asthma, clearing heat, resolving phlegm, strengthening the spleen, promoting digestion, sobering up, and eliminating irritability; Modern medical research has found that pomelo flesh contains very rich Vitamin C and insulin-like components, so it has the effects of lowering blood sugar, lowering blood lipids, losing weight, beautifying the face, etc. Regular consumption has an auxiliary therapeutic effect on diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, and arteriosclerosis, and has the function of physical fitness and beauty for obese people.

The weight loss mechanism of pomelo:

Compared with other fruits, it belongs to the citrus family (did you know? Eating more citrus can control weight), with rich Vitamin C content and lots of fiber, making it easy to feel full. However, its calories are very low, comparable to watermelon. For example, a piece of Satsuma pomelo (140g) contains 20 kcal. The remarkable weight loss effect of pomelo lies in its rich content of fruit acid, which can effectively stimulate the gastrointestinal mucosa and affect the absorption of nutrients, thereby suppressing excessive appetite. It also contains special amino acids that can inhibit insulin secretion, thus preventing blood sugar from being converted into fat in the liver. Pomelo is a low GI food, so even if you eat a little more, its calories will not be quickly converted into fat.
