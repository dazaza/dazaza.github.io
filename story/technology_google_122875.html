Earth Online- Map Operation Guide (View the graphic version of the tutorial)

1. Move the map: Hold down the left mouse button on the map to drag or click the direction icon on the upper left of the map to move.

2. Zoom in/out the map: Double click on a point on the map to zoom in directly. You can also zoom in and out by clicking the plus or minus sign or slider at the top left of the map.

3. The longitude and latitude dynamically displayed in the "Current coordinates" column at the top right is the longitude and latitude of the center point of the current map screen. The positive longitude is east longitude, the negative longitude is west longitude, the positive latitude is north latitude, and the negative latitude is south latitude.

4. The three buttons in the upper right corner of the map screen are used to switch the map display mode. From left to right stands for "street electronic map", "satellite map" and "satellite map with annotations". Please choose according to your needs and preferences.

5. Share map: You can click on the "Share current map with friends" at the top right of the screen, and the system will automatically generate the URL link of the current location, and anyone can access this location through this link.

6. Earth online sub-channel navigation: Landmark search channel China urban electronic map Microsoft virtual Earth channel 3D view channel Landmark exchange sharing forum encyclopedia search
