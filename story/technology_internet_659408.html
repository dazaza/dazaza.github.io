Common misconceptions about using car floor mats

by ctmjiaodian on 2012-02-06 10:31:31

1. One-sided emphasis on easy cleaning

Analysis: The purpose of using car floor mats is for water absorption, dust collection, noise reduction, and protection of the main carpet. Focusing solely on ease of cleaning and avoiding rain/snow pollution may lead to counterproductive results and losing sight of what's truly important.

2. Repeated placement of floor mats

Analysis: Placing PVC, 3D, rubber or other types of mats on top of the original velvet floor mat provided by the car manufacturer in order to avoid damage or contamination of the velvet mat is not advisable. This increases safety risks affecting braking and acceleration.

3. Failure to remove the protective film of the host carpet

Analysis: The PE protective film of the host carpet is used to protect it from damage and pollution when there is no other protection. Before installing the car floor mat, this PE film must be removed to ensure that the TPR nails at the bottom of the floor mat can make contact with the host carpet for friction, achieving an anti-slip function.

4. Not cleaning car floor mats or having them cleaned at car wash shops

Analysis: While car floor mats perform the functions of "water absorption and dust collection", the pollution they themselves endure should be cleaned as soon as possible. Otherwise, it will lead to bacterial proliferation, affecting air quality and the physical health of the car owner. Especially for loop pile mats, the dirt and dust inside will accumulate more and more, making it difficult to clean.

Simple water rinsing by car wash shops cannot completely remove the hidden dirt in the car floor mats. The cleaning of car floor mats should be handled by dry cleaning shops or hand washing.

5. Using one set of floor mats for many years

Analysis: After continuous use, any item may lose its originally designed functionality even if it appears to still be in good condition. Car owners with the means can purchase two sets of velvet car floor mats for alternate use, which extends the product's lifespan and saves on economic expenditure.

6. Natural and odorless equals environmental friendliness

Analysis: Almost all modern products have undergone synthetic processing. Whether a product is environmentally friendly should be determined by inspection certificates issued by professional institutions. It is recommended to choose products from professional, well-established, and reputable companies.
