Last night, after two and a half hours of "duels", Jiang Yingrong finally stood on the championship stage of the 2009 Happy Girl competition. The controversies and criticisms created by Hunan TV came to an end with the announcement of the champion. However, whether these girls chasing their dreams will become the next Ye Yiqian or the next Chen Chusheng remains a question in people's minds.

Huang Ying went off-key and became third place.

Last night's Kuang Girl final returned to prime time. Huang Ying, Li Xiaoyun, and Jiang Yingrong faced off in six rounds: "Thank Fans", "Chinese Style", "Individual Vanguard Band", "Growth Stories", "Single Performance", and "Ultimate PK". Last night's judging panel consisted of 100 judges, setting a record for the largest number of judges in any Hunan TV talent show.

At the start of the program, all ten finalists appeared together singing "Singing Our Motherland" to kick off the showdown between Huang Ying, Li Xiaoyun, and Jiang Yingrong. In the match to decide the third place, Huang Ying, who attempted to collaborate with a rock band for the first time, sang "Our Lives Are Full of Sunshine" in an R&B style. Unfortunately, Huang Ying went significantly off-key. In the first round, only one out of ten judges gave her a vote. In the second round, Huang Ying, with her back against the wall, boldly abandoned her usual folk songs and performed "I Am a Little Bird" in a popular style, finally securing three votes from the judges. In the final decisive round, Huang Ying pulled out her trump card, singing "Yingshanhong", but the judges were still unsatisfied with her performance. She ultimately scored only five points in total, falling behind Li Xiaoyun and Jiang Yingrong.

"Double-edged staff" breaks "Blue and White Porcelain"

In fact, last night's competition was a duel between Li Xiaoyun and Jiang Yingrong. In the first round, Jiang Yingrong won applause with her versatile style in the song "Persistence". On the other hand, Li Xiaoyun moved the judges with her story of pursuing music.

In the second round themed around Chinese style, Jiang Yingrong's performance of "Descendants of the Dragon" paired with an impressive double-edged staff dance captivated everyone. In contrast, Li Xiaoyun, known for her vocal prowess, made a small mistake while performing "Blue and White Porcelain". In the final ultimate PK, Jiang Yingrong's "Dancing Alone" continued her consistently stable performance, while Li Xiaoyun's unnamed original song provided a pleasant surprise. Judge Suzy said, "This song could easily become this year's Happy Girl theme song." However, in the end, Jiang Yingrong won with an absolute advantage of 101 to 67. After winning the championship, Jiang Yingrong said, "I'm not good at talking, just one sentence: thank you all."
