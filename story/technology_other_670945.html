How to view the advantages of ball mill equipment

by hx515317 on 2012-02-21 13:58:13

Henan Hongxing Machinery Manufacturing Co., Ltd., as a ball mill manufacturer, is a production and export base for mineral processing ball mill equipment. The sales volume of ball mill equipment ranks first in the domestic industry. It specializes in producing various types of gridded ball mills, overflow ball mills, energy-saving ball mills, dry ball mills, wet ball mills and other grinding and mineral processing equipment. The ball mill equipment has advantages such as low energy consumption, strong adaptability, high energy saving, and excellent operating performance when put into production. Below is a specific analysis:

1. The entire machine adopts a unique "zero-emission" positive pressure operation system. Excellent sealing during manufacturing ensures no external leakage pollution during production. After soundproof measures are adopted for the fan, the noise of the ball mill does not exceed 70 decibels, overcoming the drawback of high noise from other ball mills.

2. The grinding balls of the rolling ball mill always maintain uniform wear, and the output remains almost constant, eliminating the phenomena of uneven wear and reduced output caused by uneven wear in other models. Moreover, due to slight vibration during operation, there is no impact damage to high-hardness wear-resistant materials, so high-wear special materials can be used as grinding media, minimizing wear consumption. For example, in the production of heavy calcium carbonate, the service life of the grinding media can reach over 10,000 hours. This is currently the lowest consumption of grinding media among all grinders.

3. The structural design of the ball mill produced by Hongxing Machinery has almost eliminated the operational obstacles and shortcomings of various ball mills currently available. Measures such as automatic discharge regrinding, adjustable feeding, adjustable fineness, and air sealing have been adopted. Since there are no components inside the grinding chamber that require lubrication, common lubrication failures have been eliminated, and the ball mill has reached ideal operating conditions, allowing for long-term continuous operation. The entire production process can be completed at once. When equipped with a raw material crusher or when the raw material feed particle size is below 10-30mm, the entire production process from raw material to finished product can be completed in one step, without the need for external classification equipment or preliminary grinding equipment.

4. The high-pressure rolling ball grinding mechanism, similar to a giant thrust bearing, consumes the least amount of energy in motion. The material is in a thin layer bed and under high pressure reaches its breaking limit state, so the energy required for movement and crushing is minimal. At the same time, the operation adopts a material/gas mixing ratio working method, reducing fan energy consumption; secondly, the grinding system adopts a positive pressure powder-making system, reducing air volume by 10%, ultimately achieving highly energy-saving effects. For example, producing heavy calcium carbonate products with d97=10μm and a specific surface area reaching 18000cm²/g, the energy consumption index is only 90～95kwt/h, which is 10～20% lower than the 90～110kwt/h of similar foreign products.

5. Hongxing Machinery's ball mill has strong versatility and can be applied to all current mining powder production fields, including metal and non-metal mines of various hardnesses, sintering materials, and can complete grinding and activation in one step. For products requiring high purity, such as ultra-fine quartz and zircon sand, especially powders with strict iron content control requirements, simply specify the requirement when ordering, and it can be met. The powder fineness can be arbitrarily adjusted between a specific surface area of 5000cm²/g to 20000cm²/g.
