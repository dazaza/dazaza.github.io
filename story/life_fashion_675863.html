With the continuous development of the plastic beauty industry, hyaluronic acid rhinoplasty, which has been popular in recent years, is minimally invasive, painless, easy to shape, and highly safe. However, it is indeed regrettable that the rhinoplasty effect lasts for too short a time. Nowadays, a new injectable implant agent - Radiesse (Microsphere) has become popular on the island. Although Radiesse cannot be used in as many areas as hyaluronic acid, its relatively long-lasting and stable effects are also quite obvious. What are the characteristics of Radiesse?

1. Long-lasting Effect

Compared with commonly used injectable implants such as hyaluronic acid, Radiesse has a longer lasting effect. In most experiencers, the effect can last 18-24 months or more. Of course, each patient may vary depending on individual conditions, including personal age, skin quality, lifestyle, and muscle tissue activity.

2. High Safety Factor

Radiesse has better biocompatibility and will not produce toxicity or allergic reactions to the human body. Generally, a skin test is not required before treatment.

3. Natural Shaping

After Radiesse is injected into the skin, it remains soft and adheres to the tissue, allowing the doctor to skillfully complete the shaping. If the patient is dissatisfied with the shaping effect, adjustments can be made within two hours. Due to its calcium content, compared to other materials, Radiesse provides better support and durability.

4. Less Likely to Shift

Since Radiesse has a certain ability to stimulate the regeneration of autologous collagen, the newly formed tissue will encapsulate the Radiesse microspheres, thus reducing the likelihood of displacement.

5. Decomposability

After being injected into the tissue, the gel in Radiesse will be dissolved and absorbed within a few months. The newly formed tissue surrounds the Radiesse microspheres, and after a period of time, the Radiesse microspheres will also gradually be absorbed through normal metabolism.

6. Short Recovery Period

Radiesse can be injected into the area to be filled via a fine 27-gauge needle, resulting in minimal injection wounds and a shorter recovery period for patients.

7. Efficient Usage

In general, for those who have never received an injection, injecting only 0.3 to 0.5 c.c. in the nasal bridge area can achieve a very noticeable rhinoplasty effect. Compared to other materials, the injection volume can save half to one-third of the budget, making it quite cost-effective for first-time patients. Of course, this also depends on individual actual conditions.

8. Effective in One Injection

In clinical treatments, most patients can achieve the best shaping effect during their first treatment. If there are other special conditions, subsequent fine-tuning can be used to achieve the desired optimal results.

9. Wide Range of Applications

Radiesse injections perform well in rhinoplasty and chin augmentation, and also have good effects in smoothing nasolabial folds, marionette lines, or deep lip wrinkles. However, some facial areas are not suitable for Radiesse injections, such as the lip mucosa, tear troughs, or the thinner skin around the eyes.

Additionally, doctors have found in practical operations that due to Radiesse's subtle stimulation of collagen regeneration, when injected into originally sunken areas of the face, it produces a foundation-like effect.
