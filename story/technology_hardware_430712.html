There is a shortage of talent in the advertising design industry.

by spareribs on 2011-05-13 17:38:59

Unable to hire a designer in two months

CAD drafter 1, graphic designer 1, designer 1... Recently, at the recruitment site of the Guangxi Talent Market, a catering and entertainment company posted job openings for designers. "Our company's development scale is no longer limited to the Nanning market. Our company will gradually open branch stores throughout Guangxi Province, so we are currently building an advertising team that incorporates advertising planning and design services to promote our company," said Ms. Luo, the human resources specialist of the company. She also indicated that the recruitment information for design personnel has been publicized for nearly two months, yet they still haven't found a suitable candidate.

★ Career Prospects Comment: In recent years, with increasing enthusiasm for home decoration and greater emphasis on corporate advertising images, interior designers who lead home decoration styles and advertising design personnel have become increasingly scarce talents.

The Macao Special Administrative Region will recruit specialized medical talents from home and abroad

The Health Bureau of the Macao SAR Government recently announced that to align with the ten-year plan for improving the medical system and the future needs of the off-island medical complex, the Health Bureau is planning to introduce international medical specialists from home and abroad, including recruiting professional talents from mainland China, Portugal, Singapore, and other places to support the operation of Macao's medical system.

According to the introduction, the Health Bureau has preliminarily formulated a 5 to 10-year general practice internship and specialized training plan and is actively training doctors. It is expected that by the end of 2011, 24 general practice intern doctors will complete their 18-month training. Meanwhile, the 30 general practice intern doctors currently being recruited are expected to start their internship training in July 2011. In addition to actively training local doctors, the Health Bureau will hire senior foreign doctors according to developmental needs to assist with clinical work and teaching, thereby promoting regional and academic exchanges and supporting the development of Macao's medical system. They will continue to refine and improve the training plan based on actual conditions.

★ Career Prospects Comment: It is understood that with the completion of the off-island medical complex in the Macao SAR, approximately 200 additional doctors will be needed over the next 10 years.

Salary becomes the biggest challenge in the second quarter recruitment market

Recently, Hays China released its second-quarter human resources trends report. According to the survey, 77% of companies plan to increase their workforce in the second quarter. At the same time, due to shortages of high-end specialized talents and employees' overly high expectations for salary, it is difficult for companies to hire suitable talents.

★ Career Prospects Comment: With the rise in salary expectations among job seekers due to rising prices, companies will face their greatest challenge in recruitment.

Groupon battles eBay for technical talent in Silicon Valley

The group-buying website Groupon, headquartered in Chicago, is stepping up its recruitment efforts in the Silicon Valley area, competing with companies like eBay and Facebook for talent. Groupon is attempting to move beyond the realm of email marketing and become a platform connecting merchants and consumers. To achieve this, a large number of technical experts are required, but around the headquarters located in the Midwest United States, it is difficult to find such talents.

As Groupon conducts recruitment, many other established enterprises and startups are also seeking expansion, which will inevitably lead to a shortage of engineering resources and drive up salaries, benefits, and bonuses in the Silicon Valley region. More importantly than offering dry cleaning and childcare services, companies are striving to establish their unique image to potential employees, including providing engineers with unique technical challenges. For some companies, this includes creating the next generation of cutting-edge mobile and social platforms.

★ Career Prospects Comment: Groupon's current situation lies between established enterprises and startups, which has both advantages and disadvantages for achieving their recruitment goals.

Wuhan workplace emphasizes personal connections; welfare refined down to night snack standards

Night snacks, tea breaks, paid travel, and other welfare benefits frequently appear in company recruitment brochures and have become a good strategy for improving employee treatment. It is reported that refining welfare benefits through personal connection strategies to attract talent has become the biggest highlight of this year's job market. Company recruitment managers introduced that hiring is hard, but retaining employees is even harder. The detailed welfare promises in recruitment brochures reflect a thoughtful effort. These improvements in treatment are very attractive to grassroots position personnel with high turnover rates.

Moreover, considering the recruitment needs of units across various industries such as comprehensive education and training, pharmaceutical insurance, book media, electronics technology, real estate, etc., the most frequently recruited positions in April were sales representatives, clerks, accountants, reserve cadres, and administrative customer service.

★ Career Prospects Comment: The change towards refined welfare benefits indicates that these benefits are increasingly becoming one of the capitals for attracting and retaining talent, especially for grassroots positions with small salary gaps but high mobility. Detailed benefits with a strong sense of humanity can enhance employees' sense of belonging.
