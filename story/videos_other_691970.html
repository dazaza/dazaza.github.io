Phoenix Nirvana, Shandong Liuyun Textile Co., Ltd. 25th Anniversary Factory Celebration and Moving to the New District Celebration

by uyahd9mjik on 2012-03-05 20:19:08

Representatives from more than 180 mutual aid units around the country came to celebrate. The staff of Liuchun performed a variety of cultural and artistic programs at the celebration. Leaders from the Textile Association of Zibo City, Shandong Province, and relevant leaders from Gaoqing County, Zibo City, attended the event to give speeches and cut the ribbon.

On November 1st, with loud drums and booming firecrackers, Shandong Liuchun Textile Co., Ltd., located on the Yellow River Plain in Central Lu, held a celebration for its 25th anniversary and relocation to a new district.

Shandong Liuchun Group Co., Ltd., is situated in Gaoqing County, Shandong Province, a premium cotton base, with the Yellow River to the north and the Jiaozhou-Jinan Railway to the south. It is just 3 kilometers away from the Binbo Expressway entrance, offering convenient transportation and beautiful scenery. After 25 years of effort, Liuchun Group has developed into a large textile enterprise with fixed assets of 680 million yuan, becoming one of the pillar enterprises in Gaoqing County. The company currently employs 4,800 people, including over 1,200 technical personnel with college degrees or above, giving it a strong product R&D advantage.

Liuchun Group has 180,000 spindles, 30,000 line spindles, 48 Saurer twisting machines, 120 knitting circular machines, 2 dyeing and finishing production lines, a daily dyeing and finishing capacity of 20 tons, and an annual garment production capacity of 20 million pieces. It also has a cotton processing capacity of 100,000 dan (Chinese unit). The company comprises six subsidiaries: Gaoqing Liuchun Textile Co., Ltd., Zibo Hongfeng Textile Co., Ltd., Zibo Jinhai Knitting Co., Ltd., Gaoqing Yida Textile Garment Co., Ltd., Gaoqing Yinhai Cotton Co., Ltd., and Gaoqing Yincheng Cotton Co., Ltd. These subsidiaries have formed a modernized business system integrating seed cotton acquisition, processing, operation, cotton yarn production, sales, dyeing, knitting, and garment production and operation.

The group owns internationally advanced cleaning and combing machines, FA506 pneumatic pressure fine spinning machines, Italian PX2 combers, Italian automatic winding machines, Saurer twisting machines, top-of-the-line Swiss Uster evenness testers, and first-class standard laboratories. The company produces various types of combed, carded, and air-spun single yarns and threads year-round, producing 1,700 tons of cotton yarn monthly, acquiring and processing 8,000 tons of raw cotton annually, having a knitting and dyeing capacity of 20 tons per day, and an annual garment production capacity of 20 million pieces. It also operates 200 Belgian air-jet looms, forming a complete industrial chain from seed cotton acquisition, processing, cotton yarn production to knitting, dyeing, and garment production.

In October 1999, the group successfully passed the ISO9002 international quality system certification and obtained self-operated import and export rights that same year. It has successively been awarded titles such as "Taxpaying Major Enterprise of Zibo Private Enterprises," "Advanced Export-oriented Enterprise," and "Product Quality Trustworthy Enterprise." The company's products are stable in quality, reasonably priced, sold to well-known domestic enterprises like Lvtai and Youngor, and exported to Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, Southeast Asia, Europe, and America, enjoying high market credibility.
