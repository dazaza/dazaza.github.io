Man installs camera in bathroom to spy on female co-renter

by hj1718004 on 2011-12-20 19:35:28

China News Service, Liuzhou, May 24 (Zhou Liuning, Liang Yuan) - A man in Liuzhou City, Guangxi Province installed a camera in the bathroom to spy on a female colleague who shared the same apartment with him. After the victim discovered this and reported it to the police, the man agreed to pay 6,000 RMB as compensation after police mediation.

At noon on May 23, in a villa burglary case that resulted in the death of a female landlord, a young woman named Azhu (a pseudonym) found a shampoo bottle with a small hole on a corner shelf while using the restroom at her residence. Upon inspection, Azhu found that the bottle was empty and contained a miniature camera inside, with tiny wires connected to the wall.

Realizing she had been spied on, Azhu became furious and suspected her colleague Ahua, who shared the same apartment with her. Azhu immediately called her boyfriend and informed him of the situation and her suspicion. Half an hour later, her boyfriend arrived at Azhu's place, and together they knocked on Ahua's door.

Faced with Azhu holding the shampoo bottle and camera, Ahua admitted that the camera was specifically placed in the bathroom to spy on Azhu and apologized to her. Azhu's boyfriend then called the police. After receiving the alarm, the Yufeng police of Liuzhou arrived at the scene and investigated the entire incident. Ahua truthfully confessed to the police about his improper actions of installing the camera in the bathroom. The police retrieved photos taken by the miniature camera and found two frontal pictures of Azhu inside the toilet.

The investigation revealed that both Ahua and Azhu were employees of the same company and shared a residential house on Liaoyuan Road in Liuzhou. Both had live-in girlfriends and boyfriends, and they generally helped and cared for each other, maintaining a good relationship. When the officer asked Ahua why he spied on Azhu, Ahua claimed it was because "his girlfriend was on a business trip, and he missed her too much."

The officer severely criticized Ahua. After mediation, Ahua deeply regretted his inappropriate behavior that harmed Azhu and expressed willingness to compensate 6,000 RMB. After the mediation, Azhu and her boyfriend stated that they would consider moving out.
