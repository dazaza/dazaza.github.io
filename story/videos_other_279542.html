【Women's Health】 Women Should Pamper Their Breasts

Women should pamper their breasts. As a breast beauty expert, do you truly understand the structure of your own breasts? Do you know what your breasts really need?

A woman's breasts are not only the source of maternal love but also an important part of the curvaceous feminine body shape. Therefore, they are a symbol of femininity and a source of pride for women.

Every woman hopes to have a pair of full and elastic breasts, which form the unique smooth, round, and graceful curves that characterize women. However, most Chinese women have an incomplete understanding of their breasts and lack awareness about breast care. They generally believe that breasts are innate, and they will develop into whatever shape they are destined to be, with no improvement possible through later efforts, nor any need for regular care or maintenance.

According to authoritative statistical data: 69% of women experience breast sagging and diffusion; 76% of women admit to having flat and sagging breasts. 80% of women experience breast swelling before menstruation; 60% of women experience fibroadenosis by middle age, and the incidence of breast cancer is also increasing.

A woman's breasts are where beauty lies, but they are also a very fragile area, and one that we often neglect and rarely pay attention to. We often notice changes in our facial skin: a pimple appears today, the skin feels a bit dry these two days, the skin has become sensitive recently, and it looks a little yellowish, etc. So, we quickly take care of our skin.

Whether at a beauty salon or doing it ourselves at home, we meticulously care for our facial skin. But we don't realize that daily care for the breasts is also very important.

Don't think that premenstrual swelling is normal. Swelling indicates blocked meridians in the chest, or possibly lumps, which over time can develop into fibroadenosis or breast cancer. Just like delicate facial skin, breasts also need regular care: cleaning, massaging, detoxifying, nourishing with essence cream, and tenderly taking care of them. Nowadays, many brands have launched breast care products with tightening effects and those that help unblock meridians. Most beauty salons offer breast care treatments, not breast augmentation projects, but focusing on maintenance and adjusting the breast shape.

You can buy breast care products to take care of yourself at home, or go to a beauty salon for professional breast care treatments. This way, you can prevent breast diseases and maintain a perfect breast shape, staying healthy and beautiful.

Calm Down and Self-Inspect "Storms"

After the age of 30, the breasts enter the most active period of life, and various breast problems are most common at this time. Especially when doing self-examinations of the breasts, you may unexpectedly discover: small lumps, pain, nipple discharge, etc., but in most cases, these are not serious illnesses. 70-80% of adult women have fibroadenosis, and the incidence of breast fibroadenoma is around 10%.

Suspicious Symptom 1: Lumps - Although this is a common early symptom of breast cancer, feel again. If the lump has clear boundaries, is soft in texture, and moves well, it is likely to be a benign adenoma; if the lump changes with the menstrual cycle, then it is the most common fibroadenosis. The following might be the lump symptoms of breast cancer: hard texture, adhered to surrounding tissues, painless, and accompanied by swelling of lymph nodes under the armpit, above the clavicle, and in the neck.

Suspicious Symptom 2: Pain - If the pain is cyclical, it is fibroadenosis; if the pain persists and is near the sternum, it could be rib hyperplasia of the chest wall. The following might be the pain symptoms of breast cancer: finding lumps, inverted nipples, and skin resembling orange peel along with the pain.

Suspicious Symptom 3: Nipple Discharge - If the secretion from the nipple is serous or milky, it is likely to be breast duct dilation or a benign pituitary tumor in the brain. The following might be the nipple discharge symptoms of breast cancer: bloody nipple discharge.

In short, pamper your breasts, see them as a gift and a source of vitality, and cherish them for a lifetime.
