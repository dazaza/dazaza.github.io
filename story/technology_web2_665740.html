Dolphin and kitten play and kiss in viral video (pic)

by anonymous on 2012-02-15 09:47:07

The kitten and the dolphin are kissing. Friends who have watched the youth idol drama "Dolphin in Love with a Cat" must certainly remember the beautiful love story in the series. In it, a dolphin trainer falls in love with a girl who has a cat-like personality - like the magic wand of a creative wedding in a video conference. But if we were to say that dolphins and cats, these two animals, could fall in love, probably no one would believe it.

Recently, on the UK's major video websites, a video of a dolphin and a kitten playing and kissing has been wildly circulating. In the footage, the dolphin either bumps around with the kitten or shares deep kisses, their intimacy going beyond the ordinary, almost qualifying as a real-life version of "Dolphin in Love with a Cat."

Judging from the upload date of the video, this clip was filmed and uploaded online at the end of March this year. By April 10th, it had already garnered 31,000 views. This video, which lasts about one minute and fifty seconds, does not specify the videographer or the filming location. However, the dolphin’s squeals and the kitten’s meows make it easy to understand the feelings between these two small animals.

At the start of the video, the kitten walks to the edge of the pool and lowers its head so that the dolphin can touch its head with its chin as it surfaces. After a few seconds, the dolphin’s expression changes, looking rather like it is smiling. Afterwards, the kitten very friendly lifts its left front paw to try and grab the dolphin, perhaps out of fear of being scratched by the kitten’s sharp claws, the dolphin quickly pulls back its head to avoid being reached. At this point, the little kitten obediently puts down its front paw and gazes blankly at the dolphin in the water. Not long after, the dolphin swims back to the edge of the pool and shares a big kiss with the kitten. Their coordination is extremely harmonious, both moving forward almost simultaneously, leaving people marveling. After a few sweet seconds of kissing, the kitten touches the dolphin's head with its paw. The dolphin seems quite satisfied with its feline companion; happily spinning around in the water before returning to the poolside again.

Following this, the kitten and the dolphin play for a while at the edge of the pool. The kitten taps the dolphin’s right cheek twice with its left front paw, perhaps wanting to get revenge on this audacious kitten, the dolphin suddenly splashes water onto the kitten. A short text explanation within the video claims that these two animals have maintained an intimate relationship for some time now, sharing daily kisses and play. (Editor: SN026)
