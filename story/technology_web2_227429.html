Ugly Wudi Season 3

by d823111713 on 2009-08-23 11:46:57

The full series of the third season of Hunan TV's self-made fashion song and dance inspirational comedy "Ugly Woman Invincible" will premiere on Hunan TV on a certain day of the month. The first two seasons of "Ugly Woman Invincible" mainly focused on workplace inspiration and fashion styles, while the third season takes a different approach by transforming into a musical. The third season revolves around two storylines: Concept Advertising Company and Enough Thunder Bar, with original dances including the most shocking "Ha Ha Dance Forest Invincible", where in the grand finale, a thousand extras join in to perform the "Ha Ha Dance".

Introduction to the plot of the third season of "Ugly Woman Invincible": Xiao Fei left Concept, and An Rui became the president of Concept. Tang Ya Jun wanted to bring Tian Cai back under his control. An Xi stayed at Concept to help An Rui but always went against him in dealing with some company issues. Jia Ming couldn't stand An Rui's rule and decided to leave. An Xi tried to stop others from leaving, feeling pitiful for them and somewhat missing Xiao Fei's management. Wu Yong lost his shares in Concept and was expelled by An Rui. The members of the Ugly Women Club sympathized but didn't dare to say anything. However, Sha Sha and others told Wu Yong that if there was any help needed in the future, they would do their best. Pei Na was fired by An Rui for being late too often. Wu Yong felt sorry for her and took her in, finding her a job as a secretary. Pei Na was deeply moved and decided to turn over a new leaf and work hard with Wu Yong.

At this time, Wu Di met Lei Jiong. Lei Jiong was a rich young man who didn't want to inherit his father's fortune and instead wanted to start his own career. So he went to the bar as a bartender to be self-reliant. Lei Jiong met Wu Di at the bar, who was heartbroken after being hurt by Xiao Fei. Through their conversations, Lei Jiong admired Wu Di's character and fell in love with her, starting an intense pursuit. However, Wu Di still loved Xiao Fei, though she had been deeply hurt by him and had lost faith in other people's advances. After knowing Wu Di's experience, Lei Jiong felt pity for her and decided to change her. He vowed to make everyone amazed when they saw Wu Di. So he took Wu Di to an image stylist to transform her. Under his changes, Wu Di was completely renewed and became very beautiful. She was very grateful to Lei Jiong.

An Xi was still not giving up on Xiao Fei. She hoped to win back Xiao Fei's love and promised him that if he stayed with her, she would help him regain the company. But Xiao Fei still loved Wu Di and did not accept An Xi's offer. Xiao Fei realized that he should rise up and not always rely on others' help, so he decided to start his own business and truly compete with An Rui. Sometimes he felt a bit frustrated, so when he went to the bar to drink, he met Wu Di again. He was surprised and started pursuing her again. He confessed his love to her, but Wu Di refused him and pulled Lei Jiong next to her, saying he was her boyfriend and asking Xiao Fei to stop thinking about her. Xiao Fei was deeply hurt and got drunk. Wu Di helped call An Xi's phone to let her come pick up Xiao Fei. An Xi rushed over but only saw Xiao Fei lying on the table calling out Wu Di's name. An Xi angrily left. Later, the bar staff called Wu Yong, who hurriedly came to take Xiao Fei away. At this moment, Lei Jiong was happy, thinking Wu Di really liked him. But Wu Di's heart was still worried about Xiao Fei, who was drunk. She was very painful because she still loved Xiao Fei.

To forget Xiao Fei, Wu Di reluctantly agreed to date Lei Jiong and introduced him to her parents. Lin Dad and Lin Mom were very happy. Once, Wu Di's computer broke down, and since Ya Jun wasn't there, Lei Jiong helped fix it and read Wu Di's diary. He saw Wu Di's feelings for Xiao Fei and was very conflicted. Lei Jiong's father, in order to make his son return to inherit the family business and knowing that Wu Di was dating his son, sent someone to secretly investigate Wu Di. He learned everything about Wu Di's past, then found Wu Di, told her about Lei Jiong's identity, and advised her to leave Lei Jiong because her status didn't match Lei Jiong's. He also told Wu Di that Lei Jiong wouldn't like her; he just wanted to use her to destroy Tian Cai. Wu Di was hurt and proposed breaking up with Lei Jiong. Lei Jiong explained, and Wu Di revealed her true feelings, saying even if Lei Jiong hadn't deceived her, she would still break up with him because she never liked Lei Jiong. She had always liked Xiao Fei.

Lei Jiong decided to let go of Wu Di and help her find true love. Xiao Fei and Wu Yong decided to start a new company. They needed manpower and funds, so Wu Yong found Wu Di, hoping Tian Cai could lend him money and told Wu Di the whole story of Xiao Fei luring her, telling her that Xiao Fei really fell in love with her. He hoped Wu Di could forgive Xiao Fei for his sake. After deep thought, Wu Di forgave Xiao Fei, persuaded Tang Ya Jun to lend money to Xiao Fei, and worked at Xiao Fei's new company, managing it well. At this time, An Xi came to find Wu Di, begging her to give Xiao Fei back to her. Wu Di assured An Xi that she would never be with Xiao Fei. To keep her promise to An Xi, Wu Di didn't express her feelings for Xiao Fei but told Xiao Fei that she always considered him a good friend and hoped he wouldn't have any more thoughts about her. She also lied to Xiao Fei, saying she was going to marry Lei Jiong.

Xiao Fei was sad but couldn't do anything. Although both loved each other, they couldn't move forward. At this time, Lei Jiong found Xiao Fei and asked him to treat Wu Di well. Xiao Fei, confused, told Lei Jiong what Wu Di had said to him. Lei Jiong gave Xiao Fei Wu Di's diary. When Xiao Fei saw Wu Di's every detail about him, he knew Wu Di loved him and was very happy. So Xiao Fei confessed his love to Wu Di, expressing all his feelings for her. Amidst sweet and painful contradictions, Wu Di was moved and embraced Xiao Fei. Xiao Fei took Wu Di home to meet his parents. Father Fei didn't say much, but Mother Fei strongly opposed it, insisting that An Xi be her daughter-in-law. Wu Di was sad, but Xiao Fei chased after her, assuring her that he would make his parents accept her. When Xiao Fei met Wu Di's parents again, they also encountered difficulties. The two laughed and consoled each other.

Huang Yiming returned from London and learned about the events from others. He immediately found the disheartened An Xi. At this point, An Xi had already decided to let go. She realized no matter what she did, she couldn't get Xiao Fei's heart. She decided to go abroad with Little Huang to forget everything that happened here. Pei Na and Wu Yong, after so many setbacks, finally realized the importance of each other. Wu Yong's father's bar business grew larger and earned a lot of money. Wu Yong decided to use this money and the funds from Xiao Fei's new company to reclaim Concept. Ya Jun eventually gave up on Pei Na because he met a beautiful woman named Xiao Wu who didn't admire wealth but loved him genuinely. In the end, Zhang Ling and Xiao Wu ended up together, and Zhang Ling changed a lot for Xiao Wu, becoming less materialistic but sometimes still a little greedy. Wu Di and Xiao Fei did a lot of work to be together, and both sets of parents finally agreed. Their relationship became very sweet. As for Su Lei, after going bankrupt, she spent very frugally and experienced the coldness of human nature, but with the help of Wu Di and Xiao Fei, her husband's company greatly improved, and the wealthy life returned. Sha Sha remained as infatuated as ever but deeply loved Ruosong.
