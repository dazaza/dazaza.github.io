The bottleneck in the development of the technology market

by keyizhang008 on 2012-03-02 15:41:22

With the in-depth development of reform, China's technology market has rapidly developed from nothing to something, and from small to large. It includes standing science and technology condition markets, technology property rights trading institutions, online technology trading markets, etc. The scale of transactions continues to expand. A multi-level and multi-form technology trading system has been formed from cities to rural areas. The technology market has become an important channel for scientific and technological innovation and industrialization investment, an important way to convert technological resource advantages into actual productivity, promoted the process of market-oriented research and development, commercialization of technological achievements, and industrialization of high-tech industries in China, greatly improving the speed of achievement transformation and promoting the close integration of science and technology with the economy.

The technology market has played a fundamental and important role in optimizing the allocation of scientific and technological resources. First, it has promoted rapid growth in the volume of patent technology transactions. Second, by introducing patent technology or technical secrets through the technology market, and extending R&D and innovating on the basis of digestion and absorption, new independent intellectual property rights are formed, which is an important way for enterprises to enhance their independent innovation capabilities. However, there are still many problems and shortcomings in the current technology market.

1. The technology market is not sufficiently integrated with other types of markets, lacks coordination and supporting measures, and its market legal system is not sound. Enterprises have problems in their operating mechanisms. Enterprises are both suppliers and demanders of technology. Due to reasons such as enterprise systems, many enterprises behave very short-term oriented in the technology market, pursuing short-term benefits, failing to truly become market entities, especially most enterprises lack the motivation and pressure for technological innovation, invest insufficiently in technology, leading to an unreasonable configuration of scientific and technological resources, resulting in single and repetitive technology commodities in the market. There are few high-grade, high-technology content, and market-demand suitable technology commodities that sell well.

2. Some scientific and technological achievements or patent technologies only remain laboratory results, without undergoing pilot tests or industrial production-scale experiments, unable to produce qualified products. The non-intuitive nature of technological achievements means simple textual descriptions are insufficient to attract investors' interest, making it harder to serve as the basis for investment decisions. Worse still, some technical tricks have appeared in the technology market, such as some technological achievements having obvious defects in principles, processes, or other aspects but concealing these defects and failure records. Although the quantity is not large, the impact is extremely bad, making investors suspicious of being deceived, delaying the progress of technology transactions, seriously affecting the normal order of the technology market and the interests of the masses.

3. People's concepts need further change. The policy regulations and supervision system of the technology market (laws and regulations) are not perfect. Currently, China does not have a specific law or regulation targeting the technology market. The absence of policy regulations at the national level leads to inconsistent supervision of the technology market across regions, unstandardized trading behavior, and the inability of technology traders to have their rights effectively protected, increasing the risk cost of technology transactions and affecting the further standardization and development of the technology market. Most importantly, the circulation link of technology commodities is not smooth, and intermediary forces are weak. Technology intermediaries dominated by intermediary consulting and services develop slowly, lacking effective all-round services. Most scientific and technological achievements are still direct trades, and the intermediary role of the technology market has not been fully played. Technology commodities lack sales channels. Some technology markets lack compound talents, and team development is insufficient.

As the role of the technology market becomes increasingly recognized by the market, more technology properties and venture capital will enter the technology market. The development of the technology market still needs improvement, and its level urgently needs to be enhanced. How to effectively promote the development of the technology market is a problem that needs urgent resolution currently.
