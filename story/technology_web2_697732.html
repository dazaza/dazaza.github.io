What checks should be done before a painless abortion? Gynecological experts from Shanxi Huimin Hospital introduce that women who have an accidental pregnancy must go to the hospital for a systematic gynecological examination. Only in this way can the abortion operation be carried out safely and smoothly. For female friends, these seemingly unnecessary pre-abortion checks are all necessary. So, what checks should be done before a painless abortion?

What checks should be done before a painless abortion? The pre-abortion checks mainly include the following:

1. Pregnancy test: Before the operation, it must be confirmed by the doctor through HCG and ultrasound diagnosis as intrauterine pregnancy, and the number of days of conception and the size of the gestational sac suitable for abortion must be determined. On the day after the operation, you need to return to the hospital for an ultrasound to confirm whether the uterus has contracted well.

2. Routine vaginal discharge check: To understand whether there are trichomonads or fungi in the vagina, if necessary, chlamydia, mycoplasma, and gonococcus tests may also be required. If the above microorganisms exist, they can easily cause ascending infections. Anti-infection and anti-inflammatory treatment should be carried out before the abortion; otherwise, the infection will worsen and affect the embryo development during future pregnancies, leading to miscarriage.

3. Blood routine, urine routine, and electrocardiogram (ECG) check: If you have previously suffered from heart, lung, liver, kidney, blood, or systemic diseases (including drug allergies, recent surgeries, or treatments), please provide relevant information to the doctor before the operation so that the doctor can determine whether you are suitable for a painless abortion.

4. Inflammation check: To see if there is inflammation present. If inflammatory symptoms are found, further examinations and treatments are needed. Generally, treatment is required before surgery to prevent inflammation from spreading upwards, causing adnexitis, pelvic inflammatory disease, various types of vaginitis, and cervical inflammation.

5. Ultrasound uterine size check: The best time for a painless abortion is around 35-45 days of pregnancy. Abortion can be performed within 70 days of pregnancy, but it should not exceed 70 days. If it exceeds 70 days, a forceps evacuation will be required, increasing the surgical risk. This is one of the checks that needs to be done before the abortion.
