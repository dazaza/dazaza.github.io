The large-scale play "My Own Swordsman"

by hqhk1983 on 2008-11-27 12:12:51

Mount Heng Sect is in urgent need of help!

Master Mo Da has passed away, leaving behind a huge debt! His numerous wives are dividing the property, leaving his son Mo Xiaobao to hold the fort alone!

Mo Xiaobao, without great ambition, shortsighted, lazy, but still able to distinguish between different kinds of grains. With no way to avoid debts, unsuccessful in job hunting, and waiting for employment like years, and with no hope of practicing辟valley, he finally became a temporary worker at "Yihong Tower" under the guidance of a "wise man".

Speaking of "Yihong Tower", there is a girl named Dingchun, who stays in her room all day without any visitors, always muttering "SURPRISE". This makes Mo Xiaobao feel uneasy and awkward during working hours. The two don't talk about the past, but look to the future, commenting on national affairs, witty remarks flowing, and secretly nurturing feelings for each other.

Mo Xiaobao's second uncle sent an urgent message from 900 miles away, bringing the great news that the Longmen Escort Agency is full of emotions and righteousness, keeping promises worth a thousand golds, and Miss Tong Xiangyu will marry him as scheduled! Finally, Mo Xiaobao can escape from his misery and live a good life with his wife's dowry.

On the day of their breakup, Dingchun sent Mo Xiaobao off with deep affection, giving him a steamed bun to fill his stomach. Little did they know that there was a secret in the bun... Xiaobao became determined to make a profit without capital. Unfortunately, heaven didn't favor him, and he was caught by the authorities on his first attempt, awaiting execution in the autumn!

Thus...

The widely spread and well-known TV series "A Love for Ridicule" was born...
