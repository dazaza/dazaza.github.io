As March begins, the whole country is welcoming the beautiful spring scenery, and the spring views in various places are all fascinating. Taking advantage of the women's festival to create a "short holiday" and going out with good sisters is undoubtedly the best way to strengthen ties and relax. —— Together, we can go to places with blooming spring flowers for an outing and flower appreciation, or to the most leisurely and romantic cities to see the sea, soak in hot springs, do SPA, and shop. As long as you're willing, an exciting holiday is right before your eyes.

【Flower Appreciation and Spring Outing Section】

Women are like flowers, and on the day that belongs to women, they often unconsciously want to immerse themselves in the sea of flowers, feeling the beauty and preciousness of their youthful looks. In March when it's warm and the flowers bloom, all kinds of flowers open one after another, and all over the country, the best time for flower appreciation has arrived successively. Immersing oneself in the sea of flowers to celebrate the festival is both festive and appropriate.

● Colorful Rapeseed Flower Sea

Recommended Place: Luoping, Yunnan

Recommendation Reason: Early March is the peak flowering period of rapeseed flowers in Luoping. Especially during the 2012 Luoping International Rapeseed Flower Culture Tourism Festival, tens of thousands of acres of rapeseed flowers bloom brilliantly, and the mountains, villages, roads, and rivers are all integrated into the golden rapeseed flower sea. Beautiful ladies might as well leave their charming figures in this colorful light.

Tourism Highlights:

Jinji Peak and Niujie are the best places to view the rapeseed flowers in Luoping. Jinji Peak is located northeast of Luoping County town. The view from the observation platform is excellent, and you can take in tens of thousands of acres of golden rapeseed flower fields at a glance; Niujie is located north of the city. Here, the rapeseed flower fields are small and exquisite, and the rapeseed flowers undulate with the hills, interwoven with winter wheat, vegetables, and other color blocks, creating a unique charm.

Recommended Route: Luoping Ten Thousand Mu Rapeseed Flowers, Huangguoshu Waterfall, Wanfeng Forest, Nanman Miao Village, Yunfeng Tuntian two flights four days.

● Garden Cherry Blossom Viewing by East Lake

Recommended Place: East Lake Moshan, Wuhan

Recommendation Reason: Wuhan is a famous cherry blossom viewing place in China. By March, the beautiful cherry blossoms bloom as brilliantly as rosy clouds, white as the first snow. You can also enjoy the night cherry blossoms at East Lake, under the neon lights, the cherry blossoms emit a hazy white light in the pitch-black night sky, beautiful and dreamlike, making women's hearts flutter.

Tourism Highlights:

The cherry blossom garden in East Lake Moshan Scenic Area covers 150 mu and is one of the cherry blossom viewing places in Wuhan City, known as one of the world's three major cherry blossom places along with Japan's Hirosaki Cherry Blossom Garden and the United States' Washington Cherry Blossom Garden.

Recommended Route: Hubei Three Gorges Dam, Yellow Crane Tower, East Lake Moshan high-speed rail three days

● Peach Blossom Edges by the Lijiang River

Recommended Place: Gongcheng, Guangxi

Recommendation Reason: When ten thousand mu of peach blossoms in Gongcheng, Guilin bloom all over the mountains, the annual "Guilin Gongcheng Peach Blossom Festival" kicks off lively. Single women can come here to find happiness in the "Peach Blossom Fate," get a dose of "Peach Blossom Luck," while also enjoying the Lijiang River and mountains, making it an excellent route for spring outings and flower appreciation.

Tourism Highlights:

Gongcheng lacks the noise of the city, only vast seas of flowers and fragrant grasses in the mountain-water countryside. Besides wandering through the romantic flower sea, visitors can also participate in Yao ethnic group song and dance bonfire parties, watch performances of ethnic风情 songs and dances, admire Yao ethnic skills such as tea beating. The peach blossom festival has specially invited local delicacies from Gongcheng, Ping乐, Lipu, Guanyang, Yangshuo, Hezhou, so the spring peach blossom food street outside the peach garden should not be missed.

Gongcheng is only a 2-hour drive from Guilin and a 1-hour drive from Yangshuo. After flower appreciation, you can also visit the famous Guilin mountains and waters.

Recommended Route: Guangxi Gongcheng Peach Blossom Festival, Yaoshan Rhododendron Flowers, Lipu Fengyu Rock, Big Banyan Tree, Xingping Fishing Village, Yangshuo West Street four-day pure play tour double flights

● Elegant Manor Apricot Blossom Viewing

Recommended Place: Chang Family Manor, Taiyuan, Shanxi

Recommendation Reason: The second month of the lunar calendar is also called the apricot month, which is the time when apricot blossoms begin to bloom. At the Chang Family Manor, known as the "First Confucian Merchant Family in China," it's already "the full garden spring color cannot be contained," and many tourists specifically come to admire the apricot blossoms. In the ancient and elegant Qing Dynasty gardens, beauties can marvel at the unique northern floral world.

Tourism Highlights:

Chang Family Manor is the private estate of the wealthy Chang family during the Qing Dynasty, integrating Qing residential houses, gardens, and long streets. Every early spring, tens of thousands of apricot blossoms bloom, adorning the ancient manor beautifully. You can walk through the quiet and elegant Qing-style gardens, explore the densely packed deep courtyard mansions, and enjoy the harmonious beauty of pavilions, terraces, and gazebos. You can also admire paintings, plaques, couplets, brick carvings, wood carvings, stone inscriptions, and other fine works of Jin merchant culture, enjoying a dual feast of nature and art.

Recommended Route: Taiyuan Chang Family Manor flower appreciation tour, Wutai Mountain blessing, Pingyao Ancient City double flights four days

● Famous Mountains and Rivers Peaches Delicate

Recommended Place: Huangshan, Anhui

Recommendation Reason: March is the most romantic season in Huangshan. Peach blossoms in Wuli Village compete for brilliance, no longer just the strong green and majestic gray rocks. At this time, bringing the innate love for flowers that women have to encounter a romantic flower journey fits perfectly with the ancient phrase "peach blossoms reflect red faces."

Tourism Highlights:

Huangshan is not only majestic but also gentle under the backdrop of hundreds of flowers in early spring. On March 18th, the fifth China Huangshan Wuli Peach Blossom Festival will open at the Wuli Square in Biyang Town, Yixian County.

After flower appreciation, you can relax at the Huangshan Hot Spring located at an altitude of 650 meters on the waist of Mount Huangshan. Surrounded by layers of water vapor, you can feel the fresh charm of nature.

Recommended Route: Huangshan Special Train, Wuli Peach Blossoms, Luhu Village Rapeseed Flowers double sleeper five days

● Romantic Island Shopping and Beach Viewing

Recommended Place: Sanya, Hainan

Recommendation Reason: Enjoying the romance of the beach and shopping without restraint, such a destination can only be Sanya. The retail prices at Sanya Duty-Free Shop are 10-35% lower than those in other domestic department stores, and the scale is large, the brands are complete, and the environment is good. Strolling around in the duty-free shop, buying the latest seasonal clothing and handbags, then stretching out on the seven-kilometer-long sandy beach of Yalong Bay to relax and play in the sea is undoubtedly the best journey arrangement for women to spoil themselves.

Tourism Highlights:

The shopping area of Sanya Duty-Free Shop is divided into two floors up and down. The first floor has cosmetics and clothes and bags that make many female compatriots crazy, and the second floor is the special sales area for watches and clothes. In addition, the duty-free shop also has a dedicated Hainan specialty zone, indicating that this is an extraordinary shopping trip - the entire process is filled with a strong Hainan style.

Recommended Route: Xinglong, Wuzhizhou Island, Xidao, Duty-Free Shop Sanya Double Flights Four Days

● Soak in [Beauty Bath] Beside Taibidong

Recommended Place: Xian Ning, Hubei

Recommendation Reason: Women need careful conditioning and maintenance to remain as beautiful as flowers. Going to a comfortable wellness hot spring to soak in a beauty bath in the spring is a popular choice among many women. The climate in Hubei in March is pleasant, and you can go to Xian Ning to soak in hot springs - its mineral baths and characteristic additive baths have significant beauty effects, and you can also go to Wuhan University to admire cherry blossoms, ensuring that soaking in hot springs and admiring flowers are not neglected.

Tourism Highlights:

Xian Ning is known as "[One City Twelve Springs]," with twelve hot springs in the urban area alone. The Taibi Hot Spring, mainly based on Taoist culture, is located beside the famous tourist attraction Taibi Cave. There are many characteristic hot springs favored by women, such as milk baths with added flavors, petal baths, herbal baths, stone plate hot springs, etc. Besides soaking in hot springs, you can also go to Wuhan University to admire the cherry blossom feast.

Recommended Route: Wuhan University Cherry Blossoms, Taibi Hot Spring High-Speed Rail Three Days

【Fashionable Resort Section】

Some people love traveling across thousands of mountains and rivers, chasing rare flowers and strange grasses, while others love being innovative and pursuing ultimate enjoyment. During the holidays, it's rare to put work aside, so why not change places and treat yourself well with hot springs, SPAs, and shopping, spending a luxurious holiday with upgraded enjoyment.

● Luxury SPA at the Foot of the Snow Mountain

Recommended Place: Lijiang Banyan Tree

Recommendation Reason: All "SPA enthusiasts" have heard of the top brand Banyan Tree. Lijiang Banyan Tree is nestled at the foot of the picturesque Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. For women who are always considered to be made of water, appreciating the grandeur of Yunnan's snow mountains while enjoying top-notch luxury SPA services is a life like that of immortals.

Tourism Highlights:

Relaxing in the spacious Naxi-style villas at Lijiang Banyan Tree, opening the window allows you to enjoy the scenery of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, and what women love most about vacationing here is its high-quality SPA. Its unique spring, summer, autumn, winter treatment courses combined with the five elements of heaven and earth skincare courses have quite good beauty and health benefits. Recommend the unique "Hot Cinnamon Stone Massage," the heat it emits can wash away fatigue, allowing women to relax even more.

Besides enjoying the SPA, you can visit Lijiang Ancient Town, Shuhe Ancient Town, Yuhe Corridor, or tour the Jade Dragon Snow Mountain scenic area.

Recommended Route: Leisurely Traveling in Lijiang, Luxurious Enjoyment of Banyan Tree Self-guided Tour Double Flights Five Days

● Admire the Spring Rain in March on Su Causeway

Recommended Place: Hangzhou West Lake

Recommendation Reason: It is said that the beauty of West Lake is in March, and Hangzhou right now is the most enchanting season of spring. In the misty rain, the most representative Jiangnan charm seeps into the hearts of travelers. Find a lodge with a view of West Lake to stay for a few days, a glimpse of the lake waves, a cheongsam, and a chance meeting can weave the dreamt-of Jiangnan dream.

Tourism Highlights:

The "beauty" West Lake is an indispensable protagonist in Hangzhou. Visiting here in March, you can most fully experience the poetic Jiangnan atmosphere. The famous Su Causeway in Spring is just the right time, either walking on the causeway or renting a bike to roam slowly, both are great feelings.

On the side of West Lake, the international brand name district surrounded by Dongpo Road, Hubin Road, and Pinghai Road gathers dozens of international top brand specialty stores, and there are also many characteristic shops nearby, satisfying the blood-thirsty needs of luxury queens and shrewd petit bourgeoisie alike. Particularly recommended are the Hangzhou-style women's clothing and silk city on Wulin Road, where you might use real Hangzhou silk to custom-make a cheongsam exclusively for yourself.

The distinctive bars in the business district at night are the best entertainment for nightlife, where you can experience the joy of sipping wine and getting tipsy, and also feel the charm of the night West Lake.

Recommended Route: Hangzhou, Huangshan, Thousand Island Lake, Xixi Wetland Double Flights Five Days
