Kenwood HD10GB7 Market Price: 2880 yuan Kenwood HD10GB7 weighs 78 grams, with dimensions of only 62x44x17mm, adopting a 1.5-inch OLED display screen, offering four theme modes to choose from. It supports MP3, WMA, WAV audio file formats, supports Microsoft WMA DRM10 copyright protection.

In addition, HD10GB7 also supports Napster To Go online music download service and can browse JPEG format images.

HD10GB7 adopts a non-magnetic stainless steel alloy chassis, reducing noise and improving sound positioning.

HD10GB7 is equipped with Kenwood's high-frequency interpolation technology "Supreme", which can perform interpolation compensation up to the 22kHz frequency range.

Editor's review: The continuous playback time of HD10GB7 using built-in lithium-ion batteries can reach up to 24 hours. The built-in lithium-ion battery can be charged through a USB interface and fully charged within 3 hours. The current selling price is 2880 yuan.
