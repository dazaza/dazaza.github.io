White-collar workers make their own "little account book" to learn to be housekeepers

by fenqu on 2007-11-16 13:29:16

White-collar self-made "little account book" learning manager

White-collar self-made "little account book" learning manager - making the monthly salary expenditure clear, "online accounting" has also emerged accordingly.

[Xinmin Network · Evening Post Selection] A "moonlight clan" with N confused accounts, in the past, this could be said to be the "consumption portrait" of many young people. However, more and more white-collar workers have started to care about their "monthly income and expenses," creating a small account book to calculate a "clear account."

Almost bought an accounting ledger

Miss Xu, 26 years old, and her boyfriend together earn about 10,000 yuan per month. Every month, "money disappears like flowing water," and by the end of the month, they don't even know how much is left over. Recently, influenced by her colleagues, she discovered the fun of keeping accounts -

Find a little notebook, record every day's income and expenditure for her and her boyfriend, summarize every 10 days, calculate how much "hard-earned money" has been spent, give herself a warning, and settle the balance of income and expenditure at the end of each month. "I even thought about buying an accounting-specific ledger, but later I didn't know how long I could persist, so I gave up."

The main consumption is shopping and treating guests

After three months, regardless of whether the little account book helped save money, it made Miss Xu clearly see the consumption structure of each month:

The first item is rent, 1500 yuan per month, taking up more than one-third of the monthly consumption. The second two items are what Miss Xu never expected before: "As long as you buy clothes, treat people to meals, or send gifts for friends' weddings or childbirths, the expenses of this month will skyrocket." Usually, these expenses combined are several hundred yuan higher than the rent. As for eating, transportation, water, electricity, and gas, which were previously considered major expenses by Miss Xu, they are just "a drop in the bucket."

Accounting did not help her save money

In the past, the older generation kept accounts to use money better, but Miss Xu's account book played a minimal role in this aspect - every month the accounts were recorded, but next month was spent as usual.

She wasn't without thoughts of saving money, but once she tried to save, she felt uncomfortable, "To save a little money, lower the quality of life, is it worth it?" She said that sometimes when both of them were exhausted, to save money, they still went to the market to buy vegetables and cook at home, making the meal very frustrating. "Spending 20 yuan more, we can deal with a meal outside, it's easier." Also, "image expenses" cannot be reduced; wearing shabby clothes would make her colleagues laugh, so clothes that need to be bought must still be bought.

"Online accounting" easily done

With more young people keeping accounts, some people have taken it as a new "way to make money," establishing "online accounting" websites, such as China's most powerful online accounting website - Caike Online (www.coko365.com). Just register an account, and you can easily handle complicated income and expenditure details. These considerate accounting projects can not only record a family's accounts but also separately record the cash flow of each person, and provide financial budgeting and report analysis functions.

Some websites have also launched "SMS accounting," allowing expenses incurred while traveling to be sent to the website at any time, avoiding missed or incorrect records.

By reporter Jiang Yan and intern Cai Ziqi
