Franchising with Yumingzhai should maintain a good attitude.

by kai48nocp on 2010-06-01 11:24:42

In our development up to now, there are also some members who came in with great enthusiasm, thinking that they could make a lot of money tomorrow after entering. This kind of thought is undesirable, and everyone should not have such thoughts. The 598 yuan is equivalent to giving you the key to start this business, but it also requires your efforts every time you go online. There is no pie falling from the sky in any business, and all require certain efforts. Our business starts from the most difficult stage. Those who can persevere for about two months will find it relatively easier to develop in the future. This is a business that goes from hard to easy, so maintaining a good attitude is very important. Don't be discouraged because you just came in and don't understand anything, or because you haven't developed for several days. That's unnecessary. This itself is a part-time chat-based business, keep your attitude, practice more, fail more, all members have gone through this step by step. Don't be discouraged by others' success while you didn't succeed, and then say this business is what what what. That kind of thought is radical and bad. Because you should summarize more about where you didn't do enough or where you made mistakes, that's what you should care about!

Therefore, Yumingzhai hereby declares: Yumingzhai Network Entrepreneurship Platform is a platform that provides young people who want to start a business with choices of entrepreneurial projects and simple entrepreneurship opportunities. Yumingzhai advocates the principle of the more you work, the more you get, as long as you put in the effort, there will definitely be harvests!

Here, Yumingzhai also summarizes several key points for success in the Yumingzhai network entrepreneurship plan:

1. Be familiar with the materials! Be familiar with every corner of your website!

2. Communicate more with old members, and don't ask other new members questions! (Other new members may not know much more than you.)

3. Less talking, more practice! Accumulate experience in practice!

4. Don't complain that someone couldn't help you in time, this is your own business, you should work hard!

5. Maintain a stable mindset, this itself is a business done during spare time on the Internet!

6. When others refuse you, smile and start over!

7. When introducing to others, don't be impatient, introduce clearly and calmly!

8. Persistence is very important, persistence is the foundation of success!

9. Be full of confidence, you will definitely succeed!
