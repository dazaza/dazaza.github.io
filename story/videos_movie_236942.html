TV Series "Journey to the East 2"

From its start in late August last year until now, "Journey to the East 2" has traveled through six major scenes over 144 days, finally completing all the shoots on the eve of the Spring Festival in 2009 amidst the heavy snowfall in Shenyang. The final scene was given to Dong Xuan and Yu Yang. Dong Xuan told reporters, "The first scene when we started filming was me running in the fields with my elder sister, and unexpectedly, the last scene also turned out to be mine."

The final scene was filmed at Zhang Zuolin’s former residence, the Dashufu Mansion in Shenyang, amid falling snow. It narrates the story of Dong Xuan's character, the third sister Song Tianyue, whose husband Zhou Heguang becomes the deputy director of the Shenyang Public Security Bureau and encounters disputes with Russians while selecting properties... Director Hou Yong is someone who strives for perfection, often shooting one scene multiple times. Dong Xuan, dressed in a cheongsam, shivered in the cold snow. Anticipating standing in the snow for a long time, Dong Xuan had previously placed heat pads on her feet to temporarily ward off the cold.

For Dong Xuan, the last artist to finish filming, she expressed that no matter how tough or exhausting it was, she endured it all. Over 144 days, there were many unforgettable stories and colleagues in the production team, meeting hardworking opponents like Miao Pu and the most charitable Pu Cunxin.
