Online stores are not restricted by traditional business hours or locations. Operators can run the store full-time or part-time without needing to invest a lot of time in monitoring it.

How exactly do you open an online store?

There are countless items available online such as cosmetics, clothing, digital products... Each category has great potential for growth and success. The key is how to approach it, what suits you best. It’s important to understand the techniques involved in opening an online store. Below, I will guide you step-by-step on how to open an online store.

Step One: What to sell online?

Just like a traditional store, the first step in opening an online store is deciding what to sell. The choice of goods should be based on your interests, abilities, conditions, and the nature of the products, as well as consumer demand.

I personally love shoes and have been in this industry for many years, so naturally, I started selling shoes.

Step Two: Preparations before opening a store

After choosing what to sell, before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction safety, identity and payment method verification are also required.

I chose to open a store on Taobao because it is completely free. To open a store on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass certification; two, list 10 or more items (including 10); three, to facilitate safe transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click the "Free Registration" at the top of the page. In the opened page, input membership name, password, email, etc., then click the "Agree to the following service terms and submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation email. Open the link inside, confirm, and the user registration is completed.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction security, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols."

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, corresponding verifications (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification) must be done. Specific steps are as follows:

First Step: Log into Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to the text "Want to sell items? First, complete Alipay verification."

Second Step: On the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated. Click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the pop-up page, input real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., then click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Third Step: After successfully activating the Alipay account, go back to the original page, press F5 to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay personal real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, then click the "I have read" button to continue.

Fourth Step: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, select ID verification. You can choose "Online upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click "Next"; then, input bank card information including the issuing bank, bank card number, province, city, etc. After completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Reminder: If uploading ID copies online, the image file size should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide a picture of the back.

Fifth Step: After one day, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm deposit amount," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click the "Confirm" button.

3. Sourcing goods, taking photos

A critical factor in successfully opening an online store lies in the sourcing channel. For the same item, different sourcing channels result in different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To present the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use appropriate graphic image processing tools to convert the picture format and size, such as Photoshop, ACDSee, etc.

4. Listing Items

To open a store on Taobao, apart from meeting the certified member requirements, you also need to list over 10 items. Thus, after organizing the product information and images, you begin listing your first item.

Friendly Reminder: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can still list items, but they will only be listed under "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing the verification can you put them up for sale.

First Step: Log into Taobao, click "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. On the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" as the listing methods. Here, choose "Fixed Price."

Friendly Reminder: "Fixed Price" has a set price, allowing buyers to purchase immediately; "Auction" starts with no bottom price, letting buyers bid for purchases.

Second Step: Choose a category. Select an appropriate category based on your product. For example, I chose women's shoes details. Click the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Third Step: Fill in the item information. This step is very important. First, in the "Item Information" section, create a good title, click the "Browse" button to upload the item image, input the item description, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; next, in the "Transaction Conditions" section, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as using Alipay as the default payment method. Finally, click the "Confirm No Errors, Submit" button to list the item.

If successful, a success page will appear below. Clicking "Here" allows you to view the item's page, and clicking "Continue Listing Items" allows you to list more items.

Friendly Reminder: If buyers have not placed bids yet, you can modify the listed item information by going to "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Selling Items" to edit and modify. Wangdianle (www.wangdian6.com) teaches you how to open an online store.

After completing the item listing, it's best to update and add regularly to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Step Three: Obtain a Free Store

Taobao offers free store opening opportunities for certified members. As long as you list over 10 items, you can own a store with its independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

To get my store open as soon as possible, I initially listed 10 items, spending the entire half-day. After finishing, I eagerly opened "My Taobao - Free Store Opening." Next, in the opened page, I named my store "My Shoes, My Show;" in the "Store Category" section, I selected the category of my items; in the "Store Introduction" section, I input the store's brief introduction content. Finally, I clicked the "Submit" button, and on the appeared page, it showed "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." along with the store's address.

Step Four: Store Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, the buyer gets their own space. Just like a traditional store, to operate normally and attract customers, the store needs corresponding "decoration," mainly including store logo design, item categorization, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log into Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left "Store Management," click "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; the main business project needs manual input; in the "Logo" section, click the "Browse" button to choose the designed store logo image; in the "Announcement" section, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items makes it convenient for buyers to search. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click "Item Categories" on the left; then, input the new category name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), click the "Confirm" button to add. Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the category, and you can add the listed items through keyword searches for categorized management.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" feature provided by Taobao allows you to showcase your best six items prominently in the store. Just open the "Manage My Store" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left; then, in the opened page, select the recommended items, and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choosing a color scheme that is elegant and comfortable to look at works well. I chose the "Green Wild Fairyland" style template, which displays a preview on the right side. Click the "Confirm" button to apply this style.

After decorating the store, a brand-new page appears before you.

Step Five: Promotion is Key to Success

Your online store is open, items are listed, and features are added, but weeks have passed without any sales, not even any buyer messages. This is a common problem faced by many novice sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once encountered such troubles, so he decided to promote the store through forum advertising, link exchanges, window recommendations, and friend promotions.

1. Forum Advertising

The main method of forum advertising is through posting ads and using signature lines.

The former can be done on forums for various provinces or cities. If there is a section that allows ad postings, you can post ads. The content must be detailed, the product images must be exquisite, and regular updates and top placements are necessary.

The latter involves changing your signature line on forums to include your store's URL, logo, slogan, and store name. Post some beautiful threads to interest people who can visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to increase popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thereby promoting your store without spending money. For instance, Taobao provides up to 35 friendly links. The method is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to find all stores and note down the names of the popular store owners.

Next, log into http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download the Taobao buyer-seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and request a link exchange.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store," click "Friendly Links" on the left, then input the store owner name, and click the "Add" button.

3. Window Recommendations

The "Window Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers choose to search or click "I Want to Buy" according to categories, window-recommended items will appear on the search results page. To set up the "Window Recommendation" feature, you can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Selling Items," select the items you want to recommend to the window (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be recommended again, i.e., those marked with "Recommended"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

Step Six: After Sale of Items

After selling an item, in addition to receiving relevant sale reminders, you also need to proactively contact the buyer, asking the buyer to pay for the item, ship the item, and evaluate or complain after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller through "Taobao Wangwang," email, etc. Sellers can also log into Taobao, open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After contacting both parties, agree on payment methods and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both buyers and sellers, it is recommended to choose Alipay as the payment method.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where the goods arrive but payment is not made, sellers generally adopt the "Payment Received Before Shipping" method when selling items.

First, require the buyer to make payment, usually through Alipay. After making the payment, the seller can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Has Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the remittance, and at this point, the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Reminder: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao - Alipay Zone - Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction inquiries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm in the transaction status, and Taobao will then transfer the payment to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items again. 4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" to evaluate. Sellers can open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on the sold items, and choose good, neutral, or bad evaluations based on actual circumstances, and can also input text content.

Friendly Reminder: How to become a relatively high-credibility star-level store owner on Taobao?

To become a star-level store owner on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction. A "Good" review adds one point, "Neutral" does not add points, and "Bad" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star-level store owner, remember to provide honest service. If situations like not buying after agreeing online or not paying after receiving goods occur, sellers can open "My Taobao - Credit Management - Report" to file complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, and Wangwang screenshots need to be collected.

Although opening an online store is zero-cost and low-risk, examples of unsuccessful businesses leading to "closing shop" are also abundant. To let your small store survive online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the trust of buyers and gain good credit evaluations, allowing you to develop!
