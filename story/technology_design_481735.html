Zaozhuang City arranges and deploys the mechanized production work for the three summer seasons -- Industry Information -- China Agricultural Machinery

by maling29 on 2011-08-26 09:23:33

Recently, the Zaozhuang Municipal Agricultural Machinery Bureau issued the "Notice on Doing a Good Job in the Three Summers Agricultural Mechanization Production," making arrangements and deployments for this year's Three Summers agricultural mechanization production work.

The notice pointed out that doing a good job in the Three Summers agricultural mechanization production, ensuring that every grain of wheat is harvested and corn is sown in a timely manner, is of great significance for ensuring the city's food security, social stability, and thriving urban and rural economies. At all levels, we must stand at the height of political awareness, analyze the situation, enhance our understanding, strengthen our sense of responsibility, scientifically formulate Three Summers agricultural machinery operation plans and emergency response plans, focus on key areas, strengthen services, reasonably schedule machinery, strictly implement operations, and make active contributions to the abundant harvest of summer grains in the entire city.

The notice required that first, we should focus on key areas and fully engage in the battle for Three Summers agricultural mechanization production. We need to prepare all aspects of the Three Summers agricultural machinery work in advance. We should promote new technologies for Three Summers agricultural mechanization such as low-stubble wheat harvesting, straw chopping and returning to the field, and direct corn seeding. It is forbidden for harvesting machinery without installed straw chopping and returning to the field devices to enter the fields for operations. The wheat stubble height must be controlled below 10 centimeters, striving for the city's wheat straw return rate to reach over 97% and the direct corn seeding rate to reach over 85%. We need to strengthen the organization and scheduling of machinery, guiding the orderly flow of wheat harvesting machinery from within and outside the city, ensuring that wheat is harvested as soon as it matures. We need to increase efforts in attracting machinery, ensuring that contracted machinery from other regions arrives in Zaozhuang on time for wheat harvesting, and that machinery from Zaozhuang conducting cross-regional operations returns on time. All subsidized Three Summers machinery must be in place by the end of May to meet the needs of Zaozhuang's wheat rush harvest. We need to actively guide agricultural machinery social service organizations to conduct integrated operations such as wheat harvesting, straw returning to the field, and corn direct seeding, as well as order-based operations, contractual operations, and cross-regional operations, improving the scale level of Three Summers agricultural machinery operations and increasing the income of machine operators.

Secondly, we need to strengthen supervision and ensure the safety of Three Summers agricultural mechanization production. At all levels, safety work must run through the entire process of Three Summers agricultural mechanization production. We must conscientiously fulfill our duties, legally strengthen supervision, and ensure the safe production of agricultural machinery. We need to further implement the Three Summers agricultural machinery safety production responsibility system, strengthen agricultural machinery safety education, and strive to create a good atmosphere for Three Summers agricultural machinery safety production; we need to further improve emergency response plans for major agricultural machinery accidents, organize supervisory personnel to divide into groups and cover designated areas, going deep into the fields to investigate illegal behaviors such as blacklisted vehicles, non-licensed driving, and working under the influence of alcohol, avoiding major agricultural machinery safety accidents.

Thirdly, we need to closely cooperate and maintain the order of the agricultural machinery operation market. We need to strengthen cooperation with public security, highway departments, implement the free passage policy for cross-regional operation vehicles, and crack down on illegal activities such as forcibly seizing machinery, intercepting machinery, smashing machinery, and extorting money and goods from external machine operators according to law, maintaining the good order of our city’s cross-regional operation market. We need to provide good information services for Three Summers agricultural machinery operations, offering timely meteorological and mechanical demand operation information to machine operators; we need to collaborate with industry and commerce, quality inspection departments to crack down on the production and sale of counterfeit and substandard agricultural machinery products according to law; we need to communicate and collaborate with fuel supply departments, formulating emergency response plans for Three Summers fuel use early, opening green channels for agricultural machinery operation fuel, ensuring the timely supply of agricultural machinery operation fuel, and maximizing the satisfaction of Three Summers agricultural machinery operation needs.

The notice emphasized that we need to provide quality services and offer reliable organizational guarantees for Three Summers agricultural mechanization production. The Municipal Agricultural Machinery Bureau has already formed three working groups, which will go to various districts (cities) to inspect and guide Three Summers production; various districts (cities), towns also need to establish corresponding leadership or command organizations, deeply engaging in services such as operation machinery scheduling and technical guidance, striving to form an efficient and swift agricultural machinery production service network throughout the city. We need to set up service hotlines for Three Summers production, organizing agricultural machinery technicians to fully handle services such as guest machine reception, machinery coordination, machinery repair, and fuel supply, providing reliable logistical support for the vast number of machine operators; we need to continue providing Three Summers rush harvest operation services for households in difficulty and those who work outside, relieving their worries. We need to release relevant Three Summers production information early, guiding the orderly flow of operation machinery from within and outside the city; we need to implement 24-hour duty, promptly handling some emergencies during Three Summers production.

(Lulan)
