My Speech: "Charming Women" (April 22, 2007, 17:23:15)

Ladies and gentlemen, esteemed judges, and friends from all over, good evening. It's a great pleasure to be here at the Charming Women PK Competition. I am Bu Mei, the manager of the ESC brand. I was born in Yulin, Guangxi, a place known for its outstanding people and fertile land, and I currently work in Nanning, the Green City. Over the more than twenty years of my life, I have been a manager, an entrepreneur, and finally, a member of the beauty industry. I enjoy writing, music, and making new friends. I hope my speech will bring you some joy and unforgettable memories, and that this special evening will bridge the distance between us. (Friends, what are you waiting for? Please give me a warm round of applause!) (Thank you for your applause!)

What is charm? The standard dictionary definition is: "a very attractive quality." I believe charm is a composite beauty, a beauty achieved through effort and cultivation. It does not fade with age but becomes richer and more enduring with time, emitting an eternal fragrance that lasts as long as life itself. Only charm—not beauty or prettiness—is lasting and attainable by every woman.

1. A charming woman has grace. Graceful beauty lies in its naturalness. "A lotus emerging from clear water, naturally without decoration" is a traditional Chinese aesthetic viewpoint.

2. A charming woman has taste. A tasteful woman is sincere and calm, her demeanor composed, her character tranquil. She has a noble heart, is gentle and considerate, independent and confident.

3. A charming woman is creative. A creative woman owns her own world, full of rich and colorful interests, with eyes that discover beauty and a sensitive, rich soul. In a prosperous world, she stays far from vulgarity, boredom, and mediocrity.

4. A charming woman has thoughts. An independent woman with a distinct personality expresses her resilient inner self through sincerity, openness, and silence amidst the noise of the crowd.

5. A charming woman has a sense of responsibility. Facing the colorful world of material desires, she cares for the big picture, resists temptation, and firmly pursues her career while being a good daughter, wife, and mother.

6. A charming woman has love. With her vast love for family, colleagues, friends, and herself, her beauty is abundant and serene.

7. A charming woman has allure. Allure is a manner and sentiment, a unique landscape of women. Allure is not equivalent to sensuality or beauty; it is the essence hidden within a woman's nature. A charming woman is the one with the most beautiful inner landscape.

Today, I want to share with you a story about an experience any woman can relate to: "A Woman's Wardrobe." Xiao Yun is a beautiful young girl who loves pretty clothes. One day, she was getting ready to attend a banquet. She opened her wardrobe—filled with all sorts of clothes—but no matter how many she tried on, none seemed suitable. These were the same clothes she wore last year at the banquet, which had looked so striking then. Yet this year, they seem so different. Finding a suitable outfit takes so much effort. Faced with a full wardrobe, Xiao Yun doesn't know what to wear. In despair, she goes shopping, buys a bunch of clothes, and yet, the next day, finds herself again without anything to wear. This cycle repeats endlessly. Apparently, this girl was born to have a grudge against clothes. By now, everyone might be laughing. Yes, this woman lacks not clothes but confidence when facing others. She feels she lacks charm and thinks she can cover up her inner vanity with a wardrobe full of splendid clothes. (Friends, do you agree with my point of view? If you do, please show your appreciation with applause.) (Thank you for your support.)

If clothing can give a woman a beautiful appearance, then what gives a woman true charm? There was such a woman, a humble family teacher of modest birth and ordinary looks. Her clothing was "not even half as pretty as a servant's." Yet, this woman captivated the wealthy and intelligent Rochester, making him fall deeply in love with her. She is Jane Eyre, the protagonist of Charlotte Bronte's novel "Jane Eyre." This novel describes the extraordinary life experiences of an ordinary woman and a touching love story filled with twists and turns. Since the publication of "Jane Eyre," countless women around the world have found their own reflections in this plain-looking woman who endured hardships early in life but never lost the courage to fight. Undoubtedly, Jane Eyre portrayed a perfect image of a charming woman through her real-life experiences. Jane Eyre is a Cinderella figure, yet also a self-respecting, self-confident, self-reliant, and independent woman. Despite her suffering, she retained her pure nature. Though not beautiful, she was kind, simple, pure, and noble, with no trace of artificiality or hypocrisy. Jane Eyre shines brightly because of her extraordinary temperament, intelligence, noble character, rich emotional world, self-confidence, and personal charm. She is a truly charming woman.

I don't need to elaborate further on Jane Eyre's story; excessive words might dim the brilliance of this charming woman. Perhaps there are many women in this world just like Jane Eyre, including myself. I used to be an ordinary employee, without a prominent family background, star-like charisma, or a prestigious diploma. Nor did I have a nobleman to marry and change my fate. Should I, an unremarkable person, spend my entire life in this small position? No! Life is like a deck of poker cards; whether we can play out a handsome ending depends entirely on ourselves. This is how I played my first hand in life. When I entered the beauty industry, following trends and exploring its depths, I realized I had stepped to the forefront of the times, rewriting the chapter of my life starting right now. I firmly believe that the brilliance of my life will never pale compared to my name. I trust myself; a confident life is beautiful, and self-confidence is an indispensable prerequisite for a charming woman. (Do you agree?)

Dear female friends, if I can believe in my ability to be a charming woman, why can't you? Remember, to be a charming woman every day, you must learn:

First, to be truly confident and self-respecting. Every woman, regardless of age, if you are confident and poised, you will win the world.

Second, to achieve true physical and mental health, especially mental well-being.

Third, to be a wise woman. A charming woman needs not only beauty but also a soul; otherwise, she becomes merely a vase.

Fourth, to let love nourish yourself. Love makes women feel romantic happiness and enhances their femininity.

Fifth, to be an independent woman. Independence is a high realm requiring a high-quality mindset and new values.

Sixth, to cultivate a unique aura. The charm and sophistication exuded by a woman make her stand out.

Women friends, let us always remember this special night and cheer for all our charming women and those who will become charming women. (My presentation ends here. Thank you, judges, teachers, and all friends who care and support me. Thank you everyone!)
