How to Place Friend Links in WordPress

by jimwang on 2010-09-21 17:32:10

Exchanging links helps with interaction among blog friends, which is a good thing! However, when the number of your friend links exceeds a certain limit, problems arise. At this point, we can't place every person's link on the homepage due to limited space! Therefore, creating a page where all the links from friends who exchange links with us are placed becomes quite important.

After checking an article by "I Love Water Boiled Fish," I found a method that I would now like to share with friends who have many links:

Firstly, you can randomly display 30 links in the sidebar. The code for this is as follows:

In our case, setting the `category` parameter to `-1` will display all links from all categories. The most crucial part is setting the `order` parameter to `rand`, meaning the links will be displayed in random order. Then, we set `limit` to `30`, but you can adjust this number to display a different quantity of links. Other parameters are relatively straightforward and won’t be explained here. For more details, please refer to TEMPLATE TAG/GET_LISTS.
