Four-day Tour Essentials from Zhuzhou to Huangshan

by jj0505 on 2012-03-07 14:46:35

Day 1: Accommodation: Train Meal: /

Take the K222 train at 17:23 (or K156 at 18:11) from Zhuzhou. The train is a hard sleeper (check train schedules from Zhuzhou to Huangshan) and travels to Tunxi, which is the seat of the Huangshan municipal government. Enjoy the beautiful scenery of the fields in southern Jiangnan along the way.

Day 2: Accommodation: Huangshan Meal: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

Arrive in Tunxi at around 03:48 (K156 arrives at 04:08). After breakfast, take a car to Mount Huangshan (about 1.5 hours by car, 1 day for sightseeing), known as "after returning from Huangshan, no need to look at other mountains". It is famous worldwide for its four wonders - sea of clouds, strange pines, unique rocks, and hot springs. Walk up the mountain (cable car costs extra), visit Shixin Peak, Beihai, see Feilai Stone, Guangming Summit, Lotus Peak, Aoyu Peak, Yuping Building, and the Welcoming Pine tree. Walk down the mountain (cable car costs extra). Return to the city by car, visit the Anhui South Wildlife Park, watch a Huangshan tea art performance, and tour the millennium-old "living version of Qingming Riverside Scene" in Songcheng Old Street.

Day 3: Accommodation: Train Meal: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner

After breakfast, take a car to Yixian County, known as the "people's home in the Peach Blossom Spring." Visit the ancient Huizhou village of Hongcun (tour time about 90 minutes), which was listed on the World Heritage List by UNESCO in November 2000. Known as the "Chinese painting-like countryside," it is a bionic architecture with a bull-shaped water system and was the location for filming scenes of the movie "Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon." Then take a car to Xidi, a world cultural heritage site and a marvel among ancient residences in the world (tour time about 90 minutes). The entire village is boat-shaped, preserving 122 complete ancient residences, known as the "miniature of Chinese traditional culture" and "Chinese Ming-Qing residential museum." Return to the city by car and take the K221 train at 23:44 (or K155 at 23:08) back to Zhuzhou (check train schedules from Huangshan to Zhuzhou).

Day 4: Accommodation: / Meal: /

Arrive in Zhuzhou at around 10:33 (K155 arrives at 09:14), concluding the pleasant trip from Zhuzhou to Huangshan, including the four-day journey through Huangshan, Xidi, and Hongcun by train!
