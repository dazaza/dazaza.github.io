"20 Something Decides a Man's Life" is for men in their twenties and the women behind them.

by jqrxass on 2009-09-20 13:33:09

"20s Decides a Man's Life" is a book written for men in their 20s, those about to enter their 20s, and the women behind them. The 20s is a critical period for a man with many factors that determine his future destiny, such as: "Which of the 360 trades should I enter? Which company, big or small, should I join? It's said that 'a man should be established by 30,' how can I complete my accumulation before 30 in these ten years and lay a solid foundation for future success..."

"20s Decides a Man's Life" starts from the problems encountered by men in their 20s during various stages of growth, gradually pointing out the direction and path for men in their 20s who are still in a state of confusion...

Chapter Six: Complete Accumulation Before 30 and Prepare to Be a Successful Man

The 20s is the time when a man sheds his naivety and moves towards maturity; it's an age full of urgency. If a man achieves nothing by 30, people will no longer be as tolerant and understanding as they were when he was in his 20s. The 20s is also the time to prepare for a happy family and remarkable achievements...

In your 20s, you need to prepare to be a successful man!

Chapter Seven: In Our 20s, What Do We Use to Open Up a New Life?

In our 20s, we have passion, dreams, energy, and intelligence, but we lack background, experience, money, and platforms. With what do we win success? There are things to think about, things to confront, things to try, and things to avoid... Are you clear on all these? Men, the earlier you wake up, the sooner you'll succeed!

Chapter Five: Encountering Bottleneck Period, Should We Start a Business or Continue Working?

Zhang Ailing said, "One should become famous early." The same goes for getting rich. Recently, there's a popular saying: Before 29, it's within the "youth warranty period," after 29, it's Time out (expired). Therefore, if one wants to break through and start a career during their youth, it's best to do so before the "warranty" expires. It's said that the vast majority of billionaires had already completed their initial wealth accumulation before 30. In other words, if one hasn't found their direction by 30, they might likely miss out on prosperity for the rest of their life.
