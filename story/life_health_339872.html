According to the above beauty index, please look into the mirror immediately: what's your current beauty index?

If the answer is below 35, it indicates that your current figure is really not commendable, and you need to take immediate action!

If the answer is between 80-95, you can wear anything effortlessly. But be careful! A good figure needs effort to maintain; otherwise, you might fall into the previous two categories!

If the answer is above 95, you belong to the naturally beautiful category. However, to truly exude the elegance of a supermodel, a refined demeanor is even more crucial!
