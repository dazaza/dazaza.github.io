Academician Guo Yinglu successfully helped an intersex person.

by slznlx1q on 2010-04-19 11:20:41

The Urology Center of Beijing Military Region General Hospital has developed into a model department with a certain influence domestically, and has formed a certain advantage in the diagnosis and treatment of adrenal diseases, urinary system tumors, urinary calculi, prostate diseases, urinary reproductive infections, male diseases, and kidney transplantation. As the new generation academic leader of urology and andrology in our country, Academician Guo Yuelu, the head of the urology expert group of Beijing Military Region General Hospital, recently successfully helped a pseudohermaphrodite patient to get rid of the embarrassment of being unable to urinate upright for 39 years.

Mr. Wang, a nearly 40-year-old patient from Hulunbuir City, Inner Mongolia, was born with an indistinguishable external genitalia between male and female, clinically diagnosed as pseudohermaphroditism. Due to the limitation of medical level and family conditions, he had been unable to receive treatment. For more than 30 years, the shadow of "intersex" has always troubled him, making it impossible for him to live confidently. But the strong desire to "be a normal person and build a happy family" has always supported him, making him live on strongly until he met Academician Guo Yuelu of Beijing Military Region General Hospital...

Under the help of Academician Guo at the end of 2009, Mr. Wang successfully completed a series of surgical treatments, including: first-stage urethroplasty type I, II stage surgery, *** elongation surgery, buccal mucosa urethroplasty, as well as scrotum formation + cryptorchidism descent fixation surgery, etc. Currently, the patient's physical recovery is good, and it is believed that in the near future, Mr. Wang will definitely realize his dream and live a happy and confident life. The patient and his family sincerely thank Academician Guo for bringing health blessings to their family.
