Boracay: A Landing Without a Dock Boracay is most famous for its 7-kilometer stretch of white sand beach. When tourists arrive by boat and dock at the island's edge, they will notice a peculiar phenomenon - there are no docks on the island! Tourists have to disembark in shallow water about thirty to forty meters offshore. They take off their shoes, roll up their pants, carry their luggage, and wade ashore like the local boatmen. This method of landing is unique and unforgettable. There are countless activities to enjoy on Boracay, such as island-hopping from one small island to another. The shells on the island are renowned for being white, transparent, and beautifully shaped. The white ones resemble jasmine flowers, while the green ones look like wild fruits - fashionable yet simple. Travel buddy "DODO": After the direct flight opens in July, the cost of traveling to Boracay will be less than one-third of that of the Maldives, yet the seawater and water quality are just as impressive. The Regency Beach Resort (Boracay Regency Beach Resort) is an excellent choice. Aside from the Shangri-La Hotel, the Regency is considered one of the better resorts on Boracay. It is located in the center of Boat Station 2, and it was the first hotel to receive a three-star rating from the Philippine Tourism Authority for its location along the white sandy beach. The sea-view balcony offers stunning views of the white sandy beach scenery. Travel agency quote: 4 nights and 6 days stay at a 3A resort for 4880 yuan Samui: Welcome to the Pure Paradise Samui, Thailand’s third-largest island, has a shape resembling a pebble. The island boasts numerous beaches, with crystal-clear water and pristine white sands, offering captivating views. The liveliest and longest beaches are Chaweng and Lamai, both located on the eastern side of the island. Chaweng Beach stretches for 6 kilometers, forming a crescent moon-shaped beach surrounded by lush green hills and reefs, providing a very picturesque environment. It is also the area with the most hotels and entertainment facilities. The famous marine ecological reserve of Samui - Ang Thong Marine National Park - consists of more than 40 beautiful islands, featuring colorful corals and playful tropical fish. Some islands even have deep caves. You can first visit the lake on the Mother Island mountain, climb up the stone steps to the top, enjoy the gentle breeze, and marvel at the extraordinary view of the blue freshwater lake in the mountains. Another well-known attraction includes snorkeling or swimming at Koh Nang Yuan (Cow Island). Alternatively, you can choose to do nothing and simply relax on the beach, enjoying the sea breeze and scenic views. Travel buddy "Xiaoli Luanfeidao": If you're going to Samui for a vacation, don't miss out on experiencing Thai SPA and traditional massage. You can pick up a copy of the Samui Health & SPA Guide at the hotel reception, which provides detailed information about various SPA shops on the island. Thai SPAs use natural ingredients such as rosemary, ylang-ylang, lavender, mint, eucalyptus, lemon, sweet orange, and more, offering over ten types of aromatic essential oils to choose from. Travel agency quote: 4 nights and 6 days pure play in Samui for 5880 yuan In recent years, overseas island tours have become the preferred choice for many Wenzhou residents for vacations and honeymoons. Summer is all about revelry - sipping rum by the beautiful beach, sprinting towards the pristine white sand, and the azure sea beyond. The universe holds great beauty without words; different regions create mesmerizing landscapes. Unique islands await you in the warm summer breeze, ready for you to explore with a heart yearning for freedom.
