Regarding doing foreign trade, I still only have a superficial understanding - In this situation, it seems like your wings aren't strong enough yet, so I don’t recommend going solo, or you will end up losing money.

Conditions: Stable product sources and a stable, solid customer base. Since you're going solo without the company's financial chain to support you, your relationship with the source factory must be stable. Try to avoid situations where not paying a deposit or delaying payment by a few days results in a supply cut-off, otherwise, you'll be dragged down. Additionally, your relationship with customers must also be solid. The credibility of both you and your customers is extremely important. If your customer changes, you may face the problem of shipping goods but not receiving payment.

2. Are there any good websites similar to China's "Taobao" where you can sell products in English? - It seems you're not very professional. Taobao is a domestic retail product online platform. If you want to go international, you should look at Alibaba, which is Taobao's parent company.

3. Regarding unfamiliarity with the entire work process of SOHO, could someone provide a detailed explanation? Thanks. - It's quite simple: Find your own product sources and customers, find an export agency company. Sign a contract with the export company, negotiate settlement times, settlement rates, agency fees, and whether they handle necessary documents for you. Then, negotiate contracts with your customers and place orders with the factory. You are responsible for managing the factory's production progress and product quality issues. Once the goods are ready, under the customer's permission, the agency company will arrange customs clearance and shipping. The customer’s payment will be sent to the agency company, which will deduct its agency fee and then pay you according to the agreed time. The factory's VAT invoice is issued to the agency company, which will pay the factory after receiving the payment from your overseas customer. If prepayment is required, you need to discuss this beforehand with the agency company. Generally speaking, agency companies do not advance payments.

Requirements: Knowing English, having perseverance, being low-key (SOHO is illegal, and if found, fines will be imposed).
