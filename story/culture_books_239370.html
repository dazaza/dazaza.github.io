"Turning the Corner to Meet Happiness" - A Mental Exercise for Urban Men and Women

by jqrxass on 2009-09-17 14:57:51

"Turning the Corner to Meet Happiness" is the author's new examination of mundane life through insights into life and nature. The author conducts an empathetic and rational analysis of examples from daily life, gradually revealing the secrets of happiness. The author posits that happiness is a holistic and indivisible concept, and a happy life consists of four main components: love, career, health, and spirituality. The author elaborates on the secrets of how these four aspects can harmoniously progress and develop in balance. With profound philosophical thought and a relaxed, lively sense of literary beauty, this work serves as both mental exercise and spiritual nourishment for modern men and women in urban settings, as well as a guide for work and life.

"Turning the Corner to Meet Happiness" Related Content

Happiness is a physical and psychological feeling of joy where your body and mind feel comfortable and free. You have escaped the constraints of needs and are carefree. Happiness is more defined by its state, something that comes unexpectedly and cannot be pursued. Happiness is the moment you walk with your beloved; it’s escaping the flow of city cars and breathing in the fresh air of the forest; it’s spending time by the sea, basking in the sun; it’s the smile in your sleep; it’s being fully immersed in watching a movie. Happiness is the sensation of relaxation, an experience of comfort. It is the natural expression and manifestation of inner surges. If you pursue it, it may vanish without a trace.

Chapter 1: The Secret of Happiness

Though happiness may be a secret, the moments and scenes of happiness in life can be felt by everyone: after dinner, walking hand in hand with your girlfriend under the shade, telling unrelated jokes; at dusk, the afterglow of the sunset falls on the silvery hair of the elderly, two old people supporting each other as they return from buying groceries, time witnessing their half-century-long love – holding your hand, growing old together; a girl in love, reminiscing about her boyfriend's mischievous actions and appearance on her way home from a date, her laughter rising from her heart to her face, quickly restraining herself when she sees passersby; hearing a child babble "mommy" and "daddy" for the first time...

Chapter 2: Love and Happiness

Nature created humans, initially isolating men and women separately, allowing them to begin appreciating and admiring each other, and becoming attracted to one another. At this point, the seeds of love quietly sprout and grow in the hearts of men and women.

Men exert their charm and make efforts to court, while women move from evasion, refusal, and shyness to gradually accepting and liking them. They fall in love, both spirit and body participating, driving away loneliness and fear. In their mutually pleasurable love, they experience deep intimacy and sincere friendship! And this, is the most beautiful thing life can offer us.

Chapter 3: Work and Happiness

The years immediately following graduation from school are generally considered some of the happiest times in life. A world completely different from student life unfolds before one’s eyes. Although the income may not be high, there is no direct pressure from family or finances, and the disposable income available for socializing and enjoyment is relatively higher. Meanwhile, the drive for innovation and change in work is also at its peak. Apart from a few who cannot find satisfactory jobs and thus blame themselves, the majority of graduates become experienced, resourceful, and capable professionals after a few years of struggle.
