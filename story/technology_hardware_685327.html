The captain who died in rescuing a female passenger jumping into the river was posthumously recognized as a martyr

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-01 14:42:57

Share to: Comments welcome I want to comment. Relevant departments in Foshan city believe that Li Fugen, in order to rescue the drowning woman, disregarded his own safety in a critical moment, stepped forward bravely and sacrificed himself to save others. His heroic act of righteousness stands out and can serve as a model for future generations.

Li Fugen's heroic deeds were widely praised by the public, who affectionately called him "Uncle Fu". Various commemorative activities followed. When Foshan Party Secretary Li Yiwei met with Uncle Fu's family, he stated that Uncle Fu's spirit demonstrated that "Foshan is not cold, Foshan has great love." Afterwards, due to custody disputes, an Italian man threw his child into the river. The Sanshui District Committee and District Government, along with the Foshan Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government, successively awarded Li Fugen and Lu Weiliang, who also participated in the rescue, the title of "Righteousness and Bravery," rewarding and compensating both individuals. According to the application made by the Lubei Town Government to recognize Li Fugen as a martyr, the application was progressively reported to the civil affairs departments of Foshan City and Guangdong Province.

Our reporter in Foshan learned yesterday from relevant departments that the Guangdong Provincial Civil Affairs Department has formally approved and agreed to posthumously recognize Li Fugen, the "heroic captain" who sacrificed his precious life heroically saving others, as a martyr. The Sanshui District Committee and District Government have decided to hold a memorial service at 1 p.m. tomorrow afternoon at the Sanshui District Funeral Home.

Relevant personnel from the Sanshui District Committee and District Government expressed that the two rescuers are the most ordinary citizens of Sanshui, reflecting the most simple nature of the people of Sanshui and demonstrating the spirit of the people of Sanshui. This spirit needs to be vigorously promoted. Next, they will focus on advocating the spirit of Uncle Fu and provide care and assistance to Uncle Fu's family.

At around 1 p.m. on November 15, Captain Li Fugen of the Lubei Town Inland Transport Company was ferrying passengers across the river on the Beijiang River section near Lubei. As the ferry reached deep water in the middle of the Beijiang River, Li Fugen noticed a female passenger with a child suddenly jump into the river with her child. He immediately jumped into the river to save them. During the rescue process, the female passenger continuously pushed away Li Fugen, refusing to be saved, but Li Fugen persisted without giving up. Eventually, due to exhaustion and depletion of strength, he sank into the river and unfortunately lost his life.

Yesterday, our reporter learned from the Publicity Department of the Sanshui District Committee that after research, the Guangdong Provincial Civil Affairs Department agreed to posthumously recognize Comrade Li Fugen as a martyr.
