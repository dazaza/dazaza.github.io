**Search Term Research**

*Published: October 2nd, 2009*

Search term research is the process of selecting relevant keywords to target what people are using in a search within Google and other search engines. Targeting these keywords can drive traffic to your web pages via natural (Search Engine Optimization) and paid search engine traffic.

**Why Search Term Research?**

Search term research lays the foundation for your search marketing campaign. Build the search marketing campaign around your keywords, and you're guaranteed success! However, remember that it's not about the keywords you want to be found for but the keywords your audience is using to locate the products and services you offer.
