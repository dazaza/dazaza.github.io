With the recent release of the film "Mulan," rumors about Zhao Wei's marriage have been rampant. Yesterday, Hong Kong's Next Magazine even reported that the 33-year-old Zhao Wei is five months pregnant and currently in Singapore awaiting childbirth. When contacted by reporters, Zhao Wei's agent Chen Rong's phone went unanswered, though the ringtone suggested her phone was on international roaming, possibly indicating she might be in Singapore. A close friend of Zhao Wei revealed to reporters that Zhao Wei is indeed dating the wealthy businessman Huang Youlong, but declined to confirm whether they are married. When asked about Zhao Wei’s pregnancy, the friend exclaimed in "astonishment": "How did I not know! She's so good at keeping secrets!"

### Rumors of Waiting Out Her Pregnancy in Singapore

According to Hong Kong media reports, Zhao Wei's last public appearance was on the 20th of last month at the premiere of "Mulan" in Guangzhou. She wore a black dress, but due to its loose style, it sparked suspicions about her being pregnant, polo shirts. The report claimed that Zhao Wei had gained over 10 kilograms due to pregnancy, and that night she deliberately dressed this way, covering her belly with her hands throughout the event. She also needed both hands to support herself when getting up from her seat. Hong Kong media cited insider information that someone saw Zhao Wei wearing a hat accompanied by friends for a physical examination at Beijing Union Medical College Hospital last month. Zhao Wei also urgently halted her ongoing work, implementing a complete shutdown. Originally, she had agreed to represent the movie "Mulan" for a promotional event on Hunan TV's "Happy Camp" on the 14th of last month, but she declined citing illness. This month, she has also politely rejected several commercial activities. Hong Kong media reported that Zhao Wei flew to Singapore on the 27th of last month to reunite with Huang Youlong, who specifically rented out a cinema on Orchard Road to watch "Mulan."

### Friend Claims She Has Quit Drinking and Smoking

This isn't the first time Zhao Wei has been rumored to be pregnant. In 2007, there were rumors that she was dating Wang Litiu. At the time, during her performance on "Happy China Tour," she wore clothes that concealed her waistline, leading to rumors of pregnancy. Last year, Zhao Wei was secretly photographed singing karaoke late at night. She was wearing loose clothing and placing her hand on her waist, once again sparking rumors of pregnancy. However, at that time, people were discussing who the father of Zhao Wei's child might be. This time around, the rumor has escalated to her waiting out her pregnancy in Singapore. A close friend of Zhao Wei confirmed to reporters that she is indeed dating Huang Youlong, but when asked if they are already married, Zhao Wei's friend said they were unclear. Regarding whether Zhao Wei is five months pregnant, the friend directly stated it was impossible: "Huang Youlong wanted Zhao Wei to have his child shortly after they started dating. He said he would marry her immediately once they had a child, so Zhao Wei began quitting smoking and drinking after being with Huang Youlong. When friends offered her cigarettes, she said she needed to take care of her body for having children, Nike Shox. But it's surprising how quickly things happened."

### Hong Kong Media Reports Her Wealthy Boyfriend Is Worth 400 Million

According to Hong Kong magazines, Huang Youlong, in his thirties, is quite influential. He mainly engages in real estate business on the mainland, with branches in Guangzhou, Beijing, Shanghai, and Yunnan. He owns the "Jin Yezi Hot Spring Resort Hotel" in Guangzhou (now renamed as Sanzheng Banzhan Hot Spring Resort Hotel) and even has a private plane. His estimated net worth is around 400 million yuan. There are rumors that Huang Youlong has a 9-year-old son and was once in a relationship with former Miss Hong Kong contestant Ye Cuicui, almost reaching the point of marriage.

Hong Kong media reported that Huang Youlong spent over 20 million yuan to win Zhao Wei's heart, including gifting her 10 Hermes handbags, a VERTU diamond phone, diamond watches, and luxury cars. Witnesses claimed that Zhao Wei and Huang Youlong romantically toured Paris together when she attended the Cannes Film Festival in May this year. Moreover, Huang Youlong invested money into Zhao Wei's film and television company, which reportedly has already been established in Beijing. It is rumored that Zhao Wei plans to produce a sister version of her breakout hit "The Story of a Concubine" and become a film producer herself. There are also rumors that upon learning of Zhao Wei's pregnancy, Huang Youlong gifted her "prizes" worth over 100 million yuan, including investing millions to open a new restaurant in Beijing and purchasing two luxurious homes each valued at over 10 million yuan on the mainland.
