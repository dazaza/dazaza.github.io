TOP19. Stellar Transformation: As is well known, Tangsijixia is famous for his clever plot conception. Even though "Stellar Transformation" has a clichéd plot, its perfect conception enlivens the entire text, making it irresistible to keep reading the next chapter. As the top-clicked novel on Baidu in 2008, the strength of "Stellar Transformation" is beyond doubt. Its flaw is its simplicity and lack of depth, but as online literature, it is originally meant for entertainment, which is understandable.

TOP17. A Time of Contempt: This time, Cat Nip has chosen a pretty decent title for his book, at least giving a sense of elegance. This is a masterpiece that blends martial arts, military formations, and strategies. Though it doesn't reach a level of profound thought, Cat Nip's novels tend to have a slightly higher degree of intentionality among first-class works, but flaws do not overshadow the virtues, and this remains a complex, unconventional masterpiece. Cat Nip's writing is good, but still lags behind Jiangnan and Xiaodun. However, the readability of Cat Nip's works stands out among online novels, reading smoothly and comfortably. Even if your mood was heavy, this novel can quickly capture your attention and make you forget your troubles, allowing you to comfortably continue reading. That’s enough.

TOP16. Battle Through the Heavens: The article is excellent, starting with a refreshing feel, delivering a clear and pure water-like experience during the scarce period of good works in 2009-2010. The quality gradually declines towards the end, possibly due to dragging. The author's integrity requires no explanation; if judged by integrity, this book wouldn’t even rank in the top 50. Setting aside other factors, overall, the merits of this novel are noteworthy.

TOP15. Peerless Young Master + Lifetime Hegemon: You've never seen such a phenomenon where a book that hasn't been updated for three years still has thousands of readers waiting for updates. Yes, Peerless Young Master created such a myth. As the second-highest clicked book on Qidian for two consecutive years in 2006-2007, Peerless Young Master has borne a lot of criticism and praise. But there's no denying that the later parts of Peerless Young Master and Lifetime Hegemon are indeed top-notch works; the writing, plot, and details are well done. The gentle nature of Xueheng, the stubbornness of Shi Yun, and each vivid character make it reasonable for this novel to rank 15th. The only regret is that it was left unfinished.
