Bio-Alcohol Oil Heat Stability Enhancer 2010-01-28 16:46 Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer is a high-intensity catalyst and multi-functional additive. Adding 0.3%-0.5% in the existing Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel can effectively increase the heat value of Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel. It enhances its stability and reduces volatility, thereby simplifying and securing the production process while significantly reducing costs, but greatly increasing firepower. The functions of Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer include: 1) Increase the heat of Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel; adding 0.3%-0.5% in the existing Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel can significantly increase its heat value; adding 0.3%-0.5% in methanol and then mixing with water in a certain proportion after thorough stirring produces Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel with very high heat value, which can be widely used as combustion oil in factories, school canteens, hotels, boilers, etc. 2) Reduce the activity of methanol molecules, enhance methanol stability, and reduce volatility. We all know that methanol is a highly volatile flammable liquid that can turn into flammable methanol vapor at around 64 degrees Celsius. If not controlled, it can be very harmful and easily wasted. One of the functions of Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer is to reduce the activity of methanol molecules, enhance methanol stability, and reduce volatility, making the production, transportation, and storage processes more stable and safer. At the same time, it significantly reduces the amount used during combustion, which is why Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel burns longer than other alcohol-based fuels on the market. 3) Change the traditional emission method by converting harmful components in exhaust gases into soluble solid residues deposited in the furnace. The combustion of any fuel produces harmful gases such as sulfur dioxide and carbon monoxide. Methanol, especially raw methanol, waste methanol, and recovered methanol, contains many chemical substances harmful to human health or the environment. Gas emissions are very detrimental to both human health and the environment. Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer converts harmful components in exhaust gases into soluble solid residues deposited in the furnace. During daytime use, the temperature in the furnace can reach several hundred degrees Celsius, but at night when the temperature drops to room temperature, a large amount of water vapor forms in the furnace. These soluble solids dissolve in the water vapor and are absorbed by the dry environment inside the furnace, which is why there is a small amount of white substance left after use, and this white substance disappears in the morning, leaving water droplets on the inner walls of the burner. 4) Fully decompose methanol molecules and water molecules to ensure complete combustion. Methanol combustion produces a heat value of approximately 5300 kcal, which is insufficient for cooking temperatures and heat. We need to add high-heat-value additives to address this issue. However, these high-heat-value additives are quite expensive. For example, acetone or other ether-based chemical products have relatively high heat values, but their prices are around 15,000 yuan per ton. This would make the cost of producing Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel similar to diesel, failing to save money and making promotion difficult. Therefore, we must solve the problem from the inherent nature of the fuel. Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer can release oxygen molecules from methanol, ensuring full combustion of methanol in an oxygen-rich environment. Simultaneously, it can decompose water molecules in methanol into hydrogen and oxygen, resulting in more complete combustion, thus enhancing the heat value and firepower of the fuel. This is why adding just a little bit of Chuan Island Alcohol-Based Fuel Heat Stability Enhancer to methanol or commercially available Chuan Island alcohol-based fuel immediately changes the flame color to a red diesel-like hue, increases the flame height by one-third, and extends the burning time, as observed by Wuhan Chuan Island Qian Neng Technology Development Co., Ltd.
