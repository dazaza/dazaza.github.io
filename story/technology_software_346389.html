Driving School Exam Secrets: Pass without studying the book!! Even if you're not taking the exam now, keep these tips handy - don't wait until you're in a rush and can't find them!!! Quick memorization methods!

1. If the question contains "mouth" choose 50 meters, if it contains "station" choose 30 meters (called "mouth five station three"). For the rest, select 150 meters, or if there's no option for 150, choose the largest number. All true/false questions are correct.

2. For questions involving kilometers: Choose 50 km/h for city streets, and 30 km/h for all others. The highest speed on highways is 70 km/h, and the lowest is 60 km/h. All true/false questions are correct.

3. Revocation of driver's license results in a two-year ban, cancellation results in a three-year ban. Drunk driving revocation leads to a five-year ban, fleeing after an accident results in a lifetime ban (called "revoked for two years, canceled for three years, drunk for five years, flee for life").

4. First aid knowledge: In multiple-choice questions, choose A, B, C, or the option with the most words. In true/false questions, only "distal end" and "soft stretcher" are incorrect; the rest are correct.

5. Hazard knowledge: If the question includes "not necessary", "unaffected", "can", "three layers", or "strong and undamaged", these are incorrect; the rest are correct.

6. Points deduction questions: Deduct 1 point for "no documents". True/false questions: those involving "lights" and "reverse" are correct; the rest are wrong. Deduct 2 points for overtaking on a ramp (highway), less than 20%, using a mobile phone, violating signs, driving over four hours, no centerline, during probation period, and at intersections. True/false questions: "not maintaining a safe distance from the car in front" is correct; the rest are wrong. Deduct 3 points for lights, traffic lights, license plates, highway, and trailers. All true/false questions are wrong. Deduct 6 points for drinking, exceeding 50%, exceeding 30%, learning to drive (on highways), and improper parking (on highways). All true/false questions are correct. Deduct 12 points for drunk driving, driving without a license, fleeing, borrowing a license, unpaid fines over three months, driving an unsuitable vehicle, and forcing through. All true/false questions are correct.

7. Fine questions: If "2000" appears, choose "2000"; if not, choose "500". For amounts over "2000" and without "1000", choose the maximum fine. All true/false questions about fine amounts are correct.

8. Questions involving days: Remember that mediation selects 10 days, otherwise select 15 days if available, or 3 or 90 if not.

9. Questions involving height: If "container" appears, choose the maximum; otherwise, choose the smaller option.

10. Regarding functions: Any question with "change", "leave", "fuel", "travel" after the four characters are wrong; the rest are correct.

Driving mnemonic: As long as you remember the key actions, write down this driving mnemonic.

- Shout "report" when getting in the car!

- Pull the door with your left foot, sit well and greet the examiner with a smile.

- Close the door and take a deep breath, adjust your posture.

- Check the neutral gear position, start the engine gently.

- Don’t forget the steering wheel, shift gears decisively, release the handbrake.

- Ask the examiner before starting, check the rearview mirrors, honk the horn.

- Release the clutch smoothly, ensure half-clutch engagement, start steadily so the examiner doesn’t shake!

- Change gears promptly, accelerate quickly, brake when encountering people, but don't overdo it.

- Slow down when turning, reduce gears immediately, check frequently and pause often, keep safety in mind.

- Relax your shoulders, place both hands correctly, maintain a natural demeanor, don’t panic because of the examiner.

- Be confident, don’t fear difficulties, be bold yet meticulous, and don’t forget courtesy.

- As long as you remember, you will surely pass the exam!

Driving Test Secrets for Small Road Tests: Large and small road tests (choose six out of nine) test skills, precautions, and secrets

Procedure Steps:

1. Adjust the seat

2. Inspect the left and right mirrors and the rearview mirror, inspect the handbrake lever and gearshift position

3. Fasten the seatbelt

4. Hold the steering wheel with your left hand, place your right hand near the ignition switch

5. Press the clutch pedal with your left foot – disengage the clutch

6. Gently press the brake pedal with your right foot

7. Place the gearshift in neutral

8. Turn on the ignition, inspect the dashboard

9. After confirming no abnormalities, use your right hand to place the gearshift into first gear

10. Turn on the left turn signal with your left hand

11. Observe the surroundings and the status of the mirrors

12. Start with the three-link method

Note: Three-link refers to the coordination of the throttle, clutch, and handbrake.

Specific operation: Release the clutch to the contact point, slightly increase the throttle, and simultaneously release the handbrake gently.
