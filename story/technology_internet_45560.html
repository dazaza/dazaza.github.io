What is the reason for this phenomenon? SWJ personally hypothesizes:

1. Could it be a temporary instability in Baidu's data? Because no software in the world is perfect or flaw-free. SWJ himself has a background in software development and understands a bit. According to SWJ's observation and analysis, Baidu's general pages, such as the homepage and other pages, are built using PHP language, while the true "core" is written in JAVA.

2. Has Baidu Promotion become more humanized?

Assumption: When there are fewer promotions on Baidu, such as only one or two promotions, they ensure placement on the first page but do not guarantee which line it will appear on.

The benefits of this approach include the following aspects:

a. From the user's perspective (including us individual webmasters):

User experience improves significantly, no longer being the common phrase "Baidu, you're garbage!" but changing to "Baidu, I love you without reason!"

b. From the advertiser's perspective (also including us individual webmasters):

A more humanized method is used to satisfy both users and better meet clients' needs and "value." In situations where there are fewer promotions on the first page, if you do promotion, we at Baidu guarantee to place it on the first page but do not guarantee which line it will appear on. This avoids the previous situation where one promotion could dominate the rankings.
