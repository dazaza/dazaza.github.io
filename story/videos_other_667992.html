Golden Crown - Function Description:

1. JP multiplier function (Crown X2, X3, X4, X5), AAAA has X2, X3, X4, X5.

2. Bonus feature, each bet of 200 points not won can accumulate 10 points. If the bet exceeds 500 points and draws cards above four plums, all bonus can be obtained, or if five hands are continuously doubled with over 500 points, all bonus can also be obtained. The maximum bonus is 50,000 points, base points are 2000.

3. Can double compare (hold card 5), half compare (hold card 1). And there's an open card feature.

4. Has a manual big prize delivery system. Panda machine, plum to five plums or with JP can be delivered at will.

5. Machine adjustment and account checking can be debugged in any state.

6. Simple machine adjustment, card issuance rate divided into five levels (95---99), color fish fishing machine, and has automatic difficulty control function.

7. Suggested betting one cent per point for excitement.

8. Has an automatic difficulty detection function. If there's an "ambulance" sound, it indicates a difficulty issue, needing to enter the difficulty table by pressing "Hold 5" to exit.

Adjustment:

1. Press "Set" to enter the setting diagram, then input a six-digit password (initial password: six open cards). Display shows: INPUT PASSWORD X X X X X X. Incorrect password turns "X" all yellow, allowing immediate re-entry or press "Hold 5" to exit. Correct password turns "X" all red, then press "Open Card" to enter the adjustment diagram, or press "Set" to change the password by entering a six-digit password again.

2. Enter difficulty table: "Hold 1" moves cursor up, "Hold 2" moves cursor down, "Big" adds or Y, "Small" subtracts or N, "Hold 5" exits. Press "Open Card" and "Score" together to enter manual card delivery adjustment diagram.

Maximum bet points (250, 500, 1000, 1500, 2500) five levels

Minimum bet points (10, 50, 100, 200, 300, 500, 1000) seven levels

Points ratio (10, 100, 200, 500, 1000, 2000) six levels

Five plum points control (0, 100, 500, 800, 1000, 1500, 2000, 2500)

Comparison difficulty (95----99 larger is easier, suggested 97)

JP difficulty (95----99 larger is easier, suggested 97)

Water rate (0%---8% larger is harder, suggested 1%)

Five plum, straight difficulty (0----15 larger is harder, suggested 8)

Small straight, four plum difficulty (0----15 larger is harder, suggested 5)

Function Switches: (Y---On, N---Off)

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 N N N N N N Y N

1: Remote switch, 2: Remote display, 3: Bonus, 4: Award sound, (5) empty, 6: When N deducts from thousands place, when Y deducts from hundreds place, 7: When N pair 'J' breaks even, when Y pair '7' breaks even. 8: Graphics (Poker/Plane).

Note: After adjustment, press "Hold 5" to exit to make it effective.

Manual Card Delivery: "Hold 1" moves cursor left, "Hold 2" moves cursor right, "Big" adds one, "Small" subtracts one, "Double" adds ten.

Explanation: Card delivery type: 0―not delivered, 1---full house, 2---four plums, 3---small straight, 4---large straight, 5---five plums, 6--JP full house, 7--JP four plums, 8---JP small straight, 9---JP large straight, 10---JP five plums.

Number of cards delivered: 0---99 cards, each issuance reduces by one.

High score control for card delivery: Default is 0, does not deliver if bet points are higher than it.

Low score control for card delivery: Default is 0, does not count hand number if bet points are lower than it.

Interval hand number: Multiply by 20 to get actual hand number.

After each adjustment, start from the first group in sequence. After adjustment, press "Hold 5" to exit.

Account Checking Instructions:

Press "Check Account" to enter the first table, this table can be reset to zero by the reset switch. (Default is 0)

Press "Eliminate" to enter the second table, this table cannot be reset to zero. (Default is 0)

Total Points Added: TOTAL IN Total Points Deducted: TOTAL OUT

Press "Check Account" then "Score" to enter the record of different bet points for plum to five plums issuance, comparison hand numbers.

After adjustment, press "Hold 5" to exit.
