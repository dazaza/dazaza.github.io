Introduction to Queen Seondeok:

"Queen Seondeok" tells the story of Princess Dae-Man (played by Lee Yo Won), who was abandoned as a child due to an ominous prophecy. Through relentless effort and navigating palace power struggles, she grows to become Silla's first queen - Queen Seondeok.

Under the control of the beautiful Mi-Seok (played by Ko Hyun Jung) in the royal palace, King Jinpyeong and Lady Maya give birth to twin daughters. However, for the king, having twins is considered extremely unlucky, with the omen of "the end of the royal male lineage." Reluctantly, King Jinpyeong orders his second daughter, Dae-Man, to be sent away, but Mi-Seok secretly sends assassins to eliminate her. At a critical moment, Dae-Man is rescued by the legendary Hwarang leader Moon Nu, who devises a plan to save her and entrusts her to King Jinpyeong's servant Cho Hwa. Cho Hwa takes Dae-Man to the desert regions of China and raises her in a small inn. Dae-Man's greatest rival, Mi-Seok, uses her exceptional beauty to win the favor of King Jinheung and gain control over the palace. After King Jinheung's death, the ambitious Mi-Seok installs the young King Jinpyeong on the throne. When King Jinpyeong's wife gives birth to twin daughters, Mi-Seok plans to use their lives to threaten him and directly seize control of the royal power. Unexpectedly, Moon Nu and Cho Hwa rescue Dae-Man, thwarting her plans. Despite this, Mi-Seok does not abandon her ambitions for the throne and plots to install her husband as king. She sees Dae-Man's twin sister, Princess Jima (played by Park Yi Jin), as a threat. Under Mi-Seok's rule, Princess Jima, known for her intelligence and quick judgment, remains cautious but displays her leadership qualities at crucial moments. Although Princess Jima marries Gim Yongsu (played by Park Jung Cheol) and bears a son, Gim Chunju (played by Yoo Seung Ho), she develops feelings for Gim Yu-sin (played by Um Tae-woong), who once saved her life. The renowned Silla general Gim Yu-sin, after accidentally rescuing Princess Jima from an assassination attempt orchestrated by Mi-Seok's followers, meets Dae-Man through Princess Jima's arrangement. While helping Princess Dae-Man achieve her grand vision of unifying the Three Kingdoms, Gim Yu-sin develops feelings for Dae-Man and becomes her loyal supporter. With Gim Yu-sin's assistance, Princess Dae-Man gradually dismantles Mi-Seok's influence and ascends to the throne as Silla's first queen.
