"Movie Legend of Wind Voice" Fully Reveals the Secrets of the Spy Blockbuster "Wind Voice"

by jqrxass on 2009-09-27 22:19:00

"The Legend of Wind Voice" is an "encyclopedia" about the movie "Wind Voice". It introduces not only the historical background and plot of this movie, but also highlights some exciting moments. This allows readers to recall fragments of the movie while reading this book. Moreover, interviews with the director and actors are recorded in this book. Some less-known behind-the-scenes anecdotes are also revealed with illustrations. The book presents a comprehensive and detailed version of "Wind Voice" with its mysterious veil lifted.

First Volume: Related Content

Without a doubt, as a suspenseful spy thriller, if "Wind Voice" loses the suspense of "who is Old Ghost", it would be like watching a game with a known outcome, which is quite uninteresting. Therefore, when someone asked me how I felt about the "revolutionary changes" in the movie adaptation of my novel "Wind Voice", I said: that means this movie has the potential to succeed. Adapting a 200,000-word novel into a more than one-hour movie is like compressing a forest into a capsule; no change is impossible. The relationship between movies and novels is like the relationship between mountains and sea waves - mountains are solidified waves. If the movie "Wind Voice" tells exactly the same story as the novel in the future, the movie is definitely doomed. Changes are necessary, and adjustments in character relationships and changes in plot suspense are required. But I didn't expect Mr. Chen Guofu to pull this trick: shooting until the end without knowing who is Old Ghost.

Second Volume: Who is Old Ghost

Wu Zhiguo is both righteous and villainous, Bai Xiaonian is gentle and refined, Li Ningyu is calm and collected, Gu Xiaomeng is carefree and coquettish, Jin Shenghuo deals with things slowly. Who among them is the real "Old Ghost"?

Third Volume: Death Instant

The tormented body will eventually betray the rock-solid will. Death occurs in an instant. But the spirit can last forever.

Fourth Volume: Rising from Wind

Starting in darkness and ending in light, the process unfolds dynamically under their direction.

They created a legend for this realistic film industry.

Fifth Volume: Chasing Shadows

Linking stories with pictures, making "Wind Voice" lively and vivid in its performance, and creating a sense of tension after the words.
