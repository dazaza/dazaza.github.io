Website Optimization by Yi Xiang: Any website should fully utilize the effects of internal linking 7

by jackntav9612 on 2012-02-04 16:07:27

Any website should give full play to the effect of internal links. Relevance is an important reference factor for search engine ranking, so this is the core issue that internal links must address. Secondly, although PR value may not be valued by many people now, if it is an important inner page and has a good internal link strategy as support, the important inner page should have a certain PR value. This is also one of the positive factors reflecting the internal link strategy issues. Thirdly, observe with some keyword rankings. If the inner pages of sites with worse quality than your own site have rankings, then it's time to carefully review the internal linking strategy.

Analysis of the Value of Internal Links vs External Links: For a website, in the early stages when external links are relatively scarce, the value of external links is greater than that of internal links. Regarding website authority and ranking improvement, the importance of external links is much higher. If you want to increase the weight of your website, you should still focus on building more external links related to your site.

In the middle stage of website development, internal links may begin to play a very significant role. However, the optimization layout of internal links should also be started early.

How to implement internal linking? So-called internal links are actually mutual links between web pages within the site. From an SEO perspective, to make internal links valuable, you need to carefully select pages, meaning you decide which two pages will mutually link together. The anchor text of internal links is usually a keyword. For example, if SinoEuro wants to create an internal link with the anchor text "search engine optimization," then you need to know which page to choose for this internal link to provide greater assistance to the ranking.

To solve this problem, there are generally two methods:

1. Determine the target page for each keyword from the beginning. Whenever this word appears anywhere on the site, it is always a fixed URL throughout.

2. Search for the keyword on the search engine and see which page ranks higher, then use that page.

Note: Regardless of which method, once the target page is selected, it should not be frequently modified. Because the effect of internal links is achieved through accumulated time, and only after a long period will the rankings show good results.

The problems that an internal linking strategy needs to address: To formulate a strategy, you first need to know what issues internal links aim to solve. The role of internal links is to address: 1. Improve page relevance; 2. Enhance rankings; 3. Increase PR value transmission. These three points are the core principles of an internal linking strategy, meaning that the layout of internal links should start from these three aspects.
