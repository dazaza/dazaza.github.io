Obama Scholarships: Making Grants Available To Single Mothers

Single working mothers perhaps have the toughest job of all, as they have to take care of their families on their own. For many mothers, higher education remains only a wish or a goal that has to be put on hold in order to handle other responsibilities. However, with his new administration, President Obama is encouraging mothers to make their education a priority. The Obama scholarships are offering working mothers a great opportunity to improve their futures.

While people of all ages are encouraged to pursue higher education, Obama is specifically urging working mothers through his Scholarships for Moms program. The Scholarships for Moms initiative will build upon the federal government's long-standing offer of Pell grants. Applied for through FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid), Pell grants are the most commonly sought forms of aid for students going to college. The Obama administration, aiming to encourage more mothers to return to school, is now making some changes.

The biggest incentive for the Scholarships for Moms program is the amount of money being offered. While the maximum amount awarded through Pell grants is $4000 per student, this amount is increased to $5,000 for mothers who apply to the program. The goal of these grants is to enable working mothers to return to school and earn a degree that allows them to better provide for their families both now and in the future. By equipping mothers to earn more money as working professionals, the administration also hopes to stimulate the economy. Mothers can receive an education without worrying about school costs or repaying a loan.

Additionally, a great incentive for mothers considering this program is that it applies to enrollment in nearly any type of higher education institution. Four-year universities are covered, as well as smaller community colleges. You can choose to attend a private school or a public one. An option for busy mothers who can't stay on campus is to study through online courses.

A comprehensive effort to get enthusiastic mothers back into the classroom is the government’s Scholarships for Moms program. The Obama scholarship really alleviates much of the worry associated with returning to school. Working mothers can earn a degree in almost any area of interest, meaning they can be fully equipped to provide for their families. Higher education doesn't have to remain an unfulfilled wish or an unattainable goal any longer. The Scholarships for Moms program is designed to create a brighter future for mothers and their children.
