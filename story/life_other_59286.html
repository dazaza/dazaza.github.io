Female University Lecturer Tests Public's Ability to Endure by Taking off Her Clothes in a Bank

Core tip: On the afternoon of the 25th, a 34-year-old woman took off her clothes in the hall of a bank and was stopped halfway by security. The woman claimed she was a lecturer in the psychology department at a university and that this time she wanted to take off all her clothes in public to test people's psychological endurance. It was learned that the woman had no apparent mental issues.

The Urban Express reported on March 26 that at 1:07 PM yesterday, Mr. Ding called to say: "I am currently at a bank near the intersection of Wenyi Road and Jingzhou Road in Hangzhou depositing money. Just now, a person who claimed to be a female lecturer from the psychology department of a university came to the bank lobby for a psychological test, saying she would take off all her clothes to test people's psychological endurance. She had only taken off half of her clothes when she was taken outside by the security, who said it couldn't be done in a public place. Later, the woman was taken away by the 110 police."

The reporter then contacted the bank. According to the security of the bank, the female customer who took off her clothes was around 30-40 years old and carried an old bag. Around 1 PM, she entered the business hall and queued up to handle business. At the time, there were more than ten people queuing, and she stood beside a table.

(Note from the reporter: This table belongs to a securities company, which usually sends people to sit in the bank's business hall to open accounts for customers. At the time, the staff member sent by the securities company was a young man surnamed Lin, whose seat faced this female customer.)

"She put her bag on the ground, silently and without hesitation began taking off her top slowly. In front of me, I didn't even dare to look and quickly turned my head away," said Xiao Lin, aged 23, who does not yet have a girlfriend. Talking about this incident, Xiao Lin still felt a bit shy and his face turned red.

The bank security quickly went over and asked her to put her clothes back on and persuaded her to leave, also reporting the matter to the police. A while later, she put her clothes back on and re-entered the business hall, saying she wanted to deposit money. She opened her bag and showed it to the security; inside was about 10,000 yuan in cash.

The bank immediately dispatched an experienced female staff member to receive her, asking her to sit down, chatting with her for a while, and checking her ID card. The female customer was very calm. When asked why she took off her clothes, the female customer said that she was a university teacher, teaching psychology, and that taking off her clothes was intended to conduct a psychological test, testing everyone's psychological endurance.

Subsequently, the police officers from the police station arrived and took away the female customer. A police officer on duty at the police station said that the female customer had been taken home by her family.

It was learned that the female customer is indeed a university teacher with no apparent mental health problems, but it cannot be ruled out that she has encountered sudden emotional stress.
