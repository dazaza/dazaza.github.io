by yinyin on 2008-10-31 15:37:24

Dear Bloggers,

Thank you all for your enthusiastic attention to the new version of the Personal Center!

6. Fixed the display error of the greeting homepage under IE7.

8. Added pagination to the results of前世 (previous life) and name affinity.

9. Added a powerful component management page and individual component introduction pages.

10. Components can be activated without invitation.

11. Modified the way links to friends in friend messages open pages; now clicking on a friend's link will directly open within the existing Personal Center framework.

12. Improved the presentation of friend relationships in the Personal Center from previous components and automatically added previously activated component users as Personal Center friends.

Subsequently, the new version of the Personal Center will continue to update and optimize, including:

1. Optimizing the “people you may know” function for more accurate friend recommendations.

2. Optimizing the online friend chat function.

3. Integrating the message center to make the Personal Center platform more integrated.

4. Adding message leaving and comment viewing functions to the Personal Center.

5. Optimizing friend activity settings and adding component message setting functions.

6. Adding more fun and practical components.

Note:

The “Friend Management Tool” allows for quick access and management of friends who frequently interact with you.
