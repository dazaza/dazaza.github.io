Why did Google Ads remove Picasa's referral? I kind of get it

by dutmcse on 2007-06-06 00:37:33

A week ago, I found that google did not have the Picasa option in the promotion options, that is, I could not continue to promote Picasa to make money, and I did not care much at that time. Now looking back at the statistics of the past month, I found that my promotion income mainly came from Picasa. On average, 15 times out of 100 promotion clicks were from Picasa, 5 times from firefox, and Adsense had the most clicks, but no revenue was generated at present. I just added Adsense. Maybe there will be an earnings reflection next month.

In this case, Picasa's referral revenue ratio is too high, and Google feels that it is not OK:). Fortunately, those that have been published and promoted before can continue to be used, to save them well, and to make money on Picasa ^_^

A hundred dollars a month in the account is basically satisfied with me, after all, blog needs more interest to maintain. I hope all bloggers make more money and be happy every day!
