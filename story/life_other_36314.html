It's getting cooler. Everyone send by mail the clothes you no longer want to those who need them.

by e_wuqing on 2007-11-23 19:57:56

About donating clothes; Mailing process:

1) Sort out old clothes, shoes, and books from your home that you no longer wear (if the undergarments have large holes, it's better not to donate them);

2) Wash them clean. It’s best to soak them in disinfectant and then dry them in the sun (this is a sign of respect and responsibility to the recipients. Dear friends, could you please take the trouble to do this? They are equal to us);

3) Pack them - find your own cardboard box or non-transparent cloth bag (rice bag, flour bag, or sew your own cloth bag, which is more economical). It's best to first put the clothes into a plastic bag before putting them into the cardboard box or cloth bag because this can prevent the clothes from getting wet by rain during mailing. At this time, don't seal the box or bag yet because the post office needs to check the contents;

4) Take them to the post office for inspection;

5) Buy a parcel form for 0.5 yuan. The parcel form should be green, the cheapest and most practical one (post office staff often ask if you want to send an express package or a regular parcel. Express packages are not cost-effective and are more expensive), fill in the address clearly;

6) Seal the bag (the post office will provide thread to sew the bag shut), write down the address;

7) You can choose to insure the package, 1 yuan can insure items worth 100 yuan;

8) Depending on the distance to the destination, the charge is 2 to 4 yuan per kilogram.
