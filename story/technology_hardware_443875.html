Is it good to have a breast reduction surgery?

Breast reduction surgery mainly reduces the volume of the breasts (removing excess skin, fat and mammary glands), reshapes the contour and shape of the breasts, adjusts the position of the breasts, and eliminates the troubles caused by overly large female breasts.

What preparation should be made before breast reduction surgery? If you smoke, you should stop smoking before the surgery. Aspirin and certain anti-inflammatory drugs may increase bleeding, so these drugs should be discontinued for a period of time before the surgery. The doctor may also give other instructions. Breast reduction surgery is generally performed in hospital.

What is the situation on the day of breast reduction surgery? Breast reduction surgery is usually carried out in the hospital operating room under general anesthesia. You will be asleep during the entire surgical process. After the surgery, you will be sent to the recovery room for monitoring. Drainage tubes will be placed in the surgical area to avoid fluid accumulation around the chest. Dressings will be applied and bandaged.
