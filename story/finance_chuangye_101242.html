Twenty-three details of gaining connections on the entrepreneurial path

by huangxin27 on 2008-11-05 10:45:25

Compiled and edited by Entrepreneur China, hoping it will be helpful to you.

1. Be热情when meeting people, always smile, even if they are strangers. Do not appear cold or deeply worldly.

2. When shaking hands with others, for the same sex you can hold three seconds longer, and it should have a bit of strength, showing your sincerity. For the opposite sex, only lightly hold the four fingers.

3. When talking to others, try not to interrupt them, patiently listen to what they say. At the same time, your attitude should be sincere and gentle, looking into their eyes. You must not look askance because that is impolite. But you also cannot stare too long as this would make the other person uncomfortable.

4. It’s best not to criticize others' mistakes on the spot. Afterward, point them out quietly and tactfully or indirectly.

5. Persist in saying good things about others behind their back. Don’t be afraid that these good words won't reach the ears of the concerned party.

6. If someone says bad things about someone else in front of you, just smile. Absolutely do not express opinions or spread rumors.

7. Be honest and trustworthy to everyone.

8. When playing cards with friends, don't cheat; when luck is good, don't be arrogant or boastful, saying things that hurt the other side; when luck is bad, don't get angry.

9. When consuming together with friends, be a little more generous, don't seem stingy.

10. Think highly of others' actions and motives, and often express this meaning to the other party.

11. When you make a mistake, admit and apologize promptly and actively. Don't take face too seriously.

12. After meeting once, you must remember the other person's full name. If possible, also understand and remember their strengths, hobbies, and birthdays.

13. During various holidays, birthdays, wedding anniversaries, etc., send as many beautiful messages as possible to your friends, sincerely expressing your best wishes.

14. When with friends, try to talk about topics that interest them. This is very important.

15. Respect all people, including those who don't like you.

16. Often self-criticize, but don't self-praise. However, don't appear overly humble, as this would make people feel that you are very insincere.

17. Do not be stingy with your applause.

18. Absolutely do not insult or mock others, and never belittle others.

19. Know how to be grateful, gratitude is also a virtue.

20. Speak less in crowded places, speaking too much will surely lead to mistakes.

21. Change the unspoken "no" to: "I'll do my best", "Let me think about it", "This needs time".

22. Don't excessively flatter others, this way you will lose your personal charm.

23. During gatherings, don't get angry over trivial matters, ruining the harmonious and cheerful atmosphere for everyone.
