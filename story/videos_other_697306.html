by iqing9jin on 2012-03-08 14:42:22

A proposal is like a battle. But for women, being proposed to is actually something they are extremely proud of. Before being proposed to, they already have their ideal way of being proposed in their hearts. Of course, just having an eye-catching and flashy proposal doesn't necessarily guarantee that you will "win the beauty's heart" every time. Sometimes, the "fatal blow" is often those sweet words. Let's take a look at what kind of proposal the zodiac women yearn for.

1. Aries

Proposal Keywords: Excitement

Aries girls all like fresh and exciting things. Some too rigid proposal methods are completely unattractive to them. The proposal method they yearn for can be shouting the words "marry me" on a roller coaster, or proposing with a diamond ring during bungee jumping, rock climbing and other sports... For the straightforward Aries, whether there is a diamond ring or not is not important, even a pull-tab can get them, as long as it's unconventional and makes her feel that you are very creative, she will be satisfied.

2. Taurus

Proposal Keywords: Thoughtfulness

Taurus women like steady men. Grand and elegant proposals can give them a high sense of trust and security. However, don't think that Taurus women don't need flowers, diamonds, and vows. For the beauty-loving Taurus women, flowers are never outdated, some wine to cheer up is good, music cannot be missing... In fact, it mainly depends on whether the man really has some thoughtful ideas. As long as his sincerity is seen, Taurus women will comply.

3. Gemini

Proposal Keywords: Variety

For the ever-changing Gemini, being original, different, and unconventional is the only way to win her heart. For example, arranging for the words "XX marry me" to appear in the subtitles while watching a movie, or putting up a huge poster proposal on the subway, don't worry about Gemini thinking it's too exaggerated, because Gemini feels that whether it's getting married or being proposed to, fun is the key, touching her is the point.

4. Cancer

Proposal Keywords: Environment

Cancer women are very kind. They generally won't easily refuse their lover's proposal, but among the twelve zodiac signs, Cancer women are famous for taking care of the family. For them, the environment of the proposal location must be as warm and comfortable as home. Too ostentatious places might also work, but they shouldn't be too exaggerated. No matter where it is, as long as a warm atmosphere is created, along with plain, intimate language, Cancer women will feel safe and grounded.

5. Leo

Proposal Keywords: High-profile

In the heart of Leo big women, their wedding must be grand, and the proposal cannot be rash, simple, or taken lightly. Leo women are a very subjective zodiac sign. They usually love to show off, so the best proposal method is one that makes her feel glorious! Even on the street is fine, because in front of everyone, with everyone's attention, it could also be a surprise; even inside the company is feasible, with flowers, a diamond ring, and envious looks from colleagues... Anyway, it should not be low-key.

6. Virgo

Proposal Keywords: Details

Virgo women all pursue perfection, which also determines that they themselves tend towards perfection. Therefore, when it comes to proposals, they certainly won't accept them easily. The proposal they yearn for doesn't necessarily cost a lot of money, kneeling down is okay, but the details must be perfect, because details determine success or failure, such as whether the chosen environment is good enough, whether the words spoken are appropriate, whether the clothes worn are formal, whether the music is soft and lingering... As long as the right spot is hit, even the pickiest Virgo will agree.

7. Libra

Proposal Keywords: Elegance

For Libra women, they are quite elegant. Proposals don't have to be romantic, but they must be elegant. If planning to propose during a meal, it must be in an elegant restaurant. If planning to say the vows of love after watching a movie, it must be in a five-star cinema... The lighting must be fully coordinated, the surrounding crowd harmonious, the music just right, using a leisurely and elegant tone to cater to her preferences, using sweet words to melt her, Libra women will definitely nod in agreement.

8. Scorpio

Proposal Keywords: Guidance

Straightforward proposal methods, for Scorpios, are simply lacking in creativity and surprise. Scorpios' minds reject the ordinary. If before proposing, guidance work is done well, letting her discover it herself, such as finding each other in an amusement park, or discovering a diamond ring in the food or gifts, then the other party appears appropriately in front of her and proposes to her, Scorpio women will definitely feel happy beyond measure.

9. Sagittarius

Proposal Keywords: Journey

Sagittarius women love traveling the most! And for them, the irresistible proposal method is definitely while accompanying her on a journey, such as in the beautiful scenery in March, by the rippling lake, in the ancient buildings, witnessed by fellow travelers... If the man generously pulls out the ring and loudly says: Marry me! Sagittarius women would probably fall into his arms.

10. Capricorn

Proposal Keywords: Sincerity

Capricorn women are usually very strong and simple. Romantic and unconventional methods seem unappreciated to her, but as long as it's sincere, she will definitely be moved. Only when her heart is vulnerable, giving her a strong commitment will succeed. For example, when she is not doing well at work and in a bad mood, you quietly tell her: Let's quit! I'll take care of you for the rest of my life. It will definitely succeed!

11. Aquarius

Proposal Keywords: Unconventional

Aquarius girls are actually quite unconventional sometimes, and their thoughts are hard for outsiders to understand. For them, vulgar and common proposal methods are not attractive at all. If it's something fresh and unexpected, it might make them greatly admire. For example, playing a mimicry show, adding some high-tech elements, using remote-controlled planes, racing cars to propose... These might make Aquarius feel that it's too creative and let her submit.

12. Pisces

Proposal Keywords: Romance

When proposing to Pisces, remember it must be romantic. Pisces women, whether they appear gentle or strong, will all be overwhelmed by romantic proposal methods. Whether it's going to a certain seaside to enjoy the wind, releasing lanterns, admiring fireworks... Or making promises under the starlit sky, making vows of love amidst blooming flowers. Pisces cannot refuse these, because their hearts are softer and they can't resist sweetness and romance.
