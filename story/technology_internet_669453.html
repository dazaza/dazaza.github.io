When you talk about Mesa-Phoenix, Arizona, one of the things that might come to mind is the scorching heat of the sun. Despite a significant portion of the town being desert, there are still numerous lakes, parks, and mountains that offer oases for those looking to maintain good health while in the area.

1. **South Mountain Park Scenic Drive**: Known as the largest park in the area, it features many lookouts providing stunning views of the valleys. You can drive to enjoy the scenery, but for an enhanced experience, hike the trails and see the petroglyphs.

2. **Camelback Mountain**: Named for its resemblance to a camel's hump, this mountain's vertical cliff height makes it popular among rock climbers. The recreation area also includes trails and water fountains where you can enjoy fishing and other water activities.

3. **Phoenix Greyhound Park**: Join the fun and win prizes while watching greyhound races. Both beginners and experienced bettors can participate.

4. **Saguaro Lake Trail Rides**: If you love exploring forests and lakes on unique transportation, choose from the horses available at Saguaro stables. The trail rides last about an hour, giving you ample time to escape city life and enjoy fresh air and warm lake waters.

5. **Mesa Skate Park**: You don't need ice to skate. The Reed Park Skate Court offers opportunities to practice your skills, meet new friends, and enjoy skating year-round.

6. **Quail Run Park**: It's always great to have a companion while staying fit or living healthily. Bring your dog to this dog park, walk together, or jog around the tracks while keeping your dog on a leash.

7. **Fighter Combat International**: Experience what it feels like to ride in fighter planes through flight simulations offered here to all who want to conquer the skies.

8. **Manzanita Speedway**: Be like Speed Racer on the half-mile oval track open every Friday and Saturday for those who love driving vehicles at high speeds.

9. **Lookout Mountain Golf Club**: Choose from various greens with some of the best Sonoran desert views. The 18-hole course is perfect for those seeking alone time during vacations.

10. **Arizona River Runners**: Enjoy Arizona rivers in style by riding motorboats or oar-driven vehicles. Alternatively, fish, snorkel, or swim near the shores.
