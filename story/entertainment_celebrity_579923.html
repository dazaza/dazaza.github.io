On November 23, 2011, in Hong Kong, Chen Jiahuan (Rose) was allegedly subjected to disrespectful advances by Chen Haomin and Ma Dezhong in mainland China. Yesterday, she held a press conference to explain the incident. Looking weary and slightly thinner, Chen Jiahuan criticized the two seniors for having happy families and had expected them to treat her like a younger sister. Unexpectedly, they became someone else during the incident. She apologized for not knowing how to protect herself, which worried her master and family! After the press conference, Chen Jiahuan reported the incident to the police. It is understood that after the incident occurred, many people communicated with Master Xian Guolin of Chen Jiahuan, hoping to mediate the situation. Among them were even individuals with underworld connections. In response, Xian Guolin only admitted that recently, a large number of people have contacted him, including many influential individuals!

Chen Jiahuan was allegedly involved in an incident at Hengdian in mainland China where she was reportedly hugged and kissed by Chen Haomin and Ma Dezhong. Due to the severity of the matter, it has drawn significant attention from all sectors. Yesterday, Chen Jiahuan, accompanied by her manager and legal representative, held a press conference in Kwun Tong to detail the events, with more than fifty media outlets covering the event. Two Seniors Behaved Differently

Chen Jiahuan appeared without makeup. Whether due to the stress of the incident over several days, she looked extremely tired and slightly thinner. After standing still for photos, Manager Miss Chan began speaking first, stating that the entertainment industry has a group of public-spirited people who support each other and form a big family. There should be no disgraceful elements present, as this would tarnish the reputation of the entertainment industry in mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan. Therefore, the management company will take legal action to expose the true nature of these despicable individuals!

Subsequently, Chen Jiahuan began speaking, recounting her feelings over the past few days: "Thank you to the media for your concern. After this incident, I, my master, and everyone at the management company are very unhappy. I want to explain the details of this event. Previously, while filming a movie in mainland China, I would often dine with artists. Nothing happened in the two days before the incident. The reason I attended these dinners was because Hung Tin-ming was there, and he is a trustworthy person. His father, Hung Kam-bo, also knows my master well. That night, we were having late-night snacks, and since I was leaving with Hung Tin-ming, I stayed behind. Initially, nothing happened, but when Hung Tin-ming got drunk, this incident occurred. I was very scared. Both seniors have happy families, and I thought they would treat me like a younger sister, but unexpectedly, they became someone else, and I didn't know how to react."

Mentioning Her Family and Master, Tears Well Up

At this point, Chen Jiahuan paused and then continued: "My master reminded me to be careful, but I wasn't vigilant enough. I apologize to my master and family for making them worry!"

Throughout the explanation process, Chen Jiahuan managed to control her emotions and remained calm. However, when mentioning her family and master, she couldn't hold back, lowering her head silently, tears welling up in her eyes. She had to pause for a moment before accepting media interviews.

Reporter: Did you resist during the incident?

Chen Jiahuan: Yes, I resisted, but I couldn't react fast enough. They are people I trusted, and I was unfamiliar with the place, so I didn't know how to leave.

Reporter: Did you drink alcohol?

Chen Jiahuan: No.

Reporter: Ma Dezhong said he reached a consensus with Master Xian?

Manager: The boss never mentioned such things!

Lawyer: Actually, reporting to the police and filing charges determines who is the defendant and who is the witness, decided by the police.

Reporter: Have Ma Dezhong and Chen Haomin apologized?

Chen Jiahuan: (Pausing for a moment): It's inconvenient to talk about it now; I'll discuss it once everything is resolved.

Reporter: Chen Jiahuan mentioned resisting beforehand, but Ma Dezhong helped her adjust her clothes after touching her.

Chen Jiahuan: I did try to resist, but I lacked vigilance and caution.

Reporter: Was this your first time meeting them in mainland China?

Chen Jiahuan: Yes, I met them in Hengdian, and our managers frequently ate together.

During the questioning, there were many questions Chen Jiahuan didn't know how to face and needed assistance from those around her to answer, such as "How long did this behavior last?" "Did they touch your private parts or chest?" "Some photos show you smiling." Chen Jiahuan didn't respond, and the lawyer nearby replied that they would report to the police and wouldn't comment further due to case involvement. Chen Jiahuan's legal representative also mentioned understanding that the incident occurred in mainland China, potentially complicating evidence collection, but they would still file a report and consult opinions from mainland lawyers to determine if they should report to Zhejiang Province.

After the press conference, Chen Jiahuan was escorted by four black security guards to a nanny car and drove to Kwun Tong Police Station. Upon arrival, she was immediately surrounded by a large number of reporters, creating chaos. After about an hour inside the Kwun Tong Police Station, a senior police officer in uniform stated that Chen Jiahuan had taken a CID vehicle to provide a second statement. Later, the media received news that Chen Jiahuan went to Tseung Kwan O Police Station, where they waited. After five hours of providing statements, Chen Jiahuan left the station near 11 PM in the evening. When asked if providing the statement was difficult, she indicated that she was just stating the facts. Chen Jiahuan's manager mentioned that she would leave Hong Kong today and return to mainland China to continue working.

The police spokesperson replied that the police received a report yesterday from a 19-year-old woman claiming she was molested earlier in mainland China. The case was handled as an extraterritorial crime.

Intervention by Influential Figures: Xian Guolin Vows Justice

New favorite among otaku, Chen Jiahuan (Rose), unfortunately fell victim to the predatory actions of Chen Haomin and Ma Dezhong, sparking heated discussions across the city. It is known that many people have mediated through different channels, hoping to resolve the matter quietly. Those involved include individuals from the business world, entertainment circles, and even those with underworld connections. In response, Master Xian Guolin of Chen Jiahuan admitted that many people have contacted him recently: "People from different fields, many of whom are influential, but please don't use the term 'underworld' as it sounds too harsh!" Regarding Ma Dezhong's claim on Weibo that he has been forgiven, Xian Guolin stated that various parties have proposed solutions to him, but he hasn't committed to any yet. The matter has been handed over to the lawyer for handling, and he will understand the details and strive for justice in a reasonable and lawful manner. He said: "With the principle of leniency where possible, forgiveness can be considered, provided the parties involved give Chen Jiahuan a fair explanation!"

Afraid and Unable to Defend Herself

Xian Guolin mentioned in a radio interview yesterday morning that initially, he was unaware of the severity of the incident, only knowing that Chen Jiahuan was bullied: "Seeing the photos made me realize how serious it was, shocking! Such behaviors still exist nowadays!" He mentioned seeing Chen Jiahuan the previous night to understand the events, and her emotions have been poor: "She cried for several nights, and even when I called her, she was crying. I scolded her for not being discerning enough! But scolding her was excessive, as she is the victim!" When asked why Chen Jiahuan, who practices Wing Chun, didn't defend herself, Xian Guolin explained: "A 19-year-old girl has not experienced such situations, facing two large men in an unfamiliar place like Hengdian, without encountering wolves before, no amount of training could prepare her. She was so afraid that she was at a loss what to do."

Regarding Hung Tin-ming taking Chen Jiahuan out for late-night snacks but failing to protect her, Xian Guolin stated that Hung Tin-ming was already drunk at the time and later sent him an SMS apology. When asked if he knew Chen Haomin and Ma Dezhong, he replied that he doesn't know who Chen Haomin is and has only eaten twice with Ma Dezhong, who respectfully called him "master." As for whether anyone called to plead for mercy, he chuckled and didn't respond but emphasized that the entire matter is clear to the public, and the entertainment industry should make a fair judgment.
