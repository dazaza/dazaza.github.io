To earnestly study and implement the important speech spirit of Secretary Hu

by guogaiyanxiu on 2010-02-25 15:33:58

Recently, the Lao District Office of Sanming City consecutively held cadres' meetings to study and convey the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu Jintao during his inspection in Fujian and the meeting spirit of the province and city. In connection with the actual situation of its own unit, it determined to earnestly study and implement the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu, and take practical actions to do a good job in the Lao district and accelerate the development of the Lao district, so as not to辜负the care and expectations of the Party Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the Lao district.

I. Strengthen learning and strive to improve the quality of the cadre team. All cadres and Party members of the office should regard the serious study and implementation of the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu as an important political task in the current and future period. Focus on improving six abilities of cadres in the Lao District Office: First, improve the ability to understand policies. At present, the National Development and Reform Commission has clearly defined the scope of policies enjoyed by the original central Soviet counties and revolutionary old areas in Fujian. The provincial Party committee and provincial government will introduce support policies for revolutionary old areas that are not included in the policy enjoyment range. We must strengthen communication with the province, seize the opportunity, strengthen policy docking, and use well, fully and flexibly the policies of the seven original central Soviet counties in our city and three old area counties (Datian, Yong'an, Youxi) supported by the province; Second, improve the ability to execute, especially to improve the ability to work independently; Third, improve the ability to serve the masses, take the development of the old area as their own responsibility, be concerned about the old area, take root in the old area, and serve the old area; Fourth, improve the ability to operate projects, use well and flexibly the limited funds of the old area, and improve the economic, social and ecological benefits of assisted construction projects; Fifth, improve the ability to overcome difficulties, and make efforts to overcome difficulties in terms of funds, manpower, institutions, and project-driven; Enhance the determination and confidence to do a good job in the old area; Sixth, improve the ability to unite and cooperate. Look at the old area beyond the old area, firmly establish the concept of "big old area", strengthen unity and cooperation, strengthen coordination and cooperation with departments, and strengthen communication and contact with higher levels.

II. Emancipate mind and constantly update concepts. First, we must be good at seizing opportunities. Seize the opportunity that the seven original central Soviet counties enjoy national western policies and the provincial Party committee and provincial government introduce policy support for old area counties, increase support, help, and tilt, do a good job in project research, actively think, actively do, actively study, actively dock, and actively implement. Second, dare to pilot first. From the perspective of benefiting the development of the old area, improving the living and production conditions of the people in the old area, and benefiting the work, seriously consider the fields and channels for the old area work to pilot first. With innovative concepts, promote the old area work to a new level, produce new results, and see new effects. Third, have the courage to take responsibility. Adhere to the system of collective research on major matters. Enhance execution ability and implementation ability, achieve "zero" obstacles in work promotion, and truly achieve "four less and four more": less requests, more thinking about ways to solve problems; less explanation, more solving actual difficulties; less wrangling, more doing practical work; less formalism, more doing practical and effective things.

III. Contact with reality and focus on implementing work. In accordance with the deployment of the province and city, and in connection with the actual work of the old area, focus on doing a good job in the "five tasks" at present: First, strengthen publicity and lay a solid ideological foundation for the old area. Through various channels, publicize the important speech spirit of General Secretary Hu during his inspection in Fujian, especially the important speech spirit on the construction of the old area, combine the study of General Secretary Hu's important speeches on the development of the old area in recent years, further understand the requirements of the Party Central Committee and General Secretary Hu for the construction of the old area, and lay a solid ideological foundation for the construction of the old area; Second, strengthen research and give full play to the role of the advisory assistant of the Lao District Office. Enhance the foresight and forward-looking nature of work, focus on the central work of the Municipal Party Committee and municipal government, focus on the hot and difficult issues and deep-seated contradictions in the construction and development of the old area, strengthen research, and provide references for decision-making of the Municipal Party Committee and municipal government; Third, strengthen guidance and effectively implement assistance and construction work. With deep feelings for the people of the old area, do more work in terms of policy level, atmosphere creation, and seeking support, and implement the various support policies of the central, provincial, and municipal governments; Fourth, strengthen services and do a good job in all aspects of the "five old" personnel of the revolution. Seriously implement the "five old" living and medical subsidy policies and deliver the care of the Party and government to the "five old"; Fifth, strengthen learning and really strengthen the construction of the cadre team in the old area. Build a learning-oriented agency, improve the level of thought and policy and the ability to solve practical problems; strengthen self-construction, promote work development, and create a new situation in the work of the old area.

(Sanming Lao District Office)
