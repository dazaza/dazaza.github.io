This report was filed by Beijing News reporter Wang Wei. In order to exclusively take over the shares of a thriving hotpot restaurant, four university students from Renwen University conspired to murder their business partner. This morning, the First Intermediate People's Court sentenced Dou Shuo and Bei Fangming, the "planners" of the conspiracy, to death for intentional homicide. Of the other two participants, Yan Guangrui was sentenced to 10 years in prison. The other minor had already been sentenced earlier.

All four defendants—Dou Shuo, Bei Fangming, Yan Guangrui, and Huang—were students at Renwen University. Since the case occurred when Huang was only 17 years old, he was dealt with separately as a minor. Previously, Huang had already been sentenced by the court to life imprisonment for this matter.

According to the prosecution, Dou Shuo and the other four individuals had conflicts with the hotpot restaurant owner Xiao Zhang due to their involvement in its operation, leading them to repeatedly plot murder and purchase axes as tools for the crime.

At around 10 p.m. on April 26, 2010, the four individuals went online at the Junji Shun Internet cafe in Yanqing until the early hours of the next morning. Yan Guangrui stayed behind at the cafe to watch over the computers of the four individuals, creating the false impression that all four had been continuously online at the cafe.

Dou Shuo and the other three quietly left the cafe and went to Fang Ming Ge Foot Massage Shop located on Xinglong Commercial Street in Kangzhuang Town, Yanqing County, where Xiao Zhang and his girlfriend lived. They tricked Xiao Zhang into opening the door and then, while he was unaware in the hallway on the first floor, Bei Fangming and Huang attacked him with axes they had brought along, striking his head, neck, back, and limbs, causing his death.

Subsequently, Dou Shuo and Huang went upstairs to the bedroom where they strangled Xiao Zhang’s pregnant girlfriend Xiao Li (a pseudonym) to death.

Today marked the first time Dou Shuo and others appeared publicly before the media. Family members of the victim Xiao Li also attended the court session. Previously, they had repeatedly demanded severe punishment for the murderers. The court sentenced Dou Shuo and Bei Fangming to death for intentional homicide and sentenced Yan Guangrui to 10 years in prison.

● Greed Leading to Murder

The thriving business led to a fatal outcome.

According to case records, just after the start of the school year in September 2009, sophomore student Dou Shuo, studying 3G communication technology at Renwen University, invested together with classmates Bei Fangming, Yan Guangrui, and Huang in operating a hotpot restaurant located in Yanqing. Dou Shuo contributed 130,000 yuan, while the others contributed between 5,000 and 20,000 yuan as their share of the investment. They agreed with the hotpot restaurant owner Xiao Zhang to split profits fifty-fifty.

Afterward, due to the hotpot restaurant's booming business, daily revenue exceeded 10,000 yuan. Dou Shuo and others then conceived the idea of taking it all for themselves. They planned to kill the hotpot restaurant owner Xiao Zhang and arranged specific roles: After the hotpot restaurant closed, once Xiao Zhang and his girlfriend returned to the foot massage shop, they would kill them. Dou Shuo would be responsible for distracting Xiao Zhang and his girlfriend through casual conversation, while Bei Fangming and Huang would attack from behind with axes.

Yan Guangrui lured classmates from the same dormitory to spend the night at the internet cafe so that after the incident, they could provide alibis claiming that they were all asleep in the dormitory at the time of the crime.

Before the incident, Dou Shuo and others bought four axes, two kitchen knives, ten blue-and-white striped woven bags, and garbage bags at the market. “The axes were used for the killing, the knives for dismemberment, and the woven bags and garbage bags for storing body parts.” Afterwards, these individuals explained that to avoid leaving fingerprints and footprints at the scene, they also purchased rubber gloves and small plastic bags to cover their feet.

On the evening of April 26, 2010, Dou Shuo called Xiao Zhang to lure him to the foot massage shop, where they killed Xiao Zhang by hacking him and then strangled his pregnant girlfriend Xiao Li to death.
