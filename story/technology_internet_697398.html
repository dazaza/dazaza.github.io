Yuancheng Logistics Team won the championship at the Truck Race Panjin station on June 5th.

by yuanchenwl1989 on 2012-03-08 15:18:18

The 2011 Shaanxi Auto Cup National Super Truck Race Owees Racing Club Panjin Liaobin station was successfully concluded on June 5, 2011. Yucheng Logistics Team, Shaanxi Auto Changsha Huan Tong Team, and Cooper Chengshan Tire Team ranked in the top three of the opening match. After an intense afternoon of competition on June 5, six teams entered the semi-finals for two spots: Automobile Observer Team, Cooper Chengshan Tire Team, Ling_km Lubricants Team, Fast Gear Team, Yucheng Logistics Team, and Shaanxi Auto Changsha Huan Tong Team. In the end, the Yucheng Logistics Team and the Shaanxi Auto Changsha Huan Tong Team advanced to the final championship match. Originally poised for a sure victory, the Shaanxi Huan Tong Team suffered severe vehicle damage and tactical errors, leading to a delay in the last round that handed the championship title to the Yucheng Logistics Team. The second and third place teams were the Shaanxi Changsha Huan Tong Team and the Cooper Chengshan Tire Team respectively. The top three individuals were Gong Huan, Gong Bin, and Xin Kejun.

The excitement of the race lies not only in the thrilling process but also in the unpredictable results that provoke speculation. With the regret from this station, we look forward to the talented brothers Gong Huan and Gong Bin representing the Shaanxi Changsha Huan Tong Team regrouping and setting off again at the next station in the Wuhai Desert.

In 2011, Panjin City and the organizing committee of the race conducted large-scale renovations and upgrades to the track, adding new obstacles such as tire pits, single-edge bridges, and side slope roads, setting up surprises for the new season's excitement. This year’s new racing trucks were fully prepared, all upgraded to the latest version of the Shaanxi Auto De Long F3000 4WD off-road heavy-duty truck. The Weichai engines installed on the new racing vehicles used higher-powered models, while the Fastspeed transmissions reduced the gears to six, making simpler operations more suitable for off-road competitions. The gear ratios of the Hande axles were also improved accordingly, with all shock absorbers using enhanced types. The tires selected were off-road dedicated tires from Cooper Chengshan Company, and even the lubricating oil chose the "Iron Man" truck race special oil specifically developed by Shandong Ling_km Petrochemical Co., Ltd. It can be said that this year's super racing vehicles are both internally and externally refined, performing like fish in water on the devilish tracks of the Panjin wetlands, showcasing their impressive Chinese Kung Fu to the audience.

To meet the wishes of more truck enthusiasts and racing lovers, the organizing committee of the competition deliberately increased the number of competing teams to ten this year. Veteran truck racing experts still showed great skills, such as Li Wenli's Ling_km Lubricants Team, Hou Hongning's Yucheng Logistics Team, and Liang Xu's Cooper Chengshan Tire Team, all performing very stably. In recent years, emerging truck racing talents have become increasingly mature, becoming the backbone of truck racing, such as Li Wenbin's Liaoning Huading Team, Peng Qingguo's Automobile Observer Team, Wu Changming's Shaanxi Rocket Team, Gong Huan's Shaanxi Changsha Huan Tong Team, and Ge Dongshen's Hande Tianya Four-Wheel Drive Team, all showing excellent performances. Even more excitingly, 11 drivers including Wu Fuxi, captain of the Fastspeed Pioneer Team, and Captain Wei Hao of the Hanhai Green Oasis Team participated in the truck race for the first time, bringing a fresh breeze to the competition. The rookie race held among them once became a new highlight on the field.
