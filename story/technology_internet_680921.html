Girl wants to seek marriage in public to raise money for mother's medical treatment

by fr8yre3bfhj on 2012-02-28 10:15:33

□ Reporter Guo Changxiu, Correspondent Long Bang Core Tip Announcement "Whoever pays 50,000 yuan to help treat my mom, I will marry him." Behind this statement is a heart-wrenching story. The day before yesterday, reader Wang Ying (a pseudonym) from Hongmiao Village, Huaiqing Street, QinYang City called the reporter saying that her cousin Li Danhong (a pseudonym) hoped to place a matrimonial advertisement in the media with only one requirement for the man: to have 50,000 yuan. The reason is that Danhong's mother has heart disease and urgently needs 50,000 yuan for surgery.

1 House Just Built, Misfortune Arrived

Wang Ying told the reporter that Li Danhong came from a poor family; her father had already passed away, and besides her mother, there was also an intellectually disabled older brother in the family.

Danhong's mother and older brother had been working in some coal factories in QinYang in recent years. Their family lived in a small 20 square meter shack. Danhong, who experienced the hardships of life at a young age, went to Suzhou to work when she was 18. After several years of hard work, Li Danhong finally saved up 33,000 yuan. In early December last year, using the money they had saved together amounting to 60,000 yuan, they built several bungalows next to the small shack.

After the house was built, Danhong's mother, who had always had heart problems, fell ill. Upon going to the hospital for a check-up, she was diagnosed with coronary heart disease, requiring a heart bypass surgery costing about 50,000 yuan.

2 To Raise Surgery Costs, Danhong Considers Matrimonial Advertisement

Since all their savings were spent on building the house, Li Danhong had no choice but to borrow more than 10,000 yuan from relatives to treat her mother. "The hospitalization costs nearly 1,000 yuan per day, and the borrowed 10,000 plus yuan quickly ran out. Auntie (Danhong's mother) had to be discharged," said Wang Ying.

In order to save her mother, Li Danhong decided to place a matrimonial advertisement. Wang Ying said, "Danhong said if any unmarried man is willing to pay 50,000 yuan for her mother's surgery, she would marry him."

The reporter telephoned Li Danhong, who is currently working in Suzhou, and she admitted that she had discussed the matrimonial idea with her cousin, considering it a helpless choice.

"For the sake of saving my mother, Beijing LG refrigerator repair, I am willing to do this. No matter what others think of me, my mother is the most important person to me." To save on travel expenses, Danhong did not return home for the Spring Festival. She hopes to earn more money by working overtime to treat her mother.
