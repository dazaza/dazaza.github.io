Consultation In 2011, with the support of the Chinese Medical Association, the Chinese Medical Foundation awarded the "Outstanding Academic Leader Award" to Professor Wang Bin, a young leading figure in the field of gynecology in Henan Province. He was honored for his special contributions in the field of obstetrics and gynecology, including being a specially appointed expert of the World Health Organization and the pioneer of the U-shaped lesion resection for uterine preservation. To inspire more medical staff to adhere to the pledge of "Great Physicians with Profound Integrity," the Chinese Medical Foundation established a special medical fund named after Wang Bin —— the "Wang Bin Charity Medical Fund of the Chinese Medical Foundation." The Wang Bin Charity Medical Fund will, through various forms such as free assistance and special aid, be dedicated to safeguarding public health with high-quality medical technology and tangible assistance for patients, ensuring the health of the general population. From 2011 to 2016, over five years, this fund aims to raise one million RMB as a special aid fund for the treatment, research, and teaching related to general surgery diseases, gynecological diseases, and reproductive system diseases. For convenience, Zhengzhou Huashan Hospital, where Professor Wang Bin currently works, has been designated as the only hospital for implementing the medical assistance treatment under the "Wang Bin Medical Fund" of the Chinese Medical Foundation.
