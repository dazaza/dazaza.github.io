Parents prevent a couple who have been in love for 8 years from registering due to the incompatibility of their birth charts

by imtoms373 on 2012-02-29 16:14:32

She has been in love with her boyfriend for 8 years, and now the couple has their own "fruit of love", but this couple cannot be happy. What is the reason that causes the man's parents to obstruct the marriage? Xiao Li was originally a girl from Zhuanghe, Dalian. She is 34 years old this year. 8 years ago, she met a young man from Dan Dong named Xiao Chen by chance while working. The two soon fell in love. After more than a year of dating, Xiao Chen took Xiao Li home to meet his parents.

Xiao Chen’s parents were very satisfied with this pretty and sensible girl. During the conversation, the elders learned that Xiao Li was born under the zodiac sign of the sheep, and their expressions suddenly changed. Xiao Li felt very confused. After saying goodbye to Xiao Chen's parents, Xiao Li did not know what she had done wrong to make her boyfriend's parents dissatisfied.

Not long after the incident, one day, Xiao Chen's parents suddenly called their son, saying they wanted to check the compatibility of the two people’s zodiac signs and birth charts. This one check completely changed the originally happy life of the two. "Your zodiac signs are incompatible, and that girl also clashes with your parents' birth charts." The results of the fortune-telling came out quickly. "Nonsense, I don't believe in this nonsense!" Xiao Chen said to his parents on the phone. No matter how Xiao Chen explained to his parents, the two elders just wouldn't listen, insisting on forcing Xiao Chen to break up with his girlfriend.

Xiao Li's family, upon hearing about this matter, became extremely angry, thinking it was an insult to their daughter. In anger, they also forbade Xiao Li from associating with her boyfriend anymore.

On one side were her caring family, and on the other side was her deeply loved boyfriend. Xiao Li felt caught between the two. "We will prove with our actions that we are happy." A sentence from Xiao Chen strengthened the two's determination to continue moving forward.

In January of this year, Xiao Li discovered that she was pregnant and didn't know what to do. Xiao Chen, however, thought this was a good thing, maybe the child in her belly would make his parents agree to the marriage. "As long as I am alive, you two will never get married." Xiao Chen's mother's attitude remained resolute, and the two elders were determined not to hand over the household registration book for the marriage registration.

Afterward, Xiao Li cried every day. Watching the baby in his girlfriend's belly grow day by day, Xiao Chen also became anxious. He decided to go home and steal the household registration book so the two could register their marriage, but he searched the entire house and couldn't find the book.

The couple then sought help from relatives and friends, hoping they could persuade the two elders, but no matter how much they pleaded, Xiao Chen's parents remained unmoved and firmly believed in the results of the fortune-telling.

Helpless, the couple went to the marriage registration office to explain their situation, hoping the staff there could register their marriage for them. The marriage registration department sympathized with their situation, but they must submit relevant household registration materials to process the marriage registration. They stated that if Xiao Chen could obtain a household registration certificate from the local police station, they could immediately process the registration procedures for them.

Xiao Chen went to the local police station full of hope, but things did not go as planned. The police officer explained that with the networking of the "Public Security Population Information Network Digital Certificate Query System," only in special cases such as lost documents can household registration materials be issued. In Xiao Chen's case, because it involves internal family conflicts, issuing the materials might escalate the conflict further, and therefore, the police station has the right to refuse issuance.

Now, the two young people can only hope that someone can resolve the heart knots of the two elders so they can complete their marriage.

Special correspondent from Dandong, Wang Qi
