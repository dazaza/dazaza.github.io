Childlike Index of 12 Constellations

by kfjro082 on 2011-05-29 11:13:18

People with a full childlike heart will always be happier than some others. Recently, a song by S.H.E called "Unwilling to Grow Up" has truly voiced the feelings of many people. In fact, in everyone's heart, there is more or less a "Peter Pan".

Aries

Childlikeness Index: 85

In many times, Aries' behavior of shouting and dancing makes others feel like they are watching a stage performance. Their emotions are clearly expressed on their faces. Moreover, "joy" is "ecstasy", "anger" is "irritation", "sorrow" is "deep sorrow", and "happiness" is "extreme happiness". Their way of expressing emotions and body language seem not to have gradually become subtle as they age, and childishness is still visible everywhere on Aries.

Taurus

Childlikeness Index: 80

If one of the signs of maturity is sensibility, then, the moment Taurus is sentimental, it makes people feel that they are completely still a child. People of this zodiac sign are usually quiet, giving people the impression of being cautious and practical. Their innocence may be hard for those who don't know them well to discover, but it's not without trace. If you have the chance to walk into their room, the various dolls and decorations silently express a message to you ------ the owner has not lost their childlike heart.

Gemini

Childlikeness Index: 90

Gemini is always like a child whose brain has just developed fully, filled with strong curiosity about the unknown world, even though this curiosity sometimes brings them bad results. They are relatively unrestrained, changing like children, and being with them can most exercise your heart endurance ability. If you have been with them for a long time, you will find: they are all very pure, smart is only simple cleverness, unrelated to scheming.

Cancer

Childlikeness Index: 65

If we compare past events to toys, then Cancer is the child who constantly takes out the toys to play with and reminisce. They appear gentle and down-to-earth, giving others a sense of security, but actually lack a great deal of security inside. They are also a sentimental group, with characteristics of melancholy and love for ideals, harboring many romantic ideas in their hearts. Even if some Cancers recognize the cruel reality, they still hold many hopes filled with idealistic colors for the future.

Virgo

Childlikeness Index: 30

Virgos are sensitive and careful. Their personality is introverted, preferring quiet over activity, making them seem somewhat unsociable. They may rarely show a childlike side, instead being closer to Young Werther. Due to their perfectionism, Virgos hold many perfective views. This is a strict self-demanding zodiac sign, in their eyes: childhood should be childlike, adolescence can retain a bit of childlikeness, but after adulthood, one should gradually control their childlikeness.

Libra

Childlikeness Index: 60

Comparatively steady and rational Libras possess excellent balance and fair judgment. They act with logic and strategy, never impulsively solving things with violence, but rather with skillful means, finding the balance point between equal rights and benefits. From this point of view, Libras seem far from being childlike. Actually, it's not so. It's just that their innocence needs to be inspired and discovered by others. If you are also someone who retains a childlike heart, then when you talk to them, you will find their language is full of childlike fun.

Scorpio

Childlikeness Index: 50

The consistent impression Scorpios give is profound and difficult to fathom, having unique views on any matter. They remain calm when encountering things, with extraordinary courage, which might even make people think they are somewhat cunning. Is it possible that they also have an innocent side? In fact, they not only have an innocent side, but this side may accompany them for life. This side is their extremely clear love and hate --- like children, Scorpios are cleverly delicate to friends they like, and to those they have a bad first impression of, they will throw tantrums. Moderation? They will never have such behavior.

Sagittarius

Childlikeness Index: 95

Sagittarius holds extremely high expectations for justice and truth. They value spiritual life, pursuing spiritual happiness. However, Sagittarius easily tends towards excessive optimism, acting both bravely and daringly, rarely thinking about consequences before action. Their childlike heart comes from their pursuit of the most essential happiness, like children who don't like studying, they don't want 100 points, don't want toys rewarded by parents, they just want to have fun every day.

Leo

Childlikeness Index: 70%

The childlike side of Leos is hidden deep within their hearts, even they themselves may find it hard to discover. Yes, they are heroists, working hard to win the praise and respect of those around them, isn't this similar to how children seek the affection of adults?

Capricorn

Childlikeness Index: 20

Capricorns usually give people the impression of being serious and proper, outsiders rarely see them make cute expressions or say funny things. They are steady and down-to-earth, liking to be alone in thought, having a very typical old scholar character. The only possibility for Capricorns to show their innocent side is in front of their parents, where Capricorns will take off their serious mask, smilingly chatting with their parents and joking around.

Aquarius

Childlikeness Index: 90

Aquarius are a group of curious babies, also a group of capricious children. They are independent, radical, and forward-thinking. They do many things based on only one standard: to make themselves feel happy. As long as they are willing, even on bustling streets, they will sing loudly along with the guitar of a wandering singer. People who can become close friends with Aquarius are normally the same as them, possessing a kind of absurd spirit and the ability to entertain themselves.

Pisces

Childlikeness Index: 98

Dreamy Pisces often suffer greatly due to the contradiction between reality and dreams. They are emotionally rich, yet extremely fragile. Among the twelve zodiac signs, they can be said to have the heaviest dependence. In front of people they feel they can depend on, they are like very obedient good children, peaceful and compliant. Sometimes they will act coquettishly, doing small actions to attract others' attention, occasionally playing small pranks, making people both angry and loving. Like children, they cry the easiest and laugh the easiest.
