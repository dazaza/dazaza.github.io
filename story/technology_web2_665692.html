Foshan kiln industry troubled by "labor shortage" while working overtime during the Spring Festival

by qqfgskkgfec1 on 2012-02-15 08:46:17

The Foshan Kiln Industry Faces "Labor Shortage" During the Spring Festival Overtime - Construction Ceramics - Industry News - Building Materials Network

The Spring Festival holiday has traditionally been the peak season for new kiln construction and installation, as well as old kiln technology renovation and maintenance in the Foshan kiln industry. However, the early appearance of a "labor shortage" phenomenon this year is worrying. Many experienced kiln bosses from Huanggang, Hubei, told reporters that due to the inability to recruit skilled "second-generation kiln workers" during the Spring Festival holiday, they had to turn down some technology renovation and maintenance projects that could recover funds faster than building new kilns, which was very regrettable.

Huanggang, Hubei, is the manufacturing base for China's architectural sanitary ceramics kiln industry. For more than 20 years, over 100,000 people have been engaged in kiln manufacturing, construction installation, and technology renovation and maintenance work, becoming the main force in China's kiln manufacturing and repair industry. They are renowned domestically and internationally for their hardworking team spirit.

Due to the deeply rooted traditional concept of "whether rich or poor, go home for the New Year," especially with the opening of the Wuhan-Guangzhou high-speed railway this year, most "second-generation kiln workers" from Huanggang can reach their hometown in just 3.5 hours by train. Additionally, since old kiln maintenance is dirty and labor-intensive, some "second-generation kiln workers" who pursue "decent" jobs lack the hard-fighting spirit of their predecessors. Therefore, even overtime wages several times higher than usual during the Spring Festival holiday cannot retain many "second-generation kiln workers." Only by fostering a team spirit that keeps up with the times and creating the warmth of an enterprise family, such as having a New Year's Eve dinner, giving red envelopes, holding lottery events, and celebrating together during the Spring Festival, can employees and their hearts be retained.

In general, dozens of well-known kiln enterprises in the Greater Foshan area radiate to the domestic market and can be divided into three categories according to the market.

The first category consists of export-oriented enterprises represented by Modena, Zhongpeng, Amy, Zhongci, etc., focusing on overall kiln manufacturing. They transport numbered sections of kiln chambers via containers to Southeast Asian countries like Vietnam, India, Malaysia, and regions in the Middle East, then assemble and debug them on-site, light the fire, warm the kiln, and trial-produce. These enterprises are exemplary in factory-based overall manufacturing and on-site rapid installation. Their advantages include fewer on-site installation personnel and high efficiency. However, the manufacturing cost is relatively high, making it difficult for domestic small and medium-sized ceramic and sanitary ware enterprises to accept. These factory-manufactured kiln enterprises have relatively stable employment, with most people taking about 10 days off during the Spring Festival, leaving only 30% of mechatronics maintenance personnel to complete customers' technology renovation and maintenance requirements during the holiday.

The second category of kiln enterprises focuses on the domestic market and are also the enterprises with the most shortages of staff during the Spring Festival holidays. Retaining employees for overtime work is the top priority for these companies. As the main force in constructing domestic kilns, their work mainly involves on-site construction, with heavy end-of-year technology renovation and maintenance tasks. However, the capital recovery is fast, reducing the risk of building new kilns on credit, and the profit margin is relatively high. Therefore, undertaking technology renovation and maintenance tasks is a must. Enterprises like Foshan Xin Xinda Kiln, Zhongxin Kiln, Auspicious Kiln, Chuangci Kiln, and Yashen Kiln are all technically proficient and financially strong well-known enterprises. Among them, bosses like Gu Yongming, Wu Ziweng, and Wu Zhiyong have long stayed in Foshan, continuously working overtime with their employees during the Spring Festival for over 10 years, setting examples in solving the "labor shortage." Every New Year's Eve, they lead middle and senior-level managers to have a New Year's Eve dinner with the overtime employees; stay up until midnight to distribute lucky money, take one day off on the first day of the New Year for a lottery celebration, making the "sons of the soil" from Huanggang feel the warmth of the enterprise family, fostering an atmosphere of "dedication, joy in work, achievement in career, valuing talent, and gathering talent," thus implementing the scientific development concept centered on people. The brilliant corporate culture will surely yield more bountiful economic benefits.

The third category comprises specialized supporting kiln parts enterprises, represented by Sanshui Shanmo Inorganic Materials Co., Ltd., and Foshan Qishun Ceramic Machinery Co., Ltd. The excellent products made by Shanmo Company, such as kiln spray gun quality nozzle bricks, bridge bricks, high-precision roller hole bricks, and the roller bearings made by Qishun Machinery, are perfect complements and highly favored by manufacturers producing wide-body kilns with inner widths exceeding 3 meters. Due to their regular overtime work throughout the year, these enterprises can enjoy normal holidays during the Spring Festival without being troubled by the "labor shortage."
