Beixin Group aids in building the first new type of earthquake-resistant and energy-saving light steel structure project

by dbfgskkgfen6 on 2012-02-15 10:46:10

North China Group aids in the construction of its first new type of earthquake-resistant and energy-saving light steel structure project - Corporate Dynamics - Industry News - Building Materials Network. After the massive earthquake in Yushu, Qinghai, to help restore and reconstruct the Yushu area, China National Building Materials Group Corporation (shortened as "China Building Materials Group") communicated with relevant departments such as the Housing and Urban-Rural Development Department of Qinghai Province and determined a plan to donate the construction of two villager comprehensive activity centers covering an area of more than 700 square meters for the Zhangu Village and Gand Village in Yushu County. The group assigned North China Building Materials (Group) Co., Ltd. (shortened as "North China Group"), a key enterprise under their umbrella that had previously undertaken reconstruction tasks after the Wenchuan earthquake, to organize and implement this project.

On October 27, photos were taken at the Gand construction site. This project adopts a new type of earthquake-resistant and energy-saving housing system recommended by the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development. It mainly consists of wall panels, floor panels, roof panels, and maintenance structures made from light steel structures. The structural weight is light, only 30% of traditional brick-concrete structures, making it suitable for permanent housing construction in earthquake-prone areas. After the completion of the two villager comprehensive activity centers, they will become the first project in Qinghai province post-Yushu earthquake to use a new type of earthquake-resistant and energy-saving light steel structure system.

After receiving the aid construction task deployment from China Building Materials Group, North China Group quickly established an aid construction work leading group headed by General Manager Cui Lijun and a project implementation group headed by Vice-General Manager Liu Gui Ping, swiftly commencing the aid construction work. On September 6, the Yushu aid construction project team led by Vice-General Manager Zhao Yanming rushed to Yushu; on September 14, the project officially started.

Workers are busily engaged in construction. At the forefront of the aid construction, facing the harsh reality of thin air on the plateau, severe climate conditions, short effective construction time, and a shortage of construction personnel, the project team scientifically formulated a construction plan, coordinated the arrangement of project progress, and determined the work goal of "working hard for fifty days to complete the project on time." To ensure project progress, members of the project team ate and stayed at the construction site. Regardless of extreme weather like rain and snow or holidays like Mid-Autumn Festival and National Day, each day's working hours amounted to sixteen or seventeen hours. Each member of the project team took on multiple roles: transportation, loading and unloading, negotiations, material procurement, construction management, on-site supervision, communication coordination... Under their efforts, all works proceeded in an orderly and rapid manner.

At the rear in Beijing, both China Building Materials Group and North China Group were creating all favorable conditions for the Yushu project. To ensure timely supply of materials, the staff of North China Integrated Housing worked overtime during the National Day holiday, completing the production of all dragon bones and accessories for the project as well as the receipt and shipment of externally purchased building materials. To solve the problem of a shortage of technical workers on the front line, the project team urgently dispatched technical backbone personnel from places like Sichuan, who quickly rushed to the Yushu project front line... Company leaders at all levels attached great importance to this donation construction project. Chairman Song Zhiping and General Manager Yao Yan of China Building Materials Group repeatedly inquired about the progress of the project, requiring the Zen Gu and Gan Da projects to be built into sample demonstration projects for the post-earthquake restoration and reconstruction in Yushu, establishing a good image for central enterprises. Vice-General Manager Liu Baoying of China Building Materials Group, General Manager Cui Lijun and Vice-General Manager Liu Gui Ping of North China Group also went to the earthquake-stricken area, consoling the staff and inspecting and guiding the project.

Through the joint efforts of the front and rear, members of the project team overcame numerous difficulties, lasting one and a half months, and the main construction of the two villager comprehensive activity center houses has been basically completed. The project has entered the tight and orderly interior and exterior decoration stage. Currently, Yushu has entered winter, with even worse climate conditions, making construction more difficult. However, the project team still remains steadfast on the front line in Yushu, ensuring the completion of the project by mid-November.

In this aid construction, North China Group fully considered the actual situation of Tibetan areas, designing the villager comprehensive activity center as a bungalow compound-style building. In the middle is a sunlit atrium-style meeting hall, separately equipped with public facilities such as a village-level medical room, a library reading room, a party activity center, a police office, a cultural activity room, an office area, and a warehouse. Due to the adoption of North China integrated housing, which has an internationally advanced level light steel structure system, the villager comprehensive activity center performs excellently in aspects such as energy saving, environmental protection, and earthquake resistance, conforming to the national low-carbon economic policy requirements, and will have a positive demonstration effect on local housing construction. This system house has several advantages: excellent earthquake resistance, capable of withstanding a magnitude 9 earthquake; high building sealing performance, small indoor temperature difference, good energy-saving effect, and high comfort level; environmentally friendly materials, safe for human health; all components are galvanized treated, with good durability, and the structural service life can reach over 70 years. Additionally, since this system adopts factory production, it realizes component factory prefabrication and on-site assembly construction, shortening the construction period, reducing noise, dust, and garbage, greatly reducing the ecological load locally, satisfying the national 65% energy-saving standard. Not only that, this system house can also increase the effective usable area by 8% to 13%, conforming to the national land resource policy and sustainable development strategy.

Qinghai Province's Yushu disaster area reconstruction commander-in-chief, Deputy Governor Ma Shunqing, along with leaders from the Housing and Urban-Rural Development Department, the Prefecture Construction Bureau, and other levels of leadership successively visited the construction site to inspect and guide the work, giving high praise to the project.
