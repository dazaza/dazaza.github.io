What criteria should we use to build a website?

by hebei56cn02c on 2012-02-12 17:23:06

I. Website Style/Creativity

Style is abstract. It refers to the overall impression that a website gives to its visitors. This overall image includes layout, color schemes, typography, and browsing methods, etc. Every website leaves different impressions on people. What we need to achieve here is to create a unique style for the website based on its positioning, as seen in Shijiazhuang's website construction packages. In addition, we need to ensure consistency in style, which is no different from traditional printed publications. All images, text, including background colors, dividing lines, fonts, titles, footnotes, etc., on the web pages should have a unified style that runs throughout the entire site. This way, users will feel comfortable and smooth, leaving them with an impression of "professionalism."

II. Website Logo

Logo, by definition, is the emblematic graphic of a site (Shijiazhuang website creation). The most important function of a logo is to convey the website's positioning and business philosophy graphically, while also being easy to recognize. Generally, there are three approaches to designing a website logo: 1. Directly using the website URL as the logo. 2. Designing the logo based on the characteristics of the products/services offered by the website. 3. Featuring the transmission of the website operator's business philosophy.

III. Visual Flow

When people read information, their vision naturally follows a certain flow habit — what they see first, second, and so on (Shijiazhuang website optimization). Psychological studies show that general browsing habits are from top to bottom, left to right. Similarly, the left side feels more relaxed while the right side feels more stable. Therefore, the visual impact on a plane is stronger at the top than at the bottom, and stronger on the left than on the right (Shijiazhuang website construction). Thus, the upper part and the upper-middle part of the plane are called the "optimal viewing area," or the best location (Shijiazhuang website SEO). In web design, prominent or recommended information is usually placed in this position. Of course, this visual flow is just a sensation, not a fixed formula. As long as it conforms to people's psychological order and logical sequence, it can be used more flexibly in web design, directly affecting the accuracy and effectiveness of information delivery.

IV. Webpage Framework and Layout

Webpage layouts can generally be divided into types such as "Square" shape, corner type, "T" shape, "L" shape, comprehensive framework type, Flash type, and variant type. Actually, when we are doing design, we don't overly consider what form to use. For example, if we see a circular object on a piece of paper, it's easy to associate it with the sun, while others might think of the moon, etc... These are all form metaphors. We believe the most important thing is to capture the client's needs, grasp the website's positioning, and create a reasonable framework and layout.
