The battles in the Champions League are in full swing. In the focal match on the Champions League field at 00:00 Beijing time on April 13, Manchester United defeated Chelsea 2-1 at home at Old Trafford. Giggs assisted Hernandez to take the lead first, Ramires was sent off after accumulating two yellow cards, substitute Drogba once drew level, and Giggs assisted Park Ji-sung to score the winning goal. Manchester United advanced to the semi-finals with a total score of 3-1.

Manchester United won for the first time away from home against Chelsea in 9 years in the first leg, with Rooney scoring the decisive goal. This was Chelsea's 101st Champions League match. Since their first encounter in the second division in 1905, this was the 160th meeting between the two sides in history. Manchester United had previously won 68 games, drawn 47, and lost 44, holding an advantage. Chelsea was looking forward to entering the Champions League semi-finals for the 6th time in 8 years. The Blues had advanced 4 times in the previous 6 two-legged knockout ties in European competitions against English teams, with the two eliminated opponents both being Liverpool. Manchester United was also looking forward to advancing to the Champions League semi-final for the 4th time in 5 years, and they remained undefeated at home in all competitions this season.
