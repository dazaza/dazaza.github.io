Chapter 1, Section 1.4 Practice Questions

Fill-in-the-blank questions:

(1) Unlike the U.S.'s commerce-driven e-commerce, China's e-commerce tends more towards technology-driven. From the perspective of e-commerce, the U.S.'s e-commerce is a passive development model, while China's e-commerce is an active development model.

(2) Online store opening is a typical narrow-sense e-commerce model, but due to its low threshold, simple operation, ease of acceptance and promotion, it has higher recognition domestically than broad-sense e-commerce models, gradually becoming a dominant form of e-commerce in our country.

Answer questions:

(1) What is e-commerce? What are the prominent features of e-commerce?

Answer: Online store opening is a typical narrow-sense e-commerce model, but due to its low threshold, simple operation, easy acceptance and promotion, it has higher recognition domestically than broad-sense e-commerce models, gradually becoming a dominant form of e-commerce in our country. Characteristics: (1) Reduce costs and improve efficiency. (2) No restrictions on time and location. (3) Fair use of information resources. (4) Reduce intermediate transaction links. (5) Enhance interactivity between buyers and sellers. (6) Fast information transmission speed.

(2) What are the six characteristics of the new commercial civilization? What are the five revolutions brought by the new commercial civilization?

Answer: The new commercial civilization in the Internet age has the following six characteristics: transparency, integrity, equality, sharing, responsibility, harmony. The five revolutions brought by the new commercial civilization include: revolution in sales mode, production mode, consumption mode, employment mode, lifestyle.

(3) Compared to traditional retail, what are the differences and advantages of online retail?

Answer: Compared to traditional retail methods, online retail has unique advantages: 1. Word-of-mouth advantage 2. Information advantage 3. Communication advantage 4. Tool advantage.

Answer questions:

(1) What form does the Alipay account take? What are the authentication methods?

Answer: Alipay is founded by Aliwangwang Group, the largest independent third-party payment platform domestically, providing "simple, secure, fast" online payment solutions specifically for Chinese e-commerce. Alipay accounts are presented in email format. Authentication: Alipay authentication is divided into personal authentication and merchant authentication. Merchant authentication requires submission of business licenses, company accounts, etc., whereas personal authentication requires simpler documentation—just submitting an ID number and bank account details to process, once passed, buyer eligibility is granted, specific authentication procedures.

(2) How many types of Alipay transaction statuses are there? What does each status represent?

Answer: There are two types—"Buyer has paid, waiting for seller to ship," and "Seller has shipped, waiting for buyer to confirm." Transaction progress: After the buyer pays Alipay, the funds are placed on hold, and the system automatically notifies the seller via email of this payment information. At this point, the transaction status shows "Buyer has paid, waiting for seller to ship." Under this status, the seller needs to fulfill their shipping obligation promptly. The seller should actively modify the transaction status and fill in correct shipping information to confirm shipment. At this point, the system displays the transaction status as "Seller has shipped, waiting for buyer to confirm."

(3) What are the three types of evaluations on Taobao? How are points calculated for each type?

Answer: Evaluations are divided into "Positive," "Neutral," and "Negative" reviews, each corresponding to a certain number of points. Users gain one point for each "Positive" review, receive no points for a "Neutral" review, and lose one point for a "Negative" review.

(4) How is the risk of goods being lost or damaged determined to be borne by whom?

Answer: Before the buyer signs for receipt, the risk is borne by the seller; after the buyer signs for receipt, the risk is borne by the buyer.

Answer questions:

(1) Understanding and utilizing product characteristics should be divided into which three stages to progressively enhance? Please explain the capabilities at each stage in detail.

Answer: Product nature, product features, product benefits. Features: At a minimum, one must understand the material composition, size specifications, and applicable range of products. Knowing these product characteristics enables answering simple customer inquiries, such as the fabric of clothes, the basic raw materials used in food, and the suitability of the product for specific people or situations... Typically, product features to some extent represent advantages compared to similar products, such as more breathable and sweat-absorbing fabrics, safer foods without additives, multi-functional products, unique and personalized feelings, limited editions, and novel gifts... If the commercial advantages of the product cannot be effectively converted into customer benefits, customers will not be easily swayed during sales, because purchasing products is a professional quality and ability that a sales expert should possess.

(2) What steps usually compose the online reception process for online stores?

Answer: Greeting: Polite and courteous, when Wangwang prompts that a customer is consulting, greet before asking and answering questions. Inquiry: Actively ask what the customer needs and see if help can be provided. Consultation: Answer customer inquiries objectively and professionally using industry knowledge, product knowledge, and life experience. Negotiation: For pricing issues, handle according to specific circumstances. If unable to accept price negotiation, politely refuse or redirect the customer's attention to buy-and-gift activities or full-price promotions, encouraging more consumption and implying that they can exchange higher spending for corresponding price discounts. Avoid hard attitudes and strong postures to prevent disputes. Recommendation: While answering inquiries about one product, consciously recommend more products to the customer, such as suggesting other products, promoting sale items, recommending the latest styles, or introducing store shopping policies. Assistance: After the customer purchases the product, help modify freight or guide them through online payment. Verification: Confirm the customer's payment and verify the shopping list and delivery address with Wangwang. If the customer has special requests or needs to change the address, note it in the memo promptly. Farewell: Politely bid farewell. For customers who have already shopped, you can wish for pleasant cooperation and ask them to wait patiently for delivery. If problems arise, they can contact you anytime. For customers who haven't immediately purchased, wish them happy shopping and sincerely express your willingness to serve them. If necessary, add them as Wangwang friends for future customer management and follow-up.

(3) What are the various transaction states for Alipay transactions? What do these states mean respectively?

Answer: 1.Waiting for buyer's payment 2.Buyer has paid 3.Buyer has shipped 4.Transaction successful 5.Refund transaction. Timely verify order content and delivery address with the customer. If the customer chooses express shipping and the delivery address belongs to a remote small town, it is generally 30 days for registered mail, 10 days for express, and 3 days for virtual items.

(4) On which disk should chat software be installed? Why?

Answer: Installing chat software on a non-system disk is an effective way to preserve records. For example, installing Aliwangwang on the D, E, or F drive of your computer ensures that even if the operating system encounters problems and needs reinstallation, only the C drive will be formatted, sparing the chat tools installed on other hard drives. Thus, chat records can continue to exist and can be shared with colleagues.

(3) What in-store marketing tools does Taobao provide?

Answer: The store sign is the customer's first impression of the store. Taobao places each store's sign at the golden ratio line on the first screen, aiming to deliver the store's business content, advantages, selling points, and brand value to those browsing the store through visual design as quickly as possible. Therefore, the sign plays a crucial role in visual marketing. Wangpu can set up to six customizable pages. These quick-access shortcuts for customizable pages are located below the store sign, essentially multiplying the valuable first screen space by six times. Therefore, reasonably utilizing customizable pages can convey more marketing-related information to customers on the first screen, effectively promoting sales. The store's promotion area is the second visual focus that customers see after the store sign and customizable pages. Since the quick-access shortcuts for customizable pages are text-based, if the design of the promotion area is eye-catching enough, it may surpass the customizable pages above it in terms of visual and profitability impact. A store typically has hundreds or even thousands of products. Without reasonable categorization, customers may find it difficult to locate the items they need, making the shopping experience akin to "treasure hunting" in the sea. Therefore, reasonably setting up and optimizing product categories not only facilitates customer searches but also significantly increases product views and sales. Regular stores can display a maximum of 20 products per page, with product rankings based on the proximity of end times, executed according to predetermined design standards by the system, unchangeable by anyone. However, the home page of Wangpu offers more autonomy in product display, allowing merchants to set up displays according to their needs, thus providing ample design space and flexibility for in-store marketing, making product displays as flexible as shelves in physical stores. Compared to regular stores, Wangpu has another significant feature: the ability to expand the store's display area according to needs, increasing the display area by adding customizable modules, releasing more promotional information, allowing consumers to linger, satisfying their desire to "wander" through the store, and ensuring that online stores are no less competitive in marketing strategies than traditional brick-and-mortar stores.
