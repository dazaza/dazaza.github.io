Notice on the Organization of National Vocational Qualification Training for Personnel Inspecting Physical Properties of Materials

by judy0501 on 2009-09-19 14:11:37

All enterprises, institutions and relevant personnel:

In accordance with the spirit of the "Labor Law of the People's Republic of China" and the "Regulations on the Employment of Skilled Workers" issued by the Ministry of Labor and Social Security (Ministerial Order No. 6), to further implement the national occupational qualification certificate system, gradually incorporating the assessment of material physical property inspectors into the national occupational qualification management system, and realizing the national requirement for material physical property inspectors to work with valid certificates. To reduce the burden on enterprises and not affect the work of material physical property inspectors, the training and assessment will mainly be self-study based, combined with face-to-face instruction. Entrusted by the Vocational Skills Appraisal Guidance Center of the National Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau, China Machinery Experts Network regularly holds national vocational qualification classes for material physical property inspectors in accordance with the requirements of the assessment outline. Specific arrangements are as follows:

1. Training targets: Personnel who conduct inspections, checks, tests, and experiments on the mechanical properties of metal, polymer, and other materials (personnel using material inspection instruments and equipment to inspect, check, test, and experiment on the physical, mechanical, and mechanical properties of finished products, semi-finished products, and raw materials of metal, non-metal, polymer, and other materials; personnel using material inspection instruments and equipment to test the tensile strength, torsional strength, impact strength, bending, fatigue, hardness, conductivity, and other physical, chemical, and mechanical properties of materials).

2. Vocational qualification appraisal is divided into three levels:初级 (primary), 中级 (intermediate), 高级 (advanced).

3. Textbooks and Assessment Methods: Uniformly adopting the professional textbooks for quality and technical supervision industry skill training recognized by the Ministry of Labor and Social Security for training and employment; the assessment consists of two parts: theoretical knowledge and skill operation. The theoretical part is assessed through written exams, while skill operation is assessed through practical operations (or written exams).

4. Certificate Issuance: Those who pass both the theoretical exam and skill operation will be issued the corresponding level of the national "Vocational Qualification Certificate" jointly by the Ministry of Labor and Social Security and the Vocational Skills Appraisal Center of the National Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau. This certificate serves as a qualification voucher for the vocational skills level of material physical property inspection staff, a basis for employers to hire, use, and determine salary treatment, and an effective document for legal certification for overseas employment and labor export of Chinese citizens.
