"San Jie Qian Kun" Farming System In-Depth Analysis: The Beloved of Casual Players

by xiny177 on 2012-02-28 10:16:54

Web games are a leisure and entertainment option for many players to relieve stress and relax in their daily lives. The turn-based RPG web game "Sanjie Qiankun" has specially designed a function-rich farm gameplay for casual gamers, and through various detail designs, truly integrates the farm gameplay into the game, allowing casual players to find the most suitable entertainment method within the game.

Planting vegetables, stealing vegetables, and cooking - none of these can be missed. The classic "Happy Farm" gameplay of planting and stealing vegetables has been completely replicated. In the farm of "Sanjie Qiankun", players can plant dozens of different types of vegetables and fruits themselves, pick ripe fruits from friends' farms, or help friends weed, water, and kill pests.

In addition, players can also process the harvested fruits in the workshop and make them into dishes or delicious pastries. Fish-flavored eggplant, scrambled eggs with tomatoes, lobster shrimp... both Eastern and Western cuisines are available!

Building a farm is a long and arduous task. The farm in "Sanjie Qiankun" has a variety of facilities, including main house, warehouse, workshop, doghouse, waterwheel and other 8 different types of facility buildings, as well as a special money tree that allows players to "shake" it every day to obtain prop rewards such as fertilizer, toys, and dog food. By upgrading the buildings, the efficiency of each building facility can be improved, or the output of agricultural products can be increased.

For example: upgrading the scarecrow can increase the yield of crops; upgrading the warehouse can increase the number of storage slots; upgrading the workshop can increase the number of recipes. Among them, the level of the main house is a prerequisite for the level-up of other buildings.

The farm gameplay in "Sanjie Qiankun" is not an independent mini-game module, but an integral part of the entire game. The agricultural products harvested on the farm can be taken out from the home warehouse by talking to the NPC [Home Manager] in the capital and used to synthesize forging materials or refine various medicines in the alchemy furnace.

In addition, the experience points obtained by players when planting, harvesting, and helping friends water and exterminate pests on the farm will be stored and can also be taken out at the [Home Manager].
