Tuyuan is a photo community. This community focuses on people, you and the ones you care about and pay attention to in real life - your fellow travelers.

Perhaps you are like us, not yet an expert photographer, but from time to time use a digital camera to take some pictures - group photos at friend gatherings, family reunion snapshots, or travel photos with "I was here" messages. The photos we take may not be technically professional, but they are what we cherish most. They record the days we've walked together, the places we've been, the sunshine and storms we've experienced along the way - these are our shared sweet or bitter memories. What matters most in these memories are the people, the family and friends in the photos, the classmates and colleagues, the fellow travelers who have met and parted ways with us on our journey of life.

Tuyuan can recognize the people in the photos. So the photos on Tuyuan are no longer just digital files, but lively records of life with human information. Browsing and sharing photos on Tuyuan becomes a stroll through the photos and the people in them - you can easily share photos by person, easily find all the photos of the people you care about, photos of yourself taken by others, or group photos of you and your friends.

Come to Tuyuan, where you can safely and conveniently store and manage your photos, and easily view photos by person.

Come to Tuyuan, where you can easily find yourself in other people's photos, and see group photos of you and your loved ones.

Come to Tuyuan, where you can easily customize your sharing, and freely share your photos with different friends in your life.

Come to Tuyuan, where you can create a Tuyuan community for your real-life circle of friends, share photos, and discuss topics.

What services does Tuyuan provide?

Photo upload and storage service

Registered users have 1G of free space and can upload photos through the web or Tuyuan client, and store and manage photos on Tuyuan.

Automatic "find people" and "recognize people"

After photos are uploaded, Tuyuan will automatically detect the faces in the photos. After you label a few faces of your friends, Tuyuan will automatically "recognize" this friend in other new photos, allowing you to quickly label and then easily view photos by person.

Customized sharing

Tuyuan has powerful permission settings functions, you can freely and securely share albums with any specified person, of course, you can also share with all relatives and friends or completely open to everyone.

Tuyuan will continuously listen to your needs and develop new features. We welcome your feedback and suggestions.
