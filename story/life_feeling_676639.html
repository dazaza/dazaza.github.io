One day by chance, the boy met the girl online. He didn't know how it happened, but the girl's QQ number was among his good friend's contacts. It was just too coincidental. When the boy was chatting with his good buddy, he accidentally sent a message to the girl:

"Hey bro, what are you doing!" Soon after, the girl replied: "Bro, I'm not doing anything. But I'm a girl!"

"Haha, sorry, I sent it by mistake," the boy replied sheepishly, blaming himself for being so careless.

"Bro, can't you even tell the difference between a man and a woman?" The girl teased him provocatively.

The boy found the girl interesting, and thus they chatted more and more enthusiastically. As a result, chatting seemed to become their daily required course. They chatted about everything - life, hobbies, work, gains and losses every day, sharing their happiness and unhappiness! Thus, they maintained this relationship for several months...

One day while chatting, the girl said to the boy: "I want to see what you look like." The boy replied: "Don't bother, I am so ugly that you might be disappointed!" The girl humorously said: "It doesn't matter, I am also ugly, so neither of us will be disappointed!" The boy said: "Alright, hope we won't disappoint each other!" The video chat was opened, but the "buffering" made these two people who had never met face-to-face have accelerated heartbeats. The short few seconds felt like they waited for several centuries! Finally, they saw each other, and they were indeed both very disappointed - originally, the boy wasn't as ugly as he said; on the contrary, the boy was sunny and handsome. Similarly, the girl was also beautiful and indeed very cute. The girl looked at the boy and said: "You tricked me!"

"Aren't you also tricking me?" The boy humorously said, and the two laughed in unison!

Every day, he would wake up early and stay by the computer, watching whether the girl's QQ was still shining. Many times, the girl wasn't there, and the boy felt a bit disappointed, but he was also reassured: "Probably because we chatted too late yesterday, this little lazy bug is still sound asleep!" So he kept waiting, as if he were standing by the girl's bedside, looking at her sleeping face with a smile still lingering on her lips from last night...

Finally, the familiar QQ avatar flashed on the boy's desktop. The boy couldn't wait to open it: "Dear, you seem to be in such high spirits?"

"Hehe, dear, because I miss you!" The boy joked.

"Really?"

"It's definitely not cooked," the boy seemed to be quite good at making jokes.

"Do you like me?" The girl asked seriously.

"Do you like me?" The boy counter-asked, as he didn't dare to say what was in his heart!

"Hehe, the possibility between us is very small!" The girl answered reservedly!

The boy always said: "No problem, it's still early!" Finally, under the girl's repeated pressure - or rather, under the boy's understanding, the boy finally turned off his most precious computer and went to sleep. Each time, the boy felt that time was so short, feeling that there was not enough time. How much time would be considered long? How much time would be enough for him........

The next day, the boy woke up early as usual, wanting to see the girl sooner, but the girl disappointed him again. Still, the boy waited quietly... An entire morning passed, and the girl didn't come. "This little lazy bug really sleeps a lot," the boy started to get a bit anxious........ An entire afternoon passed, and the girl was still not there. That day, the boy spent it in painful waiting and longing.

The third day, the girl was still not there......

The fourth day, the boy was still disappointed.......

For several days, the girl hadn't shown up. Where did she go? Did she find a boyfriend? Was she just out playing for a few days? A series of speculations appeared in the boy's mind....... While lost in thought, the boy suddenly saw the familiar QQ image flashing. It was her, really her, she had finally come. The boy gathered all his longing for the girl over these days into his hands. He clicked on QQ forcefully, as if strength could make him see the girl's information faster. But when he used all his strength to open QQ, he froze: "Dear, I tried not to log in, tried not to think about you, tried to forget you, but I couldn't do it. Originally, I really love you." The girl's words made the boy pause for a long time in front of the computer. Like waking up from a dream, he then wrote a poem he had long wanted to give to the girl:

Hard to express my love in my heart!

Speechless, hard to distinguish,

Destiny has its own reasons!
