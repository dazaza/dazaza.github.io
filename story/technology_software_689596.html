Ten years ago, a man surnamed Huang from Zhengzhou was killed in Xin'an County, Luoyang. Half a year later, one of the main criminals, Lan Chengxiang, was captured.

When Huang woke up, he promised to call his family and give them 200,000 yuan. Unexpectedly, Huang's younger brother had already reported to the Lulin Police Station that his brother was missing. After the extortion call was made, Huang's family never gave the money as required.

In early 2002, Xiao Xuanzhi worked as a waitress in a roadside small restaurant in Lankao County. Huang often came to the small restaurant to eat, and Xiao became Huang's mistress after a while.

"Li Jicheng!" The arresting police officer hiding in the staircase loudly shouted out the name of the man who looked around 30 years old. It was already winter in Xiangfan in November, but the man in front sweated profusely and suddenly aged 10 years.

After understanding the relationship between the two, Li Jicheng was very angry and also wanted to give Huang a lesson. Li and Xiao rented a house near the railway station in Xin'an County, Luoyang in April 2002 and lured Huang to Xin'an County under the pretext of discussing business after learning that Huang always wanted to do brass business.

The three people were furious and afraid that Huang would report them after leaving, so they killed Huang with kitchen knives, fruit knives, and axes in the house, dismembered the body, packed it into several jute bags, and buried it beside a sewage ditch hundreds of meters away.

Police officers Wei Xiaowei and Chen Lei urgently went to Xiangfan. After a period of investigation, they found the whereabouts of "Wang Lan" with the help of the public.

Through the cooperation of local police officers, the pursuing police officers found Xiao Xuanzhi's ex-husband who was farming in Caidian District, Wuhan. According to him, Xiao Xuanzhi still had contact with her daughter. The police settled down in Wuhan and finally found Xiao Xuanzhi's daughter in Jingmen City. According to Xiao Xuanzhi's daughter, Xiao Xuanzhi now calls herself "Wang Lan", roughly in Xiangfan, but she did not know the exact address.

Originally, she co-founded a small guesthouse in a small county in Xiangfan. Her name was relatively special: "Qin Feng Guesthouse", which sounds like a person's name.

And "Qin" and "Feng" are precisely the names of Xiao Xuanzhi's pair of children.

Under the circumstances, the investigating police officers let the property management cut off his electricity. Li Jicheng was extremely cautious and opened and closed the door twice continuously. Half an hour later, he walked out cautiously and was caught by the police at the doorstep.

Stepping on two boats planted the seeds of trouble.

Originally, after killing someone, Li Jicheng took Xiao Xuanzhi to Dengzhou. After a period of time, Li Jicheng began to dislike Xiao Xuanzhi and met a girl in her twenties on the train to Zhejiang to do car parts business, so he dumped Xiao Xuanzhi.

After interacting with the 20-something girlfriend Huang Xiaoyan, Huang Xiaoyan discovered that Li Jicheng kept changing residences and realized that he might have some trouble, so she left him.

This "sugar sister" surnamed Liu is an employee of Wuhan Railway Bureau, over 40 years old. She not only gave Li Jicheng the family building allocated by her unit to live in but also got him a free national railway travel pass.

After Li Jicheng found out that the police were investigating, this "sugar sister" rented him a high-rise apartment.

Helpless, Li Jicheng boarded a train to Zhejiang to wholesale car parts again. This time, he met a "sugar sister".

Huang, who was in Lankao, dealt in timber business and had some savings. Xiao Xuanzhi told Li Jicheng about Huang's identity and said that they could kidnap Huang and ask his family for some money.

Yesterday, facing the reporter, Xiao Xuanzhi expressed her dissatisfaction with Li Jicheng: "I love and hate him. Now that I've been caught, he can't keep enjoying himself outside!"
