Longyou Chibi Hot Spring Resort is located in Chibi City, Hubei Province, 5 kilometers away from the city center. It is 110 kilometers north to Wuhan, 70 kilometers south to Yueyang, 210 kilometers to Changsha, and 40 kilometers west to the ancient battlefield of Chibi. To the east, it neighbors the famous Lu Lake Scenic Area. Longyou Chibi Hot Spring Resort is nestled at the foot of Wuhong Mountain with beautiful scenery. The hot spring of Wuhong Mountain has been there since ancient times, and it is said that Emperor Shun once stayed here during his southern tour. His two concubines, Ehuang and Nüying, traced him here and eventually reached Junshan Island in Dongting Lake. They were later deified as the goddesses of Xiang River.

The scenic spots include Chibi Longyou Hot Spring, where visitors can enjoy the natural beauty of the surroundings. Chibi City, where the Longyou Chibi Resort is located, boasts picturesque mountains and rivers and convenient transportation, making it a tourist destination in Central China. The resort is strategically located near major transportation routes, including the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway, National Highway 107, and the Jingzhu Expressway. Nearby attractions include the ancient battlefield of Chibi, which is listed as a national key cultural relic protection unit, renowned for its numerous historical sites; the Lu Lake Scenic Area with clear waters and lush islands, where CCTV built the second external shooting base for the TV series "Water Margin" called "Water Margin City," and is currently developing the national-level Lu Lake Tourist Resort; the precious medical mineral spring of Longyou Chibi Hot Spring, praised by experts as the "Divine Spring" for bathing, drinking, and health treatment; the vast clay dam of Lu Reservoir, known as the largest in Asia; the magnificent and marvelously crafted "Underground Dragon Palace" caves such as Xuan Su Cave and Shenlong Cave; the cool summer retreat of Xuefeng Mountain; and the picturesque natural landscapes of ten thousand mu tea gardens and kiwi orchards. These attractions form a poetic and picturesque panorama, creating an ideal place for sightseeing, leisure, and vacationing.

Chibi Longyou Hot Spring's water source comes from five self-flowing wells under Wuhong Mountain, with a daily flow rate of over 5,000 tons. The spring water gushes out from more than 200 meters underground through artificial drilling, with an outlet temperature of 62 degrees Celsius. Under the high-temperature and high-pressure conditions deep in the earth's layers and the mineralization effect of rare metals, the minerals in the water are all in ionized or gaseous states. The water quality is bright and clear, sterile, non-toxic, and free of sediment. Bathing in it makes the skin smooth and refreshing, providing an excellent bathing experience. According to tests conducted by the Environmental Health Monitoring Institute of the Chinese Academy of Preventive Medicine, the mineralization degree of Longyou Chibi Hot Spring is 1312 mg/L, with a pH value of 7.3, containing over 30 essential trace elements and compounds needed by the human body. Based on the standards for natural drinking mineral water and medical mineral water in China, its lithium, strontium, fluoride, and silicic acid content and mineralization degree meet the naming boundary indicators for natural drinking mineral water and medical mineral water. Its water chemical type belongs to the silicic acid-containing, radioactive radon, sulfate-calcium spring type, also known as a weakly radioactive radon sulfate-calcium spring. Compared with some famous springs at home and abroad, Longyou Chibi Hot Spring not only has a rich content of minerals but also a clever composition and appropriate proportions, making it a very rare and precious medical mineral spring.

After expert arguments, it has been proven that Longyou Chibi Hot Spring can not only treat common diseases such as arthritis and skin diseases but also effectively treat difficult diseases related to cardiovascular, nervous, digestive systems, metabolism, and endocrine, such as insufficient blood supply to the brain, bone hyperplasia, osteoporosis, and disordered digestive functions, with effects surpassing medication.

Longyou Chibi Hot Spring Resort integrates hotels, hot spring centers, and villas into a large-scale tourist resort. The main buildings highlight the Han-Tang style, novel and elegant, blending Eastern and Western elements harmoniously. The resort features stunning scenery, lush greenery, dense forests, tall bamboo, graceful osmanthus trees, shimmering lake waters of Bayong Lake, and the majestic and spiritual Wuhong Mountain. More than forty multifunctional and uniquely designed hot spring pools are scattered among the mountains and forests, creating picturesque scenes step by step, orderly arranged, and naturally integrated into the landscape. The overall design maintains the natural appearance, with hot springs flowing out from stone caves and winding through the lakeside and mountains. Soaking in the hot spring pools, one can feel the bubbles rising gently from beneath, gaze at the continuous green valleys, and enjoy the mist from the waterfall and the steam from the hot springs. Surrounded by waterfalls, green mountains, and blue skies, one can truly feel like being in a "paradise on earth." Here, you can temporarily forget the hustle and bustle of the world and enjoy a peaceful and relaxing time close to nature, experiencing the serene beauty of the surroundings while soaking in the hot springs.
