A farmer from Dao County, Hunan Province killed a police station chief with a hoe and seized a gun, injuring the chief of the justice bureau.

by ttykbss9 on 2011-08-25 12:28:15

Original URL: http://society.people.com.cn/GB/14638250.html?source=people_NEWS

A reporter learned from multiple sources and it was confirmed by the Hunan public security organs that a serious case of assaulting police officers occurred in Dao County, Hunan Province. The chief of a police station died in the line of duty, and the criminal was shot dead on the spot by the police when he seized the service pistol and fled while taking hostages.

At 11 o'clock on the 13th, Chief Yang Yongfei, Deputy Chief Guo Jianping of the Xianglinpu Town Police Station, and Director Zhou Juguang of the town's Justice Bureau went to Xiangyang Village in the town to mediate family disputes between villager Wang Huanyuan and his wife. During the mediation, Wang's wife reported to the police that Wang had stolen construction materials for the highway. Fearing that his crime would be exposed, Wang suddenly attacked Yang Yongfei and Guo Jianping with a pickaxe when the police were checking the stolen goods, causing Yang Yongfei to die on the spot and Guo Jianping to be injured. Subsequently, Wang Huanyuan seized Yang Yongfei's service pistol and shot Zhou Juguang before fleeing.

After the incident, the main leaders of the Yongzhou Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government immediately deployed the organization. The Dao County Party Committee and County Government quickly organized the Public Security and Armed Police to surround and capture the suspect. At the same time, they made every effort to rescue the injured and do a good job in relevant aftermath work, comforting the families of the sacrificed police officers.

At around 5 p.m. that afternoon, the police located the suspect under the Daohe Expressway overpass in Xiangyang Village in this town. At the time, the suspect held an excavator driver hostage with a gun and refused to surrender, coercing the hostage to drive the excavator in an attempt to break through the encirclement of the police. After repeated warnings proved ineffective, the public security police and armed police shot him dead on the spot, and the hostage was rescued safely. (Reporters Huang Xinghua and Zhou Nan)

Facts and reasons:

I am the plaintiff Zhao Yunhai, and the defendants are the Dao County People's Government and the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau.

In July 2001, the plaintiff signed a land use right transfer agreement with the Dao County Futang Fish Farm, agreeing that the fish farm would transfer its 250 square meters of land use rights to the plaintiff. The Futang Fish Farm filled in the plaintiff's name on the planning map drawn by the Dao County Planning Bureau and submitted it with the official seal. On January 12, 2007, the Dao County Planning and Construction Bureau legally issued the plaintiff with the (0701120018) No. Construction Land Use Permit. The plaintiff applied to the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau for a land use certificate with the relevant procedures. To date, the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau has neither informed the reasons nor fulfilled the relevant obligations according to law. In November 2010, the plaintiff learned that the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau had confirmed the land enjoyed by the plaintiff to Xu Shanlin for use under the Dao Jijian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Land Use Certificate. The plaintiff believes that the act of issuing the above-mentioned land use certificate by the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau violated legal procedures, contained factual errors, lacked sufficient evidence, and violated legal provisions, resulting in damage to the plaintiff's legitimate rights and interests, and should revoke the Dao Jijian (2001) No. 0814164 Collective Land Construction Land Use Certificate issued by the Dao County Land and Resources Bureau according to law.

In the first and second instance trials, the court believed that according to Article 63 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China": The right to use land collectively owned by farmers shall not be transferred, transferred, or leased for non-agricultural construction. The Futang Fish Farm is an agricultural collective economic organization subordinate to the Futang Township People's Government of Dao County, and its land nature is collectively owned. The agreement signed between the Futang Fish Farm and the plaintiff without authorization is invalid and therefore not protected by law. The issuance of the certificate by the defendant has no legal interest relationship with the plaintiff, and thus dismissed the lawsuit.

The truth:

1. Look at the evidence documents submitted by the Dao County Government, Document No. (1978)037 of the Dao County Revolutionary Committee.

In 1978, the Dao County Government organized the dispersed independent fishermen into a unit, which was a collective unit, but its land source was government-allocated land, clearly stated in the certification document. Base issue: after organizing the dispersed independent fishermen, a production and living base must be established. For this purpose, the Futang Commune allocated Dafutang (more than 200 mu) to the fish farm for building ponds and raising fish...

How can government-allocated land be called collective land? Proving the nature of the land is simple; just provide the original land certificate. Why doesn't the government provide it but instead provides a 1978 document? Can the 1978 document be found easily, but the land certificate cannot be found, or is it afraid to provide it? Document No. (1978)037 clearly states that the land was allocated by the Futang Commune. Are they trying to deceive us as if we don't understand what state-owned land, collective land, and allocated land are?

According to Article 2 of the "Land Management Law of the People's Republic of China," the state implements a paid-use system for state-owned land according to law. However, the allocation of state-owned land use rights within the scope prescribed by law by the state is exempted. According to the "Interim Measures for the Administration of Allocated Land Use Rights," land users approved by the land management departments of municipal and county people's governments and who have paid the land use right transfer fee may transfer, lease, or mortgage land use rights.

The Dao County Futang Fish Farm did not just sign transfer agreements with me alone but with many others. Evidence two: Internal Settlement Receipt of Administrative and Public Institutions in Hunan Province 湘财统字（2002）0140677 Payment Unit: Futang Fish Farm Date: September 25, 2002 Payment Item: Prepayment for handling land use procedures Amount: 60,000 yuan Seal of Dao County Land and Resources Bureau

In addition, many others have also been issued state-owned land use certificates, and I have evidence.
