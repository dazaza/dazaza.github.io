Japanese girl had sex with her father after getting him drunk in order to buy a PSV

by mlwang on 2011-12-21 18:09:24

According to reports, Sony's new handheld console, the PSVita, which was launched in Japan on December 17th, sold 321,407 units in just two days. Although this figure is less than the 3DS's two-day sales of 371,326 units when it launched on February 26th this year, it still shows a rapid momentum, with the first week's sales expected to approach 1.4 million. However, it is rumored that Sony only prepared an initial shipment of 700,000 units.

With relatively tight supply, players who have not yet been able to purchase a PSV are starting to feel anxious. Some young students with limited financial means seem to be trying hard to raise funds, and among them are those resorting to extreme measures such as compensated dating (援交).

According to a Japanese gaming forum, a high school girl living in Kanagawa Prefecture is seen by many as a typical game enthusiast. Sony's latest handheld console, the PSV, was her most fervently desired item recently. Unfortunately, just before the release of the PSV, the money she had saved up to buy it was stolen by a pickpocket. In order to quickly obtain enough money to buy the PSV, she reportedly engaged in a sexual relationship with her own father, receiving payment for it.

The report claims that after the money for the PSV was stolen, she sought help from her father, but was refused. She then took advantage of a dinner opportunity to persuade her father to drink a large amount of alcohol. While he was drunk and unconscious, she engaged in a sexual relationship with him and received payment. Her father, fearing the scandal would be exposed, agreed. Afterwards, the high school girl repeatedly initiated sexual relations with her father and received varying amounts of cash each time.

After the incident occurred, the girl's mother and younger brother soon noticed something was amiss. For unknown reasons, her mother did not speak out or intervene, allowing the inappropriate relationship between her husband and daughter to continue. The post was made by the girl's younger brother, who stated in the post that he could no longer tolerate the increasingly brazen behavior of his father and sister, and decided to run away from home.

In our general impression, Japanese male gaming enthusiasts often make unconventional moves that are hard to fathom, but we never imagined that a female high school student would go so far to buy a gaming console.
