I. Detailed information about the product [High Sensitivity Steam Pressure Reducing Valve]:

Product Model: YG43H/Y Type

Product Name: High Sensitivity Steam Pressure Reducing Valve

Product Features: This product is mainly used in steam pipelines, suitable for pipelines with large fluctuations in inlet pressure and flow rate.

II. Structural Characteristics and Uses:

This series of pressure reducing valves belongs to the pilot piston type pressure reducing valve. It consists of two parts: the main valve and the guide valve. The main valve is mainly composed of a valve seat, main valve disc, piston, spring, etc. The guide valve is mainly composed of a valve seat, valve flap, diaphragm, spring, adjustment spring, etc. By adjusting the pressure of the adjustment spring to set the outlet pressure, using the diaphragm to sense the changes in outlet pressure, through the opening and closing of the guide valve driving the piston to adjust the overcurrent area of the throttling part of the main valve, thus achieving the function of pressure reduction and stabilization.

This series of products has made the following improvements on the basis of ordinary pressure reducing valves, increasing the piston area, changing the throttling structure, changing the sealing form, hot-rolled thick-walled seamless steel pipe contact number 13963589458.13730921433, improving the structure of the guide valve and the form of the adjustment spring, thereby improving the performance of the product from the following aspects.

1. Improved the sensing efficiency of outlet pressure changes, improved sensitivity, greatly reduced the pressure deviation caused by factors such as upstream pressure changes and flow changes.

2. Improved streamline distribution, reduced noise.

3. Improved the sealing surface structure, extended the life of the sealing surface.
