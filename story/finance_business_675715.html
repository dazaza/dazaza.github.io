Growth, the most beautiful transformation

At noon, while having lunch in the restaurant, I saw a sentence on the advertisement board: "Growth is the most beautiful transformation". Suddenly, I felt it deeply, my heart was tingling. Although it was just a very ordinary sentence, I was moved. Let me think, how long has it been since I last felt this way? How long have I been numb to external things? On the way back to the dormitory, this sentence kept echoing in my ears: "Growth, the most beautiful transformation." So, so I naturally thought of myself, my growth, especially at this time, the most proud but also the most disappointed time.

So when should I start talking? I opened the door of memory and tried hard to recall those things that I once thought would be remembered for a lifetime. But I couldn't remember them. Why were they so blurred? There was only the bright sunshine, the special wind in the northern suburbs of Xi'an, and that green clover. Oh, right, I remember now. There was also Su Shi's "Jiangchengzi": For ten years, life and death have been vast and empty, no need to ponder, never forget. A lonely grave a thousand miles away, nowhere to speak of sorrow. Even if we met again, you probably wouldn't recognize me, with dust all over my face, and my temples as white as frost. Last night, in a dream, I suddenly returned home, by the small window, you were combing your hair. We looked at each other in silence, only tears streamed down our faces. I guessed that every year, the place that breaks my heart is under the bright moonlight, on the short pine hill. And there was also Li Yu's "Langtao Sha": Outside the curtain, the rain trickles, spring is fading. The silk quilt can't withstand the cold of the fifth watch. In dreams, I don't know that I am a guest, indulging in a moment of happiness. Don't lean alone! Infinite rivers and mountains, parting is easy, meeting is hard. The flowing water, falling flowers, and the departure of spring, in heaven and earth. But that's all I could remember. Maybe I just didn't want to think about it anymore. Because my thoughts were already in chaos, which was the situation every time, so each time I changed my original intention. This should also be a manifestation of a disturbed mind. Well then, if memories are forgotten, then I won't say anything more, because I've forgotten, forgotten those carefree days of youth.

You see, I went off-topic again. I was supposed to write about growth, my growth. Why do people always like to reminisce? And in those memories, there are only beautiful and innocent smiles, not bitter and sour tears. From when did I start understanding that it's because reality doesn't meet expectations? Then why does reality not meet expectations? I can't innocently say that I don't know. Who said that when you start reminiscing about the past frequently, and the past only contains good things, it means you're old, at least your heart is old. Ah, so am I... But I'm not particularly superstitious about this statement. I just like to reminisce, that's all.

Last week, I went to Xiaozhai, from the North Gate to the Bell Tower and then to the South Gate. Along the way, the flowers were blooming beautifully. It was rare sunny weather in Xi'an. And on Saturday, my mood was beautiful. The smell of the crowded crowd on bus No. 600 was truly beautiful. The excitement of seeing my friends, we wandered around the streets, as if the young us would never get tired. Under the sun, a thin layer of sweat seeped out on my forehead, it was great. How long has it been since I last smelled the taste of sweat? In my memory, it was still when I was very young. After school, we played wildly in the village, chasing, playing, always waiting until the sun set before being called home by our mothers. Even in the cold winter, we would sweat. And there was the unique fragrance of the hometown soil. This familiar smell has become strange. But that day, with my friends, I remembered it all. It was also then that I knew spring had come. Yes, spring had come. The snowless winter of 2008 has passed far away, and will never return, ever.

Do you remember Sanmao's words? "Lock up my memories, lock up my worries, never think of you again. I no longer think of you, how could I think of you again, only my stranded heart, in the lonely shadow of a thousand years." And, "Remember when we were young, you liked to talk and I liked to laugh. Once we sat side by side under a peach tree, we didn't know how we fell asleep. In the dream, how many flowers fell." And "A moment of true feelings, cannot say it's false, true feelings last forever, impossible to have only that moment." And, and so much more. The last sentence: "Goodbye, dear classmates, don't cry." Alright, now, let's cheer again for the arrival of spring, the blossoms have bloomed, and we meet again. Do you remember? I want to ask, do you remember? Alright, I won't act unreasonable anymore. Whether you remember or not, I just want to say, dear friend, don't cry, spring has come, the flowers have bloomed again, but people can never meet again. But "youthful companionship, I have experienced, it's gratitude, it's satisfaction, no regrets." Haven't you also consoled me with Cao Xueqin's "endless spring willows and spring flowers fill the painted building"? Yes, life is always full of surprises. Who said that? Ah, Sanmao, it's her again, this warm-hearted woman. Warmth, this word is really good, I like it. It seems like one of my roommates said it. She also said Zhang Ailing's writings are cold. She said it really well. Really well, they are all good, and so are my beloved family and sincere friends. How can I thank you for tolerating my capriciousness with such broad hearts? When I lost my way, only the home with parents' silent love and the dormitory with roommates' comfort were my most cozy harbors. I remember, when I couldn't stay in school, I took the last bus home on a winter night. The road to the bus station was heavily congested, I was almost crazy with anxiety, but the belief to go home didn't waver at all. I thought if there was no bus at the station, I would still go home, even if I had to stop a taxi, although I didn't have enough money in my pocket, I must go home, must, because dad and mom are at home, I want to go home, there is a bed for me at home. Every time I go home, mom always dries the quilt in advance, I can even smell the scent of the sun now. Because dad at home is slowly getting old, and I, and I still often say hurtful words. I miss them, my dearest ones, even if the whole world looks down on me, I know they won't, won't.

Let me organize my thoughts, I'm already speaking nonsense.

Alright, I still want to talk about our good roommates in the dormitory. They always pull me when I don't want to go to class. When I cry, who sits by my bed holding my hand and reads her diary to comfort me? Who listens and sees tears flow into her ears and hair, making her itch, but she keeps saying look, I was so sad then, but now isn't it better? You will be too, you will be too. And, that night when I called and cried and said angry words, who brought a down jacket to cover me, hugged me and said don't cry, don't cry. When the lights were turned off, we lay on the bed head to head chatting, our voices were very low, only the sound of gas, but I knew, it was the strongest, the most real. And, and... I won't say anymore, there's so much to say, how could it be finished? With nearly two years of close living together, we are like sisters. Alright, I won't say anymore. It's really good to have you. I'm grateful because of you, I have you, it's really good, I only wish we could walk a longer road together. I am infinitely grateful!

I know I've said too much, I always say too much, and my language is so awkward, because I can only be like this now. Do you remember the childhood ideal? To be a writer, how good, but it can't be now, and I don't want it anymore. Ask who is willing to bear that loneliness? And I, can only hesitate. Look, look at how the road underfoot was walked step by step? I seem to see the real self, but is the current self false? Perhaps falsehood is the most helpless choice. But, an undeniable fact is that life has made me grow up slowly, isn't it? We all are.

Growth requires a price, but it is our most beautiful transformation!
