2. Longer links can cause issues when exchanging content in some forums.

For instance, writing about Ford cars, using "ford" as the postname has proven impractical. Because you might write another 20 articles about Ford cars in the future, what would you do then—ford-2nd, ford-3rd…, ford-20th? This easily makes the links messy and completely loses the benefit of having clean links. Moreover, English names are prone to grammatical errors.
