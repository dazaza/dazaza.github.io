Guoxin Securities: Growth of the sewage treatment business of Bluestar Cleaning is expected to accelerate＿Stock Forum

by dx0966632 on 2009-12-03 22:44:41

Matters: On December 1, 2009, after being reviewed by the Mergers and Acquisitions Examination Committee of the China Securities Regulatory Commission (CSRC), the major asset swap and issuance of shares to purchase assets related to associated transactions of Lanxing Cleaning passed the conditional examination. The company will issue another announcement after receiving the relevant approval documents from the CSRC.

Comments: The restructuring is basically completed. From the release of the restructuring draft in June to passing the review on December 1st, the entire restructuring process proceeded smoothly. Currently, the following steps are still needed: (1) Exemption of the tender offer obligation. Since Xingrong Company holds 52.3% of the company's equity, exceeding 30%, it still needs the CSRC's approval for its exemption from the tender offer obligation; (2) Asset delivery. We maintain the "optimistic estimate that asset delivery will be completed within the year," based on (1) both parties' determination to restructure and support from all sides, as the time from the release of the restructuring draft to the submission of materials was less than a month; (2) According to experience, under normal circumstances, the exemption of the tender offer obligation will be approved within one month after passing the review, and the asset delivery matters will be confirmed. At that time, the entire restructuring process will be completed.

Taking a key step forward, the growth of sewage treatment business is expected to accelerate. The company is transforming into a leading sewage treatment company in Southwest China, owning eight sewage treatment plants of Chengdu Drainage Company (with about 1.3 million cubic meters/day capacity), responsible for sewage treatment in the central urban area of Chengdu, with clear assets. With the basic completion of restructuring, we believe that the company will shift its focus to enhancing the growth of the sewage business, which will greatly promote the resolution of the pipeline bottleneck and the expansion of external growth. The probability of achieving an annual growth rate of "10%-15%" in sewage treatment volume has increased, and the performance growth rate in the next two years is expected to exceed expectations, reaching around 20% growth.

Maintain "Recommend" rating. Basically, the end of the year can be used as a node, with almost all the benefits in 2010 coming from newly injected assets. Without considering subsequent asset injections and sludge projects, the diluted EPS for 2010 and 2011 are 0.50 and 0.60 yuan respectively, with the current stock price at 12.3 yuan, the valuation is at the bottom of the entire industry (the PE level in 2010 is close to 40), with a high safety margin. Meanwhile, Xingrong Company develops both internally and externally: (1) Located in Chengdu, the urbanization speed in recent years has undoubtedly promoted water usage and sewage treatment volumes; (2) Government support, which can be seen from the entire restructuring process; (3) Operational strength and leading position in the Chengdu waterworks market, regardless of sewage or tap water, facing significant expansion space. Maintain the company's "Recommend" rating.

The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. This order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when entrusting to buy or sell. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks bought or sold: the quantity of stocks commissioned to buy must be a whole multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks commissioned to sell does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four ways to entrust: counter submission, telephone automatic entrustment, computer automatic entrustment, and remote terminal entrustment.
