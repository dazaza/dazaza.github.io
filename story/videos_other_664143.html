Wind-Solar Hybrid Streetlight Project in the Northern New City

Bidding Number: JZZB2011-08

I. Bidding Conditions

The wind-solar hybrid streetlight project in the Northern New City has been approved for construction by the administrative department of construction of the Changchun High-Tech Industrial Development Zone. The bidder is the Changchun High-Tech Industrial Development Zone Construction and Development Center, with funding sourced from self-raised funds. The project is now ready for public bidding.

II. Project Overview and Scope of Bidding

2.1 Project Overview: The construction of the project is divided into 2 construction sections:

Section One: Yisan Road wind-solar hybrid streetlights

Section Two: Bingershi Street wind-solar hybrid streetlights

2.2 Scope of Bidding: Construction of wind-solar hybrid streetlights on Yisan Road and Bingershi Street. The scope includes: procurement and installation of solar panels, batteries, wind turbines, LED lights, streetlight poles, arms, controllers, earthwork excavation and backfilling, and the production of streetlight foundations.

2.3 Construction Location: Gaoxin North Area, Bingershi Street, and Yisan Road.

2.4 Schedule Requirement: Planned start date April 30, 2011, completion date May 30, 2011.

2.5 Quality Requirement: The project must meet the quality standards of currently applicable national construction acceptance norms for qualified projects.

III. Qualification Requirements for Bidders

3.1 Bid applicants must be legal entities or other organizations with professional contracting qualifications of level three or above for urban and road lighting engineering, or general contracting qualifications of level two or above for mechanical and electrical equipment installation, or professional contracting qualifications of level three or above for mechanical and electrical equipment installation. Applicants should have construction performance records for similar projects in the last three years and possess corresponding construction capabilities in terms of personnel, equipment, and funds. The proposed project manager must possess a registered constructor qualification of level two or above in the relevant specialty.

3.2 The proposed project manager should not have any ongoing construction projects.

3.3 This bid does not accept joint bids.

3.4 Out-of-province construction enterprises need to register at the municipal (prefecture) or county (city) construction administrative departments where their branches are located to complete relevant entry filing procedures before participating in the bid. (See Document No. [2007]17 and Document No. [2008]14 issued by Jilin Construction Management).

3.5 Enterprises listed in the government's record of poor behavior will be rejected during the bid.

IV. Obtaining Bidding Documents

4.1 All interested parties may register and purchase bidding documents from April 7, 2011 to April 14, 2011 (excluding statutory holidays), daily from 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM and from 1:00 PM to 3:30 PM (Beijing Time). At Jilin Zhengyang Engineering Management Consulting Co., Ltd., bring the following: (1) Original and copies (2 sets) of the enterprise business license; (2) Original and copies (2 sets) of the enterprise qualification certificate; (3) Original and copies (2 sets) of the enterprise safety production permit; (4) Originals (2 sets) of the enterprise legal representative’s qualification certificate and authorization letter; (5) Original and copies (2 sets) of the authorized person’s ID card; (6) Original and copies (2 sets) of the project manager's registered constructor certificate; (7) Original and copies (2 sets) of the safety production assessment certificates for the enterprise legal representative, project manager, and safety officer; (8) Original and copies (2 sets) of proof materials regarding entry procedures for out-of-province construction enterprises and enterprises outside Changchun city in this province. Late registrations will not be accepted by the bidding agent.

4.2 Each set of bidding documents costs RMB 1000, no refunds after sale.

V. Submission of Bids

5.1 Bid submission deadline: 9:00 AM on April 26, 2011.

Bid submission location: Fifth-floor bidding room of the Changbei High-Tech Center.

5.2 Bids delivered late or not to the designated location will not be accepted by the bidder.

5.3 Bidders shall provide a bid bond as required upon submission of the bid.
