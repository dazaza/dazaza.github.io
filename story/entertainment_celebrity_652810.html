Jimmy Chen (Weibo) took on the role of a movie lead actor for the first time, alongside goddess Ruby Lin (Weibo). The romantic comedy "The Soul of Love's Bread" will be released on February 3rd next year. Jimmy Chen is going all out with promotions - cycling around the island, visiting all the Three Princes Temples across Taiwan to make wishes, and even launching a "Big Stars Every Day" campaign on Facebook. Numerous A-list celebrities from the entertainment industry have come to support him. Ruby Lin (Weibo), on the other hand, uploaded a picture of herself biting into a bread roll to her Weibo, driving her fans wild with adoration.

Ruby Lin is sweetly counting down with her fans on Weibo, looking forward to the romantic release of "The Soul of Love's Bread." She playfully dresses up as a little mouse, bear cub, panda, and more, even inviting her former on-screen lover Kris Wu to pose in various cute "eating bread" poses. Fans exclaimed "Super kawaii!" causing netizens to go crazy sharing the images. Some netizens even parodied the lead poster of "The Soul of Love's Bread," placing a pineapple bread turtle on Ruby Lin's head, successfully capturing the goddess's attention. Ruby Lin even reposted it, laughing and asking, "Why do I have to wear a turtle?"
