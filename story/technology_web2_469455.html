From District Chief Quality Award to Mayor Quality Award

Recently, the first District Chief Quality Award selection in Fengxian District, Shanghai, caused quite a stir locally. A Shanghai Chaori Solar Energy Science and Technology Co., Ltd., which was established only 6 years ago, won the honor title of the first Fengxian District District Chief Quality Award. The review activity not only conveyed the quality benchmark of the district's industrial manufacturing enterprises, set up a benchmark enterprise that has achieved excellent business performance, but also told enterprises that they should have stronger and more sustainable comprehensive competitive strength.

Holding the newly off-line solar cell pieces, relevant person in charge of Shanghai Chaori Solar Energy Science and Technology Co., Ltd. introduced that Shanghai Chaori Solar Energy Science and Technology Co., Ltd., which was founded in June 2003, since 2008, Chaori's sales performance has increased by 50%, achieving sales of 1.2 billion yuan that year, net profit of more than 80 million yuan. Due to the adoption of world-advanced design processes, the company's series of solar products all have the characteristics of reliable quality, safe use, and beautiful appearance. More than 95% of the polycrystalline silicon solar modules produced by the company are exported, selling well in markets such as Germany, France, Italy, Greece, Spain, Australia, and the United States.

In 2009, after expanding territory and focusing on sharpening their swords, Chaori marched north, and the 500-megawatt solar cell production line in Luoyang, Henan, successfully trial-produced. The second phase of the 1-million-megawatt production line will soon be launched. As a new force in Luoyang's photovoltaic battery industry, the first phase of the 500-megawatt production line of Shanghai Chaori (Luoyang) solar cell pieces will increase production capacity, enabling Chaori to jump from the top ten in China's photovoltaic battery industry to the top five.

On October 30, 2009, it was a special day for Fan Bingxun, chairman of Shanghai Mitsubishi Elevator Co., Ltd. On that day, he received the first Shanghai Mayor Quality Award presented by Shanghai Mayor Han Zheng.

Leading Enterprises Set New Benchmarks for the Industry

Zhengzhou Yutong Bus Co., Ltd. is a large and medium-sized bus manufacturer and also a key enterprise cultivated by the Zhengzhou Quality Supervision Bureau. To ensure that Yutong's energy conservation and emission reduction work is effectively implemented, quality supervision personnel have repeatedly delved into the front line of the enterprise and actively communicated with the enterprise, ultimately determining the implementation of energy target management system. They established a system of employee voluntary supervisors for energy conservation and emission reduction and a training mechanism for voluntary supervisors, regularly organizing training for voluntary supervisors. They conscientiously implemented laws and regulations on saving energy and protecting the environment, strictly enforced energy consumption limits and pollution discharge standards for high-energy-consuming products, improved and formulated equipment procurement systems, and did not procure equipment that did not meet efficient and energy-saving indicators. Moreover, they implemented an energy consumption quota management system for major energy-consuming equipment and added measuring instruments. They established online monitoring systems for sewage and boiler emissions, established statistical systems for energy conservation and emission reduction, promoted advanced lean production projects internationally, continuously optimized process routes and production plan management models, improved equipment utilization efficiency, and effectively saved energy.

In recent years, Yutong has transformed frequency converters, energy-saving lamps, gas, measuring instruments, sewage treatment stations, recycled water utilization, boilers, etc., and recovered condensate water. Currently, the average comprehensive electricity saving is around 10%, saving 200,000 tons of steam annually, recovering and utilizing 230,000 tons of water, and the repeat water usage rate reached 96%. Moreover, it reduced energy waste caused by production gaps and excessively high temperatures, improving boiler efficiency by more than 15%.

Finding a path of low-carbon development is both a severe challenge commonly faced by countries worldwide and a responsibility and embodiment of future competitiveness for enterprises. Tan Wenhui, president of Shenzhen Great Wall Development Technology Co., Ltd., believes that in the new wave of transforming economic growth patterns, whoever develops a low-carbon economy and persists in energy conservation and emission reduction can seize strategic opportunities.

It is understood that Great Wall Development is a listed company engaged in the research and production of hard disk heads, electric meters, tax control products, and storage products. As early as 2007, after the successful implementation of the first-phase refrigeration system operation optimization project in low-carbon transformation, the energy saving rate reached 19.7%, and the cooling pump energy saving rate reached 39.7%, saving 840,000 kilowatt-hours of electricity annually. After preliminary calculations, the implementation of this project is equivalent to reducing carbon dioxide emissions by 838.9 tons annually. After tasting the benefits, the company implemented three major low-carbon transformation projects in 2008. According to incomplete statistics, the heat pump project and the water pump frequency conversion project reduced carbon emissions by nearly 2,000 tons, and the green lighting project reduced annual carbon emissions by 35%; In 2009, another 10 projects were promoted, saving 1.71 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, and reducing carbon dioxide emissions by 1,709.5 tons.

While undergoing low-carbon transformation, Great Wall Development also began creating a low-carbon ecological industrial park, building an information storage industry low-carbon ecological industrial park characterized by low-carbon economic development and resource optimization configuration, using renewable and recyclable substances as carriers, and industrial symbiosis as ties. It has passed expert reviews and is expected to become the second ecological industrial park in Shenzhen.

It is revealed that within the next three to five years, Great Wall Development will actively introduce low-carbon new technologies including solar technology, water-stored cold technology, natural light light guide tube technology, and air compressor heat recovery utilization technology, applying them to the venous industry ecological development, gradually reducing the proportion of traditional energy use, further perfecting the industrial chain of the park, and implementing low-carbon supply chain management.

Contributing to the Green Economy through Practice

From pursuing cost-effective to choosing energy-saving and low-carbon, accompanying the changes in consumption concepts are innovations and breakthroughs in the technical field of home appliance products.

Anhui's home appliance industry gathers a large number of well-known domestic and international brands and is an important home appliance production base nationwide. It has solid development foundations and advantages in scale economic effects and industrial agglomeration. By 2011, Anhui's home appliance production capacity will reach 58.5 million units, striving to rank first nationwide.

For many years, Meiling has insisted on independent innovation and has achieved outstanding research results in energy saving, preservation, deep freezing, green design, and other fields. Based on the research of green home appliance product development capabilities construction projects in the past two years, Meiling's Technology Center hosted and compiled three national standards: "Special Requirements for Ecological Design of Refrigerators for Household and Similar Purposes," "General Requirements for Green Manufacturing of Household and Similar Purpose Appliances," and "General Requirements for Green Design of Household and Similar Purpose Appliances." Sharing innovative achievements with the industry, over the past two years, Meiling has participated in the compilation of eight national or industry standards, promoting technological progress across the entire industry.

Given the urgency of industrial transformation and consumption upgrades, frequency conversion technology, due to its low energy consumption, low pollution, and low emissions, has become one of the main configurations of high-end electrical appliances, meeting the needs of low-carbon economic development. Hefei Sanyo will lead the promotion of frequency conversion technology upgrades through wide frequency conversion technology, guiding the industry into the era of low-carbon electrical appliances. Chairman Jin Youhua of Hefei Sanyo once stated: In the era of low-carbon economy, the popularization of frequency conversion washing machines is an inevitable trend.

Hefei Sanyo has basically established itself as a leader in frequency conversion technology in the washing machine industry. Wide frequency conversion technology, as a representative technology of energy-saving, environmental protection, and low-carbon electrical appliances, has core benefits of saving 40% energy and reducing noise by more than 30%. Currently, Hefei Sanyo is the only company in the industry with the capability to manufacture frequency conversion motors. Therefore, the layout of a fully frequency conversion product line has demonstrated Hefei Sanyo's commitment to creating a famous brand of household appliances with a concept of green environmental protection and energy saving.

Low-Carbon Economy Brings New Opportunities to Zhejiang Enterprises

In the catering industry of Jiaxing, Zhejiang, a low-carbon kitchen wind has recently swept through. A low-carbon kitchen means making a good meal while using less water, electricity, and gas, emitting less garbage, sewage, and exhaust gas. The rise of such a low-carbon trend is precisely due to the formulation, implementation, and promotion of low-carbon kitchen alliance standards.

Every year, the catering industry in Jiaxing produces more than 4,000 tons of garbage, of which more than 3,000 tons are reused, and more than 1,000 tons are discharged into the environment. Extending the standardization touch to every corner of social production and life, the Jiaxing Quality Supervision Bureau helped the local catering industry association formulate low-carbon kitchen alliance standards, requiring each catering enterprise to change its thinking, innovate and transform, save water, electricity, and gas; at the same time, promote the five constants method in the kitchen - regular organization, regular tidying, regular cleaning, regular cleanliness, and regular self-discipline. Ensuring that the catering kitchen is fresher, improving and protecting the catering environment while guiding the local catering industry onto the path of low-carbon economic development.

Using the formulation and implementation promotion of alliance standards as a means, not only does the modern service industry achieve a green low-carbon economic development model, but it also leads the traditional textile industry onto the fast track of healthy low-carbon economic development. Tongxiang City's sweater industry is developed, and the local quality supervision department actively helps enterprises innovate technologically, converting scientific and technological achievements into standards and applying them in industrial development. The sweater dyeing and finishing chamber researched a new type of composite enzyme for wool sweater post-finishing, developed a new green wool sweater post-finishing technology, formed an industry alliance, and formulated and implemented the "Green Post-Finishing Alliance Standard."

The promotion and implementation of this project can not only make the anti-pilling performance, colorfastness, and harmful substance (AOX) content of sweaters reach international standard requirements, breaking foreign technical trade barriers; but also reduce the BOD5 and CODcr content of discharged wastewater by more than 50% compared to traditional post-finishing processes, achieving energy conservation, emission reduction, and green environmental protection requirements.

The low-carbon economy has brought opportunities to the development of Zhejiang enterprises. Since the beginning of the year, the sales situation of Zhejiang Fengli Crushing Equipment Co., Ltd. has been very promising, with customers from all over the country coming in batches, and the company is working overtime to produce, still unable to meet the demand. Originally, with global resource shortages, many enterprises have urgently demanded energy conservation and consumption reduction, comprehensive utilization of natural resources, and recycling of secondary resources. The environmentally friendly and energy-saving ultrafine powder processing equipment, which uses mechanical force to crush materials without any exhaust pollution, has been widely applied in various fields of industrial production, with broad market prospects.

The crushing equipment produced by Fengli Company can not only make the crushed product texture uniform, with fineness reaching micron and submicron levels, but also its good performance, super-high efficiency, low noise, energy-saving, and environmental protection indicators all reach the advanced level of similar international products, becoming a new favorite in the market. Products not only sell well in countries like Canada and Australia but also enter many Fortune 500 companies in the United States and Germany, becoming a designated supplier for powder engineering equipment for foreign merchants in Europe, America, Southeast Asia, etc., in China.

The Concept of Brand Cultivation Is Shifting Towards Green

According to relevant persons in charge of the Quality Management Department of the Tianjin Quality Supervision Bureau, during the implementation of the brand-driven strategy, Tianjin pays great attention to cultivating green energy-saving enterprise products to create municipal famous brands and issued the "Opinion on Further Implementing the Famous Brand Strategy and Accelerating the Development of Our City's Famous Brand Products." Clearly requiring all levels of government and relevant departments to take effective measures and prioritize cultivating key enterprises with independent innovation capabilities and a large number of famous brand products with independent intellectual property rights in key industries encouraged by the state, such as high-tech industries, advanced manufacturing, energy conservation and environmental protection, and comprehensive utilization of再生resources. Based on reflecting the adjustment direction of national industrial policies and highlighting advanced manufacturing and energy conservation, emission reduction, environmental protection, and other products as priorities, the Tianjin Implementation Leading Group for the Strategy of Famous Brand Key Products proposed the "Tianjin Famous Brand Product Cultivation Guidance Catalogue (2007-2010)" oriented towards green energy-saving.

Many representative traditional famous brand enterprises, how to get rid of the old operational development model, change high energy consumption, high emissions, high pollution, and develop towards a new track? Regarding this, the Tianjin business community already has successful experience.

Tianjin Rongcheng United Steel Group Co., Ltd. (referred to as Ronggang), which obtained the title of Tianjin famous brand, is a high water consumption enterprise. With the expansion of the enterprise scale, how to effectively utilize water resources and reduce pollution emissions has become an important factor determining the survival and development of the enterprise. To play the radiation demonstration effect of famous brand product enterprises, Ronggang determined to blaze a trail of sustainable development ecological circulation mode. For this purpose, Ronggang invested 2 billion yuan to build a modern sewage treatment plant mainly handling the domestic sewage of Geju Town and the sewage in the factory area. After the sewage treatment plant went into operation, it produced qualified production supplementary water and reused it in various procedures of Ronggang. Through the measures of recycling wastewater, Ronggang achieved the goal of zero discharge of industrial wastewater. Currently, the sewage treatment plant's treatment scale is 48,000 tons per day, with an annual treatment volume of about 9 million tons, completely meeting the company's production water needs.
