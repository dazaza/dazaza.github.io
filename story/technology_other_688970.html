What is the role of Red Star Raymond mill in thermal power generation?

by hx515317 on 2012-03-03 16:43:46

According to the actual situation of the market, the coal crushing powder machine generally adopts vertical mill, desulfurization mill or high-pressure medium-speed mill, namely, various improved products of Raymond mill. The main fuel for thermal power plants is coal, oil, and natural gas. A 1 million kW coal-fired power plant consumes about 11,000 to 13,000 tons of raw coal per day.

Fuel is the basic raw material for thermal power generation, and its cost accounts for 60% to 80% of the power generation cost. Different types of grinding mills have different effects on coal crushing, and choices should be made according to the actual situation of the power plant. However, due to the many characteristics of Hongxing high-pressure medium-speed grinding mill, it has relatively strict requirements for materials and working environment, making it unsuitable for small and medium-sized enterprises.

From the grinding form of roller and ring to the design of the pressurization system, everything reflects a higher level of technical concepts. Improving the quality and combustion degree of fuels in thermal power plants is not only necessary for improving economic benefits but also essential for reducing pollutant emissions and protecting the ecological environment. This is mainly determined by China's primary energy structure, characterized by abundant coal, insufficient oil, and scarce gas.

The YGM high-pressure medium-speed mill, an improved product based on the Raymond mill, increases the height of the grinding roller. Under the condition of ensuring the stability of equipment operation, it improves the fineness of grinding and enhances the efficiency of grinding. Using appropriate Hongxing grinding mill equipment to process the burning coal not only greatly improves the utilization rate of coal but also offers significant advantages in terms of energy saving, environmental protection, and economic benefits. Hongxing Machinery is a professional manufacturer of large-scale industrial grinding mills, and the produced YGM190 super-large industrial grinding mill is particularly suitable for high-output crushing and grinding of limestone and quicklime. In the desulfurization grinding process of steel mills and thermal power plants, the desulfurization rate reaches over 92%, with convenient use and maintenance and remarkable results.
