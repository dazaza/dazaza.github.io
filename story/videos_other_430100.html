by anonymous on 2011-05-12 17:45:03

Liu Yishou exclaimed that there are so many kind-hearted people in the world! On May 12, Mr. Tang, who works in Shanghai, expressed such feelings to a reporter from Xinmin Network: This morning, his wife suffered from postpartum bleeding and urgently needed RH negative blood; less than an hour after his Weibo appeal (Weibo address), netizens and public security departments contacted him one after another, expressing their willingness to donate blood for his wife.

At around 8 o'clock in the morning, my wife gave birth at Jiading Maternal and Child Health Hospital," Mr. Tang told the reporter. Subsequently, his wife developed symptoms of postpartum hemorrhage, and the situation was critical. However, because her blood type is a very rare AB type RH negative blood, an urgent blood source was needed.

In a hurry, at around 9 o'clock, Mr. Tang posted a Weibo message online, urgently begging for RH negative blood, and left his contact information.

What surprised Mr. Tang was that not long after he posted the Weibo, the first call offering to donate blood came in. It was from a netizen in Putuo District, who said he had O-type RH negative blood and asked if it was possible.

Although the answer from the doctor was negative, Mr. Tang's heart was filled with hope. Then, three more netizens called, saying their blood types matched and they were willing to come and donate blood.

The news on Weibo was soon seen by the Jiading Public Security Bureau. The police contacted Mr. Tang by phone while urgently retrieving the blood type records of all officers in the bureau. Through inquiry, there were over 140 officers in the branch who matched the AB blood type. However, just as the public security department organized the officers to rush to Jiading Maternal and Child Health Hospital, a piece of news calmed everyone's nerves: the hospital had contacted the Shanghai Blood Station, and the blood was now in place. Both mother and child were safe, and a pair of twins, a boy and a girl, were born.

The enthusiasm of netizens and people from all walks of life moved Mr. Tang greatly. There are really too many kind-hearted people in the world!
