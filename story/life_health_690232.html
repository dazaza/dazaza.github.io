How to take care of yourself after double eyelid surgery

by wgcgen on 2012-03-05 08:56:20

Having double eyelids is a dream for many people. Even if someone is not born with double eyelids, now there is double eyelid surgery that can create them, achieving an effect that is almost indistinguishable from natural double eyelids and making the double eyelids look even more beautiful. To make the double eyelids more beautiful, it's not only necessary for the surgery to be successful but also inseparable from postoperative care. So how should postoperative care be done after double eyelid surgery? Below, experts from Wuhan Boai will introduce it to everyone.

How to do postoperative care after double eyelid surgery? Experts introduced several points of postoperative care after double eyelid surgery:

1. Keep the wound clean and prevent infection. Keeping the wound clean after double eyelid surgery is very important. If the wound is not clean, it can easily lead to infection, causing scar formation on the wound. Therefore, when washing your face after surgery, be sure not to wet the wound. The dressing covering the eyes can be removed 1-2 days after surgery. If there is blood scab or secretion on the wound, you can use sterile saline or medical alcohol to wipe it.

2. After the operation, there should be a quiet and comfortable environment for rest. The indoor air needs to be fresh and circulated while maintaining a certain temperature. In terms of diet, increase the intake of protein and eat more fruits and fresh vegetables. Within one week after surgery, avoid watching TV or reading newspapers. When resting in bed, it's best to lie in a semi-reclining position (raising the pillow) to prevent eye fatigue or having the head positioned too low, which may worsen swelling at the wound.
