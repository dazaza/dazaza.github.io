It's also beautiful in paradise - because I miss you.

"Let's break up!"

This was the first sentence the girl said to the boy! The boy paused for a moment, then nodded: "Alright!"

The girl was stunned, slapped the boy, and then left angrily. After the girl left, the boy cried bitterly in the toilet, hugging his head...

After returning home, the girl threw the scarf the boy gave her into the trash can. She didn't want to see him anymore. At the moment he agreed to the breakup, she no longer needed to ask anything.

Once, the boy doted on her like a treasure. Even if it was windy or rainy, he would run all over the city to buy the chocolate cake that the girl loved the most.

Last year, just before the girl's birthday, it rained heavily. The boy rode his bike, visited seventy-eight streets, collected nine hundred and ninety-nine one-cent RMB notes, and then clumsily folded paper boats every night.

On the girl's birthday, the boy secretly placed nine hundred and ninety-nine paper boats next to the girl's pillow. There was a note underneath: "Dear wife, happy birthday! Although the gift is only worth 9.9 yuan, my love for you is priceless. Love from your husband."

When the girl woke up, she saw the note and the gift, and was both surprised and moved. She believed that this man could be someone she could depend on for her entire life.

In recent weeks, the boy had spent less time with the girl. Occasionally when they were together, he always impatiently listened to the girl nagging about wanting him to accompany her somewhere. Listening to her, the boy fell asleep on the girl's shoulder. The girl pushed him away angrily and said, "Is being with me really that exhausting? If so, go back home and sleep! Don't come out again!" This was the last thing the girl said to the boy before their breakup.

After that incident, whenever the girl called the boy, the line was always busy or out of service area!

On the eve of her birthday, the girl went to the boy's house. She saw a pile of girls' clothes drying on the balcony, and her heart seemed to rise to her throat. She couldn't say a word. She silently sat on the sofa, waiting for the boy's arrival.

There was a "click" sound.

The boy walked in, exhausted. He was surprised to see the girl, but before he could say anything, the girl spoke first.

"Let's break up!"

"Alright!"

The girl still harbored a glimmer of hope; as long as the boy refused, she would believe he hadn't betrayed her. But he readily agreed!

At 23:59, the girl's phone rang. It was the boy calling. "Wife, happy birthday!"

"We've broken up! You shouldn't call me that anymore," the girl said painfully.

"Wife, I want to see you. I'm already on my way to your place. I'll be there in five minutes. I have something very important to tell you!" After saying this, the boy hung up.

The girl sat by the window counting the seconds passing by. It was already two o'clock in the morning, but the boy hadn't shown up yet. She turned off the light, got into bed, closed her tired eyes, and soon fell asleep...

"Wife!" The girl woke up startled by the boy's shout. She saw the boy quietly sitting by her bedside.

"You liar! Do you know what time it is now? I hate you!" The girl shouted furiously.

"Wife, there was a traffic accident on the road just now, so the traffic was blocked until now!"

The boy hugged the girl. His gaze was somewhat vacant, and his body temperature was icy cold. The girl originally wanted to pull away, but he held her tightly.

"Wife, don't be like this! I can only stay for a little while. I just came to see you and will leave soon. Don't say anything, let me talk!"

"After breaking up, you'll have to live alone! I'm worried, how will you manage without me?"

"You never eat breakfast unless I make your favorite fried eggs for you. Without me, who will make breakfast for you?"

"When your stomach pain flares up, you always fall asleep in my arms. Without me, who will give you an embrace?"

"Wife, do you know how bossy you are? Whatever you ask for, even if it's the stars from the sky, I'd get them for you, otherwise you wouldn't talk to me for days!"

"Wife, the time you asked me to donate blood at the hospital, after donating, I left alone. Later, you ignored me for a week. Finally, I shouted ten times on the bus 'Wife, I'm sorry!' before you talked to me again. Wife, actually I'm afraid of blood. After donating, my face was pale, but I didn't want you to worry, so I left alone!"

"Wife, today when you told me to break up, I cried bitterly in the toilet. You must think I'm useless, right? Why did I hide? Because my sister is coming home soon. She's been staying at my place these few days, and you haven't met her yet!"

The boy sighed deeply and smiled, "I seem to have said too much nonsense today, haha! Wife, go back to sleep! I have to go!" As the girl listened to the boy's words, her heart suddenly felt sour, a feeling of parting forever. Looking at the boy, she felt he was different from usual, but she couldn't quite tell how! Watching his retreating back, the girl had an impulse to call him back, but she was still upset. This time, she decided he had to beg her for a month before she would speak to him again!

Sunlight slanted into the bedroom, and the girl stretched lazily in bed.

The phone rang urgently, and the girl answered it irritably. A sobbing voice of a strange woman came from the other end,

"I'm Ahao's sister, Ahao, he, he, wu! He, he, got into a car accident!"

In the hospital, the boy lay quietly on the bed, his eyes tightly closed, his face as pale as the sheets. The girl hugged his corpse, stunned, unable to say a word!

The boy's sister told her that last night he collided with a truck and died instantly. He didn't have time to leave any last words, and Death took him away!

"When I saw him, his eyes were open, and he was tightly holding onto this ring. To buy this ring for you on your birthday and propose, he went to the mine every night with others to dig coal, almost losing his life several times due to cave-ins!"

The girl felt dizzy, and her body collapsed powerless!

From then on, the girl never dated again. She would always remember the boy's figure. If she could meet him in the next life, she would tell him ten thousand times on the bus: Husband, I'm sorry!

Epilogue

August 7th

In a month, it will be my wife's birthday! Today, I saw a proposal ring called "A Lifetime of Love" in a jewelry store. This year, I want to propose to my wife.

August 10th

Today, a colleague at work told me the mine is hiring coal miners, and it pays well! I insisted on going, and it really does pay well! I made fifty yuan in one night!

August 15th

Mining coal is dangerous, there are cave-ins! I almost got crushed!

August 21st

Today, my wife complained that I haven't been answering her calls recently, asking if I dislike her. This cute silly girl, I don't have enough time in my life to cherish her, how could I possibly dislike her? But I can't tell her that the signal in the mine is poor! Let her misunderstand me then!

August 27th

Today, while I was out with my wife, I accidentally fell asleep. She got angry and scolded me. It's all my fault for falling asleep carelessly! Even though I only slept at five in the morning yesterday, I shouldn't have dozed off. Being with my wife is the happiest!

September 1st

Today, my sister came from the countryside to visit me. Mom told me to definitely bring my girlfriend home for her to meet by the end of the year!

September 4th

Today is the last day of mining coal. We finished digging in the dark and were about to finish work when a cave-in suddenly occurred. My foot was crushed by the collapse, but luckily I managed to crawl out and save my life! I brought home fifteen hundred yuan in salary, finally not having to do this nerve-wracking job anymore.

September 5th

Today, I used the money to buy a ring at the jewelry store. The day after tomorrow is my wife's birthday. At twelve o'clock tonight, I will propose to my wife. I haven't seen my wife for several days, I miss her so much!

September 6th

Today, when I got home, my wife was there too. Seeing her made me so happy. I was just about to tell her how much I missed her. Before I could speak, my wife told me she wanted to break up. My heart ached, tears welled up in my eyes. I agreed with her because I promised her that no matter what she asked me to do, I couldn't say no, even if breaking up with her would make me so sad I might die!

September 7th

Today is my wife's birthday. I thought about her all day. In the evening, I picked up the ring and called my wife. No matter what, I have to propose to my wife. I want to take care of her for the rest of my life!

On the way, the ring fell out of my pocket and rolled onto the road. I hurriedly stopped my bike and ran to pick it up. A truck sped towards me, and I didn't have time to dodge! All I saw was a bloody redness!

I don't know how long it was before I woke up. The Grim Reaper stood beside me, telling me that I no longer belonged to this world. I saw my sister crying loudly, hugging my bloody corpse.

Wife, I want to find my wife! I begged the Grim Reaper, and it allowed me to see my wife for half an hour.

By my wife's bedside, I stroked her hair, reluctant to leave. I was worried that after I left, no one would take care of my wife. What would she do?

Wife, I'm sorry, I can't take care of you for the rest of my life!

Wife, you must be happy. If I am still so unlucky in the next life, then I pray to heaven to let me meet you earlier!

Wife, farewell forever! After reading, I feel sorrowful... Let's not let more people miss true love.
