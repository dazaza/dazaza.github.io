Programmer opens a Taobao store - Store opening篇

by geekzhang on 2013-05-05 22:08:13

Since graduating in 2003, I have always been working in programming-related jobs. I also consider myself a loyal Taobao user, as many cost-effective items can be found on Taobao. I've always had the idea of opening a Taobao store, but it has remained just an idea without any action taken.Time flies, and 2013 has already arrived; in fact, four months of it have already passed. It's almost been 10 years since my graduation. Looking back on the first 10 years of my career, I’ve made many friends and learned a lot of professional knowledge. But when it comes to telling my daughter what her father has accomplished that she could be proud of, I cannot think of a single thing.Before 2013, I had many ideas, but none of them were put into practice. Starting this year, I want to change that mindset. Once I've thought something through, I will do my best to execute it and explore diligently. Even if I don't succeed, I can still learn a great deal, and life will be more fulfilling!Just recently, Hong Kong introduced purchase restrictions, and many friends asked me, who lives near the border, to help with purchasing milk powder for them. Previously, I did this for free, sometimes even covering transportation costs. As more people requested my help, they themselves began to feel awkward and encouraged me to open a Taobao store. This way, I can make money, and their babies can have access to Hong Kong version milk powder long-term, which is beneficial for both parties!I thank everyone for reading through all my ramblings. Now let's get to the main topic: what needs to be done to open a Taobao store? The main aspects involve primary goods, sourcing, logistics, the Taobao store itself, and its unique features.SourcingSince I have previously helped friends purchase Hong Kong milk powder for free and traveling to Hong Kong is very convenient for me, I decided to start with sourcing Hong Kong goods. Many people advised me not to sell milk powder because of the restrictions, weight issues, low profit margins, and only making a little bit of money from the effort. They suggested instead selling cosmetics, jewelry, or gourmet food.Because my daughter used to drink Hong Kong version milk powder, I have some understanding of it and can address many questions customers might have regarding Hong Kong milk powder. For other products, I wouldn’t be able to offer any advice. After careful consideration, I decided to start with ordering milk powder. There are various kinds of milk powder in Hong Kong, categorized by stores such as pharmacies, supermarkets, and baby shops; and by type, such as Hong Kong versions or purely imported ones. To ensure quality, I chose Watsons and Mannings, the largest supermarket chains in Hong Kong. Although the price is already dozens of dollars higher than pharmacies, paying extra for peace of mind is worth it. So the selling point of my source is obtaining goods from the best and most legitimate supermarkets in Hong Kong, ensuring the quality of the milk powder!LogisticsIn China, especially in Shenzhen, SF Express has the best reputation for courier services. Since I've chosen the best milk powder, I should also choose the best delivery service. Although SF Express charges a premium to ship out-of-province, ensuring the safety of the babies' milk powder makes these costs negligible! Some friends may wonder what to do in areas where SF Express doesn't deliver. My approach is not to ship to areas where SF Express cannot reach. Everyone knows about the rough handling methods of many courier companies. To ensure the safety of the milk powder, I apologize to friends in regions not served by SF Express.Having chosen a good courier company, another crucial matter is packaging. I searched online for information related to milk powder packaging using relevant keywords. I found videos and articles, and I chose the safest method: at least four layers of packaging. The first layer is cling film, ensuring the milk powder does not come into direct contact with the packaging. The second layer is bubble wrap, reducing damage caused by hard impacts. The third layer is custom-made foam, which truly prevents the milk powder cans from being crushed. The fourth layer is a five-layer cardboard box, serving to fix the packaging. The fifth layer is a shopping bag, mainly to prevent water damage from rain;Taobao StoreSince I had previously applied for a Taobao store, I'll skip the application process here.About the store name, it should undoubtedly relate to your main product. Here I chose "Hong Kong ** Purchase," where Hong Kong refers to my source, "childhood" in ancient times means infant, and "purchase" means buying. You can interpret it as "Hong Kong Baby Products Purchase";Since the main product is Hong Kong version milk powder, it’s time to start listing products on the Taobao store. The most important part of listing products is the main image and detailed information about the milk powder. Since milk powder is a relatively standardized product, you can find relevant information from competitors on Taobao, but what to do if many images have watermarks? For programmers, this is a trivial task; watermarks can be removed, though it's quite labor-intensive. Another important point is that each milk powder brand has its own official website, where you can obtain very detailed information, enough to list the product!Store decoration is also very important. Now Taobao旺铺 (Taobao旺铺) is free, so why not use it? When starting out, you can take a look at well-done competitor stores, mimic their style, and add your own characteristics. Don’t try to create something overly unconventional. For programmers, decorating a store is incredibly simple. Especially in places where HTML is needed, which would be difficult for ordinary users, Web programmers are very familiar with it!To save valuable customer service time, it's recommended to create some custom pages and place frequently asked questions there!FeaturesOn Taobao, selling things at a lower price is important, but having unique features is even more critical. Besides the milk powder coming from the best sources in Hong Kong, another feature of my store is custom order-based purchases, complete with original shopping receipts. Nowadays, many people only have one child, who is cherished greatly. They save on themselves but want the best for their babies. Parents around the world are truly amazing!SummaryAll of the above are my personal experiences with running a store, shared without reservation. Action speaks louder than words; once you've thought something through, take action immediately. Programmers have inherent technical advantages in this field.Through the above article, you may not yet see the advantage of being a programmer. The next article will truly highlight the technical edge, so stay tuned!
