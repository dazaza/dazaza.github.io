Recruitment Prospectus for Part-time (Master's) Graduate Students in Agricultural Economics and Management (Finance Direction) at Renmin University of China

by shuoshi on 2011-08-22 17:38:02

Renmin University of China (RUC) is a comprehensive research university focusing on humanities and social sciences, and is directly under the administration of the Ministry of Education. Currently, it is one of the key universities in the national "985 Project" and "211 Project". zzy.china-b.com.

The School of Agriculture and Rural Development at Renmin University of China is the earliest and most influential school among key universities in China for studying the "three rural issues" (agriculture, rural areas, and farmers). The current dean is Professor Wen Tiemjun, a well-known expert on the "three rural issues" in China. The school is dedicated to the theoretical and practical research of China's economy and rural finance, and has several national-level research centers. It focuses on the theoretical and practical research of China's agricultural economy and rural finance, integrating agricultural economic management knowledge and financial-related knowledge into a complete system. It is an important base for cultivating comprehensively applied economic management talents in agriculture-related fields, as well as an important academic research center for the discipline of agricultural economic management in China. To cultivate high-level professional economic talents, the school offers postgraduate courses in agricultural economic management.

I. Course Advantages

Eligible to apply for a Master of Management degree from Renmin University of China

Two-year program, Beijing students take classes on weekends; out-of-town students have concentrated classes, with course materials provided during other times. Classes are taught by senior professors and doctoral supervisors from our university, and renowned scholars and experts from inside and outside the university give specialized lectures. Harvard-style case teaching is used according to the latest postgraduate training program, sharing faculty resources; foreign flexible teaching systems are introduced, using a credit system, and those who complete the required credits will be allowed to graduate.

Accompanying English pre-exam tutoring classes, increasing the success rate of applying for a master's degree by 100%

II. Enrollment Conditions

1. Uphold the four fundamental principles, be a good business backbone, be healthy, and able to persist in on-the-job study.

2. Bachelor's degree or above, with at least two years of work experience (those with associate degrees and significant achievements in work may have relaxed conditions).

III. Review and Admission

1. Fill out the enrollment registration form, provide copies of your final academic qualification certificate, degree certificate, ID card, and six 1-inch and four 2-inch color photos;

2. Renmin University of China reviews the written materials of applicants, and those who meet the requirements will be issued an admission notice for the Renmin University of China postgraduate course program and student records will be established.

IV. Certificate Obtained

Within the specified period, students who complete the prescribed learning projects of the advanced studies program and pass the prescribed course exams will be awarded a Renmin University of China postgraduate course advanced studies program completion certificate by the Graduate School of Renmin University of China, with steel seal, red seal, and dean's seal. Those who meet the conditions for applying for a degree can apply for a Master of Management degree according to the regulations of the State Council Academic Degrees Committee Office (1998) No. 54 document and relevant regulations of the Graduate School of Renmin University of China.

V. Fee Standards

1. Tuition: 23,000 yuan (two years)

2. Fees incurred during the study process are borne by the students themselves.

Note: After one week of classes, if a student cannot complete their studies due to personal reasons, it will be considered as automatic abandonment of study, and no tuition refund will be given.
