Wife's Diary, Recording Our Happiness

In the middle of the night, I woke up and felt my husband tightly hugging me. I secretly rejoiced! I thought: This guy usually appears so cool, but he inadvertently reveals his true feelings while sleeping. I was deeply moved and was about to enjoy his embrace when I heard him muttering in a daze: "Honey, it's so cold!" At that moment, I wanted to kick him off the bed.

One day, while watching TV with my husband, an actress on the screen was dancing ballet. My husband said to me: "Honey, you're also suited for ballet." I secretly rejoiced again! I thought: My husband must think I have a good figure. But I wanted him to compliment me more directly, so I held my breath and asked him: "Why do you think I'm suited for ballet?" My husband seriously and professionally replied: "People who dance ballet can't have too big chests." I almost fell off the chair laughing.

On a weekend morning after getting up, I mentioned our recent expenses to my husband, feeling that we often waste money, which isn't good. Thus, we decided to correct this habit. That evening, my husband accompanied me to the supermarket. I saw the snack "Sachima" that I like, but wasn't sure which brand to buy. So, I picked one randomly priced at 4 kuai 8 mao. Just as I was about to take it, I heard my husband shouting incessantly: "4 kuai 6 mao, 4 kuai 6 mao!" After hearing that, I couldn't stop laughing, realizing how serious he was about our cost-saving plan.

One morning, I was resting while my husband went to work. I sent him to the elevator, and as the door opened, I turned around to go home. Suddenly, I heard my husband calling me from behind. Turning back, I saw him standing at the elevator entrance, one foot raised blocking the door, leaning forward playfully saying: "Honey, there's no one inside, let's kiss!" I was both annoyed and amused!

Once, while combing my hair in front of the mirror, I said to my husband: "Imagine if my husband came home every day after work to cook and wash clothes, and all I had to do was go to work, wouldn't that be great?" My husband walked over to me, shaking me and saying: "Honey, wake up, wake up, it's not early anymore." I was completely defeated by my husband.

My husband and I like watching DVDs together. However, every time we need to change the disc, it's painful, especially in winter when we don't want to get out of bed. So, every time the screen stopped, I would immediately turn my head and pretend to sleep, even snoring. Seeing this, my husband could only get out of bed himself to change the disc. Once the disc was loaded, I would immediately wake up, pretending to be groggy, asking: "What happened? What happened? Need to change the disc? Let me do it, I'll do it." My husband said I was too naughty. A few days later, I had forgotten about this incident. When it was time to change the disc, I just wanted to call him, but he had already turned his head and pretended to sleep. Naturally, I followed the same method, laughing myself to death.

After washing the dishes, I also scrubbed the stainless steel pot very hard, making it shinier than when it was new. Excitedly holding the pot, I showed it to my husband who was standing on a stool drying clothes on the balcony. He tilted his head left and right, carefully examining the pot without praising me. Just as I was about to ask him, he nonchalantly smoothed his hair and said, "Hmm, this young man is quite handsome..."

At first, my wife said she didn't know how to cook. I said, "No way, I can cook." Now, I cook! Haha.

When he picks me up after work, I cried out that I wanted to buy bananas. Upon arrival, we found two girls from my company buying them. I knew them well, but he didn't. I called out to them: "Great! I don't have to buy them then?" The girl generously handed me a bag of bananas: "Take it! Don't be shy!" I took only one, and the girl said: "Take more! Why are you being so polite?" He also urged me: "Take two or three!" My colleague hesitated for a moment and quickly agreed: "Take more, take more!" He said no, two were enough. I broke another one off, surprised at how he could embarrass me like that, but he handed me the net bag and gave the two bananas to my colleague, seriously saying: "Thank you!" Everyone laughed uncontrollably the next day at work...

My husband loves hiding in the house and letting me find him, but our house is too small, so I always find him easily. One night before sleeping, he went to turn off the light (the switch is a bit far from the bed). After turning it off, I saw him quickly squat down. Even though I could see clearly (my night vision is excellent), I kept quiet. I saw him squat for a while, then crawl towards the bed. I tried not to laugh as he carefully crawled to the edge of the bed and peeked out. Suddenly, I pounced on him, scaring him! Ha ha, I laughed wildly!

In my husband's eyes, I am a famous nearsighted person with low IQ. However, sometimes, he falls for my tricks. The other day, while walking through a bustling mall, we got separated, but I quickly spotted him. I saw him anxiously looking around behind him. I walked up behind him and loudly shouted his name. He turned around abruptly, and I pretended not to see him, still shouting loudly and acting scared and anxious. He happily laughed and hugged me, saying, "Oh, silly!" Oh, it was so sweet!

I remembered another one: Last night after dinner, while taking a walk in the yard with my husband, we suddenly saw a cockroach on the road. I screamed, "Husband, step on it, step on it, kill it!" Then I also stretched my foot to prepare to step on it. My husband said, "Ah, it's Xiaqiang, let it go." It made me feel like I was cruel and lacked compassion.

My husband takes the bus home, and the road was jammed. He sent me a text message telling me to take a detour home. I replied, telling him to sleep on the bus for a while due to the traffic jam. He replied: No! If I dream of you, it will be scary!

One day, while watching a sports game on TV, China's team lost again, and I confidently declared: "In the future, I will make my child practice sports to bring glory to the country!" My husband looked up from reading a book and glanced at me, saying, "Then let him practice weightlifting, judging by his mother's physique, he can do it!" Wailing...

One day, my husband and I discussed the dumb topic everyone talks about: "Would you be a man or a woman in the next life?" After thinking for a long time, I said, "In my next life, I want to be a man, and let you be a woman to serve me!" My husband turned his face and looked at me, saying, "You said the same thing in your last life"...

Yesterday, my husband and I fought rats at home. My husband was very brave and stepped on the rat to death. I greatly praised his bravery, but he sorrowfully said, "Ah, I remembered the 'Shuke and Beta' I watched when I was a kid, it makes me feel so sad!"

The first time I cooked for my husband, my skills were really not refined, and the taste, aroma, and appearance of the dishes were nowhere near satisfactory. My husband cutely buried his head and ate while consoling me, saying, "Dear, it's okay, providing me with food and shelter is enough, I don't demand prosperity."
