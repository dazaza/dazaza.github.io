Today is the New Year's Eve, it's time for reckoning. NBA popular teams, let's see who can make it to the top eight in CBA. This season is a bit chaotic because of the complete opening up of foreign players, which has led to a revolutionary change in the strength of the teams. Although there is still some time left, but we can rank them for fun. According to the current ranking table, the last four teams seem to have no chance. Tianjin and Qingdao are new promoted teams, they didn't have much expectation at the beginning, mainly to adapt to the league, players need to adapt, clubs also need to adapt. Now, not being at the bottom is already a pretty good result, I guess there is no need to make big changes and spend more money to find help. Shanghai had a lot of issues this year, fights broke out from time to time, sponsors wanted to withdraw, I think the main problem is still money. This couple of poverty has many sorrows, the fight behind should still be about money, the morale of the players is gone, the spirit is lost, the things they do are not very reliable. Yunnan's lack of money is an old issue, almost every year there are issues like wage arrears, so whether Jia Bo will come back after the New Year is still a question, how can they turn the situation around? Playing sports, in the end, is all about money, achievements mostly rely on money to support.

Let's talk about the top four, currently Xinjiang, Guangdong, Jiangsu and Shaanxi. It is estimated that Xinjiang, Guangdong and Jiangsu entering the top eight is a sure thing. The remaining teams, currently ranked fourth Shaanxi and fifth Dongguan, are in a relatively advantageous position. Dongguan showed the style of rising to the next level last year, the main reason for the recent influence was the injury of foreign players, otherwise it would not be the fifth place now. Shanxi A Travel Alliance is essentially still supported by the funds of that casino, with money, the morale of everyone is relatively smooth. Just before the New Year, Da Long gave Xinjiang a hard slap on the head. As long as there is no problem with money, entering the top eight should also be a matter of course. The remaining nine teams will fight to death for the last three seats, NBA popular teams, it's called fish die net break, so there will be good shows after the New Year.

Currently ranked sixth Fujian and fourteenth Liaoning only differ by three games, anything could happen. Liaoning team is not easy to offend, last year they were the runner-up after all, even a lean camel is bigger than a horse. Liaoning team did not have a good New Year, definitely with the new foreign player and new situation, they are preparing for battle and practicing the new lineup. Bayi is in trouble this year, mainly due to injuries of Daye and Li Nan, relying on Mo Ke to hold on, it's difficult for coach A. I feel that even if Daye recovers from his injury, he won't be able to return to form quickly. In the previous period, he almost insisted on playing the whole game every match, especially in the fourth quarter, Daye was suffering a lot, watching him play was heart-wrenching. Shanxi's situation depends on Bangzi, this guy's level is there, but he is a bit lazy, actually it would have been better not to replace him. Now the fate of the entire team, honestly speaking, rests on his shoulders alone, if everything goes well, it's fine, if something happens, it's over. The two Zhejiang teams are not easy to deal with, one of them should be able to carve out a bloody path, I am more optimistic about Wanmar this year, because they have Schneider. Next year I will be optimistic about Guangsha, Wang Fei's team still needs some time to train. Currently ranked sixth Fujian is an old hand team, from the boss to the coach and athletes, they are all experienced. This kind of team is difficult to rise, but it's also not easy to fall, so Fujian has a pretty good chance to enter the top eight.

In summary, I am more optimistic about Dongguan, Shaanxi, Fujian, Wanmar, Shanxi and Liaoning, of course, it doesn't mean other teams don't have a chance. One man's opinion, just for fun during the New Year. Especially Wang Fei, Da Bin and Xiao Min, we've known each other for so many years, don't hate me. Haha, hope my predictions are all wrong like Pele's.
