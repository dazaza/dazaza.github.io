[Hebei] Gao Jinhao emphasized at the provincial conference on the construction of inter-village roads and the "seven publicities" of rural highways

Gao Jinhao, the Party Secretary and Director of the Hebei Provincial Department of Transportation, attended the meeting and delivered an important speech. He emphasized that it is necessary to pay high attention, grasp the key points, fully promote the construction of inter-village roads, serve the travel needs of farmers, and provide strong transportation support for the construction of a socialist new countryside; we need to raise awareness, strengthen measures, and accelerate the promotion of the "seven public" system to ensure the rapid and good development of rural road construction. Gao Jinhao stressed that promoting the construction of inter-village roads is a specific action to implement the deployment decisions of the Provincial Party Committee and Provincial Government, a major measure to meet the travel needs of farmers, and an objective requirement for "pioneering the construction of a strong transportation province, pioneering the modernization of transportation, and constructing harmonious transportation." The entire system at all levels and relevant departments must fully recognize the great significance of accelerating the construction of inter-village roads, enhance the sense of the overall situation and responsibility, enhance a sense of urgency and mission, scientifically plan, carefully organize, earnestly implement, and fully promote. By implementing the construction of inter-village roads, a solid foundation will be laid for "two pioneers, one well done." He pointed out that to accelerate the construction of inter-village roads, we must adhere to the "five equal emphasis": First, adhere to equal emphasis on government leadership and industry guidance. We must pay more attention to strengthening the main status and leading role of county governments, which is the key to whether the construction of inter-village roads can proceed smoothly; meanwhile, the provincial department will focus on providing support in two aspects: newly added subsidy funds and implementing encouraging policies to better play a guiding role. Second, adhere to equal emphasis on overall planning and annual plans. Quickly research and compile the inter-village road construction plan from 2012 to 2015; quickly arrange this year's inter-village road construction plan; do a good job in classified guidance. Third, adhere to equal emphasis on local pilots and overall promotion. On the one hand, we must seriously carry out local pilot work, and on the other hand, we must solidly carry out overall promotion work. Fourth, adhere to equal emphasis on construction speed and quality. We must pay attention to speed, but even more so to quality, and improve standards to ensure that construction speed is accelerated without affecting engineering quality and with standards improved. Fifth, adhere to equal emphasis on project construction and maintenance management. Strengthening maintenance management work must be placed on an equally important position as new construction projects. Actively promote the reform of rural road maintenance management, conduct a three-year "management and maintenance year" activity in depth, improve and implement reward and punishment measures. Gao Jinhao emphasized that the "seven public" system for rural road construction is another major measure taken by our province in the field of廉政construction after the "ten public" system for highway construction. Promoting the "seven public" system throughout the entire system not only provides an important guarantee for large-scale advancement of inter-village road construction, but also meets the urgent need to strengthen廉政work in the transportation system, which is of great significance and inevitable. He pointed out that the "seven public" system for rural road construction can effectively standardize bidding, material procurement, and fund use, effectively improve engineering quality and protect the interests of the masses, and effectively protect cadres from problems or fewer problems. To promote the "seven public" system, the following three points must be ensured: First, rapidly expand. Develop an implementation plan to ensure rapid, orderly, and efficient advancement; comprehensively standardize the content and time of public projects, expand the scope of publicity, and form a comprehensive monitoring chain for rural road construction, maintenance, and management. Second, highlight key points. Highlight the public announcement of construction plans, highlight the public announcement of quality supervision, highlight the public announcement of fund management, highlight the public announcement of completion acceptance. Third, strictly hold accountable. For city bureaus that fail to truly and comprehensively disclose information as required and according to specifications, are perfunctory, and merely go through the motions, the provincial department will have talks with the main responsible comrades; for participating units that fail to implement the "seven public" system or falsify information during disclosure, they will be listed on the "blacklist" and resolutely expelled from the Hebei transportation construction market; for those who cause significant losses or serious impacts due to non-disclosure or insufficient disclosure, the responsibilities of related leaders and responsible personnel will be pursued. Gao Jinhao emphasized that the construction of inter-village roads and the "seven public" work are two very important and urgent tasks in the development of rural roads in our province, relating to the quality and effectiveness of rural road development during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period. At all levels, transportation authorities must recognize the situation, seize opportunities, unify thoughts and actions with the provincial department's decision-making and deployment, list it as a "top leader" project, carefully study, scientifically plan, meticulously deploy, and strengthen scheduling. Coordination and work guidance must be carried out to effectively implement leadership responsibilities, ensuring that the main leaders personally take charge, the principal leaders focus on taking charge, and related leaders cooperate in taking charge, ensuring the smooth advancement of the two works. Special supervision should be carried out, and the results should be included in the annual rural road development goal assessment content, linked to excellence evaluation, ensuring that all work is implemented. At the meeting, Gao Jinhao signed the responsibility statement for the comprehensive promotion of the "seven public" system for rural road construction with the transportation bureaus of various designated cities. Yang Guohua, member of the Party Leadership Group and Deputy Director of the Department, presided over the meeting and made arrangements and deployments for the province's inter-village road work. Zhang Sumin, stationed discipline inspection group leader and supervisory special commissioner, made arrangements and deployments for the province's rural road "seven public" work. The main responsible persons of the transportation bureaus of Xingtai and Tangshan cities made typical speeches. Members of the Department's Rural Road Construction "Seven Public" Work Leading Group, directors of transportation bureaus of various designated cities, deputy directors in charge of rural road work, discipline inspection secretaries, directors of local road offices, and directors of planning offices participated in the meeting.
