This year, the automobile market has been booming with many merchants having already completed their annual sales targets ahead of schedule. They no longer worry about year-end sales targets, and most models even experience a supply falling short of demand. Therefore, although the year-end price cuts to "meet targets" no longer exist, does that mean discounts are also decreasing? Today, Yangtze Evening News will introduce you to four models with significant December discounts: &；nbsp； 　　

**Buick Excelle**

Guidance Price: RMB 103,800 - 139,800

Recent Discount: Around RMB 20,000

The new 2006 Buick Excelle series made its debut at the Guangzhou International Auto Show, including a newly launched three-box Excelle 1.8MT-LE manual comfort version. The 2006 Excelle series added configurations without increasing prices, enhancing cost-effectiveness. While making their appearance at the auto show, these vehicles were simultaneously listed for sale at Buick dealerships nationwide, bringing consumers an early cross-year super-value experience in mid-level cars.

It is reported that apart from the three-box 1.6LX MT model, all other models... [detailed content] have been equipped with reverse parking sensors (including Excelle 1.8LS AT, Excelle 1.8LE AT, Excelle 1.6LE AT, Excelle 1.6LE MT, as well as HRV manual and automatic versions), providing great convenience for parking. In addition, the entire series has been upgraded with new pull-out door handles, harmonizing better with the body design. The top-tier Excelle 1.8LS-AT automatic version also features an added cruise control system.

The maintenance and repair costs of the Buick Excelle are relatively cheap. A minor maintenance service involving oil and oil filter changes costs around RMB 200, while major maintenance, including oil and three filters (oil filter, air filter, and fuel filter) replacement, costs around RMB 400. This price not only offers a significant advantage compared to similar level vehicles but also over some small cars, making it a good choice for family car buyers.

Editor's Comment: As one of the mainstream products in the domestic mid-level car market, the Buick Excelle series has always been favored by a wide range of consumers. The Excelle’s exterior design is more full-bodied and grand compared to older versions, with new chrome front grille plates and teardrop-shaped tail lights adding a sense of refinement alongside its imposing presence. The interior work is meticulous, using quality materials and homey color schemes, making it very suitable for family use.
