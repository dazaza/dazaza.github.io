Mission of Letting Go (Part III)

Chapter Three: Beauty Literature Club

After being tortured by a certain girl, Xiao Ye felt gloomy for half a minute. Then he got up and slowly searched for Class 8 of Grade 2.

Fortunately, the Grade 2 building wasn't hard to find. At that moment, class was in session, and Ye Feng stood at the door of Class 8 and shouted loudly: "Speech."

As a result, he received a round of white eyes from the entire class. Was this guy an idiot? Did he think it was cool?

At this time, an object resembling a teacher on the podium stared fixedly at Ye Feng. Why "resembling"? Because this person appeared to be only about 150 cm tall. It was clear that this was someone easy to bully. (I must be honest, this is based on my Grade 2 homeroom teacher, who is actually really great.)

"You are Ye Feng, right? I am your homeroom teacher, Hu Bi Hui. Everyone, quiet down. This is Ye Feng, the new transfer student. I hope everyone can get along well. Alright, Ye Feng, you can temporarily sit next to Liu Zhe."

Looking at Ye Feng's grand manner of walking, Mr. Hu (the nickname for the homeroom teacher, get used to it) had doubts. The records showed that he ranked first in the district-wide exam last time, and the principal also mentioned that he had a strong background, so everything should be done according to his wishes. Could he be from some noble family? How did he end up in such a place?

Ye Feng didn't care about all these things. He just sat down at the only empty seat in the classroom. Anyway, it was only temporary, and there was no need to take it too seriously.

At this moment, a fat boy next to him gently poked Ye Feng with a pen.

"What?" This fat boy had thin eyebrows, small eyes, and chubby, loose cheeks, making one feel like pinching them hard.

"You're really lucky, transferring here and getting into this class. This class is famously unbothered. As long as it's not something major, you can do whatever you want."

"What's going on?" This is still high school, not university.

"Every school has students who don't study. The school uses class division to gather all these people together, intending to manage them specifically. Later, they couldn't handle it anymore, so they just let us be."

"Huh, isn't this class the most relaxed one in the whole school? Are there many beautiful girls here?" Nie Team indeed understood him very well.

"Pfft, this school only cares about the college entrance examination rate. Those willing to study are all bookworms who don't even bother to dress up. There are plenty of ugly ones."

"Is that so? Sigh." Nie Team, you're harsh.

"However, there are still some beauties, like Li Shishi from the neighboring Class 4, and... (500 words omitted)" Ha ha, my spring is coming! Nie Team, I still love you. (Nie Team: Ye Feng, you truly have distinct love and hate.)

"But speaking of the most beautiful, they are the four members of the Six-Pointed Star Literature Club. They are called... oh, sorry. Four super-beautiful girls. Unfortunately, their recruitment standards are extremely high. In three years since its establishment, there has been only one male member..."

Six-Pointed Star, Literature Club? Isn't it... tonight at 11 PM. Four beauties... hahaha... Xiao Ye was daydreaming again.

"Liu Zhe, do you know how to get to the literature club?" Big Brother Hu already mentioned this fat boy's name.

"Do you really want to go? Don't. That's a forbidden zone for boys." Although Liu Zhe said this, he still told Ye Feng how to get there.

"No problem, they will let me join." After saying this, Ye Feng lay down and went to sleep.

When Ye Feng woke up, finished eating dinner, and returned to the dormitory, his roommate still wasn't there. Ye Feng could only reluctantly take another nap.

The phone vibrated a few times in his pocket, waking Ye Feng up. He checked the phone and saw a text message: "Why haven't you come yet?"

Looking at the time, damn! It was already 11:30 PM, and he overslept again. He hurriedly rushed to the literature club.

When he arrived at the literature club and opened the door, wow! This was definitely the biggest visual impact Ye Feng had ever experienced in his life. Four stunning beauties were simultaneously in his line of sight.

A girl wore a simple white dress, her flowing long hair complemented by black-framed glasses against her pale skin, exuding a scholarly aura. She said to Ye Feng: "Why are you so late?"

"I overslept."

Everyone facepalmed...

"Uh, I'm Si Zi Mu. These are Ji Mo Mo, Xu Lian, Xuan Yuan Lu, and Shi Shao Feng."

Ji Mo Mo: Like a fairy untouched by worldly affairs, she coldly looked at Ye Feng. Her delicate features seemed carved from ice, holy yet enchanting.

Xu Lian: A full bosom, sensuous red lips slightly curled upward, sparkling eyes blinked once, and she sent Ye Feng a fiery glance.

Xuan Yuan Lu: I guess no introduction is needed. She didn't even look at Ye Feng and was playing games nearby.

Shi Shao Feng: The legendary only male member of the Six-Pointed Star Literature Club. With a fresh sporty haircut and a somewhat clumsy appearance.

Ye Feng glanced at the four beauties for the eighth time, finally satisfied, and introduced himself briefly: "Ye Feng. Two-stage lightning controller."

After Ye Feng finished speaking, even Xuan Yuan Lu, who was playing games, turned around and looked at Ye Feng with surprise. Not because Ye Feng was only at two stages—among potential energy users, there are seven stages in total, so two stages are relatively low—but they were surprised that Ye Feng's elemental attribute was lightning. Each person's physical constitution determines their elemental attribute (of course, this applies only to controllers), and electricity is the most microscopic element that the human body can stimulate. Therefore, lightning controllers among potential energy users are as rare as dinosaurs.

Although Ye Feng was only at two stages, it was because the electronic elements in the air were several times fewer than other elements, so cultivation was more challenging. However, when Ye Feng was about to leave home, his old man gave him a meteorite from outer space, rich in electronic elements. He believed it would accelerate Ye Feng's cultivation.

"Haha, finally, a male has come. Our team finally has a bit of masculinity now. Hello, Ye Feng, I'm Shi Shao Feng, call me Stone." As fellow males, Stone was very enthusiastic toward Ye Feng.

"Ye Feng, 17 years old, from Guangdong. Outstanding academically since childhood, ranked first in the district-wide exam recently. Last year, he led Da Ao Middle School to win the championship of the National High School Basketball League in Guangdong but missed subsequent matches due to transferring, causing the team to stop advancing. He also led the H Game Studio to win the championship of the Warcraft III Southern China Regional Tournament. These are your achievements as an ordinary person. But as a potential energy user, which is a secret identity, you are too high-profile." Si Zi Mu read out Ye Feng's profile in one breath.

At this moment, under everyone's expectant gazes, Ye Feng burst out with: "Do you think it's easy?"

Then, amidst everyone's anticipation, Ye Feng said this sentence: "I really want to shout to the whole world: I am very low-key."
