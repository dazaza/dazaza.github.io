Linyi, February 27th (reported by Xing Meng, with correspondents Suena Hua and Juan Na Zhu) In order to overdraw and cash out, the CEO of Weimian's email was exposed: sparing no effort to cut costs for survival. On the Hanshufang Novel website at www.hanshufang.com, updates are faster and chapters more abundant. A man from the Mudan District of Heze misused other people's identities to apply for 42 credit cards from the same bank. Using a card-to-card repayment method, he failed to return over 780,000 yuan of overdrafts.

Recently, after the final verdict by the Intermediate People's Court of Heze, the man was sentenced to ten years in prison and fined 100,000 yuan. Zhang Hua (a pseudonym) is from the Mudan District of Heze and runs a vegetable business. Between 2007 and 2008, Zhang Hua’s business suffered consecutive losses, leading to financial difficulties. To manage his funds, Zhang Hua considered using credit card overdrafts, but the limited credit limit on a few cards could not meet his needs. Thus, he misused the names of his relatives and others to apply for 42 credit cards in bulk from a Heze bank.

After obtaining the cards, Zhang Hua frequently used 40 of them to overdraw and cash out. However, instead of using this money for his business, he squandered it on luxury items. Zhang Hua adopted a card-to-card repayment method, accumulating a total overdraft of over 780,000 yuan, which he then fled without repaying.

In March 2009, due to unsuccessful collection attempts, the bank reported the case to the Economic Investigation Brigade of the Mudan Public Security Bureau, which immediately launched an investigation. Through the bank system, the police discovered that Zhang Hua had applied for 42 credit cards under false pretenses, with malicious overdrafts totaling over 780,000 yuan, an especially large amount. In September 2010, the officers from the Economic Investigation Brigade of the Mudan Public Security Bureau arrested the suspect Zhang Hua who was hiding in Qingdao.

Recently, after the first and second trials by the Mudan District People's Court and the Intermediate People's Court of Heze, the criminal behavior of the suspect Zhang Hua was confirmed. Due to malicious overdraft of credit cards in an especially large amount, he was legally sentenced to ten years in prison and fined 100,000 yuan.
