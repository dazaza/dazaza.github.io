Jilin will have obvious snowfall in the next three days, and some areas will have heavy snow weather forecast news center

by psyzems2 on 2012-03-05 23:21:37

Monitoring data shows that from February 27th to March 3rd, Jilin Province received less precipitation than usual, with an average rainfall of 0.2 millimeters, which is 80% less than the long-term average for the same period. Among these, Changchun received 0.7 millimeters, 22% less than the long-term average; Siping and Jilin were respectively 83% and 77% less than the long-term average, while other regions had virtually no precipitation. In addition, the province's average temperature was -7.4℃, 1℃ lower than the long-term average.

According to the latest forecast from China Weather Network, over the next three days, Jilin Province will experience a significant snowfall weather process, with some areas seeing heavy snow. After the snowfall, temperatures will also drop, and it is recommended that the general public adjust their clothing according to the weather conditions to prevent catching a cold.

From tomorrow day to night, the whole province will be overcast with Liaoyuan, Jilin, Tonghua, and Baishan regions experiencing heavy snow, southern Changchun and Siping having moderate to heavy snow, Yanbian and Changbai Mountain Pool North District having moderate snow, while other regions have light snow.

Affected by high-altitude troughs and surface low-pressure inverted troughs, this morning, some parts of Jilin Province have already experienced light snow or sleet weather. Meteorological experts analyzed that the rain-snow weather will gradually intensify tonight and continue until the 7th. The central and southern regions will see sleet turning into heavy snow, with some places possibly seeing heavy snow, with total precipitation reaching 6-12 millimeters; the eastern region will see sleet turning into moderate to heavy snow, with total precipitation reaching 4-7 millimeters; the western region will have light to moderate snow, with total precipitation being 2-5 millimeters.

The Jilin Meteorological Observatory predicts that strong precipitation will mainly concentrate from tonight to tomorrow, with some places seeing heavy snow. Temperatures will drop after the snowfall, with cumulative temperature drops from the night of the 5th to the 9th expected to reach 5~8℃. Specific forecasts are as follows:

Today during the day and at night, the whole province will be cloudy turning overcast, with Songyuan's southern and eastern regions, Changchun, Siping, Liaoyuan, and Jilin regions experiencing sleet turning into heavy snow, some places possibly seeing heavy snow, Tonghua and Baishan regions experiencing sleet turning into moderate to heavy snow, other regions having light snow, most regions having 3-4 level southwest winds turning into northeast winds. At night, the lowest temperatures in most regions across the province will be -3~-7℃.

Experts remind that during this snowfall weather process, precipitation will be relatively large, and ice may form on roads, power lines, or transformers and other facilities across most parts of the province. It is suggested that relevant departments do a good job in preventive work; additionally, it is suggested that relevant enterprises and vegetable farmers promptly clear snow and ice from the tops of greenhouses to prevent damage to greenhouses and other facilities; all regions should take advantage of this rainy (snowy) weather process to actively organize artificial rain (snow) enhancement operations to prevent spring drought.
