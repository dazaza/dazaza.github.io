Notification Reminder: Yesterday, the Chongyang District and Lvyuan District of Changchun City launched the opening ceremony of key projects, with 168 major projects starting construction comprehensively. Provincial and municipal leaders such as Han Changfu, Wang Rulin, Gao Guangbin, and Cui Jie attended the opening ceremonies of the two districts.

Among them, Chongyang District started 68 projects in the first half of the year, with a planned total investment of 6 billion yuan. Lvyuan District concentrated on starting 10 major categories and 100 key projects and major projects, with a planned total investment of 31.2 billion yuan, and an annual investment of 15.2 billion yuan.

Yesterday, Han Changfu and Gao Guangbin also unveiled the Changchun West New City.

Focus One

West Railway Station

In five years, with the commissioning of the West Railway Station, if you take a high-speed train southward, it will replace Changchun Station as the departure station.

The West Railway Station is preliminarily scheduled to start construction bidding in mid-May and begin construction in early June, with plans to be put into use within five years.

Yesterday, the "conceptual design plan" of the West Railway Station was revealed.

Plan

Reserved solar panel installation interface

On the exterior facade of the West Railway Station, the colors are mainly light tones. In response to the severe cold climate characteristics, the design focuses on building energy conservation and environmental protection.

On the exterior walls, the insulation structure of the building envelope is strengthened, with less use of large-area glass curtain walls and large-area roof lighting, controlling building energy consumption.

Solar panel installation interfaces are reserved on the roof and canopy roof cover.

The waiting hall inside the station and major waiting spaces adopt a geothermal underfloor heating system.

Subway transfer level is reserved

For the West Railway Station, the station building adopts a T-shaped floor plan, with the vertical functions roughly divided into three layers: the top layer mainly consists of the elevated waiting hall (designed height of 10 meters), the middle layer mainly consists of the platform level and entrance hall (ground level), and the lower layer consists of the exit channel and subway transfer level (designed height of -8.5 meters).

At the platform level, there are entrance halls, ticket offices, basic platform waiting rooms, office areas, and VIP waiting rooms on the south side.

The elevated waiting area has waiting halls, soft-seat waiting, barrier-free waiting, and commercial service facilities. Disabled elevators go directly to the platform.

West Railway Station Project Profile

Scale: Building area of 59,487 square meters, exceeding Changchun Station

Value: Construction cost of approximately 1 billion yuan

Lifespan: The station building is designed for a lifespan of 100 years

Load: Maximum gathering number of people is 7,000, with peak hour gathering numbers estimated to reach 4,775 people

Capacity: By 2020, the estimated annual passenger volume is expected to reach 17.43 million people/year (48,000 people/day)

Two tunnels built on both sides of the station building

Deputy District Chief Lin Song of Lvyuan District introduced that two tunnels will be built on both sides of the West Railway Station, allowing Nanyang Road and Shuangfeng Street to cross the railway.

Planned bus station on the west side

To the west of the West Railway Station station building, a long-distance bus station is planned.

Function

High-speed train starting and terminal station

After the completion of the West Railway Station, what impact will it have on residents' travel?

Lin Song introduced that the West Railway Station mainly handles the starting and terminal stations for high-speed trains south of Changchun, some intercity trains, and through operations for high-speed trains.

This means that after the completion of the West Railway Station, high-speed rail lines heading towards Dalian, Beijing, and other directions via Shenyang, the West Railway Station will become the starting and terminal station. Departing from there, it will take only 4 hours to Beijing, 3 hours to Dalian, 1 hour to Shenyang, and less than half an hour to Jilin.

Significance

One of the largest engines of the West New City

Yesterday, the unveiling ceremony of the Changchun West New City took place. Lin Song said: "The West New City is not an urban developed area; it is entirely a new district. Now, the 13 square kilometers of the West New City was originally on the outskirts of the city, consisting of Shuangfeng Village, Tongxin Village, and Xingwang Village in the Xinxin Town of Lvyuan District. After being planned as the West New City, it will become urban planning land and, after construction, will become a new center of Changchun City." With this land revitalized, the West Railway Station has become one of the largest engines.
