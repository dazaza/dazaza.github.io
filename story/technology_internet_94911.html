Harmonious visual marketing in store clothing displays can double sales.

by yilbi on 2008-09-26 08:06:09

Abstract: In the era of product homogenization, terminal displays have become silent promoters. An increasing number of companies are beginning to realize the various benefits that terminal presentation brings to brand communication and sales performance enhancement.

Window mannequin displays should be "three-ized"

Improving customer in-store rate

The window is a carrier for transmitting brand culture and sales information, and promotion is the main purpose of window design. To achieve marketing goals, display artists need to attract customers into the store and stimulate their purchasing desire through the combination and arrangement of windows, clothing, mannequins, props, and background advertisements. On the other hand, the window also plays a role in spreading brand culture.

Since the window has dual tasks, therefore, the design style of the window varies according to different brand positioning, seasons, and marketing objectives. Some window designs focus on emphasizing sales information, adopting a more direct communication method. Besides displaying products in the window, some posters with promotional information are also placed to pursue an immediate effect, making it clear for customers to understand and stimulating their desire to enter the store. As the focus of window displays - mannequin displays, they need to achieve:

1. Harmonious coordination

Harmonious coordination includes two meanings, one is the harmony of a single mannequin, and the other is the harmony of styles between mannequins. Let the combination of upper and lower wear of mannequins form a very clear dressing match, allowing customers to well understand the brand positioning and the currently promoted products outside the store, thereby improving the in-store rate of target customer groups.

2. Overall color

If three beautiful women wear clothes of the same color series, have the same hairstyle, and consistent height... their回头rate will be much higher. Similarly, a combination of window mannequins in the same color series gives a strong visual impact, effectively improving the in-store rate. The overall color also includes other arrangements within the window, such as accessories and advertisements. Therefore, when window mannequins form a group, the overall color effect should be considered.

3. Humanized modeling

For example, when we wear clothes, there will be creases at the knees, abdomen, elbows, etc. Making these humanized shapes in the display of window mannequins makes the effect of clothing matching appear more natural. Humanized modeling is also reflected in aspects such as whether the buttons (or zippers) are fastened, the choice of clothing size, and whether the thickness is appropriate. In short, what the mannequins wear in the window should look like what people wear.

The biggest role of the window is to improve the in-store rate, and its own role in promoting and selling clothing is not significant. So the selection of clothing for window mannequins should depend on the current purpose. For instance, during the launch of new items each season, the styles chosen for the window mannequins should first make it clear that they are next-season goods. During peak sales periods, some image styles can be chosen. During promotions, promotional styles can be used, directly indicating the discount level. In short, the clothing of window mannequins should let customers passing by the store understand the current dynamics and trends of clothing, thereby improving the in-store rate.

Four major principles of internal sales floor displays

Enhancing purchase desire

If the window display mainly serves to increase the customer's in-store rate, then the internal sales floor display should mainly serve to enhance purchase desire, extend customer stay time, influence customer walking routes, and improve try-on and purchase rates.

Sales floor displays are actually a study of customer psychology. Therefore, the thinking and steps of sales floor displays are about catering to customers' desire to buy and achieving sales.

1. Overall color system area division. Taking color-system displays as an example. Customers first choose to enter the store through attractive windows and storefronts. After entering the store, they first see the overall display effect of the shop. Therefore, it is necessary to first divide the overall color system areas of the shop.

2. Color combinations for each color system area. When dividing the color system areas according to the store layout into A, B, C..., then determine which colors compose each color system area.

3. Display combinations for each color system area. Once the colors of each color system area are determined, start combining them. Since the display rules differ for different brands, follow their rules accordingly. For example, some brands categorize jeans, jackets, etc., while others require different categories, fabrics, and styles to be combined and displayed.

4. Overall observation and adjustment. After all the color system areas are fully displayed, observe the overall effect again, check if the color combinations among them are reasonable, and adjust to the best state.
