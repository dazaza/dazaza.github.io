Original Address: Liu Yibiao's Sayings Author: Coach Bian What does wisdom mean? No one in human history has ever explained this word clearly. How to gain wisdom? It is to enter the irrelevant and be an outsider. Irrelevant generates intelligence, being outside generates wisdom. How to enter the irrelevant and become an outsider? The key is to find joy and take it as a pleasure. Those who find joy, everything is enjoyable. Whoever does not find joy, everything is a burden. The only way is to be obsessed with what you do. You enter the feeling, and then you can achieve. In the past, many of your confusions are because you live from a human perspective. To learn from me, there is a thought that must free you from humanity so that you will not be influenced by your emotions and desires, and your judgment will not deviate. For small achievements, ordinary people rely on their conscience to live; for great causes, they rely on instinct.

There are four types of life: the fourth type of person studies "yes" every day; the third type is busy doing things every day; the second type is busy grabbing the market; the first type starts thinking about problems from the perspective of "momentum". The thirteen chapters of military strategy illustrate the importance of momentum from thirteen angles.

You are very far but also very close to becoming an entrepreneur. Once you touch the essence, you will enter that category. The essence of decision-making for bosses is that countless people make decisions, but they are like drawing a circle around themselves, which means we have problems in human interaction. All bosses operate in an industry, which is operating people. All industries and products are media, through which these people are grouped together. Small bosses manage tasks, big bosses manage people. What potential energy do leaders use to stimulate motivation and dissolve resistance? Once this matter is resolved, everything else will dissipate. For bosses, no matter how things change, the core remains the same. Even if things change endlessly, I control the situation because I have touched the essence of things.

Why do we take this path? Because we have touched and experienced something. The whole of China and the world are in a global village, undergoing unprecedented changes. In fixed times, bosses only need to learn a fixed model. But in changing times, bosses should not learn fixed models; instead, they should learn adaptability! Your confusion lies in not breaking out of your old mindset and entering a new era, so you don't know where to go.

Bosses have three fatal flaws: the first is living in a democratic era with feudal thoughts; the second is living in a global era with home-bound thoughts; the third is living in a market era with agricultural thoughts.

As long as your company system and mechanism do not change, all learning is meaningless. Remember, reform always involves two questions: who is working and how to divide the results! When people come together, it’s about dividing money and benefits, issues of fame and profit. So when people come together, the first thing to do is to distribute fame and profit well. What is called "broad-mindedness"? It is the ability to share fame and profit with others, which is called broad-mindedness. One must learn to unite those who are aligned with you into a team. The realm of managing people is to use one group to influence another, rather than influencing a group yourself. This is a misunderstanding that many people cannot escape throughout their lives. Without principles, everything lacks a core, and without a core, nothing grows. Principles define why we establish a company, for whom we speak, and for whom we exist! Only when you understand these can we transcend. Without understanding, there can be no transcendence!

Classes aim to elevate the boss's realm, not just teach them how to make money. Think about it, I teach mechanisms, but if your personal mindset doesn't change, if you haven't sincerely wanted to live a good life with your employees, nothing will be solved! As long as you are liberated and your realm improves, everything becomes easier.

Running a business is about managing impressions. Customers buy based on impressions. In business, the impression you give represents everything about you. The first product of any company is the boss himself. The more people you can sell yourself to, the bigger your team can become. The culture of the boss determines how powerful he is and what kind of person he becomes. Specifically, the strength of your belief determines the strength of the boss, the size of the boss's dream determines how high his life can reach, and the size of your ambition determines how wise you will be. The culture of a boss defines what kind of boss he is. Therefore, the culture defines what kind of company it is. It itself defines the existence of the company. Corporate culture cannot be learned. It must be experienced by the boss and leadership or even the entire staff.

Why can't we grow? Because all we want is to get out of poverty! So once we do, our motivation fades, and the company loses momentum. The force driving our development is limited, it satisfies, it exhausts. When that force is exhausted, we become stagnant. Looking back at all the great companies worldwide, has any one of them become great by pursuing maximum profit? All the world's great companies that have become top-tier enterprises did not start with the idea of making money, yet they ended up achieving greatness! Regardless of who you are, your knowledge will never surpass your aspirations. If you don't increase your aspirations, how can you increase your knowledge? If your aspiration is to help 4000 or 5000 people live a better life, you will dream of the wisdom to manage 5000 people. If your heart is set on leading 30 or 300 people, you will never learn the wisdom to lead 5000 people, and that is something that cannot be taught. Why does someone continuously grow and pull along countless others while facing various difficulties? Because they sincerely solve these problems. Making people's lives happier with our products and improving customers' lives with our products is the soul of the enterprise. Once this heart is firmly set, you won't disappear no matter what. At most, you might earn less in the next three years, but in the fourth year, things will quickly turn around and even multiply! For Chinese private enterprises to achieve long-term development, they must address the commercial cultural atmosphere. In other words, when society appreciates and recognizes us, our enterprises can grow in society.

Why do many companies fail to grow? It's like planting a tree; countless bosses focus only on growing upward and never downward. As a result, the top keeps growing while the roots are shallow, like water lilies floating on the surface. Today, I teach you to continuously root deeper. With Liu Yibiao's leadership wisdom, as long as the roots are deeply entrenched, the top will naturally continue to grow without planning. Everyone progresses slowly because they seek externally. Those who progress quickly seek internally. When you seek internally, you will begin to discover a world where you infinitely extend and improve.

All great masters follow a path to become masters, and then later generations learn from the masters. Thus, the difference between teachers and masters emerges. A teacher brings students into the teacher's world, confusing them. What is a master? A master enters the student's world with their knowledge, seeing what the student lacks and teaching accordingly. A teacher makes you understand, a master awakens you. A teacher constantly fills your mind, while a master lets your mind continually grow. The essence of the Tao Te Ching is just one: learning how the teacher wrote the Tao Te Ching. Can you hear it? Turn around, use Laozi's method of writing the Tao Te Ching to write a classic for running a business. Any historical anecdote, any classic, summarizes the conditions of its time. We use its summarized thinking to distill current wisdom.

Great cultures make people brilliant in the present. This outcome drives countless people to desire brilliance in the present, reaching the highest level. In such cases, it creates a pulling force for the present. No matter how great the past masters were, we respect and admire them, but we aim to surpass them. True reverence for a teacher is surpassing the teacher. So, the heroes of today are the supreme heroes. Treat Li Ka-shing, Jack Ma, Yao Ming, Zhang Ziyi, and the top performers in your company like Confucius and Laozi. Anyone who surpasses you in any field is your teacher. When you regard this as the essence of being a teacher and following the way of the teacher, you will advance rapidly, beyond limits.

What is willpower? It is the spirit of self-guidance. A person who knows how to self-guide is already mature, representing the ability to self-replenish. A person who can self-guide can progress immensely, anytime and anywhere. So, who is the greatest teacher? The greatest teacher is always yourself. No matter what classic, none compares to the wisdom and mysteries unfolding daily in life. Throughout history, classics merely summarize the dust of their times. So, any classic or master is just a reference point. The core question is: who to learn from? The answer is the red dust (worldly affairs). Why do you prefer reading books or attending classes? Because reading books and attending classes are simpler; you directly listen to others' summaries and digest the vast and distant red dust. Therefore, you are unwilling to self-reflect and absorb what others have already digested, which is called knowledge, while what you personally connect with in the red dust is called wisdom.

Learning triggers new ideas and directions, prompting deep reflection. People today suffer because they are influenced by many conclusions. Experiencing something yourself outweighs all conclusions. Enter the red dust, and everything you need is happening every day. Every confusion you have will find a ready-made case. Someone just dealt with it yesterday, and all you need to do is enter, and you will discover a grand world where confusion dissolves. The reason for your confusion is standing on the shore. So, why can't you gain anything? It's because you haven't given yourself fully. If you want to succeed, you must be genuine. Your problem is wanting but unwilling to commit. The more you dedicate yourself to your career, the more successful you will be.

Religion elevates belief to faith and further elevates faith to trust. So, when a boss operates people, it must start with belief. Everything starts with belief. The hardest thing in the world is to make people believe. When an employee believes in the product, they speak with conviction. When a boss believes in the industry, they feel it. So, without entering this state, you are using physical effort or mental effort to work, which at best earns you a meal in the red dust. To truly enter a career, you must enter a world beyond the brain, called the spiritual world. A group of people who eat and drink enough can still work hard obviously for the spirit. Therefore, a top boss works with employees to complete a great cause. Past generations knew that what endures for millennia is spirit. Without spirit, only material things remain. Understand? Isn't it sad to regard a flower pot as a national treasure? What national treasure could surpass the spirit of the people? The spirit of a nation is its first national treasure. A person with spirit does not need proof, return, or explanation; it flows naturally from within.

Remember, I may not inspire you, but there is one thing. I might help you find it back, because everyone has it, but you have suppressed it. The most valuable thing in life, you gradually cover up and then strive to learn worthless things, resulting in a lack of money. The destruction of a boss stems from the destruction of inner wildness. When wildness disappears, everything will fade away. Today, you are a boss, an entrepreneur, saving the country through your enterprise, loving the people through your enterprise. Countless people die from mistaking the accidental for the inevitable. When we started from scratch, we didn't know these forms or modes or management rules. Why could we create something from nothing? Bosses need to learn wisdom and return to the state of creating something from nothing.

The essence of heaven consists of two points: the first is to be alive. So, as long as a person is alive anytime and anywhere, they are heaven. Those who follow the way of heaven are alive. The second is continuity, being alive and continuous. The universe runs continuously for billions of years; it does not stop and start intermittently. Today, ordinary people think and pause, act and pause, so walking and stopping prevents them from aligning with heaven. When you are continuous, you will always be. Worshipping heaven means existing like heaven. So, every person who achieves success has a different life from others; their life burns brightly. In fact, they only pursue a real life. All great achievers merely manifest a true state of life. The key is not in cultivating life but where life manifests. Various great achievers only ignite life at that point. All these qualities are innate; no one can add anything extra. They only let the original power of life manifest. Each person follows their own path, bringing you to where you want to go, not where I think you should go.

Do you realize how terrible humans can be? When someone feels bad today, they don't want to experience that discomfort; they want to avoid it. Your greatest pain comes from wanting days without pain, which is impossible. Just like when someone loses a loved one and cries, they cry. When a loved one passes away, you cry, and you quietly experience today's sorrow, for sorrow is a form of life. Why control it? You find that people who live freely and continuously live genuinely. They are wherever they are, treating themselves as ordinary people. You may be a significant figure in your circle, but here, no one knows what you do. Be a god when it's appropriate, and when it's not, blend seamlessly into life so that no one notices. This is called being divine yet worldly. When it's time to appear as a god, you appear like a god; when it's time to disappear, no one knows where you've gone. This is called being divine yet worldly.

A boss can completely soar spiritually without limits. As long as you don't set boundaries, it will manifest; returning to the original state generates infinite power. When I am truly present, others are equally present. When I am true, they are beautiful. From the deepest part of me, I found that speaking truthfully on stage does not tire me. As soon as I think of lying, I feel tired, and lying lacks impact. Speaking truthfully, however, has impact. Based on this thinking, I realized that all fatigue comes from resisting the truth. So, whenever you see someone who isn't relaxed, you know they lack martial arts skills, have no abilities, and don't need to ask. During class, among your classmates, if you see someone who is calm and composed, acting freely, you know you've encountered a master today. Guaranteed! He exists like that, unaffected by surroundings, on his own track, like an independent planet. A person must cultivate into an independent planet to be happy.

When we are with students, I am not giving them anything, not adding anything, not making them understand anything, but simply allowing their true selves to emerge. All great figures are just people who live genuinely, what we call living naturally and truthfully. In fact, the realm a boss needs to enter is living naturally and truthfully, which is the simplest, the greatest path, and what people often overlook. So, we must bring people to that point.

People live for hope; without hope, they perish. So, when you manage something, you must bring people to a place of hope, eternal hope. I'll say something simple: if you go home and see a clove of garlic sprouting in your kitchen, you feel reluctant to eat or throw it away, put it in a bowl or plate with water to grow it, and you will surely achieve great things. You must love all living things, connect with them, grow flowers or grass, travel the world to feel connected, otherwise, you lack connection. It's an experiential process that generates passion. Passion comes from connecting with all living things; without passion, you've disconnected from all living things. All demise begins with the loss of sensation. When you have sensations, whatever you do or decide, once acted upon, has tenfold, hundredfold, thousandfold, or even millionfold effect. Without sensation, doing something for ten years is not as effective as doing it for a moment with sensation. Why does Buddhism say a second is eternity?

When you connect with everything around you, you will feel beautiful. This is when you align with everything, becoming one with everything, and you feel existence. In the red dust, wherever you go, you experience no thoughts, no mind, just existing, absorbing infinite energy, directly connecting with the universe, and instantly generating wondrous effects. The realm of a boss's cultivation is: when you wake up the next morning, erase all negativity from the previous day, being carefree and waking up refreshed with the sun. So, boss, don't think about what you will become; you can never become anything specific. You can only exist and release yourself fully. Since there's no thought of becoming or failing, the only result is great achievement. Irrelevance generates intelligence, being outside generates wisdom. I am irrelevant to myself, I am outside my own self. My heart is everything, everything is my heart. The Way originally had nothing to say; everything requires full commitment!
