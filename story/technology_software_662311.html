Didn't see the scene, but the bathing center was indeed "a mess"

by baichazu3264 on 2012-02-10 09:35:17

Yesterday, a reporter learned from the Changchun police that on the 26th, the police indeed conducted a surprise inspection of this bathing club, but the police did not disclose more information. Currently, the police have noticed that some netizens have posted photos.

>>Reporter's visit:

The back of Building No.1 in Lijiang Garden Community directly faces the back of Xinyang Bathing Club. However, nearby residents all said they had never seen anyone running naked on the 26th. The rear window of the supermarket on the first floor of Unit Three directly faces the exhaust pipe used by the patrons to escape. The owner of the supermarket said: "I didn't pay attention, and no one has been talking about it these days!" Mrs. Li, who lives on the fourth floor, can see the panoramic view of the bathing club platform from her bedroom window. Although she did not see it when the incident happened, she still knows quite a lot about this bathing club: "This club has quite a few fallen women. They often hang out on the third floor of the club, dressed very revealingly. A neighbor in this building reported the club because he was worried his child would see them. Later, the club brick-walled up the windows on the third floor, but you can still often see the fallen women walking around on the platform."

>>Police voice:

>>Poster: Found the embarrassing scene while visiting a classmate.

Multiple women left the club; the sign has been removed.

Through the information provided in the photo, the reporter found the scene where the naked patron climbed the building to escape. After observation, it could be confirmed that the naked patron climbed from the second-floor platform of the Xinyang Bathing Club near the Lijiang Garden Community. Since the first and second floors are all shop houses, the vertical distance from the second-floor platform to the ground is at least eight or nine meters, and the exhaust pipe the patron climbed was severely rusted. The environment below the exhaust pipe was also quite terrible, with broken glass, household garbage, and construction waste scattered on the ground. It is hard to imagine how tense and panicked the patron was when escaping naked.

At 2 p.m., the bathing club posted the words "Internal Renovation, Temporarily Closed." At 7:30 p.m., the reporter found that the sign above the building of the club had been dismantled.

Seventh photo: This naked patron had completely hugged the exhaust pipe and was slowly sliding down the pipe...

The poster said: On April 26th, there were no classes in the afternoon, so I went to visit a classmate. Suddenly, I heard commotion outside the window. Then I saw someone running on the opposite roof, feeling that something was about to happen, so I took out my camera to observe. Later, I learned that it was due to a surprise inspection by the Changchun police for anti-prostitution operations.

The photographer might be a student (Editor: SN009).

According to the introduction of nearby neighbors, this bathing club also has a room in Room 301, Unit 1, Building No.1 of Lijiang Garden. You can walk from the second-floor platform of the bathing club into this room. In the first and second photos taken by netizens, the destination of the women and patrons fleeing should be this room.

Third photo: A naked man appeared - perhaps because of the panic, this 30-something-year-old, short-haired, robust patron actually ran out of the third floor of the bathing center completely naked. Perhaps he didn't see the woman leading the way ahead, so instead of running to the opposite residential building from the platform, he chose to climb the building to escape.

The escape route of the patron could be described as full of "thorns."

Seven photos showing the ugly state of the patrons.

The reporter hopes to find the poster to restore the situation as much as possible. Although the possible residence of the poster was found based on the shooting angle of the online photos, there was no one at home after knocking twice at noon and evening. Nearby neighbors said: "This house has been rented out, and the renter is a young man, probably still a student. He doesn't always live here."

Second photo: Two women were about to run into a residential building opposite the platform. Ten meters behind them, a slightly fat patron wearing only underwear just ran out of the bathing center, heading towards the same residential building opposite the platform.

The poster released seven photos in total, recording the ugly states of the patrons and fallen women fleeing.

Sixth photo: The left hand of this naked patron was still holding the building, but the right hand had already grabbed a rusty exhaust pipe, and both feet were stepping on the fixed bracket of the exhaust pipe.

Numerous netizens expressed their opinions after "watching" the escape behavior of this naked patron. Netizen "Cooking When Home" said: "Whose husband is this? How embarrassing it would be for his wife and children to see him!"

At 1:30 PM yesterday, just as the reporter arrived at the door of Room 301, Unit 1, a fashionably dressed young woman who was on the phone walked out. Although she said on the phone "I came out from the back door," she claimed that the room belonged to her family when questioned by the reporter. In the next five minutes, three fashionably dressed young women walked out of the room one after another. Meanwhile, several women also walked out of the main entrance of the Xinyang Bathing Center. Subsequently, the reporter hoped to enter the club for an interview, but found that the door had already been locked by the waitresses.

First photo: Two topless patrons and two women with revealing clothing fled from one side of the second-floor platform of the bathing center to the other side.

>>Reporter's investigation:

Fallen women fled everywhere; the incident platform; naked patrons; bathing center.

According to reports, the police conducted a surprise inspection of the bathing center, and the patrons and fallen women fled everywhere during the transaction. One patron, in a panic, didn't even have time to put on clothes and escaped climbing the building naked... This scene was coincidentally captured by a netizen and uploaded to the Internet. Soon, the post "Net Exposes Anti-Prostitution Operation Scene Where Patron Escapes Naked Climbing the Building" was widely reprinted. After the reporter's investigation, this incident occurred on the second-floor platform of the Xinyang Bathing Club near the Lijiang Garden Community in Changchun City.

Fourth photo: This naked patron's left foot stepped on the chimney of a restaurant on the second floor, his hands held the protruding part of the building, and he was about to descend.
