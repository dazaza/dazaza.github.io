In the blink of an eye, we've entered August, a time when various gray-market phone manufacturers fiercely compete for market share. In addition to the myriad promotions offered for popular gray-market phones, the launch of new models is also a major market strategy. Recently, many new models from different brands have been released, with several highly anticipated models receiving significant attention even before their official release. Moreover, most of the newly launched gray-market phones are designed for mid-range consumers, featuring mainstream characteristics and functions at relatively affordable prices, thus capturing more consumer interest. This article selects a few "new favorites" from the recent gray-market phone scene, providing more options for those planning to upgrade their devices.

**Recommended Model: Nokia C6-00 Music Edition (Genuine Product)**

**Reference Price: 3148 yuan**

For consumers who prefer side-slide cover touch-screen gray-market phones but have limited budgets, the recently launched Nokia C6-00 could be an excellent choice. As a model primarily focused on social networking, its stylish design and mature operating platform are its main advantages. You can consider C6-00 as the full-key version of the 5230 slide cover, with no changes in major specifications but an upgraded 5-megapixel camera and added WiFi functionality. The 5230 was once a best-seller, and it's expected that this side-slide version of the Nokia C6-00 will not disappoint.

In terms of appearance, the Nokia C6 looks familiar, resembling the Nokia 5230, with little visual difference. It comes in both white and black. The Nokia C6 features a side-slide cover design, measuring 113.4x53x16.8mm and weighing 150g. It has a 3.2-inch TFT screen with 360x640 pixel resolution, offering fine display quality. On the back, there is a 5-megapixel camera equipped with LED flash, delivering excellent imaging results.

The Nokia C6 runs on the Symbian 9.4 S60 5.0 intelligent operating system, with an internal processor clocked at 434MHz, and 240MB of body memory. With a combination of 128MB RAM and 512MB ROM, it ensures smooth operation of the gray-market phone. The body supports up to 16GB of expandable storage, and common functions like WIFI, GPS, and Bluetooth are all included, offering a comprehensive overall performance. **Recommended Model: Nokia X5-00 (Genuine Product)**

**Reference Price: 2598 yuan**

As part of Nokia's latest series, the "X" series has a clear positioning, and precisely due to its focus on the fashionable gray-market phone segment, the phones in this series enjoy good reputations. Recently, another strong contender has been added to the series - the Nokia X5-00. Currently, this model (genuine product) has been launched, with the latest price at 2598 yuan, complete with genuine accessories. Although the launch price is not high, I feel there is still some room for adjustment in the genuine price, suggesting friends wait a bit longer.

The Nokia X5-00 offers three color options: piano black, bright paper white, and China red. It adopts a simple and compact straight-board design, with dedicated music keys that allow easy access to China Mobile's Music service for downloading music. The G3 logo below the screen signifies its identity as a custom model. It features a 2.4-inch QVGA resolution 16 million color TFT screen and an embedded 5-megapixel camera, supporting automatic focusing, dual LED flash, digital zoom, and other shooting functions. Additionally, it can record VGA resolution video at 30fps.

The Nokia X5-00 uses the Symbina9.3 operating system and the S60 V3.2.2 software platform, adding common shortcuts such as schedule, to-do list, Ovi services, etc. It is equipped with a 600MHz AMR11 processor, 60MB of body memory, and supports expansion up to 16GB via micro-SDHC memory cards. Compatible with multiple audio and video file formats, it includes an FM radio, supports various sound effect settings, stereo Bluetooth output, and has a 3.5mm headphone jack. It supports GPS navigation and includes services like 139 mailboxes, Feixin, mobile maps/navigation, among others. Detailed parameter table for Nokia X5-00.
