Serie A - 10-man Roma 1-0 Lazio, Montenegrin wonderkid provides a brilliant assist

by fjhutjhid on 2009-12-07 17:14:50

Serie A - 10-man Roma 1-0 Lazio: Montenegrin wonderkid provides an exquisite assist. At 3:45 AM Beijing time on December 7, a key match of the 15th round of Serie A took place as Roma defeated Lazio 1-0 at home in the Rome Derby. Neither side managed to score in the first half. In the second half, Sarać hit the post, and Vujanić assisted Cassetti's goal. Near the end of the game, Pizarro was shown a red card. After this match, Lazio remained winless for 13 consecutive rounds, ranking 15th, just one point above the relegation zone.

Totti with the prodigy; Cruz absent. This is the 131st derby between the two teams in Serie A history. In the previous 130 encounters in Serie A, Roma had an advantage with 43 wins, 54 draws, and 33 losses, scoring 157 goals while conceding 125. Home games showed even more dominance with 23 wins, 30 draws, and only 12 losses, scoring 80 goals while conceding 54. Roma has been in excellent form recently, winning 5 out of their last 6 matches across all competitions (with one draw), while Lazio hasn't won in 12 league games and is near the relegation zone. There were rumors that if Lazio lost, coach Edy Reja would possibly be sacked.

In Roma's lineup, despite rumors of injury, Vujanić started alongside Menez and Totti in the attack line. De Rossi also started, marking his 200th Serie A appearance. On Lazio's side, coach Edy Reja made some unusual arrangements, with Burdisso playing as a right-back and captain Rocchi being placed on the bench, allowing Mauri and Sarać to operate up front.

The fans almost caused a disturbance, leading to a stalemate in the first half. The game began with fierce tackling from both sides. In the first minute, Brocchi’s long-range shot was blocked by a defender. In the 10th minute, Sarać formed a three-on-two counterattack but missed a golden chance due to too much dribbling. From the 13th minute onwards, due to constant firecracker explosions, there was unrest among the spectators in the stands, causing the referee to interrupt the game. A large number of police were deployed to control the situation. After about a ten-minute pause, the referee decided to continue the game after consulting both teams.

In the 31st minute, Menez crossed from the right flank, and Lichsteiner cleared the ball dangerously close to his own goal, but Muslera saved it with his hands. The referee did not award a penalty. In the 34th minute, Riser took a powerful left-footed shot from outside the box, which went high. In the 36th minute, Roma's corner kick was poorly cleared by Lazio, and Riser's subsequent powerful shot was blocked by De Rossi. In the 37th minute, Mauri took a left-footed shot from the right flank, which nearly found the far corner but was saved by Roma's goalkeeper. In the 45th minute, Kolarov took a left-footed shot from the left corner of the penalty area, which went high. During stoppage time in the first half, De Rossi elbowed Sarać but avoided a red card. Mexes was injured and replaced by Cassetti. The first half ended in a goalless draw.

Sarać hits the post, Cassetti breaks the deadlock. In the second half, both teams swapped sides and made substitutions. Roma brought on Brighi for Menez, and Lazio substituted Foggia for Matuzalém. In the 52nd minute, Lazio's Baroniyo took a free-kick from outside the box, which was blocked by Totti's hand, and Sarać's ensuing attempt went high. In the 55th minute, Cassetti's low shot from outside the box went wide of the left post. In the 58th minute, Cassetti's powerful shot from outside the box was saved by Lazio's goalkeeper.

In the 60th minute, Sarać, after maneuvering past Burdisso inside the box, took a shot that went through Burdisso's legs, hitting the left post before bouncing out. Mauri's follow-up shot was brilliantly saved by Roma's goalkeeper Bertoniole! In the 67th minute, Perrotta headed a cross from the left flank wide. In the 76th minute, Sarać's turn-and-shoot from the edge of the box went high.

In the 78th minute, Roma broke the deadlock when Vujanić played a low pass into the middle, and Cassetti, arriving late near the penalty spot, calmly finished into the bottom-left corner. Roma led 1-0.

In the 85th minute, Totti provided a beautiful pass to the left, and Riser's left-footed volley went high. In the 86th minute, Pizarro was booked for fouling Lichsteiner. In the 88th minute, Vujanić's shot from 20 meters out went high. In the 89th minute, Pizarro was sent off directly for a reckless tackle on Sarać. The game ended with Roma securing a narrow 1-0 victory, claiming all three points.

Starting lineups:

Roma (4-3-2-1): 27-Bertoniole / 29-Burdisso, 5-Mexes (77-Cassetti, 45'), 4-Juan, 17-Riser / 16-De Rossi, 7-Pizarro, 20-Perrotta / 94-Menez (33-Brighi, 46'), 9-Vujanić (11-Taddei, 90') / 10-Totti

Lazio (5-3-1-1): 86-Muslera / 2-Lichsteiner, 13-Djadjate, 28-Stendardo, 26-Radu, 11-Kolarov / 32-Brocchi, 33-Baroniyo (6-Dabo, 90'), 8-Matuzalém (17-Foggia, 46') / 5-Mauri (9-Rocchi, 79') / 10-Sarać
