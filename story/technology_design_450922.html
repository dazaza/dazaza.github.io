Classic Quotes from "Soldier's Assault"

"Soldier's Assault" is one of the more interesting TV series I've watched. Among its simple dialogues, there are many classics worth savoring and cherishing.

》Do not abandon, do not give up.

》Living well means doing meaningful things. Doing meaningful things means living well.

》Between thinking and getting, there are two words: doing. Only by doing can you get.

》Confidence isn't talked about; it's demonstrated through action.

》People aren't made by doing things, they're made by living!

》Some people treat small matters like they're holding onto a lifeline. But after some time, I realize that what they're holding onto is actually a towering tree that I need to look up to.

》Greatness comes from tolerance, strength comes from lack of desire. Tolerating others, desiring for all. Such a world runs comfortably.

》People shouldn't live too comfortably; too much comfort leads to problems.

》We believe good housekeeping can train soldiers' qualities.

》Liang Ming is capable but looks like a bear!

》The company commander said, life is a pile of problems on top of problems.

》Remembering someone's kindness always outweighs remembering their bad deeds.

》Every place you pass, every person you meet, requires you to invest your time and life...

》Don't waste your days, beware that the days don't waste you.

》Glory lies in the ordinary, difficulty lies in endurance.

》Don't think because I haven't spent much time at Steel Seven Company, I won't grow the bones of Steel Seven Company!

》Only those who can endure make good soldiers.

》Planes will eventually be shot down, warships will also be hit hard. In a truly cruel war, all high-tech weapons will eventually be exhausted. The essence of warfare remains human-to-human combat, human-to-human battles. Special Forces A and infantry are the most basic forms of human combat, countering all savagery and complexity. In this regard, they have no difference. Because they are the last line of defense, they are the ones who continue to hold on until the end.

》I admire an old soldier who said he spared no effort but dared not talk about success lightly. He only wanted his subordinates to survive more on the battlefield. He said this was the humanity of soldiers.

》Seven Company is a collective, standing here, taller than this house, taller than these trees. It carries countless scars but has never fallen. That's why it's called steel, with the will of steel and the men of steel.

》If you can't take it anymore, write a letter to the squad leader. This letter was never finished. That night, I realized something: enduring or not enduring is a multiple-choice question. We don't have the option to choose not to endure. This answer was already decided on the first day we joined the army.

》So am I like a monkey? Right. Feeling lively and full of energy every day, but in reality, others just see me as being crazy, following my own wild streak?

》Eat rice bite by bite, handle affairs one by one. What's the use of worrying?

》As long as today is better than yesterday, isn't that hope?
