The incident occurred in Tangxia Town, where the main character was seriously injured, and the police department is currently arresting the owner of the gutter oil workshop.

Southern Daily News (Reporter/Chen Zhiqiang) Yesterday, He Yong, a 39-year-old man from Guizhou who was seriously injured after accidentally falling into an oil pot while collecting gutter oil, finally breathed a sigh of relief. At 5 p.m. yesterday, Lu Haixiang, a member of the Tangxia Town Committee of Dongguan City, visited him at the Tangxia Hospital and said that the government had already confiscated part of the property of the owner of the illegal gutter oil processing black workshop, and the public security department is making every effort to arrest the owner of the gutter oil workshop. The government will find ways to solve his medical expenses.

Flashback: When hospitalized, he followed the boss's instructions and claimed "scalded by boiling water"

On the morning of April 8, He Yong was working at an illegal gutter oil black workshop near Hengxing Primary School in Chaoyang Village, Tangxia Town. At that time, he stood on the edge of an oil pot measuring 2 meters long, 1 meter wide, and 1.5 meters high to collect oil. The oil pot contained gutter oil being refined.

"An accident happened and he slipped in," said Zhang Cai霞, He Yong's wife. By the time He Yong climbed out of the oil pot, his whole body had been severely scalded, with a burn area of about 88%, and there was almost no intact skin on his entire body. It was only five days since He Yong came from his hometown in Guizhou to work in Tangxia, Dongguan.

"When he first came, the boss said he would help feed pigs and collect some oil on the side, earning more than 3000 yuan a month," Zhang Cai霞 said.

In the past month of hospitalization, He Yong's medical expenses have reached 100,000 yuan. Five days ago, when Zhang Cai霞 called the boss to ask for medical expenses, the boss's phone was already unreachable.

"Just when he was admitted to the hospital, we followed the boss's instructions and claimed that we were scalded by boiling water," Zhang Cai霞 said. In the first month of hospitalization, the boss paid the medical expenses, but now he doesn't answer the phone.

Zhang Cai霞 told reporters that five days ago, the boss sent someone to talk to them and wanted to settle everything once and for all, "and even said that if we dared to report it to the police or do other things, they wouldn't let us leave the hospital."

Doubt: The involved black workshop was eliminated last year but reopened this year

On the afternoon of May 9, after the incident was exposed by the media, the Tangxia Town Committee and Town Government of Dongguan City immediately formed a joint law enforcement team to dismantle an unlicensed gutter oil processing shed located in the Lintianxia Lychee Garden in the Pingshan Community of Tangxia Town, destroying the processing tools and confiscating two vehicles used by the illegal operators. Law enforcement officers also cleared another gutter oil workshop located at the boundary between Chaoyang Village and Putixinhu Community in Tangxia.

Yesterday afternoon, relevant responsible persons from the Dongguan Food Safety Committee re-inspected the above-mentioned workshops. At the scene, reporters saw that the area of the illegal gutter oil processing black workshop where He Yong had the accident was about 50 square meters. Although the processing pool had been filled, it still emitted a foul smell from time to time. Another black workshop was located by the Shima River at the boundary between Chaoyang Village and Putixinhu Community in Tangxia. The surrounding area was full of sewage flowing everywhere, and untreated sewage could be directly discharged into the river.

It was learned that the black workshop where He Yong had the accident was jointly dismantled by the Tangxia enforcement department in July last year, but it reappeared in the same location this year. Relevant departments are investigating this matter.

Difficulty: Who will manage the source of gutter oil?

He Yong said that the raw materials for refining gutter oil in the workshop come from Shenzhen and are collected locally in Tangxia. Zhu Jian, He Yong's brother-in-law, said that the boss of the black workshop where He Yong worked had been doing this in Shenzhen before, but later moved to Dongguan because Shenzhen started cracking down on such activities.

Currently, the Tangxia Town of Dongguan has established a task force to investigate the sales direction of gutter oil, fully investigating the person in charge of the workshop and the sales direction and distribution scope of the processed gutter oil.

Law enforcement personnel said that gutter oil and the extract of kitchen waste and swill oil are different. Only the extract of kitchen waste and swill oil that flows onto dining tables is colloquially referred to as gutter oil by citizens; the extract of kitchen waste and swill oil can be used as chemical raw materials, fertilizers, and other purposes, which is a relatively large market. However, due to the lack of regulation at the source in this field currently, many illegal merchants recycle the extract of kitchen waste and swill oil back to dining tables, turning it into gutter oil.

It was learned that currently within the scope of Dongguan City, only one factory in Dalingshan Town has legal qualifications to recover kitchen waste and swill oil.
