5. One day, while the rabbit was writing something in front of a cave, a wolf walked over and asked, "What are you writing, rabbit?"

The rabbit replied, "I'm writing a thesis."

The wolf asked again, "What's the topic?"

The rabbit replied, "I'm writing about how rabbits eat wolves."

After hearing this, the wolf laughed loudly, expressing disbelief.

The rabbit said, "Follow me." Then it led the wolf into the cave and continued writing outside the cave. At this moment, a fox came over and asked, "Rabbit, what are you writing?"

The rabbit replied, "I'm writing a thesis."

The fox asked, "What's the topic?"

The rabbit replied, "How rabbits eat foxes."

After hearing this, the fox laughed loudly, expressing disbelief.

The rabbit said, "Follow me." Then it led the fox into the cave. After a while, the rabbit came out alone and continued writing its thesis.

At this moment, inside the cave, a lion was sitting on a pile of bones, picking its teeth while reading the rabbit's thesis: The strength of an animal does not depend on how powerful it is, but on who its boss is behind the scenes.

15. Three little white rabbits picked a mushroom.

The two older ones asked the younger one to go get some wild vegetables to eat together.

The younger one said, "I won't go, if I leave, you two will eat my mushroom."

The two older ones said, "We won't, go ahead and trust us," So the little rabbit went...

Half a year passed, and the little rabbit hadn't returned. One of the older ones said, "It won't return, let's eat it."

The other older one said, "Let's wait a bit longer..."

A year passed, and the little rabbit still hadn't returned. The two older ones agreed that there was no need to wait any longer and decided to eat it.

Just at that moment, the younger little white rabbit suddenly jumped out from the nearby bushes, angry and saying, "See! I knew you two would eat my mushroom!"
