Three-day culinary culture-themed tour in Tianjin

by afkqxoyh on 2010-05-26 18:09:51

Preparations:

Book tickets for the first-class performance at the Mingliu Teahouse crosstalk show, 30 yuan per person. Tel: 022-27116382

Book a room at Jiā Hotel Music Academy branch (Daguangming Bridge branch, Shiyijinlu branch)

Prepare small change

Most of Tianjin's public transportation costs 1.5 yuan per person, so prepare plenty of small change.

Day One:

1. Depart at 5:30 AM, take bus 916 to Dongzhimen.

2. Arrive at Dongzhimen at 7:00 AM, take Line 2 and transfer to Line 5, get off at Puhuangyu Station, then take a taxi to Beijing South Station.

3. Buy intercity high-speed train tickets, departure at 9:00 AM.

4. Arrive in Tianjin at 9:30 AM, buy return train tickets and a Tianjin map (do not buy inside the station, walk out further to buy), take a taxi to Jiā Hotel Music Academy branch (Daguangming Bridge branch, Shiyijinlu branch), about 10 yuan. Settle in and rest for a while.

5. Depart at 10:00 AM, take bus 651 from Daguangming Bridge Station (opposite CR Vanguard Supermarket) to Gulou Station, visit the Gulou area for free.

6. At 11:30 AM, walk east one kilometer from Gulou to Ancient Culture Street, where there are small restaurants offering cheap and practical lunch options. Visit Ancient Culture Street, Tianwang Temple, Ni Ren Zhang, Bèngdòu Zhāng, Guórénér Zhāng, enjoy snacks and buy small souvenirs.

7. Leave the street at 15:00 PM, take a taxi to Chifengdao to visit Porcelain House (this house is built with porcelain), entrance fee 20 yuan. Collect tickets at 16:00 PM at the crossroads of Hēilóngjiāng Road and Xīnhuá Road at Mingliu Teahouse, listen to crosstalk for two and a half hours.

8. At 18:30 PM, eat tater noodles at Jiédìliǎng Restaurant on Hēilóngjiāng Road for 5 yuan or mixed tater noodles and knife-cut noodles for 7 yuan. Soft drinks cost one yuan per bottle.

9. At 19:00 PM, take a taxi to Xiàwáfáng Honey Bar, bar-hopping. (Cost depends on what alcohol you choose, generally a half-dozen beers will suffice.) Taxi fare should not exceed 20 yuan.

10. Return early at 22:00 PM to shower and sleep. Tomorrow will be tiring. A taxi fare of 8 yuan is sufficient.

Day Two:

1. Get up at 8:00 AM, leave Jiā Hotel and walk south, turn west at the first intersection for 50 meters, there is a Kūnchéng Snack Shop (Old Tianjin fried sauce noodles opposite), have breakfast here. Recommended food: tofu pudding, egg dumplings, Gābā vegetable, fried dough sticks, box-packed eggs (Note: it’s "box" not "egg in a box", it’s box-packed eggs). Buy freshly fried dough sticks and box-packed eggs from a small stall next to Kūnchéng, don't buy them inside Kūnchéng.

2. Depart at 9:00 AM, buy a small bottle of liquor at the supermarket near the door, remember not to open it as it won’t pass security checks. Wait for the double-decker bus 643 at Dàguāngmíngqiáo Station near Jiā Hotel, get off at Zhōngshānmén Station, take the light rail to Tánggǔ Station, 5 yuan per person. Follow the crowd after getting off, go to the Foreign Goods Market, which is not very interesting, like an expanded version of Tiānyì Market. But it's quite famous, so worth a visit.

3. At 11:30 AM, take bus 103 from Yáng Huò Market Station to North Tang Station, cross the road to buy seafood (Note: walk further inside, there is a huge market in a small alley, don’t assume it's just the couple of shops by the roadside, you need to walk further.). After buying seafood, process it at a roadside shop, drinking some white wine makes it even more enjoyable, sterilizing and invigorating.

4. At 14:00 PM, near the market is the terminal station of bus 103, take the original route back to the Foreign Goods Market. If you wish, it's best to take the light rail from the first stop of the Foreign Goods Market, Taida Station, where you can get a seat. The Foreign Goods Market is extremely crowded, so it's hard to find a seat. If you don't want to go or don't care about standing and returning to Tianjin looking like a photo, then take the city train from the Foreign Goods Market.

5. Return to Tianjin at 16:00 PM, take the same bus when you arrived, go to the Kun Cheng restaurant behind the hotel to eat bobbie fish for 24 yuan and hotpot tofu. Bobbie fish is very delicious and strong-flavored, generally two people shouldn't order too much, bobbie fish is quite large.

6. After eating at 18:00 PM, stroll around the supermarket near the hotel entrance, buy necessary items. If not tired, you can go for a milk bath or foot massage. You can also go to the Hai River under the Liberation Bridge to see the night view.

Day Three:

1. Get up at 8:00 AM, check out. If your luggage is heavy, you can store it at the hotel and retrieve it later in the afternoon. Breakfast is the same as before.

2. Take the double-decker bus 643 to Nankai University, admire the prestigious school, remember to enter from the East Gate and exit from the South Gate, where there are statues.

3. At 10:30 AM, take a taxi to Quanyechang Pedestrian Street, no more than 20 yuan. Go shopping, ladies will be happy.

4. At noon 12:00 PM, in the hutong opposite Jiangbin Building in Quanyechang Pedestrian Street, visit the main store of Goubuli. It's quite expensive. It's recommended to try it. Buy a set meal on the first floor, don't listen to the waiters' advice to go upstairs, upstairs is for out-of-towners, extremely overpriced, a beef bun costs 9 yuan each, note, it's not a typo, one bun costs 9 yuan. Eight pork buns cost 36 yuan. Each bun is smaller than my eyeball. The set meal downstairs costs over 20 yuan, have a taste.

5. After eating, continue shopping until your feet blister, then take a taxi to Xinan Jiao. About 10 yuan. Experience the Tianjin subway, fewer people than in Beijing and quite advanced. Subway ticket costs 3 yuan per person.

6. Exit at D出口 at Xiawafang Station, across the road is Jinmen Crepe, a small storefront with a red sign, 5 yuan per set, two fruit bars and two eggs, experience what real crepe bars are like. Walk north while eating crepes, about 300 meters, there is a Xiaoli Roast Chicken, buy two, pay attention to the server's calculations and scale. Servers often miscalculate, and every time they miscalculate, they overcharge. After buying roast chicken, do a big thing, take a taxi from Xiaoli Roast Chicken to the entrance of Límín Road, Gui Faxiang Twisted Dough Stick Store, buy the famous eighteen street big twisted dough sticks, note only this one is genuine, all others are fake. Ask the driver to wait outside, after buying twisted dough sticks directly get back in the car and continue back to Jiā Hotel to pick up luggage, ask the driver to wait, pick up luggage and head straight to the train station, end the Tianjin trip. Car fare between 20~30 yuan. Note: Tianjin taxis charge for waiting time, the amount depends on how fast you act.

Notes:

1. Taxi drivers are interesting, you can chat more with them, but most will say your choices aren't good, that the attractions aren't fun, but traveling is about going to places you haven't been, they've always been in Tianjin, so naturally they think it's not great. Don't believe these things, we don't always go to Mutianyu either.

2. If you don't know the way, just ask, Tianjin people are like Zhang Damin, very funny and very热情, especially easy-going.

3. The Five Great Avenues and Italian Style Street are optional attractions, actually there's really nothing much to see, but the photos taken there look okay. If you really want to see them, it's suggested to take a taxi to the train station and drive around there a bit, it's a bit detour, but spending an extra 10~20 yuan to visit two attractions is actually pretty worthwhile.
