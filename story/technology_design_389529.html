False "One-Night Stand" Post: "Sinology Spicy Girl" Wins $4500 Compensation

Published on: October 11, 2007 - Read: Times - Source: Chongming, Shanghai Website Construction

The post titled "Fourth-year student at the China Conservatory of Opera: My one-night stand is worth 100,000 yuan" made the "Sinology Spicy Girl" an instant online sensation. Bai, a female university student claiming to be the "Sinology Spicy Girl," believed that Rednet published false articles for their own click rates and economic benefits, severely infringing upon her right to reputation. She sued Hunan Rednet News Network Communication Company in court, demanding 300,000 yuan in compensation for mental distress. Yesterday, Fengtai District Court announced its verdict, determining that Rednet's actions infringed upon Bai's right to reputation. The court ordered Rednet to publicly apologize and eliminate the influence on its website, and compensate Bai with 30,000 yuan for mental damages and 1,100 yuan for notarization fees.
