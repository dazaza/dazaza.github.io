Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial and Trade Development Co., Ltd. strengthens publicity to promote enterprise development

by anonymous on 2012-02-06 15:59:59

The publicity and reporting of Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial & Trading Development Co., Ltd. is an important part of the company's party building and spiritual civilization work, as well as an important method for promoting products, showcasing the corporate image, and enhancing cohesion. Whether the publicity and reporting work is vivid, innovative, and dynamic directly affects the level of public opinion guidance by the Party and the overall situation of production safety work. Therefore, during the process of strengthening party building and spiritual civilization construction, the company has always regarded publicity work as a key task. We have made many positive explorations and taken various measures to complete the publicity and reporting tasks, achieving certain results.

(1) Establishing a team of correspondents and improving the correspondent network. Building a team of correspondents with strong political awareness, good quality, and excellent business skills is the foundation of doing a good job in publicity and reporting. For this purpose, the company has designated 1-2 comrades in each department who are familiar with their department's or unit's work situation, have a sense of responsibility, high-level writing skills, and are good at conducting surveys, research, and comprehensive analysis of problems to serve as correspondents. This has established a correspondent network that spans horizontally and vertically.

(2) Providing proper training and focusing on the training of the correspondent team. Our branch company has taken a series of effective measures to improve the writing ability of the correspondents. For example, organizing business training classes, purchasing a large number of books related to writing, subscribing to relevant newspapers and magazines, and providing correspondents with various conveniences for writing. These measures have promoted the further maturity of the publicity and reporting work and achieved good results.

(3) Improving the incentive mechanism and conducting regular evaluations. The branch company has uniformly formulated management regulations for publicity and reporting work, assigning quarterly publicity and reporting tasks through an积分 system, evaluating the completion of the departments' publicity and reporting tasks, implementing an incentive mechanism, and formulating specific and highly operational reward and punishment measures. This reward and punishment system is strictly enforced according to the system, directly reflecting new measures within the branch company, and motivating all departments to actively engage in publicity and reporting work.

(4) Implementing a manuscript submission reward system. For manuscripts with remuneration, depending on the media level, the author will be rewarded 1 to 5 times the amount of the remuneration paid by the media.

(5) Selecting advanced individuals and giving commendation and rewards. For those who meet or exceed the assessment indicators, commendation and rewards will be given every quarter, and these will serve as reference criteria for year-end evaluation and selection of excellence.

Looking back on the work of the past year, although we have achieved certain results, there is still a gap compared to the requirements of the higher-level party committee, and there are still shortcomings in our work. Xinjiang Jintianhai Industrial & Trading Development Co., Ltd. needs to further construct a publicity and reporting work system, expand the scope of work coverage, consolidate and extend the channels and links of publicity work, play the role of carriers, improve the level of work, keep pace with the times, and strive to create a new situation in publicity and reporting work.
