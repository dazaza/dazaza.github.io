Perhaps some places have no electricity, or even lose mobile signals... Maybe when you call his office, he is often not there. Please don't worry because he is recording data or doing routine inspections. A little negligence may cause serious accidents... If your boyfriend is a petroleum worker, please don't complain because he is not always by your side. His working environment is the wild. They use the sky as a quilt and the ground as a bed. He wants to go back, but he always tells himself helplessly that he is a strong soldier, guarding the frontier like an idol, gazing at his wife from afar... Perhaps after he comes home, he still can't go shopping with you or watch movies together. In fact, he does know how to enjoy life! He still has a lot of work to finish. He is thinking about how they can stay together without being apart for such a long time, and he is making efforts that you don't know about... On the first day he comes home, he might sleep for 24 hours. When he is tired, please don't argue with him because every day, every 24 hours at his workplace, his nerves are tightly strung, ready to face whatever happens... When he is upset, please don't quarrel with him. He pays attention to every detail in his work and tries to make it perfect. Even so, he may still be criticized and dissatisfied by his leader. The only person who can make him relax and feel comfortable is you... If your boyfriend is a petroleum worker, maybe when you are sick, he can't take care of you all the time. Please don't blame him. If your boyfriend is a petroleum worker, when he can't spend anniversaries with you, please understand his reasons. His work often does not allow him to take vacations on time. He also follows arrangements because he once secretly vowed to work hard and dedicate himself to the oil industry, making future anniversaries more beautiful... When you rest, he works; when you work, he rests. Please don't complain. In fact, the person he thinks about the most is you. Once he has time, even if it means sacrificing his hard-earned rest time, he will come to see you, even when you're busy. To avoid disturbing you, he secretly looks at you from a corner where you can't see him. Then he sends a message or makes a call, telling you that he loves you, that you look beautiful today, saying a few sweet words like "you've lost weight, dear, make sure to eat well, I can't take care of you, please forgive me"... If your boyfriend is a petroleum worker, please try to learn to understand, sympathize, and tolerate... In fact, the tense work makes him endure both psychological and physical pressures. Long hours of work, anxiety, and tension make him get sick more easily and age faster than others. Looking at such a him, don't you feel heartache...
