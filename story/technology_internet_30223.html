Merchants Aim at Mid-Autumn and National Day: Experts Interpret Two Tricks of Internet Promotion

by now on 2007-09-21 13:27:59

According to the current "80-20" rule of business promotion, the peak season in a year is mostly concentrated in holidays. According to incomplete statistics from relevant departments, sales of all kinds of goods during holidays are more than three times of normal days, or even more. In recent years, with the rapid development of China's Internet, the proportion of online consumption has become larger and larger. According to the latest statistics, in the first half of 2007, on average each person spent 186 yuan per month on the Internet, and it was predicted that this year's total amount of Internet consumption would reach 364.114 billion yuan RMB. With the approach of the traditional Chinese festival Mid-Autumn Festival and the National Day Golden Week, businessmen were eagerly preparing for promotions, and experts analyzed the promotional situation of the Internet service industry recently.

Currently, how to implement online promotion is a new issue for any enterprise. It has similarities with traditional promotion but also its own characteristics. Compared with traditional consumption, online consumption is more virtual. Many Internet products themselves have virtual properties, such as domain names, virtual hosts, and corporate mailboxes. They are used on the network, and tangible products cannot be seen, which brings more uncertainty factors to consumers, and also brings certain difficulties to the promotion of Internet products.

Relevant experts from the Marketing Association believe that the character "促" (meaning "promotion") consists of "人" (meaning "person") on the left and "足" (meaning "foot") on the right, meaning "there must be sufficient people", i.e., there must be popularity. Only when the product is promoted can it be sold. This applies equally to online promotion, where gathering popularity is the first thing merchants need to consider. This popularity not only needs new customers but also support from old customers. For example, Now.cn (www.now.cn), one of the "Top Ten" Internet service providers nationwide, in order to attract popularity, not only did they offer discounts for new customers purchasing products during the recent Mid-Autumn Festival promotion, but old customers could also enjoy 100% cash coupons. Every customer can get benefits, naturally attracting a large number of old customers, creating a certain influence and publicity effect within the industry, and ultimately achieving the goal of attracting new customers to consume.

In addition, consumers are accustomed to various promotional activities, and most online discount coupons have date restrictions or other limitations. Moreover, consumer shopping is becoming increasingly rational. Therefore, experts remind that if merchants want to gain an advantage in promotions, they must follow the principle of visible benefits. To eliminate consumer concerns, thereby encouraging them to purchase confidently and achieve the purpose of promotion. Looking at Now.cn's Mid-Autumn Festival promotion this time, it adopted a cash coupon activity. Compared with previous discount coupons, it is more real and practical. With it, you can buy any product from the company without additional restrictions, which effectively eliminates the consumer's guard.

In summary, compared with traditional promotion, online promotion has undergone significant changes in terms of time and space, information dissemination models, and the degree of customer participation. As merchants in the Internet industry, promotions must break out of the limitations of physical markets, adopt the thinking and methods of virtual markets, and consider more from the consumer's perspective, ultimately achieving the purpose of online promotion.
