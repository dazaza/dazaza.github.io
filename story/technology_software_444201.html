11 Small Tips to Teach You Prosperous Feng Shui, Turning a Dull House into a Prosperous One Immediately

by zzfdhdb on 2011-06-10 17:03:42

A house is a place for human habitation, whether you buy a house to live in or rent one. The Feng Shui of a house has a great impact on a person's fortune, which was discovered by the ancients thousands of years ago. If the people living inside feel that their luck is good, then the Feng Shui of the house they live in may be good. If the people living inside feel that their luck is bad, especially after living there for a long time and the luck does not improve, this indicates that there is definitely something wrong with the house. In Feng Shui, such houses are called "decline Qi houses."

Home Feng Shui

If it's a rented house, it's easier to deal with; just move out. But if it's a house you bought yourself, then the problem becomes bigger. Houses are so expensive nowadays, and there isn't much opportunity to switch. Especially if you buy a house with bad luck, even though you know it's not good, you can neither switch houses nor rent another one to live in, you can only tough it out. And this situation is extremely common, with two-thirds being such decline Qi houses.

Whether the luck of a house is good or bad, there are many factors involved, at least twenty or thirty situations that can lead to bad luck for a house. Based on over twenty years of experience, Master Haishen has found that at least three points must be paid attention to when it comes to affecting the luck of a house: the entrance door, the auspicious direction, and the couple’s bed position. These three positions govern the family's misfortunes, luck, and marital feelings, which are also the main issues faced by every household. Therefore, as long as the energy field of these three positions is adjusted well, it will bring good luck to the home.

1. Entrance Door Brings Luck, Attracts Wealth and Blessings

The house door, or our entrance door, is as important as a person's throat. This is because it is the only passage for entry and exit. Whether it's objects or each member of the household, they all pass through here. Additionally, according to Haishen Feng Shui theory, the main entrance is the primary intake point for Qi in the home. The annual fate stars in Haishen Feng Shui start from the entrance door. Whether it's the annual career luck or the annual wealth luck, it all enters through here. It also affects the happiness of our family and our health. According to the Feng Shui data we have collected, with nearly 1000 cases, more than two-thirds of those who encounter unfavorable circumstances in their annual destiny are related to the entrance door.

From this, we can see how important the entrance door is for our daily life, and it cannot be overlooked. There are three methods to enhance the luck of the entrance door.

Home Feng Shui

1. Paste Haishen Door Fu on the Entrance Door

Chinese people have a characteristic, which is to paste the character 'Fu' outside their own doors during the New Year. This symbolizes the arrival of blessings, attracting wealth and good fortune for a year of prosperity and continuous good luck. Almost all families do this, showing that people definitely believe in the entrance door bringing auspicious Qi and attracting wealth. Some families also post the door gods Qin Qiong and Jingde, serving as protectors of peace. However, whether it's an ordinary 'Fu' character or door gods, they merely serve a symbolic purpose and cannot truly attract wealth or ensure peace. Haishen Door Fu can play the aforementioned roles, resolving various Sha Qi outside the door, attracting wealth, gathering blessings, and ensuring peace. The Door Fu has been consecrated. The backside is written personally by Master Haishen with symbols for protecting the residence and enhancing wealth.

2. Place Haishen Diwang Coins Under the Door

Ancient doors had high thresholds, which could block the entry of external Sha Qi in Feng Shui. Modern building designs have eliminated thresholds or made them very low. A modern Feng Shui solution is to use step mats inside the door to resolve this issue. Different colored step mats can be used for resolution, and placing a set of Haishen Diwang Coins under the mat can help enhance wealth and resolve disasters.

3. Hang "Lian Nian You Yu" Inside the Door

Inside the door is also an important spot for enhancing wealth, as the entrance foyer is a place for hiding wind and gathering Qi. This is also where Feng Shui practitioners resolve Sha Qi entering through the door. Everything brought in from outside can be resolved here. Feng Shui practitioners often use "Lian Nian You Yu" (abundant surplus every year) as a Feng Shui object to enhance luck. This involves using a consecrated jade fish, where the word 'fish' sounds like 'surplus.'

1. Plants

Plants have a growth-promoting effect and can place a vibrant plant in this direction to enhance and promote wealth. If the plant leaves turn yellow or grow poorly, please remove it quickly, as it is detrimental to wealth.

2. Fish Tank

We know that water has the effect of enhancing and promoting wealth. A fish tank can be placed in this direction. The tank should not be too large, and six goldfish should be kept inside, preferably black ones. Avoid keeping red goldfish.

3. Lighting

In Feng Shui, the auspicious direction should be bright, not dark. Good fortune comes openly and honestly. A colored light can be installed above the southeast direction of your home, shining on the wealth position. Light has vitality and can attract and promote wealth.

4. Deity Paintings

We know that some deity paintings can enhance wealth, such as the God of Wealth image, and some special talismans, such as wealth-enhancing talismans, financial benefit talismans, and Grand Annual Fortune Talismans, can all be posted in the southeast direction of the home for better results.

5. God of Wealth

The God of Wealth is known to everyone, and many businesspeople have offerings in their homes. The God of Wealth is divided into Civil and Martial Gods of Wealth. Families should place the Civil God of Wealth. If the God of Wealth is placed and worshipped in this direction, the effect will be better.

6. Auspicious Objects

Many people like to place auspicious items that enhance wealth in their homes, such as money cats, money pigs, and money jars. If these auspicious items are placed in the auspicious direction of the home, the effect will be exceptionally good.

Three, Enhance the Marital Bed for Love and Harmony

A family is jointly formed by the husband and wife, so in residential Feng Shui, the marital bedroom is the most important. People will find that if the family is harmonious, the career is good, and the wealth is abundant. If the relationship between the couple is not good, the career or work luck will also decline accordingly. The Feng Shui here governs the happiness of the entire family, especially the relationship between the two partners.

The bed in the marital room is the most important. Its Feng Shui directly affects the relationship between the couple. Make sure that the head of the bed in your home is not facing the door; this is called violating the door alignment. We know that marriage likes harmony and dislikes conflict, and conflict leads to separation. If the door directly faces the bed, how can it be good? Additionally, check if the furniture placement in the home aligns with the bedhead, whether mirrors face the bed, whether the TV directly faces the marital bed, and whether there is a beam pressing down on the bedhead. Finally, check if there are large trees, utility poles, or chimneys outside the window; these are all detrimental to the relationship between the couple. If both of the following two points can be achieved, it would be even better.

1. Auspicious Direction

According to Haishen Feng Shui, this year's favorable directions are the northwest, southeast, and southwest. Since the southwest is the yearly conflict direction, it should not be used. The other two directions can be utilized. The marital bed can be placed in these two directions or aligned toward these two directions. This can enhance the relationship between the couple.

2. Auspicious Colors

The colors of the marital bed and bedding can use this year's lucky colors. This year's annual lucky color is yellow, followed by white. Therefore, bedding items including sheets, bed covers, and quilts can use these lucky colors to enhance luck and make the marital relationship more harmonious and happy.
