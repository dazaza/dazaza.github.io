Collapse of Wang Fi's Concert Stand Leaves 64 Injured and 6 Fractured (Photos)

by vorx2473 on 2012-02-20 08:42:06

On February 17th, at the Olympic Sports Center, the audience seating collapsed during Wang Fi's concert, and the audience was evacuating. At about 8:10 PM on the 17th, a collapse of the audience seating occurred during the Chongqing leg of Wang Fi's tour concert held at the Chongqing Olympic Sports Center. This incident resulted in at least 64 audience members injured, with several suffering fractures. The concert was subsequently canceled.

According to additional reports from Xinhua News Agency, journalists at the scene observed that the collapsed seats were located behind the stage. These seats were placed on metal frame supports, with the highest point being approximately 1.5 meters above the ground. After the seat collapse, some injured audience members were seen being escorted out of the venue, with several having foot injuries and others bleeding from head wounds. They have been sent to the hospital for treatment. Currently, most of the audience at the scene has been evacuated. The concert will be rescheduled for 8 PM on the 19th.

- Scene Report: Collapse possibly due to the audience collectively standing up and sitting down.

Audience member Wei Jun described last night that around 7 PM, he and his group of nine friends entered the venue. Wei Jun explained that the concert stage was set up on the large lawn inside the sports center, and the audience seating was also on the lawn. The seating was supported by scaffolding, with wooden boards laid on top, followed by densely arranged plastic chairs, about one meter above the ground.

Wei Jun continued that the concert began at eight o'clock, and around 8:10, as Wang Fi was about to enter the stage, most of the audience stood up excitedly cheering her entrance. Wei Jun said that it might be due to the collective standing and sitting of the audience that caused the scaffolding under the seats to shake and then disintegrate. He saw that sections E and F collapsed successively, and the G3 section where he was located also collapsed, covering an area of "several hundred square meters."

His knee was caught between the scaffolding, causing the skin to peel off.

By around 10 PM last night, the journalist arrived at the Olympic Sports Center and observed that the audience had already been evacuated. Security personnel closed off the scene. Firefighters used stretchers to carry out the injured.

- Treatment: 64 people injured, 6 with fractures

Chongqing Medical University’s First Affiliated Hospital is only 300 meters away from the Olympic Sports Center. Last night, the hospital's president Ren Guosheng stated that after receiving the emergency call, ambulances were promptly dispatched to the scene for rescue operations.

President Ren introduced that the hospital treated a total of 64 patients. X-rays were taken for 40 patients, and CT scans were performed on three individuals. President Ren explained that the current examination results showed five fractures and one suspected fracture. There was also a pregnant woman, for whom the hospital had provided fetal preservation care. Upon examination, it was found that the fetus was unharmed, with the mother sustaining only minor abrasions. President Ren mentioned that currently, most of the patients suffered only minor injuries, with no critically wounded individuals.

As of 11:30 PM when the journalist went off-duty, some lightly injured individuals registered at the hospital and left on their own. Wei Jun said they initially saw representatives from the concert organizers at the hospital but later they disappeared. So far, the organizers have not made any statements regarding compensation for the injured.

The concert organizing committee announced that the concert would be rescheduled for 8 PM on the 19th. Wei Jun said they still plan to attend. Another injured individual, Liu Dandan, expressed that she wouldn't go again and hoped the organizers would quickly provide post-incident compensation for the victims.

Audience members suspected of having fractures are currently undergoing plaster treatment.

- Response:

"I hope everyone is okay, we'll continue tomorrow or the day after, sorry for scaring you all and causing inconvenience. Sorry and apologies." - Wang Fi

Currently, the event is still dealing with the evacuation of the audience and other safety issues. We have learned from the staff that a few audience members sustained sprains and abrasions during the evacuation, fortunately without causing more serious accidents. After the safe evacuation of the audience, all units will immediately hold meetings to discuss and strictly require the organizing unit to seriously address the safety issues of the audience seating... As for whether the concert can proceed as scheduled tomorrow, further discussions among multiple units are still required. - Wang Fi's agent, Chen Jiaying

At around eight o'clock tonight (February 17th), the edge seating of Area A3 at the Wang Fi concert site at the Olympic Sports Center collapsed due to the displacement of the padded board, resulting in minor leg injuries to 11 people. Currently, all audience members at the scene have been evacuated, and the injured have been sent to the hospital for treatment. Accident investigation and handling are ongoing. Additionally, the organizers have announced that today's performance is canceled and will be rescheduled for 8 PM on the 19th. Tickets can be refunded at the Marriott Hotel on the 6th floor. - Press Office of the People's Government of Chongqing Municipality
