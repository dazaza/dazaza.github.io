Experts from Xi'an Gynecological Hospital remind you: What to do in case of an accidental pregnancy?

by betty2012 on 2012-02-22 17:15:46

Unintended pregnancy refers to pregnancies that result from the failure of contraceptive measures or occur by chance. Many women who experience unintended pregnancies feel at a loss as to what to do. So, what should be done if an unintended pregnancy occurs? Experts from Xi'an Gynecology Hospital tell you: What to do in case of an unintended pregnancy?

1. Face it rationally

Women who experience unintended pregnancies may feel helpless and panic-stricken, not knowing what to do. In fact, after an unintended pregnancy, women should face it rationally without avoiding it. Check your physical condition for symptoms such as amenorrhea, enlarged breasts, nausea, and vomiting. Don't panic; first, conduct a test to see if you are really pregnant. If so, decide whether you want to keep the baby. If not, you should undergo an abortion as soon as possible.

2. Testing methods

Women can use home pregnancy tests to check if they are pregnant. These tests can be purchased from any legitimate pharmacy and use urine samples for testing. However, there is some degree of inaccuracy with home pregnancy tests, so it's best for women to go to the hospital for an ultrasound examination.

3. Relax your mind

After an unintended pregnancy, women should try to relax mentally and seek help from someone they trust most. They should also face the situation bravely since the event has already occurred. Worrying won't help. You can talk to a doctor who will give you specific advice. Therefore, women who find themselves pregnant shouldn't be nervous.

4. Undergo an abortion as soon as possible

The purpose of painless abortion surgery is to terminate the pregnancy. The earlier the surgery is performed, the safer it is. If the pregnancy is less than three months, the surgery is relatively simple. After the surgery, rest for a few days and you can resume normal activities. However, if the fetus is more than three months old, the situation becomes more complicated, making the surgery more complex and potentially causing greater harm to the body. Therefore, it is important to undergo an abortion as soon as possible and not miss the optimal time for a painless abortion.

5. Prepare well before the abortion

Before undergoing an abortion, preparations must be made. Conduct various pre-surgery checks to confirm whether it is an intrauterine pregnancy. If there is inflammation, it needs to be treated actively. Then, based on the results and suggestions from the doctor, determine the method of the abortion.

6. Post-abortion care

After the abortion, attention should be paid to post-operative care. Rest in bed for about a week. Avoid cold, spicy foods and cold water for a month. It’s better to wash your hair less or not at all, and if you do, dry it immediately with a warm blow-dryer. Do not take a bath sitting down. Eat nutritious food. An important point is to stay in contact with the doctor. If any abnormal symptoms occur, report them to the doctor immediately. Maintain a calm mindset.
