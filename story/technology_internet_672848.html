The most beautiful girl in Qingdao

by abcsmhqt on 2012-02-23 10:59:41

Hello everyone! Long time no see...

Do you still remember back in July, I got to know the friends from [Quan Sou Jia] because of the [Most Beautiful Girl] competition.

For these few months, although I am far away in Changchun busy with all sorts of my report performances, I have been keeping an eye on our most beautiful girls.

I can already see that the preliminary selection has come to an end. Each match sees countless beauties emerging. Everyone's wonderful performance is admirable. How I wish I could return to Qingdao immediately and cheer for all my sisters at the scene!

The finals are coming up. Here, I would like to sincerely wish everyone to carry forward the beauty to the end and achieve satisfactory results in the competition! Fighting!

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Although I couldn't be in Qingdao and fight together with everyone on the front line of [Most Beautiful], I have also been studying, working, and living diligently these past few months. After all, to become a [Most Beautiful Girl], one must not only have a beautiful appearance but also possess rich connotations, noble character, and a heart that loves life. Below, I will share with everyone some recent aspects of my life.

[This is a behind-the-scenes photo of me participating in the shooting of the static movie "33 Days of Lost Love" in Changchun. It was my first time shooting outdoor scenes in the rain, it was very tiring.]

[In November this year, I shot wedding samples for a Changchun photography studio. I really like this kind of clean and neat makeup look.]

[On that legendary "Singles' Day" with six "1"s, my self-directed and self-performed adapted version of "The Wind Voice" officially premiered. For this play, I spent countless brain cells, but hard work always pays off, and flowers and applause can prove everything.]

[In addition to acting and directing courses, photography is also one of my required courses. During the National Day holiday, I returned to Qingdao, picked up my camera, and captured the beauty of autumn forever.]

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

In the future, if there are any new things, Ha Ni will update the posts in the first time to share with everyone.

Finally, once again, I would like to cheer and encourage all the participants in the [Most Beautiful Girl] competition.
