Shoe Store Showcase Decoration Design Guide

Shoe store showcase style positioning, shoe store showcase design, Guangzhou cosmetics showcase production, must clearly understand whether the brand you are doing is casual shoes or shoes worn in the workplace, men's shoes or women's shoes. The sales area showcase for casual women's shoes should give people a sense of spontaneity and relaxation. Strong rhythm background music can be provided, with contrasting colors and brilliant lighting. Folded display, front display, and side display should interweave with each other. The placement of shelves should have an overall feel amidst spontaneity. Formal wear showcases are the opposite.

The color of women's shoe sales area showcases should have a feminine touch. Light blue + white, red + white, crimson + white, beige + white, white, black + white, etc., are all good choices. The lines of the sales area should be streamlined and delicate, with soft lighting and more mirrors (women naturally love looking in the mirror, when there are more mirrors here, even if they don't look at your shoes, it will attract them). Men's shoes, on the other hand, feature rough lines and deep colors, often made from materials like walnut wood.

After we've determined our positioning, the next step is to introduce the elements of the sales area showcase design:

1. The color of the sales area showcase needs to be unified. Women's shoe showcases and the decoration colors should harmoniously blend together, allowing people to immediately see the main color tone of your sales area. However, the unity I'm referring to does not mean that the women's shoes and the decoration colors need to be completely consistent, as that would make the sales area showcase appear monotonous and rigid. Instead, there should be local contrasts while conforming to the overall design.

2. Purposefulness of lighting. In women's shoe sales area showcases, lighting plays a crucial role. The display effect of the same pair of women's shoes with lighting versus without lighting is entirely different, especially for some individually displayed high-end women's shoes, spotlights must be used for emphasis. The color of the lights should also be appropriate; blue light gives a cool, cold, and mysterious feeling (for selling sandals, slippers), yellow lights give a warm feeling (for selling autumn and winter shoes).

3. Shelf placement should leave space for walking, divided into main and secondary aisles. The width of the main aisle must not be less than 120 centimeters, and the secondary aisle must not be less than 80 centimeters. The image background board should face the main entrance or the main aisle of the sales area.
