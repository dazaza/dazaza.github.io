QQ Show: The Magic Cards of QQ Show are out, Red Diamond LV6 and LV7 get the first experience.

by lwf225 on 2009-09-07 18:59:09

Magic Cards is a casual game similar to "QQ Farm" and "Stealing Cars," inspired by the childhood pastime of collecting raccoon cards. In this game, the system freely distributes material cards to players within the game scenario. Players need to exchange with friends or purchase to collect the required material cards. With lower-level material cards, they can synthesize higher-level ones, eventually forming complete sets of cards.

After successfully collecting a set of cards, players can boast in front of their friends. Imagine you have already collected a full set of Three Kingdoms cards while your friend is still struggling to do so. Besides that, the system also rewards players with a free set of exquisite QQ Show as a prize. Below is the gameplay flowchart for Magic Cards:

1. **Drawing Cards**

You can draw opportunity cards from your card box. Opportunity cards increase by one every 30 minutes, up to a maximum of 16, after which they no longer increase.

2. **Trading Cards**

You can trade cards with your friends or the card market owner. You can only exchange cards of the same value with your friends. If your friend has set specific trading conditions, you must use the cards your friend needs to make the trade.

3. **Buying Cards**

You can purchase desired material cards from the market. However, market material cards are relatively expensive, uniformly priced at 50 coins each.

4. **Refining Cards**

Some cards can only be obtained through refining in the card refiner. Refining cards follows certain rules, which you can find in the card guide. Refining consumes the material cards you place inside and requires a waiting period. You can place up to three cards in the refiner at once, but only one card can be refined at a time. The remaining cards will automatically queue up and begin refining once the previous card is finished. After refining is completed, you obtain the refined card and gain some experience points.

5. **Placing in the Card Album**

After successfully collecting a complete set of cards, you can place them into the card album via the card guide. Placing cards in the album rewards you with a big gift pack, containing experience, money, and QQ Show item prizes. Placing cards in the album frees up card slots, but once placed, the cards cannot be removed. Therefore, consider carefully before placing them.

6. **About Levels**

The role of levels:

- More card slots (every level-up increases two safe deposit box slots; reaching a certain level unlocks the swap card box slots).

- Ability to refine higher-level cards.

Sources of experience:

- Completing card refinement grants experience.

- Placing synthesized set cards into the card album grants experience.

Currently, Magic Cards allows priority access for Red Diamond members at LV6 and LV7. Early access link: http://card.show.qq.com. Members who have activated the service get two chances to invite friends with lower Red Diamond levels or non-Red Diamond members!
