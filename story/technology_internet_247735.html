The domain name industry is once again in turmoil, with the price reduction of COM domains triggering a frenzy of agency activities.

by now on 2009-10-15 10:57:47

As the saying goes, a rainbow appears after the storm, but the rainbow in all walks of life after the financial storm needs to be created by themselves. In the situation where the economy stabilizes but lacks growth momentum, each industry comes up with its own tricks to create a better development prospect for itself in the post-financial crisis era, and only in this way can it keep pace with the times and maintain stable growth. Facing such an economic environment, the domain name industry has also made frequent moves, triggering wave after wave of trends. Just as the big wave of CN price reduction has calmed down, there are new trends in COM domain names.

Recently, the author learned from Era Interconnection (www.todaynic.com) that after the previous round of CN domain name price reduction, in order to help its agents improve sales performance, the company launched the promotion activities of .com and .net domain names when returning from the National Day holiday. The "high rebate for buying domain names" in the activity attracted the attention of many agents, and price reduction promotion was inevitable.

An agent of Era Interconnection revealed to the author that currently, the actual price of the COM domain name he got, including rebates, equals 40 yuan. Based on this calculation, the agency price of the COM domain name has dropped to 40 yuan. In the domain name circle, COM domain names rarely have large price reductions, and this time's 40-yuan agency price is expected to cause quite a stir, which may bring a new upturn to the recent domain name investment field.

In fact, the price reduction and promotion of COM domain names have been long-awaited by many agents. COM domain names have always been the first choice for website construction and investment internationally, and their resources are becoming less and less. Under this background of resource scarcity, its price has always been high, and this price reduction is a rare opportunity.

A few days ago, when the Insure.com domain name was sold for 16 million US dollars, everyone exclaimed that the investment value of COM domain names had warmed up again. In fact, the COM domain names that could be sold at high prices this year are not just Insure.com; the sales of candy.com, toys.com, and other domain names were also very expensive. It can be seen that COM domain names are still the investment objects for agents to grow bigger and stronger.

Although the domain name industry is a special industry, it also needs continuous injection of vitality to promote the development of this industry. At the moment when the economy stabilizes, in order to maintain the steady development of domain name agents, it is inevitable for registrars to adopt price reduction promotions. Appropriate price reductions can bring more profits and broader development space to agents, and the domain name industry can develop more vigorously.
