As long as you turn back, I will be here...

As long as you look back, I will be here...

Xin'er, you are a little sprite.

You are the calamity that fate has arranged for me. I know I can't avoid it, and there's nowhere to hide!

--- Preface

Xin'er, he once called her softly, his voice so gentle. It was as if a warm stream had flowed into her heart!

She turned around and gave him a smile, but suddenly her tears burst forth. In the moment she turned away, they shattered into lines!

All these years, Zuoyu Chen, I thought we would never meet again!

16 years old

At 16, Cheng Xinuo was a famous figure in that high school, with excellent academic performance. She was immediately a person of great attention, with bright eyes and youthful vigor!

She was the undisputed host of all kinds of activities in the school, praised by teachers and students alike.

She had an aura about her, even though she was only in the first year of high school!

The high school at the time was very chaotic, especially classes two and three. These two classes were notorious troublemakers in the school. The guidance teachers all turned a blind eye. They showed no respect for their elders, often wreaking havoc on campus, and frequently blocking female students on their way home, laughing loudly at the angry faces of the young girls!

Just as Cheng Xinuo returned from the guidance office, she saw this scene from afar. The flustered girl in the crowd was her good friend, Su Ruoli, so she quickly stepped forward to protect Ruoli!

Since such actions by females were unheard of in the school, the group of troublemakers were stunned. Someone said, "Jing, there's someone!"

Instantly, silence fell. All eyes focused, and finally, a tall, thin boy emerged from the crowd. With a stride of his long legs, he stood in front of Cheng Xinuo and Su Ruoli!

Ruoli moved closer to Cheng Xinuo, who gave her a reassuring smile, then looked at the boy called Jing. His hair was brown, his face still retaining some childishness, and his gaze was so brazen, looking straight at Cheng Xinuo as if trying to see through her soul!

"Move aside," Cheng Xinuo coolly said.

He lightly lifted the corner of his lips and made way for her!

The next day, news spread across the campus: That Cheng Xinuo from School No.5 was not afraid of Xiao Jingyan!

Xiao Jingyan was one of the delinquents in that school. Many teachers and students were afraid of him, taking detours when they saw him!

2. Zuoyu Chen

On the day of school enrollment, Zuoyu Chen was encountered.

In September, the clouds were pure white and soft like cotton. Cheng Xinuo's face carried a lazy expression. Turning around, she saw Zuoyu Chen. That forever clean and neat boy, whose smile was so gentle!

"The weather is nice," Zuoyu Chen said to Cheng Xinuo. This was the first sentence they spoke. Many years later, Chen recalled their encounter during his youth with Cheng Xinuo, and it was still vivid in his memory!

The sky outside was silent and azure, and the wind lazily blew. Zuoyu Chen heard his heart pounding heavily!

That day, Cheng Xinuo wore a white top, her short hair fluttering. When she turned around, it was that plain and innocent face. Like a gardenia flower in the southern seasons, its fragrance spreading, so pure and charming!

For three years of high school, they were classmates. Three years passed too quickly, too fast to reminisce before it became a memory!

Xin'er, in the hallway during afternoon breaks, Zuoyu Chen would gently call her from behind, so indulgent, calling her cautiously, as if afraid that a loud voice would make Cheng Xinuo disappear from his world!

It wasn't until Cheng Xinuo was 23 that she realized Zuoyu Chen had liked her back then. Then she understood how good Zuoyu Chen had been to her, quietly sending her home after each class and bringing her late-night snacks!

And back then, at 17, Zuoyu Chen defined all of his happiness and love.

3. Confused Love

Su Ruoli fell in love with Xiao Jingyan.

On that night in the second year of high school, Ruoli's face was full of shyness. On that misty night, she blossomed her youth, giving everything she had!

The arrogant Jing, the long-haired Jing, the bewitching Jing.

Xin'er, I really like Jing, I love him so much. Now I am so happy. Jing also likes me. Do you know? I want to be with him!

Love can make a woman incredibly beautiful. At 16, Ruoli, with her smooth hair and crystal-clear face, her eyes filled with tenderness and happiness. Like the roses in the corner of the east wall of the campus, they bloomed luxuriantly, showing no signs of wilting!

After a while, Jing came to pick up Ruoli. Ruoli flew into Jing's arms like a little bird, her pure white dress fluttering like a butterfly, beautifully happy!

But the arrogant Jing lazily leaned against the side, looking at the running Ruoli with cold eyes, devoid of any warmth! And those spark-filled eyes landed on Cheng Xinuo standing behind!

Ruoli said, "Xin'er, wouldn't it be wonderful if we could always be together, never growing up, never separating! We'll always be together, you and Chen, me and Jing, how wonderful it would be! But why do I feel that Jing is getting farther and farther away from me? Even though he's right in front of me, lying next to me, I feel like I can't reach him, he seems so unreachable?"

4. Angels and Sprites

In the second semester of senior year, so many things happened, making us a bit melancholy in that season of flowers!

Chen transferred schools, he went to a distant city!

Xin'er, if I have the chance, I will come back to see you.

Chen, why are you always so gentle to me, always speaking to me in such a careful tone?

Hehe, because, you are the angel in my heart, sleeping as pure and beautiful as a baby!

When bidding farewell to Chen, Cheng Xinuo only said 'take care' to Zuoyu Chen.

Life returned to normal, some students were preparing hard for the college entrance exam, most classmates were aiming for graduation certificates, then going out to work with family or friends!

The future is a habitual road, unrelated to academics. Seeds drift everywhere, wherever they land is life! Early romance in school was a prevailing trend, Ruoli still played and laughed with classmates every day!

But Cheng Xinuo was different. She was a focus of attention in the school, destined to shine and unable to settle down. She participated in and hosted various school events, using the remaining time to review. She hoped to study well and get into the university she desired!

Every time she walked through the school corridor, she always felt someone calling her name 'Xin'er,' but when she turned around, there was no one. She often thought of Zuoyu Chen's gentle smile!

Receiving letters from Chen, one after another beautifully crafted letter, using Cheng Xinuo's favorite pale pink paper. Xin'er, I miss you, take care of yourself.

Cheng Xinuo kept these letters in a locked small box, but never replied!

When Cheng Xinuo met Xiao Jingyan again, it was on an afternoon after duty. Jing, you should be kinder to Li, Cheng Xinuo said!

Do you not know that I like you? What am I supposed to do with you? Xin'er, you're a little sprite, you're the calamity that fate has arranged for me. I know I can't avoid it. Xiao Jingyan used a helpless voice to say what he had wanted to say for years!

I'm not afraid of what you have to say. I just hope you treat Ruoli well, make her happy and joyful!

5. As Long as You Look Back, I Will Be Here

A few years later, one evening, Zuoyu Chen finally returned. Cheng Xinuo had just finished work not long ago, thinking she had seen wrong, so she rubbed her eyes a few times to confirm it wasn't an illusion.

All these years, Yu Chen, I thought we would never meet again!

Xin'er, it's been a long time since we last saw each other. Remember today is your 23rd birthday. I brought you a gift, a crystal shoe. The shop owner said that when angels fly, they lose their shoes and fall to earth looking for them. Xin'er, I only give you one. Later, Yu Chen took Xin'er out to eat. He carefully ordered her favorite dishes, indulgently watching her satisfied expression!

After eating, Zuoyu Chen adjusted Cheng Xinuo's collar, pinned her hair up with a hair clip. Xin'er, I always fear you will disappear. I always worry I won't find you, can't find you... So I left a crystal shoe, so you can't fly away unless I choose to let go! Why do I always feel you're not happy? Even though your face looks so happy, no matter what, as long as you look back, I will be here.

Zuoyu Chen's thin lips lightly touched Cheng Xinuo's forehead.

Later, every holiday, she would receive greeting cards and gifts sent by Chen. He always wrote: Angel, be happy, I'm by your side!

Occasionally, there would be his phone calls: Xin'er, how are you? Today I saw a pretty hair clip, you'd look great wearing it, very suitable. I really want to see you wear it.

6. Everything Seems Perfect, Yet Filled with Lies

If appearances can cover everything, as long as it's perfect, why bother questioning authenticity?

The truth always represents harm, sometimes leaving people with no retreat, resulting in scars!

That day, Cheng Xinuo's Weibo post, a few lines of text, already surpassed age-appropriate calmness and insight!

Her home was no longer a home, adult dialogues. Hypocritical marriages, as long as they persist, eventually remain marriages, eventually remain families, but why must both sides be so determined. Endless arguments lead to a final split.

Cheng Xincheng had lost the strength to deal with these adults' matters.

Xin Cheng, why doesn't Jing want me anymore? He said sorry to me, told me to live well. How could the arrogant and proud Jing also say sorry? I truly thought I would spend my whole life with him, I just want to love him well.

One day at 16, Jing confessed to Cheng Xincheng. Xin'er, I like you!

But Cheng Xincheng just raised the corners of her mouth without smiling, Jing, we are not meant to be together, different paths shouldn't intersect. If we were together, it would be sinful.

Xiao Jingyan held Cheng Xincheng tightly, and a crisp sound echoed!

Cheng Xincheng, you will pay for what you've done today. I won't hurt you, but I will return everything you've done to me onto your good friend Su Ruoli. Just wait and watch the show.

7. Half-Life Conclusion

That summer at 23, the sky was always quiet. Cheng Xinuo always wore black clothes, her bangs so long, covering her clean forehead.

Jing followed his father to Shenzhen.

Ruoli and Jing's relationship didn't escape the notice of both sets of parents, and Ruoli refused to reveal Jing's name. That year, she married a man from Northeast China.

While Cheng Xinuo's family seemed intact, the cracks in the mirror couldn't be mended perfectly, the cracks remained!

Only Yu Chen, who continued writing letters to Cheng Xinuo every day, sending gifts as usual during holidays. But Cheng Xinuo no longer opened them, instead putting them back in the box and returning them along with the gifts to Chen.

Days passed silently, pushing and resisting quietly. That night, Zuoyu Chen called Cheng Xinuo: Xin'er, give me a definite answer, let me know whether I should stay or leave!

Cheng Xinuo said: Sorry, Chen, I can't accept you! Too many things have happened, your presence only makes me remember the past, so, I want a new life, you need to disappear from my world!

After a few seconds of silence, Chen faintly said: Alright, if my departure can bring you back to your former joy and happiness, I am willing! I only hope you live well...

8. Looking Back, Memories Fade Like Smoke

Looking back again, it's another spring. Cheng Xinuo is now twenty-four. Recalling the events of her youth, she couldn't help but tear up, even after all these years!

At the class reunion, Cheng Xinuo greeted her classmates constantly, only to realize that most around her were couples. For so many years, she had been alone!

In a quiet corner, she met her old best friend from school days. They hadn't been in touch for years. Ruoli had transformed from the naive girl she once was into a weary housewife;

It was only now that she learned from Ruoli: Their relationship with Xiao Jingyan was discovered by both sets of parents. She refused to reveal the boy's identity, and Xiao Jingyan directly fled to another place! Ruoli's parents directly married her off to a young man from Northeast China. Glancing at her husband, he seemed quite honest, treating Ruoli well and considerately. Perhaps this was her best destination!

Chatting with Ruoli at the reunion about the various amusing incidents from their previous class, it felt like they were back in those days. Back then, they really had a lot of fun! Not long after, Xiao Jingyan arrived. He had matured. The once reckless youth was now an editor at a certain company.

Seeing Su Ruoli, Xiao Jingyan felt guilty and said, "Sorry!"

Ruoli said, "Jing, memories fade like smoke. Those things have passed, you don't need to feel sorry for me. I am living well now, happily. I hope you can be happy too!"

"Where is Zuoyu Chen? Have we not been in contact with him for all these years?" Several classmates asked.

...No...

After the reunion, Cheng Xinuo and Su Ruoli strolled leisurely, chatting about the things that had happened to each of them over the years...

Unconsciously, Cheng Xinuo pondered the scenario of meeting Zuoyu Chen, recalling the crystal shoe she had kept at home! How much she wished that Chen was behind her now, but ever since that day she asked him to disappear from her world, Chen had not shown up again. However, the greeting cards during each holiday were still received regularly...

"As long as you look back, I will be here"... But now I have looked back, why can't I see you?
