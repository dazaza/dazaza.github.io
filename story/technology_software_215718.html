**Function Introduction:**

**Affiliate Program:** The new Lodoeshop system includes an advanced affiliate module. Each member has a unique promotional code and URL automatically generated by the system. Website owners can place these codes on their websites to earn commissions. Through this "affiliate program," every member becomes a salesperson and repeat customer, maximizing the power of the crowd and significantly increasing website traffic and sales.

**Discount Plans:** The system offers various discount plans, including free gifts for purchases over a certain amount, discounts for reaching thresholds, shopping coupons, cash vouchers, point redemption for gifts, buy-one-get-one-free deals, and free shipping for orders above a specific value. Flexible application of these promotions can attract more customers to your store.

**Optional Orders:** The system has a unique optional order feature. When customers select items and proceed to checkout, the system suggests additional products or services for a small extra cost, such as gift wrapping, greeting cards, or other perks. This effectively stimulates purchasing desires and significantly increases sales.

**SMS Integration:** The system seamlessly integrates mobile SMS functionality. Administrators can send mass or individual SMS messages to registered members or external phone number databases. SMS notifications are sent automatically when users register, place orders, make account changes, recover passwords, or receive shipment confirmations. This enhances the credibility and friendliness of the store. Additionally, administrators can receive SMS notifications for new registrations, orders, and payment confirmations, improving efficiency and allowing real-time monitoring of business operations.

**Static Page Generation:** The system can generate pure HTML static pages with customizable folder settings.

**Online Upgrade:** The system supports one-click online upgrades, making system updates effortless.

**Membership Levels:** Supports unlimited membership levels with different pricing tiers, minimum purchase amounts, etc.

**Product Management:** Easily add, modify, or delete products. A single product can belong to multiple categories. Customize related products, multi-dimensional product displays, inventory alerts, and pricing for different membership levels. Products can also automatically include custom watermarks.

**Product Categories:** Categories support user-defined attributes, copying, and unlimited hierarchical classification.

**Brand Management:** Easily add brand images or text descriptions with customizable brand introductions. Brands can be linked to product categories.

**Keyword Library:** Use tag (label) editing functionality to set unique titles, keywords, and descriptions for each page. Website optimization becomes flexible and customizable.

**Order Management:** Clear and simple order management with eight order statuses (unprocessed → paid → shipped → completed → canceled → refunded → returned). After placing an order, the system automatically sends email confirmations. Administrators can query orders by status in the backend.

**Sales Analysis:** Six types of analytical rankings (product sales, member sales, product visits, product type analysis, member logins, payment method analysis) assist users in integrated management.

**Advertising Management:** Built-in support for various ad formats (expandable ads, floating ads, pop-up ads, changing ads, top ads, bottom ads, lower-right corner ads, category ads, extended ads, promotional ads). Extended ads allow infinite customization across any page of the online store.

**Payment Management:** Seamless integration with dozens of famous online payment gateways such as Alipay, PayPal, UnionPay, QuickMoney, Yibao Payment, etc. Users can choose or simultaneously use multiple payment methods based on preference.

**Template Management:** Default templates are included. Users can select suitable templates from the official website, download them, and import them into the system. Advanced users can use a template editor for custom designs.

**Email Sending:** Automatically sends emails during events like registration, password recovery, order creation, payment confirmation, and shipping. Email content is customizable.

**Message Management:** In the backend, administrators can view visitor messages along with their real IP addresses and message times. Control whether messages are displayed or deleted to prevent vandalism.

**Visit Statistics:** Built-in detailed site visit records allow real-time tracking of visitor dynamics.

**Polls & Surveys:** Create polls and surveys to collect visitor feedback, providing references for store operations.

**Permission Management:** Supports assigning permissions to each user, meeting the needs of multi-user store management.

**More Features:** The above is just a small part of all available features. For more details, refer to the official website.
