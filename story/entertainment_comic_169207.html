Jiang Tong Animation Co., Ltd is a cultural and creative enterprise that focuses on the young children market, operating animation copyright content and animation brands. The company has an annual production capacity of 5,000 minutes of international standard animated films, making it one of the few animation enterprises in China that has been awarded both the "National Animation Industry Base" by the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television and the "National Cultural Industry Demonstration Base" by the Ministry of Culture.

The company possesses a mature and efficient production management system and a team of directors with rich experience in planning, providing services for the pre-production, mid-production, and post-production of animated films. The large-scale original animated film "Tian Shang Diao Xiao Ge Zhu Ba Jie" produced by the company won the special prize for series animation at the second China International Animation Festival, as well as the excellent long-form art film award at the 23rd China TV Golden Eagle Awards.

Currently, in terms of animation industry chain operation, the company mainly relies on the domestic market, focusing on brand authorization and marketing channel construction, dedicated to developing derivative products of its own brand, forming an animation derivative industry chain that integrates TV program broadcasting, ground-level brand promotion, animation book distribution, cartoon brand authorization, and derivative product research and development and specialty stores. In 2008, under the backdrop of the global financial crisis, the company still maintained steady growth in its main business revenue.

With enhancing the creative ability of original planning as its core competitiveness, Jiang Tong Animation is committed to launching more competitive national animation brands and striving to build the company into one of China's main animation content providers and one of China's main animation production bases.
