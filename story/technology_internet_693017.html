Lawyer sentenced for bribing judge with 430,000 yuan on behalf of client

by lovei0c1 on 2012-03-06 13:58:48

Lawyer Sentenced for Bribing Judge with 430,000 RMB; Specific Case Details Remain Unreleased

Southern Daily (Weibo) [http://weibo.com/nfrb?zw=news] reported (by reporter Chao Dao Sen): Yesterday, it was learned that a well-known entrepreneur from Sanshui bribed Assistant Judge Yang Chongkang of the Foshan Intermediate People's Court through his agent lawyer to obtain a favorable verdict. The lawyer, Li Xiaoping, has recently been sentenced by the Chan Cheng District People’s Court to one and a half years in prison.

This bribery case occurred during an investment contract dispute. After discussing with Lu Cancuang, chairman of Sanshui Hongtong Earthwork Engineering Co., Ltd. (hereafter referred to as Hongtong Company), agent lawyer Li Xiaoping subsequently handed over a total of 430,000 RMB in bribes to Yang Chongkang, the assistant judge responsible for hearing the case at the Foshan Intermediate People's Court. The Chan Cheng District People’s Court ruled that Li Xiaoping had committed the crime of bribery and sentenced him to one and a half years in prison.

It is understood that the trial for Judge Yang Chongkang’s case of accepting bribes has already taken place at the Chan Cheng District People’s Court, but the details of the trial have not yet been made public. Meanwhile, this case also involves the well-known entrepreneur from Sanshui, Lu Cancuang (who is being handled separately).

The entrepreneur from Sanshui bribed the judge through his lawyer.

Li Xiaoping was originally the director and partner of Guangdong Kunxiang Law Firm. In 2006, Lu Cancuang, the legal representative of Sanshui Hongtong Company, approached Li Xiaoping, asking him to represent the company in an investment contract dispute involving Sanshui Qianye Garden Real Estate Co., Ltd., Guangzhou Oriental Catering Entertainment Co., Ltd., and Sanshui Forest Grand Hotel Co., Ltd.

According to the information obtained by the reporter, while representing the case, from 2004 to 2009, the case had already gone through several legal procedures including second instance trial, retrial, remand for retrial, and second remand for retrial.

The prosecution stated that after the second remand for retrial, in order to obtain a favorable judgment, Li Xiaoping and Lu Cancuang discussed giving money to Yang Chongkang, the assistant judge in charge of the case at the Civil Division Five of the Foshan Intermediate People's Court. In the second half of 2008, downstairs from Yang Chongkang’s home, lawyer Li Xiaoping handed over 200,000 RMB and a navigation device given by Lu Cancuang to Yang Chongkang. After receiving them, Yang Chongkang returned the navigation device and 100,000 RMB to Li Xiaoping.

In 2009, the Foshan Intermediate People's Court made a ruling on the case, determining that Hongtong Company’s actual investment ratio in cooperative investment operations was 57.1%, and companies such as Qianye Garden were ordered to pay compensation exceeding 90 million RMB.

To thank Yang Chongkang for making a favorable judgment for the side he represented, in January and March of 2011, Li Xiaoping gave Yang Chongkang a total of 230,000 RMB in two separate instances.

The lawyer was sentenced to one and a half years in prison.

On July 22, 2011, defendant Li Xiaoping assisted in the investigation and voluntarily confessed to the aforementioned criminal facts during the investigation period, leading to his criminal detention on the same day. On August 4 of the same year, after approval by the procuratorate, Li Xiaoping was arrested. Subsequently, after the investigation by the anti-graft bureau of the procuratorate concluded, the procuratorate prosecuted Li Xiaoping for the crime of bribery at the end of last December.

After being reviewed by the Chan Cheng District People's Court, it was found that in 2008, the Higher People's Court of Guangdong Province ruled that the case be remanded back to the Foshan Intermediate People's Court for retrial. In this case, as the lawyer representing Hongtong Company, Li Xiaoping started giving a total of 430,000 RMB to Judge Yang Chongkang from the second half of 2008, constituting the crime of bribery.

On February 14 of this year, the Chan Cheng District People's Court legally sentenced Li Xiaoping to one and a half years in prison for the crime of bribery.

Upon multiple inquiries by the reporter, it was learned that the case of Assistant Judge Yang Chongkang accepting bribes had already been tried at the Chan Cheng District People's Court, but the details of the trial have not yet been disclosed to the public.
