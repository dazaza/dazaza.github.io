When prices soar, you have to keep accounts online!

2007 was an unusual year in China. Why was it unusual? Because prices soared while salaries remained stagnant. The chili peppers at the entrance of our neighborhood used to cost 1.5 yuan, but now they've doubled to 3 yuan. Pork used to be 6 yuan, and now a pound of pork ribs costs 20 yuan. The stock market went wild, with everyone investing in stocks. Why did everyone invest in stocks? Because keeping money in the bank didn't earn much; it only led to currency devaluation. Therefore, the only way to make money grow was through stock investment.

In 2008, what will China be like? It's an undeniable fact that prices are soaring. The Chinese economy continues to develop rapidly. So under the current circumstances of rising prices and stagnant wages, apart from investing in stocks, are there any other ways to make more money? Yes or no? There definitely is - learning how to save money. Saving money is easier than making money.

Yesterday, I opened an online accounting website I've been using - Caike Online (www.coko365.com). One member sent me a message saying, "The money you earn isn't your money; the money you save is your money." This statement is absolutely correct. What's most important right now is knowing how much money you've saved. If you want to save money, you must cultivate good habits and stop being a 'moonlight clan' member who spends all their income every month. To save money, you must first learn how to economize. To economize, you must clearly understand where every cent of your money goes. Naturally, keeping accounts is the most crucial step. Therefore, I suggest that everyone, like me, persistently keep daily accounts, recording all the money we spend each day. Then, every week or every month, review where your money has gone, how much you've spent, where you shouldn't have spent money, and where you shouldn't have spent too much. Only by understanding your financial situation can you better manage your finances. Keeping accounts is the foundation of financial management, which is aimed at saving more money. Learn to reasonably plan your finances and life.

Housewives, as the custodians of household finances and the finance ministers of the home, naturally should understand the family's assets and income, keeping clear accounts in mind. Therefore, many housewives choose to keep accounts online. They open their computers every day, record the money spent in the household, and then tally up the statistics monthly to manage their homes in an orderly manner.

Friends planning to get married are busy preparing for the wedding, which involves spending a lot of money. However, I dare say that many married friends, when they look back on their weddings, probably can't give specific and detailed figures of how much they spent, only a rough estimate at best. If you could know exactly where each cent of your money went, when it was spent, where it was spent, and how much was spent, you could naturally save more expenses and better plan for the future. As long as everyone persists in keeping accounts online every day, this can be easily achieved.

Renovation is not an easy task. The cost of renovation is almost equal to buying a house, but renovation is even more troublesome, requiring daily trips to check materials and supplies. If you could understand the destination of every cent during the renovation process, you could have a clear idea and control over your financial status. Online bookkeeping can help you easily grasp your financial situation during the renovation process.

Small business owners, distributors, online shop owners, wholesalers, and store owners have large amounts of accounts to handle every day. Writing them down in a notebook is outdated and tiring. So why not try online bookkeeping? Currently, white-collar workers in major cities are very popular with online bookkeeping and financial management. At the same time, many online bookkeeping and financial management websites have emerged in China. For example, Caike Online is one of the most representative ones, with powerful functions, convenience, simplicity, and practicality. Thousands of people keep accounts online simultaneously every day. The website already has tens of thousands of registered members, making it currently the online bookkeeping site with the most members. The active members every day are also the highest, with tens of thousands of data entries in forums and daily financial diaries of members. Being the most chosen and active online bookkeeping site is undoubtedly correct. If you don't know how to choose, just follow everyone else's choice. Choose Caike Online for bookkeeping.
