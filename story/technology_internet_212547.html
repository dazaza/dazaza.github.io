Domain names focus on e-commerce to help enterprises accumulate strength for future development

by now on 2009-07-22 15:42:11

Ten years ago, the e-commerce market in our country was still a barren land, and ten years later today, the e-commerce market in our country is already bustling with activity. Online shopping and online trading have carved out their own niche in the internet era. With the continuous growth of internet users in our country, the pace of e-commerce advancement will continue to accelerate. Following this development rhythm, many companies in our country have already realized the importance of developing e-commerce and have begun to implement online marketing strategies.

Using platforms is not a long-term solution

Developing e-commerce requires a certain amount of human and material resources, and also requires companies to accelerate their informatization process. To save effort, many companies prefer to use existing platforms like Alibaba for business operations. Of course, these platforms are not entirely free; they require companies to pay a certain fee, after which they provide a platform for companies to conduct trade on the internet. Currently, many small and medium-sized enterprises in our country are taking this development path, without their own independent platform or their own independent profit model. This can play a certain role in the early stages, but it is ultimately not a long-term solution. Without their own online platform, development will be restricted. To secure a place on the internet, companies need to understand how to build their own development platform for long-term growth.

Activating domain names: Small investment with long-lasting benefits

A domain name is a network asset that every company enjoys, and it is an important foundation for companies to develop e-commerce. However, there is a widespread lack of understanding among Chinese companies about using domain names to develop e-commerce. In fact, the cost of a domain name is not very high. Companies can completely register relevant domain names related to themselves. Even if they do not yet plan to establish their own website for business operations, they can point the domain name to their corresponding business platform. This is beneficial for expanding current business operations and also advantageous when the time is ripe to establish their own independent brand. A company's investment in a domain name to secure the development of e-commerce does not require a high cost and can lay a solid foundation for the company's long-term development.

For companies that want to develop internet economy, purchasing a good domain name is always the first step. For example, 161.com was purchased by a merchant for 6 million RMB to enter the clothing B2C market, fully demonstrating the importance of a good domain name for a company’s e-commerce development. Regarding this, a relevant person in charge from the top-tier domain registrar, Now (www.now.cn), stated that to stand out in the vast network, build a lasting brand, and achieve successful commercial operations, one must have their own unique identifier — a domain name. Only a simple, memorable domain name with profound implications for the company can shoulder the responsibility of promoting the company's brand, gain more recognition from internet users, and achieve the purpose of the company's long-term development of e-commerce.

Although developing e-commerce is not as simple as registering a domain name and building a website, to grow strong and large, companies cannot solely rely on related platforms for development. Developing e-commerce in the internet era is inevitable for companies. Using domain names to establish brands and develop e-commerce is a long-term strategy. In this information age where only the strong survive, seeking shortcuts for breakthroughs is no longer the way for companies to survive in e-commerce development. Only by being down-to-earth can companies carve out a broad path.
