3 urban management office staff were caught for taking drugs, and 2 temporary workers were fired

by suntdn3hur on 2012-03-01 09:29:32

Yesterday, Yang Xiujin also denied the rumor of "being confronted by urban management officers when investigated by the police on that day". Yang Xiujin said that at more than 10 p.m. on November 18, he received a call from Zhou Guofa, captain of the law enforcement team, saying that the police found several officers were taking drugs in the office of the law enforcement team and would take them to the county public security bureau for questioning. He immediately made the following requirements to Zhou Guofa: firmly support and cooperate with the public security organs to investigate according to law, and not to obstruct. And he learned afterwards that no one in the municipal law enforcement team had hindered the police from enforcing the law that night. Yang Xiujin said that he was attending a meeting in Chongqing on the day of the incident. On November 19, he urgently returned to Xiushan from Chongqing, and convened a meeting of all staffs of the bureau on that day, making a report of this matter at the meeting, and Zhou, captain of the county municipal law enforcement team, made a self-examination at the meeting. Subsequently, the Party Leadership Group of the Bureau held a meeting to study and decide to give Zhang Shaojun, a formal urban management officer who participated in taking drugs, a serious warning within the Party (which is under approval by the Party Committee of the Directly Affiliated Organs of Xiushan County), and dismiss Zhang Ke and Wu Ping immediately. "Three urban management officers in Xiushan County are too unreliable, actually inviting idle social persons to gather and take drugs in the office of the law enforcement team of the Municipal Landscape Administration Bureau", on November 19, "Uncle Ma" exposed this matter on the tip platform of Dayu, and said that the Xiushan Public Security Bureau had arrested several drug takers on the evening of the 18th. Yesterday, the Municipal Landscape Administration Bureau of Xiushan County replied in the post: Zhang Hongbo, a formal employee involved in this matter, had been given a serious warning within the Party, and the other two temporary employees were dismissed immediately. Voices of Dayu netizens Yesterday, the reporter verified this matter with the public security bureau of Xiushan County, and relevant person in charge of the Xiushan County Public Security Bureau said that it was true. The person in charge introduced that at more than 10 p.m. on November 18, the Xiushan County Public Security Bureau received a report from a man, saying that someone was taking drugs in the office of the municipal law enforcement team of Xiushan. The police attached great importance to it, and since the triangular tower patrol platform was nearby, the leader of the county public security bureau immediately instructed the police of the platform to go and investigate. After receiving the report, the police of the platform only took about 3 minutes to arrive, broke open the door of the municipal law enforcement team's office, and indeed found four men taking drugs. Several men tried to escape when they saw the police come to investigate, but were controlled successively by the police. Yesterday afternoon, the reporter interviewed Yang Xiujin, director of the Municipal Landscape Administration Bureau of Xiushan County by phone, and he introduced to the reporter in detail the preliminary handling situation of the persons involved in the case. Upon investigation, three of the four drug-taking men were staff members of the Xiushan Municipal Management Law Enforcement Team, and the other man surnamed Tan was an idle social person. The four men usually have interactions. That night, when the four men gathered and felt "bored", they decided to find a place to take drugs to find "feelings". The four men were "worried" about the safety of the drug-taking place, and then someone proposed that "it would be safer in the urban management law enforcement team", so the four men agreed and hid in the office of the municipal management law enforcement team to take drugs. They thought that no one would know once the door of the law enforcement team was closed, but they were still noticed and reported. Director of the Urban Management Bureau: "private settlement" is purely a rumor Yang Xiujin told the reporter that Zhang Shaodun and the other three people had no criminal records, and their units had not found any abnormal behavior such as drug use before. So far, they have not had major conflicts with small vendors and others during the law enforcement process. He believed that the urban management department paid attention to what should be paid attention to when recruiting, and it was possible that they had just been addicted to drugs recently, so he could hardly accept the accusation that the urban management department "failed to scrutinize personnel". For the online transmission of "the urban management team captain wanted to 'settle privately' with the police that night", Yang Xiujin said that this was purely a rumor. He said that after the incident, he learned that when the police wanted to take Zhang Shaodun and others away from the municipal law enforcement team's office that night, the duty personnel did not know the situation and reported it to Zhou Guofa, captain of the law enforcement team. As the head of the team, Zhou Guofa had the responsibility and obligation to rush to the unit to understand the situation when something happened in the unit, and he did not ask the police to "give a break" after he arrived. Yang Xiujin said that the three urban management officers involved in the case were Zhang Shaodun, Zhang Ke, and Wu Ping (all pseudonyms) of the municipal law enforcement team of the bureau. Among them, Zhang Shaodun was transferred to the unit after retiring from the army and was a formal employee, while Zhang Ke and Wu Ping were recruited not long ago and were temporary workers. Do the three have law enforcement certificates? Yang Xiujin said that Zhang Shaodun was formal and only he had it, and the other two did not have law enforcement certificates and could only cooperate with formal law enforcement officers to patrol and enforce laws on the streets. It took only three minutes to catch the drug takers As soon as there is an accident, it is said to be a temporary worker "The urban management of Xiushan used the secrecy of the office of the law enforcement team to invite idle social persons to gather and take drugs in the office!" On the morning of the 19th, netizens like "Uncle Ma" tipped off on Dayu Network that several drug users had been caught by the Xiushan police. The post said that at more than 10 p.m. on November 18, several employees of the Xiushan urban management team gathered to take drugs in the unit and were reported by those who knew, and the Xiushan Public Security Bureau responded immediately and arrested four drug users. "On the night of the incident, Zhou, captain of the Xiushan urban management team, rushed to the unit after knowing and negotiated with the police, hoping to "settle privately" and calm down the matter, but the police enforced the law impartially, without favoritism, and still apprehended the drug users." It is understood that after this matter was exposed, the leaders of the Chongqing Municipal Commission and the Xiushan County Party Committee and County Government have made instructions successively, believing that this matter reflected serious problems in the Xiushan urban management team, requiring serious investigation and punishment of the persons involved, and rectification of the urban management team throughout the county.
