Wuhan Railway Station "6 Yuan Woman" specifically scams young and middle-aged men 6 Yuan Woman

by goodman on 2009-07-11 13:50:17

Yesterday, the group of "six-yuan women" who had been cheating people for half a year at Wuchang Railway Station were concentratedly rectified and cleaned up by the police.

The other day, a netizen posted online that at the underground exit of Wuchang Railway Station, there was a group of "professional" female beggars who always gave the excuse of "being short of 6 yuan to buy tickets". This netizen hoped that the media would expose this phenomenon of cheating.

This post drew the attention of our newspaper. On July 1st and 2nd, the reporter made undercover visits for two consecutive days, which verified what this netizen had seen and heard. During the undercover visit, an uncle who helped passengers drag their luggage at Wuchang Railway Station revealed that if they begged for 5 yuan, the person being begged would only give them 5 yuan. But begging for 6 yuan was different. Most people being begged would give them 10 yuan or even 20 yuan because they didn't have change, and of course, they wouldn't ask for "change" either. Over time, everyone started calling them the "six-yuan women".
