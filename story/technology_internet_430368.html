1. Buy domains cheaply. Apply for an agent number; generally, it costs around 1000 RMB, and the domain price will be about 60 RMB. For 3-5 domains over 3-5 years, this cost is roughly the same, but full control makes domain transfers, management, and shifting easier.

2. Buy space according to usage—use space proportional to your traffic needs. For example, if you have only 100M of data and 100 IPs, you don't need an entire server. Spending 100-200 RMB annually on a reliable, stable hosting service will suffice.

3. When building a website, try to use free programs. Nowadays, many CMS programs are more stable than those costing 100,000 to 200,000 RMB, and they come with free upgrades. Maintenance, tutorials, and user experience feedback are also more abundant.

4. As a webmaster, use online banking. Different banks have different fees for receiving and sending payments. Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC), Agricultural Bank of China (ABC), Alipay, China Merchants Bank, and Construction Bank each have their own advantages. For instance, Alipay charges nothing for transactions under 10,000 RMB. ABC's mobile banking has no inter-bank fees, ICBC caps at 13-27 RMB, while China Merchants Bank offers the best value for payments between 1000-2500 RMB, capping at just 5 RMB.

5. For international payments, PayPal is commonly used. Yajiao has already enabled PayPal, which also has money-saving tricks—selecting batch payments can reduce costs to 1 USD, saving thousands of dollars previously wasted.

6. New websites might not reach 100 IPs in half a year. Exploring unfamiliar fields can be costly in terms of time and effort. Instead, buy a small website. On Admin5, spending 500 RMB often buys a site with 500 IPs, essentially saving a year's worth of time for 500 RMB.

7. Doing websites involves learning how to communicate and share, which is also a form of savings. Techniques, ideas, and positioning can all save resources. In a webmaster QQ group with 200-500 members, what you don’t know, others do, and vice versa, making it very efficient.

8. There are also some webmaster forums where exchanges can happen, such as Admin5, Chinaz, im286, Weibo circles, Sina and QQ Weibo, as well as entrepreneurial circles like Alibaba and Tianya. These places offer numerous practical examples of savings.

9. Task creation savings—if you lack graphic designers or a technical team, find technical task collaborations. Making a logo costs only 100 RMB, and secondary development might cost a few hundred RMB, much cheaper than hiring or training technicians yourself.

10. In promotion, soft articles are the first step. Writing good soft articles is a very cost-effective promotional method. Soft article writing doesn't require specialized learning; practice, real-world application, information gathering, and connecting ideas create effective soft articles. Promoting subtly provides users with useful information and materials alongside advertisements.

11. For video and large data content, sharing is possible. If large websites can share, then do so. For example, using Youku and Baidu’s data, sharing is a spirit that Baidu and Xunlei have embraced from many webmasters. Conversely, webmasters can also share other websites' data to save bandwidth and effort.
