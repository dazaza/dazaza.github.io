100G space, who can rival me!

by scavin on 2006-02-20 20:33:56

This was done by BlogCN, and all users will be automatically upgraded for free. I uploaded 4.85M of music, and they still think it's not enough... If you can hear the music above~~ You can have 100G of space, just go claim it yourself ~~

It's incredible how generous BlogCN can be, giving 100G to each person, and even refunding the money in full to those who previously purchased paid space, truly impressive...

Just because of this, even if you don't have a blog, you should try it out here.

However, I am quite worried. 100G is at least equivalent to half a hard drive. Such a scale means money... The most important thing is, how long can this space remain stable?

In any case, good things should be shared. This time, you won't have to worry about having no place to put your MP3s, right? If you want to store movies, that's fine too, just don't blame me for calling you boring (a lot of people asked me where to store movies, come on, I could have watched two while you were figuring out where to store them.) Other formats haven't been tested, but if it doesn't work, just change the extension and store it.
