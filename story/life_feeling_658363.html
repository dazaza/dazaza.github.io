Lost but Not Regretful

What is clearly a simple sensation must be overlaid with a bewildering hue; what is naturally an ordinary act of going with the flow must be endowed with a preconceived notion and controversy over who will win. Countless straightforward conversations, when laden with endless emotions, become incomprehensible.

I don't know from what time onward imagination about everything ceased to be as innocent and fantastical as in our youth. When all begins to be touched at the far end of sight, we realize that, unintentionally in time and space, we and those across from us have truly started to struggle for our dreams. Those once flashy and pure things gradually evolve into romance and monotony.

When everything goes its natural course, the heart finally experiences ups and downs akin to those in novels. Thus, in my most cherished words, layer upon layer of nothingness and splendor emerges. Countless sentences, which cannot withstand scrutiny and form under my fingertips, are accompanied by boundless imagination in my mind, romantic to the extreme.

Thus, I've asked myself more than once: when pursuing everything with tenacity, does the emotion in my heart resemble the nothingness and vanity in the text? However, such a question is so hard to evaluate. Apart from the impression of persistence growing day by day without change, everything else is blurry.

Finally, it's time for me to bear the responsibility of my own growth. The future is full of difficulties, yet ironically bright. But my vision of the future is too sunny, clear blue skies stretching endlessly, and precisely because of this, there's nothing in my line of sight.

Again and again, I question myself: on this capricious spirit, how much of the so-called steadfastness can still hold up? Looking at the dust rolling behind, a great deal of besieged feelings suddenly rise, with gaps and loopholes everywhere. Ridiculously, apart from the struggle before me, I am utterly powerless.

Perhaps, all futures are like this - bright yet essentially empty. Therefore, so many beautiful and ordinary persistences stand out, bringing countless surprises and joys. Easter eggs don't necessarily need to be placed right in front of you; sometimes, you need to dig them up yourself.

The most comforting thing is that some moods finally have their rightful attachments. Even if the sea being observed remains calm, at least there is a scenery one likes. Regardless of whether it belongs to oneself in the future, it has become a place where one willingly stays - a field of hope.

What should I say to myself? It's not lamentation, not repentance, not encouragement, but perhaps the most perfect praise in the world. Although I've achieved nothing, my pursuit surpasses everyone's fantasy. My pursuit and persistence are something at least half of the world's population can only look up to.

Fortunately, not every loss turns into regret. More often, it encourages us to continue searching forward.

People of the future, events of the future, everything of the future, wait for me. I will eventually come rushing through the dust.
