There was a girl named Xi, whose father had passed away before she was even born. She lived with her mother, grandmother, and stepfather, but neither her grandmother nor stepfather liked her. So when she started high school, her mother sent her to a private school. The private school was full of children from wealthy families, and among these rich kids, Xi stood out as an oddity. Her monthly allowance was only 123.5 yuan, just enough for her to eat every month. Therefore, she would always wait until her classmates had finished eating before hiding in a corner to eat steamed buns and pickled vegetables.

However, the girl's strong-willed character made her excel academically; she always ranked first in all grade-level exams. Many parents of wealthy students hoped she could tutor their children, but she refused them all because she didn't like the attitude of the rich kids. Not long after, a boy named Gong from her class asked Xi, "Can you help me with my studies?" Xi smirked disdainfully and said, "Why should I tutor you?" Gong confidently replied, "Because my grades are bad!" This response made Xi take another look at him, and that one glance made her realize that Gong was different from other rich kids. He always wore an old overcoat and lacked the arrogance common among nobles. Thus, she agreed to tutor him. Each day, Xi tutored Gong, which also increased her income. Gradually, they became familiar with each other. When they learned that Gong's birthday was on December 31st, the last day of the year, and Xi's birthday was on January 1st, the first day of the year, Gong joked, "I'm a whole year older than you!" Xi coldly retorted, "Just by one day!"

One day after school, a BMW was parked at the school gate, a car many rich kids hadn't seen before. It was Gong's family coming to pick him up. As Xi watched Gong walk toward the car, she thought, "Why can they be so rich? Why can they drive a BMW?" At that moment, Gong turned around and gave her a faint smile before getting into the car. Realizing this, Xi thought, "Why should I envy him? He's not better at studying than I am!"

Days went by, and despite daily tutoring from Xi, Gong's grades showed no significant improvement as the college entrance exam approached. They both focused on their own comprehensive reviews and stopped tutoring sessions. Xi was determined to get into Tsinghua University because she didn't want others to look down on her. When the exam results came out, Xi was admitted to Tsinghua University as expected, surpassing the second-best student in the entire school by a margin of 50 points.

Since the university term hadn't started yet, Xi wanted to go home and see her mother, whom she hadn't seen for many years. However, upon entering her home, her stepfather beat and scolded her, and her mother shielded her. In desperation, Xi left home. As she walked downstairs and looked up at her balcony, she hoped to catch one last glimpse of her mother before leaving, but instead, her grandmother threw all her luggage down. Heartbroken, Xi left home and returned to Beijing.

Back in Beijing, Xi prepared to find a place to live. Just then, she saw an old grandma cleaning out a storeroom. She approached and asked, "Grandma, is this house for rent?" The old grandma smiled and said, "Young lady, do you want to rent a house?" Xi nodded, and the grandma responded, "Then it's 100 yuan!" While Xi was tidying up the house, she encountered Gong, whom she hadn't seen for a long time. Unwilling to let others see her pitiful state, she angrily asked, "Why are you here?" Gong, dressed casually as usual, said strangely, "I'm visiting my aunt!" Feeling exposed, Xi remained silent. Gong asked, "I heard you got into Tsinghua, congratulations!" Xi, not being someone who didn't know how to appreciate, said, "Thank you! What about you?" Gong unhappily replied, "My dad wants me to study abroad!"

Gong then said to Xi, "Would you mind doing me a favor?" Xi curiously asked, "What is it?" Gong said, "Would you like to work as a typist at my mom's company?" Xi immediately got angry and said, "Are you feeling sorry for me?" Gong hurriedly explained, "No, my mom's company isn't doing well recently, and many employees have left. We need people now. Consider it as helping me, okay?" Xi agreed and started working at Gong's mom's office every day, earning 600 yuan per month, which she found very satisfying. As Gong was about to leave for abroad, Xi invited him for dinner before he went.

After Gong left, Xi continued to balance her studies and work. By now, her monthly salary had reached 2000 yuan, and Gong's mom, considering it inconvenient for her to travel back and forth, provided her with a laptop. To others, it seemed as if she were wealthy, but Xi neither admitted nor denied it.

In Xi's dormitory, there was a Beijing girl who was quite spoiled but never bullied Xi. Xi remained as competitive as ever and couldn't stand anyone excelling her academically, but this time she met her match. A boy named Lin consistently scored higher than her, making Xi feel determined to surpass him. During a review class, Xi thought, "Next exam, I must beat him!" Suddenly, the teacher asked a question and called on Xi to answer, but since she wasn't paying attention, she shook her head. The teacher then asked, "Is anyone able to solve this problem?" ... Lin voluntarily stood up and answered the question, receiving praise. Xi thought bitterly, "Do you think I don't know? Do you have to tell me?" After class, Xi intercepted Lin and said, "Next exam, I will definitely surpass you!" The classmates present were surprised. When the results came out, Xi indeed took first place. As she searched the score sheet for Lin's name, she discovered his scores were all zero. Furious, she thought, "If you don't want to compete with me, just say so!" Her roommate informed her that Lin was waiting for her by the lake in the campus. Fuming, she ran over and said, "What do you mean? Is this some kind of joke?" Lin didn't say anything but handed her the test paper. Confused, Xi asked, "How did you get the test paper?" Lin said, "My aunt works in the academic affairs office..." Before he could finish, Xi interrupted, "You rich people are all the same! No wonder your scores are always so high!" Calmly, Lin explained, "But I've never used such connections. I've already completed the test, you can add up the scores!" Xi calculated the total score, and it was still higher than hers, leaving her perplexed... Suddenly, Lin hugged her and said, "I like you!" Being held by a boy, all her pride disappeared, and thus they began dating. Soon, they became intimate. Every time they went out, Xi would give Lin money because men paying when going out together always boosts their face. Over time, she would regularly give Lin pocket money, which became a normal occurrence between them.

Xi's birthday was approaching, and she fantasized about what gift Lin would give her, thinking, "The pocket money I've given him is enough to buy me a necklace or a diamond ring." The thought made her happy!

On the day of Xi's birthday, Lin didn't contact her all day. She assumed he was doing it intentionally to give her a surprise. Then the phone rang, and Xi picked it up saying, "Where have you been?" Surprisingly, the voice on the other end said, "Ah?" She asked, "Who are you?" "I'm Gong!" Annoyed, she said, "Why is it you?" Calmly, Gong said, "Happy birthday!" "Thanks! Nothing else, I'll hang up!" Gong said, "Alright then! Bye!" Gong didn't wait for Xi's reply but only heard the beeping sound... The phone rang again, this time it was Lin. After answering, Xi hurriedly ran downstairs, but Lin looked very unhappy. Thinking he might be preparing a surprise for her, Xi hesitated but still agreed to accompany him. Arriving at the hotel, Xi realized that sadness and pain cannot be faked. Lin told her a story she least wanted to hear: Lin originally had a girlfriend named Rui, who attended a film academy. He hoped his girlfriend would be different from other girls, hoping she wouldn't be tainted by worldly influences. But when he went to find Rui, she was with a wealthy man, speaking words that belittled him. Lin, however, was not ready to give up. That morning, Lin used his accumulated pocket money to buy Rui a diamond ring, but Rui threw it back at him. Xi held the diamond ring in her hand, seeing the word "Rui" engraved on it. At that moment, Xi deeply resented herself, regretting why she had worked so hard in her studies? Why did she learn to read? If she couldn't read, she might have thought the word was her name. But Xi held back her tears and consoled Lin, saying, "It's okay, it's alright!" Soon, Xi stopped working at Gong's mom's company. Since the marketing management she studied was exactly what Gong's dad's company lacked, she started working at Gong's dad's company. Graduation was near, and everyone would soon part ways, so Xi and Lin broke up.

Xi followed Gong's dad to Shenzhen for development. After several years of effort, Xi had shown remarkable performance. Through various connections, she was now the manager of the British headquarters' branch in China. She bought a house and a car for herself, just to prove that others have things she would also possess, it was just a matter of time.

Gong returned from abroad and invited his family, including Xi, to dine together. Xi noticed that Gong hadn't changed much from before, still somewhat disheveled and lacking ambition. After the meal, Xi asked, "Where are you going?" Gong replied, "To Bai Swan!" Xi sarcastically said, "Rich people always stay in those star-rated hotels." Gong didn't respond to Xi's comment but instead asked her, "Can you come with me?" "Why?" Gong pleaded, "Please, come with me, alright?" After pondering, Xi agreed to accompany him upstairs. As soon as they entered the room, Gong said, "Close your eyes! Countdown from five!" When Xi opened her eyes, many clowns jumped out from the corner of the room playing music and singing songs for her. Then, Gong pushed a five-tiered big cake to Xi's front and said, "Happy Birthday!" Xi was deeply moved because she hadn't celebrated her birthday for many years. Holding the diamond ring in his hand, Gong knelt down on one knee seriously and said to Xi, "Will you marry me?" Xi said, "Give me a reason!" "I love you!" Gong replied. Xi nodded in agreement, though she herself didn't know whether it was right or wrong because she still couldn't forget Lin, but Gong's parents were also very good to her. Soon, Gong and Xi got married.

After marriage, Xi remained as busy with work as before, while Gong stayed at home feeding fish, tending flowers, and surfing the internet, which Xi found very annoying. Once, Xi indignantly said, "Why do I have to hustle outside all day while you idle at home all day?" Gong chuckled and said, "Or we both stay at home! Anyway, the money my parents earned is enough for us to spend our whole life!" Upon hearing this, Xi angrily shouted, "Why should we spend your parents' money? Aren't we capable of earning it ourselves?" Seeing his wife angry, Gong quickly laughed and begged to placate Xi. This happened every time. One day, a strange man added her on QQ, and she vaguely felt that the man was Lin, but she still added him. It turned out to be Lin, and they chatted for a long time. Xi learned that Lin was a manager in a company in Northwest China, and she felt her husband was too unambitious. After returning home, Xi saw a fish floating belly-up in the aquarium and scooped it out and threw it away. When Gong returned home and found one less fish, he asked his wife, "Where's my 'Second Concubine'?" Xi coldly said, "I saw it floating belly-up and threw it away." "Not really, it rests like this every day!" After hearing this, Xi became furious, "You just stay at home all day, unlike others!" Gong expressionlessly asked, "Are you referring to him?" Xi slapped Gong hard across the face, and without saying a word, Gong quietly closed the door and left home. It had been two days, and Gong hadn't returned home. Xi knew she had gone too far, but she was embarrassed to call him actively. Suddenly, she thought of Gong's diary, which he had never let her read. Now, she could take a look.

Opening the diary, the first page read: "Year X Month X Day, there is a girl in our class who is different from others. After the classmates have eaten, she always hides in a corner to eat steamed buns and pickled vegetables. I really want to help her, but she seems to dislike children from wealthy families. I need to think of a good way!"

"Today, I asked my dad to get me an old overcoat. I think this way it will be easier to approach her, and she won't dislike me. She has decided to tutor me, so to spend more time with her, I deliberately perform poorly on exams. Although she calls me stupid every time, I am still very happy. But I can't explain this to my parents."

"My birthday has arrived, and my dad drove to pick me up. I'm a bit unhappy because I'm afraid she will distance herself from me. Tomorrow is her birthday, I wish I could invite her to get in the car with me, but I know she definitely won't agree, so I can only give her a helpless smile. After getting in the car, I took off my old overcoat and changed into new clothes. My dad even asked if I had any mental problems for loving this old overcoat."

"I think I've fallen in love with her!"

"My parents know I like her, but my dad said if I get into Tsinghua University, he will allow us to be together. So, I started to study hard, but she is so competitive, I can't surpass her scores. Therefore, I deliberately left a few questions unanswered in each subject, resulting in me scoring 50 points less than her and becoming the second-best student in the entire school. But my dad wants me to study abroad, and if I refuse, he will reveal the truth about how I helped her, so I agreed!"

"Before going abroad, I met her. I invited her to work as a typist at my mom's company. I was afraid she would know I intentionally helped her, so I discussed with my mom and said the company was having financial difficulties. Before I left, she said she would treat me to dinner. I said I wanted to eat beef noodles, but she disagreed, so I lied and said I loved eating beef noodles because I knew even if we both ate, it would only cost 3 yuan, leaving her with 597 yuan of pocket money for the month. After eating, she returned to school. Not far from there, I threw up because I had never eaten such terrible food."

"It's so coincidental! There is a girl in her dormitory who was my junior high school classmate. I told my classmate not to bully her, otherwise, I wouldn't be nice!"

"From classmates, I learned that she has a boyfriend now. I want to ask her how she's doing recently, but I can't find a reason to call her!"

"On her birthday today, I finally found a reason to call her, but her voice sounded urgent, as if she was waiting for someone, so I hung up quickly. Classmates told me she didn't return to the dormitory tonight, and I'm so scared. I can't imagine what might happen! My heart is so troubled!"

"While abroad, I worked hard, but I couldn't tell her about my achievements, fearing she might ignore me because of it. After striving hard, I finally managed to establish a branch of my company in China, and the general manager is her. Naturally, I feel reassured! I'm about to return to the country, but I can't tell her about my efforts over the years. Regardless of whether she has a boyfriend now, I will propose to her."

"I proposed to her, and she accepted me!"

"On the wedding night, I deliberately cut my finger to stain the bedsheet red. Even though we both know she's not a virgin anymore, in my heart, she will always be the person I cherish."

"After marriage, she continues to be busy with work every day, while I can't bear to be apart from her even for a day, so I only communicate with the deputy manager of the headquarters via computer every day. Even though she thinks I'm useless, as long as it doesn't hurt her, it's better than anything."

"I feel she's contacting him again. I'm so afraid I'll lose her, because I can't live without her now. I want to spend the rest of my life with her."

"Wife, I really don't want to lose you, because I can't live without you!"

By now, tears streamed down Xi's face. The man beside her had gone to such great lengths to help her, sacrificing so much! She no longer cared about saving face and called Gong, saying, "Husband, I know I was wrong, please come back!" "Give me a reason!" Gong asked. "I love you!"
