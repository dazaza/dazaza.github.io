She is no ordinary woman.

by bantaozap on 2011-09-19 18:41:56

They borrowed a friend's warehouse, which they briefly tidied up and used as a bedroom. The freezing cold warehouse was like an ice cellar, without a warm quilt to cover her body, she often woke up frozen in the middle of the night. He tightly held her, trying to bring her close to his chest, using his own body heat to warm her.

A few years later, he gradually improved, became wealthy, started his own company, and soon bought a house and a car. They bid farewell to their days of hunger and cold. The interior decoration of their home was extremely exquisite; the floor tiles and wallpaper were imported, even the faucets were of the highest grade. He wanted to give her the warmest home possible. Living in such an environment, she felt some hesitation. When they moved, all the things from the old warehouse were discarded, but she insisted on keeping that quilt, which they had been using for years and was already torn in several places. He said, "Throw it away, let's buy a new one." Everything around them was elegant, and this quilt would be out of place. She said, "No, this quilt has accompanied us through many cold winters, covering us with warmth." He shook his head and no longer insisted.

Once, he went to the bathroom and forgot to turn off his laptop. She accidentally discovered that he had kept a personal blog, updating it every day. In one of his diary entries, he wrote: "That day, she came in from outside, her pale face scared me. To earn enough money to buy a quilt, she actually handed out flyers. That evening, we slept under a new quilt, so warm, she had never slept so soundly. By accident, I found a red swelling on her hand, pierced by a needle. Handing out flyers was actually her gentle lie; she had gone to sell blood! This quilt was actually bought with her blood! Her body was so frail! That night, I cried silently all night long, I swore to make something of myself, to give her happiness. After these few years of effort, I have finally succeeded. Yesterday, I also went to the blood station, asking them to draw my blood, I just wanted to feel the tiny needle prick into the vein, that icy pain caught me off guard, but it was also so happy. I took the money and bought this quilt."

One day, he came back from outside, holding a new quilt, demanding that she throw away the old one and replace it with the new one. Having no choice, she complied. From then on, the old one was replaced by the new one. Every night, she didn't sleep as comfortably as usual, a streak of pain passing through her heart, often crying silently in the dead of night, unknowingly wetting her pillow. She said in her heart: "Do you know how much effort it took to buy this quilt? That day, I didn't go out handing out flyers, I went to sell blood! The first time I sold blood was to buy a quilt! How important this quilt is to me! And you, threw it away like trash." She felt that he wasn't as loving as before, although they were covering themselves with a new quilt, it lacked the warmth of the past.

She was not an ordinary woman; on the contrary, she was quite excellent, with many admirers. But she turned them all down, resolutely choosing him. At the time, he was penniless, working in a factory, his income insufficient to handle both their hunger and cold. For him, she lost her family and her job.

One day, she returned from outside, looking dazed, her face pale. He asked, "What's wrong? Are you sick?" She said, "Nothing, just a bit tired." Then she immediately happily pulled out a red bill from her pocket, waving it in front of him, excitedly saying, "We have money now, let's go buy a warm quilt." Where did the money come from? Earned. He asked, "How did you earn it?" So easily. Handing out advertisements, one by one, standing from morning until now, earning 100 yuan as a tip. They went to the street to buy a quilt, unable to choose carefully, buying according to the price of 100 yuan. From then on, the cold winter days had a quilt, she no longer woke up frozen in the middle of the night.

Her eyes were already blurred, originally, his heart was as detailed as his love for her. In the cold winter, he gave her a warm quilt, bringing along the entire warm spring.
