Product Advantages: Bamboo Leaf Green liquor is a uniquely characteristic nutritional health wine in China with over 1700 years of history. As evidenced by the famous lines from poet Xu Xin during the Northern Zhou Dynasty, “Three springs of bamboo leaf wine, one tune of Kun chicken strings,” Bamboo Leaf Green wine was already renowned at that time. Emperor Xiao of Liang praised Bamboo Leaf Green with the line "Lan food offerings, bamboo wine clear fragrance." After the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Fenjiu Group closely collaborated with renowned medical institutes and universities, based on the secret recipe provided by the great physician Fu Shan from the late Ming to early Qing dynasties, and using principles of food nutrition and Chinese herbal medicine, added many health functions while preserving traditional styles. In 1998, Bamboo Leaf Green received the "Health Food Approval Certificate" from the National Health Department, becoming the only health wine among national famous wines. This wine is made from aged high-quality Fenjiu as the base, adding low-polyfructose, light bamboo leaves, tangerine peel, wood incense, sandalwood, cardamom, mountain pepper, and more than ten other precious Chinese medicinal herbs as effective components. The liquid is golden emerald green, fragrant, thick, and refreshing. Animal function tests and human trials have proven that this wine promotes the proliferation of intestinal bifidobacteria and improves the intestinal flora, offering health benefits. Moderate long-term consumption can improve intestinal function and enhance human immunity.

Honors Achieved: Bamboo Leaf Green wine is one of the leading brands of Chinese white spirits exported overseas, popular in Southeast Asia and Europe and America, recognized worldwide as an international brand. Recommended by the Chinese Geriatric Health Medical Research Society and related well-known professors, it was awarded the Golden Elephant Award for International Green Health Excellence at the Third International Medical Development Conference in August 2003 in Bangkok, Thailand.
