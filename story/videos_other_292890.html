(5) Before you kiss me —— The rich heir falls in love with me, a young widow

I think, apart from being honest, there's another reason why Manager Liang wouldn't fire me: we both know full well that the real author behind any of her plans is me.

As the head of the planning department, Liang Lan is widely recognized as a "talented woman," with outstanding and fresh writing and planning skills. But privately, I understand that Manager Liang's strength lies in her eloquent oratory and socializing abilities. When it comes down to actual work, she probably can't even match an ordinary employee.

All the writing is done by me.

Every time we receive a planning task, she hands it over to me first, letting me write it up, then she "reviews" it. All she needs to do is correct a few typos and adjust the order of paragraphs in the document, and all the credit becomes hers.

Every time she receives praise from our superiors, I see her accept everything with a straight face, and secretly, I've complained about it. However, Liang Lan gives me some bonus money appropriately, which makes all my resentment disappear.

I'm not an ambitious woman. My entire dream in life is to have a stable income, not too much work pressure, so I can focus on raising Su Chang and slowly find a suitable partner for myself.

I am a woman, just like other women. I believe that a woman's entire value lies in marrying a good man. I've already lost one marriage under mysterious circumstances, and this time, I absolutely cannot be careless. If I find the ideal man who can support me, what does this job matter?

The plans I wrote for Manager Liang made her famous in the advertising industry, but I remain an unknown little employee. Manager Liang has never praised or commended me, so in the eyes of all my colleagues, I am someone who is dispensable, and also a "flatterer."

Sometimes, when I look at my diploma from a prestigious university in advertising, I feel like I'm dreaming. Is the Kang Nan who graduated with honors and the woman now crushed by the weight of life the same person?
