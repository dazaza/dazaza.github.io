The Hottest Set of Jordan Footwear for Basketball

Webster’s Dictionary defines the word well-liked as, "commonly liked or approved." Jordan shoe fanatics would illustrate the environment around Jordan in a similar way. Essentially, every single edition in the Jordans signature sneaker line could be described as well-known. However, when it comes to pinpointing the most well-liked Jordan shoe ever made, there is an obvious standout winner. The Jordan 11 stands head and shoulders above all the other shoes that comprise the most desired line of basketball sneakers in the history of sneakers.

The legendary Jordan 11 was first introduced during the 1995-96 NBA regular season. This shoe became the most famous and widely recognized design of the Air Jordan line. Michael Jordan wore the Jordan 11 during this season, the year he returned from retirement for the first time. After attempting a career in professional baseball, Michael came back to the NBA and quickly re-established himself as the dominating force he had been before retiring. He led the Bulls to their fourth NBA Finals Championship, was named the Most Valuable Player for the NBA regular season, All-Star game, and NBA Finals. He averaged 30.4 points per game, all while wearing the iconic Jordan 11.

Perhaps what makes the Jordan 11 so popular is the outstanding silhouette established during the design process. The man responsible for this superior silhouette was the legendary Nike shoe designer Tinker Hatfield. This shoe not only marked the return of Michael but also the return of Hatfield. Hatfield had been creating the world's favorite Jordan shoes starting with the Jordan 3. For the Jordan 11, Hatfield added patent leather to the upper part of the shoe and placed a clear rubber sole on the outsole, completing what many consider the best Jordan shoe ever designed.

On December 23, 2011, the most popular colorway of the most favored Jordan shoe will make a triumphant return to the sneaker world. The Jordan 11 "Concord" might be the most beloved edition of the most epic shoe ever worn by MJ. This predominantly White shoe with Black patent leather accents and a clear, translucent sole will be available for purchase and marks the third year in a row that the Jordan 11 Retro will launch during the holiday season.

If there is still any doubt about which is the most popular set of Jordan sneakers for basketball, keep this in mind. In 2000-2001, when the Jordan 11s were retroed in 15 different colorways, they sold more units than any other Jordan shoe in the shortest amount of time. The upcoming Jordan 11 "Concord" is expected to sell out within hours. This shoe is widely liked and approved by sneaker enthusiasts. Simply put, it's the most popular.
