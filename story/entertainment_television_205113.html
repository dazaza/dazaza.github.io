Drama Introduction:

Sisters fight for husband, becoming enemies

More than thirty years ago, pastry chef Gan Tai Zu met the boss's daughter Zhong Xiao He at a bakery workshop. The two hit it off and had already recognized each other as their life partners. At that time, Yin Hong and her husband had just smuggled themselves into Hong Kong. The two were in dire straits. Through the introduction of fellow townspeople at the workshop, He extended a helping hand, allowing them to work at the workshop and treating Hong like a close sister.

Later, He and Zu got married and had Gan Yong Jia, Gan Yong Hao, Gan Yong Yuan, Gan Yong Qing, Gan Yong Zhong, and an adopted daughter Sun Hao Yue. In addition, after her good sister Hong divorced her husband, she independently raised her daughter Yu Su Xin, naming them Jia, Hao, Yue, Yuan, Qing, Zhong, Qiu, symbolizing a complete and warm family, gathering together every Mid-Autumn Festival.
