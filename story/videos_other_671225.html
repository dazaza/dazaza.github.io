【It's good now, and I can still take care of you】 In the prime of their youth, they met, fell in love, got married, and planned to have a baby. However, she was diagnosed with leukemia after suffering from continuous high fever. After chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and bone marrow transplantation, her condition was finally under control. Her pelvis had already necrosed, and even if there was no recurrence after five years, she could only live by wheelchair. During this period, her parents forced him to divorce, but he moved to the hospital directly. He only said to her: "It's good now, I am still working, and I can still take care of you."

【Six years, I've been waiting for this one sentence from you】 A strong woman didn't say a good word to her husband in six years of marriage. One day, she couldn't help asking her husband: "Why don't you fight back? It makes me feel uncomfortable inside." The husband smiled and said: "For six years, I've just been waiting for this one sentence from you." The wife cried, as all the scenes of the past six years came flooding back into her mind, tears streaming down her face. From then on, this beautiful world gained a good wife.

【The Streetlamp Beside the Road】 She accompanied him through the hardest times of his entrepreneurial journey. After his success, he unexpectedly fell in love with someone else. She didn't say anything; on the night he broke up with her, she simply pointed at the streetlamp. Later, after his investment failed and his company went bankrupt, on the night he was drunk, he looked up unintentionally and suddenly burst into tears. He saw the streetlamp beside the road and finally understood her feelings: the streetlamp, using all its strength to accompany you through the darkness, is forced to exit your world when light comes.

【I spent my youth loving you secretly, but we cannot be together】 She liked him for four years, collecting everything about him, enjoying the way he bowed his head, feeling angry when she saw him with other girls, and writing countless love letters. But all of this, he did not know. Several years later, they were no longer in the same city. When she bought flowers online before her wedding, she received a gift from him - an earring she had accidentally lost once. On the note: "I spent my youth loving you secretly, but we cannot be together."

【"I'm back" like ten years ago】 She walked alone on the bustling noisy street. She looked at the candy shop where he had fed her cotton candy by hand; she looked at the banyan tree where he had hugged her under it and let her cry and vent; she stared at a bridal shop where he had said he would make her wear that crimson wedding dress... Unconsciously, she arrived at the end of the street. Suddenly, he gently embraced her from behind. "I'm back," like ten years ago when they were 17, he said: "I will come back."

【I traversed the seasons, just to melt in your embrace】 He was a sick child, and no one was willing to play with him. Until the first snowfall, he built a small snowman. He asked: "Can I hug you?" The snowman countered: "Why?" He said: "Because I like you." The snowman silently threw itself into his arms. The next second, he heard the snowman whisper softly in his ear: "I traversed the seasons, just to melt in your embrace, thank you for daring to love me."

【He learned how to resolve singlehood, and she finally learned how to leave him...】 He grew up with her. He learned the guitar, and she learned to listen quietly. He learned to fight, and she learned to bandage. He learned to stay out all night at bars, and she learned to find him at fixed points. He learned to flaunt himself around class beauties, and she learned to secretly send flowers for him. He learned to work overtime without returning home, and she learned to make late-night snacks but lied that it was takeout. He learned the waltz steps, and she learned to watch him from afar at parties. He learned how to resolve singlehood, and she finally learned how to leave him...

【I mean it: "I like you"] At the graduation ceremony, he suddenly leaned close to her ear and whispered softly: "I like you." She laughed: "Hey, teasing me for three years is enough, isn't it?" For three years, she hid her feelings while facing the repeated flirtations of her secret admirer, and she was really exhausted. "I mean it!" He saw that she didn't believe him and said seriously again. "You always mean it." He lowered his head: "In fact, every time was true."

【The deepest three words are 'School's out'】 On that twilight when grandma passed away, grandpa accompanied her through the last part of her life in the ward. Before grandma passed away, she said to grandpa: 'School's out.' Grandpa, who had been pretending to be calm, burst into tears like a child upon hearing these words. After the funeral, I asked grandpa about the meaning of these three words. Grandpa told me that these were words grandma used to say when they were still in elementary school: 'School's out, let's go home together.'

【Only a dime, but it's 'perfect' already] When they first met, the first gift he gave her was a very expensive LV bag; during their passionate romance, the gift he gave her was a luxurious villa; at their engagement, his business had failed, and he was penniless, unable to give any gifts. Touching the few dimes in her hand, he said: "Sorry! I can't make you happy anymore." She shook her head and gently pried open his big hand: "Although it's only a dime, it's 'perfect' already."

【She always said he wasn't romantic】 She always said he wasn't romantic, so she ordered flowers for her birthday. After breaking up, she sat silently in the house, hoping he would call her. After waiting for a long time, she picked up the phone and realized her mobile phone had automatically turned off; wanting a drink, she found the kettle was empty; wanting to go out for a walk, she didn't know where the keys were. She sat down and realized she had lost love.

【Who will give you happiness?】 He encountered an air crash, and out of more than a hundred people on board, he was the only survivor. When the rescue team found him, the doctors repeatedly said it was a miracle that he could survive with such serious injuries. Later, she asked him how he managed to hold on. He smiled: "At that time, I kept thinking, if I'm not here, who will give you happiness?"

【Everything is fleeting clouds, as long as we love each other】 The family discussed their thoughts after becoming wealthy at the dinner table. Dad laughed and said: "When we have money later, I'll change your mom." Mom didn't think much of it. Dad became serious and said: "No, I won't." Just because of the two yuan when they were poor, mom accompanied him for several days. I will never betray her in this lifetime. Mom looked at dad and smiled happily. Happiness is the simple mutual support in everyday life.

["Happy Valentine's Day"] For three consecutive Valentine's Days, he would receive a blessing message from the same unknown number, with only five short words: "Happy Valentine's Day." He never replied. In the fourth year, on Valentine's Day, that message didn't appear. After hesitating for a long time, he finally sent a "Happy Valentine's Day" to that number. Quickly, there was a reply: "Thank you, who are you?" Love won't wait for anyone, and in a blink, it was taken away by time.

He liked a girl, and everything about her fascinated him. But he was troubled by how to meet her. It took him a month to figure out the girl's living habits and created countless chance encounters. One day, the girl finally noticed him. "Hey, what a coincidence, I've met you many times." "Yes, what a coincidence."

[The most beautiful encounter is a coincidental meeting]

[A man is both dark and ugly] The man is dark and ugly, and when he smiles, he has a mouthful of yellow teeth. This marriage proposal. She absolutely disagrees, but her parents have decided it anyway. Right after getting married, she couldn't stand it; he snores when sleeping and doesn't wash his feet. She couldn't sleep, sitting up all night on the bed, wondering how she could endure this life? The next evening, he brought a basin of hot water and washed his feet in the way she did. Then, he got into bed but didn't sleep. "Why are you still awake?" "I snore, you sleep first."

[Romantic Waiting and Persistence] Back then, she was young, soft, and fond of dreaming. Bus stops, cherry blossom roads, garden corners, libraries, even her breath carried his shadow. Back then, he was also young, sharp, humorous, and calculating. Cherry blossom roads, school gates, garden stairs, turns, he could find her everywhere.
