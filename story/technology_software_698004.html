Spanish well-known mattress brand Pikolin expands into the Chinese market

by seo17519 on 2012-03-08 22:54:38

Professional youth and children's mattresses have long been a void in the Chinese market. In September 2010, the world's first mattress designed to assist in the healthy development of the spines of young people and children - Spain’s ECUS Youth and Children's Mattress (hereinafter referred to as "ECUS") - officially entered the Chinese market. In just over a year, the ECUS brand has rapidly developed in the Chinese market like a prairie fire, with nearly a hundred specialty stores making perfect appearances. Its good brand image, professional quality, and excellent service have gained market approval and consumer favor.

The ECUS brand is a well-known mattress brand in Spain that has consistently been dedicated to the cause of human sleep health, focusing on scientifically aiding the spinal growth of children aged 0-18 years old and effectively inhibiting kyphosis. The brand has decades of research, development, and production experience in medical equipment and hospital beds, along with technical advantages from cooperation with numerous authoritative medical institutions.

From childhood to adolescence, a child's height, weight, and body shape curve changes rapidly, with the spine being at the core of this growth. Spinal growth can be scientifically divided into four stages: 1-7 years, 8-10 years, 11-13 years, and 14-18 years. During these different age stages, the spine has varying demands on the mattress. Therefore, it requires choosing a mattress that can effectively support the S-shaped spinal development during different ages and heights of the child. However, parents cannot replace the mattress for their child every two or three years. In response to this need, ECUS proposes a solution, which is also one of its important patents (the ECUS mattress brand has obtained 38 patents). This patent involves adjusting the density distribution of internal materials and the method of use on a single mattress, allowing it to be used by people aged 1-18.

Relying on decades of research, production, and long-term usage certification in youth and children's mattresses, ECUS has an absolute professional advantage. It is the sole designated supplier of youth and children's mattresses for the Spanish Royal Family and the Madrid Royal Hospital in Spain, demonstrating extremely high brand reputation.

Relevant background of ECUS:

50 years of European medical technology heritage, clinical certification from 350,000 young people and children in Europe and America, eight years of in-depth research on the growth and development of Asian youth and children, and finally, a mattress that can bear the future of Chinese children has been born.

"ECUS" was born in Valencia, Spain, in 1945. It is a world-renowned brand specializing in the production and sales of high-end medical beds and high-quality medical mattresses. With decades of R&D in the field of medical beds, it has been highly regarded by many world-famous medical institutions. In 1978, "ECUS" began to enter the field of production and sales of high-tech civilian health mattresses. After continuous market promotion and increasing public recognition, "ECUS" mattresses have now become a famous civilian high-end furniture brand in Europe and around the world.

In 1995, "ECUS" built Spain's first solar-powered mattress production factory in Yecla, equipped with the world's most advanced automated production equipment, maximizing the application of artificial intelligence, and truly achieving fully automated unmanned intelligent production line operations.

Currently, "ECUS" is popular in 47 countries worldwide, including Europe, North America, Asia, and South America.

Now, international furniture brands are trending towards Asia, especially focusing on China. After nearly eight years of research in China, in 2010, ECUS brought the "world's first mattress to assist in the healthy development of the spines of young people and children" to China, aiming to bring revolutionary change to the poor sleeping habits of Chinese youth and children, and to bring blessings for the healthy growth of the spines of 290 million Chinese youth and children!
