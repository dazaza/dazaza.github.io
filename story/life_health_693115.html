In recent years, it has been successfully used in the treatment of gastric stones. Two days before treatment, liquid food should be taken. The patient is instructed to drink 500 ml of water to make the stomach full, then lie prone for ultrasound positioning. A voltage of 12KV is discharged 80 times per minute, and the stones are usually broken into pieces.

2. Manual lithotripsy: For asymptomatic or non-complicated gastric stone patients, external abdominal massage can be tried, such as persimmon stones or hawthorn gastric stones. This makes the gastric mass break into small pieces, then perform gastric lavage or give a laxative to accelerate the discharge of the mass.
