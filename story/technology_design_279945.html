My car washing adventure - Opalijie mobile car washing

by bgvftybj4u on 2009-11-27 10:11:21

My wife always said that it would definitely rain if I washed the car. At first, I didn't believe it, but later I found out it was true. Yesterday, before I went to wash the car, in order to refute her superstitious view, I specially checked the weather forecast online before washing. The forecast said there would be no rain for three days. After work, I happily went to the car wash, cleaned the car spotlessly, and then went home to cook and eat. My wife said that if it didn't rain today, it would definitely rain tomorrow. I sneered at her, saying this time you lost, I've checked the weather forecast! After dinner, she watched TV while I turned on the computer to fiddle with photos. Around eight o'clock, I heard thunder outside. My wife said: see? It's raining again! I said, what do you mean "again"? Oupailijie cleans the car at your door, thunder doesn't necessarily mean rain, just thunder is quite common! The weather forecast has already reported no rain. She said, aren't you going to argue anymore? Why don't you go open the window and take a good look? Alas, it was indeed drizzling outside. It's strange, my luck is really bad, is the sky related to me?! When I got to the unit and told my colleagues, he said you're not unlucky, Lao Wang is even more unlucky than you. Lao Wang is the maintenance supervisor of our hotel. His home is in a nearby village with several acres of land. Whether he sprays pesticides or waters the crops, it will rain, especially when he sprays pesticides, it's always accurate. According to their manager, after the Spring Festival this year, it hasn't rained due to drought, so he judged that everything would be fine this time, so he decided to water the spring wheat. His colleagues tried to persuade him but he wouldn't listen. The day after watering, several managers ran to the engineering department and asked: Lao Wang, did you water your field yesterday? Because it rained again today. Lao Wang didn't answer, feeling depressed.
