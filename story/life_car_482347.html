What are the categories of car beauty?

There are many categories of car beauty, mainly including the following:

Body Beauty

Body beauty mainly includes high-pressure car washing, rust removal, removal of asphalt, tar and other dirt, waxing and mirror treatment, new car waxing, steel rim, tire, bumper refurbishment and chassis anti-corrosion coating treatment. Regular car washing can remove dust, acid rain, asphalt and other pollutants from the car surface, preventing the paint surface and other body parts from being corroded and damaged. Timely waxing not only gives the body a bright effect, but also multi-functional car wax can take good care of your car, which can prevent ultraviolet rays, acid rain, high temperature resistance and anti-static. Waterless car wash.

Interior Beauty

Interior beauty is mainly divided into in-car beauty, engine beauty, trunk cleaning and other contents. In-car beauty includes instrument panel, ceiling, carpet, floor mat, seat, seat cover, sunshade board, car door lining dust suction cleaning protection, as well as steam sterilization, odor removal from air vents, in-car air purification and other items. Engine beauty includes engine cleaning, spraying gloss protector, doing refurbishing treatment, three filters cleaning (referring to fuel filter, oil filter, air filter) and other items.

Paint Treatment

Paint treatment service items can be divided into oxidation film treatment, flying paint treatment, acid rain treatment, paint scratch treatment, paint damage treatment and whole vehicle painting. Paint treatment not only keeps your car "youthful", but also restores scratches and damages caused by your negligence. Better protect the body and keep the value of the car.

Car Protection

Car protection items include applying explosion-proof sun film, installing alarms, installing static dischargers, installing car voice alarm devices, etc. Although car protection does not have a direct impact on the appearance of the car, it can take good care of your car.

Car Accessories

Car accessories are the finishing touch of car beauty, and also a manifestation of car life culture. It is committed to creating a mobile living space in the car, such as car perfume, wax dusters, sunglasses, handle covers, seat cushions, etc. Car accessories bring people a close concern. Efforts pay off. Behind the beauty, it's not just the impulse to chase fashion, but more importantly, it's about grasping another material culture.
