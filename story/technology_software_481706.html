Which hospital in Xi'an is the best for treating fibrocystic breast disease? What are the common symptoms of fibrocystic breast disease? Some women often feel that their breasts are swollen and painful. Because there are no other discomforts, they usually do not seek medical treatment. Some people even naively think that this can enhance the female figure beauty. However, swelling and dull pain may be the initial manifestations of fibrocystic breast disease. Delayed treatment of fibrocystic breast disease may even cause lesions. Below, experts from Xi'an Ciai Gynecology Hospital will give you a detailed explanation.

Experts from Xi'an Ciai Gynecology Hospital said: The clinical manifestations of this disease are mainly breast lumps and breast pain. About 80% of patients have breast pain, mostly bilateral, but sometimes unilateral pain. The nature of the pain is divided into distension pain, pricking pain, darting pain, dull pain or palpation pain. The manifestation of breast pain is often unstable, which may worsen before menstruation and also often worsens with emotional changes, fatigue, and weather changes. Breast lump is the main basis for diagnosing breast disease. Most of them are multiple, varying in size, hard or firm texture, not adherent to the skin, often with an uneven surface, and a granular sensation when touched. In addition to the above symptoms, some patients have nipple itching, discharge, bitter mouth, rib distension, chest tightness, loss of appetite, menstrual disorders, etc.

Different types and their clinical manifestations are as follows:

(1) Liver depression and qi stagnation type: Menstrual periods earlier or during menstruation, breast swelling and pain disappear with anger. One side or both sides can palpate bead-like nodules of varying sizes, mostly pea-sized nodules or coarse cord-like masses, tough but not hard, movable upon palpation, not adherent to deep tissues, unclear boundaries, insufficient menstrual cycle, more menstrual flow, chest tightness, belching, mental depression, irritability.

(2) Chong-Ren imbalance type: Breast has lumps, pain worsens before or during menstruation, and lessens or disappears after menstruation. Menstruation is often delayed, mild dysmenorrhea, less menstrual flow, fatigue, weakness, sore waist, cold limbs, abdominal coldness. Prolonged untreated cases may develop cancerous changes in a few cases. Resected specimens are often yellowish-white, tough without capsule, and sometimes many scattered small cysts can be seen on the cut surface. These are actually cystically dilated ducts. The cyst walls are mostly smooth, containing yellow-green or brown sticky liquid, sometimes yellowish-white cheesy material overflows from the duct openings.

The following preventive measures can be taken for this disease:

Firstly, maintain emotional stability and reduce mental stimulation. Statistics show that 85% of patients had adverse emotional changes before the onset of the disease. Mental stimulation can lead to elevated estrogen levels and endocrine disorders.

Secondly, pay attention to balancing work and rest. Units with a concentration of intellectuals have a higher incidence rate. Based on this point, intellectual women should especially pay attention to balancing work and rest. In addition, breastfeeding can reduce the incidence of the disease because breastfeeding can lower the incidence of fibrocystic breast disease and also reduce the number of abortions, thereby lowering the incidence.

Thirdly, drug treatment. Traditional Chinese medicine treatment is currently the main treatment method, and clinical evidence shows that Chinese medicine treatment is very effective.

Fourthly, surgery. For fibrocystic breast disease, local excision surgery cannot achieve the therapeutic goal, but rather focuses on excluding malignant breast lesions. When the lump is hard and difficult to differentiate from breast cancer, surgical treatment for diagnosis is necessary.
