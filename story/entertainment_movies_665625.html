Wen Jun and Ye Weimin collaborate again to create a high-quality thriller

This time, Wen Jun and Ye Weimin join forces to enter the horror film market, carefully crafting a series of death cases to present the audience with a high-quality horror feast. In response to the enthusiastic speculation of the media and netizens, the two said that the most terrifying events are generally those that cannot be specifically explained. The appearance of the "red-dressed female ghost" is by no means as simple as it looks in the stills. It is precisely due to the accumulation of a series of suspense elements that can create the most shocking effects. As elements such as the "red-dressed female ghost" are gradually disclosed, the mystery of "Embroidered Shoes" becomes stronger. For the entire film, this is just the beginning of revealing the bloody red cheongsam.

It is reported that the movie "Embroidered Shoes" is a high-quality suspenseful horror film with multiple thrilling elements, scheduled for nationwide release in March.

Recently, the first stills of the main actress Kara Hui from the suspenseful horror film "Embroidered Shoes", co-created by Wen Jun and Ye Weimin, were released. In the stills, Kara Hui, hailed as a legendary figure in Hong Kong cinema, appears as a chilling "Black Widow" in black attire. It is reported that this is Kara Hui's first role after winning the Best Actress award at the Hong Kong Film Awards for the second time with her performance in "Mad Detective".
