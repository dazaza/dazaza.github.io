TCL Group's mobile phone sales reached 12.373 million units from January to April.

by geekzhang on 2013-05-07 21:29:27

On May 7, TCL Group released an announcement of the sales volume of main products in April 2013. The LCD TV sales volume reached 5.812 million units from January to April, increasing by 22.8% year-on-year; mobile phone sales volume reached 12.373 million units from January to April, increasing by 14.94% year-on-year, among which smart phone sales volume reached 2.274 million units, increasing by 104.26% year-on-year; Huaxing Optoelectronics realized liquid crystal glass substrate input of 415,900 sheets from January to April, increasing by 445.7% year-on-year.Data shows that TCL Group's LCD TV sales volume (including commercial displays) reached 1.8132 million units in April 2013, increasing by 25.28% year-on-year. Domestic and overseas market sales volumes reached 961,400 units and 772,200 units respectively, increasing by 30.08% and 14.91% year-on-year respectively. Monthly 3D and smart TV sales volumes reached 275,100 units and 306,100 units respectively, increasing by 225.66% and 109.84% year-on-year respectively.Huaxing Optoelectronics' liquid crystal glass substrate reached 108,000 sheets in April, with combined liquid crystal TV panel and module sales reaching 1.7637 million pieces. It was also learned that TCL Group announced on May 3 that it successfully transferred 30% equity of Huaxing Optoelectronics, and industry insiders predicted that the equity transfer would increase the group's capital reserve and net profit.TCL Communication achieved a mobile phone sales volume of 3.8867 million units in April, increasing by 33.31% year-on-year. Domestic and overseas market sales volumes were 431,100 units and 3.4556 million units respectively, with overseas market increasing by 37%. Additionally, the company continued to focus on intelligent terminal products, with this month's smart phone sales volume reaching 820,200 units, increasing by 123.38% year-on-year, and smart phones accounting for 21%.In terms of TCL home appliance business, air conditioner, refrigerator, and washing machine product sales volumes increased by 31.52%, 26.42%, and 86.73% year-on-year respectively. Additionally, on April 17, the company held a groundbreaking ceremony for the TCL Home Appliance (Hefei) Industrial Park project, which is aimed at creating mid-to-high-end refrigerators, washing machines, and other home appliances, at the Taohua Industrial Park in Feixi County, Hefei City.
