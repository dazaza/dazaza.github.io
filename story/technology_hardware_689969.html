White Lotus: A drunk driver hits someone and flees but is forced to stop by a taxi driver and passenger working together.

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-04 18:05:12

The driver, reeking of alcohol, drove recklessly and hit a woman before continuing on his way. Witnessing this, Mr. Liu Changming, a taxi driver in Nanjing, and his passenger, Mr. Fan, spontaneously reacted: "Chase the hit-and-run driver!"

Mr. Liu quickly stepped on the gas, starting from the entrance of No. 4 Gate of Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), covering nearly a kilometer while driving parallel to the offending vehicle and shouting at the driver through the rolled-down window to stop. Finally, near Gate No. 7 of NJUST, they managed to force the hit-and-run driver to stop.

At this point, the offending driver, drenched in alcohol, laughingly defended himself, saying, "Did I drive?" Subsequently, the man was taken away by traffic police for investigation.

After hitting a person, the Guangben car fled the scene. Around 8:30 PM the previous evening, Mr. Fan, a citizen, flagged down a taxi belonging to the "Transportation" company outside Yindragon Garden Residential Area in Nanjing, sitting in the front passenger seat.

This taxi driven by Mr. Liu Changming passed by Gate No. 4 of Nanjing University of Science and Technology. It was a small road about four meters wide, with many pedestrians due to it being the weekend. Therefore, Mr. Liu slowed down the speed of the taxi.

At this moment, Mr. Liu noticed not far ahead, a silver Guangben car swaying forward unsteadily, traveling at a speed of around forty to fifty kilometers per hour. Suddenly, the right rearview mirror of this Guangben scraped against a young woman.

"The car's speed wasn't slow; the girl was knocked down, her back of the head hitting the ground first. She lay there without moving for quite some time, likely suffering serious injuries," Mr. Liu Changming recounted. After hitting the person, the offending vehicle drove off directly.

Witnesses quickly gathered around the girl lying unconscious on the ground to offer care, and someone promptly dialed 120. Mr. Fan took a glance out the car window at the unconscious girl lying on the ground, exchanging glances with Mr. Liu, and both said simultaneously, "Quickly, chase down the offending driver!" Immediately, Mr. Liu stepped on the gas and chased after the vehicle honking the horn.

The taxi driver and passenger jointly surrounded and blocked the offender. Mr. Liu kept a close eye on this Guangben car, turning northward onto Victory Village Road from the small road. After chasing for seven or eight hundred meters, his car was already driving parallel to the Guangben.

Mr. Liu rolled down the window and shouted loudly at the other driver while gesturing, "Stop! Stop quickly! You've hit someone!" However, the other party did not slow down but continued to accelerate.

Mr. Fan asked Mr. Liu to focus on driving while he shouted. "I yelled five or six times, and they slowly began to reduce their speed. There were two men in the car besides the driver." Meanwhile, Mr. Liu continued to accelerate, circling around to the front of the Guangben and forcing it to stop.

After the male passenger got out of the car and tried to run away, Mr. Fan and Mr. Liu quickly approached the Guangben. They saw a middle-aged man sitting in the driver's seat. "Do you even know that you hit someone?" Mr. Fan questioned. The man got out of the car, reeking of alcohol, yet smiled foolishly and said, "Did I drive?" As he spoke, he attempted to run, but Mr. Liu and Mr. Fan caught up and stopped him.

When the fast reporter arrived at the scene, the offending driver admitted to drinking alcohol. Traffic police soon arrived at the scene, escorting the offending driver into the police car, and under the guidance of Mr. Liu and Mr. Fan, inspected the accident site. Mr. Liu felt that what he had done was very ordinary, "It just so happened that only my car passed by at that moment. If another driver had seen it, I believe they would have done the same." Mr. Fan admired and exclaimed, "Chasing such an offending driver is still quite dangerous. Mr. Liu is a righteous guy."

According to reports, the girl who was hit suffered serious head injuries and was rushed to a nearby hospital for emergency treatment. Yesterday, the reporter contacted the Second Traffic Police Brigade, and the police stated that the blood test results of the offending driver are not yet out, making it unclear whether it was drunk driving. The cause of the accident is still under further investigation.
