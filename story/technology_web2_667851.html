Announcement Regarding the List of Registered Level 1 Construction Managers for the Ninth Batch of 2011

Announcement No. 1203 of the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of the People's Republic of China

In accordance with the "Regulations on the Management of Registered Construction Managers" (Ministry of Construction Order No. 153), the "Implementation Measures for Level 1 Construction Manager Registration," and the "Announcement of Provisions and Contents of Administrative Licensing Matters Directly Implemented by the Ministry of Construction" (Ministry of Construction Announcement No. 278), after review, Zhang Guoxin and 13,931 others meet the registration requirements for Level 1 Construction Managers and are hereby approved for registration.

This announcement is made accordingly.
