The two cities appear slightly tired, with the performance of heavyweight stocks showing weakness - Stock Forum

by fj0953210 on 2009-12-05 22:02:15

The forestry and papermaking sector, which has been unnoticed by investors all the time, finally gained momentum recently. With the improvement of performance and the promotion of low-carbon concept, the price of the sector's stock rose quietly, with half of the individual stocks reaching a new high since this year. Yesterday, after the entire rise in the previous trading day, the forestry and papermaking sector again achieved "full marks", with the overall increase of the sector approaching 3%, performing very prominently in the two markets. It has been nearly a year since the last rise of the forestry and papermaking sector. The performance of the sector this week can be described as "silence is golden". Individual stocks soared collectively, among which some stocks continued to reach the limit. Regarding the recent strength of the sector, we believe that from its own basic surface, the low valuation of the forestry and papermaking sector provides space for its rise. The beginning of this year was the low point of the papermaking industry's prosperity, and then the industry has been in recovery. Since May, the prices of pulp and paper have started to rise. By the third quarter, the production and operation conditions of most papermaking enterprises have tended to be normal, and the gross profit margin level has been comparable to the level before the international financial crisis. Moreover, public information shows that the forestry and papermaking sector has been favored by institutions. In the third quarter report, almost all leading papermaking stocks were increased or heavily held by funds. Another reason for the sharp rise of the forestry and papermaking sector is the promotion of the external low-carbon concept. With the upcoming Copenhagen United Nations Climate Change Conference, the low-carbon concept has become the main line recognized by all kinds of funds in the recent A-share market, and forestry has become the new topic concerned by the bulls. At the same time, as the downstream industry of forestry, many listed companies in the papermaking industry also own forestry assets. Industry insiders predict that the papermaking industry will hopefully further improve and upgrade the existing model in the future, thus greatly enhancing the comprehensive competitiveness. From the perspective of news, the cement industry has taken "heavy measures" to eliminate backward production capacity, and the coal chemical industry may follow closely. On December 2nd, it was learned from relevant industry associations that more stringent measures to eliminate backward production capacity in industries such as cement and coal chemicals are being formulated. This time, the government is determined to eliminate backward production capacity, which is both due to the consideration of structural adjustment and closely related to China's emission reduction commitment, which has more of a "combination punch" flavor before the Copenhagen United Nations Climate Conference. "Previously, the notice issued by the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology on issues related to the clean-up work of cement and flat glass construction projects was already a strong medicine, and more policies may be introduced in the future." The output of the cement and steel industries is seriously excessive, and the pollution is serious, making them key industries for low-carbon emission reduction. Strict control of output will be beneficial to the reorganization of the entire industry, and large cement plants can still be paid attention to. From a technical point of view, the two markets maintained a volatile trend throughout the day, but the technical indicators of the two market indexes continued to improve. Especially, the green column of the MACD index of the two markets has significantly shortened; the KDJ index of the market has all turned up. This indicates that there is a technical requirement for short-term continuous rise. The key is that the current volume has not been effectively amplified, which means that the trading volume is the key to ensuring the sustainability of subsequent rebounds. In general, after pulling three consecutive positive lines, the market began to show obvious fluctuations and adjustments today. Judging from the market situation, some mainstream hot spot sectors in the previous period could still be maintained in the market today, with automobile and pharmaceutical stocks performing relatively well, maintaining a certain popularity. Meanwhile, after several days of continuous rebound of the stock index, the morale of the bulls has been somewhat consumed. It is reasonable to have a certain degree of short-term fluctuation and consolidation. In the future operations, investors can focus on the trend of blue-chip stocks, because whether the blue-chip stocks can stabilize and strengthen is the key to determining the medium-term trend of the market. In addition, the market may face a style conversion, and for varieties with excessive short-term increases, appropriate reductions can be made.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. And this order is only valid on the day the order is placed. The contents of the order include the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of the stock is usually three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when entrusting to buy or sell. At the same time, there are certain regulations on the quantity of buying and selling stocks: that is, the quantity of entrusting to buy stocks must be an integer multiple of 100, but the quantity of entrusting to sell stocks does not have to be an integer multiple of 100. There are four types of orders: counter submission order, telephone automatic order, computer automatic order, and remote terminal order.
