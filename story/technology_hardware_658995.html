The Tax Bureau of Shaanxi Hanzhong Development Zone takes five measures to improve the efficiency of the health creation work

by cp3287bm on 2012-02-04 21:53:22

The Development Zone Tax Bureau regards the task of creating a national health city as an important opportunity to improve the quality of cadres, popularize service levels, and build a national tax brand. They have been continuously improving the working environment of their units, vigorously implementing beautification projects, standardizing work order and the placement of appliances and objects, enhancing the education and cultivation of cadres' qualities, and promoting civility. This has effectively promoted the coordinated progress of various tasks. The tax service hall was successively honored as the "Pacesetter Post for Women" at the administrative level and the "People Satisfied Grassroots Station/Office" at the provincial level. The bureau was commended by the Municipal Committee and the municipal government as the "Model Civilized Unit", "Advanced Unit in Health", and "Exemplary Unit in Law Enforcement".

Dali Tax Bureau has taken several measures:

1. Improving organizational structure: A leading group for creation work was established, headed by the "top leader" with members from various departments, clarifying work missions and requirements, strengthening job responsibilities, and providing strong organizational support for creation work.

2. Advocating diverse content: Using carriers such as internal meeting networks, electronic display screens, and educational exhibition boards, they vigorously promote creation advocacy, striving to create a humanistic ecological working and living environment. Within the unit, there are designated "smoke-free zones", "etiquette mirrors", and health education columns, with reminders for saving electricity, conserving water, and cherishing flowers and plants visible everywhere, motivating all staff to actively participate in creation activities, starting from small things around them, and consciously maintaining the image of the national tax unit, forming an atmosphere where everyone pays attention, supports, and participates actively.

3. Improving social systems: Combining work practices, they formulated and improved systems such as greening beautification maintenance, health management, tax services, attendance review, etc., laying a foundation for creation work. They advocate each tax cadre to take care of one pot of flowers, reduce the use of disposable items, purify and conserve resources, starting from the basics, implementing various measures in specific actions, ensuring the solid development of creation work.

4. Increasing financial investment: More than 100,000 yuan has been invested to continue energy-saving and emission-reduction efforts in the unit canteen, water heaters, toilets, etc. Regular inspections of water and power lines are conducted, and the lighting fixtures in the unit have been replaced with energy-saving lamps. Efforts are made to strengthen sewage treatment. Standardized signs, anti-corruption education walls, and system plaques are planned and constructed, enriching and improving collective activity rooms and sports venues.

5. Strengthening education and training: Organizing all personnel to carry out training on civilized etiquette knowledge, cultivating good and healthy work and life habits among cadres and employees, actively carrying out cashier qualification knowledge training, singing competitions, knowledge contests, essay contests, etc., creating a good atmosphere for the creation of a national health city work, and promoting the deep and rapid development of creation work.
