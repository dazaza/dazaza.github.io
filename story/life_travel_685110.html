Mount Huang

by jj0505 on 2012-03-01 13:43:38

I am from Yangzhou. I took the train K434/431 from Yangzhou at 12:42 PM and arrived at Huangshan Station (Tunxi) at 10:03 PM. After getting out of the train station, I took a taxi (starting price 6+2 RMB), stayed overnight in the city, and then took a bus from Tunxi Bus Station to the Huangshan Scenic Area Transfer Center the next day. At the transfer center, I transferred to the Xinguo Line bus to reach the YunGu Temple entrance of the back mountain ticket office. After purchasing the ticket, you can choose to walk or take the cable car up the mountain. It is recommended to take the cable car to save energy because there are no scenic spots between YunGu Temple and BaiHe Ridge (6.5 km). Taking the YunGu new cable car up the mountain, it takes about 10 minutes to reach the top (Little Mysterious Dream). The tour route includes: Harp Pine - XinFa Peak - HeiHu Pine - MengBi ShengHua - BeiHai Hotel - Dawn Pavilion - QingLiang Terrace - Monkey Watching the Sea - Unity Pine - XiHai Hotel - PaiYun Pavilion - XiHai Grand Canyon Loop One - Loop Two - return to PaiYun Pavilion, pass Flying Stone to reach GuangMingDing. Stay at BaiYun Hotel. After check-in, you can go to GuangMingDing or AoYu Peak to see the sunset.

The next morning, get up early to watch the sunrise at GuangMingDing or AoYu Peak. After the sunrise, go back to the hotel for breakfast. After breakfast, check out and carry your luggage to AoYu Peak - AoYu Cave (or YiXianTian) - Hundred Steps Cloud Ladder - Lotus Pavilion - Lotus Peak - YuPing Building Welcoming Guests Pine (you can take the cable car down the mountain to CiGuang Pavilion) - PengLai Three Islands - TiDu Peak foot (TiDu Peak is closed, bypassed) - Half Mountain Temple - LiMa Bridge - Crescent Pavilion - CiGuang Pavilion. Take the scenic area bus back to Tangkou, take the bus back to Huangshan City Tunxi, and stroll around Tunxi Old Street in the evening to enjoy the night view of the old street and taste the local specialty snacks. Take the K432 train at 23:56 PM back to Yangzhou, ending a pleasant journey.
