Big S learns to "seduce" men on set; "only loves handsome guys" is purely coincidental

by zhlnjxvr80 on 2011-12-18 15:54:32

In her latest film, "Imperial Detective Lingling Dog," Big S completely reinvents herself as the eccentric and love-struck female knight "Mei Wish." During a recent appearance on a program, she joked about seducing Gu Tianle in the movie: "Before the official filming, the mother figure in the film, Yuan Qiongdan, was on set teaching me how to tempt a man. I almost laughed myself into stitches after watching her performance! But after laughing, I started to get scared—this scene is mine, what am I going to do? Later, when I watched my own performance, I couldn't help but laugh again. The effect should be okay, right!"

Big S also revealed that despite her delicate appearance, she has a strong "manly" side within her. She considers herself the "big brother" of her sisters and the "husband" of her mother. "My mom and sisters are all people who don't like making decisions, so at home, I play the role of the man. It's my duty to protect them and make them happy."

With multiple women in the household, there's inevitably some disdain for men. Big S humorously remarked, "When several women get together, it can get very noisy, so my dad basically doesn't live at home." She further disclosed, "On the surface, my mom makes the decisions at home, but in reality, it's me who calls the shots behind the scenes. Ha ha! I'm like the empress dowager ruling from behind the curtain!"

From Lan Zhenglong to Zhou Yu Min, the men Big S has dated have all been top-tier handsome guys. Little S even revealed on a show, "My sister only likes handsome men; her men can be poor, but they cannot be ugly!" In response, Big S cried out in indignation, "I've never said that—it's all my sister talking nonsense! I like their personalities, and coincidentally, they happen to be quite handsome. Maybe heaven has been particularly kind to me!"

Even though Big S repeatedly clarified that she does not have a special preference for handsome men, after breaking up with仔仔 (Zai Zai), her attitude towards love became both yearning and fearful. "Maybe my brother-in-law is too perfect, which has unconsciously raised my standards for a husband," Big S laughed. "Actually, my requirements aren't high—I just hope to find someone filial, who shares common language with me, and can turn me from 'big brother' into a little woman."
