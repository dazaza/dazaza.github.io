Check the QQ level, and you'll know what kind of Internet user you are.

by yinyin on 2008-06-19 10:17:28

The level of QQ, apart from the fact that one sun can upload avatars and create two groups, can also prove what level of netizen you are.

Network Novice

Level 1 - Online for 5 days

Level 2 - Online for 12 days

Level 3 - Online for 21 days

Level 4 - Online for 32 days

Level 5 - Online for 45 days

Level 6 - Online for 60 days

New friends who have just started using the internet and only know a little about it. Sometimes they suddenly tell their friends, "Let me tell you a secret, I found out that using Smart ABC input method can produce various symbols @! # $! %! @±℃" or "I discovered that QQ can modify friend notes, I can change them to all sorts of weird names" ~~~~~~ etc., naive and funny words.

Network Knight-errant

Level 7 - Online for 77 days

Level 8 - Online for 96 days

Level 12 - Online for 192 days

They have some familiarity and understanding of the network. Some start crazily adding people for chats, some start chatting in QQ groups, some start online romances.

They can recite the URLs of major portal sites by heart. They look up news and information on various websites.

They start playing various types of online games. Landlord, Mahjong, Poker, Legend SF, World of Warcraft...

Network Knight

Levels 13 to 16

They become half-webworms.

They start creating groups, becoming group owners or leaders. They read and post threads on various forums. Every time they post a thread, they will check it ten times a day, always paying attention to others' replies.

They have met more than five netizens and have a history of several failed online romances.

They enter voice/video chat rooms to look for the next target for an online romance.

Their online gaming has reached a certain level.

On the street, whenever they hear someone talking about topics related to the internet, their ears perk up.

Network Enthusiast

Levels 16 to 30

Full-fledged webworms.

They start trying online shopping.

In front of the computer, they can do anything they want. They can find any character they want to look up, download any software they want. They reach the level of doing whatever they want.

They disdain chatting, only chatting with a few known friends. They rarely add other people.

They are even more disdainful of online romances, but every time they meet an object of interest, they have the impulse to start a new online romance. But the passion soon dies out.

They focus on decorating their BLOG and space, starting to write network diaries. Recording the bits and pieces of life and the network.

In online games, they have reached the level of boredom. They only occasionally go on previous games to chat with friends or play some puzzle games.

Web Immortal

Levels 31 to 47

Became immortal.

They are often called by friends to help look up certain materials or handle some network problems.

At this point, they start focusing on something specific. They no longer aimlessly surf the net.

But more often, they still sit in front of the computer staring blankly, or simply find some online novels to read, or download some movies online to watch.

When chatting with friends, they type fewer and fewer words, they read posts but rarely reply (except for moderators) and rarely post new threads.

They have already become immortals in the network. The time they spend sitting in front of the computer is decreasing, but they feel uneasy if they don't turn on the computer, so they might as well open the computer and then go to another room to watch TV...
