7 Killer Tips for Working from Home and Making Money with Affiliate Marketing

by michalchan on 2012-03-06 19:10:54

Marketing or advertising other people’s goods on your site could make you a highly lucrative living that can allow you to leave your career and begin living the life you deserve, fake sunglasses! You could do this just by joining one of the many affiliate programs. Here are the top several tips to succeed in affiliate marketing/advertising:

1. Sign up for the Google Adsense program. I suggest you start with Google Adsense. This is the easiest way to begin, plus it's the most widely accepted affiliate program on the internet. It's a pay-per-click affiliate program where Google ads are added to your site, linked to your site's content. You can also add the Google web and website search button to your site, which allows you to earn money every time your visitor uses it. I recommend placing the Google Adsense link on all of your web pages, in a place that is clearly accessible and visible to your visitors.

2. Sign up for a 2-Tier Affiliate Program. These are programs that allow you to employ hundreds of sub-affiliates and receive a commission from their efforts every month. You just buy a product, add it to the network, and let others make you money!

3. Sign up for affiliate programs offering lifetime commissions. These programs provide lifetime commissions for revenues from one sale or sub-affiliate. This is also known as residual income. A single transaction could bring you a monthly paycheck for life, unlike trying to persuade someone to buy your product every month.

4. Sign up for an affiliate network. These networks offer you a wide range of products and services from various companies to advertise or promote. Every affiliate network offers multiple affiliate programs from different companies, some even include world-famous brands and products. Good affiliate networks will track all your commissions from the various programs and pay you each month on time, usually via check. This is essential and will allow you to focus solely on selling.

5. Sign up for affiliate programs that offer high-level commissions. These are programs that generously give away commissions ranging from 35% to nearly 80% of every sale. So don't waste your time advertising other people's products that don't reward you for your hard work.

6. Optimize your site for maximum exposure. You need to optimize your website to make it search engine friendly and appealing to your target audience. Add quality, creative writing to your web pages. Choose keywords related to your website's subject matter. Also, reduce the graphics on your site to allow your pages to load faster. Don’t forget to place your navigational menu in the most convenient part of your website, allowing potential customers to easily navigate your site.

7. Start promoting your website. To promote your affiliate items, you need to increase your site's popularity. You will have to promote it by submitting your websites to Google, Yahoo, MSN, AskJeeves, DMOZ, and other recognized search engines and directories. Exchange links with popular related websites. Write articles and submit them to article directories. Sign up for popular forums that will allow you to put your site link in their signature area.
