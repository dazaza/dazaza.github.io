Shengdiao: A man takes care of his animal-loving wife for 5 years without leaving or abandoning her.

by hzxfsmsrg907 on 2012-02-09 16:45:02

Before 2007, Qiu Youfu, who lived in Liu Ying Village, Luhe Township, Suiyang District, Shangqiu City, had a happy family of four and was living a joyful life. However, one day in December 2007, his wife Guo Suoling suddenly suffered from a cerebral infarction and fell into a vegetative state. For five years, Qiu Youfu has been taking care of his wife without leaving her side, and now, Guo Suoling, who was given no hope by the doctors, is showing signs of improvement.

Reported by Chen Tao of East News Today and intern Yang Xiaogang with text and pictures

[Incident] Wife Suffers Sudden Cerebral Infarction and Becomes Vegetative

Qiu Youfu's home consists of several bungalows, but the yard and house are tidied up very neatly.

"I clean the house every day because I want Su Ling to wake up one day and see that the house is still the same as when she was not bedridden," said Qiu Youfu, aged 47, on September 26th.

Qiu Youfu's wife, Guo Suoling, aged 49 this year, lies in bed and cannot understand any words. But the good news is that through the care of her husband Qiu Youfu, she can now eat without a gastric tube, and even after using the bathroom, she has some awareness and asks her husband to help her clean up.

According to Qiu Youfu, one evening in December 2007, he returned home and found Guo Suoling lying in bed. He called out to her, but she had lost all consciousness.

Qiu Youfu immediately took his wife to the First People's Hospital of Shangqiu City. After examination, it was discovered that Guo Suoling had suffered a sudden cerebral infarction, and her brain cells had already suffered extensive necrosis. She basically became vegetative, and recovery would only be possible if a miracle occurred.

Qiu Youfu was unwilling to give up. After borrowing more than 30,000 yuan, his wife stayed in the hospital for over a month, but her condition did not improve. "The doctor said that since she was already like this, there was no point spending more money. Go home. If she could survive three or four more years, that would be good enough," said Qiu Youfu.

With no choice, Qiu Youfu brought his wife home and began taking care of her.

[Treatment] Over 200,000 Calls, Wife’s Condition Improves

Qiu Youfu said that at first when they returned home, Guo Suoling relied on a gastric tube to eat, which needed to be changed every four days. As Qiu Youfu was not a medical professional, how could he insert the gastric tube? Helpless, he specially went to the hospital and learned the technique of inserting a gastric tube from the nurse.

At that time, Qiu Youfu, who had never cooked before, also learned how to cook. Guo Suoling could only eat liquid food at that time. Eggs had to be stirred into the noodle soup and beaten finely to avoid choking, and then injected into the gastric tube with a syringe to enter the stomach.

After half a year, a miracle happened, Guo Suoling's condition improved somewhat, and the gastric tube was removed. She could accept her husband's feeding. Later, she even regained some awareness and made some humming sounds after using the bathroom, prompting her husband to clean her up.

Qiu Youfu said that he calls his wife more than 100 times a day, and now it has exceeded 200,000 times. At night, he sits by his wife's bedside and talks about past events, hoping to awaken her one day. "As long as there is a glimmer of hope, I will not give up. As long as she is alive, no matter what, our family will remain complete, and our two children can still call her mom," said Qiu Youfu emotionally.

[Impact]

Qiu Youfu's Persistence Touches Everyone

Neighbor Yang Huayu said that after Guo Suoling became like this, many people had advised Qiu Youfu to give up, but he continued to take care of her without leaving her side, and everyone who mentioned him gave him a thumbs up.

"Friend Fu not only takes care of his wife, but also farms eight acres of land and raises pigs and sheep. These years have been tough for him, and the neighbors try to help as much as they can," said neighbor Zhang Xueling.

It is understood that after Guo Suoling became bedridden, the entire household depended on Qiu Youfu. Both their children are studying at university, and the annual tuition fee of more than ten thousand yuan is the most headache-inducing issue for Qiu Youfu.

After being moved by Qiu Youfu's story, Mr. Song, the manager of a car rental company in Shangqiu City, bought rice, oil, and other daily necessities to visit Qiu Youfu and handed him 2,000 yuan. Mr. Song also expressed that he would continue to help this family.
