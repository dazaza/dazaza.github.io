The hometown of Zhang Xinyi, the lead actress of "Pifu" (A Hero), is Ziyang, a place with heavenly resources in Sichuan.

When we see Zhang Xinyi, the lead actress of "Give Me A Cigarette", we are deeply impressed by her vivid performance as the female protagonist. However, at that time, we didn't deliberately remember Zhang Xinyi's name. Finally, we recognized Zhang Xinyi again in "Beijing Love Story". Her distinctive performance made many audiences like her very much. While we were applauding her, we are about to welcome the premiere of Zhang Xinyi's new movie "Pifu". Such a hardworking and prolific beautiful actress makes us unable to resist liking her. The reason for her personality must be related to the environment she has lived in. Therefore, we here trace back to Zhang Xinyi's hometown where she spent her childhood in Ziyang, Sichuan...

Zhang Xinyi's hometown, Ziyang, is rich in cultural relics, especially the Anyue Stone Carvings which originated in the Northern and Southern Dynasties and flourished in the Tang and Song Dynasties. They are praised as "another great treasure house of ancient Chinese sculpture" for being "ancient, numerous, exquisite, and beautiful".

The Woji Chan Master Pavilion at Mumen Temple in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang is a provincial-level cultural relic protection unit in Sichuan Province. Here, the mountains rise and fall, clouds swirl around, and there is an inscription on the stone archway in front of the temple called "Dongpu Zen Forest", inside which Woji Chan Master is buried. Because the door except for the wooden one is all stone-built, hence the name.

In the south suburbs of Jianyang City in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang, there is Shengde Temple. Inside the temple, there is a tower originally named Shengyin Tower, but because the tower body is painted with gray powder and appears white, it is called White Tower. The locals also call Zhang Xinyi's hometown Shengde Temple as White Tower Temple. The tower was built during the reign of Emperor Ningzong of the Song Dynasty. It rises from the ground, 60 meters high, with a circumference of 20 meters at the top and 53 meters at the platform. The tower is hollow and curved, with stairs leading up and down. The tower has 12 levels, each level painted with Buddha images, and Buddha statues are sculpted on the walls, with windows open on all four sides to let light in. Climbing to the top of the tower, looking far away, the rivers, mountains, and city can be seen clearly.

The People's Park of Jianyang City in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang is located on the north bank of the confluence of the Jiangxi River into the Tuojiang River in the northern part of Jianyang City. Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang Jianyang White Tower Garden was initially built in 1929, advocated by Li Qingting, the deputy commander of the Sichuan Frontier Army and the county magistrate. The park covers more than 100 acres. At that time, a famous garden designer from Chengdu designed and constructed the park according to the Ming Dynasty's Jiangnan garden art. Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang Jianyang White Tower Garden dug ponds, piled stones, stacked mountains, built pavilions, set up platforms, and created scenic views, planted flowers and trees. The already well-scaled, lush, and fragrant building complex was respectively named with elegant names such as "Yu Linglong", "Lotus Qu Yuan", "View Fish Hua Gang", "Gu Mountain Yi Mai", and "Ying Yue San Tan", making a new garden become a vivid replica of the Suzhou-Hangzhou landscape, unique in its own way.

The Huaxi Valley in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang originates from Zhongjiang County, flows through Jintang and Jianyang, and converges into the Tuojiang River at the Two Rivers Mouth of Yanjiaba Town in Baohe Town, Yanjiang District. It is 136.54 kilometers long, with 34.4 kilometers within the Yanjiang District. Currently, 25 natural landscapes and 13 cultural landscapes have been discovered. From the Two Rivers Mouth in Baohe Town to the Shuanghe Bridge in Laojun Town, it is the downstream area of the Huaxi River in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang. This section has a wide river surface, calm water, green trees on both banks, bamboo shadows, and the clear stream water reflecting the two banks, forming a unique landscape of "mountains in the water, boats on the mountain".

Sancha Lake Scenic Area in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang is located in the southwest of Jianyang City, centered around the Sancha Reservoir. It is the core scenic area of the "Two Lakes and One Mountain" new five major premium tourism routes in Sichuan Province. The lake area has abundant rainfall and pleasant climate. Sancha Lake has a beautiful shape and clear water. Looking far away from a high place, it resembles a huge emerald coral. Its southwestern lake forks wind gracefully, while its northwestern side is surrounded by mountains. On the east shore, there are nineteen embankments like jade belts, decorating the scenery beautifully. In winter and spring, thousands of water birds gather near the islands, flying up and down, making Sancha Lake full of vitality and vigor.

Longquan Lake in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang is located in Shidan Town, Jianyang City. The main attractions of Longquan Lake include Qigu Ridge, Shibupo, Waleng Temple, and Ten-li Long Gorge, among which the "Ten-li Long Gorge" stretching for 5380 meters is particularly enchanting. It connects ten water bays and winds around twelve peaks. The two shores feature strange peaks and rocks, lush grasses, continuous blue waves on the lake surface, gentle breezes, and ships sailing through them, creating a breathtaking view. Every year, the Peach Blossom Festival held on Taohua Island within the Longquan Lake scenic area features blossoming flowers competing for beauty, flocks of butterflies flying, tourists mooring their boats and landing on the island, walking through the flowers, entering a realm of "stepping over the waves into the forest seeking flowers," making people reluctant to leave.

Half Moon Mountain Buddha in Longquan Lake of Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang

Sichuan has many large Buddhas in China, with a total of 15 large Buddhas over 10 meters tall in Sichuan, and one third of them are in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang, creating the saying that "there are more large Buddhas in Ziyang, Sichuan."

The Half Moon Mountain Buddha in Zhang Xinyi's hometown Ziyang belongs to the cliff carvings, which are Buddha statues carved on cliff walls. Especially the seated statue of Maitreya Buddha belongs to the Pure Land Sect carvings. The Buddha sits in a rectangular stone niche, facing south and north, 22.24 meters tall, and 11.2 meters wide across the chest. The structure is majestic and simple, inspiring awe at first sight. The Buddha's face is full, ears hanging down to the shoulders, expression peaceful and tranquil, eyes calm and looking down, as if kindly observing the worshipers, making people feel both respectful and endearing.
