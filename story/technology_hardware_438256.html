Fashion Daily. You're OUT if you are still showing off your famous bags! It's hard not to love Ipad!

by hjdsk24319 on 2011-05-27 20:32:07

What's the trendiest thing this summer! What should you flaunt while out and about? If you're still blindly chasing after designer handbags and luxurious jewelry, then you're totally out of style! You can only be described as a vulgar person full of "coppery stench"! Although I am still single-mindedly following my beloved "BV Caba woven bag", recently I've been eyeing the CHANEL tweed collarless coat worn by ZING, and I absolutely love it! Beyond these expensive luxury items, an Ipad is definitely a practical and trendy cool product. Today, I want to say that if you stroll around with an Ipad, the attention you'll receive will far surpass carrying an LV handbag!

Perhaps you don't see it much in domestic areas yet, but this little gadget has already become a "street item" in Europe and America. Whether it's Miley Cyrus from the 90s generation, Lindsay Lohan from the Mean Girls, or even racing legend Jenson Button, they are all loyal fans of the Ipad! When I interviewed Li Chen before, I asked him what fashion item he most wanted for Spring/Summer 2010, and his answer was also the Ipad! Talk show queen Oprah Winfrey even gave them as gifts to her employees, reportedly spending nearly ten thousand dollars on prizes! Regardless of what makes this thing so special, not considering its functions, just looking at its appearance is enough to make one's heart race. Look below at how big stars use it as a street "accessory", indeed very "pleasing to the eye"!

Miley Cyrus has long become a fashion darling of the 90s generation. A white tank top, with the lace edge of the bra slightly exposed, carefully showing off skin! Skinny pants and ankle boots are the best partners for elongating legs, and of course, the most eye-catching star of the "Apple" family in her left hand is today's protagonist!

Emma Roberts wears a retro print tee paired with skinny pants on the street, her sequined bag blinging brightly on the streets. The silver Ipad tightly held in her hand doesn't let go! To be honest, that bitten apple logo is indeed the most eye-catching!

Even when attending parties, Lindsay Lohan brings her Ipad along. Perhaps since the last "Vienna no-show" incident, Lohan now keeps a tight grip on her schedule, with this gadget in hand, she won't miss another money-making opportunity again!

Although racing champion Jenson Button always dresses low-key in simple tees and jeans, his toys are anything but lazy. Recently, his newly "acquired" Ipad is something he clutches tightly to his chest no matter what, proudly flaunting his new favorite. Just look at the neon packaging to know how well-protected it is!
