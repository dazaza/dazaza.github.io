After the holiday, many group buying websites have accelerated the speed of product listing.

by beetgk2958 on 2012-02-06 08:35:29

, Beijing group buying After the express industry resumed work after the festival, the enthusiasm of group-buying lovers warmed up again. The other day, the group-buying statistical report of Hangzhou in January issued by Lington.com, an authoritative group-buying navigation website in China, showed that there were totally 12,717 group-buying products in Hangzhou in January, with a total sales volume of 126 million yuan and 1.4885 million buyers. Among them, the top ten group-buying websites accounted for 80% of the total sales volume. However, some group-buying websites also had financing problems after the Spring Festival. Ren Chunlei, founder of TuangouBao.com, denied the rumor of "owing salary and running away" on his Weibo, saying that he was raising money from all sides and there had been some progress. The total amount of group-buying in Hangzhou in January exceeded 1 billion yuan. According to statistics, the total sales volume of group-buying in Hangzhou last month exceeded 1 billion yuan. However, 80% of this sales volume was "contributed" by the top ten group-buying websites in Hangzhou. Among them, JuHuaSuan, the sales champion, provided 610 group-buying products in its home base Hangzhou in January, with a sales volume of 84.8438 million yuan and 900,300 buyers. JuHuaSuan accounted for 67.6% of the total sales volume of group-buying in Hangzhou in January, while other group-buying websites accounted for 32.4% of the total sales volume of group-buying in Hangzhou. The ratio of physical goods group-buying and local service group-buying in the sales volume of the Hangzhou group-buying market last month was 7:3. From the market share of physical goods group-buying and service group-buying, we can see that JuHuaSuan has an absolute advantage in physical goods group-buying. Although the proportion of service group-buying of JuHuaSuan was relatively low at 20.31%, its sales volume of 17.2316 million yuan was equivalent to the sales volume of 19.1706 million yuan of service group-buying of other group-buying websites nationwide. The total sales volume of the top 10 group-buying websites in the Hangzhou group-buying market in January was 101 million yuan, accounting for 80.8% of the total volume of the Hangzhou group-buying market in January. Among the top 10 group-buying websites, except JuHuaSuan, 8 group-buying websites had a sales volume exceeding 1 million yuan in Hangzhou in January, among which Meituan and Qianpin.com became the two group-buying websites with a sales volume exceeding 3 million yuan in that month, with sales volumes of 3.2003 million yuan and 3.0913 million yuan respectively. The best-selling single item of service group-buying in that month was the group-buying product of tourism and hotel, from which we can also see the popularity of tourism and hotel group-buying products during the long holiday of the Spring Festival in January. Some group-buying websites have "run out of food", while others are seeking to go public against the trend. After the Spring Festival, many group-buying websites accelerated the speed of product launch. However, some websites were trapped in the problem of "running out of food". The other day, Ren Chunlei, CEO of TuangouBao.com, denied the rumor of "owing salary and running away" on his Weibo, saying that there had been some progress in the new financing. In the past two years, the financial reserves of TuangouBao.com supported its big spending behavior. Under the condition that the cumulative total amount of financing did not exceed 3 billion yuan RMB, TuangouBao.com encountered financial difficulties. And in the previous year, TuangouBao.com became one of the most watched group-buying websites with an advertising investment of 550 million yuan RMB. However, there are still group-buying websites seeking to go public against the trend. WoWoTuan said at the end of last month that the IPO work was proceeding according to the original plan, and it was waiting for a suitable capital market window period. At the end of last year, many of the top ten group-buying websites were reported to have laid off personnel and closed branches, affecting the overall image of the group-buying industry. Moreover, 30% of the websites have already exited this market. Ren Chunlei said on Weibo that entrepreneurs should be prepared to withstand all the storms. Industry insiders and investors both said that the real test for group-buying websites has arrived.
