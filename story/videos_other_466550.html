Shanghai Electric: A strategy of focusing on key industries will be implemented

by xiaoling4430 on 2011-07-27 11:11:04

At the 2008 annual general meeting of shareholders held yesterday, senior executives of Shanghai Electric stressed that within this year, in accordance with the overall goal of mechatronics integration, they would continue to implement industrial restructuring and mergers and acquisitions. They would also pursue a strategy of focusing on core business development based on industry prosperity and the company's own strength and position.

Shanghai Electric currently has seven major categories of core businesses. Among them, elevators, refrigeration, special engineering machinery, and hydraulic pneumatic equipment saw their main business revenue increase by approximately 23% to 31% year-on-year last year. However, printing and packaging machinery, welding equipment, and plywood businesses declined by double digits due to various reasons. Vice Chairman Fan Bingxun stated that they would adopt a strategy of focusing on key industries, which means concentrating resources on industries with large market capacity, solid foundation, and promising prospects, especially increasing investment in new technologies and equipment. Newly appointed General Manager Chen Hong of Shanghai Electric also pointed out that, around the overall development strategy framework of Shanghai Electric Group, the company would continue to implement industrial restructuring, strengthen resource allocation, and seize opportunities for low-cost expansion under the current economic situation to carry out necessary mergers and acquisitions and expand the scale of the industry.

It is understood that as the most profitable industry sector of Shanghai Electric at present, the elevator industry will continue to be highly regarded. Special engineering machinery and hydraulic pneumatic systems, as the fastest-growing sectors in recent years, will receive key support. Although the printing and packaging machinery industry was significantly affected by the financial crisis last year, the company's management is optimistic about the industry's development trend and thus holds considerable confidence in this industry, which already has a relatively complete industrial chain. It is reported that despite the current challenging economic situation, the board of directors of Shanghai Electric still requires the management team to achieve an estimated 10 billion yuan in main business revenue and nearly 1.8 billion yuan in main business profit, meaning that the company will achieve an operating target basically in line with the previous year.
