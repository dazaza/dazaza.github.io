Introduction to the Plot of "The Love That Lasts a Lifetime":

In the 1930s, in Shanghai, the Bai family, once extremely prosperous with their ancestry tracing back to the Qing dynasty's Hanlin Academy, has been declining year by year, living off their savings until they can barely afford daily life. In arranging a decent wedding for her sixth granddaughter Bai Liushu, Old Lady Bai had to ask all branches of the family for financial contributions. However, the third and fourth sons' families, Bai Liangyue and Bai Liangyou, respectively, kept passing the buck, and the sisters-in-law were haggling over small accounts, making Bai Liushu experience the coldness of the world for the first time. Bai Liushu married into the Tang family, one of the top nouveaux riches in Shanghai at the time, marrying Master Tang Yiyuan. This marriage was sensational at the time.
