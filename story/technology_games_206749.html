Who can诉 the bitter tears of Emei?

by lk0gamek on 2009-07-20 00:40:14

First of all, wish everyone a happy New Year and have fun in the game!

Time flies, it has been several months since I started playing Tian Long. If I'm not mistaken, it was around early January 2008 when I started. From knowing nothing at the beginning to now having a little understanding, every day after work or on holidays, when I have nothing to do, I use the game as entertainment, just to pass the time.

When I first started this game, I was really confused, with no one to guide me, I had to explore everything slowly by myself! Back then, I didn't even know what "three rings" were, let alone other things! Later, I pulled my classmates in to play with me.

Let me express my own feelings: I think this game is really unique, male Emei (Buddhist nun sect) and female Shaolin (monk sect), I won't say more about others, haha! Maybe this is one of the characteristics of this game? Even in such a game, there are so many "yao yao" (female characters)! It's difficult to find a wife in the game, could it be because I lack charm? Friends who play "yao yao", please don't scold me, haha! Just mentioning it in passing, it's actually nothing, I've also played "yao yao" characters. I remember when I was playing Sword World 2, I always played "yao yao", can't help it, all the Emei disciples there are female, and I like those two professions! Now Tian Long has given me this opportunity, I am very grateful.

When I was level 10, I thought for a long time, which sect should I join? I looked at forums and asked friends in the game, and finally made the final decision: Emei. Thus, I proudly joined Emei and became a male Emei flute player. I choose, I like, no regrets! Since I have chosen the horizon, then what remains for the earth can only be my back.

When I was level 29, I had saved enough to upgrade to level 30 but didn't. In the game, I found a master. Who specifically? Let's keep that confidential, give him some dignity! But anyway, I still thank the old man (now he might be dead, haven't seen him online for a long time, haha). After all, he did take me under his wing. At the time, after finding a master, the happiness was indescribable, feeling warm inside, smiling happily outside. After I became his disciple, he took me up a level once, bringing me to level 34, which wasn't bad either, after all, he brought me up four levels, once again expressing my gratitude! I also have an elder brother, who was there the time he took me. The master told us: "I will only take you two as disciples, no more." Another wave of joy surged in my heart, feeling like I had gained a treasure! Later, when I asked the master to take me, he always said he didn't have time. Actually, I can understand that, after all, he also needs to play the game and level up, right? But I noticed several times, when I was online, he didn't call me, secretly taking my elder brother to level up instead. At the time, my heart almost broke, it felt really unpleasant! Why? Later, I finally learned the reason from my elder brother, which was also confirmed by the master's actions. Originally, my elder brother promised to give him high-level gems, no wonder, people die for wealth, birds die for food, why are you poor? My doubts were finally resolved: the master asked me for things several times, and told me what your elder brother gave me, what do you give me? My answer was: If I had it, wouldn't I respect and offer it to you, Master? Here I ask everyone: Is being a master to ask for things from disciples? Later, when I reached a higher level, I also found a disciple. At least I never asked my disciple for anything, whatever I have, I give to him. Since it's a game, we're all here for entertainment, happiness is the most important. Being poor doesn't mean lacking ambition!
