A passenger fell from the bus when it was crossing the bridge, and the old man forced the door open and squeezed out.

by mlwang on 2011-12-13 14:29:30

You don't stop the bus, I will jump off. I will open the door first and then get out! This is the astonishing move made by a domineering old man surnamed Huang recently to protest against the bus driver not stopping the bus. The old man began to pry open the car door, creating a crack, and then squeezed out of it, almost entirely going outside.

Recently, a video provided by the Security Department of Minyun Fuzhou Bus No.2 Company has gone viral online. The video shows that after a passenger failed to get off at the station on time, he forcibly pried open the car door to get off when the bus left the station.

The video lasts for 51 seconds and presents the general process of the incident. The video shows that the old man surnamed Huang was sitting in the second seat in front of the TV behind the driver's side. When the car door opened, another passenger was preparing to get off at the car door, while Mr. Huang was just getting up to leave his seat. When Mr. Huang reached the car door, it had already closed, and the bus had started moving.

Subsequently, Mr. Huang grabbed the car door with both hands, facing the driver's direction, indicating that he wanted to get off. Seeing that the driver didn't stop the bus, Mr. Huang began to beat the car door. Then he grabbed the handles of the two car doors, put his foot against the car door, and pulled towards the inside of the car. A few seconds later, seeing that the car door couldn't be pulled open, Mr. Huang leaned sideways, inserting one hand and one foot into the crack of the door, pulling with the other hand. After prying it open a little, Mr. Huang moved his body into the gap created by prying open the door, eventually squeezing more than half of his body outside, leaving only one hand inside grabbing something. While the car was still moving, seeing that Mr. Huang had already extended more than half of his body outside, the driver opened the car door. The video finally shows that after Mr. Huang landed, the video ended. Director Liu of the Security Department said that when the bus turned off the engine, it could no longer be monitored.

The driver said that at that time, the car was driving very slowly, and he kept persuading the passenger. Since the two bus stations were relatively close, they would arrive at the next station soon, but Mr. Huang kept prying the car door forcefully. Later, seeing that most of Mr. Huang's body was outside the car, he slowly stopped the car and opened the car door, but possibly due to inertia, Mr. Huang lost his balance and fell down.

Director Zhou of the Quality Service Department of Minyun Fuzhou Bus No.2 Company said that according to regulations, the bus is prohibited from opening the door halfway between stations from the departure station to the next station. This is to ensure the safety of passengers, as getting off halfway can easily cause accidents. However, Director Zhou admitted that the parking time at the Cha Yuan Bridge Station was 10 seconds, slightly faster than the specified time. For Mr. Huang's such extreme special behavior, the driver may not have known what to do at the moment.

Yesterday, Mr. Huang told the reporter that at that time, he found his money stolen at Cha Yuan Bridge Station, looked for it but couldn't find it, and saw the bus starting. So he ran to the car door to indicate that he wanted to get off, but the driver never opened the door. Because he was anxious, he pried open the car door himself, but the driver never stopped the car, so he got off and was injured.
