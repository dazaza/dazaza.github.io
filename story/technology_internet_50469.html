Baidu Hi will adopt a Gmail-style invitation registration method for public testing.

by e_wuqing on 2008-03-03 10:48:03

"Do not be too fixated on what kind of model is a good model, as long as it can provide the best service to people." This statement made by Pony Ma on July 13th, 2006 at the "2006 Baidu World" conference perhaps foreshadowed Baidu's earliest move into Instant Messaging (IM).

The much-anticipated instant messaging software (IM) from Baidu has finally revealed its true form. Baidu confirmed yesterday to our newspaper that the Baidu IM product is officially named "Baidu Hi," and began restricted internal testing on February 29, 2008.

Unlike some online media reports suggesting an all-staff internal test, Baidu confirmed that employees must submit an application to the IM product team through the company intranet and obtain login permissions before they can download the software package address. Currently, Baidu has not disclosed the specific scale of the planned number of participants in the internal testing.

At the same time, Baidu reminded netizens that since Baidu's instant messaging product Hi has entered the restricted internal testing phase, some lawless individuals have started using this hotspot event to spread Trojan viruses or engage in account trading activities. Baidu stated that Baidu Hi currently does not offer any public external downloads, and hopes that netizens remain vigilant to avoid becoming victims of illegal actions.

Baidu claims that it has received tens of thousands of emails and phone inquiries about the status of Baidu IM, with many hoping to try out this product. To ensure delivering a more perfect and responsible product to netizens, Baidu Hi is currently only in the internal testing phase within the company, and the exact launch date in the future remains undetermined.

Internal messages from Baidu indicate that after this restricted internal testing, Baidu will adopt a viral method similar to Gmail invitation registration for public beta testing, gradually expanding the user base.

Sources close to Baidu's management confirm that Baidu IM has been clearly defined as a "strategic-level" product for the company. All aspects from product design, development, to testing are independently completed by Baidu, breaking previous rumors that Baidu might enter the IM field through acquisitions or mergers. According to an insider close to Baidu’s senior management, Baidu has been substantially involved in IM product research and development for at least a year, with extensive efforts put into maintaining project confidentiality.

Based on the internal test installation program of "Baidu Hi" obtained through "internal relations" by industry insiders, the installation process of Baidu Hi is very simple, without bundling any other related Baidu products. Information displayed during the installation process shows that the current version of Baidu Hi is 1.0 Beta1. After logging in, users can search and add friends, add friends via email addresses, and group contacts according to their needs. Once successfully added, users can chat with their contacts/friends. In addition, Baidu Hi also supports group chat functionality. Like other instant messaging software, Baidu Hi allows users to customize their chat interface settings.

Industry insiders who tested the Baidu Hi 1.0 Beta1 version said that it's currently unclear how Baidu Hi differs from existing instant messaging tools, except for the absence of pop-up ads that often trouble users.

Recalling that Tencent CEO Pony Ma personally attended the "2006 Baidu World" conference held in Beijing on July 13, 2006 and engaged in a mutually appreciative dialogue with Baidu CEO Robin Li, the industry is curious whether Baidu Hi and Tencent QQ will reach some form of cooperation in the future.

According to the 2007 China Internet Survey Statistics, the current size of the Chinese IM market has reached 4.75 billion RMB, and it is expected to reach 8.76 billion RMB by 2009, with an annual market growth rate exceeding 30%. Currently, in China's IM market, Tencent and MSN have formed a monopoly advantage.

Industry analysts believe that the strategic value of Baidu Hi for Baidu lies in: connecting the intrinsic relationships between Baidu's news search, web search, image search, mp3 search, Baidu Knows, Baidu Tieba, and Baidu Space products, and transforming them into a comprehensive advertising platform to retain users and open up more revenue sources.

Upon learning that Baidu IM has started internal testing, industry experts and analysts have expressed various opinions. Founder of 5G Consulting Hong Bo stated that Baidu entering the IM domain is beneficial for breaking the original monopolistic structure in this sector. President of Zhenwang Consulting Lu Bowen pointed out that Baidu's entry into the instant messaging market at the historical moment when China's internet population surpasses 200 million holds significant importance for pushing China's internet from competition towards a cooperative era.

Some netizens, however, have differing views on Baidu Hi, arguing that there are already many IM tools available, and if Baidu Hi's function is merely chatting, it lacks appeal. Additionally, the name "Baidu Hi" is quite similar to Baidu Space's hi.baidu, and gives off a feeling of just "greeting", which seems rather rustic.
