It is enough to have a bosom friend

Do you think there are true bosom friends in this world? Some people say yes, some say no. The following story tells us all! They met online, and it was also coincidental. This story is an original one. It can be shared and reprinted. Here I wish everyone a happy day. Whether you like my blog or not, please take a serious look!

One day, a boy was bored at home and surfing the Internet. Suddenly, a girl added him as a friend. The girl asked, "Where are you from?" The boy ignored the girl. The girl saw that the boy was always online on QQ and felt curious. She wanted to get to know him. The boy actually hung QQ to eliminate loneliness and because of certain things he had no job to do and could only stare at the computer. The boy's friends also helped him find a job, but he refused them all.

The girl often paid attention to the dynamics published by the boy on QQ space, just wanting to know what the boy had experienced. However, the boy never chatted with the girl. He even refused to reply to every friend who chatted with him. Later, the boy got annoyed and deleted them all. Why was the boy like this? Please continue reading!

Remember that it was a Christmas day. After work, the boy went to his girlfriend's company to pick her up. They had agreed on it. In a very short moment, the boy received a text message. The boy was stunned. The message said, "Dear, this is the last text message I will send you. We won't contact each other anymore. Don't ask me why. Don't come find me!" The boy immediately used a public phone to call the girl and asked, "This is the last time I call you, please don't hang up. I just want to know why?"

Let's meet for the last time. No matter what the result is, I have to see you once." His girlfriend agreed. The boy was thinking all the way why they would break up after five years of relationship. They met. At that time, the girl brought a particularly handsome boy and said to the boy intimately: "You useless thing, you gave me nothing after we were together for five years, we will not contact each other anymore," the handsome man next to the girl scolded the boy: "Friend, if I were you, I would crawl into the wall crack, men like you will never get true love." The boy looked at this scene, then hesitated and said: "Wish you happiness."

Then he turned around and left! On the way back, the boy was very sad, and drank a lot of wine that night. The next day when the boy woke up, he decided not to have a girlfriend for the rest of his life. The boy hung QQ to pass the time and relieve loneliness, and the boy often wrote diaries!

The girl said again to the boy: "A person who often hangs QQ has been hit hard inside. I don't know if I can enter your inner world. A person will experience a lot of things in his life." After seeing this, the boy didn't know why, but he actually said a sentence to the girl, "Do you really want to be friends with me?" The girl happily said, "I am willing to be your bosom friend, can you introduce yourself?" Afterwards, they often chatted. Later, the boy learned that they were in the same high school before, just not in the same grade. They often video chatted, and later the boy was deeply moved by the girl.

He started to like the girl a little. But the girl said a sentence, "You can't like me, because I'm already married, I just want to have a good male friend in my life." The boy has matured. He understands this kind of situation. Later, he said to the girl, "Are you willing to be my bosom friend?" Surprisingly, the girl agreed and said, "This is our secret!" Because the girl likes to watch the boy smoke and smile on video chat, she also wants the boy to hug her. So-called affection grows with time!

One day, they met. The boy was very handsome, and the girl was also very pretty. They didn't eat, just accompanied the girl shopping all the time. Because people couldn't see clearly at night! So the boy kept chatting with her in the community under the building where the girl lived.

It was very cold. The girl let the boy sit next to her and lean on her. Later, the boy hugged the girl. For about half an hour. The girl was very satisfied. Finally, the boy sent the girl home. So they often contacted. Bad things need not be said. The girl's husband knew and insisted on divorce with the girl. The boy felt very guilty, affecting the girl's happiness. Later, he received a gift from his ex-girlfriend. It was a wedding invitation.

The boy really couldn't bear these things. So he went to find that shameless woman. When he walked into the wedding hall of his ex-girlfriend, he was very angry. But loving someone is to bless them deeply in your heart, not to possess them forever. Seeing the girl finish her wedding without eating dinner, he left!

Later, the boy received a letter, "Are you XXX?" "XX has died!" It was sent by the best friend of the boy's ex-girlfriend. There was a reason why she left you at that time. She had a terminal illness and didn't want to drag you down. She just wanted to find someone to end this life, and XX's husband turned out to be a drug dealer. At this moment, the boy's heart was truly buried deep inside. Such a lover, such a good boy, why is he so unfortunate. The girl who often chatted with him later also stayed away from him...
