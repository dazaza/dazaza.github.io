"Return to Love" - the emotional entanglement between a Chinese woman and a Japanese flight instructor.

by jqrxass on 2009-09-17 14:59:49

"The main storyline of "Return to Love" is the romance between Qi Qi and Wan Yan Hong Hong. Additionally, the book delves into the emotional entanglements between Gao Yuan and Zhang Yiwen, Wang Lihui and Zheng Xiaona, the changes in Dai Liying's fate, Shi Qing's foreign exchange dealings, the exposure of civil aviation secrets, and the unbreakable love and hatred between the Japanese and Chinese people. The unresolved love and hate of the older generation torments two passionate hearts.

Grandpa passed away suddenly, as cherry blossoms fell. Where should I go from here? How much courage does it take to protect a sincere love?

Return to Love - Part One

After so many years, I've come back again. The tremendous changes over time are etched into the land of the airport. As I touch the marks of time on my face, I see my 20-year-old self in a daze. Back then, the city’s roads weren’t as wide, the buildings weren’t as tall, and the nights weren’t as colorful, but spring was filled with birdsong and dancing flowers, and autumn skies were clear blue. Back then, I had rosy smiles and eyes like dawn, my young heart yearned for freedom, longing to fly freely...

Return to Love - Part Two

It was very quiet around me, only the sound of 'thump thump thump' could be heard, unclear whether it was his heartbeat or mine. In the cold winter air, we felt each other's warmth, breathing in each other's scent, and at that moment, I felt all the torment I had endured dissipate instantly.

Return to Love - Part Three

The tear stains on his face pierced my heart, and I turned and rushed out of the door. After dating all winter, this was the first time I had come to him on my own initiative and left alone in such a dejected manner. I swore it would also be the last time. If love cannot be openly and sincerely possessed by both parties, what's the point of keeping it?"
