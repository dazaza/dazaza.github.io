Legend of the Online Game: The Path of Gods

After the nuclear war, the Earth's sky was perpetually covered in dust. Neither the sun nor the moon could be seen, there was no blue sky or white clouds. A once beautiful blue planet had turned into a ghostly wasteland abandoned by gods and despised by ghosts. Just as humanity’s spiritual life was at its most deprived, a game called "The Universe" appeared out of nowhere and quickly swept across the globe.

And it was through this game that Shadow achieved his legendary life...

In the game, as a priest, Shadow gradually explored the essence of magic—starting from using skills to cast spells, then progressing to using incantations, and finally reaching the stage of instant spellcasting... Step by step, Shadow advanced, exploring until he reached the eternal peak.

Within this world, you will find that both martial arts and magic in the game are real; as long as certain conditions are met, all realities can manifest in the real world.
