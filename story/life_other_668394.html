Passed by, stopped

Times Online Submission

Forgetting you, I can't do it!

I once gave myself a deadline to forget you, the deadline has passed but your memory seems never to have disappeared. I asked myself why, why is this so. Facing such an immature, such an ignorant, such a thoughtless you who doesn't care about others' feelings, I ask myself why I still think of you, miss you, and worry about you?

My heart always hangs on your occasional care, I always burst into tears at your unintentional greetings, even analyzing every word and punctuation mark in your four-word sentence with one punctuation mark, what each word means, even one punctuation mark, one punctuation mark, oh... I am so stupid, so humble, so deeply in love with you. But, do you? Do you know? I always remember our past, holding hands in the campus, intimacy under the green shade, you always look at me with deep affection, quietly, you say that you like looking at me like this, seeing only me in your eyes, and only you in my eyes, I am very happy, very happy. This sentence once made my body go limp and the air seemed tense and ostentatious. I think at that time, we were so in love with each other.

Sometimes, a turn, really is a lifetime!

The most painful distance is when you are not by my side, yet in my heart. I don't want to calculate how many years I've been in love with you, because then I would feel guilty towards others. Staying rooted in someone else's heart not only needs love but also courage. When you hurt me, I simultaneously hurt another person who loves me. I want to forget you, but I don't want to force myself to forget you. I can't forget, I can't forget the vows and forever that you once gave me. Darling, what should I do, how should I cherish our past? I can't forget, I really can't forget. I always find your shadow in others, I always see many versions of you on the street, watching you getting ready to board the bus, watching you being intimate with other girls, watching you sitting on the stone bench looking at the sky, watching you, watching you, all I see is you. I miss you so much, I'm so obsessed with you. And you, are you also thinking of a girl who likes daydreaming as much as you do? Darling, I am so sad, so sad. Why don't you come back? Can't we go back to the past? Can't we be close again? Why are you so ruthless, so heartless? Is it because I'm not good enough? Is it? Is it? Tell me, please tell me, isn't it okay?

Is it because I can't keep up with your steps or time flies too fast and makes you forget to take me with you? Does my unwavering loyalty make you feel suffocated? Am I taking away your freedom? Has my care for you turned into an unbridgeable chasm between us? But I really love you so much. I just express my love, and you don't want it anymore? You don't want me anymore? You don't want our past or our past vows anymore? Your departure was so decisive. Why don't you look back at me, at this tearful me? Am I not worth your lingering thoughts!

No one will understand, that there is a you like this in this world. Only you can make me reminisce, only you can make me drunk with emotion.

I named our story "Secret Garden". Inside, there are only you and me, no one else. Every moment between you and me will not just be in the past. I will store these vows, these stories of blooming seasons in my garden. There are the promises you made to me, your care for me, your attachment to me, your deep love for me. I will protect you, even if you don't love me anymore, I will still love you well, love you like this, I know love is selfless, I won't be capricious anymore, I won't cling to you anymore. But, darling, please be happier than me, okay! I will always love you.
