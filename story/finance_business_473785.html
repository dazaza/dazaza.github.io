The more I think about some things, the more confused I get, the more I feel that many things are beyond the comprehension of my simple mind. Moreover, things have already gotten out of control, otherwise Mrs. Lan wouldn't be sweating cold sweat.

For example, right now, the sharpness and hatred, as well as anger in her eyes, have left her speechless. She just stared at me, and the Joe behind me, and then left in a huff after not getting a satisfactory answer from her.

Meng Yao, perhaps finding Meng Yao will lead to some important clues. I sincerely hope Meng Yao is still alive. Being alive is better than anything.

However, my stomach isn't good, I can't drink too much, otherwise I'll lose control and have diarrhea again. Sigh, everything is hard to balance, even drinking requires consideration.
