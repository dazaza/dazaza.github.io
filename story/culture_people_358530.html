The mahjong game ended around 3 pm because the banquet was about to start. I lost 1700 yuan, and that "Aunt" won about 2000 yuan. During the meal, since I had become quite familiar with "Aunt" through mahjong, I naturally sat at the same table as her. During the meal, I asked her if she would continue playing mahjong after the meal. She said no, because playing for too long made her headache. I said I would continue, as losing money means more money at home, right? She said watching me play would be a kind of entertainment for her, and she could also help me serve tea and water. She even joked that if I won, I should treat her to a late-night snack. I said no problem, absolutely!

In the afternoon, my mahjong luck was still not ideal, so naturally, my mood was a bit worse. Every time it was my turn to play, Aunt sat behind me and leaned her head on my shoulder to help me analyze my hand. Sometimes, maybe because her body was too tilted, she unconsciously placed her hand on my thigh. At first, I didn't pay much attention, but once, when I discarded the wrong tile and got dealt a big hand by the opponent, "Aunt" actually lightly pinched my inner thigh and said I shouldn't have discarded that tile. If I hadn't, the next player wouldn't have picked it up, and the opponent wouldn't have won such a big hand. However, when she pinched me, I had a physiological reaction and became very hard. After "Aunt" pinched me and took her hand away, she might not have noticed my reaction. But before my little friend calmed down, her hand was back on my thigh again, accurately landing directly on my proud and upright little friend. "Aunt" immediately felt it and quickly withdrew her hand. However, she lightly patted my back and said to me, "You little pig, how can you not lose money playing mahjong? Because you're absent-minded!" I didn't know how to respond, so I just turned and smiled at her. The moment our eyes met, my heart rate quickened a bit. I thought there might be a chance with this "Aunt", so I said to her, "Aunt, thank you for helping me serve tea today. After the mahjong game is over, I'll invite you to drink tea." "Aunt" readily agreed. The fellow mahjong players at the same table kept praising my good character in playing mahjong, saying that even though I lost so much money, I still remembered "Aunt's" hard work. Haha!
