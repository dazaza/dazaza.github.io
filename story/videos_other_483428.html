Sina's marriage with JD.com to launch "Weishopping" prospects unclear

by jdhgsa078 on 2011-08-30 16:37:39

In recent years, Weibo has become very popular, but how to make a profit has been a headache for Sina. The other day, Sina Weibo cooperated with JD.com to launch Weibuying. Consumers will be able to shop through the window of JD.com set up on Sina Weibo. Industry opinions are mixed; some say it's just a normal advertising cooperation, but many people have shown great interest in this form of cooperation and consider it a new way for Sina Weibo to accelerate profitability. However, whether the two parties can effectively combine remains to be tested.

Sina Weibo's Urgent Desire to Profit?

Weibo, although a very popular concept, has been a headache for Sina regarding how to make a profit. Previously, Sina launched Micropay to explore commercialization, and later invested $40 million in Tudou, which the industry said was also for accelerating Weibo's profitability. Now, with the launch of micro e-commerce and Weibuying, it may also be a sign of its urgent desire to profit.

An unnamed insider in the industry stated that first of all, from the market effect, the cooperation between the two parties is a very sharp and smart move. However, if Sina Weibo really wants to cooperate with B2C, it might as well choose any F-commerce model. With the current cooperation with JD.com, the advantages that Weibo should have are not reflected. This cooperation seems more beneficial to JD.com, and it is hard to see what advantage Sina gains.

This insider speculated that this might be a step taken by Sina due to the urgency of making Weibo products profitable. However, he believed that instead of cooperating with JD.com, it would be better for Sina to go for an F-commerce platform. This cooperation is questionable for Sina.

However, he also indicated that the prospects of the cooperation cannot be determined at present, and it depends on how the product plan specifically is. For now, we can only observe.

Trial Phase, Unclear Form

A netizen who has tried the new function of the cooperation between the two parties said that when entering the promotion page, there is a prompt that becoming a fan of JD.com now will get you one coupon. After clicking to follow, there will be a prompt to log in first and return to the homepage to successfully add attention. When returning to the promotional page, it is found that you need to forward again to get the coupon, indicating operational bugs and lack of credibility in promotion. However, insiders in the industry said that although it is rudimentary, it is after all the first step.

The COO of EC Training Network, Liang Hongwei, stated that Sina Weibo gave JD.com a promotional opening, and everyone said the Weibo e-commerce era has arrived. He feels that this function is too simple, shopping and Weibo are still not integrated, like two separate layers. Sina is also boldly thinking and carefully trying. Still afraid that the shopping atmosphere will affect the Weibo experience. Weibo and e-commerce will surely have deeper integration in the future. He also stated that both Sina and JD.com are experimenting. He is very curious to know, after this novelty phase, how much traffic and conversion rate JD.com will bring from Weibo.

For this new method, many people in the industry have given affirmation. Analyst You Tianyu of iResearch Consulting Group believed: The most direct effect of the two parties' cooperation is to facilitate consumers. From JD.com's perspective, using Sina Weibo's good user resources is a very nice way to display products. From Sina's perspective, Sina will not engage in the e-commerce industry in the short term.
