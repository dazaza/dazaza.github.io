PK Reasons Improved Significantly

This year's Kuai Girl competition places great emphasis on selecting opponents for PK battles. In previous competitions, various reasons for selecting opponents became hot topics among netizens. In this match, it seemed like the director deliberately arranged it, requiring the four Kuai Girls to select challenge contestants in the first round. Yuke Wei chose Li Xiaoyun, her "rival" from the last game, because "last time I PK'd her and I chose her again, where I fell, I'll get up," while Li Xiaoyun chose a new opponent, "I choose Jiang Yingrong, I want to choose someone with comprehensive strength"; Jiang Yingrong and Li Xiaoyun were in sync, "the Ballad Prince will have a great challenge." Anyway, this time the PK reasons seemed to have been carefully thought out by everyone, unlike the last two games where the reasons were too ridiculous, leaving behind talk for the netizens to discuss.

Jiang Yingrong Three Times Cover Girl

The first round PK was exceptionally intense. In previous matches, Huang Ying, Yuke Wei, and Li Xiaoyun were all quiet types who sang quietly, but they all had to challenge dancing. Jiang Yingrong was originally a dance expert, but she still chose the more difficult Indian song "JIA HO", her collaboration with the Latin dance master had slight flaws, but she still defeated Li Xiaoyun, who again challenged rap music, with a large margin. In this match, Jiang Yingrong once again won the cover girl title and was the first to enter the finals. Whether it was the initial jazz dance or the extremely cool stool dance, it seemed like a new generation of dancing queen was born from this stage.

Rivals Meet Again, Yuke Wei Eliminated

The biggest highlight of this match was the repeated PK between Yuke Wei and Li Xiaoyun, "not rivals, not meeting." In the first round dance PK, the three judges chose Li Xiaoyun, and Yuke Wei subsequently turned the tables, winning the second round duet segment. In the third round "killer move" segment, Yuke Wei's rendition of "Itch" moved the hearts of the audience, defeating Li Xiaoyun by a large margin again. However, Yuke Wei did not laugh until the end. Among the six eliminated Kuai Girls, one was chosen to advance directly, and the decisive vote was cast by Liu Xi Jun, who was eliminated by Yuke Wei in the previous episode. Without hesitation, Liu Xi Jun raised Li Xiaoyun's hand, sending her directly into the top three. In the PK against Huang Ying, Yuke Wei unexpectedly was overtaken by Huang Ying's votes, regrettably stopping at the top three. She gave a deep bow to all her beloved audiences and disappeared from this stage she loved.

We cannot assess the social value and influence created by talent shows, but we have recognized a batch of overnight sensation talent show stars such as Li Yuchun, Zhang Liangying, Zhang Jie, and Ji Jie. From Super Girl, Kuai Boy to Absolute Singing, talent shows emerged, dominating the ratings king throne for several years. In 2009, "Happy Girl" made a strong comeback, with rule reforms, shortened competition periods, cancellation of text message voting... All these changes were calmly faced by Hunan TV.

Based on the five regions of previous Super Girls, the 2009 Happy Girl expanded to 18 ground-based singing zones across the country, integrating 18 cities including Changsha, Nanjing, Chengdu, Shenyang, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, Lanzhou, Kunming, Hangzhou to form a Happy Alliance. In addition, six major online direct access areas led by the official website Golden Eagle Network were opened, conducting a carpet-style search for contestant selection.

The "Happy Alliance" is considered a countermeasure to deal with the regulations of the State Administration of Radio, Film, and Television regarding talent shows. The events of the "Kuai Girl" zones will not be satellite live broadcasted, instead, they will cooperate with local stations, solving the problem of the "Kuai Girl" preliminary rounds and first rounds not being able to be broadcasted nationwide through the publicity and reporting efforts of local stations, compensating for the lack of national impact. Under tight time constraints, "broad sowing and broad harvesting" is also a good choice.

In fact, from the current results, the "Happy Alliance" has made a huge contribution to the "Kuai Girl" competition. From the start of registration, local TV stations have specifically created a program to promote the "Kuai Girl". Afterward, the 18 cooperative TV stations and six online media outlets have provided full support for every match, effectively spreading the "Kuai Girl" competition. After the top 300 gathered in Changsha, under the "limited satellite broadcasting" situation, the collective efforts of 18 ground media and six online media not only broadcasted the eight "breakthrough matches" within the largest range possible, but also sent media personnel to Changsha for interviews, creating a media craze, reflecting a powerful dissemination effect, and "Happy Girl" had already firmly attracted the eyes of a large number of viewers before going on satellite.
