Website Optimization by Yi Xiang: How to Make Your Website as Perfect as Possible

by jackntav9612 on 2012-02-28 17:23:30

1. Poor inclusion focuses on website structure: The inclusion volume of the website http://www.glechina.com.cn/glass mold prevention is small, so we should pay attention to the theme of the website first, because the theme of many sites determines that the content that can be expanded is not very broad, such as http://www.glechina.com.cn/mold-proof powder. In this case, a small amount of inclusion does not affect the income of the website. If it is a large site and the website weight is already very high but the inclusion is still very few, such as http://www.glechina.com.cn/glass mildew remover, then we should consider the internal structure problem of the website. We can start from analyzing the website log, analyze the internal link construction, make structural adjustments, and the inclusion will gradually increase.

2. Poor ranking requires "steady progress": Many webmasters blame the decline in website rankings or inability to rank on the lack of external links. However, if you analyze other websites, you will find that many sites have very few external links but still have good rankings. External links just need to be updated regularly. Ranking is not something that happens overnight. You must know that website ranking needs both "internal and external cultivation", and internal optimization is the root. So don't put too much energy into external links.

3. Low traffic focuses on promotion methods: Website traffic sources are multifaceted. If the website mainly relies on SEO for traffic, then we should see whether the long-tail keywords of the website are well done, because long-tail keyword traffic often accounts for the majority of the website's traffic. As long as the main keywords rank well, there is no need to worry. Long-tail keywords require effort from selection to optimization. In addition, soft article promotion is also an important way of traffic. When using soft article promotion, we should focus on the quality of the soft article, whether the post has been deleted, and whether the posting position is appropriate.

4. Poor conversion rate focuses on website content: Only when the content of a website is credible and substantial can its advertisements be convincing, leading to clicks and purchases. If the website content is original but of poor quality or irrelevant to the website theme, users will distrust your website, and the success of such a website is predictable. Besides website content, the aesthetics of the website is also an important factor affecting conversion. No matter how good the website ranking is, or how large the traffic is, without a comfortable appearance, the conversion rate will be pitifully low.
