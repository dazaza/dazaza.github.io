4.0 is still far away, recommendation of 2.3 Android smartphones within 2000 yuan

by bhjdfghjsf41 on 2012-02-09 22:11:30

By Wang Yanan

Every Android upgrade causes quite a stir, especially the latest 4.0 version which has captured the hearts of many. Models that can be upgraded and their users are celebrating joyfully, while those unable to upgrade or whose preferred model brands do not offer upgrades start to complain and feel frustrated. However, there's no need to let this affect your mood because with the current versions 2.3 or even 2.2, they are more than sufficient for our daily operation needs. Whether an upgrade is possible should be faced with an attitude of going with the flow. It can be seen that apart from a few well-known manufacturers clearly stating that they will provide future upgrades to version 4.0, most other models have yet to provide any definite news. This means that Android 4.0 still has some distance from the general public. So why not take a look at the eight recommended smartphones within 2000 yuan running on version 2.3 that we present today? They not only offer excellent value for money but also won't disappoint you in terms of practicality.

Lenovo LePhone A500 (specifications pricing forum software)

The Lenovo LePhone A500 is a new model launched in the fourth quarter of last year. From its model number, you can tell it also belongs to the well-known LePhone series. The phone itself is positioned towards the masses and aimed at entry-level users, so it performs quite well in terms of price. However, a mass-market price does not mean simple configuration. This LePhone A500 features the popular Android 2.3 smart operating system, along with GPS functionality, offering good performance in both daily operations and overall practicality.

The Lenovo LePhone A500 adopts a mainstream straight-board touch design, with a simple and stylish overall design, available in two color options: white and black. The front of the phone is equipped with a 3.5-inch capacitive touchscreen with a resolution of 320x480 pixels, providing average display results. Inside, the phone runs on the Android 2.3 smart operating system with a vast selection of applications. The rear camera has a pixel count of 5 million, with imaging quality that can meet everyone's daily shooting needs.
