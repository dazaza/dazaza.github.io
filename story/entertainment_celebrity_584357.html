In her Weibo post, An Wen said that she would do her best to help Su Yue. Although they were not married, there was true love between them.

by edivista on 2011-11-24 20:19:05

Click to enter An Wen's Weibo

Su Yue, the creator of "Loess Plateau", was recently found guilty of contract fraud by the Beijing No. 2 Intermediate People's Court and sentenced to life imprisonment. On the morning of November 14th, An Wen, who lived with Su Yue for many years and played Qingwen in the 1987 version of "Dream of the Red Chamber", launched her Weibo account, stating that she had never had a legally recognized marital relationship with Su Yue and did not want to be divorced anymore. She also emphasized that she would not only help Su Yue but also those who had been hurt and suffered losses because of Su Yue. (Click on the picture to enter An Wen's Weibo)

An Wen first denied in her Weibo post that she had ever evaded responsibility, saying, "From the first day this matter occurred, I have always been here! I have never dodged! And I have never thought of escaping!" She also stressed that she had never had any legally recognized or valid marital relationship with Su Yue and did not want to be divorced anymore. An Wen also said that whether or not we truly had a spousal relationship, in terms of our unwavering true love, it is just a meaningless formality! I don't care about it at all!

An Wen emphasized, "Thank you all! I will work hard to make things develop positively and effectively, not only to help Su Yue, but also to help those who have been hurt and suffered losses because of Su Yue! There are still some ways now, which are under discussion, but we also need everyone's support and help very much! Thank you all again!"

An Wen finally stated that she would participate in Sina's micro-interview in the afternoon today, hoping that everyone would join together to discuss the most perfect, credible, and effective methods.
