Century Fengyi Handan Music and Dance School: the cradle of children's art.

by endifer on 2008-09-05 17:10:53

Shijifengyi Music School is a long-term formal professional school. It is an ideal place for the physical and mental training of teenagers and children. The teaching equipment is complete, the faculty strength is strong, and the rehearsal hall is equipped with sound, air conditioning, piano and other rehearsal facilities. Teachers graduated from Central Conservatory of Music, China Coal Mine Art School, Hebei Normal University and other prestigious universities, with rich teaching experience. They have led students to participate in various provincial and national dance competitions many times and won various honorary awards.

Shijifengyi Music Examination Training School

This dance class recruits all year round, four hours of training time per week. Teaching content: ballet basic training, mat work, classical dance and various ethnic dances. In addition, a ballet body shaping class (no age limit) is also offered. In order to make parents understand our semi-annual open class, we report the children's achievements to the parents, allowing parents to evaluate our teaching quality (we promptly solve and adopt the problems and suggestions raised by parents).

Dear parents, do you want your child to have a healthy figure and elegant temperament? Do you want your child to have an artistic specialty and lay the foundation for their growth? Then, come and sign up quickly, let's dance together!
