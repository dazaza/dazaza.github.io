For a reward of 8000 yuan, Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. is soliciting for corporate image logos.

by shejizhixing on 2007-06-19 00:50:41

Announcement on Soliciting the Corporate Image Logo of Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd.Under the strong support of Hami Prefectural Committee, the Administrative Office, Xinjiang Regulatory Bureau of China Banking Regulatory Commission (CBRC), and the local governments of two counties and one city, as well as the guidance from Hami CBRC Branch and People's Bank of China Hami Central Sub-branch, with the approval of CBRC's "Replies to the Formation of Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd." (Yinjian [2007] No. 206), five urban credit cooperatives — Hami City Credit Union, Hongda City Credit Union, Jinyin City Credit Union, Petroleum City Credit Union, and Sandaoling City Credit Union — have merged and reorganized to establish Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (preparatory).The newly established Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. will be guided by the corporate mission of "Sincerely Serving Society and Seeking Common Development", adhering to the principles of "Integrity, Stability, Development, Service for the Masses, and Regional Economy". It will carry forward the corporate spirit of "Pragmatism, Dedication, Innovation, and Unity", serving urban residents and small and medium-sized urban economies while actively supporting regional economic development. The reorganized urban credit cooperative will play a positive role in promoting and facilitating the development of small and medium-sized urban economies and regional economies.In order to present the reorganized Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. with a completely new image to all ethnic groups in Hami, Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (shortened as Hami City Credit Union) is now publicly soliciting corporate image logos (LOGOs) from the entire society. The relevant matters are announced as follows:1. Design Theme:Integrity, Stability, Development, Service for the Masses, and Regional Economy2. Design Requirements:(1) It must have significant financial industry attributes and characteristics.(2) It must be clearly distinct from other bank logos.(3) The design should be aesthetically pleasing, simple, dignified, modern, with harmonious color application. The overall design should have a strong visual impact, making it easy to remember.(4) The meaning should be appropriate, creative, and innovative.(5) It should be highly applicable, usable in various occasions and carriers.(6) The participating works must be newly created and never published before.3. Deadline:From June 8, 2007, to July 2, 2007. (Works must be submitted via express mail or email before July 1, 2007, based on the local postmark or email date. Works received after the deadline will not be accepted. Local works can also be directly submitted by the author to Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (preparatory).)4. Solicitation Targets:Design companies, advertising companies, designers, and individuals with unique creative ideas who possess high-level design skills.5. Submission Content:(1) The design draft may include three-dimensional, colored effects, etc.(2) The work should be a harmonious combination of the main pattern of the logo and the font of the credit union’s name (the author should attach a creation explanation for the logo and font on the work).(3) Basic information of the participating organization or individual (organization name or name, gender, occupation, ID number, contact address, telephone, E-mail, etc.).6. Submission Guidelines:(1) The design drafts must be provided in duplicate, i.e., one colored draft and one coordinate draft, A4 paper size (210×297mm), indicating standard proportions and standard colors. The resolution of electronic drafts should not be less than 150dpi (pixels/inch), width × height being 1240×1754 pixels, JPG or TIF format. Those who have the conditions can provide vector graphics (AI, CDR format files), which can be used for standard colors and dedicated fonts in documents, stationery, promotional items, and media promotion.(2) Email submission or physical file delivery is acceptable.(3) The submitted works must be original creations of the author, unpublished or unsubmitted in any form. Legal issues such as copyright disputes are the responsibility of the submitter.(4) Submitted works will not be returned; please keep a copy for yourself.(5) If there are identical designs among the submissions, the first proposer will be determined by the time of submission.7. Evaluation Methods and Reward Amounts:(1) An evaluation committee composed of experts and professionals invited by Hami City Credit Union will independently evaluate the image logos. Combining public comments, one design award winner (rewarded with 8000 yuan) and five finalists (each rewarded with 2000 yuan) will be selected, and certificates will be issued. The design award will not be counted repeatedly, and the winners must pay personal income tax on the prize money themselves.(2) After further improvement, the winning design of the logo will be officially adopted by Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. All rights including copyright and ownership belong to Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd.(3) The list of winners will be notified by phone, certificates will be awarded, and announcements will be made in related media.(4) Participants in this solicitation activity should accept the relevant rights and obligations specified in this announcement. The final interpretation right of this activity belongs to Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (preparatory).8. Submission Address and Contact Information:1. Attachment: Introduction to Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (preparatory)

    Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd. (preparatory) was established under the strong support of the Hami Prefectural Committee and Administrative Office, following the spirit of the notice from the China Banking Regulatory Commission regarding the merger and reorganization of single legal person urban credit unions based on the original urban credit unions (Yinjian Office [2004] No. 289), taking into account the economic development of Hami region and the current status of the development of urban credit unions in Hami. To further expand the scale of operations of urban credit unions and enhance their ability to resist risks, the fiscal funds of Hami region and two counties and one city, along with renowned enterprises in Hami region and city, jointly invested to merge and reorganize five urban credit unions — Hami City Credit Union, Hongda City Credit Union, Jinyin City Credit Union, Petroleum City Credit Union, and Sandaoling City Credit Union — into a single legal person Hami City Credit Union Co., Ltd.Since the establishment of the first urban credit union in Hami in 1987, Hami urban credit organizations have gone through 20 years. Over these 20 years, the five urban credit unions, with the strong support and help of all levels of party and government leadership and all sectors of society, and through the hard work of all employees, have taken on the responsibility of supporting urban economic development, serving small and medium-sized enterprises, and serving urban residents. Their businesses have achieved remarkable growth and played an active role in supporting the development of small and medium-sized urban economies.As of the end of May 2007, the five urban credit unions in Hami had a total of 13 business outlets and 148 employees. They held deposits totaling 1,274,060,000 yuan and loans totaling 880,640,000 yuan. Their support for small and medium-sized urban economies has become an indispensable important force for the development and strengthening of regional and urban economies.
