2012 MG6 Full Series with an 8000 Discount - A Choice for Young Consumers

by rabijja883 on 2011-09-13 09:01:51

Currently, the 2012 MG6 series offers a cash discount of 8,000 yuan, and the price of the 1.8L manual comfort version has dropped below 120,000 yuan.

In terms of financing, calculated based on the central bank's benchmark interest rate with a 30% down payment for a three-year term, the down payment for the MG6 is approximately between 60,000 to 90,000 yuan. Monthly payments are around 2,700 to 4,000 yuan (including full insurance, registration, purchase tax, and guarantee fees). The MG6 features a uniquely distinctive exterior design, with strong lines at the front that can appeal to many buyers of sports cars in this class.

For maintenance, the MG6 comes with a warranty of three years or 60,000 kilometers. The 4S store provides a free first service, which occurs after three months of vehicle purchase or after driving 3,000-5,000 kilometers. The regular maintenance cycle is every 10,000 kilometers. The cost for a minor service is less than 300 yuan, and a major service costs less than 450 yuan (including replacement of three filters + labor fees). The MG6 is equipped with the Kavachi 1.8T all-aluminum turbocharged engine. Although the power isn't as impressive as some imagined for a turbocharged engine, its maximum output of 118 kW at 5,500 rpm and a maximum torque of 215 Nm between 2,500-4,500 rpm can meet the needs of most car owners.

The MG6 is Shanghai Automotive's first independently developed model designed, produced, and sold for the global market. Its body design adheres to the UK Design concept, successfully creating a pure British style.

The MG6 is an intermediate-class car designed to meet the needs of global consumers, targeting young individuals full of vitality and personality. By breaking the boundaries between hatchbacks and sedans, both the internal space and external contours are more suitable to the tastes of Chinese consumers. The MG6 measures 4,653 mm in length, 1,827 mm in width, and 1,478 mm in height, with a wheelbase reaching 2,705 mm. Combined with a rationalized body layout, it optimizes internal space comprehensively, making it highly competitive among its peers. When the rear seats are folded down, the internal space of the MG6 reaches 1,379 liters. This new benchmark sedan, with its dynamic and attractive appearance and economic practicality, suits the preferences of young consumers well.
