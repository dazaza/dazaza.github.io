Based on the characteristics of global water quality and monitoring standards, combined with our own technical advantages, we have adopted the concept of modular configuration to ingeniously combine the electrode method with the microcomputer photometric measurement principle. We have thus launched the latest ET9909 series of multi-parameter water quality rapid analyzers. These are not only suitable for various outdoor environmental water quality testing requirements but also for rapid water quality detection during emergencies as well as routine water parameter measurements in laboratories, allowing users to meet their water testing needs with minimal investment. The spectrophotometer has built-in programs for over 100 testing methods and electrode measurements, enabling it to measure more than 115 critical water parameters, widely applied in water quality monitoring in industries such as industry, municipal services, environmental protection, and education.

The device allows for quick measurement and detection of various water parameters, featuring user-friendly and convenient operation, large capacity storage functions, and data interfaces including RS232, USB, and IR data transmitters, facilitating data storage and management by transferring results to a computer for further analysis, printing, etc. It is suitable for comprehensive water quality analysis both in the field, at production sites, and in laboratories.

- G: Economical and practical power supply modes, including built-in rechargeable batteries and universal power adapters, suitable for different measurement environments.

- H: Configured portable packaging, waterproof and shockproof design, economical consumables, suitable for field and laboratory analysis and testing.
