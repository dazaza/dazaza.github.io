Yuan Li responded to the Weibo abuse by saying that she is a rebellious person

by edivista on 2011-11-23 08:01:25

Click to enter Yuan Li's Weibo

From the controversy over her revealing outfit at the Huabiao Awards, to the Weibo war of words with a certain female star, Yuan Li has recently often been in the spotlight in the entertainment industry. Yesterday, Yuan Li was dressed to the nines as she attended a commercial event in Nanjing. Wearing a black feathered halter mini-dress with a short jacket, along with her signature short hair, Yuan Li exuded even more sex appeal. When asked by reporters about the recent controversies, Yuan Li didn't shy away from the questions, frankly admitting that she is just like Ou Yang Lanlan and Du Xiao Yue, a very rebellious person.

Keyword: Weibo War of Words

What I said is all true

Since posting a series of blog posts on Weibo subtly mocking a certain female star, whenever Yuan Li appears in public, the conversation always circles back to this topic. When reporters brought up the Weibo war of words yesterday, Yuan Li didn't dodge the question but also didn't directly respond. She lightly remarked: Are we still talking about this? Let's not talk about it anymore, it's all last century's news already. However, when the reporter mentioned whether these Weibo posts were written by herself or if they were orchestrated by her team for publicity, Yuan Li solemnly stated that of course her Weibo posts are all written by herself. Isn't it very laughable to have someone else do such simple things for you? Moreover, everything I said is true. Yuan Li said that although she insists on telling the truth, she hasn't offended anyone. I always speak under the premise of protecting others. I've never named names; it's all everyone guessing. (Click on the image to enter Yuan Li's Weibo)

Keyword: Revealing Outfit

Occasionally sexy

Yesterday, Yuan Li appeared in a black feathered halter mini-dress with a small jacket, looking both sexy and dignified. Along with the shine of jade accessories worth tens of millions, she was radiant. Compared to her revealing outfit from a while ago, this was much lower-key. When asked if she was going to return to the pure and innocent route, Yuan Li paused for a moment, then laughed and said: "Innocent girl? That's too beautiful an image for me." From Ou Yang Lanlan to Du Xiao Yue, my characters have always been somewhat rebellious, which is quite close to my personality. Recalling the incident of the revealing outfit, Yuan Li said it was absolutely not an act of rebellion. I absolutely did not do it intentionally. The clothes for that time were indeed something I hadn't checked well enough; I was too careless. Next time, I will definitely look carefully! Yuan Li expressed that women's clothing should still be decent and appropriate, but occasionally one can allow oneself to be sexy.

Keyword: Tired from Filming

Filming 17 hours a day

"From 6:30 in the morning until now, there are still two night scenes left to film, probably until 11 o'clock, getting back to the base around midnight, the makeup is old, and the body is already tired," Yuan Li complained about being tired from filming on her Weibo recently. During yesterday's interview, Yuan Li also didn't avoid the topic, frankly stating that sometimes having to consider personal relationships makes her very conflicted. It's tiring to film, my role in this movie is heavy. I signed a contract for 14 hours of work per day, but in reality, we end up filming for sixteen or seventeen hours. But there's nothing we can do about it. When people say 'help us out,' how could you refuse them? For example, before I attend an event, taking photos wasn't written into the contract, but how could I not let them take pictures? People would say I'm difficult to deal with.
