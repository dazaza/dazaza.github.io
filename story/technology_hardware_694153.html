In the eyes of the elderly couple, although He Hua is a little introverted, she doesn't seem like an impulsive or heartless person. At the scene, he and his son were shocked and immediately shook Yunyun, but there was no response. However, her body still had warmth, so he quickly picked up the child and ran to the town hospital. In the end, the doctor told him that the child had already died.

For this matter, the elderly couple felt a little angry, but also found it strange. The father said that they have two daughters, He Hua is the youngest daughter, who has been very introverted since childhood, not talking much, dropped out of school after finishing the second grade of elementary school, and at the age of 19, she married into Shaanxi through an introduction and had a child. Later, because her husband treated her badly, she divorced and returned to Sichuan. Losing his granddaughter, the grandfather was deeply saddened.

How could Yunyun suddenly be bound and die at home? Jin Shunming and his son returned home with their granddaughter and saw He Hua still watching TV. "Was the child tied up by you?" Jin Shunming thought that there was no one else in the house at the time, and no strangers had come to the house. Before leaving for work in the morning, he had fed his granddaughter a steamed bun, and she was fine then. Therefore, the person most likely to tie up his granddaughter was He Hua.

●The girl's grandfather suspects that because Yunyun was not obedient, her mother tied her up with a rope and covered her with a quilt to prevent her from making any noise, resulting in suffocating the child to death.

●Facing the family's inquiry, Yunyun's mother remained silent all along. The family said that later she admitted to tying up the child and covering her with a quilt.

Currently, the local police station in Dujiangyan is investigating the matter.

Subsequently, I learned from Jin Shunming's eldest daughter, Jin Lin, that He Hua is usually a quiet person who does not greet people when she sees them, and feels very introverted. Once, she saw her younger brother Jin Qiang and He Hua arguing, and He Hua impulsively scratched her younger brother's face. She also heard that the couple often argued.

Yesterday afternoon, Grandpa Yunyun, Jin Shunming, recalled the incident. At around 8:00 AM, he went to Daguan Town for errands and returned home at around 11:00 AM to cook. At that time, his daughter-in-law He Hua and his granddaughter were in the bedroom, his son Jin Qiang was working outside, and he was busy cooking in the kitchen. Unexpectedly, the power suddenly went out, so he decided to buy some food from the town.

Facing the family's questioning, He Hua remained silent all along. In anger, Jin Qiang tied He Hua to the iron railing of a neighbor's house and loudly asked her what happened, but she still didn't say anything. "Later, she admitted that she tied up Yunyun and covered her with a quilt," Jin Shunming said. Later, the daughter-in-law admitted it, but never explained why she tied up her own daughter.

Yunyun's family suspected that her biological mother He Hua tied her up and covered her with a quilt, which led to the child being suffocated to death, and pointed out that He Hua admitted to the matter.

She is introverted, but not a cruel person.

Yesterday, I tried to learn more from the Qingcheng Mountain Police Station, but they said that the case was under investigation and it was inconvenient to disclose information.

Jin Shunming still remembers the situation at the time. Two quilts on the bed were tightly covered, and when he lifted them, he saw Yunyun lying on the bed, bound from head to foot with ropes. One leg was bent in a struggling posture, her whole body had turned black, and there were bruises near her forehead.

At around 12:00 PM yesterday, when he returned home, he saw He Hua watching TV, and soon Jin Qiang also came back. "He couldn't find his granddaughter anywhere and realized something had happened." Jin Shunming said that Jin Qiang called for his daughter everywhere, but no one answered. When he asked He Hua, she didn't answer either. Finally, they found Yunyun on the bed in the bedroom.

Yesterday, I met He Hua's parents in Shuangle Village, Daguan Town.

Neighbors living nearby said that at the time of the incident, they didn't hear any sounds, and no one witnessed the process. They only learned about it later from others. Jin Shunming also said that he did not personally witness Yunyun being smothered, and no one else did either.

In 2007, He Hua married Jin Qiang.

Subsequently, local police officers arrived at the scene and took He Hua away for investigation.

Jin Shunming suspects that He Hua tied up Yunyun because she was disobedient. Worried that the child would make noise, she covered her with a quilt, resulting in the child being suffocated to death.

At around 12:00 PM yesterday, in the Xinhhe New Residential Area of Daguan Town, Dujiangyan, 2-year-old girl Yunyun was found dead on her own bed, her whole body tied up with ropes and completely covered with a quilt.
