Cardispa Bladeless Fan - Trade in your old fan for a new one - A fan's journey

by scseoer on 2011-07-06 17:38:10

The product concept of the second-generation bladeless fan is the same as the first generation, which is to fundamentally replace air conditioning refrigerators and other machines that cause too much harm to the human body. There are two models of the second-generation bladeless fan products launched this time, namely SNF2-1 and SNF2-2. A wireless remote control has been added, which can be directly placed on the top of the fan by using the magnetic force on the top of the machine, making it convenient to use. At the same time, due to the improvements made in the outlet and the body of the second-generation Cadispa bladeless fan, its products look more fashionable, and the wind power is stronger than the first generation, with a wider effective range than the first generation. In addition, the noise aspect of the first generation product performance has also been addressed.
