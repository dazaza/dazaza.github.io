Overlooking the entire world

by kuailaira2 on 2012-02-14 13:59:35

I don't want to be too safe. In the movie "Drunk Masters", Mi Gao's friend Langzi said, "I've already broken your record, I've been to 32 countries." Yes, Langzi has his own meaning of life, he has traveled far as a bounty hunter. Even if it's a competition, the shoes with dust and mud can prove that he has walked with heart.

It was Mi Gao who changed his dream, began to want others' recognition, chose stability and stay, ended the restless and free-spirited adventure. Then he had FUSION. But he didn't understand why his authentic French cuisine couldn't get others' good reviews - because he didn't understand at all that traveling is a process that only needs self-profit and loss. And stability, is so easy to be disrupted balance.

The state of balance requires equality from both sides. On the left is you, on the right is the whole world. To achieve balance, you can only increase or decrease your own weight.

Do you envy? Do you remember the ambitious you who wanted to travel around the world? At that time, you were held high, looking down on the whole world, flying over the Tropic of Cancer and Capricorn and still wanted to conquer the International Date Line. You know the injustice of this world, so you don't seek balance. With dreams, you can forget about security. You thought your dream was more important than the whole world, forgetting that the weaker side of the scale was you.

But in the end, you choose stability, you open a small shop, cooking the French food you like. You think you have changed, on the surface, you no longer fight against the world, but rather you are using your French taste to appeal to the whole world for your existence. Your door is deserted. Look clearly, the other side of the scale, the weaker side of force, is you. Then you start to pick up your dream again.

I like the adventurous you. The you who got drunk and said a bunch of pure French that others couldn't understand, the pretend-to-be-drunk you who staged crazy car racing to confess, and the you who gave up FUSION, handed the throwing knife to Xiao Min and said sincerely "You decide my next destination" to reclaim your dream.

Set you free, or take me away. I hope we can be very arrogant and look down on the whole world, despise the people in this world who work hard to maintain balance. Like the song "Nightlight Route" sings, "I don't want to be too safe." I want us to grow old, to boast about our youth spent together adventuring and conquering the world, rather than sitting in a rocking chair sighing about the passage of time and waiting for aging to find out that we only passively witnessed the changes of the times.
