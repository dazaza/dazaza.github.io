I will share my own e-books.

by dongguahehe on 2006-11-19 21:52:14

I myself have provided a platform for everyone to share and download the e-magazines I've collected, as well as comment and exchange ideas. At the same time, it incorporates the Digg model to discover good e-magazines.

E-magazines are not the product of WEB2.0, but the spirit of sharing is the essence of web2.0... It's the sharing spirit I admire... Hooray...

Because I myself combines the Digg model, you may more easily find the best e-magazines without being at a loss on which one to choose from a sea of e-magazines... The name "I myself" sounds very imaginative, it might be easy for everyone to misunderstand that I am talking about myself...

One thing that annoys me is that you need to install a plug-in to upload magazines, but it's still acceptable...
