Fashion 2008 From A to Z

by fearless on 2008-07-26 09:03:56

I have distilled the most practical 2008 fashion attitude for "Tide 2" from the shows in 2008, various new product launches, the fashionable clothing that has just hit the spring shelves, and the most popular movies and programs.

Animal Print

The currently very popular American TV series "Ugly Betty" has already proven: to embody moderate luxury and sensuality, animal prints are the best choice. Pairing them with simple and low-key black, white, and gray adds even more elegance.

Beijing 2008

The Olympics is absolutely the topic mentioned most by Chinese people this year. Not being able to get tickets to the events doesn't matter; Olympic products are definitely the fashion buzzword for August.

Changing

At the beginning of the year, on the dazzling award ceremony red carpets, celebrities used their outfits to tell us that gradient colors have the effect of slimming the waist and adding height. Louis Vuitton has even launched an entire gradient series, so what are you waiting for?

De-tech

Why not join people around the world who advocate "getting away from technology" and try turning off your computer once a week, leaving your mobile phone behind, stepping away from your PDA, and avoiding technological distractions.

Environmental

Thank Marc Jacobs for his "I'm Not a Plastic Bag," because it made environmental protection exceptionally trendy. In June 2008, plastic bags disappeared from supermarkets in Beijing? Are you ready to buy a fashionable cloth bag?
