The years flow like water, calm and silent.

by meimei234 on 2012-02-04 21:31:22

Time flows like water, with composure and silence.

The years pass like flowing water, the crescent moon becomes full, then diminishes by half, repeating in cycles, and so does life. Even after reading hundreds of books and letting one's heart roam free, worldly affairs are always up and down, while one's mood is much like the moon—bright and full when happy, but devoid of light when gloomy. Real life is destined to be a mix of joy and sorrow. Looking around at all living beings, each has their own hidden depths, and only time, along with those intangible yet real days, can soothe one's mood like a gentle breeze or fine rain, clear and pure, bringing us back to our true selves.

After crossing paths, we eventually go our separate ways, but only kindness and repeated understanding remain. For friends and family, there should always be endless support and understanding. There is no perfect happiness in this world, only incomplete happiness. Rare and scattered moments of genuine happiness shine like stars in the night sky. The ultimate ideal, such as Qian Xuesen and his wife Jiang Ying, they possessed not only knowledge and talent, but also great personalities and sincere emotions. It’s best to fall in love once in a lifetime, and for that love to culminate in marriage because the initial goals and love were clear. They remained steadfast towards each other, contributing not just as scientists and educators, but embodying a highly responsible attitude and spirit. I may not have the words to express my admiration and respect, but as a guiding light for my spirit, it is enough. Not only did they accompany each other, they never became dull, combining science with music to create a miracle of intellectual culture and an unattainable peak that others cannot reach, maintaining emotional purity and loyalty throughout.

As ordinary people, if we can understand and treat each other kindly, that's enough. In daily life, common folk are always busy and rushed. During this process, countless trivialities and life's incessant troubles will inevitably arise. However, we must learn to manage our moods while walking through this world, ensuring that our own mood doesn't affect others'. I hope my mood can bring a ray of light to friends or relatives.

Life goes on. Unfinished matters and past events are all inevitable experiences on our path of growth. Whether we want to face them or not, we must. No matter how much we talk, action must come first in life!
