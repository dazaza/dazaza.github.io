"The MV of 'County Party Secretary' Draws Netizen's Attention, Zhangbei Locals React Calmly"

by yixiaoyanw62 on 2011-07-13 15:57:35

Reporters randomly interviewed a few passers-by on the street, and their responses were surprisingly consistent: "This is nothing, those who don't believe it can come to Zhangbei and see," "It's not excessive at all," "In three years, Zhangbei has undergone tremendous changes without a single case of petitioning due to demolition." They were more concerned about Li Xuerong being "hard-pressed to defend herself against slander," and worried that the internet might have a negative impact on their "ideal secretary" in their hearts.

(Editor: SN013)

The Zhangbei County sent a letter to Rednet explaining the video titled "Party Secretary."

And recently, the three events that caught public attention are all related to Party Secretary Li Xuerong. The first event was the hosting of two sessions of the Grassland Music Festival, and even a Rock Music Festival, attracting over 200,000 spectators in just three days, making people remember the "change" in Zhangbei. The second event was last year's commendation meeting for Hebei's "Three-Year Transformation," where Zhangbei County was awarded the title of "Advanced County (City) in the Three-Year Transformation of Urban Appearance in Hebei Province," ranking first; Li Xuerong and 120 other comrades were awarded the title of "Model in the Three-Year Transformation of Urban Appearance in Hebei Province," also ranking first and delivering a typical speech at the meeting. The third event was the "County Secretary" MV incident.

The "sudden popularity" of the "County Secretary" MV was unexpected by Tieshiguang, a young man. In the reporter's interview, this MV's producer turned out to be a child born in 1986, who had just graduated from university. His original intention for producing the video was very simple, just wanting to express his personal feelings, believing that Zhangbei County's "three-year transformation" was closely related to the new county secretary, who valued talent above all else, allowing him, a college graduate with no background, to become the deputy secretary of Wansheng Community of Nanshan Road Office. It was understood that although "Tie production" passed through the public selection and became a "rural worker," he was not officially employed and certainly not a civil servant, earning only around one or two thousand yuan per month. His video production technology was even simpler, using a set of Corel VideoStudio software to splice images and songs from the internet, which was merely a rough "first attempt."

Currently, the county secretary as a special class has been categorized as a high-risk group. As the county secretary holding great power, once there is any negative topic, it will quickly attract the attention of netizens, and positive publicity or praise for them instead triggers resistance and doubt. Previously, Xue Weijun, director of the Institute of Economics at the Hebei Academy of Social Sciences, said that the parody of netizens on the "County Secretary" MV reflects the currently widespread "collective reverse psychology" in society, viewing "party cadres" with colored glasses, making a good cadre an anomaly in people's eyes, and such psychology should be taken seriously and appropriately guided.

Tieshiguang: A Child Intimidated by the Internet

Li Xuerong and Zhangbei's "Major Events"

"In fact, with a little understanding, one should know the origin of this song. Any person with common sense knows that if Secretary Li had instructed someone or some department to produce it, it wouldn't be so clumsy. If you think further, this song has been uploaded for seven months, why do the people of Zhangbei react so calmly? Are the people of Zhangbei slow to respond?" A media colleague told the reporter that after the MV was uploaded, the first ones to see it were the people of Zhangbei. However, they were particularly calm, thinking that what was reflected online was still far from enough, and the development of Zhangbei was evident to all. "The incident has drawn attention from national mainstream media, and even overseas media. What needs reflection is not Secretary Li and a child, but the internet and our media. Can't a child express his feelings? Aren't his feelings public opinion?"

Zhangbei County, located on the plateau of Zhangjiakou, is a national-level impoverished county with only 370,000 people. Li Yi, former editor-in-chief of the Zhangjiakou Daily and chairman of the Zhangjiakou Journalists Association, recalled that on January 10, 1998, a 6.2-magnitude earthquake occurred in Zhangbei, combined with extreme weather, drawing national attention, leaving a deep impression on the country.

At the provincial commendation meeting for the "Three-Year Transformation," Li Xuerong said: Zhangbei used to focus solely on grain and vegetables, unable to break free from the "agriculture" word, and unable to solve the "poverty" word. "In the past three years, we have invested 23 billion yuan in implementing more than 3,000 key projects, increasing fixed asset investment from 1.6 billion yuan to 13.2 billion yuan, with deposit and loan balances and total project investments ranking first in the city for two consecutive years. The change in Zhangbei stems from the transformation brought about by the 'three-year transformation' in terms of thinking."

Unlike the flowers and applause received before, this time Li Xuerong and the "Tie family father and son" were pushed to the forefront together. However, different from the Tie family father and son's active "testing the waters," Li Xuerong appeared quite passive. After learning about the matter, he told the reporter from China News Service, "'Suspected' of flattering me, this I really cannot bear. I am far from the county secretary in the song," and expressed that he "does not encourage such behavior." When ridicule, blame, denunciation, bombardment, and even criticism came, Li Xuerong told the reporter: "A child, acting on impulse, made something like this and put it online. I have no right to stop it, nor can I do anything to the child!"

What "Iron Dad" did not expect was that his son's "adaptation" of the theme song from the same-name TV series "County Secretary" seven months ago would cause such a commotion, shifting from mocking the county secretary to mocking a "child." "Iron Dad" was merely a vice-chairman of the People's Congress in a rural area, in a place where no one wanted to go, at the age of over 50, yet "helped with good intentions but caused trouble." Old Iron now deeply regrets it: "I really want to apologize to Secretary Li, I didn't expect to cause Secretary Li so much trouble, completely going against the child's original intention."

When interviewing Li Xuerong, upon reaching his mobile phone, the ringtone sang: "North of Beijing, north of North China, north of Hebei, there is Zhangbei," This is a promotional song for Zhangbei, especially beautiful, named "The Most Beautiful Grassland is in Zhangbei." (End)

The reporter immediately interviewed the "Tie family father and son." Their simplicity was hard to believe, both father and son had equally dark complexions, with no trace of the "flattery god song" director's appearance. The parody online has pushed them to the "abyss," yet Tieshiguang stubbornly said: "I feel in the hearts of the people of Zhangbei County, he is truly a sun, let those who doubt come to Zhangbei and ask."

Despite the "County Secretary" MV being "bombarded," many netizens have voiced different opinions: On April 8, netizen "Baiyangdian Reed" wrote an article titled "Perhaps We Should Be More Tolerant of Songs Praising County Secretaries." On April 9, Cai Fanghua wrote in the China Youth Daily, believing that "Li Xuerong has greatly changed the face of Zhangbei in just over three years. The grassland music festival he introduced brought high visibility to Zhangbei. Such achievements are not only related to the power of the role of county secretary but also related to Li Xuerong's own ambition, dedication, and public servant consciousness." Professor Kong Qingdong from Peking University said, according to some people's logic, there shouldn't be any praising literary works in the world, "this kind of thinking is wrong," he hopes everyone will "calmly look at the sudden popularity of the county secretary's MV."

Calm Zhangbei residents and another voice

On April 11, China News Service, Zhangjiakou -- The "County Secretary" MV suddenly became popular overnight, by 11 a.m. on April 10, the number of viewers reached as many as 320,000, with direct references reaching 170,000. And when searching "County Secretary MV Li Xuerong" on Baidu, the relevant entries reached over 55,000, while Google results reached approximately 100,000, showing its fiery degree in 2012. However, China News Service reporters found that behind the shocking words such as "too sweet," "flattery," "flattery god song," voices of calm reflection are growing increasingly louder. In the location where the incident happened, Zhangbei County, Hebei Province, the public's response was quite "calm."
