Weibo user "Away from Home for 7 Years", who is enthusiastic about public welfare, gave away train tickets. The three winning users displayed their train tickets on Weibo with much gratitude.

by edivista on 2011-01-25 11:36:37

Weibo News Network: A few days ago, we reported on the story of a Sina Weibo user who had been away from home for seven years and wanted to give away 50 train tickets to help 50 kind-hearted netizens return home (see previous report: http://www.7weibo.com/blog/post/101.html). Several days have passed, and now many netizens have received the train tickets sent by the person who has been away from home for seven years and boarded the train to go home.

In the Weibo posts of the person who has been away from home for seven years, the netizens who received the train tickets posted photos of their gifted tickets to express their gratitude, as shown in the figure:

The netizens who received the train tickets expressed their gratitude:

@KangarooProvence: This morning, I received the train ticket sent by the OP. It's the Z55 on the 26th. I can hardly believe it's true; thank you so much!

@Maxiaoqing: Hahahahahaha, today I'm so happy!!! Early in the morning, I received the train ticket shipped by @AwayFromHome7Years! And it's even a soft sleeper! Tomorrow I can go home!!! Thank heaven and earth, thank Weibo for letting me meet such a good person!! Thank you!

@Strawberrytian: As evidenced by the picture, I really got the ticket and can go home the day after tomorrow. Thank you again.

Currently, AwayFromHome7Years has received a lot of attention, but also increased a lot of troubles:

@AwayFromHome7Years: These few days I've almost collapsed. It's not because of the countless private messages asking if I'm a scalper and how much extra money I want that made me collapse, but because I promised several people I would buy tickets for them, but I couldn't get any! This year's train tickets are too hard to buy. My friend helping me buy tickets is almost driven crazy by my constant inquiries. When I started this blog, I said I would help 50 people go home for the New Year, but now I haven't even helped five! All the Weibo users who have been paying attention to me, encouraging me, and waiting for my updates, I really feel very sorry! I've disappointed a lot of people!

For such enthusiastic netizens, what we should have is gratitude, tolerance, and understanding. Perhaps the coldness of society makes more people feel confused or think that AwayFromHome7Years is a scammer or a scalper. However, when the truth is right in front of us, please open your eyes and distinguish between good and evil.
