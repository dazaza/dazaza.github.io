Rainbow Island: Guardian vs Dual Blades - PK Skills Sharing

First of all, this post is specifically for the claw and shield guardians who are struggling with dual blade players in PK. Other classes, please press Alt+F4, thanks for your cooperation.

Let's start with the stats distribution. Personally, I think you can almost ignore Heavy Punch, Energy Wave, Iron Elbow Strike, Rulai Palm, Shockwave. As for Wind God Kick and Chi Gong Wave, they're not used often so just put one point in each. After all, you're still a shield guardian, so Vitality and Iron Armor must be maxed out. Weapon enhancement accuracy should also be fully upgraded. Put one point into Mobile Tactics, max out Critical Hit Tactics, and upgrade Awakening as much as possible because after the second transfer, your strength will increase. You can decide how to upgrade other skills on your own.

As for combos, there's no need to explain the martial arts critical hit infinite combo. Since the guardian's skills are basically (note: basically!) all able to connect with COMBOs, you don't have to worry about them not connecting. A perfect combo would be something like Straight Punch -> Haore Foot -> Crescent Moon Foot -> Heavenly Dragon Foot. If your strength is high enough, the opponent will be dead by now. If not, he'll definitely be lying on the ground. When he gets up, there are three possibilities:

1. Charge straight at you. These people are generally reckless types willing to trade blows. While he's still some distance away from you, throw a few waves at him. If he doesn't die, run away quickly.

2. Flash away faster than lightning. These people usually don't have much blood left and want to use a flanking strategy to take you down. For these kinds of opponents, add Mobile Tactics and go up close to throw a few waves at him until he dies.

3. Jump towards you. In this situation, the opponent has a 90% chance of using the dual blade mobility skill, so you have to be careful about someone sneaking up behind you. If you don't want to be too passive, then when he hasn't landed yet, jump up and use Gale Kick or Thousand Dragon Wave to knock him down instantly. Then follow up with a few waves to finish him off. Dual blades like close combat, meaning that if you get caught in their combo, there's a high probability you'll be killed. At this moment, you need to escape quickly. To break the combo, C. Rotation is the first choice (Combo me? Do you think my C. Rotation is useless?). If you don't want to waste too much mana, then just use Crescent Moon Foot, followed by Heavenly Dragon Foot (depending on personal preference, you can also follow up with Gale Kick). Then return and release an Awakening, which is fine, but this move has a higher element of luck.

When fighting against dual blades, the most important thing is to move around unpredictably like a fly. Of course, it's not really random, but rather to make the enemy lose track of your whereabouts. Mentally repeat: "You can't hit me!" while jumping around. When you find an opportunity, kill him with one combo. At the same time, be cautious of being caught in his combo. Escaping isn't elaborated here. Seize the opportunity in the air and release skills timely. For example:

It needs to be explained that this tactic is only applicable when both parties' levels are similar and equipment is also comparable. Of course, there are more than these PK skills for guardians. More combos need to be discovered by the players. If you think I'm wrong, please reply and point it out. If you think my writing is bad, please leave a direct comment.
