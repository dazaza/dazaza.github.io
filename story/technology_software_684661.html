3 urban management office staff were caught for taking drugs, and 2 temporary workers were fired.

by u4gje3po on 2012-03-01 09:37:01

An urban management team found 3 drug users at once. For this, many netizens can't help but ask: how did such people mix into the urban management team? Regarding the online rumors that "the head of the urban management team wanted to privately settle with the police on the night of the incident", Yang Xiujin said that this was purely a rumor. He said that after the incident, he learned that when the police officers wanted to take Zhang Shaojun and others away from the office of the Municipal Administration Enforcement Team, the on-duty personnel did not know the situation and reported it to the head of the enforcement team, Zhou Guofa. As the head of the team, Zhou Guofa had the responsibility and obligation to go to the unit to understand the situation when someone had problems in the unit, and after he arrived, he did not ask the police to "turn a blind eye". Yesterday, the reporter verified this matter with the police of Xiushan County, and relevant persons in charge of the Public Security Bureau of Xiushan County said that this matter was true.

Netizens asked whether the three people all had law enforcement certificates. Yang Xiujin said that Zhang Shaojun was formal and only he had one, the other two did not have law enforcement certificates, and they could only cooperate with formal law enforcement officers for street patrols and law enforcement under normal circumstances. For this, Yang Xiujin, Director of the Municipal Administration and Garden Bureau of Xiushan County, said that due to the tight manpower, among more than 80 urban management team members in Xiushan County, quite a few were temporarily recruited. He said that the municipal administration department was very strict when recruiting people, they would conduct physical examinations for them during recruitment, and would also understand through the public security organs whether the proposed recruits had any illegal behaviors, etc.

Netizen: Excessive. It's a shame for the people of Xiushan County. Yang Xiujin told the reporter that the three people including Zhang Shaojun had no criminal records, and their units had not found any abnormal behavior such as drug use before. So far, there has been no major conflict between them and small vendors during the law enforcement process. He believed that the municipal administration department had paid attention to what should be paid attention to when recruiting, and it was possible that they had just recently picked up the habit of taking drugs, so he found it hard to accept the accusation that the municipal administration department had "misjudged" people.

Yang Xiujin said that the three involved urban management team members were Zhang Shaojun, Zhang Ke, and Wu Ping (all pseudonyms) from the Municipal Administration Enforcement Team of the bureau. Among them, Zhang Shaojun was transferred from the army to the unit, belonging to a formal employee, while Zhang Ke and Wu Ping were recruited not long ago, belonging to temporary workers.

Did the four drug users have any criminal records? Who suggested setting the drug use location in the office of the enforcement team on the night of the incident? Where did their drugs come from? The police of Xiushan County said that the case is still under further investigation and handling, and specific details are not convenient to disclose.

As soon as an incident occurred, it was said that they were temporary workers.

Yesterday, Yang Xiujin also denied the rumor that "when the police investigated on the day of the incident, they encountered confrontation from urban management team members." Yang Xiujin said that at around 10 p.m. on November 18, he received a call from the head of the enforcement team, Zhou Guofa, who said that the police found several team members using drugs in the office of the enforcement team and wanted to take them to the county public security bureau for questioning. He immediately made the following requirements to Zhou Guofa: firmly support and cooperate with the public security organs to investigate according to law, and do not obstruct.

"Three urban management team members in Xiushan County are unreliable, actually inviting idle social elements to gather and use drugs in the office of the Law Enforcement Team of the Municipal Administration and Garden Bureau of the county," on November 19, "Mama Ge" exposed this matter on the Da Yu Reporting Platform, and said that several drug users were arrested by the Xiushan Public Security Bureau on the evening of the 18th. Yesterday, the Municipal Administration and Garden Bureau of Xiushan County replied in the post: the formal employee Zhang Hongbo involved in the matter was given a serious warning within the party, and the other two temporary employees were immediately dismissed.

Netizens: As soon as an incident occurs, it is said that they are temporary workers, since temporary workers are so prone to incidents, why hire temporary workers?

Director: "Private settlement" is purely a rumor

The formal worker was warned

"The urban management team of Xiushan used the secrecy of the office of the enforcement team, inviting idle social elements to gather and use drugs in the office!" In the morning of the 19th, netizens like "Mama Ge" reported on Da Yu Network that several drug users had already been caught by the Xiushan police. The post said that at around 10 p.m. on November 18, several employees of the Xiushan Urban Management Team gathered to use drugs in the unit and were reported by someone who knew about it, and the Xiushan County Public Security Bureau then sent out police, arresting 4 drug users.

The person in charge introduced that at around 10 p.m. on November 18, the Xiushan County Public Security Bureau received a report from a man, saying that someone was using drugs in the office of the Xiushan Municipal Administration Enforcement Team. The police attached great importance to it, since the triangular tower traffic patrol platform was nearby, the leader of the county public security bureau immediately instructed the platform police officers to go and investigate. After receiving the report, the platform police officers arrived in about 3 minutes, broke open the door of the Municipal Administration Enforcement Team's office, and indeed found 4 men using drugs.

Temporary workers were dismissed

In the afternoon of yesterday, the reporter telephoned Yang Xiujin, Director of the Municipal Administration and Garden Bureau of Xiushan County, who gave a detailed introduction to the preliminary handling situation of the involved personnel.

Yang Xiujin said that he was attending a meeting in Chongqing on the day of the incident. On November 19, he urgently returned to Xiushan from Chongqing, and convened a meeting of all staff of the bureau that day, informing the situation in the meeting, and the head of the Municipal Enforcement Team, Mr. Zhou, made a self-examination in the meeting. Subsequently, the bureau's Party group held a meeting to study and decide to give a serious warning within the party to Zhang Shaojun, the formal urban management team member involved in drug use (currently being reported to the direct organ committee of Xiushan County for approval), and dismiss Zhang Ke and Wu Ping immediately.

It is understood that after the exposure of this matter, the leaders of the Chongqing Municipal Commission and the Xiushan County Party Committee and County Government have successively made instructions, believing that this matter reflects serious problems in the urban management team of Xiushan, requiring serious investigation of the involved personnel and rectification of the urban management team throughout the county.

"Why are there so many drug users mixed into the urban management team?"

Netizens: They had bad luck and were discovered.

After investigation, among the four male drug users, three were staff members of the Xiushan Municipal Administration Enforcement Team, and the other male surnamed Tan was an idle social element. The four usually had interactions. That night, when the four gathered together and felt "bored", they decided to find a place to use drugs to find "feelings". The four were "troubled" by the safety of the drug use location, then someone suggested that "going to the Urban Management Enforcement Team would be safer", thus the four agreed, hiding in the office of the Municipal Administration Enforcement Team to use drugs, thinking that closing the door of the enforcement team would make no one know, but they were still detected and reported.
