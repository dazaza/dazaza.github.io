Man borrows money from an acquaintance, is refused, kills him and dumps the body

by zxyasdn20 on 2011-08-08 14:36:03

Our reporter Li Deqing and correspondent Qin Huachuan - Recently, under the cooperation of Changchun Public Security Bureau and Jiutai Public Security Bureau, Yushu Public Security Bureau successfully solved the "09.12.18" Extraordinary Homicide Case after a month of investigation, tracking down thousands of kilometers from Dongguan, Guangdong to Qiqihar, Heilongjiang.

Alert: Male Corpse Found on Jiangqiao Bridge

On December 18, 2009, Yushu Public Security Bureau received a report about a male corpse found on the Jiangqiao Bridge in Dapo Town. Assistant Mayor of Yushu City and Director of the Public Security Bureau, Liu Hongshuang, led the criminal investigation team to the scene where they discovered multiple stab wounds on the body, determining it as a homicide. After investigation, Yushu police determined that the deceased was not a resident of Yushu. Based on the location of the dumped body, it was speculated that he could be a resident from Jiutai or Dehui, which were not far from Dapo Town. The police immediately issued an assistance investigation notice to the authorities in Jiutai and Dehui.

Investigation: Acquaintance Invited Him for Dinner, But Never Returned

A week later, the police received a clue that Zhu, a citizen of Jiutai, went missing on December 18th. On January 4, 2010, after family identification, it was confirmed that the victim was indeed Zhu. The family revealed that before his disappearance, Zhu had received a call from Duan, another Jiutai citizen, inviting him for dinner. However, Zhu never returned after heading out.

The investigators rushed to Jiutai to continue their pursuit, discovering that Duan had disappeared after the incident, making him a major suspect. Later, it was found that he had fled to Heilongjiang Province after the crime, prompting the Yushu police to form a capture team and head there.

Apprehension: Police Officers Traveled Thousands of Miles

For two weeks, the officers tracked Duan's possible hideout locations. They endured freezing outdoor conditions while staking out and sometimes went without food for an entire day.

Following the route taken by the suspect, the police tracked down through Shenyang, Liaoning Province, Wuhan, Hubei Province, Dongguan, Guangdong Province, Harbin, Daqing, and Qiqihar in Heilongjiang Province, covering thousands of miles.

On January 16, the police followed the clues to Fuyu County in Qiqihar City. At 16:00 on January 20, Duan appeared near a rented house. The officers quickly surrounded him, but Duan resisted arrest, causing one officer to sprain his ankle. Together, the officers managed to subdue him and escorted him back to Yushu overnight.

Cause: Borrowing Money Led to Resentment and Murder

Duan confessed that he had known Zhu for many years and knew that Zhu’s family was financially well-off. Recently, due to a shortage of 100,000 yuan needed for his business, he thought of asking Zhu for help. When Zhu refused to lend him the money, resentment built up inside him.

On the night of the incident, Duan and another person invited Zhu for dinner. On the way to the restaurant, he once again asked Zhu for money and was refused, also receiving some verbal insults. Consequently, both men killed Zhu, tied stones to his body, transported him to a section of road in Dapo Town, Yushu City, intending to dump the body into the Songhua River. However, because they were noticed by a passing vehicle, they abandoned the body on the bridge and fled. Currently, this case is still under further review.
