According to China News Service, at midnight on August 6, crosstalk performer He Yunwei posted a statement on his personal blog, announcing that he and Li Jing had officially left the De Yun She. The statement did not mention the reasons for their departure from the Deyun Club. However, He Yunwei stated that the two would hold a press conference soon to explain the situation to the public. A 1'38'' video: "Li Jing and He Yunwei Leave the Deyun Club – Is Guo Degang Being Forsaken by Everyone?"

Full text of the resignation announcement:

Due to frequent requests for interviews from all sectors of the media in recent days, we are making a unified response here.

Firstly, we announce: We have officially left the Beijing Deyun Club.

Regarding any questions about the Deyun Club, we will hold a press conference in the near future, and we kindly ask for the understanding of the media friends.

The Deyun Club has greatly contributed to our growth. From the initial difficulties during the early stages to the current success, leaving the Deyun Club was not a rash decision on our part. This decision was made after careful consideration.

Although we have left the Deyun Club, in the days ahead, we still wish the Deyun Club to continue thriving. Finally, we thank the media friends and audience friends for their care, understanding, and support!
