She is very tired now. Please do not disturb her. Sad diary of QQ Zone.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:08:55

She said,

Suddenly, she felt very tired, extremely tired.

Her body and the world quickly fell down, into an unfathomable abyss.

Single track replaying "Sleep Alone".

She said,

Many times, she was just calmly doing some things.

Drinking water, insomnia, doing nothing.

And then suddenly, she had a painful longing for him.

It hurt. Really hurt.

The pain made her bend over and squat down to cry.

She said, she was so sad.

She always thought that by struggling alone, one day she would wake up-

But it let her fall into confusion again and again.

She finally thought the sky would be blue for her,

But it turned dark again.

She wanted her tears to stop, to find an outlet for herself.

In the ruins, she dragged her weary body,

Stretching out the shadow of loneliness.

She said, she always remembered,

His promise of eternal love.

He said he would always be by her side.

He said there was only her in his world.

He wanted to spend every birthday with her.

He fantasized about their life together.

He promised her that as long as she wanted to go, no matter how remote the place,

He would go with her. With her, there was him.

He used to call her baby so affectionately.

He said he would be her dear husband.

He proudly said that she belonged to him.

He said he would never let her cry. Never ever.

He said he would bear all the hardships for her, as long as she was fine.

She wondered,

Is "Our Song" still theirs?

He no longer gave her any promises.

Even the most special nickname is gone.

Even her tears mean nothing to him anymore.

Will that long-absent "I love you" still be remembered and said to her?

The two of them now seem to have less contact than friends.

In a flash,

She finally felt the wound; she saw herself becoming haggard.

She fell in love with Esse again, smoking nonstop day and night.

Hurt herself. Pained herself. But. No one cared.

After his nth broken promise, her tears flooded the city.

She thought, he probably wouldn't call her anymore, right?

She told herself, her heart was truly hurt.

Full of fresh blood, where could it flow?

She asked, why. Why did it come to this.

Why couldn't she see the value in what they had?

She bit herself hard, just like the time when he was most angry with her.

He hated it when she didn't cherish herself.

But stubbornly, she promised to take good care of herself for him.

With one condition: if one day she still hurt herself this way,

Then it's because of him.

Blood came out, so red, she had never been this fierce before.

She smiled, glad that she hadn't broken her promise.

This wound was for him.

Her blood-soaked hands were still touching Esse.

She continued to smoke without expression, humming -

"I love you like a moth to a flame. Yes, like a moth to a flame. Definitely fatal."

Coldly, a woman—

The sky. So blue. Just a little too heartbroken blue.

Life. Free. Just a little too lonely free.

Living. Happy. Just a little too bitter happy.

Time still intertwines.

She said,

She feared she couldn't hold on any longer.

She thought he might also get annoyed.

And she also felt so tired, so powerless.

Even though she has so much unwillingness in her heart.

She... was really tired, tired.

Tired.
