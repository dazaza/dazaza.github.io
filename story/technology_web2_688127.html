■ The Year Festival has never returned home Xie Haishan, who is currently pursuing a deputy senior professional title, although from the post-80s generation, is a veteran volunteer with over ten years of experience. Xie Haishan, who comes from a legal background, started to "learn on the job" psychology and massage techniques due to volunteer work, while his childhood hobby of street dancing became a tool for motivating children to study.

■ Being an older brother in the Baby Village

Xie Haishan was even more concerned about the elderly living alone and left-behind children. For example, after the Wenchuan earthquake, 36 children from the disaster area were unable to return home during the Spring Festival. Xie Haishan specifically launched the project "I have a family in Beijing," finding 18 host families in Beijing that welcomed two children into each household to make dumplings and enjoy the New Year's Eve dinner together.

■ Volunteering as a lifelong career

Xie Haishan said, "Skills will never burden you. As long as it benefits volunteer services, I am willing to learn." Currently, he has started applying psychology in his volunteer work. Talking about volunteering, Xie Haishan picked up a photo of himself with an elderly person. The two looked very close, like a grandfather-grandson pair. "This was taken last Spring Festival when I visited Grandma Wei. If I have time, I visit her every week!" Xie Haishan said.

Every week without fail, Xie Haishan would go to the Baby Village, first checking on the children's academic performance, then teaching them dance. He also made an agreement with the children: "If you study well and finish your homework, I'll teach you street dancing." During a celebration event, his street dance performance captivated two boys from Family No. 5, making him their idol and prompting them to want to learn. Within a month, not only did the two boys improve academically, but they also became proficient dancers, becoming the "little stars" of the Baby Village.

Xie Haishan, coming from a legal background, began studying psychology at Peking University and eventually earned a master's degree in psychology. "Through interacting with more elderly people and children, I realized the importance of psychological counseling for the elderly living alone, orphans, and other vulnerable groups," he said.

During the "5·12" Sichuan earthquake, he collaborated with psychological experts to compile the book *With You*, which was promptly delivered to the earthquake site. In the Baby Village, he also established a psychological counseling room to provide timely support to the children and to relieve stress for the "mothers" hired by the Baby Village.

After arranging a massage session for Grandma Wei, she closed her eyes contentedly and said, "Where did this child learn all this?" While soaking her feet and massaging, Xie Haishan chatted with Grandma Wei about his volunteering experiences and recent news. Gradually, Grandma Wei developed a reliance on Xie Haishan, always asking him when he would come back next time.

Xie Haishan stated, "On the surface, I am giving to these children, but in reality, this makes my life more fulfilling." He emphasized that the Spring Festival should be a joyful time not just for us but also for those elderly people living alone and children without homes.

In 2007, to address the issue of some people having nowhere to contribute their love or find volunteer opportunities, Xie Haishan founded the Starlight Volunteer Association, focusing on helping impoverished children, orphans, and lonely elderly people among other disadvantaged groups.

"I can strive to learn more skills based on the needs of volunteer services, and all these skills will serve the cause of volunteering," Xie Haishan expressed. His actions reflect the spirit of Lei Feng, whose dedication to the party, country, and people has inspired generations of young people to grow healthily.

Our newspaper has launched the "I am Lei Feng" column to comprehensively report on the exemplary models emerging in various industries across the city in practicing Beijing's spirit and learning from Lei Feng, vividly showcasing the vibrant vitality of Lei Feng's spirit among the new generation of young people.
