Like many women do not know what kind of hairstyle suits them. It's better for young people; otherwise, if they still can't find their own beauty even after numerous attempts, that would be a tragedy! Carefully reflecting on this, figuring out a suitable hairstyle isn't difficult, but we have never seriously tried to do it. Imagine if we set aside three days to determine our hairstyle for the next three days, or try a new hairstyle in a week, then two weeks of training will lead to day-to-day care. I believe that within just one month, you will certainly find a hairstyle specifically suited to yourself and won't need to seek advice from friends, colleagues, or image consultants anymore.

In Japan and Europe, a good hair salon is equipped with a large mirror and photo-taking facilities. Your hairdresser will pull you in front of the mirror, considering 11 aspects such as your face shape, height, and the length of your hair to achieve the best effect. In contrast, our situation here is often that when you see the hairdresser, they are busy wrapping towels around you, putting on work clothes, and don't even notice your style or height.

To get an accurate reminder from the hairdresser, you must identify your own face shape and corresponding hairstyles. Take a positive photo, brush up and down, left and right across your face, draw some marks and corresponding links, headrest DVD, and you will get a plan tailored to your face shape. Next, remember the golden rule: face width matches hair thickness. If it's a long face, the fat content should be on both sides; if it's square or round, the fat content should increase so as to stretch it out. In short, you will know how to find your own hairstyle direction.

Another issue is whether to get a perm. You know, dress styles, though varied, can generally be categorized into three types: linear, curve type, and intermediate type, each featuring everyone’s appearance differently. Straight perms make people look obviously older, while only curved perms suit beautiful people. Therefore, recall your feelings about straight hair and perm styles. If the answer is clearly unbalanced, you should stick to the side that feels better; if both sides feel good, then you're lucky, being an intermediate type person who can go either way.

Cultivate your own face and hairstyle with a hair diagram, then comes the stage of interacting with a hair stylist. To find a makeup artist with a great personality and styling skills, ask him in the context of your hair development, how many kinds of hairstyles you can try. Most people after a new haircut always pick out some problems and then commit a fatal error by replacing the hairdresser every time. Remember, in order to have a hairdresser who loves your style, we should give them a chance to understand you. Learn to share with hairdressers and tell them your feelings, even your age, height, and career. This mutual understanding is a delicate process, not an immediate success. Therefore, as long as a hair stylist understands you spiritually, you should visit them at least three times, slowly training them to be your personal hair stylist.

Training yourself to become half a hair stylist's level will certainly help you soon find your heart's desire, the question is how to take care of it on ordinary days. In more stringent hair salons, you often hear: "You better give me hot as usual, don't do nice hair." My heart smiles: How can something so cheap be done well? Nice hairstyles have always been hard to achieve. The hairdresser only gives a basis for modeling, and the rest will all depend on yourself. When visiting the hair salon, the hairdresser will also check your hair with various props and practices, getting ready to come back Yigai. Then continuously "steal strokes" from the hairdresser and practice again and again. Within a month, you will suddenly find that in a busy morning, you only need five minutes to "fix" your hair!
