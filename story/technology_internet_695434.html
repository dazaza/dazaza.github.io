Notification on the 2011 Master Professional Degree Graduate Students Recruitment and Interview for Part-time Students

The interview process is an important component of graduate student recruitment. According to the requirements of the State Council Academic Degrees Committee Office’s "Notification on Recruiting Part-time Master's Degree Students in 2011" (Academic Degrees Office [2011] No. 28) and "Notification on Doing Well in the Recruitment Work of Part-time Master's Degree Students in 2011" (Academic Degrees Office [2011] No. 80), and combining with our school's actual situation, we hereby notify the relevant requirements for the recruitment and interview work of part-time master's degree students in 2011 as follows:

I. Principles of Recruitment and Interview

1. Adhere to the principles of ensuring quality, scientific selection, merit-based admission, and preferring quality over quantity.

2. Uphold fairness, justice, and transparency, ensuring all working procedures are conducted according to regulations.

3. Uphold people-oriented principles, enhance service awareness, and improve management levels.

II. Organizational Leadership of Recruitment and Interview Work

1. The school's graduate student recruitment work leading group is responsible for formulating recruitment plans, basic interview requirements, interview methods, handling special issues, and reviewing and approving the list of admitted candidates.

2. Each college (department, institute) establishes a recruitment interview work leading group, specifically leading and organizing the unit's recruitment and interview work, and formulates the unit's "Part-time Master's Degree Graduate Student Recruitment and Interview Work Details" based on the basic requirements of the Ministry of Education, each teaching guidance committee, and the school.

3. The divisional leaders of each recruiting college (department, institute) are responsible for the unit's interview work, and must assign dedicated staff members who are strong in principle, experienced in work, and conscientious to review the candidates' eligibility.

III. Basic Interview Score Requirements

In accordance with the spirit of the documents from the State Council Academic Degrees Committee Office and relevant professional degree education guidance committees, combined with our school's actual situation, after research and decision-making, the score requirements for the recruitment and interview of part-time master's degree students in 2011 are as follows:

1. Landscape Architecture Master

GCT Total Score: 174 points

Note: In general, candidates should have a bachelor's degree.

2. Public Health Master

Joint Exam Total Score: 136 points

Epidemiology Foundation: 30 points

English: 37 points

Social Medicine: 30 points

Note: In general, candidates should have a bachelor's degree and three years of work experience.

3. Vocational Teachers

GCT Total Score: 162 points

Note: In general, candidates should have a bachelor's degree and three years of work experience.

4. Engineering Master (excluding Project Management)

GCT Total Score:

With Bachelor's Degree: 178 points

Without Bachelor's Degree: 202 points

Note: In general, candidates should have a bachelor's degree and three years of work experience.

5. Engineering Master (Project Management)

GCT Total Score:

With Bachelor's Degree: 204 points

Without Bachelor's Degree: 230 points

Note: In general, candidates should have a bachelor's degree and three years of work experience.

IV. Qualification Review for Candidates

1. All online candidates must submit qualification review materials, and those who do not pass the qualification review will not be allowed to participate in the interview and recruitment.

2. Qualification Review Requirements

Recruiting colleges (departments, institutes) must carefully verify whether the candidates meet the application conditions, whether the qualification materials are true and effective, and whether the application materials are consistent with the registration information.

(1) Verify the candidate's admission ticket and valid ID card

Candidates must hold their admission tickets and valid ID cards for the national joint examination to participate in the interview. Personnel of the recruiting colleges (departments, institutes) must carefully check whether the candidate's appearance, age, etc., match the admission ticket and valid ID card to prevent proxy exams during the interview.

(2) Verify the original certificates of the candidate's academic qualifications and degrees

Certificate requirements for candidates (except for individual professional fields):

Bachelor's Degree Holders: Must have obtained a bachelor's degree before July 31, 2008;

Non-Bachelor's Degree Holders: Must have obtained a university undergraduate graduation certificate from the national education sequence before July 31, 2007.

(3) Each recruiting college (department, institute) must verify the candidate's academic qualification certification report issued by authoritative institutions (see relevant notifications).

(4) Candidates holding overseas academic qualifications or degrees must submit an authentication report from the Ministry of Education's Center for Overseas Study Services.

3. Staff members of the colleges (departments, institutes) must sign opinions and names on the qualified candidates' "National Joint Examination Qualification Review Form for Part-time Master's Degree Applicants ___ Year" (hereinafter referred to as the "Qualification Review Form"), and the divisional leader must sign and affix the seal of the recruiting college (department, institute). The qualification review work adheres to the principle of "whoever reviews, whoever signs, whoever is responsible."

Recruiting colleges (departments, institutes) must strictly conduct qualification reviews according to the document provisions before the interview. Candidates who do not meet the application conditions, provide false information, or cannot provide qualified qualification review materials will not be allowed to participate in the interview. For recruiting units that fail to conduct strict reviews or engage in fraudulent behavior, corresponding measures such as public criticism, limited recruitment, or cessation of recruitment will be taken.

Candidates who do not meet the application conditions, provide false information, or cannot provide qualified qualification review materials, regardless of at any stage, once discovered and verified, will have their admission qualifications revoked, and the responsibility lies with the candidates themselves.

V. Interview-Related Requirements

The exam scores of all subjects in the 2011 National Joint Examination are valid for one year.

The political theory, specialized courses (or basic specialized courses, etc.) question setting and grading work organized independently by each recruiting college (department, institute) must ensure the seriousness, authority, and confidentiality of the examination work. The reference answers and scoring standards for specialized course papers belong to confidential-level materials before activation and use, and no one may disclose them externally or to candidates in any form. In organizing the interview work, recruiting colleges (departments, institutes) must be serious and responsible, safe and accurate, and avoid making mistakes.

Other matters shall be implemented according to the "Huazhong University of Science and Technology Second Stage Examination Methods for Recruiting Part-time Personnel Pursuing Engineering Master and Landscape Architecture Master Professional Degrees" (Research Word [2007] No. 10).

VI. Violation Handling

Providing false information by candidates, forging documents, certificates, files (including electronic files), etc., are all considered violations. If these behaviors are discovered before enrollment, the school will not enroll the candidate; if discovered between enrollment and degree application, the school will cancel the enrollment, render the enrollment notification invalid and confiscate it, simultaneously invalidate the credits of the courses studied by the candidate, not refund the related fees paid, and revoke the candidate's degree application qualifications; if discovered after degree granting, the degree will be revoked according to the detailed rules of degree granting. The school will inform the candidate's unit of the violation and handling situation. If the circumstances are severe and constitute a crime, the judicial organs will pursue criminal responsibility according to law.

VII. Time Arrangement for Interviews and Recruitment

1. January 9, 2012: Announce the interview score line.

2. January 9-10, 2012: Colleges (departments, institutes) submit their interview details and send emails to ylzha@mail.hust.edu.cn.

3. January 11 - February 12, 2012: Colleges (departments, institutes) announce their interview details and organize interviews and recruitment.

4. Submission of Interview Scores:

Before February 12, 2012, fill out the "2011 Second Stage Examination Score Sheet for Recruiting Part-time Personnel Pursuing Master's Degrees" and submit it to the Graduate Admissions Office.

5. Online Enrollment System:

February 10-12, 2012: Colleges (departments, institutes) submit enrollment lists online.

Online Enrollment System URL: http://202.114.15.170/zzlk/

6. Verification and Return of Enrollment Lists:

February 12-13, 2012: The Graduate Admissions Office prints the enrollment lists, and colleges (departments, institutes) verify and return them.

After being reviewed by the Graduate School, the enrollment lists are printed and distributed to each college (department, institute) for verification without errors, signed and sealed by the responsible person of the college (department, institute), and then submitted to the Graduate School Admissions Office for汇总and reporting.

At the same time, colleges (departments, institutes) submit second-stage interview papers and the "Qualification Review Forms" of enrolled candidates to the Graduate School for record keeping.

Colleges (departments, institutes) must strictly implement national and school policies and regulations regarding graduate student recruitment and complete all tasks according to the school's required graduate student recruitment progress. All personnel engaged in graduate student recruitment must strictly abide by work discipline, rigorously explain recruitment policies, and take real responsibility for their own work.

Additionally: Candidates applying for education master's degrees and vocational teachers in other provinces need to pay a 30 yuan per person fee for the basic specialized course exam, which will be collected centrally by the colleges (departments, institutes) and then submitted to the Graduate School.
