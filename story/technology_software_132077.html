Novel Download Reader, completely free!

by youxiruanjian88 on 2009-03-18 08:52:13

[Basic Introduction]

Just knowing the name of a novel allows you to quickly download the contents of each chapter, read the novel content in your preferred style, and package it into various styles of e-books for convenient reading. You can not only read novels but also listen to novels and write novels.

[Software Features]

1. Supports downloading novels from various novel websites, as well as downloading all kinds of online articles.

2. Automatically retrieves the novel directory page URL by novel name and updates with the website's updates.

3. Can download novels with images.

4. Automatically optimizes chapter content, removing unnecessary content.

5. Simply and conveniently set background color, font size, font color for reading, and double-click for automatic smooth scrolling.

6. Can package the entire novel into CHM or TXT format e-books for easy reading on mobile phones or MP3 devices.

7. Supports background music playback and synchronized lyric display.

8. Supports bookmarks, annotations, phonetic notation, simplified/traditional Chinese conversion, splitting/merging chapters, split-volume/full text reading, etc.

9. Freely edit chapter content, search and replace corresponding content, and automatically format.

10. View and set whether chapters have been read, and easily adjust chapter order.

11. Download individual chapters via different URLs, obtain download addresses through chapter names for downloading.

12. My bookshelf, which can categorize all created e-books and click to read.

13. Supports reading novels with different voices.

14. The novel writing assistant can conveniently help you write novels.
