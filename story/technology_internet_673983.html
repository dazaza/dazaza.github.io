Analysis of the current status of foreign trade export and overseas promotion

by yahoobing on 2012-02-24 09:18:38

Most domestic foreign trade enterprises are relatively blindly following trends when carrying out overseas promotion. Without the reference of objective and scientific opinions from professionals, they impulsively make investment decisions. Therefore, our foreign trade companies should consider choosing appropriate means to carry out online marketing.

I believe that the foreign trade staff, managers or decision-makers of foreign trade companies have received sales calls for online promotion, online marketing, website building, overseas promotion, trade platform promotion, search engine promotion, yellow pages promotion, and industry website promotion, either free or paid. While being deeply [harassed] by these telemarketers, they are also troubled by the lack of a company that provides professional foreign trade online marketing services from the perspective of corporate strategic development and offers full-service support for the overseas network promotion of export enterprises. The following points are what a professional-level foreign trade online marketing consultant should do. Companies can also compare the aspects covered by the e-commerce company that provided internet services at the time and consider whether to switch to a more professional service provider.

The common problems in the online marketing process of domestic export enterprises mainly include the following three:

First, excessive reliance on B2B trading platforms, resulting in a single channel.

Nowadays, domestic export enterprises have become quite serious in over-relying on B2B platforms. Some enterprises invest as much as 500,000 yuan in a B2B platform, putting all their promotional expenses and hopes on this platform, hoping to open up overseas sales channels through B2B websites. This is really not a wise approach because many domestic B2B platforms now have insufficient product classification and their professionalism is not obvious.

Have our export enterprises analyzed who would look for products on B2B platforms like Alibaba and what kind of products they are looking for? It can be said with certainty that individual consumers generally would not look for products on B2B platforms like Alibaba. Thus, the customers visiting Alibaba are all business clients. However, large enterprises should not come because they usually have relatively fixed supplier systems, and it is difficult for general small and medium-sized enterprises to enter their supplier lists.

Therefore, most of the buyers coming to B2B websites to look for products are small and medium-sized buyers abroad. What kind of products do these small and medium-sized buyers want to find? Obviously, because they are at a disadvantage in competition, these small and medium-sized buyers come to B2B platforms like Alibaba to find price-competitive products provided by small and medium-sized enterprises, so they can compete with other companies domestically by bypassing importers or agents and selling products at low prices.

Although we can sell our products abroad through these small and medium-sized buyers, their loyalty is very low, making it easy for them to be taken away by other manufacturers, thus failing to become stable overseas sales channels. Moreover, there are countless manufacturers on B2B websites. Customers will negotiate with multiple suppliers and repeatedly bargain. Even if a deal is made, the profit is pitifully low. But those B2B platforms keep raising prices, leaving our companies in a dilemma: doing it isn't good, and not doing it isn't good either. In the long run, this will cause China's export enterprises to lose their core competitiveness in online marketing.

Second, many export enterprises do not pay enough attention to their own website construction and promotion.

A company's own website is the center of its online marketing. Search engines, trading platforms, and other promotions revolve around the company's website. However, many companies neglect the construction and promotion of their own websites, causing them to fail to play any role.

Third, lack of effective monitoring of promotional effects.

Many companies assess the effectiveness of their promotions by the number of orders completed. We know that online marketing is a long-term project, and the assessment of promotional effects needs to be evaluated from a more comprehensive perspective, including the status of website construction, search engine performance, buyer traffic, valid inquiries, and transaction volumes. Some companies may abandon or stop promotions because they don't see orders or only see a few orders in the short term, causing the original promotional resources to be lost.

The Role of Professional Foreign Trade Online Marketing Consultants

What kind of online marketing consultants do export enterprises need, and what aspects should online marketing companies help enterprises improve their online marketing levels?

First, foreign trade online marketing consultants can help foreign trade enterprises analyze and summarize their existing online marketing strategies. Understand the client’s previous understanding and practices regarding online marketing, the funds invested, manpower, and returns obtained. Specifically, this includes the starting point for the company to build a website, what was considered when building the website, whether there is a dedicated person responsible for online promotion, which network advertisements or marketing software were purchased, which service providers were used, and the specific service models and cost situations.

Second, a good foreign trade online marketing consultant can help foreign trade enterprises diagnose and analyze their websites. Currently, more than 90% of enterprise websites have significant issues in their marketing functions. For example, many web design companies use a lot of Flash animations to please clients during website production, which is undesirable. Although Flash looks beautiful, it gives users a poor experience.

Currently, the servers of the vast majority of small and medium-sized enterprises' websites are located domestically, so when foreign users visit, the speed will be affected. A professional-level foreign trade online marketing consultant needs to help enterprises analyze their websites, including analyzing both customer friendliness and search engine friendliness, and analyze the impact of these issues, helping enterprises improve the operation level and utilization rate of their websites.

In addition, professional foreign trade marketing consultants can also help foreign trade enterprises conduct competitor analysis, helping enterprises analyze the online marketing methods of specific industry competitors, estimate their advertising investment and generated results, and compare them to find areas worth learning from and problems to avoid. Competitor analysis also includes comparison and analysis of both parties' websites and promotion channel comparisons.

Foreign trade online marketing consultants can also help foreign trade enterprises develop marketing strategies and choose advertisements or service providers. Based on the analysis of the company's product situation, discuss suitable online marketing strategies with the client, including search engine optimization, online ad placements, online information releases, and registration in trading platforms. During discussions, help clients analyze the pros and cons of various online marketing tools and provide corresponding suggestions for the client's decision-making. At the same time, based on personal experience, help enterprises choose service providers, even including discounts on certain services.

In addition, foreign trade marketing consultants should also do the following four aspects of work:

Guide and train relevant employees of foreign trade enterprises. Understand the working methods of employees related to the company's online marketing and conduct targeted analyses, providing them with professional training to enhance work efficiency. For enterprises, theoretical discussions should not be emphasized; instead, practical operations should be focused on, including how to select good online marketing platforms, what ways can be used for online promotion, how to post information, and simple search engine optimization techniques.

Help foreign trade enterprises maintain their websites. Many companies do not have professional network administrators, or their personnel do not understand website maintenance, so they need guidance from online marketing consultants, or even regular checks and maintenance of the website, including simple content updates and information modifications.

Track and maintain the online marketing effects of foreign trade enterprises, including helping enterprises perform traffic statistics analysis and answering daily questions about online marketing.

Based on the technical development of foreign trade online marketing, recommend new tools and methods to clients, passing on the latest concepts or tools and methods to ensure that the company's online marketing stays ahead in the industry and leads among peers.

At this point, a professional consulting team undoubtedly can provide a lot of practical experience sharing and help to export enterprises, allowing companies to achieve the best results with the smallest promotional budget, improving return on investment and overseas market share, standing out from competitive peers.

Foreign trade companies doing online marketing cannot simply follow trends anymore, doing B2B website promotion, bidding rankings, or building websites without fully leveraging the true intelligence of online marketing. Online marketing is just part of traditional corporate marketing. One must learn to effectively utilize various online marketing tools, carefully selecting one or several methods according to the company's specific situation to achieve good results.
