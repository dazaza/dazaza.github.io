**Exhibition Overview:**

From May 15 to 17, 2012, the West Africa Agriculture Expo (AGRIKEXPO) and the Food Exhibition (FOOSBEXT2012) will be held simultaneously at the exhibition center in Lagos, Nigeria's largest city. The exhibition is co-organized by the Nigerian British Chamber of Commerce (NBCC), the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC), and 151 Products Ltd. It is expected that 20,000 visitors from various countries in West Africa will attend, including major African producers, traders, distributors, government delegations, and associations. A special area will be set up inside the exhibition hall for new product launches and displays, and related seminars will also be held during the event.

This exhibition is a high-end trade show, providing an excellent opportunity for foreign exporters to engage in face-to-face conversations with local African importers, distributors, and traders. It also offers local manufacturers the chance to collaborate with foreign importers. This year’s exhibition is expected to attract participation from 300 exhibitors from over 80 countries.
