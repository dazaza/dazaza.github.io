Policy for the Transfer of Household Registration of Graduates to Guangzhou in 2008 (Application for Guangzhou Hukou)

Graduates from 2007 who have not yet signed an employment agreement and have registered for deferred employment can also be processed as fresh graduates.

Policy for the Transfer of Household Registration of Graduates to Guangzhou in 2008 (Application for Guangzhou Hukou)

I. Academic Requirements for Application:

1. Fresh graduates from 2008, with a bachelor's degree or higher (including bachelor's), and having obtained a degree certificate.

2. Graduates from 2007, with a bachelor's degree or higher (including bachelor's) who have registered for deferred employment and have obtained a degree certificate.

II. Required Documents:

1. One copy of the school’s employment recommendation letter (original).

2. Three copies of the employment agreement (original).

3. Copy of ID card.

4. One copy each of the current employer's "Organization Code Certificate" and "Business License".

III. Household Registration:

1. If there are relatives or friends with household registration in Guangzhou, the hukou can be transferred to their location, subject to the approval of the local police station;

2. If there is property in Guangzhou, the hukou can be transferred to the location of the property;

3. The rest will be collective hukou;

4. All processed hukou will be formal Guangzhou hukou (can be registered in Panyu and Huadu districts).

IV. Procedure:

1. Fresh graduates submit the materials listed in section II to our company;

2. Our company submits the materials to the Guangzhou Personnel Bureau to apply for the acceptance plan;

3. After approval by the Guangzhou Personnel Bureau, a Guangzhou acceptance letter will be issued to the student, along with a notification for payment;

4. The student submits the acceptance letter to the school, and upon graduation, the school issues the "Reporting Certificate" and "Household Registration Transfer Certificate" for Guangzhou;

5. After graduation, the student personally reports to our department with the diploma, degree certificate, reporting certificate, party/youth league relations, and household registration transfer certificate.

6. Our department applies for the Guangzhou household registration quota card on behalf of the student. Once the quota card is received, the student personally goes to the certification center of the district where the hukou is being registered to handle the household registration and ID card. For collective hukou, after we complete all procedures, the student comes to collect the ID card.
