Business Ethics Drop Shipping Platform Memo (The memo is updated weekly, please pay attention. It concerns the major issue of whether the product can be smoothly signed and settled. We hope each student reads it carefully.)

1. Official Website:

Drop shipping platform website: http://vip.sumdao.com (unique)

(Original drop shipping platform: Bidding Alliance: http://www.jingjialianmeng.com has been canceled)

2. Contact Information:

Drop shipping customer service QQ: 976442956, for urgent matters, please directly call the business ethics drop shipping supervisor's mobile phone: 15006045071

3. Fee Calculation:

The calculation formula for drop shipping product fees has been issued, website: http://vip.sumdao.com/js.asp.htm

Approximate freight as follows:

EMS: First weight one pound 20 yuan, subsequent weight one pound 9 yuan, collection fee 15 yuan, collection point 7%, rejection fee double the freight + 15 yuan

SF Express: First weight one kilogram 20 yuan, subsequent weight one pound 9 yuan, no collection fee, collection point 5%, rejection fee double the freight + 10 yuan

EMS fees are high, but coverage is wide, all places in the country can be delivered. SF Express fees are low, fast speed but narrow coverage, generally unable to deliver to towns or rural areas. Students should first understand if the drop shipping platform can use SF Express, confirm during the customer service process if SF Express can be delivered. This is very critical.

4. Working Hours:

Customer service working hours are from noon 12 pm to midnight 12 pm. Due to the busy work of customer service, leave a message describing specific issues directly, customer service will handle them one by one.

5. Customer Orders:

Before VIP students place orders on the backend, please contact customers carefully. Placing an order defaults to shipping, and any resulting costs must be borne by the students themselves. If you want to cancel the order, you must contact customer service, cancellation of the order by customer service is the standard.

6. Rest Time:

No work or shipping on weekends. From Monday to Saturday, orders submitted before 4 pm on the same day will be shipped that day. The tracking number will be updated by midnight at the latest. Orders after 4 pm will be shipped the next day.

7. Product List and Price:

Refer to the "Special Download" section of the drop shipping backend for the product list and price table. Products marked [SF] indicate that the product can be shipped via SF Express, others can only be shipped via EMS. The supply price of the product is noted behind each product, products marked with a question mark are out of stock.

8. Courier Selection:

Whether the product can go through SF Express can be seen from the notes in the special download section. You must clarify whether the customer's location can use SF Express. When placing an order, choosing SF Express or EMS is important. Using SF Express where it cannot reach easily causes rejections, and the cost must be borne by yourself.

9. Follow-up and Settlement:

Due to the varying quality of personnel across the country for EMS delivery, VIP students should follow up on their own orders. For unclear parts, call the area code + 11185 to inquire. Once the order is signed, notify customer service immediately to settle the payment as soon as possible.

10. Shipping Point:

Currently, there are three shipping points for the Business Ethics Drop Shipping Platform: Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Zhangzhou.

If customers inquire about the shipping point without special instructions, it is uniformly stated that the goods are shipped from Shenzhen. The Zhangzhou shipping point is the location of the customer service work, mainly for large client students' shipments.

11. Cooperation Methods:

Currently, the Business Ethics Drop Shipping Platform has three cooperation methods:

(a) The drop shipping platform provides products and shipping services.

(b) Large client students provide their own products to the Zhangzhou shipping point, and the drop shipping platform provides shipping services.

(c) The drop shipping platform provides sources, and students purchase in bulk themselves for their own shipping.

12. Suggestions and Complaints:

The operation of the drop shipping platform requires the support and understanding of all students. Any good suggestions to improve the drop shipping platform can be directly reported to customer service. If the work is not done well, complaints can be made to the Business Ethics complaint department.
