The content of this book revolves around the protagonist Qiao Zhiyong's dream of saving the country. It depicts his intricate and tumultuous lifelong struggle with fellow merchants, high-ranking officials, Empress Dowager Cixi, as well as bandits and the Taiping Army.

"The Qiao Family Compound" Part One

Zhenxiang was explaining to his father the origin of a mandarin duck jade ring: "Ordinary mandarin duck jade rings usually come in pairs, with the ducks carved separately. However, this jade ring has a pair of mandarin ducks carved on it, making it a solitary ring that can only be used as a betrothal gift from a young man to his beloved." Lu Yuhan wanted this jade ring, but her father, known as the "Number One Miser of Shanxi," instructed her to put it back for sale in the store. Not long ago, Lu Yuhan had a frightening experience on Taiyuan's business street when the horse pulling her carriage got spooked and ran amok. At that moment, a tall man emerged from the famine-stricken crowd, forcefully stopping the runaway carriage and saving Lu Yuhan before collapsing from hunger. Lu Yuhan personally revived him by feeding him rice soup spoonful by spoonful. This man was named Tie Xinshi, and seeing his strength, Lu Dake hired him as a carriage driver for his daughter.

"The Qiao Family Compound" Part Two

Xueying became so angry she nearly fainted, shouting, "Qiao Zhiyong, you stop right there!" Zhiyong stopped but did not turn around. Xueying threw the mandarin duck jade ring he had given her at him and snatched back the sachet she had given him. Zhiyong walked away quickly, knowing that if he stayed any longer, he would waver. Xueying collapsed on the ground, her hands reaching upward, crying wildly, "God of Wealth, please tell me, is this heaven or hell?" Cuier and Aunt Li rushed in and carried her out, unconscious.

"The Qiao Family Compound" Part Three

When Zhiyong left Baotou, he left a plaque for the Fu character, inscribed with the two large characters "Hou De." Zhiyong said to Ma Xun, "Ma Da Zongguan, do you know how we merchants save the world and help the people? We must have great aspirations and ambitions, conduct business on a scale as vast as the world, and create benefits for millions of people. This is the way merchants can save the world and help the people!" Ma Xun nodded and said, "Master, only now do I truly understand how to be a merchant for the rest of my life. You watch, Master. No matter how difficult the road ahead may be, Ma Xun will bring the Qiao family business into the Mongolian grasslands, helping the master achieve a business empire as vast as the world, benefiting all people!"

"The Qiao Family Compound" Part Four

The fleet traveled day and night, breaking through the waves. One night, several large ships suddenly appeared on the river ahead, and the soldiers on board forcibly took Zhiyong onto their ship. It turned out to be General Hu Yuanpu's warship. Upon recognizing each other, Zuo Jigao had already told Hu Yuanpu about Zhiyong's daring southern expedition to trade tea, deeply moving Hu Yuanpu. After staying for one day, Zhiyong insisted on leaving the next day. Hu Yuanpu reluctantly let him go and gave him an official document hidden in the sole of his shoe for verification by troops along the way. Before departure, Zhiyong admired the German-made monocular telescope in Hu Yuanpu's possession, who generously gifted it to him.

"The Qiao Family Compound" Part Five

Changshuan traveled day and night, finally arriving in Beijing. Li Deling, upon reading the letter, hastily gathered 300,000 silver taels and sent them to the imperial treasury on behalf of the Guangdong-Guangxi governor's office, obtaining a receipt which he handed over to Changshuan. After enduring countless hardships, Changshuan finally returned to Guangzhou within the one-month deadline. The next day, Zhiyong met with Mao Cai and Mr. Cao to see Ha Fen. Ha Fen was very pleased and told Zhiyong, "Specific details of how to proceed should be discussed with Director Li." After leaving, Mao Cai suddenly said to Zhiyong, "Master, don't worry about the rest. You shouldn't handle this; leave it to me and Mr. Cao." Zhiyong stood there stunned, watching them take charge and accompany Director Li from the governor's office as they departed without consulting him.
