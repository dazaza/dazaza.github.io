"Yuan Hong" captures the beauty of young love between adolescents.

by jqrxass on 2009-08-23 13:06:23

The story begins in the Su Bei water town region of Lixia River in the 1970s, describing the protagonist Ding Cunkou's life journey from the age of nine to thirty-five; from being an ignorant child, a love-struck teenager, an ambitious youth, until his experience of giving up teaching for business because of love.

Part One: Gu Village (Upper)

Cunkou sat slumped under the crooked necked bitter almond tree behind the village, staring blankly at the calm and bright river water of Beida River. His little mouth was pouted, with two streaks of tear marks on his face.

Part Two: "Gu Village" (Lower)

It wasn't until 1979, when Cunkou started junior high school, that he vaguely felt he was about to become an adult. The change began during the summer vacation of that year. Once, after Cunkou went down the river to catch clams, upon getting ashore, he saw a bit of red flesh poking out from the front of him. He wiped it forward with his hand, but it quickly retreated. He went home and asked his brother, saying, "Brother, why is my skin broken in front?" His brother laughed, touched his head, and said, "No, it's just that our Cunkou is about to become an adult." Cunkou blushed.

Part Three: Wu Kiln (Upper)

In 1982, the results of the high school entrance exam were announced, and over a dozen students from Gu Village Middle School were admitted to high school. Cunkou and Xiuping had high scores and were both accepted by the famous Wu Kiln Middle School in their county.

Part Four: Wu Kiln (Lower)

Returning to campus, the familiar environment immediately brought Cunkou back to the sadness of yesterday. The person has gone, things remain the same, and the familiar place no longer sees that familiar figure. But Cunkou feels that Xiuping is omnipresent. When female students walk into the classroom together, he hears Xiuping's laughter mixed in; after evening self-study, sitting under the lampshade studying, he always feels that Xiuping is sitting opposite him gracefully. He subconsciously looks up, but where is she... At night, he sleeps with Xiuping's name on his pillow, often dreaming of her. Waking up in the middle of the night, his face is wet.

Part Five: Field

"Don't steal, it's best not to steal!" Liu Guihai smiled as he said to everyone, "It's like this. I was lazy last night and didn't pour out the foot washing water. This morning, I felt someone take water from under my bed and run out. At the time, I thought they must not have taken the wrong basin. After getting up, I looked and saw that the clean water basin was full. It seems it was still taken wrongly. Alas, it's all my fault for not pouring out the dirty water, and putting it in front of the clean water."

Part Six: Stone Bridge

Cunkou failed the college entrance exam. He fell three points short of the second batch of specialized colleges' admission line. Cunkou simply couldn't believe this fact. His classmates didn't believe it either. Li Jinxiang wrote a letter to comfort him, saying it was all due to the unfortunate cold and trachoma during the exam. Taking the exam while sick or diseased, how could it not be affected? Otherwise, he would definitely have been admitted.

Part Seven: Yangzhou

Cunkou stared at the back view of this girl for a long time. The rain got heavier, and she didn't have any rain gear. She rushed towards the girls' dormitory in the rain curtain, like a bouncing little deer, with her ponytail jumping on her head. He suddenly felt three people's shadows flash before his eyes - Xiuping, Axiang, and Aishiang.

Part Eight: Flower Field

When Cunkou was about to finish his third year, one day Chunni shyly said to Cunkou, "Do you want it? If you want, take it, it's fine." Cunkou was startled, his face changed color, and his body began to tremble uncontrollably. He stammered, "This, this can't..." Chunni hit his sore spot directly: "Are you thinking for me? Are you worried that if we can't be together in the future, you want to protect me fully. But you're wrong, my entire heart and soul are yours. You've already entered the deepest part of my soul..."

Part Nine: Yancheng

Today is the weekend. As soon as school let out, Chunni took Miao'er in a taxi to her aunt's house in Xiangshui County Town to celebrate her uncle's fiftieth birthday. Businessman Cunkou did not go. Businessman Cunkou always has something he can't put down. Businessman Cunkou can only stay at home alone. Being a businessman is considered a free profession, yet the free-profession businessmen are not free.
