One acre --- gathers the culture of blogs and social networking, and ushers in the arrival of the Web3.0 era!

by yinyin on 2007-04-28 20:01:04

The One Mu website, the most comprehensive Chinese blog gathering and interactive social network. Since the release of the Beta version of the site, it has already gathered more than 7 million blogs and 20 million blog posts, becoming an authority in the field of Chinese blog search. Currently, it continues to grow at a rate of over 50,000 blogs and 500,000 articles per day. Leading the trend of blog culture, focusing on the hotspots of the blog world, One Mu will always provide the latest and most comprehensive excellent blogs for everyone.

The original design concept of One Mu was to break through the isolated islands on the Internet and explore human society. We will pay more attention to people's needs on the Internet, starting from people, and build a new type of social network community. In this network, we can promote ourselves through the blog media via the platform; we can also satisfy individual personalized attention to the Internet; furthermore, we can meet the demand for making friends through the blog media. Blogs on our platform will truly become a tool for individuals to meet their own needs. On one hand, it can push oneself into the spotlight of society and focus on the focal points in society, and on the other hand, it can also recognize more like-minded people through participation in the interactions provided by One Mu. We do not think that blogs merely reflect one's thoughts, but further extend the idea that people can lead more meaningful lives through blogs; blogs should not be closed, nor should they be simply a re-evaluation of news or events. It is a tool for people to realize their own needs, so blogs will become an effective medium for connecting relationships between people.

We will use unique search tools to complete content aggregation and individual aggregation, thereby forming a super-media platform and content-individual aggregation place in the blog world. On this platform, people can not only search and customize content based on their own needs, but also achieve searches and studies about others, thereby realizing their social connection needs. We believe that interpersonal interactions are multi-faceted and multi-layered, and through the coupling of blog forms, trust can be generated between people, thus forming more valuable behaviors and interaction patterns, which traditional social models cannot solve. We hope to form a new type of interactive community during the process of satisfying interpersonal interactions, where the interaction between people and the interaction between people and the website will be a core mechanism, making the Internet truly become an equal society.

One Mu, gathers blogs and social culture, and ushers in the era of Web3.0!
