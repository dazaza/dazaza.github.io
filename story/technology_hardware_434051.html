1. Mix liquor with feed to feed chickens, shareholders are limited to their holdings, which can improve the disease resistance and winter egg-laying rate of chickens. Use 5-10 ml of liquor for every 500 grams of feed for chicks, and 15-20 ml of liquor for every 500 grams of feed for adult chickens.

2. Use a cotton ball dipped in liquor to apply to the skin where chicken lice are parasitic, repeat 3-4 times for a complete cure.

3. Treat white diarrhea in chickens. Mix 100 ml of liquor with 50 grams of brown sugar evenly. Large chickens should be given one tablespoon each time, small chickens one quarter tablespoon, twice a day, continue for 2-3 days.

4. Treat rabbit diarrhea. Soak 250 grams of crushed garlic in 500 ml of liquor, take 2-3 ml of the soaking solution each time, dilute three times with water and then administer orally.

Adult rabbits should be given 2-4 ml each time, younger rabbits less accordingly. The wine-garlic liquid can also be mixed with feed for rabbits to eat.

6. Treat white and yellow diarrhea in piglets. Mix 5 ml of liquor (Quxiang liquor is better) with 8-10 ml of soda water or cold boiled water, shake well, inject at the posterior sea acupoint (located at the base of the tail, above the anus) for piglets under one week old. Inject 1 ml per piglet, 2 ml for piglets over one week old, twice a day, they will recover in two days.
