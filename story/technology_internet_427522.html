"Fake identity" partners in fake cigarettes

After nearly a month of hard work, the case has made a breakthrough: On September 17, 2007, the policemen and the tobacco monopoly bureau enforcement officers in Xipu Town, Pixian County, caught the suspect Lai Jinhua who transported counterfeit cigarettes on the spot, and seized fake cigarettes worth more than 150,000 yuan.

In order not to alert the suspects, the police investigated Zhou Ping and Luo Chaoming for three months. Last year, on January 5, they discovered the warehouse of fake cigarettes in Sandhe Town, Xindu District, and arrested Zhou Ping and Luo Chaoming who were loading and unloading fake cigarettes, seizing fake cigarettes worth more than 260,000 yuan. More importantly, the police found an account book with detailed records at the scene, which recorded more than 100 buyers of fake cigarettes, covering within the province and Chongqing, Shaanxi, Xinjiang and other places. The account book also showed that only in November 2007, fake cigarettes worth more than 3 million yuan were sold.

The special investigation team conducted more than a month's investigation on all social relations of Zhou Jiandong, and finally found his fake cigarette factory in Poluo Village, Majing Town, Shifang City, capturing 17 people making fakes on the spot. When the policemen told this news to Zhou Jiandong, he completely collapsed, and voluntarily confessed to his fake cigarette packaging factory located in Yiqiao Village, Zaojiao Town, Shifang City.

When Zhou Jiandong was looking for a place, he thought of his relative Ye Mou who served as a village cadre in Poluo Village, Majing Town, Shifang City. Ye Mou agreed to build his own reserved land into a fake-making factory, with an annual rent of more than 20,000 yuan, and worked in the fake-making factory, responsible for buying vegetables and cooking for the workers. In order to cover up, the gang built the fake-making factory underground, while on the ground there was a "prop" - a chicken farm.

● The "4・08" case uncovered more than 100,000 packs of various kinds of fake cigarettes, involving an amount as high as more than 30 million yuan; the criminal network covered more than 10 provinces and cities, and 34 suspects were arrested.

● The "4・08" major tobacco-related case is the first case supervised by the Ministry of Public Security and the National Tobacco Monopoly Bureau in Sichuan so far, and it is also the largest fake cigarette case solved by the police in our province so far.

The fake cigarette factory was destroyed

The special investigation team repeatedly compared the call records and text messages from multiple mobile phones on Zhou Jiandong, and mastered that a large number of fake cigarettes and raw materials for producing fake cigarettes would be transported from Fujian to Sichuan recently. They separately uncovered two trucks of fake cigarettes and tobacco silk being transported to Chengdu by the gang on April 12 and April 17, with a case value of nearly 10 million yuan.

Small cases lead to "big sharks"

On April 8, 2008, after using technical means, the public security organ locked down the suspect Zhou Jiandong, and captured him in Chengdu in the afternoon of the same day. Thus, the case achieved a major breakthrough and was officially named as the "4・08" special case.

The public security organ found out that since 2004, Lin Zhineng and Ou Yang (at large) had known Zhou Jiandong who was doing fake liquor and fake cigarette business in Chengdu. They specifically sold a large number of fake cigarettes purchased from Fujian, contacting each other in single-line links, and gradually established a huge sales network centered in Chengdu. In September 2007, in order to gain greater profits, the three of them respectively invested 330,000 yuan to establish a fake-making factory. Lin Zhineng was responsible for contacting machinery, equipment, raw materials and fake-making technicians, DNF continuous fire tool, Zhou Jiandong was responsible for contacting the site, and Ou Yang was responsible for production and management.

Facing the reporter's question, Lin Zhineng gave such an answer: "Everyone has put money in, single-line contact, jointly seeking wealth, once someone gets into trouble, it will not easily expose others, it is a 'protection' for everyone. But still got caught..."

In August 2007, the Jinjiang Tobacco Monopoly Bureau of Chengdu reported to the Pixian County Public Security Bureau that someone had been selling fake cigarettes in Pixian County for a long time.
