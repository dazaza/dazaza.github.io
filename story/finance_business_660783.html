Mood essay

by tiany4676 on 2012-02-08 10:56:36

Yesterday I originally intended to never touch QQ again, but now thinking about it, it was impulsive! Actually, I feel that during free time, opening some friends' spaces and carefully savoring their inner worlds can be quite alright. Some of the words may be reprinted just because of liking them, some may be occasional random remarks typed on the keyboard by friends themselves, maybe intentional, maybe unintentional, paired with soft music and colorful animations, transforming an originally empty space into one full of color and extra brilliance. I used to look down on this, but now I think this is also a commendable way to release one's inner world! In fact, if you can calm down and carefully read some articles, we can find that whether they are reprints or personal essays, they all reveal one's inner world; you only re-post because you like it; you only reveal bits and pieces because you like it; because you like it, talented doctor, you let these articles fill your space. A space might be the margin of a person's emotions, joys, angers, sorrows, and pleasures sometimes aren't convenient to share with others, so why not here, lightly tapping the keyboard, pouring out in your small space, which is not bad either. If you don't have the energy or time to write, then why not take a look at your friends' spaces? You'll suddenly discover that some articles are exactly the reflection of your own inner world, you can also write them down, and when you're free, take them out and appreciate them carefully! You will find a unique taste in your heart, when I'm tired, I always like to open my good friends' spaces. From their spaces, I experience more than just pleasant songs and dynamic pictures, most importantly, I gain a sense of resonance akin to finding a kindred spirit through these articles, and at such times, we have many feelings! I hope that I can, through each space, this small online platform, make myself understand life better and get closer to life. On this small platform, our feelings are released without limit, even the passion for life can be fully expressed, and the deep longing for someone can be quietly poured out between the lines of text, perhaps it's just a one-sided wish, perhaps the visitor comes to understand your hard-to-read heart, perhaps you are confused and troubled by the pressures of life, unable to express, only here can you vent thoroughly and unrestrainedly! I've found that many spaces are locked by their owners, perhaps there it's a forbidden zone not to be touched lightly, just a small sanctuary for the owner to vent and release their helplessness, to pour out their yearning, not wanting anyone to step in, not wanting anyone to see their unique scenery, their unspeakable troubles, these are all human nature, everyone has their secrets! I've also locked mine, but I'm not a very subtle person, big Zhou royal family, my troubles are easily guessed by friends, whenever I'm burdened with heavy thoughts and quickly tap the keyboard, I feel that all my troubles helplessly fly away with my five fingers, maybe it's a habit, every day when I log on, I check who visited me yesterday, who watered and fertilized my little garden, who left me warm blessings and sincere hopes, I hope that under the warm gaze of my friends, I become happier and stronger day by day!
