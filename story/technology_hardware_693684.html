Great Wall Haval: Brand Introduction

by myc0n5o52 on 2012-03-06 17:27:42

Haval is positioned by Great Wall Motors as a CUV model (City Utility Vehicle). In Chinese, this refers to a multi-functional urban vehicle, also known as a Crossover. In English, Haval means "free roaming." It is the first independently developed model of Great Wall Motors and initially achieved an SUV legend upon its release. Despite being surrounded by numerous joint-venture brands, it has maintained the top sales position up until now.

In terms of appearance, Haval lacks the ruggedness of Great Wall's previous products. The body lines transition naturally, making the overall style much softer. The bodywork craftsmanship has improved significantly, with seams on the doors and engine hood becoming much smaller. The front face is one of the more distinctive aspects of Haval's appearance; the diamond-shaped headlights combined with the grille and bumper design are quite prominent. Notably, although it is an SUV, Haval's fuel consumption surprises many people. Diesel models, especially those with the 2.5TCI engine, according to most owners, consume only 7 liters per 100 kilometers in actual use. Some long-term owners have even achieved a fuel consumption of 4 liters per 100 kilometers in fuel-saving competitions. Gasoline models, mainly the 4G69 Haval, consume approximately 10 liters per 100 kilometers. Recently, the diesel 2.5TCI Haval has been particularly popular. Thanks to the high-tech engine jointly developed with Bosch of Germany, fuel consumption has dropped significantly, and noise levels are comparable to gasoline vehicles. Therefore, the prospects for this car are very promising.

The overall performance of Haval has comprehensively surpassed all previous products of Great Wall Motors and should be considered as their transformation piece. It is evident that the manufacturer has begun shifting its strategic focus, gradually moving towards mid-to-high-end product series.

In 2009, Great Wall Motors re-planned its SUV products. According to the plan, Haval will be divided into three main categories: small, medium, and high-end. The medium-sized Haval will be the M series, the medium Haval will be the H series, and the high-end Haval will be the S series. This reorganization has greatly promoted the brand focus and market sales of Haval. At Great Wall's 2010 marketing conference, Haval was highlighted as the mainstay product, aiming to secure the national sales champion title in the SUV category.

On December 20, 2011, the 600,000th Haval was officially delivered.

For Great Wall:

Basic Overview

Great Wall Motor Company Limited is China's first large-scale private automobile enterprise listed on the Hong Kong H-share market. It has over 30 subsidiaries under its control, with more than 42,000 employees. Currently, its products cover three major categories: Haval SUVs,滕翼 (Tengyi) sedans, and风骏 (Fengjun) pick-up trucks. It possesses independent supporting capabilities for core components such as engines and transmissions.

Great Wall Motors has twice been selected for Forbes' Top 100 Chinese Companies list; it has consecutively ranked among the top 500 most valuable brands in China for four years; it has been rated as the most valuable automotive listed company, the best-performing automotive listed company in China; it is a recommended export brand by the China Chamber of Commerce for Import and Export of Machinery and Electronic Products and is also a national automobile whole vehicle export base enterprise granted by the Ministry of Commerce and the National Development and Reform Commission.

Since 2004, it has successively been selected for the top ten private listed companies, China's top 500 enterprises, China's top 500 machinery enterprises, China's top 500 manufacturing enterprises, China's top 500 industrial enterprises, and China's top 30 automobile industry sales revenue enterprises.

In 2011, it was awarded the top 30 main business revenue enterprises in the Chinese automobile industry, again selected for China's top 500 enterprises, and Great Wall Motors received the China Famous Trademark.

Sales and Operating Status in 2010 (Chinese Standards)

Great Wall Motors operates prudently, with solid foundations, total assets reaching 23.7 billion yuan, net assets 10.4 billion yuan. In 2010, the group's turnover reached 23 billion yuan, and its growth potential has been recognized by the international capital market, with Great Wall stocks being favored by investors in Hong Kong and internationally.

In 2010, Great Wall Motors sold a cumulative total of 360,000 units, an increase of 73%, far exceeding the average growth level of the entire industry, making it one of the brands with the highest increment and fastest growth rate. Among these:

- Haval SUVs sold 140,000 units, an increase of 135%

- Tengyi sedans sold 120,000 units, an increase of 76%

- Fengjun pick-up trucks sold 100,000 units, an increase of 31%.

Great Wall Motors (2333.HK) 2010 Financial Results Announcement (Hong Kong Standards)

Recently, Great Wall Motors (2333.HK) announced its 2010 financial results, showing leading operating quality and performance among domestic brand automakers.

The post-tax profit attributable to the parent company shareholders was 2.698 billion yuan (RMB), an increase of 163.7%; pre-tax profit reached 3.039 billion yuan, an increase of 226.1% year-over-year; the share company's revenue realized 22.175 billion yuan, an increase of 78.9%.

Earnings per share (calculated based on the number of circulating shares before capitalization issuance) increased from 0.93 yuan last year to 2.46 yuan, an increase of 164.5% year-over-year.

As of December 2010, the company's total assets reached 23.8 billion yuan, with net assets exceeding 10 billion yuan.

Market Position of Three Major Categories

Great Wall Motors consistently maintains a leading advantage in both domestic and international markets, having continuously held the first place in the number of automobile exports and export value in China for many years. In 2009, four main models passed the EU Whole Vehicle Type Approval, which was the first among domestic brands in China.

The Haval SUV category has continuously maintained the top sales and export volumes among domestic brands nationwide for 8 years. Currently, the main products include the medium-sized H series and the small-sized M series. In January this year, famous driver Zhou Yong drove the Haval SUV to participate in the Dakar Rally again, eventually achieving the first place among the Chinese team and the 22nd overall ranking.

The Tengyi Sedan category, each model has its own characteristics and has established a good reputation for quality in the industry. In January this year, the Tengyi C30 won the crown of the 2010 CCTV China Annual Compact Passenger Car.

The Fengjun Pickup Truck category has continuously maintained the top sales and export numbers nationwide for 13 years. It has formed a range of high, medium, and low specifications, demonstrating unique competitiveness in competition with international pickup trucks.

Major New Models Released This Year

A total of 6 new models will be launched in the second half of 2011:

- 3 Haval SUV models, including 2 facelifted models and 1 completely new model. Main products include: 2012 Haval H5 Euro Style Edition, 2012 Haval H5 Supreme Navigation Edition, Mid-to-High End Urban SUV Haval H6.

- 3 Tengyi Sedan models, including 1 facelifted model and 2 completely new models. Main products include: 2012 Tengyi C30, Tengyi C20R, Tengyi C50.

Haval H6: Produced at Great Wall Motors' world-class factory in Tianjin, it is a mid-to-high end urban SUV. Equipped with a fuel-efficient, powerful 1.5L turbocharged gasoline engine, as well as 2.0L and 2.4L gasoline engines and the Green Silent 2.0T turbocharged direct-injection diesel engine, it comes with 5-speed and 6-speed manual transmissions and a 6-speed automatic transmission. The model is planned for release in the second half of the year.

Tengyi C50: Produced at Great Wall Motors' world-class factory in Tianjin, it is a completely new sedan. It will be equipped with 1.5L and 1.5L turbocharged gasoline engines, especially the 1.5L turbocharged engine with a maximum power of 98kw and a maximum torque of 188Nm, which will be the first domestic small-displacement turbocharged model. It will come with a 5-speed manual transmission, a 4-speed automatic transmission, and a CVT continuously variable automatic transmission, expected to be released within the year.
