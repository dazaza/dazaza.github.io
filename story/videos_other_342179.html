Important information affecting tomorrow's stock price at a glance

====Securities News====

The first old revolutionary area revitalization plan is about to be released, Nanjing catering companies will cover many cities in Shaanxi, Gansu and Ningxia.

Following the approval of the "Guanzhong-Tianshui Economic Zone Development Plan" last year, the northwestern region may welcome a new national-level development plan this year.

Wang Yanwu, Director of Tongchuan City Circular Economy Office in Shaanxi Province, revealed yesterday in an interview with the "First Financial Daily" that the preliminary research for the "Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Revolutionary Old Area Revitalization Plan" (hereinafter referred to as the "Plan") has been completed. This plan will cover 9 cities in the three provinces of Shaanxi, Gansu, and Ningxia. However, the specific geographical scope has not been finally determined.

Chongqing launches a 1 trillion yuan investment plan involving 13 categories of projects

Last year, Chongqing ranked among the top three in terms of economic growth rate nationwide. This year, it has launched another 1 trillion yuan investment plan, implementing 323 key projects throughout the city, aiming to complete an annual investment of 200 billion yuan.

The State Council announces the target for eliminating outdated production capacity in ten key industries including electricity

The State Council issued a "Notice", clearly defining the specific goals for eliminating outdated production capacity in key industries such as electricity, coal, steel, cement, non-ferrous metals, coke, papermaking, leather-making, and dyeing within the near future.

====Industry Dynamics====

Catering and tourism industry ranks in the "Top Three" in the first quarter

According to Wind statistics, calculated by the weighted average method based on total market value, from the beginning of this year to the close on March 31st, the overall increase in the Shenwan Catering and Tourism sector was 8.32%, while the Shanghai Composite Index fell by 5.42%. This sector outperformed the market by nearly 14 percentage points. Meanwhile, among the 23 industry indices of Shenwan, the increase in the catering and tourism sector ranked third, only behind the comprehensive and electronic component industries.

Restart of bancassurance growth leads to a sharp increase in matching pressure for insurance funds

In 2009, under the structural adjustment requirements of regulatory authorities, bancassurance premium growth for various life insurance companies dropped sharply to 8%. However, even after the structural adjustment, bancassurance premiums remained the most important source of premiums, and due to their mainly lump-sum payment structure, they were highly volatile and have become a decisive factor in premium growth.

From January to February, large-scale electronic information manufacturing industry continues to stabilize and recover

From January to February 2010, China's large-scale electronic information manufacturing industry continued the stabilization and recovery trend since the second half of last year. Production, exports, and benefits all showed restorative growth, with a year-on-year increase exceeding 20%.

Proposed real estate injection "gives way" to the World Expo, ST Xingmei restructuring encounters setbacks

ST Xingmei's restructuring path encountered further complications. ST Xingmei announced today that it received notice from the reorganizer, Fengsheng Holdings, stating that due to the impact of the World Expo, the land use plan for Hong Kong New World (600628) Garden under construction in Shanghai has changed, which could affect its assessed value.

New management team of Shanghai Medicine debuts, "three fires" burn towards internal integration and external mergers and acquisitions

With the announcement by Shanghai Medicine (600849) on April 1st, the long-awaited new management team of Shanghai Medicine officially made its debut. The management team announced several transactions right from the start, demonstrating strong execution capability to the market.
