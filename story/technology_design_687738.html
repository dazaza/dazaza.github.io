Li Yuchun doesn't shoot movies but holds concerts, come to Shenzhen to listen to Li Yuchun's 2012 songs.

by anonymous on 2012-03-02 17:48:23

In recent years, Li Yuchun has been crazily filming movies, making many of her fans eagerly anticipate her work. Finally, on March 24, 2012, Li Yuchun will hold a distinctive concert in Shenzhen to meet everyone. Fans of Li Yuchun can not only listen to their idol's songs but also enjoy the beautiful scenery of Shenzhen.

**The Eight Scenic Spots of Shenzhen that Li Yuchun Visited**

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Dapeng Fortress**

Dapeng Fortress, located in Dapeng Town, Longgang District, eastern Shenzhen, is officially called "Dapeng Guard Command Post." It was established to resist the Japanese pirates and covers an area of 110,000 square meters, originally built in the 27th year of Hongwu during the Ming Dynasty (1394). It is currently Shenzhen's only national key cultural relic protection unit. The residence of the famous anti-British general Lai Enjue during the Opium War, "Zhenwei General's Residence," and the "General's Residence" of Fujian naval commander Liu Qilong are grand in scale and impressive in atmosphere. In September 1839, Lai Enjue commanded the victorious Kowloon Sea Battle against the British, which marked the beginning of the Opium War. This place is ideal for visiting ancient relics, understanding history, and appreciating the ancient style of the Ming and Qing Dynasties.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Lian Mountain Spring Morning**

Lotus flowers have long been revered for their beauty and purity. Lian Mountain, located at the northern end of Shenzhen's Central District, gets its name from its resemblance to a lotus flower. Covering a total area of 166 hectares, it is the largest public green space in the central district. Lian Mountain is one of Shenzhen's most important municipal parks, with rich landscapes and picturesque mountains and waters. There is a statue of Deng Xiaoping on the rooftop square, offering a panoramic view of the entire central district.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Overseas Chinese City Splendor**

The Overseas Chinese Town scenic area, located in the western part of Shenzhen, includes theme parks such as "Splendid China," "Chinese Folk Culture Village," "Window of the World," and "Happy Valley." It integrates traditional Chinese culture, Chinese folk culture, and the essence of world cultures, blending natural and cultural landscapes. Modern technology like sound, light, and electricity is used to enhance performances akin to Broadway-style song and dance shows in the East. Combining dynamic and static elements with ever-changing attractions, it leaves visitors spellbound.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Shenan Street Overflowing Colors**

Shennan Avenue runs through the central section of Shenzhen and is the city's busiest road. Starting from the intersection of Yanhe Road near Jiujiu Grand Hotel in the east and ending at Nantou Checkpoint in the west, it features eight lanes in both directions with wide green belts separating the lanes, stretching 17.2 kilometers in length. Along this avenue lies the essence of Shenzhen’s architecture, its most important tourist attractions, and renowned enterprises. It is one of the few streets domestically that exhibits highly modernized characteristics. The Diwang Tower stands as Shenzhen's tallest building along this street.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Wutong Mountain Mist**

Wutong Mountain's main peak reaches 944 meters, making it the highest peak in the Pearl River Delta region, with a natural feature known as "Wuling Tianchi." Currently, there are botanical gardens like Xianhu Botanical Garden, Donghu Park, Forest Park, and Sports Park constructed here, making it one of the best spots for mountain climbing and sightseeing. Standing on the Hao Han Slope, you can enjoy views of Hong Kong, Dapeng Bay, Yantian Port, and Sha Tou Jiao bathed in morning light, creating a sense of vastness and tranquility. At the foot of the mountain lies Hongfa Temple, the largest Buddhist temple in Shenzhen.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Meisha Waves**

Meisha Beaches, large and small, are located along the coast of Dapeng Bay in Shenzhen. The mouth of the larger beach spans about 2000 meters, while the smaller one stretches around 800 meters. The sand is yellowish-white, fine, flat, and soft, resembling a crescent moon embedded between lush mountains and azure seas, earning it the nickname "Oriental Hawaii." It is a great place for vacations, leisure activities, wave-walking, and fitness. Not far away, the Eastern Overseas Chinese Town in Sanzhoutian is one of China's first ecological tourism demonstration zones.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to One Street Two Systems**

Zhongying Street is located in the Shatoujiao Town of Yantian District, Shenzhen. The boundary stone inscribed in 1898 marking "Guangxu 24th Year Zhongying Boundary No." divides the original Shatoujiao into two parts: the eastern side being the Chinese territory of Shatoujiao and the western side the British (Hong Kong) territory of Shatoujiao, hence named "Zhongying Street." It still serves as the symbol of the "one country, two systems" border. Zhongying Street is renowned worldwide for its unique political and historical significance.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Yangtai Stacked Emeralds**

Yangtai Mountain, located in Bao'an District, has a main peak elevation of 587.3 meters, abundant rainfall, and a pleasant climate, making it an important source of Shenzhen's rivers. The mountain boasts lush forests and wildlife. Below Yangtai Mountain lies the Hakka residential area. During the Anti-Japanese War, the Yangtai Mountain guerrillas rescued hundreds of renowned cultural figures and patriotic democrats, including Mao Dun, Zou Taofen, and He Xiangning, from the Japanese-occupied Hong Kong, transferring them safely and secretly to the Yangtai Mountain area. Hence, Yangtai Mountain is also known as the "Hero Mountain."

**Other Landscapes**

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Window of the World**

Window of the World is a large-scale cultural tourism scenic area located in Overseas Chinese Town, Nanshan District, and one of the most famous tourist attractions. Covering an area of 480,000 square meters, it integrates world wonders, natural scenery, folk customs, and folk dances into one park, recreating a wonderful world. The World Plaza, World Sculpture Garden, Paris Spring Shopping Street, and Jurassic World together form a diverse and stunning artificial theme park.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Mangrove Nature Reserve**

The Mangrove Birds Natural Reserve is located on the northeast shore of Shenzhen Bay at the mouth of the Shenzhen River. Covering an area of 368 hectares, it is China's only nature reserve within a city and the smallest one, also referred to by foreign ecological experts as a "pocket-sized reserve." Every year, over ten thousand migratory birds of 189 species, such as white egrets, black-headed gulls, and little curlews, stop or winter here.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Shenzhen Safari Park**

The Safari Park is built beside Xili Lake in Shenzhen, covering an area of 1.2 million square meters. It is China's first subtropical new-style ecological garden integrating zoo, botanical garden, and science popularization garden functions. The daily large animal square art performance is a grand production unseen in any other world zoo, featuring thrilling and mysterious spectacles that give visitors a sense of "experiencing wildness and returning to nature."

**Li Yuchun's Visit to China Folk Culture Village**

China Folk Culture Village is the first large-scale cultural touristic area in China that gathers various ethnic folk arts, customs, and residential architectures in one park, located beside Shenzhen Bay. Covering an area of 200,000 square meters, the village reflects Chinese folk customs from different perspectives based on the principle of "originating from life, surpassing life, gathering scenic spots, and selecting carefully."

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Green Green World**

Green Green World is located at Moon Bay on the southern slope of Da Nan Mountain in Shenzhen, covering an area of 200,000 square meters. Here, natural charm blends with ethnic culture, refined agriculture complements tourism, forming a modern mountainous wild interest. Small wooden houses built on the slopes offer short stays for tourists. The horticultural hall has introduced and cultivated hundreds of varieties of flowers from Japan, the Netherlands, and other countries and regions. The orchard gathers more than ten kinds of fruit trees, bearing fruit all year round.

**Xianhu Botanical Garden in Shenzhen**, primarily a tourist attraction with scientific research and popular science as supplementary functions, is located on Wutong Mountain. It preserves over 3,000 plant species and owns the world's largest fossil forest.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Xiantouling Ruins**

Located 4.46 kilometers southwest of Wangmu Village Committee in Dapeng Town, Longgang District, on the seaside sand dunes behind Xiantouling Village on the eastern shore of Dapeng Bay, these ruins represent a typical coastal prehistoric sand dune site in the Pearl River Estuary region. Compared with other sites, this site has yielded the most systematic artifacts and the most advanced craftsmanship in vessel production. Archaeologists have developed a relatively mature technique for excavating sand dune sites, successfully discovering the earliest known human activity relics in the Pearl River Delta region.

**Li Yuchun's Visit to Chawan Cannon Fort**

Chawan Left and Right Cannon Forts are located at the tip of Shekou Peninsula, where "the dangerous terrain controls three sides." They control the east and west sides of Chawan Port, overlooking Lingding Ocean. Chawan's geographical location is crucial; according to historical records, ancient ships traveling between Guangzhou and Southeast Asian countries all passed through this area. Since the Ming Dynasty, the government had set up watchtowers near Nanshan Mountain to guard against pirates. The construction of the cannon forts began during the Kangxi era of the Qing Dynasty.
