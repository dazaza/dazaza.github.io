Together with the leader of the Taiping Dao Sect, Zhang Jue, and Ji Caiyao, the renowned astrologer and master of the Xuanji Palace, they discovered signs of the ancient mechanism [Seven Stars Wheel] that seals the Demon Realm Gate beginning to weaken.
