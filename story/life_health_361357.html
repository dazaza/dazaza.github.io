Health and Wellness - Sun exposure can improve sleep quality. For insomnia caused by stress, experts suggest getting more sun exposure. This is because the human body has a hormone that controls sleep - melatonin. Getting more sun exposure during the day inhibits the secretion of this hormone through light exposure. At night, it is fully released to help you fall asleep quickly.

Health and Wellness - Going to bed early helps protect liver health. Medical evidence shows that apart from genetic and infectious factors, most liver diseases are related to staying up late. When you stay up, blood supply shifts to the brain, reducing internal organ blood supply and causing liver hypoxia. Prolonged lack of oxygen can damage the liver. From 11 PM to 3 AM is the optimal period for liver detoxification, so it's recommended to go to bed before 11 PM.

Health and Wellness - Patients with liver disease can eat bamboo shoots to prevent liver cancer. For patients with hepatitis B, cirrhosis, and other liver diseases, consuming lettuce, bamboo shoots, and asparagus regularly in three meals a day can significantly protect the liver and prevent cancer. These foods have the effects of eliminating phlegm, diuresis, detoxification, and helping to expel waste through feces.

Health and Wellness - Ten fingers can help strengthen your body and remove diseases. The ten fingers are actually ten acupuncture points in acupuncture science, called well points. Exercising them can invigorate the spirit, refresh the mind, and open the senses. How should one exercise them? It's quite simple: just slap your palms together. Use the fingers of one hand to slap the palm of the other hand, doing both sides of the fingers once; then reverse the process.

Health and Wellness - Adopting the correct posture when taking medicine can better enhance its efficacy. Taking medicine requires proper posture to maximize its effectiveness and avoid adverse reactions. How should it be done? 1. Bedridden patients should take medicine in a sitting position, using 60 milliliters of lukewarm water. 2. Regular individuals should drink at least 100 milliliters of lukewarm water while standing for one and a half minutes to achieve good results.

Health and Wellness - Women eating red meat can replenish iron and combat fatigue. Studies show that eating more red meat rich in heme iron is crucial for maintaining women's energy levels. Iron is an important component in hemoglobin production, responsible for oxygen transport and metabolism within the body. After women supplement iron, their physical stamina, mood, and concentration improve. Therefore, women must pay special attention to their dietary iron intake.

Health and Wellness - If frequently exposed to smoke, supplement more vitamin C. If you often live in a smoky environment, don't forget to supplement vitamin C! Cigarettes contain high concentrations of reactive oxygen species and free radicals, which can severely damage the lungs. Persistently taking vitamin C can break down nicotine toxins and effectively reduce the incidence of lung diseases.
