In recent years, the general trend of Shanghai's college entrance examination bonus policy is only reduction and no increase. Not only will the existing bonus items be continuously adjusted and reduced, but it is also unlikely that new bonus items will appear. However, compared to the high school entrance examination where competition bonus points have been canceled, the college entrance examination policy has clear regulations on competition bonuses, and although some science and technology competitions do not offer bonus points, they are highly regarded by universities in their independent recruitment processes. Shanghai is one of the four provinces or cities in the country that have increased the number of participants, so the competition among senior three students appears relatively "heated".

According to reports, high school science competitions are mainly divided into two modes: subject-based competitions and non-subject-based competitions. In subject-based competitions, the most prestigious is the "National League" organized by the Science Association, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science. The Education Commission stipulates that students who win first prize in the National League can receive bonus points in the college entrance examination. It is reported that most of the students who won first prize in previous National Leagues were admitted to prestigious universities like Tsinghua and Peking University.

The Olympiad of the Mind is an international creativity activity for young people. Its purpose is: to develop the creativity of young people, cultivate two spirits - the spirit of innovation - encouraging uniqueness; team spirit - encouraging seven members to work together. It requires participants to achieve the combination of thinking and doing, science and art, nature and society.

The top winners of the following competitions can receive bonus points in the college entrance examination: the first prize winners in the individual category of the National High School Physics Competition (Shanghai Division), the National High School Mathematics League (Shanghai Division), the National High School Chemistry Competition (Shanghai Division), the National High School Biology Competition (Shanghai Division), and the National Youth Informatics Olympiad Regional League (Shanghai Division).

Mr. Yao Huiqi, the organizer, introduced that the OM competition is very helpful for developing students' creativity. Moreover, high school students who win awards can get many opportunities for further education. Some students can directly receive university bonus points during independent recruitment interviews, and many universities also value this competition highly, making it a non-subject-based competition with considerable prestige.
