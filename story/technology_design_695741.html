Hongchang Paint's first station theme "20 Years of Paint Craftsmanship" Wealth Forum was successfully held in Henan.

by hongchang81 on 2012-03-07 17:04:38

Embarking on the Path of Brand Strategy Transformation and Upgrade: Building a Century-Old Brand in China's National Coatings Industry - Hongchang Paint's First Station with the Theme "20 Years of Lacquer Skills" Wealth Forum Held in Henan

Report by our staff reporter Huang Changjun: On December 7, Hongchang Paint's first station wealth forum themed around "20 Years of Lacquer Skills" was successfully held in Zhengzhou, Henan. With this, Hongchang Chemical's themed promotional activities will be fully launched nationwide. More than 100 people attended the forum, including Director Zhang Zuiduan of the Henan Decoration Materials Quality Inspection Association, Chief Technical Engineer Yang Zesheng of Hongchang Chemical, Director Liu Fang of Hongchang Chemical's decorative paint department, General Manager Wang Xianmin of Hongchang Paint (Henan) Marketing Center, Hongchang Paint Henan marketing center team, outstanding national dealers, new customers, and media representatives.

Before the meeting began, the host read out the congratulatory message sent by Chairman Chen Haowei of Hongchang Group from the UK. Subsequently, Liu Fang and Wang Xianmin delivered keynote speeches on "Hongchang Paint's Breakthrough Strategies in the New Era" and "Understanding the Way, Cultivating Virtue, Unity of Knowledge and Action: Hongchang Paint's Development Plan and Marketing Strategy for the Next Three Years in Henan."

In his keynote speech, Liu Fang provided a comprehensive introduction to the future development of the coatings industry, Hongchang's 20-year journey in lacquer skills, its management model, marketing strategies, and future plans. Liu Fang stated that during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan," the coatings industry would experience a "warm sun" with significant potential market forces, where architectural coatings are expected to capture 70% of the market share. He also introduced Hongchang's 20-year lacquer skills development history, its "148" management system model, and its distinctive marketing mode. Additionally, he emphasized the interpretation of Hongchang's lacquer skills culture. According to Liu Fang, Hongchang will be the first brand to use the internet to establish a painter culture and will continuously conduct lacquer skills conferences across the country as part of its new marketing strategy, vigorously promoting lacquer skills culture and striving to build it into a century-old brand in China's national coatings industry.

Wang Xianmin expressed in his speech that Hongchang would develop into one of the top three in the coatings market through three major marketing strategies: network-based operations, smooth channels, and terminal-centric focus. Wang Xianmin also expressed confidence in achieving this goal. Subsequently, Li Jianxing, director of Henan TV station’s production department, and veteran dealer Li Jianzhong of Hongchang Paint made speeches respectively.

During the conference, Ms. Chen Xuetao, the brand promotion specialist of Hongchang Chemical, introduced the franchise plan for Hongchang Paint and the chain promotion. The forum also included on-site franchising, ordering, and lottery draws. It is understood that the total on-site orders at this conference reached 1.28 million yuan.

It is known that Hongchang Chemical has achieved remarkable accomplishments in its 20 years of development, which can be marked by "2, 3, 5." Hongchang Chemical is currently the coatings company that undertakes the most provincial and national-level coatings research projects and is one of the companies with the most national invention patents and scientific progress awards. It is also the first national-level post-doctoral research workstation in the coatings industry, the first domestic brand of wall paint exported to the US, and the first coatings company to undertake the national "863" high-tech project. It was among the first batch of companies to pass the 3C mandatory certification, adopt international coating production standards, be rated as a key national high-tech enterprise, pass quality and environmental system certifications, and receive recognition as a well-known trademark and brand product in Guangdong Province.

This conference themed around "20 Years of Lacquer Skills" aims to promote Hongchang Chemical's determination and overall direction for brand strategy upgrades, clarifying the positioning of Hongchang's brand strategy and corporate culture for 2012 and beyond. It also aims to clarify product positioning, market customer positioning, and brand positioning. At the same time, it strengthens the cooperation between distributors and the company, guiding distributors in maintaining and operating the brand, enhancing distributor confidence, building a win-win path for manufacturers and distributors, and collectively making the Hongchang brand stronger to realize the beautiful vision of "Building a Century-Old Brand in China's National Coatings Industry."

The successful convening of the first station marks the overall direction of Hongchang's brand strategy development, laying the foundation for Hongchang's future development. The attending distributors and new customers expressed their determination to closely follow Hongchang's steps, actively participate in Hongchang's brand transformation and upgrade, and work hard to strengthen the Hongchang brand for the realization of "Building a Century-Old Brand in China's National Coatings Industry."

It is learned that Hongchang Chemical started its national recruitment journey from Zhengzhou, the first station. This first journey in Henan will initiate the path of Hongchang Chemical's brand strategy transformation and upgrade. In 2011, as an important year for Hongchang's brand transformation and upgrade, Hongchang will achieve greater success in the national market with even stronger brand power, advancing towards the goal of "Building a Century-Old Brand in China's National Coatings Industry."
