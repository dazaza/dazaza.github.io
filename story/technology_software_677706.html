The deceased, Pan Dengliang, was from Fengting Town, Luodian County, and had come to Jiaosuan Village, Luotuo Township in the same county over a decade ago as an "in-law son." "He didn't talk much usually, but he liked drinking," this was a common response from villagers during the police investigation.

On February 12th, China News in Duyun reported (by Zeng Song and Yang Fan) that the Guizhou Qianan police announced on the 12th that a sixty-year-old man in Luodian could not bear the domestic violence anymore and killed his in-law son with a stone. Relevant police officials stated that when facing various family disputes that are difficult to resolve, people should use legal weapons to protect their legitimate rights and interests.

On February 10th, the police officers of the county public security bureau went to the village and conducted publicity activities to prevent and stop domestic violence by explaining cases to the villagers. The police told reporters that "the public security organ will increase legal propaganda work for such cases, warning the masses, especially women, children, and the elderly, to enhance self-protection awareness, and when facing various family disputes that are difficult to resolve, they should use legal weapons to protect their legitimate rights and interests."

After investigating the deceased's life situation, family, and social relationships, the police found that the deceased's father-in-law, Luo Xiaoen, had significant suspicion of committing the crime. In the interrogation room, Luo Xiaoen confessed to the process of killing Pan Dengliang.

On the afternoon of February 8th, Pan Dengliang went to a neighboring village to drink alcohol. To avoid being beaten and scolded by Pan Dengliang after drinking, his wife left home with their child. After drinking, Pan Dengliang returned home and started cursing when he saw Luo Xiaoen cutting pig feed. Luo Xiaoen picked up a stone by the stove and threw it...

Recently, the body of Pan Dengliang from Jiaosuan Village, Luotuo Township, Luodian County, Guizhou Province, was discovered by villagers who were chopping firewood on the mountain. The deceased lay in a vegetable field behind his house.

When the police examined the scene, they found bloodstains in the living room and the vegetable field of the deceased's house. There was an open wound on the left temple of the deceased's head. Based on the scene, the police determined it to be a homicide.

"This is a family shame, I have been enduring it so as not to be laughed at by the villagers. Now, what I am most worried about is my grandson and my father who is over 80 years old. Can I see my grandson?" Luo Xiaoen expressed his greatest wish to the police.

"I chose him because he was honest and hardworking, and let him become an in-law son," said Luo Xiaoen. "In recent years, Pan Dengliang has changed like a different person. He drinks every day, and after drinking, he beats and curses around the house... Later, even my father who is over 80 years old got beaten."
