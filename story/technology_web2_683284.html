Just as Luo Lin was waiting for his girlfriend's answer, the surrounding passengers shouted, "Marry him! Marry him!" Luo Lin saw his beloved accept the flowers and immediately took out the prepared ring, placing it on He Wenjing's finger.

"Because of love, we don't easily feel sorrow, so everything appears in a happy form; because of love, it grows simply, still able to make me go crazy for you anytime." A song by Faye Wong and Eason Chan, "Because of Love," touches people with its gentle emotion. In reality, due to love, a 'bullet train romance' unfolded. Yesterday, on the D5183 train from Nanchong to Chengdu, two young people who met through bullet trains proposed in the train, reaping the sweet fruits of their bullet train love.

Holding the ticket, Luo Lin proposed and successfully won the heart of his beauty. Actually, He Wenjing did not fall for Luo Lin at first sight, but eventually, Luo Lin won her heart through persistence and thoughtfulness.

On April 17, 2011, on the D318 train from Chengdu to Beijing West, Luo Lin was woken up by He Wenjing. When he opened his sleepy eyes, he was struck by a sudden feeling: wasn't this girl the one who often appeared in his dreams?

Why choose to propose on the bullet train? In fact, they met and fell in love because of the bullet train. Two young people who met through bullet trains completed their proposal on the train.

To clarify the situation, He Wenjing called Luo Lin three times. When Luo Lin woke up, "I instantly felt that this girl was the one who often appeared in my dreams." Ten minutes later, he decided to find He Wenjing, but she was cold towards him. During the last three hours of the journey, whenever there was an opportunity, Luo Lin would talk to He Wenjing and managed to get her phone number.

In August last year, the sleeping car service stopped, but their feelings did not fade because of this.

Among the attendants on that sleeping car was He Wenjing, responsible for carriages 10 to 12. Luo Lin, who should have been in compartment 3 of carriage 11, inexplicably stayed in carriage 10. He Wenjing thought Luo Lin was a soft seat passenger who sneaked into the sleeping car overnight when no one was looking, "At the time, I asked him if this was his berth? He answered 'yes'."

Yesterday at 12:28 PM, Luo Lin boarded the D5183 bullet train operated by He Wenjing from Nanchong, but his destination was not just Chengdu. Luo Lin held eleven pink roses anxiously waiting for He Wenjing in carriage 4. After five long minutes passed, she still hadn't appeared.

The male protagonist of this 'bullet train romance' is named Luo Lin, a white-collar worker in Beijing. The female protagonist is named He Wenjing, a bullet train attendant - a charming and sweet-looking attendant with a friendly smile. Both are from Dazhou.

On the platform, the waiting love

In April last year, the sleeping car between Chengdu and Beijing was still in operation. Luo Lin, who had briefly stayed in Chengdu, was preparing to return to Beijing, "Originally, I wanted to buy a plane ticket back, but while buying tickets for my parents to return home to Dazhou, I casually bought a sleeping car ticket to Beijing."

When this sleeping car journey ended, Luo Lin asked He Wenjing when they could meet again. He Wenjing said, "Take more sleeping cars." During their second meeting, since He Wenjing's train arrived at Beijing West Station at 7 AM, to avoid missing her, Luo Lin waited near Beijing West Station the day before. The next morning, he appeared on the platform holding roses and two handwritten love letters, confessing his feelings to He Wenjing. Unexpectedly, He Wenjing rejected him gently via text message.

Luo Lin did not give up. Afterwards, whenever there was a sleeping car with He Wenjing on duty, Luo Lin would come to the station with flowers, expressing his love. To let He Wenjing eat the freshest cherries, Luo Lin even flew specially to deliver them to her. Gradually, He Wenjing accepted this persistent and thoughtful young man.

With the help of the train conductor Huang Jia, He Wenjing was called to carriage 4. At this moment, Luo Lin knelt down with roses in hand and loudly said to her, with whom he had been in love for nearly a year, "On April 17th last year, this ticket made us meet and fall in love. I am willing to spend my entire life protecting you and loving you. Will you marry me?" Luo Lin took out a ticket that marked the beginning of their love, the 2011 April 17th Chengdu to Beijing West D318 train ticket, officially proposing to He Wenjing.

Luo Lin, who succeeded in his proposal, said that to be with his beloved day and night, he would resign from his job in Beijing and develop in Chengdu.
