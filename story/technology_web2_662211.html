In the league, Dortmund still have to face the competition from Bayern, Schalke and Monchengladbach, but in the cup, Dortmund are only two wins away from lifting the championship trophy. In the quarter-finals of the German Cup, Klopp's team easily defeated the dark horse Kiel team 4-0. The only thing that made Klopp dissatisfied was the condition of the pitch, he strongly criticized: "If you have to play on a field without underfloor heating in February weather, then the venue should be changed."

The Kiel club made many preparations for this match, the grass was newly laid, but due to the lack of underfloor heating, the whole field was very hard, and if players fell, there was a possibility of injury. In 2006, while playing for Bremen, Klose injured his shoulder during a game against St. Pauli under similar conditions. This made Klopp worried throughout the entire 90 minutes, as Bender and Goetze's injuries had already caused significant losses for Dortmund, and if anyone else were absent, it could affect the upcoming league matches.

Fortunately, the match wasn't intense, and the Dortmund players did a good job protecting themselves. Within just 18 minutes, Dortmund led their opponents by 2 goals, and in the second half, Barrios and Perisic each scored another goal, leaving no doubt about the victory. Captain Kevin Kyle once again played an outstanding game, and he might soon receive a reward: a new contract until 2013, with a one-year priority renewal clause.

Despite winning the game and having no player injuries, Klopp couldn't help himself, describing the match as "playing hockey on grass." He said, "Anyone who thinks I shouldn't complain should come here and try jumping themselves." The only thing that moved him were the fans here, many local Kiel fans applauded for Dortmund's performance, showcasing the defending champions' charm. German media reported that to catch a glimpse of Dortmund, many fans paid up to 1000 euros for tickets.
