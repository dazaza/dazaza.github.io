Forum Promotion

The forum promotion mentioned here absolutely does not mean posting advertisements one by one on forums, nor does it mean adding the online store address to the signature and then spamming crazily. That consumes energy and the effect is not good either. Forum administrators can delete all your posts with just a few clicks of the mouse, and even ban your ID. Moreover, the impact brought by such actions is negative, just like sending spam emails which people find annoying. Once on a certain forum, I saw a promotional method used by a website that was much more sophisticated.

It was a small website providing job-seeking, employment, and learning materials. Their promotional staff turned various articles on the website into link forms, categorized them properly, and posted them on different forums. This way, it could bring convenience to those who need it, while at the same time not being deleted due to overly direct advertisements. The promotional effect was undoubtedly better. If these materials were posted in the corresponding sections of forums that fit their positioning, they would surely attract a lot of clicks.

Search Engine Promotion Methods

Search engine promotion refers to methods of promoting online stores using search engines, classification directories, and other network tools with online information retrieval functions. Since the basic form of search engines can be divided into web spider-type search engines (simply called search engines) and human-based classification directory search engines (simply called classification directories), the forms of search engine promotion also correspondingly include methods based on search engines and methods based on classification directories. The former includes search engine optimization, keyword advertising, bidding ranking, fixed ranking, content-based advertising, and many other forms, while the latter mainly involves website registration in appropriate categories of classification directories. With the further development and changes in the form of search engines, some other forms of search engines have also appeared, mostly based on these two forms.

The methods of search engine promotion can be divided into various forms, common ones include: registering free classification directories, registering paid classification directories, search engine optimization, keyword advertising, keyword bidding ranking, web page content positioning advertising, etc.

From the current development trend, the position of search engines in online marketing remains important, and is recognized by more and more enterprises. The ways of search engine marketing are also continuously developing and evolving, therefore, suitable ways of search engine marketing should be chosen according to environmental changes.

Email Promotion Method

Using email as the main promotion method, common methods include e-publications, member newsletters, professional service providers' email advertising, etc.

User-permitted Email marketing differs from spamming (SPam). Permission marketing has obvious advantages over traditional promotion methods or non-permitted Email marketing, such as reducing ad annoyance for users, increasing the accuracy of potential customer targeting, enhancing customer relationships, improving brand loyalty, etc. You can let your customers leave their email addresses, obtain their consent, and then send them some product information, discount information, etc.

Resource Cooperation Promotion Method

Through link exchange, ad exchange, content cooperation, user resource cooperation, etc., mutual promotion between online stores with similar goals is achieved. The most commonly used resource cooperation method is the online store linking strategy, which uses the traffic resources of partners to promote each other.

Each online store can own its own resources, which can be expressed as a certain amount of traffic, valuable content and functions, network advertising space, etc. Using the website's resources to cooperate with partners, achieving resource sharing, and jointly expanding benefits. Among these resource cooperation forms, link exchange is the simplest form of cooperation, and surveys show that it is also one of the effective ways to promote new websites.

Link exchange, also known as reciprocal links, is a simple cooperation form between online stores with certain complementary advantages, which means placing the LOGO or store name of the partner's online store on their own online store and setting up a hyperlink to the partner's online store, so that users can discover their own online store from the partner's online store, achieving the purpose of mutual promotion.

The role of link exchange mainly manifests in several aspects: obtaining traffic, increasing users' impressions when browsing, gaining advantages in search engine rankings, increasing the credibility of visitors through recommendations from partner online stores, etc. Link exchange has a deeper meaning than whether it can achieve direct effects. Generally speaking, each online store tends to link to other online stores with high link value, so obtaining links from other online stores also means obtaining recognition from partners and similar online stores within a field.

Information Release Promotion Method

Releasing relevant online store promotion information on other websites that potential users may visit, using the opportunity for users to obtain information on these websites to achieve the purpose of website promotion. Websites suitable for these information releases include online yellow pages, classified ads, forums, blog websites, supply and demand information platforms, industry websites, etc.

Information release is one of the commonly used methods for free promotion, especially in the early stages of internet development when the amount of online information was relatively scarce. Often, satisfactory results could be achieved through information release. However, with the explosive growth of online information, the role played by this method relying on free information release has gradually decreased. At the same time, with the emergence of more effective online store promotion methods, the importance of information release in the commonly used methods of online store promotion has significantly declined. Therefore, relying solely on sending large amounts of free information has little value. However, some targeted and specialized information can still attract significant attention, especially when these information are released on highly relevant platforms.

Online Advertising Promotion Method

Online advertising is one of the commonly used online marketing strategies, and has obvious roles in online brand building, product promotion, and website promotion. Common forms of online advertising include: Banner ads, keyword ads, classified ads, sponsored ads, Email ads, etc. BANNER ads rely on web pages as media, keyword ads belong to a form of search engine marketing, and Email ads are a type of permission-based Email marketing. It is evident that online advertising itself cannot exist independently and needs to be combined with various online tools to achieve the function of information transmission. Therefore, it can also be considered that online advertising exists in various online marketing tools, just in different specific forms.

Using online advertising for online store promotion has the advantages of a wide range of selectable online media, diverse forms, strong applicability, and timely placement. It is suitable for any stage of the online store's launch and operation.

Flash Promotion

For example, when a new MP3 or sketch comes out, turn it into a flash animation, add a small strip of advertisement at the bottom, because well-made flashes spread widely. Of course, you can also contact existing flash authors to place advertisements and pay a small fee.

Link Promotion

Regardless of the method (QQ, MSN, forum, MAIL, etc.), publish the link to your online store, or hire someone to help promote it. As long as someone clicks on this link and visits your online store, give them a certain amount of commission or a consumption coupon from your online store. People recommended to your online store for shopping will also receive corresponding discounts.

Keyword Optimization

When people buy things, they usually start by searching for related products. When setting product names, design commonly used keywords to allow buyers to search for your store and products multiple times, or use words like lowest price and special offers. Describe the products as detailed as possible.

Data Promotion

If your online store operates certain products, write product data and small tips. In the text, you can include your online store address, or set it up in the signature for promotion.

In-store Event Promotion

Analyze the buyers of your online store

Based on previous buyers, analyze the characteristics of these customers, such as their age, occupation, age, hobbies, etc. Corresponding promotions can be carried out on forums and places they frequently visit, promoting according to the different characteristics of buyers. At the same time, actively seek business opportunities and target purchases accordingly. Success or failure is secondary; the key is to have an active awareness.

Additionally, always pay attention to "buyer purchase request" information. There might be buyers who need what you have in your store. Even if we don't have what the buyer requests, we can discover many potential customers. Add them as friends and recommend products similar to what they requested. It's easy to make a deal.

Therefore, everyone can work on keywords and also take advantage of holidays to carry out promotional activities. This will bring more customers. Remember, the T-shirts must be of medium quality or above; otherwise, they'll become rags after one wear, which isn't worth it! Reasonable Use of Auctions

Auctions can be single-item auctions or multi-item auctions (Dutch auctions). Taobao has a dedicated section for auctions on its homepage. Buyers see it as soon as they open the homepage. Choosing the auction method means buyers can easily find us, and since auction prices are generally low, they are more likely to attract buyers. Therefore, auctions are very helpful in increasing store popularity.

Have you noticed that Taobao actually takes care of sellers in many aspects? They launch many promotional activities. Some shopkeepers occasionally put out one or two items for one-yuan auctions. This should be done according to one's ability. Moreover, once a one-yuan auction is done, one should be prepared to lose money. After the buyer successfully bids and makes a purchase, ensure the quality of the item sent to them, even if it costs money. One-yuan auctions are for earning good reviews and popularity, so avoid receiving bad reviews, which would be counterproductive.

Offline Promotion

The offline promotion methods introduced in this article all require some cost. Online store owners should act according to their capabilities.

Gift Advertising

The following gifts are distributed for free, in bustling areas with high foot traffic. Eco-friendly bags, because they are used frequently now, with low production costs, print the online store's relevant information on the bag. Calendars, with the online store's information printed on them. Mouse pads, with the online store's information printed on them, distributed for free, given out for free in internet cafes and office buildings.

Campus Agents

Nowadays, many college students have spare time, no base salary needed, only commission is provided, allowing them to promote both online and offline, unlimited means. Provide them with free business cards (with titles like campus general agent of the online store) and a set of materials, let them promote and sell, earning money while effectively training their marketing skills, spreading word-of-mouth among student unions.

Street Questionnaire Surveys

Hire part-time workers for this, where the survey is secondary and the main goal is promotion.

Before the publicity campaign, conduct an online survey

Targeted surveys, such as piloting on Xici, conducting surveys and answering questions on products and usage issues, safety issues that people care about. During the process of answering questions, subtly promote your online store.

Roadside Lightbox Advertising

Primarily using bus stop lightboxes, bus advertisements, bus body advertisements, internal banner advertisements, etc.

Hire a few students to dress up as popular cartoon characters like Superman, Ultraman, Pleasant Goat, Big Big Wolf, etc. in busy downtown areas.

Of course, they should have online store advertisements on them, and tourists may take photos with them, resulting in good promotional effects. The media is always looking for news, and they love stories like this. Once it hits the newspapers, the traffic will come.

Internet Cafe Collaboration

Collaborate with various internet cafes, paying a small fee to set the default homepage of browsers to the online store's homepage. Find 30-50 internet cafes, and when more people go online at these cafes, it will bring some traffic.

Place a small standing sign at the front desk or cashier of restaurants, hotels, and leisure venues

This small standing sign should include a brief introduction of the online store and its URL, etc. Customers might glance at it while consulting or paying. Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., sometimes they will take a look.

Purified Water Advertising

The customer base for ordering purified water is very broad, including office buildings, hospitals, schools, government agencies, families, etc. It's not difficult to look at an advertisement while pouring water, right? We can collaborate with manufacturers to stick advertisements on the water barrels and pay a small fee.

Printing Business Cards for Wide Distribution

You might say that since most of the promotion is done online, people don't meet each other, isn't making business cards a waste of cost? Wrong! The store may be online, but you live in real life, and mailing items to buyers happens in reality. Design a unique business card, print your contact information and the store's address on it, and include a few in the mailings. Due to the exquisite design, buyers won't throw them away, and if there's an opportunity, they will definitely show it to others, thus helping you promote.

Distributing Business Cards or Flyers

After printing the business cards, it's time to distribute them. Printing flyers is cheap now, costing only a few cents per piece. Print them and distribute them in places where potential customers gather, such as near schools, etc. Different products face different customers, so when applying this strategy, choose the right place.

Tell Your Family and Friends

You've opened a store online, does your family know? Do your classmates and friends know? Be sure to tell them as soon as possible. Leave messages on alumni records and share the joy of opening a store with your classmates.

Organize Relatives and Friends to Help Promote

Since relatives and friends already know you have an online store, ask them to help you promote it.

You've grown up knowing quite a few friends, from elementary school to junior high, and then to high school and university, you've had many classmates. So mobilize them, ask them to help us promote.
