NOVOKING: A Fashion Bar in the Virtual World

by yinyin on 2008-07-05 15:31:31

The bar in the NOVOKING virtual community is not only a true mirror of real bars. Through a 3D platform, one can also create a bar of their ideal style and carefully manage their bar life.

When people are still immersed in the trend of going to bars, the ever-trendsetting new generation has already discovered another whole new world - the virtual bar. The birth of virtual bars has allowed those who love urban bar culture to express all uniquely styled bars they have experienced in reality through a 3D platform in a virtual community. This allows each resident who visits here to feel as if they are truly experiencing everything in a real bar via their computer screen. Meanwhile, the NOVOKING virtual community that has emerged domestically is gradually becoming the new generation's entirely new leisure fashion bar community.

This virtual community known as the "Bar Paradise" houses bars and nightclubs with distinct styles, realistically depicted according to the geographical environment of real bars. Some, nestled by water, are built on the edge of the sea; others, finding peace amidst the hustle and bustle, are built on city streets; some, boldly individualistic, are located above discos; and others, warm and romantic, are situated in the center of islands. The music in the bars varies according to the different styles of real bars, featuring primarily rock, blues, and jazz, which remain timeless. More popular among residents, however, are the original works of the residents within the virtual community, performed and shared publicly through avatars, using this method to stage authentic live music performances in the virtual world. The people in the bars are all “bar hoppers” who love bar culture, dressed fashionably, behaving elegantly, moving about various corners of the virtual world. In this infinite space, they relax, enjoy music, exchange friendships with passersby in the city, and perhaps even meet someone special.

Thus, the virtual bar transcends being merely a reflection of reality, offering an innovative platform where creativity, social interaction, and entertainment converge seamlessly, creating a unique experience for its users.
