Introduction to "Heart of Fire 3":

Hero Saves the Day, Survives Great Calamities

Fang Luan'an (played by Huang Zongze), born with a great fortune, was once predicted not to live past the age of thirty. However, having survived numerous calamities since childhood, his character has become one that craves excitement and indulgence. Unable to stop them in time, Luan'an fled the scene in panic and met with an accident, falling into a fast-flowing water pit with his car. Fire Captain Zhong Youcheng (played by Wang Xi) happened to drive by and saved both of them with great effort. After this incident, Luan'an not only regarded Youcheng as his savior but also admired his courage and skills immensely. Just as Luan'an thought he had found his life's purpose, he was arrested by the police, accused of being involved in the arson case at the dog track. Luan'an, falsely accused, decided to find the real arsonist to clear his name. Eventually, after much searching, Luan'an found the youth who started the fire at an old shopping mall karaoke. At that moment, due to the illegal storage of excessive dangerous goods in the mall's restaurant, a fire broke out, trapping both of them inside. Youcheng arrived on the scene upon receiving the report and managed to save Luan'an and the youth at the last moment. After this incident, Luan'an became even more convinced that Youcheng was a great hero and yearned to be able to save people like Youcheng one day. Therefore, he resolved to take the firefighter exam. Unfortunately, misfortunes never come singly. The youth Luan'an saved not only refused to confess but also accused Luan'an of being the real arsonist. Feeling utterly disheartened, Luan'an planned to give up taking the firefighter exam when he encountered Youcheng again. Encouraged by Youcheng's own experiences...

Suffering Brothers, Love Quarrels

Luan'an learned that all of Youcheng's achievements were hard-earned. Thus, he decided not to abandon his ideals, continued to train physically, and resolved to take the firefighter exam. Baiyu realized he needed to complete his father's wishes and took responsibility for caring for his mother and sister, so he applied for the position of fire captain. Seeing how Youcheng took responsibility for his family, Baiyu admired Youcheng for doing what he himself could not do and encouraged Youcheng to become a firefighter. After Youcheng transferred to become a firefighter, he became Baiyu's subordinate. They risked their lives together and became good brothers who shared hardships. At that time, Baiyu met a woman named Gao Huiying (played by Hu Xinger). Although attracted by Huiying's innocent character, Baiyu did not pursue her because of his unwillingness to take responsibility. With Baiyu's help and encouragement, Youcheng finally obtained the position of apprentice fire captain. When Baiyu learned that Youcheng had a crush, he became Youcheng's love advisor, teaching him how to pursue girls. Later, he discovered that the person Youcheng liked was actually Huiying. To cherish their friendship, Baiyu kept his feelings for Huiying hidden. Seeing that Youcheng was a responsible man and her father Gao Houde also liked Youcheng, Huiying began a simple dating relationship with Youcheng. In terms of work, Youcheng continuously achieved success and eventually became a fire captain just like Baiyu. When both reached the same rank, Youcheng began to feel overshadowed by Baiyu, suspecting that Baiyu only helped him superficially while secretly looking out for himself. He even began to doubt the relationship between Baiyu and Huiying, causing friction between the two brothers.
