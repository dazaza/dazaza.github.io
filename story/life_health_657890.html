Toothache is not a pleasant experience, so how to relieve toothache quickly?

Firstly, treatment for toothache should be based on different causes:

1. For residual roots, residual crowns, and diseased teeth that cannot be retained, they should be extracted as soon as possible, and then get dentures after two months.

2. Food impaction pain can be relieved by using dental floss to remove food debris.

3. Acute pulpitis and acute periapical periodontitis should be treated with pulpotomy, and the placement of phenol-soaked cotton balls in the cavity can relieve pain.

4. Acute periodontitis and periodontal abscess. When the abscess has not formed, you can clean the tartar, take periodontal Ning, antibiotics, and apply iodine glycerin externally; if the abscess has formed, it should be incised and drained, while using antibiotics to control infection.

5. Dentin hypersensitivity. You should avoid brushing horizontally, and avoid too strong movements. Use vertical brushing method. Choose anti-acid, desensitizing toothpaste or 10% soda glycerin toothpaste, which has certain efficacy. You can also go to the hospital for desensitization treatment.
