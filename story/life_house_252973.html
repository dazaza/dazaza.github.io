How to schedule renovation time

by longersing on 2009-10-28 14:40:07

How many days does it take to renovate? How to reasonably arrange the renovation time? Home renovation generally takes about 45 days, depending on the size and style of the house. Some simple renovations can be completed in about half a month, while others, including both renovation and decoration, may take more than two months. Below are some experiences.

It is understood that renovating a three-bedroom house of 140-150 square meters takes about 45 days from start to finish. Smaller two-bedroom small units can save four or five days, while duplexes over 200 square meters will require an additional four or five days. The house renovation process can be divided into three phases: early stage, middle stage, and late stage.

The early stage of renovation mainly involves design work, from selecting a renovation company to finalizing the design plan, which takes 10-15 days. During this period, customers also need to select the main materials for renovation, especially tiles and cabinets, as most cabinets are custom-made and take 20-35 days to complete. Professional cabinet designers will design the kitchen based on actual conditions, such as determining the location of water and electricity circuits, so that timely water and electricity modifications can be made after the project starts. Tiles are the first material used after the project begins, so during the early stage of renovation, you must first select the cabinets and tiles.

The middle stage of renovation is mainly the construction period. After the project begins, plumbers and carpenters enter the site first. Water and electricity circuit modifications take about 4 days to a week, and carpenters take about 15 days to make ceilings, background walls, and on-site made cabinets, etc. About seven days later, after the water and electricity circuit modifications are completed, masons begin laying tiles, starting with the kitchen and bathroom, and finally the common areas, continuing until around day 30. Around day 15, when the carpenters have finished their work, painters enter the site to perform wall putty leveling, painting wooden works and walls, etc., continuing until two or three days before the renovation is completed. During this time, you can go to the market to look for furniture you like.

After the above construction is completed, the remaining part is basically the late stage of renovation. At this point, there are only two or three days left until the renovation is completed. Carpenters return to install curtain rods, hardware fixtures, etc. Floorboards, doors, sanitary ware, cabinets, etc. ordered from the factory also begin to be installed sequentially, and the kitchen and bathroom ceilings (different from the public area) are also suspended at this stage. These tasks are relatively simple and quick, each taking about one day, and all work can be completed in about three days.

Furniture: It's advisable to order about 10 days in advance

It is understood that the production cycle of furniture manufacturers in Beijing is generally around 10 days. If the ordered furniture is a product of an out-of-town manufacturer, the delivery time will be longer. According to market information, the production cycle of Guoan Jiamei is 10-15 days, Aieruisi is about 10 days, Supu Li's production cycle is usually 12 days, and Dongfang Baisheng is several days.

Industry experts suggest that customized furniture like wardrobes requires 10 to 15 days and can be customized around day 30 after the project begins, allowing the furniture to enter the site promptly after construction ends.

Imported Products: If the ordered products are imported and not available domestically, it may take more than a month for delivery. However, if there is stock, the time is similar to domestic products.

Customized Products: Some products are ready-made and can be picked up directly, while customized products require a certain amount of time for the manufacturer to produce them. Consumers should clarify the situation when selecting products.

Factory Production Cycle: The production cycles of different factories vary, and arrangements should be made according to the factory's production schedule.

Different building materials have different ordering times

The time required to purchase floorboards depends on whether the factory has inventory. If there is stock, delivery and installation can generally be done within a week. "A three-bedroom house of over 100 square meters can be installed in one day," said Wang Qingqiang, manager of the marketing department of Shengxiang Flooring. "But if the selected color and style are not in stock, the time is uncertain." He suggests customers pre-arrange the time with the factory.

Doors and cabinets are mostly custom-made products, and the production cycles of different factories vary. Generally, they take 20-35 days to produce and should be ordered during the early to mid-stage of the renovation.

Tiles and sanitary ware generally have stock and can be picked up directly, but some tiles may require cutting, which could take some time.

Custom-made fabric curtains take about two or three days and can be made during the later stages of the project.
