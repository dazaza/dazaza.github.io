1. Objective: To strengthen the transparency of the purchasing process, embody the principles of openness, fairness, impartiality, and honesty, and strive to reduce purchasing costs.

2. Organizational Structure and Functions:

2.1 The Bidding and Tendering Work Group leader shall be the President, with the Finance Director as the convener. Members will include the President's assistant, procurement price comparison personnel, the responsible person for the tendering unit, the procurement responsible person for the tendering unit, other relevant personnel from the tendering unit, and other individuals (such as external experts).

2.2 The primary function of the Bidding and Tendering Work Group is to directly participate in large-scale bidding and procurement activities of the shareholding company and its various industries, supervise and guide the bidding work organized by each industry.

2.3 Small-scale bidding and procurement activities will be organized by the general manager of each industry with relevant personnel, but should be reported to the procurement price comparison personnel of the shareholding company. The procurement price comparison personnel of the shareholding company may directly participate in the bidding or conduct post-bid supervision.

3. Scope of Bidding:

3.1 Basic construction projects carried out by the shareholding company and its various industries, including exploration, design, construction, supervision, and the procurement of major equipment and materials related to engineering construction.

3.2 Equipment or technical renovation procurement for the shareholding company and its various industries.

3.3 Bulk material procurement for the shareholding company and its various industries that are suitable for bidding should all undergo the bidding process.

3.4 Other material procurement besides bulk material procurement that meets the bidding conditions should also undergo the bidding process.

3.5 For purchases exceeding 50,000 yuan throughout the year that meet the bidding conditions, if they can be bid in stages, they should be done so to frequently adjust purchase prices and reduce procurement costs. For some simpler bulk material or small-scale material procurement, a simplified bidding procedure can be used.

4. Methods of Bidding:

4.1 Open Bidding: The company invites unspecified legal entities, organizations, or individuals to participate in the bidding through a bidding announcement.

4.2 Invited Bidding: The company invites specific legal entities, organizations, or individuals to participate in the bidding through a bidding invitation letter.

5. Work Procedures:

5.1 Bidding Request and Process

5.1.1 If the procurement unit applies or the shareholding company deems it necessary to carry out bidding procurement, the bidding plan must be submitted to the bidding work group ten days before implementation. The bidding plan should specify the name, quantity, estimated unit price, estimated total amount, supply time, technical requirements, quality, specifications, models, etc., of the intended procurement items, and provide the names, addresses, telephone numbers, contacts, and other necessary information of six or more qualified suppliers.

5.1.2 After receiving the bidding plan, the bidding work group will convene a bidding meeting within two working days to discuss the materials provided by the bidding unit. If the submitted materials are incomplete, they should be supplemented within a specified period. Simultaneously, the work procedures, bidding methods, and bidding requirements will be studied.

5.1.3 Prepare the bidding documents and announce the bidding information. Based on the requirements of the bidding project, the bidding work group will request potential bidders to provide relevant qualification certification documents and performance records in the bidding announcement or bidding invitation letter, and conduct a qualification review of the potential bidders. If the state has specified qualification conditions for bidders, those regulations will be followed.
