She stared at Yi Lumi's black long hair and her own being blown together by the wind, intertwining strand by strand. Before Dreaming of Emperor Qianlong's Past and Present (the previous publication title was Qing Dragon Han Phoenix), it was probably because her hair wasn't black that even though the wind was strong, she could still tell whose the black silk was. ?? ?? The interwoven silk forms dragons and phoenixes, carved with clouds and mist, an inch of the same heart thread, a hundred years of longevity flower. She suddenly remembered this poem - in this moment, it seemed as if the wind blew sand into her eye socket, as if it wanted to make her cry before it would be satisfied, making her eyes red. ?? ?? Yi Lumi didn't look at her slightly red eye socket, didn't say anything, just pulled her off the deck.
