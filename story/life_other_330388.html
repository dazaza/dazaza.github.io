Expo Love Visit Fund sponsors 1,000 teenagers to visit the Expo

by hongsesiyuan on 2010-02-26 10:59:18

November 20th News: It was learned from the Expo Group recently that in order to allow more young people to visit the Shanghai World Expo 2010 and experience the achievements of civilizations from all over the world, the Expo Group specially established the "World Expo Love Visit Fund". On the occasion of the establishment of the group for 10 years, the "World Expo Love Visit Fund" has exceeded 1 million yuan, and the funds raised will invite 1,000 children from poor areas to visit the Shanghai World Expo 2010.

The World Expo is a stage for the application and display of the latest scientific and technological achievements, and a grand event for cultural exchange between the East and the West. A successful World Expo can not only promote the development of the national economy, but also immeasurably improve the civilized quality and spiritual outlook of the people. An increasing number of young people in Shanghai are actively participating in the Expo, but teenagers in revolutionary old areas, ethnic minorities and remote areas also hope to get out of their homes and get closer to the Expo. The establishment of the "World Expo Love Visit Fund" helps them realize their dreams.

Participating in the Expo, serving the Expo, and dedicating to the Expo have always been the goals pursued by the Expo Group, which has participated in many Expo projects. With the progress of the Expo, the design and construction of landmark engineering projects such as the "Expo Center", "Theme Pavilion", and "China Pavilion" constructed by the Expo Group have been carried out one after another. The direct subordinate enterprises of the group have also achieved remarkable results in serving the Expo project. Modern International Exhibition Company participated in the entire "Enter the Expo" exhibition tour, and Shanghai Advertising Company planned a large number of Expo activities, including the knowledge competition of the countdown 1000 days activity of the Shanghai World Expo and the selection of the first best Expo sponsor.
