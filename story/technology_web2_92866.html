Free music player: Ewin Interactive

by xjyzhenai on 2008-09-18 14:08:16

Adding music to your blog and showcasing your own musical taste is a great idea. Xiantian IT.Net uses the 8Box player. Here, Jarry would like to recommend another unique free music player to everyone: Yiying Interactive.

The Yiying Interactive player has absolutely personalized and cute player skins, super cool animation effects, and allows for skin changes and song editing without altering the player's code. It supports various playback modes such as shuffle, repeat, and sequential play, and also features song sorting functionality. You can directly select songs from a song library, and when the webpage opens, the player can automatically start playing songs. It also supports transparent display, which allows the player to seamlessly integrate with your blog.

Click here to preview the player skin styles. The only downside is that Yiying Interactive does not come with a media server, so the added tracks can only be sourced from the internet and cannot be uploaded or stored by users from their computers. Over time, the song links may become invalid, which could affect the user experience on your blog.
