Introduction to the plot of "Silver Tower Golden Powder":

The first largest silver shop in Foshan, Guangdong, "Shang Silver Tower", is facing a business crisis. Lord Shang (played by Qin沛) obtains a loan from the Shen family by forcing Second Master Shang Yun (played by Ng Wai Kwok) to marry Miss Shen Yongtong (played by Yeung Man Yi) with the help of his wife Sai Xiaodie (played by Carmen Cheung), causing Yun's beloved Cheng Xiuxing (played by Eva Wu) to angrily marry into the Shang family as the third concubine. From then on, Yun becomes enemies with Kun and travels far overseas, cutting off relations with his brother. Not until the day of the old master's birthday does Yun return home, but suddenly his youngest son is kidnapped. Kun refuses to admit that he wrongfully accused a clerk at the Shang Silver Tower, which eventually led to his death, causing another conflict between the brothers. As a result, Yun decides to split the family.

On the other hand, since Yun returned, Xiuxing has been constantly neglected by Kun. Faced with her uncle-in-law, she feels deep pain. She is also falsely accused of causing the miscarriage of the fourth concubine Feifei (played by Dixie Hu). After much thought, Xiuxing decides to retaliate against the Shang family, using Yun's remaining feelings for her. She secretly manipulates the entire Shang family and exposes that the close maid Qiujun (played by Stephanie Yeung) is actually the illegitimate child of Kun and a married woman. This series of blows causes the once prestigious family to lose its reputation and fall into ruin...
