The currently on-sale Area A mainly offers 120-150 square meter fully renovated duplexes, with an average price of around 13,000 yuan per square meter. Area C offers 67 square meter two-bedroom units, 85 square meter three-bedroom units, and 100-120 square meter duplexes, with an average price of 10,500 yuan per square meter for the flat floors and 11,000-12,000 yuan per square meter for the duplexes, all fully renovated.

[Project Status]

AVIC City - International Community, as the first compound-type large community in Xinglin Bay, is developed by the powerful central state-owned enterprise AVIC Real Estate, located beside Yuanboyuan, the largest water park in Asia. The first phase includes plots A and C, which are fully renovated high-rise residences, sky villas, and townhouses, with a total construction area of 780,000 square meters. In the future, the scale of Xiamen AVIC City could reach 1.6 million square meters, becoming a fully functional living city integrating international residential areas, villa areas, libraries, science and technology museums, large urban complexes, and waterfront commercial streets. In December, Xiamen Foreign Languages School moved into plot C, with two provincial-level prestigious schools shaping the children's future. The wonderful life of AVIC City meticulously crafted by top domestic teams is gradually unfolding!
