The largest C2C intelligent trading platform, Taozhi.com, has quietly revamped its website.

by vanwins on 2008-06-16 11:22:55

IT Observer Room News: China's largest C2C intelligent trading platform (www.tallzhi.com) quietly went online on the 15th, giving a completely refreshed feeling to the new version. The website's navigation, column settings, functions, trading process, crowdsourcing tasks, and personal intelligent stores have all been fully revamped.

In the official redesign announcement, Taizhi.com stated that in order to better meet business needs and enhance customer experience, this 2008 edition of Taizhi.com is the largest-scale redesign in history.

Taizhi.com COO Qiu Qiping said: "After more than a year of development, Taizhi.com has evolved from an initial intelligent goods trading platform into a comprehensive intelligent trading platform, and launched a crowdsourcing task reward channel at the end of 2007. During this process, we received strong support from a large number of netizens and media. Currently, the website has over 700,000 members, nearly one million intelligent products and crowdsourcing tasks. Thousands of enterprises and individuals have gathered satisfactory works and solutions through Taizhi.com, and tens of thousands of members have earned their first income on the Internet through Taizhi.com; numerous domestic and foreign media such as Southern Metropolis Daily, Shenzhen Special Zone Daily, Fortune Times, Computer World, Russia's International Wire Agency, Britain's Economist magazine, Hangzhou Television, Huasheng International Media, etc., have comprehensively reported on Taizhi.com, and it was selected as one of Shenzhen's first key cultural enterprises in December 2007. In such a context, the website must keep up with the times, both in terms of presentation and functional application, requiring improvements that match its development. This is not just a superficial change; we will further strengthen the application process of reward tasks and the intelligent trading model, laying the foundation for the website's upcoming expansion."

Finally, Qiu Qiping also revealed that with the launch of the new version, they will soon cooperate with Kadang.com, China's largest personalized gift customization platform, to jointly create the top brand in creative design. This will also mark the beginning of Taizhi.com's business cooperation.
