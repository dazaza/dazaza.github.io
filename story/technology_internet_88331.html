Taobao Story: A 1500 Yuan Order Gained in One All-nighter

by longyi on 2008-08-31 14:17:34

The store has been open for more than a month, and I have almost every day stayed by the computer to do publicity and post messages, which has now made me become "the lowest rank official"; even when sleeping, as long as I hear the "ding dong" sound, I will immediately jump up...

Although very tired, my heart is sweet.

There is always a reward for effort; in a month, customers have gradually come "to the door", sometimes I am too busy to handle it all. I was so excited that I couldn't sleep well for several days. Hehe, don't laugh at me.

That day, it was already past 1 o'clock in the morning, I was just about to log off and rest when I heard someone send me a message. It turned out to be an inquiry about my product, and all of a sudden my sleepiness disappeared, and I patiently explained it to him.

After nearly an hour of conversation, he bought 7 pieces of my dd!

I was so surprised that I couldn't close my mouth for half a day. At that time, I only had a few credit points, how could he trust me so much?

I started to doubt. But after a subsequent round of haggling, until he paid through Alipay, my mouth opened even wider. When I came back to celebrate, that gg left me a sentence: "Little sister, I really doubt whether your product craftsmanship is exquisite. If I am not satisfied with the goods received, I will still return them without hesitation." Then he went offline. Only then did I realize: actually this deal wasn't truly done yet. Don't be too happy too early.

The next morning, I started going to the factory to pick goods.

In order to compare the craftsmanship, I ran to more than 10 factories, picking each item one by one, not letting any flaw pass. Some factories were even annoyed and said I was too picky, introducing me to many low-priced products. Although the craftsmanship was good, the bronze content was very low. (You can tell from the weight) At that moment, I refused outright. I cannot deceive such a trusting big brother for more profit!! Putting myself in his shoes, if I were the customer, I would not want to be deceived!

So, after hours of hard work, after repeated comparisons, I finally selected the dd. That's when I realized my trouser cuffs were soaked (Xi'an was having a heavy snowfall at the time). But my heart was warm and cozy. Hehe!

When preparing to ship, I hesitated. Although I felt the craftsmanship of the dd was exquisite, Big Brother might not agree, and when he receives the goods, he might not be satisfied, wasting two days of his hard waiting! After deep thought, I finally decided: use a digital camera to take pictures of each angle and detail of the dd and send them to him. If he's not satisfied, I'll change them!

After anxiously waiting, the big brother finally came online at around 12 o'clock at night. I first apologized to him for not shipping on time today and explained the reason, then sent the pictures over...

Anxiously waiting for his reply, I also had a little unease, fearing that he wouldn't like any of them (sigh, doing business is really hard). Finally, with a "ding dong," I eagerly read each word of the message he sent: Satisfied! He said he was very satisfied!! And praised me for being very careful! I cheered happily, drawing a chorus of "woof woof" barks from a group of stray dogs, only then realizing it was already past midnight (scary oh, luckily no one "roared" at me, hehe).

The next morning, I found a lot of newspapers to repackage. Because they are bronze items, there are many edges, so they must be carefully packaged, otherwise they will break during transportation.

It took me 3 hours to package all 7 items, my hands were cut by many wounds from those sharp edges, and my back hurt so much I couldn't stand straight, but I didn't feel the pain at all, I was still very happy.

Finally, I also gave the brother 3 terracotta warrior series gifts, hoping to give him a surprise!

After receiving the goods, the brother immediately replied: saying he had been worried the dd would be damaged during transportation, but he didn't expect me to package them so carefully, exceeding his expectations even further! What he didn't expect was that I also gave him practical and delicate gifts! (moved)

That's how I sold 1500 yuan worth of goods in one all-nighter. (To be precise, it should be two all-nighters.)

In fact, no matter what dd you sell, we should think from the other person's perspective. If you were the customer, what would you worry about when buying a product? Your concerns are the customer's concerns, patiently explaining every question for them; the most important thing is: integrity!

Well, I've said so much, what you see is just the result. Many people might say: this is luck!

Actually, I don't think so. So far, every customer who has consulted me has completed the purchase 100%. When you're unprepared or not enthusiastic enough (various factors like poor quality products, unreasonable prices, inadequate service, untimely shipping can all affect the final consultation results for "strange customers"), even if luck comes, you may not be able to seize it; facts prove that my service and products moved the customer, and this customer purchased another 2 bronze items from me before the Spring Festival!

The two characters "integrity" are easy to say, but putting them into practice requires a long-term vision!
