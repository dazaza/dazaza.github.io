Tencent QQ 2009 SP4 Official Version, the latest version of QQ2009.

by sam000 on 2009-10-12 15:59:03

QQ2009 brings you a brand-new user interface and experience. Based on retaining the essential features and functions of QQ2008, it provides QQ users with a more concise and elegant usage experience.

- Mini Profile Card: Rich content presented step by step

The mini profile card has added multiple instant status carousel display functions. When friends have multiple updates, the mini profile card defaults to showing up to two business update information items, while remembering your current selection. This keeps the mini profile card simple while being highly personalized.

- Group Friend Dynamics: Summary updates you can't miss

Group space dynamics are displayed in the group window in real time, allowing group members to stay updated on important information within the group at any time. The group dynamics bar supports expansion and collapse for richer dynamic displays.

- Message Box: Collects all unread information

The new message box integrates unified receipt, convenient operations, and clear column separation. The asynchronous message processing function ensures that all your unread messages are securely stored. You can close QQ at any time, and simply open it next time to continue viewing and handling messages.

- Gaming Life: Cool player growth witness

The mini profile card has added a "Gaming Life" module, comprehensively showcasing your glory and achievements in Tencent games. Hovering over a friend's avatar allows you to easily check their gaming growth history and understand their gaming skills.

- Friend Impressions: Interactive evaluations share happiness

Want to know what your friends' first impressions of you are? The new Friend Impression feature lets you enter a personal profile page, share your impressions, and see what they think about you.

- Friend Updates: Synchronized preview in chat window

A fresh and simple chat window brings you a new chatting experience. You can also be the first to know about updates from your friends' QQ Spaces, Gaming Life, and Paipai. Open the chat window with your friend to have all updates at your fingertips and keep track of dynamic information!

- Horizontal Navigation: Smooth switching between contact panels

The contact panel presents contacts, groups, and recent contacts through horizontal switching. Clicking the panel allows free list switching for more convenient viewing. The left sidebar of the contact panel can also be freely collapsed.

- Personal Signature: Convenient changes to showcase yourself

Want to share your emotions with friends anytime? Simply click on the personal signature bar on the main panel to add a personal signature and instantly share your mood dynamics.

- Clean Interface: Visual experience under your control

The new user interface offers smooth and light operation experiences while providing a clean and elegant visual enjoyment. A new "Change Appearance" button, along with an entirely new palette and background replacement design, allows you to become your own QQ designer.

New Features Added in QQ2009 Official Version SP4:

1. Folder transfer without compression, one-click transmission.

2. QQ Paintbrush tool for freehand drawing on screenshot interfaces.

3. Folder capacity reminders for convenient management of historical files.

4. Lock status disables quick web login, protecting QQ account privacy.

5. Network memo optimization for customizable sorting of reminders.

6. Wireless chat windows display QQ Show avatars, showcasing individuality and fashion.
