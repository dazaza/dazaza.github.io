The latest version of Kerry Auto Club Management Software advocates intelligent management. For membership reminder SMS, there is no need for manual intervention as the number of notification days can be set in advance and post-event SMS will be automatically sent to members. The software also features automatic call screen pop-up recording, added functions for salary attendance, telephone sales management, and insurance processing modules, making its functionality more comprehensive.

Kerry Club Management Software Features:

1. Wizard-style menu installation, no separate database installation required.

2. Wizard-style initialization management.

3. One-stop convenient operation.

4. Flexible configuration and customizable function list.

Basic Functions of Kerry Club Management Software:

1. Advocates intelligent management where membership reminder SMS can be set to automatically send notifications to members without manual intervention.

2. Automatic call pop-up recording with historical call recordings available for listening at any time.

3. Added salary attendance and financial income/expenditure management.

4. New telephone sales management module effectively manages the telephone sales process of salespeople; categorizes customer status based on different sales stages.

5. Comprehensive functions with powerful report chart analysis capabilities. Chart analysis of membership composition allows timely understanding of membership growth and loss conditions; effectively monitors employee work situations.

6. Utilizes a workstation management system that sets different work permissions for personnel in different positions, providing a clear overview of tasks to be handled upon opening the software.

7. Supports uploading member pictures and scanned images, allowing easy printing of member copies when needed.

8. Unique branch management functionality enables franchise company and chain branch management.

9. Specialized permission management using user roles and affiliated company concepts in user management. User roles include salesperson, customer service representative, and manager. Personnel with different roles can only view or manage members or customers within their respective permissions.

New and Modified Functions in Kerry Club Management Software v2011 Version:

1. Provides motor vehicle proxy management services, enabling proxy handling of vehicle-related matters and driving management business cash register operations.

2. Offers insurance proxy services for clients who need assistance with insurance matters.

3. Special Merchant Cash Register Platform: Can be extended to special merchant card-swiping consumption management, allowing members to use cards at designated merchants for consumption. Member cards support counting uses, recharging, discounts, and point accumulation management.

4. Member Self-service Platform: Can be expanded to online membership application services, allowing members to make online service appointments, file complaints or suggestions, and check member information, member card information, member consumption records, and member card recharge details online.
