Paris Fashion Week: Designers introduce this spring's popular clothing and silver accessory pairings. Now, let us take you on a tour to create your own exclusive Paris Fashion Week experience.

Trend 1 —— Hollow-out Design

At the Spring/Summer Paris Fashion Week, it is not hard to find that the designs are very youthful and fresh. The most obvious feature is undoubtedly the hollow-out design. Looking back, it turns out that many brands have used the hollow-out design to varying degrees: elegant hollow-outs, lace hollow-outs, cloud-pattern hollow-outs. We also discovered similar floral hollow-out patterns with a retro taste.

It can be seen that hollow-out design will definitely be a big hit this year. Here's a recommendation for everyone:

A hollow-out knit top paired with tight-fitting skinny pants is a great combination for shopping or going to work. This year, waterproof platform color-block high heels are quite popular, elongating the overall proportion of women and showing off their long legs. Of course, fashionable small accessories are essential. The new hollow-out large bracelet from Chuanxi greatly enhances the fashion sense of the outfit. If you're a cute short-haired girl, rose-shaped long earrings are also a great matching item.
