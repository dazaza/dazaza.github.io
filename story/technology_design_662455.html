Yuan Cheng Group Co., Ltd. was founded in 1988. It is a comprehensive enterprise group integrating logistics industry as the mainstay with express railway freight, special express parcels trains, road express, warehousing, distribution, logistics solution planning for freight special lines and other industries, real industry investment, international trade, etc. It has a multi-level logistics network service system with "railway trunk transportation as the basis, road express as an extension, and regional freight special line distribution for deep penetration". Resources and Network of Yuan Cheng Group Co., Ltd.: 1) The total assets of Yuan Cheng Group are about 1 billion yuan, with registered capital of 230 million yuan, and business revenue of about 1.5 billion yuan in 2004, with a history of nearly twenty years in the railway transport and road transport industries. 2) Yuan Cheng Group now owns more than 1000 box trucks, exclusively contracted to operate railway freight special lines by the Ministry of Railways, including 14 fixed schedule trains, 6 parcel special trains, 6 special express mail trains, more than 6000 excellent employees, and more than 60000 long-term cooperative partners. In 2004, the heavy freight volume of railway freight reached 2.03 million tons, the light freight volume of railway freight reached 1.5 million cubic meters, the heavy freight volume of road freight reached 54000 tons, and the light freight volume of road freight reached 110000 cubic meters. 3) Yuan Cheng Group has more than 50 branches and subsidiaries across the country, hundreds of business outlets, will continue to increase the investment in exclusive operating rights of lines in 2005, and plans to reach 1000 outlets and self-owned vehicle configuration reaching 3000 vehicles by 2008. 4) Yuan Cheng Group has 300000 square meters of warehousing area, and all branches have road transport distribution capabilities. 5) Yuan Cheng Logistics Information Network can provide logistics information query and feedback of various business reports for key customers.
