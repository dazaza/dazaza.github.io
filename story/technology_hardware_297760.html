The exchange on the network is an exchange of hearts, a dialogue between souls and sincere words. This earns each other's trust, and those who meet let everything beautiful be magnified in their hearts with the collision of keyboards and fingers, thoughts flying.

"You are my friend." A single word "friend" you understand most, the care between friends moves my heart and pleases your feelings. I am moved by this beautiful encounter. We appreciate each other, worry about each other, and bless each other. On the road, we hold hands and collect every bit of our encounters together, leaving behind a wonderful memory. We are happy here, we laugh here. Although sometimes arguments cannot be avoided, no matter what, we will not forget what we once had.

Friends on the internet are like rain, mist, or wind, coming without a trace and going without a shadow. Because the brilliance of the internet is everywhere, the vast sea of networks has its unique scenery at every corner. Everyone’s perspective of appreciation is different, and the scenery appreciated at each period also varies. None of us would stay forever in one scenic spot, so there is always the saying "An army camp remains constant while soldiers keep flowing."

I welcome friends who walk with me and miss those who have passed by. We won't forget old friends and will make more new ones. Whenever we meet, we should cherish it. We don't need to linger or sigh over a friend's departure; as long as we meet, it's fate. The coming and going of fate all follow the flow of destiny, everything goes with the flow. "A friend is someone you don't need to remember but never forget." For friends on the internet, no matter how long we've been together, how far we've come, if you spend just one minute with me, I'll make you happy for 60 seconds because you are my - friend.
