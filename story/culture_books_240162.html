Women's Emotional Dilemma in the Red History: An Epic of a Woman

by jqrxass on 2009-09-19 15:16:14

A romantic history in the midst of emotional contradictions and red history, as well as the survival trajectory of little people in a great era, is depicted through the life experiences of someone deeply involved in this red history.

The Epic of a Woman Part One

Two hours ago, Xiaofei didn't feel sleepy at all but somehow fell asleep. She had never slept so soundly that she even missed the secret signal. Xiao Wu meowed like a cat on the left and right side of the window. Finally, he pushed the window, found it not properly locked, climbed into Xiaofei's room, dragged her out from under the quilt, and said angrily in her ear: "You traitor!" From waking up to climbing out of the window and running onto the road, Xiaofei performed a series of continuous actions.

The Epic of a Woman Part Three

Zi was born at the end of October. After struggling for two days and two nights without making a sound, Xiaofei finally gave birth to her daughter. On the first day of entering the maternity hospital, both Xiaofei and Ouyang Yu received punishments; one was a severe warning within the party, and the other was a demerit record leading to a transfer from the army. When Xiao Wu came to see Xiaofei, he was very angry with her: "How could you have done such a foolish thing? Fortunately, Ouyang is good at talking. If it were a jerk, he wouldn't do it! Just because you got pregnant, you must marry me? Two people having fun should take responsibility for themselves! It might not even be from having fun with me!"

The Epic of a Woman Part Four

She couldn't wait to tell Ouyang Yu that she would be playing Juliet. It was the weekend, and people had bought meal coupons and dance tickets to have fun at the club. Xiaofei wore a deep rose布拉吉 (Blag) dress and deep rose lipstick, both of which were bought for her by Ouyang Yu. She declined the first invitation to dance and pulled Ouyang Yu up instead. Ouyang Yu was usually a lazy and scatterbrained person who avoided movement whenever possible, yet he danced extremely well. Xiaofei watched him, full of grace, thinking how such a playboy had been involved in revolution since childhood made her love him even more, not knowing what to do with her feelings.
