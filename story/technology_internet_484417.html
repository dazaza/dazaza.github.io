Will HP TouchPad be sold in mainland? It has passed the domestic 3C certification.

by diglog on 2011-09-01 18:03:13

According to the information from China Quality Certification Centre, HP TouchPad has passed the national 3C product certification in China recently. However, as HP announced earlier that it would abandon all operations of the TouchPad tablet computer, it is still unclear whether the TouchPad will be launched in the Chinese market. Information from the 3C product certification platform shows that HP TouchPad has already passed the national 3C product certification in China. The certificate numbers are: 2011010902494174 and 2011010902494158, with the issuance date being August 22, 2011, and the certificate expiration date being August 22, 2016. The certification information also shows that the model number of the product passing the 3C certification is HSTNH-I29C, which can be found through public information query to be a Wi-Fi version product. 3C certification is the abbreviation for China Compulsory Product Certification. In order to honor the commitment to join the WTO, on December 3, 2001, China issued the mandatory product certification system. Starting from May 1, 2002, the National Certification and Accreditation Administration began to accept certification applications for 132 products in 19 major categories listed in the mandatory product directory.
