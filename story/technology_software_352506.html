Today: April 25th, Weather: Light Rain

Today was exhausting, and in my memory, it's been a long time since I've felt this tired...

The weather in Nanjing is unbearable, with sudden changes from cold to hot. Maybe it's like this everywhere else too, but I'm in Nanjing now, and I truly dislike the weather here. I absolutely hate it.

Unfortunately, I caught a cold again. I was supposed to get up at 7 to learn how to drive, but I had such a bad headache that I couldn't get out of bed. When Tingting got up, it was probably around 7, but I just couldn't force myself to get up - I was too exhausted!

I hate having a cold, and I hate the rain!! It's been really terrible. I've been feeling unwell, and my mood has taken a turn for the worse...

It was my first day on the car, and everything felt so fresh. The instructor was female, and all four students in the car were women. Hmm... who says there are more men than women in China? Where are they? It's like a sea of women. The two women in front drove while I watched from the backseat, which felt quite interesting. But sitting in the back for too long made me extremely uncomfortable, with snot dripping non-stop. I was drowsy. Finally, it was my turn. Sitting in the driver's seat felt magical. Clutch, brakes, accelerator. Turning, starting, stopping. Everything seemed so fascinating...

The lesson ended at 4:02 PM, and the shuttle bus left at 4:00 PM. So unfortunate... There was nothing I could do but take bus No. 108 back. When I returned to the dormitory, I was completely drained...

Under the urging of my beloved, I checked my train ticket. That unethical person didn't manage to buy a ticket for me and didn't even inform me. I had no choice but to immediately go to the ticket agency to buy one myself. In the past, I definitely wouldn't have bothered. Upon leaving, I realized I didn't bring any coins... Sigh... I had to go to that unethical person to retrieve my money, and she even made me wait downstairs for 5 minutes. I wanted to strangle her. Most of the tickets available either had no seats or were already sold out. However, luckily, I managed to buy a ticket... On the way back, I didn't have any coins again, so I had to buy a face mask at Jin Eagle for 3.5 yuan... Suddenly, I realized how dumb I was - why didn't I just buy a 3-yuan one... Alas... I could only insert 1.1 yuan... I feel sorry for 97 (possibly referring to a specific bus route). I had no other choice... It started raining on my way back... Walking in the rain, I truly felt my own existence...

Since my sophomore year, I haven't been this tired. I hate catching a cold, and I hate the rain!!

My dear, I wasn't being impatient with you earlier. Right now, I'm very dizzy... My head is spinning... The sounds I hear seem somewhat muffled...

From now on, I don't want to be this tired anymore.
