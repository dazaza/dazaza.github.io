Public institutions plan to hold public recruitment of staff

According to Shenzhen Testing Institute, Shenzhen public institutions plan to publicly recruit 924 staff members distributed across the education, health and cultural systems in Futian District, Nanshan District, Yantian District, Bao'an District and Guangming New Area.

Based on application requirements, graduates of 2011 and overseas returnees graduating in 2011 can only apply as new graduates. The following people can apply either as new graduates or as social applicants: Shenzhen native graduates from 2009 and 2010; participants of the Western China Volunteer Program and Three Supports and One Assistance program who have completed their service term satisfactorily and are applying within one year after completion (Note: must provide relevant proof, those exceeding one year will be considered as social applicants), when applying for positions that meet the conditions, the age limit for application can be relaxed by two years. Other individuals can only apply as social applicants.

★ Career Insights: This exam only accepts online registration and does not accept on-site registration. For more details, please visit the Shenzhen Testing Institute website.

Jiangxi introduces new policies to support vocational training

In order to strengthen Jiangxi Province's vocational training work and achieve full coverage of vocational training for urban and rural workers, expanding and stabilizing employment. According to the Jiangxi Human Resources and Social Security Department, recently, the Jiangxi Provincial Government issued the "Opinions on Strengthening Vocational Training to Promote Employment" (hereinafter referred to as the "Opinions"), proposing to achieve the goal of training one person and employing one person, and employing one person and training one person. The "Opinions" clearly pointed out that the policy of free admission to technical colleges for children from urban and rural disadvantaged families will continue to be implemented, with an annual sponsorship of 10,000 people.

Over 280,000 people in Jiangxi participate in the college entrance examination

According to the head of the Jiangxi Education Department, this year, Jiangxi's general higher education enrollment plan is a total of 259,100 people, including 111,600 for undergraduate programs and 147,500 for junior colleges (specialty programs). This year, there are 288,616 candidates registered in Jiangxi Province.

The Jiangxi Education Department requires that universities further adjust and optimize the discipline and professional structure of university admissions. The arrangement of招生plans by major should focus on strengthening the cultivation of applied, composite, and skilled talents, actively expanding the scale of talent cultivation needed for scarce talents, strategic emerging industries, and modern service industry development. Recruitment of liberal arts or science students should be determined based on the talent cultivation goals and teaching plans of the recruiting majors, and blind recruitment of both liberal arts and science students should be avoided.

★ Career Insights: As institutions of higher learning that cultivate talent, they should set up majors and recruit relevant students according to current or future talent demands, striving to ensure all graduates can put their learning into practice!
