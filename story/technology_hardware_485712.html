by cranepx03 on 2011-09-06 05:56:45

An exhilarating indoor racing event, the MG3 Driving Mall, featuring the legendary British premium compact car MG3, is set to launch with great fervor in Hangzhou's Jiabai department store! This is a groundbreaking national-level underground garage drifting show where you can witness high-difficulty racing stunts up close that are usually only seen through screens. It’s also an extremely exciting competitive challenge where those who have always dreamed of becoming prominent figures in the racing world will for the first time race on an underground track and personally experience the thrill of racing against time. All this is aimed at igniting your passion for speed, filled with the heartbeat of British style. MG3, if you have the guts, come and join!

A spoiler alert: Hangzhou Jiabai's underground garage is about to become the hot shooting location for a British version of "Fast and Furious": The first-ever 90-degree high-speed tail-out drifts in confined indoor spaces, single-car 360-degree fast tire-burning around stakes, high-speed tail-out weaving through stakes, stylish high-speed formation driving, and bullfighting performances by the fleet, all part of the unique charm resonance competition between MG3 and the urban youth. Each segment is tightly connected, leaving the audience breathless with extreme racing challenges combined with the allure of trendy cars and beautiful women, providing an adrenaline-pumping experience. Behind all this, akin to a Mission Impossible, lies the ultimate expression of the European standards and British premium product appeal of the legendary British compact car MG3, highlighting its efficient power, agile and stable control precision, as well as the five-star safety assurance that dares challengers to let loose. The best dynamic genes of the MG brand live up to their reputation.

As a distinguished British car brand with 87 years of brilliant history, the essence of MG's personality, temperament, and creativity shines through wherever it goes: In the UK, MG is not only a regular guest at various touring car rallies but also the annual star of the Silverstone circuit; globally, the largest single-brand car club, the MG Car Club, gathers millions of fans worldwide in celebration. The British car culture shaped by MG always leaves her enthusiasts in awe! Now, this wave of British car culture is sweeping across China with great momentum, and the upcoming MG3 Driving Mall will fulfill the dream of Chinese MG fans witnessing thrilling high-speed races in their own country for the first time! The MG3 Driving Mall disrupts the rules, ignites passion, and continues the trend of British fashion, making your excitement unfold at wind speed. The MG3, having undergone six generations of UK Design innovation changes, transcends not just the underground obstacles but also the disruption and reform of traditional rules.
