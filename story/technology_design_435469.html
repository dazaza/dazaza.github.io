2011 Cabinet Trends

by f34ttr4t5ye on 2011-05-23 10:07:58

After millenniums of development, human civilization has reached this extremely prosperous era. Our living standards have also reached a new level. Especially modern kitchens which are more of a spiritual enjoyment. In 2011, the trend in cabinet development will become more professional and humane.

When it comes to decoration, cabinets are a very important item. And with consumers' increasingly mature consumer attitudes, ordinary styling designs and simple functional applications can no longer meet the high requirements for cabinets. To better understand the criteria that consumers use when purchasing cabinets as well as the latest selection needs, China Kitchen conducted an online survey.

According to the survey results, consumers' attention to the environmental performance of cabinets and product reputation is increasing day by day. Moreover, their interest in buying kitchenware sets is also on the rise. In response to such consumption trends, how to improve brand recognition and gradually realize the concept of an overall kitchen has become an unavoidable topic for all merchants in the 2011 cabinet market.

Health and environmental protection are highly focused on

Environmental protection has always been one of the topics that consumers in the home market care about the most. But compared with other products, people have stricter environmental requirements for cabinets. According to the survey results, compared with shoddy workmanship and price traps in cabinet decoration, more consumers still worry more about environmental issues. In addition, nearly 40% of consumers prefer to buy solid wood cabinets. Apart from their collectible value, the most important reason is that the environmental coefficient of pure natural wood is higher.

Zhang Qing said: Cabinets are products that come into direct contact with food every day. If they do not meet environmental standards, they will threaten the health of family members. Therefore, when I choose cabinets, I not only look at whether the brand has an environmental inspection report issued by an authoritative department, but also ask in detail whether the basic panels, accessories, and later processing technology of the cabinets all meet environmental standards. In short, I must do my homework before making a choice. It is understood that with the increasingly mature consumer attitude of consumers, when buying cabinets, in addition to paying attention to environmental inspection reports, whether the panels reach E0 standard, and whether they can really achieve zero formaldehyde have all become topics of concern for consumers.

Brand reputation is the key to purchase

When it comes to product reputation, there are two main keys: quality and service. Only by doing these two aspects well can the brand achieve greater development. In addition, since cabinets belong to customized products, the service requirements for merchants are relatively high. Therefore, for the cabinet industry, detailed and comprehensive services are important conditions for capturing more market share. This service does not just refer to pre-sales door-to-door measurements and mid-sales door-to-door installations, but also includes perfect after-sales services, such as timely problem solving and regular follow-ups. Dai Wei, manager of Debei Cabinets, mentioned quality service: We have always insisted on following the brand route. No matter how we promote sales, our quality and service will never be discounted. We offer a warranty period of 10 years for all our products, which is the highest in the same industry because we have great confidence in our products. The importance that merchants place on brand shaping and reputation accumulation can be seen from this.

Facing consumers' increasingly high requirements for environmental protection, major cabinet merchants in the home market are becoming more and more concerned about the environmental performance of products. Regarding green decoration, Debei Overall Cabinets Chengdu region said: Nowadays, people advocate green, low-carbon living. Debei can say that we are doing pretty well in this aspect because the boards we use are quite good domestically, and we will ensure four-sided sealing for all the boards we use, so as to prevent formaldehyde exposure. Besides, we also consider consumers in terms of hardware parts; we use damping hinges to effectively prevent noise pollution. Debei has been around for over a decade, so we can still take these details into account for consumers.

Improving the quality of life starts with the kitchen.
