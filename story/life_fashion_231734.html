1- Try not to get close to high temperatures, as the material of the wig is not heat resistant

2- If the wig gets tangled after long use and is difficult to comb, do not pull forcefully. Instead, spray non-oily wig-specific maintenance liquid and slowly untangle it carefully

3- Using non-oily wig-specific maintenance liquid is also very simple: just lightly spray a few times on the wig before wearing it. This will make the wig soft and shiny, prevent static electricity, and keep the wig moisturized as if it was newly bought!

4- For longer wigs, divide them into several sections and comb from bottom to top. Be sure to be gentle and patient.

5- During the process of wearing and arranging, a small amount of hair falling out is normal.
