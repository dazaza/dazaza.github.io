China Unicom 156 section in October to achieve tariff DIY combination

by vottot on 2007-10-10 13:08:17

On October 10, relevant people of China Unicom told Tencent Technology that Unicom 156 has ended the internal pre-release, and began to officially release numbers from October.

It is understood that the number can be applied for online from September 1, and from October, a large-scale number allocation began across the country.

At the same time, the 156 paragraph adopts a more advanced number allocation method than before, that is, the use of online application number, some numbers with auspicious numbers will also be announced in 156.chinaunicom.com for users to buy.

The scale of the release is very large, and the number of releases in various provinces and cities is millions. Segment 156 is Unicom's GSM segment, which is mainly used by postpaid users, while the previously opened segment 153 is used by the CDMA network. Unicom staff said that it will open more value-added services for users of No. 156. In addition to traditional voice calls, it can smoothly support quasi-3G applications such as WAP Internet access, MMS, video and audio streaming media.

Tencent technology learned that in order to facilitate the choice of consumers, China Unicom has divided communication products into basic voice products and value-added products two categories, basic voice products including local phone package, long-distance package, business travel package, local business package; Value-added services are divided into SMS package, GPRS traffic package, MMS package, and local service package. Users can set tariffs according to their actual situation through free customization and free combination of DIY.

Unicom insiders revealed that in order to reflect the personality of the product, the company provides a 64K large-capacity aerial download smart card for each "Ji" brand product, from the perspective of the actual needs of users, Designed I am VIP, hot recommendation, news and finance, business travel assistant, mobile phone finance, self-service, popular SMS, ringing, mobile music, local channels and other 8 personalized function menus, for the hot recommendation Unicom will be replaced in real time. At the same time, users can manage the download and update of the business through the OTA application menu, and create a personalized menu.

In addition, in order to ensure that customers can truly achieve global roaming, Unicom has also opened dual-mode card business, and provides a wealth of dual-network dual-standby mobile phones for users to choose. This enables free switching between CDMA and GSM networks while maintaining the original number, ensuring that users in both GSM and CDMA countries do not need to change numbers to achieve global roaming.
