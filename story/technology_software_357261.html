I'm so angry, the server is hosted in Beijing, and my hands are still shaking while typing. I... I hired an aunt from a small confinement nanny company, but it has been bumpy from the start (see previous posts about unscrupulous nannies). I said the baby is sleeping, come to our house and settle the accounts clearly, pay whatever is owed. Server rental.

They came around ten in the morning. It was fine at first, but when we talked about the service fee, we started arguing. The company said you shouldn't withhold the service fee. The more I said, the angrier I got, and I started pounding on the table (a bit rude, but even if I'm angry, I shouldn't pound the table), and I said: You still want the service fee? My husband loses 200 yuan per day, and I lose 100 yuan per day. We're already not asking for compensation for lost wages. The other side said: Our company can't work for free either, why don't we wait until your husband comes back to talk again? No food provided. The other side said: Food doesn't need to be provided, I'll just wait. I said: Okay, he will be back at six in the evening. Please speak softly and don't wake up my daughter. The other side said: Why do you care so much about these 200 yuan? I said: If I can afford a nanny, I don't care about this amount of money, but the money should be given when it's due, and shouldn't be given if it's not due. The other side said: You still owe Aunt Zhang (the unscrupulous nanny) money. I said: Tell me how much, Beijing server rental company, I'll give it to you, you get out of my house quickly, I don't even want to see you. The other side also said they didn't want to see me and said: That 200 yuan can be considered as a contribution to us. I said: Thank you. After giving the money, I opened the door and let them leave. I'm so mad, I've never seen a company run like this. They clearly wanted those 200 yuan, yet pretended not to care, and couldn't communicate well with me. Talking was useless, it wasted so much of my time.
