First, remove the old nail polish. Soak a cotton ball with nail polish remover and wipe forcefully from the base of the nail to the tip several times to ensure complete removal. Then use nail tools to shape and file the nails. Afterward, soak your hands in soapy water to soften the cuticles. Gently dry your fingers and spaces between them with a towel. If necessary, use a cuticle clipper and small tweezers to remove ingrown skin and uneven dead skin around the nail roots.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

When applying nail polish, start by prepping the nails. Apply oil or cream around and at the base of the nails. Also, apply a protective layer on the nails themselves. Shake the nail polish well before use to mix the color evenly inside the bottle.

Choose the right color

&；nbsp；

Nail polish colors include rose red, bright red, flesh tone, bean paste color, silver, etc. The currently most popular nail polish is semi-transparent pink and beige. Light-colored nail polishes are very trendy in Jiangsu nail art training [http://www.jsainisi.org]. Nail polish can be mixed freely. You don't need to buy a large quantity of nail polish to match different clothing colors. Just buy a few main colors and use overlapping color matching or sectional color matching methods to adjust the nail polish for unexpected results.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

For overlapping color matching, you can first apply a high-pigmentation bright color, then add a layer of pearl-textured space color to create another color effect in Jiangsu nail art training [http://www.jsainisi.org].

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

For sectional color matching, you can apply contrasting colors at the tips of the nails. Alternatively, divide the ten nails into two zones (left and right), using two or more colors, applying contrasting or harmonious colors alternately.

&；nbsp；

&；nbsp；

French manicure involves painting a different color on the crescent-shaped area at the top of the nails. For example, if the base color is white, paint the crescent with bright contrasting colors like red or black. Or, on a white base, paint soft orange, light yellow, powder blue, or pink tones. Nail decoration styles can also be divided into full-style and crescent-style.

&；nbsp；

Careful application

&；nbsp；

When applying nail polish, it's best not to let the brush bristles soak up too much polish. Wipe gently on the bottle opening and adjust the bristles smoothly. When applying nail polish, note the following key points:

&；nbsp；

Apply the nail polish usually starting from the middle of the nail root forward, then along each side edge of the nail. That is, brush neatly and evenly three times in the order of central and then sides to prevent the nail polish from peeling off quickly.

&；nbsp；

To increase the durability of the nail polish, you can first apply a layer at the front end of the nail, then polish the entire nail. Alternatively, apply the polish in two batches: the first batch should be less to make it dry easily. The second batch should be more. After polishing, clean up any stains on the surrounding finger skin with a cotton swab.

&；nbsp；

Nail care

&；nbsp；

Regardless of the type of nail polish, if applied for a long time, the nails cannot breathe and may easily turn yellow. Therefore, it’s best to apply a layer of moisturizing oil before and after removing nail polish. This product, similar to transparent nail polish, is applied as described above and has the function of preventing pigment deposition.
