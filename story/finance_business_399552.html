One, Capricorn Woman

(1) Capricorn Woman with Moon in Fire Signs:

Venus in Fire Signs: Likable, lively, sociable, humorous, one marriage, perfectionist, hard worker, publisher/editor, autobiographer, lecturer, good at expressing.

Venus in Earth Signs: Creative, adventurer, different marriage (2 or 3 times), attention-grabbing, likes cats, broad education, active movements.

Venus in Water Signs: Flatterer, easily impulsive, judgment errors, nervous, utopian dreamer, unusual birth circumstances, astrology fortune, wealthy, entrepreneur, involved in fashion or beauty fields.

Venus in Air Signs: Long-lasting relationships, perfectionist, traumatic events related to children - such as child death, one enduring marriage, extramarital affairs, widowhood, mysterious, gossip astrology, creative, involved in medical or therapeutic fields.

(2) Capricorn Woman with Moon in Earth Signs:

Venus in Fire Signs: Stubborn, possessive, easily dissatisfied, dignified and humble, easily fatigued, well-known family (members), different marriage, psychological tests, 1 or 2 children, loved performing since childhood, dancing - or acting, unusual birth circumstances.

Venus in Earth Signs: Mature and worldly, principled, financial success, controlling spouse, desire for control, independent, mysterious, usually has two marriages, eccentric, tense relationships.

Venus in Water Signs: Silent, patient, abstract, unusual birth circumstances, loves animals, sensitive constitution, noisy, usually has one child, fertility issues.

Venus in Air Signs: Strong, optimistic, independent, strong principles, abusive behavior, one long-lasting marriage, eating disorders, easy to survive, difficult birth, social worker.

(3) Capricorn Woman with Moon in Water Signs:

Venus in Fire Signs: Cold, fragile, one enduring marriage, works alone, lonely, beauty or model, unusual death, traumatic events related to children, fertility issues, rape victim.

Venus in Earth Signs: Teamwork, decisive, mysterious, contact with mysterious fields, astrologer, writer.

Venus in Water Signs: Independent, sensitive, dreamy, charming, eating disorders, widowhood, surgery, writer.

Venus in Air Signs: Sensitive, romantic, altruistic, stubborn in love, open relationship, sociable, relationships become cold and distant over time, husband has an affair, religion, mysterious.

(4) Capricorn Woman with Moon in Air Signs:

Venus in Fire Signs: Thoughtful, party animal, strong, artist, involved in mysterious fields.

Venus in Earth Signs: Hard worker, good at expressing, sociable, ambitious, unique, highly talented.

Venus in Water Signs: Nervous, superficial, curious, stubborn, mysterious, tall, moved abroad, moved/traveled many times, attention-grabbing.

Venus in Air Signs: Perfectionist, different marriage, hard worker, inferiority complex, idealist, likes exercising (dance, aerobics, cycling,...).
