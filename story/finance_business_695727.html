The concept of eight major development initiatives aims to build a wellness capital in Northeast Asia.

by ncgpvrph06 on 2012-03-07 17:01:41

There are mountains - the place of origin for self-cultivation and nurturing one's nature in the Quanzhen Sect of Taoism; there is water - five natural hot springs that nurture the heart and soothe the spirit, and 12 kilometers of golden sandy beaches containing magnetite that strengthen the body; there is land - dozens of square kilometers of state-owned construction land available for large-scale development, a rarity domestically; there are people - the "three norths" (Northeast, Northwest, North China) customer base gathers here, with continuous streams of customers from Japan, South Korea, and Russia...

The city of Wendeng, located at the "miraculous latitude" of 37 degrees north, is abundant in resources, has a beautiful environment, and is suitable for living and health preservation. It has been awarded the title of "China's Longevity Homeland" and is an excellent place for physical well-being, mental peace, spiritual cultivation, elderly care, beauty enhancement, disease recuperation, and emotional relaxation.

Nowadays, combining favorable timing, geographical advantages, and human harmony, Wendeng, under the eight major development concepts of "based on the three norths, serving the whole country, radiating internationally, four-season resort, compound health preservation, resource integration, platform construction, and standard setting", is moving towards the grand blueprint of becoming the "Oriental Health Capital".

The Eight Major Development Concepts

Creating a Full Health Preservation Industry Chain

It is understood that the creation of Wendeng's health preservation strategy will follow the aforementioned eight concepts and strive to become a model for land development in third-tier cities.

To the south, there is Hainan; to the north, there is Nanhai. The city of Wendeng, located in the eastern part of the Shandong Peninsula, is attracting more and more investment and holiday homes from the "three norths" customer base. Based on the three norths, it aims to meet and explore the health preservation and vacation needs of the three norths customer base, striving to make Wendeng the preferred destination for health preservation and vacations for northern Chinese customers.

On the basis of targeting the northern customer base, Wendeng strengthens its brand by integrating resources, building platforms, and setting standards, thereby serving the whole country, radiating to Northeast Asia and even the Asia-Pacific region, achieving maximum value.

Peng Jianbo, Deputy Director of the Strategic Planning Center of Tsinghua Urban Planning and Design Institute, suggested that Wendeng could consider striving to become a "National Health Industry and Health Resort Demonstration Base" to attract health preservation and vacation customers nationwide.

In this way, not only will "Wendeng" break through the current bottleneck of overly single industry and overly inward-looking and low-end market, but it will also enhance Wendeng's city brand and elevate Wendeng to the "national strategy" level.

Furthermore, based on the existing overseas customer base mainly consisting of Japanese and Korean health vacationers, a foreign market oriented towards Japan and South Korea as the main focus and Russia as auxiliary will be formed, gradually radiating to other international regions, providing international-standard health vacation services.

According to Tan Yuanguo, Vice Mayor of Wendeng, Wendeng is located by the Yellow Sea, with a pleasant climate, warm winters and cool summers, and summer temperatures rarely exceeding 30 degrees Celsius, generally around 28 degrees Celsius. The annual average temperature remains at 11 degrees Celsius, making it very suitable for four-season health preservation and tourism vacations. Therefore, Wendeng's health preservation and tourism development has the foundation to break away from the past single-season type of vacation area development model and possesses the ability to create spring, summer, autumn, and winter four-season health preservation and tourism products.

With unique natural resources and unmatched geographical advantages, the development of Wendeng's health preservation industry will follow the concept of compound health preservation, integrating air health preservation, hot spring health preservation, dietary health preservation, marine health preservation, mountain forest health preservation, sunlight health preservation, climate health preservation, cultural health preservation, and medical health preservation.

In addition to the above development concepts, it is understood that Wendeng will also vigorously integrate domestic and international successful health preservation demonstration resources (such as Switzerland's Quiet Harbor Medical Center, America's Houston Medical Center, Japan's Hakone Hot Spring Resort Area, Jinyun Mountain Taoist Cultural Health Preservation Resort Area, etc.), along with successful models and experiences of world-renowned health preservation resorts, technical and service resources for health preservation resorts, and promote the shaping and healthy rapid development of Wendeng's "Oriental Health Capital" through summits, exhibitions, and conferences.

Tan Yuanguo stated that while transforming Wendeng into the "Oriental Health Capital," Wendeng will also establish a national "service platform" for the health preservation industry, providing achievements exhibition, market trading platform, technology promotion, education and training, consulting services, truly becoming a national innovation center, information center, and exchange center for the health preservation industry.

Moreover, during the development and construction process, Wendeng will assist relevant national departments in formulating various standards for the "health preservation industry," such as infrastructure standards, ecological environmental protection standards, quality of life standards, service standards, management standards, etc., making Wendeng the "standard formulation and promotion center" for the construction of the "health preservation industry," establishing its authoritative position.

"Three-step Plan"

Moving Towards the "Oriental Health Capital"

"Hainan is too hot in summer for northerners to bear, and the Liaodong Peninsula is too cold in winter. After extensive research, we finally concluded that only the southern sea of Wendeng is good." Wang Mingjian, chairman of Shandong Hengxingmaoye Real Estate Company, who successfully developed the "Bihai Yunju" project in the new southern sea area of Wendeng and won the "2008 United Nations Human Settlements Programme Dubai National Best Example (China) Promotion Award" and the "2009 CIHAF China Famous Property" title, told reporters.

Chen Taichuan, standing vice-chairman and executive president of China Bao'an Group, simultaneously expressed that Wendeng has unique natural resources, a long history, rich culture, abundant produce, beautiful environment, simple folk customs, and occupies favorable timing, geographical advantages, and human harmony. He is full of confidence in Wendeng's blueprint to build the "Oriental Health Capital."

It is understood that following the aforementioned eight development concepts, Wendeng's plan to build the "Oriental Health Capital" will be divided into three phases: the start-up phase, the growth phase, and the maturity phase.

From 2010 to 2012 is the start-up phase. Through the point layout of starting projects, Wendeng will quickly become a highlight that attracts external consumer populations, forming a distinctive mark and tone of differentiated development within a short period. High-end health preservation and vacation facilities with landmark significance will serve as the leader, triggering the agglomeration of other health preservation service facilities.

During this stage, the Kunyu Mountain Land Health Preservation Resort Area and the Bible Mountain Taoist National Academy will be launched first to capture the high-end market and shape characteristic health preservation and vacation products. A high-end health preservation and vacation residential area in the mountains will be created, and the Tianmu and Tangbo hot spring health preservation and vacation areas will be enhanced to establish urban brands and images. This will form two mountains, four springs, and a sea health preservation product sample area, a health preservation and vacation tourist destination for the "three norths" customer base, and a new calling card for peninsula leisure vacations.

From 2013 to 2015 is the growth phase. Along with the agglomeration of health preservation and leisure facilities, corresponding leisure and vacation facilities as well as tourism service facilities facing broader markets will also form agglomerations, thus creating various large-scale health preservation and vacation areas covering diverse consumer groups, forming a process of developing from breakthrough points to areas.

During this stage, under the impetus of rapid channels between mountains, springs, and seas and the ring-city expressway transportation, Wendeng will become an important destination for high-end health preservation and vacations in northern China, forming a relatively complete system of health preservation products and services, creating high-end health preservation resource residential and lifestyle sectors near mountains, seas, springs, and forests. Regional land values will rapidly increase, and a beautiful image of a health preservation and vacation city will form.

From 2016 to 2020 is the maturity phase. On the basis of multiple health preservation and vacation areas with different themes, urban supporting functions and public service systems will gradually improve, radiating across the entire city to form a health preservation and vacation city.

During this stage, the health preservation and vacation clusters of mountains, seas, springs, and cities will mature, the Nanhai health preservation product and equipment manufacturing base will steadily develop, and the health preservation industry will become one of the city's pillar industries.

By then, Wendeng's urban industry, space, and functions will be comprehensively reshaped, health preservation and vacation areas will be fully developed, and the scale of health preservation and vacation populations will significantly grow. The region will have a high profile, and the image of the "Oriental Health Capital" city will form.

It is introduced that during the start-up phase, Wendeng will adopt a strategic overall operation model of "government-led, enterprise-based, citizen participation, market-driven operations", initially starting from health preservation-related leisure and vacation initiatives. While developing land projects, it will focus on creating four major landscape projects: urban greening engineering, rustic pastoral charm, urban blue water systems, and the Blue Sea Golden Sand Project, to provide complementary services, ensuring and promoting the steady progress of Wendeng towards the blueprint of the "Oriental Health Capital".
