Unexpectedly, the blog was named. Let's participate in the game.

by xjyzhenai on 2008-09-26 14:56:58

I've known about this game since ancient times. But I, being an idiot, have never really understood what's so fun about it. Unexpectedly, today I saw a comment notification on my own blog - I've been tagged. So I plan to spend a bit of time and continue this nomination relay, to see what's so fun about it. First, let me explain what the blog nomination game is:

Quote:

1. Whoever it's passed to must continue, otherwise they'll be punished. Please take it seriously, don't be afraid of exposing your privacy.

2. The person whose name is called must write their answers on their own blog, then delete one question and add another, still forming 26 questions, and pass them to another 10 people. They must also leave a comment on these 10 people's blogs to notify them - you've been tagged.

3. Those who are tagged cannot refuse to answer the questions, cannot pass it back, and must note on their own blog where they received the questions from, then think of a new question to pass to another 10 people, allowing the game to continue. If you don't want to continue the activity, you don't have to.

4. Those whose names are called will receive everyone's blessings, and all good wishes will come true in the near future.

The person who tagged me is Daniel, original text: http://www.allove.org/index.php/archives/call-name.html.

Now, let's begin. Response: http://www.allove.org
