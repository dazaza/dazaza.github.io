1. Register your account on Taobao

First, let's go to Taobao's homepage. After opening the website, click "Free Registration", where you can choose to register via mobile phone number or email. We usually choose "Email Registration". Fill in all the information and click "Agree to the Agreement and Submit Registration Information". If everything goes well, the website will prompt that registration is successful.

Next, enter your own email to receive Taobao's confirmation email. Click the confirmation link to activate the account, completing the first step of opening an online store. Please note that if you have already shopped on Taobao before, there is no need to register again. On Taobao, one account can simultaneously be both a buyer and a seller.

2. Complete Alipay Real-name Authentication

How to open a store on Taobao? What work needs to be done? We have successfully registered a Taobao account in the first step. Next, we need to complete Alipay's real-name authentication, which is a necessary step. After clicking "My Taobao", you can see the tip "Please complete real-name authentication before selling items". Click it and follow the prompts.

Alipay is equivalent to the money bag of Taobao users and also ensures the normal and honest transactions between both parties. Alipay's real-name authentication confirms your true identity. This certification adds a certain level of complexity to the process of opening an online store, but greatly increases the security of the entire Taobao transaction. In the past, uploading ID cards for manual verification by Taobao was required. Now Taobao has cooperated with banks nationwide, and if you have a real-name registered bank card from some banks, Taobao can verify your identity through the banking system, making it much more convenient than before. 3. Publish More Than 10 Items

Publishing more than 10 different items (and keeping them in the "for sale" state) allows you to open a free store. The steps to publish items are: Enter "My Taobao" - "I am a Seller" - "I Want to Sell". Taobao considers having more than 10 items for sale as qualifying to open a store. Publishing fewer than 10 items only counts as personal idle item trading, so publishing fewer than 10 items does not allow you to apply to open a store.

4. Pass the Taobao Store Opening Exam

Enter "My Taobao" - "I am a Seller", find the "Open Store" button. After clicking, a prompt to take the exam will appear.

To open a store on Taobao, you must pass the Taobao store opening exam, which is a new rule for new sellers that Taobao started at the end of 2010. The main content of the exam is "Taobao Rules", and the score must reach 60 points to pass, with a 100% accuracy rate required for the basic questions part. After passing the exam, read the honest operation commitment letter, then fill in the shop name, shop category, and shop introduction according to the prompts, check the agreement to "Product Release Rules" and "Consumer Protection Agreement", and then confirm and submit. If everything is normal at this point, you now own your own Taobao store! Successfully opened!

Real-name Authentication Process

To open a store on Taobao, real-name authentication is required. The store owner must be a legal citizen of China aged eighteen or older and have a bank card account with a bank that has a cooperative relationship with Alipay. During real-name authentication, Alipay will deposit a few cents into your card (free gift) to confirm your identity. After checking at the bank counter or online banking, input the exact amount of this transaction into Alipay's webpage. If correct, the authentication will pass.
