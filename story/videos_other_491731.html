The court made a ruling on the case in court. The principal offender, Zhang Dong, was sentenced to death. The accomplices, Li Haijun and Zhang Guiyu, were sentenced to life imprisonment and 12 years of fixed-term imprisonment respectively.

According to the prosecutor in charge of the case, when the principal offender Zhang Dong was questioned, he said that when he proposed the idea of kidnapping a girl, the other two both opposed it. However, when they heard that they could get a large sum of money, the two immediately agreed. When asked why they caused the death of the girl after kidnapping her, all three said, "Because the little girl knew us."

On the evening of November 20 last year, the three defendants planned a child kidnapping case in Songjiang. They stuffed a four-year-old girl into a suitcase and hid her under the bed in a hotel room, then demanded a ransom of 7 million yuan from the girl's parents over the phone. Although the police quickly solved the case, the girl had already been killed.

Yesterday, after deliberation, the Intermediate People’s Court of Shanghai believed that the actions of the three defendants had violated the criminal law with exceptionally serious circumstances. All three suspects admitted their guilt without reservation.

Oriental Net reported on August 30 that according to 'Oriental Morning Post', yesterday, the Shanghai No.1 Intermediate People’s Court sentenced defendant Zhang Dong to death for kidnapping, depriving him of political rights for life. The accomplices, Li Haijun and Zhang Guiyu, were sentenced to life imprisonment and 12 years of fixed-term imprisonment respectively.

All three kidnappers were born in the 90s, the oldest just turning 20, and the youngest only 16. All three dropped out of junior high school and worked odd jobs.

The girl's parents ran a hotel on Renmin North Road in Songjiang District. After the incident, they received a call from the "kidnappers", demanding a ransom of 7 million yuan.

After receiving the report, the police immediately launched an investigation. The girl was once hidden by the suspects, including Zhang Dong, who used packing tape to bind her mouth and limbs and stuffed her into a suitcase. She was later found dead. Following the incident, the police arrested the three criminal suspects.
