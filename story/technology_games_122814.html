A transparent pool table

by vinbnet on 2009-02-05 09:45:40

Can pool tables be transparent? How can we play with transparency? Anyone who has played billiard knows that the direction of the stroke requires friction with the table, and if the table is too smooth, it will naturally lose the essence of billiard. It seems to be nothing more than a cosmetic display. In fact, the brand design company used a transparent material called Vitrik to make the tablecloth, which is glass underneath. The feel and friction of the Vitrik are the same as that of a traditional billiard tablecloth, so the billiard ball will not lose friction.
