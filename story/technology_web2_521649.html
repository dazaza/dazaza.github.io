Character Introduction:

Zhou Jingyi: 22 years old, born into a wealthy family, a top student in the dance department of an art college, and a campus beauty. Gentle and elegant, outwardly graceful and inwardly intelligent, pure and charming. Graduated with love for Mo Yan but unable to stay together. Despite Xiangyu's persistent pursuit, she remained unmoved. Searching for relatives in Lijiang, she unfortunately encountered an avalanche. Although she received Mo Yan's full-hearted care again, she was caught in an emotional whirlpool, finding it hard to choose…

Suya: 22 years old, grew up in a Mosuo dance family, inheritor of the dance king. Handsome and beautiful, passionate and outgoing, mischievous and cute, tough on the outside and gentle inside. Accidentally meeting Mo Yan, she was mistaken for Jingyi, and the two supported each other, developing feelings. Her twin sister's arrival completely disrupted her peaceful life and love. She had higher pursuits in life but was lost in the world of love and family, unsure of where to go.

Mo Yan: 24 years old. A Mosuo orphan with extraordinary musical talent, he became a composition student at an art college with the help of Xiangyu's mother Xiao Xiangyun. Depressed in nature, introverted, indecisive, sentimental. Graduated in love with Jingyi but unwilling to hurt Xiangyu and defy Jingyi's parents' wishes, painfully missing the opportunity. After returning to Lijiang, he missed Jingyi bitterly and mistakenly thought the similar-looking Suo Ya was Jingyi. Just as he and Suo Ya were getting closer, Jingyi arrived in Lijiang with Xiangyu and suffered a snow avalanche, falling into a coma. All this plunged him back into emotional torment.

Chen Xiangyu: 24 years old. The "Prince" of the management department at the art college, from a noble background. Proud and unconstrained, outgoing in appearance, fragile inside. Graduated loving Jingyi like his best friend, passionately pursuing her but never gaining her favor. Nearly desperate, he gained the opportunity to study in the U.S. with Jingyi, taking advantage of the situation to seemingly win her heart. Just as he was proud of his love and career, everything was ruined by the trip to Lijiang, losing his true love once again.

Ke Weiping: Over 40 years old, her father once held a government position. Ignoring family opposition, she insisted on marrying Zhou Sheng, who already had two daughters at the time. For 20 years, she lived in constant fear of losing Zhou Sheng. Under the instigation of her old friend Chen Tie, she assisted in stealing the "Great Tang Music Scores." After the scheme was exposed, she lived in exile abroad alone.

Zhou Sheng: Over 40 years old, once passionate about Naxi ancient music. While stationed in Lijiang, Yunnan, he met the Mosuo woman Su La, fell in love, and had twin daughters. Unfortunately, Su La fell into the snowy valley, and due to time constraints, he returned to Beijing with his daughter Jingyi and married Ke Weiping. Back in Beijing, he engaged in the antique business while continuously sending people to look for Su La and the other daughter in Lijiang.

Chen Tie: Over 40 years old, an old acquaintance of Zhou Sheng, harboring deep feelings for Ke Weiping. After Ke Weiping married Zhou Sheng and returned to Beijing, his whereabouts became unknown. Twenty years later, he came to Beijing and began a crazy revenge against his old friend Zhou Sheng.

Xiao Xiangyun: Over 40 years old, a famous musician, gave birth to Xiangyu, adopted the Mosuo orphan Mo Yan in Lijiang when she was young. Devoted her life to cultivating her two sons as successors.

Su La: Over 40 years old, beautiful in her youth, skilled in dance. Fell into the snowy valley after having children with Zhou Sheng, rescued and cared for by her grandfather, living in Lijiang with her daughter Suya.

The female lead roles of Zhou Jingyi and Suya, twin sisters, are played by Shu Chang, who portrayed Tian Shan Tonglao in "Demi-Gods and Semi-Devils." Since her debut at age five, Shu Chang has played different types of characters in many films and TV series. After entering Beijing Foreign Studies University last year, Shu Chang became a star university student. Without attending a professional acting school, Shu Chang's natural and simple acting style has won more attention.

Selected as the first drama of 2006 "Love When It Comes," casting was widely discussed in major media outlets, and it was rumored that Yan Chengxu would play the male lead. Planned and invested by Beijing Star River Source Culture Co., Ltd., the 22-episode TV series "Love When It Comes" started filming in 2006. This is a story reflecting modern urban life. The TV series tells the story of college classmates Xiao Mo Yan from Lijiang and Chen Xiangyu from Beijing, who are very close brothers. By chance, they both fall in love with a dance student named Zhou Jingyi. Amid complex emotions, Xiao Mo Yan and Chen Xiangyu both pursue the person they like fiercely, unwilling to give up. They follow their love to the remote Lijiang... The plot twists and turns, gripping the audience.

Twin sisters separated due to parental negligence, each ending up in different places, near yet far, like watching a detective movie—everyone wants to know the outcome. According to General Manager Li Zhen of Beijing Star River Source Culture Co., Ltd., the production of "Love" took three years from planning to filming, with high requirements for the script, which has been revised dozens of times. Li Zhen said that revising it so many times was to achieve perfection, ensuring the story was substantial and close to the common people, close to real life. Additionally, the show invited several well-known domestic actors: Yang Lixin plays Dad Zhou Sheng; Gailili makes a guest appearance as Mom Ke Weiping, and renowned performing artist Li Ding plays Uncle.
