The real culprit behind the "Domino Murder" series of killings

by jqrxass on 2009-09-06 12:37:53

Detective Yan Ting, with the help of criminologist Lu Yu, solved the Honeymoon Killer serial case, only to find the killer himself became a victim in another murder case. Next came the self-proclaimed "Oracle Killer," driven by a sense of mission, targeting individuals with moral flaws. Expert Yuan Qingzhou from the provincial department's criminology research lab arrived to assist in the investigation. With an outstanding criminology report, he identified the suspect and apprehended him with irrefutable evidence. However, this was merely part of a larger conspiracy. The deranged killer was not the real murderer; instead, he had created a complete killing mechanism to escape police investigation. Even after his death, the killing system he called the "Domino Killing Array" could still function normally...

**Domino Killing Array - Part One**

Luo Xiaofeng opened his eyes and smelled the special scent of new bedding.

For a moment, he was disoriented, but when he looked up and saw the wedding photo hanging on the wall, he returned to reality. He had married Zhuang Liyun. The wedding a few days ago wasn't very lively, but it was just a ceremony. What mattered was that from then on, he would live together with Zhuang Liyun. Their long-term relationship had exhausted his passion, but finally being able to fulfill his wish seemed like something worth celebrating.

Thinking about not having to be alone anymore made him feel happy.

**Domino Killing Array - Part Two**

"How can secretly taken photos and videos harm the parties involved?" Yan Ting asked Du Hai Ming.

Without hesitation, Du Hai Ming replied, "I think there are two ways. One is to use the photos or videos to threaten the party, demanding money, goods, or making sexual demands. This method is extremely secret, and as long as they reach a private settlement, it's hard for others to discover. Another way, I believe, should be considered extremely malicious behavior—publishing the photos or videos and spreading them through some channel, such as uploading them online. In that case, the damage to the parties involved would be enormous."

**Domino Killing Array - Part Three**

After contacting the deceased's family, Bao Guoliang, the younger brother of Bao Guozhong, agreed to come to the criminal investigation team that evening to identify the body, but by the next morning, he still hadn't shown up. Subsequent attempts to contact him revealed that his phone was already turned off.

In the forensic medicine report submitted by Zheng Chao the next day, it was mentioned that the stab wounds' entry points and tracks matched the lethal stab wounds in the Feng Wenshan case. Similar small rough marks were also found inside the stab wound tracks, confirming that they were caused by the same weapon. Additionally, multiple old scars were discovered on the deceased's abdomen, shoulders, back, and legs, indicating injuries over a long period. The latest scar, a leg abrasion and bruise on the back, occurred one or two days ago and had been treated.

**Domino Killing Array - Part Four**

The ceremony began, with the bride and groom making their entrance to the stirring wedding march. Wang Quansheng looked spirited and confident. Although short in stature and shorter than the bride by a head, he still tried to stand tall and proud, looking extraordinarily pleased with himself. Looking at the bride Huang Lijuan beside him, the guests understood why Wang Quansheng was so proud. Huang Lijuan was tall and slender, with skin as white as snow. She appeared full-figured but had a slim waist. A low-cut white wedding dress, simple in design, perfectly highlighted her figure. Standing side by side with Wang Quansheng, the contrast between them was striking. Many people in the crowd couldn’t understand how Wang Quansheng managed to find such a beautiful wife.

**Domino Killing Array - Part Five**

Ou Xiaolan walked slowly along the sidewalk, hugging herself tightly. Despite the sunlight bathing her, she still felt cold.

There were more than 20 days left until the Spring Festival, but the streets had already begun to take on a festive atmosphere. Walking amidst the bustling crowd, Ou Xiaolan looked around and noticed that everyone appeared cheerful, their faces filled with satisfaction and comfort. This realization made her even more depressed. She suddenly felt an urge to run far away. But even if she escaped this city, she still couldn't evade her predetermined fate. So, all she could do now was face it calmly.
