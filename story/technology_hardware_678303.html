Profile of Beijing Bosi Translation Company

Beijing Bosi Translation (a subsidiary of Beijing Hongsheng Bosi Technology Development Center) is a professional translation company registered with the State Administration for Industry and Commerce. It is also the designated translation company for various foreign-related departments of the Beijing Municipal Government, as well as a certified and guided institution by the China Foreign Languages Publishing Administration's Translation Professional Qualification Assessment Center. Additionally, it is the designated training institution for the National Translation Qualification (Level) Examination - CATTI, providing multi-language interpretation and translation services. After years of effort, Bosi Beijing Translation has developed into a well-known brand in the domestic translation industry, especially excelling in high-end interpretation such as simultaneous interpretation at international conferences, talent cultivation and selection for foreign affairs, and premium written translation services. The center has signed contracts with hundreds of foreign experts, overseas returnees, national-level translators, and experienced professional translators.

Bosi Translation always adheres to the principle of "spare no effort to serve customers, always prioritize the customer," placing the interests of every customer first. We ensure that customers receive satisfactory quality and service while maximizing cost savings, truly safeguarding the interests of our clients, earning praise from numerous customers. We offer 24/7 hotline service 365 days a year to promptly address client needs. In terms of quality control, we have a strict quality control process to ensure your satisfaction with both quality and price.

Bosi Translation upholds the concept of "speed and efficiency," dedicated to building an expert team of highly skilled and efficient translators. We consistently adhere to the principle of putting customers first, sincerely serving enterprises, institutions, government departments, research organizations, and social groups with professional, timely, and convenient translation services. Bosi Beijing Translation insists on "outstanding quality" as its foundation, committed to promoting the exchange of economy, culture, and technology. A reasonable translation operation process, a stringent quality control system, and unique translator training and review standards guarantee our high-quality translation services, earning the trust of many customers.

Bosi thanks each customer who has cooperated with us, willing to join hands with new and old customers to build a long-term friendly, efficient, and stable communication platform. Faced with opportunities and challenges in the future, we will continue to innovate with a perfect service attitude to achieve our sincere and trustworthy entrepreneurial philosophy, allowing customers to obtain faster and better services. We sincerely hope to establish long-term friendly cooperative relationships with more customers, becoming a top-tier translation enterprise with substantial strength domestically and in Beijing.
