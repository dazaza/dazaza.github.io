The latest film, 150 filmmakers recall 30 years, special feature "The Flowing Years" aired today - Chinese Film

by liteemjt on 2011-06-16 13:12:05

To commemorate the 30th anniversary of reform and opening-up, a 30-episode large-scale special 《Our Screen Memory from 1978 to 2008》has been produced by gathering domestic film, television, sociology circles as well as famous planners and excellent directors. The latest movie will premiere on December 12th on CCTV Film Channel and sort out the common image memory since the reform and opening-up with audiences.

According to producer Chen Bin's introduction, "Flowing Years" abandoned the commonly used annal form. By classifying and selecting more than 300 excellent realistic subject films, it told the evolution of each type of film in the promotion of society and film in the form of keywords. Hot topics such as intellectuals, reflection, business, and emigration fever will also re-enter the audience's line of sight.

This special documentary gathered more than 300 realistic subject films since the reform and opening-up, from the early well-known "Night Rain on Mount Ba", "The Legend of Tianyun Mountain", "Midlife", to "Lovers", "Neighbor", "Wild Mountain", etc. Each film has become the best witness of the times. It is reported that in nearly 1 year from the idea to the completion of production, the program successively interviewed more than 150 filmmakers such as Ge You, Jiang Wenli, Gu Changwei, Zhao Huangzhang, and Wang Fuli, interpreting this era with their own feelings.
