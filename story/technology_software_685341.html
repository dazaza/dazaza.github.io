All the way, mother and daughter were laughing and chatting. Around 11:30, when the car stopped at the intersection of Hefang West Garden waiting for the red light, the daughter found that her mother had her eyes tightly closed, leaning against the seat. "Mom, Mom, what's wrong with you!" Sun Yanfeng was startled: just now her mother put a candy in her mouth, how could she lose consciousness in the blink of an eye! No matter how Sun Yanfeng shouted, Liu Shanmei didn't respond. Sun Yanfeng touched her mother's hand, it was icy cold, then she put her hand under her mother's nostrils, already unable to feel any breathing. "Driver comrade! My mom is not okay! What should I do!" Sun Yanfeng shouted loudly.

Four minutes later, the car stopped in front of the Armed Police Fire Hospital. Just then, there was a nurse by the door. The nurse brought a stretcher with Yang Chunlin, and the passengers helped lift the stretcher and the patient, carrying the patient into the hospital.

"On the bus, my 86-year-old mother Liu Shanmei suddenly suffered from myocardial infarction and collapsed on the seat. Especially thank this driver whose license plate ends with 77!" Yesterday, a thank-you Weibo post moved the citizens of Nanjing surrounded by cold rain. Many netizens forwarded the Weibo post, expressing their admiration for the lifesaving bus driver. Yesterday, Ms. Liu's family came to the fleet to thank the lifesaving driver. They recorded 48 nursing diaries while taking care of their centenarian mother for 13 years.

When the family came to express their gratitude, the stationmaster thought they were there to complain.

"Please leave me your mobile phone number," Sun Yanfeng pulled a female passenger because many passengers delayed for 40 minutes to help her. "We don't need to leave our numbers. I just saw the last two digits of the driver's license plate, which are 77," said the female passenger.

Ms. Sun said that her mother had a history of heart disease and hypertension but had never had an episode before. She did not know why this time her mother suddenly suffered from myocardial infarction on the bus. "Although my mother's illness came suddenly, the driver reacted faster and took better measures," Ms. Sun said. If the driver had parked the car by the roadside and called 120, the long wait for the ambulance would have missed the best rescue time. "The doctor said, if it was a few minutes later, we wouldn't have seen my mother again." Currently, Liu Shanmei has returned home to rest quietly. To thank the driver, Ms. Sun thanked this good-hearted driver on Weibo the same day.

On the morning of November 11th, Sun Yanfeng accompanied her 86-year-old mother Liu Shanmei to visit relatives at the retirement home on Central North Road. At 10:40, after leaving the relatives' house, the two took a No. 25 bus and transferred to No. 72 at Zhongyangmen North Station to return home. The old lady sat on the "elderly" dedicated seat after getting on the bus, while her daughter sat opposite her.
