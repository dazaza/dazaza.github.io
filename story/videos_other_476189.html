The US economy is struggling, and the shadow of the European debt crisis lingers. The Fed's quantitative easing policy has limited stimulation on the real economy but to a greater extent triggers a speculative frenzy in the derivatives market. The international commodities market experiences great fluctuations, posing a serious challenge to China's price stability. Facing the new financial situation, there is an urgent need to select and cultivate talents in the derivatives financial market, and Lanhai Mijian has been making efforts.

Over a thousand participants, the Blue Sea Warriors team scales up

After the first two competitions, the scale and influence of Lanhai Mijian have further expanded. By June 17th, a total of 1,293 participants registered for the Lanhai Mijian competition, with 1,047 actual traders, including 36 foreign traders, a significant increase compared to the previous edition’s 868 actual participants. Participants in this edition come from 29 provinces, cities, and autonomous regions across the country, expanding both in scale and influence. Additionally, among the participants, 332 people made profits, accounting for 32.1% of all participants. 38 participants had a yield exceeding 100%, and as many as 16 participants had a yield exceeding 200%. The competition boasts 2 generals and as many as 37 officers at the regiment level, meaning that 2 accounts have made profits exceeding 3 million yuan, while another 37 participants have made profits exceeding 300,000 yuan.

Daily records set new highs, legendary Wang Xiangyang shines in the expeditionary force

After three months of competition, the event refreshed the highest yield record. The daily highest return record of 94.38% held by participant Qiang was replaced by Wang Xiangyang from the expeditionary force with a record of 108.28% on April 21st. Participant Wang Xiangyang received the High Ground Flag Bearer award and corresponding prize money from the organizing committee. Wang Xiangyang has long been a prominent figure in the futures world, having emerged as the champion in the first East Airlines Financial Submerged Dragon Futures Real Account Competition held in 2008-2009, where he set an annual half-year return record of 5474.92% and an annual return record of 4239.09%, neither of which have been surpassed to date. In this quarter's race, Wang Xiangyang once again set a new record, continuing his legend.

Market is calm, yet winning participants' operational styles are intriguing

This quarter, the top three winners who received the quarterly pioneer medal based on unit net asset value growth were: Lin Feng with a 113.19% increase in unit net value, Ge Yunhua with a 110.81% increase, and Blue Coast with a 95.72% increase. The three winners will receive the quarterly pioneer medal and corresponding prize money awarded by the organizing committee, with the first-class quarterly medal prize being 30,000 yuan, the second-class quarterly medal prize being 20,000 yuan, and the third-class quarterly medal prize being 10,000 yuan.

Although the market conditions were relatively calm this season, participants in the Blue Sea Secret Sword competition still managed to fully exploit market opportunities and achieve excellent results, as objectively demonstrated by the evaluation indicators. Captain Lin Feng of the Army Group joined the competition in January this year. Even though he missed the sharp rise and fall of the commodity market in November last year, Lin Feng was still able to achieve stable returns in the volatile market this year. With a trading success rate of only 37%, he maintained steady profit growth, achieving a closed position profit ratio of 1.62. His outstanding stop-loss ability and distinct trading style left a deep impression. Although Ge Yunhua of the Army Group is just an ordinary reservist soldier, his performance is no less impressive than the big shots. Observing his return curve reveals a very distinctive investment strategy. He can resist the temptation of market fluctuations for more than 80% of the time, patiently waiting; once an opportunity arises, he goes all out, firmly committing all his funds to chase trends. His unique and suitable trading strategy allows him to achieve both a high success rate of 73% and a high closed position profit ratio of 3.4. Lieutenant Colonel Blue Coast of the Expeditionary Force Group is the most stable performer in this edition of the competition. Since the start of the second season, he has won the quarterly pioneer medal for three consecutive seasons. As of June 17th, his unit net value reached as high as 10.7181, with an annualized return of 1282%. On the overall ranking list of the first three seasons, he ranked second, also being the top-ranked participant in the Expeditionary Force group.

Seed fund profitability tends to stabilize, exciting events continue

With the aim of exploring and accumulating experience in managing derivative funds, East Airlines Finance launched the Seed No.1 Fund Cultivation Plan to cultivate excellent managers for future hedge funds. As of June 17, 2011, the cumulative unit net value of the fund was 1.2123, with its profitability trending towards stability. This edition of the competition still has three months left. Who will be the winner of the next quarter? Who will set a new record in the competition? Who will win the annual championship and the million-dollar prize? We wait and see. The scale of the Seed No.1 Fund will continue to expand, Lanhai Mijian will continue to host exciting matches, and the dreams of futures traders will continue.
