Core Vocabulary Analysis

Account for 1) Explain, clarify; 2) Account for...

Escape from... escape from, shirk responsibility

I admit that most of the photos being circulated on the Internet were taken by me. But these photos are very private and have not been shown to people and are never intended to be shown to anyone. These photos were stolen from me illegally and distributed without my consent.

Core Vocabulary Analysis

Circulate = circul(circle) + ate v. Circulate, circulate

Intend to v. Intend to...

Illegal = il(not) + legal (legal) + ly adv. Illegally

Word Group Memory: distribute, contribute, attribute, tribute

Distribute = dis(away)+ tribute(give) = give away vt. Distribute, spread

Contribute = con(fully) + tribute vt. Contribute; submit [Aid memory] All given

Attribute to v. Attribute to...

Tribute n. Tribute, eulogy [Aid memory] A gift for the king

Word Group Memory: consent, dissent, resent, sentimental

Consent = con(together)+ sent(=sense emotion) n. Agree, agree＝agreement

Dissent = dis(not) + sent n. Disagree [Aid memory] Different emotions = disagreement

Resent = re(against) + sent n. Hate [Aid memory] Opposing emotions = hatred

Sentimental[Homophonic] Three-drawer steamed bun, after breaking up only eat three-drawer steamed buns, so---a. Sentimental
