FOTON Daimler was born at the FAW headquarters in Shaghe, Beijing.

by jintaimqr on 2012-02-22 14:50:40

On February 18th, a new type of joint venture —— Foton Daimler [http://www.jt077.cn/] Automotive Co., Ltd. (short for Foton Daimler) was born at the Foton Motor headquarters in Shaghe, Beijing. According to the new joint venture model, the new joint venture will take China as its operation center, play to their respective advantages, and jointly create the Chinese self-own brand [Foton Auman]. It is reported that the total investment of the joint venture is 6.35 billion yuan RMB. Foton Motors participates in shares with brands and factories, while Daimler Trucks and Buses and Daimler Northeast Asia invest in cash. The share ratio between Foton Motors and Daimler is 50:50.

According to the agreement, the joint venture will produce [Foton Auman] medium and heavy truck products, while the other party of the joint venture, Daimler, will introduce a Mercedes-Benz OM457 heavy-duty engine into the joint venture, which will be used in the [Foton Auman] models produced by the joint venture.

Some professionals said that the joint venture between Daimler and Foton Motors broke the old pattern of multinational companies only setting up assembly plants in China over the past 30 years of reform and opening up, and pioneered the creation of a Chinese self-own brand [Foton Auman] with China as the operation center.

Unlike many other domestic automotive joint ventures, the President and CEO of Foton Daimler is Wu Yuejun appointed by Foton, the Executive Vice President is Stefan appointed by Daimler, the Chairman is Ren Shile appointed by Daimler, and the Vice Chairman is Wang Jinyu, General Manager of Foton Motors.

According to the statements from the senior management of Foton Motors, the establishment of the joint venture has significant meaning for the development of Foton Motors, which will promote the business development of the Foton Auman medium and heavy truck brands, and accelerate the internationalization process of Foton Motors. Currently, Foton Motors has an advantage only in the domestic and Asian markets, while the expansion in developed countries such as Europe and America will rely on Daimler's resources.

According to the plan of the joint venture, both parties will first cooperate globally on Foton medium and heavy truck products, and set up Foton Daimler in China as the first step of their cooperation. It is reported that Beijing's second Foton Auman factory has completed digital transformation, with a production capacity of 200,000 units.

Currently, from a global perspective, the high-end heavy truck market is already a mature market with relatively slow growth, while the mid-low end medium and heavy truck market is developing rapidly. Therefore, both parties will take China as the center to jointly cultivate a globally competitive mid-low end truck brand —— Foton Auman, and jointly develop the global market.

Reporter's observation —— Foton's confidence

After 9 years and three important rounds of negotiations, Foton Motors and Daimler finally got married, which cannot but be considered a major event in China's commercial vehicle industry, showcasing Foton Motors' confidence as an outstanding Chinese commercial vehicle enterprise.

In 2011, under the market environment where the automobile market experienced a decline, Foton sold 640,400 vehicles throughout the year, although it declined by 6.2% year-on-year, but the cumulative sales of medium and heavy trucks grew by 4.6%, becoming the pillar of Foton Motors. Currently, Foton Motors is a manufacturer in rapid development producing full series of commercial vehicles and has become the largest commercial vehicle manufacturer in Asia.

Looking at the medium and heavy truck products of both parties in the joint venture, their medium and heavy trucks positioned in the mid-low end market have leading advantages in low cost and product scale, with great development potential, while the market growth of Daimler Trucks leading globally in the high-end heavy truck field is slow, therefore, their joint venture is seen as a complementary win-win situation.

It is precisely Foton's advantageous position in the mid-low end medium and heavy truck market that has enabled Foton to take the dominant position in the 9 years and three rounds of negotiations, allowing its self-own brand [Foton Auman] to be retained in the new joint venture, which is the first time in the 30 years of China's automobile reform and opening up, and also the beginning of self-own brands going global with the help of international automobile giants.
