Post-90s entrepreneurial prodigy Wu Langjian

by hileyao8 on 2012-02-28 10:08:45

Origin: Hc gifts and handicrafts network. At the age of 16, most people may still be in school, knowing nothing about the society, let alone the distant concept of doing business and making money. But at the age of 16, he already had his own company, which made a profit of more than 500000 yuan in less than a year. His greatest wish is to make his family live a prosperous life through his own efforts, and become a person who contributes to the society. He is Wu Langjian, the "post-90s" young superman of entrepreneurship. During the registration process of "China New Power - Top Ten Evaluations of China Gift Operation Industry in 2010" held by Hc gifts and handicrafts network, Wu Langjian, only 16 years old, stood out among many applicants and attracted the attention of the organizing committee. How did this sixteen-year-old boy in a Superman T-shirt with a smiling badge break into the stormy business world? How did he break through all obstacles and achieve remarkable achievements? With these questions, the Hc gifts and handicrafts network interviewed Wu Langjian, the business general manager of Pengfeng Hardware Products Factory in the first time. During the conversation, the writer found that this entrepreneurial little superman, who went to the society for training early, still retained the shyness and honesty peculiar to teenagers. In order to do what he was good at and prove his ability at the same time, Wu Langjian, who didn't have ideal academic performance, decided to give up school and start a business, and set up a small company operating hardware products with the help of his father, starting his own entrepreneurial life. Wu Langjian designed the corporate image logo himself. "At the beginning, I don't know what to do every day." Wu Langjian recalled the situation at the beginning of his career, and smiled embarrassingly. The first battle after entering the society is to independently operate his own company. Facing the not easy company management and complex business dealings, Wu Langjian, the "post-90s", can't help feeling lost and at a loss. "This situation didn't last long. Fan Hongsheng of Hc.com contacted me by phone. After understanding, I joined the membership of trading channel and received orders one after another." Everything is difficult at the beginning. When orders gradually increase, you can gradually find the "sense" of entrepreneurship. When the editor asked about the most unforgettable experience in the process of entrepreneurship, Wu Langjian spoke without hesitation about his participation in the Spanish purchasing fair organized by Hc.com in Guangzhou. The purchasing fair was held at the白天Goose Hotel in Guangzhou. Many large foreign buyer groups were present, which made Xiao Langjian very excited for the first time. "Although the products I brought were not selected, this experience has enabled me to see the broad prospect of the gift promotion market, and make me more confident to do a good job in the business." When it comes to the biggest difficulty at present, Xiao Langjian's answer is beyond the writer's expectation, which is "because of the age limit, I can't get a driver's license". Xiao Langjian, who can't drive himself, can only take a bus when visiting customers and running business, which makes him feel very helpless. However, he complained little about the current dilemma, because the difficulties and setbacks in the process of entrepreneurship are far from this. He has confidence to overcome them with his perseverance and patience. The main products of Wu Langjian's company are small hardware products such as badges, bottle openers and refrigerator stickers. Although these products are cheap, with the enrichment of social activities, more and more places need these goods, and they can also be widely used in advertising promotion and other fields. The market demand is huge and the prospect is very broad. Therefore, even if selling a badge can only make a few cents profit, Xiao Langjian can still make a profit of 500000 yuan within a year. Speaking of the achievement of making 500000 yuan in a year, Wu Langjian was not satisfied. Because his dream is much bigger: "when I grow up, I will be worth more than 100 million yuan!" Xiao Langjian looked forward to it with childish face. This is not the greedy talk of a "little money lover", but the most simple and sincere desire in the heart of a sixteen-year-old teenager: to make more money for his parents to live a good life.
