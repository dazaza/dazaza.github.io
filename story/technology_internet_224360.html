We met online. I don't know what to do, because she hasn't given me a reply for such a long time. Must I keep waiting like this?

1. Girls want others to compliment them on their beauty. Everyone likes to hear praising words. Women are more prominent in this aspect. They will get a sense of being recognized from people's compliments and flattery. Understand the psychology of women. If you want to gain the favor of the girl you love, you need to often tell her how beautiful she is. For example, if your girlfriend spends half a day in front of the mirror getting ready for you, or changes her outfit every time you go out, shouldn't you sincerely praise her when you see her?

2. Women like giving small favors. There are subtle differences between men and women in giving and receiving gifts. A woman giving a valuable gift to a man indicates that she is willing to trust him. First, consider the gift as a sign of respect or a way to show attentiveness. Therefore, women like to receive small souvenirs from others, and they also like to give their boyfriends small gifts. If her boyfriend doesn't take the gift seriously, she will definitely be disappointed. A smart man will not forget to give her a passionate kiss when accepting a gift from a girl.

3. Respect her and understand her. Women like it when others listen to them. They hope that even if their feelings are childish or unreasonable, they can still have an effect on men. They hope that others respect and understand them. Women's hearts are easily hurt. You must protect her, care for her, and look after her.

4. Be consistent with her interests. Most women believe that a man who shares her views or interests and helps her achieve a certain goal will be a trustworthy person. Therefore, they are likely to feel closer to such men and open up their hearts to them. Generally speaking, men who won't let go of a point once they've made it will make women unhappy. If a female colleague does not meet her work quota, and you directly criticize her, you will definitely not win her favor. You should think more from her perspective while ensuring that work and the group are not affected. At this time, her heart will inexplicably lean towards you. Women generally have strong guard up. If you share the same goal with her, for example, if you both are dissatisfied with a certain colleague's actions, you will be easily accepted.

5. Pay attention to her minor desires. Women always think that men are forgetful. Therefore, if she unintentionally mentions something she is interested in, and you remember it firmly, and bring it up again one day, she will be deeply moved. For women, if you can understand even her minor desires, she will certainly feel that you understand her the most and are the most reliable. As a result, she will become closer to you.

6. Confide in her. It's true that women love the masculinity in men. Strength and bravery are characteristics of men. But under the premise of strength and bravery, men also possess emotions and tenderness. Women fall in love with a man, often because of his vulnerable side. The deeper reason for this phenomenon may be due to the innate maternal nature of women. A man easily reveals his innermost thoughts to a woman he trusts, and does so naturally. Women have sincere sympathy for such men. "A real man doesn't shed tears easily," unless he is truly heartbroken, a man won't cry, and unless he feels genuine emotion, a man won't confide his innermost thoughts to a woman. Women are easily conquered by such gentlemen.

7. Notice the subtle changes in her attire. For women, if someone notices the subtle changes in her appearance, she will feel a sense of being recognized and satisfied. Women like to pursue fashion; beauty is their nature. If your girlfriend changes her hairstyle or clothing today, and you compliment her, she will be very happy. She thinks you always have her in mind and are always watching her. She feels satisfied and won't suddenly "lose her temper" or sulk alone. Attentive men will notice the subtle changes in a woman without needing her to prompt them. This way, the feelings between you will be very harmonious.

True love is selfless, and love is mutual. Are you sure about your love? Then you will have your answer.

Wait patiently!

The end of one relationship means the beginning of another. It depends on whether fortune will smile upon you. Give her some time to help her forget the previous relationship. Whether you can be together depends on fate.
