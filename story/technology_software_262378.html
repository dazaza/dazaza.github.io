6th Grade Girl Opens Stock Account, Only Makes Profits, Passes English Level 3

by 9khm958o9x on 2009-11-20 14:27:30

Fang Ruilin said that the person she admires most is the "Oracle of Omaha" Warren Buffett. "In the future, I want to open my own investment bank." She passed the Public English Test System (PETS) Level 3 and won the second prize in the National English Super League... Fang Ruilin, a sixth-grade student at Changjiang Road Primary School in Nanjing, not only excels in English but also surprised the reporter by opening an account for stock trading at such a young age. So far, she has maintained a record of only making profits.

What level is equivalent to the Public English Test System (PETS) Level 3? Fang Ruilin's homeroom teacher, Teacher Yu, explained that it is close to the College English Test Band 4 level, "It's very rare, very rare. We were also very happy when we heard this news." Fang Ruilin's father told the reporter that when she took the exam and entered the same examination room as her peers, they were all junior high school or high school students. Although she successfully "passed," Fang Ruilin quickly prepared to take on the Jiangsu Province Advanced Interpretation Exam and Shanghai Intermediate Interpretation Exam. Previously, she had already passed the Jiangsu Province Intermediate Interpretation Exam. What use does an elementary school student have for taking interpretation exams? Fang Ruilin's mother smiled and said that the little girl has strong goal orientation; she hopes to continuously "pass" exams to prove herself.

Tricks for Memorizing Words

Like other classmates, Fang Ruilin started learning English in third grade, "I wasn't very interested when I first came into contact with it." Fang Ruilin frankly told the reporter. However, later, during an English top student competition held at school, she won the third prize, "At that time, I thought I should strive to win the first place." Due to her competitiveness, Fang Ruilin began putting effort into studying English, and unexpectedly found it increasingly engaging. Even memorizing words, which many people find difficult, seemed full of fun to her.

"Actually, many words have stories behind them." Fang Ruilin smiled and said, for example, describing heavy rain: "Rain cats and dogs." This phrase comes from European medieval mythology, where "cat" represents the rain god, and "dog" represents the wind god. When the wind god and the rain god come together, the rain naturally falls heavily. The teacher mentioned it once, and she remembered it immediately. How to remember words without stories? "Many words are derivatives. As long as you remember one original word, then add prefixes and suffixes, you can remember many words at once."

Stock Trading Attracts Grandma's "Investment"

During the summer vacation class this year, due to free time between morning and afternoon classes, it was hot at noon, so her mother took Fang Ruilin to a nearby securities company to rest. Unexpectedly, this little girl became interested in stock trading. After pondering for a while, she strongly requested to engage in "actual combat." Using her father's name, Fang Ruilin opened an account and invested all her accumulated more than 10,000 yuan of lucky money.

"We didn't participate at all from start to finish. Originally, we wanted her to suffer some losses so that she would understand that nothing is as simple as imagined." But what Fang Ruilin's mother did not expect was that the little girl seized the information about swine flu right away and began buying pharmaceutical stocks, "When they reached the expected price, she entrusted me to sell them." Through multiple transactions, Fang Ruilin managed to make profits without any losses, earning more than 1,000 yuan so far. Seeing her good results, her grandmother couldn't help but want to "invest."

Wants to be China's "Buffett"

"This child is really interested in English." Fang Ruilin's mother sighed. During the summer vacation this year, she had intermediate interpretation classes every morning and "New Concept English 3" lessons every afternoon. She felt very tired, but the little girl replied saying, "Mom, that's a kind of enjoyment." For such a student, Fang Ruilin's homeroom teacher, Teacher Yu, expressed great appreciation as well. Not only does she study herself, but she also uses the class bulletin board to set up an English corner, leading her classmates to learn together. To encourage Fang Ruilin, the school also provided various platforms for her to showcase herself, enhancing her sense of achievement.

What does she want to do in the future? "I want to be a banker." This answer was somewhat unexpected to the reporter. Originally, it was assumed that with her excellent English skills, she might want to be a diplomat, but surprisingly, she aspires to the financial industry. Fang Ruilin said that the person she admires most is the "Oracle of Omaha" Warren Buffett, "In the future, I want to open my own investment bank."
