At around 11:30 AM on March 1st, a Jinbei branded minivan with the license plate Sichuan F13811 traveling from Guangyuan to Deyang in Sichuan province, was driving along the Jing-Kun expressway towards Chengdu. When it reached kilometer 1555, it swerved to avoid a large truck. Due to its high speed, the driver had to make a sharp turn, causing the minivan to slide into the drainage ditch on the left side of the highway. It only came to a stop after colliding with an uneven part of the mountainside. The driver was firmly trapped inside the cab and unable to move.

After receiving the emergency call, the 119 Command Center of the Guangyuan Fire Brigade immediately dispatched a rescue vehicle and six officers from the Chuiyun Avenue Fire Station in Jiange County to rush to the scene for rescue operations.

At 12:05 PM, upon arrival at the scene, the rescuing fire officers found that the deformed minivan cab had tightly pinned the driver's left leg, leaving him immobile. After carefully observing the situation of the trapped driver and the deformation of the car body, the on-site fire commander immediately issued the rescue order. The firefighters first used hydraulic spreaders to expand the space in the cab, gradually increasing the rescue area. Then, one officer entered the interior of the cab through the rear door of the minivan, using a hydraulic prop to expand and support the steering wheel that was pinning the driver. To avoid secondary harm to the trapped driver, the rescuing officers carefully performed every action. As time passed, the rescue space gradually increased until finally the trapped driver’s left leg could move. The firefighters successfully lifted him out and handed him over to the on-site 120 medical staff who then sent him to the hospital for treatment.

Currently, relevant departments are investigating the cause of the accident!
