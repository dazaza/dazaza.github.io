Anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women [Post a comment] Publication date: December 20, 2011 Source: China Intellectual Property Network Summary: CCTV exposes the truth about anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women, which may make radiation stronger. Does it really have an anti-radiation effect for pregnant women? Perhaps this can be verified from the origin and material of the anti-radiation clothing. Related technical dynamics: This utility model discloses a portable anti-radiation suit for pregnant women, including a front placket and a back placket. A hood opening is set at the connection between the front placket and the back placket. A back placket with Velcro is set on the back placket. The front placket and the back placket are integrally formed during manufacturing. Fixed buckles and pubic bones are set on the front placket. An elastic band is set on the fixed buckle, and the fixed buckle is solidly connected to the elastic band. A Velcro strip is set on the elastic band, and the elastic band and the Velcro are integrally formed during manufacturing. This utility model has a simple structure and is easy to use, www.qswxw.com, and can be used throughout the pregnancy period of pregnant women, avoiding waste of resources and reducing the economic burden on pregnant women. Pregnant women's anti-radiation clothing This utility model relates to anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women, including: a main body of the garment; an anti-radiation structure connected or integrated with the garment main body; and a flexible or rigid luminous structure and/or reflective structure disposed on the outer surface of the garment main body. Through the provision of this utility model, luminescent and/or reflective effects can be provided visually, thereby increasing protection for pregnant women and enabling them to effectively avoid accidental injuries from external sources when wearing anti-radiation clothing and traveling at night during pregnancy. Through the above analysis, we know: 1. The development of anti-radiation clothing only suits the consumption habits of Chinese consumers, there is no saying that pregnant women must wear anti-radiation clothing in foreign countries; 2. In China, there are only three effective patent technologies for anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women, none of which are invention patents, indicating that the technical foundation of anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women is relatively weak and does not have pioneering social significance; Therefore, anti-radiation clothing for pregnant women is not necessarily an essential item for all pregnant women. In certain situations, this kind of anti-radiation may prevent sunlight exposure and even condense more radiation sources. Thus, the use of anti-radiation clothing should be rationally judged and analyzed based on different usage environments. (Editor-in-Charge: Xiao Xin)
