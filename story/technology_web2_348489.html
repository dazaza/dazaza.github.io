So, how do you keep your hair healthy and beautiful? What are your hair care experiences?

A: My hair care knowledge has grown over time. Let me share it with you carefully. Hair care is a very important yet challenging aspect for every actor. When we're busy, there's often no time for professional hair maintenance, especially when shooting outdoors where we face rain, fog, and sandstorms. To maintain a good appearance, we have to use a lot of pomades and other chemical products for styling during the day's shoot, leaving our hair both dirty and dry. However, since filming locations often lack proper facilities and we need to continue shooting the next day, stress can accumulate, so here are my hair care tips in several aspects:

Clean: Careful cleaning and thorough massage. Cleanly washed hair is my second face and an essential part of my image. Many people tend to overlook hair care, but I feel that like skin, hair is also quite fragile. Therefore, I have always placed great emphasis on hair care. No matter how late I finish work, I will wash my hair seriously first and never slack off. Shampoo, conditioner, and thorough massage should not be skipped and should last at least 3 minutes to ensure the hair is fully cleaned and nourished. Also, pay attention to water temperature in winter. We tend to seek comfort by making the water too hot. However, water hotter than 40℃ can damage the hair. For protection, a temperature around 37℃ would be more appropriate.

Blow-dry: Natural drying is best. If you must use a hairdryer, please use cold air. Imagine if each time after washing your hands, you place them under heat until they dry; soon enough, they'll become dry and cracked. The same goes for your scalp. Natural air-drying is the best option. If not possible, use a cold air setting on the hairdryer and remember to protect your scalp daily. Since my work involves frequent hair treatments like straightening or curling, a hair stylist once told me that when blow-drying, make sure to move the hair from the root to the tip in the direction of the hair, which makes the hair shiny, bright, and easier to style without causing damage.

Care: Do not neglect the health of the scalp. Just like the skin on the rest of your body, the scalp can be particularly sensitive in autumn. Most people focus only on keeping their hair soft while ignoring the health of the scalp. Many professionals have advised me to massage the scalp, which I diligently follow. During breaks, I gently massage my scalp to ease tension and enhance blood circulation, making me feel refreshed. In some seasons, the scalp can get very dry, causing static in the hair. It's best to use a moisturizing shampoo again to let the hair breathe freely.

Besides filming, I often change hairstyles, alternating between straight and curly hair using my hairdryer. So, when I bathe, I apply hair masks and wear a shower cap, using the steam generated from the bath to enhance the absorption of the hair care products.

In terms of diet and mood: Zinc-rich foods and a good mood are essential. For a woman, true beauty comes from health, and hair is no exception. Normally, I like eating zinc-rich foods such as sesame seeds and walnuts, which play a crucial role in scalp health. Additionally, maintaining a happy state of mind and appropriately handling stress helps keep the scalp and hair healthy, allowing my hair to withstand the test of dryness and remain fresh and lively.

Q: Many people find that you are very good at utilizing hairstyle changes to bring a fresh feeling. Sometimes you have free and easy natural short hair, sometimes elegant carved waves. Can you often change your look because of your special hair quality?

A: I think constantly changing looks and bringing fresh experiences to the audience is an actor's vocational skill. Viewers need rich visual experiences rather than static monotonous repetition. Of course, most actors may choose to change clothing styles and colors. However, apart from clothes, I pay more attention to hairstyle changes. A great style and charm complement clothing, but if the hair isn't good, it causes a discount in impact. I want to thank my parents for giving me good hair, so stylists also like to try new hairstyles on me. However, even the best hair can deteriorate over time if not properly cared for. Therefore, I have always paid much attention to hair maintenance. I believe this is the foundation of a good hairstyle. With meticulous care, good hair today will lead to beautiful hair tomorrow.
