Asia netizens account for the most in the world. .Asia is expected to become a sought-after domain name

by now on 2009-07-29 15:14:48

Recently, the author learned from Era Interconnection that the DotAsia registry launched a "Register .asia domain name for two years, and get a free exquisite USB flash disk" promotional campaign. This campaign started on July 6, 2009 and ended on October 6, 2009, lasting for three months in total. During the campaign period, as long as you registered the .asia domain name for two years or more through domestic domain name registrars such as Era Interconnection, you could receive a 2G USB flash disk, which would be sent directly by the DotAsia registry. This campaign covered a wide range and was carried out on a large scale, indicating that the popularization of the .asia domain name was reaching its peak.

The .asia domain name is the latest international top-level domain name specifically established for Asia, serving as a strong identifier for the region's online presence. According to relevant responsible persons from Era Interconnection (www.now.cn/domain/), owning an ".Asia" domain name can first prove a company's Asian identity, and secondly, it can transcend the limitations of country-specific domain names, providing companies using it with more abundant resources and opportunities. It has the characteristics of vast geographical coverage, clear concept, and strong group recognition. When the ".Asia" domain name becomes a trend, it will bring a sense of group identity to enterprises. In terms of developing the Asian economy, .Asia is the most targeted among many domain names, capable of promoting the development of online trade activities in Asia.

Moreover, in recent years, the number of Internet users in Asia has been continuously increasing, and the role of the .Asia domain name as an Asian identifier on a global scale will become increasingly evident. Reports indicate that by 2013, Asian Internet users will account for 43% of the global total. Compared to developed countries in Europe and America where the growth of Internet users is gradually slowing down, the growth rate of Internet users in the Asian region can be described as rapid progress. Following this development trend, the .Asia domain name will also welcome a great opportunity for widespread application because the network population of a region is the foundation for the popularization and application of domain names in that region. Only with an increase in the number of Internet users can the development of the regional Internet economy be promoted. Therefore, considering that Asia has the largest proportion of the global population and the highest number of Internet users, Asia possesses the world's largest Internet market. In the near future, many companies will rush to enter the Asian Internet economy, and the .Asia domain name will be the most powerful weapon for developing the Asian Internet economy.

With the continuous improvement of Asia's economic and political status on the international stage, combined with the region having the densest Internet users globally, Asia will develop into the world's largest Internet market. Currently, the application of the .Asia domain name is becoming increasingly popular. If companies want to seize the huge market share in Asia, they should pay attention to the application of the .Asia domain name, enhance their strategic preparation, and thereby obtain better development opportunities.
