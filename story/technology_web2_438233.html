In addition to the necessary equipment of the previous two days, usually a lot of outdoor activity enthusiasts will prepare many practical outdoor products. I will introduce some of the most commonly used equipment here.

Trekking pole, usually made of aluminum alloy, with three to four sections that can be telescopic structure, the tip is made of highly wear-resistant tungsten manganese alloy, the handle is made of rubber materials, and there are nylon wrist straps. The main function is as an auxiliary tool for climbing. In fact, it is not very useful in the process of climbing, but instead consumes more energy. Its function can help support when going down the mountain and walking on relatively gentle roads, reducing the pressure on the knees, thereby reducing the probability of injury.

Headlamp, an outdoor lighting device. Usually this kind of lighting equipment is formed by light-emitting diodes, with very low power consumption. A set of batteries can last for about 20 hours, so a normal two or three-day activity only needs one set of batteries. It mainly provides personal range lighting for outdoor activities after dark.

Water bottle, the main feature of outdoor water bottles is that they are extremely lightweight and easy to carry, with good sealing performance. These features provide a good basis for use in various outdoor environments. Better water bottles have anti-corrosion coatings on the inside. Even if organic beverages or carbohydrates are stored for several days, they will not spoil, providing safe water protection equipment for wilderness survival.

Stove, the usual stove for outdoor activities uses GAS gas as fuel in the gas system. A portable stove can meet the daily needs of five people. So under normal circumstances, a team of ten people only needs two stoves to satisfy the catering needs.

Walkie-talkies, due to the difference in physical strength among outdoor individuals, the speed of progress varies greatly. Maintaining good communication and contact with teammates during the journey is essential. Long-distance communication can make a big contribution to saving energy for the entire team. It is better to have at least three walkie-talkies that can communicate with each other in a team.

Knife, most outdoor enthusiasts like Swiss Army knives because of their practical functions, especially in the wild. Whether advancing or having a picnic or in special situations, knives can be used.

GPS and other guiding devices, for regular travel, the role of GPS is not very significant, but for outdoor exploration or unfamiliar routes, GPS can play a great guiding role.

Up to here, the most common outdoor equipment has been introduced. Of course, this is not all. For example, there are also camp lights, water bags (bladders), spray guns, polarized sunglasses, tableware, some backpack accessories, and outdoor skincare products, which will not be introduced one by one. Interested friends can go to some outdoor product websites to learn more about the equipment and its content.
