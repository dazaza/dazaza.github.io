Man Rapes and Kills 17-Year-Old Girl from the Same Village, then Buries Her by a Stream (Picture)

by mingdaolin on 2009-11-27 12:01:12

On her way home from work, Xiaoyan was intercepted by Zheng Mouyi, a man from the same village, at a secluded stream 200 meters away from her home. She was brutally murdered and raped, and then her body was buried by the grass near the stream.

More shockingly, Zheng Mouyi had dug the grave for burying the body a day in advance. Moreover, when the police were digging for the body, he stood among the onlooking villagers.

Yesterday, in December 2009, the Longhai Public Security Bureau of Zhangzhou announced that Zheng Mouyi was suspected of intentional homicide and was approved for arrest. At the same time, his brother, who was suspected of harboring a criminal, was also approved for arrest.

[Investigation Report]

The naked body of the teenage girl was buried by the roadside

In Xiabanlin Village, Xiaoyan and her younger sister were considered the "two golden flowers" of the village. Xiaoyan left a good impression on the villagers, especially being very obedient.

Xiaoyan worked as a salesgirl in Xiangcheng District. Every day after work, she rode her bicycle for about half an hour and arrived at her home in Xiabanlin Village around 7 p.m. However, on the evening of September 19, she did not return home all night. The next day, Xiaoyan's family and relatives searched everywhere but could not find her, so they reported it to the police.

Xiaoyan had to pass through a small stream on her way home, with lush lychee trees on both sides. Her family suspected that she might have accidentally fallen into the stream. On the morning of September 21, the police officers from Yan Cuo Police Station and Xiaoyan's family searched along the stream and drained the water in the stream. Unexpectedly, Xiaoyan's handbag, bra, sunglasses, and other items were found in the riverbed.

"The girl was likely killed nearby." The police immediately searched for clues in the grassland beside the stream. Sure enough, the grass beside a vegetable field looked different from the surroundings. When they dug about half a meter down, they found Xiaoyan, who was completely naked. After the forensic examination, Xiaoyan died of suffocation and had been sexually assaulted before death.

Locking onto the suddenly missing villager

The burial site was only 200 meters away from Xiaoyan's home. The police judged that the murderer was familiar with the terrain, and it was highly possible that a local person committed the crime. "That place is very remote, rarely passed by people, and the dense forest blocks the view," said Deputy Captain Kang of the Major Case Squad of the Longhai Criminal Investigation Brigade. Therefore, the police conducted a large-scale investigation in Xiabanlin Village.

At this point, the police learned that Zheng Mouyi, a 19-year-old villager, had suddenly disappeared on the day of the incident. Previously, someone had seen Zheng Mouyi showing off Xiaoyan's mobile phone.

Zheng Mouyi was a major suspect, so the police implemented full surveillance on his brother, Zheng Moulin. Sure enough, early in the morning on September 22, Zheng Moulin took a package and hurried out of the house, heading towards Jiaomei in Longhai. The police followed them to a cyber cafe outside Jiaomei Town, where Zheng Mouyi walked out of the cyber cafe. Both brothers were arrested. After interrogation, Zheng Mouyi confessed to the fact of killing Xiaoyan.

[Suspect Confession]

Digging a hole in advance to prepare for murder

In the eyes of the local villagers, Zheng Mouyi was idle, often doing petty thefts in the past, and merely relied on catching some fish to make a living.

Zheng Mouyi confessed to the police that because his cousin was a good friend of Xiaoyan, he often saw Xiaoyan and developed evil intentions towards her. In addition, Zheng Mouyi knew that Xiaoyan had to pass through that stream every day after work, and the dirt road beside the stream was very secluded.

On the afternoon of September 18, Zheng Mouyi dug a hole beside the stream in advance, preparing to kill and dispose of the body. The next evening at around 7 p.m., Zheng Mouyi brought the pre-prepared wire and hid in the lychee grove. When he saw Xiaoyan riding her bicycle coming, he rushed forward and forcibly pushed Xiaoyan into the woods. To prevent Xiaoyan from shouting, Zheng Mouyi strangled her with the wire, then committed violence, and subsequently buried Xiaoyan's body.

Watching the excavation scene

During the police interrogation, Zheng Mouyi's audacity shocked the investigators, repeatedly saying "unbelievable."
