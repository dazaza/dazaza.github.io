Company Profile:

Information Industry Electronics Eleventh Design and Research Institute Co., Ltd. (EDRI) was founded in 1964, as a holding company of China Information Industry Group Corporation. It focuses on engineering design, consulting, project management, and construction general contracting. It is a well-known high-tech engineering design institute domestically, the largest IC and photovoltaic solar engineering design institute in China, and also one of the first large-scale design institutes to undergo overall restructuring. EDRI has comprehensive Class A design qualifications, Class A engineering consulting and supervision qualifications, as well as Class I general contracting qualifications for construction projects. EDRI leads in the domestic fields of new energy, electronic engineering, biopharmaceuticals, LCD displays, optical fibers, and cables.

Nanjing Branch is located at No. 23 Huashen Avenue (Wenzhu Road), Building 1, Yuhua District, Nanjing City, Jiangsu Province, with an office area of 1,300 square meters and nearly 60 employees. The branch inherits all business scopes from the headquarters and has undertaken nearly a hundred design and general contracting projects in Nanjing and surrounding areas in the past seven years.
