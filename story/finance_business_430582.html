Salty Vegetable Ultra-Man Chapter Nine: Salted Vegetables and Dogs

by anonymous on 2011-05-13 15:13:43

The reason why the pickled vegetable wanted to take the dog as his teacher was that the dog's sense of smell was very sensitive, more sensitive than the nose of a pregnant woman. Other animals could not smell the smell that the superman of pickled vegetables smelled. The dog can smell it. It can perceive unusual things without looking or touching. Even if the thing just passed by here, he can smell it. This is especially useful at night. Because of this superhuman ability, man and dog became the best friends. Animals all know that people are smart because they can use the special skills of other animals to serve themselves. Elephants have good temper, so they let elephants carry heavy things. Cows are honest and obedient, so they use cows to plow and pull the plow to do hard work. Horses are good at running, so they ride him to travel. Donkeys don't get dizzy when turning around, so they use them to grind. Chickens are easy to get pregnant, so they let them keep getting pregnant and lay eggs. Only pigs have no special skills, lazy and gluttonous, they hate that it always affects the enthusiasm of other animals to serve people, so they kill pigs and eat them for revenge. Other animals, such as wolves, tigers, foxes, etc., also have their own specialties, but because human beings cannot control them, they stay away from them and ignore them.

If people make friends with dogs, on the one hand, they take advantage of the dog's specialty, and on the other hand, people say bad words about dogs. Whenever there are words related to dogs, there is no good word, such as "dog flies", "dog thing", "dog leg", "dog skin ointment", "dogs can't help eating shit", "puppy", "nonsense", "a poor ending", "dog jumps over the wall in an emergency", "dog blood spits", "dog catches mice", "dog relies on human power" and so on. Many animals can't understand whether human beings and dogs are good friends or enemies.

The pickled vegetable wanted to learn the sense of smell from the dog. The dog didn't want to take him as a disciple. So the pickled vegetable wouldn't go away and stood in front of the dog house for three days and three nights. No matter it was windy or rainy, he made a pit in front of the dog house. One time it snowed and almost froze the pickled vegetable to death. The dog was deeply moved and said to the pickled vegetable: "Pickled vegetable, it's not that I don't teach you, but I'm afraid you can't bear the hardship. Do you know how our dog's sense of smell is trained? We train by eating shit, especially children's diarrhea. Can you stand it?" After hearing this, the pickled vegetable felt nauseous and was about to say that he couldn't stand it. But thinking of roses, the words coming out of his mouth were: "I can."

Although he said so, the dog had taken him as a disciple, but when it came to eating shit, the pickled vegetable saw the shit and vomited, and couldn't eat it at all. He asked the dog: "Why do I have to eat shit? Can't I eat something else?" The dog said: "Only when eating shit, the nose will be stimulated to stimulate the sense of smell, and the sense of smell will become more developed. Even if you don't eat shit, you have to eat smelly things like shit. But other smelly things are hard to find, and shit is economical and practical, so it's better to eat shit." After hearing this, the pickled vegetable wanted to give up. The dog was moved by the pickled vegetable's sincerity in seeking a teacher, so he taught him: "When heaven is about to place a great responsibility on a person, it must first make his mind suffer and make his muscles and bones work... What you need to change is not the shit, but your attitude towards eating shit. Don't think of the shit as shit. When you eat it, imagine the shit as meat, then the taste will be different." For example, people, whether it's tiger meat or rat meat, it's all meat, all fragrant. They distinguish and imagine it, and the feeling when eating is very different. If you want to be a superman, you must first surpass ordinary people in thought, and then you can become a superman in action. The pickled vegetable tried the dog's method, and the taste was indeed different. After eating for a period of time, he went from rejection to acceptance, and then from acceptance to liking, until he was like a dog, rushing to eat when seeing shit. After eating for a period of time, he could smell strange smells from afar like a dog. Only his nose was like a dog's nose, often wet and sticky. This made him a little worried. Although he had learned the dog's skills, would the rose dislike it if his nose was always like this? The pickled vegetable was quite troubled thinking about the rose's reaction.
