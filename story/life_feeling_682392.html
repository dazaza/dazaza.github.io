Missed it, will love still be there?

Part I

Zhenbao, who betrayed Zixia, recalled the most sincere feelings that once stood before him but were discarded by him in the vast desert. Xiaofeng, who lost Ajuzhu, plunged into an abyss of ten thousand feet in endless regret. Only when you lose something do you realize how precious it is. This old-fashioned saying has now been verified on me.

But I, have neither the determination to wander with a sword, nor the courage to leap down. I can only express my regret after being reckless in my youth here. But Mengmeng, is this still useful?

That year, I was studying at a vocational school in Kaifeng. Mengmeng should be considered our class flower. Among so many chattering girls aged seventeen or eighteen, she was undoubtedly the best. Her delicate features and graceful posture made her ponytail sway back and forth as she walked. I don't know why, but every time I saw her, I would always think of a lively deer or a young horse.

Mengmeng was a quiet girl who didn't talk much in class. Because of this, although we were in the same class, we didn't interact much. One day, a classmate had a birthday and invited a lot of classmates, including Mengmeng. Maybe because she was there, I drank particularly generously that day, and I got drunk without realizing it. The next day when I woke up, I couldn't even remember how I got home.

During the break in the morning, I was talking about drinking with the classmates around me, and Mengmeng from the front row suddenly turned around and said: "You were so drunk last night, I helped you back to the dormitory." I was surprised and opened my mouth wide: "It was you? So kind?" Mengmeng's face turned red: "I didn't expect you to be so heavy. If I knew, I wouldn't have sent you."

Looking at her rosy face, my heart started to beat fast. I, who was usually eloquent, suddenly became stuttering: "Thank you, how can I thank you?" That's how we got closer. In August 2007, the school was on vacation, and I invited Mengmeng and a few other classmates to my house. In the fields of August, there were all large green corns, like the legendary green gauze tents. We took pictures in the cornfield, laughing, running, and playing hide-and-seek. Out of breath, Mengmeng was finally caught by me. Looking at her rosy smile, I knew that I could not let go of this girl anymore.

Part II

I told my parents about myself and Mengmeng. My parents did not clearly oppose it, but just said I was too young and didn't know what liking was. I said: "You always think I'm a child, but I know we really love each other."

I returned to school gloomily and talked to Mengmeng about the conflict between me and my parents. Mengmeng didn't say anything, just lowered her head. I asked: "What do you mean? Why don't you tell your family?" Under my repeated questioning, she said: "There's no need to tell the family, even if we do, they won't agree. Your house is so far from mine, and we are both five siblings, you are the youngest at home, while I am the oldest at home. My parents definitely won't let me find someone like you..." Mengmeng's voice choked, and my heart felt like it was pierced by needles. I tightly hugged her, tears streaming down my face.

I learned to smoke and drink. My academic performance began to drop significantly. In September 2007, the teacher told my parents about me, and the family stopped me from continuing to interact with Mengmeng by making me drop out of school and return home. I thought, can I leave? I want to be with Mengmeng, even if my parents no longer take care of me. Books say that unblessed love won't last long. I asked myself: will Mengmeng and I be the exception?

In the blink of an eye, it was graduation time. Mengmeng was assigned to an electronics factory in Suzhou, while I stayed at school. I heard from the teacher that she would come back to school to pick up some materials at night. I was very excited, waiting for her outside the school gate. At 11 PM, the school gate was locked, so I crouched by the iron gate, staring at the people passing by, afraid of missing the time when Mengmeng came back. I waited until two o'clock in the morning, and finally went back to the dormitory because I was too sleepy. The next day, I asked the teacher, and the teacher said plans couldn't keep up with changes, and she had already left by train from Zhengzhou last night.

Back in the dormitory, I suddenly felt blood rushing to my head. I went crazy, throwing everything I could get my hands on all over the floor. I regretted not seeing her off at the station. After knowing my parents' attitude, Mengmeng had been hesitating. These days, I've been busy contacting work, and we didn't have much time to talk. Now it seems that she thinks we can't solve the problem with our parents, and wants to escape from my life.

Part III

In the Spring Festival of 2007, classmates gathered with their girlfriends, and I, alone, got drunk, and only then could I stop thinking about everything.

On the eighth day of the first lunar month, my parents asked me to go to Guangzhou with my cousin. I didn't want to go, fearing that Mengmeng would come back and I wouldn't be able to find her. But looking at my parents' disappointed faces, I gave in.

The train went southward, and I remained silent all the way. I thought: Mengmeng, it depends on whether we have fate or not. If we do, even if we go to the ends of the earth, we will meet again.

I worked as a salesperson in a garment factory in Zhongshan City. In the second month after starting work, one day I went to the computer room of the factory to look up information, and met a classmate from vocational school online. I almost asked without hope: "Do you have Mengmeng's contact information?" I had asked so many people back home and got nothing. Unexpectedly, in Guangzhou, I found out Mengmeng's whereabouts. God, you really are taking care of me.

On the phone, I finally heard Mengmeng's voice. I asked how she was doing in Suzhou. She said it wasn't good, and it was especially tiring. I said: "Then come here. There are more relatives here, and there will be more support." Mengmeng hesitated for a long time, and finally said, okay.

In January 2008, Mengmeng finally appeared at the train station. It was about 9:30 PM, and when Mengmeng dragging a suitcase finally appeared in front of me, it seemed like all the lights in the world were on, and my angel was walking towards me under the spotlight.

Mengmeng started living with me. We lived in different male and female dormitories. She shared a room with my cousin-in-law. To be honest, my cousin-in-law was not bad, but she was particularly stingy in dealing with people. When Mengmeng first arrived, she didn't bring facial cleanser, shampoo, and other toiletries, so she had to use my cousin-in-law's first. My cousin-in-law was a little unhappy, and Mengmeng told me when she got off work. I pulled her and went to a nearby mall. I bought Mengmeng all the necessary living supplies. I didn't want her to suffer any委屈. But when we returned to the dormitory, I still reminded Mengmeng: "My cousin-in-law has that temperament, don't be like her. We're all relatives, don't hurt the harmony."

In May in Guangzhou, it was already very hot. One evening, I bought three bottles of beer on the way home for dinner. During the meal, I handed the beer to my cousin and cousin-in-law, and opened another bottle for myself. My cousin-in-law said she didn't drink, and Mengmeng looked serious and was about to drink, but I stopped her: "Little girls shouldn't drink alcohol. You shouldn't develop this habit." Mengmeng put it down with a bang and went into the bathroom. My cousin-in-law was still making sarcastic remarks: "See, can't control her, right?" I suddenly got very angry, and said: "Why are you so nosy?" Then I went in to comfort Mengmeng. Mengmeng was crying. Seeing me come in, she coldly asked: "What are you doing here? You all are so close, why bother about me?" I said quietly: "Don't say anything, we're all family." Mengmeng raised her voice: "Your cousin-in-law treats me like this, and you still protect her?" I suddenly felt inexplicably irritated, and said: "How can you be like this? I shouldn't have let you come in the first place." Mengmeng froze for a moment, slapped me across the face. We wrestled together.

Mengmeng ignored me for several days. Although we reconciled later, the crack had already appeared. I knew I was wrong. I loved her, and really wanted her to live happily. But I used my own way, maybe this way was wrong from the beginning.

Part IV

In August 2007, my cousin had an argument with others in the workshop. No one expected that a few days later, when we went out for a walk in the evening, a group of people surrounded us, and one of them stabbed my cousin's left leg with a knife. My cousin stayed in the hospital for four days. After he was discharged, we were worried that those people might cause trouble again, so we took a bus to Shiling, Guangdong, where my cousin said he had friends and it would be easier to find jobs.

When we actually got there, we couldn't contact the friend. At this point, complaining was useless. We could only rent a house together, and my cousin-in-law took care of my cousin at home. Mengmeng and I started looking for jobs.

Not long after, Mengmeng and I found jobs at a nearby mall. In the blink of an eye, it was almost a month. One evening after work, when we returned home, Mengmeng and I suddenly found the door locked, and no matter how we knocked, no one responded. The landlord came out at this time and said that the couple had moved away. "If you pay the rent, you can continue renting."

I paid the rent, but I felt extremely blocked inside: is this the kinship that I have always valued? My mood showed on my face. While working, my partner, Lian, asked me: "Why are you frowning? Did you break up?"

Lian was a Sichuan girl, small and lively, with a face so tender it seemed like water would come out if you pinched it. She and Mengmeng were completely different types of people. Maybe because I was in a bad mood, I didn't go home directly after work that day. We ate together and talked a lot. I felt really relaxed and happy with her.

On a rainy night, when I finished work and walked to the door, I saw Lian standing under the eaves. I asked: "Didn't you bring an umbrella? Let me send you home?" "Good, I'd be grateful," Lian held my arm and stepped under the umbrella.

We walked on the road, hearing each other's breathing. Whenever thunder sounded, Lian would unconsciously tighten her grip on my arm and snuggle into my chest. I felt a bit awkward, but also somewhat pleased.

At this time, Mengmeng said she was pregnant. I was stunned. I said: "Are you joking with me?" Mengmeng said: "Three months already, do I have to joke with you?" I took her to a nearby hospital for a check-up, and the doctor confirmed that she was indeed pregnant. I said let's abort the baby. We are drifting outside, if we give birth, how can we support him?

Mengmeng agreed. After getting off the delivery bed, Mengmeng lay in the hospital, and I sat by her bedside. As it was getting dark, I went out to buy food for Mengmeng. On the way, it suddenly rained, and I was holding the lunch box ready to enter the ward, but strangely, I thought of Lian.

I put the food on the bedside table, lied that I was going home to get things, and ran to the mall to send Lian an umbrella. Sure enough, she was standing there, lonely and helpless.

A few days later, Lian unexpectedly came to my house to return the umbrella. Mengmeng happened to be at home, and upon hearing Lian's words of gratitude, she immediately understood everything. Mengmeng cried and said: "I aborted the baby for you, but you went to send umbrellas to other girls at that time, are you even human?"

I knew I was wrong. I was ruthless, how could I do such a thing? I didn't say anything. I just hit my own face repeatedly.

I cut off all communication with Lian. On November 19, 2007, Mengmeng and I boarded the train home. What made me desperate was that Mengmeng proposed breaking up as soon as we got off the car.

Self-inflicted calamity, cannot survive. Do I have any face to beg for her forgiveness?

Since then, I haven't received any news from Mengmeng. I don't seek her forgiveness, I just want her to know my regret. "Mengmeng, I'm sorry. I've wronged you, I've wronged our child. There are only 21 days left until your birthday, although I don't have the chance to celebrate your birthday again, I hope every day of your 20th year is happy."

"Can you hear this regret and blessing?"
