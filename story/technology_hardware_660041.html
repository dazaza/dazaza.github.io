Give full play to the leading role of all types of talents in starting businesses, innovating and creating excellence in various social causes.

by jdak2990 on 2012-02-07 11:03:21

Sunke County is vigorously promoting the talent-strong county strategy, giving full play to the leading role of all kinds of talents in entrepreneurship, innovation and excellence in various social causes, effectively promoting the scientific and harmonious development of Sunke County's economy and society. Typical guidance radiates and drives all kinds of talents to start businesses. Sunke County implements tracking management for outstanding talents in various fields in the key talent information database. Each county leader and talent worker directly grasp and contact a group of key talents, and through various channels, they are made into advanced entrepreneurial models with good reputation and influence. Xu Yanli, an agricultural technician in Bianjiang Village, Qik Town, contracted the land of villagers who went out to work, operating on a large scale. Now, through land transfer, the scale of operation has reached 3,500 mu. By means of loans and self-raising funds, he purchased various large-scale agricultural machinery and started the construction of agricultural mechanization modernization, implementing cross-regional operations, achieving industrialized and intensive operation development, and becoming a well-known large-scale agricultural machinery operator in the vicinity. Zhou Xianbin from Xin Feng Village, Qik Town, founded Honghu Poultry Industry Co., Ltd., which has now become the largest quail breeding base in Heihe City, with fixed assets of 800,000 yuan. The company oversees poultry breeding cooperatives, quail farms, quail specialty farm restaurants, and feed processing. After more than ten years of development and expansion, it has formed a one-stop operation model of breeding, development, processing, and sales. In 2009, it was awarded as a practical talent with outstanding contributions by Heihe City. Currently, there are a total of 2,247 rural practical talents in the whole county, including agricultural science and technology demonstration households, large-scale planting households, large-scale breeding households, and large-scale agricultural machinery households, actively promoting the economic construction of the new countryside in Sunke County. Enriching carriers focuses on guiding various talents to innovate. Sunke County has carried out the "Talent Service New Rural Construction" activity. In the form of a science and technology fair, a service team composed of 56 science and technology personnel from 13 relevant departments such as the Personnel Bureau, Health Bureau, Education Bureau, Agriculture Commission, Family Planning Bureau, and Culture and Sports Bureau of the county enters villages and towns, providing services such as science and technology consultation, science popularization exhibitions, free medical diagnosis, and distribution of information materials, answering questions about farmers' production and life on-site, spreading scientific ideas, scientific knowledge, and advanced culture, and actively guiding the masses of farmers to cultivate scientific, civilized, and healthy lifestyles, providing timely and effective services for agricultural production and farmers' lives. Optimizing services focuses on playing the role of various types of talents to excel. Sunke County emphasizes the role of various types of talents, encouraging, guiding, and supporting position excellence and industry excellence, serving the people from their positions. When major drought occurred in the county, professional technicians from relevant departments such as agriculture, water conservancy, agricultural machinery, and meteorology played an active role, entering villages and towns to guide the masses in drought relief face-to-face. Technical personnel from the water conservancy department strengthened technical guidance, inspected and installed drought-resistant equipment, guided farmers to promptly activate existing water-saving spray irrigation equipment for drought resistance, helped farmers install spray irrigation equipment, provided free drought wells for farmers, and actively utilized all available irrigation facilities to irrigate and resist drought in multiple ways; Technical personnel from the agricultural technology department proposed targeted drought relief measures based on different drought levels and crop growth stages, actively implemented early maturity measures; Meteorological department personnel implemented artificial rain, actively alleviating the drought situation. The County Directly Subordinate Organs Working Committee takes "Making Contributions in Position, Striving for Excellence as a Pioneer" as the theme of the activity for party and government talents to excel, organizing party and government talents to carry out dedication day activities. Zhao Yuncolor, Deputy Director of the Agricultural Technology Extension Center and Senior Agronomist, is committed to serving the grassroots of rural areas, spending most of his time deep in the grassroots providing guidance. Whether in the fields or on the farmhouse beds, there are always his figures teaching scientific and technological knowledge. Multiple agricultural yield-increasing new technologies such as precision sowing technology, large ridge double-row dense planting technology, comprehensive supporting assembly technology of agricultural machinery and agronomy, soil testing and formula fertilization technology, new variety demonstration promotion technology, corn ventilation dense planting technology, etc., have been well demonstrated in the "experimental fields", recognized by the masses of farmers, and then quickly promoted. In more than 20 years, he has established 110 "experimental fields" in the whole county, promoted 160 agricultural applicable new technologies, more than 150 agricultural new varieties, and demonstrated 58 scientific research results; the cumulative promotion area is 18 million mu, the increase in production reaches as high as 12.8%, the cumulative total increase in production is 216,000 tons, and the cumulative total income increase is 32.8 million yuan.
