An alternative introduction to the heroes of the Three Kingdoms~

by ufo on 2008-09-18 17:38:41

1. Emperor Xian of Han: A descendant of a wealthy family in decline. His ancestor, Liu Bang, was once very prominent, but his academic performance and leadership skills were average. He obtained the nominal position of class leader due to his ancestors' reputation. He was often manipulated by others, bullied by the class bully Dong Zhuo in his early days, and exploited by the nouveau riche Cao Cao later on, which made him extremely depressed.

2. Cao Cao: Pale face, cunning, and good at winning people over. In his early years, he had secret confrontations with the class bully Dong Zhuo, who discovered him. By being clever, he narrowly escaped and made a comeback. Afterward, he frequently fought in groups with classmates like Sun Quan and Liu Bei under the pretext of helping class leader Emperor Xian of Han.

3. Liu Bei: Tearful, large ears. His family conditions were average in his early years and gradually declined afterward; he made a living selling bamboo mats. He had some kinship with Emperor Xian of Han and often called him "big nephew." He was rich in strategy, often accompanied by Guan Yu and Zhang Fei, and recently became close to classmate Zhuge Liang.

4. Sun Quan: Slightly purplish complexion. His early performance was average, but he improved significantly under the guidance of his elder brother Sun Ce. Although his sister once sat with Liu Bei, they frequently had disputes, even to the point of irreconcilable conflict.

5. Guan Yu: Red face, valuing righteousness, an iron buddy of Liu Bei. Originated from playing with knives, his academic performance was average, but he was fierce in battles. Classmates like Yan Liang, Wen Chou, and Cai He were severely beaten by him.

6. Zhang Fei: Big eyes, black face, irascible, mature for his age, with a bristly beard on his chin. An iron buddy of Liu Bei, he rode a black horse and used a long spear. During the group fights between Liu Bei and Cao Cao, he stepped forward and roared so loudly that he broke the Dangyang Bridge.

7. Zhuge Liang: Smart and studious. Before joining, he was uncompetitive, but after being recruited by Liu Bei, he joined Liu Bei's small team. After joining, he worked hard to improve the relationship between Liu Bei and Sun Quan, making them allies and gaining the upper hand in the group fight against Cao Cao. The only downside is that he loves to set fires and has already started fires three times in different places since entering school.

8. Zhou Yu: An iron buddy of Sun Quan, a handsome man with strong leadership skills, who sat next to the class beauty Xiao Qiao. He was successful in his early years, but later often engaged in intense competition with the smart and studious Zhuge Liang, often getting sick out of anger.

9. Huang Gai: An iron buddy of Zhou Yu, fond of playing with sticks. In his early years, he was mischievous and often got spanked by his parents, eventually developing an iron butt. During the joint operation between Sun and Liu against Cao Cao, he used his butt as the carrier for the flesh-wounding strategy and achieved remarkable success.

10. Jiang Gan: An iron buddy of Cao Cao, and a kindergarten classmate of Zhou Yu. Average ability, eager to show off. During the group fight between Cao Cao and Sun-Liu, he attended a kindergarten reunion hosted by Zhou Yu and couldn't help but show off, stealing a secret letter from Zhou Yu and handing it over to Cao Cao, causing classmates Cai Mao and Zhang Yun to be unjustly beaten by Cao Cao. After Cao Cao realized this, he almost got beaten himself.

11. Dian Wei: An iron buddy of Cao Cao, one of the three strongest students in the class. When Cao Cao competed with Zhang Xiu for a girlfriend, he stood up and was severely beaten by Zhang Xiu and his gang of social loafers.

12. Diaochan: The only school beauty in the class, one of the four greatest beauties in history, stunningly beautiful. After marrying, she was bullied by the class bully Dong Zhuo, but was rescued by classmate Lü Bu, to whom she gave herself in marriage. Unfortunately, heaven envied her beauty, and soon after, Lü Bu was severely beaten by Cao Cao. She left home afterward and has been missing ever since.
