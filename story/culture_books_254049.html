"Maybe It's Love" - a pure romance between a naive girl and a president, but is it real or fake?

by jqrxass on 2009-10-30 17:59:19

The nineteen-year-old campus girl, Zhou Shi, is pure and adorable, unrestrained and capricious. Wei Qing, the nearly thirty-year-old company president, is handsome and suave, a man of charm and charisma. When she meets him, he becomes infatuated with her, but she does not succumb to his wealth and power; instead, she tries to avoid him. As falsehood turns into truth and truth into falsehood, over time they develop feelings for each other, unable to resist their hearts. However, there are many gaps between them: family background and age... And at this moment, the intervention of another young man causes more ripples in their already contentious relationship...

**Part 1 of "Perhaps Love"**

Zhou Shi threw the large and small packages in her hands to the ground, squatted at the school gate under the blazing sun, catching her breath. She could barely walk anymore, exhausted, her hands were all red from carrying the bags, and sweat poured down her face. After resting for a while and just as she straightened up, she heard a horn honk. She looked up and saw the electronic gate of the school slowly opening. Zhou Shi lazily moved the stuff on the ground to one side and turned around. Hey! A luxury car! Another rich kid showing off, she thought, and whistled loudly at the car.

**Part 2 of "Perhaps Love"**

After working continuously for three nights, she was already feeling overwhelmed because during the day, she had to force herself to memorize English words and deal with numerous homework assignments. She was busy day and night, almost unable to keep up. With the postgraduate entrance examination approaching, things were much tenser and busier than before. Many students said that preparing for the postgraduate exam was not something human beings should endure—it made people look haggard, pale as vegetables, mentally collapsed, even worse than the college entrance exam (Gaokao). Moreover, Zhou Shi also worked in a bar at night, leading such a hard life. It was unimaginable.

**Part 3 of "Perhaps Love"**

Wei Qing drove around the streets for a few rounds and finally arrived at Zhou Shi's school. Knowing that she wouldn't answer his calls, he dialed the dormitory phone. Liu Nuo answered, telling him that Zhou Shi wasn't there—she had gone for an interview and hadn't returned yet. He glanced at the time, it was already so late, why hadn't she come back? Could something have happened? So, he called again. As expected, after only two rings, the call was hung up.

**Part 4 of "Perhaps Love"**

Wei Qing frowned, “Zhou Shi, watch your words! Don’t be disrespectful!” He was truly angry; how could she become increasingly audacious! Zhou Shi glared at him, knowing she had indeed gone too far. For a moment, she didn't say anything, then after a long pause, she said seriously, “Wei Qing, let me make this clear today: don’t think that just because you kissed me, I’m your girlfriend now. Isn’t it ridiculous these days? People who go out to play have no issues. I can only consider myself unlucky. Besides, it’s not a big deal anyway. Let’s call it quits from our past encounters. You go your way, I’ll go mine. We won’t interfere with each other anymore.”

**Part 1 of "Perhaps Love - The Next Chapter"**

Zhou Shi trembled and grabbed a windbreaker of Wei Qing’s to wear. Not daring to stay longer, she steeled herself and came out, seeing her mother sitting on the sofa. Quickly, she said, “Auntie, please wait a moment, I'll get you some tea.” She sneaked into the kitchen to find tea and cups, trying to comfort herself, thinking it wasn’t a big deal. Since she had already lost face, she must act as if nothing happened, maintaining a thick-skinned attitude, handling any situation calmly. No matter what, even if the sky falls, she won’t be afraid! Once she made up her mind, she felt calmer.

**Part 2 of "Perhaps Love - The Next Chapter"**

Wei Qing helplessly said, “Zhou Shi, if you really want a part-time job, why don’t you just come to my company? There’s plenty of work available, and we can negotiate the pay.” Zhou Shi shook her head, “I’d rather not go to your company!” Wei Qing felt a bit frustrated, “Zhou Shi, given our current relationship, do we still need to be so formal? What’s wrong with coming to my company? Unless you’re deliberately avoiding me.” Zhou Shi quickly replied, “I’m not.” Due to the recent engagement issues, both were sensitive and often argued. Zhou Shi always avoided bringing up the topic.

**Part 3 of "Perhaps Love - The Next Chapter"**

Upon hearing this, Zhou Shi’s face darkened, feeling deceived. Apparently, her previous friendly demeanor was just a game! Coldly, she said, “That’s Wei Qing’s past, nothing worth mentioning.” Feeling cornered, she added, “Who doesn’t have a couple of messy affairs in their past? Even I do.” This was her way of covering up the peace. Compared to Wei Qing, she was like a blank sheet of paper! But surface words still needed to be spoken since she was now his person. Wei Qing, you wait and see how I deal with you later!

**Part 4 of "Perhaps Love - The Next Chapter"**

Seeing her frail state, Wei Qing felt pity and said, “It’s a mess here. Let’s go back to my place first, and we can tidy up tomorrow.” He picked her up horizontally and started walking out. Zhou Shi was startled and weakly protested, “Where are you taking me like this? Put me down—” Wei Qing shushed her, “Be good—don’t move around. Just lie there properly.” She was too weak to walk now.
