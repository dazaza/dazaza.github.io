China News Service, Xian, February 25 (Zhang Yuan), at 10 a.m. on February 25th, 4086 citizens of Xian gathered in Daming Palace National Heritage Park and successfully refreshed the Guinness World Record for collective rope skipping as challengers, Lingtian Chuanqi.

Rope skipping, as a kind of bodybuilding sport, has quite a lot of benefits to various organs such as cardiopulmonary system, coordination, posture, weight loss, etc., which is an appropriate sport for both the old and the young. On January 23, 2005, 2472 people from Hong Kong Special Administrative Region created the Guinness World Record for the most people skipping ropes at the same time and place. On July 12, 2007, 3009 citizens of Changsha refreshed this record, Fantasy Magic Popular fantasy novels recommendation 2012-02-26 (Recommendation of the day).

At exactly 10:00 a.m., the challengers all wore red T-shirts and jumped together under the direction of the organizers. The whole rope skipping process lasted five minutes. It was introduced that compared with individual events, it was very difficult to challenge the Guinness Records in a group unit. This challenge required more than 4000 people to skip ropes for five minutes at the same time and place.

After the rope skipping, Mr. Chen Hailong, the certifying official of Shanghai Daworld Guinness and the notarization personnel of Weiyang District Notary Office of Xian City checked and counted the relevant data together and then recognized the success of the challenge.
