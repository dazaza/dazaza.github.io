Small whitening recipes, big collection of beauty tips:

1. Milk Whitening: Prepare a small glass of fresh milk (in summer, you can also put the fresh milk in the fridge for a cooler and more comfortable experience). Steam your face and soak a piece of cotton pad with fresh milk, then apply it on your face for about 15 minutes. Remove it and wash your face with clear water. Persisting long-term can make your skin tone white and even.

2. Prepare a three-finger-wide and two-finger-long aloe leaf with spots, remove the thorns and wash it clean, then prepare a three-centimeter-long cucumber, an egg white, 23 grams of pearl powder, and an appropriate amount of flour (used to adjust the consistency). Put the aloe and cucumber into a juicer, pour the juice into a small bowl, then add the egg white, pearl powder, and an appropriate amount of flour to form a paste that does not flow down. Wash your face, apply the mixture on your face, wait until it dries, then wash it off, pat on softening water, and use skincare products. Do this once or twice a week.

3. Tomato Honey Whitening: This whitening recipe can be used for both facial and hand whitening. It is especially effective for acne-prone skin, as it can effectively remove oiliness, prevent infection, and make the skin white and delicate.

Formula: Half a tomato, an appropriate amount of honey.

Usage: Blend the tomato into tomato juice, then add an appropriate amount of honey and stir until it becomes a paste. Apply it evenly on your face or hands, wait for about 15 minutes, then wash it off. It is recommended to do this 1-2 times a week.

4. Blend green peppers, cucumbers, one-quarter bitter melon, celery, green apples, etc., into beauty vegetable juice to drink. Such vegetable juice contains sufficient vitamin C, which is very effective for whitening.

5. Take a fresh egg, wash and dry it, then soak it in 500 milliliters of high-quality vinegar for a month. After the eggshell dissolves in the vinegar solution, take a small spoonful of the solution and mix it with a cup of warm water, stir and drink it, one cup per day. Long-term consumption of vinegar egg liquid can make the skin smooth and delicate, eliminating all black spots on the face.

6. Honey Protein Mask: One fresh egg, a small spoonful of honey, mix them evenly. Before bedtime, use a clean soft brush to apply this mask on your face. During this time, you can massage, stimulating skin cells and promoting blood circulation. After some time when it dries, wash it off with clear water, preferably twice a week. This mask can also be diluted with water to rub on your hands, preventing chapped hands in winter.

7. Traditional Chinese Medicine Whitening: Ingredients: Angelica, licorice, apricot kernel, danggui, green bean powder, sandalwood, etc., ground into fine powder equally.

Usage:

(1) Washing face: Add an appropriate amount of water.

(2) Applying mask: Add honey, milk, and one-quarter of an egg white, adjust appropriately and apply all over the face (excluding eyes and mouth), wait until it dries, then wash it off with clear water. Once or twice a week (not too many times, the skin may not be able to handle it), suitable for acne, black spots, freckles, and shrinking pores around the nose. People who have used it find it good, making the skin whiter and tender, and it's cheap.

8. Ingredients: 6 grams of Angelica; one egg yolk; one tablespoon of honey; one teaspoon of cucumber juice; three teaspoons of olive oil.

Method: (1) First put the Angelica powder in a bowl, add the egg yolk and stir evenly.

(2) Then add honey and cucumber juice, mix evenly and apply on the face, after about twenty minutes, rinse with clear water.

(3) After washing the face, use a cotton pad soaked in olive oil, apply on the face for about five minutes.

(4) Then cover the face with a hot towel, at this time the cotton pad does not need to be removed.

(5) After the towel cools down, remove the towel and cotton pad, and wash the face.
