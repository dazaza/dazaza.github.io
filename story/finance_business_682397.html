Grandma Throws 32-day-old Granddaughter From 15th Floor, Suspected Family Discord Between Mother-in-law and Daughter-in-law

by dahslkh6 on 2012-02-28 21:13:40

Grandma threw her 32-day-old granddaughter off the 15th floor, suspected due to discord between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law. Before the incident, neighbors heard loud arguments from their home. The grandmother jumped from the 15th floor with her granddaughter in her arms. Witnessing the tragedy, the child's mother attempted wrist-cutting but was luckily saved.

The 32-day-old girl had just received blessings from relatives at her one-month birthday banquet two days ago and became a victim of family anger when her grandmother jumped with her from the 15th floor.

Upon landing, she was thrown aside, her tiny face serene as if sleeping. Nearby, a man ignored the police officers on the scene, squatting beside her cold body, in prolonged silence.

The incident occurred at around 12 noon yesterday, at the intersection of Nongye Road and Tongbai Road in Zhengzhou City.

The gatekeeper hurriedly opened the gate wider to allow the police car, fire truck, and ambulance to smoothly enter the courtyard. Neighbors gathered downstairs, sighing over the tragedy that had just happened.

According to everyone, around 11:40 am, they heard intense arguing coming from the 15th-floor apartment. Five people lived there: a couple with their newly born daughter, and the husband's parents.

They said that after the argument went on for a while, someone shouted: "Help! Someone is jumping!"

Immediately following, the grandmother jumped down holding her granddaughter, landing on the balcony of the fourth floor. The child landed several meters away from the grandmother.

Seeing this, the child's mother cut her wrists.

After the 120 emergency personnel arrived, they first checked on the ones who jumped and found them beyond saving. They quickly took the child's mother to the Fourth People's Hospital of Zhengzhou.

The scene has been cordoned off by the police. From afar, one could only see the child lying on the ground. Just two days prior, the family joyfully celebrated her one-month birthday. Her birth undoubtedly brought happiness to the family. More than one person noticed that the child's father had been smiling and greeting everyone recently, even the night before the incident.

"Regardless of the reason for the argument, the child is too innocent. How could the child be involved?" The neighbors sighed, believing this family was ruined.

Fortunately, by yesterday afternoon, the child's mother who attempted wrist-cutting was successfully saved.

An informed source claimed that the child's grandmother might have suffered from depression, but this was not confirmed by the family.
