Nanami Mimi, virtual PK real community life!

by dongguahehe on 2006-11-15 00:49:22

Nanami Community is a 3D interactive virtual reality community. The virtual and the real get wonderfully combined here. You can chat, play games, and make friends in the virtual world just like in the real world. You can linger on Huaihai Road in virtual Shanghai; you can whisper to your lover while listening to HipHop music in Naname Love Bar; you can also practice English conversation in the virtual iCanSay training center.

Nanami has independently developed world-leading 3D interactive technology, and all content is based on the WEB browser. Compared to more powerful 3D+SNS, there's no need to install a client. You can even sit on a bench to rest or hop around (though the hopping motion looks a bit like a zombie - no part of the body bends -_-|||)! Or invite your friends to your home for instant chatting, because Nanami provides easy communication with each room being an independent 3D interactive chat room, complete with audio and video inside the room.
