Jiangsu Zhangpu Stainless Steel Materials Co., Ltd. (Dongguan Branch) is a subsidiary of Jiangsu Zhangjiagang Posco Stainless Steel Materials Co., Ltd., specializing in providing various domestic and imported stainless steel plates, strips, pipes, bars, and wires. The company mainly deals with 300 series chromium-nickel austenitic stainless steels (301, 304, 304L, 321, 302, 303, 316, 316L, 317L, 314, 309S, 310, 310S), special stainless steels (630, 631, 632, 840, 904L). Meanwhile, it collaborates with large stainless steel enterprises such as Baosteel, Taigang, Lianzhong, Yongda for friendly cooperation and joint sales. The supply specifications are complete and diverse.

(Note: L represents low carbon, such as 304L having lower carbon content than 304. S represents high-temperature resistance, like 310S being more heat-resistant than 310. High-temperature resistant stainless steels include 317L, 314, 309S, 310, 310S. 630 and 631 are martensitic precipitation hardening stainless steels, whose performance features can adjust strength levels by changing heat treatment processes. 840 and 904L are super austenitic stainless steels, possessing superior corrosion resistance and high-temperature resistance (maximum temperature up to over 2000 degrees), often used in strong acid containers due to their excellent corrosion resistance.)
