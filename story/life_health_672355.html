What are the advantages and disadvantages of each? Below, experts from the Xi'an Gynecology Hospital will specifically explain the strengths and weaknesses of painless abortion and medication abortion.

**Medication Abortion**

During a medication abortion, no instruments enter the uterus, reducing the likelihood of infection post-abortion, as well as minimizing the possibility of injury to the uterine lining. Additionally, medication abortion is convenient and quick. These are the advantages of medication abortion. However, medication abortion has certain limitations; it is only applicable during the early stages of pregnancy (from day 39 to 49 after the first day of the last menstrual period). Before undergoing a medication abortion, an ultrasound must be performed to rule out ectopic pregnancy and to measure the size of the gestational sac. If the gestational sac exceeds 2.3 centimeters, a medication abortion is not suitable.

Generally, the fetus should not be too large for a medication abortion, ideally less than 12 weeks. There are risks associated with medication abortion, including incomplete abortion, prolonged bleeding (which can last up to 1-2 months), and eventually requiring a curettage. Medication abortion increases the risk of infection and may cause other adverse reactions such as lower abdominal pain, nausea, and diarrhea. Even the smallest side effects can lead to menstrual irregularities, characterized by shortened or extended menstrual cycles and increased menstrual flow. For women who have not yet given birth, repeated use of medication abortion not only increases the risk of infection or heavy bleeding but can also lead to infertility, causing lifelong regret. Therefore, women should be cautious when choosing medication abortion.

**Painless Abortion**

The painless abortion procedure uses advanced self-controlled analgesic technology. A short-acting intravenous anesthetic is administered, quickly distributing throughout the body and putting the patient to sleep. After the surgery, the patient can rest briefly before leaving the hospital on their own. The entire process takes only 3-5 minutes, with minimal trauma, ensuring safety and reliability, and allowing for rapid recovery. During the abortion, a suction device enters the uterine cavity to remove the attached material from the uterine wall. If the doctor performing the operation is experienced, there will be no damage to the uterine lining. However, improper handling can injure the uterine lining.

Choosing a professional and appropriate hospital can minimize the harm caused by painless abortion surgery.
