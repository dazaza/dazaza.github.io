Zhengzhong Golf Club is hosting a top-level Chinese golfing event.

by golf8279 on 2010-05-27 18:38:52

Sohu Sports News: "The inclusion of golf in the Olympics means that for Chinese golf, which has been developing for over 20 years, it's time for a breakout. As one of the top golf clubs domestically, we are willing to make every effort to promote the development of Chinese golf and spread Chinese golf culture. Moreover, as a pure membership club, we hope our members can share in the glory of China's top tournaments!" said a responsible person from the Zhongsheng Golf Club in an interview with reporters.

Based on this hope, during the golden season for southern golf clubs, the Zhongsheng Golf Club joined hands with the Chinese Golf Association, becoming the qualifying and first competition venue for the Chinese Professional Golf Championship.

A Pure Membership Club is Rising

The Zhongsheng Golf Club is committed to becoming "the best-managed golf club in China," positioning itself as "Urban • High-end • Pure Membership." It comprehensively improves its management and service standards, providing high-quality hardware facilities and efficient services, earning recognition from its members and praise from all sectors of society!

With large-scale hardware construction, relying on the planning and design of internationally renowned master designers, the low-key yet luxurious clubhouse, the perfect combination of river and hill-style courses, top-notch green maintenance levels domestically, orderly daily operations, and pure membership operation among other high-end elements have left professional players and guests who visit in awe. Based on an analysis of the inner needs of high-end members, the Zhongsheng Club adopts a "pure membership" operational model, adhering strictly to serving only members, receiving only members, while at the same time strengthening the management of the entire operational process. Through effective, humanized management, it ensures high-end golfing services, drawing attention from the industry. After nearly two years of successful pure membership operations, the Zhongsheng members, through easy booking, enjoyable golfing, and experiencing warm services under the pure membership system, began to dispel their initial doubts about whether the club could successfully implement a pure membership system. Nowadays, the pure membership system of the Zhongsheng Club has risen to the forefront of the few pure membership clubs in the country, achieving a magnificent transformation in history. The club’s comprehensive quality ranks among the top of the 14 clubs in Shenzhen, truly becoming a "Urban • High-end • Pure Membership" club.

Peak Collaboration, Spring Glory

Starting this year, to align with golf’s inclusion in the Olympics, through the integration of golf events and golf equipment, the Chinese Golf Association launched the concept of a "Grand China Tour." Compared to previous China Tours where players could only win prize money without gaining world ranking points, the new round of the Chinese Professional Golf Tour, led by the Chinese Golf Association, introduces the 'Grand China Tour' concept, including five tiers of events: professional championships, professional challenge tournaments, professional elite tournaments, professional open tournaments, and world-class professional golf events. Besides tournament prizes, the more significant goal of the 'Grand China Tour' is to allow Chinese players to earn world ranking points, which is crucial for realizing China's Olympic strategy in golf. All sectors of society have given high attention and expectations to the integrated Grand China Tour, especially the Chinese Professional Golf Championship.

"The reason we chose the Zhongsheng Golf Club as our qualifying and first competition venue this year is not only because Zhongsheng Club is a pure membership club with first-class green speeds, high-end hardware quality, and increasingly mature operational systems, but more importantly, we recognize the active exploration made by the Zhongsheng Club management team in building a sustainably developing club. By holding both the qualifying and first two matches at the Zhongsheng Club, we hope to work hand-in-hand with this pure membership club to give the Chinese Professional Golf Championship a good start and offer Chinese golf a splendid spring!" said the event organizer's responsible person.

March 30 (Tuesday) - Second match of the member-professional-amateur pairing competition at the Zhongsheng Golf Club

April 9 (Friday) - Second round of the first station tournament of the Chinese Professional Golf Championship

April 10 (Saturday) - Final of the first station tournament of the Chinese Professional Golf Championship

April 11 (Sunday) - Final of the first station tournament of the Chinese Professional Golf Championship
