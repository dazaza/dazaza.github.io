A Little Strategy of QQ Farm at 7 O'clock

1, Raising - The animals that can be raised in different levels have different changes. If you want to raise more animals, you must expand their residence. The larger the expansion, the more animal quantity will increase accordingly. At the same time of the expansion, you should also distinguish the place where the animals you want to raise live. Different animals live in different places.

2, Growth - The growth period of each kind of animal is different. When they grow to a certain extent, they will enter a production period. At this time, it is time to produce. Different animals have different production times. After the production period is completed, meat can be harvested. (For example: chicken - harvest chicken meat. Meat price is relatively high.)

3, Food - Animal food is hay. Every 4 hours eat one hay (the higher the level, the more hay eaten, the time remains unchanged. Hay can be planted in the farm or stolen from friends; at the same time, you can also add or let friends give hay, click on the box to put the hay and you can add hay, up to 200. When hay is lacking, animals will be in a state of hunger, thereby stopping growth.

4, Egg-laying - When animals enter the growth phase, a blue scarf will be tied around their necks, and at this time they can be caught for production. You need to operate the "hand" key of the farm's production to catch the animals for production. Be sure to aim accurately. Then move the animal to the designated production area. (PS: When the animal reaches the production period, friends can help catch the animal for production ^_^).

5, Harvest - Three minutes after the production period, the results of the animals' production can be harvested. Occasionally, you can also visit your friend's farm. When it's time to act, act. Help with production if you can help, and steal quickly if you can. The harvest can be sold to the market to earn coins.

6, Experience - Raising new animals, catching your own or your friend's animals for production (adding 5 points of experience each time), harvesting agricultural by-products can all gain experience. Different animals have different experience rewards. Slapping mosquitoes for yourself or your friends can also gain experience, with a daily limit of 300 points of experience (one mosquito is 3 points of experience). At the same time, you can go to your friend's house and release mosquitoes: only eight mosquitoes can be released at a time, and after your friend's mosquitoes are eliminated, you can continue to release them. Each person has only 25 chances to make trouble every day.)

7, Level - Farms and ranches both have levels, and the experience required for upgrading is: N * 200 (N is the next level.) The higher the level, the more high-level animals can be raised, making your farm more diverse. Level upgrades can also expand nests and sheds. Tasks can be found in the lower right corner, and completing tasks can get corresponding experience rewards.

This experience and coin reward is quite an attraction for newcomers!
