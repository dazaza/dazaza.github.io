New Mentor-Apprentice System Under Your Control

The treasure-hunting internal test of "The Lost Tomb" will officially begin at 10:00 AM on April 15th. Besides numerous exciting activities and luxurious internal test gift packs, the highly anticipated mentor-apprentice system will also launch for the first time. After the treasure-hunting internal test begins, players will see the new NPC Liu Xifeng in the Sun City. Yes, all operations related to mentoring and apprenticeship will be completed under Liu Xifeng, except both parties must be friends and form a team to reach Liu Xifeng. Additionally, there are certain qualifications required for both mentor and apprentice when establishing the relationship.

After reaching level 30, players can obtain mentor status from Liu Xifeng and then recruit apprentices. Players need to be below level 45 but at least level 10 to become apprentices. Thereafter, when apprentices reach levels 30, 45, 60, 70, 80, and 90, they will give corresponding experience rewards to their mentors. These experiences are stored in an experience pool and must be claimed from Liu Xifeng. At the same time, morality points will also be rewarded to the mentor directly. The amount of experience in the pool and the number of morality points depend on the evaluation given by the apprentice to the mentor at each level, which can be good, average, or poor. Higher evaluations result in more experience and morality rewards.
