Creating a Soothing Massage

Lymphatic Massage with Warmth

For the skin to perform well, it needs sufficient warmth. The simplest method is to first rub your hands together until they're warm, then open them and press the palms against your face or entire body, allowing the skin to feel slight pressure. Below the skin lies our lymphatic circulation system; gentle pressure and slow movement can also promote lymphatic circulation. This action can be performed before and after applying skincare products. Doing so before applying skincare products heats the skin surface, helping pores open and the skin enter an absorptive state. Doing so afterward helps the applied skincare products be better absorbed.

If time permits, it is recommended to perform the aforementioned massage while applying body lotion all over the body. This allows the skin to feel cared for, sending signals to the brain. After receiving this information, the brain makes a judgment and then conveys a sense of calm to the skin.
