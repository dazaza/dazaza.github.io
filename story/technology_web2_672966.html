For health preservation in winter, drink more porridge made of five grains.

by cshelive9520 on 2012-02-23 12:05:26

Winter health preservation: drink more five-grain porridge (reference image) Five-grain health porridge, not only simple to make, but also contains ten kinds of nutrients. It has a positive preventive effect on various winter diseases and can meet the needs of people of all age groups for winter health preservation. Let's first learn about this health porridge.

Five grains combined: the most nutritious porridge in history for winter health preservation; "a bowl" takes care of it.

[Ingredients]: 50 grams of rice, one tablespoon each of buckwheat, coix seed, black rice, water chestnut, brown rice, red beans, barley, 10 jujubes, dietary health preservation, 10 longans.

Buckwheat: Rich in fiber with health care effects, it contains vitamin P which other cereals do not have. It has the function of softening blood vessels, lowering human blood lipids and cholesterol, and is effective in preventing and treating hypertension, cardiovascular disease, and diabetes.

Coix seed: Contains various vitamins and minerals, promotes metabolism and reduces gastrointestinal burden. It also enhances kidney function, clears heat, and promotes urination. Coix seed also has anti-cancer effects and can be used as an auxiliary treatment for stomach cancer and cervical cancer.

Black rice: Has the effects of nourishing yin and replenishing kidneys, strengthening spleen and warming liver, brightening eyes and activating blood circulation. When used as medicine, it has excellent therapeutic effects on dizziness, anemia, gray hair, eye diseases, etc.

Water chestnut: Ancient herbal books say it is a fine grain and vegetable product that "babies who eat it will not age, and old people who eat it will live longer." It has the characteristics of "tonifying without being too strong" and "preventing dryness without being greasy", making it the preferred food for autumn and winter tonics.

Red beans: Rich in starch, hence also called "rice beans". It has the functions of generating body fluids, promoting urination, reducing bloating, eliminating swelling, and stopping vomiting. Li Shizhen called it "the grain of the heart."

Jujube: Has the effects of replenishing deficiency and invigorating qi, nourishing blood and calming the spirit, strengthening the spleen and harmonizing the stomach. It is a good health and nutritional supplement for patients with weak spleen and stomach, insufficient qi and blood, fatigue, and insomnia. It also has liver protection and strong anti-cancer and anti-allergic effects.

Longan: Has the effects of tonifying and strengthening the body, replenishing the heart and calming the spirit, nourishing blood and strengthening yang, benefiting the spleen and opening the appetite, moisturizing the skin and beautifying.

Rice: Neutral in nature, sweet in taste, nutritive dietary. It has the effects of tonifying the middle energizer and nourishing the stomach, enriching essence and strengthening willpower, sharpening hearing and brightening vision, harmonizing the five viscera, unblocking the four pulses, relieving irritability, quenching thirst, and stopping diarrhea.

Barley: Has the effects of nourishing the heart, benefiting the kidneys, removing heat, and quenching thirst. It mainly treats irritability, thirst, diarrhea, carbuncles, external injuries bleeding, and burns.

Brown rice: Brown rice is processed slightly, the shell is removed while retaining some of the outer layer tissues such as bran, aleurone layer, and germ. Brown rice has excellent therapeutic effects on obese patients and those with gastrointestinal dysfunction. It effectively regulates metabolism within the body, corrects abnormal endocrine functions, and helps treat anemia, constipation, purify the blood.

[Method]:

Wash buckwheat, coix seed, black rice, water chestnut, brown rice, red beans, barley, then soak them in water for two hours. Remove the shell from the longans, wash the jujubes and rice and keep them ready for use. Put all the ingredients into a pressure cooker, add an appropriate amount of water, cover the lid and press the valve. After the steam comes out on high fire, turn off the fire. Once all the steam is released, turn on the fire again until the steam comes out, then turn off the fire. Repeat this process three times until the steam is fully released.
