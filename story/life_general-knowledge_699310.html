Balcony renovation techniques for small apartments by Henan Workwear Company

by rujia100 on 2012-03-09 14:23:07

For small apartment balcony decoration, due to the limited area, it is either connected to the bedroom or the kitchen. It is often used by the owner as a storage space or for placing items like washing machines. Over time, if not organized, the already narrow balcony will appear even more cluttered. The following are some small apartment balcony decoration tips from Rujia Decoration Company on how to make full use of the small balcony space:

The balcony becomes the third cleaning area: If the balcony is connected to the kitchen, then it's best to set up a storage cabinet in the corner of the balcony for storing uneaten vegetables, fruits, or infrequently used items. Additionally, you can transform the balcony into the third cleaning area in your home. The so-called third cleaning area refers to the existing bathroom and kitchen spaces related to cleaning. We can arrange according to the special cleaning needs of the family, moving tasks such as cleaning rags, washing mops, and drying clothes to the balcony. This way, even if the bathroom and kitchen areas are small, there is no need to place an unsightly washbasin in the crowded kitchen just to meet daily cleaning needs.

Integrating the small balcony with the living space: Whether the small balcony is connected to the bedroom or the kitchen, in order to effectively utilize the space, it’s best to integrate it with the living area, only needing to be separated from the outside world by floor-to-ceiling windows to obtain better privacy and decorative effects. If during renovation, the small balcony and the bedroom are paved with the same color flooring, it will significantly increase the perceived space.

Transforming the balcony into a study: Due to the small size of the living area, there is usually no separate study or workspace. If the balcony is integrated with the living area, it can be utilized as a new study. Install fixed shelves along the wall, place a small desk, and block out outdoor noise with your favorite curtains; at night, you can enjoy reading your favorite books under the soft light of a desk lamp.
