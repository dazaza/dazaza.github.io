China's self-developed SD card is available

by fifun on 2007-07-04 09:49:42

After Lang Ke ranks among the most competitive U disk leading manufacturers at home and abroad, recently, Shenzhen high-tech industry has burst a "dark horse" - Shenzhen Chipbang Microelectronics. The company's self-developed U disk control chip, in less than two years, has seized about 40% of the global market share; And its just launched the first domestic independently developed SD card master chip, breaking the monopoly of international manufacturers, will be widely used in mobile phones, digital cameras, MP3/4, GPS and other digital products memory card.

The reporter learned from the intellectual property special meeting of the National mobile memory Standards Working Group held in Shenzhen recently that with the strong support of the municipal science and technology department, a number of pioneering enterprises in our city have seized the business opportunities of the rapid popularity of the mobile storage industry and successfully achieved leapfrog development in recent years, including: With the global flash technology basic patents, Langke has rapidly grown into the most competitive U disk manufacturers at home and abroad; Shenzhen chip design company, represented by Chipbang Microelectronics, has successfully entered the larger "upstream" market of mobile memory control chips through independent research and development, and has become a "dark horse" leading the mobile storage industry.

"A few years ago, although China has become an important base for the global mobile storage manufacturing industry, domestic manufacturers mainly rely on the main control chip and chip design scheme provided by overseas companies for packaged processing and production, and the hand did not really master the core technology." However, in recent years, with the successful launch of the U disk and SD card control chip independently developed by Chipbond, the monopoly of overseas manufacturers on mobile memory chips has been broken!" Zhang Hualong, president of Chipbang Microelectronics, recently told reporters that the company's first domestic U disk control chip developed independently in 2005, the market has been very popular in the past two years, while ending the product field has been monopolized by overseas manufacturers, the total sales have exceeded 70 million pieces, and the current monthly shipments reach more than 5 million pieces. It has accounted for nearly 40% of the global U disk control chip market share.

Zhang Hualong revealed that on the basis of mastering the core patented technology of U disk control chip, Chipbang has entered the field of SD/MMC memory card control chip, which is larger in the market, and successfully developed and launched the first SD/MMC memory card with independent intellectual property rights in China recently.

"SD/MMC memory card control chips are widely used in mobile phones, digital cameras, GPS and other digital products, belonging to the high-end products, the market prospects are very promising." Zhang Hualong said that this chip launched by Chipbang has reached the international advanced level in terms of compatibility and stability with terminal equipment such as mobile phones and has been mass-produced. Driven by the expansion of global market demand for USB flash drives and SD/MMC memory card control chips, Chipbang's sales this year will exceed $20 million and strongly promote the development of Shenzhen's mobile storage industry.
