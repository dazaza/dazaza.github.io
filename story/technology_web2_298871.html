(I) An untouched cake: Large market blank, promising market prospects

Mr. Chen Yuanbin, a franchisee from Jiangsu, entered the crystal jewelry industry 20 years ago and made his first fortune through hard work. With the continuous improvement of people's living standards and aesthetic tastes, he thought that this business would flourish even more, but reality proved otherwise. The reason is: ten years ago, there were only about three crystal jewelry stores in the city, while today there are nearly a hundred of all sizes. Ten years ago, a mid-range crystal jewelry piece was priced between 200-300 yuan, but now some stores sell them for as low as 30 yuan. Today's jewelry industry may seem prosperous on the surface, but in reality, it struggles day by day. Mr. Chen Yuanbin deeply realized: the jewelry industry has already reached its saturation point, and this piece of cake has been divided up almost completely. He needs to find a new industry. But what kind of industry still has market blanks? Fashion, catering, car beauty care - which one isn't already overcrowded with numerous participants?

Once, while flipping through magazines at a bookstall, he accidentally saw the Foyunlai mechanical incense-making project and couldn't help but feel enlightened: with the further opening of religious beliefs, the continuous improvement of people's living standards, the demand for various incense products is increasing. Not only during the Spring Festival, Qingming Festival, Ghost Festival, worshiping Buddha, praying to gods, temple fairs, funerals, and ancestor worship does one need to burn incense, but also in daily life, for wealth seeking, evil repelling, official promotion blessing, academic success, wedding nights, moving into a new home, shop openings, air purification, disinfection, and sterilization, incense is burned.
