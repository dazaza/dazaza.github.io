The Six Pickled Supermen: Pickles and the Weasel

Columns: Fairy Tales Added Time: February 8, 2011 1:50:52 PM Source: Admin Clicks: 582

Pickles knew that becoming a superman was not something that could be achieved overnight. In the past, he would have needed to master astronomy, geography, the teachings of various philosophers, ancient and modern Chinese history, music, chess, military strategy, swordsmanship, as well as all the unique skills of Shaolin monks. But now times had changed. He not only needed these skills, but also the abilities of a space superhero - to emit light or catch fire at will, possessing the power of thunder. However, even with these skills, it still seemed far away from his current situation. He decided to start from where he was, first learning to release stinky farts from the weasel.

He chose this path because he believed that a superman must possess biochemical weapons; relying solely on Shaolin boxing had become outdated. For example, if someone pulls out a gun from dozens of meters away, with just one shot, you wouldn't even have time to squat into a horse stance or prepare for battle before you were finished. Moreover, using biochemical weapons didn't come with legal responsibilities. If you punch someone and they die or get injured, everyone sees it happen, and there's irrefutable evidence against you. But with biochemical weapons, there's no trace left behind. Who would know? Mastering biochemical weapons is what truly makes one a superman!

The weasel's fart, like Uncle Big Belly's, comes and goes without a trace, can be carried around at all times, and is completely natural - truly an excellent biochemical weapon. Pickles had personally witnessed how one stinky fart from the weasel could knock out a large group of mice and frogs. Therefore, he decided to first learn how to fart.

Pickles became friends with the weasel, and everyone was puzzled. They said, "How could such a honest child like Pickles turn bad after gaining some skills, hanging out with the chicken-stealing little thug like the weasel every day?"

Pickles practiced for a long time. His farts became louder and more powerful, but they never smelled. The weasel's farts could knock out mice and frogs, but Pickles' farts sounded like cannon blasts, creating holes in the ground, yet they never smelled. This made Pickles feel helpless, and he admired the weasel greatly. Whatever the weasel told him to do, he did. Once when the weasel went to steal chickens and needed help, he deceived Pickles into going along to scatter rice and lure the hens out. Not only did they fail to steal any chickens, but they also lost a whole bag of rice. Everyone then knew that Pickles had participated in stealing chickens.

When the news reached Mapo Bing, she scolded Pickles and forbade him from associating with the weasel. Pickles stayed home for three days, reflecting on himself, and realized that he had gone off course. Hedgehogs have spines, and weasels have farts - these are innate abilities passed down through generations, honed over hundreds of years. If he tried to force himself to learn them without thinking, it would take years just to learn how to produce smelly farts. How would he learn other skills and become a superman at this rate? By the time he finished learning, roses would already be married. No, things couldn't continue like this. Pickles thought about it carefully and realized that what he wanted was the ability to create a smell that could knock people out, not necessarily the fart itself. This way, he wouldn't need to strain his face red every day trying to hold in farts; he could focus on producing the smell.

Pickles locked himself in his room, neither eating nor bathing, desperately trying to figure out how to produce a foul smell. After being locked up for several days without coming up with a good method, when he finally stepped outside, he found that everyone who saw him covered their noses. It turned out that because he hadn't bathed for so long, his body had started to become sticky and smelly. Seeing others react this way, Pickles was overjoyed, thinking, "There really is a god watching over me. I wanted a foul smell, and here it is."

Half a month later, Pickles finally devised a method to make himself incredibly stinky. His smell was so potent that it could not only knock people unconscious but also make them vomit uncontrollably, even more powerful than the weasel's stinky farts. Even the weasel gave him a thumbs-up, saying, "Pickles, you've graduated."

Since acquiring this skill, Pickles has helped the villagers countless times. That day when the urban management officers came to dismantle Magpie's nest, claiming it was an illegal construction affecting the city's appearance, Magpie was at a loss and called Pickles for help. As soon as Pickles stood there, he knocked out several urban management officers on the spot. When the police arrived, they couldn't do anything either because hygiene is a personal choice, which the police cannot interfere with. After this incident, the urban management officers no longer dared to bother Mapo Bing's stall at the market. Seeing how useful this skill was, Mapo Bing suggested that Pickles use it to make money. Pickles replied that helping the villagers was fine, but making money was out of the question because he still needed to learn other skills. He was eager to become a superman and meet Rose. Making money? Too vulgar.
