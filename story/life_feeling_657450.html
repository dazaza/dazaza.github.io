The departure in late autumn has disordered the blooming of flowers for a whole season.

by zhouzhou56 on 2012-01-08 22:42:17

Parting in late autumn, the flowers bloomed in disorder for a season.

The dying sun is like blood, dancing lightly and gracefully, charming smiles. But the fallen flowers do not understand, exhausting sorrow.

-- Written at the beginning

Swinging an arc of lonely shadow, fading into silence, the coolness of autumn fades.

An embroidered umbrella walks through Jiangnan, stepping on and shattering thousands of years of beautiful dreams. The sound of hooves echoes, who can wait for the anticipation, or has time passed by, beauty turns old with a snap of fingers.

Parting at the twilight of late autumn, withered leaves fall, taking away the remaining few traces of fatigue and loneliness from the branches. The bitter wind disrupts the predetermined trajectory, blurred tearful eyes obscure the entire world,

She still stubbornly believes that leaving is not the tree's abandonment.

Butterflies break for flowers

Flowers fly with the wind

The dance of the seasons conceals an irresistible force, it is the butterfly-like falling leaves, elegantly and decisively bidding farewell.

Tightly holding in her hands, are their initial feelings, once those fiery vows, steadfast promises, once those forever unbreakable agreements, now, do they still count?

Have their beautiful story been rewritten by the ruthless time? Have their unforgettable memories been sealed by long years?

Farewell smoke, frozen into a permanent painting. Why does the heart still ache faintly?

She leans on the railing and looks out, not knowing when the bronze mirror has been neglected. Covered with a faint sadness, a cold light shines through, just like her deeply affectionate gaze.

A woman adorns herself for the one who appreciates her. She has no interest in light or heavy makeup, only wishing to dwell in plainness. Playing music for the one who understands her, but the soulmate has gone, playing the lute with heart affairs, who listens when the strings break? Even if the yellow flowers spread fragrance all over the courtyard, what intoxicates her not is her calm heart.

The wind continues to blow, messing up the pine shadows, red leaves fall, streams break...

Several times we made appointments in late spring, colorful notes and letters. Several times we met in dreams, tears sprinkled on the remaining red. How many times have I looked out the window, distant mountains like eyebrows, clouds and mist vast and indistinct. How many times have I watched the snowfall, asking myself, when will be the return date?

Peach trees accompanied by drizzle, until dusk, drop by drop. Who is drunkenly dancing in the gaze of the crimson tear-soaked gown? And who is telling sorrow in the midst of wind, flowers, snow and moon?

Vows remain, but love has become empty.

The solitary sail fades into the distance, the music ends, people disperse, leaving only the sad winds and rain. Reunions and partings, joys and sorrows, cry and damage the remaining years, leaving only a dream of prosperity.

The woman with flowing sleeves drinks cup after cup of parting sorrow.

If youth really flows like water, why can't the vicissitudes of time be washed away?

If life is really like a clear dream, why can't we wake up from the dust of the sunset of old times?

Past events cannot be pursued

One yearning turns to ashes

In the next life, turn into lotus pickers

Meet you by the pond water...
