Subscription Guide for Lianfa Co., Ltd. (002394)

Basic Information of This Public Offering:

(1) Stock Category: The shares issued this time are RMB ordinary shares (A-shares) listed overseas, with a par value of RMB 1.00 per share.

(2) Issuance Quantity: The total number of shares issued this time is 27 million shares, including 5.4 million shares issued offline and 21.6 million shares issued online.

(3) Issue Price: The issue price this time is RMB 45.00 per share, corresponding to the following P/E ratios:

1. 23.62 times (earnings per share calculated by dividing the lower of net profit after removing non-recurring gains and losses audited as of the end of 2009 by the total number of shares after issuance);

2. 31.47 times (earnings per share calculated by dividing the lower of net profit after removing non-recurring gains and losses audited as of the end of 2009 by the total number of shares before issuance).

Rules for Subscription Quantity and Frequency:

(1) For original shareholders subscribing online, the maximum subscription quantity per securities account shall not exceed 500 shares, and any quantity exceeding 500 shares must be an integer multiple of 500 shares. The upper limit of subscription quantity is 20,000 shares. Any subscription exceeding the upper limit will be automatically canceled by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system.

(2) Investors participating in the public offering of stocks can only use one securities account. Each investor can only subscribe once for each stock issue. Once confirmed by the Shenzhen Stock Exchange trading system, the subscription cannot be canceled.

(3) Investors must comply with relevant laws and regulations and assume corresponding legal responsibilities.
