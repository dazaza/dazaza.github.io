F.I.R Band Profile

Name: F.I.R Band

Birthday:

Gender:

F.I.R was formed in May 2002, consisting of one female and two male members. It includes the creative producer Ian Chen who gathered two outstanding new-generation singer-songwriters, Faye and Real Ah-Chin. After more than a year of creation, they jointly completed entirely new creations that reflect the unique style of F.I.R. The music covers various styles such as Folk Rock, Rock, Jazz, Reggae, etc., and hopes to explore this generation's views on love, social phenomena, religion, psychology, philosophy through lyrics rich in cultural significance. They aim to return to the cultural heritage of the new wave of music and hope to reignite the rock spirit of "music with a purpose."

●Music Experience

In junior high school, I was seen as a somewhat solitary little girl, but deep down, I was immersed in the world of music. I fell in love with the feeling of listening to music; I also fell in love with singing. While using my voice to express myself, my inner dreams and desires were released through my voice. I still remember my first time on stage, I closed my eyes and sang wholeheartedly, as naturally as if I were at home. There was no tension, only a strange sense of security. That day, I knew that this stage would one day belong to me...

In high school, because I liked singing, I joined the choir, from which I began to delve deeper into various singing techniques and music theory. Also influenced by Western music, I developed a strong interest in foreign languages. In university, I chose to attend Fu Jen Catholic University's English Department, where I joined music-related clubs and formed several bands. Later, after participating in many inter-school music competitions, I gradually gained recognition, almost always being the champion.

After catching the attention of musicians, I came into contact with the music industry. I kept in touch with one of the music producers, Mr. Ian Chen. By chance, I met Mr. Chen again when he was preparing to form a band. After a simple audition, I joined the F.I.R train, starting my arduous journey in music.

Competition experience: First place in individual category, duet category, and composition category in Fu Jen Catholic University's Youth Rhythm Award singing competition; First place in Fu Jen Catholic University's Advertisement Tomorrow Star.

●Music Experience

*Germination: Falling in love with music was an unexpected event. During junior high school, I originally had a great interest in Japanese cartoons and comics, and at the time, I aspired to become a professional cartoonist and illustrator (I once won the first place in national Western painting... independently published two short comic books and illustrations)... As I loved drawing, I gradually started liking Japanese cartoon soundtracks and Japanese pop music... Starting from the well-known music groups at the time such as T.R.F, Globe, Mr.Children, Glay, X-Jp, etc.'s cartoon TV soundtracks, the seeds of a deep love for music were planted in my heart. Although not entirely a fan of Japanese culture, Japanese-style pop culture had already begun to sprout in my mind, deeply influencing my later music creation...

*Left hand playing notes, right hand holding a brush: Entering high school with curiosity about music, I joined the guitar club. Although both are forms of artistic creation, auditory music seemed more impactful than visual art, so I gradually put down the paintbrush and resolutely declined the position of president of the Western painting club. Although it made the instructor teacher disappointed and felt guilty, I do not regret immersing myself in the world of music (started performing folk songs under the immense pressure of exams in the second year of high school, and this singing lasted for more than five years...) The stage experience accumulated during the three years of high school had a significant impact on my later music creation. Although I hadn't started creating heavily yet, many beautiful melodies were already deeply imprinted in my mind.

*Creation period & the magic of Tetsuya Komuro: During the three years at Fu Jen Catholic University, I was an academic underachiever but absolutely a prodigy in music... Accumulated nearly 100 creations within a year, although not necessarily mature, it shocked myself greatly ^^ Formed a music studio with friends, started semi-formally connecting with the music industry... Except for attending classes, eating, and sleeping, almost all my time was spent on things related to music... During this period, I also studied keyboard instruments and various arrangements in depth, and early creations had many influences from Tetsuya Komuro. Remember, at that time, the entire Japanese pop music scene was monopolized by Tetsuya Komuro...(P.S. Rather than saying my music was influenced by Komuro, it might be better to say I was moved by his spirit).

Friends around me have advised me more than once to give up on the path of music... But I told them
