"Freezing Girl" Spotted at Yichun Ski Resort: Daring to Challenge the Harsh Winter in a Thin纱Short Skirt

by mlwang on 2011-12-21 14:28:35

On December 17, 2011, at the Meihua Mountain Ski Resort in Yichun, Heilongjiang, a girl dressed in a red chiffon dress took photos on the ski field in weather that was minus twenty degrees Celsius, attracting many tourists. On that day, the girl was only wearing a low-cut chiffon dress and played with snow while taking photos; she also wore pink stockings, forming a striking contrast to the fully bundled-up crowd on the scene.

According to forecasts from the Central Meteorological Observatory, the lowest temperature in Yichun, Heilongjiang, that day was minus 27 degrees Celsius. This girl, who demonstrated great self-sacrifice, was called "Yichun Freezing Sister" by netizens. First, she appeared in a strapless top, unafraid of the cold in her thin clothing as she played shooting games in the snow, her bold gunplay driving away the winter chill. Perhaps it was too exhilarating during the hunting~ or maybe because she hadn't had enough fun at the Meihua Mountain Hunting Ground, "Freezing Sister" then headed to the ski resort to continue her "freezing" performance, and her outfit became even sexier.

Many netizens expressed that this was just another forgettable and boring hype. It is reported that her identity has not yet been revealed, but it is known that she is from Beijing, and this trip to Yichun was part of a warm-up. It is rumored that after adapting to the temperature, she plans to take large-scale snowy landscape art photos. Just hearing this makes many people admire her dedication.
