Seize Olympic business opportunities to accelerate layout

by tfbgfnbv on 2009-11-25 09:07:18

Previously, we mentioned that the impending Olympics would inevitably drive the rapid development of the digital TV and 3G mobile phone markets. Tang Jianxing, General Manager of Guanghong Group, believes that in the near future, the application of SMT equipment for 3G should be relatively noteworthy. "In 2006, our company began to focus on the mobile phone product series, purchasing a large number of expensive comprehensive testers. We must clearly see that first, there is a strong growth momentum in China's self-branded mobile phones; secondly, with the Olympics approaching, it is driving the 3G mobile phone market. The beginning of the 3G era in China has opened a large-scale communication revolution. Now, at peak times, we have 18 SMT devices producing CDMA and GSM mobile phones, kiosks, with a peak monthly output of up to 1.3 million mobile phone motherboards. This production capacity is equivalent to that of a large domestic enterprise with a mobile phone license," Tang Jianxing stated.

Heinz Dommel, General Manager of Asia at Universal Instruments, although believing that the SMT market will not see significant growth in 2008, remains optimistic about maintaining growth and increasing market share. In electronic consumer goods, it is estimated that the demand for mobile phones, routers, and flash memory will continue to rise, and the introduction of 3G should further boost the demand for telecommunication products. Our laboratory will continue to research the technology of integrating semiconductor and surface-mount production. Additionally, we are exploring new technical fields in SMT equipment, namely system-in-package, where the market demand for SiP technology is gradually increasing.
