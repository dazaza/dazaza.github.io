As long as you've done your best.

by ruydyir02 on 2012-02-09 16:23:26

1: Family

Blood is thicker than water. The bond of family ties is always the deepest and strongest link in the world. How many people are willing to do all kinds of illegal and rebellious things just to keep their loved ones by their side. And how many people only regret damaging their closest relatives after making mistakes in a moment of excitement. We should not be those kind of people, because we deeply understand the value and priceless nature of family affection. A complete family enjoying the joy of family life versus a broken family, the happiness or unhappiness does not need to be explained. The kindness of our parents' upbringing, the brotherly affection between siblings, we receive and then give back. That is the beginning of happiness.

2: Love

Butterflies come to this world to find the flowers that belong to them, and people come to this world to find the other half that belongs to them/her. How many people disregard everything for love, how many people spend their entire lives pursuing true love. We do not support殉qing (those who die for love), but we understand, because they deeply understand the essence of love. Life is indeed precious, but love is even more valuable, they regard it as the truth of love. People who possess true love are happy, more people spend their lives with marriage. Once experienced true love, one's life is not in vain.

3: Friends

Friends are the third most important thing for a person, after family and lover. They have no family ties with us, originally complete strangers, yet they enter our lives under various circumstances, playing indispensable important roles. Whether you are enthusiastic about widely making friends, or satisfied with a few close friends; whether you are the type who would do anything for a friend, or the type who maintains a light friendship like water... It is undeniable that people with friends are happier than those who are alone. And friendships that last a lifetime are even more precious.

4: Rivals

Who says that being at the top makes one lonely? Loneliness of heroes, loneliness of geniuses, are all due to the lack of rivals. Even for ordinary people like us, having a rival with similar abilities and opposing attitudes in learning, work, or life often has significant meaning. In exams, races... such examples are countless. The existence of a rival is a motivation, pushing us to strive to improve ourselves and continue climbing towards higher peaks. Because they stand at the same height, they are also people who can understand us from the same standpoint. Being both an enemy and a friend is the highest realm of a rival. Discarding the prejudice and jealousy of "If only Zhou Yu had never been born," you will find that having a rival is also a very happy thing.

5: Ideals

Ideals are the wind direction indicators of life's sails. Without goals, a person is like a ship sailing on a dark sea at night, pitch black without finding the lighthouse's glow. Some say our ideals are inversely proportional to our age, the grand ambitions of childhood shrink into specific goals during adolescence, and further shrink into the obedient desires of adulthood. Realistic, yet also restrictive. Of course, there are also numerous examples of those who maintain high aspirations and broad horizons from start to finish. But it must be understood that there is no decisive difference between these two. As long as one strives and succeeds, then one can be content. However, for those without ideals, the gap is huge, because they lack the motivation to push themselves forward. Even if they still live day by day without much impact, they will eventually encounter a day of confusion. Therefore, if you want to pave the way for happiness, start by establishing your ideals.

6: Memories

If ideals look forward, then memories look back. While we gaze into the future, we cannot forget the past. If the memories are sweet and warm, of course we should cherish them and savor them carefully; if they are sour and painful, we should not deliberately try to forget them. People only grow after experiencing certain things, and these memories are the spiritual wealth accumulated bit by bit. When time passes and things change, on a certain year, month, or day, when we revisit those past experiences, whether bitter, sour, sweet, or sad, they have all lost their emotional colors under the rinse of time, settling into calmness and沧桑(cynicism). Even though the sky may not bear the traces of my flight, I have flown. That is enough.

7: Change

Have you ever imagined one day, someone suddenly intrudes into your life, bringing earth-shattering changes, thus stepping onto a completely new path, leading a life entirely different? This fantasy embodies the hope for change and difference. In fact, whoever it may be, even deep down inside, everyone hopes for change, seeks novelty, and calls for challenges. No one would deny that repetitive life cycles, full of laughter and tears, are so boring, monotonous, and dull. No one truly enjoys doing the same things forever, even if they don't have the power to initiate change, at least they should try to create opportunities. A life rich in changes is what we pursue, isn't it?

8: Hardships

The goddess of fate is fair, she gives laughter but also brings tears. Everyone's life will inevitably encounter ups and downs, big or small, among which those that could be called thunderbolts from a clear sky are the greatest hardships. They might change a person's life forever, even their entire life. Once the nightmare becomes reality, we can cry, but we cannot lose hope. We allow temporary sadness, but we cannot let it linger indefinitely. Only by facing it squarely, actively and bravely confronting it, and firmly striving to overcome it can we succeed. Although easier said than done, we must still hold onto this belief. Hardship itself is not happiness, but happiness can be found within hardship. As long as we are strong enough and full of hope.

9: Quarrels

Of course, here the quarrels refer to situations akin to playful teasing within the bounds of love. Once they exceed these bounds and turn into confrontations, it is absolutely a tragic situation. From passionate lovers to married couples, many people enjoy belittling each other and engaging in verbal sparring, and often take pleasure in it, belonging to the type where the more they argue, the better their relationship gets. There is a saying: "Beating is a sign of intimacy, scolding is a sign of love, and loving to the extreme means kicking." Always harmoniously playing music together and respecting each other like guests is a fairy tale, not reality. So, what more should we say? Let's wish them, no, all lovers, to continue being happy.

10: Labor

Labor is joyful, labor is glorious... imbued with such educational thoughts, perhaps we once had disdain and resistance, but after truly living independently, we realize that labor is destined to accompany us for life. Whether consuming physical or mental strength, those who do not labor shall not eat, those who do not labor cannot survive in society. If one cannot survive, how can one talk about happiness? Labor represents sustenance, and material well-being is the basic condition for pursuing a higher spiritual realm. Since this is the case, why not change our mindset, reduce complaints, increase enthusiasm, and actively engage in life, enjoying the process of labor. Those who waste their years without accomplishment cannot be happy.

11: Leisure

We've just mentioned that life requires hustle and bustle. Yes, but excessive busyness can lead to another extreme. Looking up and walking too fast can make us miss many beautiful sights along the way. At this point, we need to temporarily stop and admire. Comfort is not only a necessary relaxation and adjustment in today's fast-paced, high-pressure era, but also a philosophy of life characterized by optimism, composure, and a sense of detachment, akin to Zhuangzi's "Free and Untrammeled Excursion." There will always be a way when the car reaches the mountain, and the boat will naturally straighten when it reaches the bridge. There is no need to rush, no need to be restless. Calm down, give yourself some space, rest, and think. In fact, the scenery is always so beautiful.

12: Moving

In today's world dominated by materialism and utilitarianism, too many people are deeply trapped, blinded by the dust of greed, and deafened by the noise of the mundane world. At this moment, please ask yourself sincerely, how long has it been since the last time you were moved? While being worn down by society and life, losing our sharp edges, have we also become insensitive and numb, stripping away even the instinct to be moved from the bottom of our hearts? Those who do not understand being moved, those who do not shed tears, are pitiful. Perhaps sometimes we need to deliberately seek out something to stimulate ourselves, to prove that we are not numb. A touching article, a moving movie, an event that stirs the heart... whether in the fictional world or real life, there are so many things around us that touch the heart. Use your heart to feel and explore, they will piece together many happy moments.

13: Innocence

It is often said that only children are the purest, and that people inevitably become worldly as they grow up. The lyrics go: "Who can really remain unstained when stepping beyond the boundaries of humanity?" We will not arrogantly claim that we are detached from the world and do not care, for those who are indifferent to fame and fortune do exist, but they are extremely rare. But that does not mean we cannot, even occasionally, maintain a pure heart and uphold a moral bottom line against the edge of the world. That does not mean we choose indifference due to concern for trouble, nor avoid believing out of fear of deviation. Believe in sincerity, trust in human hearts. There are too many helplessnesses and involuntariness in the world, but as long as we adhere to our true selves, learn to believe, happiness will eventually come.

Editor's Note: Happiness is used to feel, not to compare. Life is used to manage, not to calculate. Emotions are used to maintain, not to test. Loved ones are used to care for, not to hurt. Money is used to spend, not to measure. Rumors are used to break, not to cover up. Trust is used to accumulate, not to challenge.
