Thai Drama: *Angel's War*

Also known as: *Air stewardess rivalry*, *The story of air stewardesses*

It is reported that this series was adapted from articles written by a former flight attendant of Thai Airways on her personal blog. The original author wrote about her emotional experiences with pilots and various workplace encounters during her time as a flight attendant, turning these stories into a novel that was published online and immediately gained enthusiastic attention and popularity from netizens. After a Thai entertainment company acquired the TV adaptation rights, they had their popular young actors and actresses bring the story to life, sparking a viewing craze in Thailand. When aired on Thailand's CH5, the show achieved an average viewership of 1.2 billion for the entire series.
