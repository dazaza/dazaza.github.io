Yangpu police in Shanghai introduced on the 23rd that after this incident happened, the police immediately formed a special investigation team to conduct an investigation, retrieved surveillance footage from roads surrounding the incident location, and looked for eyewitnesses near the incident location and at various stops of the RT-Mart shuttle bus. They also conducted interviews and relevant technical appraisals.

The involved driver posted a Weibo update which drew attention. If the driver cannot find any eyewitness evidence to prove and restore the scene, he will bear the responsibility for traffic manslaughter. After this matter spread over the internet, it immediately received widespread attention, with netizens dubbing it "Support Gate."

Driver's action violated regulations

On October 18th, a post titled "I hope enthusiastic citizens can help me. I don't ask others to call me a hero, I just want to clear my name" gained widespread attention on Weibo. The content roughly stated that a free shuttle bus driver for RT-Mart Supermarket's Huangxing Road store in Yangpu District, Shanghai, discovered a passenger fall down after getting off the bus while driving on October 16th. He then got out of the car to help and sent the injured person to the hospital for treatment. However, the victim died despite medical efforts, leading the driver to be suspected of causing a traffic accident resulting in the passenger falling.

Based on Article (1) of the first paragraph of Article 62 of the Implementation Regulations of the Road Traffic Safety Law, which stipulates that motor vehicle drivers must not "drive when doors or compartments are not properly closed," the traffic police determined that driver Yin's act of driving without closing the door had a direct relationship with the passenger's death. As such, Yin was held fully responsible for the accident.

Driver started the vehicle with the door open

The police confirmed that at the time of the incident, driver Yin started the vehicle without closing the door, causing passenger Zhao to fall and get injured. Despite medical efforts, Zhao passed away. There was a direct link between Yin's actions and the passenger's death, thus holding him fully accountable.

After extensive investigations, the police ascertained that on October 16th, a traffic accident occurred on a road near No. 510 San Dao Road in Shanghai, where a passenger disembarking from the RT-Mart Supermarket Yangpu store shuttle bus fell and got injured. The next day, the victim died despite medical efforts. At the time of the incident, RT-Mart shuttle bus driver Yin started the vehicle without closing the door, causing passenger Zhao to fall and sustain injuries. Despite medical efforts, Zhao passed away.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 23rd, the Shanghai police informed that the event of the "Shanghai RT-Mart driver caught in 'Support Gate' seeking eyewitnesses to prove innocence" that occurred in mid-October has now been clarified.
