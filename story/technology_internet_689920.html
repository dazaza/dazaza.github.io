In the securities industry, CITIC Construction Securities is very well-known. Not only does it have good business performance, but it also highly values improving employee benefits. CITIC Construction believes that by ensuring employees are satisfied both economically and in their work environment, they will feel the superiority and pride of being part of CITIC, thus cherishing their jobs and viewing service to the company as a lifelong career.

&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；&；nbsp；

Indeed, CITIC Construction has acted on this belief. As early as January 2011, CITIC Construction signed a bulk purchase agreement with Daboli Ergonomic Home for 55 units of the Luxurious+E leather version ergonomic computer chairs. Daboli's excellent service earned the recognition of CITIC Construction's leadership, and the robust quality and comfortable design of the Luxurious+E won widespread praise from employees. Consequently, in January 2012, CITIC Construction once again signed an agreement with Daboli for 50 units of the Golden Respect+E-HBL-M, which includes custom-made leather seat cushions, mesh backrests with headrests ergonomic computer chairs.
