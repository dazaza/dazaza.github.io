But the stronger the feelings, the more concerned about the other person, the stronger the tendency to seek common ground until it reaches its peak, the highest point, approaching complete agreement. Knowing that the other person has strong feelings for them, they become unreasonable, critical, even somewhat authoritarian. Therefore, after having a boyfriend or girlfriend, please keep a certain distance from members of the opposite sex. Cherish the person by your side who restrains you and gets jealous. Don't think they are unreasonable because if they were reasonable, they would just be ordinary friends. They value you and everything between you, which makes them emotionally sensitive and intense. If you're currently upset because your partner is being unreasonable, please pick up the phone and call them. There's no room for a grain of sand in a lover's heart, no matter how small it is. A person who truly loves you cannot tolerate you chatting alone with or going out alone with a member of the opposite sex. Why should you cherish your partner? Because they love you very much, willingly doing many things for you that ordinary friends wouldn't do. In summary, the fundamental reason for fighting is the mixture of true love and concern!
