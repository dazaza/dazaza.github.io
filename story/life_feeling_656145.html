Helpless thoughts...

The flowers fall silently but people have feelings, sitting alone in the twilight of loneliness.

These days, I've always been swayed by my thoughts, shuttling back and forth between the worlds of Xuanhua (prosperity) and silence. Many times, I feel lost and even become somewhat distant from myself. I've been like this for a long time already. The days of being melancholic seem to have become commonplace; without ripples in my heart, it feels as if something is missing. I try to think about problems unnecessarily, and my heart cannot find peace for a long time.

I sought a friend to tune the strings, but unexpectedly, my friend said that this might be the last time they would help me tune them. Upon asking, I learned that my friend was about to stop studying. I felt suddenly surprised, yet such unexpected events occur most frequently in my life. This time, he tuned very seriously.

I am somewhat reluctant to let go of my friend.

I can't forget the days we spent together, with tears that carried smiles, and wounds that were hard to erase. I always remember those years when we hustled for life, when we had minor quarrels over certain matters, and when we became indifferent to each other's interests. Now, when we meet again, we pass by with smiles. I really don't know why I'm so focused on joy and sorrow, reunions and partings. To me, if there were no complicated thoughts, it would be the strangest thing.

I always think, if only I could hold onto all of life, how wonderful that would be. In that case, there wouldn't be any joy and sorrow, reunions and partings, nor would there be sorrowful backs. Such thoughts are truly laughable. Imagine if there were no joy and sorrow, reunions and partings, then there wouldn't be the final moment of moving emotions; without backs, there wouldn't be that fleeting moment of remembrance. Fate always jokes with us, letting dawn fade away too early. Wanting to own the warmth of early spring, in the end, turns out to be the changing of the four seasons. Thus, I am truly helpless.

It started raining again at night, with lightning and thunder outside.

Many days have passed during rainy weather. I don't particularly love rainy days, just as now, unable to sleep, wanting to stay quietly through the night, but the thunder makes me feel helpless. The atmosphere on rainy days is somewhat dead silent, and rainy nights are even more so. I strive to recall everything from the past, hoping to gain some solace. Unexpectedly, my heart becomes a mess, leaving me feeling utterly helpless. Fortunately, there are still some memories, lying quietly in my heart, even though it's pouring heavily outside. That love once was sincere and pure, but when I looked back, I realized I hadn't properly grasped it. Isn't love extraordinary? Yet, I missed it, which isn't a heart-wrenching pain for me?

What exactly has made me understand life? Love and hate? Joy and sorrow?

It's them - the people who have left, the things that have vanished. It's the rainy days, the cold nights, the lonely hearts. Have I grown up again? These changes are more like a trade-off - gaining the fruits that belong to me, but losing the once beautiful flowers. I dare not balance them once, fearing that the gains and losses will be too much for me to accept, leaving me unsure where to go in the coming hurried years. The rain continues to pour heavily. I feel that these raindrops are like the thoughts in my heart, cut off from their thread, making them hard to collect. I try hard to understand the meaning of rainy days, leaving behind questions that I cannot foresee. I begin to fear rainy days more than sleepless nights. Helplessly, I must watch the rain continue, listen to the incessant thunder, and sort out these inherently helpless and messy thoughts. However, it seems that they cannot be sorted out clearly.

In the end, perhaps they remain unclear.
