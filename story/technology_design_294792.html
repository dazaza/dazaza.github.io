Introduction to "Sword Spirit: A World Where the Strong Are Respected"

In a world where the strong are respected, the cultivation realm is bloody.

Peng Yuxuan's family has been oppressed for a hundred years, but he knows nothing about it.

In Peng Yuxuan's world, there are celestial immortals, yet he is unaware.

---

Peng Yuxuan falls off a cliff and rescues a trapped sword-cultivating hermit.

From then on, he changes.

He comes to know a whole new world; the cultivation realm exists. It’s a world where the strong are respected.

Peng Yuxuan embarks on the path of vengeance...

He steps into the threshold of the cultivation realm........

How will Peng Yuxuan carve out his own bloody path...
