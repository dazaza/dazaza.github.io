Three Key Points Copywriters for Medical Websites Should Know

by qiyeseo5774 on 2012-02-29 19:30:48

I believe that many copywriters in charge of medical websites have experience in planning special features. Moreover, with the development of medical websites, an attractive special feature is very beneficial to a website. For medical websites, special content planning plays an important role. Below, I will exchange ideas with everyone on three points that medical website copywriters should understand:

First, the theme of the medical website special feature.

Online news specials are suitable for presenting various major news topics. For medical websites, they may create a special feature around some major events or high-search-volume keywords. Below, I will introduce them according to different topics.

1. Major sudden events.

You can use sudden events and media to provide the public with relatively extensive and comprehensive information. Generally, such themes are more eye-catching and meet the needs of the majority of people. Moreover, you can choose a certain news event, which allows you to concentrate limited resources on one angle and dig deep, making it easy to form a characteristic. However, if the angle is not chosen appropriately, it may backfire. In addition, most audiences still need a comprehensive understanding of the event, and a too narrow special angle may affect their reading interest. For example, when I did this depression special, I highlighted the harm of depression through several recent depression-related news stories and guided towards treatment.

2. Use high-search-volume keywords for a special feature.

People who have worked on bidding for medical websites know that high-search-volume keywords have a very high conversion rate for websites and can bring great marketing benefits to hospitals. Therefore, how do you increase the conversion rate of traffic brought by a high-search-volume keyword? This requires using high-search-volume keywords to create an attractive special feature.

Let me give an example using a special feature I created:

The theme of this special feature is [the best hospital for treating insomnia]. The reason for choosing [the best hospital for treating insomnia] is that this keyword has a large search volume, and most patients searching like this have the intention to visit the hospital. Therefore, such a theme is more eye-catching.

Second, the column planning of the medical website special feature.

Regarding the column planning of online news specials, it needs to be based on your special theme. Taking the previous example, [the best hospital for treating cervical erosion], then your subsequent columns must revolve around this theme. In fact, the coherence of a special column is more important for the special. When planning columns for network news specials, the following aspects need attention:

1. Many news specials cover [old talk] topics. If you don't want your special to be the same as others, you must have your own innovation, so you must put effort into the column page.

2. Special advertising. It must attract attention; if your advertisement lacks appeal, it will directly lead to customers closing and leaving the website.

3. You need to know who your customers are and what they need. Many medical websites find that doing a special yields no meaning or benefit. For example, if you want to promote whitening products, what you need to highlight most is the whitening effect, which will attract the attention of a wide range of women.

4. Coherence between columns. Each column must be connected and cannot lack continuity, otherwise, it will make customers close halfway through. It is recommended that before creating a special, you should have a reasonable plan, allowing your special to link step-by-step, preventing customers from leaving midway.

Third, the design of the medical website special feature.

1. Website color. Choose appropriate colors based on your theme. For example, if you are doing a depression special, you definitely cannot use bright red but should use warm tones.

2. Website layout. The layout must be clear, letting users know what your special is about. Do not let users finish viewing without knowing its purpose.

3. Effectively insert marketing information. If you insert too much marketing information, it will definitely cause user annoyance. However, effectively inserting marketing information will make users feel comfortable, leading to inquiries.

4. Content needs to be rich. The article links inside the special must be rich and closely related to the theme of the special. This not only enriches the content but also makes users increasingly interested. If your articles are boring, users will not read them carefully, reducing the conversion rate.
