Chenghua Food Co., Ltd. of Jianli County

by juick on 2008-10-31 23:37:21

Chenghua Food Co., Ltd. is a time-honored enterprise specializing in the acquisition and processing of poultry eggs, located in Jianli, a provincially-administered economic development zone, which was the hometown of Wu Zixu, a famous prime minister during the Warring States period. It is situated on the north bank of the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, in the heart of the Jianghan Plain, to the east of Hong Lake and to the south of the Yangtze River. It is a national base for commercial grain, commercial fish, high-quality oil, cotton, and fast-growing forests, as well as a major county for livestock and poultry egg products. The factory site covers nearly 10,000 square meters with a construction area of 6,000 square meters. There are currently 60 employees, including 10 professional technical personnel. The company processes 30 million eggs annually, with an annual output value of 18 million yuan. The main products include: bulk packaging in cartons, gift box packed red yolk salted duck eggs, wrapped salted duck eggs, double yolk salted duck eggs, lead-free preserved eggs, and local specialty free-range chicken eggs.

Building upon the traditional egg processing techniques inherited from the original Jianli County Food Company, combined with modern egg processing technologies, Chenghua has developed a unique egg processing process. All processed eggs use fresh eggs from ducks naturally raised in lakes and rice paddies as raw materials. Therefore, the red yolk salted duck eggs have a protein as white as jade, a yolk as red as cinnabar, with oil oozing out, and a non-salty, fragrant taste. The lead-free preserved eggs are crystal clear, elastic, with pine flower patterns like brocade, resembling colorful glass, offering a unique taste that lingers. These products are excellent choices for family banquets, travel, and gifting to relatives and friends. The products are popular nationwide, and the company has established purchasing and sales partnerships with multiple provincial leading enterprises.

The company was awarded the five-star civilized unit of Jianli County in 1997, the advanced unit of the county in 2002, and the same year it was honored as a "reliable quality unit" by the County Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau. In 2003, it was awarded the title of "contract-abiding and credit-worthy enterprise" by the Jingzhou Municipal People's Government.

Adhering to the principle of continuously enhancing the brand effect of its products, the company will continue to provide top-notch products and maintain good credibility, sincerely welcoming new and old customers to visit and offer guidance.
