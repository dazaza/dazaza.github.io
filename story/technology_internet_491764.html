There are some goods missed.

by wenyyslove on 2011-09-19 21:33:29

Some things, some people, once you turn around, it's a lifetime! A great sentence from the Four Great Classical Novels! ?

A promise is a blank sheet of paper; even the thickest script has its end. We should know the taste of tears, and even if we pay every minute and second, there is no escape. In this world, there is no lasting happiness, only fleeting comfort and ease.

A heart belongs to one person. There’s nothing fair in the red dust (worldly life)! The deeper the love, the deeper the hurt. Loving someone you shouldn’t, leads to endless sighs; loving someone who doesn’t love you back, begins the flood of tears.

That person, will you still remember me?

If emotions were a game, neither side could afford to play or lose. Sweet words – who knows the pain behind the smiles? Love begins with a smile, grows with a kiss, and ends with a tear. After being hurt, scars form, leaving traces behind. Forcing each other to forget, but knowing that sometimes silence is the best comfort, and perhaps memories are the best conclusion. Fools are all the same; none can escape sorrow because dreams linger in our hearts, so we willingly wander.

Some connections are destined to be lost, some are fated not to have good outcomes. Loving someone doesn’t mean you have to possess them, but possessing someone means you must love them well and not easily give up, otherwise, you betray yourself.

Many events are destined to have an outcome. Enjoy the beautiful process while it lasts. When passing by each other, we should learn to let go. Laugh loudly once, cry bravely another time, look up and see a bright sun. Believe that time can change everything! Don't easily make yourself shed tears. Spring has its charm, summer its liveliness, autumn its harvest, winter its elegance. Life has its own beauty and freedom. You laugh, and those around you laugh with you. You cry, and you're left crying alone. When you cannot have something, the only thing you can do is to try to forget as soon as possible!

Don’t fail to enter spring just because there’s no sunlight, don’t abandon your pursuit because there’s no song, and don’t discard your ideal because there’s no applause. Actually, every path leading to the sunshine is filled with ups and downs. Every road to one's ideal is filled with difficulties and sweat!

Some things, once missed, are missed for a lifetime. People change. Holding onto an unchanging promise might not hold an ever-changing heart. Sometimes persistence is a burden, and letting go is liberation. No one is perfect, and happiness isn’t always 100%. Knowing that you can't have everything at once, you also don't have the right to demand too much, otherwise, you'll suffer yourself and embarrass the other party.
