A long-distance online romance on QQ has moved tens of millions of people.

by lwf225 on 2009-09-29 08:44:44

It was a cold December 5th, and he walked out of the gate of a company with disappointment, his face filled with sorrow. Apparently, his job search had failed again. The weather seemed to be deliberately against him, as snowflakes began to fall heavily, driven by the north wind, hitting his face and feeling cool. He wandered alone like a lost soul, not knowing how much longer he would have to drift.

Back at home, he locked himself in his room and turned on his computer, venting a lot of inner anger in his QQ space. In the following days, he continued to look for a job, but encountered nothing but setbacks and made no progress. That evening, after returning home, he opened his QQ space again wanting to write more about his feelings, but he noticed that a stranger named "Wangyoucao" had left him a message. The stranger told him to lower his expectations, as a recent university graduate should not expect too much from companies, only then could he find work. Unexpectedly, these words resonated with him, so he replied: "Thank you for caring about me and pointing out the problem. Can I add you as a friend and chat with you?" Actually, he didn't have high hopes for adding her as a friend; it was just a polite gesture. But to his surprise, Wangyoucao added him as a friend the next day. He greeted her: "Hello, Wangyoucao." At that moment, she happened to be online but was invisible. After a while, she replied: "Hello." He didn't expect that Wangyoucao was really online, so he started a conversation with her about his job search. They asked each other's age and found they were exactly the same age, further deepening their relationship. That night, they chatted about many topics, from work to life and philosophy. He discovered that no matter what they talked about, they always clicked. She was also surprised at how much they had in common, probably due to their similar age. They had so much in common that they chatted until two o'clock in the morning. He felt that she was great and told her a lot about his situation, but she did not reveal her real identity to him because she knew the internet was virtual and she had never expected to find anything real on the internet.

In the following days, he kept applying for jobs, but this time he put aside the arrogance of "the emperor's daughter doesn't worry about marriage." After several twists and turns, he finally found a job in a dye factory. He was very excited, "It's all thanks to Wangyoucao," he secretly rejoiced. "Looks like I need to thank her; she's really great." A few days later, they met online again. He told her a lot, and he found that she was a nice girl. He thought their meeting was destined, so when the chat was about to end, he sent her a request: "Wangyoucao, I really want to become your real-life friend. Do you agree?" After seeing the request, she smiled, but suddenly looked sad. She didn't know how to answer him, because two netizens had asked her similar questions before. However, after they learned about her unknown story, they both left her. Since then, she no longer believed in so-called online romance, but instead saw the internet as a paradise for emotional scammers. She didn't want to get entangled again. But when the same situation arose again, she couldn't make a decisive decision. She kept wondering if he was just another passerby in her life. After much hesitation, she didn't give a clear answer but ended their chat that night.

After that, they almost chatted every night. As time passed and their understanding of each other deepened, he gradually fell in love with her. However, she was a very rational girl. She repeatedly told herself not to continue, because she began to doubt whether their relationship was what people called "online romance." But every time they chatted, all her concerns were thrown to the winds, leaving only excitement and agitation, because she had quietly fallen in love with him.

On December 29th, it snowed lightly again. He hid in his room, possibly to keep warm or to muster courage. He drank two sips of white wine because he decided to video chat with her today. It took several requests before she agreed. In fact, she wanted to agree the first time, but she was always worried about her physical imperfections. But when he requested the third time, she no longer hesitated and accepted happily. At that moment, her heart was beating so fast, and her cheeks even had a hint of redness. This was their first time seeing each other, and she found him handsome and dashing, just like her ideal prince. He found that the girl in the video was quite ordinary, but he still liked her very much. He noticed that she always looked so melancholy, as if she had lost something important. Indeed, she had lost something significant to her. He asked her, "You look very tired. Why are you unhappy?" "Oh, it's nothing, I've always been like this," she replied. She didn't tell him why, not because she dared not, but because she didn't want him to share her pain. She believed that the highest level of loving someone was trying to make them happy, and she felt she lacked the ability. That night, the usually quiet girl acted unusually abnormal, telling him her address and phone number. That night, she couldn't sleep. She was considering whether she should let him go, as she didn't want to keep "deceiving" him anymore. She wanted him to find his own happiness, and she clearly knew that such happiness was something she couldn't give him. With determination, her trembling hand finally added him to the blacklist, and at that moment, her eyes became moist. A tear rolled down and landed on the keyboard, as well as in her heart.

He disappeared from her contact list like this. He tried several methods but couldn't get in touch with her, and her phone was off. On New Year's Day, he got up very early. He wasn't willing to separate from her like this. He sent her a QQ voice message which read: "Wangyoucao, I don't know why you stopped contacting me. If you're avoiding me, it's useless. I won't give up easily. Once you fall in love with someone, you'll love them forever, until the sea dries up and the rocks crumble. I couldn't participate in your past, but I will closely accompany your future." When she heard this voice through her phone, the tears accumulated in her heart for many years finally burst out uncontrollably. She no longer cared and decided to meet him at the city park at 4 PM.

That afternoon, he dressed up and arrived there very early. He waited and waited, and time seemed to slow down. He kept looking at his watch - 3:30, 3:35... His heart was pounding with excitement. But by 4:10, she still hadn't appeared. "She will definitely come. She must have been delayed... Nothing has happened to her, right?" He muttered to himself. In this unease, he endured another hour. He was utterly disappointed. Winter days are short, and the sky gradually darkened. The park visitors gradually left. He was ready to leave dejectedly when he saw a girl walking towards him with a cane. He stopped, and slowly, under the dim streetlight, he recognized her face. He was stunned. He couldn't believe that the girl with the cane in front of him was the netizen he loved. Without hesitation, he ran forward and tightly embraced her. Their embrace under the light was extraordinarily beautiful.

It turned out that she was his classmate, and four years ago, an accidental car accident during the holiday caused her to lose one leg. Since then, she lost the joy of normal people, and her world collapsed. For four years, until she hugged him that moment, her face finally showed a smile again, like a blooming snow lotus, incredibly beautiful. Finally, they came together, and the characters in the story are my cousin and sister-in-law. I can only wish them a long and happy life here. Thank you, Tencent QQ, for letting them meet.
