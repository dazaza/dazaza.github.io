Happiness in disguise all along

by mimi456 on 2012-01-06 21:30:47

Happiness has always been disguised

I like quiet but hate the dead silence in the deep night.

People are always contradictory, living in the moment but always like to think about something out of reach. In fact, it should be enough to live happily. How many decades are there in life? One blink and one open eye, and ten years have passed. By then, there may not even be the courage to think.

I am a girl with a contradictory personality, but I am also extremely obedient. I think that every gesture of my relatives is an expression of love.

Have you ever heard Chen Rui's "No One Loves You More Than Me"? The lyrics talk about the feelings between lovers, but I prefer to use it for family affection.

I like magnolia trees and often compare myself to magnolia flowers.

Its nobility, purity, fragrance, and pleasantness resemble a delicate girl who seems detached from worldly affairs and can be compared to the goddess Luo Shen emerging from the water.

I prefer to use splendid and magnificent words to embellish more beauty.

On the contrary, I detest many dirty people and things.

I don't like resorting to any means, although some close relatives have said that this society is characterized by bullying the weak and relying on the strong.

I still indulge in the lie that good people will be rewarded with goodness, and continue to weave it carefully.

One day, the goddess of luck will favor me...

It turns out that I didn't know how strong I was,

I once thought that the fortress I built would collapse at a touch,

The vicissitudes of time, the changes in the world

It turns out that I've forgotten how to cry long ago

No more wounds, no more pain

Learned to hide that line of defense in my heart alone

I don't want to make myself so sad, once so proud

Saying "it's nothing" is all a lie, I'm creating a beautiful lie just for myself

Taking care of myself, then even if I lose, it's not so complete, right?

Forgive me again for my disguised happiness, and continue proudly!
