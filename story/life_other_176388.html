Runqi launches a new color effect experience mode - making color selection no longer based on imagination

For a long time, when consumers choose colors, they either look at samples or model rooms. However, the overall effect can only be imagined by consumers, making it difficult to truly experience the final result. Recently, Runhua Paint has launched the "Runhua Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" for the national market. Through this software, consumers can intuitively experience the real effect of colors on a computer screen, so selecting colors no longer requires imagination.

The Homeplayer module mainly showcases the "Living Colors" system, vividly presenting the main recommended colors from Runhua's 456 colors and their wall and wood surface matching schemes in three dimensions.

The Easy Coloring module completely stores Runhua Paint's 456 colors and the common board colors of wood paint. Based on consumers' knowledge of color matching, personal feelings, and preferences, they can freely combine color matching effects until they find their favorite choice. This module also specifically sets up a simulated children's room space for the "Wonderful Home" of Wonder Paint. While selecting colors, consumers can also display the children's room effect that their kids like in a spatial manner, completing the color selection and effect experience for adult rooms and children's rooms in one stop.

The New Home Decoration module allows consumers to accurately create a three-dimensional space according to the actual data of their house type. They can set up a new room according to their own needs from scratch, assigning colors, furniture, decorations, and even lighting, with a strong DIY interest.

In the paint sales market, rich, diverse, and personalized colors are what consumers most want to have. However, many times, they cannot intuitively understand the effect of the colors they choose and whether the effect meets their needs. Now, whether it is an integrated overall color matching or special color effects, any demand for space coloring can obtain matching suggestions through the functional modules of this software and see intuitive three-dimensional effects.

The "Runhua Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" was developed based on Runhua Coatings' 2008 "Living Colors" system. The main functional modules include Homeplayer, Easy Coloring, and New Home Decoration, comprehensively meeting consumers' different levels of color service needs.

Runhua Coatings advocates providing the best coating surface effect to end customers through services. It not only provides more environmentally friendly, healthy, and safe coating products for consumers but also creates satisfactory personalized home color matching effects through services. In 2005, Runhua Coatings' Color Matching Workshop was launched, solving the problem of many consumers wanting to use but not knowing how to use coating colors in the past; In 2008, Runhua Coatings upgraded its color service, focusing on launching the "Harmonious Color Matching" service, truly achieving full color matching for wood and walls, realizing the coordinated matching of home wood and wall colors, pioneering the precedent of simultaneous use of latex paint and wood paint color matching services in China; Now, the launch of the "Runhua Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" has solved the "imagination" obstacle for consumers in choosing color matching, allowing them to freely select their favorite color effects.
