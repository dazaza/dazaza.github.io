A dog laying eggs in its dreams.

by mgvfeifeix on 2011-09-18 18:01:37

Before the ostrich finished speaking, the little monkey led her into a secret room for a long talk. After some haggling, this case was settled privately.

Seeing is believing. In order to let more tiger egg enthusiasts see the once-in-a-century real thing with their own eyes, the little monkey held a tiger egg exhibition at the same time. One hundred animal coins for one ticket. Every day, visitors lined up in long queues.

The tiger egg is even bigger than an ordinary ostrich egg, with obvious tiger skin stripes on the shell. To avoid accidents, the tiger egg was placed in the center of a glass cabinet, and visitors were not allowed to touch it closely. Even so, people are still satisfied and amazed.

Among the long queue of visitors, an ostrich suddenly appeared, stretching its neck and staring at the tiger egg again and again. Suddenly, it started shouting: "Wow, isn't this my stolen treasure egg? Although there are tiger stripes drawn on it, I can still clearly see the code I've written!"

For days, the Animal Satellite TV phone has been busy dealing with inquiries, criticisms, and suspicions. For a time, the whole city was in an uproar.

This shout shocked the security guards in the exhibition hall. The guard quickly grabbed the ostrich into Little Monkey's temporary office. The ostrich kept shouting: "Two months ago, I laid two extra-large ostrich eggs, breaking the record of ostrich egg-laying history. Just as I was about to hatch the eggs, my precious eggs suddenly disappeared. I cried bitterly and immediately searched all over the world for them. I never expected that some unscrupulous person would steal them, draw tiger stripes on them, and exhibit them as tiger eggs to make ill-gotten gains..."

As soon as the lawsuit ended, the Animal Satellite TV News Channel announced important news during prime time: A wild South China tiger laid two tiger eggs.

Who would have thought that this little monkey had tricked everyone!

A dream of a dog laying eggs triggered a lawsuit.

After a few days, the front page of the "Daily Curiosities" published a long article. It detailed the process of discovering the tiger eggs, from finding the tiger eggs and the left tiger paw prints to finally locating the South China tiger. It was understood that the South China tiger that laid these tiger eggs was so embarrassed that it hid itself in the monkey's cave and refused interviews. The two tiger eggs are now kept by the little monkey.

The explosive news made people suspicious: First, according to estimates, wild South China tigers had already become extinct, so how did this big news of a wild South China tiger emerge? Second, tigers are placental mammals, so how could they lay two tiger eggs?

If you don't believe it, the TV screen clearly broadcast photos of the two tiger eggs.

After the tiger egg news was aired, top experts from all walks of life in the animal kingdom came continuously, but unfortunately, the truth has not yet been uncovered, and the exposition has closed. A notice was posted at the entrance: "Due to the theft of the tiger eggs last night, the exhibition hall will be temporarily closed."
