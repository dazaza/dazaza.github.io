The Two-Year Stormy Road of Grassroots Entrepreneurs

by fenqu on 2008-05-14 00:08:15

Two Years of Wind and Rain for a Grassroots Entrepreneur

February 10, 2008 was a special day for Caike Online (www.coko365.com), as it marked its second anniversary. Unconsciously, two years had already passed. Caike Online Bookkeeping Network had grown from a child learning to speak into a vibrant teenager. The online bookkeeping market had evolved from being dominated solely by Caike Online to one with numerous competitors, and now back to Caike Online leading the trend. Reflecting on these two years of challenges, I couldn't help but feel overwhelmed with emotion.

One: The Cause of Entrepreneurship

Recalling the development of the Caike Online Bookkeeping Network project, it started from a very accidental reason. It was in December of 2005, shortly after I arrived in Shanghai, living in a dilapidated small house in Zhangjiang, curled up in a cold bed. At that time, my salary was not high, but expenses were enormous. Every month, I earned several thousand yuan, but I didn’t know where it all went. I thought I should carefully record my expenses to understand where my money was going. There were already some similar desktop software available on the market at that time, such as Cai Zhi Family Finance. However, there was a defect with this software: it could only be installed on one machine, which was very inconvenient. I thought: if I could use it on my phone, it would be more convenient. Furthermore, if I could record on my phone and view it online, it would be even better.

With this idea, I became very excited. After checking online, I found that there was nothing similar domestically at the time. When I mentioned my idea to my good friend "Li Hua," I still clearly remember what he said after pondering for a moment:

"This can work!"

Later, my project faced opposition from countless people, including friends and relatives who didn't see its potential. But because of Li Hua's decisive "yes," we persisted until today.

Two: On the Road

Because I had experience operating a "Yishu Network" before, I knew that in China, once you create something, there will immediately be countless imitators. I told Li Hua at the time: "I predict that within half a year, these kinds of websites will sprout up like mushrooms after rain." Later, the facts indeed proved this point.

Once the idea was decided, I immediately began assembling a team for planning and development. At that time, there was no prior experience in online bookkeeping and financial management, so we didn't know exactly what to develop or which path to take, everything was done through trial and error. Many things are like this: once they are done, they don't seem like much. But before they are done, no one knows what they will be like. Just like Google, before it came out, who could have imagined that a website could be like this: a large page with just a big text box. In terms of technology, there were me and Li Hua, and we also needed a graphic designer, so we recruited Stella.

After more than two months of development, the most primitive prototype of Caike Online emerged: it was then called "Wealth Management Ala," with the domain name www.licaionline.com, a homophone of the domain name. The older users of Caike Online must certainly remember this name. The domain name was activated in February 2006. It was the second online bookkeeping platform in China at that time. Subsequently, Caike Online diligently worked on its own projects.

The first version released basic income and expenditure functions. However, since both me and Li Hua had backgrounds in large-scale projects, the technical architecture of Caike Online was made very complex, making subsequent development inconvenient. Moreover, the interface inherited from a previous project had potential copyright infringement issues. Technical personnel tend to make similar mistakes. Therefore, during the following months, we focused on simplifying the architecture and redesigning the interface. During this period, the development of Caike Online was slow.

In April, a simplified architecture version was launched. Around this time, another member of the Caike Online team, Menter, joined. Menter's joining greatly accelerated our development work. By June 2006, a completely new architecture for Caike Online was born. Also, after widely soliciting opinions, "Wealth Management Ala" was officially renamed to "Caike Online," and the new domain name "www.coko365.com" was adopted. And simultaneously, we gained a professional financial person, "Hei Zi."

From June to October, the market remained calm. Searching online for "online bookkeeping" still only yielded Caike Online. Perhaps due to this reason, Caike Online developed slowly. The database layer changed repeatedly, and the design direction kept fluctuating. Initially, it supported multiple users, but later it was deemed unnecessary and removed. Then it was added back. At this time, the users were mostly family and friends, without any real external users.

Three: The Development of Caike Online

Caike Online's first true user was named "Lu'er," a user from Inner Mongolia. This first user made me extraordinarily excited! That night, I noticed from the backend that she failed to log in multiple times. I immediately looked into the cause and didn't find the reason until three o'clock in the morning: it turned out to be due to the use of Chinese characters in her name. I immediately sent her an email informing her of the reason. The scene is still vivid in my mind. Now Lu'er might have left. All the members of our Caike team bless her!

Entering October, the development direction of Caike became a problem. On this point, I had serious disagreements with Menter: I advocated focusing on practicality first, achieving functionality before developing other aspects. Menter advocated improving the interface and developing the community first. Since both of us were technically inclined and inexperienced in the market, the development of Caike Online temporarily fell into a stalemate. Entering 2007, as I predicted, this kind of website sprouted up like mushrooms after rain, almost every month new ones appeared, and some had very aggressive promotional campaigns. Several even surpassed Caike Online. Moreover, some of Caike Online's original ideas, such as SMS bookkeeping, were plagiarized. At this time, we realized that continuing this way wouldn't work. We needed capable marketing personnel to reverse the situation.

Four: The Soaring of Caike

At this critical moment, Xiao Tian joined. Xiao Tian's joining resolved our crisis quietly. Xiao Tian had operated websites for many years and had a wealth of experience. After several months of promotion and offensive strategies, the website progressed rapidly. In a short time, Alexa ranking improved from over a million to around 150,000. Daily registrations increased from less than ten people to a maximum of 1,500 people per day! Daily IP numbers continued to rise. And it received increasing media attention, having been reported by "Financial Weekly", "Shanghai Morning Post", "Shenjiang Service Guide", "Market Newspaper", "Hangzhou Daily", Beijing Television, CCTV Channel 2, and others. After two years of development, Caike Online finally rose from the bottom few ranks to become the largest online bookkeeping platform in China. Due to Caike Online's outstanding performance in technical development experience, strength, and product function innovation, it has now formed a strategic partnership with Alibaba to develop online bookkeeping software for Taobao.

Five: The Difficulties of Caike

When Caike Online was still relatively small, many latent problems did not surface. However, as Caike Online continued to grow, more and more difficulties presented themselves before us. September 2007 was a critical moment for the survival of Caike Online. Due to a sudden surge in traffic, Caike Online had always rented virtual hosting, and the limited virtual space could no longer meet the huge traffic and member account data. To make matters worse, Caike Online was just undergoing a revamp and unable to optimize data in time, causing Caike Online to fail to open normally for a while. This situation lasted for more than a week, resulting in the loss of many members. Fortunately, a group of loyal members continued to support us. Every day, we dared not open Caike Online or QQ, as the website received many complaints from members about slow speed and inability to open. We also dared not open QQ, as each time we opened QQ, there were a lot of complaints and grievances. Finally, we made a firm decision, raising funds from various sources, and purchased a new server, resolving this crisis. More than a week was truly an unprecedented ordeal. This difficulty gave us a huge lesson and blow, but fortunately, we persevered.

Not long after purchasing the server, Caike Online suffered another major setback, which was a ruthless hacker attack. One day in November, when we started work in the morning, many members wrote letters and left messages for us, saying that all their recorded account data was gone. We immediately checked the server data and found that it had been hacked. If we couldn't recover the member data quickly, Caike Online would face complete extinction, and no member would come to record accounts again. The technical staff of Caike Online immediately took action, seeking help from various sources, and managed to recover 80% of the data within an hour. In the next two to three days, we basically recovered all the data of the members. Once again, we turned danger into safety, gaining more trust and support from the members.

Five: Conclusion

Caike Online has experienced two years of ups and downs. Although there have been joys and worries, bitterness and sweetness, developments and difficulties, we have never been frightened by difficulties each time. After each big wave, we have more confidence to face greater challenges. In these two years of development, it couldn't have happened without the support of a wide range of members, friends, and the outside world. It is precisely the support of a wide range of members that gives us more strength and confidence. There is no prominent background in the Caike Online entrepreneurial team. All the entrepreneurial members are ordinary grassroots entrepreneurs with no successful experience. Everything needs to be explored and moved forward step by step. We firmly believe that under the efforts of the Caike Online team, we will be able to provide everyone with a more excellent online bookkeeping financial management software.
