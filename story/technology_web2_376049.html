"Interview" Guokr: Hopes to Become a Community for Tech-Savvy Youth

by ybcz0519 on 2010-12-27 16:05:52

This article is simultaneously published on Mobinode. Business media, please do not reprint! Guokr.com is a science-themed website with social networking attributes, composed of different content-based thematic sites, providing a large amount of responsible, intelligent, and life-related content. Here, you can:

- Follow different thematic sites based on your interests for precise reading of your favorite content and exchange ideas with netizens;

- Follow people who interest you, read their recommendations, and share interesting content with those who follow you;

- Pose your perplexing scientific questions in "Guokr Q&A" or provide reliable answers.

Many people pay attention to Guokr.com, possibly because they are familiar with the former Science Writers Club (Scientific Squirrels). Guokr.com is an important brand under Guokr Media, which was originally named Beijing A Group of Squirrels Cultural Communication Co., Ltd. It is the physical support institution for the famous public welfare project "Science Writers Club." Last month, Guokr Media received a million-dollar level investment from ZhenFund Capital (Via), attracting much attention again; its founder Ji Shisan hopes that Guokr Media will become the financier for the non-profit organization Science Writers Club, using business to nourish public welfare—a very meaningful attempt. All this makes us curious about the new brand under Guokr Media, Guokr.com. To better understand Guokr.com, we interviewed Ji Shisan (Sina Weibo: Ji Shisan), the founder of Guokr Media via email. Ji Shisan provided detailed introductions regarding the initial intentions behind founding Guokr.com and the website's positioning, as well as discussed the future direction of Guokr.com.

Q1. Please introduce Guokr.com and your team.

A: Beijing Guokr Interactive Technology Media Co., Ltd. was established in January 2009, initially named "Beijing A Group of Squirrels Cultural Communication Co., Ltd." As a comprehensive media company dedicated to promoting scientific concepts and disseminating scientific content to the public, Guokr Media's current main businesses include the science-themed website Guokr.com (Guokr.com), the reading brand "Guokr Reading," and the science brand cooperation platform.

Guokr.com is a science-themed website with social network characteristics, launching multiple thematic sites such as fun technology, DIY technology, health, psychology, and pet love, covering various topics and interest focuses related to technology among urban youth. These online science interest groups will lead users to rediscover lost curiosity and experience the joy that technology brings to cultural life. Guokr.com also launched a distinctive "Guokr Q&A" service, where users' questions will be attended to, answered, and shared here, realizing a knowledge creation mechanism in the form of Q&A, helping people look at the world through a technological perspective.

We believe that Web 2.0 has brought content production rights to users, but how to create professional, high-quality content becomes a new challenge. Previously, the Science Writers Club cultivated a group of influential knowledge leaders from grassroots efforts; Guokr.com will further expand this model, combining the decentralization of the Web 2.0 era with the content responsibility and editorial stance of traditional media, using scientific content to address all aspects of public life.

Q2. What were the initial intentions for founding the Guokr.com website?

A: During the entrepreneurial process of the Science Writers Club, I discovered several levels of demand disparities. First, companies hope to promote technology brands, but the general public may find it difficult to understand them; secondly, the government also needs to make the public understand the country's progress in scientific research by investing in research; thirdly, the educated public wants to understand changes in the world's technology and keep up with the times. Technology is the fastest-changing element of an era, understanding technology means understanding the times, and the public also needs a good channel to keep themselves, including their next generation, up with these changes.

This demand can be resolved through good science media like Discovery, but we have found that domestic media still has a long way to go in this regard. The Science Writers Club itself is a public welfare project, and to shoulder the mission of science media, commercialized media operations must be carried out. Therefore, I gradually considered separating the two sides. Although both are within the scope of science communication, we need to achieve different goals with different methods, teams, and management approaches.

Next, you know what happened: we separated the commercial and public welfare institutions, respectively establishing Guokr Media and the public welfare NGO Hasai Center. Fortunately, my concept has been recognized by outsiders, including investors. Our goal is to make Guokr Media China's Discovery, a great science media. Now, Guokr.com is one of the core brands under Guokr Media.

Q3. Before Guokr.com, many people were already familiar with the Squirrel Club website. Can you briefly introduce the differences in positioning between the Squirrel Club and Guokr.com?

A: The Science Writers Club is essentially not just a website or a group blog but a club for young science communicators. In addition to presenting serious scientific discussions on the website, behind the scenes, it conducts many activities and services for this group of people and the entire science communication cause, such as popular science lectures, viewing parties, and offline activities. It also expands the coverage and influence of science communication by opening collective columns in many domestic print media outlets, and through science writing training programs, it enhances the overall level of science communication authors.

The work done by the Science Writers Club is purely public welfare in nature. From a content perspective, the Science Writers Club delves deeper and more professionally into science.

On the other hand, Guokr.com is a commercially operated media. We position our website as a "general science" theme, including science popularization, science, and science fiction. Now, Guokr.com's slogan is "Science, Intelligence, Life." In fact, intelligence encompasses all rational human knowledge. Therefore, if you look at the content of our 15 thematic sites, you'll find many interesting topics at the boundary of science and humanities, including literature, art, design, music, and film.

However, Guokr.com is not just a content website. The existing "Guokr Q&A" on Guokr.com has already attempted to combine professional knowledge with the content production method of Web 2.0, providing users with high-quality, search-engine-unfindable knowledge. Guokr.com is currently developing its second phase product, and in the future, we will feature more social networking characteristics.

Q4. In the introduction of Guokr.com, it defines itself as a "science-themed website with social networking attributes." What factors made you consider introducing social elements into a science-themed website?

A: This is an all-things-social era, but you'll find domestically that tech-savvy youths lack a place to gather, entertain, discuss, and interact. Artsy youths can go to Douban, but tech-savvy youths? There's nowhere to go. Professional forums can meet some of these needs, but they are limited in both functionality and topics.

We hope that Guokr.com will guide tech-savvy youths to gather, communicate, and learn here. Besides simply providing articles for everyone to read, we hope readers can do more here. Guokr.com is undergoing its second phase of development, and soon, you'll find Guokr.com even more fun.

Q5. Each thematic site on Guokr.com has some resident editors. This reminds me of Demand Media, which ensures article quality through a group of high-quality editors and uses these high-quality contents as sources for other media. What are the similarities and differences between Guokr.com and Demand Media?

A: Demand Media’s demand for articles mainly looks at whether the article can occupy a good position in search engines, and its model is heavily dependent on search engines. Guokr.com is more "idealistic," we hope to create truly good articles. In fact, in this era, the role of search engines in diverting traffic has diminished. Only truly good articles will be widely spread. Moreover, apart from Guokr.com, Guokr Media has developed other dissemination channels such as books, and in the future, we will comprehensively utilize this content.

Another point I want to mention is that good content is always inseparable from excellent people. One valuable practice we discovered during the Science Writers Club period is cultivating our authors. After Guokr.com went live, some users who have been following us since the internal testing stage often give feedback saying that our content has become much more appealing now. Many young members of our editorial team have backgrounds in science and engineering and haven't received much media training before, but we frequently communicate experiences, conduct training and discussions internally, greatly improving the whole team in a short period. In fact, there shouldn't be any barriers between science and the public, and we hope to cultivate more interdisciplinary talents like "scientific deserters" to break down this barrier.

Q6. Can you talk about the future development direction of Guokr.com?

A: Guokr.com hopes to become a community where tech-savvy youths gather. Due to the particularity of the technology field, strategically, the site will create a large number of valuable original contents and develop rich interactive forms. We expect that through the efforts of Guokr.com, technological interests will become an important element in people's entertainment and leisure lives. Besides Guokr.com, Guokr Media has also developed Guokr Reading (book brand) and will expand other channels in the future. Guokr.com is the core of our entire strategic layout.
