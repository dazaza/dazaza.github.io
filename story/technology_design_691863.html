Beijing Outward Bound Company - Practice is more valuable than thought

by chandadada on 2012-03-05 18:57:02

Beijing Outward Bound training is a new type of outdoor training model. It uses some outdoor activities to introduce difficulties under natural conditions, and allows everyone to challenge themselves while ensuring safety. This activity aims to tap into human potential and enhance team awareness through participation and challenges. Regarding the issue of practice leading to thought, Beijing Outward Bound companies have been diligently researching for a long time how to incorporate this issue into their training programs. This also proves the importance of addressing modern living issues.

There was a person who wanted to become a writer, and he did indeed have some talent in this area because he had a lot of inspiration. However, before putting pen to paper, he wondered: Would his novel be published? Would it make him famous? Then, after writing a few paragraphs, he suddenly remembered that someone had written a book on management and immediately became famous. So, he abandoned his novel and started studying management. Before he could fully understand management, a marriage novel became a bestseller. He thought about his own marriage, which was filled with contradictions and dramatic elements, and began writing about it. Just when he had written halfway, a book on the "I Ching" became an instant hit, and he immediately started studying the "I Ching." In this way, he followed what others wrote and gained fame, but in the end, all he had were half-finished drafts on his computer. He knew a little about everything but not enough to master any one thing, and as a result, he could never write anything thoroughly. Of course, he became a "master of rejections."

Having a good idea is just the beginning; next, you need to guide that idea onto the right development track. If you are fickle and lack planning in your actions, you will never achieve anything. Thought is the most active and creative element in the spiritual world, but without conscious, systematic, and constructive guidance, it cannot lead to any creation. This is the difference between idle thoughts and constructive thoughts. Idle thoughts waste time and energy, whereas constructive thoughts mean innovation and success.

When we have a good idea, we must strive to put it into practice. Without action, it's hard to succeed, just like how pies don't fall from the sky. There are many such people in our lives, and they also hinder the development of our society. In today's corporate work environment, we also need this spirit. It can greatly improve the company's work efficiency and everyone's enthusiasm, playing a huge role in promoting the company's development.
