Four principles that green decoration needs to follow

by longersing on 2009-11-28 23:31:15

Green interior decoration needs to have a reasonable decoration plan, including three aspects: green environmental protection design, use of green decoration materials, and green environmental protection construction. It also needs to follow the principles of safety first, health, comfort, economy, etc.

Principle of Safety First

For the quality of home decoration design, there are currently three popular standards internationally, namely the so-called three concepts: S (safety), H (health), C (comfort). These represent safety, health, and comfort respectively for evaluation. In China, architectural design standards are generally applied, which means that home decoration design must adhere to four principles: safety, health, comfort, and economy.

The order of these four principles must not be confused as it reflects their importance from one side. Safety is the most basic and important aspect in home decoration because human life, production, and enjoyment all depend on the continuation of normal life.

Principle of Health

In recent years, people have become increasingly concerned about health. Therefore, the health issues related to architecture and interior decoration have also received significant attention. Currently, China is also taking corresponding measures and actively responding by proposing the concept of "healthy housing." So-called "healthy housing" refers to creating a natural environment in people's homes that is beneficial to physical health, does not or minimizes pollution harmful to health, and can meet the normal living needs of special groups (such as the disabled, elderly, etc.). To ensure healthiness in home decoration, the following points are usually considered: ensuring good natural conditions, establishing a good home natural environment, and controlling indoor environmental pollution.

Principle of Comfort

The purpose of home decoration is to make family life more comfortable. What kind of home environment can make family members feel comfortable? This mainly depends on how well it meets both material and spiritual needs. The former involves fulfilling the functional requirements of family life and providing a natural environment that makes the body feel comfortable. The latter creates an atmosphere suitable for family life, giving the home certain aesthetic value, and through associative effects, adding emotional value as well.

Principle of Economy

Home decoration consumes a tremendous amount of social wealth. According to incomplete statistics, just in Shanghai, the cost of home decoration every year reaches 30 billion yuan. For individual families, home decoration can cost anywhere from tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands or even millions of yuan. Therefore, it is necessary to establish the concept of achieving the same effect with the most economical methods.
