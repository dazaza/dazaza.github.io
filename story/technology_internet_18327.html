Tickle - a site that offers various tests.

by andegg on 2007-05-01 23:16:06

Previously, in order to get free call minutes from Globe7, I took an IQ test on the Tickle website just for the sake of it. Since then, I've often received test invitations. Today, I specifically checked it out and found it quite interesting. For example, there's this basic classic IQ test below:

"Our Classic IQ Test is the most thorough and scientifically accurate IQ Test on the Web. Previously offered only to corporations, schools, and certified professionals ― it’s now available to you from Tickle. It’s free, private, and developed by PhDs."

The gist is: Our Classic IQ Test is the most comprehensive and scientific IQ test on the web. It was previously only provided to companies, schools, and proven professionals, but now Tickle offers it to you. It's free, confidential, and developed by PhDs. (My English isn't very good, so there might be some errors.)

With the help of Google Toolbar's built-in translation feature, I completed the entire test.
