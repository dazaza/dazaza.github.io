Never fight an unprepared battle. - Mao Zedong

A party outside the Party, imperial thinking; no factions within the Party, all kinds of strange things. - Mao Zedong

The enemy rots away day by day, and we get better day by day. - Mao Zedong

Soldiers and civilians are the root of victory. - Mao Zedong

Elementary particles are also divisible. - Mao Zedong

Reorganize, clean the house before inviting guests, take one side. - Mao Zedong

What decides the outcome of a battle is people, not things. - Mao Zedong

Nuclear war cannot happen. - Mao Zedong

Study hard, make progress every day. - Mao Zedong

Seizing national victory is only the first step in a long march. The most humble sentence. - Mao Zedong

If the enemy advances, we retreat; if the enemy camps, we harass; if the enemy tires, we attack; if the enemy retreats, we pursue. - Mao Zedong

If we can win, we fight; if we cannot win, we retreat. - Mao Zedong

Imperialism is a paper tiger. - Mao Zedong

Developing some atomic bombs, hydrogen bombs, intercontinental missiles, I think it's entirely possible in ten years. - Mao Zedong

If the enemy has it, we need to have it too; if the enemy doesn't have it, we still need it. We must have atomic bombs and hydrogen bombs quickly. No matter what country, no matter what kind of bomb, atomic or hydrogen bombs, we must surpass them all. - Mao Zedong

Either the east wind prevails over the west wind, or the west wind prevails over the east wind. - Mao Zedong

A calm lake emerges from high gorges. - Mao Zedong

So many things, always urgent; heaven and earth turn, time presses. Ten thousand years is too long; seize the day, seize the hour. - Mao Zedong

One who does not reach the Great Wall is not a true hero. - Mao Zedong

The power of examples is infinite. - Mao Zedong

Develop sports activities, enhance the physical fitness of the people. - Mao Zedong

Revolution is not like inviting guests to dinner, writing articles, painting or embroidery, which can be so elegant, unhurried, polite, and gentle. Revolution is an uprising, a violent action where one class overturns another. - Mao Zedong

No matter what winds blow or waves crash, it feels better than strolling in a courtyard. - Mao Zedong

Enhance discipline, and there will be no revolution that cannot be won. - Mao Zedong

In twenty years, the French will have a big headache. - Mao Zedong

There is not the slightest hope for negotiations between the KMT and the CCP to succeed. - Mao Zedong

De Gaulle coming to power has its benefits; he likes to quarrel with Britain and America. - Mao Zedong

Women hold up half the sky. - Mao Zedong

Adhering to the unity of opposites is the fundamental law of the universe. - Mao Zedong

Chen Yi was a good comrade; his contributions to the Chinese revolution and world revolution have been judged. - Mao Zedong

A child leaves his hometown with determination, vowing not to return until he achieves success. - Mao Zedong

Even if it takes ten thousand years, nuclear submarines must be developed. - Mao Zedong

The noblest are the clumsiest, the basest are the wisest! - Mao Zedong

Whatever the enemy opposes, we support; whatever the enemy supports, we oppose. - Mao Zedong

Educators must be educated first. - Mao Zedong
