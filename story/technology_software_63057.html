Shenzhen Anges Intelligent System Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, design, and service of fire electronics, intelligent building, smart community management systems, as well as home smoke, gas, and anti-theft alarm controllers and sensors. It also engages in the production and sales of home alarmers and intelligent sensors. The company has the capability for bulk export production processing, and it is the only domestic enterprise that has passed the SGS-TUV product and factory certification. Its home smoke alarm has passed the GS test of European international standards, with over one million units exported.

Anges Industry (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. focuses on the collection, research, and application of foreign intelligent system product technologies, as well as the export trade of Anges products. Its products have been widely sold to regions such as Germany, Norway, and the United States.

Chengdu Anges Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd., invested by Shenzhen Anges Company, has been approved by the Ministry of Public Security as a national designated R&D and production enterprise of fire electronics, intelligent building, and security system products. The company has advanced production and testing methods, forming the ability for batch production of various controllers and sensors. It has continuously passed the national GB/T9001-1994 quality system certification for eight years. All produced products have passed the national testing center's tests and obtained quality certification certificates.

Anges Company has a group of excellent professional scientific and technological talents engaged in research and development work, with strong independent development and design capabilities for intelligent alarm and control products. Currently, the company has involved itself in two fields: fire electronics and intelligent buildings, with over ten series and more than three hundred varieties of products. Among them, the successfully developed CA2000 type distributed intelligent fire alarm control system is at the forefront of industry technology. This system emphasizes future development directions, featuring four major characteristics: decentralized centralized intelligence, digital signal transmission, integrated alarm linkage, and networked system composition. The CA5000 series of smart community systems includes a variety of products such as video intercoms, home theft prevention, parking lot patrol management, and remote meter reading, truly providing customers with professional smart community safety management solutions.

Currently, Anges products are widely sold domestically and internationally. Anges' marketing service network covers the entire country, with four sales companies, six general distributors, 22 offices, and multiple product service centers carefully set up to provide users with fast, timely, and comprehensive services. Thousands of projects already in use have shown excellent operational conditions, widely praised by both contractors and users.

Creating a safe new era, crafting a peaceful new year. With an extraordinary entrepreneurial spirit, superlative innovation awareness, and strict scientific management, Anges Company adheres to the quality policy of "Quality First, Service First, Pursuit of Excellence," sincerely cooperating with friends from all sectors to build safety and contribute a worry-free living environment to society.
