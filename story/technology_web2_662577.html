Specialized agency for the import of small instruments from Hong Kong.

1. For larger shipments of cowhide and sheepskin goods, general trade import can be adopted. The specific process is as follows:

a. Based on the import packing list information provided by the customer, we will organize the best import plan.

b. According to the plan and according to the relevant customs supervision conditions of the goods, declare to the relevant departments and prepare all the customs declaration documents.

c. Organize the customs declaration documents and submit them to the competent customs for declaration.

d. With the cooperation of the customs document reviewers with whom we have a long-term cooperation, the customs document reviewers will review the documents according to the agreed declared value, issue a special payment form of the customs.

e. Pay according to the amount of the tariff and VAT payment slip issued by the customs, complete the payment procedures, and the customs will stamp and release it.

f. The operator notifies the transport vehicle to pick up the goods and pass through the customs.

2. For smaller shipments, express import method is used. Express import adopts a form of centralized declaration to apply for customs clearance. Because they are small quantities of goods concentrated for customs clearance, some reduction in import tax and customs supervision conditions can be enjoyed. In general, if declared in the morning, the goods can be released and delivered in the afternoon of the same day. It can save the freight of dedicated vehicles, complicated customs declaration documents, and enjoy reduction of tariffs, while maintaining high efficiency. Express import is mostly charged by weight for convenience.

(1) Extremely simple procedures. No need for the cargo owner to specifically provide customs declaration forms, clearance documents, or licenses (such as 3C national mandatory certification, environmental protection certificates, import quotas, etc., are not required).
