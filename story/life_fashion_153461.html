Funny Office

by email1013 on 2009-05-26 14:59:31

From nine to five, in a constant temperature throughout the four seasons, one cubicle, one monitor, piles of documents, and an endless amount of tasks. Are you happy at work? The fast-paced modern work life suffocates us, causing headaches, fatigue, insomnia, indigestion, soreness in the lower back, confusion, frustration, boredom, anxiety, and feelings of helplessness. These negative emotions cast dark clouds over our moods, and we urgently need relief and relaxation from the heavy physical and mental pressures - we need to find joy in our work! Because we refuse to be slaves to our work; instead, we want to be the masters of it!

We believe that happiness is an attitude. We advocate a lifestyle of working happily. With just a little change, we can reap happiness! We hope that through relentless effort, like Starbucks' rich coffee culture, we can create our own culture of happy work.

We will strive to provide fun, stylish, and creative office entertainment supplies to foster a joyful, healthy, and stress-free office environment. This will allow office workers to reduce stress, relax, ease their emotions, enhance creativity, promote physical and mental health and happiness, and improve the quality of work and life!
