With the development of modern medicine, especially after China's entry into WTO, traditional Chinese medicine is facing unprecedented opportunities. International pharmaceutical institutions no longer require TCM products to be single products with known structures, but can be mixtures with fixed components, stable efficacy and safe reliability, providing favorable conditions for TCM to go global.

Centipedes have been used as traditional animal-based Chinese herbal medicines for thousands of years. In terms of nourishment and health care, centipede-based health products with health benefits are increasingly attracting people's attention and affection. Shenlong Liquor is a healthy therapy integrating traditional food therapy, food treatment, and food supplement. It is a fitness drink produced with modern technology. Toxicological research, nutritional analysis, and efficacy studies on this product show that Shenlong Liquor is highly nutritious, containing more than 15 kinds of amino acids and trace elements needed by the human body, and has obvious analgesic and detoxifying effects. In addition, centipede health products have multiple functions such as tonifying kidney, strengthening yang, and enhancing the body's immune function. Therefore, vigorously developing centipede farming and vigorously developing centipede series products to gradually develop them in the direction of industrial exploitation is the need of contemporary production.

For a long time, the country has been calling for the vigorous capture of centipedes. In some main producing areas of centipedes, seasonal capture and seasonal purchase have gradually evolved into cross-seasonal capture and year-round purchase. The purchase price of each centipede in the major Chinese herbal medicine markets has soared from a few cents to over 1.5 yuan, with higher export prices and still in short supply, with prices rising year by year. However, due to the frequent deforestation, pesticide and fertilizer pollution and other ecological environment destruction, wild resources are becoming increasingly scarce, leading to a severe shortage of centipede resources and rising prices. Therefore, a once-in-a-century opportunity is falling from the sky. In the next few years, centipede farming will surely become a shining highlight in the special breeding industry, with significant economic and social benefits.

As a long-term development project, when breeders sell commercial centipedes, they can choose to retain the best female breeds for reproduction, which naturally increases the benefits year by year. Breeding advantages:

1. Easy to breed: Century Xinyu Golden Headed Centipedes are gentle and can be bred at home or in factory-stereoscopic breeding. Factory-stereoscopic breeding is our company's original creation, the most efficient and fastest breeding method for centipedes.

2. Strong reproductive ability: One laying 60-80 eggs, constant temperature breeding lays eggs twice a year.

3. Less food consumption, wide range of feed: Large-scale breeding can artificially breed bait, natural breeding only needs wheat bran, vegetable leaves, fruits, insects, animal offal, bone powder, etc., almost no cost.

4. Strong disease resistance: Strong adaptability, strong vitality, not easy to get sick, absolutely no risk of avian influenza (chickens), foot-and-mouth disease (pigs), mad cow disease (cattle).

5. Time-saving: Generally fed between 6-8 pm, can be farmed during leisure time.

6. Low investment: Except for introducing species, almost no investment is needed. Ordinary families can use empty rooms, balconies, courtyards, front and back houses, cylinder breeding, box breeding, pool breeding, etc.; large-scale breeding can be enclosed breeding or stereoscopic breeding, self-growing green feed, self-breeding insects.
