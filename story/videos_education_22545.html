National Senior Business Management (EMBA) Advanced Seminar for the Pharmaceutical and Medical Device Industries

by bjbjbj on 2007-06-15 11:41:40

The Training Center of the National Food and Drug AdministrationPeking University Health Science CenterFood and Drug Training and Education [2007] No. 13

Notice Regarding Jointly Hosting the "National Pharmaceutical and Medical Device Industry Senior Business Management (EMBA) Advanced Study Program" with Peking University

All Relevant Enterprises and Institutions:    After China's entry into the WTO, the domestic pharmaceutical and medical device market is gradually becoming fully internationalized. The traditional pharmaceutical and medical device industry requires a large number of compound senior management talents with systematic management knowledge, professional knowledge, and forward-looking strategic thinking.    To enhance the competitive strength of China's pharmaceutical industry and ensure the healthy and sustainable development of pharmaceutical and medical device manufacturers and user units, aiming to adhere to precise legal policies as guidelines and rely on scientific management knowledge to increase communication and understanding between supply and demand sides, and establish cooperation and exchanges at the senior level, the Training Center of the National Food and Drug Administration has jointly launched the "National Pharmaceutical and Medical Device Industry Senior Management Personnel Business Management (EMBA) Advanced Study Program" with the Peking University Health Science Center.    It is hoped that all relevant enterprises and institutions can start from the height of development strategy, attach importance to cultivating senior management talents, and actively support management cadres in participating in this program’s learning.Now we hereby notify the relevant matters as follows:

One, Purpose of the Program: To cultivate a large number of compound senior management talents with systematic international management knowledge, professional knowledge, and forward-looking strategic thinking for China's pharmaceutical industry.

Two, Faculty Lineup:(One)  Renowned economists, management scientists, human resources experts, pharmaceutical experts, and legal experts from Peking University, Tsinghua University, Renmin University, etc.(Two)  Relevant officials and policy-making experts from the competent authorities of the industry(Three)  Outstanding managers and industry management experts from well-known hospitals, famous pharmaceutical companies, and medical device enterprise groups.

Three, Admission Targets:(One)  Presidents and senior managers of the pharmaceutical and medical device industries;(Two)  Responsible persons in charge of pharmaceuticals and medical devices, directors of hospital pharmacy departments, and directors of medical equipment departments;(Three)  Responsible persons from relevant government departments, research institutes, and industry associations.

Four, Features of the Program:    Senior management personnel from the pharmaceutical and medical device production and operation industries, along with responsible persons from the pharmaceutical and medical device user industries, will attend classes together, achieving comprehensive communication and exchange among producers, sellers, and users. This will promote the healthy and orderly development of China's pharmaceutical and healthcare industry. Through formal EMBA advanced study courses, your own management level will be enhanced. Knowledge-wise, "the learning content has a large capacity, wide-ranging knowledge scope, prominent industry characteristics, strong core competitiveness, and fast learning enhancement speed." This forms the optimal learning model integrating modern management classic courses, industry elective courses, hot topic lectures, expansion training, and practical simulation with core competitiveness. Students will be exposed to the latest and most advanced management concepts. During the course period, students from different classes will attend classes together, allowing them to discover the valuable and rare learning experience of mutual exchange and communication among students during high-level interactions. At the same time, they will build a high-quality network of personal connections, helping to win more business opportunities and broaden the space for career development.

Five, Teaching Methods:    Introduction of Harvard Business School case teaching mode - case analysis; integration of theoretical advantages of Peking University, Tsinghua University, Renmin University - theoretical improvement; network-assisted teaching, practical simulation, and observation visits.

Six, Core Courses:    ■ Strategic Management        ■ Credit Management                  ■ Management Psychology    ■ Management Economics        ■ Advertising and Communication Studies            ■ Human Resource Management    ■ Economic Law Practice        ■ Business Etiquette and Commercial Negotiation        ■ Financial Management and Capital Operation    ■ Marketing          ■ Organizational Behavior and Leadership Art      ■ Risk Investment and Financing Practice

Seven, Special Lectures: Related leaders from the competent authorities of the industry, health system-related department leaders, and policy-making experts will host special lectures on hot issues in pharmaceuticals and healthcare:     ☆ Development and Opportunities and Challenges Facing China's Pharmaceutical Industry              ☆ Medical Device Management    ☆ Reform and Function Transformation of the National Drug Supervision System              ☆ Reform of the Pharmaceutical Distribution System and Pharmaceutical Advertisement Management    ☆ Public Health System Reform and Policy Analysis                  ☆ Hospital Preparation Management and Clinical and Non-clinical Drug Management

Eight, Course Characteristics:    Expert face-to-face instruction combined with modern distance education methods; integration of classic management courses, industry-specific courses, series of lectures, expansion training, and practical simulations to enhance core competitiveness.

Nine, Recruitment Regions: Nationwide.

Ten, Learning Time: Twelve months (once a month, three full days on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday).

Eleven, Start Date: June 29, 2007 (Beijing Class)   June 22 (Shanghai Class)

Twelve, Class Locations: Peking University Health Science Center, East China Region, South China Region, Southwest China Region.

Thirteen, Learning Fees:      Tuition 20,000 RMB per person (this fee is collected by Peking University);      Additionally, application fees, qualification review, and textbook reservation fees 500 RMB per person, industry training fees and miscellaneous fees 9,300 RMB per person (these two fees are collected by the National Food and Drug Administration Training Center, including textbook lecture materials fees, industry courses, expert forums, visits, discussions, expansion training, and lunch fees during the teaching period).

Fourteen, Certificate Issuance      After completing the required credits and successfully passing the graduation thesis review, both Peking University Health Science Center and the Training Center of the National Food and Drug Administration will issue the "Senior Management Personnel Business Management EMBA Advanced Study Program Completion Certificate"; simultaneously issuing a national continuing education certificate for 25 credits. (Stamped with Peking University's steel seal.)
