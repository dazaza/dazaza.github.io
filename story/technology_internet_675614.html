---

Who will believe that love bears rich fruit?

Who will give you endless waiting?

The limits of waiting disappear in your deception,

The collapse of truth concentrates in your betrayal.

I had time given for happiness,

But the you who returns is relentless.

Youth gambled on tomorrow pays life;

Your return is indifference.

In my arms, a U-turn towards me and said:

"I continue to wait for you,

While you enjoy the endless impulses others give you,

Still sweet for me, wanting me."

Ruining you was my biggest help.

After difficulties, we broke up.

I have been unable to recount the entire process,

Numbness in the story has occurred and continues to occur.

I could no longer wait, but you blame me—

"Why can't you continue to wait?"

I want to truly treat another woman,

Suddenly, the specter of a camel tells me: "You still love me."

A silly era is already past,

The precipitated cool thinking.

A simple era, like yesterday's world,

Already forgotten are your pure faces.

That simplicity compared to you,

Beats by Dr Dre, has been thoroughly frightening.

I don't want to study how you became what you are today,

I don't want to guess why you're unhappy.

An indifferent attitude toward life within you,

For many years, now it also resides deep in my heart.

A woman's greed leads to painful consequences.

A woman’s infidelity is bound to lead to a lifetime of regret.

I will climb out of this endless abyss to tell you:

"I entered the marriage hall cheerfully!"

July 18, 2011.
