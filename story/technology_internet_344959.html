The first window displays the charm of Panjin - hotels and guesthouses in Panjin city are actively preparing for the tourism week.

by suian1492 on 2010-04-13 17:00:16

The second China · Panjin International Wetland Tourism Week will be held in Panjin from August 7 to August 13. With the approaching of the tourism week, various tasks of the hotel industry in Panjin City are being carried out intensively.

Renewed to welcome the grand event

Crystal chandeliers are dazzling, and the red carpet is noble and elegant. The newly added VIP meeting hall of Kunlun Hotel is fully equipped and carefully arranged. As the main venue of this tourism week, Kunlun Hotel is committed to improving the functionality of its facilities. It has added high-end dining rooms, luxury suites, small shopping malls and other facilities, allowing guests to experience all-round convenience and speed. It also specially purchased high-end tableware and towels in Shenyang and Beijing, striving for perfection in details.

Neatly decorated, with advanced facilities, the renovated guest rooms of Guomao Hotel are elegant and comfortable. Since the beginning of the year, the hotel has invested a cumulative total of 2 million yuan to renovate the guest rooms on four floors below the 8th floor according to five-star standards, updating the bedding to welcome the grand event with a completely renewed appearance.

Smiles for visitors from all directions

A smile and a greeting, the service attitude of the staff best showcases the spirit of the people of Panjin. Strengthening training and assessment is the focus of the major hotels and restaurants. Walking into Kunlun Hotel, the smiles and greetings of the service staff are more intimate and thoughtful. The recent service etiquette knowledge competition and job skill contest have comprehensively improved the team's service skills and the application of service etiquette; specialized training for employees on the special knowledge of the tourism week has enabled them to proficiently master the geographical features, customs, and culture of Panjin, making the service staff another window to promote Panjin.

One of the activities of the tourism week - the 2009 China · Panjin International Wetland Leisure Development Forum will be held by Fudehui Refined Catering Co., Ltd. With last year's successful experience, Fudehui will use international five-star tea break services to receive all the guests throughout the conference. In terms of conference settings and staff uniforms, it highlights the local and cultural sentiments of Panjin, perfectly combining international high-end elements with the local culture of Panjin.

Panjin elements facing the world

The magnificent reed marshes and red beaches, rare birds such as the black-mouthed gull and red-crowned crane... These iconic Panjin elements will add brilliance to the tourism week. At last year's welcome banquet of the tourism week, the centerpiece carving with a diameter of 2.3 meters fully showcased the characteristics of the oil city, attracting many guests to stop and admire. According to reports, this year's centerpiece carving design is even more ingenious and is sure to become one of the highlights of the tourism week. Guomao Hotel plans to move the unique Panjin element carvings into the hotel lobby, choosing different materials such as vegetables and pickles for clever combinations, allowing guests to feel the unique charm of Panjin as soon as they enter.

Food best embodies the regional characteristics of Panjin. Rice steamed with reed leaves, fried river knife fish, crystal wenchah dumplings, Tianzhuangtai rice cakes, clear water ducks... These specialty dishes will bring heart-stirring taste experiences imbued with Panjin sentiment. All hotels strictly control the procurement, storage, and processing of food, while establishing a "finished dish retention system" to ensure catering hygiene quality and the dietary safety of guests.

This grand event will be the most beautiful cultural feast presented to the citizens of Panjin and domestic and foreign friends, and we look forward to it with great enthusiasm.
