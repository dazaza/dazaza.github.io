The aquaculture industry of tropical freshwater fish fry is a rising industry that integrates economic benefits, social benefits, ecological benefits, and sightseeing benefits. Since its establishment over a year ago, the Ba River Aquatic Products Professional Cooperative has achieved significant results. The breeding of tropical freshwater fish fry is led by Zhejiang Yida Biotechnology Co., Ltd., implementing greenhouse breeding combined with outdoor pond breeding. By the end of 2008, 56,000 square meters of intelligent ornamental fish breeding greenhouses were constructed, along with 1,200 mu (about 80 hectares) of outdoor ecological breeding ponds. The broodstock breeding room, seedling hatching room, seedling breeding room, and adult fish breeding greenhouse are scientifically designed with complete facilities, equipped with corresponding water supply, heating, power supply, gas supply, light control, and water treatment systems. A batch of breeding tanks and hatching tanks, with specifications of 40cm (length) x 50cm (width) x 55cm (height), are also provided, featuring aeration, water inlet and outlet facilities. Circulating running water temperature-controlled breeding ensures a suitable growth environment for tropical freshwater fish fry. Moreover, the advanced water treatment system in each greenhouse allows the breeding water to be treated through a series of physical, chemical, and biological methods before being recycled, achieving high-density breeding and saving water resources. This is an environmentally friendly, water-saving, and high-output breeding model.

To expand production scale and simultaneously drive surrounding farmers to breed tropical freshwater fish fry for increased income and prosperity, the company organized and established a tropical freshwater fish fry cooperative, forming an industrialized operation model of [enterprise + cooperative + farmer], establishing a [providing seedlings, providing feed, providing medicine, providing technology, guaranteeing purchase, guaranteeing profit] [four-provide-two-guarantee] interest linkage mechanism with farmers, realizing win-win for enterprises and farmers, promoting fishermen's income increase and fishery efficiency improvement.

High-quality native fish can be supplied all year round. The fish fry farm has established stable business relationships with fish farms across China, having priority purchasing rights for multiple varieties of fish fry. Mainly producing the fry of the four major domesticated fish species, mandarin fish fry, and yellow catfish fry. The fish fry farm regards quality as life, sincerely serving a wide range of customers, consistently adhering to the principle of product quality first and customer paramountcy. With the service philosophy of meeting the needs of fish farmers and solving their urgent problems, any customer purchasing fish fry from our farm will be provided with the best on-site service, ensuring high-quality, uniform size, low price, disease-free, fast-growing fish fry for aquaculturists, while imparting breeding techniques, helping customers increase income and create wealth, generating higher benefits for society.
