"The Tough Girls of the Drama Academy" tells in a realistic way the hilarious and heart-wrenching lives of three girls studying screenwriting at the academy: Mu Zi, Lao Ma, and Wei Wei.

It starts with Mu Zi receiving her admission letter and enthusiastically planning to confess her secret love to her senior, only to unexpectedly encounter a thug. Gradually, the humorous, warm yet sorrowful college life is unveiled before the readers. It fully demonstrates the bitter path of a group of young people who are devoted to art as they struggle and sink in this special environment of the Drama Academy.

In fact, this story does not only depict the lives of the female students in the drama academy but also everyone's college life. Those days when we always thought we were cool but kept doing foolish things, unforgettable first loves, those happy and sour pasts that can never come back, those youthful years spent singing and walking freely with a group of friends, all flow out in these tough, sharp, almost cruel words, making us laugh through tears and savor for a long time.
