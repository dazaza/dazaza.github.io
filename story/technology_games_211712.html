Warlock leveling method from 1-40 and skill point distribution

by lk0gamel on 2009-07-21 04:00:34

I won't go into details about naming the new character. After the character is born, first go to Mr. Lou, the magistrate of Qixia Town, to accept the newbie mission (if you don't know where Mr. Lou is, you can use the NPC search function, which is the magnifying glass on the right side of the small map). The NPCs in the mission are mainly concentrated in the lower left corner of Qixia Town (you can open the mission prompt bar with ALT+Q, check the NPC location mark at the bottom, and just follow the direction of the red arrow). This mission must be done, not only can you get a newbie headband and shoes, but also the most important Nine-Fu Great Supplement Pill (it can replenish a total of 30,000 HP, each use has a minimum blood replenishment of 200).

After completing this mission, you can smoothly level up to level 2, and your character's position should be at Tongxiangyu's Tongfu Inn. First, find Tongxiangyu to accept the "Test Your Skills" mission (kill 10 hugging rabbits), then leave the inn and find the quest manager to accept bounty missions. At this level, there can only be two missions: finding the green land and Gongsun Wulong's cat meat mission. First, exit the north gate of Qixia Town, the map shows a small path leading to the pavilion there. Walk a few steps in front of NPC Zhu Wushuang, and you will get the task hint for finding the green land. Then enter the north gate, go to Gongsun Wulong on the left to accept the cat meat mission, and then go to Dr. Xue, the pharmacist, to complete the green land mission (the mission reward is 80 experience points, during the public test when there are many people, leveling up to levels 4 or 5 by doing this mission is no problem).

After completing the mission, exit the east gate, and you can see two types of monsters, cats and hugging rabbits, at the gate. Kill 10 hugging rabbits and deal with 10 cat meats, then return to complete the mission, you should be able to reach level 3. Then you will receive the "Qualification Acquisition" mission from Tongxiangyu (kill 20 squirrels). It is not recommended to do it at this time, as being bitten by squirrels still hurts before reaching level 4. So continue to go to the quest manager to accept missions (recommended to accept the green land mission), after running several rounds, you can smoothly reach level 4. At this point, there are obviously more missions available from the quest manager. Recommended missions include saving rabbits and hunting rabbit rats. Then form a team and run to the eastern suburbs of Qixia Town, where there are a large number of mission monsters (the reason why rabbit rat missions are recommended is because tasks in "Wulin Waizhuan" are completed one by one according to the order of acceptance. Even if the dropped items come from the same monster, they will not drop when you have a monster killing task. Therefore, the only shortcut is to accept nearby missions involving task monsters), while your teammates kill squirrels, you kill rabbit rats, so you can complete multiple missions at the same time without running around looking for monsters (during the public test, teams can distribute tasks well).

Then go back to Tongxiangyu to complete the mission, and you can continue to receive the mission of "Guanzhong Hero Lu Qinghou" (kill rabbit rats to get the imperial gold medal). For the same reason, at this time, accept the squirrel crisis or pine cone mission. If you see a green mission, make sure to accept it. If there are time-limited missions, make sure to prioritize them. Highly recommended is the blacksmith's entrustment (kill squirrels within 30 minutes, and gain experience points based on the number of completions, 100 kills give 1500 experience, which is one of the most rewarding early-stage experience missions).
