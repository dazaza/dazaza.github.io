Nanning holds a special report meeting on the China-ASEAN Creative Amusement Park project

by anonymous on 2012-03-06 22:15:39

On February 26, the Nanning Municipal Party Committee convened a special briefing meeting to seriously study and deploy the construction work of the China-ASEAN Creative Amusement Park project, and accelerate the development of cultural industry projects in the Wuxiang New Area.

At the meeting, the person in charge of the planning unit of the China-ASEAN Creative Amusement Park project introduced the progress of the project's planning and design. Everyone conducted an in-depth study on the difficulties and problems that need to be coordinated and resolved during the planning and advancement of the project, and proposed the next steps for work ideas and measures.

The China-ASEAN Creative Amusement Park project aims to create a "domestic first-class, internationally advanced" cultural brand, planning and constructing artistic reality shows and tourism leisure projects in the Wuxiang New Area. It aims to inherit and protect Guangxi's national culture and reflect Nanning's positioning as a regional international city facing ASEAN. Currently, the project has successfully completed the preliminary research work and has initially formed a project planning and design plan.

Promoting the development of the Wuxiang New Area and creating a new Nanning is a major decision made by the Autonomous Region Party Committee and the government. Currently, all relevant planning departments are focusing on the project construction to formulate scientific planning schemes for water systems and road networks. Time is being seized, carefully organized, and various relevant departments at the district and municipal levels are working together pragmatically to promote the rapid implementation of cultural projects such as the China-ASEAN Creative Amusement Park.

The city has established a special working group and formulated a project promotion work plan. All relevant departments and parties concerned are taking practical measures to accelerate the advancement of all aspects of this project construction.
