Complete with black and white colors, Sharp flip phone SH7218U for only 2158 yuan

by yanr201txue on 2012-03-08 09:16:05

By Wang Yanan [Beijing Market] On March 5, 2012, the Sharp SH7218U (official version with invoice) was listed at a new price of 2158 yuan by the retailer "Honest Youth Mobile Phone Specialty Store", which is lower than its previous price of 2278 yuan. The accessories include a single battery, single charger, headphones, and data cable as official standard configurations. The Sharp SH7218U is an Android smart 3G mobile phone with a flip design.

The Sharp SH7218U features a flip design, making it simple, stylish, and uncommon among Android phones. It has a 3.4-inch ASV display with a resolution of 854x480 pixels, offering clear and detailed visuals. There's also an 8-megapixel camera on the back that provides excellent shooting capabilities. The system runs on Android 2.2 and contains a Qualcomm Snapdragon MSM7227 processor with a clock speed of 600MHz, ensuring smooth daily use for users.
