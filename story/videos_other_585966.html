With a lot of new tricks, Mercedes-Benz E-class redefined the standards of high-end executive sedans in terms of vehicle insurance, comfort and control, Mercedes-Benz once again proved its position as a global technology pioneer through E-Class cars, adopting a new six-cylinder engine with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The maximum price drop for the Mercedes-Benz E-Class is 1.4 million yuan, and the lowest selling price is 3.85 million yuan. The ancestor of the E-Class is generally recognized to be the Mercedes-Benz 170 in 1947, one of the first models developed by Mercedes after the war. Compared with the 170S of the same era, the shape of the 170 was less decorated, and the equipment was further simplified. In 1953, the improved versions of the 170, the 180 and 190 were introduced. Since then, the Mercedes-Benz E-Class has gradually formed a series, with two types of body styles: sedan and station wagon. The gasoline engine displacement ranges from 1.8 to 2.0 liters, and diesel engines can also be selected. By 1961, a total of 468,000 units of the 170 series had been produced. The domesticated Mercedes-Benz initially came in two models: E200K and E280. However, due to the lower configuration of the E200K and poor sales, it was temporarily discontinued. After increasing the configuration, Mercedes-Benz reintroduced it in China and added a higher-end E350 model, forming the current product line. In fact, with Mercedes-Benz's "Paris-Beijing Long March," the domesticated Mercedes-Benz cars have undergone some minor changes, mainly focusing on the appearance design of the front and rear of the car and interior color details. The modified models look more dynamic.

As one of the most famous executive-level rides, there is no need to elaborate much on the quietness of the new E-Class. Its unique riding experience can only be personally experienced. While ensuring comfort, the new E-Class also has excellent handling. Whether it is on crowded urban streets or highways, driving the new E-Class will make you feel at ease. The heavier accelerator pedal might not be favored by women, but if you keep the RPM above 2000, you can experience the continuous power brought by the turbocharger.

The second-generation gasoline direct injection engine significantly reduces fuel consumption by 10%, while also noticeably increasing output power. Compared to vehicles with traditional supercharged engines, vehicles equipped with turbocharged direct injection engines have a more efficient energy management standard. Its main advantage lies in the average direct injection system; compared to the previous intake port injection system, this injection system significantly improves energy efficiency. The vaporization of fuel in the cylinder lowers the temperature of the combustion chamber, thereby reducing the tendency of engine knock while improving compression efficiency.

A comfortable and suitable atmosphere is a hallmark of the E-Class sedan - thanks in part to the automatic climate control system (THERMATIC) which allows passengers to choose their preferred temperature according to their needs. Additionally, the optional intelligent air conditioning (THERMOTRONIC) luxury automatic control system (standard equipment on the E 500) enables independent temperature control in four zones. When controlling airflow and temperature, the four-zone sunlight sensor takes into account the angle of sunlight on the four seats. A multifunctional sensor monitors the humidity and pollution level of the outside air, and when necessary, the intelligent air conditioning automatically switches to recirculation mode. If an electric tilt/sliding sunroof is selected, it provides even fresher air supply. The sedan can also be optionally equipped with a panoramic sliding sunroof, with a large glass area extending from the front windshield all the way to the rear windshield.
