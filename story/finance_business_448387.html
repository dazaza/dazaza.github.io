Apple iPad2 Sparks Another Craze: Analysis of the Advantages and Disadvantages of Four Purchase Channels

Yesterday, an Apple spokesperson confirmed that the iPad2 would be globally released as scheduled on March 25th, responding to doubts about whether Apple had sufficient supplies of the iPad2 for over 20 countries. Although China was not included in the initial release list, the editor believes that the iPad2 will also be introduced to the domestic market at the first opportunity. It is understood that the first iPad2 entering Beijing's Zhongguancun was priced as high as 9000 RMB, while its price in the US is only $499, equivalent to around 3300 RMB. After removing shipping fees, proxy purchase fees, customs clearance fees, etc., dealers still make substantial profits. Therefore, the purchase channels for the iPad2 have become a focal point of attention for many netizens. For this reason, the editor introduces four purchase channels below for your reference.

The price of the iPad on Apple's US official website

One, Official Website Pre-order

Advantages: Exempts the hassle of queuing, cheaper price

Disadvantages: Need to wait 4-5 weeks for delivery time, requires a local US address, need to go through customs

Two, Domestic Retailers

It is understood that an Asian scalper group paid people to queue up to buy the iPad2. On the day of the launch, there were approximately 200 people queuing outside the flagship store on Fifth Avenue. Within a few minutes of opening, all the iPads inside the store were purchased, thus marking the start of the iPad2 being brought back to China. The price of the first batch of iPads arriving in Zhongguancun reached as high as 9000 RMB.

Advantages: Direct purchase, no waiting

Disadvantages: Extremely high price. Reasons: Paying high queuing fees, paying local tax rates, shipping costs to Hong Kong/Shenzhen regions, customs duties. Finally shipping them nationwide.

Three, Online Proxy Purchase

With the increasingly improved internet payment systems, combined with advanced courier services and lower prices, purchasing through online sales platforms has become the preferred choice for many modern buyers.

Advantages: Cheaper than physical stores

Disadvantages: Most online stores do not have stock, transaction risks exist, shipping can easily result in loss or damage

Four, US Proxy Purchase

Proxy purchases by overseas Chinese compatriots are the most cost-effective. Even if it's the same online proxy purchase, there can be significant price differences, so proxy purchases from the US have become the most economical way to buy.
