"The most outrageous flight attendant" claimed she would kill the policeman

by canxinyang on 2009-06-27 17:24:59

On June 19, 2009, at Chengdu Airport in Sichuan, a flight attendant of "Eagle Union Airlines" wanted to leave after parking her vehicle in violation of regulations at the airport. At this time, a policeman on duty saw her and kindly reminded her to park her vehicle according to the regulations, not to block the passage of vehicles behind, and also take responsibility for the safety of her own vehicle. However, the flight attendant did not pay attention to the policeman and even said arrogantly: I am from a certain airline company, it's none of your business to park at the airport, go away if you have nothing to do!
