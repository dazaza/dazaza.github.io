Life advice for all men and women

by 313754597 on 2009-04-06 15:12:33

For Girls:

Your work plan conflicts with your boyfriend's dating schedule, choose the former... The former will not betray you, and it will be even more so as time goes by, unless your current job is being an escort.

Only little girls use smoking, night wandering, and having multiple boyfriends to show maturity; you don't need to do that.

It's best not to let the man you meet for the first time know where you live. If he insists on seeing you home, then just go to the building entrance...

Believe me, he won't die of thirst if he doesn't "go upstairs for a cup of tea."

If you buy more than ten pieces of clothing each month for six consecutive months, consider buying a house.

Don't leave your phone number for men you meet in bars.

If you don't have a car, don't buy white leather shoes. (Mobile e-book: www.5d1d.net)

If unfortunately, the man you love has another woman, don't linger, leave whenever!

A man's lust is like a lawyer defending a criminal: knowing he's guilty but still defends him... You know what I mean.

Even if you are beautiful like a fairy, you still need to be reasonable.

Love your work, but don't love your boss.

Romance is like a beautiful evening gown, but you can't wear it all day long.

Marrying a rich man is like robbing a bank, the gains are always huge, but the aftermath is endless. If you can avoid trying, it's better not to try.

Don't force a man to lie, he will hate you. Don't take his words seriously, or you will hate him.

No matter how much you love him, you don't need to get breast implants for him.

For Boys:

Be loyal to your girlfriend.

If you don't have a girlfriend, it's better to play tennis, which can also consume physical strength.

You must marry a gentle wife.

If your female friend's husband is a lawyer, doctor, broker, publisher, computer expert... you might as well ask him for advice on buying property, insurance, investment, publishing books, installing software... But remember to bring your wife along every time.

Honesty is a virtue, but you don't have to follow along when your female friends criticize their husbands, or join colleagues in attacking the boss.

A gentleman is a man who knows how to support his own weight with elbows and knees.

To avoid future troubles, only use the best condoms.

For Everyone:

Regard love as a luxury: it's great to have it, but you can still live without it.

Read any contract at least three times before signing... If it's a marriage contract, weigh it carefully.

Go with the flow, but it doesn't mean you shouldn't make efforts.

For your mental and physical health, you may keep a pet. For the pet's mental and physical health, don't keep one... It's said they can also get depression when too lonely.

The fewer people there are, the bigger the refrigerator should be. Mental emptiness can be filled with food. If there's no food in the fridge, it can motivate you to work harder.

Buy insurance.

Keep a thick coat and an umbrella in the office.

Colleagues' flattery is like perfume, you can smell it, but don't drink it.

Never ask this question: "Why don't you love me?"

Nothing or anyone is important enough for you to stay up past midnight thinking.

If you don't have a five-digit appearance fee, don't participate in TV programs like "Very Men and Women."

Smile sincerely, don't fear wrinkles.

When your ex calls to ask how you're doing recently... say fine.

Learn to cook a few good dishes.

Stew soup for yourself on weekends.

At any time, don't drink too much. Cleaning up the mess from the night before in the morning will make you vomit again.

Think about it, then speak. If you don't want to, just say no.

When someone compliments you on looking young, you should still be happy.

Falling in love is like playing mahjong, it's no fun if you don't take it seriously, but taking it too seriously can hurt easily... Cultivate a bit of gaming spirit.

If you really like something, just buy it.

For a lover who once betrayed you and wants to return, say no. For a lover you once betrayed and asks you to return, say no.

Smile while saying yes to requests, and say no clearly and firmly.

The secret to staying young is to have an unquiet heart.

Whether it's lace underwear or work reports, simplicity is the best.

For a wrong promise, you must have the courage to break it.

Nothing should be exchanged for health.

Whether a person is reliable depends entirely on the means you use to control them.

There's no need to argue about anything, just give the result.

Men always flatter women who don't take them seriously... Fate does the same.

Don't borrow money, if you must, borrow from the bank. Don't lend money to others, if you must, don't expect repayment.

If you're fickle, never use your lover's birthday as a password... Changing it often is annoying.

Caring too much about someone often leads to nothing... Money is the same.

Keep a good book by your bedside.

After work, try to do outdoor activities as much as possible.

Don't cry in front of people who want to make you cry.

Get a physical check-up every year to understand your health condition.

Always keep a set of keys at your parents' or close friends' place.

Even if you really don't like children, don't say it out loud.

Don't be curious about how others evaluate you, think about how you evaluate them.

Love is not making love... Love is wanting to sleep with the other person.

Simply put, a person who often makes you smile is a good person.

Don't socialize with people whose moral views differ from yours.

If you don't start respecting your parents now, it will be too late!!

Dignity is like a bra, lifting women to great heights, but if intentionally exposed, it becomes vulgar.

How old you look is how old you actually are.

Don't be obsessed with beautifully packaged things.

Be happy! Remember, you only live once.
