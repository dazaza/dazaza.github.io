More than a hundred citizens competed in Qinhuangdao's first ever "shrimp peeling" competition.

by lwxisjzuh on 2010-05-25 22:29:13

A person in charge of the Tourism Bureau of Qinhuangdao said that, with the implementation and improvement of "establishing a city based on tourism" strategy, the "peeling shrimp contest" of Qinhuangdao would gradually enrich its content and expand its scale. Eventually, it would be developed into Qinhuangdao's tourism characteristic food culture activity brand -- Qinhuangdao Seafood Delicacies Festival.

In the contest, more than a hundred contestants were divided into four groups to take turns on the stage to compete for "shrimp peeling skills" within five minutes. Many citizens used both their hands and mouths to show the old port city people's "shrimp peeling skills", which was amazing. In addition, dozens of types of dishes made from shrimp cooked by the hotel chefs at the Fisherman's Wharf were available for the audience to taste for free. In the end, ten contestants surpassed others and respectively won the titles of "shrimp peeling fast hand", "shrimp peeling expert", and "shrimp peeling master" in the port city, as well as the big prize of seafood gift package.

April 20th is "Grain Rain" in the twenty-four solar terms. The first "shrimp peeling contest" of Qinhuangdao was specially held on this day of "full boats of fresh shrimp" at the Fisherman's Wharf Grand Hotel in Qinhuangdao. More than a hundred citizens, tourists from all over the country, and migrant workers from the Qinhuangdao Electric Power Project Department of China Railway Sixth Bureau's "Tianjin-Qinhuangdao Passenger Dedicated Line" project gathered together to participate in an unprecedented "shrimp peeling" contest while tasting the delicious food of the port city. Each contestant showed their unique skills to make the port city's food famous.

This event was jointly organized by our newspaper, the City Tourism Bureau, Fisherman's Wharf Grand Hotel, City Radio Station, Traffic Station and other departments. It aimed to promote the port city's food culture through the unique Qinhuangdao local food "shrimp peeling" activity, enrich the city's tourism food, attract tourists and citizens to participate, and promote the development of Qinhuangdao's "establishing a city based on tourism". The preparation period of the "shrimp peeling" contest has received much attention from readers and citizens. Many folk "shrimp peeling experts" came to participate in the competition. Our newspaper's previously launched consecutive special feature on "Sixty Ways to Cook Shrimp" was also very popular among readers. Many citizens were amazed: "I have lived by the sea for several decades, and I have never heard of so many ways to eat shrimp."

At the invitation of our newspaper, the migrant workers from the Qinhuangdao Electric Power Project Department of China Railway Sixth Bureau's "Tianjin-Qinhuangdao Passenger Dedicated Line" project, who contributed to the development of Qinhuangdao, were invited as special guests to participate in the competition with citizens and tourists. A contestant from the community specially taught the railway builders his unique "shrimp peeling skills" before the competition, hoping that these heroes could also experience the port city's food culture and spread the reputation of the port city's food across the country along with their footsteps.
