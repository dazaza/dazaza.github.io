"Love Apartment 3" is mainly about the return season, with two beauties at the opening show.

by tvbing21 on 2011-08-13 22:37:18

"Love Apartment 3" is mainly about the return season. At the opening ceremony show, the return of two beauties - Mei Jia and Wan Yu - finally brought back the original cast of the first season to the stage. The appearance of Chen Mei Jia sparked the highest climax of this launch show. As one of the actors with the highest call for return, Li Jinming, who played her, had briefly left the crew in the second season due to contract issues. This return can be said to be highly anticipated, and Li Jinming herself shed tears several times. Just when it seemed like the press conference was coming to an end, Zhao Ji as Lin Wan Yu suddenly appeared, causing a wave of exclamations on site. Originally, the eight-person combination wasn't just a legend. Thus, all the main actors of "Love Apartment 3" finally returned to their positions.

At the press conference, Mei Jia's return stirred up various emotions in the audience. After all, someone who once had conflicts with the crew to the point of issuing mutual statements, now returning and saying 'I give Mei Jia back to you,' is indeed very touching.

However, Tang Youyou was truly pitiful before. When the promotional video was played, the calmness below the stage was noticeable...

After going on stage, after a few calls of names, it was quiet...

It turned out that, both inside and outside the drama, she was treated the same way - working hard but not being appreciated, destined to be a bit-part actress.

Personally, I think the most outstanding new character from the second season was Tang Youyou. Of course, the setting of Lu Zijiao's aunt was rather awkward.

Zhang Wei, I feel, has problems with the script setting, making the entire character lack appeal. Imagine, if this character was designed with more obsessive justice and more miserly tendencies (instead of having to flip through legal books as a lawyer or even a trainee), this character might have been richer.

As for Qin Yumo, originally the design of her being a mistress was quite impressive, but apart from this (which also credited to the script), there was nothing particularly noteworthy. The screenwriter's approach was clearly designed as the second generation of Wan Yu, making her completely unremarkable, and the actress's own temperament far from reaching the standard of Wan Yu's second generation. If the background of the mistress was strengthened and described as an ordinary girl who admires vanity and materialism, contrasting her with Zhang Wei would have made for some good drama.

Tang Youyou's performance was not particularly outstanding, but with her presence, the arrangement of the play-within-a-play was quite interesting, and should still be present in the third part.

Zhang Yiduo's stingy lawyer setting is pretty good, and his acting brings a lot of laughter, which is why many people chase him calling him Zhang Yiduo~

Anyway, gathering the original cast under the banner of the return season, and decisively eliminating the two failed newcomers, Love Apartment 3 is worth looking forward to.
