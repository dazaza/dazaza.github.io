Young woman lost her life for 20 minutes of pleasure

by uins9492 on 2010-04-18 23:04:30

A well-dressed young man drove a BMW 735 to a bicycle repair stall, scraping a bike that was awaiting repair.

The woman slowly got out of the car and asked the repairman to compensate for the damage, hurling insults at him. She claimed that the bicycle had scratched her BMW. However, the repairman firmly disagreed, explaining that it was her vehicle that had collided with an object outside his area, and she should bear the primary responsibility. The well-dressed woman refused to back down and pushed the repairman. As the repairman raised his hand to stop her, he accidentally soiled her clothes. Upon this unexpected turn of events, the woman became even more relentless, demanding compensation not only for the car but also insisting on 3000 yuan for her clothes.

As the situation escalated, many onlookers gathered, some attempting to mediate. The repairman, unable to tolerate any more, apologized to the woman and offered to clean her clothes. However, the well-dressed woman showed no mercy, continuing to insult both the repairman and those who tried to intervene, all while recording the scene on her phone. This decision would prove to be one she would regret for the rest of her life, as her life was now measured in less than an hour.

The woman called for help from none other than her husband, and they lived in a nearby upscale community. Upon arriving at the scene, her husband did not inquire about the details of the incident but immediately grabbed a bicycle pump from the ground and struck the repairman's head repeatedly. Blood gushed from the repairman’s head instantly.

Some onlookers began to criticize the man's actions and attempted to mediate, but he threatened anyone who dared to approach. He continued to kick the repairman lying on the ground as his daughter stood by, berating those who spoke up for the repairman. Meanwhile, the well-dressed woman remained inside the air-conditioned BMW, watching the unfolding drama smugly.

After a few minutes, the father and daughter grew tired of hitting and yelling. The woman said to the repairman: "If you don’t see 3000 yuan within fifteen minutes, you'll never work here again. Your life isn't worth much; get rid of you, so others won’t see you..." The repairman struggled to rise from the ground, spat out a mouthful of blood, and weakly replied: "Wait, help is coming." He then staggered towards the poorer district near their upscale community. At this point, the emotions of all involved were vastly different, but each had taken a path from which there was no return.

About ten minutes later, the repairman returned to the scene and approached the woman’s husband from behind. Just as the man turned around, the repairman swiftly drew his left hand from his pocket. Instead of money, it held a gleaming melon knife. In a flash, he stabbed the man in the throat and delivered two more strikes in the same spot. The man fell silently to the ground. The repairman then quickly moved to the woman and stabbed her three times. Not satisfied, the repairman dragged the still-shocked well-dressed woman out of the BMW, stabbing her multiple times before tossing her body to the side of the road.

Minutes later, the police and ambulances arrived at the scene. The perpetrator was arrested without resistance. The three lives that had been so recently full of vitality were lost before any medical assistance could be provided. Four lives were lost over such a trivial matter. Was the perpetrator excessively cruel, or did the deceased bring it upon themselves? The witnesses were unanimous in their opinion.

This incident, which occurred in Fushun, Liaoning, was a tragedy and a profound social issue!! Was it worth losing precious lives for the fleeting satisfaction of twenty minutes of verbal abuse?

Certainly, a close friend once remarked: "In today's world, anger and resentment are everywhere." Indeed, this pervasive atmosphere of hostility seems to have quietly developed and intensified over time.
