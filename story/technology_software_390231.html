March of the online store

by anonymous on 2011-03-25 14:44:58

My online recharge store (http://shop64131029.taobao.com/) has been running for exactly three months from its establishment until today. I deliberated for a long time before finally deciding to open the store. From having nothing to now achieving one-diamond credibility, only I know what I've gone through.

To be honest, the threshold for starting an online store is quite low, but it all depends on whether you can persist. In order to build credibility and minimize investment, at the beginning, I acted as a middleman for Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., just to gain some experience without any profit. After the Spring Festival, I made up my mind to buy software to become an official agent. Taking advantage of the software's special mechanism, I adopted a strategy of losing money to attract customers, which significantly increased my credibility. Finally, I reached one diamond, and the feeling was truly amazing. However, I must thank my friends for their encouragement. They shopped at my store when I needed it the most, helping me take the first step.

I originally thought that reaching diamond-level credibility would improve things, but then I realized I had started too late. Virtual items are already everywhere online. When I wanted to switch to physical goods, I found that sourcing was a problem. Without geographical advantages and considering shipping costs, I couldn't compete with sellers from the south. I read many success stories, but none of them were really suitable for me. This made me realize that successful cases are merely a form of boasting by those who have succeeded, as everyone takes a different path. Since there's nothing to learn from others, it's better to explore on my own and achieve my own goals.

It’s been exactly three months, during which I spent a great deal of time building this store, yet I still don’t know how the future will turn out. I hope my friends will continue to support me, visit more often http://shop64131029.taobao.com/, and even if you don't need anything, come take a look, add it to your favorites, so I can keep moving forward with confidence.
