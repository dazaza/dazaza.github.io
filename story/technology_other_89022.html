"放牛班"的星星

by zobuy on 2008-09-03 15:07:43

Xia Zhi Xing, a 24-year-old female fraudster, was granted parole. A woman who went to jail for love has finally waited until today to meet her lover, number thirteen. She even specially went to a six-star hotel to pick out a gift.

Today is the wedding day of E.SHINE Group's second young master Zhong Tianqi's former lover Ou Ya Ruo and her brother Zhong Tianjun, as well as the auction for the cutting of their mother's heirloom blue diamond, "QUEEN MARY". Zhong Tianqi, who deliberately avoided the wedding, was forcibly brought back from the airport by Monster. He sneaked into the backstage of the wedding and pulled Ya Ruo into his arms, kissing her. At the same time, he stole QUEEN MARY... partly to get revenge on Ya Ruo, and partly because he didn't want his mother's legacy jewelry to become an auction item.

With QUEEN MARY lost, the wedding was halted. By a twist of fate, security mistook the thief that Ya Ruo referred to as Xia Zhi Xing, handcuffing Xia Zhi Xing. Unwilling to implicate the innocent, Tianqi returned, pulling away Xia Zhi Xing. Thinking quickly, Xia Zhi Xing quickly handcuffed the other end to Tianqi. This fateful encounter happened as Xia Zhi Xing, who had just been paroled for taking the blame for her lover, and E-Shine Group's second young master "Zhong Tianqi", who operates a jewelry business, fled with their hands cuffed together. This handcuffing created an unbreakable bond.

On the run, A Xing worried that Tianqi might harm her. Having just been released from prison, she feared being labeled as a jewel thief, shouting at Tianqi: "Let me go immediately...", and threatening Tianqi to call her boyfriend, number thirteen, thinking it would scare him off. Unexpectedly, the call was hung up in panic. In front of the small house where they once loved, A Xing failed to find Brother Thirteen. Dragging the cuffed-together Tianqi, she rushed to the address on the invitation given by the landlord. It turned out that Thirteen had concealed his past with A Xing and was about to marry Ming Mei.

As A Xing questioned Thirteen, Ming Mei, Thirteen's wife, went into premature labor. The kind-hearted A Xing felt awkward and angry, realizing that words were futile as this man no longer had feelings for her. But she wouldn't lose face, claiming Tianqi was her boyfriend and they were almost getting married, asking Thirteen to bless them too. On the same day they both lost something precious, they became friends in adversity, drinking heavily.

A Xing used a hairpin to unlock the handcuffs. This was a trick she learned in prison. A Xing was completely drunk, so Tianqi had to half-carry, half-drag her into a motel to rest. The motel employee found it suspicious and suspected Tianqi of being a rapist, calling the police. Tianqi's family came to bail him out. A Xing and Tianqi should have parted ways then, but Tianqi was enlightened by A Xing's words and decided to go to "Tomorrow Town" with her to explore ten thousand possibilities in life.

Because of Tianqi's impulsiveness, the Zhong family's business was in dire straits... even causing a crisis of forced confession by the board, demanding Tianjun find the lost blue diamond within a certain period... When the Zhong family was in chaos over the engagement banquet, a photo shocked Zhong Tianjun. It was taken by paparazzi at the wedding banquet, showing Ya Ruo passionately kissing her younger brother Tianqi in the lounge. The paparazzi asked for a gag fee, shocking Tianjun internally as he never knew about the relationship between his younger brother and Ya Ruo. Tianjun began to doubt, who does Ya Ruo truly love?...

Just as the suspicion towards fiancée Ya Ruo caused a shock in Tianjun's heart, Tianqi and A Xing boarded a bus heading to Tomorrow Town. Two strangers tightly connected by chance saw the seeds of love slowly sprouting, and it seemed like ten thousand possibilities in life were about to unfold. However, they didn't know that more tests lay ahead...
