I think, perhaps dreams can only be illusory and beautiful, and we, still lost to departure------

Night deepened, I looked up at the drifting sky, it was as dirty darkness as my name, but I did not know that I could not see clearly. Unknowingly, a crystalline tear dropped from my eyes. It was another piece of unforgettable memory that I had lost.

A solo song praising something incomprehensible, a certain melody of the song or from the city would make me think of myself during that period of time, and my heart seemed like being shattered into pieces....

Looking back at the past, I didn't recall more things that I had lost, which should mean that I have forgotten them in this autumn. I no longer saw you coming in the crowd, I just silently wished you well. Wherever you are is your happy place, a place I cannot take you to, because there are people you need.

I was drowned in the sea, spending 365 days alone, my thoughts were still so messy every day, I pretended to be strong every day, but I passed through sadness.

Lost love

In fact, when you left, I started to learn how to forget. We are free now, you are even freer. The once so strong waiting ended up with the same result - leaving, unable to be retained by anything. Your departure became an eternal wound, life encounters are originally a countdown to separation, why bother to investigate the length of time!!!!!!

Farewell to that dead love!

Now, you have found happiness, forget those sad stories, be happy for you, the sorrowful happiness has been taken away by you long ago. I have hidden myself in life for a year, and escaped all the things in life, just don't want to be sad anymore, because I realized that I really couldn't afford to be hurt, the promises you said before were all false, no longer thinking about the results nor asking for reasons, just like leaves slowly drifting away with the wind....

Farewell, to that dead love, the rise and fall of fate is like a breeze, crying, laughing, and hurting were all a dream, eventually you and I became passersby. Life's tragedies always leave some imperfections, love always makes people cry and feel unsatisfied, the sky is vast but unclear, so lonely....

Everyone will have different emotional experiences in their lives, liking someone or loving someone, the feelings in one's heart can only be known by oneself. As for how fate arranges a relationship, we can only submit to it!!!!!!!
