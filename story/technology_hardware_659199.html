February 5, Beijing time, the twelfth round of six matches of the national men's volleyball league in the 2011-2012 season were played today. The two strong teams Bayi and Shanghai defeated Shandong and Beijing teams with scores of 3-0 and 3-1 respectively, continuing to lead the points table. Liaoning also achieved its first victory in the second stage after completely defeating Henan.

Group 1 (1st to 8th place):

Bayi 3-0 Shandong

The match between Bayi and Shandong men's volleyball teams this round was highly anticipated due to the participation of several new and veteran national team players. Despite having more national team players, Bayi still managed to win all three sets with more solid strength and stable performance. This victory brought Bayi their tenth consecutive win while giving Shandong their fourth consecutive loss.

In the first set, Bayi used the opponent's continuous mistakes to take the lead after reaching a 5-all tie. Xu Jingtao's quick attack helped Bayi enter the first technical pause with an 8-5 lead. After the pause, Liu Tingwei and Xu Jingtao's powerful attacks and fast balls successively scored, along with Zhao Yichen's effective service, increasing their leading advantage to five points and entering the second technical pause at 16-12. After the pause, Shandong's powerful attacks were constrained by Bayi's high block, resulting in a lower hit rate. Bayi took advantage of this situation to create a small climax at the end of the set and won the first set 25-18.

After winning the first set, Bayi started the second set even stronger. Their serves and powerful attacks frequently hit the mark, taking an early lead of 10-2. Eventually, they locked down the second set with a significant score of 25-10, leading 2-0 overall. In the third set, Bayi entered the first technical pause leading 8-6. However, after the pause, Bayi made more mistakes, allowing Shandong to seize the opportunity and score five consecutive points to take the lead. Both teams then experienced noticeable fluctuations. After Zhong Weijun's spiking mistake gave away a point, Shandong entered the second technical pause leading 16-13. After the pause, Bayi alleviated the issue of too many offensive errors, once scoring five consecutive points to take the lead at 21-19. Although Shandong caught up to 22-all at the last moment, Zhong Weijun's three successful powerful attacks helped Bayi win the third set 25-23, thus defeating Shandong 3-0 and achieving ten consecutive wins.

In this match, national team player Zhong Weijun alone scored 21 points, becoming the biggest contributor to Bayi's victory. Shandong only had one player, Kou Zhichao, scoring over ten points with 12 points.

Shanghai 3-1 Beijing

The defending champion Shanghai men's volleyball team faced off against the "big dark horse" Beijing team in a focal match of this round. However, the more experienced Shanghai team ultimately won 3-1. After this match, Shanghai's winning streak increased to five games.

Shanghai replaced Li Chunhui and Yang Jianbin with Dai Qingyao and Chen Longhai in the starting lineup for this game. In the first set, Shanghai relied on Shen Qiong, Fang Yingchao, and Dai Qingyao's powerful attacks to take the lead and entered the first technical pause leading 8-6. After the pause, Beijing began to retaliate with powerful attacks, even Shanghai's serve and attack transmission continuous mistakes once gave Beijing the chance to surpass the score, after tying at 15-all, Beijing scored three consecutive points to lead 18-15. However, the trailing Shanghai did not appear overly anxious, relying on powerful attacks from both wings, high-blocking pressure, and Dai Qingyao's two consecutive direct-scoring serves, creating a 5-1 small climax to successfully reverse the score. In the final crucial stage, Beijing's multiple mistakes ultimately led to their failure with a 22-25 loss in the first set.

In the second set, Shanghai performed strongly in the early stages, once leading 5-1 and 7-2, and entered the first technical pause leading 8-4. However, after the pause, Beijing, trailing 10-13, scored six consecutive points to successfully achieve a reversal. Despite Shanghai continuously catching up with the score in the final stages and even successfully saving a set point, Beijing's powerful attacks continued to hit, allowing them to tie the score at 26-24.

In the subsequent third set, Shanghai started Li Chunhui and Zhan Guojun in the replacement and setter positions respectively. However, Beijing led 8-7 and 16-14 at the two technical pauses. After the second technical pause, Beijing's lead over Shanghai reached three points. However, Shen Qiong, Li Chunhui's powerful attacks, and Shanghai's blocking continuously showed their prowess, allowing them to catch up with the score at the final crucial moment. Despite Li Chunhui's spiking mistake giving Beijing a set point at 23-all, Li Chunhui's two consecutive successful spiking and Beijing's mistakes ultimately allowed Shanghai to lock down the third set victory at 26-24.

In the critical fourth set, Shanghai entered the first technical pause leading 8-6 in the early stages. However, after the pause, Beijing, trailing 8-12 by four points, created a 5-0 small climax through Wang Chen's powerful attacks and Shanghai's continuous mistakes to successfully reverse the score. After entering the second technical pause leading 16-14, Beijing further advanced to increase their leading advantage to 18-14. Fortunately, Wang Zhiting's powerful attacks and serving scores, along with Beijing's continuous mistakes, allowed Shanghai to create a 6-2 small climax to tie the score at 20-all. At the final moments, Shanghai's continuous serving scores and Beijing's serving mistakes also allowed Shanghai to first obtain the match point. With Wang Chen's powerful attack failing, Shanghai ultimately won the fourth set 25-22, thus defeating Beijing 3-1 to gain the full match victory.

In this match, five players from Shanghai scored double digits, with Shen Qiong collecting the highest 20 points for the entire team. Four players from Beijing also scored double digits, with Wang Chen obtaining the highest 27 points for the entire game.

Liaoning 3-0 Henan

In the first set, the two teams competed fiercely in the early stages. After tying at 7-all, Henan led into the first technical pause through Ji Zhe's quick ball hitting. After the pause, Liaoning tied the score twice to successfully reverse the lead, but immediately gained five consecutive points again to take the lead and entered the second technical pause leading 16-11. Fortunately, under the trailing situation, Liaoning's offensive hit rate was higher, combined with excellent performances in the blocking phase and more opponent's mistakes, Liaoning gradually narrowed the gap with the opponent. After successfully tying the score at 22-all, Yuan Zhi's powerful attack scoring and Henan's mistakes ultimately allowed Liaoning to come from behind and win the first set 25-23.

In the subsequent second set, Liaoning relied on Yuan Zhi and Song Yuchen's powerful attacks and the opponent's mistakes to score five consecutive points and surpass the score when trailing 6-7. Despite Henan tying the score again through Cui Jianjun's powerful attacks and Liaoning's mistakes later, after tying at 15-all, Song Yuchen's powerful attacks continuously hit, helping Liaoning regain the lead at 18-15. With Yuan Zhi's successful attack, Liaoning ultimately won the second set 25-23.

In the third set, Henan launched a counterattack in a desperate situation. They initially led 5-2 and entered the first technical pause leading by two points at 8-6. However, after the pause, Liaoning maintained a high hit rate in both powerful attacks and quick attacks, preventing Henan from widening the gap. Despite Henan leading 16-15 into the second technical pause in the middle stage, after the pause, Liaoning achieved a critical reversal through a 5-0 small climax. Ultimately, Liaoning locked down the third set victory at 25-20, defeating Henan 3-0 and ending their opponent's five-game winning streak.

In this match, Liaoning's Song Yuchen scored the highest 17 points in the entire game, while veteran Yuan Zhi also contributed 12 points. Sun Kai from Henan scored the highest 14 points for his team, while Cui Jianjun performed poorly, only gaining 7 points.

Guangdong 1-3 Zhejiang

In the match between Guangdong and Zhejiang men's volleyball teams, despite Guangdong playing at home and performing at a high level, causing considerable trouble for the opponent, Zhejiang ultimately reversed the situation after losing the first set, winning three consecutive sets. The scores for the four sets were 23-25, 30-28, 25-20, and 25-23 respectively. Jin Zhihong from Zhejiang scored the highest 22 points in the entire game.

Group 2 (9th to 12th place):

In the two matches between the 9th and 12th places, Jiangsu fought hard for five sets and ultimately defeated Sichuan 3-2. The scores for the five sets were 25-16, 21-25, 25-19, 18-25, and 15-9 respectively. Zhang Yubo from Sichuan scored the highest 20 points in the entire game, while Chen Ping from Jiangsu scored the highest 17 points for his team without playing the entire game; in another match, Fujian men's volleyball team playing at home defeated Hebei team 3-1. The scores for the four sets were 25-22, 21-25, 26-24, and 25-17 respectively.

On the points table, Bayi team leads with 7 wins and 1 loss accumulating 19 points, while Shanghai team remains in second place with the same 19 points due to one less win. Beijing team ranks third with 5 wins and 3 losses accumulating 14 points, while Zhejiang team surpasses Henan to rise to the third position with 5 wins and 3 losses accumulating 13 points. Henan team ranks fourth with 3 wins and 5 losses accumulating 12 points, Liaoning team ranks sixth with 4 wins and 4 losses accumulating 11 points, Shandong ranks seventh with 2 wins and 6 losses accumulating 7 points, and Guangdong ranks eighth with 0 wins and 8 losses accumulating only 1 point. In the relegation group, Jiangsu team ranks first with 4 wins and 0 losses accumulating 11 points, Sichuan team ranks second with 2 wins and 2 losses accumulating 7 points, Fujian team ranks third with 2 wins and 2 losses accumulating 6 points, and Hebei team ranks last with 0 points.

The thirteenth round of the men's volleyball league will be held on February 9. In the top eight matches, Bayi and Shanghai will continue to play at home against Beijing and Henan respectively, Liaoning will face Zhejiang at home, and the other match will be between Guangdong and Shandong. In the two matches between the 9th and 12th places, Jiangsu and Hebei will move home to face Fujian and Sichuan respectively.
