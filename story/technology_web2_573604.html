Miaoxiu: A picture social sharing app with interesting features

by daodao on 2011-11-21 05:11:11

Miaoxiu is a completely free picture social networking and sharing application that provides powerful image processing and entertaining drawing beautification functions. Miaoxiu is an image social networking and sharing app based on the Android platform; in addition to social elements similar to Instagram, such as making friends through pictures, its biggest highlight lies in the powerful image effect processing and entertaining drawing functions it offers, allowing users to freely doodle on photos, place text bubbles, frames, and various rich and fun decorative elements. Moreover, all these interesting decorations are not permanently fixed but can be continuously downloaded to the device according to the user's needs.

For overly homogeneous internet products and the teams behind them, I always wish I could be more angry, hoping to expose their misdeeds so that more people can join me in seeing the fake indignation of these "bad guys" when they are condemned by all. I hope to use this almost cruel way towards entrepreneurs to guide and promote the rise of some innovative forces. However, due to various reasons, I did not take this route, at most ignoring them and refraining from writing articles to introduce them.

Actually, what I want to say here is, is innovation really that difficult? For the image social sharing application market which was once a blue ocean a year ago and has now turned into a red ocean, almost no one would think that there would be any opportunity for entrepreneurs who came in later. However, Miaoxiu has indeed made me feel refreshed. I am not saying that Miaoxiu will cause a big sensation, but I can clearly tell my friends in one sentence why they should try it out? What is the biggest difference between it and Instagram? —— Not only does it make the pictures shared in the community more exquisite, but it also makes the pictures more interesting! And this kind of grassroots micro-innovation and micro-mixing, isn't it already reaching the difficulty level of ascending to heaven for many domestic entrepreneurs?!…
