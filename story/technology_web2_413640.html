>>> Question One: Do you love me?

>>> Wrong Answer A: "Yes, I do."

>>> Wrong Answer B: "Do I need to answer that?"

>>> Wrong Answer C: "Are you annoying or what?"

>>> Standard Answer: Gaze at her with loving eyes for three seconds, then solemnly nod your head while emitting a 'Mmm' sound, and then pull her into your arms...

>>> Analysis: Answer A will make her feel like you're too casual about it and just messing around; Answer B will make her feel like your love isn't firm enough, and she'll definitely nag you for a clear answer until you give her one, which will be quite troublesome for you; Answer C will surely lead to a big fight or silent treatment. In fact, women have a pretty good idea in their hearts whether men really love them or not. She's just asking this out of coquetry. Hugging her and kissing her would make her happier than any verbal response.

>>> Question Two: Do you think this dress looks good on me?

>>> Problematic Answer A: "It looks pretty good."

>>> Wrong Answer B: "It's okay."

>>> Wrong Answer C: "It looks great, my wife looks good in anything."

>>> Standard Answer: Come on, turn around so I can see... After she shyly turns around, pull her over, take her hand, smile at her and say, "Actually, it looks pretty bad."

>>> Analysis: Answer A will make her feel like you're just going through the motions; Answer B will make her feel inadequate because in her mind, whatever she wears should look good to you; but if you simply say it looks good as in Answer C, she may think you're just being insincere with sweet talk. So, you need to show some seriousness about the question she raises, and you must carefully examine it before saying anything.

>>> Question Three: After introducing her best friend to you, when she gets home, she asks: "What do you think of my friend?"

>>> Wrong Answer A: "She's very beautiful."

>>> Wrong Answer B: "Not much, she doesn't come close to you."

>>> Wrong Answer C: "I didn't pay much attention to her."

>>> Standard Answer: I think she is very sincere towards you, and such a friend should be cherished.

>>> Analysis: Answer A is purely asking for trouble, as women absolutely cannot tolerate hearing you praise another woman in front of them (of course, mothers are an exception); Answer B sounds too fake; Answer C might seem clever to you, but she won't believe it at all, and she will start imagining why you don't want to evaluate her friend. Therefore, avoid the direct question and answer indirectly, making her feel that everything you do is for her, giving her a sense of being cared for and protected.

>>> Question Four: Do you think I've gained weight?

In fact, in women's hearts, they hope men don't care about their weight too much. Though they might say they're dieting for you, it's actually for themselves to look more beautiful and wear more fashionable clothes. So, showing with physical affection that you like her with a little meat is enough.

>>> Question Seven: If I grow old and become unattractive, will you still love me?

>>> Wrong Answer A: "Impossible, you'll still be beautiful even when you're old."

>>> Wrong Answer B: "Women have charm at different stages of life..."

>>> Wrong Answer C: "Don't worry, when you're old, I won't be looking good either, neither of us should despise the other."

>>> Analysis: Answer A might make her happy, but she knows it's absolutely impossible; Answer B, please stop, it's like Tang Monk talking; Answer C, isn't that just saying she will definitely be unattractive when she's old? How could she accept that? Therefore, the smartest answer is to both reassure her and let her know how much you care about her and how afraid you are of losing her.
