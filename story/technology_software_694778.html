by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-07 11:35:42

Inspired by the various popular "creative writing styles" on the internet recently, I have written an entertaining essay titled "Refuse Mediocrity" which I hope will bring some fun to parents and students and reduce some pressure.

Private Fleeing Style - "Refuse Mediocrity"

Dear friends and relatives, I have abandoned everything, including the laziness, idleness, mediocrity, and inaction that have accompanied me since birth, and fled with a sense of unwillingness. This life may not necessarily achieve extraordinary excellence, but it will definitely strive for progress and continuous struggle. Thank you all for your care and help over the years, wish you all happiness! Unable to face everyone's expectations and trust, unable to explain to everyone, and also embarrassed, hence leaving without saying goodbye. Please forgive me! Mediocrity bows.

About Private Fleeing Style: Wang Gongquan, a senior figure in the investment banking circle, announced his private fleeing on Weibo late at night on May 16th, abandoning everything for love, causing a huge wave on the Internet, and netizens followed suit, creating the Private Fleeing Style. Wang Gongquan's private fleeing Weibo: "Dear relatives and colleagues, I have abandoned everything and fled privately with Wang Qin. Thank you all for your care and help over the years, wish you all happiness! Unable to face everyone's expectations and trust, unable to explain to everyone, and also embarrassed, hence leaving without saying goodbye. Please forgive me! Gongquan bows."

3Q Style - "Refuse Mediocrity"

When you see this article, we have just made a very difficult decision. Before the Ministry of Education stops neglecting physical education and music and does not include them in the college entrance examination, we have decided to stop mediocre answers on all college entrance examination papers. We deeply understand that this will cause certain inconvenience to the grading group, and we sincerely apologize to you. For twelve years, we have had the privilege of growing up with various test papers; in the future, we look forward to continuing our journey with various test papers.

3Q Style: Originating from the dispute between 360 and QQ, a letter sent by 360: When you see this letter, we have just made a very difficult decision. Before 360 stops invading and maliciously slandering QQ, we have decided to stop running QQ software on computers with 360 software installed. We deeply understand that this will cause you certain inconvenience, and we sincerely apologize...

Bao Dai Style - "Refuse Mediocrity"

Baoyu approached Daiyu, sat beside her, and after examining her carefully, asked: "Sister, did you participate in the college entrance examination?" Daiyu replied: "No, I only went on Weibo for a while and learned a little about the composition topic." Baoyu then asked: "What is your Weibo ID?" Daiyu told him. Then he asked: "Have you ever written about refusing mediocrity?" Daiyu thought about it, considering that he must have written it, so she answered: "No, I think mediocrity is a rare thing, how could everyone write about it." Baoyu became frenzied and tore up his admission ticket: "What rare thing, it doesn't distinguish between high and low! Even a celestial sister like you hasn't written it, I won't write it either!" Grandma Jia embraced Baoyu: "Wretch! Why destroy that life root? You were born precious, how can you associate with mediocrity!!"

The original text of "Dream of Red Mansions": Baoyu approached Daiyu, sat beside her, and after examining her carefully, asked: "Sister, have you studied?" Daiyu replied: "No, I only went to school for a year and recognized a few words." Baoyu then asked: "What is your honorable name?" Daiyu told him her name. Then he asked Daiyu: "Do you have a jade or not?" Everyone didn't understand his words. Daiyu thought about it, considering that he had a jade, so she asked if she had one or not, and answered: "I don't have that. I think the jade is a rare thing, how could everyone have it." After hearing this, Baoyu immediately went into a frenzy, took off the jade, and threw it hard, cursing: "What rare thing, it doesn't distinguish between high and low, and still talks about 'spiritual' or not! I don't want this nonsense either!" Grandma Jia was anxious and hugged Baoyu: "Wretch! If you're angry, it's easy to beat or scold someone, why destroy that life root!"

Gu Long Style - "Refuse Mediocrity"

A solitary lamp burns like a bean.

A world-class swordsman, dressed in white as snow, with a sword like autumn water.

A tall figure, slightly hunched, is the only person in this small tavern standing drinking wine wearing a long robe.

"Are you waiting for someone called Ye Gucheng? He won't come."

"You dare talk to me like that, believe it or not, I'll kill you!"

After the sword light flashes, a fennel bean becomes nine.

"Good swordsmanship," the man in the long robe praised, "But, do you know how many ways to write 'fennel'?"

"..."

"The more characters I recognize, the more I find myself ignorant; the higher your swordsmanship, the further away you feel from being the best in the world."

"Who are you?"

"A long time ago, a Mr. Zhou told me, a swordsman is not about killing people, but saving people; a scholar is not about becoming an expert professor, but contributing to society. What's wrong with being mediocre?"

From then on, Ximen gave up the sword, no longer using a sword but still practicing swordsmanship, wandering as a knight-errant, and ten years later became famous throughout the world.
