Geng Phone has no highlights. For 2780 yuan, you're basically buying Han Geng.

by anonymous on 2013-11-17 19:27:01

Han Geng, a man who causes countless girls to scream at his every appearance. Han Geng is a singer and an actor. As the first foreign artist to debut in Korea, he was once the only Chinese member of Super Junior. After parting ways with his Korean company and returning to China, Han Geng began developing as a multi-faceted artist. Besides being a singer, he has also starred in several TV series and movies. However, all this seems to be insufficient for Han Geng, as he has now ventured into the mobile phone industry and today launched a phone product named 'Geng Phone'.

No Highlights in Hardware Configuration

It is understood that from design and development to its final form, Han Geng was almost fully involved in the creation of the Geng Phone. Like most current smartphones, the Geng Phone is also a large-screen Android 4.2 operating system phone. This phone has a thickness of 8.4 mm, equipped with a MediaTek MT6589T quad-core processor with a clock speed of 1.5 GHz, and features a 5-inch HD screen with a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. In addition, the Geng Phone boasts a 13-megapixel main camera and a 5-megapixel front-facing camera, with a standard configuration of 32GB of storage for users to store various data.

From the official information released, it appears that the Geng Phone's hardware configuration does not offer any standout features and cannot be considered mainstream among currently available smartphone products.

Geng Elements as the Biggest Selling Point

Since the hardware isn't particularly impressive, there must be something else that is attractive. After all, fans aren't fools; no one would pay several thousand yuan for a phone based solely on such hardware specifications. The most appealing aspect of the Geng Phone is that it is a Han Geng customized phone. And this customization is not as simple as creating a few Han Geng themes or making a phone case with Han Geng's image. This Geng Phone offers privileges unavailable to other phone users, including a personal center, Han Geng milestones, a Geng Phone forum, and a theme store.

Owners of the Geng Phone will activate their Han Geng membership code in the personal center to enjoy exclusive services. For example, instead of an alarm clock waking you up every morning, it will be Han Geng...’s voice. On your birthday, you will also receive a voice blessing from Han Geng. Additionally, within the built-in Han Geng milestones app, you can watch videos exclusively premiered by Han Geng. Through the Geng Phone forum on the phone, you can interact with other fans who like Han Geng and use the same phone, and you can even participate preferentially in Han Geng-related activities.

Besides these built-in functions, the developers of the Geng Phone have even thought of using the phone to replace glow sticks. By integrating a yellow background with the character "Geng," the phone can serve as a glow stick during concerts when fans collectively wave their phones. Of course, this scenario can only be seen at Han Geng's concerts.

Paying 2780 Yuan is Not for the Phone, but for the Idol

The Geng Phone is available in three color versions: black, white, and yellow. Among them, the special edition in "Geng fan yellow" is limited to 5000 units and will be available for pre-order on Amazon China at exactly 14:09 on November 15th, Han Geng's birthday, priced at 2780 yuan. In fact, if you compare it slightly on e-commerce websites, it becomes clear that the Geng Phone offers no cost-performance advantage compared to other products in the same price range. It remains to be seen how many Geng fans will be willing to purchase this phone.
