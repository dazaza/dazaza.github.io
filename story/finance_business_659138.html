A year of reality, old and fading - often resentful and suspicious

by 2209dtae on 2012-02-05 14:26:38

The literal and ornate words, with the passage of time, have gradually detached from their original meaning to people. Unconsciously, we also began to imperceptibly diminish the "resentment against the ground" that forged a bright soul - like wandering in the wild fields of Changde Real Estate Network; our classmates from days past, the women among them are fighting for their careers, sincerely nurturing their love; the men slowly entered the early halls of marriage, perhaps having found the fruits of their love. Moreover, the most exciting news is that my sister's early marriage plans have finally come to fruition. As her older brother, I've actually fallen behind! Ha ha.

Today, when I suddenly saw this photo of a classmate at the entrance of the foreign language department, I realized there was so much I needed to reflect on. As I recognized my own maturity, I also realized how much I had lost, just like the two Soviet-style red buildings inside Yi Wei High School, which, while enduring the vicissitudes of time, have also gradually shed their former red paint.

In fact, nothing is absolute. The vows of life and death, meeting and parting may sound romantic, but in reality, who can truly live up to them? In the face of life and death, once an unexpected event occurs, everything becomes a memory. Such is life, such is reality, and such are promises as well (Changde Information Port). Sometimes, people make mistakes, or forgive, or it might just be a matter of a few words, but its starting point, process, outcome, and impact are all very important.

In truth, friends are valued not by quantity but quality, based on genuine emotions rather than superficial benefits (Changde Map). Being honest and open, if you see me as your closest friend, I would die for you, especially regarding these brothers of mine. Actually, I feel quite guilty. During the Spring Festival, when you proposed a reunion, I was drinking alone on a mountain. My regret isn't about you not keeping me company, but that I couldn't join you to uphold our bond of righteousness. You may not count me as one less, but every time I think about those proposals, those hazy nights spent together, and now seeing us all scattered across different skies, it's... helplessness...

In fact, this year, I really wanted to go back, missing the hills of my hometown; this big river of Changde; the Xiangnan Mother Road at the doorstep, leading right to Jinshi and Lixian, left to Changde city; the big pot restaurants, Dingmu Bridge in Lixian, and those remote corners, naturally including those little guys, a few bosom friends. But unfortunately, though I carry a lot in my heart, I remain in another place. Yet, I really miss you all.

In fact, life is about accepting fate, like someone indulging in flowers and moonlight, and in the end, gaining only a sincere heart, or perhaps nothing at all. Is that cruel? No, it's simply a rule. Therefore, whatever we do, we must take it seriously and cautiously. Like me, I should clearly differentiate between humor and playing jokes, and from now on, never use lies to start a joke, because that can only lead to others' misunderstanding and distrust. "Virtue spreads throughout the world," forging a civilized spirit.

Often, imagined things always leave one unable to let go. Once obtained, you'll desire even better possessions. However, if you lose it suddenly, you must gradually accept it. For unattainable things, besides pursuing them, you must also console yourself to face the best possible outcome. Some things are like blowing up balloons; you hope they get bigger, but if they burst suddenly, everything vanishes. Or if you suddenly can't hold onto them, they will mercilessly fly away. I can only say this isn't pessimism, but merely a kind of thought! Therefore, I caution myself: in the future, no matter what I do, I cannot harbor illusions...

In fact, life is like a chess game. Reflections are good, but the future is long...
