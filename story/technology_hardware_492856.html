Pregnant woman with two boyfriends doesn't know who the father of her baby is

by hj1718004 on 2011-09-21 19:14:20

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan (reporter Xiong Linhui, correspondent Gao Xiang) - A 30-year-old woman in Jiang City simultaneously dated two boyfriends and found herself two months pregnant, but unsure which of the two men was the father of her unborn child. Yesterday, through a paternity test, Ms. Jiang identified the father of her unborn baby.

The nearly 30-year-old Ms. Jiang had been hoping to find her ideal partner soon. She was dating both men as she couldn't decide between them due to their similar conditions. Half a month ago, Ms. Jiang discovered that she was pregnant. Since she had sexual relations with both men, she could not determine who the father was. Thus, she quietly collected their hair samples and brought them to the Parentage Testing Center at Zhongnan Hospital for analysis.

The staff drew blood from Ms. Jiang's finger and extracted amniotic fluid from her uterus to extract DNA for gene sequence comparison, confirming that the fetus had a parent-child relationship with one of her boyfriends.

Yesterday, after receiving the test results, Ms. Jiang expressed her intention to establish a monogamous relationship with the father of her unborn child and to get married as soon as possible.

Mr. Xin Sheng, director of the center, introduced that the hospital performs nearly a thousand parentage tests each year. In the past, most people seeking these tests were husbands suspecting their wives of infidelity. Nowadays, more and more women are requesting parentage tests to protect their own rights.
