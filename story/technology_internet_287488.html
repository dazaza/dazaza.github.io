The homosexual bar aims to conduct "peer education" for more potential AIDS patients. The Huaxi Metropolis Daily reported on November 30, 2009 that in November 2009, a bar mainly for homosexuals, funded by Dali City Health Bureau of Yunnan Province and run by more than 10 anti-AIDS volunteers, was about to officially open for business. The Health Bureau and the volunteers planned to carry out "peer education" for more potential AIDS patients through the platform of the bar. The theme of this year's "World AIDS Day" is "universal access and human rights". On November 24, Minister of Health Chen Zhu expressed in Shanghai that AIDS transmission caused by homosexual sexual behavior has accounted for 32% of the total transmission.

On November 25, in a forest near the ancient city of Dali, Yunnan, several men walked into the deep part of the forest. Xiao Ma stood at the crossroads of the dense forest with a plastic bag in his hand. Whenever someone came, he would talk to them in dialect and then take some condoms from the bag and hand them over. For those who were not familiar, Xiao Ma also opened the box and told them how to use them.

"Male homosexuals intervene in male homosexuals"

The condoms distributed by Xiao Ma were specially made for male homosexuals. These people were mostly rural male homosexuals who came to participate in this secret meeting on market day. Among them, the oldest was over 70 years old, and the youngest was less than 20 years old. This secretly formed venue had a history of more than 3 years. Xiao Ma, 25 years old, also came from the countryside and was a homosexual. He was now a volunteer and belonged to "Dali AIDS Health Promotion Association, Dali Good Friends Working Group". On that day, Xiao Ma distributed more than 20 condoms.

Zhang Jianbo, the founder of Dali AIDS Health Promotion Association, said that the working method of developing male homosexuals as volunteers, entering their hidden meeting places, and distributing condoms to other male homosexuals was called "male homosexuals intervene in male homosexuals". He believed that it was impossible to stop their sexual behavior, and could only make these behaviors safer.

On November 24, Minister of Health Chen Zhu expressed in Shanghai that sexual transmission has become the main route of AIDS transmission in our country. Especially homosexual sexual behavior, the AIDS transmission it caused has accounted for 32% of the total transmission. Most of the homosexual sexual behavior that leads to AIDS transmission comes from between male homosexuals.

"Rolling snowball plus following the vine to find the gourd"

Dali City started AIDS prevention work among male homosexuals in 2003. Zhang Jianbo recalled that the most difficult problem at the beginning was finding "male comrades". After spending a year, he still couldn't find the shadow of "male comrades". By chance, he called an "suspected comrade" classmate from middle school to his office, closed the door and scared him by saying that he knew his identity, and explained that he had no ill intention but just wanted to do AIDS prevention work in this group. At first, the classmate was surprised and then denied it outright, but finally understood Zhang Jianbo's purpose and agreed to cooperate. After breaking through, this group emerged.

Zhang Jianbo called this working form "rolling snowball plus following the vine to find the gourd". Over the years, they have mastered the basic situation of more than 1800 male homosexuals, formed a group of more than 10 backbone volunteers, established a special studio, and regularly carried out activities called "peer education". In the activities, methods such as games and training are used to attract them to participate. Many male homosexuals from rural areas were invited in for the first time to come into contact with AIDS prevention knowledge.

"Universal access and human rights"

In the middle of 2009, at the AIDS prevention work conference in Dali City, "Dali AIDS Health Promotion Association, Dali Good Friends Working Group" proposed the idea of opening a bar mainly for homosexuals, planning to attract and intervene more people through the platform of the bar. After being argued, this plan was reported to the municipal government and got approved. The Health Bureau invested 120,000 RMB, and the volunteers selected the location, decorated, designed, and operated the bar. Now the bar has been trial-operated and will officially open on December 1, "World AIDS Day".

The theme of this year's "World AIDS Day" is "universal access and human rights", which is interpreted as hoping more people to have more channels to understand AIDS prevention knowledge and provide more effective protection for relevant groups.

On November 27, the "Investigation Report on the Discrimination Status of Chinese AIDS Infected People" was released in Beijing. This report investigated the discrimination experiences of more than 2,000 AIDS-infected people in China. Vice Minister of Health Huang Jiefu stated that "the understanding and care for AIDS patients and infected people, eliminating discrimination against AIDS infected people and patients is a key link in AIDS prevention and control." According to the Beijing News.
