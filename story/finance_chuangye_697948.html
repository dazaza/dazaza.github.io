Action Achieves Dreams

By Binbin

Regardless of which successful person, there is always an unknown story behind them - a bitter tale of fighting for their dreams. And each story can be condensed into two words: action and perseverance. This is the essence of being a "Dream Actionist"! - By Binbin

The other day, Xiamen University held a rich and lively event titled "I am a Dream Actionist." The entrepreneurial mentors at this event included investor Charles Xue, violinist Siqing Lu, and war photographer Teng Tang. Idol representatives from the post-80s generation were singer Yingrong Jiang and lifetime member of the International Magicians Association, Yu Wang. These individuals shared life wisdom and dream-chasing experiences with the students on site. The event was warmly praised by the university administration, local media, and students alike, achieving great success.

Behind the numerous celebrities and successes lies a renowned woman in the fields of finance, fashion, and culture - Binbin.

She is the chief producer and host of the "Hall of Fame" program on the Finance Channel; the initiator and host of the most influential New Economy Forum (e-talking) in the domestic high-tech and information industries; and the founder and secretary-general of the "Creative China" Gala. In 2001, she founded Bedouin Institution, a professional PR company offering services such as event management, large-scale event planning and hosting, and film/TV production.

In 2010, driven by her own dreams and sense of social responsibility, Binbin initiated the first season of the "Love Dreams, Be Yourself" activity to find Dream Actionists. When asked why she started this activity, Binbin said, "Post-80s individuals are facing various pressures and need more attention and support from society. Therefore, I hope to rally and gather the strength of many business elites, celebrities, and experts to pass on life wisdom to the younger generation, share entrepreneurial experiences, guide career development, provide motivation for every dreamer, convey the spirit of the times, and encourage more young people to stand up under pressure and actively take action for their dreams."

It is understood that in 2010, under Binbin's call, a large number of well-known figures such as Kaifu Lee, Ke Xiao, Guang Su, Chunping Li, Ran Wang, Tong Chen, Fei Tan, and Bo Jin provided strong support. They engaged in deep communication and exchanges with the post-80s generation, offering advice and promoting the implementation of dreams for nearly ten thousand post-80s individuals across the country. In 2011, the second season of the "Love Dreams, Be Yourself" activity, "I am a Dream Actionist," attracted the support and attention of numerous celebrities, including venture capital guru Nanci Shen, famous investor Charles Xue, violinist Siqing Lu, chairman of Zhongkun Group Nuobo Huang, chairman of Zendai Group Zhi Kang Dai, and president of Telecom Greater China Region Hui Huang. Not only did they personally attend the events in Xi'an, Xiamen, and Shanghai to share life wisdom and entrepreneurship experiences with the post-80s generation, but also nearly a hundred celebrities vied to recommend new idols from the post-80s generation around them.

Many young people today often ask, how far is the distance between dreams and reality? Faced with this question, Binbin always firmly says, the distance between dreams and reality is just a moment away. Action or waiting idly – only action is the gateway to success. Step inside, and we will shorten the gap between the two.

Binbin herself, who has twice initiated the "Dream Actionist" activities, is a proactive Dream Actionist, continuously taking action for her heart's dreams. Her greatest dream is to inspire confidence in the dream actions of contemporary young people and encourage more young people to realize their dreams and create greater value for society. Binbin frankly admits, people with dreams are the cutest. People with action are the most "realistic." There is nothing you cannot achieve if you think it through. Take immediate action, and the future may not be a dream; perhaps the realization of your dream is just a moment away. Do not envy any success. In fact, the difference between successful and unsuccessful people is merely that the former acts upon their thoughts while the latter does not.

Binbin stated that the "I am a Dream Actionist" activity aims to inspire the dream actions of contemporary young people, enhance their confidence, and encourage more young people to realize their dreams and create greater value for society. In today's era, every dream is great and deserves respect, and every dream is possible to achieve. Over 200 years ago, the "Mayflower" carried a group of young people onto vast lands, initiating a new American Dream and giving birth to a great new civilization. Today, the dreams of countless Chinese young people will surely, with the rise of China, convey and promote the greatest aspirations of this nation and this era, bearing a portion of their own social responsibilities.

The struggle stories of successful individuals have been showcased on TV programs like the "Hall of Fame" series, where Charles Xue, known as the "investor," believes that Binbin's initiation of the "I am a Dream Actionist" activity not only encourages the post-80s generation but also ignites the youth and dreams of the post-70s, post-60s, and even post-50s generations. Famous violinist Siqing Lu, also one of this year's Dream Actionist mentors, believes that Binbin's initiation of this activity greatly helps young people. As a powerful woman, Binbin's pursuit of her career and dreams, her growth experiences, and her struggle journey are worth learning from and emulating by every young person. Mentor Teng Tang, during his participation in the Xiamen station of the Entrepreneurial Dream Mobilization Activity, said on Weibo: "Dreaming is resting, dreaming about marrying a wife, picking up treasures... If you really dream of something practical, then it’s not a dream anymore, but rather planning, designing, and strategizing. Don't waste your dreams! Dreaming - originally such a beautiful thing!" Therefore, we need to take our dreams seriously, even if we cannot turn beauty into reality, we must walk towards it with hope!

Binbin is inspiring more and more young people with her dream actions and struggle stories. Just after the conclusion of the Xiamen station's activities, she turned to organizing the Shanghai station's events, demonstrating once again her ability to endure hardships and persevere. As Binbin herself said: regardless of which successful person, there is always an unknown, bitter story behind them - a story of striving for dreams, which can be condensed into two words: action and perseverance. This is the essence of being a Dream Actionist, and this is the struggle that Binbin conveys to the post-80s generation through her life experiences and struggles.
