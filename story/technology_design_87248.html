Common types of domestic enterprise registration

by violet0807 on 2008-08-25 18:01:21

**Limited Liability Company (LLC)**

**Concept:** A Limited Liability Company is established by contributions from shareholders, with a maximum of 50 shareholders.

**Characteristics:** Shareholders of an LLC bear responsibility for the company’s debts only up to the amount of their investment. The company itself is responsible for its debts using all of its assets.

**Conditions for registering an LLC:**

- No more than 50 shareholders;

- Shareholder contributions must meet the minimum limit prescribed by the **Company Law**;

- Company name;

- Articles of association;

- Fixed business premises.

**One-person Limited Liability Company**

**Concept:** A one-person LLC is established by a single natural person or legal entity shareholder.

**Characteristics:** A one-person LLC is a special form of an LLC. The minimum registered capital for a one-person LLC is RMB 100,000. A natural person can only invest in and establish one one-person LLC, and a one-person LLC cannot invest in and establish another one-person LLC.

**Sole Proprietorship Enterprise**

**Concept:** A sole proprietorship enterprise is a non-corporate business established by a single natural person.

**Characteristics:** The investor bears unlimited liability for the enterprise's debts with their personal property.

**Conditions for registering a sole proprietorship enterprise:**

- The investor must be a single natural person;

- Business name;

- Investor-declared capital contribution (no proof of capital contribution required);

- Fixed business premises.

**Partnership Enterprise**

**Concept:** A partnership enterprise is a non-corporate business established by two or more partners jointly contributing capital.

**Characteristics:** Each partner bears unlimited joint liability for the partnership enterprise's debts.

**Conditions for registering a partnership enterprise:**

- Two or more partners;

- Written partnership agreement;

- Business name;

- Actual capital contributions from each partner (no capital verification report required);

- Fixed business premises.

**Documents Required for Registration of Domestic Enterprises**

- Proposed enterprise name;

- Registered capital of the company, shareholding ratio of shareholders, and business scope (if involving special industries, corresponding approval documents or certificates should be provided);

- Actual business premises and postal code;

- Telephone number and mobile phone number of the company's legal representative (or enterprise manager) and contact person;

- Original ID card and 2 photos of the company's legal representative (or enterprise manager);

- Original ID cards of the company's directors, managers, and supervisors;

- If the shareholder is an enterprise, provide the original copy of the business license and the original tax registration certificate.

**Registration Process for Domestic Enterprises**

**Enterprise Registration Process:**

- Submit the **Enterprise Registration Overview Form**;

- Name check;

- All shareholders sign and affix seals on the registration materials, verify the registered capital, and handle the industrial and commercial registration;

- Organizational code registration;

- Tax registration;

- Purchase invoices.

**Relocation Registration Process:**

- Qingpu Branch of the Administration for Industry and Commerce agrees to relocation;

- Original registration place handles the cancellation of tax registration;

- Original registration administration handles the relocation procedures;

- All shareholders sign and affix seals on the change registration materials, and handle the industrial and commercial change registration;

- Change registration for the **Organization Code Certificate**;

- Tax registration;

- Purchase invoices.

**Documents Required for Change Registration of Domestic Enterprises**

**Power of Attorney**

**Shareholders' Meeting Resolution**

**Articles of Association Amendment (or New Articles of Association)**

**Submit corresponding documents for changing the following matters:**

- Changing the company name: Provide at least 3 proposed company names.

- Changing the company address: Provide proof of the new address.

- Changing the company registered capital:

- Capital increase: Provide an audit report on the newly added registered capital or company assets, and re-validate the capital;

- Capital reduction: Provide proof of capital reduction announcement and company asset audit report, and re-validate the capital.

- Changing the business scope: Provide details of the changed business scope (if involving special industries, provide corresponding approval documents or certificates).

- Changing the company shareholders:

- If the transferor is a natural person or private enterprise, first conduct an equity transfer agreement, then handle the industrial and commercial change registration;

- If the transferor is a collective enterprise, first conduct asset evaluation, then property rights trading, and finally handle the industrial and commercial change registration.

**Enterprise Restructuring**

Enterprise restructuring refers to transforming a non-corporate enterprise into a corporate enterprise through industrial and commercial change registration.

Enterprise restructuring usually involves changes in registration items such as "enterprise name, registered capital, shareholders," etc.
