▇ Misfortunes never come singly: A piece of clothing can be life-threatening! It's more serious than just a bad review! ▇

by lovea2z1 on 2010-07-11 07:59:14

No fragrance of flowers, no towering trees, I am a little grass that no one knows.

Without skills, without capital, what I rely on is my sincere heart for Taobao!

"Are you brain-damaged? You quit being an enterprise planning manager at a hospital with a monthly salary of 8000 to open a store on Taobao. Can you even support me and our daughter?" My wife, whom I had been married to for two years and who had just given birth to our child, questioned me over the phone.

I answered silently but firmly: "You and our daughter are my treasures. How could I joke about your happiness? Just wait and see how your husband will make you happy." This dialogue was from a real conversation with my wife in April this year. Please don't laugh at this tough guy for speaking so sentimentally. The truth is, I said it completely to move my wife, as I myself wasn't sure about Taobao either.

But why do I want to do business on Taobao? I know this question well. In short, I want to start a business. To freely do what I like is perhaps the initial intention of many Taobao sellers. The difference lies in the entrepreneurial experiences. As Tolstoy said: Happy sellers are mostly similar, but unfortunate sellers each have their own misfortunes. It has only been a month since I opened my store, yet unfortunate events have come one after another, truly proving that misfortune never comes alone!

Encounter One: Clothes torn, local buyer causes trouble at home.

Perhaps few shopkeepers dare to reveal their home address online like I do. But I don’t think so. A physical store should provide a real address just like an online store does. This can enhance credibility. Therefore, I left my home address without hesitation on a local forum and expressed that I welcome local netizens to visit and discuss clothing matching tips. My original intention was to make more friends online, but unexpectedly, this good-hearted idea almost got me into trouble. On June 20th, a netizen ordered a large skirt from my online store. Seeing the address, it turned out to be a local netizen. So I quickly shipped the item according to the address. I was very confident in this product. Since it was listed, three pieces had already been sold, and customers were very satisfied. However, due to my negligence, I did not notice the defect in the clothes when I purchased them until the netizen came to my house to cause trouble, and then I realized the problem. On June 22nd, the day after this netizen received the goods, suddenly, a loud knocking sound rang out. I opened the door and saw a man and a woman standing in front of my door. The man shouted at me: “Are you the owner of Lisa’s Showroom! What kind of clothes do you sell? Sounds good online, all lies! Look, there's a big seam!” I was a bit dizzy and passive from his shouting, so I could only obediently follow his instructions. After carefully examining the clothes, I chuckled because there was indeed a big seam under the armpit. I quickly recalled the scene of shipping two days ago, remembering that I didn't check this place at the time. Then I smiled faintly and said: “I admit the clothes have problems. Please calm down and don't get angry. Come in and have a drink first, it's hot outside.” The woman seemed to soften a bit after hearing what I said, but the man was still shouting, but this time not at me, but at the woman: “If you buy things online again, I'll cut off your hand!” This sentence made my face turn red, and the woman dared not say anything, looking down like a child. I had never encountered such a situation before, not knowing the true reason behind it, and fearing that something bad might happen, so I dared not argue with the man. I said: “Don't argue anymore. I will refund the full amount for this dress. The problem with the clothes is my negligence, but I hope you won't have conflicts because of this matter.” The man kept silent after hearing what I said. I refunded the full amount through Alipay in front of them. They then quietly left, the woman seemed to feel guilty and didn't dare to look up at me. Watching them leave my house, I sighed in relief. Luckily, no one died, which is a success.

That night, Wangwang (instant messaging) sounded, it was the girl. She secretly told me that the man was her father. She didn't perform well in this year's college entrance exam, so he was particularly dissatisfied with her, saying she only cared about eating and wearing. Indeed, the crack in the clothes existed as soon as she received it. She said, I'm a pretty good person. To compensate, she said she would give me a positive review. At that moment, I remembered that I directly transferred the refund to her account while her order was still active. So I gratefully said: “When you enter university, I will send you a beautiful skirt.” She happily replied: “I will definitely pass next year. Thank you, I will definitely invite you to my wedding banquet next year.” I said: “Definitely, definitely. Next year, my store will definitely be different.” Thus, a misunderstanding ended happily in pleasant chat, and I felt particularly cheerful.

Encounter Two: Extremely cold weather: malicious attack by competing sellers

The incident occurred at the end of June, a week after the aforementioned event. In the evening, Wangwang rang, ding dong ding dong, a treasure was sold! Excitedly, I entered the backend of the online store to check, indeed, a customer placed an order for one item. I quickly started chatting with him, asking for detailed shipping requirements. Unexpectedly, he said: “Just ship according to the description in the store.” I didn’t realize it was a trap.

Immersed in joy, I quickly shipped the item. Three days later, the customer proactively sent me a message saying that the goods had been received, but they did not match the description. I asked where it didn’t match, and she said the pattern was different. After careful recollection, it should be the same. This T-shirt only had one type, and the print only had one design. I went to the warehouse and compared it carefully with the picture on the online store. Chuckling, there was indeed a slight difference. The patterns on the picture were somewhat larger and brighter, while the actual item was smaller and darker. So, I explained to her that this situation was relatively normal and hoped for understanding. But she firmly refused to listen, rudely demanding a full refund, return of the clothes, and even insisted I pay for the shipping fee. I was quite annoyed, almost ready to yell back, but I still held my temper and said to her, if you can't give a positive review, then give a neutral one. What I didn't expect was that a minute after I said that, I received her negative review: "The clothes are terrible, very old, selling old clothes as new ones, no humanity!" After seeing this malicious negative review, I was furious! I wanted to communicate with her again, but she had already gone offline. Until now, I haven't been able to contact her.

Just the other day, a friend of mine who works in the garment industry told me that she also encountered the same situation, and the language of the bad reviews was exactly the same. Only then did I realize that I had encountered a malicious attack from competitors and understood what professional negative reviewers were all about.

But I deeply despise those opponents. Why take such despicable means? If you encounter difficulties in opening a store, we can learn from and exchange with each other, why be so narrow-minded? Your actions will only increase my determination to do Taobao.

Also, remind everyone, the Taobao world is perilous, enter with caution!

Both incidents happened last month. Although they weren't very pleasant, they made me understand Taobao more deeply and strengthened my resolve and courage to continue doing Taobao. I know that in the future, there will certainly be more storms waiting for me on the Taobao road. But with the support of Taobao enthusiasts, the enthusiastic help of countless sellers, and the sincere encouragement of kind buyers, I believe that small sellers like me will surely grow strong!

To fulfill the promise I made to that local girl, I will walk step by step!
