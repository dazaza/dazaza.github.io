Environmental concepts are favored - Activity Week

Similarly focusing on the theme of "creativity" was the recently concluded "2007 Shanghai International Creative Industry Activity Week." Since it is related to creativity, naturally the exhibits were diverse. Simple bags attracted many environmental enthusiasts to participate; they even ignored their appearance while lying on the ground doing graffiti. It turned out they were participating in the TVSN environmental bag creative graffiti event organized by Wendy House Company - "Comforting the Earth," where visitors could freely create graffiti on prepared white environmental bags. The purpose was to advocate using self-designed tote bags and minimize or avoid using plastic bags altogether. Many people participated, and there were also many spectators, surrounded by layers of people. Those who finished painting had to literally squeeze through the crowd, but since it was an environmentally friendly initiative, a little embarrassment was not an issue. Additionally, there were various daily necessities made from dry pumpkins, introducing 3D street art elements into urban construction... These creative ideas and works greatly broadened people's horizons, which is why those present couldn't help but praise them.

Fashion shows reveal themselves - Fashion Space

When it comes to the embodiment of creativity in fashion, people naturally think of clothing first, and those few design masters known as "geniuses." In fact, China also has such creative designs. The recent "Now - Fashion Creative Space • Exception Night" is one of them. As the opening performance of the fashion creative space, the models walked gracefully under simple white lights, with earth yellow cloth margins beneath their feet. This unpretentious, non-flashy style, along with the unique mirror-image LOGO and distinctive clothing styles, all reflect the uniqueness of our Eastern culture. Renowned international architect Zhang Yonghe even said: "Before 'Exception,' Chinese women dressed for others; after 'Exception,' Chinese women dress for themselves." It shows the affection for this domestic design. Creativity itself has also extended beyond the runway. At the entrance, people just need to screw in the signed light bulbs into the screw holes on the scenic board, and the bulbs will light up. As more and more bulbs light up, downward arrows become prominent, expressing "Where are you?" which perfectly matches the theme of feeling the present moment. It seems the designer put a lot of thought into it!

Relevant thematic articles: Wendy House Kids' Wear

Currently, the children's wear market is in a state of polarization: On one hand, some high-end brand clothes are of excellent quality, but their market prices are generally too high; on the other hand, 60% of children's wear sales in our country are still concentrated in wholesale markets, belonging to low-end products. Their styles are overly rigid, styles are similar, fabric selection and clothing quality can't compare with branded children's wear. The imbalance in the development of the children's wear market greatly suppresses market purchasing power. The market urgently needs products that are reasonably priced and of good quality. Brand discount stores have emerged to fill this gap, making up for the shortcomings of both.

The location of the Wendy House kids' wear brand discount store is slightly better than that in malls because discount stores generally have relatively smaller areas, so clothing classification is denser. Moreover, in discount stores, adults tend to be generous when buying clothes for children at brand discount stores, so the economic sales performance of the children's wear section in discount stores is not bad. This is also the reason why children's wear holds a higher status in discount stores than in malls. The brand discount store near my home is like this, with the children's wear section located next to the escalator on the second floor, which is extremely rare in malls.

I also discovered a relatively common consumption phenomenon here: most consumers buy at least two pieces of children's wear. Analyzing this phenomenon, it's not difficult to find that discounts are an excellent model in the sale of children's wear. For most ordinary families, facing rapidly growing children, and being single-child households leading to no successors to continue use, thus when buying children's wear, facing high unit prices, saying there's no burden is impossible. Therefore, many working-class families choose to buy children's wear at wholesale markets or other places with lower prices. But after all, parents with only one child still want to give their best within their means, and the quality of children's wear at wholesale markets is still a concern.

Discount stores provide quality assurance for clothing, thus becoming a favorite place for parents to shop. Parents who hesitate in front of the latest seasonal styles costing hundreds of yuan feel much more relaxed here. Prices here are mostly around a hundred yuan, fitting the psychological price range of ordinary urban working-class families. There are also very cheap double-discounted items, almost equal in price to wholesale markets, but with guaranteed quality. Therefore, parents can freely choose here. For brands, this is undoubtedly a good way to handle inventory and activate funds. According to industry insiders, even the best-selling styles of brand clothing will have about 30% inventory. Therefore, selling these inventory products to recover funds is highly beneficial for brands.
