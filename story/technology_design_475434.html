Daughter died under her father's beating after she was caught stealing for the sixth time

by zzfdhdb on 2011-08-12 16:25:47

A family is from Sichuan, the daughter has a habit of stealing. She had been taken to the police station six times for theft. Yesterday noon - the "six-time" teenager was beaten to death.

According to the news (by observer Liang Jun, reporters Chen Zhu and Hong Xue), yesterday at noon, Liu, who came to Beijing to work, accidentally killed his 12-year-old daughter. His original intention was just to teach this child who liked to steal things a lesson.

Yesterday afternoon at around 1:30 PM, I saw in the morgue of Luhe Hospital in Tongzhou district that the face of the girl in the body bag was pale, with wounds around her mouth.

"Just wait a little longer, her heartbeat just stopped, maybe she can come back to life in a while." The girl's father, Mr. Liu, choked up and begged the staff not to push his daughter into the cold storage.

After a few minutes, the body bag was sealed by the staff and pushed into the cold storage. The sound of the door closing made Mr. Liu almost fall down. At over 40 years old, he bent over and sobbed shakily.

Mr. Liu told the reporter that they are from Sichuan province, and he currently works in Beijing.

Liu's daughter is in the fourth grade of elementary school and has always had a problem with stealing. She has stolen things from her classmates, family, and relatives, even stealing more than 1000 yuan from others at one time.

"I just wanted to teach her a lesson, but I didn't expect to accidentally beat her to death." Mr. Liu cried and told the reporter that because of stealing, his daughter had been to the police station more than six times.

Yesterday at noon, in order to teach his unrepentant daughter a lesson, Mr. Liu hit her again. Half an hour later, he found that his daughter's body was cold, so he quickly sent her to the hospital, where she was pronounced dead after unsuccessful resuscitation.

"I only hit her on the buttocks, how could I have beaten her to death?" Mr. Liu said incoherently.

This morning, I learned from the Tongzhou police that Mr. Liu has been criminally detained. According to his confession, he just wanted to teach his daughter a lesson, but he didn't expect to accidentally beat her to death.

Currently, the case is still under further investigation.
