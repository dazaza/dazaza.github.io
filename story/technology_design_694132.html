What are the methods of traditional Chinese medicine for treating azoospermia?

by sjjkb4 on 2012-03-06 22:17:57

How does Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treat Azoospermia? TCM believes that the main cause of azoospermia is due to insufficient kidney essence and obstruction in the seminal ducts. When treating insufficient kidney essence, it is advisable to tonify the kidneys and replenish essence, whereas treating seminal duct obstruction mainly involves clearing heat, resolving stasis, and promoting circulation. Below are seven methods summarized by experts regarding TCM treatment for azoospermia:

1. Modified Bān Lóng Wán (Spotted Dragon Pill)

Ingredients: 600g each of deer horn gelatin, turtle shell gelatin, Cistanche, Schisandra; 240g each of Epimedium, Allii Tuberosi Semen, Cuscutae Semen, and Dioscorea; 200g each of ginseng, Poria, Psoralea, and Prunus seeds; 2400g of black beans; 1 pair of geckos; 2 placentas; 2 pairs of bull testicles. The above ingredients are ground into powder, mixed with honey to form pills weighing 10 grams each. Take 3 times daily, 1 pill each time, swallowed with rice porridge. This formula tonifies the kidneys and generates essence, suitable for treating azoospermia.

2. Main Formula: Shēng Jīng Zhòng Zǐ Tāng (Essence-Generating Seed-Sowing Decoction)

Prescription: 15g Epimedium, 10g Dipsacus, 15g Cuscutae Semen, 15g Polygonum Multiflorum, 15g Lycium, 15g Mulberry, 10g Raspberry, 6g Schisandra, 15g Plantago, 20g Astragalus, 10g Angelica. Boil twice and divide into two doses, taken daily.

3. Prepared Chinese Medicines

(1) Wǔ Zǐ Yǎn Zōng Wán (Five Sons Progeny Pill), oral intake, 9g each time, three times daily.

(2) Hǎi Mǎ Bǔ Shèn Wán (Seahorse Kidney-Tonifying Pill), oral intake, 9g each time, three times daily.

4. Single Formula or Empirical Formula: Modified Wǔ Zǐ Yǎn Zōng Wán

Prescription: 15g Epimedium, 15g太子参 (Prince Ginseng), 15g Plantago, 12g Cistanche, 12g Raspberry, 12g Cuscutae Semen, 12g Lycium, 12g Polygonatum, 12g Dog's Backbone Fern Rhizome, 12g Vaccaria, 12g Angelica, 9g Curculigo, 6g Schisandra, 3g Placenta Powder. Boil in water and take as a decoction, one dose daily. There are many ways to treat azoospermia, varying from person to person.

5. Yù Yīn Fēng Jīng Tāng (Nourishing Yin and Enriching Essence Decoction)

10g Anemarrhena, 10g Alisma, 15g raw Rehmannia, 15g cooked Rehmannia, 10g Moutan Bark, 10g Jujube Peel, 10g Sesame Seeds, 3g Cinnamon, 6g Safflower, 15g Deer Horn Gelatin. Boil in water, one dose daily, divided into two servings. Treatment course lasts for 3 months. This formula nourishes yin and enriches essence, suitable for treating azoospermia.

6. Shēng Yù Wán (Fertility Pill)

40g Red Ginseng, 10g Deer Antler, 60g Deer Horn Gelatin, 60g Cornus, 80g Lycium, 80g cooked Rehmannia, 80g Astragalus, 80g Schisandra, 1 pair of Sea Dog Kidneys, 1 pair of Geckos. All the above ingredients are finely powdered and mixed with honey to form pills, taken twice daily, 10g each time. Warm water is used to swallow the pills. This formula tonifies the kidneys and invigorates qi, suitable for treating azoospermia caused by kidney qi deficiency.

7. Zēng Jīng Wán (Essence-Increasing Pill)

For azoospermia treatment, choose appropriate amounts of male silkworm moths, deer horn gelatin, Epimedium, processed Aconite, Cuscutae Semen, Achyranthes, Raspberry, Dendrobium, Allii Tuberosi Semen, Cistanche, Cinnamon, Morinda, Sea Dog Kidneys, etc. First, boil deer horn gelatin powder in cool water until boiling, then add 100-200ml of rice wine and silkworm moth powder, simmer over low heat, remove from heat, and mix with the remaining powdered herbs (Cuscutae Semen needs to be soaked in 65-degree rice wine for 12 hours beforehand). Stir and form into pills weighing 9-12 grams. Take 1 pill in the morning, afternoon, and evening. Each course of treatment lasts 15-20 days. This formula tonifies the kidneys, strengthens yang, replenishes essence, and promotes fertility, suitable for infertility due to azoospermia, where the condition belongs to kidney yang deficiency.
