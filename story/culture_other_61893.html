CCTV: Originally refers to China Central Television. It has been extended to a verb which means harming others' interests by improper means (especially in voting or elections), and can also be used as an adjective meaning despicable and shameless. This meaning originates from the cheating scandal targeting Sky in the online voting activity for the selection of the Top Ten Sports Figures in 2006 hosted by China Central Television.
