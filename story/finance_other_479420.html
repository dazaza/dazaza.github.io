The global iron ore supply is expected to continue to increase on a large scale until 2015.

by 51lsd on 2011-08-18 13:33:29

[China Hardware Tools Network] Due to India's plan to use its own mined iron ore to meet the needs of domestic steel enterprises expanding production capacity, last year, India's iron ore exports, which fell for the first time in a decade, will be reduced by half in the next five years.

The reduction in shipments of iron ore from India, the world's third largest iron ore exporter, will further drive up iron ore prices, which have tripled since the end of 2008. This is because it is expected that the large-scale increase in global iron ore supply will continue until 2015, and China, the world's largest iron ore consumer, will see an increase in demand for iron ore.

Graeme Train, a commodities analyst at Macquarie Bank in Shanghai, pointed out: "India's iron ore exports will experience a structural decline as resource nationalism will become a bigger driver behind policy. This policy will definitely not encourage iron ore exports."

In order to curb the sale of iron ore to foreign countries, India has increased freight rates, and the export tax on fines has quadrupled. Typical iron ore powder with an iron content of 55%~65% accounts for about 70% of India's annual production of approximately 200 million tons of iron ore.

In the previous fiscal year ended in March, India's iron ore exports fell 17% to 97.6 million tons, mainly due to the ban on shipping iron ore from Karnataka, a key iron ore mining state.

Analysts polled by Reuters expect that India's iron ore exports will fall again this year, hitting an eight-year low, due to rising iron ore mining costs and delays in shipping iron ore from Karnataka, which accounts for about a quarter of India's iron ore exports.

India's tight iron ore supply and China's growing demand for iron ore have driven up spot iron ore prices. The spot price of iron ore IO62-CNI=SI hit a new high in mid-February, reaching $190 per ton. Currently, iron ore is trading near $176 per ton.
