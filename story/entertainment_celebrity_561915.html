Han Xue was harassed by a crazy fan, and she angrily tweeted that the consequences would be on his own.

by mlwang on 2011-11-16 12:37:06

On November 15, Han Xue angrily condemned a fan on her Weibo, saying that her patience had reached its limit. She hoped that the fan would stop imagining his love for her and stop harassing her family. She warned that he would be responsible for the consequences. It is reported that Han Xue has been harassed by this fan for more than two years. The fan sent Han Xue flowers, harassed her family, and even imagined himself as Han Xue's "lover". Han Xue angrily posted on Weibo that he would be responsible for the consequences.

At 17:40 in the afternoon today, Han Xue angrily condemned the radical behavior of a fan on her Weibo, hoping that he would go home and see a psychiatrist, and warned that if he continued to harass her family, he would be responsible for the consequences. Han Xue wrote on Weibo: "Xu Shukun, my patience with you has reached its limit! Go home and see your psychiatrist! Stop following me! Stop imagining your love, it has nothing to do with me! Don't take things out of context on my Weibo, not a single word is written for you, no emotion is related to you! The police can't handle you, right?! If you go to Suzhou again to harass my family, you will be responsible for the consequences!"

It is reported that this crazy fan is a middle-aged man who has been harassing Han Xue for more than two years. At first, he just went to the filming site to visit, sent flowers or chatted with Han Xue for a while. Later, he became more aggressive. When he couldn't find Han Xue, he went directly to her hometown in Suzhou to look for her. This man suddenly "visited" Han Xue's hometown in Suzhou twice, which made Han Xue very angry. The first time Han Xue learned that this man had gone to her hometown, she tore her face off with him on the set and told him to "get lost." But this man persisted, and on the 15th, he went to Han Xue's hometown again to harass her, making Han Xue finally unable to bear it and post this Weibo condemnation.

In addition, this man has been constantly imagining his love for Han Xue and foolishly believes that Han Xue's Weibo posts are secretly confessing her love to him. Now Han Xue is trying to avoid this person as much as possible. Netizens have left messages expressing their concern for Han Xue, teaching her to be careful, not to go out alone, and not to open the door to strangers. It is also hoped that the male version of "Yang Lijuan" will learn from past mistakes, stop his ridiculous behavior. By the time of publication, Han Xue's Weibo post had been forwarded more than 1,000 times and received over 1,200 comments.
