"Ma6 Man" Beaten Up Mother and Daughter in Public, Enraged the Crowd, Car Was Smashed

The incident occurred near Hongqi Street. The Vice Mayor of Changchun City and Public Security Bureau Director Gao Xue Zhang arrived at the scene first to direct the investigation and on-site handling, mobilizing police forces from multiple departments and units.

The attacker Jiang Xiao Zhou is a resident of Zhenlai County, Jilin Province, with no fixed occupation. He was administratively detained for 15 days and fined 500 yuan for assaulting others and wearing a police uniform without authorization.

The public security organs will investigate the parties involved in the incident who incited trouble and smashed vehicles, as well as those who threw objects causing injuries to police officers, and hold them legally accountable.

"I have money, what can you do if I beat you to death hh." At around 14:00 on the 5th, a man dressed in a police cotton coat, while driving near a supermarket on Hongqi Street, got into an argument with a middle-aged woman. Subsequently, he got out of the car, assaulted the middle-aged woman, and made arrogant remarks. This behavior attracted the attention of over a thousand people. Many onlookers angrily smashed the red Mazda6 (referred to as Ma6) driven by the man. "Ma6 Man" and another woman in the car were taken away by the police after being trapped inside the car for more than three hours.

Incident Scene

Hundred of citizens surrounded the car.

The red Ma6 was smashed up and down unevenly.

At around 16:00 on the 5th, when the reporter arrived at the scene near Hongqi Street, there were thousands of onlookers gathered, with hundreds surrounding a red Ma6 with license plate number Ji G2E222. Dozens of policemen set up warning lines to maintain order. The reporter noticed that there was one man and one woman sitting inside the red Mazda. Onlookers shouted at the people inside the car, demanding they get out. Some onlookers attempted to break through the police line to attack the man inside the car, but were stopped by the police several times.

Since the people inside the car refused to get out, onlookers began picking up stones and wooden blocks from the ground to smash the car. Despite the police's attempts to stop them, the onlookers would not cease. The red Mazda body was already dented and uneven, the front windshield was completely shattered, three side windows were broken, and the rear bumper was completely detached. At the scene, there was also an elderly man with a cane who angrily used his cane to hit the Ma6 car. "Aren't you arrogant? Come down ttt." No matter how the police persuaded, the onlookers would not let go of the man inside the car.

Arrogant Behavior

Both the man and the woman in the car participated in the assault.

"Just beating you, I have plenty of money."

A citizen surnamed Zhang at the scene told reporters that at around 14:00 on the 5th, they saw a man in a police uniform punching and kicking a middle-aged woman. The woman in the passenger seat also got out of the car later; both of them participated in the assault, acting very cruelly and cursing while hitting.

Witness Ms. Diao introduced that when the crowd saw the man punching and kicking the middle-aged woman, several people went forward to stop him. However, the man in the police uniform and the woman in the car continued to beat the middle-aged woman arrogantly, ignoring the advice. After the man in the police uniform finished beating and got back into the car, the onlookers surrounded the Ma6 and demanded the assailants get out and apologize, but the man in the police uniform refused.

Witness Mr. Chen said that during the assault on the middle-aged woman, the "Ma6 Man" hit her particularly hard. When the daughter of the middle-aged woman tried to stop him, the woman in the passenger seat also got out of the car and helped beat her. After their behavior was stopped, the onlookers demanded they publicly apologize, but they refused and made arrogant remarks, pointing at the beaten middle-aged woman saying, "I have money, what can you do if I beat you to death? I'm not short of money, I'll just beat you, I have plenty of money hh."

The onlookers at the scene suspected that the man in the police uniform was a police officer. Afterwards, some witnesses said that initially, it was just an argument between the "Ma6 Man" and the middle-aged woman, which eventually escalated the situation.

Police Actions

Policemen were injured while maintaining order.

3 hours later, the driver was "rescued."

The arrogant attitude of the "Ma6 Man" aroused the public anger of the onlookers, who started condemning the "Ma6 Man," and some young people lost control and rushed to the front of the car to fight for justice. Seeing this public outrage, the "Ma6 Man" quickly hid inside the car with his companion and locked the door. However, since the car was surrounded, they could not drive away.

Hundreds of onlookers surrounded the scene tightly, and the police continuously increased manpower to maintain order. "Get out, apologize," "Severely punish the attacker hh." The onlookers demanded that the "Ma6 Man" get out of the car and make a public apology. The police also tried to communicate with the man inside the car, but the "Ma6 Man" refused to get out. "If he doesn't get out, we won't let him leave." The onlookers firmly refused to let the police take the "Ma6 Man" away. During the maintenance of order by the Jian She Square police station, a piece of wood flew out from the crowd and hit a policeman on the head.

After multiple communications between the police and the onlookers, and assurances that regardless of the identity of the attacker, they would be severely punished, the emotions of the onlookers gradually calmed down. Around 17:20, under the protection of the police at the scene, the two people in the car were "escorted" into a supporting anti-riot vehicle and taken back to Chaoyang Public Security Bureau for investigation. Around 18:00, the damaged Ma6 car was towed away by the police.

Victim's Recall

The beating lasted about 5 minutes.

Mother's hands were bruised and swollen like steamed buns.

After the incident, the reporter went to the emergency department of Jilin Provincial People's Hospital and met the middle-aged woman, Gao Li Jie, and her daughter, Wang Nan. Gao Li Jie's hands were all bruised, and her hands were swollen like steamed buns. Wang Nan had some external injuries, and her mouth was cut. Gao Li Jie's family introduced that her injuries were unclear now, and the doctor said there might be some problems with her head, so she needed to continue hospital observation.

According to Wang Nan's introduction, her mother is 55 years old. At around 14:00 on the 5th, when she and her mother were shopping and passed by that Ma6 car, the driver accidentally hit her mother's leg and then kept honking. Her mother was very angry, and then an argument ensued, resulting in the driver getting out of the car and starting to hit people.

Gao Li Jie said that she was pushed to the ground by that man, and then he started punching and kicking her. No matter how she begged, it didn't work, and he kept hitting her. The woman in the car finally joined in the fight. Wang Nan saw the "Ma6 Man" trying to escape in the car, so she sat in the car. Initially, the woman in the passenger seat of the Ma6 wanted to pull Wang Nan out of the car, "I was afraid he would leave, so I stayed in the car. That man came over and pulled me out, and pulled my hair!" Wang Nan said, "After pulling me out, he kicked me hard, and then he knocked my mother down again."

The man in the police uniform tried to drive away again, and Wang Nan blocked him with her body. Then the man in the police uniform started reversing, and Wang Nan immediately shouted to the crowd, "He's trying to escape!" The crowd immediately surrounded the car, and after that, the man in the police uniform and the woman did not dare to move inside the car. After the crowd surrounded them, Wang Nan helped her mother, who was beaten and lying on the ground, up, called 110 to report the incident, and sent her mother to the hospital for treatment.

There is a large shoe print on the left side of Wang Nan's abdomen. Wang Nan claimed that the attackers beat her and her mother for about five minutes. If it weren't for the passersby stopping them, their injuries might have been worse.

The Attacker Was Detained for 15 Days and Fined 500 Yuan

Dongya News (Reporter Wei Hui Xuan): On the evening of the 5th, the reporter learned from the police that regarding the fight between the driver of the Mazda sedan and two women on Hongqi Street, Vice Mayor of Changchun City and Director of the Public Security Bureau Gao Xue Zhang arrived at the scene immediately after receiving the report, mobilized police forces from multiple departments and units, and quickly carried out various investigations and on-site handling works. Currently, the police have administratively detained the attacker for 15 days and fined him 500 yuan.

After receiving the alarm from the public, the Jian She Square Police Station of Chaoyang Branch of Changchun Public Security Bureau quickly rushed to the scene, maintained order, and carried out handling work. Changchun Public Security Bureau highly valued this incident. Vice Mayor of Changchun City and Director of the Public Security Bureau Gao Xue Zhang arrived at the scene immediately after receiving the report, together with Deputy Director Lu Feng of Changchun Public Security Bureau and other relevant leaders, directing on-site operations, mobilizing police forces from multiple departments and units, quickly carrying out various investigations and on-site handling works, effectively controlling the chaotic situation caused by the crowd's watching and individual people's incitement and cheering. After more than three hours of persuasion and patiently explaining the situation to the crowd, this incident was properly handled, and the related parties were taken back by the police for investigation and interrogation.

Upon understanding, the young man driving the red Mazda car is named Jiang Xiao Zhou, 27 years old, a resident of Zhenlai County, Jilin Province, with no fixed occupation. According to Jiang Xiao Zhou, that day, he and his wife came from Zhenlai to Changchun to shop at a mall. When he was driving near the mall, he slightly bumped a female pedestrian passing by, and then the two got into an argument and struggled together.

Jiang Xiao Zhou assaulted others and wore police uniforms without authorization. According to the provisions of the "Law of the People's Republic of China on Administrative Punishments," the public security organ imposed administrative detention for 15 days and a fine of 500 yuan on him. Since the other party involved in the incident is currently undergoing medical examination and treatment at the hospital, the public security organ will further investigate the claim by Jiang Xiao Zhou that he was also attacked during the incident. At the same time, the public security organ will investigate the parties involved in the incident who took advantage of the situation to incite trouble, smash vehicles, throw objects causing injuries to police officers, and hold them legally accountable.
