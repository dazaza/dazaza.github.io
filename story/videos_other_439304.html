So don't be silly.

by was25135 on 2011-05-30 13:44:53

1. If someone calls you every day without any doubt, he loves you, at least he has you on his mind. If it's always you calling him, then you don't need to ask. Every time I take out the camera, no matter how painful her expression is, she basically doesn't have you in her heart. You can respond with a silent smile. Do not believe the lie that he has no time and is very busy. A person who doesn't even have three or five minutes to make a phone call, how busy is he? Can he still be alive?

2. If someone takes you to a private and secluded place when meeting you, then he does not love you. On the contrary, if he takes you to public places like parks, then he likes being with you.

3. If someone only calls or texts you for casual greetings without any hint of flirtation, then don't be foolish, he has no feelings for you anymore. His greetings may just be routine formalities.

4. If someone loves you, he will tell you that he misses you without you having to ask.

5. If someone gets angry with you over trivial matters, it shows that he cares about you. However, if after you admit your mistake, he still ignores you, let me advise you, ignore him too. If he loves you, he will reach out to you. Let me share with you all a very fun mini-game website. If he doesn't love you, he uses this as an excuse to let go of you.

6. If someone loves you, he will tolerate all your flaws. If someone does not love you, no matter how well you behave, he will eventually find fault with you and leave you.

7. If someone loves you, he will accept everything about you and not ask you to change this or that.

Therefore, you should let go a bit and not torment yourself because of love. Do you know that whether it's the men in love or those who are undecided, after reading this, they must share it. We will all be happy.
