Following the wind, is the pain of falling flowers.

The night is long, and I am sleepless.

I got up and turned on my computer. My mind was in turmoil, and the words typed by my fingertips were as messy as my thoughts. I might as well sit quietly, brew a cup of tea, and let the bitter taste of tea dissipate in the air with a gentle tune, gradually spreading and intensifying.

I always thought that you are the most fortunate person. On the journey of encountering dust relationships, there has always been someone silently waiting for you, accompanying you to watch the sunrise and sunset every day, bearing the coldness of the four seasons' cycles for you, transplanting the warmest ray of sunshine to your window, allowing you to be immersed in the pleasant morning flower fragrance all day. Your happiness is his greatest happiness.

I thought that you would hold hands and walk the long road of life, no matter what, you would not easily give up the happiness right in front of you. But I never expected that at the next turning corner, you let go, without any reason, leaving me a sense of melancholy that I could never understand. Helplessly, I laughed at your foolishness and stupidity.

I thought that letting go would not cause you pain, deep pain is just a simple wound. You tied a knot for the love you let go, deeply buried in your heart. Then you held hands with a loveless game at the corner, betting yourself like a gamble. Every evening, you were alone in loneliness, watching the flowers bloom and fall, tears streaming down your face. As days went by, you directed a scene of chrysanthemums yellowing and scattering all over the city, filling the ground with sorrow. Finally, you couldn't suppress the hidden pain in the softest part of your heart, so you placed your heart in a quiet corner and quietly left.

The afternoon sun scattered on your face, my tears fell like rain. From then on, you gradually moved further away, first the warmth, then the sound, and later the increasingly blurred appearance, until only a little memory remained. Love slowly withdrew from your life, and you helplessly watched it become blurry, powerless to change it. You were like a person watching a movie alone, sitting in a dark and empty cinema, lonely watching the curtain open step by step. Those touching encounters and passionate processes eventually led to a desolate ending.

Two people's fireworks, one person's loneliness. Without love, there is no familiar heartbeat, no warmth of interlocked fingers, but rather an accumulation of more and more coldness, so cold that even the eyes carry a sense of estrangement. Without love, there is no impulse to embrace, this feeling of being unable to touch warmth under the same roof makes your heart feel like wandering in the cold winter. You feel tired, you don't want to continue anymore, thus, the 37-degree body temperature you possess gradually cools down...
