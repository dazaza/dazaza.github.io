How to paint simple and beautiful nails

by vm2s3f0d0y8 on 2012-02-21 12:23:10

If the beauty feels bored at home, she can paint her nails at home. The beauties who don't know how to do it don't have to be afraid. The steps and skills I will teach you are very simple and easy to handle. So let's start our nail art training! First of all, wash your hands clean and polish your nails bright (unfortunately, my nails have been cut off.

Prepare materials: colored nail polish, transparent nail polish, tissue, toothpick.

Use a toothpick to dip the colored nail polish (I use purple).

Then draw on the tissue, any pattern you like, just pay attention to the size, because it will be pasted on the nail finally, I drew a random shape.

Since some tissues have several layers, you need to separate them and only leave the surface layer with the drawing.

Then cut out the drawn pattern. Don't cut it too big, just follow the edges of the drawing.

The cut-out shape is ready, next comes the key part.

First, apply a layer of transparent nail polish on the nail, or other ones with a little color are also fine as long as they are not dark colors, I used the transparent one.

While the nail polish is still wet, place the cut-out tissue on the appropriate position of the nail, you can press it down with something.

Then, apply another layer of transparent nail polish. This step must use transparent nail polish because only in this way the tissue won't be visible. See, the half that has been painted no longer shows the tissue.

The final result, everyone can draw more beautifully in video passion. (My drawing is not beautiful, alas, I don't have that talent. If you don't have so many colors of nail polish, what should you do? Let me tell you there is another way, hehe! Secret weapon coming! Any colored pen will do!

It looks better if you keep your nails a little longer! Alright, that's all for now. Simple, right? You can also try it out!
