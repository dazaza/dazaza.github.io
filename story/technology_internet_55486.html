China Builds First Virus Automatic Analysis System

by 127702 on 2008-03-20 18:17:41

Rising recently released "Rising Kaka Internet Security Assistant 5.2 Edition". Rising claims that this marks the official launch of China's first world-class "Virus Automatic Analysis System."

Rising stated that this system is based on artificial intelligence technology and integrates several world-leading technologies such as malicious program behavior analysis and unknown virus analysis. Rising Kaka 5.2 Edition can directly obtain Trojan virus samples from user computers, analyze them, and automatically eliminate them in the shortest time to help Kaka users clear out account-stealing Trojan viruses. Under maximum load capacity, the system can automatically process 30,000-50,000 Trojan virus samples per day, fundamentally solving the industry-wide problem of the exponential increase in Trojan viruses.

The Rising 2007 Security Report pointed out that due to virus makers producing account-stealing Trojan viruses in a group-based and automated manner, the security industry is facing unprecedented challenges. The number of new viruses each year has increased from 60,000 in 2004 to 910,000 last year, and it is estimated that there will be 2 to 3 million new account-stealing Trojans in 2008. Rising security experts pointed out that the traditional "interception - analysis - upgrade" antivirus mode is completely unable to handle such a massive amount of Trojan viruses.

Rising's newly built "Virus Automatic Analysis System" allows users to install the Rising Kaka Assistant without performing any operations. The samples are processed by the "Virus Automatic Analysis System," and the results are instantly fed back to the user. Rising Kaka can then remove most account-stealing Trojan viruses, and the corresponding analysis results will also be provided simultaneously to Rising Antivirus Software.

Rising Vice President Mao Yiding said that this is a huge change in the industry model. Previously, virus interception and analysis were entirely dependent on manual work by antivirus vendors. The construction of the Virus Automatic Analysis System will allow tens of millions of Rising Kaka users to participate in the fight against Trojan viruses. This free security tool will become a powerful weapon for users to detect and eliminate Trojan viruses. By Gufeng
