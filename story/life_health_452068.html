Hysteroscopic treatment of ovarian cysts:

If the ovarian cyst is located under the uterine mucosa, medically referred to as a submucosal ovarian cyst, this type of cyst is suitable for removal using hysteroscopic electrocautery. Hysteroscopic surgery involves inserting a long, thin medical endoscope through the body’s natural passage (the cervical opening) into the uterine cavity to perform the operation. This method avoids any damage to the abdominal wall, offering advantages such as minimal trauma, short surgery time, minimal bleeding, and quick postoperative recovery.
