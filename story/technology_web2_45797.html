Web Hot or Not

by plidezus on 2008-01-29 18:56:33

I admit that I've engaged in clickbait with the title... This website is so simple it only has one function: to let you score randomly generated pages based on your first impression, rating them from one to ten points. Of course, if you're confident in your own site, you can click the 'add site' button to submit your own website; you'll likely see your page appear after about an hour or so. Also, if there's a website you're particularly dissatisfied with, you can submit it and see how others react.

If you want to check out sites you like or dislike without continuously clicking random and not finding them, you can visit this link: http://webhotornot.com/site/nhacdj.com/. Just input the site you wish to check after 'site/', for example, for qq.com, it would be http://webhotornot.com/site/qq.com/.

Go ahead and rate the sites you love or hate!
