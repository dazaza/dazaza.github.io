"Living with the Campus Beauty" - Hilarious Campus Co-rental Days

by jqrxass on 2009-10-12 14:34:43

Kang Jian is a cynical but idealistic college student. His ex-girlfriend's sudden departure made him lose faith in love, and he became depressed about life for a while. One day, Kang Jian accidentally spilled a bowl of soup on the body of the school beauty, Qin Keyi, at the school canteen... After their awkward first encounter, this pair of rivals kept bumping into each other until they eventually became co-renting roommates...

Living with the School Beauty Part 1

Right at this moment, I saw my ex-girlfriend on the opposite sidewalk, or rather, a girl who looked very much like my ex-girlfriend. Without confirmation, I could only think so. But my intuition told me that she was the girl who used to make me cheer her up when she was unhappy, and share her happiness with me when she was happy; the girl who asked me to warm her feet when it was cold; the girl who wanted me to peel chestnuts for her; the girl who left without saying goodbye, leaving me only a text message - Tong Chen. As for why she left without saying goodbye, I still haven't figured out the reason, so I dream of having the chance to face her and clear things up, even if she doesn't love me or never loved me...

Living with the School Beauty Part 2

"Stop looking down on people, do I look like a virgin?" Perhaps Qin Keyi also realized that her words were inappropriate, and her face quickly turned red after she spoke. Originally intending to ease the atmosphere, I didn't expect it to become more awkward. A thought suddenly flashed through my mind: Is Qin Keyi a virgin? Actually, this thought is quite normal for a guy. Based on my summary and induction over these many days, when a male encounters an attractive female for the first time, he will have the following thoughts in order: She's so beautiful - Does she have a boyfriend? - Is she a virgin? - It would be great if she were my girlfriend. And all these thoughts can occur within just a few seconds.

Living with the School Beauty Part 3

"Hmm... Miss Qin, I remember I already said it clearly, I wouldn't have any thoughts about ordinary girls. Although you might be very confident about your looks and figure, I'm sorry to say, you belong to the type of girls that won't make me have any thoughts. No need to apologize! You didn't do anything, right? You just hit me hard on the head and then slapped me hard, what's the big deal?! I'm tough! There's no need to mention the incident from the night before last either. I acted purely based on the principle of a conscientious and passionate young man. If it had been a seventy-year-old old lady who was being taken advantage of that night, I would have stepped in as well!"
