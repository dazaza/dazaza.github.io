1. "You Were Born When I Was Not Yet Born, I Was Born When You Are Already Old"

You were born when I was not yet born, I was born when you are already old. You regret that I was born late, I regret that you were born early.

You were born when I was not yet born, I was born when you are already old. Regret not being born at the same time, to be with you every day.

I was born when you were not yet born, you were born when I am already old. I am far from you in the horizon, you are far from me in the sea corner.

I was born when you were not yet born, you were born when I am already old. Transform into a butterfly to look for flowers, stay on fragrant grass every night.

2. "Divination Son" by Li Zhiyi

I live at the head of the Yangtze River, you live at the tail of the Yangtze River. I think of you every day but cannot see you, we drink the water of the Yangtze River together.

When will this water stop? When will this hatred end? I only wish your heart is like my heart, definitely not betraying the meaning of yearning.

3. "The Book of Songs•Bei Wind•Drum Beating"

The drum beats resoundingly, soldiers leap and use weapons. Building walls and cities in the land of Guo, I alone go south.

Following Sun Zi Zhong, pacifying Chen and Song. Not allowing me to return, my heart is full of worries.

Where to live? Where did I lose my horse? Where to find it? Under the forest.

Life and death are apart, with you making promises. Holding your hand, aging with you.

Alas, how far apart! Not reviving me! Alas, how distant! Not trusting me!

4. "Heaven!"

Heaven! I want to know you, may our lives never fade.

Mountains have no peaks, rivers dry up, winter thunder roars, summer rains fall as snow, heaven and earth merge, only then dare I part with you!

5. "Walking Again and Again"

Walking again and again, parting with you. We are more than ten thousand miles apart, each at the edge of the sky.

The road is difficult and long, who knows when we'll meet. Horses rely on the northern wind, southern birds nest on southern branches.

We are getting farther apart every day, clothes become looser day by day. Floating clouds hide the sun, wanderers don't care to return.

Thinking of you makes one grow old, years pass quickly. Discard and don't talk about it anymore, strive to eat well.

6. "Magpie Bridge Immortal" by Qin Guan

Fine clouds play tricks, flying stars transmit hate, crossing the Milky Way quietly. Meeting under the golden wind and dew, surpassing countless encounters in the human world.

Tender feelings are like water, good times are like dreams, how can one bear to look at the magpie bridge returning path! If two hearts are truly lasting, why must they always be together?

7. "Goose Hill Ci" by Yuan Haowen

Ask the world what love is, directly teaching life and death vows.

Flying together from the south to the north, old wings withstand cold and heat many times.

Joyful pleasures, painful partings, among them there are more foolish children.

You should have something to say, in the vast layers of clouds for ten thousand miles, in the evening snow on thousands of mountains, whose shadow would you go to?

By the Fen River road, lonely in those years with flutes and drums, desolate smoke remains level with Chu.

Summoning souls in Chu, what lamentations reach, mountain ghosts secretly cry in wind and rain.

Even heaven envies, not believing that orioles and swallows all turn to yellow earth.

For thousands of autumns and tens of thousands of ancient times, waiting for talented people to come, wildly singing and drinking, visiting the Goose Hill place.

8. "City of Rivers" by Su Shi

For ten years life and death have been vast and empty, not thinking about it, but never forgetting.

A lonely grave a thousand miles away, nowhere to talk about sadness. Even if we met, you probably wouldn't recognize me, dust covers my face, hair like frost.

Last night in a dream I suddenly returned home, by the small window, just combing makeup.

Looking at each other without words, only having thousands of tears. Guessing the place where intestines break every year, moonlit nights, short pine hills.

9. "Parting Thoughts" by Yuan Zhen

Having seen the vast sea, it's hard to see water, except for the clouds of Wu Mountain. Passing through flower clusters lazily looking back, half because of cultivation, half because of you.

10. "Butterfly in Love with Flowers" by Liu Yong

Standing leaning against a dangerous building with fine winds, looking far into the spring sorrow, dimly appearing at the horizon. Grass color and misty light remain in the sunset, silently who understands the intention of leaning on the railing.

Thinking of indulging in madness to get drunk, drinking wine and singing songs, forced joy still has no taste. Clothes belts gradually widen but never regret, for her I become emaciated and haggard.
