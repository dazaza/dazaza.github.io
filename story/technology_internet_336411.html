Rongcheng Tourism Caravan Heads to Five Provincial Capitals

Starting on the 21st of this month, Rongcheng's tourism promotion team will embark on a promotional tour in the form of a tourism "caravan," successively visiting five provincial capitals: Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and Taiyuan. The team aims to promote Rongcheng's tourism resources, including hotels at national bus stations. It is understood that this promotional event is expected to last for 11 days, focusing on promoting the city's "one center, two wings, three clusters" tourism layout resources.

According to available information, in 2009, Rongcheng hired domestic authoritative experts to plan and formulate the overall tourism development plan of "one center, two wings, three clusters." Starting from this year, Rongcheng will follow this new tourism master plan, integrating resources and coordinating development, striving to enhance the core competitiveness, international appeal, and openness of Rongcheng's tourism. "One center" refers to building the central group, transforming the city center into Rongcheng's tourism hub and high-end leisure health-care core area. "Two wings" refer to creating two major groups in the north and south. The northern group, relying on abundant mountain and sea resources, centers around Chengshan Head, developing ecological leisure resorts in Chaoyang Port and Chengshan Forest Park, modern characteristic countryside resorts in Xixia Kou, and international leisure resorts in Mashan Island and Swan Lake. The southern group, based on Chishan, Chashan, Haudangjia Group, and Shidao Fishery Port, focuses on creating health-care leisure resorts centered around Shidao Bay and Haudangjia Wento Resort Village.

It is introduced that this year Rongcheng will actively draw lessons from the bundled marketing model of "Friendly Shandong," guiding various enterprises to jointly invest, comprehensively plan and package, achieving unified publicity. Meanwhile, it will actively organize various tourism enterprises to participate in large-scale tourism trade fairs in Beijing, Chongqing, Shanghai, etc., in 2010, conducting various marketing activities through forms such as the "tourism caravan." This tourism promotion event will take the five provincial capitals as the center, radiating and driving surrounding cities for joint marketing. Special tourism products are popular, and hotel reservations can be made by calling the 7*24-hour free hotline 4007840076, extension 1. Additionally, in the overseas market, while consolidating the Korean market, this year will focus on the Russian Far East region market, coordinating related enterprises to conduct touring promotions.
