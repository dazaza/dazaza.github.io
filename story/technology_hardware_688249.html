Fire in residential house ignites one million cash, fire fighters save more than 400,000 (Picture)

by lpoihjkmkk on 2012-03-03 09:08:38

More than 400,000 yuan in cash was salvaged. The house burning scene. At 12:45 PM, the fire was effectively controlled. At 1:33 PM, the remaining fire was extinguished. At 1:40 PM, the officers and soldiers of the company handed over the scene to the local police and packed up their equipment. The landlord, holding the salvaged cash, insisted on giving it to the young men who had saved his life from the fire, but all were refused by the firefighters. The onlookers at the scene applauded enthusiastically and gave a thumbs up. In the severe cold weather with extremely low temperatures where water turned to ice instantly, and with the crowded and narrow space of the residential area on fire, the firefighters struggled to fight the fire and control the flames. At this time, the person who reported the fire panickedly informed the commander that there was nearly one million yuan in cash in the cabinet inside the room. The female owner had already fainted beside them. When the property safety of the people was threatened by the fire demon, the officers and soldiers no longer cared about the danger in the fire scene. Overcoming the fire demon and protecting the people's property was the call of the soldiers! Platoon leader Zhi Jinzhu led the assault team into the fire scene three times. Thick smoke, high temperature, and fierce fire did not stop the actions of the officers and soldiers. More than 400,000 yuan in cash was salvaged. If found one minute later, it might have turned to ashes.

On January 4th, at 11:53 AM, the Mizhi County Fire Station in Yulin, Shaanxi received an emergency call from the command center: A civilian house located 50 meters east of the Xijie Village Committee in Zhuchuan Town, Yuyang District, Yulin City, caught fire. After receiving the order, Captain Li Zhi of the Mizhi Station led seven commanders and fighters, quickly boarded the vehicle and rushed to the scene.

Before reaching Zhuchuan Town, they could already see the black smoke rising from the residential area far away. The fire truck struggled to enter the Xijie alley, approaching the burning building. After investigation, it was found that the burning residential house was a two-story building, connected to other houses on both sides. The fire source was on the second floor with fierce flames. Captain Li Zhi decisively ordered: First, set up two water guns on the first and second floors opposite the burning house for firefighting and preventing the fire from spreading; Second, a firefighter went around to the back side of the burning house to break the glass for smoke extraction; Third, set up a cordon to prevent onlookers from getting close and establish an observation post to report any potential collapse or falling debris hazards during the firefighting operation at any time; Fourth, the communicator quickly contacted the local police to arrive at the scene and coordinated with the Zhuchuan Airport Fire Team for water supply at the fire scene.
