China Telecom will release 189 number segment nationwide - Age1983

by hexiaoyao on 2008-11-15 20:57:07

On November 13, it was learned from inside China Telecom that this month, China Telecom will take the opportunity of the nationwide launch of the new CDMA number segment - the 189 number segment - to massively roll out its first centrally procured 3 million mid-to-high-end CDMA mobile phones. It will also adopt a subsidy method of giving away airtime and free phones to attract users to join the network.

In May this year, after the restructuring of the telecommunications industry, China Telecom spent 110 billion yuan to purchase the CDMA network and customers of China Unicom. Afterwards, China Telecom carried out a large-scale centralized procurement of 3 million CDMA mobile phones from mobile phone terminal manufacturers, mainly focusing on mid-to-high-end phones. However, on October 1 this year, when China Telecom officially took over the users, it was difficult to buy the latest centrally procured customized CDMA mobile phones at China Telecom business halls or social channels, and most of them were low-end products from the China Unicom era.

It is understood that the customized CDMA terminals by China Telecom will be launched on a large scale only during the release of the 189 number segment. Li Wang, Vice President of Yulong Communication, which has the largest share in the centralized procurement of China Telecom's CDMA mobile phones, revealed that Yulong's five products procured by China Telecom account for 70% of the mid-to-high-end market share, and all these products will be launched this month. Currently, the factory's daily shipment volume has reached the highest historical level.

According to insiders from China Telecom, the centrally procured mobile phones this time will not be sold to users at a higher price, and in order to attract users to adopt the new mobile phones and the new 189 number segment, China Telecom will also adopt promotional methods such as giving away mobile phones and airtime.
