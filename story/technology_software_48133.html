The current Internet age is indeed a bit crazy, with many netizens favoring traditional characters over simplified ones, and even creating their own characters. Although this trend may have long-term impacts on the education and development of young people, it can be very interesting to occasionally use it for entertainment or gaming purposes. Some people choose non-mainstream styles just for the sake of being unconventional. Many web users who usually use the simplified Chinese input method may not know how to input traditional characters. Apart from online conversion tools for traditional characters, today we will introduce a method to input traditional characters using the most common simplified input method.
