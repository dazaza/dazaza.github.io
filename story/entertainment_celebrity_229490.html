Andy Lau and Zhu Liqian return to Hong Kong | Andy Lau's underground relationship comes to light

by mu4527 on 2009-08-27 13:57:46

At about 6 pm on the evening of the 25th, Andy Lau returned from Malaysia's airport to Hong Kong. Around 10:35 pm, Subi arrived at Hong Kong Airport with Zhu Lixian by his side. Both were dressed in white and had several Buddhist beads on their hands.

A large number of media had already been waiting at the scene. As soon as Subi appeared, a large number of reporters rushed forward, causing great chaos, and one reporter even fell down. Along the way, Subi kept apologizing to the reporters. Throughout the journey, Subi tightly held Zhu Lixian's hand. He was very considerate and fully protected her, fearing that the reporters might collide with her. Meanwhile, Zhu Lixian guarded her belly throughout and seemed slightly heavier, leading the media present to speculate whether she was already pregnant.

When asked if Subi would make their relationship public, Subi did not give a direct response. However, judging from this appearance in public holding Zhu Lixian's hand, it was equivalent to indirectly confirming their relationship. When reporters asked if Lixian's mood had recovered, she quietly leaned on Subi without saying a word. Subi said: "I don't have anything special to say. Thank you all for your concern. You can think whatever you want. I don't plan to explain so much. Now I want to take her home to rest first, and we will talk to everyone later."

Zhu Lixian, the woman behind Andy Lau, recently lost her father Zhu Jincheng at the age of 73. The Zhu family residence is located in the upscale residential area of Bukit Jalil (Wuji Jiali) in Malaysia. After the news of Zhu's father's death spread, a large number of media gathered outside the Zhu residence.

Andy Lau, who is regarded as the "son-in-law of Malaysia," accompanied Zhu Lixian back to Malaysia to mourn her father. However, during the period when Subi attended the funeral of Zhu's father, he did not appear publicly.

In recent days, Andy Lau managed to evade interviews from nearly a hundred media outlets, going to great lengths to prevent the media from capturing images of him and Zhu Lixian at her father Zhu Jincheng's funeral. But finally, both appeared yesterday morning at the seventh-day memorial ceremony for her "father-in-law."

The relationship between Subi and Zhu Lixian has been shrouded in mystery. They met and started dating in 1986, and after Zhu Lixian became the invisible woman behind Subi, their romance was handled low-key for over 20 years, and they were never photographed together. There were rumors in 1992 that they had secretly married and had a daughter in 1996, although Subi consistently denied these claims. Their relationship has long been an open secret. In February this year, they were photographed together in Malaysia, sparking rumors that Subi was preparing to officially marry Zhu Lixian on her 43rd birthday. However, after the news broke, the wedding plans were once again put on hold.
