- Achieving Beautifully Fair Skin

To meet the needs of many beauty-conscious women, products with quick whitening effects and long-lasting results are constantly being developed. This year, numerous whitening products have been introduced, allowing you to whiten around the clock.

◆ Intensive Care

Up to 80% of Asian women hope for fairer skin. To achieve this quickly and effectively, Hau Tianzi, PR Associate Director of Olay Greater China, tells us that providing the skin with large amounts of nutrients over a short period can enhance intensive care. With concentrated care time and high concentrations of skincare ingredients, strong penetration capabilities can significantly improve the skin's condition.

Suitable for: Sisters who want to immediately feel the whitening effect.

How to apply: Masks are the most effective method for concentrated care. They allow whitening ingredients to be absorbed by the skin within minutes, thereby improving its dullness.

◆ Nighttime Repair

If the skin's metabolism is normal, it regenerates fastest at night. When lying down to sleep, the lymphatic and blood circulation in the upper body stagnates, leading to facial swelling and dullness upon waking. Therefore, nighttime is the best time for whitening repair.

Suitable for: Women with regular biological clocks who go to bed on time every night.

How to apply: After completing all skincare steps each night, don't forget to reward yourself with a good night's beauty sleep, allowing the skin to rest properly.

◆ Farewell to Dullness

Irregular biological clocks and ineffective resistance to free radicals lead to skin aging, making it impossible for the natural complexion to remain fair and flawless. Professor Zhang Liqing from the Department of Cosmetic Applications and Management at Hungkuang University points out that while using whitening products aims to suppress melanin production and lighten existing melanin, excessive use reduces the melanocytes that protect the skin from UV rays. Therefore, whitening should also focus on anti-oxidation to effectively remove free radicals and restore healthy skin tone.

Suitable for: Those not only pursuing fair skin but hoping for a healthy complexion.

How to apply: Choose products with antioxidant properties or select a complete series of whitening products for cleaning, conditioning, and repairing for better whitening effects.

◆ Makeup Whitening

If there's no way to instantly brighten your face but you still need to go out and see people, try letting whitening powder compacts save you! Brand Manager Huang Yiwen of Dior Cosmetics points out that whitening powder compacts shouldn't just be sunblock powders. Besides offering protection with SPF, they should genuinely provide whitening effects while applying makeup.

Suitable for: Those wanting their skin to appear fair and flawless while achieving makeup effects.

How to apply: After perfecting your daily whitening routine, use whitening powder compacts for makeup. Not only will it offer makeup effects, but it will continue to whiten while applying makeup.

- Radiofrequency Whitening Treatments for Immediate Brightening

Whether you're a natural bronze-skinned beauty or a woman with dullness issues, medical-grade whitening techniques can help you appropriately "whiten back." The whitening effects of Vitamin C have already been medically validated. Increasing the concentration of Vitamin C in the body and applying it correctly can turn your skin into "tender white tofu." ◆ Enhancing the Efficacy of Vitamin C Infusion

Dr. Huang Wencheng from the Plastic Surgery Department of Taipei Mackay Memorial Hospital points out that ultrasound infusion uses mechanical physical oscillation principles to open up cell gaps in the skin, increasing space and enhancing the permeability of agents entering the skin, allowing L-ascorbic acid to penetrate; iontophoresis uses voltage principles, exchanging positive and negative ions to rearrange the skin's surface charges, guiding agents into the skin.

◆ Strengthening Effects with Combined Whitening Treatments

To enhance the whitening effects of L-ascorbic acid, new combined whitening treatments and "triple-whitening cream" have been introduced to strengthen whitening, making the effects more lasting. Combined whitening peels are second-generation fruit acid peels, incorporating glycolic acid, lactic acid, citric acid, kojic acid, salicylic acid, etc., for micro-peeling, moisturizing, inhibiting, and lightening melanin. After thinning the stratum corneum, combining it with L-ascorbic acid infusion allows deeper penetration but costs more per session, approximately NT$2000.

The "triple-whitening cream" includes tretinoin, hydroquinone, and steroids. Hydroquinone whitens, tretinoin removes wrinkles, and steroids reduce inflammation. Economically affordable, monthly medication costs only a few hundred dollars, making it very popular and suitable for use with L-ascorbic acid.

◆ Triple Beauty Treatment for Dull Complexion

Sisters with dull complexions can choose Vitamin C infusions via iontophoresis, opening pores and skin gaps to absorb whitening components, penetrating tissues, removing melanin, and effectively suppressing melanin activity. Weekly 1-2 times, the entire course lasts about 6-10 sessions.

Besides the widely popular Vitamin C infusion, combining microparticle physical peels, L-ascorbic acid infusions, and ultrasonic spray cold compresses as a "triple beauty treatment" clears acne, reduces dullness, and increases stratum corneum hydration, improving skin dullness.

- Hot Spring Whitening Treatments for 100% Whiteness

To own fair and charming skin, sometimes it's worth raising your budget and visiting professional beauty salons for a full set of whitening treatments. Afterward, your face may really appear much fairer!

◆ Combining Massages for Better Results

Professional hot spring spas' skincare whitening treatments usually use smaller molecules or essential oils, paired with different massage methods such as lymphatic or venous massages, enhancing the effects. Generally, various whitening treatments show temporary results after one session. For long-term maintenance of fair skin, it's recommended to do about five sessions, each spaced about a week apart. Afterwards, combine home whitening DIY routines and thorough sun protection for long-lasting whitening.

◆ DIY Whitening Treatments

Whitening treatments on the market aren't cheap, costing around NT$2500-5000 per session. You can decide based on your financial capability. If your budget is limited, consider doing whitening treatments yourself for good results.

Materials: Whitening essential oils, choose a mix of 2 drops of rosehip oil, 2 drops of lavender oil, 2 drops of marigold oil, or about milliliters of bog mud or seaweed mud, and 2 teaspoons of rose powder.

Where to buy: Pure natural hot spring stores sell these.

Time: Apply once every 5 days.

Steps: 1. After washing your face with cleanser, apply the prepared whitening essential oil, massaging outward from the inside to allow absorption.

2. Mix bog mud + rose powder, then apply directly onto the oiled face.

3. Cover with a mask and leave on for 15 minutes.

4. Rinse off with water.

- Eating for Radiant Skin

Achieving radiant skin through diet is actually not difficult! Balanced nutrition is crucial. Nutritionist Zhou Yuru from the振兴医院(Revitalization Hospital) indicates that to own naturally radiant skin, consume foods rich in proteins, vitamins A, C, and E, minerals, fiber, essential fatty acids, and water. These nutrients assist in natural metabolism, expelling toxins, removing old keratin, replenishing collagen, and achieving antioxidant functions, making the skin fair and rosy.

◆ Proteins Make You Rosy and Fair

To have fair and radiant skin, balance your intake of collagen-rich proteins like soy milk, milk, tofu, and dairy products; and vitamin A-rich foods like ripe papayas, mangoes, citrus fruits, carrots, tomatoes, etc. These darker-colored fruits and vegetables supplement phosphorus, helping normal keratin layer metabolism. Vitamin C-rich foods like cherries, strawberries, kiwis, lemons, tomatoes, etc., are abundant sources, not only whitening but also possessing antioxidant functions and aiding collagen synthesis, making the skin fair and smooth. Vitamin E is mostly found in vegetable oils, so consume more sunflower oil and rapeseed oil.

Minerals like iron and zinc enhance skin metabolism, aiding a rosy glow. Other nutrients like fiber and essential fatty acids found in fruits, vegetables, and various foods, when consumed balancedly, expel toxins and boost metabolic functions. Additionally, if you love fruits, remember not to eat more than two servings (two pieces) daily, as fruits are high in carbohydrates and can cause weight gain, reducing beauty scores. Stimulating foods like coffee, tea, alcohol, and caffeinated beverages should be minimized to prevent melanin deposition in the skin.

Doctor Zhuang Yahui further supplements that in dietary habits, reducing excessive sun exposure and providing temperature and humidity-appropriate environments not only increase skin moisture but keep it consistently hydrated. Moreover, Zhuang says to get sufficient beauty sleep, as the low peak for skin repair is between 5 AM to 10 AM, aligning with Traditional Chinese Medicine's liver health theory. According to TCM meridian theory, the blood and qi in the liver and gallbladder meridians are most active from 11 PM to 3 AM. Resting during this time ensures proper detoxification, preventing liver qi stagnation or liver-gallbladder fire excess pathological reactions, ensuring good skin metabolism and appearance.

- Dressing for Fair Spring Colors

Diligently whitening daily, but melanin still sneaks in. In fact, clothing colors can create an illusion of fairness! Hereditary dark beauties who have little chance of becoming fair rely on clothing to enhance their complexion. Understanding color attributes reveals how clothing colors can slightly alleviate the "dark face alert" and save money.

◆ White Makes You a Spotlight

Colors differ in hue, brightness, and saturation, creating various feelings. Generally, blue, blue-green, and blue-purple cool tones give a comfortable and refreshing sensation, while red, orange, and yellow warm tones evoke warmth. In terms of brightness, high-brightness colors are refreshing, while low-brightness ones are warmer. Additionally, neutral colors like white and black are highly versatile. For sunny and hot springs and summers, white surpasses black. Pure white without saturation acts like a spotlight, highlighting other colors, so regardless of whether your skin is black, white, or yellow, white makes you stand out.

◆ Emphasizing Pink and High Saturation

This season's popular colors include pink, high saturation, and metallic tones. By targeting trendy colors, we can find the most suitable saturation for both fair and dark beauties. For instance, pink varies in brightness. Snow-white girls can use light pink to enhance their complexion, making their already fair skin more transparent; darker-skinned girls should avoid pale pink as it could make them look even darker and yellower. Saturated pink or bright peach adds value to dark beauties, making them look healthier and brighter.

◆ New Concept of Home Whitening

Whiteness goes beyond the surface. Replacing everything at home with white isn't the essence of home whitening. Only by addressing "anti-bacterial, sun protection, and dehumidification" comprehensively can you maintain a clean and beautiful home "face."

As seasons change, while focusing on "skin whitening," don't neglect home sun protection, moisturizing, wrinkle prevention, and deep antibacterial care.

◆ Sun Protection Lowers Your Home Temperature by 5 Degrees

White appears in spring and summer, bringing a fresh and pleasant visual sensation. Compared to other colors, it seems to exude a pure and restrained quality, which is why many become "white advocates."

Liao Shaojian from Horace Force and Happy Planning suggests six ways to lower your home's temperature by "5 degrees":

1. Use light-colored home decor fabrics: Replace curtains, tablecloths, bedding covers with lighter and paler materials.

2. Use curtain fabrics: Opt for blackout curtains with white adhesive backing. To achieve 100% light-blocking, 2-3 layers of adhesive backing are usually required. Bamboo blinds and linen roller blinds with natural textures bring a simple coolness to your home. Of course, Venetian blinds are excellent for shading but hard to clean and visually monotonous. Adding a pretty curtain fabric on top solves this, maintaining brightness while offering good light-blocking.

3. Place a flowing fountain, small fountain, or fish tank in your home for decorative purposes and the coolest sounds of summer.

4. Replace vibrant art decorations with natural floral designs or items with floral motifs.

5. Remove heavy carpets and replace them with hemp "area rugs" for decoration.

6. Add a cool rush grass cushion to overheated cloth sofas or leather seats.

◆ True Whiteness Comes from Within

Home whitening isn't just about surface freshness. Like human skin, mere superficial whiteness isn't enough. True "home whitening" must start from deep antibacterial concepts. Especially during the transition between spring and summer, when temperatures and humidity are high, it's prime time for mold and dust to multiply. Besides regular cleaning, understanding the latest developments in preventive and antibacterial technology is essential knowledge for home whitening.
