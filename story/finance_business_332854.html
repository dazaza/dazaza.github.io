Two sessions appear new functions, Yan Qi reveals truth after drinking, Tao Ran Ju brand increases value by nearly 5 billion. A committee member wrote a rhyming poem: "Want to make money in the meeting, still Tao Ran. Astonishing people for self-interest, too shameless."

National Committee member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Han Fangming revealed on Weibo that Yan Qi was proud of her proposal to "shut down internet cafes" which increased the value of Tao Ran Ju brand by 5 billion.

Yan Qi, general manager of Tao Ran Ju Catering Group and national committee member of CPPCC from Chongqing is very interesting. Her suggestion to close internet cafes caused a huge stir, and yesterday, national committee member of CPPCC, Han Fangming revealed that Yan Qi herself was not troubled, but was "proud" of the topic that made her Tao Ran Ju brand increase by 5 billion.

Yan Qi first leaked to the media about submitting a proposal to "close all social internet cafes and let the government run public internet cafes." The reaction was enormous, netizens strongly opposed, criticism came from all sides, and her Tao Ran Ju company website was frequently hacked.

Many media outlets contacted her for interviews. This Yan committee member claimed she received over 200 calls from media reporters in one day. She said she was surprised, "I never expected such a big reaction." She clarified, "I am not suggesting the government completely ban internet cafes, but rather abolish unlicensed, illegal internet cafes and problematic ones, no longer issue licenses, and gradually implement government-led internet cafe operations." She showed the "official proposal" to the reporters, which did not mention banning social internet cafes, but talked about the government running public welfare internet cafes.

Yan Qi even denied the authenticity of the initial report, claiming she did not give an interview to that reporter, despite the clear dialogue in the article. Yan Qi also complained to the reporters, being harassed by the media and pressure unable to sleep, "My mind is like glue."

Unexpectedly, her words at the dinner table were accidentally leaked by other committee members. Besides worrying about the country and children, perhaps she had her own calculations. National committee member of CPPCC, Han Fangming revealed on the People's Network Weibo that on the evening of March 10th, "a group of people were invited to Yan Qi committee member's Tao Ran Ju banquet, during which she was quite proud of her proposal to close internet cafes. She also claimed that her Tao Ran Ju brand increased in value by five billion due to this topic." The committee member Han heard this and was very unhappy, thus mocking her, "So you made five billion from attending the two sessions?"

Han's Weibo also mentioned that a committee member wrote a rhyming poem: "Wants to make money in the meeting, still Tao Ran. Astonishing people for self-interest, too shameless."

If what committee member Han Fangming said is true, if it wasn't just a joke at the dinner table, then from what committee member Yan said offhandedly, we can only say, committee member Yan, you are truly a good actor.
