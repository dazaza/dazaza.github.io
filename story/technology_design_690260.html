Deputy director is suspected of still receiving salary after transfer for many years, but the local authority claims there's no double payment

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-05 09:20:51

A staff member of the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County, Yancheng, reported to the news hotline 025-96110 of Jiangsu News Radio that a vice director of the bureau had been transferred to work in a town for several years, but his salary was still paid by the Finance Bureau. This raised doubts among the personnel. A reporter from our station rushed to Sheyang for investigation and interview, and found that the reported vice director did not receive double salaries. The treatment method for hemorrhoids. In Sheyang, the reporter met with the complainant Mr. Song. Mr. Song told the reporter that he is an employee of the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County. He found that the name of the former deputy director of the bureau, Tang Youbiao, was still on the list for housing provident fund contributions. Mr. Song recalled that Director Tang Youbiao had already been transferred away from the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County in 2008 to serve in Panwan Town of Sheyang County, and in 2011, he was transferred again to Haihe Town. According to convention, his salary and benefits should have been transferred along with his personnel relationship, but as of January 2012, Tang Youbiao's salary was still being paid by the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County. Mr. Song thought this was very suspicious: "I saw the list for housing provident fund contributions, and I saw the name Tang Youbiao. He was transferred away from our unit in 09, so how could his name still appear on our unit's housing provident fund contribution list? I feel very skeptical. This has never happened in the history of our Finance Bureau. He is no longer a staff member of our unit." As for whether Tang Youbiao received money after being transferred to the town, Mr. Song said he was not sure. To verify this matter, the reporter went to the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County. After understanding the reporter's intention, Director Jiang Tingbin of the Finance Bureau introduced that since the complainant had also reported the matter to the local Discipline Inspection Commission, the Party Group of the Finance Bureau had already conducted an investigation into the issues raised by the complainant. After their investigation, they found that Tang Youbiao's salary was indeed still being paid by the Finance Bureau of Sheyang County, but the town where Tang Youbiao currently works does not pay him a salary: "After we investigated, we did not find any evidence of double salary payments, this is definitely certain. Secondly, we checked our system, and there is no possibility of duplicate names." Director Jiang Tingbin explained that the civil servant salary payments in Sheyang County are made with one number per person, and all county and township civil servants' salaries are paid by the centralized payment center of the Sheyang County Treasury. The reporter went to the center to check the system and requested the staff to randomly extract the salary payment table for Panwan Town in January 2011 and the salary payment table for Haihe Town in January 2012. Indeed, Tang Youbiao's name did not appear on either of them. Then, if Tang Youbiao's job was adjusted twice but he still received his salary from the Finance Bureau, would it violate regulations? Director Jiang Tingbin of the Finance Bureau explained that it does not: "Normally, the salary follows the person. Because of the county-township coordination, all salaries are uniformly distributed, so whether he goes or not has no effect on his salary. Anyway, his salary remains the same, and it is impossible for him to receive double payments. Whether he goes to the township or stays here, his salary remains the same. The salary standard is the same, all salaries are transparent." Director Jiang Tingbin told the reporter that the Finance Bureau has already sent someone to contact Tang Youbiao and suggested that he immediately transfer his personnel relationship to the township where he is located to avoid misunderstandings.
