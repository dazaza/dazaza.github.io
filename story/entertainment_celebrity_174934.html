Southern Metropolis Daily, July 10th report: Recently, Zhou Libo, a popular figure in Shanghai and the founder of "Shanghai-style clean mouth," has been caught up in a "bigamy scandal." On the 5th of this month, an online blog titled "Zhou Libo's Wife Zhang Jie" appeared, publishing 23 articles over four consecutive days, revealing many of Zhou Libo's "ugly affairs," such as "abandoning his wife for a rich woman," claiming that they were not divorced and stating "I will sue him for bigamy!" causing a huge uproar. Yesterday afternoon at 2 PM, the blog posted the latest article, "Lawyer Statement of Zhang Jie," stating that a divorce lawsuit had been submitted to the court.

Subsequently, our reporter contacted Miss Tang, Zhou Libo's agent. She stated that she would not give an interview and sent the lawyer's statement via fax to the reporter. The statement mentioned that Zhou Libo had signed a divorce agreement in January of this year regarding the "ugly affairs" rumors, Zhou Libo said, "The clear will be clear, and the turbid will be turbid." Thus far, both parties have held different opinions on whether they are divorced or not.

Zhang Jie, Zhou Libo's wife's blog claims "Zhou Libo and Zhang Jie are still legally married"

Since the 5th, a series of articles have been published on the online blog titled "Zhou Libo's Wife Zhang Jie," revealing that Zhou Libo and his wife Zhang Jie were not yet divorced but cohabited with a certain rich woman. The blogger claimed, "I am still his legal wife now. I hope the media contacts me; I will not only sue him for bigamy but also hold a press conference to prove that everything I say is true!"

Yesterday afternoon at 2 PM, the blog published another article titled "Public Lawyer Statement of Zhang Jie," stating that a divorce lawsuit was filed with the court on the 9th, which has been accepted.

The statement detailed three points regarding related matters: "One, the marital relationship between Zhang Jie and Zhou Libo has not been dissolved. Legally speaking, Zhang Jie and Zhou Libo are still legally married. Two, if someone lives with another person under the name of being married while having a spouse, it should be handled as bigamy. Three, Ms. Zhang Jie has officially filed a divorce lawsuit with the Changning District People's Court of Shanghai, which has been accepted and will soon go to trial."

Additionally, the reporter found that starting from around 4 PM, the content of the blog articles could no longer be displayed. Clicking on the titles would directly redirect to the homepage of the blog.

Zhou Libo's side claims "A divorce agreement was signed in January of this year"

Yesterday afternoon, our reporter contacted Miss Tang, Zhou Libo's agent. Upon hearing the reporter's intentions, she immediately stated, "We do not accept interviews. A statement has already been issued, and we will no longer respond to this matter." Subsequently, Miss Tang sent a fax of the "Lawyer Statement of Zhou Libo" to the reporter.

In the statement, Zhou Libo's lawyer said, "Both parties have confirmed to me that they indeed signed a divorce agreement in January of this year. In the agreement, both parties clearly stipulated the ownership of property, and Mr. Zhou has already begun to earnestly fulfill it. Several months later, Ms. Zhang changed her demands. I have not seen any evidence of fraud or coercion during the signing of the agreement. If the blog posts were indeed written by Ms. Zhang, it is regrettable."

Blog revelations

In the blog titled "Zhou Libo's Wife Zhang Jie," the blogger revealed many negative news about Zhou Libo. Regarding this, Zhou Libo stated in the lawyer's statement, "There is no need to respond to these rumors. The clear will be clear, and the turbid will be turbid."

1. Abandoning his wife for a rich woman?

Since the 5th, the blogger successively published multiple articles accusing Zhou Libo of "abandoning his wife for a rich woman," such as "Zhou Libo's Birthday Gift - A Porsche from the Rich Woman," "The Rich Woman Supporting Zhou Libo May Be Zhejiang Merchant Hu Jie," and "Severely Ill and Abandoned by Zhou Libo." Among them, it was mentioned that in 2001, when Zhou Libo was out of luck in business, Zhang Jie took him in. However, in 2008, when Zhang Jie fell seriously ill, Zhou Libo abandoned her and threw himself into someone else's arms.

2. Resorting to domestic violence for divorce?

On the 7th, the blogger published an article titled "Zhou Libo's Domestic Violence": "The taste Zhou Libo talks about seems very elegant, but at home, he always expects others to serve him food and clothes, and whenever things don't go his way, he flies into a rage against me. When I discovered his affair with the rich woman, he threatened me by choking my neck, saying that if I told anyone or refused to divorce him, he would kill me and our child..."

3. Future plans to expose his drug use?

On the 6th, the blogger published an article titled "Zhou Libo's Drug Use," but the content only had one sentence, "To be continued..." without further details. (Source: Southern Metropolis Daily, Author: Lu Xin, Yuan Xin)
