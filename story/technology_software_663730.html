Common questions about ticket purchase

by anonymous on 2012-02-12 14:02:39

I. Order-related questions

Q: Can I book the train ticket I need by phone?

A: Hello, currently we do not support phone booking. You can submit an order online.

Q: Do you support cash on delivery?

A: No. Train tickets are special commodities with time limits. Therefore, we cannot conduct transactions in a guaranteed manner. The service concept of 114 Ticketing Network is agency purchase. The concept of agency purchase means that customers need to prepay for their orders first, and then 114 Ticketing Network will process your order within the advance sale period of train tickets.

Q: I am not a member of 114 Ticketing Network. Can I book tickets?

A: Hello, for the first booking, you can directly fill out the order. After submission, the system will automatically register you. The username is the mobile phone number you filled in, and the login password is the last six digits of your mobile phone number!

II. Confirmation related questions

Q: After I place an order and pay successfully, can you guarantee that there will be tickets?

A: Hello, because train tickets are sold nationwide and are booked and sold at any time, we cannot guarantee that there will absolutely be tickets. However, we will help you buy them on the day of the advance sale period as soon as possible and notify you as soon as possible whether there are tickets.

III. Advance Sale Period related questions

Q: What is the advance sale period for train tickets?

A: The advance sale period for train tickets refers to the number of days in advance tickets can be purchased. That is, the maximum number of days in advance you can purchase train tickets. The advance sale period may differ for trains of different levels; it may also differ for trains of the same level at different stations; even at the same station, the advance sale period may vary at different times. Specific details are subject to the notification from the railway department.

IV. Ticket agency purchase issues

Q: Can I book round-trip tickets, connecting tickets or through tickets?

A: Since each order can only book up to five tickets with the same date, same train number, and same seat type, if you need to purchase round-trip tickets, connecting tickets or through tickets, you need to place separate orders. And we cannot guarantee that the round-trip tickets, connecting tickets or through tickets you reserved will definitely have tickets. If any of the round-trip tickets, connecting tickets or through tickets has been issued, you must accept it, and we do not bear the responsibility for ticket refunds.

Q: Can I book student tickets, children's tickets, military personnel tickets, group tickets on 114 Ticketing Network?

A: Hello, sorry, 114 Ticketing Network (http://www.114piaowu.com/) does not currently provide agency purchase for all special train tickets, including student tickets, children's tickets, military personnel tickets, group tickets, etc.

Q: If I book multiple tickets at the same time, can our seats be arranged together or at designated locations?

A: Hello, we will try our best to help you purchase tickets for adjacent seats. However, the issuance of train tickets is random. Generally, tickets bought simultaneously are adjacent seats. The specific arrangement is subject to the issuance result.

V. Logistics distribution issues

Q: If the tickets are delivered by courier and I don't get the tickets, what should I do?

A: Hello, there are rules for placing orders. You can only place an order within our delivery range. After the successful issuance of the tickets, we will deliver them to you within the specified time of your chosen delivery method. Please rest assured with our agency purchase service. (If the delivery fails due to incorrect recipient address or no one to sign for it, the loss will be borne by the customer; if the delivery is delayed and the train has departed without the tickets being delivered, the loss will be borne by us.)

Q: When can I receive the train tickets?

A: For the delivery of train tickets, we offer next-day delivery and express delivery. Please check the delivery standards based on your order information.

VI. Refund issues

Q: If I want to return the train tickets after signing for them, what should I do?

A: Due to the special nature of train tickets, we do not provide refund services. If you need to refund the tickets, please go to the station indicated on the ticket to process the refund. (To refund real-name system train tickets, relevant documents need to be carried.)
