Recently, everything has been going wrong. The original plan for the housekeeper fell through due to family issues, and after switching several nannies, I still wasn't quite satisfied. It left me both physically and mentally exhausted. So tired!! Lately, I've been wondering if I have depression? I'm constantly feeling disheartened, unable to muster up energy for anything, and my mood is at its worst. Every day, I sit in front of the computer watching boring Korean dramas until 1 or 2 in the morning, only to be woken up by little Yo at 7 a.m.. After finishing all the tasks, I continue sitting here watching Korean dramas. When nearing the end of one, I thought: No, this can't go on! I need to cheer up and bake a cake as a treat for myself! A ZA~. The cake is still in the oven, and the smell of it fills the room, lifting my spirits significantly. It seems that only baking can help me get out of this depression.

This Tiramisu was hastily made to celebrate our fourth wedding anniversary. I wasn't too satisfied with how it turned out. Today, I'll focus on recommending these ladyfingers.
