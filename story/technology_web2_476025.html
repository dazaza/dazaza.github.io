Love Tips

To be or not to be, that is a question; to go online via QQ or BBS, that is a question; to pursue a girl or wait for a girl to pursue you, that is a question, and it is even a big question.

The highest technique of picking up girls: tearing your face off, throwing away your self-esteem, emptying your wallet, racking your brains, using up all your time, exhausting all your energy. There will be nothing unachievable.

Love complication: depression due to secret love; stuttering at the beginning of passionate love; hyperactivity after passionate love; amnesia due to extramarital affair; mania plus intermittent self-mutilation due to breakup.

When a woman completely ignores the bill but notices how much tip a man pays, she begins to date this man; when she starts to notice items on the bill and tells the man not to pay too much tip, she has fallen in love with this man.

Quick marriage: newly married like lightning, divorced like autumn wind. The flowers are bright and the moon is round tonight, and we will go separate ways tomorrow morning.

Men are like grandsons who obey every order while pursuing women; like sons who learn to backtalk after engagement; like fathers who give orders after marriage; like rabbits who run away in the end.

Rabbits don't eat the grass beside their burrows; old cows like to eat tender grass; good horses don't eat cut grass; there is always grass growing somewhere over the horizon. Once getting married, people turn into grass.

I cry not because I'm sad, but because I'm extremely happy. I can't live like normal people. If you leave me, I don't know what my life will be like.

Girls' psychology is very subtle sometimes. You may stay by her side faithfully for many years, and she won't fall in love with you; but once you fall in love with others, she can't stand it anymore and realizes that she actually likes you.

Passionate people always think like this: I have moved myself, so what reasons does she have not to be moved by me? But persistent pursuit can only prove that you are a persistent person, no more than that.

Don't say women are mean, but the heart also needs space and time to move. If you pursue her too tightly, how can she have time to savor your goodness?
