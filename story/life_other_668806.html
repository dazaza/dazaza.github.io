Chopped to death while looking for an old friend

by 173764792 on 2012-02-18 15:11:26

The incident occurred in Liangchong Village, Panyu. The injured person is out of danger but still unable to speak. The police are making every effort to apprehend the perpetrator.

Just because they knocked on the wrong door, two brothers were hacked, one dead and one injured. At around 2 PM on February 15th, Xiao Fan and his brother, who hadn't lived there for over a year, came to apartment No. 90, Dakou North Dong Street, Liangchong Village, Panyu District, Guangzhou, to look for a friend. Unexpectedly, they were attacked by two men. Xiao Fan's brother was hacked to death on the spot, and Xiao Fan was severely injured and rushed to the ICU of Panyu Central Hospital. By yesterday evening, he had come out of life-threatening condition. Currently, the specific situation is under investigation.

Friend-seeking ends in death

Apartment No. 90 on Dakou North Dong Street is an old yard with a brick house without paint on the first floor, marked with a house number. Inside, there is a two-story residential building, each floor divided into six rooms, self-numbered from 01 to 05. The incident happened at the door of Room 205. Yesterday afternoon, when reporters from Xin Kuai Bao arrived at Dakou North, there were still two security guards guarding the entrance of the apartment where the incident occurred. The yard was quiet. Going upstairs along the stairs, there were still several dark red bloodstains on the stairs. Room 205, where the incident occurred, has been sealed with a seal, but the window was not locked. Through the window, daily necessities placed inside could be seen.

Mr. Su, the tenant of Room 101, told reporters that the injured person was named Xiao Fan. He used to live in this courtyard for many years, and many people in the courtyard were familiar with him. After working in Zhaoqing last year, he never returned. This time, while traveling from Sichuan to Zhaoqing, he passed through Guangzhou and thought of meeting his former workmates. Around noon, he drank a bottle of brandy with a workmate on the first floor. At around 2 PM, he went upstairs to find another workmate, "Fat Kid." However, no one knew which room "Fat Kid" lived in, so the brothers went up themselves. Unexpectedly, shortly after going upstairs, Xiao Fan ran down covered in blood seeking help.

According to the boss of a nearby store, the incident occurred at around 2:30 PM on the 15th. A "bloody person" ran to her door asking for help, claiming someone was chasing him to kill him. She was scared and dared not go out, immediately calling the police. Only after the police arrived did she dare to go out. "He was covered in blood, stabbed several times in the abdomen, with intestines coming out. Later, the 120 emergency services arrived and rushed him to the hospital for treatment," said the boss. The suspect fled after hacking the victim.

Speculation it was done by a fugitive

An 84-year-old grandmother in the courtyard told reporters that the courtyard belongs to her daughter-in-law, but she doesn't live there; instead, she manages it and collects rent. Most tenants are outsiders. Recalling the events of the afternoon of the 5th, the grandmother said she was taking a nap when she suddenly heard someone shouting for help. When she went outside, she found blood all over the road, and she was also scared and didn't know what had happened. Later, when the police arrived, she learned that someone had been stabbed to death. However, regarding the cause of death, many neighbors have different opinions.

However, Mr. Xu from Room 105 thought it was unlikely. His house is directly below the incident site, Room 205. He told reporters that although he usually didn't pay attention to who lived upstairs, he could confirm that it was a man living alone, walking loudly and making the floorboards bang. "Knocking on the wrong door shouldn't lead to someone being killed! An apology would suffice!"

Some workmates suspected that the resident of Room 205 might be a fugitive, killing someone possibly mistaking them for a police officer.

Injured person out of danger

Regarding this case, reporters learned from the Panyu District police yesterday that after the incident, the Panyu police immediately sent personnel to the scene to handle the situation and conduct investigations. Currently, the police have formed a special task force dedicated to solving the case and apprehending the suspects.

Yesterday afternoon, when reporters arrived at Panyu Central Hospital, Xiao Fan was still receiving treatment in the ICU, with two police officers standing guard outside. According to relevant persons from the Panyu police, the two victims surnamed Fan, the older brother was 46 years old, and the younger brother was 34. Both are from Longchang, Sichuan. Since the suspect fled after committing the crime, more specific details need further investigation. It is understood that after several days of emergency treatment, the younger brother with serious injuries had come out of life-threatening condition by yesterday evening, but he was still unable to speak.
