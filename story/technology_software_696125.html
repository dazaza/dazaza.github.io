□ Correspondent Yang Xijin, Witness Wuzhe You Yanran. Calmly, Mr. Feng spent several days tidying up his belongings and discarded the clothes he wore during the crime. At around 6 p.m., he boarded a water truck heading back to Hebei Nenye, detouring through Ningbo.

The store had closed for the day, but that evening an incident occurred. Why did Mr. Feng suddenly attack the boss, Mr. Gao? Upon investigation, it was revealed that Mr. Hong's attitude greatly angered Mr. Feng, reminding him of the grievances he faced while job hunting. Unable to contain his anger, Mr. Feng took out a fruit knife from his bag and stabbed Mr. Hong. He didn't know how many times he stabbed, but when he saw the blood, it seemed like he woke up from his frenzy and ran away.

A few lottery scratch cards became the trigger for this murder case.

● During the trial

On October 24th, Mr. Feng planned to visit a friend in Dong'an but couldn't get in touch with them. Three days later, he returned to Zhengzhou to seek another friend. On the night of October 30th at about 9 p.m., police successfully apprehended Mr. Feng at his friend's residence.

The welfare lottery shop was next to a hair salon. A worker there told reporters that the lottery shop officially closed on October 20th, and the victim was one of the workers there - a stout man wearing glasses. However, they did not know his name.

The next day after the incident, reporters arrived at the scene. The shutter of the lottery shop was tightly closed, and about two meters from the entrance on the sidewalk, there was a large pool of dried blood.

On October 20th at around 7 p.m., a homicide occurred outside a welfare lottery shop on Kangzhuang South Road in Jiangbei district. A young man was killed, and the suspect fled. After several days of meticulous investigation, the police of Jiangbei District captured the criminal suspect in Zhengzhou, Henan Province on October 30th.

During the trial, Mr. Feng anxiously asked about the condition of the deceased, Mr. Hong, hoping that she was still alive. But there is no such thing as regret medicine in this world.

Mr. Feng stopped running only at the intersection of Kangzhuang South Road and Beinei Ring Road. Then he hailed a taxi and returned to the construction site.

【Neighborhood recollections】

In August of this year, introduced by a classmate, Mr. Feng came to work on a construction site in Ningbo’s Hongtang area. By October, the workload at the construction site increased, and Mr. Feng's savings were almost depleted. Often short of money, he wanted to find another job but failed. The immense pressure often left him anxious and irritable.

The suspect inquired about the victim's condition, hoping she was still alive.

On the second day after the incident in the afternoon, when reporters arrived at the scene, the lottery shop's rolling shutter was tightly closed. About two meters from the entrance on the sidewalk, there was a large patch of residual blood.

On the evening of October 20th, a fatal case occurred at a welfare lottery shop on Kangzhuang South Road in Jiangbei district. Outside the shop, a young man was killed, and the suspect fled. After several days of meticulous investigation, on October 30th, the police officers of the Jiangbei Public Security Bureau captured the criminal suspect in Zhengzhou, Henan.

After detailed investigation, on October 30th, the 1987-born suspect Mr. Feng was arrested in Zhengzhou, Henan.

According to Officer Sun from the Jiangbei Public Security Bureau, after being captured, Mr. Feng said that he knew he would eventually be caught by the police. He originally planned to stay in Zhengzhou for a while before returning home to wait for the police to come, "I didn't expect you to catch me so quickly."

Why did he go so far? According to the suspect's confession, it was just because of a dispute with the victim, Mr. Hong, over buying lottery tickets, which led to him stabbing her with a knife.

After being arrested, Mr. Feng confessed to the events leading up to the incident.

Finding a job was very disheartening, and a few words of argument led to the use of a knife.

At around 7 p.m., most shops on the road were closed, but the lottery shop was still open. I was hungry, so I ate some barbecue. After finishing my meal, I passed by the lottery shop and decided to buy some lottery tickets, hoping for good luck.

The lottery shop where the incident occurred was located on the outer section of Kangzhuang South Road in Jiangnan, west of Ningbo's Freight Market. On both sides were various car repair shops, fast food restaurants, and other large establishments with heavy pedestrian traffic.

After buying a few lottery numbers, I purchased 78 scratch cards. When I scratched them, I found that I won 30 yuan. So, I handed the scratch cards to Mr. Hong (the victim). Later, without saying anything, she gave me 30 yuan worth of scratch cards instead of cash. When I scratched these new cards, I found they contained no money. I demanded she return the previous 30 yuan, but she refused, claiming I hadn't clearly stated whether I wanted the cards or the cash.
