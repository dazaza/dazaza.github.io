It was claimed that the boy was admitted to Peking University after talking with the former president of the university for 5 minutes.

by trgfh70wdf on 2012-03-02 09:34:03

What are the qualifications that enable Wang Chenzhou to charm the examiner in just a few minutes of chatting? Wang Chenzhou, as the sports minister of the Student Union and winner of the first prize in the National High School Mathematics League, has thus obtained the qualification for guaranteed admission. This sunny young man is good at summarizing and reflecting during his normal study, and he is most enthusiastic about sports activities in his spare time. He was lucky enough to be a Nanjing student representative to go to Beijing to watch the Olympic Games, precisely because of his passion for sports that led to his brilliant exchange with Academician Xu later on.

Academician Xu Zhihong and his team also admitted science experts Ji Jing and Li Fan. Both students won awards in science competitions to secure their guaranteed admission qualifications. Being in a foreign language school, they both possess excellent English proficiency and a solid foundation in humanities, and are poised and confident.

The original itinerary in Beijing did not include the Olympic Table Tennis Hall at Peking University (PKU), but Wang Chenzhou, who harbors affection for PKU, specially went to visit it. At that moment, he associated that PKU must have done more than just build a venue for the Olympics; he had always wanted to find out the answer. This question opened up Academician Xu's conversation box. Xu Zhihong said that the table tennis hall was built by PKU raising over 200 million yuan itself, and the process was extremely challenging. More notably, PKU had more than 3600 student volunteers serving the Olympics. Academician Xu left a deep impression on this sunny boy, and after reviewing his outstanding resume, decided on the spot to admit him. Wang Chenzhou could never have imagined that his guaranteed admission examination would be so brief.

"In my meeting with former PKU president Xu Zhihong, I asked a question: about PKU's Sinology education, later..." This sentence appeared in a short article written by Li Junjie, who was directly admitted to PKU's Geography major. Due to the absence of subsequent exams, she could only put ellipses after the word "later". This girl who loves reading Zhuangzi and asking "why" is the first-prize winner of the national geography competition and has a particular fondness for classical Chinese literature. In her dialogue with Academician Xu, she didn't rush to "sell" herself but instead wanted to understand whether PKU's Sinology education suited her. Academician Xu Zhihong said that PKU indeed places great emphasis on literature and history, even establishing a special Sinology Research Institute. However, he frankly admitted dissatisfaction with the current level of Sinology among college students, so all students entering PKU, regardless of their discipline, must undergo Sinology cultivation. Talking about Sinology, he believed that classics never become outdated; for example, the concept of sustainable development is derived from Sinology. Clearly, the girl who posed such a question impressed Academician Xu, leading to her fortunate direct admission.

Reporter Huang Yan

"What contributions has Peking University made to the Olympics?" Last December, Academician Xu Zhihong, former president of Peking University, came to Nanjing Foreign Language School to converse with students, and student Wang Chenzhou boldly asked the question. Unexpectedly, within less than five minutes of dialogue, Wang Chenzhou was named and "taken away" for direct admission, without needing to participate in PKU's guaranteed admission written or oral exams. Similarly, boys Li Fan and girls Li Junjie and Ji Jing were also directly admitted to PKU. It is understood that at the start of the new semester, apart from 76 students being selected by prestigious overseas universities, Nanjing Foreign Language School received good news of 165 students being recommended to 26 top domestic universities.
