"Introduction to the Episodes of 'Hawk Squadron' - Film and Television Information - Xuntu Network"

by yuliangnet on 2009-11-10 22:31:38

Episode 1:

After returning from a flight training mission, Colonel Chen Ning of the Flying Regiment received an urgent summons from Commander Yang at the base. Commander Yang ordered him and Political Commissar Huang Jia Shi to form an offensive rapid response unit - the Falcon Squadron - consisting of elite Air Force pilots and the latest equipment. Commander Yang required them to conduct creative flight missions and, with the cooperation of the Central Aviation Service Team, become a sharp-edged Air Force unit in the shortest possible time. They were also allowed to independently select pilots from the entire Air Force. Chen Ning went to a certain aviation division to select pilots. Ma He was selected for the Falcon Squadron due to Xiao Bang's performance, and they were set to compete in the sky. However, Xiao Bang's aircraft had an accident and he had to parachute out. Ma He shot down the unmanned aircraft in the air. Impressed by Ma He's bravery and calmness in the air, Chen Ning decided to choose Xiao Bang instead. After Xiao Bang's accident, Ma He became hesitant about joining the Falcon Squadron.

Episode 2:

Xiao Bang was under investigation due to the aircraft accident. Chen Ning and Huang Jia Shi disagreed on whether to transfer Ma He, but ultimately Huang Jia Shi was convinced by Chen Ning. They also decided to transfer mechanic Zheng Wu Yi to the Falcon Squadron. Ma He, along with other elite pilots like Wu Wei Tao, Zhou Kai, and Shang Zhan, joined the Falcon Squadron. Each of them had their own strengths and were competitive with each other. During a missile test launch conducted by Ma He using a J-10 fighter jet, there was an accident where the missile failed to detach from the launcher. Jiang Jing Chao, a missile engineer from the Central Aviation Service Team, showed great courage while dealing with the dud missile, catching Ma He's attention. Meanwhile, Wu Wei Tao quietly developed a crush on Jiang Jing Chao.

Episode 3:

Through the efforts of Political Commissar Huang Jia Shi, Xiao Bang was also transferred to the Falcon Squadron. Huang Jia Shi introduced everyone through text messages on their phones and educated them that individuality should be protected, but coordination and unity are more important. They should help each other as a team. The Falcon Squadron then engaged in an eight-on-eight air combat exercise with friendly forces. After the exercise began, Chen Ning suddenly ordered only Ma He and Wu Wei Tao to face off against the eight enemy aircraft. Despite being outnumbered, Ma He and Wu Wei Tao achieved complete victory with their superior flying skills. Unexpectedly, during the air combat, Chen Ning discovered an outstanding pilot on the opposing side, Mao Xiao Yi, and transferred him to the Falcon Squadron. Ma He's performance impressed Jiang Jing Chao, and she began to take notice of him.
