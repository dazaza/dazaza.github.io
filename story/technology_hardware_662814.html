Graduates shoot micro-films to encourage college entrance examination students - Kuai Xun one second release

by shishi8654 on 2012-02-10 16:30:46

The film crew members took photos during their spare time. Still photos. Do you still remember the days of hard work and eventual success in your senior year of high school? At that time, what you needed most was inspiration and blessings. As someone who has been through it, you might feel that a few warm words, some inspiring gestures, and a few sincere smiles are the best encouragement for younger students. But for the "@Dongguan 2012 College Entrance Exam Inspirational Short Film Crew", this is not enough. They have just entered the ivory tower. Looking back at their life a year ago, they decided to give a different kind of strength to the warriors about to set off. This is a short film. A student who used to have average grades made a comeback in the college entrance exam and got into his dream university, and this is completely true. Yesterday, the main creative personnel said that they hope to move the audience with a story instead of words. Written by reporters Zhou Ruiming, Chen Chen, Zhong Dawen. Photos provided by interviewees. Origin Inspired by an inspirational short film created by seniors Zhan Zilin, the initiator of the short film, was still a senior student at Dongguan No.1 High School a year ago. Yesterday, he told the reporter that when there were less than a hundred days left until the college entrance exam, some graduates from the class of 2010 initiated the production of a video full of blessings and inspiration, which was played within the school. According to Zhan Zilin's introduction, this short film named "College Entrance Exam, What's the Big Deal! 50 College Students Give Infinite Strength to High School Students" involved students from Zhongshan University, Jinan University, South China Normal University, and other provincial schools who graduated from Dongguan No.1 High School in 2010. The messages in it were very touching. "I remember when this film premiered at No.1 High School, some female classmates even cried." "After this film premiered at No.1 High School, I thought, after I graduate, can I also make such a film and give it to the next generation of juniors?" The online popularity of the film strengthened Zhan Zilin's determination. After entering Zhongshan Institute of Electronic Science and Technology, Zhan Zilin began to prepare for this matter. He brought in his same-class friend Zhang Yikang, who graduated from Guangming Middle School, and then found Wang Jiaxin, who graduated from Dongguan Senior High School, and others to create together. Filming Drawing on Real Stories to Resonate with Juniors In Zhan Zilin's words, this yet-to-be-born short film was different from the start. At 8:38 PM the night before yesterday, their official Weibo account "@Dongguan 2012 College Entrance Exam Inspirational Short Film Crew" officially went online. Netizens can learn about the story outline, main service groups, and shooting progress related to the short film here. They don't forget to post stills here either. However, Zhan Zilin said that starting an official Weibo account doesn't count as the biggest innovation. As the screenwriter, he only wants to portray one character: someone who had average grades until the college entrance exam but ended up getting into their dream university in the final battle. Zhan Zilin said that this is not a plot created for dramatic conflict needs, but a real story. "It is based on one of my close friends, showcasing his joys and sorrows, his happiness and anger throughout his senior year. Authenticity makes it easier for juniors to resonate." Saying is doing. Several people, a few copied scripts, a DSLR camera, starting from February 5th, they worked continuously for three days for a movie that lasts over ten minutes. According to the plan, today they will go to Dongguan No.1 High School to film scenes, recording a few words of blessing and encouragement with several graduates from the class of 2011 recruited from their official Weibo account. In addition, they will also go to Beijing and film scenes at the school where the lead actor is located. Perspective Hoping to Set an Example for Junior Students Preparing for the College Entrance Exam It is understood that many scenes of this movie will be filmed at Dongguan No.1 High School, and the film crew has received support from the school administration. Yesterday afternoon, the reporter interviewed Lu Xuchang, principal of Dongguan No.1 High School. He told the reporter that although he has not yet seen this short film and does not know how the students will shoot it, "but a few days ago, some senior students applied to the school, saying that some alumni wanted to film some scenes at the school, and I agreed immediately." Lu Xuchang said that after graduation, alumni still have feelings for their alma mater, coming back to encourage and bless their juniors, and this starting point is worthy of affirmation. However, when expressing his expectations for this short film, Lu Xuchang said that he hopes the short film first maintains the correct guidance, then realistically reflects the protagonist's journey from having average grades to getting into their dream university, thereby setting an example for junior students preparing for the college entrance exam, achieving an inspirational effect. Regarding the students' approach to filming an inspirational short film, Hu Bin, the teacher responsible for publicity at Guangming Middle School, said that he had previously watched a similar self-shot student film, feeling that overall it was good, but there were some flaws in content, such as some complaints and parody content. "Therefore, if the students want to show this film at the school, it is necessary to first invite some teachers to watch it in a small range. Since it will spread among the student population, the school and teachers must consider the impact of the film on the majority of students." Additionally, regarding the crew members' hope to use this short film to promote the excellent image of Dongguan middle school and college students, Teacher Hu believes that the students' strong sense of responsibility and mission is worth affirming and admirable. Netizen Opinions Hope the Film Can Record Real Life Since going online on the evening of February 6th, the popularity of "@Dongguan 2012 College Entrance Exam Inspirational Short Film Crew" has started to increase. As the Weibo account continues to release new content, netizens' attention and support have also continued to grow. "@Huang Yudong" commented, hoping to see something different. "Recording reality and innovative angles are value." "@Huang Yuyu Yu Min" called out: "Everyone should help when they have time! Bless the third-year high school students!" The crew didn't forget to mention "micro-needs" here. Today, they will be filming scenes at Dongguan No.1 High School and need 12 graduates from the class of 2011 to participate in the shooting; additionally, they need two actors to play the roles of dormitory manager and teacher. Yesterday, Zhan Zilin also mentioned to the reporter that the crew urgently needs post-production equipment such as Apple all-in-one machines, hoping that qualified netizens will @ "@Dongguan 2012 College Entrance Exam Inspirational Short Film Crew" to support them. Dilemma: Unable to Secure Sponsorship, Can Only Pay Out of Pocket Zhan Zilin said that Zhang Yikang is the producer of the short film, but so far, Zhang Yikang hasn't secured any sponsorship for the crew. Using more common terms, all crew members are "zero salary". He looked at Zhang Yikang teasingly and said: "No choice, we can only pay out of pocket." Expectation: Premiering Before the Hundred-Day Countdown to the College Entrance Exam It is understood that soon this film will enter post-production. Zhan Zilin told the reporter that like last year, they hope this short film will premiere before the hundred-day countdown to the college entrance exam.
