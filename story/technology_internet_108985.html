Affected by the US financial crisis, more than 42 million SMEs, which account for over 99% of the national total, have been hit hardest. The slowing down of profits brought by traditional business models has forced many SMEs to start looking for new ways to promote business growth, and e-commerce combined with advanced IT technology has become the first choice among these new approaches.

To carry out e-commerce, one needs to establish a website first to introduce and promote products to customers or potential customers. A good domain name pointing to your website is the foundation of building a website. Obtaining a top-level domain name is the first step for enterprises to engage in e-commerce and online marketing.

In fact, as an emerging interactive media, the Internet still has enormous growth potential. After the 3G transformation of mobile phones, the number of Internet users worldwide will be even larger. Precise online promotion and e-commerce mutually reinforce each other, and reasonable combinations yield the best results. The pioneers with sharp insights have already started preparing for the winter. Have you begun to prepare? Integrating resources, increasing revenue while cutting costs, and making rational use of funds are effective strategies to survive the winter.
