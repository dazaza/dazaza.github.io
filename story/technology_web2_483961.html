Theme: How to Utilize Social Sharing and Recommendation in App Development and Promotion

Time: Saturday, September 3, 2011, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Location: Share Coffee (No. 22 Baotun Road, Shanghai, near Tibet South Road Station, Line 8)

Social sharing and recommendation services, as the name suggests, are about discovering and spreading the value of products or services based on people's existing social relationships. As social networks permeate every corner of life, the power of social dissemination has become even more prominent. Whether it’s application development based on the Web or mobile platforms, rediscovering and utilizing the effects of social sharing and recommendations have been placed at a critical core position. Properly leveraging social sharing and recommendation can enable new applications to achieve great results with minimal effort.

Currently, active social sharing and recommendation services in the market generally adopt two directions for expansion: one is based on relationship-building features that make relationships more sticky; the other is based on functionality, using relationships to make these functions more user-friendly.

This NTalks event focuses on how to optimally utilize social sharing and recommendations during the development of internet and mobile platform-based applications, creating and delivering value, opening up new imaginative spaces for both relational and functional extensions.
