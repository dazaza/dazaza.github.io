Designer's car MG6 - the first choice for working families to buy a car

by rabizmf526 on 2011-09-16 15:51:01

The birth of the MG6 has clearly demarcated MG from those traditional car manufacturers. While everyone is focusing on segmenting the market for two-box and three-box sedans, a brand new Fast-Back is already planning to dominate both large and small markets! The British style sedan MG6, with standards of intelligence, safety, and personalization, has comprehensively upgraded personalized configurations such as dedicated satellite positioning navigation systems, Bluetooth hands-free, premium full-grain leather seats throughout the vehicle, and xenon headlights for both high and low beams. Not only does the entire vehicle equipment reach the highest level of its class, but it also reflects the unique personality and taste of the driver.

MG6 and Roewe 550 are based on the same platform. The powertrains, chassis suspension, etc., of these two cars are completely identical. Compared to the Roewe 550, the MG6 has a slightly longer body length than the Roewe 550, but the wheelbase remains the same at 2705 millimeters. Because the MG6 adopts the popular fastback design abroad, this car is also a two-and-a-half-box hatchback. The MG6 comes with two engines: one is the naturally aspirated 1.8L DVVT version, and the other is the turbocharged 1.8T version. Although the 1.8T engine delivers stronger power output, the fuel economy of the 1.8L DVVT still stands out.

There are five models of the MG6, among which the 1.8t luxury model is the most fully equipped. This car uses a reinforced USD body and European standard pedestrian protection design in terms of safety. Its main safety configurations include ABS+EBD, CBC cornering control system, MSR acceleration anti-slip system, TCS traction control system, BDC automatic brake disc cleaning function, and TPMS tire pressure monitoring. Additionally, the crucial VSC (ESP) body stabilization control system is also standard equipment.

Furthermore, the MG6 1.8t luxury model is equipped with dual front SRS airbags, chest-abdomen integrated side airbags, and curtain-type side airbags at the front and rear. The MG6 1.8t model not only boasts powerful performance but also exhibits outstanding handling. Moreover, the safety features and safety configurations of this car make consumers feel very reassured.

The MG6 Fast-Back's stylish design extends the streamlined roof contour all the way to the rear of the car, innovatively transforming aerodynamic aesthetics into spatial efficiency. This design allows the MG6 to possess both the smooth appearance of a traditional two-box sedan and the spacious interior of a three-box sedan. With a luggage space of 429L and an optimized dynamic space of 1379L, combined with a super-long wheelbase of 2705mm across categories, it maximizes spatial potential, bringing dual breakthroughs in driving and storage efficiency, setting new benchmarks for spaciousness! Beneath its attractive exterior, the MG6 is equipped with Kavachi series 1.8T and 1.8L high-performance full-aluminum gasoline engines. Both power and fuel efficiency are far ahead of its peers in the same class. The all-new MG6 will become the pioneer of a new generation of sedans in automotive history.
