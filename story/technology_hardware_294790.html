Introduction to the Star Butcher

The Star Butcher, a great general of an empire, carried out an unprecedented massacre at a terrorist base, burying two million people alive. He was sentenced to hanging by the Federal Empire for crimes against humanity. Before his execution, the general made ample preparations and transplanted his soul into a perfect clone body. However, the plan failed, and his soul was instead transplanted into the body of a civilian engineering mech technician...
