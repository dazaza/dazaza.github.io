How did the 1998 Information Port obtain high Baidu weight?

The 1998 Information Port (http://www.1998.cn) has obtained methods to increase Baidu's high weight, as weight is the core of Baidu rankings. The webmaster of the 1998 Information Port has also summarized some experiences in order to obtain Baidu's high weight.

1. Website Content

For Baidu, website content is very important. Baidu's algorithm is more like a bucket principle, doing subtraction operations. "Content is king" does not mean that only content needs to be done well, but refers to the fact that the content of the 1998 Information Port website holds a relatively important position in the overall structure design, program design, and internal/external link construction of the website. This encourages the network to value content, free information release, and innovation.

2. Inbound Links

High-weight websites are visited by spiders more frequently, and this weight can be transferred. The more times the 1998 Information Port website's outbound links are crawled, the more effective values these counts will be for a high-weight classified information website, gradually increasing the weight of the outbound website. Alternatively, through purchasing links or using black hat techniques such as hidden links to artificially interfere with search engine crawling results, although there are no explicit restrictions, many sites are still penalized by Baidu every day for these reasons. Therefore, illegal links carry certain risks.

3. Keyword Distribution

Based on the experience of the 1998 Information Port (http://www.1998.cn), the distribution of keywords is roughly as follows: the first keyword in the title (such as "information port") > appearance of the keyword in the title > the first word in the website content being a keyword (usually an H1 tag) > appearance of the keyword in the description > appearance of the keyword in the side column > appearance of the keyword in the navigation bar > appearance of the keyword at the bottom. Keywords should naturally and prominently appear, without excessive stacking to avoid being suspected of cheating.

4. Other Factors

The stability of the server where the 1998 Information Port (http://www.1998.cn) website is located, network speed, whether other websites sharing the same IP have been penalized; the correctness of the robots.txt file; the age of the domain name; the relevance of friendly links, etc., may all affect the weight of your own website. Although some of these factors are very subtle, it is precisely from these subtle aspects that you need to start. Only then can your website maintain a more stable ranking on Baidu.
