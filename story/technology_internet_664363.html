Secondly, understand how much space you need. Different-sized websites require different amounts of space. For a regular website, around 100-500M is usually sufficient; for business or gaming/entertainment sites, more space may be necessary. This depends on your website's growth potential. Thirdly, which script do you need? The type of script used during development is crucial. Otherwise, if the script is developed and the purchased hosting space does not support the script type, that would be a waste. Or, if you later decide to change your website from ASP to PHP and the hosting space doesn't support PHP, it could cause trouble. Therefore, ensure that the virtual host supports the required scripts before purchasing.

Fourthly, where is the data center located? It’s important to know the location of the data center and whether it can guarantee data security.
