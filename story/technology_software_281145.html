8 Dietary Remedies in Winter for Anti-wrinkle and Beauty Secrets

by onebelles on 2009-11-28 11:01:58

In the cold winter, the skin has to resist harsh weather and deal with the dry indoor environment. How can you have healthy skin without too much hassle? Besides doing basic skincare in the morning and evening, are there any other ways to make your skin glow? The editor of Life has specially collected various beauty secrets for you, so that you can easily deal with winter skin problems! NO.1 Pig Trotter Anti-wrinkle Use several old sow trotters (if you can't find them, you can use regular pig trotters), wash them and cook them into a paste. Apply it on your face before going to bed at night and wash it off the next morning. Persisting for half a month will yield significant anti-wrinkle effects. NO.2 Red Date Paste Anti-wrinkle Red date paste contains abundant protein, calcium, iron, phosphorus, etc., which naturally moisturize and reduce wrinkles on the skin. It can be made into a mask to apply on the face, making the face smooth and reducing wrinkles. NO.3 Rice Anti-wrinkle After the fragrant rice is cooked at home, pick some soft, warm but not too hot rice and knead it into a ball. Gently rub it on your face to extract the oil and dirt from the skin pores. Keep rubbing until the rice ball becomes greasy and dirty, then wash it off with clear water. This allows the skin to breathe freely, reduces wrinkles. NO.4 Chicken Bone Anti-wrinkle Most of the dermal tissue of the skin is composed of elastic fibers. Without them, the skin loses elasticity and wrinkles gather. The chicken skin and chicken cartilage contain large amounts of chondroitin sulfate, which is the most important component of elastic fibers. Wash the leftover chicken bones and cook them with chicken skin, not only rich in nutrition, but also helps eliminate wrinkles and makes the skin delicate. NO.5 Tea Leaf Anti-wrinkle Tea leaves contain more than 400 kinds of rich chemical components, mainly including tea polyphenols, aromatic oil compounds, carbohydrates, proteins, multiple amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and pectin, making it a natural health drink. Besides enhancing health, it can also keep the skin clean, delay the appearance of facial wrinkles and reduce wrinkles, and prevent various skin diseases. However, strong tea should be avoided. NO.6 Chewing Anti-wrinkle Chewing gum for 5-20 minutes every day can reduce facial wrinkles and make the complexion rosy. This is because chewing exercises the facial muscles, changes the facial blood circulation, and enhances the metabolic function of facial cells. NO.7 Chondroitin Sulfate Anti-wrinkle Chondroitin sulfate is mainly found in chicken skin, fish skin, shark fins, salmon heads, and the cartilage of chickens and sharks. Some people discard the skin or cartilage when eating chicken or fish, which is really a waste. Unknown to many, chondroitin sulfate is the most important substance that constitutes the elastic fibers of the skin's dermis. If the diet lacks chondroitin, the skin will lose elasticity and develop wrinkles. NO.8 Meat Skin Anti-wrinkle Eating more meat skin can also supplement the synthesis of collagen, thereby reducing or eliminating wrinkles, keeping the skin smooth, moist, elastic, and delaying skin aging.
