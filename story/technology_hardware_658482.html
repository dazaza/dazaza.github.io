※ Transmission system: The drive method of this mixing drum is powered by the engine, taking power from the power take-off unit at the rear end of the engine, transmitting it to the variable displacement piston hydraulic pump through the drive shaft, then via a fixed displacement piston hydraulic motor, and finally through the reducer to drive the rotation of the mixing drum.

Basic configuration of concrete mixer transport vehicles:

Concrete mixer trucks mainly consist of the chassis frame, mixing drum, transmission system (drive unit), hydraulic drive system, water supply system, loading (feeding) and unloading system, discharge chute, operating platform, control system, and protective devices.
