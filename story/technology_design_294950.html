Looking for disaster relief volunteers...!

by mudehata4a on 2009-12-06 01:41:40

As the breathing under the ruins becomes weak bit by bit. As the life of one who desires to survive passes away second by second. As Chengdu water quality is being polluted. My compatriot, can you still sit there? What can we young people do? Can we just watch these tragedies happen? Can we only gradually accept more terrible numbers and make our hearts numb? Besides making donations, can we do other things that are more capable of saving our compatriots? We seek like-minded people from all walks of society, together go to the surrounding areas of Chengdu and try our best.

(People supporter) Short message donation method. Mobile China, China Unicom mobile phone users, as well as China Telecom, China Netcom Little Smart users can all edit a short message with "1" or "2", send it to 1069999301, and donate 1 yuan or 2 yuan to the "Red Cross Rescue Action". Meanwhile, repeated donations are allowed. The communication expenses generated by this donation activity will be donated to the disaster area for disaster relief.

"Yellow Ribbon Action" Sichuan earthquake rescue volunteers recruitment On May 12th, forex, an earthquake of magnitude 7.8 occurred in Wenchuan County, Aba Prefecture, Sichuan Province. In order to mobilize the participation of the whole society in earthquake relief, the Youth League Committee of the Communist Youth League of Sichuan Province, Sichuan Youth Volunteers Association, Sichuan Youth Federation, and Sichuan Students' Union decided to publicly recruit volunteers from the society to carry out the earthquake relief "Yellow Ribbon Action" ("Yellow Ribbon" implies "Praying for family safety").

Volunteer slogan: One more rescue volunteer means one less compatriot death.

Now the announcement is as follows:

I. Recruitment time

May 14, 2008 onwards

II. Recruitment method

According to the principle of recruiting locally according to needs and providing services nearby, volunteers are recruited through radio, television, newspapers, and networks to participate in earthquake relief in various places.

III. Types of recruitment

1. Blood donation volunteers: Donate blood for free at the blood donation station and the blood donation love house;

2. Medical volunteers: Focus on recruiting brain surgery and orthopedic medical workers;

3. Rescue volunteers: Limited to males, healthy body, with certain rescue knowledge;

4. Care volunteers: Conduct psychological comfort, take care of the lonely and disabled and other volunteer services.
