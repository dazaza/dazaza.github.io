The Romantic Theme of Qixi Festival Explored: The Equipment Donation System in "Battle for the World" is Activated

Legend has it that in July of the year 208 AD, during the early stages of the Battle of Red Cliffs, Cao's army and the allied forces had been facing each other across the river for several days. Zhou Yu, the Grand Commander of Wu, seeing that Cao’s army had no intention of launching a strong attack recently, plotted a preemptive strike. It was the Qixi Festival, and under the moonlight, Zhou Yu observed a bright light on the river surface, suspecting it to be a spy from Cao's army. He ordered his men to retrieve it and found a treasure box. Zhou Yu had the treasure box moved to his tent and opened it alone, discovering a golden-threaded robe, countless treasures, and the character "fire" written inside. Zhou Yu then presented the golden-threaded robe to Xiao Qiao as a gift for the Qixi Festival, winning her favor greatly. He also devised an ingenious plan to use fire against Cao's army, later conspiring with Zhuge Liang.

In December of the year 208 AD, the Battle of Red Cliffs concluded with a great victory for the allied forces. It is said that the "Qixi Treasure Box" was placed in the river by Zhuge Liang as a gift for Zhou Yu. It is also rumored that since then, every Qixi Festival sees the reappearance of the "Qixi Treasure Box". Whether true or false remains unknown.

Today, would you still like to witness the mysterious charm of the "Qixi Treasure Box"? As the full-dynamic, client-free strategy online game "Battle for the World" (http://zz.qeedoo.com) undergoes its non-deletion internal testing in a fiery manner, the battles of the Three Kingdoms are reignited. Accompanying the sweet toll of the Qixi Festival bells, the smoke of war will also become romantic at this moment.

Capture cities, compete for fame. A new face of the festival, the equipment donation system is now open. There are new tricks to obtaining a title. With such rich activities and fresh fun, how can all the lords miss out?

True Adventure Forum Gifts

Have you played Truth or Dare? The moment to witness your sincerity is approaching. From 0:00 on August 26th to 24:00 on August 30th, the forum will open a special post for the "Qixi Truth or Dare" activity. All players participating in the activity must reply to the main post for their replies to be considered valid. Each floor owner must answer the previous owner's question and pose a question for the next player. Everyone must answer truthfully, without deception or reservation.

Each player can only post once, occupying one floor. Once the building reaches 826 floors, the activity will conclude successfully. At that time, lucky participants will be selected from those who replied, and they will receive mysterious grand prizes.

Reviving Prestige, New System

Prestige is an indispensable element for every lord, not only concerning the lord's honor but also intricately linked to various ranking rewards and domestic alliances. The newly activated equipment donation system will help you revive your prestige and dominate the world.

The so-called equipment donation system involves donating your own equipment to the court in exchange for corresponding prestige points. Regarding the acquisition of equipment, by attacking various wildlands and cities, there will be a chance to obtain equipment of various qualities. And depending on the attributes of the donated equipment, the rewarded prestige will vary accordingly.

In the domestic interface, open the daily section, and under the court recruitment options, you can accept relevant tasks. A single epic piece of equipment can exchange for a high prestige value of 1000. Title enthusiasts, take action quickly.
