At the request of many forum friends, I found some information online. I hope friends with love take action!!! You in the city worry every year about how to deal with old clothes. Keeping them takes up space, throwing them away seems wasteful, giving them to relatives or friends doesn't seem appropriate. They in the mountains worry every year about their livelihoods, with annual incomes under a thousand, unable to send their children to school, unable to afford medical care for the elderly, wearing tattered clothes. We may not have high incomes, we may not have extra savings, we may not be able to donate a Hope School, but we can pass on our unneeded items to those who need them, it only requires our love. Please come here if you need to dispose of old clothes, let's give these old clothes new luster with our love. Let's all take action, do our small part, help those we can help. It will be our happiness!

Regarding donation and mailing procedures:

1. Gather unused old clothes, shoes, books, etc., underwear with large holes should be cut off.

2. Wash thoroughly, soaking in disinfectant is best, then dry.

――― This point is about respect and responsibility for the recipient. Kind-hearted friends, please don't mind doing this step.

3. Pack in bags - post offices have specialized boxes for mailing packages, but they're expensive, even the smallest ones cost 8-9 yuan. Therefore, we suggest finding your own cardboard box or opaque cloth bag (rice bags, flour bags, homemade cloth bags), more economical. Best to first pack clothes in plastic bags before putting them in the cardboard box or cloth bag, to prevent rainwater from wetting the clothes during mailing.

Note: Don't seal the box or bag at this point because the post office needs to check the contents.

4. Take to the post office.

5. Purchase a package form, 0.5 yuan, use the green one, cheapest and most practical. Post office staff will often ask if you want express delivery or regular package, express delivery is not practical and more expensive. Fill out the address firmly.

6. Seal the bag (post offices provide needles and thread for sealing bags), write down the address.

7. You can choose to insure, 1 yuan insures goods worth 100 yuan.

8. Send! Cost per kilogram depends on the distance, ranging from 2 to 4 yuan.

Road freight process:

1. Collect clean old clothes, shoes, books, bags, etc., wash and dry (preferably soak in disinfectant first).

2. Use a plastic bag to protect against water damage, multiple bags if necessary.

3. Find a cardboard box (we find cardboard boxes sturdier than cloth bags), pack clothes, seal with tape.

4. Call or visit the freight company (or courier company) to inquire about prices, standard boxes usually cost around 20-30 yuan, depending on the city. You can either deliver yourself or have the company pick up.

5. Fill out the consignment form, pay (you are donating your love too).

Notes:

1. Try to send simple, rural-appropriate clothing. Not too fashionable, no skirts, no tank tops.

2. Focus on cold-weather clothing, minimize summer clothes. Quilts, sheets, etc. are also needed.

3. Specifically: cotton coats, down jackets, sweaters, coats, cotton underwear, shirts, long-sleeve T-shirts, pants, wool pants, fleece pants, jeans, long pants, cotton shoes, sneakers, casual shoes, socks, scarves, hats, gloves.

4. Clothes should not have holes, no obvious stains, buttons, zippers, shoelaces must be intact as buying these accessories in impoverished areas is difficult.

5. If donating used clothes, please disinfect and wash thoroughly before drying (disinfect using Wielushi or 84 disinfectant, soak for 15 minutes then wash normally).

6. If donating books, select positive, healthy reading materials. Chinese and foreign classics, science books, word cards, children's storybooks, youth science books, fairy tales, dictionaries (including Xinhua Dictionary, idiom dictionary, riddle dictionary, Tibetan-Chinese dictionary, English-Chinese dictionary) are acceptable. Do not send books promoting superstition, violence, pornography, fashion magazines. Also, textbooks and supplementary materials from western provinces differ from inland areas, inland ones would be useless.

7. Stationery, chess games, sports equipment, toys can all be sent (please include instructions; if not new, please wash and disinfect before sending).

8. These are all remote rural areas, many places couriers cannot reach, recommend using regular postal parcels!
