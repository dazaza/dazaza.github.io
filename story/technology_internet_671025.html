Kayue Interconnect: Embarrassing incidents of e-commerce domains: Netizens mention 8 hard-to-remember domains

by cnidc3653 on 2012-02-21 14:42:32

E-commerce domain names face a tough situation: Internet users list eight hard-to-remember domain names. Recently, e-commerce domain names have become a focal point of attention. The entry of 38 e-commerce websites into Taobao Mall's Tmall.com has drawn industry attention. Although e-commerce websites seem to be enjoying continuous good fortune, one cannot overlook their "flaws." Recently, netizens have compiled a ranking of the worst domestic e-commerce website domain names, and these domains have caused many awkward situations for e-commerce sites due to being difficult to remember.

Overseas enterprises' Chinese websites encounter awkward situations with their domain names.

When overseas companies enter the domestic market, they often choose phonetic domain names suitable for local conditions, such as Groupon's China site Gaopeng.com. "Gaopeng" originates from "a room full of distinguished guests," and the domain name value is decent. It was rumored that this domain name was traded for 980,000 yuan. The results brought by Gaopeng's choice of a phonetic domain name are evident. However, Amazon China's Joyo.com domain (amazon.cn) is still hard for many people to remember, even though "Amazon" sounds similar to the pinyin "yamaxun."

Vancl.com - Confusion in the Vanke Chengpin Domain Name

Vanke Chengpin independently established the "VANCL" brand domestically and has a significant influence in the domestic e-commerce market. However, many new users can't remember whether Vanke Chengpin's domain name is "vancl" or "vancle." Often, in the subconscious minds of these users, they input the pinyin "fanke." Fanke.com is not owned by Vanke Chengpin, so much of the traffic ends up at fanke.com instead.

Yixun Network's icson.com and 51buy.com

Previously, Yixun Network’s official website domain was icson.com. I couldn’t figure out what it represented—neither a phonetic domain nor an English domain name, and its creative domain left its meaning unclear, which seemed like a failure. It appears that Yixun realized the shortcomings of icson.com and recently changed the domain to 51buy.com. At first glance, it seems related to shopping because of the word "buy," but why do others shift to phonetic domains while you opt for a creative one?

Chuke Network's crucco.com Creative Domain

If there were another Chuke Network online called chuke.com, without doubt, many people would inadvertently be directed to chuke.com. Compared to crucco.com, this creative domain is truly hard to remember, even for insiders who keep shaking their heads and mixing up "crucc." Chuke Network crucco.com, you should quickly learn from this lesson!

Masamaso.com - An Overly Unconventional Domain

In today’s society, if you dare to be unconventional and unique, you're not far from fame. However, whether you'll be "renowned for millennia" or "infamous for eternity" remains to be seen. In the world of e-commerce, Masamaso.com, a men's clothing brand, has an unconventional domain. Personally, I don't applaud this domain. Is the Chinese name "Masamasuo" a tongue-twister? Is the website domain "masamasa.com" or "masamaso.com"? Can't tell the difference.

Currently, there are many other hard-to-remember domain names for e-commerce websites online, such as Moonbasa.com. The name "Moonbasa" is beautiful, dreamlike, and elegant, but remembering the domain name Moonbasa.com is mentally taxing. Is it "moon" or "meng"? For Redbaby.com.cn, this is an English domain name. Translated literally, it means "red child," but why choose the .com.cn suffix instead of simply using .cn for more simplicity? Another confusing domain is Vcotton.com from the young e-commerce site Weimianwang.

Winning the hearts of the people wins the world. Websites on the internet also need to follow this principle. A good domain name can be easily remembered, allowing a company to gain user recognition in one step. Clearly, the influence represented by a domain name is crucial for e-commerce websites to "win the hearts of the people." With fierce competition in the e-commerce industry, the battlefield of competition among industries has shifted from product and service quality to domain names. The role of domain names in the development of e-commerce websites is becoming more prominent. Choosing a good domain name is a step that e-commerce websites cannot neglect.
