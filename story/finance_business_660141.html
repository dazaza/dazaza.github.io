Leping Procuratorate: Plaque presentation for 7 disciplinary inspection and supervision studios

by glsd5678264 on 2012-02-07 13:52:26

In the morning of April 29, the LePing Discipline Inspection Commission and Supervision Bureau dispatched the grantee conference in the LePing Hotel. JiuZhenChuan city committee standing member and discipline inspection commission secretary Liang ChaoChao and LePing city secretary Zhang LiangHua granted plates to seven discipline inspection studios and delivered speeches. LePing leaders Zhan DongSheng, Sun YanFeng, Sun GengJiu, Fu JinLin, Luo JianGuo, Wang ZhiMing, Wan YuHua, Chen YiQi and other counties and cities' discipline inspection secretaries attended. The meeting was hosted by LePing city vice secretary and mayor Liao YunDong, and LePing city vice secretary Shi JianHua announced the employment decision on each discipline inspection studio director and deputy director. In the grantee conference, Liang ChaoChao delivered an important speech. He required that: First, unify thoughts and fully recognize the important role of the dispatching mechanism for discipline inspection agencies. The dispatching mechanism is beneficial to further strengthen effective restraint and supervision of power; it is beneficial to further strengthen anti-corruption leadership system and working mechanisms; it is beneficial to further strengthen the intensity of investigating illegal and disciplinary cases; it is beneficial to further strengthen the intensity of solving prominent issues harming people's interests. Second, high-level promotion, further strengthening all aspects of work for the dispatching mechanism for discipline inspection agencies. Increase importance, strive to achieve high-standard selection, ensuring a top-class working team; strive to achieve high-start requirements, ensuring first-class working achievements. Third, focus on key points, aiming for the dispatching mechanism for discipline inspection agencies work to obtain actual results. Discipline inspection studios should focus on serving the overall situation, performing duties and self-construction. In his speech, Zhang LiangHua pointed out that unified management of dispatched institutions is a highly political, policy-based, and business-oriented task. All units' party and government leadership teams and members should further strengthen their cooperation awareness and fully support the discipline inspection studios in carrying out their work. Each discipline inspection studio should seriously perform their duties, actively coordinate, and creatively carry out their work. Regarding current anti-corruption work, Zhang LiangHua required that: Strengthen supervision and inspection of various funds and projects involved in the central, provincial, and municipal expansion of domestic demand; continue to increase supervision and inspection efforts on behaviors affecting LePing's development, such as illegal small coal mines, illegal river sand mining, and illegal housing construction, and severely investigate corruption among party members and cadres; conscientiously carry out special governance work on three prominent issues in the livelihood field, stopping behaviors that harm the interests of the masses at the grassroots level. The LePing Discipline Inspection Commission and Supervision Bureau should strengthen guidance, supervision, and strict management, promoting the studio to carry out its work. Discipline inspection studio cadres should consciously achieve self-respect, self-reflection, self-alertness, and self-motivation, accepting supervision from cadres and the public of corresponding supervisory units, winning recognition from a wide range of cadres and the public of corresponding supervisory units with outstanding work achievements and good images. Liao YunDong required that all units fully support the work of the discipline inspection studio, ensuring smooth advancement of reform and reorganization work. Newly established discipline inspection studios should quickly enter roles, carry out work, and strive to become "demonstration posts for reform and innovation, main forces against corruption, boosters for central work, protectors of people's interests, and spokespeople for maintaining justice." At the conference, representatives of LePing discipline inspection studios and relevant unit discipline inspection work comrades made commitment speeches.
