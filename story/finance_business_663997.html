Police interrogation uncovers insider thief

by henrui5401 on 2012-02-13 01:17:36

Before the investigation, it was believed that the case was not due to collusion between an "outsider" and an insider. It turned out that Ms. Deng Ling had orchestrated the entire event to help her younger brother borrow money. She enlisted the help of a brother from Qingyuan and her cousin to stage this "bitter flesh plan."

After examination, He Mouyong's account of the situation was found to be largely true. However, Deng Mouling was evasive when questioned about being alone at the crime scene. Furthermore, she claimed to have been chased and beaten, yet there were no visible injuries on her body. The police also found two mobile phones on her person; one of them had multiple call records with outside contacts just before the incident. All signs pointed to Deng Mouling as the likely "inside accomplice."

According to Deng Mouqing's confession, last year in Qingyuan, he accidentally hit and killed a motorcyclist while driving. He subsequently paid 110,000 yuan in compensation, leaving him with nearly 100,000 yuan in debt. Seeing her brother being harassed by debt collectors, Deng Mouling felt compelled to come up with the "bitter flesh plan" — staging a robbery to steal the company's funds.

Currently, the Baiyun police are actively pursuing another suspect involved in the case.

"Afterwards, he (Deng Mouling) said he was also chased and beaten by the bandits and sprayed with pepper spray, so he ran away immediately. He only dared to return after the bandits got into their car, robbed the money, and left the scene," recalled the boss of the company, Mr. Kuang Hou. After inventory checks, it was found that the money hidden in the vehicle's dashboard compartment was stolen, but there were no other losses, prompting them to immediately report the incident to the police.

■ Source: Reporter Li Banghui, Correspondent Hu Min

The police conducted consecutive interrogations of Deng Mouling, whose initially well-thought-out plan quickly unraveled. Caught off guard, she confessed to conspiring with her younger brother and cousin to stage the robbery. On the night of November 16th, Baiyun police legally detained Deng Mouling (from Qingyuan). On December 2nd, the investigating officers arrested Deng Mouqing in Chongqing, China.

On the morning of November 16th at 3:36 AM, after collecting 150,000 yuan in payment in Dongguan, business staff members Deng Mouling and He Mouyong drove back to their company located in Shijings Ya Gang Industrial Park within Baiyun District, Guangzhou. When they were about 300 meters away from the company, they noticed several small stones blocking their path. Sensing something amiss, they stopped the car. Standing near the driver's side, He Mouyong got out of the car to investigate and prepare to move the stones when suddenly two men wielding electric batons appeared from the roadside and began assaulting him without warning. He Mouyong hurriedly crawled away and ran back to the company to seek help from his boss.

A few minutes later, when He Mouyong returned with the company boss, Mr. Kuang Xuesheng, they found that the assailants had fled, leaving only Deng Mouling lying by the roadside.

The police's sudden interrogation exposed the inside accomplice, who turned out to be the sister plotting the "bitter flesh plan" to help her brother repay debts.

On November 16th, while returning from Dongguan after collecting the company's payment, business staff member Deng Mouling from a Guangzhou bird breeding company, along with her colleague, was attacked by two bandits wielding electric batons. The 150,000 yuan in payment, hidden in a concealed spot in the car, was entirely stolen.

The special investigation team later discovered during the investigation that since the company was relatively small, apart from the owner couple, Deng Mouling and He Mouyong were the only two business staff members. After the incident, only these two people knew about the trip to Dongguan for collection and bringing the money back to Guangzhou. Based on this, the police determined that the case was likely an inside job and immediately separated the two for questioning.

After the money collection, they encountered a robbery on their way back to Guangzhou.
