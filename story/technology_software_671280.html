Product Introduction: 40 Linglong Baiyu Fenjiu 225ml

Baiyu Fenjiu was first created in 1904. It uses Fenjiu as the base liquor, combined with the plant-based Chinese medicine Cinnamomum Cassia for soaking and distillation to extract its aroma, resulting in a unique and naturally pleasant fragrance. During the Republic of China era, there was a popular saying: "Fenjiu is pure, bamboo leaf green, deep affection rose sweet Baiyu," where "sweet Baiyu" was used to praise its smooth and sweet taste.

As time passes, quality endures like gold. Today, Shanxi Xinghuacun Fen Wine Factory Co., Ltd. has infused Baiyu Fenjiu with a new brand essence and precise positioning. To meet different consumer demands, Baiyu Fenjiu has launched two series: 20 Baiyu Fenjiu and 10 Baiyu Fenjiu. These are respectively packaged in Chinese celadon bottles and crystal frosted glass bottles, targeting high-end and mid-range markets respectively. As the national marketing general agent, Shanxi Xinghuacun Zhuyeqing Wine Marketing Co., Ltd. will focus on the lifestyle trajectory and consumption patterns of the post-80s generation for marketing promotion. They aim to perfectly interpret the concept of "liquor like jade, feelings like jade" and "custom-made for the post-80s generation," striving to make Baiyu Fenjiu the pioneer of smooth and fragrant Chinese liquor.

The post-80s generation, as the emerging force in today's society, enjoys relatively stable incomes and advanced consumption concepts. They prefer soft and sweet liquor flavors, and amidst complex interpersonal relationships, they yearn for pure friendships and sincere emotional communication. The brand proposition of Baiyu Fenjiu—"liquor like jade, feelings like jade"—resonates highly with the emotional needs of these target consumers. This unity between the liquor's style and emotional pursuit will surely make it the preferred choice for emotional寄托 among new-age mainstream consumer groups.

"Custom-made for the post-80s generation" locks onto the target consumer group, entering the market boldly with the claim of being "the pioneer of smooth and fragrant Chinese liquor." By integrating the emotional appeal of "liquor like jade, feelings like jade" into consumers' self-interpretation of Baiyu Fenjiu, this series of perfect connotation exploration and marketing interpretation opens up broader prospects for the market.
