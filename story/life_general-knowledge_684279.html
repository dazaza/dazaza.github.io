Love should not wait until the next life

The boy's name is Ning, and the girl's name is Niu Niu. They met online. He called her girl, and she called him Ning Bao.

The boy has been very dissolute since he was young. He likes the underworld and enjoys the unrestrained lifestyle. The girl is in high school. She doesn't expect the boy to become a prominent person, she just hopes that the boy can live safely and healthily.

The girl's love touched the boy, and for the girl, the boy tried to give up that kind of life. Every day, the boy would send text messages to the girl: what are you doing, girl? Have you eaten, girl? Do you miss me, girl?... No matter what the girl is doing or where she is, she will receive the boy's care.

Even if she does not reply, the boy will keep sending messages because he wants the girl to know that he is thinking of her. In fact, the girl's heart is full of contradictions. She doesn't know what kind of feelings she has for the boy. Online dating, in everyone's eyes, is so ridiculous, and they have never met each other. She doesn't know how this relationship should proceed.

One evening, while the girl was having evening self-study, she received a text message from the boy saying that he had fought with someone.

The girl's heart tightened immediately. She slipped out of the back door when the teacher wasn't paying attention, called the boy, and asked him where he was injured and how serious it was, and why he fought with others. The girl thought the boy had started to mess around again, so she was very angry.

How could the smart boy not understand the girl's heart? He consoled the girl, telling her that he was fine, he didn't mean to cause trouble, but was accidentally injured while trying to mediate. Hearing that the boy was fine, the girl felt much relieved. After scolding the boy, she hung up the phone. The boy's heart was warm because he knew that someone cared about him. After hanging up, the girl cried. She realized that she had fallen in love with this boy whom she had never met.

Life went on peacefully. The girl lived a simple campus life, but this life became more colorful because of the boy's care.

They talked about the past, discussed the present, and imagined the future. In fact, the boy is very attached to his home. He always imagines a home that belongs to him and the girl. The girl thought that she would really have a future with the boy, but... The girl's sister found out about their relationship and asked the girl what exactly was going on. The girl told her sister the truth and expressed her love for the boy without reservation.

After hearing this, the sister slapped the girl. It was the first time the sister had hit her since she was young. The sister said to the girl: How many people on the Internet are real? You're still so young to talk about love, and you don't study. How can you face your parents? If he deceives you, what will you do then?

The girl委屈地对姐姐说：不姐，宁不会骗我。他会给我幸福！

姐姐哭着对女孩说：妹妹你别太天真。他连自己都养不了凭什么给你幸福？如果爸妈知道了，他们怎么能接受这样的打击？再说了，他又能等你多久？你要念书，还得六年，六年对一个青春花季的人来说多重要，人的一生又有多少个六年能浪费？

说不定他在等你的时候就错过了该和他相伴一生的人，这样你不是害了他吗？听姐一句，断了吧！算姐求你了！看着姐姐哀求的眼神，女孩的心动摇了，她不知道自己该怎么办，他真的能给自己幸福吗？

想想爸妈，女孩流着泪点了头，一瞬间，泪决堤，她抱着姐姐痛哭：姐，我是真的爱他，我真的放不下。

女孩的每一滴 tear dripped into the sister's heart: It's over, everything will pass, trust me, it's for his own good. He will understand. If you two are truly destined, no matter what happens now, you will be together in the future.

Cry it out, put everything in your heart after crying. The girl cried even louder, she really couldn't let go! That day, the girl took the initiative to send a text message to the boy. Before the boy had time to be happy, he saw the most heartbreaking sentence: Let's break up!

The boy didn't understand why the girl suddenly wanted to break up, so he asked her. The girl told him: We are not suitable for each other. You have your own life, and I still need to study. I don't know if I will love you as much as now after I graduate from university. If I don't love you anymore, I would have made you wait for six years in vain. That wouldn't be fair to you. I don't want us to regret, so I can only let go. The boy was unwilling. He asked the girl: Were you playing with me all along?

The girl replied with three words: I'm sorry! The boy was heartbroken. He stared at his phone blankly. He couldn't understand why the girl he deeply loved would deceive him, but the fact that they broke up was undeniable!

He will never know that the girl on the other side of the phone had already cried herself out. Thinking about the boy's sadness, the girl's heart felt empty. She didn't want the boy to regret one day and leave her, which would hurt herself and also ruin the boy. If that day ever came, she didn't know if she would have the courage to go on living, because the boy was her whole world.

After the breakup, the girl was not used to the lack of the boy's greetings. She stared at her phone every day, hoping to receive a text message from the boy one day, but none came.

The girl cried. Their love turned out to be so fragile. The girl began to get used to life without the boy, but when she saw the text messages the boy once sent her, she still felt heartache. She knew that she could never forget the boy. One day, the girl went online and the boy sent her a message asking how she was doing. The girl was stunned. She didn't expect the boy to contact her again. She replied that she was okay. In fact, only the girl knew how unbearable her life was.

They chatted like they used to, but every word from the boy seemed so strange, making the girl realize clearly that the boy was no longer hers. A message shocked the girl: I am engaged! The boy said calmly. The girl didn't know what to say for a moment. She asked the boy: Are you happy? How long has this been going on?

The boy smiled faintly: There's no such thing as happiness or not. I just got engaged four days ago. The girl laughed dumbly. A month ago, he was still her boyfriend, and a month later, he became another girl's fiancé. This was really hard to accept.

The girl didn't know how to face it, said "Wish you happiness!" to the boy, and hurriedly logged off. The girl went offline, so she didn't see the boy's subsequent words: Girl, do you remember? I once told you that I wanted to take you home to meet my family during this year's vacation. You said we were too young, and it wasn't appropriate to meet the parents yet, we should wait until we are older.

Do you know? At that time, I was so scared. My dad told me that if I couldn't bring the girl I liked home this year, I would have to follow his arrangement and get engaged to a girl I didn't like. I really like you! I want to take you home, but I don't want to make you feel uncomfortable, so if you don't want to, I won't force you.

But... You broke up with me. I was so sad, why did you deceive me? I like you so much! Why don't you believe me? Girl, no matter what, I love you, I really really love you!

A long time later, when the girl went online, she saw the boy's message and cried bitterly.

It was her distrust that ruined their love. The girl went to the boy's space and saw his diary left a long time ago, which expressed his full love for the girl. Those words pierced the girl's heart like needles. Originally, the boy loved her so much.

But it was too late. She left such a message in the boy's space: Ning Bao, I'm sorry, I thought you were deceiving me before, because I couldn't believe that you would fall in love with a girl you've never met, I'm sorry, I shouldn't have doubted you, but it's too late now. You are already someone else's fiancé, I have no way back, I didn't know you would get engaged so soon, I thought this breakup would lead to a reunion next time, but... it's impossible now, we really can't go back.

Even though I'm not your girlfriend anymore, I really love you. It's my misfortune that I can't be by your side. Even though I can't be your lover, I hope we can be friends, because I want to see the person I love happy with my own eyes.

Promise me, be happy, live happily and healthily. If there is a next life, I will meet you again, Ning Bao, I will never let go of your hand...

Dedicated to the heroes and beauties on the front line of love, you must hold his (her) hand tightly, otherwise letting go will be a lifetime regret!!
