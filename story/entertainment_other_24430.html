Luoyang encourages residents to kill flies and kill a reward of 50 cents :)

by cacard on 2007-07-10 19:51:12

Dahe Network - Henan Business Daily News Recently, a Luoyang office in order to kill flies, publicly promised to "kill a fly award 50 cents", the residents of the area actively started to kill more than 2,000 flies in two hours, and the office also took out more than 1,000 yuan in cash to exchange.

This approach has indeed promoted the enthusiasm of residents to fight flies, but some people think that this approach is hype and "neglect the essentials".

[Scene] A dead fly for 50 cents

At about 10:30 a.m. on July 1, Ge Uncle, who lives in the No.14 community of Xigong District, Luoyang City, went out to buy food in the vegetable market and walked to the community gate, and was attracted by an activity scene under the shade of a tree.

There were two tables with a red sign that read "Fly Control Prize Office." Several staff members were busy, holding dead flies on a piece of paper with sticks and counting them. The staff told him that the community is now carrying out fly acquisition activities, a fly 50 cents, if the kill fly to take over, immediately change the money.

"A fly costs 50 cents, how could anyone pay for such a disgusting thing?" Grandpa Ge memories. He went closer and looked carefully, and sure enough. After the number of flies killed was checked by the staff, a woman took the money and rode away happily on her bicycle.

From the propaganda page, Uncle Ge knew that this was the Luoyang West work office in the fly eradication activities. In order to encourage residents to take the initiative to combat flies, the office used the method of buying flies with money.

Hu Guisheng, director of the Luoyang Xigong Office, explained that as a grassroots organization, under the premise of strict law enforcement, they would not want to push the fly control work to a deep way through fines. Later, when we got together, we thought that the purchase of flies could more easily mobilize the enthusiasm of the public for the eradication of flies, so it was implemented.

Hu Guisheng said that after the entire activity, the six communities of the office purchased more than 2,000 flies, spending more than 1,000 yuan.

[Controversy] Spending money to kill flies is hype

Luoyang West construction office's alternative approach, although only in a few communities, spent more than 1,000 yuan, or attracted many people's controversy.

"That day, I saw a woman kill some flies outside the family yard where there are many flies, and then exchange them for money. Isn't that a waste of money?" "Said a shop owner at the entrance to the community of 14. The shopkeeper also believes that at present, Luoyang is creating a national health city, leaders attach great importance to it, so some people think of some strange tricks to hype, to attract the attention of superior leaders, is a typical "spend money to buy image."

On the Internet, some netizens have also debated. Netizen Jiejia Guitian believes that the government's starting point for buying flies is good, but the effect is worrying. "If the basic civic duty of killing flies is incentivized with money, the social situation can be imagined." In the end, such behavior will become the laughing stock of society." Whether it is appropriate for the Government to spend money to encourage the residents to catch flies and eradicate flies is not the fundamental issue. However, the current practice of the Office is to neglect the essentials and attract attention.

The show is also to make residents pay attention to it

Hu was not surprised to hear the noise after local media in Luoyang reported on the office's practices. He said he was just making a show, a hype. "In today's society, can people pay attention to something without hyping it?"

According to him, at present, the work of killing flies in Luoyang is very heavy, and large and small government departments are acting, and what is lacking is the enthusiasm of citizens.

"Usually the government is hanging banners to promote the eradication of flies, these forms of common people are familiar with, the effect is not ideal." By buying flies, the initiative of residents is improved, and the health knowledge is deeply rooted in people's hearts. This is money well spent." 'he said.

Hygiene is much better than in the past

No matter how outsiders argue, Ge Uncle has felt the changes brought about by the "government buy flies" fly control measures: "Several people that day was the first time to participate in fly control activities, very hard, but also dedicated to those who are easy to breed flies hygiene dead corners thoroughly clean, see people say, do not throw watermelon skin in the summer..."

Zhu Guoqiang, secretary of the Party branch of the community neighborhood Committee of the West construction Office, said that the environment and people interact, and through unremitting efforts, residents' health awareness has indeed improved, and health is much better than in the past, and the community environment is more and more beautiful. The reporter walked around the community for a long time and found that the ground was indeed very clean. Business daily reporter Wang Xiangqian article/photo
