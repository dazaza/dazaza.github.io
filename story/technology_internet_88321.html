Li Tanghua, One of the Top Ten Online Merchants in 2008: The Business Philosophy of a 59-Year-Old Online Merchant

by longyi on 2008-08-31 14:01:43

Since joining Alibaba's TrustPass in 2004, Li Tanghua began his journey as an online merchant. He frankly admitted that at his age, engaging in online trade came with many difficulties. At the beginning, he didn't know how to write blogs or post messages, so every day he would go online to learn from others, spending much of the time that many elderly people use for playing with their grandchildren on reading others' experiences and posts. It was precisely because of adhering to the philosophy of "Heaven rewards diligence, make up for clumsiness with hard work, persevere, and let the slow bird fly first" that Li Tanghua's business began to flourish online. In 2007, Li Tanghua's turnover reached 12.5 million yuan, which was basically all brought about by e-commerce. Compared to the phase before using e-commerce when the best annual turnover could only reach 3 million yuan, such a doubling performance would be impossible for a small company with only ten employees.

Behind the success, there is always unknown disappointment and failure. Li Tanghua also experienced the pain of losing his well-established empire due to not doing a good job of self-protection. In his own words, it was "heartbreaking." However, even so, Li Tanghua still sees a bright future for the development of e-commerce and online merchants. He said that China's e-commerce is still in its infancy, and everyone is feeling their way forward. He is willing to be a pioneer and experimenter in e-commerce, and calls for more small and medium-sized enterprises to participate, to share the experience and gains of e-commerce. Now, Li Tanghua already owns two registered trademarks and five utility model patents. He said that Chinese are no worse than foreigners. From initially acting as an agent for Japanese companies' products to now owning his own K.O brand and making 85% of domestic men's clothing brands his customers, the secret, Li Tanghua revealed without hesitation, lies in making one's own unique features in small products. Although pant hooks are small, what excites Li Tanghua is the national sentiment of an industry!

Just as the interview production was nearing completion, we were surprised to hear the good news that Li Tanghua had been awarded the honor of being one of the Top Ten Global Online Merchants in 2008. At the award ceremony, Li Tanghua said: "The Internet has given a 59-year-old Chinese old online merchant the same youthful vitality as young people. With e-commerce, I can still easily work for another 20 years! I will take this honor as a mission, continue to promote and publicize e-commerce, influence and lead more people to develop together and create wealth! Be an excellent online merchant with social responsibility!"
