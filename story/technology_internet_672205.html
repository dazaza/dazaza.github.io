The new models of MINI COUPE and MINI ROADSTER will be officially launched today.

by jintaimqr on 2012-02-22 16:18:57

MINI China launched two new MINI models today, the MINI COUPE[http://www.jt077.cn/] and the MINI ROADSTER[http://www.jt077.cn/], announcing their domestic prices. These are also the latest models introduced by MINI in the Chinese market, making the product line of the MINI family in China more diverse.

MINI Coupe

The production model of the all-new MINI Coupe has not changed much compared to the previous concept version, except for the addition of an automatically adjustable rear spoiler at the back. The MINI Coupe adopts a two-door, two-seat coupe design element, with a helmet-style roof that highlights its personality. In terms of body styling, the MINI COUPE continues the MINI family style while incorporating some modern design languages. The high-performance version, the MINI Coupe JCW, is equipped with a rear spoiler that can freely adjust when the vehicle is traveling at high speed for better stability. Meanwhile, the front windshield angle of the MINI Coupe is 13 degrees smaller than that of the MINI Cooper, making it more inclined and conforming more to the design requirements of a small sports car.

Power-wise, the MINI Cooper Coupe and MINI Cooper S Coupe models will be equipped with 1.6L and 1.6T engines, with maximum power outputs of 120 horsepower and 184 horsepower respectively, and peak torques of 160N·m and 240N·m respectively, which are the same as the engines used in the current MINI Cooper and Cooper S models. Additionally, after calibration, the 1.6T engine in the high-performance MINI Coupe JCW exceeds 200 horsepower, reaching 211 horsepower, making it the most powerful hatchback model under the MINI brand.

MINI Roadster

The MINI Roadster is the sixth model in the brand's current product lineup and also the first two-seater convertible in MINI history. The all-new MINI Roadster features a coupe-convertible design style, based on the coupe body but redesigned in detail, making the overall lines of the car fuller and rounder.

The MINI ROADSTER is 20mm lower than other MINI convertible sports cars, with a body length reaching 3728mm, while the standard three-door model is 3723mm, and both cars have the same wheelbase. Additionally, to control the vehicle weight and complexity of operation, the roof needs to be manually folded.

In the overseas market, the base model of the 2012 MINI ROADSTER has a maximum power output of 121 horsepower, priced at $25,050 (approximately RMB 159,000). It is expected that the acceleration process from 0 to 60 miles per hour takes 9.2 seconds, with a top speed of 124 miles per hour.

If you need higher power and better handling, then the 2012 MINI Cooper S Roadster is priced at $28,050 (about RMB 178,000), with a maximum power output of 184 horsepower. The acceleration process from 0 to 60 miles per hour takes 7 seconds, with a top speed of 141 miles per hour.

If you need even higher power and larger brake pads, only the 2012 MINI John Cooper Works Roadster with 206 horsepower can meet your needs. The starting price of this model is $35,200 (about RMB 223,000), with an acceleration time from 0 to 60 miles per hour of 6.5 seconds, and a top speed of 147 miles per hour.

Of course, the above are the prices of MINI Roadster in the overseas market. After entering the Chinese market as an imported car, the price will align with the current MINI models, and is expected to be comparable to the MINI Countryman model. For those small car fans who are fond of the MINI brand, these two convertible models will surely attract their attention. It is believed that not long after the launch of the new cars, we will see various colors of the all-new MINI sports cars on the streets.
