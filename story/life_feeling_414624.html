Teaching you how to properly go to a girl's house to fix the computer

by wangweimei on 2011-04-12 18:05:46

Our department recently conducted a census, the results of which showed that: girls generally have the experience of asking boys to come to their place to repair computers, and boys generally have the experience of being called upon by girls to repair their computers. These experiences usually occur during the unmarried stage, and after marriage, the probability of needing to repair a computer or being asked to repair one is almost zero. In order to understand this issue, we carried out a special study on this topic.

Research Process:

1) Why do girls ask unmarried boys to come to their place to repair computers? Is the computer broken on purpose or accidentally? Can it be repaired, is it repaired intentionally or deliberately not repaired? Is it repaired during the day or at night? Is it repaired indoors or outdoors? And so on. All these factors combined create many uncertainties. Repairing a computer itself does not possess any attributes, but the motives behind it are worth pondering. Therefore, we boldly hypothesize: asking unmarried boys to come over to repair computers is to create an opportunity to show affection towards the boy they like.
