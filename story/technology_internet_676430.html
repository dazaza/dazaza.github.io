The object to be weighed or the loaded vehicle is placed on the load-bearing platform, under the force of gravity, transferring the weight through the load-bearing platform to the weight sensor, causing deformation in the elastic body of the sensor. The strain gauge bridge circuit attached to the elastic body loses balance, outputting an electrical signal proportional to the weight value, which is amplified by a linear amplifier. Whether it’s a scale or a balance, the basic principle is to use sensors (force sensors) to convert the weight of the object into an electrical signal, sending it to the conditioning, A/D, and computing circuits (MCU), and then converting it into a digital display.
