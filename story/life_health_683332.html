Here are some methods you can try: Metabolism, from a biological perspective, refers to the process by which living organisms take nutrients from their environment and convert them into their own substances, while at the same time converting their existing components into waste and excreting it back into the environment — an ongoing renewal process.

1. Eating fish is related to metabolism speed

Ladies, you may not know that according to research, people who eat fish regularly have lower levels of a hormone called "Leptin" in their bodies. The level of this hormone is closely related to the speed of metabolism. If the level of this hormone is low, our metabolic rate will increase, burning more fat. Therefore, we should eat more fish to boost our metabolism, ideally eating fish 3-4 times a week.

2. Eat small meals frequently

Eating smaller meals more frequently is a great way to speed up your metabolism. When you eat, your body's metabolism speeds up. If ladies eat something every now and then, the body will adapt to this habit, keeping the metabolism rate high. You can divide your daily food intake into five meals.

3. Drink more tea

Tea contains caffeine and catechins, both of which are related to metabolism. When these two compounds enter the body, they will accelerate the metabolic rate and burn more fat. So, drinking two or three cups of tea every day can help the body consume an extra 50 calories.

4. Water, drink plenty of it

Water is very important for the body's metabolism because the body needs water to participate in the metabolic process. This means that if our body lacks water, the metabolism will slow down. Therefore, we must develop a habit of drinking more water so that our metabolism will speed up and fat will burn. Especially after eating pickled foods or pancake-like foods, we should drink more water.

5. Spicy foods promote metabolism

Many women think that eating spicy foods easily causes heatiness or acne, so they rarely eat them. In fact, spicy foods can speed up the body's metabolism and burn fat faster. This is because spicy foods contain a compound that promotes rapid metabolic activity, increasing the metabolic rate by 23%. So, ladies, if possible, add a little chili powder to your meals.
