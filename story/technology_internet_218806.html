Is the mixed air force of ORC 3 heroes = invincible?

by lk0cswa on 2009-08-06 06:38:48

On maps with taverns, such as the Sea Turtle Island map, you often encounter this situation.

Last time I played against an ORC player using UD on Howling Fang, his first release was a Tauren! He then released a Huntress, quickly leveling up to 2 through MF. When we met, I was at a disadvantage in terms of numbers because I was using ZZ flow. So I switched to Ghouls, but unexpectedly, after his base quickly upgraded to level 3, he converted his army into Wyverns + BF + a few Shamans. By 2H he used Seer, and by 3H he used Naga. With Shockwave + Lightning Chain + Forked Lightning, it was the most powerful group kill I had ever seen. Combined with the point shots from Wyverns, my heroes died very quickly, forcing me to switch back to ZZ. I also added some Golems, but they still died quickly because the fixed damage from Shockwave (225), Lightning Chain (180), and Forked Lightning (225) equaled one word for ZZ—death! The Golems, whether bombed by BF or pointed at by Wyverns, died instantly. Because the level 3 aura of the Tauren + Bloodlust increased the attack speed of Wyverns... Also, his Huntress troops made me afraid to use large amounts of air force.

Please give advice, how should I deal with this when playing HUM or UD?

Firstly, Shockwave cannot target air units.

Forked Lightning and Chain Lightning affect only 3 units effectively. (I personally fear CFF Chain Lightning + Forked Lightning + fire.)

Secondly, all 3 heroes are level 5, so if you randomly press CNE + targeted kills, you can also take out their heroes. (Specifically referring to Seer and Naga; Tauren is another matter.)

Then let's compare: Undead has 3 level 5 heroes + 7 spiders 2 vehicles + 6 flying ghosts.

OC has 3 level 5 heroes, 8 double-footed + 4 bats, which are roughly products of the same time (because spiders can be saved from book 1), if OC has ground troops, there won't be as many air forces.

Personally, I feel that OC poses a significant threat. They can't drink 3 invincibles before battle, and if they get hit by CNE, they will be stunned for a while, probably not having the chance to drink unless they're BT, and all 3 heroes carry healing scrolls, but that would waste money, and there wouldn't be enough troops. In short, it's hard to say who will win, but it doesn't seem as terrifying as you described. It should be evenly matched in terms of strength.

Returning to the previous topic, even if he releases them, I've said Shockwave doesn't target air units, and dual lightning affects only 3 units effectively (the power of Chain Lightning is negligible afterward). Not much damage can be dealt anyway. Even if you don't use NOVA to target heroes, the damage to troops is still significant. And since C can't keep up with healing, just attack his troops, or simply use DL + sleep to put his heroes to sleep without using Hydralisks.

It feels like your troop and hero configuration is too weak. Like the OC spider flow climbing to air force should form earlier than his, why didn't you suppress him?
