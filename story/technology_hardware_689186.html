TEE with printed patterns

by wgyuvs8a on 2012-03-03 20:00:06

The original meaning of UT is "Tee with printed patterns". All UT series products have two label plates. In addition to the ingredient and barcode plates that other Uniqlo products usually have, there is also a tag introducing the designer's background for this product. The designers come from all walks of life: it could be a London graffiti artist or British supermodel Agyness Deyn. Disney's new movie "Alice in Wonderland" has just premiered globally, and UT quickly launched the Alice limited series. Sometimes things that only exist in people's memories can be reborn through UT: such as the covers issued by "VOGUE" in the past hundred years, or the mobile suit Gundam which first appeared on the silver screen in 1979. UT has become a platform to showcase more designs. UT equals influence. "For me, so-called trendy brands are those brand stores I want to visit anytime," said Shi Yiwen, a 25-year-old office worker, "because they always tell me about new trends and new things." In fact, Kazuhiko Sato hopes that Uniqlo will always be a hot topic. He set the slogan for the new flagship store as "From Shanghai to the World", because "Shanghai is close to Tokyo, rather than emphasizing differences, we should face the world together." Previously in the New York store, Sato emphasized Tokyo, because this "makes young people in New York feel cool." For the flagship store on Nanjing West Road in Shanghai, Uniqlo invited BCJ (Bohlin Cywinski Jackson), the company that designed Apple Store and Bill Gates' mansion, to undertake the store design. The triangular terrain at the intersection of Nanjing West Road and Wujiang Road focuses the attention of passers-by on that tall, transparent building, while the nearby pedestrian street and subway exit ensure a steady flow of people.
