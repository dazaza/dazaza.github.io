Who knows the heartache of the lotus seed

by musicshow5 on 2012-02-08 10:20:21

Oppressed by someone, I feel indignant in my heart and write an article to record it, just to express my aspirations. As the saying goes: "The lotus seed has a bitter heart, and the pear has acidity in its belly." Rooted in this complex and ever-changing mundane world, there can be no avoiding entanglements or frustrations. No matter how brilliant one's life may be, no matter how dazzling one’s lifestyle appears, we are all made of flesh and blood. Facing the ebb and flow of life, the rise and fall of careers, we will all experience feelings of helplessness. How much more so for those who work tirelessly in front of small screens, already battered and bruised? Yet, in today's cold world, choosing to be weak often leads to a hard life; wanting to be strong might even lead to an unfortunate end. However, how many people are unwilling to strive for a better life by becoming stronger?

Since I have chosen the Lotus Shop, I should walk with pride. Every step I take, every experience I go through, is like walking on a beach, leaving clear footprints in the mud. These winding traces record my growth journey on Taobao: joyful and sorrowful; smooth and bumpy; illustrious and humble; full of hope and at the end of the road... Regardless of where I am, even if it is unrelated to my peers, the passing time and changing years cannot alter the malevolent hearts around me.

As the newly appointed manager of Xianglian Shop, choosing the lotus seed, which embodies both bitterness and sweetness, means that my journey is destined to be one of mixed hardship and sweetness. Until now, I've been hit while lying down, silently enduring until I was struck in the knee. Yes, you have temporarily triumphed, trampling on my pain! Some wounds never heal, even if forcibly stitched up, they will always leave scars, and there are even those who sprinkle salt on them, causing me unforgettable pain. The shocking eyes lurking around me are like tumors hidden within my body, defiling my kind heart or quietly consuming my spirit. No one knows when I might suddenly fall ill, no one knows what the result will be when my illness strikes: disappointment or loss of composure?

Can human sorrow ever be avoided? Once forced to close my business, yesterday I suddenly lost my privileges, and I realized that I could also lose my composure. I complain that my lotus flowers won't bloom, while others mock me for refusing to kiss the dust. Is this their disdain or our incompetence? My heart is restless, standing there blankly, and I suddenly realize that in this chaotic world, perhaps only I am here lamenting alone...

In this predicament, during this anxious journey, there is no one to rely on. I keenly feel the confusion of having no sales volume and no credibility. In the lowest point of my shop, I want to persist without compromise or discouragement. Only I know that this is the last hurdle in my heart. If I cross it, everything will prosper afterward; if I fall into it, I will be lost forever. As the spring breeze gradually rises, the picture shows a frozen heartache!
