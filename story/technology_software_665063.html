Men's fashion trends for early spring 2012

by shenwu134p on 2012-02-14 11:29:54

What are the men's fashion trends for early spring 2012? Ou Ruizong has specialized in men's fashion design for over 20 years and enjoys a prestigious reputation in the Minnan clothing industry. He believes that, from the perspective of trends and fashion, Minan men's fashion has been playing the role of a trailblazer in recent years. From the Chinese standing collar that swept the nation a few years ago, to the Korean 3D style last year, and this year's return to simplicity, it is almost always Minan men's fashion that leads the national trend.

Ou Ruizong said that although men's fashion this year does not have as many variations as women's fashion, designers still make every effort to unleash infinite creativity within the limited range of men's fashion styles. Looking at the performances on various runways, despite each brand boasting different design styles and runway themes, there are still commonalities to be found overall, whether in color, style, or material. Of course, designers use their own talents to make changes in elements such as collars, stitching lines, fabric splicing, and patterns, and the design changes in details are also commendable.

A clear trend in men's fashion this season in terms of color usage is simplicity. The colors used in the minimalist style are mostly black, white, and gray, with simple and elegant hues that not only go with everything but also make men's outfits appear more generous and neat. This season, many big brands have incorporated ethnic-style patterns into their designs, which are not only eye-catching but also full of personality and tension, especially shorts covered in ethnic-style patterns that can highlight a very special charm of masculinity. Geometric variant plaids, colorful checkerboards, large plaids with painted effects, and small plaids with a sense of refinement... all present a surprising variety of styles in men's fashion pairings for spring 2012.

Plaid men's fashion can either be an overall matching plaid outfit or a mix of different plaids, though the latter is much harder to pull off than the former. For example, traditional Scottish plaid in black and white stripes and small or enlarged plaids appear on silk evening jackets and trousers, paired with delicate silk cotton white shirts and satin bow ties, presenting a unique and fashionable look.

Looking back, what was the men's fashion style like in 2011? Ou Ruizong said that last year's men's fashion world was filled with popular elements such as purity, convergence, traces, and fantasy.

With the help of powerful modern technology, last year's mainstream men's fashion design restored the intimacy and comfort of old clothes made from recycled materials, even new recyclable eco-friendly materials (ECOCIRCLE) derived from soda bottles, giving clothing lasting vitality; inspired by the ecological environment, imitation natural textures and functional designs such as the mottled colors of marble and cell-like structure prints appeared in large quantities in men's fashion designs; the application of new techniques such as simulated wear and tear, water washing, natural fading effects, and crumpling treatments created a comfortable and natural fashionable appearance.

The culture of different regions outlines the trend of men's fashion decoration through detailed or rough traditional ethnic patterns and vibrant yet subtle contrasting tones. A large number of local details, decorative patterns, and fabrics are combined, traditional graphics with modern shapes, hard lines with casual loose contours — the "hard and soft" random splicing, along with mysterious tones, creates a subtle and diverse mixed effect... all making us feel exquisite and full of wonder.

Pausing before the nostalgic mirror corridor of the 1950s, the beautiful scenes of the past and the longing for simple life formed distinct Taobao men's fashion styles. The elegant straight silhouette that was once popular in the 1950s has become fashionable again, with classic double-breasted suit jackets combined with striped and plaid fabrics, and fresh design details presented a perfect atmosphere. The denim style full of Western spirit brings the MARLBORO spirit back to modern cities, being both modern and practical.

This once again proves that fashion is a cycle: wearing something that was popular a year ago: outdated; wearing something that was popular five years ago: old-fashioned; wearing something that was popular ten years ago: nostalgic; wearing something that was popular fifty years ago: becomes a retro classic.
