Details for a Perfect Wedding

Choosing Attendants - Eight Points to Consider

Selecting who should be your bridesmaid can be tricky. Here are some tips to help:

1. Family first - Consider close female relatives

2. No obligation - You don't have to reciprocate if someone was your bridesmaid previously

3. Location matters - Think about what you want your bridesmaid to do

4. Don't be stingy - Don't exclude friends who may not afford their own attire

5. Male bridesmaids - Consider male friends as attendants too

6. Other roles - Friends can have other roles like reading poetry

7. Announce early - Once decided, announce your bridesmaid choices promptly

Groom's Responsibilities

The groom should immediately start selecting best man and other groomsmen. The best man is an important assistant who helps with various tasks during the wedding process.
