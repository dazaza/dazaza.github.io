Shanghai announces investigation result of children's blood lead level exceeding standard, three enterprises exceed standard discharge

by zibojsq11 on 2012-02-26 14:21:30

The Shanghai municipal government officially announced the investigation results of the elevated blood lead level incident among some children in the Kangqiao area of Pudong on the 25th, identifying it as a sudden public health event triggered by increased levels of lead in the environment. The relevant enterprises will be held accountable by the government authorities according to law.

Forty-nine children were found to have elevated blood lead levels. In early September last year, during routine physical examinations before entering kindergarten or school, some children in the Kangqiao area were successively found to have blood lead levels exceeding normal indicators. Subsequently, relevant departments conducted blood lead tests on 1,306 local children and discovered 49 cases of elevated blood lead levels, mainly among children aged 1 to 3 years old.

Currently, the treatment of the 49 patients is progressing smoothly overall. Three people still need hospitalization, but their conditions are gradually improving.

Three lead-related enterprises were found to have exceeded emission standards. Investigations revealed that there were three main lead-related enterprises in the Kangqiao area: Shanghai Johnson Controls International Battery Co., Ltd., Shanghai Xinmingsource Auto Parts Co., Ltd., and Shanghai Kangshuo Waste Materials Utilization Co., Ltd. It was found that Johnson Controls had expanded its production scale without approval and exhibited phenomena of excessive lead emissions in exhaust gases; Xinmingsource擅自adjusted its production process, adding lead alloy counterweight production; Kangshuo mainly engaged in waste material recycling, and the lead and zinc concentrations in the soil at the northeast corner of the factory premises exceeded specified standards.

Joint investigations and expert evaluations also determined that the increase in lead content in the environment of the Kangqiao area was mainly due to the long-term accumulation of lead pollutants emitted by enterprises such as Johnson Controls and Xinmingsource, with Johnson Controls being the main source of lead pollutant emissions in the region. Based on the distribution of children with elevated blood lead levels, atmospheric lead emissions status, and soil lead accumulation trends, there was a relatively clear association between the elevated blood lead levels in children in the Kangqiao area and lead emissions from Johnson Controls, and a certain association with lead emissions from Xinmingsource. In addition, other lead pollution sources like Kangshuo also had an impact on the environment.
