Yin Xian: One of the 3-year-old twin boys died and the other suffered serious injuries in a fall from a building - their parents were not at home

Zhang Dashuang (a pseudonym) lay on the ICU bed with a respirator. This 3-year-old child, visiting the Hanshufang Fiction Network website, www.hanshufang.com for more updated chapters, didn't yet know that his brother would never be able to play with him again. More than 10 hours ago in "Xing Feng Legend," they were inseparable twin brothers. After more than 10 hours, they had been separated by life and death.

At around 11 PM on March 3rd, the father and son from the Xuanhua Fantasy genre died of gas poisoning due to boiling water extinguishing the fire in their stove.

Hanshufang Fiction Network www.hanshufang.com offers more updated chapters. At 500 meters north of the intersection of Tantu Road and Linquan Road, on the 5th floor of Ju Xian Mountain Villa Building No.1, a pair of 3-year-old twin brothers fell from the building. During the fall, both parents were not at home. The children were rushed to the hospital for emergency treatment; one died and the other was severely injured. The police and neighbors searched everywhere for their family, and it wasn't until after 1 AM that the twins' parents returned home and learned about the condition of their two children.

The two children were found lying on the ground in their pajamas. On the first floor of Building No.1 of Ju Xian Mountain Villa was a row of storefronts where Xiao Yu worked in one of the advertising companies. Around 11 PM on March 3rd, Xiao Yu faintly heard a sound similar to a cat's meow. Following the sound, he suddenly saw two children lying on the ground. "They were both wearing pajamas, one person lying on top of another. The child underneath had no response, and there was a pool of blood on the ground. The child on top kept crying." Xiao Yu walked over and saw the child on top slowly getting up and sitting down on the ground. His face and body were covered in mud, and when he saw Xiao Yu walk over, he extended a hand wanting Xiao Yu to pick him up.

"I guessed that they might have fallen from upstairs. I was worried he might be injured, so I didn't dare touch him and quickly returned to call the police."

After receiving the alarm, the police officers arrived at the scene, and an increasing number of people gathered around. "When I saw it, next to them was also a bottle of Wantai milk, which probably fell from upstairs with them," said one eyewitness.

Subsequently, the 120 ambulance arrived on site and took the two children to the Second City Hospital. One of them already showed no signs of life, while the other severely injured child was transferred to the Provincial Children's Hospital.

In the morning, reporters visited the Provincial Children's Hospital. In the ICU bed, the severely injured Dashiang lay motionless. A respirator was inserted into his mouth, and his arms, legs, and head were all wrapped in bandages. Nurses were taking care of him beside the bed. The little guy looked very painful, waving his bandaged hands.

According to the doctors at the Provincial Children's Hospital, "When he arrived, he was still conscious but restless. His face was pale, and his lips were dry. We immediately conducted a full-body examination on him."

Currently, Dashiang is still in critical condition, undergoing treatment and observation in the ICU.

By noon, the reporter arrived at the incident location, Ju Xian Mountain Villa. Although it was drizzling, the bloodstains on the ground hadn't been washed away. Many neighbors nearby knew about the previous night's fall accident. Until the children were sent to the hospital, their parents had not been seen. With such a long time without seeing the family come out looking for the children, the police also found it strange, so they went door-to-door asking questions. However, neither the fifth nor sixth-floor apartments responded. "We could see the light was on in the fifth floor apartment, and the balcony window was open. The sixth floor was dark, with its windows closed," said a neighbor nearby, speculating it might belong to the fifth floor household.

It wasn't until after 1 AM that the twins' parents returned home. According to a neighbor, the father claimed he was coming back from taking a bath. When they opened the door, someone noticed a table placed near the balcony window. "We don't know if the two children climbed onto the window using the table."

Currently, the police are investigating the cause of the accident.
