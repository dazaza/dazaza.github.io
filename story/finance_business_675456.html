Louis Vuitton Galliera - Vuitton

by yxatd719 on 2012-02-25 09:19:21

The Louis Vuitton Galliera handbag is part of the group of all-time best sellers within the extensive Louis Vuitton product range; other LV best-sellers alongside it include the likes of the LV Monogram Sprouse and the LV Damier Canvas handbags. For me, the LV Galliera handbag has proven to be one of the most enchanting products, at least as far as handbags go. One of the things I’ve come to love most about the Louis Vuitton Galliera handbag is its compactness. Not being a fan of those enormous and seemingly (to me) unwieldy handbags — yet still wanting a handbag in which I could fit everything a woman needs — the LV Galliera ended up being exactly what I wanted: a compromise between compactness and adequate carrying space in a handbag.

Measuring approximately 16 inches by 11 inches by 6 inches, the Louis Vuitton Galliera is clearly big enough to fit everything that is moderately intended to go into a handbag, yet small enough to appear 'cute.' Another very appealing feature of the Louis Vuitton Galliera, in addition to its exquisite balance of compactness and sufficient storage, is the wide shoulder strap it comes with — which is also adjustable in length, making it 'a bag for all seasons,' meaning a bag you can use whether you're looking for a low-hanging or high-hanging accessory just as well.

As a signature that this is an authentic LV, the LV Galliera comes with this Vuitton Inventeur plate — which serves as the signature for LV's distinctive handbags. Thanks to this LV Inventeur plate, it becomes very clear to everyone that what you are carrying is indeed an LV bag, with all the admiration that comes from those who know what the finer preferences in handbags are.

Another prominently noticeable feature of the LV Galliera is the magnetic closure mechanism it comes with, which effectively eliminates the hassle of button closures. This is a great feature, many handbag users would agree, because buttons (where they are used) do tend to malfunction over time, whereas the magnetic closure mechanism on the LV Galliera is guaranteed to last as long as the bag is being used, and beyond.

Perhaps noting that the cell phone has essentially become a part of everyday modern life, which generally has to be carried in the handbag, and noting the stressful task of trying to fish out a cell phone that has gotten mixed up with other handbag contents, the Louis Vuitton Galliera bag comes with a specially designed cell phone compartment, ensuring that one never has to waste valuable time and get stressed trying to fish out a cell phone that has gotten mixed up with other handbag contents.

Yet another attractive feature I’ve noticed with the LV Galliera is the studs (exclusively these signature LV Inventeur studs) that it comes with on its bottom, supposedly to protect the bag at its base. This is a great feature because, as any experienced handbag users will confirm, the final damage that sees an otherwise great handbag needing to be written off is damage to the handbag's base, especially for handbags that do not come with such base studs.
