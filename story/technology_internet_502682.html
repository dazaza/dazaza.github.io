At the scene of the incident, the reporter saw several colleagues of the deceased, some of whom were dressed the same as the deceased. One of the men said: "He is not an law enforcement officer, he is the safety officer of the construction site opposite."

According to the introduction, the deceased named Kang Qi (sound), over 40 years old, was the safety officer of a large shopping mall decoration construction site opposite the scene of the incident. Colleagues said that their clothes look a bit like security officers, and they also wear armbands, so there may be witnesses who mistakenly thought they were law enforcement officers. At the time of the accident, Kang Qi had just finished lunch and was preparing to return to the construction site when he was hit by a car. "The BMW has no license plate, it has been modified, it must be very fast, otherwise it would not have crashed dozens of meters away," a person in charge of the construction site said.

Kang Qi's colleagues all said that the car was too fast, but when asked why he was hit on the main road, they were unwilling to say more. The reporter saw that there was a small gap in the middle green belt where the accident occurred. Witnesses said that the person who was hit was crossing the road and wanted to take a shortcut through this gap, which resulted in the accident.

When the reporter got back to his senses and looked ahead, a white BMW car stopped not far away. A young man got out of the car, looking extremely frightened. In front of the BMW car, a man was curled up motionlessly. There was already blood flowing from the head position on the ground, which was about 30 meters away from the car.

Shop owners at the street corner said that originally the middle green belt of the Second Ring Road was completely closed and pedestrians were not allowed to pass through. To cross the road, one should go to the zebra crossing at the Kehua Road intersection on the east side. After the opposite construction site started work, many people came to eat at restaurants on Zongnan Zheng Street. For convenience, they took a shortcut through the green belt.

Yesterday at noon 12 o'clock, an accident occurred on the main road at the intersection of the Second Ring Road South Section 2 and Zongnan Zheng Street in Chengdu. Initially, someone live-tweeted that a suspected law enforcement officer was run over by an unlicensed BMW. Huaxi Metropolis Daily reporter confirmed on the scene that the person who was hit was not a law enforcement officer, but a safety officer with an armband from a nearby construction site.

Some eyewitnesses said that the BMW was driving at high speed and directly threw the man dozens of meters away, "bouncing up 3 meters high and falling heavily to the ground." The man who was hit wore dark blue clothes and also wore an armband. Mr. Fu said that everyone initially thought that the person who was hit might be a law enforcement officer, somewhat like a security patrolman.

The female boss of the "Qi Li Xiang" restaurant at No. 3 Zongnan Zheng Street said that the person who was hit was over 40 years old and often came to her restaurant for lunch, so she was quite familiar with his face. The man came around 11:30 am, together with several colleagues wearing the same "uniform", and left after finishing the meal quickly.
