GGwan "Divine Devilish Edict" is China's first card battle web game. Since its release, it has become lively and joyful with its unique selection of materials, new game methods, and rich and wonderful fantasies. The easy game battle mode allows players to finish a battle in 3 minutes, letting them experience the maximum gaming pleasure in the shortest time possible. Qile Park, guided by the concept of premium service, starts from the needs of players and continuously pursues the perfection and enrichment of game content. It has pioneered many considerate system settings, such as the card refining system and card merging system, bringing players the best gaming experience. Today, let's take a look at GGwan "Divine Devilish Edict"'s card refining system.

GGwan "Divine Devilish Edict"'s upgrade is divided into two parts: character upgrades and card upgrades. As a card battle game, the most important part is obviously the cards. Therefore, the selection and upgrading of cards directly affect the player's performance on the battlefield. Perhaps due to a busy work schedule or heavy academic workload, you may not have enough time to improve your card level. Seeing others with full-level cards, how can you not be anxious? The introduction of GGwan "Divine Devilish Edict"'s card refining system completely solves this problem. Not having enough time to refine cards? No worries, put the cards into the card refining furnace. Even if you're not online, the card levels will still increase. GGwan "Divine Devilish Edict" makes it so easy and enjoyable for you.

Below is a simple introduction to the card refining process:

Step one: Click on the card refining option and select the card refining furnace.

Step two: Select the cards.

Step three: Refine the cards and check the status of the card refining furnace.

Step four: Choose the type of furnace charm and extract experience points.

Step five: Take out the cards.

Step six: Card refining completed.

Easily, card upgrades are just that simple, allowing you to own full-level cards without sufficient gaming time, and still dominate the stage of the three realms war, achieving royal supremacy.
