10-Year-Old Girl Stabbed Dozens of Times at Home, Neighbors Suspect Step-Mother

by aia7892c on 2012-02-10 15:54:17

At around 10 p.m. on the evening of the 26th, in a residential building in the Sanyang community of Weicheng district, a 10-year-old girl named Wenwen (a pseudonym) was stabbed dozens of times inside her own home, resulting in hemorrhagic shock.

■ Incident

The little girl was stabbed dozens of times at home

Yesterday, after just having undergone surgery, Wenwen's condition was still unstable and she remained in the ICU of the Xianyang Central Hospital. According to the emergency room doctor who was on duty the night of the 26th, when Wenwen was brought in, she was suffering from hemorrhagic shock, with dozens of stab wounds to her head, a fractured skull, three cuts on her face, a broken nasal bone, and both of her pinky fingers were missing.

In the morning of yesterday, in the Sanyang community, some people were discussing the matter of Wenwen being attacked at home. Some said that Wenwen had been injured by her stepmother, Liu Fei (a pseudonym), but no one knew why the stepmother would hurt Wenwen.

One neighbor said that at around 10 p.m. on the 26th, his family was playing mahjong when someone suddenly knocked on his door asking for help. He followed them upstairs and saw Wenwen covered in blood, with much blood on the floor, the house in disarray, and many people present. Subsequently, he helped carry Wenwen downstairs, but he did not know who had injured Wenwen or how it happened.

Another neighbor said that at the time he was watching TV at home, and heard hurried footsteps on the stairs.

■ Neighbors

Good relationship between mother and daughter

When talking about the incident where Wenwen was injured on the night of the 26th, neighbors all expressed difficulty believing that Wenwen was hurt by her stepmother. "The relationship between the mother and daughter was very good. Often we saw the mother carrying the schoolbag to send her daughter to school, walking hand in hand on the road, even closer than biological mother and daughter," one neighbor said. Although Wenwen is already in the fifth grade, her stepmother still insists on picking her up and dropping her off at school every day.

"I've never seen them argue, nor have I ever seen Liu Fei scold or beat Wenwen." A neighbor from the same unit building said that although Liu Fei is Wenwen's stepmother, she treats Wenwen exceptionally well, just like her own child.

Another neighbor said that at noon on the 26th, she still saw Liu Fei taking Wenwen to her grandmother's house in the same Sanyang community for a meal.

According to an informed neighbor, Wenwen's parents divorced many years ago, and Wenwen was raised by her father. Because her father works away from home and only returns every few months, Wenwen used to live with her aunt. It has only been in the last couple of years that she started living with her stepmother. Usually, there are only Wenwen and her stepmother at home, and the stepmother has always treated Wenwen well. The stepmother is in her thirties and has no biological children.

There are also neighbors who claim that they saw Wenwen's father return a few days ago, so he suspects that problems arose between the couple, leading to the stepmother using a knife to injure Wenwen.

■ Impression

Wenwen is obedient, the stepmother is honest

In the impression of their left and right neighbors, the 10-year-old Wenwen is tall, big-eyed, fair-skinned, and very likable. Due to her introverted nature, Wenwen usually doesn't talk much, but she is very sensible and polite. From afar, she would smile and greet the uncles and aunts in the courtyard.

The neighbors' evaluation of Liu Fei is "honest person". In the neighbors' impressions, Liu Fei is not only a good wife, but also a good mother. "She usually doesn't talk much, but she takes good care of the child." The neighbors say that although her husband is rarely at home, Liu Fei alone takes good care of Wenwen, not only picking her up and dropping her off at school every day, but also often buying pretty clothes for Wenwen, making sure Wenwen is dressed both beautifully and cleanly.

And when seeing the neighbors, Liu Fei would also greet them friendly.

After the incident on the night of the 26th, someone called the police, and then the officers from the Dongfeng Road Police Station of the Weicheng Branch of the Xianyang Public Security Bureau arrived at the scene. But as of 5 p.m. yesterday afternoon, the station chief said that since the night of the 26th, Liu Fei hadn't said a single word, and they were allowing relevant family members to work on her thoughts.

At the police station, reporters were unable to meet any relatives of Wenwen, and her aunt who was caring for her at the hospital refused the reporter's interview.
