Several perfect alternatives to Google Form

by zhangyuanyi on 2010-06-07 12:04:12

Since Google Form left us for various reasons, creating forms and questionnaires has become a headache. People often ask me if I have a replacement product for Google Form. Today, I will introduce a few replacement services for Google Form.

Foreign chapters

We will start with foreign services first, but foreign services are charged, and add a lot of money, so students who are not good at English or need free services please jump directly to the domestic part.

Wufoo

Wufoo can be considered as the ancestor of online form applications, has been established in 2006, so its advantages are very powerful, support for a wide range of types of questions, the operation of the page is also extremely simple, good Chinese support, but also very easy to customize, can be well integrated with your website. However, Wufoo is a commercial paid service, so the downside is that the free version can only create 3 forms, each form can only have 10 questions, of course, if your budget is enough, Wufoo is definitely your first choice.

FormStack

FormStack is another form tool comparable to Wufoo, and it is also extremely powerful, not only supporting regular form creation, but also supporting online payments. FormStack also provides an Excel-like result management tool, which can completely replace Google Docs, but like Wufoo, FormStack also provides a complete replacement for Google Docs. FormStack is also a paid service, but as a competitor, it is slightly cheaper than Wufoo.

Icebrrg, FormSite and JotForm

Icebrrg, FormSite, and JotForm, as lower-tier competitors, differ from Wufoo and FormStack in that they have fewer features (which would be enough if they were just a replacement for Google Form), but they are also much cheaper. Although these three also have free programs, but that is to show that you want to use the comfortable, or have to pay, if your budget is limited, then you can consider these services.

Domestic chapter

Questionnaire star

Wufoo is one of the earliest companies to provide form services in China. Compared with foreign Wufoo, except for payment function, other functions are basically the same. Meanwhile, Wufoo also provides many features to meet the needs of domestic users, especially for the needs of enterprises, such as SSL support. For individual users, the free service is also basically enough.

investigatory

The function of the survey is much simpler than the questionnaire star, the question type is not as diverse as the questionnaire star, only the most basic few, but for individual users, it is still enough, the survey has just launched a new 2.0 version, all functions are completely free, if you are looking for a free program, then the survey is undoubtedly what you want.

ask

Compared with questionnaire star and survey party, Ask was established a little later, but in the function is not weak, Ask is to take the basic service is completely free, while providing a paid enterprise version, to provide support for some special needs of the enterprise, the only disadvantage is that my account activation mail into the trash, it seems that the domain name did not add SPF record.

Rush to answer

Answer is a new domestic Form service for individual users, all functions are completely free, the biggest feature is that the user interface is fresh and simple, the form editing interface is very close to Google Form, of course, the function is relative to the previous three are slightly thin, but for the simple needs of ordinary users is still enough, if you favor simple style, Not used to the first few heavy weights, then may wish to try to answer.

Sum up

In this field, in addition to the payment function, the domestic application level and foreign countries have been comparable, and the free version of domestic services are very limited, for ordinary users, basically enough, the price of the fee version is also very advantageous, undoubtedly, for most people, several domestic services are a better choice.
