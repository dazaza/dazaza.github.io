"A Curtain of Dreams" - A Classic Masterpiece by Qiong Yao, Grand Summer Presentation on Hunan TV

by jqrxass on 2009-09-07 15:35:15

Lü Ping, a beautiful and gentle, talented daughter of heaven, is the apple of the Wang family's eye. Zi Liang, the Wang family's second daughter who always appears less prominent in front of her sister, feels particularly inferior after failing the university entrance exam. But what makes Zi Liang even more disheartened is Chu Lian, the handsome and suave man she admires and worships. Chu Lian was destined from childhood to be the perfect match for Lü Ping, leaving Zi Liang with no choice but to deeply bury her feelings in her heart, guarding the strings of pearl curtains by the window, and singing her dreams alone.

"The Dream Behind the Curtain" Part One

I suddenly felt my eyes moisten. Chu Lian, his young, handsome face flickered before my eyes, those pitch-black shiny eyes, that healthy, brown skin, those spirited eyebrows... I seemed to return to my childhood, when I, Lü Ping, Chu Lian, and Chu Yi played together all day, went crazy together. Lü Ping was always quiet and composed, while I was always wild and unruly. Thus, Chu Lian called Lü Ping "Little Princess" and me "Wild Girl." In the blink of an eye, we've all grown up. Lü Ping has already graduated from university, Chu Yi is in her third year of university, and Chu Lian, having undergone reserve officer training, is now an engineer at a famous construction company. How quickly time passes! Among these childhood companions, I am the least accomplished, but Chu Lian's eyes watching me are so bright! However, this brilliance also radiates for Lü Ping, doesn't it? After a series of wild dances, the music changed to a slow waltz. Chu Lian did not let me go; he pulled me into his arms, gazing at me as he said, "Why did you come out so late?"

"The Dream Behind the Curtain" Part Two

"We grew up together," I muttered in explanation, a lump in my throat, "I knew from childhood that he and Lü Ping were the most suitable pair. Lü Ping, she is so beautiful, so excellent, so outstanding. In fact, I have never thought about competing with her for anything. Really." I couldn't help saying these things, not knowing why I had to say them. He placed his large hand on mine. "Stop talking!" he said roughly, "Let's go buy a guitar! I bet I can teach you within three months!" He started the car.

"The Dream Behind the Curtain" Part Three

I stared at him with wide eyes. He lit another cigarette, his face hidden behind the smoke, making him unclear. I only felt that his gaze was deep and unfathomable. This man, this strange Fei Yunfan, was he trying to tell me something? Had he foreseen something? Was I going to lose Chu Lian? Lose Chu Lian! I shivered. The sunlight outside the window was good, the twilight under the sunset, the charming little forest, the beautiful Lü Ping, revealing a sincere passion... Heaven, Chu Lian is just a mere mortal after all! I jumped again.

"The Dream Behind the Curtain" Part Four

I will never forget the scene at the airport during the farewell, I will never forget my father's profound gaze and my mother's swollen eyes from crying, I will never forget Chu Lian's expression when he held my hand, his difficulty in expressing himself, and his sorrowful, reluctant eyes. Lü Ping did not come to the airport, so I could only tell Chu Lian, "Kiss Lü Ping for me!" He took advantage of the crowd to whisper in my ear, "Can I kiss you for Lü Ping?"
