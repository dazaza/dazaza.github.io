【How to Wear Wigs】

1 - Divide your own hair into 2 bundles or more (depending on your hair volume and length)

2 - Braid the divided hair into plaits (I believe everyone can braid, right=.=)

3 - Use clips to fix the hair at the back of the head (use more clips if you have more hair)

Note: When winding up your hair, never coil the braids on top of your head, because the place will bulge. You should coil the braids in the concave area at the back of the head.

4 - Wear a hairnet (so when you wear the wig, it won't be seen)

5 - Wear the beautiful wig (the wig cap has an adjustable buckle, adjust to a suitable size by yourself)

【Wig Care】

1 - Try not to get close to high temperatures, because the material of the wig is not heat-resistant

2 - If the wig tangles up after long use and is hard to comb, don't pull it forcefully. Instead, spray a non-oily maintenance liquid specifically for wigs, then carefully untangle it slowly.

3 - The method of using non-oily maintenance liquid specifically for wigs is simple: just lightly spray a few times on the wig before wearing it) This can make the wig smooth and shiny, prevent static electricity, and keep the wig moisturized like when it was first bought!

4 - When combing longer wigs, divide the wig into several sections, comb from bottom to top, be gentle and patient.

5 - A small amount of hair loss during the process of arrangement and wearing is normal.
