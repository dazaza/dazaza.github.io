A completely redesigned user interface

The TM2008 beta interface has undergone a complete redesign, making it more visually pleasing and operations smoother. The professionally considerate interface design achieves perfect integration with Windows XP and Windows Vista. The whole new experience is so exciting.

Comprehensive optimization of program performance

TM2008 has comprehensively optimized its technical framework, greatly improving performance for worry-free online communication. Whether it's launching, logging in, managing friends, or conversing, everything is easy and simple.

Instant contact search

TM2008 introduces an instant contact search function that helps you quickly find the person you want to communicate with from a large number of friends. If you remember any information about a friend (such as nickname, remark name, QQ number, or email address), just type the relevant information in Chinese, English, or pinyin into the search box, and the instant search will quickly return precisely matched and intelligently sorted results, helping you quickly find your friend and easily initiate a conversation.

Convenient handling of unread messages

To handle various offline and unread messages, TM2008 has added the unread message box function. This novel design eliminates the disturbance of various unread messages, allowing you to conveniently and efficiently handle the unread messages you care about, even during a busy day.

Free switching of personal status

In addition to inheriting the classic "online", "away", and "invisible" statuses, TM2008 has also added the "Q me" and "mute" statuses. "Q me" allows you to fully express your desire to communicate; in "mute" mode, TM2008 automatically filters out all kinds of messages and notifications, quietly storing unread messages and notifications in the message box. You can also edit all statuses except "invisible" and "offline", fully showcasing your individuality. Moreover, TM2008 automatically reminds you of the settings for each status, and you can set your preferred reminder method at will.

Free expression of personal image

TM2008 provides rich ways to showcase your personal image. In addition to system-recommended images and QQ Show, you can also add any photo from your local computer as your personal image, and even crop and resize it freely to achieve the best effect for personal presentation.
