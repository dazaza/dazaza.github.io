Edison Chen bursts out upon hearing about a comeback; father Chen Zemin helps celebrate the opening of a new store. Source

by yuwei365 on 2009-12-06 15:06:37

Columbia Jacket; Edison Chen in great mood —— Sohu Entertainment reports, is Edison Chen shamelessly trying to make a comeback in the entertainment industry? Perhaps this is just our one-sided wish. Yesterday (December 5), Edison Chen came to Shanghai to attend the opening ceremony of his invested trendy store JUICE. His father, Chen Zemin, also personally showed up to cheer for his son. When asked about making a comeback at the scene, Edison Chen immediately turned sullen and angrily said: "I don't have any plans to make a comeback, cut the previous footage."

- Happiness: Father and good friends rally for new store opening

After the "sex photo scandal," Edison Chen could be described as having a notorious reputation. However, at yesterday's trendy store opening ceremony, besides family members, a group of steadfast friends were also present. Trendsetters like Hiroshi Fujiwara and good friends such as Charlie Yeung all made appearances. At the opening ceremony, Edison Chen was in high spirits. Whether it was during the eye-dotting ceremony for the lion dance or raising a toast in celebration, he always wore a smile on his face. During the group photo session, Edison Chen continuously waved behind everyone, playing around. During the event, Edison Chen's father, Chen Zemin, also personally attended to support his son. It seems that even after being exposed as bankrupt, he deeply understands the principle of raising children to take care of him in old age. When asked what words he had for his son, Chen Zemin said: "I hope he works hard." Edison Chen’s father, Chen Zemin, was present to congratulate him. Edison Chen took photos with his friends.

- Anger: Immediately loses temper upon hearing about making a comeback in the entertainment industry

"Making a comeback in the entertainment industry?" This sentence has seemingly become forbidden language in front of Edison Chen. When asked about this matter yesterday, Edison Chen immediately turned sullen and lost his temper. He stopped the journalist's questioning and filming on the spot, puffing up and calling over the staff saying: "Don't let them ask similar questions anymore, alright? You (pointing at the journalist), cut the last footage, start asking from the next question." Edison Chen's displeasure caused the interview to end hastily.
