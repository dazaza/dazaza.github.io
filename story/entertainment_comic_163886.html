"Ultimate Three Kingdoms 19" Episode-by-Episode Plot Introduction [First Episode]

Since they were young, Zhang Fei and Guan Yu have been righteous and protective of their female classmates, which led to them being expelled from kindergarten by the principal. They kept getting expelled for similar reasons. One day, in front of a high school, they were blocked by a fake eight gates and couldn't enter. They met Liu Bei, a poor noble. To take advantage of the two, Liu Bei proposed the Peach Garden Oath, binding them as brothers, which would elevate their social status and allow them to attend the prestigious "Donghan Academy." During the oath, when they pledged to "die on the same year, month, and day," Liu Bei was knocked unconscious by a flying rock. Thinking that their elder brother Liu Bei had died, Guan Yu attempted suicide to honor his promise, forcing Zhang Fei to knock him out. It turns out that Da Dong, who had traveled from the Gold Timeline to the Silver Timeline, was responsible for the accident. Since Xiu looked exactly like Liu Bei, they swapped places, allowing Xiu to replace Liu Bei in the Silver Timeline while Da Dong and the others took the real Liu Bei back to the Gold Timeline for treatment. Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei then attended classes at "Donghan Academy," the location of the "Alliance Master."

On the first day, Xiu (posing as Liu Bei) discovered that the academic level in the Silver Timeline was incredibly low; they didn't even know the multiplication table. Principal Wang Yun promised that if Hua Xiong could be driven away, Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei would not be expelled. Guan Yu agreed to fight.
