"My Wife Had an Affair This Week" - A Man's Troubles

The wife dates her lover in a hotel for pleasure, and the husband seeks help on an online forum in urgency... What are the reasons for the breakdown of marriage, and where is the secret to a long-lasting union?

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part One

My wife has had an affair, I seek advice from all netizens on how to resolve this. My wife works at a local branch company, and her lover is her superior. Their extramarital affair started around August last year when they were on a business trip. Although I have evidence from the past, since they work at the local branch and usually communicate via email, I did not intervene.

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part Two

I get off work late, so it's my wife's responsibility to pick up our child from daycare. However, my wife starts work early, she has to wake the children, feed them breakfast, and change their clothes for going out, while taking them to daycare is my job. After I send the kids home, I do the laundry and hang it out to dry before going to work. These are the household chores that I take care of, meaning that my wife does not handle all the housework. Even when I work night shifts and come home in the morning, I still need to take the kids to daycare.

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part Three

If your wife's superior (the third party) has social status and a family, there is a high possibility that he will break up with your wife if you get serious. Please use your brain and come up with clever ways. Similarly, when asking him to write a statement of assurance, consider how he should write it, what to write, and make it undeniable evidence... In short, make up your mind to learn relevant knowledge, and push towards a direction favorable to yourself.

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part Four

I feel like I am indeed wearing armor, and it's impenetrable armor... But when I think about everything she has done so far, no matter the outcome, it is necessary to end things here. I can no longer extend a helping hand to her. Has she considered how I feel? What she should say to me? These are necessary for me. For this, I've racked my brains.

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part Five

If I were in your wife's position, I would be shocked when you burst into the hotel room, not knowing how to explain myself, just being dazed and confused. Needless to say, knowing she has done something wrong to her husband, she also knows it cannot be undone. Even now, she must regret every day when she thinks about that incident, and regrets it endlessly. However, as much as she regrets, it is endless regret, and there are also questions. Why did you burst into the hotel room? If you knew your wife had an affair, why didn't you stop it earlier? Do you really want a divorce? In the future, can you really continue living with a wife who has a history of infidelity? I feel like you should tell her that you only burst into the hotel room after accumulating troubles.

"This Week, My Wife Had An Affair" Part Six

During weekdays, I never got a chance to talk to my wife, neither did I have the opportunity on Saturday. Today is Sunday, and I hope to talk to my wife. During the day, we spent time like a couple in disguise. In the evening, after the older child took a bath, my wife went to sleep in the inner living room. (Bitter laugh) I drank a little while pondering, hardly sleeping during that time. Later, I fell asleep too. Around 2 AM, I noticed my wife coming to the bedding area. I vaguely stopped her saying, "Go to sleep!" Then, while washing my face with water to refresh, I thought. "This kind of feeling I have, my wife definitely doesn't." Still the same sentence, either preserve or abandon.
