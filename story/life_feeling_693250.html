The budding love of youth

At a certain budding time,

I met my first lover in life.

It can't be described in words,

Studying at a key provincial university,

We were together for more than a year and a half,

And three years have passed since then.

Each of us is busy with our own lives and work!

An accidental contact let me find him again.

After half a year, I occasionally asked how he was doing.

That happiness and concern will never fade!

What drives feelings to grow step by step?

Time makes a person more mature.

It's the memory of his youth that dances when he didn't truly understand love back then.

Everyone is unforgettable about their first love,

From beginning to understand being loved, to letting go at last, continuing to search for true happiness.

Thinking back to us at that time,

We were innocent, approaching and demanding without any ulterior motive.

Silent contributions turn into spring water, flowing eastward!

Sweetness is always short-lived.

Recalling the bits and pieces of the past, the excitement and unforgettable moments still linger in my heart.

Let this life be without regrets!

Not understanding love but still loving each other, that's the outcome.

It's the cherishing of unwritable years!

Looking back, we should all think that the school days were the best.

Reality is such. The older a person grows, the more worries they have.

Lonely life, growing up alone.

When will you meet your true confidant, to hold hands together towards the end of life!

Feelings are really not the entirety of life,

Career and pursuit have become part of it.

Feelings cannot be forced either,

If you're willing to love someone, it might take only a second.

If you're unwilling to love, even years won't make a difference.

The youth of the past lies dormant,

When will your heart be stirred up again?

We possess firm beliefs,

We have brave hearts,

Let's strive together for tomorrow's dreams!

Let that youthful love of the past

Fly freely in our hearts,

Forever exist in our hearts, no one can take it away.

This is intangible love. A faint pain, subtly remembering you at a certain specific moment.

I can only silently ask in my heart: Are you okay? I hope everything is fine with you.

Maybe this is also a way to release emotions for comfort.

... Pursuing that budding love of youth
