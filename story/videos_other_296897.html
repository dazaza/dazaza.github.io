Electric vehicle violations are common, and strengthening management is imperative.

by dtjhsb119 on 2009-12-06 16:17:19

Now there are more and more electric vehicles on the street, it brings convenience to people while also bringing a great safety hazard to traffic safety. Riding an electric vehicle, wearing a ship life jacket, no need to pedal like riding a bicycle in order to move forward, you can see how popular they are by looking at all the electric vehicles on the street. However, with the increase in speed of these vehicles, how is it faster than riding a bicycle? Look at those who run red lights, can't they wait for a few dozen seconds at this red light?同期声 Citizen At this crossroads, electric scooters and bicycles tend to run red lights more often. It seems that running red lights has little constraining effect on electric vehicles. Originally, running red lights has always been a long-standing problem for electric vehicles. But besides running red lights, there are many other complaints from citizens about electric vehicles. Citizens The main violations are speeding and running red lights. Electric vehicles driving against traffic is very unsafe. Electric vehicles are non-motorized vehicles and generally should not be driven on motor vehicle lanes, but I still see it happen frequently. Nowadays, it's quite common for children and elderly people to ride electric vehicles. Children definitely cannot have as strong safety awareness as adults, and elderly people sometimes react slowly. As non-motorized vehicles, electric vehicles lack relevant license plate management systems. Moreover, their high speed and poor braking performance objectively bring certain difficulties to the enforcement of traffic police.同期声 Traffic Police Because electric vehicles are fast and have poor braking performance, accidents easily occur when we try to intercept them. The difficulty in law enforcement and poor braking performance causes a vicious cycle of frequent traffic accidents caused by electric vehicles. The reporter learned from the accident handling department of the city's traffic patrol that currently about 35% of daily handled traffic accidents are caused by electric vehicles. Because electric vehicles are fast and most people do not wear helmets, once an accident occurs, it will cause serious bodily harm to the parties involved, and may even lead to casualties.同期声 Captain of the Accident Handling Department of the City Traffic Police Brigade Jiang Ronghua There was recently a case where an electric vehicle caused a traffic accident resulting in death, so it is important to follow traffic laws and drive at low speeds when riding an electric vehicle to ensure your personal safety. In conclusion: Currently, relevant departments still lack effective management methods for electric vehicles, which is one of the reasons for frequent traffic accidents. If this continues, the goal of creating a good traffic order might be difficult to achieve. Therefore, it seems that solving this problem has become inevitable.
