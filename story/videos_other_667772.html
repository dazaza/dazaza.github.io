Chongqing will develop 11 equipment industry clusters including instruments and meters

After achieving a "remarkable transformation" in its industrial sector during the "Eleventh Five-Year Plan" period, Chongqing has set a target of 3 trillion yuan for its total industrial output during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period. During this period, while consolidating its advantages in the automobile and motorcycle industries, Chongqing will also focus on developing 11 equipment industry clusters, including numerical control machine tools, intelligent wind power equipment, rail transit equipment, energy-saving and environmental protection equipment, intelligent precision instruments and meters, and smart grid equipment.

Today, Mu Huaping, Director of the Chongqing Municipal Economic Information Commission, interpreted the newly released "Chongqing Industrial Transformation and Upgrading Plan for the Twelfth Five-Year Plan Period." He stated that in the grand blueprint of 3 trillion yuan drawn up during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan," the scale of the electronic information industry will reach one trillion yuan, and it will become the world's largest intelligent cloud-end industry cluster.

The total industrial output is expected to reach 3 trillion yuan by 2015

Mu Huaping introduced that during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, Chongqing will speed up the transformation and upgrading of its industry with the electronic information industry as the leader; increase the added value of the industry by focusing on high-end R&D manufacturing and processing trade settlement and other productive service sectors to improve the quality of industrial transformation and upgrading; reduce logistics costs by taking vertical integration as a breakthrough point to enhance the benefits of industrial transformation and upgrading; and transform traditional industries by taking the integration of informatization and industrialization as an entry point to improve the efficiency of industrial transformation and upgrading.

Through these transformation paths, the cumulative investment in industry will reach 1.5 trillion yuan by 2015, ensuring that the industrial sales output value reaches 2.5 trillion yuan, and striving for 3 trillion yuan.

Chongqing's electronic information industry exceeds one trillion yuan during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan"

Mu Huaping revealed that according to the plan, by 2015, Chongqing's electronic information industry will have cumulatively invested 300 billion yuan, achieving sales revenue of more than one trillion yuan, becoming an important pillar industry, and building a national-level electronic information industry base, the world's largest notebook computer production base, and Asia's largest offshore cloud computing center.

Currently, the city is vigorously promoting the "Cloud-End Plan" to build a 200 million unit-level electronic terminal industry base and a one million unit-level server cloud computing center. "Gathering 200-300 million servers is the ultimate goal!" he said, adding that for this purpose, telecommunications operators such as China Mobile and China Unicom, multinational giants, internet tycoons like Alibaba and Tencent are all targets that Chongqing is actively seeking to introduce.

It is understood that Tencent has planned to build a cloud computing center in Chongqing with a total of 300,000 servers, possibly located in the Liangjiang New Area.

Chongqing will develop 11 equipment industry clusters

According to the plan, Chongqing's traditional manufacturing industry will shift from pure manufacturing to a combination of manufacturing and creation, especially focusing on the development of high-end equipment manufacturing such as numerical control machine tools.

Mu Huaping introduced that during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, apart from consolidating the advantages of the automobile and motorcycle industries, making the automobile production capacity reach 3.2 million units, increasing the proportion of passenger cars to over 80%, and making the motorcycle production capacity reach 12 million units, Chongqing will also focus on developing 11 equipment industry clusters, including numerical control machine tools, intelligent wind power equipment, rail transit equipment, energy-saving and environmental protection equipment, intelligent precision instruments and meters, and smart grid equipment.

Chongqing plans to add 25 listed companies in 5 years

Mu Huaping stated that within five years, Chongqing plans to add 25 listed companies, introduce more than 30 Fortune Global 500 enterprises, and create more than 1.5 million new jobs. In terms of opening up the industrial sector, foreign investment utilization will accumulate to 20 billion US dollars, accounting for 40% of the city's total foreign investment utilization and 9% of industrial investment; outward investment will exceed 12 billion US dollars; the total import and export value of the industrial sector will reach 100 billion US dollars, with an average annual growth rate of more than 50%; the industrial sector will add 25 listed companies and introduce more than 30 Fortune Global 500 enterprises.
