Build apartments in outer space, enjoy playing "New Stellar Home"

by 56963tg on 2008-07-07 20:04:25

The Star Wars is getting more and more intense, and the competition for the throne is just around the corner. The public testing of China Game Center's "New Stellar Home" has been launched. With its unique home system, it allows you to own a five-star home in the virtual gaming world.

China Game Center's "New Stellar Home" (official website http://newspace.chinagames.net) is based on a space story. Imagine humans in the 23rd century, manned spacecraft surpassing the solar system and entering the embrace of the Milky Way. Since then, humanity faces a whole new universe. In the year 2303, humans first established the first space city - Longteng City - on the Bet行星 planet in the nearby River Wave star system. As members of the expeditionary force of the space age, we are entrusted with the mission of going to other planets to open up new human colonies, protect and build a new home for humanity.

The "New Stellar Home" by China Game Center will open a housing system, so players need to seize the opportunity to acquire properties and wait for appreciation. A large-scale land grab is about to begin! The housing system of "New Stellar Home" allows you to have a completely personalized living space. While PK-ing and entertaining, you can also buy apartments. Just like modern life, all decorations of the apartment can be personally crafted by yourself. You can choose your favorite furniture and arrange them at will. You can choose different plants for decoration. You can also buy safes as storage warehouses for your trophies. You can invite friends over to visit and strengthen your bonds, or even share your culinary skills, play cards and entertain. In short, this is your own personalized cozy space, where you can arrange it as you wish! Players who want to acquire properties should seize the time and not miss the good opportunity.

During the public testing period, there are plenty of benefits and continuous surprises. All newly registered players during the public testing period will receive various equipment and big gift packs for new soldiers as rewards upon their first login. When the character's overall level reaches different levels, there will also be different rewards, from rich interstellar coins, to powerful missiles, to various double-effect gland hormones, and even luxurious battle vehicles. All gifts can be claimed once every 50 levels, the higher the level, the more prizes.

Action speaks louder than words. Click your mouse now, download the client, start your stellar journey, and fight alongside us! New Stellar Home: http://newspace.chinagames.net
