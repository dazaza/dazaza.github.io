by uyahd9mjik on 2012-03-07 00:36:05

On November 28, the PTA spot price was adjusted, and the MEG price trend declined. The prices of high-grade wiping cloths, semi-dull polyester chips, and bright polyester chips remained stable for the time being. The CDP chip price was consolidated, and the polyester bottle chip price remained unchanged. The prices of acrylic short fibers (1.5D×38mm) and acrylic tow (3D×102mm) were both adjusted. CPL and nylon-6 chip prices showed a downward trend.

Supported by the raw material costs of this month's polymer settlement, the overall trading situation in the polyester filament market maintained temporary stability, with most transactions being small batches. However, some factories lowered their quotations. For example, in Shengze, one large factory’s semi-dull DTY had a reduction of 100-200 yuan/T on certain specifications, while FDY quotations remained stable. Another factory saw a decrease of 300-500 yuan/T for matte FDY and a drop of 400 yuan/T for matte POY. The POY factory quotes in Tongxiang and Taicang remained stable, with the current mainstream cash price for POY 150D/48F at 11,400 yuan/ton. Factories in Wuxi and Zhangjiagang also kept their polyester filament quotations stable, but there were still differences in actual sales prices across different regions. Currently, the upstream polymer material prices are showing fluctuating trends, which provide insufficient subsequent support for the polyester filament market. Downstream weaving and texturing enterprises have strong观望attitudes, making it difficult to increase the volume of polyester filament purchases. Moreover, spinning manufacturers' inventories continue to increase, and to promote sales, some discounts remain in place. Some varieties of all-cotton yarns have seen further price reductions. From the variety trends, the demand for all-cotton yarn 30s remains steady. The viscose rayon market conditions are weak, but the transaction volume of viscose rayon 30s has slightly increased. Prices of individual varieties of polyester-viscose yarns and polyester-cotton yarns have been reduced. In the pure polyester yarn market, except for 45s which has some sales volume, the trading volume of other specifications is very low, and the pure polyester yarn market quotations are stable with slight declines. The market central price of viscose short fibers is around 15,800 yuan/ton, with a slight decrease of 100 yuan/T compared to the weekend price. The market central price of polyester short fibers remains temporarily stable at around 10,650 yuan/ton. It is expected that the yarn products market will continue moderate adjustments in the future.
