The Land Bureau's work summary on land conservation and intensive use, related results: Accelerating the pace to promote the intensive use of land for the conservation and sustainable development of our city's economy. Land is an important foundation for economic construction, as well as a crucial regulation for maintaining macroeconomic balance and sustainable economic and social development. All developments must take land as a condition.

Our city is a typical example of a place with many people but little land, a region with rapid economic development, and the contradiction between land supply and demand is very prominent. The pressure to satisfy and bear the load with limited land resources is very heavy. Accordingly, in order to implement people-oriented, comprehensive, coordinated, and sustainable development, we must rationally face and respect the objective conditions and rigid constraints for development. Starting from national and city conditions, we should unite the scientific concept of development with a scientific land perspective, pay attention to the protection and rational utilization of land resources, promote intensive land use, improve management mechanisms, and seize good land conservation and intensive use as an important solution to this contradiction.

This is an important measure to fulfill the provincial Party committee's eight strategies for stimulating comprehensive, harmonious, and sustainable economic and social development, as well as an inherent demand to improve the utilization and management level of land resources. It also relates to our city's goal of enriching the people, strengthening the city, and taking the lead in development being smoothly realized.

Firstly, according to the unified deployment of the municipal Party committee and city hall, we have actively participated in a series of service years, adopting various effective measures to serve economic development, truly catching up with and firmly serving, achieving certain results.

From a scientific and long-term perspective, adjust the layout of construction land, strengthen the planning's foresight and current situation, improving the program's scientific nature and maneuverability.
