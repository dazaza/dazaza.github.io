When supermodels become naked models

by sepsea on 2006-07-21 12:58:41

The former girl next door has overnight become a star watched by thousands of people. For China, where the entertainment industry is not yet fully developed, such a way of becoming famous has indeed surprised all of us!

They are lucky ones and they do have something extraordinary.

However, it's not necessarily a good thing to be famous too quickly. It's impossible to adapt rapidly to such a great difference in status, especially for some little girls. Money and fame must be pursued, but one must not accumulate them crazily like newly-rich people. That's not a good way.

Fortunately, there are parents, there are adults. "Zhou Bi" and his family may well be the most clear-minded people. They just need to do what they should do now. Want to make money? Fame has been acquired already. Occasionally releasing several singles can still stay in the top rankings. There will be a lot of opportunities in the future. Why be in such a hurry?

Finally, let me quote a maxim widely used nowadays:

-- The key to success lies in establishing a reform path for sustainable development!
