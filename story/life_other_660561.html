Lonely Night, Gazing at the Moon and Sending Longing Thoughts

In a lonely night, I look up at the bright moon outside the window. The autumn atmosphere is strong. With a cup of wine, myriad emotions surge in my heart. In the season when leaves fall, there are more farewells. How I wish to hold your hand and place it on my heart. Though we cannot control our meetings and partings, as long as you remember me, my longing for you, drop by drop, will be like fragrant dew, permeating my heart.

"The most beautiful scene of my life was encountering you, quietly gazing at you in the bustling sea of people, unfamiliar yet familiar. Even though we breathe the same air under the same sky, I cannot embrace you. If time and space were to change our identities and names, I hope I would still recognize your eyes. Where will you be a thousand years from now? What kind of scenery will surround you then? Our story may not be beautiful, but it's so hard to forget..." Watching the TV drama "Myth" and listening to this melancholy yet elegant insert song, sorrow and reflection inevitably fill my heart. Stories in reality and those in the plot are always so similar, yet they can never compare because they are myths, mythological stories. Our lives are not myths; I cannot transcend time and space to search for our predestined encounter. I could not even wait for you for two thousand years, but I want to love you for ten thousand years right now.

Perhaps our love only lasted an instant, but how long must I wait for you? Deciding to choose something always requires great courage because I lost my last love due to one choice. We are separated by two cities, unable to sense even a trace of each other. I have no way of knowing any information about you. They say that if you look up, tears won't easily fall, but why, despite my head being raised all along, do tears involuntarily stream down like a flood, impossible to stop?

Time slips away bit by bit, but my longing does not weaken with it; instead, it grows stronger over time. Countless sleepless nights, tossing and turning, dreams where I meet you and dance together, and countless times waking up startled in the middle of the night with tears soaking my pillow, still unable to see your figure. I don't know, nor can I calculate, but I only know that my longing feels like an endless nightmare, repeatedly awakening the scenes in my dreams where I embrace you. Yearning is a kind of torment in love. Love is simple, yet difficult. It might be distant or near. Life is full of so many "maybes," but I don't want our love to be filled with so many uncertainties.

I have never regretted loving you. Because of our love, I am determined to give my whole heart and sincerity. Farewell is too hard, yearning too bitter. For me, autumn always brings so many reluctant choices. Each choice comes with a price that intimidates. But this autumn, I chose you, cherishing and loving each other without ever giving up. This wandering year, you are like a gift bestowed upon me again by heaven. How could I bear to just stare at you from afar?

Thinking of you is a kind of pain, a subtle ache! It doesn't come often, but it lingers.

Thinking of you is a kind of pain, a deep, unforgettable pain! It doesn't come often, but it remains profound!

Thinking of you never tires me, yet it hurts deeply! Unknowingly, it has seeped into my blood, tearing apart my heart, making the past not painful, but memories.

Memories linger in the days gone by, lingering thoughts stranded in the time tunnel, halted in darkness, hanging in the vast expanse. Tonight, I accompany the stars and moon alone, finally realizing that all searches have a process. Once far away, now close at hand, all endings have been written by fate. All bitterness, sorrow, joy, and happiness have already embarked. Yet, in this process, I suddenly forgot how it all began, in that distant yesterday that will never return. No matter how much I try to seek, everything passes like fleeting clouds. The traces of time are faint and shallow. A rugged road is destined for me to walk alone, always masking my sorrows with smiles, fearing others might discover this hidden wound until I met you. Only then did I realize how deeply I invest in feelings. Though tears mourn this affection, even as they drip from the corner of my eye, I still strive to maintain a smile. I cannot cry, I cannot regret, I cannot complain.

The wind stirs my hair. If you knew, you would feel the sadness, loneliness, and concealed solitude in that wind. I thought I had great endurance, able to withstand everything, including loneliness and sorrow. But I forgot that I am merely human, overestimating myself. Guarding a promise, pretending to be strong, the flowing years of time slip through my fingers unnoticed. The loneliness amidst prosperity cannot be resisted. I am like a pale flower, dispersing in the air, gracefully dancing, trickling like a dream. The moon above, the tears in my hands, fall aimlessly, landing in the deep valley. On this lonely night, I gaze at the moon alone, sending my longing thoughts. The absurdity of yesterday lingers until today, dripping heartbreak scattering in my heart, leaving an indescribable taste.

Everything has changed, only to look back with tears streaming down. It is fated to have an eternal attachment to you. Truly, when feelings run deep, obsession reaches its peak. Alas, the flowers fall with intent, but the water flows without care. Wine does not intoxicate, yet people get drunk. When emotions hurt, people are also hurt...
