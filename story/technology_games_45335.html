Blizzard pressures the cessation of development of the fan-made Starcraft DS.

by athw2002 on 2008-01-27 14:04:12

Perhaps many players know about the fan-made game "StarLite DS" from the NDS version of "StarCraft". This game was developed by overseas enthusiasts and is made entirely according to the first installment of the PC version of "StarCraft". The latest version, 0.03, already allows for basic gameplay and supports WiFi multiplayer. However, recently one of the developers announced externally that development on this fan-made game will cease.
