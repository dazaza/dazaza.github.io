Women's Day Gift Three: Goldfish originated in China and are among the earliest varieties of ornamental fish in the world. As the most culturally rich ornamental fish in the world, they embody the legend of beauty between motion and stillness, and have established themselves as noble national fish in the hearts of Chinese people. Goldfish symbolize wealth and auspiciousness; giving someone goldfish wishes them prosperity and abundance.
