Hottest game news in October 2009

by bestmen1988 on 2012-02-28 21:08:53

October delivers on its promise to be a huge month for all gamers and in some ways, it does! This is a big month for consoles with the launch of the new PlayStation Portable, Sony's PSPgo, news of a new 250GB PS3 coming out early November, and Nintendo announced a new larger screened DSi LL. Also making waves in October was the much anticipated music record DJ Hero, FIFA 10's record breaking launch reaching a milestone franchise release by selling over a hundred million copies. Other key releases in October include Dragon Age: Origins, Ratchet & Clank Future: A Crack in Time, and NFS Nitrous all going gold, plus rumors that Modern Warfare 2's PC version will be delayed.

Sony talked up a lot about the new handheld gaming console PSPgo hitting retail stores in October and threw a lot of excitement and buzz among the gaming community with its digital content distribution, sliding screen, sleek look. But Sony emphasized that it would not be released to replace the existing PSP. Next came the release of Activision's DJ Hero, a much anticipated music-based title. The game adds innovation to innovation and comes with a turntable controller allowing players to mix and crossfade like a successful DJ from scratch.

There was also news of another slimline PS3 with 250GB hard drive space. This console pushes the limits of a gaming, media, and entertainment console which can be used to store a massive amount of pictures, movies, games, and even watch Blu-ray movies. October also saw the Need For Speed franchise reach a record-breaking two hundred million sales with the launch of the highly anticipated EA Sports champion FIFA 10. Besides these captivating titles, the much awaited games Ratchet & Clank Future: A Crack in Time and Need For Speed: Nitrous both went gold. The latter is set to hit consoles on November 6, 2009, with an amplified soundtrack.

Another notable game that went gold last month is Dragon Age: Origins. BioWare released the gold for PS3, PC, and Xbox 360 platforms. Players can grab their copy when it releases on November 3, 2009.

Coming to more console news, just a couple of days ago, there were words about a new Nintendo DSi LL being announced by the Japanese gaming company, Nintendo. Apparently, this latest round of DSi sports a larger 4.2-inch screen. Along with its regular standard stylus, the new handheld system also features a fat stylus, expected to come in natural white, black brown, and burgundy vibrant colors.

We finally rounded off the month with one of the hottest rumors speculating around the gaming world. Rumors say that Modern Warfare 2's PC version has been delayed by a full two weeks. Earlier thought to be released on November 10th, this date has evidently been pushed forward to November 24th, 2009. No word yet whether the PS3 and Xbox 360 versions will be out on their designated time or not.
