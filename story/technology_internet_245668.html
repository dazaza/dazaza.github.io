Today is the first day after the National Day holiday. Everything is a new beginning.

During the holiday season, Microsoft's My Phone service got a major upgrade, and dropped the BETA tag after the LOGO, and released the official version. 2009-10-03 Received a notification email, was informed that the upgrade from 6th to 6th, when the system will not be accessible. Today, when I opened My Phone at work, I was able to access it normally, the interface was completely changed into Chinese, and I saw the new functions added by this upgrade.

The My Phone Space service displays the last known location of the phone on a map (based on where photos were last synced or shared using My Phone Space). This service needs to be activated by the user. Learn more

Lock your Lost phone If your phone is lost or stolen, My Phone Space can help protect your information by remotely locking your phone or erasing its contents. This feature is only available for select markets and may incur a fee. Learn more

Share photos on major social networking sites Now you can easily and conveniently post photos to Facebook, Flickr, MySpace, and Windows Live from your My Phone Space Web account or your phone. Learn more

Save space on your phone When you delete content from your phone, it will still be accessible through your Web account (until deleted from the archive folder). Learn more
