Ms. Zhang has been married for many years and has always been busy with her career. As her career develops steadily, she and her husband particularly value each holiday. She reviewed many vacation products and ultimately concluded that Dali stands out. In the future, it will take only two hours from Kunming to Dali. **Safe Preservation Against Inflation**

As a senior professional in the banking industry, Ms. Zhang places great emphasis on the preservation and appreciation of her family's assets. Especially during inflationary periods, nothing preserves value better than vacation properties. She is very optimistic about the future popularity of vacations in Dali. Owning a sea-view apartment allows personal vacations, health preservation for her parents, rental income, and taking advantage of the low cost now is the best choice against inflation.

Although Ms. Zhang's child is still young, as a mother, she started considering family wealth inheritance early. A sea-view apartment occupies rare mountain and water resources, with automatic renewal of 70-year property rights. Once her child grows up, she plans to pass it down, letting the ocean-view luxury residence be passed through generations. Additionally, Ms. Zhang emphasizes that social relationships are wealth. Once owning such a high-end private club, she can invite clients, business partners, leaders, and colleagues to enjoy seaside vacations together in Dali, easily building social networks. The wealth center of the Erhai International Eco-city Peninsula has helped her career advance three levels. Such a good residence naturally must be purchased.
