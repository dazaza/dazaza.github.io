Yin Yang Chaos诀: A man was criminally detained for kidnapping his former tenant's son for 120,000 yuan due to the need for decoration money

by sznstejx3q3 on 2012-03-02 19:57:20

The text message sent by the kidnapper to Mr. Cao. An 8-year-old boy was kidnapped, and the kidnappers demanded 120,000 yuan. The police climbed up the water pipe and broke the window to rescue the hostage. The suspect turned out to be the victim's landlord, who claimed he had no money for his wedding house renovation and wanted to make a "big deal". An 8-year-old boy was kidnapped at the entrance of the residential area. The kidnappers demanded a ransom of 120,000 yuan. Two hours after the incident, the boy's family dared to report the case. The Feixi police quickly launched a secret investigation and determined that it was an insider crime. In order to stabilize the suspect, the police decided to conduct a "transaction". Nine hours after receiving the alarm, the police climbed up the water pipe and broke the window, successfully rescuing the kidnapped boy. At this time, the boy was completely bound with wires and adhesive tape. On February 29, the Feixi police introduced the case details, stating that the suspect Sun had rented a house to the boy's parents. Due to financial difficulties caused by wedding house renovations, he took a reckless risk. Currently, he has been criminally detained on suspicion of kidnapping.

A

The boy went missing

The kidnappers extorted 120,000 yuan and threatened not to report the case

In Taohua Town, Feixi County, Mr. Cao has an 8-year-old son named Xiaoqiang (a pseudonym). At noon on February 9th, unlike usual, Xiaoqiang did not return home for lunch. Where did the child go? The Cao family quickly began searching everywhere.

From school to home, it was only a few hundred meters, but the family could not find the child. Just as they were anxious, at 1:03 PM, Mr. Cao's mobile phone received a text message. Upon seeing its content, the whole family was shocked. "Mr. Cao, hello, your son is in our hands now. If you want your son to live, prepare 120,000 yuan in cash and wait for our call. My people are tracking you 24 hours a day. If you report the case, you will have to prepare your son's body! We only seek wealth. As soon as the money arrives, we will release your son. Your son is very safe now, in Feidong."

Mr. Cao requested a conversation to confirm his son's situation. In the phone call, Mr. Cao indeed heard his son's voice. The kidnapper soon hung up the phone.

Suddenly, the son was kidnapped, and the Cao family was momentarily stunned. They didn't dare to act rashly until around 3 PM when Mr. Cao decided to report the case.

The Feixi police immediately formed a special task force. After investigation, the officers discovered that this case was very suspicious because Xiaoqiang was kidnapped at the entrance of his own residential area.

Secret Investigation

The kidnappers were actually nearby, and there was only one person

The hostage was only 8 years old. Any mistake could put him in danger. Therefore, the special task force decided that all work would be conducted secretly.

In the text message sent to Mr. Cao, the kidnapper mentioned being in Feidong. Regarding this detail, the police analyzed that it might have been deliberately done to confuse the issue.

During the investigation of the mobile phone card used by the suspects, the police found that the card was unregistered, with no information about the owner.

From 1:03 PM onwards, until 6 PM, the kidnappers kept sending text messages to Mr. Cao, urging him to raise the money quickly and threatening that a partner was watching Mr. Cao. If he reported the case, his son would be dead.

Was the kidnapper alone or part of a gang? After investigation, the police found that there was no so-called tracker monitoring Mr. Cao. It was likely that the kidnapper was bluffing Mr. Cao, aiming to prevent him from reporting the case.

"Based on our understanding, the kidnapper should be acting alone," said the investigating officer.

C

Breakthrough in Doubts

The kidnapper only sends text messages, confirming he is familiar with the victim

The police investigation was progressing rapidly. While analyzing the time and location of the incident, an important doubt emerged - no one saw anyone forcibly taking the child away; the child went with the other person voluntarily.

"Xiaoqiang is 8 years old and already has cognitive abilities. He followed that person, which means he knew that person." According to Mr. Cao's account, the police also found that the kidnapper communicated entirely through text messages. Even when Mr. Cao spoke to his son, the kidnapper remained silent, "This indicates that the kidnapper feared his voice being heard by Mr. Cao, meaning that Mr. Cao knows this person and is quite familiar with him."

It was already 7 PM. Although Mr. Cao kept saying he was raising the money, the words between the lines of the text messages clearly showed that the kidnapper was getting restless.

"Are you ready with the money? Reply, we can't wait any longer." "I see you're still going to report the case, there's nothing to talk about." "Our boss is angry, saying you don't have the heart to negotiate! If you want your son, it will be in the next life!"

...

To stabilize the kidnapper, the police decided to let Mr. Cao agree to pay the money, continuing the investigation while attempting to find the suspect's whereabouts through the transaction. Subsequently, Mr. Cao, following the kidnapper's instructions, came to Building 23 of the nearby Shunhe Community, placing the ransom in the cargo compartment of a truck on the east side.

After the money was placed, the suspect did not appear.

Through the text messages sent to Mr. Cao, the police confirmed that the suspect was nearby, "We were dressed in civilian clothes, following Mr. Cao, and the suspect saw someone near Mr. Cao, refusing the transaction and demanding everyone to leave."

D

Encircling and Rescuing

The police climbed up the water pipe and broke the window to rescue the boy

Everyone left, but the kidnapper still did not appear. One team of police squatted near the area until 10 PM without any sound. Meanwhile, another team of police, after investigation, confirmed that the suspect was on the 4th floor of Building 2 in Huafeng Xinyuan community next to Shunhe Community. Around midnight, the police began encircling the area.

The police climbed up the water pipe to the fourth floor, smashed the bathroom window, and rushed in. The scene before their eyes shocked the police - Xiaoqiang was completely bound with wires and adhesive tape, lying there looking at the suddenly appeared police.

Nine hours after the incident, the hostage was successfully rescued, but the suspect disappeared.

"We have been controlling this place, he should not have escaped." The police began carefully searching the house and soon found a man curled up on the cement platform outside the window, "We approached and pulled him in."

It was understood that the suspect surnamed Sun, 25 years old, confessed that the house where the hostage was hidden was his own, which he had once rented to Mr. Cao, and they had disputes over rent issues.

According to Sun's confession, he was recently renovating his house for his wedding, but due to lack of economic resources, he was financially strained, so he kidnapped Mr. Cao's son and demanded ransom from Mr. Cao.

Currently, Sun has been criminally detained by the police on suspicion of kidnapping.
