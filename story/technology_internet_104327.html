FreeRinger: Free PC to PHONE worldwide calls

by xjyzhenai on 2008-11-18 09:41:44

FreeRinger is a free PC to PHONE global calling service based on the Talkster network.

Charging: Talkster free Internet phone: domestic and international long-distance change local!

FreeRinger lets you use your web browser. Free calls to phone numbers in more than 30 countries around the world. Do not need to download, do not need to install any software and plug-ins, simple to only embed a FLASH in the page, on the line! Never a payment requirement, no credit card required, no time etc. any restrictions! FreeRinger's calling service is based on the Talkster network, and the FLASH is only an interface plug-in for the Talkster network. But the difference is that Talkster is Phone to phone, while FreeRinger is PC to Phone.

The process of using image is also very simple, enter your friend's phone number (country code first) in international phone format and press Call. FreeRinger will connect you to your friends and generate a local callback number for you. Of course, FreeRinger doesn't have to care where your friends are. (If your friend is on Mars, hopefully he brought a working phone to JRZ.) Then you have 10 seconds to confirm your identity with your friend, tell him to call back the number, and you can talk as much as you like. Only your friends have to pay for local calls. This is especially true in some countries where local calls are free.
