Dear experts: My Diablo 1.11 version doesn't have difficulty selection. It directly goes to character selection and starts the game. What should I do?????????

by lk0zswa on 2009-08-21 07:03:05

Please help me.

Supplement to the question: Is there a way to directly select other difficulties?

You haven't completed the normal difficulty level, so you will directly enter the normal difficulty.

After completion, options will appear.

Have you completed the normal difficulty?

If not, you must complete everything before you can choose the difficulty level.

Below are all the quests:

The first quest: Den of Evil - The Nest of Evil

Your first quest is given by the witch Akara in the village. She says she is the leader here, and to lift the curse, she wants you, this brave warrior, to go to the nest of little devils. This nest is not far from the city after leaving (actually quite far). Moreover, the appearance of the caves here differs from the previous generation, so don't miss them. After entering the cave, you'll find that the cave has only one floor, and the enemies aren't hard to deal with. However, it's best to practice a bit on the surface first. After all, more experience points and levels won't hurt. In this nest, you need to kill all the monsters and deal with the devil's leader: Dark Labyrinth. This devil will rush towards you with a large group of enemies. When you first encounter him, you won't know which one is Dark Labyrinth! Remember during the battle, don't attack him first; take out the nearby devil wizards first, then kill Dark Labyrinth, otherwise the enemies will keep resurrecting, and you'll never finish killing them. Finally, after completing the task, report back to the witch. She will give you a skill.

The second quest: Sister’s Burial Grounds (Sisterly Affection)

Right after solving Witch Akara's task, you immediately get the next task from Captain Kashya here; she says Akara may indeed be the leader here, but the one actually giving orders is her, so you need to gain her trust. She says she has a sister who disappeared while on a mission at the graveyard. Without further ado, you need to bring her back. To do this, you need to pass through the Cold Plains to reach the graveyard. Note, you need to "cross the Cold Plains," not be in the Cold Plains. After entering the graveyard, a large group of monsters will attack you, and Kashya's sister has already become the leader of the monsters - Blood Raven. This will be a tough battle. Not only will enemies keep coming, but Blood Raven will keep standing far away shooting fire arrows at you. Moreover, she runs fast, making it difficult to kill her. Therefore, it's best to fight while running, escaping her range to deal with the smaller enemies first. Once you've cleared most of the smaller enemies, chase her down and kill her. After killing her, report back to Kashya. She will give you a mercenary, and from now on, you can hire mercenaries from her whenever you want. These mercenaries can be very useful sometimes. At least when you're losing, they can serve as "meat shields."

The third quest: The Search For Cain (Rescue Master Cain)

Once again, you will receive a task from Witch Akara immediately. You need to rescue Master Cain just like you did with Soldier Lees. Here, you must listen carefully to what Akara says. She says you need to go through the Underground Passage to the Dark Wood, find an Inifuss tree and a scroll. However, once you leave, you will see a cave entrance. That is not the Underground Passage Akara mentioned. The Underground Passage is called Underground Passage, and that cave has only two floors where you can practice and find some small treasures. The real Underground Passage is in Stony Field. Once inside the Underground Passage, there is something important to note. On the first floor of the Underground Passage, there are two exits. One leads downward, which is a dead end, only for practicing. The other exit leading upward is the way to the Dark Wood.

After exiting the tunnel, you can find a big tree, which is the Tree of Inifuss. Clicking on the tree will give you a scroll. Then take that scroll back to the village and talk to Akara. She will translate the scroll for you. At this point, return to Stony Field, where you will find a stone circle made of five stone pillars. Open the scroll you translated earlier, and you will see the order in which to touch the stone pillars. Follow the sequence to touch the five stone pillars, and a portal will appear in the center of the stone circle. Through this portal, you can reach Tristram. There are some stronger little devils and wizards here, so be careful dealing with them. As you kill them, you will see a cage hanging with someone inside, that's Cain. Once you kill near him, you can release him. He will first return to the village. If you have killed enough here, you can also return to the village. After completing this task, if you need to appraise items, you can find Cain. And when you buy things from Akara in the future, you can get a 10% discount!

The fourth quest: The Forgotten Tower

This task is obtained along the way while doing the previous task. You will see a bookshelf with a book on it. Once you open the book, it will give you this task. Before starting this task, you can talk to Charsi, Akara, or Kashya in the village. Anyway, there is a countess living in this tower. She uses the blood of a hundred virgins to sustain her life. The Dark Wood leads to the Black Marsh, and there is also a cave nearby where you can practice. Once you're ready, you can enter the tower. The monsters in this tower are not impressive. There are only six floors in total, and each floor is small, so it doesn't take long to clear them. On the sixth floor, you will encounter the countess. She will first cast a wall of fire. Charge through it and teach her a lesson. It won't take long to defeat her. Then, a treasure chest behind her will continuously drop large amounts of money and treasures like a slot machine. After collecting them, return to the village, and this task will be completed.

The fifth quest: Tools of the Trade (Barter)

This task is given by the blacksmith Charsi. She says she dropped an iron key before fleeing here, so she needs you to retrieve it. Once retrieved, she can forge magical weapons. Her iron key was dropped in the Horadric Malus Monastery Barracks. You need to go through the Black Marsh to reach Horadric Malus. You will see a castle, which is where the iron key is located. There are many and tough monsters here. It is difficult to pass without being at least level fourteen. Keep fighting your way in, and you will encounter a big guy. He is the blacksmith (Smith) here and the leader of this task. The guy is incredibly strong, but after killing him, you can find the iron key on the nearby rack. Take the iron key back to the village and hand it over to the blacksmith Charsi. She will help you combine a weapon and a gemstone into a magical item. The weapon must be SOCKED!

The sixth quest: Sisters to the Slaughter (Dangerous Beauties)

This task is given by Master Deckard Cain. You need to return to the monastery, find the underground crypt, enter the crypt to find Andariel's lair, and of course, kill her. The entrance to the lair is on the other side where you killed the blacksmith. This dungeon has five levels and is currently the toughest level. The first three levels are manageable, but starting from the fourth level, you will face a tough battle. I suggest that as soon as you feel overwhelmed, go back up and practice. Because the difficulty of the fifth level is much higher than the fourth level. Once you reach the fifth level, you will immediately encounter the female demon lord. This female demon lord is ruthless, with long-lasting poison attacks. Therefore, before entering the fifth level, make sure to buy some antidote potions. Also, healing potions cannot be missing. Fortunately, the exit of this level is very close. If you can't handle it, immediately retreat to the exit and go back upstairs. Unlike the first game, enemies won't wait for you at the exit to kill you, so it's a good strategy to retreat upstairs to replenish your supplies and then slowly grind through the fifth level. If you are lucky enough to kill the female demon lord, you can proceed to the Eastern world after returning to the village.

Eastern Side

The East is a brand new world where no one trusts you, so you have to earn their trust again.

The first quest: Radament’s Lair (Sewer Battle)

This is given by a woman named Atma, who is near the inn. She says her son and husband were killed by the monsters in the sewer, and those monsters have killed many people. If you kill the sewer monsters, she will reward you. The sewer entrance is under a Trap Doorfa in front of the city gate. Just open the door on the ground and go down to start this task. The enemies in the sewer are skilled in fire and poison, so it's best to prepare before going down. The deepest part of the fifth level of this sewer is where the boss is located. After killing it, remember to check because it will drop a book that can teach skills and a Horadric scroll. You can leave everything else, but these two items must be taken. After taking these two items, return to the city and report to Atma. She says she will inform others of what you have done for her, so people will trust you more. Then take the Horadric scroll to find Master Cain, and he will give you the next task.

The second quest: The Horadric Staff

Cain says the tomb where the corpses are stored is sealed and can only be opened with the Horadric Staff. So you need to go to the Halls of Dead in the Dry Hills to obtain the Cube that can combine the staff, then find the staff body in the Maggot Lair in the Far Oasis, and finally find the Headpiece with embedded gems in the Claw Viper Temple. This task is quite troublesome, especially since the wilderness is vast, and you need to climb the mountain to see the entrances to the Halls of Dead and the Far Oasis. However, these two underground mazes are not difficult, and you can easily pass through them. Especially the first Hall of Dead, after obtaining the Cube, go back to find Master Cain. He will tell you that you can use this Cube in the future to combine gems with the weapons you want, turning them into magical items, and the Cube can also be used as a box, so you don't have to worry about your storage box being too full! On the way to the Far Oasis, the quest log will light up, asking you to return to the city to talk to Drognan. At this point, the third quest is also activated. However, you can still go to the Far Oasis first and return to the city when you have time; the monsters in the Far Oasis are easy to deal with, but they can poison you, so it's best to prepare before entering. After obtaining the staff body, return to the city to find Master Cain.

Finally, you need to go to the Claw Viper Temple. This place is far away, and you need to go through the Lost City all the way to the Valley of Snakes to find the entrance. The monsters here are tough, so be careful. It's best to advance cautiously, killing until the second layer...
