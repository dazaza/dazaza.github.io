Incorporate the capital occupation of subsidiaries into the group's assessment and management scope.

by gal678hygd on 2012-02-17 14:40:54

First, the fund occupation index is synthesized and implemented in each unit, decreased by 3.98% year-on-year, vigorously reducing the capital occupied. Second, seize the opportunity of higher transaction prices in the steel market, increase spot sales, fully reduce spot inventory; clear warehouse to benefit the inventory, maximize the adjustment and use of accumulated, left-behind, scrapped, and eliminated materials; use the monitoring procedure, monitor and clean up the pending inspection warehouse every day to keep the finished products in the pending inspection warehouse under control. Third, strengthen the management of monetary funds and receivable bills, ensure the normal operation of production with the least amount of capital occupied. Fourth, deepen public bidding procurement, carry out benchmarking potential mining activities in depth, optimize financing structure, innovate financing models, broaden financing channels, effectively avoid financing risks, and reduce financial costs. Fifth, strengthen the management of fixed assets, intangible assets, long-term investments, etc., incorporate the capital occupied by subsidiaries into the group's assessment and management scope, so that asset management enters a virtuous development track. By the end of April, the capital occupied was reduced by 2.6 billion yuan compared to February.
