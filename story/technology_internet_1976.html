Feedo Style New template subscription for update browsing

by meetrain on 2006-04-10 19:20:59

A new service from Feedo Style is similar to what we've seen with desktop rss feed updates. By use

Feedo Style You can create a fixed Feedo Stlye template in your blog or personal web page, so that you can learn about subscription updates directly through the template.

This template is not only displayed in a blog or personal home page, you can also place it in your Google personal page, windows live personal home page, and other pages. This is a good way for you to keep track of subscription updates.

The style of the template is completely up to you, you only need to fill in the style you like when setting, everything is ok, the more special may be the template has dynamic and static points, that is, a content update title is static in the template, one is a rolling display presented to you, as to which choice is entirely your personal preference. Personally, I still think that the static will be more practical, and the rolling can be seen far away, and it will feel very messy after a long time.

It seems that not all blogs can use feedo style, at least not msn space. You can try it out on your own blog if you like. Enjoy
