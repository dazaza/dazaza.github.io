Beijing Municipal Leaders Meet with the Beijing Guoan Team, Secretary of the Municipal Committee, Liu Qi, Gives Four Points of Instruction

According to Sina Sports news on December 14th, the Beijing Guoan team, champions of the Chinese Super League, were warmly received by Liu Qi, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee. Liu Qi gave four points of instruction, encouraging the Beijing Guoan team to continue striving, uphold the spirit of always striving for first place, and promote the development of sports in Beijing.

After the Guoan team won the championship, the Beijing Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government sent congratulatory telegrams celebrating the team's victory in the Chinese Super League. On the morning of December 14th at 11 o'clock, while the team was preparing to attend the evening celebration banquet, the club received a call from the Beijing Sports Bureau informing them that the leaders of the Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government planned to meet with the players and staff of the Beijing Guoan team.

Subsequently, all players and coaches of the Beijing Guoan team were warmly received by Liu Qi, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee. Other leaders who also met with the Guoan team included Mayor of Beijing, Guo Jinlong, Executive Vice Mayor Ji Lin, Minister of the Publicity Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee Cai Fuchao, Vice Mayor Liu Jingmin, Huang Wei, and others.

During the meeting with the Guoan team, Liu Qi gave four points of instruction to the entire team. First, Liu Qi requested that the Guoan team continue to uphold the spirit of always striving for first place and maintain this positive corporate spirit. Second, while striving for excellence and improving results, the team should also focus on the cultivation of young talents and strengthen youth team development. Third, going forward, the government, enterprises, and clubs must work together—not just the Guoan club—to improve performance but also ensure that other sports projects in Beijing grow and enhance their level as well. Fourth, all sectors should learn from Beijing Guoan, showcasing the spirit of an international metropolis, promoting mass sports activities, and contributing to the accelerated construction of "a humanistic Beijing, a scientific and technological Beijing, and a green Beijing."

During the meeting, Beijing Mayor Guo Jinlong also shared his passion and attention towards sports with the Beijing Guoan team. Moreover, Guo Jinlong mentioned that he had once worked as a coach in the sports committee. It turns out that when Guo Jinlong was working in Zhong County, Sichuan, he served as a coach in the sports committee and was the启蒙教练 (initiating coach) of Zhou Jian'an, the current head coach of the Chinese men's volleyball team. Guo Jinlong expressed to the Guoan team that this year he only watched two of their matches: one was the home game against Chongqing, which they won 3-1; the other was the final match against Zhejiang, where Guoan triumphed 4-0. He hoped that next year the Guoan team would continue to strive, uphold the spirit of unity and hard work, and aim for good results.
