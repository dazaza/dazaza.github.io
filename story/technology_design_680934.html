"Heaven's Blade" new server opens on February 27th in the name of the gods

by qqwebgame on 2012-02-28 10:18:51

"Blade of Heaven" is the first Q-version fantasy web game that was highly anticipated in 2011. It's set against the backdrop of disputes among the divine, demonic, and immortal realms, rich with heroic elements. The game features dazzling professional skills, a powerful battle and instance system, various rare treasures and exotic pets, and an ever-changing array of gameplay options. Let us create our own myth in this breathtakingly beautiful fantasy world! Come play "Blade of Heaven". The double-line server 4 "Holy Grail War" will officially open at 11:00 on February 27, 2012! A variety of opening activities and exciting game content await your participation! Hurry up and join "Blade of Heaven" to craft our shared, stunningly beautiful fantasy world!

Activity Three: First Three Days of Server Launch, Big Recharge Returns!

Activity Scope: Dual Line Server Four "Holy Grail War"

Activity Time: February 27, 2012 - February 29, 2012 23:59 (First three days of new server launch)

Activity Content: During the first three days of server launch, players who recharge more than 500 Yuanbao per transaction (including 500 Yuanbao) can enjoy an additional 10% recharge return. Limited to 3 days, don't miss out!!

[Activity Example] Player A recharges 600 Yuanbao once during the activity period, then besides receiving the original 600 Yuanbao, the system will also give an additional 10% recharge return and notify via email, such as receiving a 60 Yuanbao return for 600 Yuanbao.

Mystic Ice Stone: Embed into ring or necklace, has a chance to make enemies fall into a frozen state.

Dizziness Stone: Embed into weapon, necklace, or ring, has a chance to make enemies fall into a coma state.

Mad Warrior Stone: Embed into weapon, necklace, or ring, gains extra damage bonus.
