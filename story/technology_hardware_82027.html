I. Do the different names for sandblasting suits have different meanings?

For sandblasting suits, we commonly refer to them as sandblasting suits, sand spraying suits, sand beating clothes, sand beating suits, sand prevention clothes, sand prevention suits, sand protection suits, sand protection clothes, etc. All these terms represent the same product - a sandblasting suit.

II. What materials are sandblasting suits made of?

The commonly used domestic sandblasting suits are made of canvas and rubber.

Canvas material sandblasting suits have good breathability, strong white reflection performance, comfortable and light wear, higher wear resistance, and a wider field of vision.

Rubber sandblasting suits, also known as leather sandblasting suits or skin sandblasting suits.

Rubber sandblasting suits are lined with fine canvas, soft and comfortable, and have high sealing performance.

III. What styles do sandblasting suits come in?

The commonly used sandblasting suits are one-piece style with a helmet and jacket, and there are also separate jackets and pants styles.

IV. Can sandblasting suits only be used in sandblasting work?

Sandblasting suits are essential protective equipment for sandblasting work and are also widely used in arc spraying, paint spraying, anti-corrosion projects, metal anti-corrosion, etc., providing health protection for operators.
