Goats carrying monkeys run to the vegetable field to steal mustard greens - this scene has been repeating recently. Uncle Ye, the vegetable farmer, informed the reporter that for the past two weeks, the goats and monkeys from the Licheng Animal Performance Troupe have been coming to his vegetable field every day to steal vegetables. Connie Princess House: http://www.connystyle.info. The monkey stands on the back of the goat to see if anyone is working in the vegetable field. If no one is found, it directs the goat over. They boldly enter the vegetable field and feast heartily.

The trainer, Mr. Liu, who came to drive away the monkeys and goats upon receiving the complaint, said that the goat and monkey are a performing duo, with fixed performances each day. After their performances, the trainer goes to direct other animals to perform. During this time, they take the opportunity to come out and steal vegetables. Regarding the "collaborative" thievery of the goat and monkey, the head of the animal performance troupe, Wu Jun, said that compensation for the loss has already been given to Uncle Ye. The animal performance troupe is also hiring more trainers to ensure such incidents do not happen again.

Report from Dongguan (reported by Chen Ming, with photos by Zhang Liangyu): "The monkeys and goats from the nearby animal performance troupe are too naughty, almost every day they come to steal my vegetables!" Yesterday, Uncle Ye, a farmer in Licheng, Changping, Dongguan, helplessly told the reporter.
