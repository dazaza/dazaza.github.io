Beijing Unicom online hall launches iPhone trade-in program

by anonymous on 2013-10-01 21:52:05

On September 24th, after the domestic launch of the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c, China Unicom in Beijing announced that the iPhone trade-in program has begun. Users can purchase the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c through a trade-in at either the Beijing Unicom online service hall (10010.com) or one of the 24 designated service centers.

The online service hall and physical service centers both offer the iPhone trade-in program.

It is reported that China Unicom in Beijing is running an iPhone trade-in campaign in 24 designated service centers. Additionally, the online service hall at 10010.com has also started the trade-in program for iPhones. Users with genuine iPhone 4/4s/5 models are eligible to participate.

Among them, the iPhone 4 can be credited up to 1300 yuan, the iPhone 4s up to 2000 yuan, and the iPhone 5 up to 3200 yuan. When users sign up for a contract plan for the new iPhone 5s and 5c, they only need to pay the difference between the contract price and the value of their old phone.

Unicom version offers higher subsidies on bills.

Currently, the contract package prices for the Unicom version of the iPhone 5s and iPhone 5c have been announced. The iPhone 5s 16GB model's contract price is 5499 yuan, the 32GB model's contract price is 6299 yuan, and the 64GB model's contract price is 7099 yuan; while the iPhone 5c 16GB model's contract price is 4699 yuan. Moreover, all these models support a zero-cost purchase contract plan. By choosing a 386-yuan package and staying connected for 30 months, you can obtain the iPhone 5s 16GB for free. For the iPhone 5c 16GB version, selecting a 286-yuan package within a 36-month contract period will allow you to acquire it for free.

Although compared to the Telecom version, the Unicom version has a higher contract price, the bill subsidy is greater. Taking the iPhone 5s 16GB model as an example, the bill subsidies for the 24-month contract period with the 126-yuan, 186-yuan, and 226-yuan packages are 1900 yuan, 2500 yuan, and 2900 yuan respectively, whereas the Telecom version's subsidies for the same 24-month period with the 129-yuan, 189-yuan, and 229-yuan packages are only 1290 yuan, 1890 yuan, and 2290 yuan respectively, differing by 610 yuan per tier.

In addition, the Unicom version of the iPhone 5s/5c perfectly supports China Unicom's HSPA+ high-speed 3G network, with speeds reaching up to 21 Mbps, ensuring users receive the ultimate internet experience. The Unicom version of the iPhone also supports global roaming services for both voice and data, meaning that Unicom iPhone 5s/5c users can stay connected globally, always having access to the internet and making calls without any obstacles, bringing convenience to iPhone users.
