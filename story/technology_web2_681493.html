Mission County four or five year work summary _ 217

by eawdqrxbh on 2012-02-28 13:58:35

Mission County "four or five" year work summary: The # # League Committee has always attached great importance to adolescent legal education and law-based governance, seriously implementing the county's "four or five" year plan. To this end, the Mission Committee developed a "# # County Juvenile Legal Publicity and Education in the Fourth Five-Year Plan", organizing, planning, and progressively implementing the entire legal framework and governance according to law, effectively ensuring that the content of the "four or five" year plan is implemented. Over the 5 years, we adhered to the key points and implementation details of the request for law-based governance, working along two lines, focusing on cadre and juvenile legal education, leveraging the advantages of various organizational works, organizing a series of rich and colorful activities with fruitful outcomes.

1. Enhance Knowledge, Strengthen Leadership, Coordinated Arrangements for Juvenile Legal Governance

The level of youth legal concepts and legal knowledge directly determines the progress and success of ruling the county according to law. # # Mission County regards the entire county’s youth leadership, strengthening the legal education of young people, and administering the job lawfully as leadership priorities, positioning the work significantly within the overall plan and firmly executing it.

a) Formation of Mechanism, Strengthening Leadership:

Mission County established a mechanism under the "four or five" year plan, headed by the Secretary of the County Party Committee in charge, the Deputy Head of the Office, responsible for daily operations, thereby strengthening leadership.

The Secretary of the County Party Committee's League Office listens to a special report on law-based governance each quarter, summarizing the first phase of work and deploying the next phase.

b) Overall Planning, Unified Deployment:

Mission County Party Committee placed legal governance at the forefront, joining the focus of work, including it in the committee's work report and annual working points, making clear the cutting points, key areas, and basic aspects of youth organizations' roles in legal governance, providing overall planning and unified deployment for legal governance.

c) Sound System, Perfect Mechanism:

We established a monthly and quarterly information feedback mechanism for timely finding and summarizing good experiences and typical cases from grassroots units conducting legal governance work, promoting them through the "# # Team Monthly" across the entire county, and promptly implementing ranking requirements and relevant spirits among various organizations.

We also established an end-of-year supervision mechanism, signing responsibility agreements with grassroots groups regarding juvenile legal governance, conducting evaluations, and effectively mobilizing the base to carry out the work enthusiastically.

2. Strengthening Legal Knowledge Popularization and Education, Enhancing Legal Ideals, Improving Teenagers’ Legal Quality

Firstly, dividing the youth levels and determining different contents for legal education:

Mission County Party Committee issued a "two zero zero four year working point", identifying legal knowledge and Deng Xiaoping Theory as five key elements for youth theory education in the county, focusing on constitutional knowledge for Youth League members, civil laws closely related to work and life, and legal knowledge related to maintaining social stability, enhancing young people's awareness of upholding the dignity of the law and using legal weapons to combat illegal and criminal acts;

For the vast number of children, education focuses on preventing juvenile crime and other relevant legal knowledge, helping them cultivate law-abiding habits, enhance their ability to distinguish right from wrong, and use the law to safeguard their legitimate rights and interests.
