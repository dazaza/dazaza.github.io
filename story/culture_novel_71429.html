The first person of the "God's Tomb" --------- Chen Zhan

by fendan on 2008-05-28 01:27:23

There is no doubt that Chen Zhan deserves to be the first person in the Divine Tomb.

Analysis of Chen Zhan's strength:

As the seals were broken layer by layer, Chen Zhan's lost power gradually returned.

However, at this time, he caused endless disputes because he controlled a "broken world." A few people with unfathomable strength came after him.

In order not to alarm the Tian family in the heavenly realm, Chen Zhan could only secretly fight these people. He once used his divine powers to control the mighty primordial force with his own spirit and was confined in a strange space with a certain big figure for four years, while his main body only retained part of the spiritual consciousness, and his cultivation was almost gone.

It includes Sheng Ji (the Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maidens), who absolutely possesses the strength of the middle stage of the Celestial level.

If you don't believe it, see:

"I am not the pure Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden. The true Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden is Sheng Ji, but she had an accident. Ten thousand years later, I was formed by the essence of heaven and earth, born near the Dansai Holy Land. In recent days, I have only known these things. I just want to be my original self and do not want to merge with others, so I shattered the spiritual consciousness that should not exist."

"Sheng Ji?!"

Chen Nan was extremely astonished. Wasn't Sheng Ji the sister of Feng Mo? How did she become the Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden? This...

Chen Nan's ignorance does not mean that the Fourth Ancestor and Fifth Ancestor are unaware. They know quite a bit about the hidden matters and quickly figured out the situation.

"That's right, Sheng Ji is indeed the Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden. She is not the biological sister of that madman!" said the Fourth Ancestor: "The Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden is formed by the essence scattered in heaven and earth, not born as mortal flesh. In every era, there will be a Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden appearing in the world. Sheng Ji was raised by Feng Mo..."

Through the Fourth Ancestor's explanation and analysis, Chen Nan understood the reasons.

Ten thousand years ago, behind Dansai Xuan, there was a powerful figure. She once competed for the broken world and also killed the guardian of the Western Lands. This person was Sheng Ji, who was raised by Feng Mo.

The reason Sheng Ji chose Dansai Xuan as her spokesperson is that they were both Seven Ultimate Women. However, at that time, Dansai Xuan had not yet 'realized', and her cultivation was not deep enough, making it not the best time for fusion.

Unexpectedly, in a fierce battle, Sheng Ji was nearly annihilated by an unimaginable Celestial-level strong enemy. However, she could not truly perish because she was the true Seven Ultimate Heavenly Maiden. Finally, all her essence dispersed, preparing to coalesce her true body in the next era and manifest, which led to Meng Ke'er's appearance.

Furthermore,

The ancient god sighed deeply and said: "Chen Zhan's benefactor said that he would go to face a strong enemy. It has been proven that the soulless Xuanwu Armor cannot withstand the opponent's fierce attacks. Rather than damaging the divine armor during the great battle, it would be better to leave it for someone destined to gather the soul of the divine armor and make it more effective in the future." From then on, they disappeared forever.

In contrast,

At this moment, Chen Nan already wore the Xuanwu Armor, but it was already broken! Songzande Bu's terrible strength made even the soulless Xuanwu Armor unable to bear it!

This proves that even the soulless Xuanwu Armor can only be destroyed by someone with Celestial-level or higher strength.

Furthermore,

Finally, the man holding the Bronze Ancient Spear and riding the celestial horse said: "Part of the broken world has been divided by someone with great magical power."

They seemed to value words like gold; each person briefly said one sentence.

"The coordinate gate is not here."

"It is in the space that has been divided."

"Use the aura of this broken world as guidance."

"Go and search!"

This explains what kind of great magical power the ancient kings referred to.

At least having the strength to fight with them.

And that was done by Chen Zhan ten thousand years ago.

This shows that Chen Zhan had Celestial-level strength ten thousand years ago.

"But you may not know, your father never reversed his mystical arts. He has always been cultivating in the forward direction, constantly suppressing the reversal of his mystical arts. Because he accidentally heard some secrets from the older generation of the Chen family that he shouldn't have known, so he wanted to take a completely different path, he wanted to open up another cultivation path. But the Call Demon Classic of the Chen family is too domineering, once entering this path, it's hard to extricate oneself. Your father, Chen Zhan, was extraordinarily talented and had unimaginable opportunities, his cultivation advanced rapidly, achieving remarkable accomplishments. But after reaching extraordinary heights, it became increasingly difficult to suppress the reverse changes of the Call Demon Classic, and eventually, in the tenth level of hell, he transformed into a demon!"

Chen Nan believed the words of the first generation Pope of Light, because in that distant past, his father, Chen Zhan, once warned him not to easily reverse the mystical arts.

It can be imagined that Chen Zhan suppressed his extraordinary powers for thousands of years, and in the end, he couldn't change it, and when it finally reversed, how intense it would be!

The first generation Pope confirmed his conjecture, saying: "Your father suffered for thousands of years, but unexpectedly, his mystical arts not only reversed, but reversed again and again, until they stopped automatically, and the result is what you've seen."

"What?" Chen Nan was extremely shocked and exclaimed: "Does the mystical art turn into a towering and peerless demonic body after the final reversal?!"

"Not necessarily, your father reversed eight times in total, I don't know if it counts as the ultimate completeness."

Ten thousand years ago, before Chen Zhan's mystical arts reversed, his strength had already reached the Celestial level.

Imagine what kind of strength it would be after 8 reversals!!!
