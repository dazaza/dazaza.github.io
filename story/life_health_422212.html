For Xiao Zhang, who is about to graduate from university, correcting his "buck teeth" has always been a difficult issue: traditional orthodontic techniques not only take a long time but also yield little effect. In Nanjing, many people with severe alveolar bone deformities (commonly known as "buck teeth") share the same feeling as Xiao Zhang.

Recently, the most advanced tooth correction technology in China - Beauty Crown - has been introduced to our city by Art Star Plastic Surgery and Cosmetic Center. This technology can quickly correct severe adult malocclusion and alveolar bone deformities, and the appearance of "buck teeth" can be improved within a day.

Suitable for correcting adult alveolar bone deformities. For adults with severe malocclusion and alveolar bone deformities, since growth and development have already stopped, bone metabolism and alveolar bone remodeling are relatively slow. Traditional orthodontic techniques not only require one to two years or even longer to complete treatment, but the correction effect is also not obvious. Currently, the Beauty Crown technology is considered by the medical community to be a relatively ideal correction method.

The "Beauty Crown" is a new and emerging dental beauty method that is currently popular in Europe, America, Japan, and South Korea. It adopts advanced international dental technologies and concepts such as the latest digital positioning technology and German Cerec 3D ceramic technology. It has advantages such as beautifying teeth without extraction, painless, short duration, strong teeth, and no black lines appearing on the teeth after many years, thus achieving lasting and effective results. The "Beauty Crown" uses 3D digital positioning technology, combining face shape and skin tone to design a dental beauty plan. After correction, the teeth are strong and aesthetically pleasing. On the day of surgery, lip shape, face shape, and occlusal relationship can be improved immediately, and within seven days, you can instantly achieve a standard smile exposing eight teeth.

Who is suitable for dental aesthetics? Experts indicate that those over eighteen years old with protruding teeth, canine teeth, small teeth, underbite, tooth damage, malocclusion, sparse or crowded teeth; yellow teeth, black teeth, tetracycline-stained teeth, and adults who have missed the optimal period for correction can all choose the Beauty Crown.

Not all patients with dental deformities need surgery. Since orthodontic cosmetic surgery requires high technical skills from doctors, currently, only a few hospitals in China offer it. Moreover, the cost is higher than general orthodontics. Experts suggest that if there is local or single tooth skewing, there is no need for surgical correction. Currently, the "Beauty Crown" technology launched by the 454th Hospital of the Chinese People's Liberation Army specifically targets adult orthodontics, requiring only seven days of correction time to easily obtain bright, white, and neatly aligned teeth.

The Chinese Dental Health Association cautions patients: Since the "Beauty Crown" imposes very high technical requirements on plastic surgery and cosmetic hospitals, patients need to carefully choose reputable hospitals to ensure the safety of treatment and its effectiveness.
