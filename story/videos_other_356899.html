When talking about the situation at the time of the incident, a worker at the gas station recalled: "The bullet flew past my head, if people were hit, they would have lost their lives!"

Wang Housheng, the person in charge of the Party-mass Affairs Office of China National Petroleum Yunnan Sales Company, introduced that the incident happened at exactly 8 p.m., just at the early peak of refueling. At that time, two cashiers were busy working when suddenly two men wearing helmets broke into the cashier's area, holding guns and threatening the cashiers to hand over the cash. When they encountered the refusal from the cashiers, one of the gas station managers quickly turned around and ran out to report the police. This action might have scared the two men, so they fled out of the cashier convenience store and in the chaos, one of them fired a shot at the cashier. According to Mr. Wang, the two men did not take away any property. Apart from one cashier having his waist skin scraped and being severely shocked, no other people were injured.

According to his recollection, it was just at the early peak of refueling at the gas station. They were busy refueling for customers when suddenly there was a loud sound. Before he could react, another explosion-like sound was heard. The worker recalled that the sound of gunfire was similar to firecrackers exploding, and one of the bullets flew right past his head.

Last night at 8 o'clock, both the staff and citizens who were refueling at the China National Petroleum gas station on the Dacaiyuan overpass in Kunming were startled by two sudden gunshots. After hearing someone whispering "robbery," people realized that two helmeted men had rushed out of the gas station on a motorcycle, leaving dust behind. According to the Kunming police notification, there was an unsuccessful armed robbery at the Xiaocaiyuan gas station, fortunately without casualties or property loss.

At 9:40 AM, a policeman released a high-power telescope, constantly searching for something on several buildings around the gas station, while several gas station workers were pointing and guiding. Journalists speculated that the police might be using the telescope to find bullet holes.

Last night, journalists again contacted the police. According to the police, after the incident, they promptly retrieved the surveillance video of the gas station during the incident. Meanwhile, after the initial investigation of the scene, the suspects did not use police handguns but possibly a homemade rifle.

According to Mr. Wang's introduction, the two men were all wearing helmets at that time, with somewhat robust bodies. Due to the speed at which the two men fled on a motorcycle, no one could see clearly.

"The bullet flew past my head"

"I heard that there was a robbery just now!" A driver who was refueling quietly said to the reporter. He had just entered the gas station and heard about the incident. The convenience store was robbed. This convenience store is also the cash register area of the gas station, where two workers were continuing to work as cashiers. The reporter saw several plainclothes policemen going in and out of the cash register room, constantly searching for something on the ground. Several policemen were also taking detailed photos of a yellow gas station worker's uniform placed in a corner. The reporter noticed that the clothes seemed to have damage marks. On-site, the reporter also found that the police had placed three exploration markers outside the gas station. A bystander told the reporter that from the distance, these three points are respectively the firing position, the place where the cashier's clothes were hit, and the possible location where the bullet was found.

Subsequently, the Kunming Municipal Public Security Bureau and Wuhua District Public Security Bureau have established a special case team to investigate this case.

At 9 o'clock in the morning, when the reporter arrived at the gas station, more than ten police cars were already parked along the road. The side of the gas station near the cash register convenience store had been sealed off by the police. Although one refueling lane was still in operation, due to dozens of police officers investigating the scene, the atmosphere at the gas station was very tense.

Mr. Yu also heard a gunshot. When he looked towards the cash register area, he saw two helmetless men riding a motorcycle trying to escape. One of the cashiers quickly chased out. Among them, a woman holding a gun fired a shot at the cashier running ahead. Mr. Yu said that when they came over, they saw that the cashier was not injured, but there was a shell left on the ground. At that time, they also searched for the second shell together, but failed to find it.

Last night, the reporters saw the cashiers who were threatened at that time in the hospital. Outside the emergency room, this cashier surnamed Guo was still full of fear. When the reporter asked her about the situation, she said: "Just got scared, nothing serious!"
