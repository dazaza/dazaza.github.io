A year ago, Gui E (a netizen) posted this song on the MaiTian Music Sharing Community. She recommended it to me many times but I ignored it...

The song "Lost and Found" is from the album "Open Field" by the band Taken By Trees. Simple guitar and piano, with various percussion instruments added behind the female voice, create a naturally cute, fresh and casual style. The elegant arrangement paired with the clean and unique beautiful voice of the lead vocalist Victoria allows us to hear sweetness in the sadness.

Let me emphasize the lead singer again - she's another one from Sweden. Why "another"? Because of my Nordic complex - I am always captivated by those who sing from that area, unable to resist and constantly dreaming of them... (Gui E's words)
