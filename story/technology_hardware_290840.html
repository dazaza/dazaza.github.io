Bohai Securities: Bank of Ningbo adheres to the characteristics of serving small and medium-sized enterprises, enjoying the warming of exports.

by dx0966632 on 2009-12-03 22:47:23

Investment Highlights: Expectations for export improvement are strengthening, and the economy is returning to a prosperous period. According to data from Ningbo Investigation Team of the National Bureau of Statistics, in the second quarter of 2009, the entrepreneur confidence index and enterprise prosperity index of Ningbo exceeded the 100-point mark, returning to the prosperity period, showing obvious signs of stabilization and recovery in Ningbo's economy. The Ningbo economy also shows signs of warming up: In the second quarter, the product order prosperity index of Ningbo city was 97.7, rising by 30 points compared to the previous quarter, still operating below the prosperity period, decreasing by 19.4 points compared to the same period last year.

The growth rate of credit is accelerating, with high growth expected in the second half of the year. By September 30, the total loan amount reached 79.606 billion yuan, increasing by 15.357 billion yuan in the third quarter, while increasing by 15.093 billion yuan in the first half of the year. The proportion of loans from branches in other places has increased rapidly, with Shanghai branch increasing by 2.09%, Nanjing and Suzhou branches increasing by 8.17%, and Shenzhen branch increasing by 2.70%. Deposits have grown by 37.5%, reaching 104.8 billion yuan.

Adhering to the characteristic of being established for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), Bank of Ningbo has a different business feature from other banks - focusing on developing SME loan business. The company has a strict access system for SME customers, segmenting customer groups, with only customers who have been in business for more than two years becoming potential customers. For the loan approval of potential customers, not only financial indicators are focused on, but also non-financial indicators such as utility bills and customs declaration forms, while paying attention to the preferences of the enterprise owner.

Investment Recommendations: We expect the company's net profit in 2009 to grow by 9%, with a net profit of 1.452 billion yuan, EPS of 0.58 yuan per share, a net profit growth of 41.2% in 2010, reaching 2.05 billion yuan, and earnings per share of 0.71 yuan (after dilution). The corresponding PE is 19.03X, PB is 2.52X, maintaining a buy rating.

The procedures for buying and selling stocks are as follows: When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, quantity, and price. And this commission is only valid on the day it is issued. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of buying or selling the stock. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the code of the stock is six digits. The code and abbreviation of the stock must be consistent when entrusting to buy or sell. At the same time, there are certain regulations regarding the quantity of stocks bought and sold: The quantity of stocks commissioned to buy must be an integer multiple of 100, but the quantity of stocks commissioned to sell does not have to be an integer multiple of 100.

There are four ways to entrust: counter submission entrustment, telephone automatic entrustment, computer automatic entrustment, and remote terminal entrustment.
