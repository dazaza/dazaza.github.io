Twitter Grader: Scores Twitter accounts

by hooooooo on 2008-10-29 16:13:54

Previously, McIntosh Ants introduced a tool for comprehensively evaluating website SEO called Website Grader. Website Grader analyzes website-related parameters and integrates 50 calculation conditions to provide an overall SEO score for the website.

Also from HubSpot, Twitter Grader evaluates scores for Twitter users based on factors such as the completeness of the Twitter account profile, the number/frequency of updated Tweets, the number of Followers, and their influence, with the highest possible score being 100.

Additionally, Twitter Grader provides widget calling codes for evaluation scores. You can also view a ranking list of the most influential Twitter users in Twitter Elite.

Similar Twitter evaluation tools include Twinfluence and WhatsYourTweetWorth.
