The Communist Youth League is a mass organization of progressive youth led by the Party, and youth work is an important component of the Party's cadre work. The main thought of the "Three Represents" not only provides a comprehensive guideline for strengthening the Party's construction but also points the way for the construction of the Communist Youth League; it is not only a new requirement for party organizations at all levels and a large number of Party members but also a new standard for organizations and cadres of the Youth League at all levels. Strengthening the construction of the Youth League in accordance with the requirements of the "Three Represents" is not only an inherent requirement for implementing the requirements of the "Three Represents" in the cause and various works of the Youth League but also provides a solid organizational guarantee for better implementation of the "Three Represents" in the cause and various works of the Youth League. Seriously studying, deeply understanding, and comprehensively implementing the important thoughts of the "Three Represents" has very important guiding significance for strengthening the construction of the Communist Youth League facing the new century, promoting the development of youth work and the cause of the Youth League, cultivating a generation of "four-have" new people across the century, and uniting and leading the broad masses of young people to shoulder the historical mission of realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation. The Youth League must follow the requirement of "Party building leading Youth League building", adhere to the direction of the "Three Represents", comprehensively strengthen its own construction, so that Youth League members always become the advanced representatives of the broad masses of young people, Youth League cadres always become the advanced representatives of the broad masses of Youth League members, and the Youth League always becomes the core of youth organizations.

Several Important Issues of the Communist Youth League Under the New Situation:

1. Political and Ideological Quality

Through the ideological education work carried out by Youth League organizations at all levels, the ideological awareness of most young workers is relatively high. Although they spend little time studying political theory, they are very concerned about international and domestic situations and have strong patriotic enthusiasm. The vast majority of young workers are full of confidence in the future of the country and the current prospects of state-owned enterprise reform, support the Party and government's determination and policy to severely punish corruption, and believe that by the middle of the next century, our country can stand as a major economic power in the East. Young workers generally believe that China's economic construction must be carried out in a peaceful and stable social environment. They have deep hatred for the stubbornness and serious disruption of normal social order by a small group of "molecules." Young workers pay close attention to the reform actions of enterprises and have strong enthusiasm for participation.

2. Cultural and Professional Quality

Currently, 64.5% of the youth in our institute have a diploma or higher education, and among the 35.5% of young workers who have not yet reached higher education, more than 60% are attending various adult education programs to further improve their cultural quality. Through in-depth investigation, it was found that their motivation for learning comes from four aspects. First, personal interests: currently, the employment model of grassroots units in our bureau has changed compared to before, especially in the selection of responsible persons at the team and section level, where competitive job postings are used. Only those with excellent professional skills will stand out in fierce competition, and in the recruitment of cadres and title evaluations, educational requirements are indispensable. Second, enterprises: the monopoly operation and "iron rice bowl" of power enterprises will eventually break down. The layoffs of employees from struggling enterprises have already sounded the alarm for them. The party, government, labor union, and Youth League organizations at all levels have created good external conditions for young workers' study, and a large amount of educational work has shown results. Third, public opinion: various information media have entered the high-speed internet information era from traditional radio and television. These media widely promote the concept of a knowledge-based economy, which subconsciously influences this generation of young people. They have no reason to refuse knowledge or refuse to study. Fourth, life and social circles: relatives out of concern and love exert pressure on them, and then, friends in their social circle are either studying or have equivalent academic qualifications. To have common language for communication, they are forced to improve their cultural quality.

3. Development of Youth League Activities

In recent years, the activities organized around the core work of enterprises such as "Double Striving and Double Creation," Youth Shock Teams, and Youth Volunteers have achieved remarkable results and have been recognized by party and government leaders. Over the years, the Youth League organizations have formed a complete system and set of methods, especially the activities launched like "Youth Civilization Posts," Project Hope, Youth Scientific Innovation, Mentorship Programs, and Skills Training, which have injected new vitality into the work of the Youth League. However, with the further development of the reform situation in power supply enterprises, some traditional activities or certain organizational methods of activities can no longer adapt or fully meet the needs of the situation. New activity carriers that are more in line with the characteristics of the times still need further research and exploration.

4. Status of Youth League Organizational Construction

The basic structure of grassroots Youth League committees, general branches, and branches, along with the staffing of Youth League cadres, is basically complete. There has been no merger of grassroots Youth League organizations due to institutional cancellations, but with the shift of focus towards production and operation, there are signs of dilution and neglect of Youth League organizational construction in some grassroots units. All Youth League cadres in our bureau are production backbone members, forming a team of grassroots Youth League cadres with high quality and strong style. However, problems exist: some Youth League cadres lack a comprehensive understanding of the difficulties of Youth League work under reform conditions, resulting in reduced work enthusiasm; some part-time Youth League cadres, due to work reasons, significantly reduce the energy devoted to Youth League work. Due to policy factors affecting the centralized acceptance of graduates from colleges and technical schools in previous years and the negative increase in employment personnel in recent years, the Youth League membership is showing a trend of annual shrinkage. Grassroots organizations cannot replenish fresh blood, reducing vitality. Some members, influenced by factors such as labor intensity, personal interests, and value orientations, lack enthusiasm for the Youth League organization, directly affecting the role of the Youth League organization.

5. Several Unfavorable Factors in Developing Youth League Work

1. Strong Profit Motive: Some young workers do not clearly understand or even completely do not know about the work and activities conducted by the Youth League committee. Because the basis for evaluating the performance of various organizations is the number of honorary titles or commendations received, Youth League organizations, in carrying out their work, focus on the macro process rather than the micro details, emphasizing results over processes, leading to relatively coarse work methods.

2. Complex Intersecting Nature: This issue is reflected more prominently among grassroots Youth League committee directors. Most Youth League secretaries at various levels spend 20% of their energy serving as party office assistants handling some party affairs; another 20% serving as labor union assistants organizing some activities; 20% as publicity assistants acting as correspondents in frontline production; 10% dealing with administrative matters; leaving only 30% of their energy for Youth League work, resulting in a significant shortage of energy.

3. Broad Social Nature: The target audience of Youth League work is young people, who are part of society. Due to the diversification and complexity of the social environment, our young workers often engage in much socializing, have many friends, and participate in many social events, consuming a lot of time and energy. This is one of the main reasons why some young workers lack enthusiasm for participating in Youth League activities.

4. Changing Concepts: Young people have their own thoughts and ideas. Being in a highly formative period of thinking patterns, they quickly accept new things, have limited ability to distinguish right from wrong, but weaker resistance to bad habits, making their ideas changeable and malleable.

5. Significant Resistance: Some people suspect that activities like "reaching standards," "creating first-class enterprises," and "reducing staff while increasing efficiency" are merely formalities. They strongly resist these activities because they prioritize personal benefits and mistakenly believe that the primary goal of enterprises should be to create economic benefits and improve employee economic treatment, advocating less formalistic work.

Basic Thoughts on Promoting the Deep Development of Youth League Work in Power Supply Enterprises

In accordance with the requirements of the State Power Corporation to establish a modern enterprise system, our bureau has determined to vigorously develop the "Golden Bridge Project," focusing on production and operations while balancing social benefits and advocating quality service. Therefore, first, we must closely integrate Youth League work with the central work of the enterprise, placing the starting point of Youth League work on how to better participate in the key work of the enterprise, so that the results of Youth League work are directly reflected in the production and operation of the enterprise. Secondly, we must enhance the integration of Youth League work with the key work of the enterprise, transforming surrounding the core into penetrating the core. We must not only strive for excellence but also achieve solving difficulties, accurately identifying the main attack areas and breakthroughs. By combining growth points in our own development with the key points of enterprise production and operations, we become an indispensable assistant in the process of modern enterprise production and operations.

For Youth League organizations to achieve new growth in the transformation and establishment of enterprises, they must break away from the traditional pattern formed under the long-term planned economic system and firmly establish new concepts adapted to the growth of modern enterprises. First, there must be an integration consciousness. Youth League work is not isolated or fleeting; it is an indispensable part of enterprise work that grows with the enterprise. As a youth organization accounting for a large proportion of the total number of employees in our bureau, Youth League organizations must meet the needs of enterprise reform and growth, constantly exploring new ways and summarizing new experiences, continuously maintaining control over work, and opening up broader spaces for Youth League growth. Second, there must be a quality consciousness. A significant increase in enterprise economic benefits largely depends on product quality. For power supply enterprises, the product is electric energy. Our electricity sales volume, line loss situation, safety production situation, power supply reliability, and power supply service quality are crucial to the survival and growth of the enterprise. Enterprise Youth League organizations must educate the youth through various forms to firmly establish quality consciousness, improving the quality of enterprise products and work, establishing a good enterprise image, and expanding the influence of the enterprise. Third, there must be an effectiveness consciousness. Enterprises focus on economic benefits, and Youth League work should abandon the original notion that "work is just about conducting activities," making improving enterprise economic benefits the goal of Youth League work. Focusing on the key and difficult points of enterprise production and operations, we organize Youth League members to contribute ideas and efforts, conducting activities such as the "Marketing Golden Ideas" competition. Fourth, there must be a technology consciousness. Enterprises must stand at the forefront of technological innovation in increasingly fierce market competition. Therefore, Youth League work must always highlight technology, leveraging the intellectual advantages of the youth to promote enterprise technological innovation and scientific and technological progress. Fifth, there must be a talent consciousness. It must be clear that the function of modern enterprise Youth League work is to cultivate young talents, reinforcing ideological education and service functions for the youth. Youth League organizations must incorporate methods and paths for nurturing talent into a meticulous modern enterprise management system, truly integrating Youth League actions with Party work and enterprise production and operation work.

Recently, the Party Committee of the Bureau timely launched a series of publicity and education campaigns such as "Quality Service is the Lifeline of the National Grid," making everyone realize that the fate of the enterprise is closely related to each individual's interests. Only when the enterprise achieves long-term growth can our personal interests be guaranteed. We must establish the concept that "we and the enterprise rise and fall together, sharing the same boat," ensuring that everyone, always, and everything has standards and systems, and everyone, always, and everything considers the enterprise. We must strengthen operational awareness and establish the working mindset that "production revolves around operations, operations revolve around the market, and the market revolves around customers." Combining the work characteristics of the power supply department, how do we demonstrate "production revolving around operations," and how do we generate benefits in specialized work? Through practical work, we fully realize the importance of safe production work. Ensuring safe production is the foundation for achieving all work goals. Stability in safety ensures stability in the overall situation. At the same time, we seek benefits from the following six aspects, ensuring that we ride the waves and stay ahead in the major trend of power system reform.

1. Strengthening reform and innovation, seeking benefits from reform. Reform and innovation are the driving force for the continuous healthy growth of enterprises. Only by continuously innovating can we bring vitality and dynamism to the enterprise, ensuring its continuous improvement and adapting to new situations and environments. Each class and stockholder combines its own work, continuously learning and borrowing from the advanced experiences of power enterprises throughout the province and city, creatively carrying out work, and achieving new breakthroughs in reform and innovation;

2. Refusing violations, seeking benefits from safety work. Deeply carrying out the creation of "Violation-Free" teams and individuals, firmly establishing the concept that "violations are accidents," treating violations as if they were accidents, achieving "zero violations" and "zero accidents," ensuring the safety of the power grid, equipment, and personnel;

3. Strengthening technological innovation, seeking benefits from technological achievements. Technology is the first productive force. For enterprises to grow, they must strengthen technological investment, encouraging employees to fully utilize their intelligence and actively contribute techniques and suggestions to the enterprise, organizing and conducting "five small activities" such as small inventions and innovations, and establishing reward mechanisms to actively promote the conversion of achievements;

4. Strengthening business training, competing for benefits in time. With the acceleration of our specialized management steps and the expansion of our jurisdictional power supply area, the business quality of personnel may vary. Through strengthening training work, improving business quality, thereby enhancing efficiency in handling daily and abnormal tasks, ensuring power supply;

5. Reinforcing safety awareness, creating a "Violation-Free Enterprise." Regardless of the size of the work or the distance traveled, construction units will dispatch vehicles and arrive at the work site one day in advance, carefully analyzing safety matters to be noted during the work and noting them on the work ticket. For large projects, the secretary and director personally participate in supervision;

6. Carefully controlling the acceptance checkpoint, seeking benefits from construction units. The quality of construction techniques by construction units and the quality of equipment acceptance by acceptance personnel directly affect the long-term safe operation of the power grid. We must carefully inspect installed equipment as if it were our own wealth.

Because the power industry belongs to a naturally monopolistic industry, the traditional mindset under the planned economy has left a deep impression on our employees, lacking market awareness, competitive awareness, and a sense of crisis. In response to this, we will strengthen the following four aspects in our future work.

1. Unifying thoughts and changing concepts

Thoughts being in place is the premise and foundation for actions to be in place. Due to the long-standing situation where power supply enterprises operated under a seller's market condition of "supply replacing sales," the marketing concept lagged behind, and the mindset of "the emperor's daughter doesn't worry about finding a husband" was deeply ingrained. Now, the system has changed from "ministry" to "company," and the thoughts and concepts of our broad base of power employees must keep pace with the times. We must quickly establish market awareness, competitive awareness, and a sense of crisis, actively and promptly understanding new trends and issues in the power industry, strengthening benefit concepts, quality service concepts, innovative effectiveness concepts, and legal marketing concepts.

In our enterprise, we must fully leverage the role of the Communist Youth League organization. We must timely grasp the ideological dynamics of employees, guide them to study the guidelines and policies of power reforms, assist them in establishing the concept that "reform is the objective requirement of market economy and social development, and the only path for the survival and growth of power enterprises," enabling them to make improvements in their ideological awareness. Once the ideological work is done well and the concepts are updated, employees will actively cooperate.

2. Changing styles and providing enthusiastic services

In recent years, we have successively carried out activities such as commitments for high-quality power supply services, demonstration windows, and the "Golden Bridge Project" series, promoting improvements in work ethics, basically eliminating phenomena such as "eating, carding, taking, and demanding," and significantly improving the level of high-quality power supply services. However, we must also clearly see that there is still a gap between the construction of work ethics and the requirements of market development and customer expectations, with some unsatisfactory places. For example, the "official-business style" still exists, and the "bodies" of power employees are not sufficiently placed appropriately; although the system for standardized services is basically sound, the personnel lack the mindset of proactive service.

3. Broadening perspectives and strengthening publicity

The broadening of the power market requires broadened perspectives first. We must spare no effort to increase the sale of electricity and find new growth points for power consumption. Conducting activities such as "I offer one suggestion for the enterprise" among employees is not a bad method. Encourage everyone to speak freely in production, operation, and management. Modern enterprise publicity is necessary. We must not have the thought of "Who doesn't use electricity now, does it still need promotion?" We must increase the intensity of publicity and strengthen exchanges and communications with society. Make full use of media such as television, radio, and newspapers. For example, focus on promoting the advantages of electric energy: safety, economy, cleanliness, convenience, efficiency, and environmental protection.

4. Valuing science and technology and catching innovation

Scientific and technological innovation is the first productive force. Science and technology determine the future of power supply enterprises and their position in the market economy. Therefore, we must: actively use various means to promote the advancement of electricity usage technology and guide users to reasonably use electric energy; further enhance the current level of power supply services and technical means, providing users with comprehensive and efficient services; actively broaden the scope of power supply services and change the existing service models.

The new situation brings new circumstances and new problems to the work of the Youth League. The new century calls for innovation in youth ideological and political work. With the deepening of innovation and the continuous emergence of innovative results, the topics of innovation in the ideological and political work of the youth in power supply enterprises also increase. We must continue to explore innovation in the practice of youth ideological and political work, accurately grasp the work positioning of youth ideological and political workers serving the whole picture, serving enterprises, and serving the youth, to be adaptable to changes, standing at the forefront of the times at any time, playing the due functions, realizing the heavy entrustment of history, and striving for perfection in innovation in the process of exploration, to promote the healthy development of youth ideological and political work.
