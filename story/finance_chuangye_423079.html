Posted on April 30, 2011, 12:41 PM | Only see the author | Reverse browse | Print External links and internal links have always been the focus of webmasters, and search engine websites have their own judgment and punishment mechanisms for external and internal links. In order to better prevent being punished by search engine websites, friendly construction of external links is particularly important. How to build friendly external links? What are the channels and forms to obtain external links? What are the things that need attention? Let's first understand the basics. What are external links?

External links are also known as backlinks, which are exactly opposite to internal links. Simply put, they are some links from outside the website. All links from outside the website that point to our website can be collectively referred to as external links. From the perspective of search engine optimization, external links are understood as votes. The more external links you have, the more websites have voted for your website. Having more external votes is very beneficial for increasing the weight of a website. Of course, there are many things to pay attention to when building external links, which we will discuss below. Let's first look at the ways to increase external links.

Forms of Obtaining External Links

The forms of obtaining external links are diverse, and common ones include but are not limited to: friendship links, blog comments, soft article placement, question and answer platforms, directory listings, URL navigation, forum signatures, bookmark websites, link bait, paid links, plugin tools, etc. These forms do not necessarily have to be done all at once; you can choose the ones you are good at or possess resources and maximize them. Jinhua will write an article later to comprehensively introduce these forms. No matter which form you choose to build external links, please make sure to pay attention to the following points.

Matters to Pay Attention to When Building External Links

1. Pay attention to the naturalness of external links. Here, the naturalness refers to the anchor text (also known as website keywords) used in external links should be natural. Not all external links should use only one target website keyword as a link. Natural external link keywords should be varied and show diversity. For example, Jinhua's blog focuses on sharing SEO, SEM, and online marketing content, so when building external links, you can link related keywords such as SEO, Jinhua SEO, online marketing, SEM, SEO blog, Jinhua's SEO blog, etc. Such natural links have a lower chance of being identified as cheating by search engines, and diversified external link anchor texts may even not trigger the aversion of search engines. Of course, the following situations also need attention.

2. Pay attention to the relevance of external links. According to Google engineer Matt Cutts, irrelevant links are not the best links. For example, a bus website linking to an SEO website. Even if the bus website has extremely high weight, it won't help the SEO website much. In other words, such links are not high-quality external links, nor are they effective votes, because the bus website and SEO are not relevant. SEO websites need SEO-related backlinks, which are more effective. Therefore, when implementing external link construction, we must pay attention to this point of relevance. High-quality relevant external links are the best external links.

3. Pay attention to the breadth of external links. In addition to the naturalness and relevance mentioned above, we should also pay attention to the diversity of external links. The diversity here is similar to the naturalness of anchor texts, but the object here is the website. The types of websites, IP addresses should be diverse, for example, links can be made to blogs, corporate websites, CMS news websites, forums, etc., and can also be linked to websites relevant to your own website, such as Jinhua's blog can be linked to SEO, online marketing, SEM, etc. relevant websites, making the external links more extensive. It should be noted that websites under the same IP address should try not to link mutually, which may lead search engines to determine that all websites are operated by the same person, thus causing the result of being penalized.

4. Pay attention to the frequency of external links. The frequency of adding links varies depending on the website's situation. For example, new websites that have not yet been indexed by search engines should not create too many external links, just two or three to attract crawlers is enough. After the website is normally indexed (it is recommended to index more than 50 articles), then start building external links. The frequency of links is generally suitable for two or three per day, three to five are also okay, naturally increasing in a curved manner without regularity. It is essential to avoid adding 100 external links one day and none the next day, which is very likely to be penalized by search engines.

Unlike internal links, external links do not have controllability and cannot be fully deployed before the website is built. Through the above four points, we know that external links can actually be selected based on the specific situation of your own website. The continuity, diversity, and relevance of external link construction are what we should focus on. Does your website pay attention to these?
