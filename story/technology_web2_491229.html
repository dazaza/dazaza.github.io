I know, no matter where I go

by wenazjlove on 2011-09-18 20:14:22

There is always a leading role and a supporting role in the play of love. The leading role is always the one who gets tired, while the supporting role is always the one who gets hurt.

At the age of seventeen, romance is just an unreachable word that brings nothing but pain and sorrow.

I know that wherever I go, you will be by my side, crying with me, laughing with me, waiting with me, and blooming with me.

Love is like a sugar coating that I swallow whole, savoring the fleeting sweetness.

I know we will all part ways, and none of us will ever come back.

Love is a destined tide, and I am a little boat awaiting to dock, rising and falling with the tide, unable to escape its pull.

I don't have the courage to break my wings, yet I can't fly anywhere.

We live in the same warm waters, sometimes surrounded by aquatic plants, but because of our mutual warmth, I believe we will never be entangled in a fatal knot. If I say I love you, and I always have, would you believe me?

Do you only feel my devotion when I'm not around?

Love is a battle; I'm not afraid of getting hurt, I'm just afraid that you're not happy.

Listen quietly to the songs you love, and watch from afar the ones you care about.

No one stays seventeen forever, but there are always seventeen-year-olds.

Because I rely on memories that only I remember, I've grown up enough to bravely face all the storms of life.

We grow old this way, from one end of time to another. Please don't say goodbye, there's no need for it.

Money, position, beauty, and even love, they all flow away like water in the end.

The boy who might have changed my life without me noticing, I believe someday we'll meet again at the rooftop of the world.

Don't easily use the past to measure the happiness or misfortune of life. Everyone's life can blossom beautifully as long as you cherish it.

I've always liked the afternoon sun; it makes me believe that everything in this world has a turning point, and trust in the generosity and beauty of fate.

We must grow up, with a heart free of resentment, quietly growing up. After all, growing up is a kind of happiness.

Flattery, let it be heard by the left ear (Sweet talk is ready for the left ear)

Our love has been covered with dust, waiting for a storm to cleanse it.

Growing up is like this, painful yet joyful. You have to accept all the harm this world brings, then grow up fearlessly.

I hurry along, hoping to find a face similar to mine in the crowded and bustling crowd. She shares a similar destiny with me. I can see my own destiny reflected in her, giving me a clearer direction in life.

I don't have the moon. This moon belongs to many people, but not to me. Its warmth is beyond reach.

In fact, the structure of the character "ren" (person) is mutual support. As long as you're willing, anyone can bring happiness to another!

She won't disappear. What disappears is time. And the vanished time will make past wounds bloom into pure and grand flowers, standing in the simplest posture, becoming the best proof of our mutual warmth and existence.

We are all single-winged angels. Only by embracing each other can we fly.

The greatest pain is forgiveness, and the darkest black is betrayal.

A bit lonely, a bit painful, a bit ostentatious, a bit at a loss, a bit in need of comfort. So, click on it, and find a bit of beauty.

Loss, doesn't matter.

I dance on the tip of a blade, whether heavy or light, from the soles of my feet to my heart, the pain spreads inevitably.

Some people, some things, can you really forget them just because you want to?
