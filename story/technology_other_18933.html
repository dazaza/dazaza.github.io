The Pitiful Dad

There was a pregnant woman, but when she reached her 9th month...her husband couldn't resist anymore, so he forced his wife to do "the loving thing" with him. Then, after a month, the baby was born! It was a boy, and he was a genius! He could talk right after being born.

As soon as he turned his head and saw the doctor, he asked: "Are you my dad?"

The doctor said: "No...I'm the doctor."

Then he saw the nurse and asked: "Are you my dad?"

The nurse said: "No...I'm the nurse."

Finally, he saw his biological father and asked: "Are you my dad?"

His father happily said: "Yes! I'm your dad!"

As a result, the little boy got angry and poked his father's head with his finger...while cursing: "Does it hurt when I poke you? Does it hurt...?!!"

Later...his mother got pregnant again...when she reached her 9th month, the husband still couldn't resist...so...

This time, a girl was born, also a genius who could talk right after being born! Like her brother, she asked one by one who her dad was.

This time, the father had experience, so he lowered his head and stepped forward saying: "I...am your dad." As a result, the baby girl spat a mouthful of saliva onto her dad's face..."Pfft!" and angrily said: "Is it dirty like this? Is it dirty...?!?"
