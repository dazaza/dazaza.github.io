SEO optimization puts user experience as the top priority.

by seo17519 on 2012-02-20 16:48:15

The number of website external link optimization methods is countless, but if we talk about the simplest optimization method, it must be adding external links through "forum signatures." One post, one external link is its basic feature. If a large number of links are stably indexed, the weight they bring can be significant. However, in recent years, many webmasters have found that forum signature optimization is far less effective than before. So, how much value does forum external link optimization still hold? Binary analysis believes: the value of forum signature optimization has not decreased; it's just that search engines have stricter requirements. So how can we fully leverage these values and make search engines further recognize them?

1. Forum Weight Determines

Many webmasters have the habit of registering on forums, then bringing connections in their signatures, and posting or replying with signatures. But not all links can be indexed by search engines, nor can all indexed links bring weight to the website. Why is this so? If the forum's weight is insufficient, the more links you post, the greater the possibility of encountering a penalty. It is well known that search engines always follow a rule - eliminating external link phenomena. The fundamental reason is that the website publishing the links lacks sufficient weight, especially forum signature external links which are more likely to be removed. Therefore, webmasters should not blindly publish links. More often than not, they should not overlook the inherent weight of the forum itself, otherwise, it could lead to diminishing returns, and the more you post, the more severe the penalty might be. In the webmaster circle, SEO software, Binary Analysis recommends A5 Forum, Seowhy, Luowu, and Chinaz. These forums still have good weight.

2. Post Quality Determines

Although signatures may be the simplest way for external link optimization, you cannot just reply to posts to create external links. Blind optimization often leads to diminishing returns. To fully leverage the maximum value of forum signatures, first, you need to ensure the quality of the posts. How do you judge the quality of a post? First: Content freshness. An article that has been reprinted hundreds of times may not necessarily be indexed by search engines even if posted on a high-weight forum, let alone give much weight to the "signature." Second: Content quality. What quality can a post with only dozens of hastily written words have? Such posts should not be considered. Even if they are indexed, they will often be removed. If both points are fine, then the post quality is okay.

3. Post Floor Determines

There are quite a few people who see a post and immediately go to leave a link, but Binary Analysis believes this is really not worth it because some posts are simply not worth replying to. For example, a post that has been replied to dozens or even hundreds of times, what’s the point of replying again? Hoping that the signature will bring weight to the site? Generally speaking, in forum posts, the earlier the floor where the signature link is located, the better the effect. If you flip to the second page, it basically has no effect. Therefore, the floor of the post determines the quality of the link, and you should focus on replying to posts with fewer replies.

4. Whether Content is Indexed

If the post you are replying to has already been indexed by search engines, the weight obtained from replying will certainly not be as good as from an unindexed post, and may not gain any weight at all. But we can't possibly check each content one by one whether it has been indexed, right? The simplest method is to look at the posting time of the thread, find fresh posts to reply to, making it easier for the signature link to realize its value.

5. Section Quality

Even within a forum, the weight given by search engines to each section will definitely differ. For instance, in a webmaster forum, the "AD Flood" area and the "Soft Article Release" area will undoubtedly receive different weights for signature links. First, the quality of posts in the AD area may generally be low since they are mostly advertisements, with external links everywhere and content quality generally poor. On the other hand, the Soft Article Release area will be different. All posts have high-quality content, and search engines will give different weights to sections. At this time, even after replying to a forum floor post, the role of the signature will vary. Therefore, when using forum signature external links, if you want to maximize their use, you should also consider the quality of the forum section.

6. Forum Signature Techniques

Apart from the above five points, the last point we emphasize is about the design techniques of forum signatures. First: Signature hyperlinks vs. text links. I believe that if possible, try to use described text hyperlinks instead of plain text links. This not only provides a backlink for the site but also enhances the role of specific keywords. Second: If the forum supports it, try to add "special effects" to the links, such as bolding or changing colors. Third: Number of links. One link appearing once in the signature is enough. Constantly changing keywords for the same link has no effect. If you want to link to different pages of a website, search engines visiting the site will capture all the links and distribute the weight accordingly.
