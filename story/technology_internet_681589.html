**International Automotive Parts and Aftermarket Exhibition in Argentina**

**Exhibition Date:** November 14-17, 2012

**Exhibition Venue:** La Rural Exhibition Center, Buenos Aires, Argentina

**Exhibition Cycle:** Biennial (every two years)

**Scope of Exhibits:**

- **Components and Systems:**

Components and assemblies for vehicle drive, chassis, body, electrical equipment, and electronic systems; internal and external powertrain components, electronic control systems, and modules.

- **Accessories and Customization:**

Vehicle accessories, special equipment, customization systems, and performance systems.

- **Maintenance and Repairs:**

Vehicle repair services, repair equipment, bodywork repairs, painting services, and construction/management of repair stations.

- **Gas Stations and Car Washes:**

Gas station equipment, car maintenance services, and vehicle cleaning solutions.

**Exhibition Introduction:**

Automechanika Argentina is one of the largest and most influential automotive parts exhibitions in South America. This exhibition is a branch of the world-renowned Automechanika series, specifically held in Argentina. For global automotive parts suppliers, Automechanika Argentina serves as a highly competitive platform, while also providing manufacturers of service station equipment with an opportunity to showcase their products.

The Frankfurt Argentina International Auto Parts Exhibition is the largest and most professional auto parts exhibition in South America, attracting top players in the industry. The exhibition is held biennially, with the sixth edition taking place in 2010. Organized by Messe Frankfurt (South America), the 2010 edition featured 531 leading companies from 48 countries and regions, spread across 40,000 square meters of exhibition space. These companies showcased their high-quality products and excellent after-sales services, drawing the attention of 41,132 visitors. Of these exhibitors, 427 were from Argentina, while 104 came from other countries.

This exhibition has become a convergence point for one of the fastest-growing sectors of Argentina's economy. Both domestic and international automotive markets continue to grow in value. The Automechanika brand provides Tier 1, 2, and 3 suppliers in the automotive industry with excellent opportunities to conduct new business with local and global customers. Building on the success of previous editions and the resurgence of the automotive industry, interauto, the 2012 Frankfurt (Argentina) International Automotive Parts and Aftermarket Exhibition, will serve as a strategic starting point for opening new markets.

**Market Introduction:**

Argentina is a member of Mercosur and one of the fastest-growing economic zones in recent years. Despite global economic instability, its currency remains stable, and it has plans for investment projects in the coming months. Its development has expanded its participation in regions such as the United States, Mexico, Uruguay, and France. Between 1990 and 2000, Argentina invested $6.75 billion in the automotive industry, underscoring its importance not only in South America but globally. Currently, many of its spare parts manufacturers have achieved international certification standards and enjoy a strong reputation in the global market.
