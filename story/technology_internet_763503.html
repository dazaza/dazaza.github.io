Sina and Alibaba will announce account interoperability, deny launching e-commerce platform Yunmao

by anonymous on 2013-08-02 10:31:12

July 31 news, Sina and Alibaba will hold a press conference in Zhongguancun tomorrow morning and announce the interconnection of user accounts on both sides. This means that users can directly shop on Taobao using their Sina Weibo account in the future, and users with Taobao accounts can also directly post on Sina Weibo.

There are rumors that Sina will launch an e-commerce platform called Yunmao in August, aiming to organically integrate the media, social, and e-commerce aspects of Sina Weibo. The Yunmao platform is planned, developed, and laid out based on the core concepts of big data and cloud computing. In response, several internal employees of Sina Weibo denied this claim, emphasizing that it is merely account interoperability.

A person familiar with the cooperation between Sina and Alibaba told Tencent Technology:

Account interoperability is only the first step in the cooperation between Sina and Alibaba, and there are other collaborations being considered for the future. Many people see a lot of Taobao advertisements on Sina Weibo and assume that these measures were taken after Alibaba invested in Sina Weibo. This is actually a misunderstanding. "Since the beginning of this year, Alibaba has also placed many advertisements on Baidu. Does that mean Alibaba has invested in Baidu?"

In April this year, Alibaba purchased preferred and common shares issued by Sina Weibo for $586 million, accounting for approximately 18% of Weibo's fully diluted total shares. Alibaba also obtained the right to increase its stake in Sina Weibo to 30%. Many people are very interested in the next step of cooperation between Alibaba and Sina Weibo.

Previously, both Alibaba and Sina emphasized that they would conduct in-depth cooperation in areas such as user account interoperability, data exchange, online payment, and online marketing, and explore a social e-commerce model based on effective interaction between Weibo users and Alibaba consumers.
