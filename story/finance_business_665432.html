Misconception 1:

Experts remind: Be prepared for the first year of study abroad expenses.

Misconception 2:

Once admitted, there's no need to worry.

Experts remind: Falling behind in studies can easily lead to elimination.

Misconception 3:

No need to provide proof of study hours.

Experts remind: Studying French for 500 hours is the basic requirement.

Misconception 4:

No need for professional alignment between domestic and overseas studies.

Experts remind: Changing majors abruptly isn't easy.
