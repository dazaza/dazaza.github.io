Kingsoft: Baidu IM is malicious; users should be careful when downloading

by 127702 on 2008-02-29 15:26:18

Februrary 29th news from Tencent Technology:金山 has provided a message that users have reported that after downloading an IM instant messaging tool newly released by Baidu, their antivirus software immediately reported a virus and prompted that there was a virus present.

On the morning of February 29th, Mr. Huang heard about a new IM instant messaging tool launched by Baidu and wanted to download it for trial use online. Unexpectedly, just after the download finished, the antivirus software on Mr. Huang's computer immediately reported a virus. Although it turned out to be a false alarm, Mr. Huang was still very angry and couldn't figure out why a perfectly good software had become a virus.

According to Mr. Huang's description, he first searched for "baidu im" through Baidu and downloaded it, finding 624,000 related web pages. The journalist chose a link titled "Baidu IM Download XXX Premiere," clicked into it, and followed the prompts to download. The result was exactly the same as Mr. Huang's description.

Kingsoft Antivirus Engineer Li Tiejun stated, "Similar virus incidents are very common. Trojan viruses often disguise themselves using social hot events to deceive users into downloading them for propagation, thereby stealing private information from users' computers. Such as the previous 'Lust Caution' virus and the 'Exposed Photos Scandal' virus."

Kingsoft Antivirus engineers analyzed the virus disguised as Baidu IM and found that the virus was named "Delf Trojan Downloader." After being implanted with this Trojan, users can download more other Trojans via the network, especially some account-stealing Trojans, posing a significant threat to users' online property security.

Li Tiejun said that this virus can be removed using Kingsoft Antivirus. At the same time, Kingsoft Antivirus engineers reminded users to install genuine antivirus software (www.duba.net), and before downloading files via the network, they must enable real-time monitoring and firewall functions; at the same time, develop good internet habits, and when downloading software, try to choose large download websites and avoid logging into unknown small websites.
