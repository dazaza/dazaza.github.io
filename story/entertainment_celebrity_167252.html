Michael Jackson

Michael; Joseph; Michael Joseph Jackson (August 29, 1958 - June 26, 2009) has always been a name with a lot of meaning, and his life is full of changes. Singer, dancer, creative genius; The King of Pop, the legendary master of the stage. Yes, Jackson represented all that and more. He interprets this complex and rich subject with his life. As the embodiment of popular culture for a century, his life was unparalleled. Michael Jackson, born in Gary, Indiana, USA, is an American popular singer, songwriter, dancer and record producer. Jackson began his musical career as the lead singer of the Jackson 5 in the 1960s and 1970s, and began a solo career in 1979, going on to become the most successful African-American singer in pop music history and one of the most successful pop singers in the world. He is known as the "King of Pop" or the "King of Music Videos". Jackson's successful singing career earned him the support and affection of many fans around the world, including his songs and trademark dance moves. The media, however, were more interested in his changing skin tone and eccentric lifestyle. Jackson won many music awards, including 18 Grammys. He has had 13 number one singles in the United States and has sold an estimated 170 million records worldwide. His Thriller album is the world's best-selling album of all time, selling nearly 60 million copies worldwide, and is one of the best-selling original albums in the United States.
