Special House Xiang'an Three Discs Enjoy 9.7% Discount, Activity Ends on the 18th. In celebration of the 2012 Spring Festival, Special House Group presents a special offer to Xiamen: currently three projects in Xiang'an - Lian'an Town, Meidi Yaden, and Jinxiu Xiang'an - are offering a 9.7% discount on property purchases, with the promotion ending on the 18th of this month.

It is reported that Lian'an Town is the monumental masterpiece by Special House Group in Xiang'an. The new products from Block Two, Building No.1, ranging from 38 to 87 square meters, will be launched in March. Following this, shops with areas between 30 to 200 square meters may also be introduced. Currently, buyers can apply for the "Happiness Passport" to enjoy more discounts during the opening sale.

Lian'an Town Effect Diagram

Special House Meidi Yaden's Phase III includes nine buildings from No.4 to No.12, out of which eight are currently on sale, with an average price of about 7,300 yuan per square meter. Additionally, the 12 garden-style townhouses are being heavily promoted, averaging around 8,200 yuan per square meter. Phase IV has already been capped and is expected to be launched in the first half of the year.

The sales of Phase I and II of the Special House Jinxiu Xiang'an project are nearing completion, with only a few premium garden-style townhouse units remaining. Phases III and IV, Buildings No.41 to No.49, are under construction and are expected to be launched within the year.

[Special House Lian'an Town]

Special House Lian'an Town is another monumental work by the state-owned enterprise Special House Group in Xiang'an District following Meidi Yaden and Jinxiu Xiang'an in 2011. Located in the mature Mawan area, Xiang'an No.1 Middle School, Mawan Middle School, and Mawan Central Primary School are right at your doorstep. Nearby are shopping complexes like Xin Hua Du, Da Qian Department Store, Mawan Commercial Street, Xiamen First Hospital Tongmin Branch, various banks, post offices, and more, providing excellent educational, commercial, medical, and other living facilities.

The Lian'an Town project is divided into four plots: A, B, C, and D, with a community scale of 400,000 square meters, including nearly 50,000 square meters of self-contained commercial space, making it a truly large community and a perfect place for young people to live.
