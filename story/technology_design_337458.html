Love, marriage and house prices are continuing between the game, in the face of high house prices, young couples' nerves are tested. Prices are changing young people's love and values; for this social phenomenon, Shenzhen sociological expert Dr. Dai Xinming considered it to be "a socially-oriented result of serious deviations." In his view, the entire community now has rendered a "sky can Diaoxian Bing" bad atmosphere. Indeed, the skyrocketing stock and property markets have made many people feel that speculating in stocks does not require going to work to develop wealth. For the younger generation left in the community, this rendering no doubt causes some distortion of society. "You can see in films and TV series that the atmosphere of love is too idealistic. True love is more about loving the common population," said Dai Yan Ming. "Take for instance housing prices in Shenzhen, it is simply unaffordable for young people, even Dongguan's home prices are the same now. For young people's ideals, beliefs, and values, we should guide them to have ideals and ambition, not to be too materialistic. Under the current circumstances, young people should be more realistic, more down-to-earth in doing these things, so that their lives become more meaningful."

Dongguan Heng psychological edge Counseling Center sociologist He Xiaoyu told reporters that from graduating from college to 28 years old, this time is for young people to explore career direction, industry interest, and is the period of time when young people should focus on accumulating ability, experience, and contacts. Do not be envious of those of the same age who have a house, for the sake of face or family pressure to buy a house. In Chinese tradition, the mark of a young person being settled in the city is whether they have a home in the city. After home ownership, young people often discuss with their beloved girlfriends when to get married. Regarding this, He Xiaoyu said: "Love, marriage, and housing are things for two people to discuss; putting all the burden on one person’s head is very unfair. The external pressure brought by house prices is an indisputable fact, and this external pressure mainly comes from one's own ideas. If two people share common goals, support each other, and communicate with each other, this pressure can be resolved."
