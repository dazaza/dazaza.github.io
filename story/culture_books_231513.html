"The City Procurator" - A Storm of Anti-Corruption in the Officialdom

by jqrxass on 2009-09-01 10:45:27

The city of Donghai was hit by a big storm, during which the fishing boat Donghaiyun No. 652 sank. Zhuang Haisheng perished in this storm. When his wife was searching for his body, she discovered another corpse - Deputy Procurator of the Donghai City People's Procuratorate, Li Zhihua. The newly appointed Procurator of the Donghai City Procuratorate, Ye Dasheng, began an investigation and found a piece of text on Li Zhihua's computer that led to a cold case from two years ago.

A taxi driver who had some knowledge of the situation surfaced, but before he could reveal the truth, he died under mysterious circumstances...

While looking for the taxi driver, investigators unexpectedly learned that a bank card had been lost in the taxi Li Zhihua had taken. In order to track down the owner of the bank card, they conducted a roundabout search and disguised themselves for reconnaissance. They surprisingly found out that the true cardholder was actually the handler of the cold case from two years ago - the captain of the Criminal Investigation Team of the Public Security Bureau. It turned out that the party involved in the cold case from two years ago met with a fatal accident due to an unexpected encounter with someone!

Power and money, profit and justice, the battle between righteousness and evil unfolded layer by layer...
