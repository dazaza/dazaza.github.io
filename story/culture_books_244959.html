"The first person of Chinese family marriage novels," famous best-selling author and screenwriter Hai-ling Wang's latest cross-generational emotional drama "Companionship," tells a story of a unique, supportive love and marriage that developed during an extraordinary time. Integrating war, love, and family affection, the drama portrays the tumultuous life of fast-paced book artist Tianpeng Gong (played by National Zhang) and his marriage that spans half a century, narrating the life journey of a master who rose and fell with the fast-paced book art form, expressing the sighs and helplessness of individual fate being pulled and restricted by a great era... The same-name TV series is currently airing on prime-time channels, starring famous actors National Zhang and Dan Dan Song!
