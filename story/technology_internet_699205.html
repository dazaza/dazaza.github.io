The 2012 jewelry design is themed on nature and advocates the natural beauty

by szys885561 on 2012-03-09 13:50:15

Source: Chuanxi Jewelry's Old Silver Shop [http://www.wuyuelaoyinpu.com/cooperation.asp] Silver Jewelry Franchise

[Chuanxi Jewelry] launched its 2012 High-End Custom Series last week. A series of jewelry themed around nature has started to hit the market. These designs incorporate auspicious animal patterns such as peacocks, dragonflies, phoenixes, and dragonflies into this batch of jewelry designs, highlighting the natural beauty traits of women.

Last week, the jewelry exhibited at Place Vendôme revealed the following fashion trends:

1. Spinel replacing high-priced rubies

2. Rare colors and unique textures of sapphires

3. Various brightly colored gemstones

4. Mixed-match earrings, mostly featuring contrasting colors and unique pattern designs

As a leader in China's silver jewelry trends, Chuanxi Jewelry will surely keep up with the latest trends. Designer Dex stated that we will also launch a few new silver jewelry pieces in March with the theme of [Fashionable Women, Admiring Nature]. This new collection mainly aims to return to the original charm of the Chuanxi brand - simple but not simplistic, pursuing the concept of returning to one's true self. The designs feature birds, flowers, and vines, giving people the feeling of being immersed in nature.

The sales director of Chuanxi also expressed: This season's new products are absolutely worth everyone's anticipation. After the new products are launched, we will also hold a new product launch event under our two brands, Old Silver Shop and Chuanxi Silver Workshop. At that time, it will definitely give everyone a refreshing feeling.
