Our shop's purple clay teapots:

1 - All are made by the artisans, assistant artisans, or craft workers themselves. The artisans who make teapots for us are all true craftsmen, not commercial speculators. Their workmanship is very meticulous, and they use high-quality clay, paying great attention to their market reputation. The authors who supply us with teapots are definitely our relatives or old friends (or have relatives as guarantors), and we both know each other well.

2 - Our shop is particularly particular about the clay material and strictly controls the quality of the clay material. There is absolutely no addition of any industrial pigments in our purple clay materials, all of which are natural purple clay materials. We do not substitute inferior materials for better ones. We do not perform any artificial "beautification" on the surface of the pot. Our store has top-grade clay teapots: Tianxing clay, pear skin Zhu clay, first-class Di Cao Qing, Jiazi year old purple clay, Ben Shan Green, top-grade Qing Hui clay, first-class Jiangpo clay, old Duan clay, etc.

3 - Every purple clay teapot in the store is a carefully selected fine product personally chosen by our main shop owner in Yixing. We do not make or sell poor quality pots at all.

4 - Also place old pots and antique pots collected from the folk in Yixing in the store for collectors to appreciate and identify. Our store also has precious-level pots in collection: legendary reverse-threaded pots, natural kiln change pots, Qing Dynasty old pots, Dahongpao pots.

5 - The shop owner will impart his knowledge and experience to customers who are new to the art of purple clay teapots, making sincere friends with customers. Free appraisal of purple clay teapots for customers, free transmission of the secrets of purple clay teapots. We also customize your favorite style for old customers and engrave the words and pictures you need. Our main store has also specially prepared some "teaching pots", primary ore clay, and ores for everyone to learn.

The store supports UnionPay card, credit card swiping for shopping.
