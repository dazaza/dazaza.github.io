The Foxtail Grass Is Crying Secretly

When Yan Lu called me, I was reading "Boys and Girls"! I admit that there is a purpose for reading such magazines, but I was still shocked.

Yan Lu said he had said goodbye to his girlfriend and was feeling troubled, so he found me. I was a little angry at the guy, as he usually ignored me when there was nothing going on. I said let's go out and clear our minds.

We found a drink shop called "Meng Chaye", which is my favorite place because it's quiet like a romantic coffee shop. I thought it would suit Yan Lu. Although I have never been in love, I have a certain persistence, that is, if you are hurt, you should forget.

Yan Lu started talking about his romantic love history. He said he always insisted on sending her a text message to wake her up in the morning; he always got used to buying her fried dough sticks and milk and waiting for her downstairs; he could always show up when she needed him. When crossing the street, he always walked on her right side; when surfing the internet, he always found some idol dramas she liked to watch together. When she was going to participate in a dance competition, he would take notes for her; when she wanted to eat old rice porridge from the east side of the city, he would take several buses to buy it for her... As he was talking, Yan Lu drank a lot of apple vinegar, because there was no alcohol here, and this was my reason.

I couldn't find any words to comfort Yan Lu, so I just listened silently to Asang's "Always Quiet". I know that a young boy who has never tasted the flavor of love won't deeply understand heartbreak.

When Asang sang "My love for you has always been quiet...", Yan Lu sighed and continued talking. I really didn't do this with any impure purpose. I like her, I am willing to do anything for her. But how could she misunderstand me and date other boys.

When he said this, Yan Lu drank 50 milliliters of apple vinegar in one breath. Then, he left.

I think I am not suitable to be anyone's listener, especially not Yan Lu's. From beginning to end, I didn't say a word, silent to the point that I thought Yan Lu was talking to a utility pole. Until Yan Lu left, until Yan Lu called me on the road. Through the line, I clearly heard the voice of Yan Lu's heart turning to ashes. He said, she said if I could find ten thousand foxtail grasses, she would agree to be my official girlfriend. I remained silent, believing that he would do it.

After leaving "Meng Chaye", my mood was somewhat affected by Yan Lu, as if it was covered with something. Fortunately, the summer breeze was refreshing, making the way back not too long.

When I received Yan Lu's call again, I had almost forgotten about that matter. I am like this, unable to remember others' sad stories. On the other end of the phone, I clearly heard his trembling voice mixed with a bit of excitement.

Yan Lu said, Xiaode, I finally gathered ten thousand foxtail grasses. I'm going to find her and tell her that I am willing to cherish her for a lifetime. These are nice words, but I don't like them that much. However, when Yan Lu appeared in front of me, I suddenly felt that those words sounded so pleasant.

The Yan Lu in front of me had changed, I didn't recognize him, if it wasn't for his hoarse call of my name. His eyes were full of desires I couldn't understand. It was only a few days since we last saw each other.

I saw that girl, not the kind I like, ordinary to the point that there are plenty if you grab randomly. She was holding a boy's hand while shopping, our appearance disrupted their original warmth. If it were usual, I would definitely not destroy the beautiful dating time of this couple.

Hey, I finally did it, I found the foxtail grass you wanted for you, will you come with me? Yan Lu said this gently, his voice nearly pleading.

Then, Lala kindly said, Yan Lu, give up, we won't be together. After saying that, she tightened her grip on the person beside her.

Yan Lu wouldn't give up, he forcibly grabbed Lala's hand, crazily pulling, muttering to himself.

In the end, that guy hit Yan Lu's right cheek. With the dim yellow street light, I saw fresh red blood oozing from the corner of his mouth. I pulled the guy from behind, and Yan Lu took the opportunity to punch the guy's stomach. Unfortunately, the guy's muscles weren't fake. He easily broke free from me, and with one punch at Yan Lu's stomach, then another, two punches... When I struggled to get up, Yan Lu was already lying on the ground curled up. I was dumbfounded at the moment, and when I looked around, Lala and the guy had run far away. I didn't care anymore, and sent Yan Lu to the hospital.

The development of events should have been Lala accepting Yan Lu's foxtail grass, and then they held hands like other couples. I thought. Unexpectedly, Yan Lu ended up injured.

Since there was no serious harm, Yan Lu was discharged after a few days. He told me, Xiaode, I've decided to forget her and start a new life.

I know this is a very difficult thing, but I still patted his shoulder and said, good job.

A few months later, Yan Lu left here and returned to the city originally belonging to him. We are still in contact, and he is no longer the reckless boy he was back then.

I think, love always makes people grow up.

Sometimes I also think about the scene I saw in the hospital. Yan Lu held Lala's photo, constantly stroking it, and said, I will leave, as long as you are truly happy. Then I saw a boy who was tolerant for love quietly wiping away the tears about to fall.

Suddenly, I remembered someone saying: If the foxtail grass is crying secretly, then he is truly wronged.
