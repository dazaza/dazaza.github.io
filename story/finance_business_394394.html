Part One: Preparatory Work Before Opening an Online Store

After choosing what to sell, before opening an online store, you need to select a website that provides a personal shop platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, you also need to undergo corresponding identity and payment method verification. Choosing to open a store on Taobao is completely free. It needs to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass certification; two, publish 10 or more (including 10) items; three, to facilitate safe transactions, activate online banking.

1. User Registration Log into Taobao and click on "Free Registration" at the top of the page. On the opened page, input your username, password, email address, etc. (You can apply for an email address for free on large websites such as Yahoo, Sina, Sohu, NetEase, etc.) Click on the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the registered email will receive a confirmation message. Open the link inside and confirm it to complete user registration.

Security Tip: To ensure the safety of transactions, note that passwords should not be set too simply. It is recommended to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols" as your password.

2. Identity Verification

Taobao stipulates that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, you also need to undergo corresponding verification (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification). The specific steps are as follows:

Step 1: Log into Taobao, click on "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click on "Want to sell items? Please undergo Alipay verification" text beside "Please click here."

Step 2: In the opened page, it will prompt that the Alipay account has not been activated yet. Click on "Click here to activate the Alipay account." In the pop-up page, input your real name, ID type and number, Alipay password, etc., and click on the "Save and immediately activate the Alipay account" button.

Step 3: After successfully activating the Alipay account, return to the original page and refresh it by pressing F5. Click on the "Apply for Alipay personal real-name verification" button, read the Alipay verification service terms, and then click on the "I have read" button to continue.

Step 4: First, fill out personal information according to the prompts, click on "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID, click on "Next"; then, input bank card information, including the bank where the account was opened, bank card number, province, city, etc. After completing the input, wait for Alipay to transfer funds within one day.

Friendly Tip: If you choose to upload copies of your ID online, the size of the image file should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID, you also need to provide a backside picture.

Step 5: After one day, reopen "My Taobao," click on the corresponding link in the certification area to open the "Alipay Certification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click on "Confirm Transfer Amount," then input the amount of funds injected by Alipay into your bank account, and click on the "Confirm" button.

3. Procurement and Photo Taking (if you're an agent, you don't need to do this)

A key factor in the successful operation of an online store lies in the procurement channel. For the same product, different procurement channels mean different prices.

After passing identity verification, you need to organize the items you already have. To display the items for sale more intuitively to consumers, taking pictures is crucial, and it's best to use corresponding graphic/image processing tools for format and size conversion, such as Photoshop, Magic Photo Editor, etc.

4. Publish Items

To open a store on Taobao, besides meeting the certification member conditions, you also need to publish over 10 items. Thus, after organizing product information and pictures, you need to start publishing your first item.

Friendly Tip: If you haven't passed personal real-name certification and Alipay certification, you can publish items, but the items can only be published in "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing certification can you put them on the shelf for sale.

Step 1: Log into Taobao, click on "I Want to Sell" at the top of the page. In the opened page, you can choose between "Fixed Price" or "Auction" publishing methods. Here, click on "Fixed Price."

Friendly Tip: "Fixed Price" has a fixed price, and buyers can purchase immediately; "Auction" starts with no bottom price, allowing buyers to bid for purchase.

Step 2: Choose a category based on your item. For example, I chose women's shoes item details. Click on the "Selected, Continue" button to proceed to the next step.

Step 3: Fill in item information, which is very important. First, in the "Item Information" area, choose a good title, click on the "Browse" button to upload the item picture, input item description information, quantity, start time, validity period, etc.; then, in the "Transaction Conditions" area, input the item's selling price, location, shipping fee, payment method, etc.; other information can remain default settings, such as default use of Alipay payments, etc. Finally, click on the "Confirmed No Errors, Submit" button to publish the item.

If the publication is successful, a success page will appear below. Clicking "Here" allows you to view the published item page, and clicking "Continue Publishing Items" allows you to continue publishing items.

Friendly Tip: If the buyer hasn't placed a bid yet and you want to modify the published item information, you can edit and modify it under "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Items for Sale."

After the item is published, it's best to update and add regularly to avoid the store being deleted by the system.

Two: Acquire Free Stores

Taobao offers free store-opening opportunities for certified members. As long as you publish more than 10 items, you can own a store of your own with an independent URL. On this webpage, you can place all your items and decorate it according to your style.

To let your store open soon, at least publish 10 items. Next, in the opened page, name your store and input a brief introduction in the "Store Introduction." Finally, click on the "Submit" button. In the appeared page, it shows "Congratulations! Your store has been successfully created..." and provides the store's address.

Three: Shop Decoration is Very Important

After free store opening, the buyer gets a space of their own. Just like traditional shops, to operate normally and attract customers, you need to decorate the store appropriately, mainly including store logo design, item classification, recommended items, store style, etc.

1. Basic Settings

Log into Taobao, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd., open "My Taobao - I Am Seller - Manage My Store." In the left-hand "Store Management," click on "Basic Settings." In the opened page, you can modify the store name, store category, store introduction; manually input main business projects; in the "Logo" area, click on the "Browse" button to select the already designed logo picture; in the "Announcement" area, input the store announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!", and click on the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Classification

Classifying items helps buyers find them easily. In the opened "Manage My Store" page, you can click on "Item Classification" on the left side. Then, input the new classification name, such as "Four Treasures of the Study," and input the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), click on the "Confirm" button to add. Click on the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and through keyword search, add the published items for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" function provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best 6 items, showcasing them prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Store" page, click on "Recommended Items" on the left side; then, in the opened page, select the recommended items and click on the "Recommend" button.

4. Store Style

Different store styles suit different items, giving buyers different feelings. Generally, choosing a light-colored, comfortable-looking style is sufficient. I chose the "Green Wild Fairy Tale" style template, which displays a preview picture on the right side. Clicking on the "Confirm" button applies this style. After decorating the store, a refreshed page appears.

Four: Promotion is the Key to Success

Your online store is open, the items are on the shelves, and you've established your uniqueness, but weeks have passed without any sales or even buyer messages—this is a common problem encountered by many beginner sellers. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such troubles, so I decided to promote my store through forum promotion, link exchange, window recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and utilizing signature lines.

The former can be done on forums of various provinces or cities. If there is a section that allows advertisement posts, you can post advertisement threads, ensuring detailed content and exquisite product pictures while maintaining regular updates and keeping them pinned. You can choose to promote in the Taobao community, well-known forums, local lifestyle communities, etc.

The latter involves changing your signature line on forums to include your store URL, logo, promotional phrases, and store name. Post some exquisite threads so interested friends can visit your store through your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, thus advertising your store for free.

First, use Taobao's search function to search for all stores, record the names of popular store owners.

Then, log into and download Taobao's buyer-seller exchange tool—"Taobao Wangwang," add the names of these popular store owners, and propose a link exchange request.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao—I Am Seller—Manage My Store," click on "Friendly Links" on the left side, then input the store owner's name, and click on the "Add" button.

3. Window Recommendation

The "Window Recommendation" function provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers choose to search or click "I Want to Buy" and search by category, the window-recommended items will appear on the search result pages. To set the "Window Recommendation" function, open "My Taobao—I Am Seller—Items for Sale," select the items to be recommended to the window (items already recommended to the store homepage cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., those marked with "Recommended"), and click on the "Window Recommendation" button.

Five: After Selling Items

After selling items, besides receiving corresponding reminders, you need to actively contact buyers, request payment, ship the items, and conduct evaluations or complaints after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller through "Taobao Wangwang" or email. Sellers can also log into Taobao, open "My Taobao—I Am Seller—Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" area, click on "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer doesn't use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also record the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After contacting each other, the buyer and seller agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both parties, it's suggested to use Alipay as the payment method.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where goods arrive but payment is not made, sellers generally adopt the "payment received before shipping" method.

First, request the buyer to make the payment, usually through Alipay. After the payment is made, the seller can open "My Taobao—I Am Seller—Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it means Alipay has received the payment, and the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: For sellers using Alipay transactions, you can open "My Taobao—Alipay Zone—Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction inquiries, refund management, etc.

After the buyer receives the item, they confirm it in the transaction status, and Taobao will then transfer the payment to the seller's Alipay account. This completes the transaction. Don't forget to maintain contact with the buyer to increase the chances of them revisiting your store and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both the buyer and seller can open "My Taobao" to evaluate. The seller can open "My Taobao—I Am Seller—Sold Items," click on "Evaluate" for sold items, and choose positive, neutral, or negative reviews based on actual circumstances, with the option to input textual content.

Friendly Tip: How to become a relatively high-credibility star store owner on Taobao?

To become a star store owner on Taobao, the credit rating must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction—if it's a "positive review," add one point; "neutral review" does not add points; "negative review" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star store owner, remember to provide honest service.

If situations such as online transactions without purchase or receipt of goods without payment occur, the seller can open "My Taobao—Credit Management—I Want to Report" to file complaints and reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, and Wangwang screenshots must be collected.

Although online store opening is zero-cost and low-risk, examples of failing to make a single sale and then closing down abound. To let your store survive online, the most important thing is "integrity." Only integrity can win the hearts of buyers and gain good credit ratings, allowing you to develop!
