Introduction to free online marketing methods and their online resources, are you ready?

by caihua800 on 2010-04-10 16:28:19

Free online promotion, Beijing website optimization, is a topic that many enterprises and webmasters are concerned about. You may question whether free online promotion is effective. Here, I would like to tell everyone that it definitely has an effect. The effectiveness of online promotion is closely related to the professional level, attentiveness, and persistence of the implementer. Next, I will briefly introduce some commonly used free website promotion methods.

Firstly, search engine promotion: Currently, mainstream search engines (Baidu, Google, Yahoo, Sogou, Sou, Bing...) all provide free web page indexing and retrieval services. This is also what every website optimization company does before the website is indexed.

Secondly, blog marketing: Establish some corporate blogs, encourage employees to write professional knowledge introductions about the company's products and corporate culture, etc. This is also a good way to attract customers. Optimization companies often use these blogs to promote websites.

Thirdly, classified directories: Submit your website to URL navigation sites, Beijing online marketing industry information network, and other free resources, which can also achieve certain effects of free online promotion.

Fourthly, post supply and demand information on large B2B business websites and industry portals, which has a pretty good effect.

Fifthly, soft article marketing: Regularly write valuable articles, mention recommendations for your company's products intentionally or unintentionally, and publish them on forum websites where potential customers are mixed.

Sixthly, EMAIL marketing: EMAIL is not just for sending and receiving emails. You can regularly provide the latest product information, discount information, or even just greetings. However, the prerequisite is to obtain the customer's permission and avoid disturbing the public, Beijing online promotion.

Seventhly, frequently visit popular communities such as Baidu Zhidao, Baidu Baike, Yahoo Knowledge Hall, Soso Wenwen, answer professional questions in the industry that you are good at, and recommend your products and websites accordingly.

In addition, there is promotion through chat tools like QQ and MSN.

In fact, there are many more good promotion methods, and these are the ones I often use, with pretty good results, website optimization. For now, I will say so much.
