Sanates and Ye Yin knew each other thousands of years ago, but they couldn't be together. "The Beast Consort" Author: Zhou Yu Type: Fictional History Main Characters: Ding Ding, later renamed Yun Qing (Female Protagonist) Dugu Jue (Male Protagonist) (Others) Dugu Xing, Qi Zhiqian, Chu Xingtian, Huayang Empress, Ding Feiqing, Sheng Tianyu, Fei Lin

Content Introduction (to be honest, this isn't much of an introduction): Using sound as a sword, using music as killing. Controlling ten thousand beasts, reigning over the world. However, it doesn't just attract animals, but also "human beasts". The ferocity of wolves, the speed of leopards, the majesty of tigers, the cunning of foxes. Such men, such world-dominating men. Are they destined tribulations or destinations? Continuous warfare, fallen beauties. You have imperial power, I have ten thousand beasts at my command. Let's see who controls the rise and fall of the world. When they first met, she was bathing in a stream, and her smooth white body was fully exposed to him. A violent and enchanting man, his eyes like a demon when open, like a fiend when closed. He tightly gripped her jaw, and his dominant lips harshly kissed her pale red ones. Their skin was pressed together through thin clothing, thus being heatedly confined together. He made her swallow the deadliest poison in the world, lowering his charming gaze and throwing down: "If you die, I will too!" And she, merely returned his bloodthirsty coldness with pride, lightly spitting out: "I don't like you!" Cool as the wind, elegant as water, challenging this world-dominating man, tying an unbreakable bond.

Personal thoughts: It's really good! At first, I didn't think it was very interesting because the author introduced the main character as a small figure, and I thought her sister Ding Feiqing was the protagonist, but it wasn't. After reading a little bit more, it became very interesting. If you're reading, don't be deceived by the beginning. Originally, I thought the start was boring and not appealing, but luckily a classmate told me it got better, so I patiently continued reading. After reading a bit more, I found it was incredibly captivating. I admire the protagonist, and the relationship between the male and female protagonists is very loving. There are many touching parts in the book. [The female protagonist uses sound attacks and can control ten thousand beasts.]

"Stealing Passion" Author: Zhou Yu Type: Gangster Novel (very exciting)

Characters: (Female Protagonist) Mu Lixin (Male Protagonist) Qi Mo

Content Introduction: She is one of the most renowned thieves of the 21st century and a legendary car king in the black market. She is a woman as swift as the wind, coming and going freely, enjoying life to the fullest. He is one of the top tycoons in the underworld, ruthless and cruel. Unexpectedly, someone dared to challenge him, stealing his property. Since someone wanted to test their luck, he would take care of them. Is it love, passion, hatred, or injury? Everything is shrouded in mystery. This gangster novel features intense battles, grand scenes, bloody and dark plots. If you only want to read about emotional entanglements, don't come in. Here, we show the dark side of the entire world, and the male protagonist is absolutely not a good person!

Personal feelings: Extremely captivating, a bit humorous. I sighed deeply, and the male protagonist is absolutely cold... Written by the same author as "The Beast Consort," but personally, I feel "The Beast Consort" is slightly better because I think the ending here is too simple, and adding more details would make it even better. The female protagonist is a master thief, and the male protagonist is the head of a criminal organization.
