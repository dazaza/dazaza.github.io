Drug dealers disguised as pharmaceutical companies cheat to buy asthma medicine for drug manufacture

by j0371zxy on 2009-12-08 16:00:54

The "4?08" drug case investigation team, after following up on credible leads and subsequent failures, discovered that illegal elements had used the legitimate credentials of the Nanxi County Pharmaceutical Company illegally and fraudulently purchased or obtained ephedrine-containing compound formulations for use in manufacturing drugs.

"Despite China's continuous reinforcement of its control over precursor chemicals for drug production, due to external factors, the flow of precursor chemicals and ephedrine-containing compound formulations through pharmaceutical channels remains a serious concern. There is a trend towards more severe loss, an increasing variety of substances, and higher levels of organization," said Zhao Yu, Director of the National Narcotics Control Commission Office and Director of the Ministry of Public Security's Anti-Drug Bureau.

According to regulations, when pharmaceutical production and retail enterprises operate ephedrine-containing compound formulations, they must establish customer records, strictly verify and retain relevant qualification certificates, issue invoices as required, prohibit cash transactions, and ensure that medicines are delivered to the purchaser's warehouse. When retailing ephedrine-containing compound formulations, prescription drugs must strictly adhere to the management regulations for prescription and non-prescription drugs. Non-prescription drugs should not be sold in quantities exceeding five maximum packages per transaction.

The "4?08" drug precursor case, jointly cracked by the National Narcotics Control Commission Office and the National Food and Drug Administration, was the largest in scope, with the most suspects arrested and the highest quantity of seized drugs. The case resulted in the seizure of 44 tons of ephedrine-containing compound formulations, 415 kilograms of ephedrine, 955 grams of methamphetamine, and illicit funds amounting to 18.209 million yuan.

Shockingly, illegal entities were found to have fraudulently purchased ephedrine-containing compound formulations from pharmaceutical companies for use in drug production. Experts pointed out that the flow of ephedrine-containing compound formulations through pharmaceutical channels poses a severe problem.

"For enterprises violating regulations, there must be severe penalties," emphasized Mao Zhenbin. The National Food and Drug Administration has been guiding enterprises to comply with legal operations while warning and strictly investigating illegal activities. For enterprises found violating regulations during inspections, strict actions will be taken according to Article 79 of the "Drug Administration Law." Enterprises directly causing the flow of ephedrine-containing compound formulations into illegal channels will have their "Drug Production License" or "Drug Operation License" revoked. Cases suspected of criminal offenses will be promptly handed over to public security authorities for handling.

With stricter supervision, it has become increasingly difficult for illegal entities to obtain pharmaceutical precursors. Consequently, foreign illegal elements began seeking unregulated cold medications containing ephedrine as substitutes. For instance, the prescription drug Contac contains ephedrine hydrochloride, which criminals have fraudulently purchased in large quantities or bought at low prices on the black market to extract ephedrine for drug production.

The Yibin Food and Drug Administration in Sichuan received a verification letter from the Jilin City Food and Drug Administration in March, requesting confirmation if the Nanxi County Pharmaceutical Company had purchased 197 boxes of Juan Kechuan from Jilin Weikang Pharmaceutical Company and whether the drugs had reached the company.

"The '4?08' drug precursor case highlights the importance of strictly implementing all supervisory measures," Mao Zhenzhu told reporters. Sometimes it’s not about having no laws to follow but rather failing to enforce existing regulations strictly. The Jilin City Food and Drug Administration identified credible issues during routine inspections and issued timely verification notifications.

"Ephedrine extraction from compound cold medications for drug production indeed poses a challenge to related pharmaceutical supervision," said Mao Zhenbin.

Cold medications have been used as substitutes for methamphetamine production.

"This is a new tactic by criminals, with greater concealment, harder to combat, and more harmful," said Mao Zhenbin of the National Food and Drug Administration.

Ephedrine is a primary ingredient in methamphetamine production. In China, ephedrine is classified as a pharmaceutical precursor chemical under strict supervision by food and drug administration, public security, and commerce departments. For example, the production and operation of pharmaceutical precursor chemicals require approval from the drug regulatory department; these compounds must be sold by designated narcotic drug operating enterprises and cannot be retailed.

The issue lies not in the absence of laws but in the failure to enforce existing regulations strictly.
