Summary

The Birth of the Black Warrior

Mai Ti Shuang has always possessed a sharp business mind since childhood. He understands the truth that only through business can one achieve success. Thus, he diligently studies and earns his MBA from Stanford University in the U.S., seeking opportunities abroad.

After graduation, Shuang sees potential in He Wentian's company and proactively approaches him, offering partnership instead of employment, even if it means owning only 0.01% of the shares. Impressed by Shuang's uniqueness, Wentian lets him join as a partner. Their relationship grows both as friends and mentors, excelling in acquiring companies and breaking them apart for profit, becoming renowned snipers in the American business world.

Later, Shuang becomes known as the infamous Black Warrior in the business circle, ruthless and merciless. As his power grows, so does the gap between him and Wentian. For the sake of the company's interest and development, Shuang decides to fully acquire Wentian's company. Unable to bear the blow, Wentian drives drunk, and Shuang learns that Wentian's car lost control and plunged into the sea. After the incident, Wentian's body was never found, but Shuang firmly believes he is dead, feeling immense guilt. Is the value of a deal really more important than friendship or life?

Shuang reflects on everything and decides to stop being the Black Warrior, exiting the business world. From the newspaper, Shuang learns that Wentian's widow and children are struggling financially in Hong Kong. Feeling responsible, Shuang decides to return to Hong Kong to take care of Wentian's family.

Miao Miao's Debt Collection

Back in Hong Kong, Shuang finds Wentian's wife She Mo Lian, coincidentally staying at Tang Ji's seedy hotel. He meets Tang Ji, who is troubled by the hotel's operation problems. Tang Ji is a middle-aged playboy from a commercial family, notorious outside but filial to his father, surrounded by beautiful women.

By chance, Shuang meets Lin Miao Miao at a famous store, creating animosity between them. Miao Miao is a compulsive shopper, owing large credit card debts, forcing her to work as a debt collector. With a spirit of no success, no fee, she becomes the ace of the debt collection company.

Mo Lian owes Miao Miao's finance company a large amount of debt due to her grandmother Zhou Xiao Qiao being scammed. Miao Miao is responsible for collecting the debt, thus meeting Shuang again. Shuang takes on Mo Lian's debt, making Miao Miao mistakenly believe that Shuang is Mo Lian's ex-husband who abandoned his family, giving Shuang a terrible impression.

Mo Lian is unaware that her husband's death was indirectly caused by Shuang and continues to believe that her husband Wentian is still alive. Mo Lian's grandmother often accuses Shuang of being the murderer of her son Wentian. Initially shocked by the truth being discovered by Mo Lian, later they find out that the grandmother suffers from early-stage dementia, deeming her words unreliable.

Shuang Turns into a White Knight

Shuang is a regular customer at Qi's Noodle Shop. Seeing Mo Lian's financial difficulties, he introduces her to work at Qi's Noodle Shop. Mo Lian grew up in the U.S., straightforward and uncompromising, but works hard with passion at the noodle shop, injecting new vitality, earning praise from Zhong Qi.

Qi's Noodle Shop faces threats from a newly opened cart noodle shop nearby. Shuang gives the term CEO, and Miao Miao, to save the noodle shop, entrusts her brother Lin Mu Sen as the shop's CEO. Mu Sen takes over the noodle shop but fails miserably. Shuang advises Zhong Qi not to let Mu Sen run the shop and recommends Mo Lian for a key position due to her hardworking nature. Miao Miao doesn't take it seriously, thinking Shuang is just talking nonsense.

Until Shuang helps the Bo De Group acquire the old building where the noodle shop is located, planning to turn it into a hotel, and persuades Sen and Qiao to sell the shop. To protect her father's hard work, Miao Miao decides to stand firm and appoints Mo Lian as the CEO. Mo Lian, as the CEO of the noodle shop, collaboratively negotiates with Bo De Group, resulting in Jia Gi regaining autonomy for the first time, allowing Qi's Noodle Shop to overcome its difficulties, achieving a win-win situation for both parties. Since then, Miao Miao deeply believes Shuang is a business genius and decides to learn business skills from him, hoping to escape poverty. Miao Miao keeps pestering Shuang, though Shuang hasn't verbally agreed, secretly teaching Miao Miao business strategies. Unexpectedly, Miao Miao meets her ex-boyfriend Tong Yi Ben on her business journey.

Miao Miao Gradually Falls for Shuang

Forced by work, Mo Lian sends Xiao Qiao to an old people's home. To comfort Xiao Qiao, Shuang also requests to stay there. Unbeknownst to them, this old people's home is funded by Tang Ji for his girlfriend, facing operational challenges. When Tang Ji is caught cheating by Shuang and Miao Miao at the old people's home, knowing Shuang's capabilities, Tang Ji hopes Shuang will help solve the home's problems. Miao Miao relentlessly pressures Shuang, eager to learn business tactics from him. Reluctantly, Shuang decides to step in.

After agreeing, Shuang seems indifferent, causing Miao Miao to think Shuang is not taking the reform of the old people's home seriously. Determined to test him, Miao Miao proposes the "Plant Affection, Get Bonus" plan, which backfires disastrously. Ultimately, Shuang steps in to resolve the chaos at the old people's home and surprisingly merges it with the neighboring kindergarten, creating a new generation of old-young facility, solving all issues. After this battle, the master-apprentice relationship between Shuang and Miao deepens significantly.

Miao Miao discovers her growing feelings for Shuang, but Shuang pays no attention. Miao Miao assumes Shuang is a deeply emotional man with unresolved feelings for another woman. Later, she finds out this woman is Shuang's mother, Qing Qing. Shuang transfers his maternal affection to Miao Miao, and despite Miao Miao's efforts to resolve the issue, seeing Shuang's refusal to accept her, she concludes that Shuang looks down on her. Determined to strengthen herself, she vows to make Shuang see her differently and win the heart of the man she loves.
