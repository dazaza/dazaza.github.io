With the popularity of SNS, there are more and more communities. The most recognized are mainly friend-making communities. MyFolio, an online community for art, was launched recently. While MyFolio also needs videos, pictures, audio, and files to communicate with people, MyFolio positions itself in the extraordinary "art" category.

In fact, MyFolio is a new online community with art content and advertising content. MyFolio allows users to upload and share their videos, pictures, audio, files, and at the same time build a personalized product portfolio and showcase them from an artistic point of view as well as the owner's word of mouth. MyFolio's goal is to build the largest art exchange site ever.

image

image

Foreign WEB2.0 service ideas are more realistic and more closely related to profit. This is also one of the biggest differences with domestic WEB2.0 services. It shows respect for creativity and thinking. The ultimate goal of MyFolio is to find and connect art lovers with other artists, make it possible for the general public to become future employers, and generate direct or indirect economic benefits. The MyFolio community has "Helping each other" as its theme. Although MyFolio is still in beta, its strong appeal has already flooded the community with great art.
