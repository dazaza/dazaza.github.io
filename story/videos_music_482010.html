I. Ultimate Healing Technique Manual

People who live in the Jianghu (martial arts world) inevitably get hurt due to its treacherous nature. At such times, healing techniques prove extremely useful. This great technique requires not only strong internal energy but also music, which is indispensable. Consequently, various excellent healing techniques have emerged. Among them, some stand out: "Huxin Pavilion," "The Tide of the Blue Sea," "Smiling Proud Wanderer," and "Scenery of the Original Hometown." These techniques are essential for martial artists. The emergence of healing techniques has made people in the martial arts world more actively involved in the Jianghu. Therefore, the musical healing technique holds an important position in the Jianghu due to its immense charm.

II. Characteristics of the Techniques

1. Healing music generally has a gentle rhythm and basically does not require percussion instruments. The melody is very graceful and melodious, and listening to this kind of music feels like entering a fairyland, truly delightful.

2. There are basically three forms of expression:

1. Flute and Xiao (a vertical bamboo flute), which with their soothing and melodious sounds delight the ears. When one enjoys the sound, wounds tend to heal easily.

2. Qin (a stringed instrument) and Xiao duets. This form of music is the most moving in martial arts novels and represents the ideal destination of martial partners - one playing the Xiao and the other the Qin, obviously a divine couple. Qin and Xiao duets play a huge role in martial arts stories.

3. String instruments such as the guzheng and violin. The smooth pulling of the violin and the soft plucking of the guzheng easily produce a calming effect on the heart.

3. Healing music is quite similar to the currently popular heart-nourishing music, making one desireless and peaceful. Moreover, martial healing music shares similarities with love music and nostalgic music, especially love music.

III. Practice Scenarios

1. Severe injury requiring inner force for healing.

2. Qin and Xiao duets for carefree seclusion from the world.

3. Outing to enjoy the scenery, vast and ethereal.

4. Fairyland-like scenes, where the atmosphere nourishes the heart.

1. Scenery of the Original Hometown

① Chinese album name: Wood Path

② Album category: New Age

③ Artist: Sojiro

④ Release time: 1991

⑤ Musical analysis: The beginning violin always rotates gently among a few notes, while the piano's tapping becomes rhythmic. Under the constant floating of the piano, the violin seems to show the rhythmic operation of all things. Subsequently, the ceramic flute plays a spiraling and ethereal tune, producing a sense of having experienced much hardship and sorrow. Although the melody repeats, it remains pleasant to listen to.

⑥ Audio-visual analysis: Use the prelude part of the violin for healing, and the subsequent ceramic flute part to express sadness and helplessness.

① Chinese album name: East Sun Flow

② Album category: New Age

③ Artist: Composer: Jishen; Erhu performance: Xuke

④ Release time: July 21, 1994

⑤ Musical analysis: A grand, ancient, and ethereal atmosphere is created by synthesizers. With the addition of drum sounds, water wave sounds creatively appear in the music.
