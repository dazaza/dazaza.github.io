From the market perspective, in terms of stock knowledge, the overall trend of indicator stocks is weak. Affected by the statement from the General Manager of Shenzhen Stock Exchange that margin trading is ready, broker stocks performed well in the early session, with Haitong Securities leading the rise at 3.11%. Small-cap thematic stocks remained active. Agricultural stocks were stimulated by reports that as the world's largest rice producer and consumer, the Chinese government has approved the commercial planting of the first genetically modified rice variety, resulting in a comprehensive strengthening. The leading individual stock, Denghai Seed Industry, was locked at the upper limit.

From the information perspective, funds are busy with year-end research and portfolio adjustments. The annual fund ranking battle has already begun. China Central Bank Research Bureau Financial Markets Department Director Bu Yongxiang said yesterday that it is expected that domestic credit will increase by 5-7 trillion yuan next year, with broad money supply (M2) growth rate maintained between 16%-17%, and interest rate policies may adjust next year. Previously, the market generally expected that new credit next year would be at least 7 trillion yuan or more.

Hengji Information believes that after the continuous rise of the market in the first half of this week, today's adjustment was within our expectations. From a technical analysis, currently, the middle rail of the large institutional Yin-Yang Kunlun Belt and the golden life line converge around 3215 points, which will form effective support for the short-term market. Investors do not need to worry about the market index, but should take advantage of the market fluctuations to actively adjust their portfolios and layout quality varieties at low positions. Reviewing the market situation since the beginning of the year, as long as the market operates above the large institutional golden life line and the golden life line is red, each time the market retraces to this line, it is an opportunity brewing moment. This exactly verifies a saying: history often repeats itself, and opportunities always recur.

Looking ahead, the agricultural stocks in the large consumption sector that we have repeatedly mentioned recently showed strength today. Although the overall increase was only about 2%, in the current environment, it is enough to attract the audience's attention. Therefore, we must reiterate our unchanged view: with the year-end market already underway, considering the long-term, we should focus on the consumption sector that benefits greatly from economic development, and focus on capturing small-cap quality stocks with high distribution topics and potential for exceeding expectations in annual reports.

The procedure for buying and selling stocks is as follows:

When buying or selling stocks, you must specify whether you are buying or selling, the name (or code) of the stock, the quantity, and the price. Furthermore, this order is only valid on the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviation (code) of the stock you want to buy or sell, the quantity, and the price of the stock you want to buy or sell. The abbreviation of the stock usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, and the stock code is six digits. When placing a buy/sell order, the stock code and abbreviation must match. At the same time, there are certain rules regarding the quantity of stocks being traded: the quantity of stocks to be purchased must be a whole multiple of 100, while the quantity of stocks to be sold does not have to be a whole multiple of 100.

There are four ways to place an order: counter submission orders, telephone automatic orders, computer automatic orders, and remote terminal orders.
