Not long ago, Tongka Company, the leader in providing accurate marketing services for members of domestic consumer service chain enterprises, announced in Beijing that Nalian Wang, a marketing service website aimed at helping merchants attract new customers, was officially launched.

Tongka is a third-party service company dedicated to member management and accurate marketing for chain enterprises. Its developed member accurate marketing service platform not only perfectly realizes traditional basic functions such as integrated points, electronic coupons, and networked storage but also focuses on using the member database to analyze information about member consumption, storage, consumption frequency, consumption habits, and consumption levels. By utilizing the system's powerful fine analysis capabilities, members are segmented, and different categories of member groups are sent appropriate coupons. Meanwhile, SMS and email platforms are embedded within the system to establish quick and smooth communication channels with members. In this way, under cost-saving conditions, it greatly enhances the accuracy and timeliness of merchants' marketing activities and allows members to enjoy suitable consumption discounts, improving their consumption experience.

In recent years, Tongka has gradually taken steps in the field of member accurate marketing for catering enterprises, establishing its leading position in the CRM sector of the catering industry. To date, Tongka has provided member accurate marketing services to thousands of companies, including well-known catering chain enterprises such as Xibei Youmian Village, Dadong Roast Duck, Jinhans, Big Pizza, Jinfeng Chengxiang, Million Manor, Lao She Teahouse, Quanjude, Jian Catering, Li Laodie, Chuan Chengyuan, Jiutouying, Yama Teppanyaki, Dekawa House, Shangyufang, Laobeiping, Wanfeng Pavilion, Latin Restaurant, Dezhuang, Liu Yishou, Yuxiang Spicy Grandma, Guangming Fish Port, and Ping Shui Xiang Feng. Millions of members daily enjoy personalized member consumption services provided by enterprises through Tongka Marketing Access Service Platform.

This time, the launch of Nalian Wang, the marketing service website under Tongka, represents an upgrade in marketing services for merchants to attract new customers, based on retaining old customers and awakening dormant ones.

In today’s fiercely competitive group-buying market, Tongka, as a third-party platform offering comprehensive marketing services for merchants, uses the group-buying method to provide new customer marketing services for its service merchants, opening up online and offline full-range membership-based marketing services. Unlike other group-buying websites which focus on price competition and heavy advertising spending, Nalian Wang, powered by Tongka Data Service Platform, transfers customer data from in-store consumption to the merchant membership service platform. While helping merchants attract and create new customers, it pays more attention to how to retain them and ultimately develop them into loyal customers. In the future, merchants can independently conduct marketing activities such as holiday events, promoting new dishes, and awakening dormant members through the membership marketing service platform.

The launch of Nalian Wang has received strong support from Tongka's service clients. Currently, merchants such as Dekawa House, Herba Herba, Lao She Teahouse, Twelve Seasons Private Kitchen, Jin Baiwan Xierqi Store, and Gentle Town have become the first to use Tongka’s new customer marketing service platform. They have also prepared highly cost-effective products for consumers, making us look forward to what's next.
