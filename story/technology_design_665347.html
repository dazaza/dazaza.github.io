An average annual growth of over 13% in the minimum wage standard during the 12th Five-Year Plan period

by anonymous on 2012-02-14 15:48:52

During the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, the annual average growth rate of the minimum wage standard will exceed 13%. (From Dahan Newspaper on February 9, 2012, I want to comment (114)) Font size: T|T. The "Employment Promotion Plan (2011-2015)" proposed that during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, China will establish a normal wage growth mechanism. Employees' income level will increase reasonably and rapidly. The annual average growth rate of the minimum wage standard will be over 13%, and in most regions, the minimum wage standard will reach more than 40% of the average wage of urban employees.

This is the goal put forward by the "Employment Promotion Plan (2011-2015)" broadcast by Xinhua News Agency on February 8th. This plan was jointly formulated by the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, National Development and Reform Commission, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Agriculture, and Ministry of Commerce. It has been recently approved by the State Council and forwarded to all regions and departments for implementation.

The plan pointed out that during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, China will implement a fiscal security policy more conducive to promoting employment. Public finance will tilt towards small micro-enterprises and labor-intensive industries that conform to the national industrial policy orientation. Fiscal expenditure will gradually tilt towards people's livelihood and increase support for disadvantaged groups.

The plan proposed that by the end of the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, the enterprise labor contract signing rate in our country should reach 90%, the enterprise collective contract signing rate should reach 80%, working conditions will significantly improve, the social security system will cover all workers, and employment stability will obviously increase.

During the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, the annual average growth rate of the minimum wage standard in our country was 12.5%. By the end of the "11th Five-Year Plan" period, the enterprise labor contract signing rate in our country was 65%, and the enterprise collective contract signing rate was 50%.

The plan also proposed that during the "12th Five-Year Plan" period, China needs to reform the total wage management method of state-owned enterprises, implement dual control over the total wages and wage levels of some industries, and narrow the wage level gap between industries. Strictly regulate the remuneration management of senior executives in state-owned enterprises and financial institutions.
