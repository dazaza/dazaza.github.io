How to light up the Battle King icon (AVA Merit System)

by lwf225 on 2009-09-01 14:33:10

Tags: Light up the Battle King icon, Light up the AVA icon, AVA icon, How to light up the Battle King icon?

As long as you reach the rank of Corporal (required experience points: 8500) or achieve 100 Merit Points, you can light up the AVA icon! For those who haven't played AVA, please watch the Beginner's Video Tutorial for Battle King first.

Rules for upgrading Battle King icons and Merit System Description:

Warm reminder:

Merit system currently does not support lighting up/upgrading/extinguishing icons. It only calculates and ranks the acquisition of merit points. All data may be adjusted according to player conditions. For fairness, this system data will be cleared during the non-deletion internal test. Any inconvenience caused is appreciated for your understanding.

1. What are Merit Points?

Merit Points are honor values accumulated from in-game experience points and game medals.

2. How to obtain Merit Points?

Merit Points consist of in-game experience points and medals; on non-holiday days, a maximum of 9 Merit Points can be exchanged daily; on holidays, double the amount, one game medal can be exchanged for one Merit Point.

3. If I change my bound zone, how will the Merit Points be calculated?

The Merit Points you acquire each day will be calculated within the character of your bound zone. If you change your bound zone, the Merit Points will be cumulatively calculated in the new zone character starting the next day after modification.

4. How do I claim my Merit rewards?

Enter "My Merits" — "My Merit Rewards" tab to check and exchange. 50 Merit Points can be exchanged for 10,000 AVA game coins.
