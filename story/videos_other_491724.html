Liu Yuan's marriage to her ex-husband lasted for seven or eight years, during which they were unable to have children. However, when Liu Yuan married Wang Wuming, she was already pregnant. The question of who the father of the child was became a mental knot for Wang Wuming. After marriage, Wang Wuming had questioned his wife Liu Yuan several times about the identity of the biological father, but Liu Yuan remained silent.

In July 2009, 26-year-old Wang Wuming met 29-year-old Liu Yuan through an introduction. Liu Yuan had previously been in a seven or eight-year marriage without children. Liu Yuan was one of four sisters in her family with no male offspring. Her family was looking for a man willing to move into their home as a son-in-law, and they proposed this condition to Wang Wuming.

Wang Wuming discovered that Liu Yuan had a close relationship with her second brother-in-law, behaving ambiguously. One time, while the Liu family was gathered around a table playing cards, it was the second brother-in-law’s turn to play. Liu Yuan urged him, saying, "Dear, hurry up and play your card!" This scene was witnessed by Wang Wuming as he entered the room.

There were rumors among the neighbors that after Liu Yuan divorced her previous husband, her family arranged for one of their sons-in-law to test if she could bear children. However, the specific person involved was unknown to the neighbors.

The conflict erupted on Liu's mother's birthday.

On August 27, Liu Yuan, who was no longer in critical condition, was discharged from the hospital to recover at home; meanwhile, Wang Wuming, who had fled, was captured by the police in Baojing County.

Subsequently, Wang Wuming moved out of the Liu family home with Liu Yuan.

On August 12, coinciding with Liu's mother's sixtieth birthday, after much deliberation, Wang Wuming still went to the Liu family home but was refused entry, as they claimed he was an "unwelcome person." Having no other choice, Wang Wuming left. After walking less than 200 meters, Wang Wuming called his wife and asked her to leave together, soon after which his wife, holding their two children, returned home with him.

Liu Yuan’s family consisted of four sisters. The first brother-in-law, the second brother-in-law, and Wang Wuming all lived in the Liu household. Since becoming the Liu family's son-in-law, Wang Wuming became disinterested in work, spending his days idling outside without ambition. Over time, the Liu family began to show dissatisfaction with Wang Wuming.

Wang Wuming began to suspect that he was being used by the Liu family, merely a tool to legitimately produce a boy. With the birth of this boy to carry on the Liu family name, the Liu family planned to discard him and drive him out of the house.

On the way home, filled with anger, Wang Wuming asked Liu Yuan if she would be willing to live with him. Her response plunged Wang Wuming into despair: "You can't even support your wife and children, what's the point of following you? I've already remarried once, I don't care about doing it a third or fourth time!"

Connecting these observations, Wang Wuming immediately became enraged, leading to a fierce conflict on the spot. The next day, the second brother-in-law brought a few friends to severely beat Wang Wuming at home, while Liu's parents watched indifferently.

Being a son-in-law in the Liu family proved difficult.

Charged with intentional injury by the Xinbao police, one victim died and one was injured — the latter being his wife Liu Yuan, who suffered twelve stab wounds and was barely alive; the former being his four-month-old daughter Xi Xi, who succumbed to injuries after her thigh artery was severed. Why would a man harm his wife and kill his own daughter? The convoluted story behind this tragedy is thought-provoking.

Starting from the county town of Xinbao, after an hour-long bus ride, another hour-long motorcycle ride, and then a two-hour walk along muddy mountain paths, one arrives at Wang Wuming's home. It is a mud-brick house containing only a wardrobe for clothes, a bed, and a few pots, far from prosperous.

At 11 AM on August 30, at the first detention center of Xingbao County in Shaoyang City, "I love her so much, I would never kill her. I just wanted to scar her face so she would commit to me forever," confessed the suspect Wang Wuming, tears streaming down his face.

Since the birth of the child, Wang Wuming's position in the Liu family plummeted.

Eager to escape poverty, Wang Wuming did not hesitate to agree. Before marriage, Liu Yuan's mother told him, "Liu Yuan is now pregnant, this child will be yours in the future, taking care of you in old age. You must treat him well." After much hesitation, Wang Wuming finally agreed. They held their wedding ceremony in December 2009, and in April 2010, a boy was born. Wang Wuming registered the child under his name with his marriage certificate and birth permit, but the child carried the surname "Liu."

During this period living outside the Liu family, Wang Wuming supported himself by doing odd jobs. In April of this year, Liu Yuan gave birth prematurely to twin baby girls, further exacerbating the financial difficulties of the family. Wang Wuming had gone to the Liu family to borrow money shamelessly, only to be met with the reply, "Not a penny."

After hearing this, Wang Wuming jumped up as if electrocuted, pulling out a fruit knife from his bag and lunging towards Liu Yuan. In the chaos, the younger daughter Xuanxuan was thrown to the ground, the older daughter Xixi's thigh artery was pierced, Liu Yuan suffered twelve stab wounds and collapsed in a pool of blood... According to eyewitness Wang Lao Han, after committing the crime, Wang Wuming stood stunned at the scene for quite some time before leaving. Subsequently, Wang Lao Han immediately reported the incident to the police.
