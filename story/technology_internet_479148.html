The effect of marriage certificate photos becomes outdated due to constraints such as size, background, shooting environment, etc., and cannot be as perfect and fashionable as wedding photos. However, it's not entirely without solutions. How can we make the marriage certificate photo that will follow you for life more fashionable? In fact, it only takes a little caution.

Brides with long hair over their shoulders appear more feminine.

Long hair over the shoulder makes brides appear more feminine than the coiled hairstyle.

Since when taking the marriage certificate photo, the couple is required to face the lens directly, hairstyles like the coiled hairstyle which excel in "background scenery" are not suitable for marriage certificate photos. On the contrary, gentle long hair over the shoulder highlights the bride's femininity. Hair must be treated with conditioner to avoid rough and messy hair that affects the visual experience. If you have bangs, they should be neatly combed to show the bride's gentle charm.

At the Milan Men's Fashion Week, here are some tricks to make your marriage certificate photo fashionable. Versace's (VERSACE) 2012 spring men's collection focuses on bright-colored clothing. Bright blue, yellow, and pink as the main colors, interspersed with some gray-brown tones, giving a feeling of youth while not losing seriousness.

BOBO hairstyle with airiness, youthful and fashionable.

Airy BOBO hairstyle, a great trick for brides to look younger and fashionable.

Because marriage certificate photos are half-body shots, hairstyles become the "main prop" for brides to show their fashion sense. The airy BOBO hairstyle is youthful and fashionable, making it a go-to choice for fashionable brides taking certificate photos. The chestnut color hair dye result is "surprising," even more trendy and fashionable.

Shooting tips: Innocent expressions, sweet sister-style smiles are all good choices.

The navy sailor shirt with red background color blocking adds fashion sense to the newlyweds.

Senior couples always recommend sailor shirts for couples.

Many online "novice" couples ask what clothes are the most stylish for taking marriage certificate photos. The "seniors" always recommend sailor shirts for couples. Since most domestic marriage certificate photos use a red background, the color-blocking combination of sailor shirts with the red background enhances the couple's fashion sense, and the stripe pattern makes the clothing more noticeable.

Shooting tips: Although marriage certificate photos have strict requirements for facial features and require facing the lens directly, there are no clear restrictions on body angles. Couples can "slightly" turn their bodies and cuddle closely with each other since the most beautiful wedding photos are those filled with love.

Big S's carefully combed smooth long hair also demonstrates the "queen's" strict pursuit of details.

Sun Li and Deng Chao: Retro-themed marriage certificate photos.

Sun Li and Deng Chao: Theme shooting for marriage certificate photos.

Shooting tips: Poses and expressions are crucial. Retro styles can be serious or quirky.
