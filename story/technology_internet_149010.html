Strengthening supervision and creating a harmonious network environment is imperative

by now on 2009-05-14 16:24:38

With the acceleration of informatization, the Internet has been fully integrated into modern life. The power of the network in information dissemination is particularly prominent, so relevant national departments pay more attention to the healthy development of the network, thereby strengthening the rectification and management of the network. Following a special operation to rectify the vulgar online trend nationwide not long ago, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) officially released the "Implementation Measures for the Notification of Internet Network Security Information". The aim of this measure is to promote the sharing of Internet security information and improve the level of Internet security early warning, prevention and emergency response. It is understood that this measure is the first specific response policy proposed by MIIT targeting Internet network security phenomena. This reflects the determination of relevant departments in our country to create a safe and harmonious network environment, and also shows that related measures for network regulation are gradually improving.

The Internet is a huge platform for information exchange, which requires strengthened management by the government and network service institutions to promote the healthy development of the network. Regarding the disposal methods of computer viruses in the "Implementation Measures for the Notification of Internet Network Security Information", it clearly stipulates the responsibilities and obligations that Internet access service providers and domain name registrars need to bear. Under the call of the policy, various Internet domain name registration service institutions have responded actively. For this purpose, Now Telecom launched a network rectification activity with the theme of "A near neighbor is better than a distant relative", making great efforts in website ICP filing and investigation of illegal website information, strictly supervising the network environment, and creating a better prospect for network security.

Today in the 21st century, the Internet meets people's needs in all aspects, and a safe and stable network environment occupies an important position in the development of informatization. People cannot completely eliminate the spread of negative online information or prohibit the risks existing in network security, but we can curb their development from aspects such as strengthening information regulation and improving service quality. A relevant person in charge of the gold-level domain registrar Now Telecom (www.now.cn) told the author that creating a safe and healthy network environment requires coordinated cooperation among the government, network service institutions, and a large number of netizens. Starting from oneself, fulfilling relevant responsibilities and obligations will lay the foundation for creating a safe network environment. The author also learned that Now Telecom has been promoting the "Quality Service Year" activity for two consecutive years, aiming to provide quality services while further improving product quality. Only by starting from product quality can we fundamentally enhance the level of Internet security prevention and ensure the basic safety of the network.

Believe that under the promotion of relevant national policies, vigorously creating a harmonious and safe network environment is inevitable. Each network service institution should start from itself, make contributions to the healthy development of the Internet, and promote the healthy development path of China's Internet.
