The main reason is that I've been busy recently or in the past year. Perhaps this is just an excuse, not wanting to write or not daring to write, or maybe what's written is wrong. After writing about rising things, the stock market will go in the opposite direction, which is the basic reason. However, despite this, I have never lacked attention to the stock market. I have always followed the habits I've developed over the years, buying high and selling low in the stock market, studying and analyzing stocks day and night, and often having K-line charts appear in my mind.

When it comes to the stock market, I can almost describe myself as being terrified. Since entering the stock market in 1993, I have never had a full lunch break during trading hours. I have always been restless when there was no computer during trading hours, and I always felt empty on weekends. Perhaps I have already written a lot about these things before. Today, describing them again may seem repetitive. The reason I use the name "failure will never knock me down" stems from the previous phrase "as long as I am determined to succeed." I am determined to succeed. I have devoted so much to the stock market. For nearly 20 years, the stock market has almost played out as a tragedy for me. But as long as I am determined to succeed, comedy will be brilliantly performed after the extension of tragedy. I believe my comedy will soon make a brilliant appearance. Now, I am so confident because I have my own technical support. In the past two months, I have caught three stocks that hit the daily limit: Wenshan Electric Power, Bensteel Sheet, and Shanxi Securities, and sold all of them at the daily limit or above. This is the source of energy for starting again today. I want to prove myself here. Recently, I also helped a friend operate an account worth 480,000 yuan. It has been two months, and it has now reached 530,000 yuan. The plan is to reach 600,000 yuan before Children's Day and 1 million yuan before National Day, provided that the market index stays above 2500 points. If the market index reaches above 4000 points, the goal should be correspondingly increased.

There is still one more week of trading time before May Day. Last night, I saw several guests invited by Ningxia TV predict a downward trend for next week, with three guests expecting a fall and one expecting stability. However, I still believe that next week will see an upward trend based on this week's consolidation. In other words, I feel that the market has stabilized around the 3000-point mark. Next week, the market will oscillate upwards and will not drop below 3000 points. Considering the impact of May Day, the market should not rise too much, but the tourism sector will perform well. The stocks in this sector have already acted in advance. Next week, the tourism sector will perform outstandingly.

Because I noticed that the non-ferrous metals sector hadn't moved for a long time last weekend, I bought Western Mining. Judging by its technical indicators, after the recent decline, it should have a decent rebound. After making profits, I will choose stocks in the tourism sector.

Incidentally, last night while watching TV on Ningxia Channel, I was immediately attracted by the carefree protagonist - Wang Shutong, a successful woman who is both financially savvy and beautiful. Living to this extent can be considered "a person beneficial to the people." Normally, I rarely have time to watch TV, but sometimes when I'm up early or late at night flipping through channels, I find that Ningxia TV and Chongqing TV programs better suit my viewing preferences. These two channels each have their strengths. In today's era where impatience and materialism are rampant, I think Chongqing TV and Ningxia TV lead the way. These two TV stations are guiding people according to correct thinking and concepts, helping people realize their self-worth. Chongqing TV emphasizes history and education, while Ningxia TV focuses on economy and culture. They have few advertisements, very humanistic programs, and align well with traditional Chinese moral education. If we follow Premier Wen Jiabao's requirements to improve people's moral cultivation and reduce their impatience, then watching these two channels' programs would help. I believe that if the leaders of these two channels could become the directors of CCTV, and the provincial leaders responsible for propaganda work in these two provinces could work in the Central Propaganda Department, the moral standards of the entire nation would significantly improve.
