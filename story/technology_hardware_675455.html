Quicksand

by wangquan1986 on 2012-02-25 09:17:33

The winter might be over, occasionally a warm breeze blowing, though still unable to drive away the cold, but quickly without that bone-chilling cold. And this is not important. The transition between winter and spring has its own rules. When it's cold, we won't be afraid; when it's warm, we won't be overly happy. Because we understand too clearly. Yes, clear... However, there are always too many unknowns that make us hesitate or even fear... 2012, this year that was hyped by all kinds of media as something special. It was also defined as the year to fight for love. Various media have emerged like a hundred schools of thought contending, such as "Men's Club", "Beijing Love Story", "33 Days of Heartbreak", "Those Years, We Chased Girls Together" and the new wave "Ant Tribe Struggle". I must say, all kinds of media, you've been very successful. With Beijing's increasingly fast-paced life rhythm, frequent interactions every day, everything here is changing. Reality, like this, has been openly distorted by various media, yet lacking kindness, sharp lenses revealing bloody realities, thus clearly and explicitly displayed on the screen. Grassroots love, in such a city, at such a time, sounds somewhat unreliable. Maybe personal value concepts differ, so the pursuit of love may vary. But, when you're awakened from that sweet and romantic warmth by a cuckoo's call; when you're woken up from those touching vows of eternal love by the need for shelter from the cool wind; when your romantic stroll by the river under the moonlight is interrupted by a passing car kicking up dust. Please ask: what role does love play here? Love, you say it has a price, or no price. Priceless? Yes, ancient examples like Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai can be cited! If possible, I am willing to believe. Today? I swear, I've really thought about it carefully, no! Price? Yes, I take it as a joke. A professor did a market survey, ten couples, ten thousand yuan to break up, the couples ignore it, one hundred thousand yuan to break up, some people start to move, one million yuan to break up, hehe, most people take the money and go their separate ways. Of course, real grassroots love, today's naked marriage, I've really seen it. They are also very happy! They truly live around me, desire-less, small love beats big family, speaking of which, I envy them, but really, I can't do it... Perspective determines our pursuit, pursuit determines our happiness level. However, there are so many people in this city, crowded to the point where everyone feels lonely. In fact, many times it's not that we're not good enough, but the people living here are all so excellent. Everyone measures others by their own standards, but forgets themselves, these standards gradually expand, the level gets higher and higher, happiness gets further and further away. "Beijing Love" really explains this. I, since stepping out of the university gate, have been swept up among millions of others who carry dreams and root themselves in Beijing, lost. Time, like sand slipping through our fingers, disappears in moments we don't pay attention... Have the initial desires been fulfilled? Please believe, I'm not melancholy! Just that, when our lush dreams shatter instantly, and I finally see some things, my maturity... Love, isn't just mutual affection, it's a real matter between two people. I once foolishly said: I love you, it has nothing to do with you! But, does it really have nothing to do with you? Even if you can hold your sincere heart, will someone really accept it? A man's life will be changed by two women. One woman turns you from a babbling child into a mentally mature man, she is your mother. One woman turns you from a mature and cautious man into a dreamy child, she is your lover. Please forgive my lack of words, up to this point, for love, such a beautiful synonym, I can no longer accurately describe. Economic foundation determines superstructure. The fortress of love is also built on this foundation. Don't say I'm realistic, I just occasionally glimpse my once-dreaming self in reality, naive to the extreme. When a man has nothing, what can he use to talk about love? You want to encompass her entire life, but have you considered whether you have the ability to lay a solid foundation for her fortress? There will always be a woman who changes you greatly, she is not your mother, she makes you turn from a mature man into a dreamy child, and then she will make you turn back into a real man. If such a woman appears in your life, regardless of the outcome, please thank her. She gives you the soul of a man, the soul of a true man. In her limited youth, she took part of her time to help you grow. I, also thank you, who always says to stick with you. Everyone has their own persistence, just different methods, please understand, one method is called growth. "Fate Hard", I only now understand her meaning... Although, there is also your past hidden there. At this age, girls mature earlier than boys. Hopefully, my awakening is not too late... Men look forward to belonging, women look forward to security. And these, is there a scale? If so, please lend it to me, let me measure... Being born a man doesn't mean you'll become a man in the future. Losing is incapable, avoidance is cowardly, running away is disgraceful. Accumulating time to overcome the weak self is the way to truly become a man. A man's dignity does not allow me to lose at the starting line. Speaking of which, time is really stingy! But, I can only accumulate within my limited time, overcome the weak self. Tighten my fingers, let the limited flow of sand draw out the happiness level I want. Without realizing, it's midnight again, haha, insomnia, this word, I think should also flow away. Tomorrow, busy, it should restart, let me slowly forget, and delve deeper. Since I accidentally walked to that display window and saw the happiness that moved me, so, I won't miss it...
