Teacher forces late students to slap each other 100 times and finally intervenes to add more slaps

by mlwang on 2011-11-09 14:44:48

Just because they were a few minutes late for the school bus, two fifth-grade students from a migrant workers' children school in Fangcun, Liwan District, Guangzhou, were asked by their teacher to slap each other for more than three minutes. Later, the teacher slapped them several more times. This process was filmed by another student on the bus and uploaded online, sparking heated discussions. Currently, the teacher who hit the students has been dismissed by the school. Faced with reporters, this "post-90s" young teacher deeply regrets his impulsive actions.

Two students slapped each other over a hundred times

As soon as the reporter opened the video, they saw two elementary school students wearing blue and white uniforms slapping each other's faces on a moving school bus. The video is 4 minutes and 13 seconds long. From the first second to the 3 minute and 28 second mark, the two students continued to slap each other. Judging from their expressions, the two seemed very wronged and unwilling, but also seemed to have no choice but to do so. By the end, both elementary school students started to have weak fingers. The reporter roughly counted that in about 30 seconds, the two slapped each other over 40 times. Calculating by 3 minutes and 28 seconds, the two slapped each other nearly 300 times, meaning each was slapped nearly 150 times. In the video, the school bus was filled with students wearing uniforms. However, while the two children slapped each other, the other students just turned around and expressionlessly watched everything. As it went on, many people seemed to get annoyed and turned back around to stop watching.

The teacher actually hit the students again

At the 3 minute and 28 second mark, the two stopped slapping each other. A young man dressed in a white shirt, looking like a teacher, walked from the front of the bus to where the two students were standing at the rear of the bus. After saying a few words to them, the man suddenly slapped one of the students hard on the face, catching the student off guard and making them dodge backward. The man said a few more words, then suddenly slapped again, hitting the student twice. Still not satisfied, he gave a harsh third slap, all three landing on the child's face. After hitting them, the two children unhappily returned to their seats, and the video ended there.

What exactly did the two children do wrong to be physically punished by the teacher like this? According to the person who uploaded the video, the two students were just late for the school bus. The beaten students are from Xinhui School in Fangcun, Guangzhou, and the person who hit them is the school's accompanying teacher. After carefully watching the video, the reporter found that during the more than four-minute video, the school bus passed through several tunnels and related sections in Fangcun multiple times. Many of the motor vehicles passing by the school bus had Guangdong A license plates, suggesting that the video was likely filmed in Fangcun.

Subsequently, the reporter found out that there indeed is a private school called Xinhui in Shachong, Kangkou, Fangcun, Guangzhou. Yesterday afternoon, the reporter went to the school to verify the video.
