How to Prevent Freezing of Medium Frequency Quenching Equipment and High Frequency Induction Heating Power Supply in Winter

by gaopin5m2w on 2012-02-13 17:25:50

With the arrival of the cold winter in 2011, to ensure the normal use of high-frequency induction heating power supply in winter and prevent low temperature from affecting its use and causing damage, it is necessary to take anti-freezing measures for production equipment and facilities before entering winter. This will ensure the safe and stable operation of high-frequency induction heating power supply through the winter without affecting the production requirements of various customers.

Currently, most high-frequency induction heating power supply equipment is water-cooled. During winter when the machine is not in operation, it is essential to blow out the water inside using an air gun; otherwise, once it freezes, thermal expansion and contraction can easily damage the transformer.

General anti-freezing measures are as follows:

1. Keep the circulating water pump in a constantly open flowing state. Ensure all pipelines have proper heat tracing and insulation to prevent freezing.

2. If the water pump is not kept running, after work every day, first drain the water remaining in the equipment, then use an air gun to blow out the remaining water.

3. After stopping the machine, use a fan to dry the water in the inlet and outlet pipes.

4. Closely monitor weather changes, stay highly vigilant, truly achieve prevention before problems occur, and ensure the normal operation of the company's production and business activities.

Quenching technology is widely used in modern machinery manufacturing industries. Important parts, especially steel components used in automobiles, airplanes, and rockets, almost all undergo quenching treatment. To meet various technical requirements, different quenching processes have been developed. For example, according to the treated part, there are overall, partial, and surface quenching; according to whether the phase transformation is complete during heating, there are complete quenching and incomplete quenching (for hypoeutectoid steel, this method is also called subcritical quenching); according to the content of phase transformation during cooling, there are分级quenching, isothermal quenching, and under-speed quenching, etc.
