Recently, survey data showed that among males over 40 years old in China, 80 million suffer from varying degrees of erectile dysfunction (ED). Moreover, most patients prefer to buy kidney-nourishing products or purchase "aphrodisiac drugs" from street shops for self-treatment. This has driven the development of the male health industry. However, some counterfeit and substandard products chasing exorbitant profits not only affect the development of the entire male health industry but also directly endanger the health of a large number of men. Professor Yang Mingzhi, head of the Lijia Li Male Health Project Group at the China Academy of Chinese Medical Sciences, recently reminded male friends to be cautious when choosing sexual health supplements.

How can we effectively distinguish between good and bad male health supplements? Below are a few tips for male friends to consider.

First tip:

A high-quality male health supplement must be based on authoritative research institutions. For example, "Lijia Li" capsules are developed by the authoritative Medicinal Health Products Research Center of the China Academy of Chinese Medical Sciences, while counterfeit and substandard products either lack support from authoritative research institutions or fabricate fictitious organizations to bolster their image.

Second tip:

High-quality male health supplements usually have excellent packaging. The packaging will clearly indicate approval numbers, manufacturers, and consultation phone numbers, with clear text and vibrant colors. The outer packaging of "Lijia Li" capsules clearly shows the approval number G20040825, along with explicit manufacturer information and consultation telephone details. On the other hand, counterfeit and substandard products are often "three-no" products—no approval number, no manufacturer, and no consultation phone number. To pursue excessive profits, they often cut corners and produce poor-quality packaging.

Third tip:

Top-notch male health supplements typically include anti-counterfeiting marks to prevent imitation by counterfeit products. Counterfeit products themselves imitate quality products and naturally won't have any anti-counterfeiting marks.

Fourth tip:

Only large brands with premium quality have the strength to continuously place substantial advertisements on influential mass media for promotion. Those who bombastically advertise during certain periods, make money, and then disappear are mostly tactics used by counterfeit and substandard products.

Fifth tip:

High-quality male health supplements often gain recognition from relevant authoritative institutions due to their excellent efficacy. "Lijia Li" capsules were approved by the National Food and Drug Administration and obtained the national food health registration number G20040825. During the 3.15 Consumer Rights Day period in 2006, it was awarded the "Most Admired by Consumers Award in 2005" by the Guangzhou Consumer Council. Conversely, counterfeit and substandard products, due to their inferior quality, not only fail to gain recognition but also face crackdowns.

A high-quality major brand male health supplement generally meets all of the above "five looks" requirements. In the future, male friends can use these "five looks" as a guide when purchasing male health supplements.
