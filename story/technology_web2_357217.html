Three Days After the Pig Wife Left

by cha1214n on 2010-05-31 22:21:13

The weather today is just as bad as yesterday, raining all day long in Shennongjia, leaving us at a loss! The only thing is that when I return to Shanghai, this timely rain has become a humorous and entertaining prank, driving away the scorching heat of the past few days! As for me, I slept all day in this cool weather, waking up feeling groggy. When I was sleeping, I was truly dead asleep because last night's activity kept me out until nearly 10 in the morning. So when I was sleeping, people who didn't know thought I had passed out. But let's not talk about me anymore. Let's talk properly about the weather in Nanjiang and my longing for my wife!

Dear, today is the third day since you left Shenzhen. Although our hearts have never been apart, sometimes I still feel lonely. Why does the weather during National Day always seem so good? I've asked myself countless times why it feels this way. When you're not by my side, no matter the weather in Chang'an or Heilongjiang, my world always seems desolate and bleak. Every day, I find time to be alone and tell myself that I'm not lonely! Because you will always be with me, no matter how far you are. I will continue to wait for you as before. Twenty-four Solar Terms - Analysis. I believe we will never feel lonely because we will always be with each other, whether at the ends of the earth or the corners of the sea, we will always be in each other's hearts. We will never feel lonely because our hearts are always together, never apart!

Regarding our future, I have great hopes and aspirations: I want to tightly embrace you upon your return and make a wish for you, telling you that I am willing to bear your burdens on my shoulders. And also tell my piggy wife that I am willing to hold your hand and grow old with you! Piggy wife, no matter how far you are, I will wait for you at the edge of the sky, making sure you never feel lonely or unsafe because I will forever be by your side. Whether in the corners of the earth or the ends of the sea, you will always be in my heart. Rainy days, at this moment, we will never feel lonely because our two hearts are tightly connected!
