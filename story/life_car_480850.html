Car Beauty and Maintenance Knowledge

People who drive themselves will find: the fine cigarette ashes that fall on the foot pads, the stains on the surface of the seats, the spotty shoe prints on the lower side of the inner door panels, and the dusty dashboard and roof. How to solve the cleaning and maintenance of car interior? Beauty experts point out that the cleaning and maintenance of car interiors is not an easy task, so it's important to pay attention to daily cleaning inside the car and regularly visit professional stores for interior maintenance.

Professional Cleaning and Maintenance

If you are a busy car owner, then all the daily maintenance mentioned above can be done for you by a professional car beauty shop. Professional interior maintenance includes three parts: full-car interior cleaning, leather protection, and steam sterilization.

Full Car Interior Cleaning: This strictly follows the three steps of dust removal, cleaning, and maintenance for a thorough clean and comprehensive maintenance of the instrument control panel, ceiling, rear cylinder platform, seats, floor mats, inner door panels, etc. The whole process is operated by four staff members and takes about two and a half hours. In the cleaning process, neutral or weakly acidic cleaning agents are used, whose residues do not produce harmful gases and automatically evaporate within a few seconds, making it safe and environmentally friendly without causing any harm to the human body.

Leather Protection: Professional car beauty shops are equipped with complete sets of leather care tools, using different tools for different parts.

In addition, professional leather protection has certain requirements for the dosage of cleaning agents and maintenance agents because excessive liquid agents will remain on the leather surface and absorb dust from the air, polluting the car environment.

Steam Sterilization: Professional steam sterilization not only performs high-temperature sterilization of the car's air but also focuses on sterilizing the air conditioning outlets, seats, floor mats, and other areas that easily accumulate dust and bacteria, ensuring the complete killing of invisible mites, mold, and microorganisms, protecting the health of your family and yourself. At the same time, it won't damage the car's interior decoration or cause secondary pollution to the environment.

Instrument Control Panel: The instrument control panel is prone to accumulating dust and has many dead ends. When the owner cleans it themselves, they can use a brush to brush the dashboard, air conditioner inlets, switches, buttons, etc., every day to prevent dust accumulation that is hard to remove. If the instrument control panel is dirty, it needs to be sprayed with a special instrument panel cleaner and wiped clean with a clean soft cloth. After cleaning, a layer of dashboard wax can be sprayed.

Ceiling: The ceiling is a place that is often overlooked, and once cleaned, there will inevitably be liquid residue dripping onto the seats or carpets.

Experts suggest: Owners can use a car vacuum cleaner to remove dust regularly, and when doing comprehensive interior maintenance at a car beauty shop, thoroughly clean the ceiling.

Rear Cylinder Platform: We usually pile paper towels, toys, cushions, and other miscellaneous items on the rear cylinder platform. Paper towels and cushions are often used by people sitting inside the car, so the cleaning and dust removal of the rear cylinder platform cannot be neglected. Its cleaning method can refer to the instrument control panel.

Seats: Seats are the interior components that owners touch the most and are also the easiest to get dirty, so maintaining the seats,

First, cover the seats with fabric seat covers and have another set ready for replacement and washing.

Second, try to avoid food or liquids spilling on the seats, especially alcohol-based or perfume-based items. Once these items stick, if they are not cleaned within 12 hours, these stains will be difficult to remove.

Third, try to avoid scratching the leather surface with metal objects.

Fourth, regularly use leather cleaners and conditioners for cleaning and maintenance to prevent leather aging, fading, and cracking.

Experts suggest: Every six months, the leather parts inside the car need to be cared for.

Floor Mat: When the floor mat gets dirty, it is difficult to clean because it cannot be removed from the car, so most owners choose to go to a professional car beauty shop for cleaning. On a regular basis, they lay a foot mat on the floor mat for easier daily cleaning. After washing the foot mat, squeeze out the water. Professional car beauty shops have dedicated pad washing machines, which make cleaning the foot mats both quick and clean.

Inner Door Panels: The main stains on the car’s inner door panels are shoe prints, concentrated on the lower edge of the door panels. Attention should be paid to this area regularly and frequently cleaned. Owners can use professional foam cleaners for cleaning and then wipe slightly with a clean soft cloth.
