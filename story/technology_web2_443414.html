Little Liu poked his head out of the car window.

by g8aq8r6jnjj on 2011-06-09 12:19:12

On the weekend, I went out to play with my colleague. I originally wanted him to take my Lifan car, which could save some fuel cost. After all, the current oil price is quite high. However, he insisted on taking his own BYD car. This matter cannot be forced, right? My car was bought earlier than Xiao Liu's. Thinking about when I bought my car, I almost couldn't bear being separated from it every day. Moreover, his car is more expensive than mine. As some people often say, "price determines quality". Judging from the automobile forum, I think he just wants to show off. Nowadays, young people only care about face and don't know how hard it is to make money.

On the way, Xiao Liu was always in front of me. After all, in his view, his car is a newly bought one, so it should be better than mine in power, speed and other aspects. I kept following him at a steady speed, neither too fast nor too slow. Suddenly, Xiao Liu slowed down. Could there be something wrong with his car? I thought, and unconsciously slowed down.

"Lao Li, let's compete in speed. Since we are still on the highway now, we don't have to worry about speeding," Xiao Liu poked his head out of the car window and shouted to me loudly.

"Our cars are not sports cars, they can't run very fast. But if you want to try, we can also give it a go. Anyway, we have nothing to do now," I could see what Xiao Liu was thinking, and I also had a competitive spirit. I wanted to see how my Lifan was performing now. So, we started our race.

Now I finally understand the use of the engine. It turns out that my Lifan 520 uses the same TRITECH engine as BMW MINI. Although its displacement is only 1.6 liters, its maximum horsepower reaches 115hp, and its maximum torque is 149Nm. This engine has achieved considerable success in the configuration of other domestic manufacturers' models. LIFAN's choice of this engine can be said to be a mature consideration, and it provides certain guarantees for the performance of the product power system. In actual driving, this point was verified. Just talking about the performance of the engine, it can be described as excellent. Whether it is the initial start-up or the continuous acceleration in the middle and high speed, the power output of the engine is relatively smooth and solid, and it performs moderately, fully capable of meeting the requirements of the 520 for power. The gearshift of the 520 is relatively clear, but the feeling of shifting gears lacks some operational feel, not very heavy. Although the stroke is short, it doesn't feel crisp and smooth. There is room for improvement in terms of feel. However, I am still handling it well.

However, the result was very disappointing. Xiao Liu's car had problems and later the engine couldn't even start. We had to call around and ask for a tow truck. I deeply regret allowing the race with him, turning my vacation into a car repair vacation. Xiao Liu also felt very embarrassed and our relationship became awkward.

"Lao Li, your car is really good. It seems that price does not necessarily determine performance," Xiao Liu was still a little embarrassed.

"Ha ha, yes. It seems that I have good luck. Therefore, when buying things in the future, performance should come first!" My Lifan finally made me proud. Low price, high performance, it is a must for car enthusiasts.
