A Ticket number:

Indicates the number of consecutive ticket sales for a ticket window or terminal, consisting of letters and six digits. Sometimes there are numbers in front of the letters, generally indicating the ticket window number.

Q Origin and destination:

Travel 400 kilometers below the Wei tablet body, 400 kilometers above the black body. Note that the final stop on the pass is not the first stop at which you get off.

C Train number: The specific train number you selected. Note that your first ride is listed in the pass. Please note the direction and position of the arrow.

D Seat number or shop number:

For most green cars and air-conditioned trains: Seat 1 must be by the window, and the numbers ending in "0", "4", "5" and "9" are also by the window. For the car where the conductor's office is located, the number ending in "3", "4", "8" and "9" is by the window.

For EMU trains:

In first-class cars, the window seat number is "multiple of 4" or "multiple of 4 +1". The number of the second class seat is "multiple of 5" or "multiple of 5 +1", and of course includes the number 1 seat.

B Ticket Office type:

Beijing A: Beijing Railway Station; Beijing B: Beijing West Railway Station; Beijing D: Beijing North Railway Station; Beijing E: Ticket Management Center (located on the main building of Beijing West Railway Station); Beijing F: Fengtai Station; Beijing AD: Beijing station sales point; Beijing BD: Beijing West Station sales point. Beijing South is not replaced by letters.

R Via: This section, available only on passes, shows the short name of the station you pass through between your origin and destination, in parentheses.

S Transfer method: Only the pass has this part. It shows the way to transfer after your transfer, which can be "universal passenger in the end", "universal fast in the end", "express in the end" three choices.

P Driving date and time: pay attention to the date of the cross-zero train, determine the date according to the driving time, and implement the 24-hour system.

E Ticket price: consists of RMB symbol and number, the number is accurate to the point.

Model F and transfer station: Indicate the model of your first ride and the transfer station in your pass.

G Validity: Note that this is the time period when you finally arrive at the station (because you may stop or change), not that the ticket is valid for a few days from the starting station.

H student tickets show "learning", as well as "children", "group", "residual" and "return" types.

I shading and watermark: The light can see the shading and watermark of "China Railway", "CR" and railway signs. Real tickets have the kind of reflective Chinese railway logo when viewed sideways in the light, but fake tickets don't.

J bar code part: The bar code of the real ticket is different, and the bar code of the fake ticket is the same. (Usually buy more than one ticket for comparison)

K station codes: the first 6 are the sale station codes; Digits 7-10 are the ticketing window codes.

L Digits 11 to 14 are the booking date (not exactly the sale date, it may be one day later than the sale date).

M 15-21 digits are the ticket number, which should be the same as the red digital ticket number in the upper left corner.

N Encryption check code, only the station machine can recognize the core part of the check ticket authenticity
