Will you miss me?

One day, when you call my number and the voice tells you that I've been disconnected. Please promise me not to be sad, not to feel lost; not to miss me, and especially not to remember someone like me.

One day, when your phone no longer rings frequently, please don't wait, don't expect, and most importantly, don't try to find me. Only when I see such a you can I leave with peace of mind.

One day, when there's no one by your ear saying annoying things or hating something, no one stubbornly claiming they're always right, no one rudely losing their temper at you.

No one bargaining to talk on the phone for a few more minutes, no one insisting you give kisses and hugs before hanging up. If such a person as me disappears, will you be sad?

One day, when your text message inbox no longer has anyone pitifully counting down the seconds until you return home, no one threatening to beat you if you don't talk, no one coquettishly calling you hateful or bad, no one regretfully blaming themselves after making a mistake. No one talking nonsense, no one sighing deeply, no one obediently following your words and then suddenly yelling at you. If you lose such a me, will you feel lost?

On such a day, I still hope you have a little sadness, a little loss, a little longing for me, just a little memory about me, really just a little is enough.

One day, when you turn on your computer and my avatar turns permanently gray, don't say I didn't keep my promise. It's because I feel tired, weary, and truly hurt.

One day, when there's no longer me in your life, please remember the good I've done for you, my capriciousness, my stubbornness; my tolerance, my care. My endless childlike talk, my crazy talk, foolish talk, the words I said while crying in sorrow, and sighed in helplessness.

But you must remember, although we are in different corners of the earth, we are under the same blue sky, stepping on the same green grass, breathing the same air. Perhaps here you can find my scent.

One day, when there's no longer me in your memory, don't forget every moment we spent together, don't forget what I like, what I hate, what I think is happiness, and what is pain. And I will never forget any fragment of memory about you, your habits, what you react negatively to, what you think is joy, what is sorrow.

In the world of feelings, there's no such thing as fairness. I won't count these. The two months we spent together will be the most beautiful memories of my life. I want you to remember what you promised me, but I was bad, naughty, and unfaithful. I left without waiting for you to completely forget me and start anew happily; without accompanying you until the pain ended, without waiting for you to truly become happy before I fled.

One day, when there's no longer me in your life, you must not remember my existence, my traces, because I'm afraid you'll feel lost, sad, and miss me. All this isn't because you like or love me, but because you've gotten used to my daily calls, daily messages, my quarrelsomeness, and my dependence on you. When one person gets used to another person being in their life, even without liking or loving, they will still feel lost, a little sad, and miss them, even though I am someone who likes to be jealous, has a big temper, is domineering, and cannot tolerate the person I like loving others. But I still hope you live better than me, hope to see you happy every day.

One day, when there's no longer me in your past and present lives, when the originally not-so-strong me faces this moment, I don't know how I will be? And you remain you, will you see my hidden troubles and regrets? Will you feel my constant presence by your side?

Even though I won't console you when you sigh anymore, won't share your sadness, won't accompany you through heartbreaks anymore, all the things I once did, you never noticed, never saw. When your memory, your life, your world no longer has me, I realize even clearer that at such a time, you won't have a bit of sadness, a bit of loss, a bit of longing for me, or any memory about me.

When this day comes, it means I'm truly desperate, truly heartbroken, truly exhausted. Because too many times, I've been pretending, always pretending to be indifferent, but do I really not care? And you, will you care about everything about me?

But I will feel very guilty, hate myself, because I became someone who doesn't keep promises. I promised you I would never leave you anytime. You told me, you don't know what you would do if one day I left you. I understand, actually it's all my fault. I shouldn't have let myself exist in your life and everyday routine. I should have been a silent admirer, silently bearing everything, secretly waiting for you, thinking about you. But I revealed everything, you knew, understood, and were touched, yet I left.
