Analysis of the Top Ten Chinese Heavy Truck Manufacturers by Sales Volume in the First Half of 2010 by Hubei Chengli Group

by hbszzyqcw on 2012-02-08 10:45:15

According to the data from the Direct Automobile Property Association, the top ten heavy truck manufacturers by sales volume in the first half of 2010 were: Direct Sinotruk, Shanxi Auto, Beijing Foton Auman, Beiben Heavy Trucks, JAC Motors, Dongfeng, Sanhuan Public Vehicles, FAW, Xin Dayan Automobile, and Zhejiang Phenox. Among these, Beijing Foton Auman showed the fastest growth in sales volume, rising from 13th place in the previous year's first half to 3rd place this year. The top ten manufacturers for non-complete heavy truck chassis sales were: Dongfeng, Direct Sinotruk, FAW, Shaanxi Auto, SAIC Iveco Cursor, Beijing Foton Auman, Hua Ling, XCMG Automobile, Qingling, and Hebei Changzheng. Notably, XCMG Automobile ranked 8th with a significant growth rate (924.00%), but its market share in the non-complete heavy truck chassis sector remains relatively small. This market is primarily dominated by the top seven companies, especially the top three—Dongfeng, Direct Sinotruk, and FAW (see Table 3, Figure 3). The top ten manufacturers for semi-trailer tractor sales were: FAW, Dongfeng, Beijing Foton Auman, Shaanxi Auto, Direct Sinotruk, Beiben Heavy Trucks, Hua Ling, SAIC Iveco Cursor, GAC Nissan, and Shanghai Huizhong.
