China Resources Paint Rights Protection Takes Effect, China Resources Paint Eventually Becomes "China Famous Trademark"

by jinqingyang on 2009-07-14 11:33:07

Huazhong Paint's Rights Protection Efforts Pay Off as Huazhong Paint Becomes a "China Famous Brand"

Xinhua News Agency - A "Famous Brand" refers to trademarks with high recognition and reputation at the national level. Compared to ordinary trademarks, the scope of legal protection is broader and more forceful. On April 25th, the State Administration for Industry and Commerce announced a list of newly recognized famous brands. Among them, Guangdong Huazhong Paint Co., Ltd., a leading enterprise in the coatings industry that has long been plagued by "piggybacking on famous brands," was also included, with "Huazhong Paint Huarun and its logo" being recognized as a famous brand. Fang Xin, Vice President of Huazhong Paint Group, stated that after being selected as a "China Famous Brand," Huazhong Paint will receive more legal support in protecting its brand and intellectual property rights.

It is understood that international protection of famous trademarks began after the establishment of the Paris Convention for the Protection of Industrial Property by European and American countries in 1925. The purpose of protecting famous trademarks is to safeguard the legitimate interests of high-value-added brands, protect the legitimate rights and interests of trademark owners, improve the intellectual property protection system, encourage independent innovation, optimize the innovation environment, maintain market competition order, and promote the rapid and sound development of the economy and society. After joining the Paris Convention for the Protection of Industrial Property in 1985, China began to fulfill its obligation to protect famous trademarks. The Trademark Law revised in 2001 and the subsequently promulgated Implementation Regulations of the Trademark Law provided clear legal basis for the recognition and protection of famous trademarks.

Experts pointed out that enterprises recognized as "famous trademarks" are relatively strong in technology, quality, and corporate brand strength within their industries, and enjoy good reputations, consumer appeal, and strong market competitiveness. Therefore, these enterprises are most likely to be counterfeited and infringed upon by illegal manufacturers. Taking Huazhong Paint as an example, in March this year, Huazhong Paint launched a campaign nationwide against "piggybacking on famous brands." The legal department of Huazhong Paint Group went to various provinces and cities such as Beijing, Jiangsu, Fujian, Sichuan, Yunnan, Hubei, and Jiangxi, cooperating with local industrial and commercial departments to carry out anti-counterfeiting and rights protection actions. As of March 16, 2009, industrial and commercial departments across the country seized more than 2,500 counterfeit Huazhong Paint infringing products, involving over ten types of suspected counterfeit products, with a case value exceeding RMB 300,000.

Fang Xin said: "In recent years, as Huazhong Paint products have become better and our company has grown larger, we have also felt increasing resistance to our development. We need to develop new technologies and products to grow the business while also fighting against counterfeit and substandard products to clean up the market environment. These factors have somewhat diverted the company's attention and severely damaged its economic interests and brand image. More seriously, counterfeit paint products pose a serious threat to consumers' lives and property safety."

It is understood that Huazhong Paint was founded in 1991. Through its commitment to quality and credibility, Guangdong Huazhong Paint Company has grown from a small factory into a leading enterprise with annual sales exceeding RMB 2 billion. Currently, under the Huazhong umbrella are brands such as "Huazhong Paint," "Ai De Paint," and "Century Pearl," among which the main brand represented by "Huazhong Paint" enjoys high recognition and product market share in China's coatings industry. After Huazhong Paint's brand was recognized as a famous trademark by the national administrative authority, it will further enhance its brand awareness and obtain special policy protection.

Regarding this, Fang Xin, Vice President of Huazhong Paint Group, stated: "'Huazhong Paint Huarun' trademark being recognized as a famous brand fully acknowledges and encourages Huazhong Paint's rights protection efforts, enhancing Huazhong Paint's international profile and brand influence. With the law as a powerful tool, Huazhong will have more confidence in future intellectual property rights protection and rights protection efforts. At the same time, we will seize this opportunity to make full use of the special protection policies for famous trademarks and spare no effort in brand building. While consistently adhering to quality and credibility, we will continue to pursue rights protection actions to provide truly high-quality products to consumers."
