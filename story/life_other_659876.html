My Love

This winter, I think of you in the cold. Are you cold? Is where you are the same as here? How nice it would be if you were by my side. That way, I could pull your little hand and without a word, tuck it into my chest, watching your warm smile...

But that's all in my imagination. If we were together, would you want to be? Would you want to hide in my arms during these cold days? Would you let me hold you tightly and keep you warm? Would you allow me to lift you up and spin you around in the snow?...

I don't know. No, perhaps I do know, but I'm afraid to face the answer...

I stay in the cold, thinking about you with sadness. Do you know that in my sight, there's only you, my dear? I love you, so why have you left me behind? Has my love put too much pressure on you? Or has my love taken away your freedom?...

Look at how this cold wind blows, making one forget to breathe from the chill. No, it's not just the cold wind; it's your indifference and distance that makes me forget to breathe. And still, I wear thin clothing, just as I persistently and stubbornly hold onto love...

My hands are icy, no matter how I try to warm them, they remain cold. I thought that if I became numb, my heart wouldn't hurt anymore. But I was wrong; the loneliness of missing you still pierces my heart...

Do you know what I both want to do yet am afraid to do? It's finding out and collecting news about you. I want to because I still deeply love you. Loving you means caring about everything about you, hoping you're doing well. I'm afraid because even a careless or intentional word about your love and marriage can shatter my heart into pieces...

Actually, I really want to know what the distant place you're in looks like. Why is it so attractive to you that you can completely forget about my kindness, concern, and deep affection for you...

Even if you are far away, my dear, I love you. Can you let me come to you? I want to walk through life with you. Even if I can't keep up with your pace, please let me try. Because on the path where I chase after you, I can smell your scent, and even from afar, it makes me feel happy...

In the familiar scent, I fantasize that we're still together. I feel that you're still beside me, warming me, giving my heart a home and my emotions a place to rest...

Leaving, for you, might be a liberating thing. Freedom seems to be all you desire. But you should know that when heaven gave me a whole heart full of love for you, it won't grant another heart as sincere as mine in this lifetime. Everyone needs peace and warmth in love because people need warmth and safety. If you refuse, I fear you'll regret it someday...

Whether for yourself or for me, I truly don't want us to have a day filled with regret and guilt. After all, you once loved me, didn't you?...

My dear, I miss you. In the cold of winter, my heart isn't frozen stiff, yet it still aches unbearably. In the passing years, my heart hasn't gone numb. You must know that the real me doesn't want you to just be an unforgettable thought in my mind...

It's very cold outside. Are you cold? Would you like a hug or a warm stove? My dear, if we could go back to the first time we met, I could hold you. I know that warmth would be enough to withstand the entire winter's cold. If we could love each other for a lifetime, why would we need countless reincarnations of forever? Because we don't know if there is an afterlife, we can only cherish this life!

Isn't it true? For me, being happily and sweetly together with you for one lifetime is enough. What about you?...

Wishing you a warm winter.
