Sword Network 3 Bashu风云 skill modification, physical service skill changes

by 173xsk on 2011-03-20 08:36:00

The changes here are based on the skill descriptions from the Jianwang 3 Experience Server. Since I haven't personally experienced them, the actual effects are unknown. Some skill changes may have been omitted or incorrect, so everyone is welcome to supplement and correct them.

The following are the modified skills:

Jianwang 3 Bashu Test Server Venom Skills Leak >>>

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云TianCe Skill Changes

Pierce

Pierce distance adjusted, group slow changed to single target slow?

Soul Sever

Soul Sever knockdown changed to stun.

Riding Freely

Riding Freely grants immunity to crowd control while mounting and cannot be dismounted.

Charge

Charge removes root effects from self.

Lift

Lift duration reduced to 5 seconds.

Hear Drum

Hear Drum enemy suppression effect removed.

Dragon Roar

Dragon Roar no longer requires suppression, changed to 8 second cooldown.

Steady as a Mountain

Steady as a Mountain changed to 6 minute cooldown, continuous effect changed to 8 seconds.

Dai Mai Brightness Reset Tiger Roar changed to reset Steady as a Mountain.

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云Shaolin Skill Changes

Earthquake Buddha

Earthquake Buddha changed from reducing internal defense percentage to reducing fixed internal defense value.

Living Beings Decree

New skill Living Beings Decree added.

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云Chunyang Skill Changes

Four Symbols

Four Symbols slow changed to 40%.

Tai Chi Wuji

Tai Chi Wuji damage changed, and under certain conditions increases critical hit chance and attack bonus.

Heaven Earth Wuji

Xuan Yi Wu Xiang changed. Details see link below.

Ten Thousand Swords Return to Their Source

Ten Thousand Swords Return to Their Source triggers twice under certain conditions.

Five Directions Exhausted

Five Directions changed to selected area, limited to maximum of 6 targets, slow changed to root for 9 seconds.

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云Wan Hua Skill Changes

Big Needle

Big Needle changed to 2 minute cooldown, increased blood consumption, increased MP recovery for teammates.

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云Qi Xiu Skill Changes

Busy Sounds Urgent Rhythm

Busy Sounds Urgent Rhythm changed to 3 seconds, many people already know this, no explanation needed.

Dark Gate

Due to the change in Busy Sounds Urgent Rhythm, Dark Gate changed to reset Full Hall Posture.

Sword Dance

Sword Dance changed from original 4 layers full heavy to 10 layers, each layer adds 2% healing effectiveness and damage, stacks one layer every 3 seconds standing still.

When running, loses one layer of Sword Dance per second, disappears after 10 seconds of running.

Additionally, if Sword Dance Buff is active while running, can use Mother Wave, Sword Spirit Universe, Jump Pearl Shock Jade.

Jianwang 3 Bashu风云Zang Jian Skill Changes

Crane Returns

Crane Returns changed to 25 second cooldown.

Surging Waves

Surging Waves 12 second cooldown, consumes 20 points of sword energy instead of previous amount.

Plum Concealment Fragrance

Plum Concealment Fragrance range increased to 6 feet.

Spring Moon Condensation

Spring Moon Condensation changed to 1 minute cooldown, lasts 12 seconds.

Pine Waves

Pine Waves root changed to immobilize.

Control Heaven

Tiger Run changed to reduce damage by 60%, everyone knows this, no explanation needed.

Strange encounter on the road with the Shushan Sect, delivering food by flying with a sword.
