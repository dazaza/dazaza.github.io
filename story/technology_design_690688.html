Chapter 979: Saving You Incidentally, but Lin Yushi's expression seemed somewhat reluctant. Qin Chao thought there would be a show, so he quietly followed and hid in a corner to watch.

Could it be that these two were about to have sex outdoors?

The one pulling Lin Yushi was a very handsome and sunny boy. However, his face now looked rather gloomy.

"Xiao Fengwu, let go! You're hurting me!" Lin Yushi frowned and said.

"Lin Yushi, what do you mean? Can't you give me another chance?" The boy gritted his teeth, loosened his grip on Lin Yushi's wrist, and asked.

"It's impossible."

Lin Yushi stood there, adjusted her glasses, "Xiao Fengwu, don't you already have a girlfriend? Why are you still coming after me?"

"Yushi, how can she compare to you!" Xiao Fengwu reached out to embrace Lin Yushi's waist but was dodged, "I just played around with her; there was no real feeling."

"Hmph, disgusting," Lin Yushi laughed, "Playing around on the bed, is that all men talk about? If women did the same with men, would you mind?"

"That, that's all in the past," Xiao Fengwu quickly added, "I promise it won't happen again."

"Sorry, there's no more chance," Lin Yushi's queen-like demeanor was evident, "It's not that I can't find a boyfriend, but I temporarily don't need one. Xiao Fengwu, please leave. As the Secretary-General of the Student Union, I have a lot of things to take care of today for welcoming new students."

After saying this, Lin Yushi turned to leave.

"Stop!"

Xiao Fengwu grabbed Lin Yushi's arm and pulled her towards him, shouting, "You think you're so high and mighty, don't you really think you're an untouchable goddess?"

"What are you doing? Let go of me!" Lin Yushi pushed Xiao Fengwu.

"What am I doing? Naturally, I'm going to deal with you, my so-called goddess!" Xiao Fengwu's eyes were bloodshot, "I, Xiao Fengwu, will taste what it feels like to have a goddess beneath me! We'll see if you're still so haughty when you're on the bed!"

"You bastard!" Lin Yushi struggled fiercely, "If this continues, I'll call for help!"

"Call for help? Call your sister!" Xiao Fengwu gripped Lin Yushi's neck and pushed her against a tree, "Go ahead and shout. If you shout, I'll strangle you to death and then whip your corpse! Then I'll strip you naked and hang you on this tree so all the new students can admire the grace of their goddess. Hahaha!"

This man seemed almost insane. Lin Yushi couldn't break free and was starting to suffocate.

Meanwhile, Xiao Fengwu panted heavily as he began unfastening Lin Yushi's belt. Qin Chao shrugged, knowing he had something to do.

Just as he was about to step in, suddenly a male student jumped out from the side.

This student's face bore red marks, clearly being Lin Yushi's bodyguard captain.

"Let go of Yushi! You beast!" This guy kicked Xiao Fengwu in the back.

"Phew!" Xiao Fengwu stumbled back three steps, releasing Lin Yushi.

"Cough... cough..." Lin Yushi finally managed to breathe, kneeling down half.

"You, are you alright!" The bodyguard captain rushed to support Lin Yushi.

"Ji, thank you..." Lin Yushi said gratefully.

"This is what I should do," Ji was thrilled to be able to play the hero saving the beauty, "My goddess, stand behind me. With me here, no one will dare to harm you!"

"Ho ho ho..." At this moment, Xiao Fengwu turned around, coldly looking at Ji.

"Xiao Fengwu, you beast, how dare you do such things to our goddess! Today, I must teach you a good lesson!" Ji advanced slowly, displaying the demeanor of a Taekwondo expert.

"Get lost!" Suddenly, Xiao Fengwu extended his right hand towards Ji, with a white light glowing in his palm.

"Boom!" Ji's body was hit as if by an invisible train, flying through the air until colliding with a tree behind him.

"Phew!" The tree trembled, Ji was badly injured, spitting out a mouthful of blood and falling to his knees.

"Ji, what's wrong with you!" Lin Yushi was startled, seeing Ji rush forward only to be thrown back.

And Qin Chao, who was watching from the side, furrowed his brow.

Angel power.

"In the presence of the great god's power, you are all insignificant." Xiao Fengwu stood there, laughing coldly, "Lin Yushi, called a goddess, yet you are merely a frog at the bottom of a well. I, Xiao Fengwu, am the one who has truly received divine power! Come over here!"

As he spoke, Xiao Fengwu waved his hand at Lin Yushi, and she felt as if an invisible arm was pulling her towards Xiao Fengwu.

"Ahh!" She screamed in fright.

"Xiao Fengwu, you..."

"I am a god! I am your god, Lin Yushi!" Xiao Fengwu embraced Lin Yushi's waist and laughed wildly.

"Let go of her..." Ji struggled to get up but was struck by another shockwave from Xiao Fengwu, flying far away and fainting completely.

"Xiao Fengwu, have you become a devil?" Lin Yushi's face was filled with terror, her glasses falling off.

"Devil? No no no, I am an angel, a true angel!" As Xiao Fengwu spoke, a pair of white wings sprouted from his back, though only one appeared.

He held Lin Yushi, kissing her neck.

"Let go of me!" Lin Yushi was extremely repulsed, pushing Xiao Fengwu hard.

"Bitch!" Xiao Fengwu reached out and tore off Lin Yushi's coat, revealing a thin blouse underneath, so transparent that even the pink bra inside could be seen.

Seeing this, Xiao Fengwu became even more excited. He couldn't resist wanting to rip off this blouse too, his hand already touching the edge of Lin Yushi's clothes.

"Hey hey hey, friend, even if you're desperate, why not go home? Having sex out here isn't exactly civilized, is it?" Just at that moment, a lazy voice came from a tree.

Xiao Fengwu immediately turned his head, seeing a boy in a black windbreaker sitting on a tree branch, looking at them.

It was him!

When Lin Yushi saw him, a glimmer of hope shone in her eyes. This boy seemed quite capable.

But soon, this hope faded. Even if he was strong, Xiao Fengwu wasn't human anymore...

"Is someone else ruining my fun? Well, I guess I'll just send you to hell then." Xiao Fengwu extended his right hand towards Qin Chao.

Qin Chao smirked, saying, "You consider yourself a god, but you're just a pitiful worm staring up at the gods."

"Die!" Xiao Fengwu's palm emitted a white glow.

But Qin Chao casually swatted it away with his right hand, as if shooing a fly. A nearby tree snapped in half.

Seeing his shockwave easily countered, Xiao Fengwu's eyes widened in shock.

"How can this be? How can you resist the power of a god!"

"What power of a god!" Qin Chao leaped down from the tree, covering a distance of several meters in an instant, landing before Xiao Fengwu and sneering at him, "I told you, you're just a pitiful worm!"

"Insulting me? Die!" Xiao Fengwu pointed at Qin Chao, and a cross descended from the sky, hurtling towards him.

This man seemed to know some angelic magic.

But he was still too weak.

Qin Chao lifted a single hand, and the massive descending cross stopped mid-air as if held by an invisible force.

"Who are you?" Xiao Fengwu finally showed signs of shock upon seeing Qin Chao's strength.

"I'm just a nobody." Qin Chao pinched his fingers, and the cross exploded, sending a gust of wind towards Xiao Fengwu and Lin Yushi. Lin Yushi's long hair was blown up, fluttering in the breeze. "Specialized in destroying false gods like you!"

"You die!" Xiao Fengwu didn't believe Qin Chao's words. He threw Lin Yushi aside, flashed forward, and slammed into Qin Chao's chest, trying to knock him back.

But Qin Chao didn't even move a step, only smirking coldly at the person rushing towards him.

"How can this be!" Seeing Qin Chao's feet remain firmly planted, Xiao Fengwu was shocked.

"Tiny insect, daring to call yourself a god!" Qin Chao extended a finger and tapped it against Xiao Fengwu's forehead.

"Boom!" Xiao Fengwu's body flew out, crashing through the woods and becoming a projectile that collided with a dormitory building behind him, leaving a dent in its wall.

The commotion drew the attention of many people.

"Huh? What happened over there?"

"So loud, are they setting off fireworks?"

"Fireworks during the day?"

A group of students looked towards the smoke-filled direction of the teaching building.

In an instant, Qin Chao appeared before Xiao Fengwu, grabbing his collar and entering space-time movement, returning them to the woods.

The disturbance had grown too large, and Qin Chao needed to resolve this quickly.

"Impossible, impossible..." Blood dripped from Xiao Fengwu's mouth as he coughed twice, glaring defiantly at Qin Chao holding him. "I am a god! The supreme god!"

"God my ass!" Qin Chao placed a hand on Xiao Fengwu's forehead, activating the demon core within him.

After entering the thunderstorm phase, Qin Chao could control the demon core well.

His palm pressed against Xiao Fengwu's forehead could effectively distinguish between soul and angelic power, extracting the latter cleanly from the former.

Simply put, if Qin Chao wanted to do that kind of thing with Su Ji now, he could.

However, he dared not attempt it, considering the risks involved with using the power of the Nine Dragon Ring to enter the thunderstorm phase. Any sudden situation would be disastrous for Su Ji, and he'd regret it deeply.

There was no reason to cause irreversible harm for fleeting pleasure.

But there was nothing to worry about concerning the person in front of him!

"Perish, false god!" Saying this, Qin Chao activated the demon core.

The angelic power was extracted from Xiao Fengwu's soul and forcibly absorbed into Qin Chao's body.

"No, stop it! I am destined to become a god!" Xiao Fengwu's eyes were filled with bloodshot veins as he screamed in pain.

"To hell with your god!" Qin Chao increased the power of the demon core.

The remaining bit of resistance from the angelic power crumbled instantly, all being absorbed into Qin Chao's demon core.

Xiao Fengwu fainted completely, collapsing onto the ground.

"He, what's wrong with him?" Lin Yushi asked worriedly from the side.

"Nothing, just that unbelievable power has been drained from him." Qin Chao withdrew his hand coldly.

"You, who are you... why save me..." Lin Yushi sat there, her clothes slightly disheveled, staring blankly at Qin Chao.

"Ho ho, I'm just an unwelcome person. And actually, I came to watch the outdoor action, saving you was incidental." Qin Chao glanced at her, smiled, ignored the girl, and walked away.
