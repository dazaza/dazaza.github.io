Gambling-addicted man kills his father, son and daughter before hanging himself

by baimao55537682 on 2012-03-05 16:37:05

According to the preliminary police investigation, on the evening of February 3rd, Xue Dexiong, aged 34, had an argument with his father over family trivialities. Afterwards, he stabbed his 61-year-old father to death with a pair of scissors. He then killed his 3-year-old son and 10-year-old daughter, who are suspected to have died from asphyxiation. After committing these crimes, Xue Dexiong hanged himself on the door of the living room in his home.

At around 2 PM on the 4th, two villagers, Mr. Pu and another villager, went to Xue Dexiong's house to retrieve a borrowed wood planing tool. They found the main gate closed tightly, and after knocking for a long time without any response, they noticed through the gate crack that a wooden ladder was propping up the back of the gate. The house was unusually quiet, making them suspicious. They then forcibly opened the courtyard gate, only to find the living room door also tightly shut. Although it wasn't locked from outside, they couldn't open it. Through the window, Mr. Pu and the other villager were shocked to see Xue Dexiong hanging by a rope around his neck inside the locked living room door, his feet dangling in the air. Nearby on the kang (a traditional bed), lay Xue Dexiong’s father, daughter, and son. There was no sound or movement in the room.

Thinking quickly, Mr. Pu and the villagers climbed through the window into the living room. First, they lowered Xue Dexiong from the rope, but found him already dead. On the kang, the elderly man and the children showed no signs of life. Upon examination, it was discovered that the elder's head had been stabbed with scissors, causing significant blood loss. Xue Dexiong’s daughter had a purple face, while his son’s face was pale. All three were confirmed dead. Subsequently, Mr. Pu and the villagers immediately called Xue Dexiong's relatives to conduct a second check on the four individuals. After confirming their deaths, Xue Dexiong’s brother, Xue Xionghui, dialed 110 to report the incident.

On February 4th, which was the thirteenth day of the first lunar month, when the villagers of Xueji Village in Luhexiang Township, Xihé County were still immersed in the joyous New Year atmosphere, a shocking message spread among the crowd watching a performance: Villager Xue Dexiong and his father, daughter, and son - a total of four family members - were found brutally dead at home.

From the villagers’ accounts, there were four people in Xue Dexiong’s household before the incident. His mother had passed away many years ago, and his wife died in a foreign place while working in Shanghai with him two years ago. At home, only his 61-year-old father and two young children remained. Xue Dexiong would only return home during farming seasons and the New Year period each year. The villagers' impression of Xue Dexiong was rather vague. He returned home from Shanghai just before the Lunar New Year, and no one noticed anything unusual about him compared to previous years. However, it is certain that he was a compulsive gambler, often gathering with some young people to play cards. This behavior had been consistent every year he returned home, leading to frequent education from both his father and older brother, Xue Xionghui. Xue Xionghui told reporters that this tragedy occurred due to gambling, as family members often quarreled with him over gambling issues. Unfortunately, this time the situation escalated tragically.

Our reporter in Longnan (Du Bin) reported that on February 3rd, a heinous crime against humanity occurred in Xueji Village, Luhexiang Township, Xihé County, Longnan City. Villager Xue Dexiong had an argument with his father at home, subsequently stabbing him to death with a pair of scissors, cruelly killing his young daughter and son, and then hanging himself. It was understood that the perpetrator was a habitual gambler, and the argument over gambling funds with his father was a crucial factor in triggering this incident.

Currently, the case is still under further investigation, and the Xihé police have launched a comprehensive investigation into the issue of group gambling.
