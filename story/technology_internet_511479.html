300 Students Wash Elders' Feet, 39-Year-Old Father Weeps (Photo)

by suppermane on 2011-10-19 11:09:05

Yesterday, at the football field of Jiangjin No.5 Middle School, students washed their elders' feet. Pictures in this edition are taken by reporter Yuanbin Xu. Students washed their parents' feet on the scene. Children hugged their parents and cried. Yesterday, on the playground of Jiangjin No.5 Middle School, more than 300 foot-washing basins were neatly arranged. More than 300 students from the second year of junior high school washed their grandparents', fathers', and mothers' feet under the guidance of professional foot-washing teachers. Many parents admitted that when their children's little hands touched their feet, they were moved. When the son touched the mother's foot callus, he cried. "Classmates, follow my command, pour water!" On the playground of Jiangjin No.5 Middle School, a professional foot-washing teacher held a microphone and stood on the stage to explain the process of health foot-washing. In the music, more than 300 students worked together, poured hot water from the water bottles beside them into the basins, then added cold water, adjusted the water temperature to a suitable degree, and waited for the next command. "Take off the elders' shoes and socks, put their feet into the water, gently push the top of the foot, the ankle, the sole in sequence, then slowly massage the calf from bottom to top..." the instructor commanded step by step on the stage. Zhengyu Yang from Class One of the Second Year touched his mother's foot, and tears flowed silently down his face. The 14-year-old said, "There are hard calluses on my mother's soles, and it feels a bit rough." Then, he couldn't say anything more, just wiped his tears with his shirt continuously. After washing his mother's feet, he immediately began to beat her legs and rub her shoulders. Seeing her obedient and sensible son, Liu Ying, the mother, couldn't help crying. Liu Ying said that she and her husband went out to work when Zhengyu was three years old, and only came home once a year during the Spring Festival. Normally, he lived with his grandmother and grandfather. Liu Ying happily said that this time her son washed her feet, which made her feel deeply. Liu Ying expressed that she decided to stay at home for her son. Hearing his mother say that she would stay at home to accompany him to grow up, Zhengyu Yang lowered his head and massaged his mother's feet while tears streamed down like rain, not daring to look at his mother's eyes. A 39-year-old father cried like a baby. At the scene, many parents were laughing at first, but soon fell silent. Many parents shed tears while enjoying their children's filial piety. Xiaojiao Yang's father, Jiongwu Yang, a 39-year-old manly man. When his daughter washed his feet, he cried like a baby. Jiongwu Yang said that before May this year, he had been working in Xiamen, and spent little time with his daughter. He just came home not long ago. When his daughter washed his feet, he suddenly felt sorry for his daughter. "Xiaojiao hasn't received good care from us since she was small, and has been fostered in her aunt's house. To avoid troubling her aunt, she helped with laundry and cooking..." Jiongwu Yang became more and more sad as he spoke, and his tears wouldn't stop. He said that his daughter usually lived in the school, and out of her weekly living allowance of 40 yuan, she saved part of it to buy fruits to take home on weekends to respect her aunt and uncle-in-law. Jiongwu Yang said that after coming back this time, he didn't want to work outside anymore. "I plan to find a job in Jiangjin, and I will shoulder the responsibilities of a father." A 36-year-old mother wants to be a "foot washer". The mother of Quanbi Cao, a student from the second year, is 36 years old. She also attended the foot-washing activity yesterday, and cried uncontrollably with red eyes. She said, "Thank the school for teaching children to respect their parents, and let the children understand their parents' hardships. To be honest, when I was a child, I never washed my parents' feet either. After this activity, I will go home and wash my parents' feet well." "This activity is very simple, but it has educated two generations of us," a student's mother said. Before coming, she didn't think it was a big deal, but after her son seriously washed her feet, she felt a special kind of family love and affection permeating the scene, making her discover a whole new son. As she spoke, her voice trembled a little, tears welled up in her eyes, and she kept stroking her son's head. On the scene, many parents also expressed that they would go home and be "foot washers" themselves, washing their parents' feet well, letting them enjoy the "treatment" of being parents. Sound: Let love flow freely in the instant when toes and fingers touch. "Feel the elders' calluses on their feet, sincerely feel the grace of raising by the elders, think about how to fulfill filial piety in the future, repay the elders, and let the filial piety heart last forever." Xiaowen Xue, the principal of Jiangjin No.5 Middle School said, "The foot-washing activity" is one of the activities of "Devoting Filial Piety to Parents" in the middle school. Principal Xue said that Jiangjin No.5 Middle School is a rural middle school, and eighty percent of the students' parents are not around all year round, and the students' academic performance and ideological quality vary greatly. In September 2007, the school conducted a "Family Affection Questionnaire" survey. The survey results showed that more than sixty percent of the students said that their relationship with their parents was "very ordinary", and nearly ten percent of the students thought that they had "nothing to talk about" with their parents. In order to improve the relationship between parents and students, the school conducts large-scale activities such as "Gratitude Chongyang Festival, Washing Parents' Feet" every Chongyang Festival. Through these activities, let parents understand their children's progress, let children understand their parents' hardships, let family affection flow freely in the instant when toes and fingers touch, and let both parties open their hearts, understand each other better, and cherish each other more.
