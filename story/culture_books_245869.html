Story One: The Night Witch

The will of a Chinese coffee tycoon is intriguing and foretells the possibility of him being a victim of murder. To uncover the truth, his family invites Song Yurito to provide expert advice in South Africa. Unexpectedly, the corpse seems to lie. Each member of the wealthy household has their own agenda, and another family member becomes a victim. Investigating alone, Song Yurito falls into a trap, leaving his life hanging by a thread...

Story Two: Mr. Wednesday

A bizarre murder occurs on Wednesday, leading police to believe that the serial killer "Mr. Wednesday" has resurfaced, causing panic throughout the city. Wu Qinghuai, tasked with solving the case, receives a deadly challenge from the murderer who threatens to harm someone he loves. Meanwhile, female journalist Meng Xi, secretly in love with Wu Qinghuai, also investigates the case. In this story, Dr. Song Yurito's life and emotions are both under threat.

At noon, the sunlight couldn't reach the ground as thin rain clouds occupied the sky, bringing persistent drizzle. The cemetery wasn't the overgrown wilderness one might imagine. The lawn was carefully trimmed, the green grass dotted with tiny water droplets, sparkling like emeralds. A path made of bluestone bricks led visitors to the tomb. Within an iron railing stood a stone mausoleum about ten feet high and over a thousand square feet, designed in traditional British style, silently displaying remnants of the colonial era. On the south side of the stone tomb, there was an iron gate flanked by stone pillars topped with two strange stone beasts, their hollow eyes gazing down at every visitor entering the tomb.
