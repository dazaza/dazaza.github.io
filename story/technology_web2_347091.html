This article comes from Taobao 8848 http://www.8848.cm Chapter 037 The Adorable Yu Shangtai. Ou Shicheng took large strides up the stairs and stood in the corridor on the second floor. It was very quiet there, and Yu Shangtai and the elder brother had disappeared just as they were speaking. He cursed angrily and quickly ran towards the end of the corridor. Could it be that they went to the elder brother's room...

This Yu Shangtai is really going too far this time. Knowing full well the elder brother's preferences, he still managed to provoke him. Regardless of the reason, it's unforgivable. This time, he can no longer indulge this young servant.

Ou Shicheng's face looked extremely displeased, and his pace quickened. Before reaching the elder brother's room, Ou Shicheng heard a scream from a secluded area—it was Yu Shangtai. He was startled, immediately stopped in his tracks, and quickly moved towards the secluded spot.

"Don't... " Yun Xiaoxiao was about to throw up. She covered her cheeks with both hands as Ou Shi Jing's hands roamed freely over her cheeks, his lips close to her ear.

"No one can see us. Play with me for a bit, I'll give you money. How much do you need..." Ou Shi Jing laughed arrogantly, "Among all the male servants in the villa, you're the most alluring! Your tender little face is truly seductive!"

"Master, you... have gone too far. I am not the kind of person you are looking for!" Yun Xiaoxiao angrily pushed away Ou Shi Jing's hand, cursing him silently in her heart: bastard, go away, or she wouldn't be so polite next time.

But Master seemed to be an utterly shameless guy. Not only did he not leave, but he also grabbed Yun Xiaoxiao's hand. Yun Xiaoxiao felt her body tilt, and instantly she was pulled into Master's arms.

From Ou Shicheng's perspective, it appeared as if Yun Xiaoxiao had thrown herself into the elder brother's arms. His brows furrowed, and he became angry. So, he shouted loudly and rushed forward.

"Yu Shangtai!"

Yun Xiaoxiao heard someone calling her name loudly; it was Ou Shicheng's voice. Help me quick!

Then, a large hand gripped her wrist. Before she could react, she was suddenly yanked, almost causing her to fall.

The elder son was also stunned. Upon closer inspection, it turned out to be his third younger brother, who then let out a cold snort.

"Younger brother, can't you just turn a blind eye to things like this? You have everything you want—women, money, status—but what about me? I have nothing, just a servant. It's just a game!"

"Do you really lack anything?" Ou Shicheng didn't actually want to confront his elder brother head-on, but the elder brother kept provoking him.

Annoyed, Ou Shi Jing retorted, "I don't have shit. Dad doesn't even glance at me properly. So, whatever he dislikes, I do it! I'm not gay, you know that. I'm just playing around!"

Ou Shicheng pushed Yun Xiaoxiao behind him, shaking with anger. "Elder Brother, your games are getting out of control. The more you act like this, the angrier Dad gets!"

Yun Xiaoxiao tightly held onto Ou Shicheng's clothes, watching helplessly as the man justified himself. At this moment, no matter how great their enmity was, she had to compromise. She hoped that the third young master would teach this scoundrel a good lesson.
