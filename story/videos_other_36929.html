"Come on, Yi Bing!" ---- Follow-up information

The dog is named "Yi Bing", and it is a dog with some Black Back bloodline. It was probably born in the spring of 2006. Its owner originally planned to sell all seven or eight of its siblings. "Yi Bing" ran out of the cage at night, hid in our store and never left. Our family used to run a mahjong parlor, so we named it "Yi Bing".

Less than two months after "Yi Bing" came to our home, an accident happened. One day, a hot pot restaurant in the village was moving. The driver of the bread van backed up and ran over the dog's hind legs with two wheels. Since I wasn't at home at the time, my son heard the惨叫声and quickly came out to see the dog being run over by the car. We were so busy saving "Yi Bing" that we let the driver escape. My family quickly put a splint on it, gave it Sanqi伤药pills, and sprayed Yunnan Baiyao aerosol. Since its pelvis and hind legs were completely crushed, the doctor could do nothing.

At first, "Yi Bing" was very afraid of motor vehicles and would hide under the refrigerator whenever a car came. Because of his leg injury, he could only crawl around dragging his two hind legs. So I often massaged him. Later, I found that "Yi Bing" always tried to stand up. He failed again and again, but he never gave up. Finally one day, he was able to support his body with his two not-so-strong front legs. As he grew day by day, his front legs became stronger. Now, not only can he run fast on his two front legs, but sometimes he also walks by supporting himself with his right hind leg.

It is also very humanistic. Whenever my son goes to school or I go to buy goods, it will run fast with its two very strong front legs to send us to the entrance of the village. The speed is so fast that it is often unbelievable. It still remains loyal to its master as before.
