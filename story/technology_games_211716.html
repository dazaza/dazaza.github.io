Written for my dearest angel_Wanwan (2)

by lk0gamel on 2009-07-21 04:03:48

Since then, Wannan finally recovered her lark-like laughter and began to follow me around like before. She still managed to drive me half crazy from time to time, but there was one thing different from the past: when I wanted to hold her, she wouldn't refuse me anymore and became very obedient.

Everything was developing in the direction I hoped for, but my heart grew heavier and more distressed, as if being pressed by a big rock, making it hard for me to breathe.

Because I never dared to say those words to her, the words that expressed my desire to truly take care of her and protect her, not as a friend, but another kind of relationship.

I lacked confidence; I feared she would reject me, which would make me fall apart.

The intelligent Wannan seemed to start noticing my changes. She asked me what was wrong, and I said I wasn't feeling well. She advised me to rest, but I said no, I wanted to accompany her. She made a smug expression with drooling and bounced over to me, surprisingly saying, "I want a hug."

"God! The little enchantress' spell is coming again." I barely restrained the nosebleed about to happen, picked her up in amazement, and rode the wolf to gallop wildly in the sky. Looking at her expression while stroking my head, I felt even more painful.

Good girl, do you know what I'm thinking? Can't you be any smarter? Can't you guess my intentions?

But Wannan seemed not to notice, curling up quietly in my arms like a little cat, motionless.

Maybe the string was too tight, or maybe I was neurotic due to distress. With my impulse, our first quarrel came.

That day after I logged on, I found her at Eighteen Li Inn. Oh, she couldn't even beat the imprinted monsters, why did she go to Eighteen Li Inn? I immediately typed and asked her, "Stupid girl, why are you going there? To commit suicide?" She quickly replied, "Hehe, just committing suicide, dying is straightforward anyway, since no one cares about me." "Who says? If you die, I'll die with you. We'll be a pair of lovebirds, how's that? (This is the biggest hint I can give normally)." Wannan sent an expression of disdain, "Dream on!" "Sweat, Miss, your cruelty will hurt my male self-esteem. Can't you consider marrying me?" Wannan sent an expression of beating me up, "You talk nonsense." "Hehe! Seriously, why did you go to Eighteen Li Inn? Did you find some good scenery?" "Nope, someone brought me." "Ah? Who?" "Not telling you." "..." "You go play first, I'll find you later." "Okay."

I went killing monsters with full of doubts, absent-mindedly. While killing, a terrible thought suddenly came to mind. Could it be... "No, I have to check!" I immediately gave up leveling up and rushed to her location as fast as possible. Seeing the scene in front of me, my heart turned cold.

Wannan sat on a high big stone, below which a high-level male spearman was brushing large numbers of monsters. He must be at least level 55, his body glowing blue-purple, indicating his equipment was already +8.

This was my strong rival (although he might not consider me, who was only level 50, as an opponent)! I saw him laughing at 'my' Wannan while fighting, almost wanting to beat him up immediately. However, I hadn't lost my mind yet. Because I knew that if I went out, the one getting beaten up would definitely be me. At that moment, if Wannan saw me lying on the ground, I would lose all face. No way would I let my rival have a chance to show off in front of my beloved.

I quietly left without them noticing, returned to the city, and said to Wannan, "That man is really strong. Do you think you don't need me to bring you along anymore?" Wannan was surprised, "Wow! Did you see that? Where were you? How come I didn't see you?" "I've returned. You two probably don't need me around anymore." Wannan (covering her mouth and laughing), "Why does the boss smell sour?" "Is that so?" It seemed Wannan noticed something was wrong with me. She stopped teasing and seriously said, "What's wrong? Are you mad? That person is just my friend. He was only brushing money today. I didn't see you, so he brought me for a while, really." I replied indifferently, "Oh, is that so? Alright, you two continue, I'm going to sleep." "What are you doing?" She seemed to be a bit angry too. I sent the same big laugh expression as that man, "Nothing, I'm not your husband, you don't have to explain to me." "You...""What? Anyway, with him bringing you, you'll surpass me soon. Maybe I'll need you to bring me then." "Fine! I didn't expect this from you, acting so unreasonable. You're right, you're nothing to me, why should I explain to you?"

She was really angry. Seeing her agitated words, I started to regret, but I didn't apologize because I strangely needed to vent. Why?

Wannan continued, "Am I not allowed to have friends? Am I only allowed to play with you? If that's the case, I regret knowing you, I misjudged you. Goodbye." I cried! Wasn't it just a little anger? How did it ignite a volcano? I sent her a few cheeky smiles, then changed to tearful ones, but Wannan fell silent and didn't speak to me anymore. I went on QQ, sent her a video call, but got no response. Then I called her phone, but no one answered. Sigh! Wannan, I know I was wrong, but please don't be serious!

I logged off with an extremely depressed mood and didn't go online all day.

The first thing I did the next morning was to call her, but it was the same situation. This usually gentle girl had such powerful anger, making this tall guy tremble. But then again, if it wasn't my Wannan, I wouldn't even look at her. After all, this angry person happened to be our angel, happened to be the charming girl I liked.

After logging into the game, I didn't talk to her. I figured she must have seen me, but women rarely take the initiative to seek peace when they're angry. Although Wannan was a good girl who disliked having tantrums, once she got angry, she was the same.
