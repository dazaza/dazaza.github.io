[Elf Legend Traditional Chinese CD Version][Real-time Strategy]

by qinrui on 2006-11-08 19:05:26

Since ancient times, the magic crystal has been protecting the world from chaos. Somewhere in the center of the world, it creates a protective shell for all living things around it. Outside the protective shell, real life is distorted: there is no sunlight, and everything in life is incredible, and magic doesn't work there. The environment inside the protective shell is more livable: the sun shines, there's plenty of rain, rivers flow gently, grasslands are lush.

At first, the internal world was divided into three parts - the Human Kingdom, the Elf Kingdom, and the Gnome Kingdom. After the Gnome Kingdom moved to an underground palace, the emperor of the Human Kingdom and the empress of the Elf Kingdom swore that their countries would never covet the land of the Gnome Kingdom, and these lands would be "forever free". Gradually, some settlements appeared in these places, with residents of various races living peacefully and tranquilly.

However, after a period of time, a magician appeared in the Elf Kingdom and gained the trust of the Elf Empress. He gave the empress a magical crown, which made her decisive in handling affairs and became a true monarch. The country reached unprecedented prosperity and won the trust of other ethnic groups.

The Elves suddenly remembered that they came to this world because of a disaster called the "Great Earthquake". Under the leadership of the magician, the Elves began to formulate plans to return home, which required the destruction of the magic crystal. A cruel war began. The Elves resolutely advanced towards the center of the world, and it seemed inevitable that the center of the world would be breached. At this moment, a mercenary appeared in the camp of the Human Kingdom and quickly became a general due to his commanding genius. Under his command, the Human Kingdom began to force the Elves back to their own territory and started peace negotiations.

The protagonist is a graduate of the National Magic School. According to tradition, each graduate must undergo trials organized by the Combat Magic Union. This union consists of a vast network of magic towers spread throughout the entire internal world. The protagonist accidentally learned that the attack of the monsters could only be stopped by a treasure of the Gnomes, but this treasure had been lost long ago. Time was running out because the magician already knew about the threat. It was necessary to quickly find the treasure of the Gnomes, destroy the magician's black castle, and protect the integrity of the world.
