The first MMORPG operated by Beijing Quwan Technology: Bai Bian Ling Jing

by geekzhang on 2008-06-19 23:19:07

On June 19, 2008, the first MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) operated by Beijing Quwan Technology - "Magic Baby" was launched in anticipation of many players. After a three-month dormant period and incorporating valuable feedback from both new and old players, Beijing Quwan Technology finally confirmed that this internal test would be the ultimate non-deletion test! This means that the accounts from this internal test will be retained until the public test, allowing players to start their Magic Baby journey with peace of mind.

What can make you feel icy cool refreshment in the scorching summer? What can bring you a serene joy amidst the hustle and bustle of the city? Perhaps it's a pure and beautiful memory, or an upcoming adventure journey, or maybe it's the simplest and purest love in each of our hearts... So, take action!

As a large-scale fashion Q-version pet game, "Magic Baby" features elegant yet lavish game graphics, versatile cartoon character designs, and ultra-cool magical visual effects rendered with modern online game production technology, painting a splendid magical world for the vast number of players.

Compared to traditional online games, in "Magic Baby", players not only experience the fun of self-challenge and growth but also can domesticate various monsters as pets, leading them into battle side by side. Moreover, the most unique feature of the game is that players can synthesize these pets to evolve them into more powerful companions. Through the pet synthesis system, you can even gradually evolve your pets into the latest, most distinctive ones. Furthermore, these pets will inherit the personality traits of their owners. ● Fresh and cute character designs, dozens of stylistically diverse scene maps, complemented by pleasant background music, create a colorful and intricate game world.

Each character has its own unique weapon, skills, and magic. Characters have four ability values: strength, agility, accuracy, intelligence, plus corresponding resistance capabilities against attribute attacks. In "Magic Baby", characters have five major skills: evasion, strong attack, confusion, counterattack, HP absorption, which are automatically obtained as the character level increases. As the character gains skills, their levels rise, and the skill levels also increase.

● Completely different from traditional pet-based online games, the MCC (Multi-Character Control) operation system makes the game more operational and competitive.

The MCC operation system, or Multi-character Control, allows players to operate multiple characters simultaneously. The MCC system enables players to use close-range combat-strong pets to charge into battle while using healing-skilled babies to replenish themselves, fundamentally improving the regrettable situation of being unable to attack and defend simultaneously in previous online games. The MCC system also supports players to form pet combat clusters with different skill combinations, playing different roles in leveling up or castle battles, greatly enhancing the playability and fun of the game. Squad battles further allow "Magic Baby" to break through the constraints of traditional online games, possessing the strategy of real-time combat.

● Hundreds of uniquely shaped super-cute pets and an original pet synthesis system, along with a complementary pet-raising mechanism, let players fully experience the quality and true essence of pet games.

The concept of pet growth stages, emphasis on pet growth systems, and growth randomness introduced in "Magic Baby" makes pet growth in the game infinitely varied. When players combine two differently performing pets, they can produce a completely new pet with higher abilities. The synthesis methods for higher-level pets vary and are relatively rare and precious. In "Magic Baby", players can also transform into various cute monsters, making themselves the fourth pet. Transformation not only enhances abilities but also avoids active attacks from other monsters during the transformation period.

● As the first real-time combat pet online game, the guild castle war not only lets players fully experience the thrilling excitement of real-time strategy but also satisfies mainstream users' deeper gaming experiences.

When players reach level 30, they can establish a guild through the family administrator. After forming a guild, they can occupy their own city and collect taxes. Players who form a guild can initiate a castle war. If the attack is successful, then the guild can occupy this city, and the guild leader can adjust the tax rate and enjoy the benefit of collecting taxes in that city until it is taken over by another guild.

● Ingeniously designed refinement and resonance systems fully demonstrate the unprecedented creativity of the game world, allowing players to enjoy growth pleasures in character, pet, and item cultivation.

If players feel their equipment is not perfect enough, they can refine their equipment to obtain various attributes. Players can prepare a set of devastating strength gear or a set of blood bull gear that extends combat endurance according to their needs or cultivation path. If players want to use pets as front-line defenders and focus on skills as rear support, they can wear suits that add wisdom and MP limits. To make their pets stronger, players can use resonance elixirs on their equipment to add resonance effects for certain pet types. Wearing such equipment will enhance the attack and defense capabilities of that type of pet.

The sprites change endlessly, and the brilliance is about to be presented to you! Players in "Magic Baby OL" will no longer fight alone. Besides your beloved BB companion, there are many other sprite friends filled with magical dreams just like you. Whether you want to become a member of the King's Temple Knights or a top student at the Royal Magic Academy, don't forget to be your most fashionable, ever-changing sprite; don't forget, the excitement continues.
