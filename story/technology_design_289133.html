Yishui was still stunned, but Zhn Nan and Yifei each grabbed a chair and rushed over, smashing it wildly at those people. Instantly, the group scattered, all avoiding the two people with the chairs. "Shit! Who dares to come over, if I leave you alive, I'm your grandson!" The one speaking was Zhu Nan. Yi Shui didn't expect Zhu Nan to have this side. Just now, Zhu Nan's fierce manner was extremely similar to Zhao Feng's. Yi Shui thought that's why Zhu Nan could figure out Zhao Feng's background. In fact, Yi Shui should have realized that people like Zhu Nan are mostly righteous yet evil, meaning they walk on both sides of the law. Otherwise, how could they possibly make such a big name for themselves?

Yishui took the opportunity to go over and help Li Xiang up. Li Xiang actually still painfully smiled at her despite being seriously injured and unable to stand.

Sun Ming and his group glared at Yishui and others red-eyed. Although they were still looking fierce, it was clearly visible that they were intimidated. No matter what, they could see that the other party wasn't someone they could easily provoke.

Zhu Nan threw away the chair and pointed at those people: "Whoever wants to kill themselves, just block us!" After saying that, he led Yishui and others out. Li Xiang was carried out by Yi Fei. Just as they were about to leave, Sun Ming suddenly lunged at Zhu Nan with a dagger. Yishui quickly pushed Zhu Nan away, and the dagger tore open her down jacket, white feathers splashed out and fluttered onto the ground.

Zhu Nan became angry, went up and threw Sun Ming to the ground, then grabbed his neck and lifted him up against the wall. Zhu Nan's strength was great, Sun Ming tried hard to pry Zhu Nan's hand off his neck, his face turned red from being choked. Those who came with Sun Ming were scared stiff, but when some wanted to help Sun Ming, they were frightened back by Zhu Nan's furious eyes. Yishui also panicked, took out her mobile phone and called Xiao Mingchuan.

The whole room was in a standoff like this. Sun Ming was always pressed against the wall by Zhu Nan, suffering greatly, while Zhu Nan kept glaring at him fiercely. Zhu Nan later told Yi Shui: "What I regretted at the time was not protecting you."

Xiao Mingchuan arrived, bringing more than 10 strong young men. As soon as he entered, he first asked if Yi Shui was alright. Yi Shui said she was fine. Then Xiao Mingchuan saw Sun Ming, went over and said to Zhu Nan: "Brother, take a break." Zhu Nan then let go, and before Sun Ming could catch his breath, he was slapped by Xiao Mingchuan and teeth scattered everywhere. Following were his so-called "friends", they were all beaten by Xiao Mingchuan's subordinates. The next scene would probably be unforgettable for Sun Ming and others for a lifetime. Their resistance was completely useless. Xiao Mingchuan's subordinates were all professional fighters, dealing with them was a piece of cake. Soon, Sun Ming and others were beaten so badly that they screamed and had nowhere to hide.
