On the Eve of the Spring Festival, I drift off to sleep with thoughts of you.

The midnight of the Spring Festival Eve has passed. The once bustling and splendid night sky is now tranquil, restored to its rightful peace. I turned on my computer, and suddenly, I saw your figure in my online space... You are still as elegant and refined, as gentle and affectionate...

At this moment, my heart seems to have stopped beating, my blood has begun to coagulate, unable to breathe or think, my mind a blank...

After a while, I couldn't help but touch the you on the screen with my hand. Through that cold screen, I actually felt a warmth... I know that's your body temperature, transmitted through the cold tips of my fingers into my heart. A warm current surged through my body, my heart swayed with an uncontrollable impulse. Though I sat there, my soul had already taken steps driven by desire, crossing vast distances of time and space, traveling towards your side... I've woven myself a beautiful dream...

I came to your side, standing before you... You looked at me in surprise, then crazily embraced me. We gazed deeply at each other, our eyes filled with tears. I wrapped my arms around your neck, you murmured my name, lightly kissing my trembling lips filled with longing... The feeling of happiness, once so distant, and those bitter tears flowed down from the corner of my mouth to my tongue, sweet yet bitter; your tender caress made my heart beat irregularly, my blood boiling with passion... Thus, on the cold, snowless Central Plains earth, under the dark moonless night, two souls consumed by the fire of love tightly embraced, indulging in the marshes of love...

The sentimental clock has struck three times, endless tears soaking the black keyboard. What are you doing at this moment? Can you sleep peacefully? I know you can't. You'll be thinking of me just as I'm thinking of you... We both tried to forget each other, but the result was the opposite. The more I want to forget you, the more I think of you. Every corner of my heart is filled with you, every day I miss you; the more I want to leave you, my heart stays in your embrace unwilling to return, I can't control myself.

I know it's the same for you. Even though you appear in different spaces, I can understand the language of your heart. Because your presence has merged into my blood, we share an extraordinary connection. Every word flows with thoughts of me, every article stained with blood and tears. I read them every day, listening to the spring of your heart, my heart tormented as if roasted by fire, the pain unbearable. Do you know what I prayed for at that moment? I prayed to find a drug that could erase feelings, after taking it, I would forget everything. That way, you would be happy, you would be content, you wouldn't be in such pain, you would forget me... Because I can't bear to see you cry, can't bear to see you sad, can't bear to see you lose sleep every night for me, can't bear to hear your blood-curdling calls for me, can't bear to see you suffer so much...

They say heaven has feelings. If heaven has feelings, why let us meet but not allow us to see each other; let us love but not allow us to stay together; let us love so profoundly but not allow us to possess each other... If heaven is heartless, why did I encounter you, who I've waited for a thousand years, in the vast sea of people; why did I feel so familiar and close when I first saw your words; why did I become inexplicably infatuated even before meeting you, proud as I am...

Dear! Do you know how much I love you, how much I miss you... No matter how far apart we are, you're always in my heart. If you're happy, I'll be glad for you; if you're in pain, I'll be heartbroken...

The morning star on the horizon keeps twinkling, I know it's your teary eyes. The torment of yearning has made us shed countless tears, our hearts torn apart by our blood-curdling calls...

The long night of the Spring Festival Eve is almost over, and I continue to caress you behind the screen, feeling your presence, leaning on your thoughts, shedding sorrowful tears, drifting off to sleep in front of the computer...
