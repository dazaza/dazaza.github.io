On January 9, 2012, the award ceremony of the sixth "People's Social Responsibility Award" hosted by People's Daily Online was grandly held in Beijing. The theme of this year's selection was "Innovation and Transformation". Ten institutions and ten individuals respectively won the "People's Social Responsibility Enterprise Award" and the "People's Social Responsibility Outstanding Contribution Figure Award".

The Sixth People's Social Responsibility Award focuses on innovation and transformation

This year's People's Social Responsibility Award evaluation activities are evaluated with "Responsibility, Innovation, Transformation, Contribution and Influence" as evaluation indicators. Responsible persons of major media and well-known figures in society serve as evaluation experts. Nearly tens of millions of netizens actively vote, and the highest number of votes is nearly 3 million. Since 2006, the "People's Social Responsibility Award Evaluation" has been for five sessions, many excellent enterprises and individuals have received honors. When they show their dedication and integrity, and become examples for society to learn from, the social influence of the "People's Social Responsibility Award" becomes increasingly profound.

Li Junru, a standing member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and former vice president of the Central Party School, said in his speech that looking at today's world and contemporary China, in the political field, the responsibilities of the people and the government have become important elements in the design of democratic politics together with the rights and obligations of the people and the government; In the economic field, corporate social responsibility has become an indispensable factor in market competition along with product quality, service reputation, patents and brands of enterprises. He said that there have been many enterprises and individuals who performed excellently in the practice of social responsibility in the past sessions of the "People's Social Responsibility Award". These excellent representatives set benchmarks for the progress of the times, enhance national soft power, and inject more "warmth" elements into the rapidly growing society. They are our role models.

Liao Gang, President and Chief Editor of People's Daily Online, introduced that the evaluation activities of the "Sixth People's Social Responsibility Award" began preparation in August 2011, and officially started on October 1st. It has attracted widespread attention from all sectors of society since then, and the number of votes has reached a new high in recent years among the evaluation activities of People's Daily Online. From the results, the outstanding contribution figures selected cover three generations: the old, middle-aged and young. There are generous entrepreneurs as well as ordinary laborers who contribute silently; The ten People's Social Responsibility Enterprises recommended include some brands familiar to the public, and some latecomers discovered by netizens.

"This fully shows that whether in the high temple or far away in the rivers and lakes, as long as they make contributions to social progress, they will be supported by the public." Liao Gang said.

The People's Daily and People's Daily Online jointly launched the "Public Welfare 2012 - Action Power" event

2011 was the transparency accountability year of China's public welfare charity, the year when Chinese society paid special attention to the information transparency of the charity cause, especially the public welfare charity organizations, and also the year when the government had the greatest supervision over the public welfare charity cause, especially the information transparency.

At this year's award ceremony, the "Public Welfare 2012 - Action Power" event jointly launched by the Public Welfare 2012 column of the People's Daily and the Public Welfare Channel of People's Daily Online was officially launched. The event will be prominently featured on the sixth page of the People's Daily every Wednesday and on the homepage of the Public Welfare Channel of People's Daily Online. It aims to promote the reshaping of social credibility in China's public welfare cause with a broader vision, more enthusiastic attention, and more persistent reporting. Promote the whole society to pay attention to public welfare and devote itself to public welfare, let love turn into real good deeds, use the power of action to promote social progress.

The "Public Welfare 2012 Declaration" stated that watching will not change indifference, only action has hope.

Breaking monopoly and opening up new fields, non-ban means entry to boost further

"Encouraging the development of the real economy requires practical measures in breaking monopolies," Zhang Xiaojing said. Currently, investment channels for China's real economy are still narrow, and private capital finds it difficult to enter many monopolized industries. For example, in the oil and petrochemicals industry, private capital can actually enter, but since CNPC and Sinopec almost monopolize all oil resources, even the construction of "non-public" gas stations must be registered with them. This restrains private enterprises from entering the upstream of the industry. How can private capital loosen its limbs?

Nowadays, the private economy often encounters "glass doors" and "spring doors." For example, although some industries appear open on the surface, the design, construction, and management of related projects are controlled by subordinate institutions or companies of the original monopoly department. Would they willingly give up the "fatty meat" right in front of them? Thus, private enterprises often face the situation of "surface welcome, threshold restriction." A person in charge of Tianjin Membrane-Tian-Membrane Technology Company said that they had once suffered discrimination during the bidding process and were informed that "some projects only allow specific products to bid."

Only through open competition can economic vitality be stimulated. Zhou Tienong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, pointed out that the reform of monopoly industries lags significantly behind, causing unfair competition, which has become one of the key issues urgently needing to be solved to accelerate the transformation of economic development mode. The person in charge of Membrane-Tian-Membrane Company suggested that private capital should enjoy equal policy conditions and status with state-owned capital, open the market to private capital, and give private capital new space. Not only should private capital be allowed to enter newly opened fields, but also survive in these new fields. Especially, "pseudo-openness" should not be implemented, and private capital should not be used merely as decoration or to save face.

Monopoly areas often have intricate ties with government departments, and dealing with government departments has become a headache for many private entrepreneurs. An entrepreneur from Shandong engaged in furniture manufacturing said: "Sometimes we want to handle affairs with certain government departments, being humble and respectful, but the result may still not be achieved." Industry insiders believe that if the government sincerely encourages private capital to enter monopoly industries and public investment, it must strengthen its service awareness and reduce the cost of handling affairs for private enterprises. Otherwise, no matter how many attractive fields are opened, private capital dare not enter on a large scale.
