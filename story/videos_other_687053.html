Methods of psychological adjustment to overcome insomnia:

One, maintain a positive and contented good attitude. Have a full understanding of social competition and personal gains and losses, avoid psychological imbalance due to setbacks.

Two, establish a regular daily life system, keep the normal sleep-wake rhythm.

Three, create favorable conditions for falling asleep. For example, take a hot bath, soak your feet, drink a glass of milk half an hour before bed, as long as you adhere to it, you will establish a "conditioned reflex for sleep".

Four, moderate physical exercise during the day helps with sleeping at night.

Five, develop good sleep hygiene habits, such as keeping the bedroom clean, quiet, away from noise, avoiding light stimulation; avoid drinking tea or alcohol before sleep.

Six, self-regulation and self-suggestion. You can play some relaxing activities, or repeatedly count numbers, sometimes just a little relaxation can help fall asleep faster.

Seven, limit daytime sleep time. Except for the elderly who can have a proper nap during the day, avoid napping as it will reduce the desire and duration of sleep at night.

In addition, for some more severe patients, under the guidance of a doctor, short-term and appropriate use of sleeping pills or small doses of anti-anxiety and depression agents may achieve faster and better treatment results.

One. Experience formula

Massage a group of acupoints: Baihui, Taiyang, Fengchi, Yifeng, Hegu, Shenmen, Neiguan, Waiguan, Zusanli, Sanyinjiao, Yongquan. The number of massages depends on the severity of insomnia, fewer times for mild insomnia, more times for severe insomnia. After massage, immediately choose a comfortable sleeping position, you can fall asleep in about 10 minutes. If you still cannot fall asleep, continue massaging once more to fall asleep.

2. Drink a glass of water every morning

After washing up every morning, drink a glass of warm water, which is also effective for colds, heatiness, constipation and other diseases.

3. Two pillows, one soft and one hard, for good sleep

It's suitable to use two pillows, each not exceeding 8 cm in height, and preferably the top one being soft and the bottom one being hard. The top soft pillow is convenient for adjusting position for comfortable sleep. The bottom hard pillow is mainly used for supporting height. Using such pillows makes sleep comfortable and relieves fatigue quickly.

5. Rubbing Yongquan acupoint treats insomnia

When lying in bed and unable to fall asleep, place the sole of one foot on the big toe of the other foot, rub back and forth until the sole of the foot feels warm, then switch to the other foot. Alternate between them, focusing your brain's attention on your feet. Over time, you'll feel tired and sleepy, wanting to fall asleep. Persisting over the long term also has health benefits.

6. Pillow filled with Chinese cedar leaves promotes sleep

Pick some Chinese cedar leaves, wash and dry them, fill a pillow. Placing the cedar leaf pillow under your head gives off a fresh scent, making you feel comfortable and achieving a calming and sleep-inducing effect.
