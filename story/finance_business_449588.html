Tenth Place: KID

Lost his father at a young age. To avenge his father, he had to give up a bright future and take on the unpromising career of a "thief," frustrating; even as a thief, he couldn't be a suave one, always having people like Shinichi Kudo and Hakuba Saguru around to ruin his plans and steal his thunder, frustrating; this is still when he has good luck. When things go badly, he can only waste time with Inspector Nakamori Ginji’s incompetent police force, almost demeaning, frustrating; despite having so many female supporters, he has to stay by the side of Nakamori Chizuru, who is both aggressive and jealous, constantly having to tell lies to placate her, frustrating.

Ninth Place: Dr. Agasa

Not very famous despite his age, living among a group of dramatic characters, obviously being looked down upon, frustrating; unable to do serious work, spending every day tinkering with strange gadgets, being seen as a freak, frustrating; though clearly an old bachelor fraudster, he still has to hang out with a bunch of kids all over the place, what will others think? Frustrating; jokes that are supposed to be funny never get laughs when I tell them, frustrating.

Eighth Place: Inspector Megure

Always getting scolded by superiors above and criticized by the public below, it's hard to be in the middle, frustrating; almost becoming a synonym for "incompetent," not to mention people like Yuusaku Kudo and Shinichi Kudo, but also always having Mouri Kogoro, the unqualified detective, stealing the limelight, frustrating; the police under him are all handsome men and beautiful women, when they appear, who would still look at me, frustrating; because of wearing a hat, no one noticed my new hairstyle, frustrating.

Seventh Place: Officer Takagi

All the dirty work, tiring work, and heavy lifting in the department falls on me, sometimes working hard without receiving praise, frustrating; also have to endure Officer Shiratori's cold remarks, frustrating; what is Officer Sato thinking? Guessing riddles every day is exhausting, frustrating; face gets red easily, and the words you want to say just won't come out, frustrating.

Sixth Place: Ai Haibara

Originally a mysterious member of the Black Organization, "pfft" suddenly became a little elementary school kid, having to pretend to be innocent while hanging out with real elementary school kids every day, frustrating; even if she could enjoy some peace after returning to youth, GIN and VODKA keep following her like fans, disturbing her peace moment by moment, frustrating; such a beautiful girl, having to look at Dr. Agasa's old face every day, isn’t that frustrating; cannot confess to the person she likes, hiding her feelings and causing herself more frustration, frustrating.

Fifth Place: Shinichi Kudo

These days, being a public idol is too difficult. You can't tolerate even the slightest flaw. It's not like my singing was just a bit off-pitch, right? Can those singers solve cases, play soccer, hh, or fly planes? Stop bringing that up, frustrating; originally a talented and charming high school detective, overnight turned into a child less than a meter tall, and now have to humbly call my former lover "Sister Ran," wouldn't anyone find that frustrating? If there were an award for "the most unfortunate son in the world," it would definitely be me. What kind of parents are these, leaving their son alone in Japan to suffer while they go abroad to enjoy themselves, are they even fit to be called parents? Frustrating; it's all Aoyama's fault for drawing all the handsome guys the same way. Why do I have so many twin brothers out of nowhere like Kaito Kid, Hattori Heiji, Hakuba Saguru, and that Ante Satoshi guy? Frustrating.

Fourth Place: Mouri Kogoro

I am a detective too, don't always make me seem as ignorant as a rock on Flower Fruit Mountain. What? I'm a supporting character? Used to highlight the main character? Alright, Shinichi Kudo, you wait, you've frustrated me to death; that woman Eri Kisaki is really something, leaving home for ten years over a trivial matter, making life hard for her husband and child, the worst part is, she doesn't even file for divorce! Frustrating; I may be a bit older, but I still have manliness. Don't scream at the sight of those young ones, the real handsome guy is here, frustrating; that Shinichi Kudo kid not only steals my business but also lusts after my daughter, making Ran get lovesick, frustrating;

Third Place: Rachel Moore (Ran Mouri)

Having Shinichi as a boyfriend, frustrating; having Mouri Kogoro as a dad, frustrating; having Conan as a little kid around, frustrating; having Ai Haibara, Mitsuhiko, and a bunch of rivals, frustrating;

Second Place: Conan

Driving cars, speedboats, airplanes, 73, are you trying to tire me out, huh? Do you really think I'm a superhero? Frustrating; every time Inspector Megure calls Uncle Mouri "plague," I know, actually, it's referring to me, could it be that I'm possessed by the plague spirit? Otherwise, why am I so unlucky, frustrating; watching your girlfriend being lured by bad men, yet unable to do anything about it, can only cause minor disruptions, frustrating; how many episodes of "Detective Conan" will there be? Don't let the "Junior Detective Team" become the "Golden Years Detective Team," your pocketbook is full enough, stop tormenting us, okay? Frustrating;

First Place: All loyal fans of "Detective Conan"

To buy "Conan," selling pots and pans, turning into penniless, frustrating; waiting patiently for a long time, looking forward eagerly, gazing through autumn waters, the latest movie version and TV version come out slowly, frustrating; waiting and waiting, finally comes out, by the time it crosses the sea, the new one becomes old, frustrating; experiencing joy or sorrow, anger or laughter due to the fate of the characters in the drama, being called "crazy" by others, frustrating.
