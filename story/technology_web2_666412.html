How to Whiten Skin: 7 Quick Tips to Brighten Your Skin Tone

by angber27 on 2012-02-15 17:41:44

How can people with naturally dark skin whiten their skin? When it comes to whitening methods, there are actually a plethora of options available online. But which whitening method is the most effective? I believe that whitening the skin should not only be effective, but more importantly, it should not harm the skin. What we aim for is healthy whitening. Below, I will introduce 7 simple tips for skin whitening using homemade masks that you can make at home. If you want your skin to become fairer, take action now!!

1. **Vegetable Juice Whitening**: Blend green peppers, cucumbers, one-quarter of a bitter melon, celery, and green apples into a beauty vegetable juice to drink. This juice contains sufficient Vitamin C, which is highly effective for whitening.

2. **Vinegar Egg Liquid Whitening**: Take a fresh egg, wash it clean, and soak it in 500 milliliters of high-quality vinegar for a month. Once the eggshell dissolves in the vinegar, take a small spoonful of the solution, mix it with a cup of warm water, stir, and drink daily. Long-term consumption of vinegar egg liquid can make the skin smooth and delicate, eliminating all facial spots.

3. **Aloe Vera Mask**: Prepare an aloe leaf about three fingers wide and two fingers long (with spots), remove the thorns, and wash it clean. Also prepare a piece of cucumber about three centimeters long, an egg white, 23 grams of pearl powder, and an appropriate amount of flour (used to adjust the consistency). Blend the aloe and cucumber in a juicer, pour it into a small bowl, then add the egg white, pearl powder, and an appropriate amount of flour to form a paste. Apply this paste on your face after washing it, let it dry, rinse it off, and finish with a toner and skincare products. Do this once or twice a week.

4. **Milk Whitening**: Prepare a small cup of fresh milk (in summer, you can also refrigerate the milk for a cooler sensation). Steam your face, soak a cotton pad with fresh milk, and apply it on your face for about fifteen minutes. Rinse your face with clear water afterward. Persistent use can make your complexion fair and even.

5. **Tomato Honey Whitening**: This formula can be used for both facial and hand whitening, especially effective for oily acne-prone skin, preventing infections, and making the skin white and delicate.

- Ingredients: Half a tomato, an appropriate amount of honey.

- Method: Blend the tomato into a juice, add an appropriate amount of honey to form a paste. Apply evenly on your face or hands, leave it on for about 15 minutes, then rinse off. It is recommended to do this 1-2 times a week.

6. **Honey Protein Mask**: Use a fresh egg, a small spoonful of honey, mix them evenly. Before bedtime, use a clean soft brush to apply this mask on your face. You can massage during application to stimulate skin cells and promote blood circulation. Leave it on until it dries, then rinse with water. It is advisable to do this twice a week. This mask can also be diluted with water to rub on your hands, preventing chapped hands in winter.

7. **Traditional Chinese Medicine Whitening**: Ingredients: Equal parts of Angelica dahurica, licorice, almond kernels, angelica, green bean powder, sandalwood, etc., ground into powder.

- Method: For washing your face, add an appropriate amount of water. Add honey, milk, and one-quarter of an egg white, mix appropriately, and apply on your entire face (excluding eyes and mouth). Rinse with water after it dries. Do this 1-2 times a week (not too often, as it may irritate the skin). This can be used for acne, dark spots, freckles, and shrinking pores around the nose. People who have used it find it quite effective.

These are some DIY whitening methods that are not only cost-effective but also beneficial for achieving healthier, brighter skin.

For those interested in egg white masks, simply beat the egg white in a bowl (remove the yolk) until it forms white foam. Add 6-8 drops of fresh lemon juice, mix well, and apply directly on your face. This has astringent properties, helps reduce inflammation, and prevents wrinkles.

Lastly, for those interested in traditional Chinese medicine whitening methods, explore herbal remedies that suit your skin type. Remember to always patch test new ingredients before applying them widely.

Stay beautiful and healthy!
