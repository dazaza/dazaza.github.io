Tianhe District:

1. Tianhe Sports Center Business Circle:

The business framework of the Sports Center has matured: from properties, there are leading figures such as Tianhe City, Bookstore Center, Hongcheng Plaza, and Zhengjia Plaza; from operators, there are powerful merchants like Tianmao Nanda and Jusco. The influx of many large properties will continuously promote the commercial development of this business circle in the coming years.

2. Tianhe North Business Circle:

Tianhe North is currently Guangzhou's most business-intensive CBD. It is understood that there are more than 20 high-grade office buildings in the Tianhe North area, over 1600 foreign-related large enterprises, more than 560 foreign institutions permanently stationed in Guangzhou, and an annual growth rate of over 15% in the foreign rental market. With its superior geographical location, the Tianhe North Business Office Circle centered around CITIC Plaza has become the preferred location for foreign companies entering Guangzhou, including well-known international companies such as Procter & Gamble, HSBC, AIA, IMG, etc., which have rented office buildings in this area. Here, there are also top-tier office buildings such as Zhongtai International Plaza, Jinlai Tower, Wealth Plaza, as well as over 20 high-end residential complexes like Zhongyi City Garden, Dijing Garden, Fangcaoyuan, Eaton 18, Hilton Sunshine, etc. The commercial atmosphere within the Tianhe North residential areas has attracted well-known large merchants such as IKEA, PARKnSHOP, and Wanjia to enter, elevating the commercial atmosphere in the region. Currently, Tianhe North has become a modern landmark integrating office, business, dining, entertainment, and shopping functions.

3. Tianhe Shapai Business Circle:

Positioned as an IT product comprehensive trade zone, technical service zone, and entertainment leisure zone, with the regional brand of "IT Street" as its characteristic.

4. Tianhe Tangxia Business Circle:

Centered around the Tangxia Superstore, with multiple large residential complexes nearby, densely populated, adjacent to science parks and software parks, it serves as the main consumption hub for people working and residing in the surrounding areas.

5. Tianhe Huajing New Town Business Circle:

Located at the core of the new city center of Tianhe District, south connected to Zhongshan Avenue, north linked to Tianhe North Road, west facing the South China Expressway, adjacent to Tianhe Park, South China Normal University, Jinan University, postal telecommunications research institute, Tianhe Software Park, etc., with a strong cultural atmosphere. Positioned within the focus area of Asian Games development, it gathers four advantages: the platinum business circle of Tianhe North CBD, the South China university scientific research center, the ecological circle of Tianhe Park, and the eastern Asian Games sector. The main roads of the community connect to the extension line of Tianhe North Road, with five bus line terminals set up inside the area, and over 20 bus lines passing through Zhongshan Avenue, providing convenient transportation access throughout the city. Additionally, the community's living facilities are complete, featuring top-tier schools like No. 113 Middle School of Guangdong Province, Huajing Primary School of Guangdong Province, kindergartens, clubs, commercial streets, markets, etc.

6. Tianhe Park Business Circle:

With the deepening of Guangzhou's "eastward shift" strategy, the Tianhe Park block has become the "dreamland" of Guangzhou's commercial development, occupying the core position of the "golden triangle" formed by the Tianhe North CBD, Zhujiang New Town CBD, and Pazhou Exhibition Business Circle. As Guangzhou's economy develops, the city expands, and the Tianhe District government moves in, this area gradually becomes the political, technological, and educational center of Tianhe District.

Second, Yuexiu District:

1. Beijing Road Pedestrian Street Business Circle:

The Beijing Road Business Circle naturally centers around the Beijing Road Commercial Pedestrian Street. This famous "First Street of Guangzhou" has held the dominant position in Guangzhou commerce for over 2000 years, being where large malls and renowned brands gather. Guangbai Department Store stands prominently here, and connected Zhongshan Fifth Road not only has Xin Danxin but also the "northern-flavored" Hualian Department Store. After a pedestrian street renovation project costing over 300 million yuan in recent years, today's Beijing Road connects with the Great Buddha Temple, Nanyue King Tomb, and ancient academy clusters, preserving the rich Lingnan style while also possessing a modern urban atmosphere. The underground "thousand-year-old road" cultural relic pit, after seismic glass cover treatment, allows visitors to admire historical relics while shopping, greatly beautifying the shopping environment. Besides the traditional commercial function, the Beijing Road Business Circle also takes on the responsibility of inheriting Guangzhou history. Whether for out-of-town tourists or local citizens, it is an indispensable shopping paradise and tourist attraction. The once widespread commercial wave initiated by Gaodi Street and Xihu Road Night Market has passed, now standing at the forefront are Guangbai Department Store, Xin Danxin, and newly opened Ming Sheng Plaza, Mayflower Plaza, Guangming Plaza, gathering various brand-name clothing specialty stores, Benetton, Bossini, etc., with areas often exceeding hundreds of square meters. True Beauty, Learsdon, Senbu, Cardis, Timmy, etc., shoe brands also settle here. Xinhua Bookstore, Children's Bookstore, Ancient Books Bookstore line up, adding literary charm to Beijing Road. As the commercial level of Beijing Road gradually improves, the strong commercial atmosphere begins to spread to surrounding areas, forming a radiating commercial area driven by the Beijing Road Business Circle.

2. Haizhu Square Business Circle:

Haizhu Square Station is located at the intersection of Qiyi Road, Yide Road, and Taikang Road, at the northern end of Haizhu Bridge. Nearby are Guangzhou Hotel, Haiyin Fashion Square, Huaxia Grand Hotel, Taikang Plaza, Taikang Decoration City, Art Garden Wholesale Center, Bar Street, etc. Centered around Haizhu Square, a traffic transfer and leisure shopping place forms around it.

3. Nonglinxia Road Business Circle:

Nonglinxia Road is located in the heart of the old East Mountain area, being a long-established business circle with strong Guangzhou characteristics. After the opening of the Dongshan Kou station on Metro Line 1, its role in gathering foot traffic on this section has been quite prominent. Currently, Nonglinxia Road together with nearby Dongshan Kou, Shuqian Road, Guigang, and other old East Mountain commercial hotspots form a relatively mature business circle, with well-known merchants like Wangfujing Department Store and Dongbai Department Store moving in. It has high market recognition, and rent levels have remained relatively high for a long time. Recently, plans for upgrading the Nonglinxia Road business circle have further surfaced, with hopes to transform it into a mid-to-high-end consumption venue, and its commercial development prospects will continue to improve.

4. Zhonghua Plaza Business Circle:

The Zhonghua Plaza Business Circle is located at the Hero Plaza site on Zhongshan Second Road, mainly consisting of large commercial, park, and sports venues such as Zhonghua Plaza, Underground Shopping Mall Pop Frontline, Diwang Plaza, Lingyuan West Communications Specialty Market, Martyrs' Cemetery, Guangdong Provincial Stadium, etc., presenting the initial form of a comprehensive business district, becoming the newly emerging large business circle between the traditional business districts of Beijing Road and Nonglinxia Road in the mother city of Guangzhou - Dongshan District. The annual commodity sales in this area have already exceeded 2 billion yuan, maintaining a double-digit good growth trend.

5. Taojin Business Circle:

In the mid-1990s, due to the rise of the commerce and office area along Huanshi East Road, it drove the development of retail commerce in the surrounding areas, and the ground-floor residences in the Huanshi East Taojin residential area began to evolve into specialty stores and professional stores. After more than 10 years of development, the Taojin Community Business Street has gradually formed. Strongly influenced by the business office area - since the street is located along Huanshi Road, affected by the Huanshi East commerce and office area, a large number of middle-income white-collar consumers provide strong energy for it. Meanwhile, business travelers making short stays in the business area also become important consumers of the Taojin Community Business Street. Driven by landmark businesses in the area - Guangzhou Friendship Store has numerous international first and second-tier brands internally, representing avant-garde, fashion, and high quality. Influenced by it, the trendy consumption image of the Taojin area gradually grows, attracting a large number of middle-to-high-end consumers to come for shopping. At the same time, the Taojin Community Business Street not only continues the high-end route of the Friendship Store but also has relatively lower consumption levels compared to the Friendship Store, allowing it to fully capture the middle-to-low-end consumer group lost from the Friendship Store. Moreover, the "RT-Mart" wholesale store will attract more middle-to-low-end customers to consume, providing significant help in attracting customers for the Taojin Community Business Street. Close to the residence of people with strong purchasing power - the third important reason for the formation of the Taojin North Community Business is its proximity to the residential areas of overseas Chinese villages and the Taojin Road neighborhood, where residents have a large demand for fashionable consumption, providing stable market support for the development of the business street.

6. Wuyang New Town Business Circle:

On the new urban axis of Guangzhou, Tianhe North, Zhujiang New Town, and Pazhou Exhibition Business Circle divide the world into three parts, while Wuyang New Town occupies the core position of these three major business areas. Various enterprises and white-collar groups converge here due to the business circle economy, bringing strong driving force to the Wuyang New Town business circle.

7. Dashatou Business Circle:

People familiar with Guangzhou all know that the electrical market, fabric market, and second-hand market in Dashatou have a huge reputation locally and even nationwide. Regardless of the uneven operation of the electrical market and second-hand market, just talking about how it once led local electrical consumption and the trend of home appliance upgrades deserves praise for the unique insight of local businessmen.

Electrical and household goods trading have now become synonymous with Dashatou. The earliest appearance in the Dashatou Haiyin area was the Haiyin Electrical Market, which was the earliest and largest electrical wholesale and retail market in the country, forming scale at the end of the 1980s. In the early 1990s, Shaw cooperated with others to rent some commercial buildings and old factories near the Dashatou wharf, eventually developing into the later-renowned Haiyin Electrical City. The enormous success of the "Haiyin Model" focused on professional market operations has had a profound impact on the direction of investment in Guangzhou shops in recent years. Today, this place has formed the Haiyin Electrical Street in Guangzhou and even across the country. Near the Haiyin Bridge on Dashatou Road and Donghu Road, there are gathered over ten large electrical wholesale and retail markets such as Haiyin Electrical General Assembly, Golden Haiyin Electrical Plaza, Hui Guang Electrical Market, Haiyin Electrical Trade Center, Xin He Haiyin Electrical Plaza, Haiyin Kang Le City, etc., totaling more than 3000 merchants. They operate various imported and domestic brand-name household appliances, making it the largest commercial electrical professional market in Xidi, Guangzhou, almost gathering all global home appliance brands.

Third, Liwan District:

1. Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street Business Circle:

Shangxiajiu Commercial Street is 800 meters long, with 238 arcade-style buildings constructed at the beginning of the 20th century. It is the first commercial pedestrian street approved by the Ministry of Commerce of China in Guangzhou. The Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street is filled with various medium and high-end department stores and restaurants, gathering famous dishes and snacks known both domestically and internationally. Nearby are historically significant specialized streets and the largest agricultural and miscellaneous market in the country. Even if you don't buy anything, walking through the Shangxiajiu area allows you to experience unique Lingnan commercial culture, making it an "enjoyable" shopping center.

2. Zhongshan Eight Business Circle:

Zhongshan Eight Road has always been the main westbound passage in the old town. The traffic network system formed around this section almost covers the entire central-western region. I've carefully observed that Zhongshan Eight Road is actually a commercial section composed of three main elements: supermarkets, toys, and clothing. Zhongshan Eight Road is not only the terminal station for many eastbound bus routes but also a major node for westbound vehicles. Together, the bus stations form a massive traffic network system. Every day, tens of thousands of people gather in Zhongshan Eight Road before dispersing to various places in the city, including permanent residents in the area and transient populations. Such a large crowd, once they pause slightly in Zhongshan Eight Road, is enough to make a group of forward-thinking merchants rich first. Taking the clothing mall on this section as an example, the retail business during holidays alone accounts for more than half of the annual business volume. People returning home for the New Year from other places always remember to bring a pile of distinctive clothes to give to their relatives' children. Some merchants in Nantian Commercial City have even collaborated with dealers from some towns in the western part of Guangzhou to conduct cross-regional transactions. Smooth roads lead to smooth finances; the traffic network brings more than just convenient entry and exit to Zhongshan Eight Road, but also a foreseeable commercial atmosphere. Zhongshan Eight Road is only 1.3 kilometers long, yet there exist two different types of supermarkets. The supermarket inside Fuli Plaza and the Gangwan Warehouse inside Zhoumen Residential Area belong to the community supermarket category, where the community residents are the customer base and the fundamental guarantee for the survival of the supermarket. Community supermarkets do not need to engage in diversified operations; they only need to sell food and groceries targeted to the community. Community residents clearly accept this warehouse-style food trading method, except occasionally seeking the fun of bargaining at the meat and vegetable market, most of their shopping will be completed in the supermarket. The "Wanjia Baihuo" inside Dongjun Lijingyuan adopts a centralized operating model, targeting the elderly residents in the western city under the premise, "Wanjia" has set up food, home appliances, and clothing special sections, with fast-food restaurants, sushi restaurants, and pharmaceutical stores as auxiliary wings. However, residents are only interested in food, especially cooked food and bread being the most popular. Calculating solely based on food sales, the department store definitely has an advantage; but considering the overall cost of goods procurement, community supermarkets are obviously superior.

3. Shamian Business Circle:

Today, Shamian retains 53 cultural relics buildings and structures, along with the streetscape layout, canals, and ancient trees formed in those years, creating unique tourism resources, attracting a batch of domestic and international investors to build multi-element, high-end dining, shopping, entertainment culture, and exhibition venues successively. Shamian has become a calling card of Guangzhou tourism.

4. Fangcun Huadi City:

The intersection of Huadi Avenue North and Xianhua West Road in Huadiwan Commercial Circle is approximately 80 meters from Exit C and D of Huadiwan Station on Subway Line 1, 100 meters from Fangcun Passenger Station; connecting Zhujiang Tunnel and Inner Ring Road; multiple bus routes go directly to the market, without traffic restrictions for out-of-town vehicles; close to subway, buses, and main traffic arteries, convenient transportation, radiates to the Pearl River Delta; has good geographical advantages.

5. Train Station Business Circle:

It is understood that Guangzhou, located in the Pearl River Delta and neighboring Hong Kong and Macau, has always been at the forefront of China's fashion trends. The Liuhua Clothing Business Circle near Guangzhou Railway Station is one of the largest clothing wholesale distribution centers in the country, with annual turnover reaching tens of billions of yuan. This business circle gathers large clothing production and wholesale merchants from Guangzhou, Humen, Zhongshan, Fujian, Zhejiang, Chongqing, Shenyang, and other places. Merchants from more than thirty provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities, as well as Russia, Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, and other countries and regions, come here every day.

Fourth, Haizhu District:

1. Jiangnanxi Business Circle:

The Haizhu District is fully committed to building the Jiangnanxi Business Circle, transforming 650,000 square meters of residential buildings on both sides into commercial use. At the same time, integrating the 18,000 square meters of underground civil air defense projects nearby, turning Jiangnanxi Road into a distinctive commercial pedestrian street. The convenience brought by the subway makes Jiangnanxi one of the most prosperous areas in Haizhu District, with a daily average of more than 300,000 people consuming, and its purchasing power cannot be underestimated.

2. Shi Er Gong Business Circle:

Shi Er Gong Business Circle is the oldest residential commercial center in Haizhu District. This area has the largest wedding supplies distribution market in South China. The surrounding residential areas are dense, with convenient transportation and broad consumption levels. It remains a place of interest to merchants to this day.

3. Zhongda Business Circle:

The Zhongda Block is dotted with multiple professional wholesale markets, gathering numerous merchants. Among them, the Zhongda Fabric Wholesale Market has the greatest influence. The Guangzhou Textile Expo Center has been designated as a key project and a key commercial project in Haizhu District's "Eleventh Five-Year Plan." Industry insiders predict that the Zhongda Textile Business Circle will be a major focus in Guangzhou's commercial planning over the next ten years.
