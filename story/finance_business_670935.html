Public Security Striking Down the "Lean Meat Powder" Crime, 989 Criminal Suspects Captured

by hdghu19056 on 2012-02-21 13:52:23

Under the unified deployment and command of the Ministry of Public Security, a nationwide police campaign to crack down on "clenbuterol" cases has achieved significant results. More than 120 cases have been solved, 989 criminal suspects have been arrested, and "black factories," "black dens," and "black markets" for the production, storage, and sale of "clenbuterol" have been dismantled, destroying the "clenbuterol" production and sales network at its source and effectively curbing the actual harm of "clenbuterol" to the people.

A responsible official from the Ministry of Public Security stated that the major achievements in the crackdown on "clenbuterol" crimes have strengthened the determination and confidence of the public security organs to carry out the "fight against four black markets and eliminate four harms." At the same time, it also makes us more acutely aware that although the main production source of "clenbuterol" has been eliminated, all regions must remain highly vigilant to prevent a resurgence and to prevent "clenbuterol" held by individuals in society from causing new harm. The Ministry of Public Security will mobilize public security organs across the country, especially grassroots police stations, to work with relevant departments to actively investigate within their jurisdictions, share information, move the checkpoint forward, and strive to discover, test, and deal with clues of food safety and other criminal offenses early, reducing harm. At the same time, the public is also encouraged to report such activities positively. Once such criminal activities are discovered, the public security organs will seriously investigate and severely punish them according to law.

In March this year, according to the instructions of central leaders and the deployment of the State Council's Food Safety Commission, the Ministry of Public Security organized an investigation into clues of crimes involving the production and sale of toxic and harmful pharmaceuticals and foods, especially focusing on the prominent situation of "clenbuterol" crimes. After analyzing individual cases handled by local public security organs and clues provided by relevant departments such as agriculture and industry and commerce, the Ministry of Public Security determined that there exists a network for the production and sale of "clenbuterol" domestically, with a considerable market and serious harm. The Ministry of Public Security decided to launch a nationwide campaign to crack down on "clenbuterol" crimes, requiring deep investigation and severe strikes. It was required to thoroughly investigate the source of the crime, completely dismantle the "black factories" and "black dens" for production and storage, and destroy the "black markets" for sales. From the source, the criminal activities of "clenbuterol" would be severely hit, fundamentally curbing the spread of criminal activities, firmly protecting the food safety of the people, and truly safeguarding the fundamental interests of the people. For this purpose, the Ministry of Public Security specially established a case-breaking campaign command post, sorted out key clues, supervised and urged major cases; public security organs in all involved places established special investigation teams headed by the competent directors, and mobilized more than 2,000 police forces to participate in the case-breaking campaign.

Deputy Director Xu Hu of the Public Security Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Public Security introduced that the production, warehousing, and sales of such crimes are mostly geographically separated, and often use aliases, fake names, and forged goods names for storage and transportation, making them highly concealed. In response, the Ministry of Public Security deployed multi-police-force联动and cross-provincial synthetic operations in various places, dispatched supervision teams for specific command and supervision, unified command and scheduling, unified clue investigation, unified case jurisdiction and action, and cracked a series of major cases such as the production and sale of "clenbuterol" case in Shaoyang, Hunan, the production and sale of "clenbuterol" case in Chengdu, Sichuan, the production and sale of "clenbuterol" feed case in Wuhan, Hubei, the production and sale of "clenbuterol" case by Tianjin Guoying Liao Bio-Technology Co., Ltd., and the production and sale of "clenbuterol" case by Zhengzhou Daming Animal Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. and Zhang Mou in Xinxiang, Henan, etc. So far, 2.5 tons of "clenbuterol" have been seized, six laboratories for the research and development of "clenbuterol" have been dismantled, 12 illegal production lines for "clenbuterol" have been investigated, 19 processing and storage black dens have been sealed off, 32 black factories producing and selling "clenbuterol" have been investigated, and a super-large network for the production, sale, addition, and use of "clenbuterol" covering 63 prefecture-level cities has been destroyed, effectively curbing the real harm of "clenbuterol."
