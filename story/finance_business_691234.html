International Women's Day, March 8th special recommendation: Get drunk with feelings in Wuyi Mountain, make an appointment for beauty and health together (with photos)

by anonymous on 2012-03-05 14:59:18

In the sunny month of March, spring has arrived! Everywhere you look, there's vibrant green, brilliant red, adorable pink, playful yellow...all exuding an invigorating vitality. March is also a uniquely romantic month for women. In these days, put aside the busyness, kick off the high heels, and leave behind all sorts of trivialities and worries. In the season that belongs to women, embrace the beautiful nature, breathe in the fresh air, shout out your own joyous voice, get intoxicated by Wuyi Mountain, and make an appointment with beauty and health.

The gentle Jiuxi Creek intoxicates the heart. It’s often said that "women are made of water," and water and women always have endless stories and inseparable ties. When women come to Wuyi Mountain, Jiuxi Creek is a must-see. The clear waters twist into nine curves, like a jade belt stringing pearls, connecting the 36 peaks and 99 rocks of Wuyi as one, embodying the soul of Wuyi Mountain. The creek water flows from west to east, winding freely, with mountains surrounding the water and water running through the mountains. The crystal-clear stream contains different interests at every curve and hides beautiful scenery in every bend. Boarding the simple bamboo raft and drifting into this scenic wonderland is like entering a mythological realm, where water winds around the mountains and mountains stand beside the water. Rapids and pools alternate, the mountains are not tall but possess the grandeur of high mountains, the water is not deep but gathers all the best water views. Being here feels like walking through a magical gallery of landscapes.

Recommendation: Taking a bamboo raft down Jiuxi Creek is a premium tourism project in Wuyi Mountain and also the most distinctive tourist attraction in the Wuyi Mountain scenic area. Hence the saying goes, "If you visit Wuyi Mountain and don’t take a bamboo raft ride, it’s like you haven't been there." Under the warm sunlight of spring, sitting on the bamboo raft without any barriers, comfortably and freely immersed in the emerald waters and red cliffs, free from noise and pollution, you can see mountain views when looking up, observe water colors when looking down, hear the sound of the stream when listening closely, and touch the clear waves when reaching out. There is stillness in motion, motion in stillness, a balance of strength and gentleness, appreciating both mountains and waters—beautiful, enjoyable, and not tiring.

Boluo (bolvv.net) Tip: There are three bamboo raft docks upstream of Jiuxi: No.1, No.2, and Xianfanjie, located in Xingcun, 6 kilometers away from the resort area and 20 kilometers away from the city center. Tourists can take public buses or taxis to Xingcun to board the bamboo rafts and disembark at Wuyi Palace Pier in the First Curve, drifting downstream from the Ninth Curve. The journey takes about 90 minutes.

Da Hongpao warms the heart tenderly. Women are like tea, becoming more flavorful with each sip. Su Dongpo once wrote: "I jestfully compose a small poem for your smile, for excellent tea is akin to a beautiful woman." This land of Wuyi Mountain produces abundant tea. Wuyi Rock Tea started in the Ming Dynasty, thrived in the Qing Dynasty, and was exported to Western Europe in the 17th century, gaining worldwide fame. The well-known "Da Hongpao" is its immortal representative among rock teas. Speaking of Da Hongpao, the mother trees of this "King of Teas" grow on the cliff walls of the Nine-Dragon Hollow Grand Canyon in Wuyi, with a total of six plants protected by stone embankments like potted landscapes, making them extremely precious. Beneath the potted tea garden stands a pavilion called the Nine-Dragon Tea Room, where you can taste famous tea while enjoying the beautiful scenery and listening to the ancient legends of "Da Hongpao."

Recommendation: Wuyi Rock Tea belongs to the Oolong tea category, with green leaves edged in red, presenting a beautiful form, deep orange-yellow brightness, and a soup color like carnelian. Its rich flavor and floral aroma are refreshing and pleasant, leaving a lingering aftertaste. It combines the sweetness of black tea with the freshness of green tea, being a rare treasure of tea that embodies "alive, sweet, clear, fragrant." If Wuyi Rock Tea were compared to a woman, she would be a learned and elegant young lady, full of talent, sweet and smooth, with a profound aftertaste. Drinking Wuyi Rock Tea regularly can invigorate the mind, eliminate fatigue, aid digestion, reduce fat, and has special effects in preventing cancer, lowering blood lipids, and resisting aging.

Boluo (bolvv.net) Tip: Tea is ubiquitous in Wuyi Mountain, even if it's the same type of tea, prices can vary greatly. First, do not follow the tour guide to buy tea (quality may be guaranteed, but the price might be questionable); second, do not buy tea from street vendors (prices may be cheap, but quality cannot be assured). Generally, tasting before purchasing is recommended. You can buy tea from some large specialty shops in Wuyi Mountain. If buying directly from a tea factory, be cautious of potential substitutions, as the tea you purchase may not be the same as the one you tasted.

Qinglong Waterfall ignites passion within. The Qinglong Great Waterfall is located upstream of Jiuxi Creek in Wuyi Mountain, situated in a fault zone with steep terrain. The entire scenic trail is 2,300 meters long. Entering the canyon, walking along the damp and smooth pebble path, following the river upstream, what meets the eye is boundless greenery, which becomes denser the deeper you go. Ancient trees are lush, flowers bloom profusely, insects chirp melodiously, springs murmur peacefully. Sometimes the view opens up, sometimes it remains mysteriously deep, each place being a natural masterpiece. Looking afar at the waterfall, it seems like sourceless water reaching straight to the sky, resembling a roaring green dragon; up close, the waterfall sprays pearls and jade under the sunlight, colorful and breathtaking.

Recommendation: The Qinglong Great Waterfall is acclaimed as the "First Waterfall in East China." Here, not only can you admire the magnificent and vast waterfall, but it is also an excellent place to cleanse your lungs, known as a "Natural Oxygen Bar." According to expert measurements, this is the place with the highest negative oxygen ion content in Wuyi Mountain, averaging 67,000 per cubic centimeter, with peak areas reaching between 112,000 to 136,000 per cubic centimeter. Being here allows for the alleviation of physical and mental fatigue and great relaxation and adjustment of nerves.

Boluo (bolvv.net) Tip: The main attractions of the Qinglong Waterfall Scenic Area include One-Step Bridge, Shilü Qinqang, Qingxi Tan Shui, Ling She Lian Shi, Jinban Huifeng Butterfly, Chaotic Stone Silver Waves, Cuiyu Menglong, Wuyi Mountain Qinglong Great Waterfall. Other attractions include Shenkan, Wang Tian Pavilion, Tongtian Plank Road, Tongtian Bridge, Wen Tian Stone, One Step to Heaven, Tongtian Grand Canyon, Eighteen-Fold Mountains, Qinglong Mountain, etc. The entire touring process involves proximity to mountains and water, with slippery paths, so safety should be prioritized during the journey to avoid injury.

"The Impression Dahongpao" captivates the heart with talent. "Impression Dahongpao" reenacts the profound and weighty Wuyi tea culture using artistic forms, transforming it into a tangible, perceptible cultural tourism project, condensing the beautiful natural landscape into a high-level artistic feast. "The hermit left his stone stove, seemingly in the middle of the water. After drinking, I set off in a boat, and the tea smoke wafts delicately." This is how the famous Song Dynasty poet Zhu Xi vividly depicted the "mountain-water-tea" of Wuyi Mountain. "Impression Dahongpao" uses its highly artistic means to recreate such scenes. "Impression Dahongpao" was led by the creative team of Zhang Yimou, Wang Chaoge, and Fan Yue, collectively known as the "Impression Iron Triangle," presenting a unique perspective on the distinct "mountain-water-tea" culture of Wuyi Mountain.

Recommendation: The launch of the "Impression Dahongpao" mountain-water live performance broke the traditional tourism and aesthetic methods of "climbing mountains during the day and floating on Jiuxi Creek." It showcases the beauty of Wuyi Mountain at night. The same Wuyi Mountain offers a different visual feast. Watching the mountain-water live performance on the 360-degree rotating audience seats presents the tragic love story of Dawaang and Yvnu, the hardworking tea farmers amidst the clear mountains and rivers, and the ink-painting-like beauty of Wuyi Mountain dreamily and poetically.

Boluo (bolvv.net) Tip: "Impression Dahongpao" is the world's only large-scale mountain-water live performance showcasing Chinese tea culture. "Real-scene movie" is the longest segment of the performance and also the biggest highlight of "Impression Dahongpao." The seventy-minute performance takes place in the completely open "mountains and waters," known as the world's first "mountain-water panoramic theater." Since it is watched outdoors, although it is already springtime at night, additional clothing should be worn to stay warm and prevent catching a cold.
