Military expert Ma Dingsheng, together with frontline host Dong Jiayao, analyzes the latest military developments in today's world, classic battle examples, and weapons dictionary knowledge. Join them as they take you into the wilderness battlefield, video game wars, and view the sea, land, and air world through the eyes of a military enthusiast.

Phoenix Television 2005 New Edition of "Military Observation Room"

After the revamp, the first broadcast of "Military Observation Room" will be on Wednesday, January 5th at 9:45 PM, with a replay the next morning at 3 AM. The program is broadcast once a week during prime time and is one of the high-rated key programs on Phoenix Television.

1. Host Introduction: Dong Jiayao and Ma Dingsheng

Host Dong Jiayao graduated from the television program hosting major at the Beijing Broadcasting Institute (now Communication University of China) Department of Television. He has previously hosted news programs at CCTV and Guangdong TV and also worked as a field reporter for special news segments.

Dong Jiayao's hosting style is grand and steady, with a tough approach, clear thinking, and broad vision. He is the ace host of Phoenix Television's news and political affairs programs and also one of the youngest members of the management team at Phoenix Television. A versatile talent, he hosts several important programs on Phoenix Television. Known for his boundless energy, he is called the "Phoenix Ironman."

Currently, besides hosting "Military Observation Room," he also hosts other key Phoenix programs such as "Dialogue of Titans," "Current Affairs Talk," and "Current Affairs Express." Additionally, he serves as the producer of "Current Affairs Talk," the executive producer of "Military Observation Room," and the assistant director of Phoenix Information Channel, making him one of the youngest members of the management team at Phoenix Television.

The program "Current Affairs Talk," which Dong Jiayao hosts, was awarded the "Best News Program" award in China's television programs in 2002 and has received the company's "Special Contribution Award" multiple times.

"Military Observation Room" was born amidst the smoke of the Iraq War. In March 2003, when the Iraq War broke out, Dong Jiayao and Ma Dingsheng co-hosted "Military Observation Room," providing a comprehensive and vivid analysis of the war situation, captivating audiences worldwide. Since then, "Military Observation Room," originally a special program created for the Iraq War, has become a regular feature and one of the high-rated programs on Phoenix Television.
