Hebei Airlines Investment Group was established, and JiZhong Energy fully holds the share with an investment of 5 billion yuan.

by ditan360 on 2010-06-29 14:04:56

Low Carbon Network exclusive report: One party hopes to promote regional economic development through the operation of local aviation industry, while the other is trying to enter the aviation market in order to get out of the困境of the "black industry". On the 28th, the Hebei Aviation Investment Group was announced to be established after the combination of multiple interests.

At a press conference held by the Hebei Provincial Government on the afternoon of the 28th, Vice Governor Sun Ruibin of Hebei Province said that as the "engine" to promote regional economic development, the establishment of Hebei Aviation will have an open and comprehensive impact on the development of local economy and industrial upgrading. "Hebei Province values exactly the strategic driving force of the aviation industry to promote the transformation of the economic pattern."

The newly established Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd. is wholly owned by the leading state-owned coal enterprise in Hebei Province - Jizhong Energy Group, with a registered capital of 5 billion RMB, directly supervised by the Hebei Provincial SASAC.

Hebei Airlines will serve as the core enterprise of the group, co-founded by Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd., Sichuan Airlines Group Corporation, and Shenyang Zhongrui Investment Company.

Today (the 29th), relevant departments of the state and Hebei Province held the founding meeting of Hebei Aviation Investment Group and the maiden flight ceremony of Hebei Airlines at the south apron of Shijiazhuang Zhengding International Airport. Zhang Yunchuan, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Xia Xinghua, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, unveiled the plaque for "Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd." and simultaneously cut the ribbon for the maiden flight of Hebei Airlines.

Subsequently, an Airbus A320 aircraft painted with "Jizhong Energy" took off from Shijiazhuang Zhengding International Airport to Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport, with 160 passengers becoming the first flight experiencers.

Solving the problem of insufficient aircraft capacity

At the press conference on the 28th, Wang Sheping, Chairman of Jizhong Energy Group and Chairman of Hebei Aviation Group, told reporters that the establishment of Hebei Aviation Investment Group is another important measure of Jizhong Energy Group's diversified development strategy after the reorganization of North China Pharmaceutical.

He said that considering the unstable factors in the traditional coal market, as well as the vigorous development of new energy and the irresistible trend of low-carbon economy bringing huge impacts and challenges to the coal industry, Jizhong Energy Group believes that to enhance its ability to resist market risks and make the enterprise grow stronger and larger quickly, it must implement a diversified strategy based on coal as the mainstay and low-cost expansion.

Wang Sheping stated that Hebei Airlines will focus on building a fast, direct, and economical "rapid air bus" system centered on Shijiazhuang with direct flights to nearby cities.

Hebei Province is located in the core hinterland of the third economic growth pole in China, enjoying unique geographical advantages, forming a "golden triangle" with Beijing and Tianjin with strong economic strength and growth potential.

Data provided by the Hebei Provincial Government shows that in 2009, Hebei Province ranked sixth in the country with a GDP of 1,702.66 billion yuan, entering the national "trillion GDP club." Nearly 750,000 passengers flow annually from Hebei Province to the four major airports in Beijing, Tianjin, Henan, and Shanxi, sufficient to support the normal development of an airline and an airport. Currently, the per capita GDP of Hebei Province exceeds 3,000 US dollars, entering the stage of accelerated growth in aviation demand.

However, for a long time, Hebei Province's aviation market has completely relied on the capacity investment of airlines from other places. Jiao Yanlong, Director of the Transportation Department of Hebei Province, introduced that although Shijiazhuang Zhengding Airport opened navigation as early as 1995, Hebei Province did not have a local airline in the following more than ten years, which not only restricted the formation of a comprehensive transportation system but also directly affected the rapid healthy development of Hebei Province's economy and society.

Data shows that in 2009, Hebei Province ranked 29th among the 31 provinces in terms of airport passenger throughput, accounting for only 1.8% of the share in the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region.

"The biggest problem for the airport currently is the lack of aircraft capacity," admitted a responsible person from Shijiazhuang Airport. The establishment of Hebei Aviation Group will accelerate the opening of intra-provincial feeder routes, not only activating the intra-provincial feeder market but also significantly improving Shijiazhuang Airport's ranking on the passenger traffic list and effectively alleviating the operational pressure on Capital Airport.

Sun Ruibin revealed that within one year of Hebei Airlines' operation, Hebei Aviation Investment Group and Sichuan Airlines Group will continue to increase capital, making the total registered capital reach 7 billion yuan, and the equity structure will be adjusted to 62% for Hebei Aviation Investment Group, 35% for Sichuan Airlines Group, and around 3% for Shenyang Zhongrui Investment Company.

The recovering aviation market also brings hope to Hebei Province's civil aviation operators. "Currently, the situation of China's aviation market is characterized by no slackness during the off-season and even greater prosperity during the peak season. The global aviation industry's center is shifting to China," said Zhang Baojian, Chief Representative of IATA in China.

The latest prediction data from the civil aviation industry shows that in the first quarter of this year, China's aviation industry demand continued to grow well, and the fare level increased beyond expectations compared to the same period last year. It is expected that after suffering consecutive losses in recent years, the main businesses of the three major state-owned airlines will achieve profitability for the first time this year.

Aiming for the top five domestic aviation companies

"To promote the development of Hebei's civil aviation industry and fill the gap in Hebei's aviation operation entities, at the beginning of this year, the provincial government specially convened a provincial civil aviation work conference and made a strategic decision to accelerate the formation of Hebei Aviation Group and implement integrated development of Hebei's civil aviation industry, airport economic zones, and related industries," Zhou Jie, Director of the Hebei Provincial SASAC, said to reporters. After repeated research and careful deliberation, it was determined that Jizhong Energy Group would take the lead in piloting the formation of Hebei Aviation Group.

Jizhong Energy Group is the largest coal enterprise in Hebei Province, with three well-operated listed subsidiaries under its nine subsidiaries: North China Pharmaceutical, Jizhong Energy Stock, and Jin Niu Chemical. The group's main business is coal, involving power, chemical, pharmaceutical, machinery, building materials, logistics, and many other industrial sectors. Its overall strength ranks 149th among China's top 500 enterprises (excluding Huayao), 117th among China's top 500 large industrial enterprises, 88th among China's top 200 large enterprises with excellent economic benefits, and in the top 10 of China's coal industry.

Wang Sheping revealed that in 2010, the group's coal production will reach more than 60 million tons, and sales revenue will exceed 100 billion yuan.

Zhou Jie said that there are successful experiences available for reference in forming provincial airlines. The recently formed Chongqing, Tianjin, Kunming, Henan, and Capital Airlines mostly rely on the strength of large provinces (cities) and the financial resources of state-owned large enterprises, quickly forming new companies through mergers and acquisitions of existing small and medium-sized airlines and renaming after relocation.

A plan by the Hebei Provincial Government titled "Plan to Form Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd." proposed that developing the aviation industry will positively promote the construction of Hebei Province's modern industrial system and the transformation of economic development patterns. It can effectively drive the development of related industries such as logistics, tourism, business exhibitions, finance and insurance, catering and accommodation, processing and export, as well as promote the innovative development of a series of related high-tech industries including materials, metallurgy, chemicals, mechanical manufacturing, special processing, electronics, and information, and give birth to a batch of strategic emerging industries.

According to the plan, after the establishment and operation of Hebei Airlines, the strategic focus and phased goals of the overall work are: adjusting and optimizing the structure of airspace routes and the structure of transport capacity, improving the aviation industry chain and aviation economic layout, enhancing the comprehensive operational capabilities of aviation transportation and related industries, cultivating aviation-related industrial clusters and benefit growth points, strengthening the foundation of land-air integrated passenger and cargo circulation, promoting the comprehensive integration of aviation resource elements with full coverage, achieving organic connections between diversified general aviation and trunk and feeder aviation transportation, creating multi-field modern aviation demand, and promoting multi-level social aviation consumption.

The plan determines that by 2015, a basic framework for the configuration of a reasonable and complete aviation passenger service system, aviation logistics distribution system, and land-air related industrial system will be initially formed, achieving operating revenue of over 10 billion yuan and entering the top ten domestic airlines; by 2020, a modern civil aviation industry chain and advanced productivity cluster of the aviation industry will basically be formed, completing the historical transition from regional hub aviation to domestic backbone aviation, achieving historical leaps with international comprehensive industrial competitiveness, radiating modern services and capital operation high-end formats, and reaching operating revenue of over 30 billion yuan, possessing first-class standards of the international civil aviation industry system, and entering the top five domestic airlines.

I learned that the Civil Aviation Administration of China has positioned Shijiazhuang Zhengding International Airport as an "important diversion and alternate airport for the capital." At the same time, the Jingguang High-Speed Railway has set up a stop at Zhengding Airport and is constructing a direct passage from the platform to the terminal building. By the end of this year, Hebei Province will also complete the dedicated expressway from the urban area of Shijiazhuang to Zhengding Airport.

In response to the characteristics of the aviation transportation industry being "high investment, high risk, low returns, and public welfare," Sun Ruibin said that during the cultivation and growth period of Hebei Aviation Investment Group and Hebei Airlines, the Hebei Provincial Government and all relevant departments will provide strong support in aspects such as land, finance, taxation, route subsidies, and talent introduction.

Relevant Information:

Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd.

Hebei Aviation Group Co., Ltd. (English full name Hebei Aviation Investment Group Co., Ltd., hereinafter referred to as Hebei Aviation Group) was founded and inaugurated on June 29, 2010. The company was solely funded by Jizhong Energy Group and is a large state-owned enterprise group supervised by the Hebei Provincial SASAC. The headquarters is located in Shijiazhuang, with a registered capital of 5 billion RMB.

Hebei Airlines Co., Ltd.

Hebei Airlines Co., Ltd. (Chinese short name Hebei Airlines, English short name HEBEI AIRLINES, hereinafter referred to as Hebei Airlines) is the core enterprise of Hebei Aviation Group Co., Ltd. It is a modern airline approved by the Civil Aviation Administration of China, controlled by Hebei Aviation Group Co., Ltd., with Sichuan Airlines Group Corporation and Shenyang Zhongrui Company holding shares. The main operation base is Shijiazhuang Zhengding International Airport, mainly engaged in passenger, cargo, and mail transportation services, and concurrently operates general aviation services and businesses related to aviation transportation.

Jizhong Energy Group Co., Ltd.

Jizhong Energy Group Co., Ltd. (short name: Jizhong Energy) is a state-owned sole proprietorship company belonging to Hebei Province, overseeing 9 subsidiaries including Fengfeng Group Co., Ltd., Jizhong Energy Stock Co., Ltd., North China Pharmaceutical Group Co., Ltd., Handan Mining Group Co., Ltd., Zhangjiakou Mining Group Co., Ltd., Jingxing Mining Group Co., Ltd., Xingtai Mining (Group) Co., Ltd., Mechanical Equipment Co., Ltd., and Shanxi Jizhong Energy Mining Co., Ltd., and owns three listed companies: Jizhong Energy, North China Pharmaceutical, and Jin Niu Chemical.

Jizhong Energy takes coal as its main business and develops multiple industrial sectors including power, chemicals, medicine, machinery, building materials, and logistics. The group has 130,000 employees on its payroll, with total assets of 73 billion yuan and coal reserves of 26 billion tons. In 2009, it completed a coal output of 42.37 million tons, with sales revenue of 55 billion yuan and profits of 2.01 billion yuan.

In 2009, Jizhong Energy ranked 149th among China's top 500 enterprises (excluding Huayao), 117th among China's top 500 large industrial enterprises, 88th among China's top 200 large enterprises with excellent economic benefits, and was among the top 10 in China's coal industry, making it the second-largest main coking coal base in China.
