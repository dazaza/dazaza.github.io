★[Practical Tips] Winter management of Angora rabbits

[Oral Introduction] With the rise in the purchase price of rabbit hair, the Angora rabbit breeding industry is reviving, and many farmers are shifting their breeding focus to Angora rabbits. However, in the process of feeding and management, we find that some breeders spend a lot of money buying quality rabbits, but still use the old ways in feeding and management, especially in winter, unscientific management leads to a significant decrease in the hair production of quality rabbits. In a short period of time, these quality rabbits turn into ordinary ones. Therefore, in today's "Practical Tips" section, we will teach you how to raise Angora rabbits well in winter.

[Voiceover] The most prominent feature of winter is the low temperature, strong wind, and short daylight hours. For Angora rabbits, various green feeds are lacking, so winter is the slowest growing season for Angora rabbits. However, winter is often the peak breeding season for Angora rabbits. Doing a good job in winter management lays a good foundation for the coming year. First, the winter feeding and management of Angora rabbits should pay attention to cold protection and warmth preservation (subtitle: cold protection and warmth preservation). In cold weather and windy snowy days, the doors and windows of the rabbit house should be closed, especially for rabbits that have just had their hair cut or pulled.

[Contemporary Voice] Chairman of Dafeng Snow Rabbit Hair Production Professional Cooperative Xiao Zhengwang: Winter is also the breeding season, especially for newborn baby rabbits, more attention should be paid to keeping warm. Use nesting boxes, add more grass, and add some rabbit hair, inferior rabbit hair, to keep warm.

[Voiceover] Secondly, attention should be paid to timely hair cutting (subtitle: timely hair cutting): After entering winter, the temperature gradually decreases. Although this change is not beneficial for cold protection, the lower temperature is very advantageous for rabbit hair growth. When the rabbit hair reaches the standard, choose a sunny noon day to cut or pull the hair. When cutting the hair, leave the abdominal hair longer to protect the internal organs from wind and cold invasion. Thirdly, reasonable feeding (subtitle: reasonable feeding). In winter, apart from increasing the supply of concentrated feed, farmers with conditions should try to add some high-energy feed in the feed mix, such as corn. Baby rabbits not only need to be fed high-energy feed, but also need more protein supplementation than adult rabbits, mainly oil cakes, such as soybean cake and rapeseed cake, which can fully provide protein content. Green feed is relatively scarce in winter. To ensure normal reproduction of Angora rabbits and rabbit hair growth, farmers with conditions should plant some multi-season growing forage grasses to ensure that they can eat green feed every day.

[Contemporary Voice] Chairman of Dafeng Snow Rabbit Hair Production Professional Cooperative Xiao Zhengwang: To raise good rabbits, green feed is indispensable. Our farmers raising rabbits usually have land, where they can plant forage grasses and cereal feed. We have carrots, black grass, and chicory, all of which are excellent green feed.

[Voiceover] It should be noted that baby rabbits that have just been weaned should not be fed excessive amounts of green feed to prevent diarrhea caused by intestinal discomfort. The nursing time for winter baby rabbits can be appropriately extended during breastfeeding to enhance their physical fitness. The fourth point to note is to strengthen winter management (subtitle: winter management), and the rabbit droppings in the rabbit house should be cleaned frequently to keep the rabbit house clean and ensure fresh air. To prevent bacterial and viral infections, disinfection should be carried out every 7-10 days, and it should be done on a clear and windless day. Although the temperature is low in winter and bacteria are not as active as in summer, the epidemic prevention work for Angora rabbits cannot be relaxed (subtitle: epidemic prevention and disease treatment).

[Contemporary Voice] Chairman of Dafeng Snow Rabbit Hair Production Professional Cooperative Xiao Zhengwang: Even in winter, rabbit plague prevention, pasteurella, E. coli, and Clostridium should be fully vaccinated according to the immunization schedule, so that you can develop better in the spring of the coming year.

[Editorial Note] If Angora rabbits can be raised well in winter, it will lay a good foundation for the spring breeding and raising of Angora rabbits in the coming year.
