    Recently, I met a customer online. During a casual conversation, we talked about her love story, which was quite surprising. With her permission, I would like to share it with you. This is what she said:

 

 

When talking about this story, even now, I can hardly believe that it is true. My boyfriend and I met under such circumstances.

 

 

I remember one day while shopping, I saw a store called "Old Silver Shop" holding an event - "End Your Single Journey". Out of curiosity, I went in to take a look. It turned out that the silver accessories shop was having a promotion on wedding rings. Each single person could buy one of the pair of rings and leave a note with a short message and their QQ number. When the other half of the ring was bought, the note would also be taken by the buyer. My classmates thought it was fun and encouraged me to buy one. I myself also had a curious mindset and ended up buying a ring. It was very simple but elegant and generous.

 

 

Later, I didn't pay much attention to it. Friends just said that the ring was very beautiful and asked who gave it to me. I just smiled. Suddenly one day, someone added me on QQ, and the information prompt was about the ring. I was stunned at the moment. After adding each other as friends and chatting more, I found that the two of us were very compatible, and then it started like this.

 

Finally, she said: [To this day, we are still wearing those silver rings from back then. Although they are not the most expensive, for us, they mark the beginning of our relationship and bear witness to our love.]
