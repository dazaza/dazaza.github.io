Can not be eliminated from the human body with currently available drugs

by jc74976315 on 2012-03-07 15:07:43

This is a grand one-way transparent research and experiment core. Staff dressed in white bio-suits were orderly passing through the graduation certificates outside. Like a gust of wind, Mei Qi slipped out following the people coming out. In the command center, a doctor was reporting to his superior over the phone: "The snake coils from ancient times cannot be driven out of the human body with current medication. Forcing it will cause the host's death. Are you saying, clean all the hosts? But..."

Mei Qi stopped at her driver's license, her heart turning cold. What does 'clean them all' mean? Because she stopped moving, Mei Qi's figure appeared before everyone. She stared at the doctor intently, asking word by word, "What does 'clean them all' mean?"

"Who are you? Why are you here?" The doctor asked Mei Qi with surprise and caution. Around Mei Qi, an invisible pressure began to gather, "Answer my question. What does 'clean them all' mean?!" The unique pressure of the Tian Yin clan made the doctor unable to breathe.

Mei Qi's flying sword flew out, directly cutting off the gun barrel of someone far away who was raising a gun! After the snake coil grows in the human body for a certain period of time, it will mature, then use the body's nutrition to hatch more baby snake coils, and find more hosts for them. This is a biological plague more terrifying than viral infection. Cleaning them all, for some people, is the best choice. But Mei Qi couldn't accept that. They are living people, not numbers. At least they can be frozen using freezing techniques, and then methods can be found to successfully separate the snake coils from their bodies.

Mei Qi's flying sword pointed at the doctor's brow center, her beautiful face showing a resolute expression, "I am in charge here now." The doctor chuckled helplessly; he also disagreed with the cleaning plan and shrugged his shoulders helplessly. Mei Qi picked up the phone, "I'm truly sorry, but I'm in charge here now. I hope you change the cleaning plan."

"You need to think carefully about the consequences." The voice on the other side of the phone was filled with anger. "My family and friends are within the scope of cleaning. I have no other choices. I suggest you try large-scale freezing techniques, and then come up with a safe way to drive out the snake coils." Mei Qi's words made the doctor's expression relax as well. Perhaps this is a feasible method.

"The large-scale freezing technique requires spiritual energy that I cannot provide, and even if I could, we must prevent the worst-case scenario. The snake spirits have already been destroyed. These snake coils on these people cannot remain in this world. The longer they stay, the greater the risk. I've already sent special executors there, so you don't have any bargaining chips with me anymore." That voice was exceptionally cold.

At the same time, the special executor calmly passed through the wall and appeared beside Mei Qi. He wore a deep blue school uniform, his figure slim and upright. He looked at Mei Qi's dangerous flying sword and smiled.

Mei Qi was stunned. Chun Ri Yue? Chun Ri Yue had a calm demeanor like post-rain green tea. He smiled lightly at Mei Qi, "Is that you? Do you plan to rob this place? Need my help?"

The doctor looked boldly at the flying sword, "Young Master Chun, shouldn't you be on our side?"

Chun Ri Yue laughed purely and harmlessly, "Why should I be on your side when you're not a beauty?"

His cold joke made the atmosphere even colder.

"Senior Chun, you don't need to do this," Mei Qi knew that Chun Ri Yue was originally supposed to be the special executor sent to deal with her. He was highly talented but hidden in the campus, probably to investigate matters related to another location.

"I will stand by you no matter what you do." Chun Ri Yue gently held Mei Qi's hand, "Fei Lin has already been taken care of by me. You don't need to worry."

Mei Qi felt the warmth in Chun Ri Yue's palm and her heart skipped a beat. She pulled her hand away and forced a smile, "Thank you."
