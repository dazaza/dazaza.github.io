From Top to Bottom Dryness

Countermeasure: Rehydration plan for roots and tips.

Recognizing hair dehydration levels: Normally, hair contains approximately 15% water. However, if it drops below 10%, various signs of dryness will immediately appear. Now, please touch and gently rub your hair from root to tip. If it feels dry and makes a rustling sound during rubbing, then your hair is severely dehydrated and needs immediate replenishment.

Weakly Acidic Shampoo: In winter, people with dry hair should choose weakly acidic shampoos because alkaline ones can strip away too much oil, making the scalp even drier. Using moisturizing shampoo and maintaining a washing frequency of 4-7 times per week can ensure the hydration level of the hair.

Problem 2 &；nbsp； Friction-induced [Flyaway Hair]

Haircare Countermeasure: Complete anti-static treatment.

Frequent Washing: Regular washing prevents pollution particles from attaching to the hair, keeping the strands clean and reducing friction caused by these particles, thus preventing split ends and breakage.

Post-Wash Conditioning: Cationic active substances in conditioners align the surface molecules of the hair, reducing charge and lowering resistance, enhancing the anti-static effect of the protective layer.

Quality Combs: Before combing, apply some smoothing agent or leave-in conditioner to effectively reduce static electricity. Additionally, use wooden or horn combs instead of plastic or metal ones, as the latter are more likely to generate static and damage the hair.

&；nbsp；

Problem 3 &；nbsp； Frizzy and Unruly Hair

Haircare Countermeasure: Add a smoothing agent to your hair.

Correct Styling Method: Many people experience frizz and unruliness due to rushing to completely dry their hair after washing. It is recommended that after washing, when the hair is halfway dry, use the low-temperature setting on your hairdryer to blow-dry it to 80% dryness. Then, apply a leave-in conditioner to the hair except for the roots, comb through while drying the rest of the hair.

Protein-Based Hair Care: Adding a small amount of protein to your shampoo, massaging gently into the scalp, provides excellent hair care results.

Secrets of Hair Care

Since hair tends to be dry in winter and oily in summer, when hair becomes dry, apply many softening agents and braid the hair, keeping it for 24 hours. This method is especially suitable during exam periods when focus is on studying rather than hair care. After washing off the oil, the hair will shine and avoid dryness. Sometimes, a 30-minute home softening agent can be used, along with an egg yolk and a small amount of castor oil mixed and applied to the head, wrapped in plastic, and absorbed for 30 minutes before being gently rinsed off with warm water and shampoo.

If the hair gets oily and there's no time to wash it, baby powder can be used. Sprinkle the powder on the hair, comb gently starting by leaning forward, add more powder as needed,
