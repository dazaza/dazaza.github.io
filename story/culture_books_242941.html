Restlessness, two girls, three loves. The same growth, different fates.

Zhong Xiaowen, she is inferior, but more self-respecting. She is used to hesitating between indulgence and salvation, wandering between innocence and evil. She fears loss and gain, so her fate is destined to be tragic.

Ye Yu, she is proud, radical, and rebellious. She has long been accustomed to confronting the whole world with an oppositional attitude. She would rather be shattered into pieces in order to obtain a complete love.

Is it the attraction of souls, or the entanglement of fate? They are like standing at both ends of a mirror, finding that each other is actually another self.

Thus they sympathize with each other and get closer.

They think that by breathing they can warm each other, relying on each other's temperature to forget the coldness of life. Unfortunately, they are too similar, even in their pursuit of love.

And between love and hurt, is there really a place where one can live in peace and warmth?

Fate, none can defy. Therefore, they are destined to betray each other, using cruelty to interpret restlessness.

Is it true that every girl's growth must go through so much pain? Is it only after pain that one can understand the meaning of growing up?

Restlessness, not just a youth novel, but also our real growth. If you have ever felt restless in your soul and yearned for happiness.
