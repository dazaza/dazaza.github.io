If he said so, then it's settled.

by whboaiaa57jq on 2011-04-27 15:10:09

Ge Wei made a guess, associating with the identity of An Yun's father, he immediately figured out the background of this girl named Di Ling, couldn't help but feel secretly shocked, but was helpless. After all, having members of the highest national intelligence agency in his own company wasn't a good thing, and this girl was a high-level agent that others had been cultivating since she was young.[5201]

As the boss, Ge Wei decided to go out first and try to deal with these employees.

Lin Xiaoru's impression of Ge Wei was not bad. She had no objection to Ge Wei taking over Fantasy City, smiling and said: "That day I thought you were here to poach talents, but I didn't expect you to actually take away our entire company."

In order to show his generosity, Ge Wei treated everyone in the company to a nearby tea restaurant during lunch break. Now, a few people were sitting together, chatting and laughing.

After hearing Lin Xiaoru's words, Ge Wei felt proud inside, but his face showed an expression of indifference. Finally, he slowly said: "In fact, I have had a dream since I was young, which is to promote the cultural progress of the whole world with my own hands!"

Seeing everyone showing expressions of trying not to laugh, Ge Wei put on a serious face again, "Don't laugh, I'm being serious. But now that I've grown up, I found that many ideas from my childhood were just dreams. However, I still don't want to give up on my past dreams. Maybe you will think I am too arrogant, but I believe there are ways to achieve my dreams. Have you ever thought about, one day, our company's products could be spread all over the world? All computer users would have our antivirus software installed on their machines, our products might even be more widespread than 'smelling death', and our software can comprehensively protect users' personal data from being leaked. Don't you think this, to some extent, promotes the development of society?"

After hearing Ge Wei's words, everyone was shocked on one hand, but on the other hand, they secretly laughed. This was almost impossible. In today's rapidly evolving computer software industry, wanting to become a monopoly enterprise like 'smelling death' was almost impossible, let alone surpassing it. Wasn't this just a joke?

Seeing everyone showing expressions of disdain, Ge Wei felt quite helpless. He even wanted to shout out loud: My software is a brand new technology that exceeds current technology by ten years. With enough marketing strategies, sweeping the globe is inevitable!

But he couldn't do that. In today's era of rapid technological development, being ten years ahead is a huge concept. Everyone present knows that if he really said that, others would first think he was boasting, then consider him crazy.

However, among the few people present, one believed Ge Wei, and that was An Yun. Compared to others, An Yun knew the most about this North Shield. With her talent, she easily discovered the extraordinary nature of this software. It almost doesn't consume any memory resources, yet it can comprehensively and seamlessly protect the computer. No security software on the market currently can achieve this, which is almost an unreplicable achievement!

Gao Ya pinched her chin, looking at Ge Wei who was talking eloquently with great interest. She also spent a lot of time studying North Shield, second only to An Yun among the company personnel. But so far, she has been unable to analyze its source code. Thus, she couldn't help but develop a certain degree of admiration for Ge Wei. However, she was always a proud girl when she was in school, and now it was difficult for her to completely admire this man who looked somewhat like a spoiled rich kid.

"Mr. Ge, if what you said is true, it would indeed be a good thing. But currently, we are unable to analyze the true principle behind North Shield. You wouldn't be planning to complete the entire software compilation yourself, would you? Then wouldn't we all be redundant?" Wen Ya's words were not polite at all, sharply pointing out the biggest problem currently existing in the company. That is, although these girls have high technical skills, they are based on existing technology. To fully understand the subtleties contained in North Shield would take an unknown amount of time.

After Gao Ya finished speaking, several others immediately agreed.

Ge Wei also found this issue troubling, but suddenly had an idea. Since his management authority over Ruobing has increased, wouldn't it be easy for her to set up an online classroom?

"Don't worry, I've already thought about this problem. Starting from 10 PM tonight, I will set up an online classroom on the internet. Everyone can come to the online classroom to learn, uh, not exactly, to follow me, hoping to be of help to everyone." A master needs to maintain a certain pose as a master to some extent. Only through such condescending teaching activities can these talented girls be completely convinced.

"Ruobing, prepare a teaching plan tonight and teach those few girls in my company. There's no need to teach them overly profound things. Just teach them some core technologies of the software, that's fine. Don't teach them anything too exaggerated, to avoid prematurely triggering the end of the world," Ge Wei instructed Ruobing as soon as he got home, eager to avoid being flustered later.

Then after dinner, he personally visited Hua Yuande. Coming to the gate of the Hua family mansion again, Ge Wei's mood was completely different. Recalling how he sneaked in last time to save Hua Ningsnow, he couldn't help but call it thrilling. When he felt good, he'd definitely do it again.

Upon hearing that Ge Wei came, Hua Yuande immediately ran out. Looking at Ge Wei with Hua Yuande's eyes, Ge Wei felt chills all over his body. How did he feel like a little chicken being stared at by a weasel? Who was deceiving whom here?

Sitting on the luxurious sofa in the living room, Ge Wei closed his eyes to rest for a moment, gathering his momentum. He thought silently that he must overwhelm Hua Yuande in terms of presence to avoid a messy situation caused by this old rogue's outburst, "Hua, uh, uncle. I came here today because I have something to tell you."

Hearing this, Hua Yuande smiled and looked at Ge Wei, saying: "Little Ge, just say it, I'm listening."

The more he heard, the less it seemed right. Ge Wei gritted his teeth, "Actually, Hua Ningsnow is indeed with me, and furthermore, she and I have been in love for a long time."

"Ah, this is wonderful!" Hua Yuande was so happy that he stood up from the sofa as if he had found billions of dollars, full of joy, chuckling non-stop.

Ge Wei was dumbfounded, confused. What scheme was Hua Yuande playing?
