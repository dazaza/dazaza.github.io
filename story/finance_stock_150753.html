The grain market has three major highlights - facing directional choices

by longersing on 2009-05-18 23:47:25

Last week, the Shenzhen and Shanghai stock market indices showed a trend of repeated high-level oscillation consolidation. The Shenzhen and Shanghai indices increased slightly by 0.89% and 0.75%, respectively, both closing with Doji stars. It was noticed that the market trading volume last week showed a gradually decreasing trend. The reduction in trading volume indicated a convergence in the market's upward momentum, which will also constrain the future upward space for the indices. It is worth noting that after the repeated high-level oscillation consolidation, the short-term market will face a directional choice.

From the performance of hot spots in the market last week, influenced by factors such as the continued spread of the H1N1 influenza pandemic and two confirmed cases of H1N1 influenza domestically, the biopharmaceutical sector was heavily pursued by mainline funds. Representative varieties like Daguangene (002030) surged by 60.97% this week, while Haitang Biological (00078), Huale Pharmaceutical (00607), and Tianmu Pharmaceutical (600671) all saw increases exceeding 20%.

Additionally, it is worth noting that last week, the State Council's executive meeting discussed and in principle approved "Several Policies to Promote the Accelerated Development of the Biotechnology Industry," which became an important opportunity for speculation in the biopharmaceutical sector. "Several Policies to Promote the Accelerated Development of the Biotechnology Industry" proposed that we must seize the opportunities presented by the global biotech revolution and industrial revolution, cultivating the biotechnology industry as a pillar of high-tech industries in our country. Focusing on biopharmaceuticals, bio-agriculture, bio-energy, bio-manufacturing, and bio-environmental protection industries, efforts will be made to vigorously develop modern biotechnology industries. At the same time, the State Council has deployed further measures to leverage technological support and accelerate the implementation of national major science and technology projects. Over the next two years, 62.8 billion yuan in funding will be arranged to expedite the implementation of 11 major scientific and technological projects, including the cultivation of genetically modified biological new varieties, the development of major new drugs, and the prevention and treatment of AIDS and viral hepatitis and other major infectious diseases.

It is worth emphasizing that the strengthening of the biopharmaceutical sector is a continuation of policy-driven thematic investment opportunities. The密集 issuance of policies is an important driving factor for this round of market conditions, with related thematic investment opportunities repeatedly running through the development of the market. In addition to policies accelerating the development of the biotechnology industry, regional development measures have also been issued intensively. For example, this week, the State Council released "Several Opinions on Supporting Fujian Province in Accelerating the Construction of the Straits West Coast Economic Zone." Following the approval by the State Council of the construction of the "double center" in Shanghai, it is understood that the "Overall Plan for the Comprehensive Supporting Reform Pilot in Shenzhen" has also been approved by the State Council. Driven by regional revitalization policies, it is expected that related regional thematic investment opportunities will continue to perform.

From the perspective of the overall market trend, as the rebound deepens and the divergence between bulls and bears increases, the technical resistance to the upward movement of the market gradually grows. After the high-level oscillation consolidation last week, whether the market can continue to expand its upward space has become the focus of market attention. In the medium term, under the background of loose liquidity and economic improvement, the Shenzhen and Shanghai markets still have the potential for further gains. However, in the short term, the author believes that three key signals should be closely watched: First, whether there will be a collective sell-off of small and medium-sized capitalization stocks that have seen large increases in price earlier, and whether the massive release of restrictions on China Unicom (600050) on May 19 will affect the short-term trend remains to be observed. Second, the performance of heavyweight sectors should be closely monitored, especially the trends of heavyweights like PetroChina (601857) and Sinopec (600028). Additionally, the performance of other weighty sectors, such as banks, securities, and other lagging blue-chip stocks, needs to be tracked; successful switching of hot spots is a major driving force for the continuation of the market. Third, the changes in market trading volume should be closely monitored; the close cooperation of trading volume will be crucial for the market to reach new levels.
