Kaspersky official forum adds 10,000 KAV 7.0 activation codes as a gift

by yinyin on 2007-09-26 20:22:24

On September 21st, Kaspersky's headquarters in Russia deactivated some of the severely misused activation keys, including those that had been publicly shared and the "earthquake-specific keys" provided during the undersea communication cable break at the end of 2006.

In order to allow more users to use genuine products, Kaspersky will increase the number of slots by 10,000 in the ongoing member gift-giving event on the forum. This means Kaspersky will give away 20,000 one-year free activation keys for KAV 7.0. The rules for our event giveaway remain unchanged; as long as you are one of the first 20,000 members with 30 points or more, strictly follow the forum rules when posting, and have no violations, you will receive Kaspersky's KAV 7.0!

For detailed giveaway rules, please see:

http://bbs.kaspersky.com.cn/viewthread.php?tid=2846&extra=page%3D1

The activation codes for the first batch of 1,009 winning members have been sent to their registered email addresses. For more details, please see:
