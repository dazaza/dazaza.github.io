The old type of Juyye decoration's simulation animal light - Goya LED Sakura Tree Light (some also call it Goya LED Peach Blossom Tree Light). Nowadays, whether domestically or internationally, such as in the U.S., Europe, Southeast Asia, etc., it has quietly become a new trend, and during holidays, it is also a great choice for gifting to friends!

Introduction to Goya LED Sakura Tree Light:

1. The Goya LED Sakura Tree Light is a new type of simulated landscape light, environmentally friendly, with a long lifespan, and aesthetically pleasing. The tree trunk and leaves are made of simulated materials, and the light source is LED. The height generally ranges from 0.8 meters to 6 meters, widely used in home decoration, courtyards, leisure squares, halls, etc. The energy-saving and durable features of the Goya LED Sakura Tree Light make up for the shortcomings of traditional landscape lights, making it a revolutionary product among similar lights;

2. The Goya LED Sakura Tree Light integrates beautification, illumination, and greening;

3. The Goya LED Sakura Tree Light can be chosen in various shapes like peach trees, plum trees, and cherry blossom trees, and color combinations such as red, yellow, white, green, blue, and purple;

4. The main trunk of the Goya LED Sakura Tree Light is made of steel pipes, and the branches are either steel pipes or rebars, with an outer layer of simulated bark. All materials are highly flexible and anti-oxidation environmental protection materials. The leaves are rigid anti-oxidation materials, and the light flow is LED particle lights, all waterproof treated, evergreen, low voltage, and long-lasting.

5. The tree trunks and branches of the Goya LED Sakura Tree Light are welded together, making them more reliable and better looking. Additionally, green insulated adhesive paper is wrapped around the trunks and branches, making the tree more beautiful and realistic compared to ordinary trees.

6. The Goya LED Sakura Tree Light uses a 220V voltage input, and the actual voltage of the tree light source is 12-54V (external transformer), energy-saving and environmentally friendly, ensuring safe use. Unlike other LED products, direct high voltage can extend the life of outdoor use due to weather reasons, and adding a single-layer heat shrink tube around the light flow can effectively prevent damage from severe forces and water flow. The protection level is IP65, suitable for both indoor and outdoor use;

7. The leaves and LED particle lights of the Goya LED Sakura Tree Light are fixed on each branch, making its maintenance simpler and more convenient than other decorative lights. Simply replace the damaged light string by detaching and attaching a new one.

Advantages of the Goya LED Sakura Tree Light:

1. Energy-efficient, high brightness, significantly better than non-LED lights;

2. Safe, especially using low voltage, providing peace of mind;

3. Vivid colors, surpassing any other light flows;

4. Long lifespan, incomparable to other lights;

5. Realistic design, completely simulating real plants, immersing people in natural landscapes.
