NewWebPick E-zine Issue 6

by kdinsky on 2008-06-06 19:01:00

The world's largest circulation art and design electronic magazine, *NewWebPick*, has officially released its 6th issue globally! It should be an essential item for every designer, in my opinion. :)

The *NewWebPick* e-magazine is mainly aimed at people who love art and design. Its content primarily includes: graphic printing, industrial design, web design, FLASH, 3D, illustration, UI, experimental art, digital photography, etc. We hope this magazine can inspire the creativity of designers, and we will collect various design news and information from all over the world.

Currently, *NewWebPick* has a professional readership of 2.6 million from 9,200 cities across 150 countries worldwide, making it one of the most influential design e-magazines globally.

Starting from the 6th issue, we have made significant adjustments to the content structure. The new sections are as follows:

Event Reports, Recommended for You, Special Features, Global Competitions, Elite Interviews, Designer Companions, Graffiti, CG Planet, Tutorials, Close-ups, Hunter's Corner, Photography Collections, Commercial Design, Experimental Base, and Website Selections — a total of 15 sections.

Reading the magazine is very convenient; no special reading program is required. After downloading, you can simply click to run it. Besides using the mouse, starting from the 5th issue, keyboard operations have been added. Page turning, jumping pages, first and last pages, and specific page navigation can all be accomplished with single key presses.

We hope you enjoy this issue!
