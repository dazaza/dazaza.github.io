Edison Chen apologizes in a statement, says he will permanently exit Hong Kong's entertainment industry

by czp on 2008-02-21 16:00:05

Here is the full text of Edison Chen's apology

I am very sorry for everything that has happened. I want to apologize to all of you for having to go through all this. I also want to thank you for giving me this opportunity to say everything I have bottled up inside.

I hope that after today, I can gain your forgiveness for this incident and everything it has caused. I am truly very sorry.

I hope that all of you can accept my apology.

In the past few weeks, I have been with my mother, my family and relatives. They have given me sincere support. They have always supported and helped me. I admit that most of the photos were taken by me, but these photos are very private. I never intended to show these photos to anyone. These photos were illegally stolen from me. At the same time, they were circulated without my consent. There is no doubt that those who obtained these photos maliciously spread them on the Internet. This incident has deteriorated to the point where it has affected the whole society. From this aspect, I am very sorry.

I now want to apologize to everyone for the hurt they have suffered. I also want to apologize to all the relevant women and their relatives for any harm they have suffered. I am sorry. I also want to apologize to my parents because I have caused them pain. Most importantly, I want to apologize to all the people of Hong Kong. I sincerely apologize to you, holding nothing back, and sincerely apologize to you.

I know that many young people in Hong Kong admire the idols of this society. In this regard, I have not set an example. However, I hope that this incident can teach everyone, especially the young people of our society, a lesson. What I have done is not something you should look up to. During my absence, I made an important decision. I will fully fulfill all my promises. After this, I will completely withdraw from the entertainment circle of Hong Kong. I made such a decision so that I could give myself a lesson, heal my wounds and my soul, and spend some time doing charity work.

I have been helping the police since the photos were leaked. I have been assisting them after the press conference. I have a responsibility to help them with the investigation. I also hope that this case will end soon. I think everyone hopes that it will end as soon as possible. I would like to take this opportunity to thank the police for their hard work on this case. Thank you!

I believe that the top priority of everyone, including myself, is to stop all the pain and not let this incident happen. We should protect all innocent people and young people from being harmed by this incident. From this perspective, I ask my lawyer to help the victims of this incident as much as possible within the limits of the law. I believe that when I held the press conference, my lawyer suggested that we would send you a press release.

Finally, I would like to thank all of you who are present today for listening to my explanation. I would also like to apologize again to all the women affected by this incident, their relatives, my family, and all the people of Hong Kong and the entire society.
