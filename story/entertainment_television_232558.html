"No Stopping Now" is a sad yet beautiful story about how an intelligent and innocent young woman gradually turns bad while trying to protect herself and her mother. This drama makes the audience ponder whether human nature is inherently evil or if it becomes evil due to dire circumstances.

The female lead (Hong Yeon-seo) is played by Kim Gwi-ri. Yeon-seo is a warm-hearted, intelligent, and optimistic person. However, due to her husband's betrayal and discovering that her mother-in-law may be involved in her own mother's death, Kim Gwi-ri gradually transforms into a cold-hearted person.

The male lead (No Soo-reuk) is portrayed by Lee Ji-hoon. Soo-reuk is a liberal financial analyst who is passionate, easy-going, enjoys life, and prefers light work. Watching Yeon-seo suffer and being unable to help, Lee Ji-hoon remains steadfastly by her side, loving her through it all.

The supporting male role (Lee Byung-gu) is acted by Won Ki-jun. Byung-gu is Yeon-seo's husband, someone who has never lacked for anything and always gets what he wants, making him self-centered and selfish.

Yeon-seo's mother (Gok Hye-sun) is played by Lee Bo-hae. The character of Hye-sun's sister (Gok Hye-sook) is portrayed by Seon Yeong-yo. The mother of Byung-gu and mother-in-law of Yeon-seo (Lim Bong-ja), a key figure in all the mystery surrounding this drama, is acted by Jung Ae-lee. Park Ha-sun will portray the role of Byung-gu's sister (Lee Kook-ae).

Jung Han-yong will play the role of Soo-reuk's father (No Man-jae), with his secretary played by Lee Sun-geum.
