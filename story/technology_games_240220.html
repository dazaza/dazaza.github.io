World of Warcraft resumes formal operations; Lich King continues to be "coming soon"

by cheryl098 on 2009-09-19 16:24:08

Netease issued a new announcement today declaring that the Chinese version of "World of Warcraft" will start charging from today. However, for players, how much attraction does this world's only "The Burning Crusade" still have?

Netease seems to also realize that simply announcing the restoration of "The Burning Crusade" cannot attract many old players. Therefore, in this announcement, they quietly stated that the expansion pack of "World of Warcraft", "Wrath of the Lich King", is about to be submitted for review. Just like The9, Netease also believes that "Wrath of the Lich King" can open in the near future. For players, they can only hope that Netease and The9 do not end up with the same misfortune and once again put "World of Warcraft" into difficulty.

The following is the full text of Netease's latest announcement:

"World of Warcraft" resumes formal operation

2009-09-19

We are very pleased to announce today that "World of Warcraft" has resumed formal operation in mainland China. Now players all over the country can create new accounts, purchase and recharge game time, officially return to the global "World of Warcraft" family, and enjoy this world's most popular massively multiplayer online role-playing game. "World of Warcraft" game cards are available for sale nationwide. For more information on game cards, please visit http://www.warcraftchina.com/sales/intro.html.

New "World of Warcraft" players can log on to the Battle.net Passport website www.battlenet.com.cn to create a Battle.net Passport and a new "World of Warcraft" account. Details can be found on the New Account Creation Guide page at http://www.warcraftchina.com/services/account-creation-guide. Returning players will see the remaining game time that was recharged before the transition period, which can be used to continue playing. In addition, all eligible returning players will receive a special in-game pet - Gladiator Mogrimus, a loyal and vigilant companion who accompanies players on their adventures. For questions about Mogrimus, please refer to the FAQ.

In addition, we would like to inform everyone that the game version of the second expansion pack of "World of Warcraft", "Wrath of the Lich King", has been prepared, and we will submit it to the government for approval as soon as possible, hoping that everyone can set foot on the brand-new continent of Northrend and start a new journey in the near future. For the latest progress of "Wrath of the Lich King" and related news of "World of Warcraft" in mainland China, please stay tuned to the official website of "World of Warcraft": http://www.warcraftchina.com.

Finally, as the 60th anniversary of the founding of the country approaches, we wish everyone a happy and harmonious National Day holiday! We sincerely wish our country to flourish and become even more prosperous!

"World of Warcraft" Operation Team
