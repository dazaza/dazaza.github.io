Taurus and Scorpio's seven-year itch (Picture)

by motianyang on 2011-03-31 11:28:26

Sina Netizen: s224z218 Emotional Story Submission

I am a typical Taurus woman, sometimes, the so-called persistence in love makes me not understand myself.

He is a typical Scorpio man, I don't know how to describe him.

We fell in love in the first year of high school. The relationship back then was very pure, holding hands would make us blush. In college, we were in the same city, meeting once a week, going shopping, watching movies, and the good times passed quickly... In the first year of college, he said he was tired and wanted to break up. Naive and stubborn Taurus thought that she made him so tired, so she kept tolerating him, not knowing that there was another her in his heart at that time. But eventually, Taurus didn't let go and chose to forgive. In the blink of an eye, we both graduated. Taurus continued to pursue her postgraduate studies, while Scorpio started working. He told her that he would wait for her for 3 years, and after graduation, they would be together, so Taurus believed him and was happy every day. Until one day, Scorpio told her that he was tired, and Taurus understood what "tired" meant...

Three years of pain, seven years of itch, Taurus was very confused, persisting in something impossible, how much damage it would cause to herself.

But giving up on a seven-year relationship was so painful, it was excruciating pain.

Taurus chose to forgive, just stubbornly thinking that this was a momentary mistake, stubbornly sticking with the man who didn't love her anymore. Our future is like a watch with almost no power left, stopping at a point where no one knows.
