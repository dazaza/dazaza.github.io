Return journey to hometown

by c104235028 on 2009-10-05 00:06:58

Years after returning home with his new girlfriend, Elizabeth, Mac was warmly welcomed by his fellow townsfolk. However, his high school girlfriend Shelby still had lingering feelings for him. She had hoped to use this opportunity to develop something new with Mac, but she realized he already had someone in his heart. Frustrated, Shelby one night drove her car and ran over Elizabeth, then kidnapped her to her own house where she planned to brutally torture her. Mac searched everywhere for Elizabeth. Would he be able to save her in time? When love enters a wrong and obsessive dead end, it often infinitely triggers one's anger, which can only be released through revenge on others. Mike, played by Matt Lanter, is undoubtedly a star figure in the town where he was born and raised. Not only was he a well-known figure in high school, but he was also the first in his family to attend college. His football talent was recognized, and he entered the prestigious Northwestern University with a scholarship to continue his studies. The annual Christmas family reunion was approaching, and Mike, as expected, returned to the small town to celebrate the holiday with his family and friends. However, what surprised everyone slightly was that Mike was not coming back alone; standing beside him was his new girlfriend from college, Elizabeth, played by Jessica Stroup - a beautiful and wealthy girl from Chicago. But no one was more shocked than Shelby, played by Mischa Barton - Mike's former high school girlfriend and the real campus queen. Even though they had broken up, Shelby could not forgive Mike for moving on so quickly. When love eventually turned into hatred, she began to methodically implement her plan. She wanted to help Mike get rid of Elizabeth's influence and do so secretly to handle this urgent issue properly. Meanwhile, Shelby decided to use every means possible to win Mike and his heart back, making Elizabeth the biggest stumbling block in the process - one that Shelby desperately wanted to remove.
