Club Profile

Tianjin Teda Football Club was founded on February 16, 1998, with a registered capital of 50 million yuan, jointly invested and managed by Tianjin Teda Investment Holding Co., Ltd., Tianjin Teda Group Co., Ltd., Tianjin Development Zone Construction Group, North International Trust Investment Company, and Development Zone State-owned Assets Operation Company. Among them, Teda Investment Holding Co., Ltd. has total assets of 22 billion yuan, Teda Group 8.2 billion yuan, Construction Group 2 billion yuan, CCTV5 live broadcast, North International Trust Investment Company 3 billion yuan, and State-owned Assets Operation Company 4 billion yuan.

Tianjin is the hometown of Chinese football and has a deep football foundation. For many years, it has been home to many famous coaches and talented players. The Tianjin football team was established in 1957, based on the original national white team, with comprehensive technology, strong style, and solid play, forming its own school in Chinese football. In order to develop football in Tianjin, players from all over the country such as Zeng Xuelin settled in Tianjin, promoting the development of football in Tianjin. Since then, Tianjin has become a major center for Chinese football. It has consistently ranked high in national competitions, especially becoming the double champion of Chinese football in 1960. The Tianjin football team has won five championships, five runners-up, and five third places in the National Games, the Chinese Football First Division League, and the Chinese Football Championship.

At the beginning of professionalization in 1994, Tianjin started from the Jia B league, under the leadership of coach Lin Xinjiang, they promoted to Jia A. After that, the Tianjin team experienced coaching from Lin Xinjiang, Zuo Shusheng, Chen Jingang, and others, showing good performances in some matches every year but always worrying about relegation at the end. Finally, in 1997, the unfortunate reality came true. This was a wake-up call for Tianjin, this traditional football city. Determined to reform, the Tianjin people restructured the club, merged the original Samsung and Vanke teams, and established the Teda Football Club. They finally returned to Jia A in 1998 with an undefeated record, winning the Jia B championship. Afterwards, Jin Zhiyang, known for his oratory skills, was hired. Under his leadership, the Teda team broke some fateful win-loss records, built confidence, and became a formidable force in the Jia A league.

In 1997, Tianjin football fell into a trough, with the Samsung team dropping to Jia B and the Vanke team to the second division. Finding someone capable to take over the football club became a topic of concern throughout the city. At this time, Tianjin Economic-Technological Development Area (Teda) stepped forward and formed the Tianjin Teda Football Club. In 1999, the Development Zone Management Committee handed over the club to its subordinate Teda Group for management, with Teda Group Chairman Xian Hengqi serving as chairman.

To maintain past glory, in February 1998, after the establishment of the Teda Football Club by the Teda Group of Tianjin Development Zone, through the efforts of all members of the club and the entire team of Teda Football, they successfully brought the already relegated to Jia B Tianjin Football Team back to Jia A. They achieved promotion three rounds early, an unbeaten home record, and first place in points.

After entering the Jia A league, the Teda Football Club further standardized the management of the club, strengthened the training and competition of the team, selected well-known domestic coaches and foreign coaches, established a complete player ladder system to ensure the cultivation of reserve forces. At the same time, expanded the operation of enterprises, strengthening the overall strength of the club.

2004 was the inaugural year of the Chinese Super League, and the Tianjin Teda team unprecedentedly increased construction efforts, hiring renowned coach Qi Wusheng as head coach, introducing several well-known domestic players and capable foreign players, setting the goal of "maintaining sixth place and striving for third". The resurgence of Tianjin football will no longer be a dream.

The Tianjin Teda football team boasts famous national team player Liu Yunfei. Former national team member Yu Genwei scored a crucial goal in the 2002 World Cup qualifiers, securing China's spot in the World Cup. Liu Yunfei is the starting goalkeeper for the national team, possessing excellent professional athlete qualities, and was awarded the Best Goalkeeper of the 2001 Jia A League. They have made significant contributions to the Chinese national football team's breakthrough in Asia and moving towards the world.

Additionally, the Teda football team also includes famous athletes Sun Jianjun, Zhang Xiaorui, Chi Rongliang, Cao Yang, Zhang Shuo, etc. Among them, Zhang Xiaorui once won the Best Newcomer Award of the Pepsi Jia A League.

The Teda football team also has several players who have been selected for the national Olympic team, namely Cao Yang, Liu Wei, Zong Lei, and Zhang Shuo. Meanwhile, Teda Youth Team players like Hao Junmin have been selected for the national youth team.

Furthermore, the Teda Football Club has strengthened the management of the Tianjin Teda Youth Team and Junior Team, continuously improving training and learning conditions, strictly selecting promising young talents for cultivation. The Teda second and third-tier teams have performed commendably in national competitions of the same age group, demonstrating a clear advantage. The Teda Youth Team won the runner-up in the 1999 National U-21 Football League and the champion in the 2000 National U-21 Football League Qualification Tournament. Currently, several players have entered the Teda first team, showing good technical and tactical levels, and playing important roles in the league.

In the 2005 season, the team performed excellently, ranking fourth in the Chinese Super League standings.
