— Creating the Myth of Cold Weapons in Gunfight Games

The ever-changing battlefield situation, the dazzlingly realistic weapons and equipment, and an impeccable growth and interaction system have created a nearly perfect, hyper-realistic mercenary world for the gunfight game "Cross Fire" (shortened as CF) in 2008. Since its debut in China, CF has captured the attention of countless FPS enthusiasts and received thunderous applause.

FPS fans love the thrilling and exciting battlefield environment of CF; they enjoy the more humanized interactive system that surpasses RPG games. Of course, what fascinates FPS fans most is CF's unique game cultivation mode. From a private to a general, step by step, it ultimately brings immense satisfaction in the online world, something that cannot be experienced in other FPS games so far. Therefore, when "Cross Fire" was launched, it gained recognition from numerous FPS game enthusiasts and sparked a strong "gunfight shooting game wind" in the FPS and even the entire online gaming world.

Facing tens of millions of players, the CF official team not only ensures the game's quality excellence but also considers the player's perspective, continuously developing new elements and updating game versions. And today, CF's annual grand update - "Blade Warrior" makes a stunning appearance. Beyond adding more enriched and humanized game elements, "Blade Warrior" pioneers the era of cold weapons in FPS games, allowing all FPS players to experience a new mode and exciting gameplay.

New competitive mode: Special Warfare (Knife Battle) - Arena

This is the core content of the "Blade Warrior" new version launched today by "Cross Fire," and it is the favorite of players who are passionate about "deciding victory with cold weapons." In the confined arena, there is no smoke from guns and ammunition; only the gleaming daggers and sharp, bloodthirsty military axes remain. What is needed here is not marksmanship but warriors who possess willpower, courage, intelligence, and combat skills. As the saying goes, in a narrow encounter, the brave win! The honor and disgrace of the "Blade Warrior" depend on you!

New weapons and equipment: Limited Edition Gold Weapons

Have you seen an AK-47? It can be found everywhere in movies and games, nothing special. But how about an AK-47 made of gold? That might only be found in CF. The entire gun body is made of pure gold, making its color extremely eye-catching. Additionally, it increases the magazine capacity to 38 rounds based on the original model. The nobility and rarity of the Gold AK-47 determine its value; those who own this premium weapon are certainly not ordinary people.

In addition to the Gold AK-47, the new version also introduces the Razer submachine gun, which closely resembles future weapons, the essential close-combat weapon Military Axe, the AK-47-A with a bayonet and larger magazine capacity, the SPAS-12 frequently featured in movies with massive firepower, and the Desert Eagle series weapons with camouflage appearances.

New player interaction system: Gift Giving

Send guns, cannons, and explosives! The impressive debut of the gift-giving system in the "Blade Warrior" new version marks a new milestone for FPS online games. In previous FPS games, firearms and ammunition could not be gifted, leaving little room for player interaction. CF's pioneering gift-giving system fills this gap well. Through exchanges of gifts, players are sure to make a large number of like-minded friends who share their hearts and souls, experiencing the endless fun brought by CF together.

In addition, the revenge system introduced in the "Blade Warrior" new version allows players to settle scores amidst gunfire and explosions; the final kill concept rewards experience bonuses to the player who kills the last enemy; the bounty mode and elimination mode are separated from the team mode; cool new items such as battlefields, blue skies and white clouds, business cards, sunglasses, and military caps... Numerous charming new elements are available in the game's new version on October 22nd - "Blade Warrior," don't miss the official new version.
