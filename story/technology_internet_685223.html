Xiaoguang said that he just wanted to teach this "uncle" a lesson so that he would not cause trouble for his family anymore. Unexpectedly, after Wang An was beaten, his health condition worsened, he could not eat, and his complexion became increasingly worse.

Thus, Xiaoguang called a few companions and decided to teach this "uncle" a lesson. However, he never expected that this would be the beginning of another tragedy.

Wang An was lying in bed at the time. Xiaoguang covered his head with a quilt, then a few people went up and started punching and kicking him, causing multiple bruises and swellings on Wang An's body.

Xiaoguang vaguely remembered that in his childhood, besides his mom and dad, there was also a "uncle" who was very close to his mother, who would often appear. In Xiaoguang's memory, this "uncle" who earned money by riding a tricycle had always been close to his family and sometimes bought things for him to eat.

On February 3rd this year, Yongjia County People's Procuratorate approved the legal arrest of Xiaoguang and his classmates Chen Mou and three others on suspicion of intentional injury.

Four boys caused a tragedy resulting in death

Xiaoguang increasingly felt that if it weren't for this "uncle," his originally harmonious family could have remained intact. Everything became chaotic because of his intervention.

Xiaoguang longed for family harmony but had to face incessant disturbances. At the rebellious age of adolescence, Xiaoguang thought that perhaps he could do something to change all this.

Afterwards, Xiaoguang recalled that at the time he was extremely angry, which led him to say such words. However, his parents only treated him as a child and simply responded with "adult matters are not for children to meddle in."

"If you keep arguing, I will go and beat him up"

Recently, Xiaoguang's parents were arguing more fiercely, even reaching the point of discussing divorce several times. "If you keep arguing, I will go and beat him up." Finally, Xiaoguang said these "harsh words" in front of his parents.

Xiaoguang's parents often argued, even considering divorce, and in the eyes of their 16-year-old son Xiaoguang (a pseudonym), all of this was not unrelated to a certain "uncle."

Xiaoguang's academic performance was average, and he attended a local ordinary school. Last December 26th, Xiaoguang invited classmate Chen Mou and others to the home of "Uncle" Wang An (a pseudonym).

Xiaoguang's mother, upon hearing that Wang An had died, initially believed that her husband had arranged for someone to beat him. Later, when she found out that her son was the instigator, she deeply regretted it. Xiaoguang's mother said that she regretted not taking her son's "threats" seriously, which ultimately led to the tragedy.

Wang An was already divorced and usually had no one to take care of him. His family later learned about the situation and quickly sent him to the hospital and reported it to the police. Wang An eventually died due to ineffective treatment on the afternoon of December 29th last year.

Yesterday, Wenzhou Yongjia Procuratorate announced that Xiaoguang and his classmates Chen Mou and three others have been legally approved for arrest on suspicion of intentional injury.

The procuratorate introduced that during the investigation, Xiaoguang's mother initially admitted to having an extramarital affair, but later denied it, saying that nothing had happened.

As people grow up, they gradually understand many things. Xiaoguang naturally also knew what kind of person this "uncle" really was.

However, the resentment accumulated in Xiaoguang's heart made him believe that teaching this "uncle" a lesson was the only way to restore peace at home.

Moreover, compared to these events, Xiaoguang's deeper memories revolved around the frequent arguments at home after this "uncle" appeared. As a young boy, he repeatedly witnessed his parents arguing fiercely.

Fearing that he might not be able to handle the situation alone, Xiaoguang specially spent 15 yuan to buy a water pipe as a "weapon."

Upon examination, Wang An was determined to have died from heart failure due to a hypertensive heart disease attack. The beating inflicted by Xiaoguang and others resulted in multiple injuries across his body, accelerating his death.
