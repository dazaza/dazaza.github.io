Couple holding a child stole an electric vehicle and was caught on surveillance cameras (picture group)

by zzf000zxyr6 on 2011-07-28 17:02:28

A woman prepares to steal a car.

The woman uses tools to cut the vehicle's wiring.

The woman’s theft process was captured by surveillance.

At 10:55 AM yesterday, the 110 Command Center of the Municipal Public Security Bureau received a report: after being away for 15 minutes, the electric bike left in the hospital parking lot had been stolen.

The Baihe Police Station of Jiangdong District responded to the call.

The person reporting the incident is Mr. Shi, 51 years old, a local resident of Ningbo.

At 10:30 AM, he rode his blue electric bike to run an errand. For convenience, he parked it at the parking lot near the entrance of Lihuili Hospital.

He locked the rear wheel of the electric bike with a U-lock and also secured the handlebars to an iron railing.

Fifteen minutes later, when he returned, he couldn’t find his bike. After searching twice, he realized that his newly purchased electric bike, which cost him 2000 yuan, had been stolen!

At this moment, a man approached with a child in his arms and said, “I’m holding a child. Can you help me unlock my bike?”

Mr. Shi, who was already upset, refused outright. The man then unlocked his own bike and rode away.

What Mr. Shi didn’t know was that it was this man’s wife who stole his electric bike.

Surveillance footage recorded the entire process of this couple stealing the bike with their child:

First, the woman rode her own electric bike into the parking lot. She noticed Mr. Shi’s new bike, so she parked hers nearby. She bent down and quietly touched Mr. Shi’s new bike, which immediately triggered the alarm. At this point, from a distance, her husband, Mr. Qin, gave her a signal, and she turned around and left. She returned 10 minutes later.

She squatted down, used tools to cut the bike’s wiring, and then pried open the lock. Within 30 seconds, she rode off on the bike.

Mr. Qin then entered the area with the child, drove away the electric bike his wife had brought, and left.

What they didn’t know was that officers from the Baihe Police Station had already set up a trap. As soon as they left the hospital, they were arrested.

Mr. Qin is 31 years old and from Guizhou Province; Ms. Yang is 30 years old and from Chongqing. They confessed to committing a total of 10 thefts. Mr. Qin said that his role was to keep watch while his wife was responsible for stealing the bikes. Carrying a child made people less suspicious of them.

Their baby is only three months old. Considering that the child is still in the breastfeeding period, the police granted bail to Ms. Yang, while Mr. Qin has been criminally detained.

Mr. Shi also successfully retrieved his electric bike.
