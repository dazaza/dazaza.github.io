Tips for the victims of the FeedBurner incident

by fisio on 2007-08-30 23:39:08

Since feeds.feedburner.com was unavailable yesterday, I've been checking Live Feed Hits in my FeedBurner background and I've noticed that there are still feeds coming in from China. In addition, there is very little, the actual shrimp can indeed continue to grab FeedBurner in the country, which is strange. This way, even though domestic users can't access FeedBurner, by catching shrimp or Google Reader, which is already in a foreign country, the entire subscription and access process is not actually affected unless the feed is redirected using FeedBurner.
