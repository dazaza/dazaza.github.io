Google employees' happy life, enter the sound-proof space capsule when tired (picture)

by maker on 2008-07-28 00:22:33

Tencent Technology News July 27th, Beijing Time — According to foreign media reports, it is well known that Google has always been famous for providing a good working environment for its employees. However, "Hearing is believing less than seeing," if you have the opportunity to visit Google's office locations, you will definitely be deeply impressed. Although not everyone has the chance to visit Google's office locations, we can still learn about the "happy lives" of Google employees through the following pictures:

 

1. Soundproof Space Capsule

If you are tired from work, you can rest in this space capsule. This entertainment facility not only soundproofs but also blocks any light from entering.

2. Back to Kindergarten

Between each floor of the office building, there is a slide installed. Besides its entertainment function, it can also be used by employees to evacuate in case of fire.

3. Delicious Food

During working hours, Google employees can go to the cafeteria to eat at any time, with a wide variety of food options available.

4. Work Location

Each employee is equipped with at least two large-screen monitors, and on average, each office houses 4-6 employees.

5. Technological Innovation

Whiteboards can be found everywhere in the office building, making it convenient for employees to jot down various new ideas at any time. A Google product manager said: "You may not get inspiration when sitting in the office; perhaps it comes to you while you're moving around."

6. Relax a Bit

In many office areas, it's easy to find billiards, video games, and other entertainment facilities.

7. Make a Call

On each floor of the office building, there is a telephone room. Each phone is installed in a decorated small house, making it convenient for Google employees to handle personal matters.

8. Technical Support

What if a Google employee's computer has problems? No worries, just bring it here. While waiting for your computer to be repaired, you can enjoy a drink.

9. Professional Massage

Tired both physically and mentally? There are professional masseurs waiting for you here.

10. Full-body Relaxation

Lie on an adjustable massage chair, watch the aquarium, and relax thoroughly.

11. Reading Environment

In the employee reading room, there are shelves full of books, including quite a few on how to program!
