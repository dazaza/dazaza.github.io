He is a favorite child, and also an abandoned child. He was chased, and also exiled. After losing weight, he won back respect; in respect, he gained more. When he left, he had already not left. His name is David Beckham, a person who always moves the world. At this moment, he is a touching player.

On the football field, he is not a genius, without God's favor. He just runs silently and waits quietly. The beautiful curve made him gain much affection, but also let him bear too much responsibility that should not have belonged to him. Until the former stubbornness turned into today's persistence, until the Beckham shadow gradually disappeared, leaving only a sigh.

We can say that he has not aged, because the memory of the naive smile is still fresh; we can say that he is aging, because time is merciless and only time is eternal. From the enviable midfield arc line to the red card in France, the pain of falling from heaven made him realize that life would not be perfect without regret. Then, when turning around, the Beckham shadow becomes clearer. With the glitter gone, he has changed, becoming mature. He is not a genius, not a leader, but when wearing the captain's armband of the Three Lions team, he bravely took on the responsibilities that a genius leader should bear, and this courage makes us respect him. The treble winner, the battle against Greece, the revenge against Argentina, persistence and endurance finally allowed him to freely vent his suppressed emotions after victory. That face infinitely magnified in front of the camera exaggeratedly and distortedly displayed the strength of a man, very beautiful and noble.

When a person likes to keep his head high, he is ready for any choice. Leaving the Red Devils was a kind of tolerance. Although the bloodstain on his eyebrow has not faded, he still said affectionately, Ferguson is my best coach, forever. This is not rebelliousness, it is the price paid for dignity.

Leaving home, people's attention was not on football. There was only loneliness left in their eyes. He still wanted to say, actually I am a player. Not until winning the most important trophy in Real Madrid's history, not until dragging his injured leg back to the national team, did he finally reborn for football in diligent dedication and persistent waiting.

You said you would play until 2010, but this day came early with tears. You said you would dedicate your whole life to football, but after a glamorous turn, the Beckham shadow disappeared in Hollywood.

As Beckham fades away, we don't cry for you, we stay awake for you.
