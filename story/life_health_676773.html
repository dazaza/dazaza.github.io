After getting rid of those annoying pimples, have you found that a more serious problem follows? It's the aftermath of acne - pimple marks. Pimple marks are the deep red or light red scars left behind after acne has healed.

There are many methods to remove pimple marks. Below, experts from Guangzhou will explain these methods one by one for you, helping you find the one most suitable for yourself, and create perfect skin.

Massage Method for Removing Pimple Marks

Massage can remove scars and pimple marks because moderate massage can promote blood circulation, loosen tendons and bones, and is beneficial to many aspects of the body. Dealing with scars is no exception. Since pimple marks are caused by uneven growth of new tissue, massage can effectively restore the vitality of local tissues, promote the regeneration of new skin, and make the part of the pimple marks return to the same color as the surrounding skin. Therefore, massage is a good choice, it's simple, free, and the effect is quite good. The specific method: gently rub the palm root on the pimple scar, three times a day, each time about 10 minutes.

Vitamin Method for Removing Pimple Marks

Vitamins are great helpers in removing scars and pimple marks. Vitamin C is a whitening holy grail, so if the scar color is darker, we can try grinding vitamin C into powder and applying it on the scar, which can effectively reduce the pigmentation of the scar, promote whitening, and gradually restore the color of healthy skin. On the other hand, vitamin E has moisturizing effects and can enhance the elasticity of the skin, and it easily penetrates the skin and enters subcutaneously. Therefore, we can use a needle to puncture the capsule of vitamin E, take out the medicine liquid and apply it on the scar, gently rubbing for about ten minutes, twice a day. Persistent use can effectively remove scars.

Lavender Scar Removal

Lavender essential oil not only helps sleep but also has excellent beauty effects, and has been widely used in cosmetics and beauty fields. Generally speaking, lavender essential oil works well on scars formed within 1-2 years. Therefore, we can take 1-2 drops of lavender essential oil and apply it directly on the skin to fade scars and pimple marks. However, it's important to note that during use, avoid contact with healthy skin.

Ginger Slice Method for Removing Pimple Scars

Cut fresh ginger into slices, then gently wipe the pimple marks with the ginger slice, finally place the ginger slice on the scar area for a moment, then remove and replace with another slice, repeating two or three times. This method requires persistence, you can do this once or twice a day, and over time, small scars will disappear. This is because ginger has the function of inhibiting granulation tissue growth, thereby weakening the formation and growth of scars.

Chrysanthemum Mask for Removing Pimple Marks

Chrysanthemum contains a rich amount of essential oils and chrysanthemum pigment, these components can effectively inhibit the formation of skin melanin, and also have the effect of softening epidermal cells. So this homemade whitening mask can inhibit melanin, not only whiten the skin and remove pimple marks, but also has the effect of removing wrinkles.
