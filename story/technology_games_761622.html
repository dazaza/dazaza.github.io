China to release first industry standard for game publishing

by geekzhang on 2013-05-03 21:08:01

For a long time, there has been little emphasis in our country on the standardization of the gaming industry. However, in recent years with the gradual opening up of the gaming market and the increasing number of gamers, our country has decided to formulate a series of standards for the game publishing industry.

The reporter for the China News Publishing Newspaper learned that the series of game publishing standards is currently being compiled. This is the first time that our country is formulating an industry standard for game publishing. The formulation and introduction of this series of standards will closely connect with existing game publishing related laws and regulations in our country, refine the requirements for game publishing, and form a complete, reasonable, and well-structured game publishing management system. It is expected that the preliminary draft will be completed by the end of 2013, and submitted for approval and release in 2014.

In recent years, our country's game publishing industry has developed vigorously, but at the same time, there are no corresponding standard norms for editing, production, processing, dissemination, and other publishing processes, which has seriously affected the healthy and orderly development of the gaming industry. In March 2013, the former General Administration of Press and Publication initiated work on the relevant series of game publishing standards, determining that the National Press and Publication Standardization Technical Committee would be responsible for organizing the writing work, and that the Chinese Academy of Press and Publication and the Chinese Publishing Association Game Work Committee would jointly form a project working group to specifically undertake the promotion and execution of the project. In addition, during the formulation process of the series of game publishing standards, some advanced enterprise standards will be absorbed to ensure they have sufficient industry applicability and feasibility.

The series of game publishing standards will include four main standards:

- "Game Publishing Standards System Table", including explanations of terms related to game publishing and the game publishing standards system;

- "Game Publishing Procedure Specifications", proposing normative requirements for related processes such as game production, copyright registration, submission for approval, and publication;

- "Game Publishing Format Requirements", proposing requirements for format and form, such as packaging covers;

- "Game Publishing Content Review Specifications", proposing requirements for game publishing content, such as prohibiting illegal, pornographic, and violent content in game publications.

Song Jianxin, head of the relevant department of the State Administration of Press, Publication, Radio, Film, and Television, stated that the formulation of game publishing standards is a need for the development of the gaming industry, a need for digital publishing management work, and also a need for the healthy growth of gaming enterprises. The formulation and issuance of these standards will not only improve the level of game publishing management but also facilitate the production and dissemination of advanced and healthy digital cultural products.
