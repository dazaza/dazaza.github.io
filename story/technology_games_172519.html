Upgrade Tips: An Ultra-Fast Upgrade Method Designed for Lazy People

by lk0gamec on 2009-07-08 09:30:47

Xiaosang Village (Levels 1-11)

When you create your character and enter the game, your starting point will be in Kunlun Cave. There are a few quests there; NPCs with a (?) or (!) above their heads have quests for you.

The early quests are quite simple. After completing the quests in Kunlun Cave, you'll be teleported to the Novice Village. To check quests, press (Q). In the quest log, there are two categories: "Available Quests" and "Accepted Quests". If you're unsure which quest to take, open the "Available Quests" section for hints.

For the Novice Village quests, it's straightforward: from levels 1 to 11, focus on the main storyline quests, and you can skip other side quests.

Be sure to do the mount and pet companion quests. Other quests are optional and don't offer much experience. There's another quest some players might not know about.

There's a quest in Qinglin Village with an NPC named Sister Hu. This quest offers several ten thousand experience points. It's important to accept this quest early on. You'll need to turn it in at Ninghai, and completing it will unlock subsequent quests. In Xiaosang Village, sticking to the main storyline is the fastest way to level up.

It's best to join a group, especially when you get monster-killing quests. This will make things much easier. If you're unsure where to go for a quest, press (Q), and under the NPC name in the quest details, click the line next to the NPC's name, and it will automatically guide you to the quest location.

If you want to level up quickly, in the early stages, only accept quests from the main storyline. This is simple and efficient. That concludes the guide for Xiaosang Village.

Ninghai County (Levels 11-28)

After completing the quest to leave Xiaosang Village, continue focusing on the main storyline quests. Leveling up should primarily involve doing the main quests, though other quests are optional. Personally, I think the fastest way to level up is still by following the main storyline (it's simple).

Once you reach a certain level, you'll receive the Whirlwind quest.

From the NPC (Constable Zhao), you can accept two quests, and there's also a monster-killing quest from Luo Jinding that involves battling whirlwinds.

Upon arriving in Ninghai County, immediately accept the main storyline quests. Don't forget to turn in the quest from Qinglin Village (Sister Hu) in Xiaosang Village, as it has follow-up quests.

You'll need to return to Xiaosang Village with the quest items to complete the quest. Remember to bind your flight talisman so you can easily travel back and forth without wasting time.

After turning in the quest in Xiaosang Village, you'll receive the Yellow Wind Cave quest (Eliminate Uncle Huang Si). At this point, start focusing on the main storyline quests. Near the skill-learning area in Ninghai, there's a question-answering event. Each time you level up, you can answer a few questions, earning approximately 12,000-15,000 experience points.
