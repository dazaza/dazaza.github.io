There are three basic profit models for Chinese social website applications: shared advertising revenue, virtual currency income, and customized services for brand applications. Compared with Western social websites' applications, Chinese social website users often interact more with various applications and advertisements after meeting friends online.

According to surveys, more than half of the logins on Facebook are purely for playing social games. Do third-party applications in China have the irresistible power of the American version? Although the traditional copying of Facebook's successful model formed SNS ignored the core of localization innovation, it is undeniable that the operation of community networks such as Kaixin001, Renren, and QQ Space largely depends on third-party software to attract customers.

Homeland Defense

Representative of tower defense games, by adding application sharing messages to inform users, embedded advertising and prop charges are still ways of income, task rewards and login reward modes are the main ways to stick users.

Super Workplace

Satisfies people's desire for a loose state and rapid promotion that cannot be achieved in actual workplaces. Manufacturers generate revenue through direct embedding of advertisements, time-limited task reward upgrade modes prompt users to log in multiple times a day.

Email Interaction

Websites achieve multi-account association by binding user emails, users can log in through different methods such as email or mobile phone or account name, domain synchronization also facilitates website binding of users.

Space Voting

Websites seize people's curiosity about sampling surveys, the voting mode can reflect netizens' concepts to a certain extent, voting results will be displayed in friends' statuses, spreading users.

Happy Baby

Manufacturers create a dreamy and cute pet world for players, users purchase scene props to make their pets prettier, interactions with other applications like "You Draw I Guess" also increase user stickiness.

Friend Trading

The best outlet for people's "psychological compensation." Websites generate profits through embedded advertising, and friend invitation modes are also important ways to stabilize users.

Check-in Games

Websites increase user stickiness by establishing short-term and long-term goals for customers, daily login rewards and end-of-month settlement reward modes, which to a certain extent promote the click rate of other applications.

Parking Spaces

This game is the best representative of offline products going online, still a friend invitation-dominated application, while satisfying users' pursuit of luxury cars and merchants' advertising platform profits.

Music Applications

Music currently being listened to can be displayed in the user status bar for friends to share, the system automatically pops up recommendation windows based on listening types, updated menus according to rankings changes stabilize part of music-loving users.

Texas Poker

Time Machine

Micro Territory

Real-name registration updates personal status to Weibo in real time, web-based online customer service software, websites attract users by absorbing celebrities and famous brands, multi-mode check-in tasks also increase user stickiness.
