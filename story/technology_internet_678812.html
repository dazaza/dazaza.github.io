13. Prepare a large travel bag, those beautiful big canvas shoulder bags are good, they look great in photos.

Bride Preparation:

1. Take care of your hair one month in advance, trim, perm, nourish with oil,

2. Skin care: Ensure sleep! Do hydrating and moisturizing masks for a week,

Do not expose to strong sunlight for a month, select some whitening bath gels and body lotions, focus on protecting arms, chest, shoulders and neck,

3. If you want to take some unique photos, collect some small things you both like during this time, bring them along for photos.

4. A strapless bra. Underwear should be preferably seamless underwear, self-prepared invisible bras.

5. You can choose to do manicures or buy some fake nails.

Groom Preparation:

1) Hair. The groom’s hair will be simply blow-dried by the makeup artist but won't change much, best to complete the groom's haircut a week before the photo shoot. Dyeing hair should also be done in advance. Same goes for the bride.

2) Teeth. If the groom has bad teeth, it's recommended to use whitening strips or definitely don’t show teeth when smiling in photos! Otherwise, the effect will be poor.

3) Skin. The groom doesn't need intensive mask care, but on the day of the photo shoot, it's still necessary to apply some moisturizing cream, otherwise dryness and flakiness may appear, so an ampoule might be needed!

4) Beard. Some grooms grow beards easily, they regrow in the afternoon after being shaved in the morning. So on the day of the photo shoot, carry an electric shaver to shave anytime.

5) Nose hair. Many men have long nose hairs that stick out when they smile, seriously affecting the mood. It's necessary to trim them before the photo shoot. The bride should check carefully as well.

6) Shoes and socks. The groom rarely gets his feet photographed. If there are dynamic full-body shots, black shoes and socks are safer. Generally speaking, white suits are paired with white dresses, so white leather shoes can also be chosen. It depends on the preferences of the bride and groom.

7) Rings and nails. The groom’s hands will appear more often in photos, so trim the nails, absolutely no black dirt under the nails! Rings can be wedding rings or decorative ones according to needs. Let the groom carry a small velvet bag, miscellaneous items must be packed well, check pockets when changing clothes, losing rings during the photo shoot would be troublesome.

Preparations the Night Before:

1. Confirm the photography schedule again,

2. One day before departure: Apply hydrating masks to yourself and your husband,

3. Pack the bags, which may include: Money! Chocolate, water and straws, prepare the next day's clothes, wear cardigans, preferably flat shoes, your husband wears black leather shoes, dark socks as backup shoes, your husband's dark and white socks.

Your fun props, choose light and comfortable clothing styles, one principle is easy to change.

4. Take a shower, don't forget to apply body lotion, remove body hair

5. Seems about right, set the alarm clock, sleep, don't drink too much water, eyes will swell, good night...

Preparations on the Day:

1. If you wake up early on the day of the photo shoot, you can simply do a mask, after regular hydration,

2. It's best to add a transparent隔离霜 (isolation cream), isolate makeup. Don't use color-correcting ones, otherwise the makeup artist will ask you to wash it off.

3. Hair must not be sprayed with styling gel or similar products.
