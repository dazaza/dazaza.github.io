LG Electronics Launches New Flicker-Free 3D TV: More Stereoscopic Images (Picture)

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-04 09:53:46

It is reported that LG Electronics will soon release the Cinema Passive 3D TV LM6700. Since both the screen and the chip are of LG origin, this is a fully matched passive 3D TV in terms of screen and chip, providing consumers with the best visual effects. Its wider viewing angle also makes 3D watching more comfortable.

The 3D images are more three-dimensional. The main product of LG's upcoming Cinema Passive 3D TV, the LM6700, uses an edgeless hard screen that can rival cinema screens, combining design aesthetics with visual extension.

The visual effects of a 3D TV depend not only on the screen but also on the internal chips and circuit design. The LM6700 achieves the optimal coordination between internal chips, circuit design, and screen display technology. Its specially developed chip technology has the best compatibility with the ultra-narrow edge hard screen, allowing you to enjoy the best 3D cinema experience at home.

To ensure the picture quality of a 3D TV, it is necessary to increase the refresh rate of the panel and related signals to ensure the stability and continuity of the picture. The LM6700 uses an original edgeless hard screen, representing the cutting-edge technology of high-speed LCD panels. With triple XD engine, intelligent sensing, and scanning line doubling technologies, it can provide more realistic colors.

In terms of 3D technology, it adopts the second-generation passive 3D technology. It is reported that this technology is the most realistic way to present stereoscopic images that closely matches human visual perception.

In terms of image performance, the Cinema Passive 3D technology produces clear, bright, and vividly three-dimensional images, making viewers feel as if they are inside the scenes of the TV program. Moreover, it eliminates the inherent screen flickering and image ghosting problems of traditional 3D TVs. Additionally, it can identify the distance of sounds and achieve progressive changes, meeting the almost demanding requirements of 3D enthusiasts for 3D picture quality and sound effects.

According to reports, the LG LM6700 has a very powerful 2D-to-3D conversion function. Under the premise of high-definition 2D content, its viewpoint and depth adjustment can reach up to 20 units, exceeding ordinary 3D TVs by 10 units. Without spending money on 3D Blu-ray discs, just by clicking the conversion button, you can experience the thrilling adventure of climbing Dubai's tallest building against strong winds alongside Tom Cruise.

Moreover, passive 3D glasses are lighter, thinner, do not require batteries, produce no radiation, and do not cause dizziness or other health concerns.

The viewing angle is wider, allowing you to watch even while lying down. As the original creator and leader of the second-generation passive 3D TV technology, LG’s new product also adopts FPR passive 3D technology, which offers flicker-free, ghost-free images with high brightness. In China, 3D TVs are mainly purchased for family sharing, where elderly grandparents and children in their growth phase often watch television for long periods. The LG LM6700 is more suitable for the whole family to watch together. Even when lying down, it can guarantee visual effects. Long-term viewing does not cause dizziness or blurred vision, providing a healthy and comfortable 3D world for your family.
