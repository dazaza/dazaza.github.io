The internet-famous pictures of a senior three boy taking the art examination became popular online, he said it was just good luck (Picture)

by haojing12 on 2012-02-11 11:07:26

The Male Scarf Deng Xianyu

**Report by Wan Bioming, Zhang Ge, and correspondent Wang Xinshou**

Yesterday, a set of photos titled "the Handsomest Applicant at Beijing Film Academy - Male Scarf" went viral on Weibo. The person in the photos is none other than Deng Xianyu, a senior high school student from Wuhan No. 2 Middle School. When we reached Deng Xianyu on the phone yesterday, the young man candidly admitted, "I'm feeling so much pressure. Actually, I've just been lucky."

On February 8th, the first day of Beijing Film Academy's 2012 art examination registration, Deng Xianyu went to confirm his application in the morning. During lunch, while scrolling through Weibo, he noticed his feed was filled with fans, retweets, and comments. It was then that he learned his photo had been snapped by a photographer during the registration process and had gone viral online. Confused, Deng Xianyu said, "I only remember there were many journalists taking pictures of applicants at the scene. To this day, I still don't know exactly where or when the photo was taken."

Previously, Deng Xianyu’s Weibo account only had around 200 followers, but now it has surged to over 5,500. His name even ranked sixth on the Weibo Hot Search List. All of this has left the high school senior feeling "a lot of pressure." He said, "So many people are encouraging me, which creates quite a bit of pressure. But it also shows one thing: my luck really is pretty good, and maybe I'll actually get accepted." Regarding the labels such as "the handsomest" and "becoming famous before becoming a star," Deng remained calm: "I feel I don't deserve the title 'handsomest.' There are plenty of good-looking men and women, and I'm still very far from being famous or anything like that."

In his latest Weibo post, more than 460 netizens have commented and left messages for him, praising his appearance as "sunny and clean," and wishing him, "Keep going, and realize your dream soon!"

According to his mother, Ms. Huang, Deng Xianyu is 18 years old, stands at 183 centimeters tall, and enjoys playing basketball. She expressed gratitude to netizens for their affection and support towards her son, emphasizing that his growth is inseparable from the nurturing and influence of his alma mater, Wuhan No. 2 Middle School. Adhering to the principle of "cultivating virtue before pursuing artistry," she hopes her son can take steady steps toward realizing his dreams.

On February 12th, Deng Xianyu will take the exam for Beijing Film Academy's acting department, followed by an application to the Central Academy of Drama.
