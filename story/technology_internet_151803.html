CEPA serves as a bridge and guide, HK domain name targets the Hong Kong market directly.

by now on 2009-05-21 15:24:16

Hong Kong is an important financial center in Asia, its sound economic system has created an ideal investment environment, which gives many mainland enterprises the determination to establish overseas markets and sales networks in Hong Kong. Especially with the promotion of signing the sixth supplementary agreement of CEPA recently, the market integration between Hong Kong and the mainland has taken a new step forward, and many mainland enterprises are ready to enter the Hong Kong market.

According to the analysis of survey data, 67.9% of mainland enterprises planning to set up offices in Hong Kong hope to establish overseas markets and sales networks in Hong Kong, and 62.1% expect to find overseas investment and business partners in Hong Kong. However, how can these enterprises open up this vast market in Hong Kong? How can they quickly establish their image in Hong Kong?

Network marketing is indispensable for deep cultivation of the Hong Kong market

Although network marketing cannot replace traditional marketing, with the advent of the Internet era, it is also one of the important means for enterprises to develop the market. The Internet is a huge information repository, and people are accustomed to obtaining information through it. Therefore, mining network resources and establishing a powerful network promotion platform for enterprises can win more customers and achieve the goal of occupying the market. Especially between the mainland and Hong Kong, the network can build a convenient bridge for enterprises, and establish a publicity base through this bridge to make it easier to take root in Hong Kong.

Although the policies implemented in the two places now can bring a lot of convenience to enterprises, there are still no difficulties. The land price and labor cost in Hong Kong are very high. For large enterprises in the mainland, it is not a problem, but it is relatively difficult for small and medium-sized enterprises. Network marketing is a way for enterprises to achieve market operations at a low cost. Therefore, enterprises that have already developed or plan to develop the Hong Kong market should know how to use the Internet to promote their brands and achieve the effect of improving brand awareness and reputation at a low cost.

Network marketing should cater to Hong Kong characteristics

Of course, if you want to achieve publicity effects in Hong Kong, it is not enough to simply build a website and then publicize it in Hong Kong. Hong Kong has always been the focus of the world, and its development in all aspects has its own distinct characteristics. It is no exception in terms of network application foundation. It has its own domain name .HK. The .HK domain name is known as Hong Kong's uniform resource locator, with a distinct Hong Kong mark, and it is the most representative in opening up the Hong Kong market. I learned from Now.cn, the best partner of HKDNR, that with the gradual improvement of the CEPA agreement signed between the mainland and Hong Kong, the development space of the .HK domain name is getting bigger and bigger. In commercial exchanges between Hong Kong and the mainland, and between Hong Kong and overseas, it can establish a distinctive image with Hong Kong marks, which is beneficial for enterprises to better develop and promote overseas business in Hong Kong. If network marketing is to play a role in the Hong Kong market, the .HK domain name will stand out. Not only can it improve the image of enterprises in Hong Kong, but it can also help enterprises fully integrate into the Hong Kong market and eliminate obstacles in publicity and promotion.

If enterprises want to develop their business in Hong Kong for a long time and win a broader overseas market, they must understand the local customs in their business strategies and work hard on network marketing. Only in this way can they penetrate deeply into the Hong Kong market and open up a brighter future for their enterprises.
