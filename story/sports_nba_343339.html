Good Luck Logistics Co., Ltd. is a Chinese AA-rated logistics enterprise, established in 1994. Its predecessor was the Northern Freight Company. Through a series of strategic adjustments including market repositioning, resource reorganization, and business integration, our company successfully transformed into a professional third-party logistics provider. Over the past 15 years, adhering to the business philosophy of customer satisfaction and service innovation, we have provided clients with specialized services such as express transportation for large items, large-scale indoor relocation, warehousing and distribution, and freight forwarding, earning long-term trust and support from our clients.

In terms of resources, our company is located in a prime area at the intersection of National Highway 319, National Highway 107, Jingzhu Expressway, and Changyong Expressway exit. We have integrated over 1,000 return-trip vehicles of various types from all over the country, own more than 20 transport vehicles of various types, and have built a new vehicle resource database containing over 4,000 large trucks nationwide. In terms of loading and unloading equipment, our company is equipped with large cranes, various forklifts, and special tools for moving large indoor equipment, striving to respond quickly to customer needs and provide fast and safe services.

In terms of human resources, our company has over 30 senior logistics talents and corporate management elites, along with more than 100 professional dispatchers, escorts, and special loading and unloading personnel. Due to our strict requirements in personnel management, two-thirds of our middle and senior management staff hold associate degrees or above or have obtained logistics professional qualifications, ensuring reliable full-service provision to customers.

In terms of credentials, our company was awarded Model Business Operator in 2006 and 2007, Advanced Private Enterprise in 2007, and is a board member of the Hunan Logistics and Purchasing Federation. Companies that have signed long-term transportation outsourcing contracts with us include Zoomlion, Changsha Motor Factory, Changsha Machine Tool Factory, PpanPpan Security Door Industry, Swan Pump, Huatian Aluminum Industry, etc. We have successfully organized large-scale indoor relocation projects for institutions such as Central South University and the People's Bank of China, accumulating rich practical experience.

In terms of guarantees, our company is insured by Ping An Property Insurance of China and commits to fully compensating for any damage to customer goods during transportation, allowing customers to rest assured.
