by 93zt112222 on 2009-12-04 17:56:32

Today, the target is "Shabake"! Pick up your weapons and fight! Today, we all witnessed the unprecedented battle of ten thousand people fighting for Shabake. Unity, struggle, perseverance, passion, loyalty, shock, and emotion... Are these words enough to describe the grandeur of this war? Although the Shabake battle officially began at 8 PM, our Mafa warriors were eager to seize the initiative and strike first. At 10 AM, the brave warriors from each participating guild polished their weapons, put on their armor, prepared their morale, and gathered from all directions toward the center of this competition —— the city of honor, Shabake.

Soon, the vast Shabake city was overwhelmed by a sea of people, with the map densely filled with avatars. The guilds clashed in front of the Shabake Palace, instantly creating a cacophony of sword clashes, staff swings, mages casting spells, Taoist priests healing, roars, screams, cheers, encouragements, and the sounds of summoning beasts and skeletons. All these sounds intertwined into a grand and majestic war march. Legendary weapons like Heaven's Blood Drinking Blade, Dragon Slaying Saber, Martial Blood Sword, and Heavy Steel Verdict dazzled the eyes; skills like Blazing Sword Technique, Ice Roar, Fire Wall, Fire Talisman, Lightning Strike, and Cold Ice Palm flew across the screen, stirring the blood. The entire Shabake was surrounded by a sea of people and fire.

The intense battle persisted, with splendid skills and exhilarating scenes continuing nonstop. Bodies lay scattered before the palace steps, leaving devastation everywhere. Guild managers either broadcast messages or shouted encouragement to boost their brothers' morale and ignite their fighting spirit. “Unlimited medicine supply!” “Brothers, use our bodies to hold the gate of Shabake tightly!” “Second army pressuring the resurrection point, die if necessary but stay on the resurrection spot!” “Follow the command, surround, concentrate!” “Brothers and sisters, not participating in the Shabak war will be your biggest regret. Let the storm rage more fiercely!” “As long as we exist, so does the city. Hold Shabak for our honor!” Here, we see what true war spirit is, what royal demeanor is, and what resilience means.

The participating guilds also engaged in verbal battles, attempting to break the other side’s confidence while boosting their own. Many small accounts claiming to be "war correspondents" shouted, “Please don’t mistake me for an enemy,” as they squeezed to the front lines to witness this bloody battle. Around 2:30 PM, the battlefield slowly shifted inside and outside the Shabake city walls. One side defended within the city, while the other attacked from outside. The small city gates became the focal point of the war. Warriors with high health rushed forward, slashing their way through enemies. Their powerful attacks combined with fiery skills rained down on the heads of opponents. With sturdy armor, they stood firm against the barrage of swords and magic, determined to die on the battlefield! The human wall was the strongest defense. The warriors’ impenetrable defense was the mages’ solid support. Under the protection of the warriors, the mages freely unleashed their magic, summoning beasts and skeletons that charged towards the enemies. Fire talismans, lightning strikes, ice roars flew both ways over the walls. The Taoist priests summoned moon spirits, applied group healing and joint hits, protecting allies and striking enemies. The battlefield was filled with ice and fire, magic and swords, with avatars and summoned creatures everywhere.

Finally, the clock struck 8 PM, marking the official start of the Shabake battle. By this time, the guild wars had already lasted for 10 hours —— an absolute legend in online gaming history! Yet, our warriors seemed unfazed by fatigue, still battling for honor and dreams on the walls of Shabake. Although the warriors outside the city attacked relentlessly, vowing to take Shabake no matter the cost, they found it impossible to breach the strong walls, and the outcome of the war gradually became clear. After the final contest, the champion of the first Shabake Supreme Guild Championship was born.

All Mafa citizens were deeply moved by the unity, passion, and perseverance shown in this unprecedented battle, infected by the grandeur of ten thousand people taking Shabake. Strength proves everything; the victors smiled, sitting on the throne of the capital with the grace of conquerors. But the war did not end today. Tomorrow would bring even fiercer and tougher battles. True heroes never become arrogant or give up because of one victory or defeat; becoming stronger with each battle and excelling when facing challenges will lead to ultimate victory. Passion remains unextinguished, never giving up, falling down only to stand back up again! Today is merely the first day of the Shabake dominance contest, just 1/21 of the entire event. Ultimately, who will be the last one standing? Who will become the true ruler of the Mafa continent? Let's wait and see!
