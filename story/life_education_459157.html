Changchun Youth Psychological Counseling: Student Potential

by zhidian520 on 2011-07-07 16:39:39

Changchun Youth Psychological Counseling states that most primary school students have the potential to learn scientific and cultural knowledge and engage in various activities. As long as teachers are good at discovering this potential, actively support it, and create good conditions for the development of primary school students' abilities, the latent qualities of various abilities of primary school students will be fully revealed. The cultivation of primary school students' abilities can start from the following two aspects.

(1) Stimulating interest. Primary school students' interest in things directly affects their opportunities to exercise their abilities. For anything that primary school students are interested in, they will invest more time and effort, allowing their abilities to be exercised more. In this sense, stimulating primary school students' positive and beneficial interests and hobbies helps develop their abilities. Abilities are formed in the process of social practice activities. For primary school students, these activities are often specific and direct. In addition to normal classroom teaching, teachers should also make full use of various specific social practice activities to stimulate their direct interest, cultivate, and exercise their abilities. For example, establishing music groups, art groups, sports groups, science and technology groups, and other interest groups so that primary school students all have the opportunity to participate in various activities.

(2) Good coordination with other personal qualities. Ability, as a component of personality, is closely related to other characteristics of personality. To develop ability, one cannot脱离the overall cultivation and development of personality. The formation of any ability is associated with overcoming difficulties. Without the tenacious spirit of overcoming difficulties, easily becoming discouraged, losing confidence, lacking motivation, changing goals frequently, and casually negating previous activities, true abilities will not develop in a certain activity field. Primary school students should learn to strictly require themselves from a young age, correctly treat the progress of their own abilities, and not boast about themselves because they show talent in some areas, thinking they are superior to others. The development of ability and the formation of a good personality complement each other and promote each other. A person who is bold, outgoing, brave in exploration, and unafraid of difficulties will have more opportunities than ordinary people to exercise and develop their abilities. At the same time, this improvement in ability will also make his personality more outstanding. Modern society requires a person not only to be talented but also bold, daring, and willing to express their talents. Even if someone is timid and lacks initiative, they will find it difficult to fully express their talents. Changchun Youth Psychological Counseling helps students discover their own talents.
