Chai Ren War v1.3 Enhanced Edition mini-game is available for free online play and download.

by neeke on 2011-01-16 16:07:18

In the v1.3 version of Stick War, the game becomes even more exciting! Put your intelligence to work and become a great military strategist!

New content added to the game:

1. New enemies: The fiery breath of the flying dragon will burn down your walls and can only be eliminated by the wizard's new fireball spell.

2. New upgrades: Command prisoners of war to charge and fight with invaders.

3. Added a level progress indicator (observe the sun).

4. New wizard spell: The method to use the fireball is to first pull leftward on the wizard, like a rubber band, then release.

Fixes in Stick War v1.3:

1. Reduced lag through optimized system composition.

2. Improved multi-touch control, allowing you to pick up many enemies at once.

3. Fixed the bug where meteors would sometimes suddenly launch.

4. No longer need to wait for captures before the end of a level.

5. More background music added.

6. Wizard's magic recovery rate decreased.

7. Reduced hand damage.

8. Increased repair amount for the repairman.

9. Hands no longer destroy enemies you are trying to pick up.

10. Enemies can no longer rush into your walls (though "Sometimes" was added as an exception).

11. Each round lasts shorter.

12. Earn more money for each enemy defeated.

Added Cheat Mode: After starting the game, pause it, then quickly tap the screen six times, wait one second. If done correctly, you will hear the sound "cheater" and receive 50,000 yuan, 50 prisoners, and 5,000 HP. Scores from previous versions saved will be posted on the old score board.

How to play: Use the mouse to move the screen and click to select the character you want to control. A star appearing above someone's head means you can control that person.

The buttons at the top row are: The first one produces soldiers, the second one creates offensive units; the middle three slots choose retreat, defense, or attack; on the left side: money and your troop strength; finally: pause and music. When you attack and eliminate all enemies, also destroy the enemy's statue, you will successfully pass the level.
