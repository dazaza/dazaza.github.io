I, am still thinking of a splendid beginning, QQ sad diary.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:17:51

Still thinking of a splendid beginning.

Regarding the title, as everyone can see. I just haven't thought of one yet, so it's temporarily like this.

This kind of splendor is different from nobility and luxury. It's like using a low-quality wooden guitar, but still able to outline a simple and pure texture.

Some of my obsessions this summer.

Chu Qi. Iwai Jun. D. C. Faye Wong. Cheer Chen. Mayday. Fish Leong.邓Deng Lixin. Rainy days. Travel. Babe. Movies. Music. English. Sleep. Milk. Radio. Keyboard. Words. Voice. Water.

Daytime. The little kids far away on the grass are playing puzzles, missing a piece, so they cry loudly. Sometimes if we could also cry out loud like that, how good it would be. But I'm just the kid who's afraid of change, flustered.

I walked straight to the fridge after leaving the room. My face appeared in the large and clean glass door. Numb. Panicked. The unique paleness of staring at the screen for a long time. The sky above the telecommunications building in the distance has already become completely gray, but there are still dark clouds above me, without a drop of rain falling.

Looking at it, it suddenly poured heavily. The diameter of the raindrops... sorry, unable to estimate.

Standing by the window drinking Mengniu pure milk, lightning flashes one after another outside, like fireworks-like brilliance. Then I found myself starting to have cold hands and feet, so I put on denim pants. The me in the mirror wearing a wrinkled cotton sleep dress, paired with thick denim pants, what a ridiculous combination. So I laugh to myself.

It's been a month since I started working. During this period, I've been daydreaming and speechless for a long time, no communication, and rarely watching TV. Chatting intermittently with people, watching a lot of movies and dramas every day, occasionally going out on dates. Listening to the same frequency of the same radio program at fixed times. The host I like has a nice voice, she plays old songs, speaks intermittently. Then I can start to fall asleep peacefully.

Life becomes very regular due to lack of routine for a long time, so I believe I am an easily readable woman.

Sometimes the radio will play some very noisy music, at which point I always frown, waiting for them to end quickly.

Sleeping. In a tense posture. As a result, it brings continuous nightmares. Relaxing and finally entering the sleep state. Then let myself sleep until day and night are reversed, heaven and earth are darkened, and the sea dries up and the rocks rot. I sleep alone on a big bed, but I like to keep moving towards the edge. So I fell off the bed many times. Because of habit, I can continue sleeping calmly when I get up.

On rainy days, the water flowing out of the pipe begins to turn icy cold. I once thought this long summer was over, my favorite season was coming. But in fact, it's not true.

Thinking of two sentences.

If life is a comedy, then we should laugh as much as possible. Don't let the comedy lack its due beauty.

If life is a tragedy, then we should perform with all our might. Don't let the tragedy lack its due grandeur.

Suddenly, I feel very unhappy, wanting to moan without cause. Listen to a song, then cry. Not many people know that I often cry. Listening to music. Reading books. Watching movies. Arguing with parents. Falling out with friends. Losing things you like. Sometimes just because of boredom. In fact, I am very confused most of the time, not knowing what to do. And usually at these times, no one comes to help me.

Once wanted to jump down from the balcony. Don't know if flying will bring a little happiness. I know that when I'm bored, I can definitely think about death, but only think, don't do it. Some people mean too much to me, I can't abandon them.

My head is dizzy, in a daze, I want to finish typing the words on my fingertips with this glass of iced water beside me.

Suddenly thought of the music for eye exercises that played in the elementary school classroom. That was also a terrible memory.

Remember when our homeroom teacher asked us to write essays. A classmate wrote about living in a house less than 10 square meters, and the teacher criticized her for plagiarism. Later, I accidentally went to her house and found out it was really less than 10 square meters. Her parents and grandparents all died in a car accident. At that moment, I felt an overwhelming sadness spreading inside me.

Incidentally, I thought of the nightmare of elementary school math word problems. Because I made too many mistakes, I was kept behind after school by the teacher. Back then, I wanted to cry, I felt so helpless. The grown-up me fears nothing. Skipping classes to the maximum extent, laughing wildly among the crowd, spacing out during class. Keeping the maximum distance from math.

Feeling sorry for my past self. Wanting to go back and rescue her, not letting her be so helpless alone.

Always doing what I like freely.

Not long after moving here, I found myself to be the latest sleeper in this area. Sometimes around one o'clock in the morning, I stand on the balcony singing softly to myself, with a background of frog croaks. When there are stars, I look at their reflection in the river.

In fact, many people don't know that even on nights without moon or stars, you can still see patches of clouds, seemingly stationary, without any tendency to float.

The family to the right of the exit keeps many kittens, only as big as mice. Every time I pass by, I see them scurrying in all directions, but I don't like cats.

I like people who are easy to satisfy the most.

When I skipped a whole night of classes, ate a lot of food, or sang songs, I always felt very happy. Then I told the people around me that I was very happy. Small happiness, I was deeply moved. Then with a bright face, like a child. Many times I could smile happily in the dark for a certain sentence, a certain person, some memories.

Thinking of the past, I always feel extremely happy now.

Maybe it's just breathing hard in front of the air conditioner. Sometimes dry, sometimes I can smell the flavor of non-dairy creamer. I think it's amazing.

Maybe it's just humming a self-composed melody, fleeting and forgettable.

Maybe I'm listening to 'South Sea Girl'.

The average temperature in Beijing these days is seven degrees higher than in Chenzhou. For cities I like, I will always pay attention to their climate, which is a small woman habit left by someone.

In the third year of high school, I once lived elsewhere away from home, always feeling powerless. Often at night, I saw a vast sea of lights, but I couldn't find the one that belonged to me.

Waiting time always seems long. I squatted down, with my ears plugged with an mp3 player. The loud volume made me unable to hear the noise of the crowd and vehicles on the street. I squatted by the roadside looking at crawling ants, wanting to see the traces of time.

One. Two. Slowly crawling. Like time slowly crawling over the forehead full of wrinkles of the elderly.

I often watch my grandparents quarrel over small matters seriously. And I just stare at them and smile absent-mindedly.

Someone said to me, you're like an engaged happy little woman. So I started thinking about this question alone.

Still uncertain whether I really make them satisfied, but clearly understand in my heart that I like that family, cherish their kindness to me. Just feel that the future me will follow him to a town with a special name - Zengdian. Then simply and peacefully, long and lingering.

Fortunately, Aunt, Uncle, Sister, Brother-in-law are not against me, this is what I am most grateful for. I have no preparation for meeting. Rash, unknowledgeable, didn't do homework. When each person smiles at me, I tell myself, in the future, I will treat them like my own family.

Unable to use more words to describe their kindness to me. Just thank you very much. This is the luck given by heaven.

Then such a happy me, possess all the pure love except love.

Still boasting shamelessly about happiness, unmatched peace.

In June, I will still drink Mengniu low-fat milk in large gulps in the air conditioning at midnight.

Reading books. Listening to songs. Missing. Waiting for that person. Cherishing happiness. Then daydreaming until dawn.
