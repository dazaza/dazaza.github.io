Fruit shell filter media is made from apricot shells, peach shells, and walnut shells. Due to its inherent high hardness, ideal specific gravity, porous and multifaceted structure, it undergoes special physical and chemical treatment (removing pigments, fats, oils, and electric charges), giving it strong oil removal properties in water treatment. It is suitable for use in water treatment in oil fields and petrochemical enterprises. It has the advantages of high hardness, wear resistance, good compressive strength, and strong adsorption and dirt-carrying capacity, allowing direct pre-filter backwashing. It is highly effective in treating oily wastewater. The main specifications include: 0.4-0.6, 0.5-1, 0.8-1.2; 1-2, 2-4, 4-6.
