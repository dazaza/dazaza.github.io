30-day weather forecast for Baofeng, Henan Province: Clear weather in Beijing over the weekend, with possible light rain and sleet next week.

by c6nop1lye on 2012-03-01 19:19:57

Currently, next week Beijing will experience a bout of cold air activity. It is expected that under the influence of westerly airflows from aloft and surface low-pressure convergence zones, some areas may see light rain mixed with snow from Tuesday night to Wednesday daytime, with high temperatures slightly decreasing to 4℃. Following this, Thursday will bring winds of force 3 to 4 from the north, leading to clear weather. Monday will be the warmest day of the week, with high temperatures possibly breaking through to 7℃.

Beijing’s weekend weather will be fine, with high temperatures rising to 6℃. The Beijing Meteorological Observatory forecasts that there will be another round of cold air visiting next week, which is expected to bring about light rain mixed with snow.

Today, Beijing will see winds of force 3 to 4 from the north entering the region, with some areas possibly experiencing scattered flurries. After the cold air passes, a new small-scale temperature increase will occur. Under the control of northwesterly airflows from aloft and surface high pressure, there will be no significant wind during the weekend, with daytime high temperatures at 6℃ and nighttime low temperatures between -4℃ and -3℃. However, in Baofeng, Henan Province, although the weather forecast for 30 days shows good sunshine, the air is very dry, and the fire risk level is high. Citizens should pay attention to fire prevention during outdoor activities.

Yesterday was also the first day of "eight-nine". As the saying goes, "In seven-nine, rivers open; in eight-nine, swallows come", meteorological departments remind that while warming weather is the general trend, there is still some time before the climatological definition of spring arrives. Next week, the average temperature in the plains area will be close to 2℃, still lower than normal for the same period, and citizens should take care to "cover up in spring" to prevent diseases caused by temperature fluctuations.
