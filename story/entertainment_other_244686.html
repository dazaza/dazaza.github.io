To you, the guys who broke up.

by lwf225 on 2009-10-01 20:39:54

We are still friends after break-up

That's what only a few of you would say

Apart from showing your general demeanor

You hope that the new and old lovers can get together

A pair of people in love have to break up

Most of them have no choice but to do so

And to be good friends after breaking up

Isn't that bringing back some troubles of the past into reality

Why bother

Calmly

Of course, I don't approve of becoming enemies after breaking up

Grinding teeth

Sworn enemies

Suppose the other person is doing better than you

Will you be jealous

When the other person proudly brings his/her new lover to introduce you

Even if you pretend not to care on the surface

But feel heartache inside

Why torment yourself

Suppose the other person is doing worse than you

When your compassion kicks in

You will definitely feel pain for the other person

After all, the two of you also had a wonderful time

Faced with the disappointment and dishevelment of the old lover

If you can't help but reach out a helping hand

Will you fall into the trap of that emotion again

Finally, suppose both parties find new loves after breaking up

But you still make the ex-lover a friend

How does the new lover feel

Who doesn't think you and the ex-lover are still emotionally connected

The new lover will inevitably be dissatisfied

And more afraid of getting involved in a triangular relationship

Although there are also examples of peaceful coexistence

But these stories always sound a little awkward

Maybe you can pursue new while keeping old

You can compare the new and the old

And live happily ever after In this case, I can only admire and say

Your level is high

I don't have this level

At least I can't allocate time and feelings

Separating feelings means downgrading feelings

When a full-hearted feeling takes care of others

It will appear unfaithful

When you see your ex-lover

How can you forget the days when you shared joys and hardships together

Unless it was not deeply engraved in your heart

Otherwise, it is extremely difficult to be good friends after breaking up

Seeing the happy scene of him/her being affectionate with others

You will certainly feel melancholy

Why put on airs pretending to be magnanimous

Break up

Rather than deliberately maintain a friendship-like relationship

It's better to keep it in your heart, which will last longer

Or forget it and embrace the new future

There is no need to be friends after breaking up

Otherwise

What's the point of breaking up

After the break-up

I still know you

However

I don't want to see you again

If you're doing well

I won't bless you

If you're doing badly

I won't mock you either

Because

We are no longer friends

In the vast sea of people

I can no longer cherish you

Sorry

What I've lost

Is what you've lost too

If you want me to have a cup of tea with you

Sure

But

That's all there is to it

If you want me to chat with you

Sure

But
