Not long after the New Year, Mr. Tang from the wedding company has already started to take orders for the end of the year, "with a taste of explosive growth." Mr. Tang said with a smile. The lunar year 2012, because it has two "Start of Spring," is called "Two Springs," and is also considered a propitious year for marriage by the common people. Therefore, many people choose to get married in clusters this year. As a result, the wedding market has seen a surge in demand, and prices have also risen overall. Prices of over ten thousand yuan have become the norm for wedding companies. Large banquet halls are hard to come by. "The wedding banquets in May, June, and October are basically fully booked. The hotel we have our eye on only has a smaller banquet hall available." Mr. Wang, who plans to get married in June this year, was surprised to find out. "This year is the 'Two Springs,' and there are many people getting married. I was worried about not being able to book a wedding banquet, so I deliberately avoided May Day and National Day, and instead chose a weekend in June. Unexpectedly, it was still difficult to book." Mr. Wang said. After interviewing multiple star-rated hotels in the city, it was found that booking a wedding banquet during "May Day" and "National Day" is basically impossible, especially for banquets of more than 14 tables, where it's hard to secure a spot. Similarly hot are the wedding companies and photo studios. Mr. Tang told reporters that the number of newlyweds getting married this year has increased significantly compared to last year, and many couples had already started making reservations at wedding companies before the Spring Festival. Among them, the months of May, June, September, and October have the most reservations, with the latest reservation extending all the way to December. With the clustering of couples getting married in the Dragon Year, wedding prices have surged sharply. Both wedding banquets and wedding services have seen price hikes. Through investigation, it was found that the prices of wedding banquets at multiple star-rated hotels in the city have continued to rise, with prices around 2000 yuan per table being the norm, in addition to a certain service fee. "In previous years, the price was mostly over 1000 yuan per table," a staff member of a four-star hotel told reporters. Now, it's basically at the 2000 yuan standard, "with prices increasing by about 10%." In the wedding service market, the one-stop service fees are divided into several tiers, with prices of over ten thousand yuan becoming the norm. "From picking up the bride to the end of the banquet, we arrange everything throughout, including design planning and hotel arrangements, etc.," Mr. Tang told reporters. Due to increases in hotel and car rental prices, the wedding companies have also raised their prices, with tens of thousands of yuan being the most common price, and most cases costing over twenty thousand yuan. Reporters learned that the number of wedding companies has increased significantly in recent years, including larger companies like Hongniang Xipu and Qidu, as well as many smaller-scale wedding companies. Under such fierce competition, many wedding companies have developed high-end wedding planning schemes. "Good planning, high-end hotels, romantic atmosphere, and other factors combined basically make for high-end planning, with costs generally exceeding 300,000 yuan," Mr. Tang told reporters. This is also the main promoted business of many wedding companies this year.
