After a whole season of twists and turns, Michael embarks on his "iron-bar journey" once again. Unlike Fox River, the Sona prison in Panama can be described as a living hell. Ferocious criminals live in an area completely devoid of guard supervision for long periods of time; "survival of the fittest" is absolutely the survival rule at Sona Prison. Mahone, Bellick, and Michael are all thrown into Sona at the same time. Without his white pills, Mahone becomes a helpless frog at the bottom of a well; the raging Bellick has now become a plaything for the inmates, and even the usually calm and composed Michael reveals a trace of fear in his eyes. The wheel of fortune turns, and this time it's Big Bull Lincoln who speaks to his imprisoned younger brother through the barbed wire. Season three of *Prison Break* introduces four new characters: two men and two women, none of them American. Sona's boss Lucre and his girlfriend Sophia, Panamanian fisherman Whistler, and also the beautiful Susan, suspected of being under the control of the mysterious "General." As for Sarah, who got separated from Lincoln and Michael at the end of season two, she has been confirmed not to join the regular cast of season three. The mysterious "General" and Lincoln's son LJ will make appearances in certain episodes. What kind of heart-stopping experiences will Michael face this time due to his "accidental" imprisonment? Will Michael team up with Mahone as the masses speculate and escape together? And what about the $5 million that fell into the water—will it disappear without a trace forever? The sudden imprisonment leaves Michael unprepared; how can he, with his extraordinary intelligence, leave this earthly hell? Moreover, what will happen to "Backpack," Sucre, and the others? The director confidently emphasizes that season three of *Prison Break* is absolutely more exciting than the first two seasons. The oppressive and tense life inside the prison and the escape journey outside make season three a combination of the previous two seasons. Furthermore, the addition of female characters adds a touch of seductive tenderness to the masculine atmosphere of the breakout. In this season, older brother Lincoln even enjoys two nights of marital bliss, making the audience even more curious about the direction of *Prison Break* with the inclusion of romantic relationships.
