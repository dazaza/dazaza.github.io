Deeply "Expressed"

Introduction: In the warm month of June, we are about to welcome the festival of gratitude - Father's Day. Special thanks go to our respected fathers. Father's love is like a mountain, broad and majestic, sometimes silent but always present in our hearts. Our father has been behind us all along, silently supporting our dreams and encouraging us to fly bravely. The wind, frost, rain, and snow they have shielded us from have turned into silver threads quietly appearing on their heads, telling of their protection, longing, and deep affection for their children. The Swiss watch brand Tissot sincerely selects a special Father's Day watch - the Rectangular model of the Le Locle series, allowing children's love to accompany fathers through every second of time.

The elegant rectangular model of the Le Locle series has distinct edges, showcasing the generous and resolute demeanor of men; while the smooth, slightly curved lines of the case and the arched dial soften the rigidity of the square shape, making the watch more noble and profound, an outstanding square mechanical watch. It features a high-performance ETA2824-2 mechanical automatic winding movement with a power reserve of up to 42 hours, reliable and long-lasting just like a father's love. Through the transparent case back engraved with medieval patterns, you can admire the swing of the automatic hammer and the internal workings of the movement, revealing without doubt the rhythmic beauty of the automatic movement, a constant rhythm akin to a father's unceasing affection. The case back is also engraved with elegant italics, retro patterns in the medieval style, and the Tissot logo plaque, all reflecting Tissot's long history and exquisite craftsmanship. The white dial model comes with a 316L stainless steel bracelet gently wrapping around the wrist, while the celebrity black dial model comes with a coffee-colored leather strap, exuding thoughtful care whether in casual or formal settings. The overall design is simple yet powerful, classic and solemn. The perfect combination of simplicity and classicism makes it adept in any situation. The subtle luxury revealed in the raising of hands and arms is deep and simple, just like family ties.

- 316L stainless steel case

- 316L stainless steel bracelet with push-button butterfly clasp

The Rectangular model of the Le Locle series specially presented for Father's Day by Tissot is exquisitely crafted. The arched scratch-resistant crystal glass meticulously polished by craftsmen remains fresh even after prolonged wear. The crystal also features an anti-glare coating, fully demonstrating the children's meticulous care for their father. It has a unique dial design, where the engravings, scales, and pointers on the dial tell the story behind the watch. These components' designs imitate the facade of the landmark building of Le Locle - the clock tower built in the sixteenth century, giving one the feeling of being in a tranquil Swiss town.
