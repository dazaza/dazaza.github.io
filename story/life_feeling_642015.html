Who owes who's happiness

by gege on 2011-12-23 10:49:40

Zhang Wujie gave up the江湖and the江山.

He gave happiness to Zhao Min,

But left his concerns for Xiao Zhao,

Left wandering for Zhu'er,

And left regret for Zhiruo...

Yang Guo and Little Dragon Maiden eventually became a divine couple.

Perhaps he knew, perhaps he didn't,

Perhaps he pretended not to know,

Cheng Ying and Lu Wushuang sacrificed their youth for him,

Guo Xiang thought of him endlessly across the horizons.

Perhaps he remembers, perhaps he doesn't,

There was once a girl named Gongsun Lue'e who froze her entire life in one glance from him.

And all he could give her was a tune on a flute, three golden needles, or a fleeting moment of attention.

In this world, there are too few people who stay together through thick and thin, and too many who forget each other in the vastness of the world...

We have deeply loved some people.

When we love, we mistake every moment for eternity,

Mistake a fleeting passion for a lifetime of being loved.

So we make promises, so we yearn to hold hands and grow old happily together.

Then everything disappears, and we finally understand

That eternal love is such a rare and hard-to-attain thing,

That happiness is such a mysterious and fragile thing.

Maybe love has nothing to do with happiness.

Maybe ultimate happiness in life has nothing to do with the person deep in our hearts.

Maybe someday in the future, we will hold someone's hand and slowly appreciate the scenery of life together.

In fact, promises mean nothing, and their disappearance means nothing either.

Everything has its own destination.

We learn to let go, not to force things,

To deeply hide you, bury you deeply,

Bury you where time's dust cannot reach...

Yet, why, at a rainy dusk,

At a quiet night, do you still faintly appear in my heart,

Fading in and out, unable to be taken away, unable to be erased.

Ajuzhu's flower-like smile is gradually fading by the green stone bridge and beside the small mirror lake.

Jiaofeng's tears fall heavily in the pouring rain.

You gave me protection, I return you blessings.

As a hero, you need ambition.

But how can you compensate me for the happiness you owe me?

Chen Jialuo chose not to betray the masses but betrayed the beauty.

One died for him, another lived a lonely life because of him.

Perhaps his fate was already decided long ago.

In the end, it was just an empty promise about cattle and sheep on the frontier.

Empty promises, empty promises, happiness forever remains unfulfilled...

How I wish we could have a deep hug and then part ways.

Deep feelings remain unchanged, yet alliances are broken.

In the end, it always falls short by just a little bit.

Happiness vanishes in the blink of an eye.

From now on, I am alone, closing the door every day myself,

Turning off the lights alone.

Actually, it's not that bad, it's just that there is one less person's warmth in cold nights,

Just that happiness is no longer complete...

At the end of "The Heaven Sword", Zhou Zhiruo asked Zhang Wujie, among Xiao Zhao, Zhu'er, Zhao Min, and herself, who does he truly love. Zhang Wujie felt myriad emotions, recalling that he had once asked himself the same question. Back then, he only thought that if he could live together with all four young ladies, wouldn't that be carefree and joyful? But as fate changed, Xiao Zhao went far away to Persia to become the leader of a sect, his cousin Zhu'er passed away, Zhiruo went astray, only Zhao Min stayed by his side. Although there were misunderstandings along the way, his feelings for Zhao Min were mixed with love and hate, but he never stopped missing her. However, Zhiruo's interference always made his heart waver, preventing him from confronting his true feelings. Until this moment, facing Zhao Min's sudden departure, he finally realized that his feelings for the mischievous little demoness were so hard to let go. If he never saw her again in this life, he would definitely not be able to live on. He finally found the answer buried deep in his heart: he respected Zhiruo, he was grateful to Zhu'er, he pitied Xiao Zhao, but his love for Zhao Min was profound and unforgettable.

Sometimes, people only realize after losing something. Some people, some events, we think they are just passing clouds in life, thinking we can forget them in the vastness of the world. Yet when parting comes, we discover that these memories have already taken root in our hearts, unable to be removed, unable to be erased.

Yang Guo and Little Dragon Maiden eventually became a couple, living freely outside the Jianghu. Does he remember another devoted woman who thinks of him endlessly across the horizons, sacrificing her youth to wait for him her whole life? Would he recall the innocent face from many years ago, the first meeting when she smiled and said, "My surname is Guo, my name is Xiang."

People we cherish, cannot give us promises. Thus, you finally understand how rare and difficult it is to attain happiness. But why must we still pursue it like moths flying into flames, clinging to it for life? Perhaps it is as Li Mochou often softly recites: "Ask the world, what is love, that it makes people pledge their lives to each other." The most complex thing in the world, how can one person fully comprehend it?

Someone teaches you how to love, but they no longer love you.

Someone you keep waiting for, but they have forgotten you.

Someone wants to leave, and you don't try to stop them, because you gradually realize that stopping them is useless. All you can give them is freedom.

You thought that as long as you left gracefully, there wouldn't be too much pain, no lingering attachment. But why, in a noisy crowd, do you suddenly fall silent? Why, listening to a song halfway, do you suddenly choke up? You don't know what you're expecting, what you're holding onto. What lingers in your mind are all reflections of the past.

The person who loves you asks very little of you. They want to see you when they miss you, talk to you when they feel lonely. That is their entire happiness.

If making a hasty decision due to obsession leads to leaving, you can go, but don't go too far. Don't rush to define each other, don't rush to set boundaries. Look back and see if she is still there.

Treat well the person who loves you, the one who doesn't want you to worry, so forces a smile and lies saying they've let go, the one who quietly pays attention to you and has never left. If you are still hesitant, if you can't help but think of her, if you still care about her every expression, why not give her a chance, and yourself a chance?

In love, if both parties are too passive, a beautiful relationship may fade away under the erosion of time. It's not that the two people aren't suitable for each other, but that both sides are used to waiting, waiting for the other to take the initiative. Impatient ones choose to leave, leaving behind only regrets.

Therefore, love exists in the next life, like an undying spark, needing only some dry wood, and it can still shine brightly.

Jimmy says:

When you liked me, I didn't like you.

When you fell in love with me, I fell in love with you.

When you left me, I fell in love with you.

Was it that you moved too fast, or that I couldn't keep up with your steps?

We missed Noah's Ark, missed the Titanic, missed all kinds of excitement and calm, we continue to miss each other.

But, please allow me to say such selfish words:

Years later,

If you haven't married,

If I haven't married,

Then,

Can we be together?

Remember the happiness you owe me!
