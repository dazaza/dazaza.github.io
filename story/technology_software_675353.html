The antibody activity of the conjugate (3HJ1-HSA-MMC) was retained with the molecular ratio of 1: 2:60. These results indicate that these vasoactive agents can change the tissue perfusion ratio through their effect on tumor blood vessels and increase the access of the conjugate to the tumor, thereby enhancing the effectiveness of tumor-directed therapy using conjugates.
