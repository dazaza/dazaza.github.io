Li Yuchun prepared a big gift for the 2009 Kuang Girl Champion, Su Xing performed an impromptu RAP

by ladymr2009 on 2009-05-23 04:48:41

"Happy Girl 2009", which everyone has been looking forward to, finally held a national launching press conference in Beijing on the morning of May 7. Up to now, Hunan TV's selection battle, which had been silent for two years, started again. Li Yuchun, the super popular queen who filmed the promotional video for the launch, appeared at the press conference as the spokesperson for the event and encouraged "Happy Girl" together with Su Xing, the Happy Boy.

In view of brand continuity, this year's competition adopted the theme song of Super Girl 2006 "Singing Loud". On that day, a re-arranged theme song performed jointly by many Happy Boys was officially released, and the MV of the theme song was broadcast globally for the first time at the press conference. When the familiar and touching melody and youthful voice sounded, it seemed that everyone was immersed in the review of the classic pictures of previous years, and at the same time, they were full of deep expectations for this year's completely new competition. More than two hundred leaders, guests and media friends witnessed this exciting moment together at the scene.

Due to their busy acting work respectively, "R&B" young king Su Xing represented dozens of fast boys to cheer for the new event on site. Su Xing, who is good at both singing and composing, came up with an idea and improvised a RAP about his wishes and hopes for Happy Girls, presenting it to all present through his signature performance. This highly creative way of encouragement won consistent approval from everyone. Meanwhile, Su Xing said that he would pay attention to this year's competition together with other artists of Tianyu, and even go to some sub-venues to cheer and give advice to the contestants.

As the ace star of each session without exception, Li Yuchun made her appearance as expected at the end of the day, and Chun Chun, full of spring, brought a mysterious big gift today. Originally, in order to reflect the meaning of conveying dreams and conveying happiness of the competition, Chun Chun specially prepared a dazzling diamond-encrusted microphone, indicating that she would personally pass this microphone to the champion of the 2009 Fast Girl. On the other hand, Chun Chun also sent heartfelt blessings to the new competition, contestants and artists.

Under the warm welcome and encouragement of the seniors of the same school of Tian Entertainment, and with the full efforts of Hunan TV's Xiang Army, "Happy Girl 2009" officially started. Who will be the next "Li Yuchun"? Everything will be revealed in the next four months.
