Plot introduction of the last episode of "I Am A Blade Of Grass": The TV series "I Am A Blade Of Grass", which has long been called a tragic drama by outsiders, is about to wrap up. Recently, screenwriters Shen Jie and Zhang Lu reiterated that this is not a tragic drama, but rather an advanced emotional life drama. It was learned that the reason why audiences might have the wrong impression could be because "I Am A Blade Of Grass" is the sister piece of "Living With A Smile". "Living With A Smile", which has moistened the screens many times, has been hailed by many viewers as a classic in recent years' tragic dramas. Yesterday, Shen Jie said over the phone: "Living With A Smile is a tragic inspirational drama. Both I Am A Blade Of Grass and the upcoming new drama are advanced emotional life dramas, unrelated to tragedy." From the plot perspective, the level of sadness in "I Am A Blade Of Grass" is even higher than that of "Living With A Smile". The heroine Lin Xiaocao not only has two mother-in-laws who misunderstand her greatly, but also has two complex intertwined love lines. On one side is her boyfriend with whom she has been in love for many years, but who fell out of love and suffers from terminal illness, on the other side is a rich young master who harbors hatred due to misunderstanding. Even so, "I Am A Blade Of Grass" is merely an emotionally charged life drama, not a tragic one. "It tells the story of a blade of grass. This grass may live a hard life, but it still stands tall and upward! This is a hopeful story, making the audience cry because of being moved," Shen Jie emphasized again.

Audiences who watched "Living With A Smile" couldn't help but shed tears, even male audiences had many who secretly wiped their eyes while glued to the TV set every day. The true love between mother and daughter was irresistible, confusing the boundaries between tragedy and sorrow. Zhang Lu finally summarized: "Living With A Smile can arouse deep longing and yearning for family affection within people's hearts, giving much food for thought." Moreover, Zhang Lu revealed that the upcoming new drama will still focus on emotions, being an advanced emotional life drama.
