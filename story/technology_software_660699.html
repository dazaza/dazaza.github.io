My fashion shopping fun McCollin

by mickylim144 on 2012-02-08 09:43:24

I don't like shopping and going to stores like most girls. I get dizzy and overwhelmed when I enter a mall, and I don't even know what to buy. As for popular brands, I know very few of them, maybe because I was never particular or in the habit of paying attention to these things since I was young. My first encounter with Mecox Lane was when I accidentally saw a magazine called "Mecox Lane" on a colleague's desk. I flipped through it casually, but it quickly caught my eye. It was a magazine dedicated to providing lifestyle fashion products for young and middle-aged women. To be precise, "Mecox Lane" is an online merchant based in Shanghai, an online store. This same-name magazine introduces the products sold in the store, and the product updates are consistent with what's listed on the website. So I called the order number and bought a pair of earrings and a mobile phone charm for myself, which was the first time I ever shopped online. Five days later, a local courier delivered the items. Just as promised online, I could inspect the goods and try them on; if I wasn't satisfied, I could refuse the delivery; if I liked them, I could pay and keep them. Moreover, within ten days of purchase, I could return or exchange the goods unconditionally. I tasted the sweetness for the first time, and from then on, I became a regular customer at Mecox Lane.

The products there are divided into five categories: clothing and accessories, home goods, beauty and health care, pet supplies, and cute babies. I've bought from each category, but the one I purchase most frequently is clothing and accessories. Mecox Lane usually updates its inventory every one to two months, posting new items online while simultaneously sending out the current issue of the magazine, which is delivered to my home. The quality of the products varies; some are better, others slightly worse. However, the prices are mid-to-low-end, quite suitable for my budget. There are several ordering and payment methods available, allowing customers to choose according to their own circumstances. Whenever new products are launched or the company holds events, Mecox Lane sends me emails and text messages.

On the website, the familiar page layout and user interface design structure allow me to operate smoothly and effortlessly! Soon, I became the most prestigious Diamond Card member there, though this requires reaching a considerable spending amount within a certain period to qualify! I also recommended it to my sister and sister-in-law. Later, I heard they bought quite a bit too. I have visited and purchased from other online merchants, but none were as good as Mecox Lane. When I call to inquire, the service specialists are so patient and enthusiastic. From logging onto the website, to the final click of submitting the order, to the moment the courier delivers the items and gifts to my hands, every step is served meticulously and thoroughly.

I've made many mistakes too, buying things that turn out to be unsuitable or not very attractive after all, leaving many items unworn or unused. But this "wasted money" spent regretfully has been valuable, increasing my purchasing insight, taste, and experience over repeated errors. I often take them out to look at, fantasizing that if I had a little daughter in the future, I would leave all these beads, chains, hair ropes, and scarves for her to play with. I wonder if she will laugh at how crazy her mom was when she grows up!

For three years, it has been an indispensable part of my life, and I have always been a loyal customer there. Now more and more women know about Mecox Lane; more people are shopping there; the reputation, scale, and direct-operated stores in Shanghai of this company are getting better and better. It has brought me countless surprises, satisfaction, and happiness! I truly feel its convenience and speed. Having such an online store is a one-time solution for someone like me who doesn't like shopping around!
