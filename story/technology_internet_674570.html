World Badminton Rankings: Lee Chong Wei Tops the List, China Leads in Four Events

by gdf1122 on 2012-02-24 14:35:18

Benqv News - Beijing Time, November 10th: The Badminton World Federation (BWF) has released the latest world rankings. Compared to the previous ranking, there were no changes in the top ten men's singles and women's singles players. Similarly, the top five pairs in men's doubles, women's doubles, and mixed doubles remained unchanged. The Chinese national badminton team continues to hold the number one position in four categories.

In men's singles, Malaysia's Lee Chong Wei remains at the top of the world rankings with 99,147 points, followed by Chen Long and Lin Dan in second and third place respectively. In women's singles, Wang Shixian still occupies the top spot globally, while Wang Yihan, Wang Xin, and Jiang Yanjiao are ranked second, third, and fifth respectively. In doubles, China’s Cai Yun/Fu Haifeng, Wang Xiaoli/Yu Yang, and Zhang Nan/Zhao Yunlei continue to hold the first positions in men's doubles, women's doubles, and mixed doubles respectively.
