The experience of female students in school earning 6000 yuan per month while attending school and running an online store

by anonymous on 2011-03-28 10:18:56

The online store is open, but there are not many orders. I believe this is the most troublesome problem for the vast majority of store owners. Nowadays, opening an online store has become a very popular start-up project, with everyone chasing after it. But most people blindly follow the trend without conducting detailed research and learning about online stores before opening one. They just hear that others earn a lot from their online stores, so they immediately follow the trend and open their own store. The result of doing so is often that the online store is opened for several months without any attention. Everyone knows how to open an online store, even elementary school students know now, but how many can actually make money? Therefore, the skills of running an online store are very important. Only by mastering targeted skills can you remain invincible and stand out under such immense competition.

Some store owners or newcomers often say: "What kind of skills? It's nothing more than finding good sources of goods and spending more time managing the online store. It's normal that I don't make money because I don't know anyone, can't get cheap and high-quality goods, and have to work regularly, so where would I find the time to manage the online store?" Here, the author wants to ask these people: Since you have no time and no good sources of goods, why did you choose to stock up and open a store in the first place? Moreover, have you done targeted promotion and publicity? Do you check how many people visit your online store every day? Have you summarized the reasons why you're not profitable? To make a profit from an online store, you must do promotions. Promotion is the key factor.

Zhang Qiao is a college student nearing graduation. Her classmates are all busy preparing for job hunting, but she isn't worried at all. This is because her monthly income is already fixed at around 6000 yuan. When she first entered university, Zhang Qiao was just a student who knew a little bit about computers. Later, under the guidance of her roommate, she gradually got into online shopping and became addicted to it. In her second year of university, Zhang Qiao had the idea of opening an online store and gradually came into contact with cash-back online stores. For such stores, apart from regular promotions, Guangzhou VIPshop Information Technology Co., Ltd., does not require any other concerns. The key is to do good promotion and publicity. This business model is exactly what Zhang Qiao hoped for. She didn't have much capital and also had to attend classes regularly, so stocking up and opening a store definitely wasn't suitable for her. With reference from her roommates, Zhang Qiao contacted the online store developer and purchased a set of online stores.

After the online store opened, Zhang Qiao's first promotional target was the school students, as there were many students in the school who liked online shopping. Every day, couriers were constantly coming to the school. When Zhang Qiao promoted her online store, she told her classmates that her store could help customers save money when shopping on nearly 200 well-known shopping websites. Who doesn't want to save money? Moreover, naturally, if there's business, they would take care of each other. Almost all her classmates shopped at Zhang Qiao's online store.

But Zhang Qiao knew that the school was only a small market, and to make more money, she still had to rely on the internet. In the following time, Zhang Qiao used soft articles to promote her online store. She expressed the service of saving money through her online store in article form and posted them on many targeted shopping and consumption forums and websites. Combining online and offline methods, the membership registration volume of the online store quickly increased. Plus, every time a customer shopped and saved money, the return rate of customers shopping at her online store was almost 100%. In the first month, Zhang Qiao earned a commission of 2145 yuan. As the promotional efforts increased, Zhang Qiao's income kept rising. Of course, all of this was done during her spare time. Zhang Qiao knew her main task was still studying, so she spent 1-2 hours promoting after class every night.

In less than three years, Zhang Qiao already had nearly 100,000 yuan in savings. During these more than two years of schooling, she never took a single penny from her family, and all her earnings were from her own efforts. A sentence that Zhang Qiao often says is: To succeed in something, the prerequisite must be persistence. Another thing is mastering the skills. Running an online store is the same. Only in this way can you ensure using the lowest cost and least time to gain the maximum profit.

Indeed, blindly following trends will only cause you to lose out. The order volume of online stores is crucial for traffic, traffic depends on promotion, promotion depends on skills and persistence, persistently doing targeted promotion part-time without stocking goods can still make money. Since I started developing cash-back online stores, I have always adhered to this view. Through countless customer cases, this view has been verified. I welcome everyone to exchange and discuss experiences and knowledge about online stores.
