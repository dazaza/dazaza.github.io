Sweet dating tips for a romantic rendezvous

by noblesingle on 2008-11-15 06:33:40

[Repost] Sweet Dating Tips for Romantic Dates

Use different methods for different occasions:

① Park: Visiting a park is one of the most common ways for young couples to go on dates. However, you shouldn't just sit idly on the park benches but rather stroll around or swing on a swing to keep your body active, which can help both parties ease any tension.

② Walk: Strolling under the moonlight along the street is also a good method, but it's important that she walks on the side closer to the sidewalk, and try to match her pace as much as possible. The initial distance between the two should ideally be about 20 centimeters.

③ Dancing: We have previously read about the drawbacks of dancing on a first date, but if the other party is enthusiastic about it, you should accommodate their wishes, and the vigorous movement can eliminate any barriers between each other.

④ Sports: You can increase enthusiasm while shouting, but you must not be outdone by her skills.

⑤ Movies: It’s essential to know that the plot suits first-time lovers; avoid sad ones, otherwise, she might consider it an ominous sign. Also, avoid purely slapstick comedies, otherwise, she may think your taste is low. Moreover, not being able to converse during the movie is one of its disadvantages.

⑥ Restaurant: Pay attention to finding a corner seat so you can avoid others' gazes, reducing the psychological tension of your first-love girlfriend. Additionally, you should invite her to sit with her back to the door so that her line of sight will focus on you, while you yourself can see the entire restaurant situation, allowing you to guide the conversation content in a calm atmosphere. Unless particularly interesting or necessary, avoid finding topics among the crowd in the restaurant.

⑦ Museum: If your girlfriend is elegant and quiet, you can consider visiting a museum together, which may unexpectedly create a good atmosphere. But you cannot be completely ignorant or know very little about the exhibits in the museum, having your girlfriend explain them to you would put you at a disadvantage.

⑧ Competition or Performance: Let the girl watch sports competitions or circus performances, introducing her to a world she isn’t familiar with. Meanwhile, you can comment while watching, deepening her impression of you.

⑨ Amusement Park: It easily puts people in an excited state, creating a candid and open atmosphere, and bodies naturally get closer. But you must not have any notion of "taking advantage," otherwise, once she becomes wary, you'll ruin a good thing yourself.

⑩ Choice of Restaurant: Girls tend to like small restaurants with a refined and clean ambiance, exotic Western restaurants, or even rooftop restaurants in skyscrapers where they can enjoy meals while taking in the city night views.

If you're late for the date:

Rush to the meeting place, apologize sincerely while catching your breath, then naturally find an excuse such as “I got stuck in traffic on Zhongshan Road, I was so anxious that I jumped out of the car and ran here, running until I'm out of breath.” Or: “I wanted to find a gift you'd like, so I spent too much time looking…” In short, make up excuses that are reasonable and don't let her realize that it was due to your carelessness and has nothing to do with cars or gifts. This kind of promise helps alleviate your girlfriend's dissatisfaction. The motive is good and cannot be considered malicious deception. However, being late can happen once but not twice, telling too many lies will eventually expose the truth.

Farewell Techniques:

It's best to say goodbye at an appropriate bus stop, accompany her while waiting for the bus, until the bus arrives, wait for her to board and leave before you depart. This way, the girl won't feel that you're clinging too tightly. If she allows you to escort her home, it indicates that she has developed some feelings for you. At this point, it's crucial to grasp the timing of parting, meaning the first date should end when the girl still wants to spend more time with you. This is the ultimate trick to make her look forward to the next date. At the same time, you should use the following invitation techniques. For example, while waiting for the bus, you ask her: “Today is the summer solstice, do you know what the summer solstice means?” She says no, and at this moment, the bus arrives. You say: “Alright, I'll tell you next time we meet.” Then repeat the time and place of the next date again, and see her off onto the bus. Alternatively, if she says while parting: “Spending time with you, I had a great evening,” you must immediately follow up with: “That's wonderful, I'll make you even happier next time.” Then appropriately repeat the time and place of the next date.
