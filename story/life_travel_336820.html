Lipu Abundant Fish Cave, Yangshuo Big Banyan Tree, Xijie Two-day Tour

by liriting526 on 2010-03-30 15:39:41

Day 1: Depart by bus from the urban area of Guilin at 8:00 am; Arrive at Fengyu Rock, a national AAAA scenic spot at 11:30 am and check in at the VIP building of Fengyu Rock Hotel. Taste the Lychee Potato Banquet which was once a royal tribute at Fengyu Rock hotel at 12:00 pm, Explore the magnificent rocks of "the First Cave in Asia" Fengyu Rock at 1:30 pm, Start the "the First Drift in the World's Caves" a 3.3 km underground river tour by boat, Take the aerial train to cross the picturesque farmland, Go back to the hotel for rest or join various interactive activities (bicycling, playing ball, picking, fishing, etc.) within the scenic area at 16:00 pm, Have dinner at your own expense at the special farm restaurant around the scenic area at 17:30 pm, Watch the free gift - the惊thrilling Yao adult ceremony and the performance of the unique skills of Du Jie at 19:30 pm. Have a sauna and soak in hot spring, Check in at the VIP building of Fengyu Rock Hotel (standard room) at 23:30 pm.
