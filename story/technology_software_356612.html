Design Philosophy of Wuhan Oriental Express Children's Wear:

In general, most people in Wuhan, including Wuhan Oriental Myth Garments Co., Ltd., only care if children can wear clothes, but often neglect what kind of design truly suits children. Our designers consider the following points:

Since children are active and have higher body temperatures than adults, they tend to sweat more. Clothes made from general synthetic fibers have poorer water absorption compared to cotton and can easily irritate children's delicate skin, causing allergic reactions. Therefore, we are very strict when selecting materials and mainly use cotton products. After processing them to be softer, this makes children feel comfortable and natural when wearing them.

Breaking away from traditional restrictive designs of Wuhan Oriental Express Children's Wear, we emphasize the fashionable style of European and American trends while still maintaining the lively and innocent side that Oriental Express Children's Wear should have. When designing styles, attention is paid to the size of the clothes and whether they are easy to wear. Designs for toddlers aim to allow them to change their own clothes independently, helping them develop independence.
