How can high school students choose majors for undergraduate studies in Quebec at a lower cost?

by dingyi947279 on 2012-02-16 17:29:22

Due to factors such as the domestic employment environment and employment pressure, most of China's students studying abroad have plans for overseas study. Studying in Quebec, Canada is cost-effective, requiring only 70,000-80,000 RMB per year to apply and complete. International students also have a chance to receive up to 90% scholarships, with amounts reaching as high as 100,000 RMB over two years for a master’s degree in Canada. So, how do undergraduate students choose their major and school for studying in Quebec, Canada? Graduates from domestic universities have several options:

**Option One**: If you have a TCF score of over 430 points, you can directly apply for a master’s program. However, this option has extremely strict requirements for all aspects of the student. If the student's basic conditions are not very outstanding, the success rate of application will be low.

**Option Two**: Apply for a pre-master’s course in Quebec, Canada. Currently, some universities in Quebec offer pre-master’s courses that provide a transitional environment for students who wish to apply for a master’s degree in Quebec, Canada. Through the pre-master’s course, students can improve their qualifications while gaining exposure to Western culture and contacting renowned professors at Canadian universities, securing opportunities for direct interviews and ensuring successful applications for master’s programs.

**Option Three**: Apply for a graduate diploma program in Quebec, Canada. These programs have a short duration, making it easy to graduate. Additionally, since these programs combine theory with practice, graduates can find jobs in Quebec, Canada, and then proceed to apply for immigration.
