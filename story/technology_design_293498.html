Galaxy Securities: Qingdao Haier's washing machine assets injection into the listed company unfolds as scheduled ＿ Stock Knowledge

by xl0958870 on 2009-12-05 02:36:57

1. Event: On November 30, 2009, Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) announced that the company was negotiating with its actual controller, Haier Group, regarding the transfer of Haier Group's 31.93% stake in Haier Electrical Appliances Group Co., Ltd. (a Hong Kong Stock Exchange listed company, stock code "1169", hereinafter referred to as "Haier Electrical Appliances"), and had preliminarily reached a consensus, but no letter of intent or agreement had been signed yet.

2. Our Analysis and Judgment: Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) is a leading white goods enterprise we have consistently favored. Especially since the introduction of equity incentives policies in May this year and the commencement of asset reorganization, the company has become one of our main investment recommendations to investors. Currently, Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) already owns refrigerator and air conditioning assets. With the controlling stake in Haier Electrical Appliances, the company will own washing machine and water heater assets, thus possessing all of Haier's white goods assets, which are also the most profitable industrial assets under the Haier Group.

This announcement did not specify the method for future acquisition of Haier Electrical Appliances' 31.93% equity. We judge that there may be two possible ways:

(1) Cash Acquisition or Asset Swap: As of November 30, Haier Electrical Appliances closed at HKD 4.31, with a market capitalization in Hong Kong of HKD 8.735 billion. If acquiring 31.93% equity, it would require HKD 2.789 billion. From a financial perspective, Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) does not lack funds, with cash on hand reaching RMB 6.514 billion by the end of Q3; however, from the perspective of control rights, using cash to acquire Haier Group's equity in Haier Electrical Appliances is not an optimal choice. There are more uncertainties about whether an asset swap is feasible, which we will monitor.

(2) Directed Issuance to Acquire Assets: If this option is adopted, Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) will issue shares directed to Haier Group, then purchase Haier Group’s 31.93% equity in Haier Electrical Appliances. Through this scheme, Haier Group can still obtain part of Haier Electrical Appliances' rights through holding Qingdao Haier (600690.SH); simultaneously, Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) does not need to provide more funds. Therefore, we believe the company is more likely to adopt this plan.

Haier Electrical Appliances' revenue from 2007 to the first half of 2009 were HKD 8.549 billion, HKD 11.409 billion, and HKD 5.309 billion respectively, with post-tax net profits being HKD 226 million, HKD 175 million, and HKD 171 million respectively, maintaining a net profit margin between 1.53% - 3.22%. If we do not consider the factor of share capital increase, Haier Electrical Appliances will bring about RMB 0.13 in earnings to the company in 2010.

Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) currently holds 19.38% of Haier Electrical Appliances' shares. Upon completion of this transaction, the company will hold 51.31% of Haier Electrical Appliances' shares. The company and Haier Group have consulted the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission on whether the company needs to make a full tender offer to Haier Electrical Appliances shareholders, and have received a principle-based reply from the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission agreeing to exempt the company from making a full tender offer to Haier Electrical Appliances shareholders. These matters still require approval from Qingdao Haier (600690.SH)'s board of directors, shareholders' meeting, relevant government departments, and Haier Group.

3. Investment Suggestion: We believe Qingdao Haier (600690.SH) is entering a period of rapid profit growth. As the company's white goods assets are gradually injected into the listed company, the company will quickly improve operational efficiency and profitability through a strong supply chain management system and a just-in-time demand model under zero inventory, bringing continuous returns to investors. We expect the integration effect of Haier Electrical Appliances to be evident in 2010, adjusting the earnings per share for 2009, 2010, and 2011 to RMB 0.87, RMB 1.12, and RMB 1.27 respectively, corresponding to PE valuations of 27X, 20.97X, and 18.50X. We continue to maintain a "recommend" rating for the company.

Stock trading process includes specifying buy/sell orders, stock name (or code), quantity, and price. This commission is only valid for the day it is placed. The content of the commission includes the abbreviated name (code), quantity, and price of the stock you wish to buy or sell. The stock abbreviation usually consists of three to four Chinese characters, while the stock code is six digits. The stock code and abbreviation must match when placing a commission. At the same time, there are certain rules for the quantity of stocks traded: the number of shares to be purchased must be a multiple of 100, but the number of shares sold does not have to be a multiple of 100.

There are four types of commissions: counter submission, telephone automatic commission, computer automatic commission, and remote terminal commission.
