Kobo is a designer. One day, Kobo was busy from morning till night at the company, but his design drafts were repeatedly rejected and never passed. His desk was piled high with materials such as unsuccessful designs and customer feedback. After struggling until the early hours of the morning, he trudged home dejectedly, alone. At the same time, God was adjusting a projector. Once done, fantastic projections filled with creative ideas appeared in the air. God lounged on the sofa, comfortably watching the images.

God has a pet duck, his only companion in heaven. Soon after finishing watching, God turned a winding key, making the images disappear. Feeling sleepy, God went off to sleep. The pet duck was frustrated, not understanding anything, so it jumped onto God's sofa and tried to imitate turning the winding key. No matter how it twisted left or right, no images appeared. The pet duck got angry and wound it a few more times, still nothing happened. Unable to bear it any longer, the pet duck became furious, grabbed the winding key, and angrily threw it into the sky before limping away in anger.

The winding key flew through the air and began falling rapidly. Kobo, who was feeling gloomy while looking up at the sky, suddenly noticed a mysterious falling object and hurriedly tried to dodge it everywhere.

Strangely, no matter how Kobo tried to avoid it, the mysterious object seemed to have a life of its own and followed Kobo’s movements. Finally, when Kobo could run no further, the object directly lodged itself onto Kobo's head. Stunned, Kobo stood there, touching the thing on his head, trying to pull it off, but to no avail. He then tried turning it, and suddenly his brain started spinning rapidly, filling his head with famous design concepts and ideas.
