50 Sentences for Unmarried People

by zhenai986 on 2009-12-04 22:49:17

1. Don't fall in love because of loneliness. Time is a devil. Over time, if you are sentimental, even if you don't love the other person, you will develop feelings for them eventually. What will you do then?

2. Don't get married out of a sense of responsibility. You must know that marrying someone you don't love is the most irresponsible thing to do. Even though it may make the other person sad at the moment, it's better than making them sad for years or even a lifetime.

3. Regardless of how old or pressured by family and friends, never take marriage lightly. Marriage isn't like playing cards; reshuffling comes with a great cost.

4. In matters of the heart, there is rarely a clear right or wrong. If he (she) leaves you, it’s likely because there is something about you that doesn’t satisfy him. Looking back on the past days together, they were always beautiful. Of course, there are despicable people who deceive others with sweet words, but these kinds of people are few.

5. It's fine to date someone whose lifestyle differs greatly from yours, but be cautious about marrying them. Consider whether you can endure these differences long-term.

6. Some say you should date someone you like and marry someone who likes you, but this is one-sided. Why would a lover stay with someone who doesn't like them? How could a wife live with someone she doesn't like?

7. Truly loving someone means doing your best to make them happy. When they're happy, you'll be happy too, and both of you will have passion again.

8. Don't give up on pursuing someone just because you’re not as good-looking as them. Appearance only creates a first impression. The key factor in whether two people can be together lies mainly in their personalities. I've seen countless handsome men paired with unattractive women and vice versa.

9. Women should learn to dress themselves well. Don't use simplicity as an excuse or household chores as a justification. If you don't understand fashion, you're not a complete woman.

10. The longer you can prolong dating, the better. There are at least two advantages: One, enjoy the pleasure of dating for as long as possible since the feeling of marriage is very different from dating. Two, the longer you spend together, the more you can test each other's sincerity and compatibility. This way, post-marriage feelings will be much stronger.

11. Men who aren't "bad" aren't loved by women. By "bad," we don't mean cruel or selfish, but rather smooth-talking and flattering. Good men often think saying sweet nothings is insincere and superficial, so they avoid it. However, while it may be inappropriate to talk like that to others, with your spouse, being a little smooth is okay. Why not be a man who is kind-hearted yet smooth-tongued?

12. They say marriage is the grave of love, which happens when premarital relationships are already heading towards the grave. Even without marriage, couples might split before reaching the grave. So why jump into the grave without breaking up first?

13. A woman who only reads books is like a dictionary—people only consult her when needed. A woman who only dresses up is like a vase—after a while, she becomes ordinary. Dressing well and taking care of oneself are necessary conditions for being a good woman, but not sufficient ones. You also need to read more books. This way, you'll find life more wonderful.

14. Plain living is true, but it should come after passion. Then passion arises again, followed by plainness. Passion and plainness should alternate in waves. A life with only plainness and no passion is meaningless. As long as you truly love him, you will have passion until death.

15. Do you love him? If so, tell him. Why hide your longing deep inside? Afraid of mismatched looks, status, or identity? Don't be afraid—it's beautiful to love someone.

16. If my wife and mother fell into the river, I'd save my mother first because she gave me life. I cannot abandon her for any reason. If I couldn't save my wife, I could join her in death and continue our love in the tomb.

17. Marrying hastily was already a mistake, so don't divorce hastily either. Try first. If it really doesn't work out, then divorce later.

18. People often talk about masculinity and femininity, but do you know what masculinity and femininity really mean? Masculinity is broad-mindedness and bravery, while femininity is gentleness and thoughtfulness.

19. What is charm? Charm is not beauty. Beautiful women don't necessarily attract me; elegant and dignified women are the ones I prefer. So don't worry if you're not pretty enough.

20. First loves are unforgettable and seem wonderful. Why? Not because he/she was handsome or beautiful, nor because unattainable things are better, but because when we first fall in love, our hearts are exceptionally pure, free of ulterior motives, and we dedicate everything we have to loving the other person. Later loves won't be as pure. Purity is the most precious thing in the world, and it's what we yearn for.

21. Most people in first love don't understand love, so first loves tend to fail more often than succeed. For marriage, it's better to choose someone who hasn't been married before because everyone prefers something original. However, for dating, it's better to choose someone who has dated before because people who have experienced love know what love is and how to love.

22. Men turn bad when they have money. Yes, many men do this, but men who turn bad as soon as they have money wouldn't be much better off even if they had none.

23. Whether a man can give you a sense of security has nothing to do with his height but everything to do with his mindset. I've seen many tall men who are useless, and many short men who are spirited. A man should be proud and confident to be a real man. Of course, the prerequisite is talent.

24. Is eternal love possible? Of course, it is! Why don't most people believe in it? Because they haven't found the one most suited to them in life—the one destined for them. Why can't they find that person? With so many people in the world and life being fleeting, finding the one most suited to you is no easy task. You might meet your destined partner at 40, but can you wait that long? If you can't find them in your 20s and have to get married, and then find them in your 30s or 40s, you may have to let go. This is the tragedy of life.

25. Why are there so few legendary stories of eternal, touching love in real life? Because such emotions are very precious, and precious things aren't easily seen. Are gold and diamonds easily found?

26. In the past, every time I broke up, I would hate her and feel glad whenever I heard bad news about her. Now, even if I lose her, I still wish her well and sincerely hope she lives happily. I would feel sad if she didn't. This is one difference between liking and loving.

27. Dating a smart person can be joyful because they are humorous and articulate, but it also comes with risks because such people can easily change their minds. Dating a straightforward person gives peace of mind, but life with them can be very dull.

28. Women shouldn't be overly strong-willed. Some women have overly strong self-respect. Whether it's someone else's fault or their own, they are equally stubborn. They always think asking for help is a sign of weakness, and they will never ask a man for anything. Such women can be quite a headache. Smart women know when to be strong and when to be gentle. Being strong should be directed outward, not toward those you love. If you're so strong with the ones you love, do you still want them to protect you?

29. To see if someone has depth, look at their speech internally and their attire externally. You can also look at their handwriting. Speech reveals a person's knowledge and cultivation. Attire reveals a person's taste, and handwriting reveals a person's character.

30. To know if someone loves you, see if they are energetic and happy when they are with you. If they are, they love you. If not, they don't.

31. Some people always complain about not finding good partners. Once or twice is fine, but if it happens often, there's a problem. First, you should reflect on yourself and see if you have issues. If not, then you need to reevaluate your judgment. Why do you keep meeting bad people?

32. Some say once a man changes his heart, nine oxen can't pull him back. But can nine oxen pull back a woman who changes her heart? Men and women only differ physiologically; psychologically, they are largely the same.

33. Love and morality aren't closely related. A former female colleague told me she liked Yang Kang from "The Legend of the Condor Heroes" but disliked Guo Jing. I was surprised—how could she favor the bad and dislike the good? Later, I realized it made sense. Yang Kang betrayed his country and recognized his enemy as his father, which was wrong. But he was devoted in love, so why shouldn't he be loved? Real-life examples include gangsters who also experience love.

34. Some say love without bread will ultimately fail. Those who say this don't understand what love is. In the past, I hated hearing others talk about a woman's good conditions during dating. I didn't care about background, education, or status. If I loved her, it didn't matter if she shined shoes or worked as a prostitute. Adults said I was naive and asked how we could survive without money. I replied, what kind of life is it to have money but no love? Being with the person you love, drinking water and eating pickles makes me happy.

35. If you truly love someone, you will willingly change for them. If someone behaves indifferently in front of you and ignores your dislikes, they don't love you. So if you don't care enough about him or he doesn't care enough about you, then you don't love him or he doesn't love you. Don't assume you're naturally careless or believe he is. Meeting your true love will make even cowards brave, and similarly, careless people will become careful.

36. Keeping silent when both parties are interested is the highest form of love. At this point, both are fully enjoying flirtatious glances, passionate eye contact, and thrilling moments of finger-touching. Once expressed, the flavor fades somewhat because all actions become permitted and expected, eventually leading to numbness.

37. "A radish for each hole" refers to marital situations. In reality, regarding love, this doesn't hold true. Excellent people, regardless of gender, can have multiple options. That's why the world is full of stories of joy and sorrow.

38. Two types of women are adorable: one is maternal, considerate, and caring, making men feel deeply loved. The other is sisterly, timid, shy, and highly dependent on men, which brings out a man's protective nature, such as fighting rats or carrying heavy objects. There's another type of woman who neither knows how to care for others nor shows weakness to men, leaving men helpless.

39. Stinginess is a big taboo for men. Even if poor, don't act like it. Some complain that women only love men for their money, but it's not always true. Some women like men spending money on them to confirm their importance in the man's eyes. If a man loves a woman, he will willingly spend money on her.

40. Men and women complement each other, making work less tiring. In front of the opposite sex, men like to show their masculine side, which makes them more manly. Therefore, some degree of machismo is necessary.

41. Pursuing the person you admire is a common notion. However, if the other person doesn't like you, no matter how hard you pursue, it's useless. If they like you, you don't need to overthink your pursuit. Perhaps one day he is moved by your sincerity, but mostly, you will end up breaking up. Love isn't about being moved. If you're not his ideal partner, even if he accepts you temporarily, he will leave when he meets his ideal match. Of course, for those who like you, you still need to put in effort to please them because that makes dating romantic.

42. People often ask if you had to choose between a friend and a lover, which would you choose? Actually, this question is redundant. True friends or lovers will understand your actions. If they don't understand, losing them isn't a big deal. Friends and lovers should help each other rather than force concessions.

43. "Having seen the vast ocean, lesser waters cannot satisfy me; having beheld the clouds over Mount Wu, all others pale in comparison." But what if I haven't seen the vast ocean or returned quickly after just a glance, and haven't even been to Mount Wu but jumped straight into the city walls?

44. What is romance? Sending flowers? Walking in the rain? Standing outside a building waiting? If two people truly love each other, doing nothing and just quietly facing each other feels romantic. Otherwise, even sitting on the moon together won't feel romantic.

45. Whether or not you are equally matched in terms of social standing doesn't matter as much as being equally matched in interests. Without common language, even if you're together, you'll still feel lonely.

46. Learn to view the other person with understanding and appreciation, not with self-righteous concern.

47. There's no problem with two immature people being together, nor with two mature people being together. Problems arise when a mature person is with an immature one.

48. Some women expect their boyfriends to spoil them during dating and continue to be pampered after marriage, forgetting their responsibilities as wives. These women don't understand love.

49. Long-lasting love stems from mutual heartfelt affection built on equality. Anyone who only madly loves others without considering whether they are loved in return, or only enjoys being loved without genuinely loving others, will not have a good outcome.

50. If you agree with the above statements, please leave a comment... This way, more people can understand these truths.
