Introduction:

Xu Xi belongs to the organization specifically trained as painless and emotionless killers - Unit 701. Not wanting to become a feelingless killing tool, Xu Xi escapes from Unit 701 and goes into hiding in Hong Kong, using the identity of an ordinary library clerk to cover his tracks while also learning to live a peaceful life and become a normal person with feelings. Xu Xi, naive in interpersonal relationships, often receives disdainful looks from colleagues. There are only two people who are very close to him. One is TRACY, a female colleague pursued by everyone in the library, but TRACY ignores others and has affection for this Xu Xi who cannot understand emotions; the other is his chess friend Detective Stone Sir. Just when Xu Xi feels that he is beginning to turn into an ordinary person, Unit 701 suddenly appears, ruthlessly killing the leaders of Hong Kong's triads. Detective Stone Sir and the police are completely powerless against them. Seeing his friend Stone Sir in danger, Xu Xi transforms into the Black Mask out of friendship and confronts Unit 701.

The appearance of the Black Mask greatly angers Xiong Ju, the leader of Unit 701, who sends Ruo Lan, Xu Xi's former lover in the unit, to persuade the Black Mask to return. When Ruo Lan appears at the library, TRACY mistakenly believes that this woman is her rival in love and scolds her. Unknowingly, because she has shown a close relationship with Xu Xi, she gets herself entangled in this extremely dangerous vortex. In order to protect TRACY, Xu Xi transforms into the Black Mask and forcibly takes TRACY away to protect her. TRACY resists this monstrous Black Mask, not knowing that the Black Mask is actually her beloved Xu Xi. The Black Mask fights together with Stone Sir against Unit 701, torn between conflicting feelings: on one hand, hoping to build emotions with Ruo Lan, a person devoid of feelings; on the other hand, compelled by justice to eliminate these killers. The Black Mask ultimately fails to change Ruo Lan but discovers that he gradually develops feelings for TRACY. In the end, with no choice, he eliminates Ruo Lan and Unit 701 together. Although he is heartbroken, he realizes that he and TRACY have found what they have always wanted – love!
