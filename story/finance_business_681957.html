Central bank official: the management method of prepaid cards is being drafted

by b5fkvb17859 on 2012-02-28 16:28:07

By reporter Qian Feng / April 1, regarding various situations of Zhe Xin, a reporter of our journal consulted a section chief of the People's Bank of China (PBC) Payment and Settlement Department.

The "Management Measures for Payment Services by Non-financial Institutions" stipulates: payment institutions are prohibited from misappropriating customer reserve funds in any form. If a payment institution accepts customer reserve funds, it should open a special deposit account for reserve funds at a commercial bank to store the reserve funds.

If the financial accounting report submitted by an enterprise is fake and the reserve funds are not recorded, what would happen? This section chief said: "Although the regulatory authorities may not know temporarily, if it is found out, the 'License' will be unobtainable, and penalties will also be imposed."

This section chief also told the reporter that the management measures for reserve funds custody are being drafted, and several complementary measures will be released successively.

Regarding the situation where "prepaid cards are almost becoming bank current savings cards," and cannot be returned or cashed out, this section chief said: "The prepaid card management measures are being drafted, with corresponding regulations on amount control and redemption, aiming to incorporate this area into the regulatory scope. Now, all rules are set by shopping card enterprises themselves, which sometimes infringe on consumer rights."

Regarding Zhe Xin's issues of misappropriating customer reserve funds and falsifying financial reports, the reporter checked the "Measures." Article 46 stipulates: if someone applies for the 'Payment Business License' through fraudulent or other improper means but does not get approval, the applicant and its shareholders holding more than 5% of its equity shall not apply again or participate in applying for the 'Payment Business License' within three years. If someone applies for the 'Payment Business License' through fraudulent or other improper means and has already obtained approval, the People's Bank of China and its branches will order it to terminate payment business, revoke its 'Payment Business License'; if suspected of a crime, it will be legally transferred to the public security organs for investigation; if a crime is constituted, criminal responsibility will be pursued according to law; the applicant and its shareholders holding more than 5% of its equity shall not apply again or participate in applying for the 'Payment Business License'.

The aforementioned PBC section chief told the reporter: "What is called fraudulent means, the 'Measures' lists many conditions, requiring payment institutions to submit corresponding materials when applying for the 'Payment Business License', such as the conditions that investors and senior executives must meet, including the financial accounting report audited by an accounting firm, domestic and foreign capital situations, etc., as you mentioned; if it is later verified that these materials are fake, there will be severe punishment measures, not just revoking the license."

This person also indicated that we also require payment institutions to provide explanations about the use of reserve funds, and if they do not truthfully explain, this is also considered deception. We require payment institutions to submit a statement of authenticity of application materials to the local branch of the People's Bank of China, taking responsibility for the authenticity. The statement of authenticity is very important; payment institutions must ensure substantial compliance, not just formal compliance.
