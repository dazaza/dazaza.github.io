Godaddy Launches Aggressive New Year's Promotion: Domain Names and Virtual Hosting Both Available

by xwkj0983 on 2012-02-22 16:14:38

Following the Christmas promotion, GoDaddy, the world's largest domain registrar and web hosting provider, launched a new round of aggressive discounts as the New Year approached. Domain names and web hosting services were comprehensively reduced in price. Although GoDaddy has long been known for offering high value-for-money with continuous promotions, such a large-scale and significant price reduction is unprecedented.

### A Variety of Domain Products

GoDaddy’s latest promotion includes a wide range of domain products, including popular top-level domains (TLDs) and less common ones. According to the information, the TLDs .COM, .ORG, and .CO are now available for just $5.99, $6.99, and $13.99 respectively. Users can access these discounted prices through special promotional links: www.gd5.org, www.gdoff.org, and www.gdoff.co. Among all the domains, the most significant discount appears to be for the .ME domain. Users who purchase the .ME domain via the promotional link www.gdoff.me can get it for only $7.99, saving $12 compared to the previous price. The .ME domain is unique and innovative, making it an excellent choice for businesses looking to create personalized domain names.

Additionally, GoDaddy introduced the highly debated adult website-specific .XXX domain into this promotion. Users can enjoy a 10% discount by purchasing through the link www.gdxoff.com. This comprehensive range of domain discounts not only provides users with more options but also enhances their purchasing experience.

### Up to 30% Off on Web Hosting Products

To ensure that hosting users also enjoy significant discounts during the New Year, GoDaddy launched a promotional link at www.gd30off.com. Users who purchase annual plans for hosting through this link can enjoy a 30% discount. Among various hosting types, the 4GH annual plan offers the best value. Users who buy the 4GH annual hosting plan through this promotional link receive an additional 30% discount on top of the existing price reduction. After the discount, the premium hosting plan with 150GB of ultra-large space costs only $338 per year.

For business websites, GoDaddy also offers SSL certificate discounts. Users can purchase SSL certificates for only $12.99 through the promotional link www.gdssl.org and will receive a free dedicated IP address as part of the deal. GoDaddy's hosting products have always been praised for their high cost-effectiveness. This round of discounts undoubtedly represents a significant return for virtual hosting users.

### Caution Amidst the Promotions

As the New Year approaches, merchants are gearing up for another round of intense competition. The domain and hosting market is set to witness another major battle. During this period, some smaller hosting providers may take advantage of the situation by luring users with low prices to sell substandard products. Therefore, to avoid being scammed, users should remain vigilant and preferably choose reputable, well-known virtual hosting providers. This way, they won’t miss out on valuable purchasing opportunities.
