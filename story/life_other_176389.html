Experience with your eyes - CR Paint launches a color effect experience mode

Beijing message from Qianlong Wealth, recently in response to consumer needs, CR Paint has launched the "CR Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" nationwide. Through this software, consumers can intuitively experience the true effect of colors on the computer screen, eliminating the need for imagination when selecting colors, allowing them to experience it directly with their eyes.

It is understood that the "CR Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" was developed based on CR Coatings' 2008 "Living Colors" system. The main functional modules include Homeplayer, Easy Color Matching, and Decorating New Homes, comprehensively meeting consumers' varying levels of color service needs. The Homeplayer module focuses on the presentation of the "Living Colors" system, vividly presenting recommended colors extracted from CR's 456-color palette along with their wall and wood surface matching schemes in three-dimensional form.

CR Coatings advocates providing terminal customers with the best coating surface effects through service. Not only does it offer more environmentally friendly, healthy, and safe paint products to consumers, but it also creates personalized home color-matching effects that satisfy consumers through its services. The launch of the "CR Paint Color and Shape Design Demonstration System Software" resolves the "imagination" barrier consumers face when choosing color combinations, enabling them to freely combine their favorite colors at will.

The Easy Color Matching module completely stores CR Paint's 456 colors and the commonly used board colors for wood paints. Based on consumers' knowledge of color coordination, personal feelings, and preferences, they can freely combine color matching effects until they achieve their desired choices. This module also features a special simulation of children's room space for the Miao Xiang Paint "Miao Xiang Home," where while selecting colors, parents can also display the effects of their children's favorite children's rooms, completing the selection and experience of colors and effects for both adult and children's rooms in one stop.

The Decorating New Homes module allows consumers to precisely create a three-dimensional space according to the actual data of their house types. They can set up a new room according to their own needs from scratch, assigning colors, furniture, decorations, and even lighting, making the DIY process highly engaging.
