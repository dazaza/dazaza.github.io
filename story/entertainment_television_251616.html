On the 24th, at the press conference for *Play It Cool* held at SBS Mudeung in Seoul, producers Cho Nam-guk and Hong Seong-chang, along with the main cast including Jang Geun-seok (22), Park Shin-hye (19), FTisland member Lee Hong-gi (19), After School member UEE (21), Jung Yoo-mi (20), Kim In-ku (31), and others were present.

Jang Geun-seok plays the role of a talented composer and skilled pianist, serving as the captain of A.N.JELL, named "Hwang Tae-kyung." Although he portrays a stubborn and self-centered character, he is internally more vulnerable than anyone else. Jang Geun-seok said, "I'm very happy to finally be able to play a more natural, relatable character." He also mentioned that since the other actors are younger than him, instead of being taken care of, it's now his turn to take care of others.

He also stated that he currently has no plans to become a singer, and the singing in the drama is purely part of the acting setup. As an actor, he must stay true to his role. On the 10th, Jang Geun-seok released a single titled "Just Drag."

Park Shin-hye plays the roles of twin siblings "Gao Meinan" and "Gao Meinv." She described her character as stiff yet obedient but also mischievous. She said, "There are many scenes of jumping up and down, and occasionally running behind a tractor. So in the drama, you'll see many different sides of me." She added that while she does have quiet moments depending on the role, overall this movie makes her smile more and perhaps even livelier. She also mentioned that her dream is to become a singer, and since there are many singing scenes in the drama, she finds it enjoyable.

The real-life youthful idols Lee Hong-gi and UEE play roles close to their true selves. Lee Hong-gi plays the drummer of A.N.JELL, a cheerful overseas Chinese second-generation. UEE plays a character who has been a star since childhood, someone who can captivate anyone at first glance.

Lee Hong-gi said, "I will work hard to let everyone recognize me as an actor, not just as Lee Hong-gi from FTisland." UEE said, "Since it's my first time acting and I play a villainous role, I didn't know how to act at first and was nervous. But I will definitely try my hardest, and this is my greatest effort recently."

Zheng Ronghua, who has real-life band experience, plays the guitarist of A.N.JELL, named "Jiang Xinyu." He is a charming character despite his cold exterior. He also expressed that he would approach the filming of the drama with a sense of responsibility and mission.

*Play It Cool* will officially premiere on October 7th at 9:55 PM (Korean time) following *Swallow the Sun*.
