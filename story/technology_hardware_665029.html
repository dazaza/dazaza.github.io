Media Revenue Sharing Model Trilogy: Part Two - Revenue Sharing Based on Member Repeats

The second part is about repeated accounting according to membership. So far, the several major schools in the media-sharing world are as follows:

Shaolin Temple: Yishengkangjian is the creator of the media-sharing model, which matches the saying that all martial arts originate from Shaolin.

Wudang Sect: Jiajiale Shopping was established by shareholders of Yishengkangjian who branched out on their own. Originally, Zhang Zhenren was also a disciple of Shaolin.

Emei Sect: Jinglong Hall quickly rose through Guanglian Era's software services and media services. The shareholders of Guanglian Era also came from Yishengkangjian. Before Guo Xiang created Emei Sect, she also had deep ties with Shaolin.

These three major reputable schools in media sharing have avoided the drawbacks of sharing based on the effectiveness of a single advertisement, adopting a membership-based direct response marketing business model to share with media over the long term according to membership.

Due to media advertising, the direct sales revenue generated at the time/period is far from reaching the media's advertising sharing expectations. In order for the media to accept a shared cooperation, the media must be given a sharing expectation. Those direct response marketing companies (Yishengkangjian) proposed the concept of sharing over the long term according to membership.

For example, after Yishengkangjian advertised in the Reference News, readers of the Reference News saw the advertisement and ordered 1000 yuan worth of products. Thus, the identity of this member became bound to the Reference News. This 1000 yuan order was shared with the Reference News. One month later, this member again ordered 2000 yuan worth of products, whether it was an active order by the member or an order generated by an outbound call from Yishengkangjian's Call Center representative, this 2000 yuan order was shared with the Reference News.

Thus, the sharing income of the Reference News evolved from sharing the direct sales revenue generated by the current/periodic advertisement into a long-tail sharing.

To put it metaphorically, it’s like the media continuously herding cows (members) into pens, while enterprises hire a group of milkers (sales representatives) who daily use tools (advertising planning, DM, outbound, etc.) to milk cows. All the milk produced each day is shared with the media.

This type of sharing method, in the early stages of cooperation, did not significantly improve the media's earnings. Once the media could persist, when the accumulated members reached a certain scale, the sharing earnings would become very substantial.

In early 2006, Yishengkangjian made significant efforts in public relations to secure more pages in the Reference News. By 2009, even if the Reference News stopped advertising, the cumulative repeat consumption of members over more than three years allowed the Reference News to earn more than 1.5 million yuan per month in sharing.

At this point, some people might think, wouldn't Yishengkangjian lose out in this situation? Later, the Reference News made no further investment, and all the work was done by Yishengkangjian. It seems unprofitable to still share with the Reference News.

Let us consider:

1. Initially, the sharing income of the Reference News was far lower than the cost of the pages. A large part of the later sharing income was to make up for the earlier page value. It's equivalent to Yishengkangjian paying the advertising fee of the Reference News in installments.

2. Without the members generated earlier by the Reference News, there would be no DM投放 (Direct Mail) or Outbound campaigns by Yishengkangjian, and thus no subsequent repeat consumption.

Nowadays, commerce has returned to rationality. It's no longer the time to make money solely through cleverness. Anything can only last and grow big through win-win, multi-win, and mutual benefit.

But on the other hand, not every media outlet can persist in cooperation like the Reference News for five years. Yishengkangjian established shared cooperation with nearly 400 media outlets, but only dozens were truly active. Those media outlets that terminated cooperation halfway contributed considerable strength to Yishengkangjian's massive member database.

These experiences were summarized by Yishengkangjian after years of trials and tribulations. Jiajiale Shopping and Yaobao adopted these practices.
