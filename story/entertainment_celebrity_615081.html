He is handsome, loves embroidery, and even more fashionable than the Sharp Brother

The fashionable scavenger on the streets of Chengdu looks extremely similar to Nicholas Tse! With his grayish-white hair, brown sunglasses, a trendy hoodie, and half-length gloves, he exudes an overwhelming aura that instantly surpasses the Sharp Brother! Yesterday, a Weibo post about the fashionable scavenger on the streets of Chengdu sparked heated discussions among local netizens. In the photo posted by netizen Cikong on November 18th, this scavenger has a麻bag on his right shoulder, wearing from inside out a dark-colored shirt, a peach-red sweater, light-colored suit, etc., paired with brown sunglasses and a white hoodie, bearing a striking resemblance to the celebrity Nicholas Tse. (Click on the picture to enter Cikong's Weibo)

Yesterday, the reporter found the location where the scavenger in the photo appeared, Chengdu @World Computer City. Upon entering the first floor of the computer city, the reporter showed the photo, and many merchants gathered to discuss it. "Are you asking about the garbage guy? He has a few strands of yellow hair sticking up at the front, he looks a bit handsome, carrying a bag everywhere picking up garbage. He was commonly seen in August and September, but recently no one knows where he went."

Grandma Guo, who scavenges near the computer city, can be considered as the garbage guy's peer. She said that the garbage guy's hair on both sides is not the same length, the left side being much shorter than the right. Grandma Guo remembers that the garbage guy is about 170 cm tall, around 30 years old, very tall and handsome. Sometimes after picking up garbage, she even saw him sitting on the steps at the entrance of the computer city, embroidering on the ragged cloth he picked up.

Under Grandma Guo's guidance, the reporter found Mr. Li, the owner of the waste recycling stall under the overpass at No. 18, South Second Section, Yihuan Road. The garbage collected by Grandma Guo and the garbage guy are basically sold here. When talking about the garbage-picking young man who looks like Nicholas Tse, Mr. Li quickly said, "I know him, I know him. He borrowed 100 yuan from me, saying that his mother came and needed money. The next day, he returned 50 yuan to me, and then there was no news anymore."
