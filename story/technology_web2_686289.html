In the evening, many texts came from Wei, all expressing his willingness to be with Yue, filled with anxiety and urgency... Originally, Yue's phone number 13522794555 lost its signal. Everything that happened there was too sudden. Yue just let her heart guide her actions... Yue made a decision, which made Wei extremely happy. He pulled Yue into the car and put the ring he bought at noon on her finger. Yue jokingly said, "If I don't agree with you, what will you do with the ring?" Wei seriously replied, "Throw it away, it's bought for you anyway." After going home, Yue told her parents about Wei's matter. The parents did not oppose because they loved their daughter dearly. As long as their daughter was happy, everything else didn't matter... On October 7, 2010, Yue went to Wei's house with a nervous heart and met Wei's parents. Wei had previously lied to her, saying his parents were very strict, but in reality, they were very kind and also liked Yue. On October 10, 2010, Wei went to Yue's house. Before going, Yue said to Wei, "The day you come to my house is such a good day." Wei playfully replied, "Don't you know who I am!" On October 22, 2010, Yue's lunar birthday, Wei said to Yue, "This year is the last time you celebrate your birthday with your friends, next year every birthday will be just you and me!" Wei gave Yue an unforgettable birthday. The song "The Moon Represents My Heart" Yue would never forget, and the evening's text message: "I will love you well for a lifetime" — Wei, do you still remember? Yue feels now, was all this because it's her birth year, so it ended up like this? If all this hadn't happened during her birth year, would the outcome have been different? Below is Yue and Wei's 2011...

January 1, 2011 Diary: Today is the first day of 2011, also known as New Year's Day, but I didn't feel any festive atmosphere at all, except for the New Year greetings from friends (and I didn't reply to any of them). Let's start with last night. Last night was the bank's "Year-End Settlement Day," so everyone had to be at the branch. Even though I was off, I still had to go. If it were anywhere else, I'd be very happy, but I don't like the bank's style at all. Maybe this is society, I just don't understand, if I don't drink with you, it doesn't mean I don't "respect" you! What nonsense. Because some things happened at the branch, my big baby got really mad at me. I know he was doing it for my own good, seeing others hurt me, he felt pain too... As long as you love me, that's all that matters. Everything is a question.

Hehe, although I didn't say anything at the time, I was really thrilled inside. Finally, my dream was becoming a reality, finally I could live with the person I love, that must be a very happy thing. My big baby said that after the old house was renovated, his parents wanted to live there, fearing that it would be unfair to me and my big baby. Actually, it doesn't matter to me where I live, as long as the house has me and my big baby, that's enough. Uncle and auntie are already quite old, they should live comfortably... Ouch~ My hand cramped, it hurts~ I can't write anymore. Just one more day, then I get two days off. This job is weird, when others rest, I work, when others work, I rest. Big baby, have sweet dreams, goodnight! Love you~
