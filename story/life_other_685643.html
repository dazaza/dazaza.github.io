The Feeling of Falling in Love with a Dream

April fell in love with a dream, loving it without any restraint, losing the rationality and calmness she had maintained for many years. The dream also deeply loved April, wanting to be entangled with her at all times. If these two people were single men and women, such love would have been beyond reproach.

Unfortunately, they met each other after marriage. April had a husband, despite always having unpleasant entanglements, but their feelings were still acceptable. The dream also had his own family, and his daughter was already 20 years old. This relationship was destined to have more suspicion and pain than others' love.

April often couldn't see the dream who was working hard for his career like a workaholic, and every time she sent a text message on her mobile phone, she didn't get a reply from the dream. The depressed April would often think aimlessly, always worrying that the dream would leave her, abandon her, and do things that would betray her.

Finally, during the few days when the dream was on a business trip, April decided to find a way to solve her own problems, then go to the dream's place. Even if she could only be his lifelong confidante in a dream, without a formal title, it would be fine as long as they could be together every day, as long as they loved each other.

But at the same time, something was wrong with April's nerves, and she actually did something else that she couldn't even imagine. This was completely contrary to the good character and quality that April had always been praised for. Although she didn't think about anything after doing it, nor did she consider what the consequences would be.

That night, she suddenly wanted to go to the dream's office to take a look. April hadn't been there for several days, whereas before, she used to go there several times a day. She tiptoed out of her home and arrived at the dream's office as quickly as possible. Originally, she just wanted to relive the dream's presence and recall the happiness they once shared.

Unexpectedly, she found a message left by a woman on the computer on the desk, saying that she hoped to cooperate with the dream. April usually didn't inquire about the dream's private affairs or everything related to his work, but that day, she didn't know why, suddenly feeling hatred. Thinking that she hadn't heard from the dream for several days, he said he loved her but was so heartless, not even sending a text message, yet talking to others online about this and that. Although April understood that these were all work-related matters, she still felt unbalanced inside.

In anger, April replied to the message on behalf of the dream and informed the woman not to cooperate with him, warning her to be careful and not to fall for scams, without giving detailed reasons. Afterward, April left the dream's office without thinking twice, not considering how furious the dream would be when he found out. In her heart, April kept muttering to herself: "Dream, I'm sorry, I didn't want to do this, but now I can no longer tolerate any woman appearing beside you. I can't bear that you don't completely belong to me."

"Dream, no matter how you think about it, I am selfish because I love you. Whether you understand it or not, I just want you to know that I love you, I just want you to understand my love. I'm really scared, really scared of losing you, really scared! You won't understand! Since childhood, besides my father, you are the most important male in my heart!"

"I have already loved to the point where I can give up my life for you! Do you know my heart? Do you understand my love? I wish I could monopolize you, making you belong only to me. I know, I know, this kind of selfish and domineering love is inappropriate, but is there anything wrong with love? Why do I love you so much after I got married?"

"Why didn't we meet and know each other earlier? Why? Why does heaven torture me like this? Giving me so much hardship, and yet letting me suffer such a heavy blow in the path of love?"

April helplessly walked in the night, her face covered with tears that couldn't be wiped away, her mind full of the dream's shadow.

Suddenly, a speeding motorcycle came rushing towards April for some unknown reason. Unable to dodge in time, April was knocked down on the street. Fortunately, she only sustained minor injuries. After going to the hospital for a check-up, April insisted on not being hospitalized and returned home to recuperate, so she only took some medicine home.

The dream finally returned, and April knew that the dream would definitely ask about this matter because she deliberately left evidence that could be verified. She wanted to know the dream's attitude after knowing the truth, and she couldn't explain her own psychology clearly.

Maybe it was just a prank! If he couldn't understand her feelings, if he stopped loving her because of this, then April would resolutely leave, leaving the sorrow to herself, then get a divorce, and spend the rest of her life alone.

When the dream really asked April, she neither admitted nor denied it. This wasn't the straightforward style that April had always shown, but she couldn't care about that anymore. Anyway, the evidence was clear, anyone could tell it was something April did, and the dream wasn't stupid.

In an unpleasant conversation, April talked about her worries and fears of losing the dream, explaining why she did this. The dream also asked April if she wasn't afraid of ruining him by doing this. April chuckled secretly, continuously apologizing to the dream and promising not to do it again. In fact, April understood deep down that such trivial matters wouldn't ruin the dream.

The truly destructive things for the dream, even if beaten to death, April would absolutely never do them. April knew her own temperament; she was just a little girl who occasionally acted capriciously, using harmless small matters to release her capriciousness and dominance. Otherwise, growing up like this, she wouldn't know how many self-serving things she would have done!

Finally reconciled, the dream told April:

1. He still loved her;

2. He would be with her for a lifetime;

3. He would fulfill all the promises he made to her;

4. He would properly arrange for her children whom she worried about the most;

5. He would continue to help and support her in her career.

When April saw these assurances from him on the computer, her face revealed a smile that hadn't appeared for many days, and tears dropped uncontrollably onto the keyboard. With his words, knowing his heart towards her, even if she were to die tomorrow, it wouldn't matter.

April knew that no matter what the outcome was, whether they loved each other or not, in this life, she couldn't let go of this man who was the most important person in her life, comparable only to her father! But the dream, does he understand her thoughts? April was exhausted and didn't want to continue discussing similar topics.

She just wanted to cherish this pure true love for herself, hoping to be inseparable from the dream for a lifetime!
