On the eve of Valentine's Day, sweet love proclamations have quietly become popular.

by tianshiwake on 2007-08-16 22:13:40

Reporters learned recently that some Internet users are hurrying to register "love declarations" as a special gift for the Qixi Valentine's Day to give to their beloved ones. What kind of special gift is it? Curiosity led reporters to www.iloveyou.cn to find out, and unexpectedly discovered the story behind this website.

The founder of the website is Mr. Yang, who has been working in the Internet industry for many years. He told reporters that he had been with his girlfriend for many years, and every Valentine's Day gift was perfume, flowers, chocolates. Even he felt a bit monotonous, and his girlfriend didn't have the initial joy when receiving gifts, increasingly feeling like a routine matter. On the eve of the Qixi Valentine's Day, a friend found Mr. Yang and asked him if he could help make a Valentine's Day declaration website for his girlfriend. When Mr. Yang heard this news, he suddenly felt enlightened. He realized that many people need to confess and bless, but because of the lack of an expression platform, love was hidden in their hearts. So Mr. Yang decided to create a love declaration platform to provide space for those lovers to display their feelings. Coincidentally, Mr. Yang had purchased the domain name iloveyou.cn at a high price several years ago, which was just like being custom-made for a love declaration. When Mr. Yang gave the love declaration he made to his girlfriend, he was pleasantly surprised to see her radiant smile that he hadn't seen for a long time.

After reading here, I'm sure you must be curious about what functions the love declaration has. Let's listen to Mr. Yang explain: "Suppose your lover's name is Xiaoxin, then you can give her the domain name 'xiaoxin.iloveyou.cn' as a Valentine's Day gift. You can write down your heartfelt confession here, and you can also record your words that you want to say to her. When she opens this love declaration, listening to your sweet words, looking at your warm confession under the beautiful background, how would she feel?"

"The love declaration from the virtual world, especially 'name.iloveyou.cn', completely steals the thunder from many physical gifts. Compared with physical gifts, the love declaration named after the name or nickname has irreplaceable advantages over physical gifts: one is the exclusivity of resources, allowing the recipient to feel respected; the second is the diversity of functions, the declarer can express their affection for their loved ones through various ways such as text and recording. The third is that the recipient can obtain blessings through this love declaration, and the expression can be infinitely personalized, making it easy for the recipient to understand the intention of the giver. On the eve of Valentine's Day, there are many recommended declaration domains in the love declaration station as the best Valentine's Day gift, and the click-through rate remains high."

Subsequently, reporters looked at some love declarations registered by netizens, such as liugang.iloveyou.cn (meaning "Liu Gang, I love you"). Listening to the soothing background music, reading each sentence carefully written by the declarer, it really makes people feel a warm sense of emotion! While admiring the wonderful creativity of the Love Declaration Network, the reporter also wishes all lovers a happy union.
