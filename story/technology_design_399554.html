"Special Agent Group 5 - Part 2" Packaging Upgrade: Yu Zhen's womanizing leads to "Peach Blossom Tribulations"

by tadeyisheng on 2011-03-31 22:34:00

Yu Zhen plays Ma Yunfei

"Special Agent Group 5 Part II" Cast Photos

Entertainment Baidu News - "Special Agent Group 5 Part II", as the luxurious upgraded version of the first part, gathers the original cast while also focusing on a more rich and colorful story and even more powerful visual effects. "Special Agent Group 5 Part II" is currently being filmed at the Hengdian World Studios with Yu Zhen once again portraying the suave and womanizing "Prince of Changes" Ma Yunfei in the second installment. This time, his philandering gets him into no small amount of trouble.

Yu Zhen's Karmic Love Affair with a Japanese Female Spy

In the first "Special Agent Group 5", Ma Yunfei, played by Yu Zhen, got himself into quite a bit of trouble due to his promiscuous nature. He engaged in a life-and-death struggle with a beautiful but venomous Japanese female spy, often underestimating her out of chivalry, which even led to the entire Special Agent Group 5 falling into crisis. In "Special Agent Group 5 Part II", another beautiful Japanese female spy appears. This spy, named Sakai Asaka, is the sister of the Sakai from the first part, and she has an instant grudge against Ma Yunfei from the very beginning of the story. Fate's twists are truly helpless; they meet before engaging in combat, what seems like a beautiful encounter between a handsome gentleman and a pretty woman. However, neither knows that the other will become their greatest enemy later on. Before their true identities are revealed, Sakai Asaka falls in love with the suave and gentlemanly Ma Yunfei during their interactions, and Ma Yunfei also feels a bit of pity for the frail woman in front of him. This later becomes the fuse for conflicts between him and Gao Han, even causing Ma Yunfei to be misled by Sakai Asaka, losing many battle opportunities and dragging the entire group into prison. The suave and knowledgeable Ma Yunfei always finds himself troubled when it comes to beautiful women.

Yu Zhen and Wang Likun's Troubled Romantic Rivals

Yu Zhen and Wang Likun continue to play the romantic rivals Ma Yunfei and Gao Han in "Special Agent Group 5 Part II". These two former lovers reignite their old flame within the Special Agent Group 5, and their shared life and missions make them closer than ever. However, difficulties never cease to disturb this couple, princess prosecutor. Besides Ma Yunfei's innate womanizing and overly gentlemanly attitude making Gao Han extremely jealous, these conflicts are also often exploited by enemies to divide the internal unity of the Special Agent Group 5, even leading to internal strife within the group. He Jian, a member of the group who has always been both friend and rival to Ma Yunfei, also falls in love with Gao Han. This rival, who is more considerate and attentive than Ma Yunfei, poses a significant threat to him. After enduring so much hardship and still not receiving understanding or support from her beloved, Gao Han begins to doubt internally, and the relationship between the two suffers unprecedented damage. Whether the two can ultimately end up together still awaits the final outcome of the story. (Text/ Zi)
