Liu Shishi

Marter Tai Ruoxi (played by Liu Shishi)

A modern career woman, full of contemporary sensibilities, independent and self-reliant, with thoughts and vision, good at observing people’s expressions, smart and perceptive, able to weigh pros and cons, carrying a touch of modern reality, she is a white-collar beauty. After working in the office for three years, she gets into an argument with her boyfriend which leads to their breakup during which she encounters an accident and time travels to the Kangxi era of the Qing dynasty. Initially, she behaves rather rashly and courageously in ancient times but relies on her rich modern knowledge, sensitive and detailed observation and consideration, as well as her understanding of history, which helps her turn danger into safety many times. She uses her young girl identity to protect herself with her lively and spirited demeanor. She is cautious in thought, steady and meticulous in dealing with matters. In the Eight Prince’s mansion and after entering the palace, she becomes more thorough and calm in handling affairs, not easily achieving success, nor easily deceived by surface appearances. She is a rational yet emotional woman who values friendship and upholds righteousness. When her friends are in trouble, she will risk her life to protect them.

The Eighth Prince, with his clear and graceful demeanor and adept at handling all situations, made Ruoxi admire him. Added to this, she learns that he has a deep affection for her sister, who, however, has her heart set on someone else and shares the same bed but different dreams, making Ruoxi develop love out of pity. The silent protection of the Eighth Prince gives Ruoxi support in her loneliness, and her heart is finally moved. Ruoxi wholeheartedly accepts the Eighth Prince and even decisively asks him to give up competing for the throne, but unfortunately, the Eighth Prince ultimately chooses the empire, deeply shocking Ruoxi.

The Four Prince's complete sincerity without any suspicion makes her believe in him without hesitation. When the Four Prince instinctively risks his life to save her, Ruoxi decides to follow him for life. Upon learning that several princes' tragic endings were due to herself, Ruoxi's emotions collapse, and she leaves the palace dispirited... After experiencing several rounds of love and hate, Ruoxi is physically and mentally exhausted and can no longer hold on. Before her death, she seems to hear a song, drawing her into a dream...

Wu Qilong

Aisin Gioro Yinzhen (played by Wu Qilong)

Calm and reserved, with hidden depths. Prefers quietness, enjoys solitude, doesn't easily trust or accept people, but once accepted, they will be forever cherished in his heart, whether it be his brothers or his lover. He possesses the detachment and realism of an emperor, with a desire for the state and power. He isn't perfect towards his brothers and lovers; Ruoxi and the Thirteenth Prince are his weaknesses. Their passing took away all his vulnerabilities, making him achieve greatness as a famous emperor.

Zheng Jiaxiong

Aisin Gioro Yinreng (played by Zheng Jiaxiong)

A prince as gentle and refined as the wind and moonlight. Due to his mother's low status, he developed a versatile and adaptable approach to handling affairs. His romantic passion and sentimentality for love cannot compete with his desire for power. He wanted power but failed to achieve it; he wanted Ruolan but gained lifelong resentment instead; he wanted Ruoxi but lost her after obtaining her; when he realized where his true feelings lay, it was already too late forever. Decades passed, like flowers in a mirror and moon in water...

Yuan Hong

Aisin Gioro Yunxiang (played by Yuan Hong)

The Thirteenth Prince, skilled in poetry and calligraphy, all delicate and fresh, proficient in archery, always hitting the mark, galloping swiftly. The most talented prince, yet without any ambition for the throne, even yearning for the freedom of the sword-wielding world. His brotherly bond with the Fourth Prince destined him to be unable to realize his dreams and could only be bound to the court. He had deep affections throughout his life, but emotions were just embellishments; the most important thing was standing together with life and death with friends and brothers.

Lin Gengxin

Aisin Gioro Yunzhen (played by Lin Gengxin)

Young and carefree, arrogant, mature and gentle in adulthood, daring to love and hate. He has brotherhood among his siblings, willing to sacrifice his life to protect the Eighth Prince in his youth, but as he grows older, he gradually develops his own desires for power and what he wants to protect, having his own selfishness. Among all, he is the only one who dares to defy repeatedly for Ruoxi, risking his life to protect her.

Han Dong

Aisin Gioro Yunqi (played by Han Dong)

The Ninth Son of Emperor Kangxi, supporting the Eighth Prince Yunzhen and the Fourteenth Prince Yunzhen to achieve their ambitions. Among all the princes, he is known for his wealth. With him, there is ample funding for activities. In the later years of Emperor Kangxi, Yunqi was at a disadvantage, often scolded and stripped of his title by Emperor Kangxi. Yunqi carried poison with him to prove his loyalty to Yunzhen. After the Fourth Prince ascended the throne, Yunqi began to suffer misfortune, and even the maid Yutan placed in the palace was steamed to death.

Ye Zuxin

Aisin Gioro Yunru (played by Ye Zuxin)

Compared to other princes, the Tenth Prince lacks extraordinary talent and neither military nor political strategy, but he sincerely cherishes his lovers and friends, guarding his brothers with simplicity and purity. His sincere feelings are the purest among all the princes. The Tenth Prince is the only one who has no benefit calculation for Ruoxi and protects her wholeheartedly.

Liu Songren

Emperor Kangxi (played by Liu Songren)

Severe, wise, psychologically cautious, and mentally calm, a wise monarch for the country and its people. Upon first meeting Ruoxi, he was deeply impressed by her intelligence and eloquence. Later, seeing Ruoxi's serious research on her tea-serving duties, he admired her greatly. Throughout his life, he commanded politics decisively, but hesitated in the matter of succession, leading to decades of intrigue among the princes for the crown prince position, cultivating the historically famous "Nine Sons' Struggle for Succession."

Liu Xinyou

Marter Tai Ruolan (played by Liu Xinyou)

Graceful and noble, serene and gentle, generous and proper, understanding and reasonable. She also had bright moments in her youth, being with her childhood sweetheart. She was once radiant and even attracted the devoted Eighth Prince. For the sake of her family, she gave up her lover and married the Eighth Prince. After her lover's death, she became emotionally numb and no longer involved in worldly affairs. Apart from her younger sister Ruoxi, she had no connection with anyone else. When Ruoxi brought her a divorce decree, what would be the greatest shame for ordinary women became her greatest happiness. Thus, she severed all ties with the Aisin Gioro clan and attained her happiness after her death.
