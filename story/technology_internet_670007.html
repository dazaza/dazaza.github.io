Secondly, try to merge similar single items as much as possible. Many people like to lay out goods, turning slightly different goods into separate items for sale, even some repeat laying out goods, thinking the more items, the more chances of display! But they don't realize this greatly disperses the popularity of items, with each item only getting a pitiful amount of attention. How can your items appear on the first few pages of search results? Even if you pay to promote, you can't promote all of them, right? You'll end up promoting the head but neglecting the tail.

You should try to merge similar and alike items together for many benefits. First, fewer items are easier to manage, and naturally, more effort can be spent on each item since our energy is limited; second, after merging, all the popularity is concentrated on one item, which helps increase the item's popularity and pushes its natural ranking closer to the front; third, reduces the average promotion cost per single item, for every 1 yuan you invest to let consumers see your items and merged items, it's equivalent to letting consumers see all your similar items; fourth, accumulates sales on one item, making it easier to create a blockbuster!
