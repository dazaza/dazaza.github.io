My Kung Fu Girlfriend

by m827223143 on 2009-08-27 22:41:08

In the severe winter, heavy snow covered the entire Great Snow Mountain. The howling wind and snow made people hesitate to step forward. Yet, amidst the storm, a solitary figure was seen trudging along. This person was none other than our protagonist, Pu Shaojie. Shaojie carried a glimmer of hope as he searched tirelessly on the snowy mountain for his one true love. Unfortunately, fate played its tricks, and before Shaojie could find his beloved's trail, the snow mountain suddenly collapsed, trapping Shaojie in the storm with his life hanging by a thread. Just as Shaojie felt despair creeping in, he looked up and seemed to notice something, a smile spreading across his face.

The story begins three years ago. Pu Xi, the founder of Pu Enterprises, was a newly rich man in the city who made his fortune through toothpaste production. Starting from a private small enterprise, Pu Enterprises eventually became a large listed company. Despite being a millionaire, Pu Xi remained miserly and money-oriented, a trait that never changed. His wife, Dujuan, also had an arrogant demeanor befitting a nouveau riche, which many found distasteful. Shaojie was Pu Xi's only son, self-proclaimed as the second generation of wealthy elites, who grew up being pampered. Although Shaojie was inherently kind and intelligent, he was an extravagant young master who squandered his wealth. Pu Xi always wanted to pass on his business to Shaojie, but Shaojie was only interested in pursuing his ideal romance, much to Pu Xi's frustration. To escape his father's control, Shaojie went to Hokkaido, Japan, for skiing and vacation, where he met his nemesis, Yao Kaixin.

Kaixin lost both her parents at a young age and lived with her uncle, Yao Jun. On the surface, Jun appeared to be a clumsy man, but in reality, he was a hidden martial arts expert. Under his tutelage, Kaixin also mastered excellent martial arts skills. Like any other young girl, Kaixin dreamed of finding her perfect prince charming. To avoid scaring off potential suitors with her martial prowess, Kaixin never revealed her skills easily. During Shaojie's vacation in Hokkaido, coincidentally Jun brought Kaixin to practice martial arts on the same snowy mountain. An avalanche accident put Kaixin in peril, but fortunately, Shaojie happened to pass by and extended a helping hand, rescuing Kaixin from danger. Although they couldn't see each other's faces clearly, Kaixin's heart was already captivated by Shaojie. After Shaojie left behind a ski glove and a few words, "We shall meet again if destiny allows," Kaixin fainted. Upon waking up, Kaixin realized Shaojie had disappeared, but her heart was already tied to this mysterious man. Carrying the ski glove, Kaixin immediately followed Shaojie to China, determined to find her true love with just this single glove.

Back in China, Shaojie accidentally gave the remaining glove to a sycophant, Zhang Lexing. Kaixin mistakenly thought Lexing was her hero and thus altered the destinies of Shaojie and Kaixin. Discovering that Lexing worked at Guo Family Martial Arts School, Kaixin decided to work there as a helper to get closer. Once a renowned martial arts school, it now faced decline under the leadership of Guo Dingtian, who considered himself a grandmaster but was actually cowardly and hypocritical. His only disciple, Zhang Lexing, was ambitious but neglected his martial arts training. Guo Dingtian's younger sister, Guo Yajiao, transformed from a martial artist into a fashion enthusiast, often dressed up and flirted with different admirers. With the school's decline, Dingtian hoped to quickly make money to revive its glory and promote their family's healing oil, but he lacked business acumen. Accumulating debts, Lexing and others gradually lost their innocence, becoming profit-driven.

Dingtian was once a good friend of Pu Xi, but they fell out over financial disputes. However, Lexing still relied on his master's relationship with Pu Xi to flatter Shaojie, hoping to gain some benefits. Initially confused by the peculiar behavior of Lexing and others, Kaixin later recognized their kindness and became friends. One day, when Dingtian and Lexing were in trouble due to debt collectors, Kaixin stepped in to help, revealing her martial skills. Realizing Kaixin's abilities, Lexing tricked her into becoming the licensed owner of the Guo Family Martial Arts School, taking on all their debts. Using Kaixin's skills, Lexing concocted various get-rich-quick schemes, causing endless laughter. As the new licensed head, Kaixin's first challenge was dealing with their biggest debtor, Pu Xi and Shaojie, who forced them to perform martial arts at an upcoming toothpaste exhibition to entertain guests. Despite feeling insulted, Kaixin reluctantly agreed. At the exhibition, Kaixin unintentionally offended Shaojie, who retaliated by embarrassing her publicly. Unable to bear the humiliation, Kaixin angrily left, turning her and Shaojie into a pair of quarrelsome rivals.

Pu Xi received a threatening letter stating that Shaojie would become the target of kidnappers. To ensure his son's safety, Pu Xi used the debt to force Kaixin to act as Shaojie's personal bodyguard. Kaixin demanded Shaojie temporarily move to the martial arts school for round-the-clock protection, which Shaojie found extremely unpleasant. However, a beautiful woman named Tong Jingjing opened a dance school opposite the martial arts school, captivating everyone, including Shaojie. To get closer to Jingjing, Shaojie moved to the martial arts school with his cousin Du Runnan. Maintaining his lifestyle, Shaojie caused chaos by tearing down walls and moving his bed and bathtub, creating numerous jokes and confusion among Kaixin and Lexing.

One day, Shaojie was tricked by Kaixin and Lexing and left alone, allowing the kidnappers to seize him. In a dangerous situation, Kaixin bravely infiltrated the kidnappers' lair and saved Shaojie using her martial arts skills. Grateful for her bravery, Shaojie's relationship with Kaixin improved. After the kidnapping incident was resolved, Shaojie and Runnan planned to leave the martial arts school. It was then that Kaixin realized the man she sought in the snowy mountains was none other than the person she hated most—Shaojie. Disappointed, Kaixin's dreams of love shattered instantly. Meanwhile, Runnan began courting Kaixin, and they soon formed a couple. On the other hand, Shaojie won Jingjing's affection, and the two pairs found their respective loves.

However, Runnan harbored sinister intentions, plotting to seize the Pu family fortune. When Pu Xi decided to launch a massive advertising campaign for a new hair care series, he entrusted the entire plan to Shaojie. Shaojie assigned the task to Runnan, trusting him fully. Seizing the opportunity, Runnan altered contracts secretly and manipulated events during the promotional event, humiliating Kaixin and accusing Pu Enterprises of copyright infringement. Media coverage forced Pu Enterprises to halt the hair care series and face enormous compensation claims, plunging the company into its greatest financial crisis. Runnan took advantage of the situation, using financial tactics to take over Pu Enterprises completely. Realizing Runnan's true nature, Shaojie and Kaixin were too late to prevent the disaster. Runnan further demoralized Shaojie by stealing Jingjing away from him, leaving the Pu family destitute.

Fortunately, Guo Dingtian offered shelter in the martial arts school, giving Pu Xi a sense of warmth. But Shaojie, betrayed by those he trusted most, suffered a devastating blow, falling into despair and living like a walking corpse. Although Kaixin's romantic path was also severely impacted, seeing Shaojie's deteriorating state pained her deeply. She supported and encouraged him, gradually developing deep feelings for him. However, Shaojie, consumed by his misery, ignored Kaixin's affections, sinking further into despair. Heartbroken yet powerless to help, Kaixin secretly participated in a women's kickboxing tournament to raise money for Shaojie's mother, Dujuan, who fell ill from worry. Despite injuries, Kaixin triumphed, earning enough prize money to save Dujuan. Grateful, Shaojie finally understood Kaixin's kindness towards him. Realizing the importance of having a true friend, Shaojie underwent a profound awakening, vowing not to let down his family and Kaixin's expectations. He faced reality, accepted Kaixin's rigorous training, and humbly learned business skills from his father, determined to reclaim the family fortune, Jingjing, and his dignity.

Meanwhile, Lexing, feeling neglected by Jingjing and Kaixin, broke ties with everyone and joined Runnan, serving as his loyal dog. Distraught, Dingtian expelled Lexing from the school. Unexpectedly, a building collapse outside the school injured passersby, and Shaojie and Kaixin used Guo Family Healing Oil to treat them. The media reported on its effectiveness, making it famous overnight. Seizing the opportunity, Shaojie decided to use the healing oil as a comeback capital to rebuild Pu Enterprises. Seeking investments from old partners, Shaojie produced the healing oil. Sensing the threat, Runnan sent Lexing back to the school under false pretenses to steal the formula and release a competing product. Unbeknownst to others, Runnan's plan was thwarted when Lexing, inspired by Yajiao, turned against Runnan, exposing his crimes. Runnan was punished accordingly, and the healing oil was successfully launched, achieving unprecedented success. With Shaojie's efforts, Pu Enterprises became a listed company again, and the Guo Family Martial Arts School regained its reputation, attracting numerous students. With Shaojie's assistance, Dingtian fulfilled his lifelong dream of revitalizing the school and promoting traditional martial arts ethics.

Just as everything seemed perfect, Shaojie and Kaixin's relationship faced another upheaval. Deeply attached to Jingjing, Shaojie discovered her lingering feelings for him. Feeling replaced by Kaixin, Jingjing avoided Shaojie, leaving him torn between duty and affection. During this time, Kaixin's uncle, Yao Jun, arrived from Hokkaido, revealing a past feud involving Kaixin and Jingjing. Jun was once the second elder brother of Jingjing's mother, Fan Chuyu. A dispute over Chuyu's affections led to a duel between Jun and the eldest brother, Yu Lei. Defeated, Jun retreated into seclusion, while Yu Lei, despite winning, failed to win Chuyu's heart. Eventually, Chuyu married Jingjing's father, but Yu Lei remained devoted, teaching Jingjing martial arts. Eighteen years later, history repeated itself as Jingjing challenged Kaixin to settle their ancestral grudge. They privately agreed that the loser would quietly step aside from Shaojie's life.

On the day of the duel, despite Shaojie's attempts to stop them, Kaixin and Jingjing fought fiercely. Ultimately, Kaixin was defeated and returned to Hokkaido with Jun, silently blessing Shaojie and Jingjing's happiness. As the city anticipated the grand wedding of Pu Shaojie and Tong Jingjing, moments before entering the church, Shaojie realized his true love was Kaixin. He courageously canceled the wedding, earning Jingjing's understanding and encouragement to find Kaixin. Smiling for the first time in a long while, Shaojie rushed to the airport and disappeared into the crowd.

Upon reaching Hokkaido, Shaojie couldn't find Kaixin's whereabouts, only hearing occasional sightings on the snowy mountain. Braving the severe winter storm, Shaojie trudged on, driven by hope to find his one true love. Suddenly, as the mountain collapsed and Shaojie faced despair, a hand reached out to him. Looking up, he saw Kaixin. They smiled at each other, understanding everything without words.
