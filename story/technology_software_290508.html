Maruta received the Louis Vuitton replica proposal in October. We met in the Blue Room of the club, looked each other in the eyes, and immediately understood each other. The Consortium was born, 50% each, and we started. Giovanni Maspero, an entrepreneur who realized his American dream on Lake Como, brought the Joe Fly team as dowry, with helmsman Checco Bruni and tactician Tommaso Chieffi moderating the debate, nearly half an hour before it began. The one who made the introductions was PDL deputy Sever Voinescu, spokesperson for presidential candidate Traian Basescu. Motors still Louis Vuitton replica back. The name Azzurra was coined by Luca Cordero di Montezemolo, Bonadeo recalls. Today's sailors are much more competent, better prepared than we were. We were pioneers. None of the '83 team had ever done a match-race before then, nor would they have had the chance to do so without Azzurra. We created a movement, both on water and on land. And I would really like that today it would be carried forward," says Pelaschier. Full speed ahead, now. Azzurra will almost certainly participate in the upcoming events of the Louis Vuitton Trophy (the WSTA federation has been founded, with Paul Cayard as president): Auckland, Maddalena, San Francisco or New York (rather than Newport), Dubai, Hong Kong. Resources are needed (it is said that 800,000 euros are required for the French leg). We will contact the partners of the Consortium from back then, anticipates Bonadeo. And then there's the America's Cup in the team's DNA. Provided that the America's Cup recovers the spirit of old times, a reasonable formula, reduced budgets - the commodore sets the markers. Aga Khan wouldn't participate in a crazy money challenge. Indeed, the prince. It's up to him to have the last word. He says: "Attorney Agnelli would be proud that Azzurra continues to be a great name in Italian sports." And the Volvo Louis Vuitton Ocean Race chapter is closed? No, even though logic would suggest Azzurra should be in the America's Cup. Besides, the date of the start of the round-the-world race is very close, Bonadeo asserts, clearing the field of any presumed friction towards the project for the VOR launched by Giovanni Soldini, John Elkann, and the Italian Yacht Club. But not at all. Their idea to create a movement for ocean sailing in Italy is fantastic. We will support them. At the helm of the time machine now stands a figure bare-chested, in jeans, a red scarf and socks. No tacking, please. Also Riccardo Bonadeo Cotroceni entered the final stretch. The PNL presidential candidate, Crin Antonescu, declared during a meeting with professors in Piatra Neamt, that for Traian Basescu, a librarian is "a little mouse" who doesn't have enough money to buy himself a jacket for the first time. He was the CEO of the Consortium of 17 companies that had financed the tricolor debut in the Cup. Today he is the commodore of the same sailing salon in Porto Cervo. I feel very close to the team. Now, on these bases, we move forward to write another beautiful page in sports history, and radiant is the prince, aboard the yacht from which he followed the match-races together with his daughter, Princess Zahra.
