If you love, please focus.

by mimi123 on 2011-12-22 15:45:50

If there is a beautiful girl in today's society who is willing to start from scratch with you, build a life bit by bit, endure hardships and strive together, then this girl must have grown up drinking milk powder, and it must be Sanlu milk powder!

—— Preface

When I read this sentence, I felt a chill. Suddenly, I realized that I am also in such an awkward situation. The girls around me are not as exaggerated as those described in some books, but the majority tend to side with their parents when it comes to marriage. Upon careful thought, it's not the girls' fault. What have they done wrong? Shouldn't they have a happy and fulfilling life? What have their parents done wrong? Isn't it true that every parent wishes for their daughter to live happily?

Whose fault is it then? Is it society's fault? With the same reform and opening-up, the same China, why have some become rich while others remain poor? Do you dare say it's society's fault?

Perhaps you complain about market competition, you complain about the bad environment, you complain about your lack of luck. You complain that the person you like loves someone else, you complain that the girls around you are too materialistic, you complain about not having a wealthy father, you complain about being unrecognized talent. You complain a lot, and your life is filled with dissatisfaction. You begin to indulge yourself. You used to be very introverted, blushing at the sight of a girl, very quiet. But you know what Lu Xun said: if you don't explode in silence, you will perish in silence. You decide to explode. But things don't go as planned; your dream remains just that—a distant, unattainable dream.

Worrying about market competition is because you lack the ability to compete; the environment being bad is because you lack the ability to change it; feeling unlucky is because you always focus on misfortune; others not liking you is because you lack the charm to make them do so; girls being materialistic is because you lack power and status; not being a second-generation rich person means you're about to become the father of one; unrecognized talent? Talent is like pregnancy—it only shows after a long time!

You shouldn't treat everyone with the same standard, especially not your beloved one. Of course, you shouldn't give her the same treatment you give others. Perhaps you don't care about what you say, but your words will affect how she perceives you.

If you want the secret to successful love, I can tell you. But you must promise not to apply it to two people at once. If you try to sit on two chairs at the same time, you'll fall through the gap.

If you love, please be devoted. Don't let her think you're a flirt, even if she is your greatest love.

If you love, please be devoted. Don't treat other members of the opposite sex as well as you treat her; she will feel unimportant in your heart.

If you love, please be devoted. Don't tell others the poems you write for her. If you must tell, explain who they are for.

If you love, please be devoted. When you find something interesting, tell her first.

If you love, please be devoted. Don't think there's someone better ahead; believe she is the angel in your life.

If you love, please be devoted. When love leaves, don't be sad, because you have no reason to make her stay.

If you love, please be devoted. Even if your abilities are lacking, your education low, your appearance unremarkable, your eloquence poor—don’t fear. Just be devoted, good to her alone. If she gives up, then she is the most foolish person in the world, because she will never find someone who loves her as foolishly as you do.

If you love, please be devoted. The most authoritative institute for love research recently found that 98% of men and women eventually marry the person who has consistently been kind and loving to them. So, you must be devoted.

Maybe you've always been single, maybe you've frequently broken up, maybe you've struggled to find true love. If you continue to follow your old ways after reading this article, then you should find a decent brand of tofu to collide with.

Wishing all single men and women find true love soon, and all couples in love never break up! Double harvest in love and career in the Year of the Rabbit!
