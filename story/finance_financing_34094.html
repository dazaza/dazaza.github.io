Coke Moonlight Tribe Rule: The more developed the economy of a city, the more people live paycheck to paycheck!

After more than a year of market research and data collection, Coke Online (www.coko365.com) summarized a rule based on members' detailed records of consumption and income. Coke Online calls this rule the "Coke Moonlight Tribe Rule." The general meaning of the Coke Moonlight Tribe Rule is that in cities with more developed economies, there are more people living paycheck to paycheck; whereas in cities with less developed economies, there are fewer people living paycheck to paycheck.

Coke Online, as a professional online bookkeeping and financial management software, provides powerful and professional online bookkeeping and financial management services to a wide range of internet users. After two years of development, it has become China's earliest, most influential, and most powerful online bookkeeping and financial management software. It regularly releases reliable and accurate reports on living expenses and financial management based on the consumption and income records of its members.

The Coke Moonlight Tribe Rule is an important economic development rule first released by Coke Online. In cities with more developed economies, consumption is higher, and there are more consumption options and temptations. The current generation born around the 1980s serves as the main consumer group. Their consumption philosophy is forward-thinking, and they especially enjoy using credit cards for advance consumption. The richness of consumption patterns, competitive mentality, and advanced consumption philosophy have led to an increasingly large moonlight tribe in cities. In contrast, in economically underdeveloped cities, due to fewer consumption patterns, lower consumption, and fewer temptations, as well as relatively backward consumption philosophies and the lack of popularity of credit card consumption, their living expenses are relatively low, and they have a certain amount of savings each month. Given China's current high-speed economic development, continuously rising prices, and stagnant income growth, more and more members of Coke Online are joining the moonlight tribe. In response to this situation, Coke Online kindly reminds everyone to establish scientific and reasonable consumption habits and bookkeeping and financial management habits.
