First Place: The Stool

Found in every household, easily accessible, it can be used for sitting while concealing its deadly potential. When the time comes, it can be grabbed and used immediately in a fight, no bullets or safeties to worry about. Its lethality can vary depending on the user's physique. The greatest advantage is its stealth - even if the authorities find out, they can't accuse you of illegal weapon possession. With technological advancements, stools are no longer made solely from wood; steel and plastic varieties now exist, but their usage remains largely the same.

Technical Data: Length varies between 30-150 cm, Width: 20-50 cm, Height: 10-50 cm.

Attack Radius: Actual length of the stool + the user's arm length.
