Starting from February 15th, Season 2 of the long-running drama "Unforgettable" will air two episodes daily at 17:00 on CCTV Channel 8. According to Wang Guohui, director of CCTV's Film and Television Department, "Unforgettable" started airing on September 2nd last year with 168 episodes, reaching a peak viewership rate of 2.23%. Therefore, CCTV has introduced another 160 episodes, scheduled to air during the Spring Festival.

Wang Guohui stated that previously, CCTV had never introduced such a long drama like "Unforgettable", especially not expecting a show starting at 5 PM to reach a viewership rate of 2.23%, higher than some prime-time dramas. Thus, after the first season ended, another 160 episodes were introduced, making "Unforgettable" already aired for 328 episodes. The broadcast time for the remaining episodes will be decided based on the viewing rates, and there is a high possibility of continuing the broadcast. If the entire series is introduced, the 526-episode "Unforgettable" will surpass the Korean drama "Mermaid Lady" to become the longest series on CCTV.
