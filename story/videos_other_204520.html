Sauna Weekly download, Sauna Weekly e-version, Sauna Weekly website, Sauna Weekly events, online viewing address for the electronic version of Sauna Weekly in Dongguan.

An e-version of *Sauna Weekly* launched by a sauna-related website left netizens astounded (see detailed report on page 08 of our Dongguan edition from yesterday). It was reported yesterday that the weekly had been shut down by the morning. After the *Sauna Weekly* report was released, dozens of websites and forums quickly reprinted it. Netizens reacted with humor, commenting that "this is just too bold." One netizen remarked, "The writing in the weekly is elegant, and the selection of articles reflects a broad-mindedness and moral integrity that could be considered an industry benchmark!" However, many netizens expressed concern about the spread of pornographic information online and its potential negative impact on society. By yesterday morning, when reporters clicked on the link to *Sauna Weekly*, it showed "page not found." In the afternoon, reporters contacted Mr. Li, the editor of *Sauna Weekly*, posing as a manager from a hotel to discuss advertising business. He sighed and said, "This website has been closed by us." Regarding the reason for the closure, Mr. Li asked in return, "Don't you know if you're in Dongguan? Basically, everyone in the sauna industry knows about it. We'll talk again once it's back up." Some netizens stated they had anticipated the shutdown of the weekly and had saved some of its standout articles. For instance, one sauna experience report began like this: "Late spring in April, with orioles singing and grass flying. Whether you're a wanderer or a rogue doesn't matter. Fellow wolfish friends, let's put away your swords, buy a bowl of wine, and listen to me tell you about a major event soon to occur in the martial arts world."
