He even worries that saying nothing can become news.

Abbot Shi Yongxin of Shaolin Temple, it is not an exaggeration to say he is the most glamorous and well-known abbot in contemporary China. (Click on the image to enter Shi Yongxin's Weibo)

Every move of his becomes a social focus, and he even worries that saying nothing can become news.

In China, the abbot enjoying such treatment is Shi Yongxin.

At 3 PM on August 26th, during the event of Shaolin Ask Chan and Chan Debate, Abbot Shi Yongxin interacted with netizens through the official Shaolin Temple Weibo account. This was his first face-to-face interaction with Weibo users.

Is it difficult to get Shi Yongxin to speak?

News Director Li Dan answered this question with two unexpected responses: Unexpectedly, after receiving our invitation, within just one day, Abbot Shi Yongxin agreed to participate in the micro-interview. Even more unexpectedly, in the face of sharp questions from some netizens, the abbot remained so composed.

Last Tuesday, Li Dan and her team received an invitation from Shaolin Temple, hoping they would participate in the Shaolin Ask Chan and Chan Debate event held on August 26th.

Li Dan said at the time, whether it be Shaolin Temple or Abbot Shi Yongxin, their fame locally and nationwide is particularly high, very worthy of doing a micro-interview activity. Li Dan also mentioned that they had concerns because some netizens' evaluations of Shi Yongxin were somewhat extreme, fearing the abbot might refuse. Some people outright stated that interviewing Shi Yongxin would absolutely be impossible.

With a try-it-out mindset, Li Dan and her colleagues contacted Shaolin Temple and made this request. At the time, the staff of Shaolin Temple also expressed that the possibility was slim.

Unexpectedly, within just one day, Shi Yongxin replied. The staff conveyed this: The abbot felt this would be a new way to promote Buddhism.

It really exceeded our expectations, we didn't expect it to go so smoothly.

Although Shi Yongxin agreed to the interview request, Li Dan said that over the next few days, she was still uneasy.

We've already released the preview, if he suddenly decided not to do it unilaterally, we would be in a very awkward position. Li Dan said, as it turned out, everything went smoothly.

Li Dan said, what moved her most during the micro-interview process was Shi Yongxin's transcendence and calmness.

Facing some special questions, the abbot paid no attention at all. Li Dan said, for several questions raised by netizens, Shi Yongxin's answers were simple yet full of Zen meaning.
