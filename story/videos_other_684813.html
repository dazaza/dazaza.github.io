The Oath of Youth

by songsdp82pm on 2012-03-01 10:46:47

The Oath of Youth, Career, you are like this vast autumn wind, you blow the full life particles to maturity, and beat the withered yellow leaves in the heart on the road of life, and not all the branches can grow green leaves again.- I always remember that I am a son of a farmer. There are too many memories for me in this barren land. Ancestors all live here. My father said: "The land will not abandon us." Yes, we will create our happiness with labor sweat. Without saying, these are all emotional concerns. Why do my eyes often contain tears? Because I love this land deeply. I like to quietly stay on the hillside where no one is. In the past, it was intimate and friendly to me, but in the noisy school, I know I will be so lonely because there is no concern and no family there.- Fate can make people or joke about people, life can torture you and cultivate you, and reality can also crush a strong person. And I cannot find my direction in this surging new era, just like an ignorant and curious child, I want to find a place where no one is and cry out loud, but I am a boy.- My heart is towards Buddha, and Buddha's heart is towards devil. I don't want to say anything about others. Those who don't understand you will think it's your fault. The mistake is that you say too much. Sit quietly and think about your own mistakes, and don't talk about others' differences. I won't fall down like this. I will fight, I will try my best. Maybe when I re-emerge, I will get more respect because even though I submit to the strong, the brave who cannot be beaten are more convincing and influential. Treat ordinary things with a calm mind, strive for what you should get, and give up what you should abandon. Maybe what I pursue all my life may never be obtained, but the things I don't want or even dare not hope for may come unexpectedly. Some people say plans can't keep up with changes, but I will do my best to achieve my dreams. Even if I fail, I will be happy because I have made efforts to strive.- I want to treat every day of my life as the last day of my life. I would rather laugh and say failure than cry and say give up. This way, I will work harder to do what I want to do. I want my life to repay my family because they really did a lot for me. I would rather live in hell to let my family live in heaven.
