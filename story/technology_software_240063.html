II. Some Wubi users express...

The above content began to be reprinted in small circles, and some users who like gossip and use Wubi started to discuss (including me, who likes to join the crowd but doesn't comment). Opinions can be divided into the following three categories:

1. Agree: Jidian Wubi was developed by Old Du alone, inevitably limited by physiological and psychological factors. If Tencent's NB team could get involved, Jidian Wubi would surely develop at a moon-like pace. The most demanded feature is still the "vocabulary synchronization" function, plus the flashy skins favored by young females, which would basically meet the standard features of an input method.

PS: In fact, Jidian Wubi already has the "vocabulary synchronization" function, just in the form of a third-party plug-in. Users accustomed to the automatic update vocabulary window of Sogou Input Method might be surprised by such a manual and possibly unsuccessful synchronization method; but, this surprise is understandable - in an era where even fingernail covers can store several high-definition X pictures, how many people have experienced using a 3.5" floppy disk and needing to check if the write protection is removed?

2. Oppose: Some users are concerned that if, in the future, you can set 10 candidate options only if you type more than 10,000 words a day, otherwise you can only use 9, or consume one QB per month to instantly set it to 20... Such restrictions are just examples, but one thing is certain - anything related to QQ numbers is something that can make you spend RMB.

3. Neutral: There are so many Wubi input methods in the world, why bother to stick to one tree? If necessary, we can go back to Wangma 86.
