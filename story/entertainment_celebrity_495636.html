Full coverage of Zhang Jie and Xie Na's wedding banquet debunks the 30 million yuan rumor

by mlwang on 2011-09-27 10:42:41

On September 26, Zhang Jie and "Sister Po" Xie Na held their wedding in Shangri-La, the place closest to paradise. The entire wedding scene was romantic and warm, making "Fourth Brother" Wu Qilong exclaim, "This is the most beautiful wedding I've ever seen!" The event was also star-studded, with many big-name figures from the entertainment industry appearing to offer their blessings. During the thank-you banquet, the couple addressed rumors circulating online about a sponsorship fee of over 30 million yuan.

At 7:00 PM that evening, the happy couple hosted a thank-you banquet locally, sharing their joy and happiness with family and friends. At the banquet, they were not only asked about the location of their first kiss but were also requested to kiss on the spot. Regarding the recent rumors that their wedding was sponsored to the tune of 30 million yuan, the couple clarified the matter at the banquet.

Over the past four years, Zhang Jie and Xie Na have faced the ups and downs of life together, and their determination has moved many people. Today, they finally tied the knot in Shangri-La, a place symbolizing dreams and hope. That evening, Zhang Jie and Xie Na, now officially husband and wife, specially hosted an intimate banquet to thank their loved ones. The "Happy Camp" production team, as close friends of the couple, fully planned and prepared this banquet.

At the banquet, the hosts, the "Happy Family," asked on the spot about the time and place of the couple's first kiss, prompting the shy duo to sweetly reminisce about the past. A carefully crafted montage of sweet moments between the two on "Happy Camp" moved the newlyweds to tears. After watching the video, the couple played the apple-biting game amidst cheers from the crowd, creating a lively atmosphere. To thank the guests for their support, Zhang Jie and Xie Na performed a spontaneously arranged song and dance — "The Angel in the Clouds." Their seamless cooperation left the audience in awe.

Regarding the recent rumors of a 30 million yuan sponsorship, the couple clarified the matter on-site. They stated that there are too many people they need to thank for making this perfect wedding possible, as many friends offered them help. If someone believes the rumor of a 30 million yuan sponsorship is true, they probably don't know what the word "friendship" means.

Besides the sponsorship fee rumors spreading wildly online, a so-called "astronomical" guest list was also circulated by netizens. On this list, many stars gave red envelopes worth over 200,000 yuan, with Zhao Benshan reportedly giving a 6 million yuan red envelope, and "Fourth Brother" Wu Qilong giving a 3 million yuan red envelope. The rumor of these "astronomical red envelopes" caused much dissatisfaction, with netizens criticizing the growing gap between the rich and the poor.

High-profile celebrity weddings are no longer rare. The口水战 (verbal spat) triggered by the wedding of Big S and Wang Xiaofei in March still hasn't subsided. In comparison, Zhang Jie and Xie Na are quite "happy." As for the rumors of astronomical red envelopes, the editor feels that everyone needn't get angry or upset. The likelihood of someone trying to stir up controversy for attention seems greater than the credibility of the list!
