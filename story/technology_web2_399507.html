Stop reading those vague astrological interpretations in magazines. Try to simply understand the complexity and depth of astrology from the following steps, accurately interpreting the stars and constellations.

The true interpretation of a horoscope is to clearly insight into one's essence. It reflects oneself, perfects oneself, discovers oneself, astrological predictions, and guides all aspects back to a unified whole. Correctly using astrology can allow you to align with the forces of the universe, balancing personal qualities between success and arrival, and also gaining satisfaction and recognition. Therefore, the power of astrology should not be underestimated.

Astrology is actually your birth chart generated based on the time and place of your birth, showing the distribution of constellations and each planetary house. It's a quick sketch of cosmic energy. First, obtain your free birth chart, then start learning astrology according to the following steps.

Four elements for interpreting a birth chart:

1. Planets

2. Zodiac signs

3. Houses

4. Analysis

When analyzing your birth chart,

* Observe your planets, they represent different aspects of your personality. (How you self-promote, communicate, which areas you're lucky in, how you view your future development, etc.)

* Next, the zodiac adds flavor to these aspects of your character (Mercury in Taurus means your communication is pragmatic, you like repeating your statements, but are also troubled by the art of communication. This suggests that you may be good at giving practical advice to people, but unlikely to write novels. Mercury in Aries could mean you speak directly and impulsively, often leading to arguments.)

* Finally, the houses represent your performance in various areas of life. (Workplace, private life, interpersonal relationships, spiritual level, etc.) The number of planets indicates the intensity of your investment in this area of life and related ways of thinking. For example, Uranus, which represents forward-thinking, falling in the fourth house, which symbolizes family, means you have a modern family. Cancer, which represents caring, falling in the tenth house, which governs career, suggests you have a job that requires care and support, as well as work that needs to utilize intuition.

Practical application of astrology

Interpreting a birth chart is never an easy task; it requires practice to gradually feel the true nature hidden within the chart. The importance of astrology lies in its ability to help you understand why some people encounter setbacks or fail at work, or even why some are too lazy to find a job.

* For instance, if you are doing repetitive, monotonous work day after day in a regular position, but you have Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius (three fire signs) in your tenth house of career, it clearly means you will experience failure and hope to seek more stimulating things.

* If your chart lacks water signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces), then you are likely eager to express or output emotions. You can take simple measures, such as swimming once a week or engaging in water sports, injecting water into your life to balance the lack of water signs in your chart.

* People with moon signs that have water elements can easily find comfort through being immersed in water. (Taking baths or swimming)

* If your chart lacks air signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius), they might feel very uncomfortable under public scrutiny. Actively participating in such public activities can improve oneself and achieve balance. For example, joining amateur theater performances or public speaking courses.

I hope you can now see that integrating astrology into your life can make you more confident, thus achieving your dreams and moving towards success.
