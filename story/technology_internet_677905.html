Choose the right CMS system to improve the quality of the website.

by qingian007 on 2012-02-27 10:39:20

The purpose of a business building a website is not only to create a window connecting with the outside world in the internet realm, but more importantly, the website can bring more potential customers to the business and retain them. A website that can create value for the business is a good website. To build a good website, it is necessary to use a good CMS system. A good CMS system should not only be able to create a website that matches the company's image, being aesthetically pleasing and substantial, but also satisfy three principles of friendliness in terms of overall planning, functionality design, and program development:

Firstly, it must be user-friendly. As the online storefront of a business, the company website must first meet user needs and gain their trust. The enterprise website is one of the most important ways for internet users to contact the company and obtain company information. Therefore, enhancing the user experience on the company website, providing visitors with a peaceful and elegant page, and increasing visitor retention is very important. Hence, the structure of the CMS system used for website construction should be reasonable, and the template creation should be simple, maximizing the improvement of the website's user experience.

Secondly, it must be friendly to the internet environment. A website existing independently in the vast expanse of the internet is difficult for users to discover and visit. Websites created using a CMS system must have dynamic pages to facilitate search engine crawling and indexing, bringing more visitors to the site. Therefore, companies must choose a CMS system that generates pure dynamic pages to make it easier for search engines to index, accumulating online marketing resources.

Thirdly, it must be friendly to website managers. A good CMS system should be simple to use, feature-rich, and easy to manage, allowing website administrators to utilize more of the CMS system's own features to reduce their workload. Therefore, a CMS system suitable for corporate websites must completely separate from code execution, making it convenient for administrators to update and maintain. Additionally, the template creation process should be simple, making it easier for website managers to modify the website interface.

Currently, there are not many CMS systems that fully meet these three requirements; most can only meet one or two of them. Among those that can fully meet all three requirements, SiteServerCMS stands out due to its unique STL template language, DreamWeaver visual plugin, and pure dynamic page generation characteristics, making it quite suitable for businesses to choose.

A top-quality website, apart from precise planning and the selection of a good CMS system, requires attention to various details during its creation. It is understood that many companies, in order to cut costs, often skip many steps or do not execute certain steps carefully, which ultimately diminishes the value of the company itself. Therefore, when building a website, companies should not only plan reasonably and select high-end quality systems like SiteServerCMS, but also put more effort into design and management, maximizing the value that the company's website can bring in the internet world.

Just as products have different grades, websites are no exception and also have distinctions in quality. It is well-known that when building a house or constructing a skyscraper, laying a solid foundation is the first step and also the most crucial one. Without a stable foundation, even the most luxurious house is futile. For a website, its foundation is the CMS system used to build it. Nowadays, CMS systems are everywhere, and choosing a good CMS system from the start of website construction to lay a solid foundation is the most important step.
