Does high-end English training equal a high price?

by ab8v34t565 on 2012-03-05 21:45:40

In recent years, English training has continued to be a hot market trend, and many English brands have also started to focus on the high-end English training market. However, when it comes to high-end English training, the first thing that comes to mind is the issue of price. The cost of high-end English training can range from several thousand to tens of thousands, depending on the length of study time. Most people might wonder: Is it worth spending so much money to enroll in so-called high-end English training?

Advisors at West Shore English emphasize repeatedly that we win by quality. High-end English training places more emphasis on meeting customer needs. For example, in terms of flexibility with time, you can study anytime and anywhere; teaching locations or class venues can be chosen for convenience; class sizes are small, creating a comfortable environment where teachers are the focal point, making teacher-student interaction easier. From these points, it can be seen that on one hand, high-end English training maximizes results in terms of practicality. On the other hand, service-oriented teaching better meets the English learning needs of white-collar workers or office employees. In most cases, this group tends to be busy, so when choosing English learning, it's best if someone follows up, tracks, reminds, and assists with your English learning situation, much like colleagues in a team at work who help each other and move forward together.

In fact, this kind of attentive service-oriented teaching is something the high-end English training market highly values. They hope students will feel warmth throughout the entire learning process and genuinely feel that the teaching quality is good and the teaching results are evident through feedback.

West Shore English (www.aceleader.cn)’s adult English training supervisor gives everyone a brief introduction to high-end English training. It’s called high-end English training mainly because there are a few relatively distinct features. First, it can meet learners' personalized needs; second, every course has a relatively complete teaching system and unique textbooks. Third, foreign teacher-led classes—like those at West Shore English which use full face-to-face instruction—are available, while Chinese teachers may also assist students with weaker foundations. Fourth, the teaching environment is quiet and stylish, something large-class settings cannot achieve. Considering all these aspects, its costs will be much higher compared to other traditional courses and programs. Therefore, a significant part of the reason why high-end English training is priced higher than other projects is due to its distinctive characteristics. Many learners may feel that an investment of two or three ten-thousand over one or two years could be somewhat burdensome, but the key still lies in the learning outcomes you wish to achieve. Of course, this should also be combined with your personal financial situation. Additionally, this type of high-end English training truly offers value for money.
