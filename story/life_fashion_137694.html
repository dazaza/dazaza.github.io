Fashion Shopping Mall is an online shopping website mainly dealing in clothing and accessories. Since its establishment, it has been committed to providing high quality products at low prices, with a wide variety of items and a comfortable online shopping environment. With the principle of "professional standards and honest service," it allows all members to purchase their desired goods from the comfort of their homes.

In the future, we will continue to provide top-notch products and services to repay the support and love of our members. Before selecting items, please take three minutes to read some friendly reminders about shopping, which will be beneficial for you, me, and everyone!

1. Regarding ordering, confirmation, and payment

Since this site also handles wholesale business, inventory moves quickly, and ordering requires a cycle. Before making a payment, please make sure to check if there is stock available to avoid any delays. For customers who pay directly, if there is a stock shortage, we will promptly inform you and offer options such as a refund, product replacement, or use it as a deposit. For customers who have submitted orders but haven't paid yet, since the warehouse ships based on orders, we can only try our best to hold the item for you but cannot guarantee 100%, unless specifically arranged otherwise. However, timely payment is highly recommended!

2. Regarding shipping

Shipping time: Express delivery generally picks up packages at 6 PM every day, and shipments are made within 48 hours after receiving payment, following the order of payments received. Regular mail is shipped within two days. Urgent customers are advised to contact us first for confirmation, and we will do our best to assist you.

Delivery and postage explanation: Express fees are collected by the express company on schedule, and the price is negotiated at a discounted rate. Generally, we recommend using express delivery for reasonable charges and speed. The courier companies we use are Yunda Express and ZTO Express. If you prefer not to use them, please specify. Delivery issues: Many courier companies do not deliver on weekends, meaning that even if the package arrives at the destination on Saturday, it may only be delivered to you on Monday. Since this is beyond our control as the sender, we kindly ask for your understanding. Our feelings are the same as yours; we hope you receive the product as soon as possible.

3. Goods receipt issue

To reduce disputes and misunderstandings, please ensure you purchase the correct quantity of the products you need. Please fill in accurate recipient information, including detailed addresses and correct phone numbers, and keep the phone lines open. If someone else will sign on your behalf, please notify us in advance. Additionally, please note that if you choose express delivery, confirm that the courier can deliver to your area. We emphasize again!!! Please inspect the package in front of the courier before signing. After signing, we assume no responsibility!!! This is the commitment given by the courier. The reason we use the recommended logistics courier company is that it grants the right to inspect before signing. Please do not use reasons like (I didn’t sign, the courier left it at the gatehouse, the courier didn’t let me inspect, I didn’t see this statement, your terms are unreasonable... etc.) to request returns or exchanges after signing. If the address you provided is one where security or property management can collect on your behalf, please inform us beforehand so we can mark the package with instructions such as "must be signed by the recipient" or "contact the recipient before delivery."

4. Product-related explanations

1. Quality Commitment

We highly value our reputation and operate with integrity. All products are genuine and can be inspected.

2. Fair Price Trading

We operate on thin margins, with clearly marked prices. There are no discounts, and shipping is not included. The pricing is fair for every customer! To save time, we understand the desire to negotiate prices, but our prices are already the lowest. Any promotional activities will be displayed on the webpage. (*^__^*)

5. Other Common Questions

1. Are the products genuine?

We highly value our reputation and operate with integrity. All products are genuine and can be inspected. You can also check our credit evaluations to view our credit records and other buyers' reviews for a more objective perspective. We understand simple inquiries during purchase consultations, but we strongly dislike repeated questioning regarding this matter.

2. Why are your prices so cheap?

All our products come directly from manufacturers, avoiding excessive intermediary circulation. Moreover, we adhere to the principle of selling more at lower profit margins, maximizing benefits for everyone, which is why our prices are much cheaper than in specialty stores.
