Knocking on the door of Zhejiang Sports

by gdf1122 on 2012-02-22 18:28:29

Zhejiang is one of the most economically active regions in China. When it comes to the sports industry, Zhejiang boasts many impressive statistics as well: It was the first and only province in the country to release a marine sports development plan; the first province to carry out theoretical research on sports industry statistics; Fuyang City in Hangzhou became the first national sports and leisure demonstration zone; Zhejiang has 14 professional clubs and 16 professional competition home venues, leading the nation in both categories.

These "firsts" highlight the strength of Zhejiang's sports economy and allow us to hear Zhejiang's sports knocking on the world's door.

From the Spring Festival Gala stage to the Olympic stage

You must have a deep impression of the dazzling stages during the Year of the Dragon Spring Festival Gala; in five months, the exquisite "London Bowl" for the London Olympics will also broaden your horizons. What you didn't expect is that whether it's the stage for the Spring Festival Gala or the seating at the London Olympic venues, they are all "Made in Zhejiang."

Zhejiang Dafeng Sports Equipment Co., Ltd., one of Zhejiang's 600,000 private enterprises. This sports manufacturing company took on the important task of designing and installing seats for the "Bird's Nest" and the "Water Cube" four years ago during the Beijing Olympics. The outstanding quality immediately caught the attention of the London Organizing Committee, naturally securing a large piece of the London Olympic economic pie.

"A team of more than seventy designers, installers, and marketers spent three and a half years completing the bid-winning project for the London Olympic venues. Now, the vanguard team has gone to Brazil to negotiate the bidding work for the 2014 World Cup and the 2016 Olympics venues. In fact, we had already exhibited and negotiated in Rio de Janeiro two years ago, and it now appears to be very effective." Yesterday, when the reporter called Da Feng's CEO Feng Yue, he sounded full of energy.

From the Spring Festival Gala stage to the Olympic stage, Dafeng is just a snapshot of Zhejiang's sports manufacturing industry. The "Invincible Brand" rowing boats developed by Zhejiang Huayin Holding Group Co., Ltd. have entered the Olympics for three consecutive sessions, becoming the only designated boat. Huang Yongjun, an excellent athlete who retired successfully and started his own business, also included his trampoline product "High Fly Brand" in the list of London Olympic products.

The rapid rise of Zhejiang's sports manufacturing industry happened in just a few short years. The key to success lies in integrating resources through major investments, achieving asset restructuring and scale expansion, while always focusing on research and innovation, ultimately driving the transformation and upgrading of the sports goods manufacturing industry. At the end of last year, the Provincial Sports Bureau jointly evaluated model enterprises in the sports manufacturing and sports service industries with six departments including the Provincial Development and Reform Commission, allowing these leading enterprises to become new engines for Zhejiang's sports industry, playing an excellent exemplary and leading role.

According to statistics, in 2010, the total output value of sports and related industries in our province reached 71.7 billion yuan, accounting for 0.74% of the total GDP, becoming a new growth point for our province's national economy.

From small-scale operations to the creation of a sports city

Last week, Director Li Yunlin of the Provincial Sports Bureau led a team to investigate Fuyang's sports and leisure industry. As the first national sports and leisure demonstration zone, Fuyang has attracted golden phoenixes by building nests: at the Yong'an Mountain National Paragliding Training Base, the local government has already reached a cooperation intention with enterprises, preparing to invest 3 billion yuan to create a comprehensive sports and leisure experience and sightseeing base.

In Fuyang, the construction of a "sports and leisure city" has been enthusiastically carried out for four or five years. The Yong'an Mountain paragliding training base is now Asia's largest take-off field; apart from the "Invincible Brand" rowing boats, Zhejiang Huayin Group has also developed the province's first luxury yacht, preliminarily forming a cluster advantage of forty enterprises and over 2 billion yuan investment scale; the badminton racket industry in Fuyang's Shangguan occupies more than 80% of the domestic mid-to-low-end market share, becoming a block economy with broad prospects and strong momentum.

Represented by Fuyang, the sports and leisure industry in our province is flourishing throughout the province. Based on resource advantages and regional characteristics, eight major sports and leisure industries have been officially determined as key support and development areas in our province, such as business sports and leisure, hiking tours, extreme outdoor sports, marine beach sports, yachts and sailboards, automobile and motorcycle sports, electronic competitive sports, equestrian sports, etc. For example, four ski fields (venues) have already been built, more than fifty rafting routes have been developed, and eighteen golf courses have been constructed.

At the same time, the Hangzhou Bay (Ningbo) Sports City with a total investment of 20 billion yuan has completed its planning, focusing on making Zhoushan, Xiangshan, and Dongtou into a classic national sea fishing route. Next, the provincial sports and leisure base evaluation work will be launched; the Huanglong Sports Cultural and Creative Industrial Park has officially hung its sign, striving to become the first national sports cultural and creative industrial park. Zhejiang is not only suitable for business and study but also suitable for living, exercise, and health care. Sports and leisure should add points to the realization of a "Healthy Zhejiang."

From self-entertainment to enjoying quality and pursuing excellence

Zhejiang is one of the provinces with the smallest land area in the country, yet it owns 35,869 sports venues, with per capita sports venue area far exceeding the national average. The sports life enjoyed by Zhejiang's more than 54 million people has long moved from self-entertainment to enjoying quality.

Wenzhou Oriental Fitness Co., Ltd. was founded only eleven years ago but already has 24 chain stores. The data reported by the company's chairman Hou Shidong is astonishing: more than 5,000 members are received every day, and the fitness institutions have expanded to Taizhou, Lishui, even Henan and Anhui.

The industry generally believes that running fitness institutions is not easy at present. Why does Wenzhou Oriental Fitness remain prosperous? Hou Shidong's secret is: there must be quality service, and more importantly, long-term investment awareness. "It cannot be just about fitness. Apart from fitness, private coaching services, fitness and health consultations, and even online shopping services spread more information related to health and fitness." Hou Shidong stated that Oriental Fitness plans to expand another five chain stores this year, and entering the provincial capital Hangzhou is imminent.

Among the more than 35,000 sports venues, there are fitness institutions like Wenzhou Oriental, as well as competition venues for football, basketball, and volleyball, transforming sports and fitness into a service and consumption. Hangzhou Greentown competes in the Chinese Super League, men's and women's basketball teams compete in the CBA, etc. Currently, Zhejiang has 14 professional clubs and 16 professional competition home venues, allowing fans to enjoy domestic and even international top-level competitions right at their doorstep.

The operation of numerous professional clubs and hosting various large-scale sports events attract social capital investment exceeding 2 billion yuan, realizing the city's sports marketing strategy, such as roller skating making Haining well-known nationwide and women's volleyball making Beilun known globally. It is reported that during the "Twelfth Five-Year Plan" period, the investment scale of sports and related industries in our province may exceed 20 billion yuan RMB. Director Li Yunlin of the Provincial Sports Bureau said, "Sports competitions can not only bring glory to the country but also increase profits for the country."
