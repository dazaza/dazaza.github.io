Xiaomi Router Officially Unveiled!

by anonymous on 2013-11-20 11:55:30

According to previous news, Xiaomi will officially release a "new toy" at 10 o'clock today: the smart router. Now, Xiaomi's official website has launched a new page for the "Xiaomi Router" and adopted the new domain name miwifi.com.

It is understood that the public beta version of Xiaomi Router will go on sale on December 19th, with the price yet to be announced. In addition, the official website has also pre-released the official images of the Xiaomi Router.

As for the specifications, they have not been disclosed yet. However, some details can be inferred from the official Q&A below. One question asks, "Is the Xiaomi Router dual-band?" The answer hints that the Xiaomi Router will support both 2.4GHz and 5GHz dual bands by responding, "Since the country now allows two children, what do you think?"

It is reported that the public beta version of Xiaomi Router will not involve flash sales or lotteries. Instead, enthusiasts will be manually screened by the official team to prioritize testing. Specific details will be officially announced at 10 o'clock in the morning today.
