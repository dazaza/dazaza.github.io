(Bidding Procurement) China Guodian Corporation 2011 Third Batch of General Materials In-plant Heating Units, Wear-resistant Elbows, Airflow Measuring Devices, Domestic Instrument Valves Bidding Announcement March 7, 2011 21:02:00 Bidding Number: GDCX-TYZB11-03-(28-31) Opening Bid Time: March 28, 2011 Industry: Energy Chemicals Bidding Category: Domestic Bidding Location: Cross-provincial Project Name: China Guodian Group 2011 Third Batch of General Materials In-plant Heating Units, Wear-resistant Elbows, Airflow Measuring Devices, Domestic Instrument Valves Procurement (hereinafter referred to as "Project") Bidding Number: GDCX-TYZB11-03 Sub-package Number: GDCX-TYZB11-03-(28-31) Date: March 7, 2011.

1. On behalf of the final users, Guodian Luanhe Power Plant and Guodian Electric Power Dalian Development Zone Thermal Power Plant, bidding is conducted for in-plant heating units, wear-resistant elbows, airflow measuring devices, domestic instrument valves. The domestic public bidding method (two-step opening bid) invites bidders who meet the requirements specified in Chapter Two "Bidder's Requirements" Section 4 of this volume (hereinafter referred to as "bidders") to submit sealed competitive bidding documents for the supply and services of the equipment (materials) for this bidding.

2. The scope of this bidding includes China Guodian Group's 2011 third batch of general materials in-plant heating units, wear-resistant elbows, airflow measuring devices, domestic instrument valves (hereinafter referred to as "subject matter"). It also includes all necessary materials, spare parts, special tools, consumables, as well as design, technical data, and technical services. For more specific content, please refer to the relevant provisions in Volume Two of the bidding document.

3. Bidders must purchase the bidding documents from the bidding agency under their own name before they are eligible to participate in the competitive bidding. The copyright of the bidding documents obtained by the bidders belongs to the bidder and the bidding agency, and is only for use in this bidding process.

4. Sale of Bidding Documents: From March 8, 2011 to March 15, 2011 (excluding public holidays and holidays). Sale time is from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM daily (Beijing Time).
