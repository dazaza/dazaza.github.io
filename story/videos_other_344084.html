Park Dong-geun and Gao Xiaoying were reported to have started cohabiting, and their wedding will be held on May 2nd.

by silk8139 on 2010-04-12 16:46:28

The former residence of actor Jang Dong-gun and his fiancée, actress Ko So-young, is located in a Seoul apartment building in Baeksok-dong. Starting from next month, a large number of items have been moved into the house. It is reported that these are not the collectibles of Jang Dong-gun and Ko So-young, but so far, three batches of items have been moved.

Jang Dong-gun and Ko So-young, the famous Korean celebrity couple who will be getting married on May 2nd, were exposed to living together before their wedding.

Before making the decision to marry at the end of last year, rumors circulated that Jang Dong-gun and Ko So-young had already started cohabiting. Their decision to marry has been approved by both families, and everything has progressed smoothly. Moreover, the apartments where Jang Dong-gun and Ko So-young previously lived separately have been vacated, further confirming the news that they have moved into their new home together.

According to an informed source, Jang Dong-gun will be very busy before the wedding, which is why the moving process began ahead of the wedding. The preparations for Jang Dong-gun and Ko So-young's wedding are progressing smoothly. Actor Park Sung-hoon will serve as the wedding master of ceremonies, and former Minister of Culture Lee Oh-ryeong will act as the officiant.
