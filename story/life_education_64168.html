During the practice of conversational exercises with teacher LUCY, did you notice the course design in Speak2me? It is quite different from traditional spoken English practice methods and some currently popular spoken English practice systems. From the start to the end of the course, throughout the entire dialogue practice process, learners can choose one of two answers provided by Speak2me to respond to teacher LUCY’s questions. After LUCY understands the learner's response, she will ask new questions based on the different answers. Attentive learners may have already noticed that if calculated mathematically, depending on the length of the answer paths chosen by the learner when answering questions, each unit of the course could potentially produce hundreds of different combinations of conversation content. The experts at Speak2me precisely through this unique course design organically combine the necessary but tedious repetition in learning with fun, allowing every learner to persist in learning even without extraordinary perseverance.

When it comes to spoken practice, many students have tried different methods. Among them, shadow reading and role-playing fixed dialogues are the most common. However, because the content of the dialogue is fixed, learners hardly get the feeling of "conversation" and "communication", making it difficult to convert the practice content into their own language. The experts at Speak2me designed the courses with the aim of creating a virtual English environment for learners, allowing them to practice one-on-one with intelligent foreign teacher LUCY, achieving maximum results with minimal learning cost investment. To achieve a realistic effect, the experts at Speak2me specially invited a Canadian actress to dub for LUCY.
