Must-read for new stores -- How to comprehensively promote your store (Online Store Entrepreneur Edition)

First, we need to come up with a catchy and memorable member name and Wangwang nickname because a good name is easy for everyone to remember. My view on naming is either extremely local or extremely trendy, in short, go to the extremes. Follow your unique path. Speaking of promotion, it's not only applicable to newcomers but also necessary for diamond-level sellers. However, the methods and means of promotion used by diamond-level sellers are more mature and experienced than those used by newcomer sellers. For a new seller, talking about promotion can be a very headache-inducing problem. I am also a new seller, summarizing my experience over the past few months to share with everyone. Due to personal skill issues, if it's written poorly, please criticize and correct me!

1. Persistently visit various forums and communities, read posts, reply to posts, and post yourself. First, get familiar with people so that they have an impression of you. When replying to posts, try to make meaningful replies and express your own opinions. If you see a post seeking help and you can assist the poster, carefully reply to help them! Helping others is helping yourself. For discussion posts, when replying, express your own insights. If the poster or passersby notice and find common ground, they might become friends with you. More friends mean smoother paths ahead! This is both a free and effective method, and you can learn a lot of knowledge.

2. Make good use of the showcase recommendation spots. Don't underestimate the showcase recommendation spots. If set properly, traffic will increase, and with more traffic, the conversion rate will be higher. I suggest setting the off-shelf time of your items so that during peak hours every day, there are items scheduled to go off-shelf. Then, set three showcase recommendations daily. That is, during three peak hours, set all items scheduled to go off-shelf as showcase recommendations. This is my personal summary, and it's very effective.

3. Set the item off-shelf time to 7 days, which doubles the chances of your items being seen. At the same time, try to stagger your listing times. Your store frequently has new arrivals and items nearing the end of their shelf life. Not only does this make buyers feel comfortable, but it also increases your exposure rate!

4. Set up your item titles well. The maximum length of an item title is 60 characters. Try to include all words related to your item without wasting these 60 characters. This way, when buyers search for items, the chances of finding your items increase several times. This is also very useful.

5. Do a good job with friendly links. This step is also very important and should not be overlooked. It's not necessarily true that linking with diamond-level sellers will bring traffic. Diamonds also start from scratch. As long as the other party has confidence and potential, they could quickly become diamonds. Therefore, don't reject friendly links from new sellers. If they're in the same industry, you might even gain unexpected benefits! Friendly links must be fully utilized, and doing so comprehensively will bring traffic.

6. After spending a long time in the community, if the quality of your posts is good, there's a possibility of getting featured, and you can earn some silver coins. Using silver coins, we can grab ad spaces. Currently, there are three known ad spaces: 1. Community homepage image recommendation space. 50 silver coins per spot, quantity 8. Grabbing time: 1:00 PM. 2. Forum left-side text recommendation space. 20 silver coins per spot, quantity 20. Grabbing time: 4:00 PM. 3. In-site message recommendation space. 30 silver coins per spot, quantity 10. Grabbing time: 9:00 PM. At the same time, we can also use silver coins to buy ad spaces on Ali Mama. In short, as long as it's a method that lets others know about our store, we must spare no effort.

7. Another good place to promote your store is Taobao Inquiry. This section is in the Consumer Community. Based on the characteristics of your product, post questions or answer questions. Answer questions sincerely, do not advertise directly. Ensure answering at least 100 questions per day. Here are some precautions. In the inquiry section, everyone should answer questions with the mindset of helping others, professionally, and convincingly. Only by helping others will they develop a favorable impression of you. If you directly post store advertisements, it may cause resentment and lead to reports, which would be detrimental. So, everyone should maintain the right attitude and approach this with the mindset of "helping others equals helping oneself." Similarly, you can ask questions. The effects will be noticeable after a week!

8. Cleverly promote using Wangwang: Set up a link to your store on Wangwang. You can also set up some ads related to the features of your store.

9. Promote using QQ groups, promoting everywhere, but make sure to control the degree appropriately.

10. Strive for more promotional activities.

12. Promote your forum on commonly used forum promotion service websites.

For example: www.haobbs.net. We believe in your creativity and hands-on ability, encouraging you to actively exchange promotion experiences and participate enthusiastically in forum promotions.

13. Post hot topics of your forum in chat rooms of major websites.

Such as: NetEase, Sina, TOM, etc., portal site chat rooms. If posting URLs is not allowed, split the URL and post it; someone will come to see your post.

14. Post threads on forums with high traffic and active users to attract visitors.

For example: Mop (http://www.mop.com), PCPOP (http://pop.pcpop.com/default.asp), YTHT (http://www.ytht.net/).

15. Forums of various colleges and universities. These places have active users who enjoy forums. By leaving your signature on these places and including small links or ads in your posts or replies, you can attract significant traffic to your forum.
