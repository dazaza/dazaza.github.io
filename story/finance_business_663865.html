Sun Xie Zhi and Han Yu Get Married Early: In April this year, 33-year-old Sun Xie Zhi proposed to Han Yu in a cinema with a diamond ring. They had originally planned to register their marriage at the end of the year, but unexpectedly, they both appeared at the civil affairs office the day before yesterday to complete the notarization process. Sun Xie Zhi then presented another loose diamond to Han Yu for her to make into jewelry later, with the total value of the two diamonds amounting to nearly 1 million New Taiwan Dollars (approximately RMB 210,000). That day, they also showed off the wedding rings they had custom-ordered two weeks ago, interlocking fingers while radiating newlywed joy.

According to Information Times, another celebrity couple has tied the knot. The previous day, Sun Xie Zhi and Han Yu officially registered their marriage at the civil affairs office, marking the fruition of one year of love. That day, they sweetly exchanged love words in front of the media: "I will always love you." Both Sun Xie Zhi and Han Yu confirmed that they rushed to get married due to pregnancy. Sun Xie Zhi also frankly stated: "From now on, I won't worry about contraception anymore."

Tai Yu (the source of this article: Information Times) noted that Sun Xie Zhi's ex-girlfriends, Ding Xiao Qin and Zhou You Ting, did not gain the approval of Sun's mother, but Han Yu did. The day before, Sun Xie Zhi humorously said, "My honeymoon wife can go wherever she wants," and added, "I hope my wife comes first in everything," which made Han Yu very happy. However, she was also quite considerate, saying: "Getting married isn't just about our happiness; it's about the happiness of two families." They tentatively planned to hold the wedding banquet next May. However, Sun Xie Zhi grew up without seeing his father, and Han Yu's father divorced her mother five years ago and hasn't been in contact with them since.

Han Yu, in order to be a good 'Mrs. Sun,' abruptly canceled two acting contracts, losing over 20 million New Taiwan Dollars (approximately RMB 4.2 million), but she didn't regret it at all, saying she needed to prepare for the big event of marriage and family. Han Yu has already started taking care of her health, hoping to have a child with Sun Xie Zhi soon. On the day of their marriage, there happened to be a campaign encouraging childbirth. Sun Xie Zhi joked: "We've consulted, having one child costs NT$20,000 (approximately RMB 4,200), so we plan to claim NT$100,000 (approximately RMB 21,000)." Han Yu immediately responded: "Did you even ask me?" He publicly asked her how many children she wanted, to which she replied: "Two in one birth, let's settle it once and for all."
