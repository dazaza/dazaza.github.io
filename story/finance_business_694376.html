When talking about the trend of winning the championship, Galliani also expressed his opinion on the points table of Serie A at that time. "I think Lazio can challenge for the league championship. They have performed exceptionally well this season. Despite the fact that they almost got relegated last year, this year they have only made some minor changes, such as letting go of Kolarov and bringing in Hernanes. In fact, they are still the same team. We had tracked Hernanes for a period of time, but unfortunately, he didn't come to Milan to take Kaka's place. Kaka returning to Milan from Real Madrid? To be honest, I can't give you any answers."

AC Milan won 2-1 against Napoli away and welcomed their fourth consecutive victory in the league. On the points table, they surpassed their rival Inter Milan and rose to second place. Regarding the current upward trend of the team, Galliani also expressed his surprise. He said: "We are getting better and better. I believe this Milan will easily win the Serie A championship!"

In the game at San Paolo, AC Milan's starting left-back Antonini was injured and substituted off after just 17 minutes. However, Odon, who came on as a substitute, performed exceptionally well. Ibrahimovic's goal also came from his assist. Regarding this veteran player, Galliani gave him high praise, saying: "His performance last night was truly amazing. The second goal was a beautiful pass by him, and in the first goal, he also made his contribution."

AC Milan will face Juventus in the next round of matches, but Juve's core player Krasic has been suspended for two games by the Professional League for diving. This is undoubtedly great news for the Rossoneri. However, upon hearing this news, Galliani himself remained calm. He said: "I don't think Krasic's suspension is an advantage for us. Juventus is a team with a lot of depth. They have other players who can step up. In fact, the match is only four days away. I'm not concerned about these things. I just want to focus all my energy on the team."

At 2 a.m. Beijing time on October 27, according to reports by 'Milan Sports Daily', Milan defeated Napoli 2-1 away in the 8th round of Serie A, achieving their first victory at San Paolo Stadium in 12 years. After the match, coach Allegri praised it as "the best Milan," and Galliani also believed that if AC Milan could maintain this state and rhythm, they could easily snatch the Serie A championship.

The victory for Milan at San Paolo was very thrilling. In the final stages, the defense of the Rossoneri became somewhat loose, nearly allowing the opponent to equalize. Talking about this match, Galliani joked: "Last year, we encountered trouble on this field. At that time, we were still leading 2-0 at the 90th minute, but the final score was 2-2. This time, I didn't have the courage to watch the last two minutes. I could only choose to silently pray, but the final result was pleasing."
