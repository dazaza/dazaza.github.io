Meeting Room Name: Multi-functional Meeting Room, sound system and microphone provided free of charge

Hotel electronic screen: 200 yuan per banner, Meeting room banner: 200 yuan per banner;

If the actual expenses exceed the consumption or the deposit is insufficient, Party A has the right to request additional deposits at any time.

2. Party B guarantees to settle all expenses incurred during the conference at this hotel in one lump sum upon conclusion of the conference.

VI. Explanation of Party B's Signatory:

2. Party B shall not unjustifiably delay payment to Party A. In case of delay, Party B will pay a late fee at a ratio of 3‰ per day to Party A.

This agreement is made in duplicate, becoming effective upon signature by representatives of both parties.
