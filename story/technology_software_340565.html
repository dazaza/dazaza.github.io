Monthly seasons osmanthus

by vcdsaxqw on 2010-04-06 11:13:39

Since the beginning of April, I suddenly became obsessed with roses. I had never paid much attention to them before, but now I realize there are so many varieties that it's hard to imagine. And the large-flowered roses are especially beautiful. Then, I bought three kinds of large-flowered roses, two kinds of climbing roses, and added to that one small-flowered rose I had from last year. This April, the roses bloomed continuously in a variety of reds, with a rich fragrance, truly very pleasant. Yesterday, I cut off all the flowered branches, and then waited for the best time for peony roses to bloom. The small-flowered rose will continue to bloom as well.

One, Small-flowered Rose

The pot of small-flowered rose I bought last year is really quite ordinary and also very cheap, bought just for fun. Later, after it bloomed a few times, I pruned the branches after flowering, and it grew better and better. It seems to bloom several times a year. It also bears fruit, those orange-red small fruits, which are very pretty. The picture above was taken a few days ago, and the one below was taken in winter last year. Due to seasonal and sunlight reasons, there are some differences, so I find it interesting. Last time I went to the flower market, I saw many small-flowered roses, single-petaled, double-petaled, in every color, five yuan for two pots. Very cheap. People who buy small-flowered roses usually buy several plants, in various kinds.

Two, Large-flowered Rose

My obsession with roses started with this one, the osmanthus rose. The flower buds are very large, and I was really curious about how big the flowers would be when they bloomed.

Gradually, they opened, with countless petals, large blooms, and the colors were also attractive.

I haven't figured out which variety it is yet. One plant has three flowers, two of which have bloomed like this.

Another one has different petals, flower color, and way of blooming. After checking, it might be due to grafting.

This one is a peony rose, with a rich rose fragrance.

There is also another pot of yellow large-flowered rose, but when I bought it, the flowers had already bloomed, so I'll wait for its next bloom.

Three, Climbing Rose

I don't know the variety of this climbing rose, but it has a faint fragrance. When I bought it, one flower had already bloomed, and it was particularly beautiful. Somehow, the other buds bloomed into flowers that were somewhat different.

This pot has bamboo strips used to wind the branches nicely, and it smells very fragrant.
