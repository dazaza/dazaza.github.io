Champions League - Deadly Bombing by French Striker, Chelsea 1-0 Secures Top Spot in Group

At 3:45 AM Beijing Time on November 26th, the fifth round of group matches in the Champions League's Group D featured a key battle where Chelsea defeated Porto 1-0 away.

In the first half, Porto's Beluski hit the crossbar, but after 45 minutes, both teams remained tied at 0-0. In the second half, Anelka headed the ball into the net, ultimately leading to Chelsea defeating Porto 1-0 away. With a four-point lead, Chelsea secured their place as the top team in the group in advance.

Drogba Miraculously Returns

In the first four matches, Chelsea had accumulated 10 points with three wins and one draw, while Porto had earned 9 points with three wins and one loss. Both teams had already qualified for the next round, and were now competing for the top spot in the group. Historically, over five encounters, Chelsea held a significant advantage with three wins, one draw, and one loss. However, at the Dragon Stadium, Chelsea had never tasted victory, with one draw and one loss.

In this match, Drogba miraculously recovered from injury and partnered with Anelka up front. Ballack and Deco also returned from injuries. On defense, Russian international Zhirkov replaced the injured Ashley Cole.

Porto Hits Crossbar, Chelsea Half-time Lucky Draw

The match began with Chelsea kicking off away. In the 6th minute, Anelka made a wall-pass combination with Drogba inside the box and shot from a tight angle, but the opposing goalkeeper saved the ball valiantly. In the 15th minute, Anelka dribbled and turned before shooting directly from the left side of the field, but Porto's goalkeeper was focused and safely deflected the ball out of danger. Four minutes later, Deco passed diagonally into the box, and Ballack adjusted slightly before attempting a chip shot towards the upper-left corner of the goal, but it was blocked by an opponent. Subsequently, Drogba used a corner kick opportunity to head the ball toward the goal, which narrowly missed the target.

In the 21st minute, Porto tested Cech with four consecutive shots, putting Chelsea's goal under great pressure. In the 26th minute, Zhirkov failed to prevent the right-wing cross from Porto, but Mikel managed to control the ball in time within the box. Porto players committed a foul when trying to win the ball back. Four minutes later, Porto nearly scored when Beluski's shot from outside the box drew a beautiful curve that left Cech helpless, but the crossbar denied the goal. Following this, Falcao's low shot from outside the box was gathered by Cech.

In the first half hour, Chelsea firmly controlled possession, but in terms of game dynamics, both teams were evenly matched, with Porto seemingly closer to scoring. At the Dragon Stadium, British teams indeed faced difficulties. In the 36th minute, Anelka intercepted the ball in the front field and launched a counterattack, but Deco's right-footed long-range shot from outside the box missed the target. In the 45th minute, Porto's full-back Serpnaaru advanced to the edge of the box, but no Chelsea player rushed to block him. The Romanian adjusted momentarily before taking a long-range shot, which narrowly missed the upper-right corner of the goal.

At halftime, Chelsea and Porto remained tied at 0-0.

Anelka Scores with Header

In the second half, in the 50th minute, Fernando fouled Drogba, and the referee showed him a yellow card. Two minutes later, Porto's defender made a mistake clearing Anelka's cross, and Deco attempted a direct shot from 20 yards without controlling the ball, which narrowly missed the right post.

In the 56th minute, Chelsea won a free-kick on the left side of the field, but Malouda made a serious error during the set-piece, allowing Porto to launch a quick counterattack. Meireles' low-angle shot from the left side of the field was collected by Cech. Two minutes later, Ballack was shown a yellow card for committing a foul.

In the 60th minute, Porto made a substitution, bringing on Hulk to replace Varela. In the 62nd minute, Malouda cleverly avoided Alves' tackle, but his pass across the penalty area lacked quality, allowing the defenders to clear it easily. Six minutes later, Essien came on for Ballack.

In the 69th minute, Chelsea broke the deadlock: Malouda crossed from the left side of the box, and Anelka dived to head the ball into the middle of the small box, leaving the goalkeeper with no reaction. Chelsea led 1-0 away.

Chelsea Secures First Place in Group

In the 76th minute, Joe Cole replaced Deco. Two minutes later, Rodriguez took a close-range header from the center of the box, which narrowly missed the target. The Uruguayan complained to the referee about Ivan pulling his shirt, and subsequently, Meireles was shown a yellow card for committing a foul. In the 82nd minute, Hulk's shot from the edge of the box was caught by Cech diving down. Three minutes later, Terry blocked the opponent's shot with his body. Home fans believed Chelsea's captain handballed, but slow-motion replays showed the ball hit his chest.

The match ended with Chelsea defeating Porto 1-0 away. (anckie)
