UK puppy helps paralyzed owner with shopping, laundry and paying (picture)

by iface001 on 2011-04-08 16:05:14

More surprisingly, Sandy can even open the door of the bathroom at home to let her go to the toilet and always close it again after use.

The report said that this extraordinarily smart dog could do more than many people had imagined. Sue, its 61-year-old owner, was unfortunately paralyzed in a car accident 40 years ago, but Sandy's arrival changed her life.

Sue, aged 61, said: "Sandy is an indispensable part of my life. Because of it, my life has become easier, and it gave me a sense of independence I've never had before," on the registration page.

Lee Armstrong, the deputy manager of the local store, also said: "Sandy has become a star in the eyes of other customers and staff. People always can't help but focus on its amazing actions when it helps the owner shop and pay cash."

According to foreign media reports on January 6th by China News Service, the 4-year-old dog Sandy in Coventry, West Midlands, UK, has become a true "star" in the eyes of the locals.

This smart dog not only makes her happier but also helps her shop, do laundry, and even pay cash. Whenever they go shopping at the supermarket, while the owner selects items, Sandy stays close with the shopping basket in its mouth. When they reach the checkout counter, Sandy can even take money from the owner's wallet and "hand" it over to the astonished cashier.

In addition, Sandy can start the washing machine. It separates Sue's dirty clothes and closes the outlet of the washing machine before the machine starts stirring.
