### One: Which are the Five Major Acupoints to Blow With a Hair Dryer?

Around the stomach area of the human body, there are five acupoints: Juque, Zhongwan, Xiawan, Left Liangmen, and Right Liangmen, which are commonly used acupoints in TCM for weight loss. Using a hair dryer on these acupoints has an effect similar to moxibustion with mugwort, making the acupoints warm, inhibiting gastric acid secretion, and reducing food intake.

### Two: How to Locate the Five Major Weight Loss Acupoints

These acupoints are located between the stomach and navel, forming a cross shape. By measuring the width of your index finger, middle finger, and ring finger together (approximately 2 cun), first locate your navel, and then it becomes easy to find the five major acupoints.

1. **Xiawan Acupoint**: 2 cun (3 finger widths) above the navel.

2. **Zhongwan Acupoint**: 2 cun above the Xiawan acupoint (6 finger widths above the navel).

3. **Juque Acupoint**: 2 cun above the Zhongwan acupoint (9 finger widths above the navel).

4. **Left Liangmen Acupoint**: 2 cun (3 finger widths) to the left of the Zhongwan acupoint.

5. **Right Liangmen Acupoint**: 2 cun (3 finger widths) to the right of the Zhongwan acupoint.

### Hair Dryer Weight Loss Method

After finding the five weight loss acupoints above the navel, use the hair dryer to perform moxibustion on each acupoint. Avoid getting too close to the skin to prevent burns. Blow on one acupoint for about 10 seconds before switching to another, cycling through all five acupoints. Do this for approximately 25 minutes daily. Continuing this process for a month will yield results, achieving the same effects as moxibustion with mugwort. On average, you can lose 2 kilograms, and your waist circumference can shrink by 1 inch.

1. The Zhongwan acupoint is at the center of the five acupoints, forming a cross shape with each point being 2 cun (3 fingers) apart. If unsure how to locate them, consult a doctor during your first attempt.
