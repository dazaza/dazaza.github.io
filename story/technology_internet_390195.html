II. Online Store Market Analysis

In China, how many women have not yet owned their own bags? How many women desire to own beautiful bags? How many women worry about not having suitable bags when going out? And on the other hand, how many men wish to fulfill such wishes for their beloved women? Even if each person owns only one bag, such a market would be enough to astonish any businessman with foresight!

Everyone knows that European fashion is the world's model. In cities where the fashion industry is developed, the ratio of clothing stores, shoe stores, and bag stores is approximately 2:1:1. This ratio reflects the lifestyle of Europeans. They particularly focus on the coordination of clothes, shoes, and hats, requiring one bag and a pair of shoes for every two sets of clothes. In Guangzhou, Shanghai, Shenzhen, which are at the forefront of fashion, the ratio of clothing, shoe, and hat shops or the ratio of outfit coordination is at most 50:5:1, far from reaching the European level. Opening a store online has advantages in coverage and cost, making it more suitable for small and medium-sized enterprises that are easily affected by economic crises to promote and market, and more able to meet the needs of the local market, providing a superior shopping platform for more consumers.

(a) Competitor Analysis: (Further research needed)

As the saying goes, "Know yourself and know your enemy, and you can fight a hundred battles without disaster." We currently cannot compete with first-tier brands, whose advertising budgets exceed our investment budgets. There are thousands of second and third-tier brands, most of which compete on low prices, these are our competitors. Currently, our opponents have not grown mature, and there are deficiencies in the operation of many online stores. We should value credibility, provide true information to online shoppers, improve service quality, and enjoy an honest, lively, fashionable, and efficient online transaction culture.

(b) Risk Analysis

1. Advantage: We have our own bag production line, the source of goods is directly supplied by the factory, the supply is more sufficient, the credibility is higher, with the strong support of the company's human and financial resources, our competitiveness is stronger, which many online stores cannot match.

(1) Taobao store customers belong to us, but also to our competitors, which is very unfavorable for the stable accumulation of customers. The survival and development of online stores rely on credibility.

(2) Impact of transaction security, network information data transmission causing data theft or destruction; damage during transportation or incomplete carton packaging, etc.

III. Marketing Strategies

(a) Product Strategy: Find your own characteristics, create your own style! Create your own brand! Quality improves, price decreases.

(b) Pricing Strategy: In online store sales, due to lower costs compared to traditional market sales channels, a low-price strategy is generally adopted; meaning that pricing often includes cost plus a certain profit, or even zero profit, and must be lower than similar products in traditional markets. This strategy is also used to expand publicity and quickly open up a new situation in the online market.

(c) Online Promotion Strategies

1. Online Discount Promotions: Due to the inability of online sales to give comprehensive and intuitive impressions, inability to try or touch products, plus complex delivery costs and payment methods, enthusiasm for online shopping and ordering is much lower than in malls and supermarkets. By choosing the right time to reduce prices, we can attract more consumers and hit competitors.

2. Set up a Special Price Area: We can specifically set up a special price area, weekly taking out some best-selling products for discount promotions. Although the profit margin of this product is low, it can increase the popularity of the shop and boost sales of other products, naturally improving performance.

3. Limited-time Supply: This always tempts consumers. Slogans like "Special price only for 2 more days!" But it must be genuine, otherwise it will eventually lose consumer trust.

6. Indirect Online Discount Promotions

Indirect discount promotion refers to improving the quality or quantity of products or services without increasing or slightly increasing the price, significantly increasing the added value of products or services, making consumers feel they get what they pay for. Since direct online price discounts can easily lead to doubts about reduced quality, using promotional methods that increase the added value of goods is easier to gain consumer trust.

Note: To make the promotional activity achieve good results, it is necessary to conduct market analysis, competitor analysis, and feasibility analysis of online activities beforehand, combined with the overall marketing plan, creatively organize and implement the promotional activity, making the promotional activity novel, sales-driven, and influential.

IV. Establishing the Shop

1. Shop Decoration:

2. Listing Goods:

(1) All goods' online time must be 1 week, not 14 days. Taobao defaults to showing products nearing the end of their listing period at the top, and the browsing volume is highest when nearing the end. Therefore, ensure there are always goods being listed and delisted daily.

(2) Choose the time slot with the most online users to list goods. The best times: 10:30-12:00, 12:30-14:00, 15:30-18:30, 19:30-23:30.

(3) After selecting a good time slot, list one product every 10-15 minutes. Taobao Assistant can automatically complete this, otherwise, with increasing numbers of products, manual management will definitely become very cumbersome.

(4) Keep Wangwang (instant messaging tool) frequently online, especially 1-2 hours before goods delist. The closer to delisting time, the more our goods appear at the top of search results, this time period has the highest trading volume.

3. Use of Keywords:

(1) Shop Name (Very Important): Choose an enticing shop name, preferably one that clearly indicates what is sold upon seeing the name, with a maximum of 30 characters, searchable on Taobao, Baidu, Google, etc.

(2) Shop Classification: Do not use images to classify the shop, carefully plan keywords and use them in shop classification. Set keywords will appear in the blue title bar at the top of the browser window. Search tools are highly sensitive to text here!

(3) Logo, Shop Introduction, and Shop Announcement: Create a nice logo (Logo), write promotional information on the bulletin board, and include main projects in the shop introduction.

(4) Goods Name: The goods name can use 30 Chinese characters, most buyers search by goods name on Taobao, possible keywords should be written in the goods name!

Note: Online sales differ from general offline sales, the same goods can be fully searched online based on item numbers. To avoid abnormal competition and price wars, do not include item numbers or brands in the goods name.

V. Online Store Promotion and Publicity: Due to funding limitations, media advertisements are impossible in the early stages, only low-cost online promotion methods can be used.

1. Shop Credit: This is almost always checked by buyers before shopping. Write all about discounted items and big promotions in the evaluation content.

2. Taobao Wangwang: Good business comes from Wangwang being online frequently, facilitating communication between both parties.

3. Taobao Forum: Many potential buyers hide in forums, don't ignore its role. Make the avatar and signature look good, combine with good posts, whether original posts or replies, others can notice; regularly change signatures, promptly inform others of the latest news of the shop.

4. Baidu Tieba: Buyers can find our online store through it.

5. Fully utilize QQ, MSN, blogs, forums, emails for promotion;

6. Establish friendly links with related online stores to achieve mutual benefits;

VI. Online Store Payment: We choose Alipay payment method because Alipay acts as a credit intermediary in online transactions. Through Alipay transactions, "cash on delivery" and "payment upon receipt" are simultaneously accommodated, reducing risks for both buyers and sellers!

VII. Shop Sales Services (Appendix: Warm Reminder)

1. Guidance for Buyer Evaluations: Evaluation is the final opinion of both parties regarding a transaction, and is also an important reference for potential buyers. Good credit makes buyers confident in purchasing, poor evaluations often deter potential buyers. Therefore, after completing a transaction, we kindly remind buyers to make truthful and positive evaluations.

2. Handling Complaints with a Peaceful Mindset: As previously mentioned, no seller can make buyers 100% satisfied, and customer complaints will occur. Handling customer complaints involves listening to their dissatisfaction, continuously correcting our mistakes, and maintaining our credibility. Proper handling not only enhances and consolidates relationships with customers, but can even promote sales growth.

Seal the outside of the box with tape in several places to prevent it from falling apart, and preferably use a packing machine to further secure it, avoiding damage or leaks during transportation.

Inside the package, ensure all product documents and materials are complete. Write down your contact information on the product packaging, include some small but practical gifts, and provide material information about the product that the customer might want to know.

VIII. Shop Management

(a) Goods Management: If the number of goods increases, we need to frequently inventory them, checking which goods are still available, which ones are missing, whether they match the online store, and if there are any quality issues, etc., all of which need to be kept in mind.

1. Online Store Goods Classification:

(1) New arrivals and discounted goods classifications should be placed in prominent positions;

(2) Names of new arrival classifications should be updated frequently;

(3) Goods classification names should facilitate customer selection.

2. Warehouse Goods Management

(1) Goods sold out need to be delisted promptly;

(2) When manually restocking, carefully check all warehouse classifications;

(3) If goods are sold out but still available, simply modify the quantity to relist them for sale.

(4) The golden time for uploading product pictures is: afternoon: 14:00-18:00, evening: 20:00-23:00.

(b) Customer Management:

1. Establish a customer database, helping us maintain customer relationships.

(1) Understanding the buyer's occupation or city and other backgrounds during transactions helps us summarize suitable items for different groups;

(2) Buyers with strong purchasing power should be summarized as key targets, developing this group into loyal buyers helps expand our business;

(3) Set loyal buyers as our VIP customer group, establishing corresponding preferential policies within the store, allowing them to enjoy 80% off discounts, etc.

2. Set up customer service on Taobao Wangwang or QQ.

(1) Provide a platform for customers to communicate and exchange ideas, getting good opinions and suggestions from them;

(2) Group customers, stay in touch with them regularly, retaining old customers is easier than developing new ones. Greeting them during holidays, like friends interacting, we sell not only goods but also friendship;

(3) Actively convey the latest event information to customers via email;

(4) Timely contact customers to avoid losing completed transactions due to lack of timely communication.

(c) Time Management: As part-time online salespeople, we may need to invest more time and energy, and should balance work and rest accordingly. This requires us to learn strict time management, reasonable planning, and scientific time arrangement.

(d) Ledger Management:

1. Establish ledgers using Excel tools

Design good sales reports, financial statements, customer management, goods statistics and classification tables, etc., spend a little time each day filling out various statistical tables, summarize once a week, and make a serious summary each month.

2. Analyze and summarize the sales situation of goods

Carefully analyze various data, including the number of goods logged in a month, the number of goods sold, and the situation of the sales rate, etc.;

Find the operating characteristics of the small store, such as when are the peak and slack seasons? Customer consumption characteristics; capital flow status of the small store; changes in browsing volume and their characteristics; adjust the types and quantities of goods purchased according to the results obtained from the summary.

3. Write a daily reminder

For example: the buyer made special requests, or we verbally promised to send gifts to the customer, but forgot in the hustle and bustle, by the time we remember, either the goods have already been sent, or the customer reminds us, many "bad reviews" come from this. Therefore, writing a daily reminder is very important.
