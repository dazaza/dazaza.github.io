China - the world’s leading high-performance analog IC designers and manufacturers, Austria Microelectronics Corporation (SIX ticker symbol: AMS), announced the launch of the AS364X series LED flash driver IC used in mobile phones, cameras, and other handheld devices. The product features a high level of integration and a 4MHz DC-DC boost converter, making it the industry's smallest chip while ensuring the highest accuracy, thus guaranteeing the best photo and video image quality. The new product family supports an output current ranging from 320mA to a maximum of 2A, extending austriamicrosystems' Flash product series and providing the best solution for entry-level mobile phones, high-end smartphones, tablet PCs, digital cameras, and video recorders. In addition to being the smallest in the industry, the new product line offers highly accurate I2C programmable output current and time control with many safety features, combined with intelligent built-in features and proprietary processing technologies, ensuring that the flash remains operational even at very low power, thereby improving image quality in mobile environments. Overall, the AS364X series achieves stable image quality, faster product design cycles, and the highest flexibility along with the best system protection and security features. Each flagship product AS3648 can drive two LEDs up to 1A directly from a battery-powered LED flash solution, achieving the highest light output so far. Using this new product to create a complete flash solution requires less than 22 mm² of PCB board footprint, making it the smallest size in the industry. Other high-end features include LED balancing, fully programmable flash, flashlight, and AF modes, 8ms flash time control, and 8kV ESD protection on the LED pin. The AS3647 offers the highest brightness and the smallest size and cost-effective camera system for high-end applications, providing the best quality single LED current up to 1.6A. Features such as programmable TxMask current, system stop protection for the main flash, coil current limit, thermal protection, and reliable LED open and short circuit safety features make our products the ideal choice for balancing system cost and quality in both smartphones and feature phones. Entry-level products (AS3644 and AS3642) have the industry’s smallest footprint of only 11mm², offering programmable flash current and duration, video lights, flashlights, and AF lights, bringing new and differentiated functions. The new product provides up to 320mA (AS3644) and 500mA (AS3642) LED flash current, making it very suitable for cost-sensitive mobile phones and tablet PCs, as well as high-end smartphone platforms for social video conferencing front cameras. The AS3643 provides up to 1,300 mA flash current for each LED, making it very suitable for mid-range mobile phones, video recorders, and tablet PCs, including quality improvement functions with the highest level of flexibility and programmability, as well as system security.

Oliver Weber, austriamicrosystems mobile lighting product marketing manager, said: "As more consumers want to take high-quality photos with their mobile devices, the key to photo quality lies in using the flash in low-light conditions. The majority of mobile phone images have evolved from simple LED lights into high-quality flashes starting from the end customer’s needs, combined with leading OEMs’ continuous cooperation experience, launching the new series of AS364X flash driver ICs. The new product will strongly support the next generation of mobile products with higher image quality. Each product in the series is packaged in a space-saving WL-CSP package, compatible with 2.7V - 4.4V lithium batteries, and operates within an ambient temperature range of -30°C to +85°C. The products are available now, each in a 1,000-piece set. A product demonstration board is also provided. To learn more about the LED flash driver series or to request samples, please visit www.austriamicrosystems.com/LED-flash-driver/AS364X."
