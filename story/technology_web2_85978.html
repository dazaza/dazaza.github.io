Conference Marketing — Traditional Marketing Model

Conference marketing refers to a sales method where specific customers are targeted, and products are sold through family-like services and product seminars. The essence of conference marketing is the targeting and development of potential customers, providing them with comprehensive information about the company image and product knowledge, and offering hidden sales techniques while caring for potential customers as expert consultants.

Conference marketing belongs to single-layer direct sales. Currently, there is no unified name for it. Some call it science popularization (experience) marketing, others database marketing, or affection (service) marketing/advisory marketing, among other terms. However, the term "conference marketing" is used most frequently. Regardless of the terminology, conference marketing is one of the powerful sales tools created by the Chinese since the reform and opening-up period, tailored to their own realities.
