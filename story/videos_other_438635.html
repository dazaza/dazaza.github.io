[Zhuyu Xuan] Walking in the Snow at Yihe Garden [Original]

by anonymous on 2011-05-28 15:57:25

Yesterday I rested at home all day and did not go out. I felt that my body had recovered a lot. The special edition of the photography enthusiast recruitment blog conference photography competition, elderly diseases, so how could I not go out to appreciate the beautiful snow scenery in the city today? Perhaps going outdoors can breathe fresh air, closely contact with snowflakes in the Summer Palace, which is beneficial to health!

Therefore, after eating breakfast, I suggested going to the Summer Palace to admire the snow scenery. My partner was worried about whether my body could endure it. He asked me with care: "Xiangxiang, do you think your body is suitable for going to the Summer Palace? It's still snowing outside, can you make it?" I said playfully: "I can! If I am tired and cannot walk, will you carry me?" "Alright, alright, there's really nothing I can do about you," my partner reluctantly agreed.

We prepared our park annual card, brought the bus card, and left for the bus stop at 9:30. On the way, I felt the snowflakes falling on my face, with a cool sensation. I couldn't help but stretch out my hands to catch the tiny snowflakes, but I couldn't catch any! We were waiting for the bus, two vehicles of Yuntong 104 have already passed, Yuntong 115 also came, followed by No. 109 bus. In a daze, I don't know why, I boarded the No. 109 bus, quickly swiped the card. At this moment, I heard my partner shouting: "Xiangxiang, get off the bus quickly!" Before I could react, my partner went up to the driver and said: "Excuse me, driver, my partner got on the wrong bus." The driver was very nice, he pressed a button on his steering wheel control system and said to me: "Madam, please swipe your card again." Without much thought, I followed the driver's words, swiped the card, and was pulled off the bus by my partner. Seeing my performance, my partner suggested we go home. I still insisted on going to the Summer Palace. After waiting for 15 minutes, the No. 469 bus finally arrived, and the passengers boarded the bus in order.

At 10:07, the No. 469 bus arrived at the West Gate station of the Summer Palace. My partner tightly held my hand, entered from the west gate, and walked along the Unity Lake water area from south to north, stepping on the snow. Snowflakes danced softly and gently landed on our clothes, making a rustling sound like soft music, pleasant to hear. Stepping on the accumulated snow made a clear and enjoyable crunching sound. Ah, another rare snow scene in Beijing City, and it happened to be Sunday. Today, we did not walk along the West Causeway; I mainly wanted to see the Boundary Lake Bridge that I missed last time. When I saw the Boundary Lake Bridge, I quickly took out my camera and started taking photos on the bridge.

We then came to the Long Corridor and encountered several tour groups. The guides were introducing the scenic spots while walking. Almost everyone among the tourists had a camera in their hands, and many people's photographic equipment was quite professional! Some even set up tripods, carefully selecting scenes and taking pictures. I thought: they might be photographers from some magazine or newspaper. Of course, many tourists used DSLR cameras, zoom lenses, and telephoto lens cameras to capture the beautiful snow scenery of the Summer Palace, preserving beautiful memories!

Truly, the snowflakes danced gracefully, and the crowd was bustling with laughter everywhere. From the Long Corridor back to Zhi Chun Pavilion, the snow scenery was incredibly charming. Ah, the Fragrance Buddha Pavilion proudly stood on the peak of Wanshou Mountain, as if also joining the ranks of tourists. Looking far into the distance, one could take in the beautiful lake and mountain scenery covered in snow, fully enjoying the charm of the captivating snowy view.

Ah, treading on the snow in the Summer Palace, truly every place is a beautiful sight, dazzling and enchanting. Look, regardless of gender or age, standing in the snow, appreciating this graceful snow scenery, breathing in the fresh air, welcoming the steps of spring, what a delightful and satisfying experience it is!

Immersed in this beautiful silver world, my mood was exceptionally comfortable, and the discomfort in my body had long dissipated to the outermost reaches of the sky!
