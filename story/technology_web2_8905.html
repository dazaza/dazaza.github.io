yo2.cn is a website that basically aggregates many commonly used services.

1. My Profile - Your personalized homepage

Everything you do on yo2.cn can be displayed on this homepage.

2. To-Do List - Your online calendar

You might have some things to do and wish to share them with netizens, and also be interested in knowing which netizens are doing the same things as you. In this case, you can record these tasks in our "What I'm Doing" service, allowing you to exchange experiences with other netizens.

3. Albums - Your online photo album

You can upload your favorite photos to the album and share them with netizens, getting to know each other better.

4. Search Tag - A search engine for all content across yo2.cn

We also provide a bookmarking service. Just click on the "I want to include..." link on the Search Tag homepage to collect your favorite web pages anytime and anywhere.

5. Blogs - The world-popular WordPress system

By using our WordPress blog service, you can easily own a professional blog site. In the future, we will support international domain names!
