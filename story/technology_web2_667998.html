The list of successful candidates for the initial registration of Level 1 Constructor in the 9th batch of 2011 has been announced, with a total of 1 person from Guangxi.

Announcement on the List of Registered Personnel for Level 1 Constructor in the Ninth Batch of 2011

Announcement No. 1203 of the Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of the People's Republic of China

According to the "Regulations on the Administration of Registered Constructors" (Ministry of Construction Order No. 153), the "Implementation Measures for the Registration of Level 1 Constructors", and the "Announcement on the Provisions and Contents of Administrative Licensing Matters Directly Implemented by the Ministry of Construction" (Ministry of Construction Announcement No. 278), after review, Zhang Guoxin and 13,932 others who applied for the registration of Level 1 Constructor meet the registration requirements for Level 1 Constructor and are granted registration.

Hereby announced.

Attachment: List of 1 Person from Guangxi Who Meets the Registration Requirements for Level 1 Constructor

Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development of the People's Republic of China

December 7, 2011

Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region: (1 person) Li Bingbing
