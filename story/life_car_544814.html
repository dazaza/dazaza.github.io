Introduction: How to choose a location for a car beauty shop? This is a common problem troubling many newcomers to the car beauty industry. China Auto Beauty Talent Network offers the following points, hoping to be helpful to everyone.

(I) Location Selection Factor Analysis

When selecting a location for a car beauty shop, the following factors should be analyzed:

1. Vehicle traffic: number of vehicles, type and class.

2. Pedestrian traffic: number of people, type. Can judge the busyness and prosperity of the store location.

3. Parking facilities: number of parking spaces, ease of entering the store, position of street barriers, direction of traffic flow and store location.

4. Composition of the store cluster: number and scale of same or similar industry stores, distance between stores, compatibility of stores.

5. Specific location: visibility of the store sign, shape, size, and characteristics of the store building, shape of the store sign and its visual range.

6. Store opening conditions: lease terms, operating costs, taxation, regional planning and regional regulations.

(II) Location Selection

The following locations are more suitable for opening a car beauty shop:

1. Around car sales gathering points (streets) or vehicle management offices, transportation management offices;

2. Adequate nearby customer sources, with multiple functional hotels, restaurants, and government agencies;

3. The storefront is preferably located next to a parking lot, gas station, or high-end residential area;

4. Areas with a higher concentration of same or similar industry stores;

5. Wide roads around the store, large vehicle traffic, convenient vehicle entry and exit, but not too close to traffic lights;

6. The storefront must face the street, with clean and tidy surrounding environment;

7. There should be enough open space in front of the store to park 4-6 vehicles simultaneously;

8. A larger sign can be placed above the storefront for good advertising effect;

9. Abundant water supply and excellent drainage conditions.

(III) Business District Analysis Determines Store Location Choice

A business district is a geographical concept, referring to the geographic area where a store can effectively attract customers, i.e., the store's radiation range. It consists of three parts: core business district, secondary business district, and fringe business district. Before selecting a store location, analyzing the business district is essential as it not only affects the success of starting a business but also lays a solid foundation for future marketing strategies and brand image.

The core business district is the closest area to the store with the highest customer density, accounting for about 45%-60% of the total customer base. Generally, the radius of the core business district for car beauty shops is 4KM, but since most customers drive themselves, the scope may be even wider.

The secondary business district lies between the core business district and the fringe business district, with more scattered customers and weaker store radiation. The probability of occasional consumption is higher, accounting for about 20%-25% of the total customer base. Generally, the radius of the secondary business district is 5-7KM.

The fringe business district is located at the outermost part of the core business district, with customers mostly consuming occasionally or opportunistically. The radius of the business district is over 8KM.

The purpose of business district analysis: First, to grasp various consumer information of customers to provide effective targeted services; Second, to choose media for promotional activities based on the size of the business district and determine the publicity model; Third, to formulate competition plans for same-industry stores within the business district and plan whether to open a store within a business district that already has same-industry stores; Fourth, to analyze the advantages and disadvantages of the store location to adjust the business model accordingly.

(IV) Design and Decoration of Car Beauty Shop

A professional car beauty shop usually consists of five parts: operation room (including car washing area and beauty area), office, boutique, reception room, and spray booth. The basic requirements for design and construction are:

Combine with the regional service market to determine the scale of operations and goals;

The car wash site should pay attention to the environment, and the design should be aesthetically pleasing and adaptable to development needs;

The construction site should meet urban health standards (clean, neat, beautiful);

Car washing supplies should be produced by reputable manufacturers and be qualified eco-friendly products;

The design and investment of the car wash and beauty shop should consult professional companies, who will develop a plan and conduct engineering supervision;

Water and electrical pipelines should be one meter or more above the ground to ensure safety;

Large and medium-sized stores should have a 1-2 square meter restroom.

(I) Operation Room Decoration

The operation room is the main facility of a car beauty shop, where all car beauty work is completed.

Walls

The standard for operation room wall decoration in Bright Car beauty franchise chain stores is that when choosing materials, attention should be paid to: first, choosing waterproof materials; second, choosing anti-corrosion materials. Walls should have white as the main tone. If it's a chain store, the wall color tones of each branch should be basically consistent. In addition, the walls should display "Car Beauty Project Signs" and "Standard Price Signs".

Floor

The steps in and out of the operation room should be made into ramps. The size, width, and slope of the ramp should be appropriate to the store. Too small a ramp may affect the appearance of the store and the convenience of entering and exiting. The floor of the business store cannot be too slippery; floor slip resistance is an important point in interior decoration. Newly laid floors should consider drainage issues, with the side with the drainage ditch being slightly lower for better drainage.

Roof

The roof should ideally consist of a grid pattern (aluminum alloy frame plus lighter panels) and hang colorful flags promoting the brand.

(II) Office Decoration

The office is the place where store managers and finance personnel work. Besides general office decoration, the following requirements should be met:

1. Hang job responsibility signs for each functional person on the wall;

2. Set up a manager's desk, chairs, and office supplies;

3. Set up a cashier counter for financial personnel, used for cashing out and issuing tickets, generally located at the entrance or exit.

(III) Reception Room Decoration

The standard for Bright Car beauty franchise chain stores is that the reception room is a place to receive customers and for them to rest. Since cars are expensive, owners often do not want their cars to leave their sight during beautification. Therefore, the reception room should be separated from the operation room by glass. This way, it allows the owner to feel at ease and increases the transparency of the operation, allowing the owner to understand how their car transforms from old to new. The reception room should be equipped with sofas, coffee tables, and water dispensers for receiving guests.

(1) Boutique Decoration

The boutique mainly provides some car-related items and fan merchandise. Such as car care liquids, car decorations, and various car models, to meet the requirements of different enthusiasts. The boutique should be equipped with:

Display windows: Mainly used for displaying products and other goods, also for placing promotional items and other displays.

Wall cabinets: Used for exhibiting products, glass doors are preferred.

Counters: Counters are generally integrated with wall cabinets, and counters should preferably have no obstructions on the platform.

(2) Spray Booth

Larger car beauty shops also have spray booths, used for restoring severely damaged paint jobs where the primer has been compromised.

(3) Water and Electricity Facilities Installation

Water Supply: Car beauty does not require high-quality water, but there must be sufficient water pressure and supply.

Drainage: Drainage ditches should be dug along the edges inside the store to ensure no standing water accumulates.

Installation of faucets: Faucets should be installed near the corners of the walls. For larger stores, 1-2 additional faucets should be installed in different directions for operational convenience. Additionally, a faucet dedicated to a washing machine should be arranged because many towels, bath towels, and other wiping tools are used daily, so a washing machine is provided in the store to ensure cleanliness and timely circulation use of these tools.

Electrical Facilities:

1. Lighting: General car beauty workshops use fluorescent lights. However, sometimes night operations or places with poor lighting effects occur, so lighting problems should be considered during renovation to ensure adequate light.

2. Electrical outlets: Electrical outlets must use quality waterproof outlets because water splashes during cleaning processes, which is a basic safety issue. Generally, the height of outlets from the ground is 30-50 cm.

3. Power Supply: The load capacity of the main switch should consider the power consumption of lighting, polishers, cleaning machines, and other electrical appliances operating simultaneously. For beauty shops with spray booths, the electricity usage of the spray booth should also be taken into account.

Three, Equipment Configuration

Equipment configuration for a car beauty shop:

High-pressure washer, foam machine, waxing machine, washing machine, steam machine, polisher, two-in-one foot mat dryer, vacuum cleaner, vacuum oil extractor, spray gun, auxiliary tools.

Four, Procedures for Opening a Store

To open a car beauty shop, the following procedures need to be followed:

(1) Apply for a business license

As an individual or company, apply for a business license at the local business department. The enterprise name can be determined by the franchisee.

Documents required for registration include:

Identity proof: ID card of the enterprise responsible person, employee ID cards.

Capital proof: capital verification report.

Site proof: 'House Ownership Certificate' or 'Lease Contract'.

Business scope: Determined by the operator and approved by the business department. To facilitate future expansion of the business scale, the business scope should be expanded as much as possible during application. For example, main business: car beauty, sideline business: car decoration, car sundries, or car supplies.

(2) Apply for a special industry license

Franchisees with spray booths should also apply for a special industry license according to local government regulations from the public security or transportation authorities. Because spray painting changes the appearance of the car, approval from relevant departments is necessary.

(3) Application for store signs and outdoor signs

To set up a store sign or other outdoor signs, an advertisement company agent should apply to the local business department's advertising supervisor (department, section) (according to local regulations).

(4) Handle procedures related to city management

In cities where outdoor operations are not allowed, setting up auxiliary buildings or related operations outdoors, such as drainage, should consult the local city management department and handle relevant permit procedures.

(5) Handle tax-related procedures

Application for tax reduction from the tax department.
