"Leading Life" tells the touching stories of the mayor of Bei'an City, the discipline inspection commission secretary, and their entourage including drivers and maids, seeking sublime existential values in their daily lives.

This is a major revelation of leadership life! Mayor Zheng Xiaofeng consistently refused to promote his half-brother Luo Xiaoli, leading to accusations of familial betrayal from their mother. The mother went to great lengths for her goal but died in a car accident. Meanwhile, Zheng Xiaofeng's first maid Lianzi started a romantic relationship with driver Wujiang and became pregnant. After leaving the Zheng family, Lianzi became the maid of Discipline Inspection Commission Secretary Jiang Kegang. Could the single Jiang Kegang resist the enormous allure of the youthful maiden? Part Seven: Leadership Life

Recently, Zheng Xiaofeng always felt that being the mayor was harder than ever. Solving the problems within the leadership team of the Bei'an District didn't mean permanent peace. How was Luo Xiaoli doing after becoming the party secretary? Could he hold his own? Would he have effective administrative measures? Could he stabilize the political situation? These were all things that weighed heavily on Zheng Xiaofeng's mind. After all, Luo Xiaoli was promoted "skipping levels" from the position of standing vice-county magistrate. On one hand, people would doubt his governance experience, and on the other hand, they would revere his leapfrog promotion. Since Zheng Xiaofeng strongly recommended Luo Xiaoli, he bore leadership responsibility. What Zheng Xiaofeng feared most was if Luo Xiaoli proved unworthy, his painstaking efforts wouldn't bear good fruit.
