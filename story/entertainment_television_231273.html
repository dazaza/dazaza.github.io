Plot Summary

The Birth of the Black Knight

Since childhood, Mai Di Shuang had always been full of business acumen, understanding well that only through business could one truly succeed. He worked hard and completed his MBA at Stanford University in the United States. After graduation, Shuang saw potential in Ho Wen Tian's company and proactively approached him, offering partnership instead of employment, even if it meant owning only 0.01% of the shares. Impressed by Shuang's uniqueness, Wen Tian allowed him to join as a partner. Their relationship was both teacher-student and friends, working seamlessly together to acquire companies and break them apart for sale, becoming famous snipers in the American business world.

Later, Shuang became known as the ruthless Business Black Knight, whose merciless tactics gradually increased his power. However, the differences in opinions between Shuang and Wen Tian also grew. To benefit the company and ensure its development, Shuang decided to fully acquire Wen Tian's company. Unable to bear the blow, Wen Tian drove drunk, and Shuang learned that Wen Tian's car had crashed into the sea. After the incident, Wen Tian's body was never found, but Shuang firmly believed he was dead, feeling immense guilt. Was the value of a single deal really worth more than friendship and life?

After reflecting on his past, Shuang decided to no longer be the Black Knight and exited the business world. From the newspaper, Shuang learned that Wen Tian's widow and children were struggling financially in Hong Kong, and he felt responsible, deciding to return to Hong Kong to take care of Wen Tian's family.

Miao Miao's Debt Collection

Shuang returned to Hong Kong and found Wen Tian's wife, Yu Mo Lian, coincidentally staying at a hotel run by Tang Ji. Tang Ji is a middle-aged playboy from a wealthy business family with a notorious reputation but is very filial to his father and surrounded by beautiful women.

By chance, Shuang met Lin Miao Miao in a luxury store, creating animosity between them. Miao Miao is a compulsive shopper who owes a large amount of credit card debt and has been forced to work as a debt collector. With her "no success, no fee" spirit, she has become the top performer in the debt collection company.

Mo Lian owed a significant debt to Miao Miao's financial company due to her grandmother Zhou Xiao Qiao being scammed. Miao Miao was responsible for collecting this debt, leading to another encounter with Shuang. Shuang took over Mo Lian's debt, causing Miao Miao to mistakenly believe that Shuang was Mo Lian's ex-husband who had abandoned his family, leaving a bad impression of Shuang.

Mo Lian was unaware that her husband's death was indirectly caused by Shuang and still believed her husband Wen Tian was alive. Mo Lian's grandmother often accused Shuang of being the murderer of her son Wen Tian. Initially shocked, Shuang later discovered that the grandmother had early-stage Alzheimer's disease, and everyone thought her words were mere ramblings, not to be taken seriously.

Shuang Transforms into the White Knight

Shuang, a regular customer at Qi's Noodle Shop, introduced Mo Lian to work there when he learned about her financial difficulties. Mo Lian, who grew up in the U.S., is straightforward and doesn't know how to navigate social intricacies, but works diligently and enthusiastically, bringing new energy to the noodle shop, earning praise from Zhong Qi.

Qi's Noodle Shop faced threats from a newly opened cart noodle shop nearby. Shuang suggested the term "CEO," and Miao Miao, to save the noodle shop, entrusted her brother Lin Mu Sen to become the shop's CEO. Mu Sen took over the management but lacked actual skills, causing chaos. Shuang advised Zhong Qi not to hand over the shop to Mu Sen and recommended Mo Lian for a key position due to her hardworking nature. However, Miao Miao didn't take it seriously, thinking Shuang was just talking nonsense. Unexpectedly, Miao Miao encountered her ex-boyfriend Tong Yi Ben again on her business journey.

Miao Miao Gradually Develops Feelings for Shuang

Mo Lian reluctantly sent her grandmother Xiao Qiao to a nursing home for work reasons. Shuang, trying to comfort Xiao Qiao, also requested to stay there. Coincidentally, the nursing home was funded by Tang Ji for his girlfriend, facing operational difficulties. When Tang Ji caught Shuang and Miao Miao during his secret rendezvous at the nursing home, knowing Shuang's capabilities, he hoped Shuang would help solve the nursing home's problems. Miao Miao pressured Shuang further, hoping to learn business strategies from him. Reluctantly, Shuang decided to act.

After agreeing, Shuang seemed indifferent, making Miao Miao think he wasn't taking the reform of the nursing home seriously. Miao Miao decided to test him and proposed the "Planting Affection, Receiving Bonus" plan, which ended up causing trouble. Eventually, Shuang stepped in to resolve the unrest in the nursing home and surprisingly merged it with a neighboring kindergarten, creating a new generation of old-young cohabitation center, solving all issues. After this experience, the master-disciple relationship between Shuang and Miao Miao significantly improved.

Miao Miao realized she was gradually developing feelings for Shuang, but Shuang ignored her. Miao Miao thought Shuang was a deeply emotional man with an unshakable woman behind him. Later, she discovered this woman was Shuang's mother Qing Qing. Shuang transferred his maternal affection to Miao Miao, who tried her best to resolve the issue. Seeing Shuang's persistent rejection, she believed he looked down on her and decided to strengthen herself to gain his respect and win the heart of the man she loved.
