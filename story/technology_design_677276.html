The "Book of Changes" is one of China's oldest and most profound classics, said to be a summary and modification of the words of Fu Xi (which also gave rise to the Book of Changes Bagua Chart). It is the crystallization of five thousand years of Chinese wisdom and culture, hailed as the "First of All Scriptures and the Source of Great Way". In ancient times, it was the study of emperors, a must-learn for politicians, military strategists, and businessmen.

The "Book of Changes" permeates every aspect of our lives, running through our entire lives, whether it concerns personality, destiny, job hunting, career, competition confusions, or issues related to relationships with friends, spouses, children, parents, etc. It proposes the "Grasp the Rules, Create Miracles" theory of communication based on the Book of Changes zodiac signs and environmental education.
