Price fluctuation situation: The Junjie FSV had continuous discounts in 2010. At the beginning of the year, there was about a thousand yuan discount with a gift package included; subsequently, the discount increased to up to 6000 yuan in October. Entering 2011, due to fewer inventories at dealerships, the discount was reduced to 5000 yuan. This time, the dealership has further reduced the cash discount, changing it to offering a 10,000 yuan gift package.

Model review: In terms of design style, the Zhonghua Junjie FSV continues the overall features of being grand and fashionable. The Zhonghua Junjie FSV boasts a super-large body size of 4510mm x 1758mm x 1460mm, with an axle distance reaching 2580mm, approaching the level of B-class cars; the front part adopts a purebred Huali honeycomb-style grille, distinct sporty meridians, and chrome strips, giving the whole vehicle elements of fashion and dynamism.
