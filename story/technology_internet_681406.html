Hubei Zhisheng Education answers your questions: Is a self-study examination diploma useful?

by qindingdangx on 2012-02-28 13:24:58

Zhisheg Education Network [http://www.hds0110475_dbkj.com/] reports that recently, many netizens have called to inquire about self-study examination [http://www.hds0110475_dbkj.com/zikao.html] diplomas while also expressing their inner confusion: Does the country recognize self-study examination diplomas? What are the roles of a self-study bachelor's degree? Is it easy to obtain a self-study bachelor's diploma? For this reason, the editor of Zhisheg Education [http://www.hds0110475_dbkj.com/] has specially collected and organized some characteristics of current self-study exams for your reference and study.

Nowadays, academic qualifications are very important. If you have good qualifications, it will be easier to enter a good company. If you work hard, the company will focus on cultivating you, and you will have a large promotion space. Many people did not work hard enough during their studies and missed the opportunity to obtain higher qualifications. Self-study learning provides such an opportunity well.

Academic qualifications are favorable conditions for your career development. Although many companies now emphasize ability, academic qualifications are still an indispensable stepping stone for job hunting. The higher education self-study examination is a state-run, authoritative academic qualification examination. The national unified self-study examination has very strict requirements. The diploma also has a relatively high gold content. It is a test and exercise of the candidate's personal character, quality, perseverance, endurance, and self-learning ability. It reflects a very strong self-control ability and self-learning ability.

What specific roles do self-study [http://www.hds0110475_dbkj.com/zikao.html] graduates play after obtaining a bachelor's diploma? Below, some expert teachers introduce these materials about what to do after passing the self-study bachelor's exam, hoping that you have a better understanding of self-study and can better grasp the direction of development.

Firstly, some expert teachers point out that a bachelor's diploma is a stepping stone for job hunting: Many units (especially government agencies and public institutions) require a bachelor's or master's degree or above for recruitment, and those with associate degrees do not have the qualifications to apply or take exams. For example, many schools require a bachelor's degree for recruiting logistics management personnel and laboratory technicians. Some primary and secondary schools also require a bachelor's degree or above for recruiting teachers. Most civil service positions only allow associate degree holders to apply for some arduous work positions, and the work locations are basically at the grassroots level. Due to educational reasons, many ideal job opportunities will be lost. Self-study undergraduate education can help you seize more employment opportunities.

Secondly, expert teachers say that obtaining a bachelor's diploma is also the best choice on the road to postgraduate studies: With a bachelor's degree, you can directly take the national unified graduate school entrance exam without a degree certificate. Associate degree holders can only take the graduate school entrance exam as an equal degree two years after graduation. Although the state stipulates that associate degree holders who have graduated for more than two years can take the graduate school entrance exam as an equal degree, many universities actually do not want to recruit associate degree holders and will set obstacles in many aspects.

Expert teachers also suggest that if you want to become a civil servant, having a bachelor's diploma is a necessary choice: Civil servant jobs are stable, with higher salaries, less pressure, and power. Now many people want to become civil servants. According to regulations by the Ministry of Personnel, civil servant positions require passing the civil servant examination. Now most civil servant positions require a bachelor's degree or above to qualify for application, usually only grassroots and arduous work positions are left for associate degree holders.

For those who want to study abroad, expert teachers also propose the benefits of having a bachelor's diploma: Now many countries recognize our country's bachelor's degree. With a bachelor's degree, you can directly enroll in higher-level education abroad without wasting time overseas, which will save a lot of costs.

The current self-study [http://www.hds0110475_dbkj.com/zikao.html] is becoming stricter, which means that the status of self-study certificates in society will be increasingly higher in the future. Self-study represents a person's self-study ability, learning ability, and self-discipline ability. Therefore, some enterprises will also prefer students who have taken self-study exams in the future.
