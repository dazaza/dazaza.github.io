China Unicom officially announces 3G fee standards

by diglog2008 on 2009-05-16 13:01:47

China Unicom's official website 3G tariff page screenshot >> Click to view detailed comparison of 3G tariffs for the three major operators. According to NetEase Technology News on May 16th, China Unicom announced its "WCDMA-3G mobile package" tariff plan the day before the 3G trial commercial operation. The plan includes seven levels of "3G basic packages" and three "3G optional packages".

It is understood that China Unicom's "WCDMA-3G mobile package" adopts a structure of "3G basic package" + "3G optional package", including seven levels of "3G basic package" and three "3G optional packages". The "3G basic package" has seven levels from 186 yuan to 1686 yuan, divided according to voice, video and other streaming media as well as pure text charges. Among them, the 186 yuan package includes 510 minutes of domestic call duration, 20 minutes of domestic video call duration, 20M, 40T, 60MB data traffic, caller ID, and morning and evening news; the highest 1686 yuan package includes 7000 minutes of domestic call duration, 300 minutes of domestic video call duration, 200M, 250T, 5GB data traffic, caller ID, morning and evening news, and two mobile TV live broadcast basic packages. Domestic video call fees for the excess part and excess M/T are charged at standard rates.
