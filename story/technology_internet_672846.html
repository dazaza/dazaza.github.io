The Basketball Association of the island city vigorously builds up a new era for amateur basketball.

In order to further enhance the public fitness awareness and health level in our city, continuously meet the increasing fitness needs of the people, better promote the extensive development of popular basketball sports, improve the physical quality of citizens, and also increase the social influence of basketball and comprehensively create a social participation atmosphere for basketball, the Basketball Association of Qingdao will hold the [2012 'Basketball Association Cup' Qingdao City Spring Festival Basketball Open Tournament] during the Spring Festival. The competition adopts a free team registration form, players born between 1962 and 1994 (i.e., from January 1, 1994, to December 31, 1962) can register as a team. We welcome all enterprises, institutions, social organizations, communities, and individual enthusiasts to actively form teams and participate in the competition.

Competition Regulations for the 2012 Qingdao City [Sports Affinity Basketball Association Cup] Spring Basketball League

Organizer:

Qingdao Basketball Association

Qingdao Evening News

Host Organization:

Chengyang Sports Training Base, Qingdao

Co-organizer:

Qingdao Sports Affinity Sports Goods Co., Ltd.

Competition Date:

Starting from February 2012

Competition Venue:

Indoor Basketball Gymnasium of Qingdao National Fitness Center

Age of Participants:

Players born between 1994 and 1962 can freely enroll and form teams.

Eligibility for Participation:

1. Each team must pay an entry fee of 1000 yuan and a disciplinary deposit of 1000 yuan (fully refunded after the game if there are no violations or disciplinary issues). Enterprises, institutions, social organizations, communities, and individual enthusiasts can all freely register and form teams to participate.

2. In the same season competition, each athlete can only represent one team to participate. Otherwise, the results of that match for the team will be canceled. If a player's team withdraws or disbands during the competition, the player can only register for the next season.

Registration Methods:

1. Each team is limited to registering 15 athletes, 1 team leader, 1 coach, and 1 team doctor (the team leader and coach can also be registered as players, but the total number of players cannot exceed 15). After registration, no changes or additions to athletes are allowed. Teams must fill out the registration form in detail, attach copies of all members' ID cards and one one-inch color photo to both sides of an A4 paper (see attachment), and submit a disciplinary deposit of 1000 yuan to the Qingdao Basketball Association. The deposit will be fully refunded after the game if there are no violations or withdrawals.

2. There is no limit to the number of registration teams. The registration deadline is January 20, 2012.

Competition Methods:

1. The competition method will be decided based on the number of registered teams.

2. The game is divided into four quarters, each lasting 10 minutes. There is a 2-minute break between the first and second quarters, and between the third and fourth quarters. There is a 10-minute break between the upper and lower halves.

3. Scoring method: 2 points for a win, 1 point for a loss, 0 points for a forfeit. Teams with more points rank higher. If two teams have equal points, the result of their head-to-head competition determines the ranking. If three or more teams have equal points, the ratio of points scored to points conceded in their head-to-head competitions determines the ranking. If still equal, the overall ratio of points scored to points conceded in the entire round-robin tournament determines the ranking.

Rules and Related Regulations:

1. The latest version of the "Basketball Competition Rules" reviewed by the Chinese Basketball Association will be used for the competition.

2. Each team must have two sets of competition uniforms in different colors. The uniform colors must be carefully filled in the official registration form. The names and numbers of the competition players must correspond to the registration form. The front and back of the jerseys and the front of the shorts must have clear numbers that comply with the rules. Otherwise, they cannot participate in the game.

3. If there are irresistible reasons, the competition date may be changed by the organizing committee.

4. During the competition, if a participating team cannot participate due to special circumstances, it will be considered as a forfeit.

5. Basketball games have a certain degree of confrontation. Before the game, participants must undergo a physical examination. Only after being certified as physically healthy by a hospital can they voluntarily participate in the competition. Each team must independently arrange personal accident insurance for its members. If any accidents occur during the game, the organizers bear no responsibility.

Award Methods:

1. Every team that registers for the competition will be rewarded with 2 basketballs.

2. The top eight teams will be awarded certificates and the following rewards:

① The champion team will receive a trophy and a set of uniforms for each member.

② Each member of the second-place team will receive a pair of leather basketball shoes.

③ Each member of the third-place team will receive a set of long-sleeve competition uniforms.

④ Each member of the fourth-place team will receive a set of short-sleeve competition uniforms.

⑤ The fifth-place team will receive 10 basketballs.

⑥ The sixth-place team will receive 8 basketballs.

⑦ The seventh-place team will receive 6 basketballs.

⑧ The eighth-place team will receive 5 basketballs.

3. The competition will set up 3 [Fair Competition Awards], and award plaques.

4. The Best Athlete Award and the Best Coach Award will each be given to one person, and certificates will be issued.

5. The Best Referee Award will be given to 3 people, and certificates will be issued.

Qualification Review:

1. A qualification review group will be established to strictly review the qualifications of all registered athletes, and severely handle those who falsify information or violate regulations.

2. The Qualification Review Committee will carefully verify the competition qualifications of the participating athletes before, during, and after the competition. If it is found that someone does not meet the competition qualifications before the competition, their competition qualifications will be canceled, and they cannot be replaced by others. If it is found during or after the competition that someone does not meet the competition qualifications, their own or their team's competition qualifications and results will be canceled.

3. Any party who has objections to the qualifications of participating athletes (teams) and wishes to file a complaint must submit a complaint report signed by the team leader and a complaint fee of 300 yuan to the Qualification Review Committee for acceptance.

All expenses during the competition period are self-funded.

Unforeseen matters in these regulations will be notified separately, and the interpretation rights belong to the Organizing Committee of the Conference.
