The bathhouse worker was determined to undergo plastic surgery for cross-dressing.

by xue94fwsh on 2012-03-04 15:09:00

Lee Jingkui and his "Bilong" City Evening News report: Determined to continue on the path of cross-dressing art, Lee Jiaming has indeed sacrificed a lot, including his love. He never expected that being a grassroots bathhouse worker would illuminate another path in his life. After 3 years of effort, Lee Jiaming has become quite famous in the cross-dressing circle. On the 7th day, he walked into the Changchun Zhongyan Beauty Hospital with over 100,000 yuan that he had saved up through hard work (Breast enhancement massage acupoints 2009.01.14 - incident). From the bathhouse to the cross-dressing stage Yesterday morning, at the Zhongyan Plastic Surgery Hospital, Lee Jiaming told reporters his story. Lee Jiaming is 25 years old, from Jiutai, and was born in a rural area. Since childhood, he loved singing. Because of the family's poor conditions, Lee Jiaming dropped out of school after junior high school and helped his parents work at home. At the age of 19, Lee Jiaming came to Changchun and, through relatives' introductions, became a bathhouse worker. Although the salary was not much, Lee Jiaming "appreciated" artistic performances in the bathhouse and occasionally came into contact with some cross-dressing actors. What Lee Jiaming did not expect was that a cross-dressing artist said his height, figure, demeanor were all quite suitable for cross-dressing, and his vocal range was good, singing very well, so he could try this path. Considering the working environment and income of a bathhouse worker compared to a cross-dressing artist, plus Lee Jiaming's confidence in his own singing skills, at the age of 21, Lee Jiaming gradually began performing cross-dressed roles. Lee Jiaming's singing skills and performance talent were widely recognized by the audience, but as a cross-dressing actor, his sharply defined facial features and rough skin texture made his "costume" on stage less than ideal. Secretly saving money for plastic surgery The cross-dressing artist Li Yugang became famous across the country, and he became Lee Jiaming's idol. Lee Jiaming lived frugally, saving every penny, just to transform his originally handsome face into a beautiful woman's face. For Lee Jiaming's idea, neither his parents nor his girlfriend understood, but Lee Jiaming showed determination to see it through. He worked hard to persuade his parents and girlfriend, and eventually, the parents agreed, but the girlfriend left because she couldn't accept it. "My orientation is fine, I am a normal man, and in the future, I will also marry and have children. Cross-dressing is an art, and I feel that I truly found myself in this art." To be perfect, enter the plastic surgery hospital On the early morning of October 7th, Lee Jiaming entered the plastic surgery hospital. "Doctor, I want plastic surgery, I want to turn into a girl's face, the prettier the better!" Lee Jiaming's words shocked Dr. Fu Guoyou, the plastic surgery expert at Changchun Zhongyan Beauty Hospital. Yesterday, when interviewed by reporters, Dr. Fu Guoyou said that he initially thought Lee Jiaming was a patient with gender identity disorder, but after further understanding, he found that Lee Jiaming was not an ordinary beauty seeker. His demands might not be completely solvable by plastic surgery technology, so he suggested that psychological experts first evaluate his mental state. The hospital specially invited Professor Tian Yahua, a renowned expert in gender disorders research and special guest expert on CCTV's "Psychological Interview", to conduct psychological diagnosis and consultation for Lee Jiaming. In the end, it was confirmed that Lee Jiaming had normal male psychological characteristics, without any sexual identity recognition disorders, meaning that he was different from what we commonly refer to as transgender people, and his pursuit of beauty was entirely due to the needs of stage performance. Expert consultation will implement micro-plastic surgery Considering Lee Jiaming's normal male psychological state, the expert group decided to use the currently popular micro-plastic surgery combined with laser skin beautification methods to realize Lee Jiaming's dream of becoming a star through "sex change". Dr. Fu Guoyou said, "Lee Jiaming's performance schedule is arranged quite tightly, micro-plastic surgery has minimal trauma and short recovery period, almost not affecting performances; open surgery once turns into a 'daughter's face' cannot be reversed, while the changes made by micro-plastic surgery can be restored." According to Dr. Fu Guoyou, the trauma from micro-plastic surgery is very small, requiring only one week for recovery. At that time, our newspaper will follow up with reports to see Lee Jiaming's post-surgery "beauty".
