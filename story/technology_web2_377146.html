"Interview" Doit.im: API will be open in the future

by ybcz0519 on 2011-01-06 17:11:07

This article is simultaneously published on Mobinode, business media please do not reprint! We have previously introduced the service Doit.im to you all. Doit.im is an excellent GTD tool that currently provides clients for various platforms, including Windows, Mac, Android, iPhone, and iPad.

Link: https://i.doit.im/

We conducted a simple interview with Doit.im's founder, Zhe Xu, hoping that through this, everyone will have a more comprehensive understanding of Doit.im.

1. Please introduce Doit.im (founding time, website introduction)

The predecessor of Doit.im was established in August 2007, but at that time, our focus wasn't on this area. We officially started working on Doit.im in August 2009. So far, what Doit.im offers can still be considered as time and task management services. We hope users can better manage their time and tasks through Doit.im, and concentrate on dealing with current affairs.

2. Please introduce your team situation

Our team consists of more than 10 technical personnel and several product personnel. They are all young men and women in their prime. The characteristic is that there are particularly many handsome guys and beautiful girls. You've seen a lot, but you haven't seen this many. :)

3. What was your original motivation for creating Doit.im?

Initially, it was mainly to improve my own efficiency and use it myself. Being busy with projects, I was always looking for this kind of software, but none of them met my expectations, so I decided to create one myself.

4. Before Doit.im appeared, there were already many similar products on the market, such as Things, Toodledo, ToDo, etc. What do you think are the characteristics or advantages of Doit.im compared to these?

Indeed, there are quite a few. Compared with other similar applications, Doit.im is cross-platform and completely free. Moreover, we offer overall support surpassing others - most of these other similar softwares are operated by a few people or a small team, and those that perform well are mostly paid software.

5. Since its launch, Doit.im has undergone multiple revisions. What do you think is the biggest change Doit.im has experienced from its launch until now?

We have undergone three revisions in total. The biggest change so far is that we no longer consider Doit.im as just pure software, but rather as a service to operate and develop.

6. As the core of Doit.im is a GTD product, could you talk about your understanding of GTD?

Simply put, GTD helps people save time and focus on getting things done, whether it's work or life.

7. Can you talk about the future development direction of Doit.im?

On one hand, Doit.im will definitely continue to develop in the GTD direction; on the other hand, we hope that by the end of 2011, Doit.im will no longer be confined to this concept alone. In January 2011, we will open up our API, closely cooperate with third-party developers, and launch various types of software products. We also hope users will participate and create software tailored to each person's individual situation.
