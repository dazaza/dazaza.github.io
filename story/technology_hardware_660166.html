Yangcheng Evening News - Guangzhou Daily reported on February 7 that a 7-year-old girl was playing on a construction site and staged a "hanging game", which resulted in her accidental death. The girl's parents sued the person in charge of the construction site. Recently, Gaoyao City People's Court concluded this case. The judge believed that the girl's parents did not fulfill their guardianship responsibilities, and there was no necessary connection between their daughter's hanging suicide and the defendant, thus dismissing the appeal.

Fiction became reality, the little girl got too involved in the drama.

Xiaohai Fang (a pseudonym), a 7-year-old girl, was smart and lively since childhood. She liked watching TV, especially the popular TV series' "Little Swallow". When Xiaohai Fang saw the scene where "Little Swallow" hung herself but did not die, she often shouted to her playmates nearby that she wanted to learn how "Little Swallow" hanged herself.

On the afternoon of November 20 last year, Xiaohai Fang and some children were playing outside the factory wall operated by the defendant. They stacked up a pile of bricks, tied a rope to the burglar-proof net on the window of the factory wall, made a knot with the rope, and started playing the "hanging game". As a result, during the play, Xiaohai Fang's neck was strangled by the rope and she couldn't move. Her parents rushed over upon hearing the commotion, rescued her, and immediately sent her to the town health center for emergency treatment, but unfortunately, it was impossible to save her.

Due to lack of supervision, the parents bore the consequences.

After Xiaohai Fang passed away, her heartbroken parents sued the boss of the construction site, Mr. Chen, in court. They believed that the defendant violated legal mandatory regulations in the construction of the factory and did not take any measures to prevent accidents at the construction site. They demanded that the defendant bear 50% of the compensation responsibility, i.e., death compensation, funeral expenses, and other losses totaling 226,000 yuan, as well as 10,000 yuan for mental distress compensation.

Defendant Mr. Chen claimed that the plaintiffs did not fulfill their due supervisory responsibilities, leading to the death of their daughter by hanging, causing the defendant a loss of the factory being returned unrented, and the plaintiff should compensate the defendant 288,000 yuan.

After reviewing the case, the court believed that the plaintiff's daughter was only 7 years old, completely without civil capacity, and the plaintiff did not fulfill the responsibilities they should have undertaken as guardians. There was no necessary connection between the hanging suicide of their daughter Xiaohai Fang on the defendant's construction site and the defendant. Therefore, the court dismissed the plaintiff's lawsuit request.
