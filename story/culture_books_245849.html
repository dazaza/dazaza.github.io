She answers gently - Jian Xia uses her wise pen to write about women, men, reality, ideals, joy, and sorrow... She writes about all sorts of urban matters, sometimes warm, sometimes slightly cool. The renowned female writer Jian Xia's latest collection of essays, "Women Turn Left Men Turn Right," is composed in the form of questions from readers and witty answers. The content covers many aspects such as love, marriage, and career. The writing style varies from gentle and elegant to sharp and playful, with an elegant tone and incisive analysis. It is a high-quality read that combines philosophy and interest, suitable for urban men and women to understand love and life and seek happiness. A vivid depiction of urban life and a manual for refining love - this is a book that leads you to feel the beauty of life and brings you closer to love and happiness.
