Fox demon, female knight, time traveler - broadening horizons for a promising future

Since her debut, Liu Shishi has played many characters beloved by audiences. Her elegant and sweet appearance is very appealing, and the roles she has played have distinct personalities. From the fantasy genre's fox spirit Jian Ling to the martial arts world's female knight, each character has left a deep impression on the audience. In "Bu Bu Jing Xin," she plays the lead role Ruoxi, a modern white-collar worker who travels back to the Qing Dynasty to become a princess, experiencing the turmoil of the imperial court. From the first version of the "Bu Bu Jing Xin" trailer released earlier, it can be seen that Liu Shishi has made significant progress in the drama, and her brilliant performance is highly anticipated. Just entering her birth year, she has already starred in multiple major productions, with a very promising future ahead.
