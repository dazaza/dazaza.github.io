[Canghai Picture] Who is the man that cultivates the last wasteland in your heart?

by wswzjsgs on 2010-04-13 11:01:42

Who is the man that cultivates the last wasteland in your heart? Some people say that every woman has a primitive wasteland in her heart, which may never be cultivated by a suitable man throughout her life. Her father, son, husband, lover, and friend cannot enter this land. Only one person has the fortune to cultivate this land - a platonic male friend. If a woman happens to meet such a person, she should cherish him well.

He is not your husband or lover, but the person hidden in your spiritual realm. He is not necessarily handsome, rich, or of high status, nor does he have to be older than you, but he is mature and wise. Every woman inherently wants to have such a person. This person gives you a sense of security without disrupting your family; he is an ordinary man who does not compete with your husband. He will hold your hand when you are lost and hand you over to your husband when you can see the way clearly. This person is not a god; he is just an ordinary but great person.

He lacks the arrogance and neglect of a husband and the greed and desire of a lover. He has the broad chest of a man and the tender heart of a hero. You discuss life and trivial matters with him, talk about ideals and future with him. You don't need to share each other's lives in hardship, yet you endlessly confide in him. He always silently listens to your heart no matter when.

He is the man who understands you most besides your husband. Sometimes, there are things you cannot say to your husband but can tell him. Your inner stories can be shared with him. With this person, you feel as if you have a psychologist, an additional spiritual diary. He is like a punching bag that can hold all your good and bad feelings, and like an air conditioner that gives you warm wind and then cool wind.

When you are troubled, he is your most loyal listener and your most genuine friend. He won't distance himself from you because of your incessant chatter, nor will he look down on you because of your irrational behavior. He will tell you the best way to solve problems, offer strategies for you, and accompany you out of the shadow. When you are happy, he will fade out of your sight and quietly think about your happiness. He is truly a meaningful friend in your life!

In this lifetime, everyone will encounter one or several very special people. These people might be your pure spiritual寄托s, and they cannot simply be categorized as friends because your relationship with them exceeds the boundaries and concepts of general friends. Yet, you and he have never had the thought or specific actions to elevate it to love, and you have not even held hands.

The emotion between you and him, that extraordinary friendship, cannot be simply classified as the fourth kind of feeling outside of love. It lies between friendship and love. Perhaps you place it above friendship and love, or perhaps in your heart, it is a sentiment deeper and richer than friendship and love.

He might gently pat your shoulder because of your sadness, hold your hand because of your fear, or embrace you when you cry, but it stops there. Maybe he is a romantic and sentimental man in daily life, but in front of you, he will not do anything inappropriate. You two become intimate in jokes and virtualize emotions in jokes. He is the person who doesn't overly care about your words and deeds or your appearance. He is the person who can transcend your surface and enter your inner being.

You will quietly think of him, silently miss him, hide him in your heart, and keep him in your spiritual home. He always stays in your dreams. Meeting him means your loneliness and solitude have a place to rest. He is always your emotional hotline. When you encounter happiness, you want to tell him first. When you encounter pain, you also think of him because he is the only man you want to confide in. You even hope he can stay by your side, giving you sincere comfort and encouragement, making your mood gradually improve.

Perhaps as time goes by, you get used to thinking of him every day and contacting him every day. Sometimes, you dare not guarantee that your friendship will heat up or your feelings will change, fearing that the component of love will exceed friendship. Whenever at this moment, when your feelings are about to ignite, the smart and rational he will pour cold water on you, helping you maintain a calm mind because he does not want both of you to fall into the abyss of love and be unable to extricate yourselves. Because he knows: "Only friends can last a lifetime, lovers only for a short while."

To have such a friend should be the most beautiful scenery in life, a wealth that is difficult to measure with money. Keeping a distance and interacting purely, this friendship will become more lasting. It is destined that there will be no story of love in this life. However, because of having such a man, you love your life more and cherish your life. Also, because of his existence, your life gains a luster. In the future, you will slowly grow old, and in your heart, there will be endless memories and reveries...
