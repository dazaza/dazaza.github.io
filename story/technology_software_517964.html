Traffic Police Team Annual Work Summary - Strengthening Traffic Safety Management, Serving the Construction of a Well-off Society

Since the establishment of #County, the county's economy has rapidly developed, traffic conditions have continuously improved, and the number of motor vehicles has quickly increased, growing from over 100 in 1985 to more than 4,000 today. Accompanying the development process of #County, as the government's road traffic safety management functional department, the County Public Security Traffic Police Team firmly established the new management concept that "ensuring safety is the greatest stability, preventing accidents is the best development, and fewer casualties are the most practical service for the people." We comply with and serve the overall situation of #County's economic and social development, act as good advisors, provide excellent services, maintain a good image, play the leading role well, and create first-class achievements.

1. Exert Functions, Strengthen Safety Management

Road traffic safety connects with all industries and thousands of households. Over the past 20 years, the County Public Security Traffic Police Team has taken "ensuring smoothness, ensuring safety, preventing accidents, and reducing casualties" as its goal, transforming from a single compulsory management type to a comprehensive service type, improving management levels, and enhancing safety management capabilities.

- Exert functions and strengthen driver management. Carefully organize driver training and exams, effectively manage rule-violating drivers by retraining them on traffic regulations, re-educating them on professional ethics, retraining them on operational skills, and redeveloping their psychological abilities, thereby enhancing their cultural traffic safety awareness.

- Actively serve and strengthen vehicle management. In response to the reality of our county having many mountains, wide areas, and inconvenient transportation, extend the registration, insurance, annual inspection, transfer, and management of illegal and scrap vehicles to urban and rural areas. Provide services to the people in the countryside for vehicle registration and inspection procedures, greatly saving farmers' time and accommodation expenses when traveling to the city, receiving praise from grassroots officials and the public.

- Strengthen road management. Change previous sporadic and intermittent management into 24-hour continuous management. Patrol important sections and intersections, investigate key traffic violations, prevent and curb traffic accidents, protect and reduce the loss of life and property of the masses. Increase road patrol intensity, frequently go on patrol, conduct inspections, ask questions, manage while discovering problems, establish rules and regulations, and build a long-term mechanism for road safety management.

2. Highlight Key Points, Improve Passenger Transport Safety Contact System

With the comprehensive launch of building a well-off society and the deepening development of reform and opening up, the "people, vehicles, roads" situation continues to change. To highlight the focus of traffic safety management and effectively alleviate the contradictions between "people, vehicles, roads," we firmly grasp the main line of passenger safety and have introduced three measures for managing passenger vehicles, achieving governance results where no major traffic accidents involving passenger vehicles or serious traffic accidents caused by passenger drivers have occurred within our jurisdiction.

- Expand contact scope, enhance publicity and education, establish a social supervision and management mechanism.

- Coordinate and cooperate, jointly manage. With traffic police management as the main focus, departments such as transportation, highways, and public security cooperate to track and manage drivers' driving skills, psychological qualities, safety awareness, and vehicle conditions.

- Establish special management files for passenger vehicles. Regularly inspect the safety technical condition of passenger vehicles, set up safety technical files for passenger vehicles and violation files for passenger vehicle drivers, and regularly conduct safety publicity and education to improve the traffic regulation awareness of the driver team, forming a law-abiding traffic atmosphere. Collaborate with transport companies to establish a violation penalty mechanism, enhancing drivers' vigilance and self-awareness, focusing on source management, and promptly eliminating traffic accident hazards.

3. Remember Responsibility Awareness, Establish a Good Image

The relatively lax legal environment, issues of not managing strictly enough or not managing appropriately, are currently prominent problems in road traffic safety law enforcement. Therefore, we earnestly strengthen the legal administrative awareness and responsibility awareness education of the police officers.

- Uphold people-oriented principles, recognize the sacred duties of public security traffic police from the perspective of cherishing human life, organically combine strict law enforcement with enthusiastic service, enhance a sense of mission and responsibility.

- Improve management systems. Persist in using systems to manage people, and through evaluation systems, start from places where the public is satisfied, start reforms from places where the public is dissatisfied, and address issues strongly reflected by cadres, truly providing the best service to the masses.

- Strengthen law enforcement methods. Strictly manage traffic safety, increase penalties for violations that disrupt traffic order, completely changing the result of reduced legal authority due to reasons such as "power intervention," "family ties and friendship," "profit-driven motives," and "arbitrary law enforcement," ensuring orders are followed and prohibitions observed, achieving the goal of ensuring safety.

- Increase publicity efforts and create a favorable public opinion atmosphere. Traffic safety depends on the improvement of the entire population's traffic legal awareness and safety consciousness, which is the fundamental solution. Over the past 20 years, we have increased publicity efforts, striving to ensure that road safety work is covered in newspapers, heard on the radio, and seen on television, creating an excellent public opinion atmosphere.

4. Keep Pace with the Times, Forge Ahead into the Future

The comprehensive construction of a well-off society proposed at the 16th National Congress of the Communist Party of China benefits billions of people, with the goal of "making the lives of the masses more prosperous," marked by the rapid growth of people flow, logistics, and information flow, representing higher levels of economic development and social progress. This brings challenges but also historical opportunities for road traffic safety management. Safety is a fundamental need and survival right of people, and a prosperous well-off life requires reliable safety guarantees. If travel is not safe, achieving a well-off life would be meaningless. Regarding this, our traffic police department has a relatively clear understanding and will, in future work, do a good job in planning traffic safety facilities for the first 20 years of the new century, actively offering advice, playing the role of government advisors well, striving to achieve paperless offices, computer terminalization of vehicle management, informatization of driver management, and computer simulation of personnel training, working hard to create a civilized and smooth traffic environment for the whole county, shaping a good traffic atmosphere where everyone follows traffic rules, and safeguarding the economic construction of #County.
