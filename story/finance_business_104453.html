How far can the entrepreneurial dream of setting up shop on grids go?

by rodari on 2008-11-18 16:59:54

In the past two months, a “cell shop” which has already become popular in the eastern coastal areas swept through Guiyang. In the bustling commercial districts of Yawu Street, Da Cross and the Normal University in Guiyang City, a group of “cell shops” that are being decorated or have already opened enthusiastically have replaced the former shoe stores, food stores and clothing stores to become the favored objects of an enterprising group.

The "Cell Shop" is the first choice for college students to start their own business.

The prototype of the "cell shop" was the consignment cabinet which arose in Japan five or six years ago. It was originally used to display consignors' collected second-hand toys. Subsequently, the management model of the consignment cabinet was introduced into Hong Kong, where it evolved into what we now see as the "cell shop" after its content was continuously enriched and its management model updated. Because of its advantages such as low rent, low cost of starting a business, small risk, and the owner selling and managing on behalf of the tenant, etc., the "cell shop" has become the first choice for many college students to start their own business after it was introduced into Guiyang.

Zhao Hong is a student at Guizhou Normal University. Not long ago she rented a cell at a nearby "cell shop" for 200 yuan, mainly selling ornaments and beauty tools. Her profit in one month reached 220 yuan. Zhao Hong said: "Compared with renting out a store for tens of thousands of yuan to start your own business, the cost and risks of starting a 'cell shop' business are both relatively low, belonging to the range that most people can easily accept. If I cannot find a job smoothly after graduation, I will take the 'cell shop' as my business platform."

According to the owner of "Seven Treasure Cell Shop", currently a large part of the cell operators are college students who hope to try their hand, getting a space to show their personal charm for not much money each month, having a go at being a boss, and many people start to explore operating experience with a trying-it-out mentality.

When reporters interviewed at Guizhou University, Guizhou Normal University, Guiyang Medical College and other institutions of higher learning, they learned that under the current situation of fierce employment competition and increasing employment pressure, the job selection view of most graduates has changed from the past "hurrying up and finding a job" to the present "starting business positively". However, high-cost businesses let many college graduates be frightened away. As a cheap business platform, "cell shops" got the favor of most college students immediately upon entering Guiyang.

Low-cost operation models are springing up everywhere.
