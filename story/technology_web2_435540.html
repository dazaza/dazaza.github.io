### Cat Stretch

**Benefits:** Makes the spine flexible, reduces fat around the waist, beautifies the buttocks, enhances abdominal circulation, alleviates back pain. Treats dysmenorrhea and corrects irregular menstruation.

**Benefits:** Relieves physical fatigue, removes excess fat from the shoulders. Corrects hunchback and rounded shoulders, beautifies shoulder lines. Improves constipation.

**Tips:** Keep your feet straight and try to make your abdomen, chest, and forehead touch your legs as much as possible.
