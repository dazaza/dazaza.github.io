FeiTian Design Institute also freely wings according to market demand

by siji on 2011-11-10 10:51:30

In February this year, the modification test flight of the land type of A2C aircraft was successfully completed. The land type of A2C not only continues the characteristics of small size, light weight and high safety factor of seaplanes, but also can take off and land on land, expanding the applicable fields. On November 7, the staff installed the main reducer for an AC311 helicopter. Since the first phase of the Tianjin base of AVIC Helicopter Co., Ltd. was built and put into use for more than a year, nearly 10 helicopters have accumulated offline, and it is expected that more than 20 helicopters of various types will be produced next year. By 2015, AVIC Helicopter will achieve an annual production of more than 300 helicopters of various types and become one of the world's major helicopter suppliers.

Reporters learned recently that the main body of the Shijiazhuang General Aviation Industry Glider Base has been completed, and the relocation of AVIC Shijiazhuang Aircraft Industry Co., Ltd. is imminent.

The base is located in Luancheng County, with a total investment of 2.3 billion yuan. In addition to the construction of 130,000 square meters of new factory buildings, it will also build a 2B-level airport with a runway length of 1,200 meters. After its completion, it will become a large-scale aviation industry base with the development and production of whole machines as the leader, with the ability to repair high-speed helicopters, produce parts and special flight vehicles, and train pilots and maintenance personnel.

On November 7, reporters learned at the third media day held by AVIC Helicopter Company in Beijing that the opening up of low-altitude airspace has brought unprecedented development opportunities to the civilian helicopter market. According to the unified planning of AVIC Helicopter Corporation, Harbin Aircraft Industry Group Co., Ltd. of AVIC Industry has locked its R&D focus on medium-sized civilian helicopters of 4 tons, 6 tons, etc., and production will continue to increase. Next, Hafei will create an aviation supporting industry circle in Ha Nan Industrial New City. According to Tan Ruison, the second secretary of the Party Leadership Group and vice president of AVIC Industry, currently, the world's civilian helicopter fleet is more than 30,000, while China has only more than 200. To make the number of civilian helicopters reach the world average level, China still has a gap of 6,000. In order to fill this market blank, AVIC Industry will be committed to developing a series of products with a take-off weight of 1 ton, 1.5 tons, 3 tons, 4 tons, 6 tons, 8 tons, 13 tons and even larger.

As the first helicopter manufacturing enterprise established in New China, Hafei has successively developed and produced a variety of helicopter products such as Zhiwu, Zhiu, and Zhiu. Its Zhiu helicopter has had a glorious history of 30 years, and has been exported to Kenya, Zambia, Namibia and other countries. In recent years, the company has independently developed the civil 4-ton AC312. This aircraft is a twin-engine medium multi-purpose helicopter with a maximum take-off weight of 4250 kg, widely used in public security, fire fighting and other quasi-military markets; the AC352 helicopter jointly developed and manufactured by both Chinese and French parties fills the domestic 6-7 ton helicopter field, and will be mass-produced in 2013.
