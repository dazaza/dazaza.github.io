Vol.02 Turns out he's "NO.2 Xuan" The desperate scream of the "no" girl pierced through the clouds, echoing endlessly over the entire "San Xuan" expressway bridge. Only now did the "rice worms" snap out of their daze, frantically taking photos of this intimate moment. The boy stepped back and gave me a casual glance, as if what had just happened was the most normal and natural thing in the world: "I hate apple-flavored lip gloss. Remember to switch to lychee next time." My eyes widened... Goodness gracious, what is this guy talking about?! T_T|| This was my first kiss, Belulu's! How dare he steal it without my permission and then criticize the taste of my lip gloss so arrogantly! "Xianze, have you decided to date her? Xianze, you're really going to leave me behind, aren't you?!" The girl flailed her hands in the air, trying desperately to grab onto the boy, but after a while, she grabbed nothing. "I like you, I like you, I like you... I like you!" The paper airplane swayed and fell in the cold wind, lonely like a lost child. The boy stood up straight and lazily glanced at the girl: "Don't say you like me, it makes me want to vomit." These... these lines! A flash of white light instantly shot through my mind. No wonder his name sounded so familiar, turns out he's Li Xianze, the "NO.2 Xuan" of "San Xuan Wang Cheng"! How could I be so stupid? I saw this name frequently last night when I was browsing the "San Xuan Wang Cheng" website. I... *^_^* I actually got kissed by the "NO.2 Xuan" of "San Xuan Wang Cheng", how blissful... The lips that were kissed still retained the boy's unique faint fragrance, as if branded with an indelible mark. Hold on! You idiot Belulu! Now's not the time to feel happy! Don't forget the purpose of your transfer to "San Xuan Wang Cheng" Academy, you bear such a heavy responsibility, how can you possibly daydream here?! And don't forget, you like Yin Luo, Yin Luo whom you've secretly loved for ten whole years! Damn it, what should I do! I'm going crazy! After saying those words, the boy directly entered the blue-black "Bugatti Veyron". Before the "rice worms" even realized it, he had already started the engine. At the same time, the girl screamed "Li Xianze, I will always always like you" and jumped off the helicopter, plummeting towards the river like a bullet. The "rice worms" and I froze. The helicopter pilot screamed in shock. But the black-blue "Bugatti Veyron" didn't stop, resolutely and ruthlessly driving out of the "San Xuan" expressway bridge, leaving a group of ants anxiously running around on the bridge. In an instant, shouts, cries for help, and car horns all rang out together. As expected, this year I am cursed by Tai Sui. Just transferred to "San Xuan Wang Cheng" and I encountered such an unlucky event. I gritted my teeth and drove my car out of the "San Xuan" expressway bridge, unwilling to deal with the chaos behind me. There's no way out, with my terrible luck, if I don't run away now, I'd be an idiot! Three minutes later, I drove my pink princess car shaped like a rabbit to the gate of "San Xuan Wang Cheng". The tall wall made of thick black glass, the majestic pillar made of colored glass, the fountain and swan statue made of white glass, plus the blue school plaque shining with a cold light above... The entire school was like a crystal palace carved from silver and jade. And beside the school gate, there was a "San Xuan Fengxing Edition" made of transparent glass, which automatically played the latest and coolest information rankings. "San Xuan Wang Cheng" Academy has three "Xuans": "NO.1 Xuan" is Zuo Ge from the sports department - ruthless and almost brutal, revered by the girls of "San Xuan Wang Cheng" Academy as a god. He holds 101 girlfriend candidate badges, of which 100 have already been distributed. In his world, there is no compassion or pure love. Girlfriends only mean pets, vases, servants... Catchphrase: When you fall, do you want to sit, lie down, or crawl? "NO.2 Xuan" is Li Xianze from the art department - a beautiful boy like a stormy wind and a shaking earth, stunningly handsome. He can kiss any girl who doesn't like him, but he cannot accept the true feelings of a girl who does like him; he can chat happily with the ugliest girl in the world, but he cannot give the prettiest girl in the world the love she wants. Catchphrase: Don't say you like me, it makes me want to vomit. "NO.3 Xuan" is Yin Luo from the academic department - a genius youth with an IQ of two hundred, a myth in the hearts of "San Xuan Wang Cheng" girls and a heavenly son in the hearts of boys. Champion of recent international Olympiads, physics competitions, and chemistry competitions. Every one of his innovations participates in international competitions. It's said he's busier than Bill Gates, accumulating less than a week of classes in a semester. Catchphrase: Give me a problem, if it can stump me for how long, I'll date you for that long. Alas... And the person I'm chasing in the photo is the "NO.1 Xuan" of "San Xuan Wang Cheng" - the notorious bad-tempered young master of the city, Zuo Ge! I've heard of his various misdeeds since the time at "William Castle", and yesterday when I was browsing the "San Xuan Wang Cheng" website and forum, I also saw many of his wicked deeds... Why am I Belulu so unfortunate, to have bumped right into him!～～～＞O＜～～～ This school is divided into three departments - the art department, the sports department, and the academic department. The east side is the school gate, the west side is the sports department, the south side is the academic department, and the north side is the art department! Skilled in piano, violin, harp, guzheng, guitar... everything except sports, I am a complete sports idiot. To conveniently chase Zuo Ge from the sports department, I had to hide all my artistic talents and asked my father to pull some strings to get me transferred to this department. Sob sob... I know nothing about sports! If someone finds out about my artistic talent, I will definitely be forced to transfer to the art department! Stop thinking! Stop thinking! Maybe things aren't as bad as they seem! Let's just go to class quickly, if we keep dragging on like this, school will be over soon.
