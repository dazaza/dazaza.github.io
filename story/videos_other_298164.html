After surveying 100 capital holders, we found that most capital holders in Chengdu prefer catering and franchise chain projects. In addition, patent technology inventions, energy, tourism and leisure projects are also very popular among investors.

The survey shows that among 100 investors with an average capital holding of 500,000 yuan, nearly 46% chose the catering industry as their first investment choice. Mr. Wang, one of the surveyed individuals, stated: "The cost and investment in the catering industry are relatively clear, it's easier to estimate how much profit can be made and whether there is a risk of loss. Moreover, the catering industry in Chengdu is also a relatively booming investment sector."

If investing in the catering industry is to cater to local living characteristics and habits, then those who choose to invest in chain franchise industries value the relatively easier-to-control risks and returns in this industry. According to the survey data, the chain franchise industry ranks second only to the catering industry, with a selection rate of 38%.

In addition, patent technology invention projects and energy projects, which have high technical content and professional degree, did not encounter the expected "neglect", on the contrary, they were exceptionally popular. About 32% of investors have been optimistic about high-tech invention projects in recent days. "High-tech projects have a good prospect, and the fields utilizing technology will definitely increase in the future. As long as there is a development prospect, I will consider investing," said Mr. Yu, who plans to invest 10 million yuan in selecting projects on "Pair Pair".

Entrepreneurial preference: Chengdu people like to start businesses independently

Among the options of partnering to run a business, independent entrepreneurship, or directly investing for dividends, which do you prefer? The survey shows that 54% of Chengdu investors chose "independent entrepreneurship."

"I don't know him (referring to the project party) well, so just investing money without participating in management is definitely not feasible. Therefore, starting my own business is more stable," said investor Mr. Zhang, expressing the true thoughts of many investors. After all, keeping money in one's own pocket is the safest.

The data shows that about 34% of entrepreneurs hope to "partner to run a business," while only 12% choose "directly investing for dividends" —— this has quite a gap compared to the expectations of many project parties. According to our survey of 20 project parties during the same period, about 60% of the project owners hope that the investors would just provide money without participating in management. It seems that both capital and projects need to show sincerity if they want to successfully "team up."
