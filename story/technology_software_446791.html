Traffic police whistle to make way and help the injured to see a doctor in time, and the doctor said that the patient arrived at the right time.

by zzf000zxye0 on 2011-06-16 10:23:47

Our reporter (intern Li Jing) - Yesterday, during the morning rush hour, under the relay escort of traffic police, Mr. Sun, a carpenter who had his right index finger tip sawed off, was quickly taken from Daxing's Jiugong to the Jishuitan Hospital for treatment. A staff member of the Traffic Management Bureau Command Center said that after receiving the injured person's emergency call, they immediately deployed patrol police forces with sirens guiding the way, and through a series of relays, safely delivered the injured person to the hospital. After surgery, the injured person left the hospital, fortunately without serious harm.

Yesterday morning, I met the injured young man, Mr. Sun, at Jishuitan Hospital. Mr. Sun stated that he works at a wooden door company in Jiugong. Around 8:30 AM yesterday, while working in the workshop, he accidentally injured his right index finger's first joint with an electric saw, "A lot of blood came out, and I was completely dazed." Afterwards, his colleague Mr. Wang drove him towards Jishuitan Hospital. However, as Mr. Wang was unfamiliar with the road conditions and unsure how to choose the fastest route during the morning rush hour, he urgently sought help from 122 (emergency number), hoping for assistance from the traffic police, "I could see the bones in Xiao Sun's fingers, and I thought we should go to Jishuitan Hospital, but there are too many small roads around there, and I was afraid of taking the wrong one and delaying his treatment."

According to the staff of the Traffic Management Bureau Command Center, at 9:14 AM yesterday, they received a call, where the caller stated that their car carried a young man with a severely cut finger who needed immediate treatment at Jishuitan Hospital. Subsequently, the command center deployed police forces in Fengtai, Xuanwu, and Xicheng districts. At 9:18 AM, the traffic police from Xuanwu district met the injured person at Caihuying Bridge, immediately turning on the police lights and sounding the siren to lead the way for the driver. At 9:24 AM, the responding officers from Xicheng Traffic Brigade took over the "relay baton" and escorted the injured person all the way to Jishuitan Hospital, completing the entire process in only 40 minutes.

From the hospital's emergency desk, I learned that the injured person is 24 years old and is a migrant worker from Hebei who came to Beijing for work. His attending doctor indicated that the backside flesh of the right index finger's first joint was sawed off, but the bone damage was not deep, "Fortunately, he arrived in time." Yesterday morning, after undergoing surgery, the injured person left the hospital.
