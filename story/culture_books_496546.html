Yiyi Liu encounters the wife of her superior in a car accident. At the same time, her superior leaves behind a suicide note saying he is "troubled by love" before committing suicide at home. Is all this a coincidence or a deliberate arrangement? Is she the woman who troubled her superior? She becomes the subject of much gossip in the company. Yiyi Liu takes over her superior's unfinished project and doesn't know that as the project progresses, she is step by step walking into a pre-arranged trap.

Her good friend Amy is worried that Yiyi Liu will become a leftover woman and actively introduces boyfriends to her. The well-mannered JiaDong Zhang enters the scene. His mother spares no effort to please Yiyi Liu, hoping she will marry into the Zhang family soon. However, he has an unknown secret.

In her melancholy and frustration, XinZhi Yang, whose advances she rejected, pursues her relentlessly. Are they karmic enemies from a previous life, or fated lovers in this one? Unable to resist, Yiyi Liu finally succumbs to XinZhi Yang and prepares for a peaceful life with him. However, just as the proverb says, when the tree wants to remain still, the wind won't stop blowing. Yiyi Liu discovers his shocking secret - she was just a bet for him.

Feeling utterly disheartened, a netizen plants roses for her in a foreign land. Where does her heart belong? Can XinZhi Yang win her heart back?

The faint coolness on your chest is the sorrow I cannot express.

How can I meet you again, at the time when I love you the most?
