SEO Single Page Optimization Practical Case: Application of Keyword Combination

by logncn on 2008-10-21 11:01:41

Lonmen recently operated a single-page optimization case, which is shared with everyone:

Project Background: A group's investment industrial real estate will be completed soon. They hope to rent out the factory as soon as possible after completion. Considering some of their own interests, the target customers are preferably light industrial production enterprises. In terms of cost, online promotion and search engine marketing are mainly used to fully utilize unlimited network resources to save enterprise recruitment costs.

Task: Before October 30th, achieve cooperation intentions with more than five effective enterprises.

Promotion methods:

1. Release of industry website information;

2. Regional comprehensive website advertising investment;

3. Information release on classified websites;

4. Search engine promotion;

Implementation Plan: Items 1-3 are omitted. Here we focus on item 4, search engine promotion.

1. Investigation of target user search habits.

In the actual project process, it is almost impossible to investigate the search habits of the target users for each specific project. We can only use the habits of a larger group to get a basic understanding.

Investigation data shows that 73% of potential users are accustomed to using combination keywords for queries.

Keywords used by users when searching through search engines

Single keyword 14%

Two-keyword combination 33%

Three-keyword combination 26%

Four-keyword combination 14%

More than four-keyword combination 9.6%

Based on the above analysis, we know that most people prefer to use multiple keywords for queries rather than single keywords. Therefore, this case will also focus on optimizing keyword combinations.

Additionally, since the target audience of this promotion consists of middle and upper-level managers in enterprises, GOOGLE, which has a higher-end image, will be the main target for promotion.

2. Competitor and industry-related website analysis

After observation, the keywords "factory for rent", "Chengdu factory for rent", and "Chengdu factory for rent" have too much competition, and the websites ranked at the top are all authoritative industry websites. It is impossible to surpass them in the short term, so we consider finding other relatively less competitive keywords.

3. Keyword selection

Using the GOOGLE keyword tool, a large number of related keywords were collected. Then, considering factors such as keyword combinations, competitiveness, and user habits, the keywords were selected.

The final keywords determined were: "Chengdu factory recruitment", "Chengdu factory recruitment", "Chengdu industrial factory for rent", "Chengdu industrial factory for rent", "Xindu factory for rent", "Xindu factory for rent", "Xindu industrial factory for rent", "Xindu industrial factory for rent".

It may seem like there is no focus and too many keywords are determined. In fact, here we fully considered the relevance, regional characteristics, and long-tail needs of the keywords: The multiple groups of keywords mentioned above can actually be categorized into just two categories: "factory for rent" and "factory recruitment", plus one industry term "industrial" and two regional terms "Chengdu" and "Xindu".

With this explanation, isn't it clear now? And you won't feel like there are too many!

4. Page design and production

Regarding page design, we will briefly go over the overall content and then analyze the writing method of the TITLE in detail.

Page production simply followed general SEO page production factors: Using second-level columns (**.com/**/); Reasonable use of H1 and H2 tags with keyword combinations; Optimizing keywords at the beginning and end of the webpage; Flexible use of STRONG tags to enhance effects; Anchor text optimization of internal links on the same page; High-quality outbound links. That’s basically it.
