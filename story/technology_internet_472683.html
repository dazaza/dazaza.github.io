Toys for children in the 0-7 years development stage

by diujiaskxc on 2011-08-08 16:21:11

This stage is not only a period of physiological development, but also a period of psychological development, and even more so a phase for the exploration of a child's intelligence. If parents spend more time playing with their children, they can better tap into the child’s potential. Physiological development is something that parents do not easily neglect, because most ensure their children are well-fed and warmly dressed. However, many parents overlook the intellectual and psychological development of their children.

Many parents are also concerned about their child's intellectual development. Sometimes, they know to buy some toys for their children to play with, as well as some early education books for them to read. There is no denying that this is correct. However, many parents do not know what kind of toys to buy for their children at different ages to suit their intellectual development stage. A good toy can make a child happy, bring endless joy to their childhood, and stimulate their intelligence. Let your child's IQ be one level higher than other children. Since parents do not know what toys to choose for their children at different ages, I have summarized suitable toys for different age stages.

### Two: Toys Suitable for 2-3 Year Old Children

Children aged 2-3 years are in the sensitive period for organ coordination, muscle development, and interest in objects. It is also a time when actions improve, time and space concepts strengthen, sensory accuracy becomes more refined, and it is the sensitive period for learning a second language. This is a critical period for personality development and the development of receptive thinking and various senses. Games at this stage are very important. You can choose from the following types of toys:

1. Puzzles, play dough, stickers, colored books, colored pencils, finger painting tools, cardboard books, musical instruments, various animal-shaped plush toys, toy dishes, toy furniture, small buckets, shovels, funnels, small watering cans, etc.

2. Simple puzzles, sets of small boxes, interlocking toys, medium and small building blocks, various toy vehicles such as cars, trucks, tricycles, ambulances, remote control planes, electric trains, rings, etc.

3. **Riding toys**: Through riding tricycles, leg strength is exercised. Tricycles are one of the best toys for children, as they can exercise the body and coordinate hand-eye-foot movements, improving balance and control.

4. **Various balls**: Playing with balls exercises the whole body. Starting at one year old, children can begin playing with small rubber balls, hitting them with their hands. Then they can move on to small volleyball, soccer balls, and badminton, hitting them with their hands or kicking them with their feet. After two and a half years old, they can play with standard junior soccer balls, running and kicking, exercising all parts of the body while promoting coordinated movement.

5. **Building blocks**: During the process of playing with building blocks, children recognize shapes and learn how to classify correctly. This enhances their thinking abilities and promotes intellectual development. They use blocks to assemble cubes, rectangular prisms, and "buildings." First, they sort the blocks by size and length, then by color (red, yellow, blue, green), pairing them up. They can also combine multiple criteria like size, length, and color for more complex classification and sequencing games. In the end, they can create various types of cubes, rectangular prisms, triangular prisms, pyramids, cones, and building clusters of different sizes and colors.

Recommend: *Authentic 55-piece Garden City Wooden Building Blocks - Educational Toy for Children*

6. **Toy telephone**: While playing with a toy phone, children can learn basic phone techniques and polite phrases. From one and a half to two years old, children can hold the phone themselves, press several numbers continuously, and say: "Hello... Hello... Goodbye!" By the age of two and a half, with adult help, they can call mom.

7. **Stacking cups**: For a child nearing two years old, stacking cups offer endless variations in play. They can be stacked into tall towers, collapsed into a single cup, or used to hide small blocks or other small items before searching for them again. Through these games, children understand that although some things cannot be seen, they still exist.

8. **Toy cars**: By the age of two, toddlers can generally control their body movements and can drive small toy cars. If the car can carry some of their small toys and they can act as transport drivers, the enjoyment is limitless.

9. **Pull-along animal toys**: Toddlers will be fascinated by pull-along animals. As they pull, they gradually understand the pulling power of the rope, which has more educational value than electric toy cars.

10. **Electric toys**: While playing with electric toys, children can learn about police sirens, tiger roars, etc. They can also change batteries themselves, gaining an initial understanding of how electricity generates light, heat, sound, and force.

11. **Early education machines for children**: Emphasizing learning through play. Touch-screen operation makes it easy for children to master; audio-visual interfaces stimulate their enthusiasm for continuous learning. Features like "storytelling," "reciting Tang poetry," and "playing games" provide a treasure trove of knowledge, allowing children to achieve early intellectual development effortlessly.

Recommend: *I Love Lyrics Sing-Alongs for Children 2011 New Edition - Songs, Tang Poems, Song Ci, Stories, Six-One Gifts*

12. **Dominoes**: Known as a fun toy that combines manual dexterity and mental stimulation. The "fixed front and back, freely placed middle" pattern effectively cultivates children's innovative awareness and imagination. The game rule of "one wrong move, everything is lost" maximizes the cultivation of patience in children. It helps children realize the meaning of "success comes step by step" and allows them to experience the joy of success after hundreds of failures.
