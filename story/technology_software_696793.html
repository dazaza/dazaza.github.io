Handmade cloth shoes: Qingdao foreign trade export defies the trend, and many enterprises actively "focus on" Qingdao Fabric & Accessories Fair.

by uyahd9mjik on 2012-03-08 10:47:34

It was learned the other day that in the past year of 2011, Qingdao's total import and export volume reached as high as 71.263 billion US dollars, among which the trade volume with Japan, Korea and other countries has increased significantly compared to previous years, reaching 8.915 billion US dollars and 8.664 billion US dollars respectively. This marks that Qingdao's foreign trade has once again developed to a new level. Under the current situation where the textile exports of most regions and cities are not prosperous (mainly focusing on domestic demand), Qingdao's foreign trade import and export market has shown a trend of reverse growth. Relevant industries are also being fully mobilized. Many clothing enterprises are closely focusing on the 2012 Qingdao Fabric and Yarn Exhibition platform, aiming to "dig another pot of gold".

Positioned as "become stronger and bigger, assist enterprises in pursuing win-win, and enhance market competitiveness", the "14th China (Qingdao) International Fabric, Accessories and Yarn Expo 2012" has long been the preferred platform for many enterprises to develop foreign trade and open up the market. In addition, places like Jimo Clothing Base in Qingdao were initially recognized as the first batch of national foreign trade transformation and upgrading demonstration bases last year. Qingdao's position as an import and export city is becoming more prominent. The Qingdao Fabric and Accessories Show, which continues to adapt to the situation, may become a new arena for more exhibitors to pursue profits and gain market share by leveraging new development opportunities. The exhibition will be grandly held at the Qingdao International Convention Center from June 26-28, 2012. During the exhibition, four exhibition halls will be opened, with an exhibition area exceeding ten thousand square meters and over a thousand booths. There will also be highlights such as "One-on-One Trade Negotiation", "2013 Spring/Summer Trend Release", "Shandong-Henan, Shandong-Anhui Industry对接Conference", "Industry Summit Forum", "Seven Province Textile and Apparel Industry对接Friendship Meeting", which will bring a new wave of transaction climax for exhibitors and audiences. It will also create a platform for the industry to fully utilize the development space of the Qingdao market.

So far, more than 300 enterprises have successively joined the "2012 China (Qingdao) International Fabric, Accessories and Yarn Exhibition", using it as an effective platform to expand business opportunities, and continuously optimizing their own export structure to increase export volume. Among them, the Lihua Group has set up branches in the United States and Japan, and its sales terminals in Europe, America, and Japan have reached 15, effectively optimizing the order receiving method, enhancing the ability to resist risks, and promoting the healthy model of economic development. "We have developed in Qingdao through the Qingdao Fabric Show in recent years. Our export volume and development speed in 2011 were the largest and fastest compared to previous years. Now, the market potential and space are very large, which will create a huge growth space for us in 2012. We are fully confident about this exhibition!" said an exhibitor from a clothing enterprise who registered for the 2012 Qingdao Fabric Show to the reporter.

For more details about the exhibition, please consult the organizer Qingdao Haiming International Exhibition Co., Ltd. or visit the official website.
