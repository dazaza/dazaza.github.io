Thank you for your love.

by gege on 2012-01-03 00:29:01

Thank you for your love

Thank you for your love and hurt,

It made me realize that one must go through trials to achieve perfection,

Even the pain and hardships we once could not bear,

Are not completely worthless,

They can make us stronger in will and more mature in thought,

Because of the real heartache and pain, there is tolerance and forgiveness,

From now on, I cherish love even more,

Thank you for hurting me:

Without your hurt, I would still be living in a fairy tale,

I would still think that as long as I try, there is nothing I cannot do, including love,

Thank you for hurting me:

Without your hurt, I would still think that as long as two people truly love each other, understand and forgive each other carefully,

All pain can be avoided,

Thank you for loving me:

It let me truly know what it feels like to love someone, miss someone, and feel heartbreak!

Thank you for hurting me:

It left a permanent scar in my heart,

It hurts every time it rains,

Thank you for your hurt and your love:

It made me grow up, mature, and have a more rational understanding of love!

I deeply feel that I can no longer get the kind of feelings I want,

Because I have become realistic,

Thank you for hurting me:

It made me believe that I can really not care about everything about the person I love,

I fulfilled my promise,

Thank you for loving me:

It made me know that I can really go through pain for someone,

Thank you for loving me:

Every time I see someone else's action, I think of you,

When I see the clothes you bought for me, tears flow naturally,

I remember your beautiful figure in the mall, telling me about the spoils of war when we went shopping,

Your pleasant voice keeps ringing in my ears,

Every time, I hold back tears and quietly leave the room! Because I will keep these memories slowly,

Thank you for hurting me;

Although you have hurt me,

I still thank you,

You made me strong, calm in dealing with things, and more aware of my own strength,

Thank you for your hurt and love:

I don't want to leave here anymore,

I am willing to live here with your love and hurt, recalling our love from time to time, looking at the room where we once left our shadows, letting me spend the rest of my life in fantasy, and when you are hurt outside, there is still a safe haven for you here!
