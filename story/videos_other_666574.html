Golden Title Topic - The Golden Rule for Creating High Conversion Rate Titles (Part 3) On how to create the most lethal title, through successful ad titles, to increase your internet marketing effectiveness at a geometric rate. We have concluded that in order to create effective ad titles, you must make your ad content reflect the "attack the heart" effect. That is, you must effectively stimulate the inner needs of your potential customers. To quickly move and stimulate customers, you must approach from two aspects: human weaknesses: 1. Positive, proactive guidance. 2. Negative, negative stimulation! Because people always have a mentality of seeking good and avoiding harm! Therefore, we have summarized the following golden rules for creating "heart-attack ad titles," and we will analyze in-depth how these "title golden rules" play an important role in so many excellent titles. You will imagine how its charm will help you attract readers to read the main body of the ad.

1. A good title can explain how the reader, listener, audience, or potential on-site sales customer can save, win, or complete beneficial work by using your product - how it enhances his/her mental, physical, financial, social, emotional, or spiritual encouragement, satisfaction, peace, or security. In short, a good title can highlight the greatest "advantage" provided by the product for potential sales customers.

Note: This is positive proactive guidance. Because people always hope to gain more benefits, interests, results, advantages, happiness, or value, even achieving effortless gains, dreams coming true, wishes fulfilled, etc., in life, behavior, work, or business, and even in their network of relationships.

2. Intentionally use some derogatory words in the title, using these negatively charged words to remind readers or potential sales customers what risks, worries, losses, mistakes, embarrassments, hard work, or other unpleasant situations they will "avoid," "reduce," or "eliminate" after using your product or service. Or how to reduce your potential customers' or clients' concerns about poverty, illness, accidents, discomfort, boredom, etc., and/or business losses, social prestige or advantage, success, prosperity, abundance, or wealth losses.

Note: This is negative negative stimulation! Because people always hope to avoid pain, disasters, dissatisfaction, frustration, mediocrity, and unpleasant things encountered in life or persistently.

When advertising any product or service, remember the following points when building a title or opening statement:

Customers do not buy a product or service. Because customers focus on what they can obtain after paying money, they care about the result rather than the process. They are all result-oriented. So what they buy will be a result, benefit, advantage, Baidu bidding optimization, or a form of protection, or an increase in happiness, etc., all of which your product or service, company can provide them. Typically, titles focus on the benefits or specific effects that potential customers will receive.

How to make the title vivid and effective

After revising and improving the title to enhance its strong attraction and effectiveness, the released ad's effectiveness will increase several times over.
