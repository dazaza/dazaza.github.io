Living on Each Other Summary: Living on Each Other We are brother and sister without blood relations, destined to meet in a bloody massacre. As an orphan, what I desire most is family affection. In this world, the person I love most is my brother, and the person I care about is only my brother. Whether it's family affection or love, I can no longer distinguish. What I also cannot see through are the grudges and entanglements between the wealthy families. Hitmen, living lives devoid of emotion, but deep in my heart, there remains an indescribable sentiment. We live on each other.
