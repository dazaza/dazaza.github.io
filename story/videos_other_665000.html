AC Milan arrived in Dubai for winter training on December 28th. Two years ago, Milan defeated Hamburg on penalties to win this competition's championship. Due to an upcoming league match, Allegri did not field his strongest lineup but aimed to help some players regain form. Cassano wore the number 99 jersey, making his debut for Milan alongside the recovered Pato in attack, with Seedorf playing as the attacking midfielder. Boatin returned to Italy for injury treatment, and Thiago Silva played as the defensive midfielder. Al Ahly is currently ranked third in their domestic league, with former Juventus captain Cannavaro leading the team.

At 0:00 Beijing Time on January 3 (20:00 local time on January 2 in the UAE), the 4th edition of the Dubai "Emir Challenge Cup" took place at Rashid Stadium, with AC Milan defeating home team Al Ahly 2-1. Cassano made his debut, assisting Seedorf for the opening goal. In the second half, Montolongo also made his Milan debut, and young Beretta scored with a header following a pass from Robinho. Hassan reduced the deficit with a long-range strike, and Robinho suffered an unusual injury after colliding with a camera.

In the second half, Allegri almost changed the entire lineup, keeping only Odon, who switched to play as the left-back. Montolongo made his first appearance at right-back, and Ibrahimoivc, suspended for the midweek league game, played as the attacking midfielder, with young Beretta leading the line and Robinho supporting him. In the 52nd minute, Beretta turned to escape a defender and delivered a left-footed shot from six meters out on the left side, which Sayyif Yusuf managed to save. In the 63rd minute, Ambrosini broke through on the left and delivered a timely low pass to the right, where Montolongo entered the penalty area but was blocked by a defender.
