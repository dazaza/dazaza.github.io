What are the benefits of watermelon for the human body?

by uefbp54187 on 2012-02-27 14:47:48

Watermelon:

【Family】The fruit of the watermelon plant from the Cucurbitaceae family.

【Other Names】Cold melon, summer melon, water melon.

【Properties and Meridians】Cold in nature, sweet in taste; enters the Heart, Stomach, and Bladder meridians.

【Efficacy and Indications】Clears heat and relieves summer-heat, removes irritability and quenches thirst, promotes urination. Treats summer-heat syndrome, difficulty in urination, sore throat, mouth ulcers, wind-fire toothache, heat disease with irritability and thirst, as well as urinary tract infections and hypertension.

【Nutritional Components】Every 100 grams of watermelon flesh contains: 94.1% water, 1.2g protein, 4.2g carbohydrates, 0.3mg crude fiber, 0.2g ash, 6mg calcium, 10mg phosphorus, 0.2mg iron, 0.17mg carotene, 0.02mg thiamine, 0.02mg riboflavin, 0.2mg niacin, 3mg ascorbic acid. In addition, it also contains various amino acids such as citrulline, arginine, alanine, gamma-aminobutyric acid, glutamic acid, as well as phosphate, malic acid, ethylene glycol, etc.

【Therapeutic Effects】

1. Heat-clearing and Summer-heat Relieving: Watermelon contains large amounts of water, multiple amino acids, and sugar which can effectively replenish the body's water content, preventing dehydration-induced heatstroke. At the same time, watermelon can promote diuresis to expel excess heat from the body, achieving a cooling effect.

2. Nutrient Supplementation: Among all fruits, watermelon has the richest juice content, almost encompassing all the nutrients required by the human body, highly beneficial for health.

3. Beauty and Anti-aging: Watermelon juice acts like a body cleaner, eliminating metabolic waste products, cleaning the kidneys and urinary ducts. It also activates cells, achieving beauty and anti-aging effects.

4. Aids Protein Absorption: Modern research shows that watermelon juice contains protease, which can convert insoluble proteins into soluble ones, aiding in the absorption of proteins by the body.

5. Diuretic and Hypotensive, Treating Nephritis: Citrulline and arginine in watermelons can enhance the formation of urea in rats' livers, leading to diuresis. The glycosides in watermelons also have diuretic and hypotensive effects. Watermelons contain small amounts of salts that have special therapeutic effects on nephritis.

6. Disease Prevention: The green rind of watermelons is very nutritious, containing glucose, malic acid, matrine alkaloid, fructose, sucrose enzyme, protein amino acids, watermelon amino acids, lycopene, and abundant vitamin C, having effects such as anti-inflammatory and hypotensive, promoting metabolism, reducing cholesterol deposits, softening and dilating blood vessels, and preventing scurvy, enhancing the body's resistance to diseases and preventing cardiovascular system diseases.

7. Treatment of Pharyngitis and Oral Inflammation: Watermelon frost made from watermelons has an anti-inflammatory and swelling-reducing effect, applied to affected areas to treat sore throat, mouth ulcers, etc.

[Health-Preserving Recipes]

1. Fresh Watermelon Juice: Take 1000g fresh watermelon, remove the skin and seeds, crush and drink the juice twice daily. Functions include clearing heat, relieving summer-heat, removing irritability, stopping vomiting, and promoting urination. Suitable for treating fever-induced thirst, summer-heat dizziness, dryness-induced nausea, difficulty in urination, urinary tract infections, and constipation.

2. Rock Sugar Watermelon: Use one fresh watermelon of about 3kg, make a small cut with a small knife, take out some of the pulp, add 50g rock sugar, seal the opening with the rind, steam for 90 minutes over water. After cooling, eat the watermelon and drink the juice daily for 7 consecutive days. Function includes clearing heat and moistening lungs, used to treat cough with little phlegm, sticky phlegm, etc.

3. Green Rind Red Bean Tea: Use 50g fresh watermelon green rind, 50g fresh winter melon rind, 30g red beans. Wash them thoroughly, place in a clay pot, add 500ml water, simmer for 20 minutes, filter out the liquid, and drink as tea for 10 consecutive days. This tea has diuretic and edema-relieving effects, suitable for patients with edema caused by nephritis and heart dysfunction.

4. Green Rind Eel Strips: 500g coarse eel meat, 500ml cooked pig oil, 150g fresh watermelon rind, 1 egg, appropriate amount of green onion and garlic. Wash the eel meat thoroughly, slice into pieces, then cut into strips, wash once with clear water, drain, and use dry gauze to absorb the moisture; peel off the hard outer layer of the watermelon rind, cut into blocks, mash into mud, extract the green rind juice, add starch to make green rind juice starch for reserve; put the eel strips into a bowl, beat in the egg white, add green rind juice starch, salt, cooking wine, mix evenly; heat up the wok, pour in the cooked pig oil until hot, add eel strips, minced garlic, minced green onion, stir-fry until color turns white, then drain the oil; leave a little oil in the original pot, add green onion and garlic, then the eel strips, add a little rind juice, cooking wine, salt, stir-fry, thicken with starch, flip a few times, drizzle sesame oil, serve on a plate when done. This dish has the effects of replenishing deficiency and removing dampness-wind, suitable for long-term illness weakness, emaciation, rheumatic arthritis pain, and those who are physically weak in summer.

5. Stir-Fried Watermelon with Eggs: 500g watermelon flesh (yellow is best), 5 eggs, 100ml vegetable oil. Beat the eggs into a bowl, cut the watermelon flesh into cubes, wrap with clean gauze, slightly squeeze out some water, then place into the bowl with eggs, add salt and mix evenly for reserve; place the wok on fire, pour in vegetable oil and heat up, add the mixed egg-watermelon cube paste, cook until done. This dish has the effects of nourishing yin, moisturizing dryness, clearing throat, improving voice, nurturing stomach, and generating saliva. Suitable for those with yin deficiency internal dryness, lung deficiency prolonged cough, sore throat loss of voice, heat disease restlessness, gastric dryness oral dryness, short red urine, as well as patients with hypertension and diabetes. Those with yin deficiency dry-heat constitution should often consume this as a good health-preserving dish.
