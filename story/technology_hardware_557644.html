Cai Linyi revealed that she often felt depressed when being photographed, and would shout profanity at the wall to let off steam.

Jolin Tsai visits "Super Interview" - Jolin Tsai steps into "Super Interview" and was interviewed by Jing Li and Jun Dai. Although Jolin Tsai is not a talkative person, Jing Li and Jun Dai still tried their best to "guide" her to reveal her interactions with close friends in the entertainment circle. Jolin Tsai mentioned that Show Lo is her closest and most tacit friend, while good friend Leehom Wang likes to flirt with his eyes and pretend to be innocent.

Dazed upon entering the show

Jolin Tsai didn't say much during the program, admitting that she often zones out when on a show. "So usually I don't participate in shows with game segments," she said. Despite this, she was scolded when participating in a show hosted by Xiaoxi S: "She said my answers were too simple, always just saying yes or no." Beside her, Jing Li sighed, "We hosts are most afraid of guests who speak like this."

Constantly being photographed feels oppressive

The daily life and movements of Jolin Tsai often appear in newspapers, but these tracking reports make her feel very oppressed. Jing Li sympathetically told the audience on site that once, when Jolin Tsai accidentally pressed the white line while parking, the next day's newspaper published news saying she couldn't park. Jolin Tsai said she sometimes really goes crazy about it, "I just hope they won't take pictures of me, won't report on me, just listen to me sing and watch me dance." In the most oppressive times, Jolin Tsai also chooses a rather "barbaric" way to vent, "I go home and curse at the wall."

Acts as the big sister among friends

At the interview site, Jing Li revealed the friendship between Jolin Tsai and hair stylist "Cat King". Originally planning to attend "Cat King's" wedding, Jolin Tsai failed to go due to work reasons and had to let her mother and sister attend on her behalf, resulting in "Cat King" finding Jing Li to complain about Jolin Tsai breaking her promise. Jolin Tsai said that she gets along well with people she has known for a long time, and most of her friends are outside the entertainment industry, "I act quite like an older sister when I'm with my friends." She once wrote on her blog, "It's hard to make sincere friends in this circle." She explained on the interview site: "Because everyone is so busy, I rarely meet friends in the industry, and even if I do, it's often just a brief encounter, making it hard to truly understand each other." She also expressed that even when interacting with people in the industry, she tries to avoid giving others the impression that she is deliberately trying to get closer for headlines.

Most tacit with Show Lo

However, Jing Li still "exposed" many of Jolin Tsai's close friends in the entertainment industry during the interview: Huang Li Xing, Jay Chou, Leehom Wang, Show Lo, and Brendan Yan were all "spared no mercy." Jing Li jokingly asked Jolin Tsai if she belonged to the "looks association," why were all her friends handsome men? Jolin Tsai quickly clarified, "This is really a coincidence." In Jolin Tsai's view, Show Lo is her most tacit friend, "I often chat with Show Lo, although we don't often go out to meet, but when we meet at programs, company gatherings, or guest collaborations at concerts, we always have great chats, our tacit understanding is pretty good."

Show Lo loves talking as a host

Jolin Tsai revealed that Show Lo, as a host, likes to talk a lot in front of people, but is often quiet privately, so when they chat, she will never be overshadowed by Show Lo.

Leehom Wang analyzes problems

Jolin Tsai believes that Huang Li Xing and Brendan Yan are both good at pleasing girls, but the one who left the deepest impression on her is her good friend Leehom Wang. "I think he's the kind of guy who flirts with his eyes when talking to girls," Jolin Tsai said, "The worst part is he knows he's flirting, understands what it means to flirt, enjoys himself while doing it, and then pretends to be innocent."

Jolin Tsai said that whenever she has something on her mind, she generally calls a friend outside the entertainment industry, but if she has to choose a friend within the industry, she would choose Leehom Wang, "Because he's good at analyzing problems."

Not necessarily looking for a boyfriend in the industry

When Jing Li jokingly asked Jolin Tsai to choose one from five male friends to be her boyfriend, she shook her head repeatedly, indicating that she dared not, "I'll be beaten when I go out." She joked, "Choosing a boyfriend is too difficult, I'm still in the single phase. I don't necessarily have to find a boyfriend in the entertainment industry."
