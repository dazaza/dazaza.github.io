**Question in English:**

What are the application procedures, fees, and required materials for registering a domestic service company in Shanghai? [http://www.fubaosp.com]

**Answer:**

#### 1. Registered Capital

According to the regulations of the Industry and Commerce Bureau, the minimum registered capital for a limited liability company with two or more shareholders in Shanghai is RMB 30,000; for a one-person limited company, the minimum registered capital is RMB 100,000, which must be fully paid at once. Although the domestic service industry requires less capital investment and has a low entry threshold, experts still recommend having as much registered capital as possible.

#### 2. Required Materials for Registration

1. Original and photocopies of shareholders' and legal representative's ID cards, actual business address, contact information, and two photos each.

2. Proposed company name (preferably 5 or more) and scope of operations.

3. Registered capital and the proportion of investment by investors.

4. Lease agreement and property certificate copy (if registered in an economic zone, it can be provided by the economic zone).

5. Financial personnel’s job qualification certificate, ID card copy, and two photos.

6. Other prescribed registration materials.

#### 3. General Registration Process

1. Pre-approval of the company name by the Industrial and Commercial Bureau.

2. Signing of the industrial and commercial registration documents.

3. Opening of a capital verification account, completing the capital verification process, and issuing a capital verification report.

4. Processing of industrial and commercial registration.

5. Engraving of the company seal and other required seals.

6. Organization code registration.

7. Tax registration.

8. Determination of tax categories at the tax department and purchase of invoices (FAPIAO).

**Question in English:**

What are the application procedures, fees, and required materials for registering a cleaning service company in Shanghai? [http://www.fubaosp.com]

**Answer:**

#### 1. Registered Capital Requirements for Establishing a Cleaning Company in Shanghai

1. For a one-person limited company, the minimum registered capital is RMB 100,000, which must be fully paid at once.

2. For a limited company with two or more shareholders, the minimum registered capital is RMB 30,000. The capital can be paid in installments, with the first payment being no less than 20%, and the remaining amount must be paid within two years after the company's registration.

#### 2. Required Documents for Registering a Cleaning Service Company

1. Copies of shareholders' and legal representative's ID cards.

2. Proposed company name (preferably 5 or more) and scope of operations.

3. Registered capital and the proportion of investment by investors.

4. Lease contract for the registered address and the landlord's ID card.

5. Shareholders' resolution and articles of association.

#### 3. Scope of Operations Reference for a Cleaning Service Company

Examples of cleaning services: post-renovation cleaning, stone renovation and maintenance, carpet cleaning, floor waxing, office furniture cleaning, kitchen, bedroom, and bathroom disinfection services, pest control, wall and glass cleaning, advertising sign washing and painting, cleaning of public facilities in office buildings, hospitals, and shopping malls. Additionally, home services and landscaping can also be included.

#### 4. Procedures for Registering a Cleaning Service Company in Shanghai

1. Name pre-approval at the Administration for Industry and Commerce (AIC). Obtain the [Enterprise (Business Name) Pre-Approval Application Form], fill in the proposed company name, conduct an online search to ensure there are no duplicates, and receive the [Enterprise (Business Name) Pre-Approval Notice].

2. Rent an office space in a commercial building (self-owned factories or offices are also acceptable, but residential buildings are not allowed). Sign a lease agreement and request a copy of the landlord's property certificate.

3. Draft the [Articles of Association] and have all shareholders sign it.

4. Engrave a personal seal.

5. Obtain a [Bank Inquiry Letter] from an accounting firm.

6. Open a capital verification account at the accounting firm and issue a capital verification report.

7. Complete the industrial and commercial registration.

8. Apply for an organization code certificate.

9. Apply for a tax registration certificate.

10. Open a basic bank account.

11. Apply for a tax-specific account.
