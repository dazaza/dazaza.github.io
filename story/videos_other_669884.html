Lawn mowers are often categorized as either operated manually or by a source of energy. The power supply of your lawn mower could be either electricity or petrol energy. Manual push mowers are the cheapest type of mowers and are good for anyone who has extra time to spare while maintaining the backyard. They are also good to use when you wish to exercise and work out while gardening. Electric lawnmowers are ideally used for small to medium-sized lawns. They are easy to handle and lightweight. Corded electric mowers must be used with a nearby power supply. Corded electric mowers are operated with a battery pack that must be recharged properly for maximum use. Compared to a petrol-powered mower, an electric mower is easier to maintain. To prevent electric shocks, this specific mower should not be used in wet conditions. Gas mowers are recommended for use in larger lawns with longer grass because they are more effective. Petrol mowers don’t have trailing cords so you wouldn’t worry about tripping over them. It will also not overheat when you use it on large turf areas. Although more expensive compared to their electric counterparts, petrol mowers are usually better performing. They can also be used in damp and wet conditions. When compared to electric mowers, petrol mowers tend to be harder to maintain and need to be stored safely. Mowers can also be classified by the parts and processes they contain and the way the cutting blades turn. Cylinder Mowers use blades that rotate vertically and cut very close for a well-maintained turf. They may come with rear rollers to give the lawn a traditional finish; a removable container for collecting grass clippings. This lawn mower in various cutting widths is ideal for use on level and flat lawns. There are electric-powered, petrol-fueled, and hand-pushed cylinder mowers. Rotary mowers have blades that rotate horizontally and create a less polished cut. Larger areas or expanses of turf are perfect to be mowed with rotary mowers, especially lawns with grassy and hard patches, weeds, moss growth, and sloping areas. Rotary mowers come with adjustable cutting levels, a removable container for lawn clippings, and a roller for striped grass ends. Rotary mowers can be electric-powered, petrol-fueled, or manually pushed. Hover mowers have revolving blades floating above the grass surface and leave the lawn very tidy and neat. They can be used to quickly cut the turf on promising small to medium-sized lawns. They are also ideal for use on uneven grass planes. Compared to other forms of mowers, hover mowers don't create a highly polished cut but they are inexpensive, lightweight, easy to use, and maintain.
