New Knowledge of Car Beauty, Must-read for Beginners

Most 4S stores usually have spoilers, xenon headlights, stickers, waxing, spraying, chrome plating, interior decoration, personalized interior lights, night driving lights, etc.

Car beauty is not only about decoration but also requires attention to some common knowledge. The Car Beauty Talent Network gives you some tips:

It can be roughly explained in the following three points.

First, Common Car Exterior Decorations

The decoration or protection measures for the car's exterior can be selected according to the individual's different conditions as follows:

1. Wax removal and polishing: When a new car comes out of the factory, there is a layer of protective film on the shell. Therefore, your new car should go to a professional car beauty center for wax removal and polishing to restore its bright and charming appearance.

3. Paint sealing: The appearance of a car is like a person's appearance. After using the new car for a while, the surface will become dull and darker. So when the car is new, use "glaze" to seal the paint and isolate it from the outside world, and there is no need to wax.

4. "Chassis plastic sealing": It sprays a highly adhesive, highly elastic rubber resin twice on the car chassis, reducing the impact force of gravel, providing anti-corrosion, rust prevention, and sound insulation effects. It is also effective against the strong corrosiveness of winter snow water and de-icing agents.

5. Changes in body color and body frame: An application must be made to one of the six branches of the Traffic Management Bureau's Vehicle Management Office. After the vehicle confirmation and approval by the Vehicle Management Branch, the changes can be made. Changing the engine and the nature of vehicle use requires registering the change at the Vehicle Management Branch after the change is completed.

Second, Matters to Note for Car Interior Decoration

When considering the internal decoration of a new car, start with the window treatment. Applying window film to a new car provides heat insulation, waterproofing, and explosion protection, which is significantly different from not applying the film or hanging curtains. Car interior accessories mainly include car perfumes, car miscellaneous bags, cup holders, mobile phone stands, and various other delicate and ingenious small accessories.

When installing car interior accessories, four principles should be followed:

a. Comfort: The color and texture of car interior accessories should match the owner's aesthetic taste, and the perfume should be fresh and not too strong.

b. Take transparent protective agent, shake evenly, apply to cleaned leather, rubber surfaces, then wipe clean with a clean cloth.

c. Safety: Car interior accessories should not interfere with driving safety, such as items hung from the top of the car should not be too long, large, or heavy, and decorations on the rear windshield should not affect reversing visibility.

d. Cleanliness: Accessories should be neat, hygienic, and orderly placed, giving a sense of relaxation and comfort.

e. Practicality: When selecting some delicate, beautiful, and personalized accessories, choose practical items based on the size of the car's interior space, such as cup holders, perfume bottles, storage boxes, etc.

f. Coordination: The color of the accessories should coordinate with the color of the car. Do not blindly pursue high-quality, high-priced items, as this may result in unintended consequences.

Third, Legal Knowledge Related to Car Decoration and Modification

Before decorating or modifying your beloved car to make it look beautiful, understand the relevant regulations to avoid falling into pitfalls. Otherwise, it will not only fail the annual inspection but may also cause safety hazards or even legal penalties.

a. Whether the vehicle modification is legal depends on whether the vehicle is consistent with the photo on the registration certificate and the technical parameters of the vehicle at the time of manufacture. If they are inconsistent, the annual inspection cannot be passed.

b. The new "Road Traffic Safety Law" clearly stipulates that no unit or individual shall assemble motor vehicles or arbitrarily modify the existing structure, construction, or characteristic marks of motor vehicles. The structure of the vehicle includes the body color, length, width, height, and relevant technical parameters of the engine.

c. Before modifying a licensed car, register with the Vehicle Management Office. After reviewing and approving the modification technical report, the modification can proceed. After the modification is completed, the change procedures must be handled at the Vehicle Management Office.
