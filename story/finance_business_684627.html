Sun Wanxiang said that at first, he would really collide with vehicles, and he lifted his shirt to show the judge the injury on his chest. In the final statement phase, he claimed that he had no money to repay the victims. The court will announce the verdict on a later date.

Reporter: Are you at peace after getting the money? Where did you spend it?

Reporter: Do you have a job? How is your family situation?

The prosecution suggests a sentence of 6-8 years.

Previously, he was sentenced to re-education through labor once for theft and twice for hooliganism.

The old man was involved in 158 "insurance fraud" incidents.

A few minutes later, a caregiver brought the food over. Due to Sun Wanxiang's inability to take care of himself, a caregiver fed him. After eating a few bites, he started coughing, his face turning red. The caregiver hurriedly patted his back, but the food he had eaten was vomited back up. "Water, water," he slurred, then refused to eat any further.

Reporter: Are you not afraid of being injured or caught? Sun Wanxiang: Even if I'm afraid, I still go ahead because I have no money to spend. If I die, so be it, I'm already over sixty.

Sun Wanxiang: Poor, what can I do without money? I need to eat, smoke, and pay for utilities.

"I ask for 10,000 yuan, but getting a few hundred is fine too." Sun Wanxiang is unable to take care of himself and requires a caregiver to help him eat. Photo by reporter Wang Lina.

Facing the charges, Sun Wanxiang simply responded: "All the facts in the indictment are true, I admit my guilt." "How many times have I done this? I lost count, probably every week."

Reporter: Do you regret it? How is life in the nursing home?

Sun Wanxiang: No regrets. Although I am not free here, I cannot go out, and I cannot watch TV.

The prosecution suggests a sentence of 6-8 years.

After the incident, Sun Wanxiang was detained in the detention center. Upon examination, he was found to have post-stroke syndrome, including paralysis on the right side of his body, speech impediment, and inability to take care of himself, making him unsuitable for incarceration. Eventually, he was released on bail. His son pays 1500 yuan per month to send him to a nursing home.

Sun Wanxiang: I don't know. He found out from the police station. When he came to pick me up, he said this is a crime and dangerous, and he was unhappy. Before the incident, he said I had no money and no house.

Reporter: Does your son know? Does he blame you?

Sun Wanxiang: Just poor, medical expenses cannot be reimbursed, only recently did I start receiving low-income assistance, 470 yuan per month. I still need to spend 1000 yuan renting a house to live with my son. I need some money to eat, I saw such things (insurance fraud) on TV. The first time was about eight or nine years ago, one night. A taxi came, I really got fractured, the driver gave me a few hundred yuan, treating me like a beggar.

One hour later, he will stand trial. According to the prosecution's charges, from February 2001 to September 2009, at various intersections in the city, Sun Wanxiang deliberately collided with motor vehicles while riding a bicycle in the pedestrian crossing and pretended to fall to defraud 139 people of more than 124,000 yuan. From October last year to January this year, due to physical limitations, his neighbor Li Mou (charged with fraud) drove an electric tricycle to take him and his bicycle to intersections such as Jiaodaokou and Yonghegong to "insurance fraud", defrauding 19 people of more than 10,000 yuan.

Reporter: Will you continue doing this in the future? Sun Wanxiang: No. I appeared on TV, they all point at me and say, it's him, it's him, it's embarrassing.

Sun Wanxiang: About thirty or forty years ago, I worked in a model factory, but it went bankrupt, and I've been unemployed ever since. Later, my wife divorced me, and I have one child who lives with me. He is 28 years old this year, and he hasn't worked for long. Each time he looks for a job, he has to pay a 500-yuan referral fee.

Reporter: How did you come up with the idea of "insurance fraud"? How did you start the first time?

Sun Wanxiang: I go everywhere, the farthest I've gone is Miyun and Yanqing, looking for intersections and targeting taxi drivers. When the drivers turn, their speed is slow, I push my bicycle over and pretend to fall. I initially ask for 5000 or 10,000 yuan, but getting a few hundred is fine.

Finally, the prosecutor warned Sun Wanxiang: "Although you are an elderly person, we hope that wherever you are, whether in a nursing home or elsewhere, you should reflect on the unease caused to the victims and truly repent for your crimes."

Considering his poor health, the court decided to hold the trial at the nursing home. At noon, Sun Wanxiang was pushed into a meeting room by the court officer, and the trial officially began.

The defense lawyer for Sun argued that some accidents were not reported, and there were no traffic accident determination letters, so they could not be used as evidence of guilt. Considering Sun's health condition, the lawyer suggested a lighter punishment.
