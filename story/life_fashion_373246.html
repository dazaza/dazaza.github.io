Elegant puff skirt

I guess there are few people now who do not know about "vintage" retro clothing. Originally, "vintage" was a word on wine bottles that indicated the year of production, but today it has become an indispensable classic in the fashion world. Classic retro styles condensed from time are a shadow of the times - how can we erase the fashion value of that exclusive era? Below, let's follow a set of old photos together and learn the "vintage" popular elements passed down from that era.

1. Elegant puff skirts

Representative star: Brigitte Bardot

In the movie history of the 50s and 60s, there were two sexy stars known worldwide: one was Marilyn Monroe, the blonde beauty from the United States; the other was Brigitte Bardot, the French sexy kitten who sparked a new wave of sensuality. As a sexy idol of that era, Brigitte wearing a blue puff skirt with white looked not only elegant and virtuous, but her posture and eyes also made men restless.

The similar feeling of the puff skirt in the picture above and below looks completely different when the shawl is draped over the arm or on the shoulder.
