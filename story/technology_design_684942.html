Nourishing the kidneys and maintaining health: Guiding fire back to its source method + Breathing meditation technique

1. Tonifying kidney and strengthening yang, guiding fire back to its source

The method of guiding fire back to its source mainly involves pressing the Yongquan point, which is located on the sole of the foot. Every night before going to bed, wash your feet with warm water, sit cross-legged on the bed, hold your toes with one hand, and press the soles of your feet with the other hand. Each time you press down, it should feel slightly painful. Experts point out that Yongquan is a point on the Kidney Meridian of Foot-Shaoyin. Rubbing this point can tonify the kidney, nourish essence, regulate qi and blood, and when the kidney qi is abundant, the body will be healthy and life prolonged.

2. Do not talk while using the restroom

In traditional Chinese medicine, it is believed that when using the restroom for urination or defecation, one must clench their teeth, as this helps to retain kidney qi, especially for men who have suffered significant damage to their kidney essence. Why is this? Because teeth are considered the "teeth of the kidney" and are an external manifestation of kidney essence. However, one should not clench their teeth excessively, as this can deplete kidney qi; instead, one should lightly bite down as if there were two date pits between the molars. During the process of using the restroom, one is in a state of inhaling and drawing qi inward, avoiding any leakage. Clenching the teeth while lifting the heels is equivalent to replenishing kidney qi.

3. Eating Sesame Seeds

One of the methods for tonifying the kidney and promoting health is eating sesame seeds. Sesame seeds come in black, yellow, and white varieties, but only black sesame seeds, which correspond to the color of the kidney, possess better medicinal value and health benefits. Yellow and white sesame seeds are only suitable for general food consumption. Black sesame seeds embody the spring growth energy of Jueyin and are considered the leader among grains. They can tonify the five organs, build muscles, and replenish brain marrow, and are listed as superior in the "Shennong Bencao Jing." Since they are used to replenish deficiencies and fill the brain marrow, it is essential to use plump sesame seeds. To remove the unripe ones, use the washing method: during the rinsing process, the sesame seeds that do not float are plump, while those that float are either spoiled, infested with insects, or not fully developed and should be discarded.

4. Breathing Technique

Method: Stand upright with feet shoulder-width apart, raise both arms straight up, and cross them behind your head. At this moment, the abdomen slightly protrudes forward, and both hands stretch upward as much as possible, concentrating all strength and intention on the lumbar vertebrae. Then, force emanates from the lumbar vertebrae, causing the arms to move in the largest arc from behind the head down toward the sides of the body while simultaneously squatting. The hands finally cross between the knees, and at the end, focus the intention on the balls of the front feet for 5 seconds. The heels must not leave the ground. When standing up again, start by applying force with the balls of the front feet as the initiating energy. Immediately after, concentrate all strength and intention back on the lumbar vertebrae, and do not use any force in the legs while standing up. Keep the hands crossed from the chest straight up over the head to behind the head.

5. Perineum Lift Exercise

Experts introduce that in a quiet state, relax the entire body and use abdominal breathing (inhale to expand the abdomen, exhale to contract it). While exhaling, gently contract the front genital area and anus with some effort. Relax during inhalation, repeating this process 10 times.
