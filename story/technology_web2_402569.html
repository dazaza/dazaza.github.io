1. My time is your time. Feel free to ask me!

2. My hands are tied. I'm very busy and can't do anything about it.

3. To make a long story short. Cut the long talk short.

4. It’s a date. It's a promise.

5. That’s a steal. It's really cheap.

6. She has no sense. She doesn't understand (and possibly has bags under her eyes).

7. It’s not a big deal. It's nothing special.

8. What’s the fuss? Why are you arguing?

9. Don’t push me. Don't force me.

10. Have a good one. Enjoy yourself.

11. Get down to business. Let's get back to the point.

12. Does it serve your purpose? Is it useful to you?

13. It’s a long story. It's hard to explain in a few words.

14. Don’t play possum. Don't pretend!

15. Make it up. Let bygones be bygones.

16. Don’t overdo it. Don't go too far.

17. You want a bet? Do you want to make a bet?

18. Who wants? Who cares?

19. December heartbeat. Evening romance.

20. Follow my nose. Go with my gut feeling.

21. Cheap skate. A miser!

22. Big mouth. Talkative.

23. I’m going to go. I'll head out now.

24. Can-do person. A strong person.

25. Leave me alone. Don't bother me.

26. Don’t pass the buck. Don't shirk responsibility.

27. I can’t put up with her. I can't stand her.

28. She is just thick-skinned. She has a thick skin.

29. I see eye to eye with you. I agree with you.

30. We are on good terms. Our relationship is good.

31. Many people, many tastes. Everyone has different preferences.

32. One man’s meat is another man’s poison. What is good for one person may not be good for another.

33. Many hands make light work. With more people, work becomes easier.

34. I won’t live on the handouts of other people. I won't rely on others' charity to live.

35. He is on his high horse these days. He's been acting arrogantly lately.

36. You give him an inch and he will take a mile. Greedy for more.

37. He is a fast talker. He's a boastful person.

38. I can’t make two ends meet. I can't balance my income and expenses.

39. None of your keyhole. No peeking allowed.
