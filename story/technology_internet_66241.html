Internet enters the explosive growth period, and agency is a strong driving force

by now on 2008-04-30 15:32:12

According to reports from Xinhua News Agency, the penetration rate of China's Internet has reached 16%, surpassing the critical point of 10% and entering a period of explosive growth. It can be said that China’s Internet basic service industry is truly experiencing its springtime, with many service providers strengthening their terminal marketing system construction. In this situation, theories about agents being either the "main character" or the "supporting role" have emerged. How should each agent respond? Experts believe that the agency market is very broad, and carefully selecting an agency model and service provider is the key.

Channel sales remain a strong driving force for industry development

Channel sales, as a long-standing marketing model in this industry, have already been widely recognized by various service providers. The domestic Internet basic service adopting a channel sales strategy has been successful; sales achieved through agents are much better than those directly obtained by service providers. Even in today's explosively developing Internet era, since the domestic Internet basic market is still in its infancy, the enthusiasm of small and medium-sized enterprises for building websites needs to be developed. Sales personnel providing one-on-one services remain a strong driving force for industry development. Without this market impetus, the cycle for enterprises to automatically accept Internet basic service products will be extended.

Independent brand operation is the winning formula for agents

In terms of brand operations, many agents have been making mistakes. A person in charge of Era Interconnect pointed out that independent brand operations involve organically integrating "his brand" and "my brand" into "our brand." Agents need to rely on the quality products, solid reputation, and good service of service providers to firmly establish themselves in the increasingly competitive Internet basic service market. At the same time, agents should also act as the sales company for manufacturers. If product sales are good and the service provider has reliable after-sales service, a smooth win-win situation is achieved, establishing both brands at the same time, which benefits long-term cooperation.

Choosing the appropriate agency model

Due to the lowering of entry barriers in the Internet basic service market, there now exists a mixed situation of varying quality. Many agents generally express confusion when choosing a service provider, especially when it comes to selecting an agency model. Many service providers claim to offer free services but actually require a certain volume of business or other additional conditions. For new agents, this undoubtedly increases costs, making it even harder to make significant progress. Some service providers cannot even provide an online management platform, do not give agents authority over client online payments and renewals, and sometimes even require agents to operate business on behalf of clients, greatly increasing the workload for agents and making it difficult to achieve profitability.

Therefore, choosing the appropriate agency model is particularly crucial. It is understood that the SCP system developed by Era Interconnect (www.now.cn) is highly popular within the industry, being completely free, having dynamic property rights, and supporting independent branding. The service provider offers a fully online automatic system, allowing online applications, online payments, and real-time activation. Customers can independently set domain name pointers (VDNS management), modify FTP passwords, allocate mailbox sizes, etc., on the management platform.

Industry analysts believe that for agents, choosing wisely is more important than effort. If the chosen service provider has guaranteed strength, products, and services, combined with a reasonable and efficient agency model, then low investment can yield high returns, establishing a strong agency brand.
