New Year greeting SMS, blessing without obstacles

by now on 2009-01-20 08:52:46

Starting from January 15th of this year, users will no longer encounter differential pricing when sending text messages between different telecommunications operators. It has been learned that, in accordance with the previous relevant requirements of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT), China Mobile, China Unicom, and China Telecom will implement a unified pricing for both internal and external networks when sending messages to each other. The price for sending text messages in SMS format between these three operators will be uniformly set at 0.1 yuan per message. Local mobile, Unicom, and Telecom operators have already indicated that they will strictly adhere to MIIT's requirements and implement unified pricing for both intra-network and inter-network messaging.

Sending New Year greetings via text message is a convenient way to convey emotions and wishes. According to estimates by the Ministry of Information Industry, during the Spring Festival holiday from New Year's Eve to the sixth day of the first lunar month in 2008, the national mobile phone text message volume exceeded 17 billion messages, compared to 15.2 billion during the same period the previous year.

Some consumers have reported that the speed of mass text message delivery from many platforms has decreased compared to before, and during peak sending times, it may not be possible to send messages. Professionals explained: Before mass text messages are officially sent, they must go through a system review.
