Commissioner Wang Guoxin accepted an exclusive interview with Tencent Weibo.

by wangguoxin on 2012-03-04 11:01:16

Mr. Wang Guoxin, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), a member of the Central Committee of the Agricultural Workers' Party Literature Committee, the director of the Three Gorges Painting Academy, a researcher at the Central Institute of Literature and History Calligraphy and Painting Research Institute, and a first-class national artist who enjoys government allowances, was invited to Tencent Weibo at 8 pm on March 3rd to engage in online exchanges with netizens on hot topics such as the dual application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture and the development of picture-story books.

The following is an excerpt from the transcript:

Wang Guoxin: I thank Tencent.com and Tencent Weibo for their attention to me, and I hope to become a good friend with everyone in spirit!

Tencent Netizen: Should the cultural industry open its doors and go global? Are there any corresponding action plans in this regard? Is there a need for the state to provide corresponding support?

Wang Guoxin: Culture must rely on the wings of industry to transcend time and space and reach the masses to be passed down. Artworks should be healthy, majestic, unique, and strive to surpass predecessors. For example, Xu Beihong painted horses. I paint Guan Yu, showing both his divine and human sides. If one keeps imitating, it will be difficult to go global. The state should invest heavily in corresponding support.

Tencent Netizen: Is my understanding of Guan Yu culture correct that it is a kind of philosophical thought and worldview culture about Guan Yu? How significant is this culture for the modern society's ideological and moral system?

Wang Guoxin: Guan Yu culture is the core value of tradition, supporting the cohesion and centripetal force of the nation for thousands of years and will continue for many more years. Chinese traditional culture is loyal and righteous culture. Whether the country is prosperous or in crisis, loyalty, righteousness, benevolence, and courage are needed.

Tencent Netizen: What impressive events have you encountered during the process of applying for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture?

Wang Guoxin: Last year during the two sessions, I proposed a suggestion to establish a statue of Guan Yu next to the National Grand Theatre. The proposal had been accepted, but then it was questioned by some netizens, and the matter was put on hold. This made me sigh deeply: we must start with popularizing Guan Yu culture! In the past year, I have been conducting relevant investigations and planning, writing materials and submitting them to the government, and vigorously promoting the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture!

Tencent Netizen: Yichang now has clear mountains and beautiful waters, outstanding people, convenient transportation. What suggestions do you have for establishing a cultural tourism circle in Yichang?

Wang Guoxin: A Guan Yu cultural tourism circle? Haha. China Yichang, the land of great righteousness. Lead with the head: Guan Yu, the Martial Saint in Dangyang; list nine tripods, set up nine platforms, build a 58-meter bronze statue of Guan Yu. Strengthen the middle: develop industrial, medical, and tourism products with Guan Yu cultural connotations in Xiangting, Yichang, enriching the country and benefiting the people. Dance the tail: develop leisure and cultural tourist areas along Guan Yu’s military camp.

Tencent Netizen: I support the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture, but I think it should be expanded to the Three Kingdoms culture. Is there a suspicion of creating a god movement when applying for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture?

Wang Guoxin: All nations around the world revere their own gods. "God" on the left is a "notice," and on the right are three horizontal strokes and one vertical stroke, connecting heaven, earth, and man. It tells others what a god is. Transcending human power is divine power. God instills respect in people; a nation without respect has no hope.

Tencent Netizen: Some underground societies in China, especially in Hong Kong, worship Guan Yu. A symbol of justice, loyalty, martial sainthood, and many positive traits, is worshipped by the mafia. Do you find this ironic? Does this have any adverse effect on your promotion of Guan Yu culture?

Wang Guoxin: People from all walks of life hope that what they believe in is a "true god." The spirit of Guan Yu's loyalty, righteousness, benevolence, and bravery has no distinction between black and white societies.

Tencent Netizen: What do you think the biggest problem in applying for World Heritage status will be?

Wang Guoxin: Personally, I think that over time, more and more people from all levels of society will believe in and support Guan Yu culture. Guan Yu culture will become mainstream culture!

Tencent Netizen: Many hotel lobbies place statues of Guan Yu. What does this generally represent in terms of good symbolism?

Wang Guoxin: Personally, I think it should be placing "loyalty and trustworthiness" in one's heart, not making ill-gotten gains, and not using gutter oil!!!

Tencent Netizen: Guan Yu has always appeared as a loyal and righteous figure in Chinese folklore, representing the essence of excellent traditional Chinese culture. In the application for World Heritage status for Guan Yu culture, what specific material relics and cultural forms of Guan Yu culture do we mainly establish? As cultural relics, what related activities have been held and will be held in our country?

Wang Guoxin: The main forms include calligraphy, painting, sculpture, drama, folklore, place names, temples, scenic spots, etc. Regarding activities, there are the grand sacrifices in spring and autumn at Dangyang Guanling, Luoyang Guanlin, and Yuncheng Guandi Temple.

Wang Guoxin: The end of the interview marks the beginning of my friendship with the vast number of netizens! I hope Tencent Weibo can become our spiritual teahouse, and I will take time each week to chat with the vast number of netizens. That's all for today's interview. Thank you, netizens, and I hope everyone continues to pay attention to me.

Wang Guoxin, a contemporary outstanding painter, calligrapher, and poet. Since childhood, he has been fascinated by poetry, calligraphy, and painting. He has extensively studied Chinese history, philosophy, classical literature, and Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism. At the age of 16, his works were selected for the exhibition of five provinces in Central and South China. In 1969, he published oil paintings which were featured in the film "News Briefing". Currently, he serves as a researcher at the Central Institute of Literature and History Calligraphy and Painting Research Institute, a member of the Art Committee of the Chinese Artists Association, a member of the Central Committee of the Agricultural Workers' Party Literature Committee, a first-class national artist who enjoys government allowances, a member of the Chinese Artists Association, the director of the Three Gorges Painting Academy, a guest professor at Valenciennes University in France, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and the executive vice president of the Beijing Oriental Calligraphy and Painting Research Association. He has published and distributed more than 70 specialized works of poetry, calligraphy, and painting, with over 15 million copies sold. His representative works include the ten-part trilogy of the Yangtze River, the 240-picture-story book of Romance of the Three Kingdoms, and the 158-meter silk painting of the Ten-Thousand-Li Scenic Views of the Yangtze River. He has also composed over 7,000 poems.
