Over the years, Jinluo has developed a "complete industrial chain" from feed research and development, breeding pig reproduction, pig farming to production processing, cold chain logistics, and terminal sales. It has also cleverly integrated biotechnology, engineering technology, information technology and other high-tech technologies into the entire process of the complete industrial chain. For farmers who have close cooperation with various links in the chain, Jinluo provides unified technical training and door-to-door guidance to enhance the professional level and practical skills of local farmers.

Zhou Liankui, executive director of Jinluo, stated that in the future, encouraged by the title of "Top Ten Enterprises", Jinluo will continue to spare no effort in developing the backbone forces of modern agriculture, thereby effectively promoting the development of rural and agricultural economy.

Rural practical talents and agricultural science and technology talents are the backbone force for promoting strong agriculture and benefiting farmers' policies, constructing "three synchronizations", and developing various rural undertakings. Developing modern agriculture and following the Chinese characteristic path of agricultural modernization requires the joint efforts of all sectors of society to jointly build an agricultural talent system and promote the development process of agriculture, rural areas, and farmers. Recently, at the commendation meeting for the county-level economic development in the whole city, the Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government of Linyi awarded Jinluo, the leading brand of national meat products, the honorary title of "Top Ten Enterprises in the City in 2011". Its executive director, Zhou Liankui, was awarded the title of "Distinguished Figure in Revitalizing Yimeng". Regarding this, industry insiders commented that this honor is a strong endorsement of Jinluo's efforts in helping build an agricultural talent system.

For example, through effective measures such as hiring highly paid professionals, improving welfare, optimizing supporting facilities, solving household registration and schooling problems, it attracts high-end talents from outside, effectively expanding the total amount of practical talents and improving the quality of the overall talent team; through regular training and further study, it plays the exemplary role of high-level talents; by establishing and improving mechanisms for cultivating, evaluating, discovering, and incentivizing talents, the innovation and result conversion capabilities of agricultural science and technology talents have been significantly enhanced.

As a key leading enterprise in national agricultural industrialization and a leader in meat products, Jinluo has adopted measures suitable for its enterprise conditions, actively grasped the rules of talent cultivation, innovated talent incentive mechanisms, tapped the potential of existing rural labor force, and improved the overall quality of local farmers. It has achieved fruitful results in building an agricultural talent system and formed a mature and complete system mechanism.
