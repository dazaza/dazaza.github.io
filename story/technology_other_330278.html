Study and implement the important speech of President Hu carefully

by guogaiyanxiu on 2010-02-25 15:33:57

Recently, the Lao District Office of Sanming City consecutively held cadres meetings to study and convey the important speech spirit of President Hu Jintao during his inspection in Fujian and the meeting spirit of province and city. In connection with the actual situation of this unit, it has determined to seriously study and implement the important speech spirit of President Hu, to do a good job in the Lao district work and accelerate the actual actions of Lao district development, not to辜负the care and expectations of the CPC Central Committee and President Hu for the Lao district.

1. Strengthen learning and strive to improve the quality of cadres team. All Party members and cadres must regard the serious study and implementation of the important speech spirit of President Hu as an important political task in the current and future period. Efforts should be made to improve six abilities of cadres of Lao District Office: First, improve the ability to understand policies. At present, the National Development and Reform Commission has clearly defined the scope of the central Soviet counties and revolutionary old areas in Fujian enjoying the mid-western policies. The Provincial Party Committee and the provincial government will introduce support policies for revolutionary old areas not included in the scope of enjoying the policies. We must strengthen communication with the province, seize the opportunity, strengthen policy docking, and use well, fully and flexibly the policies of the seven original central Soviet counties in our city and three old area counties (Datian, Yong'an, Youxi) supported by the province; Second, improve the execution ability, especially to improve the ability to work independently; Third, improve the ability to serve the masses, take the development of the old area as a mission, be concerned about the old area, take root in the old area, and serve the old area; Fourth, improve the project operation ability, use the limited funds of the old area well and flexibly, and improve the economic, social and ecological benefits of the assisted construction projects; Fifth, improve the ability to overcome difficulties, make efforts to overcome the difficulties in terms of funds, manpower, institutions and project drive, enhance the determination and confidence to do a good job in the old area work; Sixth, improve the ability to unite and cooperate. Look at the old area from outside the old area, firmly establish the concept of "big old area", strengthen unity and cooperation, strengthen coordination and cooperation with departments, and strengthen communication and contact with superiors.

2. Emancipate mind and constantly update concepts. First, we must be good at seizing opportunities. Seize the opportunity that the seven original central Soviet counties enjoy the national western policies, and the provincial Party committee and provincial government introduce policies to support the old area counties, increase support, increase help, increase inclination, do a good job in project research, actively think, actively do, actively study, actively dock, and actively implement. Second, dare to pilot first. Start from the interests of the old area development, improving the living conditions of the people in the old area, and facilitating the work, carefully consider the fields and channels for the old area work to pilot first. With innovative ideas, promote the old area work to a new level, produce new results, and see new effects. Third, dare to take responsibility. Adhere to the system of collective research on major matters. Enhance the ability to execute and implement, achieve "zero" obstacles in work promotion, and truly achieve "four less and four more": less requests, more thinking about ways to solve problems; less explanation, more solving practical difficulties; less shirking, more doing pragmatic work; less formalism, more doing practical and effective things.

3. Contact reality and focus on implementing work. According to the deployment of province and city, contact with the actual work of the old area, currently focusing on grasping the "five works": First, strengthen publicity and effectively consolidate the ideological foundation of the old area. Through various channels to publicize the important speech spirit of President Hu during his inspection in Fujian, especially the important speech spirit on the construction of the old area, combine the study of President Hu's important speech with the study of a series of important discourses on the development of the old area in recent years, further understand the requirements of the CPC Central Committee and President Hu for the construction of the old area, and effectively consolidate the ideological foundation of the old area construction; Second, strengthen research and effectively play the role of consultant assistants of the Lao District Office. To enhance the foresight and forward-looking nature of the work, focus on the central work of the Municipal Party Committee and the municipal government, focus on the hot and difficult issues and deep-seated contradictions in the construction and development of the old area, strengthen research, and provide references for the decision-making of the Municipal Party Committee and the municipal government; Third, strengthen guidance and effectively implement the assistance work. With deep feelings for the people of the old area, do more work in terms of policy level, atmosphere creation, and seeking support, and implement the various support policies of the central, provincial, and municipal governments; Fourth, strengthen services and effectively do all the work of the "five old" personnel of the revolution. Seriously implement the "five old" life medical subsidy policy and deliver the care of the Party and the government to the "five old"; Fifth, strengthen learning and effectively strengthen the construction of the cadre team of the old area. Build a learning-type agency, improve the level of thought and policy and the ability to solve practical problems; strengthen self-construction, promote work development, and create a new situation for the work of the old area.

(Sanming Lao District Office)
