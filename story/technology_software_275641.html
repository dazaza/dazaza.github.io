Senior high school boy died in the dormitory, and the will revealed the bad school atmosphere

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-24 16:21:27

A Senior Three boy dies mysteriously

On November 8, Li Jinchuan, a Senior Three student at Xinmi Experimental High School, did not return home.

"I once looked forward to my high school life, but I never thought it would end in this way. Mr. Shang, you are hypocritical, opportunistic, and all bark and no bite. Why is my first head teacher in high school you? You have made me have阴影 about this school. Someone saw you watching pornography in the section chief's office. Are you so desperate that you can't watch it at home? The school is a place for teaching and nurturing, do you still have the appearance of a people's teacher..."

At 11:30 AM yesterday, at the Aba Hotel, when the reporter asked to see the will at the Criminal Investigation Team of the Xinmi Public Security Bureau, a police officer surnamed Sun politely refused.

"I entered the key high school full of hope, but as a result, my hope and dream were ruthlessly shattered, and what ruined my whole life was my head teacher. Students with money who treat him to food and give gifts will be moved to better seats... He (Mr. Shang) looks down on poor students and rural students, often mocking and striking poor children and rural children."

"I hope after cremation, my funeral will be simple, just scatter my ashes into the river ditch. To die takes courage, but living is harder! I want to use my death, like the sudden death of a middle school student with a history of heart disease during a school sports meet, to awaken people's reflection on education and teacher ethics." (Source: Dahe Network - Henan Business Daily)

On the evening of November 8, Li Jinchuan, a 19-year-old student from Class 305 at Xinmi Experimental High School, died in the dormitory. The next day, the local criminal investigators found seven or eight pages of his will on his desk. Li Jinchuan's father, Li Jianbin, said that most of the will exposed the "corruption" of Xinmi Experimental High School.

The people who saw the will were Li Jinchuan's father, fourth uncle, brother-in-law, maternal uncle, and cousin.

At noon on the 9th, after the family members of Li Jinchuan saw the will, they strongly requested to make copies, but the police refused.

"Also, Mr. Chen, who knows how a vice-principal of a high school drives a Beijing Hyundai..."

Before the reporter went to interview, another media journalist from the provincial capital had already seen the will at the criminal investigation team with Li Jinchuan's grandmother, confirming the content of the will as stated by the family. The following is part of the will content organized by the reporter according to the media journalist and the family of Li Jinchuan.

B Family's request to copy the will was rejected by the police

Liu Zhongyi said that in the morning of the 9th, they met the investigating police officers. "The police said they had ruled out murder because they found a will on Jinchuan's desk, which was seven or eight pages long. In the office of the criminal investigation team, only our five relatives saw this will."

At 11 o'clock yesterday morning, the reporter followed the family of Li Jinchuan to Xinmi Experimental High School. After learning the reporter's identity, the gatekeeper said, "The person has already died, why are you interviewing?" Subsequently, after contacting the school authorities, the gatekeeper told the reporter that their leader said if you want to interview this matter, you must get the consent of Deputy Minister Li of the Publicity Department of the Xinmi Municipal Committee. As of the time of going to press, the reporter contacted Deputy Minister Li many times, but the phone could not be connected.

Li Jinchuan's roommate, Yue Xiaobing, Ma Ke, Guo Zhiqiang, and Feng Weibo, students from Class 305 of Xinmi Experimental High School, testified that around 10 o'clock, after Li Jinchuan returned to the dormitory holding a bottle of mineral water without saying anything, he went to bed and slept. Soon, the roommates found Li Jinchuan on the upper bunk shaking continuously. They lifted the quilt and found some liquid overflowing from Li Jinchuan's mouth corner.

According to the recollections of several relatives who saw the will, they were deeply impressed by several points in the will: one is that Li Jinchuan's head teacher, Mr. Shang, when he was in Grade One of high school, favored rich students; second is that Mr. Shang watched pornographic videos in the grade office; third is that when students fall in love, the political and educational affairs teachers slap students; fourth is that good teachers in the experimental middle school are not given due importance; fifth is where Vice Principal Chen's Beijing Hyundai car came from.

At 10:30, Liu Zhongyi called Li Jinchuan's father, Li Jianbin. "I asked the doctor how the child died, and the doctor said they didn't know either."

C Relevant departments refuse interviews

In the evening yesterday, the reporter contacted Mr. Shang, the head teacher mentioned in Li Jinchuan's will. He said that currently, the school is responsible for handling the matter, and he only teaches classes. When the reporter asked about his name being mentioned in the will, Mr. Shang said, "What was mentioned in Li Jinchuan's will happened more than a year ago. I only taught him in Grade One, and I estimate this child had psychological problems. Don't call me anymore. This matter is handled by the school. If there's anything, ask the principal directly."

"At about 10 PM that night, the senior segment leader (grade leader) Mr. Wu Weijie came to my house and said something happened to Jinchuan at school. I immediately went to the hospital. When I got to the hospital, I saw the child being rescued," said Uncle Liu Zhongyi of Li Jinchuan. "At that time, the doctor asked me to pay 391 yuan. When I returned, I saw my wife crying bitterly at the door of the emergency room, and the doctor had already notified us to prepare for the aftermath."

Content of the will: Exposing the school's "corrupt practices"?

"This teacher is too harsh, why can't he have a good talk with the students? Although he is in the Political and Educational Affairs Office, he doesn't even know the true meaning of 'political and educational' two words."
