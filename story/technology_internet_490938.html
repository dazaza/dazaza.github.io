■ Life is short, and I'm willing to be wrong with you. Qin Fen proposed to Liang Xiaoxiao.

■ We are close friends who do not risk our lives for each other; we can entrust our children but not our wives. Xiangshan described his relationship with Qin Fen this way.

■ What's yours is mine, what's mine is still mine. At the end of the movie, Xiaoxiao said this to Qin Fen.

■ If I had known it would be so cold, I should have married someone I saw once a year! At least then I'd see them once a year! Qin Fen complained about Liang Xiaoxiao.

Come into my arms

Or let me stay in your heart

Silently in love

Quietly happy

■ Whether or not you see me,

I am there,

Neither sad nor happy.

■ You're looking for romance, I'm looking for marriage! We've taken different paths. Qin Fen summarized his relationship with Liang Xiaoxiao.

■ Whoever gets emotionally involved is doomed. Qin Fen said sorrowfully upon learning that Liang Xiaoxiao only had feelings of affection for him.

Whether you follow me or not,

My hand is already in yours,

Neither reluctant to leave nor abandon.

■ You haven't met me at my best... these past two years, I've been a bit worn out. Qin Fen was physically exhausted by Liang Xiaoxiao and looked disheveled.

Below are quotes from "If You Are The One 2," involving Qin Fen, Xiaoxiao, Xiangshan, Mango, and Xiangshan's daughter. This film could truly be called "If You Are The One."

■ No matter how you choose in marriage, it's always a mistake. A long-lasting marriage is simply one mistake after another. Xiangshan said this to Xiaoxiao before he passed away.

■ Break up the deal, but don't break the friendship. Divorce ceremony congratulatory words.

■ What? All one big mess. When alive, people crowd together, and when dead, it's just as crowded. Sun Honglei, playing Li Xiangshan, was dissatisfied with the crowded cemetery.

Xiangshan's daughter read Cangyang Jiacuo's "Seen or Unseen."

■ Qin Fen talking about talent show judges: Pretending to be upper class, where is the upper class? They're all despicable. These are just a bunch of parasites, scamming food, drink, and sex. Beside him, Mango said, stop cursing, if you keep going, you'll curse yourself too.

■ This isn't a disease, it's fate. My fate has caught up with me, and I can only accept it. Li Xiangshan's realization after getting cancer.

■ Death is another form of existence. Compared to life, only living is a kind of imperfection. Xiangshan's daughter spoke these words at the farewell ceremony.

This article is dedicated to those who like "If You Are The One 2"!

■ Life is a cultivation. Qin Fen's saying.

■ An emperor-like enjoyment is treating your feet as if they were your face. Qin Fen's insight at a foot bath center.

■ Plant me in a flower pot, and I will smile at you every day. Li Xiangshan asked Qin Fen to plant his ashes in a flower pot.

Whether you think of me or not,

The feeling is there,

Unchanging.

■ There's no need for complete sincerity in daily family life; a little pretense keeps things harmonious. Xiaoxiao criticized Qin Fen for lying too much, and Qin Fen defended himself this way.

■ Happiness doesn't necessarily mean being together, but unhappiness certainly does. Qin Fen's saying.

Whether you love me or not,

Love is there,

Unchanged.

■ Fear is like walking in the dark, knocking on a black door. You don't know whether behind the door is a colorful world or an abyss. Afraid of stepping into emptiness, afraid that it's not the end but the beginning. Someone asked Xiangshan if he was afraid of death, and he answered thus.

■ Can you swear on money that no matter how rich, how healthy, or how much they love you in the future, you won't be with them? This is what Qin Fen asked Mango during the divorce ceremony.
