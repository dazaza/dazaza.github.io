Online personal task management service - The Persistence of Bugs

by zzymale on 2008-09-14 10:36:22

A few days ago, I came across an article introducing personal task management, but unfortunately, I didn't take note of it. Today, on Tianya Haige, I found a similar article and would like to share it with everyone.

1. **Remember the Milk**: A website that provides online personal task management services. The meaning behind the name is: "The best way to manage your tasks so you never forget the milk (or anything else)." It helps you locate your tasks; simply and quickly manage daily tasks; allocate your time; manage information in your own way; add tasks anytime and anywhere; access your to-do list from wherever you are; intelligent search, etc. Detailed introduction.

2. **43 Things**: A platform where you can share what you want to do, and you can encourage each other here. You can also showcase the progress of what you want to do. You can manage your list of things you want to do. Detailed introduction.

3. **SproutLiner**: A simple-to-use online personal task management site where you don't even need to register, and the site provides source code.

4. **Backpack**: A multifunctional online personal task management service website that integrates Email, to-do lists, scheduling, project management, Blog, and Wiki services.

5. **GooToDo**: Online personal task management, a paid service at $3/month, with a 30-day free trial period.

6. **HipCal**: Provides online memo, address book, online reminder functions, and calendar services. Demo.

7. **Wridea**: A free website that lets you collect and manage various ideas and can also be considered an online personal task management site. Detailed introduction.

8. **My Tickler File**: Online personal task management, with email reminders, event reminders, or repeat reminders functions.

9. **AirSet**: Offers calendar, contact list, Todo-Lists, Blog, and bookmarking functions. You can also create your own group, and it supports Rss output of data and Email reminder services.

10. **Ta-da Lists**: Conveniently manage and track the completion status of various tasks or events. You can share your task lists with others and track the completion status via RSS subscription.

11. **ThingsBreakDown**: After registration, you will have your own second-level domain. You can create your own events, add locations, invite friends to share, etc.

12. **TracksLife**: Online personal task management with powerful tracking services. You can choose multiple ways to remind yourself of your plans and has good sharing functions. You can share your own plans or arrangements with your friends.

13. **voo2do**: Online personal task management where you can organize planned tasks, track time reminders, add tasks via email, post tasks, etc.

14. **iOutliner**: An online task, idea, and project management platform.

15. **Toodledo**: Online personal task management with powerful features that can effectively manage and organize personal affairs. Detailed introduction and comparison with some other online personal task management websites.
