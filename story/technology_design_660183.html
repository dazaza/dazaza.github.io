Rescuing a Suicidal Woman, the Special Patrol Police Were Frozen with Pale Lips and Shivering

Main Character: Special patrol police

Protagonist: Shen Guoqiang

Position: On-duty police officer on the patrol car

Water freezing, the police jumped into the river to save someone

In the afternoon at around 2 PM, Shen Guoqiang and his colleagues were driving the "Bangrong" patrol car for routine patrols from Zhanyuan Road to Daming Road area. When they reached the Daming Road area, they suddenly received an instruction from the command center that there was a woman who had jumped into the nearby Qinhuai River, and the situation was urgent.

After receiving the instruction, Shen Guoqiang immediately directed his colleague to quickly drive to the scene. He saw citizens already shouting for help by the Qinhuai River, and he immediately jumped off the patrol car and into the river. The bone-chilling river water made Shen Guoqiang's whole body tremble, but he swam hard towards the woman, grabbed her clothes, and pulled her forcefully towards the riverbank. At this time, one of Shen Guoqiang's young colleagues also swam over, and together they pulled the red-clothed woman towards the shore. As they neared the shore, the water became shallower, and they could finally stand up, but Shen Guoqiang almost lacked the strength to do so. Fortunately, some citizens gathered by the shore to help, and together they pulled the woman onto the dry land by the shore. The woman was finally saved, but Shen Guoqiang and his colleagues were soaked through, shivering from the cold, and their lips turned pale.

Six consecutive Spring Festivals on duty

Every year during the holiday patrols, he feels most guilty towards his family

"Before, in the army, it was rare to go home during the New Year. Now, in the police force, it's also rare to go home," Shen Guoqiang said. Although he works hard, he feels satisfied knowing that he helps bring safe holidays to thousands of families.
