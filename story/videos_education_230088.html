[Kaya International English] (Taught by Chinese and foreign teachers)

The Kaya International English course is divided into Youth English Courses (Kaya Levels 1-4) and General English Courses (Kaya Levels 5-9). Based on the London English exam syllabus, the course uses vivid thinking teaching methods to develop student interest and aligns with international teaching standards.

Each level of the course corresponds to various levels of the London English exam. Students who pass the exam will receive international certification. Passing the general 4th or 5th level of the London English exam (corresponding to Kaya Level 8 or 9 courses) can replace IELTS and TOEFL scores for studying abroad.

Answering Techniques for Basic Knowledge Ability Questions: The Chinese language exam consists of three parts: basic knowledge, reading comprehension, and writing.

Modern Text Reading Comprehension: Systematic explanation of narrative text, expository text, and argumentative text reading basics, answering techniques, and specialized training. Starting from the knowledge system, guidance is provided on answering techniques.

Quick Writing Explanation and Training: Through teaching how to train one's writing regularly, examination writing answering techniques are explained.
