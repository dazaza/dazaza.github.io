The court found that when the traffic accident occurred, the right front of the vehicle driven by Zhang Zibiao made contact with the victim's body, and the vehicle chassis guard touched the fallen victim's body. As the driver of the vehicle, Zhang Zibiao should have had a clear feeling at the time of the accident. Therefore, Zhang Zibiao's in-court defense that "he did not know his car hit someone at the time of the incident" and his lawyer's argument that "Zhang Zibiao did not discover the accident due to being unconscious from drinking" are against common sense and inconsistent with the facts of this case, so the court does not accept it.

At 20:40 on the day of the incident, Zhang Zibiao turned himself in to the police. According to the public security traffic management department's determination, Zhang Zibiao is fully responsible for this accident due to driving under the influence and fleeing the scene after the accident.

(Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 22nd) Lawyer Zhang Zibiao fled the scene after causing a traffic accident while driving a BMW under the influence of alcohol, resulting in the death of a 75-year-old woman. On March 22nd, Chaoyang District Court of Beijing announced the first-instance verdict on this traffic accident case: The defendant Zhang Zibiao was sentenced to 2 years and 6 months in prison for committing the crime of traffic accident.

In addition, after the traffic accident occurred, there were eyewitnesses who chased Zhang Zibiao on a motorcycle and clearly informed him of the accident. Despite this, Zhang Zibiao still refused to return to the accident scene, and his behavior constitutes fleeing the scene after a traffic accident.

During the trial, Zhang Zibiao stated that he had no objection to the charge of traffic accident offense but argued that he did not know his car hit someone at the time of the incident. After being informed, he thought it was someone trying to commit insurance fraud ("bumping porcelain"), so his leaving the accident scene was not considered as fleeing.

Regarding the result of the first-instance verdict, Zhang Zibiao told the court that he needs to consider whether to appeal.

According to the court, Zhang Zibiao, aged 39, is a lawyer at a law firm in Beijing. At around 14:10 on June 29th, 2010, Zhang Zibiao drove a small car under the influence of alcohol, traveling westward from east along Tangjia Village Road in Chaoyang District. The front part of the vehicle hit pedestrian Zhang Lao Tai, aged 75, resulting in her death. Afterwards, despite being informed by others about the accident, Zhang Zibiao still fled the scene.

During the investigation period by the police, Zhang Zibiao's relatives compensated the victim's family for all economic losses totaling RMB 650,000. The victim's family has expressed understanding towards Zhang Zibiao.
