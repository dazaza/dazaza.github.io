Copybook <br/>

<br/>

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------<br/>

Job restriction: None Remarks: The mission is also known as Amber Egg Three<br/>

Required level: Lv 1 Recommended level: Lv 20+; 5 people<br/>

Time limit: None Starting location<br/>

Elsa Island Kalab Temple<br/>

Repeatable: Yes Boss battle: Yes<br/>

Mission reward: New skills Poison Strike and Swift Decisiveness learned, obtain items [Blood Earring Replica] and [Activity Funds]<br/>

<br/>

<br/>

1. During the time from dusk to night, investigate at Yorke Temple on the third floor of Zakte Hyema Kalab Temple (39,21), and obtain [Amber Egg]<br/>

<br/>

2. Go to Mabel Pass (170,120), speak with Martins to enter a battle<br/>

*Martins LV20 HP about 650 Skills used: Attack, Defense, Attack Reflection, Magic Reflection, Healing Magic (double action)<br/>

After winning, one member of the party will receive [Magic Tome Copy]; teleported to Mabel Pass (135,104)<br/>

Then speak with Martins again and choose "Yes" or "No" to enter battle<br/>

*This method can be repeated until every member of the team has obtained the item; the mission item only needs to be held by the leader<br/>

<br/>

2. Go to Gerebren Forest (245,73) and talk with Nase, choose "Yes"<br/>

*Choosing "No" will cause the mission item to be taken away, allowing you to restart the mission<br/>

<br/>

3. During the time from dusk to night, go to Kalab Temple and speak with Hotepe (92,138), choose "Yes"<br/>

*Or speak with Hotepe in Kuroli's shop in Elsa Island (150.125) (appears all day), choosing "No" will cause the mission item to be taken away, allowing you to restart the mission<br/>

<br/>

4. Go to Elsa Island (102,115) and speak with Angela (55,32) inside the Adventurer's Inn, choose "Yes"<br/>

<br/>

5. Enter through the southern gate of Elsa Island to Mabel Pass, then cross the bridge at the southeast corner of the map (250.172) to reach Brachim Heights, find the yellow teleportation stone (202.266) to enter the wormhole, locate Nase who appears randomly, speak with him and choose "Yes" to be teleported to ???<br/>

<br/>

6. Speak with Xuanwu (195,32), choose "Yes" to enter a battle<br/>

*Xuanwu LV20 HP about 2000 Skills used: Attack, Defense, Qiankun Throw, Meteor Magic, Petrification Attack, Confusion Attack (double action)<br/>

After winning, speak with Xuanwu, then talk to the two skill masters behind him to learn skills<br/>

Speak with Nase (232,57) to learn the skill Swift Decisiveness, cost 6000G<br/>

Talk with Koben (228,57) to learn the skill Poison Strike, cost 6000G<br/>

<br/>

7. Use the teleportation stone at (230,57) to return to Brachim Heights (208,281), enter the wormhole again to reach the lowest level???<br/>

<br/>

8. From night to dawn, speak with Angela (50,113) to enter a battle<br/>

*Angela LV25 HP about 1600 Skills used: Wind Blade Magic, Toxic Magic, Summon Igsam (Igsam 0 additional)(double action)<br/>

Igsam*7 LV17~19 HP about 800 Skills used: Toxic Attack, Wind Blade Magic<br/>

<br/>

After victory, randomly obtain [Earrings?] (Replica of Blood Earrings) or [Gold Block?] (Activity Funds), teleported to Hotepe. Speak with Hotepe and hand over the [Magic Tome Copy], then return to Brachim Heights (208,281), completing the mission<br/>
