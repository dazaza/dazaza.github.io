Bald man's subway proposal goes viral online, claims engagement ring costs 10 yuan

by zxyhszzfz on 2011-06-03 09:37:21

Morning Post 96101 Hotline News (Reported by Xianfeng Li) Recently, all major video websites and Weibo have been sharing a proposal video: On subway line 10, a bald man suddenly proposed to his girlfriend, even taking out a ring he had prepared in advance "bought for 10 yuan", the woman was initially hesitant. Amidst the encouragement of passengers shouting "go for it" and "marry him," she eventually embraced the bald man.

Video: Bald man successfully proposes to his girlfriend inside the subway, source: Liaoning TV's "First Time."

The video is 3 minutes and 32 seconds long. From the content of the video, the proposal occurred inside a train on Line 10 heading towards Bagou, filmed by a passenger who witnessed the event. In the video, a bald man around 27 or 28 years old, dressed in a white suit, kneels half-way with a bouquet of flowers in front of a woman wearing yellow clothes with long hair. The bald man says that he has already found a good job and hopes to live together with his girlfriend, "I will work harder!" He also openly tells the passengers present that three years ago, he and his girlfriend met at the subway station, and he hopes that his girlfriend will definitely accept his proposal while they are on the subway. Upon hearing this confession, the passengers in the carriage were moved and shouted "go for it," "marry him!"

Faced with this "sudden attack," the bald man's girlfriend was caught off guard. As she hesitated, the bald man handed her the flowers, admitting: "The ring is already bought, for 10 yuan." Subsequently, he took out the ring, zxy_Sina Blog A, put it on his girlfriend's hand, and amidst the cheers of the passengers, kissed his girlfriend in public.

After being uploaded to the internet, the video received tens of thousands of clicks. Although there have been previous examples of proposals on the subway, most netizens still approved of the bald man's actions.
