Yang Jia's living environment

by changkaiji on 2008-07-22 10:30:15

The building No. 407 where Yang Jia lived has become a global focus overnight, but the residents living next to it did not feel anything unusual. Therefore, the police's frequent patrols back and forth seemed a bit exaggerated. As a result, Beijing newspapers published "Yang's family corridor was filled with cigarette butts, allegedly left by the police" and "Yang's door remained closed all day" such "scandals". They were not gossipy, not interesting, and lacked观赏性和可读性!

The situation of Yang's family is somewhat similar to our community. The matter is somewhat serious: because we are located next to the Bird's Nest, every move becomes international news. When walls in Beijing streets were painted with messages about prosperity and harmony, no one complained. But when our community's wall was painted, the news immediately appeared on European newspapers. The matter is also somewhat trivial: the residents here are ordinary city people, lacking prominent figures. So, no matter how much noise is made, it's just a trivial matter that can't make a big impact. Just like Yang Jia's case, wasn't it just because the police mistakenly thought you rode a stolen bike and detained you for a few days? Wasn't it just because you disliked the police's attitude? How could it possibly lead to killing six people?! Besides, the police have already apologized to you and promised compensation. You still think it's insufficient. Even if it is insufficient, it doesn't justify killing people. Maybe the police even saved you. Think about it, how much could you earn working in Shanghai?

But poor Mrs. Yang, she lost her husband, and now she has lost her son. What wrong did Mrs. Yang do?!

Our community is now quite interesting.

We received the latest notice: Starting from July 10th, our community will fully close the four small gates - East Gate, South Gate, West Gate, North Gate. It's probably asking us to fly out. Han Hong said, "That is a magical sky road, bringing us closer to paradise..." In fact, there is still one gate left open, and it's not small. Cars...
