It is understood that the Indian company R M INTERNATIONAL, which has participated in two consecutive yarn fairs, will continue to join this session of the yarn fair and introduce traditional high-quality pure cotton yarn products at this session. The company manager Rajesh Mittal introduced, "The demand for pure cotton yarn in China is extremely large, so the market prospect is very optimistic. The sales performance of our company's products in China is also relatively ideal, so we attach great importance to the large market in China." It is understood that R M INTERNATIONAL is a textile trading company mainly dealing with yarns, fibers, fabrics, and garments. Its products are mainly sold to foreign countries such as China, Bangladesh, Turkey, and Italy, and have established good cooperative relationships with some local textile enterprises.

Pakistan's Opulent Company, which plans to expand its presence in the Chinese market with blended yarns, also has exhibition plans and will showcase various colored blended yarns at this yarn fair, including acrylic and cotton blended yarns, modal and tencel blended yarns. Most of the raw materials for these products come from countries like Pakistan, the United States, and Australia, ensuring high-quality products. After years of development, Opulent has successively established offices in Malaysia and Canada, and in 2010, it also established an office in China to better expand its business in China.

The exhibits of this session of the yarn fair will cover functional fibers, differentiated fibers, high-performance fibers, special fibers, and new chemical fibers widely concerned by the industry, such as low-melting-point fibers, solution-dyed polyester ultrafine fibers, slub yarns, special cloud-spot yarns, filament short fiber core-spun yarns, compact-spun yarns, Tencel, Modal, water-soluble yarns, zero-twist yarns, and hollow yarns. These products fully reflect the current trend of yarn R&D and public consumption demands.

Foreign companies bullish on the Chinese market make strong landings

Baoding Zhongfang Yi Cotton Textile Co., Ltd. is one of the earliest domestic companies to produce Lyocell Tencel, Lenzing Modal, Tianzhu fiber, and other new fiber yarns on a large scale. In this session of the yarn fair, Zhongfang Yi Cotton will continue to exhibit differentiated fiber, multi-component blended yarn series, and other advantageous products, mainly including Tencel, Tianzhu fiber, hemp fiber, conductive fiber, water-soluble polyvinyl alcohol, fine denier acrylic, and other mixed spun yarns.

At this session of the yarn fair, visitors will be able to experience functional fibers up close, such as coffee charcoal, cool jade, and beautiful spinning series yarns. Coffee charcoal has heat preservation functions, cool jade offers cooling functions, while beautiful spinning features green environmental protection, strong anti-pilling, soft hand feel, smooth texture, and good breathability. Participants can experience energy-saving and environmentally-friendly differentiated fibers mainly composed of ATPDP fibers. ATPDP fibers are the latest eco-friendly products that can be dyed and printed under normal temperature and pressure conditions, featuring excellent normal temperature and pressure dyeing characteristics and high uptake rates. They can be interwoven with any natural fiber to achieve cotton-like, silk-like, and wool-like effects. This product has bright colors, high color absorption rates, and good lightfastness. The successful launch of ATPDP fibers creates conditions for further energy conservation, emission reduction, and environmental protection in textiles. During the printing and dyeing and post-processing processes of ATPDP, water usage can be significantly reduced, with preliminary estimates showing savings of 25-30% in electricity and dye chemicals.

Dezhou Huayuan Ecological Technology Co., Ltd. is a well-known multi-fiber blended yarn producer in the industry, specializing in the development of new fiber yarns. Its main products include new cellulose fibers, fine denier fibers, flame-retardant fiber yarns, cashmere blended yarns, silk blended yarns, hemp fiber blended yarns, differentiated functional fiber yarns, organic cotton yarns, etc. Dezhou Huayuan uses over 50 types of fibers and nearly 100 specifications. At this session of the yarn fair, Dezhou Huayuan will launch moisture-absorbing heat-generating yarns, moisture-absorbing quick-drying composite yarns, fine denier high-strength viscose yarns, and vortex-spun functional yarns. These new types of yarns have obtained patents domestically and have broad market prospects. Additionally, Dezhou Huayuan will showcase comprehensive textile yarn system solutions at the yarn fair, designing different products for customers with personalized needs.

This session of the exhibition will comprehensively display the theme of "fashion + technology + low carbon" with a brand-new concept and posture, reflecting the current trends and latest trends in the global yarn industry. To promote product innovation in the industry, enhance corporate competitiveness, and influence the Chinese fiber brand, the exhibition organizer has specially invited the Nylon Special Committee of the China Chemical Fiber Industry Association and the Textile Chemical Fiber Product Development Center to collaborate with nylon enterprises to create the "Colorful Life, Fashionable Nylon" exhibition area and events. Concentrating on new fiber functions developed independently by nylon and related enterprises, such as moisture-wicking, anti-static, antibacterial, far-infrared, negative ions, conductive, anti-ultraviolet, flame retardant, high strength, and composite ultrafine, fine denier irregular shapes, high elasticity, full opacity, whitening, and eco-friendly color spinning, among others.

Fashion leads trends, technology brings enjoyment

After years of accumulation and development, the exhibition function of the China International Textile Yarn Exhibition continues to be enriched and perfected. Based on gathering the most complete industry information and real market trends, this session of the exhibition will release the 2012/2013 yarn product trend, guiding enterprises to upgrade their technology and innovate products, showcasing yarns, fibers, knitted goods, and knitted fabrics with high technical content and a strong sense of fashion to industry professionals. This will promote the development of new technologies and products in the upstream industries and improve the fashion level of downstream products. This session of the exhibition will continue to build a fair, interactive, and flexible business platform for enterprises. Nearly a hundred well-known domestic and international yarn and fiber enterprises will not only showcase their latest products but also seek more strategic partners to enrich the variety of yarns through joint efforts.

Exhibitions are the barometers of the industry. Through exhibitions, one can observe phenomena, essence, and trends. Foreign enterprises will strongly enter the Chinese market in this session of the exhibition. Textile yarn enterprises from cotton-producing giants such as India, Pakistan, and Turkey will participate as groups. Many overseas famous enterprises will gather in Beijing at that time. Besides hoping to find agents in China through participation, they also hope to attract more professional buyers from around the world through one of the few specialized yarn and fiber exhibitions in the world. Here, yarn producers and sellers from various countries can exchange and communicate with professionals from the yarn and fiber kingdom, finding more new products, technologies, and friends through the exhibition.

In this session of the exhibition, visitors can also see innovations in traditional products, such as filament short fiber composite core-spun yarn series. Filament short fiber core-spun yarn is a core-sheath type composite core-spun yarn produced with functional filaments as the core. The short fibers in the sheath can provide a soft hand feel, full fabric surface, skin-friendly stability, and excellent moisture absorption, breathability, and wicking performance. The long filament core can provide strength, elasticity, shape retention, drapability, crispness, and other special functions to the fabric.

In the spring of 2012, the China International Textile Yarn (Spring/Summer) Expo once again made a grand appearance. To meet the needs of numerous domestic and international textile enterprises and professional buyers, the Spring/Summer Yarn Fair from March 29 to March 31, 2012, will be held regularly at Pavilion 1 and 2 of the China International Trade Center in Beijing as one of the three major activities of the "March Textile Spring Market".
