70s, 80s, 90s?

by huiming on 2011-12-22 16:36:50

Workaholics are basically those born in the 70s!

Those born in the 80s: refuse to work overtime!

Those born in the 90s: refuse to go to work!

The topics of those born in the 70s are either work or stocks.

The topics of those born in the 80s are more diverse, such as the Premier League, Warcraft...

For those born in the 90s: QQ level, QQ Show...

If those born in the 70s have a laptop, they like to use it in public places.

Those born in the 80s wouldn't carry such heavy things around.

For those born in the 90s: we only want Apple laptops, and not just one...

Those born in the 70s like to drink red wine, usually Great Wall red wine.

Those born in the 80s either don't drink alcohol or drink beer.

For those born in the 90s: Korean juice, Japanese soda...

Whenever those born in the 70s see a standing leader, they will immediately offer their seat to the leader.

Those born in the 80s advocate equality between superiors and subordinates.

For those born in the 90s: heaven and earth, I am the only one!

When those born in the 70s marry, they want to marry a virgin.

Those born in the 80s think it doesn't matter, as long as the feelings between each other are good enough.

For those born in the 90s: do we need feelings to get married?.. Do we need to get married?..

Those born in the 70s have savings.

Those born in the 80s are in debt.

For those born in the 90s: we have dad!

Those born in the 70s make friends with people who have background and status.

Those born in the 80s make friends with like-minded people.

For those born in the 90s: we befriend handsome guys full of tattoos!

On weekends, those born in the 70s invite clients for dinner.

On weekends, those born in the 80s invite classmates to play football.

For those born in the 90s: there are seven days of weekend in a week, do whatever we want!

In those born in the 70s' homes, you need to take off your shoes when entering.

In those born in the 80s' homes, you don't need to take off your shoes when entering.

For those born in the 90s: we don't even take off our shoes when going to bed!

During meals, those born in the 70s like to sit next to the boss.

It's best for those born in the 80s not to sit next to the boss, that way they can be unrestrained.

For those born in the 90s: I am the boss!
