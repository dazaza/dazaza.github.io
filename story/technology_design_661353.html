Company installs fingerprint puncher, employees buy fingerprint molds online to skip check-in

by fhfhk9rur on 2012-02-08 22:58:04

Tutorial for making fingerprint online by reporter Zhang Jie "What can I do? I was late for work yesterday morning and didn't make it to punch the fingerprint time card at the office, which is really frustrating." Xiao Huang who works in the southern part of the city was originally at a loss about this, but then a colleague gave him a solution - to buy a fingerprint mold online to deal with the time card punching and said that it's very effective.

New regulations met at the start of the new year: the company installed fingerprint time clocks. Xiao Huang lives in the eastern part of the city and works in a company in the south, with nearly an hour's commute each day. He often gets stuck in traffic and is late. Just before the Spring Festival this year, the company bought fingerprint time clocks for all departments and required them to be used for attendance starting after the Spring Festival. Being late three times in a month would affect performance evaluations, causing Xiao Huang great difficulty. On the first day back to work after the holiday on February 9th, Xiao Huang was already 20 minutes late.

One of Xiao Huang's colleagues said that there are "fingerprints" for sale online, very convenient. If one has something to attend to and comes late, an early-coming colleague could help punch the card.

When Xiao Huang checked online, he found out that this kind of "fingerprint mold" was quite popular, with glowing reviews. Xiao Huang said that if everyone deals with it this way, then the company's attendance method would be meaningless.

Very popular online: fingerprint molds clone fingers to deal with time clocks. Yesterday, we found multiple sellers selling fingerprint molds online. A seller from Shandong Province said that their series of products come in Package A and Package B. Package A includes the materials and tools for making the mold, along with instructions, where buyers make it themselves and they guarantee to teach how. This way, there is no risk of leaking personal fingerprint information. Package B is where the seller makes the fingerprint mold for the buyer.

A seller from Jiangxi Province has already sold 65 items, with such advertisement phrases on the webpage as "one fingerprint mold can escape attendance checks, benefiting many white-collar workers greatly." Among the buyers' comments, one buyer said: "Already bought three times, very useful. Bought again for friends." However, some individual buyers said "did not pass."

It is introduced that the so-called fingerprint mold is made of silicone gel imitating human body, with different prices, and the passing rate on fingerprint attendance machines is almost 100%, generally costing around 100 yuan.

Lawyer's opinion: Do not seek temporary convenience and leave behind future troubles. Although many people choose to buy "fingerprint molds" and "fingerprint sleeves" online to cope with attendance punching systems, many others worry whether letting others use their fingerprints to punch cards might pose security risks.

Director lawyer Wang Liping of Shaanxi Shangwen Law Firm said that as a type of personal biological information, fingerprint information indeed should raise enough awareness for protection among people. In legal terms, there is no explicit regulation prohibiting the trading of fingerprint information, but there is a clear prohibition against the trading of human organs; in the much-anticipated Personal Information Security Protection Law across various sectors of society, what people pay more attention to are basic personal information. With the development of society, biometric recognition technology will be increasingly applied, such as access control systems, internal authorization systems in banks, etc. Many internal authorizations in banks were previously completed through password input, but now there are those done through fingerprint recognition authorization. Therefore, the protection of fingerprint information should be taken seriously by people, and one should not seek temporary convenience and leave behind future troubles, being careful that personal information is not exploited by lawbreakers.

Company's attitude: No need to take shortcuts. Mr. Zhang, who works in a public institution, said that last year at the beginning of the year, their unit also implemented fingerprint time clocks. Because he sends his son to school in the morning and arrives at the unit relatively early, colleagues entrusted him with the task of punching cards. During the first collection of fingerprint information, six of his fingers were "borrowed" by colleagues. Later, the punching system only became a formality and was eventually canceled.

The director of the office of a real estate company said that he specifically handles attendance. After entering the unit in 2005, he found that employees lacked discipline, so he introduced the fingerprint time clock system, with very obvious results. When he heard that there were fingerprint molds for sale online, he was quite surprised. He said that he had specially conducted experiments before, pressing fingerprints on white paper and then scanning them, but they couldn't be recorded because the fingerprint time clock not only identifies fingerprints but also senses a certain body temperature. However, he didn't expect someone to invent fingerprint molds. Regarding this, he expressed that the purpose of the attendance system is to strengthen employees' sense of discipline and time management, forming self-restraint. If people take shortcuts like this, it's really unnecessary.
