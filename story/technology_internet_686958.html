Feel the warmth! 16 (This is also warmth)

by obanma520 on 2012-03-02 12:08:33

February 12, 2012, Sunday, Sunny to Cloudy

The temperature had just started to rise again, but now it's cloudy. The forecast said that there would be a cold wave recently. No wonder Xiao Cao woke up in the middle of the night with uncomfortable legs yesterday, which kept her awake and only fell asleep at around four o'clock in the morning. Therefore, she got up late in the morning! On the previous day, after Xiao Cao washed the clothes, she went online as a break. Her son called, saying that he was on a business trip with his colleagues, eating outside, and would come back soon! Oh, since the fifth day of the first lunar month, her son rushed back to his wife's home with his wife, and stayed until the fifteenth day. For a whole month, this young couple didn't have to worry about food and drink, they were so happy (dialect)! Haha! When her son came back, he didn't even change his shoes. In one big step, he sat down on the sofa. Xiao Cao knew from his posture that he wouldn't stay long. They briefly chatted, and he said that someone came to the office today, and according to YHQKJ regulations, the amount taken was still short by one thousand yuan, so he came back to ask if Xiao Cao had any? Xiao Cao quickly gave it to him. He said the car was waiting downstairs, and they had to go back in the afternoon. Therefore, this time he came in a hurry and left in a rush!

From what her son said, there would be some adjustments in work at the beginning of the year, so he would have fewer business trips here in the future. This also meant that her son would have fewer chances to come back than before, and it would not be easy to see her son often. Therefore, her son would have fewer chances to eat the meals made by Xiao Cao, so these days after he left, Xiao Cao felt a bit melancholy when she thought about it!

These days, there wasn't much real work, but there were quite a few trivial things. Yesterday was Saturday, because her grandson wanted to eat dumplings on Lantern Festival night, and there was no time that day. So Xiao Cao bought good leeks at the supermarket yesterday, made vegetable dumplings, and froze more than thirty dumplings on a steamer tray. After they were cooked and cooled, she let them take all of them away today! Since she was busy during the end of the twelfth lunar month, she didn't have time to perm her bangs, so she went to Pure Passion Romantic Story Comic after lunch yesterday. As a result, the price increased by five yuan, and it cost twenty yuan for just that little bit of hair! Haha!

Today is Sunday, her daughter and grandson are going to class, and will come back for lunch. There is also a relative's child (who delivers pork) who needs to come home for treatment after the New Year, so Xiao Cao went to the market for the first time after the New Year. It was already past ten o'clock, but the market was still bustling with people!

As soon as she crossed the street, the phone rang. Oh, it was Xiao Cao's teacher, who had a great influence on her during school, and often visited her workplace when she was working. Now that Xiao Cao has retired, they don't see each other anymore. Maybe because Xiao Cao changed her number, he didn't know, so we had less contact over the past year. Before the New Year, he called, saying that he asked her colleagues for Xiao Cao's number, and also mentioned that he saw the book "Collection of Essays" which contained several of Xiao Cao's articles. When he learned where Xiao Cao lived, he said that his daughter also lived in this neighborhood, so Xiao Cao quickly said that when the teacher comes to visit his daughter, please call and come over for a chat! Therefore, during the New Year, Xiao Cao sent the teacher a New Year greeting message! The reason for his call today was: first, asking what Xiao Cao mainly does after retirement? Second, asking if she wants to join their Elderly Association organization! Xiao Cao replied: I haven't done anything serious all day, and the things I do are all trivial, taking care of housework and wandering around. Then Xiao Cao asked about the situation of this association. The teacher said: Now that you're not working, participating in these organizations is also very meaningful for your life after retirement. I am also the HZ of this association. I think your conditions meet the requirements of the association. This is not something anyone can participate in, we also need to review. Oh, that's right, this is also an opportunity to learn from our seniors, broaden social contacts, and make our retired life more colorful. But how do we handle the relevant procedures? "This is it, when there is an activity, I'll notify you, you can listen first and then see!" The teacher knows Xiao Cao very well, so he said this. This is the teacher's concern for Xiao Cao, and it's a good thing, why wouldn't Xiao Cao be happy? Haha!

Last night, Xiao Cao took the meat dishes out of the fridge. Today in the market, there were no vegetables that could attract her appetite, but when she saw the newly listed garlic sprouts, they were all thin ones, and Xiao Cao likes thin garlic sprouts, so she had the desire to buy them!

This vegetable must have come from the south, so it's rare, and it definitely won't be cheap. 7.50 yuan, that's reasonable! But the bunches are all over a pound, which feels a bit too much. This fresh and expensive vegetable, one plate is enough to taste. A man picked up a small bunch, looked at it, and put it down. What happened? Xiao Cao saw that the layer of skin at the bottom was all rubbed off. Only heard the man say: the bunch shouldn't be so small! Xiao Cao also thought the same, "Then let's split this bunch!" Oh, the man asked Xiao Cao, "Okay, see if they agree?" "How can they disagree when it's already split?" Therefore, half for 3.50 yuan, bagged and handed over, the other half for 4.50 yuan, "Which bunch do you want?" Xiao Cao politely asked him. "Whatever! It's only one yuan difference!" "Oh, then I'll take this bunch!" Xiao Cao had fewer people eating at noon, and her son-in-law wasn't coming back, so one dish was enough! Actually, this method of "split-buying" is really good! Haha!

When her daughter came in, her grandson immediately said: Wow! Grandma has cleaned the house so neatly! Ha ha, you see, her grandson who spoke late when she was young, now talks so well, haha!

This fatty meat was given by relatives-in-law! The garlic sprouts are very tender, almost nothing to throw away, this is very practical, rice tastes very fragrant!

Her grandson said he was hungry when he saw Xiao Cao, originally didn't like eating warm rice in class in the morning! Then let's fry dumplings! "I want to eat six!" The child must be hungry, finished in one breath, so when eating rice, almost didn't eat much, even the meat he usually likes, today he didn't look at it when it was sent to his mouth!

In the platter, there are three kinds of braised dishes, leftovers from the New Year celebration, the whole pieces were taken back by her daughter-in-law to her parents' home. After the New Year, seeing meat is enough, but today after being steamed in the pot, it still smelled good, but still couldn't eat much! This green vegetable is very popular, what is it? It's wild vegetable "gray vegetable"! Haha, only Xiao Cao's family stores so many wild vegetables and green vegetables!

A soup, fried meatballs made by relatives-in-law, soft tofu, Chinese cabbage! Simple and light, all drank it up! Haha!
