Eight Natural and Effective Yogurt Beauty Methods for Soft and Smooth Skin

by lumona35 on 2009-11-20 23:06:32

Skincare masks should not use lactic acid to exert the function of exfoliating masks. Using them daily will make the skin tender and coarse. The method is also very simple, effortless.

1. Relaxing Mask

Pear and yogurt mask. Use once a day. Suitable for oily and external skin.

2. Yogurt Cleansing Mask

Materials: Yogurt and flour

Method:

1. Put an appropriate amount of yogurt and flour in a large bowl and mix evenly into a thin yogurt paste. (Don't make it too thick, otherwise you can't apply the mask evenly)

2. Apply evenly on the whole face, wait for 10~15 minutes, then wash with warm water.

Function: Yogurt mask can be used as a cleanser, no need to clean your face deliberately after use. After using for 4~5 days, your skin will feel renewed.

3. The Most Suitable Yogurt Mask for Neutral Skin

Materials: Honey and a spoon of yogurt or sour cream

Method: Mix a spoon of honey and a spoon of yogurt or sour cream evenly, apply on the cleaned face, keep for 15 minutes and then wash off.

4. Strawberry Mask Fights Your Acne

Materials: 4 strawberries, 1 small spoon of flour, some yogurt, 1 big spoon of honey

Method:

1. Wash the strawberries with clean water, squeeze out the juice.

2. Mix the flour and yogurt first, then add strawberry juice and honey, stir evenly.

3. Apply on the wet face, keep for 15 minutes and then wash off.

5. Avocado Banana Yogurt Mask

Materials: 1/4 of an avocado, 1/2 of a large or 1/4 of a small banana, 1 teaspoon of pure yogurt.

Making Method: Blend avocado, banana and yogurt into a paste, massage on the moist face to promote blood circulation. If you want better results, lay a gauze under the mask, stay on the face for 10 minutes, then wash clean, and finally use moisturizing cream. Once a week.

Effective Reason: The oleic acid in avocados has excellent moisturizing effects, bananas are rich in vitamins A, B, C and E, the lactic acid in yogurt promotes cell renewal, making the skin glow.

6. Yogurt Honey Mask

Materials: Yogurt, honey, whole wheat flour

Method: Take a tablespoon of yogurt and honey, mix them into 6-8 times the amount of whole wheat flour, form a paste, apply on the face and neck, and gently massage until the paste penetrates gradually. Then use a warm towel to wipe from the neck to the face in a circular motion until wiped clean.

7. Yogurt Cucumber Mask

Materials: 3 teaspoons of yogurt, 3 teaspoons of condensed milk, plenty of cucumber slices

Method: Mix yogurt and condensed milk, lie down flat, apply on the face, then put cucumber slices on the face. After 20 minutes, take off the cucumber slices and wash your face. You will find that your skin becomes hydrated and smooth.

8. Yogurt Hair Care Method

Method One: Wash your hair with shampoo, rinse thoroughly, then use yogurt as a conditioner. Your hair won't have the residue problem of shampoo, and feels very soft when touched. Make sure to rinse thoroughly.

Method Two: Mix an appropriate amount of yogurt, one egg yolk, and some olive oil into a paste, use it as a hair care product to massage the scalp. After about 30 minutes, wash carefully with lukewarm water lower than body temperature. The benefited hair will have a renewed feeling.

Function: Yogurt can not only be used for skincare and beauty but also makes hair stronger and shinier. If used 1~2 times per week, it can save the money spent on frequent salon treatments! It keeps the skin hydrated, smooth, elastic, and radiant.
