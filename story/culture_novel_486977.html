Wu Dong Qian Kun Chapter 113: Blood Conversion and Return to Primordial Power

by fanyete on 2011-09-08 23:19:40

"Ho ho!"

Gushes of bloody mists continuously shot out from Jiang Li's body. Finally, these bloody mists slowly shrank and coalesced into a thin blood fetter on the surface of his body. His palms were also wrapped by the blood fetter, with blood thorns protruding at the fingertips, emitting a sinister cold gleam.

All these changes were completed within a short period of time. And when the blood fetter was formed, Jiang Li's aura continued to rise in front of numerous shocked eyes!

Jiang Li was already at the late stage of the Tian Yuan realm now. This level, if enhanced further, would reach the Xiao Yuan Dan realm, which would be an entirely different level from the Tian Yuan realm!

"This is..." Xuan Su looked at Jiang Li whose body was slowly being enveloped by the blood fetter, seemingly recalling something, her beautiful eyes showing a trace of shock.

"Blood Conversion Art." Xia Wanjin beside her said with a low voice.

"Blood Conversion Art?" Xia Zhe Lan and others were stunned.

"It is an extremely ruthless secret manual technique. By using this method, one can evaporate the blood within their body, thus increasing their strength temporarily for a short period of time. However, it has significant aftereffects. After using this technique this time, Jiang Li will probably need at least half a year of recuperation to recover!" Xia Wanjin said slowly.

"To what extent can Jiang Li's strength increase?" Xia Zhe Lan asked hurriedly.

"It should be the Xiao Yuan Dan realm." Xia Wanjin chuckled bitterly and replied.

"The Xiao Yuan Dan realm..."

Upon hearing this, Xia Zhe Lan and others' faces instantly turned pale. Everyone knew about the huge gap between the Tian Yuan realm and the Xiao Yuan Dan realm. They could hold onto a侥幸 attitude thinking Lin Dong could contend with Jiang Li who was at the late stage of the Tian Yuan realm, but they couldn't hope that Lin Dong could defeat Jiang Li who had surged to the Xiao Tian Yuan realm.

Even they themselves couldn't accomplish such a feat.

"This time, we're going to lose..." The previous joy disappeared completely at this moment. Xia Zhe Lan and others became dejected like frost-bitten eggplants.

Seeing the slightly low morale, Xuan Su helplessly sighed softly. No one expected that among the three rounds, this least remarkable Jiang Li was actually the strongest person. No wonder Yue Shan remained calm throughout, he was confident all along.

"Turns out this person still has such skills, my father is indeed wise." On the Blood Wolf Gang side, Yue Feng also looked at Jiang Li who was fully wrapped in blood mist with a look of surprise, saying surprisingly and happily,

"This Jiang Li is a helper I've found with great difficulty. After he uses the Blood Conversion Art, he can reach the Xiao Yuan Dan level. Although this strength may not be top-tier in Flame City, it's invincible among your younger generation. The Alchemy Pool belongs to our Blood Wolf Gang..."

"However, that kid managed to push Jiang Li to this extent, which was beyond my expectation. Judging by the attack he used earlier, there was a fluctuation of spiritual energy, so this person might also be a Rune Master. The Wanjin Trading House does have some abilities..."

"But even so, they are already destined to lose!" Yue Feng laughed and said.
