The front-to-back order of men's clothing coordination:

Underwear / shirt / suit pants / belt / socks / tie / leather shoes / suit

General principles for men's clothing coordination:

The color of the suit set is the main tone of the overall image, so the shirt, tie, shoes, socks, and belt must match the color of the suit set. There should generally not be more than three colors!

If wearing a striped or checkered fabric suit, then the shirt and tie must be without stripes or checks; if wearing a striped or checkered fabric shirt, then the suit and tie must be without stripes or checks; the same applies to the tie.

Dark-colored suits with light-colored shirts, light-colored shirts with dark-colored suits.

People with dark skin should not wear yellow Yougou.com rebate [http://www.taobis.com], people with fair skin have nothing to worry about, but note that the color of the clothing must harmonize with the color of the skin and hair.

Suit:

Fine stripe fabric is more tasteful than coarse stripe large checkers. Long suits short jackets. When buying a suit set, always pay attention to practicality and performance-to-price ratio. For example, if you spend 2000 on a brand-name suit and can wear it more than 8 times a month for 5 years, not only will it look good, but also have a high performance-to-price ratio (average cost per day is 4 yuan). On the contrary, if you buy a poor-quality garment for 80 yuan on the street, after wearing it 5 times, you feel it has no shape and regret throwing it away. If you give it to someone else, they don't want it either, and in the end, you still throw it away. Which one do you think is more cost-effective?

Socks:

Dark-colored socks can match any color of clothing, but what about summer? If wearing light-colored clothing, the sock color can also be lighter, but it must be slightly darker than the clothing color. Throw away your white socks in the shoe cabinet unless you have white sneakers. Do not pair white socks with any color of leather shoes. Socks should cover the calf area and not just reach the ankle. Do not let others see your exposed leg hair; if so, this will be your biggest failure. Dark-colored, refined, simple fabric, and patterned socks are left for girls!

Shirt:

Which man doesn't have a few shirts? Striped shirts are classics in the world of shirts, so you should buy more striped shirts. You can also buy some shirts in soft tones like ivory, gray, and light blue. Striped shirts are easier to coordinate than plain-colored shirts. Long sleeves are more formal than short sleeves, fine stripes are more elegant than coarse stripes. Never wear short-sleeved shirts under a suit, how terrible that would be! The shirt must be well-ironed; if it's wrinkled, it's best not to wear it as even the best suit will lose its value. The shirt cuffs should be slightly longer than the jacket by 1/2 inch, the jacket cuff to the thumb tip should be kept at 5 1/4 inches, and the shirt cuff to the thumb tip should be kept at 4 3/4 inches—this is a rule that men should always bear in mind. If you want to be comfortable all day, leave some space around the collar. 90% of men have their collars tightly fastened, which looks very bloated, and after a whole day, it's either painful or suffocating. People with short necks should choose low-collar shirts, while those with long necks should choose relatively higher collars. Purple shirts look best on camera, perfect for TV interviews or recordings, remember when taking photos.

Shoes:

Laced leather shoes are more dignified and formal, slip-ons are more suitable for jackets and loose clothing. Please wear laced leather shoes in formal occasions! Black leather shoes can match any color of clothing and can be used in any occasion! Low-heeled leather shoes show more gentlemanly demeanor, rugged thick-soled leather shoes are only suitable for alternative styles! Don't let your leather shoes get dusty, clean them every two days. Bright leather shoes give people a cool and well-educated feeling.

Tie:

The soul lies here—dark-colored, small-patterned ties have high authority, diagonal stripes, dark grids, geometric patterns of ties are more acceptable. Do not wear ties with airplane, tank, or golf ball patterns—you're not a millionaire. If the outside is not too complicated and the color is simple, then you can pair it with a more intricate patterned tie. If both the shirt and tie are patterned, it will create a dizzying effect and make it hard to find a visual focus.

Checkered shirts pair well with small polka-dot ties.

Striped shirts can go with plain, diagonal, regularly dotted small floral, or polka-dot patterned ties.

In recent years, the trend has been to pair shirts and ties in the same color scheme, with the tie being a deeper shade than the shirt, making the combination more balanced.

Belt and suspenders:

The belt and shoes should match in color, preferably matching the watch strap. A black belt can be used with any color of clothing. The belt should not be too wide or too thin; too wide suits jeans, too thin suits girls. The buckle should be appropriately sized, gold being the best color, silver also being pretty good. Suspenders should match the color of the clothing. Either wear suspenders or belts, not both. But if your waist is not thick, I advise you not to use them, as Chinese people generally don't suit them!

Modern product: jeans. Nowadays, jeans are divided into formal jeans and fashion jeans. Formal jeans can be paired with casual or formal shirts or T-shirts and can be worn in formal occasions, matching formal leather shoes. Fashion jeans are more stylish and casual, pairing with casual leather shoes or sports shoes makes you look younger and more energetic.

Men's clothing accessory decoration:

Jewelry should be exquisite and simple, the simpler the better. Don't wear flashy jewelry; taste and class lie here. Tie clips are no longer in fashion, don't use them!

Men's clothing coordination for coats, overcoats, raincoats, and umbrellas.

Preferred colors for coats and overcoats are blue, black, camel, and charcoal gray. Wool material is the best choice, and the color difference from suits is irrelevant. Beige raincoats are classic, blue ones are acceptable, but never use black ones as they might be mistaken for garbage piles. China does not have good umbrellas, so without London fog, there's no need to carry one. It's not shameful for a man to get wet in the rain.
