Love, it's like a poppy!

by xiaoxiao67 on 2012-03-02 15:38:43

Love, like a poppy!

Love is always like a poppy flower. Even though we know it's full of temptation, we still rush towards it like moths to a flame. Even if we are reduced to ashes, we have no regrets. Love also carries how much darkness you don't know.

I am Su Xiaomo. My parents gave me this beautiful name, but they did not give me the corresponding happiness. They were not good parents, and I was not the "adorable princess" in their eyes.

I started dating at 16. So far, I've had many boyfriends. Generally, I break up without hesitation within three months, as if bound by a curse that cannot be escaped. Apparently, love, for me, is just a luxury. Every time a boyfriend cries after breaking up, I remain unmoved. I harshly say, "Even if you beg me to get back together now, I will still break up with you within a week!" That's what kind of woman I am - a woman who warms up quickly and cools down quickly. When I love him, I do everything for him; once I stop loving him, I start to feel annoyed. I think there may only be a woman like me in this world, so decisive and proud.

Who I gave my first time to, I've already forgotten. They say I'm a "promiscuous" woman, but I don't care. I always thought I would continue living like this, not getting married, just having someone around me. But since I met Yuwen Xuan, my small pride began to unconsciously slow down. This reminded me of the words Zhang Dacai wrote to Mr. Hu: "When she saw him, she became very humble, humbling herself to the dust, but she was happy in her heart, and flowers bloomed from the dust."

Yuwen Xuan

On a summer night in June, I accidentally saw the legendary witch - Su Xiaomo again at the disco. Her waist twisting in the dance pool was like a water snake, seductive and charming... average beauty, not tall, but with an extremely perfect figure... As a normal man, such a woman makes me want to act on impulse...

I've never lacked women, but I've never understood these women, nor have I ever wanted to understand them. However, regarding Su Xiaomo, I know a lot. Such as: she's a thoroughly promiscuous woman, with a strange personality, no close friends, many boyfriends, from a good family, and her parents are well-known in our town... etc...

That night, I sat in a dark corner watching everything about this woman. I watched her crazy dancing posture, watched her drink alone "Chivas Regal 12 Years", watched her flirt with the men around her, watched her lonely back... Finally, I saw her go out with a drunken man who came to chat her up. She swayed slightly when she walked, she was drunk... Seeing them leave the disco, I got up, brushed off the wrinkles on my body, and tried to follow them. Unfortunately, Lin Ya blocked me and said, "Xuan, my dad wants to talk to you..." Only then did I come to my senses, realizing that my soul had left my body.

In the following week, I could see Su Xiaomo in this disco every day, and I continued to watch everything about this woman in the dark. She was like a poppy in the dark, blooming brilliantly under the ambiguous lights, emitting a tempting and fatal fragrance. One day, I was inexplicably restless, leaning against the wooden fence outside the bar, smoking fiercely one puff after another, dizzy yet clear-headed, with a desire to numb myself...

"Sir, can I borrow your lighter?" I was about to get angry at being disturbed, but when I opened my eyes, I saw Su Xiaomo looking at me lazily, raising her hand with a "555" cigarette, "My lighter ran out of gas..." She skillfully lit the cigarette, took a deep puff, "Thank you," and turned away. I saw her cigarette flicker in the darkness, sharp and clear. "Su Xiaomo," I softly called out, she turned her head and smiled at me, "You've been watching me recently, I know." Look, what a smart woman.

She likes smoking because the spicy smoke helps her red-eye, allowing her tears to fall naturally, just a flimsy excuse for her own vulnerability.

Lin Ya

When I met Yuwen Xuan, I was 14, he was 16. That year, the summer was dim, the wind blew fiercely, and the flying sand blinded our eyes. Dad brought a dirty child, whose eyes were clear as water, but filled with resistance, coldly. "Little Ya, this is Big Brother Xiao Xuan, from now on, he will live with us, you two must love each other!"

We lived together for ten years. At first, his eyes were always filled with hostility, later the hostility disappeared, but he remained cold. He listened to Dad, was good to me, but I knew he only felt a sibling relationship with me. And I, fell helplessly in love with him.

That day, I noticed his gaze, with a distracted look. Following his gaze, I saw the woman named Su Xiaomo. In the early hours of the morning, when Xuan saw Su Xiaomo go out, his figure had an aura of anger. Just as he got up, I lied to him...

Su Xiaomo

At "Ran. Encounter," I always felt a pair of male eyes staring at me from behind. My intuition told me that this man was a little special. I tried to find him, but always encountered strange looks. Eventually, I found this deep pair of eyes in that dark corner. In the few seconds of eye contact, there was a predestined entanglement. I knew it was him who was watching me. I always used my peripheral vision to casually notice this man - distinct features, a strong face full of maturity. Yes, it was him.

I planned for a long time, but unexpectedly saw him alone outside the disco that day. There was a tangled pain on his face. I wanted to smooth out those tangles. I went up to borrow fire, deliberately disturbing his thoughts. His pupils dilated, surprised and delighted. I turned and left, determining that he would definitely call out my name. The long-planned conspiracy finally succeeded. That night, we chatted like old friends for years, I've never said so many words before. I found a long-lost happiness, a pure happiness that hasn't appeared since I was five.

Yuwen Xuan, handsome and strong, a man with charisma, that's my evaluation of him. We fell in love, and this feeling was different from any of my previous romances. The spell on me seemed to crumble instantly.

Yuwen Xuan

She said, she has never talked to anyone so much.

She said, she just wants a warm home, that's all.

Su Xiaomo didn't have friends since childhood. The sisters around her envied her background, envied her temperament, so no one wanted to be her friend. She dated many boyfriends, just to make herself feel safe and warm, but the passion always faded quickly, as if cursed.

Since childhood, she has lived a luxurious life, like a little princess in a palace. In others' eyes, she could have everything, they only envied her, but she always lived with her reflection in the mirror, constantly plagued by loneliness. Her father is the chairman of "Shenhua Group Company" in our small county. He has a lot of money, more than I can count. Her mother is also a strong woman, working in the government department, a high-ranking official.

We fell in love, a very subtle feeling. After meeting her, I no longer wanted to tease feelings like before. I wanted to settle down and live a stable life with her... But, what virtue or ability do I have to give her the warmth she desires? Is it destined that we can only look at each other from afar, never to meet?!

But, Su Xiaomo, I swear, you are the woman I want for the rest of my life.

Lin Ya

Xuan still fell in love with the woman named Su Xiaomo. I often woke up crying in the darkness. The fragmented scenes in my dreams were vivid, all related to their stories. I wanted to stay away from your world, I wanted to tell myself that their happiness had nothing to do with me. But, the endless entanglement devoured my fragile heart, as if I was on the brink of collapse... How could I tolerate the man I loved for ten years hugging another woman... Su Xiaomo, I won't let you go.

Everything was under control, as expected, Su Xiaomo and Xuan broke up. Under my instigation, Dad arranged our engagement, I sweetly smiled. When I saw Xuan's hollow eyes, my heart was bitten by selfishness, hurting painfully... Tears in my eyes, telling myself, Xuan, you belong to me...

Fireworks rose into the air, dazzling for a moment, then ended their destiny. Perhaps I am even more pitiful than fireworks.

But, Su Xiaomo, you lost. You are destined not to be together.

Su Xiaomo

Surprisingly, we have been together for half a year. Now, I am so afraid of losing this man; then, I have never been afraid of losing anyone. He, in my eyes, no one can match.

But, he got engaged. We have no future together. He said he would take me away, give me happiness. A helpless bitter smile crept onto my lips. But, how to leave?! He is part of the mafia, the gang has great power nationwide, his fiancée is the daughter of the gang's boss. If we escape, my parents will become the most innocent victims. I don't want to see such a scene.

Why, when we truly understand love, do we have to choose opposite paths...

The storm finally arrived, eerily calm. This silence speaks louder than words.

Lin Ya came to see me. A familiar feeling, I saw flames of anger in her eyes.

She said, "Su Xiaomo, I am Lin Ya, Xuan's future wife. Leave Xuan. The gang will not let him go. If he betrays the gang, Xuan might lose his life, and even your family will be dragged into this dispute. You're a smart woman, you understand..."

I feel like a stranded shell, letting the sea breeze blow freely, without a trace of life.

The rest of her words, I didn't hear. I only know, this is the end of the world. If we insist on moving forward, all we'll face is harm, endless harm...

Everything starts and ends. Maybe, it's time to leave. Without grand vows, without eternal promises. This simple romance, yet we lost ourselves. But I know we are both lonely children, needing each other's embrace to keep warm.

But, dear, what should I do!? Do I really have to watch our love perish in its infancy?

Yuwen Xuan

Su Xiaomo has started again, lingering between bars and men, I am overwhelmed with anger, I have an impulse to strangle this woman, but how can I bear to hurt her!? But how can I have the right to demand anything from her, what happiness can I give her! ? I can only helplessly counterattack everything.

Under gratitude, power, and suppression, I surrendered, like a defeated puppet, with a desperate posture.

I vaguely remember that heavy night, resembling the sadness before dawn. That night, there were not too many words. I knew we had a lot to say, but we were silent. In the end, only two heated bodies were crazily entwined together...

Before dawn, Xiaomei said she was going to Australia to further her studies...

"Xuan, let's end it here. Hate me if you want, blame me if you want, I can only do this much..."

Her cold eyes lost their former vitality, a sense of strangeness seeped through my spine. Is this woman in front of me the Su Xiaomo I know?!

I watched her get dressed, her delicate and curvaceous figure showing charm in the meticulously tailored clothes, but also carrying a hint of melancholy. When she left, I saw her trembling shoulders faintly. I wanted to call her back, but my throat choked up and I couldn't utter a word. Tears fell into the corner of my mouth, with a slight salty taste of blood...

Su Xiaomo, I know you must be deceiving me! Right?! But what does it matter...

Lin Ya

Su Xiaomo left, her mother and father were safe. I just threatened her a little. She obediently complied and really left, laughable.

Ever since Su Xiaomo left, Xuan has been unkempt, drinking heavily, and glaring at me with despair.

Six months later, I fell ill, sometimes going crazy and doing outrageous things, sometimes abnormally calm... It was a terrible disease, doctors couldn't diagnose it... But I knew my days were numbered... Xuan stayed by my side every day, with a restrained pain on his face.

I know, Su Xiaomo is the diamond in his heart that others cannot replace, precious and shining with dazzling light...

I also know, Su Xiaomo was deeply moved this time.

I think, if Su Xiaomo were here, it would be good...

I called Su Xiaomo...

Su Xiaomo

It was me who harmed Lin Ya. Six months ago, I asked a friend to send her a beautiful lipstick, which contained toad venom. The poison would seep into the body through the skin, and long-term use would be fatal, but no disease could be detected domestically...

Having received little love since childhood, when I encountered true love, how could I easily give up. Lin Ya, it was you who forced me to do this. No one has ever forced me to do anything, and from now on, no one can force me. Maybe, my heart started to twist from a very young age...

This war ultimately ended in my victory.

Epilogue

Lin Ya passed away quietly, with hopeful nostalgia stepping onto the "Naihe Bridge", not knowing whether she would drink the "Mengpo Soup"?

Six months later, I was still writhing in the dance pool like a water snake, waiting for Yuwen Xuan to take me away from here...

I knew he would definitely come. I succeeded. The moment I "happened" to meet Xuan, I still looked at him lazily, knowing that this was a fatal temptation to him. I understand him, so I played this move. After a brief pause of mutual gaze, he desperately embraced me, greedily kissing my beating heart, and I knew we were in love...

But, why do I no longer want to see the sun?!
