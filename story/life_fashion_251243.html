Five Concepts Leading Home Fashion

by longersing on 2009-10-23 13:38:26

The Five Concepts Leading Home Fashion: Less is more, hidden is exposed, abundance is frugality, new is beautiful, and emptiness is reality. Below is a detailed overview of these five concepts.

Less is More

The core of this concept is simplicity, but it does not reject decoration; rather, it achieves the effect of highlighting the connotation of the interior with fewer decorations. Everyone knows that luxurious decoration, the use of various moldings and exquisite materials, and complex decorative details have a strong stimulating effect on people's visual senses. For those who are usually relaxed, this is an artistic enjoyment, but for urbanites who live under stress, they pay more attention to the experience of living quality. Let everything tend towards peace, simplicity, and simplicity, expressing deeper meanings of life through the simplest home furnishings. This is what "more" means.

Hidden is Exposed

Concealing storage space is an important part of home decoration, but it may be possible in the future to break this traditional concealment. For example, cabinet doors of built-in wardrobes and large closets will adopt transparent glass structures, adding a cultural atmosphere to the home. Displaying exquisite collections within can give people the feeling of being in a museum.

Abundance is Frugality

In the choice of furniture, young people think that "multi-function, portable, and inexpensive" is good, such as sofa beds and folding chairs. However, for those who have achieved success or plan to settle down long-term, they prefer high-quality furniture that has taste and can appreciate in value. Therefore, fine furniture and top-grade furniture will be favored by some people.

New is Beautiful

The wide application and rapid development of new materials make home decoration wonderful. For example, hollow glass will bring home energy saving, sound insulation, and lighting to a new level; plastic-steel materials make home partitions flexible; and organic transparent glass-made roller shutters used between the kitchen or living room allow ventilation and convenient communication between people on both sides.

Emptiness is Reality

With the gradual expansion of residential areas, originally small-space housing will gradually be renovated and re-planned. In residential construction, more advanced designs with one unit and one large space will be increasingly adopted. Although it may look empty, it creates good conditions for rich and varied housing types.
