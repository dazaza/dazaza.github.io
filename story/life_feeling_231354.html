QQ Zone emotional diary: It's like having a wound.

by lwf225 on 2009-08-31 18:19:44

Today, she returns once again.

Because her heart aches once more, she has nowhere to hide. She searches and searches, but perhaps only here can she find some rest.

Perhaps it's due to a sorrowful story, or an old photograph, or even a certain person. Or perhaps,

Anyway, it makes her heart hurt.

Indeed, she remains as despondent as ever.

She begins to mock her own naivety, to mock her own actions.

She wants to hide away.

She has never learned how to express her innermost thoughts.

She just wants to simply hide here, then speak to herself in words that only she can understand.

But she hopes to be found, then taken home and cherished properly.

One

It was reported that if there are no clouds or rain on the 22nd, my hometown will be an excellent place to observe the total solar eclipse. I pray that this week-long high temperature of around 38 degrees Celsius continues until that day, followed by a heavy downpour.

My life has been somewhat inverted recently. I sleep in the evening and wake up at night. I watch the newly filmed TV series "Sword and Fairy 3" and play its game. Regardless of whether it's the game or the TV series, Snowgrass belongs to the Genus景Tian, and this is fate. In fact, the screenwriter has done a good job; he has made so many love stories profound under his pen. However, for most Sword and Fairy fans, the differences between the TV series and the game are still quite significant.

Two

Some people are very self-deprecating, while others live happily. I want to cut off all contact, without the internet, without signals, without phone calls. A certain female friend heard about how I am doing and asked if I'm alright. I said, don't worry. What is there really to worry about? I won't drink poison, I won't hang myself, and I won't jump off a building. Suicide, for someone like me, is like trying to match a bull's head with a horse's mouth - they don't fit together, and I wouldn't consider it. Occasionally, I get tangled up in my own thoughts, but that's not being overly self-deprecating, right? I still know how to make myself happy.

Inevitably, I recall many past scenes, during the night, during quiet moments, when I am alone. Those etched memories seem not to have faded yet. Actually, I don't want to remember them time and again; I hope they quickly become the past, merely symbols to keep the brain active and flexible when I grow old. Ultimately, I feel that no matter how many years pass, they cannot become as light as a feather; they remain significant. Only at the moment of entering the grave will yesterday truly become obsolete.

The luster of the wedding ring remains bright and clear. The pattern was chosen by myself. When I saw the silver water flow into the ring mold, that feeling of going all out formed a happy circle. We must understand that often, it's not that one cannot live without the other. We must bravely give ourselves a protective circle, making others happy and ourselves happy.

Three

Memory is like a glass of water. Full, it overflows.

The questions I ponder are roughly these: If I start to forget, will I forget the old first, or the recent? I have never figured out the essence of this. Besides, I have never been good at remembering things, whether old or recent. Those who move away from me, I will surely forget completely. Perhaps this is a bit excessive. However, the one thing I am very clear about is that I need love.

It's been a long time since I last read a book. It's been a long time since I last wrote anything. The only object I've had much contact with is the computer. My typing speed has become increasingly fast, but my mind has become increasingly slow, and my memory has deteriorated further. The only solution I can think of is to use my brain constantly. In reality, I am very lazy. Except for work where I come up with ideas, write some interesting stories, and visit some places, I don't have much more time to use my brain.

I started clearing out some people I don't recognize on QQ. Some people I seem to have never met, even though I've noted their names. Yes, my habit is to note names when adding QQ friends because I fear that if I turn around, I won't remember you anymore. I also fear that if you move away from me, you won't even say goodbye. Then we drift further and further apart, losing track of each other.

Similarly, I have been silent for a long time, but in the past few days, I have begun to actively talk to familiar people. I think this is just a habit of mine, driven by memory. One day, if you suddenly find that I've become silent again, it might be because I'm forgetting, and what I forget could be you, or it could just be me.

After all,

Memory is like a glass of water, full, it overflows.

Four

When will I truly mature? Looking at a familiar signature, I see that I have been in the long process of moving from immaturity to maturity. Perhaps a person never fully matures throughout their entire life, always in this changing process. Occasionally, I think, isn't this good? At least we are always searching, always changing ourselves repeatedly. And between childishness and maturity, a person won't be too tired, maybe this is the most harmonious state. Tender like peach red, ripe like autumn fruit.

Forget all my confidence, narcissism, and arrogance; they are nothing more than a floating debris in the river of time. I've said it before, the lack of security deep within me is hard to soothe. Although I once thought I had it, I actually never found it. Or perhaps I'll never find it. Who has so much extra security to give me? Everyone is striving to protect themselves, even those men and women who have made promises. Their lifelines will not be let go, and I can only save myself. Perhaps sinking into time is best.

A person who is humble to the bone can finally live among the dust. This is my soliloquy.

In the end, I still mention that certain someone, thank you for your kindness towards me, your unwavering care and affection.

Please remember the words you gave me.

It merely represents that I want to bring you happiness, to thaw your frozen river,

To be a moth flying into the fire for you.
