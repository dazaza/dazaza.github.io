How many roads should be built after all?

Looking forward to the transformation of unreasonable traffic signs

The violations caused by the unreasonable design of traffic signs and road markings in the section should not be paid for by the driver. We sincerely expect that the government department will get rid of all unreasonable road signs, and don't let everyone "drive by feeling the stones".

There is a true story that can be listened to as a joke. A long time ago, at the intersection of Shuiyin Road and Shuiyin Horizontal Road, there was a big traffic jam during the rush hours every day. It made the drivers very painful. However, during the period when the traffic lights were broken but there was no traffic jam every day and the cars went smoothly. Two weeks later, the traffic lights were repaired, but they were changed to go straight or turn according to the light signal direction, resulting in more serious congestion than before, Huazhou 97 gasoline price. After more than a month, the release order of the traffic lights has been changed again, so basically there is no traffic jam there now. But the innocent car owners' psychology has been "severely damaged", and they have become the white mice of the traffic management department for several months.

Recently, the government announced that it would spend about 25 million yuan to upgrade more than 3,000 road signs in six districts of the main urban area, including Liwan, Yuexiu, Huangpu, Tianhe, Haizhu, Baiyun, the inner ring road and the ring expressway area. These road signs will be upgraded in three situations: dismantling, updating old signs, and building new ones. The average cost of transforming each road sign is nearly 8,000 yuan, which will completely solve the problems of Guangzhou traffic road signs criticized by the public, such as unclear road signs, errors, omissions, too small fonts, and blocked by big trees. This is another great good thing for the government to be diligent and love the people and improve Guangzhou's traffic.

However, suddenly I remembered that these expensive road signs must be placed in appropriate places and express the correct meaning, especially before the traffic instruction road signs are hung, it is best to conduct simulation tests, so as not to cause traffic jams and rear-end collisions due to drivers looking at road signs, and not to make everyone be fined two hundred yuan because they did not see the road signs. Whether it is the installation of traffic lights or road signs, it is responsible and wise behavior to conduct research and testing before installation instead of shooting from the hip. In the June 2007 series of reports on "Guangzhou Traffic Longmen Array" in our newspaper's automobile weekly, we reported on the very "humorous" road sign at the intersection of Dongfeng East Road and Nonglinxia Road that prohibits going straight, turning left, and turning right, causing many private cars to fall into the trap, and was rated by netizens as the easiest place to be fined in Guangzhou. After the report was published, the road sign at that location has been improved. Now there are fewer vehicles being fined at that position, and the traffic police don't like to squat there anymore. Then after this huge investment in road sign renovation, can the unreasonable phenomena or loopholes in the road signs on the main trunk roads like Dongfeng Road be eliminated?

An 8,000-yuan road sign is really expensive, because in comparison, two years ago, there was an incident in Guangzhou where 60 newly installed road signs were stolen, and those road signs only cost 3,200 yuan. In fact, for ordinary people, as long as the road signs are clear and the meaning is expressed clearly, it is fine. No matter how expensive the road signs are, if they are placed in inappropriate places, it goes against the purpose of improving traffic and guiding pedestrians and drivers. Therefore, if so much money is spent to upgrade Guangzhou's road signs, it should be more worthwhile to first spend money on simulating the road signs and studying the road conditions, figuring out exactly which road signs in Guangzhou are unreasonable and need to be modified, which need to be removed, and where they need to be added. Fonts, materials, and colors are secondary issues.

For this, we look forward to it with great anticipation.

Taojin Road

Unreasonable location of identification plates

Shuqian Road

Mr. Wang was very upset. At around 5 p.m., he ate beef jerky on Shuqian Road in Guangzhou. He hadn't driven through Shuqian Road for a long time, and he didn't know until he was fined that Shuqian Road was a time-limited one-way street from 8 a.m. to 9 p.m., north to south only. Vehicles cannot exit to Zhongshan Road from Shuqian Road. As a result, when he reached the intersection and saw the traffic police, he was fined 200 yuan and lost two points. The traffic police told him to pay attention to the road signs next time, but the sign was located at the intersection where vehicles exit Shuqian Road to Zhongshan Road. If the driver doesn't know about this "story," they wouldn't see it until they were 15 meters away from the intersection! If the sign was moved forward a bit or repeated two or three times on the same road to remind the drivers, it would be hard for them to miss it.

Lack of clear identification signs

Huangpu Avenue Tunnel

In many places in Guangzhou, besides changing the road signs, sometimes the ground traffic signs should also be changed together. For example, when driving from east to west through the tunnel on Huangpu Avenue, vehicles find out after exiting the tunnel that they cannot turn to the right lane from the left lane. In other words, if you do not choose the middle or right lane when entering the tunnel, then according to the ground signs after exiting the tunnel, you can only enter the Inner Ring Road, as solid lines are painted for right lane changes, and there are cameras monitoring after exiting the tunnel. However, many vehicles entering the tunnel want to go up to the Zhongshan Yi overpass or Dongfeng Road after passing through Huangpu Avenue. In such cases, there should be a huge road sign indicating the relationship between lane selection and driving direction before vehicles enter the tunnel, to facilitate drivers.
