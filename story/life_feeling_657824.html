Thinking of you faintly, and tears fall gently.

by lili1 on 2012-01-28 22:01:21

Gently missing you, gently shedding tears

As the night falls deeper, the continuous autumn rain drifts down. The rustling sound of the rain awakens the longing within my heart. Though you are far away, my thoughts draw you closer. It's as if I can see your figure, the mist in your eyes, and the yearning in your heart. The autumn wind and rain create a hazy atmosphere that brings us together. Once again, the autumn wind rises, and though you step out of my sight, you remain deeply rooted in my heart. Even without news from you, I no longer feel anxious because I clearly understand our mutual feelings. Words aren't necessary; the bond between us remains unbroken. My joy is your joy. I strive to make life more vibrant just for your expectations.

In that fleeting moment when I turn around, tears well up in my eyes. I leave behind only a faint smile of happiness for you. No one understands the bitterness in my heart. "Pain but also happiness," my loosened hand lingers in the air, unsure where to rest. A long breath escapes me, floating into the air and dissipating with the breeze. Still, I am glad that I've finally made the decision.

Yet, forgetting is never easy. Missing you has become a kind of poetic sentiment in my life, often immersing myself in thoughts of you. In the lonely nights, I listen to the whispers of my heart. The sweetness and sorrow of past memories have become like a dream. Having experienced fervent emotions and the ups and downs of relationships, I've learned to wrap those sweet sorrows and layers of longing tightly in my heart, hiding them deep in my memories. They slowly release during the autumn winds, rains, falling leaves, dusks, and nights. Missing someone is also a form of happiness. Those sweet memories occasionally surface in my mind, bringing a gentle smile to my lips. Never before have I been so calm, so deeply missing someone. Perhaps this connection will last forever at this very moment. "The bright moon shines over the sea, and we share the same moment despite being apart." The brilliance of fireworks is an instant eternity. Let me gently miss you like this, in every drowsy morning or tranquil evening.

Tonight, listening to the beautiful song "Gently Missing You, Gently Shedding Tears," I couldn't help but think of you again:

I don't know which way the wind was blowing that day. I only remember my smile wilting in the wind. I don't know if the clouds were broken-hearted for us, but all I saw was you gradually walking away, waving your hand. Today, I am as charming as the me in your memories, yet I've grown accustomed to drinking coffee alone at night. When the longing on the horizon glimmers like tears, will you come into my dreams to comfort my sadness? How many dreams must be fulfilled on this long journey to be without regret? Why can't truly loving people stay together forever? You, wandering far away, could never understand this feeling. Perhaps you've already forgotten whose back you turned to. The passage of time has taken away the initial romance, and in the season of blooming flowers, I no longer see the roses you once gave me. Heartbreaking memories fly back and forth in the sunset. Leaning against the dusk, I gently think of you, gently shedding tears.
