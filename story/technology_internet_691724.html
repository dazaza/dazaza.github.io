72 Fashion House: Worker dies after blender suddenly starts during repair process

by wgmf3053 on 2012-03-05 17:27:05

Suspicious Misoperation or Murder? According to the workers, at the Seven Changes Fashion House (http://www.72bssw.com), in some cases, mechanical workers would bring walkie-talkies into the mixer to communicate with the workers outside controlling the switch, ensuring safety. "Perhaps this time they didn't bring the walkie-talkie, or maybe the person in the control room pressed the wrong button," one worker said. However, another worker suspected it was a murder case, "How could they be working in the rain early in the morning?"

Yesterday, the Haizhu District police reported that after receiving the report, the police quickly organized criminal investigation, public security, and police station departments to conduct an on-site investigation. At the same time, the street office, safety supervision, and other departments also sent personnel to the scene. Currently, the public security department and relevant functional departments have formed a joint investigation team to carry out the investigation. As for whether the incident is an accident or a criminal case, the police stated that they are still collecting evidence and it is temporarily difficult to determine.

The workers immediately stopped the machine and saw Afa through the entrance. His limbs were all jammed, his body covered in blood and flesh, and despite everyone's shouting, he did not move. The workers urgently called the police and helplessly waited for the rescue to arrive.

According to Captain Yu who participated in the rescue, two firefighters drilled into the mixer and found Afa lying between the huge mixing blades. His hands and feet were jammed, his chest clothing was missing, and there was a patch of bloody flesh. After nearly an hour of effort, the rescuers managed to drag Afa out. Seeing Afa's body, the workers were all shocked.

Soon, the companion climbed out first. Suddenly, the mixer roared to life. Within less than a minute, a scream of pain was heard, "Someone is still inside!"

When the reporter tried to find the responsible person of Guangzhou Jian'an Concrete Co., Ltd. to understand the matter, the security guard replied after being informed, "All the people in the company have already gone home."

Afa was introduced as a 40-year-old man from Jiangxi Province. Afterwards, the police took the relevant parties away for investigation.

In the afternoon, the New Express reporter went to the concrete company where the accident happened. The security guard revealed that at the time, two mechanical workers entered the mixer for repair. One came out, but the other worker did not. "It might be that the people in the control room thought everyone had come out and wanted to start the machine for a try, but by the time they realized, it was too late."

At around 2:30 AM, in the area near the Huangpu Village Archway on Xingang East Road in Haizhu District, there were few people and vehicles, only the sound of wind and rain remained. Not far away, at Guangzhou Jian'an Concrete Co., Ltd., several large mixers several meters high were still lit up. Mechanical worker Afa and a colleague bent over and entered the entrance which only allowed one person to enter and exit for repair. In the past few days, the cement mixer had not been started.

Around 3:00 AM, fire officers rushed to the scene, along with ambulances and police. The entrance of the mixer was very small, and the space inside was also very limited. Medical personnel entered first to provide treatment, but after diagnosis, Afa was found to have no signs of life.

Before the incident, both workers entered the mixer for repairs together. One came out, then the machine was started, raising suspicions about the accident.

At 3:00 AM, under heavy rain, 40-year-old mechanical worker Afa (alias) was found trapped in the cement mixer. When the rescue personnel arrived, he had already died, with horrific wounds all over his body. Why was he repairing it in the middle of the night? How did the mixer suddenly start? Was it an accident or a murder? Currently, the police and others have formed a joint investigation team to handle the matter.
