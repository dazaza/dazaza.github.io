However, upon closer inspection, not only is Nanning green, but its waters are also emerald green. Therefore, Nanning has also launched the slogan to build the "Water City of China!" The foundation supporting Nanning's "Green City" and "Water City" construction is this city's pulse - Yongjiang River.

Yongjiang River is the cultural birthplace of Nanning. In recent years, Nanning's urban construction achievements have been significant, with skyscrapers lining the streets, and the real estate industry developing rapidly. Especially the housing developments located on both sides of Yongjiang River have gained momentum. In a sense, the ability for Nanning's architectural clusters to rise from the ground is primarily due to the abundant water resources of Yongjiang River.

Among the many housing developments along the banks of Yongjiang River, there is one building that stands out – Pan Yu · Yongjiang Yipin, located next to Chaoyang Square, which has been sold entirely as completed properties. Pan Yu · Yongjiang Yipin will be delivering the keys at the end of this month, so let us take a look at this "next-generation" housing development that combines prosperity and scenic views.

### 1. Transportation and Location

As the name suggests, Pan Yu · Yongjiang Yipin is located by the Yongjiang River, at No. 2 Shui Street, surrounded by Renmin Road, Jiefang Road, Jiangbei Avenue, and Shui Street. This is one of the most superior and rare locations in Nanning, less than 500 meters from the city center Chaoyang Square and Department Store, and less than 100 meters from Minsheng Square on Yongjiang River. Buses such as routes 17, 18, 202, 3, 57, 58, 78, 203, 45, 202, 210, 81, 213, 606 all stop at either the Renmin Jiefang Intersection or Minsheng Square station, making it easily accessible. Facing Yongjiang River and backed by Chaoyang Square, it is a rare housing development that combines prosperity and scenery in Nanning.

### 2. Project Introduction and Sales Status

The total land area of the Pan Yu · Yongjiang Yipin project is 16 mu (approximately 1.07 hectares), with a green space ratio of 12.5% and a plot ratio of 11.6. It consists of six tower buildings combined together. At first glance, the green space ratio seems low and the plot ratio high, but we know that Yongjiang Yipin is located in a scarce central location, which determines that its land area cannot be very large. Since the project needs to build skyscrapers up to 32 stories, the plot ratio inevitably becomes quite high. However, being located by Yongjiang River and just a hundred meters from Minsheng Square, the entire Minsheng Square has become the best leisure backyard for Yongjiang Yipin, enjoying both prosperity and scenery – isn't this exactly the ideal residence for modern people?

Currently, buildings 3 to 6 of Pan Yu · Yongjiang Yipin are for sale, mainly consisting of high-rise products with unit sizes ranging from 23 to 126 square meters, including single rooms to three-bedroom units. The starting price is 6,500 yuan per square meter, with an average price of about 7,500 yuan per square meter. A one-time payment offers a 99*99 discount, while installment payments enjoy a 99% discount. With a minimum down payment of 45,000 yuan, the project is entirely sold as completed properties, with delivery scheduled for the end of this month.
