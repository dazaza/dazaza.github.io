Top 10 internet celebrities in 2009

by anonymous on 2011-06-07 13:51:25

1. Hawker Beauty

Hawker Beauty, real name Kang Xiaohan, 19 years old, from Anyang, Henan Province. She became known as the "Hawker Beauty" after selling candied haws near a university campus while carrying a fashionable leather bag and wearing large silver earrings. Her youthful and beautiful appearance attracted many male students.

2. Long Legs Sister

Long Legs Sister, real name Kong Yansong, currently a third-year student majoring in sports news at Beijing Sport University. She gained internet fame for her 1.17-meter-long "beautiful legs," which were praised by netizens who commented, "These legs are outrageously long."

3. Yunnan Nude Girl

Yunnan Nude Girl, real name Peng Chunping, 21 years old, from Chuxiong Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province. She is fond of wearing white wide-collared dresses or T-shirts with thigh-high shorts. In 2008, to find her missing mother who was working away from home and to appeal what she considered an injustice, she posted nude photos online. Recently, she garnered attention again by challenging a 1.24-meter-tall singer and host named Bei Bei from a VIP bar in Hefei for a marriage proposal.

4. Shanghai Ticketing Sister

Shanghai Ticketing Sister, also known as the "Post-90s Beautiful Ticket Seller," real name Gu Jiawen, works as a ticket seller on Shanghai's No. 934 bus route. With her shoulder-length dyed hair, purple contact lenses, and colorful nails, she is one of 27 young ticket sellers specially trained by the Shanghai Bus Tram Company for the World Expo special bus routes, showcasing the vibrant image of Shanghai.

5. Clone Zhang Baiqi

Clone Zhang Baiqi, real name Gong Mi, currently studying at Beijing Film Academy's acting department. Naturally beautiful and multi-talented, she participated in Hunan TV's Super Girl competition in Zhengzhou in June 2009, drawing much attention due to her resemblance to Hong Kong actress Zhang Baiqi. She reportedly withdrew from the competition due to illness.

6. Peony Fairy

Peony Fairy, from Heze, Shandong Province, posted seductive photos on Huasheng Blog, claiming she would "sacrifice herself" for money and fame and accept "unspoken rules." Her mature and enchanting photos along with her outspoken nature caused a stir on the internet.

7. Xidan Girl

Xidan Girl, real name Ren Yuéli, born in a remote village in Zhuozhou, Hebei Province, once worked in a small restaurant in Beijing before becoming a street performer. A netizen named "Feiwofeifei" recorded her singing half of the song "Angel's Wings" in a subway passage in Xidan and uploaded the video to Sina Blog, where it quickly spread online.

8. Second Generation Lotus

Second Generation Lotus, real name Xu Qingqing, 23 years old, from Dongtou, Wenzhou. Currently studying at Zhejiang University of Science and Technology, she is characterized by spicy photos, striking poses, exaggerated expressions, and a bold personality. She calls herself "Second Generation Lotus" and claims, "I want to show my best side to everyone," earning her the nickname "Fried Rice Version of Lotus Sister."

9. Lin Family Little Sister

Lin Family Little Sister, real name Lin Yi, is multi-talented, excelling in storytelling, singing, and dancing. She has posted many entertaining videos online, such as "Looking for a Partner" and "Encountering Ghosts." Netizens commented, "She is like a butterfly quietly flying past your window at midnight, bringing a sense of mystery and freshness that can make you revisit the tenderness of a neighbor's little sister."

10. Peking University Academic Cat

Including a cat in this article is purely for entertainment purposes, as in today's era of mass amusement, this cat has been anthropomorphized. A netizen named "PKU Old Rascal" posted a thread titled "A Respected Cat at Peking University" on the PKU BBS, describing an elderly cat attending classes alongside students. The story was quickly picked up by various websites and humorously referred to as the "Academic Cat."
