"Shennong No.9", Tianhui Duofu Lobster Price; Freshwater Lobster Farming - After years of research by the Central China International Aquaculture Scientific Research Industry Incubation Center and Hubei Tianhui Duofu Agricultural Science and Technology Development Co., Ltd., Tianhui Duofu has developed a new generation of high-quality freshwater lobster seedlings based on Australian freshwater lobster farming techniques. Through selective breeding, hybridization, purification, strengthening, and cultivation, they have produced an excellent variety that does not degenerate over generations. With this innovation, farmers can stock their ponds once and benefit for many years.

The emergence of "Shennong No.9" fills the domestic gap in artificial breeding of freshwater lobsters, as recognized by the Wuhan Fisheries Science Research Institute. It marks a significant change from relying solely on wild-caught sources to professional artificial farming. Compared with traditional wild freshwater lobster farming, "Shennong No.9" offers several advantages:

- Strong survival capabilities

- Broad adaptability

- Simple farming methods

- Omnivorous diet that is easy to manage

- One person can farm 30-50 acres or more

This breed is highly recommended by the Hubei Special Breeding Industry Association and the Hubei Freshwater Lobster Seedling Breeding Professional Committee. The economic benefits and export potential are very promising.

On December 11-12, the Hubei Provincial Government convened a provincial meeting on the development of the freshwater lobster industry. Deputy Governor Zhao Bin of Hubei Province, along with leaders from relevant departments such as the Provincial Development and Reform Commission, Provincial Finance Department, Provincial Agriculture Department, and Provincial Fishery Bureau attended the meeting, which was chaired by Secretary-General Mei Zuen of the Provincial People's Government.

The conference summarized the achievements and experiences in developing characteristic industries like freshwater lobsters in recent years, and mobilized all levels and relevant departments to take effective measures to promote the rapid and good development of distinctive advantageous industries such as freshwater lobsters. Typical exchange speeches were made at the meeting by the Jingzhou City Fishery Bureau, Xishui County People's Government, Qianjiang Huashan Company, etc. Vice Director Wang Xiangyang of the Provincial Development and Reform Commission introduced the drafting situation and main content of the "Hubei Province Freshwater Lobster Industry Development Plan (2010-2015)". Director Zhu Jinshui of the Provincial Agriculture Department summarized and deployed the current development status, existing problems, and next steps for the freshwater lobster industry.
