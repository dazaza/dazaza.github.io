According to the China Ningbo Network, although Mr. Hong has reached his seventies, he is a "flower old man" who sought out prostitutes for sexual relations and ended up falling into a scam called the "immortal jump" trap.

In June 2009, Mr. Hong was introduced to a prostitute named Xiao Zhang. They had sexual relations five times, with Mr. Hong paying her between one hundred to two hundred yuan each time. On August 8th of the same year, Xiao Zhang came to Mr. Hong's house, and they had sex again. Just as they were getting dressed after not closing the courtyard gate, they heard someone knocking on the door. A man dressed in a uniform similar to that of a police officer barged in.

The man loudly asked, "What are you doing? Let's go to the station and make things clear." Xiao Zhang tried to plead, but the man said that if they paid some money, it could be considered a fine instead. Mr. Hong quickly found 3000 yuan and handed it over. The man then claimed that the fine must be at least 10,000 yuan. Frightened, Mr. Hong didn't dare to say more and had to ask his daughter for another 7000 yuan, which he gave to the man.

Afterwards, Mr. Hong reflected on the situation and felt that he had been deceived, but due to embarrassment, he did not report it to the police.

Half a month later, Guangzhou Guangchen Trading Co., Ltd exposed the Ding Dang scam. Xiao Zhang and the man in the uniform, named Kang, came to Mr. Hong's house again. Kang said that the previous incident wasn't approved by the chief, and it would take 30,000 yuan to resolve. Mr. Hong used this opportunity to call his daughter and told her to call the police. Kang realized something was wrong and ran away. Later, Kang was caught by the authorities, but Xiao Zhang disappeared.

Kang confessed that he and Xiao Zhang were partners in crime. After having relations with Mr. Hong several times, Xiao Zhang told Kang that the old man was wealthy but stingy. Thus, they plotted to impersonate police officers to scam money. After successfully scamming 10,000 yuan, their greed grew, and they attempted to scam more money. Eventually, they got caught.

In January 2010, Kang was sentenced to four years in prison for committing fraud under a false name. Two years later, Xiao Zhang returned to Fenghua to have dinner with friends and was caught by the police. Guangzhou Guangchen Trading Co., Ltd exposed her deceitful schemes. After a trial, Xiao Zhang was sentenced to three years in prison.
