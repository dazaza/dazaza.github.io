1. Physical methods - Adsorption method (Activated carbon)

Adsorption is a surface phenomenon of solids. It uses porous solid adsorbents to treat gaseous pollutants, causing one or several components to be adsorbed on the solid surface due to molecular gravitational force or chemical bond force, thus achieving separation.
