Fao Electronics Technology Co., Ltd. lists the top ten optical fiber and cable manufacturers in China for you, highly recommended!!!!

by fuaokeji on 2011-08-20 10:40:19

The top ten fiber optic cable manufacturers in China are as follows (not ranked by order):

1. Tefan Information: Optical cables, optical fibers and preforms, communication equipment

Tefan Information is one of the earliest manufacturers to enter the optical communication field nationwide. The brands "Tefan Optical Cable" and "Tefan JiGuang" enjoy a good reputation in the industry. Its scale ranks among the top ten nationwide with a high market share.

2. Huiyuan Communication: Optical fibers, optical cables and related products

The company's revenue from optical fibers, optical cables and related products mainly comes from sales of various products such as ordinary optical cables, power optical cables, plastic optical fibers, etc., as well as providing customers with installation, maintenance, and technical consulting services for related products. The company is one of the top ten optical cable production bases in the country, exclusively possessing two national-level patents for central tube bundle-type irregular armored optical cables and ribbon optical cables that are at an advanced domestic level. The company entered the optical components industry by acquiring 12.75 million shares of Guangheng Communication to achieve an integrated optical communication strategy. The successful development of low-water-peak optical fibers significantly improved the company's technical level, demonstrating its core competitiveness.

3. Xinmao Technology: Optical fibers

In the first half of 2009, based on the original optical communication center business unit, Xinmao Technology formed a joint venture with Changfei Fiber Optic-Holding Co., Ltd. to establish Tianjin Changfei Xinmao Optical Communication Co., Ltd., further strengthening the brand and market share of the company's optical fiber products, consolidating the position of the Changfei Xinmao joint venture in the optical fiber industry. Additionally, to gradually form and improve a complete industrial chain for optical communication and enhance the competitiveness of the optical communication industry, the company invested in establishing an optical cable factory in the same year, achieving full production at the beginning of 2010.

4. Farsoon: Optical fibers, preforms, optical fibers, optical cables, and optical sensing components

In recent years, the company has actively ventured into the new materials industry, producing optical fibers and building an optical communication industry chain that includes optical preforms, telecommunications optical fibers, specialty optical fibers, optical cables, and optical devices. The main business involves the manufacture and sale of steel wires, steel ropes and their products, general machinery, wires and cables, instruments and meters, electronic products, optical preforms, optical fibers, optical cables, optical sensing components, and electromechanical products (excluding pressure vessels).

5. Yongding Shares: Communication cables, optical communication cables, and other communication equipment

Since 2008, Yongding Shares has made breakthrough progress in exporting submarine cables and optical-electrical composite cables, maintaining a good momentum of development. Yongding Shares mainly produces optical cables and cables, leading in scale nationwide, benefiting from the revitalization plan for the electronics information industry. The company is one of the largest optical cable production bases in the country, with its optical and cable product sales leading in the domestic industry, while the rapid development of the communications industry and the network investment boom provide enormous growth space for the company.

6. Tongding Optoelectronics: Optical cables

Tongding Optoelectronics is an outstanding innovative enterprise in the optical cable industry under the Tongding Group, specializing in the research, development, production, sales, and engineering services of communication optical fibers, communication optical cables, communication cables, railway signal cables, RF coaxial cables, special optical cables, distributed optical fiber sensing systems, and other products. The company successfully listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange in October 2010.

7. Yangtze River Communication: Communication, optical fibers

It owns holding subsidiaries Jifei Fiber Optic Cable Co., Ltd. and Changfei Fiber Optic-Holding Co., Ltd., and Yangtze River Communication Industry Group Co., Ltd. holds 25% of the equity in Changfei Fiber Optic-Holding Co., Ltd. Industry insiders believe that, to some extent, Changfei's brilliance has overshadowed Yangtze River Communication. According to public data, Changfei is the only professional manufacturer in China that possesses integrated production capabilities for preform manufacturing, fiber drawing, and cable formation, with annual sales close to 3 billion yuan. Since it started production in 1992, the output and sales of Changfei's optical fibers and optical cables have ranked first in the country for sixteen consecutive years. The company's products are exported to more than 50 countries and regions overseas, ranking second globally in optical fiber production and fifth globally in optical cable production.

8. Hengtong Optoelectronics: Optical fibers, optical cables, power cables, special communication cables

The company is one of the largest and most comprehensive optical fiber and optical cable manufacturers in the country, with a complete optical fiber industry chain and complementary products. It ranks second in the market share for special optical cables.

9. FiberHome Telecommunication: Optical fibers, optical cables, communication, and related communication technologies

The company is a leading enterprise in the optical communication industry and the only domestic company that integrates the three strategic technologies of optical communication - optical communication equipment, optical fibers and optical cables, and network security. The company's competitive position in the optical transmission equipment market is relatively stable. The Chinese optical transmission equipment market size is approximately 10-12 billion yuan, mainly monopolized by Huawei, ZTE, and FiberHome, with FiberHome accounting for about 10% of the market share. Annual optical cable sales exceed 200,000 kilometers, ranking second in the industry.

10. Zhongtian Technology: Optical fibers, optical cables, cable materials, and accessories

The company is one of the earliest manufacturers to develop and produce optical cables domestically, ranking among the top in the domestic optical fiber and optical cable industry, especially in the market share of special optical cables, which has maintained the industry's first place for many years. With the rollout of 3G construction, all telecom operators are accelerating infrastructure construction, providing good opportunities for the development of the company's optical fibers, optical cables, and RF cables and other communication products. The company is one of the most comprehensive professional enterprises in the domestic optical cable industry, with a domestic market share of special optical cables as high as 30% to 40%, and will benefit from the construction of smart grids.
