Company manager sentenced to death for killing ex-husband, dismembering the body and dumping remains in four cities

by jeffbcl5644 on 2010-06-23 21:13:20

News recap: From January 19 to 20, 2006, packages containing parts of a male corpse were successively found in Liaoyuan City, Dongfeng County, and Liuhe County of Jilin Province. After identification by the missing person's family and forensic examination, it was confirmed that the body parts belonged to the same corpse. The victim was Wu Xin (a pseudonym), manager of an advertising company in Meihekou City. A few days later, the Meihekou police announced the resolution of the case. The murderer was Wu Xin's ex-wife Lan Ju (a pseudonym), who had continued living with him after their divorce. She was the owner of a local beauty salon. She carried out the entire criminal process from murder to dismemberment and finally disposal of the body across four counties and cities.

According to reports by our staff reporter Shi Zhou, since the incident occurred in January 2006, the trial of the sensational Meihekou major murder and dismemberment case has been fraught with twists and turns. It was learned yesterday that due to the seriousness of the case, after two retrials, Tonghua Intermediate People's Court recently sentenced Lan Ju again to death for intentional homicide, depriving her of political rights for life.

Body parts discovered successively in three counties and cities

Victim confirmed as the same person

On the morning of January 19, 2006, the Nankang Branch of the Liaoyuan Public Security Bureau found part of a male corpse within its jurisdiction. The next day, the Dongfeng County Public Security Bureau found a woven bag containing part of a male corpse near the residential building of the Dongfeng Trade Bureau within its jurisdiction; on the same day, the Liuhe County Public Security Bureau found part of a male corpse near the public toilet of the Ring City Hospital within its jurisdiction.Free Home Goods

At 14:00 on January 21, Meihekou resident Wu Qin (a pseudonym) reported that her brother Wu Xin had been missing for two days. After preliminary identification by Wu Qin and her brother Wu Lu, the body parts found in Liaoyuan, Dongfeng, and Liuhe might belong to the missing Wu Xin. Later, after judicial identification, it was confirmed that the body was indeed Wu Xin, the manager of an advertising company in Meihekou who had gone missing earlier.

Wu Qin and Wu Lu stated that after Wu Xin went missing, his ex-wife Lan Ju, who still lived together after their divorce, did not allow them to report the case. They suspected that Wu Xin's death was related to Lan Ju.

At 15:15 to 15:40 on the same day, the Criminal Investigation Brigade of the Meihekou Public Security Bureau came to the house where Lan Ju and Wu Xin lived together and extracted trace amounts of blood from the white cement between the bathroom floor tiles and the central floor tiles.

Around 16:00 on the same day, the Criminal Investigation Brigade of the Meihekou Public Security Bureau summoned Lan Ju to the brigade. Initially, during the first three interrogations, Lan Ju claimed that the victim Wu Xin had left home.Star Girl

Under the circumstances where the public security authorities had already obtained some important evidence and provided policy education, Lan Ju confessed to the crime of killing, dismembering, and disposing of the body during the subsequent four interrogations. She also identified the specific locations where she disposed of the body parts in Meihekou, Liaoyuan, Dongfeng, and Liuhe. Based on Lan Ju's identification, the public security authorities extracted important evidence such as the victim Wu Xin's ear and bloody paper at the scene of the crime in Meihekou.

On January 23, 2006, the case was resolved.

Multiple conflicts led to the motive for murder

Lan Ju and Wu Xin got married in the early 1980s. Not long after marriage, Lan Ju discovered that Wu Xin had an extramarital affair and didn't hide it much from her. This started her hatred towards Wu Xin, leading to frequent arguments between them.

Later, Lan Ju opened a beauty parlor, while Wu Xin established an advertising company.

On September 28, 1998, after constant quarrels, Lan Ju and Wu Xin registered for a divorce. However, considering issues like their child, they continued living together under the guise of being a married couple. On June 15, 2002, Wu Xin had a son with another woman, which intensified Lan Ju's hatred towards Wu Xin.

Subsequently, Wu Xin contracted a sexually transmitted disease (STD) and unknowingly passed it on to Lan Ju.

By mid-January 2006, Lan Ju, now 49 years old, had been divorced from Wu Xin for eight years but still lived together with him. Also cohabiting with them was their daughter. The three resided in a three-bedroom apartment in the urban area of Meihekou.

Due to the STD contracted from Wu Xin that remained untreated, Lan Ju's hatred for Wu Xin reached its peak, and she conceived the idea of murder.

To reduce resistance in killing Wu Xin, Lan Ju secretly went to a pharmacy in Meihekou, claiming her younger brother-in-law had mental problems, and purchased two bottles (100 tablets) of tranquilizer pills along with some empty capsules. Upon returning home, Lan Ju ground up 70 pills and placed them into seven empty capsules.

In the following days, Lan Ju lied, saying a doctor at a clinic had given her some capsules that cured her STD in just seven days. She hoped Wu Xin would take this medication to quickly cure his STD, to which Wu Xin agreed.

Using treatment for STD as a pretext to drug

Using a dog leash to kill

Around 22:00 on January 18, Wu Xin returned home. At that time, Lan Ju was watching TV in her room, and their daughter was playing computer games in her own room. After Wu Xin came back, Lan Ju entered his room, and the two began arguing over a trivial matter.

After arguing for a while, Lan Ju stopped. After thinking for a moment, she started coaxing Wu Xin, saying he shouldn’t argue with her, only she truly cared about him, and she had specially bought him effective medicine for treating STD. Subsequently, Lan Ju took out the seven capsules she had prepared and gave them to Wu Xin, deceiving him into taking them before returning to her own room.

At around 3:00 AM the next morning, Lan Ju entered Wu Xin’s room holding a dog leash used at home, turned on the light inside, and saw Wu Xin in a deep sleep. Immediately, Lan Ju rushed forward and strangled Wu Xin with the rope. After strangling for about five or six minutes, Lan Ju confirmed Wu Xin's death and then let go.

Subsequently, Lan Ju covered Wu Xin with a quilt and returned the rope to the living room. Then, Lan Ju went back to Wu Xin’s room, turned off the light, locked the door, and returned to her own room.

While deceiving her daughter

While dismembering and disposing of the body

The next morning, Lan Ju sent her daughter out under the pretense of buying a mobile phone. Then she re-entered Wu Xin's room, using a kitchen knife and a fruit knife to dismember Wu Xin's body, destroying his face to prevent future recognition of the body parts.

Subsequently, Lan Ju first transferred the body to a neighbor’s room for safekeeping, looking for opportunities. Without her daughter knowing, she packaged the dismembered body parts and took multiple taxis to dispose of them in garbage bins and public toilets in Longshan District of Liaoyuan City, Dongfeng Town of Dongfeng County, Liuhe Town of Liuhe County, and several places in Meihekou. During this time, when drivers asked what was in the package, she either said it was pork or fish.

After committing the crime, Lan Ju did not flee. She thought her actions were flawless, but she didn’t expect that her attempt to stop Wu Xin’s family from reporting the case, along with the residual blood stains in the bathroom, exposed her.

Three trials

Two death sentences

On November 30, 2006, Tonghua Intermediate People's Court made a judgment, determining that Lan Ju was guilty of intentional homicide, sentencing her to death, depriving her of political rights for life, and ordering her to compensate Wu Xin's family for economic losses amounting to more than 220,000 yuan. Lan Ju appealed against the verdict.

On August 16, 2007, the Higher People's Court of Jilin Province made a criminal ruling, remanding the case back to Tonghua Intermediate People's Court for retrial due to unclear facts.

On June 4, 2008, Tonghua Intermediate People's Court made a second judgment, determining that Lan Ju was guilty of intentional homicide, sentencing her to death with a two-year reprieve, depriving her of political rights for life, and ordering her to compensate Wu Xin's family for economic losses amounting to more than 220,000 yuan. Lan Ju appealed again, and Tonghua Procuratorate also lodged a protest.

On December 15, 2008, the Higher People's Court of Jilin Province revoked the second judgment of Tonghua Intermediate People's Court, remanding the case back for retrial again due to unclear facts. During the litigation period, the victim's family abandoned their civil compensation claims, demanding severe punishment for the defendant.

Recently, Tonghua Intermediate People's Court made the third trial of this case, sentencing Lan Ju to death again for intentional homicide.

Yesterday afternoon, the reporter attempted to contact Tonghua Intermediate People's Court to learn whether Lan Ju would appeal again, but failed to reach them.
