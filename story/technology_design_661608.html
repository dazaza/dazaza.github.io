Inventory in the machinery industry reaches 91.9 billion yuan due to over-expansion

by baoyunx3 on 2012-02-09 10:51:21

The machinery industry expands too fast with inventory reaching 91.9 billion yuan

On August 10, according to statistics derived by the reporter of the Securities Daily based on WIND data, as of June 30, 2011, among nearly 100 listed companies that have released semi-annual reports in the machinery, equipment, and instrument industries categorized by the CSRC, the total inventory amount reached 91.9 billion yuan in the first half of the year, with a total provision for inventory write-downs of 3.9 billion yuan, accounting for 4.26% of the inventory.

Among these nearly 100 companies, CSR Corporation (601766.SH), Gree Electric (000651.SZ), Xifei International (000768.SZ), Shenyang Machine Tool (000410.SZ), and China Shipbuilding (600150.SH) ranked top five in terms of inventory amounts. Compared with the same period last year, each company's inventory amount has significantly increased, and raw materials accounted for the largest proportion in the inventory classification.

Regarding the reasons for the increase in inventory in the machinery industry, Du Pu, an analyst of the machinery industry at Bohai Securities, told reporters that although there was some decline in the development of the machinery industry at the beginning of the year, overall it was still very good. The growth rate of raw materials also reflects the good industry conditions. However, the rapid expansion of machinery production capacity poses risks if demand declines or the market becomes saturated due to excessive inventory.

Gree Electric

Inventory increased by 92.9% in six months

In the first half of 2011, Gree Electric's inventory amounted to 12.4 billion yuan, an increase of 92.9% compared to the same period last year. The provision for inventory write-downs was 103.48 million yuan, with an average extraction ratio of about 1.97%, lower than the 3% extraction ratio of the same period last year.

For Gree Electric, the total inventory in 2010 also increased by 5.7 billion yuan compared to 2009, with an increase of 98.49%. The company stated in its annual report that the increase in inventory was mainly due to the increase in inventory reserves corresponding to the scale growth of the company. In order to cope with material price fluctuations, the company actively adjusted its production plan, raw material, and finished product inventory.

The company's inventory classification mainly includes five categories: raw materials, work-in-progress, semi-finished products, finished products, and low-value consumables. Among them, the inventory amount of raw materials in the first half of 2011 was 5.6 billion yuan, compared to 2.4 billion yuan in the same period last year, an increase of 135%.

The rapid increase in the growth rate of raw materials also reflected well in Gree Electric's excellent performance. In the first half of 2011, the company's operating profit and net profit attributable to the parent company's shareholders increased by 93.43% and 40.37% respectively compared to the same period last year. The main reason for the growth in performance was the significant increase in product sales revenue due to strong domestic and international market demand.

Relevant home appliance industry analysts pointed out that the active air conditioning market and the rapid popularization of central air conditioning in villas and residential homes provided a large market for Gree.

Compared with Gree Electric, which has a relatively short production cycle, CSR Corporation has a much longer operating cycle. According to Du Pu, an analyst of the machinery industry at Bohai Securities, this industry characteristic is one of the reasons why the company's inventory amount is high.

As of August 10, 2011, among nearly 100 companies in the machinery industry that have already released semi-annual reports, CSR Corporation's inventory amount ranked first, reaching 19.7 billion yuan, compared to 14.1 billion yuan in the same period last year, an increase of 40%. The provision for inventory write-downs was 600 million yuan, with an average extraction ratio of about 13.2%, lower than the 26.3% extraction ratio of the same period last year.

Such a high inventory ratio is considered normal by relevant industry analysts. "CSR Corporation has many very large projects that take a long time, so a high inventory ratio is inevitable." Relevant industry analysts also pointed out that "CSR Corporation is an exception in the machinery industry and cannot be used as a representative of the entire industry."

Significant Increase in Raw Material Inventory

Rapid Expansion of Production Capacity Poses Hidden Worries

In the view of Du Pu, an analyst of the machinery industry at Bohai Securities, the machinery industry has developed very well in recent years. The national 4 trillion yuan investment at the end of 2008 provided new development opportunities for the machinery industry, and good development continued from the summer of 2009 until the beginning of this year.

To respond to the impact of the global economic downturn, the central government quickly deployed a 4 trillion yuan investment plan to expand domestic demand in November 2008. At the same time, the engineering machinery industry was one of the key investment industries.

A significant increase in raw material inventory is one of the manifestations of higher industry activity. The improvement in economic levels led to more orders, increasing work-in-progress and semi-finished products, naturally requiring more raw materials.

Xifei International's inventory in the first half of 2011 was 9.2 billion yuan, an increase of 900 million yuan compared to the same period last year. Meanwhile, the inventory at the end of 2010 also reached 7.7 billion yuan. The company explained that the main reason for the increase in inventory was the increase in material procurement expenditures and work-in-progress due to order tasks.

However, the obvious acceleration in the pace of production capacity expansion in the machinery industry in recent years has brought hidden concerns to the stable development of the industry in the future. "Once demand declines or the market becomes saturated, it will be detrimental to the development of the industry," Du Pu pointed out, "In recent years, this industry has rarely raised prices."

For inventory, if there are risks, listed companies can appropriately avoid risks through provisions. For the entire industry, "Currently, it is still in the stage of producing more low-end products. To avoid risks, efforts must be made to improve technological content, focus on developing new products, and strive towards producing high-end products," Du Pu told reporters.

From the 2010 annual reports of multiple machinery listed companies, I also saw that enterprises are already moving in this direction. Gree Electric's 2010 annual report pointed out that due to the significant growth in sales of new products with independent intellectual property rights, the company's profitability has been improved.
