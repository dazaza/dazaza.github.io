Letting Go and Awakening

When love has become a distant path, we suddenly awaken: what we once thought was an eternal bond is no more than a fleeting embrace of strangers.

When feelings have grown indifferent, we suddenly realize: the happiness we once thought was sealed by intertwined fingers was no more than a handhold seeking warmth.

It turns out, everything was just a profound gaze between two parallel lines! Even if their gazes are full of deep emotion, they will never intersect. When all dissipates like smoke, even if we are not far apart, we are already at opposite ends of the earth! Breaking up is not because we don't love anymore, but because we've strayed from our original intentions and can no longer love.

The price of courage is letting go of emotions, admitting failure, and accepting helplessness. A soft sigh: how sorrowful is a life without love? Blessings only mock the foolishness of our past! Not every 'I'm sorry' can be met with a 'It's okay.'

From now on, the heart will be as still water, but this is not liberation; it is merely - awakening.

The feeling of happiness is like brilliant fireworks, beautiful but fleeting! Why should we cling to the frustration of trying to hold onto something that cannot last forever? Letting go will be the only release. After letting go, you will find: fireworks cannot remain in the sky forever! And we, cannot stay indefinitely in the sadness of that lost affection.

Mortals entangled in worldly affairs, how could they possibly escape the web of love and hatred? How could they avoid the vortex of loving and being loved? Why care about broken hearts and shattered spirits? Why worry about boundless loneliness? After brilliance comes solitude, which will invade the heart like leeches.

In the future, we will no longer live for someone else! We no longer need to rack our brains guessing his thoughts, no longer need to force smiles to please him, no longer need to drift further and further away from our own happiness for him...

Although there is a faint ache in the heart, we will no longer cry easily! Because, when things remain the same but people change, there is no reason or excuse!

Temporary tears are merely memories of the heart - point by point, drop by drop, unwilling to fade. Time's medicine heals the wounds of love.

Do you fear to love me? I ask myself time and again, but there is no answer.

Perhaps, it's not fear, but rather the so-called love has passed, the heart has broken, and tears have flowed... The pursuit and enjoyment were always about the process alone. Why should we cling to the pretense of forgetting?

No matter if it's riddled with holes or heart-wrenchingly painful, it's merely proof that we've loved! Love, pain as evidence; injury, tears as proof.

Thus, I let you go! And give myself a way out. From now on, we shall meet as strangers! I just want to, in the end, crystallize you into a painting, deeply etched in my mind! Looking at, thinking of, missing, then - slowly forgetting. Unfortunately, you will no longer be the person in that painting.

Farewell, take care. Turning around, I hope my silhouette can be engraved in your eyes - even if just for a moment, it's enough.

When we can recall the bits and pieces of the past with a heart of acceptance, then, we can experience the beauty after letting go.

Because - after letting go, one awakens.
