Tahitian Noni International (http://www.haononi.com), a U.S. company, was founded on July 1, 1996 in Provo, Utah, USA. The newly constructed global headquarters spans a total area of 150,000 square feet. The current number of employees exceeds 2,000. Since its establishment in 1996, Tahitian Noni International has grown continuously and rapidly due to its strong foundation, top-notch management team, advanced research technology, and high-quality products, becoming a leader in the industry.

After nine years of operation and successfully expanding into other international markets, Tahitian Noni International officially entered the Chinese market in June 2005 (http://www.haononi.com).

Tahitian Noni Beverage (China) Co., Ltd. (http://www.haononi.com) carries forward the corporate culture, development strategy, and vision of the parent company. In just over two years, it has quickly established branches and product display/sales centers in seven locations: Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shenyang, Nanjing, and Fuzhou. It has also set up an internationally standardized production base in Guangzhou, bringing pure and healthy Tahitian Noni juice to every Chinese family. Additionally, with the continuous launch of Pu Ai Ma skincare products, Noni drinks, tea, soft candies, and more, it offers Chinese consumers more choices.

In the short span of two years since entering China, Tahitian Noni Beverage (China) Co., Ltd. has actively promoted its brand and strongly supported related activities. In 2005, it was rated as a "Model Unit for Honest Business Operations in China" and the "Most Trusted Food Enterprise in China-Guangzhou." In 2006, it participated in national public welfare activities such as protecting the Mother River and supporting major events like the Shanghai International Film Festival and the Hangzhou West Lake Expo. In 2007, Lang Ping became the spokesperson for Tahitian Noni juice, drawing significant attention from the media and the public. The theme advertisement featuring Lang Ping endorsing the slogan "Reward Yourself With Health" is currently airing on CCTV, conveying a simple, pure, healthy, and natural lifestyle to consumers.
