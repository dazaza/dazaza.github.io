Thirteen Sister

20 episodes, aired on January 24, 1983. Theme song "Heroine" sung by Paula Yee.

Extra actors:

Stephen Chow

Plot summary:

During the Qing Kangxi period, loyal minister He Daren was framed by General Ji Xiantang, resulting in his entire family being executed, except for his wife and daughter Yufeng (Wong Hing Sau), who escaped. Yufeng bravely rushed to the execution ground to save her father, killing thirteen executioners, but was ultimately injured. She was saved by the Nine Difficulties Nun (Li Linlin). Yufeng thus earned the name [Thirteen Sister]. Another loyal minister, An Daren, also suffered the same fate, with only his son An Ji (Tang Chun Yat) escaping. An Ji and Yufeng had grown up together since childhood, so they fled together. On the road, they coincidentally met Princess Yu, who was fleeing her arranged marriage. The Nine Difficulties Nun realized that the princess was Emperor Kangxi's younger sister and planned to assassinate Kangxi through her. Princess Yu, unaware of worldly affairs, did not realize her identity had been uncovered and instead fell in love with Yufeng, who was disguised as a man, causing quite a laugh. Later, the famous doctor Li Miaosheng (Ekin Cheng) appeared, and Princess Yu transferred her affection to Miaosheng. Ji Xiantang's daughter Ziwei, acting as a mole under her father's orders, infiltrated Yufeng's group, but Ziwei fell in love with An Ji over time, creating a triangle romance. At this point, Emperor Kangxi embarked on a southern tour, and the Nine Difficulties Nun prepared everything for the assassination. Ji Xiantang also set up a tight net, intending to capture Yufeng and her companions. Thus, a fierce battle ensued.

Review:

The most interesting part of the drama is still the story between Princess Yu and Dr. Li Miaosheng. Maggie Wang portrayed the spoiled,任性 yet innocent and kind princess vividly, which won her much favor when she competed for the role of Huang Rong in the same year. She fell in love with the cross-dressed Thirteen Sister, while Dr. Li Miaosheng followed her around all day with an oversized gourd, eventually winning her heart. Once, Princess Yu stayed at an inn without rooms available, leading to a quarrel with two guests who arrived before her. One of them was our Stephen Chow, whose appearance in Qing dynasty costume somewhat resembled Bao Longxing. Watching this series made me realize that Ekin Cheng became famous earlier than Stephen Chow.
