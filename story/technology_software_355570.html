The BYK-Gardner cross-cut test instruments are available in single-sided multi-blade simple type and circular multi-blade type to achieve longer service life. Material: High alloy steel.

Cross-cut method is a simple and effective way to evaluate the adhesion of single or multi-layer coatings.

Operating steps:

1. Use the tool to cut a cross-hatch pattern on the coating until the substrate;

2. Brush five times diagonally with a brush, apply adhesive tape over the cuts and pull it off;

3. Inspect the grid area with a magnifying glass with lighting;

4. Evaluate according to the grading standard of the cross-cut result (ISO grade 0-5/ASTM grade 5B-0B).

Standards specify the distance between two blades:

Paint film thickness less than 60 μm (2 mils): Blade spacing 1mm

Paint film thickness up to 120 μm (5 mils): Blade spacing 2mm

Paint film thickness exceeding 120 μm (5 mils): Blade spacing 3mm

Standards: ASTM D3002 D3359 DIN EN ISO 2409
