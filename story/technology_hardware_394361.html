If you're a newbie starting an online store selling physical goods, you must possess the following advantages to do well:

1. You have a supply chain advantage, meaning you either own a factory, produce the goods yourself, or have good connections to secure quality supplies at competitive prices. Without these, it will be hard to compete with big sellers on Taobao, especially as a new seller.

2. You have sufficient capital. Don't think you can start a physical goods store without enough funds to even join the Consumer Protection program. Waiting to make money before joining won't work. Buyers won't trust you if you're not part of the Consumer Protection program, and your products won't even show up in search results. The top-ranked sellers in search results are all part of the Consumer Protection program.

3. You have ample time to be online throughout the day. If you're looking to make quick money, physical goods might not be the best choice. Building credibility for physical goods takes time, sometimes months without making a single sale. If you're often offline or away, potential customers may leave your store quickly if no one responds to their inquiries.

4. You have marketing experience. The era of opening a store and waiting for customers to come in is over. Marketing is key, followed by the product itself. This applies both online and offline.

5. You know how to design and decorate your store, including creating templates, defining the store's style, writing detailed product descriptions, and categorizing items properly. These factors are crucial. The success of a store depends on three things: design, price, and traffic.

As for sourcing goods, that's a concern for later stages. Here we're discussing whether your business will thrive, not customer retention rates. Customer retention isn't solely dependent on the quality of your goods.

If you meet the above criteria, you can proceed with selling physical goods. I believe you'll succeed because you'll have advantages others lack!
