F: How do I play music from the music list?

Q: Double-click the music in the list.

F: Why can't some music be played?

Q: 1. The song link has expired. 2. The music website uses anti-leeching technology. 3. The song is from QQ Music.

F: Why doesn't my space popularity increase when I try to brush it?

Q: Each QQ account can add up to 100 popularity points per day to others. Beyond 100 points, no further increase will occur.

F: How do I greet city experts found through searches?

Q: Double-click the city expert in the list.

F: Why does adding someone as a friend after searching always fail?

Q: The other QQ user has set their account to reject friend requests or requires answering a question.

F: How do I leave messages for people in a specific friend group?

Q: Select a batch of friends, right-click and choose Friend Management, then right-click again in the friend management list.

F: How do I close the friend information panel?

Q: Click the right mouse button.

F: How do I import QQ numbers in bulk for boosting popularity?

Q: Right-click in the number list, import the number file. Each line should contain one number, formatted as "number-password", such as "12345-abcde".
