Professional terms frequently used in the car beauty industry:

I have worked in the local car beauty industry for many years and now I would like to share my experience with everyone.

First, Coating: Coating is a car wax containing polymer.

Second, No-wax car wash: Just use special car wash liquid to wash off the dust and dirt on the body surface, but the original wax on the paint surface still exists. This is the most common daily car wash.

Third, Clear coat: Clear coat is a transparent clear lacquer covering a layer on the color.

Fourth, Polishing agent: Polishing agent is mainly used to eliminate the grinding marks left by the grinding process, and it is also a powerful cleaning agent for the car paint surface before waxing.

Fifth, Car wash wax: Car wash wax is a kind of car wash liquid containing water-based wax, which can give the paint surface gloss while washing the car, achieving two goals at once, but the gloss does not last long.

Sixth, Enhancer: Enhancer is a two-in-one product that combines polishing and gloss (wax). Polishing wax: car wax containing extremely soft friction materials.

Seventh, Sunlines: Sunlines are micro scratches formed by the friction between the car paint and sand during high-speed driving over a long period of time.

Eighth, Grinding agent: A emulsion containing various friction materials, used to deal with defects on the car paint surface. Strong grinding agents with good friction ability are called deep-cut grinding agents; moderate friction ability and softer grinding agents are called medium-cut grinding agents; extremely soft friction ability and very slight damage to the paint surface are called micro-cut grinding agents.

Ninth, Mirror finish: A polishing agent containing high molecular release agent, mainly used for secondary polishing of car paint (removing halos and scratches formed during polishing) and forming an enamel mirror finish protective film on the car paint surface.

Tenth, Anti-explosion film: The anti-explosion sun film (anti-explosion heat insulation paper) is attached to the side window glass of the car. It has a high light blocking rate, can effectively prevent ultraviolet rays, enhance the anti-shattering ability of the glass, improve the air conditioning efficiency, and create the best aesthetic effect.

Eleventh, Gloss wax: Car wax that does not contain any friction material.

Twelfth, Protector: Protector is a cleaner or gloss agent containing polymer, which plays a role in preventing aging and corrosion while cleaning or glossing.

Thirteenth, Traffic film: A layer of film formed by the oxidation of car paint due to static electricity adsorption caused by friction with air during use, and adsorbing dust and harmful gases, etc.

Fourteenth, Regular paint: Regular paint is a paint without any transparent paint coverage.

Fifteenth, De-waxing car wash: Use car wash liquid that can wash away the original wax layer on the car surface to wash the car. Mainly used before paint repair, paint defect treatment, and repainting. For example, if you want to remove the wax from a new car, you must use de-waxing car wash;
