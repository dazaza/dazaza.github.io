Maxthon 2.0.9 Beta Download

by xloong on 2008-03-20 22:15:55

Nightfire: Personally, I'm quite satisfied with the addition of a download feature in Maxthon 2.0.8 Spring Festival Edition. The added download function is pretty good, and I haven't touched Xunlei (a popular download manager) for a long time now~ My ISP is China Unicom. Although the stability for gaming isn't great, the download speed is excellent. On large sites like Huajun, the download speed can reach 560 K/s (not a peak value). I've never used Maxthon's built-in downloader for large files before, so I didn't pay much attention to it. It's roughly around 260+ K/s. Although it's not as fast as Xunlei, it consumes far fewer resources. A friend of mine uses China Telecom, and when they were using Xunlei, the speed was only about 200 K/s. After my recommendation, they switched to Maxthon's built-in downloader and achieved speeds of about 260-270 K/s. This update to version 2.0.9 has enhanced Maxthon's download capabilities, which I am really looking forward to. However, each update still doesn't address the most serious issue of Maxthon browser —— the pseudo-freezing problem.

Maxthon's first update after the Spring Festival —— Maxthon 2.0.9 Beta.

This upgrade includes significant changes, such as a completely redesigned in-page search, enhanced popup blocking features, and improved Maxthon download functionality.
