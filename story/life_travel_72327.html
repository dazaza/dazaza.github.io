How to book a hotel room in Guilin

by zengxr840824 on 2008-06-01 11:29:48

1. What are the star-rated hotels of various levels in Guilin? And what are the non-star-rated hotels with reasonable prices?

Answer: The five-star hotels in Guilin include Lijiang Grand Waterfall Hotel, Dayu Grand Hotel, Dynasty Hotel, and Le Man Diaz Resort Hotel; four-star hotels include Guilin Hotel, Banyan Lake Hotel, Guihu Hotel, Sightseeing Hotel, Aviation Grand Hotel, Guishan Grand Hotel, and Jinn Long Grand Hotel, etc.; three-star hotels include Jinpu Hotel, Jinxiang Hotel, Jingxiu Hotel, Seven Star Hotel, Shanshui Grand Hotel, Guixing Hotel, Xiangjiang Hotel, Xin Kaiyue Hotel, Kyoto Grand Hotel, Universal Grand Hotel, Fuboshan Grand Hotel, Zhongshan Grand Hotel, Civil Aviation Building, Huaqiao Grand Hotel, Guihua Fragrance Grand Hotel, Shan Grand Hotel, and Jingxiu Hotel, etc. Non-star-rated hotels with reasonable prices include Rongde Hotel, Fengyuan Hotel, Jingui New Capital Hotel, Jinwan Grand Hotel, Noble Guest 0773 Hotel, Lizheng Grand Hotel, Guantai Grand Hotel, etc.

2. Can you recommend rooms with a view of the Lijiang River? And what are the centrally located budget-friendly hotels?

Answer: Rooms with a view of the Lijiang River include those at Lijiang Grand Waterfall Hotel, Dayu Grand Hotel, Dynasty Hotel, Jinxiang Hotel, Universal Hotel, and Fuboshan Grand Hotel, etc. Centrally located budget-friendly hotels include Rongde Hotel, Jinpu Hotel, Lizheng Hotel, Fengyuan Hotel, etc.

3. What hotels are there in Yangshuo, especially on West Street?

Answer: Hotels on West Street in Yangshuo include Yangshuo Belle Vue Resort Hotel, Li景 Holiday Hotel, Yuanyuan Hotel, Minzu Hotel, West Street Ai Hua Hotel, West Street International Youth Hostel, Venice Hotel, etc.
