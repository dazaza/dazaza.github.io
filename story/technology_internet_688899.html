Suzhou Mitsubishi PLC Full-time Training Course

Job Definition:

The Suzhou PLC [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/] programmable control system designer refers to personnel who engage in the selection and programming of Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), as well as design, overall integration, and maintenance of application systems.

Main Job Content:

(1) Overall design of PLC application systems and configuration design of PLCs;

(2) Selection of PLC modules and determination of technical specifications for related products;

(3) PLC programming and setting;

(4) Equipment parameter setting and accompanying program design;

(5) Design, overall integration, debugging, and maintenance of control systems.

Learning Content:

Our learning process is divided into three stages. The first stage is foundational courses, mainly aimed at students with no prior knowledge from Suzhou Electrician Training [http://www.bangyuanedu.com]. The second stage is professional courses on Suzhou PLC [http://www.bangyuanedu.com/], after which students will be organized to take the Senior/Level 3 Certificate Exam for "Programmable Control System Designer." The third stage is an advanced class, mainly expanding on topics like inverters, touchscreens, servos, analog quantities, communications, etc., based on PLC knowledge. Below is our course content:
