College student wraps herself in a towel to get food

by 304885068 on 2008-04-16 19:27:58

Recently, many portal sites have released a shocking video. In this short video, which is about 1 minute and 3 seconds long, a young girl wrapped only in a bath towel stands in front of what appears to be a university canteen food supply window to get her meal.

Many websites have titled this video "The Strongest Female College Student Ever Goes to the Cafeteria Wrapped in a Bath Towel", with some informed netizens directly pointing out that the video was filmed in the campus cafeteria of Nanjing Yingtian Vocational and Technical College.

This short video, about 1 minute and 3 seconds long, has average picture quality, seemingly shot by a mobile phone, but it has become the top story or hot topic on many commercial websites. The regular layout of the university canteen in the video, as well as the numerous students seen moving through the lens, are enough to show that this video was indeed filmed in a campus cafeteria.

In the video, the girl holds a meal card in her right hand, carries a plastic bag in her left hand along with a lunch box, and moves calmly between different food windows. During this time, the girl even squats down to pick up items that have fallen to the ground.

At the end of the video, the girl seems to realize that someone is filming her and quickly runs out of the camera's view.

This "news" has a very high click rate and has become a hot topic on various websites.

This video also includes on-site sound, from which it can be heard that the girl's actions caused quite a stir in the cafeteria.

"Where is she? We three are almost at the dormitory, saw this woman, then rushed over." A male voice from outside the frame approaches, clearly someone familiar to the shooter, after which the shooter tells the person who came running: "Sit, sit, sit."

"Who has the guts to go and pull her bath towel?" A male suddenly says, immediately causing a burst of laughter.

At this point, the shooter shouts loudly: "Who has the guts? Whoever has the guts should go!"

According to reports by Xinmin Network, the content of this video was first released on SonicBBS, a forum in Shanghai, by a netizen named "yorick-yang". And according to investigations by reporters, the website that first uploaded this video was "Wole.com", with an upload date of August 7, 2007.
