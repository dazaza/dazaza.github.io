Subordinate offender of homicide gets lenient sentence after self-turning in 6 years of escape

by wls297077866 on 2012-02-27 15:19:26

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing (reporter Sun Siya) - A conflict between Audi and Jinbei vehicles at a karaoke bar entrance resulted in a brawl that left Yang, a passenger in the Jinbei vehicle, dead. Yesterday, it was reported that one of the participants from the Audi side turned himself in to the police six years after fleeing and was sentenced to nine years in prison by the First Intermediate People's Court of the city.

Lu Xianglin, 35, from Heilongjiang Province, is nicknamed MaoMao. According to the prosecution, around 9 p.m. on May 19, 2005, Lu Xianglin conspired with Zhao Liguan, Chen Hailong, Ma Gang, Sun Xikun, Wang Jianjun (all already sentenced), and others outside the KTV hall in Huilongguan, Changping District. A dispute over parking led to a physical altercation with Yang and others, resulting in Yang's death due to cranial injuries. Lu Xianglin surrendered to the Hengshan Branch of the Jixi Municipal Public Security Bureau in Heilongjiang Province on August 22, 2011.

According to co-defendants who were already sentenced, on the night of the incident, they celebrated Sun Xikun's birthday with dinner. Afterward, more than ten people went to sing at the KTV hall. Chen Hailong drove an Audi car with part of the group to the Happy Google KTV hall.

On the sidewalk outside the KTV, a Jinbei vehicle was parked facing Chen Hailong’s Audi. Chen sounded the horn but the Jinbei did not move; instead, several individuals got out of the Jinbei. Chen also exited his car, and both groups began fighting over the parking issue. Upon receiving a phone call, Lu Xianglin and others rushed to the scene and participated in the melee.

Liu, who was with the deceased Yang, recounted that their company colleagues had dinner together before going to sing at the Happy Google KTV hall. As they prepared to leave, they found an Audi blocking their way. They negotiated with the driver to move the car, but due to unfriendly interaction, a fight ensued.

The court ruled that Lu Xianglin, over trivial matters, colluded with others to intentionally harm the victim's body, leading to the victim's death, thus constituting intentional injury. Given that Lu Xianglin was an accomplice and surrendered voluntarily, and considering that his relatives actively compensated for the economic losses suffered by the victim's family, gaining their forgiveness, he was sentenced to nine years in prison with leniency.
