Minnuan "Love Journey" special train will start in November

by syd17u123 on 2009-11-23 18:29:06

Do you want to travel to Sichuan after the earthquake? The "Hefei Six Awards" is promoting tourism vigorously; the "Love Journey" special train will set off on November 15th, with a total journey of 9 days, costing about 1500 yuan. Currently, love tourists are still being recruited, and registration should be done quickly.

It is understood that only six cities and states along the Longmen Mountain range were severely affected by the earthquake in Sichuan this time, with the provincial tourism routes basically intact and most scenic spots unscathed.

This "Love Journey" special train event from Fujian and Sichuan is a support and concern for post-earthquake tourism in Sichuan, hosted by the Fujian Provincial Tourism Bureau and the Xiamen Municipal Tourism Bureau, and undertaken by Xiamen Chunhui International Travel Agency. This special train has the potential to become China's first love tourism special train to the disaster area after the earthquake.

The entire special train is sleeper cars, with a total of five tourist routes. The registration fee is the same: Hard Sleeper: Upper berth 1380 yuan, Middle berth 1480 yuan, Lower berth 1580 yuan; Soft Sleeper: Upper berth 1780 yuan, Lower berth 1880 yuan.

According to relevant persons in charge of Xiamen Chunhui International Travel Agency, all profits from this love journey will be donated to the Xiamen Charity Federation. At the same time, a mobile donation box will be set up on the special train, and group members are called upon to donate one or two new warm clothes, one or two toys, one or two books, etc., which can express their love. These love items will also be delivered to Pengzhou disaster areas with the special train.

The special train will set off on November 15th and return to Xiamen on the 23rd.

"Love Journey to Pengzhou" as the main line

The "Love Journey" from Fujian and Sichuan centers around Chengdu, opening five routes: North Line, South Line, East Line, Northwest Line, and Southwest Line, among which the North Line "Love Journey to Pengzhou" is the main line.

The North Line suits tourists who want to experience Chengdu and the disaster areas; the East Line is a pilgrimage to the birthplace of Guanyin Bodhisattva, Suining, and Guang'an, suitable for pilgrims and those who wish to pay respects to the birthplace of great men due to its long history; the South Line is a trip to Zigong to explore ancient times and the Southern Bamboo Sea, suitable for tourists interested in nostalgia, technology, and nature; the West Line is a charm journey to the Hailuogou Glacier, suitable for vacationers and nature lovers who enjoy hot springs; the Southwest Line is a classic traditional line for a love journey to Pengzhou, Mount Emei, and Leshan, suitable for most groups.
