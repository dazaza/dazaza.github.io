Xinyu Imaging's introduction to shooting styles

by caiqiao122 on 2010-03-30 11:04:16

We calculate according to the styling. For Beijing maternity photoshoots, four sets of styling are taken indoors (the A package includes three sets of styling), which takes half a day, approximately three and a half hours (including one hour for makeup). The shooting content can be selected from the following styles, or you can decide on the day of the shoot at the store. Any price range can be chosen from groups 1-14, while the last two groups (group 15 and 16) are limited to the C package or higher price ranges.

Group One: "Mediterranean Journey" series one: "Take Him/Her to See the Sea". With blue and white as the main colors, it is fresh and lively. Shot in the studio's real scene room.

Group Two: "Mediterranean Journey" series two: "Blue Afternoon". This style is leisurely and quiet, suitable for home settings. Shot in the studio's real scene room.

Group Three: "Romantic Full House". Once launched, this sample was deeply loved by many pregnant mothers! It fully embodies the warm country home style, shot in the studio's real scene room.

Group Four: "Tender as Water", this set is more romantic and beautiful. Especially suitable for long-haired ladies, wearing white veils and showing the belly while taking photos.

Group Five: "Yoga Pure White". Compared with the previous group, this set is simpler and more sporty. Using professional lighting effects, it captures the healthy and full spirit of pregnant women. Very popular.

Group Six: "Black and White Imagery". This set is black-themed. As they say, "black hides all flaws", especially suitable for those who think they are overweight. It has a strong photographic art light effect and is also suitable for taking pictures with your husband.

Group Seven: "Full of Colors". Through bright and colorful clothing, it shows the lively and cute side of pregnant mothers. Taking some relaxed and lively pictures with your husband is very effective.

Group Eight: "Monet's Garden". This set imitates the oil painting style, pregnant mothers who like this style can try it out.

Group Nine: "Mickey and Minnie". Launched in 2009.09. The husband plays the role of Mickey. For more details: http://www.iiheart.cn/4/43.htm Website: http://www.iiheart.cn/
