International Online Exclusive: According to the Daily Mail on July 12, a pair of British twin sisters liked to wear the same clothes, once shared the same boyfriend, worked the same job in the same office at the same company, and their wedding ceremonies were only five minutes apart. Last year, the two underwent identical eight-hour cosmetic surgery procedures on the same day.

Diane Reeve and Christine Martin are twin sisters. In the first half of last year, the two underwent facial cosmetic surgery on the same day. Christine had her surgery in the morning, while Diane had hers in the afternoon. The results left the 54-year-old sisters very satisfied. In an interview, Diane said, "To be able to have the same surgery on the same day, we almost went all over the UK. We wanted to do it together because neither of us wanted to see a sister who looked different after the surgery."

The success of the surgery made the two extremely happy. Christine and Diane are considering undergoing other types of plastic surgery. According to Christine, since they were little, she and Diane have loved wearing the same clothes. Every time their mom tried to dress them differently, the two would cry and throw tantrums. Even now, one-third of the clothes in their wardrobes are exactly the same. When it came time to date, Christine and Diane's standards for choosing partners were also very consistent. The two would even playfully share the same boyfriend without him knowing he was dating two people. After starting work, the two ended up working the same job in the same office at the same company — as legal secretaries. During the ten years before retirement, Christine and Diane would go running together every morning.

Diane recalled that so far, the most different thing between the two has been pregnancy. Although their wedding ceremonies were only five minutes apart, and they were each other's bridesmaids, their pregnancies occurred at different times. (By Li Jie)
