Registration Process for Domestic Companies:

1. Consultation and Form Collection > 2. Name Search > 3. Capital Contribution and Verification > 4. Print Articles of Association > 5. Submission of Materials > 6. License Collection

Flowchart for Establishing a Domestic Company (excluding specialized pre-approval processes)

Handling Authority: Municipal or District Administration for Industry and Commerce (AIC)

Enterprise Name Pre-registration

Processing Time: 5 working days

Submit the application form for name pre-registration, applicant's identity proof or authorization letter, shareholder identity proof, etc.

Capital Contribution at Designated Bank

Verification by Accounting Firm

Handling Authority: Municipal or District AIC

Enterprise Registration

Processing Time: 10 working days

Provide registration application, articles of association, appointment documents and identity proof of legal representative, enterprise name pre-approval notice, company address proof, etc.

Approval Authority: Special Industry Section of Public Security Bureau

Seal Engraving

Approval Time: 5 working days

Provide business license, identity proof of legal representative, etc., to obtain approval from the Special Industry Section of the Public Security Bureau before engraving seals.

Organization Code Certificate

Handling Authority: Municipal Quality and Technical Supervision Bureau

Processing Time: 3 working days

Provide business license, identity proof of legal representative, company seal, etc.

Statistical Registration

Handling Authority: Municipal or District Bureau of Statistics

Processing Time: Immediate processing

Within 15 days after obtaining the business license, register with the business license, company seal, project approval documents, etc.

Tax Registration

Handling Authority: Tax Office

Processing Time: 15 working days

Fill out the tax registration form, provide business license, relevant contracts, articles of association, agreements, bank account proof, resident identity proof, property tax payment proof or lease agreement, etc., to handle local tax registration and invoice determination. Initial tax declaration is determined before the company starts paying taxes.
