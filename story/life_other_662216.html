You cried, and it started to rain.

Online submission

When tears are taken in by the diary, in which direction of prosperity am I approaching.

Time is like a song, and the mortal world is easy to age. I always think that one day I can be far away from the hustle and bustle of the world, and go to pursue the steps of the ancients, but alas, the real world cannot accommodate the ideal of transcendence. Therefore, I exile myself again and again in this surging mortal world.

Perhaps because I have been living alone for a long time, I increasingly like solitude and do not want to step into other people's prosperity. I just quietly look up at the sky in my own world.

I really enjoy this moment of peace, where I can clearly hear the singing of loneliness. The spring flowers and autumn moon are endless, but unfortunately, the mist and clouds disappear in a moment, and I am deeply intoxicated in it.

Sometimes, I miss those figures in the photos. Looking at those smiling faces, I always have a feeling of being separated by a world. The past events are vividly in sight, as if they were yesterday. But I know that if we part with the past, we cannot return to the past world.

Maybe, as that saying goes: "A game is a dream", but unfortunately, the prosperity in the dream lasts so long that it does not want to dissipate.

I don't know when it started, but I fell in love with the elegant tunes in light music. They are peaceful and distant, just like the fairies wandering in the bubbling stream, so natural.

How I wish to embark on a journey to the horizon, stand on the top of the cape, and embrace the whole sky.

How I wish to wander through the mountains and rivers, wield ink and brush, and dance with the sword under the pale moon.

However, the cruel reality tore my full passion apart. Silent years are like the spread-out ink painting, brushing over the hazy blur, but locking the thousands of sorrows in my heart.

Once again, I look afar, whether there is my staggering figure in the footprint of the fleeting years.

Listening to the wind and rain in a small building for a night, who is standing alone at the gate watching.

The farewell song of time, the wandering footprints, seem to be leaving, only leaving me sighing alone: As long as the heaven does not grow old, the feelings will never end. My heart is like a double silk net, with thousands of knots in it. The night has passed, the eastern window is not yet white, and the lonely lamp is still shining under the moonlight.
