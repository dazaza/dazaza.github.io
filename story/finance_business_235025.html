Party A and Party B, adhering to the principles of equality, mutual benefit, and complementary advantages, agree to form a long-term, comprehensive strategic partnership on the INTERNET. This partnership aims to achieve resource sharing and common development while establishing a solid foundation for future cooperation in other projects. After friendly consultations, the following consensus has been reached:

1. Rights and Obligations

1. Both Party A and Party B recognize each other as strategic partners and prominently display the partner’s banner logo or text link on their respective websites.

2. Both parties authorize the partner to reprint relevant information from the other party’s website, subject to mutual agreement before citation (specific cooperation projects will be signed under separate agreements).

3. When either Party A or Party B reprints or references information from the partner’s website, they must indicate the source of the information and establish a link.

4. Both parties must respect the copyright and ownership of the partner’s website information. Without the partner’s consent, the other party may not use the information for commercial purposes, nor publish information obtained from the partner’s site on media outside of their own websites; otherwise, it constitutes an infringement. The aggrieved party has the right to unilaterally terminate the cooperation and choose to hold the other party accountable for damages depending on the severity of the situation.

2. Mutual Promotion

1. Both Party A and Party B should track and report on the market promotion plans and related marketing activities of the partner on their respective sites.

2. Within an appropriate time frame agreed by both parties, both sides will open special columns on their respective sites to write about and promote topics related to the commercial behavior of the cooperative partner (specific cooperation projects will be signed under separate agreements).
