After dinner, take Y268/5 at 19:18 PM to Zhanjiang Breakfast, lunch and dinner Train

12/24 Arrive in Zhanjiang at 7:36 AM. After breakfast, take a car to Hai'an, transfer to a ferry across the Qiongzhou Strait to Haikou, take a car to Qionghai, visit Bo'ao Water City: exterior view of Bo'ao Asia Forum temporary venue, boat tour of Jade Belt Beach, Red Detachment of Women Monument, then take a car to Xinglong, car tour of Wanquan River countryside scenery, in the evening self-fee to watch red artist performance Breakfast, lunch and dinner Xinglong

12/25 Visit Xinglong Botanical Garden, visit Xinglong Coffee Factory, go to Sanya to visit the No.1 Bay in the World - Yazhou Bay Central Plaza, Shell Museum; visit Deer回头Folklore Park - listen to the touching love story, Dadonghai (diving at your own expense), watch tea art performance, taste Hainan bitter jujube tea Breakfast, lunch and dinner Sanya

12/26 Visit Tianya Haijiao, Nantian Yi Zhu, Crystal Expo, Hainan local specialties; Wanlv Park, take a car back to Haikou Breakfast, lunch and dinner Haikou

12/27 After breakfast, freely shop at local specialty supermarket, take a boat back to Hai'an, take a car back to Zhanjiang, at 17:40 PM take Y216 to Guilin North Breakfast, lunch and dinner Train

12/28 Arrive in Guilin North at 4:41 AM. After breakfast, take a boat to visit three mountains, visit national A-level scenic area - Seven Star Park (flower sedan chair, Chinese civilization light, Clinton speech platform, camel peak), visit Guilin famous cave - Seven Star Cave, Yu Mountain Park, banyan tree pond scenic area, after dinner enjoy large-scale song and dance show "Song Fairy Liu Sanjie", check into hotel Breakfast, lunch and dinner Guilin

12/29 After breakfast, buy local specialties, at 10:45 AM take Y202 back to Beijing Breakfast, lunch and dinner Train
