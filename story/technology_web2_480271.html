Pocket Yu Bo: Self-hosted podcast radio and voice microblogging service

by daodao on 2011-08-21 11:01:04

Wradio is an entertainment platform that focuses on voice. It has its own podcast radio station and also allows users to post voices for interactive friendship. Wradio provides web and mobile deployment, with 24-hour uninterrupted live broadcasts of self-organized podcast radio programs. Users can send notes to participate in the live interaction环节s, and can also enter the radio chat room to chat freely with mobile phone users around the world, including text and voice chat. Moreover, the Wradio mobile client provides a karaoke function, offering users thousands of high-quality accompaniments. Users can sing their hearts out into their phones and upload and share the recorded songs with more mobile users with one click.

In my view, there are two points about Wradio that might create trends: 1) the personal involvement of radio hosts; 2) voice microblogging interactions among listeners. If these two can be well combined, it could even lead to the next new breakout point. One undeniable viewpoint is that within a limited time, the amount of information conveyed by voice far exceeds that of text. People would prefer this way to express their joys and sorrows under certain circumstances, which is why Talkbox revolutionarily created a network walkie-talkie.

However, when users' sense of novelty gradually fades, and without a topic continuously stimulating and guiding users to interact, user stickiness and loyalty will inevitably face various tests during this process. In this aspect, Wradio precisely fills in a series of shortcomings that Talkbox may naturally have. The personally involved radio hosts are constantly injecting soul and topics into this community, giving users the opportunity to derive more talking points and interactions from the topics thrown out by DJs! More importantly, all of this happens against a larger backdrop: our society has entered the "fan" era. Fans have high recognition of public figures, public events, and cultural trends. Their enormous enthusiasm will ultimately be fully and satisfactorily released in this community through interactive entertainment, directly promoting the formation of this community's communication ecosystem!

Disadvantages: Wradio lacks competitiveness and appeal at the product level, and the internet-based aspects in the product seem somewhat barren.
