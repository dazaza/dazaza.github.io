Do you want to sell things online? Do you want to become a big boss on Taobao? Do you want to open a store?

Let me teach you step by step.

First, Preparations Before Opening the Store

Before opening an online store, you need to choose a website that provides a personal store platform and register as a user. To ensure transaction security, you also need to undergo corresponding identity and payment method verification. The reason for choosing to open a store on Taobao is because it's completely free. To open a shop on Taobao, you need to meet three conditions: one, register as a member and pass certification; two, post at least 10 items (including 10 items); three, to facilitate safe transactions, it is recommended to activate online banking.

1. User Registration

Log in to http://www.taobao.com and click the "Free Registration" button at the top of the page. In the opened page, input your username, password, email address, etc., and then click the "Agree to the following service terms, submit registration information" button. Then, the email used for registration will receive a confirmation email. Open the link inside, confirm it, and you have completed user registration.

Security Tip: To ensure transaction safety, avoid setting overly simple passwords. It is suggested to use a combination of "English letters + numbers + symbols".

2. Identity Verification

Taobao regulations state that only after passing real-name verification can you sell items and open a store. Therefore, after registering as a user, you must undergo corresponding verification (including personal real-name verification and Alipay verification). The specific operational steps are as follows:

Step One, log in to Taobao, click "My Taobao" at the top of the page. In the opened page, click "Please click here" next to the text "If you want to sell items, please complete Alipay verification first."

Step Two, in the opened page, it will prompt that your Alipay account has not been activated yet. Click "Click here to complete Alipay account activation." In the popped-up page, input your real name, type of identification document and number, Alipay password, etc., and click the "Save and immediately activate Alipay account" button.

Step Three, after successfully activating your Alipay account, go back to the original page, press the F5 key to refresh the page. Click the "Apply for Alipay Personal Real Name Verification" button, read the Alipay Verification Service Terms, then click the "I have read..." button to continue.

Step Four, first, fill out your personal information according to the prompts, click "Next"; then, choose ID verification. You can choose "Online Upload" or "Mail" copies of your ID documents, click "Next"; then, input your bank card information, including the bank where the account was opened, bank card number, province, city, etc. After inputting, wait for Alipay to transfer money within one day.

Friendly Tip: If you upload copies of your ID documents online, the size of the image file should be controlled within 200KB; if it's an IC ID card, you also need to provide a picture of the back.

Step Five, after one day, reopen "My Taobao," click the corresponding link in the verification area to open the "Alipay Verification" page. In the "Bank Account Verification" area, click "Confirm Transfer Amount," then input the amount of funds injected into your bank account by Alipay, and click the "Confirm" button.

If the amount matches correctly, it means you have completed the entire identity verification process on Taobao.

Second, Post Items

At this point, you can use Taobao Assistant to upload the shoe data I provided to your own Taobao store. (There is a detailed tutorial in my log for uploading data to the Taobao store.)

Friendly Tip: If you haven't passed personal real-name verification and Alipay verification, you can post items, but the posted items will only appear in "Items in Warehouse," which buyers cannot see. Only after passing verification can they be listed for sale.

After posting the items, it's best to update and add them regularly to prevent the store from being deleted by the system.

Third, Shop Decoration is Important

After opening a free store, buyers can get their own space. Like traditional shops, to operate normally and attract customers, it's necessary to decorate the shop appropriately, mainly including designing the shop logo, categorizing items, recommending items, and the shop style, etc. (If you need material templates or videos, feel free to ask me.)

1. Basic Settings

Log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao---I am a Seller---Manage My Shop." In the left-hand "Shop Management," click "Basic Settings," and in the opened page, you can modify the shop name, shop category, shop introduction; main business projects need to be manually entered; in the "Shop Logo" area, click the "Browse" button to select the already designed shop logo image; in the "Announcement" area, input the shop announcement content, such as "Welcome to our store!" Click the "Preview" button to check the effect.

2. Item Categorization

Categorizing items facilitates buyers' search. In the opened "Manage My Shop" page, you can click "Item Classification" on the left side; then, input the new classification name, such as "Converse," and enter the sorting number (indicating the arrangement position), click the "Confirm" button to add.

Click the "Item List" button corresponding to the classification, and through searching keywords, add the posted items for classification management.

3. Recommended Items

The "Recommended Items" feature provided by Taobao allows you to recommend your best 6 items, showcasing them prominently in the store. Simply open the "Manage My Shop" page, click "Recommended Items" on the left side; then, in the opened page, select the items to recommend, and click the "Recommend" button.

4. Shop Style

Different shop styles suit different items and give buyers different feelings. Generally, choosing a colorfully elegant and comfortable style is sufficient. The preview image is displayed on the right side, and clicking the "Confirm" button applies this style.

Friendly Tip: After successfully opening the store, if the number of items sold in your store is less than 10 for three consecutive weeks, you will receive a reminder email from Taobao asking you to replenish the item count; after another two weeks, if there are still fewer than 10 items, the store will be released; after another week, if the item count remains less than 10, the store will be deleted.

After decorating the shop, a brand-new page appears before you.

Fourth, Promotion is Key to Success

You've opened your online boutique, the items are up, and you've established your unique features, but several weeks have passed with no sales, not even any buyer comments. This is a common problem many novice sellers encounter. At this point, you need to take the initiative.

I once faced such a dilemma, so I decided to advertise my boutique through four methods: forum promotion, link exchange, window recommendation, and friend promotion.

1. Forum Promotion

The main method of forum promotion is through posting advertisements and utilizing signature lines.

The former can be done on forums in various provinces or major cities. If there are sections allowing ad posts, you can post ads there. The content must be detailed, the product images must be exquisite, and regular updates and pinning are essential.

The latter involves changing your forum signature line to include your boutique's URL, logo, slogan, and store name. Posting some attractive threads will allow interested friends to visit your boutique via your signature line.

2. Link Exchange

In the early stages of opening a store, to boost popularity, you can exchange links with popular stores, using free advertising to promote your boutique. For example, Taobao offers up to 35 friendly links, and adding them is simple:

First, use Taobao's search function to search all stores and record the names of popular store owners.

Then, log in to http://www.taobao.com/help/wangwang/wangwang.php to download the Taobao buyer and seller communication tool - "Taobao Wangwang," add these popular store owner names, and request link exchanges.

If they agree to exchange, finally, open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Manage My Shop," click "Friendly Links" on the left side, then input the store owner name, and click the "Add" button.

Friendly Tip: If you want to delete friendly links, you can check the store name at the bottom of the page and click the "Delete" button.

3. Window Recommendation

The "Window Recommendation" feature provided by Taobao is a special feature for sellers. When buyers search or click "I Want to Buy" according to categories, window-recommended items will appear on the search result pages. To set the "Window Recommendation" feature, you can open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Items for Sale," select the items to recommend in the window (items already recommended on the homepage of the store cannot be window-recommended again, i.e., those marked with "Recommend"), and click the "Window Recommendation" button.

Friendly Tip: Window recommendation promotes good items on Taobao, gaining more views and click-through rates, while store recommendations place items on the recommended spots on the store's homepage. The number of window recommendation slots is determined based on the number of your items, monthly credibility, and transaction volume, defaulting to 10.

Recommendation Tips: To increase the chances of your items being searched, you can regularly check Taobao's popular search keywords. If related to your item categories, such as "Korean Version" or "Weight Loss," you might consider changing your item titles to include popular keywords.

4. Friend Promotion

Once your online boutique is open, make sure to tell your friends and family. For instance, announce your store's opening on platforms like Alibaba, QQ, and MSN Messenger, and change your personal signature to include the store's URL. This way, word-of-mouth will spread, and more and more people will know about your store.

Fifth, During Item Sales

When buyers purchase goods, they generally inquire about information related to the product. They will ask questions online and wait for the seller's response before deciding whether to buy.

1. Check Buyer Comments

After setting up the store and running advertisements, open "Taobao Wangwang" and wait for buyer inquiries. If a buyer leaves a comment on an item in the store, "Taobao Wangwang" will automatically pop up a message notification window.

Alternatively, log in to Taobao, sequentially open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Buyer Comments/Reply," and check the comments left by buyers on the opened page. Click "Awaiting My Reply" to reply to the comments.

Item Selling Tips:

(1) Discount Strategy. Set different discount standards based on the quantity of purchases made by buyers to attract them.

(2) Promotion Strategy. Use holidays or store anniversaries to offer low-price promotions on items to increase the store's popularity.

(3) Auction Strategy. Use auctioned items to promote your store, such as adding links to other items in the auctioned items.

2. Check if the Item Has Been Sold

If the item has been sold, you can check through three ways:

One, log in to "Taobao Wangwang," and the system will notify you that a buyer has purchased the item.

Two, you can check if the item has been sold through "My Taobao" by logging into Taobao and sequentially opening "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Sold Items."

Three, log in to the email registered for Taobao, and Taobao will send relevant notification emails.

Sixth, After the Item is Sold

After the item is sold, in addition to receiving corresponding sale reminder information, you need to proactively contact the buyer, requesting them to pay for the goods, ship the item, and evaluate or complain after the transaction is completed.

1. Check Sold Items

If a buyer purchases an item, Taobao will notify the seller via "Taobao Wangwang" and email. The seller can also log in to Taobao, open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Sold Items." In the "Contact Buyer" section, click "Leave Me a Message" to leave a message for the buyer via "Taobao Wangwang"; if the buyer does not use "Taobao Wangwang," you can also note down the buyer's ID and send an internal message.

2. Contact Transaction Details

After both parties have communicated, they agree on payment and shipping methods. To ensure the interests of both buyers and sellers, it is recommended to choose Alipay as the payment method.

3. Payment and Shipping

To prevent situations where goods arrive but payments aren't made, sellers generally adopt a "payment received before shipping" approach when selling items.

First, require the buyer to make the payment, usually through Alipay. After the buyer pays, the seller can open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Sold Items" to check. If the transaction status shows "Buyer Paid, Waiting for Seller to Ship," it indicates that Alipay has received the payment, and at this point, the seller can confidently ship the item to the buyer.

Friendly Tip: Sellers using Alipay transactions can open "My Taobao_Alipay Zone_Transaction Management" to manage ongoing transactions, such as transaction queries and refund management.

After the buyer receives the seller's goods, they confirm it in the transaction status, and Taobao will then transfer the funds to the seller's Alipay account. Thus, the transaction is completed. Don't forget to stay in touch with the buyer, which increases the chances of them revisiting your boutique and purchasing items.

4. Evaluation and Complaints

After completing the transaction, both buyers and sellers can open "My Taobao" for evaluations. Sellers can open "My Taobao_I am a Seller_Sold Items," click "Evaluate" on the sold items, choose good, neutral, or bad reviews based on actual circumstances, and input text content, Guangzhou VIP Information Technology Co., Ltd.

Friendly Tip: How to become a high-credibility star store owner on Taobao?

To become a star store owner on Taobao, the credit score must be at least 4 points. The basis for the seller's credit score is the evaluation given by buyers after each successful Alipay transaction. If the evaluation is "Good," it adds one point; "Neutral" does not add points; "Bad" deducts one point. Therefore, to become a star store owner, remember to provide honest services. For specific star and credit score comparisons, refer to http://service.taobao.com/support/153-156-164/help-842.htm.

If there are situations such as online transactions without purchase or receipt of goods without payment, sellers can open "My Taobao_Credit Management_I Want to Report" to lodge complaints or reports, but evidence such as shipping receipts, buyer sign-off receipts, and Wangwang screenshots must be collected.

Finally, wish everyone a thriving business!
