Recently, I have been studying online earning projects to find one that suits me and can truly make money. I've visited many online earning forums and QQ groups to discuss and exchange ideas with others, but the results were quite disappointing. The projects they shared either don't pay or are pyramid schemes. To help newcomers avoid scams, I will list a few verified and common online earning projects for your reference! Typing Can Earn You Hundreds of Yuan Daily: This scam was popular in 2010 but was exposed at the beginning of 2011. However, typing jobs are starting to deceive people again. They claim that if you work hard, you can earn between 100 to 300 yuan daily. Some friends, thinking they type quickly, are very interested in these part-time typing jobs, hoping to use their skills to make some money. But let me tell you, it's all a trap. Why do I say this? Because there is now specialized Chinese character recognition software. By scanning the text of books that need typing and using this software, errors may occur but can be corrected easily. This greatly improves speed and efficiency while drastically reducing costs. Do you think online part-time typing jobs are still necessary? Paying for Online Earning Projects: If you frequently browse online earning forums or QQ groups, you will definitely see many "paid online earning projects." I advise you not to easily believe in these projects because they are either basic online earning information found everywhere online or outright scams. After all, if a project could make money, why wouldn't the creators do it themselves instead of selling it to you for a small profit? Generally, the fewer people doing an online earning project, the more profitable it is. If too many people are involved, it would surely affect their "profits." No one would be foolish enough to give up big profits for small ones! Of course, I'm not saying that all paid online earning projects are fake; some genuinely make money. However, compared to encountering scammers, the probability is extremely low, almost countable!

Chongqing Lottery Betting: Chongqing Lottery is currently the most popular online gambling method in China. This gambling method is mostly controlled by "illegal lottery" operators. What is "illegal lottery"? It refers to a gambling website set up by individual bookmakers. It differs from the official Chongqing Lottery website. The official method involves buying tickets in groups, which might not require much money, so the chances of winning aren't high, and even if you win, you won't receive much prize money. On the other hand, "illegal lotteries" offer a free betting mechanism with payouts as high as 19.5 times your bet. For example, spending 2 yuan on a number could win you 19.5 yuan. These rewards seem tempting and attract many "get-rich-quick" dreamers.

However, as the old saying goes, "Ten out of ten gamblers lose." Making money through gambling is basically impossible. Even if you're lucky and win every time, when it comes to withdrawing your winnings, you'll encounter problems. Most illegal lotteries involve manual payments. If you "win" too much, they won't pay you and will simply take your gambling funds without any recourse. So, wake up and don't get addicted to gambling. It's better to find a real job!

Earning Over 100 Yuan Daily by Leaving Your Computer Running: Whether domestically or internationally, leaving your computer running to earn money online usually doesn't make much. Some people might make less than ten yuan after a whole day. It's impossible to earn fifty or even hundreds of yuan daily just by leaving your computer on. Therefore, if you come across information claiming you can earn fifty or even a hundred yuan daily by doing this, you've likely encountered a scam. It's best to ignore such scams, as the result will only be wasted time and becoming a tool for others to make money! (This article is originally written by Cucumber Water)
