Yesterday afternoon, in front of the apartment where the incident occurred, there were still plenty of bloodstains. The female victim needs to stay in the hospital for observation.

The incident occurred at a rental apartment in Yongwei Fang, Wuyan Bridge, Liwan District. A nine-year-old boy is critically injured.

■ Written by Wang Lubin, New Express reporter; Photographed by Wang Fei, New Express reporter

After two divorces, 38-year-old Guangxi native Chen Haiyan lived with her third-grade son, renting an apartment at No. 103, Yongwei Fang, Wuyan Bridge, Liwan District. However, this peaceful life was suddenly disrupted. At around 1:40 AM yesterday, Chen Haiyan's second ex-husband Ahong (phonetic), wielding a woodcutting knife, first slashed her son inside the house and then chased after Chen Haiyan before fleeing. Neighbors speculate that the incident may have been caused by Ahong demanding money from Chen Haiyan after their divorce without success. Currently, both mother and son are hospitalized due to severe injuries and remain in critical condition.

Ahong calmly fled after slashing the mother and son.

Chen Haiyan's residence was a small side room in a rented apartment, only about ten square meters. Around 2 PM yesterday, when reporters from New Express arrived at the scene, the police tape at the entrance had not yet been removed. A large pool of blood at the door and a trail of blood stretching over 50 meters sent shivers down people’s spines. Talking about the incident, neighbors all exclaimed “terrifying.”

Li Auntie (a pseudonym), who lives in the same building as Chen Haiyan, told reporters that at around 1:40 AM yesterday, people were suddenly awakened by a woman screaming "Help!" After dressing, Li Auntie went out and discovered that something had happened at Chen Haiyan's home. Chen Haiyan's nine-year-old son, Jingjing, was lying on the ground covered in blood, crying loudly. Meanwhile, after running out of the house for more than 50 meters, Chen Haiyan collapsed on the ground, drenched in blood and unable to move.

"It turned out later that it was Chen Haiyan's second husband, Ahong, who slashed her and her son," said Li Auntie. Ahong used a woodcutting knife over a foot long. After slashing them, Ahong returned to the rental apartment to curse at Jingjing before fleeing. Subsequently, security personnel and police officers who responded to the alarm rushed the two to the hospital for emergency treatment.

Suspected demand for property led to violence

"She (Chen Haiyan) has no job and has only been renting here with her son for two or three months," said neighbor Ms. Wu. Chen Haiyan's son, Jingjing, was born from her first marriage. About two years ago, Chen Haiyan remarried Ahong, who was also divorced, and the three of them rented another apartment in Wuyan Bridge Village. However, Chen Haiyan and Ahong did not get along well, and neighbors frequently heard them arguing, leading to multiple police reports.

Ms. Wu said that during several conversations with Chen Haiyan, she learned that Ahong had physically abused her multiple times. Eventually, unable to endure the torment, Chen Haiyan divorced Ahong three months ago. After the divorce, Chen Haiyan moved with her son to the apartment where the incident occurred, but Ahong continued to visit every few days to argue.

"I've heard that Chen Haiyan owns a house in Nanhai, and Ahong wanted more property, which led to their arguments," said Ms. Wu. Therefore, many neighbors speculated that Ahong might have committed the crime due to his failure to obtain money, possibly out of anger leading him to act violently. Other neighbors familiar with Ahong mentioned that he had recently lost money betting on the lottery and was very short of cash. However, these claims have not been confirmed by the police.

Threatened to cut off her son's genitals

Yesterday afternoon, reporters met Chen Haiyan at the City Charity Hospital. Her entire head and left hand were wrapped in bandages, and she kept groaning in pain. According to medical staff, Chen Haiyan suffered multiple knife wounds on her head and face, as well as her left hand. Although she underwent surgery, her condition could worsen at any time. Meanwhile, Jingjing, in the ICU ward at the People's Hospital of Liwan District, remains in critical condition due to severe head injuries.

According to Chen Xingwen, Chen Haiyan's cousin, Ahong made a living driving trucks for others in Guangzhou. "My sister told me that Ahong had serious conflicts with his first wife and even chopped off her hand, resulting in a five-year prison sentence."

Chen Xingwen said that while conscious, Chen Haiyan told him that on the night of the incident, Ahong broke into the apartment and threw a pair of scissors at her, ordering her to cut off Jingjing's genitals. When she refused, Ahong grabbed the woodcutting knife and attacked them. "I haven't heard of Ahong having mental issues; it might be that something else triggered him to commit the crime," said Chen Xingwen. (Tipster: Mr. Yan, Reward: 150 yuan)

Had dinner with wife and child before committing the crime

New Express report by journalist Li Guohui: Regarding the knife attack that occurred yesterday in Wuyan Bridge, Fangcun, Liwan District, the reporter consulted relevant police personnel. They confirmed the case and stated that the police had dispatched elite forces to form a special investigation team to investigate and actively apprehend the fugitive suspect. As for the specific cause of the incident, the person indicated that further investigation is needed. It is known that the suspect was previously married to the injured woman, and they divorced just this September. The previous evening, the suspect had dinner with his ex-wife and child, and shortly afterward, the incident occurred, allowing the suspect to escape from the scene. Currently, both victims suffer from severe injuries and require intensive care treatment.

Currently, the police are actively hunting for the fugitive suspect.
