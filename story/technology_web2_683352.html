The Light of Kindness

In Zhejiang Province, Luo Xuehua has become synonymous with "selfless maternal love." At the beginning of 2006, Luo Xuehua was selected as one of the "Prides of Zhejiang - The Ten Most Influential People of the Year." In August 2007, a story film based on Luo Xuehua, titled "Mother," was released nationwide in major cinemas, moving audiences to tears.

This July, Luo Xuehua once again took her adopted daughter Xiaolu Lu to Guangzhou, planning for a thorough surgery. However, the success rate of the surgery is only about 60-70%. Luo Xuehua's kindness touched countless hearts.

High costs, misunderstanding from relatives and friends, coupled with immense mental pressure, often prevent Luo Xuehua from sleeping soundly throughout the night. Doctors once advised her to give up, saying that continuing the treatment would ultimately result in losing both life and fortune. But Luo Xuehua couldn't let go; for Xiaolu Lu, she would do her utmost.
