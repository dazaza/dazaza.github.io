#### Video Editing Software Reviews - Features to Consider - Top 3 Video Editing Software Programs

Have some fun the high-tech way with video editing software! Video editing software is a personal software program designed to make editing your movies as enjoyable as shooting them. The best video editing software is user-friendly for capturing, authoring, and burning.

Capture your life on film. Create your own action movies, capture your funniest moments, or preserve your most special memories with a solid video editing software program.

### What Can You Do With Video Editing Software?

1. **Capture Your Videos**

- Upload video files using popular formats such as Windows Media Player, Real Video, Quicktime, and more.

- The top-tier video editing software can capture video from HDV, DV, analog camcorders, microMV, and even PC and digital cameras.

- Capture video from traditional entertainment formats like TV or VCR recordings with the appropriate hardware.

2. **Edit Your Videos**

- Mix, combine, or rearrange videos using timeline editing. Drag and drop video clips wherever you need them.

- Add text, menus, and titles to your videos. Many programs come with a variety of menu templates, title templates, and text effects.

- Add narration, sound effects, and music. Have fun editing your videos by creating soundtracked wedding or birthday videos. Show off your humorous clips by adding comedic narration and sound effects.

3. **Share Your Videos**

- Create an online video album. Check out sites like StashSpace, MyPhotoAlbum, or Drop Shots, which offer family-friendly secure accounts. This creates a private online space where everyone can share your videos with friends and family.

- Post your videos online for the world to see. Share them on your own video blog (vblog), Google Video, YouTube, Grouper, or any other video-sharing site.

- Design video gifts and burn them to DVDs. Create marketing presentations or archive your collection onto Blu-ray discs.

### What to Look For in Video Editing Software

When shopping for video editing software, decide how you want to use it. Programs range from very simple to more complex. A simple program might only burn DVDs or convert formats, while others offer thorough editing tools such as special effects, complicated templates, and sophisticated timeline edits.

Video editing can put a strain on your computer, so you may need to upgrade your system. Ensure your computer has adequate memory to run the software effectively.

#### Key Features to Value in Video Editing Software:

1. **Easy to Operate**

Editing can involve several steps from start to finish. Test the software to ensure it's easy for you to operate.

2. **Broad Range of Features**

Look for enough features to complete the tasks you need. However, more features may mean a more complex program to operate.

3. **Very Easy Installation**

The installation process should be simple and have as few steps as possible, especially for beginners.

4. **Technical Support**

Ensure there is someone available to respond to emails or answer phone calls. You will likely have questions, and you want the company to provide support.

#### List of Essential Features:

- Ability to capture from different sources such as HDV/DV, Audio CD, Camcorder, VHS, TV, Hi8, and 8mm.

- Ability to capture and import video and adjust size and time limits.

- Different editing views such as timeline and storyboard.

- Ability to split, trim, add text, insert audio tracks, zoom in/out, loop playback, and divide scenes.

- Various effects like special effect filters, titles, scrolling credits, and text animation.

- Slow-motion effects and the ability to change contrast.

- Useful photo editing tools.

- Audio editing features such as trim/fade, mute, add, mixing, and crossfade.
