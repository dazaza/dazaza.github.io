---

**PART VII: Relationships between Venus/Mars and Other Planets in the Comparative Chart**

---

**★ Venus Section:**

---

**※ Venus and Mars:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Mars stimulates true love and long-lasting romance, while a challenging aspect incites lust and infidelity. If it's a difficult aspect, it's best to channel this energy into financial pursuits (refer to point 4 above).

---

**※ Venus and Jupiter:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Jupiter brings great wealth and abundance, whereas a challenging aspect leads to indulgence and extravagance. Venus represents wealth and resources, while Jupiter brings luck and relationships.

---

**※ Venus and Saturn:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Saturn ensures long-term financial and home security, while a challenging aspect creates prolonged instability and insecurity. Venus embodies virtue, and Saturn knows how to sustain it. Ideally, both Venus and Saturn should share the same element.

---

**※ Venus and Uranus:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Uranus inspires mutual creativity, while a challenging aspect causes aversion and financial instability. Venus represents cash flow, and Uranus brings innovation. Ideally, Venus and Uranus should have different natures so as not to irritate each other.

---

**※ Venus and Neptune:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Neptune fosters romance and imagination, while a challenging aspect creates emotional discord. Venus provides candlelight and dinner, and Neptune creates atmosphere and poetry. Ideally, both Venus and Neptune should share the same nature.

---

**※ Venus and Pluto:**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and Pluto brings perfect sexual chemistry, while a challenging aspect leads to degrading or obsessive love. Venus governs the body, and Pluto represents desire. Ideally, both Venus and Pluto should share the same nature.

---

**※ Venus and Midheaven (MC):**

A harmonious aspect between Venus and the Midheaven earns public recognition for your beauty and talents, while a challenging aspect results in poor reputation or incorrect evaluation of your status.

---

**★ Mars Section:**

---

**※ Mars and Jupiter:**

A harmonious aspect between Mars and Jupiter stimulates action and overall growth, while a challenging aspect leads to exaggeration and unfulfilled promises. Mars brings energy, and Jupiter brings vision. Ideally, both Mars and Jupiter should share the same element.

---

**※ Mars and Saturn:**

There is no truly harmonious aspect between Mars and Saturn. Mars is the accelerator, and Saturn is the brake. In the best scenario, Mars can provide what Saturn needs, and Saturn can make Mars think twice before acting. In the worst case, Mars' impatience constantly irritates Saturn, and Saturn's heaviness suppresses Mars' drive. Ideally, Mars and Saturn should have different elements so they can "ignore" each other.

---

**※ Mars and Uranus:**

A harmonious aspect between Mars and Uranus adds excitement and vitality to a relationship, while a challenging aspect leads to relationship breakdowns. Mars represents energy, and Uranus represents the spark that ignites (or extinguishes) explosives. Ideally, both Mars and Uranus should share the same nature.

---

**※ Mars and Neptune:**

There is no truly harmonious aspect between Mars and Neptune. In the best scenario, Mars can ignite Neptune's imagination, and Neptune can calm Mars' anger. In the worst case, Mars stifles Neptune's sensitivity, and Neptune drains Mars' energy. Both can lead to dangerous and strange situations. Ideally, Mars and Neptune should have different natures.

---

**※ Mars and Pluto:**

There is no truly harmonious aspect between Mars and Pluto. In the best scenario, Mars teaches Pluto to be honest, and Pluto teaches Mars strategy. In the worst case, Mars accelerates Pluto's destructive power, and Pluto undermines Mars' will to survive. Ideally, Mars and Pluto should have different natures.

---

**※ Mars and Midheaven (MC):**

A harmonious aspect between Mars and the Midheaven allows one partner to achieve personal success through public admiration. A challenging aspect negatively affects one's achievements.
