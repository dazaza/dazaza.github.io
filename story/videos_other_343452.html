Healthy Babies, Care Points

Feeding Begins to Have a Pattern

The baby's sucking becomes stronger and the action more proficient. The amount sucked each time is also increasing. Originally feeding without distinction between day and night, now it gradually has a pattern. Most babies start to feed every 3 hours. If your baby still does not have a pattern, don't worry, there are individual differences.

Reminder: Do not forcibly cultivate the baby's feeding pattern. You should slowly guide according to his own habits.

Magazine Tip:

For you and your baby, feeding (including artificial feeding) should be a process of relaxation, comfort, and joy. It is an opportunity for adults to express love and for parent-child mutual understanding. You are calm and confident, and the baby's response is also peaceful. You are tense and in low spirits, and the baby will also be influenced by these negative emotions, thereby causing feeding problems.

Sleep and Awakening

Last month, the baby was just feeding and sleeping, constantly repeating. Entering this month, the time the baby is awake gradually becomes longer, usually lasting about 30 minutes. Some babies may experience "day-night reversal" where they sleep during the day and cry unwillingly at night.

Reminder: During the day, when the baby is awake, consciously play with him and gradually increase the waking time. This way, the baby will sleep soundly at night. In this manner, gradually cultivate a living pattern.

Bathing

The navel has completely healed, and you can directly put the baby into the bathtub for a bath. Avoid bathing the baby when they are hungry or within 30 minutes after feeding.

Reminder: Bathing is a good opportunity for busy dads who work to get close to the baby. On rest days or when coming home early, dad might as well enjoy the fun of bathing the baby.

Clothing and Attire

Due to active metabolism, the baby often sweats. Therefore, besides bathing daily, you need to frequently change the baby's underwear. During this period, the baby should wear one more piece of clothing than adults, but don't overdress them.

Reminder: Frequently place your hand on the back of the baby's neck to check. If they are sweating, you should take off some clothes from the baby. Clothes that are wet with sweat can easily make the baby catch a cold.

Playing and Having Fun

For colorful and moving objects within their field of vision, the baby always looks at them with great interest. When you shake a bell or play an electric music box, he will stop crying, become excited, focus intently on listening, or look around with his eyes. This indicates that the baby begins to notice sounds and moving objects.

Reminder: At a fixed time every day, play and have fun with the baby so that the baby feels family affection and learns about things.

Magazine Recommendation

Toys and Games Suitable for Babies Aged 1-3 Months

* Pictures with obvious pattern contrast.

* Colorful toys with various shapes.

* Unbreakable mirrors hung on the crib.

* Bells.

* Toys that produce pleasant sounds, bright colors, and patterns.

* Singing songs personally for the baby.

* Listening to various music and dancing with the baby in rhythm with the music.
