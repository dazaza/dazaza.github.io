Li Yuchun's performance with an umbrella caused anger and was hit by mineral water bottles from the audience (Pictures)

by ed528960 on 2009-07-28 14:31:50

Last night, the Second "Green Flag" Grassland Music Festival came to a perfect close in a heavy downpour. However, during that night's performance, Li Yuchun, who was originally highly anticipated, not only failed to ignite the enthusiasm of the fans but also had water bottles and other objects thrown at her by the audience because she performed with an umbrella. In response to this situation, Li Yuchun could only hastily leave the stage after the performance. Later, a reporter learned from an informed source that Li Yuchun was the one with the highest appearance fee among all the performers, "The performance fee was 380,000 yuan, plus various taxes, totaling less than 600,000 yuan."

Before the performance started that night, it had already started raining heavily. However, the number of audiences did not decrease; instead, they increased, all because of the two major highlights of the evening - Cui Jian and Li Yuchun. Before the performance began, there were already hundreds of "corns" (Li Yuchun's fans) gathered under the comprehensive stage, wearing yellow T-shirts. They were waving a large yellow flag with the three words "Li Yuchun" written on it, constantly shouting their idol's name. It wasn't until around 9:30 pm that Li Yuchun finally made her much-anticipated appearance. As the music for "Young China" played, Li Yuchun was the first to rush onto the stage, while her four backup dancers were still on one side of the stage. Perhaps because it was raining heavily at the time, immediately after Li Yuchun appeared, a lady who looked like an assistant walked up to her with an umbrella to shield her from the rain. Subsequently, when the four backup dancers went on stage, they didn't perform any energetic dances but only moved their arms slightly according to the rhythm.

Apparently, Li Yuchun's action of holding an umbrella upset the audience on-site, and jeering began below the stage. At first, there were still some "corns" loudly shouting Li Yuchun's name to support their idol. However, as the four backup dancers also started holding umbrellas, the anger of the audience below the stage finally erupted. People began throwing various water bottles filled with sand or rainwater towards Li Yuchun. Without bottles, some threw glow sticks, toys, umbrellas, and even toilet paper was thrown onto the stage. Several times, these items almost hit Li Yuchun in the center of the stage. Despite this, she continued to perform without dodging - although this also caused trouble for the four backup dancers and the assistant beside Li Yuchun. It was seen that they all used the umbrellas as shields, surrounding Li Yuchun tightly, making the scene look rather comical. Seeing this situation, after finishing the remaining two songs, "WHY ME" and "With You", Li Yuchun quickly left the stage.
