Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Professional School

by xie11 on 2009-02-09 16:45:29

The school adopts a combination of work and study, with the best practical training facilities in the province, a simple, fast and accurate teaching mode, fully closed quasi-military student management, perfect employment placement, and the lowest tuition fee in the whole province.

Shijiazhuang Oriental Science and Technology Secondary Vocational School is a full-time comprehensive secondary vocational school approved by the Shijiazhuang Education Bureau and registered with the Hebei Provincial Department of Education. The school is located at No. 29, Shihuo South Road, Shijiazhuang City, Hebei Province, at the intersection of Xinhua Road and West Second Ring Road, with superior geographical conditions, convenient transportation, and beautiful environment.

Our school has always adhered to the development concept of "emancipating mind, being pragmatic and innovative, thinking hard and seeking change", continuously carried out teaching and management reforms, firmly established the awareness of "market competition", taken the market as the guide, and taken the enterprise needs as the standard. It explores new ideas and methods for theoretical and practical teaching, accumulates rich teaching and management experience, and students have received widespread praise from employers after employment.

First, with enterprise standards as the guide, it is equipped with first-class teaching facilities in the whole province.

Sufficient and advanced teaching experimental equipment is the basis and guarantee for doing a good job in vocational education. Our school has cumulatively invested more than 10 million yuan, and built a numerical control training workshop equipped with more than 50 numerical control lathes, numerical control milling machines, ordinary lathes, ordinary milling machines and other equipment, as well as an ordinary lathe training workshop. The school has also built communication electronic laboratories, music rooms, electronic reading rooms, grinding rooms, body shaping rooms, and new teaching buildings and other supporting facilities.

Second, with employment positions as the guide, it creates an enterprise-like teaching model.

Establishing a teaching mode mainly based on practice, during the teaching process, all students are managed as enterprise employees. Through competitive job placements, students hold positions such as team leaders, line managers, and employees in their "work". And through regular evaluations and examination results, excellent team leaders and technical experts are selected among the students. This effectively promotes the enthusiasm and initiative of students in learning.

Each day's "work" situation of the students is monitored by quality indicators, which are statistically analyzed each month. According to the scores obtained each month, wages are calculated, consisting of: workload + performance evaluation + work discipline + appearance + safe operation + bonus, etc. This effectively motivates the students' initiative to learn and competitive awareness, enabling them to master the knowledge and skills they have learned faster and better.

Through enterprise-like teaching reform, students can gradually experience and adapt to the enterprise management model while at school, adapting to the needs of employment positions and initially reaching the enterprise employment standards. The goal of enterprise-like teaching is to gradually surpass the enterprise employment standards.

Third, with enterprise demand as the guide, it creates a fully enclosed quasi-military management system for students and formulates a daily standard and practical manual for students' occupational quality training.

Fully learning from the enterprise's employee management model, cultivating qualified talents that enterprises like. Enterprises need not only skilled workers with strong technical skills but also pay more attention to employees' overall qualities, such as team spirit, obedience, execution, competitive awareness, etc. The cultivation and improvement of overall quality require efforts and cooperation from all aspects of the school, and overall quality education runs through the entire learning process of the students. To enhance the overall quality of our students, our school focuses on two distinctive features in management:

Feature Management One: Strict Quasi-Military Management

Our school adheres to the educational concept of "becoming a responsible adult before becoming talented," and hires retired military officers to specialize in quasi-military management of students. Starting from the daily life system, starting from dressing and behavior, speech and manners, etiquette and politeness, tidying up internal affairs, let students develop good living habits, cultivate students' excellent organizational discipline style, and make them quickly become disciplined talents with good military demeanor.

Feature Management Two: Vocational Quality Education Management

During the students' learning process, vocational quality courses are offered, divided into theoretical lectures and expansion practices. These two aspects complement each other and improve each other. The theoretical lecture on vocational quality mainly focuses on practical combat, doing ideological transformation work for students, and the expansion training consolidates the teaching results by adopting "personal experiences" of the students, thus making these vocational quality requirements become the students' conscious behaviors.

Vocational Quality Expansion Training, our school signs agreements with enterprises and sends our students in batches to enterprise positions under the accompaniment of school leaders and teachers. After a period of quasi-employee life, students can understand society and enterprises before truly interning and working, entering the formal enterprise employee state in advance, laying a good foundation for adapting to employment positions.

Fourth, with employment as the guide, properly completing employment placement work.

With the continuous improvement of our students' quality and employment channels, internship and employment bases have been widely established in the Pearl River Delta region, Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei-Shanxi areas, and the Jiaozhou Peninsula.

The employment direction of our school's numerical control major is mainly in the automotive parts manufacturing industry. All student internship units are world-top 500 mechanical manufacturing enterprises. The professional alignment rate of student internships and employment reaches 90%, and the student employment satisfaction rate exceeds 95%.

Fifth, considerate后勤services, uniquely creating a "free meal system" for students in the whole province.

All students in our school implement the "free meal system" for three meals a day. On one hand, it reduces the burden on students' parents, and on the other hand, the school formulates scientific menus according to the physical development needs of students, fully ensuring the nutritional needs of students' physical development and adapting to intensive learning, effectively avoiding adverse consequences caused by students dining outside the school.

The school's student dormitory building, canteen, supermarket, phone bar, bathroom and other service facilities are complete. The logistics management department always adheres to the principle of "service", striving to be proactive, considerate, intimate, and convenient in service.

Sixth, only miscellaneous fees are charged. The source of tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years of students.

Our school is a three-year secondary vocational school. With a strong sense of social responsibility originating from and returning to society, relying on powerful educational strength and forward-looking educational concepts, we aim to save costs as much as possible for students' parents. That is, students coming to our school only need to pay miscellaneous fees for three years, and the tuition fees and accommodation fees for three years will be paid through a combination of work and study, namely occupational quality training. The school has also specially established a "Hongzhi Class", fully or partially exempting all miscellaneous fees for academically excellent and highly competent students from humble backgrounds. Our students enjoy a national scholarship of 1500 yuan per year provided by the government.

Seventh, educating people with advanced school culture and shaping a strong brand.

In the process of running the school, we constantly summarize experiences and lessons, and innovatively refine our school culture.

Our philosophy (educational philosophy): Leading enterprise employment standards

Our responsibility (assuming responsibility): Creating a development environment for Hebei vocational education

Our spirit (our team): Consensus, Learning, Collaboration, Innovation, Inclusiveness

Our culture (core culture): Building blue-collar elites

Our vision (development goals): Becoming the top brand of China's vocational education

Our mission (achieving missions): Laying the foundation for corporate wealth.

The refinement of "school culture" outlines the grand blueprint and direction of struggle for our school. The process of practicing school culture is also the process of shaping our brand and further enhancing our influence.

Small victories rely on intelligence, great victories rely on virtue; small successes rely on friends, great successes rely on opponents. We wish to co-develop with all vocational education schools in Hebei and provide more and better learning environments and employment opportunities for young people with aspirations.
