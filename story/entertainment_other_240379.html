Stairs

Climbing stairs is a process, and you climb them to get to another floor or space. Unless there's a power outage or for health reasons, most people wouldn't choose such a breathless way when an elevator is available. Compared to the effortless ride of an elevator, every step up and down in the stairwell has to be walked out by yourself. Climbing stairs in a dream is much like the projection of an ambitious mindset, while walking down the stairs may reflect dissatisfaction with one’s own less smooth life. Although people aim to climb higher, you might find yourself drenched in sweat as you reach the end of your dream, only to realize the truth of "the higher you go, the colder it gets" after a breeze hits you; as you climb the stairs and admire the scenery around you, it might make you realize that the journey is more important than the result; sometimes, the stairs in your dreams can even extend infinitely, leaving you exhausted without ever seeing the destination. At this point, you might want to consider whether the destination at the top is really worth all the effort? Therefore, dreaming of descending stairs isn't necessarily a bad thing. Sometimes, as you walk down the stairs in your dream, you encounter an old friend and chat enthusiastically in the stairwell. The dream might be saying that by letting go of some things, you'll rediscover more worthy memories; if you dream of being pulled down the stairs by an invisible force and discover a beautiful scene at the bottom, it naturally becomes the best illustration of “a step back reveals a broader horizon”! Stairs have both ups and downs, "going up is delightful, going down is also joyful," though this saying may sound clichéd, as long as you're careful not to sprain your ankle, there are always more stairs waiting for you to go up and down!
