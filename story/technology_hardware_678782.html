This is a course warning from Teacher Yang of the internal training class at Beijing Outward Bound Training Company regarding employees in the workplace. I think it's worth everyone's attention and learning, which will greatly benefit us. We are busy working every day, and it's inevitable to make mistakes during work. At this time, never hide your mistakes. You must immediately admit them to your leader with a plan to remedy the error. Explain the plan clearly to your leader, clarify when the plan will take effect, and whether additional costs are needed. Having corrected the mistake is good, but the mental state and style at work should also draw our attention. A good working style is a sign of our success. Work tirelessly. Channel your energy into your profession in a new way; An extraordinary working style comes from prioritizing tasks. Don't be attracted by trivial matters and neglect important things, otherwise, time, energy, and money will be wasted. To achieve your goal, you need to be good at sorting out the threads, clearing obstacles, and distinguishing priorities. An extraordinary working style comes from focus. Doing things half-heartedly can consume more than 75% of one's energy. Many people don't know how to clear distracting thoughts because they've never thought about changing their way of thinking. Look at the achievements of those around you that you admire! See what you can learn from them? Consult with them, seek ways to focus, and concentrate on what truly matters. Firm belief. An extraordinary working style stems from confidence. Confidence directly determines success or failure. Motivated people have clear goals and will try every means to achieve them. Take immediate action. An extraordinary working style stems from action. There's an old saying we're all familiar with: "A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step." Even the smallest matter requires personal action. Start taking action from today, stop making excuses like waiting for better weather or doing it next week, etc., these bad habits that delay time. Start from today, strive to realize your dreams, this is the best time. Only in this way can we create a bright future. We will repeatedly encounter many problems at work, believing that these problems will eventually be resolved is the key to creating a better life. Whether new or old employees, in the workplace, we should all establish a good image of being serious about work, responsible, and mutually supportive. Also, set short-term and long-term work goals for yourself. With goals, we have direction to strive for, form a competitive awareness, and have more motivation to fight. The realization of work goals precisely reflects our value, demonstrates our work ability, realizes self-worth in life, and fulfills our dreams! For employees in the workplace, it is necessary to promptly clarify your development direction, set different work goals for different periods, learn good working styles, and make plans to realize your dreams.
