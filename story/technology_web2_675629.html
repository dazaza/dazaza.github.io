Taxi driver killed a passenger who refused to pay by running him over

by xue94fwsh on 2012-02-25 11:05:19

After hitting the person, Lishuguo said he was very afraid and went home without stopping to check. After dawn, he sent his car to an automobile repair department under Caihuying Bridge in Fengtai District, claiming that the car had been hit by a tree and asked them to fix it as soon as possible so as not to delay the next shift. After receiving the alarm, the police of Dongcheng Traffic Detachment rushed to the scene and determined the license plate number of the hit-and-run taxi by retrieving the driving trajectory video of the hit-and-run vehicle and relevant records, combining with the statements of witnesses and injured people. They summoned Lishuguo who was suspected of major crime. At first, Lishuguo did not admit that he had hit someone and said that the traffic accident was caused because he didn't see the other side at that time and was dodging weasels. It was not until he was detained that he admitted his crime.

According to what Lishuguo said, after the two passengers got out of the car, he prepared to turn around and continue to pick up passengers. After turning around, he found that the two were still about 10 meters ahead. He felt angry because he thought he was bullied, so he wanted to scratch them with the right side mirror of the car. Then he drove at a speed of about 20 kilometers per hour." I just want to scare them," but Tian fell on the machine cover after being hit, and his head broke the windshield and died on the spot. A young man delayed paying for the goods and reported a false emergency call saying he was robbed of 1.5 million yuan. An old woman who lived alone was supported by a young man for 17 years. After being released, he admitted his crime.

Lishuguo, 51 years old, is a local of the city with high school education. Before the incident, he was a driver of Beifang Chuangye Taxi Company. The prosecution charged that at 3 o'clock on the morning of August 18 last year, Lishuguo drove a taxi carrying two male passengers Tian and Pan. When the car arrived at the south side of the east gate of No. 11 Middle School on Jin Yu Chi West Street in Dongcheng District, both sides had a conflict over the fare. Because Lishuguo was dissatisfied with Tian's words and deeds, he turned the car around and ran over Tian and Pan standing by the roadside ready to leave, causing Tian, 30 years old, to die of cerebral trauma and Pan to be slightly injured. Lishuguo fled the scene after committing the crime.

Lishuguo said that the man on the back seat did not speak, while the man on the passenger seat kept being rude. He thought they should have turned left, but the passenger let him go straight and said he could not distinguish between left and right. "I kept quiet and followed their route". When arriving at the south side of the east gate of No. 11 Middle School on Jin Yu Chi West Street, the passenger asked him to stop. At that time, the meter showed an amount of 20 yuan. "I said there was 2 yuan fuel fee, so I asked for 22 yuan. He said 20 yuan would do? I said yes. Then he said whether not giving money would do? Considering they were drunk, I said yes." Lishuguo said that the man on the passenger seat still did not give up and said his meter was inaccurate. "Then I said it was quite accurate. He grabbed my collar and hit my chest, then got out of the car and gave me several punches on the left."

The prosecutor believed that Lishuguo deliberately hurt others' bodies, causing one death and one slight injury,涉嫌 intentional injury罪. In this regard, although Lishuguo admitted that the charge was true, he claimed that he did not intentionally hit people. According to what Lishuguo said, on the night of the incident, he picked up two men at Renjie East Entrance in Xicheng District. One sat on the passenger seat and the other on the back seat. They spoke with a foreign accent and said they were going to Liu Laogen Big Stage in Dongcheng District. "It was obvious that they had drunk a lot. Not long after getting into the car, they took off their shoes, making the car full of stinky smell", Lishuguo said that he then opened the window. Unexpectedly, the man on the passenger seat loudly asked him: "Do you feel it smelly?" He quickly said no, just wanting to cool down.

The injured Pan said that Tian was his brother-in-law, working in Beijing. He originally farmed in his hometown and came to Beijing that day to work. Both of them had drunk wine at home and then went to the bar, but neither of them drank too much. After massaging at the bath center that night, the two of them took a taxi. After getting in the car, he fell asleep on the back seat while Tian sat on the passenger seat. When getting out of the car, the driver asked for 22 yuan, but Tian said it would not cost 20 yuan and got out of the car and pulled the driver's collar on the left side. Then the driver said that it was unnecessary to pay money, so Tian let go. Afterwards, they stood by the roadside. After the driver drove away more than ten meters, he turned the car around and hit Tian, then hit him. When he woke up, he was already in the hospital, with his back and head injured.

Yesterday, the family of the deceased Tian attended the court to claim 920,000 yuan from Lishuguo, and the injured Pan claimed a total of more than 5,800 yuan for medical expenses and loss of income. Lishuguo said that he would try his best to compensate and hoped to be dealt with leniently. This case was not announced in court.

In court, he defended himself by saying that he only wanted to scrape and scare the other party.

The taxi driver got out of the car and was forced to exempt the fare by having his collar pulled.

Reporters (Peng Xiaolan) Two passengers did not pay for the taxi ride in the middle of the night and pulled the driver's collar. After the passengers got out of the car, the taxi driver Li Shuguo stepped on the gas pedal and rushed forward, hit-and-ran, causing one dead and one injured. Yesterday afternoon, Li Shuguo was tried in the Second Intermediate People's Court of the city on suspicion of intentional injury. He defended himself by saying that he only wanted to use the rearview mirror to scare the other party, but did not expect to cause a tragedy.

However, the public prosecutor did not agree with Li Shuguo's defense. He pointed out that Li Shuguo had more than 15 years of driving experience and had been a taxi driver for 3 years. As an experienced driver, he realized that running over people with a car would cause serious consequences. In addition, as a professional taxi driver, after a dispute with passengers, he should have tolerated and understood or resolved the conflict through legal means. "He drove a small bus weighing about 1.5 tons to hit a person weighing dozens of kilograms, which directly reflected that he did not consider the consequences when implementing retaliatory behavior."
