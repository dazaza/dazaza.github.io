Leon Lai held four consecutive nights of the "Dream Wedding Leon Live Summer 09" concerts, which took place from July 16 to 19, 2009 at the Hong Kong Coliseum. The concert featured a three-sided stage design with a long screen and a white staircase in the center of the stage, showcasing a simple yet elegant style. Leon appeared in a black suit adorned with a large flower and a red hat, accompanied by eight dancers, performing fast-paced songs like "100 Possibilities" and "Fire Dance under the Sun." Produced by Mark Lui, the concert also featured musicians such as Daniel Tang. During the night, Leon sang more than twenty classic fast and slow songs and even performed covers of other artists' works, including "Small Stories in a Big City" originally by Miriam Yeung, "Star" by Deanie Ip, and "Heart in Chaos" by Jeraldine Chan. One segment revisited Leon's theme songs from his films and TV series; when he sang "Love is Being Foolish," "Two People's Fireworks," and "Timely Embrace," clips of his past performances were shown on the screen.

On the night of the concert, new signee Janice Man joined as a special guest. Dressed in a red cheongsam, she not only danced alongside Leon but also performed her first new song "Man." To align with the "Dream Wedding" theme of the concert, Leon, dressed as a groom, danced with a dancer in a wedding dress during the encore session. Under the backdrop of a church projected on a large screen, he sang love songs like "Just The Way You Are" and "I Love You This Way," captivating the audience.

The "Dream Wedding Leon Live Summer 09" concert DVD was filmed in high definition, with no plans for a CD or VCD release.
