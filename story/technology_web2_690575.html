Efficient Application of 3G Smart Home Terminals

Smart home is based on the automation and intelligence of family products, achieving human-like requirements through networking. It can be defined as a process or system that uses advanced computer technology, network communication technology, and integrated wiring technology to organically combine various subsystems related to home life. Compared with ordinary homes, it transforms from a passive static structure into an active intelligent tool, providing comprehensive information exchange functions and helping families maintain smooth information exchanges with the outside world. With the improvement of the world economy and scientific and technological levels, people have put forward higher requirements for home modernization and the comfort and safety of living environments, and smart homes have emerged under such circumstances. In some areas of China, especially in coastal developed regions, smart homes are gradually entering people's lives.

3G communication was introduced onto the agenda after the first generation of analog technology mobile communication services were introduced into the mobile communication market, following the vigorous development of the second-generation digital mobile communication market. Amidst the continuous rise of Internet data services today, and with fixed access rates (HDSL, ADSL, VDSL) constantly increasing, the 3G mobile communication system also sees the dawn of the market, increasingly drawing the attention of telecommunications operators, communication equipment manufacturers, and ordinary users.

As we entered 2004, China's three major mobile network operators began accelerating the popularization of 3G. 3G refers to cellular mobile communication technology that supports high-speed data transmission. 3G services can simultaneously transmit voice and data information, generally at speeds of hundreds of kbps or more. It can handle multiple media formats such as images, music, and video streams, providing various information services including web browsing, teleconferencing, and e-commerce. To provide this service, the wireless network must support different data transmission speeds, capable of supporting at least 2 Mbps indoors, 384 kbps outdoors, and 144 kbps while driving.

The 3G entrance unit and indoor unit (smart home terminal) rely on the advantages of the current latest 3G network, combining the characteristics of the home intelligent gateway system to achieve functions such as 3G video intercom, household-to-household calls, central calls, outdoor monitoring, home control, security monitoring, child supervision, information sending and receiving, image storage, multimedia, and web browsing. Compared with traditional access control systems, the 3G access control system has many advantages. Choosing the 3G access control system eliminates the need for wiring construction, saving costs and not affecting the aesthetic beauty of indoor and outdoor decoration. Especially, the 3G signal is stable and has strong anti-interference capabilities, avoiding the shortcomings of wired or wireless access control systems that are prone to damage, susceptible to interference during transmission, and slow processing speed.

Visitors can call the indoor unit at home through the 3G entrance unit, and the owner can see the visitor's image on the screen of the indoor unit and talk directly with them. If no one is at home, the indoor unit will send the real-time image of the visitor to the homeowner's mobile phone via the 3G network, and the homeowner can also communicate with the visitor through their mobile phone, confirming their identity before opening the door for them. At the same time, the indoor unit can also call other homeowners in the same area or the management center, communicating in real-time through video and voice, realizing zero-distance communication between residents and between residents and the management center!

The 3G indoor unit uses wireless RF technology and infrared forwarding technology to transmit infrared control signals through high-frequency wireless RF communication technology. Users only need to operate on the touch screen of the 3G indoor unit to control all IR-controlled appliances at home; the 3G indoor unit can also independently control the lights and curtains in each room wirelessly, or group the curtains and lights into daily scenarios for associated control through scene modes.

When the homeowner is out, they can connect to the 3G indoor unit at home using a mobile client, and after identity verification, they can control the lights or appliances at home, while also being able to view the surveillance footage of the home and surrounding villa areas through their mobile phone, and freely switch any camera monitoring point. If there is a gas leak, burglary, or sudden illness of the elderly at home, the anti-theft alarm detection accessories at home can send the above alert information to the homeowner's mobile phone via SMS or MMS in the first instance.

With the acceleration of the pace of life and environmental pollution, various chronic diseases continue to increase, and the trend of population aging becomes increasingly severe. People's demand for health management is no longer limited to traditional medical institutions; people increasingly hope to obtain comprehensive medical and health support in the home and daily living environment. Through the 3G indoor unit as an information terminal, a health information platform can be established with medical institutions and government health departments to achieve remote medical monitoring. Using the indoor unit to collect data from various medical devices and transmitting the remote physiological and medical signals to medical institutions for analysis and diagnosis through the communication network, people can realize the remote transmission and monitoring of medical information between individuals and hospitals, between hospitals and hospitals, remote consultations, medical emergency care, remote medical education and exchanges, etc.
