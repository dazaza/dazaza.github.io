Woman spends 230,000 yuan on weight loss but gains weight instead, as Meisuguan is suspected of forcing consumption

by aia7892c on 2012-02-10 17:28:54

Harbin News Network (Intern reporter Wen Jinglu, reporter Liu Shubin) - From a free weight loss and body shaping experience to spending 2.3 million yuan over two years, Ms. Gao's weight not only didn't decrease but increased instead at Changfeng 3S Beauty Salon. Yesterday, after investigating and filing cases against 16 "problematic" beauty institutions, the city's Administration for Industry and Commerce (AIC) again issued notices of investigation into alleged forced consumption practices at Changfeng 3S Beauty Salon and Silan Baili Beauty Parlor.

At the end of 2009, consumer Ms. Gao received a free experience card from Changfeng 3S Beauty Salon while shopping in a certain mall in Dao Li’s Aijian district, with a promise of weight loss and body shaping services. Starting from an initial deposit of 10,000 yuan for the weight loss project up until November this year, Ms. Gao was repeatedly asked by the salon to pay under various pretexts, accumulating to a total of 2.3 million yuan. However, as of now, not only has Ms. Gao failed to lose weight, but she has also gained some weight. During this time, she also paid 40,000 yuan for facial spot removal, yet none of the spots on her face were removed.

In response to this, Director Wang Xukun of the Consumer Protection Department of the city's AIC stated that based on currently available clues and evidence, the Consumer Protection Department of the city's AIC has decided to initiate investigations into the suspected forced consumption practices of Changfeng 3S Beauty Salon, as well as illegal financing activities at Silan Baili Beauty Parlor.

Additionally, according to Wang Xukun, since the launch of the campaign to rectify "problematic" beauty institutions, the city's industrial and commercial departments have handled more than 240 complaints related to the beauty industry, achieving a resolution rate of 98.3% and a public satisfaction rate of 100%. Eighteen cases have been filed for investigation, over 20 consumer alerts and warning examples have been issued, and losses exceeding 7 million yuan have been recovered for consumers. Furthermore, the city's AIC has required all malls and supermarkets to prominently display large banners starting immediately to create a good atmosphere aimed at curbing forced consumption, promoting the healthy and orderly development of the beauty industry.
