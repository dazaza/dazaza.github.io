Xinyi Non-standard Door is a modern specialized enterprise that integrates research, development, production, design, manufacturing and service. The company owns advanced production equipment of domestic leading level, and gathers a large number of professional technical talents. Relying on its strong scientific and technological strength, the company has developed a series of products with domestic leading level by combining domestic and international realities. Better product quality, faster delivery time, better after-sales service and customer satisfaction are the persistent goals of Xinyi people. Facing future challenges, the company will turn pressure into motivation, be rooted in market demand, hold high the banner of honest operation, and continuously improve the brand connotation of Xinyi products which are "safe, harmonious, individualized and auspicious". All staff members of Xinyi Non-standard Door will keep pace with the times, pioneer and strive hard, and move forward courageously and innovate constantly in the fierce market with the spirit of being solid, dedicated and devoted to make Xinyi cause more brilliant!

I. Customer-oriented

"Customer-oriented" is the most basic principle of the product. Every employee and every work of Xinyi must revolve around the "customer" as the center, and all are for "customers". All employees should establish the concept of customers and partners between superiors and subordinates, left and right, upstream processes and downstream processes. Credit first and user satisfaction are our standards.

II. Pursuing the highest point of quality

Quality refers to the broad sense of quality, including product quality, decision-making quality, capital operation quality, personnel quality and so on. Xinyi emphasizes winning by "quality" and gaining trust by "quality". Quality is the starting point of corporate dignity and the starting point of respect for customers.

III. Pursuing the lowest cost point:

Cost is not only the cost at a point in time and a level, it runs through all aspects and all links of the company's business activities value chain system cost. Xinyi requires each employee to establish the concept of cost, reducing costs should start from each position, from a drop of water, a piece of paper, and truly achieve lean management.

IV. Our mission

To make each family feel assured, each customer satisfied, each merchant comfortable, each employee happy, society more trustworthy, and the future full of confidence. Xinyi promises that all customers using our company's products can enjoy free warranty for three years and lifetime repair services.
