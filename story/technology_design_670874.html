No-cost secret: Rice washing water for soft and smooth skin!

by vm2s3f0d0y8 on 2012-02-21 12:22:52

Why does rice washing water have beautifying functions? This is because the "water-soluble vitamins" and "minerals" in white rice that dissolve in water will remain in the rice washing water. Among these, Vitamin B complex is especially abundant. Since it is completely natural, it is not only suitable for sensitive skin but can also be used daily without any issues.

★ For a hydrated and smooth face: Use it as "face washing water"

Just by washing your face with rice washing water once every morning and evening, you can achieve beautifying effects. However, the rice washing water used for beauty purposes should not be arbitrary.

Materials: White rice, tap water

Steps:

Step 1. Place the white rice into a container and pour in tap water.

Step 2. After rubbing the rice, discard the first wash water, then pour in tap water again, rub it, and keep the second wash water for use.

Step 3. After leaving the retained rice washing water to settle overnight, take the milky white rice washing water and pour it into a basin for washing your face.

Step 4. Add warm water approximately 1.5 times the amount of the rice washing water. Your beauty rice washing water is ready! You can directly use it to wash your face!

Little Reminder: Rice washing water should be stored in the refrigerator if you want to keep it cool. However, it can only be refrigerated for one day and cannot exceed the second day, otherwise, it will ferment and spoil!

Enhanced Method: When performing the second rice wash, replace the clean water with "white wine", and you will obtain more nutritious "white wine rice washing water".

★ Secret for Hydrated and Smooth Face: Use as a "Face Mask"

Besides using rice washing water for skincare, applying the sediment from the rice washing water as a mask also yields excellent results.

Steps:

1. First, rinse the white rice lightly with water, then wash it thoroughly with a small amount of water, keeping the second wash water. Due to the smaller quantity of water used, the water will be thicker, and after leaving it overnight, more sediment will form.

2. Carefully pour off the water from the already settled rice washing water (but don't discard it; store it in another container), leaving the bottom sediment.

3. Before bathing, apply the sediment to your face using a massage technique. After about 15 minutes, when the sediment dries, apply the rice washing water again until all the rice washing water is used up.

4. After using all the rice washing water, let it dry naturally, and then rinse it off with warm water during your bath.

5. Finally, rinse your face with cold water to tighten the skin.

Little Reminder: The rice bran mud mask should be used once a week.

Enhanced Method: When the sediment dries, you can gently wipe it off with your hands for good results. Remember, if it's too dry, never force its removal; just gently wipe it off with warm water. You can add a little flour to the sediment to increase its viscosity.
