**Profile of Sakichihiro Kogane**

Sakichihiro Kogane was born on August 18, 1978, in Kogane-cho, Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan.

- Blood Type: A

- Height: 167 cm

- Measurements: B85cm W58cm H89cm

She graduated from the prestigious Waseda University, majoring in Art History in the Department of History under the First Division of Literature.

After graduation, she worked at a jewelry company. She later resigned and, in December 2001, co-founded the company "Kogane no Yoake" (Dawn of Gold), where she served as the representative.

In April 2002, she obtained qualifications as a psychological counselor and began working in psychological counseling, utilizing color therapy and tarot cards.

Sakichihiro Kogane is known as one of the most charismatic beauties in Asia who embodies the essence of gold. She also personally modeled for the "Kogane no Yoake" gold series.

On October 22, 2009, she collaborated with SOD to release her first AV work titled *Geinoujin Kogane Sakichihiro - Tentacle Orgasm*.

---

Sakichihiro Kogane has appeared in numerous TV dramas, films, albums, and as a host. She has also written columns for Japanese newspapers and magazines and penned lyrics for several artists. Additionally, she has released her own photo collections.
