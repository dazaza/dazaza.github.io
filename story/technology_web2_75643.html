Experience Pocket [Brief Review]: A sub-site launched by Qihoo for online note-taking, aiming to create a platform for experience sharing among users. Slogan: Put useful things in. [Operation and Features]: By submitting content online or saving selected content via a browser's right-click plugin, users can conveniently add to their favorites. It supports self-built categories, setting sharing permissions, friends, and RSS subscriptions. Through a personal pocket page (management center) with a dedicated domain name, users can easily display and manage content. Additionally, users can post requests for help to solicit answers from other users (similar to Baidu Zhidao); the forum-style "Experience Helper" offers more convenient interaction and mutual assistance; applying to be a "Class Bag Master" is more conducive to showcasing personal experiences. [Shortcomings]: Poor support for web page codes.

Personal Library [Brief Review]: A distinctive website where users can save, manage, and publish materials online, preserving web content vividly and authentically. Slogan: Your knowledge management and collaboration platform. [Operation and Features]: By saving selected content through a browser link bar or right-click plugin, users can conveniently add to their favorites. It also supports self-built categories, setting sharing permissions, recommending collected articles to websites, groups, or friends, and subscribing to RSS feeds. Through a personal homepage (management center) with a second-level domain name, users can easily display and manage content. Additionally, it provides blog display codes and article backup tools (click here for more details). [Shortcomings]: Speed instability due to server influence.

Google Notebook [Brief Review]: An online note-taking service offered by Google. [Operation and Features]: By saving selected content through the browser's right-click or plugin (click here to download the plugin), new notes can be saved as-is. Through the management center, users can set categories, paragraphs (i.e., small categories), and sharing options. The interface is simple, and the functions are powerful. [Shortcomings]: Does not offer a personal shared homepage similar to the Personal Library.

DoNote Online Notebook [Brief Review]: A comprehensive multi-functional online collection and note-taking website. Slogan: Your thoughtful assistant. [Operation and Features]: By saving selected content or URL links through a browser's right-click plugin (click here to download the plugin), both notes and bookmarks can have sharing permissions set. Additionally, through the management backend, users can add ledgers, accounts, music, etc., and manage various items. Shared content can be viewed through categorized shared pages, providing bookmark display codes. For added music, users can organize playlists and add them to blogs via code. [Shortcomings]: Slow webpage access speed, no tag (category) navigation or regular functions like saving for shared content by others, making it inconvenient to search and save.
