Sina Pinyin input method 1.0 officially launched today after a year of research and development

by cacard on 2010-10-12 16:38:13

On October 12, the Sina Pinyin input method 1.0, which took 1 year to develop, was officially launched today.

Official website (http://pinyin.sinaapp.com/index.php), according to the information in the input of core technology, input characteristics, beautiful sex and thesaurus quantity, etc., sina pinyin input method is more accurate, more rapid collection of smart technology and personalized.

Sina Pinyin input method features intelligent input, stylish appearance, independent development of the lexicon and daily update.

In terms of core input, Sina input method in addition to providing full spelling, simple spelling and other basic input, around dynamic word formation, dynamic word frequency, intelligent punctuation, intelligent deletion of words, etc., Sina Pinyin input method has a quality improvement, and launched a variety of characteristic input, such as: V mode input, website automatic completion, custom phrase input.

Dynamic word group, automatically capture the user's common vocabulary options, quickly understand the user's intention, high accuracy; Dynamic word frequency, familiar with user usage habits, priority display of common words, improve word selection speed; English complement, automatically complete the following characters, and provide candidates. V mode input, Chinese input state, enter the first letter v, turn on the v mode, you can open the input English, financial assistant, calculator function; Url automatically complete, enter www to enter the URL mode, automatically complete the URL; Enter a custom phrase. You can add a custom phrase by entering the custom phrase setting screen.

In terms of aesthetics, the skin includes Sina's small new small waves.

According to reports, the Sina Pinyin input method has an independently developed thesaurus, covering various fields, and provides a professional classification thesaurus, which classifies professional or specific field words and includes the latest network new words every day.

Yesterday, Sina internal email said that the research and development of Sina input method took 1 year, and it is not yet perfect.
