I have learned a lot of common knowledge and skills that are not found in books.

by prkwe1mwc6 on 2011-05-24 14:43:23

This year, I evaluated the business models of all public and private hospitals in Datong, changed many unreasonable processes in hospitals, making it more convenient for patients to seek medical treatment and reimbursement. This year, I walked through the streets of the county with my dreams; this year, I walked through schools and farms with love; this year, I walked deep into the mountains with passion. Every time I saw familiar farmers or received their blessing messages, I felt very happy. A lot of things that were not part of my job, I did for them: applying for major illness assistance for seriously ill patients, helping people in difficult living conditions apply for low-income assistance, small things like sending villagers home, taking them to the cafeteria for meals, to the bathhouse for a shower, helping them contact accommodation, etc., no matter how trivial, as long as they needed it, perhaps that's what I wanted too. Busy every day, my office might be the one that opens earliest and closes latest. My colleagues and I are working hard to make our cause bigger and stronger. Thank you, having such good colleagues who share weal and woe is also a kind of happiness in life. They never count their gains and losses, everyone does the work, never picky, and strive for a goal.

Time flies fast, before you know it, another year has passed with flowers blooming and wilting. The years' wheel unknowingly drew another circle. Reviewing my 2010 after changing jobs, I was busy every day. I didn't publish many articles, my garden was almost abandoned, rarely tended. Sometimes, I even forgot how to type some familiar words, feeling guilty and ashamed.

This year, my dream woke up.

Even though I went through wind and rain, running around in over a dozen counties for settlements and accounts receivable, I still feel fulfilled, with harvests. I learned a lot of knowledge and skills that are not in books. Compared to those long reports in the past that no one wanted to read or listen to, I still feel that I have done quite a few interesting things: 650 new rural cooperative medical service targets, 2.8 million yuan in revenue, 8 countryside medical consultations and agricultural support, delivering medical care and medicine to more than 2000 farmers, which may be more valuable than those words I wrote in the past.

This year, I never forgot to be filial to both sets of parents, visiting them on holidays and helping them with farm work; this year, I never imagined meeting classmates from Japan far away in my hometown; this year, I never forgot to strengthen my body, climbing mountains as usual, not famous mountains, but we just want to be happy; this year, I never forgot to ride bicycles, not scenic spots, but experiencing the difficulty of uphill and the smoothness of downhill in cycling. Occasionally, a few friends gathered together to write, drink wine, play music, dance slowly, and discuss card skills, feeling that life is colorful and full of sunshine.

In the past, I have won various awards, mostly related to writing. At this year's model worker conference, when I stepped onto the award stage, my heart surged with emotion. This is the recognition of my work by everyone. Although fame and profit fell to me, bitterness and fatigue fell to everyone, I still have a grateful heart, thanking those fellow villagers. I am more grateful for the help and encouragement given by leaders and colleagues at work. Without their support, I would not have persisted until today. Several times in stormy weather, I steadily walked on the urban road; several times when collecting debts, I encountered disdainful looks, but I persevered regardless of difficulties; several times to let farmers spend less money and get more reimbursement, I wandered through each ward and department explaining policies, advocating the "service concept of going to the ward with emotions and prescribing for farmers," until I got mouth ulcers. Hard work pays off, and I achieved the expected results.

When the new car came back, I could learn to drive, no longer trembling in the cold wind waiting for the bus. This year, our salary increased, the child started high school, our whole family are lucky ones, everything went smoothly...
