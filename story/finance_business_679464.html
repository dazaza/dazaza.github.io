Perhaps, a man can only be Xiao Bei once in his life, and a woman can only be Mi Lai once...

Xiao Bei didn't earn much money because he was saving up. He himself wouldn't splurge on good food or clothes, but when his girlfriend Hai Zao's eyes lit up at the sight of Haagen-Dazs ice cream, he would unhesitatingly buy this luxury for her. Although he couldn't take Hai Zao to high-end restaurants, he would happily leave his favorite braised pork for her. He could hand over his entire paycheck to his girlfriend and shout: "Everything I have is yours! Please kindly accept it!" Every morning, Xiao Bei would prepare breakfast for Hai Zao, even squeezing toothpaste for her; at noon, he would chat with Hai Zao on MSN: "Little piggy, where are we going to eat tonight?" In the evening after work, he would call her: "Where are you now?" He planned to marry Hai Zao within a year and save up for a house loan within two years. In all his future plans, Hai Zao was the only leading lady.

Xiao Bei was young and temporarily didn't have much money, but he worked hard, was reliable, and kind. He had clear career plans and steadily approached his goals. If Hai Zao was willing to patiently stay with him, their dream of having a small nest in a big city wasn't impossible to achieve. After all, young boys aren't afraid of not having money, but rather the lack of willingness to strive. Just like Xia Lin said to Lu Tao in "Struggle": "As long as you keep trying, whether you have money or not, I will always love you!"

I admire Xiao Bei's demeanor; he is such a big boy who always lets Hai Zao have her way, loves to joke around, and delights in making Hai Zao happy. A boy who combines love and rationality well, ambitious, hardworking, passionate about life, and capable of creating little romances. Suddenly, I envy Hai Zao, envying everything she has, admiring her beloved Xiao Bei. I wish that when I hesitate, someone would be by my side saying, "I'm here!" Then pulling me along to buy the ice cream I crave, which might be a bit extravagant for us.

I wish someone would cook delicious meals for me, treating me like a child and making me happy. I wish when I type "feeling down" on QQ, someone would immediately call to check on me. I wish someone would find time to take me out to celebrate every memorable day. I wish even after knowing Hai Zao betrayed him, Xiao Bei cried so restrained that morning, fearing to wake Hai Zao. Covering his mouth with a towel, tears streaming down his face, he said: "Hai Zao, I love you, I can't give up on you, I've loved you to the bone."

When Xiao Bei went to Hai Ping's house for dinner, he would pretend to be cheerful and give gifts to Xu Xu. During the meal, he buried his head in his bowl, forcing a smile while holding back tears. That's Xiao Bei, despite not being wealthy, but considerate, enthusiastic, serious, and deep. He would cry and tell Hai Zao, "I should let you know what loyalty means." He is the Xiao Bei who can't help but make people love him.

We all know that Song Siming could give Hai Zao things Xiao Bei couldn't - neither excitement nor material wealth. However, Xiao Bei gave Hai Zao everything, pampering her, loving her, cherishing her, saving money for a house, preparing for marriage and children. What Song Siming gave was effortless; he wanted Hai Zao, family, status, and Hai Zao's children. But without these, he would still be Song Siming. And Xiao Bei, just Xiao Bei, gave all his heart to Hai Zao. He indulged her, let her have her way, as Hai Zao said, "He knows how to care for others, unlike an only child." He called Hai Zao "little piggy," but his little piggy grew up and was taken away by someone else. Xiao Bei gave all his heart to Hai Zao, and in the end, Hai Zao abandoned that heart and left with someone else.

Young love under the sun is so beautiful, mornings with his breakfast, nights in his embrace. You can walk hand-in-hand on the street, race, eat from street vendors; instead of calling and hearing, "I'm in a meeting," or getting hung up on. Eating together doesn't have to be at fancy restaurants, he can be with you all the time, no need to flatter or deceive him into thinking you're a virgin, you can walk arm-in-arm, and don't have to worry about his first wife showing up anytime.

Until one day, Hai Zao walking on the street sees another girl standing where she should be next to Xiao Bei. She finally realizes, "I lost my own happiness." Actually, the admired Song Siming, if we turn back 20 years, was also a naive Xiao Bei. Many successful men we see now, elegant and wealthy like Li Kaifu, only his original wife understands his youth, immaturity, and even poverty.

Therefore, as long as you are confident about the future, Xiao Bei will eventually fulfill your dreams. As a young girl, if you're willing to be with a boy like Xiao Bei, don't just think about whether he has enough money to satisfy your current material needs or whether he has the potential to become a Song Siming or Li Kaifu. The only reason is: you love him, and he deserves your love. For this reason, we don't need him to be a very successful rich person, nor do we need to feel our youth is wasted and expect some form of return. We only need to sincerely and genuinely love.

Take good care of your Xiao Bei nearby, whatever happens, don't lose him.

There is a kind of love called Mi Lai.

She said, "I'll wait for you, I'll wait for you, if you're not married, I'll wait for you, even if you get married, I'll still wait. I'm waiting for you now, I'll wait for you later, I'll wait forever, I'll wait for you, I'll wait for you, I'll wait until death."

She said, "I've forgotten, I've even forgotten what flavor of ice cream there is, I haven't eaten ice cream since then."

She said, "I just like doing various things for you, it seems like I've been waiting to do various things for you."

She said, "He (Lu Tao) never knew that I needed him to hold my hand and move forward. Without him, no matter how beautiful the scenery ahead, I don't want to see it, I don't want to see anything."

She said, "I just knew that when I was being deceived by you, I felt the most satisfied inside."

She said, "I want to sing. When I was in school, I had a wish, which was to sing only for you. Left side. I'll sing the left side."

She said, "That was the happiest place for me before. Until now, I've never gone back to KFC, not even the ones in America, I hate all KFCs in the world. Actually, I'm scared of all KFCs in the world because they are my painful places."

She said, and when she said it, her face was full of calmness.

Mi Lai was like that. Giving, unconditional giving, unconditional giving for love. Not caring about anything, as long as you need, as long as I have, because I love you, I am willing. The person she recognized in her heart was the entire meaning of her life. She sang "Left Side" for Lu Tao, she had so many hesitations and pains, yet stubbornly didn't want Lu Tao to compensate her with anything, as long as he was there, allowing her to be good to him, that was happiness.

I liked seeing Mi Lai smile, her silly joy, as if Lu Tao was her whole world. Even a bowl of ice cream made her feel immense happiness. Finally, she said, "We've done everything we could, no regrets, I'll remember the days spent with you, remember that feeling, everything was real, there were so many beautiful moments, goodbye." Even leaving was straightforward and generous.

Maybe the story ends here perfectly. Mi Lai is the perfect Mi Lai. They say, a failed relationship in life is destined to teach us more things. Teach what? Teach what love is, how to express without misunderstanding, how to protect oneself.

Protecting oneself. This is perhaps something Mi Lai never did. Looking at Mi Lai makes one think of a moth flying towards fire!

It hurts so much! Hurting for her, and also hurting for girls like her who persistently love without results. Many people start out like Mi Lai, or even dumber than Mi Lai, willing to do anything, absurdly, clumsily, disregarding everything. The stronger the love, the easier it is to lose one's mind. The person no one looks favorably upon, the love no one looks favorably upon, yet one still refuses to let go even when battered and bruised. Not understanding what balance means, only knowing "I love him, I will give everything I have." For a person who doesn't love them much, or doesn't love them at all, or worse—loves someone else, they force themselves to forget, only to break down again and again when they see him.

Then, one learns to think of oneself first, learns not to give everything, learns to look back when moving forward recklessly, to see if there is a way out. Learns to doubt and distrust, feeling foolish when others don't love them enough. Learns to consider how far and how long they can go together. Such maturity is hard. Hard on oneself, because one is no longer the original self. If possible, how willing one would be to have a simple and beautiful love, where you love me, and I love you, nothing changes, everything is trusted. But the knife before was too cruel, too heavy, unwilling to be hurt again.

And that person is even more innocent. Like Mi Lai's next boyfriend, actually not Lu Tao, yet bearing the harm Lu Tao inflicted on Mi Lai. To soothe a heart already tired and cautious from love is no easy task. No one is wrong, using a cliché truth, there is no right or wrong. The previous jerk A might be the victim B of someone else. Perhaps everyone makes one person sad, while being made sad by another person. This is the conservation of energy in love, and it determines that we can only be Mi Lai once in our lifetime!

Only once in a lifetime can we be Mi Lai. This time, we don't care about anything, wanting to share everything we have with the person in our hearts. Still remember the conversation between Mi Lai and Lu Tao in the garden, maybe it really is like that, loving someone so much that one wishes to become a boy, so they can be buddies, crazy together, noisy together, unable to be lovers, but still hoping to spend all day together. We will have such a time in our lives when we disregard everything, perhaps only this once. This time, our hearts are broken, hating so-called love.

So-called love should be like this, like Mi Lai.

So-called disregard for everything should be like this, like Mi Lai.

A kind of beauty is called Mi Lai.

Only once in a lifetime can we be Mi Lai.
