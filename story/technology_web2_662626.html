The town family planning office issued a notice requiring to track the provincial inspection team (picture)

by daidi2mqy on 2012-02-10 14:00:20

Netizens posted the notice sent by Qi Town Family Planning Office to all villages.

According to Hanzhong News (reported by He Jie), the provincial family planning inspection team is about to arrive. The Qi Town Family Planning Office in Yang County has sent a written notice to all villages in advance: if the following vehicle numbers are found, immediately report to the town family planning office. In response, some netizens jokingly said, "the provincial inspection team has been wanted."

Netizens' post: the provincial inspection team "has been wanted"

On January 8, a netizen posted a thread titled "Wanted Notice of Qishi Town Government, Yang County, Hanzhong" on the Hanzhong Bar. The content was only one photo, which was a copy of an official notice, but there was no header and no issuing unit name. The content was as follows:

The leader and car number of the fifth group for the southern Shaanxi family planning spot check: if any of the following car numbers and people go to the village health room or visit households to inquire about family planning situations, please report to the town family planning office in time, and immediately organize village cadres to track and investigate. If problems are found, report them to the town family planning office in time.

Mou XX, Shaan C26XXX; Li XX, Shaan AC5XXX; Wu XX, Shaan D93XXX;

He XX, Shaan J30XXX; Li XX, Shaan E74XXX. The signature was "Qi Town Family Planning Office", but there was no official seal.

Some netizens said that the meaning of this notice was too obvious, "this is obviously to prevent higher-level inspections!" Mr. Liu, a citizen of Hanzhong, pointed to the phrase "investigate and track" and said, "does the inspection team need village cadres to investigate and track the actions of the inspection team when they come to inspect work? And 'report problems in time', this clearly means having something to hide."

Some netizens also expressed doubts: "if it represents the government, there should be an official seal, but there is nothing here."

Village doctor confirmed: indeed received a written notice

After checking, the reporter learned that Mou XX is indeed a leader of the Provincial Population and Family Planning Commission, who is recently inspecting work in various places.

Yesterday, the reporter randomly visited several villages around Qi Town. Some villagers said they had not heard of such notices, while others said there seemed to be something like that, but they did not pay attention at the time. In a village health room belonging to Qi Town, the doctor on duty confirmed to the reporter that he indeed received such a notice a few days ago.

The doctor definitely told the reporter that he received a written notice. At that time, he did not pay attention to this matter, and there were many patients, so the written notice had long disappeared. But he still remembered that there were four or five car numbers on the notice. He said that although there were car numbers, he did not pay attention to whether these cars entered or left the village.

Town Party Secretary: the wording of the notice was inappropriate

Yesterday afternoon, the gate of the Qi Town Family Planning Office was tightly closed. "I am not aware of the notice issued by the town family planning office at that time." Said Liang Xin, the party secretary of the town. When the provincial inspection team comes to the grassroots for inspection, according to past practices, local government personnel do not need to accompany them. "In previous years, we were worried that they might not be familiar with the road, so we wanted to accompany them for inspection, but they all disagreed."

"And, where the inspection team goes to inspect, which household to inspect, and when to inspect, we have no way of knowing." Liang Xin said, but as a grassroots government, they must prepare materials and report to the inspection team at any time, hence the issuance of notices by the family planning office. "The family planning office also hopes that after the inspection team arrives, they can timely grasp the situation, so as to report to the inspection team at any time."

How did the family planning office get detailed information about the car numbers and names of the inspection team members? Liang Xin said that it was possible that after the inspection team inspected other places, the message "spread" out and was known by the town family planning office.

"The wording of this notice by the family planning office was inappropriate." Liang Xin said, if the inspection team really finds any problems in each village of Qi Town, there would be no way to "remedy" it, and the inspection team would directly report to the higher authorities. "Family planning office staff just want to know the dynamics of the inspection team, absolutely not with the intention of tracking and investigating as stated in the notice, their wording was inappropriate."

Yesterday, the reporter contacted the provincial family planning commission, but the call was not connected.
