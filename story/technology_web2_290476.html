For herbal cooking: This kind of sect task is everyone's favorite, but there are still some small knowledge points within. Don't keep clicking the mouse, and don't click too fast. Look carefully, aim accurately before clicking. Once you click on the option below the task of the sect, close it and re-click the task. Very good, you've just wasted time on one task. The sect tasks are divided into four parts: requiring items, delivering messages, finding pets, and patrolling.

Equipment and finding pets: The key word is "automatic navigation". It has been a long time since I last wrote something, today I finally have some spare time to write something. Often people ask, how do you level up so high? In fact, I started brushing the sect from level 47 until level 86. And indeed, the sect tasks are currently the fastest way to level up in the game. Its advantage lies in not having to spend time looking for a team, not having to worry about unexpected accidents like burning doubles or catching ghosts, such as jumping off the train. Some might ask, isn't burning doubles boring and tiring, what's the point of playing single-player games? Let's look at it from another perspective. Every day with the same online time, we're all leveling up. To brush the sect, first prepare everything you need for your current level, set the sect flag. Below is part of the method I use when brushing the sect. This depends on personal habits. When doing sect tasks, try to clear out other things unrelated to the sect tasks from your bag as much as possible to ensure the efficiency of the sect tasks.

Delivering messages: Under the premise of setting the flag, it's important to be familiar with each point of each flag and what NPC is assigned. Familiarity breeds proficiency. As soon as you receive the task, quickly glance at the keyword description of the task, combine it with the NPC avatar that pops up on the right, and directly fly to the designated point to complete the task. It seems simple, but to be familiar with each NPC avatar and memorize the task keywords quickly takes quite some time. Once you make a wrong flight, it wastes 2-3 seconds.

Patrol: Stand near the master's position and start automatic encounter monsters. Set up automatic combat well. After the task ends, don't rush to turn off the automatic encounter monsters, if possible, continue patrolling. Start the task while encountering monsters, which also saves time. I can brush 18 million per day, you might only get 10 million even if you work hard. When I reach level 100, I can enjoy the game leisurely every day, while you are still busy finding teams to level up. I think the sect is the most relaxed way to level up except for burning doubles and hanging automatically.

For item delivery tasks without prompts, such as Taobao, fly to the store entrance and click the upper left task tracker as you enter the map, manually pop up the dialogue box, buy the goods, return to the sect automatically navigating. If you receive a task to deliver a pet, detailed task prompts will pop up. Don't close it. Drag the task bar to a place that won't affect your vision like me. Fly to the Chamber of Commerce, buy the pet, and when you return to the sect, instantly click the ghost king automatic navigation in the task bar.

To start the 40-level equipment at level 60, you must open your own item shop. With 10 pieces of each type of jewelry, weapons, and armor prepared, you can brush for 24 hours. That is, replenish the store once a day. The materials required to replenish the store can be acquired by opening a minor account during normal patrol times. On the second page of the package, buy 2-3 pieces of various 30-level jewelry.

Saving 2-3 seconds in each round and each task accumulates to a very considerable amount of experience throughout the day. I would like to share with you my small experience in brushing the sect.
